Sosnovaya Polyana is a municipal district of St. Petersburg. History, Description and Attractions

Sosnovaya Polyana is a municipal district of St. Petersburg that arose on the site of an old summer cottage village, as well as on the entire territory adjacent to it with picturesque forests. After the end of World War II, various enterprises began to appear here, so it took a larger amount of housing, which needed to be provided for workers. Thus, intensive construction unfolded in this area, thanks to which this area is now built up with high-rise buildings, entertainment centers and parks.

Place history

Until the beginning of the XVIII century, in this territory there was mainly coniferous forest, which had a length from the Volkhonsky highway to the village of Volodarsky. A little later, Sosnovaya Polyana (St. Petersburg) began to be built up from the south - from the side of the Peterhof road. At that time only suburban estates were built there.

Shortly before the revolution, Sosnovaya Polyana was a holiday village with wooden buildings, which was almost destroyed during the hostilities.

Until the nineties of the XX century, in this territory one could see private dachas with vegetable gardens, which were later demolished. Today, the Krasnoselsky district of St. Petersburg is built up with comfortable houses, and its feature is that there is not a single industrial enterprise and not a single metro station. Sosnovaya Polyana is part of the Krasnoselsky district.





Infrastructure

In this district there are: 4 comprehensive schools, 9 kindergartens, lyceum, boarding school, vocational education institution and two higher educational institutions. In addition, children can attend the house of children and youth creativity located there, as well as the art school.

Sosnovaya Polyana on its territory has 3 polyclinics, 42 consumer services facilities providing various services, and there are also 30 gyms, playgrounds and several leisure facilities in the microdistrict.





Coat of arms

A golden carriage is depicted in the very center of the symbol representing Sosnovaya Polyana district, and just below it we see a branch serving as a reminder that a suburban village was located in this place until the sixties.

The background part of the picture is presented in green and red shades, which are arranged in a checkerboard pattern. The first of them symbolizes a well-maintained and rich green district, and the second represents courage and dedication.
