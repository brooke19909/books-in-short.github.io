Basilica Apartments 3 * (Cyprus): description and reviews

The best options for accommodation and budget vacations can always be found on the island of Cyprus. 3 * hotels in the elite resort of Paphos - an ideal ratio of price and quality. Guests are accommodated in well-maintained, comfortable apartments with beautiful interiors. Most of these hotels are adjacent to the beaches.

Hotel location

The hotel, which will be discussed later, is located in the center of the tourist quarter of Kato, in the city of Paphos. Hotel Basilica Apartments 3 * is surrounded by bars, shops and cafes. It is adjacent to a fishing harbor and a supermarket. There is a promenade next to the hotel.





Description of rooms

The hotel has 197 elegant apartments. It has miniature studio rooms - one room with a kitchenette. And more spacious apartments - a bedroom (one or two) and a living room with a tiny kitchen. Kitchenettes include stoves, refrigerators and kitchenware.

In addition, there are studio suites and suites (air conditioning is not subject to extra charges). Bathrooms are fitted with a shower and a hairdryer. Almost every room at the Basilica Apartments 3 * hotel (Cyprus) has a balcony and a TV with several channels. There is also a telephone.





Hotel infrastructure

Guests use the hotel laundry services, visit the hairdresser for an additional fee, rent a safe, relax in the TV room. Luggage storage is provided. The hotel has a car park.

A playroom has been created for children's leisure, where the kids are supervised by staff. Basilica Apartments 3 * hotel is equipped with two pools: indoor and outdoor.

Nutrition

Only breakfast is included. The buffet is served with cheeses, sausages, egg dishes, fresh pastries, juices, fruits and other breakfast items. The rest of the food must be paid extra. Guests are offered a bite to eat in a restaurant and two bars. One of them is located in the lobby, the second - in the pool area. Guests in the restaurants indulge in Greek dishes and international dishes. The bars serve refreshing cocktails that taste good.

Entertainment and sport at the hotel

The territory of the Basilica Apartments 3 * hotel is equipped with table tennis tables, slot machines. Residents of the hotel have access to the tennis courts of the Paphos Gardens hotel complex, which is a 15-minute walk away.





During the day, water aerobics classes are held by the pool. In the evenings, the hotel organizes theme nights. Those who want more fun go to the vibrant neighborhoods of Paphos, built up with enchanting entertainment venues.

The hotel guide offers interesting excursion programs. Tourists really like the tireless sightseeing tour of Paphos and walks in the zoo, the main inhabitants of which are birds.

Hotel Traveler Reviews

According to travelers, the hotel is very well located. In a couple of minutes you can walk to the coastline, port, archaeological museum and other cultural and historical monuments. Some of the windows of the hotel overlook the square of the Apostle Paul.





Quite spacious rooms are available at the Basilica Apartments 3 * hotel. Reviews about them are mostly good. They are regularly cleaned, often changed bed and towels. Tips for cleaning do not take. The rooms are furnished with good furniture, equipped with excellent fixtures. Among the hotel staff there is always a Russian-speaking manager.
