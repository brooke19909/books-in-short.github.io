Clay products - where to start

Clay modeling is a very exciting process. He helps to reveal his creative potentials and develops imagination. Feel like an artist and give birth to your personal masterpiece!

Nowadays it will not be difficult to find clay for modeling in stores . This is a very common product and it can be of different types and colors. Also, certain shades can be obtained by mixing plastics of various tones.



Polymer clay (or as it is also called, thermoplastics) is a plastic that looks like plasticine, but hardens at high temperatures. Clay products - jewelry, buttons, pendants, key rings, figurines and much more that your imagination is enough for - is a very nice handmade souvenir.

So, you decided to make some polymer clay products with your own hands. What is needed for this?

There are several stages, upon which you will make excellent clay products. On the Internet there are many different workshops on modeling clay polymer.
