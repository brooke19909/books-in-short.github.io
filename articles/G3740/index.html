Security annunciator S2000-SMK

A security detector is a certain electronic device that generates and sends a specific signal in response to a change in some set parameter. And it is precisely such detectors that will be discussed in the article. To be more precise, we will consider a security detector model S2000-SMK.

What is a security detector and what is it for?

As already mentioned, the detectors are needed in order to notify about changes in certain parameters. This means that in one situation the sensor may notice movement, therefore, it will react to a change in the position of the bodies, and in another it will sense a change in pressure on the surface, thereby recording a violation and giving a signal.

About types of security detectors

The first classification is determined by the type of controlled area. Here you can find point, surface, linear and volumetric examples of such a device. They can also be distinguished by the principle of action, and this classification will already be much wider. Now we will name only a few species.





So, the first will be an electric contact detector, which is the simplest type among its relatives and is intended primarily to protect building structures (glass, doors, gates, walls and similar things) from penetration.

As for magnetic contact detectors, they are needed to block a variety of building structures for opening (the same doors, windows, hatches, gates). This type of detector is discussed below.

Detector model S2000-SMK

The C200-SMK car belongs to magnetically contact addressable detectors. Most often it is used to protect door and window openings, both plastic and wooden. Works in conjunction with the controller S2000-KDP. The detector fires and sends a signal when a door or window is opened.

It is fully protected from false positives. The security detector S2000-SMK can easily be checked for correct operation using a simple magnet. Among the advantages of this model, it is worth highlighting the following: modern design next to small sizes, as well as a small current consumption.





At S2000-QMS, the violation of the zone occurs in no more than 300 ms, its mass does not exceed 45 grams, and the average service life is 10 years. As for the dimensions, everything is very modest: 55 x 10 x 8 millimeters. It is mounted on the wall and withstands humidity of 93% at a temperature of +40 degrees Celsius. Able to work in the temperature range from -30 to +50 degrees Celsius.
