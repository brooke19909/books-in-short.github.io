Cash loan without income statement: which banks issue, registration

Sometimes there are situations when money is needed very urgently. At the same time, there is absolutely no time to collect the relevant documents for obtaining a loan.

It happens differently. Most of the inhabitants of Russia do not have formal work or work for the minimum wage. Most Russians consider the salaries in the envelope to be quite normal. True, until the moment when you have to get a loan.

Can I get a cash loan without income statement? How to do this, and which organizations issue such loans? About all this further ...



Who will be given a loan without a certificate?

Not everyone will be able to get a cash loan without an income statement. If you have earlier outstanding debts, bad credit history or other negative factors, the manager will definitely see this. In this case, you will most likely be refused. Loans without collateral and without income statement are considered high-risk. Therefore, if you do not have a very good reputation, you will not see money.

To try to get a loan, you must meet the following conditions:

  • Be a citizen of the country in which you intend to receive money.
  • Have the right age. In each financial institution, this indicator may vary. The lower limit can be set within 18-24 years. Upper - 60‚Äì70 years.
  • Be permanently registered in the region where the branch of the selected bank is located.


If you want to receive money, but cannot officially confirm your wealth, you can get a loan without a passport income statement and another document. One of the following is quite suitable:

  • SNILS or compulsory medical insurance policy;
  • car rights;
  • valid passport;
  • military ID;
  • a work book or a copy certified by a notary;
  • TIN;
  • state certificate of car registration.

Each financial institution has its own exhaustive list. The essence of presenting the second document is to further confirm your identity and establish at least some degree of your solvency.

Representatives of vulnerable groups of the population have practically no chance to take a cash loan without income statement:

  • pensioners;
  • moms on maternity leave;
  • unemployed;
  • disabled people and other categories of the population applying for social assistance.

Although, subject to certain additional conditions, the provision of a surety or a good guarantee, they can also issue a loan without income statements.

MFI

If you need a not too large amount, it is easiest to contact a microfinance organization. Reliable banks practically do not issue mini-loans in the amount of 1 to 15 thousand. Earnings of the bank for them will be so meager that you should not get involved.

Therefore, if you want to get a quick cash loan without a certificate of income, you have a direct road to the MFI. Most likely, they will not ask unnecessary questions there. They are not interested in either your bad credit history or the source of income. For such firms, the main thing is that you sign a document confirming your consent to pay considerable interest. MFIs often provide loans even to officially unemployed people.

Interest rates here are very large and can reach 3% per day. And the loan term, on the contrary, is small and is no more than a month. Conditions, of course, are so-so. But if you need money immediately, this can be a good way out.

Express loan

Such a loan without a passport income certificate is easy to obtain. In this case, we can talk about amounts in the range from 5 to 50 thousand rubles. Banks most often give out such loans most of all, including without documents confirming the level of income. More often than not, a customer simply receives a credit card.



In this case, practically any client who does not have gross violations in the credit history can apply for a quick cash loan without an income statement. Moreover, if you apply with a similar request to the bank where you have already issued a loan earlier, the percentage may be slightly reduced. Banks love to demonstrate their loyalty to regular customers. In various financial institutions, the interest rate on such loans can vary from 28 to 50%. And the decision to issue money will be made in two hours.

Amounts from 50 to 300 thousand rubles fall under this concept. Today it is one of the most popular banking products. Most often, for a standard loan, you will still be asked to prepare a complete set of documents. But it could be otherwise.

  • Renaissance Credit - will offer to overpay 27.7% per year and issue no more than half a million for up to 60 months. At the same time, you will be asked to confirm that at the last place of work you worked for at least 3 months.

Large loan amount

In the main part of banking institutions, it is almost impossible to obtain a loan in the amount of from half a million to 1.5 million rubles without a guarantee of their solvency. In addition, about 75% of institutions will ask you to provide an additional guarantor.



Although it is difficult to find a financial institution where they will give out such an amount and not ask how much you earn, it is still possible. For example, you can try your luck at Sberbank. Especially if you are their regular customers and get a salary or pension there.

If you still manage to find a bank that agrees to issue such a loan, the interest rate may make you happy. It can be from 17 to 25%.

How to get a loan without income statement?

There are several situations that contribute to the early adoption of a decision on the issuance of a loan without confirmation of financial well-being:

  • "Salary" client. This is a category of borrowers who receive wages in the same bank where they applied for a loan. For such clients, the manager may offer more favorable conditions or not ask about the level and source of your income.
  • The customer has an ideal credit history. The bank carries out a scoring procedure and analyzes previous requests for money. Based on the information received, a positive decision can be made regardless of whether you have 2-PIT or not.
  • Writing a certificate in the form developed by the bank. If you do not want to confirm the income officially, the financial institution may offer you to bring a certificate in free spelling or fill out a special form developed by the bank.
