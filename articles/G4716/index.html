Before you start knitting such a wardrobe item as a jacket for a girl (with knitting needles), you need to make a product pattern. It is necessary if sewn sleeves are knitted. If raglan sleeves are made, then you can do without it.

The front part of the blouse consists of two parts - the right and left shelves. They are exactly the same, only they are knitted in a mirror image. First, the lower part of the blouse is drawn, then the middle. The lateral line should be parallel to the middle, but it is shorter and ends where the armhole begins. From here, a semicircular line is drawn to the shoulder. The neckline is made semicircular, triangular, depending on what kind of jacket for the girl will be connected with knitting needles. The back is one-piece. Sleeves at the brush line are slightly smaller than at the shoulder. Near the shoulder, the sleeve is made semicircular.

How to knit a sweater for a girl with knitting needles

First, a small sample is knitted to determine the density of knitting and to understand how many loops to collect for each part. For example, 1 cm - 1 loop. Then as many loops are typed as how many centimeters each detail has at the bottom, since a model with a sewn-in sleeve, unlike a raglan, is knitted from the bottom.





The picture shows a red jacket with a blue trim for a girl. Even a beginner needlewoman can knit her with knitting needles. To create this product, one of the simplest patterns is used - scarf knitting. Despite its simplicity, the blouse looks spectacular.

To do such a thing, you need to knit either only facial, or only wrong. The purl loops create a little longer, so novice craftswomen can use facial ones. Then they need to start knitting from the wrong side. This is how a jacket for a girl fits with knitting needles. The diagram clearly shows how to create a garter stitch using the wrong loops.

Create a cozy beauty

A sweater for a girl with knitting needles begins to be knotted from below. To do this, first dial on the right number of loops on both knitting needles and knit a garter stitch to the armpits. A small semicircle is created here, directed inward by decreasing, and then a set of loops. For young children, you can not do this, but knit right up to the neckline. Cutout is required. Once at the top of the midline line the shelves began to gradually close the loops to make a neck, you should also start closing the loops from shoulder to neck, but in a straight line.





Also, but only one-piece and with a smaller neckline fits the back. If the armhole is not semicircular, but straight, then the semicircle also does not get knitted on the sleeves, but they end with a straight line. Now, manually or on a sewing machine, slightly stretching the knit, you need to sew all the details. They are ironed. Further, the bottom, sleeves and neck of the product are tied with blue thread. On the left half of the shelf at the place of the strapping, several air loops are made to make button loops.

The blouse is ready. Let the little princess put on her faster and walks in a beautiful designer thing created in a single copy.
