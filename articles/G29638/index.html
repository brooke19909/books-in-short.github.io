Svetloe Lake in the Urals and Altai

Ural, Altai - the regions in Russia are truly unique. Amazing energy, amazingly beautiful landscapes, healthy ecology, the charm of hoary antiquity and the cultures of the ancestral peoples - all this makes these places a place of pilgrimage for tourists, outdoor enthusiasts, fresh, vivid sensations, unity with nature.

Swan expanse



Light Lake. It is necessary to pronounce this name with the inhabitants of the Soviet district in the Altai region, as they nod their heads approvingly. Yes, it's great there. Amazing. Clear water and swans. A lot of swans. Proud handsome birds wait here for the winter. Therefore, Svetloye Lake is also called Swan. Locals will tell a lot of interesting things about Altai. They are proud of their homeland. And they are right. For example, in Russia, only in two protected areas does a rare species of screaming swans feel at ease. This is Chukotka and Altai, Lake Svetloye. The secret of the special transparency of its water, and throughout the entire territory and depth, lies in the springs. They provide ground nutrition to the lake. And along the reservoir there are enough keys, clean and very tasty. Do you know why these lands were chosen by swan flocks? The fact is that Lake Svetloye does not freeze in winter! Underground streams heat it even in severe frosts. When the air temperature exceeds -40, in the pond it is equal to +6 degrees. Here is such a natural math! By the way, since 1973 the neighborhood has been declared a territory protected by the state. They are considered reserve even now.

Geographic parameters





Svetloye Lake (Altai Territory) is located near the left bank of the Katun River, sung in verse by Nikolai Rubtsov. It, in turn, gives rise to another famous Altai river - the First Koksha. Here is the famous trout farm. This is the second reason why swans have chosen the lake. If in the seventies of the last century their number was limited to fifteen individuals, now the total number in packs has exceeded three hundred and fifty. Not far from Svetly there is a village with a sonorous name - Harvest. Its inhabitants are happy to guide you to the observation deck, from where you can watch the birds, their habits, admire the beauty and grandeur. People try to feed feathered guests, you yourself can take part in an exciting process. Of course, hunting here is by no means impossible. But if you like to sit on the shore with a fishing rod, then be sure to come to stay on Lake Svetloye! Fishing here, admittedly, is excellent!

We are moving to the Urals



We will now get acquainted with the namesake of the Altai landmark. This pond is also surprising in its own way and has long been a favorite vacation spot for both old-timers and visiting people. Where is the second Svetloye lake located? Severouralsk is the nearest large settlement, located just 55 kilometers from it. The water body itself is part of the Vsevolod-Blagodatsky lake system. Its bottom was a natural hollow inside the forest. The pond does not have drains, but it is always clean. Replenished by melting glaciers in the mountains and the Shegultan River. Recreation facilities



The recreation center "Bright Lake" is the largest of the tourist campsites in the vicinity. Service is quite up to standard. You can rent catamarans and boats to enjoy a boat trip. In good weather of the warm season, you can sunbathe and swim without fear of catching a skin infection. And in winter, enjoy skating, skiing and sledding. And even swim in the hole! Yes, fishing - and it is present! In summer, the whole lake is at your service, and in winter, of course, holes are made in designated areas. Pike, roach, crucian carp, gudgeon, ruff, carp can catch you on a hook. Get positive emotions and impressions from rest in abundance!

Log cabins with amenities and even fireplaces for winter guests. In designated areas on the grills, you can cook kebabs or fry freshly caught fish. There is even a hotel.
