Learning to make modular origami swans





"Origami" in translation from Japanese means "folded paper." Such art was very popular with samurai. These brave people made such souvenirs from paper and gave them to each other, thereby expressing their respect. Some of the figures, according to popular belief, generally brought luck to their owner. After some time, parchment crafts began to be made in China. Here, in general, the ability to create figures by folding paper was the rule of good form.

Main point

Nowadays, in China, origami has become a recognized international art. It has many types, one of which is the so-called modular origami. The main feature of modular origami is that the figure is created by adding together a large number of identical particles, the so-called "modules". Each module is made from a separate sheet. The figure is created by inserting the module into the module. The more such details are used to create a certain image, the more voluminous and believable the creation becomes. Let's look at the modular origami of swans. To create a bird according to the proposed scheme, you do not need special skills.



Work Stages

To make a modular origami "Swan", the scheme of work will be as follows:

  1. Take about 500 rectangles from paper 40x60 mm in size and one red beak module.
  2. From these (white or colored) rectangles, you need to make triangular origami modules.
  3. We place the first three modules horizontally, inserting the corners of 1 and 2 modules into two pockets of the 3rd module.
  4. The first ring of the craft will consist of five modules stacked in two rows in the outer and inner circle. Thirty modules will be present in each row.

We continue to create

So, the modular origami "Swan". The assembly diagram will be as follows. The modules are laid out in a checkerboard pattern, and the extreme module closes the ends of the chain. When folding modular origami swans, you should know that one of the features of classic origami is that no glue, tape or duct tape is needed during operation.

When you already build five rows of the body of the figure of the future swan according to this scheme, you will need to carefully turn the resulting ring on the wrong side. The shape will look like a stadium. Continuing to make origami "Swan", the modules are laid out already on top. Be extremely careful, the main thing in this painstaking work is not to get confused and not to be mistaken.

Having reached the seventh row, you need to start laying out the head and wings of a swan. It is from this series that one should begin reducing the number of modules by one. Creating the tail, we repeat the same procedure.



To create a swan neck, we insert the corners of one module into the pockets of another.



To give the figure a greater naturalness, it can be supplemented with eyes and a stand.

A beautiful modular origami of swans will turn out only if you learn how to correctly create triangular modules and get used to putting them in the right places.



Origami for complete happiness

Due to the large number of modules, any figure made in modular origami will seem much more realistic. Start and you will see how quickly this work captivates you! Even doctors claim that by creating such masterpieces from paper, a person gains a sense of peace, peace and complete happiness. And this is often so lacking in our fussy life.
