To Peter by car: what and how?

Car travel is a special category of trips that not everyone likes. On the other hand, the romance of the road may appeal to those who love to drive. One of the most popular routes in this sense is the direction Moscow - St. Petersburg: traveling by car does not take so much time, and there is where to stop along the way.





Depending on the specific route, a one-way trip will take approximately 10-16 hours. There are known problems with driving along the Leningradskoye Shosse, so it may be easier and faster to get to the small Moscow Ring along the Novorizhskoye Shosse. Most motorists travel along the M-10 highway throughout the route . Following in St. Petersburg by car along this highway, you can call in Tver, Valdai and Veliky Novgorod, especially if there is a little extra time.

The main thing, of course, is to choose the right time for departure and return. During the summer season, it will be difficult to leave before the weekend, so the trip can be much more painful than expected and could be.

By 2018, authorities promise to build a toll highway. With its help, a trip to St. Petersburg by car will become easier and faster - approximately for 1200 rubles it will be possible in 6 hours to arrive from one capital to another.

This is the charm of car travel - you can stop absolutely anywhere, look into the cities and corners of the country, where you are unlikely to ever go on purpose. Of course, for those who are not used to sitting behind the wheel for a long time, getting out of habit can be difficult. Of course, for those inexperienced in independent travel to people, such a first experience, especially with improper organization, can discourage long-distance car trips for a long time. But the main thing here is a convenient and reliable car, a good and clear organization, an approximate plan and route, as well as a positive attitude. Then travel to St. Petersburg by car, most likely, will be the first in a series of such trips.
