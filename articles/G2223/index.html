Lakes united in the system of "Five Lakes", Omsk Region: description, recreation, reviews

The Five Lakes system (Omsk Region) is of interest to both tourists and researchers. The former are attracted by the beauty of local nature, the abundance of fish, the cleanest ponds. Second - many secrets and legends associated with the origin of these lakes. That is why these edges are so popular.

The Five Lakes system (Omsk Region) combines such reservoirs as Linevo, Danilovo, Shchuchye, Urmannoe (Shaitan Lake), as well as Potenoe. Their arrangement is unusual - all are elongated in the same direction and are adjacent to each other. Therefore, it has long been assumed that once fragments from one meteorite fell into this place, leaving deep traces that were subsequently filled with water. There is also an opinion that water in all reservoirs is divided into “living” and “dead”, and if you swim in both, you can heal any ailments.



Danilovo Lake

Danilovo is the most popular lake among tourists. It is spread in the Kyshtanovsky district of the Omsk region. One kilometer from the lake is the village of Kurgan. The diameter of the reservoir reaches 1 km, the deepest place is 16 m, and the transparency of the water is 5 m.

The vegetation is mainly represented by reeds and cattail, which are located only near the coastline. Danilovo Lake itself is very clean. Around - a beautiful panorama of birch and aspen trees. The reservoir is rich in fish: tench, perch, pike. They attract fishermen here. The healing power of “living water” in the lake is not exaggerated: it treats skin diseases and gastrointestinal diseases. Of great value for tourists are deposits of blue clay, which is used here for medicinal purposes.

2 km from the lake are living rooms. There are also food shops, volleyball courts and other entertainments open for tourists. Catamarans and scuba gear can be rented on the beach.



Linevo lake

Linevo Lake is located west of Danilov . This is the second reservoir of the Five Lakes system. Guest reviews speak for themselves. Those who come here for the first time are simply amazed at the beauty of the local landscapes. Near the river Tara.
The lake is half the size of Danilovo. The deepest place reaches 11 m. The transparency of the water is 2.5 m.

There is a rich fauna on the lake: wild ducks, hedgehogs, squirrels, chipmunks. Many types of fish: chebak, tench, roach, carp, perch, pike. Around the reservoir is a pine forest rich in berries and mushrooms. Water lilies flaunt in the pond itself.

Since 2014, Linevo has become a nature reserve, and its nature and purity are carefully guarded. It is not surprising that among tourists this is a very popular place to stay. There are several tourist bases. Entertainment is best suited for youth recreation: paintball, volleyball, table tennis, bath services, as well as night discos. The beach organizes water activities as well as fishing. The advantage of Lake Linevo is the shallow shores with artificial sand on the beach.



Pike lake

Shchuchye, which is part of the Five Lakes system (Omsk Region), is 2 km from the Linevo reservoir. Refers to the Muromtsevo district. Of the settlements nearby, the sparsely populated village of Nadezhdinka.

The size of the lake is half that of Linevo, the depth is on average 6 m. There is a small artificial beach, and it is convenient to fish from moorings, where the depth is over 2 m. The place is very quiet, it is suitable for meditation, relaxation and quiet fishing. There are a lot of fish: roach, golden and silver crucian carp, bream, minnow, pike.

Near the lake there is a paid fishing base "Shchuchye". In the vicinity of the reservoir there are two small tourist complexes. By the number of tourists, Shchuchye Lake is inferior to the neighboring lakes Linevo and Danilovo.

Shaitan lake

Shaitan Lake is located in the Muromtsevo district of the Omsk region, east of the village of Incis and 7 km from the village of Okunevo. It is the fourth in the Five Lakes system. Rest on it is just wonderful and interesting. Among the inhabitants of Okunevo there are various beliefs and legends. For example, the water here is “dead”, but in combination with “living” it gives an unprecedented healing effect. Locals say they see flying saucers or female silhouettes above the lake. Not everyone will be allowed to the pond, and, according to the indigenous people, you can’t swim here, because even the horses do not come close to him to drink water.

There are many tourists here: some enjoy the marvelous nature, others are interested in secrets and legends, participate in specially organized excursions.

The water in Shaitan Lake, although it is called dead, is clean and suitable for swimming. True, it is difficult to approach the pond. There is the only boardwalk on which you can reach the coast. And everything else is a swampy surface. Fishing is not relevant here: there are few fish. From the south and north of the reservoir there is a dense forest rich in berries and mushrooms.



Lurking Lake

Submerged - this is the fifth body of water that is included in the Five Lakes system (Omsk Region). This place is little known and mysterious. The location is known only from the legends that determine its location on the right bank of the Tara River. It is supposedly located 250 meters from the coast, has smooth round shapes, and the lake is shown to the eyes of only a select few. However, there is no reliable information about its existence.

The Five Lakes system leaves no one indifferent. Tourists note the stunning air with which the lungs are overflowing, a unique nature: clear and healing water, dense forests, where, as in a fairy tale, you can collect full baskets of mushrooms and berries. The friendly local population is intriguing with riddles and legends, and for anglers there is a paradise.
