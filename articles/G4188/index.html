Hotel Delfinia Hotel 4 * (Corfu / Greece): photos and reviews

Tourists can spend their holidays on the Greek island of Corfu with great comfort. This resort, popular with travelers around the world, offers its guests comfortable beaches, a lot of all kinds of entertainment and, of course, a well-developed infrastructure. The calm sea and comfortable hotels are what vacationers on the island of Corfu primarily like . Delfinia Hotel 4 * , for example, offers guests well-equipped rooms at a low cost. Many domestic tour operators work with this hotel.

Recreation center location

This wonderful hotel is located near the island's capital, Corfu, 4 km from the center of the resort village of Moraitika . Kerkyra Airport is located just a few kilometers from the hotel. To get to the Delfinia Hotel 4 * (Corfu), vacationers who arrive by plane can take a taxi or a tour operator's transfer bus. Also, if necessary, it is easy to get to Corfu from the airport and simply by public transport. A trip by regular bus to the capital of the island takes no more than ten minutes.





In relation to the beach, the hotel is also very conveniently located. This hotel was built almost on the seashore. In order to get to the beach, its guests should go literally a couple of steps.

Hotel Description

The Delfinia Hotel complex was built in 1967. Of course, for the entire period of the hotel‚Äôs existence, renovations were periodically carried out here. The hotel was last renovated in 2001. The hotel accepts guests in one main 4-story building and two 2-story additional. The total area occupied by the hotel is 42 thousand m 2 . Quite presentable interiors of rooms and halls - this is what, according to many tourists, Delfinia Hotel 4 * (Corfu ) certainly differs . The photos presented on the page confirm this.

Some services at this hotel may be provided to guests for a fee . If desired, travelers can use the hotel in cash or credit cards. Accepted at MasterCard , Visa and American Express. Smoking in the apartments of this hotel is, of course, strictly prohibited. In some cases, the administration of the complex may allow guests to live in rooms with pets (up to 5 kg in weight). However, in order for the cat or dog to be settled in the apartment, the owners need to agree on this with the hotel manager in advance.





Hotel Rooms

In total, the Delfinia Hotel 4 * complex (Corfu) offers Corfu guests 185 comfortable rooms. 93 of them are in the main building, the rest - in the auxiliary. If desired, guests of the island can rent in this hotel a regular standard room or family apartments. All rooms for rent at the hotel are equipped with:

  • to air conditioners;

  • refrigerators;

  • projection TV panels.

The television in this hotel is satellite. For the convenience of guests, in each room, among other things, there is a balcony equipped with outdoor furniture. Also, residents have the opportunity to use the Wi-Fi network for free. Bathrooms at this hotel are in each room. Here, hotel guests can use a hairdryer and bathing kit.





Hotel room reviews

Most tourists consider the Delfinia Hotel 4 * (Corfu) to be rented out as not luxurious, but still quite comfortable. The furniture in most rooms is new and comfortable. Household appliances in the hotel breaks down infrequently. Air conditioning in the apartment works very well. Wi-Fi is available throughout the center. But unfortunately, the network here is quite slow. Most residents still prefer to use Wi-Fi in cafes operating in the city. There is also a free Wi-Fi zone on the beach next to the complex.

Soundproofing in the rooms, judging by the reviews of tourists, is not bad. At least, guests do not hear neighbors talking from adjacent rooms . On TV in the room, if you wish, you can watch one Russian-language channel.

The bathrooms in the hotel are quite comfortable. The only thing, when bathing in the shower, water can pour on the floor. However, the maids formed in the bathroom are cleaned daily. Actually the hotel rooms themselves are quite clean. Almost no complaints from the residents and regarding bedding in this hotel.

The hotel also has a small business center. Here, guests can, for example, make photocopies of the necessary documents if necessary. Also, residents of the hotel have the opportunity to play darts, chess or billiards. Organized in the complex and animation. Guests can use the sun loungers by the pool for free.





Facilities for parents

Of course, it provides a lot of services not only for adults, but also for young guests of the Delfinia Hotel 4 * (Corfu). Tours from the south are often bought by vacationers who decide to relax in Greece with the whole family. For children, the hotel provides a games room, chairs in the restaurant. Cots for little guests are free of charge. If necessary, tenants can entrust their child with a qualified nanny for a while. True, this service is provided in the complex only for a fee. Also, if desired, children can visit the club at the hotel.

Additional services

For a fee, travelers can use the laundry facilities at this hotel. There is also a car rental service on site. Residents have the opportunity to withdraw money in one of the ATMs operating on the territory of the center.

Hotel infrastructure: reviews

The service provided at the Delfinia Corfu Hotel 4 * ( Corfu Island , Moraitika) praises the majority of its guests. The territory of the hotel, many travelers find it convenient to relax and comfortable. The guests really like, for example, the fact that in this complex, among other things, there is also a lawn with trees, on which you can sunbathe even without an umbrella. For Greek hotels, by the way, the presence of such a convenience is absolutely not typical.





The pool of the hotel, many residents also find it quite comfortable. It has a significant depth. Therefore, it can not only freshen up and splash, but actually swim. The majority of tourists consider the only disadvantage of the hotel pool is that the hotel staff spills too much bleach into the water.

Unfortunately, the majority of guests in this complex do not use the gym. The fact is that it is almost always closed, and the key to it must be sought. Many residents play ping pong at the hotel. You can use tennis tables here for free. For a visit to the billiard room, the hotel takes only 2 euros.

Delfinia Hotel 4 * (Corfu): hotel food reviews

You can rent rooms at this hotel with breakfast or half board. Actually, there are, unfortunately, not too good reviews about the hotel‚Äôs restaurant itself. According to many tourists, in a four-star hotel, such an institution could be equipped better. According to the guests, the hotel restaurant somehow resembles a simple buffet. The tables here are too close to each other, but there is no dress code. Also a lack of a hotel restaurant is the lack of an outdoor terrace.

The dishes served in the complex are considered by most guests to be quite tasty. The clients are served in the hotel restaurant according to the "buffet" system. If desired, vacationers can take here dishes of chicken, pork, beef, potatoes. Also, pasta, eggs, salads are served to residents. There are at the distribution in the dining room and fresh fruits with vegetables.





Beach Hotels

The sea, as already mentioned, is located a couple of steps from the hotel buildings. Many tourists consider the beach of the hotel, unfortunately, not too convenient. The fact is that the coastline in this place is quite narrow. Therefore, crowds happen on the beach of the complex. The coast in this place is covered with very small pebbles. The most convenient entry into the sea is next to the pier.

Sunbeds at the hotel's beach are provided free of charge. However, unfortunately, they are not enough for all tourists. If desired, hotel residents can visit the beach and bar. Here in the evenings, including live music. So after sunset, the hotel bar can be quite comfortable. In the evening, this institution is visited by many residents of the complex. However, most vacationers believe that in the daytime it is not too convenient. In the morning and at lunch, hotel guests usually go to the beach to another non-owned bar nearby. Dishes here can be ordered cheaper. In addition, the furniture in this bar is more comfortable.

Unfortunately, beach towels at the Delfinia Hotel 4 * hotel (Greece, Corfu) are not issued to residents. Experienced tourists advise beginners to take their own from home. Sunbathing on the hotel's beach is quite convenient not only on the sunbeds, but also on the towels.

Available Attractions

Located hotel Delfinia Hotel 4 * (Corfu) is very convenient not only in relation to the infrastructure of Corfu. In the immediate vicinity of the city there are many various attractions popular with tourists. For example, residents of the complex can visit the Achillion Palace or the famous Greek baths.





Of course, near the hotel there are also many shops of various specializations, bars, restaurants and cafes. Prices in the city of Corfu, according to many tourists, are very reasonable. At the same time, almost all sellers in this resort are able to speak Russian.

Of course, you can buy in the city of Corfu and a tour of some attractions on the island or on the mainland. Many guests of the Delfinia Hotel 4 * do just that. If tourists leave for an excursion early in the morning, they can turn a dry breakfast into the dining room if necessary.
