The first napkin of a beginner needlewoman

For a small, but very cute and elegant napkin, you need to knit 5-7 strips of 10-15 cm long, their number can vary depending on the thickness of the threads and the density of knitting. Starting from the second strip, you should make connections for the loop. This operation is known to everyone who knows how to knit columns and loops. The stripes of this napkin lie parallel to each other, without bends. The width of the strips is 4-5 double crochets, plus lifting loops. Decorative arches of the rise consist of 7 air loops. The connection is as follows. You need to knit three air loops, then make a connecting column into the arch, make three air loops and then knit a regular row of double crochets. Pat the finished cloth through a wet cloth.

Tips for beginner needlewomen

If you decide to do knitting, then first determine the place where you will needlework. It should be well lit. Light is one of the main conditions for enjoying work and maintaining good vision.

When choosing yarn, give preference to mercerized. The finer the thread and the smaller the number of the hook, the more beautiful the lace. Knit with loose loops, do not tighten them. After washing, the item will sit down and become more dense. If you knit too tight, the product will turn out to be rough and thick to the touch.
