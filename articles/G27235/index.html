Geographical position

Kherson region is located in the south of Ukraine, within the Black Sea lowland. Its western part borders with the Mykolayiv region, the eastern part - with the Zaporizhzhya region, and the northern part - with the Dnipropetrovsk region. The southern part overlooks the Crimea and, as already mentioned, is washed immediately by two seas. Along the sea coast stretches of sand spits, bays and islets. This region occupies a large territory, about 30,000 km 2 . The landscape here is quite monotonous, far from tropical delights. Plain terrain, salt marshes, mostly steppe. But the abundance of water sources creates lush green oases, so pleasing to the eye.





Administrative division

Kherson region includes 18 districts, each of which has a number of settlements in its composition. The capital and administrative center is the city of Kherson. The population of the region is about 1 million people. The governor of the Kherson region, a former deputy, Yury Vitalyevich Odarchenko came to power on March 2, 2014. He is a prominent politician, since 2011 he has been a member of the presidium and political council. The former governor of the Kherson region Nikolay Kostyak was removed from his post during the personnel reshuffle carried out by the acting head of state Alexander Turchinov.

There are 11 cities in the region (4 of them are of regional subordination - Kherson, Kakhovka, New Kakhovka and Golaya Pristan) and 654 rural settlements. The districts of the Kherson region: Belozersky, Berislavsky, Veliko-Aleksandrovsky, Velikolepetikhsky, Verkhnerogachinsky, Vysokopolsky, Genichesky, Golopristansky, Gornostaevsky, Ivanovsky, Kalanchaksky, Kakhovsky, Nizhneserogozsky, Novovorontsovsky, sootmenynsky and Tsynyupyn Tsynyurupy - Tsynyurup - Tsuryupyn - Tsuryupyn centers.

The economy of the region

Kherson region is a rich region with a diversified industry. It produces a third of all electric machines produced in the country, a fourth of cotton fabrics. But the main direction is, of course, agriculture. This is crop production - cereals, sunflower seeds, vegetables and fruits are grown here. The livestock sector accounts for almost the same part; huge farms for raising cattle, pigs and sheep work.

In general, the food industry, machine building and metalworking, and the electric power industry are developed in the region.

Minerals

The region does not have much significance in their production, however, cement and brick clay, which are very in demand in the construction industry, is mined here. In addition, development of quartz sand, marl, limestone, salt, peat and gas deposits is underway.

Climatic features of the region

All southern Ukraine is characterized by rather hot and arid weather. In this regard, the Kherson region is most favorably located. A map of the area will show in detail the abundance of water bodies. Near each of them there is a large number of small hotels and pensions where you can relax.





The climate is temperate continental; The hot heat of the steppe, meeting with the moist streams of sea air, forms quite comfortable conditions for living and recreation. The summer is warm, the average July temperature is +22 ¬∞ C, the absolute record is + 40 ¬∞ C. Winter is mild, usually with little snow, rarely the thermometer drops below -3 degrees. Autumn and spring are warm and dry. Precipitation is quite small.

For lovers of car travel, Kherson region is well suited. A map of the region will help you find the way to all the sights, and the flat terrain will allow you to get everywhere by car. There is really something to see here.

Cultural heritage

It is rare where such an abundance of objects of nature reserve and cultural-historical funds are found. A total of 69 territories, two biosphere reserves, 5 reserves, a dendrological park, 32 nature monuments, 12 parks, which are monuments of landscape gardening art, 10 protected areas. You should plan your vacation in advance to visit the maximum number of interesting places.

In addition, tourists often visit the monuments of ancient architecture. The Greco-Sophia Church, the Grigoryevsky Monastery and the Catherine Cathedral belong to the buildings of the mid-18th century, the Svyatodukhovsky Cathedral belongs to the 19th century. An experienced guide will take you along secret paths of the distant past, you will visit the ancient burial mounds, the grave of the ataman, see the remains of the last Cossack section, the ruins of the Turkish fortress Tyagin (XV century).

Askania-Nova Biosphere Reserve

Rest in the Kherson region is not only a warm sea and golden sand. There is also a magnificent, bewitching, green oasis in the boundless steppe. It was created back in the last century by the landowner Friedrich Eduardovich Falz-Fein. Thanks to his idea to remove huge land from land use, unique vegetation and rare species of animals have remained untouched .

Later, this steppe reserve was expanded, its territory covers more than 100 hectares. A real park zone has been created, high viewing hills have been poured, a large number of ponds have been built, and beautiful glades have been broken. The whole territory is divided into a zoo and an arboretum. One half is a real green corner, gathering more than 1000 species of trees, not counting 1,500 flower and decorative plants. Here you get the full impression that you are in a fabulous green kingdom.

Azov-Sivash National Park

It has less variety of landscape; it is a steppe zone where a huge number of steppe grasses grow. Including unique and listed in the Red Book. Not all tourists can appreciate the beauty of this area, but what is fascinating is the number and variety of birds. For some, the park is a habitat or nesting place, while others are here only during the flight. But in general, during the year, the presence of about 1 million birds is recorded here. If observing a wonderful vacation for you - Kherson region is waiting for a visit.

Of the relatively common species, ducks, gulls, terns, waders, swans live here. There are rare species listed in the Red Book: sea cinquefoil, stilt, white-tailed eagle, black-headed laughter. With proper patience, one can meet bustard, bustard, cranes, golden eagles, falcons in these places.

In addition to reverent care and observation of birds, the park created a reserve-hunting farm. On its territory live such animals as red deer, doe, mouflon, kulan.

Sanatorium "Gopri"

Kherson region has long been famous for this mud bath. It began to operate in 1889, since then it has changed, but the air saturated with the aroma of the steppe and coniferous forests, sulphide-silt mud and sodium chloride brine supplied by the lake remained the same.





Holidays on the sea

At all times, such a trip was considered the crown of vacation. Warm sun and gentle sea, unique climate and amazing beauty of nature attract people here today. When comparing with the more famous resorts, such as Crimea, Sochi, Yalta, Antalya, it is clear that prices here are much lower. Such a vacation is well suited for people with modest means.

The tourism industry is well developed. Along the coast lodges are built, ready to receive guests. Locals rent visitors rooms or entire homes for the holidays. It costs a lot less than paying for a hotel room, especially if you cook for yourself.

The resort village of Iron Port

This place is extremely popular with tourists. Moreover, not only residents of Ukraine have a rest here, in the summer you can meet people from all over the CIS and even abroad. On the sites of travel companies it is noticeable that the Iron Port is always very popular. Kherson region is rich in various resorts, but it is here that the unusual hospitality of local residents, the abundance of fruits and seafood, as well as the cleanest and most gentle sea are noted.

There are a lot of options for living: youth or family hotels, boarding houses, recreation centers. You can always find suitable housing in the private sector. Rest here is much cheaper than in the Crimea, while you get the same service.

Fans of a relaxing holiday can enjoy the clean beaches. Comfortable sunbeds and umbrellas, many bars with soft drinks. Not only the golden sand and warm sea is famous for the Iron Port. Kherson region offers a number of interesting excursions that will diversify your vacation and give you a lot of pleasant experiences.

Have you ever had a hot spring bath? If not, then it's time to try. Just 15 km from the resort area there is a geyser, 3 baths are equipped here - two with hot water and one with healing mud.
