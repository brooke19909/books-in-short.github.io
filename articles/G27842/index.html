Programmable Timer Outlet

Today, most buyers prefer programmable sockets, a timer in them initiates turning the device on and off at a specific scheduled time. This innovation can significantly reduce the cost of electricity and makes the operation of electronic devices comfortable and safe.





What are timer outlets?

Timer sockets are automatic devices that have a plug and unplug timer. The timer can be either electric or mechanical. Using this mechanism of the outlet, it is possible to automate various types of equipment and power supplies of devices. A distinctive feature of programmable sockets is that they significantly reduce the cost of powering various electrical devices.

Why do we need programmable sockets?

This type of outlet is very useful for security. With the help of such devices it is possible to stabilize the work, for example, of an alarm system, lighting or all electrical devices in a residential building. The use of sockets with a mechanical timer allows you to turn on and off the installed devices for twenty or thirty minutes.





Another option for completing such a socket - with an electric timer - provides device regulation without additional settings for a week. In such outlets, there is the opportunity to adjust and select the interval of the sensors up to a minute. An undoubted advantage of the programmable outlet is the built-in power supply, which allows the sockets to function for some time even during a power outage in the house.

What kind of timer outlets are there?

Let us now consider in more detail what programmed sockets exist. There are two main types - mechanical and electronic. More about each:

  1. Mechanical - the most ordinary sockets with a timer, the response time in which is set using the drum. Such types of outlets can function automatically during the day, because they are also called daily allowance. Of course, many indicators that are in the electric version are not available to programmable sockets with a mechanical timer. Therefore, here the on and off intervals are clearly cyclical, for example, you can set the interval to 15 minutes. There can be an unlimited number of such fifteen-minute cycles, it all depends on the functionality of the outlet. It will not work to independently assign parameters in such models.
  2. Electrical - this type of equipment provides much more options for self-tuning. Most common models allow you to program the device for weeks to come. In these outlets it is possible to set the on-off timer at any time, for example, half an hour in the afternoon, an hour at lunchtime and a couple of hours in the evening, and so on, with completely different combinations every day and week. You can purchase programmable sockets with a timer in Leroy Merlin, which can monitor the work of a group of electrical appliances at once and control each of them individually. Such devices can activate the lighting at the appointed time, turn on the alarm or turn off the router at night and so on.

Where can I use them?

Programmable sockets designed to provide comfort and safety of use. Such models will allow full control over electrical appliances: home appliances, showers, computer equipment and so on. Assign on-off intervals, automating the work and thereby saving electricity in the house.





Such smart devices will be very useful for owners of agricultural farms or home terrariums, where the use of electronic and mechanical devices is extremely necessary. By adjusting the irrigation and lighting of greenhouses with the help of devices, you can significantly save time and get a good harvest.

Advantages

The main positive aspects of programmable sockets with a timer include:

  • Significant energy savings.
  • The ability to flexibly configure the on and off schedule.
  • Simplifies the work with electrical appliances and really saves time for manual tuning.

Disadvantages of programmable models

The main negative aspects include:

  • Limited programming time for mechanical outlets.
  • Sometimes you can find models with the wrong timer.
  • Reviews note the fragility of such devices.
  • Extra noise (ticking timer).

What does socket programming include?

It is worthwhile to dwell on the methodology for programming a socket with a timer in more detail:

  1. First you need to set the exact time. To do this, click on the Clock with a sharp object, then set the current day of the week (Week key), hours (Hour) and minutes (Min).
  2. We indicate the turn-on time - you will need the Timer button. After pressing this button, the display will show On 1. Next, the timer off time is already set. The time setting is also carried out with the keys Week, Hour and Min.
  3. To see if the tasks were successfully fixed, you need to hold the Timer button for a couple of seconds.
  4. After programming, you must again press the Clock key, thereby putting the device into operation.

It is worth noting that a programmable outlet (instructions for any model is supplied) can carry out scheduled tasks only in Auto mode. If the Manual on function is activated on the device, the device will act as a simple socket, and the Manual off function will completely turn off the device.





Special mode Random

Random is another interesting feature of the new generation of outlets. With this option, you can automatically connect the device to absolutely any electrical object in the house. All that is needed is to set the intermediate interval of the two devices and activate the function by clicking on the corresponding Random button.

Such a function will be especially useful when the owners are temporarily absent in the house. A power outlet can portray the illusion of the presence of people in the house and to some extent can prevent an attempted robbery.

Built-in modules

You can control such a device from a smartphone or other gadget. Many programmed outlets now have a built-in GSM module so that they can receive control commands from a distance.





This function will allow you to make changes in the operating schedule of the device if the owner returns home a little earlier or, conversely, is delayed. All you need to do is go into the application and select the appropriate options.
