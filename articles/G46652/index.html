What is a trimmer and what are the types of trimmers

The trimmer comes from the English word and means literally "tidy up" or "cut." The very first such tool appeared in 1972. It was invented by one businessman who planted a lawn near his house and figured out how to cut grass and not damage trees. Soon, the trimmer was used in production.

This is an indispensable garden tool for mowing the lawns and neatly aligning the edges.

You can trim places where it‚Äôs difficult to get with a trimmer. Due to their light weight, they can work for a long time without feeling tired in their hands. It is convenient to store, does not take up much space. You can buy it in special garden stores, where they will tell you in detail what a trimmer is, and help you make the right choice.

The device has a long handle, on one end of which there is an engine, and on the other - a cutting mechanism. There are two types of trimmers: gasoline and electric. To find out which trimmer is better, you need to familiarize yourself with the characteristics of each type.

What is an electric trimmer, easy to guess. It can operate from the network, produces little noise, does not require fuel, is considered an environmentally friendly device, since it does not produce exhaust gases. But it is strictly forbidden to work with him in the rain or after rain so that there is no electric shock. In this case, the electric trimmer has a small power. He should be given more rest to avoid overheating, and the engine must be constantly energized or recharged.



What is a gas trimmer, you can find out by carefully studying the instructions. This device works autonomously, only you need to refuel it. Gasoline trimmer has many nozzles, which expands its capabilities. At the same time, it is very noisy and has harmful exhausts. When choosing a gas trimmer, you should consider that for it you need a supply of fuel and good engine care. Which trimmer to choose is up to you.

Before starting the mechanism, you must read the instructions. If you follow the rules of operation, the tool will last a long time. After use, the trimmer must be cleaned and stored in a dry place where there is no dirt and dust.

Trimmers differ from each other by cutting heads. There may be a knife or fishing line at the end. There are also trimmers with alternate use of a knife and fishing line. The handle is T-shaped and D-shaped. The T-shaped is used for mowing in large spaces, and the D-shaped is very convenient for working in hard-to-reach places, along paths, near bushes.



Trimmers of different types and sizes have found application in other areas of human activity. We considered this device as a lawn mower above. There is also a mustache, beard and bikini trimmer . It can be used as a regulator of chandeliers and shades. It also happens in the form of an electric capacitor, and it is also widely used in shipbuilding to raise the engine.

Now, knowing what a trimmer is, you can make a choice of one or another tool. First you need to decide what you need it for, the amount of work you plan to carry out.
