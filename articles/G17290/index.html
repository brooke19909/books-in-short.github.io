This example shows well how the BO works. If the trader guesses with the direction, he receives money, if not, he loses.

Kinds

A trader can choose one of four types of contracts:

1. High / Low: if at the time of expiration the price is higher (Call) or lower (Put) of the current level, the trader will receive income. These are the simplest types of transactions for which a minimum reward of 70% is provided.

2. One Touch: the trader will make a profit if the price touches one of the proposed levels when it increases (Call) or decreases (Put).

3. Range High / Low: the trader selects a range above (Call) or below (Put) the current price. If at the time of expiration the contract value is within the specified limits, a fee will be paid.
