How to make a heart out of a paper clip: step-by-step instruction

Sometimes you urgently need to decorate a written note with something. Schoolchildren know many ways to decorate postcards with improvised means. But adults who do not really like hand-made, do not always imagine how to quickly and beautifully transform, for example, a letter to a friend. It is about such an elementary decor that will be discussed in this article. How to make a heart out of a paper clip without using other improvised materials? The answer to this question will be given below.





Step-by-step instruction

How to make a heart out of an ordinary paper clip? It's simple: we need a nice wire product. It is better to use metal clips in a plastic shell for this purpose. They are bright and catchy, which means they will look spectacular on white paper. It is advisable to choose a larger paper clip than a regular paper clip. But this is a matter of taste and opportunity.

We proceed to the manufacture itself. We will consider step by step how to make a heart out of a paper clip.

We take a paper clip and put it in front of us. With the flat side it should lie on the table, and the convex, multilevel edge up. If the person making the heart is right-handed, then it will be more convenient for the two edges to point to the right, and the ‚Äúhead‚Äù of the paper clip should be directed to the left. Now you need the edge that lies closer to the body, visually divided in half. The next stage - in the middle place you need to make a break from yourself. Be sure to ensure that the upper level does not leak down. Otherwise, there is a chance that the clip will twist, and the heart will not work. Well, the final touch remained. We bring the bend to the middle and slightly pull the upper part to the ceiling. This is necessary to make the wire more ductile, and in the future there were no problems with putting a heart on the paper.





How can wire decor be used?

From a bent paper clip in the shape of a heart, you can make a bookmark for books, decorative elements on a postcard, create a curtain or use it as a hanging decor.

How to make a bookmark from a paper clip-heart? Everything is very simple: according to the scheme given in the first paragraph, it is necessary to assemble a wire structure. At this stage, the primitive bookmark will be ready, but if you want to make creative, then you should not restrain your imagination. On a paper clip in a plastic shell, spangles, rhinestones, beads and even beads hold well. All this variety must be attached to glue or fixed using a hot gun.



How to make a heart out of a paper clip, we have already figured out, now we need to understand where it can be used. Wire structures look interesting when they weigh on a rope. In this way, a whole curtain can be assembled, or it can be used in a fragmented form, for example, attached to a balloon ribbon.

Making earrings

Such an adornment will not be an adult lady by status, but a five-year-old girl will be very helpful. First you need to do all the preparatory work from the point on how to make a heart out of a paper clip. And then the creative process begins. If at hand there are absolutely no materials other than office supplies, it does not matter. We fix an elastic band for money on one side of the heart, and without tightening it, we wind it on the other side. After three such manipulations, the gum should end. But this is a standardized parameter. Everything will depend on the size of the heart and the length of the gum. For a good result, you need to wind two to three elastic bands on one heart. If a spool of thread is at hand, then you can use it. It will turn out especially beautifully if the mesh is shiny. The final stage - we put on the earrings. All earrings are ready.



Fold letters or patterns

From paper clips you can add not only cute figures, but also letters of the alphabet, numbers and punctuation marks. With this whole wire alphabet, it's easy to build a message. Having understood how to make a heart out of a paper clip, it is easy to collect the necessary symbols by analogy.

Such an adornment will perfectly complement a postcard, a box and even a photo album. For construction, you can use both colored office paper clips and ordinary metal clips. To make the decorative inscription look festive, you can paint it from a spray can, or you can add sparkles or beads. In this way, it is good to write initials or short messages. Long labels will not look spectacular, as the effect of congestion will be created.
