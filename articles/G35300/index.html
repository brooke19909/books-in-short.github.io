Where is the hotel located

Sensimar Palm Beach Palace is located on the northern tip of Djerba - the southernmost island of Tunisia. The hotel is twenty kilometers from Zarzis International Airport. The nearest town to the hotel is Midoun. To its center, as well as the renowned Houmt Souk Market, is about thirteen kilometers. The hotel is located along with other hotels in the Touristik zone, away from mosques and Muslim life of the local population, but among palm trees, sandy beaches and entertainment venues. Sensimar positions itself as a vacation spot for adults. They accept only those guests who are over eighteen years old. Reviews say the hotel is close to evening entertainment centers. And if you want to plunge into the life of Tunisia, then you can get to Houmt Souk in ten minutes by taxi or public transport. By the way, the bus stop is just in front of the hotel.





Territory

Hotel Sensimar Palm Beach Palace 5 * photos (and reviews) are often portrayed as a garden of paradise, a huge and luxurious oasis. It is breathtaking from its main building and twenty-four bungalows, which are made in the charming eclecticism of the Andalusian and Saracenic styles. Walking through this lush garden among the neat paths is a pleasure. The hull and villas are designed so that most of the rooms have either direct or side views of the golden sandy beach and the Mediterranean Sea. The lush green of the oasis is set off by turquoise water. There are many of her, assure reviews, because the two outdoor pools are made in the form of a natural lagoon, which is located between the hotel buildings and its own beach. Freshwater tanks are equipped with hydromassage jets of different heights and impact forces. And some categories of rooms have their own small pool.

Sensimar Palm Beach Palace Hotel: room description

This is a great hotel. It has two hundred and sixty-one numbers. But, as tourists say, the hotel is very quiet and peaceful. All guests are adults, babbling kids does not run. The main contingent is residents of Western Europe. The bungalow and the building are small - two to three floors. The lion's share of the rooms is made up of standards - single and double. There are seven more suites. These rooms have a living room with a dining area and a private pool.

What is the content of the standards? Reviews say that it is fully consistent with the five-star status of the hotel. The bedrooms are spacious, bright, clean, beautifully decorated in Mujahar style. There is everything you need: slippers, bathrobes, a hairdryer, air conditioning, a plasma TV with cable channels (there are several Russian speakers), hygiene items. During daily cleaning, the minibar is replenished with water (both mineral and Fanta). There is also an electronic safe, but for a fee. Needless to say, all rooms and pools have fast Wi-Fi, access to which is free for guests? The rooms in the basement have small terraces and the upper tiers have furnished balconies. Sheets are changed three times a week.

Beach and Pools

Sensimar Palm Beach Palace is a front-line hotel. To the magnificent sandy beach you need to go just a couple of steps. It is clean, well equipped. There is a shower (for the body and legs). Many comfortable sun loungers under reed umbrellas. Sunset at sea is normal. After a storm, the beach is quickly cleared of algae. The hotel has two large and clean outdoor pools - swimming and relaxation. The latter has underwater sunbeds and hydromassage devices. There is a third pool - heated and indoors. It belongs to the spa area. Generally speaking, Tunisia is famous for thalassotherapy, and the Sensimar Palm Beach Palace Hotel did not stand aside from this national treatment by the sea. In the local spa, you can not only take a steam bath in the hammam, calcinate the bones in the sauna, soak in the jacuzzi or under the fingers of the masseur, but also experience all kinds of clay, algae, etc. wraps.

Service and Facilities

When you arrive at Sensimar Palm Beach Palace, you are greeted with a welcome drink and refreshment with a cool towel. A Russian-speaking girl works at the reception, so there will be no language barrier for those who do not know English. Animation in the hotel is present, but it is not intrusive. If you want to indulge in bliss and passive seals, no one will drag you to do water aerobics. But animators can diversify your leisure. True, the communication language of the team of funny guys is English. For sports there is a gym. The hotel specializes in organizing seminars. For this purpose, there are several fully equipped conference rooms and rooms for business meetings of various capacities. Newlyweds are not deprived of attention. Compliments are provided for them from the hotel. The hotel has a hairdresser, parking, gift shop and currency exchange.
