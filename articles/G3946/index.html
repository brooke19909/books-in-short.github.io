Hotel Baia Lara Hotel 5 * (Turkey, Antalya): description and reviews

Turkey is a country in which everyone should at least once in his life spend his vacation. And there are many reasons for this.

Why is it worth going to Turkey?

This is the only place where a beach holiday is offered on four seas - the Mediterranean, Aegean, Marmara and Black. It has an excellent climate: from May to October there is no strong heat and humidity.

The country has a well-developed tourist infrastructure with hotels of a diverse range. Excellent conditions for scuba diving and sailing. Turkey is ideal for shoppers. In all resort areas you will find many shops, boutiques and shopping centers with a large selection of products.
For citizens of Russia, a visa for a period of 60 days is not required. The flight to Turkey is short.

Tourists visit the resorts of Bodrum, Kemer, Marmaris, Side, Fethiye. But Russians are most attracted to the town on the shores of the Mediterranean Sea - Antalya. This is the most popular resort place in Turkey.

Antalya hotels have everything to ensure a romantic and family holiday. They have comfortable rooms, well-groomed beaches and parks, extensive entertainment complexes and Russian animation.

What attracts a person to a vacation at sea?

Physiologists have long established that the rhythmic sounds of the surf are very similar to the heartbeat of a person who is in a state of relaxation. That is why they soothe him. Sea water, saturated with mineral salts, protects the body from infections, improves the structure of the skin and enriches body tissues with oxygen. Guests at Baia Lara Hotel can fully verify the undeniable benefits of a seaside vacation; they have a great sandy clean beach.
