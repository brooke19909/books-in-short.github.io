Czech Republic, Prague, Chertovka Hotel: overview, description and reviews

Prague is a unique city with magnificent architecture. Tourists from all over the world come here. The historical center is the most popular place in the Czech capital. Here are all the main attractions of the country. Therefore, travelers who come here quite often choose the Chertovka Hotel (Prague, Czech Republic), located within walking distance from the city center.

Basic information and location of the hotel

The main advantage of the hotel is its favorable location. It was built in 2000 in the area 01 (Prague). The Chertovka Hotel is located just 200 meters from Charles Bridge, which is considered the city‚Äôs most famous landmark. You can walk to it in just 5 minutes. Waldstein Gardens and St. Nicholas Church are 300 meters away. Old Town Square, Town Hall and Prague Castle can be reached in 15 minutes. Around the hotel life is constantly in full swing: there are cafes, shops, restaurants, souvenir shops and wine bars.





How to get to the Chertovka Hotel in Prague? Most often, tourists arrive in the Czech Republic by plane. The nearest airport is 11 km from the city center. You can get to it by taxi. The hotel also organizes paid transfers for its guests. A metro station is located next to the complex, and a tram stop is a 5-minute walk away. Arriving here, it is worth remembering that check-in to the apartment does not start before 14:00, and the hotel will have to leave no later than noon. There are no Russian-speaking staff at the hotel. Pets are allowed at an additional cost.

Number of rooms and its equipment

Elegant and comfortable rooms were prepared for their guests by the Certovka Hotel (Prague, Czech Republic). It offers small apartments designed for single and double occupancy. The hotel windows partially overlook the Vlatva River and Charles Bridge, but a small part of them rests against the wall of the neighboring building. The hotel is located in an old building, built in the Baroque style. It accommodates a total of 21 rooms. Each room has a separate bathroom, which is equipped with a bath with shower, toilet and washbasin. Toiletries, towels and a hairdryer are provided.





The bedroom has a single or double bed. Carpeted floors. Clothes can be put in a closet or cupboards. This room features a flat-screen TV with satellite channels. In summer, the rooms are air-conditioned and in winter, the hotel includes heating. Guests can leave valuables in a small safe. The room has a refrigerator for storing food, and a minibar for drinks. True, its filling requires additional payment. The hotel uses a telephone to contact the property. Employees clean their rooms every day and change linen and towels several times a week.

Dining at the hotel

Tourists spend most of their time outside the hotel, preferring to dine in restaurants and cafes in Prague. Hotel "Chertovka" for the convenience of its guests offers them only breakfast. A rich breakfast is served every morning in the hotel restaurant, which includes European and local dishes. A buffet is served for guests. The menu consists of several types of omelettes (with mushrooms, bacon or smoked sausage), as well as pastries, sausages with mustard, sliced ‚Äã‚Äãcheese and sausage, fresh tomatoes and cucumbers. Several types of juices, mineral water, tea and coffee are served from the drinks.





Near the hotel there are a large number of restaurants, bars and cafes serving Czech cuisine. It is in them that tourists who live in Chertovka prefer to have lunch and dinner. Food can also be purchased at the local supermarket.

Infrastructure and Services

Developed tourist infrastructure boasts district 01 (Prague). Hotel "Chertovka" also offers its guests a large number of services that help to make the rest in this place the highest quality. So, for guests, the reception is open around the clock, which has the hotel administrator at all times. At the reception, guests can use a luggage storage room, a currency exchange and a ticket desk. The manager can also send your items to dry cleaning, where they are washed and ironed. This service is provided for a fee. All guests receive a daily newspaper for free.





There is no private parking on site, but there is a public car park 750 meters from the building. You can also rent a car for independent travel at the hotel. For businessmen, the hotel building houses a conference room equipped with modern computer equipment. At any time, guests can connect free Wi-Fi to their devices. The hotel is equipped with facilities for disabled travelers and an elevator. Smoking in the building is strictly prohibited.

Entertainment

Hotel Certovka in Prague does not offer its guests a wide choice of entertainment, since most tourists prefer to spend time walking around the city. In addition to the above facilities, hotel guests can book an excursion right at the reception. An experienced guide will tell you what places to visit when coming to the Czech Republic. For independent travel through the city streets, tourists can rent bicycles. They are provided for a fee, which will depend on the lease term.





Accommodation for tourists with children

A large number of amenities for kids offers tourists Prague. Hotel "Chertovka" allows children of any age. An extra bed or crib can be delivered to the room upon request. It is worth clarifying about their availability during the design of the tour. Children under 12 years who are accommodated on them can relax at the hotel for free. For guests over this age, the hotel will charge EUR 33 per person for extra beds, which are combined with the total cost of the stay.

The hotel does not provide any additional amenities for its younger guests. Coming here, tourists should take care of organizing leisure activities for their children.

Guest positive opinions

Tourists choosing the Chertovka Hotel (Prague) most often write positive reviews. They are satisfied with the rest in this place, noting that the price for accommodation is fully consistent with the quality of the service offered. In their reviews, they note the following advantages of the hotel:

  • Maids will always change towels and bed linen ahead of time if they become dirty. It‚Äôs enough just to politely ask them about it.
  • The rooms have very comfortable beds with new soft mattresses and fresh linens.

  • Great location ideal for travelers interested in the architecture and history of Prague.

  • If you open the window during the day, you can enjoy live music that plays from the streets of the city.
  • The windows are made of wood, easy to open and close.




Negative guest opinions

Travelers appreciate their stay at Chertovka. But some of them still found minor service flaws that would be nice to fix. So, the comments of the guests describe the following disadvantages of the hotel:

  • Very narrow shower in the room, which is not suitable for stout people.
  • In the evenings, a loud noise comes from the street. Sleeping with open windows is impossible.
  • Breakfasts are the same every day, so they get bored very quickly.
  • The rooms are very small and cramped.
  • Not a single Russian channel is broadcast on TV, so it is not interesting to watch it.
  • The ventilation in the bathroom is very buzzing. They tidy up the bathroom much worse than the rest of the room.




conclusions

Travelers who booked tours to the Chertovka Hotel (Prague) were satisfied with the quality of the service offered. They believe that the hotel corresponds to the declared four stars. The price for accommodation here, of course, is more expensive than in complexes located far from the center. The hotel can be recommended to friendly companies, couples and newlyweds, the elderly and businessmen.
