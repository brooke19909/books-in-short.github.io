Hotel Kaurma in Dzhemete: description and reviews of tourists

The village of Dzhemete is located about 5 km from the famous Black Sea resort of Anapa. Tourists from all over Russia come here in summer during the season. It is believed that Dzhemete just perfect for families with children, as well as young vacationers or senior citizens. A feature of the hotels in this village is that most of them are located right on the seashore. The Kaurma Hotel is no exception in this regard . In Dzhemete, guests of this hotel can walk to the beach in just a few minutes.

General description of the complex

The sea is only about 20 meters from the Kaurma Hotel. In order to get ashore, residents of this complex only need to cross the dunes. The hotel building itself is a five-story building of original architecture. The hotel's facades are painted in light "southern" pastel colors, pleasant to the eyes.





The courtyard of this hotel has its own. It is decorated, according to vacationers, very beautiful. All paths in the complex, as well as the platform in front of its building, are tiled. Exotic tropical trees were planted everywhere in the courtyard of the hotel , flowerbeds and neat lawns were broken. In general, the hotel grounds look quite attractive.

Features of settlement and accommodation

Check-out time at this hotel is 12 hours. But if guests have free rooms, they can check in here, of course, earlier. From the documents at the entrance to the hotel, guests need to show only a passport. One of the features of this hotel is that tourists can stay here including pets. Hotels, both domestic and foreign, provide this service to vacationers quite rarely. The only thing - vacationers who want to check into a hotel with some kind of pet should warn their owners separately.

Tourists can book places in the Kaurma complex only from May to October. In winter, this complex does not work.





What type of accommodation is offered to tourists?

Equipped in the hotel "Kaurma" (Dzhemete) 2 0 comfortable rooms. If necessary, guests of the resort can rent rooms of any price category here. A particular pride of the hotel are superior apartments. All rooms rented at the hotel include:

  • air conditioners;

  • TV sets

  • refrigerators.

An iron and hairdryer are also provided. Rooms at the hotel also have balconies and showers.

Reviews about renting a hotel accommodation

The opinion about the rooms offered by the Kaurma (Dzhemete) hotel was very good for tourists. Especially good reviews of the guests deserved the rooms with kitchens rented at this hotel. And all the other rooms in the hotel, according to tourists, are very comfortable and clean. Maids are often cleaned here. The furniture in the rooms at the same time is comfortable, and the household appliances are working.





Very good reviews on the web are available regarding the design of the rooms of this hotel. The decoration of the hotel rooms is very pleasant.

Complex infrastructure

In terms of room comfort, the Kaurma Hotel is considered by most tourists to be one of the best in Dzhemete. The same goes for infrastructure at this hotel. The hotel offers tourists the following amenities:

  • spacious lounge areas with televisions;

  • billiard room;

  • chess.

The hotel also has free parking. In addition, the complex has a designated place for table tennis. Of course, as in many other complexes on the Black Sea, on the territory of the Kaurma Hotel (Dzhemete) a barbecue is also equipped. This hotel also has its own bike rental service.





The hotel staff praises most of its former guests. Hotel guests are friendly and polite to their guests. The same goes for the hotel owners themselves. The owners of the Kaurma hotel in Dzhemete, according to vacationers, are doing everything possible to make its guests relax on its territory as comfortably as possible.

Services for children

Tourists consider this complex to be particularly suitable for families with children. For young guests on the territory of the guest house "Kaurma" (Dzhemete) provides many additional amenities. So, for example, the hotel, among other things, is equipped with a good, judging by the reviews, playground. In the dining room of the hotel, its guests can order including children's dishes. Also, a cot can be delivered to guests in this hotel upon request.

Kaurma (Dzhemete): hotel food reviews

Unlike many other simple Russian mini-hotels on the Black Sea, the Kaurma hotel offers its guests rooms including meals. Book here accommodation with breakfast is advised by most vacationers. Dining guests at the hotel are offered pretty high quality. The local chef cooks, judging by the reviews, delicious. At the same time, he uses only high-quality and fresh products for his dishes. Regarding the cleanliness of the dining room and kitchen, tourists also have no complaints about this complex.

Those vacationers who rented rooms at the Kaurma Hotel (Dzhemete) without meals can cook their own meals (in apartments) or visit city cafes. There are a lot of dining rooms near the hotel.





Sea

Guests of the Kaurma Hotel visit the public beach of Dzhemete. Its main feature is that it is covered with very soft golden sand. When you first visit this beach, some tourists even get the feeling that they are not in Anapa on the Black Sea, but somewhere on the Spanish or Italian coast.

The disadvantages of the beach Dzhemete tourists include its crowded. Also, the minus of relaxing on the sea in this place, according to vacationers, is a large number of mosquitoes.
