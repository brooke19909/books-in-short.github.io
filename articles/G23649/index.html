Oasis Nessebar 3 * (Bulgaria / Nessebar): hotel description, services, reviews

"Sunny Beach" is the largest resort in Bulgaria. It is located on the picturesque Black Sea coast, 32 km north of the city of Burgas. To the east, at a distance of 434 km, is the capital of Bulgaria, Sofia, and in the immediate vicinity of the resort is the city of Nessebar.





Since 1959, the infrastructure of Sunny Beach has been developing rapidly - new hotels, restaurants, clubs and other entertainment venues are being built. Currently, the resort has more than 150 hotels, numerous villas, cottages and pensions. One of the popular tourist accommodation options is the Oasis Nessebar 3 * hotel.





About the resort

Future guests of the Oasis Nessebar 3 * hotel (Bulgaria, Nessebar) would be interested to know that "Sunny Beach" is one of the most famous, popular and actively developing resorts. At one time, he was awarded one of the prestigious international awards - the Blue Flag, which is annually awarded by the European Commission for the Environment to the districts based on the results of checking the cleanliness and environmental friendliness of water and sand.





The resort has a large number of tourist facilities (restaurants, nightclubs, bars, discos, shopping centers), which leave a lasting impression on guests.

Vacationers are provided with many opportunities for entertainment and sports (parachuting, surfing, paragliding, sailing, water skiing, tennis, beach volleyball, minigolf, boating, bowling, etc.) Those who wish can also go on a yacht.

A regular bus service connects the resort with Burgas and Varna.

About climate

The best time to visit the popular resort is the period from May to October, and swimming and sunbathing are recommended in June-September. Since July, the weather in Bulgaria is sunny and warm. In summer t air: + 23-30 degrees. The water in the sea is quite warm until the very end of September.

Ski lovers value winter more. The weather in Bulgaria depends on the characteristics of the temperate continental climate prevailing here. Average t in January (the coldest month): -2 to +2 ° C. In the mountains, t can drop to - 15.

The climate of Bulgaria is considered to be very favorable for recreation, especially it is appreciated by residents of mid-latitudes.
