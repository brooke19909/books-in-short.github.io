Waterpark (Belokurikha): drinking, services, addresses

Belokurikha is a popular resort in the Altai Territory, attracting hundreds of tourists all year round. This place is known for its picturesque nature, mountain air and mud deposits. Every vacationer will find something to their taste here: adults are attracted by radon treatment and mud therapy, children will enjoy the entertainment centers and the water park.





Belokurikha has two institutions of this kind: Belovodye and Water World. Perhaps these complexes can be called one of the favorite places for families who came to relax at the resort with children.

Complex "Belovodye"

The largest and best is the water park located in the hotel and entertainment complex "Belovodye" (Belokurikha). It offers excellent treatment and relaxation services: Finnish sauna, hammam, Russian bath, modern hot tubs, for children there are water slides and pools with warm water. For beauty, the complex has a waterfall, the noise of which calms and uplifts.

Water World - Wellness Center

The complex is mainly focused on treatment, but sometimes it is also called a water park.
