Hotel Montemar Beach Resort 3 * (Rhodes, Greece): description and reviews of tourists

Today every year more and more Russians choose the Greek island of Rhodes for summer holidays. This fact is not a surprise, because this place boasts excellent beaches, a lot of entertainment, magnificent nature and the presence of interesting historical sights. The hotel base here is represented by institutions of various levels - from budget to luxury. But most tourists prefer 3-star Rhodes hotels. After all, local hotels of this category offer guests a very comfortable, but at the same time budget accommodation. One of the best three-star hotels in Rhodes is considered Montemar Beach Resort 3 * (Lardos). About what he can offer travelers, we learn today.





Location

This hotel is located in a quiet place called Pefka, on the beach. As you know, the island of Rhodes is washed by two seas: the Mediterranean and the Aegean. Pefka is located on the shores of the Aegean Sea. The distance to the nearest village of Lardos is one and a half kilometers. From the resort town of Lindos, which has a lot of shops, bars, restaurants and various entertainment, the Montemar Beach Hotel is six kilometers away. The island‚Äôs main city, Rhodes, is 55 kilometers away. The air harbor is sixty kilometers from the hotel. Thus, after arriving at the airport, the journey to the hotel will take you about an hour. You are unlikely to be bored on the road, as magnificent landscapes will open from the windows of the bus or car. As for the distance to the sea, the hotel complex consists of two parts: one of them is located across the road from the beach, and the other is only a couple of hundred meters from the water's edge. So the path to the beach will take several minutes.





Rhodes: Montemar Beach Resort - Description

As mentioned above, this three-star hotel complex belongs to the budget category and offers its guests a comfortable stay in the immediate vicinity of the pristine Aegean Sea. The hotel is large enough and includes 361 rooms. The rooms here are small, but cozy and comfortable. The hotel complex is represented by several buildings located both on the first line a couple of hundred meters from the water's edge, and on the second line (you must cross the road on the way to the beach). Montemar Beach Resort 3 * has a developed infrastructure and its own sand and pebble beach, awarded the UNESCO Cleanliness Award. The hotel complex has a restaurant, a tavern, bars, pools, a conference room and even a chapel. This hotel is perfect for families with children, as well as for fun youth. As for the guests, most of them are Europeans. There is also a sufficient percentage of tourists from Russia and other countries of the former Soviet Union.

Hotel Policies

Like most other hotels around the world, Montemar Beach Resort (Rhodes) has an internal check-in and check-out time. So, the arrival of travelers arriving after two hours of the day. However, the hotel administration always tries to meet the guests and, in case of their earlier arrival, does everything possible so that they can enter the booked room as soon as possible. Thus, if you arrived at the hotel earlier than two in the afternoon, then if your room has already been vacated by the previous tenants and prepared by the maids, you will be settled immediately. Otherwise (such situations are possible in the midst of the tourist season, when the hotel is almost one hundred percent full), you can leave your things in the luggage room and take a walk, have a snack, take a swim in the pool or go straight to the sea on the beach. As for the day of departure, you need to free your room before noon. When you hand over the keys, you should pay for your stay at Montemar Beach Resort, if you have not previously settled with a travel agency in your country of residence. You can pay at the hotel both in cash and with plastic cards of popular payment systems.





Number of rooms

As mentioned above, Montemar Beach Resort 3 * consists of two parts, one of which is located directly on the shore, and the other is on the second line across the road. In total there are 361 comfortable rooms. All buildings of the hotel complex are one- and two-story and are represented by the following categories:

- "Montemar Maine." This category is represented by four buildings located on a hill with a magnificent view of the Gulf of Lardos on the second coastline. There are 101 rooms here.

- "Montemar Beach." Four buildings of this category, as the name implies, are located almost on the beach. They include 91 rooms.

- "Montemar Village". This category is represented by 86 bungalows, comfortably located in a picturesque garden on the second line near the buildings of Montemar Main.

- "Lindos Sunny Village." This category includes 33 front line bungalows.

- "Montemar Village". This category is represented by four buildings, including 50 rooms, and located 150 meters from the beach.

Room filling

The rooms of the hotel complex Montemar Beach Resort 3 * have everything you need for a comfortable stay. So, each of them has a private bathroom with shower or bathtub and hairdryer, air conditioning, satellite TV, telephone, refrigerator and terrace or balcony. The rooms have tile flooring. For a surcharge, guests can use the safe and minibar. In addition, from seven in the morning until midnight, guests have the opportunity to order room service. Rooms are cleaned on a daily basis except Sunday, linen and towels are changed twice a week.





Nutrition

Hotel Montemar Beach Resort offers its guests all-inclusive meals. Three times a day, a buffet is served in the main restaurant of the hotel complex, serving both local and international dishes, as well as desserts and fresh fruits. In addition, during the day you can grab a bite to eat at the snack bar. You can enjoy refreshments and alcoholic drinks in several hotel bars: in the lobby, by the pool and on the beach. In addition, a lunch box will be provided upon prior request.

Beach vacation

As already mentioned, the three-star hotel complex "Montemar Beach" (Rhodes) has its own sandy and pebble beach. The distance from it to the buildings located on the first line is only 150 meters. If you are housed in the buildings of the second line, then you have to go to the beach a little longer, and along the way you will need to cross the road. The beach has sun loungers and parasols. Using them for hotel guests is free. Also on the beach there is equipment for entertainment on the water.





Pools

The hotel complex has several freshwater pools. Near them are equipped with sun terraces with comfortable sun loungers and parasols. There is also a poolside bar. In addition, for the convenience of the smallest guests of the hotel and their parents, two swimming pools for children are equipped. Here, little tourists can safely frolic in clean water.

Entertainment

On the territory of the hotel complex Montemar Beach Resort 3 *, tourists have the opportunity not only to soak up the gentle sun and swim in the sea or pool, but also have fun and interesting time for other activities. So, lovers of outdoor activities can play volleyball, indoor soccer, tennis or ping pong, do water sports and attend aerobics classes. You can also play darts or billiards here. The hotel has an animation team throughout the day and until the evening, the participants of which do everything possible to diversify leisure travelers. In the evenings, colorful shows and discos are held.

Not paid attention to the smallest guests. So, for the youngest travelers, the hotel has a mini club, there are children's pools and playgrounds, as well as mini-discos.





Infrastructure

Despite the fact that the hotel belongs to the budget category, it has everything you need for a comfortable and convenient stay for travelers. So, the reception desk is open around the clock, so if necessary, you can contact the administrator at any time of the day or night. Also at the hotel you can order room service, use the laundry services, rent a car or bicycle, book an excursion, exchange currency, visit a hairdresser and leave things for storage in the luggage compartment. Montemar Beach offers an airport shuttle service (must be booked in advance). Wi-Fi is available in public areas for an extra charge. If necessary, it is possible to call a doctor (paid service).

In addition, the hotel complex has a conference room. It is equipped with everything necessary for business meetings, symposia, seminars and other similar events. The hotel also provides services for organizing a variety of celebrations: weddings, corporate parties, etc.





Montemar Beach Resort 3 *: traveler reviews

Since for many tourists a very important factor when choosing this or that hotel is the comments of people who have already stayed here, we decided to give generalized reviews of our compatriots about this hotel complex. First of all, we can say with confidence that many travelers were satisfied with their vacation both in Rhodes in general and in Montemar Beach Resort in particular. But we offer to understand everything in more detail.

As for the rooms, there were no complaints about the cleanliness, decor and operation of the equipment. Despite the fact that the hotel is three-star, the rooms are quite spacious, the atmosphere is cozy and pleasant, and the bath has a supply of toiletries, which is constantly replenished by maids. The only thing that confused some tourists who got rooms on the upper floors was a slight interruption in water. But this phenomenon turned out to be temporary. In addition, the hotel administration in case of a complaint always tries to move guests to a more suitable room.

A lot of positive reviews relate to infrastructure and entertainment for children. So, the Russians who came to rest with their families were very pleased with the presence of water slides, paddling pools, a mini club, playgrounds and children's animation.

As for nutrition, no complaints were expressed with the quality and variety of food. So, our compatriots claim that the menu here is more than good for the level of a three-star hotel.

Tourists really liked the hotel's beach. Despite the fact that when entering the water there is a large pebble, it is quite possible to do without special shoes. As for the sea, literally everyone was delighted with it.

In general, our compatriots were satisfied with the animation program, especially the daily one. So, many liked the work of local animators who gently entertained holidaymakers, inviting them to take part in gymnastics, yoga, dancing, competitions and funny games. The evening shows seemed good to tourists, but they did not cause enthusiastic enthusiasm.

Summing up, we can say that according to our compatriots, this hotel is a very good budget option for a vacation, especially if you are going on vacation with children.
