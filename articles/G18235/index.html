Hotel Centara Grand Beach Resort Phuket 5 *, Thailand, Phuket: overview, description, features and reviews

If you want to have a great time on the island of Phuket in Thailand and are looking for a comfortable hotel with its own beach and within walking distance of the main tourist infrastructure facilities, then Centara Grand Beach Resort Phuket 5 * can be a very suitable option for you.



Location

This hotel is located on the seafront on Karon Beach. Patong City is 7 kilometers away and Phuket is 15 kilometers away. You can get to these settlements by taxi or by renting a scooter. The nearest airport is 45 kilometers from the hotel. Therefore, the road to the hotel will take you no more than an hour.

Cleaning

According to travelers, the maids of the Centara Grand Beach Resort Phuket 5 * (Thailand, Phuket) do an excellent job with their duties. Check in, departure

Tourists were pleased with the fact that upon arrival, regardless of time, the keys to the room here are issued almost immediately. So you do not have to wait for the checkout time. As for the day of departure, the occupied apartments should be vacated before noon. If your departure from the hotel is planned later, you can extend the room at an additional cost. It is advisable to notify the administrator at the reception in advance of such an intention.
