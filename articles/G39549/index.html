Protective Board Models

The 18650 batteries from the Japanese company PANASONIC have an average cost of $ 40 for 6 pieces.

Such products can be used for laptop charging cans and flashlights. It is important to consider the release date and originality of production.

These models have a protective board approximately 2 mm thick. It is necessary, since any battery can be overdischarged. Protection protects the device from such action that the voltage threshold is not exceeded.

As soon as the battery reaches the acceptable limits of 4.25 watts, the protective board disables charging and the process stops.

At a voltage of 2.75 watts, the battery simply ceases to function. When discharged below this voltage, the board will break the charging circuit.

Short circuit protection is also provided. As soon as the current reaches a voltage of 7.5 A, the system will open this circuit.

Such precautions significantly increase battery life. If it is strongly discharged, below 2.5 W, the device can go into "sleep mode", in which either the resource decreases, or it will no longer function.
