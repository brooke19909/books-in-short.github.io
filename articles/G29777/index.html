DEH-80PRS: specifications and reviews

As a rule, many motorists after a certain period of using the head unit switch to a new one purchased on their own. Studying forums, reviews of professionals and consumers, the majority of drivers make a choice in favor of the Pioneer model, namely the DEH-80PRS.

This radio tape recorder suits many in terms of the quality of its sound, design, and the presence of line outputs (there are only three of them). This device is capable of working with a bluetooth connection, and it also reads WAW with ease. In the article we will try to briefly, but as informatively as possible, consider this radio tape recorder and its competitor Alpine CDA-117RI.



Equipment

The manufacturer complements the model Pioneer DEH-80PRS (photo can be seen below) in a cardboard box, which in general is able to protect the device from unintentional deformation.

And what awaits the buyer inside? To begin with: the device itself, as well as the shaft for it. The manufacturer additionally put a lot of different documentation, deliberately attaching to it a special disk that will help with the installation. There is also a case for transferring the panel, a package for removing the device, a control panel, an extension cable and several microphones.



Radio inspection

In life, the DEH-80PRS cassette player looks much better than in photos on the Internet or on the box. It is necessary to treat it as carefully as possible, since its body is glossy. This suggests that with careless use, it will all be in small scratches. The front part is as ergonomic as possible, although consumers note a slight inconvenience. What are you talking about? Next to the volume control in the closest proximity is a button that is responsible for selecting a playback source. Because of this, any person with ease will accidentally increase or decrease the level of sound reproduction. Of the positive aspects in the design, it can be noted that motorized panels are not used here. Therefore, all parts are able to function for a long time without the slightest glitch.

Under the front panel there is a drive that is responsible for processing discs. You can immediately notice a special slot for a memory card, if necessary, it is allowed to use an adapter (in case the micro-size flash drive is used), as well as a drive release button. On the back of the radio are special outputs: for antenna, microphone, RCA (needed for an amplifier), USB (2 pieces), AUX. There is also a fuse.

There is no cooler on the radiator. This fact can be attributed to both pluses and minuses. On the one hand, this completely eliminates the noise from its operation, on the other hand, the DEH-80PRS radio heats up quite a lot.



Device menu

All consumers declare that if the buyer has long been familiar with the technology from Pioneer, then it will be as easy as possible for him to deal with the device menu of this model. In total, it has 4 functions. Briefly consider them.

  • "Entertainment". This category is one of the most non-functional. Here are just a few settings. The menu allows you to change the background for playing songs, as well as hide or show the clock.
  • "Function". Here the user can choose the sequence of inclusion of songs.
  • "Audio". The menu allows you to change everything regarding the sound reproduction of the Pioneer DEH-80PRS. Subwoofer settings, equalizer, time pauses and so on - all this changes in this category.
  • "Lighting". Everything is intuitive here - setting up the display and its backlight.
