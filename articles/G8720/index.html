Underwater Hotel in Dubai: Visiting Captain Nemo

Dubai can be called a city in which you can meet seven and even more wonders. It is here that the highest and most expensive hotel was built, and now also a hotel under water.



Hydropolis, which is the name of this unusual and exclusive underwater hotel in Dubai, is located in the Persian Gulf, at a depth of eighteen meters. The cost of this structure is half a billion dollars, and the area is more than 260 hectares. According to approximate calculations, these are as many as three London Hyde Parks.

One can only imagine the unusually high level of comfort that this underwater hotel in Dubai offers. The sophistication and beauty of its designs, the numbers of the original form, the successful combination of daylight and artificial lighting - all this is so harmonious that it really makes you wonder at the flight of imagination of architects and designers.





An underwater hotel in Dubai, the photo of which suggests that it has a land part, offers lower tiers for the accommodation of its customers, as well as premises for staff and warehouses. While in the upper are trading floors, several jewelry boutiques, a disco, as well as a business center. People move in floors in high-speed elevators. In addition, there is a clinic, a biolaboratory, etc. on the floors.

Everything around is permeated by an underwater mysterious atmosphere, starting with the approach to Hydropolis. People will be delivered to him by train through the connecting tunnel.

The underwater hotel in Dubai is built in shallow water and has the shape of a bowl mounted right at the bottom of the bay. It was constructed on land and then mounted in the right place. It was drained from the water, equipped with giant aquariums with the most exotic marine animals for viewing from the windows, because the water around was muddy and unsuitable for a natural panorama.





Living in it, clients experience peace and tranquility, admiring the underwater world and its inhabitants.

An underwater hotel in Dubai, the price of accommodation in which starts from five and a half thousand dollars a day, is considered today the most successful combination of reality and fantasy. Experts position it as a ten-star, therefore, the cost corresponds to the expected level. In shape, it resembles a submarine with 220 luxurious cabins-rooms, made of plexiglass like air bubbles. The most elite of them have small fountains that complement the already fabulous atmosphere.





In addition to luxurious rooms, the underwater hotel in Dubai has in its infrastructure chic restaurants, a beauty salon, SPA centers, and an entertainment complex for the smallest. Late in the evening, it is planned to hold a laser enchanting show, which can be admired from convenient viewing platforms.

The guests of this original building can not worry about the reliability of their temporary shelter. Hydropolis is even equipped with a security system that provides it with a high level of protection. The design provides for a unique technology, thanks to which, under danger, its underwater part emerges urgently. Architects also provided for protection against climatic changes in temperature and even against tsunamis.

Everyone staying at Hydropolis feels like a guest of Captain Nemo for a few days.
