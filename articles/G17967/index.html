Yarn "BBB". Review and reviews

Very often, yarn becomes the source of inspiration for knitters: its color, texture, composition. It is pleasant to pick up soft, light, high-quality yarn, and it is even more pleasant to realize your creative projects with it.

Consider in more detail material that can inspire. Your attention is the Italian yarn BBB.





Types of yarn "BBB"
