Side Prenses Resort Hotel Spa (Turkey, Side, Side Center): hotel description and photos

After a tiring working year in the life of an adult, there comes a vacation time, which can be spent in completely different ways. It will be fascinating excursions, visits to museums, rest as a savage or getting a dark tan, everyone decides independently. For couples with children, it is important to organize a memorable trip for their child, who wants to experience new feelings no less than their parents. Touching hot sunshine, clean air and a sense of peace can be felt when visiting Turkey, namely the city - Side.

A small village located on the peninsular and mainland of the Mediterranean attracts thousands of tourists every year. This is due to a favorable mild climate, friendly local people and excellent living conditions. Side Prenses Resort Hotel Spa is a bright representative of a high level of service and a lot of entertainment, where, regardless of age, everyone will find something to their liking. Vivacity, fun, restless enthusiasm, positive mood, strengthening immunity and improving health are just not many that will leave unforgettable memories from being in the walls of this hotel.





general characteristics

The five-star hotel complex Side Prenses Resort Hotel Spa (Pegasus) is considered relatively young, since it was erected and commissioned in 2011. The total area is 16 thousand square meters. It represents ennobled spaces rich in green spaces, including spreading palm trees, bright flower beds and exotic plants.

The main and only building of the Side Prenses Resort Hotel Spa 5 * hotel, the photo of which you see in the article, is represented by a six-story building in the shape of a crescent, which is equipped with four elevators for convenience. Exterior decoration is characterized by impressive ground floor columns and a royal dome on the roof, which causes a feeling of falling into a Turkish fairy tale full of pleasant surprises and miracles.

Getting to the hotel complex is quite easy, since the distance to the nearest international airport of Antalya is about 60 kilometers. Pets are not allowed. Due attention is paid to recreation for people with disabilities: in addition to equipped with all necessary rooms, there are special ramps for descending into the pools.

Friendly and good-natured staff speaks Russian, English and Turkish. For arrivals by car, a free parking space is provided. Smoking is strictly prohibited throughout.

Accommodation

The room fund of the Side Prenses Resort Hotel Spa (Side, Turkey) consists of more than three hundred apartments. Among them are the following categories:

  • Standard
  • Suite
  • Family

The interior design is decorated in a classic style, there is minimalism in decoration and rigor in design. The dimensions of the first category are 25 square meters, while the rest of the chambers are about 40. The prevailing color scheme is all kinds of shades of white and gray, but this does not make them gloomy and boring. Bright bedspreads, pillows, wall masterpieces of local painters and French glazing, which allows the sun to touch the rays of sleeping vacationers, give the room an unprecedented spaciousness, lightness and freshness. The floors are covered with laminate. The view from the windows differs in its orientation: the sea, park area, pool.

A terrace or balcony exit is inherent in every apartment, which makes your stay more comfortable and cozy. They are equipped with sunbeds with a plastic table, which allow you to meet the sunrises and see off the sunsets in the fresh air without leaving the chambers.

There is the possibility of installing an additional folding bed, which requires coordination with the management.





Housing Features

The furniture is represented by a standard set made of wood: soft beds, a spacious wardrobe, a desk, bedside tables and a place for luggage. A large mirror visually enlarges the space and helps induce the beauty of its appearance. An individual air conditioning system maintains the required microclimate in the room. You can watch local news programs or Russian-language channels on the LCD TV screen.

The presence of a safe makes it possible to forget about worries and not worry about important documents. However, this is an additional paid service.

To meet the morning in a good mood, starting with a cup of tea or invigorating coffee is possible with the help of the available electric kettle, tea set and cardboard cups. The minibar includes daily refreshments with drinking water. A greater variety of drinks and alcohol is not included in the price and is paid upon arrival.

An internal telephone allows you to freely use the Room Service and quickly resolve issues with the hotel administration.

The bathroom is decorated with designer ceramic tiles and equipped with modern everyday objects: sink, shower or bath, hairdryer. Here are a set of personal hygiene products and cosmetics.

Towels are cleaned and changed by qualified maids every day, bed linen is changed three times a week.





Supply system

Side Prenses Resort Hotel Spa food reviews rarely get negative, they are more likely to be enthusiastic. To quench the feeling of hunger or thirst for holidaymakers is offered in various institutions according to different systems, including bars, restaurants, bistros. The main restaurant offers four meals a day, which includes two breakfasts, lunch and dinner, organized according to the buffet. An incredible abundance of dishes, made and decorated by high-level talented culinary specialists, will delight you not only with gustatory delights, but also with an unsurpassed design that increases the appetite and desire to taste these creations of cooks. The buffet type of supply of nutritious products enriched with vitamins and useful microelements allows everyone to independently control their diet.

The interior of the main restaurant is bright, characterized by a large number of spotlights, stretch multi-level ceilings and numerous columns. Capacity - 400 people inside and 400 on the terrace. Wicker chairs, first-class serving, amazing views of the mountains and the sea allow you to combine business with pleasure. The cuisine is predominantly international. High chairs and a children's menu are provided for children.

Additional facilities

In addition, there are two restaurants operating on the custom-made A la Carte menu. A one-time visit is included in the price of the ticket, however, a preliminary reservation is required. The opportunity to enjoy Italian and Turkish dishes will present previously untested gastronomic pleasure and a good mood.

A snack restaurant and bistro, ice cream and pastries, night soup, snacks and sweets are available in a number of bars, including a beach, lobby, pool and disco. Imported alcoholic drinks and freshly squeezed juices are paid upon acquisition. Until 24:00 cocktails, local alcohol are free.

The vitamin bar allows you to keep your body in good shape with healing teas and herbal drinks, giving a charge of vivacity, positive and smile.





Aquazone

The hotel complex Side Prenses Resort Hotel Spa 5 * (Turkey) is equipped with outdoor and indoor swimming pools, a water park and a children's water area. The main outdoor pool has a circular shape, in the center of which is an island with many sunbeds. At the very core is a swimming area and the opportunity to sit in the water while enjoying a hydromassage. The island is connected with land by two wooden bridges, diving from which is strictly prohibited.

Night illumination is an indescribable sight, enthralling every traveler.

Regular water aerobics classes from qualified instructors will not let you be bored for a minute. The opportunity to play water polo will amuse both adults and kids. The team spirit in the struggle for victory will give a portion of positive, new acquaintances and friendly communication to each participant in the game in the team.

The separate pool is awarded two bright exciting slides with open gutters. Drive and joy, as tourists write in their reviews, will visit everyone who decides to go down from them.

An thirty-square meter outdoor paddling pool allows kids to splash around, dive and play near their parents, who can sit on the numerous sun loungers around.

Beach

The hotel complex Side Prenses Resort Hotel Spa 5 * (Side) is located on the first coastline. The lodgers reach the shore, having covered a distance of 150 meters. The path passes through a park area with wide alleys and beautiful scenic views. The entrance to the sea is equipped with a wooden slope.

The coastal private zone has a length of 100 meters, the beach is mostly sand, the entrance to the vast expanses of water is gentle. The seabed is characterized by the absence of stones and boulders, which is favorable for families with children, in which you can not worry about the safety of entertainment, games and bathing kids.

The coastline is equipped with numerous sun loungers for sunbathing and getting a dark tan. Umbrellas that create shady areas are perfect for those who are not a supporter of sunlight. Soft mattresses, shower, dressing rooms and a toilet are provided free of charge, but towels require an additional charge.

You can take yourself on the beach by playing volleyball, which will help maintain your body in good shape, water sports, riding a banana or scooter. Diving into the depths of the Mediterranean Sea, exploring the underwater world and its inhabitants is possible for both an experienced diver and a novice lover.





Children's leisure

Side Prenses Resort Hotel Spa 5 * receives positive reviews from grateful parents. Organization of pastimes for kids, according to vacationers, is thought out by the administration to the smallest detail. Paid babysitting services are provided for the little ones, which will take the precious child's leisure time, and parents can relax while enjoying the local beauties and wonderful atmosphere.

For children from four to twelve years a mini-club is open during the day. Bright design with cartoon characters inspire kids. Under the supervision of a qualified teacher, the children will perform a number of developmental, cognitive and creative tasks, play darts. Organized by Russian-speaking animators, sports and competitions for the manifestation of ingenuity and resourcefulness will amuse the kids and give them unforgettable memories. In addition, the children's room contains a number of toys, designers, cars, utensils and small dolls, which will take the little guest in his spare, from bathing and eating, minutes.

In the fresh air, the playground is equipped with slides, carousels, mazes and swings. It allows babies to spend energy for nighttime sleep. The evening ends with a disco, where the younger generation can feel like adults.

Upon request, a cot can be provided in the room.





Pastime adults

Most holidaymakers prefer not only to swim in the rays of the Turkish sun, but also actively spend their free time. For those who regularly monitor the relief of their body or want to lose a couple of extra pounds, the doors of the gym complex are always open. The gym is equipped with all necessary equipment. Friendly instructors work to help you make a schedule of training and the required physical activity.

Table tennis, basketball, football, a tennis court and mini-golf are free, but a game of billiards is paid extra. You can go fishing for a weighty catch, which appeal to lovers of fishing rods and fishing tackle.

At the reception you can order a horse ride with professional instructors, which will certainly bring bright colors to the whole vacation.

A talented and professional team of animators will brighten up the evening of each guest, giving an unforgettable show, national dance compositions, acrobatic stunts and various quizzes with symbolic prizes. Every evening, performances are held in the amphitheater of the Side Prenses Resort Hotel Spa. At the end - a Turkish night full of drive, adrenaline, dancing and new acquaintances.

Special attention deserves the SPA-center. Procedures for the restoration and rejuvenation of the skin, quality massage from professional masters of their craft are available here. A beauty salon where you can do manicure, hair and other procedures will delight lovers of excellent appearance. Sauna and hammam require payment upon delivery.





Additional services

Bicycle and car rental gives you the chance to visit local surroundings, which are rich in interesting sights with a fascinating history, numerous ruins of ancient cities and natural creations.

Laundry, dry cleaning and ironing services are paid extra. Payments are accepted by bank cards of world banking systems. Wi-Fi is distributed throughout the territory, and according to experienced reviews, high-speed Internet allows you to freely communicate with family and friends.

Thus, thanks to numerous entertainments, friendly staff and excellent service, the desire to return here reappears for everyone who has ever visited this fabulous country called Side Prenses Resort Hotel Spa. Tours here can be bought both by fun youth companies and family people. Here everyone will find entertainment to their liking and will be pleased with the rest.
