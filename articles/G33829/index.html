The best new restaurants in St. Petersburg

In St. Petersburg there are a huge number of different restaurants, cafes, bars and other catering establishments. Every day their number only increases. We offer information on which new restaurants in St. Petersburg deserve the attention of tourists and local residents. After reading the article, you will receive detailed information about where they are located, how they work and what visitors offer in the menu.



New restaurants and bars of St. Petersburg

The review of urban catering establishments includes those that have opened in recent years. Despite the fact that they have been working not so long ago, these cafes and restaurants have already managed to gain popularity among local residents. We also invite you to get to know them.

  • Restaurant "Grill Station". Already from the name of the institution you can determine its basic concept - the preparation of various dishes on the grill. Visitors are welcome at 139/1 Moskovsky Prospekt. The highlight of the menu is the steaks. Moreover, at the request of the customer, they can be prepared with varying degrees of roasting. The menu also has European dishes. Among the additional services that the institution hospitably provides its visitors, the following: free parking; the Internet; the presence of a children's room. On weekdays from 12.00 to 16.00 there is a 15% discount on the main menu. The average bill is from 1000 rubles.
  • Restaurant "New Holland" (St. Petersburg). This establishment was opened in 1991, and was restored in 2016, so we decided to include it in the list too. If you want to plunge into the atmosphere of the city and feel its special aura and indescribable color, by all means come to Trud Square, 3. Here you will find delicious food; great drinks; polite workers. Pleasant, dim light and a large number of interesting details will allow you to spend time in a pleasant environment. Here you can see wooden sailboats and a pleasant color scheme. The menu has a large selection of alcoholic drinks and beer.
  • Restaurant "New World" (St. Petersburg) - is located on Ho Chi Minh Street, 16. It can be reached by metro, the nearest station is Prospekt Prosvescheniya. This restaurant has a good romantic date. The presence of panoramic windows offers stunning views of the city. The restaurant itself has a greenhouse where various seasonings are grown, and under the ceiling of the restaurant you can see real canoes. Here reigns comfort and coziness. The menu includes European and author's cuisine. Varied breakfasts are in great demand. The average bill in a restaurant is from 1100 rubles.
  • "Khich" - a new meat restaurant in St. Petersburg. On Moskovsky Prospekt, house 179 - one of the most popular institutions of the city is located. Here you can not only taste the most delicious meat dishes, but also try the most delicious wine and Belgian beer. One of the distinguishing features of the establishment is the absence of female waiters. After all, it is men who are real connoisseurs and lovers of meat dishes. The level of service will undoubtedly please you, you do not have to wait long for your order. The menu has a large selection of meat dishes, fish, but also there is something to enjoy and vegetarians.
  • Restaurant of Georgian cuisine "Suli Guli". His address is Teplovoznaya street, 31. Huge panoramic windows, flowers in pots, upholstered furniture - all this creates a unique, inimitable atmosphere. Here you can have a great free time in a big company or together with your loved one. In warmer months, visitors love to relax on the summer terrace. Parents with children who like to play in the children's room love to come to the restaurant. It has everything for a child to have fun and interesting. There is even a pirate ship. The following services are offered to visitors: free internet; table games; live music; delivery of finished products and much more.

"Mad Max"

What other new restaurants of St. Petersburg are known, the opening of which became a real holiday for a large number of visitors? On Sadovaya Street 12, an unusual institution, Mad Max, began its work. They brew beer and light it on the dance floor; meet friends at the bar and spend romantic evenings on the open summer terrace. Whiskey lovers will be delighted, as their favorite drink is presented in more than a hundred options. Everything in this establishment is of the highest quality: both kitchen and service. Restaurant "La Cucaracha"

Near the metro station "Old Village" is a restaurant of Mexican and Texas cuisine "La Cucaracha". It is located on 34 Optikov Street. His cook is a real Mexican who knows a lot about national dishes. The wine list is pleasantly pleasing to visitors, alcoholic drinks are presented from around the world, including from Chile and Argentina. Cuban and Latin American music sounds here, to the rhythms of which it is very well rested. Tall windows and lots of greenery create a pleasant, comfortable atmosphere. The restaurant provides various discounts, among them: 15% on weekdays for all menus, discounts for large companies. The menu has special dishes for children. The average check at the institution is from 900 rubles.
