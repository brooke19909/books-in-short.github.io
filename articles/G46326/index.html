Materials and Technologies





Only high-quality comfortable and energy-efficient housing is being built in the Lukino-Varino residential complex, SU 22. You can hear from the developer that all the buildings in this complex are mostly monolithic. After all, only such a structure is distinguished by its energy-saving properties. It has good sound insulation, comfort. This material is also characterized by earthquake resistance. It has flame retardant, heat resistant and waterproof properties. The monolith does not form voids and seams in the building frame.

A monolithic residential building differs from others in its rigidity and strength. It gives uniform shrinkage, since the base of the heavy structure is distributed throughout the monolith, in contrast to panel houses, where the support goes only to the walls. This material in construction is the most durable of all known to date. In the house from the monolith, finishing work can be started immediately after the end of its construction.

When erecting buildings in the Lukino-Varino residential complex, they use the most modern energy-saving technologies. They significantly reduce the emission of harmful substances into the airspace formed during the cooling and heating of buildings.

During the construction of buildings, the latest technologies and modern materials are used, as well as the most high-tech engineering equipment, completely new trunk communications are created. During the construction of the residential complex, the whole range of works is carried out to restore the water supply system and create water disposal facilities in the entire microdistrict.
