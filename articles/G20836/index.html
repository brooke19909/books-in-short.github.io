Sydney Australian metropolis: Sydney Harbor Park, Hyde Park, Royal National Park and other city park areas

The city of Sydney Park has more than one in its territory. Many of them are located outside the metropolis. It is worth visiting this settlement only in order to admire the local nature reserves and national zones. It is enough for any tourist to visit a park zone only once, and he will immediately understand how delicately, carefully and respectfully the local population treats its nature. After all, all territories are very well-groomed, neat and, most importantly, comfortable and convenient for both four-legged inhabitants and visitors. Sydney can rightfully be proud of its parks. We will talk about some of them in our description.

Sydney harbor

Sydney boasts many natural areas. Sydney Harbor Park is one of those places worth visiting. Sydney Harbor is located on the coastal space of Sydney Harbor. Small islands, coastal areas that are flooded by tides, steep peninsulas are almost untouched by people and belong to the category of protected sea and land landscapes.





At the end of the century before last, the idea to break up the park ensemble arose, but only in 1965 did active work begin on this issue. Officially, the Sydney Sydney Harbor National Park opened in early April 1975. Gradually, the territory of the object expanded, and now it reaches more than four square kilometers.

Visitors can become participants in all kinds of excursions and educational programs. They are given the opportunity to participate in a sailboat regatta or ride a kayak. Also at their service are walking tours of any difficulty level. And if you want to study the ancient cave art belonging to the Kadigal tribe, then this will be available to you. The native conductor will happily become your companion in this exciting entertainment.

The oldest park in the world

The Royal National Park was laid out in 1879. He is the oldest on Earth. There is an object near a city like Sydney. This park was the first protected area to which the phrase "national park" was applied. Its area exceeds 15 thousand hectares. Here collected a huge number of different representatives of the animal and plant world. Among them there are specimens that are under the threat of complete extinction.





The territory of the park on the east side is washed by the waters of the Tasman Sea. In this natural area, you can meet many snakes, lizards and colorful flowers. The trails of the park are lined with metal or wood. This is done so as not to harm the vegetation. Famous to many tourists, the snake head that hangs over the ocean is one of the symbols of the Royal National Park in Sydney.

In memory of the Olympics

Some Sydney parks are most directly related to the 2000 Olympics. So, we are talking about the park, which is called the Olympic Park. It is located in the western part of the village, 16 km from the main business district of the metropolis.

The area where the facility is located, began its active development just before the 2000 Olympics. Here they broke up a whole sports complex that met international standards and requirements. After the event, the use of this area continued further. Important business centers have moved here.





The main cultural events are regularly organized on the sports ground. In addition, several exhibition halls are located on the territory of the complex, where exhibitions of contemporary art are often organized.

Botanical Garden for Kings

Sydney Central Park is another place I would like to talk about. The Royal Botanic Gardens are considered the central park in the city. It is located in the central part of the metropolis and is one of the most beautiful places in Sydney. Every day thousands of guests come here and enjoy fabulous, truly royal landscapes.

In the garden you can see many exotic representatives of the flora and amazing animals, such as flying foxes or possums. There are also rare species of butterflies and birds that harmoniously fit into the look of this park. Paid small train runs for the convenience of visitors.
