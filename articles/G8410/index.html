Hotel Description

However, after several decades, the hulls noticeably dilapidated and began to require repair. In 2012, the hotel was closed for two years and underwent a large-scale reconstruction. In parallel, additional buildings were erected on the territory, which significantly changed the appearance of the hotel. For its guests Novostar Premium Bel Azur Thalassa 4 * opened its doors only in 2015. Since that time, in the low season, the hotel complex annually carries out repair work. This allows you to maintain the interior of the hotel at the proper level.
