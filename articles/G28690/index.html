Hotel Amara Prestige 5 * (Turkey, Kemer): description, service, reviews

Amara Hotel Prestige is located in Kemer, Turkey. It is classified as a five-star and claims to be one of the best in Kemer. However, for many years our compatriots who love to relax on Turkish beaches have gained considerable experience, including sad experience, which taught us not to trust the promises of hotels, no matter how attractive they may look. This fully applies to Amara Prestige Hotel 5 * (Turkey). Is it true that here all the conditions await the guest, as it should be by European standards of hotel service? What can you count on?





First-hand information

The most accurate data can be obtained from those travelers who have checked the quality of service at Amara Prestige Hotel 5 *. Of course, everyone wants to believe that it will not be a problem in this hotel, especially since its cost is quite high. At the same time, experienced tour operators advise their clients to first familiarize themselves with the impressions of those who have already stayed in a hotel, and only then decide whether to go here.

However, if we analyze the reviews that are present in the Russian-speaking sector of the Internet, we can see that those guests who came here quite a long time ago (before the change of management of the hotel), as well as those who settled right after the repair, which dragged on for longer than promised, were mostly dissatisfied. During this period, Amara Prestige Hotel 5 * (Kemer) was difficult to call really five-star, but over time, the situation stabilized to the delight of those who go on a trip today.

What to expect

The hotel is distinguished by an interesting, unusual design solution that impresses even experienced guests who have traveled around the world more than once and stayed in expensive, high-level places of temporary residence. In Amara Prestige, visitors will find a high level of comfort, thoughtful convenience, utilitarianism, combined with the spectacular design decisions.
