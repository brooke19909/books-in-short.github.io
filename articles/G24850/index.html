Hotel "Europe" in Minsk: location, rooms, service, reviews

Hotel "Europe" in Minsk is the first atrium-type hotel in the city. This means that the door of each room leads to an internal open gallery, from which you can admire the beautiful hall and the sophisticated light dome. The modern seven-story building was built in Art Nouveau style with classic elements of decor. In fact, the history of the structure began 200 years ago, but in the war years it was destroyed and only in 2007 found a new life.

Location

The address of the Europe Hotel in Minsk is Internationalnaya Street 28. This is the very center of the city, 30 km from the airport. It is also worth noting a convenient location regarding tourist sites, namely:

  • Cathedral of the Descent of the Holy Spirit (300 m);

Number of rooms

To accommodate guests in the hotel "Europe" in Minsk provides 67 rooms of different categories of comfort. Namely:

  • Single - a classic single room with a view of the main street of the city (from 8600 rubles).
  • Double (twin) room - a room with a large bed (a pair of separate) and a seating area (from 10 800 rubles).
  • King Deluxe - a double room with panoramic windows overlooking the town hall (from 11,400 rubles).
  • Junior Suite - an elegant room with a bedroom and a living room (from 16 300 rubles).
  • Business Suite - a luxurious two-room suite with city views (from 35,000 rubles).
  • Apartments - a cozy two-room suite with a small kitchen area (from 33 000 rubles).
  • Presidential Suite - a luxurious three-room suite (from 85,000 rubles).

Catering establishments

At the Europe Hotel in Minsk, guests can enjoy their time in such catering establishments:

  • The Avignon Restaurant is a luxurious Art Nouveau restaurant. Here you can taste delicious French dishes from the chef Dmitry Sadovsky. In the evenings, you can listen to live music. And this place is great for banquets.
  • Wine bar - a cozy restaurant on the seventh floor of the hotel. The highlight of the restrained English interior is the glass dome. The menu offers a wide range of wines and cognacs.
  • Lobby bar - round-the-clock establishment at the first hotel as well. During the day, you can enjoy your favorite alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks, and in the evenings you can listen to live piano music and singing.

Conference facilities

The hotel "Europe" in Minsk provides all the possibilities for organizing events. Namely:

  • The conference room is a spacious elegant room for business receptions, presentations, receptions and celebrations.
  • Meeting room - a compact visit in a classic style, suitable for organizing trainings, seminars, meetings and small presentations.
  • Banquet hall - a luxuriously decorated room for celebrations, corporate parties, receptions, cocktails, private events.
