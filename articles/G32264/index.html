"Nikolaev Posad" (Suzdal): reviews

To visit this hotel is equivalent to going to a fairy tale. After all, not only the hotel is attractive, but also its wonderful environment, thoroughly saturated with the spirit of past centuries. There are also some nuances in the work of the maintenance staff, for example, with the declared daily cleaning at some point this may not happen. But there are still more positive reviews: delicious cuisine, a bewitching interior and exterior, and stunning views of old Suzdal.
