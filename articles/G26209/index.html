Negative reviews

There are some negative aspects in such an institution as Garden of Eden Apart 4 * (Bulgaria). Reviews mention the following shortcomings of this hotel:

  • mediocre quality of cleaning in the room;
  • towels and bed linen are rarely changed, moreover, maids constantly have to remind about this;
  • the beach next to the hotel is very small and groomed;
  • the use of umbrellas and sun loungers is possible only at an additional cost;
  • in the bathroom there is no outlet for a hairdryer, and it is inconvenient to dry hair in the bedroom, since there is no mirror in it;
  • old mattresses with protruding springs;
  • in the kitchen corner there is an insufficient number of dishes and cutlery;
  • during bad weather, there are serious power outages;
  • paid cots.

This place can be considered an ideal family hotel with unobtrusive service. The hotel is perfect for those travelers who do not pay attention to minor flaws and love privacy. Location in a quiet area can be considered an additional advantage.
