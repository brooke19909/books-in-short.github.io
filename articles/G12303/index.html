Nikitsky Botanical Garden, Yalta

The Botanical Garden (Yalta) contributed to the process of development of production in the agricultural industry in the south of the state. The main tools for this were introduction, as well as acclimatization, breeding methods.

Value

From time to time, the human soul reaches out to nature, where you can easily breathe, feel freedom and enjoy all that surrounds us. Staying in the office, I so want to go to some beautiful place. Fortunately, experts in the field of botany took care of this and saved for visitors all the beautiful gifts of nature.





The Botanical Garden (Yalta) became the phenomenon due to which fruit plants, flowers bred for decorative purposes, as well as crops of technical and medicinal origin, each of which had its own special benefit, became widespread.

Actively studied and used the resources of the area. In order to enrich production, the people who established the Botanical Garden (Yalta) established good scientific and business relations with similar points in Europe, companies from America and Asia.

Thanks to this, it was possible to create a collection of unique species, forms and varieties of plants.

Primary tasks

The Botanical Garden (Yalta) is a pioneer in the economic sectors engaged in decorative gardening in the south, as well as fruit growing, grape growing, and the production of essential oils and tobacco.

Now this institution is ranked among the most famous places dedicated to botany in the world. Here is the largest collection of important plants. A large range of species and varieties of fruit crops is also stored. This is about 11 thousand species.

You can also find on its territory a decorative type of wood and grass plants, flowers and cultures of aromatic species. One of the most interesting places is the arboretum, which is a business card of Russian botany for tourists who come here from abroad and from the territory of the state. It is interesting to visit experienced plant growers and specialists.

A very attractive place not only for tourists, but also for local residents is the Nikitsky Botanical Garden. How to get from Yalta? A good option may be a transfer to a minibus near the bus station. In addition, you can come from the Central Market, which is located in close proximity to the promenade.





Roads here

The Botanical Garden (Yalta) is waiting for its visitors. How to get there and which vehicles? Minibuses No. 34, 29 (from Pionerskaya), the 2nd trolleybus are suitable. If you are coming from Simferopol or Alushta, you can get to the Nikitsky Botanical Garden stop, and then follow the usual route.

You can get to this institution year-round. It works seven days a week and is always glad to anyone who looks here. From November to April you can come from 9 to 16, and from May to October - from 9 to 20.

Beneficiaries

There are categories of tourists who can go to the territory for free. These are WWII veterans, children who are not yet 7 years old, as well as orphans and those who have been left without a guardian and live in an orphanage or boarding school. In addition, these are disabled people of 1-2 groups, Heroes of the Soviet Union and the Russian Federation, as well as those who are equated with the above.

Conscripts as well as museum staff can go free of charge. Also, no fee is charged if you are the leader of a children's group consisting of 10 minors 7-14 years old or 15 children aged 14-18 years. A guide, guide and translator can also go without paying anything.





Cognitive activities

Also in its wonderful museum invites the Botanical Garden (Yalta). The pass price is 25 rubles. for kids and 50 rubles. for everyone else. An excursion from the specialists of the institution costs 100 rubles. for an adult and 50 rubles for a child. You can go on an educational trip as part of a group of over 25 people, or individually.

If you like a personal approach, for such a walk you need to prepare 2000 rubles. In 1 hour 15 minutes you will learn a lot of new and exciting. Cape Maptyan can also be an interesting place, where you can participate in a group excursion for 300 rubles, for a child the cost is less - 150 rubles. Individual service will cost 2500 rubles.

Behavior rules

When entering the complex, you must adhere to special rules of conduct. Private transport should be left in a special parking lot outside the park. You can not walk on the lawns and flower beds. Staying on them is strictly prohibited.

Also, this place is not intended for cooking barbecue, picnics and other similar forms of recreation. You can not pick off cones, fruits, flowers, leaves and stems from trees. It is strictly forbidden to walk the dogs and spoil the plants, plantings.

Do not arbitrarily take pictures and shoot yourself on video. To do this, you need to get permission from the directorate. If these rules are violated, you will have to pay a fine. After you take the ticket, save the cashier's check. Do not throw it out until you leave the park. Security may ask you to provide this document.
