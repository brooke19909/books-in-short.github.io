Do-it-yourself Masha's costume from the cartoon "Masha and the Bear"

Who has children, he very often thinks about carnival outfits: what things are needed, buy them or do it yourself. Most often, the choice falls on the famous characters of cartoons and fairy tales. To date, many girls fell in love with the cute and mischievous Masha from the cartoon "Masha and the Bear." Therefore, we suggest you familiarize yourself with the full master class on how to make Masha‚Äôs costume with your own hands.

What are the details of the outfit?





To get the most accurate image, you need to use such things in its creation:

  • flared top-down sundress on a yoke of pink or lilac shades;
  • a scarf of the same color;
  • golfiki, white embroidered shirt or turtleneck;
  • white tights;
  • slippers.

If you want to achieve complete similarity, and your child‚Äôs hair is not blond (blond, blond), then you can also wear a wig.

By and large, all things can be found separately and put together in one image. But there is no guarantee that, for example, the kerchief will fit the sundress in tone. Therefore, it is still better to sew a suit yourself. Moreover, special sewing skills are not required here.

Expendable materials





To create a costume with your own hands you need the following:

  • pink fabric for sundress and scarf (lilac is also suitable);
  • threads of the same shade as the fabric;
  • one secret zipper to match the color of the fabric;
  • 1.5-2 meters of decorative ribbon with embroidery;
  • scissors;
  • needle;
  • ruler;
  • overlock;
  • pins
  • sewing machine.

We are building a sundress pattern

Children are not adults. A sundress sewn according to the standard pattern may not work - it will be small or large, short or long. Therefore, it is better to build your drawing, based on the specific parameters of your child.





First of all, take the following measurements: chest circumference, product length, yoke height.

Suppose you have the following values: exhaust gas - 64 cm, CI - 82 cm, VK - 12 cm.

Below you will find a master class for building a sundress pattern to sew Masha‚Äôs costume with your own hands:

  1. Take paper, pencil and ruler.
  2. Draw a line on paper. Its length is half the circumference of the chest.
  3. On the sides of the line measure up four centimeters.
  4. Inside, draw another four centimeters from the side lines.
  5. Measure up about fourteen centimeters and draw the straps of the sundress. To do this, swipe in about five centimeters on each side.
  6. Build a cutout as in illustration 1.
  7. Now you need to build a sundress skirt. To do this, measure the remaining length of the product down from the yoke. That is, if you have a length of 82 centimeters, then draw a line of 64 centimeters.
  8. Make a domed extension of the skirt on the sides. To do this, step back from the bottom to the right and left by four centimeters and connect these points to the bottom of the coquette (Figure 2).
  9. Divide the skirt with dashed lines into six identical parts (Figure 3).
  10. Now move each sector apart a few centimeters apart (Figure 4).
  11. Round off the bottom of the skirt sundress.
  12. The pattern is ready (Figure 5).

We sew a sundress Mashenka

The instruction on how to sew a suit for Masha from the cartoon "Masha and the Bear" on the pattern is quite simple.





  1. Cut out the details from the previously built pattern - the yoke and the bottom of the sarafan.
  2. Fold the fabric in two layers and cut two parts of the bottom of the sundress and four parts of the yoke. Do not forget to indent one or two centimeters on the sides.
  3. Sew the yoke parts together (Figure 1).
  4. Where the coquette should be semicircular (in the armholes and neckline), make small incisions.
  5. Sew together the two parts of the sundress skirt (Figure 2). On one side, leave a small piece so that you can sew in a hidden zipper.
  6. Stitch together the bottom and top of the suit, treating the seams with an overlock so that they do not crumble (Figure 3).
  7. Insert a zipper into the left piece of the skirt (Figure 4).
  8. Sew the bottom of the sarafan with embroidered ribbon (Figure 5).
  9. Iron or steam the sundress (Figure 6).

The Masha and the Bear costume is ready! Now you need to make a scarf.

We make a scarf Masha

A kerchief is made easiest. First, build a pattern. To do this, draw a triangle on paper. Its longest side should reach about ninety centimeters, and a height of forty-five centimeters.





Now attach the pattern to the same as the sundress, fabrics, and cut the part, indenting one or two centimeters. Work all three sides of the triangle with an overlock, tuck them and sew.

Stroke the scarf so that it is even. All is ready!

New Year's Masha from the cartoon

Masha from the famous cartoon had more than one outfit. In the New Year series, she showed off in front of the audience in a blue outfit with a kokoshnik. In it, she looked like a Snow Maiden. If you also want to create such an outfit for your daughter or niece, then use the given master class.





Instructions on how to make a New Year's costume for Masha from the movie "Masha and the Bear":

  1. Build a sundress pattern, as described above.
  2. Sew a sundress on the pattern, only you need no longer pink fabric, but blue or blue. It will be even more beautiful if it contains various patterns of the same palette.
  3. Cut out the shape of a kokoshnik from very thick cardboard.
  4. Attach the cardboard kokoshnik to the fabric and cut two pieces out of it. Do not forget to indent: one or two centimeters is sufficient on the sides, and make a margin of five to seven centimeters from the bottom.
  5. Sew together the two parts of the kokoshnik on the sides.
  6. Turn out the part and insert the cardboard blank inside.
  7. Take the bezel and sew the kokoshnik to it. To do this, indent the bottom and top of the hoop.
  8. If desired, the kokoshnik can be decorated with pompons, bought at the store or made with your own hands.
  9. If you used fabric without patterns, then take white acrylic paint or a special one for the fabric, and draw patterns and curls yourself.

Mashenka's New Year's outfit is ready!

Other attributes of Mashenka‚Äôs carnival outfit





Under the finished sarafan, you need to wear a white knee-high. An ideal option would be a snow-white embroidery, like Masha's. But if this cannot be found, then a golfik or t-shirt can be found in the wardrobe of every girl.

Tips and tricks for tailoring Masha‚Äôs costume from Masha and the Bear





  1. A sundress can be decorated not with a ribbon with embroidery, but with braid. Then it would be better to let her go at the junction of the coquette and the skirt, as well as at the bottom of the sundress, retreating from the edge by five to eight centimeters.
  2. Fabric for sundress and scarf is better to choose knitted or satin. Gabardine is also suitable. It is quite cheap, but not bad in quality - it does not crease and bright coloring.
  3. If you do not have an overlock, then process all the seams on the sarafan by hand. And on the scarf, turn each side two times (take this into account on the pattern, indent not by one or two, but by three or four centimeters).
  4. If the headscarf turned out to be large, then it can be easily reduced. To do this, make a few more turns and hem them.
  5. If you took the fabric with a margin, then you can make the dress more flared. To do this, simply enlarge the pattern.
  6. If you do not want to build a yoke, you can simplify the sewing process. To do this, build a rectangle. In length, it should, like the coquette, be equal to half the circumference of the girl‚Äôs chest, and in width should be about twelve centimeters. Cut out the details and sew them together. Then stitch the rectangles to the skirt of the sundress, as was done with the yoke. Then just sew wide ribbons instead of strapless.
  7. Sasha‚Äôs sundress can be changed from any other dress. If only the color was suitable.
  8. If you want to stand out, make a sarafan and a scarf of green color in white patterns. Masha was in this in one of the series of the same cartoon.
