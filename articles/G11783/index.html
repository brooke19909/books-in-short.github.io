Ice safety

Ice skating is the most dangerous and traumatic. If your child is skating for the first time, be sure to tell him what safety rules to follow.

  • Firstly, there is no need to accelerate much. Loss of control leads to the fact that the child will lose balance and fall. And this is dangerous, since no one teaches the correct fall of children. You can accidentally hit your head or break yourself something.
  • Secondly, you can not cling to a number of standing or traveling people. They can fall and strike with their skates anywhere in the body, as they do not control themselves during the fall.
  • Thirdly, you need to ride only in the general direction, in no case not crossing the road traveling.

How to choose the right equipment

To avoid unnecessary trouble, you need to pay attention to several details when buying skates. Buy skates with leather boots only. In plastic boots, legs swell and freeze faster. Be sure to check the stiffness of the sole and boot area around the ankle. In no case should they have defects or bend.

Do not buy larger skates. In such skates, the leg is in a free position, it can slip at the most uncomfortable moment and cause injury. Do not forget that skates are recommended to be worn only on woolen socks.
