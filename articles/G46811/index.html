Sudala Beach Resort 3 * (Thailand / Khao Lak): address, description, infrastructure, photo of rooms, reviews

Thailand, being a popular tourist destination, offers potential travelers a lot of interesting leisure options.

But before you think about your leisure, you need to determine the place of temporary residence. There are many different hotels here, but now we will focus on only one. And this is Sudala Beach Resort 3 *, located in the resort town of Khao Lak.

Location

The advantage of this hotel is that it is literally at arm's length from the sea. In walking distance is the famous Bang Niang Beach, which is the best of those that are available in the vastness of the resort of Khao Lak.

In addition, the place where the Sudala Beach Resort is located is developed in terms of infrastructure. Restaurants, cafes and the local market are within walking distance. The airport, however, is far away - 60 kilometers from the hotel. But the rest will not be disturbed by the noise of planes taking off and landing.

The address is as follows: Bang Niang Beach, 56/4 Moo 5, index 82190.

  • Free Wi-Fi in the business center.

  • Transfer services.
  • Luggage storage.
  • Reception open 24 hours a day.
  • Currency exchange point.
  • Laundry.
  • Item with fax and photocopy.
  • Room and room service.

Nutrition

On the territory of Sudala Beach Resort 3 * (Khao Lak) there are two establishments:

  • The Sunny Restaurant serves international and local cuisine.
  • Bar Enjoy.

People who rested at this hotel spoke warmly about the organization of breakfasts. They are tasty, varied and fresh, nothing is boring even for a long vacation. Local Thai food is especially good - gourmets and lovers of exotic food will surely be satisfied.

But tourists recommend taking breakfast here only. In the vicinity there are many different restaurants and cafes with an impressive range of food and low prices. If you want to take away as many gastronomic experiences as possible from Thailand, then you should go to new places for lunch and dinner. And be sure to try fresh seafood that is fried in local markets.

Apartments

Sudala Beach Resort offers guests three categories of rooms:

  • Superior 2-bed apartment. Area 47 sq. M. m
  • Villa deluxe. Area 45 sq. M. m
  • Grand deluxe villa. Area 53 sq. M. m

Guests are offered a choice of sleeping options: there are rooms with one large 2-double bed, and with two twin beds.

Whatever apartments the guest chooses, he will be satisfied. Inside, there is absolutely everything that is necessary for a comfortable stay: coffee maker, safe, electric kettle, air conditioning, TV, seating area, work desk, wardrobe, bathroom with bathtub, shower and toilet. Slippers, bathrobes, and hygiene items are also provided.

Even a wake-up service is offered. Each room also has its own spacious balcony.

When and who better to come?

It is important to give an answer to this question. In Thailand, as everyone knows, the holiday season does not stop almost never. The mild climate reigns throughout the year. If we talk about Khao Lak, then the temperature of air and water is the same both in high and in low season - approximately +28 ... 31 ¬∞ C and +28 ¬∞ C, respectively.

It is best to plan a vacation in winter. December, January, February are considered the most successful time to relax in the resort of Khao Lak. At this time there are not very many tourists, and there is practically no rain.

Who will like it here? People who dream of a secluded vacation. This place is not for lovers of active pastime and not for those who crave shopping.

By the way, people who want to go on excursions are also worth coming here. This resort is a convenient starting point for the Similan Islands, Tachai and Surin. Just remember that they are closed to the public during the storm and rainy season. As a rule, this period lasts from mid-May to the end of October.

Khao Lak is a calm, quiet and Europeanized resort. There really are a lot of guests from Europe, especially Germans and Scandinavians. There are few tourists from Russia, and some consider this a serious plus (those who wish to take a break from the Russian-speaking situation).

Terms of Accommodation

People who decided to plan their trip on their own, rather than buying a tour, should get acquainted with them. The conditions are as follows:

  • Check-in starts at 14:00.
  • Check out lasts until 12:00.
  • The hotel does not accept guests with children and animals.
  • Extra beds are not available in the apartment.
  • When booking, you must provide your credit card details. The object accepts "Master Card" and "Visa".
  • A certain amount may be blocked in the account before the guest arrives. This is a guarantee that the guest really intends to stay at the hotel.

By the way, there is another nuance regarding Wi-Fi. In the rooms it is provided, but at an additional cost. It costs 350 baht per day (approximately 712 rubles at the current rate).
