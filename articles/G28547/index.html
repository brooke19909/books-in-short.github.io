Tricolor TV: setup, installation and proper use

Today Tricolor TV satellite digital television has become very popular. To save on the total cost of Tricolor TV, the setup and installation can be performed independently.



In order to start installing, assembling and tuning this antenna, you need to have at hand: wrench, drill, pencil, Phillips screwdriver, compass and protractor. First, screw the support onto the transponder bracket. Pull the cable from the antenna into the house and connect it to the LNB IN jack. Then, according to the instructions, prepare the antenna cable. Connect the satellite receiver and cable to the TV.

Now proceed to orient the antenna by pointing the plate at the satellite. As soon as you turn on the receiver, it will be in setup mode. In Tricolor TV, the setting starts from the moment when two bars appear at the bottom of the screen. In satellite TV Tricolor TV, the channel setting will depend on the quality and strength of the signals.

Now you should focus on the azimuth and calculated angles, which are indicated in the instructions and apply to your city, they are available in the table. You can orient the satellite and tilt the dishes on the satellite dish of a neighbor. Using a compass, you should measure the azimuth or remember the degree of direction of your satellite dish in this azimuth.

The correct Tricolor TV setting will give a green strip in the receiver. If this strip is not, then put on the TV an assistant who will adjust your actions, or install a mirror in which you can look at it by adjusting the antenna. It is enough if the signal is within 70%.

The exact Tricolor TV setup will begin as soon as the receiver is turned on. At this point, you will be prompted to download the update of the Tricolor TV channel list. After that, you need to register your receiver by entering your passport details, home address, receiver serial number and secret code from the card.

It is better to do it on a special site Tricolor TV. Write all data without abbreviations. As soon as registration passes, you will be informed about the contract number, and you can activate the card.
