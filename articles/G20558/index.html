Vladimir hotels in the city center: overview, description

Every year, many tourists visit the capital of ancient Russia, the city of Vladimir. Here they can admire the wonderful white-stone monuments, visit interesting museums. And so that you can walk to all the main attractions, Vladimir hotels are at your service in the city center.

Hotel Vladimir

You can walk to the railway station and bus station from the hotel in eight minutes, the distance to the Theotokos Monastery is only two hundred meters, most of the city sights are located within walking distance from here. This option is perfect for tourists who are considering Vladimir hotels in the city center. The hotel's own restaurant serves Russian and European cuisine. Parking and Wi-Fi are free. There are parking spaces for people with disabilities. An elevator is available to access the upper floors. The hotel has an ATM, a tour desk and a library. Laundry, shoe shine and ironing services are available at an extra charge. Pets are allowed at the hotel by prior arrangement. The following rooms are offered for accommodation in the Vladimir Hotel:

  • single economy - 3200 rubles per day;
  • single standard - 3500 rubles per day;
  • double economy - 4000 rubles per day for two people and 3400 rubles if one person lives;
  • double standard - 4700 rubles and 4000 rubles per day, respectively;
  • double superior - 5000 rubles per day for two people and 4300 rubles if one person lives;
  • double business class - 5600 rubles and 4800 rubles per day, respectively;
  • triple economy - 4650 rubles, 4150 and 3650 rubles per day, depending on the number of residents;
  • Suite - 6000 rubles per day for two people and 5200 rubles if one person lives.

The cost of all rooms is inclusive of breakfast, it is possible to stay without meals. Hotel Vladimir is located at 74 Bolshaya Moskovskaya St.

An airport shuttle is available at an extra charge. There is no restaurant in the hotel, but there are several cafes and a supermarket nearby. An elevator is available to access the upper floors. Private parking and Wi-Fi are free. The following room categories are offered to accommodate guests:
