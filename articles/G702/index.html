After many years of torment, Prometheus freed by Hercules descended to the sea and washed his body, covered with wounds, in the waters and was healed.

Big Utrish Attractions: Dolphinarium

It is difficult to imagine a child who refuses to visit the attraction arranged by the marine inhabitants. According to experts, he is recognized as one of the best in Russia. Its opening took place in 1992, and since then its inhabitants annually delight visitors, especially the younger ones, with their performances.

The uniqueness of this dolphinarium lies in the fact that enclosures with inhabitants were placed in a lagoon with running sea water and without a roof over their heads, thereby bringing the maximum living conditions to the natural environment. And therefore, the dolphins contained here do not feel discomfort, feeling in their environment, which determines the ease of performing the most difficult tricks.

In addition to the dolphins of the Black Sea region, kids will be pleased with their programs fur seal from cold latitudes, sea lion from the same regions, as well as beluga whale. And after the attraction, the children can perpetuate the memory of the performance of the pictures with the artists they like.
