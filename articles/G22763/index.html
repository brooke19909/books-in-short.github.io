DIY greeting cards: workshop, interesting ideas and recommendations

Greeting cards, glued and painted with their own hands , are valued much higher than purchased options. After all, you not only create unique things, but also put your soul into the work. We suggest you learn how to make a greeting card with your own hands on various topics.





Teacher card

The greeting card ‚ÄúHappy Teacher‚Äôs Day‚Äù, made in the Kraft style with your own hands, will turn out to be quite interesting. It involves the use of several separate pictures, letters and decorative elements, which are assembled into one whole. You will need to take A4-size cardboard for the base of the postcard, a picture for the background of A5 format and a few images with subjects related to the school. For example, it can be letters of the alphabet, a backpack, an abstract sheet, a ruler, a compass, and so on.

First, make a base, that is, just bend A4 cardboard in half. Paint it if necessary. Then stick a picture with the background on the front side. It will be beautiful if its format is slightly smaller (A5). Then contour the prepared images of school subjects. You can also print a small rectangle with the words "Congratulations" or "Happy Holidays." Place the pictures on the background as they should look in the final version. Glue them. You can also decorate the card with ribbons or various decorative elements. Greeting card "Happy Teacher's Day" made by hand. It remains only to sign it.





Congratulations to the kindergarten teacher

It will be very pleasant for the teacher of preschool children to receive a congratulatory card made by their pupils. As a basis, you can borrow the idea of ‚Äã‚Äãfinger painting. First prepare the base of the postcard. To do this, take cardboard or kraft paper, fold in half. Then part paint and cover its sponges. Make a few shades - so the picture will be brighter.

Now let each child dip his finger in the sponge and put an imprint on the front of the card. You can combine them by drawing wavy lines below , which depart from the lower points. Thus, you get a bunch of balls. On the side of the plastic, you can write "Congratulations!" Thus, the greeting card for the day of the educator, created with your own hands, is ready!

The presented personal greeting card to the educator will bring a lot of positive emotions. With your own hands you can make it from the most ordinary colored paper. And of the additional things you need only scissors and a handle. Fold the paper in half and place the child‚Äôs pen on it so that its thumb and forefinger touch the fold line. Circle it and gently cut the card. As a result, you will get a cute postcard, which in its shape resembles a baby‚Äôs palm. Upon disclosure, they already become two. It remains only to fill out a card and present it to the teacher.





Simple postcard

To get such a card, you need to take cardboard for paper, thin double-sided tape, several varieties of kraft paper or ordinary color pictures with a picture, as well as sheets of yellow. First prepare the base of the greeting card by folding the sheet in half. Then cut several strips of different paper. Try to make them the same in width but different in length. Now cut out shapes resembling drops from yellow sheets. There should be as many as strips.

When all the details are ready, you can proceed to the design of the front side. To do this, lay the strips on the card. It will turn out prettier if they are not parallel. Spread them slightly obliquely. Then take one strip and glue a piece of double-sided tape to its back side. Replace the rectangle. Similarly, glue all the strips on the tape. Finally, attach the drops over the paper candles.

Such a greeting card at school with your own hands is done very quickly. Therefore, it is ideal for a lesson or classroom theme. As a result, each student will have his own greeting card, which he can then give to someone for his birthday.





New Year paper greetings

DIY greeting cards for the New Year are made even easier. For example, to create such a card, you need red cardboard or kraft paper of the same color. Take also a narrow and wide ribbon of beige or gold shade, the same tone of a drop-bead or rhinestone.

Fold the paper in half. Take a special gun or Moment glue, attach a small piece of a narrow ribbon on top. It should be centered. Outline a circle under it with a flick of a pencil. Lay out bead drops inside it so that most of the space is filled. Then glue all the elements. It is most convenient to use tweezers. It remains to make a bow from a wide ribbon and stick it over the ball. Sign the card.

Volumetric card with balls

You can make very beautiful greeting cards with your own hands using thick double-sided tape. Also, for example, to create a card with balloons, you will need several types of kraft paper or cardboard with drawings, a small piece of a thin ribbon and beautiful threads (floss, woolen, decorative twine and so on).

First, make a blank for the card, folding the cardboard in half. Then take decorative paper and draw circles, hearts or ovals on the back. These are the shapes of our future balloons. Then take the scissors and carefully cut the shapes along the contour. Now cut the threads. You need as many balls as possible. Connect the thread to the cardboard figure using double-sided tape. Lay out the balls on the front of the card, and then glue them.

Tie all the ends into one bundle using a thin ribbon. Glue its back to the card. Trim the ends of the threads. Volumetric postcard is ready! By this principle, you can make many different greeting cards. Just prepare the elements and stick them on a thick double-sided tape.





Blank scrapbooking postcards

You can make original greeting cards from paper with your own hands in the style of scrapbooking. To do this, you need to take several types of beautiful cardboard, a thin ribbon and PVA glue. To get started, prepare the foundation. It can be either cardboard folded in half, or just a square or rectangular sheet. Then take the red paper and cut out exactly the same shape as the card itself, only a centimeter less on each side. Cut another exact same slice, only white or beige. In addition, it should be less by a few millimeters of the previous one. Stick on the front side of the card, first a red sheet, and then a light one.

New Year's decor of a card

Now cut three rectangles from different paper. They should differ in length by 1 centimeter. Glue a medium-sized rectangle horizontally in the middle. Cut a piece of tape and glue one end to the back of a long rectangle. Put the figure under the center piece and also stick it. Lift the tape up. Glue the last rectangle on top, having previously wrapped the second piece of ribbon under it. Make a bow from the remnants of the ribbon and stick it on top of our gifts.

Under the picture, make the inscription "Happy New Year!", "Happy Holidays!" or "Congratulations!" It is noteworthy that depending on what color paper you will use, the content of the postcard also changes. If you take a cardboard of neutral colors with a pattern of hearts or circles, then the card can be presented for your birthday.





Minimalism greeting card

For such a card you need monophonic beautiful cardboard of dark color. As well as white thick paper and several types of colored tape. From the cardboard of dark color, make the base of the greeting card. Cut a few strips of decorative adhesive tape and simply stick it on the front of the card. The strips should go horizontally, you can slightly diagonally.

Take a white cardboard and with a pencil write beautifully and accurately in two lines ‚ÄúHappy Birthday!‚Äù or in one "Congratulations!" Letters must be bold. Circle the inscription in a frame, and let it connect in some places with words. Now take a sharp clerical knife and cut everything along the contour. Using PVA glue, stick our blank to tape. All. A minimalism greeting card is ready. For all its simplicity, it turns out to be bright and stylish.





More minimalistic card design options.

Do it yourself beautiful greeting cards made with your own hands: "Happy Birthday" or "Happy New Year." Especially if you use colored tape in the process. In this case, no other materials and tools need to be used. Just stick tape on the front of the card in a different order or make different shapes out of it. It can be candles, a gift, a cake and so on. At the end of the postcard, you simply stick on top of the adhesive tape a congratulatory inscription cut out of white cardboard. Thus, you will get several options for simple, but creative and stylish greeting cards.
