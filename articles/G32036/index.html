What it borders on

The state borders with Andorra and France in the north, with Portugal in the west. The country is washed by many waters. In the east it is the Mediterranean Sea, in the north - the Bay of Biscay, and in the west - the Atlantic Ocean.

The Alhambra

When answering the question about what continent Spain is located on, whose sights are represented in great abundance, it should be remembered that it includes the island part. Among all the interesting places of the state, the Alhambra should be highlighted.

It is an impressive complex consisting of a fortress, amazing palaces and wonderful gardens. It was erected back in the distant 14th century. The initiator was the ruler of Granada. Now this complex is the property of UNESCO.

The Alhambra is an amazing territory that basks in the sun. There are carved courtyards, intricate fountains. In the gardens you can hear the nightingale singing and rustling leaves.

Many experts call this place an extraordinary example of Muslim art. Architecture harmoniously combines many styles.
