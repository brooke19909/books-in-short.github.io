Meizu M3 Mini: specifications, comparison with analogs and reviews

Anyone understands that a device from a budget segment should not look and work like a cheap device. In any case, the manufacturer Meizu adheres to just such a rule. This statement confirms the M3 mini. The phone is simple, runs on the YunOS shell. On it you can install applications that are created under the "Android". During operation makes a good impression. The article will present a comparison with the characteristics of the Meizu M3 S Mini and reviews about the device.



Specifications

The phone runs on an eight-core MediaTek processor. Its frequency is 1 / 1.5 GHz. 64-bit system. Graphics Mali-T720 accelerates to 600 MHz. RAM was 2/3 GB, and internal storage - 16/32 GB. There is support for memory cards. Their maximum capacity is 128 GB.

IPS type display. The diagonal is 5 inches. You can install a protective glass. The front camera received a matrix with a resolution of 5 megapixels, the main - 13 megapixels. This is confirmed by the characteristics and reviews of the Meizu M3 Mini.

The battery has a capacity of 2870 mAh. Built-in headphone jack and charger / sync with computer. There is a compass, several sensors, a gyroscope and an accelerometer. There is no fingerprint scanner . YunOS operating system. It supports wireless internet and bluetooth. 2G, 3G and 4G signals are recognized. There are two ports for SIM cards such as nano and one micro for an external drive. Built-in navigator. The device weighs 132 g. The housing is made of plastic.



Equipment

The box in which the phone is delivered will impress any person. It is small in size and visually resembles a box from the iPhone version 4 or 4S. Inside, the buyer will find a charger, a cable for connecting to a computer, as well as a special SIM card extractor. It has a Flyme logo.

Most often, the device is sold without a protective glass, film and case. As an optional accessory, there is an adapter for an outlet.

Performance

We continue the review of the characteristics of the Meizu M3 Mini with a description of the interface. Users note that it is convenient and trouble-free. The software does not make it clear during operation that the phone is a budget type. The interface switches between applications as smoothly as possible, without lags and brakes. Even with a full load of the device, there are no claims to work. The only thing that buyers note is that launching utilities may take three to four seconds, but this does not cause any negative impressions. Using the phone is fun.

If we talk about the characteristics of the Meizu M3 Mini smartphone in games, then it is also at a good level. Consider the example of the game in the famous "Asphalt 8". Medium and high graphics settings are not available for the owner of this phone. You can choose either low or even lower. There is no particular comfort when playing, but there are no freezes and crashes. However, smoothness at a terrible level.

Unkilled game allows you to play at high settings. Perhaps with the latest updates it just became less demanding, but the picture quality is excellent. However, there is one thing - the FPS is weak. Therefore, playing on such settings will be very difficult. The owners note that you can comfortably spend time on average.

The World of Tanks Blitz game produces at least 50 frames per second, so there are no problems.

Front-camera

The front camera according to the characteristics of the Meizu M3 Mini received a 5 megapixel sensor. The lens has an excellent fast aperture. However, there is nothing to highlight the camera. Suitable for video calls, as well as selfies in good light.



Main camera

At the back there is a 13 megapixel camera module. This phone has similar characteristics to the Meizu M3 S mini. Also, according to customers, the same sensor is installed as in the second generation model. The quality of the picture that is obtained is good. The frames of the architecture are not very impressive, but also not bad. Pleased with the availability of the HDR option. It allows you to make the photo more saturated and beautiful.

At night and in low light, the quality of the picture decreases, but the owners note that with the ability to shoot, you can make a successful shot in three to five attempts.

Given that the phone is budget, the cameras are normal. In the general category, they are not very encouraging.

Software shell

The question about the software characteristics of the Meizu M3 Mini 32Gb is quite relevant, so it should also be highlighted. This indicator is directly related to cost. It should be noted that the device fell into the budget segment by accident - it can hardly be attributed to this category.

However, localization is absent. To solve this problem, you must install the Russified firmware. This can be done by the seller, if the owner feels some kind of difficulty.

For the first entrance to the Google Market, it is recommended to use only the mobile Internet. Then you can use Wi-Fi.

If you need to download the application using a mobile network, you should go to the "Downloads" menu. There will be a special setting that will allow you to do this.

On top of the operating system is FlymeOS, which has distinguished itself by its stable operation and good optimization performance. In recent updates, the interface has been improved. Now it is more understandable for every person.



Sound quality

This phone will come in handy and appeal to all those who like to listen to music on their smartphone. Playback has excellent characteristics. The Meizu M3 Mini 32Gb sounds a lot more interesting than most expensive solutions on the market.

A special player is built in, which has many settings. Tracks can be sorted into categories. There is also an equalizer for five bands.

The interface is dark if there is no cover for the track, so not everyone likes it. If you hold the phone in direct sunlight, then in this mode it will be difficult to make out anything on the screen.

Battery life

The Meizu M3 Mini is not impressive in its battery life. According to reviews, if you use it actively, it will not last even until the evening. In the average operating mode, it can work until the middle of the night, but this indicator is maximum.

Standby time consumes up to 20%. Some users believe that this is due to the installed "Google" services, others blame the wireless signal. Advanced users have noted that this is clearly not the problem. Even when the power-saving mode is on and the checkmark is activated, which turns off the connection when the screen is locked, still a fifth of the charge disappears during the night.

The device must be charged for almost the entire night, then in the morning you can have 100%.

Reviews

As it should have become clear, the Meizu M3 Mini, whose characteristics are good, is popular among buyers.

Many praise the state of the firmware, its functionality and speed of work. Applications switch seamlessly. In games, graphics quality may not please, especially in heavy utilities. However, most popular processes run perfectly.

The above is a comparison with analogues produced by the same manufacturer. Which option to prefer should be understood by each person independently. Characteristics do not differ critically, but in some places one generation is inferior to another. Owners recommend taking either this plastic version, or the next, improved, metal one.

Total

The manufacturer, when creating this phone, made sure that its low price did not display much on functionality and materials. Even with a low cost, the device can do many things that are available only in flagships. The difference is that the phone has an easy and understandable interface.

Many may have a question about whether to purchase this device. Of course, especially if you want to give a small amount, and get a successful adaptation in return.

Choosing between plastic M3 and M3S, you should prefer the second. It costs $ 12 more, but a fingerprint scanner and an improved battery become available to the user. The average cost of the described model is up to $ 100 for a 16-GB version and 130 bucks for 32-GB.
