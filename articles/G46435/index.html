Atlant washing machine: customer reviews

Choosing a washing machine is a crucial step. It is necessary to choose exactly the machine that really suits a specific user. There are no problems with the assortment. There are a wide variety of models on the market. But what about the price? Not all of them are available. However, there is a manufacturer that produces budget models. We are talking about the Atlant plant, which is located in Belarus. The Atlant automatic washing machines are of high quality, good functionality and affordable price. Do not believe? In this review we will look at the most popular models and see what those who have already purchased such a machine say.

ATLANT 60C88

Let's start with the simplest model. This Atlant washing machine, reviews of which we will discuss a little later, has good technical characteristics and a rich set of functions. For example, the car has a rather powerful engine, which is capable of speeds up to 800 rpm during the spin cycle. The car also has an A + energy class. This means that it is very economical. You can load up to 6 kilograms of laundry into this washing machine. There are various washing modes. Among them - washing delicate fabrics, sportswear, jeans and so on. In addition, there is a very useful option to prevent creasing. There is also an option to control the level of foam and control imbalance. There is also protection against leaks. Although partial. In fact, this is an excellent budget product that everyone can afford.

ATLANT 60C88 user reviews

However, let's see what those who have already purchased the Atlant washing machine say. Owner reviews are not unambiguous. On the one hand, users note the build quality, rich feature set and high quality wash. But on the other hand, they point out minor flaws. For example, after washing, water accumulates under the rubber pad of the drum and in the powder tray. This, of course, is a miscalculation of designers.

The rest of the owners are happy with the car. They especially note the anti-crease option. It allows you not to bother long with ironing after washing, which significantly saves time. But most of all people were pleased with the price of this car. Even pensioners will be able to buy it without problems. And some did not at all believe that such equipment was assembled in Belarus. However, this is a fact.
