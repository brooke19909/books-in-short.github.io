Pudozh City: Hotels and Guest Houses

A small town in Karelia - Pudozh - is located on the right, high, bank of the Vodla River. Guests to this city are attracted by the beautiful nature and wonderful fishing, because only two dozen kilometers away is the pearl of Karelia - Lake Onega.

International route for tourists "Blue Road"

The city of Pudozh is the place where the Blue Road ends, a popular and sought-after tourist route connecting several countries. Its length is more than two thousand kilometers, the route originates from the Norwegian Atlantic, passes through Sweden and Finland. In Russia, travelers enjoy the beauty of the south and the middle of Karelia.



Throughout the route, participants in tourist trips admire beautiful landscapes, the amazing beauty of northern lakes and rivers, forests, get acquainted with cultural objects, traditions and crafts of local residents, historical sights of many places. For tourists who complete an exciting journey, a comfortable stay is needed. Therefore, in a town called Pudozh, hotels offer coziness, care and silence for guests. Here, every traveler will find a place where you can relax from the hustle and bustle of the road.

Pudozh City: Hotels

Pudozh is a small district center, its population is only about nine thousand people. The beauty of the unique surrounding nature, the location on the federal highway that connects its final points - Vologda and Medvezhyegorsk, explains that the city of Pudozh, hotels and guest houses of this place, receive their guests most often for the night, providing them with rest on the way. Only an insignificant part of tourists stay here for several days; hotel guests use the services of guests who are here on business trips.

Motel "Comfort" in Pudozh

At the entrance to the city from the side of Medvezhyegorsk, in a quiet place, there is a small building of the Uyut Motel. It has nine rooms. Guests are offered their various categories - from economy class to suites. Regardless of the class of rooms chosen, each guest is warmly welcomed at this hotel, upon check-in they offer accommodation options that are optimal for each of them. The hotel rooms are furnished, it is comfortable and provides all the needs of guests. Guests who choose economy class use a bathroom and shower on the floor, other rooms have all the amenities. It has free Wi-Fi. Laundry facilities are also available.



Motor guests enjoy free video surveillance parking. A visit to the sauna and pool will help relieve fatigue and stress; you can use the massage chair. Children will have fun in the equipped children's corner. Motel "Cosiness" has its own cafe, it offers breakfast, lunch and dinner, the menu has a lot of dishes of Karelian cuisine and pies.

Hotel Onego

Many positive reviews from guests deserve the new Onego Hotel in the city called Pudozh. His hotels often offer unique services - pets can be accommodated in this place. On-site parking is available.



The hotel has 31 rooms. This is a double and triple apartment, accommodation in a deluxe room is designed for four guests. Family rooms are available. Beds in the hotel are equipped with double or single beds. Each room has a bathroom with shower or bath.

The room has free Wi-Fi, a TV. At the request of guests, an electric kettle and a hairdryer will be delivered to the room, and laundry and ironing services will be organized. Since the city of Pudozh has low quality tap water, there are coolers with high-quality drinking water on each floor of the hotel. The cafe of this establishment offers delicious dishes, which can be ordered in the room. Guests are satisfied with the level of local service, many of them become regular customers of the hotel.



Guest houses of the city

Homely atmosphere and coziness, comfortable conditions and caring for tourists are offered not only by Pudozh hotels. Karelia is a place that is often visited by large companies, so check-in at guest houses is demanded here, where numerous groups will be happy to meet. Affordable prices and good living conditions are often decisive for booking this type of overnight stay or holiday with tourists visiting the city of Pudozh and its environs.
