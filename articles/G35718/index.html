Coma Ruga

Now let's see what they say about the resort area, in which the Natura Park 4 * hotel is located. Koma Ruga is a former fishing village that suddenly turned into a resort in the century before last, when healing waters were discovered on its territory. Now it is a suburb of the larger village of Wendrel. Here is one of the best beaches in the vicinity, and the sea in Coma Rug has the largest concentration of iodine on the entire coast. Therefore, thalassotherapy is very developed in these places. This resort has become the center of international sailing regattas. Therefore, if you are a lover of water entertainment of this kind, you will find a real paradise. Moreover, the Natura Park 4 * hotel (Coma Ruga) is located very close to the yacht port, and it is not difficult to hire an instructor or rent a boat. In the town itself there is a beautiful promenade with various shops and eateries in the Catalan style.

Rooms & Facilities

Hotel Natura Park 4 * is quite large. It has 220 rooms. They are mainly designed for two guests, but there are rooms for one person. There are very large beds, completely unusual for Spanish hotels. There are adjoining rooms suitable for a family with two children. All rooms have huge balconies with a table and chairs. Each living room is air-conditioned, but can also be heated, depending on the season. A bathrobe and slippers are provided for guests. There is a good bathroom where you can soak up. A deposit is taken for the safe, which is then returned, and they use the minibar, if paid extra. The air conditioner is powered by a key. Want to come to a cool room on a hot day? Then ask at the reception for the second key - it is issued free of charge. Tourists claim that Wi-Fi catches not only in public areas and in the lobby, but also directly in the rooms. The premises themselves are called very cozy and have an evening rest. Cleaned here every day, regularly change the shower gel and shampoo in the dispensers. Guests who come not for the first time to the Natura Park 4 * hotel (Spain) are advised to settle on the upper floors. Then you are guaranteed a panoramic view of both the sea and the town of Coma Ruga. Refrigerator, air conditioning and TV - everything works fine. There are two Russian channels. The soundproofing in the rooms is excellent, which is important for relaxation.

Sea and beach

Of course, it is precisely on vacation on the coast that tourists most often come to Natura Park 4 *. Spain, and especially Catalonia, was very fond of travelers from Russia. Here, practically the best beaches in Europe are very clean, with clear water and beautiful views. The golden sandy coast (which is why it is called "Dorada") is located near the hotel. Go there for about five minutes, and even then if slowly. To do this, cross two streets, one of which is the promenade. The beach, however, is municipal, as elsewhere in Spain, but equipped (with a shower for legs and a separate body, a toilet - everything is free), there are sunbeds and umbrellas. It is very large and wide, divided into two parts by the port of the yacht club. This, as tourists write, is not a minus, but a plus. Firstly, the very sight of these snow-white beauties causes a desire to be photographed nearby. Secondly, there are often waves at sea, and the port acts as a breakwater. Therefore, depending on the direction of the wind, you can go swimming to the right or left of the yacht club. Vacationers are always under the supervision of lifeguards, and all sorts of water activities such as catamarans, windsurfing are within easy reach. The entrance to the sea is smooth, good for children. In shallow water for them is a vast expanse. The sea is so clear that standing in it on the chest, you can see sand and shells. The hotel also has a swimming pool with a water slide - the children there really like it. If big waves or bad weather, there is the opportunity to swim. The pool is open from ten in the morning until eight in the evening.

Prices

Of course, you need to remember about the costs to those who came to Natura Park 4 *. Spain is one of the most inexpensive European countries, and therefore the cost of a hotel room is about 90 euros per day. However, discounts are available when traveling in groups or in low season. For example, with an early booking, a room for a family with two children costs 3,500 rubles per day. Since 2012, all Spanish hotels have taken a special tourist tax. It must be paid directly at the hotel, so be prepared for small but additional costs. Experienced tourists are advised to pay extra for meals for the entire period of rest in advance. It is much cheaper. One separate lunch with dinner per person will cost you eleven and a half euros. But if you pay for all the time, then the price decreases to eight. Do not forget that all drinks for lunch and dinner are paid. For example, a cup of coffee costs forty euro cents. Excursions to Barcelona or Tarragona are quite expensive, and start from thirty euros per person. A trip to Andorra will cost forty-five. However, tourists write that near the hotel, near the pharmacy, there is a travel agency with Russian-speaking staff, where prices are often half as much cheaper. A ride on a train along the promenade costs 1.80. For children to climb at different levels of difficulty on the rope tracks in the Adventure Parks in Coma Rug, you will have to pay from ten to twenty euros, depending on the attraction.
