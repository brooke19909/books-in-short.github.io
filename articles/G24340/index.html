Description and rating of real estate agencies in Moscow

It is a realtor who will help to conclude a contract that is correctly drawn up from a legal point of view, will provide housing options that meet the individual criteria of the buyer, or will enable the seller to sell his square meters in a short time, without reducing their declared value.

As in most areas, real estate services are also subject to various frauds, because of which people lose shelter, multi-million dollars or buy a house with existing residents.
