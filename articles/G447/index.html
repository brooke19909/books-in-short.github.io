Hotel Sunny Blue Hotel 2 * (Cyprus / Ayia Napa): description and reviews

Ayai-Napa - one of the most popular resort among Russian tourists in Cyprus. This city was once built on the site of an ancient Byzantine settlement. And vacationers are attracted here not only by the warm sea and the gentle sun, as at many other resorts in the world, but also by the opportunity to see a lot of interesting sights.

Removing the most suitable accommodation for yourself in Ayia Napa for domestic tourists will not be absolutely difficult. There are a lot of good hotels built here. For example, good reviews on the Web are available about the hotel Sunny Blue Hotel 2 *.





Features of the location of the complex

This hotel was built in one of the quietest streets of Ayia Napa. At the same time, the lively city center is just a 5-minute walk away. That is, the Sunny Blue Hotel 2 * hotel is located in terms of a pleasant stay in the rooms and the ability to have fun very well. For this, he deserved just excellent visitor reviews.

The airport from this hotel is quite far. Tourists who rent rooms at this hotel fly to Cyprus, usually to the city of Larnaca. It is here that the transport hub connected to the hotel is located. The distance from Larnaca to Ayia Napa and the hotel is approximately 35 km. That is, the journey by shuttle bus to this hotel usually takes about 30 minutes for tourists. This, of course, is not too much. This hotel is located almost on the outskirts of the city. Therefore, in other hotels, before disembarking its guests, the transfer bus does not call.

Sunny Blue Hotel 2 *: general hotel description

Takes tourists who come to rest in Cyprus, this hotel in one not too large multi-storey building of the original modern building. The hotel is relatively new. It was built in 2016. Unfortunately, this recreation center does not have its own yard. The hotel belongs to the type of standard city hotels.

The total area occupied by the complex is only 1100 m 2 . Even by Greek standards, this hotel is quite small.

The reception at this two-star hotel is open around the clock. That is, guests of this hotel can contact any administrators at any time. In some Greek hotels, owners allow guests to check in with pets for a fee. Unfortunately, the Sunny Blue Hotel 2 * hotel does not provide such a service. Of course, you must not smoke in the rooms of this hotel. Doing this is allowed only in specially designated places for this purpose.





For various additional services at the hotel you can pay not only in cash. They accept cards at this hotel.

Hotel rooms

In total, Sunny Blue Hotel 2 * (Cyprus, Ayia Napa) offers tourists who have chosen Ayia Napa to relax, 35 comfortable rooms. All hotel rooms belong to the most popular inexpensive tourist class standard. In addition to beds, bedside tables and wardrobes, the rooms for rent at the hotel for the convenience of guests include:

  • individual air conditioners;

  • TVs.

Photos of Sunny Blue Hotel 2 *, presented on this page and on the sites of tour operators, clearly demonstrate that the furniture, decoration and household appliances in them are completely new (at least for 2017).

On TV, residents of this hotel, among other things, can watch three Russian-language channels. You do not need to pay for the inclusion of air conditioning in the hotel rooms. This service is included in the price. For a fee, in the hotel rooms, guests can use:

  • mini-bar;

  • a safe;

  • a kettle.

Wi-Fi in the rooms of this hotel is free for tourists. This is the majority of guests, of course, attributed to the unconditional advantages of the hotel. The same goes for a set of toiletries, as well as a hairdryer in the shower. Residents can use them for free.

The rooms in the hotel Sunny Blue Hotel 2 * (Ayia Napa), as already mentioned, belong to the same class and are designed for 2 adult guests. The rooms of this complex may vary slightly only in size. In rooms of 25 m2 it is allowed to hook one person with the installation of a folding bed.





Hotel room reviews

The opinion on the rooms handed over at the Sunny Blue Hotel 2 * (Ayia Napa) hotel for tourists was very good. According to vacationers, in the rooms offered at the hotel for more or less comfortable living at the resort there is everything you need. Of course, the fact that you have to pay for using a minibar, a kettle and a safe here, most tourists attribute to the shortcomings of the hotel. But the cost of living in this complex is not too expensive.

The lack of refrigerators is, according to many guests, one of the main disadvantages of this hotel. Indeed, in the hot climate of Cyprus, products purchased in stores deteriorate very quickly. However, if desired, tourists staying at this hotel can leave food in the refrigerator at the reception. The girls working here do not usually refuse guests this.

Regarding the cleaning of the Sunny Blue Hotel 2 * hotel (Ayia Napa, Cyprus), its residents have a relatively good opinion. At least they come to the rooms in order to put things in order here, maids often. Towels and linen in this complex are changed once every 2-3 days. Moreover, guests do not need to remind the maids of the need for this procedure.

Judging by the reviews available on the Web, there have never been any cases of theft at this hotel. The hotel staff does not allow taking things for guests. But of course, everything happens in life, and to leave valuable objects in sight in the rooms in their absence is not worth it even in this safe hotel with regard to theft. It is better to take them with you, or lock them in a safe (in the room costs 15 euros).





Useful tips from experienced vacationers

The rooms at the Sunny Blue Hotel 2 * (Cyprus) can overlook the street or pool. In the latter case, the view from them opens, of course, quite beautiful. However, experienced tourists advise beginners to still take rooms with windows facing the street in this hotel. The fact is that the pool in this complex is usually noisy. This can stop tourists from resting.

The plugs from Russian technology to sockets in this hotel, unfortunately, do not resemble. But if you want to use, for example, a laptop, hotel guests can simply purchase adapters. You can buy such an accessory in the city. But it will be much more convenient to simply contact the reception. Here adapters are offered to guests for only 2 euros. This, of course, is not too expensive.

Infrastructure Reviews

In the ratings on the websites of tour operators, in the "Reviews" section, Sunny Blue Hotel 2 * (Cyprus) takes a rather high place. His former guests rate it very well. Thus, the hotel guests have to make a long path to the beach. However, Ayia Napa itself is small and not too confused. Remembering the location of its streets for tourists is not difficult. Of course, you can get to the sea and in a car rented at a hotel or city.

Most often, guests of the hotel Sunny Blue Hotel 2 * visit Limanaki beach in Ayai Napa. He earned good reviews from tourists, both in terms of cleanliness and ease of entry into the sea. If desired, on the beach Limanaki hotel guests can use, of course, sun loungers and umbrellas. It is worth renting here is not too expensive. Towels, if desired, can be taken directly at the hotel - free of charge and without deposit.
