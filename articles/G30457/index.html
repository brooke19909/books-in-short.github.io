Hotel Botanik Magic Dream Hotel 4 *, Turkey: description and reviews

Spend the long-awaited annual vacation, summer vacation or an unforgettable weekend is possible in different countries and parts of the world. Whether it is visiting historical sites, world famous monuments, fascinating museums or enjoying the hot rays of the scorching sun - everyone chooses their own. When choosing the desired place, it is necessary to take into account the interests of each participant in the trip. This is especially true for family vacations: the kids should get better, get new emotions, joy and positive.

Often the choice falls on the colorful Turkey. It combines diverse directions: environmentally friendly air, which contributes to the restoration and strengthening of health, the crystal clear Mediterranean Sea, bathing in which strengthens various systems of the human body, and developed infrastructure, which allows vacationers to diversify their leisure time.

Carefully planned, taking into account the surrounding landscape, the resort is Kemer, located at the foot of the Taurus mountain range. The rich history of these places, unique nature, easily tolerated climatic conditions determine the year-round influx of tourists.

The four-star Botanik Magic Dream Hotel provides each guest with comfortable accommodation, a warm welcome, giving a good mood and rich memories of the time spent in this establishment.

Beach

The transparency of the local waters favors swimming with a mask, which allows you to get acquainted with the underwater world of the Mediterranean. Further diving in the open sea is available for both a novice diver and a professional in this field. Every health resort seeks to receive a compliment from the hot sun rays in the form of a dark tan.
