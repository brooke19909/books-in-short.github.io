Consider the most famous beaches of Yevpatoriya

Crimea is still the most popular holiday destination among Russian and Ukrainian tourists. Beautiful nature, subtropical climate and, of course, the Black Sea attracts millions of people here every summer. All cities of this peninsula are diverse both in their infrastructure and in their natural features. We will consider the beaches of Yevpatoriya, which are located in the west of Crimea. After all, this city is considered ideal for families with children, there are a lot of entertainment and attractions, so full speed ahead!



It should immediately be said that on the western coast of the peninsula the beach season begins in May and ends in October. Of course, the maximum number of tourists falls on July-August, but this does not mean at all that it is cold in the remaining months. The sea is almost always warm, warmed up to 25-26 degrees, and the weather pleases with sunny days. The beaches of Yevpatoriya are diverse, but they are united by the fact that they are sandy.
