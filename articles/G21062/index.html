Resorts of Trans-Urals: description and reviews

Kurgan region occupies a special place among the territorial divisions of Russia. The best places for rest and recreation are located here. Every Russian at least once in his life should visit the amazing resorts of the Trans-Urals. Kurgan is the administrative and cultural center of the Kurgan region, rich in attractions. The climate in this region is continental, summers are hot, but pass quickly, and cold winters with temperatures up to -50 ¬∞ C. It happens that the snow does not melt here for 6 months. Pearls of the Mound





In the Trans-Urals, there is a little-known Gorky Lake, it is unusually salty and in its characteristics resembles the Dead Sea, that is, it is impossible to drown in its water, and its mud is used to improve the body. The village located next to it is called the Resort, but, unfortunately, the village is not really a resort, because the infrastructure around the lake is not developed and there are no suitable living conditions. You can rent a corner with a local resident with amenities on the street or come with a tent. However, this is not the only problem of the village, there are still a huge number of annoying insects that will not haunt the tourist day or night. In the daytime these will be horseflies and gadflies, and at night - endless swarms of mosquitoes.

Sanatorium treatment in the Pine Grove





Everyone who prefers wellness treatments by the sea, must visit the resorts of the Trans-Urals. "Pine Grove" is a comfortable sanatorium that accepts vacationers throughout the year. It is located on the shores of Lake Gorky, one and a half kilometers from the city of Kurgan. The sanatorium includes a park with a relaxation area and a dance floor, a health path and a beach with sunbeds and sunshades. It has such natural resources as healing mud, mineral waters and favorable climatic conditions. The resort is surrounded by pine forests and birch groves. Gorkoye Lake has the shape of a crescent, on its eastern shore is a 150-year-old pine forest, and the rest of the coastal space is occupied by a grassy steppe. "Pine Grove" according to some reviews - the best resort. Resorts in the Trans-Urals are generally positively described by visitors. They leave good reviews about spa treatment, friendly staff, prices and a favorable environment.

Sanatorium complex

The residential part of the sanatorium consists of two buildings of a sleeping type, a dining room and a clinic. Vacationers are provided with rooms of six categories: one-room rooms for one or two people of the standard category, or two or three-room rooms of the first and second categories. Each room has all the necessary furniture, refrigerator, TV, bathroom and shower. They clean here every day, and once a week there is a change of linen and towels. In addition, depending on the type of room, one more sleeping place can be added in the form of a folding bed or a folding sofa.

Infrastructure and Services

Treatment in a sanatorium covers a wide range of diseases. Spa treatment involves:

  • prevention of diseases of the nervous system;
  • complete or partial elimination of problems of the musculoskeletal system;
  • elimination of urological and gynecological diseases, diseases of the gastrointestinal tract, ENT and others.

Treatment is carried out in departments located on the territory of the sanatorium, such as inhalation, balneological and massage rooms, the department of hydropathy, apparatus physiotherapy, ozone therapy, oxygen therapy and mud therapy. Numerous coniferous vegetation growing on the territory of the sanatorium and in its environs has a healing effect on the respiratory system. Three meals a day are included in the price.

Vacationers can also visit a cafe with a hall for 400 people. In the sanatorium there is a center for spa treatments, in addition to the classic jacuzzi, hot tubs and a cosmetologist‚Äôs office, it also has a hairdresser, solarium and a fitness center. Films are regularly organized, there is a billiard room and karaoke.

In summer, sports such as soccer and volleyball are available, and in winter, ice skating and skiing. The sanatorium has all the conditions for receiving children of any age, they will be offered 5 meals a day, and for kids over four years old there is the opportunity to undergo treatment under the supervision of a pediatrician.

Do not underestimate the spa holidays in winter, because it is at this time of the year that you can ski in the snowy forest, visit the pool, as well as get ready for the spring.

Sanatorium "Foresters"





Sanatorium "Foresters" is a cozy place located 20 km from the city of Kurgan. It is located in a pine forest, on the banks of the Tobol River. Near the health resort there is an artificial waterfall. The sanatorium can accommodate up to 350 people. It is ideal for families with children, as well as for healing the elderly. The health resort can be visited for treatment or just relaxation. Here, those who wish can take a course of preventive improvement of the gastrointestinal tract, thanks to the mineral water of the Mirgorod type, which, along with its healing properties, also has high palatability. And for those who wish to treat diseases of the respiratory system, the healing microclimate of the health resort and the air rich in oxygen and volatile are perfect. For the treatment of circulatory diseases and the musculoskeletal system, vacationers are provided with mud bath therapy.

Accommodation





The residential complex consists of three dormitories, in one of which there is a clinic and treatment rooms, and the premises of the cultural center. Single and double one-room or two-room rooms of the first and second categories are provided. Each room has a bathroom, furniture, TV and fridge. Linen is changed once a week, and cleaning is done every day.
