Phone Panasonic KX T7730: manual, reviews

The Panasonic KX T7730 analog system telephone is the link between a small telephone exchange and the user. The device is intended for offices. The lineup is aimed at workers who perform simple and complex operations. Proprietary telephones make the workflow most efficient.

Design

The entire range of Panasonic KX T7730 has a concise, rigorous design. The device is released in two classic colors - white and black. The phone is not original. Simplicity of appearance, good ergonomics, comfortable soft buttons, the ability to adjust the position - ideal criteria for the working option. Its design hardly attracts attention. The handset is lightweight and comfortable to hold in your hand. The device is made of high-quality plastic, the surface is glossy, it is easily cleaned from dust.



Specifications

The KX-T7730 can be used as the primary telephone in a telephone exchange. The main technical characteristics of this model:

  • Four wire analog system.
  • Combination with all automatic telephone exchanges of the Panasonic brand.
  • LCD display. Single line, with support for the Russian language.
  • The presence of a speakerphone.
  • Twelve keys with indication.
  • Call indicator. If the phone rings, the light alert blinks; if a message arrives, it lights continuously.
  • The user can change the angle of the device.
  • There is a headset jack.
  • The ability to control the volume and brightness of the display.

In order to establish a connection with the subscriber, you must press the corresponding button or call the system function. Manufacturers took care of user comfort. A console is connected to the model to increase the number of keys. The device has a special leg, adjusting which, set a certain angle of inclination of the phone. The plus is that the user can independently change the settings and program the buttons. Speakers provide maximum volume, clarity of sound. There is a console for recording rooms.

Features

Compared to other analog phones, the KX T7730 system model has a lot of advantages.

  • The device is cost effective. Thanks to the system telephone, the productivity of employees and their work efficiency is increased.
  • The programmable buttons have an indication. You can program numbers and lines in them, and then get access to them with one click.
  • Switch the call to another party.
  • The ability to pause the conversation.
  • Organize a conference call.
  • Do a redirect.
  • Notify office staff using phone speakers.
  • Recognize received messages and calls thanks to the indication.
  • The display shows the number of the person who is calling.

If the organization is large and the console needs to be enlarged, Panasonic suggests adding an optional KX T7740 X device to the model, which has an additional forty-eight buttons. The cost of the console exceeds the price of the phone itself. The console has thirty-two keys with two-color display and sixteen without it.





Using a proprietary telephone, everyday work tasks are easily solved and negotiations are being conducted. That is why it is easy to create a telephone line in the office by setting the necessary parameters. In addition, it is easy for an office worker to communicate over the phone using the speakerphone and not be distracted from important matters.
