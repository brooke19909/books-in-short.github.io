Extremely successful stay: Vietnam. The recall of an experienced tourist will not let you lie

Exotic and mysterious sounds of the jungle, lianas overgrown with walls of Buddhist temples and riding on elephants, inexpensive but high-quality hotels and pristine beaches, bays of unprecedented beauty and bizarre cliffs rising from the water all attract tourists to Vietnam. Rest (reviews from 2013 will tell us about this) here is simply extremely successful. Of course, for those who know how to choose and understand the price-quality ratio. He also loves a varied and tasty cuisine, which very bizarrely and at the same time delicately combines national, regional and European (mainly French) notes.





Connoisseurs of this country increasingly give us recommendations on how to better spend their holidays here. Vietnam, reviews of which are beginning to occupy the top ten TOP impressions of the most rated countries for relaxation, is famous for its excellent tourist infrastructure, stunning scenery, good service and the cheapest prices in the region.

Beach holidays - Vietnam. Reviews about the sea

Both in season and in rains - it is always good here. True, when it comes to the southern part of the country, in the north in the winter the rain drizzles, as in Paris. But it is always hot here, so do not forget the good sun creams, and also avoid direct exposure to its rays. And then burns can go off for a very long time, if you do not take care. Many tourists do not boast of beaches - both public and hotel ones. And if your hotel is on the first line and has its own access to the sea, then you have a chance to enjoy Wi-Fi right on the beach. Many resorts are located so that a constant breeze blows from the sea. This helps to tolerate the local heat well.



Cuisine and leisure - Vietnam. Reviews lovers to eat

The "old-timers" of this country do not recommend buying tours on an all-inclusive basis, but they advise taking hotels only with breakfast. It is then that truly immense possibilities open before you. After all, local food is not only exotic, but also diverse, and in order to try at least one hundredth of it, you need to go to cafes and restaurants to choose an institution to your liking. Vacationers primarily value fish and seafood - because, as a rule, they live on the coast, where all these gifts of Neptune are fresh and cheap.
