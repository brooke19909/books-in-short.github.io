A trip to Illichivsk: rest. Recreation facilities, hotels, housing prices

Illichivsk is famous not only for its beautiful Black Sea coast and warm climate. It should be noted that it is also an important industrial community - there are many factories and one of the largest ports of Ukraine. However, even with such an economic factor, it remains a clean and ennobled resort of the Black Sea region. Here is exactly what attracts modern tourists so much - alleys, parks, fountains, streets where you can spend a relaxing holiday and breathe in the healing air.

The port and all enterprises operating through it are located in the Liman bay. Due to this, the industry does not affect the environmental status of the residential sector. It is separated from these structures by a wide park area.

By the way, in 2016 Ilyichevsk was renamed to Chernomorsk. This is where the Bulgarian Golden Sands begin. The clean beach that is here is interrupted by the mouth of the Danube.

This resort area has become popular due to the fresh and healing air, the beautiful sea, the warm sun and the “golden” beaches. Housing here is inexpensive, which allows people of any income to find good places. The shore is filled with park areas, groves. Here are recreation centers, pensions, hotels and hotels.

A lot of restaurants, cafes, bars, nightclubs are located in a city like Illichivsk. Rest (recreation facilities will be discussed below) promises to be unforgettable. Thanks to summer areas and gazebos, a romantic evening or dinner with the family will be forever remembered.

Local hotels, as a rule, do not have large sizes. Almost all are built on the coast. Near them there is definitely a cafe or restaurant, pharmacy, shop.



Housing choice

Various housing options are offered by Illichivsk. Rest (the most popular recreation centers ) can be spent in expensive comfortable apartments, as well as in cheaper rooms. Even without paying attention to the fact that the city is small, it should be noted that the range of housing offered will compete with any major resort.

In addition to recreation centers and hotels, apartments and rooms in houses that are located near the coast in the private sector are often rented here. Approximate prices - $ 12-16, but in the summer, the cost increases many times.

In the private sector there are hotels and guest houses. In the latter, the average cost of a room is $ 8-12. In hotels, prices are much higher: from $ 30 to $ 120. There are options for rooms with access to the courtyard, private parking.

Hotels

For a small fee you can stay in comfortable rooms of local hotels. The leaders were Nika and Elite Tennis Club. On the territory of the latter there are various tennis courts, which allow vacationers to learn to play or maintain their level, even during vacation.

The elite complexes “Fountain” and “Villa on the Embankment” set the average cost of $ 60-200. They are famous for Ilyichevsk. Recreation (recreation centers and these institutions are constantly competing) is proposed in the following rooms to choose from:

  • Suite (family, exclusive);
  • standard (double);
  • two-level room (5-6 people).

The price may include meals, parking and Internet. However, all institutions need to clarify this issue.

Recreation facilities

The minimum cost of living at recreation centers of Illichivsk is $ 4. As budget options, you can offer complexes "Panorama", "Dolphin", "Flower", "Bukovina." In them rooms for two people per day will cost from $ 14 to $ 22. The boarding houses “Fairy Tale”, “Bay” and others will cost a little more. Their average room price is $ 20-60.

Most of the local complexes have their own equipped beach. This especially pleases guests who decide to visit Illichivsk. Rest (recreation centers of this city have unprecedented demand) can be spent on sports grounds or wander in a nearby forest. Gardening is the issue that greatly worries the city administration and the institutions themselves. In summer, you need to book a room in advance, as you may find that there are no free options. Moreover, the longer the lease is, the more expensive accommodation will be.

Laguna South





Let's consider an option where it is possible to settle, having arrived to Illichivsk. The recreation center "Laguna-South" is located in one of the parts of Bugovo. It offers three meals a day, barbecue rental. The administration organizes excursions to dolphinariums, water parks. There is an opportunity to go fishing.

On the territory of the institution there is Internet, a children's and sports ground, a sauna, a cafe, parking and more. The minimum cost of a room per day is $ 2 per person.

Lukomorye





The cost of the house per person is $ 4. Before the change of leadership, this complex was constantly visited by tourists. They knew that it was always clean, and the food was delicious in the dining room. However, now the recreation center "Lukomorye" (Illichivsk) is constantly receiving negative reviews from vacationers. The most common complaints are theft. They open the houses during the day, and after that the administration evicts people and sends them back home.

"Falcon"





The recreation center accommodates people in the territory of a large complex, designed for 200 people. It is possible to settle in a house where 2-3 people can live at the same time. Meals are either three meals a day or independent in the dining room. There is a cafe and bar on the beach. The recreation center "Falcon" (Illichivsk) is famous for its clean coast and excellent service.
