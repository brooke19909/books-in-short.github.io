Hostels of Tambov: prices and description

A large number of tourists come to Tambov. Here you can relax inexpensively. Hostel Tambov "Comfort" is popular among many visitors because of its excellent location, and "Plato" is more suitable for those who want to save money. Consider the options.

"Comfort"

This is a mini-hotel. Cost - from 650 rubles per day and above. Nice staff work here. The rooms are comfortable, the staff is attentive. In addition, pleasing prices. The hotel is located in the city center, which is suitable for all tourists. Nearby there is a traffic intersection and a theater. In addition, guests also note that this hotel is cozy and comfortable.

Like many other Tambov hostels, Uyut is an ordinary room for several people, as well as a suite. The rooms have beds, bedside tables, desks. Single and double rooms do not have a private bathroom. It is located on the floor. The remaining rooms have separate amenities. Breakfast is not included. A shuttle service is available at a surcharge. You can also order breakfast in your room. At the request of guests, the administration can call a taxi. The rooms are soundproofed. There are separate rooms for families and non-smokers.

"Plato"

The cost of the hostel is 500 rubles per day and more. Previously, this hotel was called Niva. Like many other Tambov hostels, this one is located near the station and the airport. The rooms are comfortable. In addition, it is possible to rent a bed in a common room for a small fee.

Guests can cook in the shared kitchen. It has all the necessary equipment. From this hotel you can reach any city attraction. The rooms are constantly cleaned. There is an opportunity to play board games. A common living room with a TV is equipped. Internet is free. The hostel has a safe. A gift shop is located nearby. A shuttle service is available at a surcharge. There are special places for smoking. It is possible to do laundry, but laundry services are paid.

"Marseilles"

The cost of the hotel per day is from 320 rubles and more. The hotel has internet access, a sauna, a car wash, a pool and parking. The rooms are cozy, they create a homely atmosphere. In order to make the rest comfortable, all the necessary furniture and appliances were installed. Near the hotel there are shops, transport stops. In 15 minutes you can reach the station. The hostel has a cafe and a bar. They can eat, watch sporting events, and just have a good free time.
