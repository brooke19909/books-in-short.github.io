"Buran" (boarding house): location, description. Pension "Buran", Sergiev Posad

If you want to have a good rest, then it is not necessary to rush abroad. What best helps relieve fatigue and stress? That's right, an unhurried walk through a real Russian forest, the opportunity to breathe the air of clean fields and meadows and admire the grandeur of the mountain slopes. One of the places that gives you the opportunity to get an excellent rest, to increase your mood from communication with nature and cultural and educational activities is Buran, a guesthouse located near Moscow, near the city of Sergiev Posad. If you take a trip along the Golden Ring, you can also stop here for a day, take a break and gain strength.



Russian Railways-Health OJSC - these are leisure options for every taste

This is the largest company in Russia, which has created a network of 17 resorts and boarding houses in our country. All of them are distinguished by their location in the most amazing, ecologically clean places of the country. One of them is the Buran boarding house. Russian Railways provides the highest quality sanatorium services. All conditions are perfectly balanced here, this is an opportunity to relax and receive treatment at the same time.

The unique natural conditions and picturesque location give peace and tranquility, and the latest medical equipment and high-quality service allow you to quickly go through diagnostics and solve your problems. Separately, it is worth saying about the Buran boarding house. It is not in vain that Russian Railways Health chose this place, because here one more opportunity opens up: it is a winter ski resort that invites everyone to breathe frosty air and ride from the slopes starting in November.



Where is Buran located - the pension of your dreams

If you live in Moscow, you will have to overcome 150 km. The track is good, so time flies by, about an hour and a half on the road, and you are there. If you live close by or visit the Orthodox Sergiev Posad for tourist purposes, then it‚Äôs worth nothing to call at Buran. The guesthouse is located 33 km from the city center.

If you drive your car, then go on a journey along the Yaroslavl highway to the city of Sergiev Posad, then you will need to turn off at the sign for Uglich and drive another 30 km to the sign "Buran". The boarding house is waiting for visitors at any time of the year for a long time or weekend.

If you do not have a personal car, you can take a bus from the Yaroslavl Station in Moscow to Sergiev Posad Station, and then transfer to a suburban bus (route No. 53). It leaves 4 times a day: two morning flights, at 5 and 6 in the morning, one - in the afternoon, at 13.00, the last - in the evening, at 18:00. If you are a fan of hiking, you can take a tent and move out of Sergiev Posad on foot or by bicycle.



What attracts tourists

And really, what makes Buran boarding house so different? Sergiev Posad is the real heart of Orthodox Russia, bright pages of its history are revealed here. The beauty and grandeur of these places inspire pride in their country, the unique ensemble of architectural and historical monuments and museums attracts many tourists from both Russia and Europe. But not only the charm of the surrounding places is famous for this guesthouse. Of great importance is the organization of leisure, which meets all the requirements of the European level of comfort. The recreation area itself is located in the forest, which is transformed into a magnificent park. There are residential buildings that are intended for tourists, and a medical building where all types of medical procedures are performed. Nearby is the ski slope, and taking a one-hour walk, you can walk to the wonderful forest lake.



Accommodation for tourists

Up to 150 tourists can relax here at once. Four cozy buildings are at their service. Previously, because of them, tourists criticized the Buran boarding house. For the convenience of tourists, they are equipped with everything necessary. There is a TV with 14 working channels, a fridge, a telephone, an iron and a kettle. Each room is equipped with a set of dishes. Toiletries, bed linen and towels are provided.

The orientation of the sanatorium

Many have the question of whether it is possible to take a trip to the Buran with the children. Pension "Russian Railways" is a family-type sanatorium of general therapeutic orientation. Medical focus

Often, in order to restore health, people come to the Buran boarding house. Sergiev Posad (reviews confirm that being here already has a positive effect on health) has long been famous for its holy sources and has remained in people's memory as a land that heals.
