Krasnovishersk hotels: description and reviews

Krasnovishersk is the northernmost point of the Perm Territory, located about 300 km from Perm. The city was founded in 1930. Enterprises of the pulp and paper industry and non-ferrous metallurgy function here, for some time high-quality diamonds were mined. Thus, the city can be considered attractive for business. Hotels in Krasnovishersk will provide a warm welcome to business travelers and tourists.

Hotel "Vishera"

The Vishera Hotel in Krasnovishersk is located at 9A Dzerzhinsky Street. This is the very center of the city in close proximity to the park and the city administration.

The hotel provides 14 guest rooms of different categories of comfort (from economy to luxury). Total capacity 24 places. The cost of living varies from 300 to 2000 rubles per person per day.

Staying at this hotel, you can take advantage of this set of advantages:

  • parking;
  • ATM;
  • wake up service;
  • shared kitchen;
  • non-smoking rooms;
  • Russian sauna.

Reviews for the hotel "Vishera"

You can hear such positive reviews about this hotel in Krasnovishersk:

  • affordable rates for accommodation;
  • fresh repair;
  • convenient location (near a large supermarket, bus station, shopping center);
  • There is a well-equipped shared kitchen;
  • the staff does its best to maintain cleanliness.

And such negative ones:

  • no breakfast (even for an additional fee);
  • when there are a lot of guests in the hotel, there is not enough hot water for everyone in the boiler.
