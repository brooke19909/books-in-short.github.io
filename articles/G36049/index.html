Master class on how to make a rim with stones and rhinestones

A rim with stones and rhinestones can be used for everyday wear, and possibly for festive occasions. With your own hands, such an accessory is made quickly enough, and material costs will be small. And you can be sure that no one will have such a decoration.

Everyday bezel





Let's get to work:

  1. Draw on paper a sketch of the rim brooch at a size of 1 to 1.
  2. Put a piece of felt in front of you.
  3. In the center, gently glue the first large pebble.
  4. Take a chain of small pebbles and glue one end close to the crystal on felt. Put some glue points around the main pebble. Glue the chain along the contour of the crystal to make a circle.
  5. Lay out large rhinestones in front of you. Put a dot of glue over the resulting circle and glue the rhinestone with tweezers. So make another circle.
  6. Glue three small rhinestones on the left side. Then attach a chain and a large pebble. Glue them. You should get two circles.
  7. Paste around the pebbles with the chain, located on the left, with small rhinestones.
  8. Make a similar circle with a strass on the right side.
  9. Circle three circles along the contour with a chain of large pebbles.
  10. Glue a chain of small pebbles on top.
  11. Cut the learned figure from the rhinestone and pebbles along the contour.
  12. Glue the part to the elastic in the center.
  13. Attach it to the head and measure the required length so that the brooch is slightly on its side. Cut excess.
  14. Sew or glue the ends of the gum.

Such rims with stones and rhinestones with your own hands will turn out more elegant if you use a tape instead of an elastic band. Then the ends do not need to be glued or stitched together, they will be tied.

Take a decor whose diameter is equal to and greater than the width of the rim. Then a feeling will be created that the stones and rhinestones are attached directly to your hair.

Wedding band with stones and rhinestones





Master Class:

  1. Prepare glue, bezel and decor. You can use finished parts from beads and pebbles, which will only be connected together. You can also make them yourself, starting from the first workshop on creating brooches.
  2. Gather decorative elements from beads and pebbles, and then lay them out in front of you as they should look on the rim.
  3. Glue the decor together in the correct sequence, use a mesh or cloth as the substrate.
  4. Glue the finished jewelry to the rim.

The festive rim is ready.

We use curly stones





Instructions on how to make a bezel with curly stones:

  1. Take the frame of a metal rim, a wire under the color, pliers, curly pebbles (flowers) and beads.
  2. Wind a piece of wire and attach a pebble to one end. Thus, decorate the bezel. Place the pebbles close to each other and guide the wire in different directions. Make decor at different heights.
  3. Fill the gaps on the rim with wire, for this, wrap it tightly.

The rim with stones and rhinestones is ready.
