Premium Grand Horizon Resort 4 *, Egypt, Hurghada: reviews and photos of tourists

A resort city with a wonderful coast, wonderful beaches, well-developed modern infrastructure, in perfect harmony with the old areas, impressive Egyptian flavor - this is Hurghada. Premium Grand Horizon Resort is one of the most popular four-star hotels of the city, located on the seashore. Reviews of tourists about him are mixed. Location

Premium Grand Horizon Resort 4 * is located in the new part of Hurghada, about halfway between its official center and the airport. The distance to and from there is about 7 km, and the road takes 15-20 minutes. Al-Bahr is located next to the hotel - one of the central highways and at the same time a street with many shops, shops, bars and other urban infrastructure. Here you can always take a bus or minibus and get to the right place. It costs 1-2 dollars.





The famous Sheraton Street, as well as the seaport, fish market and many restaurants are within a 12-minute walk from Premium Grand Horizon Resort. Near the hotel there is a pharmacy, many shops, a shop and a small market with fruits. The beach is no further than 50 meters from the hotel.

Territory

Positive reviews of tourists, as well as negative ones, indicate that the territory of Premium Grand Horizon Resort 4 * is quite compact, but very beautiful, well-groomed, tastefully decorated with evergreen trees, bushes, flowers. Litter bins were installed in large numbers along the paved paths, so there is never garbage on lawns. Pleasantly decorate the territory of the figures of the Sphinxes and the fountain.





Among all this natural and man-made beauty, buildings, pools, sports fields, a parking zone, bars and restaurants are harmoniously located.

Infrastructure

Premium Grand Horizon Resort has a rich infrastructure to ensure its guests a pleasant and memorable stay. The hotel reception is located in one of the buildings, but not on the first, but on the second floor in a cozy lobby. Nearby there is a lobby bar, Viennese chairs with soft pillows, comfortable sofas and tables. At the reception you can leave things in the locker room, use the safe for valuables, exchange currency, buy excursions, call a doctor and taxi, hand over things for laundry and dry cleaning, order the services of a photographer. In addition, a building with Egyptian-made goods, a pharmacy, billiard and tennis tables, a SPA salon, and a fitness room are located in the hotel building. For those who are going to Egypt not only to relax, but also to work, the hotel offers a comfortable conference room equipped with new equipment and equipment.

Sea

The beach Grand Hotel Horizon Resort 4 * (Egypt, Hurghada) is famous for its chic beach. Thanks to him, this place will certainly fall in love with everyone who rested here. The seashore is literally 40 meters from one of the hotel's pools. Many tourists prefer to use sun loungers there, because on the beach due to the constant occupation of this furniture with towels and personal items, they are not always enough.





The hotel's sandy beach, with a pier, is two in one - on the one hand there is a territory for kids and those who want to calmly swim, lie on the water, and on the other, everything is equipped for those engaged in snorkeling and diving. Equipment can be rented either in the hotel itself or in the diving center.

The sea near the hotel is amazingly beautiful, clean, warm. Near the coast there are live reefs with dozens of fantastically beautiful inhabitants. For entertainment on the beach there are water rides, a small but very good water park with slides for adults and kids. Nearby are always instructors and rescuers. Also on the beach, craftsmen make tattoos to everyone, traders offer all kinds of spices and souvenirs, there is the opportunity to ride a camel. When leaving the beach, two showers and a fountain for rinsing the feet are installed.
