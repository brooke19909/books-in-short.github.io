Belarusian hotels

Consider the possible options for living in Belarus, depending on the level of income of the guests and the equipment of the hotels themselves. Here will be presented and budget, inexpensive options, and hotels of a higher level.

Hotel Renaissance Minsk

The Renaissance Minsk Hotel, located in the same city, the capital of the republic, is liked by visitors for its responsive and competent staff, cleanliness reigning inside and inside, and a huge indoor pool. The hotel has 267 rooms, 30 of which belong to the category of "luxury". Each number includes:

  1. Internet connection.
  2. TV system.
  3. Mini bar.
  4. Safe.
  5. Station for making hot drinks.

Feature of the room equipment is first-class bedding and quality furniture, as well as the presence of a working area in which there is a safe, wireless connection and a station for making drinks. For business travelers, the hotel provides meeting rooms. The hotel offers a wonderful atmosphere of Minsk, it will especially appeal to people visiting the city for the first time. All this is included in the price of the selected room; also, depending on the price, vacationers are offered a choice of food. Possible options:

  1. Lack of nutrition.
  2. Breakfast.
  3. Half board.

The menu of the hotel restaurant is very diverse and sophisticated, it presents dishes of Belarusian and other European cuisines. Meals are carried out according to the "buffet" system.

This hotel is included in the category "Belarus hotels with a pool". Therefore, on the ground floor of the establishment there is a swimming pool, a fitness room, several Finnish saunas, a professional beauty salon and a spa service area.





Hotel "Renaissance Minsk" offers prices on average from 9000 rubles per day for a double room. The price depends on the category of the room and the provision of food.





Address: Belarus, Minsk, Dzerzhinsky Avenue, 1E

Hotel Europe

Hotel "Europe" has a category of 5 stars. Most guests are attracted by the proximity of the institution to the city center.





The hotel was built according to the popular atrium type - the atrium. It is the only one of its kind in the republic. The guest can leave his room and see the elevators that open the panorama of the hotel, and the hall. The hotel is interesting because it is known for its ancient history, but it was reconstructed 11 years ago. Therefore, now there is a high quality service and the best equipment.

Hotel "Europe" offers 67 rooms of various types, including a room for people with disabilities. Each room has a floor heating system. You can choose a room with the view that you want to see from the windows, you need to choose from 2 options: a cozy courtyard or the historical part of the city for lovers of city noise. The walls of the rooms are decorated using the technique of "Venetian plaster" imitating marble, and carpeted flooring. The creators of the hotel adhered to the concept of a "healthy planet", using environmental materials in the design of the institution. Belarusian hotels are increasingly using a similar method. Room facilities include:

  1. Climate control system.
  2. Safe.
  3. Mini bar.
  4. Refrigerator.
  5. Telephone.
  6. Wireless internet connection.
  7. TV
  8. Hair dryer.

Often guests are attracted by the proximity to the metro; the nearest station can be reached in 4 minutes.

Meals at the hotel are provided only as a hearty breakfast, but there is the opportunity to eat in a restaurant or bar for an additional cost, so the price of a room depends, first of all, on this. On average, it is 9,000 rubles per day, the maximum cost is approximately 88,000 rubles.

For guests' convenience, the hotel has an elevator. For couples traveling with children, there are babysitting services. Caring for our customers - this is what distinguishes Belarus. Hotels, almost all, are equipped with a night club and an indoor pool, this hotel does not differ according to these criteria.

The hotel is located at: Minsk, st. Internatsionalnaya, 28

Hotel Minsk

This hotel is located in the historic city center, not far from the Dynamo metro station.





Judging by the reviews, most of all people are attracted by the location and proximity of the institution to the city center, competent and helpful staff, who will always help in this situation, and the cleanliness of the hotel. Here you can use the free wireless Internet connection, which is what people like in Belarus. By the way, hotels in the country often offer guests gyms, and Minsk is no exception. Each room has:

  1. Modern tv.
  2. Marble bathroom.
  3. Toiletries.

Room rates start at 5500 rubles.

Address: 11 Independence Ave., Minsk, Republic of Belarus

Hotel Victoria Olimp

The hotel is located in the capital of Belarus, in its quiet area. Therefore, people feel themselves in the zone of environmental conditions and clean air, but this does not mean that the hotel is far from the city center, you can get there in 15 minutes.





Guests staying at the hotel in their reviews note the cleanliness of the hotel, multilingual staff, and a variety of delicious food. It offers free Wi-Fi, a restaurant and a spa area. Therefore, this institution is a SPA hotel. There are a lot of such people in Belarus. It is located at the address: Minsk, Pobediteley ave., 103.





Hotel "At the Fountain"

If we consider the category of "cheap hotels in Belarus", then this hotel is definitely included in it, since here you can buy a room for 2500 rubles per day, including using the Internet, parking and billiards with a sauna.

Based on the reviews, people like it because of its location, as it is in close proximity to the metro station and is 2 km from the center. Polite staff and great food also attract guests.

Address: Minsk, st. Amurator, 4

Hotel Victoria on Castle

The hotel is within walking distance to the metro, the nearest station is 5 minutes away. It belongs to the three-star category, so the cost of rooms varies between 4-5 thousand rubles. The hotel rooms are equipped with everything necessary:

  1. Internet connection.
  2. Toiletries.
  3. Lingerie.
  4. Modern tv.
  5. Towels.

Visitors are also invited to take advantage of free private parking and a buffet restaurant, but this is not included in the room rate. There is a room delivery system.

The hotel is located at: st. Castle 25-27A, Minsk, Belarus.

Considering possible options for staying in the country, one can notice that hotel business owners are approaching European quality and service, this distinguishes the Republic of Belarus. Hotels here go to new levels almost every year.
