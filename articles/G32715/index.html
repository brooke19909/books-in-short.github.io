Solarus Imperial Hammamet - comfortable stay at affordable prices



Solarus Imperial Hammamet 4 * is located in a green park in the Yasmine zone of the city of Hammamet in Tunisia. Recommended for outdoor enthusiasts, for youth companies, for families, both with children and without them. It is located on the second coastline. From the central part of the city it is separated by a distance of 10 kilometers. The nearest Enfida Airport is 40 kilometers from the hotel. Nearby is the port of Marina. Nearby lies the magnificent amusement park of Cartago.

This is a new hotel. His housing stock is located in a three-story building.

Rooms

Solarus Imperial Hammamet offers its vacationers one of 255 standard rooms. All have a balcony, bathroom. There is a minibar (for a fee), a safe (for a fee), air conditioning, a TV, a telephone. Depending on the location of the room, the view from it may be on the pool, on the patio, on the sea, on the territory of the hotel. The type of accommodation provides double and triple rooms. In the first, an extra bed is allowed (upon reservation).



Nutrition

Solarus imperial hammamet for its guests provides All inclusive meals , which is served in the buffet. In addition, from 11.00 a central bar is open, which closes at 23.00. Here you can buy all kinds of drinks. There is a pizzeria, a themed buffet (once a week), 3 restaurants and a beach bar.

Beach

The sandy beach is 200 meters away. Professional instructors work. The entrance to the sea is convenient, without pits.

Additional Information

In order to make the rest as comfortable as possible, Solarus Imperial Hammamet provides its guests with a range of various services. For children, the mini-club provides various playrooms for different age categories: for kids there are unique sets of educational toys, and older children will be able to attend creative, animated and sports events.



For adults in the territory of Solarus Imperial Hammamet There are outdoor and indoor pools - with slides and heated. Sauna, hammamet and massage are available at an additional cost. For those who came not only to relax, but also to solve some production issues, there is a conference room equipped with everything necessary for high-quality meetings. If necessary, you can rent a car. Free parking is available on site. Among other services, there is a hairdresser, laundry, doctor, Wi-Fi.

Reviews

Despite the youth of Solarus Imperial Hammamet 4, there are already reviews about it, and there are quite a lot of them. First of all, pay attention to the rooms - they are very clean, the furniture is tastefully arranged. The food is lean, but tasty. Vegetarian preferences taken into account. The animation is active, and those who are left alone do not have to be bored. Much attention is paid to sports entertainment. To maintain a figure, you can do fitness or burn extra calories on exercise machines. There are darts, billiards. In the evening, there is always a disco. If someone does not like it, then in the immediate vicinity there is a night club.

Every day there is a guide at the hotel who conducts sightseeing trips during which you can explore not only the sights of Hammamet, but also the nearby settlements. Attendants are polite and attentive to guests.
