How to make a DIY bed for Monster High dolls?

"Monster High" - dolls developed by the Mattel Corporation, with many fans around the world. The peculiarity of this series is the character and emphasized individuality of each character. Curiously, these dolls depict teenagers who are children of famous monsters and monsters. Not without a bright design: each character has its own symbolism and color preferences. Want to emphasize the personality of your favorite characters? Try to equip a doll house with your own hands. Especially for you - a selection of the best tips and ideas that will explain how to make a DIY bed for Monster High dolls.

New sleeper in half an hour

Useful and cute crafts for dolls can be created with your own hands in just a few minutes. To make a simple bed, you will need one rectangular box, cardboard, fabric (a viscose cloth for cleaning is suitable) and decorative ornaments. Choose the basis of a suitable size - it should be a solid cardboard box on which the doll can stretch to its full height.





How to make a Monster High doll bed out of the box? Everything is very simple: glue the blank from all sides, cut the curly back from a sheet of cardboard and decorate it to your liking. This part can be painted, pasted with a cloth or colored paper. Now it remains only to connect the bed and the back. The crib can be decorated with braid, beads, sparkles or applique.

Bunk doll bed

There are a lot of dolls in the Monster High collection, and even half of them can sometimes be difficult to place in a standard size dollhouse . Try to make special furniture. An interesting idea is a bunk bed. To make it, prepare two beds with sides of the same size and shape (they can be rectangular, in the form of coffins or any others). How to make a bunk bed for Monster High dolls from two identical blanks? You will need four strong sticks. Suitable, for example, wooden skewers for barbecue. Connect the workpieces with such supports in the corners. Dry the resulting structure well. In conclusion, you can build a cardboard staircase or sticks for ice cream on the second floor.
