Climate

Tourists planning their holidays in Goa should take into account its climatic features. Despite the fact that the water temperature of the Arabian Sea is 26 - 28 ¬∞ C year-round, it is still reasonable to rest here precisely in the high (dry) season, which lasts from October to May. Humidity drops to 60 - 70%, and tropical rains do not violate the beach idyll. It is at this time that it is recommended to buy tours in Renton Manor 2 * (Goa). At the same time, tourists absolutely need to regularly use sunblock with a high level of protection from sunlight.

From June to September, monsoons blow in Goa, air humidity is set at around 100%, and long torrential tropical rains last for several days. This is a dead season for Indian resorts.

About hotel

Four-story hotel 3 lines Sodders Renton Manor 2 * is located at a distance of 32 km from Dabolim Airport. Its spacious four-story buildings are harmoniously combined with each other. Water sports

Fans of outdoor activities will not remain offended by staying at the two-star Renton Hotel. The thing is that nearby it is one of Goa's largest beaches - Baga beach with its well-developed water sports infrastructure.
Undoubtedly, the most popular sport in Goa is windsurfing - spectacular sailing on a board. Read the reviews of neophyte athletes are encouraging: taking only a couple - three lessons, they already "sailed" on their own. Experienced instructors skillfully introduce more and more amateur athletes to this interesting activity.

Water skiing is more complicated. It requires the ability to maintain balance acquired after years of training immediately after the launch of the boat. Among tourists, mainly experienced athletes are engaged in it.

Goa diving is less interesting than in Egypt. The reason is the low visibility under water and the relatively poor underwater fauna.

Excursions

Many excursions are offered to guests by the Sodder s Renton Manor 2 * hotel. Among them is a visit to the 600-meter-high Dudhsagar Falls hidden by the jungle. According to local legend, swimming in a lake accessible to tourists at the foot of a waterfall brings happiness to a person.

A fascinating excursion to the amazingly beautiful local Hindu temples. In old Goa, there are also Catholic churches listed in the UNESCO World Heritage List: the Basilica of Jesus and the Temple of St. Catherine.





To get acquainted with ancient India, you should buy an excursion to the city of Hampi, the capital of the Vijayangar Empire, discovered several decades ago in the middle of the jungle. It is located in the neighboring state of Karnataka.

Tourists at Renton Manor 2 * enjoy visiting the spice plantations, where they can purchase various oils and spices.

Various shopping tours to the day and night markets are popular among tourists. Goa also has the pleasure of riding and taking pictures on an elephant.

Night life. What is a goan trance party

Most of the guests of Jarrs Renton Manor 2 * are actively involved in the nightlife of the resort. Candlelight dinners are not honored here. There are other traditions in North Goa. Once in the 70s, very large parties were held here under the light of stars. The attention tradition of psychedelic music has proven to be sustainable. This music seems to refer to something strange in human nature. By its nature, it is dance. The season of Goan psychedelic parties, the so-called transpati, begins in November and lasts until the end of April. They take place in clubs Bamboo Forest (Anjuna); Hill Top and Disco Valley in Wagator.

Trance-style tracks include mesmerizing elements: cool mesmerizing bass, crazy drums, amazing beats. In them, the cyclic melody harmoniously encompasses with a laconic bass part and drums. The entry of such a track resembles light waves, which are subsequently gradually amplified. After the track reaches its climax, its musical structure begins to recoup in the reverse order until its completion. According to doctors, such music in itself stimulates changes in the consciousness of a person with open perception.
