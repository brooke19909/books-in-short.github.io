How to check the connected Beeline services: tips and tricks

How can I check the connected Beeline services? This topic is of interest to many modern subscribers. Especially if money suddenly started to be debited from the SIM card. Fortunately, the task has many solutions. They will be described below. Step-by-step instructions will help you to see a list of connected options and will reveal methods for managing them.



About verification options

How to check what services are connected to Beeline? There is no single answer. As we have said, there are several very simple options.

Namely:

  • submitting a USSD request;
  • short number ringing;
  • making a call to the operator;
  • use the menu "My Beeline";
  • direct request to the office of the mobile operator with a request;
  • launch of a mobile application from Beeline;
  • use of "My Account" on the operator‚Äôs official page.

How to act? It all depends on the personal preferences of the user. Next, we will consider these methods in more detail. There is nothing difficult in their implementation. All inquiries are free.

Help request

How can I check the connected Beeline services? The first and easiest option is to send a USSD request of the established form. A similar technique is used by the bulk of customers.

It is proposed to act approximately like this:

  1. Open the dialer on your phone.
  2. Print the command * 110 * 09 #.
  3. Click on the "Call" button.
  4. Wait a while.

In a couple of minutes, the request will be processed. In response to it, a person will receive an SMS with information about the connected options. Quick, easy, convenient.

Call to a short number

But this is only the beginning. Modern subscribers can approach the solution of the task in different ways.

For example, it is allowed to check the connected Beeline services by making a call to a short number. The option acts similarly to the USSD request.

To bring the idea to life, the subscriber just needs to call 067409. After clicking on the ‚ÄúCall‚Äù button, you can wait for a response message with the information we are interested in. It is done!



Mobile menu

To check whether services are activated on Beeline, some resort to using a universal self-service menu. This is not the most common, but very useful technique.

To bring it to life, the subscriber needs:

  1. Dial * 111 # on the phone.
  2. Ring the combination.
  3. Go to "My Beeline" - "My Data".
  4. Click on the button responsible for the subsection "My Services".
  5. Submit a request.

If everything is done correctly, the subscriber will see the procedure for processing the operation, and then will receive a message in the established form. It will display all the services connected on the SIM card.

Carrier prompts

How to check the list of connected Beeline services? The next trick in real life is almost never found. This is a direct call to the operator.

The step-by-step operation is presented to the company's customers as follows:

  1. Call 0611.
  2. Wait for operator response.
  3. Name the phone number you want to verify.
  4. Report your intentions.
  5. Say the data requested by the operator. Usually this is passport information, as well as the name of the owner of the SIM card.
  6. Get information about connected options. For example, a call center employee may dictate it. Or send as an SMS with a list.

All of these actions are absolutely free. Sometimes the operator refuses to disclose information about the options used on the phone. This usually happens when third parties try to get information. They will most likely be denied. Only the owner of the room and its representatives can control the SIM card and receive information on it.



Phone app

How to check what services are connected to Beeline? The next option is to work with a special mobile application. It is called "My Beeline." Available on the AppStore and PlayMarket.

The action algorithm will be similar to working with the operator menu. Just go to the "Services" or "My options" to see information about the features used. Here they can be easily disconnected or reconnected.

Nevertheless, this is far from the most popular and convenient method. As already mentioned, subscribers prefer to manage options through the "My Account".

LK Beeline

How to check the connected paid Beeline services? It's time to get acquainted with the most convenient solution to the problem. With its help, it will be possible not only to check the active options, but also to configure them.

We are talking about entering the "My Account" on the website of the mobile operator. You need to act like this:

  1. Open the site beeline.ru.
  2. Click on the "Login" button. Usually it is located in the upper right corner.
  3. Log in to your personal account. To do this, you will have to register in advance on the service.
  4. Open the "Services" section.
  5. View the proposed list of options used.

In order to refuse this or that opportunity, just click on the "Disable" hyperlink opposite the appropriate line. Confirming the operation, the person will turn off the service. In the same way, new features are connected.
