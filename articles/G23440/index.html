Remote platform: types, installation and application

The capabilities of construction hoisting equipment are very small. Often they come down only to lifting goods to the roof of the structure or to some kind of ceiling. That is why you should look for methods that will help lift the load to a specific floor of the structure. For such purposes, they also use remote sites for receiving cargo, which allow you to freely submit and receive materials to the desired floor.



Description

The portable site is widely used as an additional structure in the construction, reconstruction of high-rise buildings, in the construction of metal frames of business centers, but nevertheless, the main function of the site is the acceptance of construction cargo.





The construction site is fixed between the floors of buildings and fixed with building struts for stability.

They also use a type of lifting equipment such as a mounted platform. It is a welded metal structure. Such a structure is mounted on the external wall of the building and is used for facing work. The main advantage of the suspended platform is the ease of installation and installation on any floor of the construction object, which makes it simply an indispensable assistant in the construction of brick and monolithic buildings.

Site setup

The remote platform is installed with a crane, which is subsequently used to lift materials to a height. The platform is sent to the necessary opening of the building, where it is fixed. The remote site is a welding frame with a supporting surface covered with wood or metal, has a special collapsible fence designed for safe operation. The strength and quality of these structures must necessarily be confirmed by permits.





Types of remote sites

Today, there are several types of structures of this type. The most used are:

  • K-1,1. The carrying capacity is 1200 kg, the dimensions of the receiving area are 1.5x2.0 m.
  • K-1.4. The main characteristic features of this type according to TU is a carrying capacity of 2500 kg, the volume of the working area is 2.5x2.0 m, the flooring material is wood.
  • K-1,3. It is characterized by the highest carrying capacity, it can lift a load whose weight exceeds 2500 kg. The main dimensions of the working area are 2.5x2.0 m. In contrast to the K-1.4 design, the flooring is made of steel.

Remote sites K-1 differ in weight and in size of the entire general mechanism. The K-1.1 form weighs approximately 500 kilograms, the K-1.4 form - 960 kilograms, the K-1.3 design is considered the heaviest - its weight is 1000 kg.

On-site application

All modifications of K-1 designs are very convenient for warehousing and storage of various building materials and devices. It is important to remember that the single weight of the cargo located on the remote platform should not exceed the lifting capacity of the platform.



Only sites with a certificate and permits are allowed. If at least one document is missing, then using this design is not safe, as this increases the risk of injury to people working in construction. Therefore, remote sites pass the necessary tests to recognize their suitability for use. Auxiliary building structures have all the necessary certificates. A mandatory requirement is the availability of drawings for the remote site.





Using these sites in construction, you get a durable product with a package of all the necessary documentation, including a product passport and an instruction manual. Remote sites guarantee safe and quiet work for workers who perform construction and installation work at height, with the mandatory observance of all safety standards.
