Hotel Slovenska Plaza 4 * (Budva, Montenegro): description, services, reviews

If you want to relax well and inexpensively, then pay attention to the resort complex Slovenska Plaza 4 *. Budva, Montenegro is a rather interesting tourist destination, which, unfortunately, is not as popular as Egypt, Turkey, Greece and others. However, you will probably want to come back here.





Short description

The cozy atmosphere and high-quality service will please you at the Slovenska Plaza 4 * resort hotel . Budva, Montenegro, is characterized by an interesting, distinctive culture that you can feel in this institution, designed as a small tourist village, decorated in a traditional style. It is equipped with swimming pools, and areas for sports entertainment, and other opportunities for entertainment and relaxation.

It is worth noting that the summer season is an ideal time to visit a resort such as Slovenska Plaza 4 * (Budva, Montenegro) . The fact is that during this period in the city and in this hotel in particular, there are many folklore festivals, thanks to which you can get acquainted with the Balkan culture. It is also worth paying attention to the many dance and sports workshops in which guests can take part.

We note the uniqueness of the resort complex Slovenska Plaza 4 *. Budva (Montenegro) boasts a huge number of hotels, but this one is unique in its kind. Eleven buildings are connected by cozy streets. There are 528 guest apartments, which in 2012 underwent a complete renovation.





The location of the resort

Hotel Slovenska Plaza 4 * is located just a 10-minute leisurely walk from the center of Budva. Here you can visit popular night clubs, large shopping and entertainment centers, architectural and natural attractions, as well as luxurious city beaches (the closest is 50 meters from the hotel). Tivat Airport is 20 kilometers from the hotel, and Dubrovnik is just over 70 kilometers away.

Description of guest apartments

The high level of comfort and homeliness are characterized by the guest apartments of the resort complex Hotel Slovenska Plaza 4 *. You can choose for yourself numbers of the following categories:

  • Standard rooms are 20 square meters. m will comfortably accommodate two guests. They can accommodate in large or twin beds. You can choose a room modification with or without a balcony, which will slightly affect the cost of living.
  • For fans of traveling with family or friends, triple spacious rooms are provided. In addition to sizes, they are almost identical to the previous category of apartments.
  • Studio is a cozy room of 28 square meters. m. Within the framework of a single space, by means of decorative zoning, zones of a bedroom, a drawing room and an entrance hall are allocated. You can choose for yourself the option of accommodation with or without a balcony.
  • Executive apartments are designed for comfortable accommodation of two main, as well as 2 additional guests. The bedroom has a luxurious king size bed, and the living room has comfortable soft sofas and armchairs, sitting on which you can watch TV, enjoy reading or socializing.
  • Premium Suite impresses with its huge size, as well as an elegant interior. A pleasant moment is the presence of your own kitchen, as well as an additional guest bathroom.

You will feel as comfortable as possible if you opt for the Slovenska Plaza 4 * hotel. The rooms are elegantly, simply and homely decorated.

Useful information for hotel guests

It is natural that each hotel sets its own principles and rules in accordance with which its work is carried out. If we talk about a hotel such as Hotel Slovenska Plaza 4 * (Montenegro, Budva), then it will be useful for you to know the following information:

  • When planning your arrival at the hotel, take into account the fact that you can check into your room only from noon to midnight;
  • all procedures related to departure are carried out in the period from 6 a.m. to 10 a.m .;
  • children up to two years old stay in the apartment for free, provided that they do not need an extra bed, and if you need a crib, then you will have to pay 7 euros per day for it;
  • an extra bed for children under the age of 7 will cost half the cost of the daily stay of an adult guest (if we are talking about children under 12 years old - 70%);
  • an adult guest can stay in an extra bed for 32 euros per day;
  • the hotel does not provide the possibility of staying with pets, regardless of their breed and size;
  • You can use plastic cards to pay for accommodation and other services received;
  • if you cancel your reservation no later than two days before check-in, then the prepayment will be returned to you in full (otherwise, a penalty will be charged in the amount of the amount made earlier).
