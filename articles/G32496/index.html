Hotel Zigana Club Beldibi, Kemer: description and reviews

Turkey is one of the most popular resort countries among Russian tourists. In its resorts there are many hotels that offer the most comfortable conditions for recreation, and even at a very reasonable price.

Kemer is one of the most visited resort towns located on the Mediterranean coast. Located on its territory, the Zigana Club hotel (Kemer, Beldibi) in 2017 was recognized as one of the most popular.

Location

The hotel is located in the village of Kemer, 15 kilometers from the administrative center, where a large number of attractions are concentrated. At a distance of 50 kilometers from it is the international airport of Antalya, from where, as a rule, the journey of foreign tourists through Turkish lands begins.

Zigana Club 5 * (Kemer, Beldibi, Goynuk) is located on the territory where the tropical climate reigns, softened by the sea air, which allows vacationers not to languish from the heat and heat, but to enjoy the mild air.

general information

Zigana Club (Kemer, Beldibi) was built in 1996. Since then, regular repairs have been carried out on its territory, the largest reconstruction was carried out in 2009, after which the hotel acquired a modern look, at this time the technical equipment was also improved.

The building of the main building of the hotel, together with the territory adjacent to it, occupies a rather large plot - 68 thousand square meters. m. On this territory is located not only the entire number of rooms, but also a lot of entertainment for guests.

Rooms

One of the main points that may interest tourists in the description of Zigana Club (Kemer, Beldibi) is its number of rooms. In this recreation area there are 304 comfortable rooms, which are divided into several categories: standard, suite, suite and villa. Each of them has modern furniture and electronics, which makes the life of guests more comfortable. All apartments have an individual climate system, with which it seems possible to create the most optimal living conditions. Each room also has a private bathroom with a bathroom and a set of bath accessories.

Standard

Standard category apartments in Zigana Club (Kemer, Beldibi) consist of one room, designed to accommodate 2 or 3 people. It has a large double bed with orthopedic mattress, as well as a folding soft sofa, which, if necessary, can serve as an extra bed. The room also has additional furniture: a wardrobe, a cupboard for storing a small amount of things, a table, as well as large floor lamps that illuminate the room. Here, on the wall, there is a large plasma TV with satellite channels.

Here, free of charge, Wi-Fi access is available, and the window offers beautiful views of the surrounding flowering garden and the interior of the hotel.

Vacation with children

Tourists who come to rest at the Zigana Club (Kemer, Beldibi) together with their young children will not be deprived of attention. For kids, the hotel has a number of conditions. First of all, the presence of a small children's pool in which everything is arranged taking into account safety precautions deserves great attention. Entertainment

The hotel has a huge amount of entertainment. Zigana Club 5 * is a place where a well-coordinated and professional team of animators works who can entertain both children and adults. It consists of 30 people, many of whom speak Russian. Every night, guests can take part in parties, and from morning to lunch - in sports entertainment events and competitions, which are usually held near the pools.

Nutrition

Hotel guests have the opportunity to eat in a beautiful restaurant, which is located on its territory. A daily buffet is served here for all-inclusive travelers. For those who prefer a healthy diet, there is a separate diet menu.





In addition to the restaurant, in Zigana Club 5 * there are several bars that are located on sunny terraces, in the main building, as well as on the beach. Residents can enjoy delicious snacks here, as well as refreshing drinks and cocktails. Guests at the bars can enjoy free Wi-Fi for work and entertainment.

Beach

In their reviews of Zigana Club (Kemer, Beldibi), tourists often positively commented on the local beach, which the hotel owns. It is completely covered with sand and pebbles and has a gentle slope into the sea, which is very convenient not only for adults, but also for children. It also has three wooden diving piers. The length of the beach strip is 170 meters.

The entire coastal area is perfectly landscaped for a comfortable stay: there are several dressing rooms, toilets and showers, if necessary, guests can rent air mattresses, towels and equipment for outdoor activities.

A lot of entertainment awaits vacationers in the sea: here you can ride on air cushions or a catamaran, rent a yacht or dive under scuba diving. For lovers of outdoor activities there is the opportunity to ride a jet ski or ski.





Sports and spa

In the hotel building there are many places that will certainly be interesting for those who advocate a healthy lifestyle and monitor their health. First of all, there is a large gym equipped with many simulators. Guests can also visit the fitness center and aerobics classes - this is more popular with the female half of vacationers. There are several sports fields on the street that are ideal for playing soccer, volleyball and basketball.

The hotel's spa area is very attractive for guests who enjoy relaxing and beauty treatments. You can also visit the Turkish bath and sauna.

Prices

The cost of the tour in Zigana Club (Kemer, Beldibi) directly depends on which tour operator the vacation package is purchased from, as well as where the flight is planned from. On average, for Muscovites a trip to this hotel with all-inclusive accommodation costs about 60 thousand rubles for two with a condition of staying for a week in standard apartments.

This price already includes prices for round-trip flights, airport transfers, and hotel accommodation. It was relevant according to data cited in June 2017.
