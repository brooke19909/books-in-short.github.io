DIY fabric angel: photos, patterns

You think to decorate the house for the New Year, but do not know what? Do it yourself from the fabric of an angel. This toy can be used as decoration for a Christmas tree, window, shelf and even the ceiling. And the most beautiful crafts are a great gift for friends as a souvenir.

Felt angel



Even a child can make such a toy. Today we will make our own angel from fabric. Photo crafts conveys all its charm and personality. We will need felt, thread and two beads.

First of all, we need to make blanks. The body will look like a triangle with rounded edges. We cut out two parts and sew them together. If you fill the parts of an angel, then it will turn out to be voluminous, if this is not necessary, then leave the toy without stuffing. The torso is ready. Cut two circles of flesh-colored - this will be the head. We sew the parts and attach them to the body. Next, we make a hairstyle. Our sample shows an angel girl with two ponytails, but you can make a boy. Do not limit the flight of your imagination. Cut two white wings and sew them to the back. A self-made angel of fabric is ready, it remains to detail it. Cut out the heart and sew it to the chest. From two ropes, to the ends of which we tie beads, we make legs. It remains to embroider the angel's eyes and mouth. Optionally, you can make a nose and brow cheeks with a pencil.

Fabric angel





Such toys can be used as car pendants or as key rings. How to make a do-it-yourself angel from fabric? Above is a pattern that is proposed to be printed or copied. First we cut out paper parts, and then we make blanks from fabric.

We begin to sew the angel from the torso. Two parts of the skirt are combined with each other, stitched, twisted and not stuffed. Now make a heart. We sew it and fill it, it will be the center of our toy. It's time to make a head. We sew two flesh-colored circles and fill the blank. You can immediately draw a cute face for an angel, for example, using two beads for the eyes and drawing cheeks with a red pencil. Hair can be made from either woolen threads or satin ribbons. We sew together the blanks of the head and heart. Cut out the wings. To keep their shape, a cardboard box must be inserted between the two pieces of fabric. And you can make wings of felt or any other dense fabric. Sew our blanks to the back of the angel. The only thing left is small - we make pens. We sew circles and after we fill them, it will be necessary to sew fingers on them. We attach hands and decorate the angel with bows and pendants.

Half circle angel



We will make a toy pendant from felt. We will begin to make an angel of fabric with our own hands by preparing a semicircle pattern. Circle it on felt and cut it out. Put the semicircle horizontally, cut away from you. Now we pull its two ends to the center and take them back a little. In this position, we fix the fabric with threads. We cut out the head. It will be two circles sewn together. We will make the angel a bang from the fabric, you can come up with any other hairstyle. It was the turn of the wings. We will make them from white fabric. Cut out something like butterfly wings or dog bones. Sew the blank to the back of the angel. It remains to detail the toy. Beads will be eyes, we draw cheeks with a red pencil, and we attach a large bead to the dress.

Simple angel



You can bring a three-year-old child to the manufacturing process of such a toy. If mom buys a blank in the form of a felt circle with a carved edge, then making a Christmas angel from fabric with her own hands will not be difficult.

We take our workpiece and divide it into three parts. Cut. One of the parts we will become the body, and of the other two we will make wings. Now cut out a circle of flesh-colored fabric. This is the head. An angel can be left in such a primitive form or decorated. For example, make him eyes, draw a pattern on the body and wings. If desired, a curly hairstyle can be made from woolen threads. From several of these angels you get a beautiful garland, which decorate the Christmas tree. The child will be especially pleased that he takes part in such a magical event.

Angel sachet



To make such a toy, you will need burlap and fragrant herbs. You can buy a ready-made collection or prepare it yourself in the summer. We will make an angel doll from a fabric according to a pattern. We print the picture proposed above, or we draw the outline ourselves. We cut two pieces from the burlap, stuff them with grass, and then manually (or on a typewriter) we sew the toy with an indent of half a centimeter from the edge. On the wings, you can make the line wavy. To do this, cut the workpiece and draw the contour of the future product with soap. You can sew with contrasting threads or choose them in tone. It remains to decorate our angel. We sew a small star or heart on the chest of the doll, and attach a ribbon to the head so that the sachet can be hung. At the request of the angel, you can build a hairstyle.

Angel with beads



We will make this toy from two different materials: felt and ordinary cotton fabric. We also need a silver thread and beads. We print out the pattern. We will make the angel out of fabric with our own hands in stages. First, we cut out all the details from the material. We sew the blanks together and stuff them. Sew wings to the back. As a decor for them, you can make a decorative stitch with contrasting threads. Now you need to sew handles and legs to the body. We will produce them in the same way. At the end of the thread we make a knot. We string the bead and sew the free edge to the body. Repeat this operation three times. Remember that the legs should be slightly longer than the arms. Now you need to decorate the craft. On the hem of the angel, you can write a wish or any name. You can also embroider your face and make a toy hairstyle.

Burlap angel



This craft resembles the toy of our grandmothers. Making a New Year's angel with your own hands from a fabric is quite simple. From cardboard we need to cut a conical billet and cover it with cloth. The burlap can simply be glued to one side of the cardboard. We turn the workpiece into a cone and fasten it with a hot gun or a stapler. Now you need to make a head. We cut out the circle, stitch it with the seam forward, we stuff and tighten the resulting bag. Sew the hole from the bottom and put the head on the body. We will make a hairstyle from threads that are similar in color to burlap. We cut small ropes and sew them in the center of the head so that the seam forms a parting. We will immediately make a nimbus pendant with a white rope. We build a loop on the rope and put it on the head of the angel. Sew the halo at the level of ¬æ of the head. The joint between the head and body is decorated with a piece of lace. From the rope with which the hair was made, we construct a bow on the chest. It remains to make wings. We will make them from a rectangle of burlap. We sew a piece of fabric along the edge of the lace. Now you need to pick up our workpiece exactly in the center and sew it to the back of the angel.

We collect an angel from cardboard and fabric



Such a toy can be hung on a Christmas tree, or you can decorate it with a fireplace, a nightstand and even a mirror. How to make an angel of fabric with your own hands? To do this, we need dense cardboard and embossed fabric. You can take a gold or silver piece of fabric. We begin to make an angel.
We print or draw a pattern and transfer it to cardboard and fabric. Cut the workpiece. With a glue gun we attach the fabric to the cardboard. In the right places we duplicate cuts. They should converge on fabric and cardboard. Now we turn the angel, as shown in the picture.

The manufacturing method of this toy can be slightly modernized. For example, make it double-sided. To do this, it is necessary to glue the fabric not on one side of the cardboard blank, but on two. And you can make wings and body from different materials. For example, stick feathers on the wings of an angel. They can be purchased at any needlework store, they are sold in rolls.
