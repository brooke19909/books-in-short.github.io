Havana: hotels, subtleties of choice

Cuba is a country of exoticism, color, amazing energy. If you want to relax and recharge with positive emotions, you definitely should visit here. If you, as a tourist, want to get acquainted with the culture and mores of the people of Cuba, you should stay in its capital, Havana. But before the trip you will have to book a hotel room so as not to stay on the streets upon arrival. Even if you plan to spend only night in the room, definitely, you are counting on comfort. Havana: hotels, the subtleties of their choice, reviews of real vacationers - this is what we will talk about in this material.





Determine the area

Of course, every tourist at the planning stage of his vacation should clearly formulate the goals that he pursues. It is from them that the choice of the area for living will largely depend. Hotels in Havana (Cuba) impress with their diversity: there are luxurious villas, apartments and more budget options in areas remote from the center.

Havana has some features that must be considered when choosing a hotel. This is a compact city, all its attractions are located close to each other. Here you can often see the luxury hotels and boutiques that Havana has to offer. Hotels located in the Old Town give a certain privilege to vacationers - free admission to most museums. But you must immediately prepare for the fact that here you are unlikely to find a budget option. The cost of daily accommodation in Havana hotels starts from 2500 rubles for a double room. Good budget options are the Casa Armando hotels (3 km from the city center), as well as House In Front of The Sea (2.5 km from the city center).





The center, when compared with the Old Town, offers Havana hotels, the prices for accommodation in which are more affordable. And all because the buildings here are mostly old and dilapidated. If you want to live within walking distance from all the attractions of the city, you are interested in its culture and history, but you are limited in means, the center will be your best option.

The following areas are: Vedado and Plaza de la Revolucion. This is a few distant areas from the central part, where modest hotels are located. It is quiet, calm, and if necessary, you can always reach iconic places. Excursion groups of tourists, as well as businessmen, most often stop in the areas of Miramar and Playa, located a 10-15 minute drive from the center. If you want to stay close to the airport, you better know that there are practically no hotels here - it is better to immediately abandon this idea.

4 * Hotels

If you want to live in comfort, but do not spend a huge amount, pay attention to the four-star hotels in Havana. The reviews of tourists confirm that most of them offer really excellent conditions for living.





Perhaps the most famous hotel located in the city center is Inglaterra. Over the past 130 years, it has attracted the attention of all connoisseurs of culture and history, while being an architectural monument of a city such as Havana. You will certainly find hotels with the best service, but if you choose this option, you will not regret it.

All lovers of colonial style will surely love the Sevilla Hotel, located near the Havana Museum of Art. The hotel offers stunning views of the architecture of the Old Town and its most famous sights.

A day in a double room of the "standard" category will cost tourists 23,000 rubles.

This is really a chic option with spacious and bright rooms, quality food and a range of additional services.

The cost of daily living in a double room with breakfast will average 21-22,000 rubles.

To summarize

What does Havana offer its guests? Hotels, their location and cost are, of course, an important topic. But choosing the suitable option for ourselves, we recommend starting from the city district, and only then select a room in accordance with your financial capabilities. In general, the city has enough options for every taste and budget, therefore, problems with the choice should not arise.
