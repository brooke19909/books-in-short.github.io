Hotel near the Kurskaya metro station: description, guest reviews, photos

Kursky Station is the largest transport hub in Moscow. Every day, passengers from all over Russia and neighboring countries arrive here. To begin to conquer the capital, you need to relax, put in order yourself and thoughts. Hotels near the Kurskaya metro station will come to the rescue.

Hotel Mamaison All-Suites Spa

The hotel is located on one of the most popular streets of Moscow - Pokrovka. Passengers arriving at Kursky Station will not have to take a taxi because the hotel is a 20-minute walk at a slow walking pace.

The level of the hotel complex is five stars. This means that the staff is ready to offer its guests a range of exclusive services for comfortable accommodation. The design of the hotel is made in a luxurious Art Deco style, and the staff is ready to provide a room of any configuration for both people traveling alone and for families.

Also within walking distance are the main attractions of the capital: Nikolskaya Street, Red Square, GUM, the Bolshoi Theater.

The hotel has its own swimming pool, Russian bath, Turkish hammam. If you are leading an active lifestyle and playing sports, you should definitely be on your daily schedule, you can freely use the gym with modern sports equipment.

A wide range of personal care services will delight travelers. Experienced specialists of the center will offer a choice of several types of massage and spa treatments. As for food, no one will be hungry at this hotel.

Guest reviews at Mamaison All-Suites Spa are extremely positive. Residents note a convenient location, free parking, which is important, as well as a children's play area where you can safely leave the child and do personal work or work. This is the best hotel near the Kurskaya metro station. The exact address of the hotel: Pokrovka St., 40, building 2.

Mini-hotel "Bonjour"

One of the comfortable hotels near the Kurskaya metro station is called Bonjour. It is located on Kazakova street, 8, building 2. You can get to it on foot along the main street.

The hotel has three stars and provides services in accordance with the standards. Shuttle services are available. If you would like the hotel staff to meet you at the airport or train station, you must inform the hotel one day before your expected arrival.

The hotel offers luggage storage in a safe. There is a restaurant and bar. Each room, regardless of status, has a TV and high-speed Internet.

The Hermitage Hotel

Another hotel near the Kurskaya metro station is the Hermitage, located within walking distance from the Kursk station. The hotel has four stars and is always glad to guests who appreciate comfort and efficiency.

The exact address of the hotel complex is: Durasovsky Lane, Building 7, Building 1. A convenient location in the center of Moscow allows you to enjoy historic buildings, take a walk to Red Square or dine in the historic Metropol Restaurant, which is a 15-minute walk.

The hotel offers many services needed by travelers. In addition to necessary, such as dry cleaning and a buffet restaurant, there are relaxing areas: spa, pool, gym, sauna.

Hotel rooms are designed for a wide range of guests. There are deluxe and junior suites for those who wish to spend time in comfort, as well as comfort rooms for those who value convenience without frills.

The hotel provides business services. If you need to negotiate or solve urgent business issues, several conference rooms are at your service.

The guest reviews of the Hermitage Hotel form a pleasant impression. Everyone is happy with the location, the presence of a sports complex. Visitors also note the work of the restaurant, where you can order dishes of Arabic, Chinese, European cuisine.

Hotel "Agios"

Hotels in the Kurskaya metro area in Moscow vary in scale and quality of service. However, in order to spend several nights in the capital, a small, comfortable hotel, such as the Agios mini-hotel, is enough.

Agios is located on 16 Vorontsovo Pole Street, Building 4. This is a five-minute walk from Kursky Station and the famous Atrium Shopping Center. The convenient location of the hotel allows you to get to the main attractions of Moscow - Red Square and Zaryadye Park in 20 minutes.

The hotel has three stars, and this is enough to comfortably spend several days in the capital. The hotel offers its guests a wide range of services: free internet in all public areas of the hotel, a restaurant, dry cleaning, parking, and a safe.

Guests in their reviews thank the hotel for having hair dryers and a minibar in the rooms. Also a big plus, guests call the availability of non-smoking rooms.

The disadvantages of the hotel include non-round-the-clock service, as well as the possibility of charging a deposit for hotel accommodation.

Mini-hotel "Retro"

For budget travelers, there is also a hotel in the Kurskaya metro area. This is a mini-hotel "Retro".

The hotel has two stars and is located on the street Old Basmannaya, house 7/1. This is the most popular option among tourists who want to save money on various entertainment. The hotel is located within walking distance from the metro, only about 20 minutes walk - and you are there.

The hotel offers its guests rooms without chic and shine. All rooms are equipped with towels, slippers and toiletries.

Guests of this bed & breakfast have a significant disadvantage in the location of the bathrooms. Shared bathrooms are not located in every room, but on the floor.

The pluses of this type of hotel include guests in the shared kitchen with the necessary household items, such as a microwave and toaster. Reception is open 24 hours a day. For those planning to register and immediately leave on business, there is a luggage storage room.

Hostel "Nice"

Another budget accommodation option at the Kurskaya metro station is the student hostel Nice. This type of accommodation is popular with students who come to enter the capital's educational institutions or just spend a weekend in the capital.

The exact address of the hotel is 38/40 Zemlyanoy Val Street, Building 6. There is a 24-hour reception desk. It is very convenient for arrivals at night.

The hostel has high-speed internet, a washing machine and hairdryer.

Especially the hotel will appeal to non-smoking guests. Here, smoking areas are specially organized, hoods and air conditioners are installed. Thanks to them, cigarette smoke does not cause any concern to non-smokers.

Free parking is available on site. You can use the safe deposit box at the front desk.

Loft hotel on Kurskaya

Hotels near the Kurskaya metro station in Moscow are designed to fully meet the needs of residents and guests of the capital. The hotel with modern design is also located near the Kursk station, at the address: Mentorship lane 17, building 1.

From the loft you can reach GUM and Zaryadye Park in 20 minutes, close to Red Square and the Bolshoi Theater. It features an outdoor terrace, high-speed Internet, and smoking areas. The kitchen area is equipped with modern appliances and is open to guests around the clock.

The hotel can accommodate pets if for some reason you had to take them with you. It is only necessary to warn staff in advance that a pet will be with you.

Restroom

If you are traveling in the capital and it is inconvenient to rent a hotel room, you can use the services of the rest room directly at Kursk Station. The administrator needs to show a ticket, pay for several hours of stay - and you can safely, without rushing, clean your clothes, take a shower or cook dinner in the shared kitchen.

The cost of services in an hourly relaxation room varies from 400 to 1000 rubles. In addition, all announcements at the station about the departure or arrival of trains are broadcast in the rest room.
