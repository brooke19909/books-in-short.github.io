How to pay taxes online. How to find out and pay transport, land and road taxes online

Payment of taxes - the procedure is far from the most pleasant, but necessary. As the advertisement, often played on public television, said, "Pay taxes and sleep peacefully." Therefore, if you want your sleep to not be disturbed, you will have to pay taxes, and preferably on time. However, until recently it was troublesome to do this - to go somewhere, fill out something, wait in line ... But now there are fewer reasons for sadness, because this procedure has become much faster and more comfortable. In order to save time and create convenience for taxpayers, the Federal Tax Service has implemented a service such as paying taxes online. Now you can go through all the stages - from the formation of a payment order to directly transferring money in favor of the Federal Tax Service - while sitting at home at your computer. And now we will consider in detail how to pay taxes via the Internet easily and quickly.





Updated service of the Federal Tax Service: "Pay taxes"

You should start with the simplest - go to the website of the Federal Tax Service and go to the "Pay taxes" section. This will open the page, which is the starting point for all further actions by taxpayers. From here you can go to specialized sections depending on the purpose of your visit. Moreover, the services are offered both for individuals and legal entities, as well as individual entrepreneurs.

Online services for citizens and legal entities

Citizens wishing to use the online service of the Federal Tax Service may:

  • find out your debt;
  • pay transport tax, land or road;
  • pay state duty.

Individual entrepreneurs and legal entities can also pay a state duty or fill out an appropriate payment order to transfer taxes to the state.





Online service "Payment of taxes of individuals"

If you are not sure if you have a debt to the budget system of the Russian Federation, then it is better to check it first using the corresponding service. Then you can proceed to the main stage, which interests us - how to pay taxes via the Internet. We follow the link "Payment of taxes of individuals" and get to the page of the Internet service for taxpayers-individuals. What can be done here?

Service Features

First of all, without waiting for a tax notice, you can independently generate a payment document for the payment of a transport or road tax or in order to pay land tax via the Internet. In addition, documents can be prepared to pay debts for these types of taxes, if any.

Then there are two options: print the order and go to the bank to deposit funds in favor of the Federal Tax Service or continue using the online service and make a cashless payment.





Preparation of a payment document

In the first case, a payment document is simply generated. This will require the following data:

  • personal data of the taxpayer;
  • type of tax (transport, land, road);
  • address of the object of taxation.

After that, the remaining information for payment will be generated automatically. Next, the document is sent for printing.





Cashless payment of taxes

We choose the second option. Here is an instruction with a detailed description of how to pay taxes online:

  1. If you decide that you will pay by bank transfer, then follow the link above and fill in the following details: and TIN. The latter is needed in order to check if you have any tax arrears (for non-cash payment, this field is required).
  2. Click the "Next" button and proceed to the selection of the tax we need from the proposed list. You can pay the road tax via the Internet, transport and land, as well as personal income tax and property tax.
  3. Having chosen the necessary item, we proceed to fill in the information about the place of residence with the indication of the subject of the Russian Federation and a specific address (in the process of filling out drop-down lists will appear, from which you need to choose your option).
  4. We also choose the type of payment (tax / fine / penalty) - in our case it is "tax".
  5. We move on. We determine the payment method - "Bank transfer" and go to the next page to select a credit institution.
  6. We determine the bank through which we will pay by clicking on the appropriate logo. Credit organizations in which it is currently possible to pay taxes via the Internet: Sberbank of Russia, Gazprombank, Promsvyazbank, Center-Invest Bank, ATB and others. These banks have concluded an agreement with the Federal Tax Service of Russia on the sale of services through their online banking. It is important to note here that you can use the online service only if in one of the proposed credit organizations you have a personal account in the Internet bank. If there is none, then before you pay taxes via the Internet, you need to conclude an agreement with your bank to provide the appropriate service.
  7. If everything is in order, the online service of the Federal Tax Service will transfer you to the page of your Internet bank, from where you can make a payment.

You will only have to log in and confirm the payment using SMS. After a day, the information on the tax paid must be changed on the website of the Federal Tax Service.





Non-cash tax payment through Qiwi system

For those who do not want to activate the Internet banking service, there is another option - to have a Qiwi wallet. You can do this on the official website of the service. Pre-replenish it with the amount of tax that you plan to pay (you can pay more - the money will not be lost, but can be used to pay for any other services). So, you have a qiwi wallet, now go to the website of the Federal Tax Service. Step-by-step instructions for paying taxes using the Qiwi service:

  1. We repeat all the steps discussed above, and when we get to the choice of a non-cash payment method, click on the Qiwi Bank logo (which is presented among credit organizations).
  2. We get to the authorization page, where you need to enter your mobile phone number and password from the qiwi wallet. Fill in your details and click "Login."
  3. In the window that opens, fill out information about yourself and agree to the terms of the offer (put a checkmark).
  4. Next, go to the list of taxes that you can pay. If you had a debt, then the first line will be the main amount, and then - the penalty / late interest.
  5. First, select the first line and pay for the main part. To do this, select the method (terminal / wallet / card) - mark the icon in the middle (payment from a qiwi wallet) and click the "Confirm" button.
  6. You should receive an SMS message with a confirmation code, which we enter in the field that appears below, we confirm the action again.
  7. After that, you will be taken to a page with information that the payment was successful.
  8. If you had fines and penalties, then you need to return to the line selection item and pay all your debts in the same way.

This procedure may seem a long one, but in fact it will take no more than 10 minutes, while paying in cash is much longer and not at all as comfortable as using the Internet.





Paying taxes is now easy

Due to the fact that now you can find out the taxes (via the Internet) that you need to pay and pay them in a non-cash way, you can do this on time and avoid delays. This will help you save your time, nerves, as well as money that could go towards paying fines and penalties arising from the constant postponement of such an unpleasant and tedious procedure before. Pay taxes on time - be a responsible citizen of your country.
