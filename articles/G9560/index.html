Club "Crystal" (Kemer): description and advantages

Crystal Club (Kemer) is considered one of the best recreation areas on the Mediterranean coast of Turkey. The institution cordially opens its doors to guests from various parts of the world. It is here that you can get a lot of positive emotions, mood and meet interesting people. Today, the Crystal Club (Kemer) offers its guests a fairly wide range of services.



A restaurant

Crystal Club (Kemer) invites its guests to taste delicious delicacies, dishes of traditional Turkish cuisine, as well as dishes that are widespread in Europe. Literally everyone can find for themselves a delicious treat, which will be prepared by a talented and creative chef with an authoring approach. In the restaurant menu you can find:

  1. Salads (vegetable, with seafood, cheese, meat).
  2. Snacks (with shrimp, octopus, vegetables, cheese).
  3. Rolls and sushi.
  4. Soups (borsch, okroshka, gazpacho, vegetables, miso, with seafood).
  5. Meat and poultry dishes (saj, kebabs, steaks).
  6. Seafood Dishes.
  7. Homemade dumplings.
  8. Noodles (egg, buckwheat, rice).
  9. Side dishes.

Sweet tooth can enjoy delicious desserts, namely - ice cream, souffle, cakes, halva or chocolate fondue with fruit. If you want to try something new, you can safely order creamy fondue with shrimp or chicken. The alcoholic card of the restaurant-club presents a wide selection of alcoholic drinks and all kinds of cocktails.





Banqueting hall

For festive events, the Crystal Club invites you to use the banquet hall, which is made in a classic modern style. Soft shades of champagne, fresh flowers and snow-white dishes will help create a pleasant festive atmosphere. The banquet room allows you to have beautiful and unique receptions, family parties, corporate parties, birthdays and weddings. If guests wish, the services of a photographer can be provided, and the hall is decorated with those attributes that are necessary for the holiday. There is a special festive menu for the banquet hall.

Karaoke

Crystal Club (Kemer, Turkey) offers to demonstrate their singing talents in karaoke, where there are a large number of popular songs in English and Russian. In karaoke there is a professional sound and lighting equipment, which allows you to enjoy great sound. The design of the room is stylish and unobtrusive, thanks to which a cozy and comfortable atmosphere is created in the hall.

Chillout

Crystal Club (Kemer) has arranged for its guests a chill-out zone, in which soft poufs and mini-tables are located. The chill-out zone allows you to relax in the company of friends, with loved ones or family. Thanks to aromatic hookahs and delicious drinks, you can relax from the hustle and bustle of everyday life. Friendly staff will help with the selection of hookah tobacco and prepare it for use. If desired, guests can enjoy delicious fruit, cheese and vegetable snacks.



Disco

Crystal Night Club (Kemer) is also a large dance floor, the stage of which is equipped with high-quality equipment (lighting and sound). During a disco, a wide variety of special effects can appear. Literally every day, bright and exciting show programs and concerts are organized here. To create a truly interesting and varied club atmosphere, both Turkish and Russian, Ukrainian popular DJs and dancers are invited who will definitely not let the club guests get bored.



Guests of the Crystal Club disco are invited to taste delicious alcoholic and non-alcoholic cocktails, meat, seafood and vegetable dishes, as well as fruit and cheese snacks. The pricing policy is democratic in comparison with other night clubs located on the coast of Antalya. On the beach there are a large number of sun loungers with soft pillows, as well as large umbrellas that will help to shelter from sunlight. In the shade near the beach there is a playground with modern durable equipment: slides, carousels and swings, where children can play, and parents can relax at this time.

Club guests can spend their time relaxing on the KLUB KRISTALL beach. It is here that you can enjoy beautiful and bewitching landscapes, taste delicious cocktails and listen to music that will be presented by professional DJs. Crystal night club (Kemer), the reviews of which are extremely positive, will give not only a good mood, but also a feeling of joy.

The popular club "Crystal" is a great place not only for a great vacation, but also for meeting many interesting people from the most diverse corners of the world. If you wish, you can pre-book seats in a restaurant or on the veranda over the phone. Administrators will answer all your questions and comfortably place them in the club.
