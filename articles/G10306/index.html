Sheraton Maldives Full Moon Resort & Spa 5 * (Maldives): room description, service, reviews

Sheraton Maldives Full Moon Resort Spa is a 5-star beachfront resort located on the private island of Furanafushi amid lush greenery and palm trees surrounded by a crystal clear turquoise lagoon, just a 15-20 minute speedboat ride from Male International Airport and 1 km from the nearest inhabited island. Sheraton Maldives Full Moon Resort Spa's signature amenities are ideal for couples, families and small weddings. The hotel underwent a complete renovation in 2017.

Sheraton Maldives Full Moon Resort Spa

It offers more than 176 rooms from beach cottages ranging from 52 m2 to surface villas of 220 m2 each, all of which are decorated in a luxurious tropical style.

Deluxe

From the comfort of your Sheraton Maldives Full Moon Resort Spa deluxe room, you can enjoy the beauty of the Indian Ocean. Sit on the terrace and soak up the rays of the Maldivian sun while listening to the sounds of the waves. The blinds are made of wood; tiled floors are laid out in the rooms. In hot weather, you can turn on the ceiling fan or air conditioning.

Island cottage

Enjoy the spaciousness and privacy of these wonderful Sheraton Maldives Full Moon Resort Spa deluxe island bungalows.

Their terraces overlook the garden, and the bathroom and shower are outdoors.

During relaxation, you will be surrounded by white sandy beaches and a shimmering turquoise lagoon, which will be an ideal place for a refreshing swim. The room has a king size bed, you can create another extra bed.

Beachfront Cottage

Beach Front Cottage - 76 m 2 bungalow located on the beach. Sit on the terrace with access to the beaches and bask in the rays of the Maldivian sun, listening to the sounds of the waves. You can always order food and drinks in your room so as not to disturb a peaceful stay.

Water Bungalows

Located above the shimmering waters of the ocean, Water Bungalow bungalows are ideal for those seeking privacy and romance. A private terrace is an ideal place to enjoy the gentle sun, and direct access to the sea will allow you to refresh yourself at any time of the day without any effort.

Club Water Bungalow

30 stilt houses are located in the lagoon at the eastern end of Sheraton Maldives Full Moon Resort Spa. Their residents can use the services of the Sheraton Club for free.

Ocean Pool Villa

The spacious Ocean Pool Villas are tastefully furnished with traditional rattan furniture. Located in the remote eastern part of the resort. The cleanest pool of 13 m2 and a large terrace are the perfect place to freshen up in hot weather, to feel at home. A private deck is equipped with a daybed and a hammock for relaxing, while an open-air shower will help you refresh yourself and relax after a day in the sun.

The hotel has one Water Suite, which is located above the water with a descent into the ocean.

Couples with small children can not live in buildings located on the water for safety reasons, but if the kids are over 8 years old, then you need to leave the administration a receipt that you assume all responsibility.

Shine Spa

Sheraton Maldives Full Moon Resort Spa 5 is a tranquil retreat in a tropical paradise in the Maldives. It is located on a separate tiny island, which is connected to a wooden bridge. Indulge yourself and enjoy signature treatments in an atmosphere of peace. Here you will learn exactly what a real Balinese massage is, although it costs a lot. The salon opens at 10:00 and is open until 22:00 seven days a week by appointment.

Here you can take extremely pleasant and useful Ayurvedic treatments, make a body scrub, wrap, various types of massage, manicure and pedicure, visit the steam room.

Nutrition

At Sheraton Maldives Full Moon Resort Spa, you will find everything from continental Mediterranean cuisine to Thai delicacies. Two lounge bars are also ideal places to sit, drink and relax when the sun sets over the Indian Ocean.

Room service is carried out 24 hours a day, you will bring breakfast, dinner, drinks at any time. In addition, meals at Sheraton Maldives Full Moon Resort Spa are served in 7 unique restaurants and bars.

Entertainment and sports

A modern hotel gym and Sheraton Hotels fitness center are open 24 hours a day. Personal yoga classes, cardio training according to an individual program are offered. There is a developed sports infrastructure on neighboring islands. You can go sailing or scuba diving.

Swimming

The Sheraton Full Moon Beach Tropical Freshwater Pool is open from 08:00 to 18:00. You can always get fresh towels, enjoy the private pools in the villas and the magnificent clean white sand beach.

The island has a fitness jogging trail. You can go rowing or water skiing, kayaking. A paved tennis court is lit in the evening. There are conditions for playing table tennis.

There are small coral reefs near the shore, near which colored fish and stingrays swim.

Family and kids activities

The island offers excellent conditions for families with children aged 4-12 years. There is a children's adventure club with a playground.

You can join the team to take part in competitions, work out or just have fun. Come and play with your family or with new Maldivian friends.

Excursions from the island

From the island you can go on a cruise with dolphins, book a trip for private fishing or a boat trip on a yacht. It is convenient to go on excursions to the capital of Bali from here, you can visit a Friday mosque, go to an island dinner or a picnic.

Water sports

You do not need to be a superhero to fly. Fly Boarding allows you to experience the thrill of flying a jet engine with a watercraft. This is an impressive sport that has recently gained particular popularity. Instructors are ready to take you into the air, whether you are a beginner or an experienced athlete.

Kiteboarding

Kite Boarding is fast becoming one of the most popular water sports in the world. A powerful wakeboarding parachute will give you an unforgettable experience. Professional kiteboarding instructors are ready to tell everyone what Kite Boarding is. The pristine lagoons of the Maldives with their open spaces create ideal conditions for both amateurs and professionals. On water, a kiteboard is like a wakeboard or surfing.

May weather

In May, when the Russians have many days off, some will want to realize their dream and fly to the Maldives. And it is real. But whether you can enjoy your vacation fully depends on how quickly the Maldivian spring ends and the next wet season begins. The southwestern monsoon to the hulhanga will come into its own, bringing rain and wind, and the serene ocean begins to storm, large waves rise. It is impossible to guess the weather in the Maldives in May - whether you have sunny days or not depends on nature.

If the onset of bad weather is delayed, you can swim and sunbathe. Despite the riot of nature, the heat does not subside and remains at the level of +29 ... + 32 ¬∞ C, and since the humidity goes off scale, rising above the 80% mark, breathing is difficult and very stuffy. From damp, clothes directly on the body become wet. Heavy rains pour mainly in the evenings and nights.

You can spend time on vacation doing snorkeling and diving, spa treatments and admiring the delightful May sunsets. Many believe that at this time of the year they are especially beautiful.

Even a healthy person needs several days to adapt, and for tourists with heart disease, blood vessels, respiratory tract, rest during the rainy season and high humidity is contraindicated.

Impressions of Russian tourists from holidays in the Maldives

According to reviews of Sheraton Maldives Full Moon Resort Spa, this hotel has beautifully maintained grounds and decent food. Mali is so close that it can be seen from several places. Some are glad that it takes only 15 minutes to get to it, others are not happy.

Some tourists lived in a bungalow with 4 rooms, and they say that the partitions are too thin, so the great audibility prevented them from falling asleep. There are no such problems in the villas. Service at Sheraton Maldives Full Moon Resort Spa is at a high level and does not cause complaints. True, there is not always a Russian-speaking staff.

This is not to say that this hotel was lost in the vast ocean, but other islands with technical facilities and hotels are not far away. Those to whom this does not bother can get great pleasure from the rest. If you do not rest on the All Inclusive program, you will have to pay pretty decent money for the pleasures, for example, up to two hundred dollars for dinner in a restaurant.
