Geography

The entire territory of the Korean Peninsula north of the 38th parallel is the country of the DPRK. Decoding RK (RC) refers to the southern part, separated from the second half by wide rows of barbed wire, minefields (this is a demilitarized zone) and fortified areas. The country has two more frontier land borders: with China and the Russian Federation. The shores are washed by two seas: the Japanese and the Yellow. Pyongyang is the capital. Area - more than 120 thousand square kilometers. There is a lot of water in the country, its surface area is 130 square kilometers, but with drinking tight, it is not enough.

The climate is severe, it is very cold in winter and sultry in summer. However, there is another name for Korea - "Country of Morning Freshness", eloquently talking about the love of the inhabitants of the peninsula for their homeland, their ability to find and appreciate the pleasant moments of life.
