Such different coasts of Crimea

Crimea has long been the hallmark of Ukraine. Every year, millions of vacationers come here not only from all regions of the state, but even from countries near and far abroad. The resort attracts with excellent climatic features, beautiful landscapes, the presence of forests, mountains and two seas at once, healing mud, mineral waters and, of course, clean air that cures pulmonary diseases. The entire peninsula can be conditionally divided into four parts: the center, as well as the southern, eastern and western coasts of Crimea.



Each region of this amazing region has its own characteristics that must be recognized by those who come here not only to relax, but also to be treated. The most popular among tourists is the southern coast of Crimea, the infrastructure is most developed here, so the rest does not seem boring and monotonous. Subtropical trees, high mountains, vineyards will not leave anyone indifferent. Mountain air and a dry Mediterranean climate make this place a wonderful health resort for people suffering from diseases of the nervous, cardiovascular systems and lungs.

The best resorts, ready to receive visitors at any time of the year, are located along the coast of Crimea. Swimming in the sea, climatotherapy, viticulture - all this is at the service of vacationers. A huge number of attractions, sunny and warm summer days, breathtaking landscapes, healing air attract annually a huge number of tourists. South Coast is ready to provide an elite vacation, there are comfortable hotels, expensive restaurants and nightclubs.



Those who wish to have a good rest and at the same time save money are advised to go to the western coast of Crimea. There are no forests and mountains, but only the sea and the steppes, but the healing air replaces any beauty. The steppe spirit mixes with the sea breeze, charging with life-giving energy, it is easy to breathe here at any time of the year. Health-improving centers are always ready to receive patients and cure them of any diseases. You feel comfortable here even on the hottest days. It is so pleasant to inhale the full chest with ionized, salt-filled air with the aroma of steppe herbs.

All Crimean coasts are good, but for an inexpensive vacation, the western one is most suitable. Tarkhankut is becoming more and more equipped every year, tourists are attracted by the picturesque cliffs of Cape Dzhangul and Atlesh, as well as comfortable climatic conditions.



For the romantics, East Crimea remained. Here you can freely admire the blue bays, fabulous grottoes, mountains, cliffs. In addition, here is the birthplace of famous cognacs, wines and champagne, which are supplied from the cellars of Prince Golitsyn. It breathes easily, and the presence of mineral springs and therapeutic mud allows you to heal your body. On the eastern shore, you can enjoy everything at once: the sea, forest and mountains. Absolutely all the coasts of Crimea are worth visiting, and in order to fully get acquainted with the peninsula, it is recommended to visit all its resorts, each of which is fraught with a mystery.
