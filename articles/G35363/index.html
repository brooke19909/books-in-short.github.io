What is the October badge. His appearance

In the Soviet Union, children 7-10 years old were admitted in October. This was the name of the members of the children's organization, whose task was to captivate the children with interesting activities and social life. Each member of this organization wore a special October badge. He was pinned to the left of his chest (at the heart) on a jacket, shirt, sweater, dress, school apron, jacket. Often October people wore badges not only at school, but also at home.
