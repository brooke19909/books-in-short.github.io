Guest rooms

The total number of rooms at the disposal of Sharm Grand Plaza Resort 5 * (Sharm El Sheikh) is 547. All apartments can be divided into several main categories:

  • Standard rooms are small rooms designed to accommodate two guests (one extra bed is also possible). All the rooms of this territory without exception have balconies with a view of the beach.
  • Superior - these are spacious rooms with two beds and a sofa bed. Depending on the number of storeys, the apartments have a balcony or exit to the terrace. It is possible to rent a room with pool or garden views.
  • Deluxe - this category of rooms has an elegant design, as well as a gorgeous view of the Red Sea, which opens from the windows of the apartments. This is perhaps the best apartment in the Sharm Grand Plaza Resort 5 *. Photos of tourists made at the hotel confirm this information.
  • Family room - characterized by an impressive area (58 sq. M.), Which accommodates several beds, a sofa bed and there is still room for extra beds. All rooms of this category have a sea view.
  • Junior Suite - a large double room with windows overlooking the garden or the sea.

All inclusive

Despite the fact that the all-inclusive system operates worldwide, it can vary significantly in different hotels. Therefore, in order to avoid disappointments and troubles, you need to ask in advance about what this service implies in a particular hotel. Sharm Grand Plaza Resort 5 * (Sharm El Sheikh, Nabq Bay) means a full three meals a day in buffet format, which is simultaneously available at once in two branded restaurants.

Between the main meals in the hotel bars, guests have the opportunity to satisfy their hunger with a variety of snacks, snacks and sweets. Also, from 10 a.m. to 11 p.m. non-alcoholic and alcoholic drinks are available to guests in unlimited quantities. Wine is served exclusively during dinner. You can taste imported drinks only in restaurants (for a fee).

The all-inclusive program does not apply to alcohol from 11 pm to 10 am, as well as during discos and other recreational activities.
