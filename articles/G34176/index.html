Villa Viana 3 * (Sri Lanka, Maravila): description, service, reviews

Sri Lanka is an island rich in interesting tropical nature, as well as vibrant attractions. It has several good hotels, in which every year the agencies sell a lot of tickets. One of the most popular among them is Villa Viana 3 *. The prices for rest in it are low, and the conditions are decent, as the tourists themselves say.

general information

This vacation spot was built in 2012. Due to the recent construction date, the hotel has a rather modern look and is perfectly equipped from a technical point of view. Since then, a small reconstruction was made on its square, during which the interior was improved.

In the description of Villa Viana 3 * the administration indicates that the hotel is perfect for a quiet family holiday. Many tourists come here with children, as they have created wonderful comfortable conditions.

Location

The hotel is located on the island of Sri Lanka in Indonesia. Favorable conditions for guests from different regions of the world reign here all year round. The ideal season for a holiday in Sri Lanka begins in December and ends in March. Here, at this time, there is a high temperature, significantly mitigated by the influence of oceanic air masses.

In Maravila, where the hotel is located, there is a considerable amount of entertainment presented by restaurants, cafes and night clubs. Many tourists visit them in order to have fun.

The nearest airport is Bandaranaike, which is 37 kilometers from the hotel.

Rooms

The fund of the hotel is represented by apartments of three categories: standard, superior and luxury. Each room has only modern furniture made from quality materials. The interior has a huge number of details made of dark wood. It also has several types of textiles - light and dense.

The rooms of any category have a private bathroom with modern plumbing. The bathroom is combined. The interior also has several mirrors and a pair of wardrobes, where towels, bathrobes and slippers are stored. Each guest is provided with a standard set of bath accessories, which includes soap, shampoo and gel.



Thanks to the air conditioners installed in the apartments, guests staying in them can independently create temperature conditions that are convenient for themselves, regardless of the current weather conditions. In reviews of holidays in Sri Lanka, one can often find the opinion that the use of climatic equipment is desirable both in the daytime and at night, since the region has a rather high temperature and humid air, and therefore it often seems somewhat stuffy .

Standard

Rooms of this type are located on a small area - 23 square meters. m - and are presented as a single room. These apartments are designed for a couple of guests, as a result of which they are quite popular among tourists who want to spend time with their soulmate. Guests in their reviews of Villa Viana 3 * note that from the windows of the rooms of this group you can observe a beautiful view of the vast oceanic expanses.

As the main furniture there is a set made of wood. It consists of a large double bed, a pair of pedestals attached to it, as well as a small wardrobe, which is divided into several drawers.

From a technical point of view, the apartments are equipped perfectly. Guests staying here have the opportunity to watch satellite channels on a plasma TV that hangs on the wall, as well as use the electrical substation for making hot drinks. Tourists can use a minibar and a safe for free, and a landline telephone is available for an additional fee.



Improved

This category of apartments is presented on larger sites - in 33 square meters. Often, guests staying with their whole families stop at them, since the number of beds here is designed for a couple of adult guests and one child. In the reviews of tourists you can observe a huge number of comments about the beautiful sunset, which can be observed daily from the window, while staying in such apartments.

There are two beds installed: one of the bottom is large and designed for two, and the second is a single. There is also a great place to relax, a dressing table with a mirror, as well as a huge wardrobe.

From a technical point of view, the rooms are arranged in the same way as the standard.



Suite

Apartments "Suite" offer guests the conditions for a superior stay. They also have places for children (two or one). For adults, there is a large double bed with hypoallergenic linen and an orthopedic mattress. This type of apartment has a private balcony with an excellent place to relax. Travelers in their comments say that on the chairs that stand on it, you can quite comfortably sit down and drink a cup of tea or coffee. You can also read your favorite book here.

Special attention is paid to the interior of the apartment. It is made in a rather interesting colonial style, using small carpets, a large amount of dark wood and original lighting fixtures. The room has a beautiful dining area, consisting of a small square table and three chairs with soft seats.



Conditions for children

Hotel Villa Viana 3 * (Sri Lanka) is perfect for families with children - in many comments, parents who rested in this place with their kids share their impressions of the organized conditions for the convenience of the youngest travelers.

First of all, they are aimed at providing places for sleep, which are full single beds designed for one person. For an additional fee, a cot can be placed for guests aged 4 to 12 years - this is done on a fee basis and upon prior request.

If desired, the baby can be taken to a small children's club, which is organized here - there the animators spend time with them. Many parents recommend using the services of a nanny, which are paid, but, according to vacationers, are quite effective.

On the open area there are places for a fun and safe pastime for children. One of them is a swimming pool, equipped in a common aquazone, and it also has a playground for games.

Often, tourists with children go here to relax, as you do not need to apply for a visa to visit Sri Lanka.

Fun and relaxation

Vacationers in Villa Viana 3 * in their reviews often note that the hotel offers a considerable amount of entertainment, thanks to which guests do not have to be bored. One of them is evening programs, which are held in the form of discos. Each time there are animators entertaining the audience. According to tourists, several people from the whole team speak Russian well, which is quite convenient for guests from the CIS countries.

If desired, travelers staying at this hotel can visit the spa center, which is located within the building. It offers hot treatments in the sauna and bath, as well as massage services in a special room. According to the majority, the masseurs working here are professionals in their field, in their work they use unique techniques that give an excellent effect.



If desired, while staying at Villa Viana 3 *, fans of sightseeing can join in small groups and, having hired a guide, go on a small tour. Private tours can also be arranged.

Service

In Villa Viana 3 * a wide range of services is provided for all guests, some of which are paid, and some are already included in the price of the ticket.

The hotel has the possibility of renting a car - in this case, a free parking space is provided for cars. On it, tourists recommend going to explore the beautiful nature inherent in the surroundings of the hotel.

If desired, guests can take their personal belongings to the laundry and dry cleaning. If necessary, medical care can be provided, as well as the possibility of using the conference room for business events.



Aquazone

A considerable amount of time travelers staying at the hotel spend within the aquazone. It works all year round, however, in the winter, more vacationers gather here than usual - this is due to the hotel‚Äôs excellent attendance during the holiday season. Sri Lanka has a rather high temperature, thanks to which the water in the pool warms up perfectly without installing any automated systems.

Around the niche with crystal clear water there is an open terrace, where guests can spend time on comfortable sun loungers, hiding from the sun under umbrellas. There is a small bar on the terrace, the menu of which offers a wide selection of drinks and cocktails.



Prices

The cost of rest in this place pleasantly surprises many travelers, since it averages about 110,000 rubles for two. This price includes a standard complex, consisting of ten days of accommodation for two in a standard room, as well as all transportation costs, taking into account the transfer from the airport of Bandaranaike. Registration of a visa to Sri Lanka is not included in this complex.
