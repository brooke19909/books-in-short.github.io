Lotus Friendly Hotel 3 * (Thailand, Samui): description, service, reviews

Hotel Lotus Friendly Hotel 3 *, the description of which will be the topic of our article, is located in Thailand, on the island of Koh Samui. It is built on the coast of the Gulf of Thailand. This small bed and breakfast fast hotel has appealed to many tourists who considered it an almost perfect value for money.



How to get there

How to get to this hotel? First of all, you will have to catch a plane, the next flight Moscow - Thailand. Air tickets can be bought to Bangkok, then by bus or train to get to Surat Thani, and then by ferry - to Koh Samui. There, in the central settlement of Tongsala, you will find various modes of transport - from taxis to songteos - that will take you to the hotel. Chaweng Beach, Samui

This resort has grown around the beach of the same name. This is probably the most popular place on the island. It is located in the east of Koh Samui and you can safely call it a local tourist Mecca. It is always full of life, even in the rainy season. In Chaweng, you can find accommodation for every taste - there are five-star luxury palaces and modest backpacker guesthouses at your service. The resort is divided into three parts - northern, central and southern. But these boundaries are very arbitrary. The Lotus Friendly Hotel 3 (Samui) is located on the northern tip of Chaweng, almost where it moves to another area - Bophut, more secluded, quiet and romantic. Therefore, we can say that the Lotus Friendly Hotel combines all the advantages of the location, since it is not noisy here, but at the same time is close to entertainment. North Chaweng has very picturesque beaches, and the only drawback is the proximity to the airport, from where planes take off every hour.



Where is the hotel located? What infrastructure is next to it?

Lotus Friendly Hotel 3 is located almost in the rainforest. This is the very end of the Chaweng region, so there are never too many people here. The hotel is located close enough to the airport of the island - to go there in just a few minutes. The beach of Chaweng Beach is a few steps from the hotel, but the hotel itself is on the second line. There is parking at the hotel, and various services and a restaurant are located in the lobby. Literally next to the hotel is a small round-the-clock Seven-Ileven supermarket, where you can purchase the necessary goods, as well as a May Friend massage parlor. Very close is the local hospital, where you can go in case of any problems. They treat inexpensively and expertly. Not far away is the Thai boxing stadium. The center is very close - you have to walk on foot for just a quarter of an hour, not really in a hurry.



Number of rooms

Hotel Lotus Friendly Hotel 3 * is quite small and comfortable. It has a total of 34 rooms. They are located in a three-story building. These are mainly standard and superior rooms for one, two and three travelers. There are also several family rooms for four. The rooms are spacious, well planned. The rooms are equipped with cable TV, a fridge, a kettle, a hairdryer in the bathroom, and toiletries and toiletries. Every day, two bottles of drinking water are delivered to the room. It catches Wi-Fi, and it works very quickly. You can watch movies online. The rooms are air-conditioned with balconies. Hot water is always there, with good pressure. Children under six years stay in the room free of charge if they are not provided with a separate bed.



Services

In the Lotus Friendly Hotel 3 * hotel you can exchange rubles for baht, book an excursion. The staff provides a special room to store the luggage of those travelers who leave late or arrive early in the hotel. In the evenings, they play billiards, and for trips around the island you can rent a motorbike. Internet catches throughout the hotel. A girl who speaks Russian works at the reception. But no matter what language the staff use, all employees are very conscientious and responsive, they will always help. They give good advice on what to do on Koh Samui, where to go and how to arrange it all cheaper. To clean the room, you need to say about it at the reception. Check-in is available from eight in the morning until ten in the evening. There is a laundry in the courtyard of the hotel.

Nutrition

In the restaurant of the hotel Lotus Friendly Hotel 3 * you can have breakfast. Serve eggs, bacon, sausages. rice in different forms - with chicken and shrimp, soups, vegetables, fruits. For dessert, toasts and pancakes. Delicious coffee. Buffet breakfast is served. Some guests tried to have lunch and dinner here, especially since the hotel kitchen offers both Thai and European dishes from a very professional chef. There is also Turkish cuisine. The portions are just huge. But tourists claim that constantly eating in a hotel restaurant is quite unprofitable. Moreover, there are a lot of small cafes and street food near the hotel with a huge selection and low prices. The most delicious Thai noodles are on the macaroons.



Beach and sea

The coast is very close to the hotel. But to get there, you need to go through the road and the territory of other hotels, and it is best to do this before 10 in the morning. Later, a passage through the cafe opens onto the beach. It is located directly opposite the Lotus Hotel. The entrance to the water is sandy, gentle, sometimes there are algae. There are strong ebbs, then it becomes very shallow. To fully swim, you need to move from the hotel to the right. The sand is clean and snow-white. There are few people on the beach. Towels for sunbathing are issued directly in the room and changed along with the rest of the laundry. At low tide, you can walk to small islands near the reef, you just need to be very careful - there are a lot of sea urchins in the water.

Excursions

Most often, guests of the Lotus Friendley Hotel are interested in beach vacations, as well as everything related to water sports, especially snorkeling and diving. Therefore, most of them go on such excursions, where you can dive and watch the life of the inhabitants of the sea. In addition, they still have various popular sea trips, such as, for example, a sightseeing on a boat around the island. If you are attracted to Buddhism and its architecture, then near the beach there is one very interesting temple - Wat Savang, painted in dazzling white. You can come there not only on an excursion, but also for the purpose of meditation and communication with monks. And near the airport there is another Buddhist attraction - Khao Hua Jak Pagoda. But only courageous and hardy can get there. The temple is located on a hill 90 meters high. But your efforts will be adequately rewarded - from the views of the island that open from the mountain, you just feel dizzy.



Lotus Friendly Hotel 3 *: reviews

The price of tours to this hotel is favorable, the place is very quiet, at the same time, bars and discos are very close. The hotel itself is cozy and conveniently located. The area and rooms are very clean, even local cats are all vaccinated. The staff is friendly. Breakfast is delicious, although some tourists complain that they are somewhat monotonous. But the majority writes that it is very good to relax in this hotel. Tourists express their gratitude to the staff and are glad that they did not lose the choice. The island itself is very beautiful - wonderful beaches, great night clubs, great and varied food. And staying at this hotel is a great bonus to everything else.
