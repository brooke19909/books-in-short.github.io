How to choose a hair dryer correctly: some useful tips

To create a beautiful hairstyle at home is quite easy. For this, it is not necessary to apply for the services of a hairdresser in beauty salons. You can use the devices that any woman has on hand: curlers, curling iron. In addition to them, a hairdryer is used for styling and drying hair. It would seem how simple it is to go to the nearest store and buy yourself such a simple device. But in fact, there are useful recommendations on how to choose a hair dryer correctly and not make a mistake.

Initially, before buying, you need to determine for yourself what purpose you are buying it for: only to dry your hair, or to style it, to give volume. In accordance with this, a choice must be made between hair dryers, diffusers, brushes and hubs.





Hubs are hairdryers equipped with a nozzle. It has the appearance of a narrow gap. With its help, you can perform various manipulations: to dry and style your hair. The concentrator, due to the fact that it gives out a powerful narrow stream of air, allows you to dry your hair well in separate strands. It is suitable for owners of curly hair, and with it you can easily style your hair.

If you need a hair dryer with nozzles (for example, in the form of a brush), then a styler is suitable. With its help, you can straighten curly curls, give them volume or curl. Due to the fact that these devices have low power, there is no risk of burning hair and their roots. For a hairdryer, you can purchase nozzles separately, as they have a narrow focus and are intended for certain purposes. Styler is a professional hair dryer, as its use requires special skills. That is why stylists use the device in their work.





The hair dryers-diffusers look very attractive. They are equipped with "fingers." With their help, you can create light waves, the hairstyle will receive splendor and attractiveness. The diffuser is suitable for owners of sensitive hair and women who do perm. Such a device does not give brittle hair.

Since you need to choose a hair dryer based on its characteristics, you need to pay attention to such parameters of the device as its power, price and functionality. The hub is the most affordable in terms of material, easy to operate, multi-functional and has great power. With it, you can very quickly dry your hair. But, if you have long curls and it takes longer to dry them, it is better to stop on a model with a lower power so as not to damage them. The best option is 1400 watts. An important criterion when choosing a device is its weight, because the lower it is, the more convenient its operation.





How to choose a hair dryer, prompts and the number of modes for a particular model. The most convenient option is if there are three or four of them. Hair dryers may have some very useful additional features. Ionization of air helps to remove excess moisture from the hair, smoothes its scales, and also neutralizes static electricity. If you want to determine for yourself how to choose a hair dryer to create a spectacular and beautiful styling, then stop on models that allow you to dry your hair in separate strands. To preserve the effect and fix the hairstyle, such devices are equipped with a supply of cold air.
