Hotel Rodina (Rostov-on-Don, M4): address, description, reviews

Today, many of our compatriots choose resorts of the Krasnodar Territory for their holidays. Some of these tourists prefer to get to the sea not by train or plane, but by private car.

Of course, this method loses time to the airplane, but it is very economical, especially if a whole family of three or more people is going to rest. In this case, you will have to spend only on fuel for the car, meals during the trip, as well as on rest at the hotel, if the trip turns out to be especially long.

The federal highway M4 ‚ÄúDon‚Äù, along which many residents of Moscow and other cities go towards the Black Sea, offers motorists various options for overnight stay and relaxation. These are modest motels and decent hotels, some of which are located in close proximity to the highway. One of the great places where you can rest during a long journey is the Rodina Hotel (Rostov-on-Don).

Road to the sea

The highway with the marking M4, which is called "Don", has a total length of 1,517 km. It originates in Moscow, runs through Krasnodar and Dzhubga and ends in the coastal city of Novorossiysk. Today, many sections of this route have become high-speed, which allows you to quickly cover the entire distance and find yourself on the Black Sea coast.





For example, getting to the Rostov Region by car from Moscow in a day is very real, even making small stops for snacks and other necessities of life. This is approximately 12-13 hours of travel, subject to all speed limits and with little respite. After such a long rally, I really want to relax and even sleep, especially for drivers.

It is for such travelers, as well as for numerous truckers throughout the route, there are various hotels and motels that are happy to take everyone under their roofs. They are available in the Voronezh region, and not far from Pavlovsk, and in the Krasnodar Territory, and, of course, in the Rostov Region.

Great rest on the track

Hotel Rodina (Rostov-on-Don, M4), located just 100 meters from the exit of the highway, will be a great place to relax after a long and busy road.

The building of this roadside hotel consists of three floors and is decorated with natural stone. It is made in the style of German houses, where the half-timbered construction often prevails. Hotel Rodina (Rostov-on-Don) takes on special beauty in the dark when the lights turn on.





The institution offers its guests free parking, in which there are places for cars of those who decided to relax in the rooms, as well as for adherents of camping. There is also a parking area for trucks and regular buses.

Travelers can satisfy their hunger at a local cafe, which serves a variety of dishes and drinks. Near the cafe there is a hygiene unit with toilets, showers and a launderette. This is a great service for those who prefer to spend the night in a car, but do not want to deprive themselves of basic amenities.

Throughout its territory, the Rodina Hotel provides guests with free wireless internet, so travelers will not be left without a connection.

The exact location of the hotel

Those who set off on the M4 highway will have no difficulty finding the Rodina Hotel (Rostov-Don). The address with the street name and house number, of course, is missing from him, but this does not prevent him from getting to it easily.





Tourists traveling by car just need to drive up to 1076 km of the M4 highway and just 100 m from it. About 10 km separate the hotel from Aksay and Rostov-on-Don, and the nearest settlements are Mayakovsky and Lenin farms.

Types of rooms in the "Homeland"

The Rodina Hotel (Rostov-on-Don) is not very large: it has only 54 rooms, but any of them will give travelers everything they need for a comfortable stay, albeit not very long in most cases.

All numbers here are divided into two types: 1 class and 2 class. Each of them is equipped with two single beds or a large double bed, bedside tables, a desk, air conditioning, an LCD TV, a minibar with a variety of snacks and drinks, a telephone, slippers, a hairdryer, a set of hygiene items. Also, as a compliment, guests are offered mineral water, coffee, sugar and its substitute and tea. The rooms do not provide wardrobes, but they are not particularly needed when people come here just to spend the night.





Rooms differ in that the rooms of class 2 are attic, due to which their cost is slightly lower.

If necessary, any room can be absolutely free of charge equipped with a cot, changing table, ironing board with iron, shower cap, shaving kit, bathrobe, comb and cotton products.

Hotel prices

Depending on the season of guests' stay, the cost of rooms available at the Rodina Hotel (Rostov-on-Don, M4) varies.

Prices per day in a room of category 1 start at 3500 p. in the low season, which in the "Homeland" lasts from September 16 to December 29 and from January 11 to May 31. In the high season (from June 1 to September 15 and from December 31 to January 10), the price of a room reaches 4500 rubles. Number 2 category will cost tourists cheaper: in the low season the cost will be 2500 rubles, in the high - 3900 rubles.





Moreover, the Rodina Hotel (Rostov-on-Don, M4) offers its guests accommodation at the daily rate, which for any room is 2000 rubles. For this money, you can relax here for one day from 10 am to 7 pm.

Traveler Reviews for Homeland

A fairly large number of our fellow citizens who travel for different purposes along the M4 highway choose the Rodina hotel (Rostov-on-Don) for overnight stay.

The reviews that tourists leave about him are in most cases very positive. Guests note the excellent location right next to the road, which eliminates the need to go somewhere far away in search of a place to stay. Also, everyone likes the provided set of things in the rooms. An undoubted plus is the cleanliness of the rooms and the fact that the hotel as a whole looks very well maintained and quite new.





However, the cost of rooms could be lower, because, in fact, everyone stops here only for a few hours. Someone notes that at this price it was possible to serve breakfast for free.
