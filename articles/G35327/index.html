How to get and how to use maternity capital up to 3 years?

Maternity capital is a form of state support for families with children, which operates in the territory of the Russian Federation from 2007 to the end of 2016. Financial assistance is provided to parents with more than 2 children. Under standard program conditions, you can cash out funds only after 36 months. Read more about how to use maternity capital up to 3 years.

For whom

Financial support from the state can receive:

  • A woman who, after 2006, gave birth (adopted) a second child.
  • A man if he is the only parent of a baby under the age of 3 years.
  • A minor child or student under 23 years of age - in case of termination of the right to receive state support of the adoptive parent, who is the only parent.

The amount of benefits is increasing annually. In 2015, it is 453,026 rubles.



How to get and use funds

It is necessary to provide a package of documents to the local branch of the PF:

  • statement;
  • passport;
  • insurance certificate;
  • birth certificate (court decision on adoption).

If a positive decision is made in a month, the applicant receives a certificate.

You can use the funds on:

  • improvement of living conditions (purchase of an apartment, repayment of a part of a mortgage, reconstruction of a house, compensation for construction, repairs, participation in a cooperative);
  • payment for the education of the child (also applies to payment for the services of an institute, university, residence in a hostel, and maintenance in a kindergarten).
  • the formation of part of the mother's funded pension.

According to general rules, funds become available after the second child reaches the age of three. But there are exceptions when maternity capital can be used up to 3 years. They will be considered in more detail below.

Additional terms

  • You can use maternity capital up to 3 years once.
  • In case of changing the size of state support, the certificate does not need to be updated.
  • The term for applying to the FIU is unlimited.
  • An application for the use of funds is submitted at any time.
  • MK is not subject to personal income tax.
  • Funds are provided to parents, not to a specific child.
  • Documents for the use of maternity capital up to 3 years can be submitted to any branch of the FIU, including through a proxy or by e-mail.
  • The certificate can be restored in case of theft. But after the death of the owner, he loses power.

Anti-crisis innovations

Having figured out how to cash maternity capital up to 3 years, we turn to the issue of using funds. In 2014, legislation was amended regarding the procedure for obtaining state support. In particular, from the total amount of 20 thousand rubles. can be received in cash and used for everyday needs. Such an anti-crisis plan is valid only for persons who have received the right to MK in the period from 2007 to the end of 2015. They must submit an application before March 31, 2016 to the FIU or the Multifunctional Center for Public Services (MFC). The same documents are attached to it: parent's passport, certificate, SNILS, bank details for the transfer of funds. The application is considered within a month. After making a positive decision, the money is transferred to a bank account within another 30 days.

The second change concerns families with two children. Such parents can use the money to pay for their education, treatment of a disabled child. But this item does not apply to couples who have twins. To receive money, it is enough to provide an application and a certified copy of the contract for the provision of paid services. Inheritance

One of the ways to use MK funds is to form part of the pension. If the parent dies within 10 years from the date of application, the income from the investment and the balance of MK will be paid to the second guardian or children. Without issuing an urgent payment, MK will be sent to the funded part of the pension without the right of inheritance.
