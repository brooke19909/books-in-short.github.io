Hotel Mountain Beach 3 * (Pattaya, Thailand): photos and reviews of tourists

Thailand is one of the most popular exotic countries for a beach holiday among our compatriots. Pattaya resort is perhaps the most famous place in the country. In our article, we will talk about the Mountain Beach 3 * hotel , which is located in the Pratumnak district, which is part of the resort area of ​​Pattaya.

A bit about the hotel ...

Mountain Beach 3 * is located in Cliff Bay, 200 meters from the coast. The hotel is surrounded by tropical vegetation. And at the same time, it is located near the center of Pattaya. The hotel complex consists of one seven-story building. You will have to get to the hotel from Bangkok Airport, which is 150 kilometers from the complex. The last repair in the institution was carried out in 2001, and in general the hotel began to work in 1998.

Number of rooms

The Mountain Beach 3 * room fund is represented by 319 rooms, among which there are Superior rooms (28 square meters), Deluxe (28 square meters), Junior (consists of a living room and a bedroom, 32 square meters), family apartments (consist of two bedrooms, the room area is 66 sq. m.).



All apartments of the hotel complex are equipped with all amenities, they include air conditioning, minibars, satellite channels, sets for making hot drinks. Most of the rooms have a view of the coast.

Institutional infrastructure

Mountain Beach 3 * is a three-star complex, the infrastructure of which is quite suitable for a comfortable stay. The hotel has two swimming pools, one of which is designed for children. For guests there is one restaurant, cafe and several bars. The hotel has a business center for business guests, which has a conference room for up to 250 people. Also on site there is a shop, beauty salon, car rental, tour desk. If desired, all tourists can visit the massage sessions or go to the sauna. There is also a gym on site.

Beach vacation

Mountain Beach 3 * (Pattaya, Thailand) is built on the coast of the bay. The road to the beach takes about five minutes. Tourists should know that this part of the resort is located on a mountain, so any movement is an uphill or descent. In order to get to the sea, you need to go down the long stairs, respectively, you also have to go back along it, but already go up.

A little bit about the resort ...

As we already mentioned, Mountain Beach 3 * (Pattaya, Thailand) is located in the Pratamnak area. The southern part of Pattaya is a very beautiful place. And the location of the area is quite convenient. The resort area is small and compact. This region is very popular with tourists from the post-Soviet space, so native speech here can be heard everywhere.



The nature of Pratamnak is a stunning combination of sea and mountains. In order to attract tourists to the resort, much attention was paid to the development of good infrastructure. Pratamnak boasts an excellent selection of shops, restaurants, cafes and other objects of interest to vacationers. Literally at every step you will see more and more new establishments.

If you move a little offshore, deep into Thai areas, you can see elements of the usual life of the local population. But it is worth considering that such walks do not always leave a pleasant impression. Still, it is much nicer to travel to the recommended tourist attractions. Pratamnak is equipped with observation platforms from which you can admire the sea views and take excellent photographs in memory of the time spent in an exotic country. Further in our article we will tell you which tourist sites are worth visiting.



Pratamnak as a separate resort area has several advantages, thanks to which vacationers choose it. Firstly, a busy highway does not pass through it, as in other parts of Pattaya. Secondly, the resort has a separate compact and isolated beach, surrounded on all sides by high cliffs. In addition, within walking distance are several of the main attractions of the city.

Pratamnak is a fairly quiet place. Here you will not be disturbed by the noise of night clubs and discos. The resort is good for children and their parents. In addition, it is believed that the sea in this area is much cleaner than in the center of Pattaya. And a well-developed infrastructure is quite conducive to a comfortable stay in an exotic country.

Despite such a number of positive characteristics, the area still has one drawback. Although it’s not far to get to the center of Pattaya, you will have to use taxi services all the time, since tuk-tuks do not go here, and there is no public transport. It takes about twenty minutes to get to the nearest stop where you can take a tuk-tuk. Such isolation of the resort makes it calmer and even safer in terms of theft than other regions.

Resort beach

Pratamnak Beach is not as big as other Pattaya beaches. But at the same time it is very beautiful and is closed from the winds by the mountains. On this stretch of the coast, only those tourists who live in the nearest hotels have a rest, so there is no noise and fuss, as well as large crowds. Of course, during the high season there are many vacationers here, but still not as many as in the center of Pattaya.



And in the evenings, the coast is generally very calm and quiet, because there are no noisy restaurants and cafes. To get to the nearest establishments, you must climb the hill by stairs. Top of the coast offers an incredibly beautiful view. A little further from the coast there are shops, restaurants and cafes, bike rental points, tour agencies, massage parlors and much more. Experienced tourists believe that Protamnak is a very calm and quiet resort, in contrast to the bustling center of Pattaya.

Reviews for Mountain Beach 3 *

Continuing the review of the hotel, it is worth discussing the reviews of vacationers who have already visited it. Mountain Beach 3 * (Pattaya) is a three-star complex in the heart of Pratamnak. Its location can be called very successful. As we have already said, this area of ​​Pattaya is a great place for a relaxing holiday. Well, if you are looking for nightly entertainment in the form of clubs and discos, then you should choose a different region. Here, of course, there are entertainments, but they are of a slightly different nature. But the night club has not yet been built.

The remoteness of Pratamnak provides many benefits for holidaymakers. The only drawback is the fact that local transport is presented only by taxi. According to vacationers, it is not very convenient. And the next tuk-tuk need to go twenty minutes. The rest of Pratumnak is a very convenient place to relax. Even the famous Walking Street can be reached from here in seven minutes.

Mountain Beach 3 * (Thailand) is located in a picturesque place and is a single seven-story building. The hotel building is located on a mountain, as Pratamnak itself extends into the mountainous terrain.



The hotel complex has a small compact area on which everything is within walking distance. The only place you need to go is the beach, because you need to go down to it and climb stairs. According to tourists, the number of rooms of Mountain Beach Hotel 3 * is quite good. However, you should not take apartments of the lowest category, because their condition is worse than rooms of higher categories.

Check-in at the reception takes a few minutes, after which your bags are sent to the apartment. There is no deposit at the hotel. By the way, if for some reason you did not like your room, you can always replace it with a room of a higher category for a small surcharge. At the reception they are always willing to make concessions.

Reviews about the number of rooms

The hotel rooms have everything for a good rest. As for the configuration of the rooms, safes are not available in all apartments. But if you wish, you can use the free reception. There is no minibar in the usual sense of the hotel. Its contents are limited only to drinking water, the supply of which is replenished by personnel. One bottle of liquid relies on one person. Moreover, it is worth knowing that the glass containers can not be thrown away, it is taken away by the maids for exchange. If they do not find empty bottles, then they will have to pay for them upon departure.

Not all apartments have balconies. But in the rooms there are large windows, which allows you to ventilate the rooms during periods of shutdown of air conditioners. Maids conduct daily high-quality cleaning. Towels and bedding are also changed quite regularly. Of the detergents in the bathrooms, there is only soap and shampoo, the stocks of which are also replenished regularly. As for tips, they are a completely optional phenomenon, and therefore do not affect the quality of cleaning.



The rooms have hot water around the clock. But the lighting of the rooms is a little dim. There are no chandeliers familiar to us on the ceiling, the rooms are lit only by night lights and wall lamps. In the dark, the rooms are twilight. But this does not interfere with a comfortable rest.

Nutrition Reviews

Mountain Beach Hotel 3 * (Pattaya), like all hotels in Thailand, offers its guests a choice of food options. It can be an ordinary breakfast or half board. According to reviews, Mountain Beach 3 * (Pattaya, Thailand) offers very high-quality food. So, for example, in the mornings at the buffet there are vegetables, herbs, ham, sausages, delicious fried nuts, fried eggs, omelettes, bread, four kinds of fruits and many savory traditional snacks and dishes. It is simply impossible to call such an assortment non-diverse. No less interesting dishes await visitors in the evening.

In general, there are no nutritional problems in Thailand. Here at every step you will find cafes and restaurants with delicious food. There are many such establishments near the hotel, so there is always a choice.

General impression of the hotel

According to tourists, the hotel makes the most pleasant impression, despite its three-star status. If you are intentionally looking for a hotel in the Pratamnak area, then you can pay attention to one of the best hotels - Mountain Beach 3 * (Thailand, Pattaya City). Pattaya is a well-known and popular resort that offers all kinds of entertainment, including the opportunity to visit attractions. Some of them are in the immediate vicinity of the hotel.



Tourists are very pleased with the vacation spent within the walls of the complex, noting a good level of service and pleasant staff. The hotel has a beautiful pool with a relaxation area. Guests are advised to visit the massage parlor and sauna.

Fun and relaxation

The hotel has free internet, which can be used not only in the lobby, but also in the rooms.

According to vacationers, the beach is quite clean, like the sea. However, from time to time household garbage nails here. In such periods, it is better not to enter the water. Will have to be limited to the pool or go to the islands. Local guides generally recommend swimming on the islands, since there is the cleanest sea and the best beaches. The hotel has a lot of tourists, who spend days and nights on trips and beaches, so they don’t bother other tourists with their noise. In general, the hotel complex makes the most pleasant experience, which means that it can be recommended as a decent vacation spot.

Near Pratamnak there are a number of attractions that you can see on your own, without any help from guides. One of them is the observation deck of the resort, where you can not only enjoy the views, but also take beautiful photos. In addition, on foot you can walk to the main place - the Big Buddha, and then go to the Bali Hai pier. As for the other interesting places in Pattaya, it is easiest to see them by renting a bike. This type of transport is incredibly popular here.
