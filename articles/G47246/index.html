How much does the iPhone 5 cost?

Every year the well-known Apple company pleases us with new models of its smartphones. Not so long ago, 4S was on sale, now it is 5S and 5C. Between them, the intermediate link was simply iPhone 5. This model has incorporated all the best from previous ones and a number of new products, the main of which - external - is a larger screen size and smaller body thickness. And how much is the iPhone 5 in Russia, other countries? What is its prime cost?

How much is iPhone 5 in Russia





An indisputable fact is that before the start of official sales of a smartphone in our country, its price reached one hundred thousand rubles and even exceeded this figure. The very first copies were sold for 116 thousand (with a memory of 64 GB). All this is due to the huge popularity of Apple products. Then it dropped to 28,000 rubles, and now this is a great device, the dream of many fans, can be purchased for 19,000. This is the shortest possible answer to the question: "How much is the iPhone 5?"

And now we will understand various nuances. The starting price was a little more than the previous model, iPhone 4S, since even visually gadgets are noticeably different. In the model under consideration, the display is enlarged to four inches, and the thickness, on the contrary, is reduced. In addition, the screen itself received a resolution of 640x1136 pixels, many characteristics were improved, a number of various improvements were introduced, connectors for accessories were changed, the weight was slightly reduced. It was not in vain that he was assigned a new, next figure - and he got an iPhone 5. The price is fully consistent with Apple's policies.

Is the price of the 5th iPhone overpriced

Answering the question "how much is iphone 5", you often hear comments about its overpriced. Many say that in the US it can be bought for only $ 200, but they forget that this is the cost along with a contract for 2 years, the minimum monthly payment of which is $ 80. Well, count how much it turns out - 2120 dollars! Therefore, it is profitable to buy unlocked devices. And here the prices are already comparable. Domestic retailers must also earn something!

And although there are already many competitive models of phones from other manufacturers with similar technologies and larger screens on the market, the 5th iPhone remains the favorite device for many. He occupied his niche in time and holds the primacy. The main thing is to release a new model every year, even more perfect and advanced. Which, at least so far, has been a success for Apple.
