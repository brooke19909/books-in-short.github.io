M4 highway: motels and hotels. Where to spend the night on the M4 highway

The condition of roads in Russia is a separate topic that is familiar to all motorists. Somewhere they are better, somewhere worse, but overall the picture is not too bright. However, there are some sections that are simply created in order to rush with the breeze. One of these is the federal highway M4 Moscow - Voronezh. This greatly facilitates the movement between cities, which means that the number of motorists on it will always be quite large. And since the distances have to be covered very decent, night rest becomes relevant. Therefore, motels are located along the entire length of the route. The M4 is a busy freeway, especially familiar to travelers.

Criterias of choice

It is impossible to list all hotels and pensions; more than 1540 km is the length of the M4 highway. Motels are located every few kilometers, so travelers will not have a problem with an overnight stay. To reduce the number of frames to one article, as well as give the traveler real guidance, we will limit the choice to the following criteria: availability of parking, free cancellation option, proximity to the highway and comfort.



Moscow region

We will consider it in separate sections, due to the enormous length of the M4. We begin to consider the motels directly from the capital. And the first on our list is the Yamskoy Hotel. It is located in the village of Yam, st. Postal, d.17, Domodedovo. Of the benefits include spacious rooms and free parking. In addition, the location itself is very convenient for the traveler. He stands right on the M4 highway. Motels are also more distant from it, but they are not very convenient for a person who is tired of the road.

Another plus is the location near the airport, so it is perfect for air travelers. Of the minuses - the rooms are quite noisy, this can easily be explained by the proximity of a busy highway.

Hotel Don

This cozy hotel is located 13 km from MKAD. The hotel is located on the third floor of the road service complex. The building is equipped with an elevator, so you do not have to lift personal belongings manually. All rooms are equipped with modern furniture, household appliances, always have a bathroom. Rooms include double, triple and quadruple rooms. The cost of living starts from 2500 rubles. Hotel Don provides round-the-clock room reservation service, which is very convenient. On the ground floor there is a cafe. In addition, the complex includes a car service, which is extremely convenient for those traveling by car.



Pension "Sokolova Deserts"

And we move on. Stupino is 79 km from Domodedovo Airport. When you reach it, you can relax in a great place. This is not just a hotel, but a recreation center amidst a pine grove. In addition to comfortable rooms, guests are also offered ski equipment, so you will have something to do in your spare time. It offers wooden cottages and cozy rooms with a fridge, satellite TV and a private bathroom.

Sokolova Pustyn offers its guests to visit a cozy dining room and bar, as well as a mini market. There is a games room with billiards, table tennis and darts. On-site activities include fishing and hiking. The cost of living is from 2200 per day.

Tula region

And we continue to consider roadside hotels and move further along the highway. At the 143 kilometer you will find the Slavyansky Motel. The cost of living is from 2500 rubles per day. Convenient conditions for parents with children. Toddlers under 7 years stay free of charge without a separate bed. True, if you book not a full house, but a separate room, you will have to use the toilet in the hallway. Of the benefits, tourists note the courtesy of the staff. The cost of living can be reduced due to the lack of hot water and other inconveniences.

Hotel Ryabinushka

At 238 km of the highway is another cozy roadside hotel in the suburbs of Boroditsk. The cozy complex includes 15 rooms with all amenities. Each of them is equipped with modern furniture and high-quality plumbing, a TV. The hotel has a very good cafe where visitors can order a full meal. Truckers stop here quite often. They are attracted by low prices and excellent food quality. In addition, there is a car wash and laundry, shower and tire service.

All apartments have a private bathroom. Free parking is provided, as well as laundry and ironing services.

Instead of a conclusion

The M4 highway is a huge highway in its length, which in the summer is extremely busy. And with the end of the warm season, the influx of cars does not drop too much. Therefore, the hotel business is booming. Today we examined only a small part of the motels and boarding houses that are ready to provide travelers with an overnight stay. All of them have good ratings and customer reviews who have stayed here. Therefore, if you are planning a trip to M4, you can safely save your information. Do not forget to get a good navigator, it will help you find the right object on almost any section of the route. Cards do not always help out; modern devices are more reliable. This busy track is printed on all electronic maps, and the data is constantly updated.
