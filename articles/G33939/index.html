Hotel "Uktus" (Yekaterinburg): address, description, rooms, photos and reviews

When you are in an unfamiliar city on a business trip or travel, it is important to find a hotel in which you will not only be provided with comfortable conditions for living at an affordable price, but also next to which you will find the necessary infrastructure. Hotel Uktus in Yekaterinburg fully complies with the requirements of the most demanding traveler.

Description

The hotel has a category of two stars, has a favorable location. At a walking distance from it is the water park "Limpopo" and the ski complex "Uktus". It takes only ten minutes to get to the Ekaterinburg-Expo exhibition center. If you want to make the necessary purchases and relax, you need to drive two stops to the shopping and entertainment centers Globus and Airship. The nearest metro station is almost three kilometers away. Less than one kilometer from the hotel, the Iset River flows, it can be seen from the windows of some rooms.

For accommodation in the hotel "Uktus" rooms of different levels of comfort are provided. In addition to individual settlement, orders for accommodation of groups of up to 270 people are accepted. The hotel has its own cafe, as well as a shared kitchen where you can cook your own meals. Here you can live with your pet at no extra charge. The Uktus Hotel is located in Yekaterinburg, at the address: Proninoy Street, 25. The distance to the nearest airport and railway station is 10 kilometers.

Guest Accommodation

Accommodation is offered to guests in rooms of several categories:

  • single standard - 2000 rubles per day;
  • double standard - 2000 rubles per day;
  • triple economy class - 1800 rubles per day;
  • general female room - 600 rubles per day per person;
  • general male room - 600 rubles per day per person;
  • Junior Suite - 3400 rubles per day;
  • family - 3200 rubles per day.

All rooms except economy class are inclusive of a continental breakfast. There is an elevator at the Uktus Hotel in Yekaterinburg to access the upper floors.

Room Amenities

Standard rooms are equipped with the following amenities:

  • TV
  • an electric kettle;
  • wardrobe.

Superior rooms (junior suite and family) also have a refrigerator and a sofa. The aforementioned rooms at the Uktus Hotel in Yekaterinburg (see photo in the article) have a private bathroom. In economy and budget rooms, in addition to single beds, washbasins and wardrobes are installed, and the use of the toilet and bathroom is shared.

Hotel Uktus (Yekaterinburg): vacancies

The hotel holding at the Uktus Hotel invites an administrator with a salary of 18,000 rubles. For this vacancy, a number of requirements are put forward:

  • secondary specialized or higher education;
  • experience is desirable (administrator, secretary, office manager);
  • the ability to work with a personal computer, knowledge of cash discipline;
  • potential administrator must be sociable and responsible, competent speech is required.

The following conditions are provided by the employer:

  • training is carried out at the expense of the company;
  • salaries are paid on time twice a month;
  • administrator is provided with preferential meals;
  • the work schedule is set one day after three days;
  • employment is carried out officially, with the appropriate social package established by the Labor Code of the Russian Federation.

The hotel administrator is required to:

  • meet and accommodate guests;
  • Receive incoming calls
  • handle documents, prepare cash statements;
  • advise guests on the services provided;
  • resolve conflicts arising during his shift.

On the issue of employment at the Uktus Hotel in Yekaterinburg by phone or through the site, you must contact the contact person.
