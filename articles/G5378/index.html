Golden 5 Topaz Suites Hotel de Luxe 4 * (Egypt / Hurghada): photos and reviews of tourists

Egypt is a magical country that offers a fun beach and sightseeing vacation at any time of the year. And many tourists choose Golden 5 Topaz Suites Hotel as their vacation spot. This is a large hotel complex, which is famous for its high quality service, decent living conditions and sincere hospitality.

Since this hotel is popular with tourists, many people planning a trip to Egypt are interested in additional information. Where exactly is it located? How far is the beach? Are there appropriate conditions for relaxing with young children? How do other travelers respond about this place? These questions are asked by many travelers.

Where is the hotel located?





It is no secret that Hurghada is one of the most famous and vibrant resort cities in Egypt. It is here, not far from the Red Sea coast, that the Golden 5 Topaz Suites Hotel 4 * hotel complex is located. The international airport is only ten kilometers away. This is very convenient, because after a tiring flight you will reach your hotel in just 20-30 minutes. By the way, travel agencies, as well as the hotel itself, provide tourists with a free shuttle service.

The city center with all its entertainment centers and other entertainment venues is 17 kilometers away, which can be easily covered using public transport or taxi services. Despite some remoteness from the center, you will not feel cut off from life. Firstly, on the territory of Golden 5 Topaz Suites Hotel de Luxe you can find everything you need. Secondly, next to the hotel complex there are many shops, restaurants and other places where you can have fun.

Description of the Territory

Golden 5 Topaz Suites Hotel 4 * is part of a large complex called Golden 5 City, consisting of seven hotels of different categories. All hotels have one common territory, which is amazing in its size. There are numerous pools, and a large water park, restaurants, bars, sports grounds and terraces for relaxation. Guests can freely move around the territory of any hotel, relax and pool, and there is in any restaurant, with the exception of establishments of the √† la carte category. Here you will meet tall palm trees, flower beds and exotic gardens where you can relax while enjoying the beauty of nature.

The hotel itself consists of two-story and three-story buildings. By the way, it was opened not so long ago - in 2009. Thus, the buildings, the decoration, and the furnishings of the rooms - all comply with international standards.

Hurghada, Golden 5 Topaz Suites Hotel: description and photos of rooms





Naturally, the conditions for living on the territory of such a luxurious complex are more than worthy. Regardless of the category, all rooms are spacious and bright, equipped with modern furniture. In addition to beds, there is a dressing room, bedside tables, soft sofas and armchairs, chairs, tables. The room has a balcony from which you can enjoy views of the pool, garden or other areas of the hotel.

Of course, in the apartments you will find a set of important household appliances. In particular, each room has a split air conditioner, which helps both to cool and heat the air (it is cold in the winter months). You can watch satellite channels on TV. By the way, family rooms have two televisions - in the bedroom and living room.

There is also a telephone and a small safe where you can store documents and some other valuables. The minibar is filled immediately upon arrival - you will receive drinking water, bags of juice for the child, as well as sweet soda. All other drinks in the room will be delivered at an additional cost.

The room has a spacious ceramic-coated bathroom with good plumbing. You can take a bath and shower. There is also a wash basin, a toilet, a large mirror, a hairdryer for drying hair, a set of hygiene products (soap, shampoos), plus a set of towels for each guest.

The rooms are cleaned every day, while changing the used towels to clean. Bed linen is less frequent - every three days.

What kind of food is offered to guests?





Naturally, many tourists are interested in the issue of nutrition. The hotel complex Golden 5 Topaz Suites Hotel 4 *, as well as almost all Egyptian hotels, operates on an all-inclusive basis. Three times a day, all guests are invited to the La Perla's main restaurant, where generous buffets are served. Here, for the most part, dishes of more familiar European cuisine are served, but you will have the opportunity to try traditional Egyptian food. According to reviews, the choice of dishes here is really large - there are salads, fresh fruits, several types of meat, fish, crabs, seafood, various side dishes and, of course, delicious oriental sweets.

It is also worth mentioning that the guests of Golden 5 Topaz Suites Hotel de Luxe can eat in the main restaurants of any of the six neighboring hotels, which, of course, is quite convenient - you can choose your place to your liking. You will also have the right to receive light snacks and drinks at local bars for free. The price includes alcohol, but only Egyptian production - imported alcohol is paid separately.

In addition, on the territory of the huge hotel complex there are seven √† la carte restaurants. During their stay, guests of the Golden 5 Topaz Suites Hotel can visit each of them once for free, but only by prior reservation. Here you will have the opportunity to dine in the restaurants of German (Baroness), Russian (Tsar), Mediterranean (Promenade), Egyptian (El Tableyah), Indian (Mumbai) and Italian cuisine (La Luna). There is also a small but very lively restaurant offering fast food. On the territory of one of the neighboring hotels there is a restaurant Sea food, where guests are offered a huge selection of seafood dishes, however, you can eat here only after paying the bill.

Naturally, the Golden 5 Topaz Suites Hotel de Luxe also has several bars that are worth a visit. For example, the pool bar offers light snacks, tea, coffee, soft drinks and spirits, various cocktails. Approximately the same assortment can be expected in the bar located in the lobby. Guests are also invited to the billiards bar, where you can pass the time at the game table, as well as try some decent drinks.

From the morning until sunset, visitors are served by the beach bar, where you can get cold drinks and light snacks. Some guests even prefer to have breakfast on the beach.

Where is the beach?





The hotel complex owns a private beach stretch of one and a half kilometers. True, it is located about 800 meters from Club Golden 5 Topaz Suites Hotel 4. A road leads to the sea for those who want to take a walk. In addition, every half hour from the hotel there is a small bus that delivers guests to the sea and back.

The beach here is clean, well-groomed, covered with soft sand. The entrance to the sea is convenient, there is a shallow area. There is also a long pontoon. On the shore there are a large number of comfortable sunbeds with mattresses, as well as umbrellas. Here you can also take a beach towel on bail . There is a large volleyball court for lovers of more active recreation. This coast is perfect for families with children, as the sea is always warm, and there is a place for children to swim. By the way, it is worth getting special shoes, as there is a live coral reef nearby.

Water activities for tourists

What water activities can Golden 5 Topaz Club Suites Hotel offer? In fact, there are plenty of opportunities to have fun and try yourself in something new. Visiting the water sports center, you can rent a boat, catamaran, boat or even a yacht. By the way, a marina is also available here. In addition, you will have the opportunity to ride a banana, water skiing, do parasailing or even sign up for a boat trip.





A popular attraction for tourists is swimming with a mask. And you will have the opportunity to try your hand at windsurfing. By the way, for beginners, training classes are regularly held here, and more experienced tourists can rent a board and a suit.

It is no secret that the most striking attraction of the Red Sea are coral reefs. One of them is located here, at some distance from the coast. And popular among guests is scuba diving. A whole diving school is organized at the hotel, where you can take a training course, rent equipment and dive in the company of a professional.

Are there any conditions for relaxing with children?

Many people today prefer to travel with their children. And in such cases, the crucial question is whether there are appropriate conditions on the territory of the hotel. So what can the Golden 5 Topaz Suites Hotel 4 * hotel complex offer? Reviews indicate that this place was just made for children. Indeed, in fact, the main group of guests is just tourists with children.





For starters, it's worth talking about amenities. Upon request, an extra bed will be delivered to your room quickly - this service is free. True, for the second and each subsequent bed will need to pay extra. In the restaurant you can count on a high chair for the youngest visitors. A children's corner is also equipped here and a special children's menu has been developed. Therefore, you can easily pick up not only healthy, but really tasty food for your child.

In terms of children's entertainment, Golden 5 Topaz Suites Hotel also stands out. Golden 5 City has several pools for children. In addition, you should definitely take the child to a local water park, where there are many constantly working water slides for children of different ages. Here, at the water park, a huge sandbox has been created where your child can play and meet other children.

The Golden 5 Topaz Suites Hotel also invites young guests to a mini club. Here you can leave your child under the supervision of experienced animators and educators. Young tourists here have the opportunity to engage in dancing, drawing and other types of arts. Here, together with the animators, they organize and take part in various team games, competitions, and theater productions. Testimonials suggest that children here have a really good time. And at the mini-club a small cinema is organized, which in the morning and afternoon arranges viewing of the most popular and funny cartoons.

There are also several playgrounds with swings, tree houses, labyrinths, etc. In the evenings, children dance or dance, where children can also have fun. The hotel has several professional nannies who, if necessary, will stay with the child for several hours. True, this service is paid separately.

Additional services on site

Of course, the hotel tried to create conditions so that the vacation of tourists was not overshadowed by domestic or organizational problems. The hotel has parking for private cars, as well as a marina. Dry cleaning and laundry are constantly working, the services of which are quite affordable.

There is a pharmacy, and in the event of a deterioration in your health, a doctor will be called to your room. Almost the entire complex has Internet access, which is very important for some travelers. Women are offered to visit a beauty salon, where only masters of their craft work.

Hotel staff will call you a taxi or even rent a limousine upon request. On the territory there is a supermarket and several stores where you can buy literally everything at affordable prices, from food and household items, to jewelry, clothes and interesting souvenirs.

Golden 5 City has three spacious conference rooms that are used for business events and various celebrations.

Guests of the hotel complex are not bored, because there is almost everything for a fun and eventful vacation. In the courtyard of the hotel there is a large swimming pool, which is heated in winter. Around are sun loungers and umbrellas, where you can sunbathe, relax and just chat with new neighbors.

In addition, guests are invited to the local water park, which has swimming pools and slides for adults. You can really have a good time here. By the way, every evening from 21.00 to 21.30 "singing fountains" are included - an exciting show that amazes with the splendor of colors. Through the territory of the Golden 5 City complex, the Taf-Taf train constantly runs - you can quickly get from one part to another, as well as fully explore the vast common territory of the hotels.

Those who wish can use the gym for free, but in this case you need to think about special shoes. And in the fitness center, group classes are regularly held in yoga, aerobics, gymnastics, water aerobics, etc.

You should definitely go to the spa at least once, where you will be offered a huge range of wellness and rejuvenating treatments, as well as various types of massage, sauna, Turkish bath and jacuzzi.

The hotel has several tennis courts, tables for table tennis and billiards, a mini-golf course, as well as sports grounds for football, volleyball and basketball.

During the day, the guests are entertained by a team of experienced animators, many of whom are Russian-speaking. They organize contests, team games, competitions, dance classes and simply help guests get to know each other and communicate with each other. And in the evenings you will find interesting performances, including performances by singing and dancing groups, acrobatic performances, exotic oriental belly dancing, shows with fire and much more.

In the evening, you can also visit the local disco, which is open until 2.00 at night. Entrance is free, but you have to pay for drinks at the bar. In addition, animators regularly organize trips to discos and beach parties in the city - they cost not so much. And also all regular customers, newlyweds and those who have a birthday can expect a pleasant surprise gift from the hotel immediately upon arrival.

Naturally, this is not all that the Golden 5 Topaz Suites Hotel offers. Egypt is a country with an ancient history and interesting traditions. Using the services of the excursion center, you can visit the Great Pyramids, the ancient temples of Luxor and Karnak, make a trip through the desert, see a lot of other sights of the country and get acquainted with the traditions of the Egyptians.
