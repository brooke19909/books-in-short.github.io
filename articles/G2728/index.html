Hotel Nissi Park Hotel 3 * (Cyprus, Ayia Napa): overview, description and reviews of tourists

Cyprus today is rightfully one of the most popular resort destinations in Europe. Beautiful beaches, picturesque mountain landscapes, luxury hotels by the sea and the sincere hospitality of the Cypriots - what could be better for a vacation?

Probably every tourist knows that one of the largest and most beautiful resorts in Cyprus is the city of Ayia Napa. It is here that the Nissi Park Hotel complex is located, which is ideal for a pleasant, relaxing holiday. Of course, before booking, you should ask for more information. Is location convenient? Can I stay here with a child? Were the former guests satisfied?

Location Information





Where to look for the hotel complex Nissi Park Hotel 3? Ayia Napa is a busy resort where life does not stop its rapid course for even a minute. It is here, just 200 meters from the world famous Nissi Beach, that the hotel is located.

The city center is about 2.5 km away, so you can even get there on foot. Despite the proximity to the central part of the resort, the place is quite calm - you can have fun during the day and enjoy the silence at night.

Hotel Nissi Park 3 (Cyprus, Ayia Napa): infrastructure description

As former guests say in their reviews, the territory here is really beautiful. Nissi Park Hotel 3 is built in the style of old Cypriot monasteries. The hotel is a snow-white three-story building with a strict but elegant architecture. By the way, everything is also beautifully furnished inside, but discreet.

The courtyard of the hotel is quite large, and part of it is reserved for the pool. The territory is decorated with palm trees, bright exotic plants, emerald lawns. Everything here is literally buried in flowers. Tourists note that they clean here constantly, therefore, the courtyard is always clean.

Photo and description of living rooms





Nissi Park Hotel is a medium-sized hotel with 80 rooms. All rooms on the territory are standard, designed for two adults (and one child). Their area is 17 square meters.

The furnishings of the rooms are simple, uncomplicated, but quite pleasant. It offers a set of necessary household appliances and furniture, including a king-size large bed. By the way, almost every room has a balcony or terrace. It offers views of the garden, courtyard with pool or the picturesque grounds of the neighboring hotel.

Travelers can use the air conditioning system. There is a telephone and a TV, as well as a small refrigerator. For an additional fee, you can rent a safe with an electronic lock.

The bathroom adjoins the room with a spacious shower, wall mirror, washbasin and toilet. Clean towels in the room are available, they are changed every day during cleaning. Also, guests can use a stationary hair dryer.

Dining for tourists: what to count on?





The all-inclusive system is what many Cyprus hotels offer travelers. Nissi Park Hotel is no exception. True, payment of a full three meals a day is not mandatory - you can order only breakfast or dinner.

Guests are invited to the spacious dining room with a large open-air terrace. The menu here is varied: there are meat and cheese slices, salads, fresh fruits, a huge selection of delicious pastries, light soups. The main dishes are different every day, which pleases travelers.

The local bar serves light snacks. When paying for full board, the price also includes coffee, tea, local drinks (including alcohol). Guests are offered home-made ice cream, which is very popular not only with kids, but also with their parents. In the evenings, music is played in the bar - you can relax, enjoy a pleasant company and a relaxing atmosphere.

Where is the beach? Rest by the water





What in terms of beach holidays does the hotel complex Nissi Park Hotel offer? As already mentioned, the hotel is built almost on the shore. The distance to the famous Nissi Beach is only 200 meters, it can easily be overcome in 3-5 minutes.

This stretch of coast is intended for public use. It is always full of tourists from all over the world. Nevertheless, the beach is clean and well maintained. The entrance to the sea is convenient. For a small fee, you can rent an umbrella and sun loungers for the whole day. There are several bars and cafes right on the beach, where guests can enjoy snacks and drinks. Here you can even have dinner, enjoying the sea breeze and the view of the setting sun.

There are also entertainment for vacationers. On the shore there are several volleyball courts. There is a diving and windsurfing school, where you can not only get training, but also rent the necessary equipment. Tourists love boating, water skiing, boats, catamarans and even small yachts. Each traveler will find entertainment to his own liking.

Additional services on site





Of course, guests of the Nissi Park Hotel can count on some additional services. If you wish, you can hand over clothes to the laundry or dry cleaning - the price for these services is quite affordable.

Despite the fact that the territory of the hotel is not too large, convenient free parking is available here. In the city, by the way, you can easily rent a car or any other type of transport - it will be where to put it at night.

If desired, guests can leave things unnecessary during the rest in the storage room. Internet access is available on site, but this is a service that is paid separately.

How to brighten up leisure? Entertainment for tourists





For the most part, the hotel complex is used as a place for sleeping and relaxing, since almost all the guests are spent on the beach or in the city center. Nevertheless, an interesting lesson can be found in the hotel.

As already mentioned, the territory has a large, clean pool. Nearby there is a comfortable terrace with large sun loungers and umbrellas. By the way, according to reviews, here you can rarely observe a large influx of people. In most cases, you can freshen up and sunbathe here, enjoying peace and quiet.

If you wish, you can have a good time playing a game of table tennis. Almost every evening, live music plays, which creates a mysterious and romantic atmosphere on the territory. Cypriot nights with various performances are also organized.

A lot of entertainment can be found outside the hotel complex Nissi Park Hotel. Cyprus is a wonderful country with a very beautiful nature, interesting history and deep culture. Do not give up the opportunity to devote some time to excursions. The hotel has several good guides, and the city is full of agencies that will tell you about the most interesting places of the island state and help you choose an interesting tour for a reasonable price.

Is the hotel suitable for families?

As you know, many parents today go on a trip with their children. And in such cases, the availability of additional amenities is important. Is the Nissi Park Hotel suitable for families? Guest reviews confirm that families with children often do stay at the hotel.

In the courtyard for small guests there is a comfortable and safe pool. The water here warms up well and is regularly disinfected. Kids often spend time in the yard, meeting new friends. At the disposal of your offspring there is also a games room with a mass of machine guns and toys. Of course, the kids have a wonderful time on the beach, where there is a shallow area for swimming, and the depth increases gradually. By the way, a rescue team is working on the shore.

The kids restaurant offers simple but healthy meals. Yes, and local desserts delight the guys. If necessary, children can be left under the supervision of a nanny - her services are not too expensive.

Hotel Nissi Park Hotel 3 (Ayia Napa): Holiday Reviews





Of course, official information is important. But it never hurts to take an interest in the impressions of people who have already managed to visit here. So what do they say about Nissi Park Hotel 3?

Most tourists leave positive reviews. The hotel is really well maintained and beautiful. The rooms are freshly renovated. The atmosphere is straightforward, but there is everything you need to relax. The cleaners, by the way, are conscientious in their work.

Separately, tourists thank the local chefs who every day try to make the menu more diverse. Dishes are served fresh and tasty, there is always a choice, and pastries are beyond praise. The pool in the yard is cleaned every day.

The staff here are very polite, attentive, helpful, which creates an atmosphere of genuine hospitality. Many of them have a good understanding of the Russian language, which greatly simplifies communication. By the way, the newlyweds are congratulated upon arrival, providing a basket of fruits and a bottle of good champagne.

The undoubted advantage is the proximity to the beach. This is a lively place to have a good time. Nearby are shops, supermarkets, travel agencies, numerous cafes and restaurants. In short, travelers recommend the Nissi Park Hotel. Here you can relax in comfort, but for little money.
