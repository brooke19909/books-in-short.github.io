"Panda Park" - the rope park of Nizhny Novgorod

In search of new emotions, adrenaline, good mood and pleasant pastime, people tend to try all new types of outdoor activities. A popular form of such leisure is the passage of overhead routes and obstacles. One of the places offering this type of entertainment is the Panda Park rope park in Nizhny Novgorod.

What place?

"Panda Park" is a complex of rope crossings located at different heights. Its branches are open in Moscow, Nizhny Novgorod, Sochi and other cities of Russia.

All tracks are thought out to the smallest detail, double insurance is in effect, and an experienced instructor can come to the rescue at any time. All this ensures complete safety of visitors.

Routes vary in degree of difficulty, designed for a certain age. They can be located outdoors or indoors, like a rope park in Nizhny Novgorod in the Olympus shopping center. This option can be visited at any time of the year, regardless of the weather.

Rope Park Nizhny Novgorod

The institution was opened on September 17, 2016. Since then, it has always been pleasing its visitors with an excellent mood, a storm of positive emotions and impressions. The rope park in Nizhny Novgorod is located in the Olimp indoor shopping center, which allows you to go all year round in any weather conditions.

Crossings are located at a height of one to ten meters and represent three routes. One of them is designed for children over 90 centimeters tall, the other two for all comers over 140 centimeters tall.

Before passing any route, a detailed briefing is conducted and insurance and necessary equipment are issued.

Entertainment

The rope park "Panda Park" in Nizhny Novgorod has not only crossings and routes at a height of up to nine meters, but also a climbing wall, as well as children's climbing panels.

The simplest routes are provided for the smallest - children from the age of three.

What you need to visit

To visit Panda Park, you need to follow some rules and restrictions. For example, children under three years old and below 90 centimeters are not allowed into obstacles. It is also contraindicated to overcome obstacles to persons who have recently undergone surgical interventions or who have contraindications for increased physical exertion, other medical prohibitions.

There are no restrictions on the level of physical fitness - routes have varying degrees of difficulty.

It is necessary to wear sneakers and comfortable clothes that will not hamper movement and interfere with overcoming obstacles.

Address and mode of operation
