Lazarevskoe: houses by the sea - a vacationer's dream

The Russian Black Sea coast is becoming increasingly popular among holidaymakers. The infrastructure is developing by leaps and bounds, trying in every possible way to meet the needs of tourists.

After the Sochi Olympic Games, the flow of tourists in this direction has increased several times. The next message was the closure of some countries familiar to our fellow citizens for recreation.

One of the long-known holiday destinations, Lazarevskoye, is growing rapidly. A lot of vacationers flock here in season. What attracts guests? Why Lazarevskoe?

Location

Lazarevskoye is the most northern region of Sochi. It is famous for its beautiful beaches, unlike Adler and Sochi, with a wide coastline. The sea is clear here: the bottom can be seen even at great depths. Medium and pebble beaches.





Where to stay

Housing in Lazarevsky is for every taste and budget. Most seek to get closer to the sea.

Guest houses in Lazarevsky by the sea - a popular accommodation option. Among them are many of those whose windows overlook the sea and mountains. Others have shady terraces with beautiful sea views.





Arriving in Lazarevskoye, cottages by the sea can be found for every taste and they are much cheaper than accommodation in large hotels. The price largely depends on the amenities offered to guests. If the guest is not too demanding and only needs to spend the night, take a shower, prepare a light breakfast, then you can find very budget options from 1,000 rubles per room at the height of the season.

If you want to relax in comfort, taking advantage of all the benefits of civilization, then in this case Lazarevskoye will do. Houses by the sea are for every taste. The cost of living in luxury housing can cost 6 thousand per day.





Many of them have a common area with a cafe, sun loungers, a swimming pool, entertainment on the shore.

Because of the popularity on many specialized housing search sites, you can find Lazarevskoye, it is better to choose houses by the sea leisurely in advance.

The largest housing search site is Booking . It is international, but guests to Lazarevskoye are traveling not only from Russia. There are many local sites uniting private entrepreneurs, renting rooms, cottages.

Travel agencies also turn to the services of private entrepreneurs to accommodate customers. Lazarevskoye is no exception. You can choose houses by the sea by contacting a travel company, but this will certainly affect the cost.

For greater savings, you can search, having arrived in Lazarevskoye, a private sector by the sea, but here the distance to the beach will be a little further, but the price is lower. It must be remembered that the terrain in the village is hilly, so sometimes you will have to go to the sea and back not on a smooth road, but going up or down. If this is important for you, specify where the necessary objects are located.





Many owners do not rent housing for a day or two, so if something does not suit you, there may be problems with the return of funds. This point needs to be discussed in advance, so as not to spoil your vacation.

This is convenient for Booking , because if the tourist is not satisfied or his expectations are not met, then just call the support service, and they will try to better resolve the problem.

What to see

There is entertainment for every taste and age. Modern cinemas, night clubs, discos, 2 water parks, a dolphinarium, numerous concerts of pop stars.

Natural beauties that you must certainly enjoy: dolmens, mountains, gorges, turbulent rivers, waterfalls. In the season you will be offered many interesting excursions, regardless of where you are located. You will meet the organizers on the streets in the village and right on the beach. If you do not want to plunge into the crowd of sightseers, then it is quite possible to get to many wonderful corners on your own. The hosts will provide an invaluable service in choosing routes, because Lazarevskoye (a private sector by the sea) is for the most part indigenous people who know and love their land well.

How to get there

There are various ways to get to Lazarevsky: by car, by rail and by air.

Trains leave Moscow on average 24 hours, and most trains heading in the Sochi direction stop here.

By plane you need to get to Adler, and then taxi, regular buses, and the Swallow train.

A taxi will cost about 3 thousand a season, and will get to Lazarevsky in about an hour and a half if it doesn‚Äôt get stuck in traffic. The bus is much cheaper, but longer. A ticket for a bus, minibus and "Swallow" costs about the same - 70-80 rubles. Many tourists are advised to travel by train, traffic jams do not bother them, and this sometimes saves a lot of time.

Often hosts meet their guests by arranging transfers to Lazarevskoye for them. Houses by the sea can often be rented with such a bonus.
