How to change the sound of an alarm on an iPhone: what manipulations need to be performed

In this article, we will look at how to change the sound of an alarm clock on an iPhone, how to set it, how to change it, and much more. We will figure it out, draw conclusions and understand what an alarm clock is in this gadget and how to set it for the time you need. Let's get started!

How to set an alarm on Apple iPhone

First you need to access the alarm clock of your smartphone. This can be done in these three ways, namely:

  1. Click on the Clock application icon.
  2. Make a swipe on top of your iPhone screen and drive the word "Clock" into the search for your applications.
  3. Call the swipe control panel from the bottom up and click on the corresponding timer icon (alarm).

When you get into the Clock application, to turn on the alarm, you need to proceed as follows:

  1. Enter the tab "Alarm" that we need.
  2. Click on the "+", which you will find on the tab that opens. It gives access to the tab for changing the time of the alarm.
  3. Once in this tab, you configure the time that you need, after which you expect the alarm to work when necessary.

But there are also alarm settings. Let's see what they mean.

The first parameter is "repeat." An Apple iPhone user can choose which days it will sound. He can choose only on weekdays, only on weekends, only on Mondays and so on.

The second parameter is "name". Here the designation corresponds to the function itself. Just designating a certain word will help you remember why you put it.

The third parameter is ‚Äúsignal repetition‚Äù. The user can select a function that provides a signal with a certain frequency. When you select a snooze time, the alarm will turn off for a while, after which it will ring again after a specified number of minutes.

The general list of alarms will be replenished with a new alarm clock, namely yours.

To disable it, move the slider to the desired side.

How to change alarm ringtone on Apple iPhone?

In addition to the built-in melodies, you can put your own song / ringtone on your smartphone. To do this, at the settings stage, select the "song selection", then you will find yourself in the Apple Store. Here you will need to find the song you want, click on the "+" button on it. Thus, the question of how to change the sound of the alarm on iPhone 6 is no longer needed.

If you need a ringtone, in the same place, in the Apple store, call the item "Buy more sounds" and select the desired ringtone. Buy it and install it on your Apple iPhone.
