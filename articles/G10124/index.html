Knitted volume hat: how and what to wear?

As soon as it gets cold outside, scarves and hats come from the very depths of the wardrobe. Already many years in a row are incredibly popular knitted volume caps . And in fact, they are not only practical and comfortable, but also fashionable. In this article we will learn how and with what to wear these hats, and what they are.

Fashionable and practical

Since there are many styles of hats, they cease to play an exclusively protective role and become accessories that can change the winter look for better or worse. Like several decades ago, the most successful version of winter hats is knitted volume hat , because high-quality yarn plus an extraordinary performance is an equally interesting fashionable and practical accessory.

This year, ordinary knitted hats are replaced by lush and voluminous, such products are often decorated with lush patterns and do not fit too tightly to the head, lie freely. Knit a similar hat for a short time, you will spend from half an hour to an hour and a half, and as a result you will get a fashion accessory. Choose light unobtrusive patterns and large warm yarn.

What are the caps in the trend?

Voluminous knitted hat Not only became a fashion accessory, but also supplanted tight models, turning into the most popular detail of the image of the urban IT-girl. Not so long ago, hats were preferred to hats, hoping not to spoil the hair, but with fashionable hats this was not necessary.

Knitted volumetric hat with knitting needles It goes well with casual style and classic, it can be matched to any type of person and to any appearance. For example, large knitting will be an excellent detail for the winter look, and hat with lapels will complement casual bow. Patterns in the form of braids already promise to be the brightest trend of this winter, like the English gum. Beanie caps will help you not to be unnoticed. Lapels, braids, beanies - is it fashionable?

First of all, caps with lapels are twice more convenient and practical to use, because they give even more warmth and comfort than just oversized caps. Such an accessory model is best suited for girls with a medium-sized head, while a lapel from 7 to 10 centimeters wide will be ideal. If the circumference of your head is small, and your facial features are tiny and fragile, pick up caps with a lapel about 5 centimeters. This will help you not visually reduce your head.

Volumetric knitted hat patterns quite simple - for example, full face loops or one-on-one elastic. In addition, you can experiment a bit with the pattern: tie a few thin braids or make a two-on-two elastic band, or even knit with an English elastic band. Often, finished hats with a simple pattern are decorated with pom-poms made of fur or yarn, the same color or any suitable. You can also decorate hats with logos, rhinestones, stripes.

Knitted volume hat beanie is a type of headgear that fits your head a little and has a laconic finish, the volume in it appears due to the knitting or thickness of the yarn. Such accessories can be combined with classic coats, as well as down jackets or a ski jacket.

What to wear and how to combine?

As you already understood, the fashion of walking in winter without a hat has long sunk into oblivion, so you should take advantage of fashionable tips and get yourself a wonderful accessory that will not only protect you from severe frosts, but will also add some zest to your look. So with what to combine these hats?

Volumetric knitted women's hats matched to an existing image. For example, if you want to have an accessory suitable for a fur coat, coat and down jacket at the same time, pay attention to the hats made of mohair in a neutral color - black, white or beige. Textured patterned patterns combine better with outerwear made of smooth material.

For bright clothes, choose knitted accessories of a discreet color, and if your down jacket is pastel or muted, do not be afraid to choose hats of bright saturated colors.
