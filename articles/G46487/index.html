"Guards" (hotel, Kazan): description and reviews

If you are looking for an inexpensive accommodation option, then you will certainly like the "Guards" hotel. Kazan will open up many opportunities for you both in the field of tourism and in the field of doing business.





Brief description of the hotel

If you value comfort and quality relaxation, then an accommodation option like the Guards Hotel is just perfect for you. Kazan is considered the third capital of Russia, and therefore the service standards are relevant here. Here you will have access to wireless Internet access, as well as the opportunity to rent a conference room. It is also worth noting the professionalism and friendly attitude of the staff.

As for the rooms, the "Guards" (hotel, Kazan) mainly provides budget accommodation options. However, there are also executive apartments. As for the design, it is quite unobtrusive, made in a modern laconic style. It is worth noting that the budget category rooms have a block structure (that is, the bathroom is shared by several rooms). In the superior apartments, in addition to basic amenities, there is also a TV, refrigerator and other necessary household appliances.

For a full-fledged diet, guests of the institution "Guards" (hotel, Kazan) meets the cafe. Thanks to its location on the 8th floor, you can enjoy the fascinating panoramas of the city. The price includes a continental breakfast, but you can also order complex meals.





Hotel Location

Quite convenient location is characterized by the hotel "Guards". Kazan is a big city, and therefore, a good transport interchange at the location plays a huge role. There is a tram stop just steps away from where you can quickly get to the area you need. You will have to drive no more than 1 kilometer to the train station. The budget and comfortable accommodation option is offered by the city of Kazan - the hotel "Guards". The address of this institution: street Guards, 35.

Economy rooms

Many tourists regularly visit Kazan. Hotel "Guards" can offer a budget accommodation option for those who do not want to spend a lot of money on accommodation:

  • Single Economy offers accommodation in a compact room on a small queen-size bed. In addition, a set of necessary furniture and household appliances will be waiting for you here. As for the bathroom, it is common to the block.
  • Double "Economy" involves the placement of two single beds in a compact bedroom. This option also has a shared bathroom. At the same time, 3, 4 and 5 people can live in the block.
  • In addition to 3 single beds, the Economy Triple Room has a desk and a wardrobe. There is also a home appliance. A shared bathroom shares this room with another double room.

Hotel "Guards" (Kazan) - reviews, advantages

Tourists highlight the following positive aspects of living in this hotel:

  • polite and helpful staff;
  • the Internet works well;
  • despite the fact that the hotel is located on the outskirts of the city, there is an excellent traffic intersection, and therefore you can quickly get to any area;
  • pleased with the availability of ironing accessories in the corridor;
  • the information folder contains not only the phone numbers of hotel services, but also background information about the city.




Negative reviews

This hotel is characterized by the following disadvantages:

  • if you booked a room in advance, then during check-in, employees can change the category without warning or explanation;
  • the furniture and appliances in the rooms are quite old and have long lagged behind modern standards;
  • there is no sconce near the bed, and therefore you will not be able to read before bedtime; Carpets in rooms have not been cleaned for a very long time;
  • there are no clothes hangers in the closet; in the bathrooms there are no cosmetic sets (despite the fact that they were declared at the time of booking);
  • Registration of new arrivals takes a lot of time;
  • terrible audibility between adjacent numbers (especially when it comes to blocks);
  • unscrupulous cleaning;
  • the drain in the shower does not work well;
  • in the hotel first-aid kit there are no children's medicines;
  • no matter how long you check into the hotel, free use of the Internet is available only for two days.

General impression

Hotel "Guards" is just perfect for those who are looking for a budget option to stay in Kazan. There are economical rooms here, which imply the presence of a shared bathroom per block. This is a pretty popular option. It is quite often chosen by large delegations, as well as children's sightseeing or sports teams. The rooms are quite compact, but very comfortable. They have televisions, refrigerators and other necessary amenities.

Oddly enough, the largest number of negative reviews was received precisely from the guests of the superior rooms. This is due to the fact that the cost of living does not match the quality. It has pretty old furniture and appliances, and cleanliness leaves much to be desired. In addition, they even save on cosmetic sets, which are included in the price. Therefore, if you pay much attention to details, then this option of placement is most likely not suitable for you. In addition, when booking, it is worth considering the significant distance from the city center.
