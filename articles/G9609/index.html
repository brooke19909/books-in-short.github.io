Terramar 3 * (Spain) - photos, prices and reviews

Among the many attractive countries in the world, Spain is popular with tourists. Here you can spend a wonderful vacation in the beautiful Spanish hotel Terramar 3 (Costa Brava). Spain is famous for its many cozy hotel complexes, among which you can find options for family, youth and romantic, and for a quiet measured vacation. The picturesque nature, fascinating and informative excursions, a bewitching atmosphere, good service - all this will make your stay in Spain memorable with a lot of positive emotions.





Calella - magnificent Spain

One of the most beautiful is the Spanish town of Calella, which is only 50 kilometers from Barcelona. Despite its small territory, it occupies a high step among the most popular holiday destinations. An important merit in this is the beaches of the city, which are awarded the Blue Flag certificate. The city is considered to be the tourist center of the coast and attracts many tourists. It has many advantages, including large shopping areas in the central part of the city, many picturesque squares, walkways and parks.

The most attractive place in Calella is considered to be a trading place near Vila Square. In addition to the trade stalls, you can admire several buildings built in the Gothic style, with numerous decorative elements of the 16th century. Nearby is the chapel of St. Kirse and St. Juliet, erected in the same period, and the church of St. Mary and St. Nicholas, built two centuries later in the Baroque style. Particularly noteworthy here is the altar, preserved from the temple, which was previously located on this site. For connoisseurs of history, the museum-archive of Calella holds its doors open, which contains historical artifacts and documentary evidence of the city's historical events. An important role in the popularity of this resort town is played by a huge selection of hotels, including Terramar Hotel 3 *, Kaktus Playa 3 *, Hotel Garbi, Hotel Haromar, Hotel Tahiti Playa. Accommodation in one of these complexes will not only allow you to see the numerous attractions of the city, but also have a wonderful rest.





general description

One of the most attractive three-star hotels located in the Costa Brava resort - Terramar 3 * - deserves special attention. It rises in the town of Calella, in the Sitges area. It has a decent work experience, as it was built back in 1965. Full reconstruction was made in 2005, and in 2012 a partial renovation of the hotel took place. It is famous for its laid-back sophisticated atmosphere and an attentive and friendly team of staff serving guests. It offers guests bars, restaurants, swimming pools. The comfortable rooms are equipped with modern amenities. A huge selection of entertainment awaits here those who love the cheerful nightlife of large resorts. The excellent climate, picturesque surrounding places and the gentle warm sea will make the rest vivid and memorable.

Location

Hotel Terramar 3 (Costa Brava) is located in the central vibrant Calella area. Girona Airport is at a distance of 50 kilometers. Barcelona Airport is 20 kilometers away from the hotel. Numerous hotels, bars and restaurants, shops and discos are located nearby. 150 meters from the hotel there is a bus stop with links to anywhere in the city. The city's entertainment center is in a nearby area, located just 150 meters from the hotel complex.

Guest Accommodation

To accommodate guests in Terramar Calella 3 * there is a 6-storey building with 210 rooms of various categories: 185 standard double rooms, (max. 2 + 1 people), 25 standard single rooms (max. 1 people). The rooms have everything you need for a comfortable and cozy stay. There are several rooms that have been converted for people with disabilities.

Each room has a private bathroom with shower, bathrobe, towel and necessary bath accessories. The floor has a ceramic coating. A hair dryer is provided for drying hair. Not all rooms have air conditioning, but only those that have the Premium and Suite class. They are connected from mid-June to mid-September. Each room has a minibar, a telephone is installed, with the help of which every vacationer can order a particular service directly from his room. There is a satellite TV. Among the many channels there is also a Russian-language one. Each room has a safe for storing documents and valuables, but guests can use it only for an additional fee. In the cold season, the rooms include heating. If necessary, one extra bed is available upon prior request. All rooms are decorated in light and soft colors, which makes the room even more warm and cozy. Guests can use Wi-Fi (for a fee).

All rooms have a balcony with excellent views of the sea or the courtyard of the hotel complex.

Housekeeping is provided daily. Bed linen is replaced once every seven days.





Nutrition

Hotel Terramar 3 * offers various types of food for its guests: Bed & Breakfast (breakfast), Half Board (breakfast and dinner), Full Board (breakfast, lunch and dinner). Breakfast, lunch and dinner are served in the buffet restaurant with a wide variety of dishes. The restaurant has a summer outdoor terrace, which offers magnificent surrounding landscapes, allowing you to get even more pleasure from eating. Upon prior reservation, breakfast can be served directly in the room. High chairs and a children's menu are provided for younger guests. Refreshing drinks and light snacks are available at the hotel bar.





Beach

The beach at Hotel Terramar 3 (Spain) is public, located 100 meters from the hotel complex, through the road and rail. It stretches along shady alleys, well-groomed embankments and rocky shores with cliffs and bays. It is covered with sand mixed with small pebbles. The beach is very bright, with convenient access to the sea. There are slopes specially equipped for the disabled. Beach equipment is available at an additional cost. For lovers of outdoor activities there is a volleyball court. It offers water ski and bicycle rental. Windsurfing and kayak lovers will not be bored here. Yachtsmen will also find something to their liking. Enjoy a boat with a transparent bottom to appreciate the richness of the sea with beautiful corals and bright underwater fauna.

In addition, there are three other beaches in Calella. They are all located in a single strip along the coast and awarded the Blue Flag for cleanliness. Particularly popular with connoisseurs of the underwater world is the place of Rock Gross. In the Paseo de las Rocas area is located the equally popular beach - Garby. Various shows and entertainment events are constantly organized here. A foam party is held here for children and a circle of crafts made from shells. The fourth beach, Forest Rocus, is located in a rocky bay, not far from the center of Calella. It is especially popular with surfers.

Territory

Hotel Territory Terramar 3 * occupies a small area, only 2000 square meters. meters. It is rich in a variety of green spaces, which allows you to take refuge in the shade during the hottest periods. In the central part is an outdoor pool filled with fresh water. For those who came to rest in their own car, there is a garage (for a fee) and a parking lot (for a fee). If during the vacation there are any production issues, the hotel provides two conference rooms for up to 70 and 150 people. Representatives of the beautiful half of humanity will be able to visit the beauty salon.





Services for guests

Guests relaxing at the hotel Terramar 3 *, a number of services are provided. For those who want to take a walk around the city and its surroundings, bicycle and car rental are available. There is a currency exchange, fax. Guests can always take advantage of laundry and dry cleaning services. There is a special room for storing luggage in the hotel lobby, and a safe (for a fee) is installed at the reception where you can store your valuables. Employees serving guests assist in acquiring sightseeing tickets. For convenience, the hotel has an elevator and a rooftop terrace.





Sports and Entertainment

Those who go on vacation in Terramar 3 *, reviews confirm this, you will not have to get bored. The hotel provides its guests with a rich entertainment program. For those who seek to keep themselves in shape even while relaxing, there is a gym. Among other sports activities there are golf, billiards, tennis courts. For lovers of video games installed slot machines. For those who want to watch a movie on the big screen, a cinema works. In addition, the picturesque Montnegre Corredor nature reserve approaches directly to the city, through which an exciting journey can be made.

But during your stay at the hotel entertainment programs are not limited to the territory of the hotel complex. The cultural program in Calella offers a visit to the market with the products of local artisans, various and colorful festivals and all kinds of concerts that take place in different parts of the city. The main center of cultural life of the city is considered the alley of Manuel Puigvert. An annual fair is held here, and performances by fans of the Catalan Sardanian dance are constantly held. Not far from the beach is the Menfis Club, where discos and foam parties are held. If you go on vacation to this city at the end of June, you can become participants in the festival of evil spirits. On this day, everyone takes to the streets, blows up crackers and firecrackers, dresses up in various costumes and scares lonely passers-by, sings songs and dances.

Services for children

Terramar Calella 3 * provides a number of services for younger guests. In the large pool, there is a section for small guests. Entertainment programs and colorful shows are held daily for them. For an additional fee, you can use the services and fully enjoy a wonderful vacation. There is a mini-club for older children, where they can have a great chat and have fun in their free time. During meals, a high chair is provided in the restaurant, and a baby cot can be placed in the room upon reservation. But entertainment for young guests is not limited to the territory of the hotel. A free shuttle bus runs from here to the Marineland water park, where there are a lot of various attractions, slides and other attractions.

Holiday Prices

Terramar 3 * is almost always crowded. The cost of living for one person is from $ 112 per night. It should be noted that, according to state legislation, cash payments for accommodation cannot exceed 2500 EUR. In addition, a city tax of 0.50 EUR is charged per person per night. This tax does not apply to children under 16 years old. If you buy a last-minute tour to the Terramar Hotel, the cost of living can be much lower.





How to get there

Getting to Terramar 3 * is easiest with a comfortable train that leaves from Barcelona and travels along the coast. Only 1 hour is spent on such a trip. The ticket costs a little more than 5 EUR. Alternatively, you can hire a car from Barcelona Airport to your hotel. The average daily rental price is 25 EUR. You can hire a taxi. If you give the address below, the car will take you directly to the hotel itself ( 08370, C. Diputacion, 50, Calella, Spain).

Guest reviews

Those who rested in the hotel Terramar 3 * (Spain), reviews leave mostly positive. Most often, the reviews indicate the excellent location of this hotel complex, favorable climatic conditions and the gentle emerald sea. Numerous shows and entertainment programs make the rest memorable and unique. Reviews are not disregarded by the service staff, which pleases guests with their friendliness, attention and decent service. There is a slight variety of cuisine, but no one complained about the taste of the dishes offered. Guests of the hotel also respond positively to rooms in which cleanliness and comfort reign. In their notes, tourists note the proximity of a bus stop with which you can get to any corner of the city. Guests are satisfied with the beaches, despite the fact that they are public. They are well equipped and kept clean.
