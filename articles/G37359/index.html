What does the interest rate consist of?

The loan rate is set based on such factors:

  • Inflation: the higher it is, the higher the rate.
  • Interest on interbank loans: if a bank does not have its own funds for obtaining loans, then it asks for help from large financial institutions.
  • Costs: the rate includes interest on the salary of employees, rental of premises, office maintenance.
  • Profit: loan disbursement costs are taken into account.
  • Seasonal costs: the provision of loans depends on the season. During the holidays and before the New Year, the demand for loans increases and deposits decreases, so during these periods the rate may increase by 5-6%.




The rate in each bank sets the same. In addition, you need to pay attention to whether there are paid services. Interest in large banks is less compared to small commercial organizations. But if the rate is small, then the borrower is carefully checked.
