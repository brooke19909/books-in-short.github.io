Cellular - what is it on the iPad and what is the difference

Many users are wondering: " cellular - what is it on an iPad "? This is a symbol that indicates the presence of additional functions and technical characteristics in the device. What specific features are we talking about? They will be revealed in the process of answering the question: cellular - what is it on the iPad? However, first we will talk about the manufacturer.

A bit about Apple and iPad





These devices, however, like tablets from other manufacturers, were created with the expectation that users will access the Internet, work with various applications, watch videos and social networks. However, to reduce the cost, Apple provided several ways to solve the tasks. Almost every user knows that access to the Internet can be obtained in several ways, for example, using mobile networks, connecting to Wi-Fi or using the classic, wired Internet. The latter, of course, can not be found on any tablets or smartphones (perhaps, with rare exceptions, these will be hybrid devices like expensive transformer laptops that combine the functions of several gadgets). However, we did not answer the question: cellular - what is it on the iPad? Next, we will focus on the term itself.

Cellular





In principle, it is enough to translate this word from English - and everything will become clear. The fact is that cellular translates as "cellular communications." Accordingly, some tablets are equipped not only with a Wi-Fi module, but also with a SIM card slot. However, the iPad Wi-Fi + Cellular will cost significantly more than a regular one. Such a mark-up is primarily due to the fact that the placement of an additional communication module is quite expensive. Perhaps this is the company's marketing move , to differentiate the line of devices, which in turn will allow you to sell more gadgets. In any case, such iPads will cost significantly more, depending on the model of the device and the store, the margin can be almost double.

Apple iPad Wi-Fi





Cellular, or rather the cellular module for these devices, will be absent. Despite this, users will be able to access the Internet using Wi-Fi technology. Today, almost everyone has a home router that allows you to connect via the above wireless network. In addition, there are similar access points in many institutions, for example, cafes, restaurants and shopping centers.

Owners of the iPhone or other modern smartphones have the opportunity to use their gadgets in order to "distribute" Wi-Fi and use it on the iPad. However, this is not always convenient, and also drains the battery of mobile devices. Nevertheless, if you need a cheaper iPad or you buy it exclusively for home use, then this is a good purchase, which will save on unclaimed functions.

Other devices





There are many other manufacturers besides Apple that offer various variations of tablets and smartphones. Of course, the products of the above company are unique in their operating system, design, brand recognition, however, the choice of gadgets at the moment is very wide. There are many devices of various price categories on the market, the cost of which starts at about $ 50 and ends with a mark in the 2000 region. The latter, as a rule, are the flagships of well-known manufacturers such as Microsoft, Asus, HP and Lenovo, and the gadgets themselves, which have a high cost, - These are the previously mentioned hybrid devices that combine the functions of a tablet and laptop.

As for the average price category and more popular devices, here you can find a similar differentiation on the principle of "cellular Apple iPad". In other words, you can find the same tablet models, however, one of them will support mobile communications, and the other will not, which will itself affect the cost.

Summary

Thus, we answered the question: "cellular - what is it on ipad". However, the reason why the cost of devices so varies remains a mystery. It‚Äôs possible that this is beneficial for manufacturers and with the help of such a separation of devices, they hint to customers that, in addition to a tablet, it would be nice to have a smartphone as well. On the other hand, differentiation into gadgets with and without a cellular communication module can significantly save money if you need a device for home use. However, some tablets have full mobile phone functionality: you can make calls and send SMS messages from them.

Before buying a device, it is important to make sure that it meets your expectations, clarify the availability of a SIM card slot if you need a mobile network, or ask to show tablets designed to access the Internet only using Wi-Fi, again, if this option suits you.

If we return to the topic of margins, the latter will depend not only on the availability of a cellular module, but also on the amount of memory. This is especially true for devices from Apple, where there is no way to increase the storage space for files by acquiring a memory card, since there are no corresponding connectors for them. As a result, the cheapest iPad will be the gadget with the smallest amount of memory and without cellular, and the most expensive, respectively, - vice versa.
