Link to the Sea Monster

The abbreviation Kit, which is present in the name of any SLR camera, indicates that there is a lens in the box with the digital device. The Nikon D5100 Kit can be shipped from the factory in several trim levels that differ only in lens models. The most popular modification is a device with Nikon 18-55 VR optics. Such a camera is able to satisfy a home user who wants to shoot households indoors and outdoors.





But for creativity and semi-professional activity, it is better to give preference to a device with a rangefinder lens 18-140 VR or 18-105 VR. Their main feature is an increased focal length, which allows you to shoot distant objects. But for shooting portraits, none of the above lenses are suitable. Professionals recommend that beginners in such cases purchase a camera in the Body configuration (without a lens) and buy separately aperture optics with minimal focus.
