Holiday Village "Borodino": description, features, services and reviews

Paying attention to the choice of a place to stay is a serious task. A good solution would be a recreation center in a quiet corner, far from the hustle and bustle of the city.



Description

The recreation center "Borodino" located 160 kilometers from the city of St. Petersburg. This is a wonderful quiet place in the Vyborg district. The base is located on the banks of a beautiful forest lake. Here, amazing energy, filling with internal forces, bringing peace and serenity.

A distinctive feature of Borodinsky is, first of all, the homely cozy atmosphere. All cottages have an individual planning structure. Fisheries and farms are also available. Therefore, each of the guests will be able to fully enjoy trout fishing or watch the animals of the farm. In addition, you have the opportunity to engage in active leisure activities. The base operates year-round.

Specifications

Today in the most accessible segment the recreation center "Borodino" is presented. Prices, services, provided in this format are among the most acceptable.

The base includes nine cottages. They accommodate from 2 to 18 guests. They are located in a distance from each other, have a different layout. All houses are provided with hot and cold water, sewage, and satellite TV.



Services, entertainment

Like any modern recreation center, Borodinskoye has the whole spectrum of benefits for recreation at any time of the year. Various variations of accommodation and free time are possible:

  • The availability of sites for playing volleyball, soccer, croquet. A great opportunity to actively spend weekends and holidays. It is also possible to organize team competitions among friends.
  • Russian sauna. You can enjoy one of the two baths of your choice. Their capacity is from 2 to 8 people.
  • Rental equipment for riding (boats, catamarans, jet skis).
  • Children's playgrounds. For children there are special play areas with the necessary equipment.
  • Fishing on the lake. A great opportunity to forget about pressing problems, to escape from the hustle and bustle.
  • Exotic animals.

Thanks to farms and trout farms, you can always try products of your own production (domestic chicken, trout, sterlet, etc.).



Kitchen. Power Features

The recreation center "Borodino" involves self-catering holidaymakers. All houses are equipped with a full range of cooking equipment. You can cook from brought products, and it is also possible to purchase them directly on the spot. Another advantage is the ability to order dishes from environmentally friendly products (own production). Wuhu at the stake using alder wood, baked sterlet and many other dishes will be prepared especially for you. On site there are gazebos with barbecue facilities and dining areas.

Effective relaxation by the whole team is an excellent tool for creating a working spirit. The base has everything necessary for the implementation of this type of vacation.
