What should I do if I forget the password on the phone, and the information in the device is extremely necessary? Basically, reset methods are almost always associated with the inevitable loss of data. But there is one of the simple solutions to this problem. Let's remove the restrictive password from the Samsung Galaxy. Since the phone is an Android device, therefore, other devices running the same OS can also be successfully "released" from the state when the user forgot the password to lock the phone.

Method number 1

Launch your phone. Please note that your SIM card must be installed in the device. After the screen prompting you to enter a password appears, call this gadget. Without accepting the call, go to the “settings” menu, select the “Privacy” section, then “Reset All Data” and confirm with the corresponding consent. The password will be reset, and user information will remain in place.

Pressing the “Home” key, confirm the action. Then on “wipe cache partition delete all user”, and again “Home”. From the list, select "del" and press the "Home" button.
