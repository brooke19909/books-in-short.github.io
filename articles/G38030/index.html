Knitted suits: options, original models

Knitted clothes have firmly entered our lives, it is especially relevant in the cold season, although some people like crocheted or knitted items to wear on warm days. There are even craftswomen who make bathing suits. Fashionistas often prefer crocheted summer tops made from natural cotton fabric. Beautiful patterns emphasize the originality of products.

In warm weather, you can also wear a patterned T-shirt or top, leaving the sun most of the body. There are everyday knitted costumes, and there are products that are not ashamed to wear for a celebration. Everything depends on the originality of the style and quality of the threads. Also, the beauty of the products speaks of the professionalism and accuracy of the master.

A knitted suit should keep its shape well and emphasize the figure of the girl. At fashion shows you can find knitted suits, fashion designers try to diversify fashion for lovers of warm and soft products from year to year.

In the article, we will consider different options for knitted suits, what is better to put together, how to choose clothes to look stylish.

Tracksuits

This season knitted tracksuits have become incredibly popular. There are a lot of them on sale, but an experienced knitter can easily do such work at home. There are woolen suits, but most models incorporate cotton and acrylic. Equal proportions of such yarn contributes to the fact that the products are durable and warm.





Absolutely all female representatives like such things. In addition to convenience and comfort, sports suits made of yarn do not wrinkle at all. There are wide models with inside pockets, there are options with a hood. For slender girls fit styles that fit the figure.

They also differ in the shape of the legs. There are even ones with an elastic band below, there are flared wide models. Pockets can be patch and internal. Fashionable styles are considered options with cuffs on the sleeves and legs, having interesting rivets, zippers, ties, hooks and metal buttons, other accessories.

Corrugated models

Sports knitted suits can have both stocking with elastic bands and contain beautiful patterns. They can only be on the top of the suit, and on two at the same time. The top is made in the form of a sweater, "kangaroo" or sweatshirts on a snake with a hood and without. Such costumes are often decorated simply due to a beautiful pattern, however, two-tone options, for example, striped models, can be used.





There are also trendy youth acid colors for knitwear on sale. Such clothes are convenient not only for playing sports in the winter. It is comfortable to walk around the house. There are beautiful expensive suits that look great with fashionable sneakers and a long cardigan or coat. Colors can be taken in contrast. For example, for a gray or beige suit, pick up a bright coat, scarf or loose sack bag.

Suit - skirt and sweater

Usually, for women knitted suits with knitting needles, the skirt is made a straight, tight-fitting hips of the girl. The belt is almost always made out with an elastic band. The upper part of the suit can be wide with a fitted elastic band, as well as a loose cut. Recently, sweaters with different lengths of the lower edge, carved wedges or with a slight descent at the back are considered a fashionable trend.





The skirt can be knitted with stocking or have the same pattern as a sweater. There are both winter options from thick yarn, and summer sets crocheted. Often in such cases, a sleeve is shortened in three quarters.

The collar of a sweater can also be different: a collar, a rack, a V-shaped, a simple round neck.

Jacket plus skirt

The following types of suits can be credited to the casual clothes of business women. This is a variant of a tight tight skirt and sweatshirts. It can be on buttons or on a snake, with a bar in the middle or offset from the center. It can be wide, with an elastic at the waist, narrowed, tight-fitting figure with a belt.

Typically, a jacket is performed using corrugated patterns. Use both simple rhombuses, and the alternation of front and back loops. Patterns do not change the even structure of the product.





For festive events, you can pick up an openwork drawing that will look voluminous. The skirt is made more often with the usual stocking knit, sometimes inserting a patterned strip corresponding to the overall pattern, as in the photo above.

Cardigan Plus Dress

As an original knitted suit for women, you can consider the option of a narrow, skin-tight dress, draped over a cardigan. Such a kit is convenient in unstable weather of transitional periods of spring and autumn. If it gets hot, you can always remove the cardigan and stay in the dress. And if it gets colder, then vice versa.





The kit can be one color, as well as different shades. In winter, the dress is knitted with a long sleeve. Stylists recommend improvising with the length of both the dress and the cardigan.

In the article, we examined a small part of the possible options for knitted suits. They can be varied or worn in separate parts, adding things from other materials. The main thing is that the products look aesthetically pleasing and combine with each other. Here, the presence of taste and a sense of style already play a large role.
