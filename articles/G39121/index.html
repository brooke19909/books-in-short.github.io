A little bit about the resort ...

DIT Majestic Beach Resort 4 * (Bulgaria, Sunny Beach) is located in the very center of Sunny Beach. The resort area is a seven-kilometer strip on the coast of Bulgaria thirty kilometers from Burgas. Sunny Beach is remote from major highways, railways and major cities. Every year, the Blue Flag is awarded to him for the cleanliness and arrangement of the coast.

Sunny Beach is a great place to relax with kids. A shallow warm bay with a shallow shore is ideal for children. Ten kilometers from the resort in 2003 opened a water park, which is a favorite place for tourists. The infrastructure of the city is equipped not only for family vacations, but also is perfect for young people. The resort has a large number of nightclubs and discos, collecting numerous parties.

How to get to the hotel

To get to the DIT Majestic Beach Resort 4 * hotel (Sunny Beach), you can order a transfer from Burgas Airport, or you can cover a short distance yourself. Resort Attractions

Sunny Beach stretches along the Black Sea coast. The city was originally built as a resort, so most of its territory is occupied by restaurants, hotels, cafes, shops and other tourist infrastructure facilities. In the center are the best and most expensive complexes.

All the beaches of the city are municipal and completely free, but you have to pay for using umbrellas and deck chairs on them. Many vacationers simply prefer to purchase a beach umbrella once to avoid further expenses. The entire coast has a sand cover and a convenient gentle entrance to the sea. In season, the local beaches are quite crowded. Along the coast there are a large number of restaurants, cafes, rental equipment for water sports.

For diving enthusiasts who wish to dive, local guides offer to visit Varna and Sozopol. However, the most interesting object - Nessebar - is located just five kilometers from the resort. There, under water is part of the ancient city. Thousands of tourists come together every year to see such a sight. If you are far from diving, you can go on a boat trip on a boat with a transparent bottom. In calm calm weather, even through the water column, you can see the remains of an ancient civilization.



On the coast of Sunny Beach there are a large number of diving clubs that will help you learn the basics of scuba diving and make your first dives. Beginners will be interested in admiring the marine life.
