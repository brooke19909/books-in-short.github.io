How to make beautiful autumn bouquets with your own hands in the form of gorgeous roses? Walkthrough

Autumn plays with bright colors, pleasing the eye with a rich palette of fallen leaves. It is a pity that all these various shades can not be compared with the luxurious decoration of summer flower gardens. But, it turns out, you can make original autumn bouquets very similar to compositions from real roses with your own hands, which will delight the eye throughout the long winter. This article provides a detailed step-by-step description of working with photos.





What do autumn bouquets do with their own hands?

First of all, stock up with colorful maple leaves. Only intact and undamaged are suitable for operation. To create a lush and beautiful bud, it is best to use freshly picked and large leaves, even slightly dried leaves will crumble when bent. The color can be completely different, the main thing is that the amount of each of the shades is sufficient. It usually takes six to seven leaves to make one bud. Having gained experience, you can try to use a larger number - up to eight - ten. But then it will be difficult to name a flower and a bud. Such a bouquet of autumn leaves in the form of gorgeous large roses will look like a real masterpiece. Especially if the overall composition is decorated with bright clusters of berries: mountain ash or viburnum. In addition, any other natural material can be useful for decoration. So, be patient and you can get to work.

The procedure for making the bud

Do-it-yourself autumn bouquet in several stages:





1. Folding the middle. Place a large, even sheet on the table, directing its tail toward you. Make the first fold across the main vein. Turning the workpiece 90 ¬∞, start twisting it from top to bottom, turning it into a tube.





2. Laying the petals. Having made the middle, begin to form a bud. To do this, select leaves of approximately the same size or first use large, and then smaller ones. Take a leaf in your hands, pointing it with its tail down, and fold it in half. Attach it to the base of the bud.





Then, slightly bending the upper edge outward, begin to wrap the sheet around the middle part so that it is slightly visible.





Lay the remaining layers in the same way, evenly distributing them around the circumference.





3. Fastening the bud. After the flower is fully formed, wrap its base with thin wire or thread. It is very important to tighten and tie all the petals so that the bud does not fall apart.

Bouquet design

As you can see, making such autumn bouquets with your own hands is quite simple. Each new rose will turn out better and more accurately than the previous one. Make a few of them. The composition of buds of different colors: red, yellow, orange, looks very original. Or, forming flowers, move smoothly from one shade to another. For example, roses with a yellow center and red at the edges look exquisite. If you plan to put the resulting bouquet in a large vase, you need to be tall enough. To do this, form the stems of roses when wrapping the middle, putting a thin long stick in the leaf of the base. And then, in the process of arranging the bouquet, wrap the resulting flowers in large maple leaves. The composition will be even more elegant if it is decorated with clusters of mountain ash or viburnum and sprinkled with hair spray. Make unique autumn bouquets with your own hands from the collected natural material with your children. Refresh your familiar interior with bright colors!
