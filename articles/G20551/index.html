A practical solution - flowerpots from the tire on your own!

Create a practical flower vase

at the cottage you can from improvised materials. Old tires will come in handy: spacious flowerpots from the tires refresh the view of the summer cottage. They can be painted in any color and installed on a small piece of land. Flowerpots from car tires can be made with your own hands, but this will require physical strength.

So we need:

  • Tire from a passenger car. Rubber from trucks is not suitable, since it is much thicker and heavier, so it will be very difficult to cut from it.
  • Large sharpened scissors and a sharp knife.
  • Chalk for drawing a contour.
  • Gloves.

In addition to inventory, physical strength and caution when working with a knife are still needed.

Anyone who decides to make flowerpots from a tire should handle the knife very carefully during operation. When dealing with a sharp object, there is always a chance to get hurt. Therefore, precautions do not hurt. It is better to take a knife with a convenient handle. And in the process, it is advisable to use tight gloves.

The old tire is cleaned of dirt and allowed to dry. If your farm does not have one, visit any tire workshop. There they will gladly give you a whole mountain of tires, they will also say thanks. Choose one that is cleaner and not too worn.

So let's get started! Using a knife, we cut off the side surface of the tire (on one side). You need to cut along the edge of the tread with a pattern. It is from this part that the curly edges or ‚Äúpetals‚Äù of the future vase will be cut.

Now in this place we draw with chalk the contours of future petals. Gently cut the rubber along the intended line. If this process caused difficulties, then the knife is not sharpened enough. In such a situation, it‚Äôs good to have sharpening or replaceable knives on hand. The first petal is the most difficult. Having mastered it, you can grab the finished petal and cut, stretching the rubber a little. On a hot summer day, this procedure will be easier to do, as the rubber becomes softer from heating.

When the last petal is cut out, it remains only to turn out our vase. You can stop at this, or you can dream up: decorate the flowerpot with a stand, painting, decorative elements. To make it clear how to make a flowerpot from a tire, the photo below is presented.



Depending on what kind of rubber you had (with or without a disc) you can get a regular low flowerpot or a vase on a stand. Having mastered this simple technique, you can well decorate the courtyard of the house. Flowerpots from a tire look very colorful and esthetically if to paint them with paints for external works. It is first necessary to degrease the surface.

Indeed, on the inside (as you remember, the tire needs to be turned out) there is no less dirt and waste substances than outside. In order not to get too dirty, you can use old brushes and a solvent. If your flowerpots from the tire will soon become flowerbeds, then there is no need to paint the vase from the inside.

The process is over! The most difficult part is behind, there are pleasant trifles - choosing a place for a flower bed from a tire, laying the soil, sowing seeds or planting seedlings. Such a garden decoration is a real find for lovers of annual flowers. After all, it is the annuals that have nowhere to plant after the entire garden is seeded with vegetable crops. The flowerpot can always be attached to the corner of the garden or at the porch.
