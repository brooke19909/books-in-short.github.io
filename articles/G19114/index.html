Details on how to insert a dog in a zipper

Today we will tell you how to insert a dog in a zipper. This question may be relevant for various reasons: the dog may break, get lost or jump off the rail. Often the product with which a similar problem occurs is given in the studio. The master sews a new one in place of the previous lightning or changes the dog to the old one. This can most often be done independently.

Repairs



To solve the question of how to insert a dog in a zipper, we need a needle and thread. Let's consider in order several repair options. If the zipper is a detachable type, the dog can simply jump off and remain in the hand. In this case, insert it from above to the side on which the limiter with a special socket is located. The slider should be positioned down with the flat side.

Replacement



Now we will consider how to insert a dog in a zipper if the necessary element is lost or broken. In this case, we need a new slider. It can be purchased separately. To select the optimal item, contact the seller. He will pick up the necessary number of the lightning slider. In certain cases, you will have to purchase several dogs. For example, on the covers often put two runners that slide towards.

Carefully inspect the side of the braid on which the dog was installed. Most likely, a special limiter jumped off the rail on top. It is necessary to keep the slider in the upper position. If the limiter actually moved, we correct it with the pliers so that the dog does not jump out again.

Consider another option. If the limiter we need is lost, we do the fastening with a needle and thread. Everything is simple here. Binding is done several times over the edge of the braid. She will not allow the runner to jump off again.

One piece design

Now consider how to insert a dog into a zipper if it is one-piece. In this case, we slightly divide the ends of the rail. Next, insert each of them into the dog. Gently move the slider down. He will move with effort. Significant pressure should not be applied, otherwise the rails could be damaged. If the runner refuses to move with all the efforts, slightly bend the slots on the dog. Move the dog down. We press the slots with pliers, bringing them to their original state.

When purchasing dogs, you should pay special attention to the name and number. Runners for zippers come in various types: twisted, metal and tractor. Zippers with lost or frayed teeth must be replaced. If it does not close well or is too tight, it can be adjusted. You can do this by holding the slider with your pliers.

Secret lightning



Now consider another curious case. To solve the question of how to insert a dog on a secret zipper, you can use one of the methods described above. However, first you need to remove the stopper from the ends. After that we pass to installation of the slider. Once the problem is resolved, everything must be returned to its place.

It should be noted that the zipper is an easy and practical invention. It is more reliable than buttons, more important than buttons and more convenient than shoelaces. In order to minimize problems with this element of clothing, you should pay attention to its design, material and shape. It is on these characteristics that the properties of lightning depend.

A metal clasp, for example, is considered the most durable. Its nickel or brass teeth are fixed on a special dense fabric and adhere incredibly firmly to each other. However, such a lightning bolts more often than others, and it also scratches your fingers and clings. Therefore, on winter jackets, as a rule, they use wide, but at the same time plastic zippers.
