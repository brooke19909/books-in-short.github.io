Plasticine spider-man: how to sculpt

Spider-Man is an extraordinarily capable hero in American comics, who have gained immense popularity. It is distinguished by dexterity, strength and the ability to move in any direction with the help of a web. Films and cartoons have been made about Spider-Man; literally every person in the world knows about him, starting with children and ending with adults. This superhero takes third place in the list of the top 100 superheroes from comic books of all time. Let's see how to mold it from plasticine.

What is needed for modeling

In order to mold a spider-man from plasticine, you need a board on which the process of work will take place. You also need a special knife - the stack and clay itself: red, blue, white and black. It is also worthwhile to prepare a piece of cotton wool in advance - it will help clean the board from the material used adhering to it.

If you already prepared all this, let's get to work!

How to mold from spider-man plasticine: a process

  • First of all, we sculpt our heads. To do this, take red plasticine and roll an oval out of it.
  • Next is the eyes. We make two small black circles, flatten them so that they become flat. We do the same with white plasticine, but we take the pieces a little smaller. Impose white over black.
  • We turn to the body of a spider-man from plasticine. Here we need a blue color. We roll up a thick sausage and use the stack to divide it (vertically) at the bottom in half. Thus legs are obtained.
  • For hands we use two small sausages.
  • The hands are made like this: we roll up red plasticine so that we get two ovals. Flatten them with your fingers a little.
  • We also make boots from red. Again, you need to roll up two ovals, and slightly flatten them.
  • For a red cloak over the body, you need to roll an oval and flatten it with your fingers until it becomes flat.

Assembly

After all the details are molded, you need to start assembling the spider-man from plasticine.

  • First you need to connect a red cloak that goes over the body of a spider-man. Just fasten it on top.
  • Next, attach the head to the body.
  • We attach eyes to the head (on the face).
  • We connect our hands on both sides of the body, at the same distance.
  • We attach hands to blue sausages - hands. Then, using a plasticine knife, we cut out the thumb of Spider-Man.
  • The next step is to attach red boots to the bottom of the legs.

That's all, our plasticine spider-man is ready!
