Standard details

By looking at the last digit in the row where the bank card number is, you can see the verification number. It is the result of a mathematical algorithm performed using the previous digits. In addition, there are certain combinations to verify the authenticity of a card or determine its validity. The latter is often explicitly indicated in the form of a month and a year, in addition, on the front side you can see the name and logo of the bank, the name and surname of the owner in English, the sign of a particular payment system, the bank branch number where the card was issued, etc. the reverse side is usually a magnetic strip, to which an area adjacent to the sample signature of the owner is adjacent. Although recently issued including cards where an autograph is not required.

Where the bank card number is located may be of interest to those who wish to pay it via the Internet. However, it should be borne in mind that in some cases it may be necessary not the card number, but the number of the account attached to it, as well as data on the correspondent account and bank individual code of the credit institution. It is indicated in the contract for banking services and consists of (at the moment) twenty digits. Therefore, when calculating, it is imperative to take into account what specific details are required.

We use carefully
