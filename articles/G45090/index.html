We start to weave bracelets made of elastic bands on the sidewalk slingshot

Even a child can handle the weaving of the Sidewalk bracelet. However, such needlework will interest both young people and girls. To get a good result, you need perseverance and attentiveness.





How to weave pavement bracelets:

  1. Take two rubber bands of the same color. Cross them with a figure of eight between the two legs of the slingshot.
  2. Put two more elastic bands of the same color on the legs of the slingshot. However, this time they do not need to be twisted.
  3. From the right leg of the slingshot, remove two rubber bands of the first row to two rubber bands of the second.
  4. Put two more rubber bands of the same color on both legs of the slingshot. Remove all other gum from the left leg to the center of these rubber bands.
  5. Now again put on two elastic bands of the same color on the slingshot. From the right leg, remove all other gum on their middle.
  6. Put two rubber bands of this color on both legs of the slingshot. From the left leg, remove all other rubber bands in the middle of these rubber bands.
  7. Now put on two rubber bands of the next color on both legs of the slingshot. Repeat all the steps described in 2-6.
  8. After that, wear rubber bands of a new color and repeat steps 2-6 again. Weave the bracelet in this way, changing colors until it is the right size.
  9. In the last row you should have one pair of rubber bands on the right leg, and two on the left. Remove from the left leg the bottom pair of rubber bands in the middle of the top.

Now you just have to remove the elastic bands from the fork with a hook and put on them the hook-hook prepared in advance. Then the bracelet is put on the hand, and its free side clings to the free edge of the hook located on the other side.

Weave an original bracelet on fingers

How to weave a sidewalk bracelet on your fingers? The answer to this question is indecently simple. You only need to use the index and middle finger and weave a bracelet on them in the same way as you would do it with a crochet.

How to weave ‚ÄúSidewalk‚Äù on the fingers out of rubber bands:

  1. Put two rubber bands of the same color on the index finger and middle finger. Before that, they need to be crossed with a figure eight.
  2. Now put on the rubber bands without twisting them. Remove from the right finger the gum of the previous row in the middle of the new gum.
  3. Now put on two new rubber bands on both fingers. Remove all the rubber bands from the left finger in the middle of the new rubber bands.




Thus, continue weaving until the bracelet is finished. At the end, remove the lower elastic bands from the left finger to the middle of the upper elastic bands and fix with a hook.
