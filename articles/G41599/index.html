Location relative to tourist and other facilities

Like other apart-hotels, which are part of the Velvet Seasons mega-complex (Sochi), Alexander Garden has a developed infrastructure. In addition, guests of the resort who choose their place of stay will be able to easily reach tourist sites such as the Olympic Park, Formula 1 tracks and many others. Not far from the Alexander Garden are also located Ornithological Park and the Park of Southern Cultures.

Beach

Hotel "Alexander Garden" is located at some distance from the Black Sea coast. Vacationers can use the well-maintained urban pebble beach, the length of which is more than 2.5 km. It stretches along the Imereti Bay, and there is a rental point for water equipment, a mini market and a cafe. Sun loungers and umbrellas are installed.

The Alexandrovsky Sad quarter (Sochi) is located 1.5 km from the city beach, so you can walk to it in 15 minutes. In addition, at certain hours, a shuttle service is organized for vacationers (round trip).

Excursions

Those who are traveling for the first time to rest in the resorts of the Krasnodar Territory often ask what sights can be seen in Sochi. "Alexander Garden" is a hotel that can become a convenient starting point for interesting excursions not only in the city, but also in Abkhazia and the surrounding mountains.

In addition to the standard program, which includes visits to Olympic venues, tourists usually choose a trip to Krasnaya Polyana and the Oceanarium, as well as to Stalin's dacha. The last tour includes a visit to Abkhazia, during which travelers enjoy the beauties of this small country and get acquainted with its sights. It is very popular among tourists, and it is recommended to its friends by almost everyone who rested in Sochi.

Reviews

Many tourists who have recently returned from vacation, note that one of the best options that Sochi offers is the Alexander Garden. In their reviews, as the advantages of this hotel complex, they note the cleanliness of the rooms, well-groomed territory and a varied menu in the dining room, the chefs of which delight guests with delicious dishes. In general, tourists positively evaluate the Alexander Garden Hotel, noting that it is an excellent option for an inexpensive vacation, especially if we consider it in terms of price and quality.
