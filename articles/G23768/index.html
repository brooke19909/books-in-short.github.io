Roslavl hotels: description, reviews

In the ancient city of Roslavl, Murmansk region, many cult objects of historical and cultural value have been preserved. There are many connoisseurs of antiquity who come here to admire wonderful architectural structures. Roslavl hotels are ready to meet tourists and provide them with comfortable conditions for relaxation.

Hotel "Factory"

From the hotel and entertainment complex "Factory" to the city center is only 15 minutes walk. Nearby is a supermarket "Magnet" and a shopping center "Birch". The on-site caf√© serves European cuisine. For an additional fee you can use the services of bowling, billiards, relax in a nightclub, movie theater, relax in the sauna. If you are interested in hotels with breakfast in the city of Roslavl, the Fabrika Hotel is an excellent option. For guests with a private car, public parking is free. There is free wireless internet access. The hotel has a gift shop, luggage storage, laundry. The following room categories are offered to accommodate guests:

  • standard double with a double or two single beds - 2500 rubles per day for two and 1700 for one person;
  • Suite with a large double bed and a sofa bed - 4000 rubles per day.

The price includes breakfast. If desired, full or half board can be included in the stay. The Hotel Factory in Roslavl is located at ul. Krasnoarmeyskaya, house 9.

Hotel "Guest yard"

You can drive to the city center in ten minutes from this hotel. It will take as much time to get to the train station. The on-site cafe features Russian cuisine. Car owners can use the services of a paid secure parking. Wireless internet is free. Upon request, a shuttle service can be arranged. Laundry and ironing services are available. Hotel Guest Yard is one of those hotels in Roslavl where you can stay with pets. To accommodate guests, the following room options are offered:

  • standard double with two single beds - 2300 rubles per day;
  • standard double with a double bed - 2645 rubles per day for two and 2070 for one person.

Each room has air conditioning, cable TV, refrigerator. Complimentary toiletries are provided for all guests. The Guest Dvor hotel is located in the city of Roslavl, in the village of Pavlovka.

Hostel Transit

To accommodate guests can choose these options:

  • general triple room (single beds) - 800 rubles per day per person;

The Transit hostel in Roslavl is located at ul. Lugovaya, house 20.
