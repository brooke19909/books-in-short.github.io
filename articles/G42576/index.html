Addresses of RESO offices in Moscow. Insurance company "RESO-Garantia"

There are a lot of insurance companies in our country. There are all-Russian, whose central offices are in large cities, and branches are scattered throughout Russia. There are local companies that are usually engaged in a narrow type of insurance, for example, motor third party liability insurance, and exist only in this territory. The minus of the latter is that if something happened in you or the property insured in such a company in another region where it does not have a representative office, it may refuse insurance payments or you will have to seek financial coverage for the damage for a very long time. Also, these companies often close for unknown reasons. That is why it is worth contacting large insurance organizations with an already established reputation. One of these companies is RESO-Garantia. Most likely, everyone saw its branches in their city. In the article we will tell about the company, its services, as well as the addresses of RESO offices in Moscow and how to get to them.

About company

The insurance company RESO-Garantia has been operating in the market since 1991. It is one of the largest insurance companies in Russia - it has about 860 offices throughout the country and more than 10 million customers as of 2016. The company provides the population with more than 100 types of various insurance services.





The RESO insurance company has the highest degree of reliability, has been repeatedly recognized as the best in various categories, and the services comply with international standards.

RESO is a group of companies. It also includes SMK RESO, an insurance medical company that provides compulsory health insurance and has successfully served several million Russian citizens for over 20 years.

The group of companies also includes:

  • "RESO-Car service" (car service, service station, where the insured cars that were injured in an accident send to when the policyholder decides to repair the service station, rather than receiving hand payments).
  • Bank "RESO-Credit" (engaged in the issuance of loans, registration of deposits for individuals and mortgage lending).
  • RESO-Leasing (a company that leases cars and trucks and special equipment to companies).
  • Other insurance companies operating abroad (in Belarus, Ukraine, Kazakhstan and Armenia).

The services of the main one of the RESO-Garantia company are divided into several categories: services to individuals, commercial entities and additional. The list of the latter includes calling an agent, making an appointment with a doctor, calculating the cost of insurance, issuing an online policy and others. The first two categories of services are discussed below.

Services for private clients

In the company "RESO-Garantia" private individuals are provided with a wide range of life and property insurance services:

  • Car insurance (CTP, CASCO, online purchase of a policy, technical assistance on the road to their customers).
  • Real estate insurance (house, apartment, garage, land).
  • Life and health insurance (travel insurance for obtaining a visa and traveling abroad, from accidents for children, adults or drivers, compulsory medical insurance, voluntary medical insurance).
  • Green card for traveling abroad in your car.

The cost of all services can be calculated using the online calculator. According to the law, the occurrence of an insured event must be reported within 3 days, and documents must be brought in and an application for payments to be made within 10 days after the incident. Subject to deadlines, there will be no further problems.

If you have suffered as a result of an insured event, you will need to provide a certificate stating that you were on sick leave and therefore could not visit the insurer for an expired time. You should also act with travel paper.
