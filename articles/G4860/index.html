Hotel Haydarpasha Palace 5 *: infrastructure, rooms and reviews of tourists

Hotel Haydarpasha Palace 5 * is an excellent option for accommodation during your vacation in Alanya. It is located in a surprisingly picturesque place of Konakli, an hour drive from the airport of Antalya and half an hour from the airport of Gazipasa. The hotel is located near the city, so the main attractions of Alanya are very close.

Haydarpasha Palace 5 * is located on the second line, to the beach 200 m. There you can get through the underpass. The beach is owned by the hotel.

The hotel is constantly under reliable guard. For all questions, you can contact the reception to the administrators, who also work around the clock.



Rooms

Hotel Haydarpasha Palace 5 * has a total of 496 rooms. Among them there are standard single and double, family standard rooms, class "luxury", "economy". The hotel also offers non-smoking rooms and facilities for people with disabilities. If you are planning a vacation with a small child, then, at the request of the parents, a cot can be put in the room.

Each room is equipped with air conditioning and free Wi-Fi. For fire safety, smoke detectors are installed around the entire hotel, including rooms. For movement between floors there is a comfortable and roomy elevator.

Almost every room has a cozy terrace. It offers sea, mountain, garden or pool views.

Pets are not allowed in Haydarpasha Palace 5 *.



Facilities and amenities

  • for the convenience of tourists in Haydarpasha Palace hotel 5 * free check-in is organized: there is the possibility of late check-in or check-out;
  • transfer from and to the airport;

For tourists with children:

  • parents can leave their babies with an experienced nanny;
  • the hotel has a children's playroom, a playground on the street, a children's club is organized;
  • in the dining room for kids there are special chairs.

Nutrition

Haydarpasha Palace 5 * works according to the usual Turkish all-inclusive system. The hotel has several dining rooms, restaurants ("buffet" system), cafe, pool bar.



Fun and relaxation

On the territory of the hotel animators for children and adults are constantly working. Enchanting shows, theater performances, contests, competitions, etc. are arranged for guests.

Fans of outdoor activities will be able to go skiing or banana, play beach volleyball, order a desert safari, etc.

For hotel guests are available:

  • bathhouse;
  • massage;
  • spa treatments;
  • jacuzzi;
  • library;
  • gym;
  • bowling;
  • aerobics classes.

Rules of residence

Check-in is from 14 p.m. until midnight, and check-out takes place around the clock.

If the vacationer removes the reservation or changes it two days before arrival, then no penalty will be charged. When the reservation is canceled later than this, the vacationer must fully pay the reservation amount.
