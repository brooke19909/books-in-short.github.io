Samsung S3520: description and specifications

In 2011-2012. most buyers have already preferred smartphones with touch screens. However, there are people who trust mechanical buttons, which are more used to. It was for this audience that the Korean manufacturer developed the Samsung C3520 clamshell phone. The model does not have advanced functionality. This phone is designed to make calls. It is worth noting that this service is well developed. Buyers will not be disappointed with the quality of materials, the ability to make changes to the settings, a decent battery life. So, let's take a closer look at the characteristics of this model.

In the lineup, the manufacturer introduced several color solutions. Classic colors - gray and black. They are great for both men and women. They look solid. But fashionistas are offered a phone with a pink case. It looks interesting and fresh.

In the Samsung S3520 phone, the case material is of high quality. The surface is slightly rough, which significantly reduces the level of soiling. Also thanks to this, the phone does not slip at all. Mechanical damage does not appear on the panels. Build quality. With the lid closed, you can observe a slight backlash. There is a special mechanism with automatic tuning. The design of the rear panel is concise. Only two elements are located here: the camera lens and the speaker.

In the Samsung S3520, the side ends are not heavily loaded with buttons. The headphone jack is on the left. There is also a standard slot for a memory card and microUSB. Keyboard

What can be said about the Samsung C3520 keyboard? It is comfortable with large buttons. The latter are pressed gently. The control panel and digital block are standard. The first consists of two soft keys, a joystick with chrome trim and reset / receive buttons.

Immediately below this panel is a digital unit. The buttons are rectangular in shape, slightly convex. Separated by thin slots. When you click on them, a soft click is heard. Designations are clearly visible. There is a backlight, but it is uneven. Due to the large size of the buttons, erroneous clicks are almost completely eliminated.

Screen and camera

Samsung S3520 is equipped with a QVGA-type screen. Given that the device is designed for calls and messaging, its capabilities are enough for comfortable use. The camera is also not of the highest quality. For shooting, a 1.3 megapixel sensor is installed. No improvements in the form of flash and autofocus are provided. In order to take a picture, you have to rotate the phone horizontally, as the interface is adapted for landscape orientation.

The user can bring to the desktop 15 applications that he uses most often. In addition, you can move the gadget with the clock or remove it from the screen.
