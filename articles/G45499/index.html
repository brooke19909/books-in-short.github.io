Hotel Awa Resort Koh Chang (Koh Chang, Thailand): overview, description and reviews of tourists

If you are very tired, and the rhythm of the city begins to gradually strain, there is a desire to relax abroad surrounded by exotic nature, then definitely go to the island of Koh Chang in Thailand. Believe me, this is exactly what you have long dreamed of.

Everything that a sophisticated tourist will see here will not leave him indifferent, but, on the contrary, will give a lot of positive emotions and impressions. These are snow-white, clean sandy beaches, an amazing underwater world, an incredible amount of coral reefs, murmuring and transparent waterfalls, unprecedented vegetation and, of course, excellent, specific Thai cuisine, where every dish is a masterpiece.

What more can you want? And you can want a first-class holiday in a cozy and beautiful place where a tourist has long been waiting for an excellent room and service at the highest level. Such a place on the island of Koh Chang is available - this is the hotel Awa Resort Koh Chang 4 *.





First meeting

The hotel complex is located in the heart of a small island, namely, not far from the popular Klong Prao beach. From the airport and to the hotel the taxi driver will take the client in just half an hour.

Awa Resort Koh Chang 3 * first opened its arms to its guests in 2004. Since then, it has been reconstructed more than once, so it always looks like brand new, only built.

At the moment, everyone who wants to visit the hotel expects excellent service, comfortable rooms, amazing nature and much more.

It should be noted that each tourist is not easily settled in an ordinary room, and in apartments - a bungalow, where it is difficult to see the pristine nature from the windows, and the room itself is made in the style of an oriental epic called Ramayana.





Room Features

Each room in the hotel Awa Resort Koh Chang 4 * (Chang) has free Wi-Fi, and also for guests:

  • Air conditioning with remote control.
  • Large bathtub or shower stall to choose from.
  • Hairdryer, shampoos, balms, warm towels, slippers, and a bathrobe.
  • Large flat-screen TV with the most affordable channel list.
  • Spacious balcony or loggia, where there are several chairs and a table. Here you can see the views of the green area and the pool with warm, clear water.
  • Small fridge.
  • Minibar with a variety of drinks from alcoholic to sparkling water. There is a charge for using the bar.
  • A telephone where you can order room cleaning, as well as tea, coffee and even a delicious breakfast.
  • Wardrobe and clothes hanger.
  • Iron and laundry service are available if desired.
  • Electric kettle.

All the wishes of customers is the law for the hotel staff, which, with care and patience, ensures that all vacationers are in a good mood and are satisfied with their stay at the hotel.

In total, the hotel complex has 70 rooms, they are of various capacities and comforts. The cleaning here is very responsible. Everything just sparkles with cleanliness.

Awa Resort Koh Chang accepts both credit cards and cash payments, and sometimes a loan agreement for accommodation with subsequent payment is made.





Additional services

There are a number of additional services at the hotel, but most of them are provided at an additional cost:

  • Parking, it is one of the free services.
  • Possibility to go fishing. The hotel is located two kilometers from the village, where most vacationers fish. The hotel provides rental fishing rods and even a motor boat, as well as other equipment for successful fishing.
  • Terrace for sunbathing.
  • Large pool with warm water, lots of slides and entertainment.
  • B-B-Q. It can be prepared by both the vacationers themselves and the chef at their request, of course, not free of charge.
  • Large garden with marvelous bushes, plants and flowers.
  • Services of a professional masseur.
  • A souvenir shop where everyone has the opportunity to purchase memorable souvenirs for themselves, relatives and friends.




Leisure

For those who like not just relaxing in the shade of large palm trees and basking in the sun in a hotel, it is available for use:

  • A sports complex where there are exercise equipment, a fitness center and many necessary attributes, accessories.
  • SPA-center. Here, vacationers can relax, perform a number of wellness treatments, body wraps, anti-aging massages.
  • Entertainment programs in the hotel restaurant with a disco and participation in carnival parties.
  • Providing excursions.




Business services

For a small material reward, those hotel guests who came to Thailand for the purpose of work can use:

  • Computer, fax, photocopy and scanner.
  • Translation services.
  • Rent a conference room.
  • Banquet hall for holding and other business events.

Nutrition

Now let's talk about what tourists are fed, vacationers in a hotel complex called Awa Resort Koh Chang.

The hotel has three small restaurants, as well as a bar where you can always enjoy delicious cocktails and just a snack.

Two restaurants work according to the schedule: 10.00 - 22.00, and one receives visitors from 7.00 to 23.00.

Thai and European cuisines are prepared at the establishments, and delicious seafood is offered according to an exclusive recipe. As tourists note, the kids are provided with a separate menu, as well as high comfortable high chairs.

It should be added that any dish here is always beautifully decorated, fresh and tasty.





Children

Vacationers who come to rest with children like everything at this hotel. First of all, parents note that a special playground with swings and carousels has been built for the kids on the territory, and if necessary, it is possible to use the services of a nanny.

Also in the hotel there are a lot of toys, bicycles, scooters and other entertainments for young tourists. That is, neither children nor their parents are bored.

Beaches

At a distance of a kilometer from the Awa Resort Koh Chang there is a huge beach that can be reached on foot, if you wish, taking a walk around the small island or on a bus that runs regularly from the hotel and back. The schedule is always at the bus stop and at the reception of the hotel. In general, transport runs every half hour.

Towels and sun protection products are allowed from the room from the room.

The sandy shore itself is quite wide. It is covered with snow-white sand. The entrance to the sea is often smooth, gentle, without stones and pits.

Along the promenade are numerous shops, cafes, bars, restaurants, nightclubs.

But those who want to stay in silence and just enjoy the scenery will be able to go to the northern part of the island, there are always few people and not so hot. In the shade of palm trees, sitting on a deck chair and drinking juice, you can always meditate and forget for a while about the city bustle.





Reviews

Awa Resort Koh Chang 4 * receives numerous reviews, most of them are positive, to some extent even admired. This is not a promotion at all, but the real impressions of people who liked to relax in Thailand.

According to holidaymakers, the hotel complex is dominated by a fabulous romantic atmosphere. What is the landscaped area with numerous fountains, gazebos drowning in greenery, streams where small fish live.

Awa Resort Koh Chang receives reviews with a plus sign for excellent service and always open, helpful and welcoming staff. There is no hint of rudeness, discontent.

Numerous guests are delighted with the taste of Thai cuisine, it is something delicious, they admit. The morning buffet is also pleasing.

Add a small spoonful of tar. Tourists are not happy with the fact that sometimes the prices for excursions are too high, and it is forbidden to walk in these places on their own.

In general, everything is wonderful, the vast majority of vacationers are happy with the time spent in the hotel and on the island and recommend that their friends definitely choose a hotel when traveling to Koh Chang Island, where each client is always welcome. Here, the owners and all staff are committed to providing their guests a comfortable stay in a clean, comfortable and modern hotel complex. Until they succeed!
