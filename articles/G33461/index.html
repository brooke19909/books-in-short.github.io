Impressive Attractions



In recent years, Ukraine has been losing the glory of a country rich in sights of various cultures and historical periods. To attract public attention to historical and architectural monuments and maintain the interest of tourists, the action "7 Wonders of Ukraine" was held. Castles, fortresses, palaces and other unique objects from all over the country were considered as nominees. In total, 138 attractions were considered in the competition. Monuments were evaluated by specialists in the field of culture, history, architecture and tourism. 21 objects were selected for participation in the finals; seven of the most interesting sights were selected by online voting.

Amazing Natural Attractions



Ukraine is a country rich not only in historical architectural sights, but also in interesting natural sites. After the publication of the results of the 7 Wonders contest, the public expressed the opinion that many protected areas and miraculous monuments were undeservedly forgotten against the background of ancient palaces and castles. Many bloggers of the country began to write personally about unique sites and natural attractions worthy in their opinion to become "the very best." Why argue, because all this is invaluable information for Ukrainians who do not know what to do on the weekend, and tourists from other countries planning excursions on vacation.

Canyons and Lakes

In Ukraine, you can see a variety of natural landscapes and attractions. In the informal list of the most interesting miraculous monuments of the country, the first line is Bukovinsky Canyon. Rocks, lakes, an abundance of vegetation - this place is ideal for a quiet contemplative vacation and interesting photo shoots. Another interesting attraction of Ukraine is Blue Lake. This object is located in the Transcarpathian region, near the Sinyak massif. It is believed that the lake is not only a beautiful natural site, but also a real natural health resort. According to many, the blue waters of this reservoir are healing.

Active volcano and magic tree



In the Sumy region is one of the most unusual attractions, included in the list of "Seven Natural Wonders of Ukraine." This is a unique apple colony. At first glance, it might seem that you have a garden consisting of trees of an irregular shape. However, in reality this is one apple tree whose branches, touching the ground, take root and turn into new trunks. There is a legend according to which the tree was planted by the gardener of Prince Meshchersky. There is in Ukraine and its own active volcano, it is called "Starunya". You should not be afraid of it, because instead of lava dirt erupts, but you should definitely watch this miracle with your own eyes. In the vicinity of the unique object, the remains of a mammoth were found, according to legends, the treasures of Oleksa Dovbush are stored in the volcano itself.

Waterfall and basalt pillars



Among the 7 natural wonders of Ukraine there is a truly unusual magical zone. This is a birch bark lake, which is popularly called Black and Enchanted. The water in it is always cold, and even the most advanced equipment is not able to establish the exact depth of the reservoir. The people believe that the lake has no bottom, the version of scientists is more prosaic - the bottom is double, the first false, from moss and spruce needles. In the Rivne region, between the villages of Bazaltovoye and Novy Berestovets, you can see miraculous pillars of basalt, the outlines of which resemble traditional American landscapes.
