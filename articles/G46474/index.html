Holidays in Athens, Greece: beaches, entertainment, hotels, reviews of tourists

The capital of Greece is an attractive destination for tourists from all over the world. The reason for the popularity of the city lies in numerous historical sights and architectural monuments. But cultural leisure is not the only reason why guests come to the capital. Holidays in Athens at sea attracts lovers of beaches and sea beauty. The Athenian Riviera is the place where every tourist should visit.

Time for vacation

When planning a vacation in Athens, of course, it is important to choose the most successful time. In the midst of summer, the capital is very hot. Experienced travelers believe that the most comfortable time to travel is May and June, as well as September and October. On the other hand, if you want to spend all the time on the beach, mid-summer is quite suitable. At this time, the air temperature reaches 32 degrees. But in the coastal areas of the city, the heat is much easier. For excursions, August and July are not the right time, but for a beach holiday it is just the right time. In May, heat is set in Athens. The temperature reaches +24 degrees. But at night it‚Äôs still cool. With the advent of June, the daytime temperature rises to +28 degrees, and the night temperature to +22 degrees. By the beginning of summer, the water temperature in the sea warms up to +22 degrees. Therefore, it is time to open the beach season.

In Greece, the holiday season usually begins on July 25 and lasts until September 5. At this time, local residents, like visiting tourists, go to the resorts. This is worth remembering when choosing a time for vacation.

Beaches

If you are interested in a beach holiday in Athens, and not just attractions, you need to explore all areas of the city in advance. There are beaches not only in the capital itself, but also in the surrounding areas. From May to July, the coast is crowded with people, so the hotel must be planned in advance. Please note that on vacation in Athens, you may have to pay a visit to the beach. This is due to the fact that part of the coast is a private territory. It is believed that the best beaches are located in the southeast region.

For relaxation in Athens, you can recommend sandy beaches in the resorts of Votsalakia, Alimos, Glyfada, Voula Beach, Kavouri, Vouliagmeni Beach, Varkiza Beach, Kokkino Limanaki.

Going on vacation, you can stay directly in Athens or in the surrounding areas. The choice of location depends on your goals. If beach holidays are important for you in Athens, you can choose one of the resorts in the area, and come to the capital for excursions. You can rent a car in the city. You will need it if you decide to spend a vacation near Athens. The region has excellent transport links, so you can get everywhere by bus.

Resorts Near Athens: Faliro

For a vacation in Athens at sea, the Faliro district is ideally located, located just five kilometers from the center. The prestigious suburb is very attractive for tourists. The region is interesting for a relaxing holiday and a great beach. In 2010, the promenade, which is the center of resort life, was reconstructed here. Faliro has a sandy city beach. The water near the shore is warm, but a small storm invariably brings cold streams from the depths. In such periods, the sea acquires an uncomfortable temperature, but soon warms up again. Faliro can be called a mini-resort. It has its own promenade, taverns, shops, cafes, a large park and other entertainment venues. The choice of housing, as you know, is huge in the resort. In Faliro you can find housing for 4-8 thousand rubles. In the region there are not only 2 to 5 star hotels, but also apartments.

Vouliagmeni

Vouliagmeni is another prestigious suburb of Athens with numerous coniferous plantings, thanks to which the air of the resort is incredibly clean and healing. Here is Akti beach, equipped with wooden deck chairs, a volleyball court, locker rooms, umbrellas and other attributes. There are cafes right on the coast. Once, one of the local beaches was even awarded the Blue Flag. On the coast, tourists have the opportunity to ride a boat and engage in water sports. Playgrounds are equipped for the kids on the beaches.

In the suburbs there is a lake with thermal waters. The pond does not freeze even in winter. The temperature in it reaches +22 degrees. According to experts, it was formed in prehistoric times due to the collapse of a large cave. Tourists come to the pond to treat skin diseases and joints. Everything here is equipped not only for relaxation, but also for treatment. Holidays near Athens in different areas are different in cost. Vouliagmeni is considered an expensive resort. Accommodation in a budget hotel here will cost 15-25 thousand rubles.

The city is attractive not only in terms of attractions, but also a beach holiday with kids. Athens has everything for children - parks, the warm sea, entertainment centers. This is not just a big metropolis living at its own pace, but a cozy city in which there is something attractive. Athens is beautiful and diverse, for this reason every traveler will find something interesting for himself here.

Holidays in the metropolis are always very eventful and interesting. There are an incredible number of museums and all kinds of attractions. However, it‚Äôs important not to go too far with the kids, since they get tired very quickly.

According to reviews, a vacation in Athens with children is very comfortable. In the city there are a lot of hotels of different levels, apart-hotels, villas and apartments. What to see with children?

Family vacations in Athens (Greece) can take place not only on the beach. The city has an incredible amount of ancient attractions and other interesting places. If while your child is not interested in historical monuments, you can find other interesting entertainments. For example, experienced travelers recommend a visit to the Gulandris. The Museum of Natural History will appeal to representatives of all ages. The most famous exhibit of the institution is a huge skeleton of triceratops. Children will be interested to see stuffed animals that cannot be distinguished from real animals.

No less interesting is the Military Museum. Its exposition contains the most ancient devices for fighting at sea and on land, military equipment, modern weapons, warships and models of Greek fortresses.

Children will be interested in visiting ship-museums - the destroyer Velos and the battleship Georgios Averof. You can also go to the Piraeus Maritime Museum. Another place worth visiting with your children is the Athenian Planetarium, one of the best in the world. Modern equipment allows you to create stunningly realistic picture. Visitors get the impression that they are actually plowing the space.

On vacation in Athens with children, you can visit the amusement park, which is divided into two parts. Each is focused on a specific age of visitors. It is best to go to the park in the evening when it sparkles in multi-colored lights. The amusement park is always very fun and interesting.

Athens has many children's entertainment centers. In them, kids can have fun, and parents sit in a cafe. The city has a large zoo. It exists not so long ago, but can be compared with the more ancient menageries of Europe. On its territory there is a large collection of birds and animals. During the day there are show programs, you can also visit the dolphinarium.

Clubs

Athens has a lot of entertainment, including nightclubs that young people like to visit. The most popular place is Gyalino Plus. The club has several dance floors and a bar. In addition to dancing, guests are offered interesting theater performances. The cultural program of the institution is its highlight.

In summer, the Island Club is open for tourists. In the daytime, the institution works like a cafe, and in the evenings there are parties for fans of modern music.

Active entertainment

Not all tourists are attracted to rest on the sea. In Athens, there are so many attractions that the most active travelers will appreciate. In the Greek capital there are places worth visiting. In the city you can always rent a bicycle and ride along the old streets and main attractions. By the way, for such purposes, special bicycle tours have even been created. Their essence is that for travelers the optimal routes are selected that cover all the most important and interesting. The weather is almost always warm on the coast, so you can combine sea vacations in Athens with windsurfing and diving.
