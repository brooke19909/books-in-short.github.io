Sunshine Garden Resort 3 * (Thailand, Pattaya): description with photos, overview of services, reviews of tourists

The resort city of Pattaya, located on the eastern shore of the Gulf of Thailand, is one of the most popular destinations for tourists who want to spend an unforgettable vacation in Thailand.

You can really have a wonderful time here, but first, every traveler must decide on the hotel in which he will live. Pattaya has many options, and one of the best, judging by the reviews, is the Sunshine Garden Resort. And now it should be told in detail.

Location

The hotel was very well located. Sunshine Garden Resort is 5 minutes' walk from central Pattaya, a place where countless entertainment venues are concentrated.

The Sunshine Garden Resort itself is immersed in tropical greenery, and looking at it, it is difficult to imagine that the hotel is somewhere in the center. What is important: in 7-10 minutes you can walk to a gorgeous beach with golden sand. It is called Wong Amat Beach and is the best in town. Despite the fact that tourists in Pattaya have a rest all year round, the water here is pretty clean. And on this beach you can watch the most beautiful sunsets in the city.

It is also worth noting that from the hotel you can quickly get to various attractions (some can even be reached on foot), and the nearest airport is about 35 kilometers away.

Leisure

Immediately you need to make a reservation that the Sunshine Garden Resort 3 * located in Pattaya is a good option only for those people who are looking for a comfortable hotel just for living. Leisure in it is not very developed, since tourists staying here have fun in the city center (which is within walking distance) or on the beach.

General impressions

After studying the reviews left about Sunshine Garden Resort 3 * in Pattaya in details, you can see that many people talk not only about the hotel, but also about their vacation in this resort as a whole.

All travelers warn: spending time in Pattaya is radically different from Phuket, Egypt, Turkey, Krabi, Tunisia, etc. Here, a completely different atmosphere reigns. Some call it fervent, youthful, while others call it extravagant, even a little reckless.

People who want to visit as many excursions, entertainment venues, zoos, clubs, etc. as well are worth going here. Yes, there are beaches, but rest in Pattaya is more focused on all kinds of activities, rather than on lazy pastime on the Cote d'Azur.

Therefore, before you go here, you must familiarize yourself with all the nuances of the resort. Since some tourists, without understanding this topic, return with negative impressions and leave negative reviews both about Pattaya and the hotel. But he really does not deserve it. Many people, having once been here, often return again.

Climate

The holiday season in Pattaya never ends. But judging by the reviews, the best time to spend your vacation here is winter. In February, January and December, comfortable weather reigns here. The temperature indicators of air and water are at the level of +30 ¬∞ C and +26 ... + 28 ¬∞ C, respectively.

Also in winter there is little rain, and this is important. In the summer, for example, due to frequent rainfall, many excursion routes are not carried out. But in Thailand there is something to see - it will be a shame to leave home without impressions.

Accommodations

There are nuances that you need to know for people who decide to plan their trip on their own, rather than buying a tour. Namely:

  • Check-in at the hotel starts at 14:00 and continues until 23:30. But the reception is open around the clock, and therefore individual registration is possible, but you must first contact the property.
  • Departure starts at 6:00 and continues until 12:00.
  • No pets allowed at the hotel.
  • When booking, be sure to include your personal credit card details. The hotel accepts Visa and Master Card. Part of the funds may be blocked until the arrival of guests as a guarantee.
