Kamelya Fulya Hotel 5 * (Turkey / Side): photos and reviews of tourists

As you know, summer is a vacation season. Therefore, already in the spring, a tourist boom begins in travel agencies, during which trips to the best hotels in the most popular destinations are instantly sold out. Where do you think most Russian families are going to go in 2017? The answer to this question is very easy. Of course, in Turkey, where tourists can absolutely not think about anything, completely immersed in the leisurely rhythm of resort life. Naturally, first of all, our compatriots make out tours to luxury five-star hotels, which lure their guests with a developed infrastructure and a high level of service. If you still do not know which Turkish hotel to choose for yourself and your family, then consider the hotel complex Kamelya Fulya Hotel 5 *, located in the magnificent resort of Side.





General description of the hotel

Kamelya Fulya Hotel many tourists describe as a real oriental tale. And this is not surprising, because it has a huge territory of its own, beautifully decorated and highlighted. At dusk, the hotel complex turns into just an incredibly beautiful sight, which creates a special holiday atmosphere for all guests of the Kamelya World Fulya Hotel.

The hotel complex was built in the nineties of the last century, but the last reconstruction was carried out nine years ago. Therefore, almost all guests note that the hotel simply shines with cleanliness, while delighting with good appliances and new furniture.

Keep in mind that the hotel is part of the famous Kamelya Collection. Fulya Hotel is one of three hotel complexes located on a vast territory of two hundred and forty thousand square meters. Guests of the described hotel can visit absolutely all infrastructure facilities related to the entire complex.

In it, guests are accommodated in two hundred forty-four rooms of various categories. Among them are those apartments in which people with disabilities will feel comfortable. Interestingly, about forty-five percent of the rooms have a sea view so beloved by tourists. This is one of the advantages of Kamelya Fulya Hotel over other hotels in the complex.

The hotel complex is located on the second coastline, which, however, is characteristic of all Kamelya Collection hotels. Fulya Hotel (reviews confirm this) is very often chosen by our compatriots who prefer a vacation with a large selection of entertainment and do not tend to spend time on the beach often.

Hotel location

Kamelya Fulya Hotel 5 * is located in a very beautiful resort in Turkey - Side. Our compatriots did not immediately open it, but now it is included in the list of the most popular tourist zones of the country.

The hotel is located on the Mediterranean coast in the village of Cholakli. To get to it from the international airport of Antalya, travelers will have to travel a little more than fifty-five kilometers. Usually, those who have acquired a group tour overcome this distance on comfortable buses with other Russians. But independent travelers often book a transfer from the hotel. It is paid extra, but it allows you to get to Kamelya Fulya Hotel 5 * quickly and comfortably.

If you want to get acquainted with the sights of Cholakli, then its center is easily accessible on foot in just five minutes.





Infrastructure

The pride of most Turkish hotel complexes is the developed infrastructure. However, in the Kamelya Collection (Fulya Hotel 5 *) your expectations will not just come true - you will get an unprecedented pleasure from every second of your stay at the hotel.

First of all, the guests are impressed by the pools of the hotel complex. It will be more accurate to say that this is one pool, which stretches almost throughout the territory, forming small lagoons and cozy coves. If you find yourself in a hotel in the winter, you can fully appreciate the luxurious indoor pool with water heated to a comfortable temperature.

If you are not ready to leave your business even on vacation, then the conference hall with a capacity of three hundred and fifty people located in the Kamelya Collection / Fulya Hotel 5 * will suit you. Here you can hold an event of any complexity, and solve issues with documents (copies, sending fax or email). The hotel staff will always help you deal with all the problems that arise.

For those who do not plan to often leave the territory of Kamelya Fulya Hotel 5 * (Side), there is the opportunity to visit a hairdresser, several shops (including jewelry) and a luxurious spa center, whose total area exceeds one thousand square meters. It features a long list of healing and relaxing treatments, including a hammam. Also, vacationers can soak up the jacuzzi and spend a couple of hours in the steam room.

The gym, where the most modern exercise machines are installed, will help keep yourself in shape, allowing you to turn sports into a real pleasure.

Entertainment and sport in the hotel complex

Boredom at the Kamelya Fulya Hotel 5 *, it never occurred to anyone to complain. After all, every day you have to choose from the mass of entertainment that come up with experienced animators. And they are doing their best - water aerobics, water polo and gymnastics, archery, beach volleyball and the like, which make every new day special. If in the evening you are not too tired of all this diversity, then you definitely will like the evening show. It is always held in the best Broadway traditions, so it leaves no one indifferent.

For those who are used to spending free time actively and cannot imagine their life without sports, the hotel offers seven tennis courts with excellent coverage, billiards, table tennis and, just imagine, several football fields. There are three of them in Kamelya World Fulya Hotel 5 *. Each is equipped with a drainage system, lighting and a protective grill. The largest field measures sixty-five meters by one hundred meters; a large team of hotel guests may well gather for a friendly match.

For film lovers, the hotel complex has its own cinema hall, which can accommodate about sixty people at a time.

Especially great love among tourists enjoys the water park in Kamelya Fulya Hotel 5 *. Reviews about him on the Internet are always enthusiastic, because children and their parents ride here dreaming of remembering their childhood. There are several slides in the hotel; they have their own work schedule, which is very convenient for tourists.





Hotel service

The staff of a five-star hotel are always very demanding. Judging by what reviews about the hotel complex Kamelya Fulya Hotel his guests leave on the Internet, it is fully consistent with the declared status. The hotel staff speaks four languages, which include Russian. Therefore, communication at the reception and in other parts of the hotel complex will not be accompanied by a language barrier and misunderstanding.

At the reception you will be helped at any time of the day or night, and if necessary, they will also provide a safe in which you can leave jewelry and money. There is also a car rental service and a small gift shop on site.

If you do not want to leave the room during lunch or dinner, just call the reception and order food in the room. But do not forget that this service is paid extra.

You can also use the services of fresh press delivery - every morning for breakfast you will have a stack of ordered newspapers or magazines. There is a laundry room in the hotel complex where guests can return dirty clothes. You will get clean and perfectly ironed things back. This service is especially popular among families with small children who have tremendous ability to find dirt absolutely anywhere.

Typically, tourists coming to Turkey, make all the calculations with the help of American dollars. However, if you need a local currency, then you can always solve your question at the exchange office at the reception. There is also a luggage storage room where you can remove extra bags interfering in the room.

Children at the hotel

Kids in this hotel complex really like. Still - they are practically the main guests of the hotel, who are entertained even with greater zeal than adults.

Children can enjoy swimming in two pools with a shallow depth, and lovers of more active entertainment will be happy to ride the slides in the water park.

On the territory of the hotel there is a comfortable and bright playground with an abundance of interesting structures, made according to all quality standards. Here your child will be completely safe, because the staff carefully monitors the safety of all structures.

An unprecedented delight among the kids is caused by the mini-zoo, where you can always feed and pet cute pets.

It is noteworthy that children of any age can find something to do on the territory of the hotel complex. For example, kids from three to twelve years old are happy to attend a kids club under the supervision of animators. And teenagers spend time in a teen club, where they have prepared their own relaxation program, which includes a lot of interesting activities.

The club for teenagers works only in the summer months, when the tourist flow increases significantly.





Beach

Hotel guests have the opportunity to sunbathe and swim on a small piece of the municipal beach belonging to the hotel complex. Vacationers have the right to use umbrellas, sunbeds and towels absolutely free.

The beach is well equipped and has its own bar with a large selection of drinks and snacks. Kids usually enjoy swimming in the sand, and parents calmly let them go into the water, because the beach has a convenient and gentle entry into the water.

Hotel Rooms

As we said, the hotel has two hundred and forty-four rooms. At the same time, three are created specifically for people with disabilities.

Guests of Kamelya Fulya 5 * tell that all apartments have an excellent design and have everything necessary for the relaxation of each member of the family.

Mostly the rooms differ in view, consider this fact when booking a tour with an agency. In high season, the hotel complex is usually ninety percent full, so relocation to other apartments will be extremely difficult.

The hotel has one hundred thirty-three Superior garden view, from which a magnificent view of the best corners of the hotel area. These apartments do not exceed thirty square meters, where three people are comfortably accommodated.

One hundred and eleven Superior sea view overlooks the beach strip. These apartments do not exceed the previous ones in area and are also intended for an average of three people.

In turn, the listed rooms are divided into several types, including family rooms, having a large area and consisting of two rooms.

Each room has a TV, central air conditioning, electric kettle and tea set. In the bathroom there is a telephone, a hairdryer, a large mirror and a bath kit for each guest. The number of toiletries is replenished daily in the apartment, they are also calculated for each guest, not excluding children.

All apartments have a balcony or terrace, equipped with a set of light summer furniture.





Dining at the hotel

The hotel complex operates on an all-inclusive basis; in the summer, the ultra all-inclusive system operates. The latter is available only in the highest season - July and August.

There are six restaurants on site. Mostly breakfast, lunch and dinner are served in buffet style. And in the other five, you can have dinner or lunch upon prior request (once during the stay guests can do this absolutely free). Vacationers are available seven bars located on the territory of the hotel complex. Each has a fairly wide range of drinks and snacks.

Many guests of Kamelya Fulya 5 * claim that the food at the hotel is served almost around the clock. The day starts with a regular breakfast, followed by brunch and lunch. After it, a variety of hot and cold snacks are served before dinner, while late tourists are served a late dinner. The all-inclusive system includes non-alcoholic and alcoholic drinks of Turkish brands. In high season, this list is also replenished with foreign-made alcohol.





tour cost

It is worth noting that, despite the fact that the hotel has five stars, the price of a tourist permit does not hit the family budget. On average, two adults can relax in Kamelya Fulya 5 * for seven days with a flight from Moscow for only seventy-eighty thousand rubles.

For this money, they will be able to use the "ultra all inclusive" system and accommodation in a room category Superior garden view.

Tourists reviews

Mostly tourists respond very positively about the rest in this hotel. Of the advantages, the following points are especially noted:

  • beautiful sandy beach;
  • a huge number of pools and entertainment;
  • high level of service;
  • convenient food system that allows you to feed your baby at any time;
  • Friendly staff
  • fast and free wifi.

Among the minuses most often distinguish:

  • insufficient variety and quantity of fruits;
  • staff desire to receive tips for any help;
  • poor sound insulation in the rooms;
  • low quality local drinks.




In principle, even highlighting some shortcomings in the hotel, tourists admit that the vacation was very fun and worth the money spent on it. Therefore, this hotel complex can definitely be recommended for adults and children of any age.
