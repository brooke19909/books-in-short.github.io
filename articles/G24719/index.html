Aspendos Town

This attraction attracts guests of the Ela Quality Resort Hotel 5 * hotel, first of all, with ancient ruins and a preserved amphitheater. According to legend, the city of Aspendos was founded at the beginning of our era as a soothsayer Pug. Historians know that at first this settlement belonged to the Persians. In 333, he was conquered by Alexander of Macedon. Later, the city came under the jurisdiction of the Roman Empire. Over time, this crowded ancient metropolis began to lose ground and gradually became depopulated. In the XIII century, no one lived here.
