Hotel Silva's Beach Hotel 2 * (Sri Lanka, Negombo): photos and reviews

A person cannot constantly work, take care of domestic problems, difficulties and not give the body a period of calm and relaxation. For many, the best type of vacation is a trip to another country, to the islands, where you can enjoy the warm sun, lie on the beach and forget about the shackles of the modern world.

An excellent choice for an unforgettable vacation can be Sri Lanka - a state that hospitably meets new faces and offers the highest level of relaxation. In addition to local entertainment, food and attractions, an important part of such a trip is a temporary stay. Hotels and hotels do their best to make their guests feel the best and leave only positive reviews about the rest within their walls. Many tourists choose Silva's Beach Hotel 2 * - a hotel that provides an opportunity for an amazing pastime and where you can not think about anything.

Location

It is worth noting that the rest itself in such an incredible place like Sri Lanka cannot be bad. On this island, the main attraction is nature, as it is really possible to admire without getting tired. In Sri Lanka, harmony and tranquility reign, which allows you to feel like a part of nature, to be in its center and enjoy it. They like to have picnics and trips, diving, drinking natural teas (Sri Lanka provides them with a third of the world), and watching historical places. Silva's Beach Hotel 2 * is located near the center of Negombo (3.5 km), near Begombo Beach.

The location can be called very convenient, as the hotel can be reached from the Bandaranaike Airport in 20 minutes. Nearby are the churches of St. Mary and St. Anthony. Many people choose Silva's Beach Hotel 2 * precisely because of its location, because on vacation you need to consider the convenience of movement.

Number of rooms

A good pastime is not only delicious food, entertainment, pleasant atmosphere, but also the quality of the beds that you have to sleep on. Before you book rooms in the hotel, you need to carefully study what is offered in this regard. In Silva's Beach Hotel 2 * (Sri Lanka), guests can stay in twenty-five rooms of different levels of comfort and, accordingly, prices.

Depending on the possibilities, they choose standard rooms with a double bed, deluxe and super deluxe. All rooms are quite spacious, provided with natural light. The interior design was tastefully approached, so everyone will like a rest in the walls of cozy rooms of Silva's Beach Hotel 2 *. All options have air conditioning, ironing facilities, hair dryer, necessary furnished set, TV, kettle, bathroom with shower and bidet, Internet access. Some rooms have a balcony with sea views.

Infrastructure

Silva's Beach Hotel 2 * provides its guests with everything necessary for a relaxing, pacifying vacation and quality entertainment. All the main problems faced by tourists were provided by the founders of the hotel. For example, on the territory of Silva's Beach Hotel 2 * there is an important point - a currency exchange office. Also, a small garden where you can see many tropical trees, take good photos and just enjoy the evening on the island is suitable for walking.

Water procedures after the beach can be continued in the outdoor pool, and valuables can be taken to the storage room. You should also try national dishes in the restaurant on the territory of Silva's Beach Hotel 2 *, which, upon special request, will be brought directly to your room and please yourself with delicious drinks in the bar. For those who rented a car, there is free parking for the rest. Such developed infrastructure of the hotel confirms the fact that a good vacation for little money is possible, the main thing is to know where to look for it.

Service

Silva's Beach Hotel 2 * is an excellent choice for those who do not want to suffer from inconveniences and all kinds of problems and can entrust them to qualified and professional staff. The service of this place is represented by a considerable list of additional services. Upon arrival in Sri Lanka, guests can be picked up directly from the airport and delivered to the hotel door.

The reception is open around the clock, because at Silva's Beach Hotel 2 * new people are welcome at any time and day of the week. Large families are offered to stay in a special family room. In the rooms, polite maids maintain cleanliness and regularly change linen and towels. Laundry facilities are also available. The concierge will do his job perfectly, who will answer questions of interest and help in solving problems. For very young guests, a special children's pool is provided. There is no charge for babies, but an extra crib will have to be paid.

Entertainment

Silva's Beach Hotel 2 * (Negombo) is an ideal refuge not only for connoisseurs of a high level of service and a relaxing holiday, but also for those who want to combine them with active pastime and entertainment. On the basis of the hotel you can rent a two-wheeled friend and arrange a bike ride in the amazing places of Sri Lanka. The hotel also has an excursion table, where guests are given the opportunity to go on a certain route and get acquainted with the main historical points.

Given that a vacation at Silva's Beach Hotel 2 * is a vacation by the sea, you can try yourself in water sports. Guests are invited to try scuba diving, snorkeling and enjoy the beauty of the underwater world, stir up blood while surfing and windsurfing. A quieter canoe ride is more relaxed. Interesting events are held in local restaurants and bars, and the general stop of the place will not let even the most sophisticated entertainment guests get bored.
