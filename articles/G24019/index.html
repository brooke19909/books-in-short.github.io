What to do if you purchased a fake policy online

Unfortunately, every third policy that is sold through the Internet is a fake. In order not to become a victim of scammers, do not agree to pay the policy in advance. Let's consider in more detail how to check the CTP form for authenticity.

The first thing to do is to purchase a compulsory insurance contract with home delivery and payment to the courier. You should not pay in advance for the policy, there is a risk that you will not receive it at all.



After the courier brings the policy, you need to check it for authenticity. This is done very simply. In the upper right corner of the insurance policy is located the individual number of the form, which is registered in a single PCA database. You need to go to the site and check whether the policy really belongs to this insurance company. If it is not possible to check the database, it can be distinguished by visual signs.

Visual differences of the real CTP policy from false

This policy is a couple of centimeters longer than a regular A4 sheet.

On the original letterhead, on the back, is a protective silver thread that is sewn into the polis. A fake silver dotted line is simply printed on the fake.

A distinctive feature of the original form is the PCA inscription visible in the light. This is a kind of protective hologram.

The number of this policy is convex, which can be determined by touch.

Thanks to these tips, every motorist will be able to identify a fake policy and protect themselves from waste.
