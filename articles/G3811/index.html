Description



The tourist center, located in a closed bay, is located on the western coast of Turkey. From the airport in Dalman, the city can be reached in 1.5 hours. Marmaris, reviews of tourists about which are filled only with positive emotions, can be divided into Icmeler Bay, the city itself and the park area. Along the coast stretches an endless strip of narrow beaches with light gray volcanic sand. Since the resort is located in a closed bay, the sea is calm and there are practically no waves.

Climate

Coniferous forests, soft air, green hills, calm sea - this is what Marmaris is famous for. Reviews of tourists indicate the presence of more comfortable climatic conditions than in other resort cities of Turkey. Not too humid air is saturated with healing essential oils of needles, therefore it is recommended to relax here both for young children and elderly people. Relaxation



Tourists visiting Marmaris, reviews of which convey a lot of positive impressions, will be curious to know that the entertainment in this city is quite diverse. Day and night, bars and restaurants are located for guests of the city, located on beautiful pedestrian streets. In the center of Marmaris you can admire the bewitching show of dancing fountains. The heart of nightlife is Bar Street, where music and fun are heard until morning. A walk along the picturesque promenade of Marmaris can be memorable for tourists. In cozy restaurants and cafes located along the coastal zone, you can try all kinds of fish dishes. The following activities are offered to fans of active pastime: windsurfing, catamaran, water skiing, sailing, diving and parasailing. Extreme tourists can go on a jeep safari and go rafting. In addition to the beach and the sea, guests of the city of Marmaris (Turkey), reviews, photos and other information about which can be obtained in this article, are waiting for an interesting excursion program. Lovers of antiquity will not deny themselves the pleasure of visiting the ancient cities of Laodicea, Hierapolis, Ephesus, Knidos, Aphrodisias.
Natural attractions include Nimara Caves, a sand spit called Kyz Kumu and Turgut Waterfall.

Hotels

Mostly hotels with 4-6-storey modern buildings and a small territory prevail. They are located, as a rule, along the sea. Also found in the city of Marmaris are 5 star hotels , reviews of which characterize them more as club establishments with a vast territory. In the center, as a rule, are three- or four-star hotels.
