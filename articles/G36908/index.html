Isis Valverde - Brazilian actress and carnival muse

Isis Valverde began her career with modeling. As a result, she became a famous actress of Brazilian television short stories. In the winter of 2016, she participated in the famous Carnival in Copacabana as a muse. The girl turned twenty-nine years old on the day of the celebration.

Exciting work as a model





At the age of fifteen, Isis Valverde came to Belo Horizonte to study journalism. In one of the shopping centers, she was noticed by a representative of a modeling agency. Despite the parental ban, the girl saved the received business card and began to work as a model.

At the age of seventeen, she was offered a contract to work as a model in Istanbul and Tokyo. But her parents did not give their consent, and she had to wait until she was eighteen years old to engage in modeling business. Soon, Isis Valverde, whose photo is presented in the article, moved to Rio de Janeiro, where she had the opportunity to try herself in the cinema.

Successful career as an actress





The model agency, where Isis Valverde worked, directed her to take tests at Globo. Casting producer of the novel "Senorita" immediately approved the girl for the role of Ana. The 2006 short story was a remake of the 1986 version. Then the role of Ana was performed by Patricia Pilar.

To approve the role, she had to fulfill an important condition. The girl was forbidden to disseminate information about her participation in the project. Only parents knew about the shoot. The actress was supposed to hide until the thirty-second episode, in which the heroine revealed her appearance by refusing to vow to cover her face before the wedding. The same trick was used in the film with Patricia Pilar twenty years ago.

Isis Valverde accepted the conditions of the canal administration and led a secretive lifestyle for three months. She had to tie a tight tail, wear a cap and big glasses. She did not attend any social events, only occasionally visited the cinema.

The actress was very difficult during this period of her life. She had to rent a room with another woman, pay utility bills, do housekeeping, refuse all castings. The girl did not have enough money for a living, but she did not inform her parents about it. Her nervous and physical exhaustion led her to lose forty-five kilograms and her hair began to fall out.

But the actress did not give up. Having become famous, she moved to Barra da Tijuca (a prestigious area). She continues to collaborate with Globo, starring in new short stories.
