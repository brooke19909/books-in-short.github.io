Some tips on what to do if your phone is lost

Mobile phones, which until recently seemed like luxury, have firmly entered our lives. It is impossible to imagine how we used to live without this device. There are stored photos and videos with memorable events and contacts of friends, relatives and acquaintances. But what if one day you realize that you have lost a cell phone? What actions should be taken and what to do?





How to find a lost phone?

If the phone is lost, you first need to remember where this could happen and quickly return to that place. Then dial your number from another phone and listen, maybe it is somewhere nearby. Then, by the sound of your favorite melody set on the bell, there is a loss. But this is only if someone is not ahead of you. If the phone is still not found, you need to try to call him again, and if you are lucky and the finder picks up the phone, try to agree with him on the return for a fee. Usually, the new owners of your mobile phone willingly agree to this, because there are not so few problems with the found phone. Assign a reward of about half its real value at the moment, and the question of how to find a lost cell phone will disappear by itself. After all, the one who found him, selling the phone without documents, charging and boxes, is unlikely to succeed more than the reward you have proposed. What should I do if the phone is lost and the voice of an answering machine is heard in the handset, which indicates that the phone is switched off or is out of network coverage? In this case, there is little likelihood of finding a loss. This means one thing - the new owner threw out the SIM card. All that remains for you is to forget and buy yourself a new device.





If there is no hope

What to do if the phone is lost and there are a lot of funds on the account? If you do not want to go negative, you must immediately contact your mobile operator and block your SIM card. But then the chances that the person who has found your mobile phone will contact you are equal to zero. Sometimes it happens that you cannot contact the operator, and you don‚Äôt want to lose the amount on your cell phone account. Then one thing remains - to call without interruption to your number, and most likely, those who find your mobile will get tired of it, and it will turn off the phone. And you can contact the operator and block the SIM card.

What to do in case of theft

In the event of a loss, contacting the police is pointless; they will definitely refuse you there. Write a statement only when the phone is stolen. Such a statement is not accepted, they simply do not have the right. It is necessary to attach documents to the phone, check and box with the telephone identification number, it can be used to track by which number this telephone is being served. But in this case, the chances are not so great.



What to do if the phone is lost at home and the battery is dead? First you need to be patient and remember where the last conversation took place. Then carefully examine the apartment, inspect chairs and sofas. If you have the habit of talking and doing several things at the same time, you need to check the most unexpected places to search. They may turn out to be not only a wardrobe, but even a refrigerator and a washing machine. The loss in the house is not so terrible, because the phone will be found sooner or later. But in order not to wonder what to do if the phone is lost, you must always control your own actions and monitor your things.
