Why do I need a lamp for drying nails

Almost every woman wants to have a beautiful manicure. However, it requires constant care and caution, since breaking one nail will have to cut everything off. It was to solve this problem that a gel for nail extension was invented, which subsequently began to be used everywhere, and not only for correction, but also for the formation of artificial nails on all fingers.



To work with such a gel, you need a lamp for building, as it is popularly called, which is a small container with a light element that emits ultraviolet light. It is this kind of light that has an effect on the gel, which contributes to almost instant hardening.

Some women believe that when holding such a manicure, a lamp for drying gel nails is optional. In part, they are right, because the gel can dry on its own, in the sunlight, so if the manicure was done in the evening, it is enough to wait until the morning, and in cloudy weather you will have to sit in the light for several hours. It is also worth considering that when the gel dries independently, it has a completely different structure. It becomes brittle and impractical, often deformed and can peel off. That is why a lamp for drying nails must be present in nail salons, where gel building is performed .



It is believed that varnish can also be dried in this device. This assumption is erroneous, since the varnish requires a certain temperature and time to dry, and ultraviolet rays do not affect it in any way. For these purposes, there is a special spray that is sprayed on top of the covered nails. He, entering into a chemical reaction, contributes to the rapid hardening of the varnish. Therefore, a lamp for drying nails after staining is not needed.

To date, anyone can purchase such a device by visiting a specialized store or placing an order on the Internet. At the same time, the price for it is not very high, therefore it is available to almost everyone. However, it should be borne in mind that the lamp for drying nails is based on a light element that emits ultraviolet light. Therefore, before buying, it is worthwhile to clarify whether such elements exist in a separate sale, which of them will fit the selected model.



Many manicurers prefer to purchase more expensive devices of this type. They usually set a timer and even a sensor, which automatically turns on the device when placing an object in it and starts the countdown. Such a lamp for drying nails in quality is practically no different from cheaper models, since its device is also based on a light element placed in a special case. Therefore, the quality of all devices of this type depends precisely on the reliability of the lamp installed in them.

Thus, a lamp for building gel nails should be with any master of manicure. Those women who prefer to take care of their nails on their own can purchase it at a relatively small price.
