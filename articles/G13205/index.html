Popular hotels of Crimea by the sea

Crimea has always been a central resting place for people from the countries of the former Soviet Union. Love for this place is directly related to the unique symbiosis of climate and sea. Crimea is the only place in the region that boasts a subtropical climate. All this contributes to the development of hotel infrastructure.

Features of rest in Crimea

The resort areas of Crimea can be divided into two parts: Stepnoy and the southern coast of Crimea. The best hotels of Crimea on the seashore in the steppe part have a lower price and less crowded beaches. This is due to the less developed resort infrastructure of this region, and, consequently, less popular among vacationers. The southern coast of Crimea is a narrow strip between the Crimean mountains and the Black Sea. Here is a unique climate zone, respectively, here are the best hotels of Crimea on the beach.

Service of the Crimean peninsula is focused on all categories of vacationers. Mini-hotels in Crimea, on the seashore, are the best suited for tourists who prefer an economy-class vacation. The maximum requirements of VIP-vacationers are ready to satisfy more than two dozen five-star hotels.

Resorts of the steppe Crimea

The popular resorts of the northern part of Crimea include Evpatoria, Peschanoe, Popovka, Saki. In addition to sanatoriums and boarding houses, more than 30 percent of the apartment rental market has won a private hotel. In Crimea, on the seashore, unpretentious tourists choose such accommodation. The cost of such rooms is much lower, and the service varies depending on the hospitality of the owners of private apartments.

Resorts such as Saki and Yevpatoriya are focused on combining beach vacations with spa treatment and rehabilitation. Many specialized sanatoriums belong to the largest enterprises in Russia and Ukraine. Mud baths Saki and Moynaki are known for their healing mud, which helps to cure many diseases. The most famous sanatoriums in Saki: "Saki", "Yurmino", Saki Central Military Clinical Sanatorium. However, treatment is available not only to residents of the sanatorium, but also to those who chose other Crimea hotels on the seashore.

the south coast of Crimea

The southern coast of Crimea is a comfortable and respectable beach holiday. For a convenient and memorable vacation on the South Bank , there are numerous Crimean hotels by the sea, pensions and motels at your service. Amazingly beautiful nature, capes, vegetation of several climatic zones, relict forests, warm sea and pebble beaches are ideal for holidays or spa treatment. The southern coast of Crimea is not only beaches, but also an extensive cultural program. Numerous manor palaces built in the 19th century by Russian emperors, a large selection of wines, natural attractions. All this comes with a unique healing climate, moderate humidity and a large number of sunny days per year.

The main tourist centers of the southern coast of Crimea are: Foros, Alushta, Alupka, Miskhor, Yalta, Feodosia. Each of these resorts has its own zest and can offer both hotels of Crimea on the seashore, and on the second line, in the zone of relic plants, closer to the mountains. At the same time, the level of service will proportionally depend on the stardom of the hotel, as well as its cost.

The best hotels of Crimea on the seashore. Yalta

Yalta is the resort capital of Crimea. Therefore, it is a leader in the number of hotels of different price categories. Many of them regularly fall into the tops of hotels in the CIS countries. Among the best, it is worth highlighting the following:

  • Hotel "Tavrida". The hotel building is an architectural monument. Stylish design, luxurious furniture in the rooms. From the south side of the hotel, a beautiful view of the sea opens from the windows.
  • "Palmira Palace" is located in the village of Kurpaty, near Yalta, heads the top "hotels of the Crimea on the seashore." A chic vacation right on the seashore.
  • Oreanda, a symbol of the embankment of Yalta, is one of the most fashionable hotels in the city. Politicians and pop stars often rest here.




  • Hotel "Yalta-Intourist" is one of the most visited holiday destinations where tourists like to stay. The hotel went through several stages of renovation.
