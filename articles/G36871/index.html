Hotel rating Phuket 3 *, 4 *, 5 *

Phuket is the largest island belonging to Thailand. It is a favorite vacation spot for tourists from all over the world. There are not four, but two seasons - the hot season and the rainy season. The best months to relax are November, December, January and February. At this time, the air temperature on the island ranges from +22 to +35 degrees Celsius. The most equipped is the west coast. There are many comfortable hotel complexes, hotels, guesthouses, villas, which have their own white beaches washed by the crystal clear waters of the Andaman Sea. Many of them are included in the ranking of the best hotels in Phuket. Well-developed tourist infrastructure. The east coast is more suitable for a secluded holiday. There are also fairly well-equipped hotels, but there is little entertainment.

The island is conditionally divided into several zones: Bang Tao, or Laguna Beach (the most luxurious and expensive resort on the island), Patong (a youth recreation area rich in entertainment), Kata Beach (a resort designed for a secluded holiday), Karon Beach ( the most environmentally friendly and beautiful beach on the coast of Phuket).



Every year, international experts make a rating of Phuket hotels 3 *, 4 * and 5 *. Sometimes the assessments of specialists are summed up with the points obtained as a result of voting by vacationers. As a result of this, the most objective picture is created regarding the level of services in a particular hotel on the island.

Hotel rating Phuket 3 *

  1. Tropical Garden. This hotel is located on the shores of Kata Beach, therefore, is perfect for people who crave rest and peace.
  2. Phuket Orchid Resort. It is located in Karon Bay, near the sandy beach.
  3. Peach Hill. The hotel is located on Kata Beach. Great place for secluded relaxation.
  4. "Kata Sia Breeze." Another hotel on Kata Beach. Its distinguishing feature is fine Thai and European cuisine. The hotel windows overlook a picturesque waterfall, which is connected to an outdoor pool owned by the hotel.
  5. "Palmyra Patong." An excellent hotel for youth recreation and families who prefer outdoor activities.

Hotel rating Phuket 4 stars

  1. Kata Beach is a fairly large hotel on the Kata coast. Here is a bit of entertainment, but very tasty cuisine.
  2. "Marina Phuket Resort". The hotel is located in a picturesque palm grove. The hotel service is simply impeccable. The hotel's restaurants are famous for their amazing seafood and Thai delicacies.
  3. Karon Beach is located on the beautiful coast of the island. All hotel windows face the sea. Giant shopping and entertainment complexes, chic restaurants and others are located next to it. This hotel is not inferior in terms of service to the hotels that are included in the rating of Phuket 5 * hotels.
  4. "Royal Paradise". The hotel is located on the first line, has 250 rooms.
  5. Diamond Cliff Resort. The hotel has a beautiful SPA center, a water park with slides for both adults and children.

Hotel rating Phuket 5 *

  1. "Le Meridien Phuket" is located in the west of the island between two bays - Patong and Karon. The decor of this hotel uses Thai ethnic motifs. Another feature of this hotel is that it has the largest pool in all of Thailand.
  2. Laguna Beach Resort is a great vacation spot for everyone. Everyone can find entertainment to their own taste. The range of services is incredibly wide, and the level of service is simply impeccable.
  3. "Sheraton Garden Lagoon Resort." It is located on its own island right in the center of the lagoon. The beautiful snow-white beach has a passive rest, and the gentle sea - for diving and water sports.
  4. Indigo Pearl is a five-star hotel designed for high-ranking guests who prefer a quiet holiday away from prying eyes. It is completely buried in the rainforest. The hotel terraces smoothly descend to the sea.
  5. The Royal Phuket Yacht Club is also surrounded by greenery. Each of the guests has its own terrace with sea views.

Conclusion

Thais are a very hospitable people. The hotel staff in all hotels is well trained and ready to fulfill the slightest wish of their guests. Regardless of the level, in all hotels, Thai massage (for a fee), seaweed and healing herbs wraps are provided to clients. So, visiting Phuket, you can combine relaxation with wellness treatments.
