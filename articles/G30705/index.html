History of Vodnik

Once upon a time, when this town was not yet a resort, for those residents who had boats, a plot of land was allocated for garages for boats. The territory was significant: eight hundred meters long and 80 m wide, and, most importantly, near the sea. For a long time, this was of no value to the inhabitants of Yeysk. Those who bought boats and registered them with the relevant authorities became members of the water sports cooperative. They allocated a piece of the coast on which the garage was built (it is called "boxing"). But then the times changed.

The owners of the garages felt for their gold mine. After all, the land is actually on the beach! By re-equipping their former garages, the owners were able to lure the spa guests at a significant part of the Yeisk recreation centers. Vodnik very quickly turned from a cooperative of boaters and fishermen into a cottage village. It is very popular among vacationers. But there are some nuances.



What is the Vodnik VSE (Yeysk)?

Living in a former garage - who will it seduce? But not everything is so simple. The owners rebuilt their mini-hotels, which is much more than that, more precisely, as far as the means allowed. Someone had three-story mansions, but someone could only transform the garage into "human housing" a little. But VSO Vodnik (Yeysk) did not break up. By the efforts of the cooperative in 2004, the sewerage and water supply were brought to the cottage village, and garbage collection was arranged. Soon, the usual urban infrastructure appeared in the form of shops (some of them work around the clock) and a cafe. The only thing that is not yet available is parking. Ironically, entry into the territory of former garages is permitted only by passes.

Conditions in the houses of the cooperative "Vodnik" (Yeysk), prices for the summer of 2017

As we have already mentioned, in the cottage village many locals rent housing. There are a total of sixty-two houses. They are mainly in two floors, although there are one- and three-level. So that the tourist does not ring all the hosts, the cooperative has a website and a phone.
