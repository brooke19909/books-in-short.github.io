Floristics lessons. How to make flowers from leaves?

A wonderful decoration of the overall interior of the house are decorative bouquets. They can be made of a variety of materials: fabric, leather, polymer clay. But if you want not just beautiful decor, but also original, then opt for products made from natural material. Foliage flowers are what we have in mind. Such a product, if desired, can be done by each of you with your own hands. How? The master classes presented here describe how to make flowers from leaves. Study information, collect materials (and they are in every house) and get involved in the creative process.



How to make roses (flowers from leaves)? Master Class

To make a flower, we need the following materials:

  • maple leaves ;
  • sewing threads;
  • scissors;
  • vegetable oil.

How to make flowers in the form of roses in leaves ? We read further the description of the process.



Roll the leaf in half horizontally (across to the vein in the center). Now roll the workpiece into a roll very tightly. This item will serve as the core of the flower. Next, we form the petals. The leaf is applied to the roll with the central vein facing side. We bend the top back so that the edge protrudes 1 centimeter above the core. The side edges of this workpiece are wrapped around a roll. Use your fingers to hold the product to the bottom. The second petal is performed in the same way, placing it opposite the first. Form all other parts of the product in the same way. To prevent the rose from breaking up, wrap its base at the bottom with thread.

Bouquet assembly

How to make flowers from leaves, you learned. Now we will collect their finished composition. Iron the whole beautiful maple leaves through the newspaper with an iron. They will become very fragile, so proceed with caution. Gather the buds in a bouquet and wrap them with dried leaves. Fix the composition with threads. Treat the product with vegetable oil. This will prevent leaf wilting and will help to keep the bouquet longer. You can cover the entire composition with spray paint (silver or golden). This composition looks rich and spectacular.

How to make sunflowers?

We perform such flowers from the leaves of corn. We are preparing materials. In our work, we will use foliage and corn grain, glue or thermal gun, cardboard, wire, floral tape or corrugated paper.





We begin to manufacture a decorative sunflower. Cut a circle out of cardboard and stick a grain of corn on it. Smooth the foliage of this cereal through a cloth or paper with an iron. Put together several units of this material and cut the petals out of them. Attach them to the cardboard base in two to three rows. Bend the tips in different directions, this will give the product a natural look.

We form the stem. Bend the wire in half, place the place of folding (loop) against the cardboard circle and attach it with glue or tape. Mask this place with a piece of tops. Wrap the wire with floral tape or green paper. That's all, the sunflower is ready. Three or five of these items, placed in a vase, will be a wonderful decoration for your home.

How to make flowers from leaves? It turns out that it is very simple, fast and interesting. We hope that our workshops have inspired you, and very soon such compositions will appear in your home.
