Briefly about the village

Slavyanka is the center of the Khasansky district and urban settlement. The village was founded in 1861. Previously called Tulam. In 1943, he was given the status of an urban-type settlement. In 2016, 12.5 thousand people live in it. Since the beginning of the XXI century, the population has been declining markedly. For 15 years, this figure has decreased by 3,000 people.

Rest in Slavyanka, Primorsky Krai

Currently, a small village in the south of Primorsky Krai is becoming a popular holiday destination. This is mainly due to good transport accessibility. These places can be reached both by public transport and by private car. On the coast there are many facilities for recreation. All of them offer different entertainment programs and good living conditions. Every year in the village the number of tourists from other countries increases. For relatively little money there is the opportunity to have a great time on the seashore. Slavyanka (Primorsky Territory) provides everything you need for both active and passive recreation. Over the past few years, many recreation centers have been built in the bays. Let's look at some of them.

The recreation center "Captain"

On the shore of Baklan Bay is the recreation center "Captain". It is 300 m from the sea. The distance from Vladivostok is almost 200 km. This recreation center is ideal for those people who want to relax in peace and quiet. Accommodation tourists in small houses. There is a volleyball court. For children, swings, a sandpit, a slide are organized. It is possible to rent a boat, jet ski and banana. For those wishing to take walks in the surroundings, the administration organizes excursion programs. Also for fishing lovers there is a rental of necessary equipment. In the evening, young people have fun at a disco. The cost of living without food - from 1200 rubles (triple house).



Holiday Village "Elena"

Only 50 meters from the sea is the recreation center "Elena". It is surrounded by the sea and mountains. In order to enjoy these landscapes, you need to come to the village of Slavyanka (Primorsky Territory). For accommodation, tourists can rent houses or rooms. The latter are offered Junior Suite (double and triple) and VIP (designed for 8 people). For double houses a day you will have to pay 700-900 rubles. The minimum payment for a double room is 3000 rubles, and VIP-apartments - from 12000 rubles. Diving enthusiasts are invited to rent a diving kit. You can also play tennis, badminton and other active games on site. No pets are allowed in the base area.

You can get from the city of Vladivostok by regular buses number 526, 528, 521. Get off at the bus station Slavyanka. From it to the recreation center can be reached on foot or by taxi, the distance is one and a half kilometers.

Holiday Village "Dolphin"

Everyone who wants to spend an inexpensive summer vacation is recommended to choose a recreation center "Dolphin". It is located in the village of Slavyanka (Primorsky Territory). For accommodation, double and quadruple houses are offered for a cost of 700 rubles. It is possible to relax in tents. You will need to pay only 100 rubles. On the territory there are gazebos and a bathhouse. Parking is not guarded. There is a dining room, three meals a day will cost 450 rubles. per person. The recreation center "Dolphin" offers sightseeing boat trips. In order to get to the beach, it is necessary to overcome a distance of 100 m. Tourists come here by public, sea and private transport.
