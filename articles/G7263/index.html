Two sanatoriums "Primorye": Gelendzhik vs Vladivostok

In the view of many, spa treatment is always the southern sea, a snow-white beach, a lot of sun, magnolia and palm trees. All this is present in Gelendzhik. But high-quality services that will help improve your health and get an unforgettable experience can be obtained in the Far East.

Read and compare two of the same name sanatorium "Primorye", one of which is located in Gelendzhik, and the other - in Vladivostok.

Sanatorium "Primorye" (Gelendzhik): specialization

In this medical institution, patients suffering from diseases of the nervous system and respiratory organs are treated, in addition to tuberculosis, and cosmetology services are provided using technology from German and French scientists. It treats both adult patients and children. There are no age restrictions.

The main methods of treating diseases are magnetotherapy, laser therapy, herbal medicine, physiotherapy, inhalation and massage.

The following types of cosmetic services are available for patients:

  • vacuum, plastic and cosmetic massage;
  • myostimulation;
  • consultative assistance of a cosmetologist;
  • hardware manicure and pedicure;
  • face lifting;
  • body modeling;
  • anti-cellulite program;
  • lymphatic drainage.

The magnificent SPA center has a Finnish sauna, Turkish hammam, infrared cabin, jacuzzi, massage room and solarium. The procedures taken in the SPA center have a beneficial effect on the body. As a result, blood circulation improves, cells are more intensively supplied with oxygen, which leads to normalization of metabolism and increase vitality.





Infrastructure of the sanatorium "Primorye"

The sanatorium has 3 buildings, which accommodate 78 comfortable rooms, as well as its own pebble beach, the path to which is only 40 meters.

For the accommodation of tourists comfortable rooms of various categories are provided, including standard, studios and suites. Each room has air conditioning technology that maintains a comfortable temperature depending on the time of year, a TV, telephone facilities, a mini-safe for storing valuables and documents, a minibar with drinks, a bathroom, a balcony.

In addition to treatment, vacationers are available services of a conference room, spa, cosmetology center, bowling club, gym.

Three meals a day are provided by a restaurant and a coffee shop, also located on the territory of the health-improving complex. It serves the best dishes of Russian, Italian, Mediterranean and French cuisine. Due to the proximity of the sea, the menu is constantly present fresh seafood. A wide selection of confectionery is always available for children. In the restaurant you can smoke a hookah and experience vocal abilities in the karaoke room.





Reviews about the resort "Primorye"

Patients resting here gratefully recall the time spent here and note the high quality of the services provided.

Leisure facilities include water sports, diving, boat trips on the sea surface, a visit to a daytime beach show, dolphinarium, water park entertainment, fishing, as well as excursions with a tasting at the local winery.





Qualified staff, his attentive attitude, excellent infrastructure leave in his memory good memories and a desire to continue the treatment begun here.

And now we are moving to the other end of Russia - to the Far East, to the port city of Vladivostok.

Sanatorium "Primorye" (Ministry of Internal Affairs of Russia)

It is located in a picturesque pine forest, at a distance of 22 km from Vladivostok. From the windows of the sanatorium there is a magnificent view of the spurs of the Sikhote-Alin and the coast of Peter the Great Bay. Beautiful local nature invariably delights guests of the Primorsky Territory.

This medical institution is intended for the treatment of diseases of the nervous system, gynecological and urological diseases, and the circulatory system.

For the treatment and prevention of diseases, methods such as manual therapy, mud therapy, phytonod intake, massage, and diet therapy are used.
