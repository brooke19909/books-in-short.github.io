Hotel Golden Five (Hurghada, Egypt)

16 km from the airport of Hurghada is a 5-star tourist complex "Golden Live". Hurghada is a wonderful resort city where tourists can enjoy the sandy beaches and spend their holidays at ease and fun. The hotel is located on a vast territory, which houses 7 buildings and 40 cottages. Each of the buildings is an independent complex with an individual name and stardom.





The Golden Five itself (Hurghada, Egypt) is a five-story building, decorated in a traditional style, and a number of one-story villas. The resort center is focused on couples with children, honeymooners and business people. For each age category the hotel offers a number of services. For example, for people who combine leisure with work, equipped with excellent conference rooms, decorated with the latest technology.

The room fund of the Golden Five Hotel (Hurghada, Egypt) consists of 520 rooms, 322 of which are of standard type. 370 rooms are concentrated in the main building and belong to the Diamond Hotel. For demanding customers who prefer to stay in spacious rooms of superior type, there are 2 presidential and royal suites.





Regardless of the category, all rooms have a terrace (50% overlooking the Red Sea). A personal split system is available, a telephone with an international line is held, there is a free safe, a TV with cable (satellite) TV and a refrigerator. The bathroom is equipped with a shower cabin (bath), bidet and hygiene products of prime necessity. The price includes daily cleaning. Paid service includes round-the-clock service.

Meals : Hotel Golden Live, Hurghada, (photo of restaurant facilities is presented below) works according to the European all-inclusive concept of food. Non-alcoholic drinks, locally produced alcoholic beverages, sweets, ice cream, snacks in the hotel bars are provided free of charge.





Especially for newlyweds and regular customers a large basket of fresh fruits is provided. On his birthday, the birthday person receives a symbolic gift and a birthday cake. The landscaped area has a number of restaurants, bars and cafes.

Beach : 700 m stretches a wide coastline with a length of 750 m. All guests staying in one of the five hotels can use the beach . A cable car leads to the shore. All the amenities on the coast are free to use. There is a marina and a coral ridge on the beach. A bar is open for lovers of refreshing and invigorating drinks. From entertainment, windsurfing, diving, volleyball, catamarans are offered.





Additional Information

The Golden Live complex (Hurghada, Egypt) offers you a wide range of services: Amphitheater, large pool with five sections, bowling, billiards, aerobics, jacuzzi, sauna, tennis courts. Every day, day and evening show programs are organized, sports competitions with prizes are organized.

The hotel has a health club with a Turkish bath, a disco with live music. For guests organized excursions to amazing places in Egypt. In free use: gym, massage room, riding school. In addition, car rental, laundry, dry cleaning, photo service, a chain of retail stores, and parking are available.

Children's services: an entertainment club up to 12 years old, a disco with a ballroom, video games, an outdoor pool with water slides, a free Golden Five Water Park. Babysitting services for the duration of the absence of parents are offered.

Hotel Golden Five, Hurghada: reviews (2013)

Judging by the numerous reviews of vacationers who spent their holidays in this hotel this year, we can say for sure: the complex made a vivid impression on customers. Pleasant memories were left from water entertainments, a well-maintained sandy beach, quality food and a fun animation program. Decent service: all staff are very attentive to guests. You will not have to miss the hotel. The hotel contingent consists mainly of foreigners. With order and cleanliness here is strict, there is no noise and fights.
