Abkhazia: boarding houses "all inclusive", "buffet". Family holidays by the sea

Abkhazia is a land that has everything necessary to be called a paradise. There is a magnificent subtropical climate, there is a sea, rivers full of fish, mountains and forests inhabited by bears, red deer, roe deer, wild boars and other animals.

In addition, there are very hospitable and hospitable people, so for lovers of beauty and comfort, a vacation in Abkhazia will be a real paradise . Pensions, motels and hotels are ready to receive their guests 300 days a year - this is how much time the warm season "lives" here.

History of the region

On the territory of Abkhazia, the sites of primitive people dating back to the Lower Paleolithic era were found, but the real heyday of this region occurred during the reign of the Greeks, which lasted from the 4th century BC. e. The largest city on this territory was Dioskuriada. From 63 BC e. rule passed to the Romans, and soon the largest policy was soon used to exile Christians.

In the early Middle Ages, the territory passed into the possession of Byzantium, and Christianity became the main religion. Only in the 4th century did the gradual formation of the Abkhazian kingdom begin, which flourished in the 7th century, and complete independence from the Caliphate and Byzantium came in the 8th century under Leon II.





That time can be judged by the preserved sights, such as the Bagrat citadel in Sukhumi or the Anakopia fortress. An earlier period is represented by the pagan temple on Mount Napra.

Today, this resort region welcomes its guests with quality service and comfortable rooms - this is modern Abkhazia. Pensions ("all inclusive", "buffet") are the most popular holiday destinations for the most experienced tourist.
