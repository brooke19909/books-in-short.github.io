Best hotels of Sudak (Crimea): photos and reviews

Crimea has long attracted travelers with fabulous coasts and a rich cultural heritage, which gave it a rich history. One of the best resorts on the peninsula is the small town of Sudak, which is located on the Black Sea. Unique landscapes and ancient sights, modern hotels and restaurants - what else can you dream of on vacation? In this article we will talk about the best hotels of Sudak (Crimea), which have flattering reviews of guests.

"Grand Hotel"

The Grand Hotel Complex is located on Mount Fireynoy, 400 meters from the Black Sea coast. Next to it is one of the most popular places in the city - the water park, where you can have fun with a happy smile on your lips. In addition, magnificent views of the Sudak Bay, as well as the famous monument of medieval architecture - the Genoese fortress, built by Italians in the XIV century, open from the windows of the institution. The hotel itself pleases the eye of tourists with an exquisite facade and elaborate interior, distinguished by luxury and wealth. The complex consists of 2 buildings, including 44 comfortable rooms. Depending on the season and category of rooms, the cost per day ranges from 2500 to 8000 rubles. Speaking about the best hotels in Sudak, we can say that here is one of the best price-quality ratios.



However, the best storytellers are the tourists themselves, who left comments on the forums. Summarizing them, we can draw the following conclusions:

  • The clean and spacious rooms have chic views overlooking the water surface and memorable places.
  • The restaurant serves delicious and satisfying food, and the assortment is very wide.
  • The hotel staff is friendly and helpful, which is important.

The disadvantages include weak Wi-Fi, the remoteness of the beach, as well as poor access to the buildings, because there is no paved road.

There were some minuses, but if you are looking for private hotels in Sudak with excellent rooms and good service, the Grand Hotel will be a great choice.

The sea coast is 500 meters away, and the Sudak water park is 300 meters. Quite a few guests are upset by the distance from the sea.



Aquator

Speaking about the best hotels of Sudak, it is worth mentioning the "Aquator". This is a wonderful hotel located in the heart of the city. Its rooms delight in avant-garde stylized design. Modern appliances and furniture, friendly staff, a wide range of services and comfortable prices - all these advantages attract tourists to this establishment. Vacationers have at their disposal spacious rooms equipped with a large Smart TV, refrigerator, microwave, kettle and shower. A couple of hundred meters from the guest house, cafes and restaurants await their guests, serving delicious dishes and drinks. Finally, inquisitive travelers can take advantage of the services of staff organizing fascinating excursions in Sudak - one of the oldest cities in Europe, which has a number of unique attractions.



Reviews of tourists praise cozy and nice rooms with excellent functionality. Relatively low prices and excellent food at the hotel also leave only positive emotions. The disadvantages, as a rule, include the remoteness of the beach, to which - 1 kilometer. Otherwise, this is a terrific hotel.

Elegant

The best hotels in Sudak cannot do without the Elegant. It was designed by the famous Armenian architect Stefan Lazaryan and impresses with its exquisite and original forms. Its layout was focused on the maximum comfort of accommodating dear tourists, which is why each room delights with its spaciousness and functionality. The real pride of the hotel is considered to be the "dancing" color musical fountain that captivates the eyes of guests. In addition, the hotel boasts European quality of service. Most of the rooms are equipped with luxurious furniture, TV, air conditioning, security system, refrigerator and hairdryer - all for maximum comfort.

Guests of the Elegant emphasize the excellent layout of the rooms, the sensitive staff, delicious and satisfying breakfasts, excellent sound insulation and modern technology. The disadvantages are often the poor quality of Wi-Fi and interruptions in the operation of the elevator.



What to see in Sudak?

Many travelers visit these places for the sights that Sudak is famous for. The hotels, the reviews of which we have cited above, allow you to stay in the city with comfort. Genoese fortress - the most important archaeological site, impressive in grandiose forms. The height of some towers reaches 15 meters, which is characteristic of the impregnable fortifications of medieval Europe. These fortifications are like an open book, from the pages of which the biographical story of Sudak, numbering 18 centuries of stormy life, is pouring.

Natural objects are no less remarkable, including the Alchak-Kaya tract , Cape Meganom and Mount Ai-Georgy. Extraordinary landscapes beckon to make a memorable photo, which will remind you of a wonderful vacation off the coast of the Black Sea.



Among travelers, entertainment venues are also very popular: a large water park, noisy nightclubs, restaurants and cafes. All this attracts active people who came to Sudak. Hotels, for the most part, try to build next to well-known infrastructure objects so that guests spend their holidays only with joyful smiles.
