Hotel Maikhao Dream Resort & Spa Natai 5 *, Thailand, Phuket: overview, description, characteristics and reviews of tourists

If you love nature, then Maikhao Dream Resort & Spa Natai 5 * is the best option to spend a truly unforgettable vacation. Only here you can admire the untouched tropical nature, soak up on the clean exotic beaches, and also get acquainted with the peculiarities of the original local culture.





Hotel location

Maikhao Dream Resort & Spa Natai 5 * invites tourists from all over the world to plunge into the world of untouched nature and modern standards of service. Comfort in the bosom of the green tropics is just the perfect combination that will provide you with a sea of ​​vivid impressions and give you a state of spiritual harmony.

Maikhao Dream Resort & Spa Natai 5 * fully justifies its high star status. What is the widest list of services, which includes sports events, and opportunities for healing, and a sea of ​​incendiary entertainment.



The philosophy of Maikhao Dream Resort & Spa Natai 5 * is to introduce travelers to the original Thai culture as close as possible. To this end, yoga classes are held daily, as well as master classes from chefs, who will quickly introduce you to all the wisdom of preparing delicious national dishes.

Hotel Location

Maikhao Dream Resort & Spa Natai 5 * (Phuket) is located on the famous Natai beach, which is clean and famous for being surrounded by untouched nature. It takes 40 minutes to get to the center of Phuket and about an hour to the airport.





Hotel guest rooms

Hotel Maikhao Dream Resort & Spa Natai 5 * can offer you for accommodation several categories of apartments:

  • Deluxe is a small room, the space of which is characterized by an area of ​​45 square meters. m. It is designed for two guests, and it is also possible to provide one clamshell. These apartments are one bedroom with an attached bathroom, as well as access to a balcony. These rooms are located in the main building and offer sea views.
  • Garden Terraces is a one-room suite, or rather, a spacious bedroom. Since the apartments are located on the ground floor of the main building, they have a terrace, which is surrounded by a blooming garden. Also from it you can enjoy the sea coast.
  • Deluxe - a huge room, which is a bedroom with a separate living area. From the balcony you can enjoy the sea.
  • Suite is a two-room apartment. The living room is separated from the bedroom by an internal door. A pleasant moment is the terrace overlooking the ocean.
  • Pool-terraces - small rooms, representing a bright and comfortable bedroom. The highlight of these apartments is the terrace, which opens directly to the pool. From here, the sea is clearly visible.




Amenities & Room Service

In addition to the elegant interior, the rooms of Maikhao Dream Villa Resort & Spa Natai 5 * also boast a huge list of amenities:

  • The bathroom comes with bath and toiletries, a bathrobe and slippers, and a hairdryer;
  • a pleasant moment is the availability of a kit for making tea and coffee;
  • individual air conditioning works not only for cooling, but also for heating the air;
  • Hotel staff will replenish your minibar daily for a fee;
  • the TV broadcasts many channels, including Russian, and there is also a disc player;
  • use of the electronic safe is absolutely free;
  • for an additional fee you can use the international telephone line (for calls to the reception funds are not charged);
  • depending on the class of room you will have a shower or bath;
  • a pleasant addition to the room is a balcony or terrace with sea views;
  • thanks to the parquet made of natural wood, the floor of the apartment is quite warm.

It is worth noting that the cleaning, as well as the replacement of bed linen in the room are daily. Also, a fruit basket will be brought to your apartment every morning.

Helpful information

In an incredibly beautiful place is the resort Maikhao Dream Resort & Spa Natai 5 *. Excursions are mainly carried out in the following areas:

  • Hot Spring thermal springs, where you can see the real wonders of nature and swim in the warm healing geysers;
  • visit the colorful evening show in Siam Niramit with national dances, costumes and local food;
  • you should definitely pay attention to the sea excursion in the surrounding islands.

Any hotel has its own rules of residence:

  • check-in and check-out are carried out - respectively, at two in the afternoon and at 12:00;
  • children under 5 years old using existing beds, as well as babies under 2 years old on extra bed stay free;
  • A child from 5 to 12 years old is provided with an extra bed for 1000 baht, and older and adults - for 1500 baht;
  • upon registration, your personal credit card must be presented;
  • for each day of your stay at the hotel a security deposit of 3,000 baht will be charged to you (if the property of the hotel was not damaged by you, then the funds will be returned in full);
  • newlyweds, as well as guests celebrating the wedding anniversary, receive flowers and champagne as a gift;
  • Birthday party awaits a surprise in the form of a festive cake.




Positive reviews

Fully reflect the good impressions of the hotel Maikhao Dream Resort & Spa Natai 5 * reviews. The following comments are especially worth highlighting:

  • a hospitable meeting and quick check-in, which is greatly facilitated by the fact that the hotel has a Russian-speaking manager;
  • hotel workers help tourists bring suitcases to their rooms without extorting a tip;
  • the territory is so large and planned so well that there are no large crowds;
  • nice, clean beach;
  • hearty breakfasts;
  • the restaurant’s menu has been posted on the hotel’s website, so long before the trip you can navigate the prices;
  • a lot of greenery on the territory (not artificial plantations, but natural tropical plants);
  • the rooms are cleaned in a very high quality, and an additional advantage is that you set your own time for it.




Negative reviews

The following claims are made to the hotel by tourists:

  • monotonous breakfasts that are repeated day after day;
  • the buffet menu has very few fruits and almost no meat;
  • during cleaning, stocks of cosmetics are not replenished (you have to ask at the reception yourself);
  • due to the remoteness of the hotel from the city infrastructure, prices for all services and goods are several times overstated;
  • the whole room, and especially the bathroom, needs to be renovated (the last time was carried out in 2009, which greatly affects the condition of the hotel);
  • with the exception of one manager, none of the staff speaks Russian, and even everyone speaks English very superficially;
  • when checking out, you need to check the check very carefully, as the reception staff often include unnecessary services that you did not use;
  • the minibus that runs to the city is very small, besides it needs to be booked in advance, preferably even in a few days.
