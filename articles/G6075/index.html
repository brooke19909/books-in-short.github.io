Rentier - who is this and what does he do?

Rentier - who is it? From the point of view of the economy, this is the person who receives the main income from the money transferred to the loan or in the form of interest from securities.

Basic definition

The broad meaning of the word "rentier" means a person who has his own capital in the form of cash. Moreover, he lives on interest. In addition, the renter may be the owner of highly liquid real estate (business, securities or land) that generates income. In other words, a person receiving a certain income not from labor activity, this is a rentier. The type of assets can be different (bills, loans, real estate, etc.).

Role in society

Rentier - who is it? This is one who lives not on labor, but on legitimate incomes. The amount of money received for such a person always depends on how efficiently he manages his capital.

When asked about the renter - who is this, one can confidently answer that this is the one who receives his money from generating his own capital. For example, real estate leased to them gives a monthly stable income.
The rentier differs from investors in that he practically does not engage in independent management of his own assets. He either buys what is capable of generating passive income, or entrusts the management of funds to professionals.



The rentier - who is it from the point of view of economists? There is no single answer. So, T. Veblen was confident in the parasitism of the rentier on the real production process. N. Bukharin assigned this group of people a large role in the development of the credit economy.

What should a person have in order to have passive income?

In order to get a certain result, it is necessary to fulfill a number of conditions. Many people cherish the dream of not going to work and living comfortably with interest on available capital. So how to become a rentier? The main difficulty in moving towards your dream is the initial sufficient capital.

Income

People of a similar profession have minor changes in their earnings from month to month. The amount of income of a rentier is influenced by certain factors. Among them are the following:

- The situation in the stock market;
- level of business profitability ;
- situation in the real estate market;
- The economic situation in the country.

Sources of income

Recently, many people have appeared in the capital of Russia who have the opportunity to not work anywhere. They receive their income from renting Moscow real estate. This happened because the cost per square meter of housing is constantly growing. In terms of its level, the price of Moscow real estate is equal to the European one. Those who own two or three apartments in the capital do not need to worry about their future. On rent alone you can live comfortably. Real estate is the best guarantee of the future. At the same time, premises can be rented out both for commercial purposes and for living.

Decent income is also received from bank deposits. However, the amount in the account should be impressive. Only in this case, the interest received will be enough for a comfortable existence. It is worth remembering the depreciation of capital, which occurs under the influence of inflationary processes.

There are such rentiers who live on dividends from stocks and bonds of large firms. As a rule, these are companies in the raw material sector of the economy.
