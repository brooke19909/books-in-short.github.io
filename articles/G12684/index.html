Korali 2 * (Montenegro / Barska Riviera) - photos, prices and reviews of tourists

Montenegro, a map of which is a clear evidence of why this country is considered the East European Riviera, has a coastline of three hundred kilometers. Of these, more than a quarter are sandy beaches.

This stunningly beautiful country, due to wars in neighboring states, has until recently been closed to tourists. But today, this direction is chosen by travelers from all over the world, including Russians. And this is not surprising: almost completely the territory of Montenegro is protected by UNESCO, in addition, the clean Adriatic Sea, sandy beaches, friendly locals and cozy hotels contribute to the flow of tourists here constantly increasing.





Sutomore

There are many attractions, such as the two-altar church of St. Thecla, built in the twelfth century, the ruins of a Benedictine monastery, ancient fortresses. The Belgrade-Bar railway passes through the city, and at the local beaches there are streams of cafes and restaurants, shops and clubs. In the summer, almost the entire resort area turns into a pedestrian.

In Sutomore, there are many hotels of various categories - from luxury five-star to economy class. Lovers of a leisurely and carefree holiday opt for apartments, and many come to spend their holidays on a budget option in Sutomore. Korali 2 * is quite popular among Russians.

general information

This budget hotel is just a short drive from tourist attractions such as Bar and Petrovac. The region is called the Bar Riviera. Korali 2 * has its own fenced beach area. The hotel area - although small, but well-equipped and landscaped. In the evenings, you can stroll between the buildings, feeling like in a real forest: tall trees everywhere, inhabited by many birds and even squirrels, lush Mediterranean vegetation. The hotel has its own secured parking, access to the territory for strangers is prohibited.





Prices

Today, Korali 2 * Russians offer many tour operators. The reason is the low price. For example, the cost of a seven-day tour with departure from Moscow and the type of half-board meal for two with accommodation in a standard room will cost fifty-seven thousand rubles. The price only for a hotel without air travel is twenty eight thousand.

The price for the first option chosen includes accommodation, meals - buffet breakfast / dinner, charter to Tivat and vice versa, transfer from the airport to the hotel, insurance, as well as services of a guide-interpreter.

Those who fly to Montenegro for the first time, you need to know: the country has an airport tax in the amount of seventeen euros per passenger.

Hotel prices vary according to the season. In addition, if you buy a tour for less than three nights, the cost of living will, according to the rules, be increased by fifteen percent.

Infrastructure





The Korali Hotel is a 2-star hotel, so those who expect the services of the Five will be somewhat disappointed. Nevertheless, he has a fairly developed infrastructure, fully consistent with the declared category. The hotel has a 24-hour reception desk, a left-luggage office, a safe deposit box, and a laundry service. Throughout the territory you can connect to the Internet.

At the registrar, those tourists who have not taken care of the return transfer can arrange it without any problems. Here you can buy sightseeing tours and rent a bike for self-study of local attractions.

At a distance of one kilometer there are bus and train stations. The nearest airport is an hour away.

Housing stock

Korali 2 * is positioned as a medium-sized hotel. It has three hundred and thirty single and double rooms with the possibility of placing an extra bed. At the same time, the hotel can accommodate 687 people. Each room has a terrace or balcony with a view of the park or the sea. The rooms are equipped with a TV, double or single beds, a desk, a wardrobe. There is also a telephone.

Bathrooms combined. On the floor - ceramic tiles. Air conditioning is not provided in the rooms, as the hotel does not get hot even in high season. Thick, solid walls of the buildings perfectly protect rooms from excessive heating.





Nutrition

In addition to a shady park with many trees, shrubs and amazingly beautiful flowers, in the middle of which there are buildings that form the hotel complex, Korali 2 * also has a large restaurant, which provides meals on the concept of "full board" and "half board". The schedule of his visit is issued to tourists in printed form directly upon arrival. Meals for breakfast are fried eggs, sausages, pastes, cheese, sausages, croissants, etc. For lunch, always served a lot of meat and salads, there are a variety of side dishes for every taste.

Beach

Judging by the reviews, the Korali 2 * hotel has the largest territory on the coast. From the buildings to the sea is only two minutes walk. The sea is clean, there are few people on the beach, especially compared to the beaches of neighboring hotels, which are always full.

Sunbeds and umbrellas on the beach are issued for an additional fee - five euros for the whole day. The coating is sandy, although the entrance to the sea is small pebbles. For ease of entry into the water, you can buy special slippers that are sold right on the beach for ten euros.





Entertainment

Korali 2 * (Montenegro) offers sightseeing tours to attractions such as St. Churches. Tekla and St. Roko, the fortress of Haj-Nehaj, etc. Take a boat to Queens beach.

On the beach, you can play a variety of ball games - soccer, volleyball, etc. Water sports are also available, as well as bike rides. The hotel runs cross-country events and organizes hiking trips.

Reviews





Great stay at a modest cost. This is how many Russians characterize their stay in Korali 2 *. The rooms are spacious, comfortable large balconies with excellent views, unobtrusive service, judging by the reviews, many liked it.

Decent food, no one was left hungry. The staff is ready to help in any matter. However, in some reviews, Russians express dissatisfaction with the fact that, according to the rules of the hotel, eviction takes place at ten in the morning, which the tour operator does not warn in advance. Therefore, many advise when planning a trip, choose a return charter before noon. Although even this, judging by the reviews, did not overshadow the rest.
