Lakes of the Leningrad Region will give an unforgettable vacation

In St. Petersburg and its environs there are many palaces, museums, cathedrals, which the city visitors and St. Petersburg residents themselves tirelessly visit. The city of rains, known for its white nights, not so often pleases locals with dry and clear weather. On hot summer days, you don‚Äôt feel like sitting in a stuffy metropolis crowded with tourists from all over the globe. You can go to the lakes of the Leningrad region and spend an unforgettable weekend with family and friends.





Where to find such lakes? There are more than one and a half thousand of them in the region; there is even a map of the lakes of the Leningrad Region. Ladoga Lake is one of the largest. There are a lot of islands on the lake where you can relax in private and even stay overnight with a tent. Due to the size of the lake, the water is cool, but you can swim in the heat. There are specially equipped beaches with gazebos, changing rooms, playgrounds, etc. The beach is covered with fine sand. If you want to swim in a boat, it is rented. In the summer, there are, of course, a lot of people here, but those who have cars go to wild beaches, where it is quiet and calm.

On Lake Onega, they not only bathe and fish, but also consider rock paintings of primitive people. A magnificent view of the Kizhi Cathedral opens from the shore of Lake Onega. A variety of fish are found in the lake, there are trout among them. Berries and mushrooms are harvested on the islands, where they grow in abundance. The lakes of the Leningrad Region will make your vacation diverse and interesting.





Closest to St. Petersburg are the Kavgolovskoe and Kurgolovskoye lakes. Initially, it was one whole lake, but railway workers paved the way through it, dividing the lake in bulk by two. You can get here without a car, by subway. From the Devyatkino station to the beach is only 15 minutes walk. On the Kavgolovskoye lake, they relax quietly with children, as the depth from the shore leaves slowly. Having visited different lakes of the Leningrad region during the summer, you will find the most suitable for your vacation.

The fabulous view of the Mirror Lake in clear weather will not leave anyone indifferent. The mirror surface of the lake is so transparent that it is visible five meters inland. On Mirror Lake, you can often find divers plunging into the clear waters to observe the inhabitants of the reservoir. Holidays on the lakes of the Leningrad Region can be arranged with the whole family: while some swim, others catch fish or scuba dive. The shore of the lake is quite strong, so you can drive to it by car from any direction.





Lakes of the Leningrad region vary in size, location and history. If every person visiting reservoirs will treat nature with care, then our children and grandchildren will see this same beauty in its entirety.

Every nature lover will be able to fully relax, not only in the summer, but also in the spring, winter and autumn. Snow-white winter lakes greet fishermen with fishing. Autumn leaves lying on the expanse of water will create a beautiful background for your photos. Spring drops and snowdrops will awaken consciousness from last winter. Nature and man are the strongest union!
