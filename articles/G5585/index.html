Kim Hoang Long Hotel 2 * (Vietnam / Nha Trang): description and reviews

Nha Trang is the capital of the Vietnamese province of Khanh Hoa, and at the same time is a popular tourist destination and resort. This is largely due to the mild climate with hot summers and excellent location on the shores of the South China Sea.





If you have chosen this place for your vacation or have arrived on a business trip, be sure to pay attention to the Kim Hoang Long Hotel 2 * located in the bay. Reviews of tourists about him are mostly positive. Built in accordance with all international standards, it fully complies with the declared two stars. A high level of service and hospitality will allow you to enjoy the resort town, known since the time of emperors.

Kim Hoang Long Hotel 2 *: location





A beautiful hotel, built in a modern style, is located near Nha Trang Bay, recognized as one of the most beautiful on the planet. This vacation spot is popular not only among ordinary tourists, but also among divers, amateurs of ordinary and underwater photography. Life in the deep sea is very diverse. The bay beach is just five minutes walk from the hotel. Quite close is the international airport and the bus station 40 and 5 min. driving a car accordingly. Thus, the tourist is not limited in movement and at any time can go on a further trip around the country.

Number of rooms

Hotel Kim Hoang Long Hotel 2 * (Vietnam, Nha Trang) is quite small and includes only twenty-four rooms. They are spacious, decorated in a classic style with elements of national color, equipped with a bathroom and air conditioning, which is an important condition for a comfortable stay in a humid and hot climate.





The hotel offers accommodation in rooms of four categories: standard (double and triple) with city views, superior double with sea or building views. All are equipped with a direct dial telephone and cable television, wireless Internet access, a safe (at the reception), a mini fridge, and toiletries. Cleaning is done daily. Breakfast and personalized room service are available at an additional cost.

Accommodation with children

The policy of the hotel is that free accommodation is only possible with children aged 0 to 12 years and if they are accommodated in existing bedding. A separate children's bed is available at an additional cost.

Older children are considered adults. An extra bed can be provided depending on the type of room selected. Please contact Kim Hoang Long Hotel 2 * for further details at the booking stage.





Check-in and check-out times are standard, from two in the afternoon until noon, respectively.

Accommodation prices

One of the main advantages of the hotel is low prices with a very high level of service, excellent location. This is ideal for tourists and travelers on business trips on a budget.

The price of accommodation varies depending on the season (high or low season), the proximity of the holidays (New Year, Christmas, Valentine's Day, etc.), as well as the selected room. So, one night with a standard double occupancy will cost about 1000 rubles. Triple room costs almost twice as much. Pay attention to what is included in the price, very often a city tax has to be paid separately.

Service and entertainment





The hotel staff speaks Vietnamese and English. Throughout the room and in rooms, guests can use free Wi-Fi, as well as a workstation with a personal computer. Bottled water is delivered to rooms daily. On the territory there is a small restaurant, the menu of which contains dishes of not only national Vietnamese, but also European cuisines.

In addition, at the Kim Hoang Long Hotel 2 *, for an additional fee, you can use the laundry services, order a transfer from the airport and back, use the park or rent a bicycle. In the building of the hotel there is a travel agency that will kindly offer guests its services, organizes leisure activities and excursions.
