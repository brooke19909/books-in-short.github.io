Hotel 3 * Honey Resort (Phuket, Thailand): description and photos

If you consider yourself to be a lowly tourist category or simply do not want to spend too much on accommodation, but plan to relax on such a Thai island as Phuket, the Honey Resort 3 * hotel may suit you best. We suggest further to find out together what awaits the guests here.





Location

Hotel 3 * Honey Resort, according to many travelers, has a very good location. So, it is located within walking distance from Kata Beach. The hotel is located in a quiet side street, so the noise of passing cars will not bother its guests. In the vicinity of "Honey Resort" there are a lot of cafes, shops, supermarkets, tour agencies, massage parlors, etc. The market is located very close by. Here you can not only buy the freshest seafood, but also ask them to be prepared right there. It will cost quite inexpensively. As far as entertainment is concerned, Patong can be reached in a quarter-hour by tuk-tuk, taxi or rented scooter. Phuket Airport is a 40-minute drive from the hotel in question.





Hotel 3 * Honey Resort: description and photos

Honey Resort is a modern Thai-style city hotel. He has practically no own territory. The hotel is a five-story building equipped with a lift for guests convenience. In general, our compatriots, leaving 3 * reviews about Honey Resort, note that they really liked the hotel. It is fully consistent with its category and price of living. Sea

Judging by the reviews of tourists staying at Honey Resort 3 * (Phuket), their beach holiday was a success! So, the nearest Kata Beach to the hotel is just a five-minute walk. Also, if desired, travelers can visit Karon Beach. You can walk to it in a hurry in 15-20 minutes. According to our compatriots, many of them liked Kata beach more, since there are not many people on it. In addition, there are not such big waves that tourists who come to rest with children will like it. All beaches are municipal property. For the rental of sunbeds and umbrellas, you must pay. However, if you wish, you can always sit on a mat or towel directly on a small, pleasant sand. All beaches have a lot of cafes and bars where you can eat or refresh with your favorite drink.
