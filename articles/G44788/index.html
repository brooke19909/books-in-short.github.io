Tips on how to pack a gift in gift paper

It's no secret that receiving a gift is much nicer to take it out of the package than to see it right away. From the anticipation of a surprise, you can experience much more impressions than from the presentation itself. How to pack a gift in a gift paper to give each other exciting pleasure? By showing a little creativity, you can make this process much more spectacular!





Design Tips: Frequently Asked Questions

How to pack a gift in gift paper so that it is impossible to immediately guess its contents?

The desired result can be achieved if you change the shape of the presentation during registration: roll up the clothes in candy, put accessories in an envelope or bag, etc. Such advice is relevant only if it can be done without compromising the quality of the gift. In such cases, the traditional way always helps out - to put a present in a ready-made standard box.

How to pack a gift in an original way?

  • When decorating a presentation, you can use various objects: living twigs, leaves, cones, beads, buttons, yarn and much more.
  • If the person to whom the gift is presented is creative and extraordinary, use a simple newspaper, make a decoration of it in the form of a fringe, flower or origami. But do not experiment with older people and superiors in this way; for them it is better to choose something more traditional.
  • If a person loves traveling, and as a gift he is preparing some kind of accessory for these purposes, such as a backpack or gym shoes, then it would be logical to pack them into a card or guidebook.
  • It is better for your beloved to wrap the present in a soft cloth or just in a red scarf. A living flower, soft toy or heart as a decorative element will only add romanticism to a surprise.
  • If a gift is being prepared for one of the family members, then you can ask the children to make a bright thematic application of colored paper and make it a gift.
  • For motorists who are preparing a very necessary accessory, you can decorate the present with a toy car.




How to pack a gift unusual?

A very extravagant way is to enclose it in a glass cube. Such packaging will need to be broken to receive a gift (a decorative malleus is usually included). In this case, it is important to observe safety precautions and consider the feasibility of this choice. It is unlikely that anyone will want to get a ballpoint pen out of a glass cube by breaking the latter. Another thing is when it contains a diamond necklace or the keys to an apartment or car. For persons with a sense of humor, you can set the alarm - believe me, such a surprise will be remembered for a lifetime by everyone present!
