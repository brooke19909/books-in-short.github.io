Holidays in Turkey are in particular demand from tourists. There is a simple explanation for this - ideal conditions for rest and recreation included in the hotel‚Äôs accommodation system. With an abundance of entertainment and a choice of leisure options, Turkey remains the most economical resort holiday option. Usually, Antalya or Aegean coasts are chosen for the trip.

Holidays on the Aegean coast

More tourists choose the Antalya coast for leisure . Here is the most developed infrastructure of the leisure industry: a large selection of resorts and excursion programs.

Aegean coast is chosen less frequently. However, at the peak of the season, in July and August, it would be advisable to consider a vacation in this particular resort. Its options for beaches and the hotel base are not inferior in quality, but the tourist flow is less and the heat is not as sultry as at the same time in the resorts of Antalya. This is even more important when the trip is organized with children and the gentle sun of Aegean is more suitable for them. There is no big difference in the cost and quality of holidays at these resorts in Turkey.

Five star hotel for families with children

An excellent vacation option is provided by the rated Garden Of Sun Hotel Spa Wellness 5 * hotel, which is located a kilometer from the center of Didim. From Bodrum Airport to the hotel, the shuttle will deliver guests in an hour (90 km). Not far away is the famous Didima water park, for children its attractions will be a great bonus on vacation.

On the territory of the hotel itself there are many services for young guests - playgrounds, clubs, a children's pool with slides, an order for a nanny. The hotel is located on the second coastline, but tourists who visited this hotel note the convenience of its location on the coast and the proximity of the beach.

About Garden Of Sun Hotel Spa Wellness 5 *

The five-star rating hotel of the Aegean coast provides its guests with all the conditions for a wonderful holiday: it has its own SPA Wellness center, excellent daily animation, six categories of rooms will satisfy any taste of guests.

The rooms. 250 rooms with sea, mountain and garden views have different categories. The largest number (170) of triple rooms; 31 - double, 17 categories of rooms for family accommodation (family), 17 suites and 9 rooms for people with disabilities.



Description of rooms. Each room of the Garden Of Sun Hotel Spa Wellness 5 * has everything you need for a comfortable stay: satellite TV, individual air conditioning, a refrigerator with a replenished minibar, a balcony. The bathroom has bath accessories and a hairdryer. Internet connection is provided, but according to guests, the quality could be better.

When booking Garden Of Sun Hotel Spa Wellness 5 ROH (Run Of the House) the accommodation is carried out in any hotel room, free at the time of check-in, subject to the reservation parameters (number of places).

Service

The price includes the following services: a visit to the superior lounge to watch TV shows, swimming pools for adults and children, a jacuzzi. Vacationers have at their disposal water slides, a playground, a disco-bar and a disco, a conference hall, parking, a sauna, a Turkish bath, a pizzeria, a restaurant and bars. The following services are additionally paid for at the Garden Of Sun Hotel Spa Wellness 5: Internet cafe, wireless Internet connection, beauty salon, SPA center, thalassotherapy, solarium, laundry and babysitting services.



The territory has its own tour desk, where you can get acquainted with the offers of various tour operators for organizing excursions, get information about the programs and their cost.

Dining at the hotel is carried out according to the all-inclusive system in the summer and on the basis of breakfast in the winter. One restaurant - main, two ethnic cuisine restaurants (Turkish and Italian) provide service by appointment. Perhaps during their stay a one-time free visit.

Entertainment

Evening entertainment shows and animators' programs from morning till night cheer up guests at the Garden Of Sun Hotel Spa Wellness 5 *. Beaches, relaxing with children in the playgrounds, sports entertainment - everything can be found without leaving the vast territory of the hotel. For the organization of various leisure activities, guests are offered to visit the gym, fitness center, sports grounds. For an additional fee, training in diving courses, other water activities on the coast are possible.
