Top themes for Android: review, features and reviews

If you are already fed up with the look of the desktop - icons, animation and a standard menu, then you can diversify your mobile gadget with some new themes. The latter are special software - launchers that allow you to unrecognizably change the visual component of your gadget.

There are a lot of similar utilities on the network, and among all this diversity it is quite problematic to choose the really best themes for Android. Some programs are poorly adapted to the platform, clumsy work, or even slow down your device. So you need to be serious about choosing software.

We will consider the best themes for Android, which are very popular and have a lot of positive feedback from users. All the software described below can be found on Google Play, so there should be no problems with testing. So let's get started.

CM Launcher 3D

This is one of the best themes for Android 7.0 and earlier. Launcher offers a chic selection of tools to personalize your gadget from many categories: nature, cars, people, flowers, movies, sports, holidays and others.

One of the best topics for Android is easy to manage and does not require any specific skills or knowledge. At the first start, you will be greeted by the settings wizard and step by step will guide you through the entire functionality of the launcher. The theme has its own curtain, at the opening of which you will be presented with basic management tools.

In addition, one of the best themes for "Android" has several more useful qualities. So, she can optimize the battery and clean the registry with RAM. The theme is correctly integrated into the platform and, judging by the feedback from users, they have not seen any serious bugs.

Next Launcher 3D Shell Lite

This is also one of the best themes for Android 6.0 and older. Advanced utility has impressive functionality and capabilities. A rich library allows you to choose topics from almost any direction. In addition, the built-in editor will help to make your own design from an existing template, as well as change the animation.

TSF Launcher 3D Shell

This theme also deserves to be installed on your smartphone or tablet. It has rich capabilities for personalizing the platform interface. There are settings for curtains, animation, processing of the main menu, desktop grid and more.

The theme attracts primarily with its elaborate 3D-design. Here it is implemented as it should and is not buggy at every opportunity, as it is observed in other 3D launchers. The theme supports colorful animations for the desktop and wallpapers, and also has amazing soundtrack.
