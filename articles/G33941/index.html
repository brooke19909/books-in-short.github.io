Hotel Baumanburi Hotel 4 *: tourist reviews and photos

Baumanburi Hotel 4 * is a good hotel located in such a popular place in Thailand as Phuket. Every year, this hotel complex receives thousands of tourists from different countries and continents. And many of them are satisfied. So what is so attractive about this hotel?





Briefly about the hotel

Baumanburi Hotel 4 * is located on the first coastline, which distinguishes it from many other hotel complexes located right there. Less than 200 meters to the sea - this factor is the most attractive for tourists who only come here to sunbathe and swim.

But still, the other advantages of the location are also worth mentioning. For example, the airport. It can also be reached quickly, in less than an hour. The ideal location of the hotel is not so far from the airport, but also at a sufficient distance (the aircraft will certainly not interfere).

And the place where the Baumanburi Hotel 4 * is located is the central part of the Patong coast. 5 minutes from the beach, 10-15 - from the center of all entertainment and shopping, the same - to the most popular and most visited night clubs. In general, you can get anywhere, and on foot.

So, what can Baumanburi Hotel 4 * offer its potential guests? These are not only comfortable and cozy rooms, which will be discussed later, but also a lot of entertainment. An outdoor pool is located on site, and free towels are offered to everyone who wants to swim in it. Fans of an active, sporty lifestyle will definitely enjoy the fitness center. Those guests who come to rest with children will be provided with cots for babies.

There is also a spa, excellent sauna and tour desk. Moreover, if guests are always used to ‚Äústay on wheels‚Äù, even on vacation, they will be offered car rental. There is also a point where you can receive or send a fax.





Nutrition level

This question excites many people who intend to come here on vacation. Well, as many guests who have been here assure, you can not even worry about this topic. You won't be able to stay hungry at Baumanburi Hotel 4 * (Phuket). Guests who have been here in detail describe fresh and original dishes of local national and international cuisine. The vast majority of guests go to the Siam flavors restaurant (it works from 6:30 in the morning until midnight). There, polite waiters always serve original treats.

There is also a Restaurant Lounge. It is open to visitors from eight in the morning until half past eleven. Guests assure that it is worth a look. Because this place serves really original snacks and light drinks. Pool Bar is also worth paying attention to - this is a place where a real abundance of various cocktails reigns.





Accommodation options

In Baumanburi Hotel 4 * (Phuket, Patong), potential guests can book any room. The apartments are divided into five categories. So, there are superior double rooms (either with 1 very wide, or with 2 standard beds). Also - "deluxe" apartments (also with beds to choose from). There are also 2 double rooms of the Grand Deluxe category. You can also book apartments for two people with special conditions. This is usually access to the pool. And of course, there are apartments for families. They are designed for 3-5 people. These rooms look like real apartments, because there is not one room, but two bedrooms.

All apartments, without exception, are decorated in an original style. In the interior, you can clearly trace the Thai national traditions. Everything looks very cozy and comfortable - in these rooms you really want to relax.

Well, the cost of such apartments for two is 44-56 thousand rubles for 14 nights of relaxation. If you include breakfast in the price, you will have to pay more - 46-59 tons. Interestingly, the hotel often offers various discounts and promotions. And if you get just at this moment, then you can save a lot. If you book this apartment for three, you will have to pay approximately 70-78 thousand rubles (with breakfast).

The people who stayed in this apartment claim that the conditions there are really luxurious. The rooms are spacious (38 sq. M.), The beds are modern and comfortable, the bathroom is large and clean. About booking

Finally, a few words about how the booking process is carried out at Baumanburi Hotel 4 * (Phuket). So, check-in and check-out is traditional - after 14:00 and in the afternoon, respectively. Children are allowed to stay, and if the child is not 16 years old, then he does not need to pay the full price - only 400 THB from above. If additional accommodation is required (that is, another person who is not registered as a guest, who also paid for the apartment), then you will have to pay 700 THB from the top. You can put an extra bed in the room, but it will also be for an extra charge.

In general, this hotel is ideal for a good time and comfortable stay. People who have been here say that everything that is needed is combined in it.
