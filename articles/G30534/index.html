Rechnoy Vokzal metro station in Moscow

The Rechnoy Vokzal metro station is located on the green line of the Zamoskvoretskaya line of the Moscow metro. Residents and guests of the capital have been using it for more than 50 years. It got its name thanks to the Northern River Port, which is located nearby.





For 2017, the station is the final destination of the northern direction and one of the most congested. In this regard, two more stations were designed and built - Belomorskaya and Dybenko.

Entrance to the Rechnoy Vokzal metro station

To the right of the entrance to the station are ticket offices where you can buy a ticket for one or several trips, as well as issue a Troika card. The premises are equipped with terminals for replenishing Troika and checking balances.

The passage is through a turnstile, a descent for wheelchair users and an escalator are equipped, a security, tracking and warning system is working. There is tunnel ventilation with air heating in the cold period of time, the temperature is monitored daily. The lighting in the lobby is quite bright and comfortable, electronic monitors are installed on the platform where you can watch the exact time.





The beginning of the work of the Rechnoy Vokzal metro station in Moscow is at 5:40 a.m., towards the Alma-Atinskaya station. The usual range of movement of rolling stock is no more than 3-5 minutes.

A security and control service is operating, as well as CCTV cameras. About the rules of use and actions in case of emergency informs the reproducer.

Common areas

Bicycle parking is located to the right of the second exit (76 m), next to the Rechnoy shopping center. Another bike parking is located 130 meters from the Rechnoy Vokzal metro station near the Friendship Park.

Dry closet - 120 meters from the station (paid), next to the Friendship Park and the bicycle parking. You can visit the stationary bathroom in the building of the Rechnoy shopping center, on the second floor (paid).

In the Rechnoy shopping and entertainment center there are more than 200 boutiques, cafes, recreation areas, restaurants, and parking. It is located on the right side of the second exit, and to the left of it is a building with trading floors and McDonald's.

Interesting Facts: Subway Fossils

In red granite, which is faced with the Rechnoy Vokzal metro station, paleontologists discovered shells of cephalopods that supposedly existed in the Mesozoic era.





On the columns of the station, one can observe numerous shells of gastropods, ammonites, needles, and shell of a sea urchin. Many fossils are only partially visible and almost merge with the surface, but if you look closely, you can see the structure of the partitions. An interesting story on this subject can be found on the Moscow Metro website.
