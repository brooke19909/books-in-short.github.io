Needlework lessons: how to knit a scarf



Hand-knitted scarf is not only a warm piece of clothing, but also fashionable. In the wardrobe, both female and male, there should simply be a couple of such accessories. Of course, you can buy them in stores or in the market, as an option - order to the masters. But we suggest you knit the scarf with your own. This product is made with a direct canvas without additions or decreases, so every beginner needlewoman can make it. If you know how to type, as well as how to knit the front and back loops, then soon you will wear an exclusive handmade scarf around your neck. For all beginners in this business, we have prepared tips and useful tips on the topic "How to knit a scarf with knitting needles". Take them into service.

Choose yarn and knitting needles

The winter scarf should be warm, so for its manufacture, take threads 100% wool or wool with acrylic. Also, for knitting such an accessory, you can use yarn with the addition of mohair in several additions. Such a product will be not only warm, but also fluffy.

Autumn-spring version of this accessory is knitted from pure cotton. Such a product is pleasant for the body, it retains heat well and protects from the cold. Blend wool is also ideal for making demi-season scarves.

The number of knitting needles for a scarf is selected depending on which thread will be used. When buying yarn, look at the label. It indicates which spokes are suitable for working with this type of thread.

What pattern to knit a scarf with knitting needles?

To make this accessory, choose double-sided patterns. They look beautiful both on the front side and on the wrong side. We give examples of such knitted patterns.

  • Shawl knitting. All loops in all rows are made only facial or only wrong. This pattern is perfect if you knit a striped scarf. Change the color of the thread every two rows, and as a result you will get a beautiful accessory in a marine style.
  • Pearl pattern. Perform the first row of the product by alternating the front and back loops. Turn work over. In the next row, knit the front seamy, and the seamy front. So complete the entire product.
  • Elastic. The canvas made with this pattern is embossed. It suits more for warm scarves. The simplest version of this figure is a 1 x 1 elastic band. It turns out as a result of alternating one front and wrong loops in all rows.
  • Track. With this pattern you can knit an openwork scarf with knitting needles. It is performed like this. Even rows: * 4 front, double crochet, one of two stitches *. Repeat from * - * to the end of the row. Turn the knitting. Odd rows: all loops are purl.

Learning to knit a scarf (classic)

Type on the knitting needles of the loop. Their number depends on what width you want to have a scarf. Next, knit with the selected pattern to the desired length. The classic version of this accessory is a strip of about 1 meter. If you plan to wrap a scarf around your neck, then do it up to 2 meters long. In the last row of the loop, close, cut the thread.

How to knit a men's scarf with knitting needles? Follow it in the same description. There are no special techniques in the manufacture of this accessory for representatives of a strong half of the population.

We hope that the information and photos presented in the article will inspire you to be creative, and you can knit the scarf with knitting needles. Let beautiful hand-knitted accessories settle in your wardrobe.
