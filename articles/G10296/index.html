Hotel Oasis Del Mare 4 * Resort (Bulgaria, Lozenets): description, rooms, reviews

A summer trip to Bulgaria can be a budget option for a good rest for the whole family on the sea coast. It has everything: fabulously beautiful mountains, wonderful nature, gentle sun, endless sandy beaches stretching along the Black Sea coast and, of course, an ocean of positive.

It is noteworthy that the resorts of this country are available for any age and wallet. They differ in popularity, beaches, infrastructure development, the number of natural and historical attractions.

There are first-class resorts with a huge number of luxury hotels, restaurants, bars, nightclubs and other recreational activities, but at the same time, calm places are preserved on the coast. For example, many Bulgarian and Russian families are attracted by the warm and cozy atmosphere of the village of Lozenets, located in a quiet bay near the Turkish border, between the resorts Tsarev and Kiten.

Holidays in Lozenets

The village of Lozenets is 60 kilometers away from Burgas Airport, but it will not be difficult for tourists to get to it: in addition to public transport, almost all local hotels provide guests with, if not free, then quite inexpensive transfers. Travel time on a comfortable car is less than an hour, on a bus - a little longer.

The village of Lozenets is distinguished by its incredible nature, amazing beauty and fresh air. Hotel complexes and private houses are buried in gorgeous greenery, and on the horizon the top of Papia Mountain is distinguished. According to many experts, the sea in this area is one of the cleanest on the coast.

Sozopol is located very close to the village of Lozenets - the oldest Bulgarian city on the coast, the amazing, untouched by human civilization natural world of the Ropotamo River, as well as a beautiful botanical garden in the village of Velika.

You can explore all the listed attractions on your own, with an individual guide or as part of an excursion group.

Finding a decent accommodation in the village of Lozenets with an excellent view of the sea surface or a mesmerizing mountain range from a room, with good service at an adequate price is quite real. There are always a lot of hotels, hotels, guest houses and individual apartments in the private sector. An excellent option may be the four-star hotel "Oasis del Mare" (Oasis Del Mare 4 *), which occupies part of the territory of the former camping "Oasis".





The location of the hotel Oasis Del Mare 4 * (Bulgaria, Lozenets)

The Oasis del Mare hotel complex is located near a wide beach strip at the base of the Strandzha mountain range. The distance from the center of the village of Lozenets to the hotel is no more than one and a half kilometers on a good asphalt road.

To reach the hotel, you need to follow the direction of the Oasis beach, after about a kilometer the Oasis Beach Resort complex is located. It must be passed and, continuing the path, cross a small bridge. From there, a view of Oasis Del Mare 4 * opens.

brief information

Hotel Oasis Del Mare 4 * (Bulgaria) is a medium-sized hotel, built in 2010. It is located on the first coastline and provides tourists with accommodation in standard, family rooms and apartments with a buffet.

In total, there are 86 comfortable rooms located on 4 floors of the only building with a 24-hour reception desk on the 1st floor.





Hotel guests have a cozy classic-style restaurant, a lobby bar with free Wi-Fi, an outdoor pool with a children's area, free umbrellas and sun loungers around it, a friendly animation team, a gym and a sauna.

In walking distance from the hotel there is an equipped public beach.

Number of rooms

Regardless of the category, all rooms of the Oasis Del Mare 4 * hotel complex have a stylish design. In the interior of the premises there is only modern furniture. The floor is carpeted. There is all the necessary equipment for a comfortable stay:

  • individual air conditioning system;
  • TV with satellite channels;
  • telephone with intercity access;
  • small fridge;
  • Electric kettle.

Each room has a furnished balcony. Some of the living rooms have amazing sea views.





There is a private bathroom with a shower, hairdryer and toiletries in every unit. Room cleaning and linen change are carried out regularly. The hotel complex has rooms and all facilities for people with disabilities.

Standart room

When booking a standard room of the Oasis Del Mare 4 * hotel complex (Lozenets), tourists have the opportunity to choose not only the view from the windows, but also the type of beds installed: two single beds (Twin), standing separately, or one double bed (DBL). Also in this category, extra beds and cots are available.

The maximum can accommodate three to four guests. Rooms are 35 square meters.

Suite

In its equipment, the suite of the Oasis Del Mare 4 * hotel complex is similar to the standard, but provides its guests with much more space and comfort. Installing extra beds or sofa beds will in no way hamper the room.

The maximum number of people to check in is four guests. The suite is 40 square meters.

Conditions for children in "Oasis del Mare 4 *" (Lozenets)

Lozenets Oasis Del Mare 4 * for its young guests prepared an equipped playground, a shallow section in the pool, a mini club and many electronic games. Experienced animators devote a lot of time to children, do not let them get bored. They organize various contests, competitions, exciting games and, of course, an evening mini-disco.

Beach and sea

Only 100 meters from the sea is the hotel Oasis Del Mare 4 *. The reviews left by vacationers on popular tourist portals are the most diverse. Those hotel guests who were lucky enough to swim in the sea without waves, left satisfied and happy. But, unfortunately, many vacationers have the impression that the waves here are a constant phenomenon. Tourists see the reason for the frequent occurrence of waves in the special natural location of this part of the resort.

Objectively, we can say that the public sandy beach is very wide and kept clean. He is free. However, umbrellas and sunbeds are available for rent at an additional cost, but its cost is small. According to tourists, the beach has a lot of free space, it is not crowded here. You can not buy sunbeds, but sit on your own towel directly on a warm sand.





There are coastal cafes and water attractions. Attentive rescuers always work, in case of strong waves they diligently monitor the tourists and do not let them into the water.

The entrance to the sea is gentle, the seabed is sandy. When there are no waves, the water is clean and clear.

Guest reviews

Hotel Oasis Del Mare 4 * (Bulgaria), reviews of which are mostly positive, are loved by both the Bulgarians themselves and foreign tourists. On the forums they share their impressions:

  • the restaurant has a large selection of dishes, even for a small child, especially a lot of fruits and vegetables;
  • the hotel is located in a quiet and peaceful place, very close to the sea and a very wide sandy beach;
  • extensive animation program for children;
  • bright and spacious rooms with comfortable furniture; on the balconies there are tables and chairs;
  • Friendly and helpful staff.

The disadvantages of the hotel tourists include the almost complete lack of hotel territory and sometimes not very high-quality cleaning of the room. Some guests also speak negatively of light carpet on the floor in the room, as it is much harder to keep clean than laminate or floor tiles.

In general, the hotel is popular with vacationers. Not only foreign tourists, but also Bulgarian families like to come here. They recommend Oasis Del Mare 4 * to their friends and acquaintances, but they warn that it is better to book rooms in advance.
