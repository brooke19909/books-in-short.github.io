Barriers to completing ISS

The Interdepartmental Strategic Committee of Russia harshly criticized the latest development of the system. The fact is that the domestic organization decided to follow geological traditions in the future. The main tasks of the system

The first goal pursued by the stratigraphic scale is to identify local temporal relationships. Of great importance for the geology of all areas is the setting of the relative age of the layers. These aspects are independent of any patterns of chronostratigraphic units of a global nature. Secondly, the development of a standard scale is necessary. Chronostratigraphic classification

This system is an organization of breeds in units. It is based on criteria such as age and formation time. The main goal of the system is to organize the rocks that form the earth's crust into certain units. They, in turn, have their own names that correspond to intervals of the geological period. These elements are the basis of the temporal relationship and the system of recording geological events of history.

Subdivision

This element is a collection of all rock layers that are combined together on a specific basis. In particular, the process of their formation during the period of one interval is important. The division includes exclusively breeds that were formed in a certain period of the history of the planet. These elements are limited to isochronous surfaces. Their relative size and ranks are set by the length of the time period that they reflect. To compile a chronostratigraphic hierarchy, the thickness of deposits that are part of the units is not needed.
