"Buk Bar" (Rybinsk): cuisine and service

"Buk Bar" (Rybinsk) is a cozy and stylish establishment, in the interior of which dark tones prevail. Here you can see large leather sofas, book shelves, bright stained glass inserts that change colors from time to time. "Buk Bar" (Rybinsk) is located on Kirova Street 3. The bar is great for family gatherings and meetings with friends. In the institution, you can carry out calculations both in cash and with a credit card. For guests of the bar there is free access to Wi-Fi.





Kitchen

The bar serves European, Italian and Japanese dishes. In the menu of the institution, guests can find:

  • side dishes and sauces for them;
  • dishes prepared using WOK technology;
  • Rolls
  • grill menu;
  • hot meals;
  • various salads;
  • cold and hot snacks;
  • soups;
  • burgers;
  • poultry dishes ;
  • breakfast
  • Dessert.

All the items offered in the menu are prepared only from fresh products and are presented under the close supervision of the chef. Buk Bar (Rybinsk) offers a wide variety of alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks, cocktails, coffee drinks. For large companies discounts and promotions.





Service

The attendants in the Buk Bar (Rybinsk) are friendly and friendly. If necessary, the waiters will help you choose a dish and talk about the process of its preparation or components. The guests of the establishment can experience their singing talents in karaoke, as well as enjoy DJ music in the evenings.

Recently, the pub has been providing home delivery service, which can be ordered over the phone or on the website. The institution operates on weekdays from 8:00 in the morning, and on weekends from 10:00. "Buk Bar" (Rybinsk), the reviews of which are only positive, has established itself as a fashionable institution with a large assortment of delicious dishes and drinks. All guests will enjoy a pleasant and cozy atmosphere, allowing you to escape from the hustle and bustle of everyday life.
