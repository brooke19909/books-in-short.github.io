Partenit. Crimea. Reviews of satisfied tourists

Partenit ... Crimea ... Vacationers' comments indicate that this is actually a paradise that will appeal to even capricious and sophisticated travelers. Arriving here, the tourist must find decent options, as they say, for every taste and budget.

Section 1. Partenit. Crimea. Reviews: general description of the resort



Note that the urban type Partenit is located on the Black Sea. You will find it in the valley of the same name just between the Ayu-Dag and Kuchuk-Ayu mountains . A place with a long history has turned into a famous resort only three decades ago. But today it is considered the most prestigious area for recreation among all resorts of the Crimean coast. Partenit ... Crimea ... Sanatorium, camp site, hotel or room in the private sector - no matter what you prefer. One thing is certain: you will definitely enjoy your vacation.

In a few years, Partenit has grown; new hotels, resorts and holiday homes have appeared on the territory. Every year more and more tourists come here. They are attracted by picturesque landscapes, bright sun and warm sea. And residents, in turn, are very concerned about the preservation of natural beauty.

Section 2. Partenit. Crimea. Summer Vacation Reviews



The first summer month is the beginning of the holiday season. But vacationers do not leave the coast until mid-October. The dry subtropical climate resembles a Mediterranean. Partenitus is characterized by hot and dry summers. The average temperature is 24 ¬∞ above zero, and the water in the sea warms up to 23 ¬∞.

A strip of small pebble beaches stretches along the coast. Equipped, but wild places prevail. The most popular is Aivazovsky beach, where there are all amenities for vacationers: sunbeds, awnings, changing rooms, showers, rental items for beach accessories, a variety of water activities.

Section 3. Partenit. Crimea. Reviews and features of the rest



Partenit is a resort for people with different financial capabilities. Chebureks, samsa, buns and fruits are sold right on the beach and streets. But it is better to buy such food in network kiosks, the quality of goods of local merchants in a hot climate is a big question. The ideal option is to cook on your own or visit the dining rooms at the recreation centers. Sometimes you can afford lunch at Ararat, which serves Armenian cuisine. And you can dine in the traditions of Russian or Ukrainian cuisine in the "Brig" or "Sunflower".

Holidays in the Crimea, Partenit, of course, is no exception, it is famous for the fact that here, as a rule, everything is at hand. In the center of the village is concentrated the bulk of supermarkets and pharmacies. Some stores are located directly on the territory of hotels and resorts. In any of them you can buy small souvenirs in the form of magnets or charms with Crimean symbols. For vegetables and fruits, it is better to go to local markets. Care should be taken to domestic wines, you can run into low-quality goods. Such purchases are preferable to do in branded wine stores or supermarkets.

Entrance to many private beaches at hotels for outsiders is paid. In addition to the main water activities, tourists can take boat trips on yachts, go scuba diving and go fishing. Swimming in the warm sea can be alternated with walks in the surroundings and parks, along the way exploring historical sights.
