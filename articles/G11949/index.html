Why go to Sberbank if you can connect Mobile Bank?

In order to more conveniently use banking services, save time and ease of performing various operations on the account, banks regularly introduce new, more advanced services. So, Sberbank offers its customers to connect the "Mobile Bank" - a service designed for bank card holders and designed to facilitate their management of card accounts.

About the delights of mobile banks

This service significantly expands the boundaries of banking services in cases where there is no time or desire to come to the bank branch. Access to the account and transactions on it become possible through a mobile phone or smartphone. What allows you to pay for the Mobile Bank service?





All major payments, including replenishment of the telephone account, payment of the Internet, utilities, as well as loan installments, money transfers between cards and replenishment of deposit accounts are easy to make using this service.

In addition, the bank's client will be able to keep abreast of all card transactions - both receipts and write-offs of funds. If you suspect fraudulent transactions on the card, or if it was lost, you can also block it using this service.

How to connect the "Mobile Bank" of Sberbank? You can do this in one of several suggested ways.





The first way, the classic

For the Mobile Bank service to become available on your phone, you need to come to the Sberbank branch and write a statement, presenting your passport to the employee. At the same time, you will be prompted to connect Mobile Bank with a full list of services (full package) or with limited capabilities (economical package).

In the second case, the bank will not notify you of card transactions. If necessary, this service can be used for a fee. There is no subscription fee for the Economy package, but reuse of paid services may be more expensive than a monthly fee. The second method, available

When asked about how to connect the Mobile Bank, Sberbank gives another answer: using self-service devices. You can also purchase the Mobile Bank service through an ATM or payment terminal. For this you only need a bank card.

The third way, convenient

If you have no time to visit a bank branch or look for the nearest ATM to connect Mobile Bank, just call the hotline 8-800-200-37-47. An employee of the CALL-center will ask you to give your full name, card details (except for the PIN code!) And the code word that you indicated when registering it.

As you can see, connecting a mobile bank is not difficult, especially if there is a wide selection of connection methods.

If for some reason the need for the Mobile Bank service has disappeared, then disabling the service is also very easy. To do this, you can use the telephone service of Sberbank, an ATM, or come to the office yourself.
