Sea Galaxy Hotel, Sochi: reviews and photos of tourists

The large Russian resort on the Black Sea called Sochi, of course, most attracts those tourists who want to enjoy a beach holiday in the summer. However, there are hotels in which it is not only pleasant to simply relax at any time of the year and enjoy the local attractions, but also in which you can organize an important event at the highest level. Hotel in Sochi Sea Galaxy offers its guests great opportunities for a variety of business and special occasions.

Where is Sea Galaxy located

The embankments of any resort town are usually teeming with various types of hotels. So the Sea Galaxy Hotel (Sochi) is located in close proximity to the Black Sea: only 100 meters from the central beach, where most of the city's guests like to relax. The proximity to Kurortny Prospect - the main transport artery of Central Sochi - makes this hotel accessible to any tourist. Very close to the hotel there is a green park named after Frunze, and you can easily walk to the arboretum and circus from it.

How to get to Sea Galaxy

Due to the convenient location, the drive to the hotel does not cause much difficulty. You can come here, for example, by your own car, while it is worth considering that the entrance to the territory is from Gagrinskaya street.

From the adler airport buses or minibuses go here, you will need to get out at the Svetlana stop. You can get to this stop by the same public transport from the railway station of the city of Sochi, you will need to go in the direction of Adler. The hotel itself is only 100 meters from the stop.





Hotel Room Categories

The capacious eight-story building of the Sea Galaxy Hotel (Sochi) offers tourists comfortable rooms, the number of which is 341. They can accommodate up to 700 guests at a time. Different categories of rooms allow this hotel to become a great place to relax and organize business meetings for both very demanding and wealthy guests, as well as for tourists with average incomes.

The range of hotel rooms is presented in the following categories: standard and superior rooms, spacious studios and exclusive suites. In any room there is the opportunity to use wireless Internet and long-distance telephony. Everywhere there are reliable electronic safes, air conditioners, plasma panels with the ability to view many satellite channels. Bathrooms in each room are equipped with comfortable showers.

Hotel facilities

At the Sea Galaxy Hotel (Sochi), each guest is provided with unique services that will not leave anyone indifferent.

The wonderful spa complex of Sea Galaxy Hotel will satisfy any desires of vacationers, providing them with the best services for relaxation and rest. Here you can get professional body and face care, relax in the hammam and thermal zone. There is also the opportunity to use hairdressing services and make manicures and pedicures.





For sports enthusiasts, the hotel has a gym, which has all the necessary sports equipment and fitness equipment. With their help, you can maintain yourself in shape both on vacation and on a business trip.

Guests arriving by car can use the secure free parking. It is being monitored around the clock. And for those who need to get to the hotel from the airport or from the railway station, the hotel will be happy to provide a shuttle service.

Dining at the hotel

The hotel has an excellent banquet complex called Eclair. Using the services of this institution, you can arrange an excellent banquet, business dinner, buffet or coffee break. The chef, who works in this restaurant, participated in the first season of the television show "Infernal Cuisine", and he is ready to impress any guest with some original gourmet dish or traditional Italian food.





There is a hall in which a buffet-style food system is organized for hotel guests. Here, every day, guests are waiting for fresh dishes from the highest quality products. There are also bars where you can always have a cup of coffee or a stronger drink.

Organization of events

The special pride of the Sea Galaxy Hotel (Sochi) are several halls that are perfect for any, even the most important event. It can be a conference, some kind of negotiations or even a wedding. Due to the fact that the hotel provides an integrated approach to organizing any event, you can not even doubt that it will be excellent. The hotel staff will help to fully plan it, starting with how to arrange furniture, and ending with what kind of food will be on the tables.

The hotel has a congress hall with a cascading layout, where you can brilliantly conduct a seminar, exhibition or present any new product. Thanks to the special organization of space in this hall, different working areas can be distinguished, and it can accommodate up to 600 people. This room is located next to the banquet, which is also very convenient for certain purposes.





On the top floor of the hotel building there is a spacious Panorama Hall, which is suitable for more informal and unique events. From here the most breathtaking view of the city and its surroundings opens, so such a meeting will be remembered by guests for a long time. It can accommodate up to 150 people.

Impressions of vacationers about the hotel

Guests of the Krasnodar Territory are happy to stay at the Sea Galaxy Hotel (Sochi). Reviews about him are mostly positive. Those who stayed here really like the location of the hotel, because from here you can easily and quickly get to many attractions of the city, and the city beach is very close. Most often, guests are not always disappointed by the quick and clear work of the staff. Some also lack their own beach and entertainment.





But overall, the Sea Galaxy Hotel will be a great place to relax or business meeting. After all, leaving it, you can easily find yourself in the most popular places in Sochi or on the beach. And the views that open from its last floors make you forget about everything in the world.
