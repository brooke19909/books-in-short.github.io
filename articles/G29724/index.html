A bit about dolphins

Dolphins are very smart. According to intelligence, they overtook elephants, the king of beasts of a lion and cheerful monkeys. They are explained among themselves by a whistle. During training, the dolphin can tell the sound of his fellow pedal which pedal must be pressed, right or left, to get the fish.

They have developed echolocation, and therefore they easily recognize the size of the obstacle and report it to a friend.

Dolphins are great at imitating sounds. From them you can hear the sound of a creaking door. They can copy bell ringing, gurgling water and even human speech and laughter.

Dolphins easily remember the shape of a circle, square, ball. And they will never confuse a voluminous object with a flat figure.



They have a great eye. Therefore, dolphins deftly slip through the hoop or throw a ball into the basket with their nose.

The work of trainers who serve the dolphinarium is based on these and other skills. Novorossiysk can be proud of their well-coordinated work.

Performance in Novorossiysk

The program is based on acrobatic and eccentric tricks performed by clever Black Sea bottlenose dolphins Mike and Bella, Far Eastern whale whales, Baikal sea lion, Semen fur seal.

During the presentation, draws with gifts are held. You can take pictures of marine artists from your place for free, swim with them or in a boat.

The whole performance looks in one breath, with unflagging interest. In the Novorossiysk dolphinarium you will find new unusual sensations and, of course, delight and joy.
