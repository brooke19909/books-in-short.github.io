Hotel "Old Town", Ryazan: address, photo, reviews, how to get

On one of the central streets of modern Ryazan there is a huge hotel complex, which offers a wonderful vacation in conditions of increased comfort.



general information

Hotel "Old City" in Ryazan is a place in which travelers and businessmen who visit this city prefer to have a rest, having a huge number of historical monuments and simply interesting places.

Location

Hotel "Old City" is located in Ryazan, on Munster Street, which is located in the central part of the city. Not far from this place you can find a large number of historical monuments that you can visit when staying at this hotel. A 15-minute walk from the "Old Town" is the Ryazan Fortress, which is preferred by everyone who is first in the city.

Most tourists who arrive in Ryazan begin their movement around the city from the railway station, where trains bring them. From here, the hotel can be reached by public transport or taxi - the distance between these points is only 2.5 km.

The address of the hotel "Old City": Ryazan, street Munster, 2.



Rooms

The hotel has a total of 253 rooms, equipped with the latest technology. They have modern furniture made of quality materials in a classic style. As guests note, here every detail is filled with simplicity and elegance, which captivates at first sight. On the photo of the rooms of the hotel "Old City" (Ryazan) you can observe the beautiful interior, made in spacious rooms.

All apartments offered to tourists are divided into three categories: standard, superior and junior.

Each room has an individual bathroom, which has modern plumbing, made of quality materials. In addition, slippers, bathrobes and towels are waiting for guests at the hotel, as well as a set of bath accessories necessary for maintaining hygiene. In addition, hair dryers are equipped here, the presence of which guests rate positively in their comments.

All types of apartments have free internet access (Wi-Fi), which is also available throughout the hotel.



Standard

The apartments of the standard group in the Hotel "Old City" (Ryazan) are presented in a small area - each of them is 22 square meters. m. Despite such a small space, it has a huge sleeping area, which harmoniously combines with the work.

The standard class room is designed for two guests, who can accommodate in one large or two single beds. Each of them is covered with expensive high-quality linen and has an orthopedic mattress, necessary for a good sleep. Opposite the beds in the room there is a large pendant plasma TV that broadcasts satellite channels in various languages.

Guests of the apartments often notice that the interior has a sitting area in the form of a pair of half chairs and a round white table, located near the window. Here you can drink morning coffee and admire the beautiful city streets, which offer panoramic views from here.

For guests' use, these rooms are provided with a minibar, the range of which is updated every day with new soft drinks that do not contain alcohol. In addition, there is a safe and a telephone connected to the international communication line.
