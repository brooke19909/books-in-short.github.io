LCD "Honey Valley": reviews, overview, features and layout

It will not be a secret for anyone that everyone dreams of having their own comfortable housing. Due to the fact that new buildings are constantly being built in our country, it will not be difficult to purchase an apartment. The most important thing is to have finances to fulfill your dreams. Residential complex Barkli "Honey Valley" is a full-fledged comfort-class residential district, aimed at middle-income citizens. The project is being implemented by one of the largest domestic corporations working in the field of reconstruction of various real estate and construction of residential buildings. The company has many successful projects and extensive experience. A distinctive feature of the "Honey Valley" is that, despite the relatively low cost of housing, it has a good finish and provides maximum comfort for living.

Location and transport accessibility

Overview Barkley LCD "Honey Valley" should begin with its location. The place for construction was chosen very successfully. The housing complex is located in the village of Krekshino, located only 26 kilometers from the Moscow Ring Road. Therefore, it will be very convenient for residents to get to work, and the travel time will be no more than half an hour, taking into account the absence of traffic jams.

With transport accessibility, everything is just fine. On one side of the village is the Kiev highway, and on the other - Minsk. Both motorways have been recently expanded, so the flow of cars does not slow down even during busy hours. Among the minuses, we can only highlight that at the very entrance to the "Honey Valley" is a railway crossing. In addition, there are no overlapping parking lots near the village, so the residents will have certain problems with where to leave the transport if they suddenly decide to take the metro.

As for pedestrians, they will get to the Honey Valley residential complex (reviews about the microdistrict will be presented at the end of the article) is much more complicated. The thing is that there are no public transport or metro stations nearby, and only a train goes with a certain frequency. From it you still have to walk about 20 minutes. Regular buses go to the city from neighboring settlements, but they are quite far away. Thus, in terms of transport accessibility, ‚ÄúHoney Valley‚Äù loses to many other residential complexes. If you work in Moscow, then getting to the office will be very problematic in the absence of a personal vehicle. In the future, they plan to build a new railway station near the village of Krekshino, but when this happens, it remains unknown.

Ecological environment and living conditions

Barkli LCD "Honey Valley" in Marushkino is located near the railway lines, and Vnukovo Airport is also located ten kilometers from it, so you should not count on peace and quiet. The neighborhood is quite noisy, although it is surrounded by forest. As for the environmental component, then everything is much better. There are no industrial enterprises and factories in this area that release harmful and dangerous substances and chemicals into the atmosphere.

Separately, it is worth saying a few words about water supply. In the village itself there is no centralized water supply system, which worries many people interested in buying housing in the area. As for the ‚ÄúHoney Valley‚Äù, it will be fed from a separate artesian well, the field of which is located at a depth of 200 meters. At the same time, water undergoes additional filtration through a specialized treatment system.

The structure of the microdistrict

LCD "Honey Valley" (you can study the residents' reviews at the end of the article) consists of nine buildings, each of which has 3 residential buildings with the number of floors from 4 to 7. The designers took care not only that all the buildings were rationally located . Much attention was paid to infrastructure. The territory of the residential complex includes a kindergarten, a shopping center and entertainment facilities.

The project was launched in September 2015, and a year and a half later the apartments were handed over to the rightful owners, recorded on the first receipt. The construction of the 2nd and 3rd phases began in the middle of autumn 2016, and in the winter of 2017 the construction of the last facility was completed. The scope of the project is estimated at 7.5 billion rubles, of which 40 percent is the share of the company. The rest is borrowed funds issued by Sberbank as a loan. Thus, in Moscow LCD Barkley "Honey Valley" has become one of the largest projects of our time. The capital has not seen anything like this for a long period of time.
