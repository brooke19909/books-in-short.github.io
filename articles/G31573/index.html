On the mountain slopes of Finland you can ride not only skiing, but also on sleds, snowmobiles, snowboards, ice skates and even dog sledding.



Most tourists buy ski tours from December to April. At this time there is enough snow for a long ride.

Himos

This is a universal resort, as it is suitable for both winter and summer holidays. Himos is located near the cities of Tamter and Lahti. It regularly hosts alpine skiing and freestyle competitions.



The resort has created seventeen slopes of varying difficulty. Snow cannons are installed on all, helping to maintain the tracks in good condition. You can ride here until the end of April. The length of the tracks is 20 kilometers. They are beautifully lit, so you can ride until dawn. On Himos there are lighter trails for beginners, on which you can learn the basics of skiing.

Pello

This northern country can offer many excellent facilities to its guests. At your service are any ski resorts. On a map of Finland you can see where they are located, which slopes are nearby. Many tourists during the holidays even visit several resorts.

Pello is located in the west of Lapland, not far from Rovaniemi, and it is only 25 kilometers from the Swedish resort of Pullinki. Pello can offer vacationers 200 kilometers of beautiful trails. The longest of them stretches for almost 1.5 kilometers. Here it is possible to arrange dog sled races, ride a snowmobile.

Tourists get to the ski lifts by buses, which await them at the hotels. There are 16 excellent tracks for guests in Pello, routes for children are created, night skiing is possible, which leaves an unforgettable experience. And one more detail: the main road to the Arctic Circle passes through this resort.

Vuokatti

Listing the best ski resorts in Finland, one can not say about Vuokatti. He is very popular in the country. Not only foreign tourists like to be here, but locals also enjoy relaxing.

Quite often, freestyle competitions are held here. A special tunnel has been built for athletes on Vuokatti, in which the required temperature is maintained. Therefore, in this resort you can do your favorite sport not only in the midst of the winter season.



In Vuokatti, all conditions are created for a comfortable stay with your family. In addition to skiing, guests are offered walks on a snowmobile, deer, snowmobile. And small tourists will certainly be delighted to visit the Katinkulta water park.

Tahko

This resort is well known to professional athletes. However, amateurs can choose for themselves one of 20 tracks. The longest of them stretches for 1200 meters. Almost all of them are lit in the evening and at night. A beautiful park has been created for snowboarders.

For cross-country skiing, the length of the routes is 84 kilometers. Seventeen ski slopes of increased complexity. In addition, vacationers are invited to do winter fishing and ice skating.

Kuopio

Lovers of unspoiled nature and marvelous landscapes need to buy ski tours in Cuorio. A huge amount of snow and comfortable air temperature, the expanse of the frozen Lake Kallavesi, the picturesque hill of Puyo - all this can be seen in the popular resort of Kuopio.



More than 400 kilometers of tracks are laid here annually. True, they are partially covered. But this does not spoil the overall impression of the resort.

Levy

Today, in many countries there are ski resorts. On a map of Finland it can be seen that Levi is only 170 kilometers away from the Arctic Circle. This is the most popular place in Lapland.

Many tourists are sure that Levi resort is the best in the country. Its territory is rich in picturesque slopes, of which there are about fifty. In addition, there are two water parks and many other entertainment complexes.



Levy is a bit like the resorts of the Alps. Most cottages and hotels are close to the slopes. Today, this wonderful resort can simultaneously host fifteen thousand guests.

The resort has beautiful slopes for young skiers, modern lifts are available. A special park with a superpipe, street and halfpipe was created for snowboarders. Nearby open cafes, bars, restaurants.
