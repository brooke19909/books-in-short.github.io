Hotel White Square Nirvana Holiday Villas 3 * (Goa, India): overview, description and reviews of tourists

Planning an unforgettable vacation in Goa (India)? Are you looking for a budget but comfortable hotel away from city noise and with green areas? In this case, White Square Nirvana Holiday Villas 3 * is perfect for you.





General information, photo

The hotel is located in the village of Guirim (North Goa). Dabolim Airport is 40 kilometers away, while Tivim Train Station is 12 kilometers away. The hotel is 8 kilometers away from the nearest beach called Calangute. You can get to it by tuk-tuk, taxi or rented motorbike. In addition, the hotel provides a free shuttle service twice a day.

Hotel Nirvana Holiday Villas was built in 2008. In 2011, renovation was carried out here. The housing stock of the hotel consists of 30 rooms located in the main two-story building and several villas located in a large, green and very well-groomed area. The apartments have everything you need for a comfortable stay: furniture, air conditioning, ceiling fan, refrigerator, TV, bathroom. For an extra fee, you can use the safe and access to Wi-Fi. Rooms are cleaned and towels changed upon request.

The hotel has two outdoor pools - for adults and for children (equipped with heating). Next to them is a sun terrace with sun loungers and parasols. The hotel has a restaurant serving both Indian and European cuisine, and a bar. Guests can also exchange currency at the hotel and rent a scooter.





White Square Nirvana Holiday Villas 3 *: tourist reviews

As you know, during a trip to another country, the hotel becomes a kind of second home for travelers. That is why his choice should be given increased attention. After all, no one wants to live in a dirty and uncomfortable hotel room with unfriendly staff, even if it comes to a budget option. According to experienced tourists, in the process of finding a hotel it makes sense to definitely study the reviews of those who have already stayed in a particular place. After all, they try to tell in detail about the pros and cons of a particular hotel, which will help you not to make a mistake in your choice.

Today we offer to find out what impressions our compatriots left after living in White Square Nirvana Holiday Villas 3 *. The rating of this hotel, based on guest ratings, indicates that almost all guests were not disappointed in their choice. So, it is above average and makes up 4.2 points out of the maximum possible five.





Housing stock

Judging by the comments left, our compatriots were pleased with the rooms offered in the hotel in question. Of course, since this is an economy hotel, you should not expect luxurious spacious apartments with new furniture and modern appliances. However, there is everything necessary for living, the rooms are clean and comfortable. In addition, the vast majority of tourists spent a little time at the hotel, coming here mainly just to spend the night. A small minus, some tourists considered the lack of free Wi-Fi in the rooms. So, you could connect to the Internet for about 300 rupees for several days of stay. However, as travelers say, free Wi-Fi is available in almost all cafes and beaches, so you can always chat with family and friends staying at home.





Service

Cleaning in the rooms of White Square Nirvana Holiday Villas 3 * (Guirim), according to the guests, is carried out exclusively at the request of the residents. So, if you want a maid to drop into your apartment during the day, then when leaving the hotel, handing over the key to the reception, mention this. In this case, upon returning to the hotel you will find a cleaned room. The same goes for towels. If you want to be changed, you must notify the administrator. In general, some tourists noted that this was slightly unusual, but this approach did not cause any problems.

As for the hotel staff as a whole, they seemed to the travelers very friendly, welcoming and always ready to help in resolving any issue. Communication here is mainly in English. However, some hotel workers understand a little in Russian.





Nutrition

The cost of living in Nirvana Holiday Villas includes breakfast only. According to our compatriots, they are standard for most 2- and 3-star hotels in Goa. So, in the morning you will be offered scrambled eggs or omelet, vegetables, toasts with jam, fresh fruits, one Indian dish and tea or coffee. In general, according to most tourists, this is enough to eat at the beginning of the day.

Guests can also have lunch and dinner in the hotel restaurant. Vacationers note that the cooking here is very good, and the prices are reasonable. However, most tourists ate outside the hotel. After all, both on the beaches and in the city there are lots of cafes for every taste.

Sea

The nearest beach from White Square Nirvana Holiday Villas 3 * (India, Goa) can be reached in about 10 minutes. The hotel provides a beach shuttle twice a day. So, a free bus for guests leaves the hotel at 10 a.m. And at five in the evening you can return to the hotel territory on it. You can also take a taxi or get to the coast in tuk-tuk. Many travelers also prefer to rent a motorbike for their entire stay in Goa.

After all, this opens up unlimited opportunities for movement around the island, and you can freely visit local attractions and various beaches. In addition, renting a scooter will significantly save on taxis and tuk-tuk. However, experienced travelers are advised to soberly assess their ability to manage a motorbike, taking into account the rather chaotic and also left-hand traffic in India. However, most of our compatriots have no difficulties with this.

As for the beaches themselves, despite the fact that Calangute is the closest to the hotel, tourists are advised to visit other places. All the popular beaches on Goa have plenty of cafes and bars where you can have a bite to eat, have a drink and sit on comfortable sun loungers.
