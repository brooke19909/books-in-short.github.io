Hotel "Provincial", Kirov: description and reviews

Hotel "Provincial" in Kirov - one of the local attractions of the city. It is very popular among guests, as it is an interesting historical object, which is located in a building built in 1819. In the last century on the site of this hotel was a tenement house. Now everyone here has the opportunity to stay and relax for some time in the interiors typical of those times. Hotel "Provincial" is so named since 2003. Before that, there was the Kolos Motel.



Location

Hotel "Gubernskaya" (Kirov) is located in a very picturesque place - in a historical building surrounded by old park trees. It is around it that the most interesting historical and cultural sites are located, such as the Philharmonic, the Saltykov-Shchedrin Museum and the Museum of Local Lore. Not far from here there is a sports complex with a large swimming pool, as well as many shops.

The address at which the Gubernskaya Hotel in Kirov is located is Kazanskaya, 74, is known to all residents of the city, therefore, having arrived here, you can easily find it simply by finding out the necessary direction from passers-by. This is the central part of the metropolis.

Rooms

The Gubernskaya Hotel (Kirov) has 42 rooms, any of which can be rented by a guest. All of them are of different categories: economy, standard, junior suite, luxury. Hotel rooms are designed for different numbers of guests (from 1 to 3 people).

All of them are made in light shades and furnished with modern furniture, stylized for the last century. This is the first thing that is noted when looking at the interior, which is shown by the photo of the Gubernskaya Hotel (Kirov). Each room is equipped with the necessary set of furniture, which includes: a bed, cabinets for storing personal belongings, a wardrobe, a mirror. Many rooms have plasma televisions, telephones, and refrigerators. All rooms, except for the economy class, have a separate shower room in which there is a hairdryer and the necessary set of bath accessories. Also, some rooms have a modern air conditioning system, which allows you to control the climatic conditions in the room.



As for the cost, single rooms cost from 990 rubles, doubles - from 1430, and triple - from 2145 rubles per night. The price of your stay at the hotel may also include breakfast, lunch and dinner.

Room reservation

Before checking into the Gubernskaya Hotel in Kirov, you should book a room in advance. You can do this by calling the phone number listed on its official website. You can also fill out the online form, which is located there. Recently, you can book your desired room through a special application that works on devices with the Android operating system.

In order to pay for hotel accommodation, you can use cash. Also, electronic money (Qiwi, WebMoney, Yandex.Money) or funds on bank cards are accepted for payment.



Special offers

Guests staying at the Gubernskaya Hotel (Kirov) have the opportunity to take advantage of various advantageous offers that are offered here.

First of all, a good discount is supposed for those who check into a hotel as a large company (for example, a group of tourists, a team of business colleagues, students) or a family. The rates offered by the hotel for groups of guests can be found on the official website of the "Provincial". If the guest settles on a weekend or official holiday, the cost per day of stay is also reduced.

For newlyweds who stay at the Gubernskaya Hotel (Kirov), there is a service for renting a "wedding room". Guests who have booked this service will drop into a festively decorated room, which includes fruits and a bottle of champagne as a bonus. Within the walls of the hotel, a young married couple can arrange a festive photo and video shooting.



Hotel Services

First of all, any guest of the Gubernskaya Hotel has the opportunity to use free access to the Internet, which allows you to always stay in touch with family and friends.

Everyone has the opportunity to use the services of a left-luggage office, in which you can leave your personal belongings - it is provided for a fee.

The duties of the staff at the front desk also include the provision of a number of services. First of all, it is a "wake-up call", which means dialing an employee into a room at a certain time by order of a guest. Also from here correspondence is delivered to the room. If desired, employees can call an ambulance, taxi or police. Here, they will also be happy to answer any questions of interest regarding the infrastructure of the city, the location of objects.

Guests staying at the Gubernskaya Hotel are provided with a free-use room for ironing.

All guests who come here in private cars can use the paid secure parking, which is available here in the courtyard. It conducts round-the-clock video surveillance, which ensures complete safety for the car.



Additional Information

For the convenience of those guests who check into the hotel during a business trip, there is a conference room for up to 32 people. It is equipped with comfortable seating. It also has modern sound and lighting equipment, as well as a projector for presentations. This room can be rented by the hour.



On the ground floor of the hotel there is a Transit cafe, where visitors are prepared with dishes of European and Russian cuisine. There is also a varied Lenten menu. This cafe can be visited both by city residents and guests of the Gubernskaya hotel (Kirov).



Visitors reviews

The guests of the Gubernskaya hotel are usually satisfied with the range of services provided in it. They usually leave their reviews on social networks and on special sites.

Many of them say that the "Provincial" has a responsible and responsive staff who always cares about the comfort of the guests of the institution. Some guests are not always happy with the ban on smoking at the hotel. But here you can stay with your pets.

According to the residents, all the requests expressed by them are fulfilled by the hotel staff unconditionally and with a pleasant smile on their faces.

Also, guests note very affordable prices in the cafe menu, which is located at the hotel. The average bill here is about 300 rubles per person, which is very cheap. On request, for a fee, dishes from the cafe can be delivered to your room.

Here you can also organize a banquet or any event, which will be very inexpensive compared to other institutions. According to commentators, this is a big plus of the Gubernskaya Hotel (Kirov). The institution is one of the leaders in its field of activity.

Address of the hotel "Provincial" (Kirov)

The hotel is located at: Kazanskaya street, 74. It is not far from the city center, literally five minutes walk. Near the "Provincial" is the Seraphim Cathedral, and on the contrary - the park to them. Gagarin.

How to get to the hotel "Provincial" in Kirov? First of all, you should decide on which transport to go. If the guest plans to get from the railway station (which, incidentally, is not far away), then in this case you need to go to the hotel by buses No. 3, 40, 67, 68 to the Philharmonic stop - the trip will take no more than five minutes.

If transportation from the Kirov central bus station is planned, then in this case it will be easy to take bus No. 37 to the Philharmonic stop.

If the guest plans to travel by public transport from the Pobedilovo airport, he should take bus 116 to the Tsapeli Village stop, then transfer by train, which will take you to the railway station, and the location map from this point of the city is described above.
