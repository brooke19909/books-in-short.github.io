Hotel Suzana 3 * (Budva, Montenegro): photos, reviews

Budva, being the most popular city of Montenegro, annually receives thousands of vacationers. Surely many of those who have never been here have heard a lot of flattering and enthusiastic reviews. The rich tourist infrastructure of the resort, reasonable prices and a huge cultural heritage are what attract tourists from various corners of the globe. A modern city with large shopping centers is harmoniously combined with a colorful and original Budva.

If you decide to spend your vacation in Montenegro, we strongly recommend staying in Budva. Which housing option for yourself to choose? There are many excellent hotels, there are disgusting places, so you need to approach the choice of hotel more carefully. How do you like the hotel Suzana 3 * (Budva, Montenegro)? I must admit that for Europe the 3 * category is considered quite decent, as a rule, these hotels can offer good service and comfortable rooms. It remains only to understand what Suzana 3 * can offer its guests (Montenegro, Budva). Reviews of real vacationers will help to give this option an objective assessment.





About hotel

So, before us is not a hotel, but Vila Suzana 3 * (Montenegro, Budva), located just 150 meters from the sea. The villa boasts a prime location in relation to all infrastructure facilities. Here, the sea, cafes and entertainment are just a stone's throw away. Within walking distance there is a public transport stop with links to anywhere in the resort. All tourists note the proximity to the unique old city: only 15-20 minutes at a calm pace, and you are surrounded by stunning architecture. Be sure to specify at the stage of booking a room in Suzana 3 * (Budva, Montenegro) how to get to the hotel.





The villa is literally surrounded by greenery, located in one of the most picturesque regions of the resort, which is why it attracts the attention of vacationers.

Rooms

The villa has 22 rooms of various categories: standard double, studio apartments and four-bed apartments. If everything is clear with double rooms, then the remaining categories will probably raise a number of questions. Studio apartments are chic rooms for families with children or groups of friends. The room has two separate bedrooms, each of which has a private balcony, as well as a bathroom, a living room and a dining room with a kitchen. Four-seater apartments are similar in layout and arrangement to studio apartments, but have one obvious and undeniable advantage - they are facing the coast, due to which a stunning view of the sea opens from the balcony.





The rooms are small, but they have everything you need for a comfortable stay: a kettle with a set of dishes, a plasma TV with satellite channels, air conditioning, a refrigerator. By the way, as for the TV: tourists will find two Russian channels and will be able to watch their favorite shows and TV shows. Appliances in most rooms are working properly. The only thing that raises complaints from vacationers is the condition of furniture, plumbing and textiles. Some sheets have worn out by an order of magnitude: old stubborn spots and holes are visible, it would not hurt to replace them with new sets. The furniture in the villa is also new, sometimes it creaks, the doors of the closet may fall off, but all this is trifles. The rooms have tiled floors, which, of course, is convenient from the point of view of practicality and cleanliness of the room, but tourists with young children would like to see a carpet or even a small bedside rug on the floor , creating an atmosphere of home warmth and comfort. The bathroom has a small but reasonably comfortable shower: no problems with the supply of hot water have been detected, sometimes a faucet leaks, resulting in a puddle on the floor.





Infrastructure

If you are looking for a great place for your vacation with a small but well-kept garden surrounded by greenery, you should like Villa Suzana 3 * (Budva, Montenegro). Photos of tourists confirm that the courtyard is really luxurious - there is a lounge and barbecue area, as well as a spacious green lawn, ideal for children's games.

The hotel has no pool, which for some vacationers is a drawback. Do not forget that you go to the sea, especially since from the villa to the coast only 5-7 minutes walk. The pool may be needed only in the cold season, in the season it will certainly never become a worthy alternative to sea water.

Nutrition

The price of accommodation at Villa Suzana 3 * (Budva, Montenegro) includes meals of the selected category. Most often, tourists stay on half board, which includes breakfast and dinner. Breakfasts are built on the buffet system, do not impress with their variety, but hardly anyone will remain hungry. Breakfast includes all kinds of eggs, toasts, with jam, cold cuts, cheese, pastes, breakfast cereals, milk and yogurt. For dinner - light soup, the second one to choose from, fresh pastries, tea, coffee, juices.





With food in Montenegro, in Budva in particular, there certainly will not be any problems: prices are acceptable in all local cafes and restaurants. The quality of the products is on top, and the local cuisine impresses with its splendor and color.

Service

The villa can offer good service: rooms are cleaned daily, towels are changed every 2-3 days, bed linen - once a week. All tourists can count on round-the-clock access to wireless high-speed Internet. Signal quality largely depends on the location of the number, but in general it is enough for messaging.

Tourists note that Villa Suzana 3 * (Budva, Montenegro) is fully consistent with its category and cost of living. Reviews are mostly positive, vacationers note the friendliness and friendliness of the staff. Of course, the hotel has a schedule of check-in and check-in, but certainly no one will leave vacationers to spend the night on the street.

And for an additional fee at the reception you can use the safe, order a shuttle to the airport, as well as get acquainted with the resort's rich excursion program.

Beach and sea

Villa Suzana 3 * (Budva, Montenegro), unfortunately, does not have its own beach, which is why vacationers will have to use the public: it is only 150 meters away. The beach is sand and pebble, equipped with everything necessary for a comfortable stay. The beach is very clean, the water is clear - what else is needed for a quality beach holiday. The only thing is that all vacationers need to stock up with special rubber slippers for swimming, be sure to purchase such shoes for children as well, to exclude the possibility of injury.





To summarize

Villa Suzana 3 * (Budva, Montenegro) is fully consistent with its stardom, suitable for a comfortable budget vacation. Of course, this option is not without small flaws. If you come to the sea, to the resort and plan to spend only a night in your room, we advise you to take a closer look at this option. Do not spoil your vacation, trying to find flaws, try to enjoy the stunning climate with chic virgin nature and the unique medieval architecture of Budva.
