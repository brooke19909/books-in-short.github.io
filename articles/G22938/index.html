Cheap hotels in Prague: description, addresses and reviews of tourists

Prague is a popular tourist destination among travelers from Russia and the CIS countries. However, many of them believe that spending a vacation in the city is an expensive pleasure. In their opinion, cheap hotels either have outdated rooms or are too far from the historical center. Of course, partly they are right, because many budget accommodation options really can not boast of a good location and high quality service. However, even among them there are wonderful hotels; reviews of tourists about them are presented.

Wenceslas square terraces

An inexpensive and good hotel in Prague is the Wenceslas Square Terraces apartment complex. There is also a metro station 30 meters from the hotel. The apartments have not only a bedroom and bathroom, but also their own kitchen. Balconies and windows overlook the historic center. Dolce Vita Suites Boutique

Many tourists prefer to choose cheap hotels with breakfast. In Prague, as a rule, even budget hotels include it in the price, so finding such an option for accommodation will not be difficult. Hotel opera

In inexpensive hotels in the center of Prague, families with children are often preferred to stay. Hotel Opera is a suitable option for them. It is located in an elegant old building in the historical part of the city. Near it there are also many shopping centers, cafes, restaurants. Tourists can see a tram stop across the road. The hotel also has outdoor parking. The rooms are equipped with flat-screen TVs, wireless internet, air conditioning. On site there is an outdoor parking, sauna and jacuzzi, an international restaurant and a Czech bar.

The address of this hotel is: Tesnov, 13, Prague, Czech Republic.

At the same time, the rooms in the hotel, according to the guests, are very small. They did not appreciate the low speed of the Internet, as well as not the highest quality cleaning.

Reviews of Cheap Prague Hotels

Thus, finding a budget accommodation option in the city is not so difficult. Despite the low cost, hotels often receive positive reviews from tourists. Guests believe that such hotels are suitable for those people who do not like to sit in one place and prefer to spend most of their time in the city.
