How to recover data on Android: procedure, backup, reset

What is backup?

If you have files on your phone that you really need, and you are afraid of losing them, it would be better to periodically transfer them to your PC for safekeeping. But you must admit, it‚Äôs impossible to manually transfer some specific phone settings, for example SMS correspondence, phone book data or special smartphone settings. First, turn off the phone and then simultaneously press the power and sound buttons. In the window that opens, the engineering menu, you need to click on the backup and restore tab and then backup. The copied phone data will be saved in the flash memory and can be transferred to a PC at any time.

Now, when you once again wonder about how to recover data from Android after an accidental cleaning, just use the restore tab in the same menu.

Preventive measures
