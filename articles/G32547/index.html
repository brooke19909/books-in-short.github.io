What is the best multicooker? Customer reviews and overview of popular models

In modern kitchens, the crock-pot has become a real "queen of the ball." After all, with its help you can cook a huge number of tasty and healthy dishes, and all recipes are extremely easy to prepare. But how to get right with the choice of this technique? What is the best multicooker?





Customer reviews when choosing should be analyzed as carefully as the information provided by the manufacturer. Indeed, only those people who have already managed to test a particular model in action can objectively evaluate all its advantages and disadvantages. So, which multicookers, according to customers, deserve attention and are worth the money spent on them?

Redmond RMC-M4502

This is one of the most versatile options for those who are looking for the best multicooker. Reviews on Redmond RMC-M4502 pay attention to a huge number of programs that allow you to fully automate the cooking process. There are 34 in total. Uniform heating of the bowl makes it possible to preserve all the useful properties of the products. This is extremely important for those who care about their diet. The functions of delayed start and auto-heating (heat maintenance) are very useful in practice. The cost of Redmond RMC-M4502 is about $ 200.

Panasonic SR-TMH18

Be sure to pay attention to this model if you are looking for the best multicooker. The reviews for the Panasonic SR-TMH18 speak for themselves. The compact device has truly enormous capabilities and can cook, bake, steam and stew. The cost of the Panasonic SR-TMH18 is about $ 150.

What to consider when choosing a multicooker?

We already talked about how important it is to read customer reviews before buying a multicooker . What is the best technique to cope with its tasks, more convenient to use and does not break, can only tell people who have already purchased a particular model. However, we will give some useful tips related to the selection of a suitable device:

  • If you plan to transport a slow cooker (take with you to the cottage, on vacation, etc.), it is better to pay attention to lightweight and compact models.
  • For those who often plan to cook cottage cheese, yoghurts and other sour-milk products, those models where the auto-heating function can be turned off are the best choice.
