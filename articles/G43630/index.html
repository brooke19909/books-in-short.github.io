Hotels in Gus-Khrustalny: addresses, description, photo

The city of glassblowers with such an unusual name, Gus-Khrustalny, attracts a huge number of tourists with its attractions. It is no secret that many are interested in the issue of living in the city. Gus-Khrustalny is 70 km from the regional center of Vladimir, and 250 km from Moscow. It is not advisable to go here for one day - the road takes too much time.

In Goose-crystal, hotels offer different levels of comfort, so you can easily choose the right option. About some of them - later in the article.

Hotel "Meschera"

This hotel is located in Gus-Khrustalny in the very center of the city. Guests are offered to accommodate comfortable rooms of various categories - from standard to deluxe. All are decorated in light pastel colors, furnished with modern comfortable furniture and equipped with necessary appliances. In 2008, a large-scale reconstruction of the number of rooms was carried out here.

Guests arriving in the city in their own cars can park their car in a specially equipped area on site. In this hotel in Gus-Khrustalny you can have a hearty and tasty meal in the cafe, which is located on the second floor. Grocery stores and supermarkets are within walking distance. Nearby is a picturesque lake with a sandy beach, on the shore of which in the warm season you can relax and have a picnic. Next to the hotel is the only crystal museum in our country.

Hotel in Gus-Khrustalny - "Meshcherskaya Manor" (Internatsionalnaya St., 105)

This is a huge complex, offering guests a magnificent modern hotel with rooms of different levels of comfort, a restaurant with exquisite dishes of Russian and European cuisine, a spacious and well-equipped conference room. Cozy, spacious, stylishly decorated rooms feature comfortable natural wood furniture, modern appliances, safes and minibars.

The rooms are wet-cleaned daily. Towels and bed linen are changed every three days. The hotel provides non-smoking rooms and smoking areas. Guests of one of the best hotels in Gus-Khrustalny can visit the Russian bathhouse and billiard room, SPA complex, and an indoor swimming pool.

Ski and bike rental is available. Everything is created here for a comfortable romantic, family and business vacation. The hotel can conduct seminars, trainings and other events. To do this, use a conference or banquet hall.

"Barinova Grove" (Internatsionalnaya St., 114)

This hotel in Gus Khrustalny is located near the lake, surrounded by a picturesque pine grove. The rooms are equipped with functional and comfortable furniture, there are LCD TVs with cable TV, personal safes. The private bathrooms have all the necessary toiletries. All rooms have soft carpeted floors, plastic windows and a heating system.

The hotel staff organizes hunting and fishing tours, entertainment programs, sports.

Catering for clients is organized in a cafe according to the approved menu. If desired, guests can order ready-made meals in the room. In addition, it is possible to deliver food by specialized services or self-catering in the nearby bars and restaurants. Breakfast is included in the room rate.

The hotel staff recommends that guests visit the shore of an artificial reservoir located 500 meters away. In the vicinity of the hotel there are religious sites, numerous cultural centers, interesting memorable places. The regional center Vladimir is at a distance of 51 km.

"Land" (Transportnaya St., 30)

You can rent a room at a cheap hotel in the Gus-Khrustalny Krai hotel. The hotel is located in the cultural and entertainment complex of the city, bearing the same name. The hotel offers standard rooms, the cost of accommodation in which starts from 400 rubles.

Quite affordable prices for comfortable and cozy rooms are an indisputable advantage of this hotel when choosing a place to stay on vacation. The rooms have everything you need for guests to stay, and the friendly staff creates a pleasant homely atmosphere.

The guests of the complex can take advantage of many services: visit the sauna and billiards room, karaoke bar, play paintball in the adjacent area. This is especially attractive to youth companies. And after playing on the barbecue area you can have a good time. All the necessary barbecue facilities can be rented at the hotel. Motorists can park their car in a guarded free parking lot. Public transport is within walking distance.
