Neon Hotel 2 * (Crete): description of rooms, service, reviews

Crete is the largest island of Greece. Recently, this tourist destination has become one of the most sought after. It is here that the respectable resorts of the world are located, attracting tourists from all over the world.

Sophisticated travelers call the island of Crete a true paradise. Absolutely everyone will like the island, because the list of entertainment is really wide. Lovers of classic beach vacations, connoisseurs of excursion programs, wellness, family and youth tourism will find something to their liking here.



For those who are used to relaxing in luxury, experts recommend choosing 5 star hotels - there are many of them on the island. However, there are hotels offering a rather high quality service at a very reasonable price. One of these hotels is the Neon Hotel (2 stars). Convenient location, wonderful landscapes and the proximity of the beach made it popular among tourists from Europe and Russia.

General information about the hotel Neon Hotel

This hotel belongs to the status of a family hotel. It was built in 1986 and completely renovated in 2006. Cosmetic repairs were carried out several years ago. Now the hotel is a fully modern three-story building with clean, spacious and comfortable rooms.

Around the hotel there is a landscaped area: a magnificent garden with orange trees, flower beds and other exotic Mediterranean vegetation. About 80 meters from the hotel there is a sand and pebble beach that stretches for almost 2 km and is ideal for relaxation.

Hotel Rooms

The three-story building of the Neon Hotel 2 * hotel has 66 rooms. Travelers are invited to stay in apartments with different numbers of places and level of comfort.

1-, 2- and 3-bed rooms are made concisely, without frills, but there is everything you need for a comfortable vacation. Double rooms have a choice of 2 single beds or 1 double. Some rooms have a balcony or terrace with spectacular views - it all depends on the location of the room and the chosen level of comfort.

Each room has bathrooms and toilets equipped with the necessary fixtures. The bathroom has a hairdryer, soft towels, necessary bath and cosmetic accessories and hygiene products.

Air conditioning is available at an additional cost.



The list of services that hotel staff offers guests:

Beach

The hotel is located on the first coastline. The beach is at a distance of about 80 meters from Neon Hotel, so you can get to it in 5 minutes. The coast is sandy and pebble, the entrance to the water is gentle, perfect for swimming with the whole family.

The beach is regularly cleaned, so the area is absolutely clean.

Vacationers can use umbrellas and sun loungers, but they will have to be paid separately.

Prices for accommodation

Hotel Neon Hotel belongs to the middle price segment, so accommodation here will be quite budget. This is especially important, since the island of Crete is considered a rather expensive tourist destination.

The cost of a standard double room here is around 4,000 rubles for 1 night. Luxury rooms are slightly higher in value. For early bookings or when buying last minute vouchers, the cost of living can be 10-30% lower.
