Why are triumphal arches being built?

The tradition of installing triumphal arches is rooted in the days of ancient Rome. Thus honored the winners and heroes. Moreover, such structures could be temporary and capital. The latter have survived in many ways to this day. These are monumental buildings with many spans, bas-reliefs and statues.

Historical background on the arch from Barcelona

What triumph is immortalized by the arch of Barcelona? It turned out that it was not victories in the hostilities that caused the erection of a monumental structure. But nevertheless, an event was found that required that descendants of years and centuries remember it.





In 1888, the World Exhibition was planned in Barcelona - a significant event in world circles of that time. The main entrance to it was to become the hallmark of the meeting. It is for this that the Arc de Triomphe of Barcelona was built. It was the gateway to the exhibition, which hosted representatives from 27 countries. In total, more than two million people from around the world marched under the arches during the event.

Arc de Triomphe of Barcelona: description of the structure

The author of the project was Josep Vilaseca i Casanovas (Jusep Vilaseka). A red brick building was erected. Among such structures, such a choice was innovative. Indeed, most shades of gray and gray-beige predominated. Its height is 29.8 meters. The Neo-Moorish style was a fashionable trend of that time, which reflected the splendor of a reviving city and country. This incomparable grace of lines combined with regal rigor.
