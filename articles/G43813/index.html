Krasnogorsk hotels: addresses, list of the best hotels, guest reviews

Krasnogorsk is a city in the Moscow region, which is located on the banks of the Moskva River, as well as its tributary, the Baths. It is approximately 22 km from the center of the capital and 2 km from the Moscow Ring Road. Someone comes to the city for business purposes, and someone - to relax in the picturesque surroundings. The guests of the city will kindly accept the hotels of Krasnogorsk. Tourists will appreciate the comfort and quality service.

Hotel "Aquarium"

The Aquarium Hotel in Krasnogorsk is located at 20. International Street. This is the building of the Crocus Expo International Exhibition Center. Sheremetyevo International Airport is only 15 km away.

For guests, the following features are provided:

  • accommodation in comfortable rooms (from 3400 rubles per day);
  • free parking at the exhibition center;
  • Restaurant and bar with room service
  • conference room rental;
  • rent a banquet hall;
  • 24-hour reception desk;
  • safe at the administrator;
  • luggage office;
  • ATM;
  • elevator;
  • free wifi.
