Description of the complex





This hotel, designed for families with children, is located in a quiet corner of Budva, very close, just a few steps from the local municipal government and the bus station. Not far from this hotel there is a good beach, as well as the old part of the city with the most important architectural objects and the main pedestrian alley.

Accommodation

The hotel has the following room types:

  • 10 apartments of the category "Bubble room", designed to accommodate 2 people. The room is equipped with one French bed or two single beds (at choice) and a bathroom.
  • 4 rooms for three guests. Beds are represented by a double bed and an extra bed for a third person. This room is ideal for families with one adult child.
  • 1 room of impressive size, designed to accommodate four guests. The bedroom has one large and two single beds.
  • 2 rooms with a living room. This apartment can accommodate 4 people. The bedroom has two beds (double and single). The living room has a sofa.

All rooms have private bathrooms. Most of them also have balconies.

Rooms





Family rooms consist of two spacious rooms. The hotel does not have an elevator. The upper floors will have to climb a steep staircase. But this is rather a plus, because on vacation, you also need a little workout. The room has a small refrigerator, split system, plasma TV (shows 2 Russian channels). One room is equipped with a bed, sofa, coffee table. The balcony offers scenic views. The second bedroom has two beds and a wardrobe.

The bathroom is spacious enough, but instead of a bath a shower. Only toiletries and shampoo bags are given out of toiletries, but you should not expect anything more from a three-star hotel. There are no bath towels, only facial ones give.

But you quickly forget about all the inconveniences when you go out onto the balcony. It offers a beautiful view of the mountain peaks. In the evenings you can have a great time with the whole family.

The rooms are cleaned very well. Underwear is changed regularly. The staff is honest, you can safely leave valuables in the room.

Sightseeing trips
