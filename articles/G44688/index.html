Hotel Sentido Sandy Beach Hotel 4 * (Cyprus, Larnaca): description and reviews

In order for the vacation to work out well, you need to carefully plan it, thinking through every little thing. The most important thing is not to lose the choice of hotel. If a trip is planned to Cyprus, to Larnaca, then you should definitely consider an option such as Sandy Beach Hotel 4 *. This is a hotel complex with a lot of advantages and advantages, which we will discuss now.





Location

So, Sandy Beach Hotel 4 * is located on Dekelia Road - at arm's length from a sandy beach. It is only 7 kilometers from the city center. And from there, you can drive to Larnaca Airport in just 10 minutes.

All interesting sights are within a radius of 9-13 km. These include Theofilos Georgiadis Park, Larnaca Harbor and Fort, Europe and Finikoudes Squares, St. Stephen's Armenian Church, Tuzla Mosque, Byzantine Museum of St. Lazarus, Pattiqueio Theater, local Salt Lake, Hala Sultan Tekke, Avgoru Ethnographic Museum and much more.

The hotel is in a rather convenient place - there are a lot of restaurants, bars, shops and taverns nearby, and literally three steps away there are also two bus stops. From there, regular taxis No. 424 and No. 425 leave - they can be used to get to the center of Larnaca.

Service

The Sandy Beach Hotel 4 * has everything that a hotel of this level should have, starting with free high-speed Wi-Fi and ending with private parking.

The reception is open 24/7, there is a currency exchange in the lobby, a ticket service, luggage storage, laundry and dry cleaning services, car rental, a souvenir shop, and room service.

But what deserves special attention is the staff. The hotel speaks five languages, including German, English, Greek, Swedish and Russian. The latter is especially pleasing.





For business

In addition to the above, Sandy Beach Hotel 4 * also has a separate business center, which makes this hotel ideal for conferences and other business events. It has three rooms equipped with all necessary audiovisual equipment and high-speed Internet. Here is a summary of these facilities that may be helpful:

  • Hall of Arts. Area - 210 square meters. m. Maximum capacity - 120 people in a theater setting.
  • Hall Mezzo. Area - 67 square meters. m. Maximum capacity - 50 people.
  • Hall Blu. Area - 38 square meters. m. Maximum capacity - 40 people.

In order to arrange a room rental and to organize any event in it, you must contact the hotel in advance.

Cyprus (Larnaca) is the place where many young people would like to play a wedding, and even so that it will be remembered for a lifetime. And it is real! The staff of the hotel has professional organizers who are able to arrange a truly fabulous ceremony. Here is a short list of what can be offered to future newlyweds for their celebration:

  • The venue is either a room or a beach.
  • Creation of an atmosphere, festive decoration.

In general, the service is at its best, and this is not even all possible for the provision of services, but only basic ones. But to find out more detailed information, as well as to calculate the cost of the service, you will need to contact the property directly.





Leisure

At the Sandy Beach Hotel 4 * (Cyprus, Larnaca), the vast majority of tourists come to enjoy a beach holiday. But you can diversify your leisure here without leaving the hotel.

On the territory there are four outdoor pools with fresh water, of which two are for children. There is also another indoor, heated. Lovers of body and soul will definitely enjoy a steam bath and a sauna.

Connoisseurs of an active lifestyle can visit the fitness center, rent equipment for water sports or bicycles, and go to the tennis court. All this is on the territory of the hotel complex. In the evening, entertainment shows are held here.

It is also worth noting that a mini club is organized for children at the hotel, where they definitely will not have to be bored.

SPA salon

It is worth telling about it separately. In the SPA-salon of Sandy Beach Hotel 4 * during the procedures, only high-quality cosmetics Elemis (England) and Phytomer (France) are used. These are the leading luxury brands that use only natural active ingredients in the manufacture of their products.

It should be noted that the Elemis brand managed to bring to the market such anti-aging products and professional SPA-therapy procedures that were not previously known to the beauty industry. Phytomer, in turn, is a recognized expert in the field of cosmetics based on marine products.

What about the ongoing procedures? Their list in the salon of Sandy Beach Hotel 4 * is very wide. Here are just some of the items from the SPA menu:

  • Elemis Body Indulgence Program. Includes deep anti-stress massage of the back and muscle tissue using essential oils.
  • Anti-cellulite program. It is aimed at eliminating the "orange peel" and the full contouring of the figure. Healing clays and exfoliating masks are used. The program also includes internal cleansing of the body through massage of the abdomen (promotes detoxification, stimulates blood circulation, removes excess fluid).
  • Stone therapy. Massage with warm basalt stones, acting on energy points, maximally relaxes and relieves even the most intense stress.

In addition, Indian head massage, algal wraps, collagen facelift, lymphatic drainage procedures, scrubbing and much more are practiced here. Naturally, in the SPA center there is also a beauty salon where you can sign up for manicure, pedicure (including hardware), haircut, styling, etc.





Bars and Restaurants

Now - a few words about the institutions available on the territory of Sandy Beach Hotel 4 * (Cyprus, Larnaca). There are five of them:

  • Elia The main restaurant specializes in international cuisine and overlooks the Mediterranean Sea.
  • Aqua. Bar and restaurant overlooking the beach, located near the pool. It serves breakfast, light afternoon snacks and refreshing drinks.
  • Nissaki. A cozy little tavern located in the fresh air. Specialization - traditional Cypriot dishes.
  • Meltemi. Relaxing lounge bar. Guests are offered a wide assortment of coffee, cocktails, strong and soft drinks, which they can enjoy either indoors or on the terrace.
  • Bimini The beach bar is an ideal place for evening relaxation. Here you can admire the Mediterranean breeze, order delicious delicacies and unusual exotic cocktails, as well as relax on comfortable wide sun loungers.

It is important to mention that restaurants have a dress code.





Accommodation options: standard apartments

Sentido Sandy Beach Hotel 4 * (Cyprus) has 195 rooms. All of them, as in any other hotel, are divided into categories. The most budget option is the standard number. Its area is 24 square meters. m. It is designed for two adults.

What is in this issue? Two large comfortable beds, a bathroom with a bathtub, a shower and a toilet, a private balcony, a climate system (cooling + heating), a widescreen plasma TV with satellite channels, a safe, a minibar and Wi-Fi.

The cost of a weekly stay in such apartments for two people will be approximately 40,000 rubles. The price includes 9 percent VAT and breakfast. If in this "standard" one guest wants to stay, then for him the cost will be approximately 30,000 rubles. However, often in this hotel there are good discounts, especially in the low season (that is, not in the summer). And if you plan a vacation for the fall-winter, you will save a considerable amount.
