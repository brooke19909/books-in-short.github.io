Crochet and knitting for a newborn (with description)

All mothers want their children, regardless of gender and age, to be always beautiful, elegant and bright. Dreaming of something to tie the baby? Then you read that article. Since in it we will talk about how to make cute little things for babies, which only recently were born. Of course, in most cases, the main clothes for them are crawlers and vests, but nobody canceled hats and booties? These elements of the wardrobe of the baby, made by caring and loving hands, are unique and inimitable.



Knitting for a newborn

Crochet and knitting can create a real masterpiece: booties, overalls, and an openwork cap, a dress, and even a chic plaid. A child who was born a couple of weeks ago needs warmth and comfort. Therefore, children's clothing should be made of high-quality and natural materials: cotton, linen, etc. Not only knitted, but also knitted items are very popular. Moreover, both winter models and summer ones. So, today we will knit baby booties and an openwork cap. We hope the process will give you a lot of pleasure.

Knitting for newborns (with description)

To knit booties, you will need: acrylic yarn, pin and knitting needles No. 3. We begin the process of work, based on the following description. We start by tying the feet. We collect forty loops and knit the front surface. In every third row we make an addition. As a result, you should get 56 loops. Next, we knit three rows on the wrong side. Then we begin to reduce the loop (one at a time). We knit eight rows of facial stitch. We close the sole.

Now make out the front. We knit ten rows of facial. Next we outline the sock. To do this, remove the first 26 and last 26 loops on a pin, and leave the middle part on the spoke. Then we knit three rows of facial loops. Repeat the pattern. In each row, we knit the last loops from one removed from the pin. We do so 24 rows. As a result, forty loops should remain on the spoke. We do the upper part like this: we collect three rows of smoothness, then with facial loops. We repeat. We knit the final row with facial loops and close each of them. You can decorate booties with flowers, pompoms or ribbons. As you can see, nothing complicated, is it?

Crocheting for a newborn with a crochet will also not cause you any special problems. If you are just learning, we advise you to start with simple models. And the pattern for the canvas is better to choose also simple. And then it can be decorated with delicate elements - all kinds of flowers and leaves. Options for patterns and patterns can be found on various information resources on the topic of "Crochet". For newborn girls, you can find wonderful models of bonnets or panamos.

Knit cap

Before you begin, you need to buy a suitable yarn. We recommend acrylic (white and pink colors) - it is soft and lays well. Still need: hook number 1, multi-colored beads and satin ribbon for decoration. So, we start knitting for a newborn. We crochet a chain of ten air loops. We connect it into a ring. After tying with double crochet.

Next, we collect three air loops and three double crochets. In this way we alternate the pattern to the end. From the seventh row, we increase the air loops to five.
