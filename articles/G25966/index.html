Hotel Irene Palace 4 *, Rhodes - photos, prices and reviews

Holidays on the island of Rhodes, owned by Greece, every year becomes more and more popular among domestic tourists. This is not surprising, because this paradise has long been famous for its luxurious beaches, a developed hotel base, offering both economy and luxury accommodation, as well as plenty of entertainment for every taste and the presence of secluded and quiet places for a relaxing pastime. So, if for a vacation you are considering the island of Rhodes and looking for a 4 * hotel, Irene Palace (Kolymbia) will be a great option. We suggest that you take a closer look at this hotel complex, as well as find out its pros and cons, based on the reviews of our compatriots who have already stayed here.





Where is the Irene Palace 4 * hotel located?

This hotel complex is located in the small resort village of the Greek island of Rhodes - Kolymbia. From the nearest luxurious beach, awarded the honorary blue flag for environmental cleanliness, it is only a hundred meters away. The island‚Äôs main city, Rhodes, and its seaport are 25 kilometers away. The international air harbor is 30 kilometers from the Irene Palace hotel 4 *. Therefore, the road from the airport to the hotel will take you no more than half an hour. It is very convenient, especially if you go on vacation with young children. Just six kilometers from the hotel complex is the village of Afantou, as well as an international golf center, where fans of this game can have fun. As for the nearest bus stop, it is only a couple of hundred meters away from the hotel.

This magnificent hotel complex was built back in 1987. However, thanks to regular work to update the building, it looks quite modern. The hotel consists of one four-story building, equipped with an elevator and accommodating 247 comfortable rooms of standard and family type. It offers guests restaurants and bars, an adult and a children's pool, a mini zoo, as well as a variety of entertainment and sports infrastructure. Hotel Policies

As in most hotels around the world, check-in at Irene Palace 4 * begins after 14:00. However, in the event that vacationers arrive at the hotel earlier than this time, the administration tries to do everything possible to accommodate them immediately upon arrival. Unfortunately, in the midst of the tourist season, when the hotel is 100% full, this is not always possible, since it is necessary to wait until the previous guests have vacated the room and the maids will properly prepare it. In such cases, travelers are offered to leave things in the luggage storage room and go for lunch, take a stroll through the beautiful hotel grounds, take a dip in the pool or go straight to the beach. As for the day of departure, you should release your room before noon. Then it is necessary to make payment. You can pay both in cash and with credit cards of the VISA, MasterCard, Diners Club and American Express systems. If you have already pre-paid your tour operator the full cost of the permit, then upon departure you will only have to pay for additional services, if you have used them.





Greece, Hotel Irene Palace 4 *: Accommodation with children and pets

Since the four-star hotel complex "Irene Palace" (Rhodes island) positions itself as a family institution, tourists with children of all ages are allowed to stay here. At the same time in the rooms there is the possibility of installing an extra or cot. You must inform the hotel in advance about the need to provide this service. Please note that in some cases this service is only possible at an additional cost.

As you know, some tourists can not imagine a trip to rest without their four-pet. If you are one of them, then the following information will be important to you. So, the Irene Palace 4 * hotel accommodates guests with pets on its territory, whose weight does not exceed five kilograms. Please note that your fourth pet will need to be paid.





Number of rooms

As already mentioned, the four-star Irene Palace Hotel, located in the picturesque village of Kolymbia (Rhodes Island), has 247 rooms. All of them are comfortable and equipped with everything necessary for living. It is possible to combine numbers. Each room is equipped with a bathroom with shower / tub and hairdryer, air conditioning (use from June 1 to September 15 is free, and at other times - for a fee), minibar, safe (paid), TV with satellite channels, telephone and radio. Carpeted floors. Wireless internet access is available for a fee. Each room has its own furnished balcony with wonderful sea or garden views. Maids clean rooms daily and change bed linen and towels three times a week. Also, guests of the Irene Palace hotel 4 * around the clock have the opportunity to order a room service.

Nutrition

Meals at the four-star hotel complex "Irene Palace" are carried out according to the popular "all inclusive" system. Three times a day, in the main restaurant of the hotel you can taste delicious dishes, snacks and desserts of both Greek and international cuisine, prepared by skilled chefs using fresh local products. It also offers a large selection of fruits. In addition, be sure to try the local wines.

On the territory of the Irene Palace 4 * hotel there are bars in the lobby, by the pool, as well as a snack bar. In the first two you can order your favorite soft and alcoholic drinks. The snack bar invites hotel guests to refresh themselves with a variety of snacks in between main meals.





Sea beach

Despite the fact that the four-star Irene Palace Hotel (Rhodes Island, Greece) is located on the first line, it does not have its own beach. The distance to the nearest municipal beach is only a hundred meters. The beach here is sandy and pebble, very clean and well maintained, with a gentle and comfortable entry into the sea. In addition, he was awarded the honorary Blue Flag, awarded only to the ecologically clean beaches of Europe. Entrance here is free, but you will have to pay for the use of sunbeds and umbrellas. Near the beach there are lots of cafes and bars. Also there is everything you need for entertainment and sports on the water.

Pool

The hotel complex Irene Palace 4 * (Rhodes) has on its territory a large freshwater pool, which is subjected to daily cleaning. Near it there is a sun terrace with enough sun loungers and parasols. There is also a bar where you can always enjoy a variety of drinks. The youngest tourists are not deprived of attention. So, for them the hotel has a children's pool.





Entertainment

Hotel Irene Palace (Rhodes) is pleased to offer its guests a wide variety of entertainment. So, throughout the day and until late at night on the territory of the hotel complex, fervent animators work that will do everything possible to make your vacation interesting and fun. They will offer you to play a variety of games, do aerobics, and in the evening will arrange a vivid show with lots of funny contests.

If you prefer outdoor activities, then the Irene Palace Hotel has at its disposal basketball and volleyball courts, a soccer field, tennis courts, and a mini-golf course. In addition, you can play chess, darts or billiards, visit the gym or aerobics class. The beach has everything you need for water sports.

If you want to relax, then visit the sauna or massage.

For children

Hotel Irene Palace 4 * takes care of its youngest guests. So, during the day there are children's clubs designed for children of different age groups. In the evenings, a mini-disco is held on site, where the little one is happy to dance to funny songs. In addition, there is a children's playground, a mini zoo.

Infrastructure

The hotel complex Irene Palace 4 * (Rhodes) has all the necessary infrastructure for a comfortable holiday for tourists. So, the reception is open 24 hours a day. Therefore, in case of late check-in or urgent issues, you always have the opportunity to contact the administrator at the reception, who will settle everything.

In addition, you can use the laundry, dry cleaning, babysitting, order room service. The hotel also has a shop where you can buy both souvenirs and things needed on vacation. Turning to the reception, you can order a taxi, book a tour of interest to you. The Irene Palace Hotel also provides an airport shuttle service. This service is provided at an additional cost. If you need a transfer, inform the hotel in advance.

Cost of living at the four-star Irene Palace Hotel (Rhodes Island, Greece)

The prices in this hotel are quite consistent with its star rating. So, accommodation in a double room during the peak season (July - August) will cost you in the amount of 130 euros per day. In other months, the price of accommodation will be much lower.





Hotel complex Irene Palace (Rhodes): reviews of tourists
