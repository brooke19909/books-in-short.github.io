How to make pompons

How to make a snowman costume, you already know. Consider how to make pompons from the yarn on the front of the outfit. You will need cardboard, compasses, scissors, a needle with a wide eye, black yarn, nylon threads. Two identical circles of the same size as the future pompom are cut out on cardboard. Inside, using a compass, a smaller circle is drawn, which is then carefully cut with scissors. Two rings with a width of 2 cm are obtained. Then, a loop is tied with a thread through the inner hole of the folded rings and a knot is tied.

Then the edge of the yarn is threaded into the needle with a wide eye and stitched around the ring until the needle can no longer squeeze into the inner hole. Spread the threads around the entire ring evenly. Then the sharp edge of the scissors is pushed into the hole between the threads along the outer edge and cut in a circle. Strong kapron threads tie a strong knot around the inner ring. Only then can you cut the cardboard rings and pull them out of the pompom. The edges of the threads are not always smooth, after removing the warp, they can be cut to one length with scissors around the circumference of the ball.

Paper Cylinder Hat

Almost all the boys in the snowman outfit have a hat in the shape of a cylinder on their heads. Let's see how it can be done. A rectangle is cut from a sheet of whatman paper, the height of which corresponds to the height of the hat, and the width to the circumference of the head. In order not to make a mistake with the size, you can wrap a strip of paper around your head and grab it with pins in the right places. Excess paper is cut off, and the pipe itself is fastened on the sides with paper clips, PVA is glued together or sewn together with threads (optional). Remember to leave 1 cm of paper in stock!

Next, cut a circle for the top of the hat. To do this, it is enough to circle the manufactured pipe. When cutting this part out of paper, you need to leave 2 cm around the allowance, which will need to be cut into corners. They are subsequently smeared with PVA glue and attached to the cylinder.

It remains to arrange the field hat. To do this, the workpiece is turned over and the upper circle of the hat is encircled with a pencil. Then another circle is drawn around it with a compass, 3-4 cm larger than the previous one. Here, when cutting the fields, you need to cut the corners for attachment already on the inside of the ring. All fastenings are made from the inside so that there are no visible places for bonding allowances.

The last stage of the work is painting the craft black with gouache paints. You can then open everything with acrylic varnish so that the paint on the hat does not stain the child‚Äôs hands.
