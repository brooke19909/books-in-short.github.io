DIY Easter card, or How to give a holiday

Only a few days remain before Easter, revered by all Orthodox. Products for Easter cakes and curd Easter have already been bought , eggs and paints are waiting in the wings. However, the issue of gifts to friends and relatives has not yet been resolved. In Russia, it is not customary to make expensive gifts for this holiday, but you still want to please your loved ones and once again make them smile. And the Easter card will help you with this!

Easter card. What are they like?



This kind of greeting card came to us from the West. And without a doubt, the habit of giving cards quickly took root and blossomed in a riotous color. Moreover, postcard options are constantly updated. If about ten years ago the country experienced a boom of cool postcards in paper form, now it‚Äôs the era of interactive analogues.

You can select various postcards on Easter on specialized sites and send them to a friend. The Easter greeting card that arrived by e-mail will cheer you up and remind you of the upcoming holiday. In addition, it can be set as a background on the desktop, and it will delight for a long time the realization that someone remembers you! Interactive postcards come with a recorded voice greeting or just a beautiful picture. Or it could be a DIY handmade greeting card .

Easter card. Manufacturing principle

So, you still decided to make a card yourself. We hope that our tips will help you to get started first. And then it all depends on you and the flight of fantasy!



  • Take a cardboard that suits us in density. Someone likes it very tight, and someone so that the card is slightly bent. Any suitable old postcard can be used as a template.
  • Cut the desired number of pieces. Bend them in half. If plain cardboard seems too boring to you, stick colored paper on top of it, and then fold it in half.
  • Prepare the applique. If you are not distinguished by clearly expressed abilities for painting, you can cut out relevant drawings from a magazine or a brochure. And then circle them and cut blanks of colored paper. You can take a smooth or velor. You can also make applications of unnecessary scraps of fabric, which should be pre-washed and ironed.
  • We paste them on the blank of the card, add a congratulatory or funny inscription, decorate with beads or ribbons if necessary.
  • That's all, our Easter card is ready!

Greeting card for Easter. Who can do it?



There are lots of options to make such a card! Of course, this process will turn out to be the most enjoyable and exciting if younger members of the family take part in it. Nothing gives children such pleasure as messing with colored paper, scissors, glue and shreds with adults. After all, parents often simply do not have enough time to do needlework or crafts with their children. Therefore, if you manage to combine the manufacture of gifts and communication with the child, it will be just wonderful! Do not worry if the cards made by children are not as perfect in execution as your own. The main thing is that they were made with a soul! And relatives and friends will definitely appreciate it.
