Unique resort

More recently, the main stream of tourists consisted of the Dutch and English - true connoisseurs of quality relaxation. And they made the choice in favor of Marmaris thanks to many unconditional advantages:

  • The lack of waves. The resort is located in a closed bay. Two seas are connected here: the Aegean and the Mediterranean.
  • Water "holds" well, so Marmaris is the best place for children and people who swim poorly.
  • The perfect climate. The city is closed from the winds by the mountains, storms almost never happen, even in the most inclement weather. The air temperature is slightly lower than on the rest of the coast, and in the hot summer months there is no exhausting heat.




  • Clean beaches, which are marked by the Blue Flag - an international award awarded every year for coastal areas and moorings, whose water meets high quality standards and is safe for swimming.
  • Unique nature. The city is literally buried in incense trees and pines. Forests cover 3/4 of the total area (65 of 86 thousand ha).

And, of course, it is worth mentioning that Marmaris is the most dancing city in Turkey, called Turkish Ibiza. And Bar Street - the street of restaurants, bars, discos with incendiary music - is known throughout the world. The admiration for the guests is caused by the 12-kilometer promenade with one of the largest marinas in the world.

Introductory Information

The Adler Hotel 3 (Marmaris) is glad to welcome guests of the resort. Standard - a class of rooms corresponding to the level of this hotel complex. The 4-storey building built in 1993 provides 73 rooms: 2 and 3-bed rooms. All have balconies with stunning views.

  • safe (for a fee);
  • air conditioning;
  • telephone;
  • hair dryer;
  • TV.

From furniture: bed, wardrobe, mirror, nightstand. The toilet and shower are combined. On the balcony there is a table, chairs. An iron can be requested at the reception if necessary. Linen is changed 3 times a week, room cleaning - every day.

Entertainment at the hotel

Guests can have fun playing table tennis and billiards (for a fee). That, in fact, is all. There are no discos and animations here. In the evenings, vacationers gather at the poolside bar. The place is more intended for a relaxing pastime.





A portion of positive emotions and fun can be obtained literally 10 minutes from the hotel. There, on the embankment, nightlife is in full swing. A lot of cafes, a restaurant on the pier (the only one that goes into the sea), live music, city lights and a romantic mood will leave in your memory the best memories of your vacation.

Icmeler

The closest settlement to Adler Hotel 3 in Marmaris (Turkey) is the village of Icmeler, one of the main resort centers 3 km away. The popularity of this place among tourists is due to the wide sandy beaches and thermal springs. For divers, Icmeler is a real paradise with clean, calm waters, a seamount with caves and a mass of any marine fauna: octopuses, turtles, lobsters and other animals. In general, in the vicinity of Marmaris and the city itself there are more than 50 dive clubs. The most popular diving sites are Jennet Island, Karga Island, Kadyrga Lighthouse, Cape Kutyuk, Aksu Bay.

Beach

Adler Hotel 3 in Marmaris is located on the second line from the sea (70 meters), walking to the city beach (there is none), 3 minutes. The coastal strip is not wide, the entrance to the sea is gentle. There are changing cabins and toilets on the beach.





Towels are desirable to take with you. Umbrellas, mattresses, chaise lounges are provided for a fee. If you want to visit the private beach of another hotel for a person who is not his guest, you just need to buy a drink in the bar. This will be a kind of entrance fee.

The opening of the swimming season falls in June. The water temperature at this time is +23 ¬∞ C and above. The most attractive time to relax when the temperature of the air and water practically coincide is 27-28 of heat. It is hot in summer, but vacationers do not feel the sun because of the unique geographical location of the resort. The coolness and freshness in the evenings brings the sea breeze.

Nutrition

The chef is constantly in the hall, controls the process, helps his employees, communicates with guests.

The courtesy of the restaurant administrator, his helpfulness, willingness to help and advise were noted in many reviews of hotel guests. Waiters constantly clean dirty dishes from tables, and if they are unemployed, they help residents bring drinks.

How to get to Adler Hotel 3 (Marmaris, Turkey)

Marmaris city center is 4 km away, and Dalaman Airport is 100 km away. The Turkish Airlines company provides flights from Moscow to Dalaman with a transfer in Istanbul. Estimated cost in both directions - $ 190. From St. Petersburg, traveling with the same transplant in the capital of Turkey will cost 1.5 times more.

Aeroflot and UTair companies organize flights to Istanbul from different cities of Russia (Moscow, St. Petersburg, Yekaterinburg, Astrakhan, Sochi, etc.). From there, you have to get to Marmaris on your own (by bus or domestic flight). In the season (April-November), direct charters are launched from both Russian capitals to Dalaman. Marmaris can be reached from the airport by bus. Travel time is approximately 1.5 hours, the fare is approximately $ 6. Taxi is also a good option, however, it will cost ten times more expensive.





You can get to Marmaris by bus from different parts of Turkey: Bodrum (3.5 hours on the way), Izmir (4 hours 15 minutes), Istanbul (13 hours), Antalya (6 hours), Fethiye (3 hours). The best services are characterized by the bus companies Metro and Pamukkale. Adler Hotel 3 in Marmaris provides an airport transfer service.

Daily from April to November, 2-4 times a day, ferry departs from Greek Rhodes to Marmaris. Travel time - 1 hour, cost - about 150 TRY.

Transport

Of course, the main purpose of the arrival of many vacationers is a vacation by the sea. And here it is magical, affectionate, blue, calm, has nothing to do. You can diversify such passive leisure by shopping, getting to know the sights of the resort, and entertainment events. True, all this is at some distance from Adler Hotel 3. In Marmaris, minibuses constantly run from the hotel. It is most advantageous to travel by dolmushi (such an economy version of fixed-route taxis). Cruising time: from 7:15 to 1:30. Icmeler's dolmushi are marked with an orange stripe, Marmaris with a green stripe. On the most popular routes, such cars ply with a break of 5-10 minutes. The fare is 2 TRY, payment - to the driver.

No less popular means of transportation is a taxi. Cars are painted yellow, with checkers on the roof, equipped with counters. They have already set a fixed starting price, which grows with each kilometer traveled. Some taxi drivers can ride for a fixed fee. So sometimes even cheaper. On average, a trip around the city costs 10-25 TRY.

Along the coast taxi cabs ply. Their capacity is 20-25 people. They depart from small berths, stopping at almost every kilometer. Opening hours: from 9:00 to 21:00. The cost of a trip within the city is about 10 TRY.

What do vacationers do?

There are plenty of leisure options on Marmaris. Lovers of active pastimes will enjoy jeep safaris, paragliding, horseback riding, rafting on the Dalaman mountain river, aqua bike, windsurfing. Some may be lucky enough to witness the world famous international sailing regatta - Marmaris International Race Week. Races have been held since 1989, more than 150 yachts from all over the world take part in them.





For those who want to touch the past, feel the connection of times and feel the atmosphere of great events, excursions are organized to ancient places: the city of Ephesus, in which John the Evangelist preached, the temple of Artemis - one of the seven wonders of the world, miraculously preserved in the ruins of the temple of Troy of Athena. You should definitely go to the turtle island of Dalyan, as well as visit Fethiye - a village built on the site of Telmessos - one of the main cities of ancient Lycia.

One cannot fail to mention one of the most amazing places on the planet - the Turkish Pamukkale, whose white terraces with flowing streams of water from afar look like huge flakes of cotton wool and icebergs. Perhaps that is why in Russian the name of the natural object sounds like Cotton Castle. The mineral waters of Pamukkale are healing, so the thermal resort here has been since ancient Rome. In natural pools filled with mineral water, they successfully treat diseases of the heart and musculoskeletal system. This is the eighth wonder of the world, which tourists from all over the world come to see.
