Country Club "Pirate Harbor", Nizhny Novgorod Region: description and reviews

"Pirate Harbor" is an interesting thematic recreation center located in one of the most beautiful areas of the Nizhny Novgorod region. The administration of the institution offers to celebrate a birthday and New Year here, to have a picnic or a private party with friends, a meeting with business partners in an informal setting. Comfortable accommodation conditions, high level of service, various services and only nature around - this vacation will be unforgettable!





"Pirate Harbor": a description of the complex and its location

The institution is located in the village of Afanasyevo, Bogorodsky district, Nizhny Novgorod region, at the address: st. Povarazhnaya 8. It is a country club on the territory of which there are several cottages for rent to guests. Each house is landscaped and detached, so there is no need to worry about privacy.

Cozy cottages are made in the style of a pirate fort and fortress. It has everything you need, and also provides additional services that will make your vacation even more diverse.

The complex has 4 cottages. Total capacity - more than 26 people. The country club operates year-round; in any season, vacationers will find interesting activities that will make spending time as relaxing and comfortable as possible. The minimum cost of living is 3000 rubles per day per person.

Infrastructure and entertainment

The territory of the country club "Pirate Harbor" in the Bogorodsky district has its own parking and a children's town with slides, sandpits and other entertainments. There is also a bike rental service, in addition, there is the opportunity to take a horse ride - ideal for outdoor enthusiasts. In winter, sledding and skiing are organized. Nearby is a pine-deciduous forest, so guests can pick berries and mushrooms there.

Additional services for guests

The country club provides VIP service and honeymoon suites. It is possible to order food and drink delivery from the restaurant.

Guests of the "Pirate Harbor" can cook their own products brought with them (in the kitchen or on the grill), order dishes for breakfast, lunch and dinner (comprehensive service in the restaurant). A dietary (special) menu is available on request.

The restaurant hosts banquets, receptions, weddings and corporate parties. Such events are fully accompanied by restaurant employees, ranging from cooks to cleaners. The menu is varied, guests are offered several options, among which you can choose the most suitable for a particular event. Among them are dishes cooked at the stake.





Reviews on "Pirate Harbor" in the Nizhny Novgorod Region

The country club was opened relatively recently, it is a modern institution with the appropriate design. There are still few reviews about the recreation center, but they are positive. In their comments, guests of Pirate Harbor note a high level of service - the administration and other staff fulfill all requests almost instantly. In general, friendly and helpful people work here.

The level of banquet service is also at the highest level. The restaurant takes into account all the wishes of the guests, starting from the menu and ending with additional services. Enough space to sing, dance and organize competitions.

The cottages are clean, comfortable and cozy, maintenance staff timely clean up the area. In winter, the houses are warm, and in the summer heat you can find the desired coolness here. The layout is also comfortable. It is important that there is a private bathhouse, billiards and karaoke, so you do not need to go outside your home.

For the convenience of customers there is an office of Pirate Harbor in Nizhny Novgorod (9 Dyakova St.). Visitors can get advice on all important issues and discuss the conditions for banquets.
