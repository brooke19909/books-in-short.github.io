Russia, Anapa, Vityazevo: rest. Vityazevo: attractions and reviews

Our article will be useful to those who are going to spend their summer holidays by the sea. Vityazevo is a wonderful resort town located near Anapa. Tourists are attracted here by the clear sea, good beaches and plenty of entertainment. You can read more about rest in Vityazevo in our article.



How to get there

The most convenient and fastest way is, of course, an airplane. Vityazevo Airport accepts flights from 47 cities of Russia and is the base for five airlines. To get to the village, you will need to order a taxi or stop the private owner. The fare varies from 400 to 600 rubles. However, most guest houses organize a free shuttle service for their spa guests.

A train ride is still the most popular way of traveling among Russians. If you decide to come to rest in Vityazevo with this type of transport, then you should keep heading for Anapa. Typically, tourists land at the Tunnel station, which is located five kilometers from the village. The fare to the final destination in an official taxi will cost about 900 rubles. Anyone who wants to save money can drive 120 rubles to the Anapa bus station, and then transfer to one of the many minibuses.





Where to stay

As in any coastal village, everything here is for rent - from small rooms to large private houses. However, if you decide to relax in comfort, take care of early booking and do not forget that hotels with children's infrastructure are selling rooms instantly. Perhaps you want to settle in one of the modern hotels in the village of Vityazevo? Rest on the seashore in comfortable conditions will cost 2500 rubles per night. The territory of such resort hotels often has one or two small pools, a children's playground, a dining room and several residential buildings. Often these hotels have at their disposal small clean beaches. Perhaps the only negative of this type of housing is a small area. Therefore, the hotels have a limited number of parking spaces, few green spaces and places for a relaxing holiday. Let's consider more accommodation options that Vityazevo can offer tourists.



Relaxation. Private sector. Prices

As a rule, the owners of their own houses erect several guest buildings around the main building, arrange a summer kitchen and decorate the dining area with ivy and grapes. Small rooms without amenities cost about 400 rubles per night, and more comfortable options (with shower, refrigerator and air conditioning) from 800 rubles. If you want to save a little, then try to organize a vacation (Vityazevo) without intermediaries. There are two ways to do this. The first is quite risky - to come to the village without prior agreement and look for housing on the spot. The second is to contact the owners of private houses via the Internet in advance and discuss the conditions. Of course, we recommend choosing the second way, since it is very important to know all the conditions in advance.



Holidays in Vityazevo by the sea

We want to offer you another not too expensive accommodation option. Many local businesses offer everyone to spend their holidays in Vityazevo. Guest houses and recreation centers left over from the Soviet era were previously designed exclusively for workers in industrial plants and factories. Currently, they have hospitably opened their doors to holidaymakers and delight non-spoiled Russians with their low prices. It may seem to you that the buildings are quite old, and the furniture blows the spirit of the mid-20th century? Then add to this the high service, your own clean beaches and three meals a day. Perhaps, after weighing the pros and cons, you will stop at this option and not regret it. Treatment

Many tourists seek to combine rest in Vityazevo with joint treatment and spa treatments. Those who have not purchased a ticket to one of the local sanatoriums and decided not to spend money on a beauty salon, go to the Vityazevsky estuary - a source of therapeutic hydrogen sulfide mud. Here, citizens apply a healing slurry to the body, and after a while they wash it off. Some even collect dirt in jars so that they can treat joints, skin diseases and a number of other ailments at home. Another popular source of health is mud volcanoes on the Taman Peninsula. Local kitchen

It is very easy to arrange meals in the village. Perhaps you will like the local cafes, the average cost of a lunch in which is 200 rubles, and dinner with drinks - 300. Traditionally, merchants carry corn and churchkhela on the beaches, and along the promenade are trays with a variety of different foods. Therefore, finding a snack in the village is not a problem. Restaurants offer vacationers a wide variety of cuisines - here you can find Greek cafes, chain pizzerias, barbecue, pubs with an impressive beer list, and Japanese food restaurants. If you are afraid of catching an intestinal infection or simply want to save money, you can cook your own food yourself or entrust this responsibility to the hostess of your hotel.

Reviews

Tourists who have been here are happy to recall a summer vacation. Vityazevo is a wonderful place where you can have a fun vacation, undergo a course of treatment or spa treatments, as well as explore local attractions. Vacationers say that the best thing is to come to the village in June or early September. During these months, the sea is quite warm, housing prices and food are moderate. Another important plus is a small influx of holidaymakers. This means that there is always a place on a small local beach and children can frolic at their pleasure.
