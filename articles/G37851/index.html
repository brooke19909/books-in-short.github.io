Where is the hotel located

Dessole Mirabello Beach Village Resort is located in the east of the Greek island of Crete, about two kilometers from the center of Agios Nikolaos, where you can walk on foot in 20 minutes or take a shuttle bus (ticket price is about 2 euros). The nearest international airport of Heraklion is a little more than 60 km from the hotel. You can enjoy swimming in the Aegean Sea and soak up one of the island's most beautiful beaches.

Dessole Mirabello Beach Village offers 311 luxury guestrooms. The following categories of apartments are available to you:

  • Two-room bungalows, thanks to extra beds, can accommodate up to 6 guests.
