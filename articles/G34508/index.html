Beginner Tips

Very beautiful crocheted napkins with patterns can be found in magazines on store shelves. On them you can do the same things with your own hands. The main thing is to understand the signs, but it is not difficult. There are a few basic rules to crochet very beautiful and simple napkins with magazine schemes.

  1. It is better to take a hook for knitting 2 mm thick.
  2. The napkin always fits in from the center. Loops are drawn onto the hook and enclosed in a ring.
  3. For beginners, a thread with a size of 10 will be optimal.
  4. At the end of the work, do not tear off the thread, but cut off with scissors leaving a centimeter to fasten the thread, if it is less, it will not be possible to fix it.

Napkins of various shapes

Crochet you can knit not only small round napkins. The shapes and sizes of a lot, the main thing is to find a suitable scheme. Today it is not difficult to find the necessary scheme and explanation for it. Oval napkins often fit in different patterns and geometric ornaments or more familiar floral themes. Fillet knitting is often used for such napkins. A beautiful oval crochet napkin with a scheme from the Internet can be connected as follows.

  1. We start to work from the center in the same way as with round napkins. We collect the required number of loops, we close in a ring. Next, we knit the columns with and without crochets, alternating. It is with such a viscous napkin that will look openwork.
  2. Each row should be increased by the same number of loops. If you make a different amount, the napkin will look askance and messy.
  3. In order for the napkin not to be very dense, you need to add openwork holes. This can be done alternating with double crochet and without. Just do not get carried away, there should not be many holes.
  4. When the napkin becomes the right size, it is worth fixing the thread.
