Holidays in Turkey with children: reviews and features



The endless blue sea, magnificent sandy beaches, numerous attractions, cozy cafes, original dishes - all this is Turkey. The best vacation with children for many Russian parents is associated with Turkish resorts. After all, there are all conditions for a pleasant and carefree pastime for both adults and children. Moms and dads do not need to constantly think about what to occupy or entertain their child at those moments when splashing in the sea and building castles out of sand is already tired of him, as animators take care of this.

All parents who at least once spent their holidays in Turkey with their children leave only positive reviews about the resorts of this country. And this is not at all surprising! After all, hotel rooms are equipped with a comfortable crib, and the restaurant provides an armchair for babies and a separate menu for the most tiny tourists. All family hotels in Turkey have children's clubs and animation, where young tourists can have plenty of fun with their peers, and this factor, you see, is of great importance for them.





Therefore, choosing a hotel, be sure to take an interest in the presence of interesting activities for each member of the family, because only in this case you will provide yourself with an excellent vacation in Turkey with children. The reviews left by tourists who once vacationed in this country as a whole family are very useful for those who are just going to spend their holidays there. Therefore, if you belong to the latter category of travelers, do not neglect such valuable information. Most often, tourists in their reviews mention hotels such as LONICERA WORLD 4 * in Incekum, LYRA RESORT HOTEL 5 * near Manavgat, CALIMERA KAYA SIDE 5 * in Side, and ELA QUALITY 5 * in Belek. Tourists speak with enthusiasm about these hotels, because there are playgrounds, mini-clubs, as well as pools with slides, and you can rely on the opinion of such people, because they have already spent their excellent holidays in Turkey with children. Reviews of experienced tourists, consisting of several lines, often carry more truthful information than multi-page texts on the websites of travel agencies, which are primarily interested in selling their tour.





It is important to note one more point. Finding a suitable tour for the whole family should be long before the trip, of course, if you want to spend a pleasant holiday in Turkey with children. Reviews of families visiting this country often remind that the cost of living for children in all hotels is different. Some hotels offer significant discounts for the youngest tourists, while others offer free accommodation for 4-6 year olds and maximum discounts for children under 13 years old.

But despite the fact that Turkish hotels offer excellent accommodation and excellent leisure facilities, you should definitely visit at least a few attractions that Turkey is rich in. Holidays with children, reviews of which you may wish to leave at a travel agency, will be remembered by each member of your family for many years.
