Yaroslavl, hotels in the city center: addresses, description and reviews

Our article will be useful to those who plan to visit Yaroslavl in the near future. Hotels in the city center are very different: both cheap and luxurious. To have an idea about them, we will consider some of them in more detail.

Hotel "Bear Corner"

The hotel has the status of a three-star hotel. It is located in the cultural and historical center of the city. Many travelers are attracted to Yaroslavl. Hotels in the city center are very convenient for visitors, since everything is nearby and you do not need to go anywhere. Excessive travel makes life difficult, especially when you don‚Äôt know the city.



The hotel was built in 1985, was reconstructed in 2002, and the last renovation was made in 2004. The building of the hotel "Bear Corner" is located in a green area, away from noise, at the address: Sverdlova street, 16.

The hotel has 48 rooms from economy class to business class. The price of apartments is from 2250 rubles. Each room has a TV, hairdryer, shower. Internet can be used only in the reception area. For spoiled and demanding guests there are two-room rooms, VIP-apartments, consisting of three rooms and a bathroom.

Breakfast and lunch are available as an extra service. Parking is included in the price.

Guest reviews about the hotel "Bear Corner"

Based on good reviews from hotel guests, we can draw conclusions about the decent level of this institution. Settlement is very fast, the staff is attentive and friendly. According to some guests, standard rooms are small and too modest, but very clean. Cleaning is done every day. Towels and linen are new, clean, not washed. Decent food. In general, the most positive impression is emerging.

Like Hostel

Arriving in Yaroslavl, city guests are faced with a problem where it is best to settle. Everyone wants to get comfortable and pay an acceptable amount for housing. Then you can enjoy exploring the whole of Yaroslavl. Hotels in the city center have a rather high cost of living, and on the outskirts, few people want to settle. In this case, a hostel might be a good option.



In the center of Yaroslavl is the hostel Like. Its convenience consists in the availability of rooms of various types. Possible settlement in an eight-seater room, six-seater, four-seater. It all depends on the amount you are willing to pay for housing. The cheapest overnight stay will cost you 550 rubles. The hotel even has double family rooms. Their cost is from 1500 rubles per day.

For guests round-the-clock access to the Internet. The hostel is located in Yaroslavl, at the following address: Andropova street, 25v.

According to the reviews of visitors to the institution, we can conclude about a fairly good standard of living. True, many complain about a single shower and a bathroom, which are also combined. Therefore, queues periodically arise. Everything would be fine if not for the negative comments regarding the reserved places. People pre-book seats, and upon arrival, they discover that there are no seats. Apparently, the employees have some flaws in this regard. But in general, the hostel is an acceptable economizer.

"Kuptsov House"

Historical places make Yaroslavl very attractive for tourists. Hotels in the city center are the best option for accommodating city guests. A very worthy place is the Kuptsov Dom Hotel. It is located in the very center of the city. It is very easy to get to the Yaroslavl Kremlin from it. And the promenade is just a ten minute walk. The hotel is located at: Trefoleva street, 21.

The hotel has Wi-Fi. Guests can stay in the bright, new rooms with air conditioning, refrigerator, TV, desk and wardrobe. A buffet breakfast is served each morning. There are many cafes and restaurants near the hotel.



The cheapest room will cost you 2750 rubles. All apartments are very beautiful and decorated with paintings and prints with views of Yaroslavl.

According to guest reviews, the hotel is small and very comfortable. Excellent location is its plus. Friendly and well-mannered staff is always happy to help and suggest those historical places where visitors to the city should go. The food is very good. Rooms are cleaned every day. The hotel is quiet and calm.

"Seven Fridays"

Hotel "Seven Fridays" (Yaroslavl) is a completely new mini-hotel, which opened only in 2014. It is located in a new building, in the very center of the city. Near the hotel there is a shopping center "Aura", Yaroslavl circus, the Palace of Culture. Guests of the city will certainly be surprised at fairly reasonable prices and quality of service. Hotel address: 10a Komsomolskaya street.



The hotel has only ten rooms. The cost of the cheapest is 1900 rubles per day.

Reviews about this hotel are rather mixed. Guests complain about the loud music at night, which comes from a night club, about the discrepancy between the prices stated at the time of booking.

"Modern"

Hotel Modern is a small hotel in the city center. It is located in a historic building, built back in 1900. The building itself is a historical monument, which is included in the list of cultural heritage sites. The first floor of the hotel is a restaurant.

The hotel is located at: Pushkinskaya street, house 5. There are only fourteen rooms, which are divided into the following categories: standard, family, superior. All units are equipped with a TV and air conditioning. The cost of the standard is 2900 rubles per day. It includes breakfast. The hotel has a patio with tables, awnings and beautiful flower beds.



Reviews about this hotel are the most positive. Guests are satisfied with absolutely everything, from service to cleaning and picking a room. Friendly staff deserves special attention.

Hotel "Boulevard"

Mini-hotel "Boulevard" is located in the central part of the city. It occupies a separate two-story building with a cozy quiet courtyard. Hotel address: 12a Svobody street.

Rooms are single, double and triple apartments of different comforts. The cost of a room for one person is 1800 rubles, a double will cost 2500 rubles. Hotel guests note the convenience of its location, good friendly service, even when checking in and checking out at night. Judging by the reviews, the rooms are quite spacious and clean. They have everything you need for making coffee and tea. All this, of course, is trifles, but very pleasant, which sometimes create a good mood.

Hotel-estate "18th century"

The hotel has a recent history, it has been operating since 2011. The building is made in the style of Russian classicism of the eighteenth century. The hotel is located in the center of Yaroslavl, near the embankment of the Kotoroli River. The hotel is housed in the former Makiny-Kropinyh estate. The first mention of it dates back to 1783.



Hotel address: 49 Oktyabrskaya Street. The hotel has fourteen rooms that are completely different from each other. All of them are made in the style of classicism. You will have a unique opportunity to plunge into the era of the eighteenth century, but at the same time use the Internet and TV. Room rate - from 3000 rubles.

Based on guest reviews, you can judge quality service, good, clean rooms and delicious breakfasts.
