How to put money on PayPal in different ways

A PayPal account gives you the ability to securely transfer and receive money online. It can be used for almost all of your online financial transactions. Novice users often wonder: how to put money on PayPal? This process involves fairly simple actions and does not require much time. Anyone with an account can do this. However, keep in mind the restrictions placed on some countries.





How to put money on PayPal: instructions

Log in to your account with the email address associated with your account and enter the password. Click on the “Login” button. This will open the "My Account - Overview" page.

First way

How to put money on paypal? You can transfer from your bank account. You can easily add funds to your account using most commercial banks. All you need is a savings or other account. Click on the “Add Money” button, which you can find in the upper left corner of the page. Select the link “Add money from bank account”. If you have not previously added a financial institution to your PayPal account settings, do this by following the instructions. If you already have at least one account in the list, it will be shown in the “From” column.

Enter your bank account in your PayPal account. You must indicate his details in order to be able to deposit money. Click on the button “Add bank account”. You will need to indicate the name of the bank and the type of account (check or savings account). Next, enter the transit number and account number. When you're done, click on the “Continue” button. You will need to verify your account before you can use it. Click on “Profile”, “Bank Accounts” and then “Confirm”.

Continuing to follow the instructions on how to deposit money on PayPal, you must transfer funds from your bank account. To do this, select the entry you want to use. Enter the amount you want to transfer, and then click on the “Continue” button. You will see the balance on your PayPal account, including the money just transferred to it.

You can link up to eight different bank accounts to your PayPal account. When transferring money to your PayPal account from a current or savings account, it will take three to five business days to process the application.





Second way

Another way to put money on PayPal is to use a credit card. If you have a PayPal Plus card, you can instantly transfer money from it to your account. These are credit cards offered by PayPal, for which you need to officially confirm your account.

Third way

A more affordable method will be to transfer money from a VISA card. How to deposit with a PayPal account using it? To do this, click on the "Profile" link in your account. Select “Financial Information” and “Credit / Debit Card”, click “Add Card”. Enter the card information to be requested. PayPal will make a small deposit and withdraw funds from your card.





Go to your credit card statement (online or on paper) and write down the exact amounts for each transaction made by PayPal. After that, you need to log in to PayPal, select “My Account” and go to “Profile”. Go to the tab "Bank account" and select "Confirm." You will be asked to enter the amount that the system withdrew from your card to prove that the card belongs to you.

To add money to your PayPal account from the credit account balance, click on the "Replenish wallet" link. Select the link "Replenish wallet from bank account" and select the confirmed Visa Card as a source of financing. Enter the amount you want to transfer from your Visa card and click "Submit". Your money should be available within three to five days.

As you can see, the whole process of adding money to this payment system is not difficult.
