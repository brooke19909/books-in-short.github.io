Sun Palace 4 * (Sunny Beach, Bulgaria): description, service, reviews

In Soviet times, rest in Bulgaria could be equated with a trip to an exotic country. With the advent of the nineties, our compatriots opened the resorts of Turkey and Egypt, so the Russians forgot about the Bulgarian coast for a while. However, over time, interest in this tourist destination has grown markedly. This year, many of our compatriots decided to go overseas and the sun to Bulgaria. This has generated on the Internet a real wave of requests for resorts and hotels in this country. Most of all tourists were interested in hotel complexes with three or four stars. They can always count on a high level of service and excellent food. One of the most interesting offers in the market of travel services in this area is the San Palace Hotel. He receives quite good reviews from Russian tourists, so he is often offered in agencies to families and young couples. If you are planning a trip to Bulgaria, then you definitely should thoroughly study the information about Sun Palace 4 * (Sunny Beach). And we, in turn, will help you with this.





Short description of the hotel complex

Hotels in Bulgaria receive a lot of positive reviews, largely due to the good service and convenient location. Most of them were built in Soviet times, and later rebuilt in accordance with modern requirements.

Sun Palace 4 * (Bulgaria) is a two five-story building, the hotel also has a small area with a pool. Guests often write that the whole area looks very well-groomed, shrubs, flowers and trees are planted all around. Therefore, every minute you are in the hotel complex, you will experience joy.

Sun Palace 4 * (Sunny Beach) in the tourist catalogs is represented by the "four". However, during the check-in for tourists, the fact that one of the hotel buildings has four stars, but the other only three, comes as a surprise. Therefore, check this fact with your tour operator before booking a room.

Hotel Location

The resort, where the Sun Palace 4 * hotel complex is located, is Sunny Beach. This place is very popular not only among tourists, but also among local residents. They often come here for the weekend or for one week while on vacation.

Since the resort is very popular among travelers, the infrastructure here is very well developed. It is noteworthy that the hotel complex Sun Palace 4 * (Sunny Beach) is located right in the center of the resort, so when you leave it, tourists will find many restaurants and shops. And the city itself looks very beautiful, which makes every walk a very pleasant pastime.





Who is the hotel recommended for?

Tour operators often recommend this hotel complex to the following categories of vacationers:

  • couples;
  • families with children of any age;
  • youth companies.

It is best to go to hotels in Bulgaria from June to August. Usually travelers highly value accommodation and food, these nuances are indisputable advantages of the hotel.





Territory and infrastructure

Despite the fact that the hotel complex has a very modest territory, it boasts a large number of infrastructure facilities. Therefore, often guests write in their reviews that the hotel may well qualify for five stars.

There is one restaurant and three bars on the territory of San Palace. All of them operate on an all-inclusive basis, however guests can reserve a room without meals. This is allowed by the rules of the hotel complex

For most of the guests, the service Sun Palace 4 * (Sunny Beach) causes only positive emotions. The hotel staff delights guests with courtesy and ability to speak several languages. This greatly facilitates the solution of problems arising for some tourists.

Laundry, hairdressing and beauty services are also available. If necessary, a doctor is called into the guest room. He will provide first aid and prescribe further treatment.





Entertainment in the hotel complex

Excellent animators often work in Bulgaria, the hotel described by us is no exception to the general rule. Vacationers find themselves in a very comfortable atmosphere, where they are unobtrusively offered various entertainment during the day.

Free of charge guests can enjoy table tennis, aerobics classes and a gym, where you can go almost any time. For an additional fee, guests have access to a sauna, massage treatments and spa services. In addition, in the evening, guests often gather at the pool table. Real competitions are held here, allowing you to pass the time before dinner.





Number of rooms

The hotel has rooms of various categories, in total accommodation in two hundred and fifty-five apartments is provided. Guests can choose from standard rooms and apartments with an equipped kitchen.

In the four-star block there are only standard rooms, in the three-star block - both of the categories described above. Vacationers note that the apartments in the hotel are quite spacious. They are very nice to be in, but the furniture is already in need of updating. This is noticed by almost all the guests who once rested at the San Palace.

Each room is pretty well equipped. Guests are provided with individual air conditioning, satellite and cable TV, and a telephone with city output. The apartments have a fridge, and some have a kitchenette. The bathroom includes a hairdryer and cosmetic set. It is updated during cleaning.

By the way, the guests quite often respond with negative comments about it. Maids, unfortunately, do not seek to do their job efficiently. They try to make do with changing linens and towels, without affecting the floors in the room and on the balcony.

Tours to this hotel complex are very popular with our compatriots. Part of this demand is due to the low cost of trips to the San Palace.
