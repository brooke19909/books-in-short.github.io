"My country"

The first tariff that I would like to pay attention to is a package that provides a single cost for outgoing calls to any number in the amount of 3 rubles per minute; and incoming - 3 rubles for the first and 0 for all subsequent minutes of the conversation. The cost of SMS messages to any region of the country is set at 3 rubles.





In order to switch to the tariff, you need to pay another 25 rubles (one-time, when connecting the service). To activate the service, you must enter the command * 110 * 0021 #. The tariff is disconnected according to the same scheme, only instead of the last four digits it is necessary to enter 0020.

This option is the basic and easiest for calls to numbers in Russia that Beeline offers. Roaming in Russia on other tariff plans has some features, which we will discuss later.

"My intercity"

The next interesting package provides a minimum monthly fee (at the level of 1 ruble for each day of validity) in case the user is ready to make an advance payment for the cost of the service. If the subscriber is served on a so-called postpaid basis (pays after using traffic, minutes and other things), then the price is 30 rubles per month.





This differs from the previous tariff in that the cost of calls to numbers of other regions is 2.5 rubles per minute of conversation. Moreover, the cost of one SMS message is lower: it is 1.5 rubles. For those who are looking for how to enable roaming on Beeline for calls throughout the country, the instruction is available on the official website: you need to pay a one-time fee of 25 rubles, then call 06741. To disconnect from the option, dial 06740.
