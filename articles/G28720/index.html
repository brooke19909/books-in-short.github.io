The best tablet from China. What is he like?

Portable computer equipment has earned recognition and popularity among fans of fashionable gadgets. This is understandable, because smartphones, iPhones, tablets allow you to combine many useful functions. They are perfect for both work and entertainment. Recently, tablet computers have been in greatest demand . The buyer has the problem of choosing how to find the best tablet, which brand to prefer. It is worth familiarizing yourself with the assortment that electronics stores offer.

Strange as it may seem, buyers are in great demand tablet computers made in China. Most consumers are accustomed to taking the products of the Middle Kingdom with caution. But the electronics released in this country make us forget about past doubts. Many experts are inclined to think that Chinese products can compete with Apple. Therefore, you need to look for the best tablet in this direction.





According to the results of January 2013, the Ramos model can claim the first place, which has excellent technical characteristics that distinguish it from a number of similar devices. The tablet is equipped with a processor based on Samsung Exynos 4412. For a portable device, the display has a diagonal of 10.1 inches. The 0.3 MP front camera is designed to make video calls, for taking photos there is a camera located at the back. In addition, the best Chinese tablet has two gigabytes of RAM and thirty-two gigabytes of internal memory. It is possible to increase the device's memory up to 64 gigabytes using microSD cards. In thickness, this tablet is ten and a half millimeters. All these characteristics allow us to call this model the best.





Choosing the most successful tablet, you can not pass by such models as Ainol Novo and Onda. At the same time, the latter is the same demanded brand as Ramos. It can play 3D BlueRay video format. If you are looking for the best Android tablet, then Onda could very well become one. This electronic device has two gigabytes of RAM and sixteen gigabytes of internal memory. The battery of the tablet is designed for eight hours of work. Equipped with a more capacious battery, the Ainol Novo model can work without recharging for ten hours.





Studying the portable PC market, you should pay attention to the ratings based on the sales results that make up online stores. Undoubtedly, the most popular are devices that have Android OS. According to customer reviews, the Yeahpad Android 4.0 Tablet PC may claim the title of "best tablet." Let it be inferior in size to the Ramos model (only seven inches), but its advantages include Wi-Fi, 4 gigabytes of memory, 3G. The tablet is powered by Android 4.0.

When choosing a laptop, consider which features are most important to you. For what purpose do you purchase a device: reading books, surfing the Internet, listening to music, playing games, editing documents, watching videos. Based on these requirements, choose the best tablet, and devices made in China make a worthy competition to recognized brands around the world.
