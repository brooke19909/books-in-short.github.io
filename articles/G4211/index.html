Beautiful French Attractions: Cannes Worth Seeing!

Cannes is a beautiful city located in the south-east of France and attracts many tourists. Do not know where the most interesting city attractions are located? Cannes is an amazing place to see. We offer you a brief overview that will provide informative information about this incredibly beautiful city.





Quick reference

Cannes is a beautiful city that combines charming narrow streets and chic beaches, immensely beautiful promenades and an excellent warm climate, where almost everyone is comfortable. Cannes is a young city, because only two centuries ago it was a small village. The first step to gaining the status of a city was the arrival of Lord Brogham, who appreciated the beauty of these places and told his friends and comrades about it. Soon the fame of Cannes spread to other European countries, and gradually the small village turned into a fashionable resort. Nowadays, city sights (Cannes are famous for their beauty far beyond the borders of the country!) Dream of visiting many residents of Russia. They are sure: this place will surprise them with its unusualness, and the journey will remain in memory for a lifetime.

Where to start: the most famous sights of Cannes (France)





Inspection of Cannes is worth starting with the Suquet Square, located on the hill of the same name. From here you can see the whole city. Then we advise you to walk or get to the most famous Cannes street - Croisette, on which the most luxurious villas and hotels were built. After that, admire the historical sights. Cannes is famous for its ancient castles, among which, for example, Castres Castle, built in the distant twelfth century. Nowadays, it houses a museum of ethnology and archeology, which will be interesting to visit for many Russians.

Must-see Attractions: Cannes - a city of cinema and festivals

The first of these attractions is the Palais des Festivals, the venue for the world-famous film festival, as well as some other similar events. The second place that we recommend to all tourists is, of course, the Avenue of Stars, a place where you can see many handprints of famous personalities.

Attractions near the city

These sights, we primarily include the Lerins Islands, to reach which

You can by boat or yacht. This group of islands consists of two - Sant Marguerite and Saint Honor√©. Sant Marguerite: this is the place where the famous French fortress Fort Royal was built, which protected the country's coast from treacherous pirates. When the danger of the attack of the sea robbers ceased to be so urgent, Fort Royal became a prison in which at one time the famous prisoner Iron Mask was imprisoned. Today, the fortress houses the Museum of the Sea. Sainte Honore is the island on which the ancient monastery, built in the fifth century, is located. Surprisingly, almost all the buildings have been preserved, and the monks live in these walls to this day.
