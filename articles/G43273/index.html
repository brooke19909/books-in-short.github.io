Spring cleaning in the chiffonier, or What will we knit from?

Work on making a rug begins with a revision in the wardrobe. Select unnecessary knitwear and sort them by color. Think in what colors you want to see a rug from old T-shirts, and set aside the appropriate material. Next, you need to make "yarn" from it. How to do it? Spread garments at the seams, remove the remnants of the thread with which they were sewn. Next, cut the parts into strips 1 cm wide in a spiral from the edge to the center. Wrap the resulting knitted tape in a ball. Thus, design all the old T-shirts, T-shirts and other things.



Since the material for knitting we get is quite voluminous, which means that you will need a thick hook for work. We recommend that you prepare tool No. 10-12.

Grandma‚Äôs rug. Description of the manufacturing process

Do you want to learn how to knit a rug from old T-shirts in the way that came to us from the last century? Then we invite you to a master class. The product knitted by this method is obtained in the form of a circle.

So, from the "threads" made of knitted things, dial 5 air loops and fasten them into a ring with a connecting column . Next, execute 8 single crochet columns. Form the following rows according to the so-called "Crochet circle rule". The scheme of this motive is presented to your attention in the photo. The following loops are marked with conventional signs on it: a cross - a single crochet, a checkmark - two single crochet in one loop of the previous row. The increase in the circle, according to this rule and the figure, occurs due to the uniform additions of loops in each row. Knit a rug from old T-shirts according to this pattern up to the tenth row. And there you see: if you want to get a larger product, then continue to work further. If the diameter of the mat suits you, then do another row without increments, and then cut the knitwear and fasten the last loop. Hide the ‚Äúthread‚Äù tip on the wrong side of the product.
