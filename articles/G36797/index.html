Residential complex Borisoglebsky: reviews of residents, advantages and disadvantages of a new building

Reviews about the residential complex Borisoglebsky are important for everyone who plans to purchase an apartment in the area. This is a large-scale residential complex located on the territory of Ramensky. Its total area is almost 40 thousand square meters. The official developer is the well-known company "Garant-Stroy" in the city. By the way, a complex with a similar name ("Borisoglebskoye") is located on the territory of New Moscow, 35 kilometers from the Moscow Ring Road. About him, we also detailed the material in this article.

Features of the residential complex

Reviews of the Borisoglebsky residential complex will help buyers figure out exactly which apartments they are ready to provide, whether they meet their needs, and whether it is possible to find the desired quality of construction and finishing works here.

The residential complex itself is located on Mira Street in the city of Ramenskoye. It includes six monolithic-brick buildings, in which the supporting structures are made of a monolithic-reinforced concrete frame, and the internal walls themselves are made of bricks. It is noteworthy that all buildings of different floors - from 15 to 17. Each of them is made on an individual project, so that the impression of concrete boxes on one person that surround you every time you return home is not created.

In this residential complex there is an opportunity to purchase one-, two- or three-room apartments. In total there are 640 of them. The area of ‚Äã‚Äãresidential premises varies from forty to one hundred square meters. Many should be impressed by the height of the ceilings - three meters ten centimeters. All this creates a feeling of space, free space, even with small areas in general.

A distinctive feature of each apartment is its large and spacious kitchen, the loggias are glazed with single double-glazed windows, and double-glazed windows are mounted in the window frames. It is worth noting that in this residential complex you will not find specific apartments or penthouses. Premises for rent without decoration, but with already installed front doors. Outside, the LCD looks very presentable, since the houses are faced with colored bricks.

Territory

Young mothers in this place are guaranteed to feel comfortable, as in all the buildings of the residential complex, without exception, security rooms, concierges and prams are provided. There will be no problems with parking, which is so familiar to everyone who lives in Moscow and the Moscow region. Here they managed to be solved by building a large and spacious parking.

The house has everything you need for a comfortable and safe life: noiseless elevators, ventilation and fire extinguishing systems, modern means of communication. Moreover, an accessible environment for people with disabilities is equipped here, for disabled people there are ramps. On the territory of the residential complex, sports and playgrounds are open. Separate parking lots are provided for guests, while the entire territory of the residential complex is guarded.

It is necessary to note good transport links. The whole complex is within walking distance from Chugunov Street, which almost immediately goes into the highway crossing the village of Kratovo. There are options for how to get to the capital and by public transport. Just a five-minute walk is the Fabrichnaya railway station, through which trains to Moscow follow. You can get from here to the Kazan station or the metro station Vykhino.

Ecological situation

When it comes to why it is worth settling in Ramenskoye, many note the complete absence in the city of harmful industries. Of course, there is still industry in the city, but mainly light or agricultural enterprises are located here that do no harm to the surrounding atmosphere. The city itself and the entire surrounding area are considered very clean. In the immediate vicinity of Ramenskoye there are extensive pine forests with dry soil, around there are many sanatoriums for people with pulmonary diseases.

This residential complex is located almost in the center of this small town in the suburbs. Nearby is Lake Borisoglebskoye, the Saturn football stadium, which once played the team of the major league of the Russian championship, a recreation park, the Trinity Church.

Families with children will definitely be cozy and comfortable. Near the LCD there are ten kindergartens and three secondary schools, a children's clinic, a large number of pharmacies, a maternity hospital, supermarkets and grocery stores within walking distance.

Why is it not worth going here?

This opinion about this residential complex can also be found. There are enough dissatisfied customers who are by no means advised to purchase housing here.

In the reviews of potential buyers about the Borisoglebskoye residential complex, the main complaints so far are related to the organization of the surrounding infrastructure. At the same time, many reviews of the Borisoglebskoye residential complex in New Moscow are associated with complaints about the low pace of construction and the quality of work performed. In some reviews of residents about the residential complex Borisoglebskoye, doubts are expressed in general that there are at least some positive aspects in this complex.
