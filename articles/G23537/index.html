Sheremetyevo hotels: description, reviews

And since the influx of tourists who are expecting their next flight here is simply colossal, Sheremetyevo hotels are very popular. Inexpensive hostel

Moscow is far from the cheapest city, respectively, a stop at any hotel is an additional cost. However, we are starting to adopt Western traditions. So, the first sky-hostel appeared in the capital. Not all Sheremetyevo hotels can boast of such prices. A bed in a dormitory room can be rented for only 400 rubles, which may be an acceptable option in order to spend some time waiting for a flight. This is a cozy, modern hostel where, in addition to shared rooms, there are private rooms. Their cost is about 2000 rubles per day. The airport is located just 2 km from the hostel, so the road does not present any problems. The hostel staff is fluent in Russian and English, guests are free to use the Wi-Fi network, and bulky personal items can be stored in individual lockers. If we consider the most visited Sheremetyevo hotels, then this hostel is in the lead.





Hotel Midland

Next in line is the inexpensive but very comfortable Midland Hotel. We are currently considering the most affordable Sheremetyevo hotels, which offer their guests excellent conditions for living and recreation. Located "Midland" north of the Moscow Ring Road, in Khimki. Only a few kilometers separates it from the airport, so even with Moscow traffic jams you have every chance to get on your flight on time. For the convenience of visitors, there is a round-the-clock electronic display that informs about the arrival and departure of flights. Judging by the reviews of tourists, this is the most comfortable hotel in Sheremetyevo. The airport is located very close, the choice of rooms is very large, you can rent a single, double or family room, both regular and capsule type. It combines low prices and the highest level of service. Room with breakfast included costs only 1,500 rubles per day.



Hotel Aviator

This is the nearest hotel in Sheremetyevo. The airport is less than one kilometer away, and the hotel itself is next to the International Highway. It has three floors and 78 comfortable rooms. This is a classic hotel, there are no capsule rooms, guests are accommodated in ordinary rooms. The guests respond very warmly to the work of the hotel staff, any request will be instantly considered. Guests are provided with all conditions for a comfortable stay, as well as free airport shuttle service. It is very clean and comfortable, the cost of economy rooms is only 1,500 rubles, breakfast is included.





Romantic "Cher"

This cozy hotel is more like a medieval castle, located next to the forested area. Looking at local hotels (Moscow, Sheremetyevo), it is impossible not to pay attention to it. Despite the fact that it is much smaller than many others and includes only 12 rooms, the hotel has a special atmosphere, warmth and homeliness. It is located not far from the Klyazma river, next to the Khimki forest and the Dubki park. A double room costs only 2000 rubles without meals, but there is a well-equipped kitchen where everyone can realize their culinary talents. Judging by the reviews, this is a great option to spend time between flights. The road to the airport takes no more than 15 minutes.

Hotel "NordStar"

Very close to Sheremetyevo Airport is this small hotel. It is designed for 38 rooms, which have everything you need for a comfortable stay. Despite the fact that the hotel belongs to the economy class, and the cost of rooms starts from 2200 rubles, it surprises its guests with interesting interiors and original design solutions. Each room is decorated in an individual color scheme, and the restaurant is divided into zones, each of which is decorated in its own style. Pets are allowed at an extra charge. In addition, tourists include a well-groomed territory where you can walk and spend time.





"Bark-VIP"

A short article is not enough to describe all the hotels near Sheremetyevo, there are a lot of them. However, we will try to talk about the most popular of them, so that you can choose for yourself a decent option for an overnight stay.

This hotel is very often noted by tourists in their reviews and advise each other. Why is he so popular? First of all, because it is located between two airports, only 20 minutes from the center of Moscow. Tourists note that at a cost of 2300 rubles per day (single standard), you get a decent place to sleep, as well as excellent food in a cafe.

Comfortable three-star hotel "Union"

This is a favorite vacation spot for many tourists, whose path lies through Moscow. A modern comfortable building immediately captivates with its beauty and scope. The hotel is located in the north of Moscow, in a picturesque place, in a forested area. The rooms are located on ten floors, there are 158 in total. There is a restaurant and a bar, sports grounds to help pass the time. Standard double room costs 2800 rubles. It is relatively inexpensive by metropolitan standards.





Hotel Novotel

This is a 4 star hotel, which is located very close to the airport. This is a real paradise for business people. There are seven meeting rooms, including the largest with a capacity of 350 people. On-site emergency meetings can be held just before departure. In addition, all conditions are created here in order to relax in your spare time. To do this, there is a fitness center, sauna and pool. Professional doctors work in the massage room to help relieve stress after working days. The cost of daily rent of a comfortable room is from 6,700 rubles.

Atlanta Hotel

Another great hotel that is very popular with tourists. Of course, the hotels in Sheremetyevo are not at all deficit. Prices of such a level as in Atlanta can also be easily found, but not everywhere you get the opportunity to pay for 6 or 12 hours of stay, depending on the time of your flight. This is a business hotel, so any tourist will find its interiors and service very worthy. Guests are met at the airport and taken back for the flight, in addition, if you have a lot of free time, you can go to the center of Moscow, you will be taken to the train, and after 15 minutes you will be there. A room for 6 hours costs 1800 rubles here.

Hotel Park Inn

Like many others, it is located near Sheremetyevo. The road to the airport does not take more than 7 minutes, in addition, the hotel staff takes care of the delivery of the flight. This is a modern nine-story hotel where comfortable and cozy rooms are always available. Nearby is the Crocus Exhibition Hall, as well as Lianozovsky Park. The hotel provides free breakfast. The cost of staying here starts from 4300 rubles. You are waiting for spacious, bright rooms with beautiful interiors, the ability to use conference rooms, audio and video equipment for conferences and seminars. Judging by the reviews of tourists, this is one of the best hotels where you want to stay again and again.

Luxurious Sheraton

No wonder this hotel is a dizzying success. It really is amazing. If you want to not just spend time between flights, but also to have a first-class rest, then your best choice is the Sheraton Hotel (Sheremetyevo).





The cost of a classic room here is from 4600 rubles per day. Guests are offered free access to a fitness center, a bath and a sauna, and a hydromassage pool. In addition, reviews emphasize that the hotel is located very conveniently, only one and a half kilometers from the airport, not far from Khimki forest. Each room here has air conditioning and a minibar, coffee machine. The restaurant serves a buffet, and fine wines can also be ordered here.

As you can see, all hotels near Sheremetyevo offer quite comfortable conditions for rest between flights.
