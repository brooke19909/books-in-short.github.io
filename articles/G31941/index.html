Hotel Pifrans Holiday Beach Resort 3 * India, Goa: photos and reviews of tourists

Every person needs a rest from time to time. And many people wish not only to rest physically, but also to plunge into a new, interesting culture, while enjoying the beauties of nature and high-class service. A great option for spending such a vacation is India. This is a mysterious, exotic, but incredibly hospitable country, where I joyfully accept travelers from all over the world.

India is a country of contrasts. Here you can fully devote yourself to cultural immersion, conquer the mountain peaks and, of course, relax on the seashore. The most developed beach tourism on the island of Goa. A huge number of guest houses and hotels of different sizes and categories have been built here. And one of the good places to spend a vacation is the hotel complex Pifrans Holiday Beach Resort 3 *.

As you know, proper travel planning helps to save a lot of time and money. So what is the Arrosim Pifrans Holiday Beach Resort 3 * hotel? Can you count on comfort and quality service? What impressions do tourists have already managed to spend here a few days? The answers to these questions will be of interest to many.

Location Features





As you know, when choosing a hotel, many travelers pay attention to its location. So where to look for the hotel Pifrans Holiday Beach Resort 3 *? It is worth saying that its location is very advantageous. It is located in a picturesque resort area called Calangute. Nearby are numerous shops, cafes and other hotels. This part of the city is calm and quiet, but fun and noisy entertainment centers can be reached in a few minutes.

The distance to the beach is only 200 meters, which is important - the coast can be reached on foot in a few minutes. The nearest international airport is 8 km away in the town of Dabolim. Naturally, a travel agency provides its customers with a convenient transfer immediately upon arrival, but the transfer will take no more than 10 minutes.

What does Pifrans Holiday Beach Resort 3 * look like? Hotel Description





Many tourists immediately after arrival note that the hotel area, although small, but very beautiful. Here you will find a flowering tropical garden, secluded walking paths, terraces for relaxation and sports activities, a variety of garden decorations and cozy gazebos, fountains and alpine slides, in short, everything to give people aesthetic pleasure.

The Pifrans Holiday Beach Resort 3 * hotel itself consists of several three- and four-story bungalows located around the perimeter - they form a closed courtyard with a pool. By the way, the buildings themselves are built in European style and resemble Portuguese villas. Not so long ago, a radical renovation was carried out here, after which the hotel became even more comfortable and beautiful.

Hotel Pifrans Holiday Beach Resort 3 *: photo and information on residential rooms





This hotel is considered relatively small - on its territory there are only 30 rooms. Guests can choose from standard rooms or suite rooms. Regardless of where exactly you are settled, you can count on comfort and coziness. However, there are some differences.

Standard rooms are small, their area is 16 square meters. Of course, they are designed for 1-2 people. There is a separate exit to the terrace or private balcony, as well as a full range of furniture, including a large bed with orthopedic mattress.

The suites are more spacious - their area is 24 square meters. Visually, such rooms are divided into a sleeping and living area. There is also a small kitchenette with sink and dining table.

Of course, you can count on the presence of a basic set of household appliances, because without this it is difficult to imagine a really comfortable stay. There is an air conditioning system - not in all rooms it is new, but it works properly, allowing you to cope with the heat. You can relax in front of the TV - there are several satellite channels, so you will have something to do in the evenings. There is a telephone with which you can contact the staff at the reception at any time of the day. There is also a hot drink set.

The bathroom is small but very clean, with a new tile floor and modern fixtures. Each room has a spacious shower, and no one complains about the presence of water. Also, guests are provided with a set of hygiene products and clean towels. Reviews confirm that the rooms are really cleaned every day (unless otherwise indicated by residents). The rooms are clean and tidy, no one complains about the quality of cleaning.

Food scheme: what should a tourist count on?





It is hard to imagine a really good vacation without delicious food. Moreover, coming to India, many tourists want exotic. What in terms of food does the hotel offer Pifrans Holiday Beach Resort 3 *?

Reviews indicate that the food here is really good. The main meals are held in the form of a buffet. You can relax in the spacious restaurant, while enjoying delicious and hearty dishes. The menu is quite diverse - you can count on light snacks, salads, different types of fish and meat, fresh fruits, pastries and desserts.

By the way, when paying for accommodation, you are free to choose a convenient meal plan on your own whether it is only breakfast or half board (breakfast and dinner). By the way, if for some reason you did not pay for food, you can still eat in a restaurant, however, each bill will need to be paid separately.

In addition, the hotel Pifrans Holiday Beach Resort 3 * has a bar that will delight you with a stylish atmosphere, relaxing atmosphere and a large selection of drinks. In addition, in this area there are many establishments where you can have a good snack.

Beach and amenities





Many people go to India, wanting to have a good time on the coast. So what does the Pifrans Holiday Beach Resort 3 * hotel offer? South Goa is famous for its beautiful, clean beaches and excellent climate.

The hotel complex is located almost on the beach. Velsao Beach, a popular tourist destination, is just 200 meters away. This is a public place - here tourists relax and have fun, often various competitions and parties are organized.

The entrance to the sea is convenient, and the beach itself is relatively clean. A rescue team is constantly on duty on the shore. For a small fee, you can rent a comfortable sunbed with an umbrella. Also nearby is a bar where guests are offered drinks from the refrigerator, tea, a cafe, as well as refreshing tropical cocktails.

Fun and relaxation by the water

Guests of the Pifran S Holiday Beach Resort 3 * hotel will not have to be bored on the beach. After all, bathing and sunbathing is not the limit. On the beach, tourists can visit the water sports center. For a reasonable price, you can rent a catamaran, canoe or boat. You will also ride on a catarrh or banana, and water skiing guarantees a dose of adrenaline.

Tourists can engage in sailing, scuba diving, windsurfing. In the center you can rent the necessary equipment. Also, professionals work here who, for a small fee, will be happy to provide briefings and give some useful recommendations.

Description of services provided to guests

Despite its relatively modest size, the hotel may offer some additional services. For example, laundry is constantly available here. And guests can exchange currency for local bills, without leaving the hotel.

There is a parking nearby, where the traveler can leave the rented transport. For a small fee, you can use the safe at the reception. Internet access is available throughout the hotel, including residential rooms. On the other hand, this is a paid service.

How to spend time at the hotel? Leisure campers





Of course, in the city, and on the beach there are many ways to brightly, actively spend time. On the other hand, you can do something at the hotel. As already mentioned, the hotel has a relatively large freshwater pool. It is regularly cleaned, and the water is not too chlorinated. Around there is a well-equipped terrace for relaxation - there are sun loungers with wide umbrellas. Many guests prefer to spend at least part of the day right here, relaxing and meeting new interesting people.

In addition, there is a steam room, as well as several massage rooms where exclusively professional specialists work. You can be offered several types of massage, including relaxing and wellness.

There is no animation as such on the territory, although interesting performances are often arranged here in the evenings. You can relax while enjoying beautiful live music.

And Goa is a place for sightseeing. You can visit the most picturesque corners of nature, as well as learn more about local culture and architecture, visiting interesting historical sights. Directly on site you can pick up and purchase a guided tour. By the way, workers sometimes help to make a route for independent travel.

Are there any conditions for children's relaxation?

Can I take a child with me to the hotel Pifrans Holiday Beach Resort 3 *? Traveler reviews, as well as official information on the site, indicate that the hotel is perfect for families, so parents can not worry.

If necessary, for the child in the room can bring a folding, but quite comfortable bed. In the restaurant you can ask for a high chair for feeding the baby. There is no menu for children, but the choice of food is quite large - you can pick up something useful for a child of almost any age.

As far as entertainment is concerned, the local beach is a great place for having fun. And in the pool there is a separate small section where the children can splash around while their parents are resting nearby on the terrace. There is a small games room at your disposal - there are enough toys, as well as devices for art and other entertainment. Children of all ages have a great time at the hotel.

What type of vacation is the hotel suitable for?
