Hotel "Nicholas Park" (Fethiye, Turkey): description, service, reviews

Nicholas Park is a hotel in Fethiye that cannot be overlooked. A large hotel complex, consisting of several buildings and occupying a considerable territory, rises on a hill surrounded by a pine forest. This is a very good location. Fresh air, magnificent panoramic views, huge space for walking - what could be better? Especially considering the rich list of services provided by the hotel, a high level of comfort and excellent service. However, these significant advantages should be described in more detail.





Service

Nicholas Park Hotel has everything that should be in a hotel complex of this level - from transfer services to high-speed free Wi-Fi.

On-site parking is available. In the lobby of the main building guests are greeted by a 24-hour front desk, there is an exchange office, an excursion desk and luggage storage.

There is also a dry cleaning service and a laundry room, where guests' clothes are not only washed, but also ironed.

In addition, on the territory you can find a mini market, car rental, gift shop, beauty salon, a point with fax and photocopy.

It is also worth noting that the Nicholas Park Hotel (Fethiye) takes into account the interests of all guests, without exception. There are non-smoking rooms, bridal suites, apartments for people with disabilities and accommodation options for families.

Entertainment

Most people go to Fethiye, at the Nicholas Park Hotel, for the sake of the sea. The beach here is wonderful and special attention will be paid to it, but it is not in the immediate vicinity of the hotel. What can be done without leaving its territory?

So, there are several terraces for sunbathing and swimming pools, including children's, with water slides. There is a SPA salon and a wellness center with massage and treatment rooms, as well as a sauna and Turkish bath.

All guests have the opportunity to visit the water park, night club and unforgettable evening shows organized by animation staff for free.

In addition to the above, you can play darts, tennis (table or large), billiards, sing karaoke or shoot with an air rifle. In general, the hotel is full of entertainment - for every taste and color, as they say.

Leisure activities

It was described above about what you can do at the Nicholas Park Hotel (Fethiye) without leaving its territory. But besides this, there are many other interesting entertainments.

It organizes unforgettable paragliding, exciting excursions, jeep safaris, ATV rides in the wild, rafting (rafting on mountain rivers).

You should definitely try fishing or diving, go on a boat trip on a yacht, or even go on a tour to 12 islands, scattered not far from the Blue Lagoon, on the banks of which the complex is located. And if you want to see something truly unique, you can go on a tour organized by the hotel to the ancient city of Hierapolis and to the sources of Pamukkale - a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

  • Snack bar. Open from 11:00 to 17:30. During the day, guests can try an unlimited number of different goodies, including salads, pancakes, pastries, fast food, fresh vegetables, fruits and much more.

Budget accommodation options

Now you can proceed to the description of the rooms of the Nicholas Park Hotel (Fethiye).

The most inexpensive option is a 2-seater standard. This spacious, bright apartment features a minibar, split air conditioning, telephone for intercom, safe, widescreen plasma, satellite TV, a balcony and a bathroom. There are rooms with two separate and one with a double bed.





Cost of living

What about the cost? At the height of the season, a week of living in these apartments for two people will cost a little less than 60,000 rubles. This price includes 8% VAT and payment for all-inclusive services. If only one guest plans to check into such a room, then for him the price will be 45,000 rubles.

By the way, the price may vary depending on the preferences of the guests regarding the landscape that opens from the windows of the apartments. Rooms with sea views are usually more expensive at 15-20 thousand rubles.
