Do it yourself Christmas decoration for your home

Everyone wants to decorate their home for the New Year , regardless of age. True, souvenirs and Christmas decorations are not cheap, so making a Christmas decoration for your home with your own hands is much more profitable. Yes, and it's nice to see the results of their work in practice, especially the kids.



DIY Christmas decoration for home from pasta

It is simply amazing how the most ordinary objects in the hands of visionary masters gain a new status. For example, pasta turns into charming carved snowflakes. By the way, they can be covered with sequins and hung around the room on threads of different lengths, and can be used as Christmas tree decorations.

DIY Christmas decoration for a home from spun wool

It is possible for craftsmen to roll products from spun wool to make a variety of souvenirs as a gift to guests and to hang them both on the Christmas tree and around the room. Moreover, any options are suitable: snowmen and squirrels, miniature felt boots and funny hippos. Christmas decorations for the home: sparkling stars

This product also does not require special skills from the manufacturer. And the minimum required materials. Cardboard, PVA glue, New Year's rain and thread for suspension. A star cut out of cardboard is smeared with glue, finely cut New Year's rain is poured onto this layer. Repeat the procedure for the other side, parallel to the glitter-rain inserting the ends of the loop for hanging. Simple, but agree with the taste!



Christmas paper decorations - strip lanterns

Making such a flashlight is a pleasure! It is necessary to cut several strips of the same width from colored paper. One is made as tall as the flashlight itself will be. The other two measure a little longer. Then prepare the next pair, which differs from the previous even greater length. Then they stack all the strips in a stack so that the short one is in the middle, and the length of the strips laid on each side of it gradually increases. The ends of the strips are fastened on one side: can be glued, can be a stapler, can be stitched with threads. Now the ends remaining free are equalized and also fastened together. You can make a flashlight from 7, 9, 11 bands - this is how much the master has enough patience and imagination.



Christmas decorations - balls from cards

If you take a few bright cards or ordinary colored cardboard, you can make a charming ball. It will require some trigonometric thinking, so the baby alone will not be able to cope with such an craft. Five-petal flowers are cut out, cuts are made at the base of the petals - exactly to the middle. Then two flowers are connected, inserting cuts on the petals one into the other until they stop. They take the next flower - it will already connect to the resulting design with two petals: one with one flower, and the other with another flower.
