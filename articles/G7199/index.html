Cozy Twenty8 Inn Hotel 3

Description: The small Twenty8 Inn Hotel 3 is located one hundred meters from the sandy beach of Pattaya, 150 kilometers from the international and 50 kilometers from the domestic airport. A cozy mini-hotel, built in 2009, offers a pleasant stay in a relaxed home environment. The hotel will be convenient for tourists, sightseers, romantic couples, youth companies, families with children.

Rooms: All 28 guestrooms at Twenty8 Inn Hotel are uniquely decorated in different national styles. Having settled in the hotel, you can find yourself in sultry Africa, variegated Latin America or low-key northern Europe.



All apartments have a balcony, air conditioning and a built-in safe. The room has an ironing board, sofa, refrigerator, dishes, a minibar and an electric kettle. Every day, guests receive bottled drinking water. A welcome drink awaits the new arrivals. For newlyweds, specially decorated rooms are prepared.

Rooms The Twenty8 Inn Hotel 3 is a non-smoking area. The room changes linen three times a week and does the cleaning every day.

Meals: Restaurant Bistro will delight guests with classic dishes of Russian and Thai cuisine, as well as offer internationally recognized food and drinks. In the restaurant, open from morning until evening, some dishes are prepared directly with visitors.

Guests are offered a choice of six types of breakfast: Asian, American, French, English, Russian or diet.



Light snacks, soft drinks and spirits at any time can be selected in the bar.

Beach: The sandy beach belonging to the hotel is located a few tens of meters. Towels, mattresses, awnings and sun loungers are provided free of charge.

Additional information: Twenty8 Inn Hotel 3 offers a relaxing holiday and beach activities. In the courtyard of the hotel is an outdoor pool with sun loungers. On the territory there is a Turkish bath and a Finnish steam room. Guests can enjoy Thai massages and spa treatments for a fee. Sunbathing, beach volleyball, water sports await on the shore.

For guests there is a left-luggage office and laundry service, there is an Internet connection in public areas and a fresh press in the lobby. Special meals are provided for children and a small bed in the room is available on request.



Vacationers have the opportunity to go on excursions to Bangkok, which is full of historical attractions.
