How to get from Gomel to Minsk?

How to get from the city of Gomel to Minsk? Which vehicle will help you cope with this task faster? How much will a ticket cost? You can find answers to all questions by reading this article.

A few words about Gomel

The city of Gomel is located in the southeast of the Republic of Belarus. It is located on the Sozh River, 534 km east of Brest. This is a fairly large settlement, in which more than 535 thousand people live. Residents of this legendary region consider Gomel the second capital of Belarus. It created a huge number of entertainment centers: cinemas, theaters, exhibition halls, museums and circuses.





Several objects located in this place are dedicated to the Great Patriotic War: the Barrow of Glory, the festival park and a monument erected in memory of the defender militias. Tourists and locals love to come to the avenue of lovers, its appearance makes the hearts beat with renewed vigor.

A few words about the city of Minsk

Minsk is the legendary capital of Belarus, which has a huge number of attractions. Each tourist should take a walk from the station square to the Minsk Gate. The architectural monuments located on this street are fascinating.





You can get acquainted with the long history of this city by looking at the Museum of Literature, the Museum of Culture, the Trinity Museum, the gallery of crafts or the house of nature. Tourists claim that in these facilities there is always friendly staff who with great pleasure will tell about interesting facts of their native land. It is definitely worth a look in the cafe, tasting the national dishes of Belarus.

A train

Gomel and Minsk are two of the most popular cities for tourists in the country. Between these two points daily run trains and trains. In total, you can count eight railway runs in this direction every day.





The Minsk-Gomel train from the station after 3 hours 38 minutes is at the destination. The total cost of the trip is 5.95 Belarusian rubles.

Locals buy tickets exclusively at the box office. If you wish, you can make a purchase through the official website. However, you still have to come to the station to purchase it, but not in electronic but in printed form.

Train

How to get from Gomel to Minsk? For guests of the country it will not be difficult. Three times a day, an electric train arrives at the passenger station. It moves without stops, so you can quickly arrive at the appointed point - in less than three hours.

The first flight of this vehicle is at 7:00, the second at 14:36, and the third at 19:01. A ticket will cost a little more than a regular train, and its price will be 8.39 Belarusian rubles. There are never problems with his purchase, you can purchase it immediately before sending the composition.

Car

The distance from Gomel to Minsk is only 308 kilometers. It is easy to overcome by car. The total travel time will be approximately 3 hours 32 minutes. Between these two points, the M5 highway is laid, which represents one straight line. It is worth noting that it was completely renovated in 2016, respectively, in almost every area, the road is flat. Bright markings highlighted four main stripes. Cameras are installed along the entire perimeter of the route, therefore it is not recommended to exceed the speed limit of 120 km / h.

Bus

Every day carries out five bus trips from Gomel to Minsk. The cost of a ticket for it will be about 14 Belarusian rubles per person. The first departure is scheduled for 5:31. It is worth considering that this is the longest journey from one point to another, it will take you to spend about 6 hours. In this country there is an opportunity to buy a ticket through an online service, which is very convenient.





Many locals prefer to travel in this direction by minibus. This is due to the fact that it is possible to exit at any desired stop. Ticket price is 13 Belarusian rubles.

Airplane

The fastest means of transportation is an airplane. It can be reached from one point to another in just 50 minutes. It is worth noting that such flights are carried out only in the warm period - from early May to late September. This is the most expensive form of transport, one ticket will cost more than 35 Belarusian rubles. In summer it is recommended to book in advance.

Gomel and Minsk are two legendary cities. They have a huge number of attractions, entertainment complexes and industrial points. There are many reasons why people move in these directions. Fortunately, there are several types of vehicles that allow you to get from one point to another. It is worth choosing the most optimal option for yourself.
