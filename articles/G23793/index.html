Do-it-yourself cowboy costume at home

Often there is a problem with choosing a costume for the carnival in which the child will be. It is especially difficult to find a costume for a boy, as in the case of girls it is obvious - Little Red Riding Hood, snowflake, Cinderella, etc.





One of the options for a guy can be a cowboy costume. Making it yourself is not so difficult, so you don‚Äôt have to make much effort. The main thing is to observe the accuracy in the patterns and follow the instructions in which everything is signed in detail. Even for a novice craftswoman, this will not take too much time.

So, how can you create a New Year's cowboy costume? Simply and easily. To do this, you will need trousers, a shirt and a vest. In order to create pants, you should take as a basis a dense velvet fabric of dark color. Jeans may come up. Then you should transfer the patterns to the fabric on the side that will be the wrong side. Do not forget about the seams, which should be approximately one and a half centimeters. After the pattern is applied, you should cut it strictly along the lines and fold the future trousers so that the front side is directed inward. After that, you should sew the trousers, which subsequently go into the cowboy costume. In this process, you first need to stitch, make step seams, and then overcast the edges. After this, the middle seam is made and, again, the edges are stitched.



Then you should do the belt. In order to make it, you should carefully tuck the upper part of the pants so that you get a kind of drawstring, the width of which will be about three centimeters. After that, it must be hemmed. Do not forget that it is necessary to sew a special elastic band into the belt.





As elements of decor, a special fringe should be sewn to the trousers. This must be done in the area of ‚Äã‚Äãseams and along their entire length. You can also sew a fringe only from the knees to the bottom. The trousers that a cowboy costume should have are ready. We should move on to creating a shirt.

In order to make a shirt, you can take natural cotton fabric, dyed in bright colors or plain. It depends on taste preferences. The beginning in the manufacture of shirts is exactly the same as in the case of trousers. First of all, it is necessary to draw contours with the help of a pattern, cut out the necessary details. Having sewn the back half from the wrong side, you should overcast all the seams and smooth them well with an iron. Then do the same procedure with the shelves along the side and shoulder seams.

From the same material, you should make a stitching on the neck, fold it with the front side of the inside and stitch it. Turning the product inside out, iron it and iron over all the edges. The side seams on the sleeves should be sewn separately, after which it is necessary to sew them to the main product.

On this shirt, which all cowboy costumes have, is ready.

Making a vest is nothing but the absence of sleeves, from the process of creating a shirt. Also, from the seams, a fringe should be sewn. It is used as an additional element of the costume.

This is where the cowboy costume for the boy is ready!
