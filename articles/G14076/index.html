Samira Beach Resort 2 * (India / Goa): photos and reviews of tourists

Surely everyone knows that Goa is one of the most popular holiday destinations. Here, tourists are waiting for incredible natural beauties, miles of sandy beaches, lots of historical sights and, of course, quality service. Of course, on the coast built a huge number of hotels of different categories. One of the most popular vacation spots is Samira Beach Resort 2 * hotel.

Immediately worth saying that this is a very small hotel. On the other hand, it is very cozy here, so hotel rooms are usually reserved for several months in advance. Naturally, travelers are interested in more information about this place. For example, before booking it is important to know about the conditions of accommodation and food. Can I bring a child with me? Should the traveler count on additional amenities and entertainment? How long will it take to go to the beach? These are important questions for every person planning a vacation.

Location Features





As you know, the location of the hotel for many travelers is important. So where to look for the Samira Beach Resort 2 * hotel? Morjim, a colorful, calm village by the sea - it was here, not far from the beach, that a cozy hotel was built.

The nearest large settlement is the city of Calangute, which is located about 10 km. But 18 km is the no less interesting town of Panaji. You can get there by taxi (hotel staff, by the way, can arrange this) or by rented transport. The distance to the airport is 29 km - moving will not take much time, and travel agencies provide their customers with a transfer.

Hotel Samira Beach Resort 2 * (India, Morjim): how is the territory arranged?





This is a very small hotel - it consists of a three-story building, which, incidentally, was recently completely renovated. Despite its very modest size, the Samira Beach Resort 2 * hotel has a cute courtyard decorated with greenery and flowers - here tourists relax and enjoy the clean sea air.

On the ground floor there is a reception, a spacious room for relaxation and waiting, as well as a small dining room. All other space is reserved for living rooms. Tourists like this place, because in addition to modern comfort, here guests are surrounded by hospitality and real home comfort.

Rooms at the hotel: photos and descriptions





As already mentioned, this is a very small hotel - on its territory there are only 10 rooms. The rooms are comfortably furnished, including large, comfortable beds, as well as a table, chairs and a sofa. The design is simple but elegant.

Of course, the rooms have the necessary equipment - it is in working condition. You can count on the availability of a roomy refrigerator, air conditioning, as well as a TV and phone. The rooms have internet access. There is also an electric kettle and a set for making hot drinks.

En suite bathrooms come with showers. Guests are provided with clean towels (including large, beach ones). Cleaning is done daily.

Can I count on food?

Of course, a truly high-quality vacation is difficult to imagine without proper nutrition. What does the Samira Beach Resort 2 * hotel offer its guests?

The price includes daily breakfast. Meals are held in a small, cozy dining room. Reviews of tourists say that the dishes here are simple, but tasty and hearty - fresh pastries, scrambled eggs, light salads and snacks, aromatic tea and coffee. By the way, breakfast can be delivered directly to your room - this is a free service, so many guests enjoy breakfast in their own rooms, enjoying the view from the window.

The rest of the day, tourists eat outside the hotel. You should not worry about this, as there are many catering establishments nearby. Several quite decent cafes can be found even on the beach. The prices here, by the way, are reasonable, and there is a choice of dishes - you can enjoy Indian delicacies or prefer a more familiar international cuisine.

Beach holidays: entertainment and amenities on the beach





It is quite natural that coming to the island of Goa, tourists expect to relax on the seashore. What can the guests of Samira Beach Resort 2 * hotel count on? Morjim is a town famous for its beautiful beaches. One of them is located very close to the hotel. A convenient walking path leads to it.

The coast here is beautiful, covered with soft white sand. The beach line is quite wide, and the entrance to the sea is convenient. The mild climate, warm sea waters and the gentle sun create ideal conditions for relaxing on the beach. By the way, comfortable loungers with mattresses, as well as umbrellas that protect from the sun, are placed for guests.

There is an opportunity to spend time and more actively. For example, many travelers like to snorkel, because the underwater world is very beautiful here. You can also go boating, take a short boat trip, try windsurfing or sailing. By the way, in the village there is a good diving center. Here, beginners can take a short training course, and the owners of the relevant certificates can rent the necessary equipment for immersion.

The list of additional services





Guests of the Samir Beach Resort 2 * hotel can count on some additional amenities. In particular, the building has a functional parking lot, which guests can use for free. By the way, in the hotel you can easily rent a bicycle, car or moped (of course, you need the necessary documents). You can also rent vehicles in the city. Many travelers prefer this way to move around the island.

The hotel operates a currency exchange - you can exchange it at a favorable rate. Laundry facilities are also available. And in the whole building, as well as in the courtyard, there is wireless Internet access - you can use the connection for free.

How to have fun at the hotel?

Samira Beach Resort 2 * is a kind of "mini-hotel", so in most cases it is used by travelers as an overnight stay. This is a place where you can relax after a busy day and have a tasty meal in the morning. In the courtyard there is a terrace where guests can sunbathe, relax and just chat with each other.

But tourists, as a rule, seek entertainment outside the hotel. In the center of the village there are several shops, cafes and bars where you can spend your time. There are a lot of attractions in the vicinity - the island is ideal for excursions. By the way, the hotel will definitely advise you on the most interesting places and help you to make a busy route for independent travel.

Hotel Samira Beach Resort 2 * (Goa): reviews





Every experienced traveler knows that in addition to official information, it is important to get acquainted with the opinions of people who have already managed to spend several days at the hotel. What do they say about the hotel Samira Beach Resort 2 *?

The reviews are mostly positive - the hotel is really nice and beautiful. The territory is small, but very clean, and the rooms are pleasant to spend time. All equipment is working properly. The maids really try to keep the rooms clean and tidy. Breakfast is not too varied and sophisticated, but very tasty and satisfying.

The location of the hotel is convenient - there is a shop and a cafe nearby, and the sea is very close. By the way, the beach here is just wonderful. Tourists enjoy a vacation on the beach, without overpaying for accommodation.
