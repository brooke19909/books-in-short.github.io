The most striking sights of Novorossiysk

Novorossiysk is a port. Although one of the main tasks in the development of this city is the improvement of various tourist areas.



The city, thanks to long-term trade relations, has become modern and well-maintained, with a well-developed infrastructure. At the same time, no one can resist the romance of the Black Sea, multiplied by its legendary history.

The main attractions of Novorossiysk are somehow connected with the wars, and there were a lot of them here. Although many tourist routes can be attributed to natural formations.

Above the highway, not far from the Oktyabr factory, there is an impressive structure, which depicts 4 hands of warriors, tightly squeezing machine guns.

Further looking at the sights of Novorossiysk, one can distinguish the famous memorial complex called "Death Valley", on the territory of which there is a monument called "Explosion". This is a composition that is made up of fragments of bombs, shells and mines.



Near the city there are several small resort villages. The item Wide Shirka is very popular . This place has a developed modern infrastructure and residential complexes. Walking north, you can find yourself in South Ozereyka and see the following attractions of Novorossiysk - dolmens resembling large towers.

Abrau-Durso receives a lot of tourists every day. Their interest is primarily due to the fact that here is the birthplace of Russian champagne.

Arriving in Novorossiysk, the sights of which are not limited to monuments alone, it is worth visiting the largest freshwater lake in the region, which is located near the city. This lake Abrau is supported by underground sources. The water here is crystal clear, although you should still not drink it.

At an altitude of about 1 kilometer there is a monument-calendar. Huge numbers (10 * 20 meters) indicate the liberation of Novorossiysk from the Nazis.

Coming out on the coast of the Sujuk spit, you can see the Sorcerer Mountain. Due to the warm mountain air and sea wind, this place is a haven for those who like paragliding. From here you can get to Myskhako. In this place, vineyards grow right on the shore.



Every year, thousands of tourists come to Novorossiysk. Sights, photos of some of them can be seen above, include the Myskhako winery, which every November holds a celebration of young wine. A huge number of secluded places, arched beaches and the almost complete lack of infrastructure attract here lovers of secluded relaxation. Myskhako is the most naturalistic place in the whole city.

Novorossiyskaya embankment deserves special attention. It is suitable for hiking, almost everywhere you can go down to the sea and swim. Nearby is the Cape of Love and the water park - the famous romantic corners.
