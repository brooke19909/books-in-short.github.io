How to make a flashlight out of paper, but not simple, magical?

The phrase "paper flashlight" does not mean a device for lighting, but only carries the designation of a kind of decoration made of colored paper. True, this adornment came to modern life precisely as luminous "sky lanterns" from China. Then, with the help of such paper structures, inside which the lamp was placed, messages were transmitted over long distances during military operations. Indeed, under the influence of hot air, "sky lanterns" rose high above the ground and soared in the air, visible very far away.

This device later began to be used during the holidays, so the shell began to be decorated in every possible way. Only in 2006, Russia again began to use flying lamps in paper balloons, which were launched in memory of the victims of the earthquake. Up to this point, the concept of "paper lantern" directly determined the decoration of the holidays.

There are many workshops that tell and demonstrate how to make a flashlight out of paper. The easiest way was included in the program of labor lessons in the elementary grades of the middle of the last century. To make such a toy, it is enough to take a sheet of colored paper, fold it in half lengthwise so that the colored side is outward.
