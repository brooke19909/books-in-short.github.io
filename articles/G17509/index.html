Crochet shorts: patterns and description of the principles of knitting

For many girls, shorts are their favorite summer clothes. This is natural, because they are extremely comfortable, lightweight and perfectly emphasize the dignity of the figure.



For crochet knitters, summer is a special time, because you can wear airy lace from cotton or silk yarn, for example, shorts (crochet). Schemes and description of models are developed by eminent designers and ordinary housewives. This wardrobe item (shorts) cannot be considered classic, since it looks very eccentric. However, with the right combination of model, pattern and materials, the craftswoman can get a real masterpiece.

Features of a pattern of shorts

Those craftswomen who have never encountered sewing should pay increased attention to taking measurements and building a pattern of shorts.





Even given the certain elasticity of the knitted fabric, it should be understood that the contours of the parts must be anatomically correct. The back of the shorts is always made higher than the front. The roll-out on the back is also made higher, otherwise crocheted shorts (patterns and description of the pattern do not matter) will not fit. It is desirable to make the lower part of each part narrow so that the finished product is comfortable to wear. Of course, there are models with a straight cut, but there is a risk that the shorts will be pulled up when walking.

How to make a belt?

Usually crochet shorts (the diagrams and description confirm this) are made without fasteners, so the belt cannot be smaller than the circumference of the hips. To keep the product tightly held and not sliding, the craftswomen thread a knitted lace, satin ribbon or twisted twine along the upper edge.





If desired, you can equip crocheted shorts with an elastic band. The schemes and description of such models will include several rows of single crochet or double crochet columns. Then the products will be really practical. To prevent the gum from being visible from the front side of the belt, lining fabric is hemmed to its inside. Cotton, linen or silk material is best suited for these purposes.
