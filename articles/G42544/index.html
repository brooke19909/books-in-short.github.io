Hotel "Central" (Gelendzhik): description of rooms, service, reviews

The Krasnodar Territory has long attracted Russians with its picturesque resorts, clear sea, healing climate, favorable location and affordable prices. Every year, domestic tourism is being improved, the infrastructure of resorts is improving, the level of hotels and their service is increasing. Services familiar to Russians only for holidays abroad, are increasingly being introduced into local hotels and holiday homes. The resort cities of the Krasnodar Territory are developing and improving their attractiveness every year.

Rest in Gelendzhik

Gelendzhik is known to Russians as a wonderful resort where you can spend a vacation and enjoy a complete unforgettable vacation for every taste. In order to come here you do not need to do a passport, know foreign languages ​​and buy foreign currency - here everything is your own, native.

Holidays in Gelendzhik can satisfy the most demanding tourists. It has everything: a chic promenade, which is considered the longest in the world, modern high-class hotels, many guest houses and developed infrastructure, crystal clear sea, wide equipped beaches, fine restaurants and cozy cafes, a tour desk, a huge number of attractions, and, of course, picturesque nature and a unique healing climate.





The climate in Gelendzhik is mild, summers are very hot, not rainy, the swimming season lasts from May to the very end of September, autumn is warm and incredibly beautiful, winter does not have severe frosts, and spring is quite early. A comfortable vacation in Gelendzhik will be at any time of the year, but the velvet season is considered the most chic.

Gelendzhik in September is something. The beaches are much freer than in summer, the sea is still gentle and warm, the sun is no longer so aggressive, it gives warmth, not heat. Tanning at this time is the safest and longest lasting. The velvet season gives vacationers peace and enjoyment of the delights of the resort. Excursions at this time bring only pleasure, the heat does not oppress, there are no crowds of tourists. In addition, holiday prices in September are lower than in summer. Unfortunately, in September, not everyone can afford Gelendzhik, mainly in connection with the beginning of the school year.





Gelendzhik resort is not only a city, but also a huge number of quiet and comfortable villages with a choice of housing for every taste and pocket. The city itself has a huge number of hotels and hotels with a high level of comfort.

Hotel Central, Gelendzhik

One of the most developed and modern holiday destinations in Gelendzhik is the Central Hotel. From the name itself, we can conclude that it is located in the very center of the resort city.

Around the hotel within walking distance are concentrated all the places you need to relax: wide sandy and pebble beaches, the famous promenade, a huge market and supermarkets, many cafes and restaurants, water parks, the central square, the sea terminal and many other places that tourists need.





The Central Hotel (Gelendzhik) is a multi-story modern fashionable building with magnificent architecture. The hotel is located on the first line from the sea, just a hundred meters from the public beach.

Accommodation for vacationers

Thirty-two rooms of varying degrees of comfort are offered to guests by the Central Hotel.





Description of rooms:

  • Standard double one-room - from 16 to 23 m 2 . The furniture is necessary. Shower with curtain, toilet. Flat TV, air conditioning.
  • Standard room with sea view. This room is all the same as in the standard standard, but its cost is higher due to the beautiful view from the window.
  • Studio (family) - 29 m 2 . The room is divided into a sleeping area and a relaxation area. In the sleeping area there is a bed, etc. In the recreation area there is a double large sofa, armchairs, a coffee table, a TV stand. The room has a bathroom with shower, toilet, air conditioning and other furnishings.
  • Two-room suite - from 28 to 38 m 2 . Consists of a bedroom and a lounge. The bedroom has the necessary furniture. In the living room - a folding sofa, two armchairs, a coffee table, a wardrobe, a wall with a TV, air conditioning. Bathroom in the room with all necessary accessories.
  • Three-room apartment - 70 m 2 . The room is equipped with a private kitchen. It has two bedrooms and a living room. Each bedroom has a double bed, bedside tables, wardrobes. The living room has chic upholstered furniture. Each room has air conditioning and TV. Bathroom - shower, toilet, hairdryer, toiletries.

Each room is equipped in a modern European style. The calm non-inviting interior of the rooms in pastel colors has a pleasant rest. Hotel Central (Gelendzhik) provides tourists with rooms that meet all international standards.

Hotel prices

Prices for holidays at the hotel depend on the season. In the off-season, the price decreases, in the high season it rises.

Hotel Central (Gelendzhik) prices:

  • Standard number - from 2000 rubles to 4000 rubles per day per room.
  • Standard with sea view - from 2000 to 5000 rubles per day per room.
  • Studio - from 2500 to 4500 rubles per day per room.
  • Two-room suite - from 3000 to 5000 rubles per day per room.
  • Apartments - from 5,000 to 10,000 rubles per day per room.

Services included in the cost of rest

The price includes the use of a room of the selected category. Number of people in the room - according to the main places. Extra beds are paid.

Breakfast is provided for the main vacationers, they are included in the price.





Each room has internet service.

The room price includes the use of the barbecue, children's playroom, the use of karaoke in the bar.

Hotel "Central": service and additional services

The hotel service meets international standards and provides daily cleaning of the rooms, once every three days a change of bed linen, animator services are provided for children in the games room. Near the hotel there is a 24-hour guarded parking.

For an additional fee, vacationers can order babysitting services, laundry services , banquets, parties. The hotel’s tour desk will provide tourists with its services and organize the best excursions to the sights of the Krasnodar Territory.





At the request of customers, it is possible to organize custom-made complex meals in the hotel restaurant.

How to get to the hotel?

The hotel is located in the very center of the city, as its name suggests - the Central Hotel. Everyone decides how to get to the hotel for himself and it will not be difficult. All transport infrastructure is located within walking distance. At the request of tourists, the hotel has the opportunity to provide a transfer from the railway station, bus station or from Gelendzhik airport.

Hotel address: Russia, Krasnodar Territory, Gelendzhik, st. Ostrovsky, d.10.
