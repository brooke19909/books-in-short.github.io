Water park "Almond Grove": photos and description of attractions

Most guests come to the water entertainment complex just to swim in the pools and ride the slides. What attractions does the Almond Grove offer visitors? In total, the complex has six pools and fourteen slopes of varying complexity. Many guests of the water park really like the sea wave zone. This is a large swimming pool with an area of ‚Äã‚Äã480 m2, working cyclically - in it you can enjoy a very realistic breeze, and then swim for 10-40 minutes in calm water. As for the rides, the Almond Grove water park boasts open and closed direct and serpentine slides, varying in height and length. Some of them need to ride on special inflatable rafts.
