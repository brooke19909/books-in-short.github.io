Gorokhovets: attractions and monasteries

Gorokhovets is one of the ancient Russian cities with very valuable architectural monuments: many temples and monasteries of the XVII-XVIII centuries are perfectly preserved in it. But the main thing is that this city is a storehouse of ancient civil buildings: chambers of merchants of the 17th century, huts of ordinary inhabitants of the posad, decorated with tiles and carvings, as well as delightful works of wood masters of the 19th century. In any case, it is worth visiting Gorokhovets. Attractions will be appreciated by every tourist.





How to get there

From the city to Vladimir - 152 kilometers, and to Moscow - all 330. The bus stops in the city , following the route Moscow - Nizhny Novgorod. There is a regular message with Vladimir. The bus station is located on Moskovskaya Street.

Some major cities can be reached by train, although the train station is located outside the city. From Moscow it is easiest to get to this city on the M7 highway. In 4.5 hours you will have to cover a distance of 335 kilometers. Going to Gorokhovets, it is better to mark the sights on the map immediately. So it will be easier to navigate in the city, and you will not lose much time.





History

It is widely believed that the name Gorokhovets comes from the nickname Gorokh. But history does not report who was its bearer. The first mention in written sources dates back to 1158, but back in the 11th century, many Slavs inhabited these lands.

In the center of the city stood the Kremlin on Nikolskaya Gora. Powerful earthen ramparts and other fortifications made of wood were built around its perimeter. These wooden structures were destroyed in the Time of Troubles. The location away from the restless borders of the state saved Gorokhovets from attacks and destruction. The Kremlin and the posad on the very bank of the Klyazma River have been preserved to this day .

The winter lasts in the Vladimir region for more than 4 months, it is snowy with an average temperature of -8.5 degrees. Climate and relief contributed to the creation of ski slopes in these places. Notable houses

Travelers often notice wooden architectural forms. House of businessman Shorin is widely known in Gorokhovets. An interesting house was erected in 1902. The owner of the house was directly involved in the foundation of the shipbuilding plant of the city of Gorokhovets. Everyone knows that Gorokhovets is famous for its old streets. Attractions there are located at every step.

On Lenin Street (in the center of the city) you cannot pass by a large number of architectural monuments. Among them is the mansion of Prisheltsov, in which at different times there was a cell of the Russian Social-Democratic Labor Party and the district party committee.





Semenichev House was built at the turn of the XIX-XX centuries by a large factory owner. In the 21st century, a museum is located here.

Museum

In School Lane, the fifth house is occupied by a museum of history and architecture. It will help to form a holistic view of the development of the city. Museum exhibits are housed in the merchants' chambers of Sapozhnikov and the Church of St. John the Baptist. The museum exposition is one of the most entertaining on the Golden Ring route.
