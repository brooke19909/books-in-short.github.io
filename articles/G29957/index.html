Apart-hotel "Imeretinsky" (Sochi, Adler): reviews and photos

Numerous hotels in Adler and Sochi are waiting all year round to visit tourists from different parts of our vast country. A large number of hotels opened its doors for vacationers after the Winter Olympics were successfully held here in 2014. The excellent Imeretinsky Hotel was not an exception, its rich infrastructure will turn any vacation into an unforgettable adventure. Here guests of any income can have a great time, which makes the hotel even more attractive.

When did the hotel appear

The Imeretinsky Hotel (Adler) opened its doors for all comers a few months before the Olympics in September 2013. Even then, the hotel was ready to receive guests in comfortable and beautifully furnished rooms with designer furniture.

Old name

At the time of its opening, the Imeretinsky 4 * hotel (Sochi) had a different name - Aivazovsky. The modern name appeared a little later. The creator of the entire infrastructure of the Imereti Lowland, the Basic Element company, decided to change the original name of the hotel, but at the same time retained its style and decoration.





Location

Conveniently located near the Olympic Park, Imeretinsky Apart Hotel is located in the Adler district of Sochi, in the immediate vicinity of the Black Sea. Very close to the hotel guests can see the Olympic venues and the Formula 1 race track. It is easy to get here both from the city airport and from the railway station. The distance to them is only 6.5 and 7 km, respectively.

Those who decide to stay at this hotel can easily visit the ski resorts of Krasnaya Polyana. Indeed, within walking distance there is a railway station called the Olympic Village, from where you can drive to snow-capped mountain peaks in a short time.

How to get there

So, you are going to rest at the Imeretinsky Hotel. How to get to it, by what transport? These questions will surely arise before you start your trip. And so that at the very last moment you do not look for the necessary information in different sources, and even more so do not suffer from this problem, being already on the coast, remember the following.

The Imeretinsky Hotel itself is located, as mentioned above, in the Adler district of Sochi, or rather, on Morskoy Boulevard, Building 1. It is most convenient to come here from the bus stop Park Yuzhnye Kultury, where you can come from the railway station Adler by bus No. 125 or minibuses No. 117 and 134. From Adler airport you will have to change trains: first by bus or minibus No. 135 to the New Centre shopping center, and from it - by bus No. 124 or 124. You can also get to the Olympic Village railway station, from which you will have to go to the hotel for a short time.





What is he like

The territory on which the Imeretinsky Hotel (Adler) is located is very extensive. It has one main building, comprising seven floors, and a large number of buildings with comfortable apartments, which are located in the Coastal, Marine, Park and Reserve blocks. The last three quarters during the Winter Olympics were part of the Olympic Village.

Due to its large and beautiful territory, Imeretinsky is a hotel, a photo of which must be seen.

The Parkovy quarter is the greenest place in the entire Imereti district. There are natural lakes, near which there are residential buildings, areas for recreation and games, and cozy patios.





The Coastal quarter is located near the Adler yacht port. These apartments offer wonderful views of the arboretum, port and mountains. The territory has two outdoor pools and a playground.

Closest to the sea is the Morskoy quarter. Here, from almost every room there is the opportunity to admire a beautiful view of the sea surface. All courtyards are equipped with playgrounds and pools.

Not far from natural lakes is the Zapovedny quarter. In its buildings - rooms with large loggias and panoramic windows. This place is considered one of the most quiet and secluded in the area.

Travelers who went on vacation to the Imeretinsky Hotel (Adler) leave very detailed reviews. With their help, we can make a general conclusion that this place is a good option for relaxation. Tourists are satisfied with the quick check-in, as well as the range of services offered at the hotel.





Room Information

Apart-hotel "Imeretinsky" in its main building offers guests 196 rooms of various categories, among which there are standard rooms, suites, as well as presidential suites. All are equipped with climate control system, necessary furniture, satellite TV, hairdryer, ironing board with iron, tea set, telephone, wireless Internet, safe, hairdryer, toiletries and bathrobes.

Freestanding buildings have rooms with a different number of rooms: from one to four. Some also have kitchens, which are great for extended stays with the whole family, especially with young children. All rooms have a TV, basic furniture, a hairdryer, an iron with ironing board, air conditioning and Wi-Fi access. There are no telephones with the ability to call the city in the apartments, except for 7 and 9 buildings of the Sea Quarter.

Description of apartments

Services

All guests who have chosen Imeretinsky Hotel as their place of rest can use the fitness center, sauna and spa, restaurant and bars, a conference room, outdoor and indoor swimming pools in the main building and located in the apartment blocks. Those who came to rest in their own transport have the opportunity to park their car in the parking lots of the Sea, Coastal or Reserve blocks. For children, a club, animation and a games room are organized. The complex even has its own sanatorium.

In summer, guests can visit the Imeretinsky beach club, and in wintertime they can hire ski equipment and equipment.

Fitness and SPA at the hotel

A fitness center that meets all modern requirements is located on the second floor of the main building. It works from 7 am to 11 pm. This is a separate comfortable, stylish and very bright world for people who prefer to think about their health and figure even on vacation. There are all conditions in order to maintain and restore their strength, tone the body and even increase immunity.





The indoor pool, located in the fitness center, has a jacuzzi, relaxation area and comfortable sun loungers. If one of the guests is not happy with the weather outside, but really wants to swim, then this place will be an excellent alternative to an outdoor pool and even a swim in the sea. In the spacious hall for individual and group classes, you can work out with professional instructors, get expert advice and work on your physical form.

For those who prefer to work with weights or work out on cardiovascular machines, a gym is available that offers equipment of the popular AeroFit brand. And for lovers of steam in the fitness center there is a sauna.

The spa is located on the seventh floor of the hotel. There is a magnificent thermal area with a jacuzzi, hammam and sauna, numerous rooms for spa services, a relaxation area and an excellent terrace with wonderful views overlooking the Black Sea. You can visit the spa from 10am to 10pm.





Beach club "Imeretinsky"

What rest in Sochi without lying on the beach? The management of the Imeretinsky hotel understands this very well, therefore an excellent beach club is equipped here. It consists of two parts: the lower, where 200 sun loungers are located, and the upper with 700 sun loungers. The entire infrastructure of the club is available to vacationers from 8 am to 8 pm. Guests whose rooms are located directly in the main building of the hotel are provided with free sun loungers. Vacationers in buildings with apartments will have to pay for the rental of sunbeds.

There are sports grounds for playing, for example, tennis and volleyball, a small pool and a jacuzzi, a playground, a bar and a cafe. Right on the top beach you can enjoy a professional massage. For especially demanding guests there is a VIP zone.

And of course, everything is equipped here so that relaxing near the sea is not only pleasant, but also safe: a first-aid post and a rescue tower work. There is also a dressing room and shower with toilet.

You can get to the Imeretinsky beach club by personal car, after which you can leave it in a special parking lot located nearby. For guests in the hotel complex a free minibus runs here.





About the beach, which has the apart-hotel "Imeretinsky", the reviews of our compatriots contain very useful information for future guests. For example, vacationers note that when moving from the lower to the upper beach, you will have to pay twice for using sun loungers.

People who have diseases of the cardiovascular system, musculoskeletal system and peripheral nervous system can come here to prevent various ailments and to heal. Guests will be offered a variety of massages, treatment with local mud and mineral waters, physiotherapy, laser therapy, etc.
