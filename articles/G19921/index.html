How to connect bluetooth to the car radio

Above, we examined the more expensive and multifunctional models of the Pioneer brand rulers. They have shown themselves perfectly in business and have an excellent balance of price and quality. But what about those who cannot afford to fork out for a high-quality, albeit expensive thing with integrated wireless protocols?

For such cases, there is a special bluetooth adapter for the car radio, which supports almost any ‚ÄúAndroid‚Äù device. The gadget works from a standard cigarette lighter and has almost all the functions that older devices from the list above have: streaming audio tracks, built-in microphone, speakerphone and voice control.

Such devices work by a simple and understandable principle:

  • connect your mobile gadget to the adapter as a regular headset;
  • on the adapter itself, set a specific wave on which it will work;
  • then, on the car radio, tune the indicated wave and enjoy your favorite song through bluetooth.
