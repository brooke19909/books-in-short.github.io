How to open IIS - a step-by-step description, methods and recommendations

In the article we will consider how to open IIS.

Many people think about how best to invest money in order to increase it in a couple of years. Some want to start their own business, others trust bank deposits, others invest in cryptocurrencies. However, there is a safer and more effective method of investing - an individual investment account. Consider its features, advantages and disadvantages.

It will tell you how to open IIS and where to do it better.

What is and how does it work?

IMS is understood as a type of brokerage account with a reward from the state for long-term deposits. If the deposit remains open for three years, you can get from it a 13% tax deduction or 52,000 rubles in cash.

The idea is quite simple. The investor has a certain amount of money. He does not want to own his own business; instead, he wants to engage in trade. Acquires valuable assets, waits for them to rise in price. After some time, the market value rises, and the investor can make a profit.

People often ask where it is better to open IIS. A comparison of brokers will be given below.

Promotion of the state: what are the benefits?

Understanding people immediately have a question: why such actions are encouraged by the state, and it exempts from tax payments - its main income? But in fact, both parties benefit.

Large finances revolve in the state, but they are held by individuals, and the country cannot legally dispose of them. If rubles are not included in circulation, they will soon lose their value due to inflation.

The solution of economic problems

For this reason, ISS makes it possible to simultaneously solve many economic problems:

  • help the economy grow through the influx of new funds;
  • teach citizens how to manage money and invest rationally;
  • increase demand for domestic rubles, futures, stocks, etc .;
  • open civil income for tax inspection.

Both parties remain in a favorable position:

  • the country receives an influx of new financial resources;
  • the investor learns to increase his own savings, is actively involved in the financial world.

Why is it worth opening an IMS (individual investment account)?

Deduction Methods

There are only two ways to get your benefits:

  • Salary deductible account. It is necessary to provide information on the payment of 13% tax on official salaries, then personal income tax returns up to 52,000 rubles. The maximum investment amount is no more than a million per year.
  • Account with a deduction on income. When the deposit is closed, it is not necessary to pay 13% to the total treasury. Thus, at the end of the term, all income is withdrawn, without the payment of personal income tax.

The amount of money earned in the future depends on the right choice. Therefore, it is advisable to obtain the advice of a brokerage specialist before concluding an agreement. He will analyze the official salary, profitable strategies, risks, and based on this information he will advise you to choose one or another way.

All brokerage companies have the opportunity to provide advice free of charge.

Advantages and disadvantages, is it worth discovering?

In the financial sector, the rule applies: the risk of burnout is greater, the higher the profit. In banks, a very low percentage due to increased reliability, the state and the insurance company additionally guarantee payment obligations:

  • Return personal income tax. The country encourages the purchase of its securities, the investor is exempt from tax. 13% are extremely important, thanks to them you can return a large part of the profit.
  • The potential profit every year up to 50%. The increase in the amount is largely determined by the choice of securities. Just the same, investors are counting on their growth. If you choose the asset correctly, then within six months you can withdraw without problems 15%. When trading without any particular risks, on average for the year they receive plus 12% of the investments made initially.
  • More profitable bank conditions. Banks offer customers deposits at 6-7% per annum. A positive return on risk-free manipulations with assets is on average 12%, a tax deduction is also added to this (approximately 3.5% of the total amount), and 15% is deducted by net profit.
  • Free choice of assets. Each stock exchange player has an independent choice in which projects to invest in and at what time to close the deal. Active players and professionals consider this an important aspect. Thanks to constant observation, you can open or close positions at any convenient moment, and therefore you can always stay in the black.
  • Reliable debt to the country. The banking sector, as you know, is one of the most favorite places for thieves and scammers. A bank can often close, go bankrupt. And almost all the savings burn with him. The state prints money itself, and therefore it easily repays the debt to the depositor.
  • The percentage surpasses inflation. For 2018, inflation in Russia is planned at about 4.5%, the maximum value is up to 6%. In bank deposits, after a year of investment, the amounts practically do not increase, losing their value. Income from investments not only allows you to cover the depreciation, but also brings at least another 7% profit.

However, this type of investment also has characteristic disadvantages. There are only three of them:

  • Exchange risk. Even if the idea seems very promising, assets can become cheaper due to the influence of a large number of factors. However, here the risk can be compensated by trusting professionals and analyzing the market situation.
  • Long term. To get benefits, you do not need to use your investments for 36 months.
  • Payment intermediary. This drawback applies only to active players. With a fairly frequent reinvestment, the exchange will take its minimum interest from each bet - 0.017. These little things with frequent transactions are transformed into a significant loss.

If we compare the positive and negative sides, we can conclude that giving money to the state on loan is more profitable than entrusting it to the bank system.

Return personal income tax

Getting personal income tax has a very simple algorithm:

  • Collection of documentation. We need a certificate of availability of an IIS at the exchange, a certificate of 2-NDFL, a 3-NDFL declaration in full, a passport. All this is drawn up at the tax office.
