Trip to Vietnam: Nya Chang - resort city

One of the most economical, but at the same time quality vacations offers tourists Vietnam. Nya Chang, without exaggeration, can be called the most popular resort in the country. This city is located in the central part of Vietnam. It is washed by the waters of the South China Sea.



It should be noted that Nya Chang became famous among Russian tourists. And the Vietnamese quickly adapted to this. Local residents already speak Russian main phrases, signs, stands and billboards are duplicated in our language.

Compatible business with pleasure

Here you can not only enjoy yourself in the warm subequatorial sun, which the rest of Vietnam can boast of. Nya Chang is a city of clubs, bars, restaurants and discos. In addition, there are wonderful massage and beauty salons. In their arsenal, they have mud therapy and thalassotherapy. You can also go diving here. The city has its own cultural monuments. This is Langshon Pagoda , Monkey Island, various temples. In general, there is where to go.

In the city of Nya Chang (Vietnam), excursions can be booked almost everywhere. The local Oceanographic Museum is particularly popular. It contains a huge collection - more than 20,000 copies - sea skeletons and living representatives of tropical ocean waters. However, no matter how many Nya Chang (Vietnam) sights are stored , the reviews of tourists about it mainly relate to beach vacations.

Climate Nya Chang

The beach season lasts in this city all year round, but it is divided into low and high. In winter and summer, the average air temperature is kept at 25 degrees. Even at night, it almost never drops below 22. In rare cases, the thermometer in the dark reaches 10. In the summer, it is still hotter than in the winter months. Like the whole of central Vietnam, Nya Chang is a favorable bathing area.



The "low" season is called autumn and the first month of winter. At this time, the weather is very rainy. Precipitation is intense, but short-lived. Therefore, they do not bring much discomfort to vacationers. True, from mid-October to mid-November , the storm season in Vietnam . Vouchers at this time are cheaper, but it is better not to go, so as not to spoil your vacation. High season - the rest of the time. Rains are rare. And certainly nothing will prevent you from enjoying the beauties of nature and all the benefits of the sub-equatorial area.



Local kitchen

The world famous cuisine, which welcomes visitors to Vietnam. Nya Chang, whose culinary reviews are not so rare, is known for its delicacies. A few words should also be said about this. Vietnamese cuisine is a symbiosis of Indian, Chinese and French traditions. Especially popular are seafood cooked with special spices, fruits and vegetables. Tourists can also taste local delicacies at relatively low prices. This is the meat of a python, a frog, a dog, an ostrich, etc. Tourists need to keep in mind that restaurants and cafes on the main streets and in tourist areas are much more expensive than in other parts of the city. Therefore, in order to save money, you can simply turn into a small lane and eat the same, but for less money.
