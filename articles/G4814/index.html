Shushary (Slavyanka) - a comfortable place to live

Now, many of those who want to live in St. Petersburg, when asked about which region they would prefer to have an apartment in, answer: "In the village of Shushary, in Slavyanka." This is a modern, comfortable, beautiful, new district of the suburb of St. Petersburg.

Not so long ago

Just some two or three decades ago, between Leningrad and the city of Pushkin there was a very small village Shushary. For the most part the workers of the state farm of the same name lived in it. The village had its own school, a kindergarten, a hotel and a cultural center with a swimming pool. Nearby was another state farm - "Lensoviet". As time goes on, Leningrad, which became St. Petersburg in 1991, is growing, because so many people strive to live in the Northern capital - the cultural center of Russia. And on January 6, 2006, by Decree of the Government of St. Petersburg, a decision was made to create a new residential neighborhood Slavyanka in Shushary. It is not far from the city of Pushkin and Lensovetovsky. So the southern suburb of the metropolis began to actively develop, which received the address: St. Petersburg, Shushary, Slavyanka.





New buildings

For the development of the new district, 220 hectares of land were provided, which are already fully developed. The village of Shushary, Slavyanka in particular, has become one of the large-scale construction projects to create a comfortable residential zone, with the planned infrastructure. More than 1.5 million m 2 of residential space has already been built on the territory of the new residential area, most of which has been purchased by the Ministry of Defense for the implementation of the Housing for Military Service project. But Slavyanka is not just a sleeping area, but a real city, because there are 3 schools and 7 preschool educational institutions in the microdistrict. There is a shopping and entertainment center and a lot of shops of various subjects. The map "Slavyanka and Shushary" is proposed below. But since the neighborhood is constantly being built, its infrastructure is improving.

How to get to Slavyanka

An unusual, new, large-scale residential area in the village of Shushary in Slavyanka, near St. Petersburg. But how to get to it, if you want to buy an apartment there or just see how this suburb is equipped? The geographical location of Slavyanka is near the city of Pushkin, between the Moscow and Kolpinsky highways. Therefore, getting to Slavyanka is very easy with your own car. Buses run from public transport to Slavyanka - from Pushkin and Shushar. Therefore, when arriving in St. Petersburg and planning to visit the Slavyanka residential area, you must first get to the village of Shushary or to the city of Pushkin, for example, by a suburban train from Vitebsky Station, and then transfer to one of the buses or minibuses that go to Slavyanka .
