Details on how to draw crystals

Today we will talk about how to draw a crystal with a pencil. Next, we will provide step-by-step instructions. The operation described below does not require additional knowledge, so anyone can handle them. However, everything is in order.

Instruction manual





We proceed to the practical part of solving the question of how to draw crystals. First we need to determine the shape of the future geometric shape. There will be several options. Our task is to draw a group of crystals, which will allow us to get an incredibly beautiful composition. We depict three large figures. Breaking them carelessly in straight lines. We move on to the next step in resolving the issue of how to draw crystals, and add a few small geometric shapes below. We supply them with corners. Parties should also be straight. Moreover, the shape of the figure does not have to be correct.

Borders

We move on to the next step in resolving the issue of how to draw crystals. The foundation is already ready. Now we clearly outline the boundaries of each of the crystals. Do it with a pencil. Next, add the faces. They should consist of straight lines only. In this case, it is necessary to ensure that these elements form equal right sides. So, already the general form is drawn. Now move on to coloring. The shape of the crystals is filled in blue. To the bottom, it should gradually darken.

Finishing touches



We pass to the final stage of solving the question of how to draw crystals. It is necessary to determine the source of light. We turn to lightening with blue and white shades. Thus we emphasize the lit side. Surfaces that are extremely turned towards the light should have the lightest shade. Add the corresponding lines to the brightened edges. The described techniques add to the image a more realistic effect. Also, one should not forget about reflexes.

Crystals interact with each other. The light from each figure extends to neighboring ones. Add some dark spots. Go to the next step. On each face, apply a bright white shade. These places reflect light the most. It remains to make the necessary color correction. Done.

As you can see, the work does not have particularly difficult stages. A tool kit is also standard. To create such an image, only an eraser and pencils are useful. Also, at some stages you can use a black marker, although its absence is quite acceptable.
