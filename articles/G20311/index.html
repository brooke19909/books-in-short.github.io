Pattern of a dog from felt - the basis of your favorite toy or decor item

Dogs and bears are an indispensable attribute of the world of toys from the childhood of each person. You can buy such a toy everywhere now. But it is better to make it with your own hands, to put all the love and tenderness into it. The pattern of a dog from felt will help to quickly cope with the idea.





Comfortable material

Until recently, felt was used as a material for the manufacture of hats and warm shoes. But the development of textile production has allowed this material to become one of the most sought-after for creativity and needlework. Now felt is made in different thicknesses and colors. With it, you can do almost any thing - from a hat to a functional basket. There is no need to talk about toys - this is one of the most convenient materials for the manufacture of textile products .

It is soft and durable, does not cause irritation, is easy to use - it is easy to process, does not spill, it does not need an overlock to protect sections from destruction. That is why even beginner needlewomen love to sew felt toys. A dog, the pattern of which is proposed just below, can become a favorite of both the child and the whole family, settling on the couch.





How to sew felt toys

Felt is a very convenient and practical material, because the absence of the need for processing slices saves a lot of time and thread. Felt toys can be sewn in different ways, because it is a pliable fabric. For sewing seams using both machine and manual stitching. Seams can be made both on the front and on the wrong side. Since the felt is not strewed, the edge of the seam can be made small. Dogs made of felt, the patterns of which are very different, are designed by craftsmen or invented independently. They will become successful crafts that will decorate the interior, will be your favorite toy, a key ring, a fridge magnet.





3D toy

A stitched dog made of felt with your own hands can become a favorite toy for a child. With patterns developed by the craftswomen, this work will become quite simple. Here is a diagram of parts for sewing a terrier. To begin with, the pattern must be cut out of paper. Then lay out on the fabric, given that the barrel and tummy are sewn from two mirrored parts. It is also necessary to provide an allowance of 3-5 mm at the seams.

The toy is sewn in the following sequence:

  • the upper part of the head (forehead, ears, nape) is first fastened with one barrel, then with another, trying to combine the common points of contact of all the details as accurately as possible - nose and neck;
  • two parts of the tummy are stitched with the legs of the barrels;
  • back and tail are combined;
  • a toy through a hole in the tummy is stuffed with a synthetic winterizer;
  • the tummy connects.

All stages of work should be carried out as carefully as possible so that there are no distortions in the individual parts. When the toy is completely ready, you can arrange eyes and a spout on the face. Although, as an interior decoration, a ‚Äúfaceless‚Äù dog will look interesting.
