This hotel is a cottage complex in the Kalemya area. It is located on the first beach line. From the center of Fethiye to the hotel - only 6 kilometers. The hotel has 350 rooms, which are divided into 4 categories (A, B, C, E). All rooms have well-equipped bathrooms with showers. There is also a split system, flat-screen TV, safe and minibar. The Hillside Beach Club has 2 restaurants, bars, a conference room and a business center, as well as an amphitheater, a disco bar, ample opportunities for practicing various sports, an outdoor pool, a sauna, a Turkish bath, etc. The hotel has its own small pebble beach. but very well maintained, well equipped with all the necessary beach equipment.

Tourists who rested in this hotel write in their reviews that they have not seen such beauty anywhere in the world, that from the very first minute they had the feeling that they were in real Eden.
