The farmstead "Sosnovy Bor", Volgograd: description, service, reviews

You should definitely come here to someone who wants to visit this fairy tale, escape from the bustle of everyday life and feel all the features of ancient Russian folk life. The estate "Sosnovy Bor" vacationers call a place of bliss, with all the features for a pleasant relaxing holiday: log houses with a bathhouse, a Russian stove, restaurants that correspond to the best traditional Russian traditions. This secluded magical corner with its special atmosphere of the Russian spirit is located some 10 minutes drive from the center of Volgograd.

Acquaintance

The restaurant and hotel complex "Sosnovy Bor" (Volgograd) is an ideal place for a family holiday or leisure activities with friends. According to reviews, in this oasis of untouched nature and primordial Russian hospitality, you can come for the weekend and really relax here with your body and soul.

Location

The estate "Sosnovy Bor", made in the old Russian style and allowing vacationers to immerse themselves in the atmosphere of Russian folk tales, is located at: Volgograd, st. Angarsk 137/181. Distances from the recreation center are:

  • to the railway station: 10 km;
  • to the center of Volgograd: 6.7 km;

  • to r. Volga: 7.7 km.

Manor "Sosnovy Bor": how to get there?

Those who decide to come here in a personal car, experts advise:

  1. In Volgograd move along the street. M. Rokossovsky, then turn onto st. Angarskaya and go straight to the recreation center (there will be a pond nearby).

Manor "Sosnovy Bor": description

According to reviews, in this secluded place, decorated in the style of a Russian fairy tale, vacationers are immersed in a special atmosphere of the presence of the Russian spirit, supported by the presence of wooden huts and traditional folk costumes, aromas of Russian cuisine and sincere feast songs.

The estate "Sosnovy Bor" (Volgograd) is perfect for a carefree, relaxing holiday. At any time of the year, guests will find restaurants and banquet rooms offering a wide selection of delicious dishes of Russian cuisine cooked in a real Russian oven, and meals to the intimate accompaniment of folklore ensembles. In addition, in the estate "Sosnovy Bor" for vacationers organized sleigh rides or in a carriage drawn by thoroughbred horses, as well as other entertainments.

If desired, guests can live in one of seven 4-bed houses built of environmentally friendly pine wood. The decoration of each of the houses was created in accordance with the best Russian traditions. In any of them, vacationers can relax in their own bathhouse and bathe with mineral water from a well located on the estate's grounds.

According to reviews, the estate "Sosnovy Bor" has everything you need for a decent and quality vacation with family, friends or colleagues. In the restaurant and hotel complex, everything is filled with love, care and domestic warmth. The complex is considered one of the best establishments adapted for accommodation on vacation (No. 3 of the available housing options of a special type in Volgograd).
