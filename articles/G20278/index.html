Holiday Village "Skiff"

One of the budget vacation options on the Sea of ‚Äã‚ÄãAzov is the recreation center "Skif" in Rozhka. This option of spending summer time is suitable for everyone, because the price for living in small cottage-type houses is very low. There are 4 beds in the houses. The facilities of the base are located on the territory and apply to all categories of rooms. Several options include a fridge and a TV. In total, Skif can accommodate 50 adult guests. It is important to consider that this base accepts vacationers only in the summer. Heating of the houses is not provided.

The price per person per day is 1200 rubles.

There is a recreation center at the address: ul. P.E. Prikhodko, house number 3.

The recreation center "Vodnik"

Among the recreation centers of the Rozhka, an interesting pension called "Vodnik" has established itself. This type of rest is quite comfortable.

  • Firstly, among the places of residence not only small houses, but also a brick three-story building with various types of rooms.
  • Secondly, the number of rooms is very diverse. Guests are offered 2, 3 and 4-bed suites, junior suites and economy options.
  • Thirdly, from May to October, the base can accommodate more than 60 guests in a landscaped area with its own sandy beach and the necessary beach infrastructure - changing rooms, sun loungers, towels, a shower, a cafe, a barbecue area and a shop.
  • Fourth, the recreation center offers three meals a day, which is included in the price. The cooks of Vodnik will delight guests with original Don cuisine.
  • Fifth, a professional team of animators will entertain children and adults with any preferences. They hold contests, quests and fun discos.

In addition, an additional option of the base is a cultural program for an additional payment. This includes excursions to Chekhov places, and a visit to the water park and dolphinarium.

The cost of living in a boarding house varies from 950 to 1250 rubles per day (with meals), an extra bed for children from 3 to 14 years old costs 600 rubles, children up to 3 years old have a free rest.

The address of the recreation center in Rozhka "Vodnik": Shkolny lane, house number 2.

Holiday Village "Wave"

A miniature area with five small unique houses made of wood and brick is presented as a recreation center Rozhka, accommodating 20 adult guests. This option is ideal for people who want to celebrate an unforgettable wedding or anniversary. The room fund will comfortably accommodate all guests who wish to stay overnight.

On the territory there is a beautiful and roomy summer gazebo where you can hold a celebration or just have a snack with friends or family. The shared kitchen has everything you need for preparing various dishes. A modern brazier will help prepare delicious dishes on charcoal.

The recreation center service includes:

  • parking;
  • playground;
  • sand beach;
  • air conditioning (in each room);
  • satellite TV (in each room);
  • refrigerator;
  • shared kitchen;
  • heated shower in the yard;
  • table for tennis;
  • table for billiards;
  • meals by appointment.

The cost of rest in the Wave varies from 900 to 1,500 rubles per person per day.

The address of the recreation center: 1a Shkolny lane.

Hotels and pensions

For lovers of all-inclusive options in the farm resort is a family-run mini-hotel "Antik" and the sanatorium boarding house type "Star".

These versions of accommodation for vacationers are characterized by a high level of convenience and service. Guests are provided with everything necessary for an unforgettable and comfortable luxury vacation.

Pensions, hotels and recreation centers in Rozhka in Taganrog are popular not only among residents of the Rostov Region, but also in neighboring regions, as well as residents of the DPR.
