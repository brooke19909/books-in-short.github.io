Hotel Nicolas Color Hotel 3 * (Ayia Napa, Cyprus): photos and reviews of tourists

Nicolas Color Hotel 3 * looks original and bright, and this attracts the attention of travelers from all over the world. In addition, a good impression is complemented by comfortable rooms and high quality service.





A bit about the hotel

This hotel was founded in 2005. Thanks to the original design and convenient location, he quickly fell in love with tourists. In 2010, major repairs were carried out, as a result of which worn out funds were updated. Today the hotel is a combination of three-story buildings with elevators. In total, the institution has 100 rooms.





Location

In the heart of the popular resort, 300 meters from the well-maintained beach, the Nicolas Color Hotel 3 * (Cyprus, Ayia Napa) is located. To get here from Larnaca International Airport, you have to overcome a little less than 60 km. Paphos Air Harbor is 180 km away. And just 12 km away is the famous resort of Protaras.

Given the convenient location of Nicolas Color Hotel 3 *, it is often chosen by active travelers. Nearby attractions and tourist attractions include:

  • National Museum of Thalassa (300 m);
  • Ayia Napa Monastery (500 m);
  • City Information Office (600 m);
  • water park (5 km);
  • Cavo Greco National Park (6 km);
  • Protaras Aquarium (7 km);
  • Botanical Garden (17 km).

Description of rooms

All rooms of Nicolas Color Hotel 3 * correspond to the superior category. The original design looks modern, while at the same time it is home-like cozy. Each of the rooms has an individual design based on a successful combination of muted or bright colors. Properly selected color scheme has a relaxing effect on guests. It is worth noting the predominance in the design of natural materials.

Guests can use twin beds that can be pushed together. All rooms are designed to accommodate 2 guests. There is also the possibility of an additional adult guest. Some rooms have bunk beds for children, and there is also a spacious niche with shelves for personal belongings of guests.

Of the additional options, it is worth noting the presence of a desk and a cozy sitting area with a soft armchair. A window covering the entire wall provides good penetration of sunlight. The balcony has a table and a pair of chairs.





Room Amenities

The administration of Nicolas Color Hotel 3 * is carefully and responsibly responsible for ensuring the comfort of guests. For this purpose, the rooms include the following amenities:

  • individually controlled air conditioning;
  • centrally controlled heating system;
  • private furnished balcony (some rooms have a terrace);
  • a bathroom equipped with a washbasin and shower, as well as a hairdryer and bath accessories;
  • mini fridge for storing food and drinks;
  • radio with local music stations;
  • TV with satellite channels (a connector for a flash drive is provided);
  • a safe, the use of which is possible for an additional fee;
  • telephone with the ability to access the international line;
  • Wireless Internet signal
  • smoke detector to ensure fire safety.

  • outdoor swimming pool;
  • terrace with sun loungers and parasols;
  • indoor swimming pool;

Vacation with children

For families, many travelers choose the Nicolas Color Hotel 3 * hotel. Cyprus is characterized by very favorable conditions for children. For young tourists, the following features are provided:

  • children's pool with a mini-slide and a large number of inflatable toys;
  • outdoor play area (safe cover, swing, sandpit and other attractions);
  • early breakfast, which includes a large selection of dairy products;
  • the restaurant has special high chairs for comfortable feeding;
  • going on an excursion or wanting to devote time to yourself, you can leave the kids under the supervision of a nanny;
  • animation program for children;
  • mini disco after dinner.

Nutrition

  • three meals a day in the main restaurant (buffet is served with a large selection of international dishes);
  • night cooking shows;
  • Until 21:00 in the pool bar you can enjoy your favorite drinks, as well as light refreshments;
  • throughout the day, guests are treated to ice cream.

Positive reviews

To appreciate all the positive moments that await you during your vacation, it is worthwhile to get acquainted with the opinions of travelers about Nicolas Color Hotel 3 * in advance. Reviews allow you to talk about the following advantages of this institution:

  • beautiful clean area;
  • a good playground for children with a lot of interesting entertainment;
  • no chlorine smell is felt in the pool (more modern and less aggressive substances are used for disinfection);
  • helpful and friendly staff;
  • an electric kettle can be provided in the room upon request;
  • in the immediate vicinity of the hotel there is a large amusement park;
  • satisfactory quality of room cleaning;
  • high-quality double-glazed windows protect the room from street noise;
  • TV shows 7 Russian-language channels;
  • beautiful design of rooms and the hotel as a whole;
  • excellent quality of food in the restaurant;
  • those who eat on the All Inclusive system are guaranteed a varied menu that is constantly updated;
  • powerful air conditioning in the rooms;

Negative reviews

If you find out about the possible negative points, they will not be able to spoil your stay at the Nicolas Color Hotel 3 * (Cyprus). Reviews pay attention to such disadvantages:

  • children's animators practically do not pay attention to kids, and therefore parents have to entertain them on their own;
  • the restaurant has a schedule of entertainment events, but in fact they are almost never held;
  • the beach closest to the hotel is too crowded; moreover, it is strewn with a lot of sharp stones;
  • upon check-in, guests must pay a sufficiently large security deposit (they also take a security deposit for each piece of equipment separately);
  • there are no coolers with free drinking water on the territory (you need to buy it yourself or type in the dining room during breakfast);
  • during dinner, all drinks are paid and are quite expensive (for example, you will have to pay about 3 euros for a cup of coffee);
  • the hotel does not have superior apartments (all rooms correspond to one category);
  • the partition in the shower is small, and therefore the water pours onto the floor during bathing;
  • small tables in the dining room;
  • slow work of waiters in a restaurant;
  • cramped rooms;
  • breakfast menu is constantly repeated;
  • stocks of cosmetics in the bathroom do not update even after a complaint about the reception;
  • for using the refrigerator you need to pay 3 euros per day (this despite the fact that the cost of living is also rather big).

The hotel attracts travelers with its original colorful design and convenient location. Nevertheless, many tourists, accustomed to a high level of service, criticize this institution. There are many significant shortcomings that can ruin the mood of vacationers. It is also worth noting the inflated prices for accommodation and meals in the restaurant.
