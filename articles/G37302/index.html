Hotels of New Athos (Abkhazia): reviews of tourists

New Athos is a city located in the Gudauta district, near the Black Sea. Located near the Russian border (80 km), this resort is popular among Russian tourists. For guests in the resort area there are various mini-hotels. New Athos (Abkhazia) is one of the oldest settlements in the country. However, it is more commonly known as Anacopia. Initially, this city was the capital of the possession of the kings of Abkhazia, but in 1032 it passed to the Byzantines. In the Middle Ages, the Genoese ruled here, which were later replaced by the Turks. Toward the end of the 19th century, Russian monks settled here, who organized the construction of the New Athos Monastery. During the Soviet Union, the cottage of I.V. Stalin was built here.



Accommodation and infrastructure

Hotels in New Athos (Abkhazia) are not so common. Nevertheless, the sanatoriums and small hotels built here are distinguished by special Caucasian hospitality and a decent level of service. As a rule, the list of services that the hotels of New Athos (Abkhazia) offer does not include medical and recreational procedures. However, the healing air and the beneficial properties of sea water help to significantly improve health without it. In the resort area there are several boarding houses that Abkhazia is famous for: private hotels "New Athos" and "At the Monastery". Popular hotel "Waterfall" and KK "Kumarit". Many guests are attracted by the Griffin boarding house. Hotel "New Athos" (Abkhazia) is considered the hallmark of the resort. Also, people have a pleasant experience after staying at the Eagle's Nest hotel. All hotels of New Athos (Abkhazia) have all kinds of entertainment centers, visits that make the vacation even more memorable and eventful. In addition, the city has two markets, a large number of shops, a city hospital and a post office. In order to enjoy your vacation on the Black Sea coast, just buy a direct ticket to the resort and settle in one of the comfortable rooms of any New Athos hotel. Pension "Griffin"

This hotel was built recently - in 2012. But during this relatively short period it has become a favorite vacation spot for many tourists. Hotel "Griffin" is located in the heart of New Athos, on the banks of the river. Psyrtsha. From the boarding house to the Black Sea only a few minutes. Very close to it are unique historical sights. Tourists, in particular, will be able to visit the New Athos Monastery, the Anakopia National Cultural and Historical Reserve, the Temple of Simon Kanonit, the cave complex and the waterfall. And right in front of the hotel is the Tsarskaya Alley resort complex.





Boarding house infrastructure

The original 4-storey hotel building and the surrounding area are surrounded by a picturesque landscape. The hotel itself is made of red brick and wood. On the top floor there is a small observation deck. It offers breathtaking views of the Apsara and Iver Mountains. On the ground floor of the hotel there is a cafe-dining room and a summer cafe, where guests can appreciate the excellent taste of national dishes. Just a few minutes walk from the Griffin boarding house is a pebble beach with pebbles of medium and small sizes. For vacationers, changing cabins are provided. In addition, tourists can take a boat trip. It is worth noting that there are no sunbeds on the beach.
