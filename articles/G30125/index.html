They will require:

  • durable yarn: linen, cotton, silk
  • solid base - outsole
  • fishing line

DIY knitted boots require careful preparation. Before starting work, it is necessary to very carefully take measurements from the leg: circle the foot, measure the leg lift, and best of all make a template for the pattern.

Next, you need to decide how your boots will be made: knitting or crocheting?
