How to get to Domodedovo by Aeroexpress

In order to be in time for the start of check-in for your flight, you should think about how to get to Domodedovo by aeroexpress.

From the Paveletsky station

The main advantage of Aeroexpress is that rail transport does not depend on the presence of traffic jams on the Warsaw and Kashirskoye Highways leading to Domodedovo. In terms of reliability, this type of transport is beyond competition. Therefore, for most air passengers, the question of how to get to Domodedovo is no longer a question . By aeroexpress, the journey to the airport takes an average of forty-five minutes. It is not a problem to get to the platform from which the electric train leaves. It is located at the Paveletsky station, which, as you know, is tied to two stations of the Moscow metro - Paveletskaya-ring and Paveletskaya-radial. The exit from the Zamoskvoretskaya line metro station is carried out directly to the station building, right next to the ticket offices, where you can buy an Aeroexpress ticket. Domodedovo today is one of the most important areas with which the communication of this ancient Moscow station. Its functioning is largely oriented towards servicing air passengers.



Timeline

Rationally drawn up and schedule. Aeroexpress (Domodedovo, thanks to it, becomes easily accessible to passengers throughout most of the day) has a convenient work schedule. Electric trains depart from the platform of the Paveletsky station every half hour, and this schedule is maintained from six in the morning until half past one in the morning, which roughly corresponds to the subway train schedule. Electric trains are sent clearly at the beginning and middle of each hour. Such accuracy is very convenient, as it saves time and allows the passenger to accurately calculate how to get to Domodedovo by express train just in time for check-in. The only inconvenience here is a long break in traffic from half past midnight to six in the morning. This forces passengers to leave for the airport in advance or take a taxi, which, incidentally, is easy to do, because there are no traffic jams on Moscow highways at night.



Fare

When a passenger is faced with a choice of how to get to Domodedovo - by Aeroexpress or other means of transport, the decisive factors are time and reliability. The passenger is ready to pay for the assurance that he will not be late to the airport. Therefore, the fare cannot be called low. The base ticket price is 400 rubles. Children under five years of age travel for free; the ticket price for children under the age of seven is 130 rubles. Most passengers are satisfied with this level of tariff rates . At the same time, there is a certain system of bonuses and discounts that stimulate regular customers to use this type of transport.



Business Class

The cost of a trip to Domodedovo Airport in conditions of increased comfort is 900 rubles. The business class passenger gets a fixed seat in the passenger compartment, decorated according to the standards of the "luxury" category. It should be noted that in the reviews, many Aeroexpress passengers express reasonable bewilderment at the price of the ticket, considering the fare in such a car to be inappropriate to the declared class.
