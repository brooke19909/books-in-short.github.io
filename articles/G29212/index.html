How to register in the "My Account" "Megaphone"? "Megaphone": entrance to the "My Account"

Any modern person uses cell phone services almost every day. It is hard to believe that a few years ago everyone did without mobile phones. Then these small devices with black and white screens seemed something surprising. They could be used exclusively for calls and sending SMS messages. Today the market is filled with smartphones - phones that perform the functions of a small personal computer. At the same time, the list of opportunities that mobile operators provide their subscribers is expanding. Struggling for each client, they offer many tariff plans, additional services and options: paid and free. Their list is constantly updated, conditions, cost are changing. In order to learn how to navigate this variety, as well as to control their balance, a subscriber can register on the official website of his cellular company by receiving a password for his personal account. The well-known telecom operator Megafon has a similar system called the Service Guide. Any subscriber can use it absolutely free. How to register in your MegaFon account? Before answering this question, we will understand why this should be done.





Personal account (Service-Guide system): opportunities

Despite the fact that there is no additional charge for registering in your personal account, not all subscribers use this procedure. Why? Most likely, many simply don‚Äôt know what opportunities Megafon opens up. The Personal Account service allows you to manage your account without leaving your home or office, from any computer with Internet access. In particular, you can change the current tariff plan, connect or refuse any service or option, get information about the balance status, receipts and expenditures of funds, find out about the number of bonuses accrued, and temporarily block the number. And this is an incomplete list of actions that can be performed using the self-service service "Megaphone" "My Account". Signing up for it is very simple.



Official site "Megaphone"

First of all, you should open the official website of the mobile operator Megafon. The entrance to your personal account is located on its main page in the upper right corner. You can find the desired site by entering the name of the cellular company in the search or address bar of the browser. Having opened the main page, you should pay attention to the region indicated in it and, if necessary, change it. Each republic or region has its own Megafon website. After that, you can proceed directly to the registration procedure.

Personal account (Service Guide system): registration procedure

How to register in your MegaFon account? First you need to click on the "Personal Account" button, located on the website of the mobile operator in your region. After that, the subscriber gets to the page where he should fill in three fields. This is a password, username and security code. Each of them is a must. Login is a phone number. You can find out by dialing * 205 # from the Megafon SIM card. Security code - a combination of numbers shown in the picture. There are several ways to get access to the Service Guide system.



Mobile operator "Megaphone": personal account, password for entering the system

There are three ways to access your MegaFon account. The first of these is as follows: on your mobile phone with a Megafon SIM card, you need to dial * 105 * 00 # and press the call button. The second involves sending a message with the text 00 to the number 000105. You can also use the "Get Password" service. In this case, you will need to indicate your phone number, verification number and click on the "Get Password" button. In all three cases, an SMS message with the necessary combination of numbers and letters will come.



Personal account (Service Guide system): password change

To start working in the Service Guide system, you need to click on the link on the Megafon website "Personal Account". Login is carried out after entering the password, in connection with which it is desirable to change it to a more convenient one. To do this, send the command * 105 * 01 # from your phone. Then you must follow all the instructions of the system and change the password.

Personal account (Service Guide system): password recovery

If the password for the Megafon self-service service "My Account" is lost, you can enter the system in three ways. The first is suitable for subscribers who have indicated information about the security question in the "Service Guide" settings. They can use the "Password Recovery" service. Here you need to specify the login (phone number), select the security question in the drop-down list and write an answer to it. After that, you need to enter the code shown in the picture, press the password recovery button and wait for the SMS message to be received on your phone. When concluding an agreement with Megafon, the subscriber indicates a valid email address. You can use it to confirm the password for the "My Account" service. And finally, the third way. It involves receiving a new password by sending a USSD command: * 105 * 00 #.



How to use your MegaFon account?

So, with how to register in your MegaFon account, everything is clear. Now let's figure out what to do next. You can use your personal account with this operator in several ways:

  • From a computer connected to the Internet: for this you need to open the official website of your cellular company and go to the "My Account" section.
  • From a mobile phone by sending a USSD command: the combination * 105 # after pressing the call button opens access to the main menu of the Service Guide system, here you can request information about the balance, enable or disable the service, change the tariff, set the lock on the SIM card .
  • Using service number 0505: you can manage your account by following the prompts of the autoinformer.
  • Using the video menu: access to it is via number 0505.
  • Through applications installed on a mobile phone: the necessary links are given on the official Megafon website in the "Support" section, the "Self-Service Services" item.

Where to contact if you have questions?

All clarifications on how to register in your MegaFon account can be obtained at the contact center of the telecom operator. Call should be on a short number 0500 (within the network) or 5077777 (for subscribers of other cellular companies). In addition, you can contact the nearest salon. Information on its exact location is on the website of the mobile operator. To do this, open the "Support" section and select "Contacts". A link appears below that opens a page with the addresses and opening hours of all the Megafon communication salons of the selected area.
