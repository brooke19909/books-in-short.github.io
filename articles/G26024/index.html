How to decorate a dress with your own hands: interesting ideas, stylish tips, photos

In the wardrobe of every woman there are two or three dresses that are stale. They are out of fashion, bored or look too trivial. Just throwing out once your favorite outfits is a pity. There is only one way out - to decorate the dress with your own hands. How to breathe new life into the product using improvised means? Interesting ideas are considered in the article.

How to decorate a dress with your own hands lace

For many years, lace has not left fashionable pedestals. Designers do not get tired of experimenting with it. Decorate the dress with your own hands with lace is a great idea. It is very easy to bring to life.

The easiest way to revitalize your outfit is to sheathe a lace neckline, sleeve edges or hem. Lace of contrasting colors will look spectacular on dark matter. To transform a light dress, it is better to prefer dark lace.

Girls who prefer a more complex solution can make a lace insert on the product. A small area should be cut on the sides or on the back. Then, from the wrong side, the selected openwork material is sewn. If the stitches look sloppy, you can hide them under a lace or satin ribbon.

Beads, rhinestones

Do you need to decorate the dress for the New Year with your own hands? An outfit decorated with beads will help to look festive and elegant. For example, you can sew it on the neckline. In this case, it is recommended to use large beads, as well as sew it with a wide strip. A beaded collar is another original solution. First you need to draw the shape of the collar, and then sheathe its surface in a dense row. Instead of beads, you can use bugles.

Rhinestones can also come to the rescue if you need to prepare for the New Year's party. To reduce the time of work, you can purchase ready-made applications from them in the store. A thin strip of glue is located on the back side. In order for the application to hold well, it is necessary to attach it to the selected area, cover it with a cloth and carefully iron it with an iron that has the function of steam. It is important that the iron barely touches the fabric, otherwise there is a danger of spoiling the outfit.

You can create a picture of rhinestones yourself. On a plain product, they can be glued in a chaotic manner.

Fur leather

If desired, you can decorate the dress with your own hands with fur. It is most convenient to give preference to fur ribbons, which are easy to find in hardware stores. With their help, you can arrange the bottom and sleeves of the product.

Leather inserts are also able to transform and revitalize a dress that has not been taken out of the closet for a long time. For example, you can cut a small piece of fabric on the back or on the sides. Then a piece of leather is sewn to this place. It can be not only smooth, but also textural, perforated with a variety of patterns.

Flowers

You can decorate the dress with your own flowers. One small element is enough to make the most inexpressive and simple outfit play with new colors. Flowers can be created using satin, organza, felt and so on. Spectacular jewelry can also be purchased at a sewing store.

It is very simple to make a beautiful organza flower. To do this, cut out several circles with different diameters. Then they should singe their edges, after which the elements can be sewn to each other. They should create the shape of a fluffy flower. Such decoration can be run along the hem or sewn along the edge of the neckline.

Satin ribbons are a great choice for those who are considering how to decorate the dress with their own hands. The photo below will tell you an interesting idea. The color of the tape does not have to coincide with the shade of the product. They can also contrast with it.

The contrast strip under the chest or at the waist will look spectacular. To create a romantic image, you need to tie it to an elegant bow. This method is loved by brides, reviving their wedding dresses with red, pink, gold and blue ribbons.

Black

There are outfits that no women's wardrobe can do without. This list also includes the black dress shown in the photo below. How to decorate such a product with your own hands? This can be done, for example, using lace. An interesting and simple solution is to decorate the neckline with lace ribbon. You can also make lace inserts that match the color of the product or contrast with it.

Beads of various sizes are another suitable decorative element. They can be placed along the neckline, sewn at the bottom of the hem or sleeves, on the belt. There is also a decor tool for the lazy - a scarf. A long and narrow product can be used as a belt.

Knitted

Knitted dress may also need additional decor. It represents an excellent base for creating an original outfit. The simplest solution is to sew on the product various knitted applications, say, flowers.

You can also sew a wide fabric insert on this element of the wardrobe, and then decorate it with large, beautiful buttons. Multi-colored braid, organza appliqu√©s, silk ribbons - all this can come in handy when creating a festive image.

Bead experiments

There is another decor tool that designers are actively using. Any woman can decorate the dress with beads with her own hands. Suppose you can focus on the neck. To carry out the work, special fabric pads will be required. It is most convenient to use collars on ties, since with their help you can then transform another dress.

The fabric must be dense enough to support the weight of the beads. A simple solution is to sew the same elements over the entire lining area. More complex schemes are welcome. Similarly, you can decorate the shoulders.

Stylish Tips

Caution is required when decorating a colored multi-colored dress. If such a product is decorated with a large number of all kinds of beads, rhinestones, stones and ribbons, then the result may be too colorful, provocative. Those who love an abundance of brilliance, it is better to experiment with plain wardrobe elements.

With the help of lace, you can not only enrich your image. When used correctly, it also helps highlight the virtues and hide the imperfections of the figure. Knowing the following secrets will help.

  • A great solution is wedge-shaped lace inserts in the waist area. With their help, you can lengthen the silhouette and become visually slimmer.
  • Owners of a small breast will come to the aid of an elegant lace frill. If, on the contrary, you want to reduce a large breast, then you should prefer an openwork insert in the chest area.
  • Lengthening the neck with lace will also help. It is necessary to make a small incision on the side of the neck, and then sew a small insert of thin lace material.

For a child

How to decorate a dress for a girl with your own hands? Reviving an outfit for a child is even easier than transforming an element of the wardrobe of an adult lady. The main thing is not to limit your imagination.

  • Funny pictures. It is difficult to find a child who does not like bright applications. The solid bodice of the dress will be transformed if it is decorated with a delicate flower, a charming butterfly, and delicious fruit. You can also opt for your favorite cartoon character girls. Application technology is very simple. They can be sewn around the edge with threads or printed with a hot iron. You should pay attention to this decision in a situation where you need to hide a stain or hole.
  • 3D applications. This is a newfangled invention that delights all children. Images seem voluminous.
  • Embroidery. If mom knows the needle and thread well, then you can independently create an original pattern on the dress. Invent something unique or use ready-made patterns for embroidery - everyone decides for himself. This decor method also allows you to hide the defect on clothing.
  • Do laces and ribbons also help decorate a children's dress with their own hands? These are the best friends of babies. With their help, you can issue a hem, collar, bottom of the sleeves. If desired, you can limit yourself to a silk ribbon, tied around the waist. Such an element will enliven the most boring dress.
  • Sequins, beads, rhinestones. Little girls are attracted to everything brilliant. You can sheathe neck with beautiful material, decorate the ribbon at the waist or bodice. An interesting solution is to decorate using the same elements and shoes.
  • Session of painting. You can purchase environmentally friendly paints designed for painting on fabric. Drawings are easy to create using stencils, but are also allowed to surrender to the will of their imagination.
  • Locks, buttons, zippers. If a girl does not like lace and ribbons, why not give preference to a more brutal decor. A unique image can be created if the rows of buttons and zippers-tricks are originally arranged on the product.

If the daughter wants to take an active part in decorating her dress, then do not hinder her in this. It is possible that it is a small child whose imagination is not limited by any framework that can create a truly unique decor.
