Sevastopol, the water park "Zurbagan" - reviews

Zurbagan - the "city of dreams", once invented by the writer Alexander Green, embodied in the Sevastopol Victory Park. Near the sea on the territory of two and a half hectares lies the Zurbagan water park. Sevastopol sights replenished with a world-class attraction.





Water entertainment complex

The unique water entertainment complex, the only one on the entire Crimean coast, offers visitors a whole gamut of interesting attractions for any age. A fountain 15 meters high, a central pool with a diameter of 30 meters, hot tubs, stylized waterfalls - all this is not a complete list of services offered. Waterpark "Zurbagan" is a member of the International Association of Water Parks around the world (WWA). The complex is in the first positions of the world rating according to several criteria, among which there is a good level of technical equipment, impeccable staff training and service to the best standards.

Among all the cities of the Crimean peninsula, the most visited is Sevastopol. Waterpark "Zurbagan", a photo of which is in all directories of the Crimean coast, is one of the most important attractions. A special pride of the leadership of the water park are seven pools with slides installed in them. A variety of slopes, straight and wavy, steep and gentle - each visitor will find a suitable attraction. Children are allowed only accompanied by adults, but from this childish delight does not become less.





Slides for the little ones

"Rabbit", "Octopus", "Elephant" and "Snake" are slides for the smallest, parents also participate in the descent, mom sends the baby on a trip, and dad meets the child below. A fountain beats on the Octopus slide, and the children, before moving downstairs, play with transparent jets for a long time. Slope "Elephant" is a water copy of ordinary slides, similar to the elephant, which are in all yards, in all playgrounds. But the water flowing down the gutter in a stormy stream is a completely different matter, and the girls and boys are immensely happy, moving down the hill. Under the "Elephant" pool, 40 centimeters deep, where you can splash around, there is an instructor right there, who, together with the baby's parents, watches the games. Children's waterslides are a whole world full of pleasure and adventure.

Activities for older children

For older children, descent from the slopes "Free fall", "Rainbow" and "Children's bodyslide" is organized. The height of each slide is 3 meters, and the depth of the pool is about 80 centimeters. Children can compete with each other, swim, dive from the side or play surface balloons. Under the guidance of an instructor, you can create a team and play water polo. Children's water games are varied and quite interesting, sometimes adults also connect to them.





Attractions for adults

Waterpark "Zurbagan" provides a range of entertainment for adults. The main attraction is the central pool, an ideal place to relax after extreme jumps and descents on ski slides. In the pool, you can simply swim or slowly swim in warm water. The water temperature is maintained within 28-30 degrees, which makes it possible to splash on the side even small children, under the supervision of parents.

After the pool, adult visitors again go to the slides, the water park "Zurbagan" offers a complex of five attractions, different in height and complexity. The easiest slide is Free Fall, it is ideal for beginners and lovers of a relaxing pastime. For those who prefer dynamic exercises with extreme elements, the Bodyslide and Black Hole superhills are suitable, these attractions are completely not dangerous, but keep visitors in suspense.

The Multislide slide for adults is an entire engineering structure, 14 meters high and 49 meters long. After half an hour, the descent can be repeated. Time in the water park passes unnoticed, and the impressions of the visit remain for a long time.

Complex infrastructure

Waterpark "Zurbagan", the photo of which is presented on the page, occupies more than two hectares of the square, its territory has pools and slides, places for relaxation and cafes, sun loungers and park benches are everywhere. Those who wish can simply sit on the grass, which is planted all around. Rest in "Zurbagan" has a beneficial effect on children and adults, young people have something to do, older people also do not have to get bored.

After visiting the attractions, visitors need to dine. To do this, several cozy cafes operate on the territory of the water park, including the Tortuga pizzeria, the Mangal grill cafe, the Detsky ice cream parlor and the Like-At-Home restaurant type dining room. And when you leave the cafe, then after a short rest you can again go up to the rides.





Waterpark "Zurbagan": reviews

In the summer season, up to two thousand people a day visit the Zurbagan water entertainment complex. The entrance is open from 10 a.m. to 6 p.m., in the morning, the basic tariff is valid, after 14 p.m. entrance tickets are 30% cheaper.

Visitors to the water park note the good technical equipment of the rides, the attentive attitude of the staff. Employees, instructors, representatives of the administration hold high the brand of their enterprise. Guest reviews are recorded in wish books, reviewed and taken into account.
