How to get there

Providing excellent rest, Temryuk can not please with a variety of access roads along which you can get to it. And although there are three ports and a railway station in the city, you can come here only by road - by car or by regular buses. Ports and a railway line, unfortunately, carry out only freight transportation. But the bus service is well established. In addition, from any corner of Russia you can fly to Krasnodar or Anapa by plane. Then use the bus or taxi. There are direct flights from Anapa. Travel time is one and a half hours. There are direct and ongoing flights from Krasnodar. Buses depart almost every 30 minutes. The journey takes approximately 3.5 hours. In addition to the aircraft, you can use the services of the railway. From many cities of Russia, including Moscow, St. Petersburg, Ufa, Izhevsk and others, there are trains in the direction of Novorossiysk. You need to get out at the stations "Crimean", "Tunnel", "Protoka" (Slavyansk in the Kuban). Then again, take the bus. It takes about 1.5 hours to get to Temryuk.





Holidays in Temryuk

Those who come to Taman solely for relaxation, are primarily interested in the sea and beaches. Temryuk is located about 4 km from the coast. Minibuses and buses go to the city beach. But many vacationers prefer to go to the neighboring village of Golubitskaya, which is only 10-15 minutes away. The beaches there are clean, spacious, the sea is very warm, and the coast is shallow, which is a weighty argument for vacationers with kids. In addition, in Golubitskaya there is a wonderful water park, a dolphinarium, a crocodile breeding farm (for a fee, predators are allowed to feed), cafes, bars, and restaurants work. And all this right on the beach. So for everyone who chose a vacation in Temryuk, the village of Golubitskaya will serve as another place where you can have a wonderful time.





Treatment

For everyone who plans to combine active recreation and relaxation, Temryuk provides the broadest opportunities. Tizdar volcano is famous all over the world , in the crater of which there is a mud lake. The composition of the mud is magnesium, sodium, bromine, hydrogen sulfide, selenium, sulfides, iodine, and a host of other micro and macro elements that are extremely useful for the nervous system, joints, bones, and skin. In hospitals, this extract is used to make special hoods used in irrigation, vaginal tampons, rinses and even drinks. Self-medication consists only in taking mud baths. Tizdar is located from Temryuk 20 km, near the village of "For Homeland". The entrance to the volcano is paid (400 rubles per person of any age and gender). But not far from Temryuk there are other, absolutely the same healing volcanoes, where entry is still free. One of them is in the village of Kuchugury, which is 35 km from the city. In Temryuk itself, on its outskirts, there are also mud volcanoes, and there are many, and all of them are free. They are located on the Rotten Mountain. The most famous of them is Hephaestus. You can also take mud baths in lakes and estuaries. The most popular lake is Golubitskoye, located in the village of the same name, as well as Salt Lake, near the Bugaz Estuary. So there is where to be treated. The only thing to remember is that mud baths are taken for no more than 10-15 minutes, and they are generally contraindicated for certain categories of patients.





Where to stay

It has long been popular among tourists Temryuk (Krasnodar Territory). Rest here is provided by dozens of guest houses, recreation centers, boarding houses, as well as the private sector. In Temryuk itself, of course, there are not so many accommodation options, so most sites indicate recreation centers located in the Golubitskaya, Peresyp, Kuchugury and other nearby villages. In the city you can stay in hotels and private hotels (guest houses). All of them are located near the center and offer comfortable rooms of the categories "Standard", "Suite", "Junior Suite" with a set of all necessary equipment (air conditioning, refrigerator, TV, wireless Internet). The range of services provided in hotels is quite wide: saunas, beauty salons, taxi calls, restaurant service, gyms, billiards and others. Among the most popular hotels are the following: Victoria, Robinson, a wonderful entertainment complex and at the same time the German Sloboda hotel with the best fitness center in the city.
