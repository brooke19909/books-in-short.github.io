Phuket hotels for families with children: an overview of accommodation on the island

If you decide to go to Thailand with a child, you need to be responsive to the choice of housing. Many tourists because of the beautiful views and developed infrastructure choose the flowering island of Phuket. Hotels in which rest with children is thought out to the smallest detail, mainly have four stars. They have special meals, babysitting services, mini-clubs, playgrounds and swimming pools with slides. However, do not rush to reserve any "four"! Phuket is a special place. No wonder this island is considered the second largest sex industry after Pattaya. But do not rush to abandon Phuket - it will charm you with its beauty.



All the "hot spots" here are concentrated in the city of Patong, or rather, on its main street Bangla Road. In addition, the height of the fun falls on the time after sunset, and during the day everything is decorous and decent. If you are a young couple with a child who has a good night's sleep, then Patong is the ideal place for you. Hotels for children in Phuket in this city are located somewhat away from the center. These are, first of all, Andaman Hill, Patong Green Mountain, Holiday Inn, Swiss Palm Beach. There is everything you need for the rest of the kids, no problem you can hire a nurse or a professional nanny.



But in general in Patong it is too noisy, especially at the peak of the season (December-January). A family with a small child who just needs to make sandpipers out of sand and splash in shallow water is the most suitable hotel in Phuket for families with children in Maya Cayo Bay. Here, almost every "four" (and the "five" and even more so) provides excellent infrastructure for small guests. Mini-clubs are engaged even with two-year crumbs.
