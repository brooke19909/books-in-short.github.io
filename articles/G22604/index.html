Sights of Aleksandrov: photos with a description, what to see necessarily, reviews of tourists

Ivan Vasilyevich left a mark in the history of the city, choosing it as his summer residence. When the plague went around Moscow in the sixteenth century, he went there, and his wife, Martha Sobakina, was elected precisely in Alexandrov. Since the king took the printing house to this city, historians tend to think that the library is somewhere here. Well, it was in Alexandrov that he wounded his son, killing him. They even wanted to erect a monument to him, but somehow it did not work out.

Ancient history

A letter was discovered of Tsar Ivan I Kalita, the city was mentioned there under the name Alexander's settlement. There used to be a forest in which bears and many other animals lived. In the XVI century, a palace was built for Vasily III, as well as a church dedicated to the Protection of the Holy Virgin, now the church is called the Trinity Cathedral. Ivan Vasilievich, when he was still a boy, constantly came here to hunt. After the death of the king, the palace began to collapse. Then the Poles came and occupied a settlement, but Skopin-Shuisky recaptured the settlement and set up a camp there. Sapega, the Hetman of the Poles, came with a siege, but retreated. At the end of the 17th century, Aleksandrov became a county town from the settlement.

111th kilometer

From 1915 to 1917, the Tsvetaevs, Marina and Anastasia lived in the city, and given Alexandrov’s sights, their museum is one of them.

It should be noted that some people were evicted here, of course, that not very good citizens were among the contingent. Nevertheless, those who were subjected to Stalinist repressions also fell. Among them was Pyotr Baranovsky, not only an architect, but also an archaeologist, Victor Toot, an artist, also lived here. Aleksandrov was visited by Leo Polog, the famous physicist-chemist.

Modern time

Many tourists, when they come to the city of Alexandrov, see the sights almost immediately: this is the railway station. Here it is, in the photo below.

It has two platforms: high - electric trains come here from Moscow and the region, and low - long-distance trains come to them, an electric train from the Yaroslavl region also runs, stops just opposite this building.

City feature

If you pay attention to the elevation of Smolensk-Moscow, then in the southeast just find Alexandrov. The city is located next to the Seraya River, which is a tributary of the Klyazma River.

The most interesting thing is what many people think: the city of Alexandrov is the Moscow region, the sights here are appropriate, but according to the territorial affiliation, Alexandrov belongs to the Vladimir region. It turns out that the city is located on the border of the above region.

Architecture

In 1654 a convent was founded here, and the complex has survived to this day, it is functioning. On the territory of the monastery are the graves of the sisters of Peter I. The biggest attraction is the Alexander Kremlin. It is believed that after the Moscow Kremlin, he is the most beautiful. You can see it in the photo below.

The idea of ​​the Alexander Kremlin was submitted by Vasily III, there are high walls around the territory, they are white, and date back to the 17th century. There are still four towers on the walls. This building has never participated in any battles, so it is very well preserved.

Tsvetaev Museum

It was founded by a public foundation in 1990. Various performances, concerts, marathons were constantly held to organize expositions. They are built on the principle of metaphors, and excursions are held for any visitors. The museum is considered the only one in the region devoted to literature. It includes two houses, they are made of wood, as well as a stone house and a garden. There are some other buildings. From May to September, various events are held here, for which many people come here.

In the photo below: inside the Tsvetaeva Museum.

So if you want to visit the city of Alexandrov, attractions can be represented specifically by this museum. Especially if you are a poetry lover. So, for example, there are guided tours dedicated to Tsvetaev, various lectures on Silver Age literature. The route is quite popular, so there are places nearby where you can relax and eat. It should also be noted that this route is included in the Golden Ring of Russia.

The museum stores many different materials, not only about Tsvetaeva, but also about their surroundings.

Art Museum

It contains a collection of works by various artists, mainly the XIX-XX century. And also furniture, various exhibits. Not far away there is an outbuilding, there are household items from servants and peasants, where you can buy a souvenir as a keepsake.

This museum is located in the estate of Pervushin, it consists of three buildings. In the collections you can find the works of V.M. Antonov, who was a deaf-mute artist, Pineev, Vlasov, and many others.

Quick tour

If you want to see Alexandrov, sights - what to see in one day?

You need to make a plan where you will bring some of the most significant buildings and expositions. The first building will be the station, there is also a museum. Next, you will need the Alexander Kremlin, but the Assumption Monastery is a cult object of the city. After you visit the Kremlin, you will enjoy the art museum, which is located in the estate of Pervushin. An interesting sculpture dedicated to Alexander Nevsky. If you have enough time, love poetry, then do not miss the Tsvetaeva Museum.

City sculptures

If you come to the city of Aleksandrov, a photo of the sights is provided to you when you go to see the sculptures. These include the following:

  • Blockades of Leningrad. Once upon a time, as many as 3,000 people, residents of the city of Leningrad, found their last refuge in the necropolis, where a monument stood. Now there is a sculpture depicting a woman mourning for the dead.
  • Warriors. A tribute to the people who died during the Great Patriotic War, a monument was erected when they celebrated the 200th anniversary of the city of Alexandrov.
  • Weaving uprising. It happened in 1905, after which a monument was erected in honor of this event, it depicts a woman who holds a flag. You will find this sculpture on Sovetskaya Street. Also called the "monument to the Alexander Republic."
  • The boy is on a sleigh. He stood on one main street, but then disappeared somewhere, then found on the territory that belonged to the Saturn Cinema.

Other places

The sights of Aleksandrov are not only architectural and historical, but also some natural places. These include Dichkovskoye Lake. It is very beautiful, and scientists suggest that the passage of this reservoir is a glacier. It is possible that this natural monument is 30 million years old. A recreation center was arranged on the shore, when everything blooms in the summer, a lot of people come here to swim and relax.

Karin field is a real historical relic. Now there is a stone installed, which is dedicated to a historical battle. Once upon a time Lithuanians, Poles and Russians fought here. Every year, this battle is reconstructed at the festival "Fatherland Faithful Sons". Photo from the festival below.

Conclusion

If you go to the city market or to museums, as well as in souvenir shops, you will find a lot of things that you can bring with you. So, for example, you can buy dishes that are painted by hand. If you buy a mirror, then you will pack it in birch bark. There are sweets that are called "Alexander's Cow." There are 8 of them, and they are quite tasty.

Usually people come to the city for a day or two, rarely anyone comes for 3 days. Mostly poetry lovers travel to visit the Tsvetaeva Museum. They also come to the festival dedicated to the battle of the Karin field. Be sure to go to the museum, which is dedicated to man-made stone; It is located on Institutskaya Street, and all the stones are made artificially.
