Talk about amplifiers

Any driver who decides to equip his interior with high-quality stereo equipment is seriously thinking about which sound amplifier is better and which model is suitable for its audio system. What should I look for in resolving the issue?

- Dimensions. The driver must understand exactly where he will place his sound amplifier, and he must choose the technique based on the given parameters. Otherwise, an unpleasant situation may arise when the device is purchased, and there is simply nowhere to place it. Those. the first rule is the harmony between the dimensions of free space in the cabin and the dimensions of the amplifier.

- Device class. The classification begins, as usual, with the letter A:

  1. Class A. The sound amplifier related to it is not very suitable for its functional role. Devices of this type are characterized by low-frequency distortion, a weak sound, as a result of which, in order to hear music when the engine is at full speed, the volume has to be set to almost maximum. In addition, this sound amplifier heats up quickly due to high energy consumption. This does not add vitality to the device itself or to the machine battery.
  2. Class B devices. They are much better than the previous one in technical parameters, sound transmission quality and thermal stability.
  3. AB class. The sound power amplifier related to it is an average device between classes A and B in terms of parameters. Most often, they are found in cars and their wide selection is presented in the electrical market.
  4. Class D, in another way - digital signal amplification. Previously, devices operating at low frequencies were characterized this way . At present, this is the designation for a broadband car sound amplifier . True, the quality of his work is somewhat inferior to the previous one. Therefore, today it is the AB class that is more prevalent than others.
