Where are the Carpathians? Where are the Carpathians in Ukraine

Surely many have heard of the Ukrainian Carpathians. This is truly a wonderful and charming place that is perfect for family or romantic getaways. The mountain system is located closer to the west of Ukraine and stretches for 280 km. Sometimes tourists can meet mountains up to 2000 m high, while Goverla is considered the highest point - 2061 m.



It is quite natural that many people wish to visit this ecologically clean area and try to find out in more detail where the Carpathians are. It is worth noting that the relief is multi-tiered. So, there are high mountain areas (altitude up to 1500 m), mid-mountain (up to 1300 m), low-mountain (up to 400 m) and foothill.

Location of the Carpathians



To more accurately understand where the Carpathians are located, you need to know that they are located on the border with countries such as Hungary, Slovakia, Poland and Romania. Nevertheless, officially the mountains are located on the territory of Ukraine, namely its four regions: Transcarpathian, Lviv, Chernivtsi and Ivano-Frankivsk. Thus, from these regions, you can easily and quickly get to recreation centers and take great excursions, as well as enjoy stunning scenery and fresh air. Quite often, the region under consideration is divided into two parts: Transcarpathia and Carpathian. In addition to Goverla Mountain, which attracts tourists the most, Pop Ivan, Petros and Hamster are just as steep and beautiful. Their height exceeds 2000 m, because they are considered no less powerful and interesting. It is known that people live in the mountains, but civilization does not spoil the overall picture. At the same time, the atmosphere around is completely different: nature is almost untouched, the air is clean, sometimes heavy, and the tops of the trees touch the human eye. In the rivers running throughout the Carpathians, there are a variety of fish species, which is why fishing is one of the most beloved activities of both local residents and tourists.

Where the Carpathians are located, it is very easy to determine on the map, as this is an impressive territory highlighted in dark brown. In Ukraine there is only one such site - in the western part of the country.

Hiking trails

Tourism in the Carpathians is most developed in the cold season. Already in the middle of autumn, vacationers can visit the mountains and enjoy the first snow, climbing one of the peaks.

Most tourists do not have a clue where the Carpathians are, but they want to go there with all their heart. And all because legends go about the majestic mountains, the atmosphere reigning around fills with strength and joy, and the amazing nature surprises. Are you going to visit this region in the warm season? Excellent! You will find a lot of entertainment: horseback riding, massage and a sauna at an altitude, fishing and swimming in local rivers, talking with animals in farm yards, craft classes and much, much more. In winter, every vacationer will be able to visit the ski resort and try out the best slopes of Ukraine; taste the famous Hutsul cuisine and warming mulled wine; visit the gorges covered with ice, and find other attractions. When planning a vacation, it never hurts to consider the option of a mountain vacation. Just do not forget to specify where the Carpathians are.





Interesting places in the mountain range

Such settlements as Guta, Yaremche, Yablunitsa, Synevyr, Mizhhiria and Svalyava gained great fame in the Carpathians. Many tourists dream of going there. In some villages, you can even try locally produced water, which is popular throughout Ukraine and is considered medicinal. It is not surprising that in the Carpathians there are a huge number of sanatoriums and dispensaries, as well as recreation centers - air and water here truly work wonders!

It is also recommended to visit famous cities: Ivano-Frankivsk, Mukachevo and Slavske. In the first of them, vacationers will be able to get acquainted with the history of this people, look at amazing sights, visit the Town Hall and much more. The Carpathians of Ukraine is the main attraction that people are so proud of. Arriving in Mukachevo, you should visit the ancient fortresses, try unique wine and buy souvenirs. In the village of Slavske, you can enjoy a relaxing holiday or skiing. There is no time to be bored there, so a trip for tourists will be unforgettable.





Stunning mountain heritage

Being interested in where the Carpathian Mountains are located , tourists first of all mentally calculate the distance from their hometown to Ukrainian attractions. But on no map will they ever see those bewitching places. For example, the Pearl Cave is considered the pride of the Carpathians, on the walls of which calcite balls are formed, somewhat reminiscent of jewelry. No less interesting will be a trip to the Enchanted Valley - a picturesque gorge with powerful rocks. It is also recommended to visit the Hanging Swamp, Stone Gate, Synevir Nature Reserve, lovers' rocks and a cave of transparent walls. In spring, the Valley of Daffodils, which is completely covered with snow-white flowers, the diameter of which can reach 10 cm, will be a great place for a walk.





Treatment in the Carpathians

The Carpathians on the map occupy an impressive place, the same as in the hearts of many Ukrainians. That is why tourists come here from different countries and for different purposes. It's no secret that the Carpathian climate, fresh air, healing mineral springs help restore strength and strengthen the body. Comfortable hotels, recreation centers and other spa facilities await their guests all year round. They are located in picturesque places, usually near rivers and dense forests.





Pleasure available to everyone

A feature of the Carpathians is that almost everyone can visit them. Affordable cost and indescribable beauty of landscapes will help to make a vacation or recreation unforgettable and interesting. Also, during certain periods of time, tourists will find a pleasant surprise - discounts on tickets and various bonuses. In addition, the cost of the trip varies depending on the region chosen for the rest, the number of days of stay and the proposed cultural program.
