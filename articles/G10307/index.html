Baby monitor rating: review of the best models, features, manufacturers

When mom returns home from the hospital, she has to do normal household chores. And with the birth of the baby, there are even more of them. Naturally, the main labor activity occurs at a time when the child is sleeping. And not all mothers will agree to leave their baby unattended in another room.

It was for such cases that special gadgets - baby monitors, were developed. Similar equipment is used by millions of families around the world. The device is quite simple, akin to old push-button phones. The baby monitor consists of two blocks - parent and child. The first is next to mom, and the second is in the room of the baby.

Soundtrack allows you to find out what is happening with the child. You will hear the baby crying, his sniffing and other sounds that you can quickly respond to. A more advanced version of such a device, a baby monitor, is also on sale. But the practicality of this gadget is called into question by many. Especially when it comes to a newborn baby, where it is the sounds that are important, not the image. What to choose - a baby monitor or a baby monitor, of course, you decide, but for many mothers, judging by the reviews, the first option is quite enough.

Today's market offers a ton of solutions to this plan. And getting confused in the presented assortment is very easy. In this case, real user reviews and baby monitor ratings help. We will just try to summarize the latter with an eye on Russian realities and price sectors.

So, we offer you an overview of the best baby monitors. The list includes the most popular devices, distinguished by their quality component and a large number of positive reviews from parents. All the models described below can be purchased both in online and offline stores. So there should not be a problem with the purchase.

Philips AVENT SCD506 / 52

This is a digital device with a working range of 330 meters in open spaces and about 100 meters in closed. This distance is enough for trouble-free operation in almost all Russian apartments.

One of the main features of the Philips Avent SCD506 baby monitor is the availability of a modern DECT module. Which for some consumers is far from the last argument when choosing such devices.

The unit for mom has flexible volume control. But even if the sound module is turned off, the device will still notify the user of the crying with a vibration or light signal. Also on the children's block there is an opportunity to turn on the nightlight and play lullabies from a wide list.

Model Features

The Philips Avent SCD506 Series is compact in size and easy to use. It does not take up much space and fits comfortably next to the pillow even in small cots. If the user is out of range, the gadget will immediately notify you of this with a special sound signal.

"Motorola MBP160"
