Electric boiler: advantages and disadvantages

An electric boiler is the safest type of heating boiler. A feature of this type of device is that they can only be single-circuit, that is, to heat the water you will need to purchase a boiler or other device.



Electric boiler is simple in terms of installation, so it is quite affordable for everyone, and its cost is much lower than that of all other devices in this category. Before buying, it is worth calculating the required power. The power is calculated using the same formula. One kilowatt of boiler power per ten square meters of area. However, in any case, before choosing an electric boiler, you need to consult with professionals. It is necessary to take into account the power of a power line suitable for housing, since in addition to the boiler, other electrical appliances will be used in the house. All this should be taken into account before the direct purchase of a heating device, which will avoid future interruptions in electrical energy.



An electric boiler for heating has a lot of advantages. Among them, we can single out the possibility of instant temperature changes, as well as the fact that it is allowed to be installed directly in the living room. In addition, many people like the fact that it works completely silently. Such a device is suitable for those who from time to time heat their home, as well as if there is no desire or ability to use another type of fuel. A correctly configured electric boiler is able to automatically turn on if the temperature drops to some critical mark.



Destination

These units are focused on heating any premises: cottages, cottages, individual houses, shops, garages, warehouses, as well as other rooms where they can be used as an additional or main source of heat. An electric boiler is structurally a simpler solution than a gas boiler . Such a device does not require maintenance, while it is characterized by safety and environmental friendliness.

Device

Since this is a very simple unit, you can consider its device. Here, the main elements can be distinguished: a heat exchanger, which includes a tank with an electric heater fixed in it, a regulation and control unit. The cost of electric boilers can be called affordable, which is another indisputable advantage over all other types of appliances. Simplicity in installation and compact sizes allow not to allocate a separate room for boilers.

It is not always possible to install such equipment, due to a number of reasons. First of all, we are talking about the impossibility of connecting electricity of a given power. Another reason is interruptions in electrical energy. And the factor that pushes many away from the use of electric boilers is the high cost of electricity.
