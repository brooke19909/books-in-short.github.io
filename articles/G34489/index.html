Overview of the car radio Kenwood DDX155

This is an inexpensive but functional radio. This device has a screen with a resolution of 800 √ó 480 pixels and an aspect ratio of 16: 9. It is very bright and contrasting, even in sunny weather everything will be visible clearly and without sun glare.

To the left of the display is a menu button, an encoder for quick access to many functions and an AUX input for multimedia, which is closed by a lid from external damage and dust and dirt.

The screen of the Kenwood DDX155 radio has many settings, so to speak, for each user. It is possible to adjust the brightness of the screen, contrast, so that watching a movie or photos is flawless.

The radio tape recorder is capable of working with only one disk, but even when moving it can be easily reached with the press of a button, without being distracted from the road. But the disks are used less and less, the benefit in this radio is both an AUX input and USB with Bluetooth. The only plus of the drive is the ability to watch movies recorded on a disc.

This radio is friendly with USB-carriers. It is able to show 9,999 folders and files in a row, which will facilitate the search for music or movies and will allow you to upload a significant number of files to a flash drive. The maximum number of characters in the name of one file is 15, the remaining characters are not displayed. But this is not some serious minus. After clicking on the file to play it, Kenwood DDX155 will open its player and show the album, artist and track name, it will also make it possible to put it on repeat or enable random order of playing tracks.

For such a price, the screen resolution is pretty tolerable. Moreover, for films and photographs this is an excellent resolution, for listening to music it generally does not matter.

The output power per channel at peak is 40 watts. This radio is not suitable for building a powerful audio system in a car, but by connecting good speakers and a powerful subwoofer you can get an acceptable sound that many users will like.

Synchronization with iPhone is also a good plus in the Kenwood DXX155 piggy bank. When you connect the rear view camera to the connector located on the rear panel, you can display the image on the screen.
