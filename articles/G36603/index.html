Where to have a good rest in Crimea? Options for every taste!

Despite the fact that the tendency to go on holiday to the same Turkey and Egypt is becoming more popular, many people still prefer the richest culture and stunning nature of the Crimean peninsula. Where to have a good rest in Crimea? First of all, it depends on what exactly you expect from the trip.

Crimean sanatoriums





The mass of Crimean resorts located in Yalta, Foros, Alupka and Simeiz, which has been pleasing its guests with an excellent level of service and gorgeous beaches equipped for an unforgettable family vacation for the last ten years. If your vacation falls in the middle of summer, you can stop at one of the resorts of the South Bank. By mid-July, the sea is already well warmed up. However, the south coast is poorly adapted for families with children: there are not enough sandy beaches, and descent to the sea, as a rule, is long and difficult enough. Given the popularity of resorts, it is better to book tickets for a month and a half before the planned trip. In terms of price, those from health centers that are located in coastal villages will be more profitable. But let this not push you away, because the sea in such places is much cleaner, and you can always get to large settlements by minibus.

Rest on the east coast of Crimea



If you are looking for a good family vacation in Crimea , you simply cannot find a better place. The east coast is a place where you can fully enjoy the beauties of nature in a relaxed atmosphere, without crowded beaches. Holidays in this part of the peninsula are often recommended for people with various diseases of the respiratory system, allergies, etc. Moreover, the fact that prices here are an order of magnitude lower than in the southern part of Crimea cannot but rejoice. The water in the east is clear and warm, there are many beaches suitable for families with children. And besides, you will have a unique opportunity to visit local attractions and plunge into the events of past centuries.

Festivals

Where is it nice to rest in Crimea for young music lovers who go on vacation in the company of friends? In the midst of a sunny summer in the Crimea annually hosts a festival of fashionable electronic music on Kazantip. The format of the festival does not resemble ordinary beach discos, and therefore a charge of positive emotions and a lot of new experiences are provided to you.

Extreme Fans

Do you crave an unforgettable extreme vacation full of unexpected, and sometimes even a little dangerous adventures? Then western Crimea is best for you. Where is it better to relax youth in this part of the peninsula? A truly stunning place can be called Cape Tarkhankut. A warm, transparent and rather deep sea is an amazing gift from Mother Nature to diving lovers. Near the villages of Novyi Svet and Solnechnogorsk you can enjoy your vacation in the tent camps. This is a unique chance to reunite with the pristine Crimean nature and get a truly lasting impression.

Winter holidays in Crimea



No less good and amazing is the winter Crimea. Reviews of vacationers on the ski resorts of the peninsula are always full of bright colors and emotions. In the mountains of snow they do not fall during all three winter months, and therefore fans of skiing and snowboarding can come here at any time convenient for them, however, February and March are traditionally considered the ideal time. At the camp sites you can find all the necessary equipment, and if necessary, take the help of professional instructors. In terms of where it is good to relax in the Crimea in winter, the lower plateau Chatyr-Dag and the Angarsk pass are very popular, and the steeper slopes for skiers are located in the Ai-Petri region. Almost all camp sites meet their guests with a distinctive atmosphere and an abundance of original local dishes.
