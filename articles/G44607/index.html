How can I transfer data from iPhone to iPhone: tips and tricks

ICloud Backup

Let's start by copying information using a cloud service. This technique is in special demand. All owners of Apple products after registering an AppleID profile are allocated 5 GB of storage space. They can be transferred to any "apple" device. And copy as well.

The first step in transferring data is backing up. This is a required step. Without it, all further manipulations simply do not make any sense.

The instructions for creating backups via iCloud are as follows:

  1. Open "Settings" in the main menu of the device.
  2. Go to iCloud.
  3. Click on "Storage and copies."
  4. Click on the "Create Copy" button.
  5. Confirm the operation.

If necessary, enter information from AppleID. The absence of this profile will result in data not being copied. So, it will not be possible to restore or transfer them to another phone.

Recovery from iCloud

Now you can start downloading information to the new "apple" device. It is advisable to do this the first time you turn on the phone. This technique will avoid unnecessary actions.

To transfer data from iPhone to iPhone, you need to do the following:

  1. Choose the language and country of residence of the person.
  2. Set geolocation enable settings.
  3. Connect to the Internet.
  4. Click on the line "Restore from iCloud".
  5. Enter data from a previously used AppleID. The one in which the information was copied.
  6. Click on the "Accept" button.
  7. Select the desired backup.
  8. Wait for the process to complete.

After a few minutes, the operating system will load. A person will see a message stating that the data has been successfully restored. Now you can use them on the selected device.



If a person wants to transfer data from iCloud to him after working with the iPhone, he will have to reset the settings. Then proceed according to the previously proposed instructions. There are no more options.

Copies in iTunes

How to transfer data from iPhone to iPhone via iTunes? As in the previous case, this will require a backup of the desired information.

This is done as follows:

  1. Install the latest version of iTunes on your PC.
  2. Launch the application.
  3. Insert the USB cable into the PC. The other end is on the iPhone.
  4. Select the connected device in the "Devices" section.
  5. In the left menu, open the "General" tab.
  6. Click on the button "Create a copy now."
  7. Select data to copy.
  8. Wait for the operation to complete.

Typically, after a few minutes, a copy will be ready. It will be saved in iTunes. What's next?

Recovery through iTunes

Transferring data from iPhone to iPhone using iTunes is actually easier than it sounds. This is the fastest and most proven way for those who do not trust "iCloud".

It is proposed to act as follows:

  1. Connect to the Internet on the desired device.
  2. Reset settings. When you turn on the device for the first time, this item is skipped.
  3. Connect iPhone to PC and launch ITuns.
  4. Select "Restore from iTunes."
  5. Click on "Restore from backup". This action will have to be done on the computer.
  6. If prompted, provide a copy to restore.
  7. Confirm the operation.
