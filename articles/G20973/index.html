How to find out which Internet provider I have: all the ways

The question of which Internet service provider provides services may arise in various cases. For example, if for some time a person has not used the Internet or lost documents in which it was possible to see all the details about the account. Also make me think about the question of how to find out which Internet provider I have, maybe the cable left in the apartment from the previous owners (or tenants), etc. There are several options for determining the name of the company that provides the Internet service in your apartment or house. In this article we will talk about this in more detail.





How do I know which Internet provider I have? Simple ways

Find documents that were issued when connected to the Internet. In particular, we are talking about a contract for the provision of services. It can clarify which organization provides the Internet. Of course, not all subscribers save this document. Although it is recommended to leave it on hand until the contractual obligations are broken.

See where the cable for the Internet leads . Surely at the entrance of your house you noticed small boxes mounted on the walls, which include wires. As a rule, they have the logo of the provider company or the corresponding sticker. In order to understand which box the cable reaches for, it is enough just to carefully look at where it goes from the apartment. This method may not always be effective and will allow you to find out which provider is connected to the Internet in your apartment.





other methods

How to find out what my internet provider is in other ways?

  • Using well-known Internet services to check the connection speed, you can also get information about the name of the provider company. All the user needs to do is open one of these web services and start testing. After a few seconds, the system will provide statistics on the speed of the incoming / outgoing data stream, as well as information about which provider provides the services.
  • The second way is also connected to the Internet. There are many sites that let you know the name of the service provider by the IP address of the client. Perhaps this is due to the fact that each provider has its own range of such addresses. Accordingly, when connecting to the Internet, the user is assigned a unique IP from this range. Recognizing it, the service will inform about the name of the provider.
  • If you need to replenish the balance at the end of the billing period, often by opening a browser, you can come across a page from the provider with information about the need to deposit funds to the account.




How to find out if a home can be connected to the Internet of a particular supplier company?

If you are faced with the question of choosing a provider and are interested in how to find out which Internet service provider your home is connected to, then pay attention to the following recommendations:

  • Pay attention to the ads in the stairwell. Often you can find information signs that the house has the ability to use the services of the company.
  • Visit the websites of renowned internet service providers. A number of providers provide the ability to check online the ability to connect to the Internet at a specific address. By entering the address in a special form and sending a request, you can get information about whether the connection is available.
  • Contact the technical support service (service numbers are indicated on the providers' websites) and ask to clarify whether it is possible to connect to your address.

Conclusion

In this article, we talked about how to find out which Internet provider I have. We hope that our tips will help you get the information you need as soon as possible.
