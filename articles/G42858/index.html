Hotel "Suitcase", Pskov: description and reviews

Pskov is the capital of the region of the same name and one of the largest cities in the northwestern region of Russia. More than 200,000 people live here. Often tourists and businessmen come here, for whom there are many hotels and hostels in the city . Hotel "Suitcase" (Pskov) is a budget place to stay, which is located in the central part of the city. Consider in more detail its number of rooms, infrastructure, as well as guest reviews.

Hotel "Suitcase" in Pskov: address and general information

The hotel has a favorable location for tourists, because it is located in close proximity to the airport and the railway station. It is located in a two-story building, which has an unremarkable appearance. Visitors can recognize her by the bright sign on which the name of the hotel is written. Her address: Pskov city, Oktyabrsky prospekt, 56e. However, the entrance to the building is located at the intersection of Vokzalnaya and Tolstoy streets. Guests can open the door by clicking on the code. The station can be reached on foot in 10-15 minutes. The airport is 2 km from here. In the hotel building is open cafe "12 irons", where tourists can eat. A large supermarket and a home appliance store are 200 meters from the hotel.





Hotel "Suitcase" (Pskov) offers tourists 20 comfortable rooms. The hotel also has its own separate apartments, located on Gorky Street, near the Pskov Kremlin. The hotel allows children of any age. If necessary, they can accommodate in an extra bed. Its cost is 500 rubles per day. If you plan to arrive at the hotel with a pet, you must notify the property in advance. This service is also provided for a fee. Check-in begins here from 14:00. Checkout time - noon.

Room Amenities

Hotel "Suitcase" (Pskov) is a budget hotel, so the equipment of rooms here is not very diverse. In addition to beds, the furniture includes a desk with a chair, a wardrobe and bedside tables. In summer, mosquito nets hang on the windows to protect the room from insects. Opposite the bed on the wall hangs a plasma TV with a large diagonal. Negative reviews

However, some guests noted that the hotel "Suitcase" (Pskov) has several disadvantages. Many considered them insignificant, referring to the low cost of living. However, according to guests, the hotel administration should pay attention to the following shortcomings:

Draw conclusions
