We go to Montenegro by car

Probably, all people can be divided into those who are used to sitting at home, working and relaxing in the country in their free time, and those who like to travel, trying not to miss a single vacation. If you belong to the second category, then perhaps you have already visited a lot. Have you had many independent trips? Do you often conquer foreign countries in your own car? Did you go to Montenegro by car? And with another transport?



This article will briefly describe the positive and negative sides of a trip to a country with unique nature and attractions. A trip to Montenegro by car will be described. This country is known for a huge number of national parks and monuments (both natural and historical). Here is the world famous island hotel, a monastery literally carved into the rocks, the second deepest canyon in the world, one of the highest bridges in the world. Many say that everyone should visit Montenegro at least once.

So, when planning a trip, you need to prepare certain documents. When traveling to Montenegro by car, make a route and study which of the countries you travel require a visa (standard or transit). You need insurance - medical insurance for each of your companions and a Green Card for your transport. In addition, it is worth stocking up with detailed maps of not only the destination, but the entire route. This, when traveling by car to Montenegro, will reduce the risk of getting lost somewhere on the road.

If you use a navigator, do not forget to stock up on up-to-date electronic maps for it. When planning a trip, if you wish, you can include some famous cities outside Montenegro, which are also quite worthy of the attention of tourists from all countries (for example, Sighisoara, the birthplace of Dracula in Romania, or Krakow, an unprecedentedly beautiful city in southern Poland). It is worth pre-booking places in hotels (hostels, campsites).
