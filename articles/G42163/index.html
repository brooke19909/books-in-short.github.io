Aonang Nagapura Resort Spa 4 (Thailand / Krabi Province): photos and reviews of tourists

Holidays in Thailand for a long time does not lose its popularity. Our compatriots are very pleased to purchase tours to this hospitable country in order to spend a couple of weeks on the paradise coast under the hot sun. From November to April, this direction becomes very popular among Russians who have the opportunity to spend money on foreign vacations. Each of the tourists can name their favorite piece of Thailand, where it is so nice to come again.





In recent years, our compatriots have been confidently exploring the island part of the country, and especially note the island of Krabi, lost in turquoise waters. Aonang Nagapura Resort Spa 4 *, located on it, often becomes the place where tourists find peace and get the opportunity to enjoy a pleasant vacation right in the middle of the cold Russian winter.

A few words about the hotel complex

Aonang Nagapura Resort Spa 4 * is a cozy four-star hotel, consisting of the main two-story building and a complex of villas. They have one hundred twenty-eight rooms of various categories, which we will talk about a little later. The hotel is made in a traditional Thai style, which is characterized by an abundance of dark wood, bright textiles and accent details. Therefore, if you want to immerse yourself in the island theme completely, then Aonang Nagapura Resort Spa 4 * will definitely please you.

The territory of the hotel complex is characterized by an abundance of green spaces. This creates a special atmosphere of comfort, in which it is so easy to relax and tune in the holiday mood. Almost all guests of the Aonang Nagapura Resort Spa 4 * (Krabi) in the reviews mention that walking around the hotel during their holidays gave them great pleasure.

Hotel Location

On the island of Krabi Aonang Nagapura Resort Spa 4 * is located at some distance from the main urban infrastructure. Many tourists write that he stands on the outskirts. However, this fact often becomes what attracts vacationers here who want to stay away from the hustle and bustle.





To the beach, guests will have to overcome at least four hundred meters, as the hotel is located on the third coastline. The choice of tourists is provided with two beaches - "Pra-Nang" and "Noppharatta". The hotel complex is located approximately halfway to them, so vacationers can explore the sights of both beach areas during the holidays. From the main international airport of Krabi to get to Aonang Nagapura Resort Spa 4 * far enough. Tourists will need to drive a little over forty kilometers. Usually, a comfortable minibus delivers them to the resting place, the host is responsible for the meeting of our compatriots.

Brief description of beach areas

Since Aonang Nagapura Resort Spa 4 * (Krabi) is located away from the beach, on foot this path will take at least half an hour. Walking in the heat will be rather inconvenient, therefore guests of the hotel complex prefer to use the services of tuk-tuk. The trip will cost tourists about twenty baht per person and take no more than five minutes.

The beaches mentioned above delight vacationers with white sand and clear water. However, it is worth considering that they are practically not equipped. In some places there are sun loungers for rent to tourists. Along the coast, cafes and massage parlors are randomly located, where for a small fee you will get a wide selection of services. In general, the beaches are great for families with children, as the ebb is not too noticeable and there is a gentle entrance to the water.

Infrastructure facilities

The hotel has a magnificent swimming pool, it is very popular among vacationers. The bowl has a separate section for children and an unusual bar. It is made, like the entire hotel complex, in Thai style and begins its work almost immediately after breakfast. The pool has two small slides. In the reviews about Aonang Nagapura Resort Spa 4 * (Krabi), the rest area around the tourists is also very highly rated. Here is everything that guests may need. All guests can take beach towels to put on a sunbed. They are issued without collateral and absolutely free.





In the main building there is a roomy restaurant where you can not only have breakfast, but also have lunch and dinner. Tourists note that the choice of dishes is very wide, and the prices are relatively small. Luggage storage and an ATM are located next to the reception. At any time of the day you can withdraw cash from the card in it, which is very convenient for those who do not want to pay cashless payments in a foreign country.

If you need to solve business issues, then in Aonang Nagapura Resort Spa 4 * this can be done in the conference room. For an additional fee you will get the opportunity to hold any event and use office equipment. Please note that there is no parking on site. This nuance is worth considering if you plan to move around the island in a motobike or a rented car.

Level of service and services

Many guests leave feedback about the level of service at Aonang Nagapura Resort Spa 4 * (Krabi). Krabi belongs to those islands where the hotel business is very well developed, and therefore a hotel of any category can provide tourists with a wide selection of services. In the lobby, guests can use free Wi-Fi, here the signal is very stable. But in the rooms it will not be possible to contact relatives on the Internet. Unfortunately for our compatriots, the hotel does not have a spa, but massage services will be provided. Specialists working in the salon do an excellent job of their duties, and you will remember about the pleasure you have received for a very long time.





For a fee, vacationers can take their things to the laundry and dry cleaning. After washing, they will be ironed and delivered to your room in perfect order. Room service is available around the clock. Hotel staff will fulfill almost any whim of tourists in just a few minutes. However, keep in mind that smoking is prohibited throughout. This is strictly monitored by representatives of the security service, and in case of non-compliance with the rules, the guest will be fined. If you plan to use an individual transfer, then this service must be agreed in advance. Do not forget that it is provided for a fee. All the staff understands Russian well, but mostly speaks Thai and English.

Settlement Features

According to the established rules, settlement is made only after two in the afternoon. Those who fly to Krabi earlier can be advised to pay for early check-in. In this case, your number will be ready by the specified time. Eviction of tourists takes place before noon. Guests will have to return the room keys to the shift attendant at the reception and pay all bills previously issued.

Rules for staying with children

Children of different ages travel to Thailand, despite the fact that hotels often do not have the appropriate infrastructure. In Aonang Nagapura Resort Spa 4 * tourists are welcome with children of any age. You can place them on the same bed with parents or in an extra bed. If your child is not yet three years old, then accommodation and accommodation with parents will be free. In the case when the child is from four to eight years old, then parents should pay only the amount of one hundred seventy-five baht for breakfast. In this case, the child is not provided with a separate berth.

Five hundred baht must be paid for a small guest between the ages of nine and eleven years old, accommodated in an extra bed. Older children can only be accommodated in a separate bed, it is paid in the amount of one thousand baht. All transferred amounts are entered into a separate account and paid upon departure from the hotel complex.

Types of entertainment

Typically, Thai hotel complexes can not please holidaymakers with an abundance of entertainment. Most often they are provided for a fee, the main emphasis is on water sports. Guests are offered diving, riding a banana and jet skis, individual fishing and other interesting programs.





A tour desk is located on site. Its specialists are always ready to pick up an unusual tour for guests, which will delight all family members. It is noteworthy that the prices for such programs are always significantly lower than those of the host.

Catering

There is one restaurant in the hotel complex, it is here that guests come for breakfast. Most of our compatriots purchase packages with breakfast included. If desired, tourists can note the type of food when booking. However, Russians rarely use this opportunity. They prefer not to be tied to one place and visit different cafes, which are quite a lot on Krabi.

Breakfast at the hotel is served as a buffet. Our compatriots note that the food is extremely diverse and does not have time to get enough of it during the rest. Every day on the table there are meat cuts, vegetable dishes, potatoes cooked in different ways, sausages, meatballs and bacon. Also, vacationers can try several types of pastries, egg dishes and fresh fruits. Many guests believe that the chef is No. 1 in preparing coffee. Aonang Nagapura Resort Spa 4 * also offers a choice of local juices, teas and carbonated drinks at breakfast.

Number of rooms

Judging by the photos of Aonang Nagapura Resort Spa 4 *, found on the Internet, all hotel rooms are equipped with excellent facilities. Even standard apartments are furnished with new furniture, appliances and can please with an interesting design. Upon arrival, a fruit basket and tea set await tourists. It is updated during each room cleaning. Please note that in the bathroom there are only cotton buds, shower gel and shampoo in special containers. Toothbrushes, toothpaste and soap guests will have to purchase on their own.





In the main building there are numbers of four categories:

  • Superior room with single beds;
  • double apartments with private access to the pool;
  • double suite with garden view;
  • double suite with private pool.

Residents of the villa have their own small pool, a family of three can live here. A distinctive feature of this hotel is its focus on family vacations. This is reflected in the fact that most rooms have double beds. Only one sleeping place can be added to the apartment.

Aonang Nagapura Resort Spa 4 *: reviews

Our compatriots are enthusiastic about the rest in this hotel. Of course, they note some shortcomings of the hotel complex, such as remoteness from the sea, lack of Russian-language channels, periodic power outages and poor housekeeping.





However, all these shortcomings overlap the obvious advantages of the hotel, which he has a lot. These include an interesting design, varied food, a luxurious pool, a pleasant number of rooms, friendly staff, amazing beaches and many, many other moments. Therefore, the hotel complex Aonang Nagapura Resort Spa 4 * (Krabi) is an excellent option for relaxing on a paradise island in absolutely any company.
