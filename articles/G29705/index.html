How to rent a house correctly and safely?

Quite often, we see ads like ‚ÄúRent a private house, call tel ...‚Äù. Have you ever wondered how safe this is and what needs to be done to rent out the property correctly and without too much trouble?



A house owner can rent a house, as well as his representative, in whose name the power of attorney is issued, notarized. The search for tenants can be done independently, using advertisements in newspapers, or contact a real estate agency. In the second case, the transaction will occur much faster, but will require cash costs.

If you decide to rent the house yourself, without involving intermediaries, place ads in local mass media - newspapers, television, on the Internet, paste them on billboards that are located near public transport stops, at markets, etc. In the ads you should not indicate the address of the house, it is enough to indicate the contact phone number. The address can be given to prospective customers who want to see the house.

Carefully study the documents of counterparties. There is nothing shameful in your desire to study the passport of the alleged tenants or their other documents. If both parties are satisfied with the conditions for the delivery of the house, proceed to signing the lease.

Such an agreement may be signed for any period. The time frame should be indicated in the body of the document, otherwise it will lose its force only after five years after signing the contract. If you want to rent a house for a longer period, the contract will need to be registered with special registration authorities. But it should be noted that there are few who want to burden themselves with additional chores, so most often a lease is concluded for a period of less than five years. If necessary, the contract can be extended or renewed.



The lease agreement must indicate the details of the tenant, your data, phone numbers by which you can communicate with both parties. Enter the conditions that you consider necessary to set before the tenant.

You should know that after the contract is signed, you can come to your own home to check the safety of the property or to receive money for using your property only on the days established by the contract. If you want to rent only a house, and you intend to use the land for your own needs, then this should be indicated in the contract.

If you are a happy owner of a country house in which you are not going to spend the warm season, then it will not be out of place for your budget to rent out a house for the summer. Today, many citizens dream of breaking out of a stuffy and dusty city, but, unfortunately, not everyone has such an opportunity.

The process of renting a country house is carried out according to the usual scheme. The only difference is that the announcement must indicate "Rent a house for the summer."



After drawing up a lease, do not forget to submit it to the tax authorities. You will be charged 13% of the rental amount. If you do not pay taxes, and the inspection finds out that you are renting a house, then you will be charged a huge administrative fine and you will be required to pay all unpaid taxes.
