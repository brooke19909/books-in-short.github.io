Odessa, Langeron

In the central part of the Odessa coast, near the Marine Station, there is a place called Langeron. This part of the Odessa coast is named in honor of the Russian commander Alexander Langeron, who was the head of Odessa. It was in this part of the city that his summer cottage was located.

Today Langeron is called a separate area of ‚Äã‚ÄãOdessa by the sea. Such a kind of recreation area, with its sights, beach, square, unusual fountains and restaurants, which Odessa is rich in. Langeron begins immediately beyond the territory of the seaport. Many call Langeron Odessa embankment.





This place is also known as Langeron Beach in Odessa. But, when you get here, at first glance this beach is bewildering. What kind of beach is this, because there is no sand or pebbles here? Under your feet, from the sea and across the coast, one solid concrete. But still there is sand here, it is worth going a little further from the maritime station. The sandy Langeron beach in Odessa is not very large, and the infrastructure in this place is attractive, so in the season vacationers are located right on concrete slabs and breakwaters.

Resort and entertainment center "Nemo"

Many interesting places and many attractions are full of Odessa. Langeron is no exception.

The highlight of the beach is the resort and entertainment center "Nemo". In the very center of the embankment is a huge resort complex, which includes a five-star hotel, five restaurants, swimming pools, a spa and a dolphinarium. Hotel Nemo is the only hotel in Odessa, which is located right on the beach. Very often it is simply called the Langeron Hotel (Odessa).





This super-modern center is located right on the promenade, the area around is ennobled and accessible to anyone. Nemo is the only hotel in Europe with dolphins.

Fountain Square

The refined and unique fountain embankment is still the charm of the city of Odessa. Langeron near the dolphinarium "Nemo" is decorated very unusual. Fountains are beating directly from the ground, near which in summertime children and adults splash with pleasure.





In addition to the fountain square on the embankment, there are beautiful benches, stylized as flowerbeds, chic palm trees, and in the evening Odessa includes beautiful lights. Langeron is filled with romance.

Guest reviews

In order to find out in more detail the advantages and disadvantages of Langeron beach in Odessa, as well as pay attention to the features of this unusual place, we turn to the reviews of vacationers.

According to reviews of tourists, Langeron is a fairly popular holiday destination in Odessa. Thanks to the Nemo Dolphinarium and the adjacent ennobled territory, this part of the city is full of tourists all year round.

Many vacationers in these places are advised to visit nightclubs in Odessa. Langeron is very popular among young people, because there are modern recreation centers, clubs and restaurants. In the summer, tourists are advised to visit the night club "Roof of the Sea", which is located right on the beach. Very often concerts of pop stars take place here.

Langeron, judging by the reviews, is called the second beach in Odessa after Arcadia. Arcadia, with the famous "Ibiza", is still more popular.

It is on Langeron that it is possible to rent a boat or a catamaran and swim in the sea. By the way, if not for the reviews of tourists, this we would not have known.

Sandy beach Langeron is not particularly praised by vacationers. Many tourists in their reviews say that it is not clean enough. The beach is free - it's good, but the city authorities do not really care about the order.





Based on the reviews of tourists, it follows that a visit to the Langeron promenade in Odessa is a must, but the beach itself is optional. If you want to take a walk, enjoy beautiful views, visit cafes or restaurants, then Langeron is just the place to go, but there are better places for swimming and sunbathing in Odessa.
