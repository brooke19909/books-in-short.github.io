Kamyanets-Podilskyi hotels: a description of the best accommodations

Kamyanets-Podilskyi is an incredibly beautiful Ukrainian city, full of historical sights. Thousands of tourists constantly come here to enjoy the beautiful castles, ancient bridges and chic nature. Therefore, it is not surprising that the hotels of Kamenetz-Podolsky are extremely popular in the village. There are only 47 hotels here, but each of them is special. There are mainly three- and four-star hotels here. In our article, we describe only the best placements.

Reikartz

Some hotels in Kamyanets-Podilskyi are distinguished by the highest quality of service and modern technical equipment. These are five star hotels. One of the best is the Reikartz Hotel, located near the Kamyanets-Podilsky Fortress. She has been working since 2008. From Reikartz, you can reach the city center in just 20 minutes.

Hotels in Kamyanets-Podilsky do not have a large number of rooms. There are only 38 of them in Reikartz, but they are all elegantly decorated and equipped with a desk, wardrobe, safe, minibar and air conditioning. This property offers a complimentary buffet breakfast.
