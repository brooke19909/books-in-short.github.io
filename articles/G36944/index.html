An ideal vacation in Egypt: Domina Coral Bay Harem 5

Description: Domina Coral Bay Harem - one of the seven luxury hotels of the "town" Domina Coral Bay. Its location is determined by the highest place of the resort, six kilometers from Naama Bay and the international airport. Designed in oriental style and decorated in warm golden colors, it attracts tourists with its comfort. The owners of the complex tried to invest in every little thing a certain sensuality and mystery of the country. The hotel received its first travelers in 2003 and has two buildings: two- and three-story, made in the Moorish style.



Rooms: Domina Coral Bay Harem 5 has 212 rooms, of which 147 are standard, 63 are two-story. They are made in an exquisite design, have blue glass trim and silver candelabra. Most have sea views. Room service is available around the clock. The standard room has air conditioning, balcony, safe. Using the minibar, tourists can refresh themselves with water, soft drinks. The bathroom has a shower or bathtub, jacuzzi (not in all rooms), hairdryer. Thanks to satellite TV, guests can watch four Russian channels. There is a canopy on the bed.

Meals: Customers staying at Domina Coral Bay Harem 5 are served on an all-inclusive basis directly in the hotel restaurant. Here you will be offered dishes of international and oriental cuisine, a variety of drinks and desserts. For the convenience of guests, there are five bars in the complex, one of which is located directly in the Harem basin.



Beach: The complex has a beach about two km long, which captures a coral reef - part of the marine National Park. Towels and umbrellas are provided for guests at Domina Coral Bay Harem 5. Almost all the time there are rescuers. Beautiful fields for beach sports will allow you to actively spend your holidays, and animators create all the conditions for every tourist to find something to their liking: tournaments, beach parties, games.

The on-site pool is equipped with a jacuzzi. In addition, you can use a total of seven pools, which are located on the territory of the complex, including children's.



Additional services for vacationers: Domina Coral Bay Harem 5 animators work almost around the clock. You can attend classes in aqua aerobics, participate in team games and competitions. In the evening, visit the Mosaique Caf√©, where guests can enjoy cabaret shows, karaoke and even salsa lessons. There is a club for children where they can play, draw. You can use the babysitting service for payment . Laundry facilities and currency exchange are also available on site. Mini buses run around the complex, which anyone can use. In addition, parking spaces are available.

Digest: Domina Coral Bay Harem 5 guests describe as an excellent hotel with flowers everywhere. Everywhere is very beautiful.

Tourists note that the staff is hospitable not only in the hotel, but throughout Domina Coral Bay. The rooms were cleaned every day, and in the pool bar and on the beach, any quantity of drinks could be taken. The abundance of fruits and desserts do not leave indifferent those who come to rest.
