From the material, patterns should be made so that they turn out to be slightly larger than the surfaces to be leveled. The material is laid on a reference base, for example, on the surface of a countertop. A layer of epoxy is applied to both surfaces of the stop; getinax must be laid on top. As soon as the resin dries, you need to trim the excess, this will allow you to get an even plastic coating.

If you notice that the tool has become unstable, then you should conclude that the gearbox or motor is out of order.
