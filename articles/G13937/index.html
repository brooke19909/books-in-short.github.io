Hotels in Yekaterinburg. Hotels in the center of Yekaterinburg: photos and reviews

Yekaterinburg is the center of the Sverdlovsk region. It is the fourth city in Russia in terms of population. This is a large industrial center, its economy includes the following industries: instrumentation, heavy engineering, food, light and printing industries, as well as the military-industrial complex.



Due to this, the hotel business is well developed here. To be more precise, the hotels in Yekaterinburg offer their guests a total of 7315 rooms. Of the many different accommodation options that are offered by local hotels, city guests have the opportunity to choose the most suitable for the price and location.

Here are the headquarters of domestic business giants, among which are the Kalina concern, the Steel Industrial Company, and Enel OGK-5. People come here from all over the country to participate in conferences, seminars, to conclude various business transactions. At the same time, in the city itself, as well as in its environs, there are a huge number of historical attractions, places of active recreation.

Almost 90% of city visitors are business tourists, for productive work of which comfortable living conditions are necessary. If you are not constrained by the means, then three- and four-star hotels in Yekaterinburg will suit you. If your budget is limited, then cheap hotels in the city will open their doors with joy. They have inexpensive accommodation rates, while the level of comfort is acceptable.

When choosing hotels in Yekaterinburg, in addition to living conditions and prices, it is necessary to take into account the proximity to the transport hubs of this city. If you arrived here by train, then hotels at the Zheleznodorozhny district will be at your service. So, near the railway station is the hotel AVS. There are two bus stations in the city, Severny and Yuzhny, and if you arrived by bus in the city, then there is a mini-hotel Kolosok at your service. Near the Koltsovo Airport is the Oktyabrskaya Hotel.
