Hotel Medblue Lardos 4 * (Rhodes, Greece): description, photos and reviews

As you know, Greece is very popular among tourists who want to combine a magnificent beach holiday with a rich excursion program to interesting places. If you want to fully enjoy all this, then go to the island of Rhodes. Here you will find magnificent beaches, the purest azure sea, picturesque nature and a lot of interesting places to visit. As a comfortable place to stay, you can consider the four-star Hotel Medblue Lardos.



Location

Located "Medblou Lardos" in a town called Lardos on the Greek island of Rhodes. The hotel is located on the seashore and has its own beach, to which guests can walk in just a couple of minutes. The nearest airport is Diagoras, a 40-minute drive from the hotel. Not far from the "Medblou" are interesting sights - Lindos, the Palace of the Grand Masters and the ancient city of Kamiros.



Description, hotel photo

Medblue Lardos, reviews of which we will consider a little later, invites everyone to stay in one of the 105 comfortable rooms decorated in high-tech style. In particular, this design will appeal to young people. The housing stock of the hotel consists of standard double and triple, as well as family rooms. All of them are spacious, with new furniture and appliances. Each apartment has a private balcony with beautiful sea views. The price includes three meals a day in the hotel restaurant. It serves local and international dishes. There is also a bar on site.





The hotel has a swimming pool for adults with a section for the youngest guests. Nearby there is a sun terrace with sun loungers and parasols. Literally a hundred meters from the residential buildings there is a beach equipped with everything necessary.

Medblue Lardos 4 *: reviews of Russian tourists

Since modern travelers are increasingly taking a more responsible approach to the issue of choosing a hotel to stay in one country or another, they try not only to study official information, but also are interested in the opinions of other people who have already been here. Therefore, we decided to save your time a bit and give generalized comments from our compatriots regarding their stay in Medblou Lardos. In general, we can say that tourists liked the hotel. Of course, there were some disadvantages, but they can not be called serious. But first things first.

Hotel itself

In their reviews, most travelers noted that the rooms provided to them at Medblue Lardos 4 * were spacious, clean, bright, and stylishly furnished. The guests were especially pleased with the presence of a large furnished balcony in each room. There were no complaints about the quality of the equipment. Cleaning, according to vacationers, was carried out regularly. Linen and towels were changed several times a week. Bath supplies were replenished as needed.

The hotel area, according to guests, is small, clean and well maintained. It features a pool and sun terrace.



Nutrition Comments

With regard to this issue, the vast majority of hotel guests in their reviews note that they had no special complaints about food. Of course, the choice, according to them, cannot be called very diverse here. However, the menu always had dishes of meat, poultry, seafood. Everything is delicious and perfectly cooked. Experienced travelers recommend those who wish to diversify their diet to visit taverns in the vicinity of Medblue Lardos 4 *. So you can try a variety of local dishes.



Entertainment

Of course, almost all tourists who come to Rhodes in the summer are primarily concerned about beach vacations. As for the Hotel Medblou Lardos, it is located on the seashore. So the beach is just around the corner. According to travelers, the sea here is very clean. Sunbeds and umbrellas on the beach are free, in sufficient quantities. The only thing the guests warn about is the need to bring special rubber shoes with you from home or already in place, as large stones are encountered when entering the water. In addition, you can walk a little along the coast to the beach with a flatter entrance to the sea. However, for the rental of sun loungers there already have to pay (about 4 euros).

As for entertainment in the hotel itself, there are not many of them. So, many guests praise the large pool. There is a section for the smallest and a sun terrace. Animations at the Medblue Lardos Hotel. In the evening, only include music in the bar. Therefore, tourists are advised to those who are used to constant fun and parties to choose other hotels. "Medblou Lardos" is perfect for a quiet measured rest surrounded by beautiful nature and the azure sea.

In addition, experienced travelers recommend that everyone who first comes to Rhodes should not be limited to just spending time on the beach and pool, but be sure to go on excursions. And you can do this both independently, by renting a car, or as part of a group by buying a tour at an agency.

The opinion of the guests about the staff

According to the tourists who left their feedback, the staff of the four-star Hotel Medblou Lardos (Rhodes, Greece) are very welcoming, friendly and always ready to help. Here they respond immediately to all problems and requests. The staff is always trying to resolve any customer issues. In addition, our compatriots were pleased that many of the hotel staff speak Russian. And this despite the fact that there are not so many tourists from our country. The main contingent is travelers from Poland.
