How to get from Vyazma to Moscow?

The city of Vyazma is a small settlement located on the territory of the Russian Federation. In 2017, its number was about 45 thousand people. Every year, about a third of this amount migrates to the capital for various purposes: to get acquainted with its sights, find a prestigious job or just take a walk. In total, there are several ways to get from Vyazma to Moscow.





By car

The distance from Vyazma to Moscow is only 231 kilometers. This insignificant distance can be overcome by personal transport in just 3 hours 34 minutes. At the entrance to the capital, as a rule, there are many traffic jams, respectively, this figure can significantly increase. On the way it is worth crossing several points:





  • After driving 68 kilometers from the city border, the driver will reach the village of Maltsevo.
  • Next you need to go on a direct highway. At the 118th kilometer a sign will appear on the entrance to the village of Bolshoy Sokolovo.
  • At 201 km Odintsovo will seem. Now you can relax, because there is very little left to the appointed point.

Important routes to the capital are laid in various ways. For this reason, the state of the track is periodically monitored. Almost always a quality repair is carried out.

By bus

In just 3 hours 50 minutes you can take a bus from Vyazma to Moscow. It is worth noting that this is the cheapest way to travel, because the cost of a ticket for one passenger will be only 890 rubles. For children and preferential categories of citizens, very favorable discounts of up to 50 percent apply.





The flight from the international station of Vyazma is carried out every day. Departure takes place early in the morning - at 3:50. Already at 7:40 a transit flight arrived at the very center of the capital of Russia.

An individual entrepreneur takes responsibility for the transport of people. It provides an imported vehicle. It is equipped with air conditioning, television and adjustable seats, so the trip will be very pleasant!

By train

Every day, an electric train departs from the station of the city of Vyazma, which is at the Belorussky railway station in Moscow. During the road, she practically does not make stops, so the total travel time is only 2 hours 29 minutes. The approximate cost of such a trip is about 700 rubles.





A little longer follows the train from Vyazma to Moscow. In total there is one direct and several passing flights. The price for one ticket is approximately the same as for the train.

It is worth considering that in the summer a huge number of people go on a trip. For this reason, they buy tickets in advance. It is recommended to take this fact into account and reserve the desired seats on the official website of Russian Railways no later than one month before the expected date of travel.

By plane





It would seem that the fastest transport that will allow you to overcome the distance from Vyazma to Moscow is an airplane. But near this small town there is only one airport that is not designed to receive large passenger airliners. Therefore, moving in this way will not work, it is recommended to choose the most optimal option.

Why move in the opposite direction?

There are many reasons to come from Vyazma to Moscow. But moving in the opposite direction is surprisingly popular. Why do residents of a big city come to this small town? Of course, in order to get acquainted with its historical sights. There are several of them:

  1. It is mandatory to take a walk along Lenin Street. It is one of the oldest in Russia. On it you can see old buildings, the design of which is impressive.
  2. The museum of local lore has a very polite staff who will gladly tell about the history of the most famous Lake Smolensk.
  3. If a tourist decided to come to this city with a child, then he must visit a cozy park located on Efremov Square. It has a huge number of attractions that are not even in the capital. The surrounding nature also pleases with its beautiful view.
  4. All residents of Vyazma respect the monument dedicated to General M. Efremov, bring flowers to him. It is also worth observing this memorable tradition.
  5. The snow-white Trinity Cathedral with a winding staircase adjacent to it has a magnificent appearance that you want to enjoy forever.

Every year, a huge number of people move from Vyazma to Moscow and do the same in the opposite direction. What is the best way to do this? There are three main options. You should make your choice based on several principles. If a person is not ready to spend extra money on a trip, then it is better to choose a bus. The train will make the trip as comfortable as possible. Own car will turn the journey into the most interesting.
