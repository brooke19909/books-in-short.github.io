Hotels in Prague: luxury, cheap, in the center. Review, reviews and photos

Prague is one of the most beautiful cities in the world. It is here that hundreds of thousands of foreign tourists come here annually to simply enjoy the splendor, the huge cultural heritage and stroll along the old streets. The tourist flow here does not stop even in winter - modern tourists love this ancient city so much.

Of course, tour operators offer quite interesting excursion programs, but many prefer to travel on their own. They attach great importance to the choice of hotel or hotel. The choice depends on a number of factors. Someone is looking for a hotel in the very center of the city in close proximity to the main attractions. Others are limited in funds, they need a hotel room exclusively for sleeping and relaxing, so they are looking for more budget options, even remote from the center. We offer to consider the most interesting hotels in Prague.

Hotels in the city center

Quite often, those tourists who come to the city for 3-4 days pay attention to options located in the heart of Prague, that is, in its cultural and historical center. Their stay in Prague is limited, so I want to get to know the city, its spirit and not spend time on the road. Most often these are hotels with 4 stars, featuring a high level of comfort and first-class service.

We invite tourists to consider the hotels of Prague (4 stars) in the center. They got their stars thanks not only to their advantageous location, but also to the impeccable service that they offer to all their tourists.

Mysterius Hotel Carnival 4 *

The stunning hotel in Prague received 4 stars primarily due to its favorable geographical position. It is located in the very center of the city, just 100 meters from the Old Town Square.

All outstanding sights are located within walking distance, some of them can be seen from the window. At the same time, tourists note that the hotel is in a rather comfortable place, the noise from busy streets practically does not reach here, so it is possible to sleep and relax after long and tiring walks.

The cost of a double room starts from 120 euros. Numbers traditional for Prague: bright and spacious, are presented by two rooms. In the living room there is a comfortable folding sofa, which can accommodate another guest.

The hotel is quite small, only 12 rooms. But this is what becomes his dignity, because employees get the opportunity to be more attentive to each vacationer, to fulfill his desires and whims, including delivering breakfast to your room.

Hotel Residence Agnes 4 *

If you are looking for Prague hotels in the center, you will probably come across this option. A cozy, truly homely atmosphere is what all tourists celebrate without exception. The hotel is a short walk from the promenade, in close proximity to other cultural and historical sights of the city.

Spacious rooms in a classic style, friendly and friendly staff, delicious breakfasts and aromatic coffee are what await all visitors to the hotel.

Golden Well 4 *

If you want to enjoy the beauties of the city, look down on them, you should pay attention to the hotels of Prague with amazing views from the window, there are many of them. Small but cozy rooms decorated with natural wood, polite and friendly staff, impeccable service and, of course, amazing views from the window - something that even the most fastidious tourists and travelers are unlikely to resist.

Bohemia Plaza Residence 4 *

If you are accustomed to luxury, if you want to feel like an important person for a moment, you will like the hotels of Prague in a simple classic style. Bohemia Plaza Residence 4 * - a hotel with luxury apartments, located near Wenceslas Square.

Spacious rooms, the aroma of fresh pastries, impeccable service - what else does a tourist need after a hard day?

Cheap hotels

Not all tourists can afford to stay in luxury apartments. And then they should pay attention to cheap hotels in Prague (3 stars). In the center, you are unlikely to find an affordable option, but within a kilometer from the center you can find quite a few cheap and inexpensive hotels.

Royal Bellezza Apartments 3 *

We want to introduce modern tourists to an excellent accommodation option. He confirms that it is not so difficult to find comfortable and inexpensive hotels in Prague. A cheap room at the Royal Bellezza Apartments. This hotel is just a 10-minute walk from the city center, namely from Wenceslas Square. Vacationers will be able to use public transport, as there is a tram stop in the immediate vicinity, as well as a subway station.

The cost of a double room is only 40 euros, in addition, for an extra charge, you can accommodate two children, which will please all lovers of family vacations. The rooms are spacious, equipped with small appliances and a kitchenette. For an additional fee (4 Euros) you can have breakfast. There is a slight drawback - noise from the street. That is why when booking rooms it is better to immediately give preference to a room with windows to the courtyard - only in this case you will be able to sleep, relax and rest.

The subtleties of living

Each tourist in Prague will find the best option for a comfortable stay. Here you will not find huge hotels with many vacationers - most often these are small private hotels, guest houses with their own, sometimes centuries-old history.

Of course, many people dream of living in the center, because in the immediate vicinity are all the cultural attractions. At the same time, some tourists are not ready to put up with the noise coming from the streets, so they are looking for a quieter, quieter and more comfortable place, albeit far from the center and the whole cultural life of the city. In general, Prague boasts a developed infrastructure: you can reach any point within 25-30 minutes not only by metro, but also by tram.

We strongly recommend choosing hotels in Prague and booking rooms in advance: in this case you will save a substantial part of your budget and will be able to plan your vacation. Tourists who start looking for a suitable option a few days before departure often face a number of difficulties. The order of settlement in most cases is the same: check-in after 14-00, eviction up to 12 hours. If we talk about the deposit and its size, it all depends on the hotel, its reputation, class. That is why be sure to visit the official website of the attracted option, get acquainted with the reviews of tourists.

We sincerely hope that among the presented options you are sure to find the best for yourself. We tried to take into account the main preferences and wishes of modern tourists, got acquainted with the reviews of real vacationers. We wish you a pleasant stay in the fabulous city of Prague.
