application

Please note that the fitness bracelet only works with Android smartphones. Almost all models work with the model without difficulty. However, iOS owners will not appreciate the capabilities of the bracelet.

To fully work, you must install two applications: Charm by Samsung and S Health. You can download them for free on the Play Market. The first program is needed for the initial connection and setting up a fitness bracelet, the second one is for tracking user actions.

Pairing takes place in seconds. Everything is simple and clear. You can immediately select the color of the LED for notifications, as well as allow the utility access to phone notifications. Here you can configure what the bracelet will inform you about. Since there is no screen and vibration signal, and the only color is responsible for notifications, you should not choose many different programs.

The Charm by Samsung application is quite primitive, it is very rarely used. Using it, you can update the software, as well as find out how much battery is left.

For work, you will often have to use the S Health program. She is familiar to many owners of other bracelets from the company, as it is universal. The application will record the number of steps taken. You can walk all day with a bracelet that is not paired with a smartphone, and in the evening set synchronization to get information about the steps taken.

Autonomy

As mentioned above, the bracelet is made miniature. For small dimensions, developers had to pay a small battery capacity. The device has a battery of only 17 mAh. However, given the lack of a full-fledged screen and low functionality, this amount is enough for quite a long work.

The number of notifications and the duration of the connection with the smartphone have a slight effect on the duration of work. On average, a model runs for more than 10 days; for some users, it can function up to three weeks.
