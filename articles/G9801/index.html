Holidays in the Mediterranean Sea has always been considered one of the most popular among our compatriots. All of them appear in the tourist catalogs in the best possible light, and experienced tour operators talk about each hotel with enthusiasm. How to navigate in this situation? Hotel Location

Located Novum Garden Side Hotel 5 * near Side. They are separated by eleven kilometers, which are quite easy to overcome by rented or public transport. The immediate location of the hotel complex is the small village of Cholakly. It is quite well developed, so vacationers have a place to walk, going beyond the walls of the hotel.

It should be borne in mind that upon arrival at the Turkish international airport, tourists will have to cover a distance of fifty-five kilometers. Usually, our compatriots get to the hotel thanks to a transfer from the host, but if desired, any traveler can agree with the hotel complex when booking a room on an individual transfer. In this case, the road will take much less time, which is very important after a long flight.





A few words about infrastructure

There are four outdoor pools on site. Their maximum depth does not exceed one meter forty centimeters. All of them are interconnected and form a real cascade with waterfalls and quiet bays. There is a small pool for children, as well as several water slides.

For guests there are two restaurants, six bars, two snack bars and a pastry shop. Guests can also spend time in a small shopping center. It sells souvenirs, daily necessities and many other things that a tourist may need on vacation.

Not a single large hotel can do without a conference room; Novum Garden Side Hotel 5 * is no exception to the general rule. At the disposal of tourists is a hall with a capacity of three hundred people. It can easily hold a business event or a family holiday with a large number of guests.

Many guests use the services of a car rental located in one of the buildings. Free parking is available on site. However, guests claim that it is small in size, therefore, free space for transport is not always located here.

Free services

The hotel provides a large number of services for free. As part of this concept, guests can visit the Turkish bath, jacuzzi and massage parlor. Reviews about this pastime are always full of enthusiastic epithets.

Vacationers have access to the gym. It is quite well equipped and will help to keep yourself in good physical shape even after constant overeating at lunch and dinner.

From entertainment, guests often choose volleyball, darts and football. Together with a team of animators, guests are engaged in aerobics, gymnastics and play water polo.

Internet is available on site. The signal level is stable in absolutely all buildings and in the recreation area by the pools.

In the evenings in the hotel complex is a disco, the entrance to which does not require an additional fee. Kids also have the opportunity to visit their own disco. It begins earlier than an adult and usually ends already at ten in the evening.





Paid services

Please note that on site you will have to pay for drinks after twelve in the morning. Freshly squeezed juices and imported alcohol are also considered paid. You will have to deposit money for using the safe in the room, you will be informed about its cost at the reception during check-in.

For an additional fee, guests can use the services of a professional photographer, doctor and babysitter. The spa has its own price list for services, it can be found at the reception or directly in the beauty salon.

Of the sports, water are paid, as well as a visit to the game center. Please note that laundry facilities are available on site. The staff of the hotel complex will quickly tidy up your clothes and even hang them in the closet after washing.

Entertainment and facilities for children

Novum Garden Side Hotel 5 * (Turkey) is pleased to accept children of any age. Comfortable conditions are created for them, turning the rest into a fascinating adventure.

A kids club has been created for kids. There will be interesting to young guests from four to twelve years. When visiting a restaurant, mothers may ask waiters to take out a highchair and a children's menu. In the evening, kids can take food from a special buffet.

Children spend their free time in a small pool or on a playground with a large number of facilities. In the room you can always put an extra bed for a child. However, this nuance must be specified in advance.

If necessary, the hotel will provide guests with a babysitter and a baby stroller. But do not forget that these services are provided at an additional cost.

Many five-star hotel complexes offer their guests different compliments. In Novum Garden Side Hotel 5 * there are four options:

  • Regular guests. This category of guests is entitled to their own discount when booking a room.




Dining at the hotel

The concept, so beloved by the Russians, includes breakfast, lunch and dinner, served in the form of a buffet. Guests can also enjoy late dinner and nightly soup. This service is often used by those tourists who check into the hotel after dinner.
