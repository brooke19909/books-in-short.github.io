Nasos & Daisy Hotel 3 * (Greece / Corfu island) - photos, prices and reviews of tourists

The Greek island of Corfu is famous not only for its orange, olive and lemon groves, as well as cypress forests, but also for its luxurious beaches and developed tourist infrastructure. If you want to spend an unforgettable vacation here and are looking for an inexpensive hotel in a quiet place on the shores of the Ionian Sea, then pay attention to Nasos & Daisy Hotel 3 *, located in the resort village of Moraitika. Today we will take a closer look at this hotel, and together we will figure out whether our compatriots liked to relax in it.





Location

As already mentioned, this hotel is located on the island of Corfu, which is part of the Hellenic Republic, in the resort village of Moraitika. The nearest major city of Kerkyra is 19 kilometers from the hotel. The airport is also located here. So after landing in Corfu's air harbor, it will take you no more than half an hour to get to the hotel. This fact will be especially appreciated by tourists going on vacation with young children. "Pump & Daisy Hotel" is not located in the very center of the village, so the sound of discos will not interfere with your vacation. As for the distance to the sea, the nearest beach to the hotel is just a three-minute walk away. There is also a bus stop just a few tens of meters away , from where you can reach any village on the island. In the vicinity of the hotel there is everything you need: shops, supermarkets, retail shops, tour desks, car rental offices and much more.





Nasos & Daisy Hotel 3 *: photo and description

This hotel was built in 1993. The last major reconstruction was carried out in 2004. Today the hotel offers guests to stay in one of the 79 rooms. It has standard rooms and studios. The entire housing stock of the hotel is located in several two- and three-story buildings. The territory of the hotel complex is small, but very cozy, green and well-groomed. There is a beautiful garden with exotic fruit trees. The hotel also has two outdoor pools (adult and children's), a sun terrace, a playground, a restaurant, a bar and parking. Free Wi-Fi is available in public areas of the hotel.

Hotel rooms

Nasos & Daisy Hotel 3 * (Greece) offers guests to stay in one of 79 comfortable rooms. There are both standard rooms and studios. All apartments have their own bathroom with shower, TV (there are Russian-language channels), telephone, refrigerator, individual air conditioning, as well as a balcony or terrace. Most of the rooms offer beautiful views of the hotel garden. There are also apartments with sea views. The rooms have ceramic tiles. Cleaning in the rooms of Nasos & Daisy Hotel 3 * is carried out every day. The maids update the bed linen and towels twice a week.





Beach vacation

From Nasos & Daisy Hotel 3 * to the nearest beach can be reached in just three minutes. The beaches here, according to our compatriots, are very good: sandy, clean, equipped with everything necessary. The entrance to the water is gentle, without stones. It is very convenient to relax with children. For the rental of sunbeds and umbrellas you will need to pay. However, you can always sit on your towel or mat right on the sand, on an unoccupied area with sun loungers. Near the beaches there are lots of cafes, bars, taverns, where you can always have a snack and refresh yourself with your favorite drinks.

Entertainment and infrastructure

For guests' convenience, Pump & Daisy Hotel has two swimming pools: one for adults and one for children. Next to them is a sun terrace with comfortable sun loungers and a bar. In addition, guests can enjoy playing ping pong or billiards.

The hotel reception is open 24 hours a day. Here you can leave valuables in a safe deposit box. The hotel has a restaurant serving breakfast and dinner.





Cost of living

The price for accommodation in this hotel is consistent with its category. So, a seven-day stay here for two will cost you in the amount of 350 euros.

Reviews of tourists from Russia about the hotel Nasos & Daisy Hotel 3 * (Corfu, Greece)

As many travelers today pay increased attention not only to the description of this or that hotel, but also to the comments of people who have already stayed in it, we decided to invite you to get acquainted with the impressions of our compatriots who happened to spend their holidays at the Pump & Daisy Hotel. Running a little ahead, we note that most tourists did not regret their choice at all. In their opinion, this hotel is fully consistent with its three-star category, cost, as well as accommodation conditions and level of service. But we will understand in more detail.





Impressions of travelers about the hotel itself and its territory

As for the rooms of Nasos & Daisy Hotel 3 *, our compatriots found them clean and comfortable. Although they are small in size, they can be accommodated quite comfortably. They are equipped with everything necessary. In particular, guests were pleased with the availability of a spacious closet with a lot of hangers, which, according to travelers, is a rarity for a hotel of any category. The furniture is not new, but in good condition and comfortable. Please note that the bathroom does not have a hairdryer. Therefore, if you need it, then grab it from home. There are also no safes in the rooms. But you can always use one at the reception (for an additional fee). Many guests enjoyed the panoramic windows and large balconies with tables and chairs. Most rooms offer views of the picturesque garden. However, there are apartments with sea views. As for Wi-Fi, the rooms do not have access to it. You can use this service in the hotel lobby. As for the air conditioner, then, as in most other hotels in Greece, its use is free only in July and August. However, according to tourists, you are unlikely to need this, so you can avoid such expenses.

Pleased guests and the quality of cleaning in rooms Nasos & Daisy Hotel 3 * (Moraitika). So, it was held six times a week with the exception of Sunday. Bed sheets and towels were changed twice a week. By the way, bedding and bath accessories are of very good quality.

The hotel territory was also very popular with travelers from Russia. Guests found her very green, clean and well maintained. There is a very beautiful garden of exotic trees. According to tourists, thanks to the green spaces, neither neighboring hotels nor the roadway are almost invisible. Many guests enjoyed spending time on the balconies of their rooms with a glass of excellent local wine, admiring the picturesque views. In addition, tourists who come to rest with children will not be disappointed. So, on a green well-kept lawn there is a playground for children.





Guest opinion on the location of the Pump & Daisy Hotel

Judging by the reviews of tourists, this hotel is quite well located. So, the center of the resort town can be reached in just seven minutes by leisurely step. Near the hotel there are supermarkets, souvenir shops, rental cars, motorbikes and scooters, tour agencies, bars, restaurants and much more. At the same time, Nasos & Daisy Hotel 3 * is located in a fairly quiet place, so here you will not be disturbed by the noise of entertainment venues, and you can relax in peace and quiet. In addition, the nearest beach can be reached in just three minutes. There is also a bus stop within a five-minute walk, from where you can reach any city on the island.

The opinion of the guests about the food and hotel staff

As for food, the situation in this hotel is as follows. According to our compatriots, breakfasts cannot be called particularly varied. So, hotel guests are offered several types of sausages, cheese, yogurt, bread, eggs, cereals, milk, muffins and a number of other products. It's impossible to stay hungry. For dinner, you will find a very varied menu and large portions. Also, according to tourists from Russia, the restaurant "Pump & Daisy Hotel" serves delicious tea, which is rarely seen in Greece.

As for the hotel staff, most travelers were also satisfied with it. So, according to the guests, the hotel staff are friendly, attentive and helpful people. In addition, everyone here speaks excellent English. In addition, the host is almost always at the hotel, who can be contacted with any question or request.





Impressions of beach relaxation and entertainment

According to many travelers, Nasos & Daisy Hotel 3 * (Corfu) is perfect for a beach holiday. So, the distance to the nearest beach is a couple of hundred meters, which can be overcome in just three minutes by an unhurried step. The beach here is sandy, clean and well equipped. For the rental of a set of two sun loungers and an umbrella, you will have to pay about six euros. However, many tourists also sat on a mat or towel brought with them right on the sand in a place unoccupied by sun loungers. For this, no one will demand money from you. Near the beach there are taverns and cafes where you can always have a bite to eat and refresh yourself with your favorite drink.

As for water activities at the hotel, here you will find an adult and a children's pool. According to the guests, the adult pool is quite deep. Children's is small and very comfortable. There are water mattresses. Near the pools there is a sun terrace with sun loungers and parasols.

The hotel does not offer other entertainment. For this reason, it is more suitable for family tourists and older people. Young people, accustomed to stormy and noisy entertainment, may seem a little boring here.

Conclusion

Based on the reviews of tourists from Russia, it can be concluded that Corfu Nasos & Daisy Hotel 3 * is a very good economical option for spending a vacation on the Ionian Sea. It is perfect for a beach holiday and is equipped with everything necessary for guests with children. According to our compatriots, the hotel is the best value for money.
