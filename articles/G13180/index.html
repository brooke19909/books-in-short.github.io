Knit crochet quick and easy

In order for the image to be original and unique, many stylists recommend the use of hats. You can wear them not only in winter, but also in autumn, spring and even in summer, the main thing is to choose the right material that will correspond to the season. That is why you can knit a crochet that will become not only a worthy accessory in the wardrobe, but also a unique little thing. There is nothing complicated in this process, choosing the appropriate scheme, you can translate into reality even the most complex pattern, which will decorate and complement the image.



All berets can be divided into winter and summer. First of all, the difference lies in the quality of the yarn that is used to make this little thing. Summer crochet is the thinnest matter, which consists of patterns and weaves. It is more like a web. For the manufacture of such an accessory, only the thinnest threads are used, and also it is knitted with a small hook. This allows you to make both large and small loops of yarn. A summer hat can be supplemented with beads or rhinestones, a small brooch will also become suitable. The main thing is to ensure that these elements do not "overwhelm" the beauty of the product itself.



The winter beret is crocheted from thick yarn, most often from wool. Such a product may have an additional bottom layer that will provide more heat. Winter hats can also be decorated with all kinds of rhinestones, brooches and stripes. A logical addition to a winter beret will be a scarf knitted from the same yarn. It is worth remembering that berets are French hats, so they always look sophisticated and sophisticated. Thanks to their dignity, they are able to make the image of a girl more elegant even in winter, when a large amount of warm clothes hides all the advantages of the figure.

As a rule, a crochet is knitted in a circular pattern, and after that a base or side is sewn separately to it. The process begins with the formation of loops, which will be located in the middle of the bottom, and gradually moves to the edges. This system allows you to make it smooth and proportional, to avoid distortions and other defects. The side of the beret is sewn (or tied) using the same hook. In rare cases, spokes are used to connect parts.



It can be crocheted both from a single yarn and from different types of threads. Using materials of different density and quality, you can achieve a very extraordinary effect, and the beret will become unique. Also, when we knit takes crochet, you can use threads of all kinds of colors, only you need to make sure that they are combined with each other. Such a motley headdress can be worn only under plain clothes, since by itself it will stand out too much.

There are a whole host of options for how to make crochet berets, and they are all worthy of attention. It is only important to choose the scheme, the pattern that will correspond to the style and mood of the owner of the thing. In this case, the accessory will look attractive and unique, it will become a worthy decoration of its owner.
