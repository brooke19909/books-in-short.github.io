Deposits of individuals as a traditional way of investing money

What do you pay attention to when choosing a profitable deposit?

When choosing a financial institution to trust money with, depositors usually turn their attention to at least two of the following parameters:

  1. The size of interest rates, which makes it possible to choose a contribution with maximum profit.
  2. The reliability of a banking institution, which allows you to not worry about the safety of savings even during a crisis.

Combining high interest rates and the necessary reliability can be quite difficult.

The main types of deposits for individuals

Today, banks offer a large number of various profitable deposits for individuals. In order to understand which deposits are the highest, you need to navigate the following groups of financial investments:

  1. Urgent at the highest interest. Opening such a deposit, the client gives the company his money for a certain period, and at this time he cannot take it back without losing a percentage. Also, it will not be possible to replenish your account.
  2. Refillable deposit. Opening such a deposit, depositors can save money, replenish the account, and along with this, interest also grows. True, you cannot withdraw funds and not lose profit.
  3. Deposit with partial withdrawal of finances without interest loss. Such deposits usually have the lowest rates. But they provide an opportunity to take a share of the funds on a pre-agreed minimum balance, the amount of which will be calculated on interest.
