Riva Bodrum Resort 4 *. Bodrum 4 Star Hotels

Are you going to Turkey? Try this time not the coast of Antalya, but the amazing Bodrum or its surroundings! If you are an active person who likes to hang out, look at nature and historical sights, then this area is for you! The city will offer you a choice of fourteen exceptionally picturesque bays. It is preferred by the "traveling" nations - the French and the Scandinavians. Well, if you like surfing, you will probably think that you are in paradise. The best place for the strength and direction of the winds in Turkey simply can not be found. One of the good and budget places for vacation in this resort, tourists call Riva Bodrum Resort 4 (Bodrum). He is very decent, has a good reputation, pleasant management and well-trained staff. Having such hotels, Turkey may well qualify for European service.



Bodrum, Gumbet, Bitez

This city is unique in its attractions. The castle, beautiful museums, trips in the surroundings on wooden boats, picturesque islands and rocks - all that your soul desires! Therefore, many tourists, getting here, book themselves an "azure journey" - a real sea cruise to the local beauties. Well, and, of course, it is worth visiting one of the seven wonders of the world, located nearby - Halicarnassus. Shopping here is fascinating, but quite expensive for Turkey. If you are looking for something authentic, take a trip to the village of Chokmagdak. Here you can look at how the Turks live “in the outback”, as well as watch how to manually make silk and weave carpets. On the island of Kara Ada (Black) you can visit the hot natural springs. And if you have a “Schengen” and time, you can even take a ferry to Greek Rhodes.

The best beaches of the resort are located in the bays of Gumbet and Bitez. The first of them is very close to the airport, as well as to numerous entertainment centers. In addition, beaches are better here than in Bodrum itself, and very picturesque cliffs overgrown with pine trees. This place has long been chosen by European youth, but Russian tourists are also increasingly coming here. The nearby Bitez Bay, resembling a crescent shape, has a blue flag for the cleanliness of the beaches, and the slopes of its mountains are completely covered with citrus. There are many secluded places, and the rest is calmer than in Gumbet.



Five Star Hotels

Bodrum hotels stretched as much as twenty-five kilometers. This is evidenced by the resort map of hotels. Bodrum (Turkey) is a fairly old resort, and hotels began to be built here since the seventies of the last century. In the city itself there are small budget "treshki", and outside it - upscale palaces. Many Bodrum "five stars" hotels are better than Antalya. They work both with the all inclusive and ultra systems. In Gumbet there are also quite a few such hotels. The best of these is WOW Bodrum Resort. A huge territory, a lot of services and entertainment, its own water park ... No wonder it tops the rating of Bodrum hotels. A lot of services and interesting bonuses can be offered by this hotel even to the most avid sybarites. The first places in the reviews are also occupied by Kariya Princess and Ersan Resort and Spa.

Bodrum 4 star hotels. Budget hotels

The “fours” of the resort are good for those tourists who want to get excellent service for little money. Almost all of them have excellent stylish rooms with all necessary equipment, cosmetics and even a bathrobe with slippers. Near the sea there are many such Bodrum hotels. Line 1, of course, is mainly occupied by the Fives, but also a place near the beach was found for the Quartet. Therefore, most hotels of this class offer their guests snacks and drinks on the beach. Sun loungers and sunbeds for guests are free, the beach is clean and equipped. Examples include hotels like Bodrum Boutique, Salmakis, and Latania Beach Resort. In Gumbet, because of the rocky and mountainous terrain, “fives” are located on the first line. But the Quartet often use their beaches and even sometimes bars, and they provide their guests with a free shuttle to the sea. Gumbet's “treshki” are located even further from the sea. Here, the price of accommodation is lower, the territory too, and there is practically no animation. But its absence is compensated by the proximity to the discotheques and nightclubs of the village.



Riva Bodrum Resort

This hotel is located on a cliff, on the border of two beautiful bays - Gumbet and Bitez. It is located five kilometers from the center of Bodrum. 2 km separate it from the resort village of Gumbet. Bitez Bay is famous for its unique, stunning views that any guest can contemplate without leaving the hotel. Despite the countryside location, the hotel is in close proximity to shopping centers, the “street of bars” of Gumbet and many restaurants that Bodrum and its surroundings are famous for. This is a very fun place where music plays and various interesting events take place. To the beach belonging to the Ambrosia Hotel, six hundred meters. This hotel is urban. It was built at the end of the last century, and in two thousand and seven was a good repair.

Infrastructure and Services

The hotel has a fairly large territory - ten square kilometers. It is divided into several levels. Each has a pool and bar. Very beautiful, green, picturesque stairs lead up and down. Riva Bodrum Resort 4 features a mini market. Almost anywhere he catches "Wi-Fi", free for guests. For tourists there are many opportunities not only to relax, but also to improve their health - a fitness center, gym, sauna and hammam. There is a laundry, dry cleaning, medical office, hairdresser. In the evenings, entertainers entertain. After the show, they take guests to discos and clubs in Gumbet. The reception is open 24 hours a day.



Rooms

The Riva Bodrum Resort 4 has ninety-six club rooms. They are located in twenty-eight cottages surrounded by trees. These rooms are small - fifteen square meters, but equipped with all amenities (air conditioning, plasma TV, minibar) and have French balconies. In the main building of the hotel there are another forty-eight standard rooms for vacationers. They are larger - their area is twenty-four square meters, almost all have sea views. In addition, the hotel has fourteen family rooms and six suites. The rooms have excellent soundproofing. Drinks in the minibar are free on the day of arrival. The beds are good, comfortable, everything works. The linen is very clean. Change it several times a week. Among the television programs there are two Russian channels.



Nutrition

Riva Bodrum Resort 4 has a restaurant with an outdoor terrace overlooking the bay and sea. There are several bars, including near the pools. The all inclusive system starts at ten in the morning and ends at eleven in the evening. The main restaurant serves buffet breakfast, lunch and dinner. Bars offer snacks, drinks, cocktails and desserts. The food is good - fresh, varied. Many types of meat and poultry - lamb, chicken, turkey, beef. Drinks are poured from bottles. From alcohol they give Ephesus beer, wine and brandy. A lot of cheeses, vegetables, olives, dried fruits. Buns and puffs are baked right in the restaurant. Shawarma and grilled steaks are prepared.



Beach

The hotel has no coast. However, Riva Bodrum Resort 4 provides a free shuttle to the beach. Sand, convenient entry. There is a platform in the water. The sea is not hot here, but rather fresh. The water temperature is 22-24 degrees constantly. The beach is equipped, all accessories for guests are free. If you walk along the promenade, you can also find places with sunbeds and umbrellas. And for free. Most people go to the sea on foot, and then go back to the hotel minibus. The hotel at three levels has three outdoor pools, with children's departments. Around them are spacious sunbeds with sun loungers and umbrellas. Each pool has its own bar.
