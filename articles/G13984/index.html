Hotel Caribbean World Resort Borj Cedria in Tunisia: description, photos and reviews

Many people who dream to spend their holidays in some hot exotic country choose Tunisia. This place has various "star" hotels, where you can stay for a while. A good option would be the Caribbean World Resort Borj Cedria. In some descriptions, it is declared as a 3-star hotel. In others, it is assigned 4 *. general information

The Caribbean World Resort Borj Cedria is located in a place called Borj Cedria. This is one of the suburbs of Tunisia. They began to actively build it not very long ago - in the early 2000s. And the place is really profitable, from a tourist point of view.

Firstly, the Borj Sedria is equally close to both Tunisia and Hammamet. This town is located between them. Accordingly, the airport is also nearby. Although most tourists arrive at Habib Bourguiba International Airport, located in Monastir. It is about 150 kilometers from Borj Sedria.

The hotel itself is located in close proximity to the sea. The beach can be reached in two minutes. But you need to go to the main local attractions, since they are all concentrated in neighboring megacities. In 15 minutes you can reach the Catholic Church of St. Louis, which is located on Beers Hill. There, the tourists will see the ruins of ancient Carthage - the Phoenician state.

In general, the location at the hotel is good in every sense. In addition, in Borj Sedria itself there are a lot of different cafes, bars, shops and restaurants, a visit to which makes the rest even more interesting and eventful.

Service

The Caribbean World Resort Borj Cedria has everything you need for a vacationer. Free high-speed Wi-Fi is distributed throughout the entire complex; the lobby has a 24-hour reception, currency exchange, and a luggage storage room. In addition, the complex also has a laundry with dry cleaning, where guests can also iron clothes.

The hotel also has a souvenir shop and babysitting services that can help out guests who take their children with them on vacation. There is even a medical office. The doctor, if necessary, can be called to the room.

In addition to the above, the hotel has a mini market and a scooter rental service. Upon individual request, a parking space can be provided. By the way, the staff at the Caribbean World Resort Borj Cedria speaks not only Arabic, but also English and French. Nobody speaks Russian, but all the guests are treated with the same respect and friendliness, which is noted by almost every traveler who came here.





Entertainment

Nowadays, the hotel is not only a temporary place of residence, but also an entertainment complex. Every tourist is pleased to know that in the hotel he is waiting for not only good service and comfortable rooms, but also various ways to diversify his leisure.

In the Caribbean World Resort Borj Cedria 3 * you will not be bored. The complex has two outdoor pools, one of which is for adults, and the second for children. There are also several sun terraces with sun loungers and parasols. There is even a mini water park with several slides and a large deep freshwater pool. And in the immediate vicinity - a clean beach with white sand, which also has sun loungers and umbrellas.

The animation staff makes every effort to brighten up the leisure of its guests. In the Caribbean World Resort Borj Cedria 3 * conduct various entertainment events - competitions, games, beach volleyball and football tournaments. But that is not all. Here you can do water polo and aqua aerobics, darts and archery, attend a dance workshop, play tennis (there are three professional courts). And from 23:00 a disco is organized on site.

If this is not to your liking, then you can go windsurfing, kayaking, "bananas", water skiing, and motorboats. Parasailing is also available.

By the way, while adults are resting, their children can have fun in the mini-club, where they accept everyone, aged from four to 14 years. This institution is open from 10:00 to 17:00. Children will be supervised - educators and animators work in the mini-club.

Good to know

Many of the reviews left about the rest at the Caribbean World Resort Borj Cedria (Tunisia) reviews make avid travelers a desire to go here too. But before you book your flights, you should familiarize yourself with some of the nuances regarding hotel accommodation and booking.

Check-in starts at 13:00. Guests, if they arrived earlier, can be settled immediately, but only if the hotel is not crowded. However, it is better to play it safe and find the most convenient flight. So on arrival, in which case, did not have to wait. Although, according to tourists who were here, things can be left in the storage room, and after that - go for breakfast.
