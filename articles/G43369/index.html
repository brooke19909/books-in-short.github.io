Car DVR Neoline X-COP 9100: owner reviews, review and specifications

The combination of several functions in one device has long been an attractive solution for both manufacturers and users of automotive equipment. Moreover, among the advantages of this concept are noted not only ergonomic qualities in the form of simplified control and space saving on the dashboard, but also operational operational advantages. The direct connection between different devices for their intended purpose allows them to mutually process information with higher accuracy and speed. In part, this set of positive qualities is provided by the Neoline X-COP 9100 video recorder and radar detector, representing a middle-class hybrid type apparatus.





Model Overview

For Neoline, the development of functional combined instruments is not a novelty. And even before the model entered the market, experts in the segment were looking forward to its success, because this time the creators were faced with the task of offering not a basic hybrid with typical capabilities, but something more developed and technologically advanced. In many respects, the expectations were justified, and it is not surprising that it was the expansion of the list of functions that became the main feature of the modification. New inclusions are based on the platform of the radar detector and DVR, the operation of which is supported by a satellite navigator.

Of course, there were some flaws, but they are mostly cosmetic in nature and generally do not change the picture. Therefore, we can say that the main category of target consumers should at least get acquainted with the Neoline X-COP 9100 car DVR. Prices for the device, taking into account working qualities and functionality, are also acceptable - from 14 to 17 thousand rubles. average. This is not the top of the segment at all, but rather a solid solution for those who focus on the practicality of the proposed tools, not wanting to save on quality or, on the contrary, overpay for extra "chips" from premium link models.

Construction and design

The filling of the recorder and detector is placed in a small case, which is conveniently attached to the windshield by the mentioned complete retainer. The power cable also communicates with the mount through a special connector on the bracket. For dismantling or installation of the device, disassembling of the fixing structure is not required - the housing is easily removed from the gripping niche along the slide rails.





By the way, the 12-24V power supply connector is duplicated, which indicates the detailed thoughtfulness of the physical ergonomics of the Neoline X-COP 9100 case. Owner reviews on the stylistic performance are generally restrained. The box is compact, nothing stands out. A display was organically located on it, along the side zones of which 4 buttons were scattered for control. It is noteworthy that the DVR received a modern IPS-matrix, so there will be no problems with visibility familiar to this type of device. The main button for launching the device is placed on the right side, as it is rarely used and its concealment is justified. On the same side is a slot for a memory card.

Instrument Features

Despite the hybrid orientation, the main emphasis in the creation of the device was placed on the functions of the radar detector. In particular, the developers focused on the accurate detection of speed detectors with minimal distraction of the motorist with false positives. But to achieve this effect, a third function was introduced - navigational radar positioning. It is implemented through an integrated receiver of signals about geolocation. This is also not a new idea for such devices, but this model received a two-system module that works with both GLONASS and GPS. Therefore, the Neoline X-COP 9100 reviews in terms of protection against false signals and positioning accuracy are better than those of direct competitors of the model. It cannot be said that against this background, the operation of the DVR looks like an auxiliary toolkit, but it is made according to the established standard and offers nothing fundamentally new.

Additional functional support

The device is equipped with several sensors that allow you to record movement and shock - in particular, a G-sensor is provided. The time, date, and speed are indicated on the record. Sound recording options provided by a built-in microphone and speaker are separately presented. That is, unlike the previous generation models, this version allows you to fully obtain information about the recorded material without the help of third-party monitors and a computer.





As for the shooting itself, it provides nighttime operation, WDR function and photography. Voice prompts also facilitate the operation of the Neoline X-COP 9100. The registrar can act as a parking assistant, giving the car owner sound signals. We can say that the model comprehensively services the movement process, protecting the user from fixing violations and at the same time making driving safe.

Customization options

Immediately after installation, it is recommended to fully check the operation parameters of the device. At the basic level, the time zone, time, resolution of the video, the interval for automatically turning off the display, the sensitivity of the detectors, etc. are set. You should also remember to enter the stamp of the license plate number of the machine. By the way, there is a function to turn off the speed mode in the stamp if it exceeds 100 km / h.

In the part of the radar settings, the voice notification mode, auto-muting of the sound and the speed limit at which the device will not respond at all with warnings are indicated. Many customer reviews on the Neoline X-COP 9100 model have a positive effect on fixed-range settings. These are the parameters that can be purposefully set for different types of police radars with the designation of frequencies and threshold speed values.
