Acer P1500 Projector Overview

Choosing a good projector home, I want it to not take up too much space, have good characteristics, clearly transmit the image and last as long as possible. Today, the market has many different models at attractive prices, but not everyone combines all of the above features. You can certainly buy the first device you like, but it's better to pay attention to the Acer P1500. This very compact portable projector has excellent characteristics, gives a clear picture, and you can even take it with you when traveling.

Contents of delivery

The device comes in a small cardboard box. On the packaging you can immediately see the photos of the model, as well as read the main features and specifications. Inside the box, the user will find a completely normal delivery set, which consists of: instructions, warranty card, Acer P1500 projector itself, remote control, USB cable and power cord. In some cases, VGA and HDMI cables may still be included.

Appearance

The projector looks very interesting and attractive. In terms of size, this is a very compact device. Width is 264 mm, height 78 and length 220 mm. The device weighs a little more than 2 kg.

On the front of the Acer P1500 is the lamp eye. Above are buttons for manual control and projector settings. On the back there are all the main connectors for connecting: RS-232, micro-USB, HDMI, S-Video analog input, RCA video input, VGA In, VGA Out, 2 RCA connectors for audio and a power outlet.

The bottom of the projector has only 4 legs with rubber pads to provide better surface stability.

As regards build quality, the Acer P1500 is doing well. All parts and parts are tightly fitted to each other, there are no gaps, creaks or backlashes. As materials, good matte plastic is used, on which fingerprints practically do not remain.

Features and Features

It's time to go to the characteristics of the device. As a light source, there is a P-VIP lamp with a brightness of up to 3000 lumens. This allows you to get a good light output, through which an image with a high level of contrast, brightness and good color reproduction is transmitted. The maximum distance from the projector to the wall can reach up to 7.6 meters, and the minimum - 1.5 m. The lamp power is 210 watts, and the service life reaches up to 5000 hours.

The maximum resolution of the output image is 1920 x 1080. There is support for HDTV and 3D. The frame rate varies from 50 to 120 Hz. Also, the Acer P1500 has the ability to digitally double zoom and optical zoom.

The image quality is quite high. Thanks to FullHD-resolution, the picture looks clear. With color rendering and contrast, everything is in order. The only saturation can sometimes be not quite enough, but it can be easily fixed through the menu settings.

Of the additional features, it is worth noting the presence of automatic focusing, manual cropping of the image, the function of correcting image distortion and the presence of an economical mode that extends the life of the lamp.

User reviews

Well, at the end of the review Acer P1500 - reviews. In general, users praise the projector for its excellent image quality, compactness, affordable price, support for Full HD and 3D images, clear focus and quiet cooling system. The disadvantages are perhaps not the best built-in speaker, only the vertical projection setting and a non-standard connector for mounting on the bracket.
