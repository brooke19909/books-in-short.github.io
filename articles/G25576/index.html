"Pokrovskoye" - rest house: reviews, address. Rest in the suburbs

Escaping at least a weekend from the bustling city bustle, forgetting about the tedious everyday household chores, enjoying the fresh air, silence and the pacifying beauties of nature is the dream of many residents of megalopolises.

But where in the vicinity of Moscow is that corner, which you will later recall with quiet sadness and nostalgia, where would you like to come again and again?

In the suburbs there are holiday homes and pensions for every taste and size of wallet, from budget to VIP, the price range of which consists of indicators such as the degree of distance from Moscow, the range of services, and the features of accommodation. In country hotels and holiday homes, guests are offered a wide range of wellness services, cultural and entertainment programs.





One of the most popular is the Pokrovskoye Rest House (Odintsovo District).

Acquaintance

It turns out that it is quite possible to improve health and regain strength in 2-3 days, and at the same time do without tiring long flights, changing time zones and climatic conditions.

Rest house "Pokrovskoye" (Odintsovo district) is located 38 km from the capital, in one of the most picturesque corners of the Moscow Region, among the clean springs and green forests of the Zvenigorod region, sung by poets in poetry.

The lake with clear water, magnificent, spread over the vast territory of the boarding house, a forest park with coniferous plantings, wonderful air and beautiful views - the places have a rest and contribute to healing.

The boarding house has long been known as the winner of the competition for the best organization of the tourism industry in the region (2007), who glorified the Odintsovo district of the Moscow region. It is the center of leisure, where the residents of the capital and surrounding cities most eagerly flock to their free.

Active summer recreation, exciting New Year and Christmas festive events, substantial leisure at any time of the year, in conditions combining the impossible - the pristine nature and the benefits of civilization - all this is Pokrovskoye, a holiday home.

How to get there

Those who wish can come here by personal or public transport. The following driving directions to Pokrovskoye Holiday House are offered:

  • How to get there by personal transport? It is necessary to follow from MKAD to the 44th kilometer along the Minsk highway. Then turn right and drive another 3 km to Mozhayskoye Shosse, after which turn to Golitsyno. After 51 km, turn onto Pokrovskoye (to the right, following the sign). After 4 km, before reaching the church, there will be a fork, here you should turn left (there is a sign on the billboard) and drive another 2 km to the entrance to the boarding house.

Interest Ask

A question is circulating on the Web, which has a certain character of scandalousness: who owns the Pokrovskoye holiday home? Users retell each other a story that happened back in 2011.

In early October, with the participation of a security company, a raider seizure of Pokrovskoye Rest House CJSC was carried out. According to media reports, professional guards did not limit themselves in the methods of action, physical force was used, what was happening resembled a picture from a Hollywood action movie. The seizure became possible due to forgery of documents by a number of persons, including the general director of the enterprise and the wife of the owner of the company. The cause of the conflict was the divorce proceedings of the owner of the holiday home, businessman D. Pogorelov, and his wife Pak N.V., who filed a lawsuit on the dissolution of the marriage and acquired the company using fake documents. According to the statement of the shareholders of the company, a criminal case was opened on the fact of fraud.

It would be interesting for potential vacationers to find out that at the moment justice has been restored, fraudsters have been convicted and property has been transferred to their rightful owner. The largest recreation center Pokrovsky in the Moscow Region , which simultaneously hosts up to 1700 vacationers, belongs to D. Yu. Pogorelov, who has owned the company since the early 2000s.
