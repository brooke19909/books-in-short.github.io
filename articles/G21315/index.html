"Alol" is a recreation center. Pskov region, Alol camp site

The Pskov region has long established itself as a welcoming and fertile land. She is revered and loved for the most beautiful protected areas. Scattered ponds, deep lakes, winding rivers, swamps and forests are scattered throughout the region. This magnificence cannot but rejoice, it creates unique natural landscapes.

The earth is famous for its mineral and mud springs, which bring tremendous benefits to humans. Tourists are drawn to these lands for wellness, silence and good rest. Favorable climatic conditions contribute to this. In addition to the natural beauties of people, many historical and cultural objects attract, without them cognitive leisure is unthinkable.





They enrich our knowledge of the great Russian land. Pskov region does not experience a lack of tourists. All recreational facilities, resorts and hotels are harmoniously blended with pine forests and offer paradise conditions for a relaxed and relaxing pastime.

Compared to most hotels and pensions, the Alol complex stands out. The recreation center operates year-round. Almost always the hotel is full, so it is recommended to reserve a room in advance before leaving.

Location and Description

Among the spreading trees, near the surface of Lake Zverino, in the cozy and picturesque village of Kholuny (Pustoshkinsky district) there is a colorful tourist site ‚ÄúAlol‚Äù. The recreation center is a pure oasis, surrounded by greenery, where you can completely immerse yourself in the bliss of pleasure and freshness. The guests of the resort are couples, companies and adults who seek to isolate themselves for a while from the bustling city and be alone with nature.





The hotel has been for many years, its opening took place in 1971. For the comfort of people, seasonal cottages with comfortable rooms for 100 people and a small bathhouse were built. Educational routes were organized along the outstanding sights of the region. There were regularly held sports competitions.

The Alol resort (Pskov region) was constantly ennobled, developed, and the emphasis was on the quality of service. He enjoyed great popularity among Muscovites and local residents. A few years later, with the advent of the new management, the hotel became even better: new residential complexes, bars, a Russian sauna appeared, and excursion routes expanded.

By the way, with the same name in the Pskov region there is a flowing lake Alol up to 16 m deep. This is a favorite place for fishermen, almost every resident is engaged in fishing, which brings a good income. Crayfish, eels, pike, tench, ide and crucian carp are found in the pond.

Residence

On a well-groomed landscaped area housed residential complexes: summer houses, two-story cottages, winter buildings and one fisherman's house. The most comfortable and popular among tourists are triple cottages with heating, balconies (panorama of the forest) and a private bathroom.





Settlement in summer houses is carried out only in the warm season. They are equipped with basic furniture and a shared (in a separate building) toilet / shower.

The fisherman's house is designed for 7 people. It is located right on the lake, which is very convenient. Heated with a stove, the rooms are also equipped with modern furniture and a TV. At the house there is an outdoor terrace where you can cook kebabs and grill fish.

Nutrition

Alol Chalet has a spacious dining area and a bar. For your information - meals are not included in the payment. It should be noted that the cost of a full diet is acceptable, ranging from 500-1000 rubles. It includes a morning meal, a full dinner and a varied dinner.

Service Organization

Resting in the complex, you will not only gain strength, relax near the water's edge, but you can also have plenty of fun, take informative car and water tours to the reserved, historical and cultural places of the region. There is a boat station in a boarding house, which the whole Pskov region enjoys using. Chalet "Alol" offers everyone an extreme canoeing route, as well as hiking.





A rental point for renting sports equipment and fishing equipment works on site. Literature lovers will appreciate the rich library. Finnish and Russian baths will not leave anyone indifferent. Well, in the summer the beach opens with clear water. Here, far from human eyes, you can take sunbaths and enjoy plenty of fun.

Entertainment

Resting in nature, especially near a reservoir, it is impossible to deny yourself fishing, this is what the administration of the Alol Hotel suggests doing. The recreation center for these purposes provides the necessary equipment and boats. Even those who do not know and do not like to do this, after the first catch, become avid fishermen. In the local lake there are a lot of diverse fish from crucian carp to perch. After capture, everyone is involved in the preparation of fish soup.





For fans of extreme sports this summer opened a unique amusement park - rope routes laid along trees with swinging swings and vines. This is a real test for those who want to test themselves for strength and courage. The length of the tracks is 400 meters, of varying difficulty. You can get a dose of adrenaline on the troll (downhill), the height of which is 15 m.

Children are allowed at least 120 cm. To pass it is not at all necessary to have special knowledge - introductory instruction is carried out on the spot, and the test subject is provided with protective climbing equipment. While climbing tourists put on safety belts.

Hotel through the eyes of tourists: reviews

Most of the guests who spent their weekend in this cozy place were satisfied: the cleanliness both in the rooms and on the territory made a pleasant impression. People expressed special thanks to the attentive and courteous staff. There were no complaints about nutrition. The excellent menu and homely atmosphere make the pastime unforgettable at the Alol Hotel.

The recreation center is distinguished by hospitality and friendliness. True, some noted that it used to be quieter and calmer here than today. More and more often young companies come here who love to listen to loud music and dance, and this prevents those who want privacy and peace. The administration suppresses this kind of entertainment and keeps order.
