How to choose the right phone

What was luxury yesterday unattainable to mere mortals is a necessity today. In our country, there was a time when not every citizen could have a cell phone. The cost of the services of operators and the first devices equipped with a SIM card was fabulous. However, today almost everyone has a telephone. Manufacturers produce a wide variety of models, which allows customers to purchase exactly the version of the cellular that suits them best.



How to choose a phone? It would seem that this is the simplest question. You need to go to the nearest specialty store, take any model you like and pay a certain amount for it. Does it really matter which phone to choose? However, this is not such a simple matter. Your model should serve you for many years, working properly.

What you need to consider if you decide to choose a phone? First, why do you need it? Purely for communication or also, for example, to listen to music? Perhaps the correct answer to this question will help you save a certain amount. If you do not need any functions, then why do you overpay for them?



Also pay attention to the battery. It will depend on its type for how long the phone will last without charging. There are several types of batteries. Nickel metal hydrides have a relatively low cost and high volume. Such batteries must be periodically brought to a complete discharge. As a rule, they are installed on inexpensive phones.

Lithium-ion batteries have more capacity and compactness. They are more expensive than nickel-metal hydride, although their shelf life is limited to one and a half years. It is not recommended to keep them in a fully discharged state or use at low temperature.

Lithium polymer batteries have a large capacity.

Pay attention to options that may require additional energy costs, in particular, the presence of a keyboard backlight.
