Bagripsh is a boarding house in the most comfortable corner of Abkhazia

On the slope of the Gagra Range, only 12 km from the border with Russia, is located Bagripsh - a boarding house in which there is everything for a comfortable stay at any time of the year. At 100 meters from the residential buildings is a well-maintained beach. Palm trees, cypresses and pines grow in the complex. Cozy rooms offer breathtaking mountain views.

Bagripsh - comfortable rest with Abkhazian hospitality

The guesthouse is located on a hill in a very picturesque area. The main residential building was built in 2009. In 2011, they carried out major repairs, landscaped the territory and erected another building designed to accommodate vacationers.





Bagripsh is a year-round guesthouse. On its territory there is a heated outdoor pool, which you can swim in at any time of the year. The guesthouse is ideal for families with children. In addition to entertainment venues for adult visitors, the Bagripsh Holiday House has a children's room, playgrounds, and children's parties are regularly held.

Accommodations

If you like the city of Gagra, boarding house Bagripsh is an ideal place to stay during your vacation. It is much calmer here than in a large resort. The distance from the boarding house to Gagra is only 14 km. Bagripsh has 260 rooms for vacationers. This is a one-room and two-room apartments.





The boarding house offers its guests a choice of accommodation in a nine-story building or a four-story new building. Each room has sea or mountain views. Guests are invited to completely relax and forget about everyday problems. Each room has its own bathroom, as well as all the necessary furniture and appliances (including air conditioning). Cleaning is done daily. All rooms are double, for an additional fee it is possible to provide one more bed.

Additional services and entertainment

It is difficult to find a country as hospitable as Abkhazia. Pension “Bagripsh” is trying to comply with national traditions. The complex has a cozy restaurant with a summer terrace, a cafe and a bar. In the evenings, discos are held here, where every guest can relax and dance. The pension has a gym, a cinema, a billiard room and a table tennis area. It is difficult to imagine a vacation on the coast without swimming and tanning.





The private beach of the holiday home is only 100 meters from the residential buildings. For the convenience of vacationers, descent to it is carried out by elevator. On the beach there are sun umbrellas, sun loungers and changing cabins. Bagripsh is a boarding house that offers its guests rental of water transport and equipment for outdoor activities. In the active tourist season, children's parties are held daily. The guesthouse has its own travel agency, offering organized trips to the most interesting sights.

Prices for room and board

How much does it cost to rest on the warm coast of Abkhazia? Bagripsh is a boarding house that invariably pleases its guests with affordable prices. The minimum cost of living here for one adult is 1400 rubles per day. Three meals a day according to the "buffet" system are included in the price of accommodation. Accommodation in the most expensive double junior suite costs 1800 rubles per day for one adult. Children under 5 years old can stay with their parents without paying extra bed. For the youngest guests of the boarding house, meals are ordered separately. There are discounts when placing children under 12 years old on an extra bed in the parents room. The cost of accommodation in the pension includes meals, access to the pool and beach, Wi-Fi and use of parking. All additional services are paid separately according to the price list.

Guest reviews

Bagripsh is a boarding house, reviews of which you can often find good. This holiday home has a favorable geographical location. The boarding house was built in a very picturesque and calm place, from which it is not difficult to get to the large resort cities of Abkhazia. It is never boring, thanks to the developed infrastructure and an abundance of various entertainment.





What is especially nice, the boarding house is located very close to the Russian border. And this means that if you want to get to it you can by public transport or private car. For vacationers who value maximum comfort, a transfer is offered from the border to the boarding house and in the opposite direction upon departure. If you like Abkhazia, boarding house “Bagripsh” is a great option for accommodation on vacation. Staying here, you can relax your body and soul, as well as fully enjoy the national hospitality of the locals.
