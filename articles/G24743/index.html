How to make a titmouse costume with your own hands

A titmouse costume will help your daughter stand out from the crowd. The image of a bird, which symbolizes the advent of winter, will be appropriate to look at a children's matinee. How to make such a suit? Read about it below.

Prefabricated version





The image of the titmouse should be drawn up in the color contrast of blue and yellow. And since any girl wants to go to the party in the dress, it is necessary to choose the clothes of the festive style in the chosen color scheme. The titmouse costume will be made from a free blue dress. It should be sewn from light fabric, so that when moving it creates the illusion of flight. On top of this dress you need to wear a yellow corset. If the girl does not have such an element in the wardrobe, then you can replace it with a beautiful sleeveless blouse. The main thing is to maintain proper harmony so that the top is not too yellow. If nevertheless you have not found the optimal combination, you can complement the image with another blue layer. It will turn out very realistic - the bottom and back will be blue, and the stomach is yellow. So, the do-it-yourself costume of the titmouse is almost ready, it remains to make a hat. To do this, you need to take a blue hat and sew on it a yellow fur trim. From papier-m√¢ch√© or just cardboard, you need to make a cone-beak. Now the matter remains for small: stick eyes to the hat. If desired, you can decorate the bird's head with black fur.

Costume for the matinee





This option is similar to the previous one, but only with the difference that it will have to be sewn, and not assembled from existing clothing. To make a titmouse costume you will need yellow and blue fabric. The first thing you need to do from a blue material is to sew a sun skirt. As a lining, you can choose a yellow fabric. Now we turn to the manufacture of the top. We sew a yellow vest on a pattern of a usual rectangle. Openings for the hands should be left in the side seams. The sleeves will be blue and tiered. Originally, they will look if the edge of each tier ends in a wave symbolizing feathers. It remains to sew a hat. It will consist of three parts. The right and left will be yellow and the center blue. It remains to attach a beak and sew eyes.

Funny tit





Making such a suit is easy. To make it, you will need velveteen shorts. Put them on top of white tights. It is advisable to pick up a turtleneck of the same color. A yellow vest will help make the costume recognizable. In order for the image of the bird to look winter, it will be necessary to trim some parts of the costume with fur, for example, trouser-legs of shorts and a vest around the perimeter. The matter remained small, namely: two semicircles should be cut out of velvet or velvet, the length of which should be equal to the length of the children's hand from the wrist to the shoulder. These details should also be trimmed with fur, and you can still make an applique of stripes in the middle of the wing. The wings will be attached to the hands with rubber bands. They need to be sewn at least three. The titmouse costume is almost ready. It remains to make a fur hat, and a little lower crown should be sewn black fur. A cardboard beak and paper eyes should be attached to the front of the headgear.

Titmouse in ruffles





This image is incredibly feminine and tender. It is suitable for absolutely any girl and even a girl. It consists of a skirt, top and bolero. Sewing a titmouse costume for a girl is a laborious process. Let's start with the skirt. To make it, you will need a piece of fabric and a wide elastic band. We cut the material obliquely into strips 10, 20 and 30 cm long. Now from two edges they need to be processed with a zigzag stitch or with an overlock. On one side, lace should be sewn to each strip. Now you can collect the skirt. Sew frills to a wide elastic band. The yellow topic also needs to be decorated with lace. For this, thin strips of a beautiful frill of the same color as the decorative finish of the skirt should be sewn into the seams of the product. From blue fabric you need to make a bolero. So that it does not come off, gum must be sewn on the wrists, and ties should be sewn at the neck level. The collar and sleeves must be trimmed with lace.

If desired, you can make a shirt shirt. It lies on a slanting of yellow fabric. It is necessary to process its edges with an overlock, then sew lace to one of the sides. This decorative element needs to be attached to the bolero collar. It remains to make a hat. To do this, find a hat in tone, sew a small veil and several feathers on it.
