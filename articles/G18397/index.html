Original DIY made money cake





Sometimes you have to think about choosing an original gift. Often this happens when the person you want to congratulate has almost everything. Or, on the contrary, you know that a person does not have enough money to acquire the things of his dreams. Or a dream is intangible. Let's say it's a language course or a visit to a beauty salon. And just giving money is corny, not very convenient or indecent. Situations are different.

In this case, there is an original solution. This is a cake made of money, made with your own hands. Make it very simple. It takes quite a bit of time to make a cake out of money. And many have the material for work at hand.

Presentation material

To work, you need to have about one hundred banknotes, which, you decide for yourself, depending on the value of the gift. You will need a sheet of whatman paper, glue and paper clips. The number of paper clips should correspond to the number of notes and should be doubled. In this case, you need to add a few pieces to reinforce the design of the cake itself. And an important detail is a beautiful bow. This is the minimum set of necessary material in order to make a cake out of money with your own hands. The amount of material may increase depending on your imagination associated with decorating a gift.





Work stages

Now we will consider in detail how to make a cake out of money with your own hands. Parts of the cake frame are cut from the Whatman paper. The first detail is a circle, you choose the diameter yourself, it approximately corresponds to the diameter of the cake you conceived. Then the sides of the product are cut out, their number depends on the number of conceived floors. The width of the first side is equal to the height of the bill in a twisted form. But to this width it is necessary to add 1.5‚Äì2 cm to the hem, which is glued to the base circle. The length of the bead is equal to the circumference of the base. The width of the second side corresponds to the height of two twisted bills. The length of the second side is equal to the length of the first minus four diameters of the rolled bill, which is approximately equal to 8 cm, this size depends on how you twist the money. Further by analogy. The height of the third side is equal to the three heights of the bill. The length is equal to the length of the second minus 8 cm.

Assembly assembly

We glue the parts. Glue the bottom board to the base circle.
Then you need to assemble the structure. First of all, you need to roll up bills. We fasten them with paper clips, while leaving a tail. If you work with dollars, try to twist the front side out. Now you need to attach money to the top edge of the board. This should be done in a circle, placing each bill on the left tail. In this way, it is necessary to close each side. Making a cake is very simple. Insert a second into the circle of the lower side. In the second - the third. For rigidity, the structure must be secured with paper clips.





Decoration

In conclusion, it is necessary to decorate the cake with money (the photo of which must be left as a keepsake): at the top, fix the cooked bow. Here you can show your imagination. For example, the upper bow can also be made from notes. To do this, they need to be folded harmonica. Or, insert a firework candle into the top circle. You can add a photo of the person to whom such an original gift is intended, like a cake made of money, made by yourself.

And finally

Gifts made with one's own hand always bring pleasure, since the warmth of the soul is invested in them. A cake made of money, made with your own hands, must surely please its owner.
