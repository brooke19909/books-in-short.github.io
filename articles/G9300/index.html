Tesla tablet: specifications, description, reviews

An ingenious inventor named Nikola Tesla gave people a lot of interesting things that most have known since childhood. It was he who was named after the company that specializes in the manufacture of navigators, tablets and many other types of digital technology. Now the place of honor is the legendary Tesla tablet, which immediately attracted the attention of hundreds of people.



Equipment

Usually, before using the device itself, the buyer pays attention to the packaging. The manufacturer did not fail with an interesting design, according to which at first you might think that the usual board game is hidden inside. The box itself has a rectangular shape and durable manufacturing material.

Perfectly matched dark blue color and ball lightning with a logo make a considerable impression on the buyer. Also on the box there is a lot of detailed information that the user will definitely need.

The inside is exceptionally white; this is where the device with the power adapter and USB cables is located. Inside there is a separation into two parts: in one is the Tesla tablet itself, and next to it there is another small box with a standard delivery set.

Many Tesla tablets have only good reviews, because the manufacturer has invested all its own forces to create a masterpiece device. And the top three are considered in detail below.

Tablet "Tesla Impulse 7.0 / 8.0"

Version 7.0 perfectly copes with all the assigned work and always helps the user to work. It is made of plastic, the battery capacity is 3000 mAh. Of course, there is always a headphone jack. The camera works much better with the presence of a flash, which in this model is very good. A 7-inch display diagonal allows the user to read books or watch movies on the go without hindrance. 8 GB of internal memory and an additional slot for a memory card make it possible to store a large amount of information. Internet connection speed is 300 Mbps.

But the Tesla 8.0 tablet has some differences in technical specifications. He shares the first place with version 7.0 and, in the first place, differs in the display diagonal, which is 8 inches, which for some users is a rather important difference. In addition, there is as much as 16 GB of internal memory, an additional connector for a flash card is always present. There are only two cameras: ordinary (5 megapixels) and front (2 megapixels). The design of this tablet is exactly the same. In addition, support for the 3G mobile network should be noted, which at the moment is attracting more and more customers.

Tesla Neon

The excellent Android 4.4 operating system with a good version of the processor and 4 cores is perfect for purely working purposes, and just for fun. 8 GB of internal memory, as well as 1 GB of RAM allow you to save absolutely any information you need without any problems. The slot for a separate memory card, of course, is present. The device supports a flash drive up to as much as 32 GB.



Among other things, good indicators are the diagonal of 8 inches and a high screen resolution. The type of screen itself is glossy, which gives a great opportunity to see the image on the device even in strong sunlight. An excellent video processor has already managed to show its own power and not badly surprise users.

The present wireless connection includes support for Bluetooth 4.0, as well as wireless Internet with excellent speed. And 3G mobile communication always works flawlessly and without any problems.

Connection to a computer device is carried out using a special cable, the audio output is also present at 3.5 mm.

The 2 megapixel rear camera has a flash, and the 0.3 mm front camera allows you to take gorgeous pictures and then process them in some applications. Such features are provided specifically for photo enthusiasts. Despite the low price, the resulting photos are quite beautiful and clear.

Undoubtedly, the Tesla Neon tablet always has good reviews. All the positive features are not the first day to please buyers and attract more and more new fans.



Reviews
