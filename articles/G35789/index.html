Why do I need cleaning





To clean the screen freed from film or glass, use a damp disc or an alcohol towel soaked in alcohol. Wipe the screen carefully so that there is no dirt on it. The most important stage

There is a special film on the adhesive side of the glass. It needs to be removed. After that, we apply the glass itself to the screen.
