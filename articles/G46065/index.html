Where can I go swimming in Moscow? Paid beaches in Moscow

In the heat and summer heat, the residents of the capital will certainly want to enjoy outdoor recreation. Indeed, on the streets of the city at high temperature there is practically nothing to breathe. For the current beach season, close to 40 recreation areas with access to ponds have been identified.

Places for swimming in Moscow

The top ten are places where swimming is allowed, the rest are intended only for sunbathing. All recreation areas comply with sanitary standards. Most of them are equipped with public toilets, dressing rooms and even showers. And all this despite the free admission.

At the White Lake

Where to swim in Moscow and Moscow region is allowed in the summer? Now we will review these places. For example, the White Lake has all the conditions for recreation. The entrance is free, there are locker rooms, boat rental on site, you can also rent a tennis court and spend active time outdoors. It is not allowed to swim here every year due to the presence of bacteria in the water whose concentration exceeds the norm. In 2014, the ban was in effect, in 2015 it was canceled. This place of rest is located at: st. Zaozernaya, 2/6.

Serebryany Bor and wild beach

The areas where people bathe in Moscow in the summer include Serebryany Bor 2 and 3. You can also get here for free. Beach number 2 is located on the street. Tamanskaya, 2. Here you can visit a small zoo, as well as spend time with children in the playground. Beach No. 3 is located on the 4th line of Silver Forest. Here you can work out on the sports ground, as well as rent a variety of equipment. There are changing rooms and showers on the beaches, as well as a toilet. You can quench hunger and thirst in a cafe with summer terraces located along the banks of the Moscow River. There is also a wild beach in Moscow nearby, where nudists love to relax. It is a ten minute walk from Serebryany Bor, 3.





A similar place is also located on the Swan Lakes, on the Stroginskaya floodplain. It is located in the same area. Namely, where the Stroginsky Bay meets the Moscow River. Visually, this place is located almost opposite the Silver Forest. The wild beach in Moscow is popular among young people. Such places can also be found in the Solnechnogorsk district, as well as on the peninsula of the Lyubertsy quarry.

Meshchersky Lake, Lytarkino and Troparevo





Where can I go swimming in Moscow? You can relax and swim on the coast of Lake Meshchersky, located at Voskresenskaya, 5-31. It has a compact beach with free entrance, where locker rooms are installed for convenience. There are also open beaches in Moscow and Moscow Region in Lytarkino, located in the region, as well as Troparevo in the territory of the city park. Here you can not only sunbathe, but also find entertainment such as a shooting gallery, attractions, use water mini-transport for hire, as well as sit in a cozy summer cafe.

"School"

In Zelenograd, which is located 50 km from Moscow, you can visit the "School" beach. Here, vacationers are provided with changing rooms, a playground for children, and a toilet. It is quite easy to get here, and the rest is very comfortable. Moreover, there is picturesque nature around. Also free of charge is a visit to the coastal part of the Olive Beach, located directly under the Andreevsky Bridge.

Allowed Places

So where can I go swimming in Moscow? According to the conclusion of Rostpotrebnadzor, these places include: the BeechKlab complex, which is located at the address: 39 Leningradskoye Shosse; Left-bank beach; Big pond in Victory Park on the passage 4922; Black Lake along the alley of the same name, 6 microdistrict. Here, the sanitary indicators of water are normal. During the rest, you should pay attention to the fact that during the summer period warning signs indicating that swimming is prohibited are allowed on the territory. This is due to an increase in bacterial concentration in the pond. To identify these indicators, regular studies are conducted.





Paid beaches in Moscow:

  • "Olive" is a well-equipped area with comfortable sun loungers, a wooden terrace, a pool, a bar and lots of greenery.
  • The port at VDNH is a city beach with a water complex, volleyball and ping-pong courts, a children's area and other amenities. Estimated cost is from 700 to 1800 rubles. This is the largest artificial beach in Moscow.
  • The relaxation area with sun loungers, where you can sunbathe, is also located in the Luzhniki sports complex. There are also swimming pools, sports fields and places for a comfortable pastime.
  • The Royal Bar Club, located on the Square of the Water Stadium, has a sandy beach, volleyball courts, a games area, a restaurant with a varied cuisine. The price - 1000 rubles - includes the rental of a sunbed, as well as towels.




In addition, indigenous residents and guests of the city can enjoy a summer vacation, visiting not only the beaches in Moscow, but also the outdoor pools, which are located in the park area. For example, in Sokolniki. There are two pools - for adult visitors, as well as children. You can actively spend leisure time on the volleyball court, or playing table tennis. In order to sunbathe you need to rent a deck chair. There are locker rooms and showers on site. For a bite to eat and cold drinks, go to the caf√©. On weekends, in the evening, there are noisy parties with DJs. The entrance ticket will cost at least 400 rubles. The cost on weekdays and weekends varies greatly.

Pools by the river

Among the places where you can swim in Moscow, there are such as a pool on the river bank, belonging to the Filevsky park. The cost of visiting is very affordable and ranges from 200 rubles. Entering from the main entrance, you need to move along the Main Alley, then down the stairs to the Panda cafe, after which turn left. On the Moscow River, in the Northern Tushino Park, there is also an outdoor pool where you can swim, which is convenient for sunbathing, and also spend time in the bar on the pier. The cost of visiting is from 700 rubles. You can get from the Planernaya and Skhodnenskaya stations, then the park itself is located along Freedom Street. Here the Ponton pool is located. Children's rest can be organized in the Izmailovsky park. A special platform called "Forest of Miracles" is always happy for young visitors. On the territory you can sunbathe, swim in the pool, frolic on a trampoline, and also try your hand at laser shooting. As in many places of rest, there is a cafe. And animal lovers are waiting for an unusual zoo. In order to enter the territory, it is necessary to pay an entry ticket worth from 150 rubles, as well as present a certificate. This is one of the most interesting places for summer holidays, especially the whole family. One of the elite pools is considered to be the one located on the territory of the restaurant in the Shore House Yacht Club. It is also open and accepts visitors exclusively in the summer. Here, vacationers will be offered a lot of amenities. When swimming in the pool, you can sip an exotic cocktail right at the side. Pool lovers at any time of the year are welcome at the Luzhniki and Seagull complexes. And this is not all places where you can swim in Moscow. One of the most interesting is the artificially made beach on the upper level of the building of the Artplay Design and Architecture Center. Here you can find everything you need for a quality vacation. Namely: a swimming pool, an area with sun loungers, a bar, playgrounds where you can enjoy playing volleyball, ping pong, badminton, and, of course, chat and make new friends. There is also a trampoline and there is an opportunity to stroll through an olive mini grove. This is the favorite vacation spot of the local youth, which often sits with entire companies.

Deserted beaches

For lovers of country holidays and more relaxed and deserted areas, beaches in Moscow are not entirely suitable. The best option is to go to the picturesque outskirts. Beyond the city limits are the beaches of the Klyazma reservoir. In Khlebnikovo, one of them is sandy, the other with a grassy coating. On the territory of the beaches there is a trampoline, a platform for playing ping pong, volleyball. A catamaran rental service is also available here. In case vacationers come as a whole company, you can rent a separate gazebo. The sandy beach "Gorki" has a bathing area for kids. The most equipped is the Troitskoye recreation area. There are various water slides and attractions, a trampoline, a children's pool with a fenced beach, as well as a mini zoo. You can have a tasty meal in the summer cafe. Admission is free, only the services that vacationers use are paid.

Pirogovskoe Reservoir





Pirogovskoe reservoir also has free and very well-equipped beaches. For example, "Bay of Joy." Nearby is the Malibu complex with a regular sandy beach and a VIP area with many entertainment and amenities. The cost of admission is from 500 rubles. Myakinskaya floodplain has Rublevo beach, where you can take advantage of all the amenities and enjoy your vacation. Entrance is from 150 rubles.

In the suburbs you can also visit the pools at the hotel complexes. For example, in the Mistral.
