Resort Adler

Beach holidays in Adler

The beach strip here is about 15 km long. The beaches are equipped with everything necessary for both adults and children. Convenient entry and exit from the sea, large awnings, comfortable sun loungers, piers with stairs to exit the water. Shopping and excursions, trips to museums and the dolphinarium, mountain walks and sea cruises are just a few of the opportunities that magnificent Adler is ready to open for its tourists. Entertainment near the hotel

The water park occupies a very large territory of the town. It has over 16 attractions for adults and children. The service staff is friendly and tries to please any tourist.

Dolphinarium is the most favorite place of all tourists. Circus performances, dolphin rides and their feeding are held here.

The oceanarium is one of Adler's most important virtues. Many tourists come here to watch the life of rare representatives of the seas and oceans living in large aquariums.

All these wonderful places are located relatively close to the hotel. If possible, some of them can even be reached on foot. After all, a real vacation is not only the sea and the beach. Local attractions will diversify the impressions received from the resort and will charge you with positive emotions.

Location Reviews





Near the hotel is a clean beach. But in order to reach it, it is necessary to cross the railway tracks. This is best done at a traffic light, although other places are available. But trains run here often, so it‚Äôs better not to risk it.

The hotel area is surrounded on all sides by numerous shops, stalls and cafes. Almost everywhere you can pay with credit cards.

If you drive one stop in the direction of the railway station, you can get to the Sberbank branch. Dolphinarium and Oceanarium are a short drive from the hotel. To get to the Olympic Park or Sochi Park, you need to go 40 minutes by bus number 124c.

Nutrition





Breakfast is included in the price. Most often, sandwiches with sausage and cheese and hot drinks are served. The remaining dishes are ordered for a fee. Basically, the breakfast menu includes cheesecakes, crepes, scrambled eggs and other light snacks. Here you can order lunches and dinners. A menu for the day is posted every morning, which indicates the fixed cost of dishes. You can eat in nearby cafes, there is more choice, especially for children.

Staff work

The hotel staff does a wonderful job. Administrators are always ready to help and resolve any conflict situations. Cafeteria workers always bring food on time. A special person arrives at the train station or airport for guests by car.

Room Service

Bed linens and towels are changed according to the schedule. Some guests complain about the quality of service and cleaning (towels may be stained after washing), but this is individual. Basically, there are no complaints about the service. In the room for each guest there is a large towel for the pool and two more for other needs.

Tip vacationers: it is better to choose rooms with pool views. The sounds of railway transport are not so audible in them.

Infrastructure and entertainment





The hotel has a small pool. Swimming in it is not very comfortable, especially for those who love warm water. Ideal for walruses. Water for a day practically does not warm up (temperature is about 23 degrees).

There is no special entertainment for children, but there is a children's playground within walking distance.

Minuses

  1. Sometimes you have to wait a long time for administrators to settle.
  2. The pool is almost always cold water at a high temperature outside.
  3. Loud noise from the railway.
  4. Old furniture in the rooms.
  5. The quality of cleaning is poor. Sometimes in the room you can find dirty dishes. Stains may appear on washed laundry.
  6. There are blankets in the closet, but almost all of them are old and not very clean.
  7. In the room it is better to walk in slippers.
  8. Poor breakfast.

General impressions





A good option for a good holiday is the hotel "Calm" (Adler). Reviews 2015-2016 also indicate an acceptable pricing policy of the complex. Of the main advantages, one can distinguish a favorable location near the beach area. The only confusion is that there is a railway nearby. If you choose the right number, then excessive noise from passing trains can be avoided. The territory has its own parking. The staff is friendly and helpful. The rooms always have hot water. The air conditioner saves from heat. On the ground floor there is a special room where you can wash and iron things. Pool water is always clean. Those who prefer swimming in cool water will definitely enjoy evening dives. Each guest can count on a set of bath towels. Rooms are cleaned once every three days. If you stop only for such a number of days, then you can not get to the cleaning. Breakfast for an amateur. Some guests are unhappy with its quality and quantity. But, despite all the minor flaws, the hotel "Calm" (Adler) is a worthy choice for tourists.
