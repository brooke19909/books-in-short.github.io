Cheap hotels of Belgorod: review of the best hotels, addresses, room descriptions, photos and reviews

Inexpensive hotels in Belgorod are in rather high demand among city guests. This is due to the fact that a lot of people come to the city on business trips who are trying to save on living.

Inexpensive hotels in Belgorod have a fairly high level of comfort and service. The list of such hotels will help determine the guests of Belgorod in choosing a place for temporary housing.

"White Dew"

Inexpensive hotel in Belgorod is located in the city center on the street. Volchanskaya, 1c, but noise and vanity are not felt, because the hotel is somewhat remote from roads and various complexes. From the windows of "White Ros" a beautiful view of the pond opens.

European room service and comfort will satisfy the needs of any guest. Accommodation prices here are quite affordable, and the average cost per night is 1000 rubles.

In an inexpensive hotel in Belgorod, a choice of visitors offers several room options:

  • standard double with 1 bed;
  • with 2 beds (single);
  • 3- and 4-seater;
  • comfort;
  • Junior Suite
  • Suite

All rooms have TV, air conditioning, telephone, necessary furniture and a bathroom.

On weekdays, a delicious and nutritious breakfast is served to all guests. The ingredients for it are purchased on farms, so they are high-quality and natural.

On the day of arrival, all visitors receive a free transfer from the airport and railway station. The beach is a 6-minute walk from the hotel complex. The hotel has laundry facilities and a 24-hour reception.

"Panorama"

This hotel in Belgorod with inexpensive prices for accommodation belongs to the economy class, but the living conditions are at a fairly high level. This loft hostel features original design of rooms and all interior spaces. It is located on B. Khmelnitsky Avenue, 133m.

Here, a choice of visitors presents rooms of different levels of comfort. Especially popular are places in rooms for 6 and 8 people. They have original comfortable 2-level beds in a modern design. Each room can accommodate exclusively women or men.

The rooms are constantly maintained clean. A work area is organized and a large closet is installed. The rooms are decorated using exclusively natural materials.

Each such room has its own toilet and shower. The rooms have a TV and guests can use the Internet. The common lobby has seating areas. Also on the floor is equipped with a small kitchen with all necessary equipment. On average, living in such a room for 1 person will cost 500 rubles.

The inexpensive hotel in Belgorod also has rooms for 2 and 3 people. They are decorated in a loft style and have all the necessary filling with furniture and appliances. Breakfast is available.

"Apartment 31 Train Station"

This inexpensive hotel in Belgorod with hourly payment is especially popular among tourists who come to the city. Such demand is due to the unusual and vibrant interior and affordable accommodation prices.

In this hostel, which is located on the street. Gostenskaya, 2b, you can rent a place in the room for several hours. Thus, it is beneficial for tourists who come to Belgorod to stop here. For several hours, guests can take a shower and relax a bit.

The distance to the railway station is only 450 meters, so this hostel is very suitable as an intermediate place to relax. The rooms are decorated in the style of passenger train cars.

Comfortable bunk beds are provided for guests. The rooms are equipped with a small workplace. The rooms have spacious wardrobes. The rooms are minimalist, but with a decent modern renovation.

There is a seating area in the lobby. And also there is a small kitchen with all necessary equipment. The reception also has a 24-hour small bar with various drinks.

Bathrooms are equipped on the floors. Guests can wash themselves in the small showers, which have hot water around the clock. In the rooms, if desired, men and women can live together. Visitors throughout the hostel can use the free internet. The average cost of living is 500-600 rubles.

Hostel "Parkovy"
