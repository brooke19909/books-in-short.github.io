Building

Cosman Aparthotel was built in 1988. Its full modernization took place in 2012. The institution positions itself as an aparthotel on the market of travel services, as hotel rooms are close to apartments in functionality.

The number of rooms is in two buildings: two-and three-story. The rectangular buildings of a typical resort building with seemingly uncomplicated architecture, their facades are professionally painted with a combination of pastel brick, yellow and turquoise colors and are maintained in a presentable condition.

The meaning of such functional architecture is not in external forms, but in ensuring proper housing conditions inside the buildings themselves. The latter aspect is indeed observed. Rooms

There are 56 cozy guest rooms at the Cosman Aparthotel. It is correct to start the description of the hotel complex rooms with the pure Cretan style in their design, which is characterized by the revitalization of the interior with unexpected bright details, an expressive combination of colors, thoughtful non-contrast lighting.

Room sizes are from 20 m 2 to 50 m 2 . There are 3 types of them in the hotel: standard, family, family double. It is noteworthy that each guest room has a fully equipped kitchenette where you can make tea, coffee, warm up or cook food.





The hotel guests in their reviews indicate only one drawback in the equipment of the rooms: a too compact shower without a shower cabin.

Rooms are cleaned six times a week, bed linen and towels are changed twice a week.
