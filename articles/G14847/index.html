The lineup of washing machines LG: the best representatives

Now in the modern market a diverse assortment of washing machines is presented. On sale are models of the brands Samsung, Beco, Candy, Indesit, Ariston. Korean company LG is also on this list.

Each manufacturer has its own characteristics. They consist not only in the color range and appearance, but also in the applied technologies, innovations.

The lineup of LG washing machines is really impressive. The article describes all the advantages, features of use, nuances, the most common devices, as well as their characteristics.

LG story

LG appeared in 1947. Its production was deployed in South Korea. At that time, the main company specialized in the creation of hygiene products. It was then that plastic was first used to form containers. In 1958, In Hoi founded a subsidiary brand called Gold Star. He was creating radio. Since that time, the production of equipment has begun actively. A little later, the creation of plastic was discovered. By 1969, the first washing machine in Korea was assembled. In European countries, debut devices appeared much earlier. The lineup of LG washing machines began to replenish quickly.

By the 90s, the company was expanding sales points around the world. Her equipment is collected not only in the producing country, but also in America, Britain, Thailand, Egypt and the Philippines. Now there are a lot of branches of this company, and the main headquarters is located in Seoul.
