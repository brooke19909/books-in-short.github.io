System "MIUI"

Many owners of smartphones running the Android operating system version 4.4.x - the so-called Kit-Kat, install on their gadgets an alternative solution from Chinese developers - the MiUI OS. In it, unlike the standard one, a significantly redesigned interface is used. Its main philosophy is convenience, which was fully realized. It is not surprising that the number of gadgets with MIUI is growing almost daily. Especially when you consider that there are many custom firmwares. The owner of a mobile device with MiUI does not need to look for tips on how to activate conversation recording on Android, because everything is incredibly simple and obvious in this system. Saving is possible with both incoming and outgoing calls. The use of the provided software mechanism in these cases is completely identical.

During the call, a standard screen is displayed (here we assume that the user has not changed the "dialer"), at the bottom of which 6 icon icons are displayed. If you click the lower right, then the recording of the telephone conversation on the "Android" will begin. Confirmation that the mechanism has been activated is the countdown of the red timer that has begun. When the session is completed (saving is turned off or the call is interrupted), a window will appear at the top of the screen offering to listen and save the recording. If no changes were made, then the files are placed in the folder Sound_recorder / call_rec on the SD memory card.

Digital recording

Also worth noting is the Digital Call Recorder Pro application. Although the reviews about it are contradictory, when used correctly, everything works just fine, even on CyanogenMod 13 builds. Problems are possible only if the source from which the audio stream is removed is incorrectly selected, which the developer honestly warns about. The last update was in December 2015. After starting in the settings, you can choose whether to display notifications after the end of the communication session; make a choice whether to save files or display a dialog box; specify the required format (there is MP3 and even MP4). The choice of sources is limited to 4 points: the telephone line (high quality), microphones and voices (your own or someone else's). Vibro at the beginning of a call means that recording is not performed due to a malfunction, and you need to change the source or file type (the most compatible one is 3GP).
