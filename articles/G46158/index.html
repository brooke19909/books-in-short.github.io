Ferry "Princess Anastasia". Ferry cruise

To visit European capitals, a mode of transport such as a ferry can be chosen. Unlike a train, an airplane and a bus, this is the most comfortable way to move. To sleep while traveling on a ferry, a full bed is provided, which is located in the cabin, which has all the amenities. Various ferries go to Europe from St. Petersburg. "Princess Anastasia" is one of them.

History

Princess Anastasia is a ferry built in 1986 at the Finnish shipyard Turku. Initially, it was called Olympia. In 1993, the ferry was sold to a new owner and changed its name to Pride of Bilbao. Itinerary

The Princess Anastasia ferry (see photo below) starts its journey from the seaport of the Northern capital.



Further, his route runs to Helsinki, and then to Stockholm and Tallinn. Thus, the cruise on the Princess Anastasia ferry allows you to visit the three capitals of the European continent at once. For travelers, this is a very convenient option. In the evening and at night, passengers are on a comfortable ship, where they have the opportunity to dine in a restaurant, become a spectator of a show program, visit a sauna or a disco, and then spend the night in comfort. What do you need to get for the trip?

Many tourists prefer ferries to travel by bus. Princess Anastasia is a great option for a long trip. In order to board it, you need to issue a Schengen multivisa. This can be done independently or contact the travel agency, which will collect the necessary documents for a small fee.

To apply for a visa, you will need a certificate of employment indicating your income. Instead, a statement from the bank on the status of the account may be provided. Two photographs, a completed application form and a passport for travel abroad will also be required.

Prices

You can buy a ticket for the Princess Anastasia cruise, which involves visiting three European capitals, for 300-1000 euros. The price depends on the category of cabin. Significantly save on travel will help special offers and hot tours. So, in an economy class cabin, the prices for a trip from St. Petersburg to Helsinki start from fifty euros.



The cost of carriage on the Princess Anastasia ferry ranges from seventy to one hundred euros. Sometimes there are special offers. In this case, the vehicle will need to pay only thirty-five to sixty euros, taking into account the place in the cabin for passengers and the driver.

timetable

Princess Anastasia leaves the cruise from St. Petersburg at 18 or 19 hours. Time varies depending on the season. After departure and until the morning, the ferry is on its way, arriving the next day in Helsinki. During the day, passengers are provided with free time, tours are offered for sightseeing in the Finnish capital. In the evening of the same day, the ferry leaves for Stockholm. The next morning, the ship arrives in Sweden. Passengers can walk around the city all day long. In the evening, the ship leaves for its next route point - Tallinn.

On board the ferry is a conference room. Every evening, representatives of travel agencies collect passengers in it and offer excursions the next day.
