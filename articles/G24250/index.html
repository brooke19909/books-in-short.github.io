Hotel Side Star Elegance (Turkey) is located in a picturesque place off the coast of the Mediterranean. A clean beach and clear waters in the sea allow you to enjoy a beach holiday. Rooms

The tourist complex "Side Star Elegance" (Turkey) offers to relax in one of 310 comfortable rooms. They fall into different categories. In total, 296 rooms of standard type, 14 rooms of superior comfort are presented on the territory of the hotel.





The standard room is for 1 to 3 people. All rooms in this category have sea views. Each one has a balcony. The room has all the necessary furniture. A carpet is laid on the floor. The room has a bathroom, hairdryer. Also provided for the presence of air conditioning, TV, telephone. There is a minibar. If you need to store valuables, you can rent a safe. This is a paid service.

Suite rooms are a room with a bedroom and a living room. There is no door between them. The price of this room includes the rental of a safe. For visitors with children, special furniture is provided.

Beach vacation

The Side Star Elegance Hotel has its own private beach. It extends 150 m in length. The width of the sandy coast is 50 m. The hotel is built on the first line from the coast. The beach has all the necessary equipment. You can take a deck chair, an umbrella. Towels can also be changed at will regularly.





Getting to the beach is easy. You will need to go through the equipped territory of the hotel. You can go down to the sea along the promenade. The views of the Mediterranean are gorgeous. The beach is very clean, well maintained. The descent into the water is gentle. Swimming here is very nice. The water in the season is warm, clear.

You can also relax on site by the pool. Around it there is a terrace. Sun beds, umbrellas can be rented for free. There are bars near the pool and the beach. Here you can drink soft drinks, a snack.

Reviews about "Side Star Elegance" are mostly positive. Equipped with well-equipped infrastructure. There are 5 restaurants on site serving international and local cuisine. There are also 8 bars. There are shops, souvenir shops. Those who wish can visit the cafe.

Entertainment

For adults and children, Side Star Elegance organizes a lot of different entertainments. Those who want to relax, relax, can go to the sauna or hammam. There is a massage room. The complex also has a wellness center and spa treatments.

For sports, 2 tennis courts have been built. It is free to take equipment for table tennis, billiards, volleyball, darts. There is a gym. For kids there are water slides, a water park.
