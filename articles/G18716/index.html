How to decorate champagne with your own hands?

Today it is customary to decorate gifts. Decorated champagne for a wedding does not surprise anyone. But bottles can be decorated for any occasion. For example, on New Year's or birthday. You can even get a bottle in the style appropriate to the profession of a person. Look for ideas on how to decorate champagne below.

Decoupage





Decorating champagne is easiest with napkins. They get a good hostess for any holiday. Do not save on napkins; you should buy thick, three-layer ones. For decoupage you will need glue. You can buy a special one, but you can use the usual PVA.

Lubricate the bottle with glue, divide the napkin into layers and glue the first one on glass. You can immediately use the whole square or tear it into pieces. It‚Äôs much more convenient to give the background to the bottle in small parts. When the first layer is ready, grease it with plenty of glue and let it dry. We cover the bottle with the second layer of napkins in the same way. But from the third layer, it is usually decorated with a picture, we cut out the fragment we need. It can be bells, snowflakes or Santa Claus. When the second layer has dried out, glue the picture in the center of the bottle. Paint can finish small details or dot. As a top coat we use decoupage varnish.

Bottle christmas tree





Decorate champagne for the New Year with your own hands is very simple. You will need a beautiful ribbon, tinsel and a hot gun. Wipe a bottle of champagne with alcohol or cologne to degrease it. Now with a hot gun you need to draw a long spiral on the bottle. If you create infrequently, then you should not decorate the entire bottle at once, decorate it in parts. Hot silicone freezes quickly, so without the right dexterity, decorating an entire bottle at once will not work.

Glue tinsel on the spiral. Tapes can be attached to the bottle in two ways. The first will be similar to gluing tinsel. We draw a spiral and attach ribbons to it. The second method requires working with a sewing machine. The tape needs to be cut into 5 parts. Embed an elastic band in each of them. And then you should put the resulting "skirts" on the bottle.

Bottle decorated with sweets





Decorate champagne in this way is not difficult even for a child. Only a kilogram of sweets and a glue gun are required. Sweets should be chosen round in shape - so the bottle will look more interesting. We heat the gun and draw a circle at the base of it. Gently glue candies to it by the ‚Äútail‚Äù of the candy wrapper. Thus we complete the entire series. Go higher and higher until you reach the neck. Here you will need to tie a beautiful bow and fix it with silicone for reliability.

Wedding champagne





Want to decorate a bottle for a festive day, but you don‚Äôt like the idea of ‚Äã‚Äãtrivial outfits in white and black style? You can make costumes from ribbons. Moreover, the dress for the bride will be pink, and the groom‚Äôs jacket is executed in a gray-pink version. How to decorate champagne with your own hands in this way? Let's start with the bridesmaid dress. For its manufacture, thin ribbons will be needed. We take one of them, bend it into a ring around the bottle and slightly pull the tails down. In this position we fix. By analogy, we make loops to the very bottom. When 7 centimeters remain to the edge, we stop working. We take a wide tape and use a glue gun to fix it. Moreover, it is desirable to wring it so that the free edge forms beautiful waves. It remains to decorate the dress with beads.

The suit of the groom is made by analogy. We glue the loops of ribbons throughout the bottle, and the bottom 8-10 cm just glue the tape in a circle. You can lay in the base the shape of a spiral. The costume should also be decorated with beads.

Christmas bottle decor





In order to decorate champagne in this way, it will be necessary to remove the label from it. This can be done by placing the bottle in warm water, and then wipe the place where the paper was glued with alcohol. Decorating a bottle of champagne for the New Year is simple. Special glass acrylic will be required. If this is not available on the farm, ordinary gouache is also suitable. It will need to be mixed with glue before work.

How to decorate a bottle? Choose any pattern, for example, the muzzle of a snowman, and begin to create. Paint the top of the bottle in red and the bottom in white. At the junction of two colors, we depict a knitted elastic band. It remains to supplement the bottle with a funny face. You can draw anything you like, such as the New Year‚Äôs landscape, deer, etc.

Champagne "Santa Claus"





In this way, you can decorate not only a bottle with an adult alcoholic drink, but also baby soda. How to decorate champagne for the New Year? You can draw on it a funny Santa Claus face. First, with white paint, we outline the places where the edge of the cap, beard and mustache will be located. We paint over the face with flesh color. And give the whole bottle a red tint. It remains to work out the details of the face. Draw the nose, eyes and outline the mouth and ears. Light blue waves add liveliness to the beard and mustache.

Anniversary Champagne





If you don‚Äôt know what to present to your friends for a holiday, present them with a bottle of something alcoholic, but not just one. How to decorate champagne on your wedding anniversary? You need to take two bottles and prime them in white. You can leave only foil, and even that is better to change from gold to silver. Now you need to add roses from the ribbons. If you do not know how to make flowers, then they can be purchased at any needlework store. For each bottle we make half a horseshoe of flowers. It will be enough to glue three large roses on the bottom and two buds on the top. For splendor, you should add greens. It can also be made of ribbons or made of felt. We glue everything with a hot gun. Now you just need to tie two bottles together by the neck and stick beads or rhinestones on the bow.

Decorate a bottle for the holiday





Do not know what to give a girl for her birthday? Give her flowers and champagne. But you need to present presents beautifully. And if the bouquet is decorated in the store, then you can do the decor of the alcoholic drink yourself. How to decorate a bottle of champagne? This will require tapes. In our version, the bottle is decorated in monochrome, and you can make the decor more vibrant and catchy. We wrap the bottle with tape and fix it on the glue gun. The front of the champagne is decorated with flowers. They can be both purchased and homemade. The main thing is to follow the composition. There should be a lot of flowers below, and upwards their number gradually decreases. Cork also needs decoration. It can be decorated with small flowers and made from a ribbon skirt.

Ded Moroz and Snegurochka





How to decorate champagne for the New Year? You should dress up the bottles in suits of Santa Claus and the Snow Maiden. Let's start with making a New Year's wizard outfit. We decorate the bottle with ribbon loops. We fix them to the glass with a glue gun. Glue a white piece of fleece 5-7 cm wide to the base of the bottle. Cut a rectangle from another white piece. They decorate the front of the future Santa Claus. Glue a silver braid on the edge of the rectangle. And in the center we fix plastic snowflakes. The third rectangle will serve as a collar. Using a glue gun, glue it to the neck of the bottle. The perimeter of the collar is treated with silver tape. As a belt we use a red ribbon. From a small piece of fleece, you can make Santa Claus a hat.

Now you need to create a costume for the Snow Maiden. We make a cone out of cardboard. Glue it to the bottle. And decorate the cardboard blank with tape loops. Glue a piece of blue fabric to the bottom of the coat. It is desirable that a silver pattern be applied on it. Now you need to complete the coat. Glue a thick silver braid on the joint of the fur coat and skirt. We divide the outer clothing of the Snow Maiden into two parts with the same braid. Glue a snowflake on each of them. It remains to make a bottle of kokoshnik. We cut a semicircular billet from cardboard. Glue it on both sides with a cloth. From white threads weave two braids and fix them with blue ribbons. Glue the braids to the kokoshnik and put the hat on the bottle.

Here black tapes need to be glued vertically. You can create a clear border between white and black, or you can make it smooth. We decorate the shirt with blue ribbons and glue the buttons.
