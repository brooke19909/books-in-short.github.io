The farmstead "Semigorye" (Krasnodar Territory) - life slows down

Manor "Semigorye" is located on a hill near the village of the same name. Anapa is only 20 km away. The view from the hill offers stunning: a mysterious oak grove, and where there is enough sight - vineyards. Far beyond the borders of the region, this family estate is known as a good place for noisy corporate parties, birthdays, weddings and a serene family weekend in nature. The territory of the estate "Semigorye" (Krasnodar Territory) is protected.

Company holiday

On the territory there are several bungalows decorated in Eurostyle. Each is designed for five guests. If you plan a special privacy, you can ask to bring lunch or dinner to your room. And breakfast is included in the price of the apartment.

For lovers of barbecue on the territory of the estate "Semigorye" (Krasnodar Territory) there are belvederes with barbecue facilities and cozy wooden tables with sofas or chairs. In the meantime, barbecue is being prepared, you can go on an excursion to the winery with the obligatory subsequent tasting of various garage drinks or wander around the endless vineyards. If active games are planned in the recreation program, then welcome to the comfortable site. Everything is thought out here. For children there is a separate entertainment area.

For those who like to relax with comfort in the estate there is a restaurant in the new-fashioned "chateau" style. He stands on the shore of a beautiful lake. A cozy room (by the way, for 50-70 people) is surrounded by panoramic windows. Tables are powerful, wooden, designed in a classic style. Together with comfortable chairs with a high back, they favor close, close and confidential communication. The pleasure of tasting food prepared with love and local spill wine is complemented by stunningly beautiful views of the Anapa Valley or the mysteriously smooth waters of the lake.





The cuisine in the restaurant is Caucasian and Russian. The chefs of the Semigorye estate (Krasnodar Territory), honoring the traditions of their closest neighbors (Ossetians, Abkhazians, Dagestanis and others), lovingly prepare achma, chakhokhbili, kharcho, pilaf, satsivi and other equally tasty dishes. The restaurant is no less reverent in Russian cuisine. There are practically no fried foods. The main method of Russian cuisine is stewing and stewing. Necessarily in the Russian menu of the restaurant there are soaked, pickled and salted vegetables, pancakes with all kinds of fillings, pancakes, eggs and an amazing variety of meat dishes. Gourmet gourmet wine snails served in gourmet sauce. Naturally, local chefs have their secrets, nuances and tricks, so often the restaurant is praised separately from the estate.

Lure fishing place

Inveterate fishermen are also welcome here. The owners of the estate "Semigorye" (Krasnodar Territory) offer wonderful fishing. On a small pond with streams and dams, in which amazing lotuses grow and hand ducks swim, secluded places for fishermen are prepared. Ponds teem with sturgeon, trout, carp, etc.

Unique garage wines

The farmstead "Semigorye" (Krasnodar Territory) is a famous mini-producer of garage wines. To do this, here with special care for more than 15 years grown varietal vines Cabernet Sauvignon, Merlot, Riesling, Sauvignon Blanc.





Under the control of experienced winemakers, they produce dry white, red and dessert wines of tremendous taste - late harvest.
