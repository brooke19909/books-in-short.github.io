Guest House Panorama - Arkhipo-Osipovka, 18B Leninsky Lane: reviews

Guest house "Panorama" in Arkhipo-Osipovka is not large in size, but it has all the conditions for a memorable vacation. The owners of the establishment have created a warm and homely atmosphere here. Visitors who have visited the Panorama once will definitely come back here.

Guest house "Panorama" in Arkhipo-Osipovka: address and schedule

This complex is located in the center of the village. The village has good infrastructure, so all the necessary places for entertainment and shops are in 5-10 minute proximity to the pension.





The hotel is located in Arkhipo-Osipovka on Leninsky Lane, 18-B \ 1. The hotel operates throughout the year. In cold weather, the building has a heating system.

Features

The hotel has a relatively small size, but the house is noticeably different in its sophisticated shape and well-groomed. The area around the guest house "Panorama" in Arkhipo-Osipovka, as green as possible. Here, each plant is grown with great love and is a part of one whole composition.

The hosts offer their visitors a shuttle service from the airport or train station. Convenient parking is provided for guests arriving by car.

All rooms are equipped with conditions that are characteristic of a high level of comfort.

Accommodations

Vacationers can pre-determine the rental of a suitable room for a comfortable life during the holidays:

  • One room for 2 adults and 1 child. They are located on the 3rd and 1st floors. The rooms are equipped with: double bed, sofa bed, table and chairs, bedside tables and wardrobe. There is a bathroom, TV, fridge, air conditioning. The establishment also has a shared kitchen.
  • One room, enlarged - a spacious room with a double bed and a large folding sofa. The rooms are furnished with tables and chairs, dressing table, wardrobe. The room has a bathroom and shower. For the comfort of visitors, the room has a TV, air conditioning, kettle, refrigerator. Room capacity is 4 people. The hotel has a shared kitchen where visitors can cook their own meals.
  • Two-room apartment. These rooms have 2 rooms, 2 double beds, a sofa bed and an armchair. They are also equipped with wardrobes, side tables, a table and chairs. It has its own kitchen with equipment, a bathroom and shower. Additionally, the room is equipped with TV, air conditioning, refrigerator. The room can accommodate from 4 to 7 people at a time.




All rooms include a tasty and healthy breakfast, served in the dining room on the ground floor.

Recreation areas

On the territory of the guest house in Arkhipo-Osipovka, there are several places for active and passive pastime. An outdoor pool with a children's area and a jacuzzi is in great demand among vacationers.

The water in it is purified through filters. On cool days, the pool is heated. The area around it is lined with a special non-slip coating. Around are comfortable loungers made of a material that does not absorb water.





The sea in Arkhipo-Osipovka is famous for its cleanliness and well-maintained beaches. From the complex you can walk to it in 10-15 minutes. Along the way there are shopping stalls with fresh fruits and souvenirs.

There are also gazebos equipped with barbecue facilities. Guests enjoy roasting kebabs in the fresh air. Lovers to steam, an opportunity to visit a Russian bath. It fully respects traditions and uses fragrant brooms from different tree species in the process.

For younger visitors, the guest house has special places for relaxation. There are two playgrounds. One is for children from 0 to 3 years. Small plastic slides and small colored houses will surely attract the attention of kids and their parents. The swing has special seat belts so that the child does not fall out of there.





For older children, horizontal bars and stairs are installed. Slides and swings are also of great height, but at the same time they are safe for outdoor activities. Game zones, for the safety of children, are arranged in a place remote from the pool. There is also a small trampoline.

Food and additional services

There is a dining room on the ground floor of the guest house in Arkhipo-Osipovka. It serves a variety of breakfasts, the cost of which is included in the price for accommodation in all rooms.

Here, at the request of guests, you can order full lunches and dinners. A cook with extensive experience using fresh ingredients prepares food. The menu includes dishes that are acceptable for the diet of young children.

Hotel owners can organize a variety of excursions to the sights of the region. A separate service provides an individual bath program.

The complex has wireless internet. This nuance for guests makes the rest even more comfortable and productive. In case of urgent circumstances, vacationers are always in touch on working moments and domestic issues.

Guest house "Panorama" in Arkhipo-Osipovka: reviews

On various sites you can find many opinions about the work of the complex. Almost 100% they are positive. Vacationers are amazed at the conscientious attitude to the work of all staff and hotel owners individually.

Parents of kids are very pleased with the variety of entertainment on the playground. Very rarely, the owners of such complexes worry about the leisure of the kids. Here, a mini-town made of plastic attractions was built for them.

According to guest reviews, the pool water is always clean. Often they did not want to go to the beaches, due to the fact that the hotel has a swimming area at a very high level. Water is not spilled near it and modern sunbeds are installed. The sea in Arkhipo-Osipovka, according to customer reviews, is suitable for families. The beaches are well maintained and a gradual increase in depth makes it possible for children of all ages to swim.

Vacationers are pleased with the possibility of using a transfer service. In this case, they do not have to use public transport with heavy suitcases and then go on foot.





Dining at the hotel is varied and affordable. Outside the territory there is always care, and even on the hottest days, plants give a shadow in the recreation areas.

The village has a well-developed infrastructure, so vacationers can purchase the necessary trifles for life and recreation near the guest house.
