Hotel Paloma Garden Corina 3 * (Greece, Crete): overview, description and reviews

One of the largest islands belonging to Greece is Crete. He is amazing and unique. Its shores are washed by three seas: the Mediterranean, Aegean and Ionian, and the climate is considered one of the mildest and healthiest in Europe. For millennia, this land has carefully preserved the heritage left over from the Romans, the Venetian period and the T√ºrks. The island provides unlimited opportunities for an interesting, comfortable and memorable vacation. Sloping sandy beaches with a warm and clear sea will give peace and help restore vitality, amazing Greek cuisine will fill you with strength, and the variety of entertainment, the unique architecture of the island and its rich history will not let you get bored.





The three-star Corina Paloma Garden Hotel (ex. Paloma Garden) welcomes tourists who have decided to visit Crete all year round. Holidays on a sunny Greek island are quite popular, so the flow of vacationers subsides only in the winter season. The hotel is modest both in size and in the services offered, but it is notable for its low price and favorable location.

Hotel Location

This seaside hotel is located at 70014, Irinis St., Stalis, Crete, Greece. Despite the fact that it stands almost in the center of the village, at the same time it is hidden from prying eyes by the garden surrounding it. Around - the mountainous landscape characteristic of Crete and a magnificent view of the sea coast. Near Paloma Garden Corina is located Malia (3 km) - a popular tourist destination, especially among young people. There you can find hundreds of nightclubs and entertainment for every taste. The beach of the village of Stalida is just a few minutes walk, just cross the road (300 m). Nearby there is a bus stop with which you can leave for Heraklion (30 km) - an ancient Greek city with many museums, historical and architectural monuments. It also houses an international airport.

Corina Paloma Garden 3 *: rooms





The hotel building is two-story, small. It has only 49 rooms, decorated in the traditional style of the island, of various levels of comfort. Restaurant and Food

Choosing the Corina Paloma Garden hotel (Crete) for relaxation, tourists, as a rule, opt for the BB food system , that is, only breakfast. This option gives you the opportunity to plan your day yourself, visit those bars and restaurants that are interesting and more like. The all-inclusive system is not provided, but half board is possible.





A breakfast buffet is served in the spacious lounge with outdoor terrace. The hotel has a restaurant Paloma Garden, offering its visitors popular dishes of European cuisine and traditional Cretan, as well as a vegetarian menu. Special stools are available for younger guests. At Paloma Garden Corina's restaurant, you can enjoy the taste of home-cooked food. Fresh vegetables and fruits, meat, fish and seafood, cheeses, an abundance of olive oil and herbs famous for its quality - all this is a taste of Greece.

Hotel facilities





The hotel is a three star hotel, so you should not count on a wide range of services. Wi-Fi is free throughout. For people with disabilities, the facilities are partial. For an additional fee, guests can use fax, luggage storage, taxi services.

There is a free car park on site. Arrivals, registration and departure of guests are carried out at the set hours: after 14 hours 00 minutes and until noon, respectively.

Entertainment and sports





Corina Paloma Garden Hotel offers the minimum set of the most necessary conditions for a good family vacation: a relaxing atmosphere, comfortable rooms, delicious cuisine. For entertainment, there is a spacious swimming pool on the sunny side with a cozy terrace where guests can enjoy refreshing drinks and light snacks. Umbrellas, sunbeds and towels are provided.

On site, for an additional fee, guests can use the medical office, billiards, internet cafe, table tennis and travel desk services.

Who is the hotel targeted at?

A modest three-star hotel Corina Paloma Garden (Greece, Crete) is designed primarily for budget tourists. For an affordable price you get good service, comfortable rooms, excellent location relative to the city and the beach.

The hotel allows you to get acquainted with the traditions of the country, to see with your own eyes this stunning national flavor of the Mediterranean. It can be traced in everything, including the style of room decoration, restaurant, as well as dishes on its menu.

The national composition of tourists choosing a hotel to stay is mixed, it likes to stay including the local population. Facilities and services are designed for adults and older vacationers, to a lesser extent for families with children. The hotel involves a relaxing beach holiday, pleasantly combined with an excursion program.

Stalida

Hotel Paloma Garden Corina is located in the heart of the resort village of Stalida. Unlike neighboring Malia and Hersonissos, he was chosen not by young people having fun, but by elderly couples and families with children. One of the advantages of Stalis - sloping sandy beaches and clear sea. In fact, there are only two of them, and they are separated by a small natural rocky wall. The village has one of the island‚Äôs best promenades, along with numerous cafes and restaurants, souvenir shops, travel agencies, car and bicycle rentals. Prices are significantly lower compared to other popular resorts in Crete.

The village, and, accordingly, the hotel, has a favorable geographical position, allowing you to independently explore the sights of the eastern part of the island, including Knossos Palace, the Lassithi Plateau, excavations of the Minoan Palace, etc.
