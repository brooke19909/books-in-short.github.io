HSM Hotel President (Spain, Mallorca): Travel Reviews

Mallorca is the largest of the Balearic Islands, it is a great place for a beach holiday, windsurfing, diving and climbing. The island has long been loved by tourists as a resort due to its natural beauties. In our article we want to talk about one of the Mallorca hotels - HSM Hotel President.

A bit about the hotel

HSM Hotel President is a four-star complex built in Calcudia. The hotel began operating in 1987 and since then has hosted thousands of tourists every year. The last reconstruction and repair was carried out in 2006.





The complex is located in a residential area, close to places such as the Lluk Monastery, the old city of Alcudia, and a hydropark. The hotel was built near the sea, and in close proximity are cafes, bars, shops and everything you need for a comfortable stay. In addition, there are forest stands near the complex. The island's capital is only 65 kilometers away; a public transport stop is located next to the complex. The hotel is easy to reach, the distance to the airport is 70 kilometers.

HSM Hotel President offers its visitors comfort, excellent service and a wonderful beach holiday. The hotel has its own spa center, in which everyone can visit the procedures of health and beauty.

Number of rooms

HSM Hotel President offers Guests of the apartments of the following categories:

  • Single - single rooms equipped with everything necessary and decorated in a classic style.
  • Double - double apartments.
  • Double with sea view - a room with a sea view.
  • Junior suites - junior suite.




In total, the hotel has 242 rooms. All of them are equipped with furniture, a safe, a bathroom, a satellite TV, a terrace, bathrobes, bathroom amenities and cosmetics, a mini fridge, a telephone.

Dining at the hotel

Hotel HSM President offers tourists the beloved All Inclusive system . In the hotel restaurant, guests can enjoy the widest range of cold and hot dishes, as well as a large selection of desserts and all kinds of sweets. The administration practices theme nights with cooking shows. Meals are organized as a buffet. Tourists independently choose the most convenient meal option for them: boarding, half-board or all-inclusive.

SPA complex

Hotel HSM President has its own spa center, which can be visited by all vacationers over sixteen years of age. It offers hydrotherapy sessions, a jacuzzi, a Turkish bath, a relaxation and relaxation area. A prerequisite for visiting the institution is the presence of a bathing cap and slippers, which can be purchased at the hotel or use your own.

Hotel Pools

HSM President Hotel 4 * (Mallorca) has a completely original pool cascade, consisting of three ponds for adults and one for children. Nearby is a recreation area equipped with umbrellas and sun loungers.





Children's holiday

HSM President Hotel 4 * (Mallorca) has a children's mini-club, a playground, a games room for the youngest guests. There is also a separate children's pool on site. Upon request, a cot and a special feeding chair are available in the restaurant.

Sports and Entertainment

HSM President Hotel 4 * offers to visit its own spa and health center. In addition, tourists can rent bicycles, gym, scuba diving lessons. You can play table tennis and volleyball at the property. In the evening, guests are entertained by live music and night discos.

Hotel beach

Hotel HSM President (Mallorca) is located on the coast and has its own small but equipped beach. This place is very convenient, since it is covered from all sides, you can start swimming here at the beginning of the season.

A bit about the resort

If you come to HSM Hotel President, be sure to explore Alcudia. This is currently one of the most popular holiday destinations on the island. The city constantly has to keep a balance between the growing onslaught of tourists and the preservation of natural pristine beauty. Alcudia is surrounded by numerous reserves, one of which is S'Albefera. The unique conservation area has long been a haven for many species of birds.

The resort has a stunning thirty-kilometer coast with sandy beaches surrounded by mountain slopes. The wide coast consists of numerous bays with white sand and crystal clear sea water.

Alcudia is located in the northeastern part of Mallorca. The best resort for lovers of true natural beauty is simply not to be found. The beaches of the local coast are not only stunningly beautiful, but also comply with all European standards. Alcudia is a very calm and quiet resort with a very modern infrastructure. The population of the city is multinational. The old center of Alcudia and remains the most popular resort center of Mallorca, attracting travelers from all over the world.

The second, no less interesting area is Puerto de Alcudia, which houses the historic sea harbor. For many centuries there has been a fishing port in it. From HSM Hotel President it is not difficult to get there.

In season, numerous shops, kiosks, pavilions with goods for tourists work throughout the city. And local markets are full of seafood and fruits year-round.





Every year the city hosts an incredible number of various holidays, festivals and fairs that tourists like to visit.

Local Attractions

To see the ancient city of Alcudia, you need to drive three kilometers inland. There you can see the castle ruins, which are a historical monument. Once upon a time, it was this fortress that served as a defense of Mallorca against pirate raids. Currently, excursions are brought here, including from the HSM Hotel President. The most popular are two-hour walks.

Alcudia resort is located in the bay, most of which is occupied by magnificent beaches bordered by pine trees. For this reason, the local coast is appreciated not only for its beauty and warm sea, but also for its clean and healthy air.

The pearl of the resort can be called the Albufera Nature Reserve, where countless birds live. Every year, birds flock to Mallorca from all over Europe to nest. The reserve is closed to visitors by car, only walking is allowed on it.





Albufera is an amazing place for diving and surfing. The underwater world here is very diverse: exotic fauna and flora delight even experienced divers. If you come to HSM Hotel President, be sure to visit Albufera.

Resting on the island, it is worth a trip to its capital, Palma de Mallorca. The city is rich in historical sights, but of particular interest to tourists is the Dragon Cave, consisting of a string of mysterious halls and grottoes. Legend has it that pirates once hid their treasures here.

Hotel HSM President: reviews

Mallorca is a beautiful island on which a large number of good hotels are built. These include the HSM Hotel President (Mallorca). Reviews about him are very good, which indicates his compliance with his four-star status.

The hotel is in a good quiet location, away from the hustle and bustle. Here at night you will not be disturbed by noise and music. Near the complex there are several more hotels, but this does not bother. A little further begins the residential area of ‚Äã‚Äãthe city.

I would like to note that the HSM President Hotel SPA is not an ordinary hotel. The fact is that by some miracle it was built in the rock and has many levels. At first, the staircases seem confusing, but you quickly get used to them. In fact, the complex does not have its own territory, since it is closely adjacent to the cliff, and its front part faces the roadway. The road is not very busy, but still, going to the sea, do not forget about the safety rules so as not to get into the blind spot of drivers.





The entire hotel consists of many levels, and even pools are cascaded. Such an original building gives the hotel a certain charm. Perhaps the complex will not appeal to those tourists who need a vast territory for walking, it simply is not here. You can go beyond the fence of the hotel and walk along the promenade along the sea. With very small children in wheelchairs, it is also not very convenient to pass the steps. Otherwise, the original design of the complex does not cause any inconvenience.

According to tourists, the hotel rooms are small. They are equipped with good, but not new furniture, which is in good condition. The only thing vacationers complain about is uncomfortable beds, which could be replaced. All the equipment in the apartment is working, but even if problems arise, the reception staff will solve them very quickly. As for cleaning, it is of high quality. Maids take out the trash daily, change towels and clean the rooms.

Special attention deserves food in the HSM President Hotel 4 *. Food reviews are only positive. This is not common. As a rule, not all guests are satisfied. And here we can confidently say that the food is organized at a decent level.

Tourists have the right to independently choose the appropriate mode. As a rule, vacationers opt for All Inclusive or half board. The buffet in the restaurant pleases with abundance and variety. Seafood and meat are served daily. So many seafood is not in every hotel you can try. For breakfast in the restaurant you can order bacon, pancakes, eggs, scrambled eggs, rolls, jams, cereals, meat dishes. In addition, champagne is served in the morning. There are always a lot of fruits and delicious desserts on the table. Soups are offered for lunch, and ice cream is a must.

All food in the restaurant is quite suitable for children. There is no separate diet menu, but there is always something suitable on the buffet.





The hotel has its own beach, located next to the complex. To get on it, you must cross the road. The coast has fine sand cover, and the beach itself is very small. If you do not rest in the high season, then there will be enough space on the shore for everyone, but with a strong filling of the hotel, there are not enough sunbeds for everyone. However, this nuance is not so important, since a huge municipal beach, which always has places, is located a little further down the coast. All the beaches of Alcudia are sandy with a shallow entry into the sea, which annually attracts a huge number of families with children.

The hotel has a convenient location, it is quite convenient to get to the city center from it. Walking takes about half an hour, and by taxi - no more than five minutes. The cost of a taxi is about 6 euros. You can rent a car in the complex to see not only Alcudia, but also Palma de Mallorca. You can park your car at the hotel parking lot for the night. Local guides offer different excursions to local attractions that are worth a visit, or go on a trip by your own municipal transport. Mallorca is an amazing place with unusually beautiful nature and interesting historical places that are certainly worth a look.

Instead of an afterword

Concluding the conversation about HSM President Hotel 4 * (reviews are given in the article), I would like to note that this complex can be a great place for a beach-sea vacation. Great location, excellent food, beautiful nature - the main components of a good and successful vacation.
