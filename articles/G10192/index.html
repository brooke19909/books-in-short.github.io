Railway station of Ulyanovsk: a brief overview, services, how to get

The railway station of Ulyanovsk was built on the centenary of the birth of V.I. Lenin, in 1970. This is a large building consisting of two floors. At the first are ticket offices for the sale of tickets for long-distance trains. On the second floor there are ticket offices serving suburban trains. The station has three platforms and is equipped with 25 tracks. The total area of ‚Äã‚Äãthe structure is 4.2 thousand square meters.





Railway station Ulyanovsk Central has the ability to receive up to 2300 passengers at a time. Every year, 885 thousand people depart from this station. In front of the building there is a large ample parking for cars. The railway station of Ulyanovsk is an important railway junction, which is located at a distance of 900 km from Moscow. At least 100 passengers use its services daily. The most demanded was the route of the branded train "Ulyanovsk - Moscow".

Station services in Ulyanovsk

Railway station of Ulyanovsk offers the following services:

  • several waiting rooms, differing in the level of comfort;
  • restrooms;
  • mother and baby room;
  • various information boards;
  • buffet;
  • the shops;
  • electronic ticket purchase terminals;
  • hotel;
  • Wi-Fi
  • departments of household services;
  • ATMs
  • Museum
  • porters services;
  • home delivery service for tickets and baggage.




If necessary, you can advertise over the speakerphone, take a taxi and make hotel reservations.

Ticket office

Ticket offices of Ulyanovsk railway station offer the sale of tickets for commuter and long-distance trains. All of them have a flexible mode of operation, there are also round-the-clock windows. All cash desks have a technical break. In the 3rd window, the passenger can pay by credit card. At the 4th box office you can buy a ticket for international flights. On the ground floor of the station there is a luggage room. On the territory of the railway station there is a call center on duty around the clock. Also, the facility is under the protection of the police department.





Comfort for every passenger

The railway complex meets all the requirements for creating favorable conditions for people with disabilities. There are specialized doors that allow disabled people to easily pass in a wheelchair, telephone and help information are conveniently located for all passengers to familiarize themselves with. All ticket offices have intercoms, and the passage through the tunnel to the platform is equipped with special ramps.

Named train, running to Moscow, has a special car for passengers with disabilities. It is equipped with a specialized lift. Switches, sockets, a call button in it are located at a height convenient for this category of people. There are tablets with special embossed text that are designed to be read with the help of fingers by visually impaired people. Railway station in Ulyanovsk is maximally adapted for the convenience and comfort of people with disabilities.

How to get to the railway complex





To get to the railway station, guests and residents of the city can use the following public transport services:

  • buses No. 1, 40 and 89;
  • trams nos. 4,10 and 9;
  • fixed-route taxis No. 1, 32, 2, 33, 38, 37, 55, 69, 68, 91, 70, 94.

Bus route number 89 passes through the river. Volga to the Zavolzhsky district, the route takes about 30 minutes.

To do this, you can use the self-service terminal or the ticket office of the station. This must be done at least 1 hour before the departure of the train.
