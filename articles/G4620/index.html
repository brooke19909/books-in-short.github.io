How to photograph nails? Rules for a good photo

For a master who seeks to promote his profile on social networks, it is obvious that competent advertising is a real marketing engine. It is impossible to attract customers without beautiful photo works.

Many bloggers strive to learn how to beautifully photograph nails. Today, there are many experts in style and beauty who write about trends and interesting techniques, new techniques and unusual materials.

Beautiful photos of nails can also make money. They can not only be used to advertise their own services, but also sold on photo hosting sites.

Overview of Common Errors

Before you learn how to take pictures correctly, briefly consider a list of what you should not do.

The main mistake is negligence. Extra items that are not related to manicure, dust and a poorly polished surface - this is something that should not be in the photo.

Professionals do not recommend composing the composition so that equipment gets into the frame: lamp, hood, dry heat. No place in the production frame and bottles with professional liquids.

Unforgivable blurry photos. This happens if you take a picture while holding the camera or phone on weight. A dusty or fogged lens is another way to hopelessly ruin a shot.

Common mistakes are related to hand position. The pose should not be tense, forced.

Light works wonders

Light in photography is the foundation of the basics. Correctly selected lighting allows you to make an exquisite photo of discreet nails, which can be used even for advertising on billboards.

The main rule of beautiful photography of nails is that the light should not be yellow. It gives the skin an unpleasant hue, hands seem old and tired, small natural folds turn into wrinkles. Materials, even the best, also lose: the saturation of shades is lost, the top seems dull, burned out.

Remember: most incandescent bulbs give exactly yellow light.

To shoot a manicure, you can use the same lamp that the master uses to illuminate the workplace in the process. The vast majority of such professional lighting products give exactly white diffused light. It is advisable to direct the lamp not at the nails themselves, but at the reflector (it can easily be made of foil; even a sheet of glossy white paper is suitable).

More is not better

An important question: how many fingers should be in the frame? Some masters are sure that all 10. But such photos look inorganic. This is especially true for frames in which fingers are intertwined. In this case, it is difficult to understand the idea and track the logic.

To show how the cuticle is processed, whether there is an even flare, how the end face is framed, it is enough that there are 4 or even 2 nails in the frame.

You can put one hand in the foreground and focus the camera on it, and leave the other visible in the distance, but slightly blurred.

Hand setting

Speaking about how to photograph nails correctly, special attention should be paid to the position in which the brushes will be. The more natural the pose, the better.

Hands look good with their fingers touching a wall or table. The hand, palm down, looks dull and uninteresting. You can put it on the back side and slightly bend your fingers, turning your nails to the camera.

Background for frame

Today on sale a huge variety of gorgeous photophones that are quite cheap. Very popular backgrounds that simulate textured boards, cork, matting.

The surface can be any, even black. however, mottled spots should not be. Choose backgrounds with uniform shades.

Answering the question, on which background to photograph nails, some advise to order a special name sheet from the printing house with an anagram or initials of the master. You can also print the addresses of pages on social networks and advertising a beauty salon. But the shots made on such backgrounds look like commercial advertising, and not the art work in which the talent is invested.

Perfect highlights

Equal identical flare - a sign of skill. They say that the master made the perfect alignment of the nail plates.

But not everyone knows how to photograph flare nails. To get them, you need to use not reflected light, but direct light. For ring-shaped reflections, a lamp in the form of a ring with small diode bulbs is needed.

Of course, beautiful glare will not work if the alignment is not perfect. Errors in this arrangement of light will be clearly visible.

Post processing

The main rule of a successful photo is to take it 7 times, but publish it once. Take a few pictures, and choose the best one to publish.

It is acceptable to use Photoshop. With it, you can correct the failed horizon, crop the excess background, remove small errors.

Never try to correct your sins regarding manicure with the help of graphic editors. Do not touch the cuticle, highlights and ends. This will not bring good results. By deceiving a client, you overestimate your own bar, declare a level that you have not yet reached. If they come to you for a service that you cannot provide, scandals cannot be avoided.

As you can see, there is nothing complicated in the art of photography. As in teaching nail art, much depends on practice. Train, learn new things. Over time, you will certainly get a good result.
