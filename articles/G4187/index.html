Five Ways to Raise Finances with Money Origami

In the struggle for financial well-being, all means are good, even those that many consider to be simple fun. In any case, even if the origami out of money does not make you richer, you will still have a nice little thing, looking at which, many will ask questions about where you got it from and how to make it. If you want to settle a nice craft in origami technique in your wallet, then carefully read this article: in it you will find detailed instructions and a lot of ideas for figures composed of banknotes.

Dollar sign made from dollar bills

The first thing that comes to mind when the word money is a dollar sign. Let's figure out how to make it, having only one paper bill on hand.

  1. Fold any paper note in half along the long side and unfold it.
  2. Now bend the right and left sides of the bill to this fold.
  3. Repeat and bend the sides to the middle again.
  4. Next, bend the upper right corner down so that you get an acute angle from above. Flatten it. Repeat this action with the upper left corner. Also straighten. You should have a cross from the folds.
  5. Bend the top along the fold lines so that you get two folds that could be opened.
  6. Open the upper left corner and form a figure so that the triangle, which is in the middle, is bent inward. Your figure should resemble the letter M.
  7. Close the left corner and fold the figure. You should get a right triangle on top.
  8. Now fold this thin stripe so that it resembles the Latin letter S. From the bottom, leave the stripe, which will be the final stripe on the dollar sign. The whole figure should take you about 2/3 of the bill, and the dollar line - the remaining third.
  9. Pull this strip through the dollar.

This is what you should get.

Origami shirt made of money

Here is such a charming shirt with a tie that you can do if you follow this simple instruction:

  1. Fold the bill in half along the long edge, straighten it.
  2. Bend the edges to the center line so that you get a triangle.
  3. Bend this edge onto yourself again.
  4. From an inverted triangle, form a narrow tie. To make it more convenient to do this, first outline the future tie with a pencil and only then fold the edges of the banknote. If you did everything right, then you will get a tie, and the top of the shirt will be slightly slanted.
  5. Now put the origami of money tie up and fold the side edges to the central fold. The tie will hide under the paper - it must be carefully removed so as not to tear the origami.
  6. Next, bend the opposite edge of the banknote half an inch inward - this will be the collar.
  7. Leave the edge of the future collar free by 2.5-3 cm and bend the corners.
  8. Spread the craft along its entire length and tackle the central seam: lower half a centimeter below the tie and bend the edges at an angle of 45 ¬∞. Repeat the same action on the tie from the side of the collar - you should get a "boat".
  9. Now bend your boat so that its side edges form small sleeves for the shirt.
  10. Turn the shirt face to the table and bend the bill in half.
  11. Turn the shirt back towards you and place the collar over the tie.

Iron all folds well and enjoy the result of origami from money.

Origami heart made of money

To create a heart from a banknote, you must:

  1. Fold the bill in half lengthwise and unfold.
  2. Fold in half across.
  3. Now the right and left parts of the banknote need to be folded so that they go along the central fold. A corner should form below.
  4. Flip the origami to the back and fold the top of the origami down about a centimeter.
  5. Roughly divide the upper part into two identical halves and bend a triangle into the middle of the origami, so that a characteristic notch of the heart appears.
  6. Bend the corners on the sides. You should get two semicircles. Round them slightly with your hand if they turn out to be too angular.

Done! You got an original origami from money, which will be the highlight of your wallet!
