Night clubs in Yalta: description

Where can I relax at night? What interesting places does Yalta hide? Clubs and nightlife await everyone who came to this resort city.

Concert Hall "Akon"

In such a place you can listen to really high-quality music. Famous top entertainers perform here.

Every day in the club you can relax and have fun. Unique moments of relaxation and pleasure are available to all visitors to the institution.

Night club "Captain": description of the establishment

Let's list the night clubs of Yalta? Consider the Captain. This is the largest entertainment venue for a night's rest. It is placed in three levels. The beach is located on the first floor, the Jolly Roger restaurant with good cuisine is on the second floor, and the concert hall with a large stage, a VIP zone and a dance floor is on the third.





This place often hosts concerts of stars of the eighties and nineties. There are also discos with the best DJs.

Parking is available, for cars there is convenient access. You can pay by credit card.

Club "Riviera"

What to visit night clubs in Yalta? "Riviera" offers to plunge into the extravaganza of a bright southern night. There is entertainment for every taste. Charming dancers show a very interesting show. In a relaxed atmosphere, you can relax, enjoy socializing with friends, dance on the dance floor. There is a hookah menu.

"Orange": a description of the institution

Describing the night clubs of Yalta, we will talk about "Orange". This is not just a club, but also a cafe-restaurant, where in the daytime you can relax in a pleasant atmosphere. It serves gourmet cuisine.





In the evening, the cafe becomes a full-fledged club, which many people who like dancing parties want to get into. The institution has a very stylish interior. Soft sofas. The ceiling is decorated in a very original way: it was decorated with photographs of celebrities. The club offers beautiful views of the coast. There is a fifteen-meter bar counter in the establishment. Professional bartenders work for her.

Salon "Silver Horseshoe"

In the summer, there are daily parties in this institution. Each day has its own party specifics (for example, retro, latin and others). On Wednesday, girls can join the club for free at any time. The rest of the days, ladies can go free until ten in the evening. The atmosphere in the club is relaxed, so you can relax.

Club "Van Gogh"

Continuing to describe the night clubs of Yalta, we will tell about this institution. It is located on the waterfront. This is not just a club, but also a cafe-bar. There are also VIP sectors. The institution works around the clock. There is a special simplified menu. At night, the institution offers a variety of dance programs, shows with the participation of various DJs and famous artists. Always glad to guests of Yalta.

Night clubs: photos and description

Club "Buddha" is located in the city center, there are hot parties. Visitors have a lot of positive impressions after visiting the club. There is a crazy menu, it presents a lot of interesting drinks and dishes.





The Golden Beach Club has a restaurant serving Mediterranean cuisine. You can visit the pool and the large outdoor veranda.

In Planet Bristol, you can watch a variety of show programs. There is a show ballet, competitions are held. Musical groups also come to the institution.

The peculiarity of the Off Bar establishment is that residents of the best clubs of Ukraine, Europe and Russia perform here.





Club "The Island" sounds beautiful tracks from resident DJs. Every Tuesday, saxophonists, drummers and other musicians perform. On Wednesday there is the opportunity to listen to jazz.

Disco "Lagoon" - the best place for dancing. There is a dance floor and other activities for body and soul.

In the club "Matrix" you can listen to music of different styles (pop, house and others). Top DJs in the country follow music politics.

"Tropic" is located on the outskirts of the city. Parties are held here daily. Decorated institution in the African style. On the walls you can see figures and masks.

Club Malibu has an interesting interior, designer furniture is installed. There is also a custom-made bar counter.

The Black Sea is a fairly popular club where jazz live music sounds. There are also various dance parties and discos.

Ikon Club boasts a large dance floor. World stars perform here.





Club "Zephyr" will delight in a pleasant atmosphere. The staff in the institution is attentive. The club sometimes hosts seminars, you can rent it for celebrations.

Conclusion

Now you know the popular night clubs in Yalta. Visitors leave different reviews about such establishments, because everyone has different tastes and needs. Someone prefers big pathos clubs, others like thematic institutions.
