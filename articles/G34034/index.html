Ergonomics, appearance, dimensions

Samsung Galaxy J5 Prime (Gold, Black) looks pretty presentable. The phone differs from its predecessor primarily in the body material. Here, the developers used metal, which, no doubt, is worthy of respect. The quality and assembly are not satisfactory. During operation, users did not notice any creaks or backlash. How is the front panel of the Samsung Galaxy J5 Prime designed? 2.5D glass gives originality. It is worth noting that such a solution was first used in the J-line. Sensors

The Samsung Galaxy J5 Prime was the first J- series smartphone to feature a fingerprint scanner. It works well. Located in a very convenient location. However, with other sensors, the manufacturer did not bother. For some reason, the device does not have an elementary ambient light sensor. The developers refused from the compass, magnetometer, gyroscope. All that will be available to the owners of this model is an accelerometer, a proximity sensor and an orientation sensor. Many users have noticed that it is on such trifles that the company is trying to save. Whether this decision is correct, time will tell.

Samsung Galaxy J5 Prime: performance features

Memory

It's time to figure out what memory features are in the Samsung Galaxy J5 Prime. To begin, pay attention to the volume of operational. The developers in this model installed two gigabytes. Half is already occupied by the system, but the rest will be enough to completely forget about crashes while working with applications.

Built-in storage has a capacity of only 16 gigabytes. Is this enough for the modern user? Probably not. To work with applications, only 9 GB remains free, and this is critically small. Expand integrated memory at the expense of the drive. The device has a separate slot, so there is no need to abandon the second SIM card. There is also support for USB OTG drives .

Battery

In order to find out all the characteristics of the Samsung Galaxy J5 Prime, you need to understand the battery life. The device has an insufficiently capacious battery. Its volume is only 2400 mAh. But having learned about it, it is not necessary to be upset ahead of time. The new processor is energy efficient, so even with active use, you can safely count on one day of work. In video mode, the smartphone on a single charge lasts up to 12 hours. About as much time can be spent surfing the Internet. Users noticed that if the active state of the screen does not exceed two hours per day, then the gadget will be able to work for almost 3 days. Camera features

What cameras are implemented in the Samsung Galaxy J5 Prime? Frontal features are not very impressed by users. It is based on a 5 megapixel sensor. But it is worth noting that, in comparison with the predecessor, the aperture value was increased to 2.2 against 1.9. There is no point in expecting a lot from the selfie. Color and quality are average.

The main camera has a resolution of 13 megapixels. There is an LED flash. But users noticed that in poor lighting it is no use. Photos are gray, dull, with a low level of detail. But in the daytime you can make quite high-quality pictures. The dynamic range is not maximum, but wide. Color reproduction is close to natural shades. Image clarity is acceptable.

We draw the attention of buyers that both cameras are able to work in video mode with Full HD resolution, which is pretty good for inexpensive gadgets. The frame rate is 30 FPS.
