Hotel Kristal Sands 3 *, North Goa: overview, description and reviews of tourists

North Goa is rightfully considered the main resort of India. Thousands of tourists from all over the world come here every year. Of course, North Goa is also popular with Russian vacationers. The sea at this resort is warm, the beaches are clean, and the hotels are quite comfortable. Many tourists from Russia choose the village of Candolim for holidays in North Goa and stay at the Kristal Sands 3 * hotel. Reviews about this hotel are mostly good.

Where is the hotel

This hotel is located in a very convenient location of the village. It is only 150 meters from the beach. That is, tourists have to go to the sea no longer than 3-5 minutes. Getting to the hotel from the airport will not be difficult even for those who are traveling to India for the first time. You will have to overcome the distance of only 40 km. You can get to the hotel both by transfer bus and taxi.





Located next to the Kristal Sands Beach Resort 3 * hotel (India, Goa), not only the beach, but also the entire main infrastructure of the village of Candolim. In the immediate vicinity of the hotel there are shops, cafes, market, post office, pharmacy. Moreover, no noisy roads near the hotel do not pass. This is a must for a good hotel in India. The fact is that traffic on the roads in the villages of this country is very busy. Moreover, drivers practically do not comply with traffic rules, preferring to "communicate" by means of beeps. At the Kristal Sands Hotel, guests can relax in complete silence.

What is a hotel

Area hotel Kristal Sands 3 * has a small. It is a three-story bungalow building with facades painted snow-white. The courtyard at the hotel is very beautiful. Palm trees and other exotic plants are planted everywhere, as well as lawns. Therefore, the territory of the hotel resembles a small, and very well-groomed ik garden . The paths and the space around the pool in the courtyard are lined with large pink paving tiles, which is very convenient to walk on.

Number of rooms

In total, the Kristal Sands 3 * hotel provides 22 comfortable rooms. If desired, you can remove:

  • Deluxe Rooms with one double or two single beds;

  • Special Deluxe Rooms - rooms designed for families with children.

From the windows of all the rooms rented out in the hotel, you can enjoy beautiful views of the garden, sea or pool.





Room Amenities

In addition to beds, bedside tables and wardrobes, the apartments of the Kristal Sands 3 * hotel include:

  • mirror;

  • TV;

  • refrigerator;

  • air conditioning.

For an additional fee, residents can also use the minibar and safe. Some of the rented rooms have balconies where you can dry beach items. A shower room is available in every room of the hotel.

Apartment reviews

In most cases, on specialized forums, former guests highly praise this hotel. The rooms in the hotel are quite spacious, the furniture is good, and the air conditioners and refrigerators are working properly. Rooms are cleaned in Kristal Sands B each R esort 3 * upon request of residents. To the maid washed the floors and wiped the dust, as well as changed bed linen and towels, you need to leave the key at the reception.





Television in the hotel is satellite , but unfortunately there are no Russian channels. However, many tourists are happy to look and just at the singing and dancing Indians.

Hotel Infrastructure

In addition to the pool, on the territory of the hotel Kristal Sands Beach Resort 3 * (India, Goa), you can use other services. Contaminated things, for example, many guests do not wash in the shower, but in the laundry room. True, you have to pay for this convenience separately. Also, if necessary, guests can call a doctor directly to the room. It is also possible to book a service such as a shuttle service to the airport or railway station. There is an exchange office at the hotel. Wi-Fi in the hotel guests can use for free.

Reviews of tourists about the service

According to most former guests of Kristal Sands 3 * , the staff at this hotel is very polite. Administrators and maids are friendly and helpful towards tourists. If you encounter any problems, they can be relocated to another room.

No larceny at the hotel was noticed by tourists. Many people leave pretty valuable things in plain sight. And nothing is ever lost. Some staff at the hotel reception speak good Russian. Which, of course, is very convenient.





Hotel Disadvantages

This hotel has practically no disadvantages. The disadvantages of the hotel, some tourists attribute only the presence of mosquitoes in the room. Of course, you can take with you to India a remedy for these flying insects from Russia. But it would be better to buy some local repellent.

Some guests also consider the disadvantage of staying in the hotel to be not too new and beautiful decoration of the rooms. The walls of the hotel rooms can be decorated with old wallpaper or painted with unpopular colors. Another disadvantage of the hotel is the lack of a set of household chemicals for bathing in the shower. Only a small bar of soap is provided to tourists at this hotel.

Hotel Food Reviews

At the hotel, among other things, there is a small Italian restaurant. Breakfast at Kristal Sands Beach 3 * is included in the room rate. Judging by the reviews of tourists, the food in this hotel is quite scarce, but still better than in many other three-star hotels in North Goa. For breakfast, in addition to traditional tea, coffee and toast, fruit, fresh and omelettes are served. If desired, the restaurant can order additional dishes for a fee. They cook in this establishment well. But, unfortunately, the dishes are quite expensive. Therefore, many tourists prefer breakfast at the hotel, but lunch and dinner - in a nearby cafe. Here you can also order delicious food, but at a lower cost.





Road to the beach

Convenient location - this is what, as already mentioned, I received especially good hotel Kristal Sands Beach Resort 3 * guest reviews. To the beach from this hotel you need to go past the picturesque local village houses. In the courtyard of one of these huts, pigs are bred, which many tourists consider very cute and pretty. There is no smell from these animals.

Those who do not like village landscapes too much can go to the beach along another, more civilized road - past shops and cafes. But in this case, the path to the sea will take a little longer - about 15 minutes. Therefore, most guests find the road through the village still more convenient. Moreover, there are no dogs and cows in this part of the village.

Beach

The coastline near the Kristal Sands 3 * hotel (India) for a comfortable stay, according to most tourists, is very good. There are not too many people on the beach. Sun loungers and umbrellas can be rented even at peak season at a very low cost. Beach towels the hotel guests have the opportunity to take on the respawn. This hotel service is completely free. Beach cleaning is carried out daily. Therefore, the sand here is always very clean.





In general, the coastline opposite the hotel is popular with all tourists, without exception. Not a single bad review on the network about her. Many do not even recommend buying excursions to other, more popular and widely publicized beaches of North Goa. Even they with the beach Kristal Sands 3 * do not go to any comparison.

The Arabian Sea itself in the area of ​​this hotel is pretty clean. True, the excitement in the ocean is almost always felt here. The water in the Arabian Sea is very salty. Therefore, diving when swimming is more careful. If water gets into your eyes, they will pinch. After sunset, it is better not to swim in the sea. At this time, various animals are activated in the water. Experienced tourists advise to sunbathe in India carefully. You can get burns on the beach in North Goa in literally 20 minutes.

Where to eat without going far from the sea

Most tourists like the necks working by the ocean near the Kristal Sands Hotel. Many are advised to visit Prince of Tides or U Roma establishments on the beach. Both of these necks serve delicious food at a low cost. Prince of Tides has comfortable, neat chairs for the convenience of customers. “At Roma” you just have to sit on the sand.

Features of infrastructure near the hotel

Of course, you can eat deliciously in the village not only on the beach. Many tourists are also advised to visit the second cafe on the main street to the right of the hotel. Here, according to most hotel guests, they simply serve chic dishes at an affordable cost. The menu in this cafe is also in Russian.





What attractions are available to guests

The excursion business in North Goa is developed, unfortunately, not too well. However, guests of the Kristal Sands 3 * hotel (India, North Goa) still have the opportunity to look at different sights. This hotel is located on the outskirts of Candolim, right on the border with another popular resort of North Goa - Calangute. So its residents are more than accessible attractions of both of these settlements. If desired, hotel guests can visit, for example:

  • Tibetan market of Calangute;

  • Fort Aguada in Candolim.

You can buy all kinds of souvenirs at the Tibetan market in Calangute. It also sells original silver jewelry with natural stones. And they cost literally a penny. Some tourists checked them in Russia for authenticity. The jewelry turned out to be real. This market has received very good reviews because it sells beautiful, high-quality and not too expensive Indian rugs. The Tibetan market of Calangute is open daily.

Experienced tourists advise taking excursions not at local companies, but at Russian guides. In this case, time can be spent much more interesting. Aguada Fort is one of North Goa's most renowned sites. In order to get here, you need to go along the main street of Candolim beyond its limits all the way to the hill. The fort is open to visitors from 10 am to 6 pm. It is an ancient building built in 1612 by the Portuguese. The southern part of the fort is a current prison. In addition to the Indians, it also contains many foreign citizens, mainly for the drug trade.
