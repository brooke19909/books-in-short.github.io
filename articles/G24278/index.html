Washing machine LG E10B9LD: specifications, reviews

Nowadays, on the market you can find a huge number of washing machines that differ from each other in design, size, technical specifications, manufacturers, etc. They are divided into different groups, depending on the amount of water, electricity and, of course, prices .

Currently, an automatic machine is necessary for everyone. The range of this type of equipment is truly huge. This is due to the large number of import options. About 25 foreign brands are represented on the domestic market, while the Russian ones - no more than 5. However, even with this situation, the assortment is easy to get confused. The South Korean company, known as LG, is one of the leading household appliances in the world market. Its products are quite in demand and popular among Russian buyers. Every year LG improves its devices, comes up with more interesting models that satisfy the needs of consumers of different categories. In their assortment there is both a budget equipment and a premium class. The most popular washing machine is the LG E10B9LD. What it is, we learn by reading the article.



Short review

What is this model? Complete harmony of appearance, quality materials and, of course, technical specifications. It should be noted that this particular machine is perfect for those who aspire to a budget, but high-quality option. The model is able to automatically check for damage, which it will inform the owner. The device is equipped with a system that itself forms a foam.

Speaking of functionality, it is necessary to highlight the fact that the appliance has 9 different washing modes. Among them there are those that are designed for children's things. Also, importantly, you can independently change the temperature and spin speed. This washing machine is suitable for those who prefer silence, because the appliance works very quietly.

Testimonials from people say that in fact, due to the use of new technology, all dirt is washed off quickly enough. Clothes practically do not crease and dries instantly.



Benefits

The manufacturer, with the goal of increasing the service life, installed a direct drive on his device. In addition to increasing the time of use, this version of the drum spends less energy, and this is already quite a significant advantage. The device is able to work quietly, unlike its competitors. Due to the newly introduced program (function), the manufacturer decided to completely abandon the pulley and belt. This is also an effective method against rapid breakdown of the device. The factory gives 10 years warranty. The drive actually works quite well, which confirms the quiet operation. You can not be afraid to wash at night, for fear of waking someone up. The LG E10B9LD washing machine has an engine, which is located in the axis of the drum. This option helps to reduce vibration, but all recommendations must be considered when installing the device.

Washing modes

The machine is capable of washing things weighing up to 5 kg. It is worth noting its wonderful function, which avoids unscheduled breakdown of the mechanism. It is an automatic system that distributes things evenly, avoiding overload at one point on the drum. This function is active not only at the beginning of washing, but also before spinning. Many people like the fact that the LG E10B9LD washing machine is equipped with different modes. Thanks to these options, a person does not have to set the same parameters every time. Installation

The width of the washing machine allows you to install the appliance in the kitchen or bathroom. It does not take up much space. The floor in the right place should not have a slope (if there is one, then no more than 1 o ). The socket, into which the plug from the machine is connected, must not be used by additional electrical appliances at the same time, otherwise this will lead to burning of the wiring. From the rear panel to the wall, the recommended distance is about 10 cm, on the sides - no more than 2 cm. Foreign objects cannot be stored on the LG E10B9LD. This will help keep the original appearance longer. Do not forget that the machine has a built-in fan. If the floor is still uneven, then you should find another place. You do not need to use bars and other coasters to properly install the washing machine, since during operation it can come off them.

Before the washing process

The first time (after arriving from the store or from the warehouse), it is necessary to start the first circle without laundry, this method will remove excess moisture left from the factory during the testing of the device.

Before you put things in the hatch, you need to carefully study the information on the tags. As a rule, it is there that contains data on the material, the recommended temperature, the method of extraction and drying. It is best to wash all clothes in several passes, grouping similar options.

When loading laundry into the machine LG E10B9LD (reviews about this model are positive for the most part), it is necessary to alternate big things with small ones. Otherwise, the drum may break. You should definitely check your pockets for money, a phone or other things. Otherwise, this can lead not only to damage to these items, but also to damage to the machine itself.
