The hotel complex, owned by the Mirabell chain, consists of three-story buildings in a Mediterranean style, an intricate shape of pools, a landscaped area with palm trees and tropical shrubs.

On one side of the hotel lies the Sinai Desert, surrounded by mountain ranges, on the other hand, exotic paintings of the Red Sea and the harsh island of Tiran appear before the eyes.

Jaz Mirabel Club 5 is a great place for a relaxing holiday for couples or tourists with children.

Rooms: Guests have a choice of three room categories. It can be superior, family or large suite with a separate bedroom. The apartments have terraces where you can have breakfast and sunbathe. Non-alcoholic drinks and fresh beer are always available in the room bar. Daily maid service and linen changes are provided. Drinks and meals can be ordered in the rooms around the clock.



Beach: On a sandy shallow beach with a pontoon, canopies and sunbeds are installed. Towels and mattresses are provided.

Meals: The Jaz Mirabel has an all-inclusive system, which includes three meals a day (buffet), as well as coffee, desserts, tea, fresh juices, draft local wines throughout the day.

A la carte restaurants and several bars are open to serve tourists: in the lobby, by the pool and on the beach. The restaurant serves mainly international dishes. Chinese restaurant offers exotic from different provinces of China and traditional Japanese dishes. The Italian restaurant serves pizza and pasta with sauces.

Additional Information: Jaz Mirabel Club 5 offers standard household, transport and medical services. There is an exchange office and an ATM, there are shops and Internet access.

For children there is a playground and a shallow pool. The mini club organizes classes by age, and if necessary, the nanny will look after the child.



Particular attention is paid to entertainment and recreation. On the territory there are several pools, including heated. There is a tennis court, diving school, a small water park. At sea there are conditions for water fun and beach games. Available relaxation in the jacuzzi, relaxing massage, exercise classes. Evening at the hotel - themed dinners, dances and music programs.

The rooms of Jaz Mirabel Club 5 are spacious and comfortable. Change of linen and wet cleaning - daily. Apartments feature Russian channels, a free safe and a paid minibar.

Reviews: Anyone who loves peace and quiet can recommend the Jaz Mirabel Club 5. Reviews indicate that this place is most suitable for couples and middle-aged and older people who seek a relaxing holiday at the beach resort.
