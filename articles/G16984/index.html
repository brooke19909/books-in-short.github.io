Location

Club Phaselis Hotel (Kemer) is located in the popular resort village of Goynuk, on a small peninsula. The sea surrounds it on three sides, on the fourth - a huge forest park and Taurus mountain range. In the line of coastal hotels, the Club Phaselis is the last from Goynuk and Antalya, but the first from Kemer. It is located quite secluded. The nearest "neighbors" from it are within 2-5 km. For some tourists this is a plus, for others - a minus. An excellent road runs past the complex, along which dolmushi run every 15 minutes. For $ 2, they can go home to Kemer, about 7 km away, or for $ 1 to the center of Goynuk, which is only 2 km away. Antalya Airport is 45 km from the hotel. It takes about an hour to get to the transfer provided by the travel agency, as tourists are usually brought to other hotels along the way. For lovers of attractions, the Phaselis Club is also an excellent choice. Kemer is an amazing city with ancient history, and, of course, there are many interesting places worthy of attention. For example, only 12 km from the hotel complex are the ruins of ancient Phaselis, and 50 km away is the ancient Olympos, where you can also see Mount Chimera with always burning lights.

Accommodation in cottages

The Phaselis Club Hotel (Kemer) has the category HV-1, which means that it can be accommodated in a bungalow. Tourists can choose from rooms in the central building (Phaselis Hill). It has 11 floors, between which there are passages. The second type of accommodation is in two-story and three-story cottages, of which there are eighty units. The hotel has 497 rooms of various categories.

In the cottages rooms are called Club. There are 362 units in total. Among them are rooms:

1. Standard (306 units). Their area is different - from 20 "squares" and above (maximum 35). Number of beds - 2 plus, if necessary, 2 extra. Equipment: comfortable new or updated furniture (beds, wardrobe, bedside tables, table, chairs, mirror), flat-screen TV with Russian channels, telephone, minibar (daily replenishment of drinks), air conditioning, safe, balcony with outdoor furniture . The hygiene room has a toilet, washbasin, shower, hairdryer. Nutrition

Tasty exclusive dishes and sweets Turkey loves to surprise tourists. The Phaselis Club Hotel (Kemer) maintains this glorious tradition. They are fed here on an All Inclusive Ultra system. The hotel has several restaurants, cafes and bars. The main restaurant "Phaselis" hosts early (4:00 in the morning), regular and late breakfasts, lunch, dinner. All according to the buffet system. It also provides night service (from 12 nights to 4 in the morning).

In addition, the hotel has a cafe where you can enjoy delicious cakes, pies, ravioli, shawarma and other Turkish delicacies. Ayran with cakes are also served in the Yoruk tent, where tourists are offered to sit on soft pillows in the shade of oranges and palm trees. At several points (main restaurant, bars) ice cream is served every day. Alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks are also included in the price of the tours, so they can be received free of charge in the bars on the beach, in the lobby and by the pool. For snacks throughout the day, the Club Phaselis Hotel (Kemer) invites you to visit the snack bar and beach bars. Reviews of tourists about food are generally positive. Leisure

To all vacationers love Turkey, Kemer, Club Phaselis, the hotel organizes entertainment programs, contests, shows, parties, and a disco every day. Once a week, music groups and other artists give concerts here. The animation group organizes morning exercises, water aerobics, fun competitions in archery, darts, water polo. The losing team is invited to complete simple comic tasks. On their own, tourists can have fun in any of the hotel pools, among which there is a deep (1.5 meters deep), a reservoir with water slides (0.9 meters deep). A huge wooden ship is installed near one of the pools, protecting it from the wind. For sports enthusiasts, the hotel provides an opportunity to prove themselves on a football field, tennis court, numerous sports fields, at a tennis or billiard table. Fans of relaxation can relax in the SPA center, where there is a sauna, hammam, massage rooms, a beauty salon.

For children, the hotel has its own shallow pool and two water slides, there is a children's animation, a disco club, a games room, where tutors work with small guests.
