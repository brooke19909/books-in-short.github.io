Hotel Issara Resort 3 *, Thailand, Phuket: overview, description, characteristics and reviews

By choosing the Issara Resort 3 * hotel, you can enjoy a good rest in the best Thai traditions.





Hotel Description

The small resort hotel Issara Resort 3 * offers you to stay in small, but very cozy and comfortable rooms, which provide all the amenities familiar to a modern person. It is important that each room has its own balcony, from where you can admire the surrounding landscapes. It is also possible to use wireless Internet.

The design of the rooms, as well as the Issara Resort 3 * hotel as a whole, is a skillful combination of Thai interior traditions and modern laconic style. The design is beautiful, rigorous and artsy at the same time. An important feature of this hotel is the pool located directly on the roof.

Resort Hotel Issara Resort 3 * cares not only about comfort, but also about the leisure of its guests. You can spend time in a cozy restaurant or a comfortable bar. Also, there are many popular entertainment, such as billiards, a gym and more. Do not forget about your health. Be sure to visit the massage room and enjoy the spa services.





Hotel location

Issara Resort 3 * Hotel (Phuket) is located in the heart of Patong, where all city and tourist life is concentrated. Just a few meters from the hotel, you will find numerous shops, cafes, clubs, restaurants and markets. The road to the beach will take you no more than 5 minutes. But going to the international airport will take about 40 minutes.

Hotel Rooms

Issara Resort 3 * (Phuket) is a relatively small hotel, which has only 76 guest rooms. All apartments are divided into two categories. The first class is a deluxe double room, which is also possible additional check-in one child. This room consists of a spacious bedroom with an attached bathroom and a small balcony.

The second category is superior. These are superior double rooms. They differ from the previous category in a more spacious area, as well as a wider selection of furniture. So, in addition to the bedroom, there is also a small living area with upholstered furniture, a floor lamp and a coffee table. There is also a bathroom and a balcony.





Room features

Comfortable guest accommodation is the main rule of the Issara Resort 3 * hotel (Phuket). Guest reviews note that the following amenities are provided in each hotel room:

  • large modern TV with connecting a large package of cable channels;
  • a telephone through which you can call the reception at any time, as well as contact friends and relatives anywhere in the world;
  • so that you are not afraid for the safety of your valuables, a safe is provided in each room;
  • there is a minibar that, at your request, the hotel staff will fill up with your favorite drinks (it‚Äôs worth noting that this service is quite expensive, so many tourists use it just like a small refrigerator);
  • all hotel rooms are covered by the wireless Internet signal area;
  • You can create your own comfortable microclimate in the room using an individual air conditioner;
  • the bathroom is equipped with a toilet and shower, and guests are offered all the necessary bath accessories and a cosmetic set;
  • there are plastic chairs on the balcony , as well as a rope for drying clothes and towels;
  • you will always have access to boiled water, because the rooms have an electric kettle.




Guest Services

The hotel Issara Resort 3 * (Phuket) offers guests a variety of services, which include both paid and free services. So, the amount you paid for hotel accommodation includes the use of the following options:

  • an iron is located at the maid on each floor;
  • every morning a rich buffet breakfast awaits you in the hotel restaurant;
  • If necessary, you can take a hairdryer from reception staff;
  • on the ground floor, in the hotel lobby, there is a small Internet cafe that you can use for a certain amount of time every day;
  • Despite the fact that the hotel does not have its own beach, even when heading to the city coast, you can take towels at the reception.

Of the paid services, the following are most in demand:

  • laundry, where you can not only order laundry services for soiled items, but also ironing your clothes;
  • for the use of equipment for playing billiards an hourly fee is charged;
  • Having paid a certain amount of funds at the reception, you will have the right to fill the minibar daily with alcohol and refreshments;
  • It is possible to order services such as food delivery and massage treatments in your room.




Hotel infrastructure

Many tourists dream of enjoying a colorful and at the same time comfortable vacation and choose Thailand for these purposes. Issara Resort 3 * ensures that its tourists do not feel the slightest discomfort while living in this hotel. For this purpose, the following infrastructure was formed:

  • on the roof of the hotel there is a large clean pool with fresh water, which is regularly treated with chlorine and other substances;
  • You can play sports in a small but well-equipped gym;
  • You can taste local dishes or eat a meal more familiar to you in the restaurant;
  • a huge assortment of drinks and snacks is available to guests in the cozy bar, which is located on the roof;
  • also on the roof, directly next to the pool, there is a spacious terrace with many sun loungers, on which you can sunbathe;
  • there is a small private parking area next to the hotel;
  • if your valuables do not fit in the safe, you can contact the reception staff with the goal of checking your luggage into the storage room;
  • photocopying, scanning and faxing services are available to all guests;
  • since the hotel building consists of several floors, an elevator is provided in it;
  • the hotel has a separate block of rooms especially for those guests who do not smoke;
  • by appointment, you can use the services of a massage therapist in a special office or directly in the room.




Useful information about the hotel

Going on vacation to the Issara Resort 3 * resort (Phuket, Patong), be sure to check out useful information regarding its work:

  • Settlement hours, namely check-in and check-out, are set at 14:00 and noon, respectively;
  • It is strictly forbidden to stay at the hotel any animals;
  • You have the opportunity to pay for services with a plastic card;
  • if you leave the hotel earlier than the period for which the room was booked, the funds paid will not be returned to you;
  • use of the safe installed in the room is provided free of charge;
  • there are vending machines in the lobby of the hotel to replenish the account of local mobile operators;
  • in the immediate vicinity of the hotel there is an exchange office;
  • while using a public iron or hairdryer, you agree to leave a security deposit of 500 baht to the hotel employee;
  • during the initial registration for accommodation in the room, you leave a deposit of $ 100, which will be returned to you upon departure;
  • if at the time of booking the tour you indicated the purpose of your visit to the hotel to hold a honeymoon, then upon arrival you will expect a pleasant surprise in the form of floral decoration in your room.
