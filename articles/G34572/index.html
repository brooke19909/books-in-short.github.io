The best youth hotels of Tunisia and their description

Every year an increasing number of Russians seek to relax abroad. The benefits of such a holiday include the highest level of service, the ability to enjoy the sea and the sun almost all year round. Among tourists, holidays in Tunisia are becoming increasingly popular. In particular, he is preferred by young people. And this is no coincidence, since the country has created all the conditions for active leisure activities. For youth, there are numerous beach discos, tourists are offered all kinds of show programs. Tunisia's youth hotels are paradises for an unforgettable vacation.

Youth holidays in Tunisia





The greatest interest for young people, of course, will be a visit to the resort town of Sousse. It is famous for the largest open-air disco Bora Bora on the African continent. In addition to her, the attention of tourists will attract the disco Maracana. For fans of modern electronic music, the Arena club will be of interest . In Sousse, the most popular all-inclusive Tunisia youth hotels are located. For lovers of ethnic style, the restaurant-club Red Iguana is suitable. Ten kilometers from the city of Sousse is the resort of Port El Kantaoui, famous for its water park and casino.

El Mouradi Port El Kantaoui Hotel





This beautiful four-star hotel is located on the Mediterranean coast. It has been working since 1995. In 2010, it was completely renovated. The hotel has seven hundred and ninety-five rooms equipped with air conditioning, mini-refrigerators, comfortable furniture. The balconies available in each room offer wonderful views of the beach and the sea.

Around the hotel is a green area. Nearby are the main local attractions such as the mosque, parks and harbor. A few meters away is a beach with sun loungers and a bar. You can also ride a catamaran.

Food and Services

Meals are organized in the restaurant on the basis of the "buffet". In the cozy bar, guests are offered a variety of drinks that perfectly quench their thirst. Guests have the opportunity to visit the solarium, a sauna with a hammam. There is also a fitness center equipped with modern fitness equipment. For various business events there is a business center. For those who like to travel by car there is a guarded parking lot. Youth hotels in Sousse (Tunisia) are entire hotel complexes with lots of entertainment.

Hotel Marabout (Sousse)



The hotel was built in 1998. In 2008 it was renovated. It currently has one hundred and thirty comfortable rooms equipped with everything necessary (balconies, bathrooms with showers, air conditioning). All rooms have telephones. A private beach and private parking are available on site.

For guests available sauna, spa, massage shiatsu. Sports enthusiasts have the opportunity to play golf and table tennis. Meals are organized in the restaurant. Breakfast is served daily. Throughout the territory there is the possibility of free access to the Internet.

Almost all youth hotels in Tunisia are conveniently located. This allows tourists to quickly reach local attractions and the port. Monastir Airport is a twenty-minute drive away.

El Mouradi Palace 5 *. Port El Kantaoui



The youngest hotels in Tunisia are located in Port El Kantaoui (Sousse). The five-star El Mouradi Palace Hotel is one of them. It was built in 1996 and is part of the Spanish hotel chain SOL MELIA. The number of rooms consists of four hundred and eighty double rooms. There are also suite rooms (one room) and junior suites (eighteen rooms). All of them are provided with everything you need (air conditioning, satellite TV, a minibar, comfortable modern furniture, a bathroom).

For guests there are three restaurants, two bars (one of them is near the pool) and an oriental cafe. They serve international food. One of the restaurants specializes in Italian cuisine.

In the immediate vicinity is the famous golf club. A twenty five minute drive away is the airport. The best youth hotels in Tunisia offer their visitors excellent conditions for an active holiday.

Hotel Oasis Marine. Jerba





Another ideal place for youth recreation in the state of Tunisia is Djerba. Youth hotels here are in large numbers. In particular, let's talk about Oasis Marine.

The hotel is located in a picturesque place, in an oasis near the village of Zarzis. On the territory and around it, the palm trees grove the tourist's eyes. In the immediate vicinity is the sea coast with a beach.

The youth hotels of Tunisia offer guests the best apartments and the most sophisticated entertainment. Oasis Marine Hotel has two hundred and seventy rooms. Of these, one hundred and sixty double bungalows, seventy-two rooms for three people. In the main building there are thirty-five rooms, designed to accommodate four guests. There are also four family villas. All rooms are equipped with balconies, modern comfortable furniture and household appliances, bathtub and toilet. The comfort of your stay is ensured by the presence of air conditioning.

Guests can swim in the outdoor or indoor pools and visit the spa with massage services, as well as a sauna. For children there is also a separate pool, there is a playground and a mini club, designed for young guests aged four to eight years. Sports enthusiasts can play ping pong, minigolf, play tennis, archery or visit the fitness center. For business events there is a conference room.

Tunisian youth hotels pay special attention to the quality nutrition of their customers. For guests there are three restaurants, four bars and a cafe specializing in the preparation of Moorish cuisine. The list of entertainment includes a visit to a night club, all kinds of show programs in the amphitheater. A variety of souvenirs can be purchased at shops working at the hotel. For those who want to see local attractions, they can be rented by car.

Hotel Hasdrubal Thalassa (Djerba)



It belongs to the fashionable hotels of Tunisia with a fairly high (about two hundred thousand rubles per week for two people) cost of living. However, the level of service, design of rooms and interiors, a picturesque area, a huge selection of entertainment will fully justify the cash costs. The rooms are luxuriously decorated and equipped with the necessary modern appliances. Each room has a safe for storing valuables. Special rooms are available for couples and non-smokers.

Guests have free access to the spa, sauna with hammam. Also, qualified hairdressers will make a fashionable hairstyle, and a massage session will leave an unforgettable experience. In the room, guests can relax in the jacuzzi with hydromassage. The balconies offer gorgeous views of the sea and the green surroundings.

Guests can visit the golf course, work out in the gym and fitness rooms. The beach offers a large selection of water rides and sports. Sun loungers, umbrellas and beach mattresses are provided free of charge. On request, guests are offered a traditional three-time or special vegetarian or diet menu.

Four-star hotel Riu Club Hotel Palm Azur (Djerba)





Another of the great hotels is the four-star Club hotel Riu Palm Azur located on the island of Djerba . It consists of three hundred twenty-six rooms, designed to accommodate three or two guests. There are also suite rooms, including family ones. All are decorated in a modern style and equipped with everything necessary for a comfortable stay. Each is equipped with air conditioning, a fridge, a safe for valuables and a TV. The balconies offer beautiful sea views.

Entertainment

Guests can swim in the beautiful pools and visit the spa with massage services, as well as a sauna. Families with children should also choose Tunisia youth hotels for holidays . Reviews indicate that each similar hotel complex has a good base for children's recreation. For kids there is a separate pool, playgrounds work. During the rest of the parents, various animation programs are held with the children.

Sports enthusiasts can play ping pong or minigolf, or visit the fitness center. Bike rental and horse riding are also available. For business events, there are five well-equipped conference rooms. There is also a store.

On a wonderful beach, two hundred meters from the hotel, vacationers are provided with free sun loungers, umbrellas and mattresses, towels. They also have a huge selection of water activities.

Among tourists, Tunisia youth hotels with discotheques are especially popular. Riu Club Hotel Palm Azur hosts dance shows in the evening. Internet access is available throughout. The hotel has four restaurants, a cafe and a bar. Breakfast is free of charge.

Thus, hotels in Tunisia are distinguished by comfortable living conditions and a variety of services provided. Tourists, based on personal preferences and material capabilities, can choose the most suitable for them.
