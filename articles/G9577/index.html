Thang Long Nha Trang (Vietnam / Nha Trang): tourists photos and reviews

Our article may be of interest to those tourists who are going to spend a vacation in Vietnam. We want to talk about one of the hotels of the famous resort called Nha Trang. Thang Long Nha Trang is a three star hotel located off the coast.

A bit about the hotel ...

Thang Long Nha Trang is on the second line, 300 meters from the beach. The hotel can be attributed to fairly young institutions, since it was built in 2011. The hotel consists of one fifteen-story building located in the center, the so-called European quarter.

The hotel offers its guests to use the services of a paid shuttle service to get from Kamran Airport, located 26 kilometers away. Thang Long Nha Trang stands out from the total mass of complexes. A tall narrow building remotely resembles a candle.

Number of rooms

Thang Long Nha Trang offers 57 rooms, including apartments in the following categories:

  1. Deluxe (Deluxe room).
  2. Improved Room (Superior).
  3. Standard
  4. Suite (Suite).

All rooms of the hotel are equipped with national-style wooden furniture, air conditioning, wireless internet, flat-screen TVs, minibars, satellite channels.



The rooms at Thang Long Nha Trang are small but comfortable. They have everything that vacationers may need. You can get into your room by elevator, in total there are two of them in the building. All apartments are located on both sides of the narrow corridor and have a standard layout. It's nice that the rooms have windows. As you know, in Vietnam there are often cases when the rooms have absolutely no access to sunlight. In this sense, Thang Long Nha Trang 3 * will not bring unpleasant surprises, since absolutely all of its apartments are equipped with windows.

Dining at the hotel

Thang Long Nha Trang 3 * has a restaurant where breakfast is served in the morning. Every day, guests can see local and international dishes at the buffet. For breakfast, usually served the usual set of food: rolls, coffee, pastries, meat, sausages, eggs, bacon, scrambled eggs, soups, fruits, cereals and much more. Throughout the day, guests can order food from the restaurant's menu.

In the immediate vicinity of the hotel there is a sufficient number of different restaurants and cafes where you can always eat. As a rule, tourists prefer to try food in several places, then to give preference to one of the institutions.

Beach vacation

Thang Long Nha Trang Hotel (Nha Trang) is a ten minute walk from the coast. The insignificant distance from the beach does not bother at all. The hotel does not have its own plot on the coast, so guests have the opportunity to visit the municipal coast, equipped with sun loungers and umbrellas. Local Attractions

If you are planning a trip to Nha Trang, then you should think about what you would like to see in advance. Vietnam is a stunningly interesting country, the sea and the beach are far from all its attractions.

The most famous temples in the Nha Trang area are Po Nagar towers. It is hard to imagine that these buildings are already more than a thousand years old. The towers were once part of a single temple complex built during the kingdom of Champa. The Vietnamese believe that this amazing place is endowed with great spiritual strength. That is why Buddhist ceremonies are held here.



Tourists come to Nha Trang not only for a beach holiday, here you can also take healthy baths and mud. In the vicinity of the resort is the famous mud bath Tzap Ba. The wellness center has a very beautiful area, surrounded by flowers. Here visitors can bathe in a hot pool with mineral water, take a mud bath.

Not only adults, but also children will be interested in visiting the Institute of Oceanography. Under its roof there is also a research center, an aquarium and a museum. The institute was opened in 1922 to study the South China Sea. Over the years, it accumulated a lot of marine items, which have constituted the exposition of the museum, which has been hosting guests since 2005.

One of the most visited places in Nha Trang can be considered the Long Sean Pagoda. This place is considered one of the most beautiful, in addition, the pagoda has the status of the main Buddhist temple throughout the province of Khan-Hoa.



Tourists will also be interested in visiting Monkey Island, located twenty kilometers from Nha Trang. Currently, about 1,500 monkeys live here in natural conditions. Animals show incredible friendliness to guests, but sometimes they can play pranks by stealing a panama hat or glasses from their heads.

Many hotels have been built here, among which you can find both expensive and more budgetary establishments. A bustling and cheerful city with beautiful beaches and clear sea is one of the best places for a beach holiday in Southeast Asia.
