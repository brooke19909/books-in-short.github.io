Philately is a hobby that teaches

Almost immediately after the advent of postage stamps, the first enthusiasts and devotees of a tiny strip of paper appeared. Philately as a way of life

Collecting stamps and interest in this hobby poses collectors some requirements. Philatelic knowledge is not only knowledge of the price list or catalog, but also history, as well as the development of philatelic terminology. Philately is not only a pleasure, but also a great benefit for every collector. A postage stamp is an important educational and cultural factor from which you can learn to know the life, history and changes taking place in the homeland and in other countries. An artistically executed postage stamp is a symbol of the country.

Philatelists are unique people, in a sense they can be called the keepers of history. And their hobby is more than just collecting stamps. This is a way of life. To gain insight into what is painted on postage signs, philatelists previously spent a lot of time in libraries and reading rooms, today they successfully work on the Internet: they look for information about the postage signs themselves and what is depicted on them. Yes, this lesson is tiring, time-consuming, but extremely important, because that's how history is learned.





What about collectibles

Absolutely all postal signs are collected by philatelists. Stamps, postcards, forms, date forms, envelopes and postcards with a printed return address, telegrams, draft stamps, their samples and samples, postal stamps and postmarks. Philately is a hobby for collecting almost everything related to the work of post offices.

At the same time, collectors often encounter fakes. Indeed, modern printing methods make it possible to almost perfectly fake characters and letterheads. Given this, philatelists have their own experts who scientifically study and issue certificates or guarantees of authenticity. Today, every self-respecting philatelist will not buy a brand without a guarantee or certificate.
