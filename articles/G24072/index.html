Partenit. Reviews about the rest, or Another Crimean fairy tale

In the picturesque Partenit Valley, at the foot of Ayu-Dag Mountain, which majestically rises on the southern coast of Crimea, there is a resort town of Partenit type. Guest reviews confirm that this resort is another Crimean fairy tale that you want to come back from year to year. Its name comes from the Greek word "parthenos", which means "virgin" in translation. Of course, it has few characteristics that, by definition, will fit the word "virgin", except that the cleanest air, beautiful mountain vegetation and a unique microclimate may well be on a par with the definition of "virgin nature."



Partenit separates Big Yalta from Big Alushta. On three sides it is protected by mountains, so it is always calm and calm here. This plays an important role in choosing a place of rest such as the village of Partenit. Reviews of your stay at this resort show that the memories remain the most pleasant: walks in the picturesque area, all kinds of interesting excursions, swimming in the clear sea and relaxing on the magnificent beaches.

Before proceeding to the description of the modern village, I would like to mention its roots. The history of this settlement is rooted in the distant past. Thanks to the discovery of the Early Paleotic tools, it can be concluded that people in this territory have lived since the Stone Age.



Modern Partenit is a famous resort in which the rest is considered quite prestigious. There are several private pensions and a number of large health resorts. Resting in the village of Partenit, the confirmation of this is confirmation, you can not just relax, but also improve your health and gain strength. Modern pensions are located directly on the seashore. Sanatoria have their own comfortable and clean beaches. Among the most popular can be noted the sanatorium "Crimea", on the territory of which the dolphinarium works throughout the year. It should be noted cozy and modern holiday home "Flamingo", which is a three-story building with bright and spacious rooms. You can also stay in the Aivazovskoye rest house, which is located between the rocky cape Plaka and Bear Mountain. Of course, prices play a huge role in choosing a place to stay. The private sector can offer more economical accommodation.

When relaxing in Partenit, you should keep in mind the places that are recommended to visit. So, between the capes of Plaka and Tepeler lies the most beautiful park of Aivazovsky, whose area is 18 hectares. Here is an olive grove whose trees reach 200 years of age. Those who have already rested in the village of Partenit, reviews about this park leave only enthusiastic. There are several beautiful fountains on which sculptures are installed, and small ponds. The most visited is a musical fountain with a sculpture of Prometheus. Against the background of this attraction, almost all vacationers try to take a picture.



Holidays in Partenit will be incomplete without comfortable beaches where you can enjoy the sun and sea bathing. There is a wide selection of water activities. You can ride a scooter, a jet ski, a boat, a banana, a fun boat, a catamaran. Under the guidance of an experienced instructor, you can dive under scuba diving, see the beauty of the seabed and its inhabitants. In addition, vacationers are offered a wide selection of various excursions on the boat.
