Use of info kiosks

Information kiosks (POS-terminals) are actively used all over the world by banks and other organizations to pay for goods and services, order tickets, etc. With their help, you can use the services without visiting the department. An information kiosk is not an ATM. You cannot withdraw funds through it.

The devices are conditionally divided into two parts: some work only with cards, the second also accept cash (Cash-in function). Most often, bills fall into a special converter. Subsequently, bank employees seize it, open it and conduct an inventory. After these procedures, funds are credited to the recipient's account. There are also devices of the second type, which immediately recognize banknotes and deposit money to the client in the account. They are more common.
