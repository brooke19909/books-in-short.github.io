Le Hammamet Hotel (Tunisia, Hammamet): description, service, reviews

In search of the perfect place for a beach holiday, many travelers come to Tunisia. Here is the resort town of Hammamet. A trip here can be compared to a trip to a fairy tale: snow-white sandy beaches, noisy parties at nightfall, bewitching landscapes and endless azure of waves. These and many other attractions annually attract thousands of tourists from all over the world.

In Tunisia, you can enjoy a stay on the beach, improve your health in spa complexes and get to know the history and heritage of this wonderful country. As for accommodation, tourists will definitely not notice the lack of hotels. Hotels offer both budget accommodation and comfortable accommodation, as in the Le Hammamet Hotel 4 stars.

An undoubted advantage of this hotel is its good location, so moving around the neighborhood becomes as convenient and simple as possible.

Hotel service

Hotel Le Hammamet Hotel 4 * is perfect even for demanding tourists, because on its territory there is absolutely everything for an unforgettable vacation:

  • The front desk is open 24/7.
  • Concierge services are available at any time.
  • The hotel has a car park, which can be used free of charge.

Le Hammamet Hotel is also frequently visited by business travelers. Comfortable conditions are also provided for them. They can always use the following services:

  • One bedroom suite. This room has one bedroom (connecting rooms). 3 people can comfortably stay here. The hotel offers these apartments with garden views.
  • Suite two bedrooms ROH. This accommodation is perfect for a company of tourists from 5 people (4 + 1). The room has 2 bedrooms.
  • Suite 3 bedrooms ROH. The largest room for 8 people (5 + 3). There are three bedrooms in this case.
  • Suite one bedroom with pool view. This is a one-bedroom room that overlooks the pool area.
