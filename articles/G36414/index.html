Samsung LCD T32E310EX: reviews

A universal solution that combines a monitor and a TV at the same time and is equipped with a high-quality speaker system is Samsung T32E310EX. Reviews about this TV, its technical specifications and specifications will be discussed later.

Positioning

From the point of view of technical characteristics, sizes and cost, the Samsung T32E310EX is most optimally used as a TV. Owner reviews on this once again focus attention. Moreover, it has a full-fledged analog receiver and an integrated first-generation DVB-T receiver. If there is a need to watch digital broadcasts in DVB-T2 format or satellite channels, then it is necessary to supplement the solution in question with an appropriate prefix.





Also, the presence of USB ports and an integrated decoder of digital audio and video allows you to play such files on this TV without additional hardware. In addition to this, a similar device can be connected to any modern system unit of a personal computer and used as a monitor. That is, this device is universal and allows you to solve a wide range of tasks. It is appropriate to use both at home and in the office.

Equipment

Typical, familiar and standard can be called a bundle in the Samsung T32E310EX. Reviews, on the one hand, indicate its adequacy. But on the other hand, the manufacturer could still worry about the new owner of such a product and expand this list with additional wires and cables.





Otherwise, after purchasing this device, its owner receives the following for his use:

  • The TV is a monitor.
  • A wire for organizing its power supply.
  • Guide for setting up, using and connecting.
  • Warranty card.
  • Remote control with a set of batteries.

If you use this solution to display a television image, then this configuration to solve a similar problem will be quite enough. To play audio and video files, such a list will also be sufficient. But to connect to the video output of a personal computer, you will need to additionally purchase a special interface cord. The same thing will have to be done when using any external receiver.

Screen Specifications

Of course, the Samsung T32E310EX LED TV cannot boast premium screen features. At the same time, reviews indicate that this is a middle class solution, and for this niche, the TV in question is simply an excellent screen. Its resolution corresponds to 1920 pixels in length and 1080 in width. That is, the picture in this case is already displayed in 1080p or FullHD format.





Of course, this television solution certainly does not reach the flagship 4K, but in comparison with similar entry-level products with HD-quality, the difference will also be noticeable. The response time of the matrix in this case is 5 ms. As a result, even the most dynamic scenes on such a TV will look bright and moving. The aspect ratio of the displayed image is 16 to 9. This is a typical value of this parameter, and in this regard the T32E310EX is no different from the most advanced TV models, as well as from more affordable solutions. Sound

As noted earlier, Samsung T32E310EX - R is equipped with sufficiently high-quality acoustics for a mid-range television solution. Reviews indicate a fairly high sound quality of the built-in speakers. There are 2 of them, and the power of each of them is 10 watts. This is quite enough for most users.





But there is also the opportunity to use in this situation an external sound system with improved sound quality and a large number of speakers. For these purposes, this solution has a whole set of familiar audio ports. But such a system will have to be bought separately.

Communication kit

An impressive list of interfaces boasts a Samsung t32e310ex LCD TV. Reviews highlight its versatility and sufficiency. The South Korean giant included in this list:

  • Normal antenna input. With its help, television broadcasts are received and output in analog and digital formats. In the latter case, only support for its first revision is implemented at the level of hardware resources. To receive DVB-T2, you will need a separate receiver.
  • 2 USB ports. You can connect a flash drive to them and view the multimedia files stored on it. Also, these ports can be used as a power system for the minicomputer installed in the HDMI port.
  • There are 2 more HDMI ports in this solution. According to the manufacturer‚Äôs idea, their main purpose is to connect to a computer. But this same port can be used for switching with external receivers or consoles. Another way to use this connector is to install a small PC.
  • There is one SCART and RCA port in this TV monitor. Their main purpose is to connect obsolete satellite receivers or even DVD players.
  • A set of audio ports allows you to turn this TV into a full-fledged home theater or karaoke center.




The only drawback in this case from the point of view of the communication set is the lack of an RJ-45 port. With its help, it would be possible to organize viewing photos or videos from a laptop or computer through a computer network.

Technical specifications

The only case color in which you can find the Samsung T32E310EX is black. Reviews, in turn, indicate the practicality of such a solution. Not so much dust, dirt and even possible damage are noticeable on the case with this color. High energy efficiency can boast of the solution in question. In standby mode, it consumes only 0.3 watts, and during operation, this parameter can increase to 69 watts. Reviews and price

At present, the Samsung T32E310EX TV is priced at 15,000 rubles. Reviews at the same time focus on full compliance with the cost and technical specifications. That is, the price in this case cannot be attributed to both advantages and disadvantages. Among the minuses of the product in question, one can highlight the lack of an RJ-45 port and SMART technology. In the first case, the absence of such a communication method is not so critical for the reason that it can be replaced by USB, which in this case is 2 pieces. The second technology would have an extremely negative impact on the cost of a TV monitor, and it can be found in more expensive products.





Regarding the pluses, the following can be noted:

  • High quality image output.
  • An impressive interface set that allows you to solve most problems.
  • Unification that allows you to turn this device not only into a TV, but also into a monitor or media player.

Summary

An excellent universal device of the middle class can rightfully be considered the Samsung T32E310EX. The reviews indicate that it can be used both as a monitor, and as a TV, and as a media player. At the same time, the cost is not so high, and the functionality and communication set can be attributed to the advantages.
