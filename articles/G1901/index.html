Sandhills Beach Resort SPA, Phan Thiet, Vietnam: travel pictures and reviews

If you want to relax in Vietnam and are looking for a small comfortable, cozy and quiet hotel on the beach, then Sandhills Beach Resort & SPA 4 * (Phan Thiet) may be a suitable option for you.





Location, general information, photo

Sandhills Beach Resort & SPA (Phan Thiet) is located on the Tien Tan beach, on the shores of the South China Sea. This place can be called secluded, so the hotel is perfect for a relaxing, quiet holiday. The distance to the center of Phan Thiet is five kilometers, and to Mui Ne - 15 kilometers. As for the road from the airport, according to tourists, it takes about four hours.

This hotel is small. Its housing stock is represented by 67 rooms located in a four-story main building, as well as several villas and bungalows. Guests are invited to stay in apartments of the following categories: standard, deluxe and luxury. Some of the rooms have magnificent sea views.

On the territory of the Sandhills Beach Resort there are two swimming pools with a jacuzzi, a children's pool, a sun terrace, a bar, a spa with a wide selection of procedures, a laundry, and a scooter rental service. The hotel also has a restaurant serving both local and European cuisine.

The hotel has its own 200-meter sandy beach, equipped with sun loungers and parasols.

Sea

Sandhills Beach Resort & SPA has its own sandy beach. Since the hotel is located on the beach, guests can walk to the sea from the living rooms in just a couple of minutes. Infrastructure

Despite the fact that Sandhills Beach Resort & SPA is a small hotel, there are two large swimming pools with a jacuzzi on its territory. One of them is located next to the residential building, and the other is opposite the restaurant. Near the pools there are sun terraces with a sufficient number of sunbeds. As for animation and discos, you will not find anything like this here. Therefore, this hotel is perfect for those who want to relax and unwind from the hectic everyday life in a busy city.
