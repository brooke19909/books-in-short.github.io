People can visit Moscow for different purposes. Some come here to work or study, others for treatment, and others for sightseeing. And of course, many visitors primarily try to find affordable and decent housing in the capital.

You can stay in Moscow at a hotel or choose a cheaper option - a hostel. There are simply a huge number of such mini-hotels in the capital. For example, the Vinaigrette Hostel can be a very good option for travelers. In Moscow, many tourists choose it for living.

What is the difference between a hostel and a hotel?

People usually rent a bed in such mini-hotels in a foreign city in order to save money. Hostels differ from hotels primarily in lower prices for accommodation. Even in hotels of this type located in the center of the metropolis, they usually don‚Äôt charge too much from guests. The price of accommodation in hostels is less than in hotels. But they are also inferior in terms of the convenience of living to real hotels. In this regard, the hostel differs from the hotel in the first place in that much more people occupy the rooms here. In ordinary hotels, rooms are designed for a maximum of 2-4 people. In hostels, guests in one room can be accommodated up to 6-8, and in very cheap ones - up to 12-20 or more. In most cases, rooms in these mini-hotels have bunk beds.

Hostels provide almost the same amenities as inexpensive hotels to their guests. Such establishments usually do not have their own restaurant or even just a dining room. However, guests have the opportunity to cook their own meals on their own specially equipped kitchens. The bathroom, smoking room and shower in hotels of this type are usually large and designed for all guests living on the floor. Many hostels provide their residents with additional services such as free WiFi, discounts for stays longer than a week, two or a month (you should definitely ask), free parking, etc.
