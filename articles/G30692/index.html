How to sew a jeans skirt easily?

Each girl has a favorite thing that has long served its purpose or has gone out of fashion, but which I do not want to throw away at all. So, we put aside many unnecessary things that just take up space in the house, and there is absolutely no benefit from them. But if you have old unnecessary jeans with torn knees or a shabby hem - this is exactly what you need, because later on we will consider how to sew a skirt from jeans. So everyone who has a pair of such pants at home, be sure to try to make yourself a new thing. It is not so difficult as it might seem at first glance.





How to sew a skirt from old jeans?

First, find this thing and iron it thoroughly. Secondly, decide on the length that you prefer to wear, and mark it on the fabric. Be sure to add two to three centimeters to the seam allowances and apply the cut line with a crayon or bar of soap. Cut off all the excess. Then spread the two inner seams and straighten the front and back panels to align the seams. There is a lot of information on how to sew a skirt of jeans, but most importantly, think about which style is right for you - your figure. Perhaps the narrower version emphasizes your hips or, conversely, does even more. All individually. It is necessary to take the cut off leg, to spread it along one of the seams and attach it to the triangular cutout under the fly on the canvas of the future skirt. Cut out the necessary piece to be sewn in, and bast it or pin it with pins. Sew carefully on the sewing machine. Next, turn the product inside out and cut off the unnecessary fabric.





Fabric processing

The basics of the question of how to sew a skirt of jeans, we have already disassembled. We turn to the transformation of the element of clothing. The hem of a denim skirt can be hemmed carefully and sewn with two even or zigzag lines with a colored thread. You can make a fringe with a width of one or two centimeters. To make the skirt look more interesting, you can decorate it with beadwork, for example. Or stitch a thin tape around the edges of the pockets front and back.





Sew a jeans skirt using other fabrics

For inserting between open legs, you can use not only the same material, but also a completely different one. It can be cotton with a beautiful print. It is exactly the same you need to attach and measure out the necessary piece, then stitch and trim from the inside out all the excess. But the edges of the cotton are best treated with an overlock or tuck and flash. You can also use this fabric to create ruffles that will need to be sewn to the bottom of the skirt. This option is suitable for both straight and narrowed models. Imagine and experiment, and you can create something extraordinary. A new thing from your beloved old one will serve you just as long and bring only positive emotions. I hope you figured out how to sew a skirt out of jeans, and easily create your own unique image that will emphasize your figure and, if necessary, will hide all the flaws. Good luck in sewing!
