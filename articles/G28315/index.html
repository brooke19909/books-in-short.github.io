Popular clubs in Kiev: where to spend the evening

Restaurants where you can relax, dance and relax are located both in the city center and in the sleeping areas. Luxury Holidays

For many years, among the nightclubs in Kiev, the rating has been headed by De Luxe.

This is an expensive prestigious institution located in the city center, on the street. Grushevsky. Foreigners, respectable businessmen constantly drop in here, "golden" youth and fashionable celebrities hang out here.

A red carpet leads to the entrance, but not everyone is allowed into the club: the guards conduct strict face control, it is imperative that the dress code is followed. Entrance is quite expensive.

The three-story institution offers entertainment for every taste: on the top floor - a disco, on the second - a relaxed lounge bar. The dance floor is one of the best in the capital, it hosts not only show programs and parties, but also full-fledged concerts by world-class stars. In some places, the audience is fascinated by PJ dancers. Bar racks and soft sofas are originally inscribed in the fashionable interior.

One of the oldest city clubs

In Kiev, there are clubs at almost every step, but not everyone can get into the Caribbean Club - they frighten off expensive entrance tickets, strict face control and prohibitive prices for bar products. However, fans of high-quality sound do not stop at nothing. Indeed, it is only in the concert hall of the Caribbean Club that the largest screen in the city is installed, on which musicians perform live. The highlight of the club is the weekly parties of jazz and Latin American music.





In the Art Club Closer in the Yurkovitsa district, electronic music is played all night, and on the huge dance floor, everyone can show what he can do.

Until midnight, the restaurant, which then turns into a disco, is De Fleur. First, guests are saturated with delicious Mediterranean and Asian cuisine, and then they dance to DJ sets, fashionable on the Cote d'Azur.

Mid-priced clubs

What are trying to attract the attention of the moody public Kiev clubs? Their managers use traditional tools:

  • make discounts on entry, products at the bar, etc .;
  • hold promotions, contests with good prizes;
  • invite popular musicians, artists and DJs.

The middle price category has clubs in the center of Kiev, and there are located closer to potential customers - in residential areas.

Visiting grandmother

One of the most sought after and original is the Sorry Grandma club. The complex consists of 3 floors, each is decorated in its own style, each has its own program and special music sounds. In total, the club seats up to a thousand people. The place is famous for excellent music, fashionable DJs and constant parties from modern radio stations.

Dancing until you drop

In Kiev, clubs are at every turn, and this creates high competition. Neighborhood with expensive institutions has somewhat reduced the level of services at Patipa, but on weekends there are always people who want to go to the huge dance floor under current DJ collections or retire to VIP zones.





Near the house

Those who live and work on the outskirts of Kiev often do not have time to get to the center to dance in the club. Therefore, institutions in sleeping areas are becoming more and more popular.

Student places

There are clubs in Kiev, but there is Saxon. It is located in the Goloseevsky district, students of Shevchenko University live nearby, so there is enough public. Despite the low cost of entry, popular DJs from Europe often perform at the club, and there is also a DJ school. The complex is decorated in a knightly style, there are a lot of forged and stone elements, wood, there is an impressive fireplace.

In the basement on Lukyanovka is the Greenwich Club. The place attracts students, schoolchildren, undemanding foreigners. About 600 people fit on the dance floor, music sounds in the style of the 80s. The small security, the almost complete absence of face control and affordable entry prices make this place desirable for everyone.

Well, and another pleasant institution is located in Troieschyna, this club is called - "On the district." Bonus for customers - up to 21-23 hours. Admission is free. The main entertainment in the club is dancing and watching football.





The Forsage Club is located in the Solomensky District, and it is famous for its disco-style discos. The bar is inexpensive, there are 2 dance floors, entry is inexpensive, and after 4 in the morning it is free. What else is needed for happiness for students who study nearby ?!

So, in Kiev there is where to have fun, relax, dance, meet and have fun. It remains to choose the appropriate option - and go!
