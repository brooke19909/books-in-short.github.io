The best hotels in Yakutsk: review and photo

In the east of Eastern Siberia, the city of Yakutsk is located - the capital of the Republic of Sakha (Yakutia). It is the largest of the cities in the north-east of Russia, unique in that it was built in permafrost.

Modern Yakutsk

The climate and weather conditions in this area are severe in each of the seasons of the year - in winter there are very severe and prolonged frosts, in the summer it gets very hot and rather dry - precipitation is not often observed here.



The industry of Yakutsk is represented by mining enterprises - deposits are being developed for the extraction of fuel and energy raw materials, diamonds, sand, and timber is being harvested in forests. The city has industrial enterprises, their main activity is the processing of minerals and wood, the manufacture of building materials. In Yakutsk, conditions have been created for the development of a successful business. Therefore, this Siberian city has developed business ties with many Russian and foreign regions.

His leadership pays much attention to the development of sports in the city - there are modern stadiums, pools and ice palaces. On their basis, significant competitions are held for the country, including international ones. Yakutsk gathers many guests for scientific and cultural events.

Hotels in Yakutsk are often in demand. There are a sufficient number of them in the city, each guest can choose living conditions that will be optimally convenient for him.

Hotel at the airport of Yakutsk

Hotels in Yakutsk are located in many of its areas. Hotel Liner occupies a special location: it is located next to the main transport artery of the city - the airport. This is the largest hotel in the city, it offers 140 rooms. All of them, regardless of room category - from apartments to economy class - have recently been refurbished and have a beautiful design. The conditions here are comfortable for the most demanding guests and meet all standards in their categories.



At the Liner Hotel, guests can use free Wi-Fi throughout its territory; the business center will offer fax services and print the necessary documents. For an additional fee, guests can arrange a transfer to any corner of Yakutsk. Guests can relax in the hotel's restaurant and karaoke bar.

The features of this hotel are a ban on smoking throughout its territory and the possibility of checking in with pets.

Yakutsk: hotels in the city center

In Yakutsk, its main attractions are in the center. Therefore, many popular hotels in Yakutsk are also located here. The most popular of them are the Sterkh, Lena hotels and the Tygyn Darkhan hotel.



There is a particular competition among the hotels in the city center, so each of them creates unique comfort and coziness for the guests: the rooms, regardless of their price category, are equipped with modern furniture and the necessary appliances and a TV, they have their own bathrooms with shower or bathtub, with free toiletries.

Hotels in Yakutsk, in its center, have their own parking lots.

All the listed hotels take care of the food of their guests. There are restaurants on the menu of which there are dishes of European and local, Yakut cuisine. All establishments offer a breakfast buffet, which is included in the price for accommodation. The Lena Hotel organizes banquets and meetings. A lot of entertainment awaits the guests of the Tygyn Darkhan hotel: there is a fitness center, sauna, massage room, indoor pool. This hotel, like the Lena Hotel, has its own beauty salon.

Options for budget accommodation in Yakutsk

Many young people and children’s teams often come to Yakutsk to participate in sports and cultural events. For their resettlement, budget hotels in Yakutsk are in demand.



Location in the city center, low cost of living and good conditions often attract guests to the hotels "Nord", "Northern Lights" and "Siberia Yakutsk". These hotels offer accommodation in rooms of different categories, but most of them are economy class. All hotels have small rooms, recently renovated and equipped with inexpensive, but quite comfortable furniture. Bathrooms with showers are located on the floor. Guests can cook their own meals in the shared kitchens, which have all the kitchen equipment and utensils. All of these facilities offer free Wi-Fi.

Yakutsk Hotels: Guest Reviews

Regardless of the price category, Yakut hotels deserve frequent positive reviews from guests. They distinguish the following advantages of the local hotels: clean and comfortable rooms, friendliness in service, variety of services provided. The main disadvantages in the institutions are the interruptions in the supply of water, both hot and cold, and low soundproofing in the rooms.
