Climate features

The biggest plus of the island of Cuba (if you consider the latter as a tourist destination) is that it is convenient for a beach holiday throughout the year. This means that, despite the geographical location, there is no sharply defined seasonality. The climate in Cuba is tropical, under the influence of the trade winds. The hottest month is July, when the thermometer reaches a 36-degree mark, and the coldest is January, when the air cools to + 20-21 degrees Celsius. One of the features of the local climate is its very high humidity. Which for tourists with high blood pressure can cause discomfort. However, due to the sea wind, it is tolerated quite easily. As for the water temperature, the sea does not cool below year-round +24 ° . So, dear tourists, you can buy a ticket to Varadero.
