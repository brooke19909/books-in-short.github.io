Welcome to Velegozh!



The resort, which will be discussed here, can rightfully be called a blessed corner of the earth. The sanatorium "Velegozh" is located at the intersection of three neighboring regions - Kaluga, Moscow and Tula, on the picturesque banks of the Oka. Century pine forest gives a special charm to the area. Vacationers who have ever visited the sanatorium "Velegozh" remember for a long time then the enchanting landscapes of the river valley, the grandeur of shaggy pines, the healing aroma of conifers infused with the sun. The sanatorium "Velegozh" was built in such a way that through the windows of their rooms the people staying here could at any moment enjoy the beauty of nature.

Accommodation and meals

To accommodate guests in the sanatorium, there are 2 five-story buildings equipped with elevators. In the first building there are 2 types of rooms - "standard" (1 and 2 categories) and "luxury". Room equipment of the first type (double): TV, combined bathroom, shower. Location - 5th floor. There are balconies. The suites, also double, are located on the 3rd and 4th floors. There are wardrobes in their corridors , in the first living room there is a couch (sofa and armchairs), a coffee table, satellite TV, and a refrigerator. Naturally, there is a balcony. The bedroom has a bedroom set, bedside tables. The power mode is standard, four times a day. However, for medical indications, a 5-time menu is also provided. In addition, meals can be ordered. Mandatory fresh fruits and vegetables, juices.

Range of services and treatments



Let's figure out how you can improve your health by visiting a sanatorium. The treatment profile here is quite wide and varied. There are departments for children and adults. By the way, the sanatorium "Velegozh" reviews from vacationers receives mostly positive: attentive competent medical staff of high qualification, tasty and plentiful food, clean comfortable rooms with good living conditions and regular service.

But back to the specialization. People come to Velegozh to treat diseases of the peripheral nervous system (radiculitis, plexitis) and recover from injuries. Those who have problems with the central nervous system - atherosclerosis of the vessels of the brain and spinal cord, asthenia and neurasthenia, concussion - will also find appropriate types of treatment and competent medical care in the sanatorium. The condition of those suffering from cardiovascular ailments and circulatory diseases, musculoskeletal, rheumatism and polyarthritis of different origin will improve. Here they successfully cope with diseases of the respiratory system, organs of vision, the endocrine system, including diabetes and obesity. As for children's health, Velegozh mainly specializes in the treatment of respiratory organs.

Rehabilitation programs



The medical and rehabilitation base of the sanatorium offers more than 60 items of various procedures, including medical and cosmetic from the SPA group. It offers vacationers open hydropathic with various types of showers and baths. For parents with children, the program "Mother and Child", if the child has problems with the upper respiratory tract. The Stop Chondrosis program is aimed at combating osteochondrosis. The program "Easy breathing" is recommended for those who suffer from problems with the respiratory system. For the treatment and prevention of neuropsychiatric problems, the Antistress program has been developed. The course "Losing Weight Together" is offered to overweight patients. In addition, various sporting events and excursions to cultural and historical places, of which there are more than 30 in the vicinity of the sanatorium, are held at the recreation and treatment base. There is a movie theater and a sports club on the territory of the resort. Thus, you can visit Velegozh not only for treatment, but also for active summer or winter recreation - for walks in the woods, skiing from the hills, fishing, etc.

Prices and features



According to vacationers who visited Velegozh (sanatorium), the prices here correspond to the level of treatment and service. They are determined by the promotions carried out by the institution, types of trips, categories of residential rooms, types of treatment, full or partial board, the presence of children. For example, a ticket without treatment for one person per day costs 710 rubles. With treatment - from 1690 rubles to 1490. In addition to accommodation, this also includes food. The sanatorium is designed for 350 places, reception and resettlement are carried out around the clock. Children are accepted at any age. Thus, Velegozh is a worthy resort for a decent vacation!
