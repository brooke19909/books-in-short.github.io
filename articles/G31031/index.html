Porto Palace Hotel 5 * (Greece, Thessaloniki): hotel description, services, reviews

One of the most interesting hotels in Greece is Porto Palace Hotel 5 *. It occupies an old building, built more than 100 years ago. Mostly people who want to get acquainted with the local culture go here, as the place is intended for this. However, you can talk about all this in a little more detail.





Location

Hotel Porto Palace Hotel 5 * is located in Thessaloniki. This is a wonderful historical city of Greece with a million population, the second largest, by the way. It was founded in 315 BC. It is here that the University of Aristotle is located, which is the largest university in the Balkans. Macedonia Airport and the largest seaport were also located there.

In Thessaloniki there are simply countless monuments of Byzantine and early Christian architecture. They were inscribed on the UNESCO World Heritage List. Here you can admire the Arc de Triomphe, the Basilica of St. Demetrius, the Church of St. Sophia, visit the Macedonian Museum of Modern Art, as well as get acquainted with dozens of other amazing places.

Well, the hotel itself is located near the new harbor. It can be reached on foot.





Services

The Porto Palace Hotel 5 * has everything that you might need for a comfortable stay - from free Wi-Fi to private parking.

Guests can use the concierge and babysitting services, luggage storage, a tour desk or book tickets at a designated point. For any questions you can contact the reception - he works here around the clock. So even an accelerated check-out (as well as check-in) becomes possible.

The hotel has a dry cleaning laundry service. Ironing service is available. There is an office with a photocopy and fax machine, working at the business center, also located in the hotel. The hotel also has a large conference room.

There is also a shared lounge, shuttle services (if guests do not want to rent cars) and facilities for people with disabilities. The hotel also has bridal suites and family rooms. Soundproofed apartments are also offered. They also provide rental cars and provide room service for drinks with food. By the way, VIP services are also offered here.

With communication in Porto Palace Hotel 5 * no one will have any problems. After all, the staff here is multilingual. Workers speak not only Greek. They speak English, German, French, Italian, Spanish and Russian.





What else is there in the hotel?

Porto Palace Hotel 5 * (Greece) is designed for cultural holidays. So there is practically no entertainment here, with the exception of the outdoor pool with terrace and a small SPA-salon with a jacuzzi and sauna.

But then, like in any other self-respecting hotel, there are places where you can eat and drink. Restaurant ZAYTINYA is open from 12:30 until 00:30. There is also a lobby bar and a summer cafe located on the roof of the hotel. It offers a wonderful view of the surroundings.

People who have been here assure us that they feed excellently. The choice is great. They offer fresh and fried vegetables, fresh crispy baguettes, anchovies and olives with olives. There are all kinds of sauces, excellent fish and several types of meat (turkey, chicken, lamb). Grilled dishes are also prepared. From sweets - pancakes, cakes, rolls, pastries. Very good fruits - plums, peaches, watermelons, melons, dates. The service is wonderful - there are no queues, and polite waiters in snow-white shirts during the meal bring the guests wine (pink, red and white) and bottled water. In general, a level worthy of five stars.
