Ball decoration "Bell" how to do?

Balloons are always associated with celebration and fun. Recently, it has become very fashionable to decorate the halls with a variety of figures from them. Everyone knows that the greatest number of different holidays takes place at school. In educational institutions, the main ones are September 1, the last bell and graduation. An excellent decoration of these events can be an original decoration made of "Bell" balls.





It can be made in the form of decorations attached to the wall, or a three-dimensional figure that hangs from the ceiling, and at the end of the evening will be released into the sky. It is known that a bell is best made from balls filled without using helium, in this state they will last longer. If your decoration is prepared as a surprise, people invited to the celebration will thank you for such a miracle for a long time.
