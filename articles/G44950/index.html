Fee and restrictions

There are a number of rules related to currency exchange transactions. Before exchanging dollars for rubles at Sberbank, you need to familiarize yourself with them:

  • When exchanging up to 15 thousand rubles, a passport is sufficient for identification. Above - you must additionally provide the TIN, citizenship, contacts (phone, email).
  • When exchanging banknotes of one country for currency, another commission is not taken. The bank is already laying it in the difference between the selling rate and the buying rate.
  • When exchanging banknotes of another state for its own currency, a commission fee of 5% is charged.
  • For the exchange or purchase of a damaged bill, Sberbank will take a 10% commission.
  • The total amount of exchange money should not exceed 1 million rubles per day, and the number of transfers should not exceed two million.
  • When exchanging more than 150 thousand rubles, a commission of 3-5% of the convertible amount is charged.
