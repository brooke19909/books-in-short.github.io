Ravindra (Pattaya, Thailand): description of rooms, service, reviews. Ravindra Beach Resort & Spa 4 *

From time to time, every person needs a rest. And many seek to spend at least a few days on one of the paradise beaches of an exotic country. Often, Thailand becomes the place for such a trip, because it is a hospitable country that receives tourists all year round. It has everything you need for an unforgettable vacation, including not only picturesque beaches, but also a unique culture, exotic cuisine and a completely different flavor.

Of course, there are a lot of beaches on the local coast, because the tourism infrastructure in the country is well developed. And many tourists choose the hotel "Ravindra" as a stopping place. Pattaya, the city where the hotel is located, is one of the busiest and most popular resorts in the country. Here, travelers can find entertainment for almost every taste, from beaches and museums to nightclubs.

Of course, before you book a room, you should ask for more information. For example, you need to find out the exact location of the Ravindra Beach Resort Spa 4 * hotel and get information about the territory and the local beach. What do tourists themselves say about living here? Is the hotel suitable for holidays with a child? Do guests really offer good living conditions?

Where to look for a hotel? Location Features





For many tourists, the primary factor is the location, because you want to relax in silence, while enjoying all the amenities of the modern world. So where is the Ravindra hotel complex located? Pattaya, namely in the southern part of the resort, very close to the world-famous beach called Jomtien, it is here that you can spend your vacation.

By the way, this area is really calm, as it is reserved for hotels and pensions. But in 15 minutes you can drive to the bustling, vibrant center of Pattaya, which is always full of tourists and entertainment. A public transport stop is nearby - many tourists prefer to refuse taxi services. The nearest Suvarnabhumi Airport is approximately 150 km away. Approximately the same distance to the colorful metropolis of Bangkok, where many guests go on excursions.

Brief information about the territory of the complex

The Ravindra Beach Resort Spa is quite large. It consists of several four- and five-story buildings, built in the colonial style, but also with traditional Thai decorations. A large green courtyard, snow-white buildings with red roofs and wooden balconies - this is where you can enjoy a well-deserved vacation.

By the way, the hotel opened up not so long ago. Tourists have been accepted here since 2008, but they have already managed to carry out several cosmetic repairs in the buildings. The guests speak well of the local atmosphere and atmosphere. The buildings are clean and beautiful, and the yard is literally buried in lush greenery. You can stroll, enjoying the tranquility, solitude and bright colors of exotic flowers.

Room categories for tourists

The hotel complex Ravindra Beach Resort Spa is quite large. It consists of 277 rooms. The number of rooms is as follows:

  • Spacious Superior Rooms are 42 square meters. The rooms are designed in an exquisite colonial style, and large windows let light into the room. There are rooms with two or one king size bed.
  • Large comfortable deluxe rooms. There are double, triple (one large and one single bed) and quadruple rooms (one king size bed and one bunk bed).
  • At your disposal are family apartments, which consist of a large bedroom and a living room with beautiful wooden furniture and a large dining table (84 square meters).
  • Travelers who prefer privacy can often choose a private villa. It consists of a large bedroom and living room with access to the terrace, where, by the way, there is a small private pool, completely hidden from the eyes of other guests.
  • For large families or companies, a villa with two separate bedrooms, a spacious lounge for relaxation and, again, a private pool and a terrace with four sun loungers is more suitable.

Hotel Ravindra Beach Resort Spa: room description





In their reviews, guests are enthusiastic about staying here. The rooms at the Ravindra Beach Resort Spa 4 * are incredibly beautiful and comfortable. There are more beds with orthopedic mattresses, antique wooden furniture, a work desk, terraces and balconies with comfortable chairs and, of course, bright Thai decorations, including multi-colored silk pillows and interesting paintings.

Despite the colonial style and the abundance of antiques, all rooms are equipped with modern technology. It offers powerful air conditioning, a large plasma TV panel (with satellite antenna connection, of course), a telephone, a minibar with a refrigerator, a convenient safe and high-speed Internet access. You can also use an electric kettle and a coffee maker, because it is so pleasant to start the day, enjoying a fragrant drink, sitting on the balcony and admiring the emerald green garden.

The bathroom also does not cause any complaints. It is quite spacious, with a large bath or shower, modern fixtures, a large mirror. Every day, maids deliver soft towels. Guests are provided with slippers, comfortable bathrobes, cosmetic and hygiene products. Each room has a hairdryer.





In reviews, former guests say that in the rooms of the Ravindra Beach Resort Spa Pattaya 4 * hotel is always clean and fresh. The maids come every day and take their work very seriously.

Nutrition: what can you count on?

What can you count on when staying at the Ravinda Beach Resort Spa? The hotel provides its customers with different meal plans - you can choose only breakfasts or pay full board. Guests are invited to the main Raveena restaurant, where generous buffets are served. The menu here is very good - they serve dishes from meat and fish, seafood, pastries, delicious desserts and lots of fresh and tasty fruits.

Another restaurant works right on the beach - here you can have lunch or even dinner, enjoying bright sunsets and delicious dishes. Guests also recommend the Mediterranean restaurant The Reef. There are several bars in the hotel where visitors are offered refreshing cocktails, local beer, incredibly delicious tea, strong drinks and light snacks. Of course, you can eat outside the hotel "Ravindra". Pattaya is a large resort city. There are hundreds of restaurants where you can eat, enjoying the delights of real Thai cuisine.

Holidays on the beach: amenities





What can guests expect at Ravindra Beach Resort Spa? The beach is located just 50 meters away. This is a private site, so that outsiders will not interfere with your vacation. Reviews say that the coast here is really beautiful, and the water is clean and calm (waves rise only in the rainy season).

You can use clean large towels, relax on sun loungers with elastic mattresses, lying with a chilled cocktail. The shore is clean - workers clean it several times a day. By the way, if you wish, you can visit one of the public beaches of Pattaya. There is certainly more entertainment there, as well as tourists.

Water entertainments and leisure of guests

Travelers who want to spend time more actively are unlikely to be disappointed. Near the beach there is a water sports center all year round. For a few baht, you can rent a non-motorized transport, in particular, canoe, catamaran or boat.

It also offers yacht and speedboat rides, banana and water skiing, parasailing. Anyone can start surfing or diving - you will have both equipment and experienced instructions at your disposal.

Hotel Ravindra Beach Resort Spa: service and additional services





In the hotel buildings there are large air-conditioned rooms for rest, and workers in the territory can be found at any time of the day. Laundry facilities are available here. There is Internet access and the ability to use office equipment in the local business center. There are 9 conference rooms on the territory with a total capacity of up to 700 people. Not only business events are organized here, but also very beautiful, romantic weddings.

The hotel can easily exchange currency. By the way, employees will call a doctor upon request, arrange a transfer or rent a limousine.

How do tourists spend time? Entertainment at the hotel complex





It is worth saying that guests prefer to spend time on the beach or in the city, exploring all the sights. By the way, the hotel has its own travel agency, where you can help pick up interesting tours of the most beautiful and entertaining places in the country.

In the large courtyard there are five pools of different sizes. Guests can use sun loungers and parasols, and a delicious cocktail can be prepared at the bar at any time of the day. It offers tourists a large library with a good collection of books. There is also a fitness center - you can do it yourself in the gym or sign up for one of the group workouts. And guests love to visit local massage rooms, because leaving the country without having tried on all the wonders of Thai massage is simply impossible.

Children's entertainment and facilities for small tourists





Children need rest no less than their parents, especially when it comes to recovery on the beach. What can the Ravindra Hotel offer? Pattaya is a large resort where youth companies, elderly travelers and families with children have a great time, because there are a lot of entertainments.

As for the hotel itself, then, of course, small tourists are provided with an appropriate level of comfort. For example, travelers can stay in a spacious three / four-bedded room or live in their own villa, where there is enough space for the games of the baby. The territory here is large and clean, and in the pool there is a specially separated section of small depth. Also, young guests are invited to a mini club with a large games room and an open-air playground. You can always leave your child under the supervision of a professional nanny.
