Types, characteristics and application of internal cameras

Today, more and more you can see cameras of internal video surveillance. Together with outdoor video surveillance cameras, they make up a complete security complex that allows you to remotely monitor what is happening. The equipment can be used both privately and publicly - to ensure the protection of public institutions and businesses.

Principle of operation

The functioning of the video surveillance system is based on reading the image in front of the matrix and its subsequent transmission to the DVR via cable or wireless connection. The information received from the camera is processed by the DVR, followed by output to a monitor and recording to removable media or internal memory.

When choosing a video surveillance system, they rely on several components, such as a DVR, camera, cables and monitor. Component specifications vary. The camera is selected based on the specific installation location - on the street or indoors. Depending on the latter, two types of cameras are distinguished: internal and external.

Application area

The main tasks of the internal cameras, in addition to monitoring, monitoring, protection and warning, are as follows:

  • Surveillance online. Cameras of this type are often found in state and public institutions, banking organizations, shops and other facilities that require constant visual monitoring of visitors.
  • Continuous shooting with the ability to view recorded files, store and archive them.
  • Monitoring and control of processes occurring in rooms with limited access.
  • Concealed video recording with the recording of specific facts, for example, violations of the work process, unwanted behavior or default.

Classification

Internal video surveillance cameras are divided into several types according to the method of data transfer, the shape of the case and the presence of additional sensors:

  • Dome cameras. Protected by a special dome, installed under the ceiling.
  • PTZ cameras. Models equipped with motion sensors.
  • Hidden cameras. Compact dimensions allow you to hide them from prying eyes.
  • Wireless cameras. They do not need cabling, which simplifies installation, but limits the range.

By form factor

Indoor dome cameras are best suited for recording indoor situations. Models are made in the form of a hemisphere, some are protected by a special "glass".

The original chassis models do not include brackets or mounts. Such cameras are purchased for specific tasks. High-strength housing protection allows you to place them on the street without much harm. Despite all the advantages, case cameras are rarely used for internal video surveillance due to their large dimensions.

Hidden video recording is carried out by means of miniature video cameras. Their square or oblong body is invisible and is attached using the supplied brackets. Installed only indoors. The compact dimensions of the internal cameras make them popular and allow you to hide the fact of their presence from the eyes of strangers.

By type of data transfer

They are classified into two categories - wired and wireless.

The performance of the first type of internal cameras depends on the availability of the necessary wiring. Most models are powered by twisted pair, although relatively recently used coaxial cable. Optical fiber is becoming increasingly popular due to the high reliability of data transmission and modernity.

Difficulties with pulling the cable are considered a disadvantage of wired internal cameras. When installing them, wall chipping is often required. The second drawback is the difficulty of masking the wired wiring. However, wired transmission has its advantages - availability and signal transmission range.

Wireless data transfer protocols use modern IP, Wi-Fi and Bluetooth cameras. Unlike wired, they do not need cables, but their work requires power. The signal range is significantly reduced due to the binding of the camera to the repeater and router. Broadcast termination occurs upon any failure.

Unlike wired counterparts, wireless models require ongoing user intervention.

Functional

Save on cabling allows the function of recording to a memory card, bypassing the transfer of data to the hard drive located in the receiving unit. Slots for memory cards are located on the case of internal cameras. Models with the ability to write files directly to micro SD are the best option for fulfilling specific goals.

A motion sensor is an important component of home surveillance cameras. They resort to it in rooms that for a long time remain empty. The number of responses depends on the number of movements in the frame. It is convenient when creating security systems at rarely used objects.

The capabilities of some cameras allow you to view broadcasts online through P2P or IP technology. In this way, the operability of motion sensors can be checked.

For cameras with color rendering, HD support is relevant. In places where quick recognition of a person is required or small details play a role, technology is in demand. The quality of the transmitted image depends on the resolution of the lens. Due to the high popularity of the HD format, many camcorder manufacturers are introducing QHD and UHD support into their products. The quality of the material being shot depends on the availability of these options.

The required number of cameras

The number of cameras installed varies depending on the objectives pursued. The choice of installation location and quantity of equipment is based at least on common sense. When equipping a private house with cameras, you should not place them in each room: as a rule, it is enough to install them in front of the entrance doors and windows.

To monitor the nanny, cameras are placed in rooms where children and hired personnel are located - this will avoid unnecessary expenses.

In the case of providing a business with a video surveillance system, the number of cameras is determined by the dimensions of the room and the objectives pursued. Production control requires a large number of cameras with high quality and clarity of shooting, and one or two cameras in the common areas and at the entrance are enough for the office.
