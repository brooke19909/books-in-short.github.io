Columns "Marshal": an overview of the best models, their comparison and reviews

The Marshall company has been producing all kinds of musical equipment for a very long time. The products of this brand can always be seen on stage during any concert. The market presents not only large equipment for musicians, but also a simpler one, designed for the ordinary consumer (headphones, speakers, portable speakers). It is about the Marshal speakers and portable speakers that we will cover in detail in today's review.





Description

So, we will immediately discuss three devices of the company, and of absolutely different types. We will consider them in detail, get acquainted with the sound quality and make a small comparison among ourselves. Let's start with the Marshal wireless speaker, then move on to the usual acoustics for the home and complete the review of the concert model, so to speak.

Marshall stockwell





The first model to be discussed is the Marshall Stockwell wireless speaker. In the line of acoustic systems, it belongs to the class of portable devices, which allows you to take it with you on the road, to work or walk, and enjoy your favorite music. Despite the fact that the column has slightly larger dimensions than similar devices from competitors, it can be safely called compact. She will not take up much space in the backpack.

Contents of delivery

Options Marshall Stockwell is quite simple. The device comes in a cardboard box, which shows the model itself and indicates all the main features and characteristics. Inside, in addition to the column, the user will find a charger, an adapter for a Euro outlet and an instruction booklet.





Appearance

The design of the Marshall Stockwell column is gorgeous. The corporate identity of the company is immediately traced - retro design. The speaker is even somewhat reminiscent of a miniature guitar combo, especially in the location of the controls.





The materials here are used very high quality. The side faces of the column are made of plastic, which is made for genuine leather. The back wall is genuine leather, which cannot but rejoice. The front mesh is not made of fabric, as it may seem, but of metal.

Sound

There are no complaints about the sound quality. The speaker can cope with any music without any problems, from classical to hard rock. Of course, it happens that on some compositions the high frequencies take over, but thanks to the bass and timbre controls, this problem quickly disappears.
