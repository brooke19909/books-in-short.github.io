Hotel "Kalimera", Gelendzhik: description, reviews. How to get to Gelendzhik

Calimera Hotel attracts tourists with its calm atmosphere and quality service. Gelendzhik will give you the gentle sea, as well as the hot sun.





Brief description of the hotel

Quite a popular accommodation option is the Kalimera Hotel. Gelendzhik has a huge number of hotels, but it is here that the most comfortable and relaxing atmosphere awaits you. In addition, this hotel provides services such as an outdoor pool, private parking area, wireless Internet and much more. The polite and friendly attitude of the staff is also worth noting.

Hotel "Kalimera" (Gelendzhik) offers accommodation in comfortable rooms with all necessary amenities. It is worth noting that they are modestly decorated, but this is what provides a feeling of home comfort. Daily maids carry out thorough cleaning to make you feel as comfortable as possible.

Great attention is paid to food in an institution such as the Kalimera Hotel. Gelendzhik boasts a large number of cafes and restaurants. But why go far if delicious dishes can be tasted without leaving the hotel. You will also have the opportunity to place an order directly to your room.





How to get to Gelendzhik

Summer is a time of sea relaxation and a bronze tan. At this time, many are wondering how to get to Gelendzhik. The most budget option is rail. But since there is no train station in the city, you will first have to come to Novorossiysk. Then you can take a regular bus or take a taxi. You can also order a transfer from the hotel staff. There is also the option to fly to Gelendzhik by plane. Arriving at the airport on the Thin Cape, go to the appointed place by taxi.





Hotel Rooms

Hotel "Kalimera 3 *" can offer its guests accommodation in comfortable rooms. The following categories of apartments may be offered to you:

  • Double "Standard" gives a feeling of comfort and coziness, despite its compact area. This is a one-room apartment in which you can stay on a large or a pair of one and a half beds. There is also a desk that can be used as a cosmetic.
  • Luxury apartments consist of two spacious rooms. The bedroom has a large bed and a dressing table. The living room is a sitting area equipped with upholstered furniture. Due to the fact that the room can accommodate up to 4 people, this option is often chosen for family vacations.




Facilities at the guest apartment

In each of the rooms of the resort hotel "Kalimera 3 *" you can use the following list of amenities:

  • combined bathroom equipped with a modern shower (there is also a complete set of bath and cosmetic accessories);
  • so that you do not get bored while in the room, a modern TV is provided, which broadcasts several dozen satellite channels;
  • so that your products stay fresh for as long as possible, store them in the refrigerator;
  • thanks to the presence of individual air conditioning, your room will be cool, even if the street is very hot.




Resort Infrastructure

The hotel complex "Kalimera" offers its guests to use the following list of services:

  • playground and special equipment for organizing barbecue;
  • outdoor pool with clean fresh water, which operates in the warm season;
  • a large garden in which there are many benches for relaxation in the shade of trees;
  • there is a terrace with lots of sun loungers;
  • Pets are allowed (this service must be agreed upon at the booking stage);
  • there is a billiard room with a bar and a TV for watching sports matches;
  • own access to a clean and well-kept beach;
  • in the restaurant you can taste the best dishes of traditional Russian cuisine, as well as order complex meals;
  • all guests who arrive at the hotel by car are provided with a parking space in a private secure parking;
  • shuttle services can be provided for an additional fee;
  • the reception is open around the clock so that you can contact the hotel staff with a request or comment;
  • at the tour desk you can book an informative familiarization tour;
  • for those who want to spend a holiday or a festive event at the hotel, a banquet hall is provided;
  • guests who arrive at the hotel to celebrate their wedding are offered a romantic room;
  • There is a terminal for payment of services in non-cash form.




Positive feedback about the Kalimera hotel (Gelendzhik)

Quite a lot of tourists choose this hotel for accommodation. This is due to both proximity to the sea and fairly affordable rates for accommodation. Thus, the following positive comments on this institution can be noted:

  • the food in the restaurant is just excellent, despite the fact that prices are much lower than in neighboring establishments;
  • due to its favorable location, you will absolutely not hear the noise that Gelendzhik is characterized by (it‚Äôs especially good to relax with children, since nothing will interfere with their timely sleep);
  • the rooms are very comfortable, which resembles a home;

Negative reviews

Of the negative comments about the Kalimera hotel, the following can be distinguished:

  • the hotel has constant interruptions in the supply of water (in summer it is a particularly acute problem, because you really want to swim after the beach or a long walk);
  • ventilation in the bathroom does not work well, and therefore it not only smells of damp, but also mold develops;
  • administrators behave unfriendly and even a little rude to guests (there is no desire to make requests or comments).

General impression

Such a variant of accommodation as Kalimera (3 * hotel) is quite popular. Gelendzhik is a resort city, and therefore accommodation prices here are sometimes sky-high. As for this hotel, the price is quite consistent with the quality of service. Here you are offered Accommodation in one of the rooms of a small cottage, where you will find a well-groomed shady area, decorated with greenery and decorative elements.

As for the rooms of the resort complex, here you will not find original design solutions. The rooms are decorated very simply (we can say that at home). But this can not be called a disadvantage. The situation in the rooms is very comfortable, and therefore it is very pleasant to be in them. Nevertheless, the impression can spoil the pronounced smell of dampness in the bathroom, as well as a large amount of mold. In addition, the frequent shutdown of water can become an unpleasant surprise, and therefore be prepared that you will have to pay for some inconvenience for cheap accommodation.

In any vacation, food plays a significant role. As for this institution, there is a wonderful cafe with traditional Russian cuisine. Here, for relatively little money, you can get a comprehensive meal, which will be much tastier and cheaper than in neighboring institutions. Nevertheless, you should be prepared for the fact that in the restaurant you will expect the atmosphere of an ordinary dining room, and not a luxurious restaurant setting.

One of the most significant shortcomings of the Kalimera hotel is its staff. Already during registration you will notice a cool and unfriendly attitude of the reception staff. This greatly complicates communication. In addition, in the process of living, any problems and questions may arise, the solution of which I would like to feel interest and attention from the staff.

We can say that the Kalimera hotel is a budget vacation option for unpretentious tourists.
