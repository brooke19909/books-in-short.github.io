The most expensive phones are high-tech luxury

Mobile phones have long become a familiar attribute for every modern person. They help us to always stay in touch with our loved ones, family, colleagues and partners.

Smartphones are multifunctional devices that allow you to check e-mail at the right time, go to the Internet and even build the graphs and tables necessary for a working presentation. Some models are designed for middle-class consumers. Other devices can only afford wealthy people. However, there are also such models whose value exceeds all conceivable limits. So, let's discuss the most expensive phones in the world.

Perhaps one of the first in this list can safely be called the "brainchild" of the famous and popular Apple. The device, called the iPhone 4 Diamond Rose Edition, is designed not so much for everyday use as for storage under bulletproof glass in a bank cell. After all, such a thing costs as much as 8,000,000 US dollars. The most expensive phones of other brands respectfully retreat to the side. The same pink-colored gems depict a bitten apple logo. On the front panel there is only one function button made of platinum. At the request of the client, it can be replaced with a large pink diamond of impeccable faceting of 8 carats.

It is also worth noting that even the most expensive phones in the world are not delivered to the buyer in such original and massive packaging, as is done in this case. Such an iPhone carefully and neatly fits into a box carved from a single piece of pink granite. The box weighs 7 kilograms.

Not everyone knows a company like GoldVish. Not surprisingly, this brand is known in narrow circles of consumers and is engaged in the supply of elite models of mobile phones and accessories. His last "brainchild" was published in three different versions. The GoldVish Le Million has become a true work of art.

The most expensive phones, perhaps, can not boast of such an original and exclusive design. The case of this device is made of crocodile leather, as well as pink, white or yellow gold. The client chooses the model variant on his own. All phones are manually encrusted with diamonds. The cost of such luxury is about 1.3 million US dollars.

Another leader in the production of high-end devices is Nokia. In combination with precious metals and stones, even the most simple and affordable models in their functions become exclusive. As a rule, this company does not produce the most expensive phones compared to the same iPhone, Blackberry and GoldVish. The cost of such models usually does not exceed 500 thousand US dollars. But their appearance can easily compete in the wealth of jewelry with other leaders. Platinum, expensive leather, gold, diamonds, diamonds, rubies and sapphires - all this can be found on the cases of devices manufactured under the Nokia brand.

In pursuit of fashion trends, it is always worthwhile to maintain sobriety of reason. Remember that even the most expensive phones hide the most ordinary functions. So is the appearance worth the money?
