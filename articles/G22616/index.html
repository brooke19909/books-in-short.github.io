How to find OKPO by TIN organization

Statistics codes (OKPO, OKVED, OKOPF, etc.) the newly created company receives upon registration. They have different purposes - they may be required when preparing reports, when preparing primary documentation, and so on. It is worth noting that in addition to its statistics codes, it may be necessary to find out the counterparty codes with which the company operates. How to find counterparty statistics codes? For this, it is not necessary to contact the Rosstat authorities or use the services of companies that will provide you with all the necessary information for a fee. OKPO code concept

Abbreviation of Enterprise stands for All-Russian Classifier of Enterprises and Organizations. This statistics code is considered the most important in the Rosstat database. His entrepreneur receives upon registration of the company, and he retains it until the termination of its activities. It can change with a change in the type of activity of the enterprise.

Initially, you can find the OKPO number from the extract of the USRLE or the USRIP.

The main purpose of the code is to simplify the search for an enterprise or individual entrepreneur in the database of the Unified State Register of Legal Entities, EGRIP and EGRPO and allow obtaining all the information about them. It also allows you to systematically conduct statistical accounting and reporting.

OKPO code structure

The OKPO classifier has two sections, each of which reflects the correct data on all business entities. The first section contains information about legal entities, their representative offices and branches, and the second - about individual entrepreneurs.





Each of these sections has its own subsections, reflecting the information:

  • about classification features;
  • about the name of objects;
  • about identity.

How to get OKPO number

To obtain the OKPO code, the following documents are required:

  • for individual entrepreneurs and limited liability companies - this is an extract from the register with all changes;
  • for joint-stock companies - the Charter, extract from the register, an extract from the register of shareholders of the company.

As a rule, after legal entities and individual entrepreneurs have passed the registration procedure, the Rosstat authorities issue an information letter that reflects statistics codes, including the OKPO code. If the company has not received the letter in hand, then it will be sent by mail to the place of registration with the tax service. It is recommended to save the received letter, as it may be needed in the future. For example, when opening an account, most banks require a letter indicating statistics codes.





However, it should be remembered that when changing the type of activity of an economic entity, the OKPO number also changes. OKPO of an individual entrepreneur

As well as enterprises and organizations, individual entrepreneurs are assigned the OKPO code. Initially, it is indicated in the EGRIP statement issued upon registration of the IP. Everything is like organizations. But there is still a difference - the IP code contains ten digits instead of eight.

How to find out the OKPO IP code? The easiest way is to request information from Rosstat. To do this, you need to fill out an application stating the passport details of the entrepreneur, the registration number of the individual entrepreneur and the TIN number. The request is processed by the statistics authority within five days.





How to find out OKPO by TIN, tax specialists can tell. To do this, you must also send an official request to which you will receive a response within five days.

OKPO code for TIN

How to find OKPO by TIN?

  1. Firstly, you can submit a request to the Tax Service at the place of registration. The request is processed within five days. Information on the code is issued either by hand or by letter.
  2. Secondly, you can contact the Rosstat authorities, which, like the Tax Service, will provide information within a five-day period.
  3. You can learn OKPO by TIN from local authorities. To do this, you need to find the legal address of the company through the tax service website by entering information about the TIN. And with the information found, you can contact the district administration.

OKPO code at legal address

You can find information about the OKPO number and knowing the legal address. Learn OKPO by PSRN

How to find OKPO by TIN, we understood, but if we only know information about the PSRN? This data is sufficient. The PSRN number is entered in the search line of the website of the Federal State Statistics Service, then the OKPO number is displayed on the screen.
