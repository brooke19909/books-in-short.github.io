Dessole Pyramisa Sahl Hasheesh 5 Hotel, Hurghada, Egypt



A vacation spent in Egypt is always an unforgettable experience and a good mood. It does not matter where exactly you decide to stay, because all Egyptian hotels offer a high level of service and comfort. If you decide to make your trip to Hurghada, then you can choose the beautiful hotel Dessole Pyramisa Sahl Hasheesh 5. Why is it so attractive and what can it offer its guests?

Dessole pyramisa sahl hasheesh is a luxury 5-star hotel located on the privileged sandy beach of Sahl Hasheesh, 14 km south of Hurghada Airport and 200 meters from Luxor. This truly magnificent hotel has 850 rooms, several apartments and villas, many restaurants and great places to relax, as well as first-class service and only modern amenities. Near the hotel is one of the most popular diving spots on the Red Sea. There is a very beautiful lagoon-shaped pool, which boasts not only clean water, but also a variety of bars.

Dessole pyramisa sahl hasheesh 5 hotel is by far one of the largest and most popular in Egypt, despite the fact that it was built recently, in 2007. Dessole pyramisa sahl hasheesh has a health club that offers only the most modern treatments. Local restaurants will delight you with delicious dishes of local cuisine, as well as Asian and Italian cuisine.
