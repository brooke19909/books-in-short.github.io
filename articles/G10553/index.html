The best hotels of Balashikha: addresses, description, services

In the suburbs there are many wonderful relatively small cities and towns. One of them is Balashikha. The city has a large number of interesting places and attractions. A large number of tourists come here annually. They stay in Balashikha hotels. We will introduce you to the best of them today.

The first information about this city dates back to the 16th century. But on the territory of Balashikha people lived in the Middle Ages, and much later. Today, more than two hundred thousand people live in the city. Industry and infrastructure are very well developed. Residents of the city, as well as visitors there is where to go in their free time and how to have fun.





Balashikha Hotels: Features

There are more than fifty hotels in Balashikha. Each of them has its own characteristics. But let's see what unites Balashikha hotels. Having analyzed a large amount of necessary information and reviews, we can distinguish the following features characterizing the hotels of the city:

  • the presence of comfortable, comfortable rooms;
  • reasonable prices for accommodation;
  • hotel and hotel staff are always conscientious in their duties;
  • cleanliness of the rooms;
  • high level of service;
  • a large number of additional services for residents.

Further we offer you information about the best hotels in the city.



Hotel East gate (Balashikha)

In the very center of the city you can rent comfortable rooms at affordable prices. Employees of the institution with great hospitality meet everyone who comes here. Having received comprehensive information, many hotel guests decide to rent one of the rooms here. What about the number of rooms? Each has TVs, work desks, comfortable beds and all the necessary items for living. The toilet rooms have hygiene kits. Wifi works. Hotel guests can eat at the Vitello Hotel, which is located on the ground floor of the building. Here you will be offered a wide variety of Mediterranean and European dishes. The hotel also has everything you need for business conferences and presentations. The institution is located at: Lenin Avenue, 25.



Country complex

Many residents of our capital love to spend their holidays in nature. There is a great place for this in Balashikha. Hotel "Areal" is an excellent choice for everyone who likes outdoor recreation, as well as comfort and convenience. There is a pond, beautiful conifers, a Japanese garden and much more. What about the rooms? Here you will be offered a variety of options:

  • Eifel Suite. The two-story room has several rooms, as well as a cozy fireplace.
  • Suite "Harz". It consists of three rooms: living room, bedroom, study. There are upholstered furniture, TV, air conditioning and much more.
  • Standard. The room has everything you need for living and recreation.
  • Cottage. It can accommodate several adults and children with great comfort. There is a parking near the house.

Hotel "Oreal" is located at: Moscow region, Noginsk district, the village of Novaya Kupavna, Sirenevaya street, 21s1.

Quiet and comfortable place

Some people like to stay in small hotels. Hotel "Demidov" in Balashikha is located in a quiet and comfortable place. The building in which it is located is an architectural monument. Hotel guests note in their reviews that they are very cozy and comfortable here. The rooms have air conditioning, TVs and more. If you wish, food will be delivered to your room. Guests can engage in fitness and other sports in the sports complex, which is located on several floors of the hotel.

Address of the hotel "Demidov": Sovetskaya street, 36.



Far from city noise

Not far from Balashikha surrounded by pines is another wonderful hotel - "Artiland". What are its advantages over other institutions? Let's list them:

  • great service;
  • spacious, comfortable rooms with all amenities;
  • A large number of opportunities for leisure: Russian bath, bowling, billiards and other entertainment;
  • secure parking;
  • beautiful views from the hotel windows;
  • delicious and varied breakfasts;
  • entertainment programs;
  • live music and more.

The address of the Artiland Hotel: Balashikha, 10 Novoe highway.

Apartments on Sitnikova

The hotel is located near the Novogireevo metro station. Within walking distance are catering establishments and grocery stores. The hotel rooms are clean and comfortable. Almost all of them have a balcony, as well as cable TV, beds, and a washing machine. You can cook in the equipped kitchen. Also nearby is a large number of catering establishments: bars, cafes, restaurants. In the many reviews left after staying at this hotel, you can read the following:

  • clean and comfortable rooms;
  • convenient location;
  • quiet and peaceful place;
  • spacious parking area;
  • low prices for accommodation;
  • hospitable and polite hotel owners.




Marian Hall

We are glad to present you another wonderful place to stay in Balashikha. The hotel complex "Marian Hall" offers its guests quality service and homely hospitality. In each room, guests can feel free from all problems. It offers guests: a sauna, a summer cafe, a jacuzzi and much more. The design of each room has a great rest and sincere conversations. Hotel workers are always ready to give comprehensive answers to all questions of interest.

The Marian Hall Hotel is located at the following address: Moscow Region, Balashikha, 36 A. Shchitnikova quarter.
