Hotel "Yekaterinburg-Central": guest reviews

The pearl of the Ural region - the city of Yekaterinburg - is a large scientific, administrative, educational, cultural center. Many industrial and transport facilities are located here, which attracts a large number of guests to the city, guided by business or tourist goals.

Where to stay in Yekaterinburg?

The city successfully operates a whole network of hotels. It is recommended that guests who do not want to endure high financial costs pay attention to placements with the status of "three stars", where you can get a high level of service at an affordable cost. The best in the services sector was recognized as the Yekaterinburg-Centralny Hotel.
