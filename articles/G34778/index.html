Many probably saw an advertisement in which Mobile TeleSystems offers to try the new Smart tariff. The operator declares that the main advantages of such a tariff plan are packages with free minutes and gigabytes. Such favorable conditions naturally attract subscribers. People are wondering how to switch to the Smart tariff on MTS. However, you should not rush to give an answer. The word "Smart" is not always understood as one tariff. The mobile operator has a whole line of tariff plans.

Starting tariff "Smart Mini"

For newcomers, as well as for those people who are not too active in using mobile services, there is an inexpensive tariff from the Smart line. Its name is Smart Mini. The main parameters of this tariff plan:

  1. A small packet of Internet traffic. It makes you think about how to switch to the MTS Smart Mini tariff, as it allows you to communicate on social networks, send text messages through special applications, and check mail. Sharing photos, listening to music and watching videos at this rate is not very convenient, because with this use of the Internet the traffic ends quickly.
  2. A small package of free minutes for making calls to all networks of the home region and MTS of Russia. For calls to MTS numbers in the home region, unlimited is set.
  3. SMS package. Its size allows you to send almost 5 messages each day.

This tariff plan is not available in all regions of our country. For this reason, some subscribers may not seek an answer to the question of how to switch to the MTS Smart Mini tariff. You can find out about the possibility of connecting in a specific region on the official website of the mobile operator.

Other tariffs of the Smart line

Each region has its own line of new Smart tariffs from MTS. How to switch to any of them? First you need to find out what tariffs from the line are available in the region. For example, in Moscow, subscribers can connect Smart Mini, Smart, Our Smart, Smart Top. Each subsequent tariff is distinguished by more significant packages of free minutes, SMS messages, Internet traffic. In Moscow, the main parameters of these tariff plans are as follows:

  • Smart Mini - 350 minutes, 350 SMS, 2 GB.
  • Smart - 550 minutes, 550 SMS, 5 GB.
  • Our Smart - 1500 minutes, 1500 SMS, 25 GB.
  • Smart Top - 3000 minutes, 3000 SMS, 20 GB.

Additionally, at each tariff, unlimited MTS numbers are provided.

Sometimes subscribers ask a question how to switch to the MTS Smart Plus tariff. Unfortunately, this is not possible now. The tariff plan has been archived and is now unavailable for new connections and transitions. Previously, it offered a small Internet package (5 GB in Moscow and the Moscow region), unlimited for MTS numbers, 1100 minutes for all networks and 1100 SMS messages.

The tariff archive of Moscow and the Moscow Region also contains Smart Zabugorishte. Now it is also unavailable for connections. When the tariff was valid, it attracted by favorable conditions of communication while in some foreign countries (Armenia, Czech Republic, Italy, Spain, etc.).

Introducing a team to switch to Smart tariffs

Using a team is another answer to the question of how to switch to the Smart tariff on MTS. The combination is always published on the official website of the operator. It is important to know that each tariff from the Smart line has its own team. For instance:

  • to activate the Smart tariff, dial * 111 * 1024 # and press the send call button;
  • to connect Our Smart you need to send a similar combination from a mobile device - * 111 * 1025 #;
  • the command to connect Smart Top is * 111 * 1026 #, etc.

On the tariff plans of the Smart line, you can still use one simple and convenient command - * 100 * 1 #. This combination is provided to obtain information about the remnants of SMS message packets and minutes. To find out about the amount of Internet traffic, it is recommended to go to your personal account on the official website of the mobile operator or in the application.
