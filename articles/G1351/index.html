Citymax Hotel Bur Dubai 3 *: reviews, photos, prices

Many tourists today choose the United Arab Emirates as their vacation destination , namely, the luxurious city of Dubai, which is famous for its wonderful infrastructure, soft sandy beaches and interesting cultural and historical attractions. Naturally, in the city there are many hotels of different sizes and categories. And often, tourists choose the Citymax Hotel Bur Dubai as a stopping place.

So where exactly is the hotel located? Is it easy to get here? What conditions in the rooms await travelers? Is it possible to come here with children? The answers to these questions are interesting to many people going on a trip.

Where is the hotel situated?

Citymax Hotel Bur Dubai is located in the very center of the historic district of the city - Bur Dubai. Nearby is the beach, the distance is only two kilometers, it can easily be overcome in 5-10 minutes. Only eight kilometers away is the international airport, so you will be taken to the hotel in just 20 minutes.





There is a transport interchange, public transport runs near the hotel, there is a taxi rank. From here you can easily get anywhere in the city. Also nearby are shops, cafes and even a shopping center.

Brief infrastructure description

The hotel complex Citymax Hotel Bur Dubai has a very small territory. Nevertheless, it is correctly framed. The hotel was opened in 2011. Almost every year small reconstructions are carried out here, so the complex meets all international standards.

In the center is a large eight-story building, built in a modern style. The territory is decorated with greenery and bright exotic plants. By the way, the pool is located on the roof, which offers breathtaking views of the surroundings.

Hotel Citymax Hotel Bur Dubai: photos of rooms and their description

What living conditions await the tourist? This is probably one of the most important issues for any traveler. The hotel complex Citymax Hotel Bur Dubai can be considered quite large - there are 691 rooms. There are rooms for smokers, non-smokers, as well as rooms with special equipment for the disabled. When checking in, be sure to tell which room you need - the staff will take into account your wishes.





All rooms are spacious and bright, with large windows and modern furniture. A large bed with an orthopedic mattress will please you, where you can relax after an eventful day. There is also a wardrobe, bedside tables, even a large desk (in some rooms). Naturally, there is also a complex of necessary household appliances, without which it is difficult to imagine the life of a modern person. In particular, there is an air conditioning system, as well as a TV on which you can watch the usual satellite channels. There is also a small safe that you can use for free. Upon request, a press will be delivered to your room. There is also a kettle, tea and coffee making facilities and utensils for such ceremonies.

There is a rather spacious and well-equipped bathroom with a toilet, sink, shower and wall mirror. Shampoos and soap are available in sufficient quantities (in small and convenient containers with dispensers). Each visitor is given a set of towels that change every day. There is also a hair dryer in the bathroom.

Rooms are cleaned, and towels and bedding are changed daily.

Meal plan for guests

Immediately it is worth saying that the Citymax Hotel Bur Dubai offers its guests to choose a meal plan - you can only order breakfast, two or three full meals a day. Every day you are waiting in the main restaurant of the hotel, where visitors are offered mainly traditional dishes of international cuisine. There is a sufficient selection of fish and meat dishes, salads, delicious desserts, hot snacks and fresh fruits, so everyone will find something to their liking.





In addition, the hotel has two restaurants - Indian and Chinese. Here you can also eat well at relatively low prices. There is a bar in the lobby, as well as another located on the rooftop poolside. The sports bar offers delicious and wholesome cocktails, light meals for tourists who care about their health and appearance. There is also a 24-hour bar serving drinks, cocktails and light snacks. Naturally, next to the hotel, as well as on the territory of the city itself, there are lots of cafes, restaurants and bars for every taste - everyone will find a place for their liking.

Beach vacation

As already mentioned, a beach called Jumeirah Beach is located just 2 kilometers from the hotel building. This is a public area, which, however, is clean, well-groomed and not too crowded. The beach, like the seabed, is covered with soft sand. There is a shallow area where both children and tourists who cannot swim can have a great time.





Umbrellas and sunbeds are laid out in even rows. For their rent you need to pay separately. There is also a towel rental point, which, again, is not included in the price. Of course, fans of outdoor activities will find a suitable activity. There are volleyball courts, as well as rental boats, canoes and catamarans. If desired, you can ride a boat, water ski, do parasailing or make a real trip along the coast with a guide. These entertainments are paid separately, but their cost is not too high.

Dubai, Citymax Hotel Bur Dubai: are there any conditions for children?

Of course, this question is asked by many parents who prefer to travel with their children. Immediately it is worth saying that in the territory of the Citymax Hotel Bur Dubai 3 * hotel complex there are not so many entertainments for small tourists. Of course, you will be provided with the necessary amenities. For example, the restaurant will offer delicious meals for your child and a comfortable highchair. If necessary, for a small fee, you can leave the baby with a professional nanny. And on the beach, your child will find something to do. Nevertheless, there are no animations, children's clubs and other entertainments that fans of luxury five-star hotels are used to.

Extra service for travelers convenience

Of course, guests are offered a number of additional services. For example, a currency exchange office is constantly working here. Also, if necessary, tourists can use dry cleaning and laundry services. Luggage storage is available, which is also very convenient.

There is a small parking lot for guests who travel on their own or rented vehicles. For the needs of tourists, a pretty good business center was arranged where you can use fax, computer, copier and other equipment. By the way, wireless Internet access is in the lobby - this service is free.





Entertainment for guests

Many travelers are interested in questions about whether there is an opportunity to have fun on the territory of a hotel. Citymax Hotel Bur Dubai is a three-star hotel, which is designed primarily for a relaxing holiday, so you should not count on an abundance of entertainment events.

The rooftop has a large pool where you can refresh yourself. Here, on the terrace, there are sun loungers and umbrellas. Guests can swim, sunbathe and simply relax while enjoying breathtaking views of the surroundings. By the way, there is a bar near the pool, where for a small fee you can enjoy wonderful exotic cocktails and refresh yourself with a cold drink.





For those travelers who prefer to always keep fit, there is a small gym with the necessary amount of equipment. It can be used free of charge at any convenient time.

What else can the hotel complex Citymax Hotel Bur Dubai 3 * offer? Dubai is a city that is perfect for excursions. After all, here travelers are waiting for both amazing historical monuments and creations that have become the result of technological progress. After all, do not forget that Dubai is one of the fastest growing cities in the world. And the hotel has a tour desk, where you can choose from a host of interesting tours around the city and the surrounding area.

What type of vacation is the hotel suitable for?

Hotel Citymax Hotel Bur Dubai 3 * is suitable for many categories of tourists. Since the beach is very close, it is most often the fans of a beach holiday who stop here. Here you can meet both families and older couples, and large youth companies. Fans of sightseeing vacations will not have to be bored either, because Dubai is a city that boasts a huge number of both ancient and modern attractions. And due to the presence of a well-designed business center, a hotel often organizes corporate vacations. People who arrived in the city for business purposes stop here.

Additional Information

Many tourists are interested in some additional questions about the hotel complex Citymax Hotel Bur Dubai 3 *. For example, do they accept bank cards here, because carrying large amounts of cash is far from always convenient. Of course, at the hotel you can pay in any way convenient for you, including plastic cards.

This hotel does not allow pets, so tourists who prefer traveling with pets will unfortunately have to find another place to stay in the picturesque city.
