Hotels of Tver: "Park Hotel 3 *". "Hotel Park", Tver: address, description, reviews

It is not in vain that Tver is very popular among tourists: here, every visitor to the city will find something that will be interesting, informative and memorable for a long time. And Tver is a wonderful place where you can enjoy the beauty of Russian nature: impassable mysterious forests, great and small rivers, picturesque lakes. But since not every traveler wants to settle far from civilization, many try to book in advance for themselves and their companions, if any, a room.

How to choose a hotel?

Choosing a hotel is quite a serious matter, since it is necessary to take into account some features. The choice is affected primarily by the purpose of arrival in the city. If you plan to visit attractions, then usually choose those hotels in Tver, which are located near objects of interest. This will significantly save time on trips to the necessary attractions. The cost of living and the comfort of the hotel are of no small importance, since even small household troubles can significantly spoil the impression of a holiday.

Tver hotels are quite diverse in terms of comfort and cost. This allows everyone to choose the most suitable accommodation option for him, taking into account all the requirements.

What can Tver offer tourists? The hotels presented below are among the most popular institutions for city guests:

  1. Guest House Hotel is a new establishment, opened only six months ago. There are rooms for every taste and budget, from the comfort category to the economy category. All rooms, regardless of the level of comfort, are decorated in a minimalist style. The cozy rooms are equipped with modern furniture, each room has a private bathroom. Booking conditions are quite flexible: you can stay here at least for several hours, at least for a day.
  2. The Seliger complex is located next to the local history museum, art gallery, philharmonic society, theaters, many large enterprises and other similar objects. Therefore, it is ideal for living both for those who come to solve business issues, as well as for tourists or travelers. The rooms are single and double superior and standard type. There is also a two-room suite and apartments.
  3. Crystal Palace. It is located away from the center, but then close to the church of A. Nevsky, the Church of Our Lady, the South Park, the railway station, Babaevskaya Grove, many different shops, cafes. Thanks to public transport, you can reach anywhere in the city or drive to the center. In the hotel, everything has been done so that every guest can have a good rest. Breakfast can be ordered in the room or in the restaurant, where highly qualified staff can prepare a dish of any complexity.

But nevertheless, the most popular hotels are located on the banks of the Volga. Among them is the Tver Park Hotel.

Location



The Park Hotel (Tver) has a very convenient location - it was built on the outskirts of the city, in a birch grove at the address: Moskovskoye sh., No. 14. This allows guests to enjoy the peace and beauty, secluded in one of the pristine corners of nature. But, if you want to return to the noisy cheerful city life, you can go to the city center, where the doors of bars, discos, cinemas are hospitably open.

Description of the institution



The hotel was built in 1964 - it was built at the arrival of F. Castro, so everything in the building was done at the highest level in order to impress the Cuban leader accordingly. A little over 10 years ago, the complex survived the reconstruction. Thanks to this, the organizers managed to perfectly combine modern technology of hospitality and the pristine beauty of the surrounding nature.

The hotel is relatively small, it has only 50 rooms, among which there are rooms for family, honeymooners, non-smokers, single travelers. The level of comfort that the Park Hotel (Tver) offers is from a suite to a standard room with everything you need for a quality stay.

In every room, in addition to a TV, telephone, minibar, there is a safe. In the bathroom - toiletries, hairdryer, towels. At the same time, more than 80 people can stay in a hotel. Animal lovers say that in the institution you can live with your four-legged friend.

Service



The Tver Park Hotel hotel complex is a great place to hold a seminar, trainings, a meeting with business partners: there is for this purpose a conference room with multimedia equipment, sound amplifying equipment, fax, wireless Wi-Fi Internet. If necessary, you can use the services of a secretary.

The facility also has a left-luggage office, a laundry room, and there is a place where shoe cleaning and ironing services will be provided. The reception is open around the clock, so you can check into a booked room at any convenient time. Those who arrive in their car can leave vehicles in a guarded free parking. Many visitors noted this advantage.

Fun and relaxation



In addition to a comfortable stay and friendly staff, they offer a lot of interesting entertainments that visitors really like. As the hotel is located on the Volga coast, first of all it is water entertainments. Funny noisy companies may prefer boat trips; romantic couples on yachts or boats are suitable for couples of any age. And if you want to admire the beauty of the city and the Volga coast from a height, the Park Hotel (Tver) offers tourists the opportunity to take a helicopter tour.

In addition, vacationers can play:

  1. Paintball.
  2. Badminton.
  3. Table tennis.
  4. Billiards.

A visit to the sauna will be the perfect end to such a busy day, where tired muscles can relax. But those who like to get up early and enjoy the beauty of dawn, watch how the first rays caress the earth, can go on early walks. And not just like that, but on organized fishing. Since ancient times, the Volga has been known for its rich fish stocks, and therefore no one is left without a catch. Hunters are not forgotten either - they also have something to offer.

Nutrition



The issue of food at the hotel was taken very seriously. So, breakfast can be ordered in the room or at the bar. Customers note that it is very convenient. The restaurant located in the Tver Park Hotel hotel waits for visitors from 13.00 and is open until one in the morning. Top chefs prepare dishes according to European and Russian recipes. The cozy atmosphere of the institution, delicious food, live music with interesting concert programs, frequent theme parties - all this is aimed at making the vacation easy and enjoyable.

In the restaurant "Birch Grove" (Tver) you can not only eat, but also celebrate any event, starting from his birthday and ending with a corporate evening. A special menu and a decent wine list have been compiled for special events in the institution.



For those who want to spend the evening, specially equipped gazebos with barbecue are openly open. As they write in the reviews, those who were lucky enough to have dinner in one of these arbors, they are the best in the city. If a hotel guest leaves for nature, fishing, hunting, he can be offered a packed lunch.

For children

The Park Hotel (Tver) is chosen not only by adults, but also by families with children. This is facilitated by the excellent ecological situation and the availability of everything necessary for an interesting rich vacation of the younger generation:

  1. Traveling with adults along the picturesque Volga shores.
  2. Fascinating walks in the old city.
  3. View performances in the children's theater.
  4. Lunches in children's cafes.
  5. Visiting amusement parks.
  6. Outdoor games in the birch grove in which the hotel is located.
  7. Games on the playgrounds.

These and many other entertainments not only give wonderful memories, but also guarantee a good sleep and a healthy appetite. Exciting board games and many satellite channels, including many children's ones, will help to spend time without boring.

How to get there

You can get to Tver by any transport - bus, train, your car. Since the distance from Moscow is not very large - less than 180 km, the road will not take much time. Those who get their own transport can easily find the Tver hotel complex: it is located on the outskirts of the city from the capital. If you have to overcome the road by train or bus, then you can get from the station for a very short period of time and, importantly, for a moderate fee.
