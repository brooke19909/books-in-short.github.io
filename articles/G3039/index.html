How to crochet in a circle? What do beginners need to know?

Many models of things and accessories are crocheted in a circle. These are various hats, bags, sweaters, napkins, socks and much more. It is this method that allows you to do things without tearing the thread in a spiral way. After the end of work, the product does not require additional stitching, because you get one big element.

How to crochet in a circle?



To begin, we collect 3 or 5 air loops. Then we connect them in a circle and continue knitting with crochets or according to the pattern that you have chosen. The main thing to observe in this case is the correct number of added columns or loops. If you add a small number of them, the product will look like a bowl and will not even out under any stretching. And if you knit too much, then the thing will be wavy, and just like in the first case, it will not change its shape for the better. This is especially important when creating rugs and napkins, as well as accessories that have a flat surface. Of course, there are crochet patterns that are initially made wavy: these are usually napkins, ruffles or hat fields.

How to crochet around a bag or hat?



To make a regular bag with a hook, you need to take into account not only the number of added loops, but also that the main rows of knitting of the product will be performed without increase, only on lifting loops. Usually, for such things, the base is tied with an increase at the ends of the element, and then the execution continues along a spiral path. It turns out a form that is peculiar only to bags or hats. Thanks to this method of knitting, things come out original and individual, because the size of the executed product can be precisely controlled, and changed if necessary.

Crochet for summer



Crochet patterns in a circle are widely used for knitting light clothing, as well as accessories for the summer season. Connected things have very good indicators in terms of quality and air conductivity, which is so necessary on a hot and stuffy sunny day. Everyone knows the models of a variety of hats, topics, T-shirts, skirts, shorts and other clothes for the beach season, made exactly by crocheting in a circle. The resulting openwork products are magnificent not only in the sense of external beauty, but also in terms of the ease of the thing. And the accessories associated with such clothes complement the whole image as a whole. Beach bags, slippers, sandals, belts, jewelry - all this can also be crocheted in a circle, which has been done by leading clothing designers for several decades. They combine this method with the simultaneous knitting of beads or beads in the product, from this the thing takes on a simply fabulous look.

How to crochet in a circle patterns with beads

When performing the columns, the method of adding beads and beads to the product is often used. This occurs during the first grasp of the thread: the bead is usually worn directly on the yarn. Then the next loop is captured and the column is knitted. The bead is firmly fixed and united with the product.

Here are the highlights of how to crochet in a circle. Experiment, and you will succeed!
