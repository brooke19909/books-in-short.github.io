Money tree from beads - a talisman and a symbol of financial well-being in your home!

Today, a variety of materials and the endless imagination of people allow you to create a variety of decorative trees. Original coins or notes, precious stones or crystals can be used to make original models. And a money tree made of beads or other improvised materials can also be very attractive. But it is believed that most of the effect will be from a tree with green leaves, under the pot for which three Chinese coins will lie in red paper (or tied with a red ribbon).



Naturally, gracefully and very beautifully looks the money tree made of beads, which was mentioned above. Such a product can be left at home, but it is also a wonderful gift, especially if made by yourself. The process of creating a symbolic plant is quite laborious and painstaking, but the result will be stunning, both in beauty and in effect. This kind of plant does not need such care as is necessary for fresh flowers, and it can be put in any place. But in order to get the desired effect, the tree needs to be located in the Feng Shui wealth zone - in the southeastern sector of the house or room. Experts advise to definitely put a room fountain next to it, or just an image of water. Such paraphernalia symbolizes watering, which will certainly benefit the monetary talisman.



Before making trees from beads, you should stock up with colored (green, yellow or other) material of different sizes, wire, and it is also advisable to take a few coins with holes or ears. The use of beads in different colors and sizes will add naturalness and visual volume. Ready-made coins for decor can be purchased at the store or you can drill holes yourself in real money. To beautifully and organically decorate the finished craft, you can use building plaster, shreds of fabric, wire of various sizes (for a tree trunk), gold powder and acrylic paints, as well as scissors, glue and brushes.


If you are a beginner and want to make a money tree from beads, the scheme of which is of interest, then you can create a plant of five branches. First, a coin is threaded onto the wire (so that it is centered). The ends of the wire are brought together, twisted two times, and then five beads are strung on two joined ends. After that, the ends are bred and seven beads of different sizes and colors are put on each. Five loops are looped at each end of the wire and brought back together, twisted and strung five beads. By this principle, make a twig with ten leaves. Only six branches are needed, which are combined in three pieces.



In the next step, twelve larger branches are made. The principle of weaving is the same, but it is only necessary to make fourteen leaves, and more coins can be added, respectively. The money tree made of beads looks interesting, if some leaves are completely exchanged for coins. Finished branches with coins are combined into three large branches of the same size.
