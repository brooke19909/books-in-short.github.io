Pension "Dolphin" in Adler: photos, description, reviews of tourists, address, how to get

Many tourists who prefer to spend their holidays in the resorts of the Krasnodar Territory choose the private pension "Dolphin" in Adler as a place to stop.

What conditions for recreation and accommodation are provided here? Is there any entertainment and how are they presented? About all this further.

general information

The official description of the Dolphin boarding house in Adler states that this complex was built back in 1974. To this day, the place for recreation pleases tourists from different regions of Russia with a good reception and good living conditions, despite the fact that it has a low category assigned in accordance with international standards - 2 stars.

The boarding house owes its modern look to the fact that renovations are regularly held on its territory and in the building, during which not only the appearance of the building is improved, but also its interior decoration and the conditions for rest are improved. During one of the last, carried out in 2013, a major overhaul of the main building was completed, as well as the interior of the rooms offered for tourists to relax.

Location

Pension "Dolphin" is located in the resort town - Adler. This settlement is a peculiar region of Sochi, its suburbs. In its vicinity there are a huge number of interesting places for entertainment, as well as attractions that are visited by travelers.

Many tourists in reviews of the Dolphin boarding house (Adler) note its advantageous location. First of all, they like the fact that it is located almost on the very shore of a warm and clear sea, in an area where there is picturesque nature.

As for the location of relatively large transport hubs, the nearest international airport is located at a distance of 5 km, which is very convenient for tourists from distant cities of Russia and from neighboring countries.

The exact address of the Dolphin boarding house is Adler, Lenin Street, 219. Next to it are places for recreation such as Frigate and Izvestia.

Rooms

The room fund of the Dolphin boarding house in Adler consists of 466 apartments. All of them are concentrated in a large and modern 15-story building, inside which a major overhaul was made. For the convenience of guests, there are five elevators inside it, with the help of which they have the ability to move between floors without much effort.

All rooms presented in the pension fund are divided into three categories, depending on the conditions provided for living: first, second and deluxe. In each of them, smoking is prohibited by the rules, but pets are allowed, subject to an additional charge.

As for the amenities available in the rooms, each of them offers an individual bathroom, which features modern sanitary ware, as well as bath accessories for each guest individually. In addition, all apartments have a small refrigerator in which snacks and drinks can be stored. A favorable temperature atmosphere is created using individual air conditioners, which are installed in each room separately.

First category rooms

Apartments of the first category, presented in the fund of the Dolphin boarding house in Adler, are divided into two groups: single and double, depending on how many vacationers can be accommodated in them.

All of them offer fairly comfortable living conditions with individual balconies, from which a magnificent view of the surrounding nature, mountains and sea surface opens.

As for furniture, in the rooms of this group offers a single or double bed (depending on the category). In the double apartments there is also a folding sofa, which can serve as an extra bed. In addition to beds, the apartment has small cupboards in which guests can store their personal belongings and clothes. There are also modest corners for relaxation, represented by a pair of armchairs and a table for tea.

Guests who choose to stay in a first category room can enjoy watching satellite TV.

Second category rooms

Apartments of the second category are also divided into two groups, depending on the possible number of tourists: single and double.

Among the furniture stock they offer a large or single bed, complete with which can be installed a clamshell of the European type. In addition to sleeping places, in the rooms of this group there are working areas represented by a desk and a chair. For small accessories of guests in the rooms there are cabinets, and for clothes - a wardrobe. In addition to all this, the apartment has a dressing table and a large mirror.

Of the additional equipment, guests of the second category rooms are offered free beach mats, a radio, and a TV.

Suite

On the photo of the rooms of the Dolphin boarding house in Adler of the luxury category, one can observe the decoration that allows tourists living in them to spend their time in extra comfort. In reviews of the rooms of this group, vacationers often mention the quality repair that is done in the rooms, as well as the presence of a large set of furniture that is simple and convenient to use. The room is divided into two spaces, one of which has a bedroom, and the other has a living area.

Among the furniture facilities in the luxury apartments, tourists are offered a large king size bed, fitted with an orthopedic mattress and high-quality linen, which is changed twice a week. In addition, there is a pair of bedside tables on which vacationers can leave small accessories. The bedroom also has a wardrobe for a lot of clothes, a dressing table and a mirror.

In the living room there is an excellent sitting area, represented by a set of upholstered furniture consisting of a huge sofa, a pair of armchairs and an ottoman, arranged around a glass coffee table. For a more fun pastime, the room has a large plasma TV connected to a satellite broadcasting system. The room also has a small corner for making hot drinks, which offers all the dishes for this, as well as electrical appliances.

Guests of this type of apartment have the opportunity to use an individual telephone, as well as access to the Internet.

Conditions for children

Pension "Dolphin" in Adler is often recommended for families, as on its territory and in the rooms all the conditions for tourists of different age categories, including children, are provided.

Here, if necessary, a crib can be installed in any room, however, such a service is provided on a paid basis and by prior reservation, as the number of beds of this type in Dolphin is limited. In addition, free and very comfortable high chairs are available for younger guests at the local catering establishment.

Many parents in their reviews about this place to stay talk about the fact that there is a small club where parents can send their kids for a pleasant pastime. Here, animators play with them, conduct entertainment programs, watch interesting children's programs and cartoons.

Nutrition

Guests of Adler, who rest on the territory of the Dolphin boarding house, positively evaluate the work of the main catering establishment. It is quite large in size and is designed for a large number of guests of this place. It is presented in the form of a modern dining room "Crystal", the menu of which offers delicious dishes of European, Russian and Caucasian cuisines. Parents who rested in this place with their children respond positively that special attention is paid to the smallest travelers - they have a separate menu that contains the most delicious and healthy dishes.

A considerable number of guests of the boarding house stay on its territory, not only for the purpose of relaxation and pleasant pastime, but also on a business visit. For this category of guests there are all conditions conducive to doing business. In particular, they are expressed by the presence of well-equipped workplaces in apartments of the second category and luxury, as well as the availability of free access to the Internet, thanks to which businessmen can always stay in touch not only with their relatives, but also with business partners.

In addition to all this, the attention of such a group of guests at Dolphin is offered a large and comfortable conference room, which can accommodate up to 80 people at a time. SPA

Fans of relaxing treatments often in their reviews of the Dolphin boarding house in Adler mention that there is a beautiful spa area on its territory that attracts a large number of tourists. As they say, visiting all the procedures offered in the list of services is a paid, but very enjoyable process, since all the actions are performed by professional masters of massage, cosmetology, as well as experienced bath attendants.

The SPA center in this place for relaxation is divided into three territories: an excellent sauna, massage room, as well as a beauty salon where they can perform a wide range of procedures.

Sport

Tourists who prefer to lead an active lifestyle can continue to do this, even while on vacation at the Dolphin boarding house in Adler. The reviews they left about this place say that there are all conditions for playing sports, moreover, the gym is presented in the best possible way.

So, for fans of active classes, there is a well-equipped gym, as well as several modern playgrounds for sports (volleyball, indoor soccer, table tennis, volleyball). Visiting any place reserved for games and training is paid, however, despite this, every day there are a large number of tourists.

How to get there

Pension "Dolphin" in Adler is a place that can be reached very easily. All tourists who arrive in the village by plane can travel by regular regular buses going towards Sochi. A great option for such a trip could be bus number 105.

In the event that the arrival point is a railway station, it is best to take buses No. 61, 123, 143, 58 or 50 to the Izvestia stop. Transportation by such flights will take no more than 10 minutes. The same applies to the case if travelers begin their movement along Adler from the bus station. From here it is also necessary to travel by all fixed-route taxis, as well as by buses that travel towards Sochi.

The cost of public transport in this direction is not more than 50 rubles, which is much cheaper than using the services of private carriers.
