Water park in Kislovodsk. Tourists reviews

Kislovodsk is a relatively small town with the glory of a health resort. Every year, in the warm season, a large number of vacationers from all regions of Russia come here with the desire to improve their health and have a fun vacation. But is a full summer vacation without bathing possible? Of course not, and the water park in Kislovodsk invites everyone to enjoy the warm waters and breathtaking attractions.

Where is the water entertainment center located?



There is a water park in the city, but its address and contacts are not so easy to find in regional newspapers ads. The thing is that the aquaclub with pools and slides is located in the local sanatorium. The only water park in Kislovodsk belongs to the ‚ÄúNarzanov Valley‚Äù health-improving complex. Since there are no similar entertainment centers in the city, the entrance to the water entertainment center is open not only for vacationers living in the sanatorium, but also for everyone.

Description of rides and related services



The water park in Kislovodsk can hardly be called big. It consists of only three pools and two adult slides. There are also fountains, decorative bridges, a recreation area. The water park is suitable for visiting with children, one of the pools is an adult, the other is small, designed for teenagers, and the third is very small, for the youngest guests of the complex. On land there is a comfortable relaxation area with a sufficient number of parasols and sun loungers. Also near the water park there is a summer cafe, where, comfortably sitting at a table, you can order drinks and snacks.

Despite the fact that there are not so many attractions in the water park, it has a full infrastructure. Parking, luggage storage, modern showers and changing rooms - all this can be used by guests of the complex absolutely free of charge.

Ticket price and water park location



The water center is open daily during the tourist season, from 10.00 to 19.00. Attention: the water park in Kislovodsk is located in the open air. With the onset of cold weather, the complex closes, and it is also worth refusing to visit the complex in cloudy, rainy weather. Entrance tickets for guests who do not live in the Dolina Narzanov sanatorium are sold without a time limit for the whole day. Their cost is about 400-500 rubles for an adult and 200-300 rubles for a child. Only the order of dishes and drinks from the menu in the cafe is paid separately. Where is the water park in Kislovodsk, any resident of the city will tell you. The sanatorium is owned by Russian Railways and is located 10 minutes walk from the railway station at the address: Uritsky street, 1.

Reviews of visitors about the water park

Most tourists who visited the water center in Kislovodsk during their vacation do not regret the time spent on this entertainment. The water park, of course, is more appropriate to call a pool with slides, they say, but those who do not like too extreme entertainment are likely to like it here. The water in the pools is clean and has a pleasant temperature, and the established attractions allow you to at least somehow diversify the rest.

Children really like this place, they are ready to frolic in water almost all day. The water park in Kislovodsk has positive reviews due to the reasonable cost of entrance tickets. Everyone can afford to spend several hundred rubles. At the same time, often water entertainment complexes, with only slightly more attractions, set prices for their services at two, or even three times, higher.
