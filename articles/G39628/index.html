Victory Resort Hotel 5 * (Turkey / Side / Colakli): hotel description, reviews and photos

One of the prominent resorts of hospitable Turkey is a cozy and homely Side, located on the Mediterranean coast. Beautiful landscapes, a number of historical attractions, a clear sea and an ecologically clean atmosphere attract a large number of tourists to these parts every year. There are a lot of places for settlement: there is not only for the most fastidious traveler, but also at different income levels.

Hotel Victory Resort Hotel, which is awarded five stars, offers its guests an amazing park area, friendly staff, comfortable accommodation and a developed entertainment infrastructure.

The first travelers crossed the threshold of this establishment in 2004. Since then, their number has been constantly growing. The last renovation was carried out in 2015. Green trimmed lawns, miniature palm trees and fragrant flower beds fill the open space of the hotel.

The airport is 50 km away, and the nearest spa center of Side is 8 km away.

Pets are not allowed. Smoking in rooms is strictly prohibited, for this there are specially designated areas. Rooms for people with disabilities allow you to move this category of people into rooms equipped with the necessary devices. The estimated time of accommodation is 14:00, while leaving the apartment is necessary until 12:00.





Accommodation

The room fund of the Victory Resort Hotel 5 * hotel (Turkey, Colakli, Side) includes 312 rooms, which are divided into the following categories:

  • Standard.
  • Family
  • Superior
  • Bunkbed.

They differ in their area and views from the window. However, they are united in the feeling of being at home. The interior is presented in a classic simple design without additional elaborate elements. The rooms are endowed with unprecedented warmth and coziness, which are realized thanks to the presence of wall paintings and sconces. The floors are carpeted. The color scheme is warm sand tones. The presence of a balcony with chairs and a table allows you to meet the sunrises and the first stars in the sky without leaving the apartment. Panoramic windows allow the rays of the sun to penetrate in the early morning, touching the sleeping faces of travelers.

The order is brought in daily, linen change - three times a week. Family rooms are two-room.





Arrangement

The furniture set includes the standard set necessary for living: soft beds, a desk, an armchair, a luggage stand and a spacious wardrobe. An individual split system maintains a favorable temperature. LCD TV allows you to watch your favorite TV shows in Russian. Using a telephone to contact relatives requires an additional charge. The minibar is replenished daily with free drinking water. The use of a safe is not included in the price. Wi-Fi is a paid pleasure.

The bathroom is finished with high-quality ceramic tiles, equipped with a spacious bath, a large mirror and a hairdryer. A set of towels complies with international standards. The presence of all necessary hygiene products makes you feel comfortable and carefree.

Supply system

According to the current All-Inclusive concept, the Victory Resort Hotel 5 * hotel complex (Side, Turkey) offers buffet meals. Each vacationer himself puts the dish he likes on a plate in the amount that he can overcome. The variety of food is surprising in its scale: meat, fish, salads, fresh vegetables and fruits are just a few that are offered to holidaymakers. Excellent chefs who have won a number of international awards, invest a piece of the soul in the preparation of their masterpieces. A diet menu and vegetarian meals are also available. The main restaurant Troya has both an indoor room and an outdoor terrace.





restaurants and bars

The presence of two restaurants working on a custom-made menu allows you to enjoy delicious dishes of Ottoman and Mexican cuisines. Once a week, a visit to each of them is included in the price, however, a preliminary reservation of the table and the execution of the dress code are required.

During the day, a light snack is available at the snack bar. In addition, there are bars by the pool, on the beach, a lobby bar and a disco bar.

All drinks of local production, including beer, vodka, wine and gin, as well as ice cream, are included in the All Inclusive concept. Extra payment is required for bottled, imported drinks and alcohol at a disco.





Beach

The hotel complex Victory Resort Hotel 5 * (Side, Cholakli) is located on the second coastline. In order to fulfill its main purpose of arrival - to swim in the waters of the Mediterranean - it is necessary to overcome the distance of 350 km. For those who are tired of walking under the scorching sun, you can use the shuttle that runs regularly on both sides.

The beach is sandy, the entrance to the water has a gradual deepening. This has a positive effect on the games of kids who are safe on the beach. In order to get an excellent dark tan, the coast is equipped with sun loungers with mattresses and awnings to protect from the sun. The towel dispenser is free of charge. The length of the beach is 200 meters.

Security and rescuers tirelessly keep order. Showers for flushing salts and changing cabins are included in the beach infrastructure.





Pastime by the sea

For those who do not like long tans, it is proposed to play volleyball on a specially equipped field. Next door is a mini-football field. This brings together participants to achieve a common goal - victory. Good mood, freshness, easy communication and new acquaintances are what players get in return.

Water sports activities include canoeing, windsurfing, water skiing, scooters and jet skis. Parachuting after a boat is extreme and unusual, everyone will remember such an activity forever and will present previously unexperienced emotions.

No less common in these parts is diving. To sink to the bottom of the Mediterranean Sea to get acquainted with the flora and fauna, to see the exclusive relief of the depths - this will delight everyone: both an experienced diver and a beginner.





Aquazone

One of the activities that are useful for the body is swimming, due to which many systems, muscles are strengthened, beautiful body shapes are acquired and mood is improved. The two main large-scale pools are filled with fresh water and do not heat up. Their areas are 283 and 450 square meters. One of them is equipped with two water slides of various modifications, the descent from which will give a lot of vivid emotions to every vacationer.

Pools are surrounded by sunbeds with mattresses and umbrellas to create shade. Having a cocktail at a nearby bar, you can enjoy plenty of sun and air baths. Towels are provided free of charge. Sports instructors conduct regular classes in water aerobics: light physical exercises will help maintain the body in good shape, and sometimes lose a couple of extra pounds.

In the neighborhood is a children's swimming area, where the kids are under the constant supervision of their parents.

Children's leisure

The Victory Resort Hotel 5 * hotel complex (Cholakli) is positioning itself as an excellent option for a family vacation. That is why parents prefer to take their child to rest here. The administration has seriously approached the issue of entertainment for kids. Throughout the day, a kids' club is delighted for guests aged 4 to 12 years. Under the supervision of educators and a fun team of animators, the time of the children turns into a real fairy tale: active games for the manifestation of dexterity and ingenuity, sports and a number of cooking classes delight every child. The opportunity to view children's cartoons.

However, fresh air is incredibly useful for kids. To enhance immunity, there are outdoor game complexes. Here, children develop physically, chasing each other and running along various ladders with obstacles. Slides, swings and carousels will also appeal to every child.

In order to diversify the uniform splashing in the pool, trampoline water complexes are installed on some days. There is no limit to the joy and enthusiasm of the children.

The restaurants provide high chairs and a children's menu. Lease of prams is paid upon.

What to do with an adult?

Victory Resort Hotel 5 * receives a positive plan for the excellent organization of leisure activities for adults. No guest can be bored in this entertainment-rich complex. The most popular among vacationers:

  • Gym.
  • Table tennis.
  • Hammam.
  • Sauna.
  • Billiards.

Access to the spa and massage are at an additional cost. An incredible sense of relaxation, possible thanks to a variety of wellness treatments, will relax the body and free the soul from possible anxieties.

Every evening in the amphitheater, fun shows are organized with the participation of talented artists, dancers and stuntmen. Good mood, a charge of vivacity and positive are provided to each guest.

Additional services

The presence of three conference rooms allows businessmen to conduct business meetings and conferences at the resort. Despite their busyness, they also need to relax, but there is nothing nicer to combine business with pleasure. These rooms are equipped with screens, projectors, microphones and other innovative equipment, the rental of which requires additional payment.

In order to explore local attractions with comfort, it is possible to rent cars for which a parking space is provided for free. Doctor's services are paid if necessary. The beauty salon is open to visitors, regardless of age and gender: a beautiful hairstyle or an original manicure will make you look excellent even in the resort.

There is an Internet cafe where you can contact relatives and friends. Transfer is paid additionally: only experienced drivers who offer their services in this hotel can deliver passengers on time.

In general, summing up all of the above, we can conclude that this hotel is definitely worth considering as an accommodation option when visiting Turkey (Side, Cholakli). Here, the administration and all staff take care of a comfortable pastime for each guest. Have a nice rest!
