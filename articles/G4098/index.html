Rheme Beach Hotel 4 * (Konakli, Turkey): description, service, reviews

Konakli is one of the famous regions in Turkey, which tourists from different cities of Russia and other neighboring countries prefer to visit for recreation. Often, operators offer them to visit the Rheme Beach 4 * hotel, motivating them to choose it in their favor with the fact that the price of accommodation is low and the service is top-notch.

general information

The very first guests settled in this hotel back in 1999. At this time, this place was very modern and technically well equipped. Throughout the history of the hotel complex, some improvements have been made on its territory, due to which at the moment the Rheme Beach Hotel 4 * (Konakli) remains very beautiful both inside and out and in demand among travelers from different regions of the world, including from Russia.





This place is always recommended for families who want to relax with their children, as well as couples who want to spend time together.

Location

Rheme Beach Hotel 4 * is located in Turkey, almost on the shores of the Mediterranean Sea, in the Konakli region, which is quite quiet and perfect for a secluded vacation. It is located almost in the central part of the city - three kilometers from it. The nearest large settlement is 12 km from the hotel, and the air traffic point is located quite far - 120 kilometers in the city of Antalya.

Rooms

The hotel fund consists of a small number of apartments - there are only 84 of them. Each room is represented by a relatively small area (28 sq. M.) And consists of only one room. The central place is occupied by a large bed designed for two guests, for things there is a large wardrobe, and near the bed there are two cabinets made of light wood. The interior has a dressing table with a small stool, in front of it there is a mirror in a beautiful frame with lighting.

Tourists who are in these apartments can rent a safe or a minibar for an additional fee. Upon payment, guests have the opportunity to use a telephone that is connected to an international communication channel. If necessary, everyone can use the climate equipment installed in the room, with which you can adjust the air temperature in the room. Tourists in their reviews note the benefit of such a solution, because thanks to it, guests do not languish from the heat in an exotic country.





If desired, guests can watch their favorite TV shows on the plasma screen, which is installed in the apartment. It is connected to a satellite broadcasting system, which provides for the presence of Russian-language channels.

Guests respond very positively to the overall picture of the interior, noting its classic style and ease of design. Light and beige shades are used here, which in combination can create an atmosphere of warmth, coziness and peace. A separate series of comments is addressed in the direction that each room has a private balcony, which offers stunning views of the urban surroundings, as well as the magnificent nature.

Conditions for children

Tourists living in a hotel with the whole family, in which there are kids, often respond very positively to the conditions provided to them. In their reviews of Rheme Beach Hotel 4 *, they usually note that for this category of guests comfortable beds are provided in the form of a playpen designed for ages from 4 to 12 years.





In an open area, children can spend free time in a small pool, which is equipped with everything necessary for them in compliance with all safety rules. There is also a children's playground with labyrinths, obstacles and everything else necessary for outdoor activities.

Nutrition

Guests of Rheme Beach Hotel 4 * (Konakli) in the comments comment on all the possibilities of food at the hotel. The main place in the catering system here is occupied by a large restaurant, where a daily buffet is served. All guests wishing to taste truly delicious dishes of Turkish cuisine can come here. Many comments say that here gourmets can get real gastronomic pleasure. In addition to Turkish cuisine, culinary creations can be prepared here in European as well as Mediterranean traditions.





Guests note that meals at Rheme Beach Hotel 4 * are also made in bars, of which there are three in the territory of this place for recreation. Very tasty and unusual snacks can be served here, there is also a large selection of drinks that are presented in the bar list. Guests of the hotel recommend to all travelers, staying here, to taste seafood snacks, which have excellent taste.

Sport

For lovers of outdoor activities in Rheme Beach Hotel 4 * (Konakli) there are several platforms where you can engage in sports. Some of them are in the open territory, and some are in the building of the building. On the street, guests can spend their time playing volleyball, as well as beach soccer. As for indoor areas, a huge sports hall is very popular here, where there are several dozen simulators for different parts of the body. You can visit it for free.

There are also plenty of sports activities on the beach line. Here you can pay for the services of an instructor who will help you learn the skills of surfing, jet skiing, skiing, and also teach diving. According to tourists, local instructors are real masters of their craft, many of them have sports categories, and some even speak Russian.

Spa

The large spa area located in its building is very popular among guests. It consists of several sections, one of which is a large thermal part. It includes a large hammam and sauna. Here guests can stay for free, and about the conditions of rest provided here, they often write in reviews of Rheme Beach Hotel 4 *. A lot of attention here is paid to each tourist by members of the team of parmeisters - they always offer their professional services.





Also, vacationers like to visit the salon, where various cosmetic and spa treatments are performed. All of them are paid, but, as customers say, they are provided at the highest level, and their result is simply amazing. Also, very often, travelers staying at this hotel choose a time to visit the massage room, which is also located in the spa.

Services

In the hotel, each guest has the right to use a number of additional services that are offered for a fee. The possibility of renting vehicles, which include cars and bicycles, is very popular among vacationers. Experienced travelers recommend using this offer in order to travel to explore local attractions and surroundings on your own transport.

Often, people living in the hotel take their things to the laundry and dry cleaning, where professional craftsmen give them a more fresh and new look. On the ground floor there is an excellent beauty salon, in which make-up artists, hairdressers, a cosmetologist and a manicurist work. If desired, leisure guests can exchange cash at a special point, as well as withdraw it through an ATM located on the ground floor of the building.





The Rheme Beach Hotel 4 * service also provides for the possibility of using the services of a doctor who is on duty at the hotel around the clock every day. They are paid and priced according to a specific price list.

Beach

The hotel often attracts a large flow of tourists not only for its excellent living conditions, but also for its proximity to the beach. She is across the road. In order to get to the sandy territory, you need to pass the promenade.

Guests of this hotel in Konakli note in their comments left as a result of the rest that this place is perfectly equipped for a great pastime. It is always clean, the rooms offer magnificent views of the sea and the surrounding nature. If desired, vacationers can be accommodated on the provided sun loungers under large umbrellas. There is a point for the delivery of towels and air mattresses, which can be taken free of charge to all guests of the Rheme Beach Hotel 4 * (Konakli).





The total length of the beach is 70 meters, there is also an additional area located on a wooden pier. Throughout the territory there are evenly distributed public toilets, several showers and changing rooms, which vacationers can use for free at any time.

Prices

The description of Rheme Beach Hotel 4 * often says that the cost of living in the rooms is small, which affects the total amount that tourists give for a tour to this place. On average, it is about 50-55 thousand rubles for two tourists. As a rule, the price includes not only a round-trip flight and accommodation for a week together, but also a transfer. They are delivered, as a rule, from the airport of Antalya to a holiday destination. In the reviews, travelers say that this service is carried out on a modern car, equipped with a good interior and air conditioning.

As in many other Konakli hotels, this room is non-smoking. For violation of such a requirement, a fine is prescribed in the amount established by Turkish law. Also, pets of any kind and size are strictly prohibited at the hotel. There are also no facilities for disabled people - there are no specially equipped rooms, like in many other hotels in Turkey. Sometimes tourists consider this circumstance from the negative side. However, in everything else, the place to stay receives quite decent comments regarding the service and the situation, as well as very high ratings on various sites (about 4.7 out of 5 points).
