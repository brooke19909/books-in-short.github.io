How to crochet tablecloths and napkins?

The desire to decorate your apartment, to create a cozy nest in it led to the fact that women took up needlework. Products made with love, and even with their own hands, create a unique design in the house. There are a lot of opportunities for such work. One of the attractive ways to create beautiful products is crocheting. Tablecloths, napkins, vases - all these wonderful crafts can be not only an ornament for your apartment, but also a great gift. And the time you spend on work will be a real rest for you.



If you are a beginner, then before you start crocheting tablecloths, master the basic elements on a smaller product.

More often than not, the first thing a beginner takes on is a napkin. Its dimensions can be very small - for example, a stand for a glass. The diameter of such a product does not exceed 8-10 cm.

You can, of course, start with knitting snowflakes, then its size will be even smaller. But this is in order to master the skills and gradually move on to more complex things.



It is easier to make a round product. Starting with air loops, you gradually tie the center, coming up with patterns and sizing along the process.

The craftswoman, who already owns the skills, can independently crochet the tablecloths, she does not need patterns for this. Patterns are born in her head, but are embodied in reality. But for beginner needlewomen it is better to use a ready-made scheme.

All products, in fact, are made in columns. The density of the knit can be adjusted using the yarn.

They also add delicacy to the whole product. Now knitting has become fashionable again, so many additional accessories have appeared on sale that can be useful in the process.



An interesting option is a combined solution. For example, cloth is placed in the center of the tablecloth, and then patterns or fringe are crocheted around the edge. You can make a product that will consist of segments interconnected by inserts from openwork crochet. Tablecloths in this case are more practical and original. Especially if you use a colored cloth, and tie it with white thread. The use of braid in combination with knitting elements looks beautiful. This type of needlework is convenient in that you can use any piece of fabric, decorating it with an openwork spider line.



A round tablecloth is good if it covers the same round table. But we often have ordinary rectangular furniture. It is better to use a product of the appropriate shape. To make a rectangular tablecloth, crochet air loops. Their number is determined by the length or width of the future finished canvas. You can also use a piece of fabric, which should be tied around the perimeter with patterns. A beautiful tablecloth is obtained if not pieces of cloth are used, but pieces of fabric. First, each shred is tied, creating a specific motive, and then connect them together. It turns out a real symphony dedicated to crocheting. Tablecloths made in this way allow you to speed up the whole process of work. Which is also sometimes very important, especially if it is urgent to make an unusual and beautiful gift.
