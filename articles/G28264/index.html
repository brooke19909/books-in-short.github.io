Deprivation of liberty

What else happened in the life of the thief in law Andrei (Skif)? It is known that he was detained in Moscow in 2005, on November 2, under article 163 h. 2. In 2006, on April 20, the Ramensky court under Art. 330 h. 2 of the Criminal Code sentenced him to 10 months (general regime). The term of imprisonment began in 2005, on November 5, and ended in 2006, on September 5. Andrei, after serving his term, was released from IK-3 (Tomsk) in 2006, September 1.

The funeral

The biography of Skif (thief in law) is full of various amazing moments. And yet this man died tragically. He was killed (shot) in 2009, May 22, on Volgogradsky Prospekt, 141, in Moscow.
