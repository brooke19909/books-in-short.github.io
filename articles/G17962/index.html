Sudak ... Vacationers reviews: you need to go!

Sudak ... Responses of vacationers clearly indicate that this place on the globe deserves to start packing bags almost immediately and set off to meet the new and unknown.

When choosing a destination for a vacation, it should be remembered that the nature of the Sudak Valley in Crimea, which is surrounded by mountains from almost all sides, is especially picturesque. By the way, the Northern Mountain Range is covered with a unique oak and beech forest. Here, surprisingly well, artificial pine forest belts have taken root.

Section 1. Pike perch. Guest reviews on the climate





The Black Sea and sunny weather ... Starting in July and ending in mid-October, the weather is wonderful in these places. According to statistics, in Sudak the least cloudy days a year are compared with other resort places of Crimea. Perhaps that is why the resort "Sudak" located in the area receives the most enthusiastic reviews.

The climate, of course, is arid, but without sharp temperature fluctuations. Such conditions are especially recommended for the recovery of adults and children with respiratory diseases. In the east of Sudak is the Kapselskaya Valley, characterized by a drier climate, and in the west - a place characterized by Mediterranean weather.

Section 2. Pike perch. Guest reviews of local attractions





Mount Ai-George, at the foot of which is located the monastery of St. George, built in the Middle Ages, usually attracts Orthodox pilgrims and local residents. However, sometimes vacationers rise to the source, which is located at the top of this mountain. Cool, slightly sweet water perfectly quenches thirst.

To the east of Sudak is Cape Alchak. This is an ancient coral reef that rises above the expanse of the Black Sea. At the bottom of the mountain there is a very interesting natural object - the Aeolian harp, which is a rock with a through hole. In these places, the parking of people of the Bronze Age was discovered. A treasure was also found here, in which the Bosporus coins of the 3rd century AD were stored. e.

The natural reserve Alchak-Kaya is recognized as a natural landmark. Tourists can follow the ecological path (length - 800 m), laid along the destroyed ancient road.

The Genoese fortress is of great interest to those who study medieval fortifications. There is evidence that the Khazars, Byzantines, Genoese, Polovtsy, Golden Horde and Turks owned the fortress - this fortification has a very rich history!
