Fashionable sweaters with knitting needles: diagrams, job description

Sweatshirts remain a relevant subject of women's wardrobe for many years. Judging by the classical definition, a jacket should be called clothing for the upper body with a fastener from the neck to the lower edge of the front part. However, in everyday life the term is used to describe a variety of products from summer light tops to long warm cardigans.



This article offers fashionable sweaters (knitting with a description), as well as patterns of details and patterns of patterns.

Jacquard coquette

Let's start with the most complex, but extremely interesting model. Fashionable women's sweaters, decorated with a colored jacquard pattern, are very popular. They are enjoyed by both teenage girls and adult women.



It should be said that such a project can be taken only if there is a skill in working with a raglan sleeve. Here, knitting starts from the neck and ends with the manufacture of elastic on the cuffs and along the bottom line.

However, unlike the traditional raglan, when new loops appear due to yarn or when knitting broaches strictly at four points, this model is expanded by uniformly adding loops along the entire row. Thanks to the developer of the scheme, fashionable sweaters with a jacquard yoke can be knitted without worrying about how the part expands. The pattern has already laid the formation of new loops.



You should pay attention to the fact that they appear only from broaches, because if you make a crochet, you get a hole. When knitting a broach, you must first twist it, so the canvas will remain as dense as possible.



How to work with schemes

The proposed drawings of patterns allow you to associate fashionable sweaters for women with a variety of sizes. It is enough to carefully retake the appropriate version of the picture. It is best to draw the outline of each part (in full, not half) on a separate sheet of thick paper. In this case, it is necessary to observe all proportions, scale.

In the process of knitting, it is desirable to apply the fabric to the pattern as often as possible in order to notice the error in time, if any. This applies to those parts that are even canvases: sleeves, back and front. The yoke is already fully designed in three versions: for small, medium and large sizes. True, her scheme does not include a strap connected by a garter stitch. Usually its width is from three to five centimeters.



It is important to remember that on the left side of the jacket there will be buttonholes. They are formed by knitting one, two or three loops together with the subsequent addition of the same number of elements in the next row.

There is a sequence according to which such fashionable sweaters are made. Photos, patterns and contours of parts may be different, but the knitting order is usually the same:

  1. Making coquette.
  2. Transfer loops to a thick thread or to several sets of circular knitting needles.
  3. Alternate knitting of sleeves, backs, shelves, front.
  4. Return to the neck: working with a collar.
  5. Button sewing, steaming, washing.

A few words about the collar

Similar fashion sweaters are made in accordance with the traditional sequence:

  1. Making back.
  2. Knitting shelves.
  3. Work on the sleeves.
  4. Stitching products, button fastening, heat treatment.

The button sewing strap is sewn at the same time as the shelves are knitted, and the collar is knitted after it is closed or reassembled on the auxiliary knitting needles of the shoulder loop. This design is extremely successful, because when the back seam is sewn (it will have to be exactly at the center of the back), you get a shawl collar.

How to tie a warm sweater

To get a really high-quality product, you need to follow this sequence in your work:

  1. Make a detail of the back.
  2. Tie shelves.
  3. Sew the resulting fabric along the shoulders and sides.
  4. Tie and sew pockets.
  5. Place buttons.
  6. Wash and steam the jacket.

Here you should also remember the slats (along the shelves and on the sleeves). They are knitted with a garter stitch simultaneously with the main fabrics. Pockets and collar made of garter stitch.

Knitting : fashion sweatshirts with hoods

By and large, such sweaters should be called cardigans. Their biggest advantage is that they are incredibly comfortable. So much so that they even cause serious addiction. Often, after meeting with such a product, girls for a long time forget about the existence of jackets, jackets and capes. However, this is relevant only for those who are not concerned with corporate requirements and the dress code.



The following materials are best suited for the manufacture of such practical products:

  • Alpaca.
  • Merino.
  • Sheep's wool.
  • Angora with wool or cotton.
  • Mohair. This yarn should be used carefully, since mohair is often used by grandmothers for their knitting projects, and the finished product may look old-fashioned.

A fashionable jacket with knitting needles with a hood fits as follows:

  1. Back.
  2. Two details are given.
  3. Sleeves.
  4. Pockets (if any).
  5. All parts are stitched together.
  6. Knit a hood, gaining loops along the neckline. You can also sew the part separately and then sew it.
  7. Spend the product processing with steam.

Hood: types and methods of manufacturing

In this model, the designer offers a simplified type of hood: it is an even canvas sewn on one side. That is, if you fold it in half, you get a rectangle.

There are more complex options in which the hood is knitted out of two parts, constantly applying to the pattern or guided by pre-made calculations. Such parts have a smooth bevel, if they are folded in half, you can see a rectangle with one rounded corner. This technique allows you to get a hood that looks better and more organically on a person.



However, most often no one puts hoods on knitted products over his head, so there is no need to complicate your task.

When knitting this part, you should pay attention to the location of the braids. They are so close to the edge that the resulting fabric does not require strapping.
