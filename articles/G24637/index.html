How to pay Rostelecom for a phone and how much?

Rostelecom is one of the largest organizations providing a full range of telecommunication services throughout Russia. Subscribers of the company are available: landline and mobile communications, Internet, interactive TV and other services.

How much to pay?

Before you pay Rostelecom for the phone, you should find out the amount of the required payment. Cellular subscribers are accustomed to independently monitor the balance and replenish the account as necessary. Until recently, Rostelecom delivered paper bills to home phone users, but now they also need to monitor the balance on their own.





To learn how much to pay for a telephone, Rostelecom offers fixed-line telephony subscribers in several ways:

  • By using an automatic system or operators toll-free number 8-800-100-08-00.
  • In your account on Rostelecom.
  • Using the services of one of the offices of the company.
  • Using the Sberbank Online system.

Payment Methods

Paying for your phone (Rostelecom) is available in several ways. The most popular of them:

  • using the Sberbank Online service;
  • in terminals;
  • through personal account;
  • in post offices;
  • at the company office.

Many payment methods allow you not to spend a lot of time deciding where to pay Rostelecom for a phone.

Payment through the online service of Sberbank

One of the most convenient ways to pay a variety of bills is to use the Internet banking system from Sberbank. It can both pay for the Rostelecom phone, and pay for services of many organizations, as well as make transfers and other payments.





Attention ! To use the services of Sberbank Online, you must have a card issued by this credit institution.

In order to pay for the phone in this way, you must perform the following steps:

  1. Go to Sberbank Online.
  2. In the menu, click on the item "Payments and transfers".
  3. Find the desired Rostelecom service in the drop-down list or by using the search.
  4. Enter a phone number and select a region.
  5. Check entered data. It will also provide information on the current balance of the personal account.
  6. Indicate the required payment amount.
  7. Confirm payment with a one-time code.

Payment to Rostelecom using the Internet service from Sberbank is instant. It should be borne in mind that if the connection was disconnected for non-payment, the reverse connection will take some time.

Important ! Correctly indicate the region where the service is provided, otherwise the balance replenishment may not occur.

Payments in terminals

Many payment terminals of various services are installed in all large and many small stores. Using the services of one of the devices, you can either pay Rostelecom for a phone or quickly replenish balances with other telecom operators.





Consider the procedure for making a payment through the terminal:

  1. Select the item "Payment for services".
  2. Find a telecom operator Rostelecom.
  3. Enter the phone number, if necessary, specify the region and deposit money.
  4. Confirm the payment and take the check.

Other payment methods

In fact, the methods of payment listed in the article do not limit their number. Rostelecom is constantly expanding its network of partners accepting payments in favor of the company. Already today, company subscribers can also use the following payment options:
