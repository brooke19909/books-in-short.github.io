Crepe paper flowers: step by step instructions with photos

Flowers and bouquets made of crepe paper have a number of practical advantages. Firstly, they are much more durable, do not fade and retain their shape and appearance for a long time. Secondly, they are not inferior in their beauty to compositions from living plants. How to make flowers from crepe colored paper? Currently, there are a large number of ways.





Single Petal Method

Incredibly beautiful floral compositions from crepe paper can be made using the method in which the flower is assembled in stages. One petal after another is planted on the base. In this case, each element must be given an individual shape before attaching to the stem.

To make one of these flowers, take corrugated (crepe) paper and floral wire to create stamens. Then cut out the required number of petals and leaves. It is necessary to form the petals and attach them to the stamens with a flower ribbon. Their shape can be very different. It depends on the type of flower.





Petal mount

To give the petal a concave shape, you need to pinch it between your thumb and forefinger. Then gently stretch from the center to the edges. In order to curl the petals, you can use a pencil. To fasten all the elements together, you need to use a floral ribbon and wire. Make it easy and simple.



The tape must first be wrapped twice around the stamen. Then, when adding the petals, run it through the base of each of them. Flowers made of crepe paper will keep their shape longer if they sheathe the stem with a dense layer from top to bottom, smearing invisible areas with glue. Especially those places where leaves are then attached.

Crepe Paper Giant Flowers

Mostly used floral compositions are cream, pink, apricot or pale olive shade. Although bright colorful colors can also look harmonious. It all depends on the occasion and theme of the holiday. Giant paper flowers made of crepe paper, made by hand, can be an ideal gift and an excellent decor tool (for example, for a wedding).

For composition you will need: paper-wrapped thick floristic wire, ribbon of green or saturated olive color, glue, round pencil. 5 small petals, 15 petals in the shape of a heart, 3 shapes for leaves should be cut out of paper. As a rule, it takes about 2 hours to make flowers of crepe paper with your own hands. But the cost of effort and time is worth it. The most interesting thing is that no matter how you try to make all the flowers the same, they are still unique and inimitable.

We make a stem, a bud and fasten the leaves

In order to make a thick and flexible stem, you need to fasten together three stem wires. This must be done with floral tape and glue. Good tip: keep a damp towel nearby to wipe off your sticky fingers and not to stain all the beauty. To make a flower bud, five small petals are used. First you need to tightly wrap one petal around the stem. When adding the remaining 4 elements, you also need to rotate the bar for even distribution.

Then, using the method of separate petals, attach 15 large parts in the shape of a heart. This process requires focus and can be very tedious. However, a small mistake due to the desire to do everything faster can spoil all the efforts made before this.
When adding the last petals, it is advisable to look at the flower from above. Only then can you see minor flaws. And place the remaining petals where they will look most organically. The leaves are attached to the stalk in a checkerboard pattern using adhesive tape, floral tape and glue. Before using the finished composition, you need to give it time to completely dry.


Fairy tale peony

Crepe paper peonies are flowers that can vary in shape and color. When creating a colorful fairy-tale plant, you will need such tools and materials as crepe paper of several colors, scissors, floral ribbon. Working with these materials is fun and fairly simple. The main advantage of crepe paper is its strength. In addition, it can be stretched, giving the desired shape. We present to your attention a master class: how to make flowers from crepe paper. Namely, huge multi-colored peonies from a fairy tale.

Stage master class

To make flowers from crepe paper, you need, firstly, lay out the sheets in layers and put each of them into an accordion. The thickness of the folds is about 3 centimeters. These folds should run along a natural, horizontal line. Secondly, each colored stack should be fastened with a thin wire or any other connecting element.

To give the flower depth, you need to reduce the length of each strip by about 1 centimeter. Using scissors, cut off the ends of a rounded and pointed shape. And do not worry about excessive accuracy. Irregularities will make the flower look even more natural. Next, you need to fix the clamps in the center of each pack.

How to make the composition natural?

After that, you need to, so to speak, shred the edges of all paper bundles with scissors, while leaving the middle untouched. And put them on top of each other. In this case, the bottom should be the largest in size sheets, and on top - the shortest. Starting from the middle, it is necessary to alternately raise the side lobes: first short and then ascending. Slowly and carefully, straighten all the strips from each color layer.

You may need to adjust some of the petals. But the flower will naturally take on its own unique form. Elegant flowers made of crepe paper, made by yourself, can be used as a gift or decor element for any room. Next, it's up to your imagination. You can attach stems to them, stick them to a real tree or bush or decorate a chandelier. In a word, you are able to come up with a ton of other original options.

Useful Tips

There are several useful recommendations that will be useful to any craftswoman:

  1. When cutting such paper, it is important to take into account its structure and make sure that the cut will pass along the fibers.
  2. The upper part of the flower is usually very heavy. Therefore, it is important to use additional support with the help of three leaves, through which the entire bud will be fastened to the stem.
  3. A flower ribbon will do more good and hold better if you periodically apply glue to the ends of the petals and leaves.




Flowers made of crepe paper, created with their own hands, will present a lot of positive emotions. Especially if you cross certain boundaries and conventions. How great it is to diversify a wedding photo shoot using unnaturally huge, but surprisingly cute and romantic roses or peonies.

With their help, you can build beautiful bouquets, garlands, wreaths and other original floral arrangements that can decorate any celebration. With home- made crepe paper flowers , you can enliven the festive table and add a little brightness to everyday gray everyday life.
