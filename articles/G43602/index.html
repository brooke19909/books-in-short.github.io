Everyone wants to go on vacation in the summer to a beautiful place where you can forget about your problems, concerns and just enjoy nature. Some people like to get away from the urgent chores with the help of fishing. But it is not in vain that quite a lot of vacationers choose the Irgiz River as a good place to relax. It is located near Samara and Saratov. It has beautiful views, rich in different types of fish. Holidays in this area will be remembered for picturesque landscapes. Therefore, everyone will want to go back.

The emergence of hydronym

This river was first mentioned under the name Irgiz. Hydronym is relevant to this day. It has been known about the watercourse since ancient times - since 921. Fishing on the river

Starting the fishing season is already in April. Fishing in the Samara region attracts many due to the good conditions. By the way, it is best to do this on weekdays. This is due to the fact that on weekends there are too many vacationers. However, if it is not possible to arrive during the working week, you should stop at the junction of Irgiz and Revyaki - on the Trekhrechye. Due to the action of Revyaki, the current in this place is optimal for fishing. It is best to come to Trekhrechye in autumn. The thing is that in the summer, vacationers scare prey here.

It is possible to catch asp and catfish. Irgiz - a river on which it is best to fish for jig baits. Dam

Big Irgiz is a place where you can relax and not be afraid for your safety. On the river there is an overflow dam, which is also called "Waterfall" or "Steps". Due to the convenience of relaxing on it in the summer, a lot of tourists gather here. In the Saratov region, this is the only place where you can enjoy plenty of bathing, beautiful views, splashes of water strips. You can also see a beautiful sight - how cars overcome the path, driving through the dam. You can watch a lot of videos that filmed such lovers to shorten their path.

How to get to the dam, being near the river itself? You can drive up both from the right banks and from the left. In the first case, you must observe the path near the village of Perekopnaya Luka. How to get there

As already mentioned, you can come here by car. First you need to get to Balakovo. You can do this by keeping your path along the Saratov-Samara highway. After reaching the village, you need to get to Pugachev. After the driver reaches the Dry Spur, he will see a special signpost. He needs to turn right. Then drive 2 km along an asphalt road. On the way there will be 2 bridges in the form of a dam. After overcoming the second, you need to drive into the field, there will be a fork in three roads. Need the one that is most knurled (extreme right). She will lead to the bank of the river. Irgiz (Balakovo district). Drive about 1 km. The second way to get here is by public transport. Buses run from Saratov to the following settlements with some frequency: Pugachev, Samara and Ivanteyevka.

Flora

Although aquatic vegetation is not at all pleased with the diversity of species, however, there are many rare representatives of the flora. For example, a small population of cattail broadleaf. Egg capsules, reeds and lilies - what could be more beautiful? They fascinate the eye and do not give up on the enjoyment of nature. If we consider the territory beyond the coastal zone, then it is worth mentioning the forest area. Willows and aspens are most common. They are easy to see every few meters, approaching the river. Dandelions, wormwood, mouse peas, tansy, plantain and many other plants delight with their abundance in the forest zone. The right bank of the Irgiz River (Saratov Region) is almost overgrown. The forest stretched for 150 meters. Among the main trees are oak, poplar and aspen. Herbs that easily tolerate the lack of sunlight or like shade predominate.
