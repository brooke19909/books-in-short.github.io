How can I use old wheels in the interior?

Each house has many old disks that have long been no longer used for their intended purpose. Do not rush to throw out these shiny circles. They can give a second life. For example, Elise Morin, a French artist, created an amazing Steel Sea installation using old discs. Of course, we are not talking about such masterpieces. However, from shiny surfaces, you can create original interior details. Let's look at how old disks can be used.





Useful Tips

Before you decide how to use old discs at home, you should study the technology of working with such material.

Experts recommend listening to the following tips:

  1. Sometimes you need to make a hole in the CD. If you carry out such an event with a nail, then the surface may crack. To avoid this, it is recommended to heat the nail, for example over a gas burner. Hot metal will pass through plastic freely. You will get a neat and even hole.
  2. It is best to cut discs with pruning shears. For extremely rebellious material, use a wood burning apparatus . Consider that there will be an extremely unpleasant smell. If you use scissors, then after work they will be unusable.
  3. Different drives have different levels of resistance. Blue discs are best cut. Yellow and green may peel off the rainbow film. Worst of all is working with white discs. Many of them are completely crumbling.
  4. If you want to give the disk its original shape, then use a pair of pliers to hold a shiny circle above the heat source (gas burner). To prevent it from burning, constantly move it. As soon as the plastic begins to melt, the material can be twisted, bent, stretched.
  5. And remember the most important rule. If you heat the disk, you should work in an open, well-ventilated place!




After reviewing the main recommendations, we will understand how you can use old disks.

Scale Details

If you have accumulated a lot of disks, then you can originally decorate the wall with them. In addition, in this case, you will save on wallpaper.

The following tips will help you decide how to use your old wheels in your interior:

  1. Shiny circles can be glued to the wall. If you lay them on top of each other, you can recreate the illusion of fish scales. To fix the disks to the surface, you can use liquid nails or superglue. Tip: it is better to glue such a masterpiece on the wallpaper. After all, someday you will want to remove this beauty. That wallpaper will protect your walls from damage.
  2. From discs you can create an original curtain. This decor perfectly delimits the room. Creating it is a snap. It is necessary to make small holes in the disks and connect them together with small metal rings.
  3. From this material you can create a wonderful decoration on the wall. After all, it has an ideal shape and size. Imagine and figure out how to use old CDs. For example, a regular woolen cloth can be glued onto a round surface. Attach bright, original feathers to it. From several circles you can create a whole photo gallery. To do this, attach photos of your household to discs and think about how to place the decor on the wall.




Decorative vase

When analyzing how old disks can be used, be sure to pay attention to objects that are already out of use. If you have an unnecessary vase or ordinary vessel, you can make a unique thing out of it. It will not only decorate your home, but also become your pride.

Decorating a vase will not cause difficulties even for a novice master:

  1. Discs must be cut into pieces. Their shape and size depend on your desire. The combination of large pieces with small ones looks original.
  2. Using glue, attach the prepared parts of the discs to the vessel. If you conceived a complex design pattern, then initially mark the vase.

Hot Stands

This is perhaps the easiest way to use disks. Their shape is simply designed to be used as a hot stand. Of course, such a thing will not stand a hot pan, but it will look great under a cup of hot tea.





There are several ways to make stands:

  1. Sheathe the disk with a beautiful fabric, the tones of which harmoniously fit into the atmosphere of the kitchen.
  2. The surface can be painted with acrylics. If you want to reproduce the original drawing, use a stencil.
  3. Don't know or don't want to draw? Decorate the surface with a beautiful self-adhesive film.
  4. If you need a stand for very hot dishes, then use the disk as a basis. Stick small flat pebbles on its surface.

Original watch

We continue to fantasize how old disks can be used. A sufficiently original and unique decor can be a watch from this material.

The manufacturing process is extremely simple:

  1. You will need a clockwork. It can be purchased in a special store for creativity.
  2. The clock mechanism is inserted into the hole of the disc and secured with a cardboard strip.
  3. You can think of an additional cut. To do this, select a beautiful picture, print it and paste it on a disk.

Embroidered flowers

How to use old CDs in the country? Remember crochet, knitting, or embroidery techniques. Experiment using the disc as a base. Such decorative elements look just fine. On a shiny surface, they resemble bright flowers.

  2. Using a linked chain, the elements are easily interconnected. You will get an original curtain.
  3. Such "flowers" will be a magnificent decoration of your garden. After all, they perfectly reflect the sun's rays and provide soft light.

As you can see, the possibilities for using old discs are truly endless. Having original thinking, having free time, you can create unique and completely unusual things.
