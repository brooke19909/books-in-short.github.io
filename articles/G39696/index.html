Hotel "Parus", Adler: description and reviews

The Parus Hotel was recently built in Adler. It has four floors where vacationers can choose rooms in different price categories. There are many reasons why you can choose the Parus Hotel for relaxation . Adler has a well-developed infrastructure, here any entertainment is located five minutes walk from the hotel.





Location

The Parus Hotel, located in the Adler area, is essentially a mini-hotel. Nearby are Olympic Park, the central market, many catering points (cafes and canteens), grocery and souvenir shops. Also within walking distance are the Magnit hypermarket, the shopping and entertainment centers Plaza and New Century. At a distance of six kilometers from the hotel is the Sochi Discovery World Aquarium. Adler-Sochi International Airport is 3 kilometers from the hotel . Also very close to the hotel, about six hundred meters away, guests can admire St. Sarkis Cathedral. The sea from the hotel is also at a distance of six hundred meters. The hotel does not have its own beach. The address where the Parus Hotel is located is Adler, Stanislavsky Street , 36. The hotel has a 3 star category.

Some features of the hotel

Any hotel has its own internal rules, about which it must inform its guests in advance. Hotel Parus (Adler) is no exception and has some features.

  • Smoking is strictly prohibited on site.
  • The hotel does not have a lift.
  • Children are welcome at any age.
  • Children under 13 years of age, inclusive, without extra bed and meals are on site free of charge.
  • Unfortunately, pets are not allowed in the hotel.
  • The Parus Hotel does not have its own parking. If you plan to come to rest on your own car, first ask the administrator where parking lots are nearby.
  • Check-in at 13.00, check-out time - 12.00.
  • Mini-Hotel Parus is open around the clock.
  • Payment for accommodation is made only in cash.
  • Tourists arriving by train or arriving by plane can get to the hotel by bus or taxi.

Catering for vacationers

If you come to relax at the Parus Hotel (Adler), meals are at your discretion. Room rates may include breakfast, or stay at the hotel without meals. The Parus Hotel has its own dining room. Delicious homemade meals are prepared in the dining room. A full meal will cost you a little over three hundred rubles. If desired, vacationers can use the shared kitchen, where they are given the opportunity to cook on their own. This is especially important if the family travels with young children, and the food from the dining room is not suitable for them. Guests can also have a tasty meal in the nearby cafes and restaurants where they can taste Caucasian and national dishes.
