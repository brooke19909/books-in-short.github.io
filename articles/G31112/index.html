Crocheted napkin as an element of interior decoration

Visiting exhibitions of folk art products, you never cease to be surprised at how talented people are. Any craft made with soul and love always represents a product of amazing beauty. This also applies to knitting - it doesn‚Äôt matter if the product is knitted or crocheted. A crocheted napkin comes into fashion again, as in the days of the youth of our parents.



It would seem that there are only a few elements and figures, and the results are very diverse and beautiful. Of course, there are different styles of crocheting, but the main elements are crochets, stitches and stitches. Having picked up the scheme you like, you can begin this magical occupation, which can result, for example, in a napkin crocheted from multi-colored or plain threads. Colors can be chosen independently or those that are proposed in the scheme can be left. The main thing is that they combine well with each other, creating a single color.



By changing the size and thickness of the thread, you can get different products. For example, a crocheted napkin, after certain manipulations, can become a vase. If all the actions and steps were performed correctly and consistently, you can safely put lemons or oranges in such a vase.

And how gorgeous and unusual look lamp shades for lamps, crocheted! The light gives them an even more magical look, making them truly fabulous. And there are craftsmen who manage to make real shoes for the princess with the help of a hook and threads! These boots look very good on the leg, and they are completely weightless.



You can knit huge tablecloths and shawls, a knitted oval napkin is also suitable for decorating the table : crocheting using various patterns, you can give it a special sophistication. Flowers on such an ornament look very nice, connected from different threads: they create a motley flower meadow.

Do-it-yourself products give home coziness. If you teach your children to see beauty, awaken in them a love for this beautiful needlework and a desire to work miracles, they will be your first helpers.



At first, in order for you to get a beautiful oval crochet napkin, the scheme should not be very complicated. Over time, you will have more experience, you can create any schemes yourself and even share them with people who will also be interested. A visit to a crochet workshop will give you energy: you will surely have a desire to create your own masterpiece. And let it be just a napkin crocheted, but it will be completely created by you - your imagination, your desire, your soul.



Using a hook, you can also create shawls and knit dresses. But all this will be after reaching a certain level of skill. And in the beginning you will find air loops and posts. Do not be afraid to start, even if it does not work out right away. Here, as in any business, practice is needed. Failed on the first try - it will work on the second, in extreme cases, the third attempt. The main thing is that everything be done for your pleasure.
