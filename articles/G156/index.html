Sergey Skuratov - President of the company "Sergey Skuratov Architects"

In this material we will talk in detail about who is Skuratov Sergey Alexandrovich (architect). He was born in 1955 in Moscow. He is the president of Architects. Member of the Union of Moscow Architects. He is a professor at the Academy of Architecture. Laureate of international and Russian shows and competitions. Honorary builder of Moscow.

early years



Sergey Skuratov is an architect who comes from the family of a military pilot. Since 1963 he studied at a secondary school. Received additional education. Sergey Skuratov attended art school in Krasnopresnensky district. In 1973 he became a student at the Moscow Architectural Institute. He studied at the Faculty of Housing. In 1979 he graduated from the Moscow Institute of Architecture.

Becoming



Skuratov Sergey Alexandrovich from 1979 to 1982 was an architect at the B. S. Mezentsev TsNIIEP . He worked in the studio with Mark Bubnov. Since 1982, he worked as an artist at the Design Art Combine, as well as at KMDI. From 1986 to 1988, Sergei Skuratov led a group of architects at the B. S. Mezentsev Central Scientific Research Institute of Industrial Economics. He worked in the Larina brigade. In 1988 he joined the Union of Architects. From 1988 to 1990, this man held another important position. He was the chief architect at the architectural and art bureau called PROJECT.

From 1990 to 1995, he collaborated with the joint venture MDC "ARKSIM". Here he was the chief architect of the projects. From 1995 to 2002 he collaborated with Sergey Kiselev & Partners.

Here he served as chief project architect. In 1999 he received the title of Honorary Builder of the city of Moscow. Since 2002, he became president of Architects. Since 2003 is a professor at the Academy of Architecture

Projects



Sergey Skuratov worked on the creation of a residential complex in Butikovsky Lane. Its design took place from 2000 to 2002. Construction was carried out in 2001-2003. The object's address is Moscow, Butikovsky Lane, 5. For this work he received a number of awards, in particular, he became a laureate of a Moscow architecture review for the best project. Sergey Skuratov worked on the creation of the Copper House residential complex. Design was carried out in the period from 2002 to 2003. Construction was carried out in 2003-2004.

The object is located in Moscow, on Butikovsky Lane, 3. Thanks to this work, he became the Laureate of the Moscow show for the best work of architecture. The same person created the House on Mosfilmovskaya. The construction of the facility took place from 2004 to 2011. The facility is located in Moscow on Pyryeva Street 2. Company



The architectural bureau of Sergei Skuratov Architects is developing projects of varying degrees of complexity - urban complexes, public and cultural buildings, multi-storey and private residential buildings, high-rise buildings. Over the years, the company has implemented more than thirty copyright projects. Unique buildings erected in Moscow, as well as other cities of the Russian Federation, have received recognition among the professional community. As a result, Architects has gained international fame.
