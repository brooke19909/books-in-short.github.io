Base

This is an important part of the robotic complex. A rectangular box with rounded edges and a stand is a device to which a vacuum cleaner is parked to recharge. The power supply is external, which reduced the weight and size of the base. The cable leading from the PSU to the base can be disconnected if necessary. Its length is 1 meter. The cable leading from the power supply to the outlet is slightly shorter (95 cm), it is non-removable.

Although the platform is made of porous plastic and equipped with rubber inserts, the base is easy to turn or shift due to its light weight. Users recommend securing it more securely, for example, using double-sided tape. Navigation devices

An important element of the dust collection complex is the navigation blocks, which in the amount of two pieces are included in the basic kit, but can be purchased separately if necessary. They help the robot sensors in navigation. These devices are either a beacon or the so-called virtual wall.

The beacon function allows you to set the cleaning sequence. That is, the IRobot 780 enters a room marked with a beacon, and does not leave it until it collects all the dust and dirt. The device also allows you to protect a specific work area with an area of ‚Äã‚Äãseveral square meters. For example, when the room is clean enough, but you need to remove some kind of nook.

No less useful feature is the "Virtual Wall". According to the instructions of the IRobot 780 Roomba, the navigation device creates an invisible barrier over which the vacuum cleaner will not enter. That is, it marks a zone where the robot is undesirable (for example, in the corner where children are playing at this time).

System configuration is quite simple. Navigation blocks are activated automatically when the robot cleaner is turned on. The change of modes, as well as the length of the barrier, is carried out using special switches on the housing.

Innovation

The engineers of the Chinese company emphasize the Dirt Detect Series 2 technology, implemented in the 780th model. To improve garbage collection, the device uses an acoustic sensor to detect an excessive amount of small and hard debris (such as sand, pebbles) and an optical sensor to fix large and soft objects (such as popcorn, dust lumps).

Also new is the AeroVac Series 2 trash container with a filling indicator and an easy extraction system - dirt is simply ejected after removal. HEPA dual filters trap dirt, dust and pollen from the air.

In the 780-ke introduced a new power management software, which, according to the developers, will provide 50% longer battery life IRobot 780 compared to the previous 600th generation. According to reviews, in real conditions, this figure is about 35-40%. A cleaning session lasts about 90 minutes on average.

Cleaning Schemes

The device has several basic motion algorithms:

Reviews

As expected, user ratings for the Roomba 780 vary widely. Most appreciate the efforts of the electronic assistant, but the model cannot be called faultless.

Of course, the most important component is the quality of cleaning. Most owners note that, despite the miniature size and seemingly small power, the vacuum cleaner is quite able to cope with the dispersed garbage: whether it be sand grains or larger particles. The device perfectly frees carpets from animal hair. But long hair for the device is a problem: winding up on the brush, they provoke its wear, loss of bristles.

Admittedly, the IRobot 780 Roomba battery is a weak point in the device. If the warranty of the vacuum cleaner itself is 1 year, for the battery this period is 6 months. It is the ‚Äúaging‚Äù of the battery that leads to the subsequent mistakes of the clever technique: premature termination of cleaning (even though the floor is still dirty), ignoring the work of the beacons, improper parking at the base, etc. And it is not surprising: the volume of work of the 780-k is quite large, but you can‚Äôt accommodate a large battery in such a tiny case. However, when it is replaced, many software crashes disappear.

Some owners believe that Roomba is noisy compared to other models of famous brands. The process of cleaning the built-in filters is not very convenient. But these are trifles compared to performance. If you monitor the device, regularly clean it of dust accumulating in the cracks, and change the batteries in a timely manner - it will work for years faithfully. In just 2 hours, he is able to clean an apartment of 60 m2, stuffed with furniture, wires from electronic devices, in which people and pets live.

Conclusion

It may seem that a robot vacuum cleaner is a kind of fashionable toy designed to emphasize the status of the owner as a person holding his hand on the pulse of time. Meanwhile, the device is really a good addition to the mop and handheld vacuum cleaner. It perfectly removes solid fractions (sand, sugar, salt, cereals, seeds, popcorn, etc.) and animal hair. For many, an electronic friend becomes almost a member of the family, a symbol of a tireless hard worker, without whining and smoking, doing his hard work.
