How to weave bracelets from ribbons with your own hands

A bracelet is the best decoration of a hand. Currently, there are many different decorations. Therefore, for each outfit, you can choose something special. Exactly what will help to make the image bright and unforgettable. And lovers of unique and extravagant outfits can create a charming and unique image by making this simple jewelry with their own hands. To do this, you can choose any material you like: satin ribbons, beads, leather, threads, etc. To begin, consider how to weave bracelets from ribbons.

Ribbon Bracelets Weaving



For those who are just starting to weave and making bracelets of ribbons for the first time, you can take two ribbons, each two meters long. They can be in contrasting colors. Next, we connect two ribbons and on each we make two loops, not forgetting that the distance between them should be about fifteen centimeters. As soon as the loop on the first tape is ready, it needs to be threaded into the loop on the second ribbon and tighten the first ribbon. Then we do the opposite and continue to weave the bracelet until it reaches the desired length. After the bracelet reaches the desired length, the ends of the ribbons are tied and trimmed (after that the tips can be melted so that they do not crumble and dishevele). It should be remembered that in order for the bracelet to look attractive, the loops on the ribbons should be tightened gently. You should also ensure that the correct pattern is saved.

Bead bracelets

Another way to weave bracelets from ribbons is to string large beads on the ribbon.

They are usually fixed in knots on both sides.
And in order to make the bracelet more original, large beads can be alternated with smaller ones. The ends of the ribbon (and there can be either one or several) can be tied in a bow. Now you know how to weave bracelets from ribbons.

Thread bracelets

Bracelets made of threads also look very original.

For those who already know how to weave bracelets from ribbons, it will not be difficult to learn how to make them from threads. This is an exciting experience. In order to learn how to weave bracelets from threads, you should remember a few rules. First you need to prepare the necessary materials: thread floss (at least one meter), scissors, pin.

Weaving baubles

First, take eight threads (preferably of different colors), tie them into a knot and attach to some base (to make it easier to weave). After that, we lay out the threads in a certain order, depending on what pattern should be obtained. Now we start weaving. With an extreme thread we tie the adjacent double knot. We also tie the threads following it until the first reaches the other edge. With the next thread, which is now extreme, we do the same. When all the threads are on the other side, we do the same with them, but in the opposite direction. Thus, we get a pattern of colored lines. We continue to weave a bauble until it reaches the required length. You can weave a herringbone pattern. To do this, it is worth splitting the threads into two parts and weaving the left part from left to right, and the right one - vice versa. All threads that braid one row from opposite sides meet in the center, forming a line of nodules. This line is like an arrow pointing down. You can fix the ends by tying knots or making ties in the form of braids.

Remembering how to weave bracelets from ribbons and threads, you can easily come up with your own unique pattern and create an unforgettable image.
