Prima Hotel (Antalya) - photos, prices and reviews

The resort town of Antalya is located in southern Turkey. This port megalopolis is the administrative center of the province of Antalya. It is located above sea level at an altitude of 30 meters. The city has 1,073,794 people. In summer, the population increases to two million people. At the moment, Antalya consists of two parts - the old and the new city. It was founded in 159 BC. e. the Greek king, Pergamum, Attalus II, and originally called Attalia. This later city was renamed Antalya. In 133 BC e. it was conquered by the Romans and turned into the residence of the emperor Hadrian. For a very long time Rome owned the city, and then Byzantium.

Antalya Tourism

Antalya has tremendous recreational resources, thanks to which it has become a real "Mecca" for European tourists. The historical center of the resort is not losing out on modern buildings - they, as before, are interested in lovers of national colors. Local architecture brought together elements of the Byzantine, Roman and Ottoman schools - a consequence of the complex history of Turkey. In the territory of Antalya there are three noisy waterfalls and a large number of parks. The ski track with sparkling snow is located forty kilometers from the metropolis. A large number of different restaurants, bars and cafes are scattered throughout the resort for every taste and budget.
