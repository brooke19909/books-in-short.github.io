Eastin Yama Hotel 4 * (Thailand / Phuket): hotel reviews

Eastin Yama Hotel 4 * is a good Thai hotel, featuring a very favorable location and quality service. In addition to this, he also has a lot of other features. For them, in fact, this hotel was loved by its guests. Well, you should talk about them in detail.





About location

Eastin Yama Hotel 4 * is in close proximity to the famous Kata Beach. This is the main advantage of this hotel. But not the last. Accommodation options

Eastin Yama Hotel 4 * boasts comfortable and stylishly decorated rooms, which are furnished with nice and comfortable modern furniture and all the amenities necessary for a good rest.

So, potential guests are offered several booking options. Superior double rooms with pool access are especially popular. Ordinary apartments for two are often also booked (as a rule, the view from them opens either to the sea or to the surroundings).

There are also deluxe rooms. There are options with one very large bed or with two ordinary beds. You can choose from apartments with either sea or mountain views. And of course, there are suites at this hotel. Interestingly, all rooms in this hotel are double. But in some apartments 3-4 people are allowed.





Superior Double Room

So, it is worth starting a more detailed story about the apartments of Eastin Yama Hotel 4 * with this category of rooms. A reservation for 2 full weeks will cost about 38-42 thousand rubles for two (with breakfast included in the price). What conditions? According to the guests staying here - excellent. They assure that the apartment has everything that you might need. It has a wide bed, a balcony with mountain views, a telephone, a large plasma TV with satellite and cable channels, air conditioning, a safe, an iron with ironing facilities, and even an iPod docking station is available. But that's not all amenities! A sofa, a desk, a clothes dryer, a bathroom with bathtub and shower, a wardrobe, a furniture corner on the balcony, a dining table, a minibar, a private toilet (of course) and such nice bonuses as slippers, toiletries, towels, bathrobe and linen. And of course, wireless and free Wi-Fi. In general, guests do not lie - this number is really worthy of attention. Especially for such a ridiculous price for Thailand.





Deluxe Apartment

These rooms in Eastin Yama Hotel Phuket 4 * are also quite popular. They cost, of course, more expensive. About 56-60 thousand rubles for two and two weeks (with breakfast). Guests at Eastin Yama Hotel Phuket 4 * reviews leave this apartment very inspirational. They claim that the rooms of this class in this complex are much better than some suites in hotels of other resorts.

So, the room is spacious - its area is 41 square meters. You can choose from either one very large bed or two ordinary beds. From the conditions - all the same that is available for the above category of rooms. But even guests can be offered the so-called adjacent apartments. And deluxe rooms are decorated more original and stylish.

Only superior rooms with pool access are more expensive than this apartment. About 63-70 thousand rubles for two with breakfast included and for 14 nights. In addition to access to the pool - all the same conditions and amenities that were listed above.





Luxury apartments

The best reviews, of course, get the suites of the Eastin Yama Hotel Phuket 4 *. They must be said not so expensive. Luxury apartments will cost about 83-93 thousand rubles for two (all for the same 2 weeks of rest) with breakfast included in the price.

The room is definitely luxurious. Incredibly spacious (area is 63 square meters), with its own bedroom, where there is simply an unrealistic size bed and other amenities. The balcony offers a picturesque view of the sea, which will delight real aesthetes. For entertainment, the room features a wide flat-screen TV with a DVD player, satellite and cable channels. There is also an iPod dock. Air conditioning, a safety deposit box, a work desk, and even a dressing room - all these amenities are available in the luxury apartments. There is also an additional sofa, wardrobe, clothes dryer, hairdryer. And a kettle with coffee machine is available, as is a dining area. Naturally, as a useful and pleasant bonus, guests are offered bathrobes, slippers, towels, linen and other little things. Not surprisingly, guests are delighted with these rooms.

Service and Facilities

Eastin Yama Hotel Phuket 4 * is famous not only for its comfortable rooms, but also for the services offered here. So, the attention of the guests is an outdoor pool with a sun terrace. There is also a fitness center, massage room, three restaurants (one is branded, and the other two work on the menu and the buffet), a bar with a variety of alcoholic and soft drinks.





The staff of Eastin Yama Hotel Phuket 4 * (Phuket) organizes room service for drinks and food, transfers (free), car rental, concierge services, laundry services, etc. Every day, the rooms are carefully cleaned and regularly changed. And if guests wish, they can arrange the delivery of the press.

In general, as guests say, this is the best Thai hotel from the budget category. Of course, on its territory there is no own nightclub, but then there is excellent service and comfortable apartments, which have everything that you might need. This explains the popularity of this hotel. So if you want to stay here, you should take care of the reservation in advance. Otherwise, all available rooms are already reserved.
