Hotel Marmaris Begonville Hotel 3 * (Turkey Marmaris): photos and reviews of tourists from Russia

An entire army of tourists from different countries comes to the Turkish resort of Marmaris every year to spend an unforgettable vacation in an atmosphere of unrestrained fun. Leisure here is very diverse. Apparently, it was for this that travelers chose this small town on the Aegean coast. The tourist season in Marmaris is much shorter than, for example, in Antalya. It rains here in October, and in the winter months the thermometer does not rise above +15 degrees. But even at this time in Marmaris can be quite warm and sunny days. Therefore, some resort hotels receive guests year-round.



general information

This resort on the Turkish coast of the Aegean Sea became popular among Russian tourists just a few years ago. But Europeans have chosen Marmaris for a long time. High quality service is offered by all hotels in this resort. They can be located both near the sea or in the city center, and on elevations in the mountains.

Among the hotels there are different options: modest small and relatively cheap, luxurious complexes with a huge territory - here, every tourist can choose an option based on their wealth and preferences. The most popular are four-star hotel complexes, built near the coastline. One of them is Begonville Beach Hotel 4 * (Marmaris). This hotel will be discussed in this article.

Hotel Description

Begonville Beach Hotel 4 * (Turkey, Marmaris) is located on the seashore. The resort center is only one kilometer, which can be easily overcome with a walking step. This is where many entertainment venues are located.

The nearest international air gate is ninety-five kilometers away. Transfer from the airport lasts about an hour and a half. Bar Street Marmaris and the city beach are within walking distance.



This hotel was formerly called the Marmaris Begonville Hotel. 3 years ago a major renovation was carried out here. Repair of the building and all infrastructure facilities was made. The name of the hotel has also changed. Since 2014, he has been featured in travel agencies as the Begonville Beach Hotel 4 * (Marmaris).

It is built on a relatively small area - a little more than a thousand square meters. Nevertheless, many basic infrastructures fit on it.

Near the hotel there are a city bazaar and many shops. The hotel does not have its own parking. Guests can use the public parking, free of charge.

Hotel infrastructure

Marmaris Begonville Hotel 4 *, according to Russian reviews, is a hotel with an excellent ratio of the cost of living and the quality of the service provided. Our compatriots consider its main advantage proximity to the sea.

After a thorough renovation, the hotel looks quite modern. Despite the small area, its very well-groomed territory is everywhere planted with flowers, covered with a soft lawn cover.

Marmaris Begonville Hotel consists of one six-story building with an elevator. There is mail in the lobby. The reception and lobby are practically combined. Sitting on comfortable sofas and armchairs, guests can comfortably wait for check-in.

The reviews of our countrymen suggest that this hotel is primarily intended for lovers of "hanging out", so too large a hotel area in this case is not relevant. Tourists can walk to the center of Marmaris in about twenty minutes along the beautiful promenade. There is a bus stop near the hotel. From here, buses depart literally to any place in the resort.



As for the services provided, the hotel guests can take their clothes to the laundry room, consult a doctor if necessary. Of course, all this is not included in the price. These are paid services, as well as the use of a built-in safe in the rooms and their maintenance. On the ground floor of the building near the registration desk there is fast Internet. A free shuttle leaves the hotel daily, which takes tourists to the equipped Nirvana Beach in Icmeler.

Description of rooms

Marmaris Begonville Hotel is considered average in terms of housing. It has only sixty rooms in the following categories: standard with garden view, as well as standard and premium with sea view.



They all have the same area - twenty-two square meters. The rooms are designed for two people and one child under the age of six. The occupancy of the rooms is fully consistent with the four-star level of the hotel, although in some booklets of travel companies it is positioned as Marmaris Begonville Hotel 3 * (Turkey, Marmaris). The rooms are refurbished. Their design is dominated by white color, contributing to complete relaxation. All rooms have small, furnished balconies. The furnishings are standard: there is comfortable furniture, including a wardrobe, telephone, air conditioning, a TV with Turkish, Russian and English satellite channels. If you wish, for three dollars a day you can rent a built-in small safe in which the laptop is located.

Towels and bed linen are changed in the former Begonville Hotel 3 * (Turkey, Marmaris) every other day. Daily rooms are cleaned at a convenient time for residents. Bathrooms combined with shower, hairdryer and a minimum set of hygiene items.

Marmaris Begonville Beach Hotel 4 * (Marmaris) is mostly visited by young people, although couples with kids can often be found here. Description of the beach area

The hotel has its own swimming area. Before her, about two minutes walk at a slow pace. The beach is fully equipped: it has sun loungers and umbrellas, which are provided to residents free of charge. Judging by the reviews, the sea is very clean. The beach is cleaned twice a day. In the mornings and evenings, the water in the sea is especially clear. The beach is sandy in some places, at the very edge there is a pebble strip two meters wide. Therefore, the presence of rubber bathing slippers for tourists of this hotel is relevant. If you wish, you can go to another beach - in Icmeler.



Entertainment

The hotel has a small outdoor pool, as well as a small branch - "paddling pool" for bathing children. If it seems to tourists that the beach is too hot, they are conveniently placed on sun loungers in the shade of palm trees. Judging by the reviews of some Russians, they liked the pool even more. The sun terrace is large enough, so there are always a lot of free sunbeds. The pool itself is clean, with very comfortable steps to enter the water. The hotel also has a cinema and relaxation room, a wellness center and a spa. A visit to the Turkish bath and a reflex massage course can be obtained for a fee.
