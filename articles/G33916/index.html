Calypso Beach Hotel 4 * (Greece, Faliraki): description of rooms, service, reviews

Calypso Beach Hotel 4 * is located on the island of Rhodes, on the beach of Faliraki in Greece. This city has become a resort recently, so there is not a large influx of tourists from all over the world, which is perfect for vacationers who are looking for privacy, tranquility and peace.



Also, the resort is ideal for guests with small children. The hotel was built in 1982 and is located 12 kilometers from Rhodes and only 2 from the town of Faliraki on the shores of the Aegean Sea, which used to be a simple fishing village.

Calypso beach hotel has certificates of reliability of HACCP and ISO 9001: 2000. The hotel according to international classification corresponds to the level of "four stars".

Beach mood

Amazing sunny beach Calypso Beach Hotel 4 * is located on the 1st line (less than 200 meters), which means that a daily trip to it will not be long and tiring. The close proximity of a sandy piece of a huge paradise island will be the daily landscape that you will observe from your room (when choosing a room with a sea view), which favorably affects any beginning of the day.

The hotel provides guests with a clean sandy field with evenly laid out sun loungers, sun loungers and sun umbrellas and makes it possible to enjoy the coastal beauty of the sea, waves and wind. All beach props are free.



On the beach you can enjoy a banana ride, windsurf or canoe. Many vacationers, most likely, at first will not want to leave the room from the last floors only because all the depth and purity of the sea is visible from above.

Number of rooms

We proceed to the description of the rooms at Calypso Beach Hotel. There are 323 comfortable rooms of the following classifications: single standard, economy, standard and standard with sea view. Their difference from each other is only in the maximum number of people living in the room and the view from the window.

Single standard

This room has one comfortable room with a single bed, a balcony and satellite LCD TV. There is also a central air conditioning, a bedside table with a lamp, a dressing table with a mirror, a refrigerator, a radio, a safe and a telephone for communication with the reception. The floors in the living room and bathroom are tiled, a hairdryer and free Wi-Fi are also provided.



The maximum number of adults staying in a single standard room is 1 person. Children are not allowed in this apartment.

Economy Room

This room has one comfortable room with a large bed, a balcony and satellite LCD TV. Additionally, the room has a soft folding sofa, which is separated from the bed by a small partition. There is also a central air conditioning, a bedside table with a lamp, a dressing table with a mirror, a refrigerator, a radio, a safe and a telephone for communication with the reception. Wi-Fi is free. The maximum number of adults staying in an economy room is 2 people. Settlement with children is also not provided. The window from the room overlooks the hotel.

Standard (Standard room)

This room has one room with a bed, a balcony and satellite TV. Additionally, the room has a soft folding sofa, which is separated from the bed by a partition. The room also has central air conditioning, a bedside table with a lamp, a dressing table with a mirror, a refrigerator, a radio, a safe and a telephone for communication with the reception. Wi-Fi is free.



The maximum number of adults staying in a standard room is 2 people. The maximum number of children who can settle here is 2 people. The window from the room overlooks the hotel.

Standard sea view

This room has one comfortable room with a large bed, a balcony and satellite LCD TV. Additionally, the room has a soft folding sofa. The bathroom has a hairdryer. Wi-Fi is free. On the balcony there is a glass table and two chairs for relaxing. The view from the room is on the sea.

Service Calypso Beach Hotel

The hotel and rooms are cleaned in the morning or afternoon, when most vacationers are on site or on excursions. Cots are available free of charge. Each guest is also provided with beach towels free of charge. Smoking in the hotel rooms and on its territory is strictly prohibited. The management of the complex prohibits pets, including service animals.

The reception is open around the clock, so vacationers can resolve conflicts at any time. Hotel staff speak several languages, including Russian. The main one is English. Check-in time for visitors starts at 14:00, and check-out time is until 12:00. For guests, a business room and a conference room for negotiations with a capacity of up to 120 people are also available.



The hotel has a large number of green spaces, palm trees and small fountains. For guests there is always a swimming pool with outdoor slides (including a children's one), SPA-zones, green areas for relaxation, bars, restaurants and playgrounds. The pool bar serves refreshing drinks, ice cream, cocktails and light snacks.

The hotel provides a pleasant service for newlyweds. The bride and groom are given a standard room with sea views, and they also bring wine and fruit. In addition, loyal discounts are provided for the services of a makeup artist and photographer when ordering a wedding photo shoot, as well as discounts for arranging exit registration on a beach with clean, white sand.

Hotel reviews

Vacationers in Calypso were completely satisfied. Tourists note the gorgeous sea, a great beach, a comfortable children's pool, a good playground. You can sit quietly in the evening and relax while the kids are playing. The center of Faliraki is only 20 minutes on foot. Recommended hotel for families with children. You can take a car and go around the whole island in a day.

Great friendly staff delighted guests of the hotel. Finally, animation appeared in the hotel, though not yet in Russian, but there are not so many Russian vacationers here. At the reception there is a Russian-speaking specialist. Also noted was a convenient electronic indication system "clean the room." But for families with children, you need to consider that there is no cereal for breakfast. But there is a lot of things that can please the kids.





Another advantage was the location of the hotel, which is more than successful. The sea and the beach are clean. Every evening in the courtyard (from the sea) loud live music sounds. If there is no desire to go to the sea, then you can soak up the pool, which guests noted with a high rating. Tourists take inflatable circles at the hotel and swim in the clean pool throughout the day. The pool is so big that there is a huge water slide. And nearby is a large bar.

The air conditioner operates in three modes. The hotel area is huge, there is a lot of greenery, a tennis court for a fun game in a big company. Tourists noted that the food is very tasty. Hotel guests paid special attention to the variety of dishes and a wonderful combination of taste and quality.
