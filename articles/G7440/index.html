iPhone 5: phone features

This model has a widescreen Multi-Touch display, the diagonal of which is four inches. Screen resolution - 1136 by 640 pixels. The contrast is standard (800: 1). The screen brightness is also standard. The screen coating is oleophobic on both sides, so it is resistant to finger stains. Agree, this is very good quality for the phone.





The camera here is good, the resolution is eight megapixels. The quality of the photos is simply excellent, no one will believe that they were made using the phone. The camera recognizes faces in photographs, and the flash will not let photos taken at night to deteriorate. Video is recorded in HD quality.

Charging the battery is good enough. It lasts 8 hours in talk mode, 6 hours in internet surfing mode, 10 hours in video mode, 40 hours in audio mode, and 225 hours in standby mode.

Consider the dimensions of the phone. Its height is 12.38 cm; width - 5.86 cm; thickness - 0.76 cm; its weight is only 112 grams. iPhone 5 (reviews of fans of the iPhone line were taken into account) is available in black and white.





Of course, there is Wi-Fi support, as well as a built-in Bluetooth module.

The headphone jack has not changed, but the headphones themselves are now different. They are in a new design, and the sound quality has become much better. An interesting point - more than five hundred people participated in headphone testing. They ran around with headphones, fell in the rain with them, walked with them in both hot and cold weather. With this approach, the developers managed to bring the headphones to the ideal. They have excellent protection against sweat and water, while their convenience is on top. In general, the characteristics of the iPhone 5 leave only a positive impression.



The Internet is replete with records of owners of the iPhone 5, whose reviews provide comprehensive information about the phone. Almost all are completely satisfied with this model, the only minus that is indicated is an expensive price. Not everyone is able to afford this kind of purchase. Well, those who nevertheless become the owner of this device will certainly not be disappointed. IPhone developers are trying more and more to please their fans, and this, it is worth noting, they do very well.
