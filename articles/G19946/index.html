Ammos Resort 4 Hotel: Location





The third largest Greek island, a true paradise in the Aegean, shrouded in ancient myths and legends ... It is here that the Ammos Resort 4 hotel is located; about. Kos is by far one of the most popular tourist destinations in Greece.

The hotel is located on the north side of the island, next to the picturesque fishing village called Mastichari. By the way, the distance to the nearest airport is only seven kilometers.

Brief description of the hotel





Ammos Resort 4 is located in one of the most picturesque places of the island. In fact, this hotel can hardly be attributed to large resort complexes - it is rather a peaceful place for complete relaxation and a fun family vacation.

The hotel area, although small, is beautifully decorated. Walking paths, a garden filled with exotic plants and vibrant flowers, as well as comfortable benches in the shade of palm trees - all this will turn your vacation into a real fairy tale. In addition, there is a large terrace from where residents can enjoy sea views.

Description of rooms and accommodation





Of course, the Ammos Resort 4 hotel (Kos island) offers its guests excellent conditions for living. The hotel has 108 rooms in the following categories:

  • single standard room;
  • double standard room for 2, maximum 3 people;
  • large family room where 5 guests can live at the same time.

Immediately it is worth noting that the hotel was recently renovated, so all the rooms are in perfect condition. Of course, there is a necessary set of furniture, including a bed, bedside tables, tables, chairs and a wardrobe. Almost every room has its own balcony, from where you can enjoy sea views or watch life on site.

Each room is equipped with a modern air conditioning system. It also has a large flat-screen TV, thanks to which you can watch satellite channels. There is also a free wireless Internet connection.

The bathroom will also appreciate the convenience and modern fixtures. There is a bathtub, shower, washbasin, large wall mirror, hair dryer. Each tenant is provided with a set of towels, a soft bathrobe, slippers and a set of hygiene products.

By the way, maids clean rooms and change towels every day. Bed linen is changed three times a week.

Beach and water recreation





What conditions for a beach holiday can the island of Kos offer ? Ammos Resort 4 is located on the first beach line, so the coast can be reached in just a few minutes, which, of course, can be considered an advantage.

The beach here is neat and well-groomed, covered with soft white sand. And seawater is always warm, clean and clear. It is worth noting that the coast is rocky. However, there is a convenient and safe descent into the sea.

Sun loungers and umbrellas are arranged in even rows on the beach - guests of Ammos Resort 4 can use them for free. Do not worry about the number of sunbeds, as they are enough for all guests. Moreover, it is quite easy to find a secluded place for a relaxing holiday and unity with nature.

Naturally, lovers of active water recreation will not remain deprived. Here everyone has the opportunity to ride catamarans, boats and boats. Water skiing is also available. A scuba diving school is nearby. On the beach itself there is a spacious volleyball court. The hotel also regularly organizes boat trips and even real trips along the coast.

Sightseeing vacation





If during your vacation you want to combine business with pleasure, then you probably thought about what interesting things Kos Island can offer a tourist. Ammos Resort 4 regularly arranges excursions in its surroundings for its guests. And in this place there really is something to see.

For example, Asklepion managed to win the special love of tourists. At one time, this place was a temple dedicated to the god Asclepius, and now there is a medical complex. In addition, there are several thermal springs on the island. You will also have the opportunity to look at Platan (a tree that, according to legend, was planted by Hippocrates), visit the ruins of the ancient temple of Aphrodite, take a walk around the nature reserve, and also get acquainted with the exhibits of the Archaeological Museum and the Roman villa of Casa Romana.
