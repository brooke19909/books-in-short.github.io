Guest house "Arina" (Gelendzhik): description, number of rooms and reviews

Many Russian tourists choose Gelendzhik as a place for a summer vacation. This is one of the oldest resorts on the Black Sea coast. It has a well-developed tourist infrastructure, and the cost of living in local hotels can be called budget. Not far from the beach is the guest house "Arina" (Gelendzhik). What can he offer the guests?

general information

The guest house can be called a small complex, because it consists of one three-story building. In total, tourists are offered 15 rooms, so it is suitable for travelers who love a quiet vacation. The hotel, on the ground floor, has its own dining room, however, meals are usually not included in the price. You can check in at Arina Guesthouse (Gelendzhik) with young children over two years of age. But they will not be provided with a crib. Pets are not allowed, as well as smoking in the hotel building. When registering, you will definitely require a passport. It starts here at 14:00.





For early vouchers, tourists must make an advance payment for accommodation. The checkout time at the guest house is noon. At the end of the holiday, guests should leave the rooms by this time. The cost of living here depends on the type of room and the month of settlement. The minimum price is 1,500 rubles per day, and the maximum can reach 4,500 rubles.

Guest house location

The hotel is located in an ecologically clean area of ​​the resort. You can come to Gelendzhik in several ways. The railway station is located in Novorossiysk, Krasnodar and Anapa. From there, the resort can be reached by bus or taxi. Hotel guests can be met at an additional cost at the railway station of any of the above cities or at the Gelendzhik bus station. You can fly to the hotel by plane. The airport is located near the Cape Thin, so the distance to it is only 3 km. There is a pebble public beach next to the guest house, which can be reached in 3 minutes. To get to the sandy coast, tourists will have to spend 5-7 minutes.

The developed infrastructure attracts tourists Gelendzhik. Arina Guest House (Thin Cape) is located next to the bus stop. A shuttle bus takes you to the city center and the local amusement park. The journey time to them is approximately 10 minutes. Gelendzhik Dolphinarium and Water Park are a 7-minute drive away, and the local market can be reached in 5 minutes. In walking distance from the hotel there is a pharmacy and a grocery store.

Number of rooms

Guest house "Arina" (Gelendzhik) has 15 rooms. Repairs in them are made in the classical style, which does not differ in luxurious decor elements. Each room has a private bathroom equipped with a shower. Linen and towels are changed once a week, and cleaning is carried out at the request of guests. Extra bed in the room is available for a fee. All apartments are equipped with modern flat-screen cable TVs, refrigerators, microwaves and air conditioning.





When making a voucher, tourists can choose one of the following accommodation options:

  • Double room with amenities. Its area is 15 square meters. m. The living room has 1 double or 2 single beds. It is planned to accommodate two adults and one child.
  • Triple room with amenities. It differs from double apartments by the presence of an additional sofa bed. 4 people - the maximum number of tourists who can be settled here.
  • Family apartment. Its area is 30 square meters. m. It consists of two rooms, which are single or double beds. Here 6 people can live at once.

Local infrastructure

Guest house "Arina" (Gelendzhik) offers guests a poor selection of services. However, hotel accommodation is quite cheap, so the price here is quite consistent with their number. So, tourists can take advantage of the following amenities:

  • washing machine (provided for a fee);
  • ironing board and iron (on request and for a fee);
  • BBQ and barbecue equipment;
  • free wifi in public places;
  • free parking in the courtyard with a limited number of spaces;
  • paid car rental.




Positive and negative reviews

Tourists who come to rest in the guest house "Arina" (Gelendzhik) leave different reviews. Some liked the accommodation offered, while others would prefer never to come here again. According to guests, the main advantage of the hotel is its good location. They also highlight the friendly hotel staff, delicious food in the dining room and the availability of parking spaces. Of the minuses, one can distinguish the dirty and completely filled with tourists Gelendzhik railway station, which is very far from the hotel, old furniture in the rooms, poor cleaning and untidy area near the "Arina".





Thus, this guest house can be recommended to tourists who want to save a lot on vacation. If you are not very limited in means, then it is better to find another option with good furniture, spacious rooms and a good location.
