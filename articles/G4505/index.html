Hotel "Scarlet Sails" in Feodosia: reviews of tourists

Not so long ago, the Crimean peninsula became part of the Russian Federation, leaving the composition of Ukraine. It was these events that became a turning point in the life of the republic, because now Crimea is almost the best tourist resort in Russia. Feodosia - one of the most popular resorts of the peninsula. In our article, we will discuss in detail the local hotel and restaurant complex, which is especially popular among tourists.

Scarlet Sails is a hotel in Feodosia that has been working here for more than 9 years, which makes it one of the best. It has excellent service and a modern interior, and today we will make a detailed review of this tourist complex, find out reviews about it and the exact location, as well as discuss in detail the number of rooms and infrastructure facilities.

Description

The Scarlet Sails Hotel appeared in 2007, that is, almost 10 years ago, and by no means immediately became the best of the best. Now the project has 53 comfortable rooms, where there is everything for a comfortable pastime. Additionally, it is worth noting that almost all rooms have terraces or balconies, which offer a breathtaking view of the Black Sea.





We all know that modern people cannot live without surfing the Internet for a day, so high-speed Wi-Fi works for all hotel guests.

Scarlet Sails (a hotel in Feodosia) is considered by many to be an ideal place to accommodate both businessmen and individual tourists. Here you can hold any business events, seminars and conferences. By the way, on the territory of the tourist complex there is a large conference room that can accommodate up to 50 people. In addition, it is worth noting the presence of an excellent restaurant with comfortable tables.

All for children!

This hotel complex is universal, because here businessmen and modest couples with small children and older children can ideally relax. By the way, experienced employees are always ready to take care of the children here. In the summer, the complex has a children's room, where each child, under the supervision of a professional nanny, will have a great time. In addition, the hotel "Scarlet Sails" in Feodosia, the photo of which is presented in the article, has on its territory a modern playground for outdoor games and a large green area for walking.





In general, as you can see, this hotel and restaurant complex takes care not only of adults, but also of children!

Additional features

The hotel "Scarlet Sails", reviews of which are extremely positive, has a wonderful outdoor pool, which is a great addition to the rest. The water in it is heated, also in the pool there is an additional small area with slides and other water attractions, provided for children.

For those who come to Feodosia to swim in the sea and sunbathe on the sandy shore, a gorgeous beach located just a few kilometers from the hotel will be a bonus. Comfortable buses with soft seats and air conditioning deliver tourists to it.

Do you understand that you must always watch your beauty? In this case, you should definitely visit the health and beauty center, which is called AsSol, open to residents of the city and hotel guests. By the way, this facility is the only one in Feodosia, where there is an indoor pool, gym, sauna, massage room and many other infrastructure facilities.

Location

Where to find the "Scarlet Sails", a 4 * hotel? Crimea, Feodosia, Aivazovsky Avenue, house number 47-b - this is its official address. It is located very close to the central promenade of the city and only 50 meters from the Black Sea.





For those who do not know how to get to this object, we note two possible ways:

  • from the train station or airport of Simferopol: take a minibus to the bus station of Feodosia, then take the minibus number 2 or number 106 and get off at the stop "Listovnichnaya Street", after which you will need to walk about 100 meters to the embankment, where hotel;
  • from the railway station in Feodosia: walk about 800 meters along the promenade.

What else can be said about Scarlet Sails, the hotel (Feodosia)? Reviews of it indicate an excellent level of service.
