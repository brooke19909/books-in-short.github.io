A bouquet of sausages - a creative gift

If you think that only women admire the bouquets received as a gift, then you are mistaken. Men also do not lag behind them. The main thing is to know which particular compositions cause a storm of positive emotions in the latter. One of the options to please the stronger sex is to make a bouquet of sausages for them.





Essential Products

We will show you how to make an edible offering. To begin with, of course, you should stock up on the necessary components of the gift:

  • long wooden skewers or chopsticks made of bamboo;
  • twine;
  • wide tape;
  • scissors or secateurs;
  • wrapping paper;
  • smoked cheese "Pigtail";
  • any kind of hard cheese;
  • smoked sausages;
  • half-smoked and smoked sausage (let it be different varieties, forms);
  • bacon
  • smoked meats;
  • baguette, french loaf or sesame buns;
  • fresh herbs (needed to make a gift).

Stages of work on a bouquet of sausages for men

Get in the supermarket the whole list of necessary products, focusing on the tastes of the birthday man. When preparing the bouquet, you can do only sausage, adding to it slices of grain bread, vegetables. The products that make up red peppers, tomatoes, sprigs of greens, and various types of cheese look great. When calculating the spent funds, it turns out that such a set took at least a thousand to one and a half thousand rubles.



Check that you have wrapping paper available (ideally you need several colors). Also can not do without twine, bright ribbons. After all, how the sausage composition will be packaged directly affects the first impression. Make sure the skewers do not peek out from under the paper. To disguise them, use the branches of greenery.

Knowing that a man is not indifferent to alcohol, as a complement to a bouquet of sausages and cheese You can purchase a bottle of your favorite drink. Alcohol is located in the center of the composition to be drawn, it should be framed by various options for snacks.

Ways, how exactly to prepare this bouquet, a lot can be invented. The main thing is that every sausage and all pieces of products are strung on skewers. After adding slices of baguette, fresh vegetables (this is done for decoration) sticks are fixed. To do this, you need tape. After the packaging is made out, the composition is wrapped with twine.



Bouquet decoration

If you want your beloved man, husband or colleague to be even more surprised, work hard on decorating a bouquet of sausages. Rosettes from ham and bacon will look great. They are made as follows: the strips are twisted in the form of a spiral onto a skewer, and a bud is formed. Thin slices of cheese are also suitable for making roses, bells, callas.

Do not be afraid to use any combination of products: let it be smoked meats, vegetables, green onion feathers, bottles with alcoholic drinks and flowers. Do not be afraid of experiments, because, as in any gift, it‚Äôs important not its value, but your careful attitude and desire to give joy to a loved one.

Useful Tips

Keep in mind that the resulting composition will weigh about one to two kilograms. This is pretty hard. And it will look bulky. Therefore, take a closer look at fixing foods, do not spare either scotch tape or twine, making a bouquet of sausages with your own hands.



If you could not find wooden sticks or skewers, use edible salt straws to fix the sausages. After tasting the main products, you can also taste it. By the way, you can use a regular newspaper as a wrapper.

Possessing self-confidence and a desire to make a birthday party nice, a bouquet of sausages can be prepared at home yourself. Let it be different from those that are on sale, but it will give a loved one also a piece of your soul.

These gifts are becoming increasingly popular, because, as a rule, there are not many options for men's gifts. Especially if the budget is limited. A small amount of money is required for the same present. But thanks to creativity, a bouquet of sausages will be remembered for a long time. And perhaps making such gifts will become one of your favorite hobbies and an additional source of income.
