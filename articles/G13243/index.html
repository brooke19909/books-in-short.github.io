Types of Permissions

Modern devices usually have a Full HD scan, that is, most models on store shelves are high-resolution video recorders. The higher this indicator (measured in pixels (px), the sharper the reproduced picture.





Scan types:

  • SD - 720x576 px.
  • HD - 1280x720 px.
  • Full HD - 1920x1080 px.
  • UHD - 3840x2160 px.

The last resolution of the DVR can be called exotic, as it is extremely rare. Such a sweep have expensive models capable of performing full-scale shooting. The most popular among motorists are DVRs with Full HD-matrix. At a cost they do not overtake conventional HD gadgets by far, so many users prefer to pay a little extra for the quality of the picture.
