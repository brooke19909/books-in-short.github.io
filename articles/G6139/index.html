  • A variety of options for outdoor activities.
  • A wide selection of excursion programs.

Sunny Beach and Golden Sand

Many tourists go to the Bulgarian coast, knowing that they are waiting for the famous Golden Sands of Bulgaria. And they are not mistaken in the search for comfort. You can organize a holiday on the first coastline, the location on the dunes will be especially interesting.

This area is located in the south of the resort, where another, no less interesting water park is located. Sunny Beach offers lovers of water sports and slides a choice of two options.

One more noisy and fun, for a colorful and active holiday of large companies. The second water park is calmer, there is no mini-zoo, but there are figures of ancient animals against which you can take a picture and thus capture your memories of a wonderful vacation.

According to the reviews of tourists who decided to stay in the dune area, this was a great choice to combine a relaxing holiday and active entertainment. In this area you can book both private apartments and time-tested hotels "Kotva", "Amphora". There are other accommodation options.

The advantage of a holiday in Bulgaria

If you are traveling with children, then Bulgaria is best for you: Sunny Beach, a water park, attractions, donkey riding, excellent cuisine will ensure the safe relaxation of your children.

It is advisable to purchase an excursion to the reserved places. If your trip takes place from May 20 to June 5, you can become a participant in the Rose Festival. This flower is the national symbol of Bulgaria, and Bulgarian rose oil is recognized world-wide by connoisseurs of perfumery products.

For adults, you can recommend visiting the attraction restaurants, which can not be seen anywhere else: Caligula, Pri Pri Bombata.
