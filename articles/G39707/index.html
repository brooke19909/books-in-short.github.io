Do-it-yourself seat-chair on a chair: a variety of shapes and colors

There are still a lot of yarn in different shades and textures, but is it about the same thickness? It is worth considering how to diversify the interior. And the assistant in this matter will be a new sidushka on a chair. With your own hands it can, for example, be crocheted.





Twisted Stripes Option

Its basis is a lot of narrow stripes. Even the beginning needlewoman will cope with this. It is enough to master the knitting of the main columns. The strips should be such that their length is enough for the seats on the chairs. Moreover, you need to make a small margin for weaving. The thickness of such strips should also not be very large.

Half of them must be fastened by tying a row of single crochet along their narrow edge. Then lay one of the free weaving with fastened ones. Place the second on other strips to make it look like a basket. And so on until the end.
Then tie the other three sides. It turns out a pretty sitter on a chair with her own hands.





Square with tassels

Its basis can be any pattern for a grandmother's square. Only the size of the seat on the chairs (crochet) should be appropriate. If you knit it in one layer, you will need thick yarn. From the one that is thinner, it is recommended to make mini-pads.

As a variant of the pattern, one that is placed on the diagram below is quite suitable. Since there are only 11 rows in it, the yarn will be thick. Although no one forbids using thin in several additions.




Shaggy tassels are suitable for decoration. They will need to be made from a small number of cut threads, the length of which is twice as long as that of the final brush. In the middle, fasten with a thread and knit a short chain with which it will be attached to the seat. Connect the two halves of the brush together and tie with a thread. It remains only to sew them into the corners. Such a knitted pillow-seat on a chair with your own hands can be an original gift.

In the form of a hexagon

It will require six parts to be tied. Their shape is a trapezoid. The lower and upper base of which are 37 and 18 cm, respectively. The height of the workpiece is 14.5 cm. For such a seat on a chair (hook), the patterns should be the simplest. For example, all rows can be made of connecting posts. To make the product denser, you can choose a crochet half crochet. In order to still have some kind of pattern, it is recommended to make two or three wide stripes of different colors. Moreover, the bottom looks better from a contrasting shade.

Then they need to be sewn. The side of the second trapezoid is connected to the upper base of the first. The same side of the third will be sewn to the second side of the first and upper base of the second. Thus, it should turn out that all the sides are stitched. The outer part of the seat is formed by the upper base of the trapezoid.

It remains to tie the whole seat on the chair with the same contrasting color. Finish the edge with an openwork pattern around the entire perimeter. And the seat-maker on the chair with her own hands is ready.





On the round seat

The principle of its manufacture is based on a circular pattern. So once knitted rugs in the villages. Why not tie a bright and pretty little seat?

It would be good to choose yarn of one thickness and many different shades. And each new row is knitted in a different color. It will be bright. She will go to the kitchen, made in any color scheme.

You need to start crocheting the seats on the chairs (patterns) with a small ring of air loops. Their number depends on the thickness of the yarn. Then tie 12 single crochet into a ring. This is the first row.

In the 2nd - from each vertex, two double crochet columns are required. The middle of the seat should be fairly dense, so there will be no openwork patterns yet.

The 3rd row consists of triple double crochet knitted in the spaces between the double columns of the previous row. It turns out a beautiful pattern and a dense pattern.

In the 4th row, you should knit double crochet between each triple of the previous row. And also from each column of the three. You should get a row of slender columns.

The 5th row consists of the same columns. Only in every fourth loop should they be knitted in two. This increase is necessary to expand the circle.

In the 6th row, three double crochet needles should be knitted in every third loop. The separation between them will be one air loop. Also complete the 7th row. A fan of columns is located in the arch.

The 8th row consists entirely of double crochet stitches from each loop. In arches from air loops of columns there should be 2.

9th - skipping one loop, knit in each second two columns. 10th - repeats the 9th, the columns come from the arches. The 11th row is made up of single crochet stitches in each loop.

12th - repeats the 5th. 13th row - repeat of the 11th. If this diameter is enough, then finish the job. For a larger seat, choose a pattern from columns with a crochet or without.





Openwork of flowers

Choose any flower pattern that the needlewoman can do. Tie so many copies so that they are enough for the whole seat on a chair or stool. They can be the same color or two or three. And someone will make them all shades of the rainbow.

Then you should connect all the flowers with each other. They can simply be sewn or fastened when tying. For ease of attachment to the seat, it is recommended to tie several ties of sufficient length.
