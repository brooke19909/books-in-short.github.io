Hotel Pimnara Boutique Hotel 4 *, Thailand, Phuket: photos, reviews

Just a 10-minute walk from the water's edge on the most popular Phuket beach - Patong, the comfortable Pimnara Boutique Hotel 4 * is located. Every year, the hotel opens its doors to hundreds of travelers who want to have a good time. In the velvet season, rooms cannot be found in the hotel. You should not be surprised at this, because Patong Beach is famous for its white sand and noisy nightlife.

Where is the hotel

The popularity of living here among tourists is largely due to its convenient location. Phuket Hotel Pimnara Boutique Hotel 4 * is located directly in the Jungle Zeilon shopping complex, which is built in the center of Patong.

Bangla Road District is 250 meters away - you can get to it in just 2-3 minutes. It is here that the most popular and fashionable disco bars, restaurants, entertainment centers, night clubs and go-go clubs are concentrated.

About 600 meters from the sea, it will take 5-7 minutes to get to the hotel - the hotel is located on the 3rd coastline.

Phuket International Airport - at a distance of 40 km (the road will take an hour and a half).

General information about Pimnara Boutique Hotel 4 *

The hotel is located in a new building, built in 2011. In 2013, the premises were renovated, so guests can comfortably stay in designer rooms, and their mood will not be overshadowed by old plumbing and broken furniture.

The hotel consists of two 2-story buildings. Due to its modest size, Pimnara Boutique Hotel 4 * in Phuket is considered a mini-hotel - its number of rooms is only 67 rooms.

Services

The hotel has a parking area. You can use it at no extra charge.

Reception is open 24 hours a day. Here you can not only settle in and get all the necessary information, but also take advantage of additional services. At the reception there is a left-luggage office and safe deposit boxes for valuables.

Shuttle service is available. Tourists can book transport from / to the airport directly at the hotel. This service is paid.

There is a laundry room. Vacationers can use the services of washing, cleaning and ironing.

The hotel has two elevators.

Air conditioning is available in all public areas.

Wi-Fi is free - it can be used in the lobby and in the rooms.

For business travelers, fax and photocopying services are available.

In Phuket Pimnara Boutique Hotel 4 *, you can conduct a wedding - there are all conditions for this.

Tickets or excursions to popular attractions can be purchased at the reception.

Pimnara Boutique Hotel 4 Patong features interior design. All rooms are made in a modern style using high-quality finishing materials.

Beach

For those who are planning a beach holiday in Thailand, both Pimnara Boutique Hotel 4 * and Patong in Phuket are ideal. Accommodation in this hotel allows you to live very close to the coast and at the same time save on a room.

The beach is 300 meters from the hotel, and therefore it is attributed to the third coastline. It will take very little time on the road - no more than 10 minutes. The hotel does not have its own beach, but tourists can always go to the city beach. The sand in Patong is shallow, the entrance to the water is gentle. A married couple with young children can relax here.

The beach is well equipped for relaxation: there are changing cabins, there are sun umbrellas and sunbeds, however, you will need to pay for the rent. There is a bar on the beach where guests are offered mineral water, carbonated drinks and juice. If you wish, you can also have a little junk food here.

A little further down the coast there are "wild" beaches. There are almost no vacationers here, so they are perfect for those who do not like the noisy crowds of tourists. True, these beaches have one big drawback - they are not at all equipped for recreation. There are no changing cabins, deck chairs, umbrellas and other amenities. In addition, getting there is better by car.

  • riding on yachts or small boats;

Phuket Attractions

Patong Resort is not only suitable for swimming in the azure waters and sipping a refreshing cocktail. There are many attractions in the vicinity. To see at least a couple of them for their entire stay in Phuket is simply necessary:

  • Ton Sai Waterfall. It is the second largest among the waterfalls of Phuket. In the hot season, this is just a large stream, however, with the onset of the rainy season, Ton Sai turns into a real bubbling stream. In the shady places arbors are equipped here, so you can enjoy the natural splendor without haste.
  • Big Buddha. This attraction is one of those that is certainly worth seeing with your own eyes. Buddha statue measuring 40 m in height and 25.5 m in width is equipped with viewing platforms.
  • Sirinat is a national park. The area reaches 8000 ha. There are tropical jungles, a water park, a temple, a cafe, three sandy beaches and bungalows for tourists.
  • Oceanarium. This entertainment will appeal to adults and children. Behind thick glass you can see a huge number of sea inhabitants - from very small to huge sharks.
  • Museum of sea shells. The exhibits here are hundreds of different sea shells collected around the world. The most valuable of them are 380 million years old fossils, a left-handed shell and a giant specimen weighing 250 kg.
  • The show "Fantasy". This amazing performance was first presented in 1998. Since then, it has received several awards and immense love of the audience. The show is attended by 400 people and 40 elephants. A visit to this place will turn into a real holiday, which will leave an impression in the soul of Phuket guests for a long time.

All of the above is only a small part of what tourists can see in the hot hospitable Thailand. Entertainment is for absolutely everyone: for connoisseurs of excursion programs, couples with children and lovers just wander through the streets of a foreign country and feel the color of these places.
