Hotel "Premium Blue Lagoon". Hurghada. Description and feedback

The luxurious Premium Blue Lagoon Hotel (Hurghada) is located in the very center of the resort town near the pedestrian promenade. The beautifully decorated territory of the hotel complex fully conveys the unique national flavor of Egypt. This is a great vacation spot for youth and families with children.

Description

The five-star Premium Blue Lagoon Hotel (Hurghada) was built in 2007. The hotel was renovated in 2013. It is located 7 km from the airport and 10 km from the tourist center - Hurghada.

Residence

Premium Blue Lagoon (Hurghada) has 366 comfortable rooms located around a huge pool. They are equipped with satellite TV, smoke detectors, air conditioning, safe, small bar, telephone, bathroom and terrace. Standard rooms are designed for two adults and 2 toddlers. Apartment "Syut" (area 70 sq. M), consists of a bedroom and a living room. They also have two balconies with lagoon views. The foundation of the hotel has superior rooms with a living room and a bedroom. These apartments, designed for four people, are equipped with a shower room and one balcony.

Nutrition





Premium Blue Lagoon (Hurghada) serves all-inclusive guests 24 hours a day. The main restaurant serves buffet meals three times a day . Local alcoholic drinks and various soft drinks are provided free of charge. There is also an Italian and fish restaurant, which must be pre-booked. The hotel has four bars, where tourists are offered drinks, sweets and light snacks.

Relaxation

The animation team of the Premium Blue Lagoon Hotel (Hurghada) organizes daily sports and recreational activities for vacationers, such as aerobics, water gymnastics, beach volleyball, polo, darts, etc. The hotel also has a tennis court, handball field, mini billiards, diving and fitness center, windsurfing, playground, wellness center with jacuzzi, steam and sauna, and massage room. In the evenings, an entertainment disco program is held for holidaymakers.

Infrastructure

The complex has a conference room, lobby, currency exchange, parking, laundry, shops and dry cleaning.

Beach

Own sandy. Located 400 meters from the hotel. Towels, sunbeds, umbrellas and mattresses are provided on the beach for free.

For babies

An animation program is held for children during the day. The restaurants serve special meals for toddlers. There is a room for drawing.
