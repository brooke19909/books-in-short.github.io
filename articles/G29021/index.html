Eco-hotel "Levant": description and reviews of visitors

Summer is the time for vacations and the realization of the most incredible plans. Summer is the time for long-awaited trips to the warm sea.

For many years now, the Southern coast of Crimea has been calling and attracting tourists who want to get the best from a beach holiday: mild climate, clear sunny weather, special air with the smell of the sea, mountains and forests, a huge number of attractions, bewitching landscapes, as well as moderate prices. The unofficial capital of the Crimean peninsula is the city of Yalta. Here the swimming season begins from the very beginning of summer and lasts until mid-autumn.

In Yalta, the doors of many hotels and hotels of various levels of comfort are open for tourists, among which the Levant eco-hotel stands out as the first hotel in the city that pays special attention to the natural environment and offers its guests a real healthy stay.

About the hotel

Eco-hotel "Levant" (Yalta, Crimea) is a modern three-star hotel with a unique atmosphere of warmth and comfort on the Black Sea coast. The institution will surround its guests with warmth and care at any time of the year. The eco-hotel "Levant" is proud of its own beach fifteen meters from the building, a calm park area and excellent infrastructure. A 5-minute walk will take guests from the hotel's silence to the city promenade with its many restaurants, bars and other entertainment venues.





The main feature of the hotel, which remains in the memory of almost all tourists staying here on vacation, is that each of its rooms has a panoramic window with amazing views of the sea distance and the Yalta landscape.

The services offered by the hotel are varied and able to satisfy even the most capricious guests:

  • housing stock with rooms of different levels of comfort;
  • a restaurant with an outdoor terrace and a summer beach bar;
  • equipped beach covered with small pebbles, and a closed promenade;
  • outdoor sports simulators;
  • SPA-center;
  • a playground and other services for the youngest guests;
  • educational excursions and other entertainments.

Nutrition

Eco-hotel "Levant" (Yalta) offers comprehensive meals in a restaurant (guesthouse, half board and breakfast only), which is included in the price of the hotel. The restaurant serves European and local dishes. Several types of menus have been developed:

  • balanced eco-menu for those who really care about the health of their body;
  • barbecue menu for real fans of aromatic dishes prepared on charcoal;
  • special children's menu for the youngest visitors.




In the restaurant, in addition to the main hall, there is a chic open terrace in the shade, which offers excellent views of the coast of Yalta.

Beach

Eco-hotel "Levant" (Crimea) is one of the few hotels on the peninsula that have their own beach area, which has all the conditions for a full beach holiday. Here for vacationers there are sun loungers, umbrellas and awnings to protect from bright sunlight, showers, changing cabins, and a toilet. A friendly team of experienced lifeguards is working on the beach, a medical post is functioning, and a bar is open.





To diversify the leisure of little vacationers, professional animators will help in a specially designated area for games.

Attractions nearby

To make the vacation truly unforgettable and introduce its guests to the unique beauty of Crimea, the eco-hotel Levant offers four- and eight-hour tours of the peninsula as part of a group or individually with a guide. There is also a unique opportunity to visit vibrant sights without moving far from the hotel:
