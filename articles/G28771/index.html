Where is Stupino, Moscow Region

Stupino is the average city in Russia by population. On its territory there are more than 40 industrial enterprises, including manufacturers of oral hygiene products ROCS, metallurgical company JSC "SMK" and a factory for the manufacture of aircraft. Not surprisingly, a query about where Stupino is located is quite popular on the Internet.

Geography

The city is located 99 km south of Moscow and is the regional center of the Stupino district of the Moscow region. Its area is 35.5 km 2 . The rivers flowing through its territory: Oka, Sitnya, Kashirka and Kremnitsa. In the south, Stupino borders on the city of regional significance - Kashira. The population is 84 thousand people.

Stupino District is located in western Russia in the center of the zone of mixed and deciduous forests. The climate is temperate continental. The region is valuable for hunters and fishers. In the forest land of the Stupino district there are hares, foxes and game birds. Perch, pikeperch, pike and crucian carp live in the Oka waters.

History

All the settlements that make up the district were known at the beginning of the 16th century. Where Stupino is located, in 1578 the village of Kashirsky Uyezd was located in the possession of the Belopesotsky Monastery. In 1931, the seat was chosen by the Communist Party secretariat for the construction of a train factory. The territory was named the working village of Electric Locomotive. Around the new enterprise grew a school, hospital and a bakery. But it ceased to exist, and construction sites were used to establish new factories for the production of aircraft industry products.

In 1938, the village of Elektrovoz received the status of a city and was again named Stupino. In the Moscow region, after the Great Patriotic War, the territory belonging to the district expanded. It included nearby state farms; concrete and plastic plants were built.

Memorable places and infrastructure

The main attraction is the Trinity Belopesotsky convent, founded in the XV century. The cathedral includes the churches of St. Sergius of Radonezh, Nicholas the Wonderworker, the Beheading of John the Baptist. Significant value for history are the white-stone gravestones of the 17th century, which are in the necropolis.

Today, the area where Stupino is located is clean and green. The city has a developed infrastructure. The Palace of Culture, a club, libraries, an art gallery, and schools operate here. Since 1966, a branch of the Moscow Aviation and Technology Institute has been training specialists in aviation, rocket and space equipment in Stupino.

Industry

Unlike residents of other cities of the region, residents of the district do not work in Moscow. Most of the people have a permanent job in Stupino, where there are more than 40 enterprises. Salaries in large factories at the level of the capital of Russia, and housing prices and food prices are moderate.

In Stupino there are foreign factories for the production of Mars confectionery, Campina dairy products, Kimberly-Clark cosmetics, Italian Zambati wallpaper, ROCS teeth hygiene products. Stupino Metallurgical Company SMK JSC produces unique parts and components for aircraft engines. There are also many factories for the production of materials for repairs.
