Nasa Flora (Turkey / Antalya) - photos, prices and reviews of tourists

Antalya is one of the most popular resort cities in Turkey. Here you can always find a large number of tourists from all over the world, but this place is most popular among Russian tourists. Antalya is rich in historical monuments. The nature is amazing in its beauty, which will not leave indifferent any person who has visited the resort at least once. But do not forget the main purpose of the visit of tourists to this city. It consists in relaxing off the coast of the Mediterranean Sea. If you have already decided on a trip to this wonderful resort, then you should choose a good hotel. In this regard, Nasa Flora can serve as an excellent and inexpensive option.



How to get to the hotel

Hotel Nasa Flora (Turkey) itself is located in the city of Antalya, not far from the Mediterranean coast. Therefore, it will not be difficult to get to it. All that is required of you is to take a ticket from your city to Antalya, and then everything is very simple. Upon arrival, you can take a taxi or take the bus to the hotel. But if you are afraid to get lost, that is, an option with a transfer directly from the airport to the hotel. This is a Nasa Flora service at an additional cost. The flight from Moscow to Antalya will take about two and a half hours, the ticket costs about 300 dollars.

Services provided by the hotel

The service that you can experience for yourself while living at Nasa Flora Hotel is very diverse. Among the services there are both free ones that are included in the price of your room, and those for which you will have to pay extra. One of these services is the ability to use a safe in the room. This service is very convenient, because you have the opportunity to put all the most valuable that you brought with you to Antalya, to the safe, and not to worry about its safety. Also, as an additional service, you can call the laundry service, which will serve you quickly and efficiently. If you want to rent a car, you can do it without leaving the hotel. At your choice you will be offered a large number of machines of various models and cost. In case there is a need for a currency exchange, this can also always be done directly at the hotel. The rate of the Turkish lira to the dollar is not too different from the exchange rate at the banks of Antalya. If you want to take a sightseeing bus around the city, then for this you will also need to pay an additional amount of money. The free service at the Nasa Flora 3 * hotel, reviews of which are only positive, is wireless Internet. It is also encouraging that its speed is at a very good level, which allows you to freely communicate with the people you need or simply watch videos online.

Of course, the price may seem quite high for a Nasa Flora with two stars, but these expenses are justified in full.

Sports and recreation

This hotel provides its guests with the opportunity to relax with board games or billiards. Fans of cycling can be happy, because in the hotel there is the possibility of renting them. This is very convenient, because you can cycle through the whole of Antalya by bicycle and enjoy the sights of this amazing resort town of Turkey. Nasa Flora also has an outdoor pool, open only during the spring and summer. The water in this pool is always clean, because the hotel staff tries to change it as often as possible. If you have no desire to relax and sunbathe by the pool, you can always go to the shores of the Mediterranean Sea. And you can do this in some 2-3 minutes, because the coastline is located in the immediate vicinity of Nasa Flora Otel 2 *, the reviews on the beach of which are mostly positive. On the beach, you can not only sunbathe and swim, but also try your hand at water skiing or banana. These types of entertainment are very popular among numerous tourists of Antalya. You can also go to a designated place to go diving and see all the beauties of the Mediterranean underwater world.

But tourists will have to pay for lunch or dinner. In the bar you can find a large assortment of various alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks. Antalya - what to see?

Having been in Antalya, you can do a lot of things that will be remembered for a long time. First of all, of course, it is better to go on a city tour and enjoy the many historical monuments that have been preserved since the time of Ancient Greece. Be sure to take a ticket to the boat and ride on the Mediterranean Sea along the coast of Antalya. This will leave an indelible impression of the beauty of the city. If you wish, you can go to the largest water park called Aqualand. Evening Antalya is also very beautiful. At this time, singing fountains begin to work on all its streets, attracting the attention of numerous guests of the Turkish resort.



Nasa Flora Travel Reviews

Almost every tourist who shared his impressions about the rest at the Nasa Flora hotel spoke about him only well. The main advantages of the hotel are its proximity to the airport and the beach. Also, many guests of this hotel note fast and at the same time cheap internet. Many tourists were happy with how well the staff treated them, helping to solve any problems and issues related to settlement.

As a result, this hotel can be called a good place to settle in and then not regret it. It has everything you need for a comfortable stay, so if you decide to go to Antalya, then this hotel is worth it to be chosen.
