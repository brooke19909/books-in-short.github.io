We leave for Yuzhno-Sakhalinsk: museum of local lore

Once in Yuzhno-Sakhalinsk, the local history museum must be visited. It is there that in a short time you can learn all about the amazing island on the edge of Eurasia.





Museum past

The history of the museum begins in 1896, when the governor of Sakhalin D. Merkazin organizes an exhibition where exhibits are collected by the forces of local administration officials and exiled intellectuals. Botany, zoology, geology, archeology - exhibits representing almost all sciences found a place in the halls.

In 1905, Sakhalin was occupied by Japanese troops. Museum valuable, and sometimes priceless, materials disappear without a trace.

When the island was divided into Japanese and Russian territories, local history museums reappear. On Russian soil - in the city of Aleksandrov, on Japanese soil - in the city of Toyoharu, or Yuzhno-Sakhalinsk.

On the map and in reality, Sakhalin unites under the Russian flag only after 1945, while the funds of the Alexander Museum are transported to Yuzhno-Sakhalinsk, where the regional museum of local lore is organized. Therefore, the date of creation of the regional museum of local lore is considered to be 1953. Excursions in Yuzhno-Sakhalinsk necessarily include a visit to the museum.

Walk through the halls: exposition

More than 170 thousand exhibits are stored in collections and exhibited in the halls of the Sakhalin State Regional Museum of Local Lore. The museum collection includes a variety of collections:

  • documents, photographs and paintings;
  • postcards, stamps and rare books;
  • objects of arts and crafts and everyday life;
  • paleontological exhibits;
  • archaeological;
  • military collection.




How Sakhalin was researched and what discoveries were made, what constitutes the animal and plant world of this unique island, how its ecosystem developed - the answers to these questions can be found in the halls of the museum.

The museum space is located on 3 floors:

  1. Ground floor. A retrospective of local geology and fauna of the seas of the Far East is presented.
  2. Ground floor. Expositions of the animal and plant world, as well as the inhabitants of the ocean depths, are offered for inspection. A very interesting room with materials representing the life of the indigenous peoples of Sakhalin. Here you can see the pride of the regional museum - ancient rarities belonging to such peoples as Nivkhs, Ainu, Oroks. On the same floor is the exhibition complex.
  3. Second floor. Items tell about the development of the island, such a thing as hard labor, the pre-war era, Japanese dominion and modernity.

Street exposure

Having come to the museum of local lore in Yuzhno-Sakhalinsk, it is worth taking the time to examine the exposition on the street. The main building is surrounded by a park, which houses interesting historical sites. Walking, you can see Russian and Japanese guns (guns, tanks) the year before and last centuries, the Garden of Sakhalin herbs attracts attention.

Only in this museum you can see a unique collection of reconstructed buildings:

  • the building of the Japanese Pavilion School and the tea house;
  • prison cell;
  • the bureaucratic cabinet of the times when Sakhalin was hard labor;
  • Nivkh village - winter and summer dwelling;
  • Ainu buildings;
  • bear log house;
  • flake.

Lecture Hall of the South Sakhalin Museum of Local Lore

The Sakhalin Regional Museum of Local Lore is not only a collection of rarities, but also live communication. Employees of museums and scientific institutions, including the Far Eastern Branch of the Russian Academy of Sciences, as well as local historians, environmentalists regularly give interesting lectures on a variety of topics.





Thanks to such lectures, those who have visited the Yuzhno-Sakhalinsk Museum of Local Lore acquire invaluable knowledge about their unique region. In the lecture you can learn about the nature of the tsunami and the technology of taxidermy of fish, plant life on Sakhalin and night birds, thermal springs and mud volcanoes of the region, the archaeological past of Sakhalin and the history of gardening.

Museum - Center of Culture

For Yuzhno-Sakhalinsk the museum of local lore is a place where it is pleasant to spend a day off with the whole family, learning something new and talking. Quests and family excursions, film screenings and lectures, master classes and thematic events attract many visitors.

How did the indigenous peoples of Sakhalin greet each other ? How to make a card for Santa Claus? Who lives in the rivers of Sakhalin? What are taiga bikes talking about? These and many other questions can be answered by coming to the museum.

We go to the museum: how to get

It is easy to find Yuzhno-Sakhalinsk on the map, it is even easier to get to the museum.

It is located in the city center, on Kommunistichesky Prospekt, 29. If you use public transport, you should get out at the stop "House of Officers", which is located nearby.

Not far from the museum of local lore there are several more remarkable museum objects:

  • Museum of Chekhov's book ;
  • Art Museum;
  • History of railways;
  • Victory Complex;
  • Museum of the Bear.

How the museum works

To visit the Museum of Local Lore in Yuzhno-Sakhalinsk, you should familiarize yourself with the work schedule and choose a convenient time for the tour.





The museum is open to visitors every day from 110 to 18 hours, and on Saturday you can stay in the museum until 20 hours. The open-air exhibition has the same work schedule.

According to tradition, the day off in the museum space is Monday. When going to the museum of local lore, one must remember that the ticket office stops working half an hour before the museum closes.

Museum trip cost

A visit to the museum will not be a heavy financial burden. After all, an admission ticket for an adult costs only 100 rubles, and for children under 18 years old admission is free.

The museum has a program according to which various categories of citizens of the Russian Federation pass for free to the museum:

  • large families;
  • war veterans;
  • military servicemen and others.

For pensioners a ticket will cost 30 p.

For viewing museum exhibits as part of thematic excursions, you will have to pay 50-100 p.
