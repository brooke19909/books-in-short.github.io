Park-hotel "Berendey" (Tambov): rest, prices and reviews of vacationers

Have you noticed how wildlife has a positive effect on humans? Once you come to the forest or find yourself near a pond, you begin to feel calm and tranquility surrounded by sounds of the environment. Truly relaxing away from densely populated cities and highways. That is why the majority of hotel complexes and health resorts began to be erected away from noisy megalopolises so that people could completely surrender to a state of rest and euphoria.





The Berendey country hotel (Tambov) is no exception, it is located right in the middle of a relict pine forest, next to a river. At the same time, it is only 20 km away from the busy city life. In this paradise, a person comes to a state of equilibrium to the sound of the surf, bewitching birds singing and the "breath" of the wind. Nature is a cradle for you and me, which you don‚Äôt want to leave.

Territory

Under constant video surveillance and security is the Park-Hotel Berendey (Tambov). The territory is spacious, clean and comfortable, fenced and planted with numerous green shrubs. Courteous and hospitable staff strictly monitors the order, thanks to this there are no fights and conflicts. For the convenience and comfort of guests, the territory is constantly treated with special means from various insects. For private cars there is free parking.

Accommodation





The recreation center "Berendey" (Tambov) consists of 20 comfortable two-story villas with all the necessary amenities and communications. There are no failures with water supply and heating. In the cold season, radiators work. For comfortable living, heated floors and water heaters are installed.

All cottages have a private bathroom. Only one married couple is settled in the house, so no one and nothing will disturb the peace. Even insects will not penetrate your cozy "nest", because mosquito nets are on the windows. This is especially true for couples with children. On the ground floor you will find an entrance hall, a living room and a bathroom with a shower.

The second floor consists of a spacious bedroom. In addition to the modern interior and upholstered furniture, the houses have household appliances with cutlery, as well as a TV with satellite channels. It is allowed to bring small pets (warning in advance).

Nutrition





Three meals a day according to the European buffet system are included in the price. When the Berendey Hotel (Tambov) is full, meals are served in portions. At the same time, the cuisine is varied, quality and tasty. Tables burst with food. If desired, you can ask for diet food. The staff is very attentive to children, so if your baby is choosy in food, then they will do something special for him. The restaurant invites you to try the delicacies. Connoisseurs of quality and expensive alcohol are waiting for a bar.

Coastline

The hotel complex "Berendey" (Tambov) is within walking distance of the river. Therefore, in the summer, the main attraction for visitors is swimming. The coast is sandy. Every day it is cleaned from debris and stones. The road to the beach runs through a picturesque bridge, which is illuminated at night.

Free sun loungers and sunshades are at guests' disposal. There is a volleyball net on the shore , rental of sports equipment for winter and summer entertainment (catamarans, boats, ATVs, fishing equipment, skiing). Not far from the river is a holy spring with drinking water.

Leisure





The main advantage of the tourist base "Berendey" (Tambov) is the ability to retire, escape from the intense rhythm of life, to be at peace, while enjoying all the benefits of civilization, which is very important for a comfortable stay. There are no noisy parties and discos, but every evening animated shows and competitions are organized.

On the well-groomed territory it is allowed to grill kebabs or cook other delicious barbecue delicacies. What rest can be without a Russian bath with real brooms? All this you will find in the hotel. For those who can not imagine their life without sports, a gym is provided.

It offers guests a spacious tennis court and sports fields. There are billiards, darts, paintball and air hockey. You can always interestingly and cheerfully organize your leisure time. In winter, ski runs work. The guesthouse has a tour desk. Tourists are offered walking, cycling, water and car tours in the local area.

An experienced guide will introduce tourists to the culture, history and attractions of the region. Celebrations are also held here, including children's parties and birthday parties. Experienced staffs organize a top-level party with clowns, animators and jokes.

For children





For kids up to the age of three, accommodation is absolutely free. Qualitatively thought out children's leisure. On the street there is a playground with a slide and a swing. There is a play area where children can communicate, draw and sculpt under the supervision of a teacher (only in summer). High chairs are provided for feeding in the dining room of the Berendey Hotel (Tambov).

How to get to your destination?

By rail: get a ticket for the evening Moscow-Tambov flight and arrive at the base early in the morning.

By your own car: drive along the Moscow-Volgograd motorway (450 km) or drive south-east of Moscow to the Tambov ring road. Travel time is about 6 hours.

This concludes the description of such a popular hotel. I would like to note that a worthy alternative to expensive overseas resorts is the recreation center "Berendey" (Tambov). Reviews of vacationers say that this place fascinates from the first minutes. Every little thing here is carefully thought out. The living conditions are simply amazing.

Therefore, they often come here with infants. Pleasantly surprised by the rate - the night is within 2000 p. (with food). Many tourists praised the professionalism, attentiveness and sincerity of the staff. After such a fruitful vacation, you return home inspired, full of strength and new ideas.
