Hotel "Juliana", Yevpatoriya: description and reviews

If you want to enjoy clean sea water and comfortable living conditions, then in Yevpatoriya, located on the western coast of Crimea, you will like the Juliana Hotel. Evpatoria is a famous resort, where a huge number of vacationers gather annually. And, of course, here you will find everything for a memorable vacation.





Juliana Hotel (Yevpatoriya): description

The hotel complex "Juliana" is located in the historical part of Yevpatoriya, in its resort area. The sea from the hotel is at a distance of 400 meters. The hotel was opened for vacationers in 2005. Nearby, within walking distance, you will find many attractions for yourself:

  • cafes and restaurants;
  • night clubs;
  • aquapark;
  • dolphinarium;
  • large clean beach.

The hotel has a closed area, guarded around the clock. There is a large indoor parking, a summer pool and a cafe-bar. Also, the hotel itself will offer you spa treatments and the services of a professional massage therapist. Entrance to the beach is free for those who live in Julian. Guest Accommodation

  • one-room double junior suite with a large family bed and a sofa bed - the price for a room is from 2500 to 4800 rubles;
  • two-room triple "family comfort" - from 5000 to 7000 rubles per day, depending on the time of year;
  • one-room double suite - the cost of the room is from 4000 to 5000 rubles;
  • two-room double suite (the room has a kitchen) - the price is from 5000 to 6000 rubles per day;
