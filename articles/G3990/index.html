Key Specifications

We continue to consider the Canon MAXIFY MB2340 MFP. The characteristics of this device are pretty good. The printer uses inkjet technology and has a piezoelectric head. The monthly resource is 15,000 pages. This means that he can easily cope with the amount of work in a small office. Print speed depends on the type of document. Monochrome MFP text prints at 23 pages per minute. A color image is printed at a speed of 15 pages per minute. Not a bad result. The first page after switching on is released after 7 seconds. The print resolution is 1200 dots in length and width. There is an option of double-sided printing. And there is a very good scanner with a resolution of 1200 DPI. The copier does its job even without a computer. Also, the device has a built-in fax. Canon MAXIFY MB2340 photo sends, receives and prints in very high quality. There is also a Wi-Fi transmitter with the option of connecting to cloud-based document stores. The all-in-one has a 500-sheet paper tray and boasts low power consumption. And he has a special silent mode. Ideal for home use. Also, the MFP can print directly from a flash drive. Now consider the reviews of those who have already purchased this unit.
