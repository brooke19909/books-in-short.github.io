Guest houses in Abkhazia on the seashore: overview, description, specifications and reviews

Abkhazia is an ideal climate, clear sea, unique fauna and flora. Abkhazia is a delicious cuisine and hotels by the sea. Abkhazia is a lot of historical and architectural attractions. Abkhazia as if specially created for the next vacation.

Wonderful vacation spot

Resorts in sunny Abkhazia have been loved and popular with Russian citizens since the days of the Soviet Union. Rest here has always been associated with many positive memories and vivid photographs. Today, beautiful Gagra, calm Pitsunda, picturesque Gadauta, sunny New Athos and the greenery of Sukhum have become even closer and more accessible than in the old days. To visit Abkhazia, Russian citizens do not need a foreign passport. Entry into the territory of the republic is carried out according to an internal document of a Russian citizen.





Holidays in Abkhazia offer accommodation for every taste and budget: motels, pensions, rest houses, hotels, guest houses or just a bed in a room with amenities on the street. Recently, guest houses in Abkhazia on the seashore have been especially popular with tourists. These are ordinary private buildings in which guests have rooms with private facilities. It offers organized meals, and there is an equipped kitchen for self-cooking. In addition, the owners of guest houses are trying to diversify the leisure of vacationers, offering tours of the country, boat trips, fishing and even spearfishing.

When is the best time to go?

The beach season lasts in Abkhazia from May to September. But at the end of spring, the water in the sea is still quite cool. At this time, lovers of excursions and fresh air often come. The sea becomes truly warm only in June, and July-August are considered the peak of tourist activity in Abkhazia. Sometimes it‚Äôs hard to find a free spot on popular beaches. Prices for housing, food, entertainment at this time increase. By September, people are moving away and becoming much calmer. The sea is still warm. In addition, a lot of fruit is ripening. From mid-September, days and nights become cooler, often it rains. The beach season is gradually coming to an end.

Sukhum

To make your vacation full of impressions and become not burdensome for the family budget, you can choose guest houses in Abkhazia on the seashore for accommodation. Unlike metropolitan cities of other countries, in Sukhum you can safely stay in the private sector, which is represented here not by stone boxes, but by cozy private houses with a green garden. Almost all guest houses are well located not only relative to the sea, but also the beautiful center, as well as the urban infrastructure.





On the one hand, rest in mini-hotels and hotels of Abkhazia (Sukhumi) is silence and almost no noise caused by the hectic resort life. And on the other - within walking distance are numerous cafes with music and dancing, restaurants, souvenir shops, shopping malls, the central market. Also, beach vacations in the capital of Abkhazia can be diversified by leisurely walks along the promenade in the shade of cedars and oleanders, visits to the monkey nursery and botanical garden.

New Athos

Rest houses, pensions and hotels by the sea (Abkhazia, New Athos) provide accommodation for tourists for every taste and with a wide variety of needs. There are many attractive offers from hotel owners in the private sector. Thus, if the purpose of the trip is not only to have a great time, but also to save a little, then the latter option will be preferable. Guest houses in Abkhazia on the seashore, as a rule, are landscaped and have all the amenities necessary for a comfortable stay. In the shared kitchen, you can cook either yourself or eat with the hostess. In either case, the food will be exclusively homemade.





Gadauta

Gadauta is a rather young and less well-known resort than other cities of Abkhazia. It began to develop only in Soviet times. Today, local residents are happy to turn their homes into guest houses, which in most cases are located right by the sea. It creates ideal conditions for enjoying peace, tranquility and a free beach. The urban infrastructure has not yet received much development, but the resort is small, so all the necessary entertainment is within walking distance.

Pitsunda

A house in Abkhazia on the seashore in the summer was the dream of a Soviet man. But to get a ticket to the resort of Pitsunda in those days was almost impossible, so many tourists went to the savage Abkhazia.





Today, little has changed. Pitsunda is still the same small town with a protected pine grove along the sea, clear transparent waters and a sandy beach. Most of the houses in the city are private. Pitsunda is still loved by many tourists. During their stay at the resort, families rent a separate room, and large companies rent a guest house. The accommodation is quite comfortable, as the necessary amenities have been created.

Gagra

Today, the twenty-kilometer sea coast of Gagra is the most popular place in Abkhazia. Here guests are offered motels, pensions, mini-hotels, hotels and holiday homes. There are good options in the private sector. Abkhazia, Gagra, guest houses on the seashore will dream of you many more days after returning home. You will remember beautiful parks, clean air and hospitable hosts. In Gagra, it is gradually becoming a tradition to offer tourists for rent not only a room with amenities, but also housing with a European-style renovation and additional services (for example, meals, cleaning and sightseeing).





Fans of a comfortable stay can find a park hotel in Abkhazia on the seashore with a swimming pool and book a room there.

The benefits of relaxing in guest houses

The private sector is a lot of housing options on various conditions. The main advantage of renting from the owner is the ability to choose the remoteness of the apartment from the sea coast. With a child, you can rent a guest house in Abkhazia on the seashore, then you will not have to go to the beach far. Having bought a ticket to a boarding house or sanatorium, a vacationer receives regular meals in the dining room, visits his beach, can participate in events organized by cultural workers and adheres to the regime established by the institution.

At the same time, in the private sector, a person is free to set his daily routine without depriving himself of late evening walks along the seashore or early water procedures. He can determine the place and time of eating, independently organize his leisure. Many guest houses have a cozy home environment with all the necessary, pleasant little things: internet, dishes, appliances and household appliances. In addition, a guest house in Abkhazia on the seashore will cost a tourist much cheaper in comparison with the offer in a boarding house of similar comfort.





When is the best time to book?

The most reliable and convenient option for both the guest and the owner is earlier booking. Tourists like to stay in the private sector of Abkhazia, so in the season upon arrival to find suitable comfortable accommodation by the sea is quite difficult. You should start browsing through the various options a few months before the start of the trip. In this case, tourists are given more choice of accommodation, among which the optimal number will be much easier to find.

If you have a reservation, then, having arrived in a city on the coast, you can do without the services of an agent for the selection of housing and not look for a free room on your own, looking into all guest houses with heavy suitcases. As a rule, in order to reserve the option you like in advance, the tourist must make an advance payment (10-20 percent of the total cost of renting a house). This is a common practice that provides some guarantees to the lessee and lessor.

Cost of living

The cost of rental housing in Abkhazia is affected by:

  • Seasonality At the height of summer, you will have to pay a little more for accommodation. In October, the cost of housing is the lowest.
  • Comfort The more amenities tourists are offered, the higher the room rate will be.
  • The distance to the Russian border. The closer to the Federation, the more expensive housing.
  • Remoteness from the sea. The smaller the distance to the sea, the higher the cost of living.

Thus, it turns out that the most expensive for rent in the midst of the holiday season will be comfortable guest houses in Abkhazia on the seashore near the border with the Russian Federation. It is better to rent a house without intermediaries, using the recommendations of acquaintances or reliable information in the form of reviews of vacationers on tourist portals on the Internet. In the private sector, you can find options with amenities from 300 rubles per person to 1000. There are elite offers - from 2000-3000 rubles per day.

Guest houses in Abkhazia on the seashore. Guest reviews

Conflicting reviews can be read about holidays in this country: some tourists will forever fall in love with the country at first sight, while others complain about spoiled vacations, giving unflattering characteristics to local residents. Negative memories of vacationers are associated with such circumstances:

  • unsanitary conditions in some public places and the spread of infections;
  • sewer waste flowing directly into the sea;
  • disrespectful attitude of local residents to vacationers, their desire to deceive tourists;
  • cases of theft;
  • interruptions in water supply and power supply.

When reading reviews, you need to understand that most of them are subjective. All people are different. Some of them need only the sea and the sun for a good rest, and as a place to sleep, there is enough room with common amenities. Other, more demanding tourists, it is not enough to rent for the night even the whole guest house. Comfort-rest in Abkhazia for them is not only picturesque nature, but compliance with the standards of high-class service in the hotel and beyond.





Of course, only the positive reviews deserve the natural wealth of Abkhazia, its historical attractions, the sea and beaches. Tourists also like low prices for food and accommodation compared to the Krasnodar Territory. They distinguish among other accommodation options - comfortable guest houses in Abkhazia on the seashore. Reviews about them in most cases are positive. It is necessary to go to Abkhazia with a positive mood over the warm sea, clean air and picturesque nature. And in this case, this amazing country will reveal its soul to tourists.
