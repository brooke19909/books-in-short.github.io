Best Cordless Vacuum Cleaners: Rating

Cleaning few people enjoy. But there are assistants who can make this process interesting, simple and even enjoyable. And these helpers are cordless vacuum cleaners. The rating, which we give below, will help you make the right choice and choose an effective model for cleaning the house.

Convenient and functional

Wireless vacuum cleaners are more expensive than conventional models, but they are more versatile and easy to use. They operate on batteries and have a vertical design. For cleaning the floor in such models, only a brush is used, and the rest of the functional elements - housing, motor, dust collector, filters - are located directly in a compact vertical design. Charging such devices is simultaneously their parking space on the floor, next to a power outlet.

Pros and cons

Today, cordless cordless vacuum cleaners are becoming increasingly popular . We decided to make a rating taking into account both positive and negative sides of these devices. Among the advantages of such structures, the following can be noted:

  • lack of wire, which requires an outlet;
  • compact size and light weight;
  • easy operation of a vertical vacuum cleaner, since you do not need to carry a heavy case with a thick hose.

On the other hand, battery operation limits the life of the vacuum cleaner - until it is completely discharged. In addition, the volume of the container is very small, so you will have to clean it more often.

Another drawback is the suction power, which is lower compared to the classic vacuum cleaner models.

And now we offer a rating of the most popular models of wireless vacuum cleaners. Let's start with the most recognizable brands.

The vacuum cleaner easily works on carpets and in any hard-to-reach spots, completely removing dirt. Among the technical features of this model can be noted:
