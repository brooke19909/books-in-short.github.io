Developer

LSR Group has been known on the real estate market since 1993, and in different segments of the construction industry. It is mainly engaged in the extraction of non-metallic minerals, which it turns into building materials at its own enterprises. This company has many tower cranes - its fleet is the largest in Russia.

LSR is engaged in construction in several regions of the country - the Leningrad Region and directly in St. Petersburg, Moscow and the region, in the Ural region and Yekaterinburg. It is necessary to dwell in more detail on the features of construction using the Europe technology, which is used in the Rays residential complex. Interior and exterior finish

The finish is very good, made on the principle of "wet facade" (the name comes from the method of application: on top of the insulation - reinforcing adhesive solution, then the primer, and on it the final layer is a rough decorative plaster). Apartments, as already mentioned, are transferred to buyers without a fine finish, which for sure can be ordered separately (the cost is about six thousand rubles per square meter).

This price guarded the "mystery shopper" very much, since it seemed extremely small. The simplest finish in previous reviews was presented from twelve thousand rubles (Krasnogorsky microdistrict), for seven thousand they usually make only a rough finish. Apparently, the point is in the materials that the developer takes at his own production.
