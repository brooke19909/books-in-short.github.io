DoubleTree by Hilton Resort & Spa Marjan Island 5 * (United Arab Emirates / Ras Al Khaimah): reviews

DoubleTree by Hilton Resort offers a wide variety of entertainment and leisure opportunities. Only here you will get the most vivid impressions of the United Arab Emirates.

Hotel Description

The pride and main feature of DoubleTree by Hilton Resort, which attracts travelers, is the huge beach, which has a length of 650 meters along the Arabian Sea. It provides everything you need so that tourists can only enjoy their vacation. Lovers of active pastime will especially appreciate the huge variety of attractions.

DoubleTree by Hilton Resort pays great attention to the design and equipment of guest rooms, because during your stay at the hotel it is something like a house for tourists. The apartments are very beautiful and decorated according to individual design projects. Another nice point is that all the balconies of the rooms are furnished.

DoubleTree by Hilton Resort will be cozy and fun for both adults and children. Young tourists will certainly appreciate the kids club, special pools, as well as a modern playground. But those who are older will definitely enjoy the spa treatments, sporting events and culinary masterpieces offered by the chefs of the restaurants.





Hotel location

DoubleTree by Hilton Resort & Spa Marjan Island is located in the emirate of Ras Al Khaimah, in the Al Hamra resort area. It is located on one of the artificial islands, which is called Marjan. Very close to the hotel there are many entertainment and shopping centers, as well as clubs and restaurants. If we talk about the remoteness of the hotel relative to the airport, then this is approximately 25 minutes.

  • King Guest Room is a fairly spacious room, which is a bedroom with a small hallway area. Its main highlight is a furnished balcony. Also, part of the apartments of this class gives you the opportunity to contemplate the seascape.

  • King Junior Suite - apartments consisting of a separate bedroom and living room. There is also a small kitchenette with a breakfast bar and all necessary utensils. Pay attention to the presence of a balcony, which makes it possible to enjoy the seascape.




What is provided in the rooms

You do not have to worry about having enough amenities at DoubleTree by Hilton Resort 5 *. Here is everything that a modern traveler needs to make his stay as comfortable as possible:

  • a modern flat-screen large TV (some of the apartments are connected to cable, and some to satellite TV);
  • the bathroom has a shower or bathtub, as well as a bathrobe and slippers and a cosmetic set;
  • there is an electronic clock with an alarm clock, in which a radio and an mp3 player are built-in (there is the possibility of connecting external media to it);
  • the bathroom has a built-in hairdryer;
  • air conditioning gives you the opportunity to create an individual comfortable microclimate in the room, regardless of the general temperature in the hotel;
  • thanks to the presence of a coffee maker or kettle in the apartment, you can always treat yourself to hot drinks;
  • for those who are used to having their favorite drinks always at hand, there is a minibar.




Hotel facilities

DoubleTree by Hilton Resort & Spa boasts an incredibly developed and rich tourist infrastructure, which includes many facilities, namely:

  • car parking, where you can leave your personal or rented car absolutely free;
  • in the laundry room you can help remove even the most difficult stains from your clothes, as well as iron them;
  • Turning to the tour office, you will find for yourself an interesting and eventful tourist route;
  • By contacting the reception, you can use the safe box;
  • if necessary, you can use the conference room or premises specially designed for parties and banquets;
  • get something to remember the trip to the gift shop;
  • the front desk is at your service 24 hours a day;
  • several branded restaurants where you can try not only oriental dishes, but also international cuisine;
  • bars with lots of drinks;
  • own water park with the most popular attractions;
  • a wellness center, on the basis of which there is a fitness club, spa and medical office;
  • golf course;
  • several pools, including two for children;
  • own spacious beach equipped with everything you need;
  • kids club with toys and entertainment;
  • playground for toddlers with swings and slides.




Positive reviews

Confirm the high status of DoubleTree by Hilton Resort & Spa Marjan Island 5 * reviews of travelers who note a number of positive points that testify in favor of this particular hotel:

  • polite, responsive and competent employees of the reception, who always promptly help to solve problems and issues;
  • several large and clean pools, some of which are filled with sea water, and one of them has a hydromassage and a waterfall;
  • upon arrival, all hotel guests are greeted with a compliment in the form of cookies, which are prepared according to a unique recipe known only to the cooks of this hotel;
  • concise and at the same time elegant interior rooms;
  • hearty and very diverse menu, where a huge assortment of fruits and many types of meat are presented (there is even pork, which is very rare for hotels in Muslim countries);
  • due to the huge space and a large number of sunbeds on the beach, it is not crowded even with absolute occupancy;
  • a large selection of entertainment and sports on the sea coast and quite affordable prices for them.




Negative reviews and complaints

DoubleTree by Hilton Resort & Spa Marjan, in addition to positive and vivid impressions, can also cause negative feelings for tourists, which are associated with the following shortcomings:

  • the guests note that in the main restaurant the tables are too crowded;
  • during the period of full occupancy of the hotel, cooks and waiters in the dining room cannot cope with the load (queues are formed, not everyone has enough food);
  • Also, hotel guests complain about the rooms being not thoroughly cleaned (there is dust, and some furniture needs to be cleaned).
