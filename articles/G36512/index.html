Movie Gate Golden Beach 4 *: reviews and photos

It is no secret that Hurghada is one of the most popular resorts in Egypt. Naturally, the city has a lot of different hotels and hotels for every taste. One of the really good places is the Movie Gate Golden Beach resort. So where is the hotel located and what living conditions can a tourist expect? What will the child do here? What do other travelers say about the hotel? Answers to these questions will be of interest to readers.

Where is the hotel situated?





The large hotel complex Movie Gate Golden Beach 4 * is located in the resort area of ​​Hurghada, about 10 km from the historical center of the city, very close to the seashore. The nearest village of El Gunna is 6 kilometers from the hotel. As for the nearest international airport, the distance to it is about 15 km.

This is a hotel of impressive size with a large private territory, on which there are small two- and three-story residential buildings, as well as the main building. The courtyard is reserved for swimming pools, sports fields and places for relaxation. In addition, there is a private garden where you can enjoy the coolness and beauty of exotic plants.

Hotel complex Movie Gate Golden Beach (Hurghada): room description





This hotel can be considered large, as on its territory there are 425 rooms - for the most part these are standard double rooms, but there are also family rooms and superior rooms. The rooms here are quite spacious and bright, with access to a balcony or terrace, which offers wonderful views of the sea, garden or courtyard with pools. The room has all the necessary furniture and a full set of household appliances.

In particular, you can watch satellite channels on TV. There is air conditioning with a convenient control panel and telephone. For a small fee, guests can use wireless Internet access. The minibar is empty here - its filling must be paid separately. By the way, room service is around the clock.

The spacious bathroom includes a bath or shower. There is also a toilet, a mirror, a sink for washing, drying for towels, a set of hygiene products. Room cleaning is daily, so you don’t have to worry about cleanliness.

Travel Scheme

For many travelers, the issue of nutrition is important. The hotel complex Movie Gate Golden Beach 4 * operates on an all-inclusive basis. Lunch buffets are served three times a day in the Karnak Hotel's main restaurant. According to reviews, the menu here is quite diverse, the dishes are always fresh and tasty. You will receive drinks and light snacks in the hotel bars throughout the day - there is a bar in the lobby, as well as on the beach and pool.





In addition, if you wish, you can visit the oriental à la carte restaurant called Tam Tam. Here you will find a huge selection of dishes of traditional Egyptian cuisine, oriental sweets and real Turkish coffee. And there is a hookah, which visitors really like. Guests are also invited to the Italian restaurant El Nilo, where guests can enjoy delicious pizza, pasta, fresh salads and other Italian dishes.

Beach and water recreation

As already mentioned, the Hotel Movie Gate Golden Beach is located almost on the seashore. About 350 m stretches the hotel's own beach, which leads to a convenient path. The sea here is very clean, there is a coastal bank where children can swim. Naturally, there is a large lively coral reef, which can be considered a real attraction.





On the shore there are changing cabins, as well as sun loungers and parasols, which can be used for free. Towels are issued on bail, which, of course, is then returned. There is also a bar and a water sports center on the beach. Here you can rent a boat, canoe or catamaran. Tourists have the opportunity for a small fee to ride a boat, water skiing and even a yacht. There is also a scuba diving center where you can rent equipment necessary for diving, as well as take a quick training course.

Additional hotel service

Of course, the Movie Gate Golden Beach hotel complex offers a number of additional services that will make your stay more comfortable. For a small fee, local laundry facilities are available. There are also several parking spaces. At the front desk, you can rent a safe where your valuables will definitely be safe. In case of health problems, a doctor will be called to your room.

Entertainment and leisure guests

Of course, such a large club hotel, like Movie Gate Golden Beach, offers its guests a lot of entertainment - in order to have a good time, you do not even have to leave the hotel.





In the courtyard there are four pools where you can swim and even play sports. The hotel also has several sports fields, a mini-golf course, three large tennis courts, a billiards room and table tennis tables. Here, in the courtyard, there is a playground with giant chess.

You should definitely visit the SPA center, where you will find a sauna, a hammam, a jacuzzi, massage rooms, as well as a whole range of various cosmetic and anti-aging treatments. There is also a gym. Every day, animators conduct various group classes, including water aerobics, dance classes, water polo competitions, etc. In the evenings, vivid performances are organized in the amphitheater, and at night you can go to a local disco. And of course, do not forget about interesting excursions, as tours here are sold literally on every corner.

Are there any conditions for living with a child?

Since many tourists today travel with children, the important point is the availability of appropriate services on the territory of the Hotel Movie Gate Golden Beach 4 *. Hurghada is a great place for children, and this hotel offers additional amenities.





For example, you can get a cot in your room, and in a restaurant you can always count on a high chair. In the courtyard there is a specially equipped, safe for kids pool. There is also a well-equipped playground with slides, a sandpit, a swing and sports equipment. And your child will be able to have a great time in a mini-club with his peers. And of course, throughout the day the little guests are entertained by a team of experienced animators.

Hotel Movie Gate Golden Beach Hotel: reviews

Everyone knows that much more useful information can be obtained by asking the opinion of tourists who have already managed to visit a particular place. So what do people say about the Movie Gate Golden Beach hotel complex? Reviews about this place are mostly positive.

Guests note really spacious and comfortable rooms that are kept clean - cleaning is done daily. The food here is also at a decent level - the choice is simply huge, and there are many dishes from different types of meat, fish and seafood. Tourists like a large well-kept area, as well as access to the beach, which, incidentally, is closed to outsiders. Animation deserves special mention: the entertainment program is really rich here (daily group classes, dance classes, sporting events, fun shows) are held. Well, another important point is the price. According to reviews, the cost of living here is quite affordable, so many travelers recommend the Movie Gate Colden Beach Hotel as their vacation spot.
