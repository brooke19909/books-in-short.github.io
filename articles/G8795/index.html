"Panasonic" (cordless phones): specifications, reviews, description, an overview of the best models

It would seem that in just a few years mobile communication was able to oust all its wireless competitors from the market, which are designed for people to communicate over long distances. However, this statement is false, since satellite phones and DECT devices are still relevant in the world market. Unlike mobile communications, a satellite phone can work anywhere, there would be a visible connection with space. And cordless phones are popular among home and office users, because wired telephony is still considered the cheapest.





The focus of this article is one of the best representatives of the market, working according to the DECT standard - Panasonic. Cordless phones under this brand are known throughout the world. The reader is invited to get to know the legendary brand better and learn a lot of interesting things about the products of the Japanese manufacturer: specifications, reviews, descriptions, and an overview of the best models.

To the pinnacle of fame

At the dawn of the 21st century, radiotelephone manufacturers around the world have a real problem with the use of radio frequencies of national importance. The fact is that there was no single standard for the used frequency range among different manufacturers. Panasonic (radio telephones) were the first to enter the market with the DECT standard (1880-1900 MHz). This range is allowed for use without a license in hundreds of countries around the world, including Russia. Now all other manufacturers are guided by this standard, and the Japanese brand is considered the best-selling in the world, only thanks to research on the search for allowed frequencies in all countries of the world, literally a decade ago.

About modifications

The Panasonic radiotelephone, regardless of the modification, has a single basic configuration. We are talking about the configuration, power system, settings and functionality. All tubes have almost identical sizes and differ only in design and color. As for the batteries, here the manufacturer did not begin to breed a zoo - two AAA-size finger-type batteries and a capacity of at least 900 mAh are suitable for all cordless phones.





Color display or monochrome - there is no difference, because they have an identical menu. The difference is only in functionality. A fairly convenient solution for both office and home use. As for the base, here you can already observe the differences, because each product has different functionality.

Yes, the manual is large and requires a lot of free time and perseverance from the owner, but many buyers insist in their reviews that it is extremely necessary to familiarize themselves with the instructions. Each menu item and its settings are described in the instructions.

An interesting manner of describing the functional. The reader at each point is invited to produce the action algorithm proposed by the manufacturer and compare the result with the image that is present in the instructions. As for localization, there is no reason to worry, because the manufacturer, exporting its products all over the world, knows for sure which country the phones are for, and, accordingly, it is equipped with the necessary guidance.

Actual offer

With real Japanese quality, the buyer begins to get acquainted in the initial segment of the business class.
