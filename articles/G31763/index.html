Hotel "George" (Vityazevo): description and prices

Work, achievement of goals, constant work on oneself - all this takes a lot of time and effort. Today, the world requires a person to move, work, be ready for any situations and problems, but often, while we realize our dreams, health goes by the wayside. This is due to rest, the absence of which negatively affects the body. That is why you need to regularly arrange yourself a short vacation. You can forget about all domestic problems in the best way by visiting another city. And if this place is located right next to the Black Sea coast, there will be no price for such a vacation.

The urban-type village Vityazevo hospitably welcomes new faces. Fans of the latitudes of Anapa will undoubtedly like this place, because in it you can really enjoy your vacation. Among the large number of places of temporary stay the hotel "George" stands out. Vityazevo is a wonderful village, and in order to have a good rest, you need to approach the choice of housing.

Location

Hotel "George" (Vityazevo) is located at 17. Barkhanaya Street. The location of the hotel allows you to quickly get to the dining room, sightseeing kiosk, grocery stores, bus stop, boulevard, attractions, a water park and a dolphinarium. Just a few minutes away is a sandy beach, the road to which passes by a drinking pump room with mineral water and a fountain.

Accommodation

The main building of the Vityazevo Hotel is represented by a four-story new building with comfortable and cozy rooms. Vacationers are invited to stay in rooms of different categories. Standard rooms are located on the ground floor and can accommodate two or three people. Each of them has its own shower and toilet, split system, beds, a necessary furnished suite (bedside tables, wardrobe, mirror, coffee table).

Junior Suite can become a temporary home for two, three and four people. It has access to a balcony, a bathroom, a split system, beds, an armchair bed as an extra bed, a mirror, a table, nightstands and a wardrobe. You can check into a room with the same equipment, but a high level of comfort (luxury). Hotel "George" (Vityazevo) does everything so that guests after staying within its walls can feel rested, updated and want to return to its doors again and again.

Prices

Hotel "George" on Barkhannaya (Vityazevo) is a great option for an unforgettable vacation at a low cost. Non-standard class rooms, depending on the number of beds and the month of arrival, will cost from 1,500 to 3,500 rubles per day. The price for a junior suite ranges from 2500-4500 rubles. Luxury will cost from 3000 to 5500 rubles. It should be borne in mind that these prices are only indicative, and the final amount for staying in the room must be specified when ordering. For some, such prices may not be affordable, for others it may be quite acceptable, but, no matter how many numbers are indicated in the price list, the George Hotel will always keep the quality standard.





Territory

Hotel "George" in Vityazevo provides excellent leisure facilities for families with children and couples in love, as well as large friendly companies. The hotel territory is very well-maintained and has the necessary zones for a comfortable pastime. Those who are delighted with the coastal situation, but are not always ready to plunge into the sea, are attracted to the George Hotel by the large outdoor heated pool and relaxation area.

There are also gazebos for evening gatherings, and you can have fun in the cafeteria. On the territory there are playgrounds for eternally restless guests. For guests who traveled by their own transport, free parking is provided.

Services

High-quality service, a high level of service, comfortable rooms, the possibility of interesting leisure and relaxation of body and soul - all this is offered to its guests by the hotel "George" (Vityazevo village). Having paid for the room in its walls, you can freely take sunbaths on sun loungers and swim in the pool, use the Internet, ironing board and iron (available on each floor), satellite TV and a cooler with hot and cold water. The price also includes regular cleaning of rooms, change of bed linen and towels.

Hotel "George" (Vityazevo) organizes excursions to outstanding places of the area for an additional fee, provides laundry services and a shuttle service. The choice of many falls to this place, not only because of the variety of service and its quality, but also due to the friendliness, hospitality, professionalism of the staff and the unusual atmosphere that they create.

Reviews

Hotel "George" (Vityazevo), reviews of which after each new arrival speak of the good impressions of its guests, works for one result - to see sincere smiles on the faces of vacationers and their desire to continue to stay in it. Many say that this place captivated them not only by their convenient location, which allows them to quickly get to their destinations and due to which there are practically no mosquitoes in the evening, but also by smooth entry into the sea, a clean sandy beach, regular cleaning of the room and maintaining their comfort.
