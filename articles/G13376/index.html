Unusual sights of Kimra. Worth to visit!

Attractions of Kimra ... Have you ever heard of this amazing place? Not? Well, then it remains only to envy, because you still have the opportunity to discover a new and very unusual direction.

In general, according to experts, the small cities of central Russia to this day have been able to keep the secrets of their past - a once hectic life filled with dramatic and often tragic events ... To see with your own eyes and walk the streets of such towns helps to feel the past, or maybe to be, and to better understand yourself.

Sights of Kimra. City of artisans and merchants





First of all, we note that the small town of Kimry is located in the Tver region. The first mention of it is found in the letter of the 16th century. Based on the information contained in this historical document, at that time, far from us, the Kimry was a large village that was of purely commercial importance, and, moreover, was famous for artisans.

In the 17th century, the Kimry was considered the “boot capital”. Shoe makers of Kimr even carried out orders for the Russian army. A shoe factory was built here at the beginning of the 20th century, and manual labor was replaced by mechanical labor.

Sights of Kimra. How to get there





It’s very convenient to get to the settlement of Kimry by rail - from the capital’s Savelovsky station, an electric train leaves for Savelovo station several times a day (travel time is a little more than 2 hours).

By bus from Savelovsky station to the city of Kimra - two and a half hours, and the ticket is 3 times cheaper than by electric train. By car, you can drive 2 hours from the Moscow Ring Road (A104 highway).

Sights of Kimra. What to see first?

At the end of the 19th century, the Kimry became a fairly large settlement by the standards of the time (more than many county-level cities). Prosperous kimryaks then built houses that relate to wooden art nouveau as specialists. The charm of provincial wooden architecture is difficult to convey in words. Houses in the style of wooden Art Nouveau - as if they were not residential houses, but fabulous towers.

A terrific place these Kimry ... Attractions here are presented for every taste. Let’s say, there’s the house-tower of the merchant Luzhin, the fabulous house of the Rybkin peasants - intricate turrets, porches, balconies, windows with complex binders - what an amazing architecture!





The wonderful tented Transfiguration Cathedral, shopping malls and a tavern with arched windows in the style of classicism - the impression of beautiful architectural objects cannot be spoiled even by the fact that the condition of the modern city of Kimra is quite neglected.

The Volga is wide and deep enough. On the promenade you can get to the Kimrsky bridge. The entire bridge with the approach to it is a little over 900 meters. This is a grand technical facility. Why? Yes, because the views of the Volga and its banks from the bridge are really amazing - a feeling of admiration and joy covers the majority of people who are here for the first time.

And in Kimry there is a museum of local lore with an interesting collection of shoes produced in the 18-20th centuries. Fifteen hundred copies - boots, canvas shoes, shoes for prisoners of war, boots and children's sandals. The hall with wooden sculptures by I. M. Abalyaev and the memorial hall dedicated to aircraft designer A. N. Tupolev - there are actually a lot of interesting things here!

Well, well ... Now readers in general terms imagine what Kimry (Tver region) is. The sights of this place, although they are considered not very popular, but really deserve attention from travelers.
