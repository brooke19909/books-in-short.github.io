How to crochet a square: step-by-step description



Many craftswomen are interested in the answer to the question "how to crochet a square." This traditional motif is considered one of the easiest ways to make beautiful lace rugs, bedspreads, pillowcases, tacks, rugs, shawls, bags and many other products. We knit a simple and very delicate square.

We suggest you learn how to perform a motif with an openwork flower in the middle. It is perfect for creating spring-summer wardrobe details, as well as charming rugs and bedspreads. In order to knit such a square, you will need yarn in two colors: green and yellow. We used knitting threads "Pehorka Children's novelty" with a density of 50 g / 200 m. And, of course, you will need a hook number 3. How to crochet a square? The operation scheme is described below.

First, make a ring and one VP for lifting. Then we knit eight single crochet stitches, making one air loop between them. We finish the row with a connecting half-column. Next, we will perform four VP and 1 CH, until the end of the row we will knit according to the scheme ‚Äú1 CH - 1 VP - 1 CH‚Äù, knitting these three elements under the air loop of the base. We finish the work with a connecting half-column. We will also start the third row with 3 VPs, then perform 3 CH and 1 VP. After that, according to the scheme "4 CH - 1 VP" we knit the whole row. We complete it with a connecting loop. The fourth row is made with double crochet and air loops. We form the corners of the square according to the scheme "3 CH - 2 VP - 3 CH". We knit the fifth row in the same way as the fourth. Now you know how to crochet a square. Such a gentle and beautiful motif is perfect for creating spring things, bags, rugs and pillows.

How to crochet a square: diagram and step-by-step description

We bring to your attention another interesting motif with a flower in the center. For its implementation, it is necessary to prepare consumables: knitting threads of white, yellow and green colors. We used acrylic threads "YarnArt Baby" (50 g / 150 m). You will also need hook number 3. How to crochet a square is shown in the following diagram.

Take yellow yarn, make a ring and one air loop. Then we knit eight single crochet. Tighten the ring and finish the row with a connecting half-column. Next, we will perform in each loop of the base one lush column with one crochet. This way you get eight petals. Between magnificent columns do not forget to carry out two air loops. We finish the second row with a connecting half-column. Now take the green knitting thread. The third row will be created according to this scheme: 3 half crochets, 2 VP, and then again 3 half crochets. This will form the first corner of the square. Next, we make three more half crochets. Let's make the second corner by analogy with the first. That is, we repeat the pattern of 3 half-crochets, 2 VP and again 3 half-crochets. We knit the entire row to the end and end it with a connecting loop.

For the fourth row we use white acrylic yarn. We carry out three air loops for lifting and single crochet columns in each base loop. The fifth row is carried out by analogy with the previous one, that is, using columns with one crochet and not forgetting to make the necessary additions in the corners. Charming square motif

The product is knitted as follows: first, a sufficient number of squares are made, each of which has a side equal to 10 cm. For the manufacture of this product, we needed 154 motifs. Then all the squares are laid out in the desired order on a flat surface. After that, they are connected to each other on the front side with half crochets or half crochets. For the assembly of motifs, we use a white thread, so that our coverlet is not too colorful. We advise you to first tie motifs into horizontal stripes, and only then form a canvas from them. The outer sides of the finished product are drawn in three rows of single crochet columns. After all this, nodules are formed and unnecessary threads are cut. The finished blanket is washed before use, dried on a horizontal surface and ironed from the wrong side.
