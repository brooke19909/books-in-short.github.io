Local Attractions

Experienced tourists recommend not limited to beach vacations, but certainly visit local attractions. Thailand is an amazing country with an interesting culture and traditions that deserve close attention from the guests of the country.

Tourists can book sightseeing trips at the hotel reception. Vacationers do not recommend spending time on sightseeing trips around the resort, although they are free, they are focused on shopping. In Pattaya, there are a lot of historical attractions, amusement parks and simply interesting places worth visiting.

Acquaintance with the resort should begin with cultural and historical monuments. One of them is the Big Buddha, located on a hilltop. There are also observation platforms where you can admire the stunning landscape of the city and take beautiful photos as a keepsake.

An interesting monument is the Temple of Truth, which has been under construction for 32 years. It is believed that it should be erected without a single nail. In fact, nails are used in construction, but they are only half clogged so that they can be removed before the construction is completed. Many already doubt that the temple will be completed, because over the long years of its construction, many parts have gradually become unusable and need restoration.



In order to see firsthand all the wonders of Thai and world architecture, one does not have to travel far. Just visit the famous Mini Siam Park, which contains miniature copies of world architectural monuments. There are temples of China and Thailand, Russia and other countries.

For all lovers of nature and animals, Pattaya is a real paradise. In the Crocodile Farm's Stone Park, you can watch an amazing reptile show. And in the village of elephants you can get to know stunning animals better, see how they play football and ride their mighty backs.

In Pattaya, the Siracha tiger zoo operates, which differs significantly from other similar institutions. Here visitors can chat with pets, watch shows with their participation, ride on elephants and even touch the most harmless inhabitants of the park with their hands.

Resort guests should definitely visit Nong Nooch Park. It occupies more than 250 ha. On its territory you can see stunning sculptures created from flower pots with plants. Nong Nooch is not just a botanical garden, it is a whole complex of figuratively trimmed plants. The park was created as a prototype of the famous Versailles gardens. In order to go around it and see all the compositions, not a whole day will be enough. In addition to the stunning vegetation, here you can see an interesting show of elephants playing volleyball and football.

There is a separate area for children, as well as a large number of entertainment that will appeal to the whole family. Adults will certainly appreciate the complex with a jacuzzi and sauna, as well as a fitness center. The entertainment complex also has a go-kart center equipped with freeways for beginners.

A relatively new attraction of the resort is the floating market. It began to function only in 2008. The market is small in comparison with similar ground places. However, he takes the first place among the world water markets. Residential water village is a center with entertainment, shops and restaurants. Here lovers of shopping and entertainment will certainly enjoy it.
