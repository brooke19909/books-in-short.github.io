Recreation facilities Anapa: description, prices, photos

Recreation facilities in Anapa are in great demand. If we talk about statistics, even in the autumn period the vast majority of rooms in each of all available options are reserved. If you want to go to this area on vacation, then you need to reserve a place for yourself in a month, a maximum of a couple of weeks. Otherwise, there is a huge chance that there will simply be no free number.

The article discusses the most popular Anapa recreation centers and prices for places to stay. This kind of information will help readers decide on a vacation rental option.

"Spark"

This base is just a minute walk from the beach. What else do you need for a wonderful holiday? You can settle in a comfortable room or in any affordable cottage. On the territory there are barbecue areas.

All rooms are decorated in a classic style. The rooms are dominated by warm colors. A TV and fridge are also included. There is a private bathroom with free toiletries.

On the territory of this recreation center Anapa is a cafe. Here you can taste different dishes of Caucasian and Russian cuisine. If you do not want to eat in this institution, you need to go a little further - in 15 minutes there are other restaurants.

Guests arriving with their own vehicles are provided with free parking.

Consider housing options:

  1. Cottage. It has two double beds. The price of the stay ($ 67) includes breakfast.
  2. Double Room. It is possible to choose the number of beds: either one large or two small. The minimum price is $ 40.

"Blue Lagoon"

Like other Anapa recreation centers, the Blue Lagoon is located very close to the beach - 7 minutes.

All rooms that are in the described institution are designed only for non-smokers. This nuance should be taken into account if the choice fell on this option. In the room you can find a TV, kettle, bar and furniture, which is distinguished by its beautiful design.

In the morning, breakfast is served in buffet style. If you want to taste Caucasian dishes, you can go to the cafe "Circassian aul."

There is a water park and a theater within a five-minute walk.

Consider housing options:

  1. Studio It has one sofa and one large bed. There is a bathtub. Metric area - 30 square meters m. The minimum cost is $ 66.
  2. Standard. Depending on the number of beds, the price also changes. The minimum is $ 35.

"Pioneer"

The apartments are 47 km and 3 km from Novorossiysk and Vityazevo respectively. In the recreation center is parking. Guests will not have to pay for its use.

The rooms have a TV with satellite channels. The kitchen has all the necessary equipment, including a kettle, stove, refrigerator. The bathroom has a shower. The recreation center "Pioneer" (Anapa) provides its guests free access to the Internet.

The airport is 6 km from the apartment.

Consider housing options:

  1. Apartments. They have 1 double bed. Cost - from $ 56.
  2. Studio Consists of two large beds. Price - $ 169.

Attention! The price is for three nights. For a shorter period of time, it is impossible to rent a number.



Finally

As you can see, Anapa is a city in which there is where to settle for little money. Of course, those who prefer to give more for extra comfort will definitely find an option for themselves.

Still, the city of Anapa is quite popular and developed. Recreation facilities (prices are presented in the article above) are in great demand and freely compete with hotels. However, it is worth noting that not all of them work all year round, so you need to choose an option in advance.
