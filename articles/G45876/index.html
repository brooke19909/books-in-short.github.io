Routes from Moscow to Tolyatti

More and more people are interested in the route Moscow - Tolyatti. You can overcome this distance on a variety of vehicles. Before you talk about each of them, you should understand what attracts tourists in these cities.

Five reasons to visit Moscow





Needless to say, why do people come to Moscow? Everyone should visit the capital of Russia. Someone comes here because of earnings, good clinics, entertainment centers, educational institutions. In any case, you need to pay attention to the most outstanding sights.

  • Arbat - this street as if attracts people from all cities. There is some kind of special atmosphere, which is impossible to describe in words, you can only feel.
  • At the Bolshoi Theater, artists of the highest level perform, each performance is worthy of the highest praise.
  • It is a must to visit VDNH, on its territory there are many monuments with a rich history.
  • The Vorobyovy Gory possess incredible beauty; they conquer the hearts of every visitor to the city.
  • With children, you should go to the widest zoo, where you can get acquainted with the most exotic species of animals.

Oceanarium, water park, museums, parks, galleries and much more - all this is the reason for their coming to Moscow Togliatti.

Five reasons to visit Togliatti

  • It is worth going to the Museum of Domestic Transport and see how the very first car looked.
  • A tourist must take a picture with the chapel in honor of Christ, it has a unique design, size and shape.
  • The museum of local lore has the largest number of interesting finds.

By plane





Before choosing a route, it is worth considering the time difference between Moscow and Togliatti. It is exactly 1 hour. The most convenient and fastest way to get from one point to another by plane. Flights of this vehicle are carried out every day, and the duration of the journey is exactly 1 hour 40 minutes.





In order to make a flight, you should come to Domodedovo Airport and buy a ticket, the cost of which is approximately 3500 thousand rubles. Unfortunately, landing is carried out only at the Kurumoch airport, which is located 14 kilometers from the city center. Then you have to get on your own. And this is at least an hour and a half extra time and forced expenses.

By car

The distance between these two points is rather big, it is more than 950 kilometers. Surprisingly, someone decides to get from Moscow to Tolyatti by car. The total travel time without stops is 13 hours. To maintain such a distance can only the bravest driver.





In total, there are two ways to get from one city to another. The shortest is the intersection of the M4 highway, the shorter route through Dimitrovgrad. Both of these roads leave much to be desired, asphalt was laid several years ago, and traffic flow is constantly increasing. Accordingly, there are many bumps, pits and convolutions on it.

Fortunately, there are several gas stations, cozy cafes and even hotels on this road, which will allow you to fully relax.

By train





A considerable distance from Moscow to Tolyatti is better to overcome by train. Let the total travel time be about 16 hours, but there is a unique opportunity to sleep, have a bite and make new acquaintances. The route leaves in the evening from the Togliatti railway station at 17:30, at the famous Kazan station it arrives at 10:24. A similar schedule is observed in the opposite direction. The ticket costs about 2000 rubles, you can purchase it at one of the stations at the box office. It is worth noting that in the summer the number of travelers increases. For this reason, prices may go up.

By bus

The longest and most tedious option of moving from Moscow to Tolyatti is by bus. The travel time is 17 hours, it is almost impossible to maintain such a distance. In addition, there may be problems with the purchase of tickets, as the flight is operated only three times a week (Monday, Tuesday, Thursday). Departure is carried out at 11:00, that is, the passenger will have to go in the midst of a sunny day. The route was created by a private company, so the ticket is paid in cash at boarding, its cost is 1,500 rubles.

There are several reasons that force people to move from Moscow to Tolyatti and vice versa. Everyone really wants the trip to be as comfortable as possible. Therefore, it is worth choosing the right vehicle.
