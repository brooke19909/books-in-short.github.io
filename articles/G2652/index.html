Hotel Siberia (Kogalym): address, service, reviews

The Siberian city of Kogalym, surrounded by many beautiful lakes, annually hosts thousands of specialists in the gas and oil industry, as well as tourists and guests from all over the world. According to reviews, staying here leaves travelers with an extremely pleasant experience. Among the sights of Kogalym (Khanty-Mansi Autonomous Area) are Victory Park, the monument "Chronicles of Russia", a museum and exhibition center, and others.

Tourists and business travelers in the city are traditionally accommodated in hotels. One of the most popular accommodation options for visiting visitors is called the Siberia Hotel.

Acquaintance

The cozy three-star Sibir Hotel (Kogalym) is located in the city center, close to many business institutions, restaurants, shops, bars and sports complexes. Visitors are offered accommodation in 30 comfortable rooms, free Wi-Fi. The cost of living ranges from 3340 to 5270 rubles. overnight. According to the authors of the reviews, the hotel is perfect for tourists as well as guests who have arrived in the city on business.

Location

The hotel is located at: Russia, Khanty-Mansi Autonomous Okrug, Kogalym, ul. Leningradskaya, house 49. The hotel is separated by distance:

  • from the airport - 8 km;
  • from the railway station - 4 km;
  • from the bus station - 2 km.

Nearby Attractions

Hotel guests are advised to visit:

  • in the Church of the Assumption of the Blessed Virgin Mary (located 1.15 km from here);
  • in the museum and exhibition center of Kogalym (located 1.32 km from the hotel).

Sports enthusiasts will be interested in visiting the sports and cultural complex "Galaxy", which is 1.33 km away. In addition, those who wish to put themselves in order will be interested to know that from the Sibir Hotel it is only 0.88 km to the Orchid Beauty Salon.

Description of the institution

Sibir Hotel (Kogalym) is located in a modern 5-storey building. The hotel has a cozy cafe where residents can have fun with friends or just enjoy a cup of coffee or fruit tea. The institution also has an excellent banquet hall, designed for corporate events: evenings, birthdays, unforgettable anniversaries, weddings. The hotel offers car parking. Employees of the Sibir Hotel (Kogalym) provide taxi call services and mail delivery to the room. Near the hotel there are bars, restaurants, supermarkets. Vacationers enjoy the local scenic nature.

Infrastructure

Hotel "Siberia" in Kogalym has a developed infrastructure. The amenities of residents here are provided by the presence of:

  • reception;
  • car parking;
  • banquet hall;
  • business center (offers services of copying and printing documents, receiving and sending faxes).

Sibir Hotel (Kogalym): service

In this hotel, residents are invited to enjoy decent comfort and quality service at affordable prices. Guests can use the services of:

  • banquets and various ceremonies;
  • mail delivery;
  • awakening;
  • taxi call.

Tourists arriving by car can also use the adjacent parking lot. Internet is available in the living rooms, so guests can work without leaving their room. If desired, guests can have fun while admiring the beautiful scenery or visiting city attractions.

Catering

The services of residents at the Sibir Hotel in Kogalym include:

  • eponymous restaurant;
  • banqueting hall.

In the cozy restaurant, which is located in the hotel building, residents can have breakfast, as well as lunch and dinner. The restaurant menu offers visitors dishes of European, Russian, Siberian and Japanese cuisines, fresh pastries, desserts. Here you can drink aromatic coffee and have a good time with friends or colleagues. A banquet hall is provided for all kinds of celebrations.

Rooms are cleaned daily.

Guests called the prices at the hotel quite reasonable, affordable for an average wallet. The cost of daily accommodation in rooms:

Reviews for the Hotel Siberia in Kogalym

Residents call Sibir an ordinary modern hotel, guaranteed to provide accommodation in rooms at affordable prices. In addition, this hotel has all the necessary amenities for a business trip. According to the authors of the reviews, the hotel is fully consistent with its category - 3 *.

What do visitors especially like?

Guests at the hotel like the fact that you can use the restaurant, banquet hall, as well as a billiards and leisure room (located on the fifth floor).

Guests are pleased to respond to staying in apartment-type rooms, 2- and 3-room. According to reviews, the rooms provide everything you need: hairdryer, refrigerator, TV, Wi-Fi. Each floor has an iron and ironing board. Guests thank the hotel staff for towels of extraordinary cleanliness and whiteness in the bathroom, as well as for impeccable cleanliness in the toilets. Guests note a particularly comfortable stay in suites, consisting of three rooms and featuring a hall, a bedroom and an office. Guests here are given the opportunity to use the service of ordering food in the room. Nevertheless, many guests consider the cost of living in rooms to be overpriced.

The reviewers also liked the service at the hotel: they note the presence of polite staff who provide guests with services of a decent standard.

What do they say about nutrition?

Guests say that the price of breakfast at the hotel (included in the price) is only 300 rubles. Buns and bread are offered, as well as a list of hot dishes to choose from, consisting of milk porridge, omelet with sausages, scrambled eggs with ham. In addition, in addition, guests can choose in the refrigerator in an unlimited amount: pancakes with cottage cheese or meat, cheese or meat slices, eggs (boiled), salad and more. Of the drinks offered: kefir, yogurt, tea, coffee, milk.

On the pros and cons

As pluses, guests call the convenient location of the hotel, providing residents with excellent breakfast, the presence of more or less decent rooms. The downside is considered poor Wi-Fi, old repairs and uncomfortable sockets in the rooms, as well as the lack of an elevator in the hotel.

Summary

In general, according to the authors of the reviews, many have a pretty good impression of their stay at the hotel. Siberia is recommended as a worthy option for travelers and tourists.
