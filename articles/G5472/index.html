A bit of history

As early as the 13th century, the river was a border zone between the Ryazan principality and the Golden Horde. There are two versions of the name. According to the first, it‚Äôs all because of the black color of water, and the word itself has proto-Indo-European roots - gwer, that is, black. According to the second version, the roots of the word are Finno-Ugric. The word "thief" or "wur" meant a forest, that is, a forest river.

In the Soviet period, 3 hydroelectric power stations were built on the river:

  1. Soldatchinskaya.
  2. Peresypkinskaya.
  3. Prudkovskaya.

None of them are already working, but the preserved dams improve the grass protection of meadows due to the raised channels.

Today the Vorona River of the Tambov Region is a favorite place for fishermen and water tourists. The floodplain of the reservoir is characterized by abundant forests. Oaks, poplar groves stretch for tens of kilometers along the river. The tributaries of the river are laid in the loose rocks of the sands of Alb, Neogene and Cenomanian.





Kayaking

The Vorona River of the Tambov Region attracts tourists who like outdoor activities - kayaking or rafting. The most popular route starts in the village of Poim. At this point there is a bridge over which the Lipetsk-Penza highway passes, and a launch pad. The first obstacle is the Korsaev dam, but it takes about 2 hours to swim to it. During this time, you can enjoy the banks of the river. The next obstacle is the Peresypkinsky dam.

Fauna of the reservoir and fishing

To date, 29 species of fish have been found in the Vorona river of the Tambov region. Fishing here is simply excellent. Among this diversity there are rare species, even protected ones. But the easiest way to catch:

  • Pike
  • perch;
  • catfish;
  • bream;
  • crucian carp.

Also found in the river: sterlet, silver bream, zander or tench. Periodically, a Volga podust appears in a pond, here it comes to spawn. This is a medium-sized fish, the body length is up to 35 centimeters, and the fish weighs up to 400 grams.

There are no paid fishing spots on the river, but you should always remember that from mid-October to the end of April fishing in any way is prohibited in wintering pits in the Tambov Region. The rest of the year, another prohibition applies - to catch fish that has not reached the size of an adult. It should also be remembered that 14 species of unique fish are listed in the Red Book of the Tambov Region. If they fall for a bait, then they should definitely be let go, and this:

  • nosar;
  • Chekhon;
  • sterlet;
  • a fisherman;
  • Volga podust;
  • Rapid
  • sewn.

For violation of this prohibition, administrative measures will be applied to the guilty fisherman.





Protected areas

In the middle reaches of the river is the Voroninsky Nature Reserve. It was organized only in 1994 to protect and monitor the forest-steppe complex, the animal world. The total occupied and protected area is 10,320 ha. The reserve is located in two districts - Kirsanovsky and Inzhavinsky.

In these places, the river Vorona of the Tambov region is especially beautiful . Photos of travelers are impressive. Forests cover about 77.2% of the total area. Oak forests and aspen forests predominate here. About 14% are occupied by black alder forests.

To date, 600 species of plants have been found in the reserve, among which there are also ‚Äúredbooks‚Äù, for example, feathery feathers, Russian hazel grouse, helmet orchid, and others.

There are many representatives of the fauna on the territory, which are listed in the Red Book. This is the Ukrainian lamprey, mnemosyne, the watchman-emperor, the viper Nikolsky, the white-tailed eagle, the Russian desman.

On the banks of the river Vorona for a long time there are reserves. The purpose of the reserve is to preserve and reproduce the fur-bearing animal, the desman, which is listed in the Red Book of Russia.





Rest by the river

For those people who do not really like to spend the night in tents, they can relax on the Vorona River in the Tambov Region at tourist centers. In the village of Pushchino there is a base "Far Far Away" right on the shore of the reservoir. In the lap of nature created the most comfortable conditions for a pleasant pastime. You can come here for the weekend and settle in the summer bungalow. On the territory there is a sports ground, gazebos, tourist equipment that can be rented for:

  • kayaking;
  • rafting;
  • fishing.

Vacationers here are waiting for a gathering by a real fire and a night disco on the river bank.
