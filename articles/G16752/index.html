How much does a dentist earn in Russia? Dentist salary in Moscow in a private clinic

Among the general population there is an opinion that the highest paid specialists are gynecologists and dentists. The first article will not affect, but read about the salary of a dentist below. As well as about studying, earning at first and requirements for representatives of the profession.

About study

Many are curious how much a dentist earns in a private clinic. It all depends on the region of her location and the skills of a specialist. Before describing the earnings of the dentist, we will touch on the issue of training. Experienced experts talk about him.

The study lasts about eight years, because it is not enough for a dentist to get a diploma. They will not hire him, additional education in the form of an internship and residency is needed. After graduation, many specialists go to continuing education courses, lasting from several months to 1.5 years.

The future specialist chooses the direction in which he intends to practice in the future. The list is as follows:

  • children's dentist;
  • dentist responsible for the treatment of caries, dental canals and installation of fillings;
  • dental surgeon removing teeth and installing implants;
  • an orthopedic dentist installs crowns, bridges, prostheses, veneers;
  • and finally, periodontists treating gums and soft tissues.

In the learning process, it is advisable to get a part-time job as an assistant to the dentist. The salary is cheap, but the future specialist will get the necessary experience.

Employment after studies

How much a dentist earns in Russia depends on his skills, place of residence and clinic where he has to work. Having received the necessary education, the young specialist gets a job in government institutions, because private traders prefer more experienced doctors. The salary of a dentist in a state hospital in Moscow starts at 30,000 rubles. It is more appropriate to keep silent about the regions, for example, in Yaroslavl and the region, a young specialist will hardly receive 20,000 rubles.

Benefits of working in a private clinic

  3. Bonuses and bonuses for good work are another plus of private dental clinics.

Negative aspects of employment

Working in a private hospital, having an extensive client base and receiving excellent salaries is the dream of many dentists. Why do they prefer to work for less money than to find a job in a private clinic?

The fact is that payroll depends on its owner. They are different: adequate or not quite. To get to work for the latter is sheer hell. Dentists who have such experience are willing to share their memories.

For example, a leader may begin to find fault with trifles. The dentist working with him will have no idea why he will be fined next time. As a result, fines are regular, first at 100 rubles, then at 200 rubles, and on an increasing basis. One day, the manager will not like the look of his employee, then something else. The dentist will be fined again, after which he will sooner or later change his job at will. Tired of enduring such a boss.
