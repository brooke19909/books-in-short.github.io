Oba Star Hotel 4 * (Turkey, Alanya): description, service, reviews

One of the largest resort regions in Turkey has been widely visited by tourists from the CIS countries and Russia for several decades. In the catalogs of tourist operators there are a huge number of hotels that are offered for visiting. It is here that there is a wide selection of conditions for recreation, as well as different prices for trips to such places.

One of the most popular hotels in the region is Oba Star Hotel 4 *. Here, almost all year round the weather is warm, which contributes to a pleasant stay.

Not sure where to go to Turkey in September? Specialists offer exactly the region of Alanya, where there are the most favorable conditions for this.

general information

The hotel is often recommended for family and youth recreation, as for both, a wide range of conditions is provided here.

Oba Star Hotel 4 * was built in 1989. Even at that time he was one of the best in the region, his fame has survived to this day. At the moment, its territory has a fairly modern look, on it are built two beautiful buildings in which the entire number of rooms is located. Almost every year in Oba Star Hotel 4 * renovation works are carried out that allow tourists to continue to enjoy its beautiful view and advanced technical equipment every year.

Location

The Oba Star Hotel 4 * is located in Alanya - one of the largest resort regions of Turkey. Not far from it you can find the central part of the city - literally three kilometers away. It is here that the entire entertainment infrastructure is concentrated, guests of the rooms often come here who want to relax cheerfully and in an exotic atmosphere.

The nearest air traffic point is quite far from the hotel - 120 kilometers. It is an international airport in the city of Antalya.

Rooms

The total number of apartments available in the hotel is 140. All of them are located in two five-story buildings, one of which is the main one and the entire infrastructure of the Oba Star Hotel 4 * is concentrated in it. All of them are presented in one category - the standard.

Each room has an excellent bathroom, where, in addition to a place for swimming, there is a sink and a bathroom. The small cabinets have toiletries, the number of which is equal to the number of guests staying in the room. At the entrance of vacationers, the service staff provides the availability of bathrobes, towels and slippers.

The rooms have individual air conditioning, thanks to which guests can independently arrange a temperature regime favorable to themselves, and regardless of weather conditions.



Room Interior

Reviews of Oba Star Hotel 4 * often say that the hotel rooms are decorated in a rather beautiful style. The small apartments are 22 square meters. m, they said, to be very comfortable. As for the general design, everything in it is made in a combination of white and beige colors. Guests note that in such an atmosphere you can relax and enjoy the process of relaxation to the fullest.

The centerpiece of the apartment is a large double bed with a pair of pedestals attached to it, where you can store small items. In some rooms, this bed is replaced by two single beds. The room has a dressing table with a round wall mirror, as well as a large pedestal for guests' belongings. On the wall, which is located opposite the berth, there is a plasma TV that is connected to the satellite broadcasting system - several Russian-language channels are broadcast here.

In their reviews, tourists talk about the fact that twice a week there is room service, which includes not only cleaning, but also the replacement of bed linen, towels and, if necessary, toiletries.

Conditions for children

In many sources, including reviews of tourists about the hotel, it is often said that the Oba Star Hotel 4 * is perfect for families with children, including small ones. In connection with this, families often come here to rest.

For kids, there are some conditions. In particular, the main one is the free provision of a separate bed for this category of guests. Often, parents say on the negative side the lack of a rental service for strollers - so, according to them, it is not very convenient to transport kids around the city and around the hotel.

In the aquazone for children there is a swimming pool specially equipped according to all safety rules. There is always clean water, but its heating is carried out by sunlight, as the process is not automated.

Parents often respond positively to the mini-club working here, where there are several animators. Here they entertain the kids, play with them, and also hold small production events.

Dry cleaning is one of the most popular services that hotel guests use for a fee. The second most popular car rental. Many hotel guests recommend taking vehicles for temporary use and setting off to explore beautiful surroundings and local attractions.

In addition to dry cleaning services, the hotel also has a laundry room where tourists can hand over their laundry. Spa

The spa area available at the hotel is very popular, which is a harmonious combination of a massage room, a center for cosmetic procedures, as well as a thermal section. According to the guests, a group of parmeisters, which consists mainly of Turks, works in the local hammam. All of them are masters of their craft and can make the hover process truly enjoyable.

After visiting the hammam, guests often go to the jacuzzi, which also offers a range of relaxing treatments.

Sport

Supporters of the theory that the same spirit lives in a healthy body, often tell in their comments that the Oba Star Hotel 4 * service provides for the presence of conditions for playing sports even during leisure. Also, guests note that sports can be practiced outdoors. For this, small platforms for playing volleyball and basketball are provided. In the building, fans of table tennis can gather in a separate room for your favorite activity.

Judging by their comments, the aquazone, which is located in an open area near the main building, is very popular among guests. It is a huge summer terrace, where several dozens of large sun loungers are evenly placed, on which all those who wish can relax and sunbathe in the sun.

Beach

The main attention of vacationers is often given to the beach area where guests relax. The hotel has its own sandy part on a large city beach. It is equipped so that everyone would be as comfortable as possible on it. There are several dozens of sunbeds, above which folding umbrellas are installed, protecting guests from the harmful effects of the sun. Towels and mattresses for relaxation can be taken absolutely free of charge in a specially organized area.

Tourists note that to get to the beach area, you must pass the road that separates the coastal part and the hotel building. This can be done easily and safely through the underpass.
