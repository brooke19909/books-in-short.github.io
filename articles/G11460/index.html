Location

Kudrovo used to be an ordinary village, lying north of St. Petersburg, between the city and Lake Ladoga. But recently, new residential buildings have been massively erected here, so now it is already a small modern town. Among the others, the most convenient location is at the Birch Grove complex. Kudrovo is located from the center of St. Petersburg in some 20 km. Nearby is the Ring Road, to which from the extreme house of the complex in a straight line - about 500 meters. On one side, the Birch Grove is bordered by Evropeisky Prospekt, on the other - Prazhskaya Street, on the third - Okkervil Park, and only on the fourth lies an undeveloped area. Design

Stylish and modern looks LCD "Birch Grove" (Kudrovo). The photo shows four high-rise buildings with 25 floors, the top of which has different colors - yellow, green, red and blue. For some reason, the developer positions them as two houses, in the first of which - one section, and in the second - three. The technology of their construction is somewhat unusual. Here, two floors of each building were driven out, pouring monolithic columns from reinforced concrete, and already above they began to make monolithic ceilings and bearing walls. According to the plan of the developer, the external walls were to be made of brick, in the DDU (appendix) they indicated that they would be made of aerated concrete. As a result, they became foam concrete. The facade was planned to be insulated with fiber cement slabs. The photo of the Birch Grove holders posted on the forum shows that in some places the builders used split foam blocks, which caused curvature of the structures, and large mounting slots were visible along the windows. Now all this is no longer visible, since the facades have been laid on top.

The halls in the buildings were planned to be built with an area of ‚Äã‚Äãalmost 150 "squares". Design elements in them are made in the tone of the upper floors of buildings. Elevators in buildings are installed by Alex.
