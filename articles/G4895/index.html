The concept

In short, insurance coverage is the direct obligation of the company to compensate the customer for loss or damage resulting from the actual occurrence of an insured event.

Such coverage is a certain amount of liability, or a list of certain risks that the company assumes, undertaking, in case of their occurrence, to pay the necessary amount as part of the compensation. The insurance coverage reflects the measure of satisfaction of the interests of the company by the company. This term is used to designate the amounts provided for reimbursement and to determine the list of risks against which the property is insured.

When is insurance coverage impossible?

There are a number of cases where recovery of compensation under hull insurance is not carried out:

  1. The policy was expired.

  2. The owner intentionally caused harm to his own car.

  3. In the event that the operating conditions of the vehicle specified in the contract did not meet, then the occurrence of the insured event is invalidated.

  4. The machine was driven by a person who does not have rights to this (including the owners themselves in a state of drug or alcohol intoxication).

There may also be other reasons for refusal, which must be prescribed in the contract.
