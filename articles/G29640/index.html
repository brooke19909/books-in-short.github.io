Pattern horses from fabric: several options

A soft horse is an interesting toy that both boys and girls like to play with. For boys, she plays the role of a war horse who courageously fights in battle along with his master. For girls, a horse is an important detail in an interesting game.

Choose a horse

According to the pattern of horses from fabrics, each needlewoman will qualitatively make a suitable pony. Such a toy is presented as a gift, especially on New Year's Eve, in the year of the Horse.

Children will appreciate such a present on their merits, in addition, the types of horses made have a variety of options:

  • a horse with wings - a pegasus or a unicorn;
  • My little Pony;
  • graceful filly on thin legs.

Depending on what character, lifestyle and position the prancing animal will have, you should make a horse pattern from the fabric with your own hands.

We select a pattern

There are many options for making horses. Depending on the type of horse planned, you should select a pattern and fabric. The most suitable materials for the horse will be fleece, velor and velvet. Artiodactyls are sewn, both large and small, voluminous and flat, on four legs and even on two hind legs.

Horses in trend
