Larissa Club Akman Park 4 * (Turkey, Kemer): overview and reviews of tourists

Kemer resort fell in love with domestic tourists for a very long time. Many come here annually. This city is pretty small. The population of Kemer is a little more than 20 thousand people. At the same time, about 170 hotels have been built in this resort. That is, even more than in many coastal large Turkish cities. To rent comfortable housing at a low cost, so in Kemer it will not be difficult. For example, many tourists stay at this resort in the convenient hotel Larissa Club Akman Park 4 *.

Where built in the city

This four-star complex is located on the outskirts of Kemer, in the Camyuva area. The center of this town is 8 km from the hotel. Some tourists find this location of the Larissa Club Akman Park 4 * hotel convenient, others do not.
