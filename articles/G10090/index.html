Building

Hotel Best Delta 4 * (Mallorca) offers its guests accommodation in a comfortable 7-storey snow-white building, where each room has a balcony. It was built in 1979, then in 1999 it was completely modernized. In 2016, there was a major overhaul. The compact design of the guest rooms, characteristic of the late 70s, could only be adapted for the economy class. However, a sound economy class is now most in demand in the hotel business.

The reception area Best Delta 4 * occupies part of its first floor, with one and a half ceiling height. The pastel-verdise-colored floor lined with artificial stone sets off the whiteness of the tetrahedral columns. In the lobby, the reception desk is decorated in the same light colors. Here, on the ground floor, there is a hotel restaurant and bar.

Light brown, white and blue colors prevail in the interiors of public areas. Solid repair, all equipment is in good working order, although not of the latest generation. Lighting is professional, matte, not straining the eyes.

The hotel itself is equipped with many cozy mini-recreation areas for guests, where you can freely sit down with friends and chat. The staff of the hotel complex refers to work with the soul, which cannot but impress tourists.

Territory

The nature surrounding the hotel deserves the most flattering epithets. She is equally impressed by lovers of romantic travels, and lovers of peaceful relaxation.





On the vast territory of the hotel complex there is a fairly large outdoor pool, a garden and terraces.

The pool, suitable for bathing children, has a sufficient number of sun loungers and parasols. Some tourists prefer to relax here than at sea. Near the pool tables are set, and scurrying waiters take orders from vacationers.

The hotel has a minigolf course as well as a tennis court.

Local air is both marine and mountain pine, it is very clean, they are pleased to breathe. Rocky and seascapes are fascinating.

Rooms

The hotel staff keeps rooms clean and tidy. The rooms are small but very comfortable. For the most part, this is an economy class. The double rooms have two single beds, bedside tables, a closet built into the wall, a sofa, a table, a coffee table, a TV, air conditioning, a hairdryer, and a wall mirror.





The balcony is furnished with a plastic table and a pair of armchairs from the same material. From the upper floors of Best Delta 4 * a picturesque view of the sea opens.

The bathroom is equipped with good plumbing, spacious and comfortable. It regularly updates the set of shampoos, gels, etc.

The rooms are cleaned daily and linen and towels are changed daily.

Poolside relaxation

The relaxation area by the pool is comfortably equipped. In its arrangement, the principle of open architecture is used. The hotel restaurant located its tables both in the corresponding hotel room and on the terrace. Vacationers take food right on the terrace, sitting in cozy wicker chairs from the vine at round small tables. Those who wish can also have a meal in the spacious restaurant hall, where a pleasant pastel brown color prevails.

Small square tables covered with starchy tablecloths and compact half-chairs are harmonious with the space of the hall.

Beach vacation

We describe the path to the beach. From Best Delta 4 * you should first walk about twenty minutes along the winding road to the Beach Club, and then go down the stairs. Thus you can go to the rocky beach of Cala Blava. It is landscaped and adapted for recreation of adults, confidently swimming. The fact is that the depth here begins immediately from the coast. Resting on it, it is advisable to use silicone slippers because of the stones. But the clear water and many fish on the beach leave a good impression.





Families with young children relax on another sandy beach belonging to the S'Arenal resort, located a 15-20 minute drive from the Best Delta 4 * hotel.

Shopping

Guests of Best Delta 4 * recommend shopping in the island's capital, Palma de Mallorca. The most interesting shops are located on Passeig des Born and Avinguda Jaume III. It is best to carry them out during seasonal discounts: in January - February and in July - August.
