Dubai Marine Beach Resort & Spa Hotel 5 *

The location of the hotel is very convenient: it stands on the shore, but at the same time there is a business center near the city. Nearby are shopping streets and a small shopping center. Beautifully decorated and the area near the hotel: everything is green, among which are artificial waterfalls, streams and ponds. It has its own beach, spa, 3 pools, 8 restaurants, 2 bars and 2 gift shops. UAE hotels of this level are very popular among holidaymakers.

Jumeirah Beach Hotel 5 *

The hotel is located near Dubai Airport, and the city center can be reached in 20 minutes. It consists of 600 rooms, many of them have a balcony with spectacular views of the sea and the beach. The rooms have a minibar, fax, TV, air conditioning, safe and a bathroom with shower and bathtub. In addition, the hotel has 18 restaurants, a massage center, spa, health club, tennis courts and much more. Tourists have at their disposal three private beaches.

Hilton Ras Al Khaimah 5 *

This hotel is located near the city of Ras Al Khaimah. A picturesque place with comfortable rooms, its own restaurants and a business center. In addition, the hotel has a nightclub, swimming pool and tennis court. There is no private beach, but the nearest one is just a five-minute drive - Hilton Beach Club.

Crowne Plaza 5 *

One of the most popular hotels in Dubai. It consists of two buildings, which housed apartments and 600 standard rooms. It should be noted that there are rooms for people with disabilities and those who do not smoke. There are also special rooms for business guests, featuring great opportunities. The rooms are equipped with everything you need (bath, TV, minibar , etc.). The facilities include a spa, business center, hairdresser, jacuzzi, laundry, rooftop pool and much more.
