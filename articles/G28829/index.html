How to call China from Russia

After the advent of telecommunication systems, the possibilities of communication between people have expanded significantly. Telephone and Internet, e-mail and VoIP telephony have made mutual messaging simple and quick. Nowadays, people rarely send telegrams to each other, and letters are written even less often. Today, the most popular means of communication is the telephone. It is difficult to manage without him, especially business people.

How to contact with China

To call to any country in the world from Russia today is not a problem. You can find out how to call China, USA, France or another country by phone directories or using the Internet. By engaging any search engine, you will quickly receive complete information about the telephone codes of various countries.





The People's Republic of China is not just a neighbor of Russia, but also a very important trading partner. It is no secret that most of the goods sold in our country in flea markets and even in high-end shopping centers are mainly brought from China. Therefore, the question of how to call China is primarily of interest to entrepreneurs today.

Some businessmen fly to China themselves and negotiate with suppliers face to face. But most entrepreneurs make an order online or over the phone. Refinement of details, control over the formation and movement of orders, the search for new suppliers - for all this, businessmen need to know how to call China correctly.



To call the People's Republic of China, you need to use the rules of international calls. According to these rules, each country has its own digital code (usually a three-digit code), before you call China, you need to know it in advance.

But before dialing this code, you need to enter the international line. When making a call from a landline or payphone at a local post office, press the number 8, wait for the beep, 10 (access code for the international line) and then enter the country code 86, then the local code and phone number.

When calling from a mobile phone or through a computer, before calling to China on a cell phone, you do not need to go to an international line, you just record the phone number in international format.



Separately, it is necessary to talk about how to make calls to China via the Internet and a computer. Many Internet companies, such as ICQ, Skipe, Google and others, allow you to make a call to any country. To do this, you just need to create an account on the website of this company and replenish the account through any electronic transfer available in your country. Then you simply select the country, dial the subscriber number and call. It should be noted that very often calls through such Internet companies are cheaper than calls through telephone operators.

By creating an account with a similar company or just in any well-developed social network, you can make voice and even video calls absolutely free, paying only for the spent network traffic.
