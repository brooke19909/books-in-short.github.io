Tagil hotels: overview, features, rating and reviews of tourists

If you happen to stay in Nizhny Tagil on a trip or you arrive there on a business trip, Tagil hotels are always openly open for you. To make it easier to make a choice, get acquainted with some of them.

Park Inn By Radisson Nizhny Tagil

The Park Inn By Radisson Nizhny Tagil has a very convenient location. The train station is a ten minute walk from the hotel. Accommodation is offered in rooms of these categories (paid for 3 nights):

  • double standard - 14 250 rubles;
  • double superior - 16 500 rubles for one person, for two - 21 750 rubles;
  • Junior Suite - 27,000 rubles for one person, for two - 29,250 rubles;
  • lux - 36 000 rubles for one person, for two - 38 250 rubles.

All rooms except standard double room include breakfast. Hotel guests are provided with the following services:

  • free parking;
  • free wireless internet;
  • restaurant and bar; room service is available;
  • Fitness Centre;
  • pets allowed (paid service).

Hotel reviews by tourists:

  • excellent location, there are walking areas nearby;
  • the restaurant is well prepared;
  • modern equipment in the rooms;
  • beautiful views;
  • the presence of underground parking.

Hotel address: st. Goroshnikova, 11.
