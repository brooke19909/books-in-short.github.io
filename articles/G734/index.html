Club "Aurora" in St. Petersburg: description, menu, entertainment

Night club "Aurora" is one of the most famous institutions for recreation and entertainment in St. Petersburg. Residents of the city come here to listen to concerts of famous ensembles, to hold a festive event or just chat with friends in a relaxed atmosphere.

General information about the institution

The name "Aurora" is familiar to almost every resident of the Northern capital, as well as those who visited this city. This is the name of the hotel in St. Petersburg, which is located at Ligovsky prospekt, 21B.

Under this name, a night club is also known. This institution is significantly different from other organizations of this kind. Here, each visitor can feel comfortable and get real pleasure from listening to their favorite music. The club room consists of two rooms with a capacity of 1000 and 250 people. It is equipped with modern sound and lighting equipment. Also, the territory of the institution includes a bar and cafe. The night club "Aurora" is located at the address: Pirogovskaya embankment, house 5.

From the windows of the institution you can see the banks of the Neva River and the famous cruiser, in whose honor this recreation center was named.

Entertainment for visitors

This institution is primarily intended for lovers of modern musical compositions. The club often hosts concerts of celebrities.

Here you can hear the music of Russian rock bands, as well as songs in the style of folk, metal, electro-pop. Sometimes the Aurora club in St. Petersburg is visited by performers from other countries. There is also a bar in the establishment where you can not only taste various types of draft beer, but also follow the events that take place in the main room (they are displayed on the screens). For lovers of outdoor activities, a spacious dance floor is provided.

Club "Aurora" in St. Petersburg is perfect for celebrations. Employees of the institution select food and drinks for the participants of the celebration, decorate the room where it is held, serve tables for banquets and receptions.

Assortment of food and drinks

The menu in this establishment is represented by Asian and traditional European dishes. Restaurant visitors are also invited to try dishes cooked on an open fire.

The menu of the Aurora club in St. Petersburg is approximately the following:

  1. Salads from vegetables, seafood, chicken, greens.
  2. Shish kebabs, grilled meat.
  3. Beer snacks (croutons, fried potatoes, salted fish, pies).
  4. Fast food (sandwiches, hamburgers).
  5. Snacks for strong drinks (pickles, fish, cheese, smoked meat).
  6. Desserts (curd cakes, pastries).
  7. Alcoholic drinks, cocktails.
  8. Different types of tea and coffee.
  9. Cold drinks (fruit drinks, juices).

Participants in the Aurora nightclub are invited to hold a celebration in the format of not only a banquet, but also a buffet.

Different types of canapes, light salads, cakes are served on the table. Those who plan to organize a big celebration can order a cake at the restaurant. It is decorated at the request of customers. Cakes are decorated with fruit, a congratulation lettering made of cream, as well as cream, chocolate and candied cherries.

Customer opinions about the institution

A club called Aurora, as well as a hotel in St. Petersburg with the same name, is considered one of the most famous organizations in the city. Guest reviews about this recreation center are different. One likes the quality of work of the institution and its employees. According to those who are satisfied with the level of service of this night club, bartenders and waiters here are real professionals in their field. In addition, they are polite and attentive to customers. Visitors like the quality of the musical equipment, thanks to which they enjoy listening to concerts.

The level of organization of festive events is also highly appreciated by customers. They say that the cultural program is interesting, the room is comfortable and beautiful. The quality of food and drinks is also liked by the participants in festive events. In addition, they say that the prices in the institution are quite affordable.

However, the Aurora club in St. Petersburg also has drawbacks. For example, some visitors speak of insufficient cleanliness in the halls and sanitary facilities. They also do not like the quality of work of the employees of the dressing room, their rude attitude towards customers.
