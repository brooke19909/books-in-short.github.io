How weaving a bracelet from laces

Bracelets have always been a beautiful jewelry that is popular among men and women. Bracelets come in a variety of shapes and sizes. They can be massive, decorated with precious stones or the simplest in appearance, made with their own hands from unpretentious materials.



Today, simplicity and accessibility are back in fashion, so weaving a bracelet from laces is a very relevant activity for fashionistas. Ordinary shoelaces can easily turn into a cute decoration that will complement your look. Weaving itself is quite simple and does not take much time and effort.

There are various ways to weave bracelets from shoelaces. For example, you can take a black lace of medium thickness and four silver thinner shoelaces. The length of the black should fit under your wrist, clasping it comfortably. We leave a place for the fastener, to which then both parts of the product will be attached. We put a black lace in the middle, and four silver ones - two on each side, respectively. We begin to weave a pigtail using only silver-colored ropes, braiding them around black. So, we start two silver laces under black, weave them crosswise and tighten. We do the same with the other two. As we see, weaving a bracelet from laces is a very simple task, which even a child can do.



Thus weave the bracelet to the end. As a result, you should have free silver threads from both ends of the black lace. Next, take a small piece of black leather, cut a square out of it so that it fits a button. Then we put it on one edge and press it with your finger. We make two holes in the square and thread two silver shoelaces into each of them , tying them on a black cord. Cut off the remaining parts. Next, sew the button on the front side. At the other end of our bracelet, we bind the ends of silver laces and make a buttonhole out of them. Do not loop very large, otherwise your bracelet may be unfastened.



So, our decoration is ready. Weaving a bracelet from laces takes about fifteen minutes, does not require special materials and is very simple in execution. Such a thing can perfectly complement your image. Alternatively, you can make several bracelets on one arm. In this case, select the color gamut of such bracelets based on the color palette of your image. Such bracelets look very unusual in combination with a large number of finger rings. The main thing in this matter is to observe the measure and not go too far. Weaving a bracelet from laces is also an opportunity to do an interesting job, and the resulting product can be a great, original gift.

For daily wear, weaving bracelets from laces is also an ideal solution. Schemes of such products can be invented independently or composed of several existing ones. Today, such bracelets are replacing baubles, but they look more original and attractive. Weaving a bracelet from laces can be a good way to show your creative nature, imagination and originality.
