Artificial Plant Lighting: Reflax Lamp

For the normal growth and development of plants, light plays an important role. Without it, not a single tree, not a single flower, grass will grow, as they fall ill and die. When growing plants indoors, it is difficult to provide enough light. To compensate for the shortage, they use a variety of lighting devices, including the Reflax lamp.





Lamp Features

In crop production, sodium lamps are widely used. They are used not only when growing indoor plants in apartments, but also installed in greenhouses.

Scientists cannot accurately reproduce sunlight, the entire spectrum of its radiation. However, they managed to recreate lamps with one type of radiation. Plants in different periods of vegetation need a different light: blue and red. The latter stimulates flowering and fruiting, and blue - the growth and development of plants.

Different cultures require a certain highlight. Some need light that activates the processes of growth and development, while others need light for abundant flowering and fruiting. All this is taken into account in sodium lamps, which are capable of emitting a red spectrum. These types of lamps have special requirements, as they must be highly efficient and economical. One of the best sodium lighting devices are Reflax lamps.

Efficiency

In greenhouses and when growing indoor plants on racks, sodium lamps are used. The choice in favor of Reflax lamps is due to the high characteristics of the products:

  • Profitability. When growing indoor plants for sale or obtaining vegetables in greenhouses, the cost of production will depend on the cost of obtaining a trading unit. If the cost of growing was small, and the selling price is high, then the owner of the greenhouse or greenhouse will have a high profit. Reflax lamps consume little energy, which is why they are highly valued.
  • Long period of operation. Reflax lamps can operate up to twenty thousand hours. This type of luminaire is capable of delivering up to thirty percent of efficiency, which is many times more than alternative lighting sources.
  • Excellent light output. Compared to conventional incandescent lamps, Reflax products have several times more light output.
  • Benefits for plants. The experiments showed that the light emitted by the Reflax lamps accelerates the formation of flowers and ovaries several times. As a result, plants produce high yields.




It is recommended to use "Reflax" lamps in the late stages of plant growth, since when using sodium lamps in the early stages, plant growth is accelerated. Shoots stretch and become long. However, such lighting is suitable for indoor plants. To improve the quality of the backlight, sodium lamps are recommended to be used in conjunction with the blue spectrum of the glow.

Important Nuances

The disadvantages of Reflax lamps include their long heating and heating. Although the last drawback can be argued. For example, when using sodium lamps in winter greenhouses, you can save on heating. On the other hand, such lamps should not be placed near plants, since they can cause burns to foliage.

Design features

Reflax lamps for plants are classified as discharge lamps and are considered the brightest. In crop production, high-pressure products are used. They can be of different designs. The brightest and best for lighting plants are lamps with a mirror layer, which increases productivity.





For the cultivation of any vegetables, strawberries, indoor plants, it is best to use sodium lamps. They are highly economical and efficient. Under them, any plants grow quickly, lay a large number of buds and give high yields.
