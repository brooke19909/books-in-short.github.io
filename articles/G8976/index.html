Which bank has the maximum interest on deposits? Maximum percentage of deposit in the bank

What is the most popular way to store cash? The answer is quite prosaic: opening a deposit account in banks. It's no secret that most people want to get the maximum percentage on deposits. After all, the main thing is not only to save savings. It would be nice to multiply the existing one.

What types of deposits exist

One can easily get confused in the variety of banking products. Indeed, today there are a huge number of types of deposits. Each of them differs in several ways:

  • terms of conclusion of the contract;
  • interest rate;
  • the possibility of partial withdrawal and deposit of funds;
  • the presence of monthly capitalization;
  • deposit currency;
  • the possibility of early closure.




All contributions are conditionally divided into several large species that have gained popularity.

  • Ruble. The most understandable type of deposit.
  • Currency accounts.
  • Anonymous metal contribution. The most risky way to make a profit.

The maximum bank interest on deposits is charged to accounts opened for a long period.

Anonymous metal contribution

Not all banks work with precious metals. Sberbank is considered the hegemon in this area, which offers to place funds in gold bullion. Profit directly depends on the gold exchange rate in the world market. This method is the most risky, because you can either get a serious profit or lose some of the money. The greatest effect is achieved when placing funds for a long time.

Currency accounts

It should be noted that Sberbank, unlike the others, does not offer interest calculation. Income is calculated from the exchange rate. It is important to consider that the price of selling foreign currency and buying money by a bank is significantly different. In this connection, it is better to open an account for a long period. The maximum percentage on deposits can be obtained by opening a multicurrency account. Money in this case will not lose in value. That is, there is no dependence on the exchange rate.

Ruble deposits

Perhaps the most common type of contribution. Every citizen of Russia in one way or another came across them. The lowest deposit rate on call. Note that it is at this rate that income will be calculated in case of early closure of the remaining types of accounts.

  • down payment amount;

  • the possibility of partial withdrawal of accrued interest;

  • the presence of capitalization.

The most profitable ruble deposits at interest. Maximum rates provide a significant profit.

The most difficult choice is to choose the right type of deposit in a reliable bank. Today there is a large selection of financial institutions that attract public funds. Most of them are involved in deposit insurance. This gives a guarantee of a refund in force majeure situations. The maximum deposit amount must not exceed 700 thousand rubles.

You should trust your savings to larger banks. Although small private institutions offer the maximum percentage of deposit in the bank. Let's consider the most advantageous offers.

How to choose a deposit





When choosing a deposit, you must carefully study all the conditions for calculating income. For example, of the two accounts with the same terms and rates, the most advantageous is the one where monthly capitalization is provided. Capitalization is the calculation of interest not only on the initial contribution amount. In this case, income is accrued on previously paid interest and additionally paid funds. It is very beneficial for all customers.

For the convenience of customers, many banks put up all offers on sites where you can not only familiarize yourself with the conditions, but also roughly calculate the yield of the deposit. Deposits with the possibility of replenishment are suitable for those who decide to open an account for a short time. It is also desirable that there is a capitalization of interest. The more often capitalization occurs, the more profitable the client. If the choice is made, then you should immediately contact the institution, as the bank, at its discretion, may make changes to the conditions. However, this does not apply to the concluded contract.

To date, the classic conditions of the deposit are the most advantageous. Such conditions include a large rate without the possibility of replenishment and withdrawal of funds. In this case, the client needs to forget about the existence of savings for a predetermined period.

Good conditions for opening an account can be obtained during seasonal promotions, which in recent years are more and more. These promotions are held before the holidays. Such contributions are in most cases short-term. The total term of the contract does not exceed three years.

The risks

A banking institution is affected by the economy as a whole. And with the latest developments in this area, many investors are worried. The Central Bank has begun a mass check of all organizations for accreditation at the Deposit Insurance Agency. If the bank does not have such a document, then soon the Central Bank will revoke the license. The funds that were stored in the bank will still be returned to the owners, but this will take some time.

To avoid this, you should carefully study all the documents of the bank or consult with a lawyer. In addition, do not give in to the first impulse to open an account at a high percentage. Important: stable and reliable banks do not offer prohibitive interest on deposits.
