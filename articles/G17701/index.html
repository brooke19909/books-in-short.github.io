DIY tricolor antenna tuning

As you know, not a single satellite, and even simple one, television will not start working without installing a special antenna. Moreover, if we talk about a satellite dish, then each person has it individually. The signal that the antenna will receive, first of all, goes to the receiver, and only then to the receiver itself, in the role of which the television acts. Due to the shape of a satellite dish, almost everyone calls it a dish. But this form was not chosen at all randomly. It is due to it that the signal is formed in the required place. This article will discuss what constitutes a tricolor antenna setup.





It is worth saying right away that mounting a satellite dish is best on the roof. In this case, nothing will interfere with the receipt of the signal. If it is not possible to install on the roof, then during its installation it should be borne in mind that it should not be directed to any structure or tree - there should not be any interference at all. In addition, the antenna should be directed approximately 36 degrees to the east, then the tricolor satellite antenna will be tuned more successfully.



What is the difference between a tricolor satellite dish and other similar devices? The fact that the broadcast is from only one satellite, which is stationary. It is also worth knowing that all channels are controlled through the receiver. The remote control from the TV is needed only to turn it on and off, as well as to add and reduce the sound of the TV. When connecting the receiver to the receiver, you must make the necessary settings for you. You can also try to immediately find the channels, maybe further tuning of the tricolor antenna is not needed. Having saved the channels that were found, you should look at their quality. If it is at the proper level, then you can safely watch TV. Otherwise, the tricolor TV antenna tuning should continue.

In channel tuning, you should mainly be guided by two indicators - a level indicator and a signal quality indicator. The lower these indicators, the signal, and, accordingly, the quality of channel display is worse. In order to increase them, you will need an assistant who will talk about the results of your activities. It is necessary to gently rotate the plate up and down, left and right. It is worth doing until quality reaches the required level. It is worth knowing that even the slightest deviation to the side can radically change all the results that you have achieved.





After the tricolor antenna setup at this stage is completed, you should fix it as much as possible and more accurately. Otherwise, you can knock down all the settings, and everything will have to be done again. After that, you can again search for channels. Just do not forget to save them after the end of the search procedure.
