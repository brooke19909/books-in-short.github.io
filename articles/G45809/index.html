We select speakers for the computer

A modern computer can hardly do without speakers: we play games, listen to music, watch movies, and much more. It is difficult to do just headphones. Therefore, it is worth determining how to choose speakers for the computer.



It is important to immediately discuss one point: such devices are often simply not focused on reproducing high-quality sound. Therefore, it is worth considering such speakers for a computer that are well suited for everyday use, while not being too expensive, but will be of high enough quality. They differ from the usual ones in smaller sizes, compatibility with a sound card, an isolated magnetic field, as well as the presence of corresponding connectors.



If you choose good speakers for your computer, then for starters it is important to pay attention to their type. Any devices in this category can be active or passive. Active speakers have a built-in amplifier, while passive speakers do not. A significant advantage of the latter is their very low cost. The sound quality in this case is far from perfect, since the amplifier available on the sound card is most often designed only for headphones, so it will not allow to achieve full sound. Speakers with built-in amplifier allow you to get good quality sound and high volume.



Acoustics for computers can be made in different layout options. The simplest system may be 2.0, where there are two columns. Variations such as 2.1, 4.1, 5.1, 7.1 suggest the presence of a subwoofer and several satellites. In this case, the subwoofer has the task of realizing low frequencies, and the speakers have medium and high frequencies. Such systems work very efficiently only in a company with a suitable sound card and motherboard.

If you are interested in speakers for a computer without a subwoofer, then you need to look for the best option between size and sound quality: the smaller the dimensions of the acoustics, the worse its sound will be. Another significant point is the body material. Not the best solution would be the choice of plastic in this case. Such computer speakers are cheap, and their appearance can be very interesting, but their acoustic characteristics leave much to be desired. Particleboard or fiberboard products will be the best option, their sound is of higher quality. The speaker casing should not have extra holes; there should be complete tightness. The only possible hole may be a bass reflex, which is located next to the speaker on the front panel, taking on the task of high-quality reproduction of low frequencies. In the event that the speakers for a computer are less than one liter in volume, then such a device simply does not make sense.

Parameters such as sensitivity, power and frequency are very important. Power does not affect the sound volume; the mechanical reliability of the system depends on it. Sensitivity affects the volume, and its optimal value is 85 decibels. For greater convenience, the speakers should have a volume control located on the front panel. It is also necessary to have frequency controllers.

When choosing speakers for your computer, it is important to remember that they must be suitable for your existing sound card.
