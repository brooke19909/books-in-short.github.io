Hotel Sultan Beach Resort 4 * (Egypt, Hurghada): overview, description, reviews

Egypt belongs to those countries where the flow of tourists does not decrease. People come here all year round, even in the summer months, when it is very hot. This is explained by the dry Egyptian climate, thanks to which high temperatures and heat can be easily transferred.



Rest in Hurghada

But not only climatic conditions are the reason that many people choose this direction for their holidays. Tourists like the Red Sea, rich in coral reefs, where you can go snorkeling, diving, feeling all the charm of diving.

The Russians are familiar with many Egyptian resorts, including the oldest - Hurghada. It is believed that it was from here that local tourism began to develop. Many come to Hurghada to relax with their children. This is explained by the fact that in hotels the infrastructure for young tourists is quite well developed. In addition, you can not worry that, swimming in the sea, they can hurt their feet on corals, because the reefs are quite far away.

Those who choose Egypt to not only swim or sunbathe, but also to see local wonders, can easily get from Hurghada to Luxor and see the famous pyramids. Transport accessibility has become another advantage when choosing this resort. In addition, tourist infrastructure is well developed in Hurghada. There are a lot of big and small shops, there are water parks and night clubs, restaurants and hookah.

Infrastructure

Sultan Beach Resort 4 * (Egypt) offers its guests a lot of services. This is a conference room for one hundred and twenty people, and free parking without the need for prior reservation. The main building has a beauty salon and a currency exchange. ATMs are installed in the lobby, a supermarket and a small souvenir shop are functioning. If necessary, tourists can use the laundry, shoe shine. At the round-the-clock reception you can rent a safe box, a vehicle, and use a left-luggage office.

Housing stock

A large hotel complex - this is how Sultan Beach Resort 4 * can be described. Egypt, Hurghada in particular, is rich in such hotels in which on the territory there are not only large storey buildings, but also villas. To accommodate in this large hotel provides three hundred twenty eight rooms. They are located both in the building and in the villas and meet all the standards required for establishments in this category. The rooms of the categories "Standard", "Studio" and "Suite" with a direct view of the sea or the internal garden have everything you need for a good rest. All are equipped with smoke detectors, as the hotel is positioned as a non-smoking hotel. In addition to comfortable new furniture, the rooms are equipped with split systems, satellite TV, a pay safe and a minibar.

Apartments in two-story villas have a living room with upholstered furniture and a separate dining area. Upstairs bedrooms are arranged. Both the first and second floors have bathrooms. At the same time in the villas can accommodate up to six people.



Sultan Beach Resort 4 *, reviews of the housing stock of which are mostly good, can simultaneously receive more than a thousand tourists. Standard rooms are designed for one or two residents. Suites are designed for those who work on vacation, as they have a workplace with a table.

All rooms, including suites, have ceramic tiles on the floor. The bathrooms are combined, they have a shower or bath with shower, a hairdryer, and the apartments have bathrobes and slippers. Cleaning is carried out unobtrusively and at a time convenient for residents. The cost of rooms depends on the category and the view from the window, therefore, when booking a room in advance, this circumstance must be taken into account.

Nutrition

Sultan Beach Resort 4 *, like most Egyptian hotels, offers an all-inclusive concept. Meals are different: a buffet is also possible, and a menu order is served in the main restaurant. The all-inclusive concept also includes free ice cream for children, snacks, sandwiches and local drinks in bars. The buffet offers dishes from both the international menu and Oriental. The pre-booked restaurant is famous for its fish specialties, including seafood. In addition to it, on the territory of Sultan Beach Resort 4 * there are four bars.



Beach

The hotel owns a small private sandy beach, perfect for children's swimming. Kids will surely enjoy splashing around in shallow water. Sand and pebble bottom suggests the presence of rubber slippers, which can be bought directly at the hotel in a small store. The descent into the water is gentle, which is another plus of this hotel, especially for family tourists. Water is warm even in winter. You can relax in the Sultan Beach Resort 4 * all year round, while the hottest months on this coast are July and August.

To get to the beach located in the bay, tourists need to walk just a hundred meters past the nearest hotels. A pleasant unhurried walk takes a maximum of five minutes.

For children

The hotel is positioned by tour operators as a family. There are always many children for whom a mini club and a safe playground are provided. In addition, a shallow pool is equipped for younger customers on site. Services such as babysitting upon request and an extra cot in the room are chargeable.



Additional Information

For children, holidays are constantly held at the hotel, animators work daily in the afternoon and evening, there are entertainment programs and discos for adults. At the reception you can buy sightseeing tours around the city, as well as the pyramids and the temple complex of Luxor. In general, tourists are pleased to note many of the pleasant features of the Sultan Beach Resort 4 *.

Hurghada, hotel reviews of which indicate that most of them have an excellent price level, has become a favorite destination for Russians. And this is not surprising.
