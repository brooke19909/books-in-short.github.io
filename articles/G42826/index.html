Hotel Atlantica Sea Breeze 4 * (Cyprus, Protaras): room types, services, reviews

Tourists who are thinking about relaxing on a golden beach quite often make a choice in favor of Cyprus. This is a famous country with beautiful nature, interesting culture and, of course, developed tourist infrastructure. Here you can spend a truly comfortable stay, immersed in a different culture and enjoying excellent service.

Of course, when it comes to Cyprus, many travelers prefer Protaras. This is a large resort city, which receives tourists from all over the world all year round. Of course, there is no shortage of hotels here - you can choose a place that you like. Many tourists respond positively to the Atlantica Sea Breeze hotel complex. What does a hotel accommodation look like? What services can you count on? What type of vacation is this place suitable for? Answers are sought by many people planning a vacation.

Where is the hotel located?





When choosing a hotel, many travelers take into account its location, because this is an important factor. Where to look for the Atlantica Sea Breeze hotel complex? Cyprus, the vicinity of the resort town of Protaras - here, on the shores of the Mediterranean Sea, you have to spend a few paradise days. This is a calm area, since only other hotels, cafes and shops are located nearby - for the most part tourists live here. On the other hand, the distance to the city center is no more than 5 km, and there is a bus stop near the hotel.

Most tourists arrive at the airport of Larnaca, which is 55 km away, so moving is unlikely to take much time. By the way, the famous port of Famagusta, which many tourists want to visit, is only 15 km away. And guests always have the opportunity to go to Ayia Napa and enjoy the local attractions.

Atlantica Sea Breeze Hotel (Cyprus): Area Information





This hotel complex has a large territory that goes directly to the beach. It consists of a six-story residential building, which was recently completely renovated - today, guests can enjoy fresh repairs, modern design and equipment, spacious elevators.

The courtyard of the Atlantica Sea Breeze 4 * hotel is also very beautiful - there are emerald, neatly trimmed lawns with arranged sun loungers and umbrellas, flower beds and bright bushes, alpine slides and other attributes of landscape design. Guests here feel like a word in the middle of an exotic Mediterranean garden.

Do they provide meals?





When planning a vacation experienced tourists must take into account the food. The Atlantica Sea Breeze hotel complex offers its clients three full meals a day (all-inclusive system). Three times a day, guests are invited to the main restaurant of the Kalypso hotel - here they are equipped with buffet tables. Reviews of tourists are favorable - the food is delicious, the dishes are varied and rarely repeated, you can count on an abundance of fresh fruit.

There are restaurants of Greek and Italian cuisine here - you can have dinner and enjoy delicious local wines, but you need to book a place in advance. There is a poolside bar for guests. There is also a cocktail bar where many travelers like to spend time. You will be offered a delicious cocktail, a chilled drink, or even a glass of strong drink (of course, all local products, imported alcohol is paid separately). By the way, in the bars during the day guests are offered light drinks, pastries, ice cream and other products.

Beach: distance, entertainment and amenities





The Atlantica Sea Breeze hotel complex is located almost on the beach - you can walk to the sea in a few minutes. In the reviews, tourists say that the beach here is simply magnificent - the sand is soft, the entrance to the sea is cleared, and the garbage is cleaned regularly. The water is warm, especially in high season. Guests are provided with sun loungers with umbrellas, and towels are issued at the reception.

For vacationers, a volleyball court is equipped on the shore. There will be entertainment for sports fans. You can rent a boat on the shore. Many tourists love water skiing and boating. Swimming with a mask is also not boring, because the water is very clean, and the local fauna is rich and bright. The main service on site





Additional services are designed to make the stay of tourists as comfortable as possible. The hotel has its own currency exchange, as well as a place where you can rent a car or other transport. For a small fee, the administrator will provide you with a small safe located in the surveillance zone.

You can also visit the local souvenir shop. If you need to use a computer or send a fax, you can visit the local business center. By the way, throughout the hotel guests can use the free Internet.

Leisure: can you count on entertainment?





The hotel complex was created in order to entertain guests. The hotel courtyard is reserved for a large pool, next to which there is a terrace - a solarium with umbrellas, sun loungers and a bar serving refreshing drinks. In the evening and cold weather, guests relax by the indoor pool.

Tourists spend their free time playing ping pong and billiards. There is a large collection of various board games at the hotel. There is a steam room and a sauna, and for a small fee you can relax in the jacuzzi or on the massage table. Tennis courts are also available. Throughout the day, you will be entertained by funny animation guys, and in the evening you can enjoy colorful shows and dance to live music.
