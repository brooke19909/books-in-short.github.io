Guest House "Diana" (Lazarevskoye): description, address, reviews of tourists

Numerous cities and towns of Greater Sochi annually receive a huge flow of tourists from Russia and neighboring countries.

Rest in the village of Lazarevskoye

The village of Lazarevskoye is a Black Sea resort corner, in which there is everything you need for a real good rest: magnificent subtropical landscapes, warm gentle sea, all kinds of entertainment and a huge selection of housing. Prices for holidays in the village are significantly lower than in the glamorous Sochi, not only for accommodation, but also for food, entertainment, excursions, etc.





The village has a very developed resort infrastructure. The modern promenade is the center of evening relaxation. Numerous restaurants with live music and cozy cafes await their visitors. Children and adults can have plenty of fun in the amusement park, where all kinds of attractions, swings and carousels are concentrated. The local ferris wheel is one of the largest in all of Russia, this is not even in Sochi.

Where to stay?

In Lazarevsky, a huge number of places to relax. Elite hotels and pensions, motels and resort centers, but private guest houses are most in demand among holidaymakers. As a rule, these are low-rise cottages with beautiful modern architecture. The conditions in guest houses are usually close to home.

Almost all mini-hotels have their own landscaped courtyard, a kitchen for those who want to cook their own meals, parking, and other benefits of civilization.

Owing to great competition, owners of private houses try to create the most comfortable living conditions and warmly welcome guests, taking into account all their requests and wishes.

Vacation prices usually depend on the proximity of the guest house to the sea and the season.

Guest House "Diana"

Although Lazarevskoye is called a village, its area is rather big. A huge number of guest houses, and they are located in any corner of the resort. Fans of noisy holidays usually choose housing closer to the embankment and to the center, and lovers of peace and tranquility - on the outskirts of Lazarevka.





If you prefer a family vacation in a quiet place, then pay attention to the guest house "Diana" (Lazarevskoye, Pobedy St.), which is located in a cozy, quiet place of the village, near the sea.

The building of the guest house is very beautiful. The four-story mansion with columns and semicircular balconies attracts the attention of guests of the village.

Guest house "Diana" (Lazarevskoye) is small in size and very compact. Free Wi-Fi is available throughout the hotel.

Types of rooms - these are just nine rooms with all amenities.

On the top floor there are two family five-bed rooms with large balconies, on which there is a table and chairs for relaxation.





There are also several quadruple rooms with or without balconies, as well as triple and double rooms.

Each room has everything you need for a comfortable stay: air conditioning, TVs with satellite channels, beds with orthopedic mattresses, wardrobes, bedside tables, refrigerators.

Hotel infrastructure

Guest house "Diana" (Lazarevskoye) is located in a relatively quiet place. The nearest beach takes no more than five minutes.

Just six hundred meters from the hotel is the Lazarevsky water park "Starfish". Around there are cafes, shops, excursion bureaus.

The center of Lazarevsky and the amusement park are about two kilometers away. Only thirty minutes of a pleasant walk - and you are on the waterfront. If desired, you can use the public transport.





Guest house "Diana" (Lazarevskoye) has its own equipped kitchen, which is located on the ground floor of the building. Shops and a minibar are on site.
