Hotel Novotel Phuket Surin Beach Resort 4 * (Thailand, Phuket): photos and reviews of tourists

There are many excellent resorts in Thailand. And one of the most popular among Russian vacationers is located near the ocean Chong Thale. The tourist infrastructure in this small town is developed just fine. This also applies to housing. Finding a good budget hotel in this city will be a snap if necessary. For example, Novotel Phuket Surin Beach Resort 4 * hotel received excellent reviews from tourists . Many vacationers believe that this hotel pulls not just 4 stars, but at least 4 plus. The level of service here is largely consistent with a five-star hotel.

Location Features

The city of Chong Thale itself is located on the island of Phuket, well known to many domestic tourists. The distance from the Novotel Phuket Surin Beach Resort 4 * hotel to the nearest airport is 45 km. Russian vacationers who bought permits to Thailand with accommodation at this hotel are usually brought to the destination by the tour operators themselves (a bus is provided). At the end of the rest, these same companies carry out a return transfer.

It takes a short drive from the airport to Chong Thale. In total, the road takes no more than an hour. However, experienced tourists back to the airport, beginners are advised to still go by taxi. The fact is that the transfer bus first collects vacationers from many hotels, which takes a very large amount of time.





Actually the Novotel Phuket Surin Beach Resort 4 * hotel itself , the reviews about which are really just excellent, is located in a very quiet place near the popular Surgin beach. It is located on the second coastline. In order to get to the sea from its territory, you need to go through a small picturesque forest park.

Novotel Phuket Surin Beach Resort 4 *: general hotel description

This hotel accepts guests in four-story buildings of modern architecture. It was built in 2008. Its interior is decorated in the style of minimalism with elements of Thai culture, which looks very nice and presentable. The entire territory of the hotel is "strewn" with pools, between which "channels" are laid. In the middle of all this splendor is an island with a mini-club built on it and several children's slides. The size of the hotel yard is not too large. But there is just a huge amount of greenery planted here. Therefore, the territory of the hotel looks, judging by the reviews of tourists, it is actually very beautiful. At the entrance to the hotel, guests usually (at the discretion of the administration) take a deposit of $ 150. Most tourists are advised to leave a security deposit at check-in. The fact is that upon departure he will in any case be returned at the rate (not particularly advantageous).

Hotel infrastructure

Hotel Novotel Phuket Surin Beach Resort 4 * (Phuket) received good reviews from guests , including the fact that it has a well-equipped laundry. Dining at the hotel

There are three restaurants in this hotel . In terms of food , the Novotel Phuket Surin Beach Resort 4 * hotel (Thailand), many tourists equate even to a five-star. The choice of dishes here, of course, is not as rich as in fashionable hotels, but all of them are of high quality, tasty and prepared from the freshest products. Breakfast at the hotel is included in the price. Tourists are served as meat dishes, as well as sausages, omelettes, cheese, fruits, etc.

Lunches and dinners at the hotel also prepare delicious. But, unfortunately, the prices for the dishes here are pretty impressive. For example, a buffet costs 1,200 baht at a time. Therefore, most tourists advise at lunch and in the evening to visit any of the cafes located next to the hotel.

In addition to restaurants, there is a Novotel Phuket Surin Beach Resort 4 * hotel (Thailand) and a bar. The choice of drinks here, according to many vacationers, is quite diverse. However, the prices in this institution are quite high. For example, a bottle of good wine costs around 1000 baht. Good reviews about the hotel bar are also available because every day there is a promotion for one of the drinks. If desired, the guest can take two for the price of one.





Sport and beauty

If desired, the hotel guests, among other things, can play darts, billiards or tennis on its territory. Of course, there is a sauna in this hotel. Hotel beach

The road to the coast from the territory of the Novotel Phuket Surin Beach Resort 4 * hotel takes only about 5 minutes. Which, of course, is very small. Most tourists consider the Surgin beach to be the cleanest and most comfortable on the island of Phuket. The coastline here is completely covered with fine silky sand. There are no stones at all on the beach. Therefore, it is convenient to relax here, including the elderly, as well as citizens with disabilities.

There was no massive influx of Russian tourists in Chong Thal. And therefore, the prices for massage on Surgin beach are more than affordable ( almost twice lower than Patong ones ). The fact is that Russian vacationers, unlike European and American, really do not like to bargain for a long time. As a result, our compatriots always overpay, which pampers local merchants. Thai entrepreneurs some time after the start of communication with Russian tourists simply begin to unimaginably turn up prices.





The sun loungers and umbrellas on Surgin Beach are mainly owned by beach cafes. If desired, in one of these institutions at a low cost, you can rent both an ordinary sunbed and orthopedic with a towel. In addition to the cafe, on the coastline there are many clubs with swimming pools and beautiful recreation areas.

Sargin beach is located in a quiet bay. Therefore, the water in the sea is always very warm. The landscapes are surrounded by magnificent, magnificent and also very contrasting. Century-old spruce trees by the beach are adjacent to huge spreading palm trees.
