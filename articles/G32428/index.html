The best recreation centers in the Belgorod region

At any time of the year, you can spend time with health benefits at recreation centers of Belgorod and Belgorod region. And not only take a break from household chores and work, but also go fishing, ride horses that are on several bases, fry the kebab in nature. We will conduct a short excursion to the recreation centers in order to understand where it is more drawn to rest.

Holiday Village "Fish Paradise"

A wonderful place to relax and fish is located between the villages of Syrtsevo and Gremuchiy of the Belgorod region. The recreation center is located in the middle of picturesque landscapes on the banks of a stocked pond. You don‚Äôt have to look for a good fishing spot. It bites everywhere, so you need to come here for your goldfish. On the shore are gazebos with barbecue facilities. Fish kebab is generally an unsurpassed delicacy, especially since there are: grass carp, silver carp, pike perch, pike, catfish, carp and crucian carp.





Fishing here is paid - 300 rubles per person fisherman, the cost of a gazebo with barbecue for four is 500 rubles.

Accommodation and service

"Fish Paradise" is a recreation center in the Belgorod region, where you can relax on weekends and longer. Accommodation is available for 4 and 6 people. Each of them has a veranda canopy, where you can relax in the evening. All houses have working fireplaces, kitchen corners, refrigerators and televisions.

The house for four is a suite. It has a bathroom with toilet and shower. In the junior suite for six, showers and bathrooms are equipped next to the house. Single and double beds. On the banks of the pond there is a bar with a varied menu of fresh organic products. You can try and buy honey from a local apiary.



Since Rybny Rai is one of the recreation centers in the Belgorod Region with fishing, the service includes the full provision of fishing services. Those who wish can rent a boat and gear. There is a Russian bathhouse with brooms on the territory of the base. A covered terrace with a bar is a place where you can organize any festive event. These are weddings, anniversaries and corporate parties. At the base, horse and bicycle rides are practiced.

Holiday Village "Arcadia"

"Arcadia" is such a romantic name at the pier of the recreation center located in the village of Maslova (Belgorod Region). This is a great place to relax with your family, in the company of friends or a secluded relaxation, which is sometimes so lacking. The territory of the base is a pine forest and an adjacent reservoir. The recreation center is young, so all the buildings on it are made of environmentally friendly materials - wood and glass. Arriving with children, do not worry, what to do with them. For them, wooden towns were built with slides, swings and sandboxes on two playgrounds. For older children there is a rope park, ball hockey and a labyrinth. Summer activities include beach activities.

Accommodation and services

Accommodation options are at guests' option. It can be cottages for five and six people and hotel rooms. For a large company, a two-story house with 4 bedrooms on the lower floor, a bathroom, a shower, a large hall is suitable. The rooms have double beds, dressers and air conditioning. On the upper floor there are two bedrooms, a hall with a folding sofa. Entrance to this floor is separate.



On the territory of this recreation center in the Belgorod region there is a large restaurant for all kinds of celebrations. Being in this room, you feel that the hall and nature are inseparable. The trees outside the window look like part of the interior. Guests from Belgorod who came to the wedding can spend the night in hotel rooms to attend the celebration on the second day, and there is a double VIP room for the main characters of this event.

Holiday Village "Smile"

On the coast of the Starooskolsky reservoir, not far from the resort village of Primorye, the recreation center "Smile" is located. Belgorod region is waiting for its guests, day and night. This is a suburb of Stary Oskol. You can come here both on vacation days and for a longer time. At the base, you can relieve the stress of working days and relax. A sauna and a Russian bathhouse with a broom will help with this. Specially equipped picnic area is well suited for families or gatherings with friends.

Guests who come to the recreation center will be accommodated in any of the 87 rooms of the hotel - from standard to luxury. The apartments have everything for a comfortable stay. They are equipped with televisions, Internet and long-distance telephones. Wi-Fi is available throughout the base and is included in the price.





There is also a private car parking, there are three pools, a cafe and a restaurant. The base has a good gym, there are tracks for mini-golf, a billiard room. If there is no craving for outdoor activities and you decide to take care of your health, in the Belgorod region at the recreation center "Smile" has its own hydropathic center. For lovers of swimming, a sandy beach is equipped in the pond. You can rent a yacht or jet ski. For walking, there are also bicycles and scooters for children. Children at the base will not be bored: there are two playgrounds, a children's town, a volleyball and a basketball zone.

"Fishing Farm"

On the shore of the lake in a clean area is the "Fisherman's Farm" - a recreation center in the Alekseevsky district of the Belgorod region. She is year-round. Here everyone will find something to their liking: sports, fishing, horse riding and cycling, games, folklore holidays. Camping and recreation in a real Russian bathhouse will charge with positive energy.

Accommodation options depend on the preferences of the guests of the recreation center:

  • Cottage - three houses, each with two to six beds. Comfortable log cabins with kitchen, TV, air conditioning, garden furniture with barbecue.
  • The hotel complex - 16 rooms from two to four places (amenities per block of 2 rooms).
  • Camping - 14 houses from two to four places, amenities on site, individual gazebo for relaxing and fishing.

A short walk from the base is a forest belt with berries, mushrooms and beautiful landscapes. It is on the tracks of the forest belt that you can ride bicycles.

Service and entertainment

"Fisherman's Farm" is one of the recreation centers of Belgorod and the Belgorod Region, where folk holidays and festivals are organized. It is very interesting to spend time at the base during these events. You can take master classes on various subjects. For example, such festivals are held: "Russian meal", "Russian soul", "Golden fish", "Craftsman-made man-made". Celebrate here and holidays such as Christmas, Christmas time, New Year, Ivan Kupala. There is a souvenir shop at the recreation center. By acquiring any of the original samples in it, vacationers will thus support the local Belgorod craftsmen and craftsmen.



Fishing at the recreation center is possible from May to November, and winter - from December to February. The pond was stocked for seven years, five tons of fry annually. You can catch catfish, carp, silver carp, grass carp, perch. Fishing is paid.

There is a rental service at the base where you can take sports and fishing equipment, bicycles, catamarans and boats. For guests organized cycling routes to local natural attractions. Laundry and transport services are available. The base is the owner of the GREEN OFFICE sign of environmental responsibility, the separate waste collection service (paper, glass, plastic, batteries) ensures the cleanliness of its territory.

Food at the recreation center

At the recreation center there is a cafe "Cool place", which serves traditional Russian and Ukrainian cuisine and fish specialty. There is a large banquet hall in the "Fisherman's Farm", which allows organizing an event with 150 seats. The kitchen, providing food for guests of the base, is equipped with modern equipment that allows you to save all the useful nutrients of natural products. Only at this recreation center in the Belgorod region is there a gazebo with a Russian stove, where you can cook yourself an excellent ear. On the base you can buy food from local farms: fish, milk, fruits, pastries and homemade bread, herbal tea.





Here are the many interesting places of recreation in Belgorod and the Belgorod region. Have a good rest and a good fish catch!
