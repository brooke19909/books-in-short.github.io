Rostelecom packages for clients with various interests

The largest provider of Russia, Rostelecom, serves millions of customers who have different financial capabilities and tastes. In large cities and small towns, you can now install Wi-Fi homes, use the services of modern interactive television. With the help of Rostelecom packages, viewers are given the opportunity to choose a set of programs that best suits their interests.

High quality gear viewing

Using the latest technology allows you to view in excellent quality and stereo sound. By connecting digital television, viewers will be able to watch programs in HD, record their favorite movies, start and stop watching on different TVs, choose the time to watch programs provided by Rostelecom in channel packages. To compile them, the provider constantly studies the world experience and interests of Russian viewers, which allows you to effectively update the list of proposed programs.

What can I see in the package "Your starter"?

The list of Rostelecom packages includes basic and additional sets of channels. As a rule, becoming a new provider’s client, a connection is made to the base package “Your starter”, which is free for the first month, and then the client can expand the list of programs or pay for the existing one.

It offers over 100 of the most popular channels, among which family members of any age will be able to choose interesting films and programs for themselves, while it costs a little more than 300 rubles a month.

The Rostelecom package includes Russian news channels, Euronews, BBC World News, CNN, STS, TNT, REN-TV, NTV, popular channels about animals, geography, fishing and hunting. The set has a block of sports channels, here you can watch old Soviet films and cartoons, the best melodramas, comedy shows.

Your Optimum and Your Advanced are packages for those who want to see more.

A little more than 400 rubles will cost “Your optimal”. This is an extended version of the “Your Startup” package with blocks of music, sports and children's channels.

Even greater opportunities not to miss the best matches, new movies, TV shows appear for those who prefer the “Advanced” package of “Rostelecom” channels. Fans of travel, adventure, new discoveries, training programs can not only have an interesting time at the screens, but also learn a lot of useful things.

Five times more expensive and more interesting

The cost in Rostelecom of the package “Your maximum” is almost five times higher than the package “Your starting”, but the payment includes the cost of all additional services. At any time you can watch any program without limiting yourself in the choice.

Almost 250 of the most popular domestic and foreign channels of all subjects: sports news and news of the film industry, adventure, travel, culture, scientific discoveries. You can keep your finger on the pulse of the planet, control the viewing and subscribe to the selection “Your films”.

You can select a new package of the list of Rostelecom channels in the “Service Management” section, where you need to go to “TV packages”, mark the required one and click “Connect”.

Sports in the package "Your maximum"

Additional features

Having chosen the main Rostelecom package for a low fee, you can purchase an additional one if there is no necessary channel in the main list so as not to pay for an entirely more expensive package.
