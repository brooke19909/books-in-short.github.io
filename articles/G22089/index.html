What is the Sorochany Golf Hotel

The hotel provides accommodation in beautiful wooden cottages. They look very beautiful, especially in the evening, with backlight. The cottage has four rooms. They all have a separate entrance. Number of rooms

Sorochany Golf Hotel has more than a hundred guest rooms. Literally all the reviews mention that the rooms in the cottages are unusual - two-level. On the ground floor there is an entrance hall with a living room, and on the second floor there is a bedroom with a bathroom. The rooms are divided into three categories. The first, as in most hotels, is called "Standard". The living room has a dining table with chairs, a sofa, a flat-screen TV. The bedrooms are very comfortable. Even the one that has its own sauna offers a small set of dishes, as well as an electric kettle. Well, the kitchen provides everything you need for self-cooking: a gas stove, microwave, refrigerator, pots and pans.



Nutrition

The cost of living in the Golf-Hotel Sorochany 3 * complex includes breakfast. They are buffet style at the main barbecue restaurant. For lunch and evening, you can eat there on the menu. The waiters offer Russian and European cuisine, as well as an excellent wine list. Given that the hotel is sports, and most winter tourists use it as a base for skiing, the hotel administration has introduced a new service - packed lunches. There is a bar on site. In the restaurant you can order children's and diet menus, freshly squeezed juices. In the summer, an outdoor terrace opens. In addition to the restaurant, there is also a banquet room for weddings and other celebrations. In the summertime, it is possible to install tents for ceremonies and treats of guests. Reviews say that the hotel is fed well. Breakfast is very hearty, but standard. There are cereals, sausages, yogurts, rolls. Many tourists had lunch and dinner at the Barbecue restaurant, and praised the efforts of the cooks.



Infrastructure: services and facilities

Sorochany resort (Moscow) is a popular winter holiday destination. People from the capital come here for the weekend to ski. For winter tourists, the hotel organized a free shuttle to the ski lift. Ski bass can be purchased at a discount at the hotel reception. There is also a rental of various equipment for winter sports: skates, skis, sledges, snowboards. Summer tourists can rent bicycles. For guests of superior rooms, the use of a tennis court is included in the price. You can book group or individual golf lessons from a qualified trainer.

What else delight guests

Sorochany Hotel provides its guests with free Wi-Fi, which is valid in all rooms without exception. The beautiful lake provides wonderful conditions for beach holidays and fishing in the summer, and in winter it turns into an equipped skating rink. The hotel also specializes in hosting seminar participants. There is an equipped conference room. A business center with the necessary equipment is added to it. Many Muscovites hold weddings and similar celebrations here. The hotel has a room for honeymooners. The banquet hall can accommodate one hundred and eighty people. In summer, a festive table can be served on the terrace, which offers enchanting views of the surroundings. The hotel accepts pets, but only by prior arrangement with the administration. Large dogs may require surcharge. But secure parking in the territory is reserved for the free use of motorists.



Sorochany Golf Hotel: prices

A good stay cannot be cheap. But the hotel constantly provides any discounts. For example, on weekdays, prices for all rooms fall by fifty percent compared to weekends and holidays. Reviews warn that you need to book standard rooms in advance, as their number is limited. You can reduce the cost of leisure by dropping in a group and removing a whole cottage. Also, the cost is reduced with early booking. So, you can rent a suite with a sauna not for one hundred and three euros, but for ninety. The same goes for Comfort Apartments. For early bookings, you can get this room with a 12 percent discount, for eighty-seven euros. The reviews claim that you can take a chance and come to the hotel without booking. Then you can get to the "special offer of the day" and remove the "Suite" in general for seventy-four euros. But in the high season, this is not worth the risk.
