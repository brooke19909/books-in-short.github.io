Tsaghkadzor (ski resort). Tourists reviews

Statistics say that since 2001 the number of tourists coming to Armenia has been steadily increasing. Some are attracted by national cuisine and local wines, others are delighted with excursions to ancient monasteries and historical places, while others come here to spend unforgettable hours on the mountain slopes. In this article we will tell you what the famous Tsaghkadzor (ski resort) is located on the slope of Mount Tegenis.





How to get to Tsaghkadzor

The famous ski center is located only 75 km from the capital of the republic, in the Kotayk region. If you are planning a trip from Russia, then the easiest way is to get a plane ticket to Yerevan, and take a taxi at the airport or find a bus station. Please note that a trip by car will cost much more than public transport. In addition, it is better to travel around the country by bus, since this type of communication is very well developed here. If you still decide to get to the place by taxi, then find out in advance the public service telephone number and order a car from the airport by phone. Thus, you can save a significant amount and be sure that you will not be deceived.

Restaurants and Cafes

Tsaghkadzor (ski resort) can boast of its own cuisine, with good reason. The mountain air awakens the appetite in the best way possible, and hungry tourists at lunchtime are ready to swallow a whole bull and drink it with the best local wine. Therefore, the resort has many cafes and restaurants that offer visitors dishes of Armenian and European cuisines. They also treat guests to local drinks, among which wines and cognacs are especially popular. Before placing an order, please note that the portions are large enough, and the cost of a good lunch starts from 400 rubles.

Entertainment and Attractions

Arriving in Tsaghkadzor (ski resort), you will not be bored. Of course, the main attraction of the resort is the steep slopes and ski slopes. In your free time, you can explore several memorable places of the city and get acquainted with its history. The Kecharis monastery complex is considered the main local attraction, and it was founded in the 11th century. You should also visit the house-museum of the Orbeli brothers and the most beautiful Katogike church, built in the 13th century.

When exploring Tsaghkadzor (a ski resort), a photo of which you can see on this page, do not forget about other interesting places. From here you can easily get to any desired point in the country and get acquainted with its cultural attractions. If you wish, you can even book a tour by contacting the office of a travel company located in the resort.



Resort Trails

At the disposal of tourists are a variety of tracks that will impress experienced athletes and beginners. Those who like extreme situations will also find entertainment . If you do not have your own equipment, you can rent skis or a snowboard for rent at any time. Tsaghkadzor (ski resort) offers everyone the services of an instructor who will help to learn the basics of skiing, as well as improve existing skills.

The ski season lasts in Tsaghkadzor from November to April, but the largest number of tourists can be seen here from mid-December to March. Many vacationers specially come here to celebrate the New Year and spend the Christmas holidays. Winter does not frighten guests with cold weather, as the temperature in January rarely drops below three degrees below zero. A cable car delivers athletes to the top of the mountain , and from there you can already see Big and Small Ararat and Lake Sevan - almost the whole country. Beginners should take into account that unexpected turns occur on steep slopes, which often throw beginners off the track. If you are an experienced athlete, then ask connoisseurs where you can find the most interesting places and go down closer to the river.
