DIY idea for kids and adults

DIY gifts are the best and most original. With great pleasure, they will be expected by mothers, fathers, grandmothers and anyone else who sees how magnificent photo frames, a decorated candle, a money tree or a crocodile from beads can be. In the article you will find not only interesting ideas for children's creativity, but also schemes for making original crafts.

DIY photo frames

The desire to capture on film the happiest moments of life haunts every person. However, for a good photo there should be a good frame that emphasizes the merits of a small masterpiece and makes it more expressive. Factory products have outlived their lives, and most people tend to make their own household utensils. Glamorous photo frames are the best ideas for creativity and gifts, for the manufacture of which you can take any jewelry: buttons, shells, sparkles, beads, coins.





First you need to make a cardboard base - it can be square, rectangular, oval. Cut out the back cover to which the photo will be attached. After the base is ready, proceed to the most creative part - to decorate the frame. Gather all the most beautiful unnecessary small home decorations and glue them with double-sided tape or glue. If you decide to use beads, they must be sewn for strength.

Flowers - the best ideas for creativity from paper





Any applications can be used both as independent products, and as decorations for a gift box. Cutting flowers out of paper is interesting for both adults and children. To complete this application, it is necessary to draw a contour on a white paper with a simple pencil, fold the colored cardboard in four layers, and cut a pattern along the contour. So you get a perfectly smooth and symmetrical flower. By the way, this idea for creativity can also be useful for making snowflakes from paper or foil. It is necessary to prepare the template in advance, circle it with a simple pencil and cut along the contour.

Candles decorated with dry flowers





Traditionally, in the decoupage technique, three-layer napkins or rice paper are used as the main material . However, resourceful craftswomen who seek to improve this manufacturing method decided to try the leaves and flowers dried in advance. DIY decoupage technique using natural materials began to look much more beautiful and original. So that dry flowers do not crumble, you should choose an extremely smooth surface. It is not recommended to use glue for fixing, otherwise all the beauty will disappear in an instant. Wax candles are the best idea for creativity with napkins. So, in order to get a perfect craft, you need to dry medium-sized flowers in advance. The process can be accelerated by wrapping greens in a newspaper and placing it under a heated iron. In advance, think over the composition for candles, heat a spoon over a burning candle and level it with flowers. Repeat the procedure until the craft takes on a finished look. Under a hot spoon, the wax will begin to melt, and fragile flowers will stick and quickly take on the desired shape. A candle can be decorated with sparkles or rhinestones.

Money Tree

Perhaps this is the most original idea for creativity to date. It is believed that a person who has a money tree in his house will always be financially secure. This souvenir can be purchased in many stores, but a DIY gift is always nicer.

Money tree can be made of counterfeit notes or coins in which a hole is drilled. The most generous for a major holiday can donate real rubles, dollars or euros. The tree trunk is made of real wood or plastic, the branches are made of durable wire. The template must be wrapped with dark tape or paper. If gold or silver coins grow on a tree, the trunk must be painted with paint of the appropriate color. This spectacular gift is prepared quickly and very simply.

Plasticine animals for the smallest





Preschool children are encouraged to work with any materials so that they can successfully develop motor skills. Plasticine is where the smallest boys and girls begin. Ideas for creativity with children can be found even in ordinary children's books. Among the little animals, the favorite is the tiger cub, famous for the cartoon about Winnie the Pooh. To make it, you will need plasticine in four colors: orange, white, black and brown. Make a small ball that will represent the head of the tiger cub, then with the help of a plastic knife cut out the triangular ears and stick them. Two indentations will make up the eyes, and a black ball will make up the nose. Cut three strips of different sizes and stick them on the face of the tiger cub. Prepare your torso and legs. Collect a tiger cub with your child from all the components, and he will be happy with the received plasticine animal.

Crocodile bead

This beautiful keychain will be able to weave even novice needlewomen. This crocodile made of beads will be an excellent gift for parents, a child or a friend. Craft is carried out in the technique of "parallel weaving", which is the simplest. Two shades of green beads perfectly convey the color of genuine reptile skin.





Two rows of one bead are strung on a wire or fishing line, then six rows in two. Colors must be combined. The next four rows are made of three beads, then two of five, two of four, one of three, three of four, two of three, and the final row of four. This great creative idea will be finally implemented when you complete the crocodile's legs. Do not forget to make a loop for this keyring so that you can attach it to keys or a bag. Care for bead products should be carried out as needed: wipe them with a dry brush and do not allow water to enter.
