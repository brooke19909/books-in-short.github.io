Anapa: recreation centers. Tourists reviews

Many residents of our large country managed to visit such a wonderful resort as Anapa at least once in their life. Recreation facilities in this city amaze with their quantity. It is worth visiting at least one of them to plunge into the relaxed atmosphere. Feeling the warmth of the gentle sun, the salty smell of the sea, you can plunge into the modern industry of recreation and entertainment.



Truly a paradise is in the resort of Anapa Vityazevo. Recreation facilities located here, everyone wants to visit. This extraordinary place is located twenty kilometers from Anapa. It received its name in honor of one of the heroes of the Russian-Turkish war, whose name was Vityaz. What is this center? A completely modern resort, which some call the "Greek village." This is due to the fact that a large number of ethnic Greeks have densely settled here.

The endless local beaches, which are covered with fine sand, attract tourists who want to relax in a resort like Anapa. The recreation centers are very pleased with their guests not only during the swimming season, which lasts from May to September, but also during the rest of the year. For those who like to stretch out in the sun, a coastal zone is represented, the width of which is about one hundred meters. Reviews from tourists reflect the positive impression of this resort. A vacation spent here remains in the memory for a long time.

Family Vacation Center

Of course, a convenient option for spending time with children, where family recreation centers are represented - Anapa. Reviews of satisfied tourists speak for themselves. The sea is clean and transparent, and the average temperature of the water ranges from twenty to twenty-five degrees.





The bottom is flat, there are no hollows and depth drops here. Children can enjoy a banana ride. For a nominal fee, kids will receive ten minutes of vivid emotions and a positive charge. For the whole family, catamarans can be rented. After a sea holiday, the whole family is waiting for an amusement park. There, children will get a bunch of impressions, a ride on the carousel and swing.

Nutrition

Recreation facilities in Anapa suggest a varied menu. Nevertheless, there is an opportunity to treat yourself to unusual treats, taking a walk along the wide promenade. Along the way, you can also look into shops and cafes, which offer a huge amount of delicious dishes and products. Even the most picky foodie will be able to satisfy their tastes. Having visited the market, one can be surprised at the variety of fruits and vegetables displayed on the windows. Pricing is affordable, so you can treat yourself to exotic fruits.



Treatment
