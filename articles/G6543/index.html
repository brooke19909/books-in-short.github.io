Location

Hotel PK Resort and Villas Jomtien Beach 3 * is one of the city hotels in Pattaya. It is located south of the city, on Jomtien Beach. The hotel is located near the coast, on the first coastline. The beach is across the road, 300 meters from the hotel. Bangkok's Suvarnabhumi Airport, where tourists arrive, is located 128 kilometers away. And 34 kilometers separate the hotel from U-Tapao Airport. The road to the hotel in the first case will take a little more than an hour, and from U-Tapao about half an hour. If you use a group transfer, travel time will double by taking tourists to their hotels.

Fans of staying at the city hotels in Pattaya are often focused on active nightlife. Night entertainment center - Walking Street is 5.7 km from the hotel (10 minutes by car).

The hotel was renovated in 2009. The hotel territory has two zones - Resort and Villas. When checking in, a deposit of 1000 baht is charged (per room). For leisure tourists PK Resort Villas Jomtien Beach 3 * hotel offers 81 standard rooms and bungalows.

The bungalows also have standard rooms. Their area is 40 square meters. m, the maximum can accommodate three people in the main and additional places.

The PK Resort Villas Jomtien Beach 3 * hotel is small in scale. It is located in a tropical garden. Corporate clients can use the services of the conference room. A store is located on site.
