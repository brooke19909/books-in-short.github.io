How to remove odors in the refrigerator correctly?

Often a situation arises when a person opens the refrigerator, and the most unappetizing smells fly out of it . How to remove odors in the refrigerator? This problem can be solved.

  • Firstly, it is necessary to identify the cause of these odors. Spoiled foods, a leaking freezer, foods high in spices - all this causes stench in the refrigerator.
  • Secondly, it is necessary to thoroughly rinse all the compartments of the refrigerator. To wash this device requires special solutions, since simple soapy water will not help here. The industry produces appropriate cleaning fluids, but you can also resort to folk remedies, which are also quite effective. Among the most common folk methods that are used to rinse the refrigerator, you can name a warm soda solution, which is made in the proportion of 1 tablespoon of soda per 1 liter of water. You can also replace soda with vinegar or ammonia in this recipe. But if someone does not tolerate the pungent odor of these substances, then it is better to stop at soda.
  • Thirdly, after cleaning activities, it is necessary to leave the refrigerator in the open state for ventilation for a while (about an hour).
