"Aibga" (3 * hotel), Krasnaya Polyana, Sochi: reviews, photos

Quite attractive for tourists from all over Russia and neighboring countries is "Aibga" (3 * hotel). Krasnaya Polyana is a popular resort that is famous for its natural beauties. And comfortable living, as you know, is no less important than infrastructure.

Short description

"Aibga" (3 * hotel, Krasnaya Polyana) offers tourists a high level of service and a sense of home comfort. At any time of the day, receptionists are ready to help you, who will give answers to your questions. Among the infrastructure facilities, it is worth highlighting a small clean pool, which will be comfortable for both adults and children. And of course, in the age of modern technology, the availability of free wireless Internet is important.

Comfortable modern rooms can be offered to you by Aibga (3 * hotel). Krasnaya Polyana is famous for its natural beauties, and therefore you will be pleased to contemplate them from your own balcony. Also in the guest apartments there is a set of comfortable furniture, as well as the most modern amenities needed by any tourist.

In the hotel complex "Aibga" (3 * hotel, Krasnaya Polyana), they also remember how important food is for vacationers. The price includes a breakfast buffet. At any other time during the day, you can visit the restaurant and try the dishes presented on the menu. You will have the opportunity to taste Russian and European cuisines. There is also a bar that offers an excellent selection of drinks and snacks.





Where is the hotel located

Where is the "Aibga" (3 * hotel)? Krasnaya Polyana, "Rosa Khutor" - an incredibly picturesque place. The hotel is located by the mountain range of the same name, due to which the area has very picturesque views and a unique microclimate. You will have to travel a little less than an hour to the city of Sochi. It is worth noting that trips there are regularly organized for hotel guests.

Guest apartments

"Aibga" (3 * hotel) hospitably opens its doors to its guests comfortable apartments. The following options will be available to you:

  • In the standard room you are given the possibility of double occupancy on large or twin beds. It is also possible to provide additional space. A prerequisite in this room is a bathroom and a balcony, and from the equipment you will have access to a telephone and a TV.
  • The superior standard is suitable for fully accommodating three guests in twin beds. An additional guest will be provided with a folding bed. Unlike the previous category, here you will be provided with a refrigerator. But the balcony in this apartment is not provided.
  • If you want to save on accommodation, then especially for you there is a budget option of accommodation. It is designed for 4 guests with the possibility of providing an extra bed. Here you will have a complete set of necessary household appliances, but a balcony is not provided. As for the toilet and shower, they will be shared throughout the floor.




Studios

Studio is the most comfortable option that Aibga can offer (3 * hotel, Krasnaya Polyana). Photos of rooms made by tourists demonstrate a high level of comfort, as well as a stylish interior. You can live in the following apartments:

  • Studio is a room designed for two guests with a large bed. There is also a set of upholstered furniture. The apartments have their own bathroom and balcony, and you will also have the opportunity to watch TV and make an international call.
  • Superior studio includes triple occupancy, as well as an extra bed. If the room is located on the second floor, a balcony will be a nice addition.
  • Comfort Studio offers not only comfortable double occupancy, but also a modern kitchen where you can cook your favorite dishes on your own. Related services are also worth noting. It is about breakfast, as well as transfer to the ski lift.
  • Premier Studio is located on the fourth floor, from where an unforgettable mountain landscape opens. Despite the fact that the room is quite small, it has its own zest, one of which is a bathtub with hydromassage. It is also possible to provide a cot as an extra bed.
  • The station wagon is a fairly spacious studio, designed for four guests. There is a small kitchen with a microwave. A feature of the room is the presence of a separate entrance, which is important enough for a large company to live.

  • modern widescreen TV that broadcasts both satellite and cable channels;

Entertainment and outdoor activities

If the place of your vacation is "Aibga" (3 * hotel, Russia, Sochi, Krasnaya Polyana), you will not have to be bored, because there are a lot of opportunities for those who want to spend their holidays fun and actively:

  • on the territory there are several sites with gazebos and barbecue facilities;

Hotel Accommodation Information

A rather attractive vacation option is Aibga (3 * hotel, Sochi, Krasnaya Polyana). Accommodation in this hotel has some features, as well as the rules to which both employees and guests must comply:

  • you can check into the hotel only after two in the afternoon, and must leave before noon (the exception is the partial occupancy of the hotel, when the schedule is changed for an additional fee);
  • It is strictly forbidden to bring animals of any size and breed to the hotel;
  • in the case when a child under 6 years old does not need extra bed, no fee will be charged for his stay;
  • a folding bed for a child under 12 years old will cost 1000 rubles per night;
  • an extra bed for adults every day will cost 1,500 rubles;
  • You can use a plastic credit card to pay for hotel services in cashless form;
  • if the purpose of your visit is a working trip, then you will be provided with all official documents and checks regarding payment for accommodation in this hotel complex;
  • throughout the resort, with the exception of certain areas, smoking is strictly prohibited.

Failure to comply with these rules will incur fines.

Negative reviews of tourists

In any hotel complex, in addition to advantages, to one degree or another, there are drawbacks and shortcomings. Aibga (3 * hotel) was no exception. Reviews indicate the following negative moments of relaxation in this institution:

  • excessively high cost of living on holidays;
  • the sources give false information about the presence of three stars (in fact , the hotel is not accredited);
  • very high cost of renting a sauna;
  • there are minor breakdowns in the rooms, to which the staff does not respond in a timely manner;
  • some apartments have poor wireless internet;
  • inflated prices in the restaurant;
  • the rooms do not have air conditioning, which is especially felt in the summer;
  • construction debris is visible from the windows of a number of apartments;
  • monotonous breakfasts.

"Aibga" (3 * hotel) is simply an ideal place both for family vacations and for a weekend in a noisy company. Here you can fully enjoy the natural beauties of the Krasnodar Territory. The hotel's peculiarity can be considered a cozy home environment and a friendly attitude towards guests.
