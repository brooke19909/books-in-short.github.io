Nokia Asha 210: photos, prices, reviews

At the beginning of 2013, Nokia Asha 210 appeared on sale. Despite the fact that this model was introduced more than a year ago, it is still being successfully sold. Technical specifications, characteristics, advantages and disadvantages of the product - this is what will be considered in the framework of this article.





About the line

Conventionally, the products of the Nokia company are currently divided into three segments. The first of these is entry-level devices that only allow you to make calls and receive (or send) SMS. They are indicated by three or four digits. In common parlance, they are simply called "dialers." The second group of devices is the Asha line. It includes inexpensive and affordable devices. They have basic functionality, which is quite enough for everyday use. As the name implies, the Nokia Asha 210 belongs to this particular device group. The third segment is Lumiya smartphones. Basically, this manufacturer produces devices running the Windows operating system (although there are exceptions). Moreover, such devices can solve almost all problems. But their cost is many times higher.

Communications

Let's continue the review of Nokia Asha 210 Dual and consider all available ways to connect to this device. And here things are going well with the monitored device. There is support for Wi-Fi (for connecting to the global Web) and Bluetooth (allows you to transfer data to other similar devices). There is no support for third-generation networks, but there is only GSM, that is, 2G. Given the special technology for compressing Internet pages with a built-in browser, a speed of several hundred kilobytes per second is quite enough for a comfortable viewing of content. Also, the device is equipped with a standard 3.5 mm jack for connecting an external speaker system. For charging and connecting to a computer, a microUSB connector is used, which today has become the standard for most mobile devices.

Memory

With the built-in memory, Nokia Asha 210 Dual is doing very poorly. The feedback from the many owners of such gadgets around the Internet is another confirmation of this. Only 32 kilobytes are integrated into the telephone, which is very small. The only way to solve the existing problem is to install an external microSD card. The phone is equipped with an appropriate connector and supports drives up to 32 GB. Therefore, when buying a new such device, it is imperative to purchase it. Otherwise, it will not be possible to fully reveal the capabilities of this device. There are no other ways to increase the amount of memory in this device.

Summary

Regarding the prospects of the Nokia Asha 210, everything is clear. This model, like the brand itself, will go into oblivion in the foreseeable future.
