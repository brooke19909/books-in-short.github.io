Anapa: hotels by the sea. Anapa: private hotels by the sea

Since time immemorial, the Black Sea coast has attracted people. Here they preferred to set up their settlements. Studies by archaeologists have also shown that in the vicinity of the current city of Anapa, the first people appeared already twelve thousand years ago.

Resort Description

In 1829, Anapa became part of the Russian Empire. It has been considered a resort since 1866. Currently, the resort city of Anapa occupies an area equal to 98 thousand hectares. One hundred thirty two thousand people live in it. Anapa is located at the junction of the Kuban-Priazovskaya lowland and the Greater Caucasus. The length of the beaches of the resort area reaches eighty kilometers. Half of them are pebble. They are located in the Small Bay, on the High Bank, as well as in state reserves - Small and Big Utrish.





In Anapa you can observe an amazing variety of landscapes. On its territory is the Sukko Valley with pistachio groves and relic junipers, a flat plateau, sea estuaries and Caucasian foothills. This amazing place has a humid and mild Mediterranean climate. During the year there are two hundred and eighty sunny days. All these factors allow the swimming season to last from May to October. Sanatoriums of the resort city are invited to treatment throughout the year.

Relax in a sunny resort

Anapa is included in the list of historical cities of Russia. On its twenty-five centuries, there were many sharp turns and various events. This city-museum of antiquity was completely destroyed during the war with Nazi Germany. But already in the fifties of the last century it was restored, and today Anapa is a resort with a level of service comparable to the European one.



It has become a good tradition for residents of Russia to spend a family vacation in Anapa. The beaches of the city are a great place for children. Parents should not be afraid to let them splash around in the sea, which is warm and very shallow on the coast.
