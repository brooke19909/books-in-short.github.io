Hotel Sea Club 5 (Egypt / Sharm El Sheikh)

Description. Youth complex Sea Club 5 is one three-story building and a number of two- and three-story cottages, which are located 20 km from the airport. All these buildings are located on a large landscaped area and are surrounded by a green park with tropical plants. The buildings were built in 2001 in one of the most popular resorts in Egypt - in Sharm El Sheikh right on the Red Sea coast. In 2011, the hotel was renovated and now it is a completely renovated and beautiful guest house, offering the best conditions for living.



On the same territory with the Sea Club 5 complex there are two more hotels Sea Gardens and Sea Life - guests can freely use the infrastructure of neighboring hotels. The resort center is designed for couples with children, adult tourists, businessmen and newlyweds. You can also recommend it to fans of diving and outdoor activities, as it is here that you will find a lot of various fascinating activities for interests.

Number of rooms. The hotel has 359 rooms and most of them with views of the Red Sea, the rest overlooking the courtyard. All rooms are quite spacious with quality repairs and furniture. Each room has a bathroom and a terrace. There is a central air conditioning system, direct dial telephone, free safe, satellite TV (Russian channels are broadcast).



In addition, customers can use the minibar (water - for free) with drinks for a fee and high-speed Internet. Daily cleaning and bed changes are included in the total price. For a fee round-the-clock service.

Nutrition. Full board three times a day according to the Super All-Inclusive system is offered by Sea Club 5. Reviews from tourists are laudatory - everyone liked the varied menu and 24-hour free drinks. At lunchtime, the hotel's restaurant serves free ice cream, while honeymooners and birthday guests enjoy fruit and cake. You can always grab a bite to eat at the lobby bar and poolside bar.

There are four restaurants on the territory: Japanese, Lebanese, French and fish - during their stay, guests can eat for free once in each of them. For children - a separate menu and chairs.

Beach. A few steps from the hotel is a white sandy beach. The 500-meter-long coastline belongs to the Sea Club 5 Hotel. A lagoon has been built for small children with a convenient entrance to the water. All beach amenities are free. There is a 24-hour bar. From entertainment: diving, catamaran, windsurfing, volleyball, jet skis.

Additional Information. In the Sea Club 5 hotel you will find the following list of services: an outdoor pool (it is heated in cold weather), a business center, a gym, a health club with spa treatments. The hotel also has its own amphitheater, tennis courts, a billiard room, a disco club with live music, a shopping center, a beauty salon, a medical room and a free car park.



For children, a playground is equipped, an outdoor heated pool 0.6 meters deep, there is a development club and even a disco. From time to time, animated shows are held with cartoon characters and contests.

Digest. Truly a royal holiday offers the complex Sea Club Resort 5. Reviews of tourists about this hotel are only positive. A secluded place is perfect for a quiet, laid-back and measured pastime. There are no noisy roads, excessive traffic, round-the-clock clubs, but there is a lot of sun, a gentle sea, an abundance of coral beaches, a wide variety of entertainment and sightseeing routes.

The picturesque hotel has established itself only on the good side, because it offers high-quality service for both children and adults. A special atmosphere of hospitality and goodwill reigns in the hotel - be sure that your vacation at this resort will never be forgotten, and pleasant memories will warm you on cold days.
