Lindos Royal Hotel 4 * (Lindos): reviews and photos

The Greek islands have always been and remain the most attractive tourist destination for travelers from all over the world, and among them one of the most popular is Rhodes - the famous island that is mentioned in the legend of the Odyssey.

In this article we will talk about the beautiful resort city of Lindos and the wonderful Lindos Royal Hotel 4 *, which is located on its coast. This hotel is considered the best not only in this resort, but throughout the island of Greece. Each of the guests, having crossed the threshold of this magnificent four-star hotel, immediately feels himself a welcome guest. Helpful staff is ready to fulfill their every desire, to help in solving any problems.





Pearl of the Mediterranean

Lindos is a small seaside village, which is also a valuable archaeological site. It is located on the southeastern coast of about. Rhodes, on the picturesque cape of Cranes. From the island‚Äôs capital, where local air gates are located, this resort is 46 kilometers away. Its coast is two beautiful harbor bays, and the town itself seems to lie at the base of an impregnable cliff, which is crowned by an acropolis. Among the sights of the village is the fortress of the Knights of the Order of St. John the Baptist and the small antique temple of the goddess Athena, which was erected in the 4th century BC. e. If you purchase a tour to Lindos Royal Hotel 4 * in Rhodes, you can thoroughly, without haste, inspect these historical monuments. For tourists planning to visit Fr. Rhodes and historical places of interest, and only then - beach holidays, it is better to buy tours either at the beginning or at the end of summer or even in the velvet season. Indeed, in the summer heat, under the scorching sun, visiting tourist sites under the open sky is extremely inconvenient.

Description

The name of one of the two harbors was given in honor of the holy Apostle Paul. According to legend, he arrived here in 57 A.D. e. and began to preach to the locals the Christian faith.

Before his arrival, the Lindos revered the elder Cleobulus, one of the seven outstanding ancient Greek sages. By the way, his grave is one of the most visited historical monuments and shrines on the island. This is a round cylindrical structure, which has a burial chamber made of stone (6th century BC).

Another famous monument of ancient architecture is the "Archocrat", which is a monumental building of the Hellenistic era. It is also included in the required excursion route. Representatives of the Arkhocratic family are buried here.

Almost all tourists who came to the island of Rhodes come to these attractions. To do this, they have to travel tens of kilometers by bus, but vacationers in Lindos hotels, for example, in the Lindos Royal Hotel 4 * (in the ranking of local hotels, it occupies a leading position), can make walking tours to them, and at that time, when there are fewer tourists, for example, in the early morning or evening, after sunset.

In addition to the ancient shrines, there are many Christian ones. So on the approach to Lindos, in the village of Archangelos, there are the temple and monastery of Panagia Tsambika. It stores the miraculous icon of the Mother of God, which patronizes those women who wish to soon become pregnant. Here you will always see childless couples from all over the world.

How to get there

As already noted, Lindos from the island's capital - the city of the same name Rhodes - is located 48 kilometers. Since the island‚Äôs only airport is located in the capital, the plane you fly on will take you there, and only then you will go to the hotel either by land (by car or bus) or by water (boarding a boat in the port of Mandraki )

Location

The hotel is built on the southeast coast of Rhodes, on the second beach line. That is why the beach season here lasts a little longer than in other areas of the island. Just a few minutes walk from it, a beautiful sandy beach opens in front of tourists, which surrounds the picturesque bay. From the city of Lindos to the complex - 4 kilometers. Rhodes Airport, where tourists arrive, is 48 kilometers away.

Number of rooms

In total, the Lindos Royal Hotel 4 * 337 rooms of three categories:

  • Double Room - they consist of one room-bedroom with a large double bed and are designed for two guests.
  • Twin Room - unlike the first, these rooms have two twin beds. They are also designed for 2 guests.
  • Junior Suite - rooms of this category consist of two rooms - a bedroom and a living room. Some of these rooms have a double bed in the bedroom, while others have two single beds. In the living room there are two armchairs-beds. So in these rooms a group of 4 people can freely live. The rooms are separated by an arch.




Description of rooms

The entire interior of the Lindos Royal Hotel 4 * is created in the style of colonial architecture. The floor is tiled. All rooms have either balconies or terraces, and the rooms on the first floor have verandas. All are furnished with outdoor furniture (table and chairs). They are very convenient for relaxing here in the evenings with a cup of tea or coffee. The rooms are equipped with air conditioning, TVs with satellite channels, there is a telephone with direct international dialing, mini fridge; safe.

The bathroom includes a hairdryer, towels, and all the necessary toiletries. In their reviews, tourists Lindos Royal Hotel 4 * say that sometimes the wait staff is in no hurry with the addition of shampoo or toilet paper, and also hesitates with the change of towels and linen. However, if necessary, this can be reported to the duty officer on the floor or building, and then this issue will be resolved in a matter of minutes. Cleaning is carried out daily, room service is round-the-clock (for a fee).

Lindos Royal Hotel 4 * (Rhodes): reviews and ratings of tourists

What do the former guests write about this hotel? A lot of good and a little bad. So, almost all guests consider the hotel's pluses amazing service in restaurants and cafes. Everyone eagerly praises the chef, who masterfully prepares international dishes. They are fed exclusively with fresh products, seafood, grilled fish and even sushi. Well-groomed territory is also considered a great advantage. And most of the tourists are satisfied with the friendliness and helpfulness of the staff.

But on the issue of cleanliness in Lindos Royal Hotel 4 *, the reviews are mixed. Some praise the work of maids, while others, on the contrary, complain about their unnecessary and uncleanliness. Among the minuses is an uncomfortable beach. Tourists say that because of the unsuccessful entry into the sea, many then go with abrasions. However, this issue has a solution: next to the hotel there is another, small-pebble beach, which is much more convenient for lazy relaxation, however, for sunbeds and umbrellas here you will need to pay about 7 euros per day. In all other respects, tourists are very pleased with both the service and the internal structure of the hotel, and many of them write that they will certainly return here again.
