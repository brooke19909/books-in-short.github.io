Sundress crocheted for women. Crochet sundress patterns

Many needlewomen at least once in their life tried to crochet a sundress. For women aged, patterns with a dense pattern are more suitable, and young ladies prefer openwork and sirloin knitting with decorative elements.

Ideas for beginner knitters

It is difficult for beginners to switch to knitting large products, since many lack the patience to pore over the same pattern for many months. There are several ways to speed up the creation of large products.

  • Separate motives. Knitting individual motifs increases performance through quick results. They can be knitted in any free time, even in traffic jams. Only such a crocheted sundress for beginners requires a clear pattern.
  • Knitting and sewing. Sewing will help speed up the creative process. Sew a skirt of a sundress according to the pattern, and sew a knitted bodice to it. You can decorate the seam next to the arches, and decorative elements on the top, made in the color scheme of the bottom of the product, will create a holistic harmonious image.
  • Openwork and filet pattern. Pineapples, squares, nets knit quickly, so a sundress will turn out in a couple of days. Gleams are decorated with knitted flowers or hem fabric lining. But it is best to switch to a tight pattern in the chest and hips.

Sundresses for girls (crochet): knitting patterns





If you do not have much experience in knitting and cutting, then you can go the following way.

  • Measure the length of the product from the armpit to the knee and straps, the volume of the chest, waist, hips.
  • Start knitting with a bodice. Tie the chain, close it in a circle, continue to work with a filet net (alternating cape stitches and loops).
  • After 5-7 centimeters, go to the multi-colored herringbone pattern (* 3 loops, 4 double crochet with two loops between them *). Alternate vertical stripes of red, blue, blue, pink, white in a circle. There are two repetitions of a pattern in a bodice, in the skirt their number is gradually increasing.
  • From the waist, you begin to add loops, while the extreme rows of stripes do not change. That is, an increase is made inside the color ornament.
  • For ten centimeters from the knee, go to the filet grid.
  • You end the row with an arched pattern (in one loop of 5-10 capitals, where the extreme ones are connected by a connecting loop to the product itself).
  • From the top, knit the straps.
  • The bodice is decorated with two lush flowers.

Crochet sundress for women according to the napkin pattern

Beginners can not complain that they only knit napkins. The needlewomen came up with chic models of napkins that can be located on the sides, front, bottom or become the basis for the whole dress. The latter model is more suitable for novice craftswomen who knit a napkin, and after it is finished knit a dress according to any pattern, since in the first two cases, the napkins can ‚Äúincorrectly‚Äù take on the shape of the body, distorting the intended image.

Let us consider in more detail the description of the sundress, where the napkin becomes the support of the whole product.

  • Make a full-length pattern. Indicate on it schematically the dimensions of the napkin, outline the oblique bottom of the product.
  • Knit a napkin according to the pattern. As soon as its dimensions reach the boundaries indicated on the pattern, immediately proceed to knitting the bodice, neck and straps. In this case, from the bodice, knitting continues in a circle.
  • Systematically apply the product to the pattern to adjust the shape.
  • Stitch seams.




If you make a fabric lining, then prick on the fabric and grind. And if you want to tie such a sundress for babies, then just tie two napkins, add a strap and grind the seams.

Sundress from the elements

Elements can be large, small, regular and irregular in shape. Therefore, first make patterns in full growth. Then tie the item you like. Spend all the work with him to get clear dimensions (measure, wash, iron, measure again and compare the parameters).

On the patterns with a pencil, draw the shapes of the motifs to find out their exact number. So you will clearly see if there are gaps and what sizes. They can be closed with smaller details or simply tie the resulting motives in 1-2 rows. This can happen if it is a "floral" dress (crochet).

The scheme of such an element will be as follows:

  • dial 6 air loops;
  • tie 6 petal-cones (5 ‚Äúcolumn‚Äù columns in one loop);
  • alternate a half-column with two air loops (while the latter are above the petals);
  • knit a half-column over a similar element of the previous row, and in the air loops you knit an arch of 5 columns (at the edges are simple columns, and in the middle with a crochet).

To make the product bright, use multi-colored threads. Please note that the sundress of lush flowers is suitable for slim and young girls. It is better for full or business ladies to pay attention to flat elements. For example, squares look just as bright and unusual.

To choose your color, draw squares of the required sizes on a piece of paper, color with pencils the color of the threads and tie this scheme, where one cell indicates a certain number of columns.
