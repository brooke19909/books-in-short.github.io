A comfortable vacation in Egypt depends on many factors, among which the hotel plays a special role. In this country, most hotels rent or buy beaches near their territory, so vacationers can get beach accessories for free. And living in a hotel on the first line of the sea is much more pleasant.

Not everyone can afford to spend a vacation in a 5-star hotel, so in our article we will look at some 4-star Sharm El Sheikh hotels.

Beach Albatros Sharm El Sheikh





The presented beach resort is located on the cliff of Ras Umm al-Sid - tourists can enjoy panoramic views of Mount Sinai. The hotel invites guests to visit a private beach, play tennis on the equipped court and enjoy a swim in one of several pools.

The reserved accommodation at Beach Albatros is quite elegant and sophisticated. Like other 4- star hotels in Sharm El Sheikh , Beach Albatros offers rooms of several categories: Standard Double and Standard Triple. The interior is filled with notes of Egyptian flavor. Each room is equipped with a minibar, satellite TV and a private bathroom. Room service is available daily at a time convenient for tourists. The room price starts from 2494 rubles.

This resort has 2 adult freshwater pools and 2 children's pools. The length of the beach in the hotel is about 1200 m. Guests can go down to it in a panoramic elevator. The hotel offers buffet meals, Egyptian and international dishes.

Almost every day, the hotel organizes events and games. You can go to the Internet cafe, visit the fitness center and numerous shops.

Marmara Hotel is located on Om El Sid Hill. The hotel rooms are offered in three variations: Standard Single, Standard Double with Standard Bed, Standard Triple. Each room has a balcony with a wonderful view of the Red Sea. In addition, guests are offered the gifts of civilization: air conditioning, satellite TV, hairdryer, private bathroom. The price for a room starts to rise from 1,518 rubles.

The main restaurant of the hotel complex offers a buffet. Traditional pizza and fresh seafood are honored here. Also guests can visit 3 bars, cafe, night club. The close proximity of Marmara to the Mercato Mall makes shopping at any time possible.

The hotel has 3 freshwater pools, SPA, hydromassage. The property of the hotel complex includes its own beach, which can be reached by free bus.
