Hotel Clubhotel Riu Bellevue Park 4 * (Sousse, Tunisia): photos and reviews of tourists

Tunisia has a huge number of hotel complexes for receiving tourists from all over the world, one of which is a modern hotel called ClubHotel Riu Bellevue Park 4 *. Reviews about this complex testify to its popularity among Russian-speaking tourists. Tunisia, in principle, is called the Russian resort for its accessibility and excellent conditions for recreation.

Short description





ClubHotel Riu Bellevue Park 4 * hotel is located at a distance of 3 kilometers from the port of El Kantaoui and 14 kilometers from the famous city of Sousse. The local airport is approximately 20 km away.
Near the hotel there is a chic shopping complex of solid size, where everyone can purchase everything they need. In addition, miniature shopping pavilions are located nearby, and having rented a transfer at the hotel’s expense, you can easily go shopping. Also, the transfer will help to easily get to the fields where all vacationers have the opportunity to play golf.





The central zone of the city is located at a distance of 10 km from the ClubHotel Riu Bellevue Park 4 * complex (Tunisia). Nearby are 3 major airports: Monastir, Tunis and Enfida. To Tunis Airport, the route is 145 kilometers, to Monastir - 28 kilometers, and to Enfida - 40 kilometers. The hotel was built in 1994, and the last major reconstruction was carried out in 2006.

The building includes 4 floors. Hotel rooms can accommodate an extra bed for a small child upon request. Children's leisure is complemented by fascinating animation with many games and contests, and the kids club has age restrictions from 4 to 12 years. Arriving at the resort and checking into this wonderful hotel, no one will remain indifferent.

Accommodation

Each room of the ClubHotel Riu Bellevue Park 4 * hotel (Tunisia, Sousse) meets all modern standards of the hotel business. The rooms have balconies, climate control systems, televisions, refrigerators, bathrooms with showers and hair dryers.

They clean the apartments and change their linen regularly, the front desk is open 24 hours a day, and wireless high-speed Internet can be used at any time.

Leisure

Rest at the hotel is diverse and suitable for customers with any preferences. There is a territory for playing tennis, and a playground for aerobics, and several outdoor pools with hydromassage and heating, and a playground in the yard, and a children's development club with different types of leisure activities for younger guests.

If you want, you can have fun riding a horse. For women, massage sessions are provided in all kinds of variations, there is also a hairdresser, where you can easily change the appearance beyond recognition.

The hotel is very popular for saunas, massage services, hammam and jacuzzi. The pool is available not only for adults, but also for children, in which the main feature and plus is the shallow depth, which allows children to swim on their own, and parents not to feel fear because their beloved child can swallow water.

On your own hotel beach you can rent water activities, and sun loungers, mattresses and other devices for sunbathing are available for free use.

Nutrition





Breakfast is included in the price. The products are always fresh, and a buffet is served every morning. There are fruits, and cereals, and meat, and fish, and yoghurts, and much, much more. There are several bars with cocktails and cold drinks, and one of the bars offers grilled meat and baked vegetables in the evenings.

Each restaurant has a high chair for younger visitors. For children, dishes are prepared according to improved recipes, where there are no extra flavor enhancers, flavors and other chemical additives. Drinks and cocktails bought at the bar are allowed to take with you.

Those lucky enough to spend their holidays in Tunisia can better describe the ClubHotel Riu Bellevue Park.

Guest reviews





At the airport, guests are greeted by a bus that takes tourists to hotels. ClubHotel Riu Bellevue Park is a 40-minute drive away, provided the bus stops at all hotels along the way. By car can be reached in 20 minutes.

Friendly receptionists greet guests.

The hotel is spacious, in good repair and very comfortable. Getting into the hall, you immediately feel the atmosphere of national color.

The room is populated very quickly. When resettling, each guest wears a bracelet. From 6 am on the ground floor of the hotel there is a cafe where you can have a full breakfast. Very convenient if an early check-in or tour.

Exotic trees, flowers and shrubs grow on the well-kept area. The staff monitors cleanliness.

Comments on the number of rooms





Most often, the hotel booked a double apartment. Some of these rooms offer a view of the park, others with sea views. Tourists recommend choosing the first option, as the side of the sea coast is very noisy. In the evening there are discos and animation programs.

The rooms themselves are spacious and bright. In addition to the huge euro bed, there is also an extra bed in the form of a folding sofa. The room also has a spacious wardrobe, mirror, air conditioning, TV. Internet can be used at an additional cost, but the signal is weak. Among the shortcomings, hotel guests note some inaccuracies in the operation of the bathroom and sewage system.

About nutrition

Hotel guests recommend taking all-inclusive meals. An early breakfast is served at the café from 6am. You can also have breakfast in the main restaurant, but from 7.30 to 10.00. The guests of the complex dine near the beach bar in the open air from 12.00 to 15.00. There is a lot of food, and all of it is varied and tasty. The assortment always has ripe fruits. For sweets - ice cream and delicious desserts. Each guest will find something edible for himself and certainly will not remain hungry. All drinks, except alcoholic drinks, are in the public domain. Alcohol is served at the bar. In addition to the main meals, there are snacks in the form of pancakes and sandwiches.

For dinner, tables are served in the main restaurant. On the first visit, the restaurant administrator assigns a table to each room. You can have dinner from 18.30 to 19.45 or from 20.15 to 21.30. The food is lighter and less varied than at lunch, but the quality of the dishes is on top.

About staff

The hotel staff is friendly and quick to respond to any requests from customers, trying to please. It seems that they went through a courtesy school. Hotel guests are treated with awe and are even interested in health and mood. All misunderstandings are resolved very quickly. The staff does not speak Russian well, but is fluent in English.

The quality of service at the ClubHotel Riu Bellevue Park (Tunisia) is constantly monitored. During dinner, each visitor should fill out a questionnaire in which he indicates whether everything suits him and what he lacks.

The rooms are cleaned daily; tips on the quality of cleaning are not affected. Maids begin to work only in the absence of guests.

Hotel guides describe excursion routes in detail, without imposing anything. Issue cards with marked local attractions.

Sea

The beach area is strewn with sunbeds, there are always free places. Annoying merchants often go along the beach, offering various goods. But beach workers, caring for a comfortable stay for hotel guests, simply annoying sellers are simply kicked out.

The sea is not very clean. The water is cloudy and smells bad due to algae and small debris. But after cleaning the beach, swimming in the sea is a pleasure. Sometimes you can meet jellyfish. They can even sting. But the hotel staff will quickly provide first aid. The beach belongs to the hotel. All accessories are free.

Entertainment





Animators try to entertain guests throughout the day. During the day, various competitions, dances, aerobics are held. In the evening, arrange discos. Particularly fastidious can go to the nearest night clubs, of which there are a huge number in Sousse. The public of the hotel consists mainly of family people and senior citizens, so young people here can be bored.

Holidays in Tunisia are often accompanied by shopping. Prices in local shops and markets are especially pleasing to European tourists.

Near the hotel there are several shopping centers with fairly high prices. A little further is a four-story complex with good goods and prices. In Tunisia, it is customary to bargain, but only in markets or in private shops. On certain days, a man arrives at the hotel from whom you can cheaply buy souvenirs and other trifles.

Money can be changed at the reception. The course is even better than in exchangers.

At the hotel you can use the services of a photographer and get an excellent photo session. Photos are expensive, cheaper to buy a disk with pictures.

General impressions





For those who like to have a good rest, but are not ready to pay huge sums of money, Tunisia Sousse will be an ideal resort. ClubHotel Riu Bellevue Park - a hotel complex with many features. It has everything you need for a good rest. A huge swimming pool with a children's area, delicious varied food, a private beach, a beautiful area - all this complements the excellent service. Many tourists complain that in Tunisia they are not very good with Russians. But this is definitely not about ClubHotel Riu Bellevue Park. Staff reviews indicate a high quality service. Guests here will definitely be cozy and comfortable.

The only thing that can overshadow the rest is not a very clean sea. But it all depends on the time of stay. On particularly hot days, you can smell the unpleasant smell of rotting algae. In the meantime, diligent workers clean the beach, you can have a good time by the pool or on excursions. Local guides will be happy to tell you about all the important sights of Tunisia. And gifts to relatives and friends can be purchased without even leaving the hotel.
