Rooms Hotel Kazbegi, Georgia: photos, reviews

The Kazbegi Rooms Hotel (Georgia) is located in the small village of Stepantsminda. The territory of the complex practically abuts at the foot of Mount Kazbek. In 7 km is a unique attraction of Georgia - the Trinity Church of the XIV century.





The hotel is quite modern, built in 2012. The Gudauri, Kudebi and Sadzeli lifts are relatively close to the complex. The village center is only 10 minutes walk. Nearby are picturesque views of the river and gardens. Nearby is a bus stop. By local transport you can reach Tbilisi.

Georgia reveals all its splendor to those who choose the Hotel Rooms Kazbegi. Photos of the complex and a detailed description with guest reviews will be presented in this article. The category of the hotel is 4 stars.

Number of rooms

The hotel offers the following room categories for accommodation:

  • Standard apartment with a large bed and beautiful forest views. The cost of such a number is about 290 Georgian lari per day (1 lari is approximately 26 Russian rubles). A fridge and minibar are included. The bathroom includes a shower, a bathroom, all bath amenities, a bathrobe and slippers. It also has a work desk, sofa and seating area. There is parquet on the floor. The room is heated. The width of the bed is more than two meters. Guests can make use of the telephone and watch TV with many cable channels. Additionally, you can use the service "alarm clock". This room has a balcony and air conditioning. The apartments are designed for two people.
  • Standard double room with two beds and beautiful views of the majestic mountains worth 320 GEL. The room is equipped on the same principle as described above. Designed to accommodate two guests.
  • Executive apartment with a large bed. This room offers wonderful views of the forest. Cost of living from 430 GEL. This type of room, unlike the previous ones, is equipped with a wardrobe for clothes and an electric kettle.




  • Executive apartments with mountain views starting at 440 GEL. Two people can comfortably accommodate. The facilities are the same as in the room described above.
  • Double room with one large bed. An electric kettle and coffee machine are also available. Each day, two capsules are provided free of charge.
  • Double room with one bed. It has a balcony and a seating area. Of bath accessories, only bathrobes are provided.
  • Standard double room with two single beds. The windows offer beautiful views of the forest. Cost - from 290 GEL.

For a good rest, the Roms Kazbegi Hotel provides simply ideal conditions. It is better to book rooms in advance, since this place is incredibly popular among tourists, and there may simply not be any free places. Extra beds are subject to availability. Their number is limited, so this issue should also be discussed in advance with the administration. Guests using extra beds are not able to have breakfast for free.

Nutrition

The hotel has a restaurant, a snack bar and a regular bar. It is possible to order food in the room or ask to pack lunch. The restaurant has a special diet menu. It mainly serves dishes of national cuisine. The lobby bar has a cozy atmosphere. Here you can read books or just chat. There is access to a terrace with views of the picturesque landscapes.

Additional service

Laundry, dry cleaning and ironing services are available. For entertainment, a casino works here. There is also free access to the Internet.

Airport shuttle can be arranged at an extra charge. It is possible to rent a bike.

Arriving in your car do not worry about parking. This service is provided free of charge. Preliminary place reservation is not necessary.

Living conditions with children

The hotel accepts guests with children of any age. Children under two years without extra bed stay free. Placing an adult child in an extra bed will cost 60 GEL per night. This room can only accommodate one extra bed.

The best criterion for assessing the quality of service provided by the Hotel Rooms Kazbegi is reviews of vacationers.

Guest Comments

This hotel really deserves positive reviews. The perfect combination of price and quality is its main advantage. Tourists especially note the unsurpassed interior of not only rooms, but also other hotel facilities, such as a restaurant or lobby bar. Everything is made of natural wood. Being in a library or lobby, you can really get lost in time. Sitting in a comfortable chair, reading your favorite book and enjoying a glass of delicious wine, you can really forget about all your problems. A pleasant crack of burning firewood is heard in the fireplace, and a gorgeous view of the mountain peaks opens outside the window.





The coordinated work of the staff is especially delightful. The quality of service is at the highest level. In Georgia, they pay special attention, reverence and awe to guests. The Kazbegi Rooms Hotel is no exception.
