Grand Kaptan Hotel 5 * (Turkey, Alanya): hotel description, service, reviews

Rocky capes on the shore, picturesque bays, impeccable sandy, in some cases shallow pebble beaches, a shallow sea, inexpensive hotel complex services - this is what attracts the unique Alanya of travelers from all over the world. The town itself is located on a cape towering above the sea. Alanya is divided into two parts: modern and ancient. In the old part you can see the citadel, and the modern territory is located a little further, closer to the coast. It is in this part that the large hotel complexes are located. Every year a huge number of young people, families and even senior citizens come here. The resort will gladly accept everyone in its sunny atmosphere.

Where is Alanya located?

The town is located 135 km from Antalya International Airport. After arrival, comfortable buses of tour operators, having chosen the optimal path, will bring guests to the threshold of the selected hotel. On average, the road does not take much time, only 1-2 hours. This aspect depends on the location of the hotel, but due to the convenience of transport, the road will not take much effort, tourists will certainly be pleased.

Travelers arrive in Alanya almost all year round. The swimming season lasts from April to October. But the most favorable time for relaxation is spring and autumn. This time is characterized by consistently magnificent weather. The sea is warm, and light air reigns in the air. The climate of this time is very pleasant, which was appreciated by many tourists.

Places to visit in Alanya

The symbol of the resort is the citadel, built in the 2nd century. It is equipped with 150 towers. A string of sheds, the ruins of ancient mosques, a market - all this without exception has been preserved up to the present and is located between the inner and outer walls of the outpost.

A trip to the Red Tower, which was built back in the 13th century, to protect the town and port from the dangerous raids of sea pirates, is becoming a memorial to many travelers. In the twentieth century, the tower was completely restored. Today it houses an ethnological gallery. A trip to the ancient docks of a shipyard is also fascinating. Docks go deep underground - 40 meters. They could hide a number of ships.

Almost all travelers are attracted to the caves known throughout the resort: Damlatas, Fosforla Magara, Kizlar Magarasi. The climate of Damlatas cave can be useful for those people who have respiratory diseases. In Fosforla Magara Cave, phosphorescent mountains can be seen. Nearby is an ancient cave, it is here that you can get acquainted with the most ancient finds and relics.

Staying in these magnificent places, you must definitely visit the Dim Chai Valley. Incredibly beautiful waterfalls and mountain rivers are located here. In addition to the magnificent natural valleys, there are other interesting places in Alanya. This is a wonderful restaurant located on the water, all of whose tables are on rafts. Staying here is incredibly pleasant, but in addition to the fabulous atmosphere, guests are offered incredibly tasty and exotic dishes.

At the end of the year, a World rally is held in Alanya, which can turn the quiet life of the resort into an atmosphere of magnificent universal fun and incredible joy. As souvenirs to friends, you can bring colored pumpkins (a sign of Alanya) or handmade products, which are abundantly presented in local shops and design studios.

It is no coincidence that Alanya is so adored by many travelers. Here, anyone will find entertainment according to their own taste - both those who seek solitude, and adherents of breathtaking parties. Well-equipped beaches, pine forests, magnificent sea panoramas, many offers for relaxation, a chance to improve your health, reasonable prices in hotels and restaurants - all this can be easily found in Alanya.

One of the best places to stay in the resort is the Hotel Grand Kaptan (5 stars).
