Hotel Letoile Beach Hotel 4 * (Turkey / Marmaris): photos and reviews of tourists

Many tourists consider the village of Icmeler, located about 8 km from the city of Marmaris, the most beautiful resort in Turkey. The size of this settlement is not too large - it is approximately 1.5x1.5 km. But the nature here, according to many vacationers, is simply luxurious. Many travelers consider the disadvantage of this resort to be that, unfortunately, there are not many good hotels built there. However, you can certainly find comfortable accommodation in Icmeler if you wish. For example, Letoile Beach Hotel 4 * offers very good rooms in this resort area to Turkish guests in Marmaris. Reviews from vacationers this complex has earned good.

What tourists think of the location of Letoile Beach

This four-star hotel is located not in the village of Icmeler, but about a kilometer from it, on the seashore. In relation to the beach, most tourists find the hotel located very convenient. To go to the seashore its guests need no more than a few minutes. In the vicinity of Letoile Beach Hotel 4 *, as many travelers say on the sites of tour operators, there are many restaurants and shops. There is even a fairly large market here.





In relation to the sea and the local resort infrastructure, therefore, this complex, according to tourists, is located quite well. But the airport, unfortunately, is far enough from it. Tourists who rent rooms in this complex arrive, usually in Dalaman, to Turkey. This airport is located about 110 km from the hotel. Thus, tourists have to get to it, therefore, at least 2.5 hours. Many travelers, of course, consider this to be some of the minuses of the hotel. But in any case, as noted by vacationers working with the Letoile Beach Hotel, Russian tour operators offer their clients quite comfortable transfer transport.

The city of Marmaris with its entertainment, popular large shopping centers, cafes and restaurants, as already mentioned, is about 8 km from the hotel. Guests of the Letoile Beach Hotel 4 * complex can get here by public transport in this way in about 20-30 minutes. A bus stop is located in Icmeler near the hotel.

general description

This hotel has received its first visitors quite a long time ago - in 1993. The hotel is, therefore, quite old. However, throughout the entire period of existence in the hotel, of course, large-scale renovations were periodically carried out. The last such repair was paid by the administration of the complex in 2012.





Letoile Beach Hotel 4 * in Marmaris consists of one large 5-storey building of original architecture. The yard at this hotel, judging by the reviews, is compact and very ergonomically planned. On the territory of the complex, beautiful southern trees, shrubs and flowers grow everywhere. Against the background of the Icmeler Mountains, somewhat reminiscent of the Caucasus Mountains, this hotel looks, according to tourists, simply magnificent.

Most rooms at Letoile Beach overlook the sea. From the balconies of other rooms, guests can enjoy the mountains. Beautiful views - this, of course, is also what Letoile Beach has earned good reviews from its guests.

About 55% of the contingent of the complex are British. There are usually not very many Russian tourists in a hotel. Therefore, the infrastructure of the hotel is aimed primarily at the residents of the UK. In any case, that was exactly what happened in 2017.

Guest reviews about the rooms

The rooms in Letoile Beach, according to vacationers, are quite comfortable and cozy. The standards here have a relatively small area, the suites, judging by the reviews, are simply huge. Maids clean the hotel rooms daily. Therefore, regarding the cleanliness of the rooms, the Letoile Beach Hotel 4 * (Marmaris) hotel is highly praised by most tourists.

Among the pluses of the hotel rooms, its guests include, among other things, the fact that there are many mirrors hanging everywhere. This, firstly, is very convenient for the guests themselves, and secondly, makes the interior of the rooms even more attractive.

The furniture in Letoile Beach, despite the fact that the hotel is quite old, is installed, as vacationers note, is new and very comfortable. On TV in Letoile Beach, guests can watch Russian-language channels as well. So in the evening, occupying yourself in this hotel is something for residents anyway.

Travelers consider the poor performance of air conditioners in some rooms to be a definite disadvantage of the property rented at the hotel. The weather in Icmeler in summer is actually very hot. Air conditioning in the hotel rooms are provided, according to vacationers, too weak. And if during the day from the heat tourists can still escape to the sea or in the pool, then at night in the rooms of this hotel due to the stuffiness it can be, unfortunately, quite uncomfortable.





Also, some residents of the hotel note the fact that the hairdryers in the shower of this hotel are not provided for too high quality. Unfortunately, they are not particularly convenient in design. Therefore, many tourists advise beginners to take this accessory with them from home.

What is offered to vacationers on the territory

The yard at this hotel, despite the fact that it is very large, is, as already mentioned, small. But it is planned according to tourists, in fact, successfully. In any case, the place on the territory of the Letoile Beach Hotel 4 * (Turkey) is enough for all vacationers, without exception.

  • a gym;

  • billiard room.

Also at the hotel there are: massage room, football and volleyball courts. Many guests play on-site and table tennis. For tourists who rent cars in Turkey, Letoile Beach provides free parking.

Guest opinions on the service provided

To relax in the Letoile Beach Hotel 4 * (Marmaris) in Turkey , according to tourists, you can even very good. The service provided on its territory, as well as the rooms, in most cases, vacationers are satisfied.





The pool in this hotel, for example, according to vacationers, is quite comfortable. It is very pleasant to swim in the morning or evening. Clean and warm at this hotel is not only an adult pool, but also a plunge pool. Guests can let children bathe in it completely without fear. There are a lot of sun beds set by the pool in Letoile Beach, judging by the reviews. In most cases, they are enough for all residents of the hotel. To occupy the sun loungers by the pool, the guests of this hotel usually have only at peak season.

Many travelers and the gym of this complex praise it. It is equipped in a not very large room. But for training, there is, judging by the reviews, absolutely everything you need.

Most tourists consider the disadvantages of the Letoile Beach infrastructure to be mostly just pretty bad internet on the territory. Paying for WiFi at this hotel is quite expensive. At the same time, it catches a network in a hotel, unfortunately, not too well.

Letoile Beach Hotel 4 *: staff reviews

The staff at this hotel, according to tourists, work quite polite. They do not extort a bribe from the guests and they fulfill their duties well. The only thing - the staff of this hotel does not speak either Russian or English. The guests have to communicate with hotel employees either through a hotel guide, or through an interpreter on a smartphone. Little know the Russian language in this complex, only the waiters in the dining room. And even that, more than wish for a pleasant appetite and inquire about the mood of the guests, they are "not enough."





Hotel Restaurant

Of course, travelers book rooms at L etoile Beach Hotel 4 * with meals. The restaurant of this recreation center deserved simply excellent reviews from guests. The food in this complex, according to vacationers, is even better than in many Turkish five-star hotels. The menu at Letoile Beach Hotel is simply exquisite. Yes, and cook the hotel cooks, judging by the reviews, just fine. The hotel dining room operates according to the "buffet" scheme. And there are enough dishes for absolutely all residents.

Very good tourists appreciate the work of the staff of the restaurant Letoile Beach. Workers in the dining room at this hotel are clean, efficient and polite.

In addition to the main meals, this all-inclusive hotel offers guests additional meals. For example, from 2 days to 9 pm, residents of the complex can take in the bar free not only drinks, but also pastries, as well as ice cream.

Cons of the restaurant

The disadvantages of the Letoile Beach dining room are mostly just the not very convenient time reserved for dinner. Many guests, of course, would like to enjoy sunset at sea in Icmeler or just take a walk in the evening. However, instead, they have to rush to dinner, which is allotted from 7 to 9 pm. After nine, in Icmeler, the street usually already darkens. Therefore, some guests wish the hotel administration to extend the dinner time at least up to 10 hours.

Letoile Beach Hotel 4 * (Marmaris): reviews of the sea near the complex

Quite a good opinion among vacationers has developed on the beach of this hotel. The residents of the complex need to go to it no more than 2 minutes. At the same time, they do not have to overcome any dangerous routes on the way.





The sea itself in this place, as vacationers note, is warm and calm, but not too clean. In the morning it is still quite transparent. But after lunch, a little dregs are already rising here.

The beach of the hotel is equipped, according to tourists, is relatively good. For example, there are as many as three showers. There are also many sun loungers on the beach. You can take a free sun lounger here if you arrive at the beach before 11 a.m. There is a small pier near the hotel's beach. Small pleasure boats sometimes dock here. But boats come to the pier not too often and tourists do not interfere with rest.

The main minus of the beach

Letoile Beach Hotel 4 * in Marmaris earned some complaints from vacationers for the mud. Too much rubbish is not lying on the shore here. But still, according to vacationers, the beach at Letoile Beach is not particularly clean. In the sand here you can see paper, butts, and other garbage.
