Grand Hotel Varna: description, photos and reviews

Grand Hotel Varna is one of the most popular accommodation options in Bulgaria. Here you can fully enjoy the quality service and the gentle sea.





a brief description of

One of the most popular hotels in Bulgaria is the Grand Hotel Varna, which boasts an elegant interior, as well as quality service. Since 1977, this institution has been hosting guests, providing them with a quality stay. After the reconstruction of 2006, the hotel acquired a modern look, but did not lose its former charm.

Over the years, the Grand Hotel Varna has received many international awards. The most significant of these are the international prize from the Club of Leaders in Trade (1996), as well as from the US Academy of Commerce (1995).





Location

Grand Hotel Varna is located in one of the most popular regions of Bulgaria. The resort of Saints Constantine and Helena is considered to be elite. He is loved not only by ordinary travelers, but also by some celebrities. The hotel is located 5 km from the central part of Varna, as well as 20 km from the international airport. The hotel is located in a shady park area, which is only 300 meters from the beach.

Hotel rooms

Comfortable living conditions are offered to travelers by the resort Grand Hotel Varna 5 *. Guests are available to accommodate more than 300 rooms in the following categories:

  • The standard involves double occupancy. A pair of orthopedic beds (or one king size), a working area, a bathroom with shower, a spacious balcony - what else is needed for a good rest?
  • A suite is a great option for those who love light and space. The large studio has a huge panoramic window. Functional areas of the living room and bedroom are separated by a bar. The bathroom is equipped with a bath.
  • For lovers of luxury, executive apartments are available. In terms of size and level of comfort, they are in no way inferior to a city apartment. It has a main and a guest bedroom, a living room with leather furniture, a study and a small kitchen area.




Room features

The rooms of the Grand Hotel Varna 5 * resort boast modern equipment and a wide range of amenities. Guests can use the following options:

  • individual air conditioning system for cooling or heating the air temperature in the room, depending on the season;
  • wireless Internet, the use of which is included in the price of the tour;
  • modern widescreen TV connected to a satellite dish;
  • landline phone allows you to make calls abroad, as well as contact the restaurant and reception;
  • minibar with drinking water, as well as alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks;
  • spacious balcony furnished with garden wicker chairs;
  • combined bathroom, which, among other things, is equipped with a hairdryer.




Hotel facilities

You will not need anything if you choose an option such as Grand Hotel Varna (Bulgaria) for your vacation. The following infrastructure facilities operate on the hotel territory:

  • outdoor fresh water pool;
  • indoor swimming pool, which operates in the cold season;

  • a spa center, which is popular among guests of this hotel and among other travelers.




Catering establishments

Particular attention is paid to the full nutrition of guests at the Grand Hotel Varna 5 *. Bulgaria is a country with interesting culinary traditions that every traveler must meet. In this hotel you can visit the following places:

  • The main restaurant is a luxurious dining room with a huge number of seats. Buffet meals are available here. Themed dinners are held each week.
  • Italian restaurant Dolce Vita will give you the opportunity to enjoy traditional pasta and the most delicious pizza. A special atmosphere is maintained thanks to the musical accompaniment.
  • Grand Hot Barbeque is an outdoor cafe with a warm and friendly atmosphere. It serves grilled dishes as well as beer.
  • Susam Asian Restaurant is an ideal place for lovers of fancy food. Here you can taste both your favorite sushi and more exotic dishes with unexpected ingredients and original spices.
  • South Beach - This restaurant serves Mediterranean cuisine. Fresh oysters, mussels and other seafood can be considered a real highlight of the menu.
  • Hot Dog is a cozy snack bar. Here is a huge selection of fast food. You can create a hot dog filling yourself.
  • The Fresh Bar is a great place for fruit lovers. Fresh juices, fruit salads and much more await you at this establishment.
  • Cool Breeze is a small kiosk that serves a wide variety of ice creams throughout the day.
  • South Beach is a beach bar. Cold water, beer, cocktails, snacks - all this and much more can be found by guests in this institution.




Service for guests

You will be surrounded by care and attention if you prefer a resort institution like the Grand Hotel Varna. Here the following services are available to guests:

  • high-quality daily cleaning of rooms, as well as regular replacement of bed linen and bath towels;
  • free use of sun loungers and umbrellas located near the pool;
  • the ability to pay for services with credit cards;
  • transfer both from the airport and in the opposite direction;
  • assistance in organizing celebrations and business events;
  • food delivery from the restaurant to the room.

Vacation with children

Bulgaria with its mild climate and warm sea is a great place to relax with children. So, in the Grand Hotel Varna 4 * hotel, the smallest tourists are provided with the following options:

  • special animation program with fun sports games, competitions and contests;
  • a shallow children's pool, in which, in addition to slides, there are also fountains and inflatable toys;
  • a spacious playground with a safe rubber coating, which presents a lot of horizontal bars, swings and other attractions;
  • if necessary, you can leave your kids under the supervision of a qualified nanny (you must notify the administrator of the order for this service no later than 24 hours);
  • cots and other necessary accessories are provided in the rooms;
  • a playroom in which children will have fun if the weather is bad outside.
