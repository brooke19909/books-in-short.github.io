Divnomorskoe resort: pensions with private beach

Divnomorskoye is a village in the Krasnodar Territory, near Gelendzhik, with a population of more than six thousand people. It is located on the Black Sea, at the mouth of the Inogua River. The resort offers different living conditions, including pensions with a private beach. You can get to Divnomorskoye by regular buses and fixed-route taxis from Gelendzhik and Krasnodar.

Divnomorskoe or fake Gelendzhik

The village of Mezib was located on the site of the village, and it received the name "fake Gelendzhik" during the Russo-Turkish war. The bay, on the bank of which the aul was located, was similar to the Gelendzhik and was on the way of the Turkish squadron. They built a fake Gelendzhik from models of ships and fake coastal fortifications. Distracted by the attack on dummies, the Turks missed the attack of the Russian squadron from the rear and were defeated.

The modern name Divnomorskoe received in 1964, according to rumors, with a light hand of holidaymakers who admired the "marvelous sea". Currently, the resort offers tourists the Divnomorskoe boarding houses with their own beach, motels, mini-hotels and hotels that provide services to a large number of vacationers.



Holidays in Divnomorskoye

The absence of nearby industrial enterprises, healing air annually attract tourists of different ages and affluence. Bars and restaurants, attractions and carousels, night clubs, including the famous Kakadu, where young people gather, are located on the promenade. Therefore, lovers of a relaxing holiday with children are advised not to settle by the sea. In the village of Divnomorskoe boarding houses with a private beach and expensive hotels are adjacent to guest houses, mini-hotels and holiday homes. Do not neglect the large private sector, where you can rent a room for little money. And at the entrance from Gelendzhik there is a motor camp with all the necessary infrastructure.

Sanatoriums and pensions

In the village of Divnomorskoe boarding houses and sanatoriums are represented by several objects of a medical profile as well.

Pension "Flamingo" is a 15-minute walk from the sea, has 178 rooms, outdoor and indoor pools with hydromassage, spa, sauna and fitness center. The treatment base has modern equipment for the treatment of a wide range of diseases. The boarding house hosts cultural and animation events for children. A multifunctional sports complex allows training and training in many sports all year round.



Divnomorskoe: pensions with their own beach "Blue distance" and "Seaside"

The sanatorium "Blue Distance" was built fifty years ago in a picturesque place and is surrounded by a huge park. The boarding house can simultaneously accept more than five hundred people, and the wellness base allows you to treat diseases of the respiratory system, cardiovascular system, musculoskeletal system and many others. For organizing leisure and outdoor activities there are volleyball and basketball courts, a gym, a sauna and a billiard room, cultural events, concerts and discos are held in the concert hall. Pension "Primorsky" has three hundred rooms of different comforts and is able to accept more than nine hundred people at the same time. The proximity to the promenade makes it possible to have a wonderful time with children in the amusement park, open-air theater, which hosts performances and concerts. For lovers of nightlife, discos and nightclubs work. According to the general opinion of vacationers, this is not bad, but the price does not match the service provided.



Departmental sanatoriums

In the village of Divnomorskoe boarding houses with their own departmental subordinate beach "Fakel" and "Energetik" all year round take everyone to recovery.

Gazprom's Fakel has 360 rooms of varying comforts located in modern buildings. Medical profile of the sanatorium: urology, pulmonology, therapy, cardiology. For outdoor activities, a mini-football field, volleyball and basketball courts, tennis courts and badminton courts are equipped. Also at guests' disposal are a bowling alley, a gym, an outdoor pool with a children's area and a sauna with a solarium. In addition, rental services and active entertainment are provided on the beach: jet skis, sport boats, jet skis, parachute flying, banana riding and other water rides. Three meals a day, according to the "buffet" system, several bars and cafes additionally work in the territory. Evening entertainment can be spent at the disco and in the cinema hall, where the animation team prepares fascinating performances.



Boarding house "Energetik" at the same time is ready to accept more than four hundred people, sports grounds and a children's play complex with various attractions and inflatable slides are equipped on its territory. In the pension / complex there is a hairdresser, kiosks with beach accessories, souvenirs and periodicals, a summer cafe. The indisputable advantage of "Energy" is its own beach with a two-tier solarium. The shallow sea in this area makes it possible to quickly warm up the water and, along with a pebble beach, it is very convenient for families with children. Well-equipped tanning salons provide the opportunity to comfortably take sunbathing and, if necessary, hide in their shade.



Divnomorskoe, pensions with own beach: reviews of vacationers

Almost everyone is unanimous in the opinion that this is a paradise for relaxation, where there is a unique nature. The Black Sea and freshwater river create a mild subtropical climate all year round. The sanatoriums differ in comfort: some buildings have not seen repair and reconstruction for decades. Even in the Fakel sanatorium, guests who are not Gazprom employees will be accommodated in separate buildings (10, 10a), where the service leaves much to be desired. The beaches of the boarding houses are equipped with bunk tanning beds with sun loungers and awnings, they have medical posts and rescue stations. Entrance to these recreation areas is provided only on the sanatorium cards of boarding houses. Otherwise, the beaches of the boarding houses can be called conditionally, because the entrance to them is free in accordance with the legislation of the Russian Federation.
