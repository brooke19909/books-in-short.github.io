Bright and beautiful decoration: do-it-yourself fabric flower

The desire to make your life beautiful is inherent in many creative people. You can decorate not only your home or suburban area, but also clothes. So, a flower made of fabric with your own hands is able to simply transform an ordinary top or a straw hat. There are many options for how to make it. Let's look at some of the most successful.

Swirling roses



Making such curls is very simple. All you need is a brightly colored fabric and adhesive sintepon or non-woven fabric. The fabric must be duplicated with a synthetic winterizer and cut into stripes, which need to be rolled up and fixed with threads. Do not try to tighten the rolls with the fabric, leave gaps, do not make the edges even, so the roses will turn out more natural and spectacular. It is better to seal the back part with felt circles, you can sew a pin or any other fastening. A DIY flower is ready.

Lush peonies



These flowers will look better on hair pins. You can make a flower of fabric with your own hands, and then glue it to a hair clip or sew on an elastic band. To make such a fluffy peony, you will need 1.5 meters of fabric with a width of 14 cm. It is better if it is a shiny satin, satin or taffeta. Just fold the fabric in half with the front side outward to make a 1.5m by 7cm rectangle. Then take a strong thread in two folds and sew a seam for basting along all three edges. Once done, tighten the thread and get a flower. Now just line the fabric flower as you need. With your own hands you can make even leaves or bows from taffeta for decoration.

Kanzashi



Style "Kanzashi" should be familiar to needlewomen who tried to make flowers from fabric with their own hands. The schemes of such crafts are very diverse. Petals can be round, sharp, double and so on. Here, for example, is a flower made of satin ribbons and wrapping fabric. Each petal is obtained from a square of fabric, the corners of which are glued in pairs by a hot gun. To master this art is very simple, such a flower can decorate both a bag and a children‚Äôs rim.

From felt



The edges of the felt do not fly around, so making a flower from a fabric with your own hands from it is very simple. You just need to cut the right number of petals, fold them nicely and glue them with a hot gun, and insert a brooch or a beautiful button in the center. You will get a spectacular decoration for any occasion.

Conclusion

By showing imagination, you can make an infinite number of beautiful jewelry. Such flowers can be made of leather and suede, you get a more luxurious evening option. Combining flowers with beads, you can make a charming necklace that elegantly complements a cocktail dress. Organza and silk look very successful in such works. And with brightly colored American cotton, you get youthful and stylish little things. Fabric flowers are a very popular decoration. Your work in this area will be great gifts for family and friends. By the way, similar options in stores are not cheap.
