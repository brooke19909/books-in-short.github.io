"Park Inn Nevsky"





This hotel can be safely attributed to a level of comfort equal to four stars. It is worth saying that this institution for accommodating tourists is included in the international association of hotels called Park Inn by Radisson. The hotel is located near the Moscow railway station of St. Petersburg. The location of the hotel is known to everyone - this is the famous Nevsky Prospect throughout St. Petersburg.

Famous tourist sites such as the Mariinsky Theater and the Peter and Paul Fortress are built very close to the hotel. Moskovsky Station is only 50 meters from the hotel, which greatly simplifies the process of movement. This hotel is able to receive comfortably both business people and ordinary curious tourists.

Accommodation





The room reserve consists of 269 comfortable rooms, the equipment of which corresponds to the economy class, luxury and junior suite. In addition, there are ordinary standard rooms, each of which is equipped with everything necessary. The rooms have the necessary appliances for living, and the internal atmosphere contributes to relaxation. Each piece of furniture has a soft upholstery and a calm color, which provides coziness and comfort.

Food and service

In terms of food, hotel guests can enjoy a wide variety of delicacies at the Paulaner German restaurant. The hotel provides rental service for banquets, weddings and corporate evenings. The hotel staff is so professional that every guest receives the necessary assistance in a matter of minutes.

Perhaps one of the main "tricks" of the institution is considered to be live music performed by a well-known collective. Breakfast at the hotel is possible from 7 to 10 in the morning on weekdays, as well as from 7 to 11 on weekends. Among other things, the hotel has its own fitness center, equipped with the latest gym equipment and sports equipment.

For nonresident guests in the range of hotel services there is a shuttle capable of delivering passengers on any declared route. In addition, the hotel is ready to provide guests with a full range of information services. The language barrier is not a significant problem due to the fact that each employee is fluent in common foreign languages.

For those guests who come to the hotel by personal transport, there is a wide parking for cars under video surveillance, the placement of cars on which is paid separately. Many tourists believe that the Park Inn Nevsky is the best hotel in St. Petersburg near the Moscow railway station.

Ligotel





Ligotel is a comfortable hotel located on the territory of the famous Ligovsky Prospect in the city of St. Petersburg. The hotel complex experienced a complete renovation in 2012. The number of rooms includes family-type rooms, as well as categories of comfort and standard. Each room is available for two or more people at a time. The overall design of the apartment is done in retro style. The design details of the rooms are mainly used in light colors. The bathrooms in all rooms have underfloor heating and showers with music and lighting additions.

Room features





All bedrooms have comfortable and spacious beds with orthopedic mattresses and fresh linens. Upon prior request, it is possible to place an additional foldaway bed in the room. All furniture is brand new, made to order by the hotel according to previously developed projects. All residential rooms have a LCD panel for watching TV, air conditioning, shower, hairdryer, hygiene items, a desk with all the necessary equipment.

Ligotel is an excellent hotel near the Moscow railway station in St. Petersburg for a good rest. This complex has three relaxation areas, in which there are upholstered furniture, designer sofas, a large plasma TV. In the lounge area there are several bars with a large assortment of cocktails and light snacks.

In addition to light snacks, the cafe has a system for providing guests with hot meals for every taste - from meat, fish and poultry.

Great location

Within walking distance from the hotel there are a variety of infrastructure facilities, including cafes, cinemas and large shopping centers.
