AV receiver: description, purpose

The 21st century can rightfully be considered the digital age. We are surrounded by digital technology, digital communications, digital image, digital sound, etc. In many homes you can find home theaters, and this is not at all surprising, because watching your favorite movie or sports program on such equipment brings the viewer a lot of pleasure and experience. One of the main elements of a home theater is an AV receiver. This device plays a key connecting role between television and acoustic equipment. The main purpose of equipment such as an AV receiver is the organization of sound and image transmission, its uniform distribution in the audio system.
