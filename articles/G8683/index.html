Phone MAXVI: reviews, specifications, photos

Maxvi is known to domestic buyers. It produces inexpensive phones that cost up to 3-4 thousand rubles. There are five different rulers. Differences between devices only in design, characteristics and prices are approximately the same. The company does not plan to rest on its laurels, positioning itself young and developing.

P10

The reviews about the Maxvi P10 phone are pleasant. The phone has a pleasant appearance, performs its main functions well. The main advantage of the device, buyers consider powerful sound. The cost of the device is 1800 rubles.

The phone comes in a branded box. It shows the device and some specifications. Standard equipment: charging, battery, warranty card and manual. In reviews of Maxvi phones they write that such a configuration is more than enough.

The phone looks modern. It weighs 185 g. The body material is pleasant to the touch. Due to the fact that it is rounded, it is convenient to hold the phone in your hands. The device is made of plastic and metal. The model is on sale in gray and black shades. Most of the front is occupied by the screen. Below it is a standard keyboard. The keys have a good stroke and are palpated by the fingers. Buyers separately highlight this point in their reviews. On the bottom of the device there are openings for charging and headphones. On the top is a flashlight. The sides are empty. On the back there is a speaker and a camera. According to customers, when you click on the cover, it does not bend or crack.
