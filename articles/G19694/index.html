Where to celebrate New Year in Minsk?

A huge number of Russian tourists plan to spend the upcoming New Year in Minsk. However, not everyone knows what the program of festive events will be, where the Christmas trees will be installed and where exactly it will be possible to go to have fun from the heart on the night of December 31 to January 1.

It must be emphasized that the main highlight of the New Year celebration in the Belarusian capital is the creation of an atmosphere of laughter, joy and fun. Everyone can plunge into it with his head. New Year's Eve in Minsk is an interesting holiday option for those who like loud fireworks and noisy companies. This is the same abroad, but only without language barriers and visas. Wide avenues, always fresh produce, stork nests on telegraph poles - all this splendor is an excellent prerequisite for creating a festive mood. In general, in the Belarusian capital you will feel like a long-awaited guest. What should you remember if you intend to celebrate the New Year in Minsk? Firstly, do not spend the whole night from December 31 to January 1 in a hotel room eating delicious dishes and watching television programs. This, of course, is also an option, but it is by no means the most-most. It is better to go outside and see the grandiose scale of the holiday with your own eyes.





Only in this way the New Year in Minsk will be memorable for you.

Fair "Kalyadny Kirmash"

If you arrived in the Belarusian capital a few days before January 31, you can feel the festive atmosphere by visiting the New Year's fair, which will be held on Independence Avenue, 37a (not far from the Sports Palace). Here you can enjoy live music and choose gifts for your near and dear ones: modern gadgets, various accessories for clothes, jewelry, toys, stationery and much more.

Mass festivities start on the site located near the Sports Palace. They will begin one hour before the chimes and will end at 4 a.m. on January 1. And at half past one in the morning will be a fireworks display.

Another area for skating enthusiasts is located near the Sports Palace. Some people prefer to rest in Minsk on New Year's eve here. The skating rink is located on Pobediteley Avenue, 4A. Opening hours: from 10.00. until 23.00.

Well, a huge number of people prefer to celebrate the event at the "rich" table in their own apartment, which in itself is the "right decision", since the New Year is a home holiday. It all depends on what company surrounds you, and in what mood you are.

Horseback riding

A non-trivial way to celebrate the New Year is to ride a horse.





This entertainment is for those who are tired of television holiday programs, bars and discos. Why not ride on New Year's Eve with your loved one or girlfriend? For many, such entertainment is the limit of romance. You will spend time in the winter forest, in an atmosphere of silence and complete harmony with nature. Ethnic drumming

How else unusual to have fun on New Year's Eve if you are in Minsk? Many people are attracted to playing ethnic drums. This is a great way to melt cold winter evenings and turn them into "hot" parties. Darbuka, djemba, bongo - this is an ideal option to cheer up, make new acquaintances and move to incendiary rhythms. You can master the art of playing exotic instruments at TekaDUM School, which is located on 11 Kozlova Lane. For the New Year, a special holiday program is usually offered here.
