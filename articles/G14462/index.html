"Cote d'Azur" (recreation center), Votkinsk - a great place for solitude with nature

On the coast of the Votka River, which belongs to the basin of the majestic Kama, there is a small but cozy city of Votkinsk. Here the Urals, coniferous forests, birch groves, frosty winters and clean air already begin - this is how this area is characterized. Outside the city there are many camp sites, a trip to them will be a great vacation from the hustle and bustle. It is here that the atmosphere is conducive to relaxation and complete relaxation. One of the best places is the Cote d'Azur - a recreation center. Votkinsk is not far from it, this allows you to make a weekend trip.





Living conditions and services offered

In the summer, a comfortable camp site with its cozy buildings, sandy beaches and numerous entertainments opens its doors. Always glad to guests of the "Cote d'Azur" (recreation center). Votkinsk is famous for its natural landscapes and beautiful river banks. This is a great place to relax in the summer, because there is nothing better than spending a vacation with your family in the lap of nature. And corporate events at this camp site will please you with fun pastime, a cozy atmosphere and high service. Picturesque private promenade will be a great place for a romantic walk, to the sound of river waves and the radiance of night stars. The recreation center "Cote d'Azur" has a convenient beach area, clean yellow sand washed by the Kama waters. For lovers of traveling on the river, a comfortable pier with a boat station adjacent to it is equipped with a boat. What living conditions does the Cote d'Azur (recreation center) offer? Votkinsk invites lovers and newlyweds, couples with children and youth companies here. Individual wooden and panel houses are comfortably located surrounded by perennial pines. Near each house there is a brazier, making barbecue and sitting at a delicious dinner in nature is a great pleasure.

In addition, the territory includes a dining room, the cooks of which will always delight you with delicious dishes. A bar with a modern design will allow you to enjoy drinks in the evening and cool off on a hot summer day. There is a real Russian bathhouse at the base, a visit to this institution will allow you to cleanse the body and recharge your batteries. For lovers of outdoor activities you should definitely try water skiing, and a boat trip with a refreshing breeze will leave a lot of positive emotions. We have not forgotten about the little tourists here either, in a convenient place is a playground. And for adults, a great game and a chance to tighten your figure will be a game of volleyball. The most cheerful guests of the Cote d'Azur are waiting for an evening disco.





Guest House

One of the highlights is the comfortable guest house, which offers the Cote d'Azur (recreation center). Votkinsk goes along modern roads for the development of the tourism business, this is indicated by the excellent conditions created for vacationers. A summer gazebo with barbecue facilities is adjacent to the guest house, and inside visitors will find a comfortable kitchen, three comfortable bedrooms, a chic sauna with a pool and a relaxation room. Corporate events, birthdays and other significant dates are possible in the well-equipped banquet room, which is located in the guest house. A cozy evening by the fireplace with a cup of coffee will be remembered for a long time. Pleasant memories leave this recreation center. Votkinsk is always preparing to receive guests in the summer.
