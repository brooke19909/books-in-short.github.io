The procedure for constructing the main dress pattern

In the wardrobe of every woman there is a dress of a free cut. Pattern of its main part involves the following sequence of actions:

  1. At the very beginning, you need to draw a rectangle ABVG. The size of the sides of AB and HB is equal to the value of the semicircle of the chest + 4 cm to ensure a loose fit. This will be the width of the future product. The sides AH and BV coincide with the length of the dress by measurement (total length).
  2. The depth of the armhole is marked as follows: 1/3 of the semicircle of the chest is postponed, then 4 cm is added and point D. is marked.
  3. From point D, a horizontal line is drawn in both directions until the moment when there is an intersection with straight lines AG and BV. As a result, a chest line was obtained, which should be marked with the point D1.
  4. Point A - the beginning of the vertical line down - is the length of the back to the waist. It will be called point E. The horizontal line from this mark to the intersection with the side of the BV - point E1 is the waist line.
  5. The hip line should be located 18 cm lower than the waist line and marked with the letter L. The values ‚Äã‚Äãof the waist and hips are approximate, since in the future they will need to be lowered by about 2 cm in front.

How to quickly sew a dress of a free cut

Self-creation of a pattern is a very difficult task not only for a beginner seamstress, but also for an experienced dressmaker. Even the absolutely accurate transfer of all taken measurements to paper does not guarantee that the product will fit nicely in shape.

The reason for these inconveniences is very simple - there are no people with ideal forms in the world. It is the non-standard character of most of the figures caused by the lack of an ideal drawing-pattern.

You can greatly simplify your life and sew a free-cut dress with your own t-shirt. This eliminates the need to make grueling measurements and learn obscure terms needed to build a drawing. It will take a maximum of one evening to make such a dress.

So let's get started. To make the dress look the most spectacular and attractive, you need to choose a bright neon fabric. A blue or mint hue emphasizes a tanned body.

When calculating the amount of material, it should be remembered that sewing will be carried out ‚Äúin one length‚Äù. The presence of folds or a turn-down collar always requires additional centimeters. If the volume of the hips is 130 cm or more, then the material will require ‚Äútwo lengths‚Äù. In addition, when calculating the amount of fabric, you need to leave a margin on the seams (allowance) and on the hem of the bottom line of the product.

"Quick" pattern of a sheath dress

Since such a dress is considered the simplest, there is no urgent need for a pattern when sewing it. A template for creating such a product can be your own t-shirt.

  1. First you need to fold the knitted fabric in half with the front part inward.
  2. To create a straight dress, you should choose a t-shirt that fits as well as possible on the figure. The pre-ironed product must also be folded along the line of symmetry. The t-shirt must be applied so that the fold lines of both materials coincide.
  3. Now you need to attach the T-shirt to the fabric using pins and circle its entire contour with chalk, leaving allowances for the seams (1.5 cm)
  4. Then you need to pay attention to the back pattern and choose the optimal width and length of the product. For example, you can add 50 cm in the axillary zone and 100 cm along the bottom line. ¬Ω back + 12.5 cm + 1.5 cm = 14 cm under the armpits. 25 cm + 1.5 cm = 26.5 cm along the hem line.
  5. The hem of the product should be noted with a smooth line.
  6. Now proceed to the front. The sequence of the process is similar to the manufacture of a part of the back, although a deeper neckline should be noted here.
  7. The sleeve of the dress can be made the same as for a T-shirt, only the length value should be reduced - ¬æ, for example.
  8. Based on what type of neck was chosen, one should begin to build a pattern of its turning. It consists of two parts: one is the front side, the other is the back.
  9. It remains only to cut out all the details, while leaving an allowance for seams.
