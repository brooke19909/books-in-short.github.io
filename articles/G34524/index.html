"Blackberry" is ... Blackberry phones: review, price, photos and reviews

Even if you are not interested in electronics, you must have heard the name "Blackberry". This is one of the first manufacturers of smartphones, which gained popularity long before the first iPhones and Galaxy appeared. And despite the fierce competition in the market, he still has his fans.

Brand Details

"Blackberry" is a blackberry translated from English. What kind of ‚Äúberry‚Äù is this in the electronics market?

Many compare the success of Blackberry with the popularity of yet another appetizing brand - Apple. The devices of both manufacturers at one time blew up the market and found an army of loyal fans who refuse to buy anything else.

The history of the famous Canadian company begins in 1984. Then she was called Research In Motion and changed it to the famous name of her offspring BlackBerry in 2013.

The unusual name of the device is explained by the similarity of the small buttons on the keyboard with the berries of a blackberry.

The first Blackberry was the most common pager. In 2002, a model appeared with the ability to make calls, but it was mandatory to use a headset, because this phone did not have a microphone and speakers. Also a key change was the ability to work with email.





Since 2003, the company begins to produce models with a color display, a browser and the ability to work with programs of the Microsoft Office suite. For a long time, the manufacturer focused on gadgets for businessmen and only in 2006 joined the race for customers who use the phone for personal purposes and for entertainment.

Key model features

Blackberry is a hybrid of a personal digital assistant and a powerful portable media player. Unlike most other smartphones, the models of this manufacturer do not use someone else‚Äôs, designed for other devices, but their own operating system.

The main reason why many people use Blackberry is the ability to stay in touch almost anywhere and through various means of communication. The device can be used directly as a phone, or to access e-mail, or for instant messaging using its own free BBM service.

Blackberry is also one of the first smartphones that gave users the opportunity to use the mobile Internet and do it with convenience and ease. It won the hearts of many buyers and brought glory to the manufacturer.

A distinctive feature of the phones is the presence of a physical QWERTY keyboard. Complete with a large screen, it provides comfortable use of the phone for any purpose.





Blackberry offers a large number of other features that make it very useful. There are maps and GPS navigation that guarantee that you will not get lost. Of course, there is a camera and a multimedia player, and other attributes necessary for a modern smartphone.

Issue price and localization

To withstand fierce competition with other electronics giants in the market, new modern Blackberry models are constantly appearing. The price of the device can be quite modest or quite impressive - from about 9 thousand rubles for the 9220 Curve White phone to 90 thousand for the powerful P'9981 Porsche Design with a stainless steel case and genuine leather elements. Phones of the brand can be both concisely simple and showing the status of the owner. Therefore, it can be argued that from a teenager to a businessman, everyone will find a suitable Blackberry.

Russia is a large market, therefore, for the convenience of domestic users, it is possible to apply Russian characters to the telephone keypad using laser engraving. Of course, the automatic transliteration function has recently become available, but it will be suitable only for those who already know the location of the letters.

Customer opinion

Thousands of users around the world are happy to use their Blackberry (telephone). Reviews of brand fans are mostly positive. Most often, the main advantage is the physical keyboard, which is no longer often seen on a smartphone. Individualists are also flattered that they have a rather rare phone. Also among the advantages are good batteries and a long battery life, a good sound, an intuitive operating system that rarely hangs, and convenient work with gestures. The main drawback is the work with applications. This is a significant minus of its operating system. Users who switched from other smartphones to Blackberry do not find their favorite applications, there is no support for Google Play, and Android applications do not always work correctly.





The opinion of experts

Even the most avid fans of the brand cannot fail to notice that their favorite has a hard time. Apple, Samsung, LG, and other new smartphone makers have dealt a massive blow to Blackberry's popularity. Well-known authors and editors of magazines and websites about electronics, as well as business experts often say that the company‚Äôs time is counted. Already now its turnovers mostly depend on repeated sales to fans of the brand, while new buyers prefer other manufacturers.

Of course, with so many existing Blackberry owners, it will not disappear in one day. But if you are just planning to join this brand, you should weigh the pros and cons.

Company future

But not everyone is so pessimistic. Even the US president uses Blackberry, not iPhone, because he is not allowed by the security service. After all, the "blackberry" has a very powerful system of protection against hacking.

Therefore, companies predict the future as a manufacturer of expensive smartphones for politicians and businessmen, for whom the preservation of confidential information is important .
