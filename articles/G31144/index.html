Morocco Resorts in North Africa

Hot and mysterious Africa has always attracted tourists with its color, exotic, special way of life and, of course, amazingly beautiful nature. Arab countries, even nowadays, are reluctant to let residents of other states with a different faith, traditions and culture. Not so long ago, opened the door to tourists and Morocco. Resorts take guests all year round, because the temperature here even in winter does not drop below +15 ¬∞ . You can combine several types of recreation in the country: sunbathing on the beach and swimming in the Mediterranean Sea or the Atlantic Ocean, strolling along eucalyptus and pine plantations, climbing mountains, riding a desert safari. Such diversity is hardly possible to find on another continent.



Morocco offers a huge number of leisure options. Resorts have a well-thought-out infrastructure, the best of them are located on the Atlantic coast. One of the most attractive cities, as well as the business center of the country, is Casablanca. This is a large port with its own history, there is something to see, tourists are provided with all the conditions for a comfortable and interesting vacation.

300 sunny days a year and a comfortable air temperature, which is between +25 ... + 30 ¬∞ , made Agadir one of the most popular tourist centers in Morocco. Resorts are famous for beautiful beaches, but in this town they are especially beautiful. Vacationers have the opportunity to enjoy the picturesque view of the mountains, swim in the Atlantic Ocean, breathe fresh air in eucalyptus or pine forests.



Marrakech is the real heart of Morocco. Resorts of the country attract with their traditions, culture, preserved over many centuries. In Marrakesh, you can see the authentic life of Moroccans, get acquainted with national crafts, dresses, customs, and visit magnificent palaces, mosques, and a mausoleum. One of the most mysterious resort towns is considered Fez. Only here you can truly penetrate religion, learn its subtleties, the perception of the world by Arabs.

It should be noted that although Morocco is considered a warm African country, the locals also saw snow. If you really want to ski down from the Atlas Mountains, you should come from December to April. Ukaimeden is the best ski resort in Morocco, located south of Marrakech, located 2600 m above sea level. To get acquainted with the history of the country, you should go to Essaouira, where once there was the largest slave market in North Africa . The city has a lot of interesting architectural structures and historical monuments, and the Sidi Mohammed bin Abdullah Museum has collected numerous treasures of the country.



Another very beautiful and rich historical past resort is Rabat. The city is surrounded by greenery of parks and gardens, lovers of water sports come here. Mohammed V University is located in Rabat. Handicraft lovers can buy carpets and fabrics from local needlewomen here. The resorts of Morocco on the Atlantic Ocean attract with picturesque landscapes, interesting entertainments and local color. No one will be bored here.
