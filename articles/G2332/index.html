How to find out your FMS UIN?

Often, individuals face a problem when it is necessary to indicate the PIN number when filling out certain payment documents. This raises the question: "How do I find out my PIN? Where can I get information about this set of numbers?"

A unique accrual identifier (in other words, UIN) is required to determine any cash receipt.



What is a UIN?

A unique accrual identifier is a set of numbers that should be indicated on the receipt for any bank payment transaction. The law on the use of this number began on February 4, 2014, in accordance with order No. 107n. How to find out your UIN will be written below.

How to find out your UIN?

Due to the fact that this number must now be indicated when making any payment banking transactions, legal entities and individuals need to know the number of a unique accrual identifier. In addition, awareness of this issue is useful for independent search for information on any individual debts (fines, taxes, penalties, etc.). For example, if the computer program asks for your number in Internet banks to create a payment document online.



When paying taxes, fines, penalties or making other payments through a bank, you will be faced with the need to fill out forms. When processing a payment document before 03/31/2014, the penal control unit should be entered first in a special field called "Payment Details". After March 31, there was a change in payment documents, in them the PIN is entered in a special field marked "CODE".

Where can I get the UIN code?

You should know that the assignment of a number is made only by the tax service of the Russian Federation. In order to find out your personal number of a unique accrual identifier, you should contact the bank with a question about how to find out your PIN. When contacting you will receive the necessary advice. Using the service "State services" a bank employee will inform you on this topic.

In addition, tax officials of the Russian Federation at their own stands should post the necessary information regarding the UIN number from the end of 2013.

To pay taxes to the population should use the notification. Typically, the Federal Tax Service sends a letter to the payer address, which indicates information on debts. A special payment document (notice) is attached to the letter, drawn up in accordance with all the rules. Using this form, you must pay the debt through any banking system.

If an individual prefers to personally make a money transfer without a document from the tax service, a payment should be generated independently. This can be done using the electronic service on the website of the tax service of the Russian Federation. In this situation, the index will be assigned automatically. When filling out a notice in the form No. PD-4sb (tax), the UIN is not indicated. In the column "CODE" should be written in block letters UIN 0. This will mean that you do not know the number of a unique identifier of charges. The remaining fields must be carefully filled out, indicating exactly your last name, first name, middle name and address of registration.

UIN of legal entities

A unique accrual identifier is also assigned to organizations. It is necessary for tax reporting of their activities. How to find out a UIN organization?



2014 was a transitional year in the preparation of payment notices. In addition to individuals, the heads of organizations must know the penitentiary system. To find out the unique identifier of the accrual of a legal entity, the accountant or director should contact the city tax office directly. In addition to the Federal Tax Service, this information can be obtained by contacting the Pension Fund of the Russian Federation, as well as the territorial branch of the FSS of the Russian Federation.
