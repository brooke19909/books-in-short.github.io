Reviews

We were able to easily find recommendations about the device. It should be noted right away that most reviews are positive. Buyers characterize the device as an affordable and reasonably comfortable tablet that is ideally suited for basic tasks.

Among the negative ones, one could notice several - in particular, describing that several users did not charge the Samsung Galaxy Tab 2 tablet. Owners of problematic devices tried to understand why this was happening and what could be the reason. As the answers left by the experts show, the problem was a technical breakdown. If someone does not charge the Samsung Galaxy Tab 2 tablet , you can fix this only by replacing the adapter that connects the device to the network, or by installing a new power socket.

A similar problem can be when the tablet does not turn on (does not respond to the power button). There may be several reasons - either the device is completely discharged (connecting to the network will let you know if this is the case), or the display unlock button is out of order (again, connect the device to power). There may also be a more serious malfunction at the hardware level, for the solution of which you need to contact the service center.

There were other estimates - someone mentioned the fact that the tablet is very hot during operation. There were also opinions about the insufficiently bright (or large) screen. Of course, if the Samsung Galaxy Tab 2 does not charge, this is a more serious drawback than the fact of heating during operation. However, similar problems can be found on other models.
