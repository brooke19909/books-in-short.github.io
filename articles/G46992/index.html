Sberbank Gold Credit Card: grace period conditions

This question is of interest to customers in the first place. Among the many products, Sberbank's gold credit card is special. The terms of use of this offer are considered to give the holder some privileges. All this unobtrusively creates him the status of a premium client.

On the issue of repayment, you need to know that it is best to make payments through ATMs or cash desks of Sberbank. Today there is another payment option - through the online system.

The grace period, that is, the time when interest is not accrued on the limit amount, is 50 days. However, it is worth noting that this period does not apply to the return of money received through an ATM. If it is not possible to return all the amount used from the card, it will have to be paid in certain parts. A single payment should be at least 5% of the total amount.

It is best to familiarize yourself with the terms of debt repayment in advance if you have received a gold credit card from Sberbank of Russia. Interest on the card can be accrued not only by agreement, but also for violation of payment terms. They will make up 36% per annum.
