How to crochet a beret - an instruction for lovers of original things

Every woman wants to always be individual and vibrant. The first helpers in this are her stylish accessories, original clothing items. The area of ‚Äã‚Äãthe face and head of a person always becomes the object of initial attention. Therefore, a special approach is usually given to the selection of hats.



There are many instructions in which knitting a fashion beret with knitting needles is presented, the product comes out no less beautiful, but if you knit a crochet, it turns out more elegant, the work looks sophisticated and exclusive.

For a very long time, beret is a fashion accessory. Every season you can observe how the trends in the priorities of the color scheme of hats, their texture of the fabric change, but the shape of this unique product remains unchanged - soft, flexible, elastic. Of course, it still depends on how to tie the crochet - tight or loose viscous.

Consider crocheting a beret .

It will look very original, connected by embossed viscous columns without a crochet (using the technique of picking up under the lower half-loop).

What you need to purchase: 100 g of wool of a fashionable or your favorite shade. The hook must be selected in accordance with the thickness of the thread. Before you crochet a crochet, try to make a regular chain consisting of air loops. If this simple task is difficult to complete (it is inconvenient to grab the thread or the hook constantly slips somewhere), then you need to try to take a hook of a different size.

So let's get started:

We start by knitting a pigtail with air loops 19 cm long.

Row 1: SC

Tip: so that the first row is not curved compared to the base chain, try periodically skipping one loop throughout the row. Suppose you can evenly leave every eighth loop unbound.

Row 2: we knit with a crochet; at the end of the row you need to make three half columns.

Row 3: we knit 3 half-columns, all other loops are a single crochet, introducing a hook behind the back arch. Knitting is repeated from the 1st row.



So they knit a product about 70 cm high (you can navigate depending on your own taste and desires). It is important to ensure that the width of the canvas does not increase or decrease, but is uniform from start to finish.

Tip: in order to correct the situation, if you see that an increase in the series has nevertheless begun, then you should make one from two columns from the edge. If the situation is reversed, then create two columns from one loop. And so try to maximize the width.

When the fabric is knitted, it must be folded with short edges together. So connect them. You can do this with the same hook and thread. After joining, the thread does not need to be cut, but leave 15-20 cm to tighten the upper edge of the beret with it. Thus, the bottom of the headgear is obtained. The thread that was pulled tightly fixed from the inside. Now you need to put in order the opposite open edge of the beret. We do this by tying it with crochets. So, a few rows will be enough to complete the product. About 5-7 rows should turn out. But it depends on your style wishes.

Important: knitting this part, the beret needs to be gradually narrowed. To do this, in each row with a certain interval, reduce the number of loops. Do this until the circumference matches the girth of the head. It is necessary to try on in order to understand how comfortable the hat is sitting.



Finish the work by tearing the thread and securing from the wrong side, preferably at the back of the product.

We try on and are proud that this is the result of your painstaking work.

This is not the end to knitting a fashionable beret - You can show your imagination and decorate it with beads, flowers, any other accessories, do whatever your creative vision tells you.

Good luck, dear fashionistas, and inspiration !!!

Here is such a simple answer to the difficult question of how to knit a crochet with your own hands.
