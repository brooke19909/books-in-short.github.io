We build patterns for pregnant women

Pregnancy is a wonderful time for every woman. She is glad that she will have a baby soon. Meanwhile, this time is quite expensive. After all, the figure of a woman undergoes serious changes, and therefore she must necessarily update the wardrobe.



To this end, you can visit specialized stores, which presented a fairly large assortment of clothes for expectant mothers. However, having familiarized themselves with the cost of things, many refuse to purchase, since after the birth of the baby a significant enough amount will be required. Therefore, they prefer to purchase clothes of large sizes, which will fit a clearly visible tummy, or sew it on their own. In the latter case, a woman will definitely need patterns for pregnant women, the principle of construction of which has a number of significant features.

Having decided to do sewing on your own, it is imperative to provide for increases in the waist area, since for a woman's noticeably rounded belly, an additional volume is required. This must be taken into account when sewing all types of clothing.

In many sewing magazines today you can find special patterns for pregnant women. Especially if you try to look for a special edition. In this case, it will be possible to find patterns of dresses for pregnant women or, for example, trousers. However, if such a publication cannot be found in any way, you should not be upset. As many women have noted, you can use ordinary

Oyki, which is enough just to modify a little.

If you decide to sew a sundress for yourself, then look for a pattern of a straight model with a chest tuck. In order to fit into it together with the tummy without any problems, we lay aside from the middle of the bottom of the front 50 cm. We get the most extreme point of the pattern for pregnant women. On the back we put aside only 30 cm. This will allow us to transform a straight sundress into a flared one. To build a sideline, you need to connect the lowest point of the tuck with the extreme. Pattern is ready. It remains only to transfer it to the fabric and you can start sewing.



It is worth noting that the material for the sundress can be of very different qualities. If you plan to go to the office in it, then buy jeans. If you sew clothes for walking on a summer day, then linen is quite suitable.

Using the above description, you can also build a dress pattern. The "interesting position" of a woman does not affect the principle of sewing sleeves. Therefore, having sewed a sundress, it will be possible to remake it at any time in a dress.

Patterns for pregnant women, if properly constructed, allow you to sew very comfortable trousers. To do this, you only need to additionally build a set-in part on the tummy. The rest of the pattern remains the same. You can even use the old one, provided that the volume of the hips has not changed.

Similarly, patterns of clothes for pregnant women of any other models are built. For example, when going on vacation, you can always make a comfortable bathing suit for yourself , which will make you feel comfortable even at the latest dates.
