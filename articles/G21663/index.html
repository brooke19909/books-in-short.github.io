How to transfer money to a Sberbank card from another card

Plastic cards confidently crowd out cash, because with their help it is convenient to make purchases, pay for a wide variety of services and share money with loved ones. Do you know how to transfer money to a Sberbank card, are you sure that you are performing this operation in the most convenient way? If not, we offer you detailed instructions.

The main ways to transfer money to a card





In order to send a transfer, you will need to enter the card number to which funds should be credited. It is written on the plastic rectangle itself and can consist of 19, 18 or 16 digits. If you are going to transfer money from a Sberbank card, and you have a mobile bank, you can manage without a card number. Send SMS to a short service number with the word "transfer", the recipient's phone number and the transfer amount. After that, your request will be answered with a request to confirm the transfer and indicating the name of the recipient. All that remains for you is to confirm the operation. The commission is minimal, messages are free. There are many more ways of transferring money to a Sberbank card if the beneficiary's details are available to you. How to transfer money to a Sberbank card yourself?





If you use the Sberbank-Online service, you can perform any operations through the Network without leaving your home. You can connect a virtual office using an ATM, or in a bank office. To enter, use a permanent username and password, as well as an additional combination of numbers. You can get a temporary one-time password at an ATM or via SMS. To transfer, you must enter the card number of the recipient, select a card for debiting funds, indicate the amount and confirm the operation. If you regularly transfer money to the same card, you can create a transfer template and complete the operation faster. You can also transfer money to a Sberbank card using an ATM. To do this, select the item "Money transfers" in the device menu and enter all the necessary data.

Transfer to Sberbank card from cards of other banks and in customer service offices





You can transfer money to a Sberbank card without having an account and a card in this bank. If you want to send funds from a card of another bank, you can try to perform this operation through the terminal or the online office of your bank, similar to transfers within Sberbank. If you do not have a Sberbank card, you can transfer through the office of this financial institution. To do this, you need to contact a specialist engaged in translations, or use a terminal that accepts cash. If you do not know how to transfer money to a Sberbank card through such a device, specify the procedure for performing the procedure with a bank employee.
