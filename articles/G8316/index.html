Candolim Beach (India, Goa): description and reviews

Among travelers who love Goa, there is often a debate about which part of the resort is better to relax: northern or southern. It is possible to devote a lot of time to clarifying this issue, but since the differences between them are too striking, the conclusion is obvious that each of the debaters defends their preferences and tastes.

Candolim Beach, located in the village of the same name in the northern region of Goa, attracts those who like a relaxing holiday by the sea, away from noisy discos and nightclubs.

Beaches of North Goa

In this part of the smallest state of India, both the coastline and the infrastructure along it differ from the southern part. If the last of them is more lively, crowded with tourists and entertainment complexes, then the northern region is characterized by restraint, calmness and lack of crowding.

There is a wide coastline, which is divided into three parts:

  • Calangute, the longest part of the coast in this area of ‚Äã‚ÄãGoa.
  • Candolim, which is only a little less.
  • Sinquerim is the farthest part of the coast.




As North Goa lovers who have visited all three beaches, Candolim is considered the cleanest, most beautiful and most expensive. Mostly wealthy Germans and British prefer to relax here (sometimes Indians are found), since the quality of hotels, services and entertainment here corresponds to the most demanding taste. On average, prices on Candolim Beach are 30% higher than in other Goa resorts, but a quality vacation is worth it.

Advantages of Candolim Beach

This part of the coast of North Goa is characterized by unusually white and clean sand. A significant role in this is played by the absence of stray dogs and cows, who enjoy spending time with tourists on the rest of the resort's beaches.

If we summarize the reviews of travelers who have been here, we can distinguish the following advantages of Candolim beach:

  • Here, even at the height of the season, there are not many people and you can always find a free sunbed under an umbrella.
  • The availability of hotels directly on the coastline, which in the rest of Goa is very rare.
  • Numerous necks serving delicious seafood and freshly squeezed juices allow you not to leave the beach all day and not starve to death.
  • The lack of annoying locals who in other resorts of Goa either pester to buy something from them, or come to photograph tourists.
  • Immediately after a strip of sand, gardens begin to fill with flowers of incredible beauty and exotic trees.




We should also mention the level of service, which is consistently high both in two-star hotels, local bars and shops, and in five-star apartments.

It is important to know: Candolim Beach (Goa) is equally suitable for couples with children, and lovers of outdoor activities. Water sports are especially well developed here.

Entertainment and shopping

The resort state of Goa is so compact that you can get to the main attractions from any hotel quickly and comfortably. For those who live in its northern part, a visit to Fort Aguada will be a useful excursion into the history of India.

Candolim Beach is only 20 minutes on foot from it, so you can get acquainted with this famous attraction without resorting to the services of guides.

Attractions near the beach

Candolim Beach (reviews of tourists note this) is located in close proximity to most of the attractions of North Goa. For example, just a couple of kilometers from it is the island of Chorao, famous for its bird sanctuary.





Even those who are indifferent to birds do not refuse to ride a canoe through the mangrove swamps and watch the birds, of which there are more than 400 species.
