DIY towel shapes

Shaped towels meet guests in decent hotels, they can be presented to friends and colleagues, decorate the bathroom or originally put on a shelf in the dressing room or closet. It is interesting to simply surprise a child or guests by making some kind of animal or bird.

The article describes several popular options for folding your own towel shapes. You will definitely enjoy this easy and fascinating activity. Swans for honeymooners

For such a figure from towels, you need 4 identical plain products. Of the two, we will make the necks of the swans, and the other two will go to the drapery of the tail. With the seeming complexity of the craft, the task will take only a couple of minutes. Spend more time decorating each fold with rose petals.

To create a neck, take a large towel, folding in half, determine the center and twist the canvas in both directions towards each other. The sharp tip will be the beak. To give the necessary form, use the power of the hands.

When both swans are in contact with their beaks, depicting a heart in the middle, cover them with another towel on each side, folded with the larger half from the bottom. Then it remains to make folds, and the gift to the newlyweds is ready!
