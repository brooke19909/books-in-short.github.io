Hotel "Astra", Adler, Sochi: overview, description and reviews of tourists

Modern Russians for their holidays are increasingly choosing foreign resorts: Turkey, Tunisia, Thailand, Europe. But we forget that we can have a good rest in our country, without leaving its borders. Of course, some time ago it was foolish to even compare the quality of tourist services, the service of Russian resorts on the sea with fashionable foreign resorts. But after the Winter Olympics in Sochi, the situation has changed radically. Sochi has become a center of resort life in the country, annually collecting hundreds of thousands of tourists not only from Russia, but also from other countries. The city was literally rebuilt: many new luxury hotels and apartments appeared. The infrastructure has also undergone significant changes: new entertainments, amusement parks, green squares were awarded the highest marks by vacationers. And of course, there are all the necessary conditions for a beach holiday.





About the resort

Sochi is a great place for sea and beach holidays. The season lasts from June to September, when the optimum temperature of air and water is maintained. At this time, quite a lot of vacationers gather here: young people, couples with young children, senior citizens. In fact, it is difficult to call a vacation in Sochi, Adler in particular, budgetary, however, the improvement and development of the resort attracted a huge number of curious tourists and those vacationers who for some reason cannot leave Russia. Of course, there are enough fashionable hotels, luxury hotels and guest houses. Vacationers can find for themselves and more budget options, for example, to settle in a private mini-hotel or the private sector away from the coast. The choice primarily depends on the taste preferences of the tourist and his financial capabilities. We offer to pay attention to the hotel "Astra" (Adler). It has only three stars, but managed to fall in love with many tourists. What conditions can he offer vacationers? How much does it cost to stay in a room of the selected category?

About hotel

So, the hotel "Astra" (Adler) is a city-style hotel, remote from the coast and the beach. If you follow the established classifier, the hotel is located on the third coastline. Nevertheless, all the entertainment, shops are within walking distance from it, so vacationers will not encounter any inconvenience. It is only 8 km from the hotel to the international airport.





Location

If you are looking for a quiet and peaceful place of Adler, you will definitely like the Astra Hotel (Adler, Khutorskaya, 42). The advantage of its geographical location is noted by many tourists. Walking distance to the famous Olympic Park, racing and ski slopes, the resort's central promenade, amusement park and city beach. At the same time, vacationers will be able to relax, get enough sleep, they will not be disturbed by screams coming from the street. Near the hotel there is a public transport stop with links to the city center and train station.

The proximity to the mountains in this case does its job: the air here is clean and fresh, a lot of greenery is what you need for a great vacation.

Accommodation

Of course, every vacationer is also concerned about the rooms that the Astra Hotel (Adler) can offer. Reviews of tourists just help to make their objective and most reliable assessment. Going to the sea, it is unlikely that vacationers will spend much time in the room, nevertheless, they rely on a comfortable environment, cleanliness and the absence of interruptions in the supply of hot water.

So, the Astra Hotel (Adler) impresses and even amazes all its guests with its own design of rooms. Each of them is a work of art, a reflection of modern technology and innovation. Ergonomics of the space, a lot of light and space will give a feeling of real comfort and help make your stay in the hotel and the resort as a whole unforgettable. Rooms of various categories are what the Astra hotel offers its guests. Description, reviews only emphasize how well thought out each option.





Standard one-room rooms are designed for two people, their area is 25 square meters. You can choose a room with a balcony, which offers stunning views of the city, and if you're lucky with apartments, then on the coast. For three people are provided rooms with three single beds. Stylish family rooms designed for couples with children are spacious and functional.

If you arrive at the Astra Hotel (Sochi) for a conference or training, you want to combine relaxation with work, we recommend that you stay in a junior suite. It harmoniously combines the guest, sleeping and working areas. Room size reaches 80 square meters - there is where to turn around. Possible variation with or without a balcony. Newlyweds, couples in love often opt for a deluxe room with a huge double bed and unique authoring.





In general, check-in occurs within 10-15 minutes, even if tourists arrive late in the evening, at night or in the early morning, they will be greeted and given the keys to the room.

Room amenities

Hotel "Astra" (Adler, Russia) really can boast of the comfort that all vacationers receive. Each room, regardless of its category, has a TV with satellite channels, wireless high-speed Internet, as well as air conditioning, which is responsible for the comfort and pleasant coolness of the room in the hot and sultry summers. Each room has a private bathroom with a hairdryer, bath amenities and cosmetics. All plumbing is in good condition; no problems or interruptions in water supply have been noticed. A small refrigerator will ensure freshness and coolness of drinks, and the safety of valuable and personal items - a safe.

Territory

Hotel "Astra" (Adler) cannot boast of a huge adjoining territory. Nevertheless, for vacationers, there is a relaxation area with a pool, sunbeds and umbrellas, as well as a barbecue area. In the evenings, many vacationers gather here: splashing around in the pool, grilling kebabs and sharing their impressions of the rest.

Nutrition

The price includes breakfast, they are held on the ground floor of the building in the restaurant. The hotel bar has a lot of alcoholic and non-alcoholic products, cocktails.

Breakfasts are portioned, do not differ in variety. Most often it is porridge, toasts with butter, raw and ham, eggs, pastries, coffee and tea. All buff buffs are sure to be disappointed. You can have lunch and dinner at local restaurants and cafes - there are enough of them.





Service

Rooms are cleaned daily, towels and bed linen are subject to pollution. Tourists note that the rooms are cleaned during their absence, so nothing prevents the rest. The hotel staff not only carries out wet cleaning, but also wipes the furniture, cleans the plumbing, so the room has an atmosphere of perfect cleanliness and freshness.

Laundry and dry cleaning services are available for a fee.

Entertainment and sports

The Astra Hotel has a rental point for sports equipment, bicycles, and scooters. Theme nights are held for vacationers, and an entertainment program is organized, everyone can take part in it.

Beach and sea

Vacationers and tourists note, perhaps, the only drawback of the Astra hotel is its remoteness from the city beach and the sea. But the administration, trying to satisfy the wishes of customers, kindly provides electric cars. You will find a nice pebble beach and clear sea water. On the beach there are rescue towers, showers, locker rooms, water entertainment and sports equipment rental is open. The pebble beach does not bother holidaymakers with young children in the season; even one-year-olds can be seen here in season. The sea here is clear, the entry into the water is gentle, from the coast offers stunning views of the mountains and local beauty.

Prices

Surely tourists are also concerned about the cost of accommodation that the Astra 3 * hotel can offer (Adler). Prices primarily depend on the category of the chosen room and the number of days that tourists want to spend at the hotel. A day in a standard double room for a season will cost 4,000 rubles, which is quite inexpensive by local standards.At the end of the season, the hotel offers favorable living conditions, the cost of the room is reduced to 50, and sometimes 60%. If you are looking for the opportunity to save money, go on vacation in November - at this time the prices are low.
