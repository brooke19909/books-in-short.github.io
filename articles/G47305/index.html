Svetlana Bobrova: shooting in Moscow

Photographer Svetlana Bobrova conducts photo and video shooting in Moscow, outside the Moscow Ring Road, and also travels to other regions. She takes pictures, weddings, makes photo shoots, and in addition, architectural photography. The article will consider the types of filming that it takes, and an overview of the prices.

Types of Filming

  1. Concert. As a result of shooting, the customer will be given about 50-70 photos with some processing, such as color correction. Photos are transmitted over the Internet.
  2. Reportage shooting. This is a capture of weddings or some events. Shooting is paid for at least two hours, as a result of the work, about 100 photos are obtained, which also have some processing.
  3. Photo portrait shooting. These are photos that are taken, for example, for a portfolio. It can be family or business. Photos are fully processed.
  4. Interior photography (apartments, offices). The cost of the order depends on the area and illumination of the object. The payment already includes basic photo processing.
  5. Architectural. Shooting any objects from certain angles. You can still ask for an increase in photography, for example for printing, at an additional cost.
  6. Filming of baptisms. Includes full photo processing. This survey does not imply interference with the sacrament.

Cost of services

Svetlana Bobrova offers customers an approximate price list:

  1. Live recording costs from 5000 rubles.
  2. Sequential shooting costs from 4000 p. per hour, while you can not pay less than two hours of work.
  3. An exit photo session costs from 7000 r. in 60 minutes.
  4. Filming in the studio costs from 7,000 rubles an hour, not including studio rental, and you can not pay for at least two hours.
  5. Photosession on the street costs from 6000 r. in 60 minutes.
  6. Interior shooting costs from 500 rubles per photo, at least 30 photos are paid.
  7. Shooting apartments costs 5,000 rubles.
  8. The shooting of architectural objects costs from 7000 r. for one object, or eight hours of work are paid for 32,000 p.
  9. Wedding photos cost 5,000 rubles. In this case, the wizard only removes the registration. Hourly pay is also possible. Full photo processing is included.
  10. Filming a baptism costs from 6,000 rubles per hour.

The prices are inaccurate, it all depends on the complexity of the work. The cost of shooting is better to check directly with the master.
