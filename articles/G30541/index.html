Prague is a typical European city that has managed to maintain its originality, in which every street, every building, every wall or arch is valued, testifying to the historical past.

Prague has always been interesting for tourists with its rich excursion program. However, during the years of socialism, when the country was on the other side of the Iron Curtain, the flow of tourists decreased significantly. Over the past 15-20 years, it has become increasingly popular as a tourist destination. Moreover, tours to the Czech Republic for Catholic Christmas and New Year are especially popular, with a stop at one of the comfortable, but not very expensive hotels in the Czech capital, for example, such as Hotel Golf Depandance 4 *.

It is simply a delightful city that spreads beautifully on the banks of the Vltava River. Here you will find a unique and well-preserved ancient architecture, red tiled roofs, spiers and turrets.
