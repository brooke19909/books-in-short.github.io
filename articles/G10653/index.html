Slow Cooker Recipes

Recipes for the Redmond M90 multicooker are contained in a special cookbook, which is included in the standard package. One of the most interesting dishes that can be prepared using this device is homemade cheese. Cooking involves several stages:

  • warm a liter of fat milk in the "Milk porridge" mode;
  • add a kilogram of grated cottage cheese to the milk, let stand, drain the whey;
  • add to the remaining mass half a pack of butter, two eggs, a teaspoon of salt and soda, as well as your favorite spices or any other toppings (such as nuts, ham or raisins);
  • using the "Multipovar" mode, set the temperature to 80 degrees and the time is 1 hour so that the cheese melts and undergoes the necessary heat treatment;
  • the last step in the preparation of this dish is solidification: for this, transfer the mass to a container lined with cling film and send to the refrigerator.




How to care for a slow cooker

Extremely simple maintenance requires a kitchen device such as a Redmond M90 multicooker. Customer reviews indicate that cleaning the device and its individual parts does not take much time and effort. A bowl and other removable parts, as well as spoons, are easy to wash in a dishwasher, but details such as a removable lid and a steam valve are best washed by hand under running water. It is worth paying special attention to detergents that are used in the process of cleaning the multicooker. They should not have abrasive particles or aggressive chemical components. As for the device‚Äôs case itself, it must be wiped with warm water and a soft cloth.

Before starting to use the device, you must carefully study such a document as an instruction. The Redmond M90 crock-pot has a special compartment in which condensate accumulates. It needs to be cleaned every day, and in the case of intensive operation of the device - after each cooking session. To do this, you just need to remove the container from the back of the multicooker, drain the liquid from it, wash it and put it in place.
