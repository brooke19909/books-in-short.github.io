about the project

Where the bustle of the usual gray everyday life is replaced by silence, calm and birdsong, where you can breathe easily and calmly, the Yaninsky Cascade residential complex has appeared. The developer laid the foundation for the so popular today concept of building a "city out of town". Location

"Yaninsky Cascade" is located only 2.5 km from the city, adjacent to the Ring Road. You can get to the city in any way in just 15-20 minutes. It is an ecologically clean area, ideal for families with small children.





Security

The territory of the complex is fenced, is under round-the-clock security, equipped with a video surveillance system. The developer made sure that every resident of the complex felt as safe as possible. Moreover, the houses are equipped with a modern fire alarm system, so there is no reason for concern.

Transport accessibility

For the implementation of the project, Vsevolozhsk district was selected. New buildings will appear near the metro station "Ladozhskaya". Convenient transport interchanges, public transport stops - all this will allow residents of the Yaninsky Cascade complex to easily reach anywhere in the city. And do not be embarrassed by the proximity to the Ring Road. Residents will not care about the noise from the street, because the "green filter" - a wall of centuries-old green spaces separates from the busy highway with a very dense traffic of the new building. The complex itself is literally immersed in greenery, because it is surrounded on all sides by trees, shrubs, which makes the air literally filled with oxygen. The first stage project has already been completed. So, the inhabitants of his houses note that breathing here is especially easy: so clean and fresh air.

LCD "Yaninsky Cascade" impresses with its architectural solution.
