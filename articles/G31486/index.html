How to change data in the policy

Unfortunately, it often happens that when applying for insurance in your account, inaccuracies arise. Then the question arises, how to make changes to the electronic insurance policy. How much does it cost to enter a new driver in the policy?

Insurance companies enter a new face for a fee. Therefore, the question of how much it costs to enter the driver in the CTP insurance policy is very relevant. As a rule, the final cost of the changes directly depends on the following factors:

  • The remaining period for which the validity of the already issued insurance certificate is calculated. If you issued a policy in January, and enter a new driver in June, then only the remaining months, and not the whole year, will be taken into account.
  • MSC and FAC of the driver, the data of which will be entered into the policy.

The total amount is calculated by the formula:

Cost = new policy price (taking into account the remaining insurance period) - the initial cost of the policy √ó remaining insurance period.

The calculation of the total amount involves the maximum coefficients that are available in the database for drivers contributed. When the trustee of KBM and PIC is higher than that of the policy holder, you will have to pay the specified amount. If these parameters are lower, no surcharge will be charged. As a result, if you want to minimize the cost of compulsory motor third-party liability insurance for your car, drivers with a long experience of a trouble-free ride should enter the policy.
