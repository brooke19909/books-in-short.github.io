When is the best time to come to Aqaba?

The best time to visit this city is from October to April. At this time, the air temperature is optimal for adaptation, and the water temperature in the Gulf of Aqaba is quite high. Summer in the city is perhaps too hot for tourists from Europe.

sights

Holidays in Jordan in November for most tourists are, first of all, magnificent well-groomed beaches and a warm sea. But sooner or later, such a pastime bothers. And then tourists go to inspect the numerous historical and cultural monuments, which are the property not only of Jordan, but also of the entire Middle East.



Mamluk Fortress

This is a famous symbol of the city. The citadel was built at the very beginning of the XVI century. It is located on the seafront, surrounded by tall palm trees, and is a magnificent setting for the most vivid and memorable shots to commemorate a vacation in this country. Near the fortress there is a small museum where you can get acquainted with exhibits representing the history of this area.



Lot Cave

This amazing monastery complex has always attracted the attention of tourists. Biblical tradition tells us that it was here that the righteous Lot hid from persecution. In addition to the cave itself, the complex includes a magnificent Byzantine church and several tombs.

Mujib Nature Reserve

Rest in Jordan in November allows fans of eco-tourism in comfortable weather conditions to visit this wonderful reserve. It is located in the Wadi Mujib gorge. On the vast territory, 10 species of rare animals, 20 species of plants and more than 100 species of birds feel comfortable. All inhabitants are under state protection.

Ship "Cedar Pride"

This is another symbol of Aqaba. The ship "Cedar Pride" sank only 130 meters from the coastline at a relatively shallow depth. This is a favorite place for divers and visitors. Almost all local travel companies organize dives in the area.

Residence

Jordan in November presents a large selection of upscale, usually 3-, 4- and 5-star hotels and hotels. Usually in their territories there are health centers. The largest establishments are located in the northern part of the city. Most of them have their own beaches, for example Movenpick, Radisson Blu and Kempinski. They offer spacious rooms with luxurious sea views, furnished with modern, functional furniture and necessary equipment. At these hotels there are saunas, private pools, fitness centers, spas.

In Aqaba there are cheaper hotels, without much chic and brilliance. All clients of such establishments are guaranteed to be provided with comfortable clean rooms and a fairly hearty breakfast. Nine kilometers from Aqaba is the Tala Bay hotel complex, combining several excellent hotels. It has a developed tourist infrastructure (sports grounds, restaurants, spa centers, fitness clubs) and a two-kilometer sandy beach. The complex is designed for wealthy tourists.



Beach vacation

Jordan in November, Aqaba in particular, is ideal for outdoor enthusiasts. This picturesque resort town has many opportunities for fun and fun. The most popular here are various types of water recreation - skiing, windsurfing, snorkeling, fishing and, of course, diving.

Aqaba has several diving centers. Scuba diving will give all vacationers a new, incomparable experience: sea dogs, clown fish, parrot fish, gobies, etc. live in the most beautiful corals. Here you can meet the world's largest fish - the whale shark. Divers can not be afraid of it - this giant eats only plankton.
