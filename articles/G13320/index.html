Travel Tips

What you need to know if you want to make a trip to the hot springs (Yekaterinburg)?

  • If there are health problems, it is necessary to consult with your doctor about the possibility of a trip.
  • It is best to go here in winter. The pleasure of swimming in the open air, when there is snow all around, is incomparable.
  • You need to bring the following things with you: bathing accessories; waterproof shoes; towel; headdress; shower gel; body cream; bathrobe.
  • In no case do not take alcohol to the trip.
  • No matter how you want to leave the warm water, remember that the time spent in the source should not be more than 20 minutes.
  • You can make several visits, with a mandatory rest after each time at least forty minutes.
  • Do not forget to take a shower after bathing.
