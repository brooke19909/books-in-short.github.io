How to make Smesharika from paper and CDs

Since all Smeshariki are round, you can experiment a lot with the base for their torso. Children and their parents create these characters from fruits and vegetables, from balls of yarn, from balloons and even from CDs. In order to do such a craft, you need to have a pair of CDs, colored paper and glue stick.

First, think about the composition: how your smesharik will stand, what will be in his hands, then cut out the arms and legs of the hero, then the details of the image. Place all the parts on the disc and stick them. You can complicate the work and add other materials besides paper: Nyusha make a nose out of a button, and glue an artificial flower on his head, Glue a voluminous pompom on a hat. Experiment!
