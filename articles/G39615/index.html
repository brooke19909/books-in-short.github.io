Hotel "Fort Apatur"

Hotel "Fort Apatur" is located on the coast of the Taman Gulf. It offers stunning views from the windows of the rooms, which are designed for every wealth and taste.



The interior of this Taman hotel, made in a marine original style, will not leave anyone indifferent. Rough massive chairs and tables, accessories made of wood in a marine style, natural stone trim give the hotel a special touch.

The institution is located in a picturesque and beautiful historical place. About two centuries ago, a group of Cossack migrants landed here, who laid the foundation for the unique and distinctive culture of this region. In honor of the ancient settlement, the hotel "Fort Apatur" got its name. "Apatur" in ancient Greek means "deceiving." In fact, its impregnable and strict appearance of the institution makes an impression of deceiving. In fact, he always welcomes all his guests with warmth and joy.

Mini-hotel "Cascade", Taman

Mini-hotel "Cascade" is a large green area. It has gazebos for relaxing, a playground, a cafe with a TV room, real home cooking. There are also summer kitchens with a full set of dishes for cooking on their own for all those who chose this institution, having arrived in Taman.



Hotel "Cascade" is located ten minutes from the sand and pebble beach, promenade and sea. Nearby are a market, cafes, shops, an archaeological museum, a post office, a winemaking museum with its own tasting room, an 18th-century church, and a cinema.

It is also worth mentioning that on the outskirts of the village there is the Ataman ethnographic complex, which was already mentioned just above, and to this day unique archaeological excavations are ongoing.
