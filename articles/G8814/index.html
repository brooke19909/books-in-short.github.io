Rest in Magri, Krasnodar Territory

The village of Magri (Krasnodar Territory) is located on the Black Sea. Its uniqueness lies in the fact that it is located at two borders at once. The first - the border with Sochi passes through the village, the second - the border of the subtropical climate. This place is great for spending a vacation with family or relaxing with friends.





Resort Features

The village of Magri of the Krasnodar Territory got its name due to the addition of the first parts of the names of the parents of the first settlers. Spouses Polyakov built a summer house here with a large beautiful garden and a park of subtropical cultures. They thought for a long time how to name their possession. On the advice of a local elder, their children took the first letters of the names of their parents, Maria and Gregory, it turned out - MaGri. Since then, this resort village is called.

The climate in Magri (Krasnodar Territory) is subtropical. The swimming season is open from May to October. Water in the sea warms up to 24 degrees in the summer, and air - up to 26 degrees. Magri is located in a picturesque place with rich vegetation; mountains protect it from strong winds.

The beach in the resort village is sand and pebble, with a good entry into the sea, which is very convenient for children. The beach area is very clean and comfortable, there are not many people here, so you do not need to take places in advance. Cozy cafes and restaurants work on the coast for vacationers, for children there are water attractions, a trampoline. For lovers of scuba diving there is a place "on the breakwaters" with clear water, where you can see the rich wildlife of the Black Sea. Among the beach activities you can also name riding a banana, catamarans, scooters, boat trips.

Leisure and entertainment

For tourists who come to Magri (Krasnodar Territory), rest can be organized in the following areas:

  • sightseeing tours;
  • fishing on the high seas or on the river;
  • beach vacation.




Sightseeing tours in Magri offer tourists trekking in the mountains, where you can visit the mysterious caves, the parking of primitive man, rocky canyons, beautiful cascading waterfalls. Tourists, at will, will be able to climb the tops of the mountains, fly on a hang glider, take part in rafting on a mountain river, ride a bicycle or SUV on mountain roads. All trips, excursions and trips are accompanied by experienced guides and instructors. Also, optionally can be organized fishing on the high seas or boat trips along the coast. Those who wish can ride a water bike, water ski, go surfing or diving, or go horse riding. In the village itself, a billiard club is popular with resort guests.

Accommodation in Magri

A significant part of the village of Magri (Krasnodar Territory) is occupied by the private sector. Here, the choice of vacationers there are many cottages with different living conditions. Also, a place to stay, you can choose the hotel "Lyubava", mini-hotels "Golden Fish" or "Magri". On the territory of the village there is a boarding house "Magri" and a recreation center "Shuyuk".





Magri is considered the perfect place to relax with your family. Locals say that rest here can contribute to improving family relationships, because the village is named after bright and great love.
