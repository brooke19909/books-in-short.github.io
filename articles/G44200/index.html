Subjects and examples of embroidery of nature with a cross

Embroidery is considered a popular type of needlework, because it combines various techniques to create original things. In nature cross-stitch, you can find interesting plots for paintings and for creating stylish accessories for the interior.

Popular motives

Plots with waterfalls, colored fields and the image of animals carry not only an aesthetic value, but also a magical meaning, because through embroidery with a cross you can bring positive energy and good luck to life.

Unmarried girls choose bouquets with poppies or peonies for work, because these flowers are responsible for a quick marriage. For happiness in marriage, women embroider a unicorn or a couple of horses. The latter are a symbol of a long and strong family life. In cross-stitch nature, motifs with paired beasts are popular, because dualism helps to attract a soul mate to reality.

A background of green leaves is embroidered for prosperity and profit. Waterfalls help in terms of health and improve well-being, but you can not hang such pictures in the bedroom so that the marriage does not collapse.

The magic of paintings

To decorate the house and convey the atmosphere, embroiderers create entire plots that correspond to a specific period or seasons. In winter, cross-stitching the landscape and decorating them at home allows you to feel the spirit of the holiday.

What mood the finished work will bring to the house and how it will be combined with other elements of the interior depends on the choice of the scheme and plot. Manufacturers of embroidery kits develop not only individual subjects for a landscape theme, but also modular paintings, sets for applied embroidery.

In addition to paintings with landscapes, you can embroider a beautiful motif on a pillow, apron, tablecloth or backpack. You can decorate clothes with individual elements and create a designer thing for yourself with a unique history. Cross-stitching makes it possible not only to enjoy the process, but also to decorate wardrobe items and the interior in an original way.
