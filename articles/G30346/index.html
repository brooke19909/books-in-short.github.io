Hotel Medina Belisaire Thalasso 4 * (Hammamet, Tunisia): photos and reviews of tourists

Tunisia, as before, remains one of the most popular resort destinations among our compatriots. Therefore, in our article we want to talk about one of the Hammamet hotels - Medina Belisaire Thalasso 4 *. Exotic Tunisia leaves a unique impression on holidaymakers. Tourists choose this country to see the famous Sahara with its palm oases, plunge into the beautiful sea and enjoy the warm sunshine.

A bit about the hotel ...

Medina Belisaire Thalasso 4 * is a four-star hotel located in the north-east of the country, in the resort of Hammamet, combining vibrant nightlife and a calm, measured vacation. The calling card of the city was kilometer-long beaches with fine sand and thalassotherapy centers.



Medina Belisaire Thalasso 4 * is located in the south of Hammamet. Its snow-white buildings are decorated with columns and antique arches. The complex is surrounded by emerald lawns and a flowering garden. It was built in 2002, and therefore is considered relatively young. The nearest airport to the hotel is Enfida (38 km), Tunis (72 km) and Monastir (102 km) are a bit further.

Number of rooms

Medina Belisaire Thalasso 4 * offers guests 276 rooms in the main and additional buildings. Elegant apartments are made using a harmonious combination of light and dark colors, and with the help of paintings, furniture, flowers and floor lamps creates additional comfort. The rooms are equipped with air conditioning, TVs. Hotel guests can use Wi-Fi and satellite channels. All apartments have terraces or balconies with panoramic views of the park alleys or the pool.

The number of rooms is represented by rooms of the following categories:

  1. Standard double with one bed, its area is 18 square meters. m. The apartments have amenities: safe, minibar, telephone, work desk, bathroom.
  2. Standard Triple with an area of ​​about 20 square meters. m. The apartments have the same amenities as in the first version.

Dining at the hotel

Medina Belisaire Thalasso (ex. Iberostar Belisaire) 4 * offers its guests to take advantage of all the advantages of the All Inclusive system. The main restaurant offers tourists a buffet served with the best dishes of Mediterranean and Tunisian cuisines. In addition, the hotel has four à la carte establishments where vacationers can enjoy Italian, Mexican, Thai, Arabic and Indonesian food. The snack bar serves desserts, salads and snacks throughout the day. At the poolside bar you can always enjoy delicious ice cream and refreshments.



An Arabian cafe serves tea and coffee, while a romantic piano bar hosts theme nights with live music.

Hotel infrastructure

Medina Belisaire Thalasso 4 * (Hammamet) has three pools with fountains and green islands with stone sculptures. Tourists can sunbathe on the sun terraces. There are playgrounds for the kids and a playroom. The hotel has a modern thalassotherapy center, offering a wide range of wellness treatments based on the properties of water. Here you can visit the sauna, hammam and professional massages.

Sports and Entertainment

The animation team Medina Belisaire Thalasso 4 * (Tunisia) in the evenings organizes entertainment shows for children and adults. The hotel has several tennis courts and a golf course. Animators organize sports to keep fit. Tourists can play volleyball, shoot archery, do water aerobics, visit the gym. On the coast, vacationers can enjoy all kinds of water activities.

Hotel beach

Medina Belisaire Thalasso 4 * (Tunisia, Hammett) has its own beach with a length of 116 meters. The coast is equipped with free sunbeds, and tourists can get towels on deposit. The hotel is separated from the beach by a small boardwalk.

A little bit about the resort ...

Hammamet is located southeast of Tunisia. Its resort area stretches for 14 kilometers along the coast. Here is the hotel Medina Belisaire Thalasso 4 * (Hammamet). Yasmine Hammamet is a brand new tourist area of ​​the resort. The city was founded more than five centuries ago. Since ancient times, Hammamet has been a popular holiday destination, as evidenced by the ruins of the Roman villas. The city is considered the most picturesque and green resort of the country.



The first hotels on the local coast began to be built in the early sixties. Currently, Hammamet has become a famous resort, famous not only for its beaches, but also for thalassotherapy centers. The infrastructure is very developed here, thanks to which tourists can find everything they need for a great holiday.

Local Attractions

Relaxing at the hotel, it is worthwhile to devote time and devote it to exploring local attractions. You can start by walking around the city. During which you can visit Ribat and the old medina, a Spanish fortress of the sixteenth century, located near the sea. Here is a museum of the history of the city.

The port with the beautiful name "Marina" is the largest port in North Africa. It is in this area that the old-style Yasmine Hammamet begins.

For tourists, the house of George Sebastian, built in the twenties of the last century, will be interesting. It is surrounded by wonderful cypress trees, and in the garden there is an amphitheater, which still hosts international art festivals.

Although Hammamet is a conservative resort, it is impossible to get bored. Here, every vacationer can find suitable entertainment. Someone likes to bask on the beach, someone wander through the old streets in search of sights, and someone like to relax in the spa. Fans of nightlife will appreciate the disco area, a 20-minute drive from Yasmina Hammamet. One of the most famous discos in the city is Oasis.



The resort is filled with outdoor cafes, restaurants and small shops where you can buy souvenirs.

Medina Belisaire Thalasso 4 *: 2016 reviews

Speaking about the hotel, I want to refer to the reviews of vacationers who visited it. Especially interesting is the opinion of tourists who visited the complex this year. The hotel has a good location. It is located on the second line, here all the hotels are built along the road from which the noise comes. However, Medina Belisaire Thalasso 4 * is located in the depths of the territory, at some distance from the street, due to which extraneous sounds are not so audible. The hotel is surrounded by a beautiful blooming garden. Some rooms have private pool access. Of course, in such places it is a bit noisy during the day, but there is no need to use public corridors. The hotel is very beautiful and comfortable.

Upon arrival at the reception, employees fill out documents very quickly and check into rooms even earlier than the deadline without any additional charges.



The hotel has nice rooms with comfortable beds and quality furniture. High-quality cleaning is carried out daily, if you leave a small tip, then the beds are sprinkled with flower petals. Towels are also changed every day. The bathroom has detergents, but gels and shampoos are given in very small bottles. During cleaning, their stocks are forgotten to replenish. You can remind the maids, then they bring a new portion. During check-in, a bottle of water and a plate of fruit are left for each guest in the room. In the future, water can be taken in a bar, and its amount is not limited, which is convenient in hot climates.

The pools at the hotel are clean and staff are watching. One of them is indoor, it is filled with water. You can use it for free at lunch (from 13.00 to 15.00). There is a children's room for children, there is an animation area. The complex provides the possibility of a free one-time visit to the amusement park and water park, which are within walking distance. The hotel has animation for children and adults, but it is not as intrusive as, for example, in Turkey. In the evenings, shows and discos are organized.

What is the situation with nutrition in Medina Belisaire Thalasso 4 *? Guest reviews regarding food are only positive. On the tables there are always several varieties of meat, fish, chicken, quail. Incredibly sliced ​​vegetables. The local chef pampers guests with wonderful pastries and 4 varieties of ice cream. The main restaurant always has tea, coffee, juices, carbonated drinks, pink, white and red wine, beer. The bars also have local alcohol of an acceptable quality. There are always a lot of fruits and sweets on the tables.



The hotel has its own beach. There are no problems with umbrellas and sunbeds; there are a sufficient number of them. If necessary, the hotel employee helps to put a deck chair in the right place. The beach of the hotel is sandy and very beautiful. It is cleaned daily. The sea is clean and warm, basking on the beach is very nice. Merchants walk along the coast very rarely, so they don’t particularly bother. There is a bar on the beach where you can refresh yourself with drinks.

The hotel staff is very friendly and helpful. Despite the fact that people sometimes look tired, they are very attentive to the guests. The waiters in the restaurant serve quickly and efficiently even without a tip, and a small amount of money generally turns you into a best friend. In general, the hotel service is delivered up to standard. Tourists are very pleased with the service and the friendliness of the staff.



The hotel has a local guide, but you can get to all the most interesting places on public transport and not depend on a large group. Moreover, the cost of such a trip is several times less than sightseeing tours. In resort areas and just on the roads, there are a lot of police and military, keeping order. Patrol cars constantly ply around the city, so there is a sense of security. So, you can go on an independent trip along the coast.

Instead of an afterword

Based on reviews of tourists about Medina Belisaire Thalasso 4 * (Tunisia, Hammamet), we can conclude that this hotel is a great place to relax on the Tunisian coast. It will certainly appeal to those who value coziness and comfort, but at the same time do not want to overpay for a five-star hotel. So, the complex can be considered as an economy class vacation spot.
