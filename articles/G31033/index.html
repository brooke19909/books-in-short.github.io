Houda Golf 3 * (Tunisia / Monastir): photos and reviews of tourists

The Mediterranean coast of Tunisia is open for recreation for almost a year: in the winter months it is not cooler than 16 degrees, and the African summer heat is well tolerated due to the refreshing sea breezes. One of the tourist offers that are rapidly gaining popularity is the ancient city of Monastir, located on the north-east coast of Tunisia, the former religious-historical "capital" of this state, which received a second life as a resort. Everyone who, in addition to historical sights and the Mediterranean climate, is looking for a cheap hotel to relax, will certainly be interested in the Houda Golf Beach Club (3 stars).





Hotel info

Hotel Houda Golf Beach 3 * (Tunisia) is located 13 km from Monastir, in Skanes - a special resort area with many hotel complexes and entertainment venues. The hotel consists of two buildings, built in the mid-80s, in a large landscaped area. In the brochures, including those posted on the websites of Russian travel agencies, the Houda Golf 3 * hotel is listed as a hotel complex for a "relaxing family vacation", operating according to the most convenient service system - all-inclusive.

The hotel buildings are decorated in the spirit of picturesque Arabian color, many flowers and green plants.

For the safety of tourists living in the hotel, the hotel area is equipped with a reliable fence, the windows of the rooms are equipped with protective bars.

The infrastructure of the Houda Golf Beach Club 3 * includes catering establishments (two bars and three restaurants), shops of various kinds, a TV lounge, SPA, sports grounds and a fitness center, car rental, a water park, and three equipped swimming pools with water slides.

What to see? Excursions, sights

Of course, sunbathing, sea water and healing Mediterranean air by themselves will allow you to get an excellent vacation. But do not neglect the cultural program, which includes fascinating excursions and acquaintance with the centuries-old history of the Tunisian state.

A lot of interesting things can be seen in Monastir - a "lively illustration" of the past heyday of traditional Arab culture. Founded, like most of the coastal cities of Tunisia, by the Phoenician sailors, the city at different periods of its life was ruled by Spanish invaders and Byzantine monks, warriors of the Roman and Ottoman empires, sea filibusters and French colonialists. All this is reflected in the architectural monuments that the organizers of excursion routes offer to inspect the guests of Houda Golf 3 *. Tunisia is also known for the coastal city of Sousse (Sousse) - a large "resort for youth", which is located just 24 km from Monastir. The city has all the indispensable attributes of tourist entertainment centers: night clubs, souvenir shops, a colorful Arab market, artisan artisan shops, cafes with local sweets and Arabic coffee, bars, casinos, restaurants, an amusement park and bowling ... Sousse is on the list of cities "World Heritage" by UNESCO. Vacationers are sure to be interested in the luxurious household museum ‚ÄúDar Essid‚Äù, the Archaeological Museum of the ancient stone fort Kasbah and the Medina quarter, traditional for all cities of Tunisia, - the memory of the medieval past of the city. In the vicinity of Sousse are the legendary ruins and catacombs of Carthage and Ribat, a stone defensive fortress with two high towers.

In addition, guests of the Houda Golf Club 3 * hotel can visit longer excursion routes. For example, the city of Doug, founded by Roman legionaries, the Tunisian "green garden" - the agricultural peninsula of Cap Bon, and the pearl of excursion tours of Tunisia - a two-day trip to the Sahara desert.
