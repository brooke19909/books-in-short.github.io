Hylatio Tourist Village APT (Cyprus, Pissouri): Hotel reviews

Hylatio Tourist Village in Cyprus is a comfortable hotel, a vacation in which will be unique, unforgettable and filled with vivid impressions. The picturesque bay, the turquoise pool, the lush greenery of the garden and the colorful flower beds form the perfect holiday destination that all working people look forward to. From our article you can get the most necessary information about this hotel and study the main thing - reviews of vacationers. All this will help to understand whether to book a room in this hotel. But already in absentia it is possible to give an answer that the time spent here will not be considered wasted.





Location

The cozy hotel Hylatio Tourist Village is located in the suburbs of Limassol - the village of Pissouri, next to the bay of the same name. This place is literally lively: there are many shops with a variety of goods, catering establishments, interesting sights and much more. Paphos International Airport is 20 km away and Limassol Port is 35 km away. The famous Temple of Apollo Hilatis is 20 km from the hotel complex.

Hotel Description

Hylatio Tourist Village is a hotel complex surrounded by a landscaped garden with a large oval pool. Nearby is a small bowl designed for children. It consists of two-story houses made in the style of a Greek village, which fully justifies the concept of the establishment: blue and white shades prevail, and the furniture is mainly made of light wood. In addition, it can be noted that such colors favorably affect the emotional sphere of a person.

Small cottages boast their own balconies for residents of the second floor and terraces for those who settled on the first level. They offer a view mainly of the garden. The hotel gives all vacationers the opportunity to feel at home, being in a foreign country. After all, everyone has their own floor or a whole cottage. Rooms at Hylatio Tourist Village

Only 36 houses with rooms of three categories:

  • studio (13);
  • one bedroom (8);
  • one bedroom mezoniette (13).

Each of the rooms at Hylatio Tourist Village APT includes an entrance hall, living room or dining room, bathroom and dressing room. They have the following amenities:

  1. Large or kitchenette with a refrigerator, stove, kettle and coffee maker, dishes, cutlery, and other kitchen utensils.
  2. Bathroom with bath or shower, washbasin, toilet and necessary textiles.
  3. Furnished balcony or terrace.

For a fee, you can use the safe, fan and air conditioning that are already in the room. Additionally, the bathroom has cosmetics and toiletries, and the rooms have telephone and Internet.





Hotel Facilities

The hotel has a service for the disabled, so people with disabilities, including guide dogs, can relax in Hylatio Tourist Village (Cyprus). In other cases, pets are not allowed. It is possible to rent a car or rent a bike for an extra fee to make an unforgettable ride on wheels and get acquainted with the village. Hotel guests can use their private parking free of charge if arriving by car.

At the reception there is a safe in which vacationers can store their things of material value. You can use the fax machine, as well as drink coffee or tea in the lobby of the main building. Special smoking areas are available. The complex has a dry cleaning service, the services of which are also offered for use at an additional cost. A hairdryer, ironing board and iron are provided upon request. Housekeeping is regularly carried out, which includes a change of bed linen and towels.





Fun and relaxation

The pool at Hylatio Tourist Village is seasonal. Around it there are sun loungers for vacationers, and there is also a bar. In walking distance from the hotel complex is a sandy beach with sunbeds and umbrellas. They can be rented at an additional cost. There is also a water sports center. On-site activities include billiards, darts, table tennis and outdoor tennis. Vacationers can go yachting or windsurfing, go water skiing and find other interesting activities on land.

On the way to the beach, tourists will see many shops with necessary goods and cafes with tasty and varied dishes and drinks. In addition, as an entertainment, you can choose a sightseeing tour to get to know Cyprus better and learn the history of a small state. The hotel is designed more for a calm and measured rest than for an active one. The silence and stunning views of the hotel complex immersed in the greenery of the hotel complex predispose to completely relax and plunge into a pleasant nap on one of the sun loungers.





Conditions for children

Hylatio Tourist Village APT Hotel (Cyprus, Pissouri) for babies has its own small pool. It is possible to place a crib in the room. And the restaurant has a special menu and high chairs for feeding.

Dining at the hotel

Guests can check in, paying only the price of the room, or choose the all-inclusive rate, which implies a higher price for accommodation, but the guest can eat and order drinks in the restaurant and bar at the hotel for free (there are restrictions). Near the pool runs Apollo. This is a bar serving a variety of drinks and light snacks. The Ammos Restaurant specializes in Mediterranean and local cuisine, but only tourists who offer non-all inclusive accommodation are offered only dinner.

Hylatio Tourist Village offers self-catering accommodation. It is possible to buy products in the nearest stores and cook yourself. And when there is no desire to develop culinary skills, it is worth going to a nearby cafe. It is noteworthy that the Ammos restaurant prepares dishes with homemade olive oil, spices from the hotel's own garden and products that are purchased from trusted suppliers. Therefore, the food in this institution is delicious and varied.





Hylatio Tourist Village: reviews

In general, the opinion of vacationers about this hotel is positive. Almost all the guests were satisfied with their vacation, not at all regretting that they spent it in this place. But everywhere there are pros and cons. Indignation at vacationers is caused by paid air conditioning and a hairdryer. It would seem that the necessary items, for which other hotels do not have to pay, are not in the public domain in this hotel complex. Other tourists complain that in this village there is absolutely nowhere to go. Like, she is very small, and all the shops that are nearby are useless.

But there are more positive reviews. Most often, guests praise the dishes in the restaurant, which the cooks get very tasty. The delight of large and small guests is caused by the garden - this is a beautiful area, planted with various greens and many flowers. Multicolored, bright and juicy, this area is exactly what it is nice to admire and where you want to relax. The general conclusion about the hotel is as follows: an ideal place for a relaxing pastime.

The average cost of the tour is 43 thousand rubles for 7 nights. This price may vary depending on the type of accommodation, the selected room and the duration of the reservation. But this money is nothing compared to what impressions you can get from living in this hotel!
