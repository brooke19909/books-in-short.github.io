Vneshprombank: customer reviews

In the market of Russian banking services, one can safely note several of the most famous players who deserve the general trust of customers, due to which they attract large amounts of deposits.

Such structures, as a rule, have a fairly rich history of their activities, a ton of tools for working with clients, as well as substantial capital that allows them to work confidently in the investment industry.

One of these banks is the Russian Vneshprombank. Reviews about him, as well as features of the activities of this banking entity, we will provide in this article. In the course of preparing this information, both materials posted on the official website of the structure and reviews that can be found on extraneous resources will be used.

general characteristics

To begin with, Vneshprombank, the reviews of which we are interested, was founded in 1995. During this time, the bank began to enter the 100 largest banks of Russia from a small operational branch-structure with an 8 millionth charter . Due to this, of course, now the bank has acquired new tools in its portfolio, with the help of which it carries out its activities in the financial sector.

Banking products





As for the products that Vneshprombank is ready to offer its customers at any time (reviews confirm this information), these are deposits, credit, bank card services , working with online banking that allows you to manage your finances from a distance, investments in precious metals , service of safe deposit boxes for storing valuables. And start with a description of the most requested services.

Loans





As already noted, the bank is engaged in the issuance of consumer and mortgage loans. In the first case, we are talking about the provision of funds in different currencies for a period of up to two years for inappropriate purposes. That is, the debtor is not obliged to notify what the money will be spent on, the main thing is to meet a number of criteria that Vneshprombank set. Customer reviews indicate that even if your data matches the requirements, the loan may still not be issued.

The basic conditions are as follows: age must be at least 25 years, citizenship - RF, monthly income at the level of at least 25 thousand rubles, work experience - more than a year. The conditions for issuing loans are such that the interest rate varies between 23 and 27 percent per annum, with a minimum amount of 30 thousand and a maximum of 500 thousand rubles.

Mortgage lending is a slightly different product. It provides for the provision of a mortgage loan on the security of an object that is acquired for borrowed funds. Thus, in order to buy an apartment, a person must contribute part of its value (from 10 percent). Further, the loan is issued for a period of 5 to 25 years. As noted on the website, Vneshprombank Bank does not take any fees when applying for a mortgage. Feedback from borrowers insists that you need to carefully read the terms of such loans, since the additional costs that are borne by the client include assessment, insurance, payments to a notary, and more.

Investment activities

The client's investment opportunities with this bank are quite wide. Here everyone is offered the service of brokerage services, it is possible to transfer funds for trust management, accept funds to pay off mutual funds, as well as depository activities with investor contributions. Investor Reviews

So, we gave basic information about how the bank works and what it offers. And what about practical approaches to the client? What awaits us in practice if, for example, we want to contact one of the departments of this structure?

It should be noted that we found a lot of reviews, so among them there are both positive and negative. First, customers express their gratitude for the excellent approach, the highest level of service, the comfort with which they were provided with banking services. Separately, you can find thanks to the employees of certain departments. Also, in positive reviews, people note favorable conditions, simplicity and accessibility in work, the absence of "pitfalls".





As for the negative written by clients about Vneshprombank, it is aimed at different nuances. The first is the level of service in some branches of the bank. Somewhere the person was not offered coffee, somewhere there was no air conditioning, and there was incredible heat, someone was rude to the cashier. There are negative reviews about the bank in terms of the wording of the agreement, according to which funds on foreign currency deposits can be returned in rubles, as well as exposing some more unfavorable conditions for the client. Finally, there is a part of the feedback on the incompetence of the maintenance staff, which manifested itself in the form of a too long deadline for completing a task or neglect. You can also see complaints because of the unclear conditions of some deposits (for example, "Childhood") - customers simply cannot understand how a particular service works.

Employee Reviews

In addition to the service, I would also like to provide a little information about the work in the structure of Vneshprombank. Employee reviews will help us with this.
