Rest in Vityazevo: reviews and photos of tourists

Vityazevo is a small village located in the suburbs of Anapa. In the midst of the holiday period, tourists from different regions of Russia and even from neighboring countries fill it. What is the reason for his popularity? The fact that the resort town has all the conditions for a comfortable stay, and also has a wonderful climate.

Reviews of Vityazevo often talk about the features of the village. What are they like? Where do tourists recommend staying and where to go? About it further.

general information

Vityazevo is a small town located within the territory of the municipal district of Anapa. This resort town is very popular among tourists from large settlements, who especially miss the countryside of the countryside, which is Vityazevo.

In the village, tourists are attracted by the beautiful nature, as well as the warm and clean Black Sea, which is rather shallow along the shores of the village, which especially pleases tourists with small children. As stated in the reviews of Vityazevo in Anapa, you can relax here very quietly, enjoying the silence and clean air filled with a lot of healthy minerals and other trace elements.

Climatic features

Some fans of beach vacations in their comments admit that it is in this village that you can almost every day enjoy the sun's rays, pleasantly warming the body. Here the warm air temperature is kept even on a cloudy day - it is pleasantly pleasing. Thanks to the successful microclimate, to the limits of which the village of Vityazevo belongs, tourists resting here never suffer from the debilitating heat even at temperatures above 30 degrees, which is often observed in this place. This feature was formed due to the fact that this village is located in a good place - between the Black Sea and the estuary.

Private holiday

In the travel planning process, almost all tourists ask questions about where to stay. In fact, there are lots of possible answers to this question, however, as travelers note in their reviews of Vityazevo, the cheapest option here is the rooms located in the private sector houses - to accommodate tourists, the owners of many households erect guest houses near their dwellings.

As for the living conditions in such places, among them you can find various, most suitable for individual requests. As a rule, many owners offer economy conditions designed for 2-4 people in one room, where there is a certain number of sleeping places, beds, a mirror, a table and a place for clothes. The cost of such housing costs about 300-400 rubles per day. As tourists note, in such houses the possibility of self-cooking in the shared kitchen is offered, where a minimal amount of household appliances is provided, as well as refrigerators for storing food. This option is especially convenient for people who plan their vacation in a family structure, which includes children - kids can cook their own meals, being completely confident in its freshness and usefulness of the ingredients.

As for more comfortable conditions, they can also be provided in the private sector. As a rule, they are available in guest houses, in the territory of which there is often a swimming pool, playground and other amenities. Apartments of this level often have air conditioning, a TV with a large list of cable or satellite channels, as well as modern furniture. Amenities in the form of a bathtub and toilet are also often available directly in the room - this is very good, according to many travelers. As noted in many reviews of Vityazevo, in guest houses, tourists can feel at home. Here, as a rule, there is a garden area with landscaped courtyards, along which you can walk or just spend time in a comfortable chair.

As for the cost of living in comfortable apartments of guest houses, it is from 800 rubles per day (per person). Moreover, as vacationers managed to notice, the price can skyrocket, depending on the proximity of home ownership to the coastline.

In their reviews of Vityazevo guest houses, tourists often evaluate the conditions and services provided in properties such as Olympic, Golden Fleece, Jolie, Mango, Hellas and Asia with positive comments. Here, they said, two indicators such as price and quality are excellently correlated.

However, as tourists note in their reviews, the living conditions here are sometimes much more pleasant than in the private sector. The price for a day of staying in a standard room of a local hotel starts from 2500 rubles.

Vacation with children

As stated in many comments, Vityazevo is great for families with children, even if they are very small. This is due to the fact that the town has a considerable number of places to stay with amenities organized for such a group of tourists. In addition, the village of Vityazevo is famous for its quiet and comfortable atmosphere, clean air and a pleasant mild climate - undoubtedly, such conditions are very useful for kids.

As for places to stay, in the reviews of Vityazevo guest houses and rooms in the private sector, most tourists recommend going to the Golden Fleece or to Jolie. It is here, according to them, that the most comfortable conditions for family guests are arranged. In particular, there is an open area with playgrounds where kids can frolic, a considerable number of bright children's dishes are offered in food, and, if necessary, a cot can be provided for the smallest.

Among other things, in the village there are a considerable number of places that you can always visit with a child. Among the most popular among them are: a water park, Paralia, a large amusement park, as well as the Bathyscaphe - a tunnel aquarium.

Beach vacation

Reviews of Vityazevo beaches very often say that almost all of them are quite clean, which undoubtedly pleases tourists. Along the coastline there is a large amount of entertainment for people with different preferences and opportunities. If desired, vacationers can ride a "tablet" or a "banana", rent a boat and go on it to the Black Sea or, for example, go diving - in the village there is a small club "Chernomor" in which they offer to master such an original and very interesting kind of sport.

What are the best beaches in Vityazevo? The largest number of positive comments is received by the Central, which is a kind of visiting card of the village. It is located at the very end of the main street of the town and is the most comfortable, clean place for relaxation and swimming with a rather shallow coast.

In addition to the Central, the popular beaches include Vityaz, Pearl of Russia, Barguzin, as well as one adjacent to the Niko boarding house. The last of all is famous for the fact that its waters remain clean at any time of the year and in any weather - this happens only because the sea bottom in this place has a particularly relief structure.

In reviews of the sea in Vityazevo, tourists constantly talk about the fact that along the coast of the village its waters heat up very quickly, which ensures the early opening of the holiday season. This phenomenon is connected with the fact that the shores here are relatively shallow and a very small amount of time is required for normal heating of the water.

Aquapark

In reviews of vacations in Vityazevo, many tourists recommend to visit the local water park, which has been functioning for a long time and attracts the attention of a large number of guests of the village. There is a large number of attractions designed for both adults and children - they are divided into different sections.

In their comments, vacationers describe the emotions received during their stay on the rides. The greatest amount of attention here attracts a large slide, the descent on which is really fast: the speed of a person with an average body weight falling from it is about 12 m / s.

On the territory of the Olympia water park there is a large Medea restaurant, the menu of which invites visitors to taste dishes prepared in the best traditions of Greek cuisine. Local prices here pleasantly surprise many.

Festivals

In the reviews of vacationers about Vityazevo it is often said that the most memorable moments of the whole rest are the festivals that are held here. Indeed, such actions that take place on the local Zhar site attract a huge number of tourists every year on its territory.

In the midst of the spa season, tourists who prefer to spend their vacations on holiday in Vityazevo become participants in music festivals attended by folk groups and rock performers. The site also has a cozy restaurant that you can visit. And sitting on his summer terrace, guests have the opportunity to listen to interesting hits of talented performers live.

Treatment

Judging by the reviews of vacationers about Vityazevo, most tourists prefer to go here, since here you can not only have a good time relaxing on the shores of the warm sea, but also get better. For this purpose, in the village there are sanatoriums organized in the territorial proximity to healing springs.

The village of Vityazevo is famous for its mud springs, saturated with various trace elements useful for the human body and components that cure any disease. According to tourists, the local mud copes with the treatment of various skin diseases like eczema or diathesis. Staying in this village, you can observe a large crowd of people in various sources, among which the most popular is Tizdar.

Lotus valley

The lotus valley is one of the real wonders of nature that is available in this small town. The reviews with photos about Vityazevo often tell about the beauty of this place, as well as its unusual atmosphere. As those travelers who have observed the local beauty note, the Lotus Valley is this truly amazing sight that is certainly worth seeing.

Its rental is possible only in case of ordering excursions designed for four people - no more and no less. As part of such a trip, tourists can observe the beauty of Cossack Eric - another natural attraction of Vityazevo.

In reviews of the Lotus Valley, some travelers talk about how it was created. To do this, all plant seeds were pre-treated in a special way, after which they were germinated in buckets. After the sprouts reached a certain size, they were planted at the bottom of the reservoir.

Other attractions

In addition to all these places, the village has a considerable number of other attractions that should also be visited by tourists who came here for the first time. The reviews of Vityazevo indicate that there are several interesting religious sites in this town, including the Church of St. George the Victorious and the complex "Holy Handle", which is represented by a combination of an old church site built in the 18th century and a healing spring.

Together with children, travelers are advised to visit the Bathyscaphe tunnel, where you can get acquainted with some interesting representatives of the underwater world. Also for this purpose, the Byzantium amusement park is excellent, where a large number of attractions are represented.

One of the most interesting places in the village is the promenade of Paralia, which, rather, resembles a Greek town. Here you can often notice interesting people dressed in old clothes, as well as small objects of architecture reminiscent of the buildings of ancient times.
