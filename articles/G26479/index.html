Citin Garden Resort 3 * (Thailand, Pattaya): hotel description, reviews

If you are looking for a budget, but comfortable hotel with hospitable staff in Pattaya (Thailand), then Citin Garden Resort 3 * may be a good option for you.



Location, general information, photo

This hotel is located in South Pattaya, a 10-minute drive from the city center. The nearest beach is 2 kilometers from the hotel. Most tourists arrive in Pattaya from Bangkok Airport. This air harbor is 150 kilometers from the city.

Citin Garden Resort was built in 2009. It consists of a five-story modern building and a small green area. The hotel includes 70 superior, deluxe and family rooms. The apartments have everything you need for living. The hotel has a restaurant, bar, parking, swimming pool with jacuzzi, garden, sun terrace, laundry. Everywhere in the hotel there is access to wireless Internet.



Citin Garden Resort 3 *: reviews of Russian tourists

Every experienced traveler probably knows how important it is to approach planning a trip abroad with all responsibility. Moreover, we’ll start thinking about all the nuances and it is advisable to start collecting information long before the expected date of departure. This approach will allow you to find the most economical flight option, get acquainted with the features of the culture and history of the country that you plan to visit, as well as choose the best option for your stay. Many tourists consider the last point to be one of the most important, because your impressions of the whole vacation will depend on how satisfied you are with the hotel. Therefore, experienced travelers are advised not only to study the official description of the hotels and listen to the advice of travel agents, but also to get to know as many reviews as possible from those who have recently visited a particular place. Indeed, in their comments, former guests try to talk about the shortcomings that you may encounter in this hotel, and not focus solely on its merits. In order to save you time and effort, we offer you generalized reviews of tourists from Russia about their vacation at the Citin Garden Resort 3 * hotel (Pattaya, Thailand). We note right away that the vast majority of comments are positive, which suggests that our compatriots were quite satisfied with the chosen hotel. But we learn about everything in more detail.



Housing stock

Judging by their comments, our compatriots were quite satisfied with the rooms offered to them in this hotel. So, according to them, the apartments here are quite spacious, clean, comfortable. Guests loved the large furnished balconies. Tourists claim that the rooms have everything you need for a comfortable stay - comfortable large beds, a spacious wardrobe and other furniture, a TV with three channels in Russian, a minibar, air conditioning, a hairdryer, a bathroom, a safe. If necessary, you can take an iron and an ironing board at the reception. Please note that the minibar is stocked with soft drinks and alcohol. If you use it, then when leaving the hotel you will be asked to pay their cost. A big plus, according to many travelers, is the fact that the rooms have free Wi-Fi. Moreover, the connection speed, as a rule, is very good.

Cleaning

Our compatriots did not have any particular complaints about the work of the Citin Garden Resort maids. So, the cleaning of the rooms was carried out daily. True, the floors in the rooms were not always washed, but the guests did not see a serious problem in this. But towels and bed linen were changed regularly every other day.



Check in, departure

According to our compatriots, newly arrived guests, reception workers at the Citin Garden Resort 3 * hotel (South Pattaya), as a rule, try to place in their rooms immediately. Therefore, even if you arrive at the hotel early in the morning, then you most likely do not have to wait two in the afternoon to get the keys to the apartment.

As for the day of departure, then vacate the room should be before noon. Moreover, if you used a minibar, then you will be asked to pay the cost of drinks. If your departure from the hotel is planned only in the evening, you can either extend the room for several hours at an additional cost, or leave things in storage at the reception and continue to rest until the time of departure arrives.



Location, Territory

Most tourists who have chosen the "Citin Garden Resort" (Pattaya), from the very beginning knew about the features of its location. Therefore, those guests who, in their reviews showed dissatisfaction with the remoteness of the hotel from the sea and the city center, simply inattentively familiarized themselves with the information about it. So, according to our compatriots, the nearest beach can be reached in 10 minutes by tuk-tuk. You can also take advantage of the free shuttle service provided by the hotel. So, a minibus leaves the Citin Garden building every hour, bringing everyone to the Pattaya pier. But here you have to go back already under its own power.

To the city center, guests can also take a tuk-tuk ride. In addition, many tourists vacationing in Thailand prefer to rent a motorbike. It costs quite a bit, but gives travelers the freedom of movement.

In the vicinity of the Citin Garden Resort there are several cafes, restaurants, massage parlors, tour agencies, souvenir shops. Also nearby is a large shopping center and a night market.

As for the own territory of the Citin Garden Resort 3 * hotel, reviews of which we are considering, then it, according to tourists, is, although small, but very well-groomed, green and beautiful. It has a small pool with jacuzzi and a sun terrace. So guests can always have a good time, sitting on a comfortable sunbed in the shade of sprawling trees and palm trees.



Nutrition

The cost of living in the Citin Garden Resort includes breakfast only. According to the hotel guests, they are quite acceptable. So, breakfasts are held in the hotel restaurant in buffet style. Vacationers in the morning can eat two types of soup, meat dishes, toasts with jam, several types of rice, stewed and fresh vegetables, fruits, pastries, juice, milk, coffee and tea. Especially tourists praise pineapples. According to them, these fruits here are always very tasty.

During the day, hotel guests can have lunch and dinner both in the "City Garden" restaurant, and in one of the cafes in the vicinity or in the center of Pattaya. Here you can find establishments not only with traditional Thai, but also with European and even Russian cuisine.

Beach vacation

As already noted, the Citin Garden Resort 3 * hotel is located at some distance from the sea. As for the beaches of Pattaya, in general, according to tourists, you can relax on them, but for many they seem too dirty. Therefore, most travelers prefer to go to the nearby islands. The most popular in this regard is Koh Lan. You can get to it from the pier, which can be reached by minibus from the hotel. From here every half hour ferry leaves to Koh Lana. There are several beaches on the island. According to vacationers, they are all very clean, with a fine white sand. The sea here has a beautiful turquoise color. There are cafes and bars on the island, so if you wish, you can either have a bite to eat or refresh yourself with your favorite drink.
