Where is this city?

Pushkino is a city near Moscow located in the north-west of the capital. The distance to Belokamennaya is 31 kilometers, if we take the center of Moscow as a reference point. And if you count from the Moscow Ring Road, then 21 kilometers. The path to Pushkino lies through Mytishchi.

The fare on the route "Moscow-Pushkino" as of 2016:

  • 82 rubles for an ordinary train;
  • Express - from 140 rubles;
  • REKS - 150 rubles.

REKS and express do not differ in time - 29 minutes from the Yaroslavl station to Pushkino, and on a regular train - from 45 minutes or more.

How to get to the Yaroslavl station?

If you are going to use the metro, then you need the Komsomolskaya station. It is recommended to go along the ring line to immediately go to the Yaroslavl station. You can get off the train on the Sokolnicheskaya line. It is advisable to board the first car from the center (from the south) or the last (if you are traveling from the Rokossovsky boulevard).

So, when we go outside, we will have two stations in front of us - Leningradsky and Yaroslavsky. Go right to the white-green pavilions. It is there that the suburban box office, which we spoke about earlier, are located.



It is worth noting that at the Komsomolskaya stations of the radial and ring lines, cars often do not stop due to repairs. Therefore, in order not to be late for a train or train, you should leave your home / work at least half an hour earlier.
