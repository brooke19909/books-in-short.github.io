DIY crocheted fashion collar



A do-it-yourself collar can be created in a few days. Moreover, there are a lot of knitting patterns and patterns now. After all, collars have always been successful with fashionistas and were considered a great addition to the wardrobe. Their unique look and delicacy has always been worthily emphasized by leading clothing designers. In the fashion world, there are collections based only on different models of collars made from improvised materials, as well as from production waste. Do-it-yourself knitted collars are also famous, evolution schemes for which were developed by computer geniuses and artists.

A simple way to stand out from the crowd



Knitted collars with their own hands will undoubtedly decorate any image and emphasize individuality. After all, they can be worn not only on holidays and significant dates, but also in the office. The classic look - a light top and a black bottom - can be perfectly emphasized with a collar. It can have completely different color solutions, which is ideal for "diluting" the image. A do-it-yourself collar is another replacement for expensive jewelry and various accessories. Indeed, in addition to it, nothing is required, and if they add it, then only earrings or a small bracelet. An image with a collar is an ideal expression of the natural beauty and grace of a woman.

How to knit collars with your own hands

To make a product, you first of all need to decide on the scheme and type of collar. Then buy yarn and pick a suitable size hook. Be sure to decide on the collar model before starting work - will it really fit most of the things in your wardrobe? Then you can safely start knitting. It is necessary to start knitting a collar with your own hands from the middle of the product. From this he will take the form of a semicircle. But there are models that consist of individual motives that connect only strictly according to the scheme.

Determine the length of the product



In order for collars to fit perfectly on your shoulders with your own hands, you need to correctly measure the circumference of the neck. For this, a centimeter tape is used, and all results are recorded. According to the scheme, you can calculate what size your collar will be and approximately how long. If necessary, you can increase the product by knitting several additional rows. And with a large collar size, you can not perform the penultimate rows. But do not forget that the last row must be knit. It serves as an additional decoration and forms the main part of the entire product.

Ways to make ties or fixing elements of the product

Do-it-yourself collar, like all products, needs fixing elements. To do this, you can make ties from air loops or pass a thin satin ribbon throughout the product. But there are also models that are fixed with buttons, Velcro, carabiners, all kinds of fasteners and other elements. The method of fastening depends only on the knitting pattern of the collar and on your imagination.
