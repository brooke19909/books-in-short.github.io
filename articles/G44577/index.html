Which is better - a new building or a secondary housing?

Primary housing has not yet been registered. Typically, such a market offers objects that are not yet completed. The sale of such apartments is regulated by law. According to it, the developer is obliged to complete the house, put it into operation, and also transfer it to the participant in shared construction.





When the house is ready for operation, an act of its transfer and acceptance is drawn up. The buyer must put a signature that fixes the ownership of the housing. After receiving the certificate, the apartment becomes "secondary". Such real estate is private, state and municipal.

To choose a suitable apartment, you need to pay attention to several important criteria:
