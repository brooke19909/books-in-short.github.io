Where to begin?

Ribbon embroidery lessons for beginners relate to the very basics: with the material necessary for the work. All the needlewoman needs is a hoop, fabric, needle and ribbons.

Any suitable fabric: both on silk and velvet with skillful work excellent flowers from ribbons will blossom. Novice craftswomen should opt for a regular canvas or linen fabric with mesh weaving. It is only necessary to pick up soft loose material.

The base fits tightly on the hoop, the size of which is larger than the future work itself.

Ribbon embroidery with tulips, daisies and other flowers should be done with certain tools. Needles should be with ‚Äúflat‚Äù ears. The thickness of the needles depends on the fabric. And to begin to learn this skill should be using medium-density fabrics, on which the pattern will be embroidered with thin needles.





In hardware stores you can find a wide variety of ribbons: corrugated, silk, satin, moire, velvet. Ribbon embroidery lessons for beginners usually start with the use of ordinary satin ribbons, the width of which will not exceed 4 mm and a length of 50 cm. It is better to give preference to ribbons without a hard edge - they most successfully fit into any pattern.

Three simple stitches

What does embroidery with ribbons begin with? A master class on this needlework always starts with fixing the tape itself. Correct mounting will not create additional volume from the wrong side of the work.

The free tip of the tape folds twice. The bending width should not exceed 2 mm. He is additionally pierced by a needle. This allows you to create a "flat site". At the end of the work, the needle is displayed on the wrong side and a small loop is made under the stitches. The result is a flat, solid mount.

It all starts with the basics. So is embroidery with ribbons. For beginners, step-by-step explains all the stages of creating basic stitches. Learning how to perform them, you can combine, and in the end you will get great work.

The first stitch is called ‚Äúforward needle‚Äù. The result will be more effective if you experiment with the length and tension of the stitches.





The next type of stitches is called ‚ÄúJapanese‚Äù. It starts with a simple stitch, the length of which varies from 2 to 4 cm. The needle is displayed on the front side of the fabric, after which the needle is sent to the wrong side again, ‚Äúpiercing‚Äù the stitch along the edge or in the middle, depending on the desired shape.

The "French" knot is also very popular. Its basis is still a simple stitch. The needle is displayed on the front side of the work, after which a ribbon is wrapped around it in 1-3 layers. The needle is reinserted on the wrong side a few millimeters from the exit site. The knot turns out beautiful and dense.

There are no specific rules for embroidering stitches; there are no universal sizes of work. Embroidery ribbons for beginners step by step, of course, undersigns, but only to consolidate the basic skills. Tulips

Embroidery with ribbons of tulips, roses, daisies or other flowers will give the interior a stylish and original, will bring bright colors and a summer mood into the house.

Let's try to create tulips. How does this embroidery with ribbons come about? The master class will be small. What you need to work:

  • gabardine - 5 meters;
  • green satin ribbon with a width of 6 millimeters - 2 meters;
  • green satin ribbon 1.5 mm wide - 3 meters;
  • several tapes of various shades, their width should be equal to 2.5 centimeters;
  • hoop or wooden frame;
  • needles with wide ears.

After the fabric is firmly fixed on the hoop, it's time to understand what embroidery with ribbons is. Tulips are worked out easily, and the result will please for many years.

Flowers

First, you need to fix the color ribbon in the needle. We will embroider a bud. The tape is pulled through the fabric towards itself in a direction from the bottom up. The second petal goes in the opposite direction. It turns out a bud of two petals. In order for the picture to look neat from the wrong side, you should take care of this in advance and not make broaches.





After the bud is formed, the edges of the ribbon are cauterized with a match or lighter. The process of creating tulips gives an idea of ‚Äã‚Äãwhat embroidery ribbons. For beginners, schemes may be needed, but soon the craftswoman will be able to create masterpieces without using any templates.

Stems and leaves

When the desired number of buds has blossomed on the fabric, and the craftswoman was able to figure out how the tulips are embroidered with ribbons, it is time to create stems for them. To create them, you need six-millimeter satin silk ribbons. One of them is fixed in the needle and displayed on the front side of the product. Then the ribbon is folded into a strong tourniquet, forming a thin flower stalk. Its second end is stuck in the right place, and the ends of the tape are cauterized.

In the case of leaves, you need a ribbon 1.5 cm wide. The needle with the tape fixed is displayed on the front side. The leaves themselves are arranged in random order, at the request of the needlewoman.

Daisies

Easy to perform and embroidery with chamomile ribbons. The same tools will be required, but instead of colored ribbons, white and yellow will be needed.

A needle with a white ribbon strung out is pulled out at the place where they plan to make the center of the flower. Now make the first petal. A needle is stuck in a straight line. Next, the procedure is repeated. Petals are arranged in a circle of a flower. After the flower is completed, the tape is fixed on the wrong side. Its tip is trimmed and set on fire so that it does not bloom later. And this embroidery is done with ribbons. For beginners, schemes will not be needed. Everything is clear from the explanation.





The next desired color is yellow or orange. We will make them the core of the flower. She is embroidered with a ‚Äúneedle forward‚Äù stitch.
