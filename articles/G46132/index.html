Access to the global web through a public access point

A data exchange standard such as Wi-Fi allows you to connect to the network without using wires. But before you use the Internet for free, you must first make sure that your laptop or computer supports this technology.

If the device has a Wi-Fi module, then it turns out in restaurants, cafes and other similar institutions where this opportunity is provided, to surf the Internet for free. Those who live near large shopping malls were especially lucky, because you can try to connect to an open network without leaving your home.

Almost every day there are new Wi-Fi points, but, unfortunately, most of them are under a password. Nevertheless, there are many establishments that distribute the Internet for free using this technology to attract new customers and advertising. However, public Wi-Fi has its drawbacks:

  • connection speed is poor;
  • low level of data security;
  • access only within the institution.
