We are reviewing the review. Dishwasher: pros and cons

More and more modern kitchen is becoming like a space shuttle. The hostess only needs to press the right button in time, and household appliances will do everything for her with brilliance and right on time. And if food processors, slow cookers and hot grills have already taken root in the kitchen, then many are in no hurry with the purchase of a dishwasher. They believe that its presence is just a whim, and do not see it as an urgent need. But is it really so? Those who are still thinking, should take a closer look at the feedback left by others about her.



Once in the kitchen, the dishwasher quickly turns into an indispensable assistant. Now you do not need to wash dishes daily, standing idle at the sink for hours. In addition, the family once and for all decides who and when should do it. True, studies show that, due to ignorance of the technology of the dishwasher, disputes continue because of how to properly put the dishes in it.

Rules for loading dishes

Due to ignorance of these simple rules, often even the most high-quality equipment receives very unflattering feedback about themselves. The dishwasher can cope with the most difficult contaminants if you follow the manufacturer's recommendations during its operation. So, be sure to use a special detergent and carefully select the standard program for washing dishes. Pots and trays can be washed at a higher temperature, but to rinse porcelain cups and glass wine glasses, you need only cool water.

It is also very important to correctly put this dishes in baskets. Nothing should interfere with the water, first remove the impurities, and then all devices dry. Therefore, all pots, cups and plates should be folded in an inclined position. There should also be a small gap between them. In addition, small cutlery, knives and ladles are preferably placed in specially designed compartments for them.

Dishwasher: built-in or freestanding?



After the hostess finally becomes convinced of the need to acquire such an assistant in the kitchen, she faces a difficult choice between the built-in and separate appliances. And here professional help can come to the rescue. A dishwasher is often bought during repairs in the kitchen. And to maintain the aesthetic unity of the lower facades, it is better to choose a fully or partially built-in technique.

If the repair has already been done and it is not planned to change the furniture in the near future, then it will be more convenient to purchase a freestanding model. Moreover, such a dishwasher will be cheaper. The price for it will be about the same as for built-in appliances. The cost of dishwashers starts from about 9 thousand rubles.

Useful little things



When choosing a dishwasher, it is important to pay attention to other useful little things. So, in embedded models it will be very useful option, which displays using the infrared beam on the floor information about the execution of the program. In addition, a technique has recently appeared with three baskets and the ability to simulate the space inside. This is very convenient when loading different types of dishes.

For those who have already left their review, the dishwasher has become a real lifesaver. And they never regretted their purchase. Moreover, now there is no need to argue about who will wash dirty dishes today.
