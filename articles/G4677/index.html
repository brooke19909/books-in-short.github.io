Location Description





Citadel Azur Resort 5 * hotel is located almost on the seashore, in the picturesque resort village of Sahl Hasheesh. Nearby there is a yacht harbor. The hotel complex itself is a truly fabulous oasis in the middle of the desert.

Hurghada International Airport is about 20-30 minutes drive from the hotel 5 * Citadel Azur Resort. Tez-tour, for example, as well as almost all other travel agencies, provides its customers with a convenient transfer from the airport to the hotel and back. Therefore, you definitely should not worry about moving and the availability of transport.

What does a hotel complex look like? Short description

The hotel complex Citadel Azur Resort Sahl 5 * is amazing with its unique architecture. It occupies a vast territory, which is divided into several levels.
