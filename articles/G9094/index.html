Popular tilda snail patterns

Passion for dolls sewn in the tilde style is gaining more and more popularity. Children and adults like them. Tilde snail patterns are available to everyone, and sewing takes very little time.



MK for beginners

Beginners need to start with a simple pattern of tilde snails in order to develop sewing skills. To start, prepare the necessary materials:

  • Fabric of different colors.
  • Threads and needles.
  • Hollofiber or synthetic winterizer.
  • Scissors.
  • Chalk or a simple pencil.
  • Sewing machine.

The process of creating a snail:

  1. First, draw a pattern on thick cardboard. So she will serve for a long time and will not remember.
  2. Then we transfer the pattern to the fabric, folded in half with the wrong side out.
  3. We draw allowances for seams of 0.5 mm and cut out.
  4. With a thread of contrasting color we make a basting.
  5. We sew parts on a sewing machine. We leave a small area unstitched.
  6. We turn out the sewn parts with a wooden skewer.
  7. The body and the carapace are well stuffed with syntepon.
  8. Sew the remaining holes with a blind stitch.
  9. Sew the shell to the body of the snail.

Decorate at will, draw eyes or sew small buttons. Such a snail will be a great gift or toy for a child.

Popular pattern

DIY handmade toys are very popular. Even a beginner can figure out how to sew a tilde snail. The pattern consists of only two parts: the body and the shell. The snail carries the symbolism of life development. To stitch it, you need:

  • Fabric in two shades.
  • Synthetic winterizer.
  • Threads.
  • Needle.

How is a simple tilde snail sewn? Master class on manufacturing:

  1. First, transfer the pattern to a well-ironed fabric. For the body, choose solid, and for the shell - with a pattern or small pattern.
  2. On a typewriter we sew all the details with a simple seam. Select threads to match the fabric. Leave small gaps unbroken so that parts can be turned out.
  3. After we filled the body and the shell with a synthetic winterizer, we sew in the holes.
  4. Pin the shell to the body with pins. Sew in a blind stitch.

You can sew small pillows, crochet flowers. The snail will be a wonderful gift for the holiday.

Shabby style

Tilde snail patterns allow the use of various materials. Shebby is a very gentle and youthful style. To decorate the snail we use:

  • Pearls.
  • Ruffles and ruffles.
  • Satin ribbons.
  • Vintage fabric.

A toy made in the Shabby style should be perfect, without a touch of negligence. For the shell of a snail, you can use the decoupage technique and transfer the pattern to the fabric.



Sewing secrets

In order for the tilde snail patterns to be even, and the toy itself to keep in shape, you need to use high-quality fabrics. The best choice would be linen or cotton.

For padding, buy a synthetic winterizer or holofiber. They are quite soft, it is convenient to work with them. A toy does not need to be packed tightly, the packing should be evenly distributed over each part.

Accessories for making dolls are very diverse. For a snail, you can use beads, beads, buttons, ribbons, pendants. Sewn on artificial flowers look beautiful.

The following tools are prepared for work:

  • Tailor's scissors.
  • Chalk or marker for applying a pattern to a fabric.
  • Wooden skewer to turn parts.
  • Threads and needles.

If there is no sewing machine, you can sew the toy with your hands.

Carefully make a pattern and cut. Take your time while sewing. All seams should be neat. Remember to make seam allowances.

Tilda-style snails are very beautiful. They can be of different colors, iridescent and plain, soft pink and bright red. It all depends on the imagination and choice of fabric and decor.
