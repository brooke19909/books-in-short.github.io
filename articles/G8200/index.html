Beginner needlewomen: learning to crochet a scarf

Scarf is the first product of almost every beginner knitter. It is during the manufacture of this accessory that needlewomen reinforce the skills of performing basic types of loops and hone their skills. In this article we will tell you how to crochet a scarf .



The famous scarf "Vivien". We do the designer thing with our own hands

You will succeed, and your wardrobe will replenish with a beautiful and elegant accessory.

What do you need for work?

Before telling how to crochet a scarf, let's talk about the necessary materials. The thread will need an average thickness of approximately 300 meters / 100 grams. In composition it can be wool or half-wool (for the winter version), cotton or acrylic - for the summer. The total amount of yarn for making a Vivien scarf is 200-250 grams. In work, we use hook number 3.

We knit the base

The scarf consists of two elements: the central strip and frills around it. We start with the main part. We collect a chain of 18 air loops. 3 of them are lifting, and at 15 we are doing the ‚Äúgrid‚Äù pattern. How to crochet a scarf with this pattern?

Make 2 air loops, then insert the hook into the third loop of the chain and knit one crochet column. It turned out one grid cell. Now again, perform 2 air loops and in the third from the previous column, knit the crochet column. You already got two cells. So draw up the work to the end of the row. You should get 5 cells. With this pattern, tie the fabric of the desired length, but not less than 1.5 m, otherwise the product will not look spectacular.

Frill

We crochet a scarf . For beginner needlewomen, we describe in detail how to perform a shuttlecock for Vivienne - a women's accessory around the neck.

On all sides of the grid, we draw a border with single crochet columns. Keep in mind that in one cell you need to make 3 such loops. Turn the work. Now we knit a shuttlecock directly. In each loop of the previous row (borders) we knit 2 loops with one crochet. So make out the whole series. As a result, the number of loops will double. We do the next row in exactly the same way: we knit two out of one column. At this stage of the work, you will already see how the frill is formed. Third row: * from one loop - two, from one loop - one *. Repeat from * - * to the end. The number of loops will increase not in two, but in one and a half times. Knit the next row with single crochet. Cut and fasten the thread. Women's accessory on the neck "Vivien" is ready.
