The territory of the hotel and complex

Laguna Holiday Club 5 * reviews characterize as a small hotel. It has a total of seventy-nine rooms, located in a snow-white three-story building, built in a colonial style. The territory of the hotel itself is small, compact. But it has everything you need: a playground, a pool with slides, a mini club, a terrace for sunbathing. Wi-Fi is available throughout the hotel and in the rooms.

Tourists devote much more reviews to the hotel complex as a whole. Fortunately, access to neighboring hotels is not prohibited. The territory is truly huge. It also includes lagoons through which the ferry runs.

A tropical garden or, rather, a well-groomed jungle is simply mesmerized by the singing of exotic birds and the flowering of lush vegetation. Three elephants even live in this forest, which makes children incredibly happy.





Rooms and conditions

The five-star status of the Laguna Holiday Club 5 * hotel does not allow him to maintain "standards". Room classification starts with superior. What is typical for club hotels, in every, even the simplest guest room, there is a small kitchenette with the necessary utensils and utensils. But, unlike ordinary apartments, guests are not forced to clean the rooms themselves. The maids will sweep the floor, change the bed, change towels (or bedding), and at the same time wash the dishes left in the sink.

From the rooms there are also studios and suites. The latter, in turn, are divided into categories - a junior with one or two bedrooms. All rooms have a dedicated seating area. Spacious balconies with rattan furniture adjoin the rooms on the upper floors. And the guests of the basement have the opportunity to sunbathe on the sunbeds directly on the grass of the terrace at the door of their room.





Guest room equipment and service

Previously, the hotel was called Laguna Holiday Club 4 * (Thailand, Phuket Island ). But when the last reconstruction took place, the status of the complex increased. The rooms have brand new furniture.

In the kitchen, in addition to the stove, there is a microwave, three-dimensional refrigerator, coffee maker, electric kettle. To all this, the maids every day put bags of drinks, bottled drinking water, stylish bottles with hygiene items.

The master bedroom has a separate living and dining area, a safety deposit box, air conditioning, a satellite LCD TV and a DVD player. Reviews mention orthopedic mattresses and a large selection of pillows. Many travelers liked the size of the king-size beds.

Bathrooms with shower and bath have a hairdryer. As for the room service, the reviews mention fast Wi-Fi, round-the-clock service and fresh press delivery.

Catering for tourists

Hotel Laguna Holiday Club 5 * you can order breakfast. But many tourists were satisfied with the availability of cuisine. Reviews say that not far from the hotel complex is a large supermarket "Tesco Lotus", where you can stock up on groceries. There is also a minibar in the room, however its filling is paid.





There is a restaurant on site serving Thai and European dishes. From the edge of the pool from morning to sunset there is a bar. But the most budget option of food (after self-preparation of dishes) are cafes outside the walls of the hotel complex. Tourists urge not to be afraid to eat there. All products are fresh, and the dishes are prepared just fine.

Infrastructure and services in Laguna Holiday Club 5 * (Phuket)

A huge territory of four hundred hectares, in fact a small town, is the main feature of this hotel. Tourists liked that you can use the entire infrastructure of the complex, swim in the pools of more expensive hotels, visit their restaurants, spa centers and other places of entertainment.

You can cross the lagoons by ferry. Reviews say that a free shuttle service is available to all the restaurants in the huge complex.

In a small hotel town you can spend the whole vacation, there is no need to go beyond it. On the territory, on the artificial island of the lagoon, there are a number of shops and boutiques - "Channel Village". You can find everything in them: from food to souvenirs and clothes.

The hotel staff is very friendly. Everyone is trying to please and surround the guests with genuine concern.

Conditions for children

Hotel Laguna Holiday Club 5 * (Thailand, Phuket) does not position itself as a place of family vacation. But this does not mean that you can not come here with children, as in some resorts in Cuba or the Dominican Republic. If the child will tolerate a long flight well, then it will be a real pleasure for him at the Hotel Laguna Holiday Club Resort. The hotel does not have full-fledged adult animation, but a mini club functions for younger guests. The restaurants of the complex have high chairs for feeding babies. You can also find hotels with children's pools. Upon your request, a small cot is available in the room. Tourists say that having a kitchen and a grocery store at hand greatly simplified the issue of feeding babies. The hotel has a playground and a games room.





Entertainment and sports

At the Laguna Holiday Club 5 * hotel you can rent bicycles for free to sweep through the vast territory of the complex. What a vacation in Thailand without a quality massage? And, of course, there is one in the hotel. Reviews say that it is very diverse: with aromatic oils, and medicinal (for medical reasons), and faces, and for feet, with fish.

At the Laguna Phuket complex you can play golf and ride a horse. For those who monitor their tonus, a fitness room with free exercise machines is open.

The hotel has a tour desk where you can book fascinating trips around the island. There are also parties in the evenings and entertainment programs.

The administration of the hotel provided gifts and compliments for the newlyweds and birthday people.

Excursions and travels in the surroundings

Despite the fact that the Laguna hotel complex looks like a small town, reviews strongly recommend sometimes leaving the Laguna Holiday Club 5 *. Phuket is too interesting an island to devote the entire vacation on it to the beach. Excursions can be ordered both at the hotel and in street travel agencies (which is cheaper). The most popular are day trips. It can be diving, freshwater and sea fishing. Tourists praised excursions to the Similan Islands, James Bond Rock, Khao Lak, Khao Sok safari and Lake Cheolan. But if you want to see the islands of Phi Phi, then tourists are advised to look for a two-day excursion. But the cheapest thing is to see the sights on your own, especially if you know how to drive a motobike.
