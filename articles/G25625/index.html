You just need to choose the right option.

Method 1. ATM



The easiest way to recharge your mobile account is to use the nearest ATM. Moreover, most banks have a very extensive network of branches. In addition, a large bank will always try to install an ATM near its place of work for its payroll clients. So far often do not need to go.

Once an ATM has been found to transfer money to a phone via a card, you just need to find the point of payment for cellular services in the menu and correctly enter your number and amount. It is important not to make a mistake here, so as not to accidentally replenish someone else's account with an impressive amount. Money will be credited to your account in minutes. Perhaps the only drawback is that you still need to go out. It is unlikely to be convenient on a rainy day or night.

Method 2. Internet banking



Those who have thought in advance about how to transfer money from a card to a phone usually connect such a convenient service as Internet banking. In different banks, it has a different name, but it has one essence. Having access to the Internet, you can manage your card account: view the current balance, pay for services and block your card. Of course, it can also be used to pay for a mobile phone. It‚Äôs enough to have access to the Internet.

Next, you just need to go to your personal account, choose the payment for mobile services, enter the number and amount. After confirming the payment, the money will be credited to the account. Usually the whole procedure takes no more than 5 minutes. In addition, it is not necessary to have a credit card and even a mobile phone on hand. In fact, anyone with access to Internet banking can transfer money to a phone from a card.

Method 3. Mobile Bank



But there is not always access to the Internet, and the funds on the phone are already over. For those who have activated the Mobile Bank service, there is another way how to transfer money from a card to a phone - just send an SMS. In it, you need to specify the amount and phone number separated by a space and send a message to the service number of the service. If everything is done correctly, then the money will go to the account almost immediately. In addition, this way you can replenish the balance of any mobile phone, wherever the recipient and sender are.

Method 4. Auto payment

And the easiest way to transfer money from a card to a phone is not to do it at all by connecting the Auto Payment service once in a mobile phone salon. The system itself will debit the required amount from the bank card every time the mobile phone account approaches zero. Moreover, the client can decide when and how much to write off. Thus, you can forever stop worrying about the balance of your cell.
