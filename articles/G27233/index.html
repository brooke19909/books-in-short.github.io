Blue Lakes (Rostov-on-Don): for a vacation with the whole family

Blue Lake in Rostov is one of the most favorite vacation spots for residents of the city and the entire region. Due to various features, such as clear water, convenient location, the presence of a restaurant near the shore, the abundance of fish fauna and other pleasures of outdoor leisure, Lake Goluboye is one of the most visited places in the region.



The location of the lake, features and location map

There is a reservoir just two kilometers from Rostov-on-Don, which allows you to get not only by car, but also by any other form of transport, whether it be a bicycle or a fixed-route taxi. The location map is quite simple - you need to leave the city through the west bridge, keep to the left-bank zone and continue straight along the bridge, where you can soon see the sign.

Blue lakes (Rostov-on-Don) are very clean water, but even in extreme heat it is cool. Numerous springs, which are deep at the bottom, do not allow her to warm up. They are the main source of replenishment of the reservoir.

There are several equipped beaches on Lake Goluboy. In order to spend time there, you will need to pay an entrance fee. The fee is purely symbolic. A large sandy beach, sun loungers and umbrellas, a barbecue area, gazebos, toilets and more are offered for it. The cost of parking starts at just 100 rubles. For those who do not want to splurge, there are free beaches.



Restaurant on the waterfront

Vacationers on the shore of the pond can not only sunbathe and swim, but also eat deliciously. All tourists are happy to receive the "Blue Lake" (restaurant). Thanks to him, Rostov remains in the memory of city guests for a long time. It is located on the left bank of the reservoir.

The restaurant "Blue Lake" is a complex of buildings, which include banquet rooms, a large summer terrace. There is also a hotel that meets all the technical and modern standards of comfortable living. The restaurant will delight you with a large and prepared summer area, where, in addition to a varied cuisine, there are specially equipped places for large companies. The first floor of the summer playground is designed for 150 guests, and the second floor can accommodate about 400 visitors. The advantage of this room is its open view of the Blue Lakes (Rostov-on-Don), which brings a romantic mood and peace of mind. There is also a special offer, the essence of which is to rent the entire complex. Then it will be possible to invite more than a thousand people to the party.



The restaurant menu is quite diverse. Guests can be offered dishes of Russian, Caucasian and European cuisine. Brandy kebabs and kebabs will delight visitors.

Due to the fact that regular checks are carried out from the sanitary-epidemiological surveillance (at least once a year), the Blue Lakes (Rostov-on-Don) are ideal for recreation for both adults and children.

Offers for hunters and fishermen

On the Blue Lake, many vacationers swim and sunbathe, lying in the sun. Also, many people come here to go fishing. Mostly perch is caught, but other species of freshwater fauna are also found.

Blue Lakes (Rostov-on-Don) are ideal for spearfishing.
