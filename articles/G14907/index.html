Car registrar Lexand LR 3500

Car registrars are increasingly becoming an element of necessity for a modern car enthusiast, rather than a luxury. With their help, you can prove your case by talking with traffic inspectors, keep a record of your route and even use it as a device to record the situation during the absence of the driver.



One of the leading manufacturers of car recorders is the same company that manufactures navigators - Lexand. At the same time, the registrars of this company occupy a leading position in the global market in this industry. They are reliable, practical and in most cases have the functions of a GPS receiver.

A prominent representative of the company's products is the registrar Lexand LR 3500. Although it is not equipped with an integrated GPS-receiver, like most models, it has no less important qualities, one of which is its compactness and ease of use.



This recorder is equipped with a powerful 5 Mpx camera and can record files in Full HD format. At the same time, the recorder‚Äôs camera has a viewing angle of one hundred and twenty degrees, which allows you to capture even the curb on both sides of the road. This helps to get a more detailed video of the situation on the road, and the image is practically not distorted.

It is worth noting that the Lexand LR 3500 has the ability to record sound, because a pretty good microphone is built into it, and in the dark, you can apply infrared illumination of eight LEDs. However, it is worth noting that such a backlight is not professional, and the ideal image quality in the dark can only be obtained when parking.



Lexand LR 3500 recorders can use micro SDXC, micro SD and micro SDHC media for recording, which have a capacity of up to 32 GB. At the same time, you can use the automatic shutdown mode or allowing continuous recording, which is quite convenient when filling a memory card. When using this mode, recording will be based on already captured material.

A serious advantage of this recorder is that it can be used as a web camera, and it is equipped with an HDMI port, which makes it possible to connect it to monitors and TVs. Also a good advantage of the Lexand LR 3500 is its modern appearance and fairly compact size. They allow you to take the recorder home when the car is parked. At the same time, the device has a screen with a two-inch diagonal and high resolution, which allows you to see even the smallest details.

Considering the car registrars of this manufacturer, it is worth mentioning Lexand navigators, reviews of which have created the company's reputation as a manufacturer of reliable and high-quality equipment. At the same time, the Lexand LR 3500 car recorder also has a lot of positive reviews about the shooting quality and compact size.

Also noted is its low price compared to other models of this class and speed. In fact, this car recorder is a worthy representative of Lexand, having all the necessary functions and impeccable quality.
