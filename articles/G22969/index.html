Crete, Mare Monte Beach Hotel 4 * - photos, prices and reviews of tourists

Crete is the largest and, perhaps, the most interesting from the point of view of tourism of the islands of Greece. This is evidenced by the millions of flows of tourists visiting Crete daily. The attractiveness of the island is due to the ability to combine beach tours with sightseeing. Holidays on the island of Minotaur will appeal to both fans of lazy pastime and inquisitive tourists who travel from place to place in order to see more attractions. There are so many of them in Crete that it is unlikely to be able to inspect everything at once in one visit. As for the level of service in the hotels of the island, then this is also okay here. The most popular among Russian tourists are 4 * hotels in which you can get the highest service at relatively low prices. So, for example, a week-long rest at the Mare Monte Beach Hotel 4 * (Crete) costs from 25,000 rubles per person. The hotel is located near the city of Chania - the second city after the Heraklion island.



Chania - the pearl of Crete

Due to the abundance of canals and rivers flowing through the city, Chania is called the Greek Venice. In spring, the city is buried in the bushes of flowering jasmine, and its aroma is interrupted by the brackish smell of the sea. The city is located in the western part of the island. It is not as dry as in the eastern and southern parts, so you can see densely growing forests and gardens, flowering shrubs and a lush carpet of greenery around the hotel complexes. The most popular resorts in the Chania area are the villages of Platanias, Kavros and Georgioupolis. In the latter, among the greenery of gardens, is the snow-white building of the Mare Monte Beach Hotel 4 * hotel. But before proceeding to the description of this fashionable complex, which, according to Russian tourists, is one of the best in all of Chania, I would like to stop at the village of Georgioupolis and briefly describe its attractions.

Garden City

In fact, this resort is very green, just an eye happy. Numerous restaurants, cafes, taverns and bars are buried in flowering bushes and eucalyptus and cypress trees. The hotels here are small, mostly painted in white or very light pastel colors, which is in perfect harmony with the natural picture. The town has a small port, on both sides of which stretch gorgeous golden beaches. In a word, in this town, next to which is the Mare Monte Beach Hotel 4 *, miraculously combines white, turquoise, emerald and gold shades, forming an incredibly beautiful landscape. Georgioupolis beach is divided into several parts by small rivers, Almiros and Perastikos. At the place of their confluence, the water in the sea is cold; there are strong currents. By the way, in earlier times, this village was called Almiroupoli, in honor of the Almyros River flowing along it.

The holiday season in these places lasts from April to October. It is especially beautiful here, of course, at the beginning of the season, when the city is in full bloom. Summer is sometimes very hot, and the thermometer reaches 40 degrees or more, but the sea water is not very warm due to the currents, which helps to cool on especially hot days.

Mare Monte Beach Hotel 4 (Greece, Crete, Chania): description and location

900 meters from the picturesque village of Georgioupolis and 35 km from Chania, on the northern coast of Crete, is the Marie Monte Hotel. It occupies a fairly large territory on which among palm gardens there are several buildings, an amphitheater, a swimming pool, a couple of restaurants and bars, taverns and cafes, tennis, volleyball and basketball fields, etc. As you can see, the hotel infrastructure is more than developed, and rest within its walls promises to be interesting and eventful.

Room Amenities

The interior and interior of all living quarters are almost the same everywhere. The floors are covered with ceramic tiles, there is a balcony or terrace with plastic furniture, a well-equipped bathroom with a shower, toiletries, a hairdryer and towels. The room also has air conditioning, a safe (14 euros per week), a TV and satellite channels, a refrigerator, a telephone, etc. At the request of guests for children, either one folding bed or a bunk bed (for two children) is installed in the room. As for the service, they clean the room daily, at a convenient time for you, and bed linen is changed every three days, that is, twice a week.

Hotel service and infrastructure

The hotel has an Internet corner, where for a fee you can use the wireless Internet, as well as business services (copier, printer, scanner, email, etc.). In the lobby there is a TV room, a mini market and a souvenir kiosk. Parking for tourists renting cars is free. Dry cleaning and laundry services, as well as a medical center are chargeable. On the territory of the hotel, in the park area, there are several swimming pools (adults and children), mini-golf courses, tennis courts, billiards, restaurants, taverns, bars and an amphitheater for conducting show programs. Unfortunately, there is no spa and gym.



Entertainment

Hotel Mare Monte Beach Hotel 4 * is located 5 kilometers from the picturesque Lake Kourna, and a walking tram regularly goes to it from the hotel. Many tourists like to spend their time not by the pools, but next to it. For those vacationers who like to sunbathe in the hotel, there is a solarium area near the pools. Sun beds and umbrellas are provided free of charge. In the evenings, animation is performed in the amphitheater for both adults and small tourists. There are Greek evenings a week with the performance of folklore ensembles. By the way, this, from the point of view of tourists, is the most important feature of the Mare Monte Beach Hotel 4 *. Reviews written by former guests express gratitude to the administration for organizing such evenings. After all, Greek music, especially dancing, is something special!

Children's entertainment

In Greece, children have a special attitude, and this is felt in everything. For example, in the restaurant for babies there is a special children's menu (for babies - on request and for a fee), special high chairs are provided. In the central building for them there is a children's playroom, a mini club, in the territory there are several playgrounds with swings and slides, as well as a paddling pool for children.
