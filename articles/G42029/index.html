Seher Sun Palace Spa 5 * (Side, Turkey): hotel description and reviews of tourists

Turkey is one of the most popular tourist destinations today, according to operators and agents, which offers an unthinkable variety of accommodation options in hotel complexes - from economical options in two or three stars to luxury hotels of a premium class.

Hotel Seher Sun Palace Spa 5 is a quality type of relaxation for relatively little money. In addition, it is included in the list of the best hotels of the Turkish Antalya coast, compiled according to the results of the 2014 season. It is also part of the famous Sehers Hotels chain, which is why you need not worry about the upcoming quality of service.

Location of the recreation complex

The hotel complex is conveniently located near the resort town of Side (about 6 kilometers), which is considered an elite holiday destination due to excellent hotels and long sandy beaches, the beauty of which is accompanied by a magnificent natural landscape. Side takes the second place (after Belek) in the list of resorts that are best suited for spending holidays with children - this is explained by clean beach stripes and a gentle entrance to the sea.

Beach

Seher Sun Palace Spa has its own sandy beach with free parasols and sun loungers with comfortable mattresses. The only negative is its certain distance from the coast - 200 meters, that is, on the second coastline. Number of rooms

Seher Sun Palace Spa 5 is not famous for its large selection of rooms. However, sometimes this moment is a decisive positive fact when choosing a hotel, since not everyone loves too large and full complexes for recreation.

The hotel accommodates customers in 270 rooms - standard, family and specialized for people with disabilities.

  • Standart room. An ordinary one-room apartment (total area 22 square meters) with a private bathroom, fully furnished and very bright. Guests are provided with two soft beds or one king-size bed to choose from, equipped workplace, coffee table with armchairs, sometimes in the rooms you can find a tea or coffee set, bedside tables and satellite TV, a replenished minibar and a safe.
    The bathroom comes with a full range of cosmetic accessories, a hairdryer, bathrobe and terry slippers. Travelers who have visited this complex more than once say that the standard room can comfortably accommodate not two, but four guests - including an extra bed - a sofa.

Infrastructure

The Seher Sun Palace Spa 5 hotel complex has a well-developed entertainment infrastructure - there are entertainment options for children, for calm people, and for groovy youth.

  • Large tennis court with a professional trainer (extra charge).
  • Small tennis and Russian or American billiards for evening relaxation.
  • Sports games - aerobics on the playground and in the pool.
  • 4 huge swimming pools for outdoor swimming and several colorful water slides. The reviews of tourists noted the special purity of water in the pool, which is important when resting with children.
  • Darts for the very apt.
  • Evening and afternoon animation programs and a disco.
  • Bright gym with a variety of modern equipment.
  • Real beach volleyball.
  • The spa and beauty salon offer a variety of massages, including Indian and Thai, Shiatsu and Stone. Specialists are engaged in a variety of cosmetic procedures and can help make an amazing hairstyle for an evening entertainment event.
  • You can bask in the local sauna or bathhouse. For an extra fee, guests are given brooms, hats and large terry towels.
  • There are also entertainments for the younger guests of the Seher Sun Palace Spa 5 complex - a children's playroom with a designer, coloring books and puzzles - professional educators and teachers work here with the guys. There is a special children's disco and a mini-club by age categories, as well as a small shallow pool with safe slides.
