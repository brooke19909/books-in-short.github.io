Then he transferred his production to Abrau-Durso.

The production of champagne wines over the years only expanded here, interrupted only during the war years. The head of the enterprise in 1919 was Frolov-Bagreev, a scientist and winemaker who laid the foundation for the reservoir method of making this drink.
