The original composition of artificial flowers as a bright element of decor

Made from high quality materials, artificial flowers, herbs and

plants are made with such natural precision that it is sometimes difficult to distinguish them from a living original. Therefore, we are increasingly using such decorations to decorate the interior, without fear of accusations of bad taste and kitsch. The composition of artificial flowers is not only very beautiful, but also convenient, since it does not require care and special attention - it is enough to periodically sweep dust from the flowers. The advantages of this type of decor are also that a bored bouquet is easy to remake - you can add new colors, change the arrangement of elements, and it will again delight the eye.

The composition of artificial flowers is collected according to the same rules as from living. A decorative vessel is required for its placement, as well as a sponge or a coil of wire to secure the stems. In addition to traditional bouquets, other decorative compositions that decorate lampshades, walls, picture frames and curtains for curtains are made from these plants . In a word, composing compositions of artificial flowers is a great way to manifest creative imagination, because they do not need special conditions of detention, light and water.



Consider some interesting options for such a decor. The composition of artificial flowers will be a wonderful decoration, for example, a kitchen wall. To do this, use small stems with small flower heads, as well as imitation of ears of corn and various field herbs. The wall is pre-glued with wallpaper or painted in a light tone. Then, with the help of small cloves, staples or glue, the selected material is attached to it in longitudinal rows. It is important that the plants look harmonious and the wall is not overloaded with decor. Such a composition of artificial flowers will revive the already boring wallpaper.

Another variant. Using bright artificial elements, we will decorate a wall panel or a picture. To do this, we will collect small stems of flowers in a long garland or wreath. Connect the stems together with a thin wire, which we hide among the buds and leaves. The resulting floral decor, gluing it around the edge, frame a picture or an old reproduction. Then we insert all this splendor into a three-dimensional frame under the glass. For this work, it is better to choose plants that are not bright, but slightly muted, corresponding to the color scheme of the picture or panel. This is done with a specific goal - to create the impression of slight aging.



Another very interesting way of decor is when the wall compositions of artificial flowers are large panels imitating a lawn or forest clearing, located vertically on the wall. Making something like this is pretty simple. On a frame from a baguette we stretch a fine grid or a piece of rough matter. We select artificial plants, green twigs, exotic flowers - bright and unusual - to create a spectacular composition. We distribute them on the canvas, completely covering its surface. Among small inflorescences and buds, we insert large specimens, we supplement all this with greens, herbs and stems. Then, using glue, glue all the elements of the panel to the base sequentially. Do not want to use glue? Then we attach each element with a harsh thread to the canvas. It remains to hang the finished masterpiece on the wall.

Artificial flowers - an interesting and creative material. With their help it is very simple to decorate any room.
