Where to fly to rest in May so that there are no disappointments from the trip? Consider the most popular resorts, the weather in which on the eve of the summer will not let the traveler down.

Tel Aviv

Where to fly to rest in May? One of the most attractive destinations in the last spring month is Israel. In May, two resorts are especially popular in this country. This is Tel Aviv, as well as Eilat. These options should be considered by tourists who are trying to find the answer to the question of where to fly to relax in May with a child.

In Tel Aviv, despite the fact that summer has not yet arrived, the air temperature in the daytime reaches +30 degrees. The sea at this time warms up to +26. Judging by the reviews of tourists, the weather in Tel Aviv in May is always sunny, and it is perfect for sunbathing and swimming.

The beaches at this resort are clean and fully equipped. Moreover, they are quite safe for bathing little tourists.

The coastal zone has a developed infrastructure. Restaurants and cafes are located on the territory of the beaches, and all the equipment necessary for relaxation is offered for rent.

In the event that travelers are planning an independent trip, they should book a hotel in advance, choosing it based on the reviews of those tourists who stayed in it. The city has about fifty hotels that offer their services at prices ranging from the most affordable to fabulously high. A whole chain of Tel Aviv hotels stretches along the coast in two lines. The first of them is closer to the sea, and the second is at a distance from it. Nevertheless, the path to the beach for guests of any hotel will take no more than 5-7 minutes.

Most Tel Aviv hotels are located on Ha-Yarkon Street. This street is located parallel to the coastline with beaches. For those traveling to rest with children, experienced travelers are advised to select hotels for family vacations. The rooms in them are equipped with a small kitchen and have other pleasant attributes of comfort.

Eilat

Where to fly to rest in May at sea? An excellent destination for relaxation will be the Israeli resort of Eilat. It is located on the shores of the bay of the same name and is considered the driest region of the country. Compared to Tel Aviv, the weather here is slightly hotter. So, the air in Eilat in May warms up to + 32 degrees, and the water in the sea to + 28. At the end of the month, the thermometer rises even higher. That is why this direction should be considered by someone who has not yet decided where to fly to relax in early May. Judging by the reviews of travelers, in Eilat during this period all days are clear and sunny. They are great for swimming and sunbathing.

The resort offers a very interesting and varied excursion program to its guests. It is also possible to order the services of a guide from the hotel or an independent tour of local attractions, including ancient monasteries and tombs, grottoes, gardens and natural landscapes. The resort offers tourists and many other attractions. These are water parks, museums and art galleries.

Eilat is very popular among vacationers. That is why on its territory there are many hotels inviting guests of the resort. Those hotels that correspond to the level of 4 and 5 stars are real examples of the highest world service. The fact is that, as a rule, citizens of highly developed countries, who place high demands on leisure, tend to visit Eilat. There are hotels at the resort level of 3 stars and below, offering their services at affordable prices.

Aqaba

Where to fly to rest in May? One of the popular destinations for Russian tourists is Jordan. Our compatriots book trips there for the spring season in the winter, most often choosing Aqaba for themselves. In May, the air here warms up to +31 degrees, and water to +27.

The high season in Aqaba begins in the first month of spring. But in the summer at this resort it is hot and stuffy.

Judging by the reviews of tourists, the question of where to fly to relax in April-May can be resolved uniquely in favor of this particular resort. After all, Aqaba in this period is an ideal option for a beach holiday. She invites her guests to fully equipped beaches - both pebble and sand. Most of them are privately owned by hotels. And they are in Aqaba for every budget and taste, providing guests of Jordan with such living conditions that meet international tourist standards.

For travelers who want to truly relax and unwind, resort hotels offer spa and swimming pools, restaurants and shops. To accommodate guests here are luxurious rooms, apartments and villas.

Experienced tourists recommend choosing a hotel for relaxation, pay attention to its location relative to the airport, city center and its attractions, as well as from popular shopping places. Those who are planning a vacation with children need to choose a hotel for themselves that meets all the requirements of parents with kids.

From entertainment in Aqaba, scuba diving is well developed, during which you can admire the beautiful coral reefs located in coastal waters, as well as the fauna and flora located in the sea. In addition, guests of the resort can go on various excursions and visit the attractions.

UAE

Where to relax in May abroad? One of the most exotic destinations is the UAE. This spring month many tourists with children come here. Judging by the reviews of travelers, this country leaves unforgettable impressions not only from the architecture seen here, but also from fascinating excursions and shopping. Children here are offered interesting active entertainment.

Where in May you can relax at sea in the UAE? The most popular resorts of the country are Abu Dhabi, where the air warms up to a temperature of +38, and water to +29 degrees, as well as Dubai. Here the indicators of the thermometer are a degree lower.

In May, the UAE is already in full swing during the beach season. Resorts of the country allow their guests to enjoy the hot sun, clean and warm sea, as well as snow-white beaches on the eve of the summer. For those who prefer an active holiday, in the UAE you can either go scuba diving or go on a yacht to the open sea.

A variety of excursion programs are offered to tourists. There are extreme ones among them, for example, parachuting.

Judging by the reviews of experienced travelers, shopping in the UAE is a real paradise for those who love to shop. In addition, various water parks, spectacular shows and attractions allow you to have a great time at the resorts of this country.

Based on the reviews of tourists, the level of hotels in the UAE is quite high. For their stay, each vacationer will be able to choose one of them that matches his financial capabilities.

For example, in Dubai, the most luxurious hotels are located on the artificial bulk island of Jumeirah. From them to the main shopping centers of the city by taxi or bus about 20-30 minutes. But in the Al-Barsha area, you can choose a hotel for yourself starting from the level of 1 star and ending with 5-star ones. All are close to the Emirates Mall. This is a shopping center, in the complex of which there is a ski slope. Many hotels from 1 to 5 stars are in the old part of Dubai. Tourists living in this area are given the opportunity to visit the Iranian market, many shops, as well as markets that trade in gold and spices.

The most diverse hotels offer their services in Abu Dhabi. There are old hotels in this resort, where the level of service corresponds to 2-3 stars. Built in Abu Dhabi and hotel-palaces.

Maldives

Where to relax in early May during the holidays? Of course, in the Maldives! A trip to these islands will provide an opportunity to spend a real paradise vacation at sea. After all, here are the best beaches in the world.

Judging by the reviews of tourists, diving enthusiasts can enjoy the rich underwater world in the Maldives. In addition, travelers will enjoy beautiful landscapes, clean sea air, as well as extraordinary nature. The beaches on these islands are very diverse. Among them are those that belong to hotels, and you can find secluded coastal areas where tourists enjoy a relaxing holiday.

Those who are still considering where to relax in May should pay special attention to the Maldives. At this time, the air here warms up to +31 degrees, and the water temperature reaches +28.

For those who like to actively spend their time, various excursions are offered, including quad biking, horseback riding along the coast, windsurfing, snorkeling, scuba diving and others. Those who come to the islands can take a stroll through the rainforests or go sightseeing.

Where to stay in the Maldives? Judging by the reviews of tourists, there are no high-rise hotels on these islands. The only exception is the capital of Male. The main part of the hotels is represented by houses, cottages, villas, bungalows and chalets. Despite such a great variety in names, the essence of these buildings is one. All of them are low and free-standing, which allows each vacationer to enter their room directly from the street.

Bungalow houses in the Maldives can be divided into three types. Among them are those that:

  • located deep in the island;
  • located on the shore, and exit from them leads directly to the beach;
  • stand on stilts in the water.

Most often, to the houses that are in the sea, you can walk along the bridge. However, it also happens that tourists get to such a bungalow by boat.

Thailand

Where is it better to fly for a vacation in May inexpensively? Thailand can become a very interesting destination for Russians. On May days, air temperature rises to +33 degrees. Water warms up to +28. In Thailand, a large number of beaches with developed infrastructure. For lovers of outdoor activities, many excursions are offered.

Where to fly with my child in May? Of course to Thailand! After all, here, on the eve of the summer, you can soak up the sun, diversifying your vacation by visiting attractions and water parks.

The most popular in Thailand are water excursions. They offer visitors to the country yachting, water skiing and ATVs. With children it is best to go to the islands of Pattaya, Samui and Phuket.

The hotels of the first of these resorts are distinguished by their impeccable service. However, judging by the reviews of experienced tourists, when choosing a hotel in Pattaya, you should not pay attention to its stardom, as it can be very conditional. It is recommended to look at specific photos, study the services provided and evaluate its location. For example, those who come to Thailand for entertainment and nightlife are advised to choose a hotel for themselves near the center. But it should be borne in mind that inexpensive hotels, as a rule, are located above the massage parlors and bars, so that under the windows of a traveler it will be very noisy almost around the clock.

Those who come here with children are advised to stay in more peaceful areas - Naklua and Jomtien. The cheapest option is guest houses. These are mini-hotels of family type for the most unassuming in terms of life guests. The most popular types of accommodation in the country are condominiums. They are a cross between an apartment and a hotel. There are security and reception, as well as a shared pool for all guests.

You can stay in Pattaya in a two-story villa. Such structures are located directly on the beach.

Finding the right accommodation for yourself is easy on Koh Samui. Those who prefer a vibrant nightlife should consider options for hotels located near the beaches of Lamai and Chaweng. Fans of a relaxing holiday will prefer the southern part of the island or the Maenam area.

For those who went to Phuket and do not want to spend money on housing, experienced tourists recommend going to the crowded Patong. Connoisseurs of clean beaches and silence will pick up a hotel in Karon Beach, but scuba diving lovers should go to Nai Yang, where you can admire the magnificent coral reefs.

They have many bars and cafes where you can taste a variety of dishes, and in the evenings visit discos. This resort offers many interesting excursions with a tour of objects of distant antiquity, as well as visits to natural attractions. Anyone who wants to live near the beach, can find in Athens either a luxury hotel or a villa, as well as stay on the option of staying at home.

The island of Rhodes is perfect for those who have not yet decided where to fly to rest in mid-May with a child. Judging by the reviews of tourists, the island of Rhodes has first-class beaches, stunning natural landscapes and archaeological sites. Lovers of active life await discos and parties at the resort, restaurants, clubs and bars. You can stay on the island of Rhodes by choosing one of the most diverse hotels.
