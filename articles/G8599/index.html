Dexp Ixion e145 overview

Dexp can hardly be called a well-known company, but one way or another, many have come across it. Especially often, the products of this company can be seen in various domestic online stores, as well as in ordinary equipment hypermarkets.





In fact, Dexp is a Russian company that was founded in 1998 in the city of Vladivostok. The company specializes in the production of smartphones, tablets, desktops and laptops. In today's short review, I would like to consider one of the devices of the named manufacturer - the Dexp Ixion e145 smartphone, and at the same time find out whether our devices can compete with foreign analogues or not. Let's get started!

Description

The Dexp Ixion e145 smartphone is not a new product in 2017, but a model that was released in 2015, and in two versions - regular and EVO SE. Honestly, the difference between the variations is minimal, and if you do not look closely, then it is not at all noticeable.





What at the time of release, that now the device is extremely cheap, but more on that later. You can buy it only through the official website of the manufacturer, since in other e145 stores you will not be able to find it.

The smartphone does not have any special distinguishing features - this is the most ordinary ultra-budget device designed for the most ordinary undemanding people.





Appearance

The appearance of the Dexp Ixion e145 is pretty good. It is sold in those colors - orange, black and blue (EVO SE modification). By the way, there are no differences between the regular version and the "EVO", up to the size, weight and other parameters.





The material from which the phone is assembled is the most common cheap plastic. Build quality is above average. Parts of the body fit tightly, but in some places there are creaks. They are especially well heard if the phone is tightly and firmly squeezed in your hand.

The back cover is also not credible. She sits in her place well, but there are punching, so you have to put something under her.





The disadvantage is the coating of the plastic case itself. It is glossy, which makes fingerprints very visible on the surface. In addition, if the hand is slightly wet, then the phone tries to slip out of it thanks to the rounded corners and all the same glossy finish.



On the front side of the smartphone, there is a screen with a diagonal of 4.5 inches, touch buttons below it, as well as an earpiece on top, along with a front camera and light and proximity sensors.

On the right side is the familiar power / lock keys and volume rocker. The left side is empty, there is nothing on it.





At the top there is a micro USB port for and a 3.5 mm jack for headphones. By the way, both connectors are shifted to the left edge and are very close to each other. At the bottom there is nothing but a small microphone hole.





Well, and finally, the main camera, an LED flash and a small slot for an external speaker are located at the back of the phone. The camera eye bulges just a little above the surface, so the external speaker will not completely overlap, however, if the phone still lies on the back, there is a chance to miss a call.
