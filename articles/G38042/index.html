Hotels in Astrakhan: rating, addresses, reviews

Astrakhan is an incredibly interesting city in terms of tourism. As for the business sector, from this point of view, this place is also attractive. Astrakhan hotels will provide you with a vivid experience and a high level of comfort, regardless of the purpose for which you arrived here.

Hotel "7 Sky"

This institution is located in the very center of Astrakhan, and therefore it is a good option for accommodating both tourists and business people. The hotel is located at Krasnaya Naberezhnaya, 27.

Hotel "7 Sky" offers guests to stay in one of 44 comfortable rooms with a cozy modern atmosphere. Particular attention should be paid to honeymoon rooms, which are decorated in an original romantic style.

Separately, it is worth noting the luxurious restaurant of the hotel, where you will be offered a huge number of dishes of Russian and international cuisines. Here you can offer services for the organization of banquets and celebrations.

Based on reviews from tourists who have repeatedly visited Astrakhan hotels, this establishment was rated 7.9 on a 10-point scale. Speaking about the positive aspects, it is worth noting the high professionalism and responsiveness of employees, convenient location, excellent breakfast, as well as perfect cleanliness in the rooms. As for the shortcomings noted by the guests, these are uncomfortable beds and a noisy construction site, which is being conducted next to the hotel building.





Hotel "Orion"

The hotel has two buildings, one of which is located at Zelenaya street, 47, and the second - Belgorodskaya street, 15b. Both buildings are located in the central part of the city.

Hotel "Orion", in addition to comfortable rooms, has some more advantages. Here you can get a free transfer from the airport or train station. Also, you will be available to use wireless Internet and car parking. Quiet and calm atmosphere contributes to a good rest and productive work.

If we talk about the rating of the hotel, then its rating is 7.7 points out of 10 possible. It is worth noting that the price and quality of services are absolutely comparable. The staff is very friendly and smiling, and the rooms are all quite comfortable and clean. In addition, the buildings are very conveniently located relative to urban infrastructure.

Speaking about the negative aspects, it is worth noting very poor breakfasts (and sometimes they are not served at all). In addition, the lobby and rooms have a pungent smell of cigarettes. Also worth noting is the insufficient number of towels in the bathroom.





Hotel Pallada

Hotel "Pallada" is located at - Lyakhova street, 8a. This area is located within walking distance of the train station, and the journey to the airport takes about half an hour. In addition, from here you can quickly and easily get to the historic center.

The Pallada Hotel has provided in its rooms all the amenities that a tourist needs for a full stay. This is air conditioning, a cable TV, a small refrigerator, a set of dishes, and an electric kettle. The rooms, although small, are incredibly comfortable.

The user rating of the hotel is quite satisfactory - 7.7 points. The positive aspects of this institution can be considered a favorable location, a good layout and good equipment rooms, as well as the professionalism and friendliness of the staff. If we talk about the negative aspects, it is worth noting the terrible audibility between the rooms, old beds and leaking plumbing. In addition, the bathroom does not even have a minimal cosmetic set.

Bonotel

Fans of luxury and comfort often choose a Bonotel. Astrakhan, Lenin Street, 2 is the address of this institution. It is worth noting the unique architecture of the building, which will immerse you in the atmosphere of the old city. Previously, it was the mansion of noble merchants, which is now one of the points of excursion programs.

From the windows of the rooms of the Hotel Bonotel (Astrakhan) you will have an incredibly beautiful view of the city Kremlin. In addition, it is worth noting the abundance of modern amenities that will provide comfort and homeliness.

Like other similar hotels in Astrakhan, this institution was rated by guests rather high - 8.9 out of 10. Guests especially like the interior and architecture of the hotel, as well as its favorable location. At the same time, the rooms are not thoroughly cleaned enough. And breakfast at a hotel of such a high level could be more satisfying and varied.





Hotel "Admiral"

Hotel Admiral is a small guest house that provides its guests with the opportunity for budget accommodation. Choosing a name, the owners of the institution relied on his address. The hotel is located on Admiral Nakhimov Street, at 20b. Here guests are invited to stay in small cozy rooms that are equipped with the minimum necessary set of amenities. It has comfortable furniture, modern appliances, as well as a shower.

The hotel has free parking, where each guest can leave his car. Also, guests can use the wireless Internet. There is a shop right in the cottage with groceries and daily necessities. If you do not want to spend money on food in cafes and restaurants, you can use the shared kitchen, where there is all the necessary equipment and utensils for the full preparation of dishes.

With regard to positive reviews, guests note affordable prices that are fully consistent with the quality of services. Also, guests enjoy a quiet and relaxed atmosphere. Nevertheless, the Admiral Hotel is also characterized by a number of negative aspects, the main of which is poor housekeeping and a small room area. Another disadvantage is the lack of terminals for paying with plastic cards.

Hotel "Park Inn"

If you like everything in the hotel to be perfect, then you need the Park Inn hotel. The institution is located at 29 Henri Barbus Street, in the very center of the city. This will provide you with quick access to major transport hubs, as well as cultural attractions and infrastructure facilities.

The huge hotel has 132 comfortable rooms, each of which provides a full range of orthopedic furniture and modern household appliances, which you may need to ensure a good rest. In addition, the windows of the apartments offer incredibly beautiful views of the city or river.

The Park Inn Hotel pays special attention to the catering of guests. In the restaurant you can taste both the most exquisite and traditional home-made dishes. And in the lobby bar, guests are available around the clock a huge selection of desserts, as well as varieties of tea and coffee.

This hotel has a fairly high rating of 8.3. It is due to the large number of positive reviews, which especially mark the successful location and incredibly comfortable atmosphere in the rooms. The hotel is just perfect cleanliness, and friendly and friendly workers are always ready to come to your aid.

Speaking about the shortcomings of this institution, it is worth noting that room service does not work at night, and therefore, if you want a snack, you can not order food delivery. In addition, due to the proximity to the train station, the rooms are quite noisy from the sound of trains. And, of course, the prices for accommodation are a bit overpriced.





Hotel "Azimut"

Very popular is such an institution as "Azimut" (hotel, Astrakhan). You can find it at Kremlevskaya Street, 4. This hotel is located on the very banks of the Volga, which guarantees you a picturesque landscape from the window. In addition, it is very close to the airport - no more than a quarter of an hour's journey.

All rooms are characterized by a restrained laconic interior, as well as a large number of amenities, including air conditioning, satellite TV, refrigerator, shower and much more. In addition, here you can stay with your pets (the conditions for the provision of the service and its cost should be discussed with the administration in advance at the time of booking).

"Azimut" (hotel, Astrakhan) was awarded by tourists a rating of 7.8 (the highest possible score is 10). Guests note that the quality of the services received is fully consistent with the price. In addition, due to its convenient location, you will not only get quick access to infrastructure facilities, but also incredibly beautiful views of the river from the windows. Here you will also find delicious home cooking and a quiet atmosphere.

Among the shortcomings of the hotel, it is worth noting the wear and some deterioration (it does not hurt to make redecorating). In addition, if the windows of your room overlook the courtyard, then instead of a beautiful landscape, you will receive garbage bins. The bathroom also needs repair and redevelopment.





Hotel "Surprise"

"Surprise" (Astrakhan) is a new comfortable hotel. It is located on Kosmonavtov street, 1 - this is one of the most prestigious areas of the city, where a huge number of cafes, shops, as well as entertainment venues are concentrated. From here it is very convenient to get to both the train station and the airport.

Hotel rooms deserve special attention. They are characterized by an elegant interior, as well as a huge number of amenities. So, here there is a jacuzzi, orthopedic furniture, as well as air conditioning, TV and individual air conditioning. Room service is available 24 hours a day.

The hotel is often chosen for special occasions, as there is a large banquet hall. Other facilities include a tennis court and car parking. Business people will appreciate the presence of a business center.

High hotel ratings are associated with a huge number of positive reviews, which note reasonable prices and good quality service. However, there are some problems with the cleanliness of the rooms.

Hotel "Safari"

Hotel "Safari" (Astrakhan) is a small institution that has only 11 rooms. A feature of the hotel can be considered that each of its apartments are decorated in a certain themed style. here they will find a suitable option for both lovers of simplicity and conciseness, as well as adherents of luxury or romance. In addition to the spectacular design, you will also appreciate the presence of a wide range of amenities.

The hotel is located at 50 Mozdokskaya Street. It is approximately 2 kilometers from the central part of the city. The distance to the airport and train station is 3 and 4 kilometers, respectively.

Despite the fact that the hotel is quite compact, the infrastructure here is pretty good. there are several ATMs in the lobby for withdrawing cash from cards, as well as a grocery store (you can also buy hygiene items here). There is also a billiard room where you can have a good time.

The user rating for Safari Hotel is 8.9. This is a fairly high indicator, which is due to a significant number of positive reviews. Guests note a fresh repair, a cozy design and fairly comfortable orthopedic furniture. In addition, the hotel is very clean and quiet. The staff is attentive and friendly.

As for the negative aspects, it is worth noting the poor soundproofing of the rooms, as well as the remoteness from the city center. In addition, in winter there are serious heating problems.





Verona

Hotel Verona (Astrakhan) is a new luxurious establishment, where respectability and simplicity of home comfort are successfully combined. The institution is located on Botvina street, 47. From here to the central part of the city about 10 minutes drive. It is not far enough, in addition, the area is quite quiet and calm.

The hotel is simple ideal for business people. It provides services for copying and scanning documents, as well as the opportunity to use office equipment. In addition, you will have access to wireless Internet, as well as fresh newspapers and magazines. If you arrived in the city by your own car, then you will be provided with a parking space in the parking lot with 24-hour security.

Speaking of the hotel rating, then based on guest reviews, a rating of 8.2 can be set. So, guests especially like clean and comfortable rooms, which have everything you need for a good rest. In addition, a warm and friendly atmosphere created by qualified staff is crucial. There is also a fully equipped shared kitchen.

The main disadvantage is the remoteness of the hotel from the city center. In addition, there are virtually no cafes and restaurants in the area. Despite the claimed wireless Internet, it works with serious interruptions. It is also quite problematic to reserve a parking space.





Hotel "Astra"

Mini-hotel "Astra" (Astrakhan) offers guests comfortable accommodation in the central part of the city. The address of this institution is 55 May Embankment. From here, the main attraction of the city - the Kremlin, is only 500 meters. In addition, there are still a huge number of tourist and infrastructure facilities, as well as shops, cafes and restaurants.

Here you can be provided with such a service as the organization of sightseeing tours in the city. In addition, hotel staff constantly monitor the poster of cultural events and assist guests in acquiring tickets for them. The price includes a continental breakfast, which includes toast, scrambled eggs and other hearty dishes. The traditional Russian bathhouse is very popular. And if you want to relax in a relaxed atmosphere, then use the park area in the courtyard of the hotel.

The user rating of this hotel is 9.5, which is a fairly high indicator. Guests note the convenient location, as well as the professional work of the staff. Also, one cannot fail to say about quality repairs and new furniture in the rooms.

As for the negative aspects, immediately upon arrival it turns out that no one will help you bring the suitcases to the room. In addition, a very thin stream of water flows in the bathroom. Low-quality instant coffee is also served for breakfast.

Hotel "Emir"

Hotel "Emir" is located in Astrakhan on Bezjonova street, 152. One of the main features of this hotel can be considered as the fact that pets are allowed here. In addition, you will also be able to find necessary services such as a parking area, free Wi-Fi and a restaurant.

The hotel rooms have a cozy design and home comfort. Each apartment has air conditioning, a modern widescreen TV, and a bathroom (a shower or bath is provided, depending on the room category).

The average rating a hotel can be rated based on reviews is 7.9. The undoubted advantage of the hotel are the budget rates for accommodation. In addition, the staff is very friendly and attentive to guests. The hotel boasts not only its own parking, but also a car wash.

Of the shortcomings, it is worth noting the remoteness from the city center. Also, the rooms have poor ventilation, which causes an unpleasant, musty smell. The Internet is working intermittently. Plus, there are problems with the supply of hot water.

Novomoskovskaya

At the intersection of Sovetskaya and Kirov Streets, the Novomoskovskaya Hotel is located. This is a five-star luxury hotel in the city center, which impresses with chic interiors and ancient architecture. Within walking distance there are a huge number of attractions, and therefore this place is just perfect for those who want to get to know the city better.

At this hotel you are invited to stay in luxurious and comfortable rooms. You can also visit the restaurant or order a banquet hall. For business people, services such as conferences and computer equipment are also provided. The lobby bar offers a huge selection of delicious desserts. In addition, you will have access to the spa.

The rating of this hotel is 9.2 points. It is worth noting that the hotel deservedly bears its five-star status. All rooms are very large and beautifully decorated. In addition, the price includes a delicious hearty breakfast. All rooms are maintained in perfect cleanliness, as well as the silence in the hotel.

Among the shortcomings, the absence of an electric kettle in the room can be noted. In addition, in the rooms you can find spiders. If the windows of your room are facing the courtyard, the view will not be very beautiful (construction and garbage cans). , .
