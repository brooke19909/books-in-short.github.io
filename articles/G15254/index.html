Become a Maniac with Manhunt 2



Rockstar has released a new version of the game Manhunt. The storyline of the second part, according to the developers, is fundamentally different from the first game, but Manhunt 2 is not inferior to his older brother in the abundance of scenes of violence and cruelty.

If in a game released in 2003, gamers were asked to become participants in a survival show, where they involuntarily had to hunt their own kind, in Manhunt 2 the player appears in the role of real maniacs, one of which, professor Daniel Lamb, is a voluntary victim of medical experiments. The company to him is a former special forces soldier Leo Casper. The game begins within the walls of a psychiatric clinic, the atmosphere of which is conveyed quite brightly so that it affects the psyche of the player properly. The heroes of the game seize the moment to escape from the "psychiatric hospital" and, having brutally dealt with the medical staff, find themselves on the streets of the city. Maniacs are not very different from other rabble that lives in the backyards, therefore, apparently they do not attract much attention to their persons.
