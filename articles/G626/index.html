Achaia Beach Hotel 4 *, Peloponnese Peninsula: hotel description, reviews

Today, Greece is a very popular travel destination. Many people do not mind spending their holidays here. And, having decided to go to Greece, they choose a resort, after which - a hotel. A certain part of tourists decides to stay in a hotel such as Achaia Beach Hotel 4 *, which is the right choice for many reasons. And they can not be ignored.





Briefly about the hotel

Achaia Beach Hotel 4 * was built in 1973. The hotel is not new, however it has modern rooms. And in general, the complex looks good. The last renovation was in 2004. It is interesting that the hotel complex belongs to the Airotel Group chain. She is widely known. This is a Greek hotel chain with vast experience in the tourist field.

And the complex is located in a city called Patras. It is located in the northwestern part of the Peloponnese.

The main highlight of the hotel is its close proximity to the sea. The building is located on the first coastline, so you can walk to the beach in just a minute.

Araxos Airport is also relatively close. You can reach it in 10-15 minutes by car. The city center is also nearby - a 5-minute drive. And in the immediate vicinity of the hotel there is everything you need - shops, cafes, restaurants and much more. Most importantly, there is a sea. For the sake of him, and most tourists come here.

Fun and relaxation

While in Achaia Beach Hotel 4 *, you can brighten up your leisure time in different ways. Take a swim in the outdoor pool, for example, or sunbathe on the terrace. Guests can still play table tennis, work out in the gym (there are all the necessary equipment for organizing strength training), visit the massage room or sauna. However, many simply decide to sunbathe on the beach and swim in the sea - it is good that the hotel there has its own private area. By the way, you can still rent a bike. Many visitors love to travel around the local area on this vehicle.





Service and maintenance

In Achaia Beach Hotel 4 * there are various amenities. Private parking, for example, free Wi-Fi, storage room for your luggage and things, tour desk, currency exchange. The registration desk is open around the clock, so check-in or check-out can be carried out individually (by prior arrangement).

The hotel provides various services for cleanliness and order. Of course, the apartments are cleaned every day, but also, in addition to this, the hotel has a laundry with dry cleaning services that offer ironing services. They also offer compressing guests' trousers, if they need it.

Inside there is also a conference room, a business center, an office where you can make copies of papers or documents, as well as send / receive a fax. A car rental service and room service are also available.

By the way, about the apartments. Guests can request a soundproofed or non-smoking room. Suites are available for honeymooners and family rooms.
