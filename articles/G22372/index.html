Vela Hotel Icmeler 3 * (Icmeler, Marmaris, Turkey): description and reviews of tourists

Holidays in Turkey are mainly focused on tourists on the Antalya coast. It is worth taking a look at the accommodation option on the Aegean coast. It is less crowded, greener resorts and there is no such sweltering heat in the high season as on the southern coast of Turkey.

Choosing a hotel in Marmaris has its positive aspects. You can stay in an inexpensive three-star hotel, a short distance from the sea and get a good rest: peace of mind, proximity to water, good nutrition and a bonus - the climatic therapeutic effect.

The climate is mild, the air at this resort is saturated with coniferous essential oils, which favorably affects the general condition of the body and has a therapeutic effect.

Location

A cozy small hotel Vela Hotel Icmeler 3 * is located twenty kilometers from the city of Marmaris, on the second coastline in the village of Icmeler.

Dalaman International Airport is one hundred and ten kilometers from it and the transfer to the hotel will take a little more than an hour.

According to vacationers, Vela Hotel Icmeler 3 *, reviews which brought the hotel in the rating of "three rubles" to the level of four-star service, is a very convenient option for family vacations and holidays with children.

The hotel is located in the center of the village with affordable resort infrastructure and at the same time is only ten minutes walk from the coast of the Aegean Sea.

The hotel is calm, has a small area. Next to it there is a bus stop with easy access to Marmaris.

Number of rooms

The hotel Vela Hotel Icmeler 3 * ninety-eight rooms, which can accommodate two people in the main places. It is possible to accommodate a family of three, with an extra bed.

The rooms, clean and comfortable, have all the necessary furniture for a short stay: a bed, a bedside table, a table. In all rooms the temperature condition regulated by means of an individual air conditioner.

There is an LCD TV with satellite connection, a telephone with international dial-up access, a free safe, a fridge with a minibar, a bathroom with toiletries, and a hairdryer.

Hotel services Vela Hotel Icmeler 3 * (Marmaris)

The hotel has its own territory and garden, guests can take advantage of free car parking, an elevator, a free internet connection zone is available on the hotel territory, fax services and copying of documents are provided.

The territory of the Vela Hotel Icmeler 3 * hotel has its own pool with a relaxation area, sun loungers and umbrellas.

Guests can relax here at lunchtime while enjoying refreshing drinks and cocktails at the poolside bar. At this time of day, exposure to direct sunlight is undesirable. For children there is a children's pool, shallow and comfortable.

The hotel operates on an all-inclusive basis. According to tourists, the food corresponds to the declared level of the hotel. There is everything, but no frills. Hotel restaurant Vela Hotel Icmeler 3 * offers a large selection of self-service buffet dishes. Since the hotel is located in the center of the town, you can always buy additionally all that is needed from the products.

Entertainment and excursions

The sea is less than a ten minute walk from the hotel. On the coast, options for outdoor activities, jet skiing are offered . Only in Marmaris you can taste the melting ice cream from Marash.

From the Vela Hotel 3 * (Dalaman Icmeler), by prior arrangement, they will be taken on the excursions listed below.

Visiting historical sites. A trip to the ancient settlements of Hierapolis, Ephesus Aphrodisias, acquaintance with the history and artifacts of antiquity - for lovers of immersion in the past of the country.

Visiting natural sites. A trip to the Turgut waterfall, an excursion to the Nimara cave, to the Kyz Kumu sand spit - will add impressions and photos to the treasury of your travels.

Sea cruises. Visiting the islands in the Aegean Sea always remains one of the favorite options for sea voyages. For holders of a Schengen visa, you can book an excursion to the Greek island of Rhodes. Not far from Marmaris is a conservation area - a turtle island. This trip will impress both adults and children. A visit to the island of Sedir and swimming on the beach of Cleopatra is a great option for relaxing on the Aegean Sea. Amazing sand of Cleopatra beach, large and iridescent, which can not be found on any other beach, will give you a feeling of paradise relaxation.
