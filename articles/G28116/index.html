Push-button telephone with a good camera. Review of the best models

Manufacturers are reluctant to provide push-button phones with modern functionality. Basically, models in the classic case are designed for utilitarian use and do not involve the introduction of the latest technologies. Nevertheless, the requests of connoisseurs of devices with a keyboard grow as smartphones master new options. And if fans of a traditional mobile phone do not think about high performance, wide communication capabilities and navigation tools, for the most part, their requirements increase. In particular, a considerable part of users are looking for a push-button phone with a good camera and at an affordable price. It would seem that the huge market for mobile devices is full of devices with the ability to shoot, but in reality, high-quality models can be counted on the fingers.

Basic requirements for camera phones





When buying a mobile device, it‚Äôs difficult to focus on only one characteristic, ignoring everything else. Nevertheless, for many this is enough, since the basic functions in the form of making calls and sending SMS today are supported by any device. What should I look for when choosing a device for shooting? First of all, the number of pixels. Of course, this is not a guarantee of a certain level of quality, but, for example, a gadget with 5 megapixels will obviously surpass the 2 megapixel model. In addition, a push-button telephone with a good camera should have at least basic tools in the form of shooting settings. This can be a set of filters, different modes of photography, delay options, etc. Also, modern devices can provide shooting with autofocus, the use of optical zoom and backlight.

Nokia 515





Finnish developers in the old days were famous for their excellent button models. Many of the first devices are still operated by careful owners, which indicates the quality of products of this brand. In this case, the model 515 is noteworthy. It is a nice-looking and compact gadget equipped with a 5-megapixel camera. The module will offer the possibility of digital zoom and LED flash, which will allow you to take pictures of decent quality, but no more. Still, the lack of autofocus limits the functionality of the device. Those who want a push-button cell phone with a good camera from a reliable manufacturer should recommend this particular device. However, this model does not have an advanced option in terms of video and photography. Rather, it is aimed at universality in using the modern capabilities of mobile devices, as evidenced by the qualitatively implemented multimedia part.

Nokia E6





It should be noted right away that, unlike the other models of this review, this device belongs to the category of smartphones. A feature of the E6 is that the model is equipped with a keyboard, and in the original version. As for the camera, judging by the characteristics, it promises quality at the level of the camera, albeit a budget one. It is also worth considering that this is an inexpensive push-button phone with a good camera, when compared with competitors. So, if the price of the 515 model can reach 10 thousand rubles, then the E6 will cost 2-3 thousand cheaper.

So, the model has an 8-megapixel module, the function of which is supported by a double zoom and LED backlight. Autofocus is again not provided, but if the device is required exclusively for shooting, then it is worth choosing this option from Nokia. A push-button telephone with a good camera in this design is a rarity. Needless to say, not even every manufacturer has 5 megapixel versions. However, the practice of using this phone shows that the quality of the pictures does not reach 8 megapixels.

Philips X5500





This is one of the best representatives of the Xenium line, which is equipped not only with a decent camera, but also with a capacious battery. Also, the device will appeal to connoisseurs of "indestructible" devices that are designed to operate in harsh conditions.

The model is equipped with a 5 megapixel camera module. As an optional addition, the company provided autofocus and flash, but the developers refused digital zoom. This is not ideal for shooting, but if you need a push-button phone with a good camera and battery, then you should make this choice. All competitors that surpass this model in the quality of the images obtained provide autonomous operation for a maximum of 3 days. In turn, the X5500 maintains its performance in optimal operation for a week. By the way, its battery capacity is 2900 mAh.

Samsung S5611

Another representative of the classic form factor, which has a 5 megapixel camera. It should be noted that in assessing the quality of photographs there is usually a fraction of subjectivity. Especially when it comes to devices with similar characteristics. But in this case, neither users nor specialists have doubts. The device in terms of the quality of the resulting photographs is the undisputed leader. Therefore, if you need a push-button telephone with a good camera to get clear pictures without unnecessary noise, then it makes sense to purchase the S5611 model. The device also has autofocus and backlighting based on LEDs, so you can not worry about the camera function.





Conclusion

All the advanced technologies for mobile devices primarily master smartphones with touch screens. In turn, the aging segment of traditional devices is completely dispensed with a number of new features. Despite this, today you can find a push-button phone with a good camera and a whole host of other useful options. The only problem is that updates in the segment do not occur so often and some devices remain in the manufacturers' lines for several years. Companies pay more attention to developing budget models, supplying them with 1.3-2 megapixel cameras. Of course, the quality of pictures in such phones by modern standards is not good at all.
