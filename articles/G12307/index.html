Three-tariff meters: characteristics and main advantages

Three-tariff meters have come into our life relatively recently, gradually replacing the usual options with one and two tariffs. The key advantage is the ability to independently choose which tariff the user will be most profitable.





However, at the moment the equipment is not represented everywhere, because some regions have not completed the connection program, but are still working on the implementation of meters with two tariffs. However, experts confidently say that in the near future, three-tariff meters will become available throughout the country.

Benefit and Benefit

It is no secret that the receipt of a three-tariff meter is cheaper than when paying for electric energy at one or two tariffs. But few people think that for the resource provider and equipment manufacturers the choice in favor of exactly three tariffs also gives a certain benefit. Experts say that such meters have become the optimal solution that best meets the expectations of all parties. In more advanced countries, this calculation of electricity consumption has been used for more than one year.

And what is the benefit?

Words by words, but what is the real use? In order to understand it, you will have to figure out how to read a three-tariff counter. It contains the time intervals:

  • from ten in the morning to five in the afternoon;
  • from five to nine;
  • from nine in the evening to eleven in the evening;
  • from eleven in the evening to seven in the morning;
  • from seven to ten in the morning.

The cheapest electrical energy is supplied between eleven in the evening and seven in the morning. This tariff is called "night".





The daily rate is more expensive than others and is valid from 10 a.m. to 5 p.m., and then again active from nine to eleven in the evening. In other time periods, electricity consumption is calculated at the third, average tariff. Three-tariff email the counter allows you to save four times more at night, compared with daytime. But the device, which counts electricity at the same tariff around the clock, suggests that payment will be made at the daily, that is, high tariff. It does not take into account at what time of the day the resource was used up.

And what about the manufacturers?

Of course, the consumer is interested in what is the benefit for the supplier to sell a device that helps to save and, as it seems, reduces the profits of electricity supply companies. Payment by a three-tariff meter in Moscow, where the possibility of installing this equipment was introduced earlier than in other regions, showed that the device helps to deal with the concept of ‚Äúpeak hour‚Äù. This is the period when people wake up, as well as the time the working population returns home. As a rule, everyone wants to turn on the light, warm the kettle, blow dry their heads, and so on. Consequently, in residential buildings there are a huge number of devices that create an extremely strong load on the electric network.





The manufacturer is forced to run all of its power plants into maximum power generation to meet the needs of the population. When the peak decreases, the functioning of power plants can be weakened. And this cycle repeats daily.

And then everything breaks down!

Even without special knowledge, it is clear that with such regular fluctuations, damage cannot be avoided. The best option is when the equipment copes with the load of the average level, unchanged throughout the entire period of operation. The electricity preference for a three-tariff meter provokes people to use electrical energy-consuming equipment not only during peak hours, but also in other periods. For example, a washing machine, dishwasher, and so on can be started at night. An incentive is a significant saving on energy, since modern technology does not require regular supervision, so you can just get clean things or dishes in the morning.





And for the energy producer, periods of high demand for electricity become easier to process, as the demand for resources from the population decreases. Consequently, the equipment will not wear out so quickly, break, do not have to spend a lot of money on regular repairs and elimination of accidents.

Benefits for the world

This is surprising, but three-tariff counters are useful not only for people, but also for the surrounding world. And the reason is simple: environmental damage is significantly reduced due to increased loads on power plants. To ensure the correct operation of the station, it is necessary to have a sufficiently large amount of the initial resource in order to generate the required level of electricity. The greater the mass, the greater the emissions, and when developing a uniform level of functioning, you can adjust the process so that the amount of emissions is minimal and leveled by cleaning systems.





Thus, anyone who chooses three-tariff meters, thereby not only trying to help themselves and improve their financial situation at home, but also struggles for a clean environment. Let this be a small contribution, but feasible to everyone, and even associated with personal gain, especially since using the devices at night is possible without any discomfort for yourself. Interesting features

Visually distinguishing a meter with three tariffs from a single tariff is practically impossible, since the specificity is that internal mechanisms switch the system at a given time. How to pay a three-tariff meter? When approaching the moment of calculation for the resource, the consumer will receive three receipts. Each of them must be filled in with the numbers indicated on the corresponding scoreboard. There are no difficulties, a layman and an elderly person who is far from technology will cope with this. The main thing is not to confuse the indicators - since everything is signed. The cost of a meter with three tariffs is higher than a simpler model. But the difference pays for itself in six months, or even earlier.

Who benefits the most

Finally, all ordinary people benefit from the use of the three-tariff system. Remember that some appliances must operate around the clock. Statistics show that in an ordinary apartment of a modern person, a very large percentage of all consumed electricity is consumed by a refrigerator, which needs a resource around the clock. With two tariffs, only on this device you can save four times, and with three tariffs, the savings will increase up to 8 times, because for a full tariff of 24 hours you only have to pay 9.

How to calculate the exemption for a three-tariff counter? Pay attention to the time at which you most often turn on the lights and various appliances at home, and also evaluate whether there is a lot of equipment connected to the network around the clock. Consider not only the usual household appliances (kitchen used in the bathroom), but also the heating system, lighting, including external, if you live in a private house.

And how do you know how much energy you spent?

How to take readings from a three-tariff meter? Everything is extremely simple. Step-by-step instruction:

  • Find a counter in the house.
  • Press the Enter key.
  • Write down the readings that appear on the screen. Each digit will be marked T1, T2 or T3, which allows you to determine for which tariff the data are valid.




Further, these figures are entered into receipts, each in its own, and already on this sheet with the current tariff, you can calculate how much you have to pay. By the way, it‚Äôs not necessary to even go to the post office or to the bank to stand a long line. About options

There are two groups of electric meters:

  • induction;
  • electronic.

The former are wider distributed, as they were invented earlier and massively installed in previous years. The price for them is slightly lower, but nowadays such meters are considered obsolete. But electronic is the last word in technology. Devices conduct accurate metering of electrical energy without error. They are designed to cope with large volumes of resources for accounting. The most powerful and smart ones allow remote acquisition of energy consumption data .





When choosing a specific device, pay attention to:

  • accuracy class (the best option is 2.0);
  • tariffing.

It must be remembered that for the metering of energy it is allowed to use only devices whose types have been approved by the local authorities, officials responsible for metrology. To determine the accuracy class, the technician is tested with measuring instruments.

Meters that take into account the consumption of electric energy at three tariffs have established themselves as profitable and useful. When choosing, take into account the accuracy of the device and make sure that it supports all three tariffs, and also check with your local sales company whether it is possible to install a three-tariff device in your area.
