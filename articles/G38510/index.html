Moscow - Novorossiysk: what is the best way to get and how to go

To begin with, it is worth noting that the route from Moscow to Novorossiysk is in great demand. Moreover, this popularity is completely independent of the mode of transport. In the summer months, tickets for trains and planes are sold out in a few days, and cars in this direction are often in big traffic jams. What is the reason for such popularity?

Moscow - Novorossiysk: features of the route

The answer is extremely simple - almost all Russian resorts can be reached this way, since Novorossiysk is a large transport hub. A federal road leads to it.

By car you can get on the highway Moscow - Novorossiysk. Its official name is M4 Don. It is also possible to get to this city by rail and by plane.





Novorossiysk is a large city. It is located in the Krasnodar Territory, on the Black Sea coast. The largest port of the country is located in the village: passenger and cargo ships pass through it. In addition, there is an oil harbor.

What is known Novorossiysk, what to see

As mentioned above, Novorossiysk is a large port city. However, this is not all that he can boast of. The city has a rich history and many attractions. His story began in 1838. Until that moment, the territory where Novorossiysk subsequently appeared often passed from hand to hand. Initially, it originated as the Greek city of Bata. It happened in the V century BC. e. Then, in the II century, it was destroyed and conquered by nomads. At the end of the 15th century, it passed to the Turks, and until the 18th century was in their possession. In 1829, these territories were transferred to Russia, and, after some time, this wonderful city appeared here.

Now Novorossiysk is known for its nature. Many people come here every year, despite the fact that the city itself is not a recognized resort. However, there are a large number of tourist centers and resorts.

One of the famous natural attractions is the mountain lake Abrau. The production is located here - the Abrau-Durso factory, where champagne is produced.





Not far from Novorossiysk dolmens were preserved. These are ancient stone structures dating back several thousand years.

Moscow - Novorossiysk: how to get there by car

Many tourists prefer to get to this city by car. Indeed, this is a pretty pleasant way, given that the Moscow-Novorossiysk highway is now well repaired and organized. The distance by car is 1,478 km. Travel time will take about 22 hours if you go non-stop. You can travel with stops, because finding a place to sleep is not a problem. Along the highway there are many hotels, and you can also call in large cities and stay in them.

The M4 Don highway runs through several large cities: Voronezh, Rostov-on-Don and Krasnodar. The total length of the road is about 1,550 km. There are many paid sites that allow you to quickly go around large settlements, and not go through them.





When traveling by car, it is worth considering the traffic situation. In the summer, the track is very busy because many people want to get to rest by car. Traffic jams occur at repair sites, as well as in front of paid areas where toll points are located.

Train ride

Rail transport is considered one of the most calm and comfortable. Train "Moscow - Novorossiysk" - good a way to get to rest, especially with young children who find it hard to be in a car for a long time.

Trains leave daily from Kazan and Paveletsky stations. Travel time is: on a branded train - 22 hours 37 minutes, on a regular train - 1 day 13 hours. You can choose any convenient way of travel: reserved seat, coupe or CB. The distance from Moscow to Novorossiysk by train is 1494 km. Tickets are best purchased in advance. The cost of a reserved seat ticket starts from an average of 2,500 rubles, a compartment - from 2,900 rubles, a CB - from 14 thousand rubles.

Is it possible to fly on an airplane

Now when planning a vacation, people most often focus on the speed of movement. Not everyone can afford to spend a day or more on the way. Therefore, many tourists choose a plane as a transport.

Unfortunately, there are no direct flights "Moscow - Novorossiysk". Previously, the city had its own airport, which was located near the settlement of Myskhako. However, in 1992 it was closed. Now on the site of the former runway are garage buildings.

If you still need to get to Novorossiysk by plane, you can choose another nearest airport - Vityazevo, which is located near Anapa. It serves several cities at once: Temryuk, Novorossiysk and Anapa. Every day he takes a lot of tourists who come here to rest.





From this airport you can get to Novorossiysk by bus or minibus. The distance between them is only 49 km. Thus, to get to Novorossiysk, you can choose any convenient method.
