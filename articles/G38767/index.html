Updating the wardrobe - how to make fashionable shorts from old jeans



Renewal of the wardrobe is a phrase known to every woman. Basically, the wardrobe is updated with the purchase of new things. In this case, you can do anything with the old - throw it away, give it to an orphanage, give it to a friend. But what if the purchase of new things is not the first priority financial investment at the moment, or do you madly love your old jeans and do not want to throw them away? That's right, you can make fashionable shorts from old jeans.

Sewing machine, ruler and scissors

So, if you find those jeans that you want to turn into shorts, stock up not only with patience (since some of them will take more than ten minutes), but also with such sewing attributes as scissors, a sewing machine and a centimeter. You can also have an overlock in the arsenal for processing the edges of the fabric.

How to make fashionable shorts from old jeans

Option # 1: Neat



First of all, you need to determine the length. To do this, take the shorts that you like in length, or determine the length on jeans with a centimeter, marking it with a chalk. Using scissors, cut off the extra centimeters of fabric. Then you need to process the edges of the shorts so that they are neat: fold them two times a few centimeters. It is worth noting that since jeans are stitched quite difficult, you need to use a special needle for denim (needle-denim, sizes t 90/14 to 110/18).

How to make fashionable shorts from old jeans

Option number 2: youth

Looking in the closet and finding there frayed and frayed denim pants, do not rush to throw them away. Determine the length and cut them. Vintage scuff is now very relevant, and additional holes - a stylish element. If you want to make them in a truly fashion style, add a couple of elements. So, additional loops can be sewn onto pockets. You can apply something abstract with paint, or draw with a felt-tip pen or marker - though such a pattern will not last long. You can use colored threads by embroidering an interesting fashionable pattern on pockets.

So, before throwing away old jeans, take a look at them - because you can well come up with something new from them. It can be either an accessory, slippers for the home or an interior item.
