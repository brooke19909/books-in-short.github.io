Kursk, "Nightingale Grove" (hotel complex): description, services and reviews

The city of Kursk is considered to be friendly and quiet. "Nightingale Grove" is a hotel complex that will provide you with a good rest from the hustle and bustle of the city.





Brief description of the hotel

Many tourists choose a Kursk for a quiet and relaxing holiday. "Nightingale Grove" provides comfortable living conditions, as well as take advantage of a wide range of services. Here you can swim in the indoor pool, regardless of the time of year, as well as use the wireless Internet and private parking. Here you can fully relax, enjoying the silence and pleasant coniferous aroma.

The city of Kursk is considered friendly and welcoming. "Nightingale Grove" will make the experience even more pleasant thanks to living in comfortable rooms. All of them are decorated in a cozy classic style, and are also equipped with all the necessary amenities for a full stay. Special attention should be paid to the presence of a balcony from which you can admire the picturesque forest landscapes.

If you like peace and comfort, then choose the Nightingale Grove hotel complex (Kursk) for your vacation. The hotel restaurant offers you a huge selection of traditional Russian dishes. In addition, real museum exhibits are presented here that will tell you about the life of their ancestors.





The location of the hotel complex

If you want to relax in the lap of nature, then by all means go to Kursk. "Nightingale Grove" is located in a pine forest, next to the Seym River. Nevertheless, this does not mean that you will be cut off from civilization, because the complex is only a 10-minute drive from the central part of the city. The closest places are attractions such as the Pushkin Theater and the Trinity Monastery. Hotel address - 142 A. Engels Street

Motel "Nightingale Grove" (Kursk): negative reviews

The guests of this hotel leave a number of negative comments, the most significant of which can be considered the following:

  • the hotel is old enough and has not been renovated for a long time;
  • leaking plumbing in the bathroom;
  • the shower is represented by a tray with a curtain of oilcloth, which sticks to the body during bathing;
  • very thin walls through which all conversations and steps of neighbors are heard;
  • the rooms are rather dirty (the impression is that the rooms are not prepared for the arrival of new guests);
  • in the hall and corridors do not turn on the light in order to save money, and therefore it is very dark there and there is a risk of injury
  • the waiters in the restaurant are very slow and not too helpful with the guests;
  • if in a cafe you want to make an order on the menu, you will find that more than half of the declared dishes are not available;
  • the cost of living does not match the quality of the services provided;
  • cosmetics are issued only upon arrival and are no longer replenished;
  • When planning a visit to the hotel for the weekend, check if there are weddings on this day, because these events are too noisy and drag out until late at night.

General impression

The hotel complex "Nightingale Grove" is a good option for those who like to relax in nature, away from the bustle of the city. The hotel is located in a pine forest, so you can enjoy the clean air and beautiful forest landscapes. In addition, the distance to the city center is quite small, and therefore you will not feel divorced from civilization. Nevertheless, all this beauty is overshadowed by the dilapidated building of the hotel, as well as the fact that the price of accommodation is very high.

Holidays in the Nightingale Grove are best not planned for the weekend, because at this time weddings and other celebrations are often held here. Given that the activities are usually quite noisy, you will not be able to enjoy the silence of the pine forest. In addition, the restaurant service as well as the meager menu will disappoint you. Nevertheless, the quality of dishes is at a fairly high level. We can say that this hotel is suitable for picky guests who are ready to turn a blind eye to a number of shortcomings for the sake of relaxation in the lap of nature, as well as business people who want to hide from the city bustle.
