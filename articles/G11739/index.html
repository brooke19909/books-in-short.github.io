What is a

This is a prestigious complex that belongs to the "Business +" class. This is a huge, sixteen-story, two-section building, which is located in an area with developed infrastructure. Installment plan

RC "Caramel" (Savelovskaya) provides an installment plan with an initial payment of at least 30% of the price of the apartment. This offer is directly from the developer. It is convenient to those that have virtually no overpayment, but the timing is very limited. Therefore, most often customers resort to the service of the bank. The company cooperates on a lending program with OJSC Kredobank. Details can be found if you contact the sales department.

Each apartment has a technical passport and registration document. A notarized contract of sale is concluded with the buyer. This provides for the transfer of ownership from the developer to the buyer. After that, he has every right to independently sell it or rent it out. Customer reviews emphasize that the document flow in the company is delivered very well. All operations are carried out quickly, and competent managers are always ready to answer all questions.
