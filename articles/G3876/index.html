A brief history of the system

If Windows is installed on your mobile gadget, this does not mean that it automatically turns into a computer. This is beyond the power of even Bill Gates, coupled with Steve Jobs. The very first versions of the Windows system were installed on devices such as Sony Ericsson P1 or HP. Having a Windows phone was considered really cool. However, around 2007, Microsoft decided that the system had become obsolete and began to actively develop a new OS, which came out with the release of the controversial Windows 8.



It is worth noting that in this case, the specialists of the company from Redmond conducted a rather ill-conceived operation. They abandoned the system, proven over the years in favor of a raw product that upset a bunch of users. Microsoft could bring the OS to a sane look only after a couple of years. Phone based on Windows 8 has become a real punishment for users. But over time, the situation has improved. However, one problem remained. This refers to the scarcity of the app store.
