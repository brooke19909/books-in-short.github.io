Excursions

A very popular trip to Aspendos. This is a very ancient Greco-Roman town in the province of Antalya. Such a trip will allow you to see one of the most magnificent ancient cities. Once the city was so large that even had the right to mint moments, which speaks of its importance. Tourists can see with their own eyes the remains of an amphitheater, built in the second century BC. Until recently, festivals and concerts were organized here.

Children will be interested in visiting the Troy water park. The highlight of this place is its unusual design. Everything in it is made in the style of the era of Trojan warriors. A special pride of the park is the life-size Trojan horse, well-known to all. The water park has many slides, water rides. Local entertainment will appeal not only to children, but also to adults.

Having visited the park, you should definitely go to the Troy Dolphinarium. Here you can see an interesting show with dolphins, white whales, walruses.

Kepryul Canyon will appeal to true wildlife lovers. More than six hundred species of plants grow on its territory, and thirty of them are considered endemic. The canyon got its name from the river, due to which it was formed. A difficult waterway with many rapids has long attracted fans of extreme sports. Lovers of altitude and mountain climbing can try their hand here. In addition, guides offer horse riding in the protected area.

Not far from Belek is the historical and architectural complex of Perge, one of the main attractions of which is the ancient theater. Once the building was designed for twelve thousand spectators. The theater was built by the Romans in the second century BC on the site of a similar Hellenic structure. It is believed that this building is the best-preserved theater in all of Asia Minor. Some parts and decorations of the oldest building are still in place, and most have long been moved to the Antalya Historical Museum to ensure the full preservation of values.
