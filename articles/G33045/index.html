Kata Silver Sand Hotel 3 (Thailand / Phuket): photos and reviews

Despite the fact that the high tourist season in Thailand begins in November and ends in March, our compatriots do not leave this hospitable country even in the summer, when tropical rains reign here. Usually in the summer months, Russian tourists prefer to go to Phuket. This island is not only very beautiful and abounds with gorgeous sandy beaches for every taste, but also offers a lot of fascinating excursions that can be reached only from this point of the country. If you plan to go to Phuket, but want to relax economically, then consider the Kata Silver Sand Hotel 3 * for accommodation. This hotel complex has a convenient location and is suitable for active people who spend most of their time in search of adventure and new experiences. However, we will give you the opportunity to make your own opinion about the hotel, telling the most necessary information about it.





Kata Silver Sand Hotel 3 *: overview

If this is your first trip to Phuket, keep in mind that most hotels here do not have their own beach area. All the beaches of the island are municipal, so you will have to pay for rest on sunbeds under umbrellas. This fact should help you not to evaluate hotel complexes depending on proximity to the sea and the presence of your own coastal territory.

Kata Silver Sand Hotel 3 * was commissioned just eight years ago. However, four years later, large-scale repair work was carried out in it, so most vacationers note good design, cleanliness and comfort in the rooms, as well as an abundance of modern technology.

Information about the hotel Kata Silver Sand Hotel 3 * is quite difficult to find on the Internet, but it is often offered to our compatriots by travel agents. It is especially interesting for young people and those who are not too pretentious of their surroundings. The hotel complex Kata Silver Sand Hotel 3 * can be attributed to small hotels that have no more than sixty rooms. They are located in one two-story building with an elongated shape. All apartments are located on both sides of a long corridor. Some of the tourists claim that the farthest rooms are separated from the reception by two hundred meters.

Hotel Location

If you are planning an independent trip to Phuket and are worried about how to get from the airport to the Kata Silver Sand Hotel 3 * hotel complex, the address given to the taxi driver will help you quickly find yourself in the right place. Experienced travelers are advised to write it on a piece of paper at home in Thai, so it will be easier for the driver to understand you and he will definitely not be mistaken in choosing the direction.

The hotel belongs to hotel complexes located on the second coastline. Vacationers will need to cross the road to get to the beach. The distance separating Kata Silver Sand Hotel 3 * from the sea does not exceed six hundred meters. Guests in their reviews note that the road to the beach area does not take more than five to seven minutes. Moreover, the hotel complex is located literally in the center of Kata Beach. It is believed that this is one of the most quiet and family coastal areas of Phuket. There are always a lot of children and respectable couples, relaxing on deckchairs to the sound of the sea.

If you are closer to youth parties and noisy parties, then go to Patong, a tuk-tuk will deliver you to him literally in twenty minutes. Here the most fun people gather and a variety of entertainment shows are held every evening.

Keep in mind that from Phuket Airport to Kata Silver Sand Hotel 3 * it takes an average of about forty-five minutes. But this distance does not take much time, because the path runs through a beautiful area with high-quality asphalt surface. Most tourists do not even notice how they find themselves at the reception of their hotel.

If you have purchased a tour that includes breakfast only, which is quite common in Thailand, then the convenient location of the hotel will allow you to visit the local market often and visit numerous cafes, where you will certainly be offered a large selection of dishes of national and Russian cuisine.





Infrastructure

Since the hotel described by us belongs to the category of budget "three rubles", the Kata Silver Sand Hotel 3 * provides its guests with only the most necessary amenities. However, this is quite enough for a good vacation in an exotic place on the beach.

The hotel has one swimming pool, which created a separate compartment for babies. It allows you to watch the kids who can frolic at some distance from their parents and without disturbing them.

Guests can enjoy delicious dishes prepared by the chef of the Kata Silver Sand Hotel 3 * restaurant. Also, all vacationers have the opportunity to try all the cocktails and fragrant light snacks that the bartender will prepare for them. The bar is located in the pool area, which is very convenient for guests.

Many tourists prefer to travel around Thailand on a rented motorbike. Therefore, the issue of parking is always relevant for them. Those who live in the hotel complex Kata Silver Sand Hotel 3 * do not have to worry about where to leave their transport for a while. Guests can always take advantage of free parking near the main building.

In addition, there is a rental office right on site that will quickly arrange a motorbike or car rental for you. Most experienced travelers are advised to do this in hotel complexes, because private street companies can always fool tourists. But still, do not forget to photograph your new transport from all sides in order to avoid further financial disputes upon return.

If necessary, vacationers can use the local laundry and dry cleaning services. This will save guests from tedious hand washing or sorting things into dirty and clean before leaving home. Just do not forget that this service is not included in the price. It will be included in your bill upon discharge from the hotel complex.





Kata Silver Sand Hotel 3 *: services

Many hotel guests in their reviews note that the accommodation fully met their expectations. They received exactly the range of services that is characteristic of the standard "three rubles".

At the reception and adjacent rooms, it always catches Wi-Fi. Here you can freely surf the Internet at any time of the day, however, difficulties arise in the rest of the hotel. In the most remote apartments, it is almost impossible to catch a signal.

The reception is open around the clock, if necessary, the staff of the hotel complex will organize an excursion program or room service for you. Some vacationers in their reviews on the positive side note the security of the hotel complex. Several security guards work around the clock, ready to always come to the aid of their guests.

If you are traveling to Thailand on your own and do not know how best to get from the airport, then indicate the need for a transfer when booking. It is paid separately, the amount can be found immediately upon booking. It will be indicated in the final cost of living.

Hotel compliments

The administration of the hotel complex does everything to make it convenient and comfortable for guests, therefore, it has provided two options for compliments. The first concerns regular guests who have already rested here more than once. They can be provided with Kata Silver Sand Hotel 3 * superior rooms at no extra charge.

Newlyweds who have warned about their event at the stage of booking an apartment receive a beautifully decorated room with a basket of fruits as a gift from the hotel. However, do not forget to present your marriage certificate at the reception at any time upon request.





Apartments

Unfortunately for many guests, Kata Silver Sand Hotel 3 * room options offer not too diverse. All sixty apartments are one-bedroom apartments with a balcony, decorated in national Thai style. They are usually furnished with wicker wooden furniture.

Each room is equipped with a safe, a TV with several Russian channels, individual air conditioning, a telephone and a full fridge. The last nuance is very pleasing to tourists who are free to store products purchased on the market in it. Every day, maids replenish drinking water.

The balcony is furnished, so relaxing in comfortable wicker sun loungers turns into a real pleasure. In addition, most rooms face the sunny side, therefore, according to tourists' reviews, clothes quickly dry here.

The bathroom does not have a hairdryer and a set of cosmetic accessories, but the maids replenish toilet paper regularly. Once every three days, bed linen and towels are changed. Most guests praised the cleaning of the apartment, which was usually carried out at the same time daily.

This room can accommodate two adults on separate single beds or on one double. Kids are provided with an extra bed.





Accommodation Features

Almost every hotel has a number of rules and restrictions that every potential guest should know well. For example, keep in mind that when checking in, they take a deposit of fifty dollars. If you have baht or rubles, then you can leave them at the reception. In this case, the amount will be equal to two thousand baht (1 baht is 0.03 dollars).

If you use the services not included in the tour price during the rest, you can pay by credit card at check-out. It is also worth knowing that when booking a room on your own, you will be required to present the same credit card when checking in that you paid on the Internet.

Those who plan to take their pet on vacation will have to choose a different hotel. In Kata Silver Sand Hotel 3 * pets are not allowed.

Check In Rules

According to the rules of the hotel, check-in occurs at two in the afternoon, but if there is a free room, reception staff can arrange documents before the due date. You must leave the room no later than twelve o'clock in the afternoon.

In the hotel complex you can relax with children of any age. Those kids who are not yet four years old can check in with existing beds absolutely free of charge. For older children, you must pay six hundred baht per day.





Nutrition

This hotel provides its guests only breakfast. Of course, if you want to have lunch or dinner at the restaurant Kata Silver Sand Hotel 3 *, then this will not become a problem, taking into account the additional payment. Breakfast is served as a buffet from six to ten in the morning. Usually vacationers can choose among a wide variety of dishes:

  • eggs in several variations of cooking;
  • meat snacks - ham, sausages, sausages;
  • rice
  • noodles;
  • a large number of fresh vegetables;
  • assorted drinks;
  • Jams

  • butter.

The only negative that can be seen in the guest reviews about food is the lack of fruit. For breakfast, if you are lucky, you can get only a few slices of watermelon. This is not enough even for the usual "treshka".

Voucher cost

Naturally, the price of a tour to this hotel will directly depend on the season. In the summer you can relax here, taking into account a substantial discount. For example, seven nights for two adults will cost twelve and a half thousand rubles. Please note that airfare is not included in this amount.

If you prefer to travel as part of a tourist group and not think about anything, then you should consider permits in which flights and transfers will be included. Such a vacation for ten days with a flight from Moscow will cost about seventy thousand rubles.

A tour bought from November to March will be more expensive by an average of ten thousand rubles.





Reviews of our compatriots

For those who are seriously considering the Kata Silver Sand Hotel 3 *, reviews can help make a final decision. These comments are posted in large numbers on the Internet, so we easily analyzed them to identify all the advantages and disadvantages of the hotel complex. In general terms, we can say that the reviews about Kata Silver Sand Hotel 3 * are more positive. Vacationers who understand that they are going to the average three-ruble stay quite satisfied with the service and the hotel itself. The advantages of the hotel complex include:

  • convenient location relative to cafes, markets, shops and beaches;
  • high professionalism of employees, among which there is a Russian-speaking staff;
  • tasty and fresh food in the restaurant;
  • good housekeeping;
  • a clean, albeit small pool, where you can have fun;
  • low cost of tours.

Our compatriots found few disadvantages, but for some tourists they turned out to be significant:

  • poor sound insulation in the rooms;
  • an abundance of ants;
  • intermittent interruptions with light and hot water;
  • long and uncomfortable corridor in the main building;
  • a drain that runs along the entire wall of the residential building;
  • frogs arranging night concerts right under the windows of the rooms, because of which it is impossible to fall asleep;
  • noise from the street;
  • poor breakfasts.

Of course, it is for everyone to decide whether this hotel is suitable for him. However, the photos of Kata Silver Sand Hotel 3 *, posted by its guests on the Internet, create a rather pleasant first impression. As a small conclusion, we can say that people who are not spoiled by the five-star service and who are ready to put up with some inconvenience for little money can come here. Typically, this category includes young people, young families and pensioners, who have been diligently saving up for a trip abroad for several years. But those who are accustomed to luxury and want for their money to get all the benefits available, should look for another hotel complex on the "Kat". It will not be very convenient for them in this budget hotel, and the reviews left will be filled with negativity.
