Hotel Stella di Mare 4 * (Budva, Montenegro): overview, description and reviews of tourists

Those who wish to spend a summer vacation inexpensively and efficiently should visit the Becici resort, located in the young state of Montenegro. Becici is a popular modern tourist center located a few kilometers from the city of Budva, on the Adriatic coast. The presence of a wide pebble beach and clear sea brought fame to this resort as an ideal place for a family vacation. The beaches in Becici are municipal, but all the hotels located on the coast have small areas reserved exclusively for guests. This provides an opportunity for guests to retire, hiding from everyday hustle and bustle, and truly enjoy a well-deserved rest.





Hotel Stella di Mare 4 * (Montenegro, Becici) is considered one of the most profitable vacation options. About Becici

Rest in the resort of Becici made an indelible impression on the guests of Stella di Mare 4 * (Montenegro). Becici is chosen by many in order to be able to see with their own eyes and visit the city beach, considered one of the best beaches in the world, the length of which is more than 21 km.





On the beach there are all conditions for outdoor activities: there are fields for beach volleyball, several ping-pong courts, tennis, football and basketball courts. Fans of extreme entertainment are happy to receive diving, windsurfing or water skiing lessons, as well as jet skiing lessons. Inveterate fishermen can rent a yacht or a small boat and fish in the open sea.

One of the main features of Becici is the presence of a wide selection of private villas and apartments, which you can rent at a very affordable price. Such an opportunity is usually used by those who do not want to stay at the hotel, trying to spend their holidays in seclusion.

But the rest in the hotel Stella di Mare 4 * (Montenegro) is characterized by reviews as an excellent option for lovers of a secluded and relaxing holiday.

Acquaintance

Tourists strongly recommend the Stella Di Mare 4 * hotel for lovers of a relaxing holiday, as well as for those who come to Montenegro with children. The hotel provides free WiFi. Only 7-10 minutes walk to the sea. The hotel has a very beautifully decorated interior, say the authors of the reviews, spacious refurbished rooms with excellent bathrooms. Responsive and friendly staff also work here. The hotel, according to the guests living in it, fully justifies its 4 *.

Reviews

According to numerous reviews by guests of Stella di Mare 4 *, the hotel is very comfortable, quiet, calm and cozy. One feels the novelty of the hotel (built in 2008). Guests report that rooms are cleaned daily. The hotel is very cozy, this is emphasized by many vacationers. Everything is arranged almost homely here. The rooms have small kitchens with kitchenware. Residents note extraordinary cleanliness in rooms that are cleaned daily. On TV and radio broadcast Russian channels. Many people like having a large balcony with a table and chairs (in the evening you can enjoy the sunset with a glass of local wine). The rooms have air conditioning on the floor, guests note the good quality of the furniture on offer. For potential guests of the hotel, the authors of the reviews warn that the refrigerator in the room usually happens without a freezer. A jacuzzi is available in all rooms.

The authors of the reviews advise future guests of Stella di Mare 4 * to choose the corner numbers that are further from the elevator when settling in: here they offer wonderful views of the sea and mountains, in addition, bright direct sunlight does not hit the windows in the morning, as in others rooms.

The authors of the reviews note many advantages of this institution. Guests enjoy a variety of breakfasts: stewed vegetables, according to reviews, are very tasty, prshut, cheese and sausages, fruits (several types). Breakfast at the hotel, reviews are shared, are unusually tasty (not a huge assortment, as, for example, in Turkey, but very thoughtful, and it changes daily. There is always an omelet, scrambled eggs, slices: meat, vegetable, cheese, sausages, olives, yogurts, juices, granola, coffee (hardware, several types).





Guests note that the Wi-Fi at the hotel is working smoothly, a set of quality shampoos and shower gels is constantly replenished, slippers and bathrobes are provided in all rooms, towels are changed daily.

Many people like the location of the hotel: the proximity of the sea and shops. The hotel is very close to various convenience stores, guests say: at a distance of 50-100 m. To the sea, the authors of the reviews say that there are two roads - one 450 m (here is more abrupt descent), the other - 650 m (it is more gentle, and tourists follow it, more interesting - it passes by shops, other hotels, etc.). And such a path is overcome, as the tourists testify, somehow quickly and imperceptibly.

A separate plus is the presence of scales on the floors in front of the elevator, which remind you to keep in shape. At the reception, according to the authors of the reviews, fresh newspapers and magazines, interesting books are offered. Beach umbrellas and towels can be used free of charge, a ration is given out (in case guests go on excursions early).

Tourists claim that the staff at the hotel is very friendly, always ready to help in solving any problems.

The authors of the reviews talk about the local tradition: leaving guests leave some kind of keepsake for the hotel. At the reception there is a kind of library of books left by visitors to the hotel, also previous tourists have left many beach umbrellas.

Not without cons. Many residents do not like the choice of desserts and pastries for tea. Many people find a coffee machine simply awful. Because the hotel is small, according to the guests, it would be possible to purchase a more suitable device. The same goes for breakfast juices.

The guests of Stella di Mare 4 *, in spite of the available disadvantages, are very satisfied with the level of services received: it looks like an ordinary hotel, but a lot of pleasant little things make it ideal: quality and cleanliness of rooms, impeccable service, the presence of bathrobes and slippers provided even in the standard room ", Delicious breakfast dishes, the provision of free secure parking, etc. Tourists thank the staff for the ideal conditions for relaxation and promise, if they find themselves on the Budva Riviera, stay exclusively in Stella di Mare.
