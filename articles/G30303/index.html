Camelot Boutique Beach Hotel (Turkey, Aegean Coast): hotel description and photos

Located on the shores of the Aegean Sea, the resort of Bodrum is one of the most favorite holiday destinations for tourists from around the world. Our domestic vacationers, of course, also come to this southern Turkish town. Quite often, Russian operators offer beach lovers trips to Bodrum with accommodation at the Camelot Boutique Beach Hotel. Reviews of this four-star hotel from tourists have earned good.

Where is

This small hotel is not located in the city of Bodrum itself, but about 20 km from it - in the town of Turgutreis. This relatively small town has absolutely everything that is needed for a comfortable rest of tourists - bars, clubs, shopping centers, discos, etc. There is a Saturday bazaar in Turgutreis where you can buy souvenirs, clothes or products.





Most often, travelers who rented rooms at this hotel initially fly to Bodrum Airport. This air hub is located quite far from the hotel - 60 kilometers. So vacationers have to get to the hotel for at least an hour. Usually, tourists go to this hotel on a tour operator's transfer, even for about two hours. The thing is that the bus also enters many other complexes along the way.

Also, sometimes tourists who decide to live in Turkey in this hotel, arrive from Russia to the Milas airport. It is located about 50 km from Turgutreis. So in this case, the road from the airport to the hotel can be slightly tiring.

In relation to the sea, according to many lovers of relaxation in the south, this hotel is located very well. The Camelot Boutique Beach Hotel is located on the first coastline. That is, its guests can literally in a few minutes.

Features and a brief description of the hotel Camelot Boutique Beach Hotel

On the sites of some tour operators, this hotel is advertised as a five-star hotel . However, the information is, unfortunately, inaccurate. O operators, perhaps by mistake they simply mislead their customers. In fact, Camelot Boutique Beach Hotel is a boutique hote, that is, a small boutique hotel. And he simply does not have any stardom. But the service here, judging by the available reviews of vacationers, tourists are really provided with enough quality. In any case, the hotel corresponds exactly to three stars .

He welcomes the Camelot Boutique Beach Hotel (Bodrum) in two-story buildings, reminiscent of simple village houses in architecture. Despite the simplicity of design, the hotel buildings look very attractive and stylish. The territory of this hotel has a small but well landscaped, ergonomically planned and beautifully designed. The courtyard of the complex looks very neat and presentable. In the evenings, trees growing in the hotel courtyard are illuminated by yellow and green lights. In general, memory photographs taken at the hotel will surely turn out to be very effective and will make an indelible impression on friends and acquaintances.





One of the features of the Camelot Boutique Hotel is that you can come here absolutely free with small pets. If tourists have, for example, a small dog, they can safely take it with them on a trip.

This hotel was built relatively recently - in 2011. That is, the Camelot Boutique Hotel is, in fact, quite new. The total area of ​​this complex is 11 500 m 2 .

What you should know about

On the websites of some tour operators in the photo of the Camelot Boutique Beach Hotel you can see beautiful arbors on the beach, pantone circles with pillows, etc. But you should not be seduced by holidaymakers looking at such pictures. Unfortunately, some facilities at this hotel are provided exclusively for guests of luxury rooms. Russian tour operators most often sell vouchers with accommodation in this hotel only in ordinary rooms. So, tourists who decide to stay in Camelot Boutique are tuned in, nevertheless, have a more or less “budget” service.

Hotel Rooms

In total, the Camelot Boutique Beach Hotel , the description of the territory of which already makes it possible to judge it as quite comfortable, rents 129 rooms belonging to the standard, deluxe or luxury class. Comfortable accommodation of tourists in all apartments of this complex is provided by the presence of:

  • cable and satellite television;

  • a safe;

  • electric kettle.





Of course, air conditioning is also provided at this hotel. A hairdryer at the Camelot Boutique Beach Hotel in showers is also provided. The bathrooms of some of the expensive rooms in the complex have a jacuzzi. The apartments at this hotel also include a minibar. True, it is equipped with only two bottles of mineral water (bring daily).

If desired, this hotel can also accommodate, among other things, bridal rooms.

What guests think about Camelot Boutique rooms

Reviews about the rooms rented in this hotel are different. By cons of rooms Camelot Boutique Beach Hotel (Bodrum), most tourists attribute their very small sizes. All standards, accommodation in which are sold mainly by Russian tour operators, here have a very small area. But if desired, hotel guests can pay the administration upon arrival $ 100-200 and move to deluxe or suite. Such rooms at this hotel are quite spacious.

In particular, poor reviews from vacationers have earned the standards in this hotel, located on the ground floor. Such rooms are not only very small in size. There is also a rather unpleasant smell of mold.





There are also mixed reviews about the cleanliness of the rooms in this hotel. Some holidaymakers praise the work of the hotel maids. Others complain that they rarely brought towels to replace and did not even dust the room. Most likely, the staff of this hotel and in terms of cleaning prefers not to standards, but to deluxe and deluxe rooms.

Camelot Boutique Beach Hotel: apartment reviews

Concerning the size of the rooms and the quality of tourist services, there are thus mixed reviews. However, in terms of equipment, most of the tourists praise the rooms rented to Camelot Boutique . Furniture and appliances are installed modern and high quality. Air conditioning at this hotel saves tourists even on the hottest days. The beds in the hotel are quite wide and not hard.

Tourists also respond well to the design of hotel rooms. The hotel rooms look pretty pretty. The showers here are completely lined with natural stone.

Hotel infrastructure

The rooms at the Camelot Boutique Beach Hotel are thus comfortable enough to stay. But many tourists note that they were in the rooms in this hotel quite rarely. On vacation in Turgutreis, they had something to do both on the territory of the hotel and beyond . For a boutique hotel, the infrastructure in Camelot Boutique is developed, according to many vacationers, it is really very good. Of course, there is, for example, an outdoor pool in this hotel. Tourists who rented cars in Turkey can leave them right next to the hotel in their own parking lot.

This hotel also has its own spa. In the evening, tourists can stroll in a beautiful small garden.

Is it worth it to go with children

Quite a lot of amenities at Camelot Boutique are also provided for children. For example, the hotel has a playground. Also, small hotel guests have the opportunity to swim in the paddling pool. The restaurant of the complex has special chairs for children. Tourists can rent a stroller at this hotel, if necessary. Bathing facilities are provided for very young children in the hotel.





Of course, as in any other Turkish, this hotel has:

  • luggage office;

  • laundry for a fee;

  • safe at the reception.

Another very convenient service at this hotel is a car rental. Hotel guests can get at their disposal a car for exploring local attractions not only in the city, but also directly in the complex.

Opinion of the guests about the service provided in the hotel

With regard to the facilities on site, the Camelot Boutique Beach Hotel reviews on the Web are mostly positive. The pool in this hotel, as many vacationers note, is simply huge. You can swim in it with great comfort and pleasure.

Animation in the complex, unfortunately, is not provided. This is some vacationers, of course, attributed to the shortcomings of the hotel. However, at least Camelot Boutique Beach has a DJ. If desired, vacationers can even put their favorite music at the hotel.

You can read very good reviews about the staff of this hotel on the Web. The employees at Camelot Boutique Beach are considered vacationers to be polite and smiling. No cases of theft in this hotel were noted by any tourists on the Web (at least until 2017). The owner of this hotel, like most of his employees, is also able to speak Russian, among other things. This domestic vacationers, of course, are also attributed to the advantages of the complex.

The garden in the courtyard of this hotel is pretty small. But to hide in it from the sun if desired, as many tourists note, there is where. The presence of a garden, according to tourists, is, of course, another big plus of the hotel.





How to feed the guests

If the rooms of the Camelot Boutique Beach Hotel have more or less contradictory reviews, then the tourists mostly praise the local dining room. The menu in the hotel restaurant is quite varied. The only thing is that it is oriented mostly to vegetarians. But the meat dishes in the hotel restaurant guests are, of course, served. Moreover, tourists can take in the dining room, if desired, both grill and stew.

The guests also consider a very big plus of the hotel that a lot of dishes are prepared in the local dining room, suitable for children as well. For a Turkish hotel, this is mostly a rarity.

Tourists consider a small selection of drinks to be a drawback of the Camelot Boutique restaurant. Juices in the hotel dining room are, unfortunately, served powder. Moreover, in addition to them, tourists can take mainly only cola and sprite. No healthy drinks are provided at this hotel.

Camelot Boutique Beach Hotel (Bodrum): sea reviews

The main advantage of this hotel, many tourists consider it a truly luxurious beach. From the seashore opposite the hotel you can see picturesque Greek islands. The hotel beach, among other things, is also awarded the blue flag of UNESCO. That is, the place is almost perfectly clean.

The sea itself, according to most vacationers, next to the hotel is also truly wonderful. The water in it is perfectly transparent and has a unique turquoise blue color. The advantages of the beach of the hotel, many tourists also include the fact that loungers and umbrellas are provided to guests here for free. Moreover, sun loungers are offered to residents a very convenient design.





In addition, the beach at Camelot Boutique Beach Hotel almost always plays soft relax music, which makes your stay here as enjoyable as possible. The seashore looks very picturesque next to the complex, and the photos here are really beautiful. Tourists can descend into the water right from the beach platform, which, of course, is very, very convenient. And especially for guests who came to rest in Turkey with young children.
