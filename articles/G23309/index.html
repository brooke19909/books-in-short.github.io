Tehran became the capital of Iran only in 1785. Today it is the political, administrative and cultural center of the country. Traveling to Iran involves not only relaxing in the resorts, but also visiting interesting sights, getting acquainted with the cultural heritage of the country, the traditions of its people. Visitors all this can show Tehran, because the capital is the face of the whole state.

The architecture of Tehran is very similar to buildings in other countries of Central Asia, but it is completely different from the style of the Arab countries. Therefore, those who go on vacation to Turkey, the capital of Iran will pleasantly surprise you with mosaic panels in mosques and buildings. Rest here is quite affordable, because housing, food and transport are not very expensive. The hotel rooms are very large, without much luxury, but they have everything you need. Tehran is surrounded by mountains, so the local landscapes are impressive.



In the mountains there are reservoirs from which ice water flows, cooling the city even on the hottest day. To get to the Caspian Sea, you need to drive through mountain tunnels, such a journey will never be forgotten. The capital of Iran is famous for the Tehran Conference, held in 1943 with the participation of Stalin, Roosevelt and Churchill. Now the embassy of the Russian Federation is located at this place.

In the center of the city you can see the shah's palaces, it is very beautiful and peaceful here. Around the gardens, swans are swimming in the ponds, one cannot even believe that there is a bustling city with a million people outside the walls of the residence. Iranians are a very open and friendly people, they are always ready to help with advice and suggest. There can be problems with the language, because the Iranians practically do not speak English, it is better to contact young people for help, so there is a better chance of being understood.



All women in Iran wear hijabs, this also applies to foreigners. But there is no discrimination, it is enough just to throw a scarf on your hair. Iranian girls are very beautiful, they follow fashion, often go shopping. The capital of Iran is a storehouse of exoticism. There are a lot of different goodies that a tourist is simply obliged to try. A visit to a teahouse, where a hospitable host will offer a cup of tea and a hookah, will allow you to forget about problems and worries even for a short time.
