How to get there

Sunny Beach (Bulgaria) is located between two large cities - Varna and Burgas. And since railway and air communication is possible only with them, in order to get to the resort, you must first get to these transport hubs. Tourists write that from Belarus or Ukraine to Burgas and Varna can be reached by train, but from Russia it is more convenient to travel by plane. Drive closer to Sunny Beach from Burgas, from the South Bus Station. From there minibuses to the resort leave 3-5 times per hour in the season, from six in the morning to eleven in the evening. Best time to visit

The climate of this area is almost Mediterranean. This means that the weather in Sunny Beach (Bulgaria) is clear for almost a year. No wonder the name of the resort sounds appropriate. Tourists claim that the season here lasts for half a year. You can swim and sunbathe at the resort already from the May holidays, and the last vacationers leave at the end of October. Of course, most people in Sunny Beach in July-August. But this is not at all connected with the fact that these months are the best for relaxation. It's just that most people have a vacation schedule. But to go here sunbathing and swimming is best in May and September. The first month of autumn is especially good - a real "velvet season". April and October are more suitable for sightseeing. The weather is warm and sunny, but there are practically no people.
