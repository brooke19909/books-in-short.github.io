Club Hotel Palm Azur (Tunisia, Djerba): photos and reviews of tourists

Our article will interest those people who plan to spend their holidays in Tunisia and select a decent hotel. For active tourists, of course, the famous island of Djerba, on which the Club Hotel Palm Azur is located, will be interesting.

A bit about the hotel

Club Hotel Palm Azur was completely renovated in 2011. Since then, it belongs to the Riu network. The complex is located in the south of Djerba, in a beautiful oasis, only 23 kilometers from the capital Houmt Souk, as well as thirty kilometers from Djerba Airport and six kilometers from Midoun. The hotel offers its guests beautiful spacious rooms and a fairly high level of service. It focuses on family vacations with children. If you have chosen Tunisia as your vacation destination, you can pay attention to Club Hotel Palm Azur.

Number of rooms

Club Hotel Palm Azur has 326 rooms in its arsenal:



  1. Standard rooms are excellent double rooms in which you can place two additional (in addition to the main) beds. These apartments also have special connect-rooms.
  2. Family rooms - rooms with two bedrooms.
  3. Prestige suites - apartments with a separate bedroom and beautiful sea views.

All rooms have a terrace or balcony, and are equipped with a hairdryer, mini fridge, bathroom with shower, central air conditioning, heating, TV and several satellite channels in Russian.

Dining at the hotel

Club Hotel Riu Palm Azur operates, like many other hotels, using the popular and convenient all-inclusive system. The complex has a restaurant that serves exclusively Asian cuisine Wassabi, as well as a national Tunisian establishment called Le Jasmin. In addition, the hotel complex has three bars: by the pool, lounge bar, lobby bar.



Guests are invited to try the food in the main Royal restaurant, which has an outdoor terrace. For breakfast, lunch and dinner, guests can enjoy an impressive range of buffets. According to the All inclusive concept, Tunisian-made drinks are offered to tourists free of charge. The special menu features vegetarian dishes. In the summer, for dinner and lunch, guests are offered food prepared directly in their presence in the restaurant.

Hotel infrastructure

For tourists, Hotel Club Palm Azur (Djerba) offers the following services: visits to sports programs, leisure activities and pools, as well as free Wi-Fi.

The hotel has a shop, a beauty salon, a hairdresser, a conference room for all kinds of business events. The hotel has several freshwater pools, their area is 240 and 1600 square meters. In winter, a heated indoor pool is available. In addition, the hotel has its own spa center, where guests can enjoy massages and a steam room.

Sports and Entertainment

Hotel Club Riu Azur (Tunisia) has three tennis courts with good clay surface. During the summer period, under All Inclusive, each guest can take the opportunity to attend one completely free diving lesson (in the pool). Tourists can play mini-golf, beach volleyball, table tennis, and also visit the small gym.

Hotel beach

Riu Club Hotel Palm Azur 4 * (Djerba) is located just a hundred meters from the coast and has its own sandy beach with a smooth entry into the sea and a pebble bottom. Tourists can have fun on the coast, devoting time to water sports (surfing, catamarans, canoeing).

A bit about the resort

Djerba is the southernmost and warmest resort in Tunisia. One of its distinguishing features is the fact that the island is always several degrees warmer than the continent. It has become popular not only because of its sandy beaches, but also because of the good thalassotherapy centers.



Pleasant procedures can be successfully combined with walks to the famous medina of the capital of Houmt Souk. On Djerba, you should definitely see the pottery workshops in the town of Gellala, as well as the fort of Bordj el Kebir.

The island is the home of beautiful flamingo birds, which you can admire in their natural habitat.

The island is an incredibly beautiful and romantic place, about which many legends are composed. It was first inhabited by the Phoenicians in the seventh century BC. At different times, a good location, rich nature, unlimited reserves of fresh water attracted the eyes of a variety of nations, wishing to win a small piece of this paradise for themselves.

Djerba is a wonderful resort. The incredibly mild climate makes it possible to visit the island almost throughout the year. Palm, fig, olive groves, orchards, lotus placers make the island an incredibly beautiful place. On Djerba, traditions of various nations have long been intertwined.

Tours and activities

The capital of the island of Houmt Souk has an area called the old city, located outside the fortress walls, there are numerous mosques. They can be seen walking along the medina along the white Arab houses (Jama'a al-Gorba, Jamaa-Ettruk, Ah Sheikh). On the island you can also see the famous crocodile farm.

The soul of Djerba is considered the ancient Jewish district of Riyardh, located seven kilometers south of the capital, Djerba. Here, vacationers can watch the oldest synagogue in North Africa, El Ribe. Behind its powerful and strong walls are preserved ancient manuscripts of the Torah.



Vacationers will be interested in visiting the Museum of Guelala (ethnographic). Within its walls, unique expositions of household items and everyday life of Tunisians are presented, which, after many centuries, tell about the family lifestyle and wedding customs of the inhabitants. The city of potters is no less interesting to tourists, in whose territory you can buy beautiful dishes, figurines, vases at a good price. Absolutely all the villages of the island are engaged in crafts, so guests of Djerba can buy not only simple souvenirs, but also useful things. The island, for example, is known for blankets, wickerwork, carpets, silver jewelry.

Golf enthusiasts will love the Djerba Golf Club. The island also has the largest casino in Africa and a large diving center. While relaxing on the island, you can take the opportunity to take riding lessons, enjoy the island from above, visit fish restaurants where you can order the best dishes of fish and other seafood.

Nature endowed the island with a wonderful sandy coast with tall palm trees and blue sea. The best beaches of Djerba are located on the northeast coast of the island.
