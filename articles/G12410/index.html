Hotel Le Royale Royal Holiday Resort 5 * (Egypt, Sharm El Sheikh): overview, description, reviews

The Egyptian resort of Sharm el-Sheikh, which is very popular and has spread its expanses on the southern coast of the Sinai Peninsula, has been known in this status since the Ottoman Empire. Convenient geographical position, warm and mild climate, proximity to the sea and the richness of the natural world, coupled with the rapid development of infrastructure, made it a place ideal for relaxation.





This resort is in no way inferior to European in level. On its coast there are more than 200 hotels of various levels and price categories. One of these is Le Royale Royal Holiday Resort 5 *, built near Naama Bay, close to major shopping centers, numerous restaurants, cafes and other entertainments. The hotel declares first-class relaxation and luxurious comfortable rooms that will make your vacation unforgettable. We will talk in detail about the complex itself, as well as find out the reviews of tourists who have already visited it.

Hotel location

An interesting fact is that the complex, despite the presence of five stars in status, is located not on the first, but on the second coastline, about 800 m from the sandy coast of the Red Sea. A guide for tourists is the nearby Sonesta Club Hotel. The airport is also close - only 10 km. The old part of the city (13 km) and many attractions can be reached on their own, which saves not only money, but money.

Hotel Le Royale Royal Holiday Resort 5 *: description





The resort complex consists of a main building without rooms and additional two-story buildings for tourists, there are no elevators in them. The building is new, the year of construction is 2013, therefore it is distinguished by a stylish and modern design, cleanliness and freshness of furniture and the entire interior as a whole. The hotel occupies an impressive territory of almost 100,000 square meters. m, and is designed to accommodate a large number of guests in 432 comfortable rooms of various categories.

Rooms and their decor

Egypt belongs to the category of resorts where the all-inclusive service system is popular. All rooms in the Le Royal Holiday Resort Sharm el Sheikh 5 * have a modern design in warm colors, combining the innovations of the West and the traditions of the East. The use of natural materials and natural shades in the design contributes to relaxation and a relaxed holiday, because the environment is absolutely unobtrusive, so all guests can feel the home atmosphere.

The hotel offers three categories of rooms: family, superior suite and standard. They are all equipped with air conditioning, tea and coffee making facilities, a modern TV with satellite channels, including Russian-speaking, wireless Internet, a safe and a minibar, and toiletries in the bathroom and shower. The rooms are cleaned daily, linen is changed three times a week. An extra bed can be provided for children upon request.





All Inclusive and Restaurants

Le Royale Royal Holiday Resort 5 * hotel (Egypt, Sharm El Sheikh) offers its guests an all-inclusive food system. However, you can limit yourself to breakfast only, however, as practice shows, such a solution is not the most profitable and not the most convenient. The duration of the all-inclusive system is from 9 a.m. until midnight, after which you will have to pay extra for everything. Of alcoholic beverages, only those that are produced in Egypt are free of charge (brandy, vodka, gin, whiskey, tequila, rum).





There are 4 restaurants on site. You can try the spicy and exotic dishes of Indian and Arabian cuisine at Masala. Restaurant Vivace (photo above) is open from half-past six in the evening until 23:00, you need to book tables in it in advance, in addition, there is a dress code, although not very strict (men are not allowed in cropped trousers and shorts). You can visit it only for an additional payment. For lovers of Japanese cuisine and seafood, Numa is open.

The Cascade restaurant (in the photo below) in Le Royale Royal Holiday Resort 5 * (Egypt) serves a buffet for breakfast (from seven in the morning until half past ten), lunch (from 12:30 to 15:00) and a long dinner from half past six to 22:00, as well as late snacks from 23:00 to midnight. Specializes in international and local cuisine. The all-inclusive system provides dishes for children and a diet menu. Alcohol is limited to locally made products: gin, vodka, rum, ouzo, tequila, whiskey, beer, brandy), and wine is also offered during dinner and lunch.





Bars: location and mode of operation

On the territory of the Le Royale Royal Holiday Resort 5 * hotel there are four bars. Free on an all-inclusive system, drinks are offered by a cozy restaurant on the terrace (from 10:00 to midnight). The Cin-Cin Bar is open right by the pool (from 9:00 to 22:00).

Located in the center of the water park, you can visit the Aqua bar, which is open from nine in the morning until sunset. Great place to enjoy the beautiful view after a long day. All guests of the hotel are free to use the beach located in the territory belonging to Sonesta Beach Resort, as well as a bar on it (from 9:00 to sunset).

Inside, Le Royale Royal Holiday Resort 5 * hotel has a 24-hour Lobby Lounge, its visit is paid and is available only to persons who have reached the age of eighteen.

Beach and transfer

The hotel Le Royal Holiday Resort (Sharm el-Sheikh) 5 * does not have its own beach, it is 500 meters away. Towels, sun loungers, mattresses and umbrellas are provided absolutely free of charge. The beach has a length of about 150 meters, it is sand and gravel, with corals, there is a pontoon and a pier. The number of sun loungers is limited, there are 75 of them, so it makes sense for all sea lovers to take places early. You can get there on foot or on a free bus that runs every hour from 9:00 AM to 3:00 PM.

Entertainment and sports





For its guests and guests, the hotel Le Royal Holiday Resort 5 stars (Sharm El Sheikh) organizes a large number of various events. A professional team of 7 animators works. Sports and entertainment contests and events, quizzes are held daily, live music is played, and once a week you will find a magnificent oriental show in the amphitheater.

Absolutely free of charge, each hotel guest can play volleyball on the equipped playground, billiards, table tennis, futsal, have fun with darts and, in the company of professional instructors, do aerobics (regular and water). Various water sports and diving are available at an additional cost.
