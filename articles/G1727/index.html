Hotel Isrotel Lagoona 4 * (Eilat, Israel): room description, service, reviews

In the southernmost part of Israel, in the city of Eilat, is a cozy family hotel Isrotel Lagoona 4 * (Eilat, Israel), offering an excellent vacation. The complex is in an ideal location near the beach, promenade, shops and restaurants. Timna Park and King Solomon's Mine are a 40-minute drive away. With the all inclusive concept offered at the hotel, the tourist pays only once. All meals, drinks, beer, ice cream and entertainment are included. Isrotel Lagoona 4 * (Eilat, Israel) is an ideal place for those who like to have fun with their family or friends, in a relaxed and informal atmosphere.

Acquaintance with Eilat

The city of Eilat is located in southern Israel in the northern part of the Gulf of Aqaba, on the one hand on the border with Egypt, and on the other with Jordan. Located 2 hours from the Dead Sea and about 4 hours from Jerusalem. The resort is ideal for exploring the sights of the Negev desert and Arava.





Eilat is also known as the original resort on the Red Sea and offers visitors a great pastime. Holidays in the city are characterized by an almost European mood with a Middle Eastern accent, with numerous retail outlets along the Red Sea, including an almost perfect climate, crystal clear sea, impressive landscapes, plentiful reefs for divers and underwater floating equipment for lovers of sea travel.

The resort is an area without VAT, making it an ideal place for shopping, as it offers many designer boutiques and several shopping centers.

A wide selection of restaurants with Israeli cuisine and delicious dishes from around the world means that food lovers will not stay hungry, and cafes, bars and clubs abound in goodies. The main walk around the resort of Eilat provides an opportunity to look at a number of shopping stalls offering local artisans and national souvenirs.

Tours in Eilat provide many opportunities for family entertainment. On the coast, in addition to beaches, there is a Dolphin Reef, where children can dive with semi-wild aquatic mammals. And there is also the opportunity to see many marine creatures of the coral world in the Underwater Observatory, under the waves of the Red Sea.

The city is easy to get around on foot, and most of the attractions are within walking distance of each other. For those who want to travel a little on vacation, taxis are cheap and there are many excursions in the vicinity, including trips to the desert and mountains - all this is guaranteed by tours to Eilat.

It rarely rains in the city, and the sun is almost guaranteed to shine year-round. Since the city is located in the desert, the air temperature in the evening in winter can be uncomfortable. Winters are very mild, daytime temperatures rarely drop below 20 degrees Celsius.

You can go to Eilat at any time of the year. The hottest weather in July and August is ideal for water sports and the beach, but if you want to take surface excursions, autumn, spring and winter are the best options for visiting the resort. The city becomes very crowded during Jewish holidays.

  4. Eilat Airport is 30 km away, transfer time is about 40 minutes.
