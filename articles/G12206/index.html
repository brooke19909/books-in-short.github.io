Pyramid on Novorizhskoe highway: myths and reality

From time immemorial, the pyramids have attracted with their scope during construction and the secrets that have accompanied buildings for many centuries.

The famous pyramids of the world

Myths and puzzles accompany these buildings, endowing them with a wide variety of properties. Unusual structures around the world attract huge attention. Russia was no exception in this matter. The pyramid on Novorizhskoe highway has become the main attraction of this area, attracting tourists at various times of the year. Every year, the flow of people wishing to see the building, which has become famous throughout the world, is increasing.

Where is the pyramid on Novorizhskoe highway? How to get there

One of the 17 pyramids of the famous Russian engineer Alexander Golod was erected in the Moscow region, at a distance of 38 km along the Novorizhskoye highway from the capital.

Moving westward, it is impossible to drive past it. She gracefully rises above the ground in an open field, attracting well-deserved attention to herself, because you do not expect to meet such a miracle in the vastness of Russia. Located near the village of Chesnokovo, this building is considered the highest pyramid in the CIS, its height is 44 m. The pyramid on Novorizhskoye Highway, made with the correct combination of plastic blocks and panels in 1999, does not have metal elements in the structure, but outside dusted with concrete chips.
You can get here by car or a comfortable sightseeing bus, where an experienced guide will tell you all about this pyramid, starting with the peculiarities of its construction and ending with facts about the miraculous effects on individuals and the country as a whole.

Pyramid on Novorizhskoe highway: reviews

Famous throughout the world, the Hunger Building attracts many tourists.
