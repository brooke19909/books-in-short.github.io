Tony Resort 3 * (Patong Beach, Thailand): hotel description, services, reviews

Hotel Tony Resort 3 * is suitable for those who love comfort and convenience, and also want to live near the hectic life of Patong, on the island of Phuket. This is one of the leaders among travelers in terms of price-quality ratio. The hotel is very popular, and in high season it is not so easy to find a place if you did not take care of booking in advance. In that article we will try to review the opinions of tourists about its services, location and comfort.



Patong beach

"Pattaya in miniature" - this is the name of this resort in Phuket. Indeed, there is practically everything here that people go to have fun on the mainland for. Young people from all over the world love Patong no less than Pattaya. Tony Resort 3 * (Phuket) is located in this center of entertainment. Their center is rightfully considered Bangla Road. Of course, there is a sex industry here, but families with children also come here. The atmosphere here is very tolerant, and any traveler feels comfortable and relaxed. The main daytime entertainment here, oddly enough for a Thai beach resort, is not the sea at all, but shopping. And the local discos are unparalleled. You can also go to a transvestite show, to such an exciting sports show as Thai boxing, or just go fishing.



Territory and location

The hotel is located very well, according to most tourists who left reviews. No more than five minutes of not very fast walking - and you will be taken to any iconic place in Patong. Bangla Road, Jungseilon Shopping Center - all this is just a few steps away. Go to the sea for about ten minutes. Nearby are many inexpensive cafes and shops, massage parlors and places where you can rent bikes cheaply. Since Tony Resort 3 * is located in the thick of Patong, it can not boast of a huge garden. Nevertheless, the hotel has three five-story buildings. There are also two cozy courtyards surrounded by perimeter buildings of the hotel, and from the windows of the most expensive rooms you can see a large swimming pool. Some buildings have internal passages to the restaurant. You can park your car at the hotel - tourists claim that it is completely safe. The reception is located in the lobby and is open 24 hours a day.



Hotel rooms

Hotel Tony Resort 3 * has one hundred and two rooms, decorated in a classic Thai style. They are very sophisticated and at the same time spacious. First of all, these are junior suites with amenities. The so-called triple room, or triple rooms, are very popular in this hotel. But the most luxurious are family suites. They are simply huge - over a hundred meters in area! Their highlight are double beds made of solid wood, such as kingsize. Each room has an equipped bathroom with cosmetics (replenished every day) and a hairdryer, safe, iron, electric kettle and ironing board, small refrigerator, satellite TV (with one Russian channel) and Internet access. Furniture, although not supernova, but very solid. Wardrobes are very large, with enough hangers. All rooms are air-conditioned and have large balconies.





Services

The staff of the Tony Resort 3 * hotel quickly relocates guests, takes into account their wishes and requests, if, of course, he has such an opportunity. Quickly respond to comments and answers all requests. You can bring ordered food to your room, meet at the airport or take it there. If it happened that you got to the hotel on your birthday, just notify the administration and you will be delivered a bouquet of exotic flowers or a fruit basket. Rooms are cleaned daily and bed linen is changed twice a week.

Nutrition

If you paid for your stay at this hotel, then breakfast is included in the price. Tourists call them more than sufficient. Although the composition of the dishes does not change, they are very diverse. Thai noodles or rice with chicken, pork or shrimp, sausages, omelets and fried eggs, lots of fruits - melon, pineapple, papaya, watermelon ... There are cereals with milk and sweet yogurt, tea and coffee. Hotel Tony Resort 3 * also has a very good restaurant "Red Wood". You can choose expensive restaurants or cheap cafes on the streets. But now food courts with moderate prices and the ability to pay with special cards are becoming more and more fashionable. So, you can eat at the Jungseilon or at the fruit market, not far from the hotel.



Sea and beaches

To get to the nearest beach, you need to turn left after leaving the hotel, go past the neighboring Viridian Hotel, and then focus on the huge tower of the Patong Resort resort complex. From it begins an alley that rests directly on a road running along the sea. But on the beach of Patong rarely sunbathe and swim, except in the first days of rest. Experienced travelers go to neighboring beaches - Kathu, Karon or other famous coasts with white fine sand.

In one of the courtyards of the Tony Resort 3 * hotel (Phuket), as already mentioned, there is a large pool, and in another - a small one. Despite the fact that the hotel is large and there are always a lot of people living in it, there are always enough places here and there are free sunbeds. It works until ten in the evening, but the staff will be loyal to the fact that you bathe later. Moreover, the guests of the triple room have the opportunity to go to the pool directly from the building through the internal passages. And on the first floors you can get to the artificial pond directly from the rooms. In the large pool there is a special children's area, and they clean it every day.



Where to go

When you enjoy both Patong Beach and the nearby coasts, try the daytime shopping and nightlife of these places, the time will come to explore Phuket itself. Guests at the Tony Resort Hotel often mention that the proximity to the center of Patong allowed them to book numerous trips to various places on the island. This is a sightseeing tour of Phuket with a visit to its capital, as well as temples, waterfalls, parks and museums and all kinds of elephant, ostrich, snake and tiger farms and zoos. This includes longer trips, for example, to Phang Nga Nature Reserve with a monkey temple, floating villages, hot springs and James Bond Island. Phuket will never bore you, and you will come back more than once, or at least strive for it.
