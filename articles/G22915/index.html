Yeysk - Rostov: what is the best way to get home from the resort

But in this article we will not describe the delights of local relaxation, but rather focus on the very practical question of what opportunities people who are going to leave home have to get from Yeysk to Rostov. Indeed, among the mass of tourists, trains and planes depart to their homelands precisely from this big city. It is sometimes said that during the holiday season all roads lead to the sea. But the time is coming when you should think about how best to go back. In this article, we have collected the tips of experienced travelers on this topic. Think about it in advance so as not to run into problems at the last moment. There are several options for such a return, and when choosing one of them, you should decide what you want on the road - convenience, speed, or both.



Car

Between the cities of Yeisk and Rostov, the distance is 183 kilometers if you are traveling by car. It will take about three hours to get there. There are many routes, but vacationers usually prefer two. The first one is along the federal highway M4 (Don). But there may be traffic jams in high season. Road works can also hinder rapid progress. You can try the second option. These are secondary roads - 250 and 268. They lead through the villages of Aleksandrovka and Peshkovo. But traffic on these roads is one-way, so it will be very difficult to overtake. And in that, and in another case, you will spend about eight hundred rubles for gasoline. You can also find a fellow traveler on the Bla-bla-car service. Then the road will cost you not a very large amount - starting from two hundred rubles per person. Some experienced drivers recommend a route through the village of Kushchevskaya and the city of Azov. The roads are not the worst, they can be safely called the domestic standard. If you suddenly get lost, do not hesitate to ask for directions. Local drivers will be happy to show you and tell you everything.



Train and plane

To overcome the Yeysk-Rostov route by rail in high-speed cars, you first need to get to Starominskaya station by bus or commuter train. From there, night and other long-distance trains already run. But if you do not mind a long journey under the sound of wheels on rails and at the same time do not like transfers, there is an option for you. This is the Yeysk-Moscow train at number 232. In summer, it runs every day, and its final stop is Kursky Station. But it also passes through Rostov. A train leaves from Yeysk at about ten in the morning, and it is better to buy a ticket for it in 45 days. Trailers used to go from St. Petersburg to the resort before. Now, after a day, train 245 runs. Therefore, you can still get to Rostov on it. But this option is not suitable for everyone. In addition, if you arrived at this resort not from the capital, but from distant regions of Russia - Yekaterinburg, Magadan, Murmansk, then it is better, of course, to get to Rostov Airport by bus. If you choose the option with an electric train, you will also have to change trains at the railway station.



Route Yeisk - Rostov: bus

This is one of the most popular and popular ways to get from the resort to the regional center. There are direct flights from Yeysk bus station to Rostov. Departing from the resort and passing buses. The schedule depends on the season: at the height of summer there are more flights, in winter and autumn - less. Travel time can also be different. It is necessary to take into account which settlements the bus passes through and how much it costs there. You can spend between three and a half to five hours on the road. The cost of such a trip starts at five hundred rubles per person. Minibuses also go in this direction in season - they set off to fill, but they go faster and make fewer stops.

The way from Yeysk to Rostov: train

Unfortunately, commuter trains cannot be reached without transfers. Therefore, the Starominskaya - Timashevsk junction station serves as a tourist aid. From there, the trains leave for Novorossiysk, Anapa, Adler. You can get to this transfer station by train, and then go on. However, commuter trains from Yeysk to Rostov rarely run, and you may not be able to pay with the connection. They go to Starominskaya an hour and a half, and a ticket costs 170 rubles. You can also get to Timashevsk by bus, and most vacationers do just that. All Rostov electric trains follow exactly to this station. The usual commuter trains and express trains go further from Starominskaya. An electric train ticket costs a little more than two hundred rubles, and it goes for more than two hours. Express spends an hour and forty minutes on the road. Tickets are purchased upon boarding.



Taxi and transfer

This is the most convenient, but completely non-budgetary way to get from Yeysk to Rostov. Taxis are generally an expensive form of transportation, not to mention the fact that you have to leave from the resort town. But you will rush "with the breeze" and without unnecessary worries, without worrying about either tickets or luggage. Cars in fleets are very different - from cars to minibuses to a large family or company. The trip takes about two and a half hours. The driver will pick you up directly from the place of rest and deliver you either to the station / airport or to the desired address in Rostov. Sometimes guest houses and private owners provide their guests with a service such as a transfer to Rostov or Starominskaya. Sometimes it is even included in the price.
