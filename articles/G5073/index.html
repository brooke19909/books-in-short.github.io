The main and additional ingredients of the marshmallow bouquet

Great if you are lucky to buy several different types of marshmallows. Tiny buns made of two halves, flat marshmallows coated with glaze, and marshmallows will come in handy. The main thing is that everything bought is harmoniously combined in color (however, usually there are no problems with this, because marshmallows are painted in delicate shades).

Preparation for work

An important rule: you need to work with gloves. This will not only protect the components from the point of view of hygiene, but also make the process more convenient. For example, if you form a bouquet of marshmallows and marmalade, your hands will soon become sticky, but this will not happen with dry gloves made of cellophane or latex.

In case of using fruits, rinse them thoroughly in advance, put them on napkins, and let them dry. Cut large fruits in half or slices. To prevent the surface from drying out and weathering, you can use one confectionery trick: carefully cover the slices with a thin layer of dense jelly.

Put the marshmallows and other elements of the future composition on skewers and lay on a table covered with cling film.

The formation of a bouquet

To make a beautiful bouquet of marshmallows with your own hands will help the way florists use it. You need to start from the center, laying large elements in a spiral. Skewers must converge and cross at one point. To prevent the bouquet from scattering, periodically fix them with tape.





The gaps between large elements can be filled, for example, with sprigs of young mint.

Decor

When the bouquet seems to be sufficient in size, make a strong control fixation with adhesive tape. Use a garden pruner to trim the protruding lower parts of the skewers. Wrap the place for which you will hold the bouquet with several layers of film, foil or fabric.

Using a thin tape, fix the decorative packaging of the bouquet, and then cover the tape with ribbons, lace or braid.

Important Rules

Assembling a sweet bouquet of marshmallows, like any other edible gift, should occur in exceptional purity. In order not to get into an awkward position, carefully tidy your hair before starting the creative process.

Think about how appropriate your gift will be. Does the giftee love sweets, does he have allergies or contraindications? Will he understand this decision, will he be delighted? A person with conservative views may simply not understand what the food in the package of flowers does and what to do next.

You should not deal with the design of such a gift long before the holiday, it is advisable to do everything on the day of the celebration or at least the day before. Think in advance about how you will transport the bouquet.





Storage and transportation

A bouquet of marshmallows is a delicate and fragile creation, it will not tolerate rudeness. To reduce the atmospheric effects on fruit cuts and on marshmallows, immediately after completing work, gently wrap the bouquet with cling film or simply put a clean plastic bag on it. Prior to delivery, such a gift should be stored in a cool place, where it will in no case be crowded. It is not recommended to remove protective material during transportation. If possible, pack the bouquet in a box, because the recipient may also have to transport it.
