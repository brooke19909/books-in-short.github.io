Royal Azur Resort 5 * (Egypt, Makadi Bay): hotel description, reviews of tourists

No matter how beautiful the place you choose for your vacation is, the hotel leaves the main impression. Having preferred Royal Azur Resort 5 *, you can be sure that after visiting Hurghada you will have the best and fond memories of your vacation.

General Hotel Information

If you want to find the Royal Azur Resort 5 * hotel on a map of Hurghada, you will find that it is located on the shores of the cleanest Makadi Bay, which is considered the most environmentally friendly and famous for its pristine coral reefs. The distance from Hurghada Airport to the hotel is 25 km, but if you want to visit the city itself, then you will have to drive for 30 km.

Hotel guests will certainly appreciate the well-kept sandy beach, as well as the opportunity to enjoy underwater beauties and get acquainted with marine life. The hotel infrastructure is very developed. Here, each client will find exactly what he needs. The staff of Royal Azur Resort 5 * for their guests will open the doors of 501 rooms with a modern interior and all necessary amenities.

Accommodation with animals

One of the most interesting features of the Royal Azur Resort 5 * hotel is the possibility of checking into a room in the company of your pets, which is most prohibited in most other hotels. At the same time, no separate additional payments for this service will have to be made. Nevertheless, this feature implies the observance of certain prerequisites:

  • the body weight of the pet with which you intend to move into the room should not exceed 5 kg;
  • if the animal is in the room during its cleaning, you also should not leave your apartment until the maid finishes work;
  • no matter what variety or breed your pet is, you must always drive it on a leash;
  • You agree to clean up after your own animals throughout the hotel;
  • at the time of booking you must inform about your intention to arrive at the hotel with your pet;
  • during check-in, all guests without fail sign a special agreement allowing the pet to stay in the room, as well as prescribing all the basic conditions and subtleties.

Thanks to the possibility of settling with animals, many tourists prefer this hotel.

Beaches and Pools

The main purpose of the arrival of tourists in Egypt is to rest by the sea. Ample opportunities in this regard are provided by the Royal Azur Resort 5 * hotel. Reviews of tourists who previously visited this hotel complex, especially noted a clean well-groomed beach and a huge transparent pool.

For starters, you should pay attention to the sea coast. The hotel has its own sandy beach, the territory of which is fully refined and regularly cleaned. The distance from the hotel to the coast is about 400 meters. The length of the beach is as much as 320 meters. The following features are worth noting:

  • the beach is guarded around the clock;
  • Absolutely free of charge towels, chaise lounges, umbrellas and air mattresses are provided to you;
  • if you decide to inspect the coral reefs, you will be provided with special slippers to protect the skin of the legs from injury;
  • the entrance to the water and the swimming area are completely covered with sand and have no stones.

Despite the fact that Makadi Bay is considered one of the most environmentally friendly, many tourists prefer to swim in the pool. Hotel "Royal Azur Resort 5 *" (Makadi Bay) provides all the necessary conditions for this. In the daytime, 4 swimming pools are open for visiting:

  • the main pool is the largest - its area is as much as 1800 square meters; in the cold season, the water in it is heated;
  • the second largest pool (1200 sq. m) is filled with clean sea water from Makadi Bay;
  • right next to the family building, on the quietest and most peaceful part of the hotel, there is a 450 square meter pool;
  • for the youngest guests of this resort there is a small children's pool (45 sq. m) with slides and attractions.
