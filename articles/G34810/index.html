Sberbank replenished deposit - remote piggy bank

One of the best offers for those who want to receive passive income and save their money in an organization such as Sberbank is a replenished deposit. This deposit is available to everyone, because you can open it with a minimum deposit to the account. It is enough to start with 1000 rubles, and additional amounts can be transferred regularly, as well as receive accumulated interest on your savings.

Customers have the opportunity to choose the capitalization of interest or immediately withdraw them to their account. The term may be minimal - from 3 months, but the replenished deposit of Sberbank can be placed for 3 years. It will become a comfortable solution for accumulating the right amount by a certain date.

Terms of growing income on a growing deposit

Sberbank's replenished deposit allows you not only to keep your money safe from yourself and, of course, from intruders, but also to receive income from the fact that you leave them for a certain period in the bank.

The interest rate depends on a number of reasons: from the type of currency chosen to the amount and timing of the deposit. In dollars and euros, you can get an increase of 0.4-2.05% per year, and in rubles - from 4.6 to 7.28% per annum.
