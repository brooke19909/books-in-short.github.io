Montenegro Hotel, Montenegro, Budva Riviera, Becici: reviews. Montenegro Beach Resort 4 *

Montenegro is a favorite place for our tourists to relax. In search of new experiences, travelers come here tired of the resorts of Turkey or Egypt. Montenegrin resorts are fond of first-class service, clear sea and interesting entertainments. Choosing a ticket to Becici, pay attention to the small hotel "Montenegro" (Montenegro, Budva). It is perfect for a quiet holiday with the whole family and will not cost a lot of money.

Hotel location

The hotel is located on the east coast of the Adriatic Sea in the southern part of Montenegro. It is built right on the beach of Becici. This is one of the most popular resorts in the country, which is famous for its magnificent beaches. By the way, they are considered the most beautiful in Europe. The private pebble beach is just a 2-minute walk from the 4 * Montenegro Beach Resort. 2 km from the complex is the ancient city of Budva, which is famous for its restaurants and shops, as well as numerous attractions. Around the city formed the famous tourist area - the Budva Riviera (Becici), whose hotels are considered the best in the whole country.





You can get to the hotel from the nearest Tivat airport, which is 20 kilometers from Becici. If you get to the complex from Podgorica or Dubrovnik, you will have to overcome the distance of 60-65 kilometers. The administration can arrange a paid transfer from the airport to its guests. Hotel "Montenegro" (Montenegro) is surrounded by developed infrastructure. There are many shops and restaurants within 10-20 meters from here. Nearby is also the tourist complex "Mediteran", which has its own water park. Paying a visit to it can and vacationers from other hotels.

General information

The hotel opened its doors to tourists in the distant 1970, when the Adriatic coast was just starting to be built up. The last complete reconstruction of the building took place in 2008, and room repair was carried out in 2011. In 2006, Montenegro won the competition and became the best hotel in Montenegro according to tourists. On the territory of the Hotel Montenegro Beach Resort there is one five-story building, in which there are almost 200 rooms. Check-in at the hotel no earlier than 14:00. Check-in takes place until midnight. And you need to leave your room at the end of the trip before 12:00.





The complex is focused on families with children, whose accommodation is allowed at any age. There are all necessary conditions for them, and the hotel itself offers good discounts for the smallest guests. And the animals will have to be left at home. It is strictly forbidden to post them here. The hotel staff speaks fluent English, and some of them also speak Russian. The tourist season begins here in April and ends in mid-October. However, the best time to relax in Montenegro will be the summer months.

Number of rooms

In the main building of the hotel there are 190 rooms. Most of them are standard, but there are 18 connected rooms, as well as 9 family apartments. The interior of the rooms is made in a classic style. The walls are painted in beige and the furniture is made of light wood. Dark carpeted floors. The room also has bedside tables, a dining table and chairs, a mirror and a table for toiletries. The standard room is 20 square meters. m, and the enlarged apartments occupy almost 45 square meters. m

The rooms are decorated with wall photographs decorated in wooden frames. They depict landscapes of Montenegro resorts. There are modern table lamps on the tables, and cozy sconces hang on the walls. Sometimes in the room you can find fresh flowers. All apartments have their own small balcony. Some rooms have sea and mountain views. Hotel Montenegro (Montenegro) can provide them with special non-smoking rooms upon request.





Room Amenities

Guests can count not only on good furniture in the room, but also on comfortable living conditions. To do this, all apartments are equipped with all sorts of devices that maximize tourists' relaxation. We list the main amenities that guests can use:

  • Large plasma TV opposite the bed. Users can choose from more than 100 cable channels, including in Russian.
  • Each room has its own bathroom. For guests there are terry bathrobes, slippers, cosmetic accessories (shampoo, shower gel, body cream). There is also a hairdryer, towels.
  • Personal air conditioning, as well as in cold weather, the Montenegro Hotel (Becici, Montenegro) includes a heating system.
  • Free safe for valuables.
  • There is a free fridge equipped with a minibar, but can only be refilled at an additional cost.
  • Direct telephone for round-the-clock communication with the administration.
  • Daily quality housekeeping. Towels, bathrobes and bed linen are changed twice a week.

All about hotel catering

When ordering a tour, tourists can choose a certain type of food. Hotel Montenegro (Montenegro) offers its guests 3 types: half board, full board and all-inclusive system. Travelers are advised to take just the last look, because eating outside the hotel is expensive. At the distribution in the restaurant for tourists offer several types of soups and cereals, fresh fruits and vegetables, various types of fish and meat. Pastries, white and red wine, juices for children, instant coffee are also served. Sometimes guests can try seafood delicacies (mussels, octopuses, shrimps). The cuisine is mainly European, but there are also dishes prepared according to Italian and Montenegrin recipes. Food is not strewn with pepper and spices, so it can be given to small children.





The hotel also has several bars and restaurants for guests:

  • The main restaurant serves a buffet for breakfast, lunch and dinner. Spacious establishment with 400 seats.
  • Restaurant "Galia", serving √† la carte customers. Tourists can try the national dishes of Montenegro, fish and meat delicacies. It is located near the beach.
  • Cafe "Milka", located outdoors in the garden. Guests can enjoy homemade cakes, pastries and other pastries, as well as delicious ice cream.
  • Bar "Aqua", located by the pool. It sells soft non-alcoholic and alcoholic drinks, cocktails, ice cream and light snacks.
  • Lobby bar where tourists can have a light salad, pizza, drink tea with pastries.

Service and Facilities

Any tourist complex seeks to offer its guests the most comfortable living conditions. Of course, in addition to the amenities in the rooms, it is also important to provide them with good infrastructure so that they do not need anything during their vacation. For this, the Montenegro Beach Resort hotel (Montenegro) has prepared the following range of services for its guests:

  • Free Wi-Fi for guests throughout the hotel.
  • Own parking, located directly at the main building. It is free, and tourists can park their car here at any time. Car rental is available for guests.
  • A 24-hour ATM, from which you can withdraw money without a high commission.
  • At the reception you can hand over your luggage to the left luggage office so that large bags do not take up space in the room.
  • Laundry and dry cleaning for guests who can order laundry, ironing, and shoe shine.
  • Own business center, which can offer the services of three conference rooms. The largest of them can accommodate up to 150 people. All rooms are equipped with modern computer technology. Hotel Montenegro Beach Resort also offers photocopying and fax services.
  • The hotel does not allow the use of tobacco products, but there are special smoking areas.
  • On the way to the beach there is a store selling clothes, shoes, beach items. There is also a souvenir stall.

Leisure activities for guests

Hotel "Montenegro" (Becici, Montenegro) can offer its guests a variety of activities that are mainly focused on a quiet and relaxing holiday in the company of children. We list the most popular types of leisure:

  • A treatment center that offers guests to undergo various cosmetic procedures. This massage, jacuzzi, Turkish bath, sauna, solarium. There is also a beauty salon on the territory of the center.
  • Evening animation program, holding discos, including in a night club.
  • Two pools: one in the fresh air, and the other is indoors. Free sun loungers and towels are provided.
  • The bar has the opportunity to sing karaoke.
  • Playgrounds for table tennis and a billiard room.
  • Water sports equipment is available on the beach.
  • Fitness club and gym equipped with modern equipment.




Children's holiday

Hotel "Montenegro" (Montenegro) is focused on families with children, so there is plenty of entertainment for the youngest guests. For their leisure in the complex there are the following objects:

  • kids club;
  • playroom;
  • television channels for babies in the room;
  • shallow pool;
  • swing in the open air;
  • children's library and board educational games.




The hotel also provides a cot for guests with small children, while the restaurant provides free highchairs for them. Special meals can be created for kids, and parents can always hire a qualified nanny for an extra fee.

Positive reviews

On the Montenegrin coast there are several dozens of hotels of various kinds and cost. It is sometimes very difficult to choose one of them, so tourists resort to the help of other travelers, learning their opinions. Visitors to Montenegro Beach Resort 4 * leave mostly positive reviews. We list the main advantages:

  • the beach is within walking distance, there are always free sunbeds, the staff monitors and does not let strangers into its territory;
  • the food is varied, every day they try to cook something new;
  • friendly staff who speaks good Russian; rooms are also cleaned very clean;
  • well-groomed hotel grounds, everywhere trees, flowers and shrubs;
  • free wifi works everywhere and without interruption;
  • The hotel offers beautiful views of the sea or mountains.




Negative reviews

"Montenegro" is a hotel, reviews of which are usually positive. But there are some disadvantages that can overshadow the rest. We list the main ones:

  • the carpets in the rooms are poorly cleaned, there is a lot of dust;
  • small and cramped showers that are not suitable for tall and full people;
  • Russians are not accommodated in the best rooms, while foreigners get the best apartments;
  • every morning guests are awakened by checking the minibar;
  • instead of juice, a low-quality instant drink is served for breakfast.

Who should go to relax at this hotel?

Many tourist complexes can offer Montenegro. Hotel "Montenegro", whose prices can be called low, stands out among them with a good quality of service. For two adults, 5 days of rest in this hotel will cost from 25 thousand rubles (breakfast only, low season). For tourists with children, this hotel will be a great option, and young people, on the contrary, can be bored here. Also, this quiet hotel can be advised to elderly couples or people who love a calm and measured rest on the sea.
