Vogue Hotel Bodrum (Torba, Turkey): description and reviews of tourists

Vogue Hotel Bodrum is a suburban beach resort, one of which Turkey is famous for. The concept of such hotels is that they use their location in order to protect vacationers from the hustle and bustle. Therefore, most of what tourists usually need, they can purchase on site. This hotel is famous for its high-class service, well-trained, professional and unobtrusive staff, as well as attention to all the needs of guests. The hotel is located on the shores of the Aegean, in the midst of cliffs and forests, the air is filled with healing scents, and the windows of elegant rooms offer stunning views.



Torba

It is in this village that the Vogue Hotel Bodrum is located. The miniature resort is not too hyped, and only lovers of the Aegean Sea mostly know about it. There are not so many hotels and, accordingly, few tourists. Therefore, Torba attracts vacationers with peace and silence. Cliffs overgrown with pine trees and the sea favorably set off the unusual architecture of the local hotel complexes, which are often built by the best Turkish architects, especially when such famous brands as Vogue are customers.

How to get there

If your trip does not include a shuttle service and you travel on your own, then in order to get to the hotel you need to get to Bodrum. Vogue Hotel Bodrum 5 * is easily accessible on a dolmush which go here with enviable regularity. However, they run only in the daytime. At night you will have to take a taxi. Most flights from Russia arrive at Dalaman Airport, and from there the transfer takes about two hours. Havash buses go to Bodrum about the same time.



What is next to the hotel and where is it located

Tourists rated Vogue Hotel Bodrum very well for its location. On the one hand, the hotel is located right on the beach, and on the other, it is not too far from all sorts of interesting places and attractions. If vacationers have little entertainment at the hotel, they can always find something for themselves within walking distance. Major shopping centers and sights of Bodrum - the castle, Barov street and so on, are 6-7 kilometers from the hotel, and it is easy to get there by bus.

Territory

Hotel Vogue Hotel Bodrum 5 * (Turkey, Bodrum) is surrounded by a large, beautiful and well-groomed park, turning into a pine forest. There is so much to do here that getting around all these amazingly designed corners will not work even in a few days. Tourists write that during evening walks they were in a different place each time. It is amazingly beautiful and pleasant to walk. From everywhere magnificent views open. The park is interspersed with beautiful villas of various sizes. Many of them have their own pool. The hotel has a parking lot. Those who wish to organize a seminar or conference there can use the services of a business center and four specially equipped halls. On the territory you can ride bicycles, which is widely used by both children and adults.



Rooms

It is difficult to name the premises where the guests of the hotel are lodging, except as chic. Even the most budget rooms - Superior - are very comfortable and have a large area. Three people can freely accommodate there. All other rooms are Deluxe. Vogue Hotel Bodrum 5 * offers guests suites where four guests can stay at once. There are living and sleeping rooms. Penthouses are similar in comfort. Family Suites have two bedrooms. Garden Bungalows are one-room, but very much in demand by lovers or people seeking solitude. And for large companies or families, the Royal Suite is offered, where six can live right away. It has three bedrooms, a living area, 3 bathrooms, and a sauna with a jacuzzi. Each room has a full range of equipment and services that rely on in a five-star hotel: bathrobes and slippers, a flat-screen TV with cable channels, air conditioning, a safe, a large balcony and, of course, Internet access. The tourists characterize the rooms as chic. The beds are huge, there is a sofa and a desk. Wardrobes are large and with a sufficient number of coat hanger. The minibar is replenished daily with sprite, fanta, cola, mineral water and three types of juices. There is a bathroom and shower. Also, there are a lot of small things that are convenient for life - napkins, bathing caps, toothbrushes and pastes, creams, including shaving, nail files, combs ... In a word, you can not take all this from home. The cleaning is perfect, tourists after arriving from the beach were happy that everything smells clean and tasty.

Service and entertainment

Vogue Hotel Bodrum places a lot of emphasis on sports. Three tennis courts, a five-a-side football field, a volleyball court, the opportunity to play badminton and darts - this is not a complete list of activities of this kind. Daytime animation offers guests to do yoga, aerobics, water gymnastics, play polo in the pools. Do you want to relax during the holidays and take care of your face, body and skin? It features an excellent spa with massage and beauty treatments. There is also a sauna and a hammam, a Roman bath, a rain room and a bar where fresh drinks are brought to you. Staff

As the reviews about Vogue Hotel Bodrum 5 * assure, the hotel staff are very friendly and know their job. The waiters are quick, clean the dishes instantly and quickly set the tables. All arising questions are resolved quickly, politely and with smiles. There are special employees who are called hotel guides. They orient new arrivals in everything related to the hotel and its infrastructure. Animation is very unobtrusive - if you want to have fun actively, come, ask, participate. Nobody will pull you by the hands. Hotel workers are calm, seasoned, competent. Many of them speak Russian. Invited specialists also organize educational seminars and trainings for young guests and issue them certificates. If you dial 555 from your room, you will be answered in Russian - an interpreter for clients is provided.

Excursions
