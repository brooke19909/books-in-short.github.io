Jordan, all-inclusive Red Sea hotels: reviews, photos

Anyone who wants to be in the enchanting atmosphere of the East, who wants to visit a real kingdom, must go to Jordan. In this mysterious ancient country, civilization could not violate the charm and identity. Its shores are washed by the mysterious Dead and full of unusual underwater beauties of the Red Sea.

The city of Petra and the Wadi Rum desert, magnificent nature and beautiful beaches, unique cuisine and luxurious hotels - all this awaits tourists who decide to go on vacation to Jordan. In this article, we will recommend all-inclusive Jordan hotels in the Red Sea to tourists. Photos of the best of them you can see below.



Climatic conditions

The country has a dry, continental climate. In January, the temperature does not exceed +15 ¬∞ C, and in July it reaches +30 ¬∞ C. The most favorable time to visit the hospitable Kingdom of Jordan is autumn and spring.

Aqaba

An important seaport of the country on the Red Sea, a modern resort city. The coast of Aqaba stretches for twenty-seven kilometers. Within the city, all beaches are paid, so most tourists prefer to relax outside the city. Even in winter, the temperature in this town does not drop below +22 ¬∞ C.

In Aqaba, divers like to relax. Undoubtedly, it is difficult to find a more beautiful place where you can go scuba diving than the Red Sea. There are all conditions for diving. This resort has not so much religious Islamic influence (in comparison with other cities), so women can dress a little easier here.



This is a very original country - Jordan. All-inclusive Red Sea hotels are mainly concentrated in Aqaba. We will introduce you to some of them today.

Jordan, all-inclusive Red Sea hotels: Movenpick Resort 5 *

The main building of this luxury hotel is located across the road from the beach, but vacationers who want to get to the sea do not even need to go outside - they can pass through a special overpass. The hotel is famous for several wonderful restaurants, heated pools, one of which is located on the roof of the hotel. There is also its own gym, free sauna and SPA center.

All rooms are equipped with modern technology. There is a bath or shower. The rooms are bright and airy. Some of them overlook the Akaba (Eilat) Gulf. The beach is small, but characterized by a smooth sandy entrance to the Red Sea. Umbrellas and sunbeds are provided free of charge.



Crowne Plaza 5 *

Many 5 star all-inclusive Jordan Red Sea hotels can offer guests quiet rooms. This is important if guests prefer a relaxing family vacation. This hotel provides services such as a jacuzzi, an outdoor pool (heated). It offers a steam bath, a fitness center, a swimming pool and a sauna.



The cozy and bright rooms have a minibar and a bathroom. In the morning, breakfast is served at the hotel.

Tala Bay Resort

This small, but very attractive resort for lovers of a relaxing holiday, is a suburb of Aqaba. It is located just twenty kilometers from it. The small and clean village of Tala Bay does not have large shopping areas and entertainment complexes. There are no noisy night parties, this is a place for secluded relaxation.

This resort is famous for its crystal clear water and the bottom, strewn with incredibly beautiful and bright corals. There is no city noise and fuss; the village is built up with small pensions and cottages for demanding tourists. If you are interested in all-inclusive 5 star Jordan hotels in the Red Sea, the last minute packages offered by many travel agencies should be of interest to you. The fact is that by buying such a ticket, you will save up to 30% on the cost of living.



If you are going to relax in this picturesque village, but certainly want to stay in a five-star hotel, then pay attention to the modern complex, which we will describe below.

Sas Tala Bay 5 *

The hotel is not in the village itself, but very close to it. It was opened in the summer of 2008. The complex offers vacationers spacious and comfortable rooms, excellent service, responsive staff, a warm atmosphere, a large selection of entertainment.



The refined rooms are decorated in pleasant warm colors and feature functional and comfortable furniture. The hotel has 336 rooms. It has a magnificent sandy beach, located just fifty meters from the main building.

All Inclusive Red Sea Hotels in Jordan: With Kids on Vacation

Rest in this sunny country will surely be remembered by many vacationers. Tourists are interested in the question: is Jordan suitable for families with young children? All-inclusive Red Sea hotels have everything you need for a relaxing little guest.

When traveling to the kingdom with your children, you should ask for information about cots for babies if you are traveling with a child under two years of age. Hotels of 4 * and above provide such a service for free. 3 * hotels set a cot for a reasonable fee.

Kempinski Ishtar 5 *

This is a new, modern hotel. His first phase met his guests in May 2006, the next in the summer of 2007. This is a real hotel-palace, made in the style of the hanging gardens of Babylon. It includes three complexes - the main building, Ishtar Building and Ishtar Villas.



The hotel is located among the mighty olives. It has 318 magnificent suites and rooms. 201 is located in the main building. 117 rooms are located in the Ishtar complex.

This modern hotel is located in the center of Aqaba. It is perfect for relaxing with your family. It offers spacious and comfortable rooms. Each has a minibar and bathrooms. InterContinental 5 *

Great for families with children Jordan. All-inclusive Red Sea hotels are distinguished by thoughtful service for families with children. InterContinental 5 * hotel is a part of the famous worldwide network. It offers guests a warm, welcoming atmosphere and a comfortable stay on the Red Sea.

It offers - comfortable rooms, a modern spa, a varied diet. The hotel is suitable for lovers of a relaxing holiday. It is a ten minute walk from the center of Aqaba and a fifteen minute drive from King Hussein Airport.



Number of rooms

Hotel Intercontinental offers spacious rooms with a pleasant interior, comfortable furniture and all the necessary amenities:

  • Standart rooms - one-room rooms equipped with two single beds or one double. Rooms feature balconies.

Hilton Aqaba 5 *

Another hotel belonging to the world famous chain, which is located in the center of Aqaba. He began to receive guests in 2011. The hotel has 179 rooms: standard and deluxe. The creators were inspired by Italian design. Guests are offered to stay in spacious rooms equipped with modern appliances and excellent furniture. It offers stunning views of the Red Sea or the city. At the service of vacationers:

  • air conditioning;
  • high speed internet;
  • mini bar.

For the kids, the hotel has an excellent playground, the restaurants offer a special children's menu, and cots and high chairs are available in the rooms on request.

Marina Plaza 4 *

This hotel is located in the Jordanian resort village of Tala Bay. It is managed by Orascom Development, a world-famous company that is one of the leaders in elite design. The hotel consists of six two- and three-story buildings, which are distinguished by coziness and comfort.

Almost all rooms offer scenic views of the beautiful marina and mountains, towering on the other side of the Gulf of Aqaba. Guests of the complex use the Beach Club, which is located very close to the hotel. The entrance is free. On the beach there is a bar, restaurant, pool. Umbrellas, chaise lounges for vacationers are free. The entrance to the sea is rocky, the beach is sandy.
