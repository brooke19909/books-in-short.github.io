The timing

What is the recovery time for paper? They may vary. As practice shows, restoration often takes about a month. It is worth noting that for this period insurance companies issue a temporary policy. It allows you to receive free medical care while a permanent document is being issued.

For adults

A man has lost a medical insurance policy. How to restore it? It is worth noting that a huge role is played by who the applicant is. For each category of citizens there is a specific order of treatment. It generally converges, but there are still differences in the documents presented.



At the moment, adult citizens who permanently reside in the territory of the Russian Federation must bring with them:

  • SNILS (required from 2016);
  • identity card (namely a passport);
  • an application for the restoration of a document indicating its type.

If a person for some reason does not bring a civil passport, but a different type of identity card, then they may additionally be asked to provide certificates confirming registration. Children

Was a medical policy lost? How to restore it, if we are talking about minor citizens? For them, the procedure for contacting the insurance company is somewhat complicated. More precisely, you will have to try to bring the idea to life without any problems.

The first thing to keep in mind is that the applicant must be the legal representative of the child. Usually they are one of the parents. He writes a statement in the prescribed form on his behalf. And at the same time indicates information about his child.

What about the documents? The list of papers that insurance companies ask for when restoring an MHI policy for children is as follows:

  • minor birth certificate;
  • SNILS of the child (a mandatory item since 2016);
  • statement in the established form;
  • passport of the legal representative of the child;
  • Documents indicating the presence of a registration with the baby (optional).

These are not all the nuances that have to be taken into account. If we are talking about a teenager, then he has the right to contact the insurance company on his own. Persons over 14 years old must attach a passport to the application for restoration of the policy. This is a required item.

For foreigners

Foreign citizens are also entitled to receive a compulsory medical insurance policy. And they also have the right to restore documents. To do this, you will have to contact the insurance company with a specific list of securities.



Which specifically? In order to figure out how to restore a medical policy, foreign citizens will need to bring:

  • passport of a foreign citizen;
  • application for restoration of the document;
  • residence permit (if any);
  • certificate of registration on the territory of the Russian Federation (any evidence of legal stay);
