What is a roller pen?

A rollerball pen is the best kind of ballpoint pen. Its peculiarity lies in the fact that the ball is narrower. Water based inks are less viscous. Therefore, the line on paper is more even and clear. Such a pen practically writes itself; it does not require a strong pressure on the paper during writing. Also, these pens have a more economical ink consumption, and it ranges from 3,000 to 10,000 m.

Elite Pen Parker

Like all the best in our world, Parker began with need. George Safford Parker, 17 years old, began working at the Janesville, Wisconsin Telegraph School. The salary that he received was small, which is why he took up additional work. Parker became an agent for selling fountain pens. At that time they were not of high quality. There were many complaints and returns.

George began repairing pens on his own. And, as it turned out, he did it very well. Seeing from within all the flaws of these pens, he designed his model. And already in 1889, the first Parker fountain pen appeared .

The company was officially registered in 1892, and since then Parker has been synonymous with prestige and undeniable quality. Today this company produces the best products of this category in the world. The Parker rollerball pen is always in the hands of high-ranking officials and prosperous businessmen.

Since 1931, Parker has developed and launched the new Quink ink, which is easy to wash off. The writing unit will provide a neat and beautiful line in all conditions. Such a rollerball pen will not let you down in the heat or cold, and even on the run you will record in any conditions.
