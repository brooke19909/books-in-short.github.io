Children's railway in Yaroslavl: description, prices, how to get, history

The railway for children is a fun ride from the Soviet past. Unfortunately, in modern times it has not survived everywhere. But in Yaroslavl there is a children's railway! And we will try in this material to answer all the exciting questions about her.

Yaroslavl children's railway at present

The attraction is located in the Zavolzhsky district of the city in Yaroslavsky Bor. The railway with a total length of 5.7 km consists of 3 stations and 2 platforms:

  • Station "Yakovlevskaya".
  • Station "Pioneer".
  • Station "Meadow"
  • Platform "Green".
  • Platform "Anniversary".

Why is she a child? Because boys and girls work here! They make a schedule, manage trains, work with passengers. Every young Yaroslavl can participate in this interesting business - the first year of study in the chosen direction takes place at the school, four more years - at a specialized training center. And in the summer - the long-awaited practice on the children's railway of Yaroslavl.





If you decide to visit this attraction, then do not ignore the Museum of Extraordinary Travels, opened in 2001 at the Pionerskaya station. There are three halls, the exposition of which is dedicated to the railway history of the region from the middle of the XIX century:

  • The first hall is a real nineteenth century station! There is a platform, a station keeper, and a special bell.
  • The second hall is a passenger car decorated in the spirit of the last century.
  • And the third hall is an amazing 5D attraction, a modern cockpit!

The cost of a ticket to the museum is 150 rubles. Interesting excursion programs are also held at 11 h 10 min, 14 h 10 min, 14 h 40 min. But be careful - if a nonresident group arrives, then excursions for other visitors will be canceled. All details can be found directly at the "Pioneer".

History

Let's dive into the history of the children's railway in Yaroslavl, a photo of which is also presented here.

It was first built in 1946 - the paths walked along the banks of the Volga River. The length was almost the same as in modern times - 5.5 km. At this distance, there were 3 stations - Rodina, Pobeda and Volga. The children's road was run by two steam locomotives and five wagons. But, unfortunately, it did not delight the guys for long - in 1958, the Gorky reservoir was flooded, as a result of which the Volga shores were eroded. Traffic on the children's road could not be restored.





A modern attraction opened in Yakovlevsky Bor 17. 04. 1970. Initially, there were three stops - Lugovaya, Pionerskaya and Zelenaya. On the route 2 diesel locomotives and 4 trailers ran.

In 2009, a serious reconstruction of the attraction was made. Today it consists of 3 stations and 2 platforms. Depot is located on the "Pioneer". A modern self-locking system has been introduced, a simulator has been installed to train drivers, a layout of the highway has been drawn up.

Visitor Information

The attraction in Yaroslavl operates according to this schedule:

  • May: holidays, Saturday and Sunday.
  • June-August: Thursday-Sunday.
  • September: Saturday and Sunday.
  • August-April: not functioning.




Now consider the prices for the children's railway in Yaroslavl (the price for a ticket is shown in only one direction):

  • Adult (for passengers from 10 years old) - 150 rubles.
  • Children (children 2-10 years old) - 100 rubles.
  • Toddlers under 2 years old - no fare.

Tickets can be purchased directly at the Pionerskaya station (Yakovlevskaya is located nearby). You can pay both in cash and by credit card.

How to get there

How to get to the children's railway in Yaroslavl? By personal transport you need to move towards Third Yakovlevskaya, 54. The main thing is to remember to turn right to Dachnaya street.

If you decide to choose public transport, then you need to get off at the Yakovlevskaya stop. This item will take you to:

  • Bus No. 23, 14, 121a, 140, 33, 15, 40k, 139 (139a).
  • Minibus number 90, 86, 140, 84.




Children's Yaroslavl railway is not only a fun attraction, but also a place of summer practice for young railway workers. In addition, the Museum of Unusual Travels operates on its basis, which will be of interest to both children and adults. But before visiting, we recommend that you check with the information provided in the community of the Yaroslavl children's railway "Vkontakte".
