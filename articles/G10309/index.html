Waterpark "Victoria", Samara: address, opening hours, reviews

In the city of Samara is the largest water entertainment complex in Russia. In terms of size, it is perhaps not inferior to samples of world centers of this type. Since its opening, it has become the most popular family vacation spot among the townspeople. Therefore, if a local resident is asked the question ‚ÄúWhere can one go with a child in Samara?‚Äù, He will undoubtedly answer that he will go to the Victoria water park. Here you can have fun regardless of age and fully enjoy the water adventures that await all visitors who come to this complex. We learn a lot about this place from the article below.

General information about the water complex

Many visitors to the city are interested in how many water parks there are in Samara. One can answer this question, but this water complex is a real pride of the country, as it is considered the largest in Europe. The water park "Victoria" (Samara) is a closed type and was built back in 2001. It is located in the shopping and entertainment center "Moscow". Its territory occupies about seven thousand square meters, on which there are eleven slides: from small and smooth to high, with sharp turns, nine different pools of different depths and more than thirty exciting attractions.



The premises of this complex are decorated in the style of a rainforest. Here you can see artificial rocks, mysterious caves, waterfalls, grottoes and rivers. Such a design enhances the sensations of exoticism, which gives visitors the opportunity to experience the charm of being on any equatorial island.

Aquapark "Victoria" (Samara) is considered a worthy competitor to similar European water complexes, since on its territory there is everything necessary for a complete and comfortable stay. There is convenient parking, a large dressing room, cells for storing personal belongings of visitors, a medical office, as well as rescue and security services.

Advantages of the water complex

One of the main advantages possessed by the Victoria Water Park (Samara) is that it is open to visitors all year round, so even in the cold winter you can plunge into the tropical summer days. In this center there is always an optimal and comfortable temperature of air and water, which does not fall below twenty-eight degrees Celsius.

In addition, this water complex is a member of the international association of water parks, which may indicate its high class. Also among its advantages is the fact that, having come to this place, you do not need to think about how much a ticket costs for the next hours of staying on attractions and slides. There is a single payment system for the whole day, which includes the use of all types of entertainment.





Water recreation

In addition, the Victoria water park (Samara) can surprise its visitors with the unusual Cyclone slide, which is a novelty in the world of water entertainment. Anyone who decides to experience such a steep descent and landing in the deepest pool is guaranteed to receive their portion of adrenaline.

For those visitors who came to the water park with children or as part of a large company, it is recommended to ride a family slide. Visitors who prefer high speeds and steep turns will undoubtedly need to try the Black Hole attraction. This slide is an exciting flight in complete darkness. You can still experience extreme sports by visiting such entertainment as Kamikaze.

For a more relaxed and relaxing vacation, a wave pool or Lazy River are more suitable. Young visitors will also not be bored here, as specially for them in the complex are low slides and a special shallow aquazone.





What else can you do?

After such active entertainment, there is no doubt that there are many who want to quench their thirst and have a good snack. Especially for such clients, the water park has bars that offer delicious cocktails and a large cafe that can accommodate more than two hundred people at a time and offer its guests a wide range of various dishes and snacks. For young visitors there is an equipped children's area where they can order delicious desserts and soft drinks.

In addition, the largest bowling center in the region, consisting of thirty-three tracks, can please all those who came to relax in the Victoria water park (Samara). Employees working in this water complex with their professionalism and willingness to always come to the rescue only add positive emotions from visiting this institution.





Pricing policy of this complex

Parents of a child under the age of three do not need to worry about how much a ticket costs for their baby in the Samara water park. Since the entrance to this entertainment water complex for all children under three years of age is absolutely free.

Adults pay 1200 rubles when visiting and can stay in the center throughout the day. Tickets can be purchased at the box office only on the day of the visit. There is also a special offer for childcare facilities at the water park, thanks to which people accompanying a group of ten can go for free. In addition, the institution often holds promotions and sweepstakes, in which anyone can participate.





Impressions of visitors resting

You may not be wondering if the Victoria water park is working. Regardless of the day of the week, there is always a large queue at the ticket office out of those wishing to get into this complex, which already indicates the increased popularity of this institution among local residents and visitors.

Thanks to its beautiful design, constantly maintained cleanliness, interesting slides, various pools and a large number of attractions, I was able to find many fans throughout the history of its existence, the Victoria Water Park (Samara). Reviews about this place are mostly positive. People like to come here with their whole family or a large group of friends to escape from everyday hustle and have fun together. This complex is especially relevant in the summer heat.

A visit to this center makes it possible to plunge into the cool water, without leaving the city limits. But also the water park is very popular among the population in the winter, when it is still far from vacation, and I want to feel the summer and wash off my tiredness. According to visitors, there is always something to do for both adults and children.





Contact details of the water complex

This water entertainment complex is located at the following address: Samara city, Moskovskoye Shosse, 18th kilometer, 23, Moskovsky shopping center.

To get to the water park, you need to use the entrance from the Rakitovsky highway and go through the furniture salon, located next to this institution. To clarify the information of interest, you can call this phone number: 8 (846) 925-81-18.

Useful Information

You can get to the water complex from anywhere in the city. For example, bus No. 97 runs from the river station, and No. 1, 1k or 67 from the railway station. You can get from the central bus station to the water park by minibus No. 410, 137, 269, 373 and 492. Buses No. 45, 96 and 296 will be taken to the complex from the Aurora bus station.

The Samara water park operates for its visitors without breaks and days off, starting their work at 11:00 and ending at 19:00.



Therefore, all those who want to get a boost of vivacity, a lot of positive emotions and unforgettable impressions, must definitely go to the Victoria entertainment center. In this wonderful place you can not only have a great time, but also get rid of unnecessary stress and fatigue by spending all day with your family or friends.
