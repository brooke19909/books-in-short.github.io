One of such messages of the autoinformer is: "This type of communication is not available to the subscriber." This phrase can be heard for a variety of reasons. And the first thing to check is the amount of money in the account of the caller. It is possible that there simply is not enough money to make such a call. It is also necessary to cancel the barring of outgoing calls using the combination # 33 * 0000 # or ## 002 #.

If, after that, in response, it is heard that this type of communication is not available to the subscriber, then this is due to the called number. There may be several reasons. The most common of them is the negative balance of the subscriber while roaming. In this case, until the money arrives at the account, you won‚Äôt get through to it. True, he will still be able to receive SMS.

Another reason may be an accidentally or intentionally set bar on incoming calls. In this case, you just need to cancel it, and the subscriber will be able to receive calls. To do this, dial the combinations # 35 * 0000 # and ## 002 #. If, in this case, the system replies that this type of communication is not available to the subscriber, it is possible that his number is blocked. To resume service, you can contact your nearest office or the contact center of a mobile operator with a passport. The specialist will be able to quickly resolve this problem.



But it also happens that this type of communication is not available to the subscriber, and it is for the one who is calling. It is possible that his number is simply added to the Blacklist. In this case, you can only do one thing - to call from another phone. If you managed to get through, then most likely it is. Unfortunately, until the subscriber himself removes the number from this list, nothing can be done.

If all the reasons have been eliminated, and the situation has not changed, and this type of communication is not available to the subscriber anyway, you need to restart the telephone and rearrange the SIM card to another mobile. If this also did not help, then you need to seek help from your mobile operator. There may be a network failure and you need to resolve the problem. Just need to be prepared for the fact that it will take some time to solve the problem. Although, of course, any mobile company does everything possible to resolve issues in the shortest possible time.



In any case, do not be afraid that the subscriber is unavailable. What this auto-informer message means can always be found on the company's website or by contacting its consultants in a contact center or nearest office. And after that, take all the actions recommended by them. Surely it turns out that everything is solvable, and in the near future it will be possible to easily contact the called subscriber.
