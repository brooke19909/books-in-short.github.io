How to disable roaming on "Tele2": instructions

Every client of any cellular network operator, including Tele2, knows that to communicate outside the home telephone network service area, as well as when traveling with your SIM card abroad, it is necessary to carry out additional activation of roaming services that will provide the above opportunity.





Connecting the roaming service gives the client the opportunity to register a SIM card of his mobile operator in another region or telephone network. Of course, the question arises before subscribers, but how to turn off roaming on Tele2 when there is no need for it.

The need to disable additional services

As a general rule, before traveling to another region of our country, you should turn off all established call forwarding and options to avoid unnecessary waste of money for services. After arriving at the destination, the telephone set is independently connected to the necessary mobile network of the operator, after which it receives a message about the availability of access, indicating the rates of calls established by the tariffication of a particular area.

Abroad, the search for the desired network is carried out manually on the mobile device, by the necessary manipulations through the menu of the telephone.

Features of disabling roaming

The operator of the Tele2 cellular network offers the most acceptable conditions, including clearly giving an answer to the question - how to disable roaming on Tele2. They are easy to understand and conservative in price for their customers. A wide range of various additional options (services) is also offered, which can be used depending on the circumstances.





According to the rules of this cellular company, the roaming service is connected to all its subscribers, which implies that no additional activation is required. When it enters the roaming zone, the telephone set automatically performs automatic registration in it, after which it works stably. If this does not happen, then you just need to reboot it or find the network through the settings menu manually.

When traveling outside the Russian Federation, the question of how to disable roaming on Tele2 in Russia is very relevant. It is necessary to do this in any case, it should be noted that no additional cash payment for this action will be charged.

Turn off roaming via a cellular device

There are several options for temporarily limiting the above option. To understand how to disable roaming on Tele2 from your phone, you should carefully read the instructions in this article.





The fact is that when the cellular network operator returns the subscriber to the region of registration of the cellular number, the roaming service is disabled independently (automatically). This is very convenient, since the user is not required to perform additional manipulations. It is not necessary to dial special digital combinations to turn off roaming on Tele2.

If, upon returning home, the disconnection of these services did not occur, the mobile device will have to be manually rebooted. If there is no positive effect and during this manipulation, you should go to the phone settings, where in the menu item you need to find the subsection responsible for manually searching for the network, there you need to find the desired Tele2 network and check the box in front of it.
