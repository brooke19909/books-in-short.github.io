Do-it-yourself fly-costume for a girl: instructions

Every girl wants to be the most beautiful and unique at any holiday - whether it's a matinee in your favorite kindergarten, a home theme party or a fun costume contest. And if, as a rule, outfits of princesses, fairies, bunnies, tiger cubs or squirrels can be bought or rented in almost any store or salon, then most mothers want their daughters to wear costumes for the holidays that are very difficult to find and even surely none of the neighboring girls will have it. These include the fly-Tsokotuhi costume.

So what is needed to ensure that your child is strikingly different on the holiday from others? The Tsokotuha-Fly costume for a girl can include the following items of toilet: a yellow blouse, a beret sewn from black velvet and a skirt. On the beret will need to attach a small antennae. They can be cut from the same material or neatly twisted from black wire.





And how to make big wings for our fly? It is quite possible to cut them out of a white net, let a thin wire that gives shape to the edges of each. Each wing can be decorated with the same black velvet to make it look like a real insect.

Skirt

Skirts or dresses that are suitable in color are also useful. If the skirt or dress is green, blue or yellow, then they can be used for a suit by sewing the skirt on a swimsuit. The bolero vest will be beautifully combined with these things. It can be sewn from yellow (golden) stretch satin or dense silk fabric.





If mom needs to urgently prepare the black costume worn by the fabulous Fly, and the parent is not at all friends with a needle and thread, she will be rescued by a skirt with a funny name - tu-tu. For its manufacture you will need only an elastic band (satin ribbon) and tulle.

From tulle, it is necessary to cut several strips of the same width twice as long as the skirt length. Now, in turn, it is required to take strips and tie them around the elastic (tape), slightly departing from the edge. The loops of each tape must be tightened properly. Do this until the entire gum (or tape, which is less convenient), the length of which should be equal to the waist, is filled. After that, lay out a homemade skirt on the table and cut off all the bumps. If you use tulle of two colors for this outfit, then the costume skirt will be even more beautiful.

What should be the wings?

Mushkina hat

If at home there is a little black hat, that the costume of the Fly-Tsokotuha will be perfectly complemented by it. If not, then this element of the wardrobe can be sewn from some old blouse or t-shirt.





Cut it out easily - in a semicircle, slightly elongated from the outside in the center. Eyes fly can be made of two pieces of satin, cut out in the form of circles. Sew them on a hat, having previously placed a synthetic winterizer or foam rubber under the bottom. The work is over. The eyes of the Flies are ready. The nose is made from a cord - its end is hidden in a ball that is sewn from satin - like the eyes, and also filled with the same padding polyester.
