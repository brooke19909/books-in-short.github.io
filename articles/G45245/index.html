Capri island, Italy: photos, attractions, hotels, reviews

One of the most prestigious resorts in the world in ancient times was called the "town of sweet idleness." People come here to bask on the white beaches and have fun in full. The picturesque island of Capri in Italy is the very place where vacationers follow their weaknesses. The luxury resort is famous for its highly developed tourist infrastructure. This is a miniature island, the population of which does not exceed 13 thousand inhabitants, but at the height of the season the number of guests increases hundreds of times, and the narrow streets are instantly filled with a cheerful audience.

Island Description

Formed on the calcareous rock that gave Capri a mountainous relief, the Italian resort of Capri is located in the Tyrrhenian Sea, in the south of the Gulf of Naples, 36 kilometers from Naples. The coastline is indented with bays, grottoes and reefs, and the virgin beauty of the island for several centuries has been improved by a man who planted citruses, grapes and olives here. The earthly paradise, which became a tourist Mecca, was loved by Roman rulers, and it is no coincidence that the emperor Augustus turned it into his private possession, and the valiant commander Tiberius built a villa on which his last years of his life passed.



For the right to possess the beautiful island of Capri (Italy), whose history dates back to the Paleolithic, the Lombards, Normans, the British and French fought.

Two communities in the resort

There are only two cities on the island - Capri, where Maxim Gorky once lived, and Anacapri. The first is often chosen by young people who love noisy parties and shoppers. This is a real tourist center with shops, restaurants and hotels.



And the second community, more like a village than a city, is preferred by older people who prefer a relaxing holiday. Small settlements are connected by a Phoenician staircase carved in a rock in ancient times. Its length is more than one and a half kilometers, and the rise takes at least an hour. Tourists who visit Anacapri, always get by cable car to the highest point of the resort - Mount Solaro, from where the fantastically beautiful landscapes of the island of Capri (Italy) open.

Blue grotto

Of course, the main attraction of the majestic island of Capri in Italy is nature, and the miraculous masterpieces of the resort are of great interest to holidaymakers who celebrate the wonderfully transparent sea, the sandy bottom of which can be seen at a depth of ten meters. One of the most popular tourist sites is the Blue Grotto, named after the color of sea water. It is impossible to get into the storm, therefore, all excursions are made exclusively in calm sunny weather. The unofficial symbol of the Italian pearl is a beautiful cave located on the very shore of the sea. Ancient legends say that it was this grotto that was chosen by the sirens, which the courageous Odysseus was so afraid of.



The breaking sunlight is reflected from the snow-white bottom, and the sea water, due to the natural deepening in the rock, acquires shades of saturated blue, which gave the name of the attraction.

Stone giants of Faraglioni

The wonderful island of Capri (Italy) is rightly proud of another natural monument, the photo of which causes an irresistible desire to visit the resort area created for recreation. The mysterious limestone reefs of Faraglioni protrude from the depths of the sea and are a unique element of the island landscape. Creative people who contemplate unusual beauty have been drawing inspiration here for several centuries.



Stone colosses more than 100 meters high by gigantic obelisks rise above the sea, and in one of them wind, water and time have carved a stone arch through which tourist boats will surely sail. There is a belief that at this moment it is necessary to make a wish, and it will certainly come true. In addition, if the lovers kiss, they will always be together. And those who do not believe in signs take amazing photos.

Where to stay?

It must be warned that there are no budget hotels on the island of Capri (Italy). The tourist center of world significance is an elite vacation spot, and legends go about the high level of service at the prestigious resort. Some hotels are open only in summer, while others are open year-round.



The small and very cozy JK Place Capri is one of the few located on the beach by the beach. Vacationers can conveniently bring minibuses to the popular tourist corners, restaurants and branded clothing stores in a few minutes, and then pick up after a call to the reception. The hotel's interiors are in harmony with the azure sea, and guests enjoy the blue and white range of spacious rooms, filled with sunlight. Many say that this is one of the best hotels with friendly staff that fulfills all the wishes of visitors.

Luxury Hotels for the Chosen

A five-minute walk from the center of Capri is Capri Tiberio Palace - a luxurious complex surrounded by a park surrounded by greenery. Excellent service, amazing decor of comfortable rooms, from which windows offer breathtaking panoramas, culinary masterpieces of Mediterranean cuisine exceed all expectations of tourists. It offers a wellness center, bars in the lobby and poolside, a safety deposit box, car rental, laundry and other services.

Perhaps the most prestigious and best in the whole country is the Grand Hotel Quisisana, whose history began in the 19th century, when a sanatorium appeared on this site, which eventually turned into a luxury hotel. Famous actors, musicians, businessmen choose it, because ideal conditions for relaxation are created here.



Surrounded by well-kept gardens, the hotel offers 148 comfortable rooms with views of the park, sea or city. It offers several restaurants, swimming pools, shops, a gym, a beauty salon, a helipad and even a theater. The upscale hotel has long been recognized as the hallmark of the island of Capri in Italy.

Tourists reviews

The lively resort, which is admired by admiration, amazes even sophisticated tourists who note that there is no need to be bored here, and in a paradise everyone will find something to their liking. In addition to exploring man-made and natural monuments, in a thriving resort, you can take a fascinating boat trip, making stops in interesting places. Climbers rushing here, dreaming of conquering the peak of Monte Solara, and cavers exploring the underworld. Excursions to Naples, Sorrento and other cities of the country are regularly organized for guests.

Holidays on the island of Capri (Italy) are liked by both gourmets, as local restaurants offer delicious dishes, as well as shopaholics who purchase branded items at affordable prices.
