The best hotels of St. Petersburg: description, addresses and reviews of tourists

St. Petersburg is perhaps the main cultural center of Russia, which thousands of domestic and foreign tourists strive to get into every year. But the impression of the city is not only about the sights. Not the last role is played by living conditions. The best hotels of St. Petersburg will provide a worthy welcome to guests of the Northern capital.

Hotel "Kempinski Moika 22"

One of the best hotels in the center of St. Petersburg is Kempinski Moika 22. It is located at the embankment of the Moika River, 22. It is directly opposite Palace Square and the Hermitage. Here guests will be offered the following accommodation options:

  • Business - a comfortable room for work and leisure, decorated in a marine style. A large bed is provided for guests. There is also a comfortable desk with extra lighting.
  • Superior - a spacious room with a double bed and a cozy corner for relaxation. It offers views of the city, courtyard or atrium.
  • Deluxe - an elegant two-room room with a king size bed and a living room with exclusive classic-style furniture. From the large windows you can enjoy the sights of the city or the atrium.
  • Grand Deluxe - differs from the previous category in the increased area and luxurious design.
  • Standard Suite - Spacious Studio. In addition to the king-size bed, there is a set of upholstered furniture and a comfortable desk.
  • A two-story suite is a spacious two-room suite. Downstairs is the living room, and upstairs is the bedroom.
  • Executive suites are huge comfortable rooms with a luxurious classic interior, lots of amenities and original details.

You can hear a lot of good things about this hotel from tourists. For example, they leave such approving comments:

Mini-hotel "Tanais"

Among the good and inexpensive hotels in St. Petersburg, the Tanais mini-hotel stands out. It is located on 58 Nekrasov Street. It is only a few minutes walk from Nevsky Prospekt and the Ploshchad Vosstaniya metro station. The hotel occupies the sixth floor of a historic building.

Guests can take advantage of the following benefits:

  • round-the-clock reception;

  • wireless internet throughout;
