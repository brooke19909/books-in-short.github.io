Hotel Rainbow Hotel 2 * (Greece, Rhodes): photos and reviews of tourists

The island of Rhodes is a favorite place for tourists who decide to go on vacation to Greece. Each of them will find here entertainment to their liking: great beaches, ancient city architecture and a huge number of shops that sell not only inexpensive clothes, shoes and souvenirs, but also natural cosmetics with the addition of olive oil. If you are looking for a place for an inexpensive overnight stay, then pay attention to the cozy Rainbow Hotel 2 * hotel (Rhodes). What services does she offer?

Location

A significant advantage of this hotel is its favorable position. It is located on the outskirts of the historical center of Rhodes. It is only 900 meters away. Tourists can overcome it on foot, take a bus or order a taxi. At the same time, the coast can be reached in just 5 minutes. The famous Zephyros Beach is just 400 meters from the hotel. Near it is well developed tourist infrastructure. There are restaurants and cafes where hotel guests can have lunch or dinner, shops and tour desks. Nearby is also the marina, which offers motor and non-motor boat rental for tourists.





Rhodes Fortress, the island‚Äôs main historical attraction, is just a 10-minute walk away. The Palace of the Grand Masters is 2 km from the Rainbow 2 * Apart Hotel. In the immediate vicinity there is also a large supermarket, two city parks and a cultural center. Near the hotel there are two bus stops and a taxi rank. The nearest airport is Diagoras . It is 15 km away. By taxi to the city can be reached in 10-15 minutes. Transfer by bus will take about half an hour, but will cost much cheaper.

general information

This hotel began to receive tourists in 1992. Most often they stay here for a short night. Also, it is chosen by those travelers who are looking for an extremely budget holiday option in Greece. In a small area is a five-story building, equipped with an elevator. It is built in urban style and will not surprise guests with luxurious architecture. Next to it is a park area and an outdoor pool with a sun deck. In total, the Rainbow Hotel 2 * hotel (Greece, Rhodes) has 85 rooms. Check-in starts here at 14:00 and ends at midnight.





A distinctive feature of this hotel is the possibility of pets. However, an additional fee for their accommodation will not be charged. Before making a tour, it is necessary to notify the hotel about the type, breed, weight of the animal, as well as vaccinations. The hotel also allows children of all ages. Guests under 6 years old can stay in the room for free if they sleep with their parents. It is worth knowing that extra beds are not available in the complex. It is usually necessary to leave the room in the morning, before noon. But if necessary, you can extend your stay for several hours.

Number of rooms

The rooms in this hotel are not large. They are made in a classic style, without the use of bright colors in the design. Each has a private bathroom with shower, which is additionally equipped with a toilet, sink and mirror. The living room is divided into a bedroom and living area. There are beds, a desk, a chair, a wardrobe and bedside tables. All rooms are soundproofed. It offers views of the nearest city buildings. Housekeeping is provided daily. Maids also periodically renew bath towels and bed linen.

Rainbow Hotel 2 * (Greece) offers tourists to choose one of the following accommodation options:

  • Double Room. The small living room has two single beds. The floor is carpeted. Possible accommodation for two adult tourists and a child. The total area of ‚Äã‚Äãthe room is 16 square meters. m
  • Triple room. It differs from the previous version only in the presence of an extra bed. It is a small sofa bed that does not fold out. Designed to accommodate three adults and one child. Single beds can be pushed together upon request.




Room Amenities

Apartments of the hotel are not distinguished by rich equipment. There is a set of necessary amenities, but you should not count on excesses. For example, there is a TV in the rooms, but it is quite old, with a massive picture tube. It broadcasts satellite channels, including Russian. On the bedside table is usually a wired telephone with which you can call the reception. Guests of Rainbow Hotel 2 * can ask the administrator to wake them up at the appointed time by phone call, as well as order delivery of food, drinks and newspapers in the room. Such services are provided only for a fee.

You can store drinks and food in the refrigerator. To cool the rooms, a central air conditioner works. In winter and autumn, the hotel also includes heating. A mechanical safe is provided in the room upon request. If you are afraid for the safety of your belongings, then before leaving the hotel you can put them in it. Payment for its use is charged daily. A set of bath accessories is provided in the bathroom, but there is no hairdryer and iron. Also in the rooms there is no internet connection.

Service and Facilities

City hotel Rainbow Hotel 2 * (Rhodes) offers guests the minimum set of services that they may need during their vacation here. Consider the main infrastructure that work in the hotel:

  • Public parking near the hotel. Tourists can leave their car here for free at any time.
  • Wi-Fi , working free of charge in all public areas of the complex. To connect to it, you need to ask the administrator for a password.
  • You can always send a fax or photocopy documents at the reception. Services are charged separately.
  • Elevator with which you can quickly reach your floor. It is designed for heavy weight, so massive luggage can also be delivered on it.
  • Equipment for people who are physically disabled. There are ramps on the stairs, and the doorways are wide enough for wheelchairs.
  • Luggage storage of bags and luggage, as well as an additional paid safe - at the reception.




Is there any entertainment at the hotel?

Unfortunately, there is no well-thought-out entertainment program at Rainbow Hotel 2 *. In high season, a swimming pool filled with fresh water is open daily for tourists. Free of charge, guests can take a seat on the sun terrace, as well as take a sun lounger and parasol. The reception has a tour desk with a Russian-speaking guide. It will help you find an interesting and inexpensive tour of the sights of the island. In addition, they can be bought on the streets. As a rule, there they are much cheaper. It is also worth noting that children's entertainment at the hotel is also not provided. Therefore, when coming here with a child, tourists will be forced to think through their leisure activities on their own.





Catering

Tourists who have settled in the Rainbow Hotel 2 * are offered a choice of two food systems. Most of the guests prefer to pay only breakfast, and they go to restaurants and cafes of Rhodes for dinner and lunch. The all-inclusive system is suitable for guests who prefer a leisurely beach holiday. The main meals are served in the hotel restaurant, which specializes in European, Mediterranean and Greek cuisine. The hotel also has a snack bar serving light snacks, desserts and ice cream. Another bar is located right by the pool. Here you can buy soft drinks and spirits and cocktails.





Positive reviews

Before buying a ticket, you should definitely get acquainted with the opinions of tourists who have stayed at the hotel earlier. They truthfully tell about the advantages and disadvantages of the hotel. Travelers who rested earlier in the Rainbow Hotel 2 *, leave conflicting reviews. First, talk about the virtues that they noted:

  • Tasty lunches and dinners. Food is constantly reported, so staying hungry here is quite difficult.
  • Good housekeeping. Linen and towels are changed every two days. They regularly take out the garbage, wipe the dust and mop the floors.
  • Most often, Russian tourists relax here, so at the hotel you can find friends or just talk with someone in your native language.
  • Spacious rooms that are in good condition. Unlike other hotels in this category, bed linen and towels are new.
  • High speed internet on the ground floor.




Negative reviews

What were the dissatisfied guests of Rainbow Hotel 2 * (Rhodes) left with? Reviews note that the hotel building and its premises are very old, so they need urgent repairs. Also, tourists indicate the following disadvantages:

  • The door to the balcony in the room does not open well and is covered in rust in some places. There are small holes on the balcony railing that spoil its aesthetic appearance.
  • From a set of cosmetics, only soap is dispensed in small pieces. You have to buy shampoo and shower gel yourself.
  • The owner of the hotel behaves rudely towards the guests. Smokes and drinks in the lobby on the ground floor, so the smell there is not very pleasant.
  • Air conditioning is available at an additional cost. Instead, they sometimes give out a fan that does not work well.
  • The street on which the hotel is located is in terrible condition. There is rubbish everywhere, no vegetation. At the entrance to the hotel smells unpleasant sewer.
  • Monotonous breakfasts, the menu of which does not change throughout the rest.

Conclusion

The dilapidated condition, unpleasant odors on the territory and rude staff push tourists away from the Rainbow Hotel 2 *. Travelers note that for the same price you can stay in a hotel with better conditions. The advantage of the complex is its location. For tourists who prefer to spend all their free time in the city and return to the hotel late in the evening, it will be a good option for an overnight stay.
