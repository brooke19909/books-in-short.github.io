Hotel Sunrise (Turkey) - a chic vacation on the Mediterranean coast

On the Antalya coast of the Mediterranean Sea is a luxury hotel "Sunrise". Turkey, as you know, is famous for its high service and unrivaled hotels at affordable prices. Rest at its resorts will give only pleasure and a lot of pleasure.

Location

This amazing and beautiful hotel Sunrise Park Resort Spa 5 *, located 12 kilometers from the city of Side, can be called the pearl of Turkish resorts. The nearest airport is Antalya Airport, 75 kilometers away: approximately an hour drive. A unique beach area, covered with sand and small pebbles, stretches for almost eight hundred meters, it offers a gorgeous view of the Mediterranean Sea. 5 star hotels in Turkey will be an excellent choice for a vacation. Sunrise is the most suitable place for a romantic or family vacation with children.



The territory of the hotel. Enclosures and numbers

The huge territory on which all the buildings of the hotel complex are located is about 150,000 square meters. meters. The apartments are located in a multi-storey building and two-story bungalows, which are located in the park area. The rooms are equipped with all necessary conditions, household appliances, a bar, a refrigerator, some have a jacuzzi, and a balcony opens up a charming landscape of the Mediterranean Sea and green gardens. Attentive and friendly staff, daily housekeeping and a change of bed speaks of the high quality of services that Sunrise Hotel offers tourists. Turkey occupies a leading position in the world in tourist resorts and wellness SPA centers.



Hotel facilities

For vacationers in this complex the best conditions are created. There are several pools on the territory, including for children, there is also a water park with water slides. Cozy bars on the beach and near the pool will allow you to relax in the shade of trees and enjoy drinks. Multiple entertainment for adults and children is offered by the Sunrise Hotel. Turkey is famous for its interesting animations, discos, night clubs, gourmet restaurants and shopping centers.

Vacationers at this hotel will be able to visit a real Turkish bath, sauna, spa, cinema, Ayurvedic center. Also on the territory there is an Internet cafe, a conference room, a beauty salon, currency exchange. Tourists are invited to use the parking and car rental services. For the kids there are playgrounds, a mini club with interesting programs, there are babysitting services. Dining at the Sunrise Hotel is an all-inclusive system, which is very convenient and beneficial. The restaurant offers a menu with a varied international cuisine. Turkey was able to combine high quality and reasonable prices at its resorts. Hotel "Sunrise", whose prices are about 100 euros per day, is one of the most popular among tourists. This complex has also created excellent conditions for practicing various sports. It has well-equipped tennis courts, a fitness center, a riding club, and a football field. Water lovers are given the opportunity to go windsurfing, diving, and water skiing. Hotel Sunrise (Turkey) will give an unforgettable and delightful stay.
