Resorts of the Dominican Republic in the Caribbean: La Romana, Punta Cana, Puerto Plata, Juan Dolio and others

If you want to spend your vacation in paradise, then you should go to the resorts of the Dominican Republic in the Caribbean. Whatever place you choose - La Romana, Puerto Plata, Punta Cana or any other - everywhere you will be met by palm-covered beaches, chic underwater tropical gardens, smiling black locals and gentle breezes. The main wealth of the Dominican resorts are the warm sea and sandy beaches. Here are the best hotels in the world, working on the principle of All Inclusive. The average annual air temperature varies between +28 and +31 degrees. Travelers will forever remember the charming rainforests, coral reefs and stunning beauty of waterfalls.

General characteristics of resorts

The Dominican resorts on the Caribbean Sea today are in great demand among tourists, as they offer a good rest when organized at the highest level of comfort and constant sunny weather. The most popular is an elite type of recreation. Incredibly beautiful, one might say fantastic, beaches of the republic play a significant role in it . Vacation of tourists here is combined with national Caribbean events, fiery carnivals and expressive dances.

If you briefly describe the resorts of the Dominican Republic on the Caribbean Sea, it would seem that they are a real paradise, which is located in a stunning rainforest. Rest in any of the resorts will take place in the shade of coconut trees. The areas of beach relaxation filled with golden and crystal white sand impress with well-groomed and alluring warmth, they will give a complete sense of relaxation and absolute security.

One of the most popular resorts

One of the most popular Dominican resorts is Punta Cana. It is located on the Caribbean Sea, more precisely, on one of its shores, and its snow-white beaches stretch for 30 kilometers. It arose at the beginning of the distant 80s of the last century on a deserted sea coast. The resort includes several large tourist complexes. They are intended for families or for lovers. Only in the spring can you meet only students who come in order to have fun in the midst of a youth party.

Punta Cana Resort is known all over the world. And this is not surprising, because here there are the best hotels of the coast, historical and cultural attractions are concentrated, and only here there are many interesting and informative things. At this resort, even those travelers who make the highest demands on relaxation will satisfy their needs. In Punta Cana, guests are surrounded by peacocks, rare species of flowers, mangroves and flamingos. Unusual things for Europeans to look at will seem quite natural here.



Quiet and peaceful resort

La Romana (Dominican Republic) is a wonderful resort, which is the capital of the province with the same name. It was founded at the very beginning of the 16th century. On the coast of the modern resort is a huge hotel complex, included in the list of the most respectable places in the world. This chic tourist center is the main attraction of La Romana.

In the resort of La Romana (Dominican Republic) there are dozens of luxury cottages and hundreds of luxurious villas. This place is recommended to visit young married couples who prefer a calm, quiet vacation on the seashore. You can have a wonderful time here for athletes, both professional and beginners. For them, there are several sports complexes. At La Romana you can go underwater fishing and diving. In the Dominican Republic, the most popular sports are horse riding and tennis, and you can do them just in the resort of La Romana.



Resort on the island of Haiti

The wonderful resort of Puerto Plata (Dominican Republic) is located on the island of Haiti. It has more than ten hotels, including two-star and five-star establishments. Hotels offer guests plenty of entertainment for every taste: there is a club right on the beach, everyone will be taught diving and snorkeling, and you can also take part in sports games and competitions. In Puerto Plata , you have the opportunity to enjoy the endless white beaches and the warm Dominican sun.

In the resort of Puerto Plata (Dominican Republic) there are not so many people as in other resorts of the republic. Here you can always find an incredibly beautiful, but secluded place for a relaxing holiday. Small Puerto Plata welcomes all its guests cordially - it is designed in such a way that everyone who arrives here can feel calm and comfortable. There is a riot of greenery and there is an unsurpassed beach, and the magnificent nature surrounds both on land and in the underwater world.

Favorite Dominican Resort

Juan Dolio is a Dominican resort located in the southeastern part of the island. Many travelers love him, but he earned special recognition among the locals. People come here who like the delights of the underwater world: near the coast of this particular resort is a magical coral reef, which can be explored for days on end. Juan Dolio has more than ten upscale hotels, chic restaurants, clubs, discos and casinos.

Without the resort of Juan Dolio, it is almost impossible to imagine the Dominican Republic. The beautiful town, which is located near the capital, is full of interesting places, it is characterized by magnificent nature and a curious history. There are many museums, small churches and chapels that you can visit at any time. Holiday life here proceeds slowly, but everyone only benefits from this.

The resort capital of the Dominican Republic

The best resorts of the Dominican Republic were founded during the Middle Ages. So, for example, the now popular Santo Domingo was founded in 1496 by Bartolomeo Columbus, the brother of the very Christopher Columbus. He named the city of New Isabella, but then the resort was renamed Santo Domingo, which from Spanish translates as Holy Sunday.

In Santo Domingo, the Osama River flows, which divides it into two parts. The western region is the cultural and business center of the city, and the eastern one is historical. The most interesting regions for travelers will be the colonial zone and Gazcue. In the center of the western part of Santo Domingo is the "commercial" heart of the capital. Here are concentrated the best shops and restaurants of the city.
