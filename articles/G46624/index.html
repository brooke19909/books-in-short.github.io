Zanzibar hotels: description, rating, reviews

There are many wonderful places in the world where you can enjoy the beauty of nature from the heart. One of them is Zanzibar. White beaches, exotic palm trees, warm sea. It is very easy to believe that paradise exists on Earth. It attracts not only the island itself, but also its hotels. Zanzibar offers tourists a large number of a wide variety of options. After reading the article, you will learn a lot of useful information about this.



Where is Zanzibar located

Before we tell you about the most popular hotels in this place, let's first find out its location. Zanzibar is a group of islands that are located on the eastern coast of Africa. From all sides they are surrounded by the Indian Ocean. On the main island of the same name you will find beautiful nature, ancient monuments and a huge number of opportunities for a relaxing holiday. Many tourists are interested in the weather in Zanzibar. We can say that a warm sun always warms here. Even in the winter months, the thermometer does not drop below 30 degrees during the day and 24 at night. Precipitation is extremely small and insignificant. The best time to travel is from June to October.

How to get there

Zanzibar is one of Africa's favorite tourist destinations. It will be useful to find out how you can get to this wonderful island. One of the most convenient options is an air connection between Moscow and Zanzibar. Unfortunately, there are no direct messages. If you decide to fly from Moscow to Zanzibar, you will first get to the capital of Tanzania, the city of Dar es Salaam. Then by sea or by air go to the popular island. Now, knowing where Zanzibar is located, you can plan trips here and choose the best accommodation, which will be discussed later.



Zanzibar Hotels

The travel agency will offer you the most profitable and popular options. There are a large number of hotels in Zanzibar. The rooms are comfortable and very cozy, and the level of service meets the highest European criteria. Many hotels are located on the ocean coast and offer magnificent views from their windows. Studying the reviews of tourists who come here, we decided to make for you a small rating list of the best hotels in Zanzibar. We will tell you about six wonderful places to relax and stay.



Beautiful view of the sea and the city

Tausi Palace Hotel - start our review with him. Tourists who live in this place leave only the most positive reviews about the rest. The hotel is very popular with visitors. Let's get to know him better. The hotel is located in the center of Stone Town. This is a city that is one of the main attractions of the island of Zanzibar. Stone Town has a large number of attractions, including: palaces, mosques, souvenir shops and shops. If you stay at the Tausi Palace Hotel, then everything you need will be within walking distance. From the windows of the hotel you can see not only beautiful views of the city, but also the Indian Ocean. It has a sun terrace, restaurant and bar. What other services are provided to guests? We list the most popular:

  • 24-hour reception
  • daily housekeeping ;
  • bike and car rental;
  • transfer from the airport to the hotel and back;
  • non-smoking rooms available;
  • Free toiletries, including: bathrobe and slippers;
  • BBQ facilities are provided;
  • designated smoking areas;
  • looking after children, as well as conducting interesting programs with them;
  • Hotel staff will purchase the necessary foodstuffs if necessary.

It is also interesting to know what rooms are provided for vacationers Single, double with one and two beds, family, triple. Each room has private bathrooms, air conditioning, mosquito nets, refrigerators, comfortable beds, televisions and much more. The price includes breakfast, which is carried out according to the buffet. The hotel staff is always ready to help guests and provide them with all the necessities. They will tell you the most favorable excursion offers and advise where it is better to buy souvenir products.



Zanzi Resort

The rating list of the best hotels on the island can not be imagined without a place, which will be discussed later. Just imagine that you will rest in the middle of the jungle. Far from city noise and dust. Here you can enjoy not only the beauty of exotic nature, but also see interesting specimens of flora and fauna. The hospitable and polite hotel staff will make your stay cozy and comfortable. What about the infrastructure of this place? In a small, guarded area there are several villas. Each of them has its own pool and a separate access to the beach. Without your permission, no one will be able to disturb your relaxation and privacy. There are parking places, as well as Wi-Fi. The price includes breakfast. Meal time here may be individual for each guest. Delicious food, comfortable rooms, a high level of service, unity with nature - this is all the Zanzi Resort Hotel.



"Kisiwa House"

If you like everything unusual and beautiful, then opt for this place. It is located in one of the oldest buildings of Stone Town. All rooms are luxuriously decorated. Interiors combine modern design and vintage elements. There is a restaurant located on the roof of the hotel. It offers stunning panoramic views of the city and the ocean. The restaurant serves both local and European dishes. There is a beautiful garden where you can walk. Near the hotel is a beautiful fountain. The rooms have hydromassage bathtubs, large plasma televisions, minibars, air conditioning. Here you will be provided with a hairdryer, bathrobes, as well as other necessary things and objects. Among the rooms there are double and luxury rooms. Nearby there are cafes and night bars, where tourists from different cities of the world enjoy having fun.

"Residence Zanzibar"

Staying at this wonderful hotel, you will see a magnificent ocean upon awakening. After all, this place is located on its very shore. Mentioning the hotels of Zanzibar (5 stars) it is impossible not to recall the "The Residence Zanzibar". The atmosphere here is absolutely amazing. Sea, palm trees, high level of service. What else will be of interest to this hotel for tourists? Here you will be offered to make fascinating and exotic excursions. Fishing in the deep sea, exciting diving, a walk with dolphins and much more.



"Zanzibar Serena Hotel"

Continuing the rating review of the best hotels on the island, we offer you to get acquainted with another great place. "Zanzibar Sirna Inn" is very popular with vacationers. It is located on the beach in Stone Town, which is very convenient for vacationers. There are two restaurants, a pool. Delicious breakfasts and even champagne are offered. There is a gym, spa, beauty salon. It offers vacationers a children's pool, a conference room, dry cleaning and laundry services, and much more. The rooms are very clean and spacious, equipped with everything necessary for living.

"Baraza Resort and Spa Zanzibar"

In the village of Bedzhuu there is a luxurious place to live, as well as improve the health of the body. Here you will find spacious villas equipped with everything you need. There is a large terrace with sun loungers, as well as a mini pool with warm water. All rooms have bedrooms, living rooms. Vacationers prefer breakfast on the terrace, enjoying the fresh and unique smells of the surrounding trees and plants, as well as birdsong. Here you will find a large number of exciting activities: diving, fitness center, spa treatments and much more. The rooms have luxurious furniture, chic interiors, free Wi-Fi.
