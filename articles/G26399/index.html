A specific answer to the direct question about visas





Citizens of Russia who are sent to the Republic of Tunisia as tourists do not need a visa if their stay is up to one month. Entry into the country is based on tourist vouchers, which are issued by foreign companies accredited by the national travel agency of Tunisia. In this case, it is imperative to comply with some rules. Firstly, you need to arrive at the resort only by direct flight, and secondly, you need to return on the same route. Thirdly, a tourist who has arrived on the basis of a voucher cannot visit other countries during this period. And remember that even without explaining the reasons, the border guard can deploy you to Russia. In exceptional cases, a month's stay can be extended up to three months - at the request of the inviting party.

When do you need a visa to Tunisia

In all other cases, citizens of the Russian Federation must obtain a visa to visit Tunisia. The good thing is that, unlike some other countries, there are only two types of Tunisian visas: short-term and long-term. The first are issued for a period of up to three months, the second - up to six. If the visitor stays in the country for a period longer than six months, then it is already necessary to request a residence permit or apply for a new visa. In the event that a tourist arriving on a visa has lost his passport or was stolen, a return certificate is issued at the Russian consulate. A preliminary application to the police for loss / theft. At each border crossing, a foreigner fills out a special entry-exit card to the state. Transit visas are also issued for a period of seven days after the submission of the relevant documents.





What documents are needed for a visa to Tunisia

Now consider the list of documents required to apply for your visa. He is as follows:

  1. As usual, a foreign passport, where without him. The minimum validity period is three months from the day the trip ends.
  2. Photocopies of its first page and page with information about children.
  3. Two profiles.
  4. Photocopies of pages of an internal Russian passport.
  5. Certificate of employment. Need a letterhead, be sure to indicate the position and salary.
  6. For pensioners - a copy of the certificate.
  7. Pupils and students definitely need a power of attorney from their parents if they travel separately.
  8. For foreign citizens - residence permit, registration and work permit.

You will also need to pay a consular fee of $ 70. Now we have examined in detail how to get a visa to Tunisia.

Other nuances





If children under the age of 20 come to the country with their parents, and they are recorded in their passport, then for the purpose of tourism, no other documents are needed. The same thing when leaving. If children under 20 years of age have local citizenship, a residence permit or a long-term visa, then upon departure they must present a power of attorney issued by the child's father. This must be done even if the child leaves the country with a Russian passport.
