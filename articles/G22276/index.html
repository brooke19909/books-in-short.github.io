How to choose a good slow cooker for the family?

The modern kitchen in terms of the number of electronic devices can even compete with a scientific laboratory. A microwave, food processor, juicer, toaster - this is not a complete list of helpers in the kitchen. True, among these devices there are both highly specialized and universal ones. One of such multifunctional kitchen units is a multicooker. With its help, you can cook soup, bake a cupcake, make vegetable stew and fry cutlets. And this is not all that this miracle saucepan is capable of.



In any store today there are at least 10 different similar units. And how to choose a good slow cooker among them? Initially, you need to understand how much it is needed. A bachelor or a family of 2 will be enough and a bowl of 2.5-3 liters. Also, such a slow cooker is suitable for cooking a child. For a large family, it is better to take a larger cooker - at least 5 liters. It will already be possible to cook dishes even for a small group of guests.

How to choose a good slow cooker by the volume of the bowl, is now clear to everyone. Then you need to figure out which of the functions will be really useful in the future. Most of them have such standard programs as Pilaf, Stewing, and Croup.
