AEG S refrigerator

Big, beautiful, handsome. This is if in three words, but it is possible in more detail. Indeed, this refrigerator attracts its appearance from the first glance. With the design of the model, everything is in order. But this is not the main thing in the refrigerator.

The height of the model is 203 centimeters with the standard two other components of physical dimensions (60 centimeters by 66 centimeters). The 78 liter freezer is divided into 3 drawers. This is not the largest indicator for such large refrigerators. It is worth noting the smooth running of the boxes, not every manufacturer offers this, sometimes you need to make a lot of physical effort to move the freezer drawer full. In this freezer, drawers move along the runners.

The freezer with NoFrost technology allows you not to defrost it and prevents the growth of the "fur coat". In general, the technology is called Twin Tech NoFrost and involves two separate refrigerant circuits, which allows you to maintain optimal conditions in the refrigerator compartment and in the freezer.

The model has a special box in the refrigerator compartment for special storage of meat and fish, it quickly cools the contents to 0.5 degrees. The technology is called Quick Chill. The Multi Air function provides clear and even air circulation in the space of the refrigerator.

The model is thought out, made as competently as possible. There are standard shelves on the door. It is noteworthy that the lower of them is not at the very bottom of the door, but slightly higher. This allows you to make boxes for vegetables more spacious. A convenient shelf for storing glass bottles is provided. This is a very convenient thing, and it is very strange that many manufacturers stubbornly ignore this point in their devices.

In this model, a replaceable carbon filter is installed to absorb odors. It is worth saying that he copes with the task perfectly, absorbing all the smells, even the harshest and strongest. In general, the model is easy to use, the instruction of AEG refrigerators is almost never required. Everything is thought out, simple and intuitive.

The fridge handles are comfortable, the touch control panel. The surface is not easily soiled. AEG S is very quiet at work. Of some weaknesses, it is necessary to mention that the box for storing eggs here is designed for only 6 pcs., Of course, there is a place to put an additional box, but why should not the producer do it himself?
