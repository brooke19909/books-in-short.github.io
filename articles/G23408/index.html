Krasnodar Territory, Art. Elizavetinskaya: location, foundation history, interesting places, photos and reviews

There is a station in the Krasnodar Territory. Elizabethan, which residents affectionately call Elizabeth. This rural district center has a long history. But today the village even has its own page on VKontakte.

Elizavetinskaya is part of the Prikubansky intra-urban district, those living on its territory have Krasnodar registration, and many work in the capital of the region.

Where is the village of Elizavetinskaya

A small village is located at a distance of only seven km from the regional capital of Krasnodar.

Located on the right bank of the Kuban River, Art. Elizavetinskaya (Krasnodar Territory) is surrounded on one side by a winding channel of a river wide in this place, and on the other - by steppe expanses. The picturesque surroundings attract tourists and tourists to Elizabeth. Near the settlement on the banks of the Kuban there is a forest park Prirechny, near the main canal there is the Elizavetinsky tract.

The settlement is adjacent to the Adyghe villages of Afipsip, Khashtuk, Starobrzhegokai, in the north and east the settlement borders on the Krasnodar city line.

The structure of the village includes country partnerships, the largest of which are Rosinka, Dream, and Express.

History

The history of this settlement, like many others in the Kuban, began at the end of the 18th century, when Empress Catherine II began to grant the Cossacks fertile Black Sea lands in exchange for border protection service. Then, on a convenient place, on the high bank of the Kuban River, next to a well-worn tract, a small Cossack post appeared, a little later named after the wife of the young emperor Alexander I.

In 1821, the Dnieper Cossacks transferred their kuren to the place of the Elizabethan post, but the name of the settlement remains the same.

The proximity to Yekaterinodar, or Krasnodar, determined the administrative division: the village was always part of the Kuban region.

In 2004, the last administrative change occurred: the village entered the village district, the center of which became the village. Belozerny located nearby.

sights

Driving through the village of Elizavetinskaya, you should stop for half a day and see the sights, take a photo as a keepsake.

A lot of ancient buildings have been preserved in the center of the settlement, among them are the stanitsa board and the complex of the two-year primary male school.

St. Elizabeth (Krasnodar Territory) is a place known to historians as the Elizabethan settlement. Now the archaeological site has the status of federal significance.

Scientists who studied the ancient settlement in the 30s of the XX century, determined that there lived a large tribe of Meot, in the IV century. BC inhabiting the Black Sea coast. In Art. Elizabethan preserved two citadels, they are surrounded by a deep moat that separated the settlement from the steppe.

Pottery was produced in the Meot fort: scientists found kilns for firing pottery and evidence of active trade with the Bosporus kingdom, located on the territory of the present Kerch Peninsula.

Near the settlement there is a necropolis, where about 100 barrows are perfectly preserved in the unreplied state. In the village of Elizabethan, locals call them ‚ÄúMay Mountains‚Äù. In the high barrows, the Meots buried their nobles and their leaders. In the graves of the dead or deceased found dishes, jewelry, weapons. Artifacts from barrows are stored in the Hermitage; they can also be seen in the Kuban Museum of the leshoz.

Of the natural attractions, the village can surprise you with oak forests that grow in the Elizabethan tract. Some giant trees are 300 years old.
