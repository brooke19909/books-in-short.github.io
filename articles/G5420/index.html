How to make original hair bands with your own hands

Currently, in any store, the owner of a luxurious head of hair can purchase for herself an elastic band for hair. On the shelves presented a huge abundance of different colors and shades. A variety of materials are used to make this accessory. This is the main problem of choice, especially at the moment when you want to please yourself with something special and truly original.

Today, fashionistas find a way out in needlework, creating beautiful elastic bands for hair with their own hands. Such a thing instantly falls into the category of exclusive ones and you don‚Äôt have to worry about someone having a similar accessory. Further in the article we will talk about different ways to create rubber bands for hair.

Where to begin

Often at home you can find things that, in principle, are not needed, and sending them to the bin is not enough spirit for one reason or another. Such trifles include:

  • small pieces of fabric;
  • beads;
  • pebbles from old jewelry;
  • decorative flowers.

All of the above will be an excellent material to create an exclusive decoration. And so, how to combine all the components, tells the imagination.

What is worth considering

Before wondering how to make an elastic band for hair with your own hands, you need to think about the material, choose jewelry and a base. It is recommended to take not too narrow an elastic band. If you need an accessory for winter time, it is best to opt for discreet colors and decorations, but in the summer months you should treat yourself with bright colors. However, these are just tips; everyone is free to act in their own way.

How to make an elastic band with lace

This option is very original and beautiful. Gum can be decorated with lace of various colors. To do this, you need to sew the selected material to the rubber, for attaching additional jewelry you can use beads or rhinestones.

Rubber band

It sounds extremely unusual. But there is nothing complicated here. You need to get a wire 30 cm long and a selected piece of fabric. A rectangle having a width of 10 cm is cut out of matter, and the length should exceed slightly the length of the wire. Matter is sewn from the inside to make a cover with a small hole for the wire. This part is then closed using an inconspicuous seam. You can decorate the elastic with sequins, beads or decorative flowers.

Rubber band

Here, to work, you need a piece of plastic on which you need to make a drawing. It can be flowers or some other pattern to taste. Using glue, the plate is attached to an elastic band. To save the picture, transparent varnish is used.

How to use fabric

A rectangular patch is cut from the material you like. The size of the finished product also depends on its size. The material is sewn around the perimeter, but you must remember to leave a hole so that you can insert an elastic band. Then the workpiece is turned on the wrong side and the elastic is inserted. The remaining hole can be sewn with your hands. Decorated with such an accessory, available at hand accessories.

DIY hair bands from ribbons

Before starting work, you need to acquire the necessary materials:

  • Satin ribbon of your favorite color. Some choose solid colors, others take several shades at once. If the second method is chosen, then pieces of 4 cm are cut off from each ribbon, in the amount of 60 pieces, they will be used to create flowers.
  • You also need to stock up on floral stamens that are sold at any needlework store.
  • It will take another ribbon to make a bow, its width should be 5 cm.
  • As additional decorating elements, you can use half beads and rhinestones.

Step-by-step execution of work

  1. There is nothing complicated in creating flowers. Prepared cuts are cut with scissors so that a sharp edge remains, then you need to singe it over the fire so that the thread does not crumble and stretch a little. From below, the petals are glued together, and before that, the fabric is slightly pinned.
  2. A similar method is applied to ribbons of other colors that are selected for crafts.
  3. Then all the petals are collected in one decoration, alternating colors in the order in which you want.
  4. Now comes the turn of stamens. They are attached to the core of the flower, leaves of the remaining color are glued around them.
  5. In this way, the remaining flowers are formed.
  6. At this stage, bows are made by gluing strips of ribbons.
  7. The resulting blanks are decorated with beads or rhinestones.
  8. At the very last stage, it remains to glue the entire structure to the gum.

An elastic band made in this style is ideal for high hairstyles and ponytails, and it can also be used to decorate hair gathered in a bun. An original accessory will surely attract the attention of all friends and acquaintances, and its owner can rightfully be proud of the original elastic band for hair. As you can see from the article, making elastic bands for hair with your own hands is quite simple.
