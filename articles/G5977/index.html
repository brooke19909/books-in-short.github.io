Residential complex "Progress", Kudrovo: contacts, plans, reviews of real estate investors

Of course, any resident of St. Petersburg would like to have an apartment on Nevsky in order to admire the wonderful beauty of this city and its amazing sights. But in practice, everything is somewhat different. Firstly, such housing today is simply not affordable for an ordinary person. Secondly, today even those people who can afford to get luxury apartments are thinking about the advisability of such a purchase due to the very poor environmental situation in megacities. Of course, they have a choice.





You can buy a comfortable house in the country with a huge plot and not worry about clean air. People who have little income can only dream of buying such a property. Nevertheless, they also want to live in a good apartment and breathe clean air. And developers come to their aid who are actively developing suburban lands, erecting multi-storey complexes there. Moreover, when choosing a place, they try to put not only the infrastructure, but also the environmental component at the forefront. One of these complexes is the Progress residential complex in Kudrovo. It will be discussed.

About the complex

Vsevolozhsk district, whose territories are famous for its good ecology and are popular among developers, has provided its land for the construction of Progress residential complex. Kudrovo is one of his villages, next to which a residential complex is located . This place is attractive because it is located not far from the metropolis, there is a metro station two stops from it, a ten-minute walk is the Mega-Dybenko shopping and entertainment complex, and a huge park zone is literally nearby.

So, when planning the location of Progress residential complex, the developer - the Mavis company - very competently approached the solution of this issue. The residential complex consists of six houses of different floors - from 9 to 23. Three buildings have already been commissioned, the rest are being built. Construction is using brick-monolithic technology.

Progress

Work on the construction of the complex began in 2012. Due to the fact that the development is a large-scale project, it was decided to work in stages. In 2014, in full accordance with the planned dates, three first-stage buildings were commissioned. In the second half of 2016, it is planned to hand over the fourth building, and in 2017 - the fifth and sixth.

Ecological situation

Vsevolozhsk district is generally considered the cleanest in the Leningrad region in terms of ecology. There are no manufacturing enterprises that poison the atmosphere, there are many forests and ponds. As for Kudrovo directly, on the territory of which the Progress residential complex is being built, its distinctive feature is the close location of the Okkervil park zone, the Okhta River, Lake Ladoga and the Karelian Isthmus. Infrastructure component

Each potential buyer of an apartment in a new building considers and evaluates not only the quality of housing, but also the security of the district with social and commercial infrastructure. As already mentioned, in this regard, the future residents of the Progress residential complex are really lucky. A ten-minute walk away is the huge Mega-Dybenko shopping and entertainment complex, where you can find almost everything that a modern person needs: from shops of various kinds to cafes, restaurants, sports clubs and even a movie theater. In addition, all the first floors of the Progress housing complex will be given over to commercial facilities. They will house pharmacies, shops, hairdressers, bank offices and much more. However, the village of Kudrovo itself is developing so rapidly that even today you can find decent infrastructure facilities there.



As for the social component, there is a kindergarten near the LCD. In addition, after the delivery of the last buildings in one of them will also open a similar institution. In the immediate vicinity of the residential complex there are seven schools; there is a large medical center, several clinics, and a network of pharmacies near the metro. Even immediately after checking in, residents will feel quite comfortable. When the area is at least a little inhabited, then you can completely forget about the notorious center.

The proximity of the metro station is a great blessing for the inhabitant of the metropolis, who lives even in the center, not to mention the outskirts. And in this regard, the current and future apartment owners are lucky. Three and a half kilometers separate them from Dybenko, the station of the orange subway line. Minibuses and buses, the stop of which is located next to the buildings, will reach her in about 15 minutes, and if necessary, you can walk. In addition, there is another huge plus. Not far from the houses in the near future, another station should be opened - Kudrovo.

As for motorists, they are also satisfied. A kilometer from the residential complex is a roundabout, a convenient exit for which is equipped next to the houses. Thanks to this highway, you can quickly reach the center, as well as go to the roads of the Leningrad region.
