Kastalskaya font, Kabardinka. How to get from different cities

You can get to Kabardinka from two sides: from the side of Gelendzhik and from the side of Novorossiysk.

The easiest, fastest and most convenient way: your own car or taxi. However, not everyone wants to pay substantial money or to deprive themselves of the opportunity to taste delicious wines. No problem. From Gelendzhik to Kabardinka, only 11 km, so the journey by regular bus takes no more than half an hour.



You can also come from Anapa and Novorossiysk by bus. There will be certain inconveniences: the road will take about two hours, and there are practically no flights at night. Therefore, when planning such a trip, it is better to take care of the overnight stay.

Even from Krasnodar, you can get to this unique place in just three hours. The bus leaves from the airport. Three hours - and before the tourists there will be one of the modern wonders of nature - Kastalskaya font (Kabardinka). Prices for bus trips are quite affordable.
