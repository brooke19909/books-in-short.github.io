Cheat sheet for the buyer: how to choose a dishwasher

Comfort issues worry a modern person very much, and clean dishes are an integral part of it. That is why, quite naturally, the question may arise, how to choose a dishwasher? In this regard, some recommendations can be made.



Speaking about how to choose a dishwasher, you need to clarify that they can be in two versions: freestanding and built-in furniture in the kitchen. The choice of one or another option depends on the furniture available in your kitchen, as well as on the amount of space there. Not every device is able to fit into the interior quite harmoniously. Different models can be embedded in different ways. Some freestanding models have a removable top panel, instead of which it is quite possible to install a countertop from the headset installed with you, which will solve design issues. Fully built-in models are hidden behind the furniture.

Giving advice on how to choose a dishwasher, it should be said that a very important factor is the washing class, that is, the efficiency of its work. The highest is class A, and the lowest is E. The drying class is also important, which is denoted in the same way. It should be noted that some machines wash better and others dry, so there is no optimal model choice, everyone should choose the right solution for themselves.





If we talk about how to choose the right dishwasher, it is necessary to mention the presence of different washing programs. The more such programs the device knows, the better. They differ in temperature conditions, duration and other parameters. When discussing how to choose a dishwasher, you need to consider the issue of the location of the basket for dishes. Each appliance of this category implies the presence of several shelves and compartments for washing different dishes: separately for pans, cups, pots and plates. It is good if these compartments and shelves can be adjusted depending on the number and dimensions of the loaded dishes. Best of all, if you carry out all the necessary manipulations without moving the basket. For example, there are models that are equipped with a system that provides the ability to install shelves in the required position through special levers and handles. In some models, you can adjust not only the height, but also the angle of the shelves.
