Waterpark in Maryino - rest day and night

The water park in Maryino is located on the territory of one of the largest shopping and entertainment centers, which offers its visitors the widest selection of entertainment for every taste. Without exaggeration, we can say that this is a very popular and famous water park in Moscow. Ticket prices

Without a doubt, one of the best ideas for an original weekend break is a trip to the water park in Maryino. Ticket price varies depending on the time of day and day of the week.
