Wooden art of the Vologda region

The Vologda Oblast (the sights here are mostly old and have survived many sorrows in their lifetime) has a huge number of monuments of wooden architecture. They are represented by ancient chapels, houses, farm buildings and churches. The tree embodies all the main styles of architecture of the late XVIII century and early XX. All directions are presented here: from classicism and empire to modernism with rationalism. The following urban buildings made of wood have survived to this day: apartment buildings, mansions owned by merchants, noblemen and bourgeois, and public buildings .
