Several reasons to visit the Yaroslavl Zoo

Yaroslavl Zoo is located near the city of Yaroslavl in the village of Shevelyukha. Its official opening took place in 2008 and was dedicated to the millennium celebration of the city. The zoo covers an area of ‚Äã‚Äãmore than 100 hectares, which is home to about 250 species of animals.

An interesting and fun adventure will be a trip to the Yaroslavl Zoo, whose working hours are from 10 a.m. to 6 p.m. seven days a week. Cash desks stop working at 17 o‚Äôclock. Horse riding stops at 16.30.



You can get to the zoo by car or public transport. When planning a trip, it is better to choose buses No. 21 and No. 25 or minibus No. 93. It is worth mentioning the cost of entering the Yaroslavl Zoo. The ticket price for adult visitors is 250 rubles, for children from 7 to 14 years old - 150 rubles, under 7 years old - for free. The ticket also provides free entry to the exoterrarium. In addition, you can visit the artiodactyl park, ride a pony or participate in the "Fishing" attraction.

Yaroslavl Zoo is unique in its structure. All animals live in enclosures, the living conditions of which are as close to natural as possible. This is done for the comfort of the inhabitants, who in favorable conditions begin to breed naturally. The purpose of such an interesting approach is not to maximize profit, but to create and develop a special worldview. The founders' philosophy is to focus on the conservation of wildlife and help for the survival of rare species.



At the moment, more than 1600 individuals of various animals live in the zoo. Among them you can see kangaroos and bears, llamas and zebras, as well as deer, foxes and wolves, storks and pheasants, fallow deer and ostrich. One of the most unusual and rare inhabitants of the zoo can be called the two-headed royal snake.

Yaroslavl Zoo is famous for another interesting and unusual project. On its territory is a unique demonstration center "Ark". The three-story building is divided into several sectors, each of which contains animals of various species. Here you can observe the life and habits of fish and reptiles, plants and insects.

A special contact sector allows you to touch some animals and play with them. Basically there are pigs, goats, chickens and other non-predatory representatives of the fauna, communication with which can not harm visitors.

Yaroslavl Zoo is famous for its lectures and meetings with scientists, biologists. The purpose of the educational activities of the organization is to instill a new worldview for visitors and their children. After all, the more people learn to love and respect nature, the better will be the life of all mankind.





At the zoo you can also participate in various thematic excursions, ride animals and take pictures with them. Special group programs for students are aimed at the general development of children and instilling in them a love of the environment. For people with disabilities, special programs have been developed that help improve the emotional state of participants.
