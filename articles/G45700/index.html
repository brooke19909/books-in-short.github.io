Larissa Beach Club Side 4 * (Turkey, Side): description of rooms, service, reviews

Going on a long-awaited trip to hot Turkey, many stop at the notorious Side resort, which is famous not only for its sandy beaches and the clear waters of the Mediterranean Sea, but also for its incredibly varied entertainment: from water parks to night clubs. Here, absolutely everyone will find something to their liking. Due to its history, there are numerous historical attractions that can easily fill the needs of lovers of sightseeing tourism. Antique buildings, ruins of buildings and fortresses, museums with expositions will be entertaining not only for adults, but also for young children.

The four-star Larissa Beach Club Side Hotel service offers upscale. Here, as tourists note, everyone will find comfortable accommodation near the sea at affordable prices.

general characteristics

Despite the long history of its existence, which dates back to 1989, this hotel complex in Side (Turkey) does not lose popularity these days. The last global modernization and repair was carried out in 2014. On the open spaces, whose total area reaches 19,000 square meters, there are nine snow-white four-story buildings. No elevator. The courtyard is planted with lush trees, leafy palm trees and exotic plants. Daytime and evening walks along the well-groomed alleys give guests a lot of pleasure, which is noted in their many reviews.

Location and settlement rules

The good location is due to the proximity to the airport of international importance, where most tourists fly. This distance is 55 km. A short transfer after a flight, according to experienced, is not at all tiring. Beautiful landscapes, clean air and acquaintance with a colorful nation evoke positive emotions among new arrivals.

Pets are strictly prohibited. Smoking in rooms is not allowed. Three standard apartments are equipped with everything necessary for people with disabilities. Payment is possible both in cash and with bank cards. Check-in time is standard - 14:00. Leave the chambers before 12:00. The language barrier is eliminated thanks to staff who additionally speaks Russian and English.

Housing Options

The number of rooms is represented by 323 apartments, which vary in landscape views and spaciousness: Standard and Family Room. The smallest dimensions are 20 square meters, the largest - 36 squares. The interior of the chambers is restrained and overnight is incomparable. There are no superfluous elements - everything is organically combined and complements each other.

On the walls are paintings. The floors are covered with ceramic tiles. Light pastel colors and all shades of white prevail in the color palette. There is access to a balcony where you can meet the first rays of the sun or have a romantic dinner, admiring the starry sky. The room is equipped with numerous lamps that illuminate it in the dark.

Cleaning is carried out daily; bed linen is changed twice a week. Tourists note that maids treat their work in good faith. Everything here sparkles with cleanliness, which makes you feel cozy and comfortable.

Larissa Beach Club Side: room description

The furniture set in the hotel rooms is standard: a bed, an ottoman, a bedside table, a chest of drawers, a wardrobe and a large mirror. The presence of an individual split system and a TV with Russian-language channels allows you to watch your favorite TV show in comfortable conditions. Using the safe requires additional fees. Minibar is empty upon check-in. Wi-Fi is only available in public areas. The phone provides instant communication with the reception and lightning fast solution to all emerging problems.

The bathroom is decorated with ceramic tiles and is equipped with a sink, bathtub and hairdryer. All personal care products are present.

Larissa Beach Club Side: types of food

The richness and variety of outdoor activities requires reinforcing the body with nutritious food throughout the day. To quench hunger and thirst, the Larissa Beach Club Side Hotel adheres to the current All Inclusive system. According to this concept, four meals a day are organized in the form of a buffet. The buffet way of serving dishes and self-service allows travelers to impose their own meals in the amount that suits them.

Tourists note in their positive reviews about Larissa Beach Club Side 4 * that the abundance of served masterpieces can easily satisfy the needs of even the most demanding vacationers. The food here is amazingly tasty, hearty, prepared exclusively from fresh products. The consumption of all drinks of local production is included in the price of the permit.

The interior of the main restaurant is strict and discreet. Individual serving, fresh flowers on the tables and comfortable leather chairs make eating easy and laid back. The atmosphere is conducive to communication.

You can taste the creations of bartenders with the unique taste characteristics of their cocktails in one of the bars, of which there are three: on the beach, by the pool and in the lobby. In the daytime, various snacks are served.
