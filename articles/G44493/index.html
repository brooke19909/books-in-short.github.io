Modern versions

Attempts to understand how Nefertiti looked like, and scientists in recent years. So, the British scanned the mummy, which allegedly belonged to her. They noted that the resulting look is very similar to her bust.

In life

There is evidence that Nefertiti used cosmetics. She whitened her face, emphasized her facial features. So, she maintained her skin tone, taking a bath with incense several times a day - in the morning and before going to bed. The recipes for creams and masks that she used could not be deciphered.

One of the most famous sculptures of the legendary queen was discovered in 1912. She immediately made a vivid impression on the finders. She is kept in the Egyptian Museum of Berlin. In addition, the Germans discovered her bust. But Egyptian customs would not let them pass with him, and they covered the bust with plaster. After 20 years, the Egyptian authorities demanded the return of the historical find to the country, but were refused. Then they banned the excavation in Egypt.
