Hotel Centara Karon Resort. Karon Beach - one of the best beaches in Phuket

If you prefer comfortable, but inexpensive hotels, and the island of Phuket is chosen as a place to relax, the Centara Karon Resort 4 * hotel can be a very suitable place for you to stay during your holidays in Thailand.





General information, location, photo

This hotel is a complex of four zones with buildings of different floors. The last renovation was carried out in 2006. The hotel's housing stock is represented by 335 rooms of superior, deluxe and deluxe categories. Some apartments have sea views.

The hotel has several pools, there are restaurants, a bar, a spa, a beauty salon, a souvenir shop, a medical office, a laundry room, a conference room, a gym, sports grounds, and an animation program is held.

Centara Karon Resort Phuket 4 * on the map is quite easy to find. So, the hotel is located in the northern part of Karon Bay, across the road from the beach of the same name. The distance from the hotel to the sea is 300 meters.





Centara Karon Resort: Russian traveler reviews

Modern tourists do not need to explain how important it is with all responsibility and attention to approach the issue of planning a trip abroad. This is especially true for the choice of the hotel in which you plan to stay during the holidays. After all, everyone wants to settle in a comfortable room with comfortable furniture and properly working appliances, have a pleasant view from the window and count on good service and a friendly attitude of the staff. An invaluable source of information about hotels, according to many travelers, are the reviews of those who have recently stayed in a particular place. Indeed, in their comments, former guests try to share not only positive impressions, but also talk about negative aspects. In order not to waste time and effort searching and analyzing the opinions of tourists, we bring to your attention the generalized reviews of our compatriots who have recently visited the Centara Karon Resort (Karon Beach, Phuket). It is immediately worth noting that almost all hotel guests were very pleased with their choice. But we learn about everything in more detail.





Number of rooms

Judging by the reviews of Russian tourists, they liked the apartments in which they stayed in this hotel. True, some guests argue that some of the rooms should soon be updated somewhat. However, the overall impression is very good. So, according to our compatriots, the rooms here are spacious, bright, with good quality furniture and properly functioning appliances. Also, the apartments have good sound insulation. So nothing will stop you from resting both during the day and at night.





Cleaning

There were no complaints from the Centara Karon Resort (Karon Beach) guests on this item. So, according to the guests, the hotel maids do their job perfectly, conducting regular cleaning, changing towels and updating bed linen. In addition, as you use it, you will replenish the supplies of personal hygiene products in the bathroom and drinking water in the minibar. Also keep in mind that if you need something extra, then you can always contact the maid in your building.

Check in, departure

There are internal rules at the Centara Karon Resort (Phuket). 5 *, by the way, is the level to which the hotel is rapidly moving forward. Accommodation of newly arrived guests in the apartments intended for them begins after two in the afternoon. However, as tourists note, in practice, often everything happens differently. Thus, reception administrators always try their best to accommodate travelers who have just arrived in their rooms as soon as possible. Therefore, even if you arrive at Centara Resort early in the morning, most likely you will be given the keys to the apartments long before checkout time. Such an approach, many tourists considered a great advantage of this hotel. Also, guests arriving from Russia and other CIS countries are usually met by a Russian-speaking administrator. He will talk about the features and rules of the hotel and answer all questions.

As for the day of departure, the occupied room should be vacated before noon. If you plan to leave the hotel only in the evening, then you can either extend your stay for an additional fee, or leave things in the luggage room and continue rest until the time comes to go to the airport.





the Internet

According to guests, free Wi-Fi at Centara Karon Resort (Phuket, Thailand) can be used only in the lobby near the reception. In the rooms access is available at an additional cost. However, our compatriots advise you to purchase a SIM card of a local mobile operator (parts of vacationers are issued by representatives of the tour operator when meeting at the airport) and use mobile Internet. You can replenish the balance in one of the nearest shops. According to travelers, such an Internet is inexpensive and makes it possible to always be in touch.

Territory, location

Judging by their comments, our compatriots were very pleased with the territory of the Center Caron Resort. So, according to them, it is quite large for hotels in Thailand. There are a lot of trees, greenery, vibrant colors and nice places where you can take a walk, relax or take great photos to commemorate your stay at Centara Karon Resort.

Karon Beach, Phuket's most famous beach , is just a 10-minute leisurely walk from the hotel. Largely due to this, the choice of most tourists fell on this hotel. In general, according to our compatriots, the location of the hotel is very successful. So, in addition to the excellent beach, in its vicinity there are a lot of supermarkets, shops, souvenir shops, cafes, bars, restaurants, massage parlors, excursion bureaus, car rental points, etc.



Nutrition

When booking a room at Centara Karon Resort (Phuket, Thailand), travelers can choose one of the options: breakfast only or half board. Most vacationers stop at the first option. According to the guests, breakfast at Centara Karon Beach Resort 4 * is organized in buffet style. So, in the morning you can eat fresh fruits and vegetables, bacon, fried eggs, omelettes, boiled eggs, cheese, sausages, sausages, pastries and sweets. However, as guests of the hotel note, the menu here for breakfast, although varied, is the same every day.

As for lunches and dinners, guests can eat both in the Centara Resort restaurant and in the many cafes located in its vicinity. The prices here, according to our compatriots, are quite low, and the quality is almost everywhere excellent.

In addition, many tourists visited the market, which operates two days a week. Here you can buy fresh fruits and vegetables, as well as purchase food in a mobile kiosk (macarate). According to travelers, one should not be afraid to buy food prepared in this way, since not only foreigners, but also local residents buy it here.

Karon Beach - one of the best beaches in Phuket

As already mentioned, the Centara Resort Hotel was chosen by many tourists precisely because of its proximity to Karon Beach. After all, this beach is considered one of the most beautiful and cleanest in these places. Moreover, both young people and family people with children like to relax here. According to our compatriots, they were fascinated by the snow-white fine sand and the warm sea. True, there are often waves, and you should swim carefully, as there are strong undercurrents in this place. Despite the fact that lifeguards are constantly on duty at the beach, it is not recommended to relax when swimming in the sea, and even more so to go swimming while intoxicated.
