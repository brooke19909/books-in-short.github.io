Florence Hotels - The Best Conditions for Connoisseurs of Fine

Florence is a museum city that has managed to maintain its historical image in order to be admired by the thousands of eyes that tourists come here every year.

In the Middle Ages, the city was the center of the Florentine Republic and was under the control of the influential Medici. There are still many monuments of that era. The Medici were famous patrons who patronized many cultural figures, including Michelangelo, Botticelli, Ghiberti, Donatello, Gozzoli, whose works can still be seen in Florence today - they are stored in the famous Uffiza Gallery.

Holidays in Florence can be successfully combined with sightseeing , including the Palazzo della Signoria, the Galleries of the Academy, the Cathedral of Santa Maria del Fiore, the Ponte Vecchio bridge, Piazza Santa Maria Novella and the Boboli Gardens.

You can enjoy Florence by settling in any hotel in the city. From each of them you can easily get to the place you are interested in. However, the hotels are very different from each other . There are five-star luxury palace hotels, there are budget options that are just a few blocks away. So there are great opportunities for choosing apartments at will and means.

Florence hotels are divided into unique views: castles, 5 * luxury hotels, 5 * hotels, Sup 4 * hotels, 4 * hotels and 3 * hotels.

Reviews of Florence hotels allow you to create a rating of the most interesting, memorable and distinguished by the quality of hotel services. The best hotels in Florence are recognized as follows.

Five Star Hotels of Florence

Savoy 5 * Lux

The hotel is located in the famous historic square called Piazza della Repubblica. Nearby is the symbol of the city - the Duomo Cathedral, as well as a number of fashion houses, museums, galleries. Refined interior, high level of service, excellent Italian cuisine will not leave indifferent any traveler.

Villa Olmi 5 * Lux

This hotel is located in a stunningly beautiful old park and resembles a country palace. This residence is even called the "Gateway of Florence", which emphasizes the significance and high level of the hotel.

Residenza Del Moro 5 * Lux

The Niccolini-Bourbon Palace, in which the hotel is located today, is a unique example of Italian architecture, in which decors of different eras intertwined: Renaissance, Romanticism and Baroque.



Four stars hotels of Florence



Continentale 4 * Sup

Built in national style and emphasizing the local flavor with all the details of the interior and excellent service, this hotel is very prestigious and worthy.

De La Ville 4 * Sup

This famous hotel is one of the most luxurious and popular in the city. The hotel is located on the most prestigious street - Via Tornabuoni. Famous tourists stay here, remaining satisfied with excellent service and standard of living.

Embassy 4 *

This prestigious hotel is located near the cultural center of the city, as well as Santa Maria Novella train station and exhibition center. Incredible elegance of the atmosphere, a high level of European service, comfortable living conditions.

Athenaeum 4 *

Located in the historical center, minutes from famous attractions, museums, the congress center and the exhibition complex.

3 Star Hotels Florence

 

The best 3-star Florence hotel reviews are for the following hotels.

Hotel Le Due Fontane 3 *

The hotel was built in the era of neoclassicism, but the interior is absolutely modern. Comfortable and very cozy.

Corona d'Italia 3 *

This hotel has been recently refurbished. It is located in the city center, near the Santa Maria Novella square and the Cathedral of Santa Maria dei Fiori.

Hotel Machiavelli 3 *

Located in the business and historical part of the city, not far from the station. Here is a good service, excellent opportunities for booking excursions.

Hotel Ambasciatori 3 *

This is a very modern hotel, located near the center of Florence, very close to the Uffizi Gallery.
