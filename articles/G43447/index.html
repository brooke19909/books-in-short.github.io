Hotel Danubius Hotel Budapest 4 * (Budapest, Hungary): overview, description and reviews

Hungary is a wonderful country of the near abroad. Basically, the journey of tourists begins with the capital - Budapest. And in most cases, the question arises before them: "Where to settle?" According to travelers reviews, in Budapest, one of the favorite places is the Danubius Hotel Budapest 4 * hotel.





Hotel Benefits

The location of the hotel is perfect for those who decide to stay for a while in the capital. The hotel is surrounded by green residential areas, within walking distance of the Buddhist hills and the Buddhist fortress, which offers a wonderful view of the city. The hotel is conveniently accessible by all means of public transport. Szell Kalmanter Underground Station is just a 10-minute walk away.

Breakfast at the Danubius Hotel Budapest 4 * (Budapest, Hungary) is nutritious and quite tasty. A buffet is available upon request. For those who want to taste the true national cuisine, a restaurant is open. It serves visitors for lunch and dinner. In summer, guests can dine on the seasonal terrace with pleasant city views. The hotel also holds all kinds of promotions for customers, for example, free coffee and a croissant in the morning.





Availability and room facilities

Danubius Hotel Budapest (Budapest, Hungary) offers the following room types:

  1. Standard double room with 2 beds (from 90 euros per night). This room has satellite TV, there are 24-hour channels that must be paid separately. Present telephone, hairdryer, Internet (Wi-Fi). You can quench your thirst or just relax using the minibar. Some rooms have a safe. Beds - two separate single beds.
  2. Standard Double Room (from 102 Euro per night). In this room, the equipment is exactly the same as in the above. The only difference is that the sleeping place is presented in the form of one double bed.
  3. "Suite" (Junior apartments) - from 140 euros per night. There are only 9 rooms of this class on the hotel premises. They are located on the top floor of the hotel, as a result of which they have amazing views of the city. The room has a private bathroom, satellite TV with 24-hour pay-per-view channel, hairdryer, telephone, Wi-Fi. And, of course, a minibar. Absolutely all rooms have safes.

There is also a system of discounts when ordering a room for more than 3 days. It can be up to 50% of the cost. As you can see, in the Danubius Hotel Budapest 4 * hotel the prices for accommodation are quite acceptable. A little later we will talk about some additional expenses.





Terms of Accommodation

In the Danubius Hotel Budapest 4 * hotel (Hungary, Budapest) there is a possibility of booking through online services, and also by phone. If you really want to live in this hotel, you should book at least a week before arrival.

Also a pleasant moment is that children under 12 years old with extra beds are absolutely free. You can also check in with your pet, but you should discuss this with the manager. Additional charges may apply.

Check-in at the hotel starts from 14:00, and check-out until 10:00.

You can use American Express, Visa, MasterCard and Danubius Club cards when paying for your stay.

Additional expenses

It should be noted that in some hotels located on the territory of Hungary, additional funds may be charged for a certain number of services, the Danubius Hotel Budapest 4 * described does not become an exception. The following fees exist here:

  1. A city tax of 350 HUF per person per night (slightly more than $ 1). The tax is not applicable to children under 18 years of age. It is important to understand that this amount may vary depending on the class of the room (price is for a standard room).
  2. Self parking fee - 2800 forint per night (about 10 dollars).
  3. Accommodation with pets - 5500 forint per night (about $ 20).
  4. Payment for wireless Internet in the room - 1000 forint per hour (3-4 dollars).
  5. Payment for breakfast - 2475 forints per person (8-9 dollars).

Amounts are shown on average and may vary depending on currency rate fluctuations.





Facilities & Services

You can breathe fresh air and watch life in the city from the terrace on a small elevation.

In order to relax and unwind, you can visit the hotel's sauna. Or head to the bar and enjoy delicious drinks and cocktails to the music.
Wi-Fi is free in all public areas of the hotel.

For travelers, there is public parking on their car, it is not necessary to pre-book it. This service is paid and amounts to 11 euros per day.

The Danubius Hotel Budapest 4 * has luggage storage, and check-in is available around the clock. For foreign tourists, a currency exchange is provided.

Among the business services, the opportunity to use fax and photocopying facilities is provided. For meetings or gatherings there is a conference room. There is also a business center.

Among the general services provided by the hotel are the following:

  • paid transfer to or from the airport;
  • smoking areas are present;
  • air conditioning;
  • non-smoking rooms;
  • delivery of drinks and food to the room;
  • press delivery.

Most of the staff speaks three languages: English, German, Hungarian. This significantly reduces the language barrier for foreign tourists.





Hotel Interior

The building itself resembles a tower, it is almost round and rises above the terrain like a guard. Although the hotel does not look ultra-modern from the outside, inside it absolutely corresponds to the present time.

The lobby is executed in white-gold and chocolate tones, which adds to the hotel a certain pomp and presentability. There are halls where you can spend time lounging in comfortable leather chairs. This is convenient for both those who are registering and for guests. The entire hotel is designed in the same style and does not have a cutting-edge swings and inserts. This allows guests to truly relax in this institution.

Bathrooms are equipped with the latest plumbing, and are very clean. The rooms have plasma TVs, successfully placed in the interior. The rooms are mostly very comfortable and with a delicate decor.

Positive qualities of the hotel, marked by guests

Danubius Hotel Budapest 4 * reviews are overwhelmingly positive. Guests note such interruption advantages as a wonderful view from the window, convenient location and proximity to the center. Also pleases tourists with a large number of attractions around and around the hotel at night. Some note the perfect cleanliness both in the rooms and in the hotel itself, as well as the willingness of the staff to help in any matter for some reward. One of the main advantages of the hotel, visitors consider affordable price and price-quality compliance.





Negative qualities of the hotel, marked by guests

Danubius Hotel Budapest 4 * (Budapest) has negative reviews, however, like every hotel. First of all, they all come down to the fact that there is rather unusual food, but, as you know, food is a matter of taste, and simply many may not like national Hungarian cuisine, but the restaurant also offers international dishes on the menu. The remaining unsatisfactory reviews of tourists were based either on personal hostility to the staff, or on the fact that the hotel did not meet their luxury requirements. The most common complaints regarding the cleanliness of the rooms. Tourists note in their reviews that it is worth reminding about cleaning and changing bed linen. But it should be remembered that Danubius Hotel Budapest 4 * does not position itself as an institution with exorbitant luxury, but is the owner of a more modest interior. Moreover, the ratio of "price-quality" here, according to many, is the most optimal.





Who is this hotel for?

First of all, this is a wonderful place for those who did not come to bed in bed and watch TV, but for those who want to know the city, country. The Danubius Hotel Budapest 4 * hotel makes its location very comfortable trips around the city, at any time you can easily return to your room and relax. Breakfast at the hotel is basically all the food that is needed in it, because when traveling around the city, a tourist will probably want to go to some kind of restaurant and try something unusual there. The indisputable advantage of the hotel is its budget. It does not apply to cheap hostels, its prices correspond to what awaits you in the room and the hotel itself. This hotel is perfect for those people who want to see the beauty of Hungary, but do not have too much money.

When traveling to Budapest, pay attention to this place, perhaps it is for you that it will give the necessary comfort and good mood. In addition, due to the excellent location of the hotel you can visit a huge number of attractions, just walking several blocks.
