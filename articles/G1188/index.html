Terminology

Traffic police officers are armed with a small but very effective range of radars that can easily calculate the speed of a moving object, whether it be the Oka or a heavy truck.

Drivers, in turn, can counter radar detectors with such devices. The best models can detect a radio signal at a great distance and warn the owner about an "ambush" or approaching a stationary high-speed camera. Such devices are legal, legal and convenient - they will be discussed in this article.

Traffic police radars

The enemy must be known in person. Otherwise, this game will go blindly and with one goal. So, the guard service technique in Russia now works mainly on two bands - these are “X” and “K”, 10.525 GHz and 24.15 GHz, respectively, and the latter can be found in a variety of low-power K-Pulse-band only at 24.01 GHz.





If we talk about the functionality and principles of operation of such devices, there are three varieties in total: with constant radiation, pulsed and off-screen (Instant-on). The latter are already a thing of the past due to their inefficiency (jamming the signal by motorists). As for the first two, they come in the form of mobile or stationary systems, and the best radar detectors should easily recognize them.
