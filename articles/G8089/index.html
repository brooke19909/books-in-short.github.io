Unforgettable vacation: Babakh-Tarama - description of the village, accommodation, reviews of vacationers

If you prefer a quiet relaxing holiday, Babakh-Tarama is the village that you should pay attention to.

You can walk along the coast, however, in order to get to the village, you will need to return to the highway towards Berdyansk and then down to the sea. The village is located on a hill. A steep cliff rises above the sea, and the views of the sea surface are simply magnificent.

Housing

The village offers its guests a quiet and relaxing holiday. Babakh-Tarama as a resort has been known not so long ago, one can say that the resort infrastructure of the village is under development. In the summer, the bustling Urzuf is crowded with tourists, thanks to this a simple fishing village is slowly gaining the attention of vacationers.

Private guest houses are the main housing of the village of Babah-Tarama. Pensions, recreation centers and mini-hotels are all located near the sea, as there are only three streets in the village. In almost every yard you can rent a house for every taste and pocket. The owners of the guest houses are very welcoming and hospitable, trying to provide maximum comfort and create ideal conditions for relaxation.

Among the guest houses of the village, some recreation centers stand out. So, for example, the recreation center "Heat", which is ten meters from the sea. As many as eight new cozy wooden log cabins accept vacationers and offer ideal conditions and a wonderful vacation.

Babakh-Tarama, although a small village, but there are also large pensions here. The recreation center "Zhiguli" has a territory of a little more than a hectare.

Wild holiday

Babakh Tarama, in addition to housing in the private sector, is famous for its camping. Immediately behind the village is a fairly large tent city. For a nominal fee, lovers of wild relaxation have the opportunity to stay in their favorite place.





Between Urzuf and Babakh-Tarama there is an equipped tent camp called "Cliff". The campsite is landscaped, there is lighting, electricity, many trees, a barbecue area, water, toilets and showers.

Small summer houses are for rent on the territory, or you can put up your own tents.

Guest reviews

The main attraction of the village is the Sea of ‚Äã‚ÄãAzov. Babah Tarama is proud of its wide beaches and the cleanest sea on the coast. The guests of the village in their reviews confirm this, indicating the quality of the sea as one of the main advantages.

Another plus of rest in Babakh-Taram is housing prices. They are very affordable and much lower than in neighboring Urzuf. Value for money suits almost all tourists.

In their reviews, vacationers say that Babah Tarama is a very quiet village. Fans of entertainment and nightlife here will not be interested. But for families with children, vacationing in age and just lovers of peace and solitude with nature, a vacation in the village will be to your taste.

The local market is able to meet the needs of tourists. They say that you can buy everything here - fish, products, seasonal vegetables and fruits, beach items and souvenirs.





Of the shortcomings, some vacationers distinguish a steep descent to the sea. In order to go down to the beach, you have to overcome a number of steps.

If you prefer a quiet vacation, a clean sea and do not like the night noise of discos, then you will really like it in Babakh-Taram, because there is everything for a good rest at affordable prices.
