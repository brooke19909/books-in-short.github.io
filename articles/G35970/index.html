Izberbash hotels - holidays on the Caspian Sea

Do you want to relax in Russia, but are you tired of the Black and Azov Seas? Pay attention to the Caspian! In Dagestan, on the shores of the Caspian Sea, there is the resort town of Izberbash, whose hotels and large clean beaches are famous throughout the south.





Rest in Izberbash

Positive and negative moments of relaxation on the Caspian coast:

  1. Great free beach. It is equipped with all necessary beach equipment, sunbeds, sun awnings and changing rooms. The shower cabins are supplied with water from thermal springs. The sand is fine-grained and is cleaned daily with a special sand-cleaning machine. Rescuers are on duty daily. And another important point: the length of the Izberbash beach is three kilometers and a width of two hundred meters. On such a beach, even the most fastidious tourists will not be crowded.
  2. Mild climate. There are as many as two hundred seventy warm days in Izberbash, and there are practically no rains.
  3. Dagestan is a Muslim republic, which imposes certain obligations on guests.

Hotel prices Izberbash

The cost of living per room per day starts from a thousand rubles. As elsewhere, the final price will depend on several factors, such as the level of the hotel, the distance from the sea, the popularity of the vacation destination and time of stay.

If you wish, you can find a hotel on the seashore in Izberbash, the price of which in June will be one thousand rubles.

Examples of inexpensive holiday homes

Cost from 1000 to 3000 rubles per room per day:

1. Guest house on 38a Richard Sorge street. The cost of rest in this place in June is only a thousand rubles. The house is located right on the beach, the beach is visible from the windows. Year of construction - 2017.





2. Izberbash Hotel "Holiday Park Priboy" in the village of Primorsky. Located on the shore of its own private beach. In this place you can have breakfast, relax in the bar, leaving the children in the playground. The rooms are equipped with all necessary amenities. Room rates start at one and a half thousand rubles per day.

3. Apartments and rooms "At Nina" on the street Richard Sorge, 31. The cost is from three thousand rubles. Like previous options, this Izberbash hotel is located on the seashore. It features a barbecue, TV, and a private kitchen. Each room has a private bathroom.

4. Hotel on the street Seaside, 1, is also located on the first coastline. They even accept animals here. On the street there is a relaxation area with sea views. According to the owners of the hotel, this place is fully suitable for people with disabilities.

Hotels in the city of Izberbash costing from 3,500 to 7,000 rubles per room per night:

1. The Holiday Park Sail recreation center offers rooms of three thousand and five hundred rubles per day. The beach, as in previous versions, can be reached in 1 minute. The room has a private bathroom, air conditioning, Wi-Fi. There is a bar on site.





2. Mini-hotel "Izba" on the street Primorsky, 2 offers accommodation services for five thousand rubles per day per room. Spacious rooms with many wooden details in the interior. Each room has a TV, air conditioning, a bathroom and a terrace. Pets are allowed. In the yard there are benches for rest. There is a private parking. A piece of the beach is reserved only for Izba guests.

It is possible to find accommodation per person per day. For example, the Relax Hotel on 2nd Primorskaya Street, 35. The cost of a room in such a hotel is 400 rubles per person per day. The rooms are standard, with everything you need. There is a cafe where for a fee you can get three meals a day. In the evenings, dancing and recreational activities are held.

All the staff of the hotels in Izberbash speaks Russian. You can order a transfer from the airport to the place of rest. Entertainment services such as fishing, boat trips or sightseeing are also offered. The only negative is the distance to the Uytash airport is 33-40 km.

If desired, cheaper offers can be found in Dagestan, but such hotels will be located on the second or third coastline. If you like to walk or come with your own transport, then you can save on the cost of living.

Finally

After studying the price offers, it became clear that the cost of rest in Dagestan in many ways coincides with the Krasnodar Territory. But it must be borne in mind that the resorts of the Kuban have been developing for several decades, and Izberbash has only recently begun to focus on the tourism business.
