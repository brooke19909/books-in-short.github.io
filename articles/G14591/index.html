Camera Canon Powershot SX510 HS: reviews, photos and specifications

The popularity of budget cameras in a compact form factor in recent years has noticeably subsided. And even manufacturers' attempts to expand the functionality of cameras against the background of lower prices did not save the situation. Moreover, the optional advantages did not change the situation with the quality of images to an adequate extent. Users are increasingly interested in the result, and not the presence of additional settings. Nevertheless, in some areas inexpensive compact models remain in high demand, which is confirmed by the Canon Powershot SX510 HS, an overview of which is presented below. The developers of this version managed to combine good functionality with a price tag and good ergonomics. However, other factors contributed to the popularity of this model.

Model Overview





Development is a continuation of the concept that was laid down in the previous version of the SX500 IS. Among the main features of the family, which allowed to win the love of fans, it is worth noting the presence of a 30-fold superzoom. Externally, the Canon Powershot SX510 HS Black is slightly different from the early modification, but significant changes have occurred in the filling. In the new version, the device acquired a simplified matrix, as a result of which the number of pixels was reduced from 16 to 12.1 million.

Despite a sharp decrease in resolution, the device did not lose so much in the quality of the resulting images. This was facilitated by the new HS high-sensitivity system. This technology eliminates the need for a tripod or flash when using the Canon Powershot SX510 HS. Reviews indicate that the highly sensitive CMOS sensor made the camera virtually independent of environmental conditions. This means that even in poor lighting conditions, the operator can count on decent quality - of course, within the capabilities of this camera.

Design and ergonomics





Externally, the camera looks solid and even leads to associations with a professional DSLR. Although the model is positioned as a compact camera, compared to other representatives of the segment, it stands out in massive dimensions. In addition, the case has an irregular shape, which also adds originality to the overall image of the Canon Powershot SX510 HS. Black color in this case does not create the feeling of a cheap product, but rather makes the camera more presentable. As for the control, it is implemented by traditional mechanical elements. I must say that massiveness affected the mass, so it‚Äôs difficult to talk about the ease of manipulating the device. Simple tuning elements, which are represented by a wheel, a trigger and conveniently located buttons, save the situation.

Shooting quality





With average operating parameters and a low number of pixels, the device provides a good shooting result. This is partly facilitated by the ability to automatically adjust the photosensitivity, although not everything is perfect here. For example, evening landscapes with good detail can leave "grain" in the pictures. It is worth noting the advantages of the zoom, which is supplied with the Canon Powershot SX510 HS. The manual notes that the lens has 13 optical elements that work with a 30x zoom. Thanks to such equipment, the device can be very useful in the hands of a traveler when shooting panoramas and landscapes. However, of course, there is no need to talk about the quality of images by the standards of professional models. The creators were able to squeeze the most out of the capabilities of a modest matrix, but this model could not go beyond the level of the budget apparatus.

Positive reviews about the camera





The opinions of users regarding the merits of the camera are quite diverse. An unusual and attractive design, thoughtful configuration of the controls, balanced settings without unnecessary useless options and overall decent picture quality are noted. But here it must be borne in mind that the Canon Powershot SX510 HS is mainly used by inexperienced amateurs and beginners who are undemanding to the shooting possibilities. From the point of view of professionals, this is an entry-level model, which, however, looks good against the background of other representatives of the class. And do not forget about the price tag of the device in 10-12 thousand rubles. For the opportunities that this model has, the cost is very attractive. The presence of a 30x zoom and an extensive range of photosensitivity still not every device of this level boasts.

Negative reviews

Many users criticize the camera for an obsolete 12.1 megapixel matrix. Perhaps this drawback is the main factor that did not allow the model to rise above the budget level. There are also complaints about the Canon Powershot SX510 HS battery. Reviews for comparison lead analogues from other manufacturers of compact amateur cameras, which are characterized by longer battery life. The shooting itself also occupies a separate place in criticism, but one has to put up with shortcomings of this kind, since one does not have to expect more from the model from the lowest level. There are improvements in a number of parameters, but at the heart of the technical filling is capable of providing at best an average level of quality.

Conclusion





Increasing requirements for cameras in the budget segment forces manufacturers to make concessions in prices and, if possible, improve the operational performance of these products. Far from always improving the basic characteristics, as shown by the example of Canon Powershot SX510 HS. Reviews about this camera focus on secondary parameters, leaving modest picture quality behind default brackets. For example, a solid design and implementation of new options is noted, but the capabilities of the matrix are perceived as an unchanging given for a device from a budget group. On the other hand, against the background of compact cheap cameras, this offer also looks quite attractive - especially for undemanding beginners.
