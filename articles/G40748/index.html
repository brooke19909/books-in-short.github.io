How to get from Voronezh to Anapa?

Voronezh and Anapa are two wonderful cities located in Russia. Each of them has amazing vegetation, interesting sights, a huge number of beautiful places. To visit two legendary regions located on different ends of the country, each person should at least once in his life! And there are several reasons for this.

Several reasons to visit the city of Voronezh





Why do people buy tickets from Anapa to Voronezh? There are a huge number of famous places in this city that deserve special attention:

  • May Day Garden simply can not help but capture its beauty. Near this attraction is a cozy cafe where you can buy hot sandwiches, sandwiches, rolls and the most aromatic coffee. For many residents of the city, the park is an ideal place for breakfast.
  • In the regional square there are several ancient monuments with their own history.

Magnificent monasteries, houses with an ancient design, animal monuments from famous films - all this is the reason to visit this corner of the Earth.

A few reasons to visit the city of Anapa

Needless to say, why do you need to come from Voronezh to Anapa? This city has long been one of the best resorts in the country. The first thing that fascinates here is the amazing nature. It is worth coming here at least to take a walk along the picturesque promenade.





The main reason that tourists come to this place is the excellent weather. In the summer season, the thermometer rises to 45 degrees. What is no reason to arrange a great resort without leaving the country?

In addition, it is in Anapa that there are many attractions for children: playgrounds, a water park, attractions and much more.

Train moving

The distance from Voronezh to Anapa is about 1000 kilometers, so it is most reasonable to travel by train. Please note that in summer there is a special stir among tourists, so it is recommended to purchase tickets in advance.





This vehicle travels a distance of 16 to 22 hours. The journey begins at the local train station and ends in the very center of the resort area. The minimum ticket price is from 2700 rubles.

The train schedule from Voronezh to Anapa is very convenient: they depart and arrive in the early hours (05.00 - 09.00).

In order to get to the appointed point, you have to drive along the M4 highway and cross the border of Krasnodar. The road was recently repaired, so the trip will bring a lot of positive emotions to the driver.

The total travel time will be about 12 hours. You will need to stock up on gasoline in advance at the rate of about 10 liters per 100 km. It will have to spend 4000-5000 rubles.

Flight by plane

How to get from Voronezh to Anapa in just 5 hours? Of course, this can only be done using an airplane. Several times a week, Aeroflot operates a flight with a transfer in Moscow. This is the most convenient, but at the same time expensive means of transportation. The cost of one ticket will be about 3,500 rubles.





Bus Transfer

The longest way to get from Voronezh to Anapa is by bus. In summer, special tourist flights are organized. The approximate price per person is 1700 rubles. It is worth noting that it will turn out in another city only in no less than 20 hours if there are no traffic jams on the roads, and this is unlikely. In hot weather, sitting so much time in one place is almost impossible.





Conclusion

Which vehicle is best for traveling? Undoubtedly, the choice is to give the train. There are several reasons for this. This is primarily due to the fact that Russian Railways provides comfortable vehicles in which high-quality air conditioners, dry closets are installed, there are several sockets in the car, good conditions and removable bedding. In the summer, this is the best option for moving.

If there is no time to wait long, then it is worth giving preference to the plane. Unfortunately, a vacation in a resort town is not cheap. Therefore, not everyone can have extra money to spend it on a flight.

The most tiring, difficult and long journey is by car and bus, but during the trip people can get acquainted with interesting places of each city. The choice in favor of a particular vehicle can only be done by the tourist. Depending on what the main purpose of his trip is.

Many tourists dream of making a trip from Voronezh to Anapa. Fortunately, this can become a reality. After all, there are several vehicles that will bring this dream closer to reality.
