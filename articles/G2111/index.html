Sony Ericsson WT19I: specifications and firmware

Sony Ericsson WT19I is a typical mid-range smartphone, whose representatives rarely boast of good balance and the absence of serious flaws. This device pleasantly surprised and wins in many respects against the main competitors of its price group. Moreover, his musical capabilities are the pride of the creators, they are really very good. The smartphone also differs from its classmates in an excellent graphics accelerator and a fairly powerful processor.

Appearance



The design of the Sony Ericsson WT19I is characterized by a compact trapezoidal case, chrome buttons and a typical rear panel design. The logo, headphone jack and the Home button have bright inserts. There are two color versions of the case - black and white. The Home button is mechanical, two more keys on the front panel are touch-sensitive, standard elements are above the display. The model does not provide interchangeable panels; under the back cover there is a battery and a slot for a memory card, as well as a 5-megapixel camera. On the right side of the case, buttons for adjusting the shooting and camera control are detected. At the top there is a power button, as well as the start of the player. The screen is no different from representatives of the same class. He is not afraid of scratches due to tempered glass, he also looks pretty decent in the light.

Main components



The Sony Ericsson WT19I hardware platform is not typical of the middle class. She is similar to the flagship model. Competitors are significantly behind in this regard, so the speed of this smartphone is much higher.

Power consumption is far from ideal, like other smartphones based on Android. Charging is enough for a maximum of a day's work. 512 MB of RAM is enough, but the free space on the built-in drive is only about 300 MB, however, a 2 gigabyte card is sold in the kit. Cameras for low-cost smartphones are very decent and have good brightness of pictures, there is also the possibility of creating HD-video.

As for the sound, as mentioned, the smartphone has far gone from competitors. If you use expensive headphones, the quality can not be distinguished from the flagship. In addition, the smartphone can be considered loud.
