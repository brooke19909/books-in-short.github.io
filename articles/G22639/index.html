Zephiros Beach 4 * (Greece / Crete) - photos, reviews of tourists

Zephiros Beach 4 is a hotel that is located in one of the most picturesque places on the planet, where people from all over the world travel to spend several days of good rest. And this is the island of Crete. Indeed, this is the center where the mysterious Minoan civilization was formed - not only lovers of the sea come here, but also connoisseurs of archeology and architectural art. Because where, if not in Greece, this can be admired.





Location

Tourists, choosing the most suitable hotel option, in which one could stay for a few days and be sure that he will fully arrange them in all respects, pay attention to such an important factor as the place where the hotel complex is located. This is really important. For example, a hotel located somewhere on the outskirts of the city will not be popular, unlike a hotel that is located right on the first coastline. And in this case we are talking about Zephiros Beach 4. This hotel is not just near the sea - it is located right on the beach, less than 200 meters separates guests from clear azure water. In addition, I want to note the immediate proximity to the airport - a little more than 20 kilometers. This is also important, as some tourists stay in hotels, which take about one and a half to two hours from the airport. And this is not very pleasant, especially after a difficult flight.





About hotel

A few words should be said regarding the hotel complex itself. It was built not so long ago, but has already managed to gain popularity and establish itself as a budget option with good quality of service. Everyone knows that Greece is a very friendly and hospitable country, and the Zephiros Beach 4 hotel is just as welcoming. It is made in a traditional national style, with pronounced Greek architectural features. I would like to note that the hotel area is very well maintained and neatly decorated. The original octagonal pool, illuminated by bright lights at night, beautiful palm trees, exotic flowers, green vegetation - it's really nice to spend time here.

Near the hotel

It was said that Zephiros Beach 4 is located right on the beach and that the airport is relatively close to it, but this is not all territorial advantages. For example, it was not said that the hotel is located on a very busy street. There are numerous souvenir shops, shops, cafes and taverns. Therefore, if you want to go for a walk - without pleasant souvenir shopping, the tourist will definitely not return to the hotel. Options and cost of accommodation

There are not so many rooms in Zephiros Beach 4 - 85. They are divided into standard single, as well as triple and double rooms. Also, apartments can be offered to the attention of potential guests. And, of course, Superior rooms - that is, improved standard ones. I must say, despite the fact that the hotel corresponds to the class of four stars, the cost of accommodation here is quite small. So, for example, if we take into account a weekly tour for two, then one person will be kept in the amount of about 11 thousand rubles. This, as you can see, is very little for the services provided and comfortable rooms. When booking, potential guests can indicate their personal wishes regarding accommodation: for example, order a double bed in the room or two single beds. It is better to make reservations in advance, at least a month before the departure date, since the apartments in Zephiros Beach Hotel 4 are literally snapping up.





home furnishings

A few words should be said about how the hotel rooms look. One thing can be stated with absolute certainty - Zephiros Beach 4 apartments will appeal to people who are accustomed to home comfort and a warm, welcoming atmosphere. The rooms are done in warm colors, all interior details harmoniously complement each other. There is no excess and pathos absolutely inappropriate here. In general, relaxing in such rooms is a pleasure. In addition to the stylish interior, it should be noted that inside there is everything you need - from the terrace to the refrigerator. Fully equipped modern bathroom, radio, telephone, safe, hairdryer, air conditioning - in general, really, that's all. So tourists who plan to relax here and worry about whether they will be comfortable, have no doubt, Zephiros Beach Hotel 4 (Crete) is a worthy choice.





Restaurant and treats

This hotel has its own restaurant with an open veranda, which is surrounded by a lush flowered garden. All guests are treated according to the "buffet" system, and the food is varied, and the delicacies are always fresh. Zephyros Beach (Crete) also offers its guests an a la carte tavern serving Cretan and Greek dishes. Be sure to take the time to try traditional sweets, drinks and delicacies. Greek cuisine is really unusual - connoisseurs of exoticism and true gourmets will definitely appreciate the local cuisine. And, of course, there are several bars in the complex. There, visitors can try a variety of drinks - both strong and non-alcoholic. In total, two bars are offered - in the hotel and by the pool.





Entertainment

Zephyros Beach is not only a hotel complex, it is also a place where you can spend your leisure time pleasantly. The hotel administration made sure that guests did not have to get bored. Of course, the first thing to note is the pool. Guests can also visit the jacuzzi, enjoy the sauna, watch movies or programs in the TV lounge. You can also play billiards, table tennis or enjoy water sports. By the way, in the evening the hotel hosts various entertainment programs.

If guests come to rest with their children, then they do not need to worry about the fact that their kids will be bored. On the territory of this hotel complex there is a swimming pool designed specifically for them, as well as a children's playroom and even a mini club.

A few words should be said regarding a beach holiday. Parasols and sun loungers can be offered to guests for an additional fee. And one more thing about entertainment. This hotel is more likely an option for families. However, young people will also like it here, because Heraklion is nearby, which is less than half an hour's drive away. It is not only a cultural center, it is also the place of noisy parties, parties, nightclubs and entertainment. So if you want more ambitious fun, you should go to Heraklion and spend some amazing nights there, which will long remain in your memory as the brightest moments of life.





Comments by experienced guests

Prices in Greece, as you know, are quite low - you can keep within 30 thousand rubles (along with tickets) to spend an unforgettable week of rest here. And Zephiros Beach 4 (Crete) is an option for those people who want to relax inexpensively, but without compromising on comfort. Those tourists who have already stayed here mostly respond positively about the hotel complex. They note that the atmosphere is fertile, calm - that is what is needed for people who dream of a break from a noisy metropolis. Of course, everyone is happy to say that the sea is very close - this is a definite plus for lovers of sunbathing and swimming. The favorable location is also not disregarded - many guests, especially inveterate shopaholics, are attracted by numerous souvenir, grocery and other shops. If we talk about what advice the guests give, then many guests who have repeatedly stayed at this place say that the best rooms are on the top floor, and it is better to book them. All in all, this is a great hotel. The staff is friendly, attentive, professional. The cleaning is thorough, daily, the food is decent, and the atmosphere is what you need. For lovers of comfort and coziness - an ideal option.
