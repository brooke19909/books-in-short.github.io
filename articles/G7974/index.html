Knitted women's jacket: job description

A knitted women's sweater is a convenient and versatile wardrobe item. Strictly speaking, a jacket should be called clothes with a fastener for the upper body. However, in everyday life they call it anything: pullovers, tops, cardigans.

A classic jacket is knitted from warm yarn, has sleeves and pockets, but today this model is not too popular. Therefore, the craftswomen are engaged in the manufacture of products from wool or cotton, and also often choose sleeveless models. This article will discuss the options for creating several sweaters that correspond to different levels of knitting skills: primary, secondary, high.

Simple jacket with front stitch

Front and back loops are the basics that all beginners learn. In principle, if they are going to realize just a few small products, then they may not bother with more complicated tricks. Many patterns are based on a combination of these two types of loops. The simplest is the front surface (the odd rows are the front, the even rows are the wrong). How to tie a sweater?

For work, you need yarn in two contrasting colors, knitting needles, the size of which corresponds to the thickness of the thread, and five to six small buttons. As a rule, for a jacket of 44-46th size, 350-400 grams of material is enough.

Before proceeding to knitting, you should perform a control sample and calculate the number of loops per 10 cm of fabric. This will help to dial the correct number of loops and form a part of the right size.

Model Features

This women's jacket, knitted with needles, is very easy to manufacture, but there are several important points that you need to pay attention to:

  1. The first 5-7 rows of each part should be knitted with a garter stitch (all rows are performed with facial loops). This strap will not allow the canvas to curl into a tube.
  2. When working on the details of the back, you should form a bar for buttons in parallel with knitting the main fabric. To do this, several loops on the right (or left) side are always knit with the front ones. It should be remembered that on the bar of one of the parts there should be loops. The hole for the small buttons is performed as follows: two (three) loops are closed, and in the next row they are double-crocheted. It turns out a hole, and the number of loops in the bar remains.
  3. The armholes and neck are also decorated with garter stitch. The armholes are connected in parallel with the main canvas. The tying of the neck can be performed in the process of working on the parts or connected after all the fabrics are sewn. In the second case, loops are knitted along the cut using circular knitting needles and several rows are knitted with a garter pattern.

Preparatory stage

First of all, you need to make a pattern in size so that you can apply a knitted fabric to it. Next, you need to knit a control sample and make a loop calculation. And finally, it is worth checking the thread for shrinkage: wash and dry the sample. If its size has decreased, then this should be taken into account when calculating the loops. Simply put, it is worth measuring the already washed and dried fragment.



Any knitted women's jacket will be both beautiful and functional when using yarn with natural fibers (wool, cotton, mohair). For this model, a thread with a thickness of not more than 300 m / 100 grams is suitable.

How to form a okat?

Many knitters have difficulty knitting the semicircular part of the sleeve - okat. There are several tricks that will significantly reduce and simplify the process:

  • It is necessary to start contractions by simultaneously closing several loops: from the front by 5 cm, from the back by 3 cm.
  • Then 30% of the knit is knitted, cutting two loops in each front row (at the beginning and at the end).
  • The next 40% should be knitted evenly, without any reduction.
  • Then 20% perform the same reductions as at the beginning of the okat (two loops through a row).
  • In the process of working on the last 10%, it is necessary to close 6 loops in each row (three at the beginning and the same at the end). If the thread is thick, the last step can be only two or three rows. In the case when the yarn is thin, more rows will be required.

These simple recommendations allow you to create the right silhouettes, thanks to which women's sweaters, knitted with needles, ideally "sit" on the figure.

On average, the sleeve ridge for the 44-46th size is about 18 cm. Of course, if the craftswoman works with thick thread, the canvas will be quite rigid, so that the ridge should be at least 20-22 cm.

At the final stage, all parts of the product are steamed from the iron and stitched together. Do not forget about the rules for caring for woolen things: they are washed only in warm water (no more than 30 degrees) and dried in a horizontal position.
