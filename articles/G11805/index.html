Horizon Beach Hotel & Stelios Family Rooms - Paradise in Crete

Horizon Beach Hotel & Stelios Family Rooms - hotel, built in 1985. It must be said that reconstruction was carried out several times, for example, in 2010 the last one ended. The hotel is large, spacious and popular, so it‚Äôs worth telling a little more about it.





Location

This is a hotel that consists of a bungalow complex and a main building. All rooms have panoramic views of the garden or the sea. Friendly atmosphere, beautiful countryside and Greek hospitality attract travelers to the Horizon Beach Hotel & Stelios Family Rooms. It is located near Stalis between Malia and Hersonissos. The hotel is five kilometers from the first city, and three from the second. Heraklion International Airport is 26 km away. In general, I must say, the location is very favorable. Far enough from noisy cities (enough distance so that tourists can enjoy solitude and silence), but close to civilization. The area is very well developed infrastructure.

About hotel

The hotel has 192 rooms, so the tourist has ample opportunity to choose where he is best to stay. Double Rooms are offered, it can accommodate a maximum of three people, a fairly spacious room of 46 square meters. m; as well as Family Rooms: four people will feel free, even one more will fit. The rooms of the second type have two bedrooms, and their total area is 46 square meters. m. It is worth noting that each of them has everything you need for a comfortable stay and relaxation. It has a bathroom with a hairdryer, a shower and other appliances, cable TV, a fridge, and also air conditioning, which is so necessary in hot weather. Another guest is provided with a safe, telephone, terrace and even a kitchenette with all the necessary equipment.





Everything to relax

The Horizon Beach Hotel & Stelios Family Rooms has everything that a person needs in order to feel comfortable. This is a television, and an Internet corner, and a picturesque garden where you can enjoy the coolness in the shade of trees. All-inclusive service is available to guests, so the hotel offers a variety of delicacies. And, of course, quality free service. This round-the-clock service, daily room cleaning, a room in which you can store your luggage, parking for motorists, twice a week - linen change. And also such trifles as an elevator, an iron upon request, etc. Of the paid services, you can take advantage of currency exchange, medical care and even a mini market - everything is provided for a comfortable stay.





Entertainment

Horizon Beach Hotel & Stelios Family Rooms is a great place to stay. There is an outdoor pool, and various water sports are available on the beach. A variety of entertaining evenings are held twice a week. The hotel also has a games area, and there is a corresponding room for billiards. The pool has a bar, the hotel has a restaurant. Literally a couple of tens of meters there is a rocky beach, but you have to walk 300 meters to the sandy. You can safely come here with your children and be sure that they will not get bored. For them, the hotel also has a pool, a special games room and playground, as well as cots. The hotel management made sure that any guests feel comfortable in it, regardless of their age, interests and preferences.





Perfect vacation

Many lovers of comfortable rest on the eve of vacation decide to go to a place like Greece, Crete. All-inclusive hotels are a great solution for temporary residence. Rest should be as comfortable as possible, and housing plays a major role in this case. A vacation spent in Crete will be remembered for a lifetime. This is a picturesque island washed by three seas, with a unique culture. Crete is one of the most comfortable and large islands not only in Greece, but also in the whole world. The abundance of local attractions will amaze even the most experienced tourist. In addition, Crete is famous for its ecology, and its climate is really perfect: water warms up to 24 degrees, and air - on average up to 27. Definitely it will appeal to lovers of the azure warm sea and golden tan. Many, before arriving, carefully study such a document as a map of Crete with hotels. And after finding out detailed information about each of the most liked options, they reserve a hotel. One of the best is definitely the 4-star Horizon Beach Hotel. High-quality service, attentive staff, good attitude towards guests and the hotel itself - all these are undoubted advantages in favor of choosing this particular hotel. The cuisine of the hotel restaurant will leave few people indifferent, as experienced chefs have been working in this area for many years and prepare exclusively exquisite dishes for guests. And the beaches located next to the hotel are a real paradise for swimmers. Crystal clear water and a spacious wide coast will delight tourists. Even at the peak of the season in these places there is no beach hype, which may be characteristic of other resorts.
