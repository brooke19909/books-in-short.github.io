Pickup Needle: Overview, Views, Features and Reviews

The first long-playing record was created in 1931. RCA engineers achieved a playback speed of 33.33 rpm. The system was equipped with a chromed steel needle. In the USSR, pickups were made of two types: electromagnetic and piezoelectric. Indisputable quality distinguished the former, but mass production settled the latter as cheaper. The following pickup needles were produced:

  • GZK-661 (in modern industry - GPZ-311);
  • GZKU-631 (now - GPZ-301S).




Theoretical introduction

The pickup head is designed so that the mechanics of the vibrations are transformed into electricity. Sound quality is determined by how good the pickup needle is. When completing the system, the head is mounted on a tonearm, whose characteristics and features are combined with the properties of the gas supply station.

Having the budget technology, it makes no sense to upgrade the device yourself, installing a high-precision head. If the needle of the pickup head and the tonearm are balanced, the plate sounds rich, preserving the nuances, shades of sound.

The pickup works as follows: the needle oscillates as it passes through the plate, the device transforms the movements into electrical vibrations transmitted to the amplifier and acoustic system. In addition to the types listed above, they invented:

  • photovoltaic;
  • capacitive.

Each design has positive properties and weaknesses. These days, electromagnetic sound transducers are leading the way. Their design includes:

  • magnet;
  • voice coil.




Parameters

The pickup needle is characterized by the following parameters:

  • pressure force;
  • frequency range;
  • weight.

It depends on the detail how far the channels can be played separately, without penetration.

Noise and signal

Magnetic heads are divided into:

  • movable magnet (MM);
  • moving coil (MC).

Budget models and the average level of technology are equipped with a movable magnet when the holder in contact with the transmission of vibrations. As the magnet moves, but a stable inductor is present, an alternating magnetic field is created that initiates an electromotive force. Design Advantages:

  • ease of production;
  • output voltage reaches 0.8 mV.




The larger the pickup needle, the larger the head as a whole, the higher the inertia of the system, that is, the dynamics of reproduction is reduced. High-frequency reproduction suffers, channel separation quality deteriorates. The mechanism accepts loads, which requires the installation of capacitive phono stage.

MS - the head of a vinyl player in which a magnet initiates a constant field. Electromotive force is provoked by the movement of the coil. Since the magnet does not move, installing a larger component does not degrade the playback settings. The magnetic field maintains uniformity, which minimizes sound distortion. Miniature vinyl pickup needles provide better signal transmission.

The disadvantage of the system is a low output voltage. It is impossible to connect the pickup to the phono stage, first you have to add a transformer to the circuit. The player works well if it is shielded from spurious interference.

Another difference between MS and MM heads is the technology for replacing components. MM system allows change together with a magnet. It will be possible to do this at home, not having much experience. The MS-head will have to be taken to a repair shop or even sent to the manufacturer.

But the sharpening of the pickup needle is usually elliptical in both types of heads. This design detail helps to halve the sound distortion. The spectrum of frequencies available to the player is expanding.

Needle in detail

Physical wear is calculated by examining the part under a microscope. To understand that the time has come to change the radial needle, you can see the sawn stripes in places where the part is in contact with the plate. If the product has worn out, it becomes triangular. A worn needle can easily ruin expensive vinyls.





The records accumulate dirt in the grooves, which increases the abrasiveness of the product. The higher the initial abrasive properties and the longer the plate is used, the faster the system wears out. When choosing a product, make sure that the manufacturer indicates the life of the part. Mostly produce equipment per 500-1000 hours of playing tunes.

The degree of wear does not always correspond to the passport data. After the claimed thousand working hours have passed, the system does not lose the ability to reproduce sound, but its quality deteriorates. Visually assess the degree of wear using microscopes:

  • interference;
  • electronic.

When replacing, make a reference recording by setting the resolution to 96kHz / 24bit. This will help after a period of operation to compare the sound quality.
