Where can I get a charge of positive emotions? To do this, created the water park "Aquarius". Stavropol located it in an ecologically clean place in Victory Park. This is one of the most popular holiday destinations of citizens.





Happy day with family and friends.

Even so far before the holidays, and already the soul yearned for fresh air, and the body asks for water. How to help yourself? For this there is a water park "Aquarius". Stavropol has created a great place for fun in Victory Park. In it you can discreetly spend the entire day off. Everyone, from a baby to an adult, will find entertainment for himself. In the center of the green zone there are four pools. Water in them for every taste: warm (heated), relaxing and cool for those who want to refresh themselves and cheer up.





Water is the purest, since the most modern methods of purification are used. The depth of the pools is different. There is a small one that is suitable for those who can not swim. There is also a deep pool. Beginners will always come to the rescue help, who tirelessly watch the swimmers.

Rides

Plunging into the world of entertainment is so cool! One has only to go to the water park "Aquarius" (Stavropol), when you immediately hear a contagiously funny noise and din, like in a sea resort. A roar and a squeal are heard from the slides. Toddlers are rolling down from the little ones, there are medium-sized slides and extreme for amateurs to be breathtaking. There are entertainment for everyone who came to the Aquarius water park. Stavropol will delight both the residents of the city and its guests with outdoor recreation, here you can enjoy plenty of fun, sunbathing and relaxing on comfortable sunbeds.





While adults relax to prepare for the next assault on slides and pools, restless kids frolic on trampolines. They climb high springy slides, slide down and repeat all over again. Trampoline does not bother. And if it became hot again from continuous jumps, then the pool is nearby. It's time to go back into the water, and mom and dad rested. We need to play with the whole family - everyone has fun. Such an active warm-up unites parents who return to childhood for a while, and their children.

On simulators

Having plenty of fun and gently stretching the muscles, you can go to the simulators, which prudently installed on its territory the water park "Aquarius". Stavropol does not forget that modern citizens love to pump up certain muscle groups that are not used daily for sedentary work. Someone will take up the workout with a gradual increase in the load, someone less prepared will approach the matter in a more gentle way, so that then they do not feel discomfort.

And then what?

You can return to the pools with slides or take a steam bath in a Russian bath. The cost of visiting a Siberian bathhouse with wood with wet and dry steam is included in the ticket price. Is it possible to skip such a hygiene procedure? A wood firebox will create a unique comfort due to the crackling of firewood. And the health benefits are incomparable.

All pores of the skin open, the sebaceous glands are cleansed. In the skin, blood flow increases. Steam will strengthen and strengthen the nervous and respiratory systems. Nerves worry many. Bath will reduce muscle activity and nervous tension. Just do not abuse the steam room. A quarter hour is enough. A person unfamiliar with the bath should choose the middle shelves, and those who visit it often can start from the top. Everyone should be in it until the first sweat. The last minutes should be spent on the lower shelf, and then - directly into the pool. A steam bath with brooms and a treat for mountain tea should be paid extra.

Again in the pool - the celebration continues





In the bathhouse, the visitor of the water park strengthened his health in another way. Now you can take a leisurely swim and then have a bite to eat, soft drinks and various snacks are sold here. You can forget about any resort by visiting the Aquarius water park in Stavropol, especially if you spend the whole day in it. It is open daily from 10:00 to 20:00. On weekdays from 3 p.m. tickets are sold cheaper. Aquapark Aquarius (Stavropol) has set the following prices for visits:

  • Children (up to 12 years old) - 300 rubles.
  • Adult (from 12 years old) - 500 rubles.
  • Special 2-hour rate - 250 rubles.

Promotions:

  • When visiting the company from 10 people at the same time by appointment - 10% discount.
  • On his birthday, the birthday party visits the water park for free.
  • During school holidays for a group of children under 12 years old in an amount of 20 or more people, the cost will be 250 rubles. An adult accompanying them is free of charge.

Waterpark "Aquarius", Stavropol: reviews

In general, the feedback from visitors, especially regular ones, is not just good, but enthusiastic. Pools, especially for children, are very warm, even in an adult after 15 hours it is very comfortable. The pool is surrounded by a park, like a forest, and therefore very comfortable. There are enough sun loungers for everyone. Some people think that prices are high, but these are not frequent reviews. The heated pool is nice even in the autumn September days.
