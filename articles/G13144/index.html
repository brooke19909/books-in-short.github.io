Want to know how to draw up a Corn Card?

Plastic cards tightly entered our lives. Many do not part with them - just like with mobile phones. And the varieties of the mentioned cards are a great many. Not so long ago, Euroset offered its customers a new card with the original name Corn. She has an interesting design, but this is not the most important thing. Before answering the question of how to draw up a Corn card, let's look at its features.





Key Benefits

1. You can get the mentioned card in the Euroset company salon for free and very quickly (in a couple of minutes).

2. A debit card can easily be converted into a credit card if desired. To do this, it is enough to get a loan at any bank that cooperates with Euroset.

3. No maintenance fees for the year. Naturally, many owners really like it.

4. To improve the security of all operations carried out via the Internet, high-quality 3-D Secure protection is applied. By the way, many people are wondering how to draw up a "Corn" card, precisely after they learn about this feature. After all, online shopping these days is gaining momentum.

5. The card can be used for a long time (up to 10 years).

6. There is free informing by SMS about the operations performed.

All of the above suggests that the plastic card in question is a rather profitable financial instrument. Therefore, it will not be redundant to find out how to draw up a "Corn" card.





When can I use the "Corn" card

Using the card in question, you can perform the following operations:

  • replenish the account on the phone;
  • make utility bills;
  • pay fines of the traffic police;
  • cash out through an ATM;
  • make various purchases, including on the Internet.

So, how to draw up a "Corn" card, and what is needed for this?

Note, if you want to use borrowed funds, you need to open a limit loan on the card. But first you need to get it. This process will not take you much time and will not cause much trouble, since it is not difficult to draw up a Corn card in Euroset. Go to the nearest company salon. Present your passport and declare your desire to use their service. Employees will fill out a card service agreement and immediately issue it to you. For the issue you will need to pay 100 rubles. However, they will be transferred to your account, so this procedure can be considered free.

Some people ask how you can get a Corn actual credit card. In fact, it is universal - it combines bonus, debit and settlement.



By the way, it is not possible to draw up a Corn card via the Internet, but it is possible to make an online application for a loan on it. And after approval, go to the nearest Euroset branch to pick it up.

After you are issued a card, ask for a limit if you wish. You will be required to fill out an application, then a bank employee will ask you to wait a bit. The approved limit will be 300 thousand rubles, and it will be possible to use it immediately after approval.

Where to replenish the account?

This can be done without commission in the salons of the Euroset company, taking with you a passport. You can also replenish your account through the payment terminal. The commission in this case is normal - 2% of the amount. You can also use the wallets of domestic payment systems. Only preliminary find out the size of the commission.

An important clarification: citizens over 18 years old can apply for this card, and the credit limit is available only to those who are older than 24, but younger than 57 years old.
