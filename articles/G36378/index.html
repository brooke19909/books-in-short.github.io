Where is the hotel located, how to get to it?

It can be seen that Green Park (Novozybkov) is designed for motorists coming to or from the Republic of Belarus. The hotel is located near the entrance to the A240 federal highway. His exact address: st. Michurina, 46a. Judging by the reviews, then tourists really like the location of the hotel. It is located not in the center of the city, but rather at the exit from it, on the southeastern outskirts. The train station is located two kilometers from the hotel. Bryansk Airport is immeasurably further - almost 200 km. The center of Novozybkov can be reached by taxi in five minutes. All the sights of the city are at a distance of two and a half kilometers from the hotel. You can get to the Green Park Hotel by taxi or by buses No. 14 and 8. You should get off at the Tekhnikum stop. From there, to the entrance to the hotel is a couple of steps.



Territory and buildings

We can say with confidence that in Novozybkov Green Park is the best hotel in the city. And the fact that he is on the outskirts does not in the least diminish his merits, but, on the contrary, adds. After all, the hotel building stands in the middle of a pine forest. Pure nature and tranquility, a well-equipped recreation area lure customers from central hotels to the suburban Green Park. The hotel was opened relatively recently - in 2013. The two-story building is very stylish in cream and chocolate tones. The hotel also has a tire service, car wash and sauna with swimming pool. This once again underlines what kind of tourists the hotel is designed for. Reviews praise the bucolic silence and tranquility of the pine forest. It is nice to relax here after a busy and eventful day. The building has a playground. There is also parking.



Number of rooms

Green Park (Novozybkov) - the hotel is very small. It has only sixteen rooms. However, they are divided into categories. Moreover, in front of the tourist who settles in the hotel, a wide selection is open. The cheapest room category is the standard. Two people can live there for only 1,700 rubles a day. Increased comfort in the standard + room category. Even better are junior suites and suites. The real pride of the Green Park Hotel in Novozybkov are business and VIP rooms. Room price also includes breakfast. Reviews of tourists note the comfort and cleanliness of the rooms. The hotel is quiet on weekdays . But if you arrive on the weekend, then ask for a room away from the restaurant, residents of the city often book its banquet room for weddings and other special occasions.

What's in the guest rooms?

Whichever category you choose, comfort awaits you everywhere. Each room is individually decorated. But everywhere you will find serene comfort and the proper level of service. Reviews report comfortable furniture, a soft bed, new plumbing. But what exactly awaits the guests in the rooms of the Green Park Hotel (Novozybkov)? Photos of these rooms on the hotel website are fully true. The standard includes a telephone, cable TV, air conditioning. There are two glasses, a roller for clothes, a shoe brush and other little things (of which, however, our whole life is made up). And what is very important: in all rooms there is a fast and free Wi-Fi. The en suite bathroom comes with a hairdryer and amenities.



Superior rooms: what are they?

Green Park Hotel (Novozybkov) is able to satisfy the needs of even the most demanding customers. For those who value comfort above all else, the hotel offers rooms of higher categories. In spacious suites at a cost of 2800 rubles per day (with breakfast), a sofa, a table with an armchair, a mini fridge and an electronic safe are added to the standard suite. And in the bathrooms, along with the usual toiletries, guests are provided with toothbrushes with paste, slippers, razor and sewing kits. Real luxury reigns in business and VIP rooms. In the latter category there is a minibar, a seating area with upholstered furniture, and the bathroom even has a personal jacuzzi.
