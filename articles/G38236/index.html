Nokia 1280 - a phone for relatives

Nokia 1280 is a phone released by a well-known brand for those who do not want to pay for additional chips, but are only going to make calls. Such a position plus brand quality could not but interest a certain circle of people. Such a phone is not suitable for lovers of all-in-one combines, but for trips to the country or a gift for elderly relatives, it turned out to be just right.





The phone has a loud bell, a long life on a single battery, all the functions of a regular phone and a flashlight and radio. The display is black and white, there is no polyphony and MP3, as there are no expansion cards and Internet access. There is a calendar, alarm clock, stopwatch.

Layout and equipment

The Nokia 1280 phone was originally developed as a business solution for calls, so the phone‚Äôs equipment is minimal. The box contains a phone, charging, battery and instructions. For the radio you need headphones, which the user can purchase separately.

As with most models with radio support, the headphone cable has two functions - audio transmission and antenna. The headphone jack is on the top side, a flashlight is located nearby. Charging is connected on the left - a traditional solution for the brand in the form of a hole. The lace mount is located on the bottom, next to the microphone. The layout is thought out, practical, and the build quality is at a high level. Details fit tightly, there are no backlashes, no squeaks.

So different 1280

In the specifications submitted by the developers, polyphonic ringtones and a call editor for Nokia 1280 are indicated. The instruction, like the press releases, confirms this. But in the first versions that appeared on sale, not everything was. A funny thing happened to the melody editor. The instructions stated that there is a place to save your tunes, but ... the editor itself is NOT! Then the firmware was changed, and in subsequent sales there really was a good polyphony and a melody editor. Except for this error, the phone is a workhorse. In the early years, the phone was presented only in 4 colors - black, gray with black, blue with black and pink with black. Then the unexpected interest of the beautiful half forced the company to expand the line, and the latest models could be white-black or yellow-black.

Connection quality

For a phone planned by a simple dialer, and even from such a brand, it seems indecent to talk about the quality of communication. The design of the phone has a second speaker on the back of the case, quite impressive size. As a result, an alarm clock, a call can be heard well, but speaking on it is not very convenient. In the reviews about the phone, there were sayings that everyone around you will hear your interlocutor, with the exception of yourself. Others found an original way out - to turn the phone when talking to the other side. By the standards of Nokia (and not only her), the microphone is displayed on the bottom panel, and if you expand the phone, they will still hear you.
