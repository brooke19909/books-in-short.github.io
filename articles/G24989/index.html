Vacation - what a wonderful word! Asia - the country of smiles

Located on the mainland of Eurasia. The largest part of the world in terms of area and population is four times the size of Europe. It was here that all the most ancient civilizations and all world religions arose. Asia includes 53 states. Natural features and geographical conditions have contributed to the fact that the indigenous people are distinguished by hard work and patience.

Geographical features are very diverse. In Asia there are mountains: Tien Shan, the Himalayas, the Caucasus. The endless steppes of Mongolia, the jungle of India and Vietnam, the taiga in Siberia, there are also volcanic places. Asia, like no other country in the world, is affected by monsoon winds. Therefore, the level of humidity here is constantly fluctuating: either a huge amount of rainfall or drought. Asia is washed by three oceans: the Arctic, Pacific and Indian. The border between it and Europe runs along the foot of the Ural Mountains.

But the most amazing thing is that people live here who love to smile and generously give their joy to visiting tourists.

Asian countries to relax

Among the huge number of countries in this part of the world, there are some that are definitely worth a visit:

  • China. The oldest civilization, full of mysteries and secrets. The Great Wall of China, the Imperial Palace, the Terracotta Army, the Jade Buddha Temple, Confucius Temple and many other ancient attractions. China is one of the three most popular countries chosen by tourists who plan tours to Asia. Many people come here not only to get acquainted with the ancient history and traditions, but also in order to receive medical care. The modern medical achievements and knowledge of ancient healers, combined together, provide a powerful effect for the treatment of many diseases.
  • Japan. Going here, you can get acquainted with the rich Japanese culture. How did geisha prepare and who are samurai? The history of the country, its traditions, architectural monuments and rich rest will make your stay in Japan unforgettable. If you do not like the heat, then come here in the fall. Easy coolness and a small amount of precipitation will allow you to comfortably take walks in the Land of the Rising Sun and enjoy its sights. In addition, do not forget to try real Japanese cuisine: sushi, yakitori and, of course, the Japanese national drink - sake.
  • Vietnam. Here you can eat tasty and inexpensive. You can buy products from world famous brands at producer prices, as their factories and plants have been built in Vietnam.
  • India. Many of us get acquainted with this country even in early youth, when watching Indian melodramas. Judging by the films, all manifestations of emotions are transmitted here through dance. Prices are a pleasant surprise. Food, souvenirs, equipment rental are much cheaper here than in Russia. One of the most popular holiday destinations in India is Goa. This territory, judging by the preserved drawings on the rocks, was inhabited about 30 thousand years ago. A large number of ancient temples, diving centers for scuba diving, the sea in Asia, beautiful monuments of architecture will make your vacation unforgettable. Do not rush to conclusions if you see men holding hands in India, only male friendship is strongly developed here and there is nothing reprehensible in this.




Popular Asian Resorts

  1. Bali island in indonesia. The best vacation in Asia for parents with children and honeymooners. Swimming with dolphins, windsurfing and a variety of slides in water parks are just a fraction of the local entertainment.
  2. India, Goa. Only in order to find out what it is - the air of freedom, you should choose this place to relax.
  3. Sri Lanka. Here you can not only have a good rest, but also get popular Ceylon tea in the Soviet Union. Travelers from all over the world are impressed by coral clusters and tea plantations.
  4. Maldives. Are you depressed, are you disappointed with life? Urgently go here. A minimum of things with you, even shoes are not needed here. Walking along the coast, aromatic procedures and much more will return to you the lost peace and enjoyment of life.
  5. Vung Tau, Vietnam. This resort is compared to the popular Brazilian city of Rio de Janeiro, where life is an eternal carnival.

The most interesting places in Asia

  • The Dead Sea in Israel. One of the most saline reservoirs in the world. If a fish accidentally enters this sea, it immediately dies from a high concentration of salt.
  • Fujiyama in Japan. The height of this volcano is 3776 meters. Last erupted in 1707. Today, activity is poorly expressed.
  • The great Wall of China. It passes through all of Northern China, with a length of about 9,000 thousand meters.
  • Taj Mahal in India. It is under the protection of UNESCO and is one of the monuments that is admired throughout the world. It is visited daily by tens of thousands of tourists choosing Asian countries for recreation.
  • Raja Ampat Islands in Indonesia. The most beautiful islands for scuba diving. A large number of different fish and corals.
  • Temple complex in Cambodia. Its area is about 400 square kilometers. The complex includes about 2000 temples and buildings, which from a height are very similar to the starry sky.
  • The island of Borneo. Here are the largest caves in the world and a tree grows, which bears the unusual name of "walking". Its roots grow from the middle of the trunk and are able to move underground in search of water. Due to the unusual appearance of algae that grow near the coast, you can see the "red" tide.
  • Wat Pho Temple in Bangkok, Thailand. It was here that Thai massage appeared. The library, which stores the oldest manuscripts, will be of interest to tourists.
  • Nha Trang, Vietnam. Here is the longest cable car. Its length is about 4000 thousand meters.
  • Blue Mosque in Malaysia. At the same time, about 15,000 thousand people can fit here. When the sun penetrates through the windows of the mosque, the room turns blue.




The most exotic countries in Asia

  • Malaysia. It is always summer here. You can see the rarest plants and animals that are not found in any country in the world. Ancient temples, places of worship, exotic nature, a huge number of unusual sights. What are some names: Temple of Serpents, Bird Park, Orchid Garden, Butterfly Park and many others.
  • Indonesia. This country includes five large islands, including the one loved by many Bali tourists. One of the islands resembles a starfish in shape. Huge coral walls shock diving enthusiasts. Buddhist temples and volcanic lakes, mountain rivers and singing caves. On one of the islands, an ancient tribe has survived, which lives by its own laws and hunts animals with home-made weapons. Here, children are buried in a tree hollow, and for adults, caves are cut down.
  • Philippines. When choosing tours to Asia, pay attention to this country. If there is paradise on earth, then it is undoubtedly here. White as snow, sand of beaches and a huge oceanic space of turquoise color. Underground river and active volcanoes. Here you can play a wedding in the best traditions of the Filipino people.

What souvenirs can I bring from Asian countries?

In order to remember the trip for a long time, it is necessary not only to take pictures, but also to make small purchases that will remind you of the country where you rested. So, what souvenirs and in what countries of Asia do you need to buy?

  • Thailand - coconut oil, curly soap, ointments on natural herbs, straw hats, exotic fruits, lipstick case, orchid, Buddha figures, latex pillows.
  • India - hair dye, decorative cosmetics, elephant figures, tea.
  • China - cosmetic preparations from lotus and ginseng flowers, a thermometer with the image of the Chinese wall.
  • Japan - bleaching chewing gum, face masks, shampoos with mother of pearl, conditioners, figures of sumo wrestlers, samurai, geisha. This is one of the few Asian countries where it is not customary to bargain, do not forget about it when shopping.

  • Indonesia - handmade wooden crafts.
  • Singapore - figurines of dragons, fans of various shapes and sizes.
  • Malaysia - tin and copper products: plates, caskets, key rings, glasses. Batik fans, coral crafts, small handmade rugs.
  • Laos - souvenirs from wood, leather, fabric. Dolls in national costumes, mango wood products, vases, healing tinctures with scorpions, snakes. You can buy very beautiful shawls, tablecloths, products made by patchwork technique.
  • Burma - rosary, icons of various sizes, wooden utensils, toys for children, paintings with Asian landscapes, batik products, jewelry made of precious stones.
