How to choose a hotel in Gurzuf

The resort village of Gurzuf is located 20 km from Yalta in the south of the Crimean peninsula. Every summer, it attracts a huge flow of tourists, among which one can single out lovers of both extreme relaxation and calm. In Gurzuf you can find sandy, pebble beaches, admire the mountain scenery and local nature. Choosing a hotel in Gurzuf is necessary based on vacation plans. For example, a married couple with young children is better to stay at a hotel closer to the beach, and groups of friends will be more convenient to be located in the resort center, closer to bars and other establishments. It is only important to determine the budget of the trip, as well as to worry in advance about making a reservation in the hotel you like.

Where and when to look for good numbers

The best way to find a good private hotel in Gurzuf is to search the Internet. Thanks to the reviews of people who have already visited the resort, you can choose the best option for the price and location. The main selection criteria:

  • guest rating level and number of positive comments;
  • close proximity to the beach;
  • acceptable price;
  • favorable ratio "price - quality".

Often you can make a reservation at a hotel for free, but in some cases you will need to pay the amount for the first day of your stay. The most expensive months to relax on the Crimean coast are July and August. Therefore, it is worth thinking about the rest in advance, preferably 2-3 months before the vacation. By reserving a room in a Gurzuf hotel, before the peak season, you can save a decent amount of money.





What to do if your vacation budget is small

There are situations in life when an extremely small amount of money is allocated for a vacation. However, this is not a reason to deprive yourself of the joy of a beach holiday in the Crimea. Gurzuf is a resort village, not a city, therefore, unlike the neighboring Yalta, on its outskirts you can find affordable housing at an affordable price. First of all, it is worth paying attention to the private sector and mini-hotels. Gurzuf offers very inexpensive rooms in such places. Of course, the amenities there are not the same as in the rooms by the sea, but everything you need is present.



The best hotels of Gurzuf

Michel Villa

Among the picturesque grove "Ai-Danil", there is a small villa "Michelle", which is known for its good location in a secluded place with nature. Private beach, a wide range of services, which includes a swimming pool, hammam, sauna, restaurant, spa treatments, water attractions - this is not a complete list of the advantages of the villa. All apartments are equipped with comfortable beds, air conditioning, TVs, refrigerators, showers and personal hygiene items. Holidays in this hotel will suit couples, as well as just those who are looking for peace and quiet.





Sanatorium Ai-Danil

Visiting the resort, you can immediately notice this hotel, which stands out among the rest with its 15 floors. Sanatorium Ai-Danil offers all travelers different categories of rooms equipped with everything necessary for a comfortable vacation. Each has a bed, air conditioning, refrigerator and a bathroom. The complex has sports grounds, billiards, a swimming pool, a gym and attractions.

It is also important that the sea is only 100 m. The beach is quite spacious, wide and very clean. Around the perimeter canopies, deck chairs, umbrellas. It is also possible to enjoy a cold drink or dessert without leaving the beach. This hotel in Gurzuf is perfect for outdoor enthusiasts.



Hotel "Pearl of Crimea"

One of the best representatives of the Gurzuf hotel complex is considered to be the Pearl of Crimea Hotel. Around it is the private sector, so visitors should not be afraid of possible noise in the evenings. The hotel includes several buildings, among which there are both luxurious, luxury apartments and standard rooms. Guests are offered to use their own beach, a cozy dining room, secure parking, gym, restaurant and many other amenities. Gurzuf Hotel on the seafront is famous for its large pebble beach, which is equipped with all the attributes of a comfortable pastime.

The benefits of a holiday in Gurzuf

Who can recommend the Crimean village? Regardless of the chosen hotel in Gurzuf, rest at this resort will bring a lot of impressions. The picturesque green of emerald color, the turquoise sea, clean air and the mass of historical sights will be remembered for a lifetime.

An important factor is the good transport accessibility to Yalta. Only 20-30 minutes by car, and you can enjoy the promenade of this famous village. Gurzuf is an ideal place for a pleasant, affordable and varied vacation.
