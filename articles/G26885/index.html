Golden Sand Inn 3 (Phuket): photos and reviews of tourists

Small private hotel Golden Sand Inn 3 * is located on the island of Phuket. Holidays on this island in the Kingdom of Thailand (Southeast Asia) are particularly popular in the autumn-winter period. The riot of nature, a wealth of impressions, the friendliness of the population, absolute democracy, which does not require compliance with dress codes and conventions ‚Äúbeau monde‚Äù - all that is required from people who are tired of everyday life, stressful infrastructure of the information society.

Features of a holiday in Phuket: choice of beach

The choice of the beach depends on what the traveler expects from a vacation. There are a lot of them in Phuket. And everyone is endowed with features. Some of the most popular ones are:

  • Patong A great place for an active pastime. Lots of malls. Cafe. A large number of vacationers. Nice yellow sand. You can buy a drink and use the sunbed and umbrella.
  • Kata A fairly lively beach with a developed leisure infrastructure. The sand is shallow and white, a gentle entrance to the sea. Drowning in the green of casuarine trees.
  • Karon Less crowded beach with a length of four kilometers, with golden sand. There are no hotels on the beach, they are located across the road. Therefore, a feeling of solitude is created.
  • Bang Tao . The fashionable beach is eight kilometers long, with white sand. It houses the famous Laguna complex. You can find a large number of restaurants and cafes.
  • Freedom . For those who wish to retire. You can only get here by boat. The beach has preserved the pristine beauty of the landscape. Calmness and relaxation are its main features.

Hotel location

Hotel Golden Sand Inn 3 * is located thirty kilometers from Phuket International Airport, a ten-minute drive from the lively Patong Beach. is he located on the first coastline of the popular Karon Beach. This beach is calmer than Patong, the price of food is above average, when compared with other places of rest in Phuket. The beach is unusually beautiful, crunchy when walking, small and clean sand. A gentle entrance to the sea.

Vacationers here can visit the Dinopark, an interesting meeting place for children and adults with tropical and wildlife.

For gourmets it will be interesting to visit the Coconut Paradise restaurant. Tasty, inexpensive, you can taste national cuisine in a cozy atmosphere and on the outdoor terrace. The restaurant constantly organizes parties and promotions, which allows you to enjoy a free cocktail or a glass of wine.

Description of rooms

The hotel has seventy five rooms. Thirty of them are standard rooms for two people.

The hotel also offers accommodation in bungalows (forty-five rooms), which are surrounded by a tropical garden.

Golden Rooms Sand Hotel 3 * is equipped with everything necessary for relaxation, each has individual air conditioning, TV, refrigerator with minibar (for a fee), TV, bathroom with toiletries, hairdryer.

The rooms are small in size but very comfortable and modern. Housekeeping is provided daily.

Service

Golden Sand Hotel 3 * offers the use of round-the-clock services at the reception (reception): a safe, an Internet connection zone, and child-care services can be ordered . There is free parking and a car rental service.



The hotel has a large garden for hiking, and offers massage treatments. The hotel has an outdoor pool, bar. The hotel restaurant offers quality food, Thai and international dishes according to the buffet concept. Breakfast is included. Serving American and European food options is offered.

What is useful to know about vacation in Phuket

  • Food on the island is more expensive than on the mainland of the Kingdom of Thailand. This is a general rule of island recreation, since the products include the cost of their delivery to the island. The cheapest option is to buy food in the night markets, or eat in small cafes. You should carefully examine the surroundings of the hotel in order to choose for yourself the best option for quality and cost of service.
  • In Phuket, a special composition of the structure of water, so often on the fourth or fifth day of stay on the island, intestinal dysfunction occurs. You need to be prepared for this and take care of medications in advance in this case. This should be treated carefully and used only for drinking water.
  • Do not give preference when choosing a place of food for expensive hotels. The rules of the islanders are very strict and in case of poisoning the owner will have to leave the island. Because in expensive hotels that have plastic racks - the quality of food is equal.
  • When traveling, it is better to take out extended insurance and include additional risks in it - sunburn, inpatient treatment, sports, dental care. The medicine here is very expensive and in case of emergency situations the solution to the problem will cost disproportionately (in relation to the cost of insurance).
