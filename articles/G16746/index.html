Shapsug Reservoir (Krasnodar Territory): recreation, fishing

The Shapsug reservoir is one of the seven largest reservoirs in the region created by human hands. Located near the floodplain of the river. Kuban, on the left bank, and the mouth of the river. The history of this reservoir

The Shapsug reservoir in the administrative division belongs to the territory of Adygea. The capital of the district is Maykop. The construction of the facility took place during the period from 1939 to 1952.



The construction task was to regulate the flow of water of the river. Afips. Thanks to the project, the expenditure of the resource base became economical, which served the benefit of the national economy. Nearby lands received protection from flooding and flooding.

Fishing in this pond

The Shapsug reservoir, the rest near which is pleasant and at the same time productive, is a place where various species of fish and crayfish are found. In order to catch them, people come here from everywhere.

In spring, the season of bite of rams, great pike perch, chic carp, catfish and bream begins. They live at a depth of 1-1.5 meters in local rivers. Sometimes it is possible to catch a fish weighing a couple of kilograms. In the second half of July - August, aquatic inhabitants dive to great depths, but the nibble is still good if you swim to the middle of the reservoir in a boat, which is rented out here.

The reservoir is enriched by the flows of the Ubin (Ubinka) river, which flows from the mountains. This aquatic artery originates on the north-eastern side of Papai. It has a length of more than 60 km. Thanks to her, once in this paradise, you can catch roach, bream, crucian carp and carp. There is a pike, which, however, prefers to inhabit the depths.





Where to live?

In order to look at the beauty of nature and go fishing, tourists annually come to the Shapsug reservoir. Recreation centers accept them for accommodation. One of them is called "Hunter-fisherman."

There is also a military departmental institution. Visitors love to settle in the "Kurochka Ryaba", which is near the Novodmitrievskaya village and the river. Shebsh (tributary of the Kuban to the left). Here is a great view of the forest and mountains.

The rooms are equipped with all the necessary amenities for a good rest. Great service. The list of services includes a wood-heated sauna and a modern sauna. There are playgrounds for sports, a zoological corner, thanks to which the place is a good place to relax with the whole family. You can go jipping here. It is quite pleasant to walk on foot, leisurely exploring the surroundings, to ride a horse or bicycle. There are plenty of opportunities for a comprehensive, eventful, active vacation that brings positive emotions and strengthens health.





Reconstruction

In 2007, the renovation of the facility began, for which 2 billion rubles were allocated. It was planned that the work would take 6 years, but a wave of economic crisis hit, which prevented the timely implementation of the plan, so the event dragged on. Fishermen are embarrassed a little. They continue to come to the reservoir and get a wonderful catch on the coasts of the rivers flowing into it. The same goes for hunters.

The reason that prompted the authorities to take decisive action was the strong deterioration of the facility. His condition carried more of a threat than benefit and protection from the effects of floods. At the moment, it is believed that the project will be completed in 2017. But this is in case if the terms will not be postponed again.

The amount required for the work has increased to 2.42 billion rubles. For the local budget, this is a pretty impressive investment. In some ways, the money ‚Äúhung in the air‚Äù, because the funds have already been spent, and there is still no long-awaited result.





Reason for the delay

Now the facility is 50% ready, which was announced at a meeting on "long-term construction" in the Ministry of Agriculture of the Russian Federation. This does not sound very encouraging, given that the deadlines have already been postponed twice. Such a stretch of the job can be called operational.

In 2010, a lawsuit was instituted in this regard in court, during which the order of actions of the customer and the project contractors was investigated. We found out that the parties entered into a conspiracy in which they stole and shared the money invested in financing. They were also taken from the amount of the tranche of 2008. It is interesting that the Federal Service for Supervision of the Findings of Surveillance knew this and for some reason kept silent about its observation.

Only a year later, information reached law enforcement. Disciplinary sanctions were applied to the perpetrators, which, of course, are too small a measure of punishment for such speculation.
