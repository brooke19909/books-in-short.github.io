Hotel Marriott (Nizhny Novgorod): address, photos and reviews

The Marriott Hotel (Nizhny Novgorod) is located close to the central historical monument of the city - the Nizhny Novgorod Kremlin. Convenient location, excellent service and reasonable prices attract tourists from all over the world.



Hotel address

If you come to the city on business or just relax, then the stopping place can choose the Marriott (hotel, Nizhny Novgorod). Guest house address: 606109, st. Ilyinskaya, d. 46.

Phones of reservation service:

  • +7 (831) 429-01-34;

The city center is about 1 km from the hotel, the nearest airport terminal is only 23 km away, and the railway station is 6 km away.

This is a modern hotel located near the city center. Its prime location is great for both business travelers and travelers. The hotel is located in the business part of the city, in a place with stunning views of the main historical sights.





How to get to the hotel

So, you chose the hotel "Marriott" (Nizhny Novgorod). How to get to it? You can get to the hotel from the airport by taxi. You should leave the terminal building and turn left, following the road 22A-0007. Then go to the M-7 road and turn right to the Moscow highway. Then again to the right, passing through the Kanavinsky bridge to Maxim Gorky Square, turn right to Ilyinskaya Street. The hotel will be on the right. The cost of a taxi will be about 600 rubles. If the tourist travels on his own, then you can use the metro to the Gorkovskaya station. It is located 1.4 km from the hotel.

Marriott Hotel (Nizhny Novgorod): visitor reviews

Hotel reviews are positive. Guests praise the service and the food. They like the design and comfort of the rooms. Russian guests consider this hotel one of the best in the city at the moment. Foreigners also have a high opinion of hotel accommodation, which is confirmed by their reviews on the official website.

The negative side, according to Russian tourists, is only one - expensive rooms. They are more suitable for a stop on a business trip than for a family vacation.

The Marriott Hotel combines the comforts of home with the comfort of a modern design. All rooms of the hotel overlook the historic center, are equipped with double-glazed windows and air conditioning, as well as a private bathroom with everything you need. The rooms are equipped with satellite TV, a refrigerator and a safe for valuables. Tourists who arrived in Nizhny Novgorod on a business trip can use the hotel's business services (Internet, copying, fax, scanning). In the morning, guests can enjoy a hot and nutritious breakfast, which is included in the room rate and is served in the restaurant. The hospitable and attentive staff, calm and cozy atmosphere, favorable location near the historical center will make a tourist or business trip as fruitful and enjoyable as possible.

After spending time at the Marriott Hotel, guests of Nizhny Novgorod will receive excellent service, a pleasant experience and the best vacation in one of the most beautiful cities of the Golden Ring.
