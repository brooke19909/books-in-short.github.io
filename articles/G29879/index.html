Pension in Crimea "AzovLend": reviews of vacationers

Between two picturesque bays on the shores of the Sea of ​​Azov there is a neat and very beautiful building of a boarding house called “AzovLend”. Rest here is walking in the fresh air along the edge of the surf, the hot sand of a coastal beach, swimming in a warm calm sea surrounded by beautiful nature and much more that will be remembered for a long time.

Calm sea

In the boarding house “AzovLend” you can have a great rest with your whole family, because even the sea here has the potential to teach swimming very young children. The Sea of ​​Azov in this area is known for its transparent surface and calm state thanks to the nearby bays. To the sea from the pension is only a couple of dozen steps, and the windows of the rooms offer a wonderful view.





In the territory, which belongs to the boarding house, there is a lot of entertainment for children and adults. When you read the reviews about the rest, “AzovLend” seems to be a paradise for families with children. For example, there is a water park, a water slide with a pool, which can be tried by both children and their parents. There is a bouncy castle on the beach, where kids can frolic in plenty.

In the neighboring boarding house there is a noteworthy thematic water park “Treasure Island”, an excursion to which you can also book vacationers “AzovLenda”. If you can’t imagine life without sports activities, then you should know that the guesthouse has a bike and bicycle rental service, and you can always play beach volleyball there.

Due to such rich opportunities for recreation in the AzovLend boarding house, the reviews of vacationers often agree that this is a pretty pleasant place both for outdoor activities and for simple lying on the beach. By the way, about the beach, which has a boarding house "AzovLend" in the Crimea. Reviews of 2016 from vacationers about him are mostly positive: it is located in the area called “General's beaches”. This unusual name has roots in the history of the USSR. When the nomenclature of the Communist Party and all high-ranking officials of the state went on vacation to Azov, they liked to relax in the place where the AzovLend boarding house is now located (Crimea), reviews of which are often written on the Internet on special sites and, in fact, on the boarding house’s website .





“General beaches” is a unique nature that delights anyone who comes here on vacation. No wonder this place was so fond of the Soviet generals.
