On the handle of the device is a regulator with which you can adjust the mode of operation. However, due to the special, not very successful location of the handle, you should carefully select the appropriate mode. The controller switches are indicated by a small click.

On the handle of the iron are two buttons that control the flow of steam. One of them sprays moisture immediately in front of the iron, and the second includes vertical steam. Using the sharp nose of the unit, you can perfectly iron the fabric even in the most inaccessible places.
