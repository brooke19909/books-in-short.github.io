The capital of Tatarstan: from antiquity to the future

Everyone knows that Kazan is the capital of Tatarstan. But few people thought that this city can be called the center of the East European Plain. Located eight hundred kilometers from Moscow, at the confluence of the Volga and Kama, the capital of Tatarstan is in no way inferior to the capital of the Russian Federation in architecture, social or scientific development.

The capital of Tatarstan has more than a thousand years. Surprisingly, the modern city was able not only to acquire the features of the future, but also to maintain its old look. The historical center of the city is striking: the former apartment buildings and merchants, religious buildings, industrial buildings, standing here from past centuries, form an incredible architectural ensemble that amazes tourists.


The capital of Tatarstan is unique not only in architectural terms. It is home to more than ten universities, six academies, several theological educational institutions, three military schools and several branches of Moscow universities. The Academy of Sciences of Tatarstan is known for its achievements far beyond the borders of Russia.

Where there are many educational institutions, there are many young people. In order for young men and women to have a good rest, the capital of Tatarstan opened dozens of nightclubs, many restaurants with unique cuisine, and dozens of other entertainment venues.

Kazan is the most developed sports city in our country. That is why in the summer the World Universiade will be held here, which is another reason to visit a unique city. The Sports Village, built on the occasion of the Universiade, is the only such complex in the world. It is assumed that after the Universiade it will become a club of federal importance for the training of Russian national teams. And this is almost a thousand kilometers from Moscow!

You can talk about Kazan for a long time, but it is best to visit this city at least once. Because the capital of Tatarstan is the best example of how to strive for the future, respecting your past and cherishing the present.



And there is where to stay in the city. There are innumerable hotels. There are huge Art Nouveau buildings, there are hotels located in ancient buildings, small private hotels.

If you want to see something new, if you like to travel, remember: the Republic of Tatarstan is always glad to see you. Its capital will do everything to ensure that tourists who once visited the city come back here again and again.
