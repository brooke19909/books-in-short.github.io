Detroit City (MI): Interesting City Information and Outstanding Attractions

American Detroit (Michigan) - one of the largest metropolitan areas of the United States. Today, this city in America is similar to the exclusion zone (Chernobyl) in Ukraine. And before, Detroit was called the world automobile capital. The largest vehicle production on the planet was concentrated here. But life is so arranged that you have to pay for everything, and after rapt glory, sooner or later an eclipse comes. So it happened with this city: having survived an extraordinary rise, today it is turning into ruins and becoming a hangout for drug addicts and bandits.





The great past of a withering city

Detroit, Michigan, was founded on July 24, 1701. Its founder was the French officer Antoine Lome de La Mothe Cadillac. The future metropolis is located on a trade route, so the village where fur dealers lived has turned into an industrial center in a matter of years. At the end of the 19th century, a car factory was built near the city. And in 1903, its owner Henry Ford founded the Ford Motor Company brand, and from that moment the real boom of immigrants begins. After that, some more world leaders in the automotive industry opened their headquarters in the metropolis.

Detroit (Michigan) quickly gained the title of world automobile capital. At the state level, a program of public machines was implemented here. But since the 1970s, the sunset of the auto center has begun. Chrysler, General Motors and Ford are developing a financial crisis. Car magnates had to close their plants in Detroit and transfer them to those countries where labor was several times cheaper. During the 1970-1980s, the number of jobs in the city was reduced by more than 200 thousand. Over the past half century, the population of the metropolis has halved.

But Detroit does not give up, attracting tourists every year to explore local attractions and arrange car shows.





Entertaining facts

The ghost town of Detroit (Michigan), despite its bankruptcy, exists today. The business part of the metropolis works smoothly. Today it is a criminal center, but it was here that such famous personalities as Francis Ford Kopolla and Eminem were born. Here the musical direction of techno and the creme brulee dessert originated.

In Detroit, you can buy property for less than a hundred dollars. And not far from the center of the village are completely whole, but long abandoned neighborhoods. More than 20% of schoolchildren never receive a completed secondary education.

The modern population of the city lives according to independently established laws.

The main attraction of Detroit

Detroit (Michigan), a photo of which can be viewed in our material, is an automobile city, therefore, much is devoted to automotive equipment. One such attraction is the Henry Ford Museum. The complex is located in the suburbs of Detroit and is located on a vast territory. Henry Ford, the founder of the museum, began collecting his collection in 1906.

The open-air museum receives about one and a half million visitors every year. The complex has several sections. The largest of them is called America at the wheel. He talks about the development of the automotive industry in the United States.

But the institution is dedicated not only to automotive topics. Here you can also learn about how America turned into a technological superstate.





Downtown Attractions

Detroit (Michigan, USA) was once an amazing city, but now it is disappearing before our eyes. But still it has preserved a number of attractions that are worth seeing until they are finally destroyed.

The Renaissance Center is a complex of seven skyscrapers that deserves the attention of travelers. It is located on the banks of the Detroit River, in the center of downtown. Skyscraper towers are interconnected, and this architectural ensemble had a strong influence on the appearance of the metropolis.

Inside the skyscrapers there are many shops, four restaurants, offices of banks and various companies, a hotel for 1300 people, fitness centers and other institutions.

The height of the central tower reaches 221 meters and has 73 floors. This is the same hotel with 1300 beds.





Other sights of the village

Detroit (MI), the sights of which we are considering, is known for the Detroit Riverfront. Its length is eight kilometers. This territory has a myriad of boutiques, hotels, skyscrapers and restaurants. The aforementioned Renaissance Center is also located here.

Travelers are interested in the suburbs, especially Ann Arbor. It houses the Great Lakes Museum and the University of Michigan. The famous Greenfield Village Park can be found there.

It is impossible not to mention the skyscrapers of Detroit. In this city they are simply amazing, many of them were erected in the 1920s. Ancient buildings such as the Fisher Building and Penobscot Building are a sophisticated expression of art deco style.
