Recreation centers (Priozersky district, Leningrad region): description, services, photos

Priozersky district is popular among residents of the Leningrad Region. In the summer season, a large number of people come here in order to enjoy outdoor recreation near a pond.

Many tourists come here with tents. However, if there is no desire to completely abandon comfortable living conditions, it is necessary to pay attention to recreation facilities.

Priozersky district is ideal for both beach lovers and fishermen. Here are places, as they say, for every taste. Each tourist, regardless of income, will be able to choose for himself the most suitable option. The cost of tent places starts from 150 rubles. Rest in country clubs will cost a little more. Here you have to pay from 3000 rubles per day. And recreation centers (Priozersky district) provide housing, the cost of which starts from 250-300 rubles. Of course, depending on the price, different conditions of comfort are provided. Let's look at some recreation centers as an example of what amenities you can count on in a certain price category.

Holiday Village "Ladoga Lake"

On the shore of Lake Ladoga there is a cozy recreation center. It is called Lake Ladoga. The cost of living here starts at 3,000 rubles. Tourists can stay in cottages and hotel rooms. There are only 9 of them. The rooms have a bathroom, an entrance, a kitchen and a bedroom. Each has access to the terrace, from which you can enjoy a view of the lake. The rooms are equipped with various household appliances. This is a refrigerator, hob, microwave, TV and range hood. The bathroom comes with a shower, towels and a hairdryer. The recreation center "Ladoga Lake" provides the most comfortable conditions for recreation. Bed linen is provided. If necessary, laundry facilities are available. Kuzminki

In the village of Melnikovo there is a beautiful recreation center "Kuzminki". It was built on the banks of the Vuoksa River. The territory of the base itself is a large clearing, surrounded by a dense forest. Here cottages with rooms or tent places are provided for living. Tourists during the holidays will be able to stroll through the forest, picking mushrooms or berries, swimming, sunbathing, fishing or hunting. Rest on this base is focused not only on couples with children, but also on friendly companies. Thanks to the modern furnishings of the rooms, you can spend both weekends and long summer vacations. On the territory there is a parking lot, gazebos with barbecue facilities, a bathhouse, a play and sports grounds. The minimum cost of living is 1,500 rubles. You can relax at the base all year round.



Country Club Trava

Almost 100 kilometers from St. Petersburg is the Trava Club. The cost of living here starts from 3500 rubles. This base is ideal for holding various celebrations, corporate parties and just relaxing. There is an equestrian club on site. Cottages of different capacities are provided for living. They have a kitchen, a sleeping area, equipped with the necessary furniture, a bathroom, a shower. Cottages of this recreation center (Priozersky district) are heated in the winter. Young children are accommodated in the country club for free. The administration sets special beds in the rooms for them. If necessary, you can order an extra bed. Visitors are offered a wide range of bath complex services, those who wish can take a steam bath in a Russian bath. For children, specially equipped playgrounds are provided. On holidays or school holidays, the administration invites animators.



"Sail"

On the lake Sukhodolsky located recreation center "Sail". To the beach you must overcome a distance of 300 meters. Vacationers are offered a large selection of entertainment. For those who like to sunbathe and swim, there is a clean equipped beach. Fishermen at this base will also not be bored. The lake is home to many different underwater representatives.

For accommodation, rooms of economy, standard, elite and family categories are offered. The cheapest accommodation is houses for 8 people. One day in it will cost from 300 rubles. You will have to pay a little more for rooms with amenities, the initial cost is 1400 rubles.
