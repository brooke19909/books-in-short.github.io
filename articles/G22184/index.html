Transport accessibility of the complex

Potential residents are attracted by the good transport accessibility of this microdistrict, both on personal and public transport.

For example, you can drive your own car from the Lobachevsky new buildings to Vernadsky and Michurinsky Avenues, from where you can go in any further direction. For example, if you move five kilometers along Michurinsky Prospekt, you can go to the Moscow Ring Road.

disadvantages

At the same time, we must admit that this residential complex has enough shortcomings. For example, they are associated with a not very good location, since the LCD is located in close proximity to the railway, which runs literally under the windows of new buildings. About a kilometer - to the local thermal power station, the proximity of which significantly worsens the environmental situation in the entire microdistrict.

As a result, such a neighborhood not only spoils the air. It turns out to be so close that from the windows of the upper floors you can see the industrial zone with harmful enterprises, and the thermal power station, and the highway. Admittedly, a little inspiring sight.

In addition, a landfill is also located very close to the residential complex. It is located in the Ochakov highway. This fact can often be found in negative reviews of the Lobachevsky residential complex, for many it simply discourages the desire to buy real estate here, to invest its money, even though the microdistrict itself is considered very prestigious.

The disadvantages include the fact that the apartments are rented without finishing. Kindergarten and school are far enough away. In addition, they have a shortage of free places. Limited access to the territory of the residential complex, organized only on Aminyevskoye Shosse and Lobachevsky Street, leads to regular traffic jams in the morning and evening.

In addition, newcomers should be prepared for expensive maintenance. The management company will have to pay 60 rubles per square meter.
