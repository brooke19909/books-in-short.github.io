Kalinin Maxim: the hero of the Soviet cult film "The Adventures of Electronics"

At the age of 43, Maxim Kalinin left the world. The death of the Soviet actor and economist occurred on December 1, 2011 due to injuries that he received during his fall from a window from the fifth floor, where he lived in his apartment on Stroginsky Boulevard. There is no doubt that this was a suicide. This is evidenced by the note that Maxim Kalinin left before committing this act. In it, the actor argued that no one was to blame, that everything had happened. He ordered this act to be perceived as a personal desire to leave this world.





Biography

On August 31, 1968, Kalinin Maxim was born. The actor studied in Moscow at school number 93. In 1980, while still a schoolboy, he was lucky to play one main role in the famous film "The Adventures of Electronics". After school, Maxim Kalinin entered the Lomonosov Moscow State University at the Faculty of Economics. Since 1991, Maxim Nikolayevich has successfully worked in the finance market in Russia. He served as a stock market broker and worked for various banks.





Life after a star role

Despite the fact that the role in "The Adventures of Electronics" brought fame, Maxim Nikolayevich was not infected with star disease. He did not continue his professional activities in the field of cinema. And it would be very interesting to know how Maxim Kalinin the actor would become. Films with his participation have already managed to fall in love with the audience. Probably, his popularity would only grow. But this was not destined to happen, because he set his priorities not in favor of acting. In addition to one of the main roles, he played episodic in the series "White Horse" in 1993. His participation was in the episode Les Miserables. On this, Maxim Kalinin decided to end his acting career. Photos of him from the time of shooting in the movie and to the present are very different. But, nevertheless, looking at this cheerful person, you see him as a born actor. But all his remaining life in terms of professional activity, he devoted to finance and securities. By the way, it is worth noting that in this direction he managed to achieve high results.





Professional activity

Since 2011, Maxim Kalinin has been a member of the project team that is engaged in improving the infrastructure in the field of financing and regulation of the financial market of the working group that is working to open the International Financial Center in the Russian Federation. He also was active in the development of securities accounting infrastructure. In addition, Maxim Kalinin regularly published a number of articles. Basically, their topic was devoted to the diverse aspects of professional activity. I found free time for my own blog, which was dedicated to the professional Internet community. Since 2009, and for more than two and a half years, Maxim Nikolayevich has actively participated in discussions on issues related to the opening of the Central Securities Depository in Russia. At the same time, he participated in various conferences as a speaker. Their subject was associated with the dynamic development of the market infrastructure of securities.





Labor activity

Throughout his life, Maxim Kalinin has replaced a number of posts. These changes were due to the growth of him as a specialist in the field to which he decided to devote himself. Successfully developing, every year he became an increasingly professional employee, which contributed to his career advancement. In 1991, he began his career as a deputy head of the investment organization Vicar. For the next two years, he worked as an employee of the Moscow Finance Center in the brokerage department. From 1992 to 1993, Maxim Kalinin worked as deputy head of technology for Registrar NIKoil OJSC. In the period from 1994 to 2000, he was CEO at the same company. Since 2011, Maksim Nikolayevich has been a member of the Board of Directors of OJSC IC Prospect and CJSC New Registrar. In addition, since 1999 he has been a permanent member of the PARTAD board of directors.

According to these assumptions, an investigation was conducted. At the moment, the cause of this act has not been identified.
