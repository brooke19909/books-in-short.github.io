Where is it hot at sea in January? The hottest countries in January

In frosty and gloomy weather, I so want to get to where the summer is in full swing. Throw off piles of warm clothes, soak under the gentle sun, swim and dive in the winter in scuba diving - is this not what each of us dreams of? And to realize such a desire is not so difficult. Find out where it is hot at sea in January and hit the road!



Egypt

In winter, Egypt is not too hot, but with respect to Moscow it is the closest country where you can inexpensively relax in a good hotel with pretty good service. The Red Sea is famous for its rich underwater world, so in January you can go diving in Egyptian resorts. But at the same time, beach vacations and excursions to the magnificent pyramids have not been canceled.

Israel

You can relax at sea in January in Israel or Jordan. The temperature of the Red Sea at this time reaches a maximum of 20 ° C, if this is not enough for you, then go to the Dead Sea. In the middle of winter, it can warm up to a maximum of 23 ° C.

UAE

Despite the fact that January is considered the coldest month in the Emirates, the daily temperature reaches 25 ° C. In this country, all the conditions for outdoor activities are created: scuba diving, fishing, surfing, safaris, water parks, etc.

Thailand

From November to February, the average temperature in Thai resorts is 28-32 ° C. Locals, of course, consider this season to be very cold and not quite suitable for relaxation, but many residents of Russia perceive this weather as quite favorable for a beach holiday.



India and Sri Lanka

Where is it hot at sea in January? Warm and sunny weather in winter is promised to tourists by India, in particular, the state of Goa. The water temperature at this time is very comfortable for swimming - up to 25 ° C. This region is most often chosen by middle-class tourists who love exoticism. Goa resorts offer elephant rides and visits to ancient monuments. The beaches in the region are quite clean, and many hotels have quite decent service. Inveterate party-goers are advised to go to the northern part of the state. Most hotels here are economical, and parties are held from dusk to dawn.

While a blizzard is raging in Russia and frosts are raging, Sri Lanka is quite hot in January. The south and west of the country lie in the equatorial climate zone, this situation provides the region with year-round high temperature marks. The east and north are characterized by a subequatorial climate, there is less rainfall, but winter is cooler. Monsoons also strongly influence the climate of the island. But in general, a vacation on the island of Ceylon in January will be very comfortable. For example, in Trincomale, the air warms up to 27 ° C during the day, and drops to 24 ° C at night. Therefore, tourists will not need warm clothes.

Maldives

An honorable place in the list of “Hot countries in January” can be safely given to the charming Maldives. High temperatures on the islands are explained by the proximity of the country to the equator line. At the height of the winter season, vacation here can only be spoiled by a few rainy days. At noon, the thermometer can reach 30-32 ° C. Such climatic conditions in combination with almost complete calm will provide tourists with an excellent vacation. In the evening, the air can be cooled to +25 ° C. And the water warms up to + 28 , so even young children can swim for hours in the sea.



Mauritius

If you are looking for a resort where it is hot on the sea in January, then we recommend visiting Mauritius. Unlike countries that are in our latitude, on this picturesque island, January is the middle of summer. The weather in this period is just perfect for relaxing on the beaches. During the day, you can sunbathe and plenty of fun, and in the evening you can take a walk around the island or go to one of the local restaurants or bars. Note that in January in Mauritius there is a large amount of rainfall, but this is not an obstacle for tourists, showers pass very quickly and instantly evaporate.

It should also be borne in mind that on the ocean coast the temperature is approximately 5 ° C higher than in the central mountain region. However, it is in the center that most of the tourist attractions are concentrated.

The weather on the island of Mauritius, of course, differs depending on the time of day. During the day you can observe the heat up to 35 C, and in the evening the air can cool to 22 C about . Water warms up to 27 degrees, and air humidity in the winter is 81%. Mauritius is located in the tropical zone, so there are no sharp changes in temperature on the island.



Bahamas

Another place where it is hot at sea in January is the Bahamas. The cleanest beaches, the endless turquoise sea, picturesque evergreen vegetation, the highest level of service - all these are characteristic features of the Bahamas resorts that distinguish them from other popular places on our planet. Rest here is beautiful at any time of the year. In January, the air temperature is set at 23-24 degrees. During this period, the islands have mild and calm weather, almost no precipitation in winter. Such favorable conditions attract hundreds of tourists to the Bahamas. But in the summer the sea can be very stormy.

Cuba

If you want to hide from the cold, then go under the sultry sun of Cuba. The Liberty Island is dominated by a tropical trade wind climate. Between October and April, clear and dry weather reigns on the archipelago. Most tourists prefer to visit Cuba during this period. A beach holiday at the country's resorts promises to be entertaining. The coast of the archipelago is dotted with luxurious white beaches. Outdoor enthusiasts can go scuba diving and watch the coral reefs.



Brazil

Brazil is a place where you can safely go to the sea in January. The second month of winter in the country of the jungle, football and carnival is the real height of summer. At this time, the highest temperatures are noted here - air, as a rule, warms up to 27-30 C about , water - up to 28-30 C about . High humidity is also observed this month, because rains in January fall quite often.

In addition, tourists have another reason to visit Brazil in the middle of winter - January 20 in Rio de Janeiro and January 25 in Sao Paulo are the days of the founding of these cities. The celebration is accompanied by theatrical shows, festivities and large-scale nightly fireworks. And if you wait until the beginning of February, you can get to the grand carnival in Rio de Janeiro.



South Africa

Closing our list “The hottest countries in January”, let us recall the South African Republic. Here, at this time, it will definitely not be cold, but rather, on the contrary. On the coast in winter, air temperature is 25-27 ° C, and water is 20-22 degrees. In addition, tourists can choose for their holidays both the Indian and Atlantic Ocean.

Whatever country you choose for your winter vacation, you will surely be satisfied. After all, to come from the cold and blizzards to the warm sea is just an unearthly pleasure. Our citizens still have a stereotype from Soviet times that vacations should be planned only in the summer. But if you decide to pack up on an exotic resort in the midst of harsh Russian winter, you will get a lot of undeniable advantages: the lack of a huge flow of tourists, low prices for apartments in hotels and other services, as well as a charge of positive emotions that will definitely last until the warm days in our latitudes.
