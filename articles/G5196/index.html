Summer white openwork pullover knitting

In cool summer weather, a cozy sweater is useful. We offer you a description of knitting a summer openwork pullover with knitting needles. The product combines embossed braids with an openwork pattern of leaves. The color of the sweater can be changed, but on a white pattern it turns out very expressive. To knit such a product, you will need 600 g of yarn (44 size). It is best to use knitting needles number 3. The details of this pullover should be performed as follows:

  1. For the back to the knitting need to dial 109 loops and weave the main pattern. Knitting 56cm middle 30 loops for the neck. Each side ends separately. In order to round the edges, in each second row the loops are shortened: 1 time for 3 p., 1 - for 4 p., 1 - for 2 p. After 60 cm, the remaining 35 loops on each side are closed.
  2. The front is knitted like a back, but a deep neck is made. Having knitted 51 cm, 19 middle loops are closed. Then rounding is performed: 1-by 3 p., 1- by 2, 4 - by 1 p. Through a row.
  3. For the sleeves, 54 loops are typed and the elastic band is tied. To get the bevels of the sleeve, you need to add 3 times in the loop on each side in every sixth row. Close all the loops after 45 cm.
  4. Assembly of the pullover should begin with the implementation of the shoulder seams. Then, with the help of a hook, the neck is tied out with a "crab step". After that, the sleeves are stitched and the seams of the sides and sleeves are made.

Original zigzags

Many people think that knitting with knitting needles is not as difficult as crocheting, and such pullovers look much easier. It's a delusion! Various zigzags look especially stylish, they can be horizontal and vertical, smoothly turn into rhombuses.

It's no secret that the main loop for an openwork pattern, including zigzags, is the yarn. This is such a kind of additional loop that creates a mesh effect. If such holes are arranged in a certain order, then you can easily create a beautiful ornament. How do you get big holes? For such cases, there is a double or triple yarn. The execution of crochets requires special attention, since one wrong loop can ruin the entire pattern.

To make all kinds of zigzags of yarn, there are also crossed loops. To perform them, two loops cross each other at the base. Thus, a pattern is drawn on the canvas. "Ornament" in translation from Latin means - decoration. For openwork knitting, it means a well-organized pattern. Openwork weaving is a composition of ornaments whose motifs are constantly repeated.
