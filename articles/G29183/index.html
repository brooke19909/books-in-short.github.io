How to find out tax arrears of individuals by TIN?

Responsible residents of the state carry out this procedure independently without any reminders from the relevant services. What to do?

In the event that you are not sure of the correctness of the above deductions, you can independently find out the debt of individuals, that is, ordinary citizens. Thus, it is possible to find out tax arrears of individuals by TIN quite simply and quickly.
