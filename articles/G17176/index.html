Cafe "Floor" (Vladimir): photos and reviews

One of the most popular institutions in the city of Vladimir - cafe "Floor". An additional word "loft" is often attributed to its name, which characterizes it very well. It is within the walls of this institution that every guest who comes has the opportunity to relax well, plunging into the atmosphere of comfort.

Interior

Inside the institution is made in a youth style. However, despite this, representatives of any age category are comfortable in this institution. The walls of the cafe’s ground floor are decorated with colorful graffiti, where guests are often photographed. The mirror opposite graffiti is the perfect place to take a selfie.



On the second floor, guests can relax, for which there are brown soft armchairs and tables standing next to them. Guests can also sit in simple curly chairs of different colors and brown leather sofas. In the middle of the hall is a large dance floor, decorated with light music.

Along one of the walls of the cafe "Floor" in Vladimir there is a huge bar made of wood. Not only delicious cocktails are made behind this bar, but also excellent smoky hookah. In the institution, all the walls are decorated with neon inscriptions and signs.



Kitchen

Dishes of European, Oriental and Italian cuisine are offered to its visitors by the cafe "Floor" (Vladimir). The institution’s menu contains several pages on which rolls are presented, and their different varieties: poppies ("Tobiko", "Chuka", vegetable "Yasai", "Hokayda Maki", with eel, with salmon), uramaki ("Philadelphia", " Bacon roll, Ebi Cheese, Akenawa, hot (California Tempura, Kunsei, Oyama, Kazumi, Caesar Tempura, Unagi Spice Tempura) and baked (" Canada Spicy "," Syake Spicy "," Sea Baked "," Smoked Baked ").

Dishes of Italian cuisine in the cafe "Floor" in Vladimir are represented by a large assortment of pizza. Here they can cook "Capriccio", "Caesar", "German", "Assorted meats." The institution also prepares branded pizza, from the assortment of which guests managed to fall in love with its options: Pepperoni, European, Milano, Basilico Tomato, Mushroom, Sausage Mayhem, New York, Neapolitan. In the cafe "Floor" (Vladimir) serves delicious grilled barbecue. Here it can be made from pork and chicken, salmon, and also served juicy pork loin on the bone, grilled. As for the side dishes, you can order french fries, rustic potatoes, as well as grilled vegetables.



Here you can order several options for cold appetizers (pickles, slices, home-made herring), salads (warm with chicken, Greek with dried tomatoes, Caesar, Vinaigrette, beef tenderloin, Olivier with chicken). As for the first courses, their choice is small - there is a meat hodgepodge, chicken broth with eggs and herbs, mushroom cream soup, as well as borsch with sour cream and croutons.



On the hot side in the "Floor" they offer salmon on zucchini, tuna fried in teriyaki sauce, chicken schnitzel, beef steak in citrus marinade.

In the loft cafe "Floor" (Vladimir), the most demanding sweet tooth is pampered with an assortment of desserts. The most popular of them are ice cream with topping, tiramisu, brownie, vanilla-carrot cheesecake and apple strudel.

Bar

The bar card of the institution is represented by a wide range of cocktails. The talented bartenders who work here can surprise the guests of the cafe “Floor” in Vladimir with well-prepared cocktails “Hurricane”, “Vanilla Screw”, “Maria Andreevna”, “Bramble”, “Red Devil”, “Blue Lagoon”, “Tequila Sunrise”, “ Long Island Ice Tea, Cosmopolitan, Fresh Basil. Many of the mixes are branded.



From pure alcohol, the institution can offer vodka, wine, cognac, rum, whiskey, brandy, champagne, gin, whiskey, vermouth. Also, a separate place in the assortment is beer (bottled and draft).

Non-alcoholic drinks in the Floor serve juice, water, fruit drinks, fresh, energy drinks, as well as coffee and tea.

Service

All guests who come to the cafe "Floor" of Vladimir spend time very merrily, enjoying deliciously prepared food. In the kitchen, the cooking process is led by the chef of the institution - Grigory Orlov. It is under his strict guidance that works of culinary art are created. As for the work of the waiters, it is quite fast, as the institution is full. The staff in the loft cafe is polite and friendly, which creates an atmosphere of comfort during your stay in the institution.

Cafe "Floor" Vladimir delivers meals throughout the city. True, not all the items indicated in the menu are subject to targeted delivery, but only Asian dishes, pizza, barbecue and side dishes. You can make an order by calling the phone number listed on the cafe’s official website or in a group on the VKontakte social network.



Additional Information

The administration of the institution constantly monitors the comfort of guests within its walls. That is why, in addition to decent service and deliciously prepared food, visitors are offered various entertainment shows. The institution often hosts concerts by invited DJs, who set their compositions in a good mood for the whole evening. There are also often singers and dance groups. Every Friday in the cafe "Floor" in Vladimir, discos of the 80s and 90s are held, at which people light up under immortal hits.

For the convenience of guests, there is free parking near the establishment, where visitors who come by car can leave it. On the territory of the cafe there is a free access point to the Internet, which allows you to always stay in touch on social networks and immediately share new impressions gained from staying in a loft cafe.

Also, before a visit to the institution, you can pre-book a table for yourself and your company, which is convenient to do on the phone number listed on the official website.



Reviews

The institution, as it works, receives feedback from guests who have visited it. So, on the positive side, the dishes prepared here are noted. Guests enjoy the youth-friendly interior and the genre of playing music. The work of the waiters is often noted, moreover, good or bad - it depends on the time at which the guest visited the restaurant, because when the cafe is full, the maintenance staff does their work a little slowly, but in the daytime everyone quickly receives the ordered dishes, which subsequently gets a lot of good comments.

Address of the institution and mode of operation

The address where you can find the cafe "Floor": Vladimir, Dobroe, st. Komissarova, 16. The nearest landmarks are the EURECA Museum of Science and Man, as well as the Gingerbread House Museum.

The institution receives guests within its walls every day, but according to a different schedule. So, from Monday to Friday, you can glance here from 12 noon to midnight (Monday to Thursday) and until two in the morning (Friday), and on Saturday and Sunday - from two in the afternoon to two in the morning (Saturday) and until midnight (Sunday).
