Hotel "Omega", Vityazevo: description, attractions and reviews of tourists

One of the most beautiful places to relax on the Black Sea coast in the suburbs of Anapa is rightfully considered the coastal village of Vityazevo. There, the Omega Hotel opened its doors to guests cordially . Vityazevo is a tourist village, which includes all the necessary infrastructure elements, providing visitors with a comfortable stay.





Location

Omega is a modern fashionable hotel for families. It is a modern five-story building. Near the hotel there is a large pool. The pool is divided into two parts - for children and for adults. There is also a playground and an equipped free car park. The territory is well-groomed, spotlessly clean. The road from the hotel to the sea takes no more than two or three minutes, where guests can sunbathe on a gorgeous sandy beach and swim in the clear water. Very conveniently located, right on the central promenade, is the Omega Hotel. Vityazevo can please its vacationers with such entertainments as:

  • aquapark;
  • amusement park;
  • bowling and night clubs;
  • many catering establishments (cafes, bars, restaurants).

Address at which the Omega Hotel is located : Anapa, Vityazevo village , Barkhannaya St., 2.





Accommodation for vacationers

Tourists at this hotel can accommodate in 50 rooms. The hotel is designed for 150 people. The rooms are divided into three categories.

  • Double junior suites without extra bed. Their cost, depending on the season, ranges from 1,500 to 3,800 rubles.
  • Double junior suites with the possibility of placing one extra bed. Cost - from 1700 to 4200 rubles.
  • Triple junior suites with the possibility of placing one extra bed. Cost - from 1900 to 4600 rubles, depending on the season.

One child under the age of 4 years stays free of charge, provided that he does not need an extra bed. If children from 4 to 10 years old need extra beds, then they pay extra 200 rubles per day. The price does not include meals, but it can be ordered in the dining room owned by the hotel. All rooms have the following amenities:

  • a bathroom, which includes a shower, washbasin and toilet;
  • air conditioning;
  • plasma TV with satellite television;
  • comfortable balcony;
  • refrigerator;
  • round-the-clock access to free WIFI.

Pets are also allowed at the hotel. However, rooms are non-smoking.





Catering

The hotel has its own dining room with a large bright dining room. Food vacationers at their own expense. Here you can pre-order breakfast, lunch or dinner. The dining room menu includes both traditional and oriental dishes. When purchasing, preference is given to fresh products only from local producers. There is also a cafe-bar, which will pleasantly surprise you with a large assortment of drinks and light snacks. Those who wish to eat more densely can order the following dishes in a cafe:

  • barbecue;
  • seafood dishes;
  • charcoal dishes.




Children's recreation

The hotel pays a lot of attention to the rest of children. The following entertainments are provided for them:

  • special children's unit in the pool;
  • a large playground, in the territory of which there are swings, a trampoline and a slide;
  • animators spend free activities with children;
  • A shallow, well-warmed sea is perfect for bathing young vacationers.

But not only the Omega Hotel can entertain children . Vityazevo has its own water park "Olympia", which is located only fifty meters from the hotel and is always ready to delight both kids and adults alike with the variety of its attractions.

Attractions Vityazevo

Not only for a bright summer vacation, tourists come to the family hotel "Omega". Vityazevo, although the village, but has its own attractions.

  • Firstly, these are sources with healing clay - volcanoes. There are about 20 of them.
  • Museum "Wine Compound of the Old Greek". Here you can taste rare wines from grapes grown in the private vineyards of the courtyard.
  • Church of St. George the Victorious. It is located right in the resort. Entrance there is available to every believer.
  • The resort also has a winery, whose products are known far beyond the borders of our country. The company produces more than 10 names of wines.
  • The beautiful promenade "Paralia" is the main attraction of the Vityazevo resort. This is a favorite vacation spot for visitors. For them, on the promenade there are many restaurants, shops, souvenir shops. It is also called "little Greece."
  • The pride of local residents can rightfully be considered a pump room with mineral water. Almost every tourist will definitely come to the pump room at least once to try water with a unique chemical composition. At the source with this water, vacationers improved their health back in the 19th century.

Hotel Omega (Vityazevo), reviews

Tourists vacationing at the Omega Hotel, of course, share their impressions of their stay at the resort. The vast majority of tourists who visited the Omega Hotel (Vityazevo) , of course, write positive reviews .

  • Great location of the hotel. Near the beach, restaurants with good food.
  • There is a large pool on site, a playground for children.
  • Well-kept area, exceptionally polite staff, good dining room.
  • Clean rooms, beautiful interior. The room has air conditioning, a balcony.
  • The presence of a bar on site.
  • Free animation for kids. Friendly staff in the dining room.
  • Most rooms have sea views.

But, unfortunately, and in such an almost perfect place, there may be some flaws. Having chosen the Omega Hotel (Vityazevo) for relaxation , some tourists leave reviews , unfortunately, negative.

  • Some rooms overlook the wall of a neighboring hotel.
  • The dining room closes at 20.00, which some vacationers do not like.
  • They also believe that the price of a room for 3000 rubles should include breakfast.
  • Since mid-July, the sea blooms, making swimming difficult.
  • There is no electric kettle in the room, you have to ask the administrator.
  • Some complain about the low speed of the Internet.

Although in general, most vacationers still remain satisfied with the conditions offered to them at the resort.





Finally, a few words about how to get to the Omega Hotel. Vityazevo is located near Anapa, and visitors can easily get to the hotel by bus or by bus from anywhere in the city. And, of course, you can also get a taxi.
