Hotel "Mayak" in Blagoveshchensk: conditions, reviews and prices

Blagoveshchensk is a small city in Russia, the administrative center of the Amur Region. The village has a rich cultural heritage, so you can often meet tourists here. Although the resort town is not. Industry is flourishing here. Many come here on business trips. You can stay at the Mayak Hotel in Blagoveshchensk.

Basic information

The hotel complex is located on Voronkova street, house 15/1, near the regional hospital. The hotel has been providing its services for more than 10 years and is popular with visitors. You can stay here at any time of the year.

The hotel has a comfortable location. Public transport is nearby. From here you can easily get to anywhere in the city. In the immediate vicinity there is a car market and Amur State University. The Mayak Hotel in Blagoveshchensk is especially popular with applicants.

Reviews show that the proposed prices optimally combine the level of comfort and convenience of the hotel. Free breakfast, wireless internet and shuttle service are also included. Staff can meet and drive visitors to the bus or train station.

Number of rooms

The most popular among people who come for a few days on a business trip is a single room without a shower. For a day of living in such a room you will have to pay only 1,400 rubles. The apartments have a comfortable single bed, TV, refrigerator. For a double room without a shower you will have to pay 1,600 rubles. The room has a toilet. There is no residential block.

The cost of a more comfortable double room is 2,000 rubles per day. The apartments do not have a residential unit, but there is a shower and air conditioning. The room is popular in the summer. There is the possibility of accommodation at night (12 hours). In this case, you will have to pay 1,600 rubles for accommodation.

The Mayak Hotel (Blagoveshchensk) also has family rooms. For a triple room without a shower and a residential block, you will have to pay 2,250 rubles per day. The cost of living for 12 hours (night) is 1 950 rubles. A more comfortable triple room will cost 3,000 rubles.

Among foreigners, a deluxe room is popular. For a daily stay in it, one person will have to pay 2,400 rubles (1,900 rubles per night). The room has a comfortable double bed, a plasma TV, a soft sofa, a refrigerator, air conditioning, an electric kettle. There is an opportunity to use the bar.

The Mayak Hotel in Blagoveshchensk also offers junior suites. This is a comfortable apartment without a living unit, but with a large double bed, air conditioning, shower and refrigerator. Extras include a hairdryer and an iron. The cost of daily living in this room is 2,100 rubles.

Additional services

The management of the Mayak Hotel in Blagoveshchenka cares about the comfort of their guests. Everyone can use additional services. The hotel staff is available around the clock.

From 7:00 to 11:00 it is possible to get breakfast in the room. Food delivery from nearby restaurants is available until 23:00. The hotel has a luggage storage area. A safety deposit box is available at an extra charge. A business center is also available. If necessary, you can print or scan the documentation, send a fax.

At a high level, leisure can be organized thanks to the quality work of the staff of the Mayak Hotel. The reviews show that the administrator will always help to buy a ticket to the theater or museum, tell you where to go on a tour.

At the request of the guest, washing, ironing and drying of personal items is carried out. With a long stay at the hotel, bed linen is changed every few days.

Hotel reviews

Guests who have at least once had to stay at the Mayak Hotel speak well of her. Comfort and quality of service are fully consistent with the offered prices. The guests are also pleased with the convenient location of the hotel. Rooms with showers always have hot water. You can spend time watching TV. Each room has high speed internet.

The photo of the Mayak Hotel shows that the rooms are clean and comfortable. But some guests note that the single beds are not comfortable enough. A person with great growth is not always convenient here.
