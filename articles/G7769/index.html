Three Corners Triton Empire Hotel 3 * (Egypt / Hurghada): reviews, photos

Three Corners Triton Empire Hotel 3 * hotel (Egypt, Hurghada) is located in the old part of the world-famous resort city. Its location is ideal for tourists who want to enjoy all the advantages of the resort.

The hotel is within walking distance of the world-famous Sheraton Road with popular clubs, restaurants and shops. The road from the airport to the hotel is only seven kilometers. The beach is on the second line at a distance of 200 meters from the hotel itself.

The price of the tour for seven nights is only $ 378 per person for accommodation in a Standard room.

The territory of the hotel and its rooms

The hotel territory is landscaped and decorated with a swimming pool, which is located between two 6-storey residential buildings. She is professionally designed by masters of landscape design. Lawns, palm trees, geometrically trimmed plants - topiary are organically combined with pools.

Comfortable white plastic chairs with red seats and backs are placed here. Newcomers settle in the hotel immediately upon arrival, which is very convenient.

The hotel complex has 477 rooms. Of these, 277 are standard, 108 are improved with one double and two single beds, 24 - family: double and with three beds. They have all the necessary furniture: coffee and dining tables, chairs, beds, bedside tables. The rooms of Three Corners Triton Empire Hotel 3 * have individual climate control, TV, safe, minibar. The standard rooms have a shower, while the superior rooms have a bathtub.

Nutrition

Hotel guests generally prefer three meals a day (board) or two meals a day (half board) in the Sinuhe restaurant. The buffet is more modest in assortment than in a five-star hotel, however, the restaurant chef cooks excellently. There are also paid restaurants by appointment (Mediterranean Chez Pascal, Italian Dolce Vita and bars in the lobby area, by the pools, pub).



Most tourists who plan for themselves an interesting and varied excursion program in advance buy a half-board meal plan (so as not to unduly attach themselves to the restaurant‚Äôs work schedule). Although the hotel guests are offered another option of food - full board. In the latter case, meals in the buffets of the beach area and the pool area also become available to them.

Vacationers note the good work of the main restaurant and good quality food. They don‚Äôt save on meat and fish in main dishes, the portions are large, the menu has a lot of fruits.

Hotel infrastructure

Active free leisure includes beach volleyball, billiards, darts, table tennis. However, the list of paid services provided by the hotel is much larger.

For new arrivals, we recommend that you immediately visit the Turkish bath in the spa. The effect of this procedure is to free pores and cleanse the skin. Thus, the body prepares for a beach holiday and tan. But on the day of visiting the bath, going to the beach is undesirable. You should wait a day until the skin injured by the procedure is restored. The spa also has a sauna and massage services.



For a fee, hotel guests visit the modern fitness center, where they train under the guidance of experienced instructors. Here you can take several diving lessons.

The three-star service of the hotel complex involves round-the-clock customer service at the reception, free Wi-Fi, an ATM, as well as pre-arranged celebrations in the restaurant.

Positive and rare for three-star hotels is the presence of a mini-club open from 9-00 to 16-30, a special children's pool, a children's playground and animation.

Pool

Good outdoor pool of Three Corners Triton Empire Hotel 3 *. The area of ‚Äã‚Äãits water bowl is 322 square meters. It is equipped with water heating, so in January - February, some vacationers prefer it to the sea. In the pool area there are sun loungers and umbrellas that protect vacationers from direct sunlight.

In the pool itself, tourists have the opportunity to play water polo, and around it to do aerobics, beach volleyball, darts, ping pong.

The Trixie Kids Club, a mini-club for children from 4 to 12 years old, has a playground.

Also here from noon to 5 p.m. a buffet is open, which provides guests with tea, coffee, soft drinks, pastries, pizza and light snacks. Ice cream and freshly squeezed juices are available at the buffet.

Beach

Vacationers of the hotel complex considered in the article have the opportunity to visit the free beach of the Triton Empire Beach Resort. Thus, the owner optimized their infrastructure. There are pontoons on the beach leading to a nearby reef.



According to vacationers, the sandy strip of the beach itself is quite compact. The beaches are provided with sun loungers, umbrellas, beach towels. However, according to reviews, this inventory is noticeably worn.

However, entering the water is convenient, and this attracts families with children to the hotel.

There is a good health and sports infrastructure, a decent level of food (board and half board), even a mini club.

For experienced tourists (who save on "stars" of their lodging, but invest the money they saved in excursions, shopping, visiting restaurants and clubs), such a developed service is a blessing. Hurghada infrastructure closest to the hotel

Traditionally, guests of three-star hotels actively use the nearby resort infrastructure for their holidays. And in this regard, the hotel we are considering is no exception.



Wonderful shopping opportunities are provided by the guests of Three Corners Triton Empire Hotel 3 *. Their reviews quite meaningfully list the nodal points of the tourist infrastructure of tourist Hurghada (the impressive Hurghada Marina Boulevard hypermarket, the eastern bazaar of the old part of the city, the Pharaoh leather store, the Karkusha textile store, the Levis jeans, hundreds of souvenir and fruit shops on Sheraton Road Street).

However, those who bought a tour to Three Corners Triton Empire Hotel 3 * have the opportunity to plunge into the fun and idleness of resort nightlife. They have a huge entertainment center Senzo Mell, McDonald's, KFS, Pizza Hut restaurants, dozens of bars, massage parlors, hookahs, discos, designed for a different wallet size.

Excursions

Tours can be purchased directly from the tour operator (his guides will immediately offer you a full assortment upon arrival). However, one and a half times it will be more profitable to purchase an excursion in specialized tour agencies of Hurghada.

Having bought a tour at Three Corners Triton Empire Hotel 3 * (Hurghada), tourists have the opportunity to choose expensive and cheap excursions.

More expensive are trips to Cairo and the pyramids of Giza, as well as to Luxor. These places are branded for Egypt. And each guest of the Land of the Pharaohs should visit them at least once. But Egypt is not only a story in stone, it is also the beauty of the coral Red Sea. In addition, excursions to the North and South islands with their exotic beaches and picturesque waterfalls are cheaper than a trip to the pyramids.

Choosing an active vacation organized by numerous local diving schools, guests of Three Corners Triton Empire Hotel 3 * (Hurghada) receive a real drive from exploring the coral world of the Red Sea. Traveler reviews alternate with underwater photographs of vibrant exotic inhabitants of the underwater kingdom of Egypt: angel fish, parrot fish, butterfly fish, moray eels.

Conclusion

The hotel provides quality spa services. It is very conveniently located in the old part of Hurghada. Wonderful resort infrastructure of the city is within walking distance. However, it is not for people accustomed to a five-star vacation.



In the Three Corners Triton Empire Hotel 3 * hotel (photos of the hotel are presented in the article) both adults and children feel comfortable. Two-thirds of the guests of the hotel complex, who have already rested here, recommend it for visiting in their reviews. Having bought a tour here, a tourist gets a good launching pad for shopping, excursions, trips, diving. For all this, the service of a hotel complex of economy type is quite developed. The presence of a mini-club allows parents who come with children to combine family vacations with physical education, spa treatments and unforgettable shopping.
