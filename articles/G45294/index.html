Park inn resort 4

Description: Park Inn Resort 4 is located in the south of the Sinai Peninsula, in the Nabq area of ​​Sharm El Sheikh. The journey from the airport to the hotel will take no more than 10 minutes.



The hotel offers excellent conditions for couples with children, lovers of active entertainment and business people.

Rooms: The spacious, bright rooms at 401 feature an elegant, sophisticated Arabic design. Each has access to a balcony with views of the pool, sea or flowering garden.

The comfortable standard rooms can accommodate three adults or two adults with two children.

In the superior rooms, guests will find slippers and fluffy bathrobes, tea and coffee packages, and a welcome gift.

Large family rooms have a separate living room with a soft sofa, a bedroom and two bathrooms.



The junior suite consists of a living room, a bedroom and a bathroom with a spa bath. For guests, bathrobes with slippers, a set for tea parties, a welcome gift are prepared.

Meal Plan: Park Inn Resort 4 features an all-inclusive scheme and a buffet system. International cuisine is served in two restaurants. The Aqua Piper features a patio terrace and serves hot snacks, freshly baked pastries and fruit for breakfast. Lunch and dinner are of great gastronomic variety. The Palms Restaurant and Bar specializes in salads and other healthy foods.

An exotic evening awaits vacationers in the picturesque setting of a Bedouin tent with a hookah and an original menu. Relaxation by the pool can be combined with a visit to the Lotus and Tiran bars with a selection of fruit drinks, cold beer and light snacks. The lobby bar is a popular place where you can not only have a bite to eat, but also use the Internet connection. Chilled drinks are offered at the water park bar and on the beach.



Beach: Private sandy beach with sunbeds is a kilometer from the hotel.

Additional information: Park Inn Resort 4 offers tourists a full range of diverse services.

On site there is a multifunctional fitness center with beauty and massage rooms, steam rooms and a jacuzzi. For children and adults, a free water park with pools and slides is designed.

In the diving center, vacationers will master the technique of sea diving. A lot of entertainment awaits sports lovers, and fans of exotic are invited to the desert on a safari. A mini club and playgrounds are open for children.

The Park Inn Resort has facilities for organizing business meetings and conferences. There are several fully equipped multi-purpose rooms. The hotel is an ideal venue for an unforgettable wedding or family celebration.



Digest: A great place to stay with children in Sharm El Sheikh is the Park Inn Resort 4. Tourists reviews contain a lot of useful information about this comfortable hotel.

In the spacious rooms, everything is soundly and thoroughly, the equipment is in working condition, cleaning and towels are changed every day. The staff is unobtrusive, but always attentive and friendly.

The restaurants have any kind of food, both casual and gourmet. For dinner, various dishes of meat, fish, seafood, including barbecue, are served daily. For children - a separate room in the restaurant.

Park Inn Resort 4 is located on a vast green area with neat paths, statues, lanterns, fountains and waterfalls. There is a water park and several pools. The hotel has a beautiful well-kept beach with a coral reef. Nearby is a large shopping center and tents with souvenirs.

According to most vacationers, Park Inn is a good budget hotel for the whole family, deserving a solid four.
