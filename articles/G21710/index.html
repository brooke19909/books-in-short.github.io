How to find out debts from bailiffs by last name? Bailiff Service: finding debt is easy

Debt is an unpleasant thing, but to be a debtor is not so difficult. Forget about the traffic police penalty ticket, make a mistake when paying for utility bills and accumulate penalties, delay credit payments. You can get into an unpleasant story by acting as a guarantor of a loan.





Why are debts dangerous?

Even small amounts of debt can turn into big troubles, disrupt vacation abroad, delay the receipt of the necessary documents, make it impossible to get a loan, and prevent the registration of movable or real estate. It can be quite unpleasant if they use the favorite method of influencing defaulters - the arrest of a current account.

The sadness with overdue debts is that payment information appears, for example, in the customs authorities, within two months from the date of repayment of the debt - this is how the bailiff service works. It is recommended to find out debts and pay in advance so that the information arrives at the right address, and you do not suffer from debts that have already been paid.

Travel restrictions

Debt can cause you to not be allowed to go abroad, whether it is a vacation or a business trip, it does not matter. Therefore, if enforcement proceedings have been instituted against you and a writ of execution has been issued for a debt of more than 10,000 rubles, it is likely that a restriction on exit will become a penalty measure. The restriction can be removed only by paying off the debt or otherwise reaching an agreement with the FSSP.





The situation is somewhat simplified by the fact that, according to the law, bailiffs are obliged to send registered notifications that, in case of non-payment of arrears, a travel restriction will be imposed. However, you will receive this notice only if you live at the place of registration. Therefore, it is important to include an item on information on debts with bailiffs by last name in the plan for preparing for the trip, so that an unpleasant surprise is not waiting for you at the border.

Why donors do not always know what they should?

The fastest and most effective way to collect debt today is to go to court. Credit organizations are especially fond of filing lawsuits. The presence of the defendant is not required, the specifics of Russian law allow to consider such cases unilaterally, which means that you can not know anything about the last meeting or the sanctions imposed.





Then the writ of execution is handed over to the federal service of court bailiffs, and they are already getting down to business. Therefore, before you go on vacation to the sea or embark on important matters, you should find out the debts of the bailiffs by last name. Otherwise, your debt can become a serious hindrance in both business and leisure.

There are two options for finding out debts. Bailiffs make a personal visit to their service and learn about debts there, it is not difficult. Moreover, it is in your interests. This is the first way to find out debts from bailiffs by last name. But since the Federal Law obliged the bailiffs to conduct a bank of enforcement proceedings in electronic form, that is, a more convenient and faster way to find out debts from bailiffs is via the Internet.

Useful information in the table.

If you still find yourself in debt, then from the table you can draw a number of information that will allow you to quickly deal with an unpleasant story.

How to pay debts

How to find out debts from bailiffs by last name, figured out. Now let's figure out how to pay them. If you find yourself in the table, you will see that in the column "Service" there is a button "Pay". By clicking on it, you can pay off your debt using a bank card, electronic money or using some other payment systems (Qiwi, Robokassa). But this will not happen immediately, but within 3-7 days from the moment you make the payment. Therefore, after a week, it is advisable to use the service again and check whether the record about you has disappeared. Until you make sure that everything is in order, do not throw away payment documents. Only by presenting them can you prove that you paid the debt.
