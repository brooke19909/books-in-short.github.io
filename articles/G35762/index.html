Irons "Vitek": customer reviews

The Vitek brand appeared in Russia in 2000. Now the equipment of this brand occupies a leading position in the market and is popular among Russians. The company creates devices for everyday life, without which a modern person cannot exist. Irons "Vitek" (reviews note that the device glides easily on the surface, quickly heats up and has a stylish modern design) - this is no exception, they have gained popularity among thousands of women.

Product range

The company in a large assortment produces Vitek irons. Reviews indicate the versatility of these devices. They say that this technique is affordable and, despite this, is not inferior to more expensive competitors. So, Vitek irons are divided into several categories, these are:

  • appliances where the sole has a ceramic coating;
  • devices with auto power off;
  • products with the provided anti-scale system;
  • Irons with anti-drop system;
  • devices with a double sole;
  • non-stick coating equipment;
  • irons with a special anodized sole.

All products, regardless of category, have an affordable price and excellent quality, smooth temperature control. They easily stroke even the most delicate things. The degree of heating of the sole in each iron changes gradually.

The anti-drop function prevents water from leaking out of the tank when ironing at low temperatures. Spot steam boost makes it possible to smooth out the wrinkled fabric as much as possible. The anti-limescale option purifies water from heavy salts due to the special ion-exchange resin in the filter . Automatic shutdown mode makes the device as safe as possible. He turns off the iron if it is not used for a certain time.

Each buyer can easily choose the most suitable model for himself. When choosing a product, you should pay attention to the sole, which can be both ceramic and anodized. The latter provides a smoother glide, antistatic effect, has openings for steam or contains particles of tourmaline.

The cost of the device is influenced by the number of built-in functions, material quality, design. There are models that allow you to steam a thing vertically. This makes it possible to iron things on a hanger or to iron the curtains.

Irons "Vitek" with auto shut off





Sometimes the ironing procedure can cause a fire due to the iron not being plugged in. To prevent accidents, you should pay attention to the Vitek irons with auto power off.

Household appliances have special sensors that trigger the position of the iron. They are divided into two types:

  • Horizontal. Turns off the device if it is not used for 7-15 seconds.
  • Vertical. It works if the product takes a vertical position for some time.

Irons "Vitek" can have either one of these sensors, or both. In addition to the listed functions, devices of this category vary in:

  • power;
  • steam pressure;
  • type of sole;
  • the volume of the water tank;
  • anti-drip system;
  • steam boost power.

Devices with automatic shutdown are protected against scale, which plays an important role in the use of ordinary tap water. This function extends the life of the device.

All irons with automatic coating are characterized by safety, high quality ironing, multifunctionality, and long service life.
