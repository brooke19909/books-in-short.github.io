How to leave a request at Sberbank for a loan: step-by-step instructions, documents and recommendations

Who is eligible

Not everyone is able to leave a request at Sberbank for a loan. There are some limitations to this. Which ones? Every potential client should know about them.



First, loans to Sberbank are not issued to pensioners. More precisely, if a citizen does not have official employment and receives only pension payments.

Secondly, only an adult can draw up an application with the expectation of approval. And he must either have a permanent source of income, or official employment. As practice shows, the unemployed do not receive approval of the request in the specified organization.

Accordingly, the requirements in order to be able to leave an application with Sberbank for a loan, as well as receive it, are as follows:

  • adulthood;
  • stable income;
  • lack of criminal record;
  • citizenship of the Russian Federation (not required, but citizens of the country are most often clients of the organization);
  • full legal capacity.

Only in this situation can we hope for success. Otherwise, even sending a request to the bank is not recommended. What's next?

How can I apply for a loan? To come to Sberbank and submit a certain package of documents with a statement of the established form. Further, the citizen will have to wait for the approval of the request, and if it does, receive money with the preliminary conclusion of the contract.
