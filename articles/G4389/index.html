Chograisk reservoir - a place for productive fishing

Fans of fishing almost always know fishing places where you can quite successfully sit with a fishing rod and not return home empty-handed. One of these places is a reservoir, which is located in the Stavropol Territory. It is called the Chograisk reservoir. Everyone who fished here can confidently say that fishing in this place is quite productive.

The Chograi reservoir was chosen by some species of rare birds that nest nearby. Some of these birds are even listed in the Red Book of Russia, for example, curly and pink pelican, spoonbill. There is also a loaf and black-headed laughter.





The reservoir is located on the border of Stavropol and Kalmykia. Note that this is the main body of water in the area where the most successful autumn and winter fishing has been recorded.

How to get to the reservoir?

Many avid fishermen living in the Stavropol Territory seek to visit the Chograisk reservoir. How to get to it is a question that interests many. This is a rather complicated process. If you go from the city of Pyatigorsk, then the path must be laid in the direction of Georgievsk. Then you need to drive through Budennovsk, passing Petropavlovsk. When the reservoir is very close, you should go to the Kuma-Manych Canal, and then go along the channel to the point of flow into the coveted reservoir.

If we calculate the distance from Pyatigorsk to the reservoir, then it is about 250 km. The road in the last third of the way is not solid enough and of high quality, so you need to prepare for this. In this case, the SUV will become the best means of transportation.





After you find out where the Chograisk reservoir is located, you can safely start collecting bait, fishing rods and other fishing tackle.

What types of fish are found?

However, it should be noted that as a result of this negative experiment, only small-sized fish suffered damage. As for the large predator, then in this reservoir, it thrives.
