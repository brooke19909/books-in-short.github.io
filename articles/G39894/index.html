Mobile networks: overview, features, rates and reviews

In Russia there are different mobile networks. Each company has its pros and cons, some attractive and useful, profitable offers. To whom does the population of the Russian Federation trust more? Which operator is recommended to use for certain needs? Understanding all this is not so difficult if you pay attention to the feedback of real subscribers. Operators always praise their products. But the real picture often does not coincide with the promises. So which companies in Russia are recommended to pay attention to first of all? Who offers the most profitable and high-quality services?



Why use?

The first step is to decide for what purpose it is planned to use the mobile device and its network. Each operator is good in one area or another. And this factor will have to be taken into account. After all, there are no ideal companies.

In general, mobile networks are used for the following needs:

  • work on the Internet (Internet surfing) ;
  • telephone calls;
  • upload / view photos;
  • uploading videos;
  • video call;
  • exchange of data and messages on the Internet;
  • SMS communication.

It should be noted that mobile operators are actively promoting television services, as well as home Internet. Because of this, it has become more difficult to determine the service company.

List of operators

And among whom can I choose? What mobile networks are most common in Russia? In different cities, a variety of firms. But in Russia there is a generally accepted list of mobile network operators. They are available in almost every city.

Today, choose a company to provide mobile services from the following list:

  • Yota
  • "Tele 2";
  • MTS;
  • Beeline;
  • "Megaphone".

It is these operators that are most common in Russia. Therefore, they should pay special attention. What are the pros and cons of each firm? What can they offer their customers?



Influence factors

Mobile operators are usually ranked by several indicators. They can have a serious impact on the decision of the subscriber. Once a citizen has decided to use a mobile network, an assessment should be made of all existing companies offering this service.

But what points should you pay attention to first of all? Among the main factors that influence the decision of a potential subscriber, there are:

  • cost of services;
  • connection quality;
  • signal strength in the village;
  • tariff plans;
  • the quality of the Internet;
  • customer reviews.

Accordingly, according to these indicators, networks are usually evaluated. What can each operator offer? In what cases and what tariffs will be considered the most favorable?

The next option offered to residents is Megaphone. This company has long existed in Russia, it is considered one of the largest. Mobile device networks from this company do not work very stably, but all communication is budgetary. You do not need to pay a lot to view messages on the Internet or make a call. Subscribers recommend Megafon to those who prefer not to use the mobile network too actively.

In general, communication is good. So far, many from Tele2 are repelled by the fact that this operator is not distributed throughout Russia. But this is far from the most important factor. Beeline

The following mobile networks are Beeline services. This operator is one of the largest of its kind. It is in great demand among customers.
