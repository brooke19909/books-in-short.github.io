Logitech M185 wireless mouse: specifications and reviews

Despite the outward simplicity and seemingly insignificance of the function against the background of the tasks of the modern system unit, a computer mouse is a very important component. She is responsible for the quality of user communication with system resources, in which ergonomics are of great importance. Finding a suitable model can be difficult, and practice shows that if development is successful, then it suits a wide range of consumers. This rule is partially confirmed by the Logitech M185 wireless mouse , the photo of which is presented below. The manufacturer proposed several modern technologies for an average fee, but some technical and structural flaws also appeared with them.





Model Overview

The device is a mid-level model, implemented on the basis of the classic design, but with wireless communication technology. Actually, the developers succeeded in a combination of the familiar form factor and battery power in general, since there are almost no common complaints about the heaviness of wireless modifications in this case. However, this depends on individual requirements, which also do not always meet the Logitech M185 wireless mouse .

The description of the structural design can be reduced to such characteristics as minimalism, optimality and at the same time ergonomics. The manufacturer did not experiment and limited itself to long-established solutions. The presence of three buttons without additional support indicates a failure in the calculation of the gaming segment, although the working qualities will suit a certain group of gamers.

Model design





An example of this mouse shows that appearance is not the main factor of success in the market. The case is made in a standard concept without any frills. Soft and smooth lines are quite consistent with the modern idea of ‚Äã‚Äãthe convenience of using such accessories. But so that the model is not at all associated with something boring and unsightly, the designers diluted the color scheme with several solutions. In addition to the basic gray design that the Logitech M185 Swift Gray mouse represents, there are also versions with a red and blue top panel.

The practice of using the model shows that its sole and the entire bottom surface are optimally oriented towards universality in terms of compatibility with different surfaces of the rugs. Regardless of the material, the mouse provides accuracy and sharpness in manipulations.

Functionality





Of course, the main characteristic of this product is a wireless communication system. The compact radio is easy to use and has a wide range of supported operating systems. As the manufacturer himself notes, Plug-and-Play technology eliminates the hassle of installing software. That is, by design, the Logitech M185 wireless mouse driver should not be installed in a separate order: immediately after the integration of the module, you can start working.

However, there are many reviews that nevertheless note the need to install special software. But such cases, most likely, refer to owners of computers running on outdated OS. In the rest, the situation with connecting to a USB port and the operation of the module at a frequency of 2.4 GHz allows you to completely forget about the communication method. By the way, here it will not be out of place to note the dignity of the mouse in terms of energy saving. On average, the device lasts up to 12 months from high-quality batteries, without requiring updating the power source.

Specifications





In terms of working qualities, the model is one of the best in its price segment. True, this aspect is rather subjective, and much depends on the initial requirements, on the basis of which the Logitech M185 wireless mouse is evaluated . The characteristics of its technical parameters, presented below, will help to understand this issue:

  • Dimensions of the device - 60x99x39 mm.
  • Weight with battery - 75 g.
  • The parameters of the receiving sensor are 14x19x 6 mm.
  • The mass of the receiver is 1.8 g.
  • The resolution of the manipulator is 1000 dpi.
  • The type of battery is 1 AA battery.
  • The range of the receiver is 10 m.
  • The receiver frequency is 2.4 GHz.

The technological advantages of the mouse include an improved optical monitoring system, which ensures the stability of the wireless interaction of the sensor with the computer. It is important to take into account that the claimed signal coverage range of 10 m is possible only under optimal reception conditions.

Positive reviews





The owners of this model note several operational advantages. You should start with the quality of reception, stability, clarity of work and overall ergonomics. Separately, there is a lack of backlighting, which in some cases can be a hindrance.

Another significant advantage that users note is the energy-saving feature. It is not even a matter of the long battery life that the Logitech M185 wireless mouse is equipped with. Reviews indicate the need for resources of only one battery, and not two, as is the case with typical manipulators working without wires. For this reason, by the way, the mass of the device is within the optimal value from the point of view of ease of use.

Negative reviews





Among the critical reviews from users there are references to the general quality of the materials of manufacture, the nuances of the operability of individual buttons and the noise when pressed. As for the quality of the case, there are no complaints about its mechanical strength, but many indicate the wear resistance of the material: after a certain time of use, the surface is wiped off, and this distorts the overall appearance of the device.

Regarding the functioning of the buttons, there are complaints about the operation of the middle button - tight pressing and even complete immobility are noted. An important aspect is the sonority with which the Logitech M185 wireless mouse works when the buttons are pressed. In general, the concept of silent manipulators is not widespread in itself and is distinguished by manufacturers at the level of an optional advantage. Therefore, it is probably not worth attributing the absence of such an ergonomic improvement to the model flaws. Frankly loud clicks of the device can not be called either.

Who should use the Logitech M185 Wireless Mouse?

The manipulator is an implementation of the principles of a standard computer mouse: compact, moderately functional, universal and comfortable for both hands. By the way, the introduction of a radium sensor for communication using the example of this model already confirms the standard of wireless solutions.

In the context of the above, we can conclude that the Logitech M185 wireless mouse is the best solution for ordinary users of home PCs and office equipment. This is an average option, so the requests of sophisticated gamers, for example, this model is unlikely to satisfy.

Conclusion





Choosing a computer mouse suggests its own specifics. The fact is that even technical specifications and functionality do not always allow us to evaluate how useful and convenient the device will be during operation. Of course, parameters such as resolution, connection method and number of buttons are important, but they do not give an idea of ‚Äã‚Äãthe level of comfort of use, which can only be experienced in the "field" conditions. And in this regard, the Logitech M185 wireless mouse differs from the total mass of manipulators precisely in its hidden ergonomic advantages.

The combination of a compact body, well-thought-out geometric lines and balanced mass may have become a determining factor in the success of this model. But to this it is worth adding a democratic price tag, amounting to an average of 1100 rubles, which is not bad for a wireless model.
