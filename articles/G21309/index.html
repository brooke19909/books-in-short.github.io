Sanatoriums of Pitsunda, Abkhazia. Photos and reviews of tourists

Pitsunda is a recognized health and beach resort. The air here is saturated with volatile, which secrete numerous conifers and marine minerals. Sanatoriums and pensions Pitsunda (Abkhazia) attract many tourists and people who need spa treatment.

Benefits of a Holiday in Pitsunda

This resort is famous for the best beaches in Abkhazia. They are not only pebble, as in other coastal cities of the country, but also sandy. Almost the entire territory of Pitsunda is covered by an ancient relict pine grove, which is fragrant with needles. Great Pitsunda for families with children. Clear water, magnificent nature, a good location for visiting excursions will leave a very pleasant experience.



Prices in the city (depending on the season) can vary significantly for holidays in Pitsunda. Sanatoriums and pensions of the city do not work all year, but only from April to November, when the resort's climatic conditions are optimal and the water in the sea warms up to comfortable temperatures.

Sanatoriums Pitsunda

Health facilities Pitsunda specialize in wellness and climate therapy. Sun baths, sea bathing and air saturated with bromine, iodine, volatile and other useful trace elements will significantly improve your well-being. Due to the natural impact, people visiting Pitsunda sanatoriums (reviews confirm this), get rid of stress, strengthen and restore mental strength.

Especially relaxing in Pitsunda (in sanatoriums) is recommended for people with disorders of the nervous system, respiratory diseases and circulatory problems. There are not so many sanatoriums in Pitsunda, but they provide specialized treatment for ailments. Among them are those that Abkhazia and Pitsunda are proud of. Sanatoriums with treatment have proven themselves far beyond the city limits. Thousands of tourists from all over the country undergo treatment here.





Boxwood Grove is the most popular resort. Pitsunda (Abkhazia) today has a rich and modern medical base for the treatment of circulatory diseases, nervous and musculoskeletal systems. Not all Pitsunda sanatoriums, unlike the Boxwood Grove, have their own hydropathic facilities. Therefore, people with problems of the heart and blood vessels relax and treat themselves here. The treatment in a health resort has a beneficial effect on the skin condition in certain diseases. In this sanatorium, non-pharmacological methods are widely used - inhalation, balneotherapy, massage.

Similar services are provided in the sanatorium "Sana", which also has a hydropathic center.

Pitsunda: motels and hostels

The boarding house with a wellness profile "Litfond" offers vacationers a course of hydro and physiotherapy. This unique complex is located in the Pitsunda Nature Reserve. It is well located between the park area, Lake Inquit and the sea coast. Magnolia and boxwood, dogwood, noble laurel and laurel cherry, oleander and azalea, eucalyptus, pomegranates and acacia grow here.

All pensions and motels of Pitsunda pay for preventive and medical services separately, if desired, in pensions you can stay on vacation, as in ordinary hotels.

Resort Sanatorium "Pitsunda"

Located in the city center, the complex is located on the seafront. It is surrounded by a pine grove, creating a healing microclimate and giving a pleasant coolness even in the summer heat. The coast of the resort is located in a picturesque bay, so the sea is clean and calm. This place is as if created for lovers and connoisseurs of natural beauty and ecology.



Pitsunda is designed for 2800 seats in seventeen high-rise buildings. Guests are offered double rooms with and without amenities, a TV in the lobby of the buildings. Three dining rooms are located on the territory of the complex (one works according to the fixed menu system, two - according to the "buffet" system). On the seafront there is a restaurant by the sea, offering national cuisine, billiards, sauna, massage, tennis courts, a shopping center, a hairdresser, a concert hall. The beach is sand and pebble. It has free umbrellas, sunbeds, fun on the water. Those who wish can visit boat excursions along the coast of Abkhazia

Pension Amra

It is located 22 km from Adler. Nearby are a water park, bars, cafes, discos, restaurants. Here you can take a course of treatment of diseases of the heart and blood vessels, pulmonary, skin, urological, gynecological ailments, diseases of the spine, musculoskeletal system, nervous system.

In the main building there are rooms of different price categories. Double rooms (one and two rooms) - with sea view.

"Riviera"

This guesthouse has recently been completely renovated. It is located almost in the center of Pitsunda and is perfect for a relaxing holiday with your family. The residential building is surrounded by a park with magnificent cypress avenues, one of them leads to the beach.



"Riviera" offers guests three meals a day (buffet). The basis of the diet is made up of natural dairy products, fruits, homemade cakes. At the boarding house there is a parking lot.

Mussera

The territory of the Pitsunda-Musser reserve is closed. On it is the guest house "Mussera". This is a huge resort complex occupying 180 hectares. Subtropical forests and gardens surround the residential building, from its windows a magnificent view of the Caucasus mountains opens, which peaks rest against the clouds. Vacationers can spend time on a huge sandy beach.

"Irene"

The new tourist facility, built according to a modern design, belongs to the new generation of boarding houses in Abkhazia. The complex consists of two buildings, which are located on its own territory, surrounded by fruit trees. The guesthouse is 200 meters from the sand and pebble beach.



All rooms are equipped with stylish furniture and high-quality sanitary ware; from the windows of all rooms the views are amazingly picturesque. Meals - two meals a day (buffet).

Apsny

It is located in the Pitsunda Nature Reserve. Pitsunda sanatoriums, as well as many boarding houses, work only during the holiday season, while Apsny is waiting for guests throughout the year. Vacationers can do their favorite sports - tennis, billiards. You can visit the gym, restaurant, night club, bowling alley, sauna, beauty salon, souvenir shops. For vacationers are invited to visit excursions, horse riding lessons. Three meals a day. There is an outdoor pool, a wide and well-groomed private beach. At first there is a strip of sand, which is replaced by pebbles closer to the sea. Tourists have access to the beach: water skiing, a glider, catamarans, diving and sea hunting, a banana, helicopter rides.

"Pine Grove"

Perhaps this is the most popular boarding house in Abkhazia. It consists of two buildings and detached luxury and standard cottages. It is located on the seashore. The territory of the boarding house occupies a rather large territory (1.5 hectares), which also covers part of the Pitsunda relic grove.
