Among other resort countries, Greece is famous for the beauty of its beaches. The sea here, especially around the islands, has a unique azure-turquoise hue, it is amazingly clean and transparent.

Swim on the beaches of Greece begin in June. The beach season lasts until October. Some daredevils begin to swim earlier, for example, in May. It is especially worth noting that the air temperature at this time is quite acceptable. Sometimes it reaches even + 25-30 C. But seawater, of course, does not warm up as quickly as air. Therefore, it will still be more comfortable to start the swimming season in June. This differs from other resorts in Greece.
