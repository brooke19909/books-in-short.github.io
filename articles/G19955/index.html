Hotels in Vidnoye: overview, rating, addresses and photos of rooms

Prominent is one of the most comfortable cities in the Moscow region. Basketball competitions are constantly held in the sports complex, which means that there are many athletes in the city. Also nearby is Domodedovo International Airport, so transit passengers need rest every day. This means that there is a need to create conditions for their residence. Hotels in Vidnoye provide the city guests with the best and most comfortable accommodation.

Hotel "Vita"

The highest rating in the city is the Vita Hotel. The location of the hotel is a quiet park area. The hotel has its own concept based on the philosophy of wellness. Here you can restore your strength, recharge with positive emotions. It features a fitness center and a large indoor pool. The spa offers a range of wellness treatments. For an additional fee you can do aerobics, table tennis, bowling. For children, there is a playground and a games room. After the wellness treatments, you can breathe fresh air in the park, take a walk to the river, which is within walking distance of the hotel.

If we consider hotels in the Vidnoye Moscow Region, the Vita Hotel is an ideal option to relax from the hustle and bustle of a big city. Meals are organized in your own restaurant, children's and adult menus are separate. There is free private parking, and there are parking spaces for people with disabilities. Wireless internet access is also free. Shuttle service from or to the airport is possible.

The hotel has 41 rooms, including:

  • standard double - 5400 rubles per day for two people and 4900 rubles if one person lives;
  • double with a package of wellness services - respectively, 7500 and 7000 rubles per day;
  • luxury category - 8900 rubles per day for two people and 8400 rubles if one person lives;
  • Suite with wellness package - 12,500 and 12,000 rubles per day, respectively.

The cost of all rooms at Vita Hotel is based on a good breakfast. The hotel is located in Vidnoye at the address: Zavodskaya street, 15. The hotel has a four star category.

Hotel Kalina

The next in the ranking among the hotels in Vidnoye is the Kalina mini-hotel, which is located next to a picturesque park with a pond. The hotel has a category of three stars, is a cozy two-story building. You only need ten minutes to get to Tsaritsyno Park from here. Domodedovo Airport is a 25-minute drive away. Hotel guests can use the services of the spa, in the common area there is a table for playing billiards. Breakfast in the morning is served in a separate room; guests can have lunch and dinner in the hotel's own cafe. Parking space and wireless internet are available for free. Shuttle service from or to the airport is possible. Pets are allowed by prior arrangement.

To accommodate guests, several categories of rooms are offered:

  • the most inexpensive is a single standard - 1985 rubles per day;
  • living in a double room with a hot tub will cost the most - 8360 rubles per day.

Upon request, breakfast can be included in the price. If residents of the capital are interested in hotels located near Moscow in Vidnoye, the hotel is a good option, as it is only four kilometers from the Moscow Ring Road. The mini-hotel "Kalina" is located at: Vidnoye city, 2nd Kalinovsky passage.

Laundry and ironing services are available for a fee.

For accommodation, guests are offered the following room categories:
