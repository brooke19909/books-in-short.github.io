The invention of our days - portable air conditioning and smart bottle

Today, humanity and science are not standing still. The discoveries of scientists and the inventions of engineers convince us of this again and again. These people create incredible and amazing gizmos that shock us day after day. In this article, you will learn about the inventions of the 21st century, namely about the novelties of 2017.

Portable air conditioner

Despite the fact that Russia has winter and cold, in a few months the hot sun will burn, burning the pale-skinned bodies of Russians. Exhausting hell has not benefited anyone yet, but such an interesting invention of our days, like portable air conditioning Zero Breeze, will delight us in the warm season. This device can be taken with you wherever you want. It works on mains as well as on batteries. They charge only three to five hours.





The creators of this useful equipment equipped the air conditioner with a flashlight, which will be useful in the campaign. They built in the speaker necessary for listening to music, and also added a couple of USB sockets so that you can charge your gadget. Such an invention of our days, like air conditioning, has several modes of operation: intensive cooling, easy ventilation.

The device is made in two colors:

  • Orange;
  • blue.

Smart bottle

Another useful invention of our day for travelers is the Ecomo bottle. A filter is built into it, as well as an electronic controller that checks, disinfects and purifies water from bad impurities. As you might guess, this bottle is used to get the purest water from any natural source of water, whether it be a lake, river or stream.

Such an amazing invention of our days, like a bottle, is synchronized with gadgets (smartphones, etc.), and also comes with a smart capacity bracelet hydration sensor.





The device will start working immediately after you fill it with water and shake it. Water goes through three stages of purification:

  1. Chlorine, petroleum products, pesticides and pharmaceutical compounds will be removed with carbon fibers.
  2. Heavy metals are removed by ion exchange fibers.
  3. Bacteria and germs will be destroyed by nanofiber.

An invention of our day, like a smart bottle, is equipped with a battery for fast and wireless charging, which is carried out using a dock. One hour of charging provides up to seven days of operation of the smart bottle.

Parameters of a useful invention: bottle height - 25 cm; weight - about 570 grams; one filter works for 2-3 months.

The bluetooth version 4.0 LE is built into the case. The case of the smart bottle is available in several colors.

Conclusion
