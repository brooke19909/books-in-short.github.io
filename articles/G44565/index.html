Mini-hotel "Quiet corner", Vityazevo: overview, features and reviews of tourists

Vityazevo is a large resort village with many small private hotels, but Quiet Corner on Vinogradnaya has already found its fans and regular visitors. It is a modern three-story building with exquisite architecture in which your stay will be comfortable and memorable.





Description

Mini-Hotel Quiet Corner on Vinogradnaya has a beautiful location overlooking the Vityazevsky estuary. Tourists are delivered to the sea on the best sandy beach by air-conditioned minibus. The hotel is far from discos and attractions, so the nights here are calm and quiet. Dining at the hotel is not provided, but there is a shared kitchen in which there is all the necessary kitchen equipment so that you can warm yourself up or cook your own meals. In the courtyard of the hotel there is a large pool measuring 14 by 14 meters. Water in the pool is constantly purified using modern cleaning methods. The hotel has everything for a comfortable stay - comfortable sun loungers, a large beautiful gazebo, barbecue, tables with umbrellas, a children's playground. For vacationers with their own vehicles there is parking. In the evenings, parking is lit. Here you can have a wonderful rest at an affordable price. Check-in time is 14.00, check-out time is 12.00.





Infrastructure

From the guest house "Quiet Area" (Vityazevo), located in the most peaceful place of the resort village, you can take a minibus to the center of the village, where you can go to the market or take a walk in the park. There is a bus stop a three minute walk from the hotel. The interval between the minibuses is approximately 8-10 minutes. If you need to make purchases and or purchase medicines, grocery stores and pharmacies are located within a 5-8 minute walk from the hotel.





Accommodation

Guest House "Quiet Area" (Vityazevo) to accommodate guests offers several categories of rooms. In total there are 19 superior rooms

  • One-room "Junior Suite" with a double bed with 2-3 persons. Room rates, depending on the season, range from 1400 to 2200 rubles per day
  • One-room "Junior Suite" superior with a double bed and a folding armchair, designed for 3-4 people. The price for a room is from 1600 to 2400 rubles per day.
  • Two-room "Suite" with two double beds and a sofa bed, able to accommodate 5-6 people. Room rates from 2500 to 3800 rubles per day.

An extra bed is charged 300 rubles per night. An extra cot is available as an extra bed. Cots are available free of charge upon prior arrangement. For the reservation, a prepayment of 20% of the total cost of living is taken. The hotel rooms are strictly non-smoking. Pets are not allowed.





Room Amenities

All rooms are designed for the most comfortable stay of vacationers in the hotel "Quiet Area" (Vityazevo ). Each room has the following amenities:

  • own balcony;
  • a shower (in one of the Suite rooms, instead of a Jacuzzi bath shower);
  • restroom;
  • split system;
  • wireless Internet;
  • satellite television;
  • each room has a large mirror;
  • refrigerator (except for 2-3 local "junior suites").

All rooms are equipped with necessary furniture (bedside tables, wardrobes, chests of drawers).





Services

A quiet corner on Vinogradnaya (Vityazevo) provides a number of services for the convenience of guests.

  • A shuttle to the beach and back is provided twice a day. The bus ride to the beach takes 8-10 minutes. From the place of stop on foot go for another three minutes.
  • For cooking in the kitchen there are all necessary accessories.
  • For children there is a large inflatable trampoline.
  • If necessary, vacationers can use an ironing board and an iron.
  • It is possible to wash things in a washing machine. The cost of one bookmark is 50 rubles.
  • By prior request, a group of vacationers of at least ten people, hotel workers can take to a wild sandy beach with crystal clear sea.
  • For its guests , the Quiet Corner (Vityazevo) organizes a free meeting at Anapa stations and a meeting-off in the Tunnel (the cost of the service is 1000 rubles).

For regular guests there is a system of discounts.





sights

The Quiet Corner Guest House (Vityazevo) is not only a wonderful place for a relaxing and peaceful holiday away from the bustle of the city, but it is also close to local attractions that vacationers love to visit. Some of them can be noted.

  • The memorial dedicated to the founders of the village of Vityazevo, subsequently repressed in 1937-1939. Located at the Church of St. George the Victorious.
  • The memorial to the departed participants of the Great Patriotic War, who lived in the village.
  • The memorial to those who died in World War II (it is a wartime tank and a stele with the names of villagers who did not return from the war).

Almost all guests remain satisfied with their stay at the quiet corner on Vinogradnaya guest house, located near Anapa in the village of Vityazevo.

  • Of course, first of all, great reviews about friendly and attentive managers.

Anapa, Vityazevo "Quiet Area", negative reviews

The Hotel Quiet Corner (Vityazevo) has much less serious complaints from its guests, but the hotel administration needs to think about eliminating them.

  • A number of vacationers like to make noise in the yard in the evening. But since the territory of the hotel is compact, this creates certain inconvenience to those who go to bed earlier.
  • Some vacationers complained about the lack of a mosquito net on the windows, which they eventually did not provide. This is despite the fact that in the evenings there are a lot of mosquitoes.
  • There was a precedent when, having a preliminary agreement on preparing a room with a crib, the room was not ready for the arrival of guests. In the end they gave the crib, but they did not take out the extra single bed, so the room was very cramped.
  • Some consider it a drawback that the sea is far away, but there is a comfortable minibus for guests at the hotel.
