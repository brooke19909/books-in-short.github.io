San Marco Hotel 2 *, Dubai: hotel description, reviews

Dubai is an incredibly popular tourist city that is visited by hundreds of thousands of tourists every year. Naturally, in the city, as well as in its environs, many hotels of different categories have been built, ranging from luxury resort complexes to cozy guest houses. It is in this area that the San Marco Hotel 2 * hotel is located. So what is this place and what living conditions does the tourist offer?

It is worth saying right away that this hotel is remote from the beach, which is about 6 km away. By the way, the hotel provides its guests with transfers to the airport and the seashore, you should not worry about this.

What does the hotel look like? Brief infrastructure description

San Marco Hotel 2 * - city hotel. It is a large multi-story building, built in a modern style. The hotel does not have its own courtyard, however, inside the building there is everything you need for a comfortable fun holiday. By the way, elevators are constantly working in the building, so do not worry, even if you are settled on the top floor. There are several parking spaces near the hotel. Around it are other buildings, including some hotels. The road, and therefore the public transport stop, is close. By the way, the hotel was opened in 2005, and in 2014 a radical reconstruction was carried out on its territory.

Hotel San Marco Hotel 2 * (Dubai, Deira): description and photos of rooms





This is a medium sized hotel with 120 rooms. 112 of them are standard rooms. There are 8 luxury rooms. According to reviews, all rooms are spacious, the design is beautiful and elegant. 70 rooms have access to a private balcony with views of the street and surroundings.

Naturally, the rooms are equipped with the necessary amount of household appliances, without which it is difficult to imagine real comfort. For example, the room temperature can be controlled using an air conditioning system. There is a TV on which you can watch satellite channels (there are 4 Russian). A small safe with an electric lock stores documents and small valuables. Upon request, guests are provided with free iron and ironing board. There is an electric kettle and kitchenware for making hot drinks. The minibar is filled on demand and its contents are paid separately.

The room has a bathroom with tiled floors, a shower and modern fixtures. There is a wash basin, a large dressing mirror, a hair dryer, as well as soap, shampoos and other hygiene products. Of course, each tenant is given a set of clean towels, which are changed daily during housekeeping. Room service is available around the clock, but it is a paid service. By the way, the reviews indicate that the maids are doing their job perfectly - the rooms are always clean and fresh.

Meals: what does the guest offer?





Buying holiday packages in the UAE, travelers can independently choose a convenient meal plan. For example, you can pay only breakfast, a two-time meal, or even refuse such a service and eat in other institutions of the city or pay separately for each lunch and dinner in a hotel restaurant.

What should the guests of Sac Marco Hotel 2 * count on? The reviews indicate that the menu here is quite diverse - European, Asian and Arabic dishes are served at the table, so there will be plenty to choose from. A special delight among tourists is the local sweet pastries, which will delight any gourmet. Naturally, there are many cafes, pizzerias and luxurious restaurants in the city where you can eat and have a good time, so food problems rarely occur. By the way, there is a supermarket nearby where you can buy groceries and even some prepared meals.

Where is the beach? Water activities

What rest should the guests of the hotel San Marco Hotel 2 * count on? Dubai is a city famous for its luxurious resorts, giant shopping centers and, of course, picturesque beaches. It is worth saying right away that the beach is located quite far away - the distance to it is about 6 km. You can get there by taxi or use public transport. And several times a day, a free bus leaves the hotel from the hotel, which takes tourists to the seashore.

The beach is public, but the sea is very clean. For a small fee, you can rent a comfortable sun lounger and umbrella. Of course, tourists can count on almost all types of water recreation, including snorkeling, boating, boats, yachts, catamarans, water skiing, as well as parasailing, windsurfing and sailing.

Additional services





Hotel guests are offered a number of additional services that are designed to make their life more comfortable. For example, dry cleaning and laundry services are constantly available here. If necessary, you can rent a safe from the administrator. Near the hotel there is a small parking area, which guests can use for free. By the way, the staff will help you to quickly and easily rent suitable vehicles, which will make moving around the city more convenient. The hotel has a medical center, and in case of health problems you can count on the help of a qualified doctor. Internet access is available in public areas.

The hotel offers a business center. From here you can send or receive a fax, use a printer or other office equipment, free access to the Internet. There is a small post office. There is no conference room, but there is a small office for 10-15 people - it is usually used for business meetings and meetings. Businessmen can expect a comfortable stay at San Marco Hotel 2 *. Testimonials indicate that employees are excellent at organizing and conducting various events.

How do guests have fun? Leisure on site





The list of entertainment that this hotel offers is not too large. Nevertheless, it is quite possible to have a good time. On one floor there is an outdoor terrace with a pool where you can swim, refresh yourself, sunbathe and just relax, chatting with friends. Of course, sunbeds and umbrellas are placed around.

The hotel also has a large gym with modern equipment, which is popular with guests. Nearby is a sauna where you can steam up your muscles before a workout or relax after it. The hotel offers a tour desk service - here they will tell you about the most interesting places in the city and its environs, help you plan a route for a small independent trip or purchase a ready-made tour, thanks to which you can learn more about the country, its history and traditions.

What type of vacation is the hotel suitable for?





As already mentioned, San Marco Hotel 2 * is a city-style hotel. Tourists who come to the city for business purposes often stop here, because the hotel has the necessary conditions for this. People come here for the purpose of shopping. By the way, in the hotel you can even find experts who will help with shopping, tell you exactly where and what is better to buy. The hotel is also suitable for beach holidays, although the seashore is still far away. But families with children here may not be too much fun.
