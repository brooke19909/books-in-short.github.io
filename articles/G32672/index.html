Sberbank ATM - how to use?

Each holder of a plastic bank card will have to use an ATM sooner or later. Even to withdraw money or just to see how much money is in the account. Let's talk in this article about Sberbank ATMs.



What do I need to use a Sberbank ATM?

First of all, a plastic card. If you have a card from another bank, then prepare in advance for the fact that even for a transaction to request a balance you will be charged a commission. For those who have a Sberbank card, the service will be free.

How to start using?

In order to start using a Sberbank ATM, you need to insert a card into the hole. It must be inserted correctly, otherwise the device will not work with it. It is issued upon receipt of the card in a special envelope, and it consists of four numbers. Cash withdrawal at Sberbank ATMs



The ATM has a button "Get cash" in the main menu. When you click on this button, you will be offered several options for choosing, that is, specific amounts, or you can go to the "Get a different amount" section and enter as many as you need. Do not forget that at ATMs you can withdraw no more than forty bills at a time. The buttons that a Sberbank ATM has may differ in name, because if the ATM has a function of accepting cash, then the reception and issuance are combined in one button. Also, before giving out money, the ATM always asks whether you need a check or not. If the check is needed, you must click "Print check", if not, then, respectively, "Do not print the check."
