Hurghada, "Sultan Beach" - accessibility and comfort!

Sultan Beach Hotel (Hurghada), the photo of which you will see below, is built on the beautiful Red Sea coast. The complex was opened in 1998 and was renovated in 2008. Since the hotel offers a huge number of services, it is ideal for active and fun holidays for both a youth company and families with children.





Hurghada, Sultan Beach. Accommodation

The hotel has 328 rooms. All of them are comfortable, stylish and meet all European standards. Each of them is furnished with modern comfortable furniture and equipped with plumbing. Room categories:

  1. Standard (252) with a view of the courtyard (a maximum of three people can live); on the sea or in the pool (designed for two adults and two children).
  2. Studio
  3. Villas

Standard rooms are cleaned daily, as is a change of linen, but on request. A hairdryer is available in some rooms or at the reception (no extra charge). A stocked minibar is available for a fee. All rooms are equipped with air conditioning, TV (satellite signal, there are Russian channels). The hotel does not allow pets. Accepted credit cards Visa, MasterCard, American Express.

Hurghada, Sultan Beach. Nutrition



Restaurant La Palma operates on a buffet system and offers vacationers to try the delicious dishes of international cuisine. Capacity - 450 seats. Another equally high-quality restaurant is La nona (a la carte). It can accommodate a maximum of 70 visitors and is open from 11:00 in the morning until 23:00 in the evening. The hotel has a stylish lobby bar. Vacationers will be offered a choice of various alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks, cocktails, as well as snacks. The bar is open 24 hours a day. In addition, the hotel has an excellent Tati's Pub Bar, a poolside bar and a beachfront bar.

Hurghada, "Sultan Beach": entertainment and sports



The hotel offers its vacationers an interesting time playing a game on the indoor soccer field, during a volleyball match on the beach or water polo. Guests can attend classes in water aerobics, aerobics, play darts, table tennis. The list of paid services includes: billiards, gym, sauna, windsurfing, canoeing, banana riding, jacuzzi, tennis court, camel riding, diving, massage, rental of balls and tennis rackets.

Hurghada, Sultan Beach: service

For a fee, guests can use the services of a hairdresser, doctor, laundry and Wi-Fi in the lobby. The complex also has a conference room with a maximum capacity of 100 people. Children from 4 to 10 years old can visit the kids club, spend time with peers at the playground, as well as splash around in the children's section of the adult pool, which is heated in the winter. The smallest visitors to the restaurant will be offered highchairs, and if necessary, a cot will be delivered to the rooms absolutely free. The beach of the hotel is a small lagoon, where sand and pebbles are mixed. It is recommended to bring special shoes with you. At the coast you can use umbrellas, mattresses and sun loungers, towels for free. Have a pleasant and memorable vacation!
