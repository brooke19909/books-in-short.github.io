Rating of Russian insurance companies: company overview, working conditions, customer reviews

The economic situation prevailing on the current date in Russia requires organizations and citizens to take care of the safety of their property. Consider the largest insurance companies in Russia, the rating, as well as the benefits that they provide.

Reliability criteria

The structure of the insurance portfolio of the company, its financial condition - these are two main factors. They determine solvency. The rating of insurance companies in Russia is built on the same principle - each organization is assigned a number on a reliability scale. For instance:

  1. A ++ - a high level of reliability. This means that insurance pays for obligations in full, even with market fluctuations. There are varieties of A-ratings - AA, AAA - this indicates stability at the international level.
  2. B ++ is an average level of reliability. In this case, the company makes guaranteed payments only provided that the market is stable at the maturity date of the obligation.
  3. C ++ is a low level at which the insurance organization cannot fulfill even its current obligations.
  4. E - license revocation.

Largest in the market

The rating of Russian insurance companies is as follows:

  1. SOGAZ. The insurance group has been operating in Russia since 1993. Leading international experts have confirmed the high reliability and solvency of this company.
  2. SPAO Ingosstrakh. This company has existed on the market since 1947. Initially, the insurance company was part of the USSR Ministry of Finance, later it acquired the status of an independent legal entity. Refers to systemically important insurance organizations - participates in the control of the insurance market and carries out operations in the international financial market.
  3. "VTB-Insurance" - a large insurance company operating since 2000, the existing name in use since 2008. It is part of the All-Russian Union of Insurers, has a rating of A ++.
  4. Sberbank Insurance, a subsidiary of Sberbank, has been operating in the market since 2014. The reliability of the company is evaluated by international agencies at the level of AA +; in forecasts of further development, they are classified as financially favorable.
  5. AlfaStrakhovanie, an authoritative publication of Forbes, estimates this company as the largest on the market in Russia. Founded in 1992, is a member of the Alfa Group Consortium.

The rating of insurance companies in Russia may vary depending on the size of the financial portfolio and the universality of the services provided. When deciding on the choice of an organization, it is better to familiarize yourself with the current tariffs and conditions provided by each of them in advance.

Tariff spread

The rating of the largest insurance companies in Russia contains a list of names of organizations whose price range for services may vary up to 20%. This is due to the fact that there is an approved tariff corridor within which a company can set tariffs.

Regardless of the status of the company, it is important to remember that you can refuse the additional services that the manager proposes to include in the policy.

Study of information about the duration of the company in the Russian market will help determine the choice of insurance.
