Simple rules

First of all, I want to say that the clown costume itself should be very bright and colorful. After all, the task of this hero is to amuse others. Therefore, the brightest fabrics that you want can be used. However, most often, when sewing a clown costume with their own hands, red, yellow, green and blue pieces are used.

Head

We will go from the top to the very bottom of the child, that is, at the very beginning we will figure out how to decorate the baby‚Äôs head. It can be a beret hat with a big pompom, it can be a fun hat, an option for a girl is thick curls that can be decorated with a huge bow. Alternatively, you can put a beautiful bright wig on your child‚Äôs head. I would like to tell a little how to make a hat cap. To do this, you need colored cardboard, from which, according to the principle of a bag for seeds, a hat is folded. Everything inside is fixed with adhesive tape, then the bottom is cut so that the cap is even. It is necessary to attach an elastic band to it (so the cap will not fall from the child‚Äôs head). You can decorate the product at the bottom with rain, if the holiday is not New Year's - corrugated paper cut all around the edge. If desired, the cap can be painted on or made certain applications on it. When creating a clown costume with your own hands, it is important not to forget about makeup. So, the kid can draw a big smile (more modestly - put red dots on the cheeks and nose). Also, a soft ball can be put on the child‚Äôs nose, which will give an image of gaiety.

clothing

When making a clown costume with your own hands, special attention should be paid to the main clothes in which the child will be dressed. So, if this is a boy, you can put on him a bright-colored T-shirt, bright pants - this may be enough. However, it would be nice to also decorate such a simple suit. To do this, you can attach something to the panties or shorts by the type of suspender Carlson wore (a piece of fabric that will stretch on the left side of the front and end on the right side of the back). Such a holder can be attached with beautiful large buttons. The suit will also be decorated with colored rags of fabric with which you can sew pockets.

If you need a girl ‚Äôs children's clown costume , you can make your daughter a beautiful bright skirt with a magnificent petticoat. To do this, you need a bright fabric to the very top, as well as something to create the lower fluffy skirts. This can be, for example, a wardrobe organza or ordinary thin mesh fabric. Such a petticoat is best done with an elastic band. First, four circles of the hyoid tissue of the desired length are cut out according to the size of the girl‚Äôs waist. All pieces of fabric are sewn to the sides, assembly is done from above, everything is sewn to the elastic band - and that's it. A simple skirt is put on top (it can also be sewn). This outfit goes well with a bright T-shirt.

Legs

What else do you need to provide for a clown costume with your own hands? So, if this is a girl, she definitely needs to wear beautiful bright tights. They can be striped or monochrome - it doesn‚Äôt matter, the main thing is that the legs should not be lost against the background of the whole costume. Foot

It is also important to choose the right shoes. If the performance will be held in a kindergarten, the baby is better to wear simple slippers or Czech shoes, but they must first be decorated. In this case, pompons are useful, which you just need to attach to the top of each slipper. You can make them pretty simple. Two circles with holes should be cut out of cardboard (for example, a circle with a diameter of 8 cm, a hole - 3 cm). A thread is tightly wound around this circle (for knitting), the tip is well fixed. Further along the end of the circle, the threads are carefully cut, the auxiliary cardboard parts are removed, the pompom is beautifully aligned. That's all.

Accessories
