New Athos, Abkhazia is a private sector. Abkhazia - rest in New Athos

The country of truly amazing nature, the bright sun and the gentle sea, an original and ancient culture, located in the northwestern part of the Caucasus near the Black Sea itself is Abkhazia. Rest in New Athos, Gagra, Sukhum or Pitsunda since the Soviet era was considered elite. And although today it is accessible to many, nevertheless, those who understand a lot about swimming, beaches and good service come here.



New Athos - nature

This wonderful Abkhaz resort is located twenty kilometers from Sukhum. Those who fly to the city by plane can see the spurs of the Caucasus Range. They are called the New Athos Mountains, and they look like protrusions, in Abkhazian - anacopes. Hence the name of this medieval capital.

However, the pride of the resort is not only the mountains, but also its nature in general. The beauty of the landscapes surrounding New Athos is complemented by rich subtropical vegetation. Quite green hills, on which the resort is located, are lemon, orange, tangerine and even olive groves. Thick cypress alleys can be found throughout New Athos, and palm trees and laurels, magnolias and oleanders, eucalyptus and even bananas flaunt in its coastal zone.

And among all this splendor, a few minutes walk from the seashore, hotels and pensions are located. New Athos, Abkhazia have changed a lot since Soviet times, today they simply cannot be recognized.





The climatic conditions of this very small resort are extremely favorable. From the north, the town is protected by part of the Caucasus Range. The average annual temperature of the region varies within fifteen degrees, while the heat is not felt at all due to the presence of constant sea breezes.

New Athos Hotels

Abkhazia is famous for its many hotels. But there are no very large hotels. These are mainly compact mini-hotels or guest houses. Which is not surprising if we recall the political and economic situation in which Abkhazia found itself in connection with the latest events. New Athos, the price of accommodation in which is not lower than on the Sochi coast, is no exception in this regard. Nevertheless, it is quite in demand among our tourists.



The conditions at the hotels in this resort are very different, so before booking it is important to carefully study all the offers. New Athos hotels operate throughout the year, while in the winter season prices fall almost twice.

Very popular hotel "Swan", located by the park, a three-minute walk from the sea. Judging by the reviews, everyone who stayed in it was satisfied not only with the proposed service, but also with the location and food.

Other hotels, for example, Anacopia Club, Kumarit, Griffon, etc., offer a fairly high level of service, a lot of entertainment and great beaches.



Sanatoriums and pensions

Abkhazia is famous for its sanatoriums and rest homes. There are a lot of them. Hotels of New Athos and private mini-hotels are mostly located on the first line. Some have their own beaches. Mostly they are pebble, with breakwaters. The arrangement of the beaches is quite modern. There is a rental of various equipment, umbrellas, sunbeds, deck chairs. Most of the beaches are closed, therefore they are always kept in an exceptionally clean state.

Sanatoria in New Athos, as well as inexpensive private mini-hotels, are distinguished by a quite acceptable level of service, combined with true Caucasian hospitality. New Athos Infrastructure

The city has two markets, many convenience stores, a city hospital, a post office, restaurants, cafes, discos, and souvenir stalls.

Those who come to the resort must see the ancient monastery, cave, waterfall, Anakopia and the restored Iversky fortress, which New Athos (Abkhazia) is so famous for.



Private sector

There are no multi-storey buildings in the historic city center. There are only low private houses. They are only in the trading part of the resort, separated from the historical district by a long avenue planted with palm trees, cypress trees and an oleander. This is the main place in the city for the promenade. There are also many small seaside cafes and restaurants.



You can relax as a savage in this region by renting a house or a room not only in the city center, but also in nearby villages, which are notable for low prices. This economy class accommodation is best suited for couples with young children. There is no need to adapt to the mode of a boarding house or hotel, it is possible to cook food yourself.

It is known that in the "high" season in many resort cities it is difficult to find free places in hotels or pensions. New Athos is no exception. Abkhazia, whose private sector is very diverse, belongs to the middle price segment. Many homeowners, whose houses are located by the sea, offer for living both separate or adjacent rooms, as well as entire cottages. As a rule, they all have a private household, a bath, and air conditioning.
