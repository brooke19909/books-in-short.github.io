SBiS ++: reporting has become faster.

The modern system of electronic reporting SBiS ++ is a single, universal system that includes all levels of reporting, preparation, verification and analysis of any workflow. Using this system, you can report to all bodies controlling the business process. In addition, SBiS ++ allows you to exchange data between company employees, between various agencies, departments and organizations, without fear for unauthorized use of important data.

The SBiS system operates in the so-called single window. Online Reporting It became possible simultaneously with the pension fund, with the tax service, with the Social Insurance Fund, Rosstat, with participants in other budget processes and with various contractors. Applications of the USRLE and ION services allow you to expand the capabilities of an accountant.

Working with the system is as simple as possible. Everyone can deal with it. The taxpayer, choosing the appropriate department and the necessary form of the report, fills in all the proposed lines in SBiS ++. After this, the report must be sent for signature by EDS. The next step is to encrypt the report and send it to the service provider. The special operator is already sending the report to the supervisory authority. Completes the process - confirmation from the inspection that the report has been submitted.

Consider a step-by-step instruction on how reporting is submitted, using the example of a tax return. Before you start reporting, make sure that you have chosen the right taxpayer for whom you want to submit a report. Next, go to the registry of the Federal Tax Service, choosing from the list that is offered to you the month and year that is needed.
