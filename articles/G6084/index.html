What is it?

Before you deal with some models of this device, it is worth explaining what it is for those who do not know. The digital-to-analog converter is designed to process a digital code into an analog signal. So, the device can be considered a kind of "conductor" from one world - digital, in another - analog.

Classification

Depending on the purpose, it is possible to distribute the DACs into groups. The simplest of all is considered to be pulse-width. Most often found in devices such as Hi-Fi or where you need to control electric motors. The principle of operation of this type is a pulse sequence that passes through a low-pass filter. To get it, you need to wait until the converted digital code is proportional to the time for which a stable current source works.





Further we meet oversampling. It is based on fluctuations in the value of the pulse density. This type is designed for the correct operation of a device that has a lower capacity, with a device with a higher capacity. It is believed that such a DAC is visualized in a single-bit model. So, according to this technology, momentum exchange occurs with negative feedbacks.

The weighting type assumes that the value of the current source will be proportional to the bit weight. Nonzero bits in this case can be summed. The staircase type received a special circuit, which consists of several resistors, has two types of inclusion.

Specifications

To choose the best DAC, you need to understand its characteristics. So, a couple of key parameters should be highlighted. It is important to understand the meaning of bit depth, maximum sampling rate, monotony, dynamic range, static and frequency characteristics. So it is possible to determine the desired levels of the output signal, the frequency of stable operation, the ability of the device to increase the analog output signal.
