Excursions

Xeno Hotel Alpina is a worthy launching pad for excursionists. A good level of four-star economy service is the base, and then vacationers will have to do creative work - choose exciting objects for themselves to visit and contemplate. Of course, a special bonus is the proximity of the city of Alanya.

Corakesion (as the city was called in ancient times) was a Mediterranean pirate base. In Rome, the corsairs were defeated by Pompey, and the city itself became part of Rome. Later, the triumvir Mark Anthony presented it to his wife Cleopatra. Sounds romantic: "Alanya - a city - a gift ..."

Tourists should not buy excursions from the guides of Xeno Hotel Alpina 4. After all, this category of hotel personnel is forced, according to official duties, to sell excursions at double prices. It is more reasonable for excursionists to purchase excursions at specialized excursion agencies in Alanya.

The following excursions are especially popular: Pamukkale, Mira-Kekova-Demre, Cappadocia, Dalmatash grotto, Side excavations, Geynuk canyon, Ich-Kale fortress. Let's talk about some of them.

Briefly about excursions to Mira-Kekova-Demre



The excursion to Mira-Kekova-Demre is perhaps the most popular. After all, a great man lived and preached in Demre - the canonized bishop of this city, known to us as Nicholas the Wonderworker. Until our time, there is a church where he once ruled the service. Sailing on a yacht to the island of Kekova, vacationers can see the remains of the Hellenistic city, unfortunately, half flooded by the sea after the earthquake. In the World, the remains of the ancient agora (city center) with an amphitheater and preserved foundations and fragments of public buildings are opened to the gaze of those who have fallen.

Cappadocia

Freakish creations of nature ... What made the rocks turn into numerous freakish conical inclined objects? To contemplate them, hundreds of thousands of tourists a year come to Cappadocia. Also, local residents of these places keep the original traditions of arranging their homes. From the beginning of our era here, in the rocks, the first Christians carved their underground temples. The fruits of their titanic labor can be seen today. However, many modern residents of Cappadocia and now live in houses partially built, and partially carved in the rocks.

Grotto Dalmatash



Created by nature 15 thousand years ago, decorated with stalactites and stalagmites, it is a gorgeous sight. A huge cave is located below sea level. At the same time, a stable temperature of + 22C 0 is maintained in it all year round. It was first used as a secret hideout by corsairs. As an excursion object, this underground cavity was discovered by chance in the middle of the last century.

The spectacle of a stalactite cave illuminated by accent lighting is impressive.

Ich-Kale Fortress

A large sightseeing object, built on the land of the Land of the Stars and the Crescent, at a time when the state - Byzantium existed here. That Byzantium, which once brought the Orthodox Christian faith as a gift to Russia.

Ich-kale is well preserved. To explore this huge stronghold, a one-day excursion is organized for excursionists.
