Rixos Sharm El Sheikh Hotel for a Carefree Holiday

Coming out of an airplane in Egypt, guests of the country see a deserted, completely uninhabited landscape, somewhat similar to the moonlight. Not a bird, not a blade of grass, not a bush. Only a smooth 2-lane road recalls the existence of nearby people.

First impressions

But after driving just a few minutes from the airport, you find yourself in an oasis. And here is the Rixos Sharm El Sheikh Hotel. Tall palm trees, fountains, lush green grass, shrubs. Everything is trimmed and well-groomed. Rixos Sharm El Sheikh is a 5-star family-run hotel conveniently located in Sharm El Sheikh, in the Gulf of Aqaba, on the beautiful coast of Nabq Bay. The complex is a series of two-story buildings built in 2009. This is an amazing place full of warmth and charm, combining the flavor of the country and modern comfort.



In the main building in a large marble hall with soft armchairs and sofas, friendly young people who know Russian quite well meet their guests. Rixos Sharm El Sheikh Hotel offers its guests 695 comfortable rooms of various categories: from Superior (37 sq. M.) To Junior suite with private pool (110 sq. M.). The elegantly furnished rooms feature wired internet, a bathroom, toiletries, hairdryer, telephone, modern TV, safe, terrace or balcony.



Sea, diving and more

The territory is simply huge - 153 thousand square meters. m, renovated in 2012. There are six pools, three water slides, cleanliness, greens, smiling service staff. The beach is well maintained, sunbeds, umbrellas, mattresses, towels are free. The pier is 147 m long, its reef. And, of course, the warm Red Sea. Water emerald amazing color. There is no silt at all. Only corals and fish of bizarre shapes and colors. Fishes are teeming underfoot, not afraid. The bottom of the coast is not very good, you need shoes. Diving from the pontoon, you plunge into the amazing underwater world. No wonder the local diving is recognized as the best in the world.

Gourmet Pleasures

The Rixos Sharm El Sheikh Hotel presents a wide range of dishes in its many restaurants and bars. Chefs with international awards will indulge guests with culinary delights. The two main restaurants serve traditional and modern dishes. Italian offers typical salads, pizza and pasta with new high-quality ingredients. The Chinese restaurant with its ingenious show tricks and a truly Chinese atmosphere will not leave a single guest bored. Oriental, Japanese, sea, Indian - this is not a complete list of establishments represented in the hotel. Each tourist will be able to find their favorite dishes and taste the exotic exotic for themselves.

Having decided to rest and relax in Egypt, you can safely choose the hotel "Rixos Sharm El Sheikh." Prices will pleasantly surprise any tourist. In the amount of 1.5-2 thousand dollars it is quite possible to relax with your family, gain strength and chic impressions.
