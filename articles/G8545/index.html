Life Green Hill Hotel - relaxation on the green slopes of the Mediterranean coast

Description: On the Turkish coast of the Mediterranean, in the vicinity of the resort town of Alanya, the hotel complex Life Green Hill Hotel is located. Residential buildings of buildings are located on a high slope above the sea amidst an abundance of green trees. A cable car is intended for descent to the beach area. For

lovers of hiking - numerous steps of the stairs. This hotel is designed for beach activities.

The distance of the Life Green Hill Hotel from Antalya International Airlines is 115 km, from the central city of Alanya - 10.1 km, from the neighboring village of Konakli - 2.05 km. Tourists can visit the Damlatas cave, visit the ruins of the ancient cities of Justiniano on the Karaburun and Anamur peninsulas, see the medieval fortresses of Alanya and Kyzyl Kul, as well as visit many entertainment centers in the evening in the neighboring city.



Rooms: For guests, the Life Green Hill Hotel (Turkey) offers comfortable apartments with a total of 331. Among them, there are 166 standard rooms and 165 suites. All rooms have picturesque views of the sea coast and green areas. The hotel rooms are equipped with a telephone, split system air conditioning, a small TV, a safe, upholstered furniture, a wardrobe, a minibar and a mirror. The rooms have satellite communications and a public telephone line. The bathroom is equipped with a hairdryer, shower, washing facilities and terry towels. Daily housekeeping staff performs cleaning according to the schedule, and on request changes bed linen.



Beach: The hotel is located at a distance of 151 meters from the sea. Life Green Hill Hotel beach is sandy, the entrance to the sea water is flat and covered with pebbles. There is a bar on the beach. For spa guests, mattresses, sun loungers and umbrellas are provided free of charge. For a fee, you can rent a beach towel. In addition to the beach, the hotel has its own pool, near which beach accessories are provided free of charge. In a small water park in the open-air pool, two slides are installed.

Meals: Restaurant staff feed vacationers on an all-inclusive basis. Three meals a day "buffet" consists of dishes of Turkish local and European cuisines. During the day, local drinks in four bars are provided free of charge.

Information for vacationers: On the territory of the hotel complex Life Green Hill Hotel for the convenience of guests there are shopping shops with Turkish souvenirs, a gym for physical activity, a sauna, massage and medical rooms, a Jacuzzi bath, dry cleaning and laundry. Reviews: About Life Green Hill Hotel, resort reviews are positive and negative. There were cases of theft on the part of the attendants, the equipment did not work in the rooms. People who had health problems did not like the long descent to the beach and back. The entertainment animation program for adults is rather weak, but children's entertainment is very well organized. Regarding food and service, diametrically opposed reviews are found (from harsh criticism to enthusiastic praise). But in general, the surrounding atmosphere is favorable, the territory is beautiful, the climate is wonderful, the sea is warm. The hotel is well suited to a budget vacation option.
