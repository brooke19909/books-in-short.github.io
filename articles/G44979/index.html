How to knit men's socks with knitting needles? Schemes, description, detailed instructions

Warm socks will always come in handy. Perhaps your man is a fisherman, likes to sit with a fishing rod in the frosty winter? Then he just needs a heater for the legs. If he likes to go to the country in the cold season, then woolen socks will come in handy here. Socks made of warm, thin yarn can be worn under winter boots or worn at home when it's not hot. If you know how to knit men's socks with knitting needles, then you can create several products with your own hands and give them to your relatives or narrowed ones.





Preparatory process

That's just unlikely to make a surprise of this. After all, the sock must be periodically tried on the one for whom it fits. If there is another sock that is fit for a man, then you can take it as a reference and compare a new product with it. This is what you need to do when you knit men's socks with knitting needles.

Before you start the process, buy everything you need. If you already have a set of five spokes, then fine. If not yet, get it, and along with yarn. She will need 350-400 grams. The weakest in socks is the heels. They are wiped the fastest. Therefore, they still buy yarn called "sock additive." She will help things not to wear out for a long time.

Start knitting

Now you can begin the process of creation and begin to knit men's socks. First, we knit a rectangle, which consists of 20 loops and has 10 rows. This will be a sample. It will help to calculate the exact number of loops that will need to be dialed for the main product. Their total number should be divided by 4. Let's say the calculations showed that you need to dial 50 loops. Add 2 more to make 52. Then we distribute them equally into 4 knitting needles - 13 stitches each. First, we type 26 into 2 paired, then - the same amount to the other 2. We begin to knit the next row with the fifth spoke. In the process, gradually sort 13 loops into each of the four knitting needles.

The first rows must be knitted with an elastic band, and quite a lot so that the sock grabs the legs well and does not lower during movement. The elastic, as usual, consists of one or two alternating loops: wrong, front, etc. The product should always be on the face, as we knit in a circle.

With or without ornament?





If you want a man to flaunt in a product with a pattern, then you need to know how to make such men's socks. With knitting needles and threads of different colors, it is easy to knit a strip with a pattern. You can simply alternate in a checkerboard pattern the thread of light and dark color. The resulting squares. If you want to pleasantly surprise a man, you can create a real Norwegian ornament on the top of the socks after the elastic . Let deer jump there or fall bizarre snowflakes. It is easy to knit such socks with men's knitting needles. Schemes will help not to stray from the right path and skillfully alternate the colors of the threads so that the result is a beautiful ornament.

If you want something simpler, then knit 2 rows with a blue thread, 2 - white or combine other colors. Then the socks will also be elegant. In any case, the knitting of the ornament needs to be finished 3-4 cm to the heel and you can begin to form it.





We knit men's socks with knitting needles: a description of the creation of the heel part

Now, loops the two knitting needles with which the front part of the sock was knitted, we put it on one side and until we touch it. And the loops of the other two knitting needles should be distributed by 3. In our case, this is 26 loops. Without a remainder, they are not divisible by 3. So, the two extreme parts of the heel will consist of 9 loops (each), and the middle - reed - of 8. We knit only it. When we get to its last loop, we knit that one together with the first one, on which 9 loops of the heel side are still located. In order to make the closing line smooth, the last loop of the reed part is turned over before knitting with another. Previously, her right half was in front, and we remove and place it on the spoke so that its right side is in front. If this is not done, then the calcaneal suture will have holes and nodules.

After we get a kind of doll cap - heel, we collect as many air loops on both sides of it so that their number returns to its original level. In our case, it is only 52 loops. So, we collect 9 loops on these lateral parts of the heel and knit the fabric further, to the little finger.





Just a little bit left

Now it would be good to measure the product on the leg for which it is created. You can simply determine how many centimeters are in the foot, and see where exactly you need to start reducing loops. Usually? start doing it near the little finger. We knit the last 2 loops together at the end of each of the four knitting needles. Do not forget to flip one of the two loops as described above. Then you get a smooth line. The last 8 stitches can be closed immediately or the last 2 can be closed. Insert the main thread into the remaining one stitch and tighten it well. We pull the thread on the wrong side and cut it, leaving 2 cm.
