Hotel "Olymp", Kazan: description, location, rooms and reviews

If you like to be in an atmosphere of beauty and comfort, then you should definitely visit the Olympus Hotel. Kazan is loved by both tourists and business people, and this hotel will provide them with comfortable accommodation.





Brief description of the hotel

If you want to plunge into the atmosphere of luxury and mentally transport yourself into the atmosphere of Ancient Greece, then you should definitely choose an option such as the Olympus Hotel. Kazan is characterized by architectural diversity, and this institution will give the ensemble a touch of modernity. The interior is closer to the classical style.

Hotel "Olympus" (Kazan) offers you a choice of several categories of rooms, each of which will delight you with a cozy design and a high level of comfort. Here you will find such necessary amenities as air conditioning, a TV, a fridge and a private bathroom. Some apartments also have private balconies.

You can not worry about delicious and quality food, if you opt for an institution such as the Olympus Hotel. Kazan has a huge number of catering establishments, but hotel guests will not have to go far. Every morning a delicious breakfast is served in the restaurant, and the menu includes both Russian and traditional Tatar cuisine.





Hotel Olympus (Kazan): address

The hotel is located on Richard Sorge Street, 66, in the southern part of the city. Within walking distance is the metro station, from where you can quickly get to anywhere in Kazan. From here to the international airport you need to drive no more than 20 minutes. In the immediate vicinity of the hotel is the shopping and entertainment complex of the same name, as well as a large number of cafes, restaurants and shops.

Guest apartments

Olimp Hotel (Kazan, Russia) offers its guests accommodation in the following room categories:

  • Single standard room offers comfortable accommodation on one and a half orthopedic beds. Thanks to the large windows, a lot of light penetrates the room, which visually expands the space. Business people will appreciate the availability of a desktop desk.
  • Improved modification of a single room implies not only an expanded area, but also the presence of its own French balcony.
  • Double rooms can offer you accommodation in one and a half or one double bed with orthopedic mattress. The apartments are characterized by a large area, as well as light colors in the design, which also visually expands the space.
  • Suites are the most luxurious apartments this hotel can offer you. There are two rooms here. The bedroom has a large bed and access to a private balcony. The living room has upholstered furniture, a modern TV and many decorative elements in a classic style.




Facilities at the guest apartment

Many guests of the city choose the Olimp institution (Kazan, hotel) for their accommodation. Hotel Kazan offers its guests to use the following amenities in the apartment:

  • a landline phone will allow you, without leaving your room, to contact the reception or make an order from a restaurant, as well as make a call to another city;
  • a widescreen TV broadcasts a wide package of cable channels, and also provides the ability to play video from external media;
  • individual air conditioning, thanks to which the microclimate in your room will not depend on the weather outside;
  • In the bathroom, in addition to bath and cosmetic accessories, there is also an electric hairdryer.

Useful information for guests

  • check-in of newly arrived guests begins after two in the afternoon, and you need to leave your room strictly before noon (2 hours are given to employees to put the rooms in order);

General impression

Hotel "Olympus" is a rather attractive accommodation option in the city of Kazan. Despite the fact that it is somewhat remote from the central part of the city, there is a fairly good traffic intersection, and therefore it will not be difficult for you to get to any point you need. In addition, this institution is located near the airport.

It is worth noting that the hotel building is designed in a modern architectural style. The interior is closer to classical conciseness. This applies to both rooms and other rooms. The apartments are freshly renovated and new furniture. The atmosphere of homeliness is created by modern amenities.

As for the restaurant, opinions about it are rather mixed. If you want to try the dish from the menu, you will be pleasantly surprised by the ratio of high quality and reasonable price. Tatar cuisine deserves special attention. If we talk about breakfast, then they are a big disappointment. Not only is the choice of dishes very scarce, but also the amount of food is incredibly small, but because all the guests are simply not enough. That is why tourists do not recommend paying for breakfast (it is better to go to neighboring cafes or restaurants, where for a little money you can eat tasty and satisfying).
