Taba Hotels Prepare For You First-Class Vacation!

Taba is considered the best resort in Egypt. Previously, Egypt and Israel argued over this territory, to which Taba is very close. But for more than twenty years, it belongs to the country of the pyramids.


One of the main advantages of Taba is the extraordinary beauty of the local landscape. Golden beaches, impressive mountains, lagoons, grottoes, palm oases and other virtues of nature that fill this resort captivate vacationers with their splendor.


Taba is located near the international airport, so the path from it to the picturesque place takes very little time.


Hotels of Taba, scattered along the coast, against the backdrop of iridescent granite mountains look simply magnificent. They pleasantly surprise holidaymakers with excellent seafood, dishes of different nations and local drinks.


The main attraction of Taba is the island of the Pharaohs. All visiting tourists are eager to visit it. A couple of minutes by boat, and you will see the bay of extraordinary beauty. But this small island is a crusader fortress. Built in the 12th century, it served as a border crossing point. Now it is a museum. With the onset of darkness, a light show is arranged above him.


The island of Pharaohs is surrounded by a coral reef, which is always popular among divers, thanks to the amazing underwater world. Active sports enthusiasts will enjoy exciting windsurfing, and for romantics, evening boat trips.


The hospitable Taba hotels organize trips to the local History Museum. In addition, the Taba resort attracts visitors to other attractions within a few hours drive. This is Mount Moses and the monastery of St. Catherine. Still closer is the Color Canyon. Which got its name because of colored granite.


Also, tourists are offered trips to Cairo and Israel (for those who have an Israeli visa).


To the choice of vacationers, Taba hotels provides the most diverse, from the fashionable five to the democratic three.


From the richest choice, we can distinguish several of the most popular hotels in Taba.


Hyatt Regency Taba Heights 5 *

Comfortable hotel located on the beach. The hotel area is very cozy despite the large area. There are no six pools, an artificial lagoon, two restaurants and several bars. Visitors love the Hyatt Regency Taba Heights for their excellent service and unmatched cuisine with an impressive selection of food on offer. The hotel is suitable for families with children, has a kids club and playgrounds. Paid services include babysitting services.


Sol Y Mar Sea Star Resor 4 *


Located on the shores of a sandy beach. The rooms with terraces are very comfortable. There are all conditions for relaxing with children. The hotel will offer vacationers a lot of entertainment: discos, animation, billiards, table tennis, poolside relaxation. The hotel is served by a bar, restaurant and snack bar.


Salah El Deen Taba 3 *


A small hotel opposite the island of the Pharaohs. On the territory: two swimming pools, a buffet restaurant, a kids club, souvenir shops and much more. The rooms have air conditioning and a paid minibar. Some of the rooms have a balcony. Hotel Salah El Deen Taba is the best combination of price and quality.


Throughout Taba, hotels reviews that are full of admiration will provide tourists with maximum impressions and positive emotions.
