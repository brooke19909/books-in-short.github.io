How to sew the most summer dress: simple options without time-consuming

Needlewomen at all times were valued much more than their less talented friends, because this is an important feature for the future hostess and mother. In the hot season, when clothing prices rise higher and higher, and replenishment of the wardrobe is almost vital, the best option does not come to mind how to sew the most summer dress, skirt or top.



This will help diversify the wardrobe for every day and add to the list of your skills also by sewing clothes, which is a well-deserved reason for pride in yourself. The most preferable for this season are natural flowing fabrics that pass air well, allowing your skin to breathe, and are pleasant to look and feel. Therefore, for the manufacture of clothing for the summer, we choose linen, cotton, chintz or satin.

You will not find an option more elegant and suitable for romantic dates or cultural programs, how to sew a summer dress itself from silk, chiffon, satin or batiste. If desired, you can decorate outfits with a wide variety of elements. So, beads and shiny beads are perfect, pearl sequins, guipure lace or glossy ribbons. The image created by you personally will not look like objects of mass production and will set you apart from the crowd.

Sewing



So, before you learn how to sew the summer dress itself, select the fabric you like and take the necessary measurements. For the simplest option, we need only three - the length of the future product, the volume of the chest and the volume of the chest (measured at the level of the armpits). So, we cut out a rectangle from the fabric with sides equal to the first (side A) and second (side B) measures. We carefully process all the edges on the overlock, if the fabric is too loose, or we do hem and sew on the machine. Next, cut out another rectangle, or rather, a wide strip with a width of about 12-14 cm and a length equal to the volume of the chest, and again process the edge. Then we attach this strip and the first rectangle with side A to each other so that the front sides touch.
We make two neat, even seams with a distance of 2-3 cm between them. Next, it remains only to connect the future dress and this ribbon on the sides, and your product will be almost complete. The last point in answering the question of how to sew the summeriest dress for this cut: to put on an elastic band obtained earlier, so that the product rests on the chest.

Additionally

You can decorate the outfit at your own discretion, but it is better to wear it with a thin belt in bright colors. Changing dresses every day, you can create new images, complementing them with matching shoes and jewelry. In addition, there is no better option to spend free time with pleasure, how to sew the summer dress itself or any other item of clothing, because needlework is an easy way to relieve stress and replenish your wardrobe.
