What is an exit group?

These are employees whose task is to ensure the return of an expired obligation. Goals of field specialists:

  1. Inform the borrower the full amount of the debt, focusing on the calculation of penalties.
  2. Notify the debtor of the consequences of his refusal to pay: the creditor goes to court, and all legal expenses (if decided in favor of the collectors) are borne by the non-payer.
  3. Learn about the causes of arrears.
  4. Make an explanatory note stating the reasons for late payments. The debtor must sign it.
  5. Together with the non-payer, draw up a payment schedule for the repayment of overdue debts.
  6. With the consent of the debtor, conduct an inspection of the property and draw up an appropriate act.
