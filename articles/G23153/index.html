Hotel Eden Club 3 * (Tunisia / Monastir): photos and reviews of tourists

Tunisian resorts are incredibly popular among vacationers, which is explained by reasonable prices for vacations in this region. In our article we want to talk about one of the hotels in Tunisia - Eden Club 3 *.

A bit about the hotel ...

Eden Club 3 * is a budget hotel located on one of the good beaches of Skanes. The complex is located six kilometers from the international airport in the city of Monastir and at the same distance from Sousse. The hotel is family oriented. It was built in 1992 and since then has received thousands of guests annually. In 2014, the complex was completely renovated. In total, it covers an area of ‚Äã‚Äãabout 90 thousand square meters.

Number of rooms

Eden Club 3 *, as mentioned above, has been renovated. Currently, the complex has the following room categories: double, triple and single standards. Apartments feature balconies with sea or garden views.



In total, the hotel has 355 rooms. All are stylishly decorated and equipped with new furniture. The apartments have a bathroom with shower or bathtub, TV, satellite channels, bath accessories, hairdryer, air conditioning.

Dining at the hotel

Eden Club 3 * offers a varied meal in the main restaurant. Breakfast includes fresh and baked vegetables, omelettes, pastries, cheeses, sausages, yogurts, cereals with milk, tea, cocoa, and coffee. The morning menu is quite standard for any hotel. For lunch and dinner, a wider variety of dishes is offered: soups, salads, sauces, chicken, beef, side dishes, fruits, desserts, ice cream and wine.

The waiters in the restaurant are fast and attentive to visitors. The hotel has a bar where guests can order alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks from 9 am to 11 pm.

Hotel infrastructure

Eden Club 3 * (Tunisia) has an excellent infrastructure. On its territory there is everything that tourists may need during their holidays. There is a shop, car and scooter rental, laundry. The hotel has Wi-Fi, which everyone can use. A currency exchange service is available in the lobby.

Sports and Entertainment

Eden Club 3 * (Tunisia) has several outdoor pools, among them there is one heated. There is a water park on the territory with a variety of slides and attractions. The hotel has an excellent animation team, which is engaged in leisure tourists. In the morning, exercises, classes in water aerobics, water polo are carried out. On the coast, vacationers can do water sports: windsurfing, parasailing, catamaran, banana.



The hotel has its own spa center, where you can undergo balneological procedures, visit the jacuzzi, hammam, sauna, massage. It also has a fitness center, beauty salon and hairdresser.

The complex has an amphitheater in which entertainment shows and discos are held in the evenings. Tourists can play basketball, billiards, volleyball, golf, football, archery, tennis, horse riding.

Beach

Eden Club 3 * (Tunisia, Monastir) has an excellent location, as it is built on the beach. It will certainly appeal to those tourists who consider the immediate proximity to the coast a prerequisite for relaxation. The hotel's beach is one hundred meters from the building. It has a sand cover and is equipped with umbrellas and sunbeds. There is a bar on the coast where you can order refreshing drinks.

A bit about the resort

Monastir was once a Roman village (Ruspina). Currently, the city has become a popular resort, attracting many tourists with inexpensive holidays and beautiful beaches. There are many historical attractions and thalassotherapy centers. The city attracts with its close proximity to Sousse. The tourist area of ‚Äã‚ÄãSkanes is moved outside the Monastir, which allowed to preserve the pristine atmosphere of the city.

Local Attractions

Monastir is rich in places that may be interesting for tourists. First of all, it is worth seeing the historical center of the city. Medina, the mausoleum of Bourguiba, Ribat. The yacht port can be seen while walking around the city. For those who do not like to walk a lot, taxis that work on the meter will be an excellent option.



As a resort, Monastir has good beaches, but they are less successful than the coast of Djerba or Hammamet. Here, you can often observe the accumulation of algae and the unrest at sea. Therefore, lovers of perfect water open spaces are better to look for a hotel in other resorts of Tunisia. But in Monastir built many budget hotels that are popular with tourists. The Eden Club 3 * refers to such ones (reviews are given in the article).

In addition to local attractions, guests can enjoy numerous guided tours. In the city itself, you should definitely see Medina in the old city. The Grand Mosque is its core value. Vacationers will be interested in the wonderfully preserved Ribat fortress, which is an example of Islamic military architecture of the 8-11th centuries. Another attraction is the mausoleum of Habib Bourguiba, in which the bodies of the first president of Tunisia and all members of his family are buried.

In Monastir you can buy magnificent souvenirs for relatives: wooden and leather products, ceramics, oils, cosmetics. All these products can be bought in the largest supermarket in the city of Yasmina Center, which is located in Medina.

Monastir is a city where guests can go scuba diving, horse riding and golf. These three sports directions are incredibly developed and popular here. There are two international golf courses in Skanes. As for diving, it is not as gorgeous here as in other resorts of the country, but there is something to see here. The coast has a sandy bottom with rocky sections. And nearby are several uninhabited islets, in the shallow waters of which you can observe the fauna. In reefs and grottoes there are starfish, sponges, hedgehogs and a variety of algae.



Riding enthusiasts can visit the Skanes Ranch, which runs a saddle school.

Monastir is rich in restaurants, the best of which are located in the port area of ‚Äã‚Äãpleasure yachts. In them you can taste the best dishes of fresh seafood, as well as get acquainted with the national cuisine of the country. Sometimes the institutions in the city center offer more varied and tasty food than hotel restaurants.

Eden Club 3 * (Tunisia): reviews 2016

Speaking about the hotel, I want to refer to the reviews of vacationers who have already managed to visit it. According to tourists, the indisputable advantage of the complex is its location. For a beach resort, an important indicator is the proximity of the coast. In this sense, Eden Club 3 * (photos are given in the article) can be considered an ideal place, since the road to the beach does not take more than two minutes. In general, the hotel offers a wonderful view of the sea. Sunbathing in the recreation area near the pools, you can enjoy the beautiful coast.

Why is the Eden Club 3 * hotel so popular among tourists? Tours offered by vacationers have a fairly reasonable price. Tour operators recommend it as an analogue of a four-star establishment, but at a better price.

I would like to note that the hotel receives mostly positive reviews, which speaks for itself.



According to vacationers, the hotel has a good staff that friendly welcomes guests. The settlement of tourists is fast enough. And no matter what time you arrived. If you arrived earlier than the set time, you will be accommodated without delay, and it is not at all necessary to pay extra at the reception.

The number of rooms in the complex is not very new, although the last renovation took place not so long ago. In general, the apartments are quite spacious, but the furniture and sanitary ware in them is not new. Some rooms have terraces or balconies. Especially nice apartments with views of the coast.

Maids clean the rooms with sufficient quality, while the presence of tips is not at all mandatory. Towels are changed daily; bed linen is changed every other day. The only place the cleaners do not reach is the balconies, but this is not so important.

The hotel always has hot water. But air conditioners are not very good at their job.

Nutrition Reviews

What is the nutritional situation at Eden Club 3 *? Reviews in 2016 confirm its worthy level. During the meal, you can see the chef in the restaurant, who evaluates the work of the waiters and adjusts them on the go. It is evident that he is interested in ensuring that hotel guests are satisfied with both the quality of the food and the level of service.

According to guests, the food in the complex is better than in many four-star establishments. Such estimates speak for themselves. As a rule, it is food that causes a lot of controversy among tourists. However, this does not apply to Eden Club 3 * (Tunisia, Monastir).



All the food in the hotel is very tasty and varied, there is always something to choose for children. Baking from the chef is incredibly popular among tourists. General impression of the hotel

The hotel complex has a lot of advantages, including animation. Hotel guests do not have to be bored. During the high season, the animation team incredibly actively works with vacationers. The morning begins with active exercises and water aerobics, during the day, many sporting events await guests, and in the evening in the amphitheater an incendiary show program. Animators pay attention to the smallest vacationers. The young people from the animation team are incredibly positive and enjoyable.

The hotel staff is very polite and attentive, however, you should be aware that the local population decided to greet everyone. Observing simple rules of courtesy, you can get along perfectly with the employees of the complex and without a tip.



There are a lot of insects in Tunisia. In the morning flies get bored, and in the second mosquitoes appear. To make the rest more comfortable, it is better to bring mosquito repellent and a fumigator.

The hotel has its own beach, located close to the coast. The coastline has a sand cover and a comfortable gentle entry into the sea. There is a bar on the beach. Sometimes algae and jellyfish appear in the water, but they do not spoil the picture too much.

Near the hotel there is a bus stop with links to Monastir and Sousse. In order to see their attractions, just use public transport.
