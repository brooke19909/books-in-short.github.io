Design





Appearance Alcatel One Touch 5020D came out not particularly noticeable. Even among the cheaper devices, there are more attractive models. The use of cheap plastic reduces the attractiveness, but the case is well assembled.

Working with the Alcatel One Touch 5020D is very convenient. Small dimensions and roundness make it comfortable to hold the device. Although the appearance of the device is quite tiny, its weight is 136.5 grams.

The design turned out to be familiar to budget employees, and even the standard colors. The device is available in black and white.

Camera

Display





The manufacturer installed a four-inch screen on the Alcatel One Touch 5020D. It is a good size for an inexpensive device. The display resolution is not particularly pleased, only 800 by 480 pixels are installed. Accordingly, even with the naked eye on the image, you can see the cubes.

Matrix Alcatel One Touch 5020D is also not a delight. The manufacturer used TFT technology, which has long been outdated. Actually, the matrix already tells the user everything about the display. The viewing angles in the phone are low, and the screen fades in the sun.

Hardware

"Stuffing" casts doubt on the performance of the Alcatel One Touch 5020D. The characteristics of the device are lower than some state employees. The device received an MTK processor and only one core with a frequency of 1 GHz. So there is no reason to hope for a high power device.

Complement the picture and memory characteristics. The smartphone installed a minimum of 512 MB of RAM. This is only enough to work with regular applications. The device has 4 GB of native memory and the ability to increase the volume of a USB flash drive by 32 GB.

Autonomy





Despite the low power of the device, the duration of the work leaves much to be desired. The smartphone received a battery with a capacity of 1400 maH. Even with minimal use, the device will work for a little more than 10 hours. More active work reduces life to 3-4 hours.

Most of the charge is consumed by the Internet connection and display. The only way to increase battery life is to replace the battery.

Price

Affordable cost draws attention to the 5020D. You can become the owner of the 5020D for only 3 thousand rubles. Even for a state employee, such a cost is unusual. It will be very difficult to find devices similar in characteristics and price.

Negative reviews

Owners are faced with poor optimization of standard firmware. The native system freezes and slows down. Correct the situation by replacing with another custom firmware.

The camera also does not show its best side. A large number of noise and lack of autofocus make themselves felt. Pictures are good only in good light.

Positive reviews





The strength of the device is its cost. Most users have chosen the 5020D precisely because of its low price.

A nice feature was the presence of two card slots. Among inexpensive devices, it is difficult to find a device for several SIM cards at such a price.

The advantages of the device can also be attributed to its small dimensions. The device lies comfortably in the hand and does not cause inconvenience during prolonged use.

Total

The brainchild of Alcatel wins due to the price and two cards. The phone has many shortcomings, but for the state employee it is excusable. The phone faithfully copes with the main tasks, and more is not required from it.
