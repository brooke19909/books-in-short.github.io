Territory and equipment of rooms at the Ruby Hotel

Among the hotels in Alanya stands out "Ruby". This hotel has a significant territory. Alanya Hotel Ruby includes two five-story residential buildings. The rooms are equipped with everything necessary. Each room has:

  • telephone;

  • mini bar;
  • balcony;
  • satellite television.

Room decoration - a table with a large wall mirror. The hotel has two conference rooms with 50 and 250 seats. Type of food - all inclusive. The hotel restaurant also has an outdoor terrace with sea views. There is a poolside bar. On the territory there are two restaurants with a custom menu. Around the pool are free sunbeds and umbrellas.

Tours and attractions

But these disadvantages are more than compensated by the variety of free entertainment and the perfect cleanliness of the territory. Places popular among Russian-speaking guests are Turkey, Alanya. Hotel "Ruby 5 stars" - a good hotel complex for lovers of excursions. Not far from Alanya is the incredible natural wonder of Pamukkale. There is no such phenomenon in the whole world. Pamukkale means “cotton castle” in translation. So the locals called the sparkling snow-white valley in the sun. Groundwater here reaches the surface and evaporates. Therefore, the valley is covered with bizarre calcium crystals resembling stairs and terraces. Underground geothermal springs have useful properties. Tourists bathe in small transparent ponds.

Fans of spa resorts will suit Turkey, Alanya. Hotel Ruby (5 stars) has a car rental service at its disposal. Therefore, you can go to Pamukkale yourself. Specialists working here are constantly developing rehabilitation programs, including baths, applications, mud wraps. At the bottom of the reservoirs are ancient ruins.





For lovers of antiquity

All tourists, without exception, guides recommend a trip to Istanbul. The city for many centuries was the capital of empires - Byzantine and Ottoman. Once this territory was part of the state of Genoa. Istanbul has a lot of attractions. Fans of antiquity will find wonderful excursions in places such as Turkey, Alanya. "Ruby" is a hotel that will certainly provide you with an erudite guide. Although tourists can call a representative of their operator. Istanbul pleases visitors with ancient monuments. The blue mosque, built in the 17th century, is completely covered with blue and white tiled tiles. Its floors are covered with handmade carpets, and the glass shines with Venetian stained glass windows.

The main monument of the Byzantine Empire is the Hagia Sophia. The Christian temple, which has become a mosque, is decorated with pearls and precious stones, decorated with colored marble. Today, the Topkapi Museum houses unique collections of precious porcelain, gold and silver dishes, and jewelry.
