Beautiful pattern with knitting needles for a scarf (three options)

Hand-knitted scarf is a very warm and fashionable thing. Even a novice in this type of needlework will be able to carry out this product with his own hands. A classic scarf is knitted with a straight canvas with elastic-based patterns. Having learned to make front and back loops, you can immediately begin to make an accessory for the neck. But if you want to combine a stylish and exclusive thing, you will have to think about using more original drawings in your work. In this article, the attention of needlewomen presents a collection of patterns with which scarves can be made. Their main advantage is that they are double-sided, which means that the product will look the same both on the front side and on the wrong side. In addition, accessories made with these drawings have a smooth edge and hold their shape well.



Rice

This simple pattern for the scarf, made with knitting needles , got its name because the structure of the fabric knitted by it is similar to the scattered grains (photo of this is confirmed). We study the description of the execution of the figure.

Type an even number of stitches on the knitting needles. 1 row: 1 front loop, 1 - purl, repeat the front / purl to the very end of the row. 2 row: 1 wrong loop, 1 front loop - and so on until the end of the row. Next, repeat the pattern with knitting needles for a scarf from the first row. The canvas is dense and warm.

Chess

Dial the number of loops on the knitting needles, which is divided by 10, and plus 5 more loops to them.

Perform 1 row as follows: 5 front, 5 wrong. Repeat until the end of the row.

2 row: draw all the loops on the wrong side.

Rows number 3, 5, 7 knit as the 1st.

Rows number 4 and 6 perform as the 2nd.



In the next (8th) row, the pattern changes: 5 facial loops, 5 wrong loops, repeat to the end of the row.

In the 9th row, perform all the loops face.

This pattern is made on the number of loops, which is a multiple of 9. We learn to knit such a pattern with knitting needles for a scarf according to the description.

1 row: 2 facial loops, 5 wrong loops, 4 facial loops. Repeat until the last 7 stitches. Perform them as follows: 5 - wrong side, 2-face.

2 row: 1 purl, 7 front loops, 2 purl. Repeat until the last 8 loops in a row, which knit as follows: 7 - facial, 1 - wrong.

3 row knit with purl stitches.

4 row to perform the same as the second.

5 row knit like the first row.

6 row: 3 purl loops, 3 front loops, 6 purl. Repeat until the last 6 stitches of the row. Knit them in the following way: 3 - facial, 3 purl loops.

7 row: 4 loops front, 1 wrong, 8 front. Repeat until the last 5 loops in a row, which are arranged as follows: 1 wrong side and 4 facial.



Repeat the pattern with knitting needles for a scarf from 1 to 8 rows. The drawing on the canvas is obtained in the form of diamonds of the same size, but different in structure. Look at the photo and see for yourself the beauty and originality of this motive.

We hope that all these two-sided patterns with knitting needles for a scarf will certainly become your favorite and will soon be embodied in beautiful products. Happy needlework!
