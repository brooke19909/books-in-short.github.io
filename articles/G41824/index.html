Mysteries of the Sumerians

There was a Sumerian civilization on earth , covered in secrets and consisting of continuous riddles. How did this miracle arise in wild places with little life? Their written language is still not deciphered, in what language they spoke - is unknown. But it is known that the Sumerians were familiar with metallurgy and seriously engaged in mathematics.

Mankind owes them the invention of hours, minutes and seconds. They calculated that the circle is exactly 360 degrees. The Sumerians built buildings of burnt bricks, erected aqueducts, were familiar with astronomy. Are these not the mysteries of the most ancient civilizations? The rest of humanity on earth at that time was in its infancy.

Easter Island

And there are plenty of such inexplicable secrets on the surface of the earth. But no less interesting and numerous are the riddles of the dungeons of ancient civilizations. Amazing mysterious dungeons are in many places on the planet - under many cities they are full. But there are also very ancient ones, for example, Easter Island dungeons or mysterious Maltese labyrinths. Easter Island's multi-level and multi-kilometer artificial caves were discovered recently. They stretch under the entire island, and no one knows what is at the very bottom. Researchers, including Thor Heyerdahl, descended only to a depth of 100 meters. In 45 caves, traces and artifacts of ancient civilizations were found. The whole Easter island with its strange sculptures looking into the sky from nowhere is a complete puzzle of antiquity.
