Hotel Porto Platanias Beach Resort Cat Deluxe 5 * (Greece, Crete): overview, description, rooms and reviews

If you dream of a fabulous vacation, your wish will be fulfilled by Porto Platanias Beach Resort Cat Deluxe 5 *. Here you will find everything that a spoiled modern traveler can only dream of.





Porto Platanias Beach Resort 5 *: hotel overview

Dreaming of an unforgettable vacation, especially close attention to the choice of hotel. In a beautiful place on the seashore, the Porto Platanias Beach Resort Cat Deluxe 5 * hotel is located. It occupies a spacious territory of 30,000 square meters. m. Here, to ensure your leisure time, 5 huge pools, 3 luxurious restaurants, 5 cozy bars and many more opportunities are equipped.

Hotel Porto Platanias Beach Resort Cat Deluxe 5 * consists of the main building, as well as a complex of maisonettes and apartments, which have recently undergone restoration. The entire hotel is covered with dense vegetation, which creates a cool cool shade.

Only in Porto Platanias Beach Resort Cat Deluxe 5 * you can appreciate the Cretan hospitality in its entirety. This hotel can be considered an example of quality service and hospitality. You will also appreciate the elegantly decorated interiors and functionality of the guest rooms.





Hotel Location

Thanks to its extremely convenient location, Porto Platanias Beach Resort Cat Deluxe is an excellent base for excursion routes. This establishment is located in the Platanias region, which is one of the most popular in Crete. Overcoming only 9 km, you will get to Chania, and the distance to the international airport is about 20 km.

Porto Platanias Beach Resort Cat Deluxe 5 * - rooms

One of the most important components of quality rest is the comfort that you can feel while in your room. Speaking about the hotel Porto Platanias Village Resort 5 * ex Jasmine Village, it is worth noting the following categories of apartments:

  • Double "standard" can be equipped with one large or 2 single beds. An extra cot can be provided for an adult or child. Going out onto your own balcony, you can enjoy the sea views while sitting in cozy wicker chairs. The luxurious bathroom is decorated with natural marble.
  • Studio is a spacious one-room number divided into several functional zones. The bedroom is furnished with a king size bed, and the living room has a soft sofa bed. It is worth noting the presence of a kitchenette where you can cook your own meals. The balcony provides a panoramic view of the sea.
  • Maisonette is a two-story room equipped with a decorative staircase. On the first level there is a spacious living room with folding sofas, and on the second - 2 bedrooms with individual bathrooms. It is worth noting that in the design of apartments of this category natural materials prevail.
  • Luxury apartments include double occupancy with the possibility of providing seats for additional guests. It has a bedroom with access to a spacious terrace. The living room, equipped with soft sofas, has a niche with kitchen equipment.
  • Superior rooms consist of two floors combined with a natural wooden staircase. On the first level, there is a living room and kitchen, and on the second - two bedrooms, each of which has a pair of one and a half beds.
  • Executive apartments offer you a spacious bedroom decorated with living indoor plants. Behind the sliding door is a small living room with which you can access the terrace, equipped with its own small pool.
  • Presidential apartments will satisfy even the most demanding guests. The living room is decorated in bright colors and features designer furniture. The master bedroom has a luxurious bathroom with a jacuzzi. The guest room is equipped with a pair of small beds and has its own bathroom with shower.

Hotel Restaurants and Bars

Resting in Greece, it would be a crime not to get acquainted with its unusual cuisine. At Porto Platanias Beach Resort SPA (Crete) you can visit the following facilities:

  • Eros Restaurant is the main dining room of the hotel. It can accommodate up to 400 people at a time. Here, a buffet is served three times a day with a huge assortment of Greek and international dishes. You can stay both in the hall and on the outdoor terrace overlooking the sea coast.
  • Restaurant "Helios" will delight you with a luxurious design in gold tones. through the huge windows always penetrates a lot of sunlight.
  • The Ikarus Restaurant presents a huge assortment of fish dishes. Due to the fact that the institution is an open tent, you can enjoy the sea breeze and beautiful landscapes.
  • Restaurant "Symposium" is a special institution where, in addition to exquisite dishes, you can enjoy the performances of invited artists. concerts are held here in the evenings, during which musical and dance performances are demonstrated.
  • The lobby bar is a great place for a friendly chat over a cup of coffee.
  • The pools have 3 bars offering a huge assortment of cocktails. Thanks to the stand located directly in the water, you can enjoy drinks without stopping from swimming.
  • One of the hotel's favorite establishments is the rooftop bar. From here you can enjoy the dense flowering garden.
  • The beach bar offers a huge selection of drinks and snacks for those who prefer to spend the whole day by the sea.

Negative reviews

  • the hotel photos presented on the official website and other resources do not correspond to the real state of affairs;

  • there is strong audibility between the numbers;

  • for the entire time they were in the room they never brought drinking water;
  • very cold water in the pools;
  • beach towels are provided for a fee, and a rental service is available only three times a week;
  • disgusting quality of the internet.

General impression of the hotel

Unfortunately, the first positive impression that you get when you get to the hotel disappears after a few hours. Behind the external gloss and grooming, you begin to notice the "tiredness" of the interior, frayed walls and other signs that indicate the general neglect of the institution. As for food and service, they also do not correspond to the declared five stars. Nevertheless, the situation is saved by a beautiful green area, a well-groomed sandy beach and a convenient location with regard to tourist infrastructure. If you consider the hotel exclusively as a place to sleep, then this institution is ideal for you.
