How to connect "Smart Unlimited" through your account?

The tariff plan, which appeared relatively recently in the MTS company under the name Smart Unlimited, has already managed to gain popularity among customers. This is really a find for lovers of active communication, including via the Internet. What are the conditions for using this tariff, how to switch to it ‚Äî all these issues will be discussed in this article.





Tariff Plan Description

Before answering the question of how to connect "Smart Unlimited", we give a description of it. After all, not all subscribers are aware of the advantages of this advantageous offer from MTS. What will get the person who purchased a SIM card with this tariff or switched to it?

Firstly, it is unlimited Internet, and without any restrictions on time and speed. You can use it at any time, without fear of exceeding the available limit. As a rule, most of the additional packages of mobile operators that make it possible to use the Internet infinitely for a set monthly fee, after exceeding a certain threshold, include a speed limit. Subscribers who want to forget about additional services and traffic extension should be puzzled by the question of how to connect the Smart Unlimited tariff.

Secondly, this tariff plan provides not only unlimited Internet, but also calls to MTS numbers. You can communicate with all MTS subscribers, including from other regions of the country.





In addition, the Smart Unlimited tariff includes 200 minutes for calls to numbers of other operators and the same number of messages that can be sent to any subscribers of the home region.

Cost of transfer and use of tariff

Before telling how to connect Smart Unlimited, you need to tell potential customers what the cost of acquiring / switching to the tariff is and what is the size of the monthly fee. If you are planning to purchase a new SIM card, then it will cost 200 rubles. You can purchase it through the online store (on the official website of the mobile operator), at MTS sales offices, or at dealers. If you need to make a change in the tariff plan, then you will first need to deposit 387 rubles (this is a subscription fee for 30 days, from the next month it will be debited daily). There is no transition fee. Thus, for subscribers who managed to switch to the tariff before the end of August 2016, a daily fee of 12.90 rubles is set. For other customers who purchased a SIM card / made a change in TP after the specified date, the payment amount will be nineteen rubles.





Features of the tariff plan "Smart Unlimited"

Information about some features of this tariff will also be useful for potential customers.

  • Depending on the region, the conditions for using TPs may vary (it is better to clarify them on the official portal of the mobile operator or through the specialists of the customer service or at the office).
  • The tariff plan is available only for mobile devices (they mean smartphones, tablet PCs). How to connect "Smart Unlimited" to the modem? The answer is quite simple: it is impossible. A number of other tariff plans have been developed for modems.
  • The tariff plan "Smart Unlimited" allows you to distribute the Internet through Wi-Fi. And this means that you can connect a computer, TV or other devices to the Internet. True, not for free. When distributing the Internet, 30 rubles per day will be charged (upon the fact of connecting other devices).
  • The operator reserves the right to limit the speed of the Internet connection in cases when the network is overloaded. This condition is spelled out in the contract for the provision of communication services.
  • Checking the remaining minutes and messages is carried out through the command * 100 * 1 #. Monitoring Internet traffic is not necessary, since it is unlimited. You can see how many megabytes were used by entering the request * 217 #.

How to connect "Smart Unlimited" to "MTS" independently?

If this tariff plan seemed interesting to you, and there is a desire to use it, you need to replenish your account for the amount of the monthly payment (as mentioned earlier, it is 387 rubles) and use one of the following methods.

  1. Change TP from the phone through a USSD request.
  2. Change TP from the phone via SMS.
  3. Change of TP through a personal account (on the official resource of a mobile operator).
  4. Change of TP through the user support service (through the office staff).

Let us consider in more detail each of these methods.





How to connect via phone?

Activation of the tariff through your account

Another way to switch to the tariff plan is a personal account, access to which is carried out through the official resource of the mobile operator. How to connect Smart Unlimited via the Internet? To do this, go to your personal account using the phone number, select the ‚ÄúChange the tariff plan‚Äù section. All tariffs that are available for connection are displayed here. Switching to a tariff plan through customer service

If the implementation of the previously described actions is difficult, you can call the support service at 0890 and change the tariff through a specialist mobile operator. Do not forget to provide the data of the person for whom the number is issued (even passport data may be required). You can also drive to the office of a mobile operator.





Purchase of a SIM card with the tariff "Smart Unlimited"

If you need to purchase a new SIM card with this tariff plan, you can safely apply for a passport (note that only a person who is over eighteen years old) can make a purchase at authorized MTS sales points. This is impossible, since the tariff plan also includes the included minutes and SMS, as well as unlimited communication with all clients of the MTS company in our country. How to connect the Internet "Smart Unlimited" after changing the TP? No special settings are required on the mobile device. Conclusion

The tariff plan "Smart Unlimited" is ideal for lovers of active communication. By switching to it, you get not only unlimited Internet, but also the possibility of unlimited communication with MTS subscribers in any region, send 200 messages a month and communicate with customers of other operators for the same number of minutes. Choose a convenient way to activate the tariff plan and use its capabilities for active communication to the maximum.
