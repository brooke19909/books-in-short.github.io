Volzhsky city: attractions and photos

Volgograd is a hero city covered with military glory. Perhaps there is hardly a person in Russia who would not have heard of him. But a much smaller number of people know about the city of Volzhsky, which is called the satellite of Volgograd. From the famous brother it is separated by the Volga River and two bridges.

The largest industrial center of the south of Russia is Volzhsky. The sights of the city are worthy of the attention of many tourists. Its population is about 300 thousand people. He is bright and green, business and energetic. A large number of young families live in the city. It can be conditionally divided into two parts: with old houses built no earlier than 1950, and new buildings.

What is this city famous for? When did the first people begin to live in it? What attractions Volzhsky (Volgograd region) can offer tourists?

History of the city

The very first people in Volzhsky settled back in the 14th century. It was mostly fluent. In the XVII century. there was built a village called Bezrodnoye. By the beginning of 1917, almost 20 thousand people lived in it. During the battle of Stalingrad, the village was completely destroyed. Only a few buildings have survived.





When the construction of the Stalingrad (Volgograd) hydroelectric station began, they decided to build a new one on the site of the old village and named it Volzhsky. In 1951, the first house was commissioned. A few years later, the city of Volzhsky appeared on the site of the village. The number of inhabitants in it was about 30 thousand. Volzhsky sights begin their countdown from the first cinema "Banner". Now there are about ten of them.

Volzhsky: attractions

Despite the fact that the city is very young and only 60 years old, there is something to see and where to go:

  • Park of culture and rest "Hydrobuilder". Here you can relax perfectly, taking a ride on the carousel, or sit near the fountains, admiring the running water and green trees. The mini zoo located in the park will bring a lot of pleasant emotions to both adults and children.
  • Central Stadium. Various sports competitions and events are held here, besides this, it offers stunning views of Volgograd. You can see the world-famous sculpture "Motherland Calls!"




  • If you come here with children, then bring them to the river port. Small ships and impressive steamships are constantly sailing from here, you can take a walk along the Volga.
  • Aquapark. Its distinctive feature is that you can come here in winter and summer. Not every major city in Russia has such an opportunity, but it is in the city of Volzhsky.
  • Attractions worth paying special attention to: the Museum of Local History. Here you can get acquainted with the history of the city and get a lot of interesting information about the Volgograd region.
  • Volga Hydroelectric Power Station is equally important. It offers a wonderful view of the Volga and the surrounding area.

Among the attractions of the city should also include: theaters, museums, monuments. Let us dwell on them in more detail.

Museums

The Living Memory Tram Museum was presented to residents on February 15, 2011. On the same day, but in 1989, the withdrawal of Soviet troops from Afghanistan ended . This unique museum is dedicated to the inhabitants of the city who died in that war. Photographs of heroes, military awards, symbols of the Afghan war are presented at the exposition.

The Museum of the History of the Russian Cossacks consists of three halls, in which there are exhibits and materials representing the history of our homeland. In the museum you can get complete information about the culture and life of the Russian Cossacks, learn about their past and present.

Monuments

There are more than 20 of them. Monuments are the main attractions of the city of Volzhsky. Most of them are dedicated to construction. The most popular of them:

  • There are very interesting sights near the city of Volzhsky. A photo with a description of the monument to the builders of a hydroelectric power station shows us an unusual geometric figure - a tetrahedron, on top of which there is a metal gull.
  • One of the first was a monument to V.I. Lenin. His seven-meter figure, cast in bronze, stands on a granite pedestal, whose height is 9 meters.




  • "Unnamed Victims of War" - a monument that also enters the sights of Volzhsky. A photo of him clearly demonstrates that "nothing is forgotten, no one is forgotten." It is dedicated to tens of thousands of people who died in the civil and Great Patriotic War, whose names remained unidentified. Hundreds of citizens and visitors come to this monument, paying tribute to unknown heroes.
  • Another sad page of Russian history is captured in a granite pedestal dedicated to the victims of political repression of the 20th century. Three human figures of scarlet color, throwing their heads back, look at the sky. The pain and despair, inhuman torment and suffering that befell the unfortunate people, were able to express the creators of the monument.

Tourists reviews

Numerous tourists note that Volzhsky is very green and comfortable. A large number of trees, shrubs and flowers gives the right to talk about him as a garden city. The main attractions of Volzhsky can be seen in one day, and then it is recommended to go to the cinema or a cozy restaurant or cafe, of which there are a sufficient number in the city.



You arrive in the city and literally fall in love with it. Volzhsky kindly shows his sights to everyone. Among the advantages of the city, many tourists note the lack of gas, clean air and hospitality of local residents. After the bustling Volgograd it is so nice to come here and relax.

In the central square bearing the name of Lenin, one can read the following inscription: "Volzhsky is the city of my fate." Arriving here once, you will certainly return again. Perhaps you will stay here forever, meeting your fate.
