Hotel Hi Mimosa By Globales 3 * (Mallorca, Spain): photos and reviews of tourists

Holiday in Spain has many faces. Especially successful are the island resorts of the Bolearic Islands, washed by the Mediterranean Sea. There are only four of them, among them are the islands of Ibiza and Mallorca. If the first of them can be called a paradise for twenty-year-old tourists, then the second, much larger in size, attracts the attention of older holiday-makers, it is very good for families.

Climate and geographical location

The tourist season in Mallorca begins in April and ends in October. The island is located in a zone of humid subtropical Mediterranean climate. It is better than all the other Bolear Islands with its mountains protected from the winds, the risk of bad weather on it is minimal.





The period from the end of May to the beginning of August is the time of the high holiday season. In the velvet season (August-September), the weather becomes milder, there are practically no storms, but prices are rising during this period. Tourists arriving in Mallorca get to the hotel Hi Mimosa By Globales 3 * (Spain) from the airport using a short (about twenty minutes) car shuttle. Its address: Avenida Cas Saboners, 28.
