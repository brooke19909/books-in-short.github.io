Bowling in Zhukovsky: address, reviews, how to get

This occupation has long been one of the most popular types of outdoor activities for adults, as well as a favorite pastime for children. Bowling in Zhukovsky is represented by several institutions that offer excellent opportunities for exciting and eventful leisure for both young guests and their parents. It is no secret that today many people prefer to combine various ceremonial events with a bowling game: birthdays, family parties, corporate parties and even weddings. Undoubtedly, this indicates the great popularity of this sport. Where can I play bowling in Zhukovsky? Let's talk about this in our article.

Bowling "Planetarium" in Zhukovsky: acquaintance

This sports and entertainment center is located in one of the busiest areas of Zhukovsky in the Moscow region. According to reviews, the institution is a wonderful place for friendly meetings and family vacations. The complex has a main hall, as well as a summer terrace.

The visitors are offered modern bowling alleys, slot machines and a variety of exciting attractions for the youngest. An exciting bowling game will help guests forget about everyday worries and have a great time in a warm friendly company or surrounded by loved ones, as well as test their skills. If desired, visitors can also play billiards here, watch a sports broadcast.

The institution also has a cafe (gastro-bar), which serves dishes of various cuisines of the world - European, Italian, Caucasian, Japanese. There is also a children's menu. Those who came here during dinner at the Planetarium will be served a tasty and satisfying lunch. The cafe offers its visitors free internet (Wi-Fi), as well as a food delivery service to the office or home.

For children in the Planetarium, there is a large selection of diverse exciting and fun slot machines. Adults can relax and unwind while their children are busy at the bowling and gambling bar. Payment can be made in cash or by credit card.

Bowling Features

For bowling, the Planetarium has the most modern AMF equipment equipped with a computer scoring system, which provides the possibility of organizing competitions between tracks. Book a track by phone or online. The rental price includes the use of balls and special shoes for bowling. You can pay for the game in cash or by credit card. Reservation of a bowling alley for a corporate event or children's party is carried out by the sales service.

About location

This bowling is located in Zhukovsky on the street. Gagarina, 2a, in the Planetarium sports and entertainment center, on the 2nd floor of the shopping complex. Finding an institution is not at all difficult, since it is located on the main and longest street in the city, which is its original axis. You can get here by public transport or your own car. One of the undoubted advantages of the club, which motorists will certainly take advantage of, is the presence of its own organized parking. Entering the building of the shopping center, you should go up to the second floor - and here the guest herself will be captured and entailed by an indescribable atmosphere of excitement, games and gastronomic pleasures.
