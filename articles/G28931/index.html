  2. Spread the shred and place the selected packing (for example, cotton wool) in its center.
  3. Fold the corners of the burlap together, as if making a knot.
  4. Add more stuffing if necessary.
  5. Tie the corners tightly with an elastic band (picture 1). It turned out the trunk of the house.

  9. Attach pins to the torso and legs (picture 4).
  10. Take the thread and fold it in half several times, so that in the end you get a piece about five centimeters in size.
  11. Tie the resulting accordion from the threads in the middle.
  12. Cut the thread from both ends to get a few strings connected together.
  13. Fold what happened, again in half and bandage.

The list of tools and materials for making a domino from threads

  1. Two sheets of cardboard or two disks.
  2. Scissors.
  3. Woolen threads.
  4. Glue.
  5. Beads.
  6. Colored paper.
  7. The big needle.

    Master class: brownie from threads

    Do-it-yourself home-made threads are most often made using pompom technology. That is, to begin with, you make a pompom, then decorate it at your discretion so that you get a brownie.





    Operating procedure:

    1. If there are no discs, cut two circles out of cardboard and make a hole in the middle of each.
    2. Put the discs or circles together.
    3. Thread the needle.
    4. Wrap the circles tightly together, pulling the needle into the hole in the middle, as in the diagram above.
    5. Wrap a few more rows of thread in the same way.
    6. Insert one scissor blade between the circles and cut the thread.
    7. Spread the circles a little.
    8. Tie a knot on the thread between the circles.
    9. Remove the circles. You got a pompom.
    10. Cut out eyes and mouth from colored paper.
    11. Glue them.
    12. Glue the pupil beads on top of the paper eyes.
    13. Roll up a small ball of thread.
    14. Glue it between the eyes and mouth. It will be a nose.




    The little house from the threads is ready!

    List of tools and materials for making a dough brownie

    1. Salty dough.
    2. Paints.
    3. Tassels.

    Additionally, an oven is required.





    Ingredients for the dough:

    • two glasses of flour;
    • a glass of finely ground salt;
    • 180 grams of water.

    Master class: brownie from the test

    Make the salt dough. To do this, mix all the ingredients in a bowl and knead them thoroughly.

    Now start sculpting from the dough. This is the same as sculpting from plasticine. Take a small board and place work on it. You can make a house figure both flat and three-dimensional.

    Do each detail separately, gradually connecting them together, as the salty dough can have time to dry.

    First fashion your head, make eyes, nose and mouth. Then form the torso. After that, make a couple of pens and legs. Attach the limbs and head to the torso. Give the whole figure a shape. For example, highlight the cheeks and eyebrows on the face, fingers on the hands. Also do not forget to make a silhouette of clothes and hair on the head. In general, feel like a sculptor.

    After you have made a figure, it needs to dry. To do this, place it on a baking sheet and place it in the oven. Dry the figure for about an hour at a temperature of 80 degrees. Drying can be arranged on a battery, but this process will last much longer.

    When the figurine has dried thoroughly, let it cool. After that, proceed to decorate the house. Then show your imagination.





    The dummy is ready!

    Tip: for needlework, choose bright colors of fabric, threads, dyes and decorations. So do-it-yourself housekeepers will be much more interesting.
