Golden Pattern: reviews and working principle

The human body ideally has absolute harmony and perfection of systems. Nature also endowed people with amazing symmetry and proportionality, according to which, parts of the body are in the right proportion to each other. In accordance with this pattern, all nature is arranged in general. These laws were used by Leonardo da Vinci during his work. All things in which there is such a distribution of proportions seem beautiful to a person.





Golden ratio

Using patterns existing in the proportions of the human body, architects and artists created their creations. Such incredible calculations gave many opportunities for accurate work, the correct construction of figures in sculpture and their depiction in paintings. They called this calculation the "golden ratio." This knowledge began to be used since 1935 in sewing. Being a mathematician by education, the German Maria Lutterlo streamlined the works of the famous Leonardo.





She developed a cut system, which is translated from German as ‚Äúgolden section‚Äù or ‚Äúgolden pattern‚Äù.

Worldwide method

Today, this method is widespread throughout the world; it has been tested in practice by thousands of seamstresses. The Golden Pattern system allows you to build patterns for customers with any shape. They do not have to have perfect proportions for this. A seamstress takes only a few minutes to work. It sounds like a fantasy, but the scheme allows you to build patterns in individual sizes for any person. This is how mathematical formulas work.

An ordinary seamstress will not be able to independently make such patterns and calculate these formulas, but she does not need to do this. In specialized stores you can order the finished system "Golden Pattern". User reviews regarding the operation of this technique are pretty good. The order comes to the buyer directly from Germany, but you can also get to the intermediaries who sell ready-made sewing kits.

Set for work

The price of the order includes payment for the kit and its delivery.





The package contains the textbook "Golden Pattern". There is an unusual meter there, a special scale is applied to it, which allows you to work with patterns. For this meter, the manufacturer gives a lifetime warranty. In the folder block there are 280 patterns, they are easily removed from it and just as easily inserted back when the work is done. The package also has tools for working with the cut: cardboard pattern, acrylic rulers, button-needle. Additional patterns can be ordered separately. It is worth saying that although the set is not cheap, it is popular among professional seamstresses, and among those who only comprehend the basics of designing clothes.

Preparing to build a pattern

To build a "Golden patterns "it is required to take only a couple of measurements. The circumference of the chest (OG) and the circumference of the hips (OB) is quite enough to build the selected model of the product. The patterns are suitable for creating women's clothing, men's and even children's.





The set has the usual centimeter tape, but there are special marks on it that allow you to work specifically with the "Golden Pattern".





Labels are presented in the form of a scale on which the figures from 50 to 140 are plotted. These figures are correlated with the exhaust gas and exhaust gas sizes in centimeters.

There are holes near the numbers. About the number that corresponds to the desired size, you need to insert a needle and pin it to the center of the cross on the pattern. A meter with a pattern is mounted on a large sheet of paper, the size of which is enough to fit a pattern cut in full size on it.

Building patterns by your standards

First, the work is carried out with the measurement of OT. When all the necessary elements are pinned to paper, the scale must be scrolled around the button (needle) until it touches the stroke of the desired number. It indicates the place where you want to put a point. All points correspond to the numbers on the tape from the kit.





After all the points above the waist are laid down in this way, you need to pull out the button and insert it on the tape at the point corresponding to the OB measurement. Similar to the previous steps, insert the needle into the center of the cross on the pattern and put down all the points that are below the waistline.

When all the labels are transferred to a large sheet of paper or tracing paper, they need to be interconnected. For the connection, it is better to use the acrylic patterns available in the kit. This is how the Golden Pattern works.
