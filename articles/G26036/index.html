Contraindications

Unfortunately, you can not take water procedures in the hot spring "Wave" to everyone. Doctors do not recommend visiting it for people with such diseases:

  • condition after childbirth or abortion;
  • diabetes;
  • sexually transmitted diseases;
  • thromboembolism;
  • skin mycoses;
  • nephritis and gallstone disease;
  • heart disease;
  • hypertension and / or ischemia of the heart of the II and III degrees.




Rules and prices

In order to make the Volna hot spring not only useful, but also comfortable for staying, rules similar to those existing in water parks have been introduced at the recreation center. At the entrance, visitors put on their wrist electronic bracelets that record the time and the amount of money allocated by guests for their vacation. Then visitors go to modern locker rooms, where there are spacious showers and lockers for clothes that are locked with electronic locks. If a vacationer needs a snack, in a cafe he pays money from a bracelet. At the exit, an employee of the Volna base looks at how much has been spent.
