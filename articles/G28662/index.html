What is a freeform for beginners

Today there are a huge number of different styles and methods of knitting. The most popular at present is the new direction called freeform. For beginners, it seems unimaginably complex - and all because of the stunning beauty of the patterns. But if you try and carefully study the technique of execution, it turns out that in reality there is nothing impossible.

Freeform for beginners



What is it? This is a kind of free knitting technique, which provides an opportunity to perfectly fit a thing according to its shape, to create the very pattern that most suits your mood and style. You can make the product either in a monophonic color scheme or, conversely, choose contrasting or smoothly changing shades. Knitting freeforms allows you not to restrain your imagination. It will turn out even for those who have never knitted before.

Freeform for beginners. Knitting basis

A distinctive feature of this technique is the manufacture of not a whole canvas of the front or back, but the knitting of certain motives from which the part is subsequently created (back, shelf, sleeves, front). In order not to be mistaken with the size of the finished product and to keep all the desired contours and silhouette, you need to choose a thing from your wardrobe that completely suits you for the fit, length and size of the armhole. Having tied the required number of parts, you should decompose them on the finished

product and start collecting new clothes. If the motives are small, then adjusting the volume and size of the product, fit the figure will be much easier. Finished motifs are sewn with ordinary threads to match the yarn, the distance between them can be filled with small additional fragments or a chain of air loops. Depending on the thickness of the yarn, the size of the parts and the size of the product, you will need a different number of motives. The best option would be to knit three or four fragments of different types and shapes. Lay them out in random order. In this case, you only need to observe the shape of the desired product and monitor the organics of the resulting pattern. In the beginning, you can just pin motifs with pins to see how the result meets your expectations. Once you are completely satisfied with what you saw, you can proceed to consolidate the fragments. This is the technique for knitting freeforms. Agree that there is nothing complicated in it!

Freeform for beginners. Product decoration



Despite the fact that it is possible to carry out motifs in different colors and as a result we get quite elegant things, you can also additionally decorate an already finished thing. Most often, the craftswomen resort to the help of beads or medium-sized beads, Swarovski crystals, which are fixed in the middle of floral motifs. If the butterfly became one part of the product, then try to emphasize the silhouette of the wings or the contour of its body. Especially beautiful and elegant looks jewelry made in tone knitting. The play of light on beads or beads creates an absolutely stunning effect. If you have never tried to knit a product in the style of freeforms, now is the time!
