Bus stations in Moscow: addresses, phone numbers

Arrival and departure points of regular international and interregional bus routes or PMAM - Moscow bus stations have such an official name . Their activities are fully regulated by Decree No. 895 of the city of Moscow dated 12/17/15. Today, the following bus stations carry out the transportation of passengers in Moscow: Central (Schelkovsky), Krasnogvardeiskaya, Varshavskaya, Novoyasenevskaya, Tushinskaya bus stations, Teply Stan, international "South Gate".





Moscow. Schelkovsky bus station

The main motor transport hub in the capital is the Central (Schelkovsky) bus station. Getting to him by subway is easy. It is located near the station "Schelkovskaya". Station address: 75 Schelkovo highway. It was put into operation in 1971. In order to provide more comfortable conditions for passengers, the bus station building was completely renovated in 1997. Moscow, Schelkovsky bus station in particular, receives and sends up to thirty thousand tourists and travelers daily. For the comfortable work of carriers, they created a whole complex, the station includes a building for passengers, aprons for buses, car washes for vehicles, parking lots, porches for greeters and mourners.





From the Shchelkovo bus station, communication is maintained with 54 Russian cities, of which: 9 cities in the Moscow region and 15 - near abroad. Over a long period of operation of the enterprise, established long-term routes have developed, stable passenger flows have been established. On average, 1,667 flights are carried out per day: 23 of them are international, 54 interregional, 21 intraregional. Bus departure frequency is every 10-15 minutes. Bus station "Krasnogvardeiskaya"

The Krasnogvardeiskaya bus station began its activities in 2003. Now the passenger flow is served by 44 bus fleets. Every day 120 flights are made on 24 routes, every day there are about three thousand people. Like other bus stations in Moscow, Krasnogvardeiskaya works for the benefit of its customers. The bus station has a technically equipped waiting room, offers the following services to passengers: pre-sale of tickets, booking, ticket return, luggage storage, payment terminals, ATM, Wi-Fi, vending machines for selling food and drinks. From the metro station "Zyablikovo" on foot you can walk in five minutes. Station address: Orekhovy Boulevard, 24. Information can be obtained by calling: +8 (499) 940-08-43.

Bus station "Novoyasenevskaya"

The bus station opened in 2011. It is served by 65 fleets of the capital. The daily passenger flow is 1,500 people. Tushinskaya

In 2012, the Tushinskaya bus station began operating . Today it is served by 16 fleets of Moscow. On 26 regular routes 70 flights are made daily. Passenger traffic is about a thousand people. Tushinsky bus station (Moscow, telephone for information: +8 (499) 940-08-43) is located at Stratonavtov 4-1. The bus station is located near the metro station "Tushinskaya", walking can be reached in 3-5 minutes.
