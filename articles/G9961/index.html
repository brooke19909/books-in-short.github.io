The main thing is the team

Firefighters need to work in a team, act in concert, because here everyone performs the function assigned to him. One member of the team is engaged in the fact that he spreads his sleeves, another directs them to the fire, and at this time the remaining firefighters push the ladder out, remove people from hard-to-reach places. At the same time, the team must quickly respond and immediately arrive at the call, on average it takes no more than five minutes.
