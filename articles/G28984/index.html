Samsung C6: specifications and features

Now we will discuss in all details the smartphone Samsung Galaxy S6, the characteristics of which are of interest to many people around the world. It's about the flagship of 2015. With this model, the manufacturer wanted to compete with the iPhone 6 and rehabilitate a series of flagships after weak S5 sales.

Positioning



Regarding the features of Samsung Galaxy S6, the technical characteristics of the device primarily provide reliable protection against external damage according to the IP67 standard. For example, the device is not afraid of water. Metal is the trump card of the Samsung S6 developers, while the characteristics of the device were not affected, they can be called the highest possible at the time of release. Here you can see an attempt to create an unsurpassed design and surprise with the build quality. Among the users there are surely fans of the Samsung S6, whose characteristics are as follows: maximum camera quality, good and large screen, and battery life in one day. Next, we discuss the advantages and omissions of this development.

Control Elements, Dimensions, Appearance

In the Samsung S6 review, the specifications will be the next reference point, which we dwell on in more detail. The phone is quite compact. Its dimensions are 143.4x70.5x6.8 mm, and weighs 138 grams. It fits well in the hand, does not slip. The back wall and side frame are made of metal. Each color, thanks to the glass, is similar to metallic, there is an interesting play of shades in the sun. Traces remain on the surface of the screen, it is easily soiled, but not excessive.

The metal frame received recesses on the side edges, this adds convenience. The right side surface received a nanoSIM card tray and a power button. On the left are the keys for adjusting the volume. The top panel is complemented by an infrared port that allows you to control household appliances, as well as a microphone (another one is available from the bottom). As for the microUSB connector and 3.5 mm headset output, they also found a place on the bottom panel. The back surface housed a flash and a heartbeat sensor. The front panel is supplemented with a physical button, a fingerprint scanner is mounted in it . In addition, there is a pair of touch keys. Above the screen is a proximity sensor and a front camera, 5-megapixel.

Screen



Let's discuss the Samsung Galaxy S6 display, its technical characteristics are as follows: automatic brightness adjustment, 2560x1440 pixels, 577 ppi, SuperAMOLED, 5.1 inches. This screen can be called one of the best in Samsung products. There is an individual color setting. The backlight brightness level was increased, and the display is not afraid of the sun. The high pixel density per inch made the picture particularly smooth.
