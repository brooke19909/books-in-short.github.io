How to make a children's fireman costume?

When a child grows up, wants to become a firefighter? And why, in fact, not. This is a noble profession that benefits people. But, of course, childhood dreams often change during adolescence. Therefore, let the child dream and present himself as a hero saving people from fire right now. Make a kids fireman costume. How to make it, read below.

Costume for the matinee

You can make an image for a boy from a tracksuit. After all, it is in such clothes (free cut) that firefighters fight fire. What color the costume will be, in principle, does not really matter. It would be appropriate to look blue, red, yellow and even green. Now you need to complement the image with strips of reflective fabric. We cut them out and evenly distribute them in the costume. Decorative pads should be attached to the chest (and you can also to the belt), which will look like a fastener, but carry only a decorative function. A children's firefighter costume would be incomplete without a headgear. If you do not have a plastic helmet, then it is easy to replace it with a panama hat that matches the color of the suit. To make it look like a fireman‚Äôs hat, you can embroider a logo on it.

Holiday image

It will be quite appropriate to put on a children's firefighter costume both at a matinee dedicated to professions and at a New Year's party. And to collect such an image will be quite simple. Do-it-yourself children's fireman costume is made from materials already available at home. You need to pick up yellow pants and a helmet that matches the color. If you do not have the necessary headgear, you can replace it with a panama hat or a small plastic basin. As a sweater, you should choose a tight gray sweatshirt. It will need to be sewn or glued on lining of reflective material. What could it be? The strip that will decorate the jacket from the bottom, and the circles, they need to be sewn in two rows. They symbolize buttons. Such stripes can be repeated on the pants. To make the image look more complete, it can be supplemented with a toy hose.

Toy inspiration

Children's firefighter costume can be made very simple. Only a large hat worn on a child will give out a profession. And the rest of the costume is just formal clothes. This option is great for matinees on which you want to change the baby. Parents, if necessary, will be able to quickly remove their children's firefighter costume. The child will already celebrate the New Year holiday simply in a shirt and trousers.

Well, now about what to assemble a suit from. It should consist of a shirt and pants. It can be both classic black trousers, and more modern light models. It is desirable to choose a shirt bright - red or orange. You can tie a tie for the solemnity of the child‚Äôs neck. Well, of course, the main attribute of a firefighter is a hat, which should be made from newspapers, and then it can be painted. If you collect the outfit more than once, then it is better to sew a hat from fabric. So he will look more presentable, and you can not come up with ideas for a costume, but dress a child in a fireman one or two more times.
