Inn "Troitsky" - a successful vacation in the country

A break from the hustle and bustle of the city is the dream of any resident of the metropolis. The Troitsky Inn provides such an opportunity to its guests. Here you can retire to nature and breathe deeply with fresh and clean air.

The complex has all the conditions for a comfortable and memorable vacation. Here, like nowhere else, you can enjoy communicating with nature.

Where is it located and how to get there

The Troitsky Inn is located in the Rostov Region, Neklinovsky District. The leisure complex is located in an ecologically clean area. In the village of Troitsky you can feel all the delights of a country holiday by visiting the Troitsky Inn. Complex address: st. Lenin, 61.





The hotel is located near Taganrog. It can be reached by car or public transport from the city. Room reservation can be made online on the official website of the complex.

For the convenience of guests, a parking lot is available on site, which is guarded around the clock.

Accommodations

Visitors can stay in one of 11 rooms in a large cottage or rent individual small comfortable houses with all amenities. The Troitsky Inn provides standard rooms and cottages with additional services. For example, in the house number 5 is a modern pool table.

Number 11 belongs to the class of "luxury" with enhanced comfort conditions. Standard houses are designed for 2 guests. For an additional fee it is possible to provide one more berth.





A small cottage with billiards can simultaneously accommodate up to 8 people. The rooms can consist of one room for 2-3 guests or several rooms. So, the suite provides accommodation for 4 people.

From October 15 to April 15, Trinity Inn (Taganrog) provides its visitors with a 50% discount on accommodation. This promotion is not valid on holidays.

The rooms are equipped with everything necessary:

  • comfortable modern furniture ;
  • TV
  • air conditioners;
  • shower and toilet;
  • refrigerator.

Cottages have spacious verandas for relaxing and tea drinking. Housekeeping is regular. Bed linen and towels are changed every 3 days. The territory of the complex is well maintained and clean.

Food and arrangement of the territory

The complex provides the opportunity of 3 meals a day on the basis of its restaurant. It can be paid in full for the entire time of rest or after the menu daily. In a separate spacious room, decorated in a classic style, you can organize a banquet for any special occasion.





The property has barbecue facilities and gazebos for relaxing and picnicking. You can admire the beautiful representatives of exotic birds in a fenced aviary. The option of renting a gazebo for a certain time is provided by visiting guests who do not stay overnight at the hotel. They can also enjoy other activities for a fee.

A modern playground is available on site. Children are free to ride electric vehicles that are provided by staff. It also has an outdoor pool with comfortable sun loungers around.

Entertainment

The Troitsky Inn has at its base a stable with a sufficient number of thoroughbred horses. Here you can ride a selected animal in spacious meadows. Beginners are given the opportunity to get riding lessons from experienced instructors.

One of the guest cottages has a traditional sauna. Its interior is made of natural wood. There is a special room for a feast and 3 hotel rooms. There is a delivery order from the restaurant.

The Troitsky Inn (photo in the article) has large billiard tables. They are located in one of the cottages with additional lounge areas. Delivery of dishes from the restaurant is also possible here.





Particular attention should be paid to water activities. Here, children can go boating with an experienced instructor. Near the pier there are water rides in the form of swans.

Adult vacationers can go fishing. There is a small marina for this and boats can be used. The staff of the complex is constantly replenishing the number of fish in the pond and increasing the number of species inhabited here. Thus, the catch for each guest is provided, and the excitement from this will only increase.
