Residential complex in Volgograd "Blackberry": description, prices

In southeastern Russia, on the banks of the Volga River, there is a huge hero city Volgograd (formerly Stalingrad). The population of the city exceeded one million people, and therefore developers consider it promising for the implementation of their projects, one of which is the residential complex "Blackberry" (Volgograd).





Location of the residential complex

The complex is located in the very center of the city, Dzerzhinsky district: 42, 30th Anniversary of Victory Boulevard. It took the most favorable position. Large transport arteries pass nearby: the 3rd Longitudinal Highway and Marshal Zhukov Avenue, through which you can get to the Blackberry residential complex (Volgograd) by your own car. There are many public transport stops on the boulevard, which makes it easy and quick to get to the house by public transport.

Description of the complex

The residential complex "Blackberry" in Volgograd is a single residential building with 19 floors. The house was built over a period of four years and was commissioned in the second quarter. It is currently inhabited by residents. The building was erected from bricks; the apartments have a typical layout with pre-finishing. The height of the ceilings is 2.68 meters. The house does not have a boiler room. The entrances are equipped with one passenger elevator. Utility rooms for concierges, strollers storage under the project are not provided.





The complex offers various apartments: one-room, two-bedroom and three-bedroom. All apartments have balconies. Unlike other developers, the project of this house does not provide for non-residential premises on the ground floor of the building.

The cost of apartments

In Volgograd "Blackberry" offers the following prices for their apartments. Traditionally, the most expensive are square meters in one-room apartments. Their cost is 51 thousand per square meter. Following are two-room apartments. Their price is 50 thousand per square meter. The cheapest are three-room apartments. At 49 thousand per square meter of living space.

Infrastructure internal and external

The residential complex "Blackberry" in Volgograd took a very advantageous place - almost the center of the city. This provides its residents with not only easy transport accessibility, but also a highly developed infrastructure. Almost all the social infrastructure necessary for life are within walking distance. These are schools, kindergartens, shops and supermarkets, sports palaces, swimming pools, bank branches, pharmacies, beauty salons.

The internal infrastructure consists in the improvement of the yard area by the developer, the arrangement of a recreation area, a children's playground and open parking of vehicles near the house. The developer has not designed anything else, since all the facilities necessary for a comfortable life are already available in close proximity to the Blackberry residential complex (Volgograd).





Reviews about the residential complex

The house was commissioned more than six months ago, and you can already find reviews about it. Mostly they are positive. This also applies to such a successful location of the Blackberry residential complex. Volgograd allocated almost the most profitable land plot in the city center in the Dzerzhinsky district for its development. Near the complex is a park of Heroes Pilots, which only adds to it positive points. In the park it is good to take a walk in the evening or relax with the whole family at the weekend.

But not only positive reviews were collected by the residential complex "Blackberry" in Volgograd. Many interest holders were very concerned about the progress of construction, the postponement of its completion and the small number of construction workers, which caused delays. Some are also unhappy that the developer did not design non-residential premises for shops and pharmacies in the complex itself. But the proximity of other residential complexes with the necessary objects of social infrastructure balances this negative opinion.
