The best graphic tablets for drawing with the screen

The modern market offers a wide range of similar equipment of various brands. How to choose the right graphics tablet for drawing - we will tell in the article.

Summary

Graphic tablets with screens are excellent multi-functional assistants to artists, since they allow not only drawing, but also viewing images during work, without interrupting the process.

The capabilities of most models allow you to work with the canvas with a few touches through the touch screen, stripes, pen or additional buttons.

The color gamut of graphic tablets with a screen is more saturated, voluminous and deeper in comparison with monitors of personal computers or ordinary tablet computers. The reaction to the force of pressing and to the pen itself in such gadgets is more vivid than that of standard graphic tablets. A distinctive feature is the high speed of work with the image.

Wide functionality and special features make graphic tablets with screens irreplaceable gadgets for artists. Their only drawback is the high cost, but professional models are worth it.

When choosing a graphics tablet, you should pay attention to its dimensions, work surface dimensions, basic and additional functions, the presence of buttons, touch strips, a pen or pen, and other attributes. Regardless of the functionality, the choice of a tablet completely depends on the artist, his preferences and goals in the work.
