Possible connection difficulties

In practice, users have many difficulties, even if they know how to use a smartphone as a modem. A common problem is the lack of access to a 3G, 4G network. Another problem is also likely - the equipment does not connect via USB. The phone does not appear on the computer, so it cannot be used as a modem.
