How to transfer photos from iPhone to iPhone in different ways

If you just purchased a new iPhone 5s or iPhone 5s to replace a previous generation gadget, transfer all data, including contacts, calendars, photos, etc. quite simple if you previously backed up your information in iCloud or iTunes. Both services make this process incredibly easy, helping to transfer data from your old mobile to a new device without much effort on your part.





How to transfer photos from iPhone to iPhone using iCloud

If you previously backed up information from your mobile phone to iCloud, transferring data to a new gadget will be very easy.

Turn on your new iPhone and wait for the welcome screen to appear. Swipe down on the bottom of the display to go to settings. Now choose the language from the options presented in the menu. Select the country or region in which you are located.



Internet connection

Now select your Wi-Fi network and enter the password for it, if any. If you do not have Wi-Fi access, you can use the mobile Internet, but keep in mind that not all data in your iCloud backup will be restored. Some settings will not work until you connect Wi-Fi.

Now you have to configure Location Services - choose whether you want to use the default location determination. Keep in mind that some applications, such as maps, must use this service in order to work correctly.

How to transfer photos from iPhone to iPhone? Next, click on "Restore from iCloud Backup" and enter your credentials when they are requested. Be sure to click "agree to the terms." You will be asked to confirm this consent a second time.

If you have iCloud data backups , your iPhone will now display them. Click on the information you would like to recover.

The backup process may take some time (depending on how many files you transfer). It is also worth noting that some elements, especially applications, photos and videos, are worth copying only when Wi-Fi is working. This is necessary in order not to spend too much traffic.





How to transfer photos from iPhone to iPhone with iTunes

If you replaced your smartphone, but saved backup information in iTunes, transferring everything to a new device is easy. Today, not every user trusts iCloud so much, and many prefer to store data in iTunes. Using this service, it is also possible to copy photos and other data.

Before you drop a photo on an iPhone, be sure to make sure that you are using the latest version of iTunes 11.

After turning on your new iPhone, you will see a welcome screen, move your finger to the bottom and go to settings. Enter all required data. After that, click on "Restore from iTunes backup information."

Sync & Download

If you are not already connected to a computer containing your photos and other data, iPhone will offer to do it now. Follow the instructions on iTunes to select the most recent backup. If you use this service of the latest version, everything should be quickly downloaded to your phone - iPhone. Photos of this process can be found on many resources.

That's all there is to it. After iTunes starts the process, how do I transfer photos from iPhone to iPhone? Depending on how many photos and other data you have, especially applications, the download process may take some time. Just make sure you don't disconnect your iPhone from your computer until iTunes tells you that the recovery is complete. After that, you can turn off the device and use the information that has been transmitted. Using the above instructions, it is possible to transmit an unlimited amount of information.
