The best hotels of Vilnius: reviews and photos

One of the most popular tourist centers in Europe, of course, is the capital of Lithuania. In order for guests to receive a positive experience not only from excursions to historical places, but also from living conditions, they will be welcomed by the hotels of Vilnius.

"Panorama"

For any tourist, the location of their chosen hotel is important. Hotel "Panorama" in Vilnius is located in the Old Town, has a category of three stars. Of the attractions within a short walking distance there are the Gates of Sharp Gate, Town Hall and Town Hall Square, the Artillery Bastion. Tourists will also be able to appreciate the architecture of the cathedral, the church of Saints Peter and Paul, the church of St. Nicholas. Nearby there are several shops, a market, close to the railway station and bus station. The hotel's restaurant serves European cuisine. A parking place for guests with their own car is available at an additional cost, and you can rent a car on site. Tourists are offered several options for rooms:

  • single - the cost is 2929 rubles per one night;
  • double - the price for a room is 3143 rubles per day;
  • double with panoramic windows - its cost is 4215 rubles per day;
  • deluxe room - for one night stay the price will be 6519 rubles.

All rooms are bright and airy, many with beautiful panoramic city views. There is free wireless internet access.

Hotel Crowne Plaza Vilnius

If you are considering hotels in the city center in Vilnius, look at the Crowne Plaza Vilnius hotel . The hotel has a four star category, located in the central part of the city. On the one hand there is a business district, on the other - the old city. The hotel has a large panoramic restaurant, there is a summer terrace. On-site parking is available. The distance to Vilnius International Airport is 7 kilometers, to the international railway station and bus station only 3 kilometers. To accommodate tourists, rooms of various categories are offered:

  • double standard room - its cost is 7736 rubles per one night;
  • double standard non-smoking room - the price for the room is also 7 736 rubles per day;
  • double executive room - the room rate is 9036 rubles per day;
  • the same for non-smokers - 8386 rubles per one night;
  • deluxe room - the price is 12937 rubles per day.

The price includes breakfast buffet. Free WIFI works.

Grata Hotel in Vilnius

Hotel "Grata" has a category of three stars, is also located in the city center. The historic Old Town is a 15-minute walk away. Guests can make use of the tour desk, as well as an ATM at the hotel. Secure parking is located in the garage. One kilometer from the hotel is Vingis Park, as well as many shops and clubs. The restaurant serves a daily buffet breakfast. There is a bar. The following room options are available for guests:

  • single economy - 2143 rubles per day;
  • double - 3929 rubles for one day of stay, if one person settles, the cost will be 3572 rubles;
  • double superior - 4286 rubles per day, if one person lives - 3929 rubles;
  • Suite - 6644 rubles for one essence of living.

All rooms are decorated in warm, light colors. Breakfast is included in the price, pets are allowed.

Hotel Grand Resort

Spa Hotel Vilnius Grand Resort has a five-star category. In addition to the hotel itself, these are restaurants, bars, two swimming pools, a spa and wellness center with a wide range of services. A four minute walk from the tourist complex is the beach. The hotel owns a professional golf course, has its own tennis court. Unlike other Vilnius hotels, this one is located 20 kilometers from the city. A free shuttle service is available to drive to the center. The elegant rooms offer scenic lake views. The following options are offered for accommodation:

  • double classic room - 7144 rubles per night;
  • double superior room - 8573 rubles per day;
  • double executive room - 9001 rubles per day;
  • family room (designed for 4 people) - 11573 rubles per one night;
  • Premium Suite - 15716 rubles per day;
  • Presidential Suite - 28575 rubles per night.

All rooms include breakfast. There is access to free wireless internet.

Artis Centrum Hotels

Among the Vilnius hotels located in the city center, be sure to pay attention to Artis Centrum Hotels. The five-star hotel occupies a restored nineteenth-century building in the old town. Exquisitely decorated rooms offer views of the Old Town and the Gediminas Tower. The hotel has two beautiful restaurants. Guests can swim in the indoor pool, and there is a steam room and sauna. Artis Centrum Hotels is a good choice if you want to admire the old architecture. Guests are offered rooms of several categories:

  • double - 7858 rubles for one day of stay, if one person settles, then the price will be 6787 rubles;
  • double representative - 8930 rubles per day;
  • Junior Suite - 10358 rubles per day;
  • Suite - 11787 rubles per one night.

The price of all rooms includes breakfast, there is access to free Wi-Fi.

Hotel Congress

Of the many hotels in Vilnius, the four-star Hotel Congress is sure to attract attention with its magnificent building. Like the previous one, it also occupies a nineteenth-century building in the city center. Vilnius Cathedral is just a 10-minute walk away, while Gediminas Tower and Vilnius Castles are 800 meters away. There is a restaurant and a bar, parking has a limited number of places. There are various options for accommodating guests:

  • single room - 5358 rubles per one night;
  • double room - 6072 rubles per day;
  • mini-suite - 7501 rubles per day;
  • Junior Suite - 8215 rubles per day;
  • Suite room (there is a balcony) - 8930 rubles per day;
  • luxury apartments - 12502 rubles for one essence of living.

Fitted with wooden furniture, all air-conditioned rooms are equipped with a safety deposit box and minibar. Free WIFI available.
