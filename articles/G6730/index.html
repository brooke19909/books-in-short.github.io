Sanatorium "Turquoise" (Lazarevskoye, Sochi): rest, official site, prices, photos and reviews of tourists

Of course, today many travelers choose the Turquoise sanatorium for relaxation. Lazarevskoye is one of the most beautiful and welcoming corners of the coast. Here, any guest will find a warm welcome, decent living conditions and, of course, quality treatment.

Location of the sanatorium





Many residents of the country at least once in their life rested at the famous resort in Sochi. And very close to the sea is the sanatorium "Turquoise" (Lazarevskoye). Address - Krasnodar Territory, the city of Sochi, the village of Lazarevskoye, Victory Street, 167.

General description of the health resort

Of course, the favorable location of the sanatorium is one of its most important advantages. After all, the buildings were built almost on the Black Sea coast - the distance to the beach is only 70 meters. From the northern winds, the village of Lazarevskoye is protected by the peaks of the Caucasus Range, which creates unsurpassed, unique landscapes. And very close are the mouths of two mountain rivers - Svir and Psezuapse. Yes, and Olympic facilities are not so far away.

The sanatorium itself has a rather large territory of its own, over which a whole team of landscape designers worked. Intricate bushes, bright flower beds, exotic plants, cleaned walkways and comfortable benches in the shade of trees will delight every vacationer.

The health resort consists of two buildings - these are Turquoise and Black Sea. They not only accommodate living rooms, but also a diagnostic center and treatment rooms.

Sanatorium "Biryuza" (Lazarevskoye): description of rooms and living conditions





It is immediately worth noting that the last repair and change of furniture was carried out not so long ago - in 2008 - 2011. As already mentioned, the sanatorium consists of two large buildings. The large sleeping complex "Turquoise" is a seven-story building, which is designed for simultaneous accommodation of 386 people.

It mainly has one-room double rooms, as well as superior rooms. What living conditions does the Biryuza sanatorium (Lazarevskoye) offer? Each room is equipped with the necessary amount of furniture, including comfortable beds, bedside tables, tables, chairs and a wardrobe. Of household appliances there is a TV, air conditioning, and a refrigerator. The bathroom is also beautifully equipped - there is a separate toilet, a comfortable shower, washbasin, mirror. Every day, clean towels will be waiting for you in the room (three pieces per person). The child can sleep in an extra rollaway bed. The room also has a private balcony with a set of furniture for relaxing and a clothes dryer.

Dormitory building "Black Sea" is a three-story building - 153 people can live here at the same time. It has both spacious family rooms and single rooms. There are also cheaper rooms with partial amenities. These rooms do not have a private bathroom, but on the ground floor there are a sufficient number of toilets and showers designed for general use.

Customer Nutrition Scheme





Of course, the Biryuza sanatorium (Lazarevskoye) offers its customers full three meals a day. On the territory of the health resort there is a spacious dining room that can accommodate all customers. It is worth noting that here, in the sanatorium, increased attention is paid to the rules of nutrition, since wholesome food is an integral part of quality treatment. The diet is compiled individually by the attending physician. The dining room works on the basis of pre-orders. The service is provided by professional waiters.

What diseases are treated in a health resort?





To begin with, it is worth noting that the Biryuza sanatorium is a multidisciplinary wellness center that treats many different diseases and disorders. For example, patients with various diseases of the musculoskeletal system and connective tissue often come here.

Indications for spa treatment are also various disorders of the nervous system, including neurotic disorders. It also offers modern methods of treating various diseases of the respiratory and cardiovascular systems. The sanatorium also has a large diagnostic center, since a complete examination of vacationers is a prerequisite for quality rehabilitation.

Treatment Methods Used

What can the village of Lazarevskoye offer to its guests? The Biryuza sanatorium (photo) has valuable natural resources - next to the residential buildings is a balneotherapy center where patients are offered various procedures using mineral waters. These are hydromassage, coniferous-pearl, bischofite and iodine-bromine baths, underwater massage, etc.

In addition, massage rooms operate on the territory of the sanatorium - both manual and apparatus massage, as well as special anti-cellulite sessions are carried out here. Modern physiotherapy methods are also used, including galvanization, ultraviolet irradiation, electrosonotherapy, electrophoresis, darsonvalization, electrical and myostimulation, UHF, magnetotherapy, and laser correction.





Patients are offered therapeutic inhalations, applications using ion exchanger and bischofite. Group and individual physical therapy classes are also regularly held at the sanatorium, which positively affects the health status of patients.

There are some additional services. For example, the health resort has its own cosmetology rooms. There are also special anti-cellulite programs. Those who wish are also offered ozotherapy, body wraps, lymphatic drainage and vacuum massage.

Leisure and entertainment at the sanatorium

What kind of entertainment can the Biryuza sanatorium (Lazarevskoye) offer? The official website says that the range of services here is really large. To begin with, it is worth noting that on the territory of the health resort they often organize various corporate events, including not only recreation, but also various conferences and seminars.

Very close to the sanatorium there is a clean well-groomed beach with sunbeds, mattresses and umbrellas - a rescue team is constantly on duty and there is a medical aid point. Of course, on the Black Sea, you can try yourself in various types of water sports.





In addition, you can have a good time on the territory of the sanatorium itself. There are rooms for board games, halls for watching movies, as well as a large library, a small pool and a sauna. Women will enjoy the local beauty salon. Sports equipment and bike rentals are also available, as well as ample parking for cars and a free internet area.

Do not forget that Sochi is a large resort city with a lot of different attractions. Here you can go to museums, a water park, a dolphinarium and other interesting places. It also regularly hosts concerts and various cultural events, as well as incendiary parties and discos. Here everyone will find something to their liking.

How much is a ticket?

Of course, today many people know what exactly the city of Lazarevskoye, the sanatorium "Biryuza" can offer. Prices here can also please many. But it is worth noting that the cost of the voucher directly depends on the category of the room, as well as on the time of rest. For example, a vacation at a health resort in winter will be much cheaper than in August or September. The price is from 2100 to 5000 rubles per day per person.

In addition, the resort offers a system of discounts for children. For example, children from 4 to 8 years old can live with their parents at half price. The discount for patients aged 8 to 15 years is 30%, 15 to 17 to 20%.
