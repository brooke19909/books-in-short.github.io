Hotel YK Patong Resort 3 * (Thailand / Phuket): overview, description, reviews of tourists

YK Patong Resort 3 * Resort is an ideal place for those who want to enjoy a comfortable and colorful vacation in Thailand for a small price .





Hotel Description

YK Patong Resort 3 * hotel is quite small, but has all the necessary facilities and amenities to make guests feel comfortable here. The rooms are very bright and comfortable, and their full equipment allows guests to feel at home.

One of the world's most popular resorts is Thailand. 3 * YK Patong Resort will help you make your vacation unforgettable, and accommodation will cost you quite inexpensively. In your free time you can sit in a restaurant or in a small cozy bar, as well as visit the billiard room or swim in the pool.

The location of the hotel is quite favorable, because within walking distance are the beach, market, as well as many cafes and shops. The hotel was built in the heart of Patong, 50 km from Phuket Airport.





Hotel rooms

In the hotel YK Patong Resort 3 * guests can stay in one of 60 comfortable rooms. The following categories of apartments are available:

  • The standard room is a small room of 20 square meters. m with a large double bed. Due to the compact area of ‚Äã‚Äãthe apartment, more than 2 people can not stay here, and an extra bed is not allowed.
  • Superior room is a room identical to the standard, but its area has been increased to 30 square meters. m. Due to this, up to three guests can stay here, subject to the organization of an extra bed.
  • A deluxe room is a room that is similar in size and capacity to the previous category. The interior of these apartments is planned so that in one room there is a sleeping area and a cozy seating area.
  • Family rooms are 50 square meters. m and consist of two small bedrooms. The apartments are designed for 4 people.




Room Amenities

Each room of the YK Patong Resort 3 * hotel provides the following amenities:

  • individual air conditioning;
  • TV with satellite antenna connection;
  • a phone that is designed for internal calls within the hotel (international calls are made upon prior request);
  • a small safe for storing valuables and documents;
  • mini-bar, which for a fee will be filled as necessary with alcohol or soft drinks;
  • wireless Internet;
  • the bathroom has a complete set of all necessary bath accessories, including cosmetics.

Hotel infrastructure

Guests of the YK Patong Resort 3 * hotel can use the services of the following resort infrastructure facilities:

  • a theme restaurant that specializes in Thai national dishes, but can also please its guests with culinary masterpieces related to international cuisine;
  • a bar where you can drink tea, coffee, soda, as well as alcoholic drinks;
  • in the courtyard of the hotel there is a small pool;
  • the laundry is responsible for the cleanliness of your belongings;
  • for the convenience of guests, the hotel has several elevators;
  • You can find all the necessary information or contact friends and family using the global network in an Internet cafe;
  • in the reception area there is an opportunity to use the free Wi-Fi.




Services provided to tourists

Hotel YK Patong Resort 3 * (Thailand, Phuket, Patong) cares about its guests, so it provides them with many services. At the same time, the cost of part of the services is included in the price of hotel accommodation, and some require additional payment. So, buying a tour, you are guaranteed to get the right to use the following services:

  • You can work out in the gym.




For a fee you can get the following services:

  • give your things to the laundry, where they are carefully washed and ironed;

Positive reviews

Thailand is a fairly popular tourist destination, but quite expensive. That is why the issue of budget, and at the same time comfortable rest, is very important and relevant. YK Patong Resort 3 * hotel (Phuket) is ready to provide such opportunities to its guests. Tourists who have already visited here are happy to share their positive impressions:

  • the price is absolutely consistent with the quality of services provided by the hotel;
  • all hotel staff are very friendly, diligent, attentive and friendly;
  • daily thorough cleaning;
  • convenient location of the hotel - there are many shops, cafes, restaurants, as well as a large market nearby;
  • very comfortable atmosphere;
  • comfortable and cozy rooms;
  • if there are any problems or technical problems in the room, employees quickly respond and fix them;
  • there is a free gym (although it is small, but there is a lot of equipment in it, it is quite new and modern);
  • It is possible to use the free Internet.




Negative reviews

As in any other hotel, in YK Patong Resort 3 * (Phuket, Patong) there are some disadvantages and shortcomings. These moments are reflected in the negative reviews of tourists who have rested here earlier:

  • breakfast offers a very small selection of dishes that are repeated every day;
  • the pool is practically not looked after, because of which there is garbage in the water and in the surrounding area;
  • the hotel is located in a noisy place above the roadway;
  • the hotel staff practically does not speak either Russian or English;
  • the beach is untidy, and the shower and toilet on its territory are paid;
  • the rooms have very poor sound insulation, due to which the sounds of a busy street, as well as the voices of loudly speaking neighbors, cause discomfort.

General impression

Hotel YK Patong Resort 3 * leaves a rather mixed impression about itself. This is a budget hotel, which is unlikely to be suitable for those tourists who are accustomed to a five-star service. At the same time, this is just the perfect place for those who come to Thailand for local color and impressions, and the hotel room for them is just a place to sleep, where there is all the necessary furniture and modern (albeit slightly worn out) equipment.

Despite the fact that the hotel itself and all its equipment are quite old and worn out, the zeal and efforts of employees who strive to make their establishment better are noticeable. The convenient location of the hotel makes it an ideal tourist attraction, because it is located in the heart of Patong. From here you can easily reach many attractions, as well as visit local markets and restaurants to plunge headlong into the unforgettable Thai world.
