No one can be sure of tomorrow. Even those who carefully plan their own lives are not immune from illnesses, accidents, crimes, etc. But there is a way to be sure of the material well-being of loved ones. What is cumulative insurance?

The cumulative life insurance program makes it possible to accumulate certain funds over several years. The client undertakes to pay dues monthly or annually. Along with this, the insurance company is obliged to pay a certain amount upon the occurrence of an insured event or upon completion of the contract.





Accumulative life insurance is more developed abroad. The rating of companies abroad is annually supplemented by new young organizations that offer to conclude a deal on more favorable terms. Clients get the opportunity not only to save funds, but also to increase them. Endowment insurance involves the possibility of accumulation of capital in combination with life and health insurance of the client. Before concluding such an agreement, it is worth examining the proposals of various companies.
