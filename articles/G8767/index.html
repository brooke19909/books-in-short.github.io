Esat Hotel 3 *: rest in Kusadasi

Turkey is a globally recognized tourist region. The abundance of places of rest and resorts allows you to choose the option that more satisfies the needs of the traveler.

The Turkish resort of Belek has a reputation as the most fashionable, Antalya - the most vibrant and democratic resort, Kemer is often chosen for families with children, due to the mild Mediterranean climate and forest mountains. All these are vacation options on the Antalya coast of Turkey.

The Aegean coast opens up opportunities for those seeking tranquility and avoiding the debilitating heat. Three-star hotels, of which Esat Hotel 3 *, which will be discussed, belongs to the category provide comfortable rooms with all necessary equipment, provide a relaxing stay at a reasonable price.

Holidays in Kusadasi

Kusadasi is a famous resort on the Aegean coast. The category of tourists who choose the clean sea, relax on the water, appreciate windsurfing and yachting, does not crave a lot of excursions. Perfect for families with children.

The choice of hotels is quite large - from the level of the Amara chain (Amara Sealight Elit5 *), the five-star Aqua Fantasy Aquapark Hotel & SPA to economical aparthotels with a provided food area for self-catering. You should look at three-star hotels located at some distance from the sea. Hotels such as Esat Hotel 3 * attract quality service, good food and silence. They constitute a significant sector of budget holidays on the Aegean coast of the Turkish resort.

Hotel location

The former name of the hotel is Pigale Panorama Sunset Resort, it is located seventy-five kilometers from the international airport in Izmir, one and a half kilometers from the center of Kusadasi.

3 * hotels usually do not have their own beach or access to the sea. But this is not an insoluble problem.

There are municipal beaches with free access to the coast, you can also use the sunbed and umbrella of any hotel with its own beach for an additional fee of two to five dollars. Esat Hotel 3 * is located a kilometer from the coast, on the third line. A walk to the beach can be leisurely walked in fifteen minutes.

Accommodation and services

Esat Hotel 3 * (Kusadasi) offers its guests seventy-five standard rooms. Each of them has a balcony, air conditioning, shower. The top floors of the hotel offer beautiful sea views.

The hotel has its own small park area, outdoor swimming pools, adult and children. According to the accepted norm, free sun loungers and umbrellas are offered for guests to relax around them.

The price includes cleaning services, linen, wake-up service at the appointed time. For an additional fee it is possible to order laundry services, a doctor‚Äôs call, an individual transfer to the airport or to meet at Izmir airport and transfer to the hotel.

Nutrition

The price includes breakfast. Meals are offered in two restaurants: indoor - designed for the simultaneous accommodation of up to one hundred guests, open - for one hundred and fifty seats. Esat Hotel 3 * also has a snack bar.

You can enjoy drinks and snacks by the pool, in the lobby bar and in the rooftop bar, which offers beautiful panoramic views of the parks and the sea.

The restaurant menu offers local, Turkish and international dishes. You should definitely try the traditional Turkish delicacies: baklava and Turkish delight. For gourmets, you can offer to taste the dishes of local cafes and restaurants, generously offering their services in Kusadasi.

Entertainment and excursions

The silence and short duration of the swimming season do not exclude a sufficient choice of entertainment and excursion services. A relaxing holiday in Kusadasi can be diversified:

  1. Visit water parks. Two water amusement parks - "Adaland" and "Aquafantasy", are located near the city of Kusadasi. Closed and open slides, downhill skiing, a safari park, an artificial wave pool - you don‚Äôt get bored here. Near the water park "Adaland" is a dolphinarium, where you can become a spectator of a show of dolphins and fur seals.
  2. Travel by ferry to the Greek island of Samos. This is the birthplace of the ancient Greek philosopher Pythagoras.
  3. A visit to the Pigeon Island. Holidays on the island are combined with a visit to the fortress of the Byzantine period.
  4. A trip to the Dilek National Park.
  5. By shopping. In Kusadasi, you can buy quality souvenirs and gizmos from onyx, leather, brass, copper, jewelry of Turkish brands: Benetton, Naf-Naf. In other places, the same products can be purchased at a significantly higher cost.
