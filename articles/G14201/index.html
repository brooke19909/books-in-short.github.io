How to choose a stun gun: customer reviews and expert advice

The principle of operation of stun guns for most people consists in pressing a special key and following this discharge of current, which sends a person into unconsciousness for several minutes. In fact, the device and mechanism of action of such devices is somewhat different. The review left by each individual stun gun of the one who already used it will help to figure out which models are most suitable for self-defense and what parameters are worth paying attention to.

Bit of theory

The power of the selected stun gun depends on the voltage and current strength. For this reason, high output voltage indicators are not always a guarantee of self-defense efficiency - such devices, as a rule, can only frighten with a bright spark and loud crack, no more.

About the stun gun, the review often contains information that the voltage of the device, depending on the model, varies from 50 to 200 kilovolts. Its value should be sufficient to bring the electric discharge to the body.

Specifications

The shocker must have the following parameters:

  • Cause pain that persists for a long time after the end of the exposure of the device. Such an effect incapacitates the enemy and prevents him from attacking again.
  • Exposure to electric current leads to muscle cramps and subsequent paralysis.
  • Decreased reaction rate, loss of orientation, probable loss of consciousness.




Degree of defeat

When choosing a stun gun, you need to pay attention to its level of damage. In practice, the effect of the shocker may vary depending on certain circumstances.

In a review about a stun gun, an ordinary user indicates that the effect provided by the device depends on the amount of clothing worn by a person. For a current, a fur coat or cotton T-shirt will not become an obstacle, unlike a leather jacket and an additional layer of things.

The effectiveness of the shocker depends on the pain threshold of the person and the portability of the charge. For example, an attacker weighing 60‚Äì65 kg will quickly knock down the stun gun, while an opponent is several times harder to only inflame and anger.

The nervous state also affects the effectiveness of the shocker: if the person to whom the device is used has drug or alcohol intoxication or an adrenaline rush into the blood, then the shocker may not work or may not have the desired effect.

Self-defense experts and ordinary users about such a device as a stun gun leave positive reviews, but they all agree on one thing: when choosing and buying, you should not pay attention to sellers' advice, as they are interested in selling the most expensive model.
