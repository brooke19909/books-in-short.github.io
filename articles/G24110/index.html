How to crochet gloves? How to make fingerless gloves crochet?

Gloves are an integral part of women's spring wardrobe. They can be very different, differing in the material from which they are made, as well as in the color scheme and style. If you own knitting needles or crochet, then most likely it will not be difficult for you to make something original that is suitable for the spring season. And for those who are just starting to master needlework, we will tell you how to crochet gloves, what is needed for this, introduce various options.

Preparatory stage

So, at the very beginning of the work, you need to decide what kind of yarn the gloves will be: openwork or continuous knitting, and you also need to choose a style. If you crochet gloves made with single crochet columns, then you will need more yarn than for an openwork version or a fingerless model. Having decided on the threads, we select the hook that fits them in size. Then we take the necessary measurements - the girth of the wrist, the distance to the base of the thumb and little finger. Now let's figure out how to crochet gloves.

Starter Option

Let's start with the simplest option - gloves in a classic style. You can give them originality and originality using unusual colors of yarn and decoration in the form of an openwork mesh, as in the photo.



Where to start, so that in the end we get such crocheted knitted gloves? The execution scheme is as follows.

Beginning of work

We knit a chain of air loops with a length corresponding to the girth of the wrist with a small margin. Having knitted the first row with single crochet, we connect the resulting bar in a ring and continue knitting in a circle. Having reached the base of the palm, we make a series of uniform additions necessary to expand the glove. To do this, through an equal number of loops in four places, we knit two columns in one loop. Next, we continue knitting until we reach the base of the thumb. Now, at the location of the thumb, we knit a chain of 6-8 air loops and attach it to the main knitting so that the finger fits freely in this loop. We continue knitting further in a circle. Where we made a ring of air loops, there should be a thumb hole. After knitting 2-3 rows, it is necessary to perform a decrease, which will allow the glove to sit well on the hand. They are performed through the same number of loops four times in the following way: the columns do not fit into each loop of the previous row, but skipping one.

Knit the fingers of the gloves

We continue knitting to the base of the little finger. Next, distribute all the loops between the fingers. For the little finger, leave less by 2-3 loops, for the other three - the same amount. Remember to make jumpers of 3 air loops between your fingers. We knit each finger separately in a circle. Finish by performing a decrease. Reductions are made in the manner described above, only in smaller quantities. For example, one row has one decrease. Please note that each finger should have its own length, the longest of them is the middle one. Now you can move on to knitting the thumb. To do this, knit single crochet in a circle, gradually making uniform decreases in two through a row. When all the fingers are ready, it is necessary to complete the decoration. To do this, knit a mesh pattern of air loops along the bottom edge of the glove. To make it look harmonious, it is better not to make too large cells, 4-6 air loops are enough. The length of the cuff may be different, at your discretion. It is best to tie the edge of the net with double crochet to make a small shuttlecock. To do this, in one ring we knit 7-8 columns. Crochet gloves made in this way knit very quickly and simply, so even for beginner craftswomen it will not be difficult. The second glove is knitted similarly to the first, but do not forget that you need to place your thumb on the other side.

Fishnet gloves

These gloves can be crocheted quickly and easily. They look very beautiful, but are intended for special occasions. The photo shows that this option of gloves will not help you keep your hands warm, but rather, it will only perform a decorative function. For their implementation, thin, often cotton or silk, threads are used. The knitting pattern is almost the same as in the previous version, except for the pattern used.



The main pattern is a mesh made of air loops. An openwork pattern is made on the back of the glove, not necessarily exactly the same as in the photo. You can choose any option you like.

On the wrist, you can also emphasize in the form of an openwork neckline. It will be deep or not very, you decide. The length of such gloves can also be different. The photo shows white gloves, but you can choose any other at your discretion.

Crocheted gloves knitted with an openwork pattern will add a special charm and sophistication to the holiday.

Three options for youth gloves

And here is another fairly simple option - fingerless gloves. Crocheting such a model is even easier than previous versions, since it does not need to be tied to fingers. And this greatly simplifies the work, and time will be required an order of magnitude less. The photo shows three fingerless gloves. The simplest of them are pink, made with ordinary single crochet columns. They knit similarly to the first option, only the knitting of the fingers ends after the second or third row.



The next option, purple, is already crocheted with columns and decorated with knitted flowers. The knitting pattern is similar to the above.
