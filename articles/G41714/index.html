Hotel Beverly Park & ​​Spa (Spain, Blanes): room description, service, reviews

Blanes is a favorite resort for Russians located in eastern Spain. It is only sixty kilometers away from the bustling Barcelona.

Weather report

The village is located in the Mediterranean climate. The weather in Blanes is characterized by mild winters. The column of the thermometer in January almost never drops below 10 ° .





The swimming season opens in early June and ends in mid-October. In summer, the air temperature is 28 ° C. August is very hot. At the same time, nights do not give the long-awaited cool. After sunset, it’s stuffy outside the window. Salvation brings a wet breeze. In June, the water warms up to 21 ° C, in July - 24 ° C, and in August - 25 ° C.

In summer, the weather in Blanes is often overshadowed by prolonged and heavy rains. 25 millimeters of rain falls per month. In July and August this value is much lower, only 17. The average wind speed these days is 3 meters per second. The number of cloudy days does not exceed 4. The length of daylight hours varies in the range of 10-12 hours.

Geography and economy of the region

The city is included in the resort area of ​​the Costa Brava and occupies its southern part. The rock of Sa Palomera divides the settlement into two large areas. One is almost entirely owned by tourists and is built up by multi-story hotel complexes. There are concentrated fashionable shops, restaurants and cafeterias, boutiques, shopping and entertainment centers.

The North is the sleeping quarters in which the locals live. The population is 40,000. In the high season, this figure increases several times. A textile factory operates near the municipality, and an agricultural processing plant operates. A port is functioning, fishing is booming.

Significant sights of the region are the castle of San Juan, the ruins of the Viscount fortress of Cabrera, the gothic fountain, the basilica named after St. Barbara.

The cost of renting a kit, which includes a deck chair and an umbrella, is 4 euros. The beach is open to all visitors. Local residents and visitors relax on it. Anyone who does not want to pay for inventory spreads an ordinary towel or bedspread.
