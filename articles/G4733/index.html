New furniture from waste material, or How to make do-it-yourself poufs from a plastic bottle

A plastic empty bottle has long ceased to be just rubbish. Inventive craftsmen learned to make from it a variety of useful things for life: vases, coasters for pencils, watering cans, feeders for animals and birds, even furniture. Puffs from a plastic bottle - this is what this article will be about. A convenient, light, and most importantly, very practical piece of furniture you can make with your own hands each of you at home. How to do this is described in detail in the material below. Study it, collect empty PET containers and start the creative process.



Learning to make ottomans from a plastic bottle. What do you need for work?

To make this piece of furniture with your own hands, prepare the following materials and tools:

  • empty plastic bottles of the same size and configuration - 32 pieces;
  • thick cardboard;
  • sheet foam rubber or synthetic winterizer;
  • Scotch;
  • large scissors;
  • fabric (cotton, velor, velveteen, drape);
  • needle with thread;
  • centimeter;
  • sewing machine.

We make ottoman from bottles. Workshop for beginners

  1. Connect four bottles in a row. Attach three more to this design. You will get a square blank, consisting of 16 plastic containers.
  2. For other bottles of the same type, cut off the top with a cap and put them on those that are already fastened together. Again, carefully wrap the entire structure with tape.
  3. Place the structure on cardboard and circle its borders. Cut two identical parts along the intended contour. These cardboard blanks will become the bottom and cover of the ottoman.
  4. Glue these two parts to the plastic blank with tape.
  5. Using a centimeter, take measurements from the resulting structure, transfer them to foam rubber or any other soft material (synthetic winterizer, insulation). Cut out two squares and a rectangle equal to the circumference of the walls of the ottoman.
  6. Sew the plastic blank with this material over the seam. You will get a practically finished soft ottoman.
  7. To the measurements that were taken for foam, add a couple of centimeters from all sides, transfer them to the fabric and cut out the details of the cover. Sew a rectangle and one square manually or on a typewriter. Turn out the cover and put it on the ottoman. Sew the bottom part directly on the plastic product.

That's how simple and quick do-it- yourself poufs from a plastic bottle are made with your own hands .

Useful tips for those who want to make this kind of furniture for the first time

Bottles for making ottomans in the nursery can be taken with a volume of 0.5 liters. The kid will be happy to sit on such a small object. For the living room, you can make a large pouf, which will sometimes serve as a coffee table.

Covers on a similar product can be not only from fabric. You can sew them from leather, dermantine, oilcloth. Puffs from a plastic bottle look very cute, the upholstery of which is made from hand-knitted fabric.

Such a piece of furniture can be round, and rectangular, and oval in shape. It all depends on how you position and interconnect the containers.



Do not rush to throw empty PET containers in the trash. From it you can not just make a ottoman from plastic bottles, but also make a real exclusive and comfortable piece of furniture. Believe that sitting on it is very convenient. Good luck with your creative endeavors!
