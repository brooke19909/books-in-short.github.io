World War I. Weapons of superpowers

One hundred years after the end of the First World War will pass soon . And if in World War II Russia came out as the undisputed winner, then the results of the Imperialist are very controversial for her. To everything else, huge debts to other countries have formed. Readiness for war

New developments

The First World War launched the latest military developments of various countries of that time into its arena. These were the first tanks, advanced fighter aircraft, large-caliber guns. Small arms of the First World War had many varieties, including new models, previously unknown. Machine guns and the first semblance of anti-aircraft mounts stunned everything around with their bursts. And another new invention, the application of which distinguished itself during the First World War, is chemical weapons.





Tanks

It was at this time that the first tank came off the factory conveyor - Mark 1. Launched in the UK, he knew no equal. His crew included seven people. Its weight was 26 tons, which did not add good indicators of the speed of the tank. Mark 1 was armed with four machine guns and two guns. Subsequently, he received two varieties: male and female. The male had two powerful guns, and the female six heavy machine guns. The tanks worked in tandem, with the expectation that the male was doing the main sweep. The female is a little at a distance and covers.





But this is far from the only tank that launched the First World War. Weapons on the tracks were carefully developed by the Germans. Appreciating Mark 1, they set about developing their A7V tank, which was slightly reminiscent of its British counterpart. Only his crew consisted of eighteen people, and he weighed four tons more.

Each country tried to build its tank, which was pulled into itself by the First World War. This weapon, however, succeeded only in Great Britain and France. The Germans, if they released their model, were not able to organize its mass production (and in all they had no more than 20 tanks).
