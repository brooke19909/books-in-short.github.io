The specifics of Sharm El Sheikh

To begin with, the resort is located not in Africa, but in the Asian part of Egypt. A couple of tens of kilometers away is the border with Israel. This gives tourists vacationing in Sharm a number of advantages. First: the so-called Sinai visa. It is free and gives the right to make excursions to Israel. The second advantage: coral reefs off the coast of Sharma recognized as the most beautiful in the Red Sea. In some places, a steady wind blows here, which attracts surf lovers. True, the last two circumstances (coral and fresh breeze) do not like sybarites. On the beaches of Sharma often need to enter the sea in special shoes, and on the beaches to sunbathe under the cover of wicker barriers. And this must be taken into account when traveling to Egypt (Charm). The best hotels compensate for climatic conditions for those who prefer this place to Hurghada. There are few hotels with three stars. The lion's share of the hotel base is made up of luxurious fives and very worthy fours. Tourists leave enthusiastic reviews about the Iberotel Palace 5 * network, the luxurious Magic Life Imperial 5 * and praise Fanara Rosetta 4 *, Albatross Aqua Blue Sharm 4 * and Baron Palms Resort. Another feature of the resort is that it does not say anything about Egyptian antiquity. The pyramids, the palace in Luxor, the museum in Cairo are too far away. But the mountain of Moses (and the whole Holy Land with the Dead Sea inclusive) is very close. To feel the difference between the two resorts, you can go to Hurghada. From the port of Sharm el-Sheikh ferry runs. Travel time is about two hours, a ticket costs forty-five dollars one way.



Satellites of Sharm El Sheikh

Like Hurghada, the former small village on the Sinai Peninsula has grown tens of kilometers and has acquired suburbs. Each of them has its own specialization and takes a certain contingent of vacationers. Those who come here for diving and admiring the underwater world of the Red Sea stop in Nabq or in the immediate vicinity of the Ras Mohammed National Park. Sybarites, perceiving corals only as a threat to scratch their legs, choose Sharm El Maya Bay. Its advantage is that there is natural sand everywhere, both on the beach and at the bottom. Sites completely cleared of corals are also found in Naama Bay. But in Sharks Bay (which means "Bay of Sharks") and in Ras Umm al-Sid, the reefs begin almost from the threshold of the hotel complex. For lovers of nightlife coming to Egypt, Sharm, 5 * hotels offer a varied entertainment program. But if you want to walk outside the walls of the hotels, it is best to choose the Naama Bay area for relaxation. This is the oldest part of the resort, and the tourist infrastructure here is best developed. But the city itself with its Arabic flavor is located in Sharm El Maya. Well, in which village is our hotel Barcelo Tiran Sharm 5 * located? Reviews indicate that it is located in Nabq, in the northeast of Sharm El Sheikh. Nearby is the national reserve of the same name, and directly opposite the island of Tiran.

What is Nabq

So, when arriving on a charter flight, be prepared for the fact that you will be the last to be brought to the Barcelo Tiran Sharm 5 * hotel. But immediately, right from the plane, you will take a fascinating sightseeing tour of the hotels of Sharm el-Sheikh. You can also independently get out to the Old City or the center of entertainment Naama Bay. Minibuses shuttle without a schedule, but quite often, travel in them costs one and a half pounds. Features of the village of Nabq

The beaches of Sharm El Sheikh amaze with its diversity. The sand in Naama Bay is replaced by reefs right on the shore in Shark Bay. Ras Umm al-Side has a very steep entry into the sea. In Ras Nazran and Pasha Bay generally pontoons and ladders into the water. This must be borne in mind when going on vacation with young children. Nabq is the mecca of surfers and divers. The open coast without bays is determined by the fact that there is where the wind can roam. In the immediate vicinity of the sandy beach, corals grow. The bottom is very shallow. This circumstance allows you to relax in Nabq with children. They like to dabble in shallow water. Another feature of the local coast are significant ebbs and flows. Sometimes you have to stomp more than a hundred meters beyond the depths. Sea unrest here also happens quite often. Red and black flags warn of a bathing ban. But the Barcelo Tiran Sharm Hotel on this, in general disappointing, background looks like a happy exception. The hotel enjoys a favorable position in a place free from coral. So there is no need to buy special shoes. The wind, according to reviews, blows only until noon, and then subsides. Corals are on the right - snorkelling and diving enthusiasts can swim there.
