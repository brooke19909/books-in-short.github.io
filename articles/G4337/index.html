Ikon Hotel 3 * (Turkey, Kemer): photos, prices and reviews of tourists

Kemer is the resort city of Turkey, which will not leave indifferent lovers of great discos, hot days and the cleanest sea. If you decide to relax in this place, then a prerequisite for this is to book a hotel room. An excellent option can serve as a three-star Ikon Hotel 3 *. You can book a room in this hotel both through various Internet sites, and with the help of travel companies that arrange a tour to sunny Turkey.



Building's facade

Ikon Hotel Kemer 3 * was built at the turn of the 20th and 21st centuries, so its building is in excellent condition. It is worth noting that almost every Kemer hotel undergoes slight cosmetic repairs, and Ikon Hotel 3 * is no exception to this rule. Its facade can be called very interesting and intricate. The building was built in such a style that in winter, even with a special desire, you cannot live in it with all amenities. The thing is that the doors to the hotel rooms are not located within the walls of the building, but from the side of the street. At the same time, the facade resembles those hotels that are common in American films and are located in the backyards of various cities. In the event of rain, this option can be very losing, but, on the other hand, during the hot summer days, when you really do not want to get through a large number of walls, Ikon Hotel Kemer 3 * can be an excellent option for city guests.



Location

Often when choosing a hotel, guests of various resort places try to stay at those that are closest to the sea or that are as close to the center as possible. If you look at Ikon Hotel 3 * on the map, you can see that it is located in the very center of the city, which can be a positive moment for those tourists who like not only to lie on the beach, but also to see the sights. Regarding the beach, the hotel is also located at a very suitable distance (only 400 meters). For many people who wish to opt for Kemer and Ikon Hotel 3 *, the distance from the airport to the hotel is, among other things, very important. In order to get to Kemer, it is best to fly to Antalya, and from there go by bus or taxi to the desired resort. The distance is 55 km. You can‚Äôt call this distance great, but it will certainly take about an hour from the airport to the hotel.

Hotel travel options

Kemer is one of the best resorts in Turkey. And even the fact that there is no airport in it does not scare away potential visitors who want to relax on the shores of the blue Mediterranean Sea. In order to get to Kemer, the best option would be to fly by plane to Antalya. This can be done from virtually every major city in the country. If this is not possible, then you should fly in transit through Moscow. From the capital of the Russian Federation to Antalya, you can fly with both permanent flights and charter flights. Upon arrival in Antalya, you will have several options for how to get to the hotel. The first method is the most budgetary and consists in going to a bus stop and taking a bus to Kemer. The second way is to take a taxi to the selected resort. You can‚Äôt call a taxi in Kemer cheap, so you need to be prepared to pay a tidy sum for this service. The hotel provides for an extra charge the opportunity to order a taxi directly to the airport, which will take you to the complex. The total cost of an airplane and travel to a vacation spot can be around $ 500.





Service provided at the hotel

Hotel facilities and services are what enable them to compete with each other. This is true for such a resort place as Turkey, Kemer. 3 * hotels have the same list of services that guests can use. As standard services from Ikon Hotel you can get free parking, free internet and cable TV in your room, as well as the opportunity to plunge into the outdoor pool. In addition, the hotel tries to make the life of its guests as simple as possible. For this, it was decided to provide them with additional services. These include the ability to exchange money without leaving the hotel. The exchange rate is not too different from the state, so do not think that they can deceive you here. For those who do not want to walk around the city, car rental is provided. True, for an additional payment, therefore, the expediency of such expenses will need to be thought in advance. For check-in guests who do not want to trouble themselves with bus trips, a taxi from the airport to the hotel service is provided. A taxi driver will take you directly to the hotel entrance, and you can save extra time in order to relax by the pool or in your room.



Room Conditions

This hotel and its rooms are not too different from those located in such a resort area as Turkey, Kemer. 4 star hotels or five-star hotels, of course, have much more spacious rooms, but the cost of living in them is much more expensive. If many hotels have rooms of different classes, then in Ikon Hotel 3 * all rooms are standard. The number of rooms can not be called large, but at the same time it is standard for Kemer hotels. There are 43 of them. In the room you can use free cable TV and wireless internet. In addition, each of them is cleaned daily, and bedding is changed on the beds. The bathroom does not have an impressive size. Moreover, the toilet and shower are combined. In the bathroom you can find a clean bath towel and a hairdryer for drying hair. Each window offers excellent sea views. The proximity of Ikon Hotel Kemer 3 * from the sea is an absolute plus for those people who do not want to stay in the hotel for long, but want to spend most of their time lying on the beach and getting a Mediterranean tan.

Sports and recreation

For those people who want to not just relax, sitting on the bed of the room and watching TV, there are a number of sports activities for spending time. Firstly, the hotel has an excellent swimming pool, where you can swim and get a wonderful sunny tan. Guests can relax in the hotel‚Äôs sauna. Both the first and second options are absolutely free when booking a room. If the option with a pool does not suit you, then you can always go to the sea. It is very simple to do this, since the distance between the hotel and the beach of Kemer does not exceed half a kilometer. Ikon Hotel also has a games room. Here you can play table tennis or billiards, as well as compete in accuracy with other guests in the game of darts. The hotel took care of the smallest guests. For them, there is a shallow pool where there is no threat to children, as well as a play area where the child can play a lot, while parents go about their business.





Nutrition and everything related to it

Meals at the hotel are made according to the conditions that were entered upon check-in. If you live according to the All Inclusive concept, then you can have breakfast and dinner at the hotel. In standard conditions you can only count on breakfast, but at the same time you can order dinner at the hotel for an additional fee. Breakfast here is a buffet. As a meal, light snacks in the form of sandwiches, sandwiches and canapes are provided. If you don‚Äôt like this kind of food, you can find more nutritious dishes. From drinks you can find both freshly squeezed juices and packaged ones, as well as tea, coffee and just water. Those people who are used to eating light food for breakfast can take the opportunity to eat fresh fruits and vegetables that are neatly cleaned and laid out on the table. The hotel chefs prepare more carefully for dinner. At first you can always order both lean soup, and fish, beef or lamb soup. On hot fish dishes are very popular. As salads, they offer a fairly large assortment of dishes seasoned with both mayonnaise and olive oil. If there is no desire to dine within the walls of the hotel, then you can always find a cozy place outside. These establishments, like hotels, compete for each client, so the food and drink in each of them is always at the highest level. Fans of a drink can also be satisfied, as any room in the hotel has a minibar. Moreover, if in the conditions of settlement the room was not marked "all inclusive", then for its use it will be necessary to pay an additional fee.



Cost of living

As often happens, demand creates supply. Kemer is one of the most popular cities, so a large number of hotels are collected here, the cost of living in which can be slightly more expensive than in other regions. Especially during the holiday season and in a country like Turkey. Hotel Ikon Hotel 3 * has only standard rooms. Therefore, the cost of living in it depends only on the chosen concept, as well as on what time you want to go to Kemer. In the summer, an all-inclusive double room will cost guests $ 30 per night. As an additional service, for which it will be necessary to make an additional payment, it will be possible to use car rental. You will also have to pay if you need to use the laundry.

What to see near the hotel?

Kemer is, first of all, a resort place where people come to rest on the shores of the Mediterranean Sea, and not in order to enjoy museums and excursions. Moreover, the latter are not so many in Kemer. But you can go to Yoruk Park, where there is an opportunity to learn about the history of nomadic peoples who lived in ancient times at the site of the current resort. Here you can taste the food that was popular with people 2-3 centuries ago. The best that is in Kemer is the beach. Here you can relax on a sunbed, and swim in the sea. People can also rent special diving equipment and go diving. If you are still interested in a cultural tour of the ancient places, then it is better to go to Antalya. There are a large number of places that may be of interest to many tourists.



Recommendations to visitors

Hotel Ikon Hotel 3 * (photo above) is quite decent for a three-star hotel. This hotel may not appeal to everyone, but nevertheless there are several categories of vacationers for whom this complex may be suitable. First of all, these are pensioners. The hotel does not have a large number of rooms, which means that there will not be many people here. The hotel will be quiet and calm, which is an excellent condition for older guests. Secondly, this hotel is perfect for families with children, as it has a children's pool and a children's play area.

Ikon Hotel 3 *: visitor reviews

In general, the guest reviews are pretty decent. But there were also dissatisfied people who did not like the service. Tourists note that the hotel restaurant could work and cook better. It is precisely because the restaurant does not satisfy visitors with its cuisine that they were forced to go to neighboring establishments to eat. Among the undoubted advantages, the hotel is close to the beach, which makes it easy to get to and relax by the Mediterranean Sea.

The hotel has both pros and cons, but for a three-star hotel, the cost of a room in which is only $ 30, this option is very decent.
