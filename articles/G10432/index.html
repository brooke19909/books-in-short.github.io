Technical specifications Meizu U20

The phone runs on the Android 6.0 operating system. There is a slot for a memory card, the volume of which should not exceed 128 GB. The phone is sold in several versions, the most popular is the modification with 3 GB of RAM and 32 GB of internal memory. SIM cards must be nano type. You can use two pieces. Processor - Helio P10. The chipset has eight cores, of which one half operates at a frequency of 1.8 GHz, the second - 1 GHz. Non-removable battery. Power - 3260 mAh.

Phone Features Meizu U20 will continue to describe the display. The screen has a diagonal of 5.5 inches. Resolution - 1920 by 1080. Matrix type IPS. There is an automatic brightness control sensor. A protective glass is installed on top of the display. The main camera has a 13 megapixel sensor. Video shooting - 1080 p. There is an LED flash. Front camera - 5 megapixels. Built-in modules for bluetooth, wireless access point, navigation. The headphone jack is standard - 3.5 mm. The phone weighs 158 grams. The housing is not protected against moisture and dust. He is not separable. The phone is made of metal and glass. The keyboard is designed for on-screen input.

There are also light sensors, proximity, Hall, fingerprints. An accelerometer, gyroscope and compass are installed.
