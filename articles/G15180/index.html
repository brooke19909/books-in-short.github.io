A dizzying journey. Mexico: tourist reviews

Mexico is the birthplace of a headdress of sombrero and a tequila alcoholic beverage, a favorite place for tourists from all over the world to relax. Luxurious beaches on the Pacific coast, ancient Native American buildings dating from the Spanish colonization, snowy volcanoes, golden deserts, impenetrable rainforests and, of course, exciting bullfighting await travelers traveling to this mysterious country.





Climate

Mexico, reviews of tourists about which are filled with enthusiastic words and compliments, will delight any traveler who decides to visit this country. The climate here varies depending on the geographical location of the resorts: from mild tropical in the coastal zones to moderate dry on the tops of high mountains. From May to October, during the rainy season, heavy rainfall can be observed, usually in the afternoon.





sights

Beautiful nature, warm beaches, snow-capped volcanoes, rich history - all this is modern Mexico. Reviews of tourists and travelers tell about the most amazing corners of this beautiful country, known for its most mysterious architectural monuments. One of the oldest monuments in Mexico are the Mayan pyramids. An incredible number of them are scattered throughout the country, but many of them are buried under dense tropical vegetation, so only a few are available for guests. Holidays in Mexico - tourist reviews confirm this - will be incomplete without visiting the famous pyramids of Teotihuacan, Cholula, Palenque, Shkaret and others. An interesting archaeological zone located in the heart of Mexico City, where the ancient buildings of the Aztecs coexist with modern residential buildings. Mexico, reviews of tourists about which contain stories about famous palaces, parks and fountains, will be able to provide entertainment for history buffs as well as for travelers who prefer to relax in restaurants and cafes. In other words, in Mexico, anyone will find something for himself.





Famous Resorts

Mexico, reviews of tourists about which are also saturated with stories about the most beautiful corners of this country, invites guests to visit their most famous resorts. For example, the city of Palenque, located in the heart of the tropics. There are more than one and a half thousand buildings preserved from the time of the Mayan cities. Of no less interest are the ancient burial places of these tribes, as well as unique palace complexes. Another famous resort of the country is the city of Taxco, the most famous tourist center. The city is famous for its colonial architecture, chic museums and palaces, such as the Cuaunauca Museum and the Hernan Cortes Palace. And another popular resort is the city of Acapulco, which is able to talk about what Mexico is in July. Reviews of tourists indicate that it is here that the most gorgeous beaches, restaurants and casinos are located. Those who love gambling should definitely visit this corner of the country.
