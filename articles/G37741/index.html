DIY kinder gift

DIY kinder gifts are a good alternative to shopping souvenirs. With a little imagination, you can make a bouquet, cake or New Year's surprise, which will delight the one to whom it is intended.





How to make a gift from kinder for a boy?

Choosing a gift for a little man is a rather difficult task. Of course, you can just buy a typewriter or machine. If you want to please your little son or nephew with an original gift, then a gift from kinder in the form of a ship can be a good choice.

Making it is quite simple. To do this, you need:

  • from a thick piece of polypropylene (5 cm) cut the hull of the ship, resembling the shape of an iron platform;
  • paste over it with white corrugated paper;
  • cut a truncated oval from polypropylene (3 cm);
  • paste the oval with white corrugated paper;
  • fix the second polypropylene part on the first so that the ship turns out;
  • cut two pieces of a length of 5 and 7 cm from a sleeve from a paper towel that will depict steamer pipes;
  • glue them with white corrugated paper and decorate with a blue ribbon;
  • to fix the pipes on the "second deck";
  • glue the case with double-sided tape;
  • to fix on a sticky tape in a horizontal position of a kinder close to each other;
  • stick a shiny blue ribbon over the sweets;
  • make lifebuoys from dryers by wrapping them with a white ribbon or painting them with white gouache;
  • make a flagpole from a wooden skewer and fix a flag on it;
  • using a piece of twine to connect the bow of the ship with a flagpole and stern;
  • decorate the ship with pieces of metal chains and starfish painted with bronze paint.




Baby Present: What You Need

A gift from Kinder is appropriate for any occasion related to children. For example, for the birth of a baby, a beautiful candy bouquet with toys can be presented to his mother during discharge from the hospital. This will require:

  • floristic corrugated paper of blue or pink color, depending on who was born: a boy or a girl;
  • film for packing flowers;
  • satin ribbon, 0.3 cm thick;
  • Toothpicks
  • cling film;
  • a piece of aluminum or plastic pipe with a diameter of 1 cm and a length of 28 cm;
  • masking tape;
  • compass;
  • scissors;
  • polyurethane foam
  • plain cardboard of the desired color;
  • 2 m packing tape.

DIY gift from kinder surprises: step-by-step instructions

Making a bouquet of sweets is as follows:

  • polyurethane foam is applied on a plastic film with a thick layer and allowed to dry for a day;
  • cut out with a clerical knife a circle with a diameter of 10 cm and a height of -5 cm;
  • 2 rectangles 27 by 17 cm in size are cut from corrugated paper;
  • glue the base;
  • the aluminum tube is tightly wrapped with a beautiful ribbon using a hot gun;
  • the end of the pipe is screwed into the middle of the base;
  • cardboard semicircles with a diameter of 10 cm are cut according to the number of kinder surprises;
  • paste each of them with masking tape on both sides;
  • rosettes are glued from these blanks with a heat gun;
  • allow to dry for 5 minutes;
  • on a square piece of cling film with a side length 4-5 times longer than the length of the "egg" put kinder;
  • wrap the egg and cut the film so that there is a tail 4 cm in size;
  • on a toothpick greased with glue, wind the tail from the film;
  • they put a kinder into a socket;
  • a strip of 16 by 5 cm is cut from white corrugated paper and a ‚Äúruffle‚Äù is made along the edge with your fingers;
  • glue it to the kinder using a heat gun;
  • a rectangle measuring 22 by 50 cm is cut from dark blue corrugated paper;
  • hands make a ruffle on the upper edge;
  • glue the part to the workpiece;
  • tightly bandaged with a ribbon with a bow.




Sweet "bouquet" for the birth of a baby: assembly

When all the details are ready, begin to combine them together to get a gift from the Kinder. Assembly is carried out as follows:

  • a rattle toy is fixed in the center of the base, and "flowers" -kinders are stuck around;
  • 10 rectangles 10 by 5 cm are cut out from a colored film or corrugated paper, each of them is folded four times and the corner is glued to the toothpick so that the decoration is obtained;
  • thin ribbons are cut into 10 cm, connect the ends;
  • glue the resulting loops to a toothpick;
  • fill the voids between sweets made by decorations.

"Sweet Heart": what you need

Making gifts from Kinder with your own hands is not as difficult as it might seem at first glance. For example, for Valentine's Day you can make a "sweet heart". To do this, you will need:

  • empty square egg packaging;
  • a piece of shiny red or pink fabric;
  • packing tapes;
  • bow;
  • corrugated paper;
  • cardboard;
  • a large sheet of paper for cutting stencil;
  • pencil;
  • scissors;
  • glue gun;
  • some kinder surprises.




Sweet Heart: Work Order

A gift from Kinder for Valentine's Day is made as follows:

  • a heart is cut out of a sheet of cardboard;
  • using it as a stencil, cut off excess from the packing of eggs;
  • paste a shiny cloth over the egg packaging;
  • tuck up excess tissue under the packaging;
  • glue is applied to the bottom of the recesses and a cardboard heart is glued;
  • a long strip of 7-8 cm wide is cut out of cardboard, from which a side will then be made;
  • connect it to the heart base;
  • from corrugated paper of white and pink (red) colors cut out strips 2 cm wider than the rim;
  • on the upper edge they form a ruffle;
  • stick strips from the outside and from the inside of the side;
  • decorate the heart with a ribbon;
  • fasten a bow;
  • lay out kinder surprises.

Holiday clock

Those who go to visit the house where there are children in the first days of January will need New Year's gifts from Kinder. For example, a watch with a surprise can be a great option.

At hand you need to have:

  • 14 bars of kinder;
  • bronze light beads;
  • 3 bars "Kinder Chocolate with 3 cereals";
  • 4 candy coins;
  • New Year's card
  • Scotch
  • cardboard;
  • cones, pine twigs, spangles and other New Year's decor;
  • paper for pasting to your taste light shades.




How is the holiday watch going?

Sweet gifts from Kinder are collected using adhesive tape and a glue gun. In this case, the following operations are performed:

  • two parts are cut out of white cardboard in the form of an elongated pentagon;
  • 3 bars of ‚ÄúKinder Chocolate with 3 cereals‚Äù are connected with adhesive tape to make the base of the watch;
  • 2 rectangles are cut out of cardboard with a length twice as long as the length of one upper side of the pentagon, and a width equal to the size of the bar of the kinder;
  • one of them is folded in half to make the roof of the watch in the form of a hut, and the second is cut in half;
  • all parts are glued with decorative paper;
  • in one of the pentagons cut a round hole for the dial;
  • stick a card on the back;
  • make out a dial;
  • glue parts to make a watch in the form of a hut;
  • fix them on a candy base;
  • two parallel strips of double-sided tape are glued to the roof;
  • close to each other and fasten bars of kinder on a scotch tape;
  • Beads are glued around the dial.

Like other gifts from Kinder surprises for the new year, they decorate watches with cones, spruce branches, shiny rain, etc.
