Is it possible to get a Renaissance credit card online?

Making a credit card through the Internet can significantly save time that can be spent on going to the bank office. So, if you want to draw up a card online, you need to go to the official website of Vozrozhdenie Bank.

On the site you will be asked to fill out an express form in which you must provide some information:

Then you need to click on the item "Issue a credit card." After that, you indicate data that are directly related to the loan and its execution.
