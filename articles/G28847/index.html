PGS Hotels World Palace 5 *: description, photos, reviews

Description: PGS Hotels World Palace 5 is located on the Mediterranean coast in the resort town of Kirish. At a distance of 65 km from the airport located in Antalya and not far from the city of Kemer, at the foot of the Toros Mountains, the hotel opens its doors to guests from all over the world. An unforgettable atmosphere reigns in all six four-story buildings. The hotel was built in 1994, the last major overhaul was carried out in 2012. The hotel provides its visitors with 822 rooms. The territory is well landscaped: there are pepper trees and oaks, palm trees and pomegranates, in their shadow you can hide from the bright sun.



Rooms: PGS Hotels World Palace 5 has 665 standard rooms of 33 sq.m. 489 of them have two single beds, 176 - with one double. The bathroom is equipped with shower, bath accessories (soap, shampoo). There is also a bidet. Central air conditioning includes staff on time, depending on the weather. Going out onto the balcony, you can watch the life of the town or admire the azure sea.

Meals: PGS Hotels World Palace 5 offers its guests an ultra all inclusive program. It is valid from ten in the morning until three in the night and includes local soft drinks, as well as some imported ones, such as rum, whiskey, vermouth, liquor. For a fee, you can drink fresh fruit juice and premium drinks. From three in the afternoon until half past four you will be offered free ice cream.



The restaurant can accommodate 1,200 people and offers an adult and children's menu. For a fee you can visit the fish or Turkish restaurants. There are several bars on site, including a beach and pool bar.

Beach: PGS Hotels World Palace 5 has its own beach (sand and gravel). Everything necessary for relaxing by the water is provided for free (deck chairs, mattresses, towels, umbrellas). Directly on the territory there are swimming pools: for children, indoor heated, with artificial sea wave. There are also water slides. Directly at sea, you can play water polo, or you can play beach volleyball, canoe or surf. For a fee you will be offered a jet ski, diving, parachute.



Additional information: Those who come to rest with children are provided with a cot and a stroller. There is a kids club where you can participate in contests, watch cartoons, draw, or just dance. There are 4 Russian-speaking animators. For adults, the entertainment program begins at 9 p.m. in the territory of PGS Hotels World Palace 5. Photos taken here will remind you of a fun pastime. You can also visit one of the nine tennis courts, take a steam bath in a Turkish bath or master chess. A daily spa is available for relaxing massages.

Digest: Tourists who visited PGS Hotels World Palace 5, reviews indicate that they respond very well about the hotel and the staff who help to orient only guests who have arrived. Quite fast placement is also noted. Tourists are offered not only entertainment on site, but also many different excursions that will help to discover Turkey.
