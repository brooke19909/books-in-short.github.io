Kama Glades. Kamsky Polyany - map. Nizhnekamsk, Kamsky Polyany

Kama Polyany is an urban-type settlement located in the Nizhnekamsk district in the Republic of Tatarstan. The population is a little over fifteen thousand people. The village of Kamskiye Polyany is located on the left bank of the Kama River, 35 kilometers southwest of the regional center.

History

There is no exact information about when people settled here. According to some reports, the fugitive peasants founded the settlement back in the time of Ivan the Terrible. However, the official date is the eighteenth century, at least this is the first documented mention of the Kama Glades, then it was a village. The village in its present form was built in 1981 due to the start of construction of the Tatar NPP here . It was discontinued in 1990 by decision of the Supreme Council of the Republic.



How to get there

The village of Kamskie Polyany (the map given in the article demonstrates this) is located in the Republic of Tatarstan, in the Nizhnekamsk district. You can get here by car from the regional center of Nizhnekamsk (there is a railway station), the distance along the highway is 47 kilometers, the road to this settlement runs through Sheremetyevka. There is another way - from Kazan, the distance along the highway will be 193 kilometers, along the route you will bypass the following settlements: Sorochye Gory, Wet Kurnali, Alekseevskoye, Chistopol, Starosheshminsk.



Description of the village

Kama Glades are located in the eastern part of the East European Plain. They occupy a middle plateau between the hills of the republic. The nearest neighbor cities are Nizhnekamsk (45 km), Zainsk (50 km), Chistopol (50 km), Naberezhnye Chelny (90 km). On January 1, 2006, in accordance with federal law, the village of Kamskiye Polyany acquired the status of a municipality. From this moment, a new stage began in the history of this locality. Within the administrative border there are more than 23 square kilometers of land divided into three massifs: upper, lower platforms and residential area.



Infrastructure

Kamskiy Polyany has two schools, a gymnasium, four educational preschool institutions, a children's music, youth and youth sports, Sunday school, a children's and youth sports school in figure skating and ice hockey, a branch of the Nizhnekamsk welding and assembly college, a station for youth and children's tourism " Turgai ", as well as the Center for Children's Creativity" Rainbow ". General plan

On May 27, 2010, by decision of the local Council, the master plan of this settlement was approved. The Comprehensive Investment Plan for the Development of the Kamsky Polyany Village included projects that aim to diversify the economy and ensure employment. They are based on the development of public and private partnerships representing small and medium businesses, as well as new sectors of the economy and housing and communal services. In particular, this included a number of projects for the construction of a fish-breeding complex, an industrial park, construction of healthcare facilities , modernization of the housing and communal services sector, and a system of measures to support medium and small businesses.



Industrial park

Management Company "Industrial Park Kamskiye Polyany" is a complex of modern and cost-effective enterprises for the production of raw materials for construction, the automotive industry, agriculture, the textile industry, as well as the manufacture of packaging materials. This object was created in 2010. A network of high-tech industries specializing in the processing of polymers as the main element of the national innovation system has been formed here. Full-scale implementation of the project can stimulate the development of this depressed region. Here it is planned to create infrastructure facilities necessary for the smooth operation of industrial facilities, in addition, it is necessary to help increase the level of investment attractiveness of this territory. It is planned that the project will reduce the share of imports of petroleum and chemical products in the region, amounting to more than fifty percent of consumption. It is also proposed to create a complex of cost-effective industries, new jobs, which will lead to an increase in the living standard of the population.
