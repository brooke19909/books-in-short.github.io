Konnos Bay Hotel, Cyprus: description and reviews

Guests at Konnos Bay Hotel can enjoy the luxurious sea and beautiful nature. Cyprus, as one of the most popular travel destinations, will not leave anyone indifferent.

Location

In one of the most beautiful lagoons of the island of Cyprus , the Konnos Bay Hotel Resort is located . The hotel is located in Paralimni, in a corner of unspoiled nature, located between two capes. It is only a quarter hour walk from Protaras and 200 meters from the luxurious beach. The distance from the international airport is 55 km. If you want to stroll through the center of Ayia Napa, you will have to cover 8 kilometers.





Weather in Cyprus: when to relax

The tourist season in Cyprus lasts almost the whole year. That is why Konnos Bay beach is popular at all times of the year. Nevertheless, each season has its own specific features. So, for example, the "high season" begins in May. At this time, the weather in Cyprus is so warm that the air temperature in the room becomes quite comfortable for swimming. Only by September the flow of tourists begins to decline, which is associated with a slight cooling.

The velvet season is characterized by a comfortable air temperature, which is kept at 25 degrees. This is an ideal mode for those who want to enjoy excursions and walks. Nevertheless, sharp changes in temperature are often observed. That is why there are not so many tourists at the resort at this time.

"Low season" is a winter-autumn period. The weather at this time is quite cool, but quite sunny and quiet. As for a beach holiday, at this time it is impossible, since the temperature of the water in the sea is low. This time of year is suitable for excursions and a relaxing holiday in the bosom of Cypriot nature.

Rooms

Konnos Bay has provided guests with a comfortable stay. The following options are available for placement:

  • Studio is an open-plan room, in which, in addition to the bedroom, there is also a small kitchen area. The apartment can accommodate up to three guests. It has a private balcony.
  • The apartments have a spacious area, which are equipped with three functional areas. This is a small bedroom, a cozy living room and a full kitchen. It can accommodate up to 4 people.
  • Suite is a spacious three-room apartment. There are two comfortable bedrooms, each of which has 2 single beds. Separate living room with kitchen and dining area.




Room Amenities

The rooms of the Konnos Bay 3 * hotel provide everything you need for a comfortable stay for guests. We are talking about the following list of amenities:

  • private furnished balcony with clothes dryer;
  • radio point with local music stations;
  • telephone with direct access to the reception and the ability to make international calls;
  • air conditioning system;
  • combined bathroom;
  • satellite television;
  • kitchen utensils.

Negative reviews

A number of shortcomings is characterized by the resort hotel "Connos Bay" (Cyprus). Reviews note such negative points:

  • noisy air conditioners, which, in addition, can be used only for an additional fee;
  • the ants are often found in the rooms;
  • Hotel management sometimes behaves arrogantly with guests;
  • old repair in the rooms;
  • taking food packages is extremely unprofitable, given the availability of your own kitchen and budgetary prices for products in local supermarkets;
  • to the beach you need to go down the steep stairs, and back up is quite difficult;
  • small fish are found in the sea, from the bites of which redness and inflammation appear on the skin;
  • there is no clothesline on the balcony, and therefore you have to buy it on the spot;
  • in the kitchen area there is neither detergent nor a sponge for dishes;
  • the hotel restaurant has inflated prices, which is most likely due to the remoteness of the institution from the city infrastructure;
  • on the beach, sun loungers and umbrellas are available only at an additional cost;
  • the air conditioner is located at the very head of the bed, and therefore it has to be turned off at night;
  • small shallow pool;
  • breakfast is not a buffet, but a custom-made menu, which somewhat restricts guests in choosing;
  • the hotel has virtually no territory, and therefore have to look for a place for walking outside;
  • the beach is always crowded;
  • almost no animation and no active entertainment;
  • the hotel is located quite far from the central part of the resort, and therefore you have to stock up on products in supermarkets for the future;
  • there is no makeup in the bathroom (not even a bar of soap).

If you want to relax quietly away from the bustle of the city, this hotel is just perfect for you. The atmosphere of complete relaxation is created not only by silence, but also by the incredibly beautiful nature that surrounds the hotel. Of course, the institution is not without flaws, but they fade in front of the azure sea and clean air.
