Hotel "Corona" (Vityazevo): address, description, reviews

The village of Vityazevo is a very popular holiday destination on the Black Sea. There are many entertainment centers and equipped beaches. Hotel "Crown" in Vityazevo invites guests throughout the year. The building has 5 floors and a landscaped area. Here in summer and autumn there are always a lot of vacationers and fun reigns.

Hotel "Crown" in Vityazevo: address

The village is located about 10 km from the center of Anapa. The hotel is located directly in the village on Yuzhny Avenue, 17. Not far from it is the Dune sanatorium and the large Aquamarine spa hotel. Guests can undergo treatment there for an extra charge.





Hotel "Crown" in Vityazevo is located on the first coastline with respect to proximity to the sea. Therefore, the road to the beach takes no more than 10-15 minutes with an average step. Along the way, you can buy fresh fruits, toys, bathing accessories and other little things.

Accommodations

The hotel has a variety of comfortable rooms on 5 floors:

  • Double room;
  • 3-seater;
  • 4-seater;
  • Suite (two rooms).

Children under 3 years stay at the hotel for free. The price includes the use of a playground, animator services, internet, parking. The rooms have modern furniture. Bed linens are laid on good quality beds. Two towels are provided for each guest.





The rooms have a TV, fridge and air conditioning. A bathroom and shower are installed in each room. Most rooms have a private balcony with a table and armchairs. Some have parasols.

The rooms have bedside tables and wardrobes. Soft carpeted floors. Therefore, cold floors are not scary for guests.

Hotel "Crown" in Vityazevo: description

The hotel is designed to accept 120 guests simultaneously in 45 rooms. There is no elevator in the building, therefore it is better for older people and guests with small children to settle down on the lower floors so that by the end of the day their legs do not get tired from repeated walks along the stairs.

The area around the building is maximally landscaped and equipped for a comfortable stay for guests. On the ground floor there is a covered parking for vacationers' personal vehicles. She is constantly guarded.





On the second and fourth floors, coolers with cold and hot water are installed. Guests can use it at any time. Each compartment has an ironing board and an iron.

A special children's area for games is reserved. Around it are benches with tables where parents can relax while the children are having fun on a swing.

Recreation areas

A 10-15 minute walk is a private beach, which is shared between three hotels and including the "Crown". It has comfortable sun loungers and parasols. Entrance here is by pass only.

Every morning, sand on the beach is cleaned with a special machine that filters out debris and removes small items. Thus, you can walk here without shoes. Various entertainments work on the beach:

  • catamarans;
  • riding a banana;
  • ATVs
  • hang glider;
  • light aircraft.

The Korona Hotel in Vityazevo has a small private outdoor pool. The water in it undergoes multi-level filtration and is heated in cold weather.





The pool is divided into two zones. In one depth is 60 cm and it is designed for bathing young children. In another, it is 170 cm deep and here adults and teenagers can enjoy the coolness on hot days. Around the pool are comfortable loungers.

Fun for kids

There is a place for games for young visitors on site. It has bright swings and stairs. A fun slide in the shape of a cockerel attracts children of all ages.

There is also a wooden house for babies. They very often play here and present themselves as heroes from their favorite fairy tales. The playground is arranged in a quiet place and is closed from otherworldly eyes.

The hotel has animators. They carry out a cheerful exercise to music for all guests of the Korona hotel in Vityazevo. In the evening, from 19:30 to 21:30, animators arrange various shows for children, puppet shows, contests and games with prizes.

Additional services

The hotel provides cots with bumpers, strollers and high chairs for free for children under 3 years old. The dining room has small tables for children under 7 years old.

For an extra fee, you can call a masseur in the room. A professional specialist can conduct a relaxing or therapeutic procedure. There is also an experienced manicurist at the hotel.

The hotel has a tennis table and badminton equipment. The property provides transfers upon prior arrangement. This service is paid separately.

A picnic area is available on site. There is a barbecue and coals can be borrowed from managers.

Entertainment near the hotel and service in it

The village of Vityazevo is famous for its good infrastructure. Near the complex there are two amusement parks. There is also a dolphinarium and an aquarium in the village. On the way to the beach there is an alley on which there are trading stalls with a variety of goods and souvenirs.

There are a lot of bars and cafes in the village. Night discos work. Singing lovers can take advantage of their talent at karaoke bars. Through the hotel administrators you can arrange visits to various excursions along the Black Sea coast.

Very friendly staff working at the Korona Hotel in Vityazevo. The service here is at a sufficient level. Rooms are cleaned every other day. Bed linen and towels are changed once every 5 days.

The guest house is always clean. Garbage is taken out daily. Workers at the reception at any minute can help in resolving the issue of arranging everyday life.





An experienced chef with more than 30 years of experience is working in the dining room. The waiters are also welcoming and friendly. The maids at the hotel are always at the workplace and come to the room upon request.

Visitors reviews

On various sites you can find many conflicting opinions about the work of the Korona hotel in Vityazevo. Reviews indicate the good work of the staff as a whole, except for the hostess of the hotel.

Visitors complain that she makes a reservation for the rooms and upon arrival often happen about this incident. For example, some of the hotel guests were forced to move to different rooms three times on different floors. This nuance significantly spoils the rest of tourists.

According to guest reviews, the dining room is well fed and three meals a day for a family of three will cost about 600-800 rubles. The menu here is varied and nutritious.





Visitors are pleased with the availability of covered parking. The car is always under the roof and does not overheat in the sun. According to the reviews of tourists, animators work well. But a program with entertainment is provided for children aged 6-7 years. Therefore, children younger in competitions and games will not be interested in participating.

Some guests complain that in early autumn the sun practically does not fall on the pool and the sun loungers are always in the shade. Thus, sunbathing in pleasure is not necessary.

In general, impressions about the rest in this hotel are on average oriented to the rating of "4". Tourists are pleased with the proximity of the beach and the good work of the staff. The rooms have comfortable furniture and there is almost always cold and hot water.
