The possibility of obtaining a tax deduction for the purchase of a credit car

The types of tax deductions, as well as the features of their provision are recorded in the 23rd chapter of the Tax Code. Buying a vehicle is not a reason to get a refund. The exemption does not apply to the purchase of a car for the following reasons:

  1. In accordance with Article 220 of the Tax Code of the Russian Federation, a car does not belong to property, by purchasing which a citizen receives the right to a benefit. Previously, the issue of including a car in this list was not discussed.
  2. The cost of most budget cars does not reach 650 thousand rubles. Receiving a part of the money in this case is not a significant help for a citizen. Only some citizens purchase vehicles worth more than a million rubles.
  3. Only those persons who have paid the tax and regularly make other deductions in favor of the state budget are entitled to receive benefits. When buying a car, a citizen does not make any deductions to the budget. Thus, a deduction is not possible in this case.
  4. Citizens mistakenly think that the deduction is part of the money paid for the acquired property. It is generally accepted that the transaction and its documentation allow you to receive part of the money from the state. This opinion is erroneous. A deduction is a decrease in a taxable base. That is, it will be necessary to pay personal income tax not from the whole amount, but from some part of it.

Shopping for tax deduction

All available tax deductions are divided into three major categories:

  1. Property
  2. Social.
  3. Standard.

Tax deductions from the standard category are provided to citizens by their direct employers. In this case, the employer acts as a tax agent. To get the benefit, you must prepare certain documents, draw up a statement and provide it to the employer. As a rule, a standard benefit for children is used. The amount of deduction depends on the number of children:

  1. 1.4 thousand rubles for the first two children.
  2. 3 thousand rubles for the third and every next child.
  3. 12 thousand rubles for minor children with disabilities. If the child is a disabled person of the first or second group and studies full-time, then the benefit will be granted until his age reaches 24 years. Parents and adoptive parents can receive a deduction of 12 thousand rubles. Trustees and guardians may receive a deduction of 6 thousand rubles.

Standard tax deductions

There are also other tax deductions related to the standard category:

  1. Disabled servicemen, invalids of the Second World War can expect to receive 3 thousand rubles.
  2. People with disabilities 1, 2 groups, childhood - 500 rubles.
  3. Participants of the Second World War - 500 rubles.
  4. Heroes of the Russian Federation, USSR - 500 rubles.
  5. Chernobyl - 3 thousand rubles.

Among social deductions are distinguished:

  1. On drugs, treatment.
  2. For voluntary insurance.
  3. For funded pension.
  4. For education.
  5. For charity.

Property deduction can be obtained if operations such as:

  1. Redemption of property intended for municipal, state needs.
  2. Purchase of land for construction and housing.
  3. Realization of property.
  4. The acquisition of housing.

Obtaining a tax credit is not always possible. It is impossible to receive a deduction if funds of maternity capital, funds of the state budget, employer, other persons were involved, as well as if the transaction was concluded between persons dependent on each other.

Sale of property and tax benefits

You can count on the sale of property tax benefits. If the property was owned by a citizen 3-5 years or more, income should be declared and tax paid. The size of the tax deduction in this case is 130 thousand rubles (if a house, a room, a summer residence, a land plot, an apartment were sold).

There are also professional deductions. Self-employed citizens can get them. Such benefits can reduce the tax base by an amount equal to the confirmed income.

The nuances of tax deductions for the purchase of credit vehicles

The owner of a car can only count on a tax credit if he sells a vehicle. Therefore, it is recommended that a citizen be prepared for the subsequent sale of the car if it is planned to draw up a tax deduction. To exclude possible difficulties in design, it is recommended:

  1. Keep the contract of sale under which the car was purchased and in which its value is indicated. It is desirable that the document be notarized.
  2. During the time of owning a car, it is necessary to collect and save checks that confirm the waste of money to improve car performance. If in three years a citizen decides to sell a car at a cost less than in the contract confirming the sale, then the documents will allow him to receive a deduction.
  3. The vehicle must be registered with a citizen who will subsequently act as a seller. If all the nuances are not met, you will not be able to receive a benefit in the future.
