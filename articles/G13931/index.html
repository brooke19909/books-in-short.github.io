Egypt, Sharm El Sheikh, Sultans Gardens Resort 5 * hotel: description and reviews of tourists

Hotel Sultans Gardens Resort 5 * is a chic resort complex, where tourists expect chic interior rooms, the most delicious food, as well as high-quality service from the best specialists in the hotel industry. If you have long dreamed of the Red Sea and picturesque coral reefs, then you should definitely come here.





Hotel Description

Hotel Sultans Gardens Resort 5 * is the best resort that can only be found on the entire coast of the Red Sea. Here you will find a beautiful beach, many sporting events, opportunities for solving business issues, as well as dizzying entertainment, thanks to which you will not feel bored for a minute.

The Sultans Gardens Resort 5 * hotel has many opportunities both for those who come here to relax and rest from work, and for those who can not imagine their life without continuous movement. You can also enjoy the beautiful surrounding landscapes and visit many Egyptian attractions.

The high status and quality of service are confirmed by the numerous international awards that this hotel has been awarded. They are based not only on expert estimates, but also on the opinions of tourists. All guests will feel comfortable and at ease here, regardless of age and life beliefs, because the hotel staff creates an unforgettable friendly and warm atmosphere every day.





Hotel location

Sultans Gardens Resort 5 * hotel is located in the popular resort city of Sharm El Sheikh, on the very shore of the Red Sea. More precisely, it is located in the quiet bay of Sharks Bay, which is known for its beautiful beaches, as well as vibrant coral reefs. Another advantage is the bewitching view of Tiran Island, which can be enjoyed from the beach, from the grounds and even from some of the hotel rooms.

The distance from the airport to the hotel is only 6 km, so the road will pass for you almost imperceptibly. And if you want to visit the famous Naama Bay, then you have to drive no more than 8 km.

Hotel complex rooms

The 5-star Sultan Gardens Resort Hotel has an impressive number of rooms, which has 520 apartments. The largest category is the standard. The rooms are quite spacious, and their area is 45 square meters. m. Despite the rather modest decoration, the attention of tourists is attracted by the incredibly bright and fun design, which harmoniously combines many bright colors thanks to the work of professional designers. Waking up every morning in this room, you will be charged with good mood for the whole day.

Tourists who like to relax with their relatives or as part of large companies prefer to stay in family rooms. On a spacious territory (69 sq. M.) There are two bedrooms, as well as 2 bathrooms to ensure the highest level of comfort. It is important that all these rooms are located near the main building and reception.

The chic and luxurious apartments are amazing. Entering the room, you find yourself in a spacious living room, which is made in a traditional oriental style. It has upholstered furniture, a large table, a TV and other necessary amenities. Behind the interior door is a large and bright bedroom. In addition to the bed, there is a dressing table and a huge illuminated mirror.

A special atmosphere of luxury and comfort reigns in cozy two-story villas. They are good for relaxing with family or friends. The second floor consists of two large bedrooms, each of which adjoins a bathroom. But on the lower level a spacious living room opens to your attention. Also downstairs is another bedroom, which also has its own bathroom.

Tourists accustomed to luxury are invited to stay in premium rooms. Despite the fact that they are quite small and consist of only one room (bedroom), there is a highlight here - a jacuzzi located on the balcony or on the terrace.





Room Amenities

Even the most chic and unusual decoration of the rooms cannot bring complete pleasure if it does not come with a full range of amenities for a comfortable life. Hotel Sultan Gardens Resort 5 * provides its guests with the following opportunities for a comfortable stay in the apartment:

  • depending on the category and size of the room in your bathroom, in addition to the toilet, a bath or shower will be provided;
  • there is a built-in electric hair dryer;
  • in the bedroom and in the bathroom there are huge mirrors with illumination for the convenience of using them at night and in the evening;
  • depending on the number of floors of your room, it will definitely have a cozy balcony with a clothes dryer and wicker furniture or a spacious terrace;
  • being in the room, you can enjoy watching cable channels on the big screen of a modern flat-screen TV;
  • for a fee you can use the wireless Internet in your room;
  • the safety of your money, documents and valuables will be provided by a reliable code safe with an electronic lock;
  • You can contact relatives located in another country thanks to the telephone;
  • air conditioning will allow you to adjust the air temperature and establish a comfortable microclimate;
  • you can track your weight and figure thanks to outdoor electronic scales;
  • drinks from the minibar will refresh you in hot weather;
  • Tea and coffee making facilities are included in each room.

Hotel infrastructure

The hotel complex Sultan Garden Resorts 5 * (Egypt) boasts a rich resort infrastructure, which includes the following facilities:

  • at any time, day or night, a reception desk is at your service;
  • parking is provided for your transport;
  • there are several shops with groceries and souvenirs on site, as well as a pharmacy;
  • You can eat and drink your favorite drinks in the restaurants and bars of the hotel;
  • at your service - 4 spacious pools, one of which has a children's compartment;
  • water slides and other attractions;
  • The price of a tour includes the use of the gym;
  • in the beauty salon you can get hairdressing services, as well as get a massage;
  • at a bank point, you can exchange currency, as well as withdraw money from the card;
  • the cleanliness and tidiness of your clothes are the responsibility of the laundry and dry cleaning;
  • You can use the private beach, which has everything you need for diving and other sports.




Children's Infrastructure

The following infrastructure facilities are provided for the relaxation of the youngest guests of the Sultan Gardens Resort 5 * hotel (Sharm-el-Sheikh):

  • children's shallow pool, which in cold weather is heated to a comfortable temperature;
  • small water slide;
  • a mini-club where in the company of animators you can immerse yourself in the fun atmosphere of games and entertainment;
  • a children's playground is provided both in the garden on site and on the private beach;
  • Going on a long excursion or dinner to a restaurant, you can leave your child under the supervision of a nanny;
  • if necessary, cots will be provided in the rooms.




Hotel Restaurants and Bars

You can enjoy culinary masterpieces in the following institutions of the Sultan Gardens Resort 5 * hotel (Egypt, Sharm-el-Sheikh) :

  • in the main restaurant you can eat tightly three times a day, sitting in the spacious dining room or on the outdoor terrace;
  • the Italian restaurant invites you to try the most delicious pizza, as well as pasta prepared according to original recipes (a one-time visit to this institution is free);
  • an Indian restaurant will plunge you into the world of spices and spices, and it is located right by the pool (a one-time visit is included in the tour price);
  • Fleur de Leis Restaurant offers dinner in the finest traditions of American and French culinary cultures;
  • on the seashore at lunchtime and in the evening there is a large fish restaurant;
  • Al Shalal is a cozy snack bar by the pool;
  • there is a bar on the beach named after the neighboring island of Tiran;
  • round the clock in the lobby bar you can drink coffee and eat snacks and desserts;
  • In the disco club you can dance and enjoy your favorite drinks.

Several times a week, the hotel hosts themed gastronomic festivals, attendance and participation are paid. It is also worth paying attention to the fact that visiting restaurants in beachwear or too open clothes is strictly prohibited.
