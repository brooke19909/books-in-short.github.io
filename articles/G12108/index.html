Cute plasticine dolphin

At the mention of friendly and intelligent mammals, dolphins are certainly remembered. Positive emotions, the pleasure of their performances - just a little that gives children communication with these animals. Therefore, if a plasticine dolphin settles in your house, the child, especially the small one, will be doubly pleased.



Necessary materials

We offer to disassemble the modeling process step by step. Immediately make a reservation that this work is simple. After a careful study of each stage, you can safely demonstrate them to your child.

It is necessary to stock up on the following materials:

  • Plasticine.
  • A stack of plastic.
  • Knife for mass.
  • Little scissors.
  • A toothpick.

Work stages

So, the purpose of the material is to understand how to mold a dolphin from plasticine. We start by sculpting the body of the animal. For this, blue plasticine is needed. Also suitable colors are gray or blue. The bar needs to be kneaded and shaped into a ball. After you should work on giving it an oblong shape. The main thing is that the tips of the workpiece are sharp. Then bend it and carefully form a narrow muzzle. The lower part of the whetstone should remain unchanged.

You, of course, remember that the animal‚Äôs tummy and back are different in color. So, the stomach should be brighter than the back. To make the contrast beautiful, it is better to use white clay. Having cut off the part from the whetstone, roll it into a ball. After giving it an oval shape, flatten it into a thin cake. Attach it to the dolphin‚Äôs stomach. The edges need to be smoothed with your fingers to get a smooth line.



Eyes and fins

Do not forget about the eyes. After you use a semicircular tip of the stacks to push a couple of pits, fix small white cakes in them. To them you need to attach another pair of black cakes. Please note that their size should be smaller. Our plasticine dolphin is almost shaped.

As for the eyes, there is an easier way to make them. Need a pen. Through its back, just push your eyes in the places that you previously mark.

There are three fins to do. Blue plasticine will be used for their manufacture. Roll up three balls (do not make them large). Then give them all a flat drop shape. Cut a wide base with scissors or a knife on each part, and make the top sharper. The first fin is fixed on the back, the other two on the side. Believe me, any child will be interested in how to make a dolphin from plasticine.

It was the turn of sculpting the tail. Two large blanks are taken, resembling flat drops in shape. Attach the finished part to the bottom of the dolphin. And be sure to take shape. Your own fingers or stack will help.

Dolphin wave

You can not only mold a dolphin from plasticine, but also fix it in a jump. This is done as follows: mastering the wave on which the dolphin is placed. Plasticine is taken in two colors: blue and white. Blue needs to be kneaded into a cake, then bend it. Get a crest of a wave. White plasticine will go to highlight it. The textured wave will turn out if you hold it with a toothpick.



It remains to fix the animal on the resulting wave. Draw its tail and fold on the face. By the way, the crafts can be decorated. Just a few bright elements. For example, starfish. Having rolled up the orange balls, gently flatten them, cut the rays from the edges.

Admire the work done: your plasticine dolphin ready. So, using a minimum of simple details, it turned out to create a wonderful and cute animal. Plasticine spent quite a bit. It is hard to believe that some twenty minutes ago you were holding a soft mass in your hands, which now turned into a wonderful craft.



If you have a desire to create further, and the blue plasticine is over, use boldly other shades. And then rainbow dolphins will settle in your house. We hope that our material on how to mold a dolphin from plasticine in stages will help you with this. Having figured out the process of sculpting the animal, seat the child next to you and slowly explain how he can make the same miracle. If the young master is not yet strong in modeling, you can entrust him with the most simple and minor operations.
