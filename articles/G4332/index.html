Comfort

After appearing on the market, Beeline Tab 2 reviews gaining speed. The people are pleased with the comfortable size. The device fits comfortably in the middle palm of an adult. His weight is also quite light (only 250-280 g).

Pros and cons, according to buyers

Of course, everyone has a different opinion, because of which the Beeline Tab 2 tablet is gaining a variety of reviews - from admiring to negative.

Basically, all users note as the main advantages of the device - the affordable price for everyone, the maintenance of almost all functions of the computer, ease of use, and the ability to use the tablet as a means of communication.

There are also disadvantages, and most often it is a low screen resolution. Not everyone is happy with the camera and sound.

Users have a different attitude to the removable top cover, which covers the slots. Some people like that the cards are protected, others think that removing the cover is a waste of time.
