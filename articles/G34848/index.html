How to make a fighter out of paper: two ways

Which boy doesn‚Äôt like to play airplanes? Better yet, if the parents not only connect to the game itself, but also help the child make the toy themselves. To do this, you need only a little patience, simple materials and about half an hour of free time. So, how to make a fighter out of paper?





What is required for work

It all depends on what technique the future aircraft will be made in . If this paper fighter masters in the art of ancient Japanese origami art, then you need only a sheet of A4 paper, white or color. You can also use pencils or felt-tip pens for coloring. If you want to make a toy using the layout, you will first have to draw it on a sheet, as well as take scissors and glue.

Origami airplane: the beginning

A sheet of A4 paper is taken and placed vertically on a flat surface. First of all, it will have to be folded in half to outline the fold line in the middle. Now you can deploy back. The next stage: the upper corners are folded to the center, the tip is obtained. Side corners need to be bent again (by the way, an ordinary airplane is done exactly the same). How to make a fighter out of paper? Have to bend the top tip to yourself, down. In this case, it should be four centimeters below the opposite edge of the sheet.





Origami plane: continued work

Having done all of the above, you should turn the resulting model face down, and then bend both top corners to the center of the leaf, that is, repeat all that was done at the very beginning. Now the sheet is turned over again, and the lower corner is bent as soon as the angles already bent in the previous assembly stage are given. It remains only from the left side to the right to turn the resulting model in half, that is, along the body of the future airplane. If it does not bend easily, it means that it is holding incorrectly, on the wrong side.

Origami airplane: shutting down

How to make a fighter out of paper? Just adding some little things. It remains to make wings. For this, the lower fold is taken and three centimeters of paper are turned away from it in different directions. These will be the wings. They should be located perpendicular to the body. Everything, now only for beauty, the corners of the wings, the so-called origami stabilizers, are bent up. Fighter is made of paper very simply and quickly enough. You can also take colored pencils or even paints, draw identification marks, inscriptions or paint the model in protective colors.





Airplane pattern

And how to make a fighter plane out of paper, if you want to use other equipment? After all, origami is an amazing art, but with it you can create a limited set of airplanes, and all of them, by and large, will be similar to each other. And if there is a desire to make something more complex, bright, large and similar to real military vehicles? In this case, you can resort to the classic assembly of paper models. Here you will need: plain, colored paper, scissors, glue, cardboard for the base. For this, a model of the future apparatus is drawn on paper, and it is carefully cut out. We must not forget about the places of bends and joints of parts. Accuracy and accuracy in this case are the key to the beauty of the future model. When everything is cut out, it remains only to glue the individual parts, leave the airplane to dry. The final step is to colorize and personalize the aircraft. It can be steel-colored paint and Soviet stars on the wings or the details characteristic of American fighters. It all depends on the imagination of the one who decided to create a paper airplane.
