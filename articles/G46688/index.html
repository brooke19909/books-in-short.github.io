Grandmaster is ... What are they?

Grandmaster is a title. It is awarded to different people. Currently, this term is most often used only in chess or drafts. In the past, it had other meanings, currently obsolete and almost out of use. Among them is the head of the spiritual order, as well as the head of the craft workshop of any type of orientation. If an ordinary leader could get his position in various ways that do not provide for mastery in the business that he is to manage, then the grandmaster was simply obliged to be considered a true expert in his field. It was almost impossible to get the title in any other way.





Chess

In this kind of intellectual sport, there is a whole gradation of different titles. They start from the 5th rank. This is if you do not take into account persons who do not have any rank. Some people are interested in becoming a grandmaster. On the one hand, it is relatively simple - you need to constantly develop your skills, improve and, of course, regularly participate in tournaments in which such titles are awarded. But, on the other hand, all this is very difficult, because not everyone can devote so much energy and time to the chosen occupation. Considerable attention is paid to the age of the chess player. The younger he is, the more significant this title is considered.

It should be noted that similar titles exist in such disciplines as go and checkers. The principle of obtaining the same, but their popularity is still much less.





Order

Within the spiritual organizations of the past, the grandmaster is the highest position with enormous power. In part, its capabilities were controlled by the supreme council - the chapter, but with a strong leader, this measure acted quite rarely. At that time, when the orders could constitute a whole state, the grandmaster was actually the king. But if he was part of the monarchy, he could exist both separately and be awarded to the actual ruler of the country. An interesting feature that the title of a grandmaster has is that this person is obliged to behave accordingly, setting a personal example to other, younger brothers. If there was a need to perform some actions that were not too pleasant, it was the bearer of this rank who was the first to begin to carry them out. This was true both in peacetime and in wartime.





Artisans

Another version of this title was possessed by people who were the leaders of artisan workshops in the Middle Ages. In the framework of this concept, a grandmaster is a person who makes decisions for all employees of a certain group. They rarely did the work independently, being more responsible for controlling and searching for profitable orders, but it was physically impossible to get the title without the necessary skills. That is, such a grandmaster, if you take the workshop of the blacksmiths, could forge an excellent blade on his own. But more they were valued precisely for their leadership abilities. There are a lot of good masters, but there are very few managers who really understand the issue and are able to arrange all the work in such a way that the entire workshop receives significant profit. Typically, grandmasters received a percentage of all work performed by subordinates, but this is not necessary. Some preferred to earn their own bread on their own, simultaneously solving the affairs of the workshop.





Summary

Based on the foregoing, we can conclude that the grandmaster is a person who has achieved some outstanding indicators in a particular field of activity. Very rarely, this title was used in a different meaning. There is a board game with that name and a 1972 movie. Achieving this title is very difficult. Unlike many other cases, it is necessary to really be the best in a certain field of activity, and this must be recognized by other masters who have already been recognized earlier.
