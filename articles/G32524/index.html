A tablet for working with documents and the Internet: an overview, selection tips

Now the market has a huge variety of devices that are designed for specific tasks. Tablets for working with documents and the Internet are not found on the market so often. And in principle, such a category as such does not exist.

In general, tablets are now losing popularity. Someone replaces them with a smartphone, someone with a laptop. Therefore, the niche is no longer needed. Nevertheless, there are still consumers who need such a device.

Tablets

These devices have long been known to the user. We learned about them back in 2002. Then it was decided to produce laptops without a mouse and keyboard. A stylus was needed for the job. Over time, the touch screen was invented. So we got the option of a classic tablet.

For a long time, almost all devices were released for working with documents and the Internet. Tablets poorly cope with multimedia and games. But users needed not only social networks, but also toys. As a result, manufacturers had to focus on such an audience.

Gradually they began to forget about working tablets. Many in need began to acquire netbooks and ultrabooks. Although these options were more expensive, they were worthy of attention.

Changing trends

But time goes on, as always, everything changes. Smartphones began to produce more and more, their diagonal is growing exponentially. Even the iPhone, which was devoted to compactness, got a 6.5-inch model this year.

Many users began to purchase gaming computers and laptops, so the gaming industry on tablets has suffered. And all modern smartphones easily support gaming innovations.

In order to somehow catch up with the desires of the consumer, the manufacturers decided to rework the tablets. So there were docking stations and many accessories. Tablets have become trendy and stylish.

Despite the fact that many users do not see the point in purchasing a tablet with a laptop and a smartphone, some are looking for tablets for working with documents and the Internet.

How to choose?

If you still decided in 2018 to look after a working tablet, do not panic, several successful new products appeared this year. What should I look for when choosing a device?

First of all, you need to understand the functionality of the gadget. Many options may not be needed at all, and some will become indispensable.

Next, you have to decide on a budget. A good tablet for working with documents and the Internet can be expensive. Therefore, you will have to decide immediately on the permissible cost. Either you get an auxiliary device for 10-15 thousand rubles, or buy a full-fledged assistant for 90 thousand rubles.

After that, you can pay attention to the appearance: screen size, the presence of additional parts, etc. The main thing is also to study the technical characteristics of the device.

Tablet functionality

Which tablet is suitable for work? At least one that can:

  • play text content;
  • work with the Internet;
  • run email monitoring programs;
  • run social networking programs;
  • work with office programs;
  • support graphic and text editors;
  • Maintain good quality video conferencing
  • play video and audio files.

Of course, this list can be updated, depending on the individual requirements of the user. Someone cares about the quick work of text editors, someone is going to run image editors. Correct functionality directly depends on technical characteristics.

Appearance

Which tablet to choose for work? There is always a matter of taste. Someone wants to stand out, so he gets bright gadgets from brands. Another important "filling" of the device, and the appearance is absolutely unimportant.

Nevertheless, it will be necessary to determine at least the screen size. On the market, most often you can find devices with a screen diagonal from 7 to 10 inches. For work, of course, it is better to choose a 10-inch device.

Firstly, the larger the screen, the more information is placed on it, the easier it is to work with it. Secondly, often large screens get good resolution, which means that the picture will always be high-quality. Thirdly, the display matrix is ‚Äã‚Äãalso important. In compact models, it is budgetary, and in more expensive options it has a good viewing angle, contrast and brightness.

Specifications

A tablet capable of replacing a laptop must have powerful hardware. Therefore, when choosing, you will have to deal with the processor and RAM. It is better to pay attention to 8-core chips, since they can perform more tasks and easily cope with resource-intensive programs.

For example, the Samsung Galaxy Tab S2 has an eight-core Samsung Exynos 5433 inside. This is the company's proprietary processor. Inside it is integrated graphics accelerator, which is responsible for playing video.

A good model for work should have a lot of RAM and internal memory. RAM needs more than 3 GB. Now this indicator is optimal for working with documents and programs. Internal memory can usually be expanded, so 8 GB is enough for the base. If it is not enough, you can buy a memory card.

Well, finally, the availability of a capacitive battery is crucial. The tablet should work for a long time, because it is always at hand. The time of its activity should play a significant role in the selection. It is best to choose a tablet with a battery of 5-6 thousand mAh.

Tablet system

You can find a good inexpensive tablet to work among models with Windows, Android and iOS. The choice here is not easy. It all depends on what the user is used to. Someone has been using Apple technology all their lives, so they will feel uncomfortable using another OS.

But there are some negative factors in each of these systems. For example, Android tablets consume more energy. Many updates cause applications to stop working due to incompatibilities.

iOS can also cause discomfort in use if the user has never used Apple devices before. In addition, such models lack the necessary ports, including USB. Also, data transfer does not occur through a regular explorer. To do this, use iTunes.

The disadvantages of Windows are that the tablet requires a lot of resources. Therefore, he needs a powerful hardware.
