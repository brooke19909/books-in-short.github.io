Monastyrsky Hotel, Minsk: description and reviews of tourists

The Monastyrsky Hotel in Minsk attracts tourists with its unique concept and high level of service. Here guests can fully enjoy the monastery culture and spirituality. This place is suitable for tourists and business people. You can also have an unforgettable celebration here.

Location

In the very center of the city is the Monastery Hotel in Minsk. The address is 6. Cyril and Methodius Street. In the immediate vicinity are attractions such as the Cathedral of the Virgin Mary, the Palace of the Republic, the Great Patriotic War Museum, Island of Tears, Gorky Park and others. Also nearby are numerous catering establishments, shops and urban sites.

How to get to the Monastyrsky Hotel in Minsk from the railway station? You need to walk to the metro station "Ploshchad Lenina" (about three minutes) and get to the station "Oktyabrskaya". After about 600 meters, you will be taken to a hotel.

A bit of history

Hotel "Monastery" in Minsk is located in the historical center, on one of the oldest streets - Cyril and Methodius. The hotel building is a former Bernardine monastery for men, which was founded in 1624 and operated until the 19th century. It included a church, a fraternal corps, and a refectory, and a school, and a hospital, and stables, and many other objects that represent a whole block.

In the 1860s, the monastery was closed. After that, military barracks, fire departments, and even a prison were located here. And in the church to this day archival documents and scientific and technical literature are kept.

In 2009-2014, a restoration was carried out, thanks to which it was possible to preserve architectural objects and restore decorative elements. During the repair work, fragments of frescoes were found, which were also partially restored. And unique fragments of tiles were found, on the model of which it was possible to produce a unique finishing material for corridors.

Perhaps the most interesting find is a mysterious room in the lower level of the building. He is not on any plane of the monastery. Employees believe that the spirit of a monk lives in this room, who wanders through the dungeons at night.

Rooms

The Monastyrsky Hotel in Minsk provides 48 rooms of various categories of comfort. Here are the options presented to your attention:

  • Single standard with single bed and lounge chair.
  • The single for the disabled is located on the ground floor next to the reception and elevator. The doorways are widened, safety rails are installed in the bathroom.
  • Twin Standard - Spacious room with a pair of beds and a desk. There is also an option with a large double bed.
  • Suite - a spacious two-room suite. It consists of a bedroom with a large bed and a living room with upholstered furniture.
  • Suite - a three-room suite, consisting of a bedroom, a living room and a study. The room is equipped with two bathrooms.

Room Amenities

Despite the external asceticism, the rooms of the Monastyrsky Hotel in Minsk are equipped in accordance with modern standards and the needs of travelers. Here are the amenities you can use in your room:

  • air conditioning system;
  • safe;
  • mini bar;
  • telephone with city and international calls;
  • wireless Internet;
  • TV;
  • a bathroom;
  • bath and hygiene items.

Conference rooms

Both leisure and productive work have a place at the Monastyrsky Hotel in Minsk. The photo of the conference halls demonstrates rigor, conciseness, as well as the spirit of history. For your business events, three rooms are provided, namely:

  • Bernardine 116 sq. M. m designed for 100 people. The hall is located on the ground floor of the new building and has a separate entrance to the courtyard and lobby. Decorated in a simple minimalist style.
  • "Monastery" Hall with an area of ​​62 square meters. m is designed for 30 people. Located in a renovated building on the third floor. The interior is designed in the spirit of the Middle Ages.
  • "Monk" with an area of ​​50 square meters. m is designed for 20 people. The hall is located next to the reception. Massive wooden furniture and vaulted ceilings support the overall concept of the hotel.

Catering establishments

The hotel "Monastyrsky" in Minsk provides catering services such institutions:

  • Restaurant "Lavsky" will delight guests with traditional Belarusian cuisine. The dining room, designed for 100 visitors, is made in the old monastery style, which supports the general concept of the institution.
  • Banquet Hall "Refectory" is designed for 50 people. The room is decorated in a medieval style with elements of modern classics. The hall is open upon reservation.
  • "Bar-billiards" is designed for 40 guests. The institution is suitable for both friendly gatherings and bright youth parties. Here you can play billiards, watch sports broadcasts, as well as enjoy quality drinks with a wide selection of snacks.

Health Center

The hotel has a wellness center, thanks to which you can improve your well-being and change your appearance. Guests will be offered the following services:

  • Gym with modern equipment. Both independent training and classes with a professional trainer are possible.
  • Sauna means a Finnish steam room for 12 people, a pool, a bucket and a shower. Guests can also sing karaoke, have drinks at the bar and order food from the restaurant.
  • Specialists of the massage parlor will choose for you wellness or relaxing equipment.
  • The spa offers a wide range of services for skin beauty and body shaping.
  • Yoga - classes with a personal trainer. Suitable for both beginners and professionals.

Helpful information

Before traveling to the Monastyrsky Hotel, read some useful information about the work of this institution. Namely:

  • A single checkout time is noon.
  • For early check-in before 14:00, a fee of 50% of the total cost of the room will be charged.
  • Children under the age of 11 years are accommodated in the room free of charge without extra bed, but with breakfast and the possibility of visiting the gym.
  • Children under 3 years old are provided with cots.
  • With the permission of the administration, unauthorized persons can stay in the rooms from 7:00 to 23:00.
