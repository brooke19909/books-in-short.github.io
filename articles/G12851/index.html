How to sew a hat from fleece (pattern and instructions)

If you have never sewed or created patterns yourself, use the simple idea from this section. Here, both the rim and details resembling petals are cut out in one piece, so fewer seams will need to be made. Ears are easy to make from the template in the previous section.



So, you will get a quick and simple hat-helmet made of fleece. The easy method pattern is made as follows:

  1. Take a sheet of thin paper, the length of which is equal to the circumference of the head, and the width to the height of the cap, plus consider the bend if you plan to make it large.
  2. Fold a sheet of paper with an accordion in such a way that you end up with the right number of layers, depending on the number of ‚Äúpetals‚Äù of the top of the headgear you have chosen. The example shows a variant with four elements.
  3. On the first layer to you, draw a symmetrical detail of the "petal", or rather, its upper part.
  4. Cut the outline and lay out the sheet in front of you.

The pattern for a simple hat is ready. For the helmet, also make ears.

Sewing such a hat is also quite simple. You need to do the following:

  1. Cut out all the elements of the cap from the fleece.
  2. Sew the petals together (only the tops, as in the last version or as a whole, as in the previous case).
  3. Make a hem of the edging (rim).
  4. Sew the ears and stitch them to the rim.
  5. Make a fastener on the ears (sew a button, Velcro) or make ties from the same fabric.

If you decide to sew a model with additional decor (animal ears, horns, etc.), do not forget to make them and sweep them into the appropriate seam before completing the stitch-connection of the details of the ‚Äúpetals‚Äù.
