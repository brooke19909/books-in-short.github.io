Search for a place for SIM

Since the iPad already has several models, everywhere the SIM card slot can be located in different ways. For example, the first generation of iPads contains the hole we need on the side, on the left side of the panel. Owners of second-generation gadgets need to deploy the little thing with the back panel up and look at the upper right corner. It was there that the desired slot was arranged.



In general, finding the hole you need is the easiest thing to do. Read about how to insert a SIM card into the iPad after finding the tray.

Process description

1. In the box you will find a special little thing, similar to a paper clip. That is what we will need. However, after its loss or in its absence, you can arm yourself with a needle, push pin or other similar thing with a very thin sharp tip.


2. Insert the end of the paper clip into the small hole next to the slot that you want to extend. Push down. Do not be afraid that you will ruin something, act boldly, push harder.

3. When the tray comes out of the hole, grab it. After that, you need to put a SIM card in it, and a special, cut-off, Micro-SIM format, and put it back into the iPad.

4. Enjoy, because the question of how to insert a SIM card into the iPad has already been resolved, applaud yourself.
