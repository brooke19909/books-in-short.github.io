Do you like to do needlework and art? Do you want to create something original for a Bright holiday? Below are some very interesting ideas for Easter. DIY Easter Ideas

Despite the fact that now you can buy almost any decor, making it yourself is especially nice. Man-made things created with a soul carry warmth and kindness and look nicer and more tender than purchased ones.





So, below is a list of souvenirs and techniques for making gifts and decorations for Easter:

  • Eggs decorated in various ways.
  • Compositions of eggs and other holiday attributes.
  • Easter trees, including topiary.
  • Bunnies made both edible and decorative.
  • Wreaths to decorate the interior.

All this is created and decorated using the following techniques:

  • Painting by paints.
  • Decoupage.
  • Application.
  • Knitting.
  • Sewing.
  • Quilling.




Any combinations of both technique and objects are possible. Everything is limited only by your imagination and desire to create.

DIY Easter Ideas: Knitting

If you like to crochet needlework, you can create many different souvenirs and jewelry for the holiday. They knit (or tie) eggs, make bunnies, chickens, and baskets. All this is done as a small desktop decor, and in the form of full-fledged interior decorations.

Easter tree

If you are engaged with children, then it is worth using the simplest ideas for Easter. You can make a beautiful tree with your own hands with children.



To do this, it is enough to put half-blossomed twigs or a willow in a vase of water and decorate them with pre-decorated, eggs suspended on ribbons or ribbons. For this, two holes are made in the shell, the contents are blown out. The blank is washed and dried. A ribbon in the form of a loop is threaded into the hole. From the bottom side under the second hole, a knot is tied, which holds the loop.

Wreath or decorative composition.

With older children, you can complicate the work. Instead of decorating the finished branches, suggest creating a wreath from the same branches or from wire, cardboard blanks. For decoration use decorated eggs, figures of bunnies, chickens.



An interesting souvenir will turn out in the form of a composition in a transparent vase, container or in a simple basket.
