Hotel "Ob" (Surgut, Russia): overview, description and reviews of tourists

Surgut is a large industrial, economic and tourist center located in the Khanty-Mansi Autonomous Okrug. It was founded in the sixteenth century and is the oldest city in Siberia. In the sixties of the last century, oil and gas fields were discovered. The city received its new life as an oil and gas producing center.

This is one of the most beautiful cities in Russia, located near the banks of the Oka. The continental climate characteristic of these latitudes allows tourists to be received at any time of the year. The city has a well-developed infrastructure. There is a large train station and airport. There are many hotels, hotels and guest houses.

The famous bridge of Surgut

Arriving in the city, you must definitely look at the suspension cable-stayed bridge entered in the Book of World Records. It meets all international standards. This bridge construction is due to the need to leave the central part of the river free for shipping. Of course, the bridge is located far from the city center, but it is worth a visit.



Another place worth visiting is the main Orthodox church of the city. He stands on the bank of the river surrounded by cedars specially brought for this from Altai.
