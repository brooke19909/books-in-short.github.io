Hotel Bahia Principe Coral Playa 4 * (Spain, Mallorca): overview, description, rooms and reviews

The city of Magaluf enjoys a reputation in Mallorca as the most cheerful and daring resort. He is very fond of young people from the UK. Parties at Irish pubs and Spanish taverns continue until the morning. And if you are young and full of desire to have fun on holiday to the fullest, you are in Magaluf. Because in neighboring Santa Ponce mostly retirees from Germany rest, and nearby resorts have a reputation for quiet and peaceful places.

Magaluf is also good because from here you can very quickly get to the island's capital, Palma de Mallorca. And where to live in this resort? So, to be within walking distance of the fun, but not hear the noise at night? There is such a place. It is called Bahia Principe Coral Playa 4. This article will be devoted to a review of the hotel. To compile its description, we analyzed the reviews of those tourists who rested there recently.



Where is the hotel located

The reason for the nightly silence enjoyed by the Bahia Principe Coral Playa guests is that this hotel is on a hill. The cliff rises directly above the beach. Yes, you need to go up to the hotel from the center of Magaluf. But the resort's fun (as well as its side effect - noise and din) remain below. Maybe a person with weak legs will be uncomfortable living in a hotel, but a kind of audience is going to Magaluf. And the elevator carries the hotel guests to the beach. To the center of the resort to go five minutes (ten back). The capital of the island is fourteen kilometers away, and the airport is twenty-five kilometers away. Palma de Mallorca is easily accessible by bus. His stop is three hundred meters from the hotel. Despite its hillside location, this is a first-line hotel. An elevator takes you to the sandy beach of San Matias in minutes. The hotel is located just east of the center of Magaluf, closer to the village of Palmanova.



Territory and building

Bahia Principe Coral Playa is a chain hotel. Previously, it belonged to the Pinero Hoteles chain. Then it was called "Pinero Coral Playa 4". Later, the hotel was bought by the Bahia Principles Hotels and Resorts chain. Accordingly, the hotel changed only the first part of its name. But the thirteen-story hotel building was built in general in ancient times - in the sixty-eighth year of the last century. Of course, for the long existence of the building, he repeatedly underwent major and cosmetic repairs. The last such renovation took place in 2011.

From the side of the street, the building seems five-story. But he goes down, clinging to the cliff, like a swallow‚Äôs nest. The building was erected so that most of the rooms had a frontal view of the sea. And this panorama is an inexhaustible topic of the delight of tourists. Not a single review can do without not saying a few words about the beautiful view that opens from the balconies of the rooms. All upper floors are occupied immediately. Tourists who are accommodated on the first or second tier are waiting for their turn to move higher.



Where do guests stay

In the thirteen-story building Bahia Principe Coral Playa fits one hundred eighty-four rooms. Their lion's share, or rather 172 rooms for guests, has a direct view of the sea. Only single rooms have neither a balcony nor a terrace. All of them, however, belong to the same category - double standard. Tourists, however, can choose the type of beds. There are rooms with one wide bed (king size) or with two separate. There are also four rooms with an adjacent door. These rooms are good for families. All standards have air conditioning, a telephone, a TV with satellite channels (among them there is Russian), a minibar. Filling the latter is free only for holders of all inclusive bracelets. The balconies in the rooms are spacious. The floor in the bedroom is ceramic tile. Each room has its own bathroom, which has a bathtub and shower. The bedroom also has a safe, but use of it is only at an additional cost.

What tourists say about the rooms

The guest rooms at the Bahia Principe Coral Playa Hotel are characterized as spacious, bright, clean and incredibly stylish. The helpful staff will provide you with an iron and ironing board completely free of charge. A hair dryer is hanging in the bathroom. Rooms are cleaned daily. Bed sheets are changed three times a week, and towels - when you drop them on the floor. Many tourists appreciated the large balconies on which it was nice to sit and enjoy the view.

The cleaning is very high quality: they not only wash the floor, but also wipe the dust in hard to reach places. If any minor breakdowns occur, everything is quickly repaired. Beach and Pools

As already mentioned, the Bahia Principe Coral Playa Hotel (Mallorca) is located on the first line of the Mediterranean Sea, on a coastal cliff. You can go to the beach of San Matias both by elevator and stairs. This coast is municipal, but there are few people there. The fact is that from the city beach of Magaluf, San Matios is separated by a prominent cape in the sea. The sand is light golden, fine, very clean. The entrance to the sea is gentle, the depth starts from thirty meters. The bay is protected from the winds and waves by capes.

So that outsiders do not use the elevator leading up to the hotel, the entrance to it is protected by a code. The beach is fully equipped, there are lifeguards, a volleyball court, and water activities. Sun loungers and umbrellas are paid, but no one forces you to take them. You can sit on your mat. The Bahia Principle Coral Playa Hotel has one swimming pool. It is surrounded by a sun terrace with free sunbeds and parasols. Beach towels are issued against a deposit of ten euros.

Services

Bahia Principe Coral Playa 4 provides its guests with the service corresponding to the status of a four-star hotel. For business meetings there is a conference room for one hundred people and a business center with the necessary equipment. Guests can mingle in the lounge with a large TV. There is also a salon with slot machines. Free Wi-Fi is available in public areas . At the reception you can order a taxi to the airport or the capital of the island. There is also a currency exchange, and a car rental. There is parking nearby.

Happy guests entertained by a team of animators. In the evening, these same funny guys and girls organize shows, win-win lotteries and other fun. Of particular note is the spa. It has a jacuzzi, massage room with various treatments, a sauna. Clothes can be taken to the laundry for laundry, but this service is paid. Also for the money you can use the Internet access in the rooms.



Services for the smallest guests

Hotel Bahia Principe Coral Playa for its young guests equipped a shallow children's pool very close to an adult so that parents had the opportunity to watch their children. There is also a mini-club where animators take on the responsibility for supervising minors. Due to the mountainous terrain and the small territory, the hotel does not have a playground, but there is a games room full of various funs. A mini disco is held several times a week. To care for your baby, you can use the paid services of a professional nanny. And in the restaurant there are always high chairs for feeding very young children.
