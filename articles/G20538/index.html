Crown Resort Horizon 4 * (Paphos, Cyprus): description, leisure and reviews

Hotel Crown Resort Horizon is today considered a fairly popular destination among European tourists. Located on the seafront, the hotel complex offers its guests not only decent living conditions, but also a lot of entertainment and genuine hospitality. And today, many travelers are interested in additional information about this place.

Crown Resort Horizon Hotel: Location





The hotel complex is located on the Mediterranean coast, in the territory of the popular resort area called Coral Bay (Coral Bay). The distance to the center of Paphos with all its attractions and interesting places is 11 kilometers. There is a bus stop near the hotel - by public transport you can reach the city center in just 15-20 minutes. The nearest international airport is located 25 kilometers from the hotel complex Crown Resort Horizon.

Brief infrastructure description





It is worth noting that the Crown Resort Horizon hotel was built not so long ago - in 2002. This relatively new resort complex pleases its guests with genuine hospitality, high-quality service, as well as sophisticated surroundings. The hotel area, although small, but looks spectacular - in the center there is a large snow-white building surrounded by swimming pools, bright flower beds and huge palm trees.

What rooms does the hotel offer?





Of course, all hotel guests are waiting for decent living conditions. On the territory of the hotel Crown Resorts Horizon 4 (Paphos) there are 215 rooms - these are both standard rooms of different capacities and apartments of a higher level of comfort. Nevertheless, regardless of the category, all rooms are furnished with new comfortable furniture and equipped with the necessary equipment. In addition, each room has its own small balcony with breathtaking views of the coast or the resort town.

Naturally, the rooms have an individual air conditioning and heating system. From household appliances there is also a large-screen TV, a telephone (you can directly make long-distance and international calls), as well as a radio, a small refrigerator and a set for making tea and coffee. A minibar and safe are available for a fee.

The rooms have spacious bathrooms with toilet, shower and bathtub, as well as a washbasin. Superior rooms even have their own small jacuzzi. Each tenant is entitled to a set of clean towels and personal care products. There is also a hair dryer.

The scheme of food at the hotel

To begin with, it is worth noting that the Crown Resorts Horizon 4 hotel offers its guests different food schemes. You can choose only breakfasts, give preference to two meals a day, or pay for the all-inclusive system. In any case, three times a day in the main restaurant of the hotel called Crown Restaurant for the residents set up buffet tables. Here you will find a huge selection of snacks, meat and seafood dishes, as well as masterpieces of national cuisine, fresh fruits, vegetables and delicious desserts.

There is also a small Saphire Blue Pool Bar where guests can enjoy coffee, refreshing drinks, exotic cocktails, beer and freshly squeezed juices. From early morning until late at night, another bar is open - the Ermines Cocktail Bar, whose menu includes a huge amount of soft drinks, spirits and hot drinks, as well as light snacks and local ice cream. The hotel complex also has an a la carte Rubies Coffee Shop restaurant.

Do not forget that Coral Bay is a rather large resort center, where there is everything necessary for a pleasant pastime. Here you will find luxurious restaurants, and colorful taverns, and children's cafes, and night bars.

Beach and water activities on the coast





Crown Resorts Horizon Hotel is a beachfront resort. The distance to the sea shore is only 300 meters - you can get there in a few minutes. It is worth noting that the beaches of Coral Bay are famous for their soft sand and clear waters. Descent to the sea is also convenient, and even small children can swim near the shore.

On the shore, sunbeds, umbrellas, changing cabins are arranged in even rows. Beach towel rental is also available . But these services are paid separately.

Naturally, here, on the seashore, lovers of outdoor activities will also find something to do. For example, you can always ride a boat or boat along the coast. Here there are catamarans, water skiing and other, no less interesting attractions. And also near the beach not so long ago a diving school was opened. Here, beginners are offered diving lessons, and the first dive is carried out under the watchful supervision of experienced instructors. In addition, the center can rent the necessary equipment for swimming.

Naturally, there are sports grounds on the beach, in particular, tourists often organize volleyball competitions.

Additional services on site

Hotel owners have tried to do everything in order to provide travelers with the necessary comfort. It is immediately worth noting that there is a laundry and dry cleaning service, where you can hand over clothes for a nominal fee. For people traveling with their own or rented vehicles, a large parking lot with a modern security system is equipped. Nearby is a gift shop where you can buy gifts and interesting crafts.

It is worth noting that on the territory of the hotel quite often organize corporate vacations, so there is a fully equipped business center. A roomy conference room provides the opportunity to hold conferences, seminars and negotiations. Here you can use a computer, access to the Internet or send a fax.

Living conditions for parents with children





Today, many parents travel with their children. Naturally, in such cases, the key questions are the availability of amenities for young travelers. And the Crown Resorts Horizon 4 hotel provides all the necessary facilities. Of course, if necessary, staff will quickly deliver a crib to the room. And the restaurant has a lot of dishes that are suitable for kids.

In addition, the hotel has a large playground with swings, slides and playgrounds. Here the guys are happy to spend time and make new friends. There is also a small children's pool, created taking into account all safety requirements - here the hotel staff is vigilantly watching the children. Younger guests also like the kids club here, because experienced teachers and funny animators work with the guys to help organize leisure time.

Sports and entertainment for hotel guests





Of course, the hotel offers its residents everything that is necessary for a fun unforgettable vacation. To begin with, it is worth noting that there is a large outdoor pool of intricate shape that surrounds a residential building. There is also an indoor pool with a jacuzzi.

If you are attracted to more active types of recreation, then the hotel has several sports fields for volleyball and basketball, as well as a large tennis court. Tourists who want to keep fit will be pleased with the fitness center with a fully equipped gym - by the way, aerobics classes are regularly held here. There is also the opportunity to have fun with video games or spend a relaxing evening playing billiards.

Those travelers who want nightly entertainment can visit the local Dancing night disco. After dinner, live music is played on site and a team of talented animators entertains the guests.

It is also worth noting that the hotel often hosts various celebrations. For example, hotel staff can help you organize a romantic dinner, birthday party, or even a real fairytale wedding.

Crown Resorts Horizon Hotel is perfect for a relaxing family holiday with children, as well as for a rich and vibrant youth vacation.

Beauty and wellness at the hotel

The hotel provides its guests with a host of services. For example, on its territory there is an excellent SPA center that offers a variety of wellness, rejuvenating and relaxing treatments. For the fair sex there is a hairdresser, where visitors are served exclusively by experienced stylists.

In addition, the hotel offers guests to visit the local sauna or relax in the jacuzzi (there are both indoor and outdoor jacuzzis). And you can relieve stress during a massage session.

Excursion holidays in the city

If you want a rich sightseeing vacation, you must definitely visit Cyprus. Crown Resorts Horizon 4 regularly organizes island tours for its guests. Even in the vicinity of Paphos there is something to see. For example, children and adults will like Birds Park, where you can see the most striking representatives of the fauna.

Another quite popular destination is the coast of Petra tou Romiou. Here you will find breathtaking landscapes, a warm sea and picturesque cliffs. Moreover, according to legend, it was here that the goddess of love Aphrodite was born from sea foam.

Naturally, this is not all that the Crown Resort Horizon has to offer. Paphos is a city rich in riddles and historical sights. You will have the opportunity to visit the ancient village of Euroskipos, the Monastery of the Holy Virgin Kykkos, as well as the Bath of Aphrodite and the Tombs of the Kings.

Hotel Crown Resorts Horizon 4: reviews





Of course, Cyprus, in particular the city of Paphos, is considered one of the most popular tourist destinations. And every year, hundreds of travelers choose the Crown Resorts Horizon Hotel. Reviews about this place in most cases are positive. For example, hotel guests primarily note the cleanliness and comfort of the rooms - the maids really do their job well. In addition, they praise the local cuisine, as cooks try every day to please the residents with something new and interesting.

The undoubted advantage of the hotel is the availability of sports grounds and entertainment areas, where you can always have fun. And the fair sex responds well to the work of the local spa. Tourists also thank the hotel staff, who are always polite, welcoming and willing to help. By the way, many of the employees speak Russian, so you should not be afraid of the language barrier. In fact, many tourists like the Crown Resorts Horizon hotel. Cyprus is a great place for an unforgettable vacation, which can become more vivid and memorable surrounded by hospitable people.
