Hotel Delta (Mallorca / Spain): photos and reviews

Stylish, modern, four-star - these are the first words that come to mind when it comes to the Best Delta Hotel (Mallorca). There are many hotels on the Spanish island, but it is this complex that attracts special attention. Why? This is worth telling in a little more detail.





Location

As already mentioned, is the Best Delta in Mallorca. And to be more precise - in a village called Majoris Desima.

This place is very convenient from a tourist point of view. Very conveniently developed transport links - just 10 minutes drive to the airport of Palma de Mallorca. It, in turn, is located in close proximity to the city of the same name, which is the largest on the whole island. There is where to walk and what to see, and this is important for tourists.

And all the famous attractions are 15-20 kilometers from the hotel. Mallorca Cathedral, Palm Yacht Club, Cala Major Beach, BCM Magaluf Club, Aqualand El Arenal, Aquarium, Es Portishol are just some of them.

But the most important thing is that the hotel is in an idyllic location with incredibly beautiful landscapes, in addition, 800 meters from the beach, where guests can get on a specially designed free bus.





Service

The Majorca Best Delta Hotel has everything that a hotel of this level should have. There is free high-speed Wi-Fi, private parking, a luggage room, a tour desk, a currency exchange service, and a 24-hour reception. In addition, the hotel has a laundry room, a small business center, a conference room, and car rental. Room service is also available and guests can enjoy packed lunches on the road.

A few words are worth saying about the staff. This hotel has multilingual professionals who speak five languages. Among them are Spanish, Italian, French, German and English.

Entertainment

It is worth noting that the Delta 4 * hotel (Mallorca) has everything you need so that guests can brighten up their leisure time. Most, of course, prefer to spend all their free time on the beach. But in another way you can have fun here.

Many people enjoy spending time on the sun terrace by the large and deep outdoor pool (sun loungers and towels are provided for guests). Closed, by the way, is also there. It is equipped with heating, so it is comfortable to swim even in the coldest time of the year (although Mallorca has a very mild winter).

In addition, the hotel has a sauna, bike rental, games room, table tennis, billiard room, darts, golf course, professional court, as well as a fitness center equipped with fitness equipment. And with the offensive, parties and animated performances are held on the territory. So, being bored at this hotel will definitely not work. Needless to say, here even for the smallest there is a separate playroom and playground - so that children have something to do while adults relax.





Bars and Restaurants

It is important to note that the all-inclusive system operates at the Best Delta Hotel (Mallorca). The on-site restaurant serves its guests daily - they are offered a varied buffet with a huge selection of international dishes. There is also a bar with a wide range of drinks (both non-alcoholic and strong).

As guests who have been here assure, the all-inclusive service fully justifies itself. The food in the restaurant is excellent - an abundance of fresh vegetables and fruits, jamon every day, fish and meat dishes, salads, seafood, sausages and cheese slices, paella, grill, barbecue, and much more. And the bar serves a variety of snacks and drinks throughout the day without restrictions.

However, also guests note that there are a lot of excellent cafes and restaurants near the hotel. Therefore, for lovers to try delicacies every day in different places, it will be advisable to pay not "all inclusive", but only breakfast and dinner, for example.





Apartments

The rooms in Delta 4 * (Mallorca) are modern and aesthetically decorated. The apartment is 26 square meters. m, they all have a balcony and a bathroom en suite.

Inside each such room there are two huge 2-double beds and one folding sofa. There is a climate system (heating + cooling), a widescreen plasma TV with satellite channels, a radio, a dressing room. In the hygienic room, by the way, there is not only a shower, but also a bathtub and a bidet. Toiletries with a hairdryer are provided free of charge, as are towels and linen.

Cost

What about the price? Cost of living in Best Delta (Mallorca) varies depending on the season. As an example, we can take the period of the end of summer-beginning of autumn. A week of living in a room for two people in this case will cost about 45,000 rubles (without the all inclusive service). This price includes 10% VAT, but additionally you will have to pay a tourist fee. It is 1.65 euros per day per person.

If guests intend to pay for the all-inclusive service, then they will have to pay an order of magnitude more - about 75,000 rubles. You can order three meals a day, then it will come out about 20 thousand cheaper.
