Cheap Ekaterinburg hotels: review, rating and reviews

The administrative center of the Sverdlovsk region - the city of Yekaterinburg is a large commercial, industrial and cultural center of the Urals. It was built on the eastern slope of the Middle Urals, on the banks of the Iset River, which is a tributary of Tobol.

The city, remote from Moscow at a distance of 1667 kilometers, has two airports, a subway. The population of the city is more than 1.3 million inhabitants. In this article we will provide you with a description and reviews of hotels in Ekaterinburg. Cheap (budget) hostels also deserve the attention of travelers. We made a small rating.

1st place: Kopeyka hostel (6 Henri Barbusse St.)

For the guests of this hostel, located in the city center, the most comfortable living conditions at a minimum price are presented. Rooms are represented by several general categories. Almost all rooms have amenities, some have washbasins. In the eight-bedded room, guests can use the shower.

There is no restaurant, but there is a common kitchen in which the necessary utensils and appliances for cooking are prepared. The hostel is located next to the embankment of the Iset River, the Institute of Communications, public transport, several metro stations. From here you can easily get anywhere in the city.

According to the reviews of residents, the hostel has all the conditions for a comfortable stay for several days. It is clean and comfortable, pleased with the opportunity to cook their own food.

The cost of living is from 300 rubles per day.

3rd place: Podushkinn (8 Soyuznaya St.)

A new and modern hotel Podushkinn opened in the Chkalovsky district of the city. This cheap hotel in Yekaterinburg offers 10 comfortable rooms. All of them are equipped with single bunk beds, modern functional appliances. Towels and bed linen are available. Personal items are stored in individual lockers that are locked.

The hotel has a 24-hour kitchen, where you can cook your own food. If you do not have enough time or do not want to cook, you can order a delicious and hearty breakfast for an additional fee. Car parking is free.

In 600 meters is the South Bus Station, as well as a dining room, cafe, shopping and entertainment center, shops. The distance to the airport is about 15 km, to the railway station the road will take no more than 10 minutes.

According to tourists, Podushkinn is a stylish cheap hotel in Yekaterinburg. Accommodation prices start at 500 rubles per day. The rooms are spacious and clean with comfortably arranged comfortable furniture.
