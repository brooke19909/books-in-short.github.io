How to find out where to pour air conditioning in a washing machine?

Situations are different. For what reason, a person may not know where to pour air conditioning in the washing machine and pour powder, is not so important. Perhaps he is temporarily living in these apartments or recently acquired a new model for himself. But washing is still necessary. How to find out where in the washing machine to fill in the air conditioner, and where to pour the powder? There are three ways to do this:

  • study the instructions that came with the device;
  • inspect the device itself;
  • check in practice.

Read the instructions

The first method is absolutely logical, but not so beloved by our compatriots - read the instructions! It is not just written there, but often painted, where the containers for loading the powder and conditioner are located and how much it is necessary to pour and pour these funds there.





But if the instructions are gone somewhere or the easy way is not for you, then you should carefully inspect your household appliance. As a rule, powder compartments and air conditioning are in one place.

Inspect the washing machine

Most often, for models with vertical loading of laundry, the powder and rinse aid compartment is located on the inside of the lid (for example, Indesit) . And for machines with horizontal loading - on the front panel in the upper left corner, less often in the right. In any case, they are quite clearly visible.





How to understand which concrete container to pour air conditioning into?

  1. It can have an icon in the form of a flower, etc. For example, to understand where to pour the air conditioner in the LG washing machine, simply, the compartment for this detergent is blue. With the rinse aid container closed, the fill hole may be marked with a blue or blue rim.
  2. Typically, a container for an air conditioner is noticeably smaller than the other two (for powder) and differs in structure (for example, it may have a grill inside). In some models of washing machines, this is a closed container with a small opening.

Note! In models where the compartment in question is easily removed by pressing the button in the middle (for example, Bosch or Siemens), it must be washed periodically. Otherwise, gradually the holes through which the water with the detergents withdrawn during washing leaves it may become clogged. In this case, they will not completely enter the drum with the laundry and, accordingly, the washing quality will be reduced.

If it is impossible to completely remove this tray (for example, as in the Samsung washing machine), then the capacity for the air conditioner is precisely removed for washing. This is another sign by which you can understand where to pour air conditioning in the Samsung washing machine.

Check in practice

If we distinguish the compartment where the air conditioner is poured into the washing machine, it does not succeed in appearance, the practical method remains. It is necessary to fill all the compartments with a small amount of solution and start the pre-wash mode without laundry. The first, respectively, will empty the compartment for pre-soaking (I), the second - for the main wash (II). Well, the third - this is the desired container for the air conditioner.

In the process of executing the program, the machine will have to open the compartment and watch the sequence of emptying the compartments. It is also worth remembering that not all washing programs include rinsing cycles with the solution intended for this (for example, using the Down Jackets program in Bosch and Siemens models). Therefore, sometimes after completion of the program, the poured product may remain in the compartment. Description and features of program execution for each model are indicated in the instructions.





Conclusion

Even if for some reason a person does not use a liquid rinse (it doesn‚Äôt fundamentally consider it necessary or one of the family members is allergic to it), it is necessary to figure out where to pour the air conditioner in the washing machine. Since the powder, misunderstood in the wrong compartment, will lead to incomplete washing of washed clothing. This may necessitate re-washing or additional rinsing of the laundry.
