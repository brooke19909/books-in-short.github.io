Large online sites and stores are interested in attracting customers by organizing various promotions and discounts for them, which can be converted into bonuses. Types of cards of the club "Mnogo.ru"

You can get the Mnogo.ru card as an ordinary bonus in the plastic version. MnogoCard is just an additional feature that extends the boundaries of shopping.

As for the online card, it is available to all program participants and is a personal number by which you can track the progress of accumulation in your personal account.
