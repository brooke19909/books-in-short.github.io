Which family hostel in Moscow is better to choose?

Shared kitchen and bathroom for several people, a small room, lack of familiar amenities and freedom. Anyone interested in this prospect? Of course! This mainly applies to those people who are not able to buy or rent more decent housing. For example, over the past ten years, a type of real estate such as a family hostel in Moscow has been in demand. It is home to people who came to see the capital, temporarily reside in the city because of work or received similar housing from the government.

Dormitory at the Komsomolskaya station

The family hostel in Moscow, located at the Komsomolskaya metro station, is an excellent option for families with small children. The first advantage is clean air and the presence of a large number of natural sources, which is not so easy to find in Moscow. The positive quality of this place is the presence of playgrounds and cafes within walking distance.

The snow-white two-story building offers several options for rooms that can accommodate from two to eight people. Each of the "apartments" is equipped with a TV, refrigerator, necessary furniture. Separately, you can use the services of washing and drying clothes.

This family-type hostel in Moscow is located in a fenced, enclosed area and operates in access mode, that is, guests can be calm - no strangers will get into their room. Wet cleaning of each room is carried out daily.

The cost of living is about 250 rubles per day, but the longer a person lives here, the more convenient the payment system becomes for him. For example, for one month he will pay only 20 thousand rubles.

Dormitory at the Kursk Station

If a family needs to rent a hostel in Moscow for a short time, for example, in order to enjoy the beauties of the city, go to a concert or a football match, it is best to check into a guest house located at the Kurskaya metro station. The first advantage of this place is a short distance to three stations. That is, a nonresident person who is new to the capital will find it difficult to get lost. The second plus is the nearby metro station.





It is worth noting that there are all conditions for a comfortable stay: free parking, wireless Internet, soundproofing, a kitchenette in each room, a shower and a washing machine. The four-story building also has more spacious rooms equipped with bunk beds, which can accommodate up to ten people.

The approximate cost of renting a room is 500 rubles per day.
