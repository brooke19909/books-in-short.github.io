Villa Andrews Hotel 3 * (Greece, Kos island, Tigaki): description, service, reviews

Kos is a small picturesque island of the Dodecanese group, owned by Greece. The island is located in the Mediterranean Sea. Kos is located near the coast of Turkey, namely the tourist city of Bodrum, which allows tourists to easily get to the Turkish coast by yacht or ferry.

Holidays on the Spit will appeal to both contemplators of beautiful nature and beach lovers, fans of an active lifestyle. The weather on Kos is a combination of the Mediterranean climate and strong winds, given the location. In the warm season, this is a constant heat of +35 ¬∞ with saving cool winds, in the cold season (+15 ¬∞ ) with constant rains, a break for a squally wind and storms.

A good climate combined with a beautiful coast can create a wonderful vacation experience. To the side of Tigaki is a fine sandy beach. One of the interesting hotels located in this area is considered Villa Andrews Hotel.





About hotel

Description Villa Andrews Hotel is worth starting with its concept - it is a three-star comfortable hotel located almost in the center of the resort village of Tingaki (Tigaki). It is designed for a relaxing holiday on the Greek island. Near the hotel there are a variety of restaurants with local and international cuisine for every taste. Geographically, Villa Andrews Hotel is a 15-minute drive from Kos Island Internation Airport. Nine kilometers from the hotel, tourists can see famous sights: the Hippocratic Tree, Loggia Mosque and Amphitheater. The hotel stands very close to the sandy Tigaki beach, one kilometer from the center of the village.

You are also invited to visit the beautiful garden located next to the main building. Previously, the hotel was called Kos - Villa Andrews Hotel, later it was renamed.

Facilities

For its customers, the hotel offers the following features:

  • Free on-site parking
  • Free Wi-Fi, for example, in the lobby or restaurant;
  • expedited check-in and check-out;
  • Reception open 24 hours a day
  • round-the-clock security of the hotel;
  • multilingual staff
  • convenient elevator.

About hotel rooms

Villa Andrews Hotel consists of two floors, on which there are 37 double rooms with double and single beds.

All rooms are furnished with modern air conditioning. The hotel apartments have a refrigerator. The private bathroom includes a shower, hairdryer, and bath towels, which are changed daily by maids. Satellite TV is also provided for free. If necessary, the guest can be provided with a cot - there is no possibility of adding an extra adult bed. A daily cleaning service is also provided.





There are also 9 triple bungalows, one huge family bungalow. All are close to lush, beautiful gardens and growing herbs. The hotel rooms are in good order and the modern interior provides a comfortable stay.

Hotel infrastructure

Villa Andrews Hotel has an outdoor adult and children's pool. Lunch and dinner are held in the hotel restaurant, guests can take coffee with them at once in two cafes on the hotel territory. In addition, visitors can enjoy refreshing drinks at one of the poolside bars, as well as directly in the lobby of the hotel itself.

Additional services and information

The service of Villa Andrews Hotel is excellent. The following services are provided to guests:

  • room service food and drink;
  • You can leave luggage in the left-luggage office;
  • get laundry service;
  • ironing service;
  • shoe shine at the request of the client;
  • free sun loungers and umbrellas by the pool;
  • help with tickets and excursions in a separate bureau.

In addition, there is the possibility of renting a bike for an additional fee for more comfortable movement around the village.

Check-in is from 14:00, and check-out is from 12:00 in the afternoon. The minimum age for self-registration is 16 years. Villa Andrews Hotel has the right to demand additional taxes from the client from January 2018 - from 25 euros per night.





The hotel also provides breakfast for a fee - approximately 7 euros at a time.

Pets are strictly not allowed in the hotel. Smoking is allowed on site.

Children under 2 years old are provided with a cot for free, children from 2 to 12 years old extra bed is available on paid conditions - 10 euros per night per person.

Conclusion

Greek hotel is considered the best option for several categories of visitors. For tourists who are not tempted by the elite conditions of apartments of the highest categories.
