Rest on the Fortuna Hurghada 4 * system

For many Russian tourists, Egypt is one of the most popular tourist destinations. Of course, most seek to buy tours to Sharm El Sheikh, but Hurghada is very popular. Neither on beaches, nor in infrastructure, nor in the number of hotels, this resort is inferior to Sharm. Both here and there are many fashionable hotels of both 5 and 4 stars. By the way, the latter are most popular among Russians. So, more than 50% of vacationers using the Fortuna Hurghada 4 * system (see photo in the article) are tourists from different cities of the Russian Federation, as well as CIS countries.

Hurghada: A Short Excursion

From the capital of Egypt, this resort city is distant for 500 kilometers. It is the capital of one of the twenty-six provinces of the country. The shores of Hurghada are washed by the Red Sea. The underwater kingdom near the coast is a real paradise for fans of snorkeling and diving. The biggest advantage of resorts in Egypt, including Hurghada, is that here you can enjoy a beach holiday throughout the year. No trade winds, storms or showers will not interfere with your vacation. Hurghada as a settlement was founded at the beginning of the 20th century. Of course, this was not a city, but an ordinary fishing village. Today, beautiful buildings of hotel complexes and hotels rise here, which give the city a beautiful and modern look. Tourists arriving through the Fortuna Hurghada 4 * system will be accommodated in one of the comfortable four-star resort hotels, besides at a very competitive price.



How to get to Hurghada?

Almost all major Russian cities have regular or charter flights to Hurghada. Their frequency depends on the season. From Hurghada airport to the desired hotel can be reached in various ways: by bus, taxi or rented car. In addition, most tours include transfers from the airport to the hotel. Naturally, this is the most convenient option, many tourists use this particular service.



Egypt, Hurghada, Fortuna Hurghada 4 * - rest for lovers of comfort at affordable prices

In Hurghada, a large number of four-star hotels, arriving by the above system, you will find yourself in one of them. Many of them are aparthotels in type. In Hurghada, as a rule, hotels consist of several buildings - one main and several small bungalows. Most hotels of this category are located on the second beach line, or rather, they are separated from the beach by a road. From the airport of Hurghada to the resort area about 20 kilometers, that is, 10-15 minutes drive. Around the hotel there are many entertainment venues, restaurants, etc.



Number of rooms

Choosing a vacation according to the Fortuna Hurghada 4 * system, tourists should understand that they will be accommodated in rooms like Standard Room and Family Room. Standard rooms have one bedroom, and family rooms have two. All rooms have balconies with outdoor furniture and well-equipped bathrooms. All rooms have TVs with satellite channels, a telephone, a safe, a refrigerator, a minibar, which will be refilled for an additional fee, air conditioning, etc. Rooms are usually cleaned daily; linen is changed every other day. A similar picture can be found in almost all four-star hotels in Hurghada.

Infrastructure

Whatever hotel you are in as a result of using the Fortuna Hurghada 4 * system, everywhere you will find a fairly developed infrastructure. All hotels in this category have cafes, bars, restaurants, currency exchange offices, conference rooms with all the necessary equipment for presentations, seminars and symposiums, tour agencies, shops, mini libraries, luggage storage, parking, dry cleaning and Laundries, hairdressers and spas, where Fish SPA is practiced, massage, sauna, etc. Among the hotel services are concierge service, porters, car rental, medical services.

Nutrition

Entertainment

Tourists who choose a holiday on the Fortuna Hurghada 4 * system can take part in animations organized by instructors. In some hotels, yoga, water aerobics, etc. are taught in the mornings. Throughout the day you can play boccia, darts, etc. Sometimes there are theme nights with belly dancing, live music and Arabian food. On the territory of all hotels, without exception, there are several outdoor and, conversely, indoor pools. Next to them are tanning areas, and in the winter, when the air temperature drops to 20-25 degrees, the water in the pools is heated. The hotels have fitness centers with a gym, billiards and table tennis, for children there is a mini club, a playground, a sandbox and slides, a mini disco, etc.
