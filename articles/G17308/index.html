Park-hotel "Ozerki": address, photos and reviews of tourists

Today, every resident of a modern metropolis dreams of taking a break from the bustling city, forgetting about everyday worries. Unfortunately, these days there are very few places in nature where technological progress has not reached. Therefore, the existence of such complexes as the Ozerki Park Hotel was liked by many citizens.



This is an ideal place where you can actively relax or spend a romantic weekend. Many citizens come to the Ozerki Park Hotel for the weekend. The address of the complex: Samara region, Moscow highway, 23rd kilometer, 150a. The establishment is located in one of the few ecologically clean areas of the region. The most beautiful nature, the purest spring lake in which you can not only swim, but also fish, well-developed infrastructure ... This is not about distant Switzerland. This is the Ozerki Park Hotel.

Description

This hotel complex is perfect for a romantic weekend, for an active pastime and for organizing a variety of events. Park Hotel Ozerki, whose photo makes you think about a trip here, is located near the village of Mekhzavod, just twenty kilometers from Samara. Guests can relax here from the noisy and gassed city, enjoy nature. On the territory there is a lake, on the banks of which there is a small comfortable beach.

Many people come to the Ozerki Park Hotel just for the weekend, and there are those who prefer to hold special events here - weddings, christenings, anniversaries, etc. For this purpose, both indoor halls and outdoor tents are equipped sky, which can comfortably accommodate up to thirty guests.



Infrastructure

To relax with family and friends, visitors can rent a wooden gazebo, which has a barbecue oven, as well as a separate place with barbecue, cauldron for pilaf or fish soup. You can come here in large companies - up to twenty-five people. But if guests do not want to bother with cooking, they can use the services of a chef.

The registration desk is always open. The hotel is positioned as a non-smoking holiday destination. Pets are not allowed here. You can book rooms for any period, even for one day, and you can rent gazebos without spending the night. Park Hotel Ozerki, completely renovated in 2014, is open all year round. Its infrastructure also includes free parking, organization of fishing, rental of necessary equipment for fishing, etc., the ability to connect to the Internet.



Services

Low prices, magnificent nature, professionalism of the staff and a well-organized vacation are some of the reasons why many people choose the Ozerki Park Hotel. The services that are provided here, guests generally rate very highly. What is it about?

Park Hotel "Ozerki", reviews about which stay is mostly positive, offers those who are going to spend a holiday in the complex to use the services of a professional photographer who can perpetuate all the interesting moments so that after many years one could again feel from the pictures atmosphere prevailing at the celebration. Amazing nature, rare plants and flowers, as well as the help of professionals in organizing a wedding or thematic registration of marriage will make this day unforgettable.

Housing stock

Accommodation in the park hotel offers both comfortable rooms in a three-story building, as well as separate cottages. The standard rooms are equipped with two single beds, there is also a refrigerator, electric kettle, telephone and TV. The bathrooms have showers. The suites have two rooms - a bedroom and a living room, which have beds and a wardrobe and a sofa and two armchairs, respectively. The apartments "De Luxe Provence" - comfortable furniture and classic finishes. The entire space of a three-story residential building is air-conditioned.



Nutrition

The price includes breakfast, parking and the possibility of connecting to wireless Internet. In general, many people choose the Ozerki Park Hotel precisely because of low prices, including food. Here you can order a comprehensive table - breakfast, lunch and dinner, paying only a thousand rubles per day per person. The menu is Russian and European cuisine. If you wish, you can order diet meals or takeaway lunch. There is also a service "Breakfast in the room." The hotel also has a banquet hall called Sofroniy, designed for forty seats.

Entertainment

In addition to the sports ground, where guests can play volleyball and badminton, Park Hotel Ozerki offers billiards. You can just run on the green lawn behind the building of the building, flying a kite. A comfortable playground is provided for children.

For those who come to fish, the hotel offers rental fishing rods and other equipment. If desired, caught prey can be prepared right on the spot, on the grill. The hotel also has karaoke and a summer terrace. Those who lead a healthy lifestyle will like a real Russian wood-fired sauna. It has a guest room and a spacious steam room, designed for a fairly large company. The bathhouse is equipped with everything necessary for a comfortable pastime. Here, after a hot steam, you can relax by the barbecue area, which offers stunning views of the picturesque landscape. Going down the stairs leading directly from the steam room to the shore of the lake, you can swim in this clean reservoir at any season of the year. And the staff is happy to make tasty tea with herbs and honey.



Additional Information

On the territory of the Ozerki Park Hotel there are platforms where various competitive events can be organized, for example, team building. Most often, this opportunity is used by large companies, especially for which the organizers can arrange all kinds of corporate events.

Around the amazingly beautiful lake there are many free sun loungers. Here you can swim and sunbathe. The beach is sandy, there is a pontoon. You can enjoy nature in Ozery not only from the shore, but also from the water. The park hotel also offers a catamaran rental service. Riding on this unpretentious water transport, you can go around the entire water surface. Many, judging by the reviews, precisely because of the beauty of this spring lake, come to the Ozerki Park Hotel.



How to get there

The location of the hotel is quite good. By car, you can arrive in the direction from the city of Samara on the M5 highway, then turn left at the fork in the direction of Mekhzavod. Drive on Krasnoglinskoye Shosse, and then follow the signs.

Tent and gazebos

Judging by the reviews, many residents of Saratov held a celebration here. Park Hotel Ozerki, in addition to a banquet hall, also offers a tent overlooking a beautiful lake. The walls of the structure can be easily closed, thereby protecting guests from precipitation, and also open in sunny hot weather. Renting a tent will cost two thousand rubles per hour. The price also includes tables and chairs, rugs on request, barbecue and lighting.

For those who want to be closer to nature, a covered log cabin arbor is perfect. It works in any weather and is ideal not only for theme parties, but also for large companies that go to barbecue. The rental price is one thousand rubles per hour.



Reviews and impressions

Those who celebrated in Ozerki exit registration or any anniversary were very pleased with how the celebration took place. Many really liked the professional organization of the holiday, the work of the staff, which was at a high level. There were, of course, minor flaws, but the heroes of the occasion quickly forgot about them, and the guests did not even notice at all. Services are provided inexpensively, and most importantly - fairly high quality, almost at the European level.

Most of those who rested here speak of the park hotel as a wonderful place where you can relax without any noise. Many impressions of the lake, which is amazingly beautiful.

There are, of course, several complaints, and first of all they concern living in a cottage. According to vacationers, plumbing does not work well there and there are power outages. But, judging by the responses of the opponents, these shortcomings have already been eliminated by the administration.

For the most part, tourists were satisfied with their stay at this park-hotel, and therefore they strongly recommend visiting those who have not been here before.
