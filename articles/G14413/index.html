Hotel Panas Holiday Village 4 * (Ayia Napa, Cyprus): Travel Reviews

Cyprus is a great place to relax. Its beautiful resorts are in demand among our compatriots. In our article, we will talk about one of the hotels in Ayia Napa. Panas Holiday Village 4 * (Ayia Napa) - one of the hotels located in the city center, near the coast.

A bit about the hotel ...

Panas Holiday Village 4 * (Ayia Napa), as we have already mentioned, is located on the sea coast of Ayia Napa, near the Nissi area. Near the hotel there is a developed resort infrastructure. There are numerous shops, clubs, restaurants and bars. Within walking distance there is a water park and an entertainment park with trampolines.



The hotel is located on the coast of an incredibly picturesque bay, next to the tourist pedestrian promenade, which stretches from Nissi Beach to Cap Greco. The location of the hotel allows you to combine the vibrant life of the resort with a quiet holiday. The hotel is located in a quiet street, away from noise and at the same time close to everything you need for a good holiday.

A little bit about the resort ...

Ayia Napa is considered one of the most popular and lively resorts of the island of Cyprus. Translated, the name of the city means "Sacred Forests". This is due to the fact that once shrines were found here. Historians suggest that in the 7th or 8th century AD, in one of the caves, the Byzantines hid the face of the Holy Virgin. And only after a hundred years the icon was found by a random traveler. This message spread throughout the land, and believers began to come to the cave.



The icon was called Panagia Napa, which means the Blessed Virgin Mary from the forest. After a lot of time, a chapel was built over the cave, and then a temple. Currently, this monastery has been preserved almost in its original form.

The city became a tourist resort only in the eighties. During this period, the city began to actively develop in terms of tourism. The resort is known for beaches, vibrant nightlife. He is even called the second Ibiza.

Ayia Napa can be reached from Larnaca Airport, which is just 35 kilometers away. Many airlines fly from Russia to Larnaca. The cost of a ticket from Moscow will cost about two hundred euros on both sides. The flight takes about four hours. From the airport to Ayia Napa, buses and minibuses run.

Nissi Beach

The beaches of the resort are awarded the Blue Flag. The coast of the resort is not only very beautiful, but also attracts tourists with numerous entertainments: catamarans, fishing, diving, surfing, yachts, jet skis and more.

The most famous beach is Nissi Beach. During the high season, it is literally crowded with people. Numerous bars and restaurants are located along the coast.
