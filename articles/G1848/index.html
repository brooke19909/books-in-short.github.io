Holidays in Divnomorskoye: reviews of the beautiful resort village

Among the Caucasus mountains in the valley of the mountain river Adebara is the beautiful village of Divnomorskoe. This is a pretty cozy resort, which is separated from Gelendzhik by a distance of 9 kilometers. Large routes run far from the village. The weather here is beautiful, which is facilitated by rocks that extend far into the sea, protecting this settlement from bad weather. And marvelous vegetation makes this village truly unique. This could not but affect the decision of those people who choose a vacation in Divnomorskoye. Reviews of tourists who visited this resort are mostly positive. Here is the cleanest sea with a sandy bottom, pebble beaches, where there is always a free place, and the healing coniferous aroma of plantings of Pitsunda pine.





Despite the fact that the village Divnomorskoye is part of the group of resorts in the Gelendzhik region, it is a very popular independent resort, which is popular with many vacationers. In addition, the culture of tourist services and services is well developed here. Choosing a vacation in Divnomorskoye, reviews of which confirm that the village has a well-developed infrastructure, everyone will discover something new and beautiful. It is ideal for families. A wonderful promenade stretches along the sea, where many cafes and restaurants are concentrated, any company will find entertainment to its taste. In addition, there is a water park, there are many attractions for children and adults.



But this is not all that Divnomorskoe is famous for. Rest 2013, reviews of which have already been left by tourists, made it possible to enjoy not only the sea, the beach and the surrounding landscapes, but also to see many interesting things. So, once at this resort, you can explore the dolmens, which are able to discover the secrets of distant times. These funerary structures were erected for the burial of residents of tribes that previously inhabited this territory. Unfortunately, the burial mound located near secondary school No. 12 and dating back to the Middle Ages practically did not survive. Another dolmen is located in the western part of the village on the right coast of the Aderba River. It will be interesting to see the temple of St. Sergius of Radonezh.

In addition to dolmens, vacationers can take a walk and explore the entire village, since it is small in size and is divided into two parts by the river. There are only three central streets. The first street bears the name of Lenin, preserved according to the traditions of Soviet times. It is with her that the rest begins in Divnomorskoye. Reviews indicate that it is the most inhabited and noisy. All kinds of shops, souvenir tents are concentrated here. In addition, there are several recreation centers and guest houses. It begins at the entrance to the village and reaches the embankment itself. For those who prefer a quiet area, it is better to choose a place on Gornaya Street. There are practically no cafes and clubs, which saves her from noisy youth companies.



Hotels, health resorts, "savage" - all this is a vacation in Divnomorskoye. Reviews mention a lot about the latter option. There is enough space to set up tents. The village has many grocery stores with a good assortment. In addition, there are two markets in the village. One is located in the central part, the second, a small, mainly fruit and vegetable, is located in the western part of the village. For those who do not want to spend their time in the kitchen, there are many private hotels, hotels, guest houses, resorts and recreation centers.

When choosing housing, it should be borne in mind that the regime of accommodation in hotels is freer than in sanatoriums and boarding houses. More than ten mini-hotels are located on the coastline a few meters from the sea. However, prices for such housing are no less than those for accommodation in Turkish hotels. Rest houses and sanatoriums provide an opportunity not only to relax, but also to improve their health. One of the most popular is the recreation center "Arion" (Divnomorskoe). The reviews left by vacationers do not raise doubts that this is really one of the economical accommodation options. Here, both youth companies and families with children can relax. For vacationers there is a swimming pool, sports activities, a playground, roller and bicycle rental, a first-aid post. The beach is located 800 meters from the recreation center. Meals are ordered at will, there is an equipped kitchen. The choice of housing depends on personal preferences and financial ability.
