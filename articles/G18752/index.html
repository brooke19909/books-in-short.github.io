Hotel Corfu Chandris Hotel Villas 4 * (Dassia, Greece): overview, description of rooms, reviews

In 1973, at one of the most popular resorts in Greece, the Corfu Chandris Hotel Villas 4 * hotel complex was built. For over forty years now, the hotel has been hosting tourists from different countries and cities. Despite the age, everything is fresh and modern at the hotel. Indeed, in 1998 there was a major overhaul, and cosmetic restoration is done annually.





Location

So, Corfu Chandris Hotel Villas 4 * is located in a village called Dassia. It is located in the eastern part of Corfu, 10 kilometers from the capital (and, accordingly, approximately the same distance from the airport). To the sea from the hotel - a stone's throw. And this is the main reason why this hotel is loved by tourists.

A bus stop is located 50 meters from the building. From there you can get anywhere. Ipsos Beach, awarded the EU Blue Flag, is relatively close. Another 6 kilometers away is the Kapodistrias Museum. And a little further - a water park. You need to drive 20 kilometers to the luxurious Butrint National Park, but it's worth it.

Services

Guests staying at Corfu Chandris Hotel Villas 4 * can use a variety of services. Firstly, there is free Wi-Fi, which works both in the rooms and in the hotel itself. Private parking and car rental are available.

Luggage storage, a 24-hour reception, currency exchange and a tour desk are available in the lobby. Dry cleaning, ironing and laundry services are available. If guests come with children, they can call the nanny who will follow them.

In addition to the above, the hotel has a lot more. For example, a large conference room, an office with a photocopy and fax machine, shops, souvenir stalls, a beauty salon with a hairdresser. And, of course, here they provide food and drink delivery services. There are even facilities designed for people with disabilities.

By the way, Corfu Chandris Hotel Villas 4 * has well-trained staff. People working at the hotel speak Japanese, German, English, Greek, Italian and Japanese. And - most importantly - they speak Russian. So, none of the visitors will have difficulties explaining their wishes.





Things to do?

People who come to Corfu Chandris Hotel and Villas (Dassia, Greece) spend most of their time on the beach. However, once in this hotel, you can do many more. For example, water sports. They offer equipment specifically designed for this. Windsurfing is especially popular here.

There is a bicycle rental and a gym with exercise machines for full power loads. There is a jacuzzi, Turkish bath and sauna. Guests can also look into the massage room, play table tennis, billiards, volleyball or darts. By the way, parachuting is also very popular here. And 10 kilometers from the hotel there are all conditions for horse riding. A little closer is an 18 hole golf course. Next to it is a diving center. And on the territory of the hotel itself there are three tennis courts. And the swimming pool, of course.

For children, whom their parents decided to take with them on vacation, a playground and a mini club are organized here. So even they will not be bored.

Restaurants

Reviews of Corfu Chandris Hotel Villas (Corfu, Greece) make sure that this hotel is good in every sense of the word. Including in terms of nutrition.

The hotel has several establishments where you can eat well. Take, for example, the main restaurant Alkinoos. It serves international, national, vegetarian and diabetic dishes. They feed deliciously - even breakfasts, which are the same everywhere, try to diversify for guests. Eggs in different variations, cereals, granola, popcorn, desserts, all kinds of fruits and yogurts, hot pancakes with fragrant jams - there is everything, everyone will find something to their taste. Especially good are Greek pies served in the evening.

In addition to the main restaurant, there is an institution called Acqua. It serves international and Mediterranean cuisine. Also, meals prepared in the restaurant are delivered to the apartments.

You should definitely look into the tavern, which is located in the open air. They serve excellent light snacks and national dishes.

There are several bars. One is in the lobby of the hotel. They offer light snacks, drinks, alcohol and a variety of cocktails. There is also a bar near the pool and on the beach. In these places there is also a rather large assortment.

Reservation and rules of residence

Each review of the Corfu Chandris 4 * hotel (Greece, Dassia) gives some useful information to those guests who are only planning to come to these places. And the most important piece of advice that everyone pays attention to is the following: if you want to stay in this particular hotel, you need to reserve the apartments in advance. The hotel is popular and good. And even though there are more than two hundred numbers in it, they all take up very quickly. And before the start of the holiday season, there are practically no free ones. Therefore, it is better to take care of the booking issue a couple of months before the trip.

The hotel can accommodate with children. If guests come with a baby who is not 2 years old, then they will be allocated a cradle and installed in the apartment for free. You will not have to pay for a baby. Older children are provided with extra beds. But only to those who are not 12 years old. Older guests are registered in the same way as adults.
