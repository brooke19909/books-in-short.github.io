Yunost Hotel, Kirishi: photo with description, overview of services, address, guest reviews

Kirishi is a modern green city, approximately equally distant from Moscow, St. Petersburg and Veliky Novgorod. For the convenience of guests, several hotels have been built in the city with a decent level of service and a quite reasonable price policy. The most popular among tourists and business travelers is the Yunost Hotel in Kirishi (photo attached). According to reviews, this stylish, modern hotel is the ideal choice for travelers who want to combine work and leisure. The Yunost Hotel in Kirishi is considered one of the best options for visitors. In an institution housed in an impressive Soviet-style building, guests note the presence of a completely renovated interior, comfortable rooms with private facilities, several conference rooms, a business center with rich technical equipment, a restaurant, a sauna, parking and other infrastructure facilities that ensure the comfort of residents.

Location

The Yunost Hotel is located in Kirishi at the address: Lenin Avenue, 22, in the very center. A bus and railway station are a 5-minute drive away, and the Moscow-St Petersburg highway is 40 km away. For guests arriving by personal transport, experts recommend using the GPS coordinates: N 59 ° 26 '58.3872 "E 32 ° 1' 0.984". The nearest airport is Pulkovo (in St. Petersburg) - 130 km. The nearest railway stations: St. Petersburg - 130 km, Chudovo - 50 km.

The Yunost Hotel in Kirishi is a respectable hotel complex with a wide range of business opportunities (organizing various business and scientific forums, conferences, exhibitions), as well as conditions for a comfortable stay. The business center and conference rooms are equipped with the necessary audio and video equipment and radio-controlled.

The comfortable hotel rooms in Kirishi, represented by five categories, including apartments and suites, are equipped with modern air conditioning and ventilation systems, satellite TV (receive up to 20 European channels), international telephone service.

According to reviews, a characteristic feature of the hotel restaurant is the sophisticated atmosphere created by the institution. Guests can enjoy European dishes here. At the disposal of the guests of the Yunost Hotel in Kirishi there are such excellent anti-fatigue remedies as a sauna, a gym, a billiard room, and a beauty salon.

It also offers residents an ATM, gift shop, laundry, dry cleaning, and a parking lot (guarded). The hotel offers train and airline tickets. Payment is made through international plastic cards: Visa, Cirrus Maestro, American Express, Master Card, Eurocard.

Guest Impressions

Guests call Yunost the best hotel in town. The "stardom" of the hotel, in their opinion, is fully consistent with the level of service provided.

pros

Many people like the hotel’s convenient location (in the very center and close to attractions, the Palace of Culture, shops, a medical center and transport interchange), excellent views from the rooms on the upper floors, and well-groomed facilities.

The authors of the reviews note that the hotel has a wide range of quality amenities, first of all, they indicate the provision of delicious, satisfying, varied and inexpensive breakfasts in the restaurant ("buffet"), the availability of Internet (works in the lobby), parking lots, saunas.

Separately, guests note as a plus the good work of the restaurant (the menu includes dishes of European and Russian cuisines), a bar, a coffee shop, the presence in the hotel building of cookery with a rich assortment of pastries, pastries, salads, fish and meat dishes.

According to reviews, the hotel accommodation is quick, the staff is friendly, responsible and professional. The guests call the rooms cozy, very clean, with an updated interior. Guests note the presence in the rooms of somewhat old-fashioned but well-groomed furniture, outdated (pot-bellied), but working televisions, comfortable bathrooms with everything you need (including bathrobes). Room sizes are not very large, guests say. They can use: bed, desk, wardrobe, bathroom, TV, minibar, kettle. Separate air conditioning in the room is not provided, the heating / air conditioning system in the hotel is centralized. Somewhat strange, guests find that most of the outlets in the rooms are turned off by a special switch at the entrance.

Minuses

According to the guests, “Youth” has very poor sound insulation. Most likely, having settled here, you will have to wake up with the neighbors who first woke up and walked along the corridor, and you will be able to fall asleep with the last one who fell asleep. Residents recommend choosing rooms in the corner of the corridor when placing, it is more quiet here. But lovers of silence, according to many, it makes sense to settle in another hotel.
