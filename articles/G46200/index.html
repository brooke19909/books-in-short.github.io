Legoland in Germany - a fabulous childhood country

It seems that just recently we played colorful designers, collected favorite toys from numerous details, built fairy-tale castles, and now our children enjoy the bright and interesting world of LEGO. Such games develop a child, allow him to show creativity, ingenuity, develop hand motor skills. To make it more interesting, you can bring your child to Legoland. In Germany, Denmark, England, the USA and Malaysia there are such parks. All of them are similar to each other and created on the same principle, but still have some differences in the thematic plan.





The very first park dedicated to the beloved designer was opened in his homeland - in Denmark. He is the most popular, but it cannot be said that the best, Legolands from other countries are in no way inferior to him. If you are traveling in Germany, you are simply obliged to take your child to such an amusement park. It is located in Gunzburg, between Munich and Stuttgart. Legoland in Germany provides visitors with the opportunity to participate in interactive games, shows, shows, more than four dozen attractions await children.



In an amusement park, parents can not be afraid for the safety of their children, because here everything is thought out to the smallest detail. A special soft coating is created for the kids so that they do not hurt themselves. Legoland in Germany will introduce her guests to fairy-tale princesses, noble knights, brave Vikings, terrible dragons, cunning and cruel pirates. The reviews of parents and children are the most positive, because the day of rest here will leave an impression for life. Everyone can find something to their liking and have fun.




Legoland in Germany is a fabulous country with its inhabitants, streets, houses, cars, social services. Small copies of the main attractions of America and Europe are built here, for example, the Statue of Liberty, the Eiffel Tower and others. Both children and adults will like the Lego Cinema, the latest technical inventions are involved here, so visitors get the feeling that they are in the center of events. If you climb the Observation Tower, you can view the entire park from a bird's eye view.



Legoland in Germany provides an opportunity to witness the birth of a beloved designer, the factory will show how the parts are made. In the Test Center, you can create your favorite toy yourself and test it for durability and ergonomics. Children will be able to make huge animal figures out of large details, reaching 7 m in height. Of course, you can create such a giant only by team and with the help of adults, but working together is more fun, and the result will undoubtedly please all participants in the action.



Meeting with pirates, terrible dragons, beautiful princesses will also give Legoland in Germany. How to get to the amusement park, local residents or guides will tell you. You need to buy tickets in advance and it is better to plan a walk on a weekday, when there are fewer visitors. In Legoland, the day will pass unnoticed. Hungry, you can dine in one of the theme cafes located on its territory. Having been in the country of childhood, both children and their parents will receive a charge of positive emotions.
