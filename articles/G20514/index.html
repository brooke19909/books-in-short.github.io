The best spas and resorts of Transcarpathia

There are many corners in the world where you can relax with your body and soul. But there are unique places where you feel a special atmosphere, real magic. Such a region, in which you plunge into a completely different world, is Transcarpathia. Legends and traditions, traditions and modernity, national color and fantastic nature, clean air and healing water. This is what attracts numerous health resorts of Transcarpathia.



A bit about the region

A unique border region as if created for recreation. In winter, when the slopes of the mountains are covered with a snow cap, and in summer, when the rocks hide under the emerald thickets, you can spend time here. Ski resorts, lifts, thermal baths and healing mineral water are complemented by the hospitality of the locals. And there are ancient castles and palaces, waterfalls and lakes, interesting monuments and a secret that always attracts the traveler.

In the very center of Europe (this is what the memorial sign indicates), sights such as the Carpathian Mountains (since 1993 a UNESCO site), the unique Valley of daffodils (near Khust) are located. But the main pride of the region is the sanatoriums of Transcarpathia, which widely open the doors to meet travelers.

The peculiarity of the resorts and resorts in the region is that they are open all year round. In each town you can find comfortable hotels of different levels of stardom, recreation centers, you can also rent housing in the private sector.

The rich composition of mineral waters, which are used for external and internal use, allows you to treat a wide range of ailments. These are diseases of the nervous, digestive, musculoskeletal, cardiovascular systems, blood and blood-forming organs, skin, genital area, metabolic disorders. That is why the sanatoriums of Transcarpathia are always full of people!

Picturesque Shayan

Just eighteen kilometers from the glorious city of Khust is the resort of Shayan. Surrounded by three mountain peaks and the Tisza River, it is famous for its rare beauty, mild, almost windless climate. In this heavenly place is the sanatorium "Shayan" (Transcarpathia). The main attraction of the city is healing water - an analogue of Borjomi, Narzan and Essentuki. It is widely used for both healing and prophylaxis.



The history of the institution dates back to 1952, when the first holiday home was opened here, later turned into a sanatorium. The largest mountain rises above it - Big Shayan, on the other hand a river flows. The guests are provided with two dormitory buildings, a boarding house, a balneotherapy center, domestic premises, standard and luxury rooms. At the same time, the institution can take two hundred vacationers. In the neighboring village of Veljatino there are thermal baths where you can go at your leisure.

"Solar Transcarpathia" in Polyana

The sanatorium "Sunny Transcarpathia", which is located in the village of Polyana, Transcarpathian region, can help people with ailments of the digestive system, metabolism and urination. The institution has a dozen modern dormitories with comfortable rooms, excellent infrastructure, the latest medical equipment and experienced staff. The mineral water "Polyana Kvasova", which is very close in composition to "Borjomi", is used for the procedures. On its territory, the sanatorium "Sunny Transcarpathia" has its own pump room. Check-in is designed for a minimum of 12 days, children are accepted from four years.



Excursion program

Treatment is good and beneficial for the body, but in Transcarpathia you can relax with your soul. For example, go on excursions that can be booked at any sanatorium or hotel. The highlight of the region is the amazing Palanok Castle in Mukachevo. Erected and rebuilt several times during the 14-18 centuries, it amazes with majestic and impregnable walls, elegant architectural lines, a medieval spirit that has been perfectly preserved. The city of Uzhgorod is interesting, in which the fortress was also preserved, there are several ancient temples, the longest linden alley in Europe. What do people say?

Were you satisfied with the people who visited the sanatorium of Transcarpathia? Their reviews, of course, are different, because it all depends on the resort and the health resort itself. Almost all guests note the positive effect of the treatment, excellent service and comfortable rooms. Of course, there are drawbacks, but they do not occur in every sanatorium and are easily removable. For example, vacationers recommend changing towels more often, equipping a green cinema and so on. But in general, all travelers want to return here again.
