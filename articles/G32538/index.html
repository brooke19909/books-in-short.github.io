What is the core in the phone? Complex simple language

When choosing a new gadget for yourself or as a gift, you first of all pay attention to its technical characteristics, in which you can find the word "core". After that, the user, who is not very versed in technology, wonders why the cores are needed in the phone and how much they affect its performance. When searching for the answer to this question, search engines give out a lot of sites with abstruse, scientific information, which is not so easy to understand. In this article, we will dot all the i, answering the urgent question that worries many people: what is the core in the phone.

History

Once the processors had only one core, but the technology does not stand still, and are improving every day. That is why now it is often possible to meet phones both quad-core and eight-core, and in some cases you can even stumble upon sixteen-core devices. And yet, what is the core of the phone? At the time of single-core processors, the problem of overheating of the device arose due to the heavy load on the only available core, so the engineers decided to expand the capabilities of the processors to eliminate this problem.

The need for multi-core

Several cores in the process are necessary, first of all, for the distribution of work among themselves. They are like a small close-knit team, where each core is a responsible employee. Distributing all tasks among each other equally, each of them does not carry out too much work, which means that it avoids overvoltage. Translating this into the language of technology does not overheat. Thus, giving a brief answer to the question of what the kernel is in the phone, we can safely say that this is part of the system that is entrusted with a number of tasks that are necessary for the device to execute at the user's command.
