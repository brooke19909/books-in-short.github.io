Neapolitan Riviera - a great place for a noble vacation

With the collapse of the Soviet Union and the fall of the Iron Curtain, our compatriots had the opportunity to relax anywhere in the world, to travel to the most exotic and fashionable places. If at first Russian tourists preferred mainly neighboring countries (primarily Turkey), then they became interested in more ‚Äúadvanced‚Äù resorts. Such as the Neapolitan Riviera.



This is one of the most beautiful places not only in Europe, but throughout the world. It stretches along the coast of the Tyrrhenian Sea. Tens of thousands of tourists annually strive to this by no means a cheap place to forever fall in love with unforgettable views, the azure sea and wonderful monuments of history and culture. For many, the names of such resorts as Majori, Positano, Salerno, Amalfi become symbols of excellent service and an unforgettable experience.

The Neapolitan Riviera became a prominent place on the tourist map in the 1940s, when thousands of wealthy Italians who fled from the horrors of war found refuge in this quiet corner. They turned this coast into one of the most attractive places, and local resorts and hotels compete on equal terms with the most popular holiday destinations in Europe and Asia.





The Neapolitan Riviera, the map of which is a very bizarre mosaic, includes a number of notable settlements, each of which has some zest. So, in the village of Kassino is one of the most famous water parks in the world, where, in addition to all kinds of water attractions, you can spend your time playing a game of tennis or a volleyball game. In addition, this resort is famous for its diving and discos, while anyone can master the basics of sailing.





Naples is the soul of what we know as the "Naples Riviera". One of the oldest cities in Italy, it is literally crammed with all kinds of monuments and attractions. The entire city center is listed on UNESCO as an object of cultural heritage of human civilization. Many tourists, including those from Russia, seek to visit Naples to marvel at the greatness of Vesuvius, as well as take a walk along the restored streets of the once destroyed cities of Pompeii and Herculaneum.

Tourists who are not accustomed to counting their money, like to spend time on the island of Capri or Ischia, where once basked herself to know the Roman Empire. In addition to the fabulous landscape and the unforgettable sea, the Neapolitan Riviera in this corner has prepared restoration procedures in the form of thermal baths.

Among those who visited these places, it is very difficult to meet those who are unhappy or upset. The hotels of the Neapolitan Riviera meet the most demanding requirements, and the proposed entertainment, coupled with the picturesque area, make you forget about all the problems. The sun shines here almost all year round, and the sea awaits swimmers from May to October. The entire coast is covered with picturesque citrus groves and bordered by numerous bays.
