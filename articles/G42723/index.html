A brief history of Nokia

The Finnish company Nokia was founded in 1896. And then she was engaged in the production of paper and cardboard. After a couple of decades, the company's main profile was various rubber products (including galoshes). But the manufacturer began to produce electronics only in the eighties of the last century. And their business went so well that they decided to create a separate unit for the production of mobile phones. That is how the very Nokia that everyone knows and loves appeared. Phones from the Finns were very popular. At that time, these were high-tech devices, which, incidentally, cost a lot. But our hero today is the Nokia 105 DS. Its characteristics are such that it cannot cost much on the background of modern smartphones.





In 2010, something unimaginable began to happen with Nokia. The company simply could not compete with "Apple" and "Samsung" in the smartphone market. Attempts were made to release a modern smartphone on the Windows platform, but they failed. In the end, the production became so unprofitable that the management decided to sell the Nokia mobile division to Microsoft with all the guts. But Microsoft has managed to finally bury the legendary brand, this often happens in life. And only relatively recently it became known that the legendary Nokia is returning. And not with anything, but with a smartphone on Android. And the Nokia 105 model, whose technical characteristics are rather modest, is nothing more than a test of the pen.
