general information





Pension Energetik is located in the picturesque village of Divnomorskoye, which is 12 km from the famous resort town of Gelendzhik. Today, the health resort is a large complex of two residential buildings, a dining room, administration, parking, as well as health, sports and entertainment areas.

Number of rooms





The boarding house guests are offered to stay in one of two five-story buildings. Total number of rooms consists of 440 seats. There are double and triple rooms with one or two rooms of standard, junior and deluxe categories. All apartments have a minimum set of furniture for a comfortable stay (single or double beds, tables and chairs, wardrobe), TV, refrigerator, air conditioning. The junior suite and suite still have one extra bed. Amenities with a shower are in the rooms.

Prices in the boarding house "Energetik" (Divnomorskoe) start from 2300 rubles per day per person. For example, living in a one-room junior suite with two single beds will cost from 2500 rubles per day per person.
