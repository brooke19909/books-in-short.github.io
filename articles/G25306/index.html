DIY headphone cases: interesting ideas and step-by-step instructions

Every modern person can no longer imagine his life without gadgets and favorite music on the phone that accompanies us everywhere. At the same time, I want everything in the bag to be neat and you can easily find headphones.

DIY accessories - an original and stylish gift

So that they are always in their place and do not cling to anything, you can buy a small handbag. Of course, often it comes with the purchase, but I really want to have a unique thing. In addition, such a craft can be a wonderful surprise for a loved one who will appreciate a handmade gift.



Denim holder

A cover in the shape of a cat is sewn from any material that is in the house. In this case, jeans were used, although it is easily replaced with leather, felt or other dense matter, not so much is needed. Additionally, you need to find a small piece of red or pink fabric.

To sew a kind of headphone case with your own hands without zippers, you also need to prepare a cardboard or an old plastic card, a pair of beads, a simple button and a pencil.

On paper, a pattern is made in the shape of a cat (this may be another animal), in addition, a small pattern of the tongue is needed. The height of the cover should be about 9 cm.



Do-it-yourself headphone cases can be sewn even by a novice master. Two parts of the body, as well as two parts of the tongue (one jeans and the other from red fabric) should be cut out of the fabric. Pencil outlines the future line and the place where the language will be inserted. There you need to make an incision with scissors or a knife for cutting paper.

Two parts of the tongue are flashed together and turned on the front side. Place the mouth with black thread and sew the finished tongue there. Next, you need to embroider the cat's face, sew small beads or buttons on the eyes.



A button is sewn to the body and the inside of the tongue: a rolled-up wire of headphones should fit under the tongue. Now one more detail in the form of a cat's body needs to be cut out of a plastic card. It should be smaller than the fabric base and not reach the seam by about half a centimeter. The product is almost ready, it remains to fold all the parts and sew them along the intended line.

DIY headphone case made of felt

By a similar principle, you can make funny holders in the form of animals from felt. In this case, the finished parts are sheathed with a loop seam with bright threads. Eyes and tongue can also be cut from felt or leatherette.

Stylish do-it-yourself headphone cases made of leather

For work, you will need a piece of leather, sheet felt, glue, strong threads, a strong needle and a pencil with a leaf for a pattern.



Thinking about how to make a headphone case with your own hands quickly and beautifully, you should first take care of the pattern. The cover can be absolutely any shape, the main thing is that the headphones fit inside without any problems, a great choice is a round accessory.

A paper pattern in the form of a circle is placed on a piece of leather and outlined with a pen. It is necessary to make two identical parts, after which one of them is cut in half. This should be done very carefully, since all sections will be on the front side.

Finished elements are glued from the wrong side to felt with Moment glue. You need to wait until the details are dry, and cut them out. Now all the parts are sewn together, but before that they process the free edges with a buttonhole seam. The knots are hidden between the skin and felt, the threads need to be tightened tightly, but do not pull them, all the stitches should be the same size and placed parallel to each other.

When the free edge of the parts is stitched, two semicircles are placed on the round part so that they match perfectly. Leather elements cannot be swept as traces of punctures remain, so everything needs to be stitched clean immediately. The elements are also sewn with a loop seam; for decoration, you can additionally use beads (put on a bead on each stitch). Near the connection of the upper parts, several seams can be made. Thus, the cover is stitched around the entire perimeter and the thread is fixed on the side of the felt.



Do-it-yourself headphone cases are quick and easy to do, while they help keep the bag in order: the headphones will always be in place and will never get confused.
