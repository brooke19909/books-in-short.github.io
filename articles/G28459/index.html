Hotel 4 * Terracotta Resort Spa (Vietnam / Phan Thiet): photos and reviews of tourists

The 4 * Terracotta Resort Spa is a cozy hotel complex, the territory of which is densely planted with coconut palms. Thanks to this, the resort gains an unforgettable unique tropical atmosphere. Spend an exotic vacation in cozy and colorful bungalows!





Hotel Summary

The 4 * Terracotta Resort Spa Hotel is a fairly new resort complex, specializing in all kinds of spa treatments. It opened relatively recently - in 2006. Here you will plunge into an unforgettable tropical atmosphere and you can admire plenty of unforgettable exotic green landscapes. They have not forgotten about the benefits of civilization, which are so necessary for modern tourists.

Special attention should be paid to the modern rooms of the 4 * Terracotta Resort Spa hotel. Their laconic ecological design, based on natural wood furniture, draws attention. There is also a television, internet and other amenities necessary for a city person. The windows of the apartments offer simply incredible and bewitching views.

The 4 * Terracotta Resort Spa hotel provides many services to help fill your leisure time and make it interesting, active and eventful. Be sure to visit the restaurant of traditional local cuisine, and also plunge into the clean blue pool! You should definitely use the services of the spa center, as well as bring your figure into shape in the gym.





Hotel location

Terracotta Resort Spa 4 * hotel (Vietnam) is located on the shore of Phan Thiet Bay. From here, to the city center, where the main tourist infrastructure and entertainment facilities are concentrated, is only 5 minutes away by car or other form of transport. But the road to the airport will take as much as 5 hours.

Hotel rooms

In the resort Terracotta Resort Spa 4 * (Phan Thiet), great attention is paid to the comfort of guests. That is why all rooms are very cozy and bright, and are also characterized by a laconic design and a wide range of amenities. Tourists can stay in apartments of the following categories:

  • Standard room is a small room, which is a bright bedroom with a large or a pair of small beds. There is also a bathroom and a small balcony, upon which you can admire the green green palm trees.
  • Cozy bungalows are located right in the garden, the roofs of which are covered with palm leaves. There are large and small apartments. Part of the bungalow is just one room, or rather a bedroom, as well as a small terrace. Large apartments consist of two full rooms, one of which is designed for sleeping, and the second is a living area combined with a kitchen. A terrace is also available.
  • Several bungalow rooms are located directly next to the beach. Their terrace and windows offer incredibly picturesque views of the sea coast.
  • Family rooms consist of two large bedrooms, each with a private bathroom. There is also a spacious balcony from which you can admire the greenery of the garden.
  • The rooms of the "Suite" category, depending on the interior design, are divided into King-Room and Queen-Room. They consist of a large bright bedroom and a furnished living area. A nice addition is the seascape, which you can enjoy from the balcony of the apartment.




Room Amenities

Each of the hotel rooms with Terracotta Resort Spa 4 * (Vietnam, Phan Thiet) has the following amenities that will provide guests with maximum comfort:

  • a compact balcony or a spacious terrace with which, depending on the type of apartment, you can admire the green tropical garden or the azure sea waves;
  • individual air conditioning system, which will create a comfortable climate in your room;
  • a phone from which you can make an international call without any additional requests;
  • a modern TV on which you can watch cable channels or video from disks, flash drives or other media;
  • mini-bar, which can also be used as a refrigerator;
  • built-in safe with electronic combination lock;
  • hair dryer in the bathroom;
  • electric kettle or coffee maker.




Service for guests

Hotel Terracotta Resort Spa 4 * is ready to provide its guests with the following list of services:

  • a cozy restaurant serving traditional local dishes, as well as European and international cuisine;
  • the bar has the opportunity to try a variety of soft drinks and spirits, as well as original designer cocktails from local bartenders;
  • there is the possibility of delivering food to the room;
  • if you want to spend time on the beach or exploring the surroundings, you can use the service, which involves packing lunch on the road;
  • You can order transportation to the airport or any other communication center;
  • the reception is open around the clock, where you can get answers to your questions, make a currency exchange, purchase tickets for transport;
  • those who wish to take part in the excursion program should contact a specialized bureau;
  • to wash and iron clothes will help you in the laundry;
  • you can hand over bulky suitcases to the storage room;
  • office services can be used in the business center building;
  • Wi-Fi is available throughout the hotel;
  • if necessary, you will be provided with a conference room or room for banquets.




Recreation and entertainment

In the Terracotta Resort Spa 4 * hotel (Vietnam, Phan Thiet) you will not have to be bored, because there are many opportunities to fill your leisure time and make it as bright and interesting as possible:

  • there is a private sandy beach with all the necessary equipment for recreation (by the way, there are also instructors who will teach you all the tricks of water sports);
  • a steam sauna will help you to completely relax and get rid of negative emotions;
  • Be sure to visit the modern fitness center where you can work out on modern simulators under the supervision of professional trainers and instructors;
  • improve your health and take care of your beauty in the SPA center;
  • in the massage room you will be provided with many pleasant procedures with aromatic oils;
  • You can relax and have a good time in the hot tub;
  • You can rent a mountain bike for walking around the hotel or for trips to the city;
  • young tourists will have a good time at the playground, which is located in a shady green garden (in case of bad weather, there is a game room indoors);
  • every evening in the hotel restaurant, an animation team organizes a show program or concert;
  • at your service is a clean freshwater pool, which, thanks to the favorable climate, operates all year round;
  • one of the main reasons for the pride of this hotel is a huge tropical garden, which occupies almost the entire territory;
  • Sunbathe and enjoy the scenic views on the spacious outdoor terrace.




Positive reviews

According to tourists, one of the best hotels in Vietnam is Terracotta Resort Spa 4 *. Reviews about him contain a lot of positive information:

  • the staff includes Russian-speaking employees;
  • the rooms are always clean due to daily and high-quality cleaning;
  • delicious, varied and hearty breakfasts;
  • excellent connection quality and wireless Internet speed;
  • a very beautiful area with rich flora and fauna;
  • in addition to breakfast, upon arrival they give out coupons for such free services as massage, sauna and others;
  • absolute cleanliness on the beach;
  • quiet and peaceful atmosphere, which makes this hotel just the perfect place for a family or romantic vacation;
  • the hotel is located very conveniently;
  • On the territory of the hotel, as well as in its buildings there are many original decorative elements.




Negative reviews

Tourists have a number of comments on the work of this hotel:

  • there is practically no animation program;
  • breakfast is too early;
  • all restaurants and bars in the hotel and in the city close at 10 pm;
  • on the territory and in the rooms there are a lot of mosquitoes.
