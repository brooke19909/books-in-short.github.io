Fun party

Creating an image will be impossible if you do not work on trifles and finishing touches. The costume of the cook must be supplemented with real or fake cooking utensils. Do not use too oversized and heavy accessories - it will be difficult for the baby to carry them with him. You can come up with something useful: for example, provide the child with waffle tubes or chewing marmalade, with which he will treat his friends at the holiday.

Another option to diversify the costume, and at the same time to amuse the children is to make templates for the photo shoot. These are silhouettes of simple things - mustaches, glasses and caps attached to a high wooden skewer. They can be cut out of the simplest plain cardboard (white, black, color) or you can use beautiful embossed paper of high density from a material with a suitable print theme.
