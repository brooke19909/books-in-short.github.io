Indonesia, Bali: prices, reviews and photos

In the last decade, Indonesia has become a very popular destination for Russian tourists. Bali is an island that is part of this state. Here the tourist infrastructure is most developed. And the island itself amazes with its beautiful pristine nature, lush greenery of the jungle, Gunung-Batur, Agung and Kintamini volcanoes, ancient temples and endless snow-white beaches. When to go

Now a few words about when it is best to go on vacation. Indonesia (Bali in particular) is experiencing a tourist boom in the summer months as well as in September. The peak season occurs between Catholic Christmas (December 25) and Three Kings Day (January 6). Prices soar by a third during these periods. Therefore, experienced tourists are advised to go to Bali in the winter, but not on the holidays mentioned above. Reviews say that the weather on this island paradise is good at any time of the year. Even if the tour operator scares you with the words "rainy season" (November - March), do not believe it. A couple of drops in the evening or night rain lasting ten minutes is not a reason to refuse to travel at a time of low prices. You need to go for at least two weeks. A long flight and acclimatization require a longer rest.



How to get to Bali

Most quickly and without problems, you can fly to the resort island by direct flight, which is carried out by two companies from Moscow (Sheremetyevo) - Transaero and Aeroflot. The flight in this case will take twelve hours. As for other cities of Russia, airplanes from Khabarovsk, Novosibirsk, Yekaterinburg (Nordwind Airlines) and Vladivostok fly in this direction. But tickets for such non-stop aircraft are too expensive. Charters fly in this direction only during the New Year and Christmas holidays. Therefore, reviews advise using the services of other companies - Singaporean, Turkish, Thai Airlines or Air Asia. But then you will need to land at other airports. Bali (Indonesia) has only one air gate. This is Ngurah Rai Airport, thirteen kilometers from the island's capital, Denpasar. The hub ranks second in the country in terms of congestion, and so now the construction of the second air gate is underway at the northern tip of Bali.

How to get from the airport to the resort

How to get around Bali (Indonesia)? The reviews claim that due to the small size of the island, even a taxi ride from the airport to your resort will not empty your pockets. Direct carrier parking is located in front of the terminal. At the counter you need to name your destination and pay for the trip. There are many hotels in the resorts, the price of which includes a transfer from the airport. Also, from the building of the international terminal, a city bus runs to the capital of the island of Denpasar. You can buy a ticket for it in the airport building. Tours in Bali

This paradise island offers exactly the kind of vacation you dreamed about. This differs from other tourist countries in Indonesia (Bali). The specifics of the resorts: a relaxing beach holiday

Indonesia, Bali are famous for this. The cleanest sand, quiet warm lagoons - more than enough of all this on the island. If you are traveling to Bali over the sea and the beach, choose any resort. In this regard, they differ only in prices. Nusa Dua and Seminyak have many luxury hotels (Grand Hiyatt, Melia). There are also hotels working on the all-inclusive concept. Jimbaran - the same beach pleasures at a lower price. Legian is an ideal compromise of price and quality. In Sanur, Ubud and Chandidas a wide selection of hotels of different classes. A special feature here is the rest, which can be described as a "paradise in a hut." Newlyweds all over the world dream of spending their honeymoon here. The huts, however, seem to be huts on stilts above the water only at first glance. They have everything that your heart desires. And attendants are accustomed to be invisible if necessary. The bungalows are set so that guests do not see or hear their neighbors and feel like Adam and Eve in the midst of paradise Eden. These hotels are famous for Uluwatu, Amed and the resorts of western Bali.
