Beading flowers: delicate rose

Beading today has become a very popular hobby. This is due to the fact that from multi-colored beads you can make a huge number of decorations for room decor, as well as various jewelry. Having mastered the basic techniques for creating baubles, try beading flowers. Surprisingly, bright tulips, exciting lilies and modest daisies will come to life in your hands. Today we will see how to make a delicate rose from beads.





Material

To complete the craft, you should prepare 30 grams of beads for the bud (you can pink, red, yellow or white) and as much green. You will also need wire for the stem and leaves, floss thread (green) and PVA glue. We start beadwork.

Schemes

Flowers of roses from beads is not such a difficult task as it might seem at first glance. The fact is that the queen of the garden consists of 5 large and 5 small petals, as well as 5 leaves. All parts of the circuit are braided separately using a very simple technique.





Beading Rose Flowers

1. Let's start by making the petals of the bud. For this we use the technique of "parallel weaving". The first three beads must be strung on a wire 60 centimeters long. Then pass both ends of the rod through the extreme on the opposite side. Thus, you should get two rows: the bottom consists of 1 part, the next - two.

2. Now your ends should again be on opposite sides of the structure. We string the next two beads on each of them and again pass them through all the elements. This will be the third row, consisting of 4 beads.

3. Next, create the following rows, each time increasing their size twice. Thus, you should get 10 of them.

4. After making the last row, the ends of the wire should come out from two different sides. We string 13 pearls on them and fix the part by twisting the rod below. In total, we cook five large petals.

5. In the manufacture of small petals, we need the same flower scheme. Beadwork here will differ only in the size of the leaves, the wire should also be taken with a length of 50 centimeters. Let us dwell on the 7th row, and for the ends we use only 10 pearls.

6. Now we begin to manufacture the sepals of roses. We also use the mastered technique of "parallel weaving". We do each leaflet separately. We take three beads, string them onto a wire 40 cm long and twist them, passing through the opposite beads. Increasing the number of pearls by half, we reach the fourth row. Then we begin to remove the beads, following in the reverse order. At the end, twist both ends of the wire into one.





7. The beading of flowers on this is over, it remains to collect the rose. To do this, first we take one small petal, we add 4 more parts to it in turn, twisting the edges to the center. Then we attach 5 large petals, giving the rose volume. At the base of the bud we place the sepals and twist the wire.

8. We wrap the wire with mouline thread, pre-lubricating it with PVA glue. Optionally, you can make leaves and place them on the stem. Now the beading of flowers is finally completed. Our rose can become a worthy decoration of your desktop and a nice gift wishing you a good mood.
