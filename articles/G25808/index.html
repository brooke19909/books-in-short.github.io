Grand Yazici Club Marmaris Palace 5 *: traveler reviews from Russia

Today, tourists, as you know, are very attentive to the issue of choosing a hotel in a particular country. In addition to studying the official description and advice of tour operator managers, they try to get acquainted with as many reviews as possible from people who recently visited the hotel in question. This allows you to make a more complete and closer to reality, the idea of ‚Äã‚Äãa hotel. But today we decided to save your time and offer to study the generalized comments of our compatriots who lived during a holiday in the Republic of Turkey in Grand Yazici Marmaris Palace HV-1. We note right away that the vast majority of comments are positive, since tourists did not regret their choice at all.





The hotel itself, its territory and location

Most of the tourists were very pleased with the accommodation in this hotel. According to them, the hotel is not new, but the rooms in it are cozy, clean, and freshly renovated. They do not differ in large area, but there is enough space for all guests. Furniture and appliances are also not new, but all in good working condition. Some guests noted comfortable beds and pillows. As for cleaning, our compatriots did not express any particular complaints about the work of the maids. So, only a few tourists noted that the cleaners reached them after dinner, when some vacationers liked to take a nap in their rooms. However, this did not become a serious problem.

As for the territory of the hotel, it is quite large, green, beautiful and well-groomed. Travelers spoke positively about the location of the hotel. So, many enjoyed walking along the promenade to Icmeler and even Marmaris, where there are a lot of shops, shopping centers, restaurants, bars, etc.





Nutrition Reviews

As a rule, when it comes to this issue, guests rarely agree. However, the Grand Yazici Club Marmaris Palace 5 * (Marmaris, Turkey) was a kind of exception. So, most of our compatriots claim that the food here is very well organized. Of course, a number of dishes on the menu are regularly repeated, but the choice is always very large. Family tourists were pleased with the fact that the restaurant has a children's table. So there will be no problems with food even for the youngest guests.

Many travelers also visited the local √† la carte restaurant. According to them, they cook there very tasty.

There were no complaints about the quality of both alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks.



Beach vacation

At this point, most of the tourists who spent their holidays in Grand Yazici Marmaris Palace 5 * were also satisfied. However, experienced travelers warn that you should not count on the beach as such. So, sun loungers and parasols are placed on concrete slabs. In the sea, you can go down the stairs, as in a pool. However, thanks to this, the water, even at the very shore, is always very clean. Both adults and children bathe here without problems. Near the coast there is a pontoon where you can sunbathe and dive. As for the sun loungers, there are a lot of them on the beach. Therefore, there is no need to get up early in the morning and take a sunbed. Water sports equipment can also be rented on the beach.

Entertainment at the hotel

At the hotel, according to tourists, you can have a great time by the pool. There are sunbeds with a large number of sunbeds, a bar with a large selection of drinks. In addition, from morning to evening, the hotel has a team of animators. So, you will be offered to do water aerobics, gymnastics, yoga, dance, play team games, etc. In the evenings, entertainment shows and a disco are held at the hotel.

In addition, guests can play tennis, futsal, volleyball, basketball, billiards, darts. A local massage parlor is also available. Experienced travelers recommend that all vacationers in Turkey do not spend all their time at the hotel, but be sure to take excursions, because this country is rich in interesting sights. Moreover, our compatriots advise not to buy tours from a guide in a hotel, but in one of the bureaus in the city. According to them, the quality of the trip will be the same, but the price will be much lower.

As for the youngest guests, then at Grand Yazici Marmaris Palace they took care of their leisure. So, in the daytime, children can attend the mini club. In the evening, a mini-disco is held for them.

Hotel staff impressions

As for the employees of the hotel complex under consideration, on the whole, travelers were satisfied with them. According to tourists, most of the hotel staff are professional, friendly and always happy to help. So, for example, maids do their job well, and this is not affected by the fact you leave a tip or not. According to our compatriots, they are not too lazy to wet-clean their rooms every day, unlike many other hotels.

The tourists were satisfied with the work of the waiters. Of course, sometimes they do not have time to immediately clean the dirty tables, but this is due to the large influx of visitors.

Our compatriots considered the fact that many hotel employees speak, or at least understand, Russian as a big plus. So you will not have any communication problems here.
