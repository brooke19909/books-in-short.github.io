Hotel Crystal Family Resort & Spa 5 * (Turkey): traveler reviews

Serik is a pretty young resort located on the Turkish Riviera. This village is located in the very south of the country, in the Belek zone. The hot climate of the subtropics here is tempered by mountains covered with eucalyptus and pine trees. Picturesque nature, warm sea and clean beaches attract thousands of tourists to Serik every year, including from Russia. And of course, for the convenience of vacationers, many comfortable hotels have been built here, large and small. For example, many tour operators from Russia work with the Crystal Family Resort 5 * hotel. Reviews for this hotel on the web are mostly good.

Where is it located?

This hotel is located not in Serik itself, but 11 km from it - on the seashore. The distance from it to the center of Antalya is approximately 50 km. From the airport to the hotel, tourists who rented rooms here have to get about 40-50 minutes. This air transport hub is located near Antalya - 40 km from Serik.





Most tour operators, judging by the reviews, offer their customers who have bought permits with accommodation in this hotel, quite comfortable transfers. Buses for tourists are provided new, with air conditioning. Therefore, judging by the reviews, the trip from the airport to the hotel is usually not too tedious.

The territory at this hotel is not only convenient for a pleasant pastime, but also well landscaped. The paths in the hotel courtyard are covered with beautiful paving tiles. Therefore, walking on them is quite convenient.

Opinion of tourists about food in a hotel

The dining room of the Crystal Family Resort & Spa 5 * hotel is most praised by most vacationers. In any case, the dishes here are always very tasty and fresh. Tourists attribute to some disadvantages of the hotel restaurant that the menu here is not particularly diverse in terms of meat and fish food. Fruits, according to most vacationers, could also be served more in the dining room.

Actually the very premises of the restaurant in this hotel, according to many tourists, is quite convenient in terms of layout and area. The hotel staff is polite, and the tables, floors and utensils are always clean.

In addition to the dining room, the Crystal Hotel Family Resort & Spa 5 * hotel , of course, also has a bar. The range of drinks offered here, according to many tourists, is quite wide. Moreover, alcoholic drinks are sold in the bar, albeit not too expensive, but not local Turkish, but branded.

Many vacationers believe that the dishes in the local restaurant are served mostly quite sharp and fatty, as some lack of food at this hotel. Some children, of course, strongly dislike this food. Therefore, parents have to additionally visit cafes and dining rooms located next to the hotel.

Beach Reviews

The sea, as already mentioned, is literally a few steps from the hotel buildings. Tourists can reach it in just a couple of minutes. The hotel's beach vacationers reviews also earned good ones. The coastline in this place is covered with sand, which is very convenient to walk along. On the beach of the hotel, even simple slates are considered optional (unless, of course, the sand is not too hot), not to mention special shoes.

Hotel guests can use sun loungers and umbrellas for free. But if desired, visitors can sunbathe directly in the sand - on a towel or rug. The administration usually does not prohibit this. In many other hotels in Turkey on the beach on the sand is not allowed. The absence of such a restriction at the Crystal Family hotel, many of its guests, of course, are attributed to its advantages.

A pebble strip stretches right next to the sea entrance on the beach of the complex. However, the pebbles in this place of the beach are small and do not deliver any conveniences to swimmers. Tourists with children even consider this a plus of the hotel. After all, from the pebbles the little guests of the complex can put together different figures, which makes their vacation on the beach more diverse.

Is it convenient to swim?

The entrance to the sea, therefore, next to the hotel is quite convenient. You can enter the water simply barefoot, and in shales. Depth in the sea begins about 2 m from the coast. Therefore, experienced vacationers are advised to watch the children on the beach more closely.





Actually, the water itself in the sea in this place, judging by the reviews, is always clean. To the very buoys, even the bottom is visible through its thickness. No particularly dangerous animals in the sea in this place is found. The only thing in past years was when the hotel administration was forced to close its beach for visiting tourists. The reason then was the invasion of sea turtles. These reptiles decided to lay their eggs in the sand right on the beach. And since local turtles are a rare species, the administration and vacationers had to wait for the hatching of the cubs.
