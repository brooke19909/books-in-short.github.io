Hotels Punta Cana (Dominican Republic): rest for every taste

The Dominican Republic is a continuous resort country. This is facilitated by the location of the island in the Caribbean, and the tropical climate, and bewitching nature, and developed tourism business. The resort of Punta Cana (Dominican Republic) is in particular demand, particularly among holidaymakers from Russia . The hotels here are mostly five-star, but there are also magnificent “fours”. Hotels mainly specialize in one “target audience”: newlyweds, families with children, active youth. The following is a brief overview of places to stay.



Bavaro Beach

The east coast of the island where the resort of Punta Cana is located is called the Costa de Cocos. This is due to the large number of palm trees growing right next to the azure waters of the Atlantic. The resort has five long beaches. In terms of tourism, the most developed Bavaro. Many hotels of Punta Cana (Dominican Republic) 5 stars are concentrated here. These are Bavaro Princess All Suites, Bee Liv Grand Bavaro, Catalonia Resort and Casino, Gran Bahia Principle and many others. As you can see, there are both network (Bahia, Catalonia) hotels and small hotels, such as Tortuga Bay. There is no shortage here in the complexes, “Nau Larimar Punta Cana 5 *” is one of them. It stands on the first line and consists of different buildings.

Cabeza de Torro

This beach has been awarded the Blue Flag for its clean sand and water. Punta Cana (Dominican Republic) hotels in Cabeza de Torro are often hosted by honeymooners and couples in love. If you want to relax on such a “romantic island of love”, please note that hotels of this type do not accept guests under the age of eighteen.



Punta Arena Gorda

This beach is also proud of the Blue Flag, which indicates that the coast is absolutely clean and safe. Since the territory here began to be developed later, the hotels of Punta Cana (Dominican Republic) pleased with the novelty. This includes all the famous hotel chains in the world. “Iberostar”, “Sol Melia”, “Princesses”, “Barcelo”, “Paradisus” - this is not a complete list of them. But unlike their European namesakes, they all work in Runta Kan on an All Inclusive system.

El Cortecito Beach

These hotels of Punta Cana (Dominican Republic) “Sharpened” for an active and young clientele. Around is full of cafes, restaurants, night clubs. And in the afternoon you will find diving, windsurfing and kiting schools. From here it is easy to make excursions to other cities and interesting places of the country, as well as to Manati Park. Here you can find hotels with four stars. These are Rio Nyboa, Karabela Beach Resort, Occidental Grand Flamenco, Caribe Club and others.

Macau Beach



On this coast you can find various Punta Cana (Dominican Republic) hotels both 4 and 5 stars. For the price of a room of 100-140 Euros per night, you will be offered an all-inclusive stay with local drinks in the bar, animation and even beach activities. In this area you can find both secluded bungalows hiding from prying eyes in dense tropical greenery, and a room in a huge hotel complex, which includes several hotels at once.
