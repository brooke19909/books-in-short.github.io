Trying to determine which is better: iPhone-4S or iPhone-5

A notable progress in the production of smartphones by Apple was the release of the iPhone-4S. When the 5th came out, this was a further breakthrough of the brainchild of Steve Jobs. Immediately after her presentation, many fans and enthusiasts began to compare these two phones. Their goal was to determine which is better: iPhone-4S or iPhone-5. We will also conduct a small comparative analysis.

Visual differences between devices



The difference is visible even at first glance visually: the new gadget is thinner and longer, the dimensions are 123.8 x 58.6 x 7.6 mm and 115.2 x 58.6 x 9.3 mm, respectively. The width, as we see, is the same. The case is more elongated in order to accommodate a four-inch display. Designs, as you can see right away, are very similar.

A little about stuffing



When deciding which is better: iPhone-4S or iPhone-5 - you need to pay attention to the more convenient and compact docking Lightning connector. By the way, if you want to use old accessories, you can use the included adapter. The devices differ in color. If the "four" is monophonic, black or white, then the "five" is two-tone. The new phone is equipped with a faster and more powerful Apple-A6 processor and has a fresher and better operating system iOS 6, supports LTE, although not universally.

Cons of the new phone

By cons include the SIM card used in the new Apple device. For many buyers, the use of nano-SIM, which cannot be cut with scissors like the previous model, may turn out to be an unpleasant surprise. Fortunately, the main Russian mobile operators have already purchased enough of these cards. You can get it for free at the office of your operator.
