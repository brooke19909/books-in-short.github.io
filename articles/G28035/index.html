Krasnoyarsk hotels: list, addresses, reviews

One of the largest cities in Russia - Krasnoyarsk - is located in the center of Siberia. It is located on the banks of the Yenisei River. More than a million people live in the city. There are a large number of universities, museums, libraries, parks and fountains. Gold mines are located, a fifth of the country's gold reserves are mined in Krasnoyarsk. The city has a large number of industrial enterprises and factories. Therefore, various scientific symposia and business meetings are often held here.



People come here from all over the country and stay in hotels. There are a lot of them. What are the most popular Krasnoyarsk hotels ? Are there any inexpensive ones? What services are provided to guests? What do the numerous guests of Krasnoyarsk say about hotels? How can I get to them? The most famous sights of the city and other interesting facts that will be interesting to visitors are presented in the article.

Transport in Krasnoyarsk

The city has built airports and a train station. From anywhere in Russia to Krasnoyarsk you can fly by plane or take a train. The following types of transport work in the city: buses, trolleybuses, trams, minibus. The fare is from 14 to 22 rubles. Work is underway to commission the metro, however, while construction is frozen.

Interesting facts about the city

  1. In Krasnoyarsk there is a famous scientific center - Akademgorodok. Here, work is underway in all areas of science.
  2. The ten-ruble note depicts the famous Krasnoyarsk hydroelectric station.
  3. The remains of the first meteorite were discovered in the middle of the 19th century. It happened in the Krasnoyarsk Territory.
  4. One of the main decorations of the city are fountains. There are more than a hundred of them here.
  5. Krasnoyarsk is considered the most eastern city in Russia.
  6. In the 19th century it was a place of exile of the Decembrists.
  7. The railway bridge across the Yenisei received a grand prix and a gold medal at the beginning of the 20th century.
  8. Krasnoyarsk has the largest zoo in Russia. It is called the "Roy Creek".
  9. Krasnoyarsk hotels provide a variety of discounts. For example, with a preliminary reservation - up to 15% of the room price.




Krasnoyarsk Hotels

There are more than a hundred of them in the city. When choosing a hotel, first of all, pay attention to the following nuances:

  • Distance from the city center and major attractions.
  • Additional services: Internet, hairdresser, etc.

  • All food related issues.
  • Guest reviews.

After carefully studying the necessary information and deciding on the choice of hotel, you can pre-book a room by phone. Prepayment is sometimes required.
