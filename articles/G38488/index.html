Difficulty choosing

You should immediately warn that such drives are not intended for long-term data storage. That is, even the most advanced flash drive of the best company, due to its technical features, will not be able to store information for more than three, and in rare cases - five years. So it will obviously be superfluous to copy data to another medium once a year and check the integrity of the current one.

Standard flash drives of good quality are not suitable for working with small files. The fewer bytes in the data, the slower the copying process. This is especially true for small-sized SD-drives. Even the best micro-flash drives, when faced with tiny files, work, as a rule, from ‚Äúbad‚Äù to ‚Äúcomplete brake‚Äù. In this case, it is best to look closely at technically advanced models based on SSD technology.

It is also worth noting that the larger the volume of the drive, the faster information is written to it. Moreover, the increase in speed is in most cases steps. That is, if copying began with an indicator of 1 Mbit / s, then it can continue somewhere at 40 Mbit in a minute or two.

We should also pay attention to models with MLC chips. Manufacturers of the best USB flash drives are trying to flash them with such chips. Such technology will significantly increase the operational operational resource of the gadget. MLC-chipovka will come in handy for those who intensively use a USB flash drive.

Flash drive manufacturers: which company is better?

Many manufacturers are engaged in the production of flash drives, but the backbone of trusted brands has been formed for a long time, and newcomers are not welcome there. The cost of branded gadgets is much higher than that of products from little-known companies, but there are clearly more quality guarantees. Because the competition here is very fierce, and every venerable manufacturer understands that customer dissatisfaction promises serious financial losses.

Sandisk

This is a large company from North America, which was able to conquer almost a third of this market. The best USB flash drives of the brand are known around the world, including in Russia. Hence, very good sales, as well as excellent quality products.

On the domestic market you can find a lot of fakes "Sandisk", so do not flatter yourself when you see a model from a popular brand at a bargain price. If the company holds shares, it is only at official distribution points. And the latter, of course, are not associated with fakes. It is also worth noting that products from Sandisk often receive awards at specialized exhibitions where they select the best flash drives of the year, carefully assessing all the advantages and disadvantages of the participants.

Transcend

The second most popular brand after Sandisk. The Taiwanese company has a reputation as a manufacturer of stable and best flash drives, as well as memory cards. The brand's products are enviably popular among domestic consumers.

This demand is largely due not only to the high quality of products, but also to the more than democratic pricing policy of the company.

Corsair

In third place is another American company, which boasts not only the production of the best flash drives, but also the release of high-quality memory modules for desktop and mobile PCs. Both the first and second are very popular among domestic consumers.

As in the previous case, the company does not oppress prices and pursues a more than adequate pricing policy. It is also worth noting that the brand pays a lot of attention to the appearance of drives, where in the assortment you can find a flash drive with an original exterior.

Kingston

The company does not produce drives as such, but is the largest supplier of memory modules, flash drives and hard drives for personal computers. The brand also owns a well-known division of HyperX, under whose leadership the expensive, but extremely high-quality RAM gaming modules enter the market.

Despite the fact that the company itself does not produce chips, it conducts rigorous selection and subsequent product adaptation. As a result, the really best flash drives with an extremely high-quality component and an impressive warranty period are on the shelves.

Products of other brands can also be considered, especially if you liked the cost of the drive. But here it is necessary to take into account that for little-known or generally no-name manufacturers, not every series turns out to be successful. So before buying, it will be clearly out of place to read reviews, and not in the store itself, but not in specialized forums, or at least on Yandex.Market.
