Association of boarding houses of the resort "Pitsunda": acquaintance

DIC "Pitsunda" reviews are called one of the most picturesque resorts in Abkhazia. The tourist complex, located in the coastal strip between the pine grove and the sea, consists of 7 14-storey buildings: “Lighthouse”, “Colchis”. "Amzara", "Apsny", "Amra", "Golden Fleece", "Bzyb", The cost of living in the buildings of the military-industrial complex "Pitsunda": from 1100 rubles / day.





Location

The complex is located on the 1st line from the sea at the address: st. Gitsba, 5, Pitsunda, Abkhazia. OPK Pitsunda is characterized by reviews as a resort with the cleanest and most transparent sea water in the entire region. Even on very hot summer days, due to the sea breezes, as well as the presence of shady pine and cypress alleys , amazing coolness remains here. Tourists note as one of the advantages of the Pitsunda defense and industrial complex the location of the buildings of the complex, which are literally buried in the greenery of the legendary reserved pine grove along the border of which they were built. It is the healing properties of the unique ancient pine that provide the pensioners of the Pitsunda resort with their phenomenal popularity. The boarding house is located 61 km from Sochi Airport, the distance to the Pitsunda military industrial complex from the Russian-Abkhaz border is 56 km. It’s easy to buy tickets to the Pitsunda military-industrial complex: you should call any of the travel agencies and the managers will select the appropriate tour. The cost of a seven-day tour for two adults is from 18,900 to 34,300 rubles.





Description

The tourist complex is located in the center of Pitsunda - the most delightful corner of Abkhazia, on the picturesque Black Sea coast, surrounded by a pine grove, creating a healing microclimate and giving coolness even on hot summer days.

The buildings of the complex, built on the 1st coastline, have long been a kind of visiting card of the resort. The resort has a rich entertainment infrastructure. The complex has gorgeous beaches, as well as a large number of rooms, the level of accommodation in which varies: both rooms with conveniences on the floor and suites are presented. Nearby are famous attractions: Pitsunda temple, organ hall, ancient defensive wall, market, various bars, cafes, bars, restaurants, souvenir shops and shops.

Vacationers who have left feedback about the Pitsunda defense complex note that the coast of the resort is located in a cozy bay, as a result of which the sea water here is always calm and very clean. This vacation spot is called ideal for lovers of the beauty of nature and impeccable ecology.
