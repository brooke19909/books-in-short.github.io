Tariff plan "Your country" from MTS: description and features

The tariff “Your country” from MTS is an advantageous offer for communication abroad and in Russia. Additionally, the package has a content blocking function, thanks to which customers can save their money. The tariff does not include a lot of countries with which you can talk at a bargain price, but those that are included in the package are presented with a very favorable tariff schedule. In the review, all information is presented for customers in Moscow and the region.

Description

The tariff “Your country” from MTS has a very simple description. An offer has been developed for subscribers who often call to the CIS countries, as well as for customers who are in China, Vietnam and Korea.

The positive side is the complete absence of a monthly fee, but for the rest of the mobile services, a fee will be charged upon the fact of use. In other words, users will pay for every minute of communication, every sent message or actually used traffic for the Internet.





The tariff “Your country” from MTS will allow subscribers to connect various options that can save funds on the balance sheet. For example, SMS Smart makes it possible to send messages on a free basis, and the SuperBIT Smart option will be able to provide free access to the Internet.

Home network cost

There is no subscription fee for the tariff, but before connecting you need to know the prices for services in the home region.

All incoming calls are not paid.

When talking with customers whose tariff plan “Your country” is connected from MTS, payment per minute of conversation will be equal to 1 ruble.

Any call within the region of activation of a SIM card by MTS will be charged at 2.5 rubles per minute of communication. The same cost for communicating with people that use the services of other mobile providers in the country.

A text message that is sent within the home network will cost 2.5 rubles.

The cost of sending multimedia messages is 6.5 rubles.

Cost of calls to different countries and in Russia

For MTS clients, the tariff “Your Country” provided for the following cost of communication with other countries and cities of Russia.

Calls made to any mobile number in Russia cost 3 rubles per minute.

The price of any text message will be charged at 2.5 rubles for each sms.

An outgoing call to Tajikistan is paid for the first minute 7 rubles, the remaining 8 minutes will cost 1 rubles / min. This condition applies if the conversation is with a client using TCELL operator communication. Communication using other mobile providers will cost 8 rubles / min.

Outgoing calls to China and Korea will amount to 3 rubles / min.

Communication with residents of Kazakhstan will be paid at a cost that depends on the operator - 4.5-8 rubles / min.

Call to Uzbekistan - 4.5 rubles / min.

Conversations with Georgian subscribers - 20 rubles / min.

If you call MTS number in Ukraine, the price will be 10 rubles / min, calls to other mobile operators in the country will be twice as expensive.

The remaining calls to Europe will cost 49 rubles / min.

Conversations with countries of the world that are not on the list will amount to 70 rubles per minute.





Detailed information on the cost and the list of countries at the tariff “Your country” from MTS is presented on the operator’s website.

the Internet

The offer does not include megabytes of traffic, but do not be upset, because there is access to the network. Its cost, of course, turns out to be quite high, and debiting of funds will be made only after going online, in fact. The MTS “Your Country” tariff for Moscow includes the cost for each megabyte used at 9.9 rubles. Due to the high cost, the MTS operator recommends connecting additional services that expand the possibilities of using the Internet.





Connection

The MTS company proposes the tariff plan “Your country” to connect using different methods, but it should be mentioned initially that the transfer fee may be 150 rubles if the client has already changed the tariff for a month. In this case, 100 rubles of the specified amount will go to the company, and 50 remain on the user's balance.





You can activate the “Your Country” tariff from MTS by the following methods.

Visit the operator’s company salon in their city, where employees will quickly transfer the number to the desired rate. This will require a passport.

The tariff from MTS “Your Country” can be connected using the support service operator by phone 0890. By calling the number, the client can connect the offer using the informant’s hints independently, or wait for a connection with a live operator who will connect the offer remotely. The employee will need to provide passport details for identification.

If you can use the Internet, it is recommended to use your personal account, which is on the operator’s website. Through it, the connection will occur in a matter of minutes. In addition, it is worth noting a similar opportunity to activate the plan through the My MTS mobile application.

If the methods from the list are not suitable for various reasons, then the transition is done via telephone and using a service request. The user needs to enter the activation code * 111 * 182 # and make a call. After this, the tariff from MTS “Your Country” in Moscow will be included, which the client will find out through a confirmation message.

Shutdown

Disabling the tariff plan is completely impossible for any of the operators. Customers can simply change the offer to another one that is more suitable. After switching to a new offer, “Your Country” will be automatically deactivated. To switch to a new tariff, you must select it and use one of the above activation methods. This can be a connection through the office, by entering a request or sending a text message with a code, as well as through MTS employees, in the communication salon and through the support service.
