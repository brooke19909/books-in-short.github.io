Kemer Avrupa Hotel 3 * in Turkey - photos, prices and reviews

Holidays in Kemer, a popular Turkish resort, for the ecology of the beaches of which the Blue Flag of UNESCO has been awarded - this is a good travel option during the summer holidays. Kemer resort is rightfully considered the greenest and most picturesque, the combination of the sea and green mountains turns it into a good object for high-quality photos that will remain for tourists in memory of the trip.



The abundance of pine groves makes the resort air healing. In combination with the marine climate, this creates a unique climatic therapeutic effect.

Kemer is rich in offers for sightseeing and entertainment. You can visit the popular Pamukkale ("Cotton Castle"), a UNESCO World Heritage Site as a nature reserve, visit the city of Peace and the temple of Nikolai Ugodnik, which, according to legend, was the patron of travelers and children, is considered a prototype of our Santa Claus.
