Volgograd - Rostov: how to get there?

The path between cities such as Volgograd, Rostov, is quite far. Those who want to get from one city to another can use several types of transport:

- a train;

- bus;

- own car;

- airplane.

Rostov - Volgograd: distance

Of course, by Russian (countrywide) standards, these cities are located not so far. In a straight line from Volgograd to Rostov you need to overcome 394 km. It is clear that we are talking about the possibility of air travel between settlements, since the highway can not be laid directly. On the highway the city shares a distance of 473 km.





The practice of trips between Volgograd and Rostov showed that this path can be overcome in 7 hours 19 minutes

Way by car

On your own car, the Volgograd - Rostov route is the easiest to overcome, because there is no dependence on the transport schedule (buses and trains). How much will a one way trip cost? Let's say you plan to drive a passenger car. The average consumption of gasoline on the highway will be 8 liters per 100 km. To drive 473 km, you need 38 liters of fuel. Today, the average cost of gasoline at gas stations is 32 rubles per liter. The cost of the trip at such costs will be approximately 1200-1220 rubles. Of course, it is cheaper to travel by public transport on your own, but if several people gathered on the road at once, then a car is the best way of communication between these cities.





The average truck consumes 30 liters of fuel per 100 kilometers. Mathematical calculation gives us information that for a trip Volgograd - Rostov-on-Don on a truck you need at least 142 liters of fuel. The cost of the trip by truck will be about 4,500 rubles.

Settlements along the route

Motorists on the way between cities will pass many settlements. Having left Volgograd, the car will travel 37 minutes and reach the village of Novy Rogachik. During this time, you will go 38 kilometers. It turns out that in 1 minute of time you drive 1 kilometer on average. Further on the highway is the Prudkoboy point (11 km after Novy Rogachik). Then the vehicle will move towards the village of Marinovka and reach it in 5 minutes. The border of the Volgograd region is located at 187 kilometer of the way. After Marinovka, motorists will pass through the following settlements: Ilyevka, Kalach-on-Don, Zhirkovsky, Surovikino, Novoderbenevsky.





In the Rostov region, the first point, which is located on the highway Volgograd - Rostov - Voznesensky. In this area, motorists will travel 260 kilometers, that is, most of the route. Morozovsky, Uglegorsky, Oscar, Sholokhovsky - this is not all the villages on the way to their destination. Travelers will also see Belaya Kalitva, Molodezhny, Proletarka, the city of Shakhty, Grushevskaya, the village of Krasny Kolos and the village of Stepnoy.

Bus flights

It is worth noting that there is excellent bus service between neighboring regional centers. There are both direct and transit bus routes. 8 flights depart from Volgograd daily to Rostov. The amount of time the bus travels from start to destination is different (from 6 hours 30 minutes to 9 hours 30 minutes). It all depends on whether the bus comes to transit bus stations or not. If you want to get there as quickly as possible, then pay attention to the bus Volgograd - Rostov at 08:30, 10:00, 16:00, 18:00 (buses leave from the railway station). If you need to get off at one of the transit stations, or if you want to travel at a slower pace, you should pay attention to flights departing from the Volgograd train station at 14:00, 20:15, 21:00, 21:15. Regular buses will follow Kalach-on-Don, Surovikino, Chernyshkovsky, Morozovsk, Belaya Kalitva, Shakhty, Novocherkassk.





Railway connection

The way Volgograd - Rostov can be overcome by train. Several trains depart from the hero city of Volgograd towards the main city of the Rostov Region. Daily at 04:30 the flight Severobaykalsk / Chita / Irkutsk - Adler leaves. This train arrives in Rostov at 15 hours 26 minutes. The train passes the following stations:

- Turquoise (sucks 7 minutes);

- Surovikino (the stop lasts 2 minutes);

- Oblivskaya (2 minutes);

- Morozovskaya (parking 5 minutes);

- Tatsinskaya (2 minutes);

- Belaya Kalitva (2 minutes);

- Dashing (the train takes a break in motion for 32 minutes);

- Zverevo (two-minute parking);

- Sulin;

- Mine;

- Novocherkassk.

At 07:22, the train Saratov / Nizhny Novgorod-Novorossiysk leaves from Volgograd, which arrives at the railway station in Rostov at 21:21. At 07:41 a scheduled train Chelyabinsk-Adler leaves the Volgograd station. This train arrives in the city of Rostov at 19 hours 51 minutes. Also, Rostov can be reached by another train, which reaches the Pervomaiskaya station. We are talking about the flight Ufa / Ulyanovsk-Anapa, which leaves Volgograd 11 hours 38 minutes and arrives at Pervomaiskaya at 23:37.





How to get by train from Rostov to Volgograd?

We can offer you several options. At 01:18 daily, the train Adler - Severobaykalsk leaves from Rostov to Volgograd. The train arrives at the destination station at 13:00. The next train on the same route leaves 2 hours 46 minutes. Passengers will arrive in Volgograd at 14 hours 55 minutes. The Adler - Chelyabinsk train leaves Rostov at 07:55 and arrives at the point we need on the map at 20:55. The night train Novorossiysk - Saratov usually leaves from Rostov at 22:00 and arrives in Volgograd exactly 13 hours later.

Traveling in our country (a tourist trip or business trip) is a rather expensive and lengthy business. Each citizen must choose the most suitable type of transport for the comfort and cost of the ticket in order to reach the destination point as quickly and inexpensively as possible. The main thing is to know how to get there. Rostov-Volgograd is a long way, so we wish you good luck on the road!
