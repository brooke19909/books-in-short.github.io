A wonderful place - a dolphinarium in Yevpatoriya

When you are in Crimea, be sure to visit the dolphinarium in Yevpatoriya. Opened it in 1997. From the very first performance, the artists of this institution won the love of the audience. Every year, the dolphinarium in Yevpatoria begins its work in December and is open for visits on Saturdays and Sundays.





For groups they can show the performance on other days. In spring, regular performances begin. From June to September, performances are held four times a day. After the holiday season, marine inhabitants are sent to a rehabilitation base, which is located near Novoozernoye. They are there until December.

What is the pool in the dolphinarium?

The pool is a bowl of the wrong size. Dimensions:

  • length - 22 meters;
  • depth - 4.5 meters;
  • volume - 1300 cubic meters meters.

Mineral water in the pool. Pour it from the well. In the pool, salinity is maintained up to fourteen ppm and temperature up to 15 degrees. These are the best indicators for dolphins. A couple of times a week, the quality of water from the pool is checked by the city water canal, as well as the sanitary and epidemiological station.

The dolphinarium also has an additional pool for keeping pinnipeds.

Show program and actors

Three dolphins perform in front of the audience: Gamma, Jan and Igmas.





The duration of the performance is 45 minutes. Dolphins perform amazing tricks:

  • rotate;
  • play with people;
  • sing;
  • jump into the rings;
  • perform somersaults;
  • dancing on the tail of a lambada;
  • draw pictures (in the future, these masterpieces are sold to viewers at auction);
  • Applaud pectoral fins to each other.

Additional services

You can swim with the dolphin Dart on Lake Donuzlav, located 35 km from Yevpatoriya. The duration of this service is from ten to twelve minutes. Adults whose weight exceeds 100 kg cannot use it. It is also forbidden to swim for people suffering from skin diseases or SARS.





Are you going to visit the dolphinarium in Yevpatoriya? Then it will be useful for you to find out the price for 10 minutes of swimming with the marine inhabitants. The cost of such a bath is 3500 rubles.

A photo with a dolphin or a fur seal will cost you 500 rubles if the picture is taken with your camera. If you do not have it, then this survey will cost a little more - 700 rubles.

In the winter season, you can chat with the dolphin Dart in the dolphinarium pool.

Dolphin therapy

Studies have been conducted on the basis of the dolphinarium. As a result, it was proved that these marine animals have a positive effect on humans. Experts say that dolphin therapy is an active method of medical and psychological treatment.

It was noted that the greatest healing effect of communication with dolphins is observed in children who have suffered extreme stress. Fourteen years ago, the Crimean government recognized dolphin therapy as the best development in the spa industry on the peninsula. True, the mechanism of positive influence is not yet fully understood.

But after such a session, at least, you can get rid of depression and bad mood. In addition, marine doctors will help overcome stress. After several sessions of dolphin therapy, many patients who suffer from speech or mental pathologies, enuresis and other similar diseases recover partially or completely. Therefore, research is ongoing in this area. All this is done to increase the effectiveness of such treatment, as well as to understand in more detail the mechanism of such a healing process.

Opening hours, ticket price

You can visit the dolphinarium in Yevpatoria during the performances. You can see the performance of marine artists in the afternoon (at 11 and 16 hours), as well as in the evening (at 19 hours).

The price of an adult ticket is 500 rubles, and a child ticket (under 11 years old) - 400 rubles. Children under five can attend the dolphinarium for free (Yevpatoriya).

The address of the institution will help to find it without any problems: Republic of Crimea, the city of Yevpatoriya, Kievskaya street 19/20. If you have questions, you can call: (06569) 2-70-99.
