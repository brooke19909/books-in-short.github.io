What needs to be done before starting work?





Of course, first of all, care should be taken to restore order in the workplace, so as not to lose tools during the operation. When everything is ready, open the package and see what you can find there. The complete set in sets is usually such: a cloth made of microfiber, an alcohol wipe, a protective glass. In some cases, additional elements may be present, such as a horizontal smoothing plate. To avoid greasy stains on the screen, wash your hands with soap and dry them thoroughly. Now we move on to the process of applying the constituent elements from the kit.

Cooking screen

If a protective film has already been pasted on your phone , it should be removed. We will not use it anywhere else. After that, take the included alcohol wipe. If it was not supplied in the box, you can soak an ordinary cotton pad in alcohol and it will replace this tool for you. Then we wipe the screen of the device and let it dry. You can speed up the process using a dry rag, or better yet, a microfiber rag.

Sticking process

You may notice that on the side on which the adhesive is applied, the glass is covered with a special layer of film. It will need to be removed before gluing to the screen. After you have removed the layer, you need to properly position the glass, and then lay it on the surface of the device.

It is unlikely that someone will be able to perfectly stick the glass right away. Each user can make sure that under the new coating there will be small bubbles from the remaining air inside.
