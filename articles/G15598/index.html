Highscreen Zera S: customer reviews and experts

A fairly advanced entry-level smartphone is Highscreen Zera S. The feedback from the owners of the device, the technical specifications and hardware capabilities of this gadget are what will be considered in the framework of this short article.





Equipment

This is a budget-class device, as a result - standard equipment Highscreen Zera S. Reviews speak of the presence of only charging, cord, speaker system and, of course, user instructions. The first and second accessories are backlit. Moreover, if the battery is fully charged, then they are blue, otherwise, that is, in the process of charging the battery, red. But the stereo headset looks like Apple EarPods, but with the only difference that the sound quality is far from ideal. Therefore, lovers of high-quality sound will have to purchase a good speaker system without fail . As with most similar devices, an external drive will have to be purchased separately.





Design, ergonomics and usability

No exception among the devices of the initial segment in terms of appearance and Highscreen Zera S BLACK. This is a classic candy bar with touch input. Its body is made of plastic, and the quality is poor. A thin layer of paint is applied to the back panel of the gadget, which is erased during operation. To some extent, this problem can be solved with the help of a bumper cover, but you will have to buy it for an additional fee.

Non-standardly located physical buttons to control the device. The one that is responsible for locking the smartphone is on the left edge. But the volume swing is brought to the opposite side and located on the right side of the device. The familiar touch buttons harmoniously fit into the bottom of the front of the smart phone under the screen. Engineers did not forget about their backlighting, and this makes it much easier to control the device in the dark.

The Highscreen Zera S phone is based on a processor that has already proven itself in the market of mobile devices - this is the MT6582. Only here before the gadgets that were equipped with it belonged to the middle segment, and now these are budget class devices. It consists of four computing modules of the "A7" architecture, operating at a frequency of 1.3 GHz in peak computing mode. Graphics card and graphics subsystem

The central link in the graphics subsystem is the Mali-400MP2 graphics card. This is also a time-tested solution that has managed to prove itself well. It is this component that organizes the output of the image on the screen of the device. Highscreen Zera S has a standard display, like entry-level devices. An overview of its parameters and characteristics is not impressive, but this is enough for such a device. The matrix that underlies the display is made using one of the most advanced technologies to date - IPS. The display resolution is 540x960, and this is quite enough for high-quality output of the image on the screen: more than 16 million different color shades.

Photo and video

The main camera is based on a 5 megapixel sensor. The developers did not forget about autofocus and LED backlight. But still the photo is far from the best quality. It is preferable to take pictures in good light. Then the result will be the best. A similar situation with video recording in Highscreen Zera S BLACK. Reviews indicate flawless technical specifications - shooting is in HD quality. But the image stabilization system is far from ideal. There is also a front camera, the sensitive element of which is based on a 2 megapixel sensor. It is great for making video calls on Skype or on third-generation mobile networks. Unfortunately, the Highscreen Zera S smartphone cannot boast an impressive amount of RAM. Reviews indicate that this device has 1 GB of RAM, and the internal storage capacity is 4 GB. RAM, of course, may be less, for example 256 MB or 512 MB, but 1 GB is the minimum amount that allows you to run most applications and utilities today. 2.5 GB is allocated for the user's needs in the built-in drive, and the rest is occupied by system software. Autonomy

One of the weaknesses is the autonomy of Highscreen Zera S. Reviews about the battery indicate that its capacity (1800 mAh) is enough for 1-2 days of operation at a mediocre load level. If this device is used more intensively, then this indicator will be reduced to eight hours. But in the maximum energy saving mode, you can stretch a maximum of 3 days with a minimum load on the device. The main problem is the lack of optimization of system software. Accordingly, the battery life on this gadget is less than that of similar devices.
