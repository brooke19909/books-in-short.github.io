Rawai Beach (Phuket): reviews, descriptions, hotels, cafes

Rawai is a small village located at the very edge of the ocean. It is she who is the founder of the tourism business in Phuket, which was started by local residents. They came to the beach on weekends to swim, relax and sunbathe under the gentle rays of the sun.

General facts

Rawai, Phuket, is located about seventeen kilometers from the city. Now Rawai is perceived as a starting point for numerous excursions, which are put forward several times a day in different directions. In 2007, a mall was erected here, which was originally conceived as a walking place for residents, as well as for fishing enthusiasts. It is here that along the coast you can find "parked" light boats that you can rent for any amount of time and embark on a sea expedition to neighboring islands.

Another thing is evening. It is at dusk that Rawai pier in Phuket, Sea Jeepsie Village, and this is the name of the mall, comes to life and turns into a concentration of recreational activities that are enjoyed not only by travelers, but also by local residents. Bright lights shine with bars where local music groups, restaurants and discos perform. Cafes on Rawai Beach are notable for their unbridled fun and tear-down discos at the end of the work week.

Road to the beach

Getting to Rawai is easy, because everywhere there are many signs. The main thing is to follow the Hanging Road. It runs parallel to the coast and leads straight to Rawai beach, Phuket. You need to follow up to the dead end on the pier, then turn right - and you are on the beach. There is no further road, so you need to park in a convenient place and, taking everything you need, move on foot.

The beach itself

Description of Rawai beach in Phuket is by no means the most colorful. For a relaxing holiday and "felting" in the sun, it is not suitable, and swimming in the water is also not advisable. Attractions on the beach are numerous seafood restaurants. In addition, the owners of long boats and boats were concentrated here. At any time, you can hire one of the ships and go on a trip to the neighboring islands. The beauty of such a rental is that the captain of the boat or boat will wait for you as long as you need for a leisurely walk around the island and bring you back. Thus, no need to worry about the return trip home.

The special charm of Rawai beach, Phuket, is a gastronomic trip. Numerous restaurants provide the option of ordering food. Couriers deliver it to you anywhere. You can order delicious food and sit on the beach, suppose to contemplate the sunset. Pay attention to what institutions the Thais themselves prefer. They love local cuisine and know a lot about where they serve delicious, reasonably priced dishes. Beautiful views, coupled with great food, can stop time in Phuket and completely immerse yourself in the culture and life of this paradise.

"Thai Dream Pool House"

Particular attention should be paid to the place of residence. Hotels in Rawai, Phuket, differ not only in the full list of services needed by the traveler, but also in the magnificent views that open from their windows. For example, "Thai Dream Pool House" is the most popular hotel of the highest category among tourists. Despite its status, the room price is consistent with the quality of the services provided. This hotel must be booked in advance in any season.

A small one-story hotel created by European standards with the involvement of national motives. Each room is equipped with all necessary household appliances: air conditioning, refrigerator, in which you can store your own food and drinks, hairdryer, iron. The bathroom has a full range of personal hygiene items, slippers, a bathrobe and several towels. In the courtyard of the hotel, the administration created a lounge for midday or evening relaxation, there is an outdoor pool with views of the nature. Check in before noon. If you need any additional accessories, such as a changing table for babies, it is better to notify the hotel staff in advance.

'Rawai Art D2 Villa'

"Neko Kat Cafe"

Reviews of Rawai Beach in Phuket are closely related to food. It is not surprising, because the beach is the concentration of cozy cafes with dishes for every taste, where Thais themselves love to relax. Sweet tooth and vegetarian food enthusiasts will find it helpful to know the following address: Rawai Beach, 39/57 Soi Saiyuan. This is a cafe serving vegetarian food as well as gluten-free desserts and drinks. The interior of the coffee shop is decorated in light pink colors, where it will be a pleasure to spend time not only for adults, but also for children. Soft cozy sofas, pillows scattered on the floor, allow you to take the most comfortable pose for visitors. The cafe has its own highlight - a huge red cat that meets visitors, loves attention and affection. The cat quietly allows himself to stroke and play with him, which is very popular with the children.

The average bill in a cafe is about ten dollars, the institution works from Wednesday to Monday from ten in the morning to seven in the evening.

No matter what fish delicacies Rawai Beach in Phuket is famous for! Reviews from tourists not only praise local food and drinks, but also strongly recommend visiting the Coffe Tribe Phuket cafe. Equipped with a special track for wheelchairs. Wi-Fi is free for guests. The average bill varies from two hundred to five hundred rubles, which is much cheaper than in the city center.
