First stage

The master class (MK) "Lamb from kapron" will begin with a list of what you need to prepare for work. So, buy or get the following from the cabinet:

  • kapron (factory production or ordinary tights - both beige and color);
  • synthetic winterizer;
  • threads
  • pins
  • cardboard;
  • wire;
  • scissors;
  • glue;
  • decor (textured yarn, beads, bows, braid, eyes, cilia);
  • magnetic base if necessary.

What you need depends on the kind of souvenir you need to make.

Manufacturing technology

No matter what idea you take as a basis, so that you get a beautiful sheep with your own hands from kapron, you need to do the following:

  • take kapron and cut it into elements of the appropriate size;
  • the nodule is pulled together on the reverse side, the fabric surface is fixed;
  • all the details you have to create by wrapping the material around the filler or base;
  • each part can be given additional relief, making thread stitches in appropriate places;
  • combine all the blanks into a single object, stitching them or gluing them (depending on their size and ease of use);
  • you can paint some details with a brush and paint, and so that the pigment does not ‚Äúfall through‚Äù the nylon layer, the material should be pre-coated with PVA glue;

How to make a face

So, you decided to learn how to make a lamb from kapron. You'll have to start with making the head. It will be an indispensable part of any souvenir. Even in a small magnet, one cannot do without it. The process will consist of the following steps:

  1. Take the capron of the right size and put a synthetic winterizer on it.
  2. Fold it all into a rounded shape resembling a pouch.
  3. Tie the loose ends together with a thread and sew.




4. To make the ears, make a loop of the desired size and shape of the wire, twisting the ends into a single rod. Wrap the blank with capron and secure with thread or wire at the base of the loop. If you put a synthetic winterizer inside, the details will be softer and more voluminous.



5. Draw, embroider the expression of the face. It is good to use purchased eyes and cilia. Beads are also suitable.

The head of the animal is ready. Decorate it with a bow, attach it to a fluffy base - and the souvenir will be made.

Kapron lamb magnet: workshop

The simplest souvenir is easy to make in a few minutes. The sequence of work will be as follows:

  1. Cut a circle of the desired diameter from the cardboard according to the dimensions of the future magnet.
  2. Take a capron and put a blank on it, and on top a little sintepon, then connect the free sides of the material, giving the product the appearance of a bag, tie or sew the ends with threads. You can collect the "knot" on the front side, if you have it covered by the head of a lamb. If it will not be centered, then, so that the nodule is not visible, run it on the back. Glue the magnet to it.
  3. Make a head with ears and a muzzle according to the technology of the previous section.
  4. Connect the parts.
  5. Sew decorative white yarn to simulate the texture of the wool.
  6. The legs can be made of thread, at the ends of which beads are fixed that act as hooves.

By changing the color of the capron and decor, you can make different versions of such magnets. Souvenirs on the refrigerator are a nice gift for any holiday. In addition, this animal is a symbol of this year, respectively, such souvenirs can be presented to all born in the Year of the Sheep or under the sign of the zodiac Aries.





In addition, it is perfectly acceptable to combine materials, making the basis of any other fabric, and the muzzle and ears of nylon.
