Aya Lake Mountain Altai

In the south of Western Siberia is a mountainous country - Altai. This region is famous for its unique natural features and rich cultural heritage. Here is the highest mountain of Siberia - Belukha. The mountain is considered sacred, according to legend, it is here that the "navel of the Earth" is located, giving life energy to all living things and giving new knowledge, and also here, in the bowels of Belukha, is the entrance to the fantastic country of Shambhala - the abode of gods and saints.

The most important natural wealth of this region is water resources. There are numerous rivers, the main of which is Katun, lakes, among which the famous Teletskoye Lake, which is a symbol of the republic. Manzherok Lake is very popular and Lake Aya Gorny Altai has about seven thousand lakes, most of which are in the mountains. Almost all of them are cold.

A favorite vacation spot for locals is Lake Aya. Gorny Altai borders on the Altai Territory, whose inhabitants also like to relax here, especially since it can be easily reached both by personal transport and by bus. Aya Lake is the warmest lake in the Republic. Despite the fact that cold springs feeding the lake beat from the bottom , the upper layer of water warms up to +24 degrees on hot days.

In the vicinity of Lake Aya, many camp sites were built - they literally dotted the shores of Katun and Ai Lake. They all differ in terms of service and, of course, in price. There is also a clear separation by the age of vacationers and their preferences. Among the camp sites, Altan stands out especially. Altai Mountains attracts more and more tourists every year, so camp sites grow like mushrooms after rain, but Altan is especially popular. There are deluxe and junior suites located in comfortable buildings. There are also ten insulated houses with a veranda and fifty bungalows with amenities at the base. The base can accommodate 188 people.

If someone from young married couples or youth groups wants to spend a vacation with benefit and without going into excessive spending, then Altan would be the most suitable option. Well-groomed territory, an extensive excursion program, and the presence of extreme entertainment make the camp site well suited specifically for young people. For young families with children, there are also all conditions.
