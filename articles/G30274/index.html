Atlantica Stavrolia Gardens 3 - budget hotel in Ayia Napa

Atlantica Stavrolia Gardens 3 is a small but very welcoming hotel located in one of the most popular resorts in the world, in Cyprus. Every year, tens of thousands of visitors from different countries and cities come here to relax. Before collecting things on a trip, they have an important task - the choice of hotel. As a budget option, you can consider exactly Atlantica Stavrolia Gardens 3.





Exotic relaxation in a cozy place

First of all, it should be noted that this is not a pathos hotel complex consisting of many buildings and individual bungalows. No, Atlantica Stavrolia Gardens 3 is the perfect hotel for couples or those looking for privacy.

The location of the hotel is excellent - it is located in the heart of the resort city of Ayia Napa (by the way, the most popular on the island). Moreover, this guest house is not so far from the airport - about 45 kilometers. It is located in the city of Larnaca. This is a very good location, because such a distance is enough so that flying planes do not interfere with the quiet rest of visitors, but not so much that it is convenient to get to it.

Facilities

So, in Atlantica Stavrolia Gardens 3, guests are offered very comfortable conditions. All rooms are decorated in a cozy home style and furnished with new comfortable furniture. Each apartment has individual air conditioning, a balcony, and a private bathroom with shower. Also in the rooms there is satellite TV and such pleasant things as a hairdryer, radio, telephone and even a safe. True, for the latter it will be necessary to pay extra, but this is only if the guest needs this service.

There is also a refrigerator in the apartment. In standard rooms it is installed for a fee, and in family rooms - just like that. Coffee / tea making facilities, ironing facilities and a kitchen are also provided. Only the latter is available to those guests who have booked a family apartment.





Services and Facilities

Finally, it's worth talking about what services the Atlantica Stavrolia Gardens 3 hotel offers. In fact, the hotel has everything that is needed - both for adults and for children. For young guests, for example, there are separate pools, special cots (available on request) and high chairs in the restaurant.

By the way, the beach is very close - just one kilometer. There are sun umbrellas and sun loungers - all in order to make tourists feel comfortable. And of course, the hotel itself has an outdoor pool.
Worth a few words to say about the staff. The apartments are cleaned every day, and not superficially, but in the most thorough way. At the reception, tourists are met warmly, if guests have any questions, they are always happy to help. And in the restaurants, guests are served by polite and pleasant waiters who are always interested in what their guest would like. All in all, a great budget option. In any case, so say people who have already stayed at this place.
