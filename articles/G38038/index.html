Boutique hotel "Mona" (Lobnya): description, photos, prices and reviews

Mona Boutique Hotel is located in Lobne (Moscow Region), a 15-minute drive from Sheremetyevo Airport. This is a hotel complex consisting of three buildings, one of which houses rooms and a restaurant of the same name, and two other buildings were created to organize various events. The boutique hotel "Mona" (Lobnya), as presented by its owners, is an ideal option for a chic vacation. And is it really so, it will become clear after reading our article.





Hotel description and room stock

"Mona" is a beautiful hotel, made in a modern style, which is perfect for a business trip or for a romantic weekend. But the surrounding area is especially attractive: well-groomed paths, beautiful shrubs, trees. On the whole, the atmosphere of peace and tranquility prevails here.

Boutique hotel "Mona" (Lobnya): entertainment and services for guests

The following entertainments are provided for guests:

  • playroom;
  • darts;
  • table tennis;
  • karaoke;
  • children playground.

Those who prefer a relaxing holiday have the opportunity to enjoy the pleasant air while strolling through the park adjacent to the hotel. You can sunbathe on the terrace or use the barbecue.





Food and events

Boutique Hotel Mona (Lobnya) has a cocktail bar and a restaurant. The second institution is located directly in the garden, here you can enjoy the taste of European cuisine. Gourmet spirits and soft drinks are served in the bar.

Buffet breakfast is available at an additional cost. It is also possible to hold weddings, anniversaries and other events, including corporate ones. Prior approval by the administration is required. By contacting the hotel, the client will receive a full consultation regarding such events and answers to all questions of interest.

Cost of living in a boutique hotel

Boutique Hotel Mona (Lobnya) offers accommodation options that will suit customers with any budget. The price varies between 5-11 thousand rubles. It is important to know that children under 12 years old are accommodated in additional places for free. Older teens, as well as adults who need another bed in a room, must pay 1,500 rubles per night for it.





Boutique hotel "Mona 4 *": reviews

The hotel receives both positive and negative reviews. If you study them, it becomes clear that a negative opinion about the hotel developed among those people who lived in the simplest room (this is the Single category). The big minus is the lack of plain water, which is usually offered to people to take from coolers. The only thing that does not cause controversy is the beautiful surroundings of the hotel and the attractive exterior.

There are reviews in which guests write that they are 100% satisfied with the rest in this institution. The fashionable boutique hotel "Mona" attracts with its accommodation conditions and interior decoration. And of course, a separate plus for the surrounding park, which is simply nice to stroll.
