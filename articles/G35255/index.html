Telstar 3 * (Rimini) - photos, prices and reviews of tourists

Today Rimini (Italy) is one of the most popular resorts of the Adriatic. Every year, hundreds of thousands of travelers from all over the world come here, including from Russia. The developed infrastructure, a wide hotel base, lots of excellent shops, and, of course, 15 kilometers of luxurious beaches and the presence of historical and cultural monuments attract tourists. If you plan to visit this pearl of the Adriatic Sea and at the same time are looking for a budget option for accommodation, we recommend Hotel Telstar 3 *. Next, we will tell you more about this hotel, and also find out whether our compatriots were satisfied with the rest here.





Where is the hotel located

Hotel Telstar 3 * is located almost in the center of the Italian city of Rimini, in an area called Rivazzurra. Not far from the hotel there are such famous sights as New Fiabilandia (this is a large amusement park), the Congress Center, the Temple of Malatest, and the Arch of Emperor Augustus. Rimini is also a major transport hub: there is an airport, a railway station, and intercity buses run regularly. Since the vast majority of tourists come to the Adriatic coast, including for an excellent beach holiday, when choosing a hotel it is very important for them to find out the proximity of the beach. As for the Telstar Rivazzurra 3 * hotel, it is located on the second coastline. The distance to the beach is only fifty meters, but along the way you need to cross the road and overcome the promenade stretching along the coastline.





How to get to the hotel

Even if you plan on your own and without the help of a travel agency to go on a trip, one of the points of which will be the city of Rimini (Italy), you will not have any problems with how to get here. So, just a few kilometers from the city center there is an international airport named after Federico Fellini. Airplanes from cities in Italy and other countries regularly fly here. From the air harbor, the center of Rimini can be reached by train. By the way, if you are traveling in Italy, then moving between cities here is very convenient by rail. Also keep in mind that at the height of the tourist season, additional bus lines link the airport with the center of Rimini. So without much effort and money, you can quickly reach your destination.

In addition, if you are planning a trip to other cities in Italy or even Europe, then a good solution would be to travel by rail. As for Rimini, trains depart from its central station to cities such as Bologna, Turin, Milan, Ancona, Lecce, Verona, Ferrara, Paris, Munich and others. From the train station to the Telstar 3 * hotel can be reached by taxi.

In addition, from other cities in Italy you can get to Rimini by intercity bus. For example, a bus from Rome goes here for about five hours, and a ticket will cost 25 euros.

Description and photos of the Telstar Hotel

The building of the hotel in question was built in the late sixties of the last century. However, restoration work was regularly carried out here , so today Telstar 3 * is a completely modern comfortable hotel that has everything necessary for an excellent rest for guests of the infrastructure. In total there are 90 rooms. They are decorated in a simple style and have everything you need. Guests can enjoy the on-site restaurant and bar. This three-star hotel is perfect for people who want a comfortable and budgetary vacation on the Adriatic Sea, combining a beach holiday with shopping and a variety of entertainment.

Hotel Policies

Like the vast majority of other hotels around the world, Telstar has its own estimated time (time of arrival and departure of guests). So, the settlement of tourists in the rooms is carried out in the afternoon. If you arrive at the hotel earlier, then in case of availability of rooms you will not be forced to wait, but will be accommodated immediately. As for the date of departure, the occupied room should be vacated before 10 a.m. At the same time, it is necessary to hand over the keys to the reception and pay for the entire stay at Hotel Telstar Rimini 3 *. Please note that in accordance with local laws, you can pay in cash only for an amount not exceeding 999 euros. In other cases, the hotel will be happy to accept payment by credit card "Visa", "Mastercard", "American Express" or "Maestro". If you use a plastic card issued by another payment system, then the possibility of using it should be taken to the hotel in advance.

Accommodation for tourists with children

The hotel in question is always very happy for travelers who are on vacation with children of all ages. In this case, if you place a baby up to two years old in your room on existing beds, you will not have to pay extra for this. Also note that the Telstar 3 * hotel (Rimini) does not have the possibility of extra beds in the rooms. Therefore, when making a reservation, you should choose a room that has the required number of beds.





Accommodation for tourists with pets

No extra charge for this. Therefore, you can absolutely safely take your pet with you on holiday in Rimini .

Number of rooms

Hotel Telstar 3 * (Rimini) offers its guests to stay in one of 90 comfortable rooms. All rooms here are standard and are designed for one, two, three or four guests. Each room has air conditioning, a private bathroom with shower, TV, telephone. All rooms have balconies, most of which offer wonderful views of the azure waters of the Adriatic. The rooms are regularly cleaned, as well as linen and towels. In addition, all hotel guests have the opportunity to order food and drinks in the room. This service is paid.





Nutrition

The price includes breakfast. You can have breakfast either going down to the restaurant or ordering food directly to your room. During the day, you can also eat in the hotel restaurant. By the way, the cook in Telstar 3 * - reviews of tourists confirm this fact - is a true master of his craft. So you can taste delicious dishes of both Italian and international cuisine here. In addition, the hotel has a bar with a wide selection of soft and alcoholic drinks for every taste.

Beach vacation

The hotel in question is located on the second coastline. The nearest beach can be reached in just a few minutes. At the same time, on the way you must cross the road and cross the promenade, which stretches along the coast. In general, Rimini has 15 kilometers of excellent beaches. All are equipped with umbrellas and sunbeds. In addition, there is equipment and equipment for entertainment and sports on the water.





Infrastructure

Hotel Telstar 3 * (Rimini) has everything you need for a comfortable stay of guests. So, in addition to comfortable rooms, there is a restaurant and a bar. It is possible to order food directly to your room. The reception at the hotel is open 24 hours a day, so at any time for or at night you can contact the on-duty administrator if necessary. You can also store valuables in the safe at reception. The hotel staff will be happy to recommend the best restaurants, shops, nightclubs and other establishments in the area. The hotel has a small cozy garden where you can comfortably sit down for a relaxing holiday. There is public parking near the hotel, which you can use if you decide to rent a car.

Cost of living

As for the price of accommodation in the Telstar Rivazzurra 3 * hotel (Rimini), it fully corresponds to the conditions offered here and the level of stardom. So, accommodation for seven days will cost you in the amount of 21 thousand rubles for a single room, from 31 thousand rubles for a double room, from 40 thousand rubles for a triple room and from 48 thousand rubles for a quadruple room. The indicated prices are relevant for the height of the tourist season (July-August). In less popular months with tourists, the cost of hotel accommodation will be lower. In addition to the cost of living with travelers, the so-called city tax will also be withheld. It is one and a half euros per person per night. This tax does not apply to children under the age of fourteen.





Telstar 3 * (Rimini): reviews of tourists from Russia

In order for you to have a more accurate impression of the hotel in question, we suggest that you familiarize yourself with the comments of our compatriots who stayed here during their stay in Italy. In general, it should be noted that most of the tourists were quite satisfied with their choice of accommodation. However, we suggest learning more about everything.

As for the hotel rooms themselves, they, as stated in the description, are cozy and comfortable. The situation in them is not new, but the furniture is in good condition, and household appliances and plumbing are working properly. Clean the rooms regularly. Sometimes maids forget to change bed linens on time, but a reception at the reception quickly solves this problem.

Our compatriots also liked the location of Telstar 3 *. So, the Rivazzurra area is quite calm. The center of Rimini is within walking distance. Also pleased with the tourists and the fact that next to the hotel there is an area on which any events are constantly held. So, animators work here, artists perform, the market comes, etc. Also nearby is a large amusement park and several attractions of the city. In short, going beyond the threshold of the hotel, you will always find something to do.

As for meals at the Telstar Hotel, according to tourists, it is in full compliance with the three-star level. Everything is quite tasty and quite diverse. However, experienced travelers recommend diversifying their own menu, having lunch and dinner outside the hotel. Moreover, in Rimini there are many different restaurants, cafes and taverns for every taste.

The main delight of our compatriots who visited here was caused by the Adriatic Sea. The beaches in Rimini are sandy and pebble, very clean, with a gentle entry into the water. Almost everywhere you can stay for free on your towel. If you want to use a sun lounger or parasol, you will have to pay for it. Also for a fee on the beaches you can rent equipment for water activities.

Summing up, we can say that if you are looking for a comfortable accommodation in Rimini at an affordable price and do not plan to spend whole days in your room, then the three-star Telstar Hotel, located in the Rivazzurra district, is perfect for you.
