Hotel Ozkaymak Falez Hotel 5 * (Turkey, Antalya): reviews

Among Russian tourists, much attention is attracted to resorts located in Turkey. There is a huge amount of entertainment, as well as luxurious facilities for staying in beautiful hotels on the shores of a clean and warm sea. One of these is Ozkaymak Falez Hotel 5 *, which is located in the largest and most popular resort on the Mediterranean coast - in Antalya.

general information

This hotel was built relatively long ago - in 1990. However, at the moment he does not cease to receive guests on his territory. Moreover, Ozkaymak Falez Hotel pleases visitors with excellent conditions for a luxurious stay, as well as a large number of entertainment available on its territory.

As tourists note in their reviews, the hotel offers travelers decent conditions for relaxation, as well as comfortable rooms, which are regularly renovated to maintain their condition. Both small and large-scale reconstruction are also carried out on the territory of the hotel as a whole. Location

The hotel is located on the shores of the Mediterranean Sea, in a zone of pleasant tropical climate. Guests who visit its territory in the midst of the spa season can enjoy a large number of sunny days, as well as high air temperatures, significantly mitigated by the influence of air masses from the sea.

Ozkaymak Falez Hotel 5 * is located in Turkey, in one of the largest resort regions of the country - in Antalya. Not far from it is the central part of a huge city, in which a large amount of entertainment for every taste is concentrated.

In their reviews of the hotel, many tourists talk about one very positive point in the location of the place to stay - the hotel is located near Antalya International Airport, literally 15 km from it.

Rooms

The Ozkaymak Falez Hotel is located in a large 13-storey building of the hotel and consists of 342 apartments presented in the best categories. As the guests note in the comments, each room has been modernized and all the conditions for the most comfortable stay are provided. Guests of any apartment can enjoy the pleasant air temperature, which is ensured by a modern air conditioning system installed in the rooms. In addition, throughout the hotel there is access to the Internet (Wi-Fi).

All rooms of the Ozkaymak Falez Hotel (Turkey / Antalya) are equipped with private bathrooms, which have absolutely all the amenities needed to clean up. Attention tourists are offered towels, bathrobes and slippers, as well as sets of bath accessories. In addition, modern plumbing and mirrors are installed here.

Standard

The rooms of this category, presented at the Ozkaymak Falez Hotel 5 * (Turkey / Antalya), offer guests a small area (28 sq. M.), Which consists of one room. Such apartments are designed to accommodate a couple of guests for whom there is a large double bed, in some rooms it is replaced by two single beds. All apartments of this category, of which the hotel has 320, are equipped with individual balconies, which can be accommodated for a comfortable stay. To do this, there is a pair of armchairs, as well as a small coffee table.

A large wardrobe is provided in the room as a place for guests' clothes, as well as a pair of cabinets for smaller accessories. It is equipped with a small workplace, represented by a table and chair, as well as a large mirror.

As for the technical equipment of the room, it has a large TV with the ability to view satellite channels (in the reviews of the Ozkaymak Falez Hotel it is said that there are many Russian-speaking ones among them), as well as a safe and minibar, the content of which is updated every day fresh alcohol-free drinks.

Conditions for children

Many tour operators recommend this place for a family vacation. A large number of recommendations of this type are due to the fact that the Ozkaymak Falez Hotel (Antalya) provides all the conditions for a comfortable stay for tourists who want to relax with their children.

In particular, upon a separate preliminary request, tourists are entitled to free cots in any type of apartment. Their number in the hotel is limited, so the administration strongly recommends that you reserve a bed for a child in advance.

Among the conditions for children, there is also a small pool located in the open air in the aquatic area. In the restaurant, parents have the right to request a special child seat.

A number of reviews about the Ozkaymak Falez Hotel 5 *, left by parents who spent their time at the hotel with their children, indicate that there is no possibility of renting baby pots and prams among the services of the place for recreation. This, in their opinion, is not a positive thing.

Nutrition

Tourists who are on vacation at this hotel very often share their positive comments regarding the food offered at local restaurants. As part of the all-inclusive system, hotel guests have the opportunity to visit the excellent Turkish restaurant Royal Restaurant, which serves a daily buffet. You can visit it at any time from 7 a.m. to 11 p.m. Travelers are also pleased with the fact that in addition to delicious dishes, here you can taste local alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks absolutely free.

In addition to the huge restaurant, the hotel offers two bars, which also have an all-inclusive concept. One of them is located in the main building of the hotel, and the second - in the area of ‚Äã‚Äãthe aquazone. At the bar you can always enjoy light snacks and salads, prepared in the style of Turkish, Mediterranean and European cuisines, as well as fresh fruits, ice cream, tea, coffee and other types of drinks.

Aquazone

In their reviews, left on social networks and on the expanses of thematic sites, they share their impressions of visiting the large water zone, which is available on the territory of the Ozkaymak Falez Hotel (Turkey). Here, according to vacationers, it is always very comfortable and cozy. If desired, any tourist has the opportunity to spend time by the pool with a glass of cocktail in his hands.

The water area itself is represented by a large sun terrace, in the center of which there are two huge niches filled with clean and warm water. They have an automatic cleaning system installed, but there is no heating - the optimum water temperature is achieved under the influence of sunlight. Spa

The building of the main building of the hotel has a large spa area, which is also very popular among its guests. It consists of a center where various cosmetic procedures are carried out for the body, face, hair, as well as the massage room. All services that are provided in this center are paid. If desired, tourists relaxing in the hotel can spend time in a hammam or sauna, which is also in the Ozkaymak Falez Hotel (Antalya). You can take a steam bath in them for free.

Sport

Fans of an active lifestyle can afford to maintain their health and fitness while on vacation. For this purpose, the hotel has a large gym equipped with a considerable number of simulators designed to work with different muscle groups. Here, each visitor can pay attention to a professional and knowledgeable trainer. Next to the hall there is a large room for playing table tennis. Visiting these places is completely free, like all the services that are provided in them.

For a fee, hotel guests can relax in the billiard room, where there are several tables for the game.

Entertainment

According to tourists, some of its representatives speak Russian quite well, which is certainly good.

If desired, vacationers can take part in small sports events that are held in the aquazone area. Some of them are held in the morning, and some in the evening. At this time, the attention is also paid to the team of animators.

Beach

Many tourists who relax in the Ozkaymak Falez Hotel say that not far from it there is an excellent clean private beach, closed to unauthorized persons. They consider this a positive moment, especially in the case of holidays with children.

The total length of the beach strip is just over 50 meters, it is completely covered with small pebbles, which quickly heats up and keeps the temperature optimal for the body. As for the sea, it is clean here, descent into the water occurs gradually, which is especially appreciated by tourists who organized a family vacation with young children at the hotel.

Staying on the beach, you can use free sun loungers, as well as towels, which you can always take on a deposit of $ 10. Umbrellas and mattresses are provided to hotel guests for free.

Along the coast, vacationers can find some entertainment that will definitely appeal to extremals. These include water skiing, jet skiing, as well as inflatable pillows. In addition, tourists can always rent a yacht or boat in order to go on a short and not very long journey through the waters of the Mediterranean Sea.

Prices

As for the cost of a ticket to this hotel, then, according to vacationers, it is quite acceptable. In travel agencies of the Russian capital, a tour to Ozkaymak Falez can be offered at a cost of about 60,000 rubles. This price is set based on a six-day stay for two on an all-inclusive basis. In addition, the indicated price includes payment for a round-trip transfer, as well as a flight by plane to Antalya Airport.

Some travel agencies offer vacationers to include excursions to local attractions in the price of the tour. However, most travelers who have visited Antalya more than once recommend choosing excursion trips and paying for them while in Turkey. As a rule, this is much cheaper than in Moscow. Finding such opportunities while at the hotel is not difficult, since the reception has all the information about upcoming excursions.
