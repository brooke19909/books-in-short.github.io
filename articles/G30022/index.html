Work and activities of Sberbank in the Czech Republic

As it turned out, in the Czech Republic they have long known about Sberbank. It was here that representatives of this financial leader, long known in the domestic banking services market, first made themselves known in 1993. Four years later, Sberbank in the Czech Republic received an official license allowing him to act as an independent joint stock company.

It was then that the first branches of the bank began to open in Europe, gradually moving into a small branch network.





A brief history of the establishment of the representative

Sberbank was able to finalize its rights right after the purchase of shares in a small Austrian credit organization called Volksbank in 2012. At that time, this commercial company was part of a rather influential and well-known group √ñsterreichischen Volksbanken.

After that, a complete reorganization of the structure of this financial institution took place and a year later the name was changed. According to experts involved in rebranding, it was decided to leave the traditional and recognizable name of Sbarbank. As a result, a representative office appeared under the name Sberbank CZ.





Grand opening and first customers

After purchasing the shares of the aforementioned organization, colossal work was done to completely format the management and reorganization system. The grand opening of the main office of Sberbank, moved to one of the central streets of Prague, took place on March 20, 2013.

During a beautiful and bright opening, the representatives of the organization clearly told, as well as showed everyone the possibilities and features of their work. All those present could go into the department and see for themselves the veracity of these words. Moreover, pleasant surprises awaited all visitors in the form of advantageous financial offers, attractive loan programs, and much more. The guests happily agreed to visit Sberbank in the Czech Republic.





Representative offices of Sberbank today

Over time, Sberbank grew from a small financial division into a small branch network, opened many sales outlets and installed specialized equipment for customer self-service. So, at present in the Czech Republic there are just over 8 large branches of this credit organization, several points of sale and about 20 ATMs and terminals.





Color mismatches

Despite the similarities in the name and general principles of work, the European representative offices have some differences. In particular, they are associated with the atypical color scheme for our financial institution, which is present in the logo and design of the departments. Thus, the subsidiaries of Sberbank, when decorating their halls, somewhat departed from the usual colors of the bank. For example, although there are green tones in the gamut, there are not so many of them.

In contrast, they are black and gold. According to eyewitnesses, a striking example of this is the large branded sign in front of the entrance. She herself is made in black. And the inscription on it is made using special gold stamping.





Such color changes are associated with certain reasons. The fact is that some Sberbank subsidiary banks were opened in historically important places of the city. Moreover, according to tradition, they should not spoil the facade of buildings. Therefore, it was decided to modify the color scheme based on existing building tones.
