What is the most beautiful place on earth? Beautiful places of the world. The most beautiful places on the planet

What is the most beautiful place on earth? There can be no definite answer to this question, because, as you know, how many people have so many opinions. Someone like the creations of human hands, striking in their beauty. It can be architectural structures, botanical gardens or, for example, planetariums. And someone admires the magnificence of nature: impregnable mountains, clear blue lakes, geysers, snow-white beaches and even fire-breathing volcanoes! You will learn about such wonders of the world by reading our article.



Your attention is represented by 10 beautiful places of the world, amazing corners of the globe, created by nature itself. They impress with picturesque landscapes, amaze the imagination with a riot of colors and their grandeur ... It is difficult to even believe that such a thing could actually be. It is necessary to visit here at least in order to understand how versatile and unusual the world is, that it is fraught with many riddles and secrets that have not been solved to this day.

Yellowstone National Park, USA

The amazing Yellowstone National Park is located in the United States in three states: Idaho, Montana and Wyoming. It is famous for its delightfully beautiful geysers, but this is not all that can be admired here. There are many rivers, canyons, and caves in Yellowstone National Park. There is also a huge number of birds and animals. During the tour, it seems that you are watching the Discovery channel live. You can see with your own eyes how a bear knocked a deer or how a coyote hunts. Nature reigns here, doing well without human intervention, which is especially fascinating.



Particular attention of tourists is attracted by the king of the Valley of Geysers - Eye. This is a hot spring colored by bacteria in saturated colors. The shape of the source resembles the eye and it seems that it is watching all visitors to Yellowstone Park.

Lake Baikal, Russia

If you ask the inhabitants of Russia what is the most beautiful place on Earth, they will unanimously name Lake Baikal. Indeed, you cannot call it a miracle of the world. Every year human activity destroys the ecology and destroys nature, but Baikal still continues to please people with its crystal clearness, unusual phenomena and pristine beauty. This lake is not only beautiful, but also important for humanity. It is the world's largest fresh water reservoir. Locals even call it the sea, it is so huge.



Baikal is a source of life. It is not cut off from the mainland, but from neighboring lands is significantly different in its flora and fauna, therefore, it attracts a huge number of not only tourists, but also scientists, researchers.

Bora Bora, French Polynesia

Studying the beautiful places of the world, one can not fail to note the chic island of Bora Bora, which can be deservedly called the pearl of the Pacific Ocean. If you were looking for paradise, then this is exactly what it is: crystal clear water, green vegetation, white sand and hotels located directly on the water. Everything has been created here for a relaxing holiday away from worldly bustle and everyday problems.

In fact, Bora Bora is a mountain range crowned with three peaks. The highest point is Otemanu Peak. It is comfortably located under palm groves among the azure waters of the ocean.



The lagoons of the island are striking in their splendor. Here you can go on a fascinating underwater trip with a snorkel and a mask or scuba diving, during which there is a high probability of meeting a shark or barracuda. But do not worry. You can even feed them, but only under the vigilant supervision of an experienced instructor.

Kelimutu Volcano, Indonesia

In the heart of the Indonesian island of Flores is an unusual volcano Kelimutu. This place on earth is the most beautiful, and all thanks to the unusual crater lakes, the waters of which always have a different color. They appeared after the last eruption, which occurred about 40 years ago.



Each of the reservoirs has its own name. According to local traditions, they absorb the souls of dead people. At the western point of Kelimutu Volcano is the Lake of the Elderly. It is turquoise. The other two are located very close by, and are separated from each other only by the wall of the crater. The lakes of girls and boys are green, and Charmed is blood red. Their waters can change their color from black to red, green or turquoise. According to legend, this happens when souls become angry.

Epupa Waterfall, Namibia

On the Kunene River in southwestern Africa is the delightful Epupa Falls. Its size is not as grandiose as, for example, at Niagara Falls, but nevertheless it is an incredibly picturesque place that is worthy of composing poems about it. From a height of 37 meters, rapid streams of water collapse, and under its drops the sun plays, and a bright rainbow forms. One has only to imagine this in order to understand what feelings arouse the most beautiful places on the planet. Pictures do not convey all this, unfortunately. In order to experience a surge of emotions, one needs to take a look at the splendor of Epupa Falls at least once.





Lencois Maranjenes National Park, Brazil

The Brazilian national park with the romantic name Lencois Maraniensez is a huge space of 1000 square meters. km, completely strewn with golden sand. In fact, this is the Lencois desert, however, it has water, and fresh. The desert is very close to the Amazon, so it often rains heavily. This leads to the fact that lagoons with warm bright turquoise water form in the park.





Hamilton Pool Underground Lake, USA

In the USA, in the state of Texas, there is an unusual lake Hamilton Pool. It is both terrestrial and underground. Its underground part is located under a powerful stone arch smoothly extending deep into the massif. The finishing touch of this amazing landscape is an elegant waterfall, the waters of which fall from a height of 15 meters.



Canyo Crystals River, Colombia

The most beautiful places on planet Earth are not only waterfalls, mountains and geysers. They also include the amazing River Canyo Crystals. This is a unique place that resembles with its appearance the real art canvas of the Impressionists. The river is a series of whirlpools, rapids, hollows and waterfalls. Its waters are amazingly clear: the bottom can be seen even with the naked eye, however, there is no fish in the pond. This is due to some unique characteristics of the channel.

The bed of Kanyo Kristales is painted in five colors: red, yellow, black, green, blue. These are algae vital products, and, over the course of the year, the saturation of color schemes either weakens or intensifies again.





Vaitomo Caves, New Zealand

The most beautiful places on the planet are the corners, when you look at them, your heart begins to beat more often from the realization that everything in front of your eyes is reality. It is this feeling that overwhelms people during their stay in the unique caves of Vaitomo in New Zealand. This is a real masterpiece of nature, over which she worked for many millions of years.

For some time the master here was the ocean. He diligently created bizarre growths and mystical intricacies of moves and paths. But the water receded and left a huge system of 150 caves. In one of them live funny fireflies that glow with a bright green-blue light in the dark, which makes the cave arch look like a real starry sky! About this they say: "It is better to see once than hear a hundred times."

Various events are held in the caves of Vaitomo: on Christmas night, a chapel is sung in the Cathedral Hall, and notorious extremals periodically compete in rafting over black water.
