Hotel Sey Beach Hotel SPA 4 *: description and reviews

Sey Beach Hotel & SPA 4 * in Turkey (Alanya) is one of the most popular hotels, the popularity of which is associated not only with the excellent conditions that are provided for each guest, but also with very pleasant prices - they attract people who want to have fun in an exotic country for small payment.

Very often, trips to this place are offered to travelers who wish to spend a vacation with their family or with their loved one. Often, whole groups of friends come here - the conditions allow tourists wishing to get different opportunities to settle in a comfortable atmosphere.

general information

Sey Beach Hotel & SPA is a large resort hotel that was built recently - for the first time its doors for tourists opened in 2017. Tourists who have already managed to visit it, note that there is everything necessary for the vacation to go bright and leave a good impression for a long time. According to them, technical equipment is very developed here, which is modern, the interior is also fresh, which is evaluated positively.

Location

Sey Beach Hotel & SPA 4 * is located in Alanya, or rather in the small village of this resort region - Kestele. The city center with all its inherent attractions is literally five kilometers from the building. That is why the guests of S ey Beach Hotel & SPA 4 * very often go there in order to have a delicious lunch in a restaurant or cafe, visit a nightclub or karaoke. Seasoned travelers on social networks often share their impressions from visiting a particular place, share their features.

The nearest air traffic hub, where all tourists mostly fly, is 37 kilometers away - in a town called Gazipasha.





Rooms

The total number of rooms in Sey Beach Hotel & SPA 4 * is 100. All of them are divided into two groups: standard and family, depending on how many guests the apartments are designed for. Places for living are concentrated in the main building, which is six-story. It has two elevators, which greatly facilitate the movement of guests between the levels of the building.

Each apartment has a private bathroom, where all the plumbing necessary to maintain hygiene is concentrated and, in addition, several cabinets for storing bath accessories. Also in the rooms of the hotel there are air conditioners that can make the process of living more comfortable, due to the creation of the optimum temperature.

Standards

A total of standard group rooms at the hotel are 85. All of them are quite small in size - up to 26 square meters. m and suggest the settlement in them a pair of guests. Many of them in their reviews of Sey Beach Hotel SPA 4 * talk about how a pleasant atmosphere reigns in the interior of the rooms, filled with sincerity and comfort. Their attention is attracted by wall decor in the form of paintings and dense brown textile, which gives the overall picture a certain elegance.

There is a double bed as sleeping places, next to which are two nightstands. For guests' clothes there is a small cupboard, divided into several shelves, as well as an easy chair and a coffee table, where guests can relax, read their favorite books and drink coffee.

As for the technical equipment of the apartments, they have a large pendant flat-screen TV, satellite channels, many of which are in Russian, are broadcast on it. Guests can make tea or coffee at the electrical substation, as well as use the minibar, which offers not only soft drinks, but also delicious snacks.





Family apartments

Rooms of this class offer conditions that are ideal for three guests. Often, vacationers who rented rooms of this type note the convenience of placing them with their children. If necessary, you can request the installation of a baby bed in the apartment, which will be done completely free of charge.

For adults, the family-type apartments have two beds, one of which is double and the other is single. All of them are covered with orthopedic mattresses and rather expensive hypoallergenic linen. There is a wonderful relaxation area, as well as a part where you can work, it is represented by a large desk and a comfortable chair.

From the equipment there is a TV, telephone, tea substation, safe and minibar.





Conditions for children

The administration of the Sey Beach Hotel & SPA 4 * hotel is positioning it as a place ideal for families, this is often confirmed by guests. As they note, the main convenience when living with a child of any age is the availability of paid babysitting services, which are provided hourly. There is also an excellent mini-club, where funny animators work, able to make a holiday on any ordinary day. If desired, kids can go to the aquazone, where they have a small pool, whose area is 10 square meters. m, the same is available indoors - it becomes especially popular in the winter season.

So that vacationers do not get bored while staying at the Sey Beach Hotel & SPA 4 *, they have a whole program of entertainment organized by a team of animators. The comments about the rest often say that this group includes very active and sociable young people who can find an approach to a guest of any age category and are fluent in Russian, which is very convenient for tourists from Russia and the CIS countries.

Nutrition

Hotel guests often address a lot of positive reviews towards catering establishments, some of which are central to a large restaurant where guests can enjoy a daily buffet. As tourists note, the dishes served here have an unusual taste, they are served very original and bright. As for the cuisine, the food prepared here belongs to European and Turkish cuisine. Special attention was paid to the desserts served in the restaurant - according to the guests, they are special, many recommend them to taste.

Also, a lot of tourists' attention is paid to bars, one of which is located in the aquazone, and the second - in the building. Guests note that here, like in no other place, very original drinks and cocktails are offered. In addition, the menu offers a wide range of snacks, among which, judging by the reviews of gourmets, those made from meat are very popular.

Sports and spa

A large number of guests visit the spa area and gym, which are located in the hotel building. They also recommend that you do this for those who are going to go on vacation at Sey Beach Hotel & SPA 4 *.

Here, according to vacationers, which is reflected in their many reviews, excellent professionals work in the massage room, each of whom is able to relax the client as much as possible. At the same time, you can use cosmetic services.





Fans of "hot" relaxation often visit the sauna or hammam, which is presented here in a classic form. Visitors to this part can use the services of parmesisters, which are completely free.

In the gym, which is also located in the Sey Beach Hotel & SPA 4 * building, a small number of exercise machines are installed, which, according to tourists, is enough to carefully engage in each muscle group. It offers the services of personal trainers who can prescribe a training and nutrition program for an additional fee.

In addition to the gym, on the open square, which adjoins the building of the Sey Beach Hotel & SPA 4 * (Turkey), there are several outdoor areas, ideal for various sports games. Often they host competitions among teams of vacationers.

Beach

The coastline of the Mediterranean Sea is literally 50 meters from the main building of the hotel. You can get to it only by passing the road, which can be done through the underpass. Tourists who relax in the hotel often mark this decision as positive, since it is quite safe, which is especially true for children.

The beach, which is located here, is the responsibility of the hotel and is completely closed to visitors by people who do not reside in it. Here all amenities are provided for guests completely free of charge. In particular, sun loungers and umbrellas are evenly spaced throughout the sand and gravel area, where you can spend your time very comfortably in between water procedures. Towels and air mattresses are also available here for free.

Guests who relax on the beach can use the showers and locker rooms installed here for free.
