Hotel "Segezha": rooms, services and guest reviews

In the central part of the Republic of Karelia there is a city of regional significance Segezha. About thirty thousand people live here. The settlement belongs to single-industry towns with a city-forming enterprise - Segezha Pulp and Paper Mill.

Segezha city, Karelia

The village is located in an area with beautiful Karelian nature: here the river of the same name flows into Lake Vygozero. It was the presence of large water and forest resources that determined the construction of a large pulp and paper mill in these places. There are many enterprises involved in logging and wood processing.
