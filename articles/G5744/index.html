Vincci Resort Nour Palace 5 * (Mahdia, Tunisia): description of rooms, service, reviews

When it comes to so-called all-inclusive holidays, Tunisia comes to the minds of many tourists. This is an interesting country, which is famous for its high-class resorts, vibrant landscapes, a mysterious history and true oriental culture. And the climate here is suitable - good weather lasts almost all year round.

One of the most popular and beautiful resorts in Tunisia is Mahdia. Here, of course, there are many hotels of different categories and sizes. But many wanderers choose the Vincci Resort Nour Palace hotel complex as a temporary refuge.

Of course, when planning a trip, you should find out more about the chosen place. What does a holiday look like on the territory of the city of Mahdia (Tunisia)? What conditions of food and accommodation does the hotel complex offer? Do parents with children choose this hotel? Is there a beach nearby? What do the former guests say? The answers to these questions are worth a look.

Location Information





First of all, it is worth looking for information about the location, because this is a very important factor. Where is this hotel complex located? Madhia, Tunisia - it is in this colorful resort city that the hotel is located, and almost on the seashore.

The distance to the central part of the city with all its amusements is only 6 km. Public transport and special sightseeing buses run from the hotel to the center. The nearest airport is Monastir, 50 km from the property. If you arrived at the airport in Tunisia, then you will have to drive to the hotel for several hours, since it is located 195 km.

What does the territory look like? Infrastructure and General Information

Vincci Resort Nour Palace 5 * is a large hotel complex, which is a five-story building, built in the shape of the letter "P". The building is built in a traditional oriental style. The hotel territory goes almost to the coast.

The hotel is located in the middle of an oasis, surrounded by lush gardens. By the way, this is a pretty secluded place - tourists here can enjoy peace, silence and unity with nature. By the way, the hotel has been hosting travelers since 2002, and only a few years ago a major modernization was carried out here.

Where is the beach?





Of course, most tourists go to Tunisia, hoping to have a good time on the picturesque sea coast. What does the hotel complex Vincci Resort Nour Palace offer its guests? The beach is just a few meters from the hotel's residential building. This is a private stretch of coast, which is intended only for guests to relax - do not worry about security.

The beach here is very beautiful, which travelers confirm in their reviews. The coastal strip is wide and covered with fine soft sand. The entrance to the sea is very convenient. There is also a site where kids love to dabble.

For guests comfortable loungers with mattresses and wide umbrellas are placed - they can be used for free. Even during the high season there are enough deck chairs for everyone. The hotel also provides beach towels, however, on bail, which is then returned. Tourists say this place is perfect for refreshing and relaxing completely.

Fun by the sea

If you belong to the category of tourists who prefer an active pastime, you will not be bored at the Vincci Resort Nour Palace, because there are a lot of entertainments on the shore.

To begin with, it is worth saying that a wonderful volleyball court is equipped here - animators often organize fun competitions between guests. You can ride a canoe, boat or catamaran. For a small fee, tourists are offered to rent a pedal boat and some other motorized types of water transport. Many travelers enjoy water skiing and bananas. Near the beach you can rent equipment for scuba diving, as well as take a quick training course with the subsequent receipt of a certificate.

This coast is perfect for beginners who want to learn the basics of sailing and windsurfing. By the way, during the high season, experienced instructors work on the beach.

Additional Services Information





This is a five-star hotel, so its guests can count on additional amenities - a vacation is unlikely to be overshadowed by any household trifles.

To begin with, it is worth saying that the hotel can easily exchange currency, and the rate here is not bad. Payment cards are also accepted at the reception, which is convenient for many travelers. Guests have the opportunity to rent a safe deposit box - the fee for this service is small, but your valuables will remain under round-the-clock protection.

Part of the hotel's territory is reserved for parking, next to which you can rent vehicles, in particular a bicycle, a moped or a car (rental cost is not too high).

Business people often stay at the hotel, for whom a business center is equipped. For a small fee, you can use a computer (Internet access is available throughout the hotel), a printer, a scanner, send or receive a fax. The building is equipped with several conference rooms of various sizes - their capacity is from 30 to 1300 people. The hotel often organizes various corporate and holiday events.

Hotel Vincci Resort Nour Palace: entertainment for residents





The hotel complex is able to satisfy all the needs of vacationers and provide them with an interesting, eventful leisure. If you prefer to swim in warm fresh water, then keep in mind that most of the courtyard is reserved for a pool divided into levels of different depths. Around there is a spacious terrace with sun loungers and umbrellas - here guests take sunbathing, splashing in the water, get to know and chat with each other, in a word, have fun. And in cold weather, you can relax by the indoor pool (it is equipped with a powerful heating system).

Fans of more active recreation will not be disappointed either. There are three tennis courts on the territory (rental of rackets and balls, as well as evening lighting are paid separately). There is also a small golf course with 9 holes. Every day there is a fitness center with a large gym - the equipment is modern, and instructors are always ready to help with advice.

You can have a good time playing bowling, billiards or ping pong. By the way, guests can visit the local sauna, and all the wonders of the Turkish bath should be experienced at least once. For travelers, there is a large spa center, which offers various health and beauty treatments, various types of massage (including therapeutic). Thalassotherapy is very popular. Nearby is a beauty salon, where experienced makeup artists, stylists and hairdressers offer their services.

In the evenings, various groups perform in the courtyard - guests can enjoy live music during dinner and end the evening with dancing. At night, there is a disco bar where you can always have a good time and have fun under the fiery rhythms of local music.

Of course, entertainment can be found outside the Vincci Resort Nour Palace 5 *. Tunisia is a wonderful country with a unique culture and ancient history, so do not give up sightseeing trips. There really is something to see here. The hotel has several guides who are ready to offer you a lot of sightseeing tours and trips to various distances. Agencies can also be found in the city. Mahdia (Tunisia) is a really interesting place.

Accommodation with a child

Many parents cannot imagine their vacation without a child, because children need to be healthy and have fun no less than adults. According to reviews, the Vincci Resort Nour Palace 5 * hotel is ideal for a pleasant family vacation. Kids here will be able to have fun from the heart, and parents will enjoy comfort.

A standard double room can accommodate a rollaway bed upon request. In the restaurant, little travelers are provided with special children's menus - the dishes are simple, tasty and healthy.

In terms of entertainment, Vincci Resort Nour Palace also has something to offer. There is a pool in the courtyard for small guests - the depth is shallow, the water is clean and always well warmed up. The kids here learn to swim and just have fun in the company of peers. There is also a club in which the tutors and animators are engaged in the children - they completely take care of organizing the leisure of the little guests. Near the club there is a spacious playground with slides, swings, sandboxes and other fun attractions.

The hotel has babysitters, who are not afraid to leave their children if necessary. Their services, of course, are paid separately, but the prices are not so high.

What do tourists think? Reviews and recommendations of former guests

In general, the atmosphere at the hotel is pleasant - animators entertain guests, and you can always find a corner for a relaxing, secluded holiday. It is worth noting that the main contingent of vacationers are residents of Tunisia and neighboring countries.

Many travelers like to relax in the city of Mahdia. Vincci Resort Nour Palace is a hotel that will make your stay truly perfect. Vivid impressions and pleasant memories are guaranteed.
