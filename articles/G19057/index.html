Hotel Sumus Hotel Monteplaya 4 * (Malgrat de Mar / Spain): description and reviews

Sumus Hotel Monteplaya 4 * (Malgrat de Mar) is a hotel in Spain, which during its existence has earned tremendous popularity due to the excellent conditions provided to its guests, as well as excellent service from the staff. It is recommended by tour operators who value their reputation. It is not uncommon for families to come here to rest, as the proper conditions for this are created here.



Where is the hotel Sumus Hotel Monteplaya 4 *

This hotel is located in Spain, in the small town of Malgrat de Mar, which is located between the Costa Brava and the Costa de Barcelona-Meresme. It occupies the first line from the sea, which allows you to enjoy sunbathing right on the beach, on the Maritim embankment, without spending too much time moving from a vacation spot to the beach.

Nearest transport hubs

Sumus Hotel Monteplaya 3 * (Malgrat de Mar) is located near the bus stop, from which vacationers, if desired, can go anywhere in the town and go sightseeing. Also, only half a kilometer from the hotel is the train station, which is a link in the routes running between the town of Malgrat de Mar and other settlements of Spain.

As for air transport, the Girona Airport is 35 km from here, and the major Barcelona Airport is 75 km.

What is on the territory of the hotel

On the territory of the Sumus Hotel Monteplaya 4 * for guests and for sunbathing, there is a large outdoor terrace of 2500 square meters. m, equipped with sun loungers and umbrellas. Two large pools adjoin it: in one of them there are conditions for bathing of children, and in the second there is a jacuzzi and a decorative artificial waterfall. Near the pools there is a huge bar in which bartenders prepare refreshing cocktails.



For younger guests, there is a separate children's room where young guests have the opportunity to spend time with their peers, communicate with them and play games.

For adults, Sumus Hotel Monteplaya has a mini golf course. There are also several shops in which guests have the opportunity to purchase souvenirs and more.

There are several dining options on site. First of all, it is a lobby bar, snack bar and restaurant. There is also a banquet room where events can be organized. Fans of social networks can use all the amenities in the Internet cafe.

For foreign tourists, Sumus Hotel Monteplaya has a currency exchange office, and for connoisseurs of literature, the hotel has a large library.

Conditions for children

The hotel has a number of conditions that make the stay of children in it more comfortable. First of all, it is a separate zone in a large pool, equipped with all the norms of maintaining the safety of babies during water procedures. The restaurants and cafes that are available in the Sumus Hotel Monteplaya 4 * hotel offer separate menu options - children's. There are also separate highchairs.

Also on the territory of the hotel there is a large children's playground and a games room, in which there are toys, educational games and all the conditions for a pleasant pastime and watching cartoons are arranged.

Hotel rooms

Sumus Hotel Monteplaya (Costa del Maresme) offers its guests 171 rooms of three different categories: standard, standard plus and superior. All of them are properly equipped: equipped with good appliances and comfortable furniture.

Each room is equipped with a bathroom, where, in addition to the shower, there is a standard set of personal hygiene items, a set of towels, bathrobes and slippers. A hairdryer is also included.



Each room has a mandatory sleeping place, designed for a certain number of guests (1 or 2), chairs, cabinets for clothes. All rooms have a good air conditioning system, which allows guests to create the desired climatic conditions.

In addition to basic amenities, standard plus rooms include a plasma screen with satellite TV, a refrigerator and a safe.

Sumus Hotel Monteplaya Adults Only offers internet access for a fee.



Special numbers

The hotel has the opportunity to choose a room that will meet certain requirements. There are separate types of apartments that are designed for disabled people and people with disabilities. Also at Sumus Hotel Monteplaya there is the possibility of checking into a special type of room - family. Everything is provided for living in whole families: there are cribs and sleeping places for two adults.

A separate type of room is soundproofed, which allows people living in it to completely abstract from what is happening outside. Such apartments are incredibly popular with those who come to rest with their soulmate.



Entertainment at the hotel

The hotel has many facilities for entertaining guests. First of all, there is the possibility of playing billiards, for which tables are set. For sports guests there is a large and multi-functional gym. Also equipped for them: tennis court, volleyball and squash court. Everyone has the opportunity to rent sports equipment for a certain time.

Fans of nightlife can go to the disco, which takes place every evening at the Sumus Hotel Monteplaya (adults only). Guest reviews about such parties suggest that something incredible is happening at the dance floors: enchanting shows are organized, DJs perform, and all people dance to groovy hits. Entrance to such events is open exclusively to adult guests.

Regular educational excursions are held for hotel guests, during which participants have the opportunity to get acquainted with local attractions and learn a lot of new and interesting things.

Hotel Services

Sumus Hotel Monteplaya provides a range of services free of charge, some of which are included in the room rate package. So, his guests have the opportunity to use the services of a left-luggage office, which is located at the hotel. All questions that guests have can always be answered by a receptionist who works around the clock.

Hotel guests can take advantage of car and bicycle rental services, but this is for a fee. Also, due to the guest the hotel can provide a transfer service from the airport to the destination. It should be noted that the transfer is very popular among holidaymakers coming from other countries. The guests must notify the hotel of the need two days prior to arrival, and they should also make an advance payment.

Front desk services

Reception staff for the fee indicated in the price list provide a number of small, but very popular and useful services for hotel guests. First of all, anyone has the opportunity to contact the employee with a request to wake him at a certain time - this service is called "wake-up call".

Also, at the reception, if necessary, a taxi is called, reception and sending mail.

A restaurant

A good restaurant Show Cooking operates on the territory of the Sumus Hotel Monteplaya 4 *. In the reviews of tourists vacationing here, there are often comments specifically about the work of this institution. According to the guests, professional chefs work here who are able to surprise even the most demanding gourmets with a tasteful bouquet of the finished dish.



The Show Cooking restaurant has a buffet that impresses with its riot of colors and the variety of products on offer. All dishes presented at the buffet are freshly prepared, and exclusively from quality products purchased from time-tested suppliers.

Chefs present Mediterranean and international cuisine to their guests. During the meal there is a pleasant and relaxed atmosphere.



Lounge bar

The lounge bar offers beautiful panoramic views of the outdoor terrace and pools. It is here that guests often hide from the hot sun during the daytime.

A daily continental breakfast is served from 10 a.m. to noon . It was at this time that the hotel guests can comfortably stay here and have a hearty breakfast.

The lounge bar is equipped with comfortable armchairs and sofas for relaxing and square tables with neon lights. There is also a large bar where guests can enjoy refreshing drinks and cocktails.



Rest on the tariff "All inclusive"

Guests of the Sumus Hotel Monteplaya (Spain) have the opportunity to book an all-inclusive room. So what does this system offer?

First of all, this tariff provides, of course, the cost of living in a room. The guest has the opportunity to visit the Show Cooking restaurant and eat from the buffet during breakfast, lunch and dinner absolutely free. Also, on a free basis for such a guest, all drinks and snacks available at the bar (pizza, barbecue, sandwiches, chips, ice cream) are provided.

Rest at the rate of "55+"

Such a tariff is provided for those guests who are already 55 years old or more. It should be noted that a lot of people of this age come to the Sumus Hotel Monteplaya (Spain), and this tariff is very popular.

It includes accommodation in the room, as well as the provision of all drinks for free.

Upon entering the hotel at the reception, guests who have booked a room at this rate will be asked to present an identity document and age. The hotel administration warns that the "55+" tariff is valid only for those who have booked a room for 4 days or more.

Holiday at Love & Selebration

This rate is for young couples who decide to spend a joint holiday at Sumus Hotel Monteplaya. In this package, in addition to the cost of living in the room, the breakfast in bed service is included . Also, lovers are provided with free wine and champagne in the bar on a free basis, and when visiting the lounge bar, each of the guests is entitled to a free Mojito cocktail.

In addition to all this, at the entrance to the hotel, the couple receives an erotic set, a set of condoms with different tastes, several types of lubricants (strawberries, cherries, cranberries), as well as massage oil with the scent of strawberries and champagne (do not forget that the hotel is only for adults!).

Hotel reservation

All guests who wish to relax at Sumus Hotel Monteplaya must reserve a room in advance. This can be done through a travel operator - this option of providing relaxation is the most convenient, because employees of travel agencies know all the subtleties of this business. However, in order for the vacation to be really good, you should contact a trusted travel agency.

However, anyone can start organizing their vacation on their own. To book a room in a hotel, you need to go to its official website and in the appropriate section select the dates during which you plan to stay in the complex, as well as the desired tariff package (if required). By the way, for those who book a hotel room through the official website, an additional advantage is provided - the possibility of free Wi-Fi throughout the Sumus Hotel Monteplaya.



Guest reviews

Everyone who once was a guest at this hotel shares their impressions on social networks and on special recall sites. It should be noted that this hotel receives extremely positive comments, which indicates the professionalism of the staff working in it and, of course, the management.

Hotel guests often note in their comments the pleasant atmosphere that reigns within its walls. It is here that you can truly relax and fully enjoy your vacation by sunbathing on the shores of the Mediterranean Sea or on the outdoor terrace near the two-tier pool with an artificial waterfall.

Guests at this famous Spanish hotel often note its good location. Indeed, Sumus Hotel Monteplaya is located only 1 km from the center of Malgrat de Mar, which provides its guests with walking distance from the main attractions of the city and local attractions.

Often on the Internet you can find laudatory feedback that the hotel organizes excursions to various interesting places from which are located nearby. So, some netizens talk about how they traveled to the Montenegro forest nature park - such an excursion lasts about four hours, and its price includes lunch in a local small restaurant. Also, guests of the hotel go on a two-hour excursion to the best winery plantations in Spain for tasting jamon and wine. However, guests receive the most pleasant experience from participating in a picnic among the vineyards, which offer a magnificent view of the Mediterranean Sea.

How to get to the hotel

One of the main modes of transport with which you can reach the resort is a plane that can deliver guests to Barcelona or Girona Airport. You can get from the airport using the shuttle service, which is available for a fee. Guests can also access on their own using shuttle buses from the airport to Sumus Hotel Monteplaya.



If the guest plans to arrive by his own car, then in this case you should move along the N-11 highway.

If the train was chosen as a means of transportation, then in this case you should move in the direction of Barcelona - Hospitality de Llobregat.
