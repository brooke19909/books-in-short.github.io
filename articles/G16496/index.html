Film, tape and pencil

In order for the clothes for the Monster High doll to be created with your own hands to be truly beautiful, you need to sew it according to the correct patterns. After all, it is in the design work that the entire complexity of the work of the fashion designer is the construction of the correct patterns, after working with which the clothes will sit just amazingly.

Monster High dolls are unusual in composition. They have a thin waist, arms and legs, a small body with a chest and a fairly large head. Such disproportionateness distinguishes the collection of these toys from the like. Therefore, the clothes for the Monster High doll are also unusual. With their own hands, it is difficult to build patterns for them if you do not use a little trick: cling film, paper tape and a pencil.

The doll body must be wrapped with cling film. Then, with small pieces of masking tape, glue it. Directly on the pasted tape to draw a pattern - armholes of the sleeves, neck. To cut the film together with scissors with nail scissors, divide the resulting structure into separate parts. Those bends that are obtained on a paper structure should be carefully cut and deployed on a flat surface - in these places you will get tucks or additional parts. Patterns are ready. With a cut of fabric, it is necessary to provide allowances for seams, bending of the bottom of the product. For a thing to turn out beautiful, it would be good to observe the direction of the shared thread in all equally located parts of the garment.





Correct details

It is not enough to design the clothes themselves; you still need to think about how to put them on the doll. Since the heads of Monster High dolls are too large compared to bodies, it is impossible to put on dresses over your head. So dresses, blouses, half-loops should have convenient fasteners. It is best to place them on the back, and to fasten the cuts, use thin strips of Velcro. The sewn clothes for the Monster High doll with your own hands can, of course, be fastened with buttons, but then they should be small enough to look harmoniously on an elegant toy. Also, as fasteners, you can use small dress hooks and eyelets or buttons. This type of fastener is most suitable for overall clothing - fur coats, coats, jackets.
