Telecommunications operator

The first Beeline short number that we will look at is the one responsible for making a call to a mobile operator. It is with this combination that in most cases problems with communication and other issues are resolved.

If you want to call the operator, then dial 0611 and press the call button on the phone. Now you can wait for an answer. True, this method is not particularly successful if you want to quickly contact the operator. The fact is that, using the Beeline short number 0611, you run the risk of getting on an answering machine, then you will have to talk with a robot voice for a long time to ask your questions and get answers to them. Thus, let's try to figure out how to contact the operator.

SMS center

Our mobile operator has a special Beeline service. SMS to a short number is sent by the client, after which it is processed. The result is an independent connection / disconnection of mobile services and a variety of additional feature packages. That is, Beeline has a so-called SMS center. And for him, of course, there is a special number.



Do you want to independently activate the service or refuse it? Then send an SMS with a specific command code to the Beeline 111 short number, and then wait for the results. All SMS commands can be found on the official page of the mobile operator. There are quite a lot of them; remembering all of them will fail.
