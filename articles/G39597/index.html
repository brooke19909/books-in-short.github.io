"Tele2" - communication with the operator: phone number, support service

Today we have to get acquainted with the company "Tele2". Communication with the operator in this is supported in many ways. But which ones? How can I get through or send a letter to the technical support service of the company? Why even make calls to your mobile operator? All this is important to know and understand. After all, only then, if necessary, can you keep in touch with Tele2 without problems. Bringing an idea to life is not as difficult as it seems. Modern subscribers are offered a variety of communication contact options. Some of them require an internet connection. But to master all the proposed techniques is quite easy.



Why to call

Already, many people have connected to Tele2. Communication with the operator is what many are interested in. Indeed, sometimes technical support can help in case of questions regarding the goods and services that the company offers. So why is it necessary to know the numbers and other contacts of Tele2? You can use them if you needed:

  • Get advice on the capabilities of the operator;
  • connect / disconnect services;
  • change the tariff;
  • find out the current tariff plan;
  • get information about problems on the line;
  • complain about the work of the operator (praise too);
  • ask for advice regarding the connection of some services from Tele2.

There are a lot of reasons for communication. But how can it be done? What features does Tele2 give? Communication with the operator can be supported by various methods. Choose the one that suits you the most!

From any phone

The next answer to the question about how to call Tele2 is to use the technical support number for landline phones, a combination that allows you to talk directly with people and not with an answering machine.

To call a support service from any region of the country, dial 8 800 555 0611 on your gadget. By the way, this combination is also suitable for making calls from a mobile phone. The conversation, as in the previous case, will be completely free.

Not the most popular method of solving a problem. Typically, users use a combination of 611 to call Tele2 from a mobile phone. And the previously proposed phone is used only in rare cases.



In roaming

But this is not all the possibilities of Tele2. Communication with the operator from a mobile phone can be carried out even when the subscriber is in roaming. A very convenient feature, important for travelers.

If you are in roaming, you can use the following combination: +7 951 52 00 611. Please note that the dialing format must be international. A call to this number is charged according to your chosen tariff plan.

It is not recommended to use the combination of 8 800 555 0611. It will require you to pay for communication. Therefore, when traveling outside of Russia, it is advisable to forget this number for a while.

By region

Tele2 is a company that has branches in various regions of the country. It is not difficult to guess that in every city where there is this connection, there are own support services.

Therefore, if you need to find out the phone number of the Tele2 mobile operator , go to the official page of the company and see the landline number available in your area.



For example, in Tula you can get through with the help of 4 872 70 37 03, and in St. Petersburg and the Leningrad Region 8 123 290 022 is used. You have every right to see all numbers on the official page of Tele2. From a mobile phone, it is advisable not to call. There is a separate short number for calling from a cell phone.

Other communication

But this is not all the possibilities of our today's operator. What means of supporting communication with subscribers are used in practice? Of course, besides phone calls!

At Tele2, communication with the operator can be carried out through the official page of the company. Not the fastest, but it does not require any calls method. Find the feedback form there and fill in all the fields that appear. This technique is good when you can clearly identify the problem that has arisen, and also take your time to solve it. After sending the request, you will have to wait a while. The answer will come to you by e-mail.

By the way, e-mail is another method of maintaining communication with the operator. Just write to russia.vopros@tele2.com. You can also use the addresses:

  • t2info@tele2.com;
  • infokz@tele2.com (for residents of Kazakhstan).

Write to your e-mail about your problem, wait for an answer. It is noted that the wait for letters from "Tele2" in this case can last up to several days. The method is good, again, if you do not need an instant solution to the problem. Complaints and recommendations are also accepted at the above addresses.



The last, albeit unofficial, way to keep in touch with Tele2 is to use groups on social networks. For example, in VK there is an official community of this operator. There you can ask any question that interests you. They will answer you soon. Moreover, information can be shared by both an ordinary user and an employee of Tele2.

Now you know all the possible options for communication with a mobile operator. Choose the one that seems most simple to you, and then bring it to life. It is recommended to call "Tele2" through a combination of 611.
