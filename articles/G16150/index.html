DIY paper tulip

Origami is an ancient decorative art of folding various paper figures. Its roots go back to Ancient China, where, in fact, paper was invented. There are many origami schemes with which you can construct various shapes, flowers, animals. Crafts created according to these schemes look very beautiful and unusual. Origami paper flowers sometimes look as if they are real.



In addition, this process is very exciting and will help brighten up your leisure time with benefit and pleasure. Over a long period, you will be pleased with DIY paper crafts . Flowers made in this way can be put in a vase. They will decorate your interior, giving it a twist.

Making a tulip out of paper is pretty easy. Even a four-year-old child can do this. In addition, the result will please you - the flower turns out to be pretty cute and pretty.

To make a tulip out of paper, we need a square sheet of yellow, red, purple or burgundy color for making the bud, as well as green paper for the leaf and stalk on which the flower will be attached.

Manufacture

Fold the square sheet of paper diagonally and immediately unfold it to its original position.

Again, bend the sheet horizontally in half, expand.



Now bend inward the longitudinal folds. Thus, we get a triangle with folds inside. This is the basic form - the triangle, or "water bomb."

Bend the upper lower corners to the top up.

Flip and do the same with the other bottom corners face down.

Next, you need to bend the right corner. We got a rhombus with a fold in the middle. Now you need to flip the model and do the same thing again.

Edges and corners must look up.

Now you need to bend the right corner to the center. Bend the left corner so that the right one is completely covered by it.

Now you need to turn the figure over and repeat the procedure again.

Next, fill one corner in another. Turn the model over and repeat this step again.

Our bud is almost ready! It remains only to find a hole at the very bottom and inflate it, and then carefully bend the petals.

In order for the tulip made of paper to have a finished look, you need to add the stem and leaf.

Stem Making

Fold the stem with a leaf.

Turn the square sheet upward at an angle.

Fold right and left corners to the front.

Fold the upper side corners to the middle again.

Now you need to bend the figure horizontally in half, and then vertically in half.

Pull the inner triangle by pulling it slightly to the right. Fasten the new fold line.



Now it remains to connect the bud with the stem. That's all! Paper tulip is ready!

To create a beautiful composition, make several tulips of different colors, put them in a vase. Due to this, you can revive your interior and give it a spring mood. A composition of flowers from two shades will also look beautiful. For example, white and pink tulips or white with red (or white with purple). You can choose a shade that matches the color of the wallpaper or curtains, which will give your room a special charm and comfort.
