Hotel "Monomakh" (Vladimir): photos and reviews of tourists

The city of Vladimir is, as you know, the ancient capital of northeastern Russia. Located in picturesque places near the Klyazma River and only 176 km from the capital, it annually attracts tourists not only from Russia, but also from all over the world. The history of the founding of the city has always been associated with the name of Prince Vladimir Monomakh.

Therefore, it is absolutely not surprising that the famous historical figure appears in the names of streets, squares, restaurants and hotels. For example, there is a hotel "Monomakh". Vladimir, like a good-natured master, throws open the doors to all comers. Accommodation in the city center is very convenient. All the sights and cultural life will be at your fingertips.





Hotel Location

The comfortable hotel complex is located right in the heart of the city of Vladimir, in its business, cultural and historical center. In this regard, the audience to which the hotel is oriented is very wide: from tourists who are keen on entertainment and sightseeing, to those who come for business on a business trip.

The Monomakh Hotel (Vladimir) has the following address: 20. Gogol Street, 20. If you turn to the city map, you will immediately notice that the famous Golden Gate is located just a few minutes walk from the building. The hotel is an excellent accommodation option for those who love history, architecture and culture. The complex provides guests with sixteen comfortable rooms of various categories. Free Wi-Fi is available throughout the hotel.

Hotel Monomakh (Vladimir): what is included in the accommodation?

If you choose a hotel as an accommodation option, it should be borne in mind that some types of services are already included in the room price. Wireless internet is available throughout. Each room has an air conditioning system that will make your stay as comfortable as possible. Contact administrators and the service for ordering food by phone. Each room also has satellite TV and a small safe.

Guests arriving with their own vehicles can safely leave it in the free car park, which is under 24-hour video surveillance. In addition, breakfast is included in all room categories. The hotel provides each guest with a ticket to visit a cafe with a face value of three hundred rubles.

Paid services

In addition to the service included in the accommodation, the Monomakh Hotel (Vladimir) offers its guests a number of additional options for a fee. In the room you can order food and drinks from the restaurant, as well as a minibar. Pets are allowed, but upon prior request.

Laundry and ironing services, copy and laser equipment, and long-distance telephone services are available. For tourists, a wide range of entertainment for every taste offers a tourist office organized by the hotel. These include fascinating horseback riding in the picturesque village of Bogolyubovo near the city. Without leaving the hotel, everyone can buy souvenirs.

The restaurant is called Monomakh. He works from seven in the morning until eleven in the evening. A team led by a professional chef prepares dishes of exceptional taste. The restaurant organizes banquets for various celebrations.





Hotel Monomakh (Vladimir): reviews

According to the popular tourist portal, the hotel has a rather high rating among all accommodation options in the city of Vladimir. Travelers rate it at 8.6 points out of a possible ten, taking several criteria as a basis, including location, services, service, silence, comfort, etc. According to guests of the city of Vladimir who choose a hotel to stay, three factors deserve the highest rating. .

First, the location. The hotel is located in the cultural and historical center of the city. Many sights are within walking distance, with excellent views. Secondly, according to the guests, the Monomakh Hotel, which Vladimir can be proud of, has good employees. They are friendly, helpful and know their job. Settlement and accommodation is quick, without any hitches. The rooms are cleaned regularly and cleanly. Thirdly, there is very tasty food. The breakfasts served in the cafe were commended. Despite the modest price, the quality of the food is at a high level, as is the taste. Pancakes and scrambled eggs are as good as homemade ones.

In addition, the hotel has good wireless internet with excellent reception and speed. In the age of gadgets and technological progress, this factor is perhaps the most important. Among the minuses, hotel guests note outdated furniture in some rooms, which should be repaired or replaced, as well as extraneous noise from air conditioners, which significantly interferes at night.

When planning your vacation in one of the most ancient cities of the country, be sure to pay attention to this accommodation option. One of the best in its category, having a good quality-price ratio, is the Monomakh Hotel (Vladimir).
