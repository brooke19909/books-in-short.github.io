Hotel Restaurants

Energize and be in a good mood, guests can, subject to the provision of delicious and quality food. In Diamond Premium 5 * you can visit the following restaurants:

  • Carpaccio is a cozy Italian restaurant where everything is saturated with the spirit of this country. A sophisticated interior with a cozy home decor made of wood will take you to the homeland of pasta and pizza.
  • In the restaurant "Sultan" you will have a unique opportunity to try the best dishes of Turkish cuisine. The interior of the institution is made in the style of European ballrooms. Nevertheless, oriental patterns, arches, screens and columns will immerse you in the atmosphere of Ottoman palaces.
  • The main restaurant provides guests with full buffet meals. At the many distribution tables you will find Turkish, Mediterranean, Asian and European dishes. You can sit in a huge bright room or on the outdoor terrace.

Hotel Bars

Drinks, snacks, entertainment - all this and much more will be available to guests in the Diamond Premium 5 * bars. You can visit the following places:

  • Beach bar - is a cozy place where you can hide from the sun under a wooden canopy. Here you can not only enjoy cool beer and carbonated drinks, but also have a meal at the lunchtime with grilled dishes.
  • The disco bar is a fun night out. From late evening until early morning, you can enjoy strong drinks and incendiary dances in the light of spotlights.
  • A patisserie is a sweet tooth paradise. In addition to traditional Turkish desserts, you can taste the most delicious biscuits, airy mousses and a host of other goodies. You can drink all these sweets with tea, coffee or fruit liqueurs.
  • Poolside bars will delight you with the most unusual cocktails and soft ice cream. You can enjoy your order at the counter in the water or at a table under a shady canopy. Pizza and hamburgers are served at lunchtime.
  • The hotel has two lobby bars, which differ in design and musical accompaniment. Flavored tea, invigorating coffee, elite alcohol and light snacks are served at these establishments. Nice music and wireless internet will make your stay especially enjoyable.




Helpful information

Choosing a Diamond Premium 5 * hotel for your vacation, check out some of the living conditions. So, it will be useful for guests to know the following information:

  • registration and eviction of guests is carried out, respectively, after 14:00 and before noon;
  • throughout the territory, except for designated areas, smoking is strictly prohibited;
  • pets of any size and breed are prohibited in the hotel;
  • if children are not 12 years old, extra beds are not required;
  • to book a room, you must have a credit card on which the amount of cash deposit will be blocked;
  • in the case of corporate bookings of 3 rooms or more, a security deposit of 10% is charged, which is not refundable in case of cancellation;
  • The Ultra All Inclusive system is available on site 24 hours a day.
