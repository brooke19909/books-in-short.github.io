Knitted dresses, schemes for them and a brief instruction

Knitwear is very popular and almost everyone has a wardrobe. It is false to assume that they are only suitable for the cold season. In addition to warm wool sweaters, you can also knit summer clothes.

Knitted beach tunics, dresses, swimwear, panamas, bandages look stylish. And this is not a complete list of possible options. Each self-respecting fashionista just needs to have a couple of knitted sets. They go well with jeans, silk, chiffon.

Knitwear can be complemented and combined in an infinite number of ways. A huge plus is that they can be made with your own hands according to the original sketch.

A bright summer dress made with the use of large and even slightly rough knitting looks very beautiful. It is worth noting that knitted dresses are popular in all seasons. After all, the dress has always been an attribute of femininity and elegance. You can choose yourself any, depending on your preferences for style, color and shape features. Well, for fashionable knitted dresses, patterns can be found in special knitting training books.

Tunic dresses, sweater dresses, summer, winter - this is just a small list of possible products of this type. Your outfit created by your own hands will look advantageous.

Having chosen the most suitable knitted dresses, the patterns and styles of which should correspond to the features of the figure, you can easily emphasize your personality. Knitting a dress is easy. As assistants to the novice craftswoman are knitting patterns. They describe the technique of making the selected item. Knitting dresses patterns are greatly simplified and serve as a starting point. It is not necessary to follow all directions given there. You can and should make your own adjustments. As knitting tools, you can use both knitting needles and a hook. You also need to select threads.

Yarn for knitting is presented in stores in a wide assortment: woolen, acrylic, cotton, mohair, silk, linen. There is also blended and synthetic yarn. It differs in the thickness of the thread. A thicker thread is suitable for winter clothes, and a thinner thread for summer things. As the most suitable for summer dress, cotton yarn is suitable . Cotton passes air well and absorbs moisture easily.

There are also such knitted dresses, the schemes of which suggest combining different types of yarn. For example, a combination of silk and cotton or acrylic yarn looks great. For the most daring ideas, twisted threads that combine several colors at once are suitable. Also from this type of yarn, relief patterns are excellent.

In order to create a unique knitted dress, patterns, as mentioned earlier, can be changed, supplementing them with any of their original solutions.

There are also knitting techniques: knitting with front stitch, lace technique, knitting of elastic, a set with a fringe, a cross-shaped set and many others. What distinguishes knitted dresses favorably - patterns of models are available even for the most demanding tastes.
