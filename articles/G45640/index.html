How to make a heart out of paper with your own hands - a step-by-step description, schemes and recommendations

The heart is the most famous symbol of love. Often, people make heart-shaped crafts to express their feelings. They can be made from anything, but the widest variety of forms and techniques is available, of course, in paper form. You can make a voluminous heart out of paper with your own hands, you can roll up a small origami heart, you can make a panel of small figures - the fantasy is unlimited, practically, with nothing. Such a creation will be a great gift for any occasion for your second half or loved one. It will be especially important to make a gift in the shape of a heart on February 14 or a wedding anniversary.

Artificial flowers, beads, buttons, ribbons will look great on the lid.

Origami Heart

Origami - the technique of folding a square sheet of paper into figures of various shapes without the use of glue and scissors. To master the secret of how to make a heart out of paper - origami, even a child can. The most popular addition scheme looks very simple. The result is a puffy paper heart, inside of which there will be air. It is better to make it from double-sided paper of pink or red color.

  1. In order to get a voluminous heart, you need to take a square sheet and fold it two times in half: first horizontally, then vertically.
  2. Expand the sheet: in the middle should be a cross from additions.
  3. Fold the sheet in half: in the middle a trace from the second addition will be visible.
  4. To this strip you need to bend the corners, not reaching 5 mm to the center.
  5. Turn the workpiece so that the top of the triangle faces up.
  6. Then you need to raise the edges of the lowered shutters up so that they are flush with the sides of the triangles.
  7. Next, lower the edges of the wings again. In their place will be the fold lines.
  8. It is necessary to bend the corners of the triangle with sharp edges to the fold line, to its very beginning. It should turn out triangular "ears".
  9. Under the so-called ears, corners have formed that need to be raised, bent and hidden under the upper triangles.
  10. After these simple manipulations, a pentagon with sharp corners should turn out. So that these corners do not spoil the soft outlines of the heart, they need to be pressed a little with your fingers.
  11. It is necessary to turn the craft over and inflate it through the hole obtained from below.

With such voluminous hearts, if they are small, you can fill a box with a gift for Valentine's Day, and by hanging a thread to them, - decorate the room.

Paper heart panel made of butterflies

A panel in the shape of a heart will look very interesting. He will need the following materials and tools:

  • Thick paper is a large sheet.
  • Pastel paper.
  • A small piece of cardboard.
  • Photo frame square shape.
  • Scissors.
  • A simple pencil.

In order to make such a panel, it is necessary to pull thick paper on the frame and fix it so that the workpiece looks like a canvas stretched over the frame. Such paper can be purchased at a creative store or art store. Usually it is used for work in pencil or pastel and sold in large format sheets. Next on the cardboard you need to draw a small butterfly and cut it out - this will be a stencil. On paper of a gentle shade, circle the butterfly many times. Butterflies of pale blue, pink, beige shades will look good. A combination of two or three colors will look interesting. On an impromptu canvas, draw a heart with a simple pencil and stick along the contour of the butterflies in the middle, bending the edges up. Then butterflies need to fill the whole heart. Such a picture will look good in almost any interior.

Heart made of paper pompons

If you are wondering about how to make a heart out of paper with your own hands, then an interesting solution will be a heart made of pompons. You can buy ready-made pompons - they are sold in stores with goods for the holidays, as well as on Aliexpress, or you can make them yourself. In order to make paper pompons, you need to take crepe paper, which can be found in any office supply store. But how to make a heart out of paper in stages:

  1. First you need to cut the paper into identical rectangles with a width of about 20 cm (the width of the strips is the diameter of the pompom), and a length of 30 cm. There should be at least eight such rectangles. The more sheets of paper, the pompom will be more magnificent.
  2. Sheets need to be stacked and all folded with an accordion.
  3. In the middle of the accordion, make a mark and pull the thread.
  4. On the sides of the accordion you need to round the edges with scissors.
  5. Now the pompom needs to be fluffed, lifting the edges up and spreading them.
  6. Of the twelve such pompons, you get a very beautiful voluminous fluffy heart, which can decorate a wedding celebration or any other holiday. It is most convenient to fasten them together with the help of a stapler. Pompons can be made even smaller, then there can be several ready-made hearts. They can decorate a nursery or bedroom, decorated in the style of shabby chic.

How to make a paper heart?

In order to make a small paper heart with wings using the origami technique, you need to:

  1. Fold a square sheet with sides 20 cm in half, then again in half.
  2. Expand and fold the upper and lower parts of the square to the fold line.
  3. Next, you need to turn the future heart to the other side and bend the lower corners to the center along the fold line.
  4. The next step is to turn the craft back and bend the strip to the center. Its upper part must be bent back and straighten the corners in the middle.
  5. At the ends of the strip, it is necessary to bend the corners - triangles.
  6. Again, you need to turn the workpiece over and bend the corners on the top of the heart.

Wall decor voluminous hearts

The wall decorated with small volumetric hearts looks very beautiful and original. But how to make a heart out of paper with your own hands? In order to decorate the wall in the room, you need to cut out a lot of small hearts from thick paper. Next, you need to make small cuts on each part between the round sides. Now these sides need to be slightly crossed so that the heart has gained volume and fix everything with glue. Such a simple technique will allow you to transform the interior in the room of a romantic young lady. An interesting solution will be the alternation of hearts with volume stars.

Big heart made of paper

Recently it has become fashionable to decorate children's birthdays and weddings with large paper flowers. And if you also put them in the shape of a heart on the wall, the composition will become twice as romantic. In order to make paper flowers, you will need:

  • Large format paper - you can buy it in stores for creativity.
  • Scissors.
  • Glue "Moment" or glue gun with rods.
  • A simple pencil.
  • Thick cardboard.

On a sheet of cardboard, draw a large petal, on the other - medium-sized and small. Cut them and transfer to paper. Make flower blanks. Now collect the crafts in layers: first, roll up the inner bud of small petals, then glue the middle tier of the floral edge. Last step, attach the largest petals, as if opening a flower. Inside, glue small paper strips wrapped with scissors, like stamens. Place the composition on the wall in the shape of a heart with double-sided tape and no longer think about how to make a heart out of paper.
