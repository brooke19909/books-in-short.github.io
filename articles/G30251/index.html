Hotel Side Crown Serenity Hotel 5 * (Side, Turkey): reviews

Choosing a place for an unforgettable annual vacation, many travelers opt for a picturesque country - Turkey. The friendly population, favorable climate, developed resort infrastructure and many years of experience in receiving guests from different parts of the globe have led to high-quality service, a warm welcome and a carefree stay on the Mediterranean coast.

general information

The five-star Side Crown Serenity Hotel, located in the coastal village of Side, is gaining many positive reviews every year, attracting new tourists. The hotel management organizes a feast of the soul for each spa, giving an unforgettable experience, enthusiastic memories and a portion of immense positive. Staying in a snow-white castle, despite its scale, creates the impression of home warmth and allows you to replenish your family photo album with unique frames and captured emotions.

The resort is conditionally divided into two areas: the western one, which is characterized by a dense concentration of multi-star hotels, and the eastern one, with relatively untouched nature and wild beaches. The institution in question is located on the outskirts of a busy part of the city, however, in terms of comfort and developed infrastructure, it is not inferior to its competitors.





general characteristics

The main and only building of the Side Crown Serenity Hotel 5 * hotel was opened for receiving travelers in 2013. A four-story building was erected and equipped in accordance with five-star world standards for institutions of this kind. During the construction of a completely new building, all the features that ensure a comfortable stay, including elevators, balconies, terraces and much more, were taken into account.

Arrivals

The hotel has 346 apartments, which are divided into two categories:

  • Standard
  • Family

Standard category apartments have one room and a bathroom. Maximum capacity - 3 people. Area - a little over thirty square meters. Family - contain two rooms, united by an interior door, have a total size of 44 square meters. The interior design is made in the classic style of light shades, the walls are decorated with imitated stucco molding. Bedspreads and bed linen are in harmony with the color scheme of curtains. Numerous sconces, floor lamps and the French type of windows provide the room with freshness, clarity and spaciousness. The floors are laminated.

Each room of the Side Crown Serenity Hotel 5 * hotel (Side) has access to its own terrace or balcony, overlooking the endless Mediterranean Sea, pools or natural landscapes.

Room cleaning and towel replacement is a daily procedure for tidy and conscientious maids, bed linen is changed three times a week. Room Service is available 24 hours a day and is paid extra. It is possible to add extra beds with prior coordination with the administration.





Room facilities

The rooms of the Side Crown Serenity Hotel (Turkey, Side) are equipped with fire safety sensors and a personal split system that allows you to create the required temperature in the rooms. LCD TV, telephone, hairdryer, electric kettle contribute to a comfortable stay. The furniture set consists of wooden beds, a table, chairs and a built-in wardrobe, in which disposable slippers and a safe are located.

A tea set allows you to meet an invigorating dawn and come in contact with the first rays of the sun, tasting aromatic coffee and quality teas. There is a minibar, which is replenished for free with soft drinks and drinking water every day.

A large makeup mirror allows the inhabitants of this wonderful castle called Side Crown Serenity to look great every day.

The bathroom is decorated with ceramic tiles in accordance with modern trends, equipped with a shower or bath. There are all necessary personal hygiene products and cosmetics.

Nutrition

Side Crown Serenity Hotel reviews for catering receive mostly positive. According to the All-Inclusive concept, valid until 24:00, hunger-seekers are satisfied according to the buffet system within the walls of the main Serenity restaurant. Multiple spotlights, chic chandeliers, mirrored columns, multi-level ceilings and sophisticated serving create the illusion of a royal meal, which is carried out five times a day according to a predetermined schedule.

The buffet type of serving dishes allows everyone to independently balance their diet with fresh vegetables, fruits, meat masterpieces, seafood, personally compiling their daily diet. An incredible abundance of products satisfied vacationers, according to their many reviews.

High chairs, a children's and a diet menu are provided here.





Additional facilities

In addition to the main restaurant, you can enjoy the unsurpassed culinary delights of local talented chefs in three restaurants that operate on an A la Carte custom-made menu. Their focus is Italian, Mexican and national cuisine. Doors are open exclusively in the evening and prior reservation is required. A visit to each restaurant is once included in the price of tickets.

In the daytime you can enjoy snacks at the beach snack bar or pastries and pastries in the patisserie. A vitamin bar rich in medicinal herbal teas and drinking alcohol at a disco are a paid pleasure, as are imported alcohol, freshly squeezed juices and drinks in glass bottles.

A bar near the pool allows you to enjoy the hot rays of the sun, a light breeze of the day, quenching thirst with the creations of local bartenders.

Swimming spots

Since swimming is one of the main leisure activities, this hotel is surrounded by a swimming area. She seemed to wash it, circling a significant part of the main building. Everything here is equipped with wooden bridges and passages, diving from which is strictly prohibited. Around the pool there are countless comfortable sun loungers with mattresses. Get a brown tan under the hot rays of the scorching sun is not difficult. For those who like to hide in the shade, umbrellas are provided.

For small children, a separate children's pool is provided, where the kids get incredible pleasure from splashing and swimming in warm water.

A distinctive feature of the hotel complex is the presence of a mini water park, which includes three different slides designed for skiing for both adults and children. Direct, twisted and joint descents diversify pastime and bring bright colors and unforgettable impressions into it.

You can saturate your daytime swimming with a game of water polo, or by doing exercises from instructors in water aerobics. This will maintain health, improve body shape and cheer up in the morning.

Less popular, according to tourists, is the indoor pool, which includes a zone for the younger and older generations.





Beach

Side Crown Serenity 5 * hotel (Turkey) is located on the second line from the sea, five hundred meters from its own beach. It is mostly sandy, has a gentle entrance to the water, which ensures the babies are safe near the surface of the water and sometimes raging waves. A large number of sunbeds with awnings, a shower, dressing rooms and a toilet are equipped with a coastal zone. Towels are provided free of charge. A visit to the beach bar is part of the All Inclusive concept.

You can have fun here by playing volleyball, which will allow you to make new acquaintances, pleasant communication and good mood. In addition, the opportunity to frolic on water rides, such as riding a scooter and a banana.

For the convenience of travelers, a free bus runs every 10-15 minutes, delivering hotel guests to the beach and back. The schedule is available at the reception.





What to do for young guests

Side Crown Serenity Hotel (Turkey) receives positive reviews from guests who come to rest with their little children. For children aged 4 to 12 years, a children's mini-club operates, which is equipped with a large number of entertaining games and a variety of toys. The hours of his work are morning and time after a nap. Under the supervision of a qualified teacher and a professional team of animators, children are daily taken on a new, fabulous journey. Competitions, fun and quizzes fill the kids with joyful emotions and a good mood.

For teenagers in the main building of the Side Crown Serenity Hotel 5 * hotel (Turkey) there is a room. It is equipped with various attractions, slot machines, air hockey and other entertainments. Parents can be calm, as their child will spend delightful days at the Side Crown Serenity.
