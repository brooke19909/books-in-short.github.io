How to make a number out of balls? Now we will tell. It is quite simple if you approach the matter correctly and with proper preparation.

How to make a number out of balls?

Every day, aero design - decorating rooms and landscape with balloons - is gaining momentum. No holiday is now complete without such accessories.





But not everyone has such skills, and so I want to decorate the holiday! The simplest thing is to make from the balls the numbers that are most often needed for birthdays and many round dates. So, the number from the balls with your own hands - where to start and how to do it?

What do we need?

Before you start creativity, you should know that balls come in different strengths and sizes. To make the figure, you need fifth-size balls of green and other colors, an AVVG 4x25 cable for a rigid frame - 3.30 meters, a glue gun and fishing line, as well as a pump. Best of all, it will look in the green meadow, with which you need to start. For the frame of the meadow, you need to cut off 1.2 meters from the cable. From the rest of the 2.1 meter length, make the basis for the deuce. The edges of each piece are made in the form of loops so that the balls do not slip off after attachment. The meadow is made of green balls, which are twisted together in a special way. They bind in two and twist with another finished pair. These elements are called primitives. Each primitive is planted in a clearing. In total, they will need 16 pieces.

"Two"

Now we will consider in detail how to make the number ‚Äú2‚Äù out of balls. It needs to be made separately. To do this, balls of a different color, for example, yellow or pink, are inflated, twisted into primitives and mounted on a two-piece frame. It should be noted that much more primitives will be needed to make numbers than were used in the clearing. In total, it will take about one and a half packs of balls of the fifth size.

Now we figured out how to make a number out of the balls, it remains to assemble the composition. To do this, put a deuce of balls into a clearing and secure it with a fishing line. The height of the finished product is approximately 1.5 meters.

Final stage

Now you can decorate the meadow itself. To do this, you need long sausage balls of different colors, but among them must be dark green and yellow. The rest are red, blue, which they like. You can make flowers out of them, after which these products need to be fixed on a clearing with the help of a glue gun. For each flower, you need three sausage balls - green (stem), yellow (core) and any color (for petals). Work on a flower begins with a ball from which the petals will be made. To do this, it is necessary to inflate it not completely, but so that it is not too tight and pliable in the hands. Connect the end and top of the ball to each other, getting a circle. Now you can inflate the flower leg - a green ball, but not completely. A few centimeters of the tail should be left.





The yellow ball - the core - inflate quite a bit. It should turn out a little ball. Then cut off an unnecessary piece, but so that you can tie the tail of the core. Now you can connect the stem and the middle with each other. It remains to make the petals. Twist the resulting circle several times. Then mentally divide into three parts and twist each part again. Then fold them and twist them together. Screw the resulting petals to the stem with the core. The simplest flower is ready. You can make a couple of leaves in the middle of the stem. Now the finished flower with a glue gun is mounted on a clearing. How many such elements will be needed for decoration, everyone decides for himself.

Conclusion

Now you know how to use balloons skillfully. The numbers and flowers from them are magnificent. These products can easily decorate the premises. In addition, it is possible to make arches from balls, to give artificial bouquets. Children's matinees, birthdays, weddings and anniversaries are now complete without such decorations. They give the holiday playfulness and childlike spontaneity, but at the same time they are an integral decoration of adult holidays - weddings, anniversaries and corporate parties.
