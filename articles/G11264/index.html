Radisson Palais Hotel

This 5-star hotel is located on Vienna's most famous Ringstrabe, a 10-minute walk from major attractions in the city center. The main advantage of this hotel is the free internet offered here, and the most famous restaurants of oriental and Italian cuisine. In addition, the Radisson Palais Hotel has in its territory a huge bar and an amazing conservatory. For tourists who come to relax with their whole family, a free trip to the Vendome spa center is offered, in which a vacationer can try the most exclusive types of massage. Also, the conditions of booking a room include free access to the hotel's fitness center.

The modern design and original atmosphere of the rooms will remind you that in recent times this hotel building was a real residence for the richest aristocrats. In addition to free access to the fitness center, a tourist can also use the minibar for free, which is located directly in the room and replenished once a day. This hotel is located directly opposite Vienna's city park.

Fleming's Deluxe Hotel

This 5-star hotel was recently opened, only in January 2011, but has already earned great fame for its service and hospitality. The hotel is located behind the Vienna City Hall, and includes a modern spa and 367 luxurious rooms with a free Wi-Fi hotspot.

Fleming's Deluxe Hotel is located in the business district of Josefstadt, 400 meters from the nationwide famous National Theater. All rooms are air-conditioned and feature original dark wood furniture. Here you will find a small desk and a free minibar. In addition, the hotel has 2 restaurants serving classic Baroque French cuisine, as well as some Japanese and Oriental dishes. In general, the guests of this hotel will be happy to relax here, as a spacious fitness center with a unique Finnish steam room and sauna has been built for them.

Grand hotel wien

The 5-star Grand Hotel Wien is located in the very center of Vienna and a 3-minute walk from the famous Vienna State Opera and the unique Kertnerstrasse shopping street. For tourists and travelers, more than 400 luxurious rooms and 2 original restaurants have been prepared here, where you will be treated to dishes of various European cuisines. The hotel building itself is of historical importance. All of the hotel's elite rooms feature period antique furniture and modern TVs with wide LCD screens. For guests, the hotel provides free business center and sauna services. In addition, tourists will be able to use sports fields for outdoor activities and a large swimming pool for relaxation and sunbathing on it.
