Amuse Hotel 4: description, service, reviews

Crete is a well-known tourist destination that is in demand due to its well-developed tourist infrastructure, golden sandy beaches and a long swimming season. It is believed that a trip to Crete (especially the large resort of Hersonissos) will require a lot of costs. However, in this case there are exceptions, for example, the Amuse Hotel 4 * hotel.

General information about the hotel

This small hotel is located in the heart of the popular tourist city of Hersonissos (just 2.5 km from its center). The building was built in 1988, but renovation was carried out in 2016, so the hotel is ready to welcome guests of the city with bright, clean rooms with a fresh renovation.

The hotel boasts a convenient location. Being just a few minutes walk from the main attractions, it itself is located in a quiet green corner, right next to the sandy beach.

Services

The services provided by the Amuse Hotel 4 are standard for Crete hotels with a 4 * rank. There is everything that tourists may need on vacation, while there is nothing superfluous.

  • The reception is open 24 hours a day.
  • Elevator in a three-story building.
  • Internet - free WI-FI on the hotel territory (access to the network is in all public areas).
  • Safe, you can use it at the reception. The service is free.
  • Transfer - meeting guests at the airport and transfer to the hotel.
  • Car rental for convenient transportation around the island.
  • Laundry and dry cleaning services on site.
  • Currency exchange.
  • An experienced doctor is available for a fee.

Hotel rooms

The hotel has 61 rooms - all of them are presented in the form of standard apartments with a balcony and a bathroom. The interior is made in soft pastel colors with the addition of a noble brown color. It can comfortably accommodate 2 or 3 people (there are 2 or 3 single beds in the room).



The floor is covered with cool tiles, the bathroom has a washbasin, mirror, toilet, shower. Towels are provided on the day of arrival.

The room provides:

  • separate air conditioning with remote control;
  • TV with satellite television;
  • mini fridge with bar (can be used for a fee);
  • open wardrobe for storing clothes;
  • direct dial telephone;
  • bed linen is changed every other day;
  • room cleaning is done daily

Room service is available (on request).

Nutrition

Amuse Hotel 4 in Greece (Crete) has a cozy restaurant where guests are offered 3 meals a day (breakfast, lunch and dinner). International cuisine is served buffet style. There are always meat dishes, salads, pastries and fresh seasonal fruits.



There is a bar in the hotel where you can take soft and strong drinks. The list of alcoholic beverages includes: homemade wine, ouzo, brandy and crayfish. At the bar you can have a light snack between the main meals.

Entertainment

In the courtyard of the hotel is a small pool with fresh water. The area is equipped with sun loungers and parasols, so for swimming you do not even have to leave the hotel.

The relaxation room has a pool table. Those who wish to go on an excursion can purchase tickets from the hotel. Bicycles for walking are also rented here (the cost is to be specified at the hotel).

Beach

Amuse Hotel 4 * is built on the beach, so the sandy beach is located literally outside the gates of the hotel. Here you can use sun loungers and umbrellas for free.



From active beach activities for a fee, there are:

  • diving;
  • windsurfing;
  • boating.

Amuse Hotel 4: reviews

According to tourists, the hotel has its advantages and disadvantages.

Among the cons:

  • the simplest furnishings in the rooms;
  • monotonous menu in the restaurant;
  • small beach (in fact, it is a narrow sandy strip).

The indisputable advantages include:

  • proximity to the sea (located outside the gates of the hotel);
  • low cost of living;
  • interesting excursions;
  • The sandy beach is comfortable for relaxing.

Young people, couples with young children and those tourists who are unpretentious to service at the hotel may like a vacation here.
