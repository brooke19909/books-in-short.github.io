Anapa: hotels with a private beach. Anapa Hotel Reviews with Beach

In the summer, thousands of tourists go on vacation to the sea. Hotels in resorts provide their guests with maximum comfort. However, potential customers often prefer a vacation spot as close to the sea. Waiting for its guests and Anapa. Hotels with their own beach are always in high demand here.

Resort Overview

Anapa is a city in the Krasnodar Territory. It has long attracted vacationers as a balneological and climatic resort. Anapa is located on the Black Sea. Hotels with a private beach are located throughout the coastal strip of the city. This resort was previously one of the most popular health resorts for children. But adult guests are expected by the friendly Anapa. Hotels with their own beach are located in resort towns such as Lower and Upper Dzhemete, Supsekh, Sukko, Maly and Bolshoi Utrish.

Anapa has a well-developed infrastructure that allows guests of the Black Sea coast to spend their holidays as pleasantly as possible. Most resorts operate year-round. About 4 million people rest in Anapa annually. The city has a large network of catering facilities, such as canteens, cafes, restaurants, bars.





Anapa Hotels with a Private Beach

The most populated in the summer are hotels that have access to the sea and the beach. Among them there are large, designed for hundreds of tourists, and small, comfortable hotels, which are very nice to relax from the hustle and bustle. Almost all Anapa hotels are located in close proximity to the sea. The maximum distance is 300 meters. Most of them offer visitors organized meals, room cleaning, video surveillance, security, a cafe. Many have their own saunas and baths, spas, hairdressers, massage rooms.

All hotels in Anapa have their own specifics. Some of them were built in the resort area, and some on adjacent private territories. The first have a well-developed infrastructure. Their star level ranges from 3 to 5, which in itself speaks of the quality of service. Private hotels most often have only the minimum set of options necessary for relaxation: refrigerator, TV, air conditioning, Internet, organized meals. They usually do not have room service, a pool, a sauna, massages and spa treatments, a cafe or a restaurant. Private hotels are most suitable for a quiet family vacation. Another disadvantage of such hotels is that they are usually located at a considerable distance from the sea.

All Anapa hotels are located near sandy or rocky beaches. When choosing a future vacation spot, this also needs to be considered. For children, it is better to choose hotels near which there is a clean sandy beach. Pebbles are more suitable for lovers of swimming and diving in clean water.
