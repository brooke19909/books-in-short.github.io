Rest houses in the Yaroslavl region: list with addresses, rating of the best, services provided and reviews

More and more people in our country prefer a local vacation abroad and instead of warm seas and exotic cuisine choose quiet holiday homes located in local forests, on the shores of lakes and rivers. Rest houses in the Yaroslavl region is one of the leading destinations. Consider them in this article.

BEFORE "Big salts"

Rest house in the Yaroslavl region "Big Salt" is located in the village of Nekrasovskoye, on Sovetskaya Street, 84.

Its location is more reminiscent of a paradise, where you can read in peace and quiet a book that you have long wanted to read, watch your favorite series, just sit back and breathe clean air. This holiday home in the Yaroslavl region with a pool annually hosts hundreds of tourists who have come to rest on a package tour or in private. There is everything you need for relaxation. Comfortable rooms are available in several categories: economy, classic room, junior suite and suite. Each room is equipped with a full set of necessary furniture; the suites and junior suites have air conditioning to help refresh the air on the most stuffy days, for example, in July. Near the holiday home there is a lake with an equipped beach: clean sand, manicured lawns, children's slides and playgrounds. The courtyard is attended by several gardeners who make sure that the flowers are always watered, the trees are trimmed, the benches are repaired. In a word, they create everything in their power to make vacationers easy and comfortable.

A wide range of wellness treatments are offered. At the beginning of arrival, you can contact your local medical staff and take tests to check for any diseases. Particular attention is paid here to the prevention and treatment of the respiratory tract and gastrointestinal tract.

If you come on vacation with children, no problem. For children there is a modern playground, in the common room you can always watch cartoons or spend an evening in the company of your favorite movie characters.

Reviews about this vacation spot are positive, but there are disadvantages that can spoil the vacation. Many of the guests pay attention of the staff to the quality of cleaning the rooms, which does not always correspond to the price that guests paid for accommodation.

Rest house "Clear Dawns"

If, due to circumstances, you cannot leave the city far and for a long time, then the Yasny Dawns holiday home will help you plunge into the atmosphere of country life. It is located directly in Yaroslavl, on Kirov Street, 10. This is a place with a truly soothing atmosphere. It offers tourists rooms of various price categories, from economy to luxury, with a different number of vacationers. You can move in not only one or with the second half, but also with friends or relatives, everyone will find a place. You can book a room of interest by phone.

This holiday home in the Yaroslavl region has a pool. In addition to it there is a private exit to the lake. "Clear Dawns" offer a wide range of water treatments: pool, sauna, various spa treatments aimed at maintaining health and preventing various diseases. The sauna is designed for several people, so you can bask there with friends, and at the same time discuss all the latest news. Various events are held on the territory of the base, especially in the midst of the summer season, in which it will be interesting to participate not only for adults, but also for children. Thus, you can completely immerse yourself in the atmosphere of relaxation and be accessible from the office, if things still require personal intervention.

DO "Forest Tale"

In Yaroslavl region, holiday homes with cottages are especially popular. Forest Tale is one such place. All year round the hotel staff meets and provides an excellent holiday for guests not only in Yaroslavl. People come here from all over Russia to enjoy the comfort and silence, relax from the hectic life of a big city and recharge with the energy of nature.

The number of rooms here is very large. There are economy or luxury rooms for one, two or more people. The average price per day starts at 1,500 rubles with breakfast. The complex is located in the Yaroslavl region, in the Pereslavl region, near the city of Pereslavl-Zalessky. The highlight of this complex is the restaurant complex on the pier. There is also a summer bar and cafe where you can order dishes not only of Russian classical cuisine, but also many dishes of European cuisine. Having settled down for lunch or dinner, you can watch the beauty of the lake or in the evening enjoy the beautiful sunset. People really like it.

Sanatorium "Red Hill"

Would you like to not only relax, but also to heal? Sanatoriums and rest houses in the Yaroslavl region satisfy any, even the most demanding, taste. For example, Red Hill is very popular, located in the village of Red Hill, on Volzhskaya Street, 2, on the banks of the great Russian Volga River.

The number of rooms is amazing. Two five-story buildings, equipped with the latest safety, with elevators, individual rooms in detached houses. There is a restaurant with two VIP rooms, a Finnish bath, a Turkish sauna, a pool, billiards. Room rates start at 1600 rubles. Rest combined with medical procedures, aimed mainly at the genitourinary sphere, gynecology and the circulatory system. For healthy people, there are invigorating and tonic procedures. In winter, you can rent skis or skates and go for a walk in the nearby forest. Among the positive reviews, a special place is taken three meals a day. Guests note that the dishes are all delicious and fresh.

"Yaroslavna"

Rest house "Yaroslavna" is located at: Yaroslavl region, Rostov district, the village of Lions, 1B. This is not just a place in nature, but a real Russian fairy tale, where each guest receives what he needs for complete relaxation. Everything is included here. The rest house in the Yaroslavl region offers not only to bask in the Finnish sauna or a real Russian bath, but also to walk around the surroundings, ride bicycles, go fishing on the lake shore specially equipped for this purpose. You can swim on the beach in the lake, and in the pool, which is available in this holiday home. Those who wish can pamper themselves with spa treatments. Guests who have already visited this place unanimously say that they will definitely return here because they liked everything: service, room comfort, excursions and real, fresh, tasty food.

El Ranch

"El Rancho" - one of those holiday homes in the Yaroslavl region, where inexpensively and comfortably you can fully relax. It is located in the Pereslavl region, the village of Khorobrovo. Here, in nature, you can turn around from the heart: ride horses under the strict guidance of an experienced instructor, cut the quiet waters of the river on an ATV, go fishing or just bask in the sun on the beach. Evenings can be spent with friends or family in the sauna, watch a movie in the common room, and, of course, watch the magnificent sunsets on the veranda. In winter, a holiday home in the Yaroslavl region offers to explore the area on snowmobiles, collecting all of his friends on an excursion. Room rates start at 3,500 thousand rubles per day. You can book a room of interest by calling the reception, which is open around the clock.

Sanatorium "Golden Ear"

"Golden Ear" is a holiday home in the Yaroslavl region, which is located in the village of the same name in the Nekrasovsky district. The first mention of these places dates back to the beginning of the thirteenth century, when the great Russian princes fought for salt sources, which are rich in local land. It is on these unique sources that the sanatorium is based. The magnificent pine forest, together with medical procedures aimed at eliminating a whole range of unpleasant diseases, have a truly wonderful effect. Clean air, the Volga River, active entertainment in the form of cycling or skiing in winter make the vacation truly unforgettable. Therapeutic procedures are aimed at cleansing, losing weight, preventing bone and muscle diseases, the respiratory tract and the circulatory system. Room rates from 1,500 rubles. For holiday homes in the Yaroslavl region - inexpensive. Also on the territory there is a solarium, billiards, sauna, bathhouse, library, restaurant and karaoke.

"Brigantine"

This holiday home in the Yaroslavl region is for those who at heart consider themselves a sailor. Located near the Rybinsk reservoir, in the village of Sudoverf, on 1 Sudostroitelnaya Street, the complex offers a wide range of services. The cost of renting a room starts from two thousand rubles. There are places for mooring your own water transport, river trips are organized in an individual or group format, you can rent a pleasure boat, water skiing or a catamaran and go on a trip yourself. Special praise from tourists has earned the cuisine. Dishes are prepared exclusively from fresh products - both meat and, of course, fish. Amazing nature, peace and quiet, but at the same time, in the reach of all the entertainment points, if suddenly the evening with a book or a movie turns into a burden.

Rest house "On Chesnava"

Yaroslavl region, Breitovsky district, Gorelovo village - this is the address of the popular holiday home "On Chesnava". Located at the Rybinsk Reservoir, the holiday home offers entertainment for hunters and fishermen. Here you can rent weapons for hunting and go on a boar, ducks, moose or geese. When you get tired of wandering through the reed thickets, you can ride a jet ski or water ski, horse or bike. The cost of rental housing - from 2000 rubles per day. Both hotel rooms and individual cottages are provided.

Sanatorium "Railwayman"

Address: Yaroslavl, Pavlik Morozov street, 7. One of the best resorts for families. Room rates start at 1,500 rubles and depend on the room level. Economy, junior suites and suites are available. A feature of this sanatorium is the ability to arrange a spa stay with a full range of available services. You can meet them at the front desk. But tourists who have been here have complaints about the cook: the food portions are very small. The guests of the sanatorium left the table hungry.

For example, you can spend a vacation in a holiday home "Promcooperator", in the Yaroslavl region, in the Rybinsk district, you must submit an application. In any case, no matter what place of relaxation you choose, silence, nature and delicious food will do their job and restore your vivacity and fortitude for the next working period.
