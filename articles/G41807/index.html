Hotel Galaxy 2 Hotel 3 *, Vietnam, Nha Trang: overview, description, characteristics and reviews of tourists

The resort Galaxy 2 Hotel 3 * differs not only in a favorable location, but also in a high level of service, as well as a wide range of services.





Brief description of the hotel

Galaxy 2 Hotel 3 * offers travelers a relatively inexpensive accommodation option with a very favorable location relative to urban infrastructure and major attractions. Of particular note is the quality and level of service in this resort hotel. If you have any questions or problems, then you can contact the reception desk staff who are always ready to help you at any time.

Special attention should be paid to the room stock of the Galaxy 2 Hotel 3 * hotel. All apartments are permeated with a sophisticated oriental style, as well as homeliness. One of the main features of the rooms of this hotel is that the floors are covered with wooden parquet, which keeps the heat. In addition, the apartments are equipped with many necessary amenities, which also include a wide range of household appliances. It is important that you can use the wireless Internet absolutely free of charge without leaving your room.

In the resort-hotel complex Galaxy 2 Hotel 3 * also pays attention to the organization of leisure guests. So, turning to the tourist office, you can go on an exciting sightseeing trip. If you decide to explore the sights on your own by renting a vehicle, you can leave it in a free parking lot. And in order for you to always have the strength and energy to travel, you are invited to refresh yourself with the delicious dishes served in the restaurant.





Where is the resort located

Galaxy 2 Hotel 3 * (Vietnam) is located in Nha Trang, a few steps from a large and clean public beach. If you want to have fun and get your share of adrenaline, then visit the large water park Fu Dong, which is located no more than 100 meters from the resort. About 10 km away is the famous bamboo island, a visit to which is considered a must-have program for every tourist. The airport is not very far from here (about 26 km), in addition, you can order a transfer at the hotel.

When relaxing at this resort, be sure to visit the following attractions:

  • the ancient Tram Huong Tower (you can drive or walk on foot no more than 300 meters);
  • Take a 2 km bus ride to Dam's Great Market;
  • 3 km from the hotel is the Long Son Pagoda;
  • the architectural masterpiece can be considered the towers of Po-Nagar (4 km from the hotel);
  • the cable car (5 km from the hotel) will give you thrills.

Hotel Rooms

Resort Galaxy 2 Hotel 3 * (Nha Trang) can offer travelers a choice of several accommodation options, among which it is worth noting:

  • Standard room, which is a compact bedroom. On a small area there is a double or a pair of single beds.
  • Superior apartments, although slightly, exceed standard rooms in size. Thanks to this, it became possible to complement the furniture ensemble with soft chairs, a coffee table and a floor lamp.
  • For large companies or those tourists who are used to traveling with their loved ones, the opportunity to stay in a family room is provided. By area, it is two times larger than the standard and consists of two cozy bedrooms.
  • The most expensive apartments in this hotel are deluxe rooms. They are quite spacious and represent a bedroom and a living area, combined within the same room. A distinctive feature of these apartments is the presence of a small balcony with wicker furniture, as well as a clothes dryer and towels.




Useful information about hotel accommodation

Having chosen the resort Galaxy 2 Hotel 3 * (Vietnam, Nha Trang) for your vacation , you should certainly familiarize yourself with the following useful information:

  • The resort has 49 guest rooms; check-in of guests takes place after 14:00, but leave the room on the day of departure and hand in the key for reception no later than noon;
  • Checking into a hotel, you will be required to leave a deposit of $ 100 (a non-cash form of payment is also possible by blocking this amount on a credit card);
  • upon check-in, each guest is required to provide an identification card with a photograph;
  • When going on vacation with babies, please note that cots are not available in the rooms;
  • guests are not allowed to bring pets to the resort;
  • use of wireless Internet is absolutely free both in public areas and in apartments;
  • compact area, as well as the layout of rooms do not allow you to place extra beds;
  • if necessary, you can order an airport shuttle for an additional fee; Turning to the reception staff, you have the opportunity to book tickets for any type of transport.




Positive reviews

Many travelers stay at the inexpensive Galaxy 2 Hotel 3 * hotel. Reviews reflect the following positive aspects of relaxation in this institution:

  • from the windows of rooms located on the upper floors, a beautiful view of the sea coast;
  • the registration and settlement process is very fast and without unnecessary red tape;
  • maids try and clean very cleanly in rooms as well as corridors;
  • the hotel is located almost in the city center near transport stops, which is very convenient;
  • the staff is very helpful and diligent.




Negative reviews

Of the negative comments of travelers, it is worth highlighting the following:

  • due to the fact that there is a busy highway next to the hotel, the rooms are noisy both during the day and at night;
  • there are insects in the rooms (the most serious problem is huge cockroaches);
  • in the bathroom the whole tile is beaten and moldy, as well as dampness attracts a lot of midges, which also bites;
  • very poor breakfasts; fruits are not included in the diet of visitors;
  • Around the hotel is constantly under construction, which spoils the surrounding view, and also creates a lot of noise and garbage.
