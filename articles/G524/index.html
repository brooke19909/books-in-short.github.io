Pride of the area

More and more often today, instead of blue seas and sandy beaches, people prefer dense forests and crystal clear rivers. In order to spend the weekend or vacation in comfort, you can not travel outside the country. Moreover, all those who live in the Voronezh region will not even have to leave their native land. On this small piece of land there are many camp sites. One of them is Forest Tale. Voronezh is also proud of its nature reserve, whose goal is to preserve the integrity and purity of nature.
