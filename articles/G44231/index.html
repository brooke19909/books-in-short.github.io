Hotel Elmi Suites Hotel Apartments 4 * (Greece / Crete): description and reviews

Recently, Greece has been receiving more and more tourists. The fact that the popularity of the resorts of this country is growing, you can understand. After all, there are excellent beaches, clear sea, beautiful places, and quite reasonable prices are set for hotel accommodation. A vivid confirmation of this fact can be considered a hotel Elmi Suites Hotel Apartments 4 *.





Briefly about the hotel

There is a town in the north of Crete called Hersonissos. This is exactly where Elmi Suites Hotel Apartments 4 * is located. The hotel is located in close proximity to the sea. A pebble beach can be reached in a minute. Nearby are attractions such as the Lychnostatis Folk Art Museum and the Aquarium called AquaWorld Aquarium. They can be reached in 10-15 minutes.

There is a bus stop 50 meters from the hotel, which is very convenient. The airport is located 25 kilometers from Hersonissos, in the city of Heraklion, which is not very far.

Service

Elmi Suites Hotel Apartments 4 * has everything that tourists may need on vacation. Free Wi-Fi is available throughout the lobby, there is a luggage room in the lobby and individual lockers. The reception is open around the clock, so that tourists can enjoy express check-out / check-in, and the hotel also has a currency exchange, laundry service and tour desk. If guests are accommodated with children, then they can be offered the help of highly qualified nannies.

For travelers who need to stay in touch even on vacation, a computer center is organized, as well as a fax, printer, photocopy and telephone line.

In addition to the above, on the territory of Elmi Suites Hotel Apartments 4 * there is a mini market, a shared lounge, separate smoking areas, as well as rental cars and a stall with souvenirs. Room service and packaged lunches are provided on the way. Airport transfers are also possible.

But the fact that the hotel has multilingual staff who speaks Italian, German, Greek, English, French and Russian is especially pleased.





Leisure

In Elmi Suites Hotel Apartments Hersonissos 4 * you can not only live comfortably, but also have fun. There is everything you need for leisure activities. Active sports enthusiasts will appreciate the game of squash or a visit to the modern gym.

And what exactly to allocate time for, the guest will decide individually.
