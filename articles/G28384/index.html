Phuket Attractions

Tourists who are not ardent fans of diving, but still have a desire to see the underwater world and its inhabitants, can visit the famous Phuket Aquarium. Although not very large, several hundred marine inhabitants and fish live in it. The whole aquarium is divided into different sections. In one of them, visitors can see clown fish and butterflies, known for the famous cartoon "Finding Nemo." On weekends, aquarium workers arrange shows with feeding the inhabitants, among which there are predatory sharks. The pride of the aquarium is the underwater tunnel, a walk through which is more like diving to the seabed. A visit to this place will appeal not to adults, but to their children.

The most famous landmark in Phuket is the statue of Big Buddha. It is so huge that it is clearly visible from different parts of the island.

A lotus statue sits on Mount Nakaked in Chalong and guards the temple. Numerous believers come to worship the shrine. And tourists come here to enjoy the stunning scenery from the observation deck. The complex was conceived in the early twentieth century. The place for its construction was chosen not only beautiful, but also shrouded in legends.

No less interesting place for visiting tourists is the Botanical Garden. It is deservedly recognized as one of the most impressive places on the island. The garden was created not long ago, it has been working since 2012. Currently, it occupies almost 10 hectares. Its entire territory is decorated in the national Thai style, it is decorated with fountains, various decorative elements, artifacts and wood carvings. Guests have the opportunity to see dozens of varieties of cacti, hundreds of plant species and unique palm trees. And walks along the flowering alleys of the garden with ponds will bring great pleasure to nature lovers.

Women who come to Phuket, as a rule, do not miss the opportunity to visit local farms that grow pearls. The guides accompany the boat trip to the island of Nakha with a story about how the process of forming the real pearl occurs. Farm workers not only demonstrate all stages of production, but also demonstrate their skills in making jewelry to guests. Favorite pearl jewelry can be purchased at your local convenience store.

Oddly enough, but among vacationers, a visit to a snake farm is especially popular. On its territory you can watch an amazing show of pythons and royal cobras. True, such a spectacle is not to everyone's taste. During the show, trainers demonstrate all kinds of tricks with poisonous snakes.

In Phuket, there are currently more than thirty Buddhist temples. One of them is Wat Chalong.It is still not known exactly when it was built. Legend has it that the temple is at least 150 years old. Outwardly, the complex is more like a fairy-tale palace, consisting of several buildings. In the main temple there are statues of three monks, but the entrance to it is closed to ordinary people. Only monks can be in the building.



Tourists of all ages should visit the famous Sirinat Park, which is a favorite vacation spot not only for guests of the resort, but also for its residents. The vast territory of the park covers almost eight thousand hectares. There are three equipped beaches, as well as wild rainforests untouched by a human hand. The park has built the island‚Äôs largest water park called the Jungle Splash. The stunning water complex consists of a network of pools, extreme slides and attractions. For active tourists, the park organizes trips through virgin mangroves. Tourist routes pass through the most beautiful places.

Another amazingly interesting place in Phuket is the entertainment complex "Fantasy". A visit to this unusual place will be a real holiday for the whole family. The park has numerous activities involving tropical animals. In addition, the complex has numerous cafes and restaurants. Unusual colorful shows await guests daily.

Holidays on the island - this is the contemplation of seascapes and stunningly beautiful flowers, which the resort has an incredible amount. The most common of these are orchids. Fans of these plants have a unique opportunity to visit the Orchid Garden, in whose territory you can see 1,500 varieties of various shapes and colors.
