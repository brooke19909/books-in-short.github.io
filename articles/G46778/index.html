Beach vacation

Numerous tourist camps are located on the wide Black Sea coast. Sergeevka





This resort attracts the attention of those who plan not only to swim and sunbathe, but also to improve their body. Sergeevka is a large seaside climatic and balneological resort in the south of Odessa region. Recreation facilities (Odessa provides an opportunity not only to relax, but also to improve health), located here, offer interesting treatment programs, including for children. The combination of estuary, the sea in the south, the forest park zone and the steppe expanses has a good effect on the life-giving air of this resort. The unique healing properties of the climate are determined by the clean steppe air and the warm sea breeze, so your stay in Sergeevka will definitely be complete. What good rest is in Sergeevka?

  • Water in the sea warms up already in May and remains suitable for swimming until October.

A small village in the Odessa region - Nikolaevka - is located on the seashore, therefore it belongs to the recreation zone.
