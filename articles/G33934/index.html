Fragrant heart made from coffee beans

In our turbulent times, filled with stress and constant rush to work, as well as a rich social life, the daunting task is to fall asleep on time, and wake up healthy, fresh and alert to take every day with a smile. The coffee cult is quite appropriate in a noisy city, when the aroma from the coffee shop pushes us into friendly conversations, and in a small mountain village, where it seems to be especially tasty and smoky. I put my heart in coffee

Such a phrase is appropriate not only when you brew a fragrant drink. With the help of grains, you can create cute things. If your soulmate is a coffee lover, then create a card for her on Valentine's Day by laying out a heart of coffee beans on thick paper (brown looks good in combination with pink or blue) and securing it with glue. Write a few words about your shared love for the divine drink. You can make a textile heart of coffee beans, in other words - a pillow. Such pads come with herbs or essential oils, but a coffee pillow will help revive the mind. Grains decorate a simple frame for photos. The pictures laid out of coffee look wonderful too. Candles glued with this product look interesting. The mass of options - for every taste and degree of employment. But in the form of a tree, a heart made of coffee beans (the photo is presented in the article) long ago gained popularity among those who want to create comfort in the house. Let's learn how to make this wonderful craft.

Where to use coffee crafts?

The heart tree will be a good gift. Make yourself a small garden of these "plants" of various shapes. Place the pot on a bookshelf or desk. If you are a fan of all sorts of pendants, then choose the perfect thread: coarse or made of crystals. Then hang the hearts (or any other shapes - stars, animals, balls, cubes - most importantly, to combine) of coffee of different sizes on the base or attach them, for example, so that they hang from the bar counter.
