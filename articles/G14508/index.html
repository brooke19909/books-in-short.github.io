Making money on trash

If yesterday garbage was a panacea in developed countries, today waste recycling is one of the most developing business areas.

At that time, while entrepreneurs from other areas complain about the lack of raw materials, in this case there is always some kind of garbage that can be recycled and get a lot of money for it.

This is facilitated by the constant development of technologies that even make something worthwhile out of the ashes. Science makes it possible to use various elements of waste even to create biologically clean fuels. This is the future.

In addition, the processing of, for example, glass or metal allows you to use the material again. This means that such waste can be processed endlessly.

Profitable investment

Of course, in search of a place where to invest 1,000,000 rubles in order to earn money, you must definitely pay attention to the recycling. It will take a lot of money to open such a business, but this amount is enough if you prudently manage the money.





The main tasks will be the purchase of special equipment and the establishment of transport links with the source of raw materials and end users of the processed product.

Not a new but changing vending business

So, we continue to review good ideas for business. If you have not yet decided where to invest 1,000,000 rubles and earn, then be sure to consider a vending business.

Unfamiliar word? Remember the apparatus with sparkling water in the distant times of the Soviet Union. The essence of the vending business is to place automatic devices that can serve passers-by in a particular area.

Despite the fact that a similar type of business appeared in Europe in the 17th century, it is rather poorly developed in the Russian Federation and is mainly focused around coffee and shoe covers. The full potential of this type of service is not revealed at all.

A new trend is machines with healthy food. Yes, you will not believe! Coca-Cola and the chips that you can buy by throwing a coin in the machine are a thing of the past. The modern world cares about the state of its health.





Imagine a network of special machines that help people eat healthy and healthy. Given that low-quality products containing GMOs, preservatives and other harmful additives are increasingly appearing in today's stores, your business will flourish.

Install a minimum of 5 devices

If you like this type of business and, choosing where to invest a million rubles (1,000,000 rubles), you settled on vending, then consider the following nuances. Beginners always think that by putting 1 device in a crowded place, they will be able to earn. This is an erroneous opinion.

For payback within 1-1.5 years, experts advise installing at least 5-6 machines. Of course, crowded places are important for business, but you need to pick them up so that this crowd is interested in your products. That is, passers-by should want to use such a machine. It follows that in rich areas, private sectors, the placement of devices will be inappropriate.

They should stand near and in business centers in which mid-level personnel work. There should also be nearby places where you can exchange or withdraw money from the card to pay for the services of the machine.

Particular attention should be paid to the operability of the devices: if the client gives his money at least once, and the mechanism does not give him the desired product, then the person will never come to this place again for a purchase, having painted it in all colors before his relatives and friends.

Distribute money to several financial institutions

Never put all your money in a deposit in one bank. In this case, you risk too much to be left without funds. Experts advise dividing their finances into several parts (preferably at least 5) and, after analyzing the market conditions, put them on deposits in different banks.

Moreover, the bulk of the money should be placed in financial institutions with a worldwide reputation or national (state) status. Other commercial banks are much more often at risk of bankruptcy.

For convenience, issue a plastic card, on which interest will be transferred monthly or at the end of the deposit term. This method will not bring you superprofits, but it will keep your money safe and will allow you to earn at least a small but necessary penny.
