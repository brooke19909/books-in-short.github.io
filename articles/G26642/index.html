Payment card Visa Electron Sberbank, its functions and capabilities

Sberbank Visa Electron payment card allows its holder to pay in trading terminals and receive funds through ATMs in many countries of the world. Any resident of the Russian Federation who has reached the age of fourteen can find it in the city branch of Sberbank at the place of its registration. And in some cases - and in other branches and branches of the bank. Visa Electron Sberbank cards are basic and optional. Their main difference is a more in-depth set of functions for additional ones. In addition, its owner may be a non-resident of the Russian Federation. However, in order to receive an additional card, a written statement of the main holder will be necessary. They can also be received by relatives of the owners of the main cards.





Sberbank's rather cheap Visa Electron payment card is an affordable opportunity for a large number of Russian citizens to meet their needs in simple financial transactions, which include such functions as:

  • the ability to receive funds on the card (including salaries);
  • Settlement using trading terminals (including abroad);
  • money transfers using mobile services;
  • account management using an Internet resource, etc.
