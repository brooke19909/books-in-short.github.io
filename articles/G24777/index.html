What is the first monument to plumbing in Russia?

In fact, there are a lot of monuments to plumbers and workers in related industries in our country. Several street sculptures are fighting for the title of the very first. But still, most often the oldest monument to plumbing is considered a monument installed in Yekaterinburg. This attraction appeared in 1995 near the central office of Uralenergoserviskomplekt, it was this organization that ordered the manufacture and installation of such an unusual street decoration. Externally, the monument in Yekaterinburg is very similar to his brother from Omsk. It is also a bust of a worker in a helmet protruding from the hatch.

The most original sculptures



Most often, plumbers are depicted sticking out chest or waist from a hatch. But there are several unusual and original sculptures in our country dedicated to workers of water utilities. In Perm, near the city bath, a sculptural composition was installed, consisting of a sculpture of a plumber sitting on a pipe with an adjustable wrench in his hands.

The expression on this person is very thoughtful, it seems, another moment and he will return to his difficult work. There is an unusual monument to plumbing in Ussuriysk. The sculpture is installed near the central water utility building. The monument depicts a man in full-length workwear, it is believed that the plumber Yury Rubanov, who actually exists in the city, became the prototype of the monument.

How many plumbers are immortalized in Russia?



In total, about 20 monuments dedicated to locksmiths and other workers whose professions are somehow related to water supply have been installed in our country. There is a monument to plumbing in such cities as Chelyabinsk, Volgograd, Moscow, Prokopyevsk, Saransk, Rybinsk, Tyumen and many others. There are many sculptures dedicated to communal services in Ukraine, today there are such monuments in many other countries.
