Vietnam Vacation and Attractions Reviews

If you are going on a trip, but have not yet decided which country to choose for your vacation, then we recommend that you visit one of the Southeast countries in Asia, namely Vietnam. The first question that you probably have come up with is: "Why do I need to choose this particular place to stay?" We will be happy to answer it.

Exotic nature and a large number of attractions

Vietnam is a country with a promising and constantly developing tourist destination. It surprises not only with its exotic and superb beaches, but also with the low cost of tours, entertaining sights. In addition, tourists vacationing here speak positively about Vietnamese cuisine. We think that many readers probably wanted to go to this beautiful country. True, another question arises about Vietnam: "Where is it better to go?" Reviews of tourists who have already been here can help determine the direction. In this article we will tell you about the most popular places in this country. We also provide information on the most interesting sights.

Open-air museum

The most popular city, despite its small size, is Hoi An. This place is very loved by all travelers. After all, this city is suitable for lovers of both passive relaxation and active. There are a lot of beaches, but even more museums and various interesting sights. Among the most popular tourist activities are the following:

  • diving classes;
  • visiting temples;
  • purchase of souvenirs;
  • inspection of ancient buildings;
  • visiting museums and attractions and much more.

Here are some of Vietnam's best white sand beaches. Tourists love to sunbathe on them. To get from one point of the city to another, residents and visitors go on foot or ride bicycles. There is no public transport.

Great beaches and mild climate

They are waiting for you in the very center of the country. Resort town - Phan Thiet - tourists have opened recently. This place impresses with its beautiful sights and comfortable climate. It offers tourists the best hotels with a high level of service. You can come here all year long and not be afraid of bad weather. For tourists with children, all the necessary conditions have been created here: the best white sand beaches of Vietnam, a large number of entertainment attractions, convenient descent to the sea, experienced animators, special food, game rooms, babysitting services and much more. The city is also unique thanks to the colorful dunes that give it great beauty and splendor. On the Internet you can find reviews of people who have been here. Among the advantages of this place, they note the affordable prices for souvenirs and various entertainment.

Hue Imperial Palace

It is considered one of the main attractions in the country. If you still do not know what to see in Vietnam, then you should definitely see the Imperial Palace in Hue. But they should warn that, ideally, you need to spend at least a whole day on this, so we do not recommend visiting this place with young children. The palace will seem incredibly beautiful and ancient to you. Although the building was badly damaged, restoration work is very active.

Previously, emperors lived and ruled in this city. By their order, a large number of buildings were erected, which still amaze with their beauty and grace. Thanks to this, as well as its rich and interesting history, Hue attracts more and more tourists every year. Among the attractions of Vietnam in the reviews, tourists note the imperial mausoleums or royal tombs. Here is a huge number of unique sculptures, and you can also see exquisite patterns and drawings. If you are interested in the history of Vietnam, then you should definitely come to Hue.

Nha Trang - entertainment city

This place will delight you with a large number of restaurants, shops and beaches. Rest assured, you will never be bored here. Nha Trang (Vietnam) - an island of entertainment. From here, you can get to neighboring islands by boat, where you can take pictures of cute and friendly monkeys or spend the whole day in an amusement park. Not only children, but also adults will like this pastime.

In Nha Trang there are temple complexes, which are certainly worth a visit. Here you can see interesting performances, which include national dances, and get acquainted with folk crafts. The city also has a large number of museums and other historical attractions. And here you can buy amazingly beautiful silk paintings or just admire these unique works of art.

The most unique sights

Having studied the reviews of a large number of people who visited Vietnam, we offer you information about some of the most popular places:

  • Fragrant river in Hue. She is considered one of the most beautiful and unusual in the country. It is best to ride on it in the evening, because it was at this time that the jasmine flowers that grow along its banks exude a surprisingly wonderful aroma. Locals and tourists like to have a picnic on the river bank to relax and enjoy the beauty of the surrounding views.

  • Nha Trang Waterfalls. Here you will see an amazing sight that will remain in your memory for a long time and bring a large number of positive emotions.

  • Phong Nha Ke Bang National Forest. Tourists leave only the most positive reviews about this place. The park has a huge number of caves and grottoes. The magnificent nature and breathtaking landscapes do not leave anyone indifferent. Here you can forget about everything and enjoy the beauty of the surrounding places. What to see in Vietnam yet?

  • Phu Quoc Island Rare animals and plants are found here, there are waterfalls, as well as impassable places. It is very easy to feel complete unity with nature on this island.

Resorts in Vietnam: where is better?

Why are many tourists drawn to this amazing country? A large number of attractions, a clear sea, exotic nature, a high level of service and a huge selection of entertainment await everyone who chooses a vacation in Vietnam. According to reviews, Nha Trang and Phan Thiet are very popular. There is a large number of hotels in which quite affordable prices, delicious and varied food, high level of service. The mild climate allows you to visit the above-mentioned resorts almost all year round. We told you only about a few resort cities where you can have a great time. In fact, there are many more.
