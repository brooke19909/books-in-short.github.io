Sound Bomb by Lenovo

The Chinese concern Lenovo once again pleased consumers with a novelty. She turned out to be the flagship smartphone Vibe X3 Dual Sim. Taking into account the characteristics declared by the manufacturer and the extraordinary design, interest in the new product reached its peak long before the start of sales. I must admit that this time Lenovo even managed to exceed customer expectations.

Case and design





The case of the smartphone from matte plastic is framed by a metal insert. Details about this - http://www.foxtrot.com.ua/en/shop/mobilnye_telefony_lenovo_vibe-x3-dual-sim-white.html . The screen is protected by Gorilla Glass 3, a coating that practically does not collect fingerprints. In addition to the display on the front panel there are two stereo speakers, an activity indicator and a front camera lens. The back panel contains a slightly raised unit consisting of a main camera, a double flash and a fingerprint scanner. On the side faces are located:

  • on top - a 3.5 mm headphone jack, as well as an infrared port;
  • bottom - micro-USB connector;
  • on the left is a tray for SIM cards;
  • on the right are the on / off keys and the volume rocker.

The curved back surface allows, despite the impressive dimensions of the gadget, it is convenient to feel it in the palm of your hand. No backlashes and creaks are observed.

Display

The screen with the Full HD IPS-matrix with a diagonal of 5.5 "is able to please the most demanding users. At a height also brightness, viewing angles and color saturation.

Cameras

The smartphone is equipped with a 21 megapixel main camera and a 8 megapixel selfie camera. The quality of the images on the main camera in daylight is excellent. When shooting indoors or in low light - just good. Examples of pictures, as well as general information about the smartphone can be found on foxtrot.com.ua.

Sound

The main advantage of the Vibe X3 Dual Sim, without a doubt, is the high-quality sound. For this, the ESS Saber ES9018C2M converter, the Wolfson WM8281 codec and two OPA1612 front-speaker amplifiers are in response. All this, combined with sound processing using Dolby Atmos technology, creates a high-quality and really loud sound, ideally balanced for watching video and playing music. Recording high-quality sound on a smartphone is provided immediately by three microphones.





Iron and software

The hardware is represented by Qualcomm Snapdragon 808 - a powerful processor with 6 cores with a frequency of 1.8 MHz, controlled by Android 5.1.1, which, combined with Adreno 418 graphics, 3 GB of RAM and 32 GB of internal memory, allows you to solve most of the most complex tasks, including pretty hard games. The battery with a capacity of 3600 mAh is quite successfully responsible for autonomy: with intensive loading, its charge lasts for a day.

conclusions

Lenovo Vibe X3 Dual Sim is really a success. For a very reasonable 500 USD, the consumer gets a really decent device with excellent speed, a high-quality camera and simply transcendental sound quality.
