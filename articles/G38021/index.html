Holidays in Greece: hotel Mitsis Summer Palace Resort 5

Description: The luxurious, modern hotel complex Mitsis Summer Palace Resort 5 is very popular among tourists - this is where vacationers flock from all over the world. After all, here guests are offered only the best living conditions, as well as professional service and a real beach holiday.





To begin with, it is worth noting that Hotel Mitsis Summer Palace is part of a huge complex, which includes two more neighboring hotels. Residents of this hotel can use some amenities of other hotels (for example, water slides) for free.

Mitsis Summer Palace Beach Hotel 5 is conveniently located. For example, in just five minutes you can drive to the center of Kardamena. The airport is only 14 kilometers away. The city of Kos is 32 kilometers away.

Rooms : The hotel has 252 rooms. You can move into a standard room, from where you can enjoy views of the exotic garden and the sea. Or you can opt for individual secluded bungalows. There are also large family rooms, which consist of two spacious rooms.





All rooms are decorated in a simple Greek style. In addition to the required amount of comfortable furniture, there is also a home appliance. On TV, you can watch your favorite satellite channels, including some Russian ones. There is also an air conditioning system, a telephone with a separate line and a safe. And in the minibar you will find chilled beer, water and sweet soda.

Naturally, there is a private bathroom with a bathtub with shower, hairdryer and necessary toiletries.

Meals : Hotel Mitsis Summer Palace Resort 5 offers its guests all-inclusive meals. Four times a day there are generous tables, where guests can enjoy fresh vegetables and fruits, as well as fresh fish and seafood, traditional local snacks, beer and fine wines.

Of course, the hotel complex includes two wonderful restaurants, including Italian. By the way, tables here need to be booked in advance. There are five bars serving excellent cocktails (including children‚Äôs), as well as wines, beer and other local drinks.

Beach: A wonderful beach is located only 30 meters from the main hotel building. For a comfortable stay there are sun loungers and umbrellas, which can be used for free. Beach towels are also free, but issued only on bail. Here, vacationers play beach games, do water sports, ride boats and catamarans.

Additional services : Everything on the territory of Mitsis Summer Palace Resort 5 was created to meet the needs of guests and provide them with the most comfortable pastime. Hotel guests can use the parking services, rent convenient transportation, dry clean and laundry. In case of health problems, an experienced doctor will be called.





For every fifteen rooms there is a private pool. In addition, there is a common large swimming pool with sea water, as well as several indoor, heated. The neighboring hotel has a wonderful water park, the services of which can be used at no extra charge.

The hotel also offers some sports activities. There are tennis courts, billiard tables, volleyball and football courts, and the possibility of horse riding. If you wish, you can always relax in the jacuzzi or sauna.

There is a large open theater where fun events are held twice a day.

Pleasing and child care services. There are separate children's pools, playgrounds, a mini club, a club for teenagers, as well as a small disco where dances are regularly held.

Reviews : Hotel Mitsis Summer Palace Resort 5 managed to earn an excellent reputation. It is here that tourists from all over the world come to enjoy the warm sea waves, wonderful landscapes and excellent living conditions. Guests also praise the welcoming, welcoming atmosphere, the polite and helpful staff, and the excellent cuisine.
