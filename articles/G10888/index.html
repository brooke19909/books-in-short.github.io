Is it possible to take a mortgage without formal employment: tips and tricks

How to get a mortgage without formal employment? An urgent issue of concern to those who possess the necessary amount of money, but cannot provide the bank with the required information. This category of people includes those who receive "gray" wages and cannot officially confirm their income - freelancers, tutors, landlords and people in creative professions.

Making a mortgage without formal employment is difficult, but possible. There are several ways to accomplish this.

Methods of confirmation of solvency

Most banking and credit organizations require a minimum set of documents from borrowers, among which are certificates confirming official income. Individuals can get a mortgage after submitting the following documents:

  • Help 2-PIT. It takes into account average official earnings. A mortgage will be approved provided that the salary is sufficient to obtain the required credit limit.
  • Confirmation of the term of work at the official place of employment. It is required to provide a copy of the work book, contract or certificate from the employer indicating the position, duration of work, name and details of the organization, seal and signature of the head.

Confirmation of income is not required if the salary account is maintained at the bank in which the mortgage is issued. The lender to assess the solvency of the borrower looks at all salary accruals for a specific time period.

However, the availability of official employment and high salaries does not guarantee a mortgage. Borrowers providing all documents may face certain risks:

  • The employer has set a limit on the issuance of certificates 2-NDFL.
  • On the part of the head or the accounting department of the organization, difficulties arose that did not allow issuing a certificate.
  • The employer does not want to confirm the fact of employment of the borrower. The reason for this may lie in incorrect tax reporting indicating lower salaries of employees.

You can verify official earnings using not only the 2-NDFL certificate, which is the standard proof of income and employment.

Confirmation of solvency without reference 2-NDFL

You can confirm your own income without providing a 2-NDFL certificate, respectively, it is possible to issue a mortgage without official employment:

  1. Information on bank forms. The lender issues a standard form, which is filled by the accounting department of the organization.
  2. Help on the form of the employer. Banks allow the provision of documents indicating income in free form.
  3. Tax return.
  4. Contracts and legal documentation proving official employment and the income level of the borrower.
  5. Statements from deposit bank accounts. The best option is if the account is opened in the same bank where the mortgage is issued.

Who gets a mortgage without proof of income

An unofficial source of income, in fact, is not an obstacle to obtaining a mortgage. Mortgages without formal employment are possible for the following categories of borrowers:

  • Salary customers.
  • People whose deposits are in the selected credit institution.
  • Landlords.
  • Traders.
  • Citizens whose income depends on securities.
  • People whose source of income is proprietary products.

Specialists who know how to apply for a mortgage without formal employment, note several categories of borrowers who may be given a loan when providing a certificate of income. These include non-working pensioners, freelancers, students and other citizens.

How to get a mortgage without inquiries?

There are several ways to increase the chances of a bank approving an application: by providing free-form income statements or a larger down payment. However, such measures do not give an absolute guarantee.

Banking organizations in the conditions of high competition of mortgage lending often meet the needs of customers who wish to apply for a mortgage without formal employment. Banks come up with several suggestions:

  • Confirm the loan with security in the form of personal property, which may be real estate or a vehicle. A prerequisite is a high liquidity of the collateral.
  • Issuance of mortgages without proof of income.

Mortgage secured by real estate

An option for borrowers who own a collateral - commercial real estate or an apartment. An additional collateral reduces the risks of banks and increases the chances of the borrower to get a mortgage. In addition, the conditions under which a loan is issued will be much easier if the client provides liquid real estate on bail.

However, not all real estate can be called a collateral. The Bank may not accept the following types of property:

  • Residential premises, the redevelopment of which has not been confirmed.
  • Property under renovation or demolition.
  • Housing, sanitary and hygienic indicators of which do not meet the standards.
  • Real estate located in disadvantaged and remote areas.
  • Shabby buildings.
  • Objects already acting as collateral or under arrest by the FSSP or the court.

Depending on the type of property, certain requirements are imposed on it. For example, in the case of a private house, it should be possible to erect a free entrance, a concrete foundation, there should be no wood in the ceilings.

Mortgage loan for two documents

Will they give a mortgage without official employment under two documents? In attempts to resolve this issue, the unemployed often turn to illegal methods of proof of income, among which is falsification of documentation. However, this is not required in 2018-2019, since many banking organizations offer mortgages on two documents and a package of additional ones, including a passport, application form and documents on pledged property.

Ways to get a mortgage without formal employment

Is it possible to get a mortgage without formal employment? Credit organizations offer several ways to get a loan without providing a statement of income and without formal employment.

Consumer loan

Loans of this type are provided by banks on the basis of two documents. The maximum loan amount is not enough to purchase housing, but it allows you to pay most of it. The limit on a consumer loan can reach 1-3 million rubles.

Maternal capital

Can I get a mortgage without formal employment with the help of maternity capital? The latter can not act as a loan, but allows you to pay most of the down payment.

For young families, a mortgage is issued upon registration by the borrowers of both spouses, so one of them must confirm the income. Parents of young people can act as additional guarantors - their income is also taken into account when calculating the loan.

Preferential programs

For those who want to get a mortgage without official employment, Sberbank and other credit organizations offer preferential programs for certain categories of borrowers:

  • Large and young families.
  • Military personnel.
  • Budget workers.
  • Young families that received maternity capital.

Preferential programs include a reduction in the interest rate and down payment, payment of the initial payment or a share of the loan.

Mortgage for students and seniors

Is mortgage possible without formal employment for students and retirees? Banks offer special programs for these categories of citizens. For example, students who have reached the age of 21 and are citizens of the Russian Federation can apply for a mortgage loan and receive a deferred payment for the period of study. Their guarantors are the parents.

Can I get a mortgage without formal employment for pensioners? They can apply for a loan under the following conditions:

  • The loan is issued only in ruble terms.
  • The minimum interest rate is 11% per annum.
  • Mortgage repayment term depends on the age of the borrower and cannot be for the period in which the client turns 75 years old.
  • The size of the pension should be sufficient not only to pay off monthly payments, but also to meet the basic needs of the borrower.
  • Adult children of the client become the guarantor of the repayment of the mortgage loan, provided that they have higher solvency.

Pensioners and students are virtually inaccessible to the most favorable credit conditions. To insure their own financial risks, banks set a minimum lending period and high interest rates.

Mortgage loan for a young family

Is it possible to take a mortgage without formal employment for a young family? Spouses to take out a loan must participate in the same project.

They can confirm their own solvency in several ways:

  • Filling in the income statement in the form of a credit organization.
  • The guarantee of the labor organization.
  • Providing documentary and verbal evidence.
  • Opening an account or issuing a debit card at a bank in which it is planned to obtain a mortgage loan.

Can I get a mortgage for those who just got a job?

Banking organizations try to avoid concluding mortgage agreements with borrowers who have just got a job. The minimum experience in one place should be six months.

An exception is customers who have signed a perpetual employment contract and have worked for a long time at the same place, but later transferred to another branch or to a new position.

How to increase the chances of approving an application

Banks try to cooperate with solvent customers, work with which does not carry high risks. There are several ways to increase the chances of approving a mortgage application:

  • Attraction of guarantors. Mortgage for a married couple is approved subject to the registration of one of the spouses by the co-borrower and the presence of a high income and official employment.
  • Making a large down payment - from 40% and above the value of the property. In this case, the bank can not only approve the mortgage, but also lower the interest rate.
  • The presence of additional sources of income.

In the absence of official employment, approval of the application for a mortgage loan can be obtained subject to the provision of guarantors, which may be friends or relatives of the borrower. In this case, all calculations are carried out on the basis of joint income, which increases the chances of approval of the application.

Is it possible to take a mortgage without formal employment? It is advisable to provide the manager with documents confirming solvency. Such securities may be a lease agreement, printouts from bank cards or bank statements.

  1. Confirmation of sources of income and solvency.
  2. Providing a certificate of seniority in free form.
  3. Registration of property as collateral.
  4. Use of preferential programs offered by banks.
