Country Park Hotel Chaika (Barnaul): getting to know the complex

In the hectic workdays, everyday problems, we look forward to a long-awaited vacation or weekend. This is the time when you can spend time in the circle of dear and close people, go on a trip to nature. Increasingly, urban residents prefer rural cottages, where harmony and tranquility reign.





Such a vacation is gaining more and more popularity. Everyone is invited to visit the Park Hotel Chaika (Barnaul). The tourist complex is located near the city center, a 15-20 minute drive away. An amazing corner for a good rest, surrounded by massive pine trees and other picturesque vegetation.

Where to stay?

I am glad to offer the Chaika Park Hotel (Barnaul) comfortable accommodation from economy class to spacious apartments. The address of the suburban complex: Borzovaya Zaimka village, Pionerskaya Dolina street, No. 4. Guests can pre-book their favorite room in advance. When you pay for a room, you get a free hot breakfast.





So, for budget tourists, rooms of the category "Economy" and "Economy +" with a shared bathroom and shower are offered. The rooms are well-repaired, soft beds, wardrobes and such are installed.

Prefer a private toilet and bath? Then check in to the Standard room. The guest house has a "Junior Suite" with a large plasma TV, armchair, bed. For couples - "Studio" with an extra bed.





You can also pay for accommodation in a separate cottage with two rooms, kitchen, bedroom and living areas. Near the house there is a landscaped area tdyha with wicker furniture.

Not sure how to get to the Chaika Park Hotel (Barnaul)? How to get to the complex, local residents will tell. From the train station to the hotel no more than 12 km. You can get to the village of Borzovaya Zaimka by city or private transport.

Catering





Lunch and dinner are additionally paid. The menu is discussed with each client individually, taste preferences are taken into account. A luxurious restaurant with a spacious hall for 350 people and a VIP room with 50 seats is open for guests. Relaxing atmosphere, delicious food and live music will allow you to truly relax and enjoy. Large companies come to the Chaika Park Hotel (Barnaul) for celebrations and business events.

What to do in nature?





Active people will be delighted with the abundance of entertainment services. Gather a team and play soccer or volleyball. Spend time with health benefits in the gym. There is a spacious room for boxing, aerobics. All necessary sports equipment is rented out. In warm weather, the Chaika Park Hotel (Barnaul) offers swimming in the outdoor pool, in the river.





Be sure to stroll through the pine forest, enjoy the beauty of the local nature. After fruitful leisure, visit the Russian bathhouse with a cool pool. In winter, you will not get bored either: you can ride a sleigh, ski, sculpt snowmen, arrange sporting events. A nice end to the day will be gatherings near the fire. Throughout the territory there are gazebos with barbecue facilities. And for an additional fee you will have professional photography, which will remain in your memory for a long time.

Business services





Applications for large-scale events are accepted: business meetings, negotiations, presentations. For these purposes, a spacious room is equipped with the necessary technical equipment. The conference room can accommodate up to 250 people. At the request of the client, a leisure program can be drawn up.

Children's service

Park Hotel Chaika (Barnaul) is another excellent camp for children. People come here in whole classes to take a break from school everyday life, to get a portion of good mood. Students are accommodated in comfortable rooms, they are provided with good nutrition and an extensive game program. There is a summer pool, a relaxation room, an outdoor area.
