Smartphone LG K7 X210DS: reviews, specifications

LG has been releasing clones for quite some time. This has become especially noticeable over the past couple of years. An unusual design, a similar concept, different specifications - and here we have the new LG K7 X210DS smartphone. Reviews about this device correspond to its capabilities. Equipment

A box for a smartphone looks simple. It is white, in the middle with huge turquoise letters the model is indicated. LG, as usual, does not shine with special generosity, therefore, apart from the instructions and the charger, there is nothing else in the box.

Appearance

As mentioned earlier, the new model turned out to be similar to its previous "brothers". Got LG K7 X210DS design reviews are positive. This concept of the company's appearance was a success. Everyone wants to buy a smartphone with a rounded shape and famous control buttons.



The phone is available in three colors: black, white and gold. Most of the front panel is occupied by the screen. Around it on the sides there is a small black frame, but from below and above the place could be saved.

Housing Details

Above the screen are the front camera and earpiece. Below is the company logo. Along the edge of the case is a plastic edging painted in metal. As you know, such models do not have buttons on the sides. The main hardware keys are located on the rear panel. We will get to it a little later.

At the top there is only an additional microphone. But on the bottom placed the headphone jack and charging. Also here is the main microphone.

The back panel is made of high quality plastic. About him, the smartphone LG K7 X210DS receives only positive reviews. Many users fell in love with its rough texture, thanks to which fingerprints are not visible, and the smartphone confidently holds in your hand.



On the back is a camera with flash. Under the lens, as usual, the hardware control buttons were placed: a volume rocker and a lock / shutdown. The company logo flaunts in the center of the back cover, in case the first logo was not enough.

Under the hood

By opening the rear panel, you can find a 2125 mAh battery. Also, there are already three slots, two of them are for "SIM cards", the third is for a memory card. Such generosity is pleasing to customers. In addition, given the small amount of memory, the presence of a microSD slot will never be more useful and in demand.

Ease of use

As mentioned earlier, thanks to the pleasant texture, the smartphone lies well in the hand. It also contributes to its round shape. Despite the fact that the screen is average in size - 5 inches, due to the large frames at the top and bottom, the device seems bulky. Its weight is 158 grams.

On the convenience of the smartphone LG K7 X210DS Black reviews are getting quite good. Only girls with a miniature palm complain. For men, this gadget size is more than comfortable. They are not difficult to control with one hand. The location of the hardware buttons is unusual only at first. Literally after a week of use, everything becomes convenient. In addition, when you hold the smartphone in your hands, the fingers themselves lie on these keys.

The usual "dialer"

Thanks to the small screen resolution, the graphics chipset is fully working on performance. In work, it is fast and stable. "Stuffing" also easily copes with all installed software and launches applications without lags.
