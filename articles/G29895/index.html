Smartphone Protection

There is a whole classification system that mobile devices that have enhanced protection have. It is defined by marking - this is an IP class. It looks, for example, like this: IP68 (this is the maximum level). Both of these numbers are different types of protection that are used on similar devices. So, the first digit means accessibility to penetration of certain objects into the device (the minimum level is large objects, from 50 millimeters; and the maximum refers to dust). The second is the designation of the possibility of moisture entering the apparatus. The lowest degree is, accordingly, the absence of any protection; while the highest is the complete isolation of the smartphone.

Therefore, if we talk about which protected phones will be protected more, we recommend that you pay attention to this index. As a rule, most of the most common devices with protection have the highest level. If we talk about simple smartphones, then even they have a certain level. They will be discussed in the next article.
