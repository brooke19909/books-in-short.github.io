What is remarkable about the Yasenevo metro station? Firstly, it is interesting to know that its depth is 8 meters. It is a shallow station. There are only three flights here. Secondly, a huge number of columns supporting the ceiling, standing in two rows, attracts attention. There are more than fifty of them.

The distance between the columns is approximately 7 meters. They are noteworthy for their cylindrical shape, the marble of green color, which they are lined with, also looks beautiful. In addition, you should pay attention to the walls of the station. They consist of lime green metal tiles. It has the shape of cells. The design of the station was developed by N.V. Shurygin and N.I. Shumakov.

How to get into the subway and get out of there?

Access to the Yasenevo metro is possible only through underground entrances. There are no vestibules located on the surface at this station.

You can go outside from here through the underpasses. Where are they going? On Novoyasenevsky Prospect, as well as the streets of Tarusskaya and Yasnogorskaya. Near the station there are many beauty salons, catering places and supermarkets.

Temples, parks, estates

Near the metro there are several churches that are currently functioning. Those who like sports can go to the complex called the Commonwealth. If you just want to take a walk, the Yasenevo district is great for this. There are several large parks of the capital. The most popular of them is Bitsevsky. It is huge, perhaps only Elk Island is bigger than it. It is worth getting to the metro station "Yasenevo" to take a walk here. People come here even from afar. In addition, the Znamenskoye-Sadki estate, as well as Uzkaya, are located on the territory of the park. Purposeful and courageous inhabitants of the area

Yasenevo is located in the south-west of the capital, it is located quite far from the center. In this rather large area very purposeful and active people live. At the end of the 80s, it was decided to create a zoo and make a trip to the Moscow Ring Road. However, residents have ensured that these plans have been canceled.

And at the end of the 2000s, a garbage burning enterprise should have appeared here, but people also did not allow this. So living near the Yasenevo metro is safe for health. It is noteworthy that an apartment in this area can be bought at an average cost. Of course, housing prices here are simply astronomical, as in the whole of Moscow. But if you compare Yasenevo with other areas of the city, then buying a property here is profitable.
