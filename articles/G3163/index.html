Stroyinvest LLC (Tomsk): reviews

Buying your own home is the dream of many Russian families. At the same time, everyone wants to save a little, buy a house with available money or with a surcharge by taking a loan. And the less it will be, the more chances for citizens to live with dignity. Therefore, the proposals for the sale of cottages at attractive prices are very tempting, not so much for dealers as for such ordinary families.

In 2008, a new company entered the market under the name Stroyinvest (Tomsk). It was quite difficult to find reviews at that time due to the fact that few people worked with this developer.



Realizable objects

First of all, the company was engaged in active advertising, as well as the implementation of large-scale projects that helped to establish themselves in the market. In the Red Hill microdistrict, more than 6 hectares of land were developed and 64 standard cottages were built. Implemented the construction company "Stroyinvest" (Tomsk). Reviews on building quality are pretty good. The houses are delivered on time, the quality of construction is excellent. Therefore, the company is steadily increasing momentum and acquiring new customers.

Country project

After the successful completion of the construction of residential cottages, the Stroyinvest company in Tomsk set about providing residents with suburban areas intended for the most part for recreation. Below market prices

This is always somewhat alarming. If the work is done efficiently and on time, then why reduce prices? The company "Stroyinvest" (Tomsk) is characterized by extremely attractive price offers. What's the catch? The point is the economy. The company does not purchase building materials, most of them supply their own careers. In addition, to carry out work, its own concrete-mortar unit is used. The workshop for the production of plastic windows is actively working. The company is preparing to launch a new plant, which will provide an opportunity to provide even more opportunities for its customers.

All this allows us to occupy a leading position in the region, which Stroyinvest LLC claims to be. The problem came unnoticed

So far, we have only talked about how the construction site stood in the new village of Lesnoy massiv. But the residents of Krasnaya Gorka in the village of Kornilovo were also hit. People may lose their new homes because they were built too close to the gas pipeline. Several dozen families have already built houses here, ordinary people who have invested in the construction of everything that they had. In 2015, they received a notice that the housing was too close to the gas pipeline, which means that the houses should be demolished. This is a gross violation of safety precautions. 116 areas intended for construction fall under the restricted area, but in some of them people have already built solid houses. Is the builder to blame, in this case, Stroyinvest? Most likely not, because someone issued him a building permit. But today no one is going to be responsible for what happened. And people do not want to stay on the street at all. There is no final decision yet.

Another story

Two pensioners who saved up money to move to Russia also turned to this developer. We collected all the money, laid an apartment in Kazakhstan, borrowed it from relatives and went to place an order for construction. And then the worst began. At a personal meeting, the head of the Stroyinvest company said that there are no finished houses now, but he can sell the land and conclude a contract for the construction of the house. Gradually filled out all the documents, executed the contract, paid the full amount. And after that, the leader stopped picking up the phone and answering calls. Arriving a year later, the pensioners saw that a second house was built on their site, and at a personal meeting the head of the company said that their contract of sale was terminated by a court decision and the house was sold to third parties. So pensioners were left without anything, with debts and loans, with a pension that goes for regular trips to Russia and notary services.

Instead of a conclusion

Making a construction contract seems like a very good offer. For the price of an old apartment, you can become the owner of a real, country house. And of course, many people decide to take advantage of this offer. However, you need to carefully choose the developer and collect feedback, carefully read the contract, and it is better to consult with a lawyer.
