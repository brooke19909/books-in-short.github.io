LG G4C: smartphone review

Today we look at the LG G4C mobile phone. An overview of this device is given below in full detail. It will be about the representative of the middle category, but he has a number of strengths.

Equipment



So, before us is the LG G4C H522Y. We begin the review with the package. The package is as follows: documentation, headset, USB cable, charging unit and the phone itself. Versions of the device with other delivery kits are also available.

Design, build quality and materials



So we figured out what configuration the LG G4C smartphone comes to store shelves. An overview of its appearance is given below.

First of all, we note that we are talking about the junior representative of the G4 series. From the flagship, he received a plastic back cover. The device is a classic 5-inch smartphone. The dimensions of the device are 139.7x69.8 mm, with a thickness of 10.2 mm. Thanks to the rounded cover, the smartphone does not feel bulky. A light weight of 136 grams underlines the visibility of the compact device. The case material in this case is ordinary plastic. However, the back cover is complemented by relief and pattern. The plastic cover is simple in design. The control buttons for LG are typically located on the back of the device. The build quality of the smartphone is good. A removable back cover is provided. Under it are 2 MicroSIM slots and a microSD bay. There is also a removable battery.

Screen, color quality and viewing angles



So we figured out the look of the LG G4C. An overview of the screen is given below. The device received a five-inch display with IPS-matrix. Its resolution is 1280x720 pixels. The screen has good viewing angles and high-quality color reproduction. A decent pixel density of 294 ppi.

Tests



Now let's take a look at the performance of the LG G4C. An overview of the tests is given below. According to the manufacturer, the smartphone is built around a 64-bit Snapdragon 410 platform. It includes the Adreno 306 graphics subsystem and a 1.5-GHz quad-core processor. Performance results in tests are incredibly good. Games work smoothly, as on flagship models. The smartphone received 8 GB of read-only memory, of which about 3.3 GB is available to the user. This indicator cannot be called high. However, it can be expanded with MicroSD. The maximum storage capacity is 32 GB. However, you cannot place games on MicroSD. The removable type battery capacity is 2540 mAh. A decent result for the specified screen diagonal and performance indicators. Video viewing time was about 7 hours at maximum brightness. The charge of the smartphone will be enough for a day of battery life under intense load. An external mono speaker is located at the back of the device. Its quality is not satisfactory. The sound in the headphones pleases. It reaches, if not surpasses, the capabilities of the flagship.

Other characteristics

The review of the LG G4C phone will continue with a description of its operating system. The device is running Android Lollipop. The platform is complemented by a proprietary shell authorship LG. This upgrade brings a number of additional features to the operating system, including gesture support. Now you should take a look at the cameras. There are two of them: the main - 8 MP, the front - 5 MP. The main module is equipped with an LED flash, and is also able to record video in Full HD quality. Most often, the main camera allows you to get pictures of medium quality. However, they can be excellent if there is good lighting.

The 5 megapixel front camera shows average results. LG G4C is equipped with the necessary set of wireless modules: 4G, FM-radio, Bluetooth, Wi-Fi, GPS. The price of the device is about 15,000 rubles.

Opinions



Now let's see what the owners of the LG G4C say in their reviews. A review of the opinions of the owners of the device is given below. So, the build quality, shell convenience, design and communication characteristics deserve praise. The owners also note that the device fits perfectly in the hand. The volume is recognized as high. The sound in the headphones is often characterized as excellent. Display users consider bright and high quality. Praise also awaited the outbreak, which was recognized as effective.

Many are happy with the easy switching between two SIM cards. Some users attribute the lack of side buttons to their merits. In their opinion, such a design allows you to confidently hold your smartphone in your hand, without fear of accidentally pressing a key.

The thickness is recognized as ideal, noting that it allows you to conveniently take the device from a perfectly flat surface, while it does not slip out.

The response also earned praise. It is noted that the display responds to touch instantly. The presence of the Android 5.0 operating system allowed the phone to receive even more positive ratings from the owners.

Among the shortcomings mention the lack of oleophobic coating (this problem can be solved by purchasing a special glass or film). There are also several complaints about the sound of the speaker. Some reviews say that the battery is poorly calibrated and from 20 percent can be discharged immediately to two in a few minutes. The FM receiver is also not always praised, mentioning that it catches stations poorly in urban environments. There are complaints of lack of memory. Some owners of the smartphone note that the back cover is unstable to external damage, in particular scratches. A pair of active numbers found a response in the hearts of users. Also in the comments it is stated that the fourth-generation network module does its job perfectly. Users are pleased with the availability of Glance View. This is a function that allows you to get the necessary information by swiping your finger across the screen without completely unlocking the device. Now you know everything you need about the LG G4C smartphone. We tried to make the device overview as detailed as possible.
