Nha Trang beaches

The main beach of the city is located south of the Kai River and has a length of about six kilometers. It is decorated with a beautiful and well-groomed promenade with walking paths, a park area, sculptures and places for relaxation. The beach itself is quite wide, sandy. The sea on the coast is calm, which is very convenient for swimming. Nha Trang is one of the most beautiful beaches of Southeast Asia.

Bai Duong Beach is located north of the Cai River. This is a beautiful and comfortable place, but the sewer canals passing nearby spoil the impression. The beach is popular among Vietnamese vacationers, so in the afternoon it is completely empty. Nearby there are several seafood restaurants serving Vietnamese tourists.

Hong Chong beach is located among the rocks. Tourists can climb to the top and admire the nearby islands from there. Entrance to the beach is paid.

There are several beautiful beaches on Hon Tre Island, but they are only available for recreation to the guests of Winperl Resort. The exception is the small beach in front of the water park, which can be visited by guests who bought a ticket. The beach offers water sports, there is a restaurant serving Russian cuisine.

Beaches outside the city

Doc Let Beach is a beautiful long beach north of Nha Trang. Local fishing families prepare delicious meals here at an affordable price. The most comfortable part of the beach is the one closest to the resort hotels. The remaining sites, unfortunately, are littered with trash abandoned by local residents.

Long Beach - located in the areas of Cam La and Cam Ranh. Upscale resorts are being built on the coast. It's nice to walk along the beach, although trash can be the same problem as on the previous beach. Especially a lot of it in areas remote from hotels.

There are several other beaches in the vicinity of Nha Trang. About 30 kilometers from the city, a small peninsula overlooks the South China Sea. Here are small hotels, and on the beaches are white sand and tall palm trees. From Nha Trang to the beach can be reached by taxi or passing bus.

Communication in Nha Trang hotels

Internet access is available in almost all hotels. When using the phone, it will be useful to find out about local tariffs. Mobile cards are available and cost about 50,000 dong (local currency). They usually already have credit for phone calls.

Travel tips

During daylight hours, Nha Trang is a safe and fun city, but you need to be careful in the evening. Experienced travelers are advised to leave all the valuables in the hotel room, and especially mobile phones and cameras, which are most targeted by petty thieves.

Although there is a rather low crime rate in Nha Trang, there is still a risk of being robbed. The most beloved place of local criminals is near the Sailing Club. Often the victim does not even realize that she was robbed until she returns to her room.
