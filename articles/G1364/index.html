Hotel "Aviator", Kazan: reviews, phone, address, photo

Hotel Aviator (Kazan) offers you the opportunity to choose from a large number of rooms that are oriented to different tastes and prices. Also here you can take advantage of a huge list of services.





Brief description of the hotel

One of the most attractive accommodation options is the Aviator Hotel. Kazan attracts many tourists as well as business people. Here everyone will find a number to their taste and wallet. The apartments are rather modest and concise, but at the same time being in them is very comfortable. It has such necessary amenities as a bathroom, TV, refrigerator and other equipment.

The developed infrastructure is offered to its guests by the Aviator Hotel (Kazan). Especially guests like a gym with a huge number of simulators. Free Wi-Fi is also available. Also worth noting is the billiard room and bar, which offers a huge selection of drinks and light snacks.

Hotel Aviator (Kazan) offers you to dine in a luxurious restaurant, where the best chefs work, as well as order food in your room. After satisfying your hunger, go on an excursion, which can be organized by the staff of the tourist office.





Hotel Aviator (Kazan) - address

If you travel to Kazan by car, this hotel will be a great option, because it is only a few minutes away from a major intercity highway. If you like sports, this accommodation option is also suitable, because two large complexes are located very close by. The city center is within easy reach - only 20 minutes by bus. If you are satisfied with such a location, then the Aviator Hotel in Kazan is ideal for you. Address - Akademika Pavlova street , 1.

Economy Class Numbers

If you do not want to spend a lot of money on hotel accommodation, the Aviator Hotel in Kazan can offer you the following economical accommodation options:

  • Economy Quadruple is a small room with 4 single beds, between which there are nightstands. All guests are provided with a table, wardrobe, refrigerator and TV. It is not necessary to rent a whole room; you can rent just one berth.
  • Triple economy is a fairly small room. Three single beds are located almost back to back. Here you can stay with your family or rent a separate bed.
  • Triple standard is an impressive room. There are three single beds. Thanks to the huge window, the apartment has a lot of light.
  • Double standard can be equipped with two single and one half or one large bed. There is quite a lot of living space, as well as comfortable modern furniture.




Superior rooms

If you are used to comfortable living conditions, then in this hotel you can stay in the following apartments:

  • Business class is a large room with a spacious bright bedroom with a working area. The feeling of comfort is created due to the fact that the living part of the room is hidden from the front door by a small hallway area.
  • The business plus differs from the previous category in a larger area, as well as an elegant design. There is also a large plasma TV in front of the bed.
  • Luxury apartments can be both double and single. By design, they are absolutely identical, but differ only in the size and number of one and a half beds.
  • Premier apartments are decorated in a classic style, and the interior is dominated by light, calm colors. Within one room, not only a bedroom is equipped, but also a living area.
  • The studio, in addition to the bedroom, also has a living room. These two zones are separated by a bar.

The luxurious classic design characterizes the executive rooms offered by the Aviator Hotel (Kazan). Photo of the rooms showcases elegant decor in light colors.





The amenities provided in the apartment

In each of the guest apartments of this hotel you have the opportunity to take advantage of the following amenities:

  • a large wardrobe in which all your belongings will fit;
  • comfortable orthopedic beds;
  • comfortable folding upholstered furniture, which can also serve as an extra bed;
  • a desk on which you can work productively (it can also be used as a cosmetic);
  • large plasma TV (in executive apartments), which is connected to a satellite dish;
  • landline telephone with access to an international line;
  • an electric kettle that will allow you to make yourself a hot drink at any time without going down to the bar for boiling water;
  • if you brought along any products, you can have a bite to eat in the room due to the availability of a set of dishes and cutlery;
  • thanks to the presence of air conditioning (in the superior apartments), your room will always have a comfortable air temperature;
  • The bathroom has a full range of bath accessories.




Hotel Infrastructure

In this hotel you can take advantage of the following infrastructure facilities:

  • throughout the hotel operates a wireless Internet network, the use of which is provided free of charge;
  • in the fitness center you can work out on simulators, as well as get advice from a professional instructor;
  • in the billiard room you can have a good time playing an interesting game;
  • there is all the necessary equipment for table tennis;
  • Despite the fact that this hotel is not a resort, there is a swimming pool;
  • at the bar you can drink alcoholic and refreshing drinks, as well as tea or coffee;
  • in the restaurant you can taste the best dishes from the international menu;
  • there is a paid private car park with 24-hour security;
  • the reception is open around the clock, and therefore you can always get answers to your questions;
  • in the laundry room your soiled clothes will quickly be brought to proper condition;
  • a business meeting or seminar can be held in the conference room;
  • in the banquet hall you can celebrate any celebration;
  • you will quickly reach the upper floors by elevator;
  • there is a small souvenir shop on site;
  • if necessary, you can book a transfer to the airport or train station.

The use of certain infrastructure facilities offered by the Aviator Hotel (Kazan) requires prior registration. Phone to make an order and get a certificate: +7 (843) 204-99-02.





Positive reviews

Many tourists and business people prefer such an institution as the Aviator Hotel (Kazan). Reviews about it contain the following positive comments:

  • The rooms are equipped with large new refrigerators;
  • fairly affordable prices for accommodation;
  • on each floor there is a shared kitchen, which you can use if you do not want to spend money on food in cafes and restaurants;
  • pretty good breakfasts;
  • the staff is very friendly and smiling;
  • the rooms have recently been renovated;
  • the room has an electric kettle and service;
  • happy with the availability of budget accommodation options;
  • parking is well guarded.




Negative reviews

Not only advantages, but also disadvantages is the Aviator Hotel in Kazan. Reviews of tourists note the following negative points:

  • the rooms in the hotel are incredibly small (and especially for economy class rooms);
  • the quality of the cleaning is very mediocre;
  • the hotel is more like a hostel;
  • terrible audibility between numbers;
  • when booking an economy class room, be prepared for the fact that the bathroom is shared by two rooms;
  • very old windows that let in all the sounds from the street;
  • there are very few parking spaces, besides it is paid;
  • the location is very uncomfortable - this is almost the outskirts of the city;
  • it is better to come for breakfast in advance, otherwise you will not have enough not only food, but also cutlery;
  • the shower cabin does not close well, which is why after bathing on the bathroom floor a lot of water is collected;
  • very old creaky furniture;
  • very fast registration and check-in procedure (even if many people come);
  • very few double rooms (suites only);
  • there are a lot of spots on the sofas, and bald spots even appeared on the carpet;
  • muddy water flows from the tap due to problems with pipes;
  • the rooms have cockroaches;
  • the presence of dishes in the room, of course, pleases, but it would not hurt to wash it.

General impression

Hotel Aviator, despite being quite old and more like a Soviet hostel, is very popular among city guests. This is due to the fact that housing prices are quite affordable. Nevertheless, you should be prepared for the fact that this is the very outskirts of Kazan, and therefore there are some problems with transport. Its advantage is its proximity to sports complexes. That is why groups of fans or teams that take part in competitions most often come here. Also, this hotel is suitable for budget travelers who are not afraid of a long journey to the city center, as well as some "scuffed" rooms.
