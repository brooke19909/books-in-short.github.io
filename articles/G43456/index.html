What fabric is best for a summer dress?

Satin

If you are interested in fabrics for summer dresses for special occasions, then pay attention to satin. It is made from 100% cotton and at the same time looks incredibly rich, competing even with natural silk. Satin does not wrinkle and is able to withstand many washings. Satin dresses attract a beautiful shine and are incredibly pleasant to the touch.

Chiffon

When asked about what fabrics should be for a summer dress, most girls answer: beautiful, light and airy. All these epithets correspond to chiffon.
