Hotel Holiday Village Manar 5 * (Tunisia, Hammamet): overview, description, rooms and reviews

More and more tourists choose Tunisia as their vacation spot. They are attracted by clean beaches, the turquoise sea, a rich excursion program and high quality service. There are many hotels in the country, but one of the most worthy and prestigious is Holiday Village Manar 5 * (Tunisia). Vouchers here are not cheap, but is the level of service adequate for the price?

basic information

The construction of the hotel ended in 1987. Since that time, he began to receive tourists all year round. Most of them come here from France and other European countries. Locals who flock here after Ramadan also love to relax here. At the entrance to the territory of the Magic Holiday Village Manar 5 * hotel (Tunisia), tourists are greeted by two snow-white buildings built in an oriental style. The six-story building is the main one, the reception is located here. The four-story building also hosts guests. In total, 333 rooms are located in both buildings, among which there are rooms for non-smokers and tourists with disabilities.





The tourist complex covers a vast area (130,000 sq. M.). She is all surrounded by greenery. There is a palm grove, and many fruit trees, and flowering shrubs. This place is ideal for families with children. The hotel accepts kids of all ages. Hotel Magic Holiday Village Manar 5 * (Tunisia, Hammamet) has its own sandy beach with a length of about 450 meters, as well as a water park equipped with water slides. The last repair of all hotel buildings was carried out in 2013-2014. The average price for one night in a complex ranges from 8,000 to 19,000 rubles. Thus, the cost of a week of rest in this place will be approximately 56,000 - 133,000 rubles, not including the payment of additional services.

Where is the complex located?

The complex is located in a large tourist area, located near the city of Hammamet . This resort is perhaps considered the most prestigious in Tunisia. For example, near the Holiday Village Manar 5 * ( Hammamet , Tunisia) in the summer residence the president of the country lives. The distance from the hotel to the city center is about 7 km. Tourists can take a taxi and get there in about half an hour. A little further (15 km) is Yasmin-Hammamet - another major center of tourist life in the country. It is worth going there to visit a huge amusement park, which will give a lot of impressions to both adults and children.

The hotel is built right next to the beach, so the road to the sea takes a matter of minutes. Tourists come to the complex from the nearest airport of Enfida , the distance to which is about 60 km. For hotel guests transfers are organized in both directions. Also, travelers can always rent a car or call a taxi. Tourists can visit the public beach of Hammamet , which is located 200 meters from the complex.

A bit about the rooms

For guests who came to rest in Tunisia, the Holiday Village Manar 5 * hotel has prepared 333 comfortable rooms. Most of them are designed for small families, but there are multi-bed apartments in the hotel. The rooms are non-smoking. Three rooms are specially equipped for people forced to travel in a wheelchair. The complex offers the following types of rooms for its guests:

  • Standart room. It consists of two bedrooms, in which there is a double bed. Designed for families of three. Some rooms have a bunk bed. Room size is 37 square meters. m. Most rooms have a balcony.
  • Family apartments. Also have two bedrooms, equipped with regular and bunk beds. But their area is slightly larger (48 sq. M). The rooms are separated by a door. Some rooms have a private balcony.
  • Superior family rooms. Their area is 58 square meters. m. The simultaneous residence of six people. The room has two bathrooms, two bedrooms and a nursery.
  • Standard Swim-up Rooms. The difference between these apartments is the presence of a terrace, which has a separate access to the pool.




Room facilities and amenities

All rooms are of high quality repair. Each apartment of the Holiday Village Manar 5 * hotel (Tunisia, Hammamet) has its own unique design, designed in a simple classic style. A thorough cleaning of all rooms is carried out daily, and clean towels appear in the bathroom. Once every few days, maids change clothes on all the beds. Tourists can enjoy comfortable accommodation in rooms equipped with the following amenities:

  • Mini fridge and minibar. Both are empty. Their filling is not included in the tour price.
  • TV is a modern model. Satellite TV channels are connected, among which you can find several Russian-speaking ones.
  • Central air conditioning, which tourists can regulate at their discretion.
  • Safe. Using it is paid separately.
  • Paid, but unlimited wifi .
  • Hair dryer, toiletries (liquid soap, shampoo, bathing cap and shower gel). The toilet also has a bidet.

Infrastructure facilities and services

For guests who come to Tunisia, the Holiday Village Manar Hotel (5 stars) seeks to provide only first-class service. For them, a whole network of infrastructure facilities operates on the territory of the complex. We list the most significant of them:

  • Several elevators equipped for wheelchair travelers .
  • Free wifi in public areas. An internet cafe is also available for visiting .
  • Library. Most of the literature is presented here in English and French, but there are books in Russian.
  • Safe at reception. Valuable items can be stored here for free.
  • Private parking. Tourists can not only leave their car here, but also rent a car.
  • Paid laundry, where guests can wash and iron clothes.
  • A business center that will be useful to business people resting here. It provides three conference rooms, the largest of which can accommodate up to 500 people. Tourists can also use fax or photocopying services.
  • Currency exchange.
  • Doctor's office. The cost of admission is paid separately.
  • Shops selling clothing, alcohol and tobacco, food and the press. There is also its own souvenir shop.




Hotel entertainment program

Being a first-class hotel, the Holiday Village Manar 5 * complex (Tunisia) provides a whole range of entertainment services, among which activities for lovers of outdoor activities and tourists who prefer quiet leisure will find activities to their liking. The most interesting are:

  • Water activities: diving, windsurfing. On the beach you can ride a banana, canoe, catamaran, water skiing and sailing boat. Mass games are often held: beach volleyball and water polo.
  • Pools: outdoor and indoor heated. Sun terrace and sun umbrellas are available free of charge. Beach towels are issued as a deposit.
  • The hotel has its own water park, access to which is free for guests of the complex.
  • Animators who work both day and evening. Various shows are held on Arab and Tunisian culture, competitions and performances, as well as evening and night discos.
  • Cinema and cabaret.
  • Tour desk and guide who speaks Russian.
  • Spa and wellness center. They offer a massage room, solarium, Turkish bath, sauna and steam room. There are several hot tubs. Has its own hairdresser.
  • Sessions of yoga, aerobics and water gymnastics.
  • Volleyball and tennis courts. Experienced instructors also conduct training sessions for beginners.
  • Field and archery equipment.
  • Billiard room.




Catering: meals, bars and restaurants

Tasty and varied food is offered to guests by the Holiday Village Manar 5 * complex (Tunisia). It works on an all-inclusive basis. The price includes all meals, desserts and fruits, as well as soft drinks and spirits. A distinctive feature of the hotel are daily theme nights that introduce hotel guests to different cuisines of the world. Tourists can always eat or have a meal at the following catering establishments:

  • The main restaurant specializing in European cuisine.
  • Restaurant "Milan", which prepares Italian dishes. Once guests can visit it for free.
  • Restaurant Spice Market. It serves Lebanese cuisine.
  • Restaurant "Breeze". Tourists can enjoy delicious Mediterranean cuisine, namely seafood, grilled fish, light salads.
  • Fans of Latin American cuisine should visit La Careta Restaurant.
  • Lobby bar located at reception. Light snacks, pastries and refreshments are served.
  • Snack bar . Here you can eat a pizza or a croissant , having a cup of tea or coffee.
  • DiscoBar . Open from 23:00. All drinks here are paid.
  • Aquabar .
  • Pool Bar.




Relax in a hotel with children

Families with children having a rest in Holiday Village Manar 5 * ( Hammamet , Tunisia), reviews about the organization of children's leisure leave good. For children, the hotel has a shallow pool, as well as water slides, air mattresses and swimming toys. Six days a week there is a mini-club , which can be visited by children 3-12 years old. Here they draw, participate in educational and outdoor games. Children are also entertained by experienced animators with whom they learn songs and dances. Competitions are held. Little guests can also take their time on the playground.





For babies, a cradle will be brought to the room upon request, the cost of which is paid separately. The hotel offers for tourists with children discounts on their accommodation. In restaurants and bars in the public domain there are highchairs. Parents can also call for a nanny to sit with their child while they are busy. It provides round-the-clock service, and its services are paid hourly.

Advantages of the hotel, according to tourists

Any hotel cares about its reputation. Thanks to the quality service, Holiday Village Manar 5 * (Tunisia) receives positive reviews . Tourists note the following advantages of the complex:

  • It feels like the rooms have been recently refurbished. The furniture is new and the beds are very comfortable. Spacious balconies are available.
  • The restaurant offers a choice of many types of meat and fish. Dishes are not very diverse, but tasty and satisfying.
  • Clean but shallow sea. The entrance is smooth. Ideal for swimming with children.
  • Great team of animators. Many of them speak Russian. Very funny people who cheer up all day.
  • The girls at the reception are very friendly and polite. They will always answer the questions you are interested in , and will also help solve the problem.
  • Clean swimming pools, which are watched by lifeguards.




Disadvantages of the hotel, according to tourists

But some vacationers in the Holiday Village Manar 5 * (Tunisia) reviews (prices are the main minus of the hotel) leave negative. Apart from the cost of the permit, they note the following disadvantages:

  • Low quality alcohol. It seems to be diluted with water in order to save.
  • High humidity in the area, many flies and midges.
  • The restaurant does not wash dishes well. Juices of low quality, taste more like drinks " UPI ".
  • There were cases when money disappeared from the rooms.
  • Boring and sparsely populated discos.

Instead of an afterword

The hotel has minor negative reviews, so you can recommend it for relaxation. Great location, vast territory, friendly staff of the Holiday Village Manar 5 * complex (Tunisia) will give you wonderful impressions and a lot of positive emotions. We recommend this hotel for families with children, and for young people, and for business people who are used to working even on vacation. We can safely say that the price here corresponds to the quality of the services provided.
