Museum of Nature of Buryatia in Ulan-Ude: photos and description, how to get, reviews

People living in this northern region take care of their amazingly rich nature. In 1983, a regional museum was opened in Ulan-Ude, which is the only environmental and educational museum of nature throughout Siberia and the Far East.

The main mission of the Museum of Nature of Buryatia in the city of Ulan-Ude is to educate the population in terms of ecology.

A brief history of the creation of the Museum of Nature of Buryatia in Ulan-Ude

The Environmental Education Museum is located in the former Public Assembly House, a historic two-story building built by Austrians (prisoners of war) during the First World War.

The decree of the Ministry of Culture of the RSFSR on the creation of the Museum of Nature was issued in mid-February 1978, and in 1983 it was opened on the basis of the nature department of the museum of local lore (Verkhneudinsk). He joined the National Cultural Institution ‚ÄúNational Museum of the Republic of Belarus‚Äù in 2011.

The Nature Museum of Buryatia is part of the Open Museum association, whose main mission is environmental education.

Reviews

The Nature Museum of Buryatia, according to visitors' reviews, leaves a lot of positive emotions. The expositions of the museum fund allow you to better learn about the incredible wealth and nature of the region. Excursions for kids and schoolchildren are especially informative.

The beautiful large layout of Lake Baikal is quite impressive. Indescribable effect is achieved thanks to the original illumination from the inside. It seems that the view of the reservoir opens from space.

The overall rating of most visitors is an old, kind, very informative and interesting museum. Today it is not only a repository of valuable scientific and natural collections, but also one of the centers of environmental education of the younger generation, the promotion of environmental knowledge, as well as the most visited place by tourists and guests of the whole republic.

How to get to the Museum of Nature of Buryatia? Address: st. Lenin, 46. You can get there by public transport or by taxi. The following significant places of the city are located nearby: in 105 meters of the Sibir shopping center, in 171 meters of the Barguzin hotel, 380 meters of Sovetov Square.

A little about some of the natural wonders of the region

In order to educate children and adults of patriotism and love for the motherland, as well as respect for the natural environment, the Ministry of Natural Resources of the Republic held a competition "Seven Natural Wonders of Buryatia." During this event, everyone could vote for a favorite natural object. As a result, a wealth of information was collected about 621 natural sites of esthetic, ecological, historical, cultural and scientific value.

As a result, the inhabitants of the republic chose the most amazing natural wonders.

  1. Mount Barkhan-Uula, located in the Kurumkansky district.
  2. Mount Undar Baabay (territory of Zakamensky district).
  3. The gorge of the Alla River (territory of the Kurumkansky district).
  4. Lakes Slyudyanskie (Severobaikalsky district).
  5. Waterfall on the river. Shumilikha (Severobaikalsky district).
  6. Garginsky thermal springs (Kurumkansky district).
  7. Canyon on the river Jide (Sorgostyn Habsagay), located in Zakamensky district.
