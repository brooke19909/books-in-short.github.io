Hotel Crown Resort Elamaris Hotel 3 * (Protaras, Cyprus): description, rooms and reviews

Cyprus is one of the most popular tourist destinations. After all, this country has something to offer the traveler. There are not only beautiful beaches and comfortable hotel complexes, but also a lot of interesting sights and a unique culture. And the inhabitants of the island are famous for their sincere hospitality.

One of the largest and most famous resorts in the country is the city of Protaras, which is growing every year, while developing tourism infrastructure. Of course, there is no shortage of hotels here - travelers can choose a place to their own liking. And the Crown Resort Elamaris Hotel 3 * managed to earn a good reputation.

Of course, an experienced tourist will try to collect all available information and study the reviews of former guests and only then will book a room. So what does the hotel look like and is it able to offer truly comfortable accommodation?

Location Features





First you need to find out the exact location of the Crown Resort Elamaris 3 * hotel. Protaras, namely the coast of the picturesque bay of Pernera, is here, almost on the seashore, and the hotel complex is located. As the tourists themselves say, this location is very convenient, because the beach is only a few steps away. Moreover, there are shops, taverns and restaurants nearby where you can find everything you want. However, the area is calm, so you can enjoy the silence at night.

Protaras city center is just 3 km away. There is a bus environment near the hotel, and using public transport you can get to almost anywhere in the city or the surrounding area. The nearest international airport is in Larnaca and can be reached in 40 minutes. By the way, the transfer is already usually included in the price of the tour, and it can be arranged at the hotel.

Hotel Crown Resort Elamaris Hotel 3 * (Cyprus, Protaras): Area Information





The hotel complex has three separate four-story buildings, built in a straightforward but elegant style. Not so long ago, major repairs were made on the territory, so the hotel pleases not only with freshness and novelty, but also with a modern interior inside the buildings.

The territory of the Crown Resort Elamaris Hotel 3 * is quite large. In addition to the buildings themselves, there is a large pool with a spacious terrace, as well as its own small garden, playgrounds for sports and recreation. Around everything is decorated with bright flowers and shrubs, and tall palm trees delight the eye. Tourists themselves claim that the atmosphere here is very relaxing.

Living rooms: what living conditions can I expect?





The hotel consists of 171 rooms:

  • 41 Studio - one-room, but spacious rooms with a sleeping and living area, as well as a kitchenette;
  • 130 Apartments - Large rooms with a separate bedroom, a spacious living room and a kitchenette.

The rooms are decorated in a classic style. Each room has a private balcony with relaxing furniture. Naturally, guests are provided with the necessary equipment, including a telephone, a TV (there are several Russian-language channels), individual air conditioning, a safe and a minibar with a cooling system (you can only count on drinking water for free, you have to pay for other drinks). A huge advantage is the private kitchen with a microwave, small fridge, toaster and electric kettle. At your service are also the necessary utensils, tea, coffee and sugar (they are brought daily).

The bathroom will be pleased with the new fixtures and a spacious shower. Every day, maids deliver clean towels, branded shampoos, soap and other hygiene products to the room. Upon request, you can also get a hair dryer.

Dining: restaurants and cafes





Of course, when planning a trip, you need to consider nutrition, because without this it is difficult to imagine a pleasant pastime. The hotel complex Crown Resorts Elamaris Hotel Apts 3 * offers its customers a fairly convenient all-inclusive scheme, which includes three meals a day in the main restaurant, as well as snacks and drinks throughout the day, which can be obtained in one of the three bars. It is worth considering that for imported products (this also applies to strong booze) you need to pay separately.

According to reviews, the restaurant is clean and spacious, and the waiters are polite, and they work professionally. The menu here is not bad - there is always a choice of dishes of local, international and Mediterranean cuisine. The chefs are working in good faith, every day delighting guests with something new. There are side dishes, salads, and an abundance of fresh fruits, as well as several types of meat. Fish and seafood appear on the table regularly.

Bennigan's Grill is a small restaurant serving grilled meats and vegetables, as well as some traditional American dishes. As you can see, no one has problems with nutrition here.

Where is the beach? Conditions for water recreation





What in terms of a beach holiday does the hotel offer guests Crown Resort Elamaris Hotel 3 *? Protaras is a resort that is famous for its coast. The hotel, as already mentioned, is located next to Pernera Bay and the distance to the beach is about 300 meters - a walk will take a few minutes.

The beach here is very beautiful, and under your feet you will always have clean soft sand. The bay has very clear water, and the sea is calm - waves appear extremely rarely. That is why families with children often relax here, because kids love to splash around on the shallows.

This is a public beach, so there are many tourists here almost always. Nevertheless, there is no crowding sensation, since the coastline is long and wide. If desired, for a few euros you can get a sunbed with a mattress and an umbrella. Towels, by the way, can be taken at the hotel for free (but on bail).

Nobody is bored here, since you can practice almost any sport, from beach volleyball and water skiing to boat trips and parasailing. By the way, there is a diving school nearby, where experienced tourists can rent equipment, and beginners are offered to learn the basics of scuba diving.

List of additional services offered

Hotel guests are provided with everything in order to have time to enjoy an unhurried, pleasant vacation. For example, at the reception you can easily exchange currency. Here you can help call a taxi or book tickets.

There is a rental car service - here you can take a car or bicycle for several days. Clothes can be washed and ironed, as these services are inexpensive. And on the territory there is a small shopping center where you can buy household items, hygiene products, fresh fruits, souvenirs and much more.

How to diversify leisure time on vacation?





Despite the fact that most of the time guests spend in the city and on the beach, there is something to brighten up leisure activities on site. It features a large pool with clean water. Next to it is a bar and a sun terrace with many sun loungers.

The gym is constantly working, so you can keep fit even on vacation. You can also play volleyball or tennis (there are large courts and equipment rental). Excursions are another attraction that the Crown Resort Elamaris Hotel 3 * offers. Cyprus is a wonderful country with an incredible amount of interesting sights that are definitely worth seeing.

Vacation with a child: what to count on?





Is the Crown Resort Elamaris Hotel 3 * suitable for families with children? Of course, yes, because the guests are offered all the necessary services. For example, family apartments can easily accommodate a family of 4-5 people. The restaurant has special high chairs and even baby food for very young tourists.

As for entertainment, the kids have fun on the beach. On the territory of the hotel there is a special children's pool, as well as a spacious playground with slides and swings, where the guys play and play sports. Not only a mini-club works, where animators work with children, but also special nurseries, where you can safely leave your baby, as experienced nannies will look after him. And your child will be able to have fun in a special cinema, where they broadcast the most popular cartoons.

Hotel Crown Resort Elamaris Hotel 3 * (Protaras): guest reviews

In order to make a complete picture of the chosen place, it is worth talking with tourists who have already managed to visit it. What do Crown Resort Elamaris Hotel 3 * think about the hotel? Reviews say that this is a really good place with an appropriate level of service. The staff are very nice and welcome guests warmly. Some of them are well spoken in Russian, which is also useful.

The rooms are clean, and the equipment works (although not all rooms have new ones). The windows offer a wonderful view, and soundproofing provides a relaxing sleep. The food in the restaurant is good, no one is left hungry. Perhaps the most striking advantage is the proximity of an incredibly beautiful bay and a spacious beach. And the rest at the Crown Resort Elamaris Hotel 3 * hotel is not so expensive.
