Mexico, Tulum - a paradise on Earth

The country of eternal summer, hot sun and warm sea - this, of course, is Mexico. Tulum is a city located in the east of the Yucatan Peninsula, washed by the waters of the Atlantic Ocean Bay. This is one of those incredibly beautiful places that we all call paradise on Earth. Here, turquoise waves are washed by snow-white sands, and stone rocks meet lush tropical vegetation. There are no modern architectural monuments as such, and only the ancient Mayan tribes left a historical heritage . It is believed that it is these dilapidated buildings that keep secrets on which the whole of cultural Mexico is held.



Tulum is a very small town, on the territory of which there are only hotels, cafes and restaurants, small shops and neighborhoods where aborigines live. There is no airport here, so most often they get here by bus or rented car. You can find out the exact direction of the tracks from local residents or using a GPS-navigator. Traveling around Tulum itself by car is stupid, since the whole city can be walked on foot in half a day.

Rest areas here - this is the main feature of the resort. Tulum beach of the same name is very popular among tourists. Mexico is famous all over the world for its fine white sands, clear sea and natural wealth - and all this was embodied in one city. Despite the fact that the infrastructure here is not as wide as in Cancun, everything you need is always at hand: summer showers, changing rooms, beach toilets and schools where you can rent equipment for water activities and learn all their subtleties.



As you know, Mexico is famous for jewelry, various trinkets and cute little things. Tulum is a place where there is plenty of such good in stores and markets. Indians and European immigrants are trading here, and when buying souvenirs, you need to know how to do it. Be sure to bargain for a product you like and remember that redskins are willing to reduce the price and will be much kinder with you. Mexicans will "throw off" a penny - be prepared for this. Be sure to get a sombrero hat, a Mexican poncho and boots with long pointed socks in the local market.

A diverse and colorful cuisine is distinguished by Mexico. Tulum, despite its small size, is the city in which Italian, Thai, Mexican, Spanish and any other restaurant was built. If you want to eat exclusively local delicacies in this country, then go to the institution "Don Cafetos". It is considered one of the most popular and affordable among tourists, and at the same time, the quality of food is the highest. In the evening, it will be appropriate to spend a local bar or go to a beach party. In this city, amazing tropical cocktails are prepared, which include both alcoholic mixes and harmless coconut and milk necks.



One of the most stunning and unforgettable is the resort of Tulum, Mexico. Photos of this area are presented in the article, so you can now plunge into this summer atmosphere, full of natural colors and warmth.
