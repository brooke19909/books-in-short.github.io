Children's camp "Eaglet" (Wedge): description, important information, reviews

Children need to relax in the fresh air. But what if there is no way to send your favorite child for a vacation to the country or to your grandmother in the village? There is an exit. If you want the child to relax in the suburbs, get a ticket to the Children's Health Camp (DOL) "Eaglet". Klin is an ancient city, in its suburbs there is a comfortable resting place for children.

Important information





The camp is located in the Moscow region, Klinsky district, at the address: st. Shkolnaya, d. No. 36. You can ask all your questions by calling: 8-916-279-32-96 and 8 (499) 253-14-46 (from 10.00 to 17.00).

Not only in the summer the children's camp "Eaglet" (Wedge) works. He is also waiting for children for the autumn and winter holidays.

Already known prices for the summer season of 2015. So, the total cost of one shift is 34 thousand rubles. If the union pays part of the costs to you, the price will naturally be lower.

The first shift will take place on May 30, it will last until June 19. The second shift is waiting for the guys from June 22 to July 12. The next race is organized for the period from July 16 to August 5. And the last, fourth, shift is waiting for children from August 8 to 28.

You can book and buy tickets after April 1. This is done over the Internet. You must go to the camp‚Äôs official website, fill out an application, details and book a place. After that, you need to pay by buying a boarding pass for the camp ticket "Eaglet" (Wedge).

Necessary documents , travel

The collection of departing children will take place at the address: Staropetrovsky passage, house 8, this is the Zenit stadium. To get to it, you need to get to the Voykovskaya metro station, taking the first car from the center. Check-in for the summer shifts of 2015 will be carried out from here. Children with parents are waiting at the stadium at 10.30 in the morning. Accepted in the "Eaglet" (Wedge) children 7-15 years old.

To get to the camp itself by public transport, you need to take an electric train to Klin station, and then take bus number 22 to the Orlyonok stop.

You must have:

  • Medical certificate, form 79-U with a note that there were no contacts with infectious patients. The certificate is valid for no more than five days.
  • Boarding pass.
  • A copy of the medical insurance policy.
  • A copy of the parent's passport (with a residence permit). If the child is 14 years old, then to get to the "Eaglet" (Wedge), he needs to have a copy of his passport with a residence permit. If he is not yet 14 years old, then a birth certificate will be required.

What do parents say about the "Eaglet" (camp)?

Wedge is located in a picturesque place. This is noted by many guys and their parents. Not only the location, but the camp itself for most adults and children leaves a great impression. The average rating for reviews on a five-point system is 4.6. Consider those comments where parents show unflattering reviews about this place of rest.

They begin for the most part with the fact that, in general, dads and mothers got a good impression of the "Eaglet". But someone did not like the fact that the children were forced to attend mugs twice a day - in the morning and after a day's sleep. There is also a small number of games in the fresh air and the fact that the children are not tanned, as they spent a lot of time indoors. So, for example, in the pool during the second shift, children bathed only 2 times. Someone notes that medical care is not up to par. When the children of the younger detachment caught a cold (cough, runny nose), they were not treated.

Parents note that counselors are too young and advise administrations to place more experienced people in younger units.





Positive reviews

Regarding the camp "Eaglet" (Wedge), positive reviews are left by both parents and children. There are many more positive evaluations than negative ones. So, most parents and their children claim that the camp is wonderful. Leisure is rich and interesting: clubs, concerts, and in the evenings cinema, discos.

Parents say that the staff is very attentive. The camp has a small zoo, which is also very popular with children and parents. The children are instilled with a love of animals, instilled in them a sense of responsibility, as children help to care for our smaller brothers.

On parental days, adults who have arrived should not be in the camp, but there is where it is interesting to spend time. Nearby is the McDonald's and, of course, the children call their dads and moms there.

You can stay at the recreation center "Vympel". There is a cafe, you can ride horses. An excursion to the Fir-tree factory will be interesting and informative. Children 11-13 years old are given the opportunity to paint Christmas balls themselves.

On parental day, children will freely chat with their family, go on excursions or relax with adults in the lap of nature. This positive point is also noted in the reviews.

There is a lot of good in the camp. New friends, interesting impressions, the opportunity to attend mugs and have fun are waiting for the children.
