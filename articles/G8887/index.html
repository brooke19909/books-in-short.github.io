Where to invest 200,000 rubles

Deposit account

The first such option is to create a deposit account with a bank. This is the most popular investment method. For those who asked where to invest 200,000 rubles, this method is suitable, despite the fact that this will not be a full-fledged investment. It is rather an auxiliary way to form your airbag for a rainy day. Deposits up to 1,400,000 rubles are insured, and thanks to this, those who think where to invest 200,000 rubles will receive a certain guarantee of security.

However, there are subtleties that must be taken into account. For example, they relate to the choice of bank. So, preference should be given to the largest players in the market. They offer a set of the lowest interest rates. They are hardly likely to be deprived of their license, leaving investors with nothing.

In addition, the method of conducting foreign exchange transactions is popular. It is important to monitor trends and learn to understand this issue.

Among the main advantages of cash deposits to the bank are: guaranteed income, the ability to withdraw money invested at any time, no risk of losing money. At least they come down to a minimum.

But the main disadvantages are the small amount of income, the commission in cases where the account is closed ahead of schedule. Thus, the greatest profit is where there is a maximum share of risk.

Favorable information products - training, video, audio-master classes. They are created once, and then sold in thousands of copies. At the same time, there is competition that can be easily dealt with by highlighting a product for a particular audience segment.

In addition, advertising comes from information resources that are visited by many people every day. Also practiced selling links, placing banners, contextual block, participation in affiliate programs.

Precious metals

Of these types of investment, gold bullion is considered the most popular investment target. They are sold by weight, starting from 1 gram. Metal has a tendency to rise in price over time. In addition, they invest money in bullion coins - collectible, commemorative. They are quite liquid, in most cases they are not subject to VAT. At the same time, the payback period is increased.

A metal score is also practiced. This method of investing consists in buying precious metals at the lowest price and reselling them at the time of closing the account at an increased cost.

The main advantages of such an investment: stability, convenience. While money, under the influence of inflation, is losing value, this process does not apply to precious metals. It is also convenient to collect gold, making purchases every month. Thanks to this, investing 200,000 rubles will be completely safe. Franchise

While creating your own business is considered a time-consuming process, and the amount of 200,000 rubles is unlikely to be sufficient to open it, buying a franchise gives more guarantees for success with less labor.

The acquisition of a franchise imposes certain restrictions on the owner. Franchisees usually work in tight frameworks. There are a lot of franchise offers with an investment of 200,000 rubles in the market. Usually they are associated with online stores, bookmakers, beauty industry.

Among the advantages of this method of investment, they usually list the readiness of an already debugged business model, low entry thresholds, and quick payback periods.

In addition, it must be borne in mind that not all relations between the franchisee and the franchisor are documented. And for this reason, all kinds of pseudo-franchising firms use this, setting huge fines for the fact that the conditions are not met. For this reason, it is important to ensure that the franchise owner includes in the agreement all points that may interest the franchisee. Keep in mind that renting space for a franchise business is more difficult. It will be necessary to take into account the requirements of the franchisor to the premises.
