Pensions Loo (Sochi): addresses, reviews

One of the most attractive regions on the Caucasian coast of the Black Sea is Loo. This small resort village located in the Lazarevskoye area is only eighteen kilometers away from Sochi.



Rest in Loo

Pensions, hotels, private sector, sanatoriums - here everyone can choose for themselves the most optimal type of accommodation. Stretched along the seashore, the resort village offers guests a rest in an atmosphere of calm and warmth. Many say that in Loo time flows differently. Leaving turmoil and turmoil somewhere far away, tourists can enjoy the fresh mountain air and the amazing sea.

Even at the height of the hot summer season, the air here is refreshingly cool. And in combination with sea water, it has such a beneficial effect on the human body that it allows vacationers to get rid of many diseases. Many people come here in the direction of doctors. Experts recommend sunbathing and sea water for the prevention of diseases of the skin and nervous system, to regulate metabolism.





Hotels

Loo hotels offer their guests a wide range of services. For example, cafes almost always function on their territory, there are gazebos with barbecue facilities, a bathhouse or a sauna with a swimming pool, necessarily secured parking and much more.

The staff of private hotels often organizes excursions for their clients to interesting places in the Greater Sochi area by personal car. The traditional southern hospitality combined with the atmosphere of homeliness, attentive service and, most importantly, loyal prices will pleasantly surprise everyone, making your vacation simply unforgettable. For example, in large hotels such as Kiparisovaya Usadba Hotel or Daniel, the cost of one night in a double room starts at 1400 rubles.
