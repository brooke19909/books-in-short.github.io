The hotel complex "Ambassador" (Pattaya) - the best budget vacation option in Thailand

Pattaya is a resort city in Thailand, located on the shores of the Gulf of Thailand. There are many entertainment places. All the sandy coast of the resort is occupied by hotels, among which the largest hotel complex Ambassador Jomtien City 4 *. This hotel is very popular among tourists from CIS countries. You can get to the city from this place by taxi or by local transport - tuk-tuk.





Description: In the remote area of ​​Jomtien, on the seashore, there is a four-star hotel complex "Ambassador" (Pattaya). Five hotel buildings were erected in 1988 and occupy an area of ​​40 hectares. The hotel itself is 15 km from the central part of the city, 148 km from Suvanabum International Airport. The institution resembles a small town in which the infrastructure for a complete and comfortable stay is well developed. From the windows of all the buildings of the Ambassador City (Pattaya), a beautiful panorama of the mountains, the garden and the sea coast opens.

The main, 19-story building - “Ocean Wing 4 *” - provides 1099 rooms.

The second, 42-story building - “Marina Tower 3 *” - is represented by 1660 rooms.

The third, 6-story building - "Garden Wing 3 *" - has 342 rooms.

The fourth, 4-storey - “Inn Wing 2 *” - has 915 rooms.

The fifth, 7-story building - "Ambassador Wing 4 *" - designed for 185 rooms.





Rooms: All the buildings of the Ambassador Hotel (Pattaya) have rooms of various categories: standard rooms, economy version, superior, deluxe rooms, family rooms, mini suites, exclusive rooms. They are designed for living both together, and three, four, four. The rooms are equipped with air conditioning, hairdryer, shower and bath, flat-screen TV, telephone, small bar. The rooms have satellite and mobile communications. Superior rooms include tea and coffee sets, bathrobes and slippers, and a bathtub (jacuzzi). The housing is non-smoking. Balconies are not in all rooms. All rooms are serviced around the clock.





Meals: Dining at the Ambassador Hotel (Pattaya) is carried out in three restaurants of the BB or HB type: Hong (Chinese), Pasta (Italian), Tokugawa (Japanese). For tourists there is a buffet system or a choice of dishes on the menu. There are several cafes throughout the day - Atrium, Espresso Coffee, Espresso Terrace & Oyster Bar, Ocean Karaoke. They can enjoy drinks, snacks, desserts, relax and listen to music.

Beach: Ambassador Hotel (Pattaya) has its own sandy beach, which is 100 meters from the buildings. Free parasols, beach towels and mattresses are provided there.

Information for holidaymakers: The hotel has a gym, sauna, SPA center, currency exchange, tennis courts, laundry, shopping center, playground for children, own fruit market. Near four large pools for adults and two children installed water slides. For trips to the city, a bus is organized that runs on schedule. The beach offers the opportunity to engage in water sports.





Reviews: In general, the Ambassador hotel reviews are positive. For those who love silence and solitude, this place is a great option. Many vacationers noted the quality service of the Russian-speaking staff, daily cleaning of the rooms, varied food, and the provision of numerous sightseeing tours.
