The story of creativity and love Carrie Otis

This Hollywood actress and sultry model with a bright appearance gained incredible popularity after the erotic melodrama Wild Orchid was released on a wide screen, and her real romance with the main character of the film - Mickey Rourke - became not only scandalous, but also deadly for the star.

Modeling career

Carrie Otis, who shone on the covers of the most fashionable magazines and starred in recognizable advertising campaigns, was born in 1968 in an extremely dysfunctional family. Constantly watching parents drinking, the girl at the age of 10 tasted alcohol. It is not known how her fate would develop in the future, but the attractive Carrie, constantly dreaming of a modeling career, is leaving for New York to try her luck.



For her, a whole new streak in life sets in: a charming brunette became interested in agencies, immediately offering her a job in glossy publications with millions of copies. Expressive staged photographs in Elle magazine instantly bring her popularity, followed by contracts with the brands Calvin Klein and Guess, and the hot photo shoot in Playboy makes her not only famous, but also the most coveted woman in America.

Wild Orchid and Love

Carrie Otis, driving crazy men with an exotic appearance, realizes that the modeling career is short-lived, and begins to look for new ways to earn money and success. Having learned about the ongoing casting for the role of young Emily in the movie "Wild Orchid", she decides to try her hand. The beauty with a beautiful figure is noticed by Mickey Rourke, who chooses a film partner with the director Zalman King.



They say that on the samples between them immediately established a special relationship. The famous and vicious film actor falls in love at first sight with a woman who in an amazing way combined experience and spontaneity. Otis, who knows how to manage men, is seriously interested in Hollywood celebrity.

Failures in the movie and modeling business

The erotic film, which has received scandalous fame for the abundance of explicit scenes, does not become a springboard for a model dreaming of new heights. Carrie Otis, whose filmography was not replenished with the main roles, decides to leave the film business, realizing that she will not reach any heights in Hollywood. She tries herself on television, but new projects do not interest the viewer and fail. After disappointments in the film and television business, the modeling career is also declining.

The charming Carrie Otis, whose photo was printed by all the glamorous publications, is gradually starting to lose her model appearance. The started health problems always lead the girl with appetizing forms to anorexia. Wanting to lose weight to madness, the model brings herself to mental illness and complete exhaustion, after which the girl desperately begins to fight for her life.

Victory over oneself

Overcoming a terrible ailment, Otis is gaining the hated weight with which she fought so fiercely so long before. But I must say that this even benefited the model: Marina Rinaldi notes it and offers to work as a plus size model . Carrie Otis, whose biography has dozens of covers in the most famous glossy magazines, is once again gaining her much-needed popularity.

A history of destructive passion

The stormy romance that began on the set of Wild Orchid continued after the release of the picture. In 1992, the couple registered a marriage that lasted only 6 years. The model often comes to work wearing sunglasses, because she is ashamed to show her bruises that her newly-made husband leaves her almost daily. An agency that worked with a promising fashion model, without regrets, is breaking the contract with her: no one needs a battered cover girl who often ends up in the hospital.





Mickey Rourke and Carrie Otis, whose public life was watched by millions of viewers, realized that this marriage was destroying them from the inside. The frantically jealous actor behaved like a real animal: he broke her jaw and beat so hard that the ambulance brigade, once called by neighbors, found numerous fractures in the woman.

Revelation star
