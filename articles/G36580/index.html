Features of Pattaya and Vang Amat Beach

There are two common proverbs regarding this Thai resort . First: "Good people go to heaven, and depraved people go to Pattaya." The resort is considered the capital of the Thai sex industry. The second proverb says: "Going to Pattaya by sea is like going to Paris to swim in the Seine." It is understood that the resort has so many temptations that there simply will not be time for a beach vacation. And the city promenades of Pattaya and the coastal water area are not very clean. For clean sand and turquoise water, tourists travel to nearby islands or in the vicinity of the resort. One of the beaches that is appreciated for the harmony between well-groomed and developed infrastructure is Vang Amat. Also called the area in the northern part of Pattaya. In this respectable and quiet part of the resort is a huge hotel complex Centara Grand Mirage Beach Resort Pattaya 5 *. Exactly ten minutes to the rampant life that boils day and night on Woking Street (Pattaya's main street). At the same time, you can enjoy the tranquility, silence and comfort in the hotel itself. It is good for family people and for youth.



Hotel Territory

Resort Centara Grand Mirage Beach Resort Pattaya is a two eighteen-story tower, interconnected by the largest water park in Pattaya. The whole territory is designed in the style of a lost paradise valley, untouched by civilization. Numerous pools are connected by bridges, sometimes suspended. Tourists talk about waterfalls, cascades, lazily flowing river. Due to the specifics of the hotel, families with children choose it. Moreover, the accommodation of small tourists in the hotel is free. Reviews say that the territory of the complex is so large that it is just right to get lost. Everywhere greenery, well-groomed paths, which carefully give the appearance of paths in the jungle. Two buildings of the hotel are built in such a way that all rooms in them have a view of the Gulf of Thailand. They are most popular among tourists.



Excursions

If you came to Thailand for a week, you are unlikely to have time to get out of the water entertainment town, which is located in the Centara Grand Mirage Beach Resort Pattaya. A photo of this water park is one of Pattaya's business cards. In the evening, after putting the children to bed, you can go to the center, watch a transvestite show, go-go-show program or just have a dark Guinness in one of the airish pubs. Are there any interesting places in Pattaya not related to the sex industry? Of course! Tourists recommend visiting a crocodile farm or a zoo with tigers Si-Racha. Children will be interested in elephant trekking in the jungle. From sea excursions, tourists recommend a trip to the coral island of Koh Lan. Those who are not alien to Asian spirituality will be able to improve their karma at Wat Yan Temple. Near Pattaya there are several lakes where you can fish for catfish.

How to feed at the hotel and where else they go to eat

Centara Grand Mirage Beach Resort Pattaya does not practice an all-inclusive system. All reviews mention only breakfasts, which, according to tourists, are very satisfying and varied. Besides the fact that nobody from the Oasis restaurant went hungry, everyone was able to choose the right dishes for themselves. Fans of Asian cuisine, fans of traditional food, and parents who fed their children cereals and pancakes were satisfied. The hotel also has other restaurants. These are Ginger and Lime (Asian cuisine), Aqua (seafood) and Flames, specializing in grill and barbecue. Most tourists prefer to have lunch and dinner at the "macaques" on the beach, or in a cafe with a buffet outside the hotel. But many visited the bars of the hotel, of which several (including the pools) were satisfied. Near the hotel there is a Russian restaurant "Teremok". The local cuisine is not sharp and more familiar to our tourist.



Water park and beach

The murmur of water will accompany you all your vacation at Centara Grand Mirage Beach Resort Pattaya 5 *. The water park of this hotel consists of many pools in the form of fabulous lagoons. A river flows throughout the hotel complex. It can be fused on rubber rings. There are many water slides, both for extreme sportsmen and for a more cautious audience. What pleased the tourists was that for very young children special rides were also equipped. The shallow pool has sand off the coast. There, kids can mess with buckets and shovels, if, for example, the sea is stormy. Compared to the city beach of Pattaya, Wang Amat looks simply gorgeous. But, reviews warn, you should always carry a room key. The entrance to the hotel from the beach is protected from the public, who also would like to use the water park for free, like hotel guests.



Service and Facilities

The 5-star Centara Grand Mirage Beach Resort Pattaya features a fully equipped spa. Thai massage can be tested on yourself and on the beach. For those who cannot live without sports for a day, the hotel has a fitness room, tennis courts, billiards tables, a volleyball court and a climbing rock. Three times a day, the hotel provides a free shuttle service to the Central Festival Shopping Mall and the Lifestyle complex. In the evenings, restaurants have theme nights with shows. The bars play live music, you can sing with karaoke. The hotel is positioning itself as "gay friendly." At the reception, which is decorated like a wall of living plants, a girl works who perfectly understands the Russian language.

For children

The hotel complex Centara Grand Mirage Beach Resort Pattaya reviews are called the ideal place for a family holiday. For children in the restaurant you can always find the right dishes. For an additional fee, a child from four to nine years old can be enrolled in a mini-club, and a student from eleven to fifteen can be enrolled in a teenage club, where Russian-speaking animators will study with them. For a very small child there is an opportunity to pay for babysitting services. Many tourists note that for families with children they allocate large rooms with a separate bedroom for small guests, in which there are many toys.
