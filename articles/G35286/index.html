Projector for iphone brings reality to life

Iphone is more than just a smartphone. Having become its user once, you can hardly switch to using other devices. An incredibly convenient interface, the accessibility of all the necessary functions, a unique style and design make using it a pleasure. Every year, products from the apple brand are overgrown with new options and opportunities. Modern technologies, in particular the projector for iphone, allow you to transfer multimedia from the screen of your phone to a flat surface and see a picture of the highest quality. Today, there are two approaches to using mini projectors with smartphones. In the first case, the manufacturer embeds the projector in the device, along this path Samsung went with its Galaxy Beam model. In the second case, projectors are made by manufacturers of accessories, for example, Pocket Projector. It, like MobileCinema, has built-in batteries for charging an apple smartphone, but it costs more. The device is fully compatible with the fourth version of iOS and higher. The projector for iphone also works with Apple tablets.




Home cinema the size of a mobile phone

The very first mini-projectors were presented in 2007. After six years, they began to catch up with home cinemas in popularity. Now only moviegoers in an apartment or house install bulky and expensive devices for watching movies. Other consumers prefer to buy a projector for the iphone, which does not exceed the size of a regular mobile phone, and enjoy excellent image quality. The most attractive models have 4 GB of memory and a special slot designed for expansion cards.

Compact and simple, they are ideal both in business use and in domestic use. If your task is to present a bewitching presentation in a short time, then the portable projector will be simply irreplaceable. Sleek and slim design allows you to place the gadget even in a compact laptop bag. The device is designed to demonstrate any images, as well as films on more than 2-meter screen. In parallel with watching videos, a mobile projector allows you to listen to audio or use the Internet.


Mini projectors against LCD ?

I must say that thanks to these mini-devices, the tradition of playing video on screens can sink into oblivion. And manufacturers in the future may have to reconsider the size of the displays of smartphones, since large screens may no longer be needed due to the widespread introduction of projectors. They will allow you to project the image on any flat surface. The projector for iphone today is already indispensable for those who lead an active lifestyle, travel a lot at work, often hold presentations and are not able to install bulky equipment every time. Today, a mini projector can be purchased at a regular point of sale. For most consumers, for household use, it is enough to have a miniature model with a diagonal of 2-3 meters. But there are those who value technological excellence. They will be interested in the expensive Full HD, as well as 3D devices that will soon completely displace plasma and LCD panels.
