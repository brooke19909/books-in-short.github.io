Relax at Inside Hotel 4: reviews

Many people visited Turkey as tourists. The splendor of beaches, beautiful landscapes, excellent weather, the entire beach season, highly organized hotel and sightseeing holidays, many historical cultural monuments, quality shopping - all this attracts people from all over the world to a sunny and welcoming country. Holidays at the Inside Hotel 4 (reviews about which future vacationers read with interest) are remembered for a long time by everyone who wanted to relax well and inexpensively - and fulfilled the dream.

If you start the story about the Inside Hotel 4 stars, you should first mention the resort and the place where this hotel is located. Side is a small town on the Turkish Riviera, which today has become a major international tourist center.

Side story

This ancient city, located almost 80 km east of Antalya, was the site of secret meetings between Cleopatra and Anthony, and later - the border fort.

The name of the city - Side - is translated as "pomegranate." And he really is immersed in the greenery of the endless gardens where this beautiful fruit is grown. Side is the pomegranate capital of Mediterranean Turkey. It was founded in the 7th century BC and was used as a convenient outpost for defense against Arab invasions. Over time, Side was turned into a center for the sale of slaves and looted property. After the fall of the Roman Empire, it became a pirate center. Then survived the collapse and fall. And only at the beginning of the XX century, thanks to the efforts of Muslims who fled from the island of Crete, Side turned into that beautiful place that vacationers are used to seeing. Often, the name of the resort refers to all the surrounding land from Manavgat to Kumkeya.

Modern resort

At the present stage of development of the travel industry in Turkey, Side is a tourist center with a developed hotel infrastructure, of which Inside Hotel 4 occupies a special place.

It is the huge tourism revenues that have saved many of the country's historical attractions. Now Side is not only a resort, but also an interesting archaeological monument of the history of Turkey.

Side Attractions

Relaxing in the Inside Hotel 4 (reviews focus on this aspect), you can visit the ruins of the Temple of Fortune, Roman baths, the Vespasian fountain, and the ancient theater. And lovers of nightlife and vibrant entertainment will be able to visit many discos, nightclubs and dance floors located in the area and become the hallmark of Side.

Pine forests approaching the city, the fantastic architecture of the buildings, the lake with clean fresh water captivate. A warm sea and huge sandy beaches will satisfy lovers of swimming and soaking under the southern sun.

Hotel Inside Hotel 4

And among all this riot of colors and nature is Inside Hotel 4 (Side). Responses of vacationers in an ecologically favorable region are positive in this regard.

Firstly, the location of the Inside Hotel 4 is ideal for sightseeing and beach holidays. It is located in the green zone, one kilometer from the city of Side, close to its own beach and at a distance of 500 meters from the sea. The building was built in 2004, and in 2014 was overhauled, and based on the conclusion of the expert commission, the hotel was assigned the category of 4 stars.
