Heviz - thermal water lake

Currently, people on our planet like to relax in different countries. One of the most interesting places is Hungary. The city of Heviz, the lake near which has a similar name, attracts the attention of tourists, because it combines both treatment and entertainment. How is this name translated into Russian? "Heviz" in Russian means "hot water". Year-round the lake pleases visitors with warm water. In winter, the temperature reaches 26-27 degrees, in the summer it rises to 34, so the popular resort welcomes guests at all times of the year. Strong winter evaporation turns the surface of the water into a large inhaler. Highly concentrated steam forms a thick fog over the water.

It seems like you are in a fairy tale. Bathing in warm water and contemplating a shore shrouded in snow, you are struck by a natural mystery.

Why is Heviz (lake) so attractive? The fact that its water belongs to the radioactive thermal waters of high temperature includes a number of useful micro and macro elements. Near it there are many hotels, resorts, private hotels. To attract tourists, festivals of wine, beer, operetta evenings, street masquerades and balls, ensembles performances are held in the city of the same name. Coming for a vacation to Heviz - a lake with thermal springs - you can also improve your health: bathing in it successfully helps with many diseases of the musculoskeletal system (arthritis, arthrosis, osteochondrosis, post-traumatic period), gynecology problems, kidney diseases, and nervous system disorders .



Heviz is a lake unique in its chemical composition. An interesting factor is the presence of a multimeter treatment layer of sludge. Mineral springs can be used for drinking and treating diseases of the gastrointestinal tract. The sanatorium voucher is designed for 21 days or for 1-2 weeks. It should be remembered that you can not immediately be in the pond for more than 30 minutes, since thermal water with a longer use increases the load on the heart, can disrupt the circulatory system.

When planning your vacation on Lake Heviz, you should remember that after swimming, you should definitely relax in it, do not wipe your body. It is forbidden to use sunblock. For children, a pool with ordinary water is created here. There are a number of contraindications for hydrotherapy: some severe forms of diseases of the vascular system and heart, asthma, pregnancy, malignant neoplasms.



Water procedures are used both for treatment and for prevention and relaxation. Heviz Lake, the prices for which are quite affordable, are pleasant for guests. Here they will be offered a large selection of saunas, baths, massage and beauty courses, healthy nutrition.

The town itself is interesting and cultural entertainment. Nearby are the agromuseum and the Puppet Museum, the museums of Marzipan and Balaton, the castle of counts, various attractions. There is a huge water park 20 kilometers from Heviz, 10 kilometers is an excellent source. The peculiarity of Hungarian water parks is that they do not heat up artificially, but use the heat of the earth, because their work is based on mineral thermal water. Near Heviz is a forest growing on a mountain.
