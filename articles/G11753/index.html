How to get there

In conclusion, we will guide you how to get to each of the three recreation areas presented:

  • To the village of Komarovo:
    • Minibuses: from Prospect Enlightenment - No. 680, from Finland Station - No. 400, from the Old Village - No. 305.
    • Bus: from Black River - No. 211.
    • Train: from the Udelnaya metro station (approximately 40 minutes of travel).
  • To the High Drive beach - you need to focus on the address: Primorskoye highway, 481a.
  • To the recreation area on Shchuchye Lake: exit at the Komarovo railway station and move from it directly to the pine forest. The reference point will be Ozernaya Street - you must follow the path along it.
