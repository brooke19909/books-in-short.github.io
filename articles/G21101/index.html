Kalyazin hotels: description, prices, reviews

The observatory, the city government building, the Ascension Church, the museum of local lore - these, perhaps, are all the sights of Kalyazin. The provincial city is located 190 km from the capital, and its main symbol is the bell tower of St. Nicholas Cathedral, which is located on a small island.

In the 40s, according to the project, a decision was made to artificially expand the Volga to create hydroelectric power stations in its water area. However, due to errors, the old part of Kalyazin was flooded. The St. Nicholas Cathedral was dismantled, and they decided to leave the bell tower as a lighthouse. Around the building they created a small island and even built a pier.





Today, travelers come to see the legendary bell tower, which is associated with many urban legends. Equally popular is the Baba Yaga museum-estate, where the heroine of Russian fairy tales greets guests with jokes and jokes. After a short excursion, the old woman serves tea with cakes on a cozy open veranda.

Exploring all the sights will not take you more than three or four hours, but if you want to enjoy the peace and measured provincial life, then at your service are Kalyazin hotels located within the city and its environs.
