Paradise of the Maldives - a place that is definitely worth a visit

Among the paradise resorts that have worldwide fame, perhaps, you can not fail to name the Maldives. It is on these lands and atolls that everyone who prefers the summer hot sun and warm ocean waters prefers to relax. On the territory of the Maldives there are a number of blue lagoons, snow-white sandy beaches and unique plants. But besides all these natural features, there are many other advantages, which are simply unrealistic to let go of your own.



As a rule, guests of the Maldives are lovers of a typical paradise holiday. That is why on these lands there are a huge number of hotels with high service, where every vacationer will be able to completely relax and not think about their domestic everyday problems. Every tourist who wants to visit this paradise should know that the religion of the Maldives is Islam. First of all, this means that the import and export of alcohol into the resort is strictly prohibited. Locals are very respectful of their religion. Literally on every island you can see the mosque of extraordinary beauty. Visiting some of them is free, but there are those that are closed from the eyes of visitors.



If you are a regular visitor to this paradise on the planet, then your permanent attribute should be a map of the Maldives. There are more than 70 of them in the republic, therefore, if you decide to move a little while on vacation, and not just sunbathe on the beach, then you should arm yourself with assistants in the form of a location scheme or GPS. In most cases, transportation from one resort to another is carried out by boat, accompanied by local residents. During such a walk, by the way, you can learn a lot of interesting facts and news from your water riders, which further diversifies the vacation.

Basically, all the resorts of the Maldives are tiny atolls that are surrounded on all sides by the turquoise waters of the Indian Ocean. It is impossible to distinguish among them the best or the worst, for they are all good in their own way. Listing only the most popular that have gained fame for their excellent service, we can mention Ari Atoll, Daalu Atoll, Nilande Atoll, South Male Atoll and Laviani Atoll. In these areas, the maximum number of five-star hotels, spas, shops and other infrastructure is concentrated.
