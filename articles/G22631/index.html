Hotel infrastructure

On the territory of the hotel Petunya Beach Resort 4 * there are 2 outdoor pools, 2 slides on the water. There is a television room in the lobby. Also at the disposal of tourists - a shopping center and currency exchange. There is an internet cafe. On a paid basis, you can use Wi-Fi, laundry, hairdresser, doctor. It is possible to rent a chair for people with disabilities, also on a fee basis. Subject to a deposit, guests can play tennis on the special court. There is an ATM at the hotel. But it is also possible to pay for services and goods with cards of popular international systems. There are services for photocopying documents, as well as sending them by fax. There is a gift shop.





The infrastructure of the hotel complex is quite typical for Mediterranean resorts of this level. The set of paid services and those that do not require additional compensation corresponds to offers from competing hotels, and not only in Turkey, but also in other macroregion countries popular among Russian tourists.

Entertainment

How to entertain a traveler staying at Petunya Beach Resort 4 *? There are many ways. Tourists are entertained by animators. For guests there is a sauna, a Turkish bath, a gym, there is an opportunity to rent tennis equipment - large on the court and table, darts, indoor soccer, billiards, basketball. Paid services include: SPA, riding a sea banana, boating, parachuting over the sea, surfing, sailing, canoeing, water skiing and a motorcycle, as well as a catamaran. Car rental available.

The range of infrastructure facilities for entertainment is quite wide. In this sense, the hotel complex is probably making efforts to satisfy the needs of tourists of different ages and preferences in spending free time.

There are places to spend time also for the little guests of the hotel. A special small-sized pool, a mini-club working in the daytime, and a playground are open for children. A baby cot is provided free of charge, a stroller for an additional fee. You can also hire a babysitter.





As we noted above, the village of Ortakent, in which the hotel complex is located, predisposes to a calm measured rest. If a person wants to have fun in a bar or in a disco, you will need to go to Bodrum or another city. But if we are talking about a relatively quiet vacation, not violating the general concept of organizing pastimes at the resort, then the hotel has establishments in which it is quite possible to get together as a friendly company.

Nutrition

Meals for guests are organized according to the All Inclusive system. This is breakfast, lunch and dinner in buffet style , the provision of snacks in bars, drinks from local manufacturers. The largest restaurant in the hotel complex can accommodate 450 people. The cuisine at the hotel is presented both with local dishes and international culinary products.

Regarding the quality and freshness of the products, there are no significant complaints in the reviews of tourists. A similar service is organized for visitors to the pool. Not all Turkish hotels are ready to offer guests preferences, similar to those provided by Petunya Beach Resort 4 *. Sights of Bodrum

So, the nearest city relative to Petunya Beach Resort 4 * is Bodrum. What attractions can a tourist visit in it? Bodrum is located on the peninsula of the same name in the southwestern part of Turkey. The location of the city is between the Aegean Sea and the Mediterranean. For many guests, Petunya Beach Resort 4 * Bodrum (reviews of tourists confirm this) has become a unique place of familiarization with ancient history. Bodrum is over two thousand years old.

The most famous cultural sights of the city are St. Peter's Castle, Hellenistic Theater, Museum of Underwater Archeology, Mindos Gate, Black Ostrov, famous for its hot springs, Halicarnassus Mausoleum.

Having visited places connected with ancient and modern culture, a tourist can devote time to relaxing on the beautiful beaches of any type - sandy or pebble. The water in the Bodrum area is especially clear. Among the most famous sights of the city are picturesque bays. Interesting is Gokova Bay, located in the southern part of Bodrum. The fact is that its waters are able to change color. It can range from dark blue shades to light green.

Walking in Bodrum is not the only possible way to spend time at the Petunya Beach Resort 4 *. Turkey, Bodrum are hospitable and cultural. One of the most popular souvenirs among tourists who have visited these areas is a sea sponge. It can be bought in the area of ‚Äã‚ÄãAtaturk Avenue and other places in Bodrum. Souvenirs related to the traditional Turkish way of life can be bought in the village of Chokmakdag, which is located near Bodrum. Among the local goods that are popular with tourists are silk carpets.

Bodrum is a powerful transportation hub. From this city you can get to almost any part of the country. For example, you can drive to Izmir in 4 hours, and to Ankara, the capital of Turkey, in 10 hours. You can also get to Istanbul in a reasonable time, it will take about 13 hours. Many tourists prefer to settle in Bodrum precisely because of the fact that it is one of the key transport hubs in Turkey.
