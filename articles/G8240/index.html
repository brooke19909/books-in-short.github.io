Vincci Nozha Beach 4 * (Tunisia, Hammamet) - hotel reviews, photos

Vincci Nozha Beach 4 * is a beach hotel in the famous Tunisian resort of Hammamet, although it is quite far from the city of the same name. It belongs to the Vinci line, which has a good reputation. The hotel is intended for vacationers in the resort, and therefore stands directly on the sandy shore.

Vincci Nozha Beach 4 * (hotel reviews are the subject of our article) is very popular among European tourists, but our compatriots also often visit it. Let's take a look at what grade they give to this extremely stylish and fun Four.





Location

Hotel Vincci Nozha Beach 4 * is located between two famous resort centers - Hammamet and Nabeul. However, it cannot be said that this complex stands in the desert, far from any civilization. The place here is resort-oriented for vacationers. There is a promenade, you can walk very far along it. Near the hotel there are a lot of different cafes and shops, and there is absolutely no need to go anywhere to the markets if you want to buy something or have a snack. A kilometer from the hotel is a good water park with slides, trampolines and other interesting entertainments.

What is hammamet

This is one of the oldest and most tranquil resorts in the country. The city in which Vincci Nozha Beach 4 * is located - Hammamet has an excellent reputation. Couples with children come here to bathe their kids in the sea and not worry about their safety, and those who like to dance at night discos. Many tourists improve their health here. Well, in order not to be bored, they visit the sights of Hammamet, since there are quite a lot of them. For example, the old city, port and the new tourist center Yasmin. In the latter there are not only various cafes, restaurants and retail outlets, but also an open-air museum, which presents brilliant examples of ancient Arabian architecture, surrounded by a fortress wall. Inside is a traditional souvenir market. Children will love the Cartageland Amusement Park, which is nearby.



How to get here

The distance to the Tunisian international airport "Carthage" from the hotel Vincci Nozha Beach Spa 4 * - sixty-five kilometers. It takes about an hour to get from him. Many come here by transfer from the airport "Enfide" (the nearest). Before him about fifty kilometers. If the price of your ticket does not include a transfer, do not worry. A lot of buses that run to Hammamet and Nabeul make stops at almost all hotels. Not to mention the "piles" - local minibuses.



Infrastructure

Hotel Vincci Nozha Beach 4 * is one large white building, standing on the shores of Hammamet Bay. The building is long and is located in a semicircle near a large pool. The hotel has three elevators. On the territory there is even a small zoo. There is a boutique, a beauty salon, a fitness center with a gym and a night club.



For active holidaymakers there are tennis courts and a volleyball court. The territory itself is small, but very comfortable, cozy and green. Everywhere flowers and palm trees. Hookah is popular. Free Wi-Fi is available in the lobby, but guests are advised to use it mainly in the morning and before lunch, because the service is busy in the evenings. On Tuesdays, the local market arrives directly at the hotel, where you can buy good things, and not even very expensive if you can bargain.

Accommodation and rooms

The hotel Vincci Nozha Beach 4 * can accommodate about eight hundred people at a time. There are four hundred rooms for one, two, three and four guests. There are rooms designed for entire companies or families, which can accommodate five and six. Most of the rooms, according to tourists, see the sea. From the windows of the rest, beautiful park landscapes open. The rooms are small but clean and well maintained, recently renovated. The furniture is solid, the beds are large, and the furnishings are standard for the Tunisian Quartet. In the rooms - slippers for guests. There is a Russian channel on television. The floor in the room is tiled. Plumbing is new. Everything works well, there are no complaints about breakdowns. There is a dryer on the balcony, albeit a small one.

The room has a safe and refrigerator, but they are paid. Cleaned every day, and not bad. The floors are washed not only in the room, but also on the balcony. Bed linen is changed often, towels - four times a week. In the bathroom you can see shower gel, soap, shampoo and body cream. If you come with a small child under two years old, then it is free of charge in an extra bed.

SPA, therapy and procedures

As in any Tunisian Hammamet hotel, this hotel also has a wellness center. No wonder its name is Vincci Nozha Beach Spa 4 *. The spa in the hotel, according to many visitors, is a real Mediterranean paradise. It offers balneotherapy, and various massages, and body wraps, and a jacuzzi, and a hammam. A good beautician gives advice and makes face masks. And for those who wish to sunbathe beautifully and at the same time improve their health, vacationers especially recommend peeling after a hammam.

Intensive skin rejuvenation procedures are also popular. Thalassotherapy is not here. Therefore, in the pools the water is not sea, but ordinary, but the procedures are similar and carried out at a very decent level.

Sea and water vacations

The beach at the hotel is simply amazing, according to the reviews of tourists. The entrance to the sea is gentle, which is very good for children. The water is clear, clean and warm, with virtually no algae and jellyfish. The sand is snow-white, velvet and soft, like a feather-bed. The spectacle is somewhat reminiscent of a Bounty ad. All people have enough sunbeds, no one takes them. Catamaran, kayaks - all this is free. Beach towels - on security. The hotel Vincci Nozha Beach 4 * (Hammamet) also has three pools. One of them is under the roof and heated. It is used in cold weather. The second is in the air. This is the main pool and it has three water slides. And the third is designed for children and is equipped in a special way. Near the pools there are a lot of sunbeds on the lawns.

Excursions

Vincci Nozha Beach 4 * offers great opportunities to travel. Photos of dozens of excursions are posted on the network as illustrations for reviews. From the hotel you can take walks, ride camels and horses. Families with children are recommended to go to the water park - it's about fifteen minutes on foot. And the city of entertainment Cartageland is also good. Only they are not advised to go on weekends - there are too many people. To Hammamet, in particular, to its old center, take a taxi for five minutes. There you can walk around the city, take pictures, go to the fortress. There are very cheap products in government stores - olive oil, coffee, dates. If you want exotic, you can order a two-day excursion to the Sahara desert at the hotel . And if you like ancient sights, then welcome to Carthage! By the way, a big excursion to the Sahara includes trips to historical places and ancient ruins, visits to the mountains, a Barbary village and an oasis with a waterfall.

Is it expensive to relax here

The price for a double room at this Vinci hotel starts at sixty-seventy dinars per day for two. The hotel is considered a budget for Tunisia, it offers very good services for this price. There are several paid services that are not included in the price. For example, to use a safe and a refrigerator, you need to pay extra two and seven dinars per day, respectively. To smoke a hookah in a hotel, you will need to lay out eight units of local currency. But tourists recommend walking a distance of two hundred meters from the hotel. There will be a local hookah, where this pleasure costs half as much. Forty dinars cost to fly over the sea by parachute, eighty to go to Carthage. A bottle of wine and lobster in the old center of Hammamet will cost gourmets one and a half thousand Russian rubles (approximately).
