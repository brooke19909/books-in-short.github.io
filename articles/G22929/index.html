Hotel IKON Phuket 4 * (Phuket / Karon, Thailand): photos and reviews of tourists

If you want to spend your vacation in a budget enough but stylish hotel with comfortable and modern rooms, then you should go to Hotel IKON Phuket 4 *. This complex is located in one of the most popular provinces in Thailand, and many people come here to relax.





Briefly about the hotel

Hotel IKON Phuket 4 * is located in Phuket. This is the western province of Thailand, which is one of the most visited resorts here. What is a good hotel? Firstly, it is very close to the most famous beach, which is Karon Beach. Its length is five kilometers. The highlight of the beach is its sand with a high content of quartz. Therefore, when holidaymakers walk along Karon Beach, they feel like everything "creaks" underfoot.

So, the beach can be reached in about 10 minutes. The hotel is located on the slope of a small hill, so visitors to vacationers, in fact, can only go down. The airport is far away - at a distance of 45 kilometers from the hotel. But on the other hand, within walking distance are such famous sights as Suwan Khiri Ket Temple, Karon Night Market, Plaza Kata Shopping Center and mini-golf called Dino Park.

What is in the hotel?

Hotel IKON Phuket 4 * has all the amenities necessary for a good rest. The reception here works 24/7, there is a left-luggage office, a laundry with dry cleaning, a tour desk and a concierge that provides services. Free public parking is available next to the hotel. Wi-Fi throughout the entire complex is free.

If we take into account the concept of the hotel, then we can confidently say that Hotel IKON Phuket 4 * is a boutique hotel. Everything here is aimed at comfort and a relaxing holiday for guests. There is no animation, evening shows or other entertainment that people are used to when they travel to resort hotels. But there are exquisitely decorated rooms, polite staff, an excellent gym with modern fitness equipment and even a SPA salon (where you can try various types of massages - from Thai to stone therapy). There is also an outdoor pool.

Helpful information
