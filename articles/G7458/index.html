Sanatorium "Kachalinsky"

This health resort is located at a distance of sixty kilometers from Volgograd. An ecologically clean area in the floodplain of the Don was chosen for it. How to get to this sanatorium? You need to get to the railway station "Kachalino" (Volgograd region). The sanatorium sends its buses here on arrival days. From the regional center can be reached in another way. Another route passes through the Kachalino station (Volgograd region). "Sanatorium" - this is the name of the stop, which can be reached by bus (route - "Volgograd-Trekhostrovskaya").

The main health care treatment profiles are pediatrics, gynecology, cardiology, endocrinology and neurology. It offers vacationers - chemical and clinical studies, ECG and ultrasound, bicycle ergometry and paraffin treatment.

The sanatorium of the Volgograd region "Kachalinsky" offers its patients to undergo procedures in the office of electric light therapy. This is electrophoresis using drugs and electrosleep, an amplipulse and diadynamic currents, ion, magnet, aroma and laser therapy. Among the means of prevention and treatment of various pathologies are herbal teas and oxygen cocktails, manual and apparatus massage, auto-training, physiotherapy exercises and dry carbon dioxide baths.

Sanatorium "Elton"

This health resort is a specialized mud-bath institution. It operates year round. Sanatorium "Elton" (Volgograd region) is located in the village of the same name Pallasov district. Six kilometers from the health resort is a lake of the same name.



Sanatorium "Elton" (Volgograd region) is famous for the fact that brine and therapeutic mud are used as the main means in the treatment of patients. These nutrients are mined in Lake Elton. Mineral water from the Smorogdinsky spring also contributes to the recovery of the body.

On the territory of the sanatorium there is a water and mud bath clinic and a polyclinic, a library and a dining room, a club and a dance floor, a cafe and a shop, an aerosolarium and a billiard room. The main remedies

At Elton Sanatorium, highly qualified doctors and attentive medical staff meet patients. The prescribed courses of treatment are effective for restoring the health of vacationers. The main remedies in the sanatorium are healing brine and mud. Deliver them from a nearby lake.





Healing mud is a substance that is mineral sludge soaked in brine. In appearance, this product looks like an oily thick plastic mass with a bluish tint. At the same time, it smells of hydrogen sulfide. Healing mud can be easily rubbed. There is no sensation of sand. This natural remedy sticks to the skin very tightly, and after the procedure is completed it is washed off with difficulty.

The black color and plasticity of the dirt gives the colloidal hydrate of sulphurous iron and fatty substances. After contact with air, the healing agent changes the consistency, color, becomes covered with a crust of brown-green color. Such changes occur due to the formation of acetic, formic and other acids in the mud. Contact with air causes a loss of ductility. The dirt dries and begins to crumble.

Sanatorium "Dubovka"

This health resort is located on the banks of the Volgograd reservoir, fifty-five kilometers from the regional center. The territory where the institution is built is considered environmentally friendly.





Sanatorium "Dubovka" (Volgograd region) takes vacationers in need of treatment of the digestive tract and musculoskeletal system. In this sanatorium, people who underwent surgery undergo rehabilitation. Some precancerous diseases (such as mastopathy) are also prevented here.

Health resort "Volgograd"

In a pine forest, on the right bank of the Volga, there is a sanatorium, which is the largest on the territory of the Lower Volga. The Volgograd complex is a year-round diversified rehabilitation center. His main specialization is the prevention and treatment of pathologies of the musculoskeletal, nervous and cardiovascular systems. Sanatorium Volgograd accepts patients with diseases of the musculoskeletal system, with eye and respiratory ailments, as well as with diabetes.



Gornaya Polyana mineral water, which is extracted from two wells located on the territory of the described center, is used as the main therapeutic agent in the health resort. Many resorts in the Volgograd region apply the healing mud of Lake Elton.
