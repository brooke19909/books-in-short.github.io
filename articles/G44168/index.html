The time difference with Greece is not a problem for tourists

Every tourist who dreams of being in the cradle of ancient civilization has such a unique chance. Greece, or, as it is also called Hellas, is the center of Christianity, it attracts picturesque sea coasts, places of worship. The time difference with Greece is not an obstacle to visiting the country at any time of the year. Thousands of tourists come here in any season.

Greece





An amazing land where you can see cozy villages, high mountains, temples, beautiful beaches that are reflected in the clear waters of the Ionian and Aegean Seas. These places are filled with legends, myths. It was in these parts that famous heroes, philosophers, and talented writers lived. Greece gives inspiration to many, it pacifies and fascinates. It is no coincidence that Hellas is considered the cradle of world culture, because it was here that its foundation was laid, philosophy and democracy, art and science were born. All this left an indelible mark on the further development of history and humanity. Everyone knows that the first Olympic Games were held in Greece. Hellas is the birthplace of theater: tragedy, comedy and drama.

Greece. Difference in time

Everyone who is preparing to visit sunny Hellas should ask in advance about the time zone in which the country is located. Such information will avoid possible misunderstandings associated with the transfer of hours, even such commonplace as being late for dinner at the hotel.

Since 1916, Eastern European time (EET) has been officially used in Greece with the transfer of hands to winter and summer. The time difference with Greece is World UTC - plus two hours in winter and plus three hours in summer. At winter time, Greece changes the clock at the end of October (last Sunday).





After the cancellation of the seasonal change of hours in Russia, the time difference with Greece increased. Previously, it was one hour with Moscow, now in the winter with Greece we are two hours apart. Of course, this is not very convenient for those who travel to this country from Moscow. But Kiev has zero difference with Greece - they live at the same time all year round.

Greece itself is not divided into time zones; the whole time is the same, whether it be Athens, Thessaloniki, Crete, Rhodes or Corfu. Fraternal Cyprus is in the same time zone.

general characteristics





The Greek Republic is located in southeastern Europe, in the south of the Balkan Peninsula. The capital is the legendary city of Athens. The population of the city is five million people. The largest cities in Greece are Patras, Heraklion, Volos, Thessaloniki, Larisa. It is bordered by countries such as Albania, Bulgaria, Turkey, Yugoslavia. From the east the country is washed by the Aegean Sea, from the west - the Ionian, and from the south - the Mediterranean. The highest point in Greece is Mount Olympus. The length of the coastline of Greece is 13,676 kilometers, the country has more than 2,500 islands, most of them uninhabited. The largest islands are Crete, Mykonos, Euboea, Chios, Santorini, Corfu, Samos, Zakynthos, Karpathos, Rhodes.

Tourism

The time difference with Greece for European countries is very small, so travelers do not need to adapt at all. Thousands of tourists come here from all over the world. In the country's economy, tourism comes first. Most popular are the aforementioned large Ionian islands, as well as the Athenian Riviera, Peloponnese, Halkidiki Peninsula, and the Pieria region. Everyone who travels to Greece, enjoys here not only the magical nature, but also many attractions. This amazing country has a huge number of interesting places, monuments. Even if you just get to know them, you will need to spend several years.

Tourists primarily seek to get to Athens. The vibrant, temperamental city bears the name of the ancient goddess of wisdom. Here you should definitely visit the Temple of Olympian Zeus, the Acropolis, Plaku - an ancient district.

Leisure

Famous sights, fascinating excursions - everyone who came here considers it a holy duty to visit them. Traditional noisy markets, famous fashion houses, shops, stylish boutiques, fur factories, relaxation centers, SPA salons, thermal springs - all this is Greece. The time difference for Europeans here is not noticeable at all, so all tourists feel alert, energetic already in the first hours after arrival and are ready to immediately go on a trip to this great country. Every day of your stay here is filled with new, unforgettable experiences. Thousands of spectators fill cinemas, attend various festivals, theaters with a varied program. You can relax after a walk in traditional taverns, which are numerous both in the mountains and on the coast.

Shopping in Greece

In all major resort cities with the beginning of the beach season from May to October, all supermarkets, boutiques and shops are open seven days a week until late in the evening. In coastal areas, some shops are closed on Sundays. Small shops and small boutiques in some places close from 14.30 to 17.00 for a Greek siesta. Chain stores and large supermarkets usually work on weekdays from 08.00 to 21.00, and on weekends from 08.00 to 20.00.

Arriving in the country, do not forget to change the clock so that the time difference with Greece does not interfere with your vacation. Leaving this amazing country, everyone takes with him not only unique, rare souvenirs, but also a lot of positive emotions, a charge of vivacity and good mood.
