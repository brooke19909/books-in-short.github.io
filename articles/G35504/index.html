Hotel Golden Coast Beach Hotel 4 * (Protaras, Cyprus): description, rooms and reviews

Many people, having decided that their vacation will take place in Cyprus, are thinking of staying at Golden Coast Beach Hotel 4 * (Protaras). And this is the right choice. Because this hotel has a lot of advantages.





Location

Golden Coast Beach Hotel 4 * is located in a place called Protaras. This is a small resort village located in the southeastern part of Cyprus. The distance to the nearest airport, which is located in Larnaca, is about 45 minutes (the distance is ~ 55 kilometers).

Protaras is widely known for its beautiful sandy beaches. Also very close, 5 minutes by car, is the famous resort of Ayia Napa. True, it is more suitable for families with children.

The hotel itself is located on the first coastline. Therefore, you can get to the sea in just one minute. By the way, the beach here is sandy and shallow. The bay in which it is located is protected by breakwaters. Nearby you can find a fishing harbor with yachts and boats.

Interestingly, this is not a new hotel. It was built in 1987, but not so long ago, in 2013, a deep reconstruction was carried out. So everything inside is very modern and new. The apartments are also made in an interesting original style.





Sports and recreation

In Golden Coast Beach Hotel 4 * (Protaras) there are many ways to have fun. Guests can play tennis, aerobics or billiards, shoot archery or try squash (if anyone does not know, this is a sport similar to tennis). The game is always indoors. Only in squash do two people play with the wall, on it are marked the places that you need to get into. Loses the one who hit the ball in the wrong zone, bounced off the wall after being served by another player. All in all, quite interesting and dynamic. By the way, the hotel also offers bike rental.

The hotel also regularly hosts entertainment programs, thematic and folklore shows. Those people who like to try themselves in something new will definitely enjoy the lessons of Greek, dance and Cypriot cuisine.

Also on the territory there are two swimming pools (outdoor and indoor), tennis courts, a health club, a jacuzzi, a gym, and a SPA salon. In it you can enjoy a sauna, a bath, a solarium, hydro- and ordinary massage, or go for cosmetic procedures.

By the way, staying here, you should definitely try yourself in water sports and diving. It is all available on the beach. You can even rent a canoe.

Services

The Golden Coast Beach Hotel 4 * (Protaras) has everything that will help to make your vacation more comfortable. The hotel offers a variety of services. Naturally, there is such a necessary thing as wireless Internet, and private parking.

Concierge, nanny and caregivers are provided (relevant for people arriving with children), 24-hour reception, luggage storage. There is a point where you can exchange currency, ironing, laundry and dry cleaning, an office with a photocopy and fax machine, a business center and even a conference room.

Also on site are shops, car rental, a church, a souvenir stall. In addition to all the above, guests are provided with room service, newspapers, food and drinks, facilities for people with disabilities, and non-smoking rooms, families or honeymooners.

By the way, the staff is multilingual. The people working here speak English, Greek, German and Russian.





Restaurants

In the Golden Coast Beach Hotel 4 * there are several institutions where you can try various dishes. Atrium Restaurant is a place where guests can enjoy a varied breakfast, while Mediterranean or European and regional dishes are served for dinner or lunch. You can also try excellent wine - a huge selection is offered. And this place is ideal for romantic evenings, dinners and weddings. The hall can accommodate 200 people.

Hotel Golden Coast Beach Hotel 4 * (Golden Coast Beach) also has an institution called Calypso Restaurant. Here you can enjoy the informal atmosphere and picturesque landscapes opening from the veranda. It not only serves delicious breakfasts and lunches. This is a great place for private events. The restaurant can accommodate 100 people.

Calypso Terrace Restaurant serves international dishes. The outdoor terrace has seating for 200 people. It offers magnificent views of the sea and parks.

And the hotel also has an institution called T 'Apomesimero Ouzeri. This is a tavern where guests are treated to traditional Greek dishes. It has an amazing atmosphere. Byzantine arches, clay stoves, quietly murmuring streams - all this is the decor of the institution. And it is definitely worth a look here to try grilled swordfish, crab, shrimp, pork, chicken, shish kebab and many other dishes.





Bars

Also on the territory of Golden Coast Beach Hotel 4 * (Cyprus, Protaras) there are several establishments in which you can enjoy a variety of drinks. On the terrace, right next to the pool, you can see the Beach Bar. They serve soft drinks, juices, light snacks, and ice cream. The Poseidon Pool Bar is also located on the terrace. They serve all the same, as well as exotic cocktails and sandwiches.

Hesperides Bar, which was recently renovated, is ideal for private dates and evening vacations. It always plays lively relaxing music.

The last place worth noting is called Hesperides Terrace Bar. This is an open-air cocktail bar. It is located in a very pleasant and interesting place, all surrounded by lush greenery. In the evenings, entertainment shows are held here. So here, too, you should definitely look.
