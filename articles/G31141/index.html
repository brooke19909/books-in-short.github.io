Three ways to transfer money from Kyivstar to Kyivstar

There are a lot of subscribers of the Kyivstar mobile operator, and this figure is growing every day. Because of this, new subscribers are faced with a number of issues. Many do not even know how to transfer money from Kyivstar to Kyivstar. How to do this will be discussed later in the article. As a result, three methods will be considered, among which: transfer using the special service "Funds Transfer", using the Kyivstar website, as well as a special application from this operator.





The first method: the service "Transfer of funds"

If you do not know how to transfer money from Kyivstar to Kyivstar, then this method is suitable for you like no other. By and large, the Kyivstar operator‚Äôs "Transfer of funds" service is the only one that allows you to transfer funds from a regular mobile phone to, so to speak, new-fashioned.

The essence is simple: you just need to send a special USSD request, indicating in it the transfer amount and directly the subscriber number. The USSD request looks like this:

* 124 * transfer_amount * subscriber_number #

To send it, you just need to dial the above code on the phone, not forgetting to specify the exact data, and press the call. It will take several seconds for the request to be processed and the result will be displayed on your screen.

There may be two options:

  1. If the transferred amount does not reach 20 hryvnias, then the money will be sent immediately, without additional confirmation.
  2. If the transferred amount will be more than 20 hryvnias, then to send money you will need to confirm the transfer.

Now we consider in detail the second case.

Transfer confirmation

In response to your request, a message will be displayed on the screen in which the previously entered data will be indicated. If all of them are correct, then you need to send a second USSD request confirming the transfer. It looks like this:

* 125 #

After dialing it, you just have to click on the call, and the money will be transferred to another phone.

This was the first way to transfer money from Kyivstar to Kyivstar. But he is not the only one. And if it seemed uncomfortable to you, then you can use another, which will be presented below.

Method 2: translation on the Kyivstar website

Many do not know how to transfer money from Kyivstar to Kyivstar using the company's specialized website for these purposes. However, after reading the detailed instructions, you will quickly understand the situation.

First of all, you will need to be on that page of the official website where this transfer is carried out:





  1. You will have a form to fill out. You need to enter here the subscriber number, transfer amount and directly your number. In addition, you can immediately see the transfer fee and the amount of funds debited.
  2. Once all the data has been entered, you can click the "Next" button.
  3. On the next page you will be shown all the data you entered so that you check their correctness, and if everything is correct, then click the "Pay" button.
  4. As soon as you do this, a message with a code will be sent to the phone of the subscriber from whose account the money will be debited, which will need to be entered in the appropriate field.

Method 3: transfer using the Mobile Money application





If you are the owner of a smartphone based on Android or iOS, then you can use the Mobile Money application to transfer money. Kyivstar itself developed it, so you can be sure of reliability.

After downloading and installing the application on your device, you need to open it and go to the "Recharge mobile account" menu. Next, select the operator to whose account the money will be transferred, and fill out a special form. This form completely repeats the above presented, so fill in all the fields and click "Next". After that, enter the code that will come to your phone.

This was the third way how to transfer money at Kyivstar. And it is worth noting that all three methods are quite easy to use.
