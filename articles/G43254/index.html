Hotel rest

Rocamar 4 (Madeira) - a solid comfortable hotel, built on a rock in 1988 for celebrities. In 2006 was the last renovation, which turned the hotel into a modern, stylish holiday destination for tourists from all over the world. Its special location makes the holiday unique. Roca Mar 4 * does not have its own beach, but the presence of a pool above the water surface of the ocean and its own descent into the Atlantic waters with the help of a ladder make the hotel popular and in demand.



A free hotel bus runs to Funchal, which makes 3 flights per day (except Sunday). The hotel itself is a complex consisting of two Rocamar 4 and Royal Orchid 4 hotels, whose guests can use all the infrastructure at the same time. If you wish, you can go to relax on the city beach, located at a distance of about 800 meters from the hotel. The beach is sandy and rocky, accessible and comfortable.
