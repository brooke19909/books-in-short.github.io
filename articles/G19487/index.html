Device niche

Smartphone HTC Desire 616 is focused on youth. This is clearly indicated by the price of the smartphone, its design and technical parameters. A device with a diagonal of 5 inches and an 8-core CPU is best suited for the most demanding users, which, in essence, are young people under the age of 25 years. Moreover, their financial capabilities are very modest. Hence the price of the device at $ 200. Of course, there are certain drawbacks to this gadget, but all of them are offset by the low cost and technical parameters of the smartphone.

Communication kit

Smartphone HTC Desire 616 Dual sim is equipped with an impressive set of wired and wireless interfaces. It includes the following:

  • The device has 2 slots for installing SIM cards. In the first of them you can install a SIM card of standard sizes, and in the second - microsymbol. The device has only one radio module, the cards work in alternate switching mode. During a conversation on one of them, the second is out of range. In order not to miss an important call, you have to properly reconfigure the call forwarding system. The first SIM card allows you to work in cellular networks of the 2nd and 3rd generations. As a result, the maximum data transfer rate in this case can be up to 20 Mbps. But the second SIM card can only function in the GSM network, and the data transfer rate in this case can reach only a few hundred kilobits at best. Accordingly, in the first situation, you can upload files of any size, but in the second you can only view simple Internet portals or communicate on social networks.
  • Another important wireless way to get information is wifi. In this case, the speed will increase to 100 Mbps. This allows, as in the case of 1 SIM card, to upload information to a smartphone in any volume. At the same time, even downloading a movie in good quality will take a matter of minutes. Everything else will also be downloaded without problems (online video, serious Internet resources and large-sized toys).
  • Also in the device there is a bluetooth transmitter. This wireless technology is indispensable in cases where you need to connect to your smartphone, for example, wireless headphones with a microphone or share a small file with a similar gadget.
  • The navigation subsystem in this device is implemented on the basis of GPS and A-GPS technologies. In the first case, the location of the device is determined using the international satellite navigation system. And in the second, the nearest mobile communication towers are used. In both the first and second situations, you may need an Internet connection. In any case, it is not so difficult to turn this 5-inch device into a full-fledged GPS navigator.
  • Also, the device has a 3.5 mm audio port for connecting an external wired speaker system.
  • The last important wired interface is microUSB. It allows you to connect to a PC or charge the battery.
