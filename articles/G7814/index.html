How to check whether the restored iPhone or not? How to distinguish a restored iPhone from a new one

Apple phones have been the most popular phones for ten years, despite the high cost. In an effort to buy the coveted iPhone cheaper than its market value, their fans go to different tricks. The most popular way is to purchase refurbished phones. Let's find out what is the peculiarity of such devices, how to distinguish them from new ones, and whether it is worth buying at all.

What is a restored iPhone and how is it different from the new

A similar name is given to a smartphone that has already been repaired and again went on sale at a discount. As a rule, such devices, because of their lower price, are sold in poor countries.

A similar procedure is practiced by all major manufacturers of such devices: Samsung, ASUS, Apple and others.

Usually, devices returned to the manufacturer for some reason fall under restoration. In order not to incur losses, such a smartphone is sent to the factory, where it is carefully checked and, if there are malfunctions, put in order. Then it goes on sale again, with an official guarantee.

Such an apparatus cannot be recognized by eye. It is completely identical only to the one that came off the assembly line in appearance, stuffing, software and configuration.

How to distinguish a reconditioned device from a used one

Quite often, out of ignorance, the restored iPhone is correlated with a used device. However, this is not quite true.

  • The restored iPhone is actually new. Therefore, it does not and cannot have traces of use or activation. It is pasted with factory protective films and is fully equipped.
  • When repairing at the factory, defective iPhone parts are always replaced with new ones. If you open such a device inside, you will not see traces of soldering, gluing or other repairs.
  • Such a smartphone is covered by the manufacturer‚Äôs warranty, which lasts 1 year. Therefore, if the restored iPhone does not work, it can be returned for repair to an official service center or replaced within the statutory 14 days.

Kinds

There are two types of smartphones of this kind:

  • Manufacturer refurbished.
  • Seller refurbished.

Manufacturer refurbished

Translated from English, this phrase means "restored at the factory." That is, devices of this category were repaired by specialists who assembled them. So, everything was done at the highest level, in accordance with Apple standards. All new parts that are replaced instead of faulty ones are original, not Chinese copies.

Any manufacturer refurbished phone is almost no different from a new one. And if nevertheless the restored iPhone is broken again - it can be replaced under the Apple warranty, repaired or refunded.

Despite their excellent characteristics, such devices are sold at a lower price. Moreover, on the same official sites and stores where new ones are. However, the manufacturer officially confirms that the phone has undergone the repair process and this is indicated in the accompanying papers for the device.

How to distinguish from a new refurbished iPhone manufacturer refurbished

Although the appearance of both the new and the refurbished smartphone is indistinguishable from each other, Apple Corporation is trying to be honest with customers in this matter. Therefore, she introduced a special marking that helps to recognize the manufacturer refurbished smartphone.

How to check whether the restored iPhone or not? The manufacturer provides several simple and affordable ways to do this.

  • First method. How to distinguish a restored iPhone from a new one? Pay attention to the design of his box. The repaired devices, regardless of the model, are placed in white packaging with a gray inscription on the front. There are no other images there.
  • In addition to the design, each such box has a number of identification marks. Next to the description of the device and its serial number should be the letters RFB, which means refurbished (restored). Also on the packaging itself in a narrow oval frame is written Apple Certified Pre-Owned. This means that Apple officially confirms that this unit was under repair.
  • If for some reason there is no box at the time of check, you need to pay attention to the serial number of the device that is in doubt. In officially restored, it always starts with the letter F. For comparison, for new Apple smartphones, serial numbers begin with the letter M (for sale in stores) or with the letter P (custom-made models).

Is it worth buying

Is it worth taking an officially restored iPhone? Of course, yes. Indeed, despite the repair, in fact this is a new device, the first user of which becomes its buyer.

Even if it has already been activated before the repair, it is reflashed at the factory, and a different serial number is assigned.

In addition, the device costs less than a new one, while still under official warranty. Therefore, the probability of its breakdown is the same as that of not restored.

The main problem when buying such a smartphone is a reliable seller. Otherwise, this phone can be an excellent budget option for all fans of Apple products, with the same level of quality and service.

IPhone seller refurbished

This type of repair translated from English means "repaired by the seller." That is, the manufacturing plant has nothing to do with the restoration of such devices. This means that it does not give guarantees, so the quality of such a repair is akin to a big lottery.

In fairness, it is important to note that sometimes domestic masters can perfectly restore an iPhone, and it will serve faithfully for a long time. However, such a case is an exception, not a rule.

The main disadvantage of this restoration is the quality of the parts. Since the original ones are not cheap, masters acquire Chinese counterparts, which often quickly fail or interact poorly with the device itself.

The main methods of such recovery

To fix such an apparatus, repairmen use different methods. Many of them border fraud. Often they try to pass off a seller refurbished iPhone as an Apple restored or just used, but not repaired. For this purpose, go to the following tricks.

  • Collect one iPhone from parts of other devices of the same brand.
  • Repair breakdowns, but do not replace faulty parts.
  • They sell a smartphone with a box and documentation from another device. Or complete it with Chinese headphones and charging.
  • There are cases when, together with the original packaging and papers, they sell a Chinese fake of good quality, which only specialists can distinguish by eye.
  • It is very rare, but it happens that as a seller refurbished they sell a real manufacturer refurbished in excellent condition, but with an expired warranty.

Difference between seller refurbished and manufacturer refurbished

Although both types of recovery are aimed at tidying up the substandard apparatus, and then selling it again, it is achieved in various ways. Therefore, you should be aware of their main differences.

  • Seller refurbished does not have an Apple warranty.
  • It can be activated or blocked.
  • Such an iPhone, as a rule, has traces of use.

It is noteworthy that all these obvious shortcomings of seller refurbished may not affect its price. It often costs the same as a refurbished factory, for which unscrupulous sellers are desperately trying to give it out.
