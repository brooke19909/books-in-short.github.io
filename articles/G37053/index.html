Hotel Villa Holiday Apartments 3 * (Ulcinj, Montenegro): description, reviews

If you want to enjoy a colorful European vacation, pay attention to the resort Villa Holiday Apartments. Here, in the cozy town of Ulcinj, you can enjoy quality service at a relatively low price.





a brief description of

If you dream of a colorful holiday in Montenegro, you should go to the resort town of Ulcinj. Villa Holiday Apartments Cat A is a great option for both single and romantic or family vacations. This mini-hotel has 25 comfortable rooms that are equipped with everything necessary for a comfortable stay. Each one has a balcony overlooking the green park area or the Adriatic coast.

Despite the small size of the resort complex Villa Holiday Apartments 3 * (Ulcinj), there is everything you need for a good rest. You can enjoy your time in the cozy restaurant or in the elegant bar. Guests can also relax by the pool or work out in the modern gym. This is not a complete list of services that are available to guests of this hotel.

Excursion programs

The tour desk of the Villa Holiday Apartments Cat A 3 * hotel organizes rich excursion programs for guests. So, you can choose from the following list of tours:

  • Boyana Island is located in the river delta. According to legend, it is a sunken ship, which is covered with river sand. This is the southern outskirts of Montenegro.
  • Shkoder is a city located in the northern part of Albania, on the shores of a picturesque lake. This is one of the oldest settlements with lots of historical sights. In addition, it is one of the main economic and cultural centers of Albania.
  • Cruising Bay is the southernmost fjord in Europe. On the way to this attraction, you can admire cities such as Bar, Petrovac, Budva, St. Stephen and Tivat. Alternatively, you can take a boat ride around the picturesque bay.
  • Travel to Kanyos runs through the bar, Tunnel, as well as Podgorica. On the way, a stop is made at the Moraca Monastery, which is considered one of the main monuments of medieval architecture in Montenegro. You can also enjoy Lake Biograd, which is located in the middle of a rainforest.
  • Excursion to Dubrovnik will allow you to admire the mass of architectural monuments and the unique nature of the Adriatic. Departure from the city of Ulcinj at 07:30.
  • If you like fishing, then you should go for a walk on the open sea. For a start, you will have a walk on a panoramic boat along the Ulchinsky Riviera, Long Beach, Valdanos. You can also swim on the most beautiful beaches.
  • Ostrog Monastery is located in the middle of the rocky mountains. This is the most popular route for pilgrims. The peculiarity of this place is that people of various religious faiths are eager to visit it.
  • Cetinje is one of the most popular cities in Montenegro, which is characterized by a rich history. Here, tourists can visit the palace of King Nikola, the castle square, as well as the local history museum with several thousand exhibits. The program also includes a visit to the mausoleum.
  • Going on an excursion to Skadar Lake, you can visit the area where the largest number of birds live in Europe. You can see a lot of natural and cultural attractions.

Additional Information

A number of rules regarding accommodation and rest apply in the resort Villa Holiday Apartments 3 *. Guests should pay attention to the following points:

  • check-in and check-in of apartments starts at 14:00 and ends at 21:00;

General impression

Despite the many shortcomings of Villa Holiday Apartments (Montenegro), this hotel is considered one of the best in Ulcinj. Travelers from around the world often choose it for a quiet family vacation. It is quite cozy, quiet, and also has everything you need for a comfortable stay. The secret to success is that this hotel is a family business. The owners are constantly on the territory, showing active participation in the work of all services of the institution.

It is impossible not to say about the location of the hotel. Of course, the beach is far enough away, but this drawback can be compensated by a free shuttle service. As for excursion programs, the hotel can be considered an ideal starting point for exploring the sights of Montenegro. But the food here leaves much to be desired. It is much tastier and cheaper to eat in catering establishments located nearby.
