Grand Ozcelik 4 * (Turkey / Kusadasi) - photos, prices and reviews of tourists from Russia

Kusadasi has long been considered one of the best resorts on the Turkish coast of the Aegean . Tourists are attracted by both the beautiful local nature and the clear sea, as well as developed infrastructure with a mass of hotels and entertainment for every taste. If you plan to spend your vacation in Kusadasi, Grand Ozcelik 4 * will be an excellent option for spending an unforgettable vacation in a tranquil, picturesque place.





Hotel Location

The four-star Hotel Grand Ozzelik is located on the very shores of the Aegean Sea, just five kilometers from the center of the resort of Kusadasi. In the vicinity of the hotel there is everything necessary for recreation: shops, supermarkets, bazaar, souvenir shops, restaurants, bars, etc. The nearest airport to the hotel is in Izmir, 73 kilometers away. If your plane lands in this air harbor, you can get to Grand Ozcelik in about an hour and a half. Some flights also arrive at Antalya Airport. In this case, tourists will have to spend at least two hours on the road to Kusadasi.

Photo and description "Grand Ozzelik"

This hotel began its work in 1992. Since then, several reconstructions have been carried out here, the last of which occurred in 2005. This hotel complex includes a small area with pools and a hammam, as well as the main nine-story building and an additional four-story block. The hotel has 96 rooms, almost all of which have sea views. ‚ÄúGrand Ozzelik‚Äù, according to our compatriots who have already been here, is an excellent place for a relaxing family holiday on the beach.





Housing stock

Grand Ozcelik (Turkey) offers everyone to stay in one of 166 comfortable apartments. The main building includes 96 rooms, 90 of them are standard rooms, designed for a maximum of three guests, 5 - suites (maximum 4 people) and one room for the disabled. The additional block consists of 20 family rooms. All apartments are equipped with a private bathroom, a balcony, a split system, a TV (there are channels in Russian), a telephone, a refrigerator. Towels are cleaned and changed every day. Maids change bedding twice a week. If desired, hotel guests can order the service "room service".





Nutrition

The four-star Grand Ozzelik Hotel (Kusadasi, Turkey) operates according to the all-inclusive scheme, which is popular with our compatriots. Three times a day in the hotel restaurant guests are offered a buffet, represented by various dishes of both local and international cuisine. Here you can always try meat, fish dishes, a variety of salads, as well as fresh pastries and delicious desserts. In several bars of the hotel you can enjoy drinks for every taste.

Beach vacation

Just a few meters away is its own private beach. Guests of Grand Ozzelik can use sun loungers and umbrellas for free. For a fee, here you can ride a scooter or rent other equipment for water activities. The hotel's private beach is sandy. Entering the water here is gentle. There is a narrow pebble strip near the shore, but then sand begins again at the bottom.





Prices

Despite the fact that the Grand Ozcelik hotel belongs to the four-star category, this place can be called quite budget. So, for example, a seven-day stay here in August will cost you 525 euros for a standard double room. In low season, the cost of accommodation will be lower.

Grand Ozcelik 4 *: traveler reviews from the Russian Federation

Due to the fact that today many people when choosing a hotel in a particular country rely not so much on its official description as on the opinions of other tourists, we decided to make it easier for you and to bring to your attention the comments of our compatriots who stayed during their vacation in Turkey at the Grand Ozzelik Hotel (Kusadasi). Comments of tourists about the hotel itself and its location

As our compatriots note, Grand Ozcelik is not a new hotel, but well built, clean and well maintained. The hotel area is small. There are two not very spacious swimming pools (open-air and indoor), a hammam, a sun terrace, a bar and a residential building with a restaurant. A few meters away is the hotel‚Äôs private beach.

The rooms at the Grand Ozzelik are nicely decorated, restrained, without pathos. Almost all apartments have sea views. The cleaning, according to many travelers, is very high quality. So you will not find any ants or cockroaches in the hotel. The bathroom has a hairdryer, as well as shampoo, shower gel, soap and other bath accessories, which are regularly replenished as necessary. Towels are changed every day, regardless of whether you threw them on the floor or neatly hung them.

As for the location, from Grand Ozcelik the center of Kusadasi can be reached in just 10 minutes by shuttle bus (dolmushe). To go to the city, just leaving the hotel, cross the road. Minibus drivers themselves will stop to pick you up. Also within walking distance for hotel guests are various shops, supermarkets, bazaars and other infrastructure.

Beach Holidays & Entertainment: Traveler Experience

Just a few meters from the Grand Ozzelik terrace, there is a hotel's private beach. It is sandy. When entering the water there is a pebble strip, but then the sand begins again. The beach, according to our compatriots, is small. Experienced travelers are advised to occupy the sunbeds early in the morning, because if you come to the beach after breakfast, then finding a free sun lounger will not be easy. The beach also has water sports equipment. As for beach towels, they can be taken in any quantity without a deposit and cards, as well as changed as necessary.

Just note that there is no special animation in Grand Ozcelik 4 * (Kusadasi). So, here you will be offered to play darts, table tennis and do water aerobics. Also during the day they play games with children. In the evenings, performances are sometimes held.
