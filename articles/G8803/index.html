Basic concepts of the financial market

One of the basic concepts in the currency market is a quote, which is the exchange rate of one currency against another, expressed in its units. For example: eur - the original base currency, they plan to sell or buy it; usd - the quoted currency for which they intend to sell or buy the base currency, then the current rate (quotation) is the ratio eur / usd = 1.3327, with the last, fourth, digit in the lower order is called the item and is indicated by pip.
