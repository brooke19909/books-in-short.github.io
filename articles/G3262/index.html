Ideas for a diary - a personal friend and silent companion

Each person has those thoughts that cannot be told even to the closest person. And I want to share them! What to do in such a situation? There is a way out: a personal diary can be a great helper!





Where to begin

The first thing you need: decide how much a person needs such a friend. You need to keep a diary quite often, you should love it and just wish to communicate with it. If a girl wants to make such a silent comrade "for company" with her friends, she should not do this, there will simply be no sense in this. But if there is a desire to pour out your soul on paper, the diary is what you need!

What to write on

What ideas are there for a diary, a personal friend, and a silent comrade? It should be noted that you can record on anything, the main thing is that it is comfortable for a person. The most convenient way is a small notebook or notebook, which you can always not only take with you, but also hide in a secluded corner. You can, of course, make notes in the album, this option is perfect for creative people and lovers to draw. Great ideas for a personal diary - to create such a friend here yourself. To do this, you can simply fasten the leaves and make a cover, but you can also age the sheets (old-style diary), make the pages colored (romantic diary or mood diary), etc.





Decor

What are the ideas for the diary (personal, handwritten) regarding its design? Here it can be said that a person should not have any restrictions. Such a notebook can be painted, pasted with candy wrappers, made a repository of your favorite photos. The main thing is that it would be pleasant for a person to look at the pages of his own silent comrade. It should be noted that the diary may be thematic (for example, "Diary of my travels"). The design of such a notebook will consist of clippings and photographs of those places where a person has been, and so on. Depending on what mood is today, such may be stickers or drawings. Still ideas for designing a personal diary: make a notebook in a certain style. It can be a romantic diary (red and pink colors, the presence of hearts, etc. will prevail), a beauty diary (stylish and glamorous), or an excellent student (strict, but beloved).

Posts

Ideas for a diary (personal): how to make notes. It will suit both the usual way - to write with a pen on paper, and creative. It can be drawings that simply convey the mood, can be applications or stickers. A person can convey his mood in a few words of a song or a saying of a wise person - why not write in your personal diary?

Storage

Having considered various ideas for a personal diary, a photo of various masterpieces, it is worth considering where it will be stored. This is very important, because often ill-wishers can read a notebook and make a person‚Äôs thoughts public domain. The easiest way is to keep the diary in sight. You can make him a cover and write in capital letters "PHYSICS" or "MATHEMATICS", it is unlikely that someone will get there. If the notebook is too beautiful and bright, it can be hidden in a bedside table or under a bed, in a closet or among ordinary books, wrapped in plain paper.
