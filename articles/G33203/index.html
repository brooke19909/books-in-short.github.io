Which air humidifier to choose? Reviews Steam humidifier. Best humidifier

Very often they decide to buy a room humidifier for the first time after the appearance of a child in the family. This is due to the fact that children are much more sensitive to insufficient air humidity than adults. In infants, the drying out of a very sensitive nasal mucosa often leads to swelling of the tissues, and this greatly complicates breathing through the nose, which is especially acute during sleep and sucking - it becomes difficult for the baby to breathe.





The importance of air humidity

In kindergarten children, due to the drying up of the mucosa, a habit of picking in the nose is formed, for which it is completely pointless to scold the child. And it is difficult to wean him from her, because this reaction is quite natural. In the child, crusts form in the nose, which he instinctively tries to get rid of in all available ways.

Gradually, our mucosa adapts to this situation, and the older the child, the calmer his mucosa reacts. But this does not mean at all that it will normally "work" in dry air conditions, since only with sufficient moisture can it produce enough mucus to protect the nose from harmful bacteria.

Not everyone realizes how large the role of the mucous membranes of the nasopharynx is in the body's defense system. It is she who serves as the first barrier designed to prevent the entry of harmful bacteria and viruses into the human body. When the mucous is in order, it restrains most of them, and the immune system deactivates them even before they are in the body. And for normal operation, the mucosa needs an acceptable air humidity, which is at the level of 40-50%, not less. Most preferences

Usually, as a humidifier for a children's room, many parents prefer to purchase an ultrasonic device, which is notable for its low cost and quite compact size. Such a device and design for the nursery is much easier to pick up. At the moment, models of fairly bright colors are on sale, and if you try, then it is quite possible to find a device made in the form of the hero of your favorite cartoon or any animal. But here it is worth noting the disadvantages inherent in ultrasonic models, which manufacturers and sellers are often silent about. Any ultrasonic humidifier is equipped with only a small fan, which has enough power only to push the water cloud formed inside the device out. As a result of this, a stream of cold steam or water mist is clearly visible, which is usually shown in order to demonstrate the operation of the device clearly.





This attractive visual effect also has a downside - due to the too low fan power, all the humidity emitted by the ultrasonic humidifier will be concentrated in the immediate vicinity of the device, because there is no air movement, and moisture that simply does not have time to spread around the room settles nearby . The result of this is the formation of a local zone of waterlogging in the immediate vicinity of the device, and with high evaporation power, saturated water condensate can also be seen. If there is linoleum on the floor, then there is nothing to be afraid of, and if it comes to wooden parquet or laminate, this will already lead to undesirable consequences. Carpeting constantly exposed to excessive moisture will not get any better either.

And the humidity in the rest of the room will remain at the same level, that is, insufficient, because it simply will not have time to get there. It is clear that the result of the natural movement of air is the mixing of the humidified flow with the main one, but this happens very slowly. But the situation with humidified air in the immediate vicinity of the device and dry one and a half meters from it will always remain. It is for this reason that ultrasonic models are perfectly suitable for those cases when it is necessary to create only a local zone with high humidity, such as, for example, for a flower hill. The Boneco humidifier is perfect for these purposes.

Optimal option

If we talk about which air humidifier to choose (reviews will not always help you with this), then it is worth saying that devices based on the natural principle of cold evaporation are able to provide a much more comfortable air mode in the room. When they work, the moisture evaporated naturally is distributed evenly thanks to the built-in fans of high power, which ensures efficient air exchange throughout the room.

A traditional humidifier differs from an ultrasonic humidifier in that the second membrane evaporates moisture constantly, with a certain frequency, until the water in it runs out. And the first will saturate the air with moisture only until the moisture level is optimal, about 40-50%.

Nature itself helped to develop the principle of cold moistening, because the evaporation of water from the surface of rivers and lakes in natural conditions occurs just like that, only in this situation the role of the water surface is assigned to a special moisturizing sponge made of a porous material that absorbs moisture well.





Principle of operation

When dry air passes through such a humidifier, it absorbs precisely the amount of moisture that is enough for it to maintain a natural level, no more and no less. When the air in the room is saturated enough, it will pass through such a sponge without any consequences, that is, it will not absorb any excess moisture. Thanks to this, it turns out that the principle of cold evaporation allows you to maintain the required level of moisture without the use of any specialized electronic devices. Thanks to him, it is possible to obtain indoor conditions that are closest to natural, and therefore the most comfortable.

The use of such a moisturizing technology, as close as possible to natural, allows you to ensure the absence of side effects or allergic reactions that periodically occur when using ultrasonic devices. Despite the fact that official medicine throughout the world claims that ultrasound is completely safe for humans, there are many people who have an increased sensitivity to this radiation. If we talk about ultrasonic humidifiers, then two categories of children have special sensitivity to them - with severe allergies or suffering from bronchial asthma.

Admittedly, hypersensitivity to the operation of the ultrasound membrane is extremely rare, but it is incorrect to deny this possibility completely. Therefore, often from allergists you can hear recommendations on the use of devices based on cold evaporation - traditional, combined, ionizer cleaners.
