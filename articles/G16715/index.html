How to send a beacon with MTS and Megafon

Quite often it happens that the money on the balance ends at the most inopportune moment. What to do if you urgently need to contact your parents, colleagues or boss? It is in such situations that many people have a question, for example, how to send a “beacon” from MTS or Megafon, depending on the services of which operator they use. In this article we will tell you in detail what this option is and why it is needed.





What is a beacon?

"Beacon", "seagull" - people call this service differently. Its essence is to send a free message asking you to call back or replenish your account. This is very convenient especially at a time when you do not have money in your account. For example, with a zero balance, you can easily send a “beacon”. Megaphone, MTS and some other operators provide this service. It is absolutely free, but you should know that the number of messages sent per day is limited.





How to send a beacon from MTS?

If you faced the need to send someone a “beacon”, then you just need to dial the following combination on your phone: * 110 * phone number # and click on “call”. By the way, the subscriber number can be entered in any format convenient for you. After you click on the call button, the recipient will receive a message asking them to call him back, which will also indicate the date and time of sending. Now you know how to send a “beacon” from MTS. Clients of this operator should take into account that they can send such a message no more than five times a day, therefore, after they end, many start sending out requests for replenishment of their account. This is done like this: dial the combination * 116 * recipient's phone number # and press on the call. True, only users of MTS, Beeline and Megafon, who, by the way, are the main mobile operators in our country, can receive such a message.





How to send a “beacon” from a megaphone?

Above, we have discussed in detail how to send SMS to MTS. Russia, however, uses the services of not only this cellular company. Such an operator as Megafon is quite popular today. When you suddenly run out of money on the balance, and you need to call, just dial the following combination: * 114 * subscriber number #, and at the end press the call button. After you should receive a report on the delivery of the message. By the way, it is very convenient that you can send it to the numbers of other operators. You can use no more than 10 "beacons" per day. As in MTS, in Megaphone there is a service for sending messages with a request to replenish the account, although it is called "Pay for me." Its limit is 5 shipments in 24 hours. To do this, you will need to dial: * 143 * recipient number # "call".

After you learn how to send a “beacon” with MTS and Megafon, you will definitely not be left without help in difficult times. As soon as your “savior” receives a message that you are asking him to call back or you do not have money, he will immediately contact you on his own. But it’s still better when there is always money in the account, and you yourself can call or write to relatives and friends, because in any case they are more expensive than any money.
