Hotel "Topol" (Lazarevskoye): address, description, services, reviews

Hotel "Topol" (Lazarevskoye) can please its guests close proximity to the sea. Its visitors will have to overcome only 10 meters to the beach. The hotel complex is located at: Lazarevskoye, Kalarash street, 16. Comfortable rooms of different price categories will allow anyone to stay comfortably, because GC "Topol" (Lazarevskoye) has a large territory and floor space. Guests can choose from rooms of different classes. About them now and the conversation will go.





Children under 16 years of age are provided with a discount of 50 percent. Children under the age of 5 stay free.

Rooms

So, for example, the most budget option for relaxing is a 2-room single room, which has a large double bed, toilet, TV. The cost of housing with a view of the mountains starts at 1100 rubles, and with a view of the sea - from 1300 rubles, and may increase depending on the time of arrival.
For the same fee, you can rent a triple one-room room for a day, which, unlike the younger version, will have a refrigerator and shower.





The rental price of two-room rooms is from 1100 to 2000 rubles per day. There are triple or quadruple options with a view of the mountains or the sea.
Rooms designed for 5 beds are two-room and three-room, and their price is from 1100 to 1800, depending on the availability of a balcony.

The most luxurious rooms in the hotel are double one-room and triple two-room suites with panoramic sea views. The price of luxury apartments can reach 2.5 thousand rubles per day. You fall in love with the interior of the rooms at first sight. In all rooms, expensive repairs were made using high quality materials. Beautiful finish is complemented by solid hardware made of solid wood.





The interior is decorated with paintings and fresh flowers. In-room entertainment features a widescreen TV. The hotel complex "Topol" (Lazarevskoye) has two buildings, so everyone who wants to relax at sea can stay at this hotel.

Services

The number of services provided is enough for a comfortable stay in Sochi. Free perks include: children's room, use of wireless internet, housekeeping, and more. For an additional fee, the visitor receives a number of additional features, such as transfer, in-room Internet, as well as taxi, laundry and dry cleaning services.

Nutrition

As for food, the cafe and restaurant are ready to receive hotel guests and provide a 15-percent discount to visitors. The abundance of dishes will delight even the most spoiled gourmet. You have the opportunity to try the new culinary works of art from the chef first.





In the cafe "Topol" you are ready to offer hot and cold snacks, delicious salads and dishes on the grill, freshly prepared pastries, as well as a number of alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks. It is especially pleasant in the institution in the evening, when artists perform and live music plays. You can admire how the village of Lazarevskoye and the embankment shimmer with all the lights of the night illumination.

Room reservation

The guest house on Kalarash is working around the clock and is ready to make a personal discount to the participants of the Great Patriotic War, people with disabilities and newlyweds. The reservation system is as simple as possible. You just need to choose the case and type of room and call.





The administration will answer all asked questions and reserve a room without any prepayment. Or you can fill out an application on the site, and in the near future you will be contacted to clarify the details. To check in, you need a passport and birth certificate.

First of all, hotel guests noted spacious comfortable rooms with a TV and, which is very important in the hot season, the presence of air conditioning. Hotel "Topol": location

Hotel "Topol" (Lazarevskoye) has a convenient location. Nearby is a wonderful pebble beach. 300 meters from the hotel is the city center, a market where you can buy a lot of goods at very competitive prices. Half a kilometer will have to be overcome to get to the Nautilus water park, which is a huge entertainment complex that can accommodate up to 2000 people. The most avid fan of riding from high mountains will be satisfied, because the water park has six slides. Kids up to 3 years old ride absolutely free, the cost of admission for children under 12 years old is 900 rubles, and for adults a visit will cost 1100 rubles. Waterpark Nautilus is open from June to September from 10 a.m. to 6 or 7 p.m., depending on the month.





Due to the 50-meter distance from the beach, the village of Lazarevskoye is very popular. "Topol-2" is a second building from the main hotel complex. The assortment of rooms is about the same, housing prices are not different from the first building. For those who want a delicious lunch, there is a cafe and a dining room specializing in Caucasian and European cuisine. Guests are offered a wide assortment, including seafood, regular entertainment.

A train station is located 2 kilometers from the hotel complex, which will simplify your journey to the hotel. For those who want to maintain their body in perfect condition, even during relaxation, you can visit the local gym, located 300 meters away. Hotel "Topol" and Lazarevskoye - a great place for entertainment. There are many attractions. Within walking distance from the hotel there is a cinema where you can watch the latest movie news at the lowest prices. You can make money transactions at an ATM near the hotel complex. Also on site there is a large secure parking.

Great place to stay

An excellent option for an inexpensive and comfortable stay is the Topol Hotel. Lazarevskoye is a great place for a delightful hotel with all the necessary infrastructure. The hotel staff is friendly and ready to make your stay as comfortable as possible. Delicious food in the cafe and dining room will leave you pleasant memories of your vacation. In the village of Lazarevskoye, the embankment will be a great place for a walk in the fresh sea air.
