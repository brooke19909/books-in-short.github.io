Berdyansk, boarding house "Slavutich": characteristics, reviews about the rest

Summer vacations on the Ukrainian seaside of Azov remain the most budgetary, and therefore the most popular. Vacations are approaching, and you need to plan where to spend them. On the coast of the sea? But even better on the spit, when water splashes from two sides. It would be nice to heal ... So, rest on the spit, not far from the balneological resort. Berdyansk immediately comes to mind. Pension "Slavutich" stands out among other bases and resort hotels in an advantageous location, high level of service and developed infrastructure. Where is located

Berdyansk is a resort of all-Ukrainian significance. At the base of the spit, at the mouth of the Byrd River, there are estuaries and mineral springs, healing mud and salt lakes. Many people suffering from diseases of the musculoskeletal system and respiratory system, gynecological, nervous disorders, go to heal in Berdyansk. Pension "Slavutich" is located outside the city. It is located on the Middle Berdyansk Spit, at its far end, opposite the island of Big Dzensik. You can get here from the city by shuttle bus No. 15 (departing from the railway station and Schmidt Park). The center of the Middle Spit (a bazaar, a number of shops) is one and a half kilometers from the entrance to the Slavutich boarding house. Nearby there is a cafe "Quiet Harbor". Near the recreation complex there is a minibus stop called "Meotida".



Pension "Slavutich" (Berdyansk)

Photos of this resort showcase a vast seven-hectare area planted with flowers and shady trees. It should be noted that growing a sand garden in the steppe climate is not easy. Tourists noted this green splendor, and many reviews are devoted to landscaping. It is crossed by a road leading from the edge of the spit to Berdyansk. Pension "Slavutich" positions itself as a place of family relaxation. Small guests take precedence over adults. This is reflected in the availability of highchairs, cots and microwave ovens in the room and discounts on vacations with a child.



Rooms

The Meotida building, inherited by Slavutich from the recreation center, was planned according to Soviet standards. There has not yet been a complete reconstruction, but only partial. Therefore, he wins in relation to the other two buildings only one - proximity to the sea. However, block numbers are located there. That is, the bathroom is designed for two rooms. But also there are several "Family" rooms. These are two rooms connected by a common door. Air conditioning is only in one of the bedrooms. The buildings on the other side of the road are more comfortable. They accommodate rooms "Standard", one and two-room "Deluxe". The last category of guest rooms is equipped with a mini-safe, refrigerator, hairdryer, TV and seating area. Pension "Slavutich" (Berdyansk) prices for the summer of 2016 set such. Nutrition

The administration of the boarding house allows its guests to decide for themselves where and how to look for food. You can move in without food. You can pay for breakfast or half board (morning and evening meals). Many tourists use the hotel for an overnight stay, and in the morning they go to mud baths in Berdyansk. Pension "Slavutich" has two restaurant halls. Both work on a buffet system. But in the room for VIP clients, meals are possible on the menu. If you come to a boarding house with a small child, you can order a microwave in the room. There is no need to pay extra for the rental of the stove. Food reviews are very praised. All dishes are tasty, there was always enough, there were no queues and crowding, although the boarding house often receives participants in conferences, seminars and other collective meetings.



Services

Throughout the boarding house "Slavutich" catches a network free "Wi-Fi." For the safety of tourists, security and video surveillance are installed. Parking under the canopy is paid. Since this is a place for families, a lot of attention in the boarding house is paid to children. For them, there is a game room where you can leave your child under the supervision of a certified educator. Mini discos are held every evening. Children under six years of age live and eat free of charge, up to thirteen - enjoy a discount. For adults, a team of professional animators works. They conduct yoga classes, dance dance master classes, and in the evenings organize shows, lotteries and competitions. There is a rental of sports equipment and beach equipment.
