A calm and measured beach vacation at the Lesnaya recreation center in Inal Bay

In the Tuapse district of the Krasnodar Territory there are many beautiful places that tourists love. Inal Bay, located near Dzhubga, attracts tourists with its turquoise coast with clear water and picturesque landscapes.

What is remarkable about the bay?

The name Inal is given in honor of the legendary prince, the founder of the rulers of Kabarda, who lived here in the XV-XVI centuries. He was an excellent reformer, he loved the fleet and organized local shipping here.

The place is interesting not only by the beach, but also by a wellness vacation. On the right bank of the bay and near the neighboring lake is healing blue clay. Vacationers claim that mud treatments (baths and masks on the body) have a rejuvenating and restorative effect.





The beaches of Inal Bay are very spacious and small-pebbled. Often couples with children come here, newlyweds happily spend a honeymoon in a humid subtropical climate. The infrastructure in this area is very developed. Accommodation is offered at affordable prices and for every taste. Organized meals are possible in cafeterias and cafes, discos are created for dancing, and there are good playgrounds for children.

A short walk from the base is a picturesque beach where vacationers can enjoy various types of water activities.

Infrastructure

The territory of the recreation center is ennobled and beautifully landscaped. Along the asphalt paths neatly trimmed shrubs and trees grow, flowers are fragrant.

For living in the recreation center "Lesnaya" in the Inal Bay there is one three-story landscaped building and nine wooden summer houses. The dining room has created comfortable conditions for eating.

Travelers in cars can leave their vehicles in a guarded parking lot.

At the box office you can take sun loungers and umbrellas, balls for the game.

Food and accommodation options

In the dining room of the Lesnaya base in Inal Bay, a comprehensive balanced three meals a day is offered. In the diet, there are always dishes of fish and meat, cereals, dairy products, vegetables and fruits. Tourists resting here assure that they were fed very tasty and satisfying.

For living, you can choose a comfortable room, located on one of the three floors of the residential building. In the Standard category, the decor is done in bright colors, which contributes to complete relaxation. Interesting paintings hang on the walls, indoor plants and curtains with lambrequins create a homely cozy interior. The furniture in the rooms is quite modern and comfortable, there are TVs. The bathroom has a sink, shower, bathroom. Single, double and triple occupancy is offered. Vacationers can also choose to their liking a room with one or two rooms. In the second case, a living room with a soft zone, a table and a TV is separately allocated. Superior rooms have refrigerators and air conditioning, more modern furnishings.

The houses are quite spacious with comfortable furniture, beds with mattresses of medium degree of rigidity. Nearby is a sanitary area with toilets and a summer shower.

Tourists reviews

The only drawback that many regard as an advantage is the too calm atmosphere in this place. Indeed, some reviews of Inal Bay describe a peaceful environment that is difficult to compare with the bustling nearby resorts.





They come here to a greater extent to relax from the gray everyday life and long winter. Families with happy children spend at least 1.5-2 weeks on reviews of Inal Bay. Beach vacations have kids swimming - the entrance to the sea is sandy, very gentle and even. Tourists like that the bay beach is divided into sections that differ from each other in level of depth, the presence of entertainment areas and food outlets.
