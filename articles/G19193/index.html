Nha Trang Vinpearl Resort (Vietnam): description and photos of the hotel

If you want to spend an unforgettable vacation in Vietnam, not only enjoying a beach holiday and excursion program, but also spending time in the most famous amusement park of this country, then the most suitable option for you would be to stay at the five-star Nha Trang Vinpearl Resort.



Location

This chic hotel is located in Khanh Hoa Province (Southeast Vietnam) on Hong Che Island, near Nha Trang. Three kilometers separate it from the mainland. You can get to the island by cable car or by boat. Cam Ranh International Airport is a 45-minute drive away.



General information, photo

Vinpearl Nha Trang Resort & Spa 5 * (Nha Trang) was opened in 2003. In 2013, a large-scale renovation was held here. According to tourists who have been here, Nha Trang Vinpearl Resort is a great place for families with children of different ages, as well as for fun young people and older travelers.



Housing stock

As already mentioned, this hotel offers tourists to stay in one of almost 500 comfortable rooms. Here are deluxe apartments. Guests can also stay at the villas. The hotel has rooms with one bedroom, as well as two, three and even four. Depending on the type of apartment, some of them have their own pool. Also, when booking your stay, you can immediately order the type of food that suits you ( full board or breakfast only) and tickets to the Winperl amusement park.

In general, in all the apartments of Nha Trang Vinpearl Resort, judging by the reviews of our compatriots who have visited this hotel, there is absolutely everything that you may need on vacation. So, in the room you will find high-quality and very comfortable furniture, modern appliances (TV, telephone, air conditioning, safe, refrigerator, etc.), a bathroom with a bath and shower, a spacious balcony with rattan furniture, slippers, a bathrobe, accessories for personal hygiene and more. You can also choose the view from the window - the sea or the picturesque mountains.

As for cleaning, it is carried out every day. Also, maids will regularly update towels and bed linen in your room and replenish stocks of tea sets and personal hygiene products.

Sea

As tourists from different countries who have been here, note in their comments, a beach holiday will not disappoint guests of Vinpearl Resort Nha Trang. So, the hotel is located on the first line and has a large and well-equipped beach with a length of seven hundred meters. According to travelers, there are a lot of sunbeds, sun umbrellas, tents. The beach is sandy, very clean. Entry into the water is gentle, so it will be convenient even for children. Vacationers note that the sea is mostly clean. However, sometimes at high tide it brings various garbage that beach workers quickly clean up.





Vinpearl Resort Nha Trang 5 *: price of accommodation

Judging by the reviews of our compatriots staying at this hotel, the cost of accommodation here can not be called cheap. However, according to them, the hotel complex is well worth the money spent, because the guests here are really offered the living conditions and the highest quality service. If we talk about specific figures, then the price for a seven-day accommodation at Winperl Resort in October will be about 1100 euros for two for a deluxe room with sea view (breakfast included), 1300 euros for the same room + tickets for the whole period to the amusement park , 3,500 euros for a two-bedroom villa (full board + tickets to the park); 4,700 euros for a three-bedroom villa (full board and tickets to the amusement park included).

Infrastructure

On the large territory of the Nha Trang Vinpearl Resort, there are three restaurants (serving European and traditional Vietnamese dishes), several bars with a varied selection of drinks for every taste, three conference rooms for up to 1350 people, two large outdoor pools, sports hard-surfaced tennis courts and tennis courts, a large spa offering visitors a variety of beauty and health treatments, a beauty salon, a medical office, laundry and dry cleaning, a business center, an Internet cafe, and a diving center Center and center of water entertainment. The hotel reception is open 24 hours a day. There are always employees who speak Russian. For the youngest guests, the hotel has a mini club where children from 4 to 12 years old are accepted. Guests can also take advantage of babysitting services. But still, the main advantage of the hotel is the amusement park "Winperl". Tourists come here, vacationing even in other cities of Vietnam. Here, travelers are offered a lot of water entertainment for every taste. A big plus is the fact that hotel guests can use the attractions out of turn.
