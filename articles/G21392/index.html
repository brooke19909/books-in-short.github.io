Park "Vinnovskaya Roscha", Ulyanovsk: address, photo, reviews

Park in Ulyanovsk "Vinnovskaya Roscha" was successfully located in the Zheleznodorozhny district of the city, on the very banks of the mighty Volga River. Every day, this park is visited by hundreds of guests who value nature and love to relax in the fresh air. In the evenings, couples in love walk along its alleys.



Interesting Facts

Previously, the Vinnovsky Grove Park in Ulyanovsk was a territory owned by a landowner named Kindyakov. At one time, the estate was visited by the famous Russian writer Ivan Goncharov, who in his works sang the natural beauties of this picturesque manor. It is believed that this particular grove was described by the author in one of his famous literary works, which is called "Cliff".

The modern history of the Vinnovsky Grove park in Ulyanovsk began in the Soviet period, in 1966. Then, a well-known natural monument was created on its territory.

Park Attractions

On the modern territory of the "Vinnovsky grove" (Ulyanovsk) there are several historical sights that attract the attention of tourists. One of them is red brick crypts. They were built at the beginning of the 20th century, but under the influence of time they collapsed a little, after which no one restored them. Also, guests like to relax in the arbor of I. Goncharov, which is memorial.

Reliable sources indicate that a huge number of plants grow on the territory of the grove - more than 300 varieties. There is also a natural pond of incredible beauty, which often becomes an object for photo shoots.

"Corner of family happiness"

In the "Vinnovsky grove" (Ulyanovsk) there is a separate zone, which is called the "Corner of family happiness." It is very often the townspeople hold wedding ceremonies. There is a small forged shop of white color - it is intended for lovers. Also nearby is a gazebo where the newlyweds can hang a closed padlock in a sign of eternal love.



Ice Age Park

Both adults and children will always be very pleased to visit the Ice Age Park, recently built on the territory of the Vinnovsky Grove in Ulyanovsk. A photo of him is conveyed by the whole atmosphere of fun and ease reigning here. When children get here, they have fun and make new friends.

In this area, absolutely everything is designed in the theme of the famous and beloved by many cartoon with the same name. There are several huge figures of his main characters who, when approaching the guests, begin to growl and move. Often near them arrange photo shoots and shoot videos as a keepsake.

There are several mobile shops in the park where parents can buy toys for their children, and kids have the opportunity to have fun in the sandbox by digging up pre-buried fake bones while their parents are resting on benches around them.

Nutrition

There are two catering facilities in the park. One of them is Gourmet Cafe, and the second is Rodnichok. They are identical in their work, but are located on different sides of the Vinnovsky Grove. It serves Russian and European cuisine. Separately, guests are presented with a children's menu.

Each of these establishments is open until 10 pm, the moment the park closes. In the evening, guests can have fun here, sing karaoke or dance to their favorite hits at the disco.

Additional Information

Guards are on duty around the clock, so letting their kids go here, parents can be sure that they are completely safe. Throughout the territory there are several blocks of dry closets.

The attention of the guests are small outlets where you can buy toys, souvenirs and various other pleasant things. Cotton candy, popcorn, ice cream and drinks are also sold here. There are several points where you can rent a hoverboard, car or segway for a while.

There is a sauna next to the "Vinnovsky grove" (Ulyanovsk), which is famous among the townspeople. It is located in the complex, next to the Turkish and Russian baths. It can be rented for a certain time by a whole group of friends up to 15 people. The cost of such pleasure is from 600 to 1800 rubles per hour.

Rules for visiting the park

As in any other places of rest, there are several prohibitions in the Vinnovsky Grove, violation of which threatens to leave the park. One of the main rules is the prohibition of smoking and drinking alcoholic beverages. Also here you can not appear while intoxicated.

No animals are allowed in the park. It is also forbidden to ride bicycles and, of course, litter.

Guest reviews

Park guests often leave a lot of reviews about it on the Internet. Often, they note that this place is a real natural wealth, in the territory of which you can have a good rest with your whole family, in the company of friends or with your soulmate. Guests love birch trees growing here, near which photo shoots are often arranged.

Caring parents bring their children to rest here - in the park many interesting places are thought out for spending fun activities for kids. The comments also mention the availability of important park infrastructure objects: there are small points selling favorite sweets, you can always have a bite to eat at a local cafe. Toilets are freely available on either side of the park.

Separately, guests note the availability of prices set for entertainment and food. Residents of the city believe that any person can afford this type of vacation.



Opening hours and address

Vinnovskaya Roscha in Ulyanovsk is located at 5a Gaya Avenue. He expects his guests on any day of the week from morning to 10 pm.

You can get here both by your own car and by public transport. An ideal option would be a tram ride to the Vinnivka stop. You can also get here by taxi - any driver can take the client to their destination as soon as possible.
