Krinitsa hotels: description, photos, reviews

Hotels in the village of Krinitsa are able to please and surprise even spoiled vacationers. In just a decade and a half, a large number of different hotels and hotels have appeared here. Krinitsa offers comfortable rooms, pools, recreation areas, saunas. The only drawback of the village is the local relief. The resort is located on a significant hill. "Fairy tale"

Cozy rooms with balconies overlooking the sea and the forest - this is what the Skazka Guest House is like. Among the advantages of this Krinitsa hotel are the presence of a swimming pool, a sports ground, a high-quality tennis table, and a safe playground. Another plus is free wireless internet. In reviews of this guest house, vacationers note the amazing beauty of the surrounding area: there is a fountain, a waterfall, a pond. In addition, the yard is decorated with flowers, arborvitae, Christmas trees. For those who want to take a steam bath, the hotel has a sauna. Incidentally, it is equipped with a shower, a double oak font.





From the pebble beach "Fairytale" is only 10-15 minutes walk, it takes two minutes to get to the grocery store, five minutes to the market. Guests at this hotel are offered one-room and two-room rooms with and without balconies. The cost per day starts from 3600 rubles, the price includes three meals a day.

"Dubravushka"

Hotel "Dubravushka" in Krinitsa is one of the most attractive holiday destinations. It is located in the very center of the village, just 250 meters from the beach. Among the advantages of the hotel guests note comfortable rooms, the presence of a dining room, pool. In addition, entertainment programs are regularly held there.

At the service of vacationers at Dubravushka:

  • safe boxes;
  • tour desk;
  • playground;
  • Cafe;
  • parking;
  • sports equipment rental;
  • medical Center.




The area is 3 hectares. Majestic oaks grow in the courtyard, which are able to protect tourists here from the scorching southern sun. Dubravushka offers guests one, two, three and four-bed rooms. The cost of rest here starts at 1900 rubles.

"Maria"

Among the hotels in Krinitsa it is worth highlighting the guest house "Maria". It was built in 2012 right in the heart of the village. Among the services provided:

  • cafe-dining room;
  • large cozy yard;
  • fresh water pool;
  • umbrellas and deck chairs;
  • laundry and ironing rooms.




The hotel rooms are divided into two categories - luxury and standard. All rooms are equipped with quality furniture, TVs, air conditioning and showers. Towels and bed linen are changed by staff every five days. Vacationers here note that within walking distance of the guest house are a market, a grocery store, a pharmacy, and attractions for children. The cost of rooms in this hotel starts from 1200 rubles per day.

By the way, as in Cypress, smoking in the premises of the Maria Hotel is strictly prohibited!

"Swallow's Nest"

One of the most comfortable guest houses of Krinitsa (Gelendzhik) is the Swallow's Nest. It is only 5-10 minutes unhurried walking distance from the sea. This hotel has many advantages, among them:

  • convenient secure parking;
  • the presence of wireless Internet;
  • good restaurant;
  • laundry;
  • pool.

In addition, hotel staff greet travelers at the airport or train station.





Prepared and entertainment for vacationers: fishing, sea voyage along the Black Sea, therapeutic massage, spa treatments. In the restaurant, which is located on the territory of the "Swallow's Nest", you can order dishes of Caucasian and European cuisine, or you can take a brazier and cook something yourself. The cost of rest in this hotel varies from two to six thousand rubles.

Millennium

Another cozy hotel located in Krinitsa (Gelendzhik) is the Millennium. This place is ideal for a quiet and comfortable family vacation. Vacationers note the convenient placement of rooms, which allows guests to practically not collide with each other. Another advantage of the guest house is the presence of arbors and barbecue facilities. The Millennium is located in the center of the village, nearby is an amazing park. Its rooms offer breathtaking views of the mountains and the Black Sea.





On the territory of the Millennium there is a convenient parking for cars, laundry, a shared kitchen for self-cooking. In addition, just next to the hotel there are three dining rooms. The guest house is only 400 meters from the beach. For vacationers there are double and triple rooms of category "Standard". Each room has:

  • air conditioning;
  • refrigerator;
  • tableware;
  • kettle;
  • TV.

The cost of rest at this Krinitsa hotel starts from 500 rubles. The Millennium is open from May to September.
