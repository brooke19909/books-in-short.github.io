Analog CCTV cameras: types. How to connect an analog camera? Scheme of an analog camera. Best analog camera

Analog cameras are used in enterprises for video surveillance of objects. Modern models are made with sensitive lenses. Most cameras are made with high conductivity. By resolution, the devices are quite different. Also, the main parameters of the cameras should include the viewing angle and focal length. Additionally, the frequency of the device is taken into account. To understand in detail the analog type cameras, it is recommended to consider the standard modification scheme.





Camera layout

The standard circuit of an analog camera involves the use of a contact lens, module and control unit. Lenses apply low and high sensitivity. Video outputs are set in different series. Thyristors for models are suitable with a frequency of 400 MHz. It is also important to note that the adapters for the cameras are installed with regulators.





Model Types

There are various types of analog cameras. By the type of installation, the models are divided into internal and external modifications. The first option is most suitable for office facilities. They use low conductivity matrices. Moreover, the viewing angle does not exceed 12 degrees. If we consider external cameras, they have a wide lens. Most devices use a wired module. The maximum viewing angle for models is 160 degrees. By design, rotary and projector modifications are distinguished.

Internal cameras

Internal analog CCTV cameras are manufactured with stands for installation. Modern models stand out for their high resolution, they use a wired modulator. The focal length of devices starts at 3.5 meters.

How to connect an internal analog camera? Some models are installed directly through a linear conductor. Extenders for modifications are selected in a positive direction.

The sensitivity parameter of the internal cameras is approximately 5.5 mV. Most devices are made with PP video output. It should also be noted that there are modifications with transition modulators. The operating frequency of the internal cameras is about 450 MHz. Most models are made with a protection system. It should also be noted that the devices differ in noise level. In some cases, cameras are connected via channel adapters.

It should also be noted that there are models with high photosensitivity. Wireless cameras are connected via a linear adapter. Comparators for devices are matched to a low directional network. If we consider a simple model with a short stand, then it is fixed through one module. Modern models are produced with speaker output. Thyristors are used to connect them.
