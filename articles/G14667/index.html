Zatoka, "Cardinal" (recreation center): photos and reviews

Zatoka is a well-known center of tourism, relaxation and recreation in Ukraine. The resort is located 60 km southwest of Odessa. Zatoka beaches , characterized by unusually comfortable conditions for relaxation (sandy, wide, with a comfortable gently sloping seabed) stretch along the spit that divided the Dniester estuary and the Black Sea. In Zatoka, there are many recreation centers adapted for various types of leisure activities. Rest House "Cardinal" (Zatoka) is one of the most popular holiday destinations in this resort.





About the resort

Tourists who have chosen the Kardinal (Zatoka) recreation center to spend their vacations leave truly enthusiastic reviews about their stay here. It will be useful to get acquainted with them for those who are planning their vacation and are at a crossroads, not knowing where to go in the summer. Potential guests of the resort will need to learn about the features of local recreation.

The inflow is a sand spit (length 20 km), which is washed by the salty waters of the Black Sea, the freshwater Dniester and the bitterly salty Shabolatsky estuaries. Conventionally, the region is divided into 3 districts: Central (in which the resort village is directly located), Limansky and Solnechny. The districts got their name from the name of the railway stations.

The presence of a mild, warm climate, sea breeze, healing mud, gentle sun, convenient transport access, magnificent beaches and developed infrastructure every season attract thousands of tourists to Zatoka. Families with children especially like to come here. Mostly the holiday season here begins in mid-May and lasts until the beginning of autumn. But for those who want to relax in the off-season, Zatoka is open throughout the year.

The recreation center "Cardinal" (the photo in the article represents the territory and rooms of the pension) is appreciated by many tourists.

Nutrition

Vacationers of the Cardinal camp site in a cozy two-story dining room are provided with three meals a day (meals per day: 220 UAH). There is a convenient mode for guests and the ability to select dishes. Schedule: from 9 to 19 (daily, without a break).





Tasty, wholesome and hearty dishes are prepared in the dining room: Ukrainian borsch with donuts, dumplings, fish soup, Bulgarian milina, cabbage rolls in grape leaves, Moldavian mamalyga, etc. Odessa cuisine is distinguished by the use of a large number of fruits and vegetables, Danube fish, hot sauces and seasoning. It combines the trends of Ukrainian, Russian, Jewish, Georgian, Armenian, Uzbek, Bulgarian, Moldavian and Greek culinary traditions.
