The right smartwatch

Many buyers focus on fitness trackers, so first you need to understand what SmartWatch is and how they differ from a fitness or sports tracker. SmartWatch is a technology that connects to a custom smartphone. This device usually has a touch screen, various applications that it supports, for example, it tracks steps and heart rate.

There are several different key features that SmartWatch selects in order to best select the best Apple Watch counterparts:

  1. Health tracking, for example, Fitbit.
  2. Compatibility. You must first make sure that the watch that interested the buyer works with your smartphone. For example, Apple watches only work with iPhone.
  3. Applications Preliminarily get acquainted with the programs installed on the watch, and also study their user ratings.
  4. Notifications and messages. All smart watches notify the user, but only some of them allow the user to answer messages and calls.
  5. The battery life of the best Apple Watch counterparts can range from a few days to several months. Many of them depend on the installed functions, for example, GPS and heart rate monitoring will use the battery faster.
  6. Design. For many consumers, the design and style of smart cards is a key factor in their choice. Many SmartWatches look just like a regular watch, some of them are sports, but this is secondary. Good looks do not compensate for a clumsy interface or buttons that are difficult to operate.

Minimalistic Motorola Design

The original Moto 360, an analogue of the Apple Watch for Android, remains on the list of favorite smartwatches. A recently released version that is more suitable for sports users. The original Moto 360 is currently manufactured by Lenovo, which acquired Motorola several years ago. The model is built for people who prefer to stay active and have a lot of fun. The watch is welcomed by users due to the large number of functions, although the design is still rather weak. The Moto 360 Sport is, above all, a screen with the amazing technology that Motorola brings to the AnyLight display.

When the user looks at the watch under ordinary light, it looks like on a standard LCD screen. However, as soon as the watch is idle or there is direct light on the front side, the screen turns into a transreflective display, similar to a Pebble Time watch. This function ensures that the battery life is saved and the user can easily and simply find out the time and date.

Speaking of batteries, they are not as good as expected. Of course, SmartWatches use smaller batteries compared to other devices, they are efficient enough to power the watch for several days. Unfortunately, this is not the case with the Motorola Moto 360 Sport. At full charge, the Moto 360 Sport runs all day or a little more. This is not suitable for many people, because they will have to charge their devices before bed every day.
