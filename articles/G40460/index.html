Ski resorts in Slovenia: advantages, prices and reviews

Slovenia is a unique state, equal in area to half of the Moscow region. However, when you come here, you can admire the beauties of the Adriatic Sea, the proud Alps, crystal clear lakes and dense forests. From December to March, tourists from all over the world flock here to spend holidays in the mountains, go skiing and forget about the routine of a familiar life. Ski resorts in Slovenia are widely known among lovers of an active lifestyle. Affordable prices, polite staff, comfortable rooms - thanks to this, skiers and snowboarders come here again and again. In this article we will tell you all about the ski resorts of Slovenia. Advantages, prices and reviews of tourists will also be reflected in this material.



Kranjska Gora

If you choose one of the three resorts located in this part of the country, you will never regret it. Kranjska Gora, Podkoren and Planica are ideal for families. There are many slopes designed for children and beginner skiers. You can also learn the basics of mountain climbing, play tennis on the indoor court or spend time in the gym. At the disposal of those people who just want to have fun, there are many cafes and restaurants, billiards, a casino, a cabaret and a disco.

Fans of winter sports come here for typical alpine landscapes, steep slopes and unique local flavor. In this part of the country, winter lasts four or five months, and a short summer flies by unnoticed. Due to the mild climate, Slovenian ski resorts located in this place are particularly popular. Luxury hotels have long been chosen not only by Russian tourists, but also by guests from Germany and Switzerland.



Maribor Pohorje

The largest ski resort in the country is located near the Austrian border. Among its main advantages are the developed tourist infrastructure, the possibility of renting sports equipment, as well as training with experienced instructors of the skiing and snowboarding school. In addition, slopes are equipped here for athletes of any level, which means it will be comfortable for both amateurs and professionals. Maribor Pohorje offers its services to tourists with different levels of income, as, indeed, all other ski resorts in Slovenia. Prices here are much lower than in neighboring alpine countries, and the quality of service is in no way inferior.

This resort will have a great time for those who are not indifferent to spa treatments. Using the offers of the Terme Maribor thermal center, you can freely visit swimming pools, saunas, a solarium, a Turkish bath, as well as undergo diagnostics and get the modern treatment you need. Fans of extreme sports will enjoy unforgettable descents with burning torches, night skating and hot air ballooning.



Bled

Every winter, the surroundings of the beautiful lake turn into Mecca for fans of winter sports. Local tracks are designed more for beginner skiers and snowboarders than for professionals. You can get to the ski center by a free bus from the city center, and the price of the ski pass includes a ticket for the skating rink at the Sports Palace and the entrance to the Blaise Castle.

Bohinj

The most famous ski slopes of Slovenia, located near the beautiful Lake Bohinj, are called Vogel, Kobla and Sorishka. Couples with children and lovers of a relaxing holiday prefer to go to these places. Here you can study at a cross-country ski school or take lessons at a ski school, which, incidentally, is considered one of the best in Slovenia. In Vogel you can go sledding, snowboarding, take part in night skating or virgin snowboarding. Here you will not find stormy entertainments and you will not be able to walk in plenty at night clubs. But you will have a great time with your family, take a sleigh ride on the lake, try local wines and, if you're lucky, become a spectator of the cross-country skiing World Cup.



Bovec

Ski resort Kanin (Slovenia) is located on the highest slopes of the mountain of the same name. Its tracks are located at an altitude of more than 2000 meters and quite often there are competitions in winter sports. Skiers and snowboarders come here to relax and have fun from early December to late April. They are delivered to the ski area by cable car, and the hotel can be reached by bus or taxi. If you want variety, then you can try the mountain slopes of neighboring countries. For example, from here it is only 25 km to the Italian resort Sella Nevea, and 45 km to the Austrian Arnoldstein. If you are a happy holder of a Schengen visa, you can purchase a ski pass and enjoy skiing from the mountains in different countries.

Slovenia. Ski resort trails

Before you go to this country and enjoy a winter holiday, carefully study the types of tracks that this or that resort offers. It is very important to evaluate your real level of skiing, so that your vacation goes the way you dreamed about it. All ski resorts in Slovenia describe in detail on their sites the quantity and quality of pistes that they have. They also indicate their length, level of difficulty and offer the services of an instructor for training beginners. Do not neglect this information and then yours will be able to avoid possible injuries, and your vacation will not be overshadowed by any surprises.



Prices

It is known that guests from neighboring European countries often come to this country. As a rule, Germans and Swiss, famous all over the world for their ability to count money, choose the ski resorts of Slovenia for the winter holidays . The advantages, prices and reviews of compatriots attract economical citizens of Europe here, despite the fact that they always have their own mountainsides at their disposal.

Russian tourists who choose this country for vacation, also note the moderate cost of accommodation and food. A room in a three-star hotel in the capital can be rented for 70 - 100 euros per day, and lunch for two in a restaurant for 25 - 40 euros. Traveling by public transport in the city will cost one euro, and taxi drivers charge a fee on the meter. You can easily calculate your future expenses by compiling a cultural and sightseeing program yourself or using the services of a travel agency employee.



Slovenia. Ski resorts. Reviews

This small and comfortable country is not yet very popular among our compatriots. But everyone who has been here at least once plans to come here again and again. The friendliness of the locals, beautiful nature, delicious local cuisine and a large selection of entertainment won the hearts of Russians. Many say that the ski resorts in Slovenia are in no way inferior to the European ones. And besides, tourists have at their disposal spa centers, which are available in almost every resort.
