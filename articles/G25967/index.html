Developing a child: from toilet paper crafts

Today, you can make beauty out of anything, there are simply no boundaries, you just need imagination. So, even toilet paper can be a great helper. But what can be made of it?





About the pros

Do not be afraid of toilet paper if you want to do something yourself. After all, she has a lot of advantages. The toilet paper is soft, it tears easily, and a ball or a regular tube can be rolled out of it without problems. In addition, it is also quite cheap. And it is quite easily and reliably painted with ordinary children's paints. So why not craft it from such a subspecies of paper?

For the smallest

It is worth noting that, from toilet paper, crafts are primarily good if mom wants to work out with a small child. Even a one and a half year old baby can cope with it! So, from the toilet paper you can make a Christmas tree. It will take a base from a denser material, say, from thick paper or cardboard, to make it better in the form of a cone. Toilet paper should be torn in strips of about 5 cm long and 3-4 cm wide. Strips of paper need to be bent in half - these will be Christmas tree branches. Now, using ordinary PVA glue, they need to be glued to the base, strip after strip. Everything, the Christmas tree is ready! It remains only to paint it with gouache green. What else can I do from toilet paper crafts? Why not put a snowman under the Christmas tree? To do this, you will need to roll up three balls of paper and put them on top of each other. That's all the work! It is worth noting that such activities with the baby perfectly develop the motor skills of the hands of the child.





Caterpillar

From toilet paper crafts can be done by creating a fun caterpillar. To do this, you will need to roll scraps of toilet paper into balls of the same size and glue them one by one on the paper. The first lump will be the head of the caterpillar, on it you can draw eyes and attach horns from the same toilet paper, only twisted into a thin tube. The rest can be painted in different colors. The caterpillar is ready! By the same principle, you can create many different three-dimensional paintings.

Pen holder

There are also crafts from toilet rolls. It will be very simple to make a stand for pens and pencils. The roll will serve as the basis, which will also need to be decorated. You can do this in various ways. You can use the same toilet paper by gluing the stand with small balls. You can also wrap a roll of toilet paper so that you can bend the edges of the paper - you get such a Christmas tree stand. You can glue a roll of paper under the fabric, it will also look good.





Animals

Little animals - excellent crafts from toilet paper. Here you will need not only paper, but also a roll - the remainder of the toilet paper. It will be he who will serve as the basis, the body of the animal. It will be well painted with ordinary paints. Now, from paper, you need to make eyes, ears and nose-mouths of animals, paint them and glue them. Crafts are ready!

Garland

More toilet paper crafts: why not make an ordinary garland? Here you will need to roll toilet paper into balls, dye them, stretch the threads through them and that's it - the garland is ready. By the same principle, you can make a garland from rolls of toilet paper: the rolls will need to be cut into small circles, which are stained and hung on strings.
