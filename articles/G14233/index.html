What is a phablet?

A hybrid mobile device, a cross between an ordinary smartphone and a tablet computer - this is probably what this type of phones can be called, which by its technical characteristics can be attributed to a special class of mobile devices. However, the diagonal of the screen of the phablet is still somewhat smaller than that of the Tablet PC, which, in general, is a huge plus at the time of making or receiving calls. Let's agree: regular head-end communication devices , as well as the use of additional technical elements, do not count. The advantage of the phablet is obvious - it can be controlled with one hand, and this is one of its main advantages!
