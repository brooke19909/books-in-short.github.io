In Gelendzhik in November: is it worth it to go

Gelendzhik is a resort beloved by many Russians in the Krasnodar Territory. Despite the enormous popularity of fashionable foreign resorts, many still can not give up the usual resorts of the Motherland. Moreover, over the past few years they have changed beyond recognition: new hotels and hotels on the coast have been rebuilt, a lot of entertainments, fascinating excursion programs have appeared. Well, of course, rest in the resorts of the Krasnodar Territory still remains affordable for most of the inhabitants of our country, which certainly can not be said about foreign resorts.

Summer and early autumn are a great time for relaxing at sea, but what if you decided to go to Gelendzhik in November? What type of vacation will you get in this case? We will try to evaluate all the advantages and disadvantages of rest in such a cool season in the resort of the Krasnodar Territory in the framework of this material.





Is it possible

Holidays in Gelendzhik in early November and even in the middle of the month are possible, but only if you are prepared for it correctly is something that every modern tourist should remember. If you can afford to rest only in such a cool time of the year - do not give up the sea. Currently, the resort is developing, in the near future it will be ready for year-round recreation.

Housing

Many people are accustomed to the fact that in the Krasnodar Territory the season lasts from mid-May to mid-September - it is at this time of the year that all hotels, inns and motels are literally crowded with vacationers. And what is the situation in November? Perhaps renting a room or a modest apartment is getting a little easier?





Most of the apartments and guest houses after the end of the season are rented for a long time until the onset of the new beach season. That is why it is unlikely that you will be able to find something for your stay. It would seem: there are practically no tourists, but housing problems still exist. The only option for those who travel to Gelendzhik in November are motels, boarding houses and hotels operating all year round.

Perhaps the most preferred option is precisely the sanatoriums and boarding houses, which combine the delights of relaxation and treatment, thereby avoiding boredom. The presence of a private pool in the boarding house or sanatorium will compensate for the impossibility of swimming in the sea.

Weather

The weather in Gelendzhik in November is no less exciting topic for tourists. Nobody wants to come to rest all the time to sit in the room, wrapping themselves in a blanket or plaid. It is important to understand that the weather at this time of year, however, as in summer, is unpredictable in this region. You can control all weather changes, view the forecast every day, but no one will ever give guarantees that it will be justified. This is perhaps the main drawback of the resorts of the Krasnodar Territory, and at any time of the year.





Most often, the sun is shining, the sea is cool, but it is hardly likely to call such weather comfortable and pleasant. The air temperature on average is kept at 15-17 degrees Celsius, the water temperature - 12-13 degrees. Agree, not the most comfortable conditions for a beach holiday, but even at this time you can enjoy the sea air, the beauty of the sea.

Entertainment

What is he: Gelendzhik in November? Reviews of tourists emphasize that November is a great opportunity to visit all the attractions of Gelendzhik, for which there is no time and effort in the hot summer. Perhaps the only exceptions are mountains and waterfalls. Moreover, you will have to visit the sights on your own, as the Gelendzhik poster in November, and from the first day, is deprived of excursion programs.





You can get to interesting places not only by private car, but also using public transport - most importantly, read traveler reviews in advance and plan your mini-travel program. If you came to Gelendzhik in November with children, be sure to visit the Amusement Park, Dolphinarium, Oceanarium. You can rest assured: not only children, but also adults will get a tremendous journey. The house is upside down, Safari Park - those tourist attractions that are also open to visitors in such a cool season.

Nutrition

At the end of the season, most of the cafes and restaurants in Gelendzhik buries. But even in the middle of November, not a single tourist will remain hungry here. You can eat in the dining rooms, cafes, designed for local residents. Do not forget about shops, modern supermarkets, where you can buy the necessary products and prepare breakfast, lunch or dinner on your own.

In November, the menu prices in open cafes are very affordable, lunch for a family of three people will cost an average of 800 - 1000 rubles.





Prices

Significantly reduced prices for accommodation, food and entertainment are perhaps the main advantage of a holiday in Gelendzhik after the end of the holiday season. On average, prices fall by 30-40%, which is confirmed by numerous reviews of tourists and vacationers.

To summarize

You can really go to Gelendzhik in November, even with young children. You must immediately prepare yourself for the fact that you are unlikely to swim in the sea, unless you are wintering. Provided that you have a competent approach to recreation and plan it in advance, you can organize good leisure time for yourself and your family. But, of course, given the fact that Gelendzhik is a resort city, it is better to relax here in season to fully enjoy the quality of a beach holiday, enrich your body with vitamins. We hope that our tips and tricks will help you plan your vacation in November in the resort of Krasnodar Krai.
