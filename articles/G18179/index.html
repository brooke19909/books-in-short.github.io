Sanatorium "Lermontovo" ("Surgutneftegas"): treatment and rest on the Black Sea

According to the official website of the Lermontovo Sanatorium (Surgutneftegas), it is part of the Surgut Health Trust. This sanatorium resort has an excellent location - on the Black Sea coast of the Krasnodar Territory, in the village of Olginka, Tuapse District. The coastline runs just 150 meters from the resort.

Anyone can come for rest and treatment to the Lermontovo sanatorium, regardless of whether he is an employee of Surgutneftegas or not.

Sanatorium infrastructure

The structure of the sanatorium "Lermontovo" "Surgutneftegaz" includes interconnected buildings of a medical center and two 7-storey buildings, which accommodate vacationers, leisure and food centers. From the hill on which the sanatorium complex is located, a magnificent panorama of the Black Sea opens, mountains and forests are visible. The territory of "Lermontovo" also pleases the eye with green spaces and wonderful bright colors.

The food center provides vacationers with three meals a day on the basis of the buffet. The menu includes diet dishes, fresh vegetable salads, fruits and a wide selection of drinks. Service is carried out by waiters. For guests of the suites there is a separate room with an expanded menu.





It has its own pebble beach resort "Lermontovo" ("Surgutneftegas"). On the map it is very clearly visible. The beach area is 4800 square meters. m. Vacationers are provided with sun loungers. The promenade along the beach is equipped with showers, there are fountains with drinking water everywhere, there are changing cabins, as well as 4 solariums.





Wellness treatments

Surgutneftegaz sanatorium Lermontovo is visited by people of different ages, including children aged 1 year and over, to receive quality treatment.

Here patients are treated with diseases of the musculoskeletal system, cardiovascular, endocrine, peripheral nervous system, and organs of the gastrointestinal tract. In addition, concomitant diseases are treated.

To successfully treat the guests of the sanatorium, the following methods are used:

  • physiotherapy;
  • mud therapy;
  • hydrotherapy;
  • manual therapy;
  • acupuncture;
  • laser therapy;
  • massotherapy.

The healing effect is enhanced after visiting the vibrosauna and gym physiotherapy. At the request of patients, they can be provided with dental and cosmetic services.





The number of rooms of the sanatorium

Sanatorium "Lermontovo" is able to accommodate 756 people in residential premises. Vacationers can choose for themselves suitable single or double standard rooms or suites.

The standard rooms have a comfortable bed, shower and balcony. The premises are equipped with necessary appliances for a comfortable stay: split system, TV, telephone, as well as a refrigerator, electric kettle, dishes.

The suites are large in size and consist of a living room with a sofa bed, a bedroom with a double bed and a bathroom, a balcony. There is also a standard set of household appliances, bath and hygiene items, an iron with an ironing board and clothes care products are additionally provided.

The cost of a permit, which includes accommodation in a room, treatment and three meals a day, depends on the time of year: from the third decade of May to mid-September, it increases by an average of 25%.

To treatment - time, fun - the remaining hours

The resort has a volleyball and basketball court, several tennis courts, a gym, an indoor pool, and a gym.

Those who prefer intellectual relaxation can play billiards or visit the library. The work of the children's room is organized for children, experienced teachers are engaged in them, there are playgrounds.

On your own beach for vacationers, there are rental points for water sports, badminton, beach volleyball. Those who wish can ride on a boat or get an unforgettable experience of the attractions "Monsoon" and "Banana".

In the evenings, the resort has cafes and bars, animated performances are held. For holidaymakers, sightseeing tours are organized to familiarize themselves with local attractions.

Sanatorium Lermontovo (Surgutneftegas): reviews

Vacationers who have acquired permits to the sanatorium "Lermontovo" remain very pleased with the time spent here. They note the high level of service, the excellent work of animators who try to organize wonderful leisure for children and adults. The quality of the food and the size of the portions were also praised by connoisseurs of delicious food.





The beauty of the local nature, the cleanliness of the Black Sea and the coastal strip, the magnificent well-maintained beach, the sincere staff, the high quality of wellness procedures - all this awaits those who are thinking about where you can use your health benefits and have a fun long-awaited vacation.

Come to the Lermontovo sanatorium - here you will forget about workdays and recharge your batteries for a whole year! It is possible that after a well-spent vacation you will return to Olginka more than once.
