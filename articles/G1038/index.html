Rest in Abkhazia, in Pitsunda: reviews and photos

Abkhazia is one of the most popular countries for summer vacations among our compatriots. The names of local resorts are known to everyone. Pitsunda, Gagra, Sukhumi, Gudauta - from these words alone it seems to become warmer and sunnier. Today, these resorts delight not only with a pleasant climate and diverse nature, but also with modern infrastructure. Holidays in Abkhazia, in Pitsunda, will certainly justify all your expectations and will be remembered for a long time, if you prepare for it and organize everything correctly.

Pitsunda - the main health resort of Abkhazia and a modern resort



The city of Pitsunda is located on the cape of the same name, washed by the waters of the Black Sea. In the tourist season it is difficult to believe that there are only about 4 thousand people here. Usually from mid-March the resort is filled with a large number of guests from other countries. Rest in Abkhazia, in Pitsunda, will help to improve health and gain strength. There is a unique microclimate, because the city, standing on the coast, is surrounded by relict pine forests. This city is considered the capital of the Abkhaz health resorts, it is here that there are a lot of sanatoriums in which treatment with local mineral waters and healing mud is practiced. There are many modern and traditional resort entertainment.

Climatic features of the region

What month to plan a vacation in Abkhazia, in Pitsunda? The tourist season lasts from May to the end of October, but, of course, the largest number of vacationers comes to the resort in the summer months. The climate in Abkhazia is subtropical, with mild winters. Therefore, in recent years, New Year tours to this country have become increasingly popular. The city is located in a unique place - major storms and very high waves do not happen here in principle. And these are excellent conditions for a family vacation. Fresh and healthy air, always calm and warm sea. Abkhazia (Pitsunda) is a great place to relax at any time of the year. Therefore, when going on vacation, you can not worry about the weather: the most unpleasant thing than it can surprise you is a short rain. What to do in Pitsunda?



The beaches at this resort are sand and pebble. Many motels have their own closed access to the sea. On the common city beach today, there are also all conditions for a comfortable stay. This shower, changing cabins, umbrellas and sunbeds. In addition, traditional tourist attractions are available on all beaches: water skiing, banana, catamarans. Also enough at the resort are mini water parks. Pitsunda has a large number of different catering establishments, bars, discos and nightclubs. Do not forget that Abkhazia is a small country, and if you wish, you can always go to a nearby major city for a tour or to visit some major entertainment center.

Sights and excursions



Do not be too lazy to leave the beach for a few hours and instead of going to the cinema find time for exploring the local monuments. The most famous sights of Pitsunda are the lighthouse, the organ and the Pitsunda temple. There is a local history museum in the city, where you can learn more about the history of this settlement and its life at different times. Holidays in Abkhazia, in Pitsunda, will be more interesting if you take the time to visit the ruins of an ancient fortress and dolmens. In addition, you can always go on a one-day excursion to the most famous sights of the country. This is Lake Ritsa, Stalin's cottage, New Athos monastery, caves in New Athos. Such trips are offered by many travel agencies and agencies.

Are There Five Star Hotels In Pitsunda?



The tourist infrastructure of the resort is still in a stage of rapid development. If you are used to ultra-modern overseas hotels, most likely, even the newest and most expensive Pitsunda complexes will not seem good enough to you. However, if your goal is to live in good living conditions and eat quality, you can easily find the suitable option. The largest pensions in the outskirts of the city are Boxwood Grove, Pitius, Litefond, Lakoba Musser. All of them have good infrastructure and offer their guests not only accommodation, but also treatment, as well as a host of additional services. If you plan to relax in a city such as Pitsunda, hotels should be chosen not only in terms of comfort, but also considering the distance from the sea. Be sure to specify this condition when booking.

Pitsunda: Hotels, motels, guest houses



It is simply impossible to count all medium and small hotels in the city and its environs. Do not forget that tourism is one of the most important areas of activity of the local population. In coastal towns, a significant percentage of the population lives, offering various services and products to vacationers. Therefore, new cafes and mini-hotels in Pitsunda open every year. The cost of living in guest houses and resort complexes starts from 450-500 rubles per day per person. You can easily find the guest house that is right for you. Pitsunda offers more than enough accommodation options in this category. Many small hotels delight their customers with the possibility of catering and some additional services: visits to the gym, sauna or mini-cinema.

Abkhazia Pitsunda: Private Sector



In any tourist town, it is not difficult to find offers for rental housing from the owners. Pitsunda is no exception: the city has modern apartment buildings, but still the main buildings are low-rise. Housing in the private sector of this city has a lot of advantages and its own special romance. The first advantage of this accommodation option is its low cost. Today you can rent a room in the house for only 200-300 rubles per day per person. At the same time, finding an option without intermediaries is not difficult at all, almost every house has corresponding announcements, and many private owners start offering their housing at the railway station in Adler or immediately after the border.

Not everywhere you will be offered a sauna or billiards, if this is not a full-fledged guest house. Pitsunda is a tourist city, so you can easily find any entertainment facilities on its streets. But if you settle in a private house, you will always be treated with a natural product for a nominal fee, and in the courtyard you can fry the barbecue.

Residents of Abkhazia are incredibly hospitable. If you wish, renting housing from private owners, you can agree on food or discuss the conditions for using the kitchen. Friendly communication and completely free stories about local legends and sights are a gift.

If your vacation destination is Abkhazia (Pitsunda), the private sector is an inexpensive and very good accommodation option. Before settling, do not be too lazy to learn more about the proposed living conditions and the remoteness of the selected house from the sea. What is especially convenient, many private traders today, on the eve of the holiday season, advertise on the Internet and offer vacationers a reservation.

Holiday Prices



As a place to spend a vacation, Abkhazia is often compared with the Russian Krasnodar Territory. Indeed, there are many things in common, but there are significant differences. It is believed that holidays in Abkhazia can be spent much cheaper, while not denying yourself quality food and entertainment. Housing in the private sector costs from 200 rubles per day, and in hotels and pensions - from 500-700 rubles.

But is really really much cheaper than Abkhazia in everything? Pitsunda offers vacationers different prices. For example, cafes and restaurants in the city have various classes. Accordingly, lunch in an inexpensive institution will cost 250-400 rubles, and a full dinner in a good restaurant - from 800-1000 rubles per person. Entertainment is also available, many sights can be viewed for free. An excursion as part of a group from Pitsunda will cost about 1000-1500 rubles per person, often this amount includes lunch or a visit to an apiary / winery with product tasting.

What do tourists say about their vacation?

Abkhazia (Pitsunda) reviews are mostly positive. Many tourists prefer to return here year after year or discover new places on the map of their beloved state. No wonder they say that Abkhazia is a country of the soul. Having been here once, it is impossible to remain indifferent. The local landscapes are amazing, the hospitality of the local people is surprising day after day, and the gentle sea and various resort attractions make the vacation full and especially interesting.

Very rarely, Abkhazia (Pitsunda) receives negative reviews. Vacationers usually remain dissatisfied with a particular hotel or other establishment. And this is an additional reason to take your vacation planning as seriously and responsibly as possible.
