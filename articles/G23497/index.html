Historic beach with crystal clear water: Blue Bay (Sevastopol)

Blue Bay is one of the most beautiful places in Sevastopol. From the name it becomes clear that this place is famous for its crystal clear blue water. At the weekend, the townspeople get out of the house to spend their holidays on the small beach that the Blue Bay gives them. Sevastopol, or rather its center, is far enough away from these places, which does not prevent vacationers from completely filling the cozy beach with warm summer days.

general information





Blue Bay (Sevastopol) is a fairly crowded place, so many old-timers recommend not going here at the height of the tourist season. Only then can you relax and enjoy the beauty of the crystal clear Crimean sea, which gently washes the coast.

Now the Blue Bay is constantly being built up, whole blocks are being built for vacationers. Private traders kindly inviting visitors to an overnight stay did not go too far. Therefore, in this cozy bay lately has always been so crowded.

History





Despite all the beauty of its landscape, the place is also famous for its sad events. The Great Patriotic War did not pass by many picturesque places, among which was the Blue Bay. The Germans tried to capture Sevastopol in 1942, under their pressure, most civilians and wounded defenders of the city came to the bay with the hope of evacuation. However, they were out of luck. As a result, almost eighty thousand people were killed or captured.

To honor their memory, a memorial to the memory and a museum, which can be visited by any vacationer, was erected at the location of the thirty-fifth battery. Many of the visitors leave this memorable place with tears in their eyes.

In addition to the war, this beautiful corner of nature was remembered by people thanks to the shooting of the painting Amphibian Man.

Beach





As mentioned above, the beach got its name precisely because of the crystal clearness of its waters. Each visitor can see the turquoise clear water, which meets everyone Blue Bay (Sevastopol). Photos of these places always attract the eye with the beauty of nature. Immediately I want to be on this beach and plunge headlong into the alluring transparency of sea waters.

On all sides, the beach is surrounded by high cliffs, which on the side are more like a beautiful layered cake. Everyone can walk over a cliff, but for this it is best to take along special shoes and be careful.

In season, a lot of families come here with tents, which are literally attracted by the Blue Bay. Sevastopol is very close to these amazing places, so here you can often see the townspeople.

Due to the small size of the beach, putting up a tent here does not work. But you can bring the car almost to the beginning of the beach to leave everything you need in it.
On both sides of the beach are rocks from which you can dive into the water. There are also small recesses in which you can bathe your children and not be afraid that they will get hurt or float to the depths.

To get to this wonderful place, you need to take the bus that goes through Sevastopol. Blue Bay, reviews indicate this, is always very popular. That is why so many vacationers from all over Russia come here.
