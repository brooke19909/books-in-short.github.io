DIY clothes patterns for dachshunds (photo)

Often on the street you can meet a friendly couple: the owner and his funny-dressed dog ... Seeing this picture, someone wants to dress up their pet. There are, of course, shops selling all kinds of outfits that can be worn on dogs. And you can dress up your pet yourself. DIY clothing for dachshunds, the patterns of which are the simplest, will protect your dog from wind, rain and frost.

Dog fashion

Dachshund is a hunting dog. In nature, she does not care wind, dirt, dampness. But, having turned into a pet, she lost these qualities. Having a short coat and spending most of the time warm, when it goes outside, it starts to freeze. In order to protect their pet from the cold, dog owners, including dachshunds, came up with dog fashion.



A loving owner will collect a whole wardrobe for his pet in any weather. Here you can find:

  • a blanket that protects against frost and wind;
  • boots needed when walking in severe frost;
  • jumpsuit that saves from rain and wind;
  • knitted vest for fall or spring.

Dog outfits are not cheap. At the same time, at home there are enough failed things from which you can make a new thing for your favorite.

To please a pet with a convenient and necessary outfit, it is enough to make patterns of clothes for dachshunds with their own hands. And then you can sew elegant overalls, vests, blankets on them.

Clothing for dachshunds

Today to buy things to dachshunds is not a problem. In stores you can find everything: from a simple jumpsuit to an evening dress coat. Often they look like movie characters. At the price, such clothes are also produced in different ways: from simple cufflinks to shirts and dresses with rhinestones and precious stones. But most dog owners prefer to sew outfits for their four-legged friend on their own.



Do-it-yourself clothing for a dachshund, patterns, knitting of which is presented in our article, will be made on your favorite dog, taking into account the characteristics of his body. Such things will help to emphasize the beauty and grace of the pet and, at the same time, protect the weather from troubles.

DIY patterns for dachshunds can be used by designing products for different seasons. For example, on the basis of overalls with the size of a pet, you can sew an autumn and winter outfit.

Clothes selection

Each breed of dog has its own characteristics of the structure of the body. In addition, there are short-haired and long-haired dogs. All this must be taken into account when sewing outfits for a pet.

Owing to the length of the body, clothes for dachshunds with their own hands also have their own peculiarities: patterns, photos show these features very clearly. Everything that is sewn for dachshund has a large length in the back, and this should be taken into account when cutting.

In addition, the dachshund has very short legs, so spring-autumn dresses should be made of waterproof fabric.

The creation of any thing begins with the construction of a pattern. Therefore, starting to create outfits, you must first take measurements from your pet.

To build a pattern, you need to measure the length of the back, the length below from the front to the hind legs, chest circumference, chest width, height of the pet at the withers and neck circumference. Having completed all measurements correctly, you can sew any clothes.



Knitted patterns

The creation of such things is suitable for lovers of knitting. The principles here are the same, the main thing is to take measurements correctly.

Knitted blouses, hats, coats - all this clothing for a dachshund with their own hands. Patterns and knitting of individual details on them will greatly facilitate the work, allow you to create a thing that will sit well on the pet and protect it from wind and cold.

Knitwear, as well as sewn, require carefully taken measurements from a four-legged friend.

Here is a simple dachshund sweater. This is only knitting technology, the number of loops is calculated based on the standards of the dog.



Knitting begins with the neck (measure neck circumference). Then, using the raglan method, divide the loops into the back, chest and mitts. The breast must first be expanded (measure the width of the chest), then narrowed, and it should be longer than the back so that the sweater fits well. When we connect the back and chest, holes are formed for the sleeves. We knit them separately. We knit the body to the desired length (measure the length of the back), reducing loops on the tummy so that the knit fits snugly to the body. You can put on a hood. In such a sweater, a pet can walk in dry weather. If you use cotton threads, you get a summer outfit.

Outfits for the dachshund boy

Warm things for the dog - protection from the weather, the pet should be comfortable in it, nothing should prevent it from moving. And since the pattern of clothes for a dachshund-boy with his own hands is made according to individual sizes, then it will meet all these requirements.

Same-sex dogs can start fights, so dog owners have learned to show the floor of a dog in their outfits. Things for a dachshund boy will differ in color: they are darker (blue, gray, black). And of course, there are differences in the cutting of material, taking into account the anatomical features of boys who relieve themselves differently than girls.

Patterns of clothes for dachshunds with their own hands, on which things for boys will be sewn, will turn out to be larger, since males are more females. And jewelry decorations in the form of rhinestones, bows and ruffles are more suitable for girls' clothes, and not for boys.



There are differences in style. For a boy, you can sew a tuxedo, a shirt with a tie, and wear a stylish cap.
