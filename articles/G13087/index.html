Sanatorium "Pearl of the Sea" (Kabardinka): description, rest and reviews

Gelendzhik for many years occupies a leading position in the tourism industry. Numerous villages adjoin the city, which are distinguished by tranquility and affordable relaxation. These include Kabardinka - a paradise, sheltered from the winds by the mountains and surrounded by the azure Black Sea.

The modern resort is focused on youth and children. Over the past few years, many comfortable hotels, hotels, camps and recreational facilities have been built here, where you can undergo a full course of therapy, get a portion of good mood and recharge with positive energy. Also in the village remained and old resorts that were founded in the Soviet years.

Such is the sanctuary "Pearl of the Sea" (Kabardinka), combining active and wellness holidays. Convenient location relative to the station, city center, attractions and the beach line allows tourists to spend their holidays in a positive, interesting and productive way.

general information





No wonder the sunny Kabardinka is positioned as a recreational resort . Sanatorium "Pearl of the Sea" is a vivid confirmation of this. An amazing combination of the cleanest air, saturated with volatile products, the sandy coast and multifaceted leisure activities make this place unforgettable for a memorable vacation. The prophylaxis has been over 80 years old, and during its existence high officials of the Russian state have visited it.

The main advantage of the boarding house in its location is on the spacious territory of the subtropical fragrant garden. The air here is enriched with iodine and ozone, grace and pristine nature are everywhere - all this has a beneficial effect on every cell of our body and heals.

There are relaxation areas with gazebos and benches. There are equipped sports fields, various cafeterias, a private amusement park, a medical facility and an open-air museum, where ancient architectural structures are presented. In addition, a children's camp and entertainment complexes border the health resort on the same territory.

Residence





The resort of Kabardinka is famous for its comfortable accommodation. Pension "Pearl of the Sea" takes care of the guests, so it offers accommodation in new three-story cottages, in which standard rooms with a private bathroom and bath are concentrated. Those who wish can check into the superior apartments.





Some buildings have balconies overlooking the green park area. All rooms have air conditioning. It offers compact refrigerators and necessary furniture (bed, nightstand). A clamshell is available on request - for a fee. Children under 4 years old are not charged. As for the service, it is carried out according to a certain schedule.

Catering





A restaurant with a large hall was recently built. Meals are organized as a buffet - three times a day. In the boarding house "Pearl of the Sea" (Kabardinka) there is a karaoke bar, and in warm time - a cafeteria. Coastline





The health resort "Pearl of the Sea" (Kabardinka) has its own beach . Reviews from tourists about the cleanliness and landscaping of the coast are generally positive. People liked its close proximity - just 50 meters from residential buildings.

Pleasantly pleased that the coast is quite long. There are beach amenities (shaded umbrellas, deck chairs) and water activities (scooter, catamarans, banana, jet skis). Drink stalls are open along the shore.
