Wall-mounted TVs: mounting features, types of holders and reviews

Modern plasma panels or flat-panel LCD televisions ergonomically fit into most of the interiors of our apartments due to the original design, variety of colors and layout. Almost all such screens can be mounted on a stand or hung on a wall using a special mount.

The decision to buy a wall stand for TV and fix it on the wall is an excellent option to get rid of annoying nightstands and consoles, as well as create an original design solution and save space in the apartment. This arrangement will also allow you to set the screen under the desired angle at a comfortable height. Thus, you can block the access of young children to the technique. Often, the abundance of types, sizes and design features of fasteners causes some difficulties.

Consider the features of mounting wall-mounted TVs and the types of holders in this article.

Types of Holders

  • Fixed - the easiest holder that takes up almost no space. The inability to adjust and the design cause a fairly low cost for this type of device.
  • Inclined type is a fairly common part. It makes it possible to change the position of the installed screen vertically. The holder has smaller dimensions than a tilt-swivel, as well as a lower price.
  • Tilt-swivel is a multifunctional TV wall mount that is designed to rotate and tilt the screen in any direction. The main feature of this model is its wide scope for adjusting equipment. Tilt and rotation can be adjusted so that viewing is comfortable from anywhere in the room. The price of such a mount is relatively higher than the previous ones.
  • Ceiling - ergonomic holder. Provides a wide screen tilt and the ability to change the angle of rotation. However, for rooms with low ceilings, this type cannot be installed. But the owners of spacious apartments can allow the ceiling bracket for the TV and, according to their preference, change its positioning.

What to look for

Wall mounts for TVs have a number of characteristics that determine their compatibility with technology. Typically, television mounts are universal, but in their reviews, many recommend purchasing a model of a mounting element that is compatible with your premises and equipment. In this case, you will be sure of the compatibility of the mount and get excellent visibility from anywhere in the room.

It is necessary to pay attention to:

  • universality - additional shelves for synchronization with the TV;
  • destination - for the liquid crystal, plasma or electron beam television;
  • compatibility with screens from 20 to 65 inches;
  • maximum load - the weight withstanding the screen;
  • safety - a protective box for wires;
  • color and design.

Performance data

The most important parameter of the wall mount for the TV is the permissible load. It indicates the weight of the TV holder supports. Universality is the next characteristic that determines what size the design is designed for diagonally. Manufacturers usually indicate a size range of 32-55 inches, for example. Often brackets for TVs are equipped with a protective box in which the wires are laid, as well as additional shelves for installing a media player, tuner or home theater receiver.

Recommendations for installation and operation

If you have already chosen the most suitable wall mount for the new screen, pay the main attention to the tips on the proper installation and use of the wall shelf for the TV:

  • The assembly procedure and steps are the principal sections in the instructions that must be followed impeccably.
  • The most successful, reliable and simple fixation of the holder is in the corner of the room.
  • Accurately measure the height during installation and check how comfortable the image is perceived. The screen, which is located at a low height (just above eye level), is best suited for vision.

In numerous reviews, buyers of modern plasma panels talk about the advantages and disadvantages of this method of mounting a wall-mounted television - on the wall or under the ceiling. Most respondents noted the convenience of placing the screen anywhere in the room, as well as the ability to protect it from mechanical damage, improve the interior and save space.
