Center Ilizarov where is it located? History, description, reviews and photos

The first and only one in Russia is a museum dedicated to traumatology and orthopedics, literally by the hour allowing you to establish a chronology of the development of events that made the Ilizarov Center a bastion of scientific activity and new treatment technologies. The whole development of the famous orthopedic clinic - at a glance.





Genius

The symbol of the Kurgan region is the Russian Scientific Center for Reconstructive Traumatology and Orthopedics named after Academician G. A. Ilizarov. Its history began in the forties, with the first ideas of the famous doctor, and began to be embodied in 1966, when Gavriil Abramovich Ilizarov became the head of the laboratory at the Research Institute of Traumatology and Orthopedics, where the introduction of the method he proposed in clinical practice began.

But, probably, it is more correct to lead the story from the sources, from the poor Jewish family in the distant Belovezhie, on the territory of modern Belarus. The Civil War has just ended, with which the Red Army fighter Abram Elizarov, the father of the future celebrity, returned. And in 1921, at the beginning of the summer, a wonderful doctor, surgeon, inventor Gabriel Abramovich Ilizarov was born, to the great joy of his parents and to the happiness of all people injuring limbs,. Born, graduated from an eight-year school, and then a medical rabfak. In 1939 he entered the medical institute in Simferopol and in 1944 graduated from it. And a long journey began from the surgeon of the rural hospital to the director of the Center, who later received his name.

New method

Many hundreds of fighters passed through the hands of a surgeon from wartime to the early fifties. Already in Kurgan, heading the surgical department of the hospital, where war invalids tried to restore health, Ilizarov observed the effects of bone damage: existing treatment methods yielded practically no results. The surgeon, with all his heart, with all his thoughts, was looking for a way out of this difficult situation.

Finally, Ilizarov proposed his own method of bone fusion and an apparatus, confirmed by copyright, to implement this method. And the device did not fail! The effectiveness of the treatment has increased unusually, the treatment time for fractures has been significantly reduced. Over time, with practical use, the use of the Ilizarov apparatus has expanded significantly.





Apparatus

The Ilizarov Center still uses this very device, invented in 1950, transosseous compression distraction apparatus, which not only stably fixes bone fragments, but also controls biological processes of bone tissue development, which are very complex. It‚Äôs just, it would seem, metal rings with knitting needles attached to them, which pass through the bone, fixing the fracture. However - not easy. The rods connecting the rings have their own mechanics, which allows you to change the orientation of the rings in millimeters per day. That is, it is a dynamic universal design that allows the bones to grow together and at the same time the musculoskeletal system to return functionality. For wider use, Gabriel Abramovich unified all the details and components of the apparatus. Now the Ilizarov Center is preparing each doctor so that he can individually - for each individual case - mount the necessary variety of apparatus from the same number of parts. Injuries, fractures and congenital deformities of bone tissue, even orthopedic cosmetology - lengthening and straightening of the legs - all this has become curable and accessible for any resident of our country who comes with his pain to Russia, to the city of Kurgan, finds Maria Ulyanova Street and the house number six, where they are waiting for him.





Confession

Many, long and difficult years of work have passed before universal recognition. Only in 1968, outstanding achievements were appreciated, and for the method of transosseous osteosynthesis, Ilizarov received a degree - without the title of candidate - doctor of medical sciences. The thesis in Perm was defended, where the vast experience of curing thousands and thousands of people was presented and summarized. A comprehensive analysis made it possible to discover patterns in the regeneration and growth of bone tissue, which made it possible not only to lengthen, but also restore lost parts of the limbs - fingers, foot. It was a sensation!

The Ilizarov Center also received the first results of its experiments to restore the functions of the spinal cord after almost complete surgical intersection. Not only in the USSR, nowhere else in the world were such studies conducted either in traumatology or in orthopedics. The novelty of the inventions studied by the Ilizarov Center was unique and undeniable, therefore, our orthopedics and traumatology rightfully occupied a leading position and still holds it thanks to the fact that in 1987 the Central Committee of the CPSU and the Council of Ministers of the USSR reorganized the Kurgan Scientific Research Institute, creating in its place The All-Union Kurgan Center of Ilizarov with branches in Leningrad, Kazan, Volgograd, Ufa, Sverdlovsk, Krasnodar, Krasnoyarsk, Omsk, Vladivostok and in the Moscow Region.

Method Details

An extensive system of operational bloodless methods of transosseous osteosynthesis has been widely introduced into the practice of traumatology and orthopedics. Fractures of the extremities are treated in absolutely any localization, as well as their shortening and deformation, bone defects are removed without transplantation, as well as false joints, all pathologies of the hand, foot, and large joints. This ingenious invention calls on scientists who have fallen in love with Kurgan, Ilizarov, a medical center, to lead the team to new victories, thereby paying tribute to its creator, ascetic, and scientist. And the Ilizarov case did not stop, it is moving! Numerous designs of apparatuses and methods of operation are being developed, bone formation processes are being studied more and more deeply. The method has remained the same in essence, but it is being improved. It is now used in all regions of Russia, in all CIS countries, as well as in eighty-eight countries of the world, where they probably do not know where the Ilizarov Orthopedics Center is located - Kurgan, this distant Ural city.

In addition to traumatology and orthopedics, the Ilizarov method is now used in vertebrology, antiology, oncology and many other areas of practical medicine.
