Crimean sanatorium "Ai-Petri". Tourist recall as a mirror of service

Ai-Petri (as the name of the apostle sounds in Greek, which, according to legend, is entrusted with standing with keys at the gates of paradise) - this is a famous mountain on the Crimean coast. At its foot is the village of Koreiz, which has a unique microclimate. This is what one of the oldest health resorts of the South Coast, of the Crimean peninsula - the Ai-Petri sanatorium, uses. But we will not only talk about the number of rooms or about amenities, but analyze the responses of real people who have been here. Indeed, it is on the basis of this information that future visitors often make up their minds.

Sanatorium Ai-Petri. Reviewed by an experienced tourist

People who have already visited Crimea for the first time, say that, unlike many of the local resort and medical complexes, this health resort is clearly changing for the better. First of all, the courtesy and friendliness of the service staff is noted - which is far from always found in former "Soviet institutions." In addition, the incredibly good location and breathtaking views, especially from the upper floors of the buildings, combine with a very reasonable price category for the middle class of holidaymakers. Many write that in terms of cost and quality of services, including for treatment, this is the most acceptable Crimean health resort.
