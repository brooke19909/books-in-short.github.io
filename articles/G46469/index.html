The recreation center "Luch" - a green corner near the Olympic Park

The recreation center "Luch" is a great place for an economical vacation in Sochi on the very shore of the sea. It is located in the Adler district in the village of Vesyoloye. The resort area itself used to be the territory of the Soviet agricultural enterprise state farm "Russia". But now there is only one name left. In fact, this is a beach holiday village with a well-developed infrastructure. The base operates from mid-May to October. In total, 250 people can accommodate here. Families with children are especially fond of coming here.





Location, territory. How to get there

The recreation center "Luch" is located in the Olympic Park, in the Lower Imereti Lowland. Previously, this resort area was called the state farm "Russia". Also called a public transport stop, and the neighborhood. To get there, you need to get to the railway station "Olympic Park", and from there two stops by bus. The same distance to the Iceberg Stadium and the Ice Arena. You need to go by minibuses No. 50, 57, 100, 126. The territory of the base is a fenced site of a forest park, one hectare in size. A lot of conifers, cypresses, palm trees, everywhere flower beds. Near the base there is a Magnit supermarket, as well as other grocery and department stores, cafes, pharmacies, restaurants and a branch of Sberbank. The territory of the base is guarded, video surveillance is conducted. There is a bike rental service on the way to the beach. By bus, you can easily reach the children's theme ‚ÄúSochi Park‚Äù.





Residence

Vacationers at the recreation center "Luch" lodged in Finnish houses, which are in the shade of trees. These are Finnish-style cottages lined with wood. There are houses with amenities (shower, toilet, refrigerator, TV, air conditioning), designed for two or four vacationers. But also offers economy class housing for 3-4 people. The rooms there are only refrigerators and televisions, and all amenities are on site. Accordingly, accommodation prices range from 750 rubles per day for the cheapest room in the low season to 2600 rubles. per cottage with amenities in August. Each house has its own table. True, the furniture, bedspreads and curtains are in the style of the Soviet retro, but everything works more or less, there is nothing broken. The rooms have a sufficient number of outlets. Children are accepted at the recreation center from the age of three.





Nutrition

On the territory of the recreation center "Luch" there is a dining room with home cooking. The choice of dishes is large and varied, and the cooks are polite and professional. The food is cheaper than in neighboring cafes, the products are fresh, they cook deliciously. Meals are usually complex, three meals a day. Tour operators can buy a ticket to this base with meals at a good discount. You can get a kettle from administration for free. But he issued one for the house, in which, as a rule, several rooms. At the base there is a beer bar "Ray" and a disco cafe "Two palm trees." Of nearby canteens, tourists recommend Berezhok.

Services

Execute guests in the administration quickly - 7-10 minutes. The checkout time is noon, but if you arrive early in the morning and there are places, then you can easily settle down without any problems. For cars of tourists arriving by their own transport to the recreation center "Luch" (state farm "Russia"), there is a free parking lot. The territory is clean, it is cleaned every day. There is a good playground. Between the houses there are several swings. Bathrooms, washbasins and showers in the area are clean, always free, no queues. Cold and hot water round the clock. Trash cans are cleaned daily. Everywhere in the territory there are water taps. There is a washing machine, but it is one throughout the base. You can play billiards, visit the gym. There are two networks of free Internet (Wi-Fi). Other entertainment is all on the waterfront. There are many bars with live music, discos. But at the same time, no sounds reach the base. Therefore, it is quiet here at night.





Sea

The beach is right next to the Luch recreation center (Rossiya state farm). It belongs to the neighboring boarding house "Chernomorets". But the guests of the base can use it freely. From the houses to the beach go no more than fifty meters. There is a railway between the base and the sea, as elsewhere in Adler. Along the beach there is a new landscaped promenade, along which you can walk to the Olympic Park with a walking step. The sandy and pebble coast, admission is free. Boat trips along the coast are also popular.

The recreation center "Ray": reviews of tourists

Vacationers very positively rated accommodation in this cozy place. Tourists write in reviews that it is quiet and calm, the food in the dining room is good and inexpensive. Vacationers also note that there are not so many places on the coast here. Most of all, tourists liked that each house is separate, and there is no feeling of a hostel. The park area and shady alleys also appealed to vacationers. Literally in every second review, those who lived at this base promise to definitely return here again. Moreover, in terms of price and quality, these recreation facilities are much better than guest houses, where guests can only sit on a small balcony. And against the background of Adler flooded with asphalt, this base resembles just a paradise.
