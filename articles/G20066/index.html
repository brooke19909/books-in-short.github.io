Resort Polyana: where to go on an excursion

Polyana is the most famous resort of Transcarpathia, and in fact it is a "meadow". The fact is that a small village is located in a quiet valley, and surrounded by forests of fabulous beauty and two ridges - Poloninsky and Volcanic.



The cozy banks of two picturesque rivers, Big and Small Pini, make the views near the village even more colorful. Nature created all the conditions for the balneological resort to be located here, one of the oldest and most respected in Transcarpathia.

There are two large sanatoriums in Polyana - one is called a settlement and has been hosting guests from all over the world for more than 130 years. The second is called "Solar Transcarpathia".

This ecologically mega-favorable resort has a unique opportunity to relax your soul and improve your health. Local sanatoriums specialize in the treatment and prevention of diseases in the field of gastroenterology.

Glade is a place where diverse cultures and customs converge. Therefore, it is very colorful, for example, the local cuisine, where there are delicacies from the Romanian, Slovak, Hungarian, German traditions.



Together with fresh mountain air, forest walks, pastoral rural landscapes, Polyana promises an unforgettable vacation and relaxation. In addition to resorts, there are many comfortable and welcoming hotels and hotels in Polyana. You can choose for yourself convenient and decent housing here .
