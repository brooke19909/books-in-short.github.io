Hotel Miraluna Seaside 4 * (Greece / Rhodes): photos and reviews of tourists

Hotel Miraluna Seaside 4 * is located in a paradise of our planet, in the picturesque resort of Rhodes. This hotel complex positions itself as a family, therefore, it offers its guests wonderful spacious rooms in which it will be comfortable not only for an adult, but also for a child. The hotel includes several buildings: one central building and several bungalows. This is a truly unusually beautiful place, which attracted not only tourists, but also the Greeks themselves.

Description of rooms





In the complex Miraluna Seaside 4 * (Rhodes) there are 231 rooms. Despite such a large number of apartments, it is better to book a seat in advance before traveling.

Guests are offered accommodation in one of the following apartment types.

  1. Standard. The room is small in size. It can accommodate two adults and two children, under the age of 18 and up to 6 years. It offers beautiful views of the sea, pool or orchards.
  2. Family These are more spacious apartments. It can also accommodate two adults and two children. The windows offer a magnificent view of the coming beauty.

In the room you can find all the necessary items for personal hygiene. On the dressing table in the bathroom there is a set of cosmetics, and in the closet you can have a set of threads and brushes for cleaning shoes and clothes. The minibar offers a variety of drinks, chocolate and so on. Tea and coffee, sugar and various toppings, as well as water without gas, are provided free of charge. Each guest is given free bathrobes and towels, if the size of the bathrobe is not suitable, then it can be replaced. For Russian tourists in the room broadcasts of domestic channels are provided.

Power System Description

Miraluna Seaside Kiotari Bay provides all-inclusive meals. Guests can enjoy full breakfasts, lunches and dinners. Guests can choose what they like. You can also have a snack with all kinds of snacks in the bar. The price includes free ice cream, which will delight little guests. You can also enjoy a variety of cocktails at the bar.

Beach trip

The beach is very beautiful, sandy, there are wooden flooring, so as not to walk on the sand. Each guest of the hotel can count on free sunbeds, towels and refreshing cocktails.

The territory of the complex

There are several great restaurants on site that serve local and European dishes. There are several bars where you can enjoy refreshing drinks. Near the central building is a beauty salon. There is also free parking and a luggage area. Throughout the complex there is free internet access. The massage salon and SPA center offers its services.

Entertainment and outdoor activities





For those who like to always keep themselves in shape, the administration organized fitness and aerobics classes. You can try tennis, beach soccer or volleyball.

Those who want to get the most out of their holidays can visit discos and various entertainment programs every evening. Theme parties are also held at the hotel, the highlight of which are Greek-style evenings.

Services for little guests





The administration of the Miraluna Seaside Kiotari 4 * hotel takes care of its young guests. Specially hired animators who speak Russian. Therefore, it will be very easy for a child to find a common language with others, and the period of adaptation will pass very easily. Thought out entertainment program for children of different age categories. Here they find an individual approach to each child. Particular attention is paid to the development of creative abilities, therefore, during the period of rest, the child learns a lot of new and informative for himself, and this is a definite plus. Separately, you can pay for the services of an individual nanny, who will not step away from the child. The restaurants provide a children's menu and the original design of dishes, in order to increase the appetite of the child.

What do the guests themselves say about the Miraluna Seaside 4 * hotel (Rhodes)?

Reviews





Even employees of the tourist industry prefer this particular complex. The reason for this is its undeniable advantages over all other Rhodes hotels. The first thing that attracts attention is the relatively inexpensive price for such a wide range of services. Who cares about good food, comfortable rooms and a spacious area with many bars and pools, you should definitely pay attention to the Miraluna Seaside Hotel 4 *. Especially this place will suit those who like to relax with children. For little guests there is everything in the best possible way.

Post Comments

The reception welcomes friendly staff. Try to please customers with all their might. Among the employees there are Russian-speaking administrators.

The hotel occupies a vast territory and consists of two parts - Miraluna and Kiotary. The first complex has a category of 5 stars, the second - 4. Here everything is buried in tropical greenery. But there are minor flaws. There are a lot of hidden niches on the territory that are easy to fall into, especially for children. Wheelchairs are not very convenient due to the large number of stairs. But there are no complaints about cleanliness. Trash cans are at every turn.

The complexes have different positions with respect to the sea. Miraluna occupies the upper tier and looks more impressive. The territory has 3 large outdoor pools for adults and 3 for children. In the spa area is another pool. Water is chlorinated only in children's waters, the rest is treated without bleach.

In the lower tier of the Miraluna Kiotari Seaside 4 * complex there are excellent children's aquazones with an interesting design.

About nutrition





The hotel has many cafes, bars and restaurants. In the Miraluna section you can visit the excellent tavern, which serves delicious hot drinks, snacks and ice cream. There are bars, a pizzeria and a snack bar with fruits and snacks near the pools.

The dishes in all bars are made of high quality plastic.

The food at the hotel is not very diverse, but it is cooked with high quality. In assortment there are always fresh fruits in huge quantities and delicious desserts.

Eating can be done indoors or outdoors.

Breakfast consists of cereal with milk, hot drinks, yogurt, cottage cheese, sweet pastries, a variety of dishes from eggs, cheese, sausages, fruits and sausages. Porridge is not provided.

For lunch, you can enjoy several meat dishes, seafood, soups. From the side dishes of pasta, cabbage, potatoes. Fresh vegetables and cheese are always plentiful. For sweet pastries, croissant cakes. Especially inspiring to visit the Miraluna Seaside 4 * hotel are reviews of parents who have come to rest with children.

Children's holiday

Younger guests can have fun at the playground, equipped with swings, slides, horizontal bars. In a closed club with experienced children are engaged in babysitting and animators. Children are delighted with all kinds of games and entertainment. Especially interesting for kids in the evening programs. Here they can play and even show their talents by singing a song or dancing.

Children are always surrounded by the care of animators. For older children, there is a club with exciting games such as the mafia, golf, volleyball, polo, roller coaster. You can even get to the photo shoot. Every evening for adults and children there are entertaining shows in the amphitheater.

Room Service





Apartments in the Miraluna Seaside 4 * hotel (Rhodes) are distinguished by categories. The cost is different for everyone. Especially popular with tourists are rooms with private pools. Clean them after each departure of visitors. But with children it is better to book a room in the main building. There you can rent apartments with garden views. There are also rooms located in a lowland. There are practically no mosquitoes.

The minibar is filled independently. Rooms must be booked in advance. The hotel occupies a top position in the ranking, so there may not be any free places. Moreover, relocation to another room is also almost impossible.

The equipment of the apartments is beyond praise. Incredibly comfortable beds, a large balcony, a good chest of drawers with a huge mirror, several nightstands and a wardrobe. Hangers need to be taken with you. The TV is old, shows almost nothing. But on vacation this is an extra attribute. There is a tile on the floor, you need to take slippers with you.

The rooms are cleaned relatively well, but without fanaticism. Daily wet cleaning is provided for you. In the bathroom everything is working properly. Toiletries include soap and shampoo. You can blow dry your hair. Everything is replenished as necessary. Maids do not require tips, just do their job.

In order to change towels and bed linen, they must be put in the bathroom or ask for a replacement.

The furniture in the rooms is not new, but solid. A paid key safe is located in each room. Beach towels can be taken on bail or brought with you.

It is almost impossible to use the Internet at the hotel. It is better to bring a Tour SIM card with you if you need to work on vacation.

Sea and beach

The road to the beach takes only a few minutes. The coastal zone is covered with large pebbles. Sand is brought in for the season and artificial mounds are made. Those with sensitive legs will have to wear slippers. Entrance to the water with stones. After a meter and a half depth begins.

But very close is the sandy beach, which is quite comfortable to relax even with young children. In order not to pay for sunbeds and umbrellas, you can sit in front of them on the sand with your uniform. The beach has a shower, changing cabins.

General impressions





Almost ideal place for a budget holiday in Greece is the Miraluna Seaside 4 * hotel (Rhodes). Reviews of tourists confirm that the complex offers its guests good value for money. You should not go here for noisy parties and festivities until the morning. The rest is designed for family people with children for whom excellent conditions of stay are created here. Service and food were rated average by guests. The food is not very diverse, but of high quality. Cleaning and linen change are available upon request.

The tourists were especially impressed by the pools, of which there are several in the complex. Tasty snacks and drinks are served at establishments near the aquazones.

Many families go to this hotel for their children. No other Rhodes hotel has such a wonderful program for children as in the Miraluna Seaside Kiotari Bay 4 * complex. Animators do children better than parents. The kids leave this place with tears in their eyes, because here they find new friends and get a lot of positive emotions. Adult leisure is also varied. In the evenings you can watch interesting shows or go to dinner at one of the local restaurants.
