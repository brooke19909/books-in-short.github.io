How to sew a bag quickly and economically?

Those who once tasted the joy of creativity would like to create a unique thing again with their own hands. Needlework not only makes it possible to be original, but also helps to save significantly.

Women do not already think of their existence without a bag. Large and small, winter and summer, classic and extravagant, bags and handbags, they always catch our eye. How to sew a bag with your own hands?

An optimal and very economical option for a youth bag is old jeans clothes. Before starting work, jeans, a skirt or a jacket must be washed. Ripping them, of course, is very dreary, so just cut the seams, leaving decorative seams and trim. We select the most preserved parts that have a decent appearance. Having thus prepared the material for work, we proceed to modeling.

Draw up a technological map of tailoring.

A) Sew the wedges to each other with the seams out. We cut the seams to 0.7 cm, smooth it on both sides, sew the decorative braid on each seam.

B) Sew the bottom to the bag, also seam out. After we cut the seam, wrap it with braid and stitch the edge.

C) We process the upper edge of the bag with decorative braid.

D) From the top of the bag to retreat ten centimeters down, mark the horizontal line. Stitch the braid outside so that it breaks in two opposite places. Pull the handle of the bag sewn from raincoat fabric through the braid. Sew the ends of the handle together, hide under the braid. Pull out the handles on both sides, tighten the bag.
