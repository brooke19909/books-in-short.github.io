User opinion on the appearance of the smartphone

It so happened that Chinese manufacturers rarely offer original solutions. Most often, new items are a copy of some popular smartphone. Meizu M6: screen reviews

Many users in their comments indicated that they chose this model because of the 5.2-inch display. If you do not delve deeply into its characteristics, then it may seem that the novelty received the display from the predecessor without changes. All the same HD quality with a few pixels (282 ppi). However, not so deplorable as it might seem at first glance. If you put two phones nearby - M6 and M5, then the difference is still noticeable, albeit small, but users are seeing shifts for the better. The reviews say that the color of the image has become brighter and more saturated, the viewing angles have noticeably expanded, and the level of contrast has become more comfortable. Those who want to adjust, for example, temperature, need to go to the settings of the Meizu M6. Here you can change other parameters.

Owners attributed the presence of a state employee to the presence of 2.5D glass. It is oleophobic coated and scratch resistant. Due to the absence of an air gap, the image remains readable even in the sun.

So to summarize. In user reviews, you will not find comments on the display. Most believe that the characteristics of the screen are fully consistent with the price of the device. The optimal diagonal size, decent resolution, does not consume much energy - what else can a user want when buying a smartphone for 120-130 dollars?

Meizu M6: performance features

Unfortunately, the most disappointing users felt when they learned the characteristics of the hardware platform. Given that in just one year the manufacturer released five M-series devices, many thought that the last instance would still receive a powerful Snapdragon processor, but the hopes did not materialize. The Meizu M6 smartphone runs on the Chinese MediaTek MT6750 chipset . In total, eight cores are laid in it. A 64-bit system of the 4 * 4 type functions. The first half of the computing modules accelerates to 1500 MHz, and the second reaches a frequency of 1000 MHz. This processor model is popular with manufacturers from the Middle Kingdom, so it can be found not only in state employees, but also even in the middle segment. Unfortunately, it is impossible to say that he copes with the tasks set perfectly. The speed of work is average. Many applications and games will run on the phone. Camera
