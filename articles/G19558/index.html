The program "KIA is easy!"

Since mid-June 2016, KIA representative offices launched a new loan program in St. Petersburg and Moscow, combining the efforts of KIA Finance and Setelem Bank. A loan called "KIA is easy!" based on a residual value guarantee. You can get it by acquiring the most popular and popular Rio and Sid models. The purpose of this loan program is to attract a new customer base due to the availability of payments. Campaign to issue a loan "KIA easy!" will give customers the opportunity to purchase additional options or accessories without significantly increasing the amount of the monthly payment. This rule also applies to the choice of vehicle equipment, as the program allows you to purchase a package of options that is better than cash. Reviews on the loan program "KIA easy!" This is confirmed.





Benefits

Unlike conventional lending programs, ‚ÄúKIA is easy!‚Äù It has three significant advantages. These include:

  1. Minimum down payment on a loan, starting from 20% of the cost of the car.
  2. Minimum monthly payments. Approximately half less than with conventional lending schemes.
  3. Warranty on the residual value of the car. Issued by a banking organization.

Two options to choose from

The loan term is 2 or 3 years under the contract. As soon as this period passes, the buyer has two ways:

  1. Make an exchange of a car under the trade-in program for a new one and continue to use it, closing the balance of the loan amount (approximately 45%).
  2. Make a return guarantee car to the bank. The residual value of the guarantee is slightly less than half of the original price of the car and, as a rule, it becomes enough for full early repayment of the loan. Reviews on the loan program "KIA easy!" presented below.

Reviews on the program "KIA easy!"

This directly depends on the individual conditions of the bank and the borrower's credit history, as well as on the package of documents provided to them.
