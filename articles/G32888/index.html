Error codes for washing machines. Helpful information

Samsung automatic washing machines are one of the most modern and high quality in our time. Nevertheless, very often, various malfunctions arise during operation, and the smart device reports them, displaying the corresponding code on the screen. How to decrypt washing machine error codes? It turns out that this is not so difficult to do.





Samsung washing machines with a display are equipped with the function of displaying error codes that appear when malfunctions and some malfunctions occur. Very often, understanding the meaning of the displayed code, the user is able to independently fix the breakdown or malfunction without contacting the service center for servicing household appliances.

Let's list the most common washing machine error codes that appear on Samsung devices. Here we look at the code, the causes of the problem, and possible solutions.
