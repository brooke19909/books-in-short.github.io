Hotel Golden Tulip Hotel 3 * (Vietnam, Nha Trang): photos and reviews of tourists

Golden Tulip Hotel 3 * is a small but beautiful and comfortable hotel, which many tourists choose as a stop. After all, here you can count on a pleasant pastime, good service and, of course, relaxation on one of the most popular beaches in the country. So what to expect from a hotel?





Location Description

Where to look for the hotel Golden Tulip Hotel 3 *? Vietnam, namely the vibrant, vibrant resort town of Nha Trang, is here, next to the seashore, and this cozy hotel is located. By the way, very close, within walking distance, there is a large market, small shops, dozens of cafes and some famous attractions. The train station is also close, which is suitable for tourists who want to travel a bit around the country. The airport is located in Kamran, at a distance of approximately 28 km. The hotel itself is a modern building with beautiful finishes and necessary amenities.

Hotel Golden Tulip Hotel 3 * (Vietnam, Nha Trang): photo of rooms and a brief description

The hotel can be considered relatively small, as it consists of fifty rooms. These are mostly standard rooms, but there are several deluxe rooms. The rooms are furnished with new comfortable furniture and pleasantly impress with elegant design. Naturally, guests are provided with a set of household appliances, without which it is difficult to imagine a truly comfortable stay.





In the evenings, you can relax watching TV. A modern air conditioning system will make it possible to maintain pleasant temperature conditions in the room. There is also a minibar with a cooling system. You can rely on daily replenishment of drinking water, although you will have to pay extra for other drinks.

The bathroom is adjacent to the room, where you can take a shower at any convenient time. It also provides clean towels and toiletries. There is also a hairdryer for drying hair.

Food conditions

Like most hotels in Vietnam, Golden Tulip Hotel 3 * provides its guests with continental breakfasts. The local restaurant organizes a buffet in the morning, which offers light snacks, soups, pastries, fruits, drinks and some Vietnamese dishes. All the rest of the day, the restaurant is √† la carte and at an additional cost. On the other hand, there are dozens of different places to eat nearby.

Can I relax on the beach?

Can a guest at Golden Tulip Hotel 3 * count on a beach holiday? Vietnam is a country famous for its beautiful coast. And, of course, in the city of Nha Trang there are many popular public beaches where you can have a good time. The nearest is about 800 meters away. You can cover this distance on foot or take the services of a taxi, which, by the way, are quite inexpensive here.





Since the beaches here are public, then, of course, renting sunbeds and umbrellas is paid separately. On the shore there is a cafe where you can eat, as well as entertainment centers where visitors can engage in more active recreation and water sports.

Additional services

Golden Tulip Hotel 3 * is an urban-type hotel, so there are not too many ways to have fun. However, guests can enjoy some additional services. For example, the hotel has a modernly equipped parking lot where you can park cars. By the way, renting a transport in the city is not difficult. Moreover, most travelers do just that. There is also a local laundry service and ironing service for a small fee.





By the way, throughout the territory, including the rooms, there is access to the Internet, which is important for almost every modern traveler. For a small fee, you can order a shuttle to the airport. And do not forget about sightseeing holidays, because Vietnam is a country with an incredibly interesting culture, traditions, history and breathtaking nature.

Hotel Golden Tulip Hotel 3 * (Vietnam): reviews

Of course, reviews of people who have already managed to visit the hotel will provide a lot of useful information. Golden Tulip Hotel 3 * is an excellent option for a budget holiday, as the prices for accommodation and meals here are really affordable. Count on luxurious rooms and a lot of entertainment is not worth it. Nevertheless, travelers say that the living conditions are quite decent, the breakfasts are delicious, and the location is favorable, as it is easy to get to anywhere in the city center.
