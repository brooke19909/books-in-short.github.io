General impression

The most mixed opinions can be heard about such an institution as the Club Hotel Falcon 4 * hotel. Turkey, Antalya attracts a wide variety of categories of travelers, with a variety of requests. To summarize, we can say that this hotel will appeal to unpretentious guests who like to relax fun and actively. This is a great place to enjoy the clear sea and magnificent views. But not for families with children, because swimming takes place at great depths from the pier, which is unsafe for kids. It is also a great accommodation option for travelers who are looking to explore local attractions. Convenient location in the center of the resort as well as possible contributes to this. Perhaps, such active tourists simply will not have the strength and time to notice the flaws in Club Hotel Falcon 4 * (Turkey). And unfortunately, there are a lot of them. This mainly concerns the quality of repairs and the range of food in the main restaurant.
