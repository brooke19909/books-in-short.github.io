Hotel Anthoula Village Hotel 4 * (Greece / Crete): description and photos

If you decide to spend your holidays in Greece, then you did not lose, because this is a hospitable country with a wonderful climate and rich history and culture. In Greece, you can fully enjoy your vacation and feel truly rested and happy. Well, if you are going to relax in Hersonissos, then pay attention to Anthoula Village Hotel 4 *. And we will tell you in detail about this hotel, the services it provides, and give a general impression of tourists who have already rested in its comfortable rooms.





Hotel info

Anthoula Village Hotel 4 * is located, strictly speaking, not in Hersonissos itself, but in the small village of Analipsis, literally five minutes drive from the city. This hotel can be safely called a small family hotel, it will be most preferable for families with children or for those who seek peace and solitude, refuge from the hustle and bustle. Everyone who has ever been to this hotel knows its owner, Katarina. The owner of Anthoula Village Hotel 4 *, reviews of which say that she is a friendly, helpful and very pleasant woman, manages the hotel with her husband, who is also a cook. Spouses are always on site and are happy to come to your aid.

Most often, hotel guests note a cozy homely atmosphere, incredibly delicious cuisine, silence and comfort that await you at Anthoula Village Hotel 4 *. Often, newlyweds come here who want to spend some time together, not being distracted by other people, as well as just those who value village life and want to be alone with themselves and nature. Separately, it is worth noting that the hotel has quite a lot of Russian-speaking tourists, as it is popular with residents of our country.





Hotel building and rooms

The hotel was built in 2004 on Pefana Hill, which offers a picturesque view of the town and the mountains surrounding it. The hotel has twenty eight rooms located on two floors. All apartments are equipped with necessary furniture, which is maintained in excellent condition, the rooms have TVs and air conditioning. The entire fund is equipped with refrigerators and electric kettles, some rooms have balconies, as well as a small kitchen. Each apartment has a private bathroom with shower, and hairdryers are also provided.

The hotel is designed in the typical manner that Greece is famous for. Anthoula Village Hotel 4 * is a small white building with balconies from which you can enjoy views of the surroundings. A variety of plants are planted on the territory, comfortable paths are laid, there are places for recreation. There is also a path from the hotel to the beach so that tourists can conveniently get to the seashore.

Guest Accommodation

In total, the hotel has five types of rooms. For a family of two adults with two children, a family studio or family apartment (1 bedroom) is offered. For those traveling with three children, there is also a family apartment. It is important to know that children must be under 12 years old.

Also in Anthoula Village Hotel 4 * there is a one-bedroom apartment that can accommodate up to five adults, and a standard studio that can accommodate up to four guests.

The maximum number of extra beds in a room does not exceed one. It should also be remembered that if you need extra beds and cots, then this should be warned in advance. Wait for the hotel to be notified that it is possible to provide a service. At the same time, children under two years old are provided free of charge when they are placed in cots. This also applies to children under 12 years old, if they are accommodated in extra beds, then accommodation will not cost anything.





Entertainment on site

Despite the fact that Anthoula Village Hotel 4 * (Crete) is located in a small village, entertainment can be found here. More often than not, this hotel is chosen by couples craving privacy, or families, therefore, the appropriate entertainment is chosen here. Near the pool you will find a table for tennis, the building has darts and billiards. You can use the fitness room, massage services. There are also pools, which we will discuss in detail below. Once a week, an evening of national culture takes place in Anthoula Village Hotel 4 *. Reviews indicate that here you can enjoy national cuisine, listen to Cretan music and dance.

There is a playroom for kids, a real playground where they can frolic in plenty on the territory of Anthoula Village. Anthoula Village Hotel 4 (8.2 rating points out of 10 possible for good reason, according to reviews, this hotel is gaining!) Can also boast a small but good library that will please reading and leisurely lovers. The spacious lounge area features soft sofas and a TV with plenty of entertainment. There is also free Wi-Fi, but tourists show that it works best in the lobby and on the balconies, the signal in the rooms sometimes disappears.

Well, those who are still looking for fun, dancing and entertainment can go to Hersonissos, since the bus stop is literally a few minutes walk from the hotel.





Beaches and Pools

On the territory of Anthoula Village Hotel 4 * (Analipsis) you will find two outdoor pools, the water in which is constantly cleaned. One of them is intended for adults, the other for children, both of them are equipped with everything necessary for a comfortable rest by the water. Pools are seasonal, as they are in the open air, next to them is a terrace and a small garden.

If you do not like artificial ponds and want to enjoy the real sea, then you have to take a walk to the city beach. The road will take you about 10-15 minutes at a leisurely pace; Hersonissos beach is sand and pebble. The entrance to the sea is rocky, but, moving a little further away, you will find a sandy convenient entrance to the water. If you go a little further from the main coastline, you will find a slightly more secluded beach in the bay behind the breakwater, where you can swim without fear of the wind. Sun loungers, parasols and sun loungers on the beach are provided for a fee. On the beach there are lifeguards who monitor the order and behavior of vacationers in the water.

Dining at the hotel

As mentioned above, Anthoula Village Hotel 4 * (Greece, Analipsis) is famous for its cuisine, as the hostess's husband conjures the dishes himself. The hotel boasts rich breakfasts and lunches, where you will find both dishes of national cuisine and more traditional food, familiar to Europeans and Russian tourists.

You can dine in a cozy restaurant, ordering dishes to your liking from the menu, or you can use the buffet, where you will also have fresh and tasty food. The hotel has a snack bar, upon request, you can prepare dietary or vegetarian food. The cook will certainly take into account your preferences if you suffer from food allergies. There is no children's menu at the hotel. At your request, lunch will be packed with you if you suddenly decide to have lunch on the beach or go for a long walk. Please note that for dinner drinks are not served here, they must be purchased at the bar for an additional fee. However, you can bring ordinary water and drinks to your children with you. Highchairs are also provided if necessary.

You can choose various modifications of the diet, order only breakfast and dinner, if you plan to lie on the beach during the day. Then it will be more convenient to have lunch in a cafe on the waterfront, which are abundant in the village.

There is also a bar in Anthoula Village Hotel. In the evenings, you can enjoy a delicious cocktail with incendiary Greek music, and the selection of drinks here is quite large and will delight any gourmet.

Attractions near the hotel

Since Anthoula Village Hotel 4 * (Analipsis) is located near the center of Hersonissos, the hotel is within easy reach of the main attractions of the city. From here, you can easily get to museums and palaces, as well as to more modern entertainment. Here is a list of the main attractions that are nearby the hotel or located accessible, and getting to them is convenient.

  1. Knossos Palace of King Minos. According to legend, it was there that the labyrinth of the Minotaur was located, known to all of us according to legends and myths. Previously, the palace impressed with its luxury and size, but now it remains one of the most impressive architectural monuments of Greece. Tours come here, but you can get there yourself.

  2. The Lychnostatis Folk Museum is a unique open-air venue that will interest any visitor. The museum exposition tells about the life of the island and its history. Here you will find the most prominent representatives of the flora and fauna of Crete, samples of folk art, as well as the production of alcoholic beverages, olive oil, fabrics and pottery. In addition, the museum has a small shop, a cafe, and various events are often organized.

  3. House-Museum of Nikos Kazantzakis. Here is an exposition telling about the life and work of the great Greek writer and philosopher. The museum opened recently and is very attractive for tourists and locals.

  4. Aquarium Aquaworld. This small aquarium is also a reptile rescue center. Here you can admire a variety of bright marine inhabitants, in a habitat that is almost completely consistent with the natural. You can get to know the reptiles closer - the aquarium is a contact. A nice bonus is that, having bought a ticket, you can stay in the aquarium all day.

Village Details

Just around the corner of the hotel there is a tour desk where you can book interesting excursions. Nearby is also a car rental agency where you can rent a car and drive through the picturesque surroundings on your own.

You can also take a walk around the village itself without having to travel to large cities. Despite its small size, it looks very well-groomed and has a unique Greek flavor. You will find here small taverns, shops and bars. Standing apart is a small church whose bell ringing announces the whole village several times a day, be sure to go and admire it in the rays of the setting sun. Locals are very good-natured and love tourists. More than once or twice you will stop to politely chat and tell interesting places in neighboring cities.

Additional Information

Check-in time starts at two in the afternoon and lasts until nine in the evening. Departure of guests is carried out in the morning - from 07:30 to noon. If you arrived at the hotel before the appointed time, then you have to wait for check-in - you can leave your suitcases in a locked room and go to relax on the beach or find refuge by the pool.

The reception is open daily from six in the morning until two in the morning. You can pay in cash or by credit card, but you will be required to pay a deposit and present an identification document. Guests over 18 years old can stay at the hotel on their own. Reservations are made on a prepaid basis, after which representatives of the hotel will contact you and clarify all the details of the upcoming stay, as well as answer your questions.

Parking near the hotel is free for guests, it is not necessary to inform about the availability of a car in advance.

Choosing this hotel from many others, you will not regret it - an excellent vacation awaits you, filled not only with sea and sun, but also with the friendliness and good nature of the owners, the cosiness and hospitality of a small hotel.
