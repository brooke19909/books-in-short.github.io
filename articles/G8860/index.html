Okeanos Beach Hotel (Ayia Napa) - Room Overview

The five-story residential building provides guests with 64 guest rooms, 45 of which have recently undergone major repairs. Conventionally, they can be divided into the following categories:

  • The standard room provides double occupancy on large or twin beds. Opposite is a dressing table with a mirror, as well as a TV. The entrance hall has a built-in wardrobe and coat rack. There is access to a private furnished balcony.
  • Superior rooms are spacious and more functional. In addition to the orthopedic bed, the room also has a folding soft sofa, which can serve as a rest or additional accommodation.
  • Family rooms are essentially two standard rooms. The rooms are separated by a sliding door. One of the bedrooms has a sofa for an extra guest. The room is equipped with two bathrooms.

Okeanos Beach Hotel (Ayia Napa, Cyprus) can be considered a quite affordable option for a seaside vacation. Accommodation prices range from 6,000 rubles to 12,000 rubles per day.

Helpful information

Before going on vacation, you should familiarize yourself with the rules of the hotel where you are going to stay. Pay attention to the following points:

  • the time of registration and eviction is set at two in the afternoon and noon, respectively;
  • additional folding beds are not provided in the rooms;
  • pets of any breed and size are not allowed in the hotel;
  • there is the possibility of paying for services using plastic cards;
  • throughout the hotel, with the exception of specially designated places, smoking is prohibited;
  • upon check-in, the tourist must present a credit card, the details of which were indicated at the time of booking (otherwise the order may be canceled);
  • upon cancellation of the reservation, the deposit amount is not compensated;
  • Late check-out fee is EUR 35 (until 17:00).
