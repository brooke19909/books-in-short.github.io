How to decorate denim shorts to look stylish?

Our article today is devoted to how to decorate denim shorts with your own hands and surprise others with your ideal taste and desire for a stylish life. You will learn not only the basic methods of decoration, but also how to choose the right pattern for a particular type of fabric. You will definitely need these skills in your life, so we begin to tell how to decorate denim shorts in all details.



Summer, sea, sun ... The first association that arises in the head is a chic vacation on one of the sunny beaches of Europe or the East. And even if we rest only in our native country, any vacation needs appropriate clothes that would be convenient, comfortable and meet all fashion trends. But the main problem lies in the fact that almost all summer things are distinguished by the absolute absence of form, rhinestones, prints and all else. That is, most often summer things are loose T-shirts from light fabrics, which are also monophonic to all this. The only thing you can find is light sundresses with images of flowers (although they are already boring). That is why many people prefer to buy jeans for the summer, for example short shorts. After the purchase, a logical question arises: "How to decorate denim shorts?" - because most of them also do not differ in brightness and the presence of any drawings.



The first thing that can be advised for decoration is rhinestones, since they are publicly available and look quite good. If you are looking for decoration of denim shorts, then we recommend that you go to any fabric and accessories store and choose the right-sized strasses. The order in which they are glued is entirely up to you. But it must be recalled that the golden mean must be present in everything, and therefore in this matter too. That is why it is not worth showering a thing with rhinestones. Let it be a small drawing or just a sparkling strip along the contour of the pocket - this is quite enough for a summer and short thing.

Next, of course, are rivets and zippers, which have gained wide popularity among young people. Of course, they will have to tinker with longer, however, if you are wondering how to decorate denim shorts, this option is pretty acceptable. But it should be borne in mind that it is worth choosing one kind of decoration, but not all at once. The presence of rivets and zippers is only welcome on leather things, most often jackets, and should there be pants

either one or the other.

Jeans shorts with bead embroidery or applique look beautiful. However, jeans can also be different, and if you bought enough rigid material, then sewing on beads to it is not recommended, because due to such fabric the fishing line can quickly fray and tear. But if the material itself is soft and supple, then decorating the shorts with beads will look very nice and stylish.

Thinking about how to decorate denim shorts, connect your own imagination. Then you will definitely find the most original idea that can surprise your friends and relatives, as well as tell about you as a very creative and stylish person.
