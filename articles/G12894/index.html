Hotel "Elvira" in Ryazan: rooms, prices and reviews

If you plan to visit the city of Ryazan, the Elvira hotel will be an excellent accommodation option. Here you will have everything you need for a comfortable stay, as well as a good rest after a busy working or sightseeing day. The institution has been operating since 2005. During its operation, the hotel has served about 20,000 customers.

Location

The convenient location attracts guests of the Ryazan hotel Elvira. Address: Karl Marx Street, 1/6. There are several ways to get here:

  • From Ryazan-1 train station, take an electric train to Diaghilevo.
  • From Ryazan-1 railway station by minibuses No. 53, 87 or trolleybus No. 17. Exit at the stop "DK Prioksky".
  • From Turlatovo airport by minibus No. 121 to the Sheremetyevo stop. Then take minibus No. 53 to the Prioksky Palace of Culture.
  • From the central bus station by minibuses No. 53, 87 or trolleybus No. 17. Get off at the stop "DK Prioksky".

Services

In addition to accommodation, guests at the Elvira Hotel in Ryazan have access to a number of additional services. Namely:

  • round-the-clock cafe;
  • food and drink delivery, if desired, to the rooms;
  • a beauty salon is located in the hotel building;
  • sauna with pool and relaxation area;
  • banquet room with a capacity of up to 100 people for organizing celebrations and corporate events.

Positive feedback from guests

When planning a trip, be sure to check out the reviews about the Elvira Hotel in Ryazan. Here are the positive experiences travelers share about this place:

  • good living conditions and quality of service, when compared with other similar institutions in the city;
  • good enough quality food in the restaurant (you can eat in the dining room or order room service);
  • acceptable conditions for staying at night - there is a necessary minimum of amenities;
  • in the hotel restaurant you can have an inexpensive and satisfying breakfast;
  • There are two grocery supermarkets near the establishment;
  • the cafe at the hotel is open around the clock, at any time you can eat and drink coffee;
  • Affordable prices for accommodation in comparison with other institutions of the city;
  • It is possible to pay for accommodation by the hour.

Negative reviews

Before the trip, try to learn as much as possible about the Elvira Hotel in Ryazan. Photos and reviews on the official website do not always objectively cover possible shortcomings. From the reviews of guests it is worth highlighting such negative comments:

  • outdated and not very comfortable atmosphere in the dining room of the restaurant;
  • the quality of cleaning the rooms leaves much to be desired (it seems that the maids do not use a vacuum cleaner at all);
  • the drain in the bathroom does not work well;
  • the rooms do not have slippers, and walking barefoot on the tiled floor is quite cold;
  • you can not open the windows for ventilation, and the air conditioner does not give a feeling of fresh air;
  • the rooms are quite dark (even in the daytime you have to turn on the light);
  • plumbing in the bathroom is poorly attached to the floor, staggering;
  • food delivery from the restaurant to the room takes a very long time, sometimes you have to remind;
  • breakfast is not included in the price;
  • many rooms require repair (although, apparently, the situation was very comfortable before);
  • the windows of the rooms face either a dirty courtyard or a busy street (and the windows are almost at the level of the sidewalk);
  • in the rooms there is a distinct smell of tobacco smoke (apparently, it has already absorbed into the furniture);
  • the bathroom smells strongly of the sewage system (apparently, ventilation works poorly);
  • stained old dirty mattresses;
  • old poorly functioning TVs;
  • the hotel is located on the outskirts of the city, and therefore getting to the main infrastructure and tourist facilities is not very convenient;
  • weak and intermittent wireless Internet signal.
