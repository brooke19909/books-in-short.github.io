Jamming protection

The use of sirens in modern car alarms is causing more and more negative disputes and related problems. By themselves, sirens - as a means of preventing theft - were abolished by long-range remote controls with feedback, and even later they began to completely abandon them - cellular communication channels appeared. Instead of a loud siren, which sometimes wakes up half a block, the system will personally inform the owner of the car about problems or other dangers.

But progress does not stand still and the flip side always, sooner or later, will make itself felt. In almost any radio store or on the Internet, you can find devices that suppress the GSM signal. Such gadgets have become a real phobia among the owners of such alarms. Indeed, similar security systems based on cellular reception are extremely vulnerable to such jammers, thereby depriving the signaling system of the ability to send any ping to its owner.

Installation of car alarms Pandora DXL 5000

Do-it-yourself installation of such a modern alarm system as Pandora is practically unrealistic, so do not be surprised that most of the most advanced installation services will not be able to help you in this matter.

Only certified units (companies, installers) can install the DXL 5000 system, that is, those who have been accredited by the alarm manufacturer - Alarm Trade. The system adjusts and works only with a specific car in online mode, therefore the entrance and further access to all the functionality, and therefore to the alarm setting for the owner, is exclusively from a certified and approved master.





Many users express dissatisfaction in their reviews with such a rigid system of control and selection of specialists, but the company explains its actions with some facts. Installing a Pandora-type car alarm requires the skills of a highly qualified master and is significantly different from installing similar and competing devices, therefore, in order to save its customers from poor-quality installation of such an expensive premium system, the company decided to limit the number of installers by introducing strict accreditation.
