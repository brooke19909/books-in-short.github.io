Olympik Praha 4 * (Czech Republic, Prague): hotel description, photos and reviews

Olympik Praha 4 * is located in the heart of the Czech capital and is an excellent option for accommodation in Prague, whatever the purpose of the trip: a family or romantic trip, a business meeting or a vacation with friends. The hotel has a good location, so getting to the right place in the city is not difficult. In addition, on the territory of the Olympic Hotel and next to it, tourists will find classes to spend their free time pleasantly and with benefit.





Hotel location

Hotel Olympik Praha 4 * is located on Sokolovska Street 138. This is a quiet and peaceful residential quarter in the historical district, the heart of Prague - the Old Town. The distance to the modern center is only 4 km, to the international airport. Vaclav Havel is 17 km away, and the Main Station is 3.5 km away.

Within walking distance of the Olympik Praha there are various shops, cafes, restaurants, bars and nightclubs. Invalidovna metro station is 200 meters away, and a tram stop of the same name is 300 meters away. From here you can quickly get to any area of ​​the city.

Description Olympik Praha 4 *

Olympic is a four-star hotel, built in 1976 and renovated in 2009. It is a twenty-story building with an area of ​​315 rooms. Today, Olympik Praha is a modern hotel with a stylish interior and exterior. On the facade of the building you can see colorful splashes that highlight the structure against the background of other houses. The interior is made in a modern style. A lot of wood elements were used in the design, the color palette is calm, consists mainly of pastel colors. The rooms and common areas are equipped with comfortable furniture.





Room Types at Olympik Praha 4 *

The hotel has 315 rooms in five categories:

  1. Single - single, 13 m².
  2. Double - double, 17 m².
  3. Triple - triple, 19 m².
  4. Suite - two-room: a living room with upholstered furniture and a bedroom with one double or two single beds (19 m²).
  5. Wedding Suite - apartments for newlyweds, decorated in white (19 m²).

Each room has its own bathroom with hygiene products, bathrobes, towels, slippers and a hairdryer. The rooms have a LCD TV with satellite channels, a minibar, a safe, a small refrigerator, a split system (air conditioning / heating), telephone, radio, WI-FI, a kettle. Two-room numbers are divided into a drawing room and a working zone with upholstered furniture, a table and chairs.

In addition to ordinary rooms, the Hotel Olympik 4 * (Prague) provides facilities for people with disabilities. Pets are allowed. When visiting the hotel with small children, a cot is provided. There are also special rooms for allergy sufferers.

The rooms are regularly cleaned, bed linen and towels are changed. If necessary, it is possible to use the Room-Service, for example, order food and drinks from a restaurant right in the room.





Infrastructure and services for customers

The hotel has two elevators for shared use. Infrastructure includes:

  • business center;
  • ATM / currency exchange point;
  • tour agency;
  • laundry / dry cleaning / shoe shine;
  • medical office (doctor);
  • shop / souvenir shop;
  • car rental point;
  • Private guarded parking (12 euros per day);
  • administrator's left-luggage office;
  • 3 restaurants;
  • club bar.

Hotel Olympik has its own business center, in the territory of which there is wireless high-speed Internet, several conference rooms are equipped with multimedia equipment. A translator and secretary are at guests' disposal. One of the halls has access to a small garden.

For celebrations, such as weddings, anniversaries or private parties, hotel guests are invited to rent a restaurant on the top floor of the building, which offers a beautiful panorama of the Old Town.

Other services include shuttle service, babysitting for children and a doctor. Free Wi-Fi is available in public areas of the hotel, and paid for in rooms.





Leisure and entertainment at and around the hotel

In order to remember the time spent in Prague for a long time and only on the positive side, the administration of the Olympic Hotel offers its guests a number of entertainments and events for an interesting and useful pastime. For example, at the hotel you can buy a ticket for a tour of the sights of the Czech capital. Those who lead an active lifestyle can use bicycle rental for a walk, play bowling or tennis (there is an indoor and outdoor court).

On the ground floor of the hotel there is a small casino with slot machines and roulette. A games room is equipped, and golf courses are located right behind the hotel fence.





Dining at Olympik Praha

The hotel offers accommodation on the system Bed & Breakfast, that is, the price includes only breakfast - buffet. In addition to this restaurant, in Olympik Praha 4 * there are two more establishments where the service is carried out according to the “menu-order” type. One of them is French, the second serves local and European cuisine, the taste of which can be enjoyed for lunch and dinner. There is also a bar and a pub. A wide range of alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks, light snacks and snacks are selected for the guests.

In addition to the ability to order food in the room from the restaurant, vacationers can use the service of collecting lunch on the road. Great idea for a snack on excursions.

Living conditions and approximate cost

Since time in Prague is 2 hours behind Moscow time (GMT + 01: 00 time zone), you must take this into account when traveling in order to correctly calculate arrival. Check-in at the hotel is from 15:00, check-out is until 11:00. For registration, you must have an identification document with you and a booking confirmation. Hotel Olympic accepts payment by money transfer, credit card and cash. Important: each hotel guest will be charged EUR 1 per night as a city tax.

The cost of living at the hotel varies depending on the season and type of room. The average price is from 4 to 11 thousand rubles per day. For example, a double room with one bed will cost about 8,000 rubles, a single room will cost 7,000 rubles, and a triple room will cost 11,000 rubles.





Reviews about the hotel "Olympic"

Tourists who stayed at the Olympik Praha 4 * hotel write in their reviews that the hotel is decent, but does not reach four stars - a maximum of 3. There are often cases when tourists had to call in to check-in masters to check out certain flaws in their rooms, for example, a blown lamp or a broken door lock. Another disadvantage is the quality and quantity of items for body hygiene.

On the other hand, the hotel meets the price-quality parameters. This is a very ordinary hotel, which is perfect for people who come to get to know Prague, when the room is needed only so that there is where to leave things and spend the night.

Separately, it is worth paying attention to service - the staff does everything to make guests feel comfortable. Housekeeping is provided regularly and the reception is open 24 hours a day. The staff is friendly and welcoming. Whenever possible, the administrator populates holidaymakers on the upper floors, from where you can see the Old City. But getting to your room is not so easy, especially when there is a large flow of tourists - both elevators are constantly busy.

And another huge plus is transport accessibility. Many people in the reviews highlight such a moment as ease of movement, due to the location of the "Olympic" near the metro station.

Opinions differ on nutrition. Some people think that breakfast is full and varied, while others thought it was the same type and tasteless. But in restaurants and bars there is where to roam, because the menu is very well thought out.

Based on the foregoing, we can conclude that Olympik Praha is a great option for those who do not need frills, looking for an inexpensive and convenient hotel for a few days stay in Prague.
