Night club "Zephyr", Petrozavodsk: address, opening hours, reviews

If you have a question where to go in Petrozavodsk in the evening, pay attention to the Zephyr night club. The city is small, but tourist (the capital of the Republic of Karelia is a rather popular destination among travelers), therefore, this place is always crowded.

Here you can eat, drink, dance and listen to youth music. In areas with soft chairs - relax and smoke a hookah. The presence of three halls with dance floors will delight visitors with different musical tastes. The club capacity is eight hundred people. To services of visitors: free parking, wireless Internet, cocktail card, hookah service. Please note that the club does not have billiards and karaoke, so if you want to spend time this way, you need to look for another place.

Address and opening hours

The address of the Zephyr club in Petrozavodsk is 10. Krasnaya Street, nearby there is a park called Senatorka. You will immediately recognize this place by the sign "Zefir club". It is located in a building of gray plates, in the same place as the rhythm dance school.

It is open daily from eleven in the evening until six in the morning.

Cafe and Bar in Zephyr

The average check in Zephyr is about five hundred rubles, you can pay the bill in cash or by card. There are two bars and three dance floors. The menu includes European and home-made cuisines, there are such popular dishes as Caesar salad, several types of pizza, french fries, burgers.

Since the club operates at night, there is no business lunch here. At the bar you can order beer, cocktails or stronger drinks (vodka, whiskey). Of non-alcoholic - tea, coffee, soda. Also in the club of Petrozavodsk "Zephyr" you can smoke hookah on water, milk with different tastes (light fruit or sweet chocolate). If you doubt the choice of tobacco, ask the advice of a hookah man, he will tell. Every Tuesday they have a half price discount.

Music in the Marshmallow

The club plays mainly youth music, such areas as techno, deep house. Visitors can offer their own tracks - for this you need to put them in a conversation in a group of the Vkontakte network. The main requirements for the sent compositions: without foul language, dance rhythm. No more than five tracks are accepted from one user.

Marshmallow Events

Petrozavodsk club "Zephyr" is not only a dance floor and cafe, but also a concert venue. Popular DJs perform here, for example: DJ Parade, World DJ and famous performers. Also in "Marshmallow" holidays are celebrated: March 8, Halloween - discos are held for all these events. On International Women's Day, girls were offered a completely free passage throughout the night and a fifty percent discount on all sparkling drinks. During the disco, draws of pleasant gifts took place. Visitors in costumes were allowed to go to the Halloween party freely. Events that are not related to calendar holidays ("Shotomania", "Equator") are periodically arranged.

Upcoming events

At the end of October, the Moscow lyric group playing in the style of indie rock, "Date" will perform here. There will be a presentation of the album "III", as well as already known compositions will sound. Tickets cost from five hundred rubles.

On the night of the twenty-fifth to the twenty-sixth of October in "Zephyr" will be a party of student youth Student. Come. The singer will be Dana Sokolova. Entrance tickets will cost four hundred rubles, however, privileges are provided for students. So, if you study at Petrozavodsk State University, you can book a free ticket on the website of the University. At other institutes, you can buy tickets from trade unionists for two hundred rubles. Entrance to the event is strictly from the age of eighteen, so bring along a passport confirming your age.

In November, a concert is planned for rappers Harry Topor (known for his performances at the Battle of Versus) and Tony Rauta as part of their tour in honor of the anniversary (ten years) of joint creative activity. In addition to the speech itself, an autograph session is also planned.

Past events

On October 12, in Petrozavodsk, at the Zephyr club, a concert of the famous rock band Slot took place. The concert was interesting in that fans selected their own tracks by voting. Also, for the first time during the concert, a light show program was held. The ticket price on the day of the concert reached 1800 rubles. Petrozavodsk connoisseurs of the Slot group chose such famous hits as, for example: Alfa Romeo + Beta Juliet, They Killed Kenny, Dead Stars.

In November last year, a concert was held on the tribute of the project of the film group "Heavenly Shepherd". This event was of particular interest, as it was also charitable. Part of the money from ticket sales was used to help children with disabilities. In addition to the famous songs of the legendary Viktor Tsoi ("Blood Type", "Change", "Star by the Name of the Sun"), hits of the groups "Spleen" and "Bi-2" and his own compositions from the album "On the Road" were also performed.

In the same month of last year, the so-called "shotomania" took place. Bartenders offered special prices for shots (5 cocktails for three hundred rubles), a set of sets was played for repost. One could try such original drinks as Bloody Masha, Energyhip, Universal, Twenty-Five Hours, Boyarsky, Peppermint, Double Mint.

Photographers and videographers are invited to the events. Pictures and recordings are later posted on social networks such as Vkontakte and Instagram. You can follow the poster in the same communities. The administration promptly spreads information on upcoming events and opportunities to attend them at a discount or freely.

How to get free?

Entrance to the club is paid, both for a disco and for a concert (from a hundred rubles). However, the administration of the institution often holds various repost contests, allowing winners to go free of charge. Participants must follow certain rules:

  • repost should be done in advance, if you made it at the entrance - they will not count;
  • at the entrance to the club it will be necessary to show the phone with the record, it is impossible to transfer it to friends and acquaintances;
  • the winner will be able to go free until midnight, so you should come early so as not to stand in line and not to lose because of this.

You can also go for free once a month on the night of the birthday, if your birthday is on this one. In this case, you will definitely need a passport.

Reviews

Reviews about the club "Zephyr" in Petrozavodsk are very different. Someone liked the atmosphere and the service, and someone had comments. Guests enjoy events here and the music.

But negative comments can be found in the address of the staff of the institution, mainly security.

Conclusion

In Petrozavodsk night club "Zephyr" is a pretty popular place. This is explained by the fact that concerts of famous performers are held here, holidays are celebrated, and there is also the opportunity to just relax and have a good time with friends. At the same time, prices for food, drinks and hookahs are not very high, which also pleases visitors. The main complaints are the presence of minors, employees of the institution and the cooling food at the time of serving.
