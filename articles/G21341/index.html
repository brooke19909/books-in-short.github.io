Hotels Side (Turkey) - the best holiday complexes

Side ... a beautiful and ancient city that keeps the secrets of past centuries. This is the land of magnificent Toro Mountains and amazing nature, golden beaches and fantastic landscapes. Numerous hotels are located along the coast. Side (Turkey) is especially attractive for tourists. For relaxation, there is everything you need: it has a unique climate and excellent service.

Resort Description

The city of Side is located 75 kilometers from Antalya, it is rich in many attractions and well-developed infrastructure. Here you can see fragments of the walls of ancient cities, amphitheaters, visit the temples of Athens and Apollo. Golden beaches stretched along the coast. Luxury hotels are located throughout the resort ; Side (Turkey) is noted as one of the most fashionable and prestigious holiday destinations. The city is surrounded by amazing nature with beautiful hills and magnificent waterfalls.



Luxury Hotels Side (Turkey)

The hotel complexes of this resort offer a variety of services, excellent conditions for relaxation and high service. One of the leading hotels in the ranking is Aska Washington Resort. This complex will be an excellent choice for family vacations, as well as for vacation youth companies. It is located on the enchanting sea coast, thirteen kilometers from the city center.

The hotel tourists will be met by friendly and attentive staff, rooms with all the comfort, a variety of services and entertainment. Also, Asteria Sorgun Resort 5 *, which is only a couple of kilometers from Side, also belongs to prestigious recreation areas. It offers beautiful apartments with sea views, restaurants, bars. There are swimming pools with water slides on the territory; there is the opportunity to visit the Turkish bath, sauna, massage sessions and water sports. There is a wide selection of apartments for tourists; three-, four-, and five-star Side hotels offer their services. Turkey is a country with the most developed tourism business, all the conditions for a perfect vacation are created here, from comfortable rooms to various show programs and excursions.


The combination of comfort and economy

No less attractive is the three-star hotel called Aydinbey Kings Palace, this huge complex was built in 2008 and has an elegant design, bright and spacious rooms, well-groomed territory with a huge swimming pool, bars and restaurants. The hotel has created good conditions for families with children, kids can spend time in mini-clubs and watch interesting animation programs. There will be no problems with food, the restaurant has developed an excellent children's menu. For active tourists there are sports clubs, tennis courts, a swimming pool, water slides and a spa. A private beach area near the hotel will allow you to enjoy the warm sea waters and the gentle sun. A map of hotels in Side (Turkey) will help you orient and choose the right option for yourself. Most tourists make their choice in favor of this resort. A vacation in Turkey (Side) will leave the best impressions, hotels will give you only good positive emotions, and you will want to come back again next season.
