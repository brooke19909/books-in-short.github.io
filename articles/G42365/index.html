Hotel Fame Residence Lara Spa Hotel 5 * Antalya, Turkey: photos, tours, reviews

Not far from Antalya, in the resort town of Lara, there is a beautiful Turkish hotel - Fame Residence Lara & Spa Hotel 5 *. Every year, the number of vacationers from different parts of the world, measured in thousands, comes here. All of them are united by the fact that they prefer exotic. Here guests are surrounded by excellent service, as well as beautiful interiors. The hotel has a considerable number of places for entertainment of various kinds, designed for the traveler with any needs and preferences.





Location and Summary

Often, tour operators recommend this hotel to tourists because of its proximity to the major international airport, which is located just 14 km from the territory of the recreation area - in Antalya. Thanks to this, guests do not have to stay on the road for a long time before they reach their destination.

As for the surrounding places, then around the Fame Residence Lara & Spa Hotel 5 * (Antalya) there are beautiful picturesque landscapes that can conquer with their beauty. It also offers views of the endless Mediterranean Sea, over which the sun rises and sets daily, showing a beautiful picture. As for the climate, it is tropical here, but the air masses are softened due to the proximity of the resort to the water, therefore, in the summer, tourists do not languish from the heat.

Nutrition

Entertainment

Reviews of the Fame Residence Lara & Spa Hotel 5 * hotel often say that guests are never bored. This is primarily due to the excellent entertainment program that is available in this place for recreation.

Pool

One of the favorite places for tourists in the hotel is the aquazone, where there are five pools filled with crystal clear fresh water. They also built a small water park, consisting of four slides. Near the pool there is a beautiful sun terrace, furnished with large white sun loungers and umbrellas. Here, tourists can send to the local bar, and enjoy desserts, as well as soft drinks.

Do not forget about beauty

The SPA zone of the Fame Residence Lara & Spa Hotel 5 * hotel (Turkey / Antalya) is located on a rather large territory, which is divided into three zones: aqua, thermal and massage rooms. Each of them is designed for different procedures.

The massage room has a large number of devices for relaxation, in addition, there are many tables at which professional massage therapists work. Visiting this site is free. But for the provision of services by craftsmen you need to pay separately from accommodation.





Absolutely free of charge guests can visit the thermal part of the SPA center and the aquazone, which are located in the neighborhood. The thermal zone includes a sauna and a Turkish bath - hammam. Experienced parmeisters who are specialists in high temperature issues work here. As for the aquazone, it is represented by a large indoor pool, in which the automatic water heating function is installed. This place is very popular in the winter season.

Warm sea

The coastal part of the hotel is very popular among guests, especially from May to October, when the peak season is observed. On many photos of the hotel Fame Residence Lara & Spa Hotel 5 * you can watch the beautiful beach, which is available here. It is a picturesque natural area where guests can sit perfectly on comfortable sun loungers, under the huge umbrellas that are provided here for free. The entire territory of this recreation area is strewn with fine sand and pebbles.

This place is completely safe for children to stay, as the descent into the water is gradual, and the depth near the shore is small. However, despite this, local rescuers do not recommend leaving the kids without parental supervision.





If desired, vacationers have the opportunity to use swimming mattresses and large terry towels. There is no rent for this. In some sections of the beach there are attractions that will be interesting to different groups of guests. In particular, fans of extreme sports will certainly appreciate the possibility of water skiing, parachuting or snowboarding. You can also learn all of this here, using the services of an instructor.

On the coast of the Mediterranean Sea there are several rental points for yachts, boats and catamarans. One of them is located on the beach Fame Residence Lara & Spa Hotel 5 *. In the reviews of tourists, there are often recommendations that, if you are staying with your loved one, you must definitely rent a boat and go on a small cruise along the waves of the Mediterranean Sea.
