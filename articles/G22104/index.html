VTB 24: reviews of employees and customers

VTB 24 Bank is one of the largest Russian and European brands in the field of finance. This organization is represented by a wide network of branches throughout Russia. The financial institution under consideration provides services in the segment of lending, registration of deposits, remote services, and is actively working with private companies. What do his customers say about the activities of this largest banking brand? How do employees respond to the financial institution?





General information about the bank

Before exploring the opinions of VTB 24 Bank, reviews of employees and customers about its services, we will study the basic facts about the respective brand.

The total assets of the financial institution exceeds 2 trillion rubles. The bank was established on the basis of another institution - Guta-Bank, whose shares were acquired by Vneshtorgbank in 2004. Judging by some public data - with the assistance of the Central Bank of the Russian Federation. In 2005, the Vneshtorgbank Retail Services brand appeared on the Russian market. In 2006, the new bank was renamed VTB 24. Now 100% of the shares of the financial institution are owned by VTB Bank, the main owner of which is the state. What do VTB 24 customers say about their deposit experience? Citizens, if you study the relevant points of view on thematic online portals, try to form reviews based on 3 main indicators:

- requirements for the amount of invested capital.

As for rates, such a financial institution as a whole is trying to bring it closer to the average market indicators. Mortgage Reviews

The largest segment of credit and financial services is mortgage loans. In it, the bank in question is among the leaders. What are the reviews of VTB 24 on loans issued for the purchase of housing? The corresponding orientation of opinion, in turn, is determined by such factors as:

- interest rate on loans - in combination with the duration of the mortgage agreement;

- requirements for the documents of the borrower, for his income, field of activity, credit history.

As for the rates, it is clear that due to the rather strict policy of the Central Bank of the Russian Federation to provide funds to private financial institutions, they cannot be so low as to meet the wishes of most customers. The Central Bank's key rate now is 11%, and this is almost twice as much as, for example, at the beginning of 2014. However, this indicator is actually at the level of the crisis of 2008-2009, when the mortgage market, although it slowed down, but still continued to develop.





Citizens of the Russian Federation have the right to issue loans for the purchase of housing under state programs - in particular those aimed at subsidizing the purchase of new buildings. VTB 24 Bank (customer reviews of mortgage loans confirm this) is trying in the most active way to ensure that a citizen can take this opportunity to purchase an apartment. Bank specialists explain in detail the requirements for documents required for a person to participate in the corresponding state program, the income of the borrower, as well as his credit history. Based on the opinions that appear online, it is quite difficult to track the statistics of positive decisions on housing programs from VTB 24. However, judging by the fact that there are few negative reviews indicating a bank‚Äôs unjustified refusal to satisfy applications for a mortgage loan, we can conclude that this financial institution is more likely to give a citizen a housing loan with a state subsidy, than to decline his request for a loan.

As for standard housing programs - those that are implemented on the secondary market, and therefore, in the general case, are not drawn up on preferential terms - the demand for them, which is understandable, is not yet too high due to excessive credit rates. However, the demand for such loans remains - firstly, because Russian citizens need housing, and secondly, real estate prices in many regions of the Russian Federation are significantly reduced during the crisis period. Therefore, people tend to quickly buy an apartment, albeit at higher rates, while it is relatively cheap. As for the terms of such loans in VTB 24 (customer reviews of housing loans confirm this), they objectively cannot be the most attractive due to the reasons that we have indicated above. But from the point of view of the process of organizing decision-making on loans by a bank, a financial institution does its job well. VTB 24 specialists explain in detail to people the essence of the requirements developed by the organization for borrowers, help to collect the necessary package of documents, analyze available sources for significance when making decisions on credit applications.

In general, citizens are satisfied primarily with the attitude of the bank towards mortgage borrowers, note the interest of a financial institution in giving a person a desired loan for an apartment based on an objective analysis of his solvency.

Reviews on consumer loans and car loans

In the segment of consumer loans, as well as loans issued for the purchase of automobiles, the conditions of VTB 24 Bank, as noted by the Russian clients of this financial institution, are quite consistent with current market trends, and in some segments they look more attractive.
