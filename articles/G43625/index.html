How to make camomiles from plastic bottles with your own hands?

This question is increasingly being asked: "How to make daisies from plastic bottles with your own hands?" This is due to the fact that such an ornament is made easily and naturally. And all the necessary components are always at hand. There are several different ways to create such crafts. This material contains two of the simplest ones that are best suited for beginners.





To start

Before you make daisies from plastic bottles, you need to prepare materials and tools that you will definitely need in the process. They should be at hand, this will allow you to do everything in the shortest possible time with the highest level of quality. Among the materials you need to pay attention to the following:

  1. A plastic container from under dairy products for a flower (containers from milk, kefir, yogurt or fermented baked milk are suitable). It will be better if it has a yellow cap, but this is not critical. You can also use other colors, but in this case, you need white paint.
  2. Green capacity for petals. Again - you can and another design, but then you will definitely need paint.
  3. Thick (5-8 mm 2 ) and thin (up to 1 mm 2 ) wire.
  4. In some cases, yellow nail polish may be needed.
  5. Adhesive tape or tape.
  6. Glue like PVA.
  7. Shrink green.

In turn, from the tool you need to prepare:



  • scissors;
  • pliers;
  • knife;
  • a ruler;
  • awl;
  • pencil;
  • a brush;
  • matches or a lighter.

If all this is at hand, then you can begin to figure out how to make daisies from plastic bottles on your own.

A simple solution

In this case, we need a small container (0.5 l is perfect) with a large lid. Cut it at a distance of 5-7 cm from the top. In the lid we make a hole for mounting the stem and install it. At the edges, cut out the leaves of the required shape. Extend them. If the cover is not yellow, it must be varnished with nail polish or oil

yellow paint and allow to dry. A green shrink is put on the stem and heats up. Further leaves are made. They are cut from green beer containers. Attached to the stem with a thin wire. This is the simplest and most common design of how to create daisies from plastic bottles. Photos of this performance are most often found in needlework magazines.

More complex options

In the second case, the sequence of actions is similar, but the algorithm for creating a flower is a little more complicated. If in the first case we relied on the shape of the bottle and made a craft on its basis, then this is not necessary. We take any container and cut out two circles - this will be the core. In one of them we make a hole for attaching the stem and fix it with a thin wire and glue. We cover them with yellow paint, let dry. Then we cut the petals themselves and glue them to the lower circle. Then glue the second circle over them. Petals are created according to the previously described methodology, like the stem. In this way, a large daisy can be made from plastic bottles. To do this, you only need to use a large plastic container with a capacity of 5 liters or more.

Conclusion

This article provides two ways to make daisies from plastic bottles. One of them is simpler and relies on the external shape of the bottle; it is better suited for the novice master. And the second is more complicated, but allows you to get more diverse solutions. It will be better for more experienced creators of such beauty.
