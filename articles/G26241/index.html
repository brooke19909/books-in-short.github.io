The uniqueness and attractiveness of the island of Bali

There are more and more places on our planet where crowds of tourists go every year in huge streams. Someone closer to the classic direction - Egypt, Bulgaria, Turkey, Ukraine. Someone, on the contrary, prefers exotic and goes to completely unknown places. First, it should be recalled that Bali is a small island in the Pacific Ocean, which is part of the Indonesian state. The population of the island of Bali - about four million people - is increasing significantly due to the influx of tourists. And this influx is gaining more and more volumes every year.

The island is characterized by its topography, climate and landscapes. Thanks to volcanic rocks, soils are especially fertile, which allows you to grow a large number of crops and harvest several times a year. Bali is famous for its coffee, rice and lots of fruits. A significant number of exotic plants that are familiar to us on the island, explains the same large number of national parks.

Residents of the island of Bali also have a lot of features. First

the turn is religion. Unlike the rest of the population of Indonesia, mainly Muslims, Bali professes Hinduism, and when visiting these amazing places, tourists can see the unique beauty of Hindu temples. So, on the slope of the volcano in the northern part of the island of Bali is a real Hindu temple complex, including 22 temples. Locals relate to religion with special trepidation, as well as to their traditions and culture. A tourist, seeing the dances of Balinese women - an unforgettable sight - certainly wants to learn them. Balinese people make world-famous wooden sculptures, in addition, the whole world learns from them the art of batik.

Separately, mention should be made of the restaurants of Bali. They offer tourists a large number of exotic dishes. The basis of food on the island is rice. Locals came up with more than a hundred different dishes from it, each of which is unique. Perhaps, however, the local cuisine seems a bit spicy for some. Here you will try several types of bananas, the most juicy pineapples and watermelons with yellow pulp. But apples and strawberries are fresh.

If you plan your trip in advance, you can save a lot. Last-minute tours to Bali can be booked as early as next week.
