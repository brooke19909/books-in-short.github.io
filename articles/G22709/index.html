Hotels: reviews

The rating of hotels is formed by estimates of tourists. And Vietnam hotels are no exception. Over the past twenty years, tourism in this Asian country began to develop so rapidly that the hotel infrastructure today does not lag behind the European one. Vietnamese hotels are comfortable facilities of a modern level. Many of them are included in well-known hotel chains. Tourists can choose to their taste as high-rise buildings, and bungalows, decorated in national style and having all the necessary amenities. What forms the popularity that hotels have? Reviews The rating is compiled on the basis of the assessment given by tourists. Phan Thiet

This beach town located in the south of the country is known for its pink dunes. It is a three-hour drive from Ho Chi Minh City. Here, tourists have amazing landscapes. Among the greenery of coconut palms along the coast, many small club-type hotels have been built. Each of them has its own beach, where sun loungers and umbrellas are issued to residents free of charge. The city is famous for its wide selection of seafood and local fish sauce, known throughout Vietnam. Phan Thiet - a cozy quiet resort, which is ideal for families.



The nature surrounding this city is also amazing. Of particular interest is the water stream that breaks through the sandy mountains. And the unique climate conditions of Phan Thiet have made it popular all over the world. There is always stable weather and the sun is shining. And only in August and September at night there are short rains that bring the desired coolness.

The best period for visiting the resort is any month from October to March. Nearby is the small town of Vinhao, which is famous for its mineral water throughout Vietnam. Phan Thiet is popular not only among lovers of beach vacations, but also among surfers. The hotels here are located along the entire length of a twenty-kilometer beach along Muineh Bay, called Vietnam Hawaii. Among them is Swiss Village Resort Spa 4 *.

Description

The hotel is located in the central part on the beach of Mui Nye, the name of which is translated from Vietnamese - "sacred (sacred) land." Nearby there are many restaurants, bars, night clubs. Ocean Dunes Golf Club is a five-minute drive away.

Swiss Village Resort 4 * (Vietnam) is built like a traditional Asian Imperial Palace. It is a combination of the national colors of Vietnamese, Japanese and Chinese architecture and Swiss quality and comfort. The hotel building is buried in thickets of coconut trees, which contrast favorably with fine white sand on the coast. The international airport terminal is two hundred kilometers away.



Swiss Village Resort 4 * (Vietnam) was built in 2001. The last reconstruction was carried out in 2014. the rooms were completely updated, furniture and plumbing replaced. The hotel is part of the Swiss hotel chain.

The territory of Swiss Village Resort 4 * is quite large. Its area is sixteen thousand square meters. Lawns everywhere, flowering shrubs and palm trees. You can stroll along the well-groomed paths in the evenings, admiring exotic plants and listening to the birds singing. Fountains, arbors, benches - everything is provided for a comfortable pastime.

Infrastructure

Swiss Village Resort Spa is a 4-star property. Therefore, the service offered at this hotel is quite impressive. On its territory there is a conference hall, a post office, a business center. Secure parking is provided for residents free of charge. Advance booking is not required. There are also several shops, an ATM.

Laundry facilities are available in the administrative building. Reception works seven days a week. Here, tourists order a taxi, arrange a transfer to the city and the airport, send a fax, and propagate documents.

Settling tourists quickly. If a room is available, keys are handed over immediately. Otherwise, visitors can relax in the lobby, enjoy a cold cocktail - a compliment to the hotel.

Housing stock

The Swiss Village Resort 4 (Vietnam) has ninety-eight rooms. They are located in a floor building, in villas and bungalows. Room categories are: standard, suite, studio, deluxe and superior.



Two VIP villas have private pools, four family cottages have direct access to the sea. Room sizes range from twenty to fifty square meters. For the comfort of those living there are dark wood furniture, comfortable sofas and armchairs, a work desk, chairs, a TV with satellite channels and a minibar. There is also a fridge, tea and coffee making facilities, an electric kettle and a direct dial telephone.

All rooms are air conditioned. They are tastefully furnished and equipped according to international standards.

Nutrition

Swiss Village Resort 4 * (Vietnam) offers visitors only breakfast. As evidenced by the reviews of many Russians, the buffet in the morning literally abounds with tasty and quite varied food. The menu includes cereals and yoghurts, fruits, salads, etc. In addition, by reservation, residents can have lunch or dinner at the √† la carte restaurant, as well as visit the nearest cafes. Relatively inexpensive in such establishments you can taste shark soup and fresh seafood, as well as exotic dishes from crocodile meat and even scorpions.

In addition to two restaurants, the hotel also has a bar where you can order excellent pizza. Drinks and snacks must be paid separately.





Near the hotel, right on the street, there are aquariums with live sharks, lobsters, crabs, shrimps and other marine life. Tourists order their favorite fish, which is cooked right before their eyes. Some take exotic underwater inhabitants still alive in their hands and take photos with them as a keepsake.

rules

At Swiss Village Resort 4 * (Vietnam), check-in is at two in the afternoon, check-out is at noon. There is no room charge for a child under the age of eleven only if they are accommodated in a room with parents without an extra bed. At the same time, children are not supposed to have a free breakfast. The hotel does not allow pets. Internet in both public areas and rooms is free.

Beach

Hotel Swiss Village Resort (4 *) is a hundred meters away from the sea. The beach is private, sandy. You can reach it in two minutes. Everything you need for swimming and sunbathing - sunbeds, umbrellas - can be obtained for free. The beach has a lot of fun. Tourists can rent a catamaran, rent a surfboard, scuba diving and snorkelling mask to go scuba diving and explore underwater beauty under the supervision of a licensed diving instructor.

Additional Information

Swiss Village Resort (4 *) offers its customers to buy sightseeing tours. The office is located on the ground floor of the administrative building. Here you can book a tour called "Emerald Triangle", which lasts two days and costs $ 100.

The hotel has a large pond in which fish swim, two pools 1.8 and a meter deep, respectively, a jacuzzi. Around are sunbeds and umbrellas. Golf lovers can visit the nearby club, which is just a five-minute drive from the Swiss Village Resort 4 *.
