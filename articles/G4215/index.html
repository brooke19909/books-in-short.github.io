Chokrak Lake: where is it located and how to get there?

The lake is located in the eastern part of the Crimean peninsula. The nearest village is the village with the corresponding name Kurortnoye. And twenty kilometers away is the large city of Kerch. Chokrak Lake annually attracts a huge number of vacationers. Especially in the summer.





In what area is Chokrak Lake located? How to get to it the easiest?

The resort is located in the Leninsky district of Crimea. From the Kerch bus station, minibuses leave here every thirty minutes. You can get to the lake and on your own, by car. In this case, you need to go through the village of Voikovo. But you should be prepared for the fact that immediately after the village the asphalt road will end. Further, up to Kurortny, you will have to drive along a dusty primer, along pits and potholes.

Mud Chokrak: composition and medicinal properties

The mud of Lake Chokraks is distinguished by great environmental cleanliness. And due to its healing properties, Chokrak is one of the most famous mud deposits on the planet.

The composition of the local mud includes hydrogen sulfide brine, sludge, as well as various trace elements. The sludge mud is saturated with the latter due to numerous mineral springs located at the bottom of the reservoir. The colloid content in the Chokrak mud is quite high (about 3.5 percent).





The resources of Lake Chokrak are now actively exploited by several Crimean health resorts. The leader in this list is the sanatorium "Sunrise", located in the city of Feodosia.

Chokrak mud treatment

Lake Chokrakskoe gives the world unique healing mud that is successfully used to treat the following diseases:

  • gynecological (including inflammatory processes of the female genital organs);
  • diseases of the musculoskeletal system;
  • digestive organs;
  • peripheral nervous system;
  • circulatory system.




A full course of treatment involves at least ten mud procedures. It should be noted that Chokrak mud is effectively used not only medicinally, but also for cosmetic purposes.

At the same time, the use of therapeutic mud of Lake Chokraks has a number of contraindications. This is arrhythmia, bronchial asthma, tuberculosis (in active form), diabetes mellitus, angina pectoris, cirrhosis of the liver, as well as pregnancy. In addition, treatment may be accompanied by fatigue and slightly elevated body temperature. Some vacationers feel numbness of the fingers, tingling in the joints. However, after a few days, these symptoms usually disappear.

Surroundings of Chokrak: interesting places and attractions

The surroundings of the lake are very picturesque and interesting. First of all, it is worth mentioning the so-called General's beaches (or the coast of a thousand bays, as it is also called). This is a series of wonderful sandy beaches, rocky cliffs and cozy coves. These places in a striking way attract yogis, esotericists and simply creative personalities. The coastline of General's beaches is pretty rugged. Therefore, here you can swim under the arched rocky arches and even find small grottoes. Water in the bays, as a rule, in the summer is very fast and warms up well.





There is a lake near Chokraksky and its own mountain Ararat. She, however, is much lower than her Armenian namesake. Its height is 175 meters. The mountain is located five kilometers south of the lake. In the relief - this is a pronounced plateau massif, completely covered with shrubs and herbs.
