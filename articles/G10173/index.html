Hurghada Resort City: hotel rating

If you are planning to go on vacation, then first of all you need to choose a resort and get acquainted with the rating of hotels. To do this, you need to find the hotels offered by the agency, and get acquainted with the reviews on them. Pay attention to both the list of the best hotels and the black list of hotels that Hurghada offers. Hotel ratings can be found on many sites related to tourism. But keep in mind that the opinions of tourists are always subjective.





The sun is shining all year

Hurghada is a popular resort that attracts with the gentle sea, the hot sun, plenty of entertainment and excursions, as well as high-quality and affordable service. Rest throughout the year offers Hurghada. The rating of hotels is therefore at a high level. Here the sun always shines. The coldest month is February. But at this time, the minimum air temperature is 23-25 degrees of heat, and water - 20-22 0 . The hottest month is August: air temperature reaches 35 0 heat, and water warms up to 28 degrees and above. Due to the fact that Egypt has a dry climate, summer heat here is easier to tolerate than in the Mediterranean. In addition, the heat is not so noticeable due to the constant sea breeze.





Leader in the number of hotels

Hurghada is the leader in the number of hotels. The rating of hotels in this city is not inferior to the rating of Turkish hotels, and in some parameters even surpasses them. New complexes are constantly being built. Most hotels are suitable for families with children. In addition, vacation in Egypt is quite affordable. A middle-income Russian can easily afford to stay in a five-star resort hotel. Hurghada can afford housing for all vacationers. The rating of hotels is enhanced by a convenient location, high-class service, comfort and a full range of diverse services. The staff of 5-star hotels can easily communicate with European tourists.

Buffet

Hurghada hotels provide a buffet. It is characterized by abundance and diversity. There are daily dishes for every taste: fruits, vegetables, meat and fish. Breakfast and dinner at five-star hotels for free, as these services are included in the price of the permit. You can dine on the beach or anywhere in the city.
