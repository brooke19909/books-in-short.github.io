Hotel Callisto Holiday Village 4 * (Ayia Napa, Cyprus): overview, description, rooms and reviews

Hotel Callisto Holiday Village 4 * (Ayia Napa, Cyprus) is considered by many people as a place where you can stay during your stay in Cyprus. He has several positive aspects, and in short I would like to talk about them.





Location

To begin with, a few words about where Callisto Holiday Village 4 * is located. Ayia Napa (Cyprus) - it is in this famous resort town that it is located. Moreover, in close proximity to the sea. The beach can be reached in 1-2 minutes. And this is not an ordinary resort area. This is the beach "Nissi", which is the most famous in Cyprus. Snow-white clean sand is its main feature. Due to its grooming, this beach received the EU Blue Flag .

Foam parties, dances, shows with incendiary music are regularly held here . Very close to the beach (and therefore the hotel) there are countless restaurants and bars.

True, many consider the minus that the airport is far away. It is located in Larnaca, from there it takes about an hour and a half to get to Ayia Napa. But Protaras is located nearby - a rather famous resort village.

Service

Callisto Holiday Village 4 * (Ayia Napa, Cyprus) has everything that visitors may need. If we talk about infrastructure, the hotel has an excellent conference room, car parking, an Internet corner, and a 24-hour reception.

Facilities include facilities for disabled guests, as well as babysitting services, a doctor and concierge services. On the territory of the complex there are shops, a business center, a library, a meeting room, as well as laundry and currency exchange.

Guests can take advantage of the shuttle service, but if you want an individual vehicle, you can rent a car and rent a car. To do this, you need to go to the reception and tell about your wishes. Or write this request in the reservation request.





Leisure

People who come to Callisto Holiday Village 4 * (Ayia Napa, Cyprus) prefer to spend most of their time on the beach. But in this hotel there is still entertainment of a different nature.

There are two large outdoor swimming pools where you can enjoy water games. There is another indoor (popular out of season). The hotel also has a jacuzzi, sauna, massage room and gym. Sports enthusiasts can play tennis, beach volleyball, basketball, billiards or water polo. In the evening, enjoy an animated entertainment show. For children, which parents took with them on vacation, there is a playground and a good pool.

By the way, the Callisto Holiday Village 4 * hotel (Ayia Napa, Cyprus) has its own SPA center and beauty salon. These places are very popular among fans to relax body and soul.

Nutrition

Many people, before heading to Callisto Holiday Village 4 * (Cyprus, Ayia Napa), are interested in how good the cuisine is in local restaurants and bars. If you believe the description and the reviews left, then in this plan the hotel succeeded.

To begin with, it is worth listing the institutions. There is a restaurant called Hesperides. It serves excellent international and Greek cuisine. Another restaurant known as Artemis serves Cypriot and international treats. Oceanus also offers great snacks, cocktails and cold drinks.

There are also two bars in the hotel. One is in the hotel lobby. There you can drink tea or coffee with cakes, for example. The pool bar serves exotic cocktails and fresh drinks.
