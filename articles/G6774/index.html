About the rules

In order for embroidery consisting of Slavic charms to be useful for its owner, it is necessary to subordinate the work process to a number of rules that have come down to us from the sages from ancient times. In particular, it is forbidden to make a talisman for yourself. The product made by the consanguineous will have the greatest strength. Forcing another to do a thing for himself will not work - it will not have power.

Connoisseurs in this area urge to select the most appropriate pattern and color with which it will be performed as responsibly as possible. These aspects play a significant role, have a certain meaning. Only having mastered the subtleties of meaning, they make the final decision. They sit down at work when the mood is good. The embroiderer should think positively about who the item is intended for. Do not work in a very noisy room. Turn off the music. In order for embroidery to have power, those working on it must immerse themselves in the process.

You can not embroider amulets, being sick. Girls are forbidden to work during monthly bleeding. Traditionally, Slavs do not use fire. If embroidery is made using these devices, it will only be an ornament that does not carry a protective function.

Subtlety is the key to success

If you plan to make a Slavic amulet for embroidery for a man or a woman and at the same time a small-format product is planned, it is recommended to use only one color of threads for its design. The classic way to embroider is with a cross. It should be placed in a discreet place. If a person is a believer, before starting work on embroidery it is worth praying. It will not be superfluous to conspire if a person sincerely believes in his strength.

According to experienced people, it is better to decorate decorative things with beads, and when creating a talisman, cross stitch with natural threads.

Moon and work

In order for the subject to help get rid of something, the third quarter of the cycle is suitable, and the best day is the 20th. If possible, all work should be completed at the right time.
