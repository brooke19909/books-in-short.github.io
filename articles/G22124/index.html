Verkhnyaya Dubrovo: infrastructure, ecology, communications, prices

Tired of crowds, traffic jams, smog and dust, the inhabitants of megacities not only have long considered housing outside the city a great opportunity to breathe fresh air and enjoy the surrounding greenery - many see it as a reasonable alternative to city registration. In the cottage village of Verkhnyaya Dubrovo, proximity to real wildlife makes you forget about the views of the neighboring high-rise buildings, and the presence of all the infrastructure necessary for a comfortable life changes the usual feeling of concern for the city dweller for peace and tranquility.

In the suburban quarter of Upper Dubrovo, a modern person can live comfortably and carefree, without worrying about any household problems. This housing complex, located in the suburbs of Yekaterinburg, combines the ecology of the province and the dynamism of the metropolis.

Upper Dubrovo is a village on the Tyumen tract, which is by far the most optimal offer for suburban real estate of economy class. Many consider the Verkhnee Dubrovo cottage village in the Sverdlovsk Region an ideal option for families with young children or newlyweds, as well as senior citizens, as well as lovers of good ecology.
