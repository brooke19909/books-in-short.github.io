LCD "Stolichny" (Kazan): description, prices

Kazan is one of the most beautiful cities in Russia, the capital of the Republic of Tatarstan. Today the city is actively developing and rebuilding. There is nothing strange and surprising in the fact that more and more residential complexes appear here, where excellent living conditions are created. LCD "Stolichniy" (Kazan) is a striking representative of modern complexes, quite popular among local residents. Our task is to evaluate the project, understand all its advantages and disadvantages, and also familiarize ourselves with the pricing policy of the developer.





about the project

Residential complex "Stolichny" - three modern multi-sectional new buildings built using monolithic technology. Convenient location, developed infrastructure of the district, spacious apartments of improved layout and affordable prices are what every apartment owner will receive.

Construction Features

Buying an apartment in a new building, I want to once and for all forget about all domestic issues and problems, enjoy the comfort and convenience. Houses in the LCD "Stolichniy" (Kazan) are being built using modern technologies and energy-efficient materials. Thus, in the summer, every resident will enjoy a pleasant cool without the use of climatic equipment, and in winter - comfort and warmth without additional warming. The correct microclimate in each apartment, excellent soundproofing properties, stained-glass panoramic glazing - all that every new resident will receive.

But the Suvar Holding company in the entire history of its existence in the construction industry managed to prove its reliability, decency, and to implement several successful projects, each of which is only getting better. Location

The picturesque view of one of the main attractions of the city, the Kazanka river, sunsets and sunrises that you will meet every day - all this is the residential complex "Capital".

One of the most prestigious areas of the city - Novo-Savinovsky was chosen for construction. If necessary, you can reach the city center and all its attractions in a few minutes. You enjoy stunning views, stroll through parks, squares, the river embankment, all the important events in the cultural life of the city take place near you. Is this not a fairy tale? And it was embodied for you by the developer Suvar Holding.





Transport accessibility

For all residents of the modern metropolis, the issue of transport accessibility is quite significant. Not a single complex located in a picturesque and ecologically clean area, remote from the hustle and bustle, will never be popular if it is simply impossible to get to the city without a personal car. LCD "Stolichny" (Kazan) is located just a 10-minute drive from the central part of the city. You can walk to it in just 20-30 minutes. You live surrounded by all significant objects, you can walk every weekend in the most beautiful places of Kazan, while you will live in an isolated complex, free from fuss and noise.

Nearby is the Kazanka River. All those who have already visited the construction site note that this is the main advantage of the project. The enchanting look of water really calms and pacifies, here I want to stay for a long time.

Apartments, layouts

Buying real estate is perhaps the largest acquisition. That is why we are so anxious in choosing a residential complex. Apartments in the residential complex "Stolichniy" will satisfy the requirements of even the most demanding resident. All apartments in the complex are rented out completely ready to move: all finishing work has been completed, plumbing, entrance and interior doors have been installed. All that remains to be done by the owner is just to bring in furniture and add the necessary comfort. Layouts of apartments meet all the requirements of modern buyers. The correct forms allow you to place all the necessary pieces of furniture and use every square meter of area.
