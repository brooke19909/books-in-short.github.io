Hotel Sofitel Taba Height 5 * (Egypt / Taba): photos and reviews of tourists

If you plan to spend an unforgettable vacation on the Red Sea in the resort of Taba (Egypt), Sofitel Taba Heights 5 * can be a great option for a comfortable stay.

Location, description, photo

This hotel is located on the seafront, 20 kilometers from Taba and 40 kilometers from the airport. The distance to the air harbor of Sharm El Sheikh, where tourists from Russia usually fly, is about 250 kilometers.

Sofitel Taba Heights 5 * hotel was built in 2004. Since then, several renovations have been carried out here. The hotel complex has 442 comfortable rooms located in three four-story buildings. All apartments are decorated in a traditional Arabic style. There is a TV with cable channels, a balcony, a safe, air conditioning, a telephone, a minibar and a bathroom with a hairdryer and shower.





Meals at Sofitel Taba Heights are all-inclusive. The hotel has a disco bar, two restaurants, bars, swimming pools (including those with mineral and salt water), a kids club, sports grounds, spa, garden, conference rooms and much more.

Today we decided to save you time and energy and offer to familiarize yourself with the generalized comments of our compatriots about their stay in Sofitel Taba Height 5 * (Egypt). Running a little ahead, we note that most tourists were satisfied with their choice. According to them, the hotel is an excellent combination of price and quality. But we learn about everything in more detail.





Residence

Judging by the reviews, almost all tourists were quite satisfied with the rooms offered to them in this hotel. So, according to their statements, the apartments in the hotel complex are spacious, with good repair and excellent views. Tourists are advised to check in rooms located above the third floor if possible. This will provide you with truly magnificent views of the Red Sea, the beautiful grounds of the hotel and the surrounding area.





As for the furniture and appliances in the rooms, they are not new. But everything is in good and working condition. If something goes wrong, then contact the reception. Malfunctions will be resolved quickly enough.

Check-in, cleaning

As our compatriots note, administrators of Sofitel Taba Height 5 *, as a rule, do not seek to accommodate newly arrived guests before two in the afternoon. Therefore, if you plan to arrive at the hotel in the morning, then be prepared for the fact that you will need to wait for check-in. However, this does not mean that you have to patiently sit near the reception. So, you can go for breakfast or lunch, change clothes and swim in the pool or go straight to the beach. You can leave your things at the reception.





As for the cleaning of the rooms, almost all the guests were satisfied with its quality. According to them, thanks to the efforts of the cleaners in the apartments it was always clean. True, here no one will twist you figures of towels in the form of swans, etc. But this is fully compensated by the quality of cleaning.

Territory, location

As for the own territory of Sofitel Taba Heights 5 * (Taba, Egypt), then it is very large, well-groomed, clean and beautiful. There are a lot of blooming greenery, palm trees, nice places for walks and photo shoots. In addition, the hotel offers stunning views of the lights of Jordan and Saudi Arabia in the evenings. Tourists enjoyed this spectacle with pleasure.

As for the location, people who prefer a measured rest without having to visit nightclubs every day, liked the location of the hotel. After all, Sofitel Taba Heights 5 *, the reviews of which we are considering, is located at some distance from large cities. But the location of the hotel is very convenient for those who want to go on an excursion to Israel.

Some domestic tourists also complained about the fact that Russian tour operators do not offer direct flights to Taba. So, almost all travelers are forced to get here from Sharm El Sheikh Airport. And this makes the path quite long and difficult. Indeed, on the road you have to spend about 3.5-4 hours.

Nutrition

In general, most tourists in their reviews showed unanimity regarding this issue. So, in their opinion, the food in Sofitel Taba Height 5 *, although not bad, is still somewhat less than the level of a five-star hotel. According to our compatriots, they would like to see a wider selection of dishes offered in the restaurant. Of course, no one was left hungry here. After all, there were always on hand dishes from meat, fish, chicken, side dishes, snacks, salads, fresh vegetables, fruits, pastries and desserts. But still, many travelers expected a little more from a five-star hotel.





As for the bars, tourists were generally satisfied with their work. Of the drinks, many praise the local beer and the cocktails offered at the bars. But the quality of red wine, some guests were not satisfied.

Sea

Since almost all tourists come to Egypt for an excellent beach holiday, this issue plays a very important role in choosing a hotel. As for the hotel complex Sofitel Taba Height 5 * under consideration, then, according to our compatriots who have visited here, lovers should swim in the warm sea and bask in the gentle sunshine. So, the hotel is located just 50 meters from its own large sandy beach. As tourists note, sun loungers and parasols are abundant and enough for everyone. So the guests will not have to get up early in the morning and run ashore to take a deck chair. On the beach Sofitel Taba Heights 5 * (Egypt, Taba) there is a bar and a Bedouin cafe, where you can comfortably arrange on soft pillows and smoke a hookah.

As for entering the water, according to travelers, stones are found at the bottom. However, there is a site where you can enter the sea along the sandy bottom. Therefore, in general, rubber shoes will not be superfluous, but you can do without it too. Also on the beach there is a pier along which you can go down to the depths.

Near the coast there is a small coral reef. Its inhabitants are not as bright and varied as, for example, in Sharm el-Sheikh, however, armed with a mask for snorkeling, you can quite admire the funny marine inhabitants.
