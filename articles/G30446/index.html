Sony Xperia J - model review, customer reviews and experts

Key Specifications

The smartphone from the Japanese manufacturer was introduced in 2012. Therefore, at the moment it no longer has outstanding technical characteristics compared to the budget options of today's market. Android 4.0 was chosen as the operating platform for this phone. The "filling" of the phone consists of a single-core processor with a clock frequency of 1 GHz. Of course, the current budget models for the most part have dual-core processors, but do not forget that two years ago such models of smartphones were just entering the middle segment of the market. The manufacturers decided to install 512 MB as RAM. The internal memory of the phone has 4 GB, of which only 2 GB are free, which can be used as storage for small files. But there is an opportunity to increase the total memory of the phone up to 32 GB. The battery capacity is 1750 mAh.

As for the performance of the smartphone, it leaves much to be desired. In the process of working with the phone, obvious slowdowns and freezes were noticeable. In many respects, the reason for this is not too large RAM, and if you take into account that the Android 4.0 operating system requires a sufficiently large amount of it, then there is even less memory left for third-party programs.



Design

The Sony Xperia J smartphone reviewed in this article has a very interesting design that is similar to many other models of this company. Moreover, you can buy this smartphone in various variations. Fans of a more conservative style in phones can buy a black version. People who are bored with the standard color for many smartphones can buy the white, pink or even gold model Sony Xperia J. But when choosing, remember that the color will change only for the back panel, while the entire screen and its edging will always be made in black.



Ergonomic features

If you take the phone in your hands, you can understand that it turned out to be quite average in size. You can easily control it with one hand, while it does not seem that you have at your disposal a small phone the size of a pager. With a width of 61 mm and a thickness of 9.2 mm, the length of the smartphone is 124 mm. The weight of the smartphone is 124 g.

Having carefully examined the design, several conclusions can be drawn about the Sony Xperia J. smartphone. Sound and communication

In general, the sound of a smartphone is hardly outstanding. It is clearly inferior in this aspect to its competitors from companies such as NTS and Samsung. In the headphones, the sound is heard at an acceptable level, but the volume may not be enough to interrupt the hum of knocking wheels. In addition, the sound from the speakers is not optimistic. The melodies that he publishes are somewhat flat, and the low frequencies are completely trimmed. As for communications, the voice of the interlocutor is heard well. But if you are sitting in a noisy crowd, you need to strain your hearing even if you set the maximum volume level. The vibrating alert is also quite weak. Therefore, if you set the silent mode using vibration, then be prepared for the fact that there may be missed calls.

Camera and photos

Japanese developers equipped their phone with a five megapixel camera. The video management process is quite simple. For example, if necessary, you can set the shooting both by pressing the screen of the smartphone, and using the button. In addition, you can set the shooting sound. It is also possible to set a timer if necessary. At the same time, the timer can be set to 10 seconds of delay directly on the Sony Xperia J. camera. Photos that result from shooting cannot be called outstanding. Of course, one should not expect good detail from the budget model of the objects being shot. However, photos taken with the camera are slightly blurry. Their detailing is also at a low level. As a result, we can conclude that the Sony Xperia J phone has a camera, which is standard for a budget model.



Screen

Are there any features that the budget model Sony Xperia J has? The touchscreen of the phone is 4 inches diagonal, which is the normal size for a budget smartphone. The screen resolution is 480x854. Moreover, the latter can display up to 16 million colors. The features of the screen of this smartphone include the fact that it is covered with specially tempered glass from Gorilla Glass, which is famous for its strength. Typically, budget smartphones are installed cheaper glass options. Viewing angles and screen brightness are at a good level even in daylight. The only thing that raises questions is the contrast of colors. When you look at the phone, it seems that the colors are reproduced slightly embellished.

Smartphone Options

When purchasing this model of smartphone, its owner will receive not only the phone itself, but also several additional accessories that can be useful when using the phone. Firstly, the phone comes with a brief instruction manual that can help in the operation of the smartphone in case of questions. Secondly, a USB cable, which can be used both in the process of connecting the phone to a computer, and when charging. Thirdly, the charger. Fourth, this is a proprietary headset from the Japanese company Sony in the form of headphones with a microphone, which is convenient to use when talking.



Prices for this model

Depending on where you will purchase this model of smartphone from a Japanese manufacturer of smartphones, the prices for it may be different. In general, the price of Sony Xperia J, the firmware of which uses the Android 4.0 operating system, ranges from 150 to 200 dollars. In addition, the cost of a smartphone is also determined by its configuration. For example, if you purchase a phone with an additional memory capacity of 16-32 GB, then you will have to pay another 30-40 additional US dollars for the phone.

Sony Xperia J, customer reviews and experts

Since the phone was launched on the market a relatively long time, because two years is a lot for the smartphone market, today you can find a large number of reviews that describe the smartphone both on the positive and negative sides. Moreover, if ordinary buyers used the smartphone only for practical purposes, and their reviews are appropriate, then the experts' opinion is based on a comparative analysis of this model with competitors. As pluses, many ordinary smartphone users highlight its screen and build quality. Indeed, if we take as an example a budget competitor represented by the NTS One V, then the Gorilla Glass coating against its background looks much more respectable. As a minus, many people who bought a smartphone highlight low performance. Indeed, even with a slight smartphone load, you can notice the interface freezes.

The opinion of experts on this phone is generally positive, as they understand that for a price of $ 150 it is difficult to find a model that will not slow down and have outstanding characteristics. However, if we consider this phone in the context of competition with such communicators as the NTS One V or Samsung Nexus S, then in some aspects the smartphone from the Japanese concern is inferior. For example, such aspects are the sound level and quality of the photographs taken by the camera.
