The most famous hotels in Cheboksary

Cheboksary, as the capital of the Republic of Chuvashia, is visited annually by a huge number of tourists. Not surprising, because in this city they have something to see. There are many museums, theaters and galleries, as well as various architectural monuments, among which religious buildings (churches, churches, cathedrals, mosques and chapels) are not the least. Before the guests of the city many hotels openly open their doors. Cheboksary are always ready not only to meet guests, but also to offer them a great vacation.

Rich choice

The number of tourists who want to get acquainted with the nature and history of Chuvashia is constantly growing. In the capital, comfortable and very comfortable hotels have been built for them. Cheboksary is a city that always welcomes guests. Here for visitors all conditions are created. Any tourist can choose an institution for living that matches his desires and financial capabilities. There are about forty of them in the city. For guests of the capital are provided:

  • more than 30 hotels of different star categories;
  • hostels
  • mini-hotels;
  • apartments.

Each of these establishments has its own advantages. The choice of a specific client depends on his desires and needs.





Those who decided to come to Cheboksary are welcomed by hotels. Every visitor is welcome here and ready to offer him decent living conditions. The city has facilities for every taste: large and small, located on the outskirts or in the center, luxurious or family-friendly. In addition, hotels in Cheboksary, in addition to decent service, always offer quite reasonable and reasonable prices. This factor also cannot be ignored.

Hotel "Tourist"

For people who came to rest in Cheboksary, the Tourist Hotel is considered an ideal option. Of course, because the hotel is located on the outskirts of the city in an ecologically clean area. In fact, this is a hotel and entertainment complex, in which everything is provided for a pleasant and active holiday.





The facility is located on 52 Universitetskaya Street. It includes two separate buildings. The first category numbers are placed:

  1. Economy - with comfortable furniture, TV and shower.
  2. Standard - with a double bed, a plasma TV (with satellite TV) and a mandatory set of personal hygiene in the shower room.
  3. Improved. The room is more spacious than the standard. In addition, a bathrobe is provided for each visitor.
  4. Apartments. This is a two-room suite in which truly royal living conditions are created.

In addition, the building also has a separate fitness room. In the second building there are numbers:

  1. Suite It is additionally equipped with air conditioning and individual safe. And after taking a shower in the bathroom, visitors can use a hairdryer.
  2. Studio. It is decorated in a romantic style and provides visitors with a maximum range of additional services, including even a kettle with cutlery.

In addition, the building also houses the Dacha cafe, a sauna with a swimming pool, and the Dozari karaoke bar. For those who came to have a good time in Cheboksary, the Tourist Hotel is best suited. Even many locals choose this place for personal meetings, small celebrations and official events.

Hotel Sport

For those who are limited in means, but prefer to live closer to the city center, the Sport Hotel, opened in 2001, is suitable. Cheboksary is a place where people with different abilities come. Not everyone is able to afford to stay in a luxury hotel. This is especially true of city guests who have arrived on a business visit. They are offered modest, but quite decent living conditions.

The hotel has a total of 154 rooms. Of these, most are budget and comfort (for one, two or four people, with amenities on the floor). There are also several suites and junior suites (with kitchen). But each of them must have air conditioning, a refrigerator, TV, telephone and a minimum set of dishes.





The friendly staff tries to maintain a homely atmosphere in the hotel and a fairly high level of service. Not far from this establishment is the Olympic Stadium. This is probably why athletes are constantly staying at the Sport Hotel for the period of the competition.

Hotel Volga

One of the most prestigious hotels in the city of Cheboksary is the Volga Hotel. It is located directly in the historical center of the capital within walking distance from the main attractions. The establishment has a total of 43 rooms. Basically, these are standard rooms - with a telephone, a TV and a combined bathroom. For two people there are rooms with an increased area, in which, in addition to comfortable furniture, there is an additional work desk. These rooms are ideal for business travelers.





In addition, the hotel has a chic two-room suite and a separate room designed for people with disabilities. But the hotel management does not forget about those who are accustomed to increased comfort. For such clients, the hotel has a specially equipped presidential suite. In addition to the refined interior, it even has a Turkish steam room.

Throughout the hotel, visitors enjoy free internet access. There is also a sauna, indoor pool, gym, laundry, dry cleaning, luggage storage and ATM. Guests eat, usually in a restaurant. If you wish, you can order food in your room or purchase separately packed lunches. This hotel is considered one of the most prestigious in the city, so it is better to reserve places in it in advance.
