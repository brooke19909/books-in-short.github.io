Hotel "Victoria", Minsk: address, photos and reviews

The right choice of hotel is one of the most important components of a stay in any city, as well as a good vacation. Sometimes we do not attach much importance to this parameter, but it is precisely the problems and inconveniences during the stay at the hotel that can nullify the whole vacation or spoil the impression of a business trip.

Hotel "Victoria" (Minsk) is one of the best options provided for guests to stay in the Belarusian capital.





It is located in the city center, a 5-minute walk from the embankment of the Svisloch River and Victory Park. What can residents like here? What facilities does the hotel provide?

  • Often guests pay attention to the place where the hotel is located, they are interested in how quickly you can get to all the main attractions from there. Sometimes the level and category of the hotel does not matter much if the place is required only for an overnight stay.
  • When choosing a hotel, guests need to inquire whether this place is the best option for young people to relax or whether it is designed for older people, whether families or tourists who travel alone prefer to stay there.
  • It will not be superfluous to take an interest in the hotel's infrastructure, as well as how much breakfast (lunch or dinner) costs in the local restaurants and cafes.
  • A very important and almost decisive factor when choosing a temporary residence is its cost. After all, city guests often plan exactly what amount they are willing to pay for housing.

About hotel classification

About 30 hotel classifications are known . Classification systems of stars, crowns, letters, etc. are common. The most commonly used stellar classification system. The number of stars indicates the level of comfort and service at the hotel. The more of them, the hotel is considered better and more suitable for a comfortable stay.

Hotel "Victoria" (Minsk) belongs to the category of "four stars."

What does the "star" of a hotel mean in practice?

  • One "star" means that in the hotel all rooms are of the same type, their area is up to 10 square meters. m. Facilities are located on the floor: the bathroom and toilet are shared by several rooms, the TV is in the lobby. In a one-star hotel, meals are not included in the list of services provided.
  • Two "stars" indicate the availability of several types of rooms in the hotel. Breakfast is usually served here. The room has a bath (shower) and toilet.
  • Three "stars" are a guarantee of the presence in the room (10-12 sq. M) of a TV, telephone, mini-bar (refrigerator). Such a hotel provides, as a rule, the provision of at least two additional services. There is a pool, sauna, hairdresser, gym, massage room. In addition, the hotel has a conference room or a business center. The rooms must have a bathroom where you can find soap, shower gel, shampoo.
  • Four "stars" are evidence that the level of service and comfort in such a hotel is above average. A bar and a restaurant will be open here around the clock. The services of the guests will be provided with a currency exchange office, laundry service, business center, a fitness center with a sauna and a swimming pool is required. In addition to a TV, a telephone, and a minibar, the room will have air conditioning, a hair dryer, and a safe. Typically, the room area is about 13 square meters. m
  • Five "stars" are the designation of a luxury hotel. The service here is organized at the highest level. The doorman will certainly meet the guests, the porter will take care of their luggage. The rooms in these hotels are served around the clock, if desired, the room serves breakfast or dinner. In addition to the standard set of services, exclusive ones are also provided. The size of the rooms in the luxury hotel is from 16 square meters. m

About check-in and check-out

When checking into a hotel, travelers should not forget about two important things: check-in time and check out time. Each hotel has its own parameters. As a rule, they check into the hotel after 14:00, release rooms from 11:00 to 12:00.

The Victoria Hotel (Minsk) also complies with this rule.

But some hotels, if there are free rooms, allow early check-in, in most cases late check-out is fraught with the need for surcharge.

If the room is released with a delay of several hours, its full price may be required. Guests will be well advised to pre-arrange the possibility of early check-in or late check-out from the hotel. Mostly hotels are to meet guests. When you release a room with a little delay, usually there are no problems.

Hotel Victoria (Minsk)

The photo represents the exterior of the hotel. Those interested can admire the presentable design of the facade and the modern design of the building's architecture.





The four-star Victoria Hotel (Minsk, Pobediteley 59, Minsk) is a complex that includes a hotel, restaurants, a business center, a casino, a club, a fitness club and a beauty salon. The building is located on the main avenue of the country in the immediate vicinity of the Minsk-Arena sports complex. Victoria is one of the most popular hotels in the capital. Since 2007 (opening date), it has gained popularity thanks to the wide possibilities of creating conditions for a decent living and recreation, as well as for full business.

For the convenience of guests, the Victoria Hotel has 169 modern rooms of various types equipped with the latest technology, from individual air conditioning systems to high-speed Internet access. A favorable impression of the location of the hotel will help to make a view from the windows of the Svisloch River.

The business center of the Victoria Hotel consists of 7 conference rooms where you can rent audio and video equipment, a multimedia projector and other equipment.





Those who wish to spend leisure time in the hotel complex "Victoria" will find classes for everyone to suit themselves. For those who like to try their luck, there is the opportunity to spend time at the Victoria Casino. The night club "Nika" also offers active leisure.

In addition, the complex is equipped with a wellness center, a beauty salon and 2 restaurants with 120 seats.

It offers guests

Hotel Victoria is located near Victory Park, where guests can stroll along the Svisloch embankment and enjoy the fountains. Some rooms have the opportunity to enjoy beautiful views of the river and the park. Not far from the hotel is the Republican Tennis Center, here you can rent a court.

Free Wi-Fi is provided on site, and a guarded parking lot is also equipped for guests.

Reviews

Guests of the Victoria Hotel share their impressions of their stay in the networks. Their reviews are mixed.

Many thank the staff for the hospitality and warm welcome, note the hospitality, courtesy, friendliness of the staff, the exceptional cleanliness of the hotel.

The authors of the reviews consider comfortable rooms in which you can relax, thank you for comfortable, soft beds.

Separately, former guests thank for organizing a free multi-level parking.

The location of the hotel is considered successful, many like the view from the windows of the rooms.

Some liked the organization of smoking areas, as well as the availability of smoking rooms. But at the same time they complain that the specific smell of tobacco is difficult to erode from them.

Visitors were also satisfied with the quality of breakfasts, the smooth operation of the Internet.

Many reviewers positively characterize the hotel infrastructure, the quality of the additional services provided, and believe that everything is there for relaxation.

There are those who remained dissatisfied with their stay at the hotel: they came across cold, damp rooms, they had to wait a long time for an order in a restaurant, the pool turned out to be shallow, etc. Someone considers hotel accommodation to be too expensive, speaks about the discrepancy of prices with the level of service.

But in general, the hotel is characterized as a solid, calm, comfortable and worthy place of stay, perfectly suited for relaxation and work.

Everyone who knows how to appreciate the pleasant atmosphere and courtesy of the staff, comfort and substantial leisure, is looking forward to the Victoria Hotel (Minsk).
