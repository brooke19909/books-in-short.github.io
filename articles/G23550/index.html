The location of the guest house. How to get to him

How to get to the guest house "Valentine" (Gelendzhik)? The address of this fabulous place is as follows: Academician Shirshov Street, house number 14. Those who prefer to travel by private car can purchase a map.

And now we will share information for those who arrive:

  • By plane. It lands either in Gelendzhik, or in Anapa, or in sunny Krasnodar. There, a tourist can take a taxi or warn the hotel about his arrival; he will certainly be met.
  • By train. He arrives at the station of Anapa, Novorossiysk or the city of Krasnodar. From there you can leave by car, bus or with representatives of the guest house.
  • By bus. He arrives at the bus station of the same cities. It can also be reached by bus or taxi.




Features of the guest house "Valentine"

The cozy hotel consists of three buildings - two- and three-story. The whole territory is completely well-groomed, paved with tiles, while walking, guests will not be able to pass by the beautiful flowers and trees carefully planted by the owners.

Total guest house "Valentine" (Gelendzhik, Blue Bay) has 24 rooms. Each guest has the opportunity to choose something to their taste.

Each of the rooms has beautiful upholstered furniture, a TV with cable TV , a split system, as well as a private bathroom and toilet. A refrigerator in the room is also available.

The rooms are regularly cleaned and linen changed, but only by agreement with the client. Without prior agreement, no one enters the room.

If you wish, you can accommodate a few additional beds due to folding chairs or sofa beds.

For those who came to the guest house "Valentine" (Gelendzhik) on their honeymoon, there are special luxury apartments. These are suites with an increased comfort zone. The room offers a large double bed and a small gift from the hotel - a bottle of champagne in the refrigerator.

On the territory of the house there is a guarded, free parking for cars.

Guests can order a delicious breakfast in the kitchen for a nominal fee (200 rubles).
