And so the pools, services, animation - as in the "five". Convenient, isn't it?

Pattaya and the nuances of relaxation in the most scandalous resort in Thailand

A budget tourist who can‚Äôt afford to sail or fly to the islands, chooses for recreation the northern coast of the Gulf of Thailand. Pattaya can be reached by bus or train from Bangkok in just two hours. But this resort arose from a small fishing village where American soldiers rested during the Vietnam War. Ladies of easy virtue from all over Thailand came to the guys who missed women with money. Yes, so they stayed there.

For respectable (and often family) vacationers, hotels have been built on the outskirts of the city. There the sea is cleaner and the beaches are more beautiful. Location

The huge complex "Ambassador" is located on the southern outskirts of Pattaya. Jomtien Beach is so clean that tourists come here from the city to relax. The complex is located on the first line from the sea. And almost all the buildings are close to the beach. The inhabitants of Ambassador City Tower Wing can only go a minute to the coast.

This is the largest resort in Southeast Asia located near the Sukhumvit road connecting Jomtien with Pattaya. Some tourists consider this location to be excellent, especially if the windows face the opposite side of the road. After all, traffic on Sukhumvit Road is quite busy. But on the outside of the hotel, tourists will find many infrastructure objects.

The southern outskirts of Pattaya is not a wilderness at all. Across the street from the entrance to the hotel there is a grocery and clothing market, near many inexpensive shops and massage parlors. There are scooter rental offices, and excursion bureaus, currency exchange, ATMs, and a pharmacy. Also within walking distance, tourists will find a large selection of cafes, restaurants and eateries. Pattaya U-Tapao Airport is 30 kilometers away and Walking Street is 15 kilometers away.

Nutrition

Unlike Egypt and Turkey, in Thailand few hotels operate on an all-inclusive basis. So in the Ambassador City Tower Wing hotel guests are offered only breakfast. But during the day you can eat without leaving the hotel. Firstly, upon arrival you can upgrade your breakfast to half or full board. In the first option, it is allowed to replace dinner with lunch. Secondly, you can pay a la carte restaurants in the complex.

Guests of other buildings are fed breakfast in separate rooms, and the residents of Ambassador City Tower Wing are ordered entry there. But you can visit other restaurants and bars. Upon check-in, guests are given a map of the complex, where catering establishments and their specialization are indicated. It:

But the complex "Ambassador" has a reserved stretch of coast, with its sunbeds and umbrellas.

Another thing is that the guests of the Ambassador City Jomtien Tower Wing should share all this beach ammunition with the guests of Ocean Wing, Inn Wing and Garden Wing. The largest swimming pool is located at Ambassador City Tower Wing Hotel. Its dimensions are impressive: 80 x 80 meters, a real lagoon! Near the beach there is another pool (25 x 50 m). Free services at Ambassador City Tower Wing (Thailand, Pattaya)

For guests of the whole complex there is a fitness room with brand-new gym equipment. Guests can park their vehicles in a guarded parking lot. The hotel also has a 24-hour front desk.
