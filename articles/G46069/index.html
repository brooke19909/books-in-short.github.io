Hotel "Sevastopol" in Moscow: photo, address, reviews

In the south of the capital of our vast Motherland, not far from the picturesque Bitsevsky Park, the three-star Sevastopol Hotel is located. In Moscow, this hotel is popular with guests arriving through Domodedovo Airport due to its proximity. The choice in favor of this place is due to the location of the hotel directly in the city. Despite the fact that the hotel complex was built back in 1977, both buildings are in good condition. And one of them has already undergone major repairs in the new millennium. The hotel complex was being built for the opening of the 1980 Summer Olympics in order to accommodate numerous tourists from all over the world. Since those years, the tradition of introducing multilingual staff into the state has been preserved.

Bitsevsky Park is the second largest in the capital and is included in the historical territory specially protected by the state. Hotel address

As noted earlier, the nearest metro stations from the hotel complex are Kakhovskaya and Sevastopol. From the designated stations, you can walk to the place in 5 minutes, if you have bulky luggage, you can use the bus if you wish. Accommodation prices

Despite the difference in the class of buildings, housing prices are not much different from each other. For comparison: a double room in a renovated building will cost the guest 3,600 rubles per day, the same room in the old-style building will cost 1,000 rubles less. Accommodation alone will cost guests more. By tradition, children under 12 years old have the right to stay free of charge, but without providing a separate bed. If desired, breakfast can be ordered in the rooms of the 2nd building for an additional fee of 250 rubles. A deluxe continental breakfast is already included. Future guests should be warned that you need to be prepared for some changes in payment for accommodation at the Sevastopol Hotel complex in Moscow. The price market in the hotel business is able to adapt to the current economic situation. Therefore, before booking rooms, it is better to clarify the rates. The complex also provides discounts on accommodation for numerous (at least 20 people) groups. Guests planning to check-in at the Sevastopol complex should be warned that cancellation of a room reservation is not possible.
