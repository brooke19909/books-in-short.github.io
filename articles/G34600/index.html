Excursion into the past

According to one version, the name of the city means "white bride", and all because many years ago the Turks captured the coast, who were fascinated by the beauty of local girls.

Despite the fact that the city is a little over 150 years old, the history of human settlements here is rooted in the distant past. The coast in these parts has been inhabited since ancient times. The settlement of the Lower (Early) Paleolithic is located a few kilometers from the city and reveals to us the unique attraction of the city - dolmens. These are stone buildings overgrown with moss of incomprehensible form and obscure purpose, which are already more than 200 thousand years old. Perhaps these are the tombs of the ancient nobility or pagan constructions. In any case, they have the strongest energy, and it is considered a good sign to visit here and touch them. It is known that the people who left these cultural monuments were quite developed, were engaged in agriculture and cattle breeding, traded with the Greeks.

Foundation History

When the Slavs and Byzantines appear in the southeastern territories of the Black Sea coast , they begin to equip their settlements, which were ravaged by the Mongol-Tatars in the 12th century. Since the end of the 15th century, Turkish rule has been firmly established here. In the place of modern Gelendzhik there was a village from which the export of young slaves was carried out.

The yoke continued for three centuries, until in 1829 Russia liberated the Black Sea coast, winning victories in the Russian-Turkish wars. On July 23 (according to the new style on August 4), 1831 the city of Gelendzhik was founded, the sights of which are striking in their antiquity and beauty. One of the first attractions is the Gelendzhik fortress, built during its foundation. During the Crimean War, the fortress was blown up and abandoned by Russia. In 1857, the fortress was returned by the Russians.



The development of the city in the twentieth century

The population of the city consisted mainly of descendants of peasant immigrants from the far reaches of Russia, as well as representatives of some other nationalities, in particular Greeks, Poles, Turks, and Armenians. In 1900, the first health resort, a library was opened in the city, temples and churches were built, a post office appeared, parks and squares were broken up, and the construction of a pier near the sea was completed. Soon Gelendzhik gains the status of a city. During the Great Patriotic War, it became a hospital city. It was practically ruined by bombing. After the war, an intensive restoration began, and it was rebuilt anew. Horticulture and viticulture flourish here.

A few words about climate

If you think that this city can be visited only in the summer, then imagine the dark piercing waters of the bay and snow-white snow all around, as well as the healing sea air. Winters here are mild, with temperatures around zero. Often you can meet waterfowl near the water, swans are frequent guests here. The climate here is dry, so in the summer there will be no sweltering heat when there is nothing to breathe, as, for example, in Sochi.

Gelendzhik: attractions and attractions in 2014

Every year in the summer, the city hosts a traditional costume summer carnival, leads its brass band in an old form, and in the finale, the audience will enjoy the fireworks. In July, you can taste the best wine from producers for every taste at the festival "Wines of the Kuban", as well as find out the history of production. You can also try your hand at the summer dance festival "Russian Coast", contestants come from different countries.

In September, Alexander Pankratov-Cherny holds the annual Southern Nights Art Festival. Also, the Hydroaviation show is held every two years on the shore of the bay, the capabilities of amphibious aircraft are demonstrated on this show. On September 3, the final of the race for the Michael Mil Cup (the famous designer of domestic aircraft) is expected.

Gelendzhik will not make you bored! Attractions and entertainment will not leave tourists indifferent. City guests will enjoy visiting Stvorny Lighthouse - the oldest building in the city center, Old Park - the only architectural theme park on the Black Sea coast, as well as a safari park, water parks, dolphinarium, and Olympus park. Tourists will admire the famous waterfalls, and on the beach they will see the famous attraction "Parachute", during which you can see the city from a bird's eye view, and if possible, then dolphins in the sea.

A special place in the reviews is given to waterfalls, which are abundant here. All of them are unique in their own way, and most importantly different: wide, narrow, small, cascading, tall, with pools and without ... Most often, tourists visit the waterfalls on the River Zhan. They are small but very picturesque. The highest waterfall is called the Devil's Mouth, located at the mouth of the Teshebs River . 69 slopes and 49 slides in the Golden Bay water park do not leave indifferent parents or children. This water park gives unforgettable emotions. He gained fame throughout the Krasnodar Territory. A special place in the reviews is given to the safari park. This is a truly unique place, a rehabilitation center where people admire the life of the wild world in natural conditions. Do not forget to go down the cable car to the Olimp Park, judging by the reviews there are horseback riding, an exhibition of exotic fish, museums, attractions, a planetarium, and fishing.
