Camp sites of the Samara region. Rest in Samara and the Samara region: reviews, prices

Tourist camps of the Samara region, hotels and health camps annually receive more than 500 thousand tourists from all over Russia. Someone visits the cities of the Samara province in passing, and someone comes purposefully in order to get acquainted with the sights of this region.

Tourists choose Samara because of its countless natural monuments - there are more than 300 of them in the province: these are the Volga with its many tributaries, national natural parks, the Zhiguli mountains. Others go to the camp sites of the Samara region for treatment, since the region has a very developed spa treatment, balneology, herbal medicine. Fans of active tourism conquer the local mountain peaks, rafting down rapids, walking along the winding paths of the taiga with a backpack on their backs, exploring mountain caves.

In addition, the Samara province is a leader in the number of various events that are held during the year, more than 400. To accommodate tourists in the region there are about 60 sanatoriums and health camps, 259 hotels and more than 120 recreation centers. In this article, some of the most popular camp sites in Samara and the Samara region are presented to readers.

Rest in "Crane"

The base "Zhuravushka" is located 12 km from Samara, on the Podzhabny peninsula. The living conditions at this base are very ascetic, and it may even seem Spartan to the city dwellers, as the rooms lack amenities, the shower is only available on the street, and electricity is supplied only three hours a day - from 21 to 24 hours. Tourists staying at this place choose it because of the beauty of the places that surround Zhuravushka: it offers beautiful views of Samara, the Volga and the forest.





Rooms

Guests of this recreation center can stay in a separate house for two people or on the landing stage, in rooms designed for 1-3 people. Bathrooms on the landing stage are located on the deck. There are no amenities in wooden houses, the bathroom is represented by a rustic toilet and a summer shower - on the street. Inside, the furniture is very modest and consists of two metal beds, a plastic table and two chairs.

Nutrition

Three meals a day are available at an additional cost.

Entertainment

There are cafes, billiards, tennis tables, a volleyball court, and a dance floor on the camp site.

Cost

Summer houses cost vacationers 570 rubles per day per house, a room on the landing stage is slightly cheaper - 550 rubles per day.

About the "Crane" tourists say that this is the best place in terms of price and quality of services provided. Of the benefits, vacationers note the cheapness of rest, good food and animation in the evenings. Dissatisfied guests complain about the dilapidation of the rooms and the territory, interruptions in electricity, lack of communications in the room.
