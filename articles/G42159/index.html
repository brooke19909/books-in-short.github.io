Holiday Village "Pink Cottage": address, rooms, booking conditions and reviews of vacationers

Currently, country holidays within the country have become popular. Many people want to go on a weekend to take a break from the noisy city and dust, some prefer to spend their entire vacation away from the metropolis.

The article will focus on comfortable rest in the forest in the Leningrad region. The recreation center "Pink Cottage" provides an opportunity to relax both families and groups of friends in spacious cottages and houses.

Overview

The territory of the recreation center is adjacent to the largest lake in this area, Otradnoye. The address of the "Pink Cottage" is the village of Kutuzovskoye (Priozersky district of the Leningrad Region, Plodovskoye rural settlement). You can get here by your own transport or by using the transfer services (for a fee) from anywhere in the lake or Otradnoye station. There are also regular buses from bus stations and an electric train from the Finnish railway station.

The Pink Dacha complex, whose telephone and other contacts can be viewed on the official website, is located in the forest, but it will not be difficult to get here. It is worth paying attention to those traveling by their own transport that after you leave the asphalt highway, a regular dirt road will run to the base.

Forest, lake and clean air - this is what makes this place attractive for vacationers. A variety of entertainment, comfortable conditions for rest, food and spa - all this helps people to relax and rest.

In the immediate vicinity of the hotel complex is another beautiful lake - Ladoga. Thus, fishing and water sports are becoming priorities in this holiday.

The territory of the complex "Pink Cottage" is large enough so that the houses are located at a sufficient distance from each other. The paths between the cottages are lined with beautiful stone tiles, which is convenient for both walking and cycling, scooter and so on.

Around the houses - a beautiful, well-groomed lawn grass and flowers. All this resembles a small alpine village. Recreation areas are equipped with everything necessary: ‚Äã‚Äãgazebos with roofs and comfortable tables and benches, playgrounds for active games, access to the lake.

Gazebos

For all guests at the recreation center 2 gazebos are organized. There is a wood stove and tables. Rental price - 600 rubles for 3 hours. The capacity of the gazebo is up to 10 people. Everyone can sit here at a family dinner and prepare dishes on the grill.

Barbecue area

"Pink Cottage" offers guests to rent barbecue grills. The brazier itself in the barbecue area is provided for free, as well as skewers and grills. One condition: return clean! If the equipment is not in good condition, then guests will need to pay 50 rubles for it for each hour of use.

You can also purchase a set of disposable tableware for six people (cost - 50 rubles), charcoal (packaging - 90 rubles), ignition (70 rubles). For those who prefer to wait until coal is formed from burning wood, you can buy fragrant birch logs (100 rubles for one ignition).

Entertainment

The recreation center "Pink Cottage" offers guests table tennis. Cost - 150 rubles per hour. In a special room there are three tennis tables that can be rented by both large companies and families.

For many guests, it becomes a pleasant surprise that there is a shooting range on the territory. You can shoot at targets for 150 rubles for 10 shots.

You can rent tennis rackets and enjoy this sport. Such a pleasure (renting rackets) will cost 200 rubles. For this cost, rackets are rented for a day.

Billiards is a favorite sport for many men. The complex has a large hall with several tables. Large Russian billiards - 350 rubles per hour. Average Russian billiards will cost slightly less - 250 rubles per hour.

For lovers of small Russian billiards, it is necessary to inform that such a pleasure will cost 100 rubles per hour. The base "Pink Cottage" makes it possible to play the American pool for 250 rubles per hour.

Vacationers are happy when they find out that you can rent a segway at the base. Such transport costs 100 rubles for 20 minutes of use. You can take several automatic cars for the whole family and ride around the territory.

Everyone can also enjoy and have fun with air hockey or table football. Board games (chess, checkers, backgammon and dominoes) brighten up the evenings of residents.

Sports lovers should visit the gym in the main building of the "Pink Cottage" with the necessary equipment. There is a large sports field on the territory, fenced with a net, with a rubber coating.

Bicycle and roller skate rental will diversify the relaxation of adults and children. Rental price - from 100 rubles. In winter, residents can rent skis, sleds or skates, and for a more active holiday - a snowmobile.

But many people living in the surrounding areas know about the pool at the recreation center. Not only vacationers come here, but also local residents. Spacious pool can accommodate a maximum number of floating - 10 people. Residents of the recreation center can visit the swimming complex for free, the rest of the guests must pay 300 rubles per hour of bathing.

In the pool there is equipment for learning to swim, and in showers - shower gel and soap. To visit, you must have a bathing suit and hat.

Finnish sauna

It's no secret that the Finnish sauna is not only rest and relaxation, but also health benefits. The sauna is located next to the pool and can accommodate a maximum of 10 people. Large groups of people often rent a sauna with a pool. The air is heated for two hours and reaches 100 degrees. That is why a trip to such a wellness place should be planned in advance. After a visit to the sauna, you can plunge into the pool from 13.00 daily. The complex ends its work at 21.00. The cost of a sauna and a swimming pool is from 500 rubles per hour per person.

Turkish sauna

Unlike the Finnish bath, the Turkish steam room has lower temperatures. The air here warms up to 30-50 degrees at 100% humidity. Thus, the ideal climate for a stay in the bath. Many people prefer the Turkish steam room for a comfortable stay. The steam room can accommodate 6 people. You need to bring a towel or sheet. During a stay in hot and humid conditions, pores quickly open, the skin is cleansed and excess fluid leaves the body.

The cost of an hour in such a bath is from 500 rubles per person. The steam room works in the same way as the Finnish bath.

Russian sauna

The classic version of the Russian steam room is welcomed by many men. The bath is located in a small birch grove. Inside, in addition to the bathhouse and sun loungers, there is a shower room and a table where you can drink your favorite drinks.

In the bath can be no more than 6 people. The cost of stay is from 1200 rubles. You can also rent a broom, bathrobe or sheet.

Beach

If you walk from the base to the lake, you can go to the equipped beach. This is a special place for swimming, where there are umbrellas, sunbeds and places for changing clothes. The cost of visiting is 100 rubles per day.

Organization of children's leisure

In the central building of the base there is a spacious game room in which there are toys, drawing albums, a flat-screen TV with broadcast cartoons. On the territory there is a playground where a child of any age will find something to do. There are also trampolines in which older children can have fun.

Renting catamarans, riding on "cheesecakes" or "banana" - all this brings a lot of fun to both adults and children. The recreation center "Pink Dacha" offers children and parents free feeding of rabbits and lamb.

Fishing

Fishing on the beautiful Lake Otradnoye is a pleasure. Boating along the beautiful spots of the lake will delight you. At this time, you can enjoy the clean air, the tides of the water surface, as well as make out in the purest water a lot of small and large fish.

The recreation center offers organized fishing for 2-4 people. Here you can also rent a fishing rod and all the necessary equipment.

Number of rooms

For accommodation, the recreation center "Pink Cottage" offers several options for tourists. These are cottages with different levels of comfort and capacity and studio rooms. All apartments of the recreation center "Pink Dacha" (Priozersky district of the Leningrad region) differ in terms, cost and location. You can book a house or a room either by phone or on the official website. It is worth planning your vacation in advance, because there are a lot of people who want to get to this base at any time of the year. The cost of living varies depending on the number of days spent at the base (the more, the cheaper).

Studio room

The rooms are classically decorated in light colors. The main room has a double bed or two single beds, as well as a TV and bedside tables. In the kitchen area there is a refrigerator, sink, microwave, kettle, table and chairs. There is a bathroom and an entrance hall with a wardrobe. The cost of living is from 3000 rubles. Possible accommodation for 2 people with children under 6 years.

Cottage for a family with a child

Can accommodate 5 guests (including children). The spacious room on the second floor has two beds (for parents and for the child), as well as a wardrobe and bedside tables.

On the ground floor there is a kitchen with a dining area, a shower room and a place to watch TV (sofa and a TV with a cupboard). The sofa can accommodate guests or teenagers.

The cost of living is from 4,500 rubles. Meals are paid separately.

Cottage "comfort"

This type of accommodation is also designed for 5 guests. It is suitable for long stays. In the spacious kitchen, in addition to appliances and utensils, there is a gas stove. Therefore, the family will be able to cook their own meals. A large sofa in the TV viewing area can accommodate the whole family. The cost of living - from 5000 rubles per day.

Cottage for several families

Cottages for several families are also marked marked comfort and ordinary. They differ in the presence of a gas stove and a large sofa. The house has two large bedrooms, which have beds for parents and children. Large kitchen with dining table can accommodate the whole family. In the evening you can watch a movie or your favorite show. The cost of living - from 6000 rubles per day.
