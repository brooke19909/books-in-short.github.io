Knitting cap: description and schemes for beginners

Which yarn is better?

Hats with ears are worn mainly in the cool season - from mid-autumn to early spring, such a headgear will come in handy, becoming an unusual part of the wardrobe. To knit it, you need to choose yarn and knitting needles. Most of all, for hats without inserts, ordinary threads are suitable - half-woolen, acrylic. If you want to knit a fluffy cat hat, then you can choose "grass" yarn. And even such a headdress can be quite complicated when two types of threads are used in the work - for the cap itself and for the ears, and the texture of the threads should match their purpose. Depending on the thickness of the yarn, you should choose knitting needles. Usually, the cat‚Äôs hat is knitted on two knitting needles, and the fabric is then joined by a regular seam, and it depends on the model, one will be a seam or two. Although you can knit a hat on five knitting needles - in a circle.





Top ears

A knitted hat with knitting needles can be completely straightforward - knitted ears are sewn onto a regular knitted hat. The headgear itself is more convenient to carry out with facial stitch. To get a knitted cat with knitting needles, the description will be used the most primitive. Let's say the head circumference is 55-56 centimeters. Then, on knitting needles No. 3, 120 loops of suitable semisynthetic, woolen yarn of thickness are dialed. Yarn and knitting needles are selected standardly - a thread twisted in two folds should be equal to the thickness of the knitting needles. That is, for the proposed model, the thickness of the yarn should be 1.5-2 mm. For more accurate calculations, it is better to first knit the sample and calculate how many loops per 1 cm of canvas.

The first 5-7 rows are knitted with a 1x1 elastic band. Then, to the height of the cap, and this is about 20 centimeters, knitting continues with the front surface, that is, the front rows are knitted with the front loops, and the wrong rows are with the inside loops. Having knitted a straight section, you should begin to reduce the loops evenly in a circle, knitting 2 loops together. For cats, such reductions are best done in different places of the row, without indicating the seams of the reductions. When several loops remain in operation, they must be pulled together with a thread. Sew the finished product at the seam. Now the ears fit. On knitting needles, dial 20 loops and knit with the front stitch, making decreases from the beginning and end of the row. The height of the hat will depend on how the decreases are performed. 4 pieces of such identical parts need to be connected, and it is in this case that you can make ears with pink, for example, inserts, then the back half of the ear will be the color of the cap, and the front half will be pink. Sew each pair of ears over the edge or crochet with a "crustacean step" and sew in place. Double ears will be stiff enough not to ‚Äúfall over‚Äù. A simple hat with cat ears is ready.

Just a hat with ears

Another model will tell how a cat hat can be knitted with one cloth. The scheme of work is as follows, for a head circumference of 55-57 centimeters:

  • on knitting needles No. 3, dial 56 loops with a suitable yarn thickness and tie with a 1x1 elastic band for a better fit of 5-7 rows;
  • knitting is distributed in this way - 1 hem, 12 stitches with a pearl pattern, 30 stitches of a royal braid according to the pattern, 112 stitches with a pearl pattern and 1 hem;
  • continue knitting to fit the headgear;
  • knit 5-7 rows with 1x1 elastic, as at the beginning of knitting;
  • to sew side seams;
  • measure the hat;
  • on the head make marks of cat ears;
  • fasten the back and front halves of the cap with a decorative cross to mark the cat ears and fit the cap on the head.

The royal braid should be knitted according to the pattern.





The work is completed, the cap is ready.
