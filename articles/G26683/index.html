One Touch Select General Information

The principle of research using the One Touch Select device is that glucose, which is contained in a drop of blood, enters into an electrochemical reaction with the enzymes of the test cavity, and as a result an electrical signal (current) appears. Its value depends on the level of glucose in the blood. One Touch Select allows you to measure the strength of the current, calculate the blood sugar level and, of course, view the previous results from the device‚Äôs memory on the monitor.
