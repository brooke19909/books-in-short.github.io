Hotel More Hotel 3 * (Kemer): reviews, photos, description

Turkey is a resort loved by Russian tourists. Thousands of inhabitants of this huge country come here every year to rest. Such wide popularity is explained by low prices for accommodation and services, as well as excellent climate. There is high temperature throughout the year, so swimming in the sea is sometimes possible even in the winter months.

There are several famous resort areas in Turkey, one of which is Marmaris. It is here that a large number of good hotels are located, which offer comfortable living conditions and a lot of entertainment of a different nature. One of them is More Hotel (ex Adress Hotel 3 *).



Location

The hotel is located in Marmaris, in the village of Beldibi, which has direct access to the warm and clear sea. In order to get there, you will need to cross the road along which vehicles regularly move. The beachfront of the hotel is washed by the waters of the Mediterranean Sea.

As for the large transport hubs, Antalya International Airport is 35 kilometers from the hotel, which is very convenient for tourists whose journey through Turkey starts from here. In reviews of More Hotel 3 * in 2017, tourists very often noted just such a plus of the favorable location of this place to stay.

As for urban entertainment, all of them are concentrated in another major tourist city of Turkey - Kemer, which is 15 kilometers from the hotel, however, if you wish, you can easily get there using public transport, taxi services or renting a car from the hotel.

general information

More Hotel 3 * (Beldibi) was built in 2000. Since then, regular reconstructions have been carried out on its territory, the largest of which was carried out in 2010. Currently, the hotel maintains cleanliness and order, as well as the modern look of each room.

The total area of ‚Äã‚Äãthe recreation area is 3000 square meters. m. - this is not much, but in this territory a huge amount of entertainment for adults and younger guests is very compact. As guests in the reviews about More Hotel 3 * (Kemer) noted in 2017, here you can relax inexpensively, but with taste and complete comfort.



Rooms

The hotel has a small number of rooms - it consists of 70 well-equipped apartments, concentrated in two separate buildings, each of which rises up to three floors. They are built in a modern style and do not differ in exquisite design.

Among the rooms here are two types of housing: standard and luxury. Each of them has a set of furniture necessary for life, as well as an individual bathroom with a shower, toilet, sink. Guests are also provided with a set of bath accessories, which without fail includes a towel, bathrobe, slippers, as well as shower gel, shampoo and soap. The bathroom comes with a small hairdryer.

The apartments have a personal air conditioning, with which residents can adjust the temperature conditions to the most comfortable, based on individual preferences. The rooms have carpet flooring and white tiles in the bathroom.



Standard

The hotel More Hotel 3 * (Kemer) has 66 rooms, which the administration classifies as standard. They are designed for convenient accommodation of guests, so there is a minimum set of furniture accessories. The room has the required number of beds (2 or 3) in the form of beds with hard mattresses. Guests can put their personal belongings in a wardrobe or on bedside tables. If necessary, they can use an electronic safe to store small but valuable items. For his rent, guests will have to pay a daily fee of $ 2. If desired, vacationers can use the minibar, the assortment of which is updated daily with cool non-alcoholic drinks. Each room has a telephone.

In their free time, tourists have the opportunity to watch satellite channels on plasma TVs, which are installed in each room. Here, by the way, Russian-language channels are also broadcast, which is very popular with tourists from Russia - they very often mention this fact in their reviews of More Hotel 3 * (Turkey).

The area of ‚Äã‚Äãstandard category apartments is from 25 to 30 square meters. m. Each room has access to a private balcony, where you can sit for sunbathing or just enjoy the surrounding nature.

Suite

The luxury apartments, of which there are only 4 in More Hotel 3 * (Turkey / Kemer / Beldibi), provide the most comfortable conditions for accommodation from 2 to 4 people. They are often populated by guests who come to rest with the whole family. Rooms of this class have a vast area - 42 square meters. m and are divided into two rooms, each of which is a sleeping area.

In total, the deluxe room has four beds, each room has two bedside tables, as well as a large wardrobe. Satellite TV is connected here, there is a replenished minibar. From each room there is access to a balcony from which you can admire the blooming garden, views of the sea or mountains.

Living conditions for children

Often at More Hotel 3 * (Kemer / Beldibi) people come to relax with their whole families, in the company of small children. As you know, they require the organization of individual conditions that take into account the special needs of the guests. And in the hotel in question they are.

Each room can be additionally equipped with a special cot. You can get it for free. In restaurants, tourists have the opportunity to seat their kids in special comfortable chairs and offer them a separate menu, which includes only the brightest and most delicious dishes.

The hotel has a small, but well-equipped for active and fun pastime, a playground - here, kids can not only play, but also get to know their peers. A separate pool is also open for them, the depth of which is shallow. It is equipped with all the safety requirements for guests of this age category.

In reviews of More Hotel 3 *, young parents often note the fact that strollers are not available for rent, which is very inconvenient. However, the presence of a swimming section, which is organized for kids, pleases many.

Nutrition

The guests of this hotel have the opportunity to eat without going beyond it. To do this, there is an excellent restaurant and a small bar where guests are treated to an all-inclusive system.



As for the restaurant, it is the main catering establishment in More Hotel 3 * (Turkey). It is here that a daily buffet is served, for which all vacationers are invited. It can be visited during breakfast, lunch and dinner from 10:30 to 21:30. On a free basis, you can also drink all non-alcoholic drinks and alcohol that is produced in Turkey. Everything else, including hookah, is provided for a fee. As for the cuisine, Turkish and European dishes are traditional for this place.

Tourists can enjoy light snacks, desserts and cold drinks at the bar. You can also always have a cup of hot tea or coffee here, but such drinks are popular in the evening. This establishment works daily from 10:30 to 21:30.

Pool

The territory of the More Hotel 3 * hotel (Turkey) is small, so there is only one pool here. It is in the open air, and its area is 100 square meters. m. You can visit this place only at a time specified by the administration. As stated in the reviews of More Hotel 3 * (Kemer), in 2017 such a period was set for the time period from 10 a.m. to 6 p.m. According to tourists, the water in this pool is always crystal clear, as it is daily cleaned here.



Around the pool area there is a small sun terrace where vacationers can sunbathe. For their comfort, large sun loungers are provided here, as well as beach umbrellas that protect the skin from direct sunlight.

In the evenings, discos are held at the summer venue, during which guests can have fun and dance to the fiery compositions of foreign performers. Russian tourists are very fond of events of this kind and attend them in large numbers.

SPA area

In one of the buildings of the More Hotel 3 * hotel (Kemer) there is an excellent spa area, which is very popular among tourists who come here to relax. It includes a sauna and a traditional Turkish bath (hammam). Visiting these places is paid, and the cost of the visit is calculated based on the duration of the visit and the number of guests. Also, if desired, tourists can visit the massage room, which offers several types of massage: relaxing, healing, Thai and others. This service is also paid.

Sport

Fans of a sports lifestyle can always take care of themselves. In reviews of More Hotel 3 * (Kemer), the absence of a gym in the buildings often stands out with negative comments. However, guests are positive about the presence of a good volleyball court and area for playing table tennis - this is where you can have fun in your free time.

Water sports enthusiasts can do it, as there are all the necessary conditions on the sea coast: guests can learn surfing skills or dive to the depths accompanied by an instructor.

Beach

In the photo More Hotel 3 * you can often watch footage taken on its own beach. It is located at a short distance from the main building - only 100 meters, so getting to the beach is not difficult, even accompanied by a small child. In their reviews of More Hotel 3 * (Turkey), hotel guests note that this beach is ideal for visiting with children - it is completely covered with coarse pebbles and fine sand. At the same time, descent into the water is gentle. The total length of the coastline is small - it is only 50 meters.

This recreation area is very well equipped, it is always clean. For guests there are free and comfortable sun loungers, as well as umbrellas. If you wish, you can always rent a towel. There are several toilets, changing rooms and showers on the beach.

As for entertainment, there are a lot of them. If desired, you can learn some types of water sports. Fans of extreme sports will not miss the opportunity to go water-skiing or try to tame the wave while surfing. Children can ride on air cushions, and adults will certainly enjoy scuba diving.

Additional paid services provided at the hotel

Hotel guests can always use services that are not included in the price. So, if necessary, here you can rent a car and go to explore local attractions. Many reviews of More Hotel 3 * (Kemer) recommend that you visit the center of Kemer, as this city has a large number of attractions that will surely appeal to all lovers of nightlife. There are also many excellent restaurants and cafes where you can eat deliciously. By the way, for a rented car, a parking space at the hotel will be provided free of charge.

If necessary, any guest can use the services of a doctor who is constantly in the hotel. All actions are carried out on a paid basis.

Tourists have the opportunity to use the laundry and dry cleaning services, for which you also have to pay at the established rate. For guests who monitor their appearance, a beauty salon is open here, where you can make a new haircut or make a number of cosmetic procedures (wrapping, peeling, applying masks and others).

On the ground floor of one of the buildings there is a mini market where you can purchase some food products, as well as souvenirs for relatives and friends.



Accommodation Features

The hotel sets the standard requirements that apply to absolutely all tourists staying at the hotel. For example, it is impossible to move in here with your pets, therefore, when planning a vacation at More Hotel, it is advisable to think about a place in advance where you can leave your pet.

It is undesirable for people with disabilities to check in here due to the lack of suitable rooms in the fund of the More Hotel 3 * hotel (Turkey, Kemer). The resort has many hotels where such apartments are provided, so it is better to choose another place to stay.

It is also advisable for all vacationers to remember that smoking is prohibited in the apartments.

Prices

About 45,000 rubles - about such a cost of a weekly stay for two in a standard room of the More Hotel 3 * hotel (Turkey, Kemer). In the resort, such a price for such conditions is considered quite normal and average. However, different tour operators can offer individual options. So, the final cost of a voucher, for example, will directly depend on the airport from which the departure will be carried out, as well as on the number of services included in the package.

Apartment reservation

Check-in at this hotel is subject to prior reservation of apartments. There are several ways to do this. For example, call the hotel directly and leave the reservation verbally. Some administrators working at the front desk speak a good Russian language, so the implementation of such an action will not be difficult.

Tourists can also book a room by filling out a special online form. It will be necessary to indicate the number of people arriving at the hotel, the dates of arrival and departure, and then choose the most suitable room from all the proposed ones. When booking, future guests will be charged in advance.

Finally, the easiest way is to contact a travel agency and purchase a ticket. Here, experts will tell you everything in detail and help you choose the most suitable living conditions. As a rule, tourists who use this method will not have to organize an independent stay at the hotel - a transfer service will be included in the ticket. Guests are delivered to the hotel by a comfortable, air-conditioned bus. After resting on it, tourists are taken back to Antalya Airport. In addition, the price immediately includes a list of all necessary insurance. However, before flying to Turkey, a traveler needs to independently open a visa, the cost of which is about $ 80, and check the validity of the passport - the agents will do the rest.



Guest reviews

A large number of reviews about More Hotel 3 * (Kemer) can be found on the Web. In their comments, vacationers, as a rule, share their impressions gained during the holiday, and sometimes give valuable advice to those tourists who wish to spend their holidays in this place.

Very often, tourists say that the hotel provides excellent living conditions. Guests note that the hotel is superior to many three-star complexes in this resort. The buildings are always clean and comfortable. Special attention is paid to the arrangement of rooms. According to vacationers, everything is done here simply, but comfortably. Guests enjoy the homely atmosphere of the hotel.

As for the staff, most travelers in their reviews of the More Hotel 3 * hotel also share positive comments about their work. So, at the reception are administrators who can answer any question of interest regarding accommodation, the provision of services, as well as the location of urban and entertainment infrastructure facilities around the clock. If necessary, they can call a local taxi. This service is provided completely free of charge. Tourists are very pleased that almost all the hotel staff speak Russian, which makes the rest of Russian tourists in the hotel more comfortable. Maids clean twice a week, leaving behind a completely clean room and fresh linen. As for the service as a whole, it is fully consistent with the European level.

Residents of the hotel often share a lot of positive impressions received from the taste of dishes served in local catering establishments.According to them, the restaurant offers hearty, nutritious and original dishes, many of which are prepared according to traditional recipes of Turkish cuisine. This allows Russian tourists to at least get a little closer to the culture of this country.

Travelers who spend their holidays here with their children notice the excellent attitude of the staff towards the small guests. The most comfortable conditions are always created for them, and, as a rule, without requests for additional payment. The kid at the hotel always has something to do, where to have fun and meet other children. Guests note a small minus of this recreation area - the lack of a working team of animators in it and the ability to use the services of a nanny.
