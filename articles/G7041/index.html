Hotel "Onega Castle", Petrozavodsk: description, reviews and photos

A great alternative to sea resorts is a trip to Karelia. Especially it will appeal to lovers of relaxation with a tent, fishing, descents on a mountain river and other outdoor activities. Wildlife here, undoubtedly, admires its greatness, but at least a couple of days must be devoted to Petrozavodsk - the capital of Karelia. The question immediately arises: where can I stop for this time? And it is advisable to find a place so that the impressions of visiting the city remain the most pleasant. The answer is: this is the Onega Castle Hotel. Petrozavodsk offers its guests a fairly wide selection of hotels, but staying in this particular hotel you will not regret, as the comfort of the guests and the European level of service is the paramount task of the hotel owners, which they can do perfectly.



Onega Castle, Petrozavodsk: description of the hotel

The hotel is located in the historic center of Petrozavodsk, on the shores of Lake Onega. Its location is a definite plus that sets the hotel apart from its competitors. In walking distance - Lenin Avenue, the main street of the city. For guests of the capital, the doors of the best restaurants and cozy cafes are open here. Art lovers can visit theaters and museums, which are abundant here.

Getting to the hotel is not difficult: the road from the railway station will take about fifteen minutes, half an hour - from the airport of Petrozavodsk.

The external facade of the building is made in a modern laconic style, which, as it might seem at first glance, has nothing special. And only by going inside, guests will be amazed at the originality and beauty of the interior, which was developed specifically for the Onega Castle Hotel. Petrozavodsk is not in vain called the cultural capital of Karelia - the best designers create their masterpieces here. The two main colors in the interior design are white and red. The walls of the hotel lobby are painted with motifs of flora and fauna, which, according to legend, are amulets. The textiles in the rooms are decorated with embroidery in the form of geometric patterns, which also protects against damage.

The hotel will delight guests not only with the comfort and beauty of the rooms, but also with magnificent views from the windows, most of which are facing Lake Onega and one of the attractions of Petrozavodsk - the embankment.

Number of rooms - the pride of the Onega Castle Hotel

32 rooms, which are made in a simple style, are ready to receive visitors to the Onega Castle Hotel. Petrozavodsk offers its tourists quite affordable prices for vacations. Room rates vary. So, a day of living will cost from 3400 for a single room to 6900 rubles per room for 4 people.

The exterior of the surrounding area is unique. The strict and at the same time very harmonious view of the territory makes the Onega Castle Hotel, Petrozavodsk unique. Photos taken at the hotel will be a great addition to the family photo album.

Restaurant Onega Castle

On the promenade, which is a favorite place for local residents to walk, and most likely will delight city guests, there is a cozy place. Here you can have a good time, and the institution is called Onega Castle. Petrozavodsk, whose restaurant daily receives visitors, will be revealed in all its glory. After all, the interior is made in the national spirit of Karelia. Everything is made of wood and stone: floors and beams, walls, floor, massive furniture.



The restaurant serves dishes of both European and national cuisine, and the signature dish from the chef will not leave indifferent any of the visitors of the Onega Castle Hotel (Petrozavodsk).

The restaurant starts work quite early, so here you can already at 8.00 a hearty breakfast or a cup of strong invigorating coffee. In the afternoon, there are more visitors in the small room of Onega Castle. As you know, while eating delicious food, all matters are resolved more easily, so lunch of business partners is a common occurrence here. In the evening, usually at the table you can watch a family dinner or a romantic evening of two lovers.

Also, the restaurant is provided for corporate events, weddings and other celebrations. In summer, the function of receiving a large number of guests is performed by a veranda and a gazebo, which can accommodate 60 and 10 people, respectively.

Hotel Sauna

After a long walk around the city, the Onega Castle sauna will help to warm up. Petrozavodsk is still a non-resort city, the climate here is quite cool.

Having steamed in the Finnish sauna, it's time to plunge into the pool with cold water, the bottom and walls of which are laid out in blue mosaic, which creates the feeling of swimming in a mountain lake. And after water procedures, fragrant tea brewed on local herbs will come in handy. The whole atmosphere of the sauna is conducive to relaxation and complete immersion in nirvana.

The interior of the sauna is decorated in the same colors as the Onega Castle Hotel. Petrozavodsk subsequently remains in memory as a place that gives peace and tranquility, and is associated with warm shades of red and lace patterns.

Also, visitors will be offered the services of an experienced masseur, and you can give cheerful notes in the evening with the help of singing in karaoke.
