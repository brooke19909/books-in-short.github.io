Making ugg boots with your own hands: pattern and sequence of actions

In the 20th century, ugg boots confidently inspired the trends of modern fashion.

Warm and comfortable, not always attractive in appearance, but practical and comfortable, many women, men, and also children of all ages liked them.

These boots came to us from Australia and were originally made from natural sheepskin. They were worn by shepherds. Such shoes keep warm and wear well.

Since then, ugg boots have undergone many changes, and today you can find countless models, so to speak, for every taste and color, and they can safely complement almost any outfit.

Also, many fashionistas are able to sew them at home, make a pattern, create ugg boots with their own hands.

Women's and men's uggs

At first glance, it may seem that the uggs are kind of ridiculous and ugly in appearance.

This is not their advantage. Warmth and comfort - this is where they are not inferior to anything. And everything else is not so important.

The most famous brand of original uggs is UGG, the price of products of this brand is not small. These are boots made of natural suede on the outside and with fur inside.

But on sale you can also find fairly cheap analogues from a wide variety of materials, no worse in quality and design.





There are a large number of them, in different colors and with original inserts made of fur, suede, leather. With the addition of decorative elements: buttons, rhinestones, stones, embroidery. You can find models with zippers ..

The length can also be different. The shortest ones are just above the ankles. They can reach the knees or mid-calf.

As an option, you can instantly transform the model in one motion - turn up part of the top - and you have completely different boots on you.

Men also fell in love with these shoes, as a rule, men's ugg boots are much shorter.

How to sew ugg boots with your own hands? Patterns can be found or independently created both for yourself and for your beloved man.

Ugg boots and style

These boots love loose style. Perfectly combined with jeans as loose-fitting, and narrowed to the bottom.

Carelessness of the boots will emphasize the harmony of the legs with tights or leggings in the color of uggs.





Outerwear should not be long - all kinds of jackets, parkas or sheepskin coats above the knee or to the middle of the thigh will be a great option. In warm autumn, these shoes can be combined with wide, not very long tunics with a belt at the waist.

Men are advised to choose down jackets or coats to match. Jeans, corduroy pants and plaid shirts, warm sweaters or cardigans accentuate the image of a stylish and modern man.

Do not neglect accessories: monophonic sets of hats, scarves and gloves will complement the outfit well.

Ugg boots can be trimmed with fur or flirtatious chatterboxes that young girls and children love. You can independently design a pattern and children's uggs with your own hands can be sewn in just a couple of hours.

Ugg boots and health

But the comfort of this shoe can turn into a side effect. Since ugg boots do not have a fixed hard back, the presence of a solid sole (without anatomical and orthopedic reliefs) can cause improper foot formation and flat feet, especially in childhood and adolescence.

If you wear such shoes constantly, ugg boots tend to wear out. They become quite free, which can lead to impaired gait.





It is not recommended to wear shoes without a heel for more than 2 hours a day. In preventive measures, you can place an orthopedic insole.

Children and home uggs

Well ugg boots and at home. These home slippers are loved by both children and adults. Cozy, warm and very, very soft - what else does the leg need to relax at home?

You can find quite a few patterns. How to sew children's uggs with your own hands, because the children are happy to run around at home in these cute boots? Especially for owners of cold floors, this is always true.

Many mothers are interested in how to sew baby uggs with their own hands. It is not very difficult to make a pattern, as it might seem at first glance. Below you can see an example.

How to sew home uggs with your own hands? The pattern is constructed in the same way as for any other model.

To start building a shoe pattern, you first need to select the necessary materials for the job.

Choose the right material

You will need: material for the top and material for the sole.

These are universal boots, and almost any fabric is suitable for tailoring : drape, felt, faux suede or leather. You can use an old or unnecessary jacket, fur coat or sheepskin coat.





You can find different patterns. How to sew ugg boots with your own hands from an old sweater? Easy enough too! Any scheme will help.

The basis for the sole may be leather, oilcloth or any other very dense fabric.

How to sew

To sew ugg boots with your own hands, the pattern must be prepared in advance. Tailoring itself will not require much effort from you. If you do not have professional sewing skills, then a little patience and persistent desire will certainly lead to a result.

The simplicity of sewing is also determined by the fact that you yourself have the right to adjust the height of the shaft, lifting and other details. This will be your individual model.

Patterns can also be different. Consider a model without a hermetic. It is the simplest and will not take much time to make.

We begin to sew ugg boots with our own hands, we will build a pattern on thick paper.

You need to circle the foot. Give it a rounded shape.

On the front of the toe, make marks for the height of the lift.

Wrap paper around the heel, mark the desired height and length on the sides. And then smoothly connect all the marks.

Measure the required width and height of the shaft on the calf. Build a rectangle.





It turned out four details: two soles (one is the outer part of the sole, the other is the lining of the sole), the front part with a fold, the bootleg with a fold.

That's when all the parts are ready, we cut them out and transfer them to the fabric. Two pieces are cut out. And sewn according to the scheme of the outer seam.

Given the density of the fabrics used, most likely, machine assembly is not suitable for you, of course, if you are not the owner of a furrier machine. Otherwise, stitching the seams outward is most convenient on the hands. Threads can be selected in the color of the material, or you can play in contrast.

First we sew the upper parts of the shaft, then all the rest.

Bows, flowers, buttons will help to add uniqueness to your uggs.
