Hotel T5 Suites Pattaya 3 * (Thailand, Pattaya): photos and reviews of tourists

One of the most popular resorts in Thailand is Pattaya. Our compatriots come here primarily, of course, in order to sunbathe on comfortable beaches, swim in the sea and visit all sorts of attractions. Of course, having a good time in Pattaya, like in any other resort, without comfortable accommodation, it will not work. Almost all the hotels of the Thai capital of the sun and entertainment offer excellently equipped rooms to their guests. In this case, one of the best hotels in the city, judging by the reviews of domestic tourists, is T5 Suites Pattaya 3 *.

Hotel Location Features

Located in the southern part of the city, T5 Suites Pattaya is a 7-minute drive from the famous pedestrian street. Most tourists consider its location quite convenient. The city center with its nightlife, popular bars, discos, etc. is very close to it. At the same time, the hotel itself is located in a quiet and peaceful place.





The famous Bali Hai Pier is 1.5 km from the hotel. It is from here that boats and boats departing, transporting tourists to the white sand picturesque Thai islands. To reach the most beautiful and clean Pattaya beach, Jomtien, from this hotel you can just 10 minutes.

Two minutes walk from the T5 Suites Pattaya 3 * hotel (Thailand, Pattaya) is one of the busiest streets of the city. Here, tuk-tuks, taxis and motorbikes, which are local inexpensive public transport, constantly drive around. If you need to visit remote parts of the city, hotel guests can always use it. And this, of course, adds to the hotel attractiveness in the eyes of tourists.

Address and how to get

U-Tapao Airport is just 45 km from Pattaya. You can get to the T5 Suites, for example, by taxi. In time, such a trip will take no more than an hour. You can also order a transfer at the hotel itself. This service, of course, is paid. On average, the cost of a trip from the airport to the hotel costs tourists about 2-3 thousand rubles. The exact address of this hotel is: 162/210 Moo 10 Thappaya Soi 5, 20150, Pattaya, Thailand.

general description

Represents the T5 Suites Pattaya 3 * hotel a new building designed in a modern style. The size of the hotel is not too large, and therefore no fuss or noise in its hall and corridors is never observed. In total, the hotel has 6 floors, each of which has several rooms for rent. The interior of the hotel is designed in pleasant to the eyes golden tones. Residents have the opportunity to climb the upper floors by elevator. Since this hotel is located on the third coastline, of course, it does not open particularly beautiful views from its windows. But most of the guests do not usually express any discontent about this. Judging by the reviews, the area around the hotel is pretty clean and there is no horror like garbage mountains, walking homeless people or drunk gatherings from the windows.





Number of rooms

In total, the T5 Suites Pattaya 3 * hotel (Pattaya) offers city guests 36 comfortable rooms. If you wish, you can book:

  • Standard Single Room and Double Room (30 m 2 );

  • Superior Room (32 m 2 );

  • Deluxe Room (35 m 2 ).

Many tourists praise this hotel, including the fact that all its rooms have air conditioning. And they work properly. If necessary, hotel guests can use a safe or a bar. Of course, each of the rooms of this three-star hotel has a modern TV. TV in the hotel is cable.

A bathroom with shower, sink and toilet is provided in all rooms. Tourists have the opportunity to use a set of toiletries, towels and a hairdryer. Replenish stocks of shampoo, soap and gel in the shower, as many vacationers note, the maids at this hotel regularly and without delay. Washing in most rooms is very convenient. But in some rooms in the shower room, judging by the reviews of the guests, there is no curtain. As a result, water is sprayed throughout the room. This, according to some tourists, is a small minus of the hotel.

In addition, the rooms of T5 Suites Pattaya 3 * hotel (Thailand) are equipped with:

  • a safe;

  • mini-bar;

  • a kettle.

In the refrigerator of each room, the maid daily puts a bottle of mineral water for free. Each room has a balcony.





Infrastructure

The reception at the T5 Suites Pattaya 3 * hotel, like most others in Pattaya, is open 24 hours a day. The hotel infrastructure is represented by the following amenities:

  • free parking for cars;

  • well equipped laundry room.

Wi-Fi is also available on site. Pay for this service is also not necessary. True, the Internet in the hotel, according to many tourists, is very slow. But this is a problem, rather, not of the hotel, but of Pattaya as a whole.

Accommodations

All rooms are non-smoking. However, the hotel administration also did not bypass the adherents of this bad habit. If desired, you can smoke in the hotel in specially designated rooms with ventilation. Pets are not allowed in the hotel T5 Suites Pattaya 3 *.

One child under 10 years old can be accommodated in a hotel room for free. If necessary, the hotel can also rent a crib of a suitable size.





Cost of living

Of course, Russian vacationers savages in Thailand rarely travel. Usually, our compatriots purchase tours to this country in companies of the corresponding specialization. The cost of trips with accommodation at the T5 Suites Pattaya is about 43-45 thousand rubles for 8 nights.

T5 Suites Pattaya 3 *: nutrition reviews

Hotel rooms can be rented with or without breakfast. Reviews about the food in this hotel on the network are mostly positive. Breakfast includes toast, jam, butter, tea, coffee, orange juice. Also, if desired, you can order scrambled eggs, sausages, bacon, potatoes.

At the hotel, among other things, there is a restaurant. This establishment offers hotel guests good dinners and lunches. The cost of visiting the hotel restaurant is inexpensive. Judging by the reviews of guests, for two hot dishes they take about 200-300 baht. A variety of drinks can be ordered at the local bar.

Jomtien Beach

This beach from the T5 Suites Pattaya 3 * hotel in tuk tuk can be reached in just a few minutes. It is located behind Pratamnak Hill, a little south of central Pattaya. The total length of the beach is 4 km. Sunbathing and relaxing here is very convenient. The coastline is covered with sand. The cost of a deck chair is 40-100 baht. The infrastructure on this beach is well developed. Along the coastline there are many cafes, bars, restaurants, etc.





Other beaches

Of course, not all hotel guests choose Jomtien for swimming. Some prefer to visit closer beaches. You can also sunbathe comfortably next to the hotel. Many tourists, for example, go to the beach belonging to the nearby Asia Pattaya Hotel. This choice is primarily due to the fact that the coastline near this hotel is absolutely clean, and the entrance to the sea is very convenient. Sunbeds on this beach cost only 80 baht. You can get to it from T5 Suites in literally 10-15 minutes.

Service Reviews

The hotel staff does not speak Russian. However, according to many tourists, girls at check-in, maids, etc. can be explained very simply and quickly with gestures. The staff in this hotel, judging by the reviews, is friendly, helpful and polite.

Hotel rooms are only cleaned upon request. In order for the maid to wash the floors in the room and wipe the dust, you just need to hang a special sign on the door. Linen and towels in the hotel rooms are changed in a timely manner.

Unfortunately, there is no pool in this hotel. Of course, this is not particularly convenient. However, the soul of the hotel, most tourists respond well. Hot water is supplied through the instantaneous water heater. Therefore, its temperature can be adjusted independently. The water in the hotel is never turned off.





Infrastructure near the hotel

Of course, in the immediate vicinity of the hotel there are many different shops, bars, restaurants and markets. You can reach almost any desired place from the hotel gate in just a couple of minutes.

Available Attractions

A huge number of various kinds of interesting places located in the immediate vicinity is also what tourists praise the T5 Suites Pattaya 3 * hotel for. Thailand (photos on the page confirm this) - the country is very beautiful. There are many kinds of attractions here. For example, hotel guests can go to the Central Pattaya gym. Also nearby is Wat Khao Phra Bat Pattaya. The hotel also has a tour desk. Here you can purchase tours to:

  • Prasat Mai Temple;

  • Stones Park, etc.

Prasat Mai is an ancient completely wooden cathedral built without a single nail. Judging by the reviews of guests of Pattaya, this temple is really very beautiful both from the outside and from the inside. All tourists, without exception, advise visiting this attraction. Currently, restoration work is underway in the cathedral. But this does not spoil the calm, peaceful atmosphere of the temple.





Stone Park is one of Pattaya 's most renowned sites . T5 Suites Pattaya 3 * offers its guests excursions to this wonderful place. Represents the Park of Stones a magnificent garden in east style. Here, tourists have the opportunity to look at tropical trees, admire beautiful flower beds, relax on the benches. Strange stones are arranged throughout the park. If desired, you can also feed fish in local ponds.
