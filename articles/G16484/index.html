Oceanarium Anapa "Nemo"

If you and your children spend your vacation in the Anapa area, be sure to visit the Nemo Aquarium!

It will be interesting for both children and adults to look at all kinds of inhabitants of the deep sea that live in the huge aquariums of the Anapa Oceanarium . This is not only a lot of diverse fish, but also electric stingrays, toothy sharks, seahorses and bloodthirsty piranhas.

During the tour, you will be accompanied by a guide who will tell you a lot of interesting facts about the underwater inhabitants of both the salty seas and freshwater rivers and lakes.

Description, photos and reviews about the Anapa Aquarium





The aquarium is located in a semi-basement dark room, which is a comfortable environment for its inhabitants, because very little sunlight comes into the water column where they live in nature. The aquariums themselves are slightly highlighted so that the guests of Nemo can carefully examine all its inhabitants.

In addition to their inhabitants, many marine aquariums contain interesting marine displays that make each fish house individual and not like neighboring aquariums.

If you study the reviews on the Internet about visiting Nemo, you can conclude that not everything is so wonderful: in many aquariums the fish are crowded, there is dirt and an unpleasant smell. But in general, emotions after a visit to Nemo are positive, children are especially happy, because they are the most interesting creatures in the world!





Opening hours and Nemo address

The Anapa Oceanarium is open daily from 9 am to 10 pm, without days off and holidays. The cost of a complex ticket today is 750 rubles, children under 5 years old admission is free. In addition to visiting the aquarium, the ticket also includes a visit to the terrarium and dolphinarium.

Address of the Aquarium: Krasnodar Territory, the city of Anapa, Pioneer Avenue, 20A / 6. This place is located near the city center, and it is easy to get to it for vacationers from Vityazevo, because a direct bus from there runs quite often.

Summary

Even for lovers of a relaxing beach holiday, it will be interesting to visit such fascinating places as the Anapa Aquarium. Do not miss the opportunity to enjoy a guided tour with your children!
