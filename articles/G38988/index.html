How to deposit money from MegaFon to MegaFon and other replenishment methods

Sometimes it happens that you urgently need to call someone, but you have run out of money on your phone bill. Do not rush to despair - there is always the opportunity to replenish the balance. Moreover, if you do not have money, you can ask your friend to transfer funds to your mobile.





This article will discuss how to put money from MegaFon to MegaFon. However, this article is not limited to. In addition, it will tell you how to fund your account in three different ways: using a Sberbank card and online.

We transfer money from MegaFon to MegaFon (the first method)

The first method of replenishment is called "Mobile Transfer" from Megaphone. This method is an official service from MegaFon, so you can be sure that your money will not be lost somewhere along the way. As for the limit, it will be possible to send 5 thousand rubles per month within the region and 15 thousand rubles between the different ones per month.

Well, now let's talk in more detail how to put money from MegaFon to MegaFon. To do this, you will need to send a USSD request. Its form is as follows: * 133 * "amount" * "number" #. Please note that you need to start entering the number from 7.





After you send a USSD request, an SMS will be sent to the sender in response, which will ask for confirmation of the transfer. Replenishment of an account with a Sberbank card

If you do not want to disturb your friends by asking them to send you money to your phone, and you have a Sberbank card, then this method is just for you.

The bank presented has a special service called "Mobile Bank". It is it that allows you to make instant payments to your mobile account by debiting them from the card. In addition, you can connect "Auto Payment". This is a special function that will automatically replenish your account as soon as the balance reaches a minimum level. The main trump card of this method is the fact that the commission is 0%, which will help to significantly save on topping up an account.

To carry out this operation, you will need to send SMS to 900. In the text field, you must specify the recipient's number, transfer amount and the last four digits of the card number. The format of filling is as follows: "beneficiary number" "transfer amount" "4 digits of the card number".
