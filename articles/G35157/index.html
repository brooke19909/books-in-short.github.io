Where to listen to jazz in Moscow: popular places

The Russian capital is rich in concert venues and talented musicians. There are many places in Moscow where to listen to jazz. You can choose whether to listen only to this direction of music or in combination with other genres. Jazz can be both in traditional performance, and in modern interpretation.

Conservatory

You can listen to jazz in Moscow, for example, at the conservatory. This is a place where it is pleasant to enjoy music in a relaxed atmosphere and in pure performance. The Tchaikovsky Conservatory is located on Bolshaya Nikitskaya Street, 13/6.

The building has excellent acoustics, there are two halls - Big and Small. In addition to classical works, jazz often sounds in the conservatory. For example, the program "Kuryokhin and Aigi." The cost of admission to the conservatory varies from 600 to 3500 rubles.



Moscow International House of Music

Another place in Moscow where to listen to jazz is the Moscow International House of Music. It is located on Kosmodamianskaya Embankment, 52, building 8. The House of Music was opened in 2003 and immediately became very popular with the townspeople and guests of Moscow. The complex has three halls, in which jazz performers also perform. They represent both solo numbers and entire programs.

Classical music is performed by Yu. Bashmet, P. Domingo, D. Matsuev and many others. You can buy a ticket for a concert of the Big Jazz Orchestra. The approximate cost of an admission ticket to the House of Music is from 600 to 1300 rubles.

Bars

Where in Moscow to listen to jazz today? On any day, you can visit the Bourbon street bar. Every evening, enchanting jazz tunes are played there. The bar is decorated in the style of the American city of New Orleans, as it was in the early twentieth century. This metropolis is the birthplace of the famous Louis Armstrong. New Orleans is called the "cradle of world jazz." The atmosphere of those times is completely recreated in the design of the bar. Musicians play their pieces every night until morning.

The Rhythm Blues bar is located next to the Lenin Library metro station. In the institution every day you can hear live concerts of jazz, country and blues. The bar has a summer terrace and a large lounge. The walls of the room are decorated with graffiti, and the cost of tickets is quite small.



Moscow House of Blues "BB King"

The ideal place in Moscow, where to listen to jazz and blues, is the institution "BB King." The building of cultural recreation is located at: Sadovaya-Samotechnaya street, 4s2. Any visitor can be lucky to receive a bonus. It consists in providing the guest with a table at which Sting or Ghris Rea was sitting. The approximate ticket price is from 1000 to 4500 rubles.

Art cafe

There are several places in Moscow where you can listen to jazz in a cafe. Durov Art Institution is located at 6. Pavlovskaya Street. The cafe occupies a large building. In addition to performances and fashion shows, Durov became famous for the famous project that appeared in the walls of this institution. On the stage of the art cafe, jazz musicians perform weekly. In "Durov" you can hear both old works and modern ones.

It is recommended to visit the FAQ-Cafe. This art cafe is not just jazz. There are such directions as blues, jam sessions. And on Mondays, the best jazz Moscow artists with different formats come on the scene. The art cafe is located on Bolshaya Polyanka street, 65/74, building 1.
