The Phulin Resort 3 * (Phuket, Thailand): reviews and description

If you plan to relax on the island of Phuket (Thailand) and are looking for a comfortable quiet hotel, then The Phulin Resort 3 * may be a good option for you.



General description, location, photo

This three-star hotel is located ten kilometers from Phuket Town and a few hundred meters from Karon Beach. The nearest airport is 45 kilometers away.

Housing fund "Phulin Resort" is represented by 79 standard and deluxe rooms. Each of them has the necessary furniture, air conditioning, TV, hairdryer, minibar, safe, bathroom.

The hotel has a swimming pool with a sun terrace. The hotel also has a restaurant, bar, tour desk, currency exchange, car rental, parking.



The Phulin Resort 3 * (Phuket): reviews of Russian travelers

According to many tourists, an excellent help when planning a trip abroad is to study the comments of those who have already stayed in a particular hotel. This allows you to get unadorned information about the advantages and disadvantages of hotels. That is why we decided to put together and summarize the available comments of our compatriots about the Phuket "Phulin Resort" hotel. Just note that almost all the reviews of tourists are positive. So it is safe to say that travelers were satisfied with their choice and did not regret the money spent.

Number of rooms

Judging by the reviews, our compatriots were satisfied with the accommodation conditions offered by him at The Phulin Resort (Karon) 3 *. So, the rooms, according to them, are quite spacious, very bright, pleasantly decorated and comfortable. In addition, there is everything you need - a minibar, a safe, a hairdryer, air conditioning, a TV, etc. However, as some tourists say, air conditioners work a little noisy. However, this does not create any special problems, since in most cases the guests come to the room after an active day and fall asleep very quickly. A big plus of the hotel rooms, many call excellent sound insulation. So, you will not be disturbed by neighbors or loud sounds coming from the street. As for the minuses, some tourists were not very pleased with the fact that the apartments did not have balconies.



Cleaning service

As guests note, they were completely satisfied with the work of the maids of The Phulin Resort 3 * hotel (Phuket, Thailand). Rooms were cleaned every day. Towels were also updated daily and supplies of drinking water in the minibar and shower accessories in the bathroom were replenished. The maids changed the bedding a couple of times a week.

As for the service as a whole, tourists have no particular complaints about it. According to them, administrators at the reception always try to quickly resolve every issue that arises. In addition, in most cases, they do everything possible to accommodate newly arrived guests in their designated rooms immediately after arrival, without condemning them to waiting for the check-out time. If something goes wrong in your apartment, then after contacting the reception the master or another hotel employee will fix the problem as soon as possible.



Nutrition

When booking accommodation at The Phulin Resort 3 * (Phuket), you can choose both breakfast and full board. But the vast majority of guests, according to them, preferred the first option. So, as for breakfast, they generally seemed lodgers ordinary. In the mornings, you could always try scrambled eggs or omelettes, boiled eggs, toasts with jam, soup, vegetables and fruits. For a change, some tourists sometimes preferred to have breakfast in one of the neighboring cafes. As for lunch and dinner, you can eat both in the hotel restaurant (for an additional fee), and on the beach or in the vicinity of the hotel. According to our compatriots, they cook very tasty almost everywhere, and the prices do not bite at all. However, as some guests have noted, lunches and dinners at the hotel may cost you a little more than meals outside.

Sea

According to tourists, from the hotel to the popular Karon Beach you can walk on foot in 15-20 minutes. Some might think that this is far, but most of the guests knew about this feature in advance and were ready for it. Moreover, in their opinion, it is always pleasant to take a walk while admiring the surroundings. The beach itself is municipal, so if you want to use a sun lounger and a parasol, you will have to pay for it. But most tourists take mats with them and are located right on the sand. By the way, beach towels are provided free of charge to guests of The Phulin Resort 3 *. You can take and exchange them at the reception. As for the beach itself, it is sandy and very clean. The entry into the water is gentle and even. So it will be comfortable here even for families with small children. In addition, there is a mass of cafes on the beach where you can enjoy a drink and a snack.
