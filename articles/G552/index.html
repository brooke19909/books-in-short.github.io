Cleopatra Apartments 3 *, Cyprus: photos, prices and reviews of tourists from Russia

One of the most popular resorts of Cyprus, which has long been loved by our compatriots, is Ayia Napa. In particular, this place is suitable for lovers of vibrant nightlife. The hotel base of this resort is also diverse. Here everyone will find a hotel for themselves in accordance with their taste and financial capabilities. If you are planning an economical vacation in Ayia Napa, then the three-star Cleopatra Apartments hotel will be an excellent option. We offer you to learn more about what this hotel is like, what services it offers its guests, as well as what our compatriots remember here.





Cleopatra Apartments 3 * (Cyprus): where is it

This hotel is just 800 meters from the center of the famous Ayia Napa resort. The nearest city beach is 500 meters away. Thus, you can walk here in leisurely steps for 10-15 minutes. The famous Nissi Beach and Limanaki beaches can be reached in five minutes. From the nearest airport in Larnaca, the hotel can be reached in an average of 40 minutes. Such a transfer is easy to book at the hotel. It will cost around 55-60 euros. However, you can get to Ayia Napa by shuttle bus or bus, which will be much cheaper.



What is Cleopatra Apartments

Cleopatra Apartments 3 * is a very small apart-hotel, consisting of only fifteen rooms. It includes one building. The surrounding area has parking and a pool. This hotel is located in a fairly quiet area and is perfect for both youth recreation and families with children of different ages.

Hotel Policies

According to the internal rules of the Cleopatra Apartments 3 * hotel (Ayia Napa), check-in to the rooms booked by tourists is made from two in the afternoon. If you arrive earlier, you can be settled immediately only if the previous guests have already vacated the room and the hotel staff managed to clean it. You must leave the hotel on the day of departure until 12 noon. Cleopatra Apartments accepts cash only.

The hotel allows children of any age. If you come with a baby up to two years old, then its placement in a crib in your room will be free. Also, you do not have to pay separately for accommodation in existing bedding for a child from two to ten years old. For placing a child aged 11-16 years on an extra bed, you will have to pay seven euros for each day of stay. The maximum in each room can accommodate one cot or extra bed. This service is available upon request and must be sent to Cleopatra Apartments in advance. As for pets, their accommodation is not allowed. The exception is guide dogs accompanying their owners who wish to stay at the hotel.





Number of rooms

As already mentioned, the three-star Cleopatra Apartments (Ayia Napa) has 15 rooms at its disposal. They consist of one-bedroom apartments for two adults and two-bedroom apartments (for four adults). All rooms include a kitchenette, dining area, bathroom with shower, air conditioning, balcony, satellite TV and fridge. The rooms are quite spacious (50 and 80 square meters) and very comfortable. Rooms are cleaned regularly and sheets and towels are changed. If necessary, you can order room service. A hairdryer, iron or ironing board can also be provided upon request.



Nutrition

The price at Cleopatra Apartments 3 * hotel includes breakfast only. It takes place in the hotel restaurant, where a buffet is served . The √† la carte restaurant also serves dinner.

Infrastructure

Despite the fact that Cleopatra Apartments is a very small hotel, it has all the necessary infrastructure for a comfortable stay. So, the reception at the hotel is open around the clock. Therefore, even if you plan to arrive at the hotel during off-hours or if you have an urgent situation, you can always contact the on-duty administrator who will resolve all issues. You are also entitled to pre-order a transfer from / to the airport. If you arrived at the hotel ahead of schedule, and the previous guests have not yet vacated the room you booked, then you can leave your things in the luggage room and go to the pool or the beach.

The hotel provides wireless Internet access. This service is paid. Also, the hotel can take advantage of laundry and dry cleaning services, exchange currency, rent a bicycle or car, book an excursion that interests you. In addition, valuables can be stored in a safe at the reception.

There is also parking on site, the use of which is free for guests. Thus, if you want to rent a car, you will not have problems with parking.





Entertainment

An outdoor pool is on site. It works seasonally. Near the pool you will find sun loungers and umbrellas. There is also a bar where you can order both non-alcoholic and alcoholic drinks.

You can play billiards, table tennis and massage services at the hotel.





Sea, beaches

Cleopatra Apartments (Cyprus) does not have a private beach. The nearest city beach is 800 meters away. Thus, you can reach the sea in just 10-15 minutes. There will have to pay separately for the rental of sunbeds, mattresses and umbrellas. The beach itself is sandy.

As for the whole beaches of Ayia Napa, they are considered here as one of the best in all of Cyprus. They are covered with fine white sand, which is so pleasant to walk on. And due to the fact that the resort itself is located in a quiet bay, the sea here is very calm and warm. Not only the natural beauty of this place is impressive, the authorities tried to create an excellent infrastructure for recreation. The most popular beaches of Ayia Napa are: Nissi Beach, famous for its DJ parties, more relaxed and family-friendly Macronissos Beach, and the very picturesque Ayia Thekla.

In addition to standard swimming in the sea and sunbathing, on the local beaches of Nissi Beach, Limanaki, as well as on the New Golden Beach, you can do various kinds of both beach and water sports. There is also the opportunity to go diving.





Cost of living at the three-star Cleopatra Apartments Hotel

As for the cost of living in this apart-hotel, it is quite consistent with its level of stardom. So, a ten-day tour with a flight from Moscow and accommodation in a double room will cost you an average of 57 thousand rubles.

Cleopatra Apartments: Travel Reviews

We bring to your attention generalized comments from travelers staying in Ayia Napa in the three-star hotel described. So, most of the tourists found the hotel quite consistent with their stardom and cost of living.

Most of all, vacationers were pleased with the location of the hotel. After all, it is located almost in the city center, near the bar street with many bars and nightclubs. Many "party people" appreciated the fact that after visiting fun discos they did not have to get to their hotel for a long time. There are also many shops, restaurants, cafes, etc. The city beach is also located very close. However, many tourists preferred to go to other beaches, mainly to Nissi Beach. This could be done either by bus or taxi.

The hotel itself also attracted many tourists. Vacationers found it, though small, but very comfortable. The rooms also made an extremely positive impression on our compatriots, because there is everything for living: from a TV and phone to an air conditioner, refrigerator, stove. In some rooms, guests even found a hand blender and air mattresses for the beach. Also, hotel guests appreciated the good soundproofing of the rooms, so that sounds coming from nightclubs do not interfere with sleep.
