Necklace "Pigtail"





This simple, but very beautiful jewelry can be made even by a novice craftswoman. The number of colors can be unlimited, as well as the method of braiding. All that is required is a lot of beads, a strong fishing line, a high-quality fastener and a little patience. Choose a color palette for the outfit with which you are going to wear this bead necklace. The weaving pattern is extremely simple: measure several pieces of fishing line of equal length depending on the thickness of your neck, tie one end to the clasp. Start stringing the beads to the end of the line. To speed up this procedure, use a thin needle. After the required amount of beads is strung on each piece of fishing line, braid the pigtail. Fasten the clasp from the other end and check the necklace for strength. It will turn out quite heavy, so it is important to choose a dense material so that it does not dissolve.

Elegant bead and bead jewelry





DIY handmade jewelry set - every fashionable girl dreams of it. Many jewelry is sold in thousands of copies and is in the box with almost every third beautiful lady, but in the pursuit of originality should not be like the rest. Make yourself a necklace and earrings from beads yourself and be sure that you will no longer find the second such set. Get the most beautiful beads of any diameter and three large brooches in the needlework store. You will also need earring hooks and a necklace clasp. Begin to string in a row beads of different shapes and diameters, which successfully combine with the overall color scheme. The number of tiers can be arbitrary. Attach each piece of fishing line to the clasp. When the necklace is ready, attach a large brooch to one edge. Now you can start making earrings that will perfectly complement your look. Attach brooches to each hook and, if desired, attach loops from beads that you used to make the necklace. As you can see, weaving a bead necklace is extremely simple, and having spent a little effort, in a day you will become the owner of this magnificent kit.

Elegant necklace for a young lady (weaving pattern)

Braid the other side of the pearl in the same way, but do not forget to weave one smaller pearl.
