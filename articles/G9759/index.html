General impression

If you dream of a sea vacation, then you will not lose by choosing the resort Palmariva Beach. One of its main advantages is its private beach, which is very carefully looked after. Despite its small size, all guests will be able to comfortably stay here, given that the entrance is unauthorized. Sun beds are always enough. The only reason to arrive at the beach early is to take a deck chair in the first line near the sea. So, if you want to sunbathe and swim, this hotel is just perfect for you.

If you are an active person who does not want to lie on the beach all day, then this option of accommodation is most likely not suitable for you. The fact is that there is practically no infrastructure around the hotel. You will have nowhere to go for a walk, have a bite and see something interesting. The only salvation is the bus station, from where transport runs regularly to the resort center, as well as to neighboring cities. There is also nothing special to do on the territory. Animation is good only for children, but adults here are unlikely to be able to have fun. Thus, before choosing a hotel, determine your trip objectives and carefully review the reviews.
