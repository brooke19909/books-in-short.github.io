"Braer Park" in Belgorod - a great solution when choosing a residence

At a distance of 700 kilometers south of the capital of Russia is the city of Belgorod, considered the most environmentally friendly in the country. It is economically developed, with a powerful construction industry and aimed at improving the living standards of its inhabitants. Thanks to this trend of prosperity, the care of the authorities, its population is growing steadily from year to year, wages are rising. People from many cities of the country flock here for permanent residence. The developers consider Belgorod to be very promising for the implementation of their projects. A vivid example of this is the Braer Park residential complex in Belgorod.

North Village

Only 2 kilometers separates the village of North from Belgorod. Justifying its name, it is located just on the same side - the north.

This is a fairly large urban-type settlement with a population of more than 10 thousand people and all the necessary infrastructure. It was he who was selected by the developer of Braer Park in Belgorod. You can get into it along a major transport highway - Shosseynaya Street, turning onto the Main.

Description of the residential complex

Braer Park in Belgorod is still under construction. In total, the construction project provides for the construction of eight multi-storey buildings of different floors - 3 and 5 floors. The end of construction is the end of 2018. During the construction of houses, innovative technologies, natural ceramic building materials are used.

Infrastructure

Residents of Braer Park in Belgorod can take advantage of the entire infrastructure of the village of Severny, where there are preschool educational institutions, schools, many shops, banks, pharmacies and everything that is necessary for a comfortable existence.
