The best pastime, as the reviews show, is rest on Lake Baikal

Thousands of tourists from all over the world prefer traveling to Lake Baikal. This is one of the few places where there are still opportunities for eco-tourism, as evidenced by numerous reviews. Rest on Baikal is silence and beauty, there you can feel the atmosphere of pristine nature. In some incomprehensible way, the soul of people who have visited this place is changing. It is filled with positive energy, which helps to find peace of mind and balance.

According to reviews, rest on Lake Baikal is suitable for any category of tourists. Travel lovers are offered a large selection of excursion routes. Outdoor enthusiasts can enjoy scuba diving, snowboarding, ATVs, and buggy riding . Fishing lovers here can carry it out in one of the most beautiful bays. Those who value peace and tranquility will also find a corner.



One of the most popular places is the village of Gremyachinsk, located 138 kilometers from Ulan-Ude, on the very shore of Lake Baikal. This settlement got its name thanks to the stream of the same name, which flows near the village and flows into Lake Wild. There is a beautiful beach and a small waterfall. According to reviews, rest on Lake Baikal in the village of Gremyachinsk will allow you to see a number of attractions, including a monument to air travelers, Turuntaevskaya cave. Among the architectural structures, the Holy Trinity Selenginsky Monastery should be noted. The main natural attractions of this village are the Lakes Wild, Small and Big Winds. One of the main beauties of the area is the Haim River, which means “warm water”.



Goryachinsk is considered to be no less attractive, or rather, its therapeutic mud. This settlement is located 180 kilometers from Ulan-Ude. It is here that the first balneological resort on Lake Baikal is located, about which you can read only positive reviews. Holidays on Lake Baikal in the village of Goryachinsk include sandy beaches, coniferous forests and ozone-rich air, a picturesque bay and hot springs that can cure many diseases of the spine, respiratory system, nervous system, etc. A number of guest houses are offered for tourists, among which Hot Key and Bai-Kul.

Rest on Baikal in 2013 showed that many tourists prefer the village of Enkhaluk, located in the Kabansky district. From Ulan-Ude it is separated by a distance of 170 kilometers. Near the village are hot springs.



There are many interesting places on Lake Baikal, from the view of which they are simply breathtaking. One of these is the island of Olkhon. He is full of mysteries and secrets. Locals call it the abode of spirits and shamans. To this day, caves preserved rock paintings of ancient people. Many consider the island sacred. While traveling on the way of vacationers, there are sacred places designed to appease the spirits. You can see the wish tree with numerous ribbons.

Rest on Baikal will be incomplete without a visit to the largest lake Shara-Nur. It is the only body of water in these places filled with mineralized water. The best rest on Lake Baikal can be provided if you visit a ski resort. Already in December, the famous winter resorts, including "Sable Mountain", have been waiting for lovers of such a holiday.
