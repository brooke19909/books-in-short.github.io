How to knit a vest with knitting needles and crochet?

Are you an aspiring craftswoman and would like to create a stylish, original thing? Then you should pay attention to such a wardrobe item as a vest. After all, it can perfectly fit into the festive attire, emphasize the office dress cod. And it will always create your chosen image, cheer up. We will tell you how to crochet a vest and on knitting needles.

Crochet a vest

The hook, unlike knitting needles, allows you to create airy, openwork patterns. They can create a dense canvas, and lace motifs. Depending on your personal choice, the vest will look different. Consider how to crochet a vest, and what you need to know about it.

To knit a lightweight openwork vest, not so much thread will be required, when compared with knitting a dense fabric. It knits quickly and can become a real decoration for a festive outfit if you make beadwork on it.

As you know, a vest is a sleeveless jacket that can be fitted, loose. Strict in style, or vice versa, bright and unusual, which is welcomed in clothing for cultural activities. The vest is suitable for any weather and is easy to manufacture, because it consists of only three parts: a back and two shelves, left and right.

Before knitting a vest, calculate the number of loops based on the quality of the chosen yarn and your size. For example, for the forty-second size you need to dial ninety air loops. When knitting at the end of a row, be sure to make one air loop for lifting. Knit openwork pattern. In the first row, crochet columns. The second row - a single crochet, four air loops, through two columns of the previous row in the third knit a single crochet. Then embossed relief loops. At the same time, in the 3rd row they will be located between 7 arches, and will be in the middle in the fifth. In each of the three air loops of the arch, knit double crochet, and then connect them into a loop.

Having knitted six rows, it is necessary to make a decrease in half of the arch. Count thirteen centimeters from the edge of the canvas and begin adding on two sides in each fourth row half of the arch of the pattern. For armholes, make reductions when there are thirty-one centimeters from the edge of the canvas. To do this, cut half the arch of the pattern six times, the result should be nine arches. At the height of fifty-three centimeters, counting from the edge of the canvas, finish the work. The right shelf fits symmetrically. Sew details. Tie the edges of the finished product and armholes.

Knitting vests

Now consider knitting patterns made from woolen threads. The following tips will tell you how to knit a vest with knitting needles. First of all, choose a pattern for the future product. The simplest option is a garter stitch, it allows you to make both sides the same. In order to make the vest more original, you can use different thicknesses of knitting needles, threads of different colors. Knowing how to knit the main loops - the wrong and the front, and choosing how to knit a vest, you can perform simple patterns. For knitting armholes and necks, it is necessary to reduce the loops, while two adjacent loops are knitted together. To form a beautiful bend, you need to knit short rows. Most often, knotting the neck, the cuts are done as follows: insert the needle from right to left, grab the thread from above and away from you, stretch it through two loops and then remove them. Often, in order to create a truly beautiful and original thing, some especially complicated techniques are not needed. Finished accessories will decorate the finished vest . It can be embroidered with beads, glass beads. It depends on your imagination.

Knitting is a fascinating activity. And now that you know how to knit a vest, you can decorate your wardrobe with a new thing.
