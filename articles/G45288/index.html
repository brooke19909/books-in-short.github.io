Hotel Retreat Anjuna 3 * (Anjuna, India): photos and reviews of tourists

Anjuna is perfect for tourists who love outdoor activities. It will be a great place for young people who like to spend time behind active classes. There are many nightclubs everywhere, and discos are held even in the open. Sandy beaches are also nearby. Therefore, nightlife can be combined with swimming in the sea. The budget vacation spot here is the Retreat Anjuna 3 * complex (India). You can read the description of the hotel in our article.

Location

The hotel is located on the third beach line. To get to the sea you have to overcome a fairly large distance (about 1.5 km). The beach here is public, so not only tourists from other hotels, but also local residents rest on it. Sun beds, mattresses and sun umbrellas are provided for a fee. Guests of the Retreat Anjuna 3 * hotel (India, Anjuna) can reach the coast or the central part of the city by bus. Tourists will find many infrastructure facilities within walking distance of the hotel. There are local restaurants, souvenir shops, which also sell inexpensive clothes and shoes of Indian and Chinese production.

However, the nearest major trading market is quite far. It is removed almost 15 km from the hotel. Most often, tourists come here from Dabolim Airport , which is located 50 km from here. For guests, a paid transfer is organized on a comfortable bus, and they can also come here by taxi or their own car. On average, the trip to the hotel takes a little more than 1 hour.

Housing stock

This small complex is designed for budget accommodation, which means that here you are unlikely to see modern equipment, quality furniture and luxurious interiors. In total, Retreat Anjuna A Boutique Resort 3 * hotel has 26 rooms, which consist of a living room and a private bathroom. The apartments also have a rather large balcony. It offers pool or tropical garden views. Guests can expect daily housekeeping. Maids will also regularly change bed sheets and towels in the bathroom. For a fee, you can always order room service, namely the delivery of food, drinks or newspapers.





Guests are offered a choice of two accommodation options:

  • Deluxe Room. The hotel offers 10 rooms of this category. The apartment is 18 square meters. m. No more than two adults can live here at the same time. They are accommodated in a double bed. Also, a child can be additionally accommodated in the room.
  • Super Deluxe Room. They differ from the previous room only in a larger living room area. She here is 22 square meters. m. In total, the hotel has 16 rooms of this category.

Room features

In addition to a set of furniture, each room of the Retreat Anjuna 3 * hotel has certain amenities that make living here as convenient as possible. We list the main ones:

  • Plasma TV. A modern model to which several cable TV channels are connected. Most of them are Indian.
  • Individual air conditioning that keeps the room cool. You can adjust the cooling level using the remote control.
  • A telephone that is commonly used to contact the hotel manager. The rest of the calls are paid.
  • Iron and ironing board. Available upon request for free.
  • A hairdryer, soap, toilet paper and a set of towels in the bathroom.
  • Mini bar. It is located in the room for free, but filling it is possible only at an additional cost.
  • "Wi-Fi" is connected to the room only for a fee.




Food options

The Retreat Anjuna 3 * hotel has a food system that includes breakfast only. They are served in a small dining room, which is located on the ground floor. International dishes are served in the morning. The menu includes scrambled eggs or scrambled eggs, sandwiches with sausage or cheese, pastries, tea, coffee and cereal, filled with hot milk. Fresh fruits or vegetables are sometimes served. The complex also has a banquet room, which you can rent for a fee. Celebrations are held here, as well as a buffet for business meetings. Dinner or lunch is available at an additional cost. Tourists can enjoy local or Chinese dishes. There is also a bar on site that serves refreshments, cocktails, ice cream, fruits, desserts and spirits.





Local infrastructure

Hotel Retreat Anjuna 3 * ( Anjuna ) has few stars, so the infrastructure here is not very diverse. However, for guests here are offered all the necessary amenities for a quality stay. We list the main services that the hotel offers its guests:

  • A conference room equipped with computer equipment. It is suitable for small business meetings, negotiations, conferences and seminars. Its rent is possible only for a fee.
  • Fitness center with gym. There is equipment for strength exercises, as well as treadmills and balls for gymnastics.
  • Free Wi-Fi in public areas: in the lobby and by the pool.
  • 24 hour hotel security. Also, tourists are not allowed to bring guests who do not stay at the hotel.
  • Doctor services on request. All his receptions are paid separately.
  • Laundry and dry cleaning. Their services are also paid.
  • Outdoor pool with sun deck. Tourists are provided with free sunbeds and umbrellas. There is a shallow compartment for children.
  • Spa and wellness center. They offer massage courses, anti-aging and anti-cellulite treatments. For a fee, you can also visit the sauna and jacuzzi.
  • Tour desk with a Russian-speaking guide.
  • Badminton equipment.
  • Scooter and car rental.
  • A safe where you can leave valuables. Located at the reception.




Positive reviews

Many tourists prefer to read reviews about a future vacation destination before issuing a ticket. Hotel Retreat Anjuna 3 * has a mixed reputation among travelers. First, we list the advantages of the complex, which the guests noted:

  • Good and thorough cleaning. Maids tidy up rooms upon request, without requiring a tip.
  • Not very large, but comfortable rooms, which made a fresh repair.
  • Friendly staff who not only tries to quickly solve the problems of tourists, but also just loves to talk with guests.
  • A clean pool in which there are never many people. There are always free sunbeds and umbrellas on the tanning bed.
  • Quiet and relaxing atmosphere in the evenings.




Negative reviews

Often, the Retreat Anjuna 3 * hotel receives negative reviews. Among the guests there are many tourists who did not like their stay here. They regretted the money spent. Such travelers describe the following serious shortcomings of the hotel:

  • There is no refrigerator in the room, therefore, food purchased outside the hotel territory immediately spoils.
  • Poor breakfast. If you come to the end of the meal, you can stay hungry, because the food has already ended.
  • At the rented scooters in the hotel, staff drains gasoline at night.
  • Some rooms have access to the road. A lot of cars go there in the morning, so it becomes very noisy.
  • The hotel is often visited by locals. They drink a lot, make a lot of noise and constantly pester other guests.




Should I relax in this hotel?

We can say that the Retreat Anjuna 3 * hotel is suitable for economical tourists who plan to use it only as an overnight stay. It should be noted that most often adults, businessmen and youth stop here. Families with children and older people are advised to choose hotels closer to the beach.
