Apple Watch Series 3: customer reviews

A smart watch with a set of different functions is released today by many manufacturers. Apple made them popular. This gadget has become a life partner of modern people who care about their health.

Today, smart watches are highly functional. One of the best devices in this area is the Apple Watch Series 3. Reviews about this gadget, a review will be discussed later.

general characteristics

In the USA, new smart watches from Apple were presented. This device does not differ in appearance from previous series. This is a stylish gadget that is very popular today. The differences between the Apple Watch Series 3 and its predecessors are significant. They are the functionality of the device.



The device allows a person to control the level of his physical activity. The watch gives tips on the need to get up and stretch. Also in this generation of Apple Watch has been improved many applications. One of them is the Pulse program.

The powerful processor opens up new possibilities for the user when using the Apple Watch Series 3. An LTE antenna is integrated into the display. This allows you to receive mobile network calls directly to this gadget, bypassing your smartphone. It was in this series that it became possible to avoid tying the watch to the iPhone.

Description

The third generation of smart watches presented by the manufacturer include truly multifunctional devices. They differ not only in the mass of possibilities. Their appearance is also interesting. The body of the device is made of aluminum. You can change the straps in accordance with the mood.

The company produces watches with a regular sports strap and Apple Watch Series 3 Nike. The second type of devices allows you to use stylish, original straps. Their design is being developed by world famous manufacturer Nike.



The bright display is made using special technology. It is covered with glass made using Ion-X technology. Two sizes of watches are on sale. Apple Watch Series 3 (42mm) has a resolution of 390x312 pixels. The second model is smaller. Its display has a size of 38 mm and a resolution of 340x272 pixels.

Contents of delivery

Apple Watch Series 3 Cellular has replaced the second generation of smart watches company. It is noteworthy that the first generation model remained on sale. It is distinguished by its simplicity and smaller set of functions. The second model with the release of Series 3 will no longer be released.



The delivery kit includes, in fact, the gadget itself. In the kit, depending on the chosen model, there can be either a simple or designer strap. The choice of textures, shades of accessories is impressive. Apple Watch Series 3 Nike is very popular among buyers . You can choose a strap made by other brands.

Inside the box with the device there is an induction-type charger, a power supply (identical for all iPhones) and instructions. Additional devices for using the presented gadget are not and are not required. There is a red circle on the body of the device that supports the type of LTE connection. If desired, you can attach the device to the iPhone or use the connection using this module.

Application Reviews

Apple Watch Series 3 (42 and 38 mm) have been tested by many users. They leave their feedback and impressions of the use of the presented gadget. Many buyers liked the feeling of a light watch on their hand. They do not cause discomfort. You can choose a model that is optimally suited to the user's clothing style. There is also a universal gadget design. For example, a black watch on a black strap will harmoniously complement both a sports and a business look.



The watch fits snugly on the hand. Tactile sensations are pleasant. The material does not cause allergies, discomfort. A large screen allows you to clearly view the information on the display. The thickness of the smartwatch is quite large. However, the weight remains minimal.

The watch will be comfortable to wear both left-handed and right-handed. There is an opportunity to turn them over in order to ensure comfortable operation on both hands. On the back of the display is a sensor that measures the pulse of the owner of the device.

New features

Apple Watch Series 3 (42 and 38 mm) differ in a number of advantages from the first series. The GLONASS GPS module is installed in the presented device, and there is also a barometric type altimeter. These functions are not provided in the first series.



Apple's third generation smartwatch is water resistant. And not only from splashes, as it was in Series 1. The presented watches can not be removed from the hands during training in the pool. With them you can dive to a depth of 50 m. However, this device is not intended for diving. Also, do not expose it to contact with water at high speed. For normal swimming in the sea or pool, this device is quite suitable.

After water procedures, it is necessary to activate the corresponding function. The system will push the remaining moisture out of the speaker. Also, after taking a shower, you need to wash the device with clean water.

In addition, the difference of the presented series is the presence of voice messages Siri. This allows you to receive information in the most convenient way for the user.

Main functions

Considering the review of Apple Watch Series 3, it is necessary to consider the main functions that remained in the software of the device from the first series. This primarily includes the gyroscope and accelerometer. The company also uses a cardiac activity sensor in the design of its devices.



It should be said that the heart rate monitor in the new version has a number of improved characteristics. This application is not only capable of controlling the heartbeat of its owner. In Series 3, the program determines the acceleration of the heart rate when a person is not engaged in training. This is a very useful feature for people with heart disease.

Also in the third generation of smart watches from Apple, as in the first model, there is a sensor that detects ambient light. The memory capacity is 8 GB in both generations of devices. The back panel of the device is made of composite.

Control

Before buying a smart watch, you should definitely consider the review of the Apple Watch Series 3 . This will assess the comfort of management and use of the new gadget. To make the settings, you need a free hand. In the new version, many functions allow you to configure the virtual assistant Siri. However, the full configuration of all systems will have to be done manually by touching the buttons or display.



Users claim that the first setup and mastering of the basic functions can be completed in about an hour. In this case, all functions are logical. Set the necessary parameters is not difficult.

Using the wheel, you can scroll up or down. With it, you can zoom in or out on pictures, such as a photo, application screen, and more. Pressing the wheel closes open programs, transfers the dials. This button can double-click to open the previous application. Other settings can be made by touching the display.

operating system

Apple Watch Series 3 (42 mm or 38 mm) received the watchOS operating system. In the presented model, it has already reached the 4th integration. The presented operating system has not received global changes. Some new dials have been added to it. One of them is controlled by the Siri voice assistant.

The developers have improved the capabilities of the music application, as well as the Workout program. They got a new design. Smart watches can be synchronized with exercise machines (including cardio).

Cards stored in the device‚Äôs memory are distinguished by good details. The Apple Maps app offers up-to-date routes. If you set the direction of movement, the watch will warn you about a turn with a slight vibration.

Almost all updates orient the user to sports. Relevant applications monitor the health of the owner of a smart watch.

Integrated communication module

As already mentioned, the presented model has a built-in LTE module. Apple Watch Series 3, as conceived by its creators, was supposed to be a standalone version that does not depend on the iPhone. However, in our country such a function will not be available yet.

In order to be able to use the cellular services using smart watches, they have a non-recoverable chip built in, which is a SIM card. However, it is smaller. It is called eSIM, it supports the same network number as the iPhone.

According to forecasts, when the presented feature of Apple Watch Series 3 in Russia will be available, the cost of communication will be much higher than the usual level. Using a smartwatch without an iPhone will still be impossible. It will be required at least in order to activate eSIM.

Technical features

When describing the Apple Watch Series 3 (42 mm) , attention should be paid to some technical features. In the third generation of smart watches of the presented brand, a barometric height meter has been added. This will allow the device to determine whether its owner rises to a height (stairs, in the mountains, etc.). This is a useful feature for snowboarding enthusiasts.

Siri virtual assistant voice messages are made possible by the use of a new powerful processor. It has 2 cores. This is an S3 processor. In normal use, the device is able to operate on a single charge for approximately 2 days. If the device receives a large number of notifications, energy is consumed faster. Charging will need to be done every day.

Among the additional features that the new model received was the possibility of wireless charging using the AirPower station. Connection is via Bluetooth 4.2 or Wi-Fi 2.4 Hz.

Design and cost

According to reviews, the Apple Watch Series 3 has many different design options. The cost of the new items is at least 25 thousand rubles. For this price you can purchase a device with a 38 mm display. Color can be black, silver or gold. If the display is 42 mm, the price will increase by 2 thousand rubles.

If the user wants to buy a new strap, a Velcro nylon product costs about 4 thousand rubles. You can pick up any strap, depending on the mood, style of clothing.

But the model of the first series has become somewhat cheaper. It can be purchased at a price of about 18.5-19 thousand rubles. Some models that have a built-in chip for cellular communications will not be sold in our country yet. This category presents a wide variety of models that differ in a number of parameters and, accordingly, in price.

User reviews

Reviews on the Apple Watch Series 3 are mostly positive. Despite the fact that the design of the third generation of smart watches has remained unchanged, they have many new interesting features. Users highlight a number of programs that they liked the most.

Watches actively set up a person to maintain a healthy lifestyle. The tunes that the user listens to in everyday life can be transmitted to wireless headphones. During training, this significantly increases the comfort of using the device.

Since in our country the gadget will definitely have to be tied to a smartphone, using the clock you can control the settings on both devices. For example, you can turn off the sound, get a call on your smartphone, and a notification on your watch, etc.

The third generation model is much faster. Moreover, the presented device consumes less electricity. This is made possible through the use of wireless modules. Users are satisfied with their purchase. This is a high-quality, reliable and versatile device that will become a real helper in adjusting your lifestyle.
