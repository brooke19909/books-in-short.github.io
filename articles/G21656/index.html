Sberbank Active Age program: description, conditions and features

Sberbank is a popular credit institution offering its customers a large number of different programs and services. Not only officially working citizens, but even students and pensioners can use them. A rather interesting offer is a special program called Active Age. It is offered to people over 50, leading an active lifestyle, preferring to travel regularly and constantly use the Internet.



The main advantages of the program

The program is to issue a special MasterCard card to a person over the age of 50, after which he can become a member of the corresponding club. Economically, "Active Age" is considered a profitable tool of the bank, as the following are its advantages:

  • free annual maintenance is provided, so no funds are required to pay for it;
  • 3.5% per annum is accrued on the balance on the card, which makes it possible to earn good money additionally if the Active Age card is regularly used;
  • a preferential mobile bank is offered that has such a simple interface that it will not be difficult to figure it out, and for the first two months you don‚Äôt have to pay any funds for its use, and then its use will require only a transfer of 30 rubles. monthly.




Due to these many advantages, the Sberbank Active Age card is well-deserved among adults, single-minded and modern people.

How to use a card?

If the program "Active Age" is chosen for cooperation with the bank, then the client of the organization receives a special card. It can be used for different purposes:

  • payment of numerous purchases on the Internet, and this process is considered incredibly simple due to the full, unique and understandable functionality;
  • conducting foreign transactions, therefore, you can use the card not only in different domestic stores, but even in retail outlets located abroad;
  • contactless payment is possible, and such actions allow you to make purchases really safely.

Thus, widespread use of the card is considered to be a popular parameter among users.

What additional features can holders use?

The Active Age program offers users some other unique features:

What are the requirements for customers?

The Sberbank Active Age program for retirees is provided only when certain requirements are taken into account:

  • suitable age, with women over 54 years old and men over 59 years old;
  • the card recipient may be a Russian citizen or a foreigner;
  • registration in the Russian Federation is required, and it can be not only permanent, but also temporary.

All requirements are considered simple and understandable, so many people of the appropriate age are happy to use this banking offer. After joining the club, citizens can use various unique opportunities, communicate with each other and receive encouragement from the organization.

How to draw up a card?

To obtain this banking product, you need to come to a branch of Sberbank. You just need to have a passport with you, confirming the appropriate age of the citizen.

A special application is filled out, the form of which can be taken from an employee of the organization. It is presented in the form of a questionnaire. Next, you need to wait a few days for the release of the card.

Basic principles for the proper use of the card

The Active Age card is convenient and in demand, but in order to use this offer safely, some principles are taken into account.

Never give a PIN code to unauthorized persons. If the phone number is changed, then you need to notify the bank about this. All purchases made on the Internet are protected by the 3D-Secure system, due to which an SMS message with a code will be sent to transfer funds to the phone. It is not allowed to leave the card unattended in suspicious places. If funds are withdrawn at an ATM, you must close the keyboard so that unauthorized persons do not recognize the PIN code.
