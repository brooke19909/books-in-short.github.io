Water park "Ramayana" in Pattaya: photos and reviews of tourists

Thailand is an alluring fairy-tale country, a great place to spend long-awaited vacations and unforgettable weekends. Several tens of thousands of tourists from all over the world fly here every year. The magic kingdom attracts people with the scorching sun, hot climate, unimaginable diversity of the underwater worlds of the Pacific and Indian oceans, as well as its resort towns. They are the focus of all kinds of services that are provided to tourists for an excellent and comfortable stay.





Paradise of tourists

One of the most popular and affordable resorts in this country is the city with the fabulous name Pattaya (by the way, this word will be correctly pronounced when emphasizing the last vowel - "I"). The resort is known for its beaches, bars, dances, delicious cuisine, a variety of entertainment and attractions.

In 2016, one more unusual and interesting place for visiting tourists became more. In May in Pattaya, the opening of a huge water park with the epic ancient Indian name “Ramayana” took place. I must say that the water park "Ramayana" in Pattaya is the largest not only in Thailand, but throughout Southeast Asia! He promises tourists unimaginable entertainment, during the day - exciting adventures that will cause amazing sensations and leave an unforgettable experience!

Below you can see the sacred symbol in Thailand - the elephant at the entrance to the water park "Ramayana" in Pattaya. Tourists begin to take photos and videos without stopping from the very first steps along the territory of the entertainment center.



Work on design and construction

A new water park in Pattaya "Ramayana" opened in early May 2016, but the construction of the current entertainment water center lasted a long five years (construction began in 2011). Many specialists from all over the world worked on the creation of this place. Huge amounts of money were allocated to the Ramayana water park in Pattaya - over thirty million dollars. The creators carefully thought out the plan of the territory, exceeding its area of ​​one hundred eighty thousand square meters, and also designed the correct location of the main zones of the park.



By the way, the name was reflected in the design of the water center. The proud and sacred name is the Ramayana Water Park in Pattaya. The description of the eponymous ancient Indian epic and narration are freely available and, if desired, can be found. This information will help to understand why the aquacenter in front of visitors appears in the form of a ruined city (what is left of it), in ruins and with surviving statues.

Water park opening

The entertainment center has already managed to gain popularity among lovers of travel and entertainment. Many, visiting Thailand, drove into the Na-Jomtien area in order to visit the resort of Pattaya, water park "Ramayana". The opening took place on May 5, 2016, most recently. A few days before this date, everyone (these are residents of Pattaya, as well as all those who were knowledgeable) visited the water park and rode the roller coaster rides for free. Free entry to the territory and entertainment on the eve of the opening were due to the test mode. In other words, people rode at their own peril and risk - without payment, in exchange for their consent to test the safety of all equipment, which, by the way, meets all the latest requirements and international safety standards. These days, the Ramayana water park in Pattaya caused positive reviews, it was rated highly, after which many tourists came to the expected event - the opening of the park itself.



It is important to note that all the attractions of the park are made of high quality materials that have passed the necessary tests and checks. Among the personnel working in the water park there are trained rescuers and highly qualified medical workers.

Huge world of entertainment

A diverse world of entertainment is located on almost two hundred square meters of Pattaya. Water park "Ramayana" in Pattaya will captivate the imagination of tourists: fifty rides, clean water from the depths, supplied to the slides and filling the pools, romantic bungalow arbors, nearby the picturesque beauties of Silver Lake.

Tourists will find a variety of adventures and entertainments on the rides in the water and on land. Here are some of them (the names will be original).



  • Dueling Aqua Coasters are the longest roller coaster that exists. Rises and descents at two hundred thirty meters in length in a tube for two people. There are two of them in the water park. Absolutely the same. Designed for the competition of tourists with each other at speed.
  • No less unique - Python and Aquaconda . Here you can raft a company - a four-seater raft. Ahead are expected six meters (in falls and take-offs) in huge tunnels in diameter. These two attractions are unique. Their analogues are not yet in the world.
  • The Mat Racer is an attraction, also created for lovers of fast driving (or rather, alloy) and competition. To go down the hill you have to lie on the mattress and swiftly fly down on it, being almost in free fall.
  • Boomerango - a thrilling attraction will not leave you indifferent. Overcoming and conquering a vertical wall is provided to every brave man!
  • Aqualoop - starting acceleration gives tremendous speed at which it is difficult to imagine a dead loop of three hundred and sixty degrees!
  • Freefall - a direct descent down in the spray of water in a free fall and a soft landing on the waterway.
  • Spiral , as well as Serpentine - attractions that guarantee an exciting descent through the tunnels with a huge number of turns and accelerations.
  • It is worth mentioning separately the entertainment of the River (Lazy River) . The descent down the hill opens a relatively long trip on a tube along the perimeter of the entire water park, where fusing tourists are driven by an artificial current.

This is only a small part of the entertainment program, but it is already amazing!

Children's area



Water park "Ramayana" in Pattaya also offers two separate children's zones with a special design, which include small pools, secret caves artfully arranged on a separate island, numerous slides, water cannons, sports water stairs and countless small fountains. Separately, the attractions Aquaplay and Kid's Aquasplash, designed for children aged three to fourteen, should be noted. The entire territory of the children is closely monitored by water park staff, including professional rescue instructors. However, the staff serving the Ramayana water park in Pattaya warns that children should not be left unattended!

Safety and comfort

The Ramayana staff (which is three hundred and fifty employees) cares about the amenities for their visitors. On the territory of the water park (in front of the entrance to the entertainment center) there is a parking lot for tourists arriving by car or tourist buses.

There are also paid services for a more comfortable stay of tourists: tourists can freely change clothes, take a shower in special rooms for this, divided into male and female.



Before entering the territory of the attractions themselves, luggage rooms are located where you need to leave all your belongings. The safety of things is guaranteed. You can use the locker during the day as often as you like. The key is an individual and unique bracelet that is issued to the tourist at the entrance and is attached to the wrist.

It offers visitors all day long special fast food restaurants with a variety of delicious food, separate bungalow arbors (with fans), if necessary, for a secluded vacation. They also come with sun loungers, umbrellas and even sockets for charging phones and other equipment, allowed to be carried to the territory of the water park.

Tourists themselves leave the best reviews about the Ramayana water park in Pattaya. The staff of the entertainment center cares about the comfort and safety of their guests. The park’s feedback with visitors is instant in the form of thanks, smiles, happy faces and feedback on the work of Ramayana at the end of the day.

On a note

You will need to prepare a little to visit the water park and check your bathing suit for the absence of large zippers, freely hanging ribbons and shoelaces, etc. All kinds of jewelry in the form of earrings, bracelets, chains, hairpins, etc. are forbidden, respectively. tourists in outer clothing that is not designed for swimming will be skipped. It is recommended that while using the attractions, leave phones, cameras and camcorders in storage cells for your own safety.

Most importantly, the weather ...



“Ramayana” in Pattaya works three hundred sixty-five days a year without holidays and weekends. Working day from ten in the morning to six in the evening. Only the weather can interfere with the entertainment water park - heavy rain with a strong wind. When the weather calms down, the center is restored.

Is it all for the money?

A visitor pays a ticket for entering the water park territory - this allows him to use any attractions as much as his heart desires. However, all the additional services in the form of using the left-luggage office, visiting restaurants, providing VIP services, ordering bungalows and other services.



However, there is no need to carry cash. In this case there is an electronic bracelet. Previously, funds are transferred to the number of this bracelet, funds can be spent on the territory of the water park. If the money remained on the bracelet by the end of the day, it is easy to withdraw and turn it into cash.

A trifle, but nice

The water park offers a small discount as a bonus to guests for joining the ranks of its numerous club. For such a membership, visitors receive a five percent discount on all types of services provided by the park. As they say a trifle, but nice.



You should familiarize yourself with Ramayana promotions and news in advance in order to be able to use them in a timely manner due to the seasonality of the event. The discount on admission of such tickets is quite significant. For example, the September action is a new outburst related to the Ramayana club members, giving grandparents and children the opportunity to enter the water park for free!

Joining the Ramayana Club is very simple. You need to go to the official website (the Russian version is available) and just register.

Another relevant and good news. If you are going to Pattaya and plan to visit this amazing water entertainment center, then you can buy tickets now, at home, without leaving your computer.

How to get there



One of the most popular questions about the Ramayana water park in Pattaya: how to get to it. This place is located about twenty minutes from the resort of Pattaya. If guests travel by car on their own, then they simply use the navigator or navigate on a previously captured map. Taxi is also a fairly suitable option, solving the issue of movement. And one more way. The Ramayana water park in Pattaya also offers its solution: how to get there. Visitors need to pre-order on the official website or call a transfer service at the right time. And the problem is simply resolved.

Water Park "Ramayana" in Pattaya: reviews of tourists

It is no exaggeration to say that everyone who has visited the walls of the largest water park in this area is impressed by what he has seen and lived through, and therefore advises others to visit the Ramayana.

Many tourists remembered the “Lazy River" - this is the largest attraction of five hundred meters. Lying on a circle and moving with the help of artificial currents, you can get acquainted with the territory of the entire water park, taking a break from active entertainment (or lunch) and not hurrying anywhere.

Among the local attractions, tourists left a lot of positive reviews about the jacuzzi bar. This is a bar located in the pool along with a bar, tables and seats, which is already unique.

Someone in the reviews recalls the green maze, impressive in its design. By the way, it was created in the form of the emblem of the Ramayana. Other visitors recall that before entering the park they were met by elephants. Still others advise to drop by the floating market, where you can buy unusual souvenirs and taste the sweets of Thai cuisine.

Separately, the services of masseurs and an underwater photographer are noted, which make the rest in the water park even more fabulous.



It is difficult to list absolutely all the advantages and advantages of this place, because everything that is in this amazing territory is worthy of attention. One thing is certain for sure: if you are going to Thailand, you must definitely build your holiday itinerary in a certain way so that it passes through the Ramayana water park. So you can see for yourself the reliability of all the reviews and, most importantly, try out all these amazing rides!
