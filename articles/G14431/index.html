Ripario Hotel Group (former boarding house "Coastal") in Yalta

In the summer, many are interested in information about where you can go to relax and heal. If you do not plan a trip abroad, then one of the domestic resorts, for example, Crimea, can be considered as a vacation destination. Any tourist will like the peninsula, because here you can have a good rest at the sea, undergo a course of spa treatment and see numerous historical and natural attractions. In our article we want to talk about the resort complex Ripario Hotel Group. Perhaps this is what you like.

A bit about the complex ...

Ripario Hotel Group is a modern complex on the Black Sea coast of Crimea. It is located on the territory of an ancient park, which once belonged to Count Orlov-Davydov. Currently, the hotel complex occupies a very large territory, more than 10 hectares. It was organized on the basis of the former boarding house "Coastal" after a complete reconstruction in 2010-2011.

Fans of beach and sea holidays will certainly appreciate the advantageous location of the hotel, on the beach near Yalta in the village of Otradnoe. By the way, Otradnoe is located between Nikita and Yalta.

The hotel is located on the beautiful Massandra beaches, surrounded by mountains and the sea. Nearby is the famous Nikitsky Botanical Garden and the tasting room of the Magarach wine factory. Ripario Hotel Group is located at: Otradnoye village, Morisa Toreza street, 5.

Hotel Residential Fund

Ripario Hotel Group is a fairly large complex consisting of a number of buildings (one-, two-, three-, and six-story buildings) scattered throughout the park. Each building has its own characteristics and advantages, so guests have the opportunity to choose the most liked building and room.

Infrastructure "Modern"

The complex offers its guests an extensive and varied number of rooms. The Ripario Hotel Group Modern is a six-story Modern building, renovated in 2010. The building is equipped with two modern elevators. On the ground floor there is a Spa clinic, and on the first floor there is a reception desk, a modern conference room and the Repario Modern restaurant, which offers guests the best dishes of European cuisine.



From the second to the fifth floor of the building there are rooms of the following categories:

  2. Superior North - apartments with views of the vineyards and mountains. The rest of the room equipment does not differ from the previous category.
  3. Comfort - holes have a more modest size compared to other categories, but their equipment is no different.

On the sixth floor there is a billiard room, a meeting room and apartments:

  1. Apartment A is a three-room apartment with an area of ​​240 sq. M., Consisting of two bedrooms, a lobby with a pool table and a fully equipped kitchen. The apartments are equipped with new furniture, a safe, a kettle, all appliances and a set of dishes. It also has a furnished balcony with views of the coast and Yalta.
  2. Apartment D - apartments are 90 square meters. It consists of a living room bedroom and a lobby with a pool table. The apartments are elegantly furnished and have a spacious balcony overlooking the sea coast and Yalta.
  3. Apartment C is an extensive room with two bedrooms, a kitchen and a balcony. The apartments have an area of ​​about 195 square meters.

Ripario Econom Enclosures

“Economy” - the buildings are located in the park 50 meters from the beach. Their number of rooms is represented by the following apartments:

Medical Center

Ripario Hotel Group 3 * is of interest from the point of view that it is not just a hotel with an incredibly diverse number of rooms, but a complex in which vacationers have the opportunity to recover during their holidays. Of course, the hotel cannot be called a full-fledged sanatorium, but still there are some medical procedures for guests.

Ripario Hotel Group has its own medical center, where you can get advice from the following specialists: otolaryngologist, pulmonologist, physiotherapist, pediatrician, balneologist and nutritionist.

The basic package of each guest of the hotel includes a one-time free visit to a balneologist who will be able to give his recommendations regarding the need to undergo certain medical and preventive procedures. The resorts of the South Coast have long been famous for their healing effects on the body. Therefore, being on the peninsula, it is worth paying attention not only to beach-sea vacations, but also to pay attention to medical and recreational activities. In this regard, the village of Otradnoye in the Crimea is quite suitable not only for swimming in the sea, but also for healing. The fact is that a mineral spring (the first on the South Coast) was found on the territory of the complex, which has a unique chemical composition and healing properties. Each guest of the hotel has a daily visit to the pump room for taking mineral water.



If the guests have spa cards, the staff of the medical center offers to take a health course of respiratory diseases, urological problems (Saki mud is used) and general therapeutic ailments. In the arsenal of the complex there is a physiotherapy room, a bathroom, an inhalation room, a fitness room, a hydromassage and a swimming pool. A number of massage therapists and a dentist also work.

Weather in Yalta

Every year, many tourists tend to the Black Sea resorts. Among them, the southern coast of Crimea is especially popular . Here, nature created unique natural conditions. Big Yalta is located in the Mediterranean climate zone, due to the fact that it is protected by the Main Range of the Crimean Mountains. The southern coast has long turned into a single resort, which includes Simeiz, Alupka, Alushta, Livadia, Koreiz, Oreanda, Gaspra and Miskhor.

The unique climatic conditions make the region incredibly attractive. In winter, the Black Sea gives off heat, softening the cold. The weather in Yalta is always an order of magnitude warmer than on the mainland. And in the summer, due to the proximity of the sea, there is no strong heat. The uniqueness of the climate on the south coast is that winters are mild and warm, and summers are very hot, but at the same time not sultry. Spring, as a rule, is long, but autumn, on the contrary, is long but warm, it is called the “velvet season”.



According to many indicators, the climate of the southern coast belongs to the subtropical type. The average annual temperature here is +13 degrees. July is considered the hottest month, during this period the temperature reaches +24 degrees. Even in February, the harshest month, the temperature does not drop below +4 degrees. Numerous guests of the resort have long appreciated all the benefits of such a favorable climate in all respects and incredibly healing sea air filled with volatile pine needles. Due to this, Yalta is known throughout the world as one of the best climatic resorts. The peak of the tourist season, of course, occurs in the summer months, when the sea warms up and allows vacationers to soak up its waves. However, in the remaining months the resort does not stop receiving guests.

It is worth noting that summer on the south coast is dry, warm and long. Sea breezes greatly reduce the scorching effects of heat. In the summer months it is very warm, there is no sweltering heat. The Yalta climate is drier than in the areas of the Black Sea coast of the same Caucasus. Sea water on the South Coast in the summer warms up to a temperature of +22 - + 24 degrees. The swimming season begins in late May and continues until early October. From the first days of September comes the time of the "velvet season". Thanks to the influence of the warm sea, good clear weather is maintained on the coast until December.

Even the winter I Yalta is usually mild and fairly short. Snow sometimes falls, but it melts soon. But in the mountains at this time lies a dense snow cover that allows you to ski and engage in other winter sports.

Ripario Hotel Group: reviews

Continuing the conversation about the hotel, I want to pay attention to the reviews of people who visited it. Is he really as good as tour operators imagine him. Analyzing the reviews, it is worth noting that the complex belongs to those places about which there is a mixed opinion, since the reviews of tourists are quite contradictory. But let's talk about everything in order ...



As we mentioned earlier, the hotel is located in the village of Otradnoye in Crimea, near Yalta. This location is both an advantage and a disadvantage at the same time. On the one hand, the buildings are located in a park area, away from numerous discos and clubs. But on the other hand, you need to understand that the hotel is in some ways divorced from the outside world. If in the daytime you want to go to Yalta, then you can take the bus by spending about ten minutes on the way to the stop. Unfortunately, public transport runs quite rarely (once every half an hour). In addition, you can take a taxi, but their choice is also not great. But in the evening there are problems with transport, it just isn’t there, so it’s difficult to go for a walk, and it’s boring enough to be in the boarding house all the time. According to tourists, the hotel is suitable for those people who do not plan active trips along the coast.

The territory of the complex itself is quite well-groomed, but small. Very pleased with the proximity of the beach. According to guests, the coast is beautiful and clean.

Special attention should be paid to reviews of the hotel room stock. Tourists recommend not booking apartments from the “economy” category, since such rooms are small in size and the windows face a blank wall. Higher-level rooms are located in the Modern building, located by the sea, the windows and balconies of the building overlook the sea coast or the park area.

Meals at the hotel are organized like a buffet. When booking rooms, you can choose the appropriate food option: half board, full board or breakfast. Regarding the quantity and quality of food, vacationers postpone conflicting reviews. Tourists note a fairly simple and simple set of dishes on the buffet. Of course, no one remains hungry, but there is not much variety either. In the evenings, you can visit the hotel’s bar or restaurant, but for this, you must first obtain a hotel card on deposit to be able to pay for the order. Unfortunately, staff does not accept conventional payment cards.

In the summer, children's animators work at the hotel, there is a playground with horizontal bars. Activities for adults are not carried out, except for the evening disco. Children's programs do not differ in variety; the standard set of contests is repeated daily. The hotel offers guided tours of natural and historical sites.



There is also a gym and tennis court. A pleasant moment is the presence of a heated pool with fresh water, which can be an alternative for swimming in the cold season, as it is located in the spa. In the summer, vacationers try to spend most of the time on the coast by the sea.

The complex has its own pump room with mineral water.All guests have the opportunity to visit it, since this service is a standard set of services. In addition, there is a medical center for tourists, where you can get advice from some specialists and undergo a number of procedures that are paid separately. In general, the opportunity to recover a little is happy, but you need to understand that the complex does not have the status of a sanatorium.

A beautiful green area with centuries-old trees is a real highlight of this beautiful and secluded place.

In general, the hotel makes a good impression, if you do not pay attention to minor flaws, and follow the advice of tourists on the choice of apartments for accommodation.
