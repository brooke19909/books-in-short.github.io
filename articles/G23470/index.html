Polymer clay "Artifact". Reviews of needlewomen

Polymer clay as a material for creativity and decor appeared not so long ago, but has already managed to gain quite serious popularity. Clay baked is suitable for some works, and for crafts with kids you can use self-setting clay. In this article, we will tell you about the "Artifact" polymer clay.
