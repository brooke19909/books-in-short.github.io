"Starbaz" (electronic hookah) - reviews, price

Electronic hookahs "Starbaz" are widely popular all over the world. After the official ban on smoking in public places, a wave of popularity of new devices came. Electronic devices are less harmful to health and also easy to use. The manufacturer offers reusable devices that can be filled with different liquids and create a unique taste.

What does it look like?

The device is made of leatherette, however, genuine leather and other materials can be used on an individual order. At the top and bottom of the device are metal plates with the brand logo. If it is connected to the network or the user inhales smoke, one can notice a beautiful illumination of the device.

A mouthpiece with a metal tube is screwed into the upper part of the plate. The main feature of the hookah is the presence of two cartridges. The manufacturer provided for the possibility of their joint use. One cartridge is enough for one and a half thousand puffs (according to the users of this device). Before using an electronic hookah, it is necessary to remove the protective cap and screw in the replaceable element.

Characteristics

The company managed to combine ergonomics, convenience and mobility in the electronic Starbaz hookah. Devices have a modern design, and the taste - the special splendor of a true hookah. Products are presented in different colors. The brand produces an original device, the length of which is 38 centimeters and a mini analogue that fits easily in the palm of your hand. It sounds very cool, but there are extremely negative reviews on the Internet. Electronic hookah does not smoke, is bitter, sits quickly - this is a small part of what users tell about the device. This is largely due to the falsification of products.

How to distinguish a fake

In our country, fakes are quite common. However, there are some secrets that allow you to determine the original device. Many scammers assure customers that the goods arrived directly from the United States. However, the original devices are manufactured in a closed factory in China. You should pay attention to the cost, since the price of an electronic hookah is at least 4,500 rubles.

Each original device is accompanied by a warranty card. On the device should be patent number 1014 and the inscription RoHS CE. The next secret is that the light at the bottom should match the color of the hookah. Original devices are qualitatively and beautifully packed, and cartridges are in polyethylene.

Many users have appreciated the design of the device, as it differs from other mass products for smoking.
