What types of connection are there?

In order for interactive television to work, you must connect the appropriate equipment. However, this can be done in a variety of ways. They can be conditionally divided into three types of connection:

  • Using a router.
  • Via a Wi-Fi hotspot.
  • Using a special PLC adapter or adapter.

And so the interactive television "Rostelecom" is connected. Instructions on how to configure it using all of the above options will be considered with each means of connection separately.





Interactive television "Rostelecom": instructions for connecting a router

One of the most common options for connecting a television is to use a special router. To join it, it is enough to perform the following steps:

  • Pull the Internet cable from the box (in the entrance) to the console.
  • Connect the cable to the router.
  • Connect the set-top box (using the HDMI-connector) to your TV.
  • Plug the device cord into a power outlet.
  • Securely remove excess wires and hide under special protection or carpet.

And if everything is done correctly, almost immediately after switching on you will get access to the Rostelecom TV program package. Payment for the services of the master in this case is not provided, but on the condition that you perform the connection on your own.





Wi-Fi Quick Start Guide

For this connection, you first need to purchase a special wireless adapter. Then it will be necessary to install it on the provider's console itself and connect it. You do not need to configure anything here. It is enough to use the standard settings.

This method is suitable for those users who do not want to pull the cable throughout the room. However, before buying an adapter, be sure to consult with company representatives, since not all store devices are suitable for a branded set-top box.

How to connect a PLC adapter?

To connect you will need a special device (PLC adapter). It connects through a conventional outlet and acts as a kind of adapter that connects all the wires through a single local network.

To get started, connect the cable to the adapter and the TV or computer. Connect all devices to the network and run channel scan. The advantage of this connection method is the ability to connect several Rostelecom devices to the network simultaneously. Internet connection services are paid shortly before all devices are connected to the network.
