Samsung Galaxy S8 Plus: owner reviews, descriptions and specifications

Samsung has changed the standard form with its Galaxy S8 phones, releasing two devices that have the same specification, in addition to screen size and battery capacity. If you liked this phone model, you just need to choose the size that is more suitable without any compromises or additional considerations.

This is a very rare occurrence in the industry. Apple's iPhone has different resolutions and cameras; Huawei's P10 models vary in a wide range of specifications. Google‚Äôs Pixel, the most modern of Android phones, offers a different display resolution for a larger device. Even with the 2018 update, the Galaxy S9 Samsung has placed different cameras in the model.

Infinity Technology Development

While the Galaxy S6 + and Galaxy S7 rounded screen models had some changes, the innovations in the Galaxy S8 matrix shapes made the model the phone of the future. There are a lot of changes in this smartphone. Double curved edges, sculpted sides and a glass back cover have additionally increased water resistance modes.

Samsung Galaxy S8 Plus often gets reviews because of its design. This is the first model in the smartphone market that has similar shapes. On the front panel, a big change is a decrease in the upper and lower sides of the front panels, a change in aspect ratio - 18.5: 9. The company's goal was to increase the screen-to-body ratio and give more display, without leading to a significant increase in the size of the case. The Samsung Galaxy S8 + has a 6.2-inch display, but its width is only 73.4 mm. This is already larger than the iPhone 8 Plus, but the new technology increases the screen matrix itself by half an inch.

Of course, changing the diagonal leads to an increase in the screen, but it is more elongated and not wider, therefore, although the diagonal becomes larger, the client receives the same display area as with a 6.2-inch 16: 9 phone.

Reviews about the "Samsung Galaxy S8 Plus" are mixed about the usability of such a screen. Whether the S8 is too large and bulky is a moot point. For some, perhaps yes, but there is the same Galaxy S8 specification that offers identical technology, but in a smaller 5.8-inch package.

Fingerprint scanner and face recognition

The scanner in the Samsung Galaxy S8 Plus smartphone has the following characteristics:

  1. Iris touch processing technology.
  2. Face recognition processor.
  3. Fingerprint scanner on the back.
  4. Pressure sensitive button below the screen.

It is worth examining the fingerprint scanner and the Home button in detail. The reader is now located at the back, but it is not executed, as, for example, in the Google Pixel 2 XL. This is, according to users, the most successful solution in the location of the sensor on the back side, and the Galaxy S8 +, unfortunately, is not.

It is worth turning to the history of the development of the model. Back in 2010, the original version of the Samsung Galaxy S had a mechanical button. Each new S-Series model followed suit as Samsung avoided the temptation to go on-screen controls. This physical button has recently contained a fingerprint scanner, the result is a result quite similar to the iPhone, but more efficient and convenient to use.

Moving the display into a space without mechanical control means no space for this button and no space for the fingerprint located on the front of the scanner. Its rear position can push the camera away from the point of view of a simple user - this is simply inconvenient. Devices like the Google Pixel avoid this because it is only a fingerprint scanner in the center and the camera is in the corner. With Samsung, the buyer needs to touch the scanner with his finger and not touch the nearby camera lenses.

However, after a week of active use, unlocking becomes more convenient. However, as noted by buyers, problems still remain, since everyone‚Äôs palm sizes are different, and unlocking using a fingerprint scanner will be inconvenient for children or teenagers. Therefore, fortunately, Samsung offers a number of biometric unlock options. There is an ordinary password and a pattern key, but an iris scanner is more suitable.

To do this, you need to scan your eyes by looking at your phone, and it will then automatically give access to work. Of course, for this you will need to activate the scanning mode by long pressing the virtual Home button. In fact, using the new virtual home button and eye scanner is as simple as the old fingerprint scanning system.

For those who do not want to look at the red lights that are used in the front camera for scanning, it is possible to use face recognition technology. This lock system has been under closer scrutiny because people could unlock your phone from a photograph. This is evidenced by tests conducted by users of smartphones with this technology.

Samsung smartphone buyers can now be satisfied with the on-screen buttons. The company has long adhered to this technology off-screen, while most of the Android gadgets have already tried to completely get rid of the mechanical parts on the front panel.

Now you can simply tap the bottom of the screen to go to the main screen. When performing this action, pressure sensitivity and tactile feedback will give the feeling that the user has pressed a mechanical button, although it is completely virtual. This change is quite significant for smartphone owners. Samsung took a step forward, discarding one of the remnants of the old that held the flagship.

It also means that the navigation bar can be dynamic. If users of the Samsung smartphone said that the buttons were located on the panel in the reverse order compared to any other Android device, now all the virtual controls are fully editable. Clients say that, if necessary, you can configure special game modes by adding additional elements to the navigation menu if necessary.

Software

The flagship device is also distinguished by the presence of modern software, which also includes artificial intelligence technologies. The Bixby system has been integrated into the Galaxy S8. After a rather slow start, Bixby managed to expand its appeal by bringing the voice of the software bot to a wider circle of users. You can ask questions, but the most useful part is the ability to change device settings using Bixby voice control. It cannot be ruled out that the Galaxy S8 + is a complex device, and Bixby can help a novice user quickly master the gadget.
