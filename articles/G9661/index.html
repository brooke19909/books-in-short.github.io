Preparatory stage

Making a box

So, we make a gift for March 8 with our own hands for mothers and grandmothers. To do this, in one loop of the base, you need to knit two columns. After that, the walls are made of 4 rows in the same way. If the product seems shallow, the quantity can be increased. Casket of bottles: what is needed?

How else can I make a gift for mom on March 8 with my own hands? For example, you can make a box of plastic bottles. It can be similar to the previous design box or casket. You will need: 6 bottles of plastic, corrugated or any decorative paper, hole punch, hook, thread, scissors.

Manufacture

We make a gift for March 8 with our own hands as follows. First you need to cut off all the tops and bottoms of the bottles. Having received cylinders of the same size, we fold them in half and cut them. We make the same size paper blanks. We take two plates and fold inward inward. Between them we put paper, and fix on the edges with the help of paper clips.





Next, a hole punch is taken for work. On the perimeter of each workpiece, you need to make holes with the same interval. For simplicity, a hole punch is best used on one side only. So you need to pierce all the elements necessary in order to make a casket or box by March 8. With your own hands, it is necessary to thread the thread into the outer hole and tie it with a loop. Then, using a hook, we drive yarn into each hole and knit a crochet column. By analogy, all workpieces intended for the product should be processed.





We have already made a practical souvenir by March 8 with our own hands. Now you should combine all the details into a single whole. In each loop of the bottom and walls we knit a regular column. In the same way, you need to connect all the elements of the box. We attach the lid at the last moment. As in the previous embodiment, the product can be simplified. That is, without a cover.

If you want more complicated ..

We also consider how to make a lockable structure. Taking another workpiece, along its perimeter, several rows should be made with an ordinary column. Then first skip a couple of loops, make a connecting seam with a knitting thread. At the end of the row, also leave several air loops free. In order for the box to close firmly, you need to consider a lock or clasp. In our case, these will be ties from the used thread. In the center of the bottom from the outside, we knit the base. From its middle we make so many air loops so that the segment reaches the lid with a hike. We also perform a tie at the top. At the ends, you can attach beads or tassels from the same thread. The ties will help keep the box closed so that small objects do not fall out of it. So, a souvenir made by March 8 is ready. Now you can give it.
