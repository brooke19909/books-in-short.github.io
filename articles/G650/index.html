Car Alarms "Sherkhan" - truly high quality!



Sherkhan car alarms have been produced at modern factories in South Korea for many years and rightfully earned the title of one of the most popular brands on the Russian market of security systems. Most of the official dealers prefer devices of this particular manufacturer for installation in their service workshops. This is due primarily to the high level of reliability of the Sherkhan alarm, ease of use, unpretentiousness and wide functionality. Key Benefits of Alarm





If we talk about the main advantages possessed by Sherkhan alarms, for starters we can mention the built-in intelligent data transmission system via a special radio channel, which is a unique development of the company and which allows completely eliminating intellectual hacking. In addition, special attention should be paid to the turbo timer designed to work with turbo- type engines , and a special mode that allows you to turn off the alarm for a specified period and thus prevent unexpected operation. In addition, the Sherkhan alarm, which is only the most positive reviews, has a reception range of about eight hundred meters, as well as the ability to programmed arming. It is impossible not to mention such a built-in function as working in parallel with the engine running, and the warranty period of service, which is five years.

Alarm Functionality





As for the functional characteristics of equipment of this brand, it is possible to note the possibility of a two-step disabling of the guard, the turbo timer mode associated with the variable cooling period of the automobile engine turbine, and the ability to connect both positive and negative door sensors. Among other things, experts emphasize the presence of two independent control outputs, the so-called blocking relay and an independent input of an additional sensor. Also, Sherkhan alarms have the ability to track the intensity of a car engine by a tachometric signal.

Purchase and installation

You can buy equipment of this brand in almost any salon specializing in the sale of cars, or at service stations. Moreover, both in the first and second, as a rule, a fairly wide range is presented, which is constantly updated. In addition, the installation of a Sherkhan alarm will be offered to the car owner on the spot. This task is far from the easiest, especially in the case of devices with autostart function, however, thanks to the help of qualified specialists, its solution should not take a lot of time.
