Costa Smeralda: beautiful beaches, warm sea, unusual excursions, impressions and reviews of tourists

Italian resorts are very popular among tourists from different countries. One of the best places is the island of Sardinia, which is considered the center of elite tourism. Beautiful beaches can boast a resort on the island of Costa Smeralda. Its sandy coast stretches for 55 kilometers in the north-east of Sardinia. Experienced travelers believe that this is the most popular and beautiful region of the island. It is he who will be discussed in our article.

A bit about the resort

Costa Smeralda in Sardinia is often called the Emerald Coast. The amazing beauty of the region struck even Prince Karim Aga Khan VI, who was only 22 years old. Together with his friends, he organized a consortium of Costa Smeralda to give the region a tourist development. Since then, the Emerald Coast began to take on a completely different look.

The construction of the first hotels began on its territory, which in the summer turned into a meeting place and communication between representatives of the upper class and the powerful. The resort gained popularity not without the help of the press, which sought to describe in great detail the rest of celebrities. Stunning beaches, clear sea, cliffs and Mediterranean thickets with centuries-old trees attracted countless tourists.

Erected villas, restaurants, hotels, night clubs, discos and a port were intended for the elite. Construction work was carried out as carefully as possible so as not to harm the environment. The designers were faced with the task of organically integrating new objects into the landscape. Later, the resort began to be visited not only by financial and industrial tycoons, famous politicians and stars, but also by less significant individuals and ordinary people. Not so long ago, the Costa Smarald consortium bought the emir of Qatar for 600 million euros.

Popular resorts

Among the many places of rest, it is worth highlighting the most significant. The most popular resorts on the Costa Smeralda are Cala Granu, Porto Cervo, Pantochia and Porto Rotondo.

Porto Cervo in the summer becomes the second capital of Italy. The city got its name due to the fact that the shape of its harbor resembles a deer. An incredible amount of celebrities gather here. Tourists visiting Porto Cervo should definitely take a walk along the Piazzetta delle Chiakker square, which is known throughout the world. A city tour is a walk through a series of winding streets where you can see boutiques and shops of the most famous brands.

No less beautiful is the city port, which is considered the most equipped in the Mediterranean. You will not see such a number of beautiful and expensive yachts anywhere else. Their largest concentration is concentrated in Porto Cervo Marina. This is a new port, in which the most luxurious ships and light ships call. There is also the Costa Smeralda Yacht Club.

Beaches

The emerald coast stretches for many kilometers, delighting tourists with the opportunity of a good rest. There are a lot of beaches in Costa Smeralda, they attract tourists. One of the most famous places is Piccolo e grande Pevero, which is located three kilometers from Porto Cervo. The beach is covered with the finest white sand, and its coast is washed by emerald waters. The flat bottom is pleasantly pleasing here, and the approach is very smooth, thanks to which you can swim with children. Famous people usually relax on the beach. It is well equipped, in its territory there is a restaurant, bar and hotel.

Prince's Beach is considered one of the most beautiful on the Emerald Coast. The name of this place is associated with the name of Prince Aga Khan, who loved to relax here. The beach has a crescent shape. It is surrounded on all sides by cliffs and tropical vegetation. The entire coast is covered with fine sand. Tourists say that the water on the beach of Prince is very clear, which is associated with the seabed, consisting of sand and granite.

Capriccioli Beach is eight kilometers from Fox Bay. Its beauty attracts many tourists, among which there are a lot of Germans. The advantage of this stretch of coast is that it is reliably protected from the winds. And the sea here is gaining depth gradually, which is greatly appreciated by couples with kids.

Interesting excursions

Resting in Costa Smeralda, you can visit unusual excursions, thanks to which the resort will open from a completely new direction. In Sardinia, shops sell numerous cork souvenirs. If you like such trinkets, then you can see with your own eyes the process of their manufacture.

If you are a fan of Italian alcohol, you can visit the local Wine Museum. During the tour, visitors can taste the best varieties of alcoholic drinks in Sardinia.

Experienced tourists recommend visiting the ancient city of Alghero, which was built in the twelfth century. It is famous for its beautiful streets and hospitable residents. In the center of the city is the church of St. Francesco and St. Michele.

Boats depart from the port of Alghero every hour, where you can ride on the sea and see the grottoes of Neptune. In them, tourists can admire the amazing stalactites that grow sideways. In addition, the town is famous for corals, so souvenirs made from them are worth buying in local shops.

Tourists reviews

Many experienced travelers believe that the Costa Smeralda is the most beautiful in the world. No wonder it is called emerald. Amazingly clear and clear sea water shimmers with turquoise. Tourists are amazed by the surprisingly white and fine sand, which alternates with massive rocks.

The aroma of the sea and lush Mediterranean vegetation sets the guests in a romantic mood. Off the coast of the Costa Smeralda is a scattering of the smallest islets, bays and capes. The resort is interesting for archaeological sites and natural attractions.

Resting in the Costa Smeralda region, you should definitely visit interesting excursions. The resort offers amazing opportunities for tourists, because here a beach holiday can be combined with sightseeing and interesting places. Holidays in Costa Smeralda are a real holiday for travelers.
