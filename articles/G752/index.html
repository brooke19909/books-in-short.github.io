How to make an iPhone 6 phone out of paper. Master Class

Gadgets have long become one of the important aspects of the life of almost every person, they are used everywhere and always, sometimes, and not for their intended purpose. Many people are interested in how to make an iPhone 6 phone out of paper, for example, for a child or just for fun drawing friends. You can make it yourself in one of the easiest ways in literally a few minutes.





Why is it needed?

Such a do-it-yourself gadget immediately has a number of advantages:

- it can be safely given to children without fear of breakdowns, and they so often ask us to play the phone;

- such a phone, executed carefully, from afar does not differ at all from its original, which means that it can very well be used in the process of shooting videos or in photographs as a replacement for a real expensive iPhone;

- Well, of course, this is an indispensable thing for drawing friends, especially girls.

Template preparation

Before making an iPhone 6 phone out of paper, find the template that is most suitable for all parameters for it. They are now quite a large selection, the main thing here is to consider a number of important details:

- the dimensions should be fully consistent;

- on a good template there are necessarily folds that you do not have to outline yourself;

- the pattern, color and details should ideally match the original ones, it is important here to print photos on a high-quality printer.

How to make an iPhone 6

We cut out our template from paper and proceed to manipulate it.

  1. Work through all the fold lines so that the flat stencil takes on the convex shape of the future phone.
  2. To strengthen our model, add to it a dense piece of cardboard, cut to size. It can be any colored cardboard (it will not be visible), but it is even better to use more durable plastic or plywood for these purposes. Such a weighty device looks much more natural in the hand and, of course, will last longer.
  3. Correspondence with the natural thickness of the sixth model must be accurate, consider this important detail, cutting out the inside of our device, and to make the edge smooth, it can be sanded a little.
  4. We‚Äôll give a little more attention to one of the main details of the iPhone - the ‚ÄúHome‚Äù button. On the original models, it is always a little sunken, to achieve this, carefully cut the inside of our workpiece in the right place in the form of a small pit.
  5. It is most convenient to work with a clerical knife, you can purchase it in a regular store with school supplies.
  6. Place the cardboard of the desired size and thickness or a piece of plastic inside the stencil and connect them with glue.
  7. The gluing process of the valves must be carried out in stages and slowly so that there are no visible defects and all sides of the resulting paper apparatus are smooth. You can safely glue paper to the internal template, it will not be visible, and a high-quality glued stencil will not allow the picture to move and will not spoil the finished appearance. It is best to apply an extra thin layer of glue over the entire surface of the front and back covers.




Important details

Having figured out how to make a phone out of paper with your own hands, we proceed to finish it.

  1. Here you will need a piece of transparent thick film or special plastic, which is used to wrap photo albums and books. Put on it an already prepared phone sample and cut out the shape you need to cover the entire screen.
  2. Pay maximum attention to the aspect with gluing the film, the smoother and denser it lies, the more natural the finished result will be. The best option for clutching parts in this case is a transparent double-sided tape.
  3. Stretch the film well when gluing it, this will provide it with smoothness and excellent appearance of the finished device.




Having figured out how to make an iPhone 6 phone out of paper, you can attract children to this interesting and uncomplicated process, little gadget lovers will absolutely enjoy this activity.
