Philips - a vacuum cleaner for perfect cleanliness in the house

Dust control for any housewife is part of the mandatory cleaning of the apartment. There are a great many devices that help in this process. Progress does not stand still - well-known companies always offer new models and modifications. They use the latest technology in the manufacture of their products. But if you are interested in the quality of cleaning, give preference to a well-known manufacturer and its products. The Philips-9174 vacuum cleaner deserves close attention of buyers. It is designed for dry cleaning.





The unit has a classic dust bag that holds four liters. Removing it is very easy. There is a washable HEPA-13 filter and an AirSea fine filter. The device provides dust suction power up to 500 watts. During its operation, an air flow of 47 l / s is formed.

It is not for nothing that Philips products enjoy well-deserved recognition. The vacuum cleaner from this company always has additional accessories. The kit usually includes special nozzles: small, crevice, Tri-Active, turbo brush. The unit from this manufacturer is easy to move around the apartment. Its length is nine meters. In addition, there are carrying handles. They are located on top and in front of the device. The device is connected to an electric network, and then its 2200 W motor works at full capacity. Its radius of action is twelve meters.



A vacuum cleaner manufactured by the Philips company of any modification has rubber wheels that make it all-terrain, and a telescopic metal tube consisting of two parts. The weight of the appliance is usually about 6 kg. When the dust bag is full, a special indicator lights up, which is very convenient. After cleaning, the device can be turned off using the foot button, which is located on its body. There is also a power regulator. The power cord is rewound automatically.

On Philips vacuum cleaners, customer reviews are exclusively positive. Consumers are delighted with the power of the unit, the quality of cleaning, ease of use. So, after processing the carpet, one gets the feeling that he was dry cleaned. Not only dust disappears, but also the hair of pets. Thanks to convenient wheels, the vacuum cleaner can be easily moved around the apartment. At the same time, it does not scratch the floor and equally easily moves on any surface, even on parquet. According to customers, a Philips-made vacuum cleaner is ideal for cleaning. It works silently. During cleaning, even a child does not wake up with him, and pets do not express their concern. Thanks to a special HEPA filter, the device is able to capture the smallest dust. And several special nozzles allow you to extract dirt from the most inaccessible places. In addition to a disposable bag for collecting garbage, a reusable kit is supplied with the device, which also can not but rejoice.



Buyers who chose the Philips brand name a vacuum cleaner of any model from this manufacturer is called powerful and easy to use. It has maneuverability, a capacious capacity for collecting dust. When cleaning, there is no need to bend over. In general, owners of household appliances from Philips are satisfied with their purchase and recommend buying the products of this company without a doubt.
