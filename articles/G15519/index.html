Huawei Y3 II (Huawei LUA-L21): specifications and description

Phone Huawei LUA-L21, the characteristics of which will be discussed in this review, is a typical representative of the budget segment. The smartphone debuted in the spring of 2016. Today it is the simplest model in the line of mobile devices. The reason for this was the weak hardware, which we have yet to familiarize ourselves with. Who should use this model? Only undemanding users. Most often, Huawei Y3 II is purchased as a second phone.



Appearance

Before you consider the characteristics of Huawei LUA-L21, you need to find out what it looks like. Unfortunately, the design did not cause any emotions in the buyers. The simplest plastic case, which is framed by a silver frame. The surface of the back side remotely resembles metal. The dimensions are small - 134x68x9.9 mm. Everything, as for such dimensions, is impressive - 150 g. The assortment contains five color options: Gold, Roze Gold, Black, White and Blue.

The assembly is the most disturbing. There is absolutely no stiffness. The plastic is quite thin, so when pressed, you can feel how the lid bends.

Huawei LUA-L21: battery specifications and reviews

For autonomy in the Huawei Y3 II meets the lithium-polymer battery with a capacity of 2100 mAh. It is removable, so if you wish, the user can carry a spare battery with him in case the main one is discharged. Record indicators from this model should not be expected. Everything is within the budget class. For example, watching a video for four hours will use up all of its battery life. If you reduce the time of the active screen to 2.5 hours, you can expect about 18 hours of work, and during this period you can use the Internet, connecting via Wi-Fi.

The smartphone has a special program that optimizes power consumption. With its help, the operation of applications is controlled, so autonomy increases significantly. During testing, the device was able to work on a single charge for 4 hours with an active screen.



Conclusion

Having considered the characteristics of Huawei LUA-L21, we can say that this model is suitable only for novice users. Its indisputable advantages are its low price (about 6,000 rubles) and compact dimensions. Due to the size of the gadget, you can carry it in your shirt or trouser pocket, and it will also not take up much space in your purse. As for the rest, it is difficult to imagine that such a modest smartphone in all respects will appeal to most buyers. Its most significant disadvantages are weak speakers, a low camera resolution, a mediocre screen, and a poor-quality case.
