Hotel Chc Club Lyda Hotel (Crete, Heraklion)

Description : The budget tourist center Chc Club Lyda Hotel, renovated in 2012, invites you to spend time in the lap of nature, soak up the rays of the gentle sun, away from gray everyday life and noisy megacities. The hotel is recommended for a romantic weekend, youth and family vacations. It is located in the beautiful resort town of Hersonissos (Crete). Heraklion Airport is less than 15 minutes (16 km) away.





Due to its very convenient location, tourists will be able to freely visit the entertainment and cultural sites of the city: the Cretaquarium, a golf club, etc. The Chc Club Lyda Hotel 3 complex consists of 3 and 4-storey buildings decorated in a national style. The entire hotel area is planted with vegetation, flowering shrubs and trees.

For the convenience of guests, there are leisure places on the territory: an outdoor terrace, benches, several pools and sports grounds. The hotel policy prohibits smoking in the entire surrounding area - the complex is designed for non-smokers who lead a healthy lifestyle. Young children under two years old stay free of charge in existing beds. Extra beds are available at surcharge.





Rooms: All 140 rooms of the Chc Club Lyda Hotel have the following amenities: air conditioning, bathroom, TV, refrigerator, safe (paid), hairdryer, free Internet (wired and wireless). Each room has a balcony with sea or mountain views, an outdoor pool.

If desired, ironing equipment and an additional set of bed linen / towels will be delivered to the room. Available 2-level Family room with living and sleeping areas. Room service is provided daily.

Meals: Hotel Chc Club Lyda Hotel operates on the All Inclusive system (from 11 to 23). The price includes hot and cold snacks, various drinks (including alcoholic drinks), freshly baked muffin, ice cream. For a fee - premium products (elite alcohol), freshly squeezed juice. Three more bars and a restaurant are open during the day.





Beach: You can get to the public beach on a free shuttle that runs all day (1 km from the coast). The beach is sandy, with convenient access to the sea and equipped places for relaxation. On the shore there are changing cabins, a toilet and a sports equipment distribution point.

Additional information: Every day at the Chc Club Lyda Hotel hosted exciting sports competitions and competitions with the issuance of symbolic prizes. Near the hotel there are many sports and entertainment facilities: a riding school, a training golf course, a school of parachuting and bungee jumping; boat trips on a yacht, windsurfing are possible, there are cycling routes, a diving center, you can engage in tennis, helicopter tours, eco-tours are provided.





Directly in the guesthouse there are 3 external pools, a jacuzzi, a spa, a sauna, free parking, a retail chain of stores, a billiard room, a medical office, an exchange office, a left-luggage office, a night club, and gaming machines. Children's service includes two shallow pools, a seasonal game club.

Digest : First-class amenities, impeccable service and a wide range of entertainment are available at Chc Club Lyda Hotel. Reviews of tourists regarding this complex are mostly positive. Many emphasized the cleanliness of the premises, well-groomed and landscaped area, attentive staff and excellent food.

While staying at the hotel, you can fully experience the advantages of this place: easy access to the tourist facilities of the resort, the availability of shopping stores right on the guesthouse, free Internet. Whether you are lovers of outdoor activities or just looking for relaxation and entertainment after hard work, the hotel will help you to truly feel easy, relaxed and comfortable.
