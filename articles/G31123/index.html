How to sew a sling scarf with your own hands

Modern mothers love to improve and are open to everything new. Therefore, sling culture in our society is slowly, but taking root. Now I want to talk about how you can sew a sling scarf with your own hands.





A few words about sling culture

First of all, I want to say that sling culture is much older than it might seem at first glance. Using various methods of winding and pieces of fabric, even ancient women, for convenience, wore their children like that. It is worth noting that at that time there was not even a concept of prams. Today, many doctors, including pediatricians, speak about the benefits of slings in comparison with backpacks for children.

Types of slings

Today there are not many varieties of slings. The most common ones are a sling with rings and a sling scarf. It is worth noting that the second option is by its nature more universal, because it is suitable for carrying even a newborn. In addition, from this sling, you can safely make a semblance of a sling with rings. Often women, not understanding the issue, look for options on how to make a sling out of a scarf. It is simply not possible. This device got its name only due to the fact that, like a scarf, this sling is quite long and has many different coils.





Fabric selection

So, if a woman decided to figure out how to sew a sling scarf with her own hands, you need to start with the choice of fabric. It is worth noting that this is a very important point. A lot of things will depend on the choice of the mother, but the most important thing is the baby's comfortable position and his comfort while wearing. The best choice is knitwear. It stretches in moderation, you can choose both dense and lighter (depending on the time of year when it will be used). Sewing slings from coarse calico or fleece is practiced. However, these fabrics are best used for decoration or when combining several pieces of fabric.

What is important to remember

If a woman decided to sew a sling scarf with her own hands, she must remember that it would be ideal if it was sewn from a single piece of fabric. After all, the child grows and changes, and at some point the seam can be in a very inconvenient place and rub the baby's skin.

Dimensions

Also, to sew a sling scarf with your own hands, you need to determine the size. If the fabric stretches well, a width of half a meter will be enough. If it is quite strong, then it is better to take 70 cm. The length of the sling depends on the parameters of the mother herself. If you take a medium-sized woman (height 160-165 and weight about 55-60 kg), a length of 4‚Äì4.5 meters will be enough for her. If the woman is tall, you need to take a margin of width (more by 10-15 centimeters).

How to sew

Sewing a sling scarf with your own hands will not be difficult even for a woman who is not familiar with sewing. To do this, you need to cut a single piece of fabric of the required size (this will be a rectangle). If there is a desire, you can make the ends elongated, the essence of this will not change, but if tied , the scarf will look prettier. Next, you need to process the edges. You can, of course, overlay them (if there is no overlock, an ordinary zigzag on a sewing machine will do), but it is better to hem the product with a double hem. The first time the fabric is wrapped (for convenience, it can be ironed or swept), after the second turn it is sewn on a typewriter (you can use a double needle for reliable seams). That's all, the product is ready! Each woman herself can make sure that stitching a sling scarf is not at all difficult!

Decor

It is also good to decorate your sling. To do this, you can make various applications, you can provide an edge for the winter or a rain cover for the summer (they can be attached with a few buttons, which even by themselves can look like a decoration).
