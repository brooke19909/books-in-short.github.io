Location of the Meryan Hotel

The hotel is located on the sea coast, in one of the warmest regions of Turkey - Okurcalar, 30 km from major cities, Side and Alanya, and 90 km from Antalya Airport. The Meryan 5 * address is: Alara River Tourism Center 07415 Okurcalar Antalya - Turkiye. The hotel's location on the first coastline allows you to fully enjoy a beach holiday. Okurcalar, a young tourist area, has few attractions. The main hotels located in this area are Club Sidera 5 *, Kirman Club Sidera 5 *, Holiday Garden Resort 5 *, etc.

Not far from Meryan 5 * lies the busy Alanya - Antalya highway. Therefore, if you may be disturbed by the noise of passing cars, choose rooms with a balcony and windows facing the courtyard. A short distance from Meryan 5 * is the largest market in the region - Alara Grand Bazaar. All kinds of leather goods, vegetables and fruits, excellent spices and, of course, jewelry are sold here. You can get to the market on foot, but keep in mind that you will have to go about 1.6 km. Better accessible by public transport. By the way, there is a stop 100 meters from the hotel, and buses run quite often - once every 10-15 minutes. If you wish, you can also use taxi services. In addition to Alar Grand Bazaar, not far from the hotel there is another small market. It works on Fridays and offers its customers a wide range of Turkish sweets, souvenirs, tea and coffee.

Sand and Pebble Beach

A clean, well-equipped beach is located just 50 meters from the Meryan 5 Hotel (Turkey, Alanya). Its length is approximately 100 m, there are showers, sunbeds and umbrellas. The coast is very clean and well-maintained - garbage bins are everywhere. In addition, there is a children's playground on the coast, a volleyball court and a water sports station.

The beach is sand and pebble, so entering the sea can be somewhat uncomfortable. To solve this problem is simple - you need to prepare rubber slippers in advance, or walk along the special wooden walkways and paths that are available around the perimeter of the beach. It is important that, despite a sufficient number of vacationers, you can always find yourself a good place. Free sunbeds are available at any time of the day.

On the beach you can not just lie and sunbathe. For outdoor enthusiasts, we recommend doing non-motorized sports. You can ride a catamaran, canoe, learn to manage a sailing board. If desired, you can ride on a motor boat, jet ski, water skiing and even paragliding for a fee.

The cost of food and accommodation in the hotel complex Meryan

Many travel agencies offer packages with accommodation and meals in the hotel complex Meryan. Their cost can vary from 70 thousand rubles to almost 200 thousand rubles. Such a serious difference in price consists of the conditions of food and accommodation, the number of days spent at the hotel and additional services.

For example, a tour for two, including accommodation in Meryan 5 * (standard room), economy class flight, medical insurance and all-inclusive meals, without additional services and fuel surcharge can be purchased for 70-80 thousand ( for 7 days), depending on the date of the trip.

Accommodation in a family room in the conditions of increased comfort, flight by plane in a business class lounge, transfer, guide services will cost much more - 130 and more than thousand rubles. When buying a ticket, you should pay attention to whether the fuel surcharge is included in the package of services or if you have to pay it separately.

If the tour provides for accommodation in a regular room of the Meryan 5 - standard hotel (Alanya), and also includes air travel, transfer to the hotel, medical insurance and meals with accommodation, then it can be cheaper than an individual room reservation and self-purchase of tickets.
