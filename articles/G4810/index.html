Hotel Senza Hotels Inova Beach 4 * (Alanya, Turkey): description, photos, reviews

Senza Hotels Inova Beach Alanya 4 * (Alanya) - one of the most famous budget hotels in Turkey. Here every year a large number of tourists relax, who prefer decent service and comfortable living conditions. Even before the very beginning of the resort period, almost all of its rooms are booked in advance.

General information about Senza Hotels Inova Beach 4 *

The date of construction of this hotel is in 1994. At that time, a not very attractive building was built, and a swimming pool was equipped near it. After some time and a series of renovations, the last of which took place in 2013, tourists can enjoy the modern building, which is equipped with the best technical systems.

To date, the hotel occupies the TOP positions of many tourist ratings. Its area is about 12,000 square meters, which allows it to accommodate a wide variety of recreational areas. There is also a beautiful garden, which at any time of the year pleases the eye of tourists.



Location

Senza Hotels Inova Beach Hotel 4 * (Alanya) is located in the village of Konakli, almost on the shores of the Mediterranean Sea. From the central part of the city of Alanya to this place of rest is only 13 kilometers. Taking advantage of the proximity to the historical region, many tourists go to the city by regular transport in order to get acquainted with local attractions and mysteries of nature.

The nearest air traffic hub from Senza Hotels Inova Beach 4 * (Antalya Airport) is quite far away - 110 kilometers. That is why almost all travel companies include a transfer from the point of arrival without fail - this service is provided in a comfortable minibus with air conditioning - this is how its provision is described by tourists who were relaxing at the hotel.

Rooms

All apartments in Senza Hotels Inova Beach 4 * apartments are located in a modern six-story building, inside which a fresh repair has been made. There is also one small bungalow, which also has a place to stay. In total, the hotel has 155 rooms. All of them belong to the same category - "Standard".

All apartments have a private bathroom equipped with the latest technology. It has absolutely all the plumbing installed, which includes a bathroom, sink, as well as a shower. There is also a hairdryer. For every traveler who decides to stay in the rooms of Senza Hotels Inova Beach 4 * (Konakli), the hotel provides a set of bath accessories, as well as a personal bathrobe with a towel.

Each room has a modern air conditioning, which is managed by the hotel staff. If the weather conditions on the street change, tourists will not feel this, because with the help of this equipment the room always has the optimal air temperature for the body.



Room Interior

Each room has a modern and stylish interior, which is made in compliance with all safety standards and in order to create the maximum level of comfort for the guest relaxing here. The general color scheme is dominated by such colors as white, gray and rich beige. As tourists note in their reviews of Senza Hotels Inova Beach Hotel 4 *, here you can relax and spend time in complete tranquility - the atmosphere of comfort contributes to this. As for the view from the window, it opens up a beautiful picture with a seascape or blooming gardens.

The apartments have large double beds, which are replaced by single beds in some rooms. Each of them has an orthopedic mattress, as well as hypoallergenic bedding, which is replaced three times a week. If necessary, upon an additional request, one more place is installed in the room - a European folding bed. Guests can place their personal belongings in a large wardrobe made of wood. For small things, there are bedside tables. In their free time, guests can watch a TV that is connected to a satellite broadcasting system.

Guests note that in the rooms everything is arranged in such a way that nothing bothers them. They pay special attention to the presence of a minibar in the apartment, which, unlike many Turkish hotels, is provided here for free, and even with filling. If necessary, tourists can request a safe and a telephone in their apartments.



Conditions for children

Very often the guests share in their comments about the rest with information that the hotel is perfect for families, including with young children. All this is due to the fact that the hotel administration and staff for this category of guests offer a wide range of conditions designed to create amenities.

First of all, tourists turn their attention to the fact that, upon first request, a playpen-type bed for children's sleep can be installed in the room - it is great for guests under the age of 12 years. You can also rent strollers here for the movement of babies, however, payment for this service is charged hourly.

The hotel has a well-organized kids club where parents can send their child for a certain time, however, many tourists recommend using a babysitting service. They are paid, but the nanny will pay exactly as much attention to the baby as necessary.



In the open territory of Senza Hotels Inova Beach 4 * there is a beautifully arranged pool for kids, as well as a playground where they can actively and have fun. Here, every child has the opportunity to make new acquaintances, developing communication skills.

Entertainment

As guests note, being in the territory of Senza Hotels Inova Beach 4 * (Turkey), they do not have to be bored. This is primarily due to the round-the-clock work of the animation team, which is in the hotel. Tourists note that it consists of five people, and some of its representatives are fluent in Russian.

Here entertainment is held both in the morning and in the evening. In the period before lunch, various relay races are held near the water zone or on outdoor sports grounds, the main participants of which are the guests themselves. With the onset of the evening, a disco is held at the same place, which can continue throughout the night.



Nutrition

Fans of delicious food talk about the fact that in a local restaurant you can perfectly satisfy your gastronomic appetites. In particular, this applies to tourists visiting the buffet, which, according to vacationers, serves the best dishes prepared in Turkish traditions. You can visit it at any time from 10 am to 11 pm.

If desired, guests of the hotel can look into the local bars that work near the pool and in the building. According to tourists, it serves excellent taste appetizers and drinks. Special attention should be paid to designer cocktails performed by talented bartenders.

For vegetarians in institutions there are separate menus. Also, a special list of bright dishes with mouth-watering names is offered for kids.



Aquazone

The pool area in Senza Hotels Inova Beach 4 * is quite large and is 200 sq.m. Guests note that under all weather conditions, the water here is completely clean - this effect is achieved as a result of the operation of a special system. However, the heating of the filling is carried out using sunlight, as this process is not currently automated here. The pool has a large slide designed for outdoor activities for adults. She works on a specific schedule. There is also a lower slide - for kids.

Around the pool there is a sun terrace, where guests relax on wooden sun loungers, standing under huge beach umbrellas. If desired, everyone can visit the bar, which is located here, and cool off with a cocktail.



SPA and sport

All guests who prefer to monitor their health and keep their body in good shape, are invited to visit the gym, where aerobics classes are held. On the ground floor of the building there is also a room equipped for playing table tennis. Next to it is a billiard room.

The beach has an excellent volleyball court. You can also engage in water sports, but all of them are paid, as this process is possible only under the supervision of a professional instructor.

Senza Hotels Inova Beach 4 * (Alanya) has a beautiful spa area where, according to tourists, real masters of massage art and professional parmeisters work. All these people know a lot about true relaxation - this is how vacationers often respond to them. It should be noted that all services of the SPA center are paid.

Everyone can visit the traditional Turkish bath - a hammam, or a sauna. In some cases, guests can combine the process of staying in a given place with the massage procedure performed by experts on the matter. There is a zone in the SPA center where various cosmetic procedures are performed, the list of which includes such as applying masks to different parts of the body, body wraps, as well as peeling.

After completing all the spa procedures, or in between, tourists can swim in the pool located in the building. Its area is small - only 40 sq.m, but the automatic heating works in the niche, so that the water temperature is pleasant for the body at any time of the day or year.

Services

For hotel guests, a number of services are provided here, which they use very willingly. One of them is laundry in the laundry, often guests use dry cleaning and ironing services.

Attention vacationers on the ground floor of the building are presented shops with souvenirs and interesting goods, as well as a beauty salon. If desired, the hotel can rent a vehicle - a car or a bicycle. Often travelers use them to travel to local attractions and explore the surroundings.



Prices

The cost of living in a hotel, judging by the reviews of Senza Hotels Inova Beach 4 * 2017, on average, is about 55,000 rubles. The package of such a package without fail includes a two-way flight, transportation of a tourist from the airport to the hotel, as well as room rental for 7 days. This package includes accommodation in Senza Hotels Inova Beach 4 * (Alanya) according to the all-inclusive concept, which implies free meals throughout the entire stay of travelers at the hotel. And also the provision of room service is completely free.

In a word, if you plan to relax in Turkey and do not know which hotel to stay in, pay your attention to Senza Hotels Inova Beach 4 *.
