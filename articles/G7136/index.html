Hinged doll - a toy of amazing beauty

Recently, the phrase "articulated doll" is increasingly heard. In our country, these amazing toys have only recently appeared, which do not leave indifferent not only children, but also adults. Many wealthy people collect entire collections of exclusive articulated dolls, despite the fact that most of them are real works of art and are very expensive.

General information



It is believed that the articulated doll is an invention of Japanese masters. The inhabitants of this country have elevated the anime style to the cult, in which the main characters are characters with characteristic beautiful faces. So most of these toys, even without looking at their adult outfits, have an image that is characterized by a peculiar "childishness" and beauty. Today, the hinged doll is the main product of many companies producing toys in Japan, China and Korea. They are also released in the United States. Recently, some manufacturers are trying to move away from the style of "anime", creating more and more realistic products.

Jointed dolls: manufacturing technology

According to its technical solution, this toy is a figure consisting of various parts fastened together by a special cord. The hinged doll has ball joints between the elements (hinges). They repeat all the main joints of a person (neck, carpal, ulnar, shoulder, knee, hip). This provides the toy with high mobility, very reminiscent of the movements of people. When creating articulated dolls, designers pay special attention to their balancing, which allows them not only to sit, but also to stand even in the absence of a rigid skeleton.

The hinged doll, made in mass production, has dimensions from 9 to 90 cm in height. These toys have a special marking: Tiny - 9-19 cm; Small - 20-39 cm; MSD - 40-49 cm; SD - 50-69 cm; 70+ - height 70 cm and more. Distinctive criteria for such dolls: body parts are connected with special mounts (elastic bands); the toy has a head consisting of 2 or more parts with interchangeable eyes; they can have a replaceable wig, shoes and clothes; may undergo individual sculptural or cosmetic changes.

Large dolls often even move their fingers. Durable elastic bands are stretched inside the hollow body, connecting all the details of the doll. The body and face are fashioned mainly by hand, although a special shape can be used for this. To create cavities inside the parts of the toy, tubes of different sizes are used.
