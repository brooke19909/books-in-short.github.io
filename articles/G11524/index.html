Comfortable rest in inexpensive hotels of Vyborg

Until 1939, Vyborg was Finland's second largest city. It was founded in the XI century, it has many historical attractions, a center with medieval architecture, it has a trading port, it is constantly visited by many people who come on business trips, and tourists who want to relax on the shores of the Gulf of Finland.

One can only lament that such a magnificent city several decades ago is collapsing before our eyes, and instead of restoration, unique houses are demolished to give way to faceless high-rise buildings. Vyborg is located 130 km from St. Petersburg at the intersection of the sea route, railway communication and numerous routes of international importance.

The city has opened more than a hundred hotels, many of which are located in historical buildings of the city, if necessary, you can rent a hotel in Vyborg inexpensively.
