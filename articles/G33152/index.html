Don't know how to knit a sock? For beginner needlewomen, now this is not a problem!

In the cold season, the legs should always be warm. Therefore, each needlewoman prepares knitted socks for all family members in advance. And if you still do not know how to do this type of clothing with your own hands, then welcome to our master class. This article provides readers with information on how to knit a sock with knitting needles. For beginners, this material will become a lifesaver, as the instructions for the implementation of this product are given in great detail.



Preparation stage

What should be done before knitting a sock with knitting needles? For beginners in this matter, needlewomen suggest - take measurements from the foot. This is done simply. With a measuring tape, measure the leg in girth above the bone. Write down the data, you will need them during the calculation of the loops when typing.

Now you should decide on the type of yarn that you will use in your work. For the manufacture of this type of clothing, it is advisable to take a ‚Äúwarm‚Äù thread: pure wool or wool with acrylic and cotton. As for the thickness of the material for knitting, the most optimal option is the ratio of 50 grams of yarn to 225 meters. Knitting socks for children (for beginners knitters this is important information) are made from peeled yarn. Such a thread in the factory lends itself to additional processing, it does not cause skin irritation and allergic reactions. Knitting needles for work will need No. 3 (5 pieces).

Loop set

As a rule, in a fabric knitted from yarn of the specified thickness, 2 loops are placed in 1 centimeter. Therefore, look at the number that you got as a result of measuring the legs, and multiply it by two. The resulting figure indicates the number of loops required to complete the product. Type them on two knitting needles. It is important that the number of loops is divided by 4, so if necessary, add the missing number. Spread all the stitches on the four knitting needles.



Top part

So, we learn to knit a sock with knitting needles. For beginners, we tell the technology of the cuff. Knit the elastic pattern in circular rows, alternating the front and back loops 1 x 1 or 2 x 2. When the fabric reaches a height of 4-5 cm, proceed to stocking: in the front rows, all the loops are made in the front, in the wrong rows are the back . Shape the cuffs to your desired height.

Heel

The loops of the 2nd and 3rd knitting needles do not participate in the formation of this part of the product. Knit the stocking stitch loops of the 1st and 4th knitting needles in straight and reverse rows for 10-12 cm. Next, we form a heel bend. Divide this number of loops into three parts and knit according to this technology:

  • 1st row: to make facial loops of the first part and the second, with the last loop knit with the first loop of the third part. Work turn over.
  • 2nd row: knit the loops of the second part on the wrong side, with the last stitch knit with the first loop of the first part. Work turn over.
  • 3rd row: knit the front center loops (middle part), performing together the last loop from the first side part. Thus, draw a heel until only the central loops remain on the knitting needles.

Foot

We knit socks. For beginners, we explain how to perform the lower part of the product.

We turn to knitting in a circle. To do this, make the loops located on the knitting needles, then along the lateral edge, raise the number of loops that were in the third part at the stage of formation of the heel bend. Next, knit the loops of the 2nd and 3rd knitting needles and now raise the side loops (second part). There should be an initial number of loops. Spread them evenly over 4 knitting needles. Knit the product to the little finger. Next, reduce the number of loops, knitting 2 together the last loop of one knitting needle with the first loop of the next knitting needle. When 8 loops remain in operation, pull them off, tear the thread and fasten.

Knitting a sock with knitting needles for beginner needlewomen will not be difficult, because the master class was presented in great detail. We wish you to get great creative results.
