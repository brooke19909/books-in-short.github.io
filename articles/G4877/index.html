Mamedovo gorge: rocks, waterfalls and ancient dolmens

Holidays in Sochi and its environs are not only accommodation in comfortable hotels and modern entertainment for tourists for every taste, but also the opportunity to visit unique natural areas. One of them is the Mamedovo Gorge, a landmark located just 3 kilometers from the village of Lazarevskoye.

General description of the attraction



Waterfalls, mountain streams and rivulets, ancient dolmens - these are the natural objects that tourists in the Krasnodar Territory want to see first of all. All this is available in the unique natural zone of the Mamedovo Gorge. There are three hiking trails in the nature reserve, which differ in their length and complexity. During the walk you can enjoy amazing landscapes, see numerous waterfalls and dolmens, comparable in age to the Egyptian pyramids. Each object here has its own mystical story, and many stones and water cascades here are able to fulfill the most secret desires.

The Legend of Mammad



Where did the name Mamedovo Gorge come from? Lazarevskoye, like the surrounding settlements, is shrouded in many legends and folk traditions. This unique natural object is no exception. If you believe the stories of local residents, once from one village all the men went on holiday to a neighboring village. The Turkish robbers learned about this and decided to attack the village, where only children, women and old people remained. Old man Mamed came up with a trick. He ordered all the inhabitants of the village to hide in the mountains, and he remained to wait for the robbers. The bandits were very angry when they saw an empty village. They tortured Mamed for a long time and demanded to tell where the other people hid. The old man agreed and led the robbers along the edge of a steep cliff. When the bandits understood the deception, they walled up Mamed in a cave. And what happened to the bandits themselves is a story that is silent, but nobody has ever seen them alive again. Since then, the picturesque gorge has been called Mamedov.

Interesting places in Mammadov Gorge

At the entrance to the gorge, tourists pass through the so-called Stone Gate. Further, the trail passes three waterfalls with romantic names: "Happiness", "Youth", "Love". If you take a dip in each of them and make a wish related to the sphere of life, after which the waterfall is named, then everything you make will come true. After three water cascades, the Mamedovo Gorge turns into a narrow stone corridor. On a short stretch of path, the rocks literally close above the heads of those walking. After exiting the stone tunnel, the Wanderer opens to the White Hall - a large open space, the fifteen-meter walls of which are formed by natural limestone. If you go further along the trail, you can see the ancient dolmens. These are cult stone structures, the exact purpose of which is still debated by experts. An excursion to the Mamedovo Gorge (Lazarevskoye) can be combined with a tasting of mountain honey. This product, as well as other treats and souvenirs, are sold at the Walnut Glade.

Useful information for tourists



Entrance to the territory of the nature reserve is paid: 100 rubles per person. Guided tours are available only for organized groups at an additional cost. It is best to visit the Mamedovo Gorge in the dry period. After rains, the hiking trail is very wet and dirty. How enjoyable your trip will be depends to a large extent on the choice of shoes. The sole should adhere well to the surface and not slip. In one section of the gorge, the trail is laid right along the channel of a shallow mountain stream. Especially prudent travelers here prefer to change their shoes into bathing shoes, which is not a pity to soak in the whole.
