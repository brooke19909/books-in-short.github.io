Is perspective a vision or a device?

Let's see what is the angle, and what role does it play in photography. Is this a little trick of the photographer or his own outlook on things? In simple terms, the angle is the location of the photo lens in relation to the subject. And if you move the camera in space, then you can get completely different images of the same subject. Moreover, they can be so different from each other and from the original that each time they will create a completely new impression.
