Fir-tree made of fruits on a festive table. How to make a fruit tree?

Any holiday is an opportunity to realize their creative ideas, because it is very important not only to prepare delicious food, but also to decorate the room and even the table at which guests will gather. Today we will talk about how to create a fruit tree. Step-by-step instructions will allow you to follow the rules exactly and prepare a dish that will become a bright decoration of your table.





Actual not only in the New Year holidays

Someone decides that this decor is only suitable for the New Year's table. However, such a Christmas tree can complement any event, and the fruits that will be located in a certain composition will look much more interesting and tastier. By the way, such decorations were used in ancient times, when tables simply burst with the number of dishes. Today, fruit assortment in the form of various figures and compositions is a great opportunity to decorate a festive table, realize your design skills and delight guests with the beauty of decoration.

How is a Christmas tree made of fruits?





To perform carving, we need to prepare products. We will make a herringbone of a large apple, carrot, cheese, two-color grapes , kiwi, marmalade or gelatin figurines. So, the first step is to prepare the ingredients: first you need to wash them, then peel them. The apple needs to be removed the upper part, you also need to cut out all the seeds. Remember that the more carefully you prepare the apple, the more reliable the base of your Christmas tree will be.

At the next stage, we begin work on carrots. It will act as the trunk of our Christmas tree, so you need to try to give it the appropriate look. By the way, if you want your fruit tree to be tall, choose a plump and big carrot. Using large toothpicks or wooden skewers, we fix carrots on an apple base. For structural reliability, it is better to use several fastening elements.

Getting to the twigs

To create a frame for our desktop Christmas tree beauty, you need to stick the barrel well and tightly with toothpicks. To achieve maximum similarity, you need to leave the lower branches long and the upper ones short.





To perform twigs, you need to prepare fruits, namely kiwi and grapes. Kiwi should be cut into circles, while working with ripe fruits, you need to cut them off thicker - this will allow you to maintain their shape. But first, grapes need to be threaded abundantly on each toothpick, and you can combine green and red berries - thanks to this, our Christmas tree will look very voluminous of fruit. How to make it become voluminous? For this, we will use kiwi circles. Need to randomly string kiwi on toothpicks. This is a very thorough process, as the fruit can sprawl. Therefore, you should act extremely carefully. What a Christmas tree of fruit do without a star on the top? In our recipe, it will be edible and healthy, because we will make it from cheese. It is best to take hard varieties, preferably without holes. We cut a star from the top of the cheese, as well as various toys that will complement our delicious and healthy beauty. At the last stage, you need to decorate the Christmas tree with the remaining fruits and berries.

Simpler recipes

Of course, not everyone wants to string fruit and spend their time on it. There are simpler recipes that allow you to create no less beautiful edible compositions on the table. For example, you can lay out a beautiful Christmas tree in a suitable triangular shape. Grapes are suitable as the bottom layer, you can even red. Twigs will imitate long apple slices, and as a garland you can put strawberries, currants or other round berries. You can use pineapple to create a star.





Fruits from fruits and vegetables

Today it is very popular to decorate a festive table with the help of carving - the art of cutting out various shapes and forms with the help of special knives. For lovers of not only fruits, but also vegetables, you can offer to create the appropriate Christmas tree. For this we need the following products: cauliflower and broccoli, carrots, bell peppers of different colors and grapes. Using broccoli and cauliflower inflorescences, we will create an incredibly voluminous Christmas tree that will look very magnificent. Again, we use carrots as a trunk, to which we attach pieces of different cabbage. The remaining tips of skewers are decorated with grapes and berries, for example, strawberries or currants. The crown of our Christmas tree will be a star cut from the same cheese. By the way, it should correspond to the size of the tree: that is, not to be too small or too large, and therefore heavy.





Symmetry and Conciseness

How to make a fruit tree that will look neat and harmonious? To do this, you can simply alternate the layers of decoration in a certain order. For example, we create a Christmas tree traditionally from apples, carrots, slices of pumpkin and zucchini, and also decorate it with berries - grapes, strawberries, currants. We cut out the core in the apple, where we then firmly insert the peeled carrots (this will be the trunk of our design). We symmetrically place toothpicks on it, due to which we get a neat composition. The point is small - to decorate it. To do this, you can alternate layers: red currant, yellow pumpkin, green grapes and so on several times. We will get a very beautiful and harmonious Christmas tree.

As you can see, there are many opportunities for creating a fruit tree. Someone uses traditional fruits, which are always in the refrigerator, someone will prefer a more original and expensive solution. But it is worth remembering that the main thing is fantasy, and therefore, if desired, any dish can be presented in a completely new light. After all, as you know, food should be not only tasty and healthy, but also beautiful. Create, discover your creative possibilities, and you will see how easy it is to surprise your guests.
