Talea Beach Hotel 3 * (Greece / Crete) - photos, prices, description and guest reviews

One of the most popular among travelers and at the same time the largest island of the Hellenic Republic is Crete. Every year, tens of thousands of people from all over the world come here. Our compatriots are no exception, moreover, many Russians have long chosen this Greek island as a regular place for spending summer vacations. Why is Crete so attractive? The advantages of this island can be written for a very long time. In a nutshell, we can say that, in addition to the magnificent Mediterranean climate and picturesque nature, there is a wide hotel base, represented by cheap camping sites, mid-range hotels, and five-star luxury complexes. In addition, here everyone will find entertainment: whether visiting historical and architectural monuments, relaxing on luxurious beaches, hanging out in nightclubs, etc. If you plan to spend your vacation in Crete and are looking for a good mid-range hotel for these purposes , then we recommend paying attention to Talea Beach Hotel 3 *. Next, we will get to know this hotel more closely, and also find out what impressions our compatriots have already stayed here and how much they spent on Crete.





Hotel Location

This hotel complex is very conveniently located on a sandy beach off the coast of the Cretan Sea in a village with the beautiful name of Bali. This is a fishing village in the past, and in the present it is a popular holiday destination famous for its magnificent nature, represented by the green slopes of the mountains and cozy bays. The leisurely atmosphere prevailing in Bali will set you up for a comfortable and carefree vacation.

The resort town of Rethymno is 32 km from the hotel. You can get to it both by taxi and by bus, especially since the stop is only a couple of hundred meters from the hotel complex. As for the air harbor located in Heraklion, the hotel is 48 kilometers away. So after landing at the airport, the path to the hotel will take you about an hour. Time on the road will fly by unnoticed, because you will have the opportunity to admire the magnificent landscapes of Crete through the window of a bus or car.

Talea Beach Hotel 3 *: photo, description

This three-star hotel complex was erected in a style close to the traditional architecture of Crete. It was named after the Greek god Talos. Despite many references to antiquity, the hotel complex is still a completely modern building both in appearance and in the services provided to guests. The hotel has 120 comfortable rooms located in the main building and several bungalows. They are furnished with restraint, but very functional. This hotel complex is an excellent place for a measured family vacation.





Own territory of the hotel

Despite the fact that Talea Beach Hotel 3 * (Greece) has a small territory, it is very well planned and used as efficiently as possible. So, here you will find picturesque gardens, flower beds with exotic flowers, a mini-golf course, a basketball court, children's playgrounds, a figured pool with a sun terrace and much more. In addition, you can be sure that you can find a lot of places for excellent photo shoots at the hotel.

Posting Rules

As in most hotels around the world, in Talea Beach Hotel 3 * (Crete), there are internal rules regarding the time of registration of arriving and leaving guests. So, the settlement of new guests here is carried out from 14:00. However, if you arrive at the hotel earlier than this time, the administration will do everything possible so that you can enter the booked room immediately. But it should be borne in mind that at the peak of the tourist season, when the hotel complex is filled to capacity, it often happens that the previous guests still do not have time to vacate the room and the maids prepare it for check-in. In this case, you will be asked to leave your things in the locker room and take a walk around the hotel, have a bite to eat in the restaurant, refresh yourself in the pool or go straight to the long-awaited sea. As for the day of departure, you need to free your room before noon. When you hand over the keys, you should pay for the entire stay if you have not paid for your room with a travel company in advance. For payment at Talea Beach Hotel 3 * (Crete), both cash and plastic cards of the popular payment systems "Visa" and "Mastercard" are accepted .





Number of rooms

As already mentioned, the three-star Talea Beach Hotel, located in Crete (Greece), has 120 comfortable rooms. They are located in the main building and several bungalows. They are designed to accommodate two, three or four guests. The rooms are equipped with individual air conditioning, heating system, LCD TV, telephone, mini fridge and private bathroom. Each room has a balcony or terrace. We draw your attention to the fact that there are no hairdryers in the bathrooms, so take this appliance with you. The rooms are cleaned every day, linen and towels are changed twice a week. All guests of Talea Beach Hotel Rethymno 3 * (Rethymno, Crete) can order a room service.

Nutrition

Meals at the Greek three-star Talea Beach Hotel are all-inclusive. Three times a day, the hotel chefs serve a buffet in the main restaurant. In addition, guests can enjoy snacks and drinks at several bars throughout the day. It should be noted that the food in Talea Beach Hotel 3 *, reviews of tourists only confirm this fact, it is organized very well. Here everyone will select dishes for themselves in accordance with their own taste. In addition, the restaurant also has a children's menu.





Sea and beach

Like most hotels in Greece, Talea Beach does not have its own beach, despite the fact that it is located almost on the seashore. The beach here is municipal. In this regard, the use of sun loungers and parasols will have to pay. Also on a paid basis, you can use the equipment for entertainment on the water. There are also bars and restaurants on the beach where you can enjoy a variety of drinks and snacks. As for the beach itself, it is very clean and sandy. Entering the water here is gentle, without stones, which is especially convenient when resting with children.

Pools

Despite the fact that most people come to Crete mainly for a great beach holiday, there are many tourists who enjoy spending time in the pools. Hotel Talea Beach Hotel 3 * also provides such an opportunity. On its territory there is a large figured pool filled with fresh water, which is cleaned daily. Near it is a sun terrace with free sunbeds and parasols. There is also a poolside bar where tourists can enjoy a variety of snacks and drinks.





Entertainment

In addition to swimming in the pool, as well as sunbathing, the territory of the Talea Beach Hotel 3 * hotel complex has created conditions for outdoor activities. So, guests have the opportunity to play basketball, mini-golf, tennis or ping pong. You can also visit the gym, and relax in the sauna after exercise.

On the beach for an additional fee there is the opportunity to engage in a variety of water sports.
