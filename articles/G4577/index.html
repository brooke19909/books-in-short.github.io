Aslykul Lake is an unforgettable vacation. "Aslykul" - recreation center on the lake: accommodation, services

Aslykul Lake is known not only to residents of Bashkiria, but also throughout Russia. Here, both the local population and tourists often appear. Its width is 5 km, its length is 8 km. In Europe, this lake is considered one of the largest.

Those who have visited Aslykul speak of him as a great place to spend a vacation. Many begin to perceive it as the sea because of the vast expanses, beautiful landscapes and a great atmosphere. This impression is especially reinforced in windy weather, when small waves begin to crash into the coastal space. This is what allows you to spend your holidays unforgettably.

"Aslykul" - a recreation center, which is named after the lake. It will also be described in the article, because all those who want to swim and enjoy the expanses of the reservoir of the same name stop here.



Description of the lake

Since 1993, the lake has been awarded the title of National Park. However, when the streamlining of the federal and regional systems of legislation began, it became clear that Aslykul is a reservoir that cannot be a park. After 2004, it was considered a cluster site, and in 2011 it was isolated as an independent natural system.

After that, residents began to complain about the absence of at least the minimum conditions for spending holidays. They were also struck by a paid entry. Now the cleanliness of Lake Aslykul (the reservoir map is in the article) is monitored by the local administration.

Even despite the low level of pollution, many people still come here to spend their holidays. And thanks to the arrangement of the recreation center of the same name, comfortable living conditions appeared.





Nature of the lake

The history of the appearance of the reservoir is quite interesting and fascinating. Earlier, a karst failure was located on the site of the lake . Over the years, it has been filled with water, and now everyone can enjoy swimming in it.

Due to the fact that the lake does not have drains, it is filled with useful minerals. Water has a small percentage of salinity.

The plant world of Lake Aslykul (Bashkiria) is filled with rare species that are listed in the Red Book. Near the reservoir there is a small peak, on which these unusual flowers and herbs sprout.

The animal world is also known for its diversity. There is a hawk, a loon, a peregrine falcon, an Apollon, a geese and others. Among the birds live swans, pelicans and geese.

In the event that the campsite is more suitable for the vacationer, you need to consider that there are not enough places here, and you need to come only by your car. Description of the recreation center

Have you decided to come to Aslykul Lake? Where will the most complete rest be? "Aslykul" is a recreation center that welcomes tourists with pleasure. It is located 138 km from Ufa. This picturesque place is located directly on the shoreline of the lake.

On the territory of the base there are several comfortable and cozy houses where residents are resettled. For the summer season, an additional three cottages are used, which belong to the economy class.

Among the entertainment services, one can note the presence of a billiard room, a bathhouse, board games, skis, a pool. You can rent a snowmobile or go fishing. Hiking in the area is organized.

Services

The services provided by the complex will make an unforgettable vacation. "Aslykul" is a recreation center on the territory of which there is a food stall, as well as a cafe. The latter offers three meals a day. A day for a person you need to pay 450 rubles. The Internet here is paid (100 rubles).
