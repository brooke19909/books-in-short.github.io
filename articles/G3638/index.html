Hotel Morjim Resort 3 * (Goa, India): description and reviews of tourists

Hotel Morjim Beach Resort 3 * (Goa, India) offers very comfortable living conditions and a fairly high level of service at a very reasonable price.





Brief description of the resort

Hotel Morjim Beach Resort 3 * (Goa, India) is located in an incredibly beautiful place, on one of the best beaches on the north coast. In addition to natural attractions, it is also important to note the infrastructure of the hotel itself, which offers free Wi-Fi, delicious food in the restaurant and many other services.

One of the main advantages of the hotel Morjim Club Beach Resort 3 * (Goa, India) is its comfortable rooms. Here, every tourist can feel the comfort of home. It is very important that the hotel provides a full range of amenities, without which a modern traveler cannot imagine his vacation.

Resting at the Morjim Club Beach Resort 3 * hotel (Goa, India), you can visit the cleanest nearby beach or drive a few kilometers to explore other sections of the coast. Guests also have the opportunity to swim in the pool, which has a children's section. Do not forget about young tourists in the restaurant, where they are invited to use comfortable furniture.





Hotel Location

Resort Morjim Resort 3 * is located in India, in the northern part of Goa. It is located some 300 meters from the beach of Morgimi Beach, which is read by one of the cleanest and most beautiful in all of Goa. To get here from Dabolim Airport, you have to overcome 60 km.

Also, guests of the hotel Morjim Resort 3 * can visit two more beaches. One of them is located in the resort village (7 km), and the second - in the town of Baga, which is 12 km from the hotel. It is important that near the resort there are as many as two transport hubs. So, 20 km is the railway station of the village of Pernem, and a similar station in Mapusa is already 50 km away from the hotel.

Guest Apartments

Hotel Morjim Club Resort 3 * is very small and has at its disposal only 6 rooms. They are miniature one-room cottages located in two rows. Inside there is a complete set of furniture and appliances. Guests can stay in double or single beds. Each room also has its own veranda, and its windows offer ocean views.





Facilities in the rooms of the resort complex

For any hotel, it is extremely important to ensure an adequate level of comfort in the guest rooms. Speaking about the hotel Morjim Resort 3 *, it is worth noting that the following list of amenities is provided in his apartments:

  • wireless Internet, the signal of which extends not only to the entire territory of the hotel, but also to its rooms;
  • the bathroom can be equipped with either a shower or a bath;
  • the TV in the apartment can be connected to a cable or satellite package of channels;
  • each room has a split system that allows you to warm up or, conversely, cool the air temperature in your room, depending on weather conditions;
  • without fail you are provided with a set for making tea and coffee, which includes not only the drinks themselves, but also a service, as well as an electric heater for water;
  • there is access to a private balcony or terrace, if we are talking about the first floors.




Resort Infrastructure

The resort Morjim Resort 3 * for tourists provides infrastructure, which includes the following services and facilities:

  • The hotel has a reception open 24 hours a day: if you have any questions or problems, they will be immediately resolved;
  • if necessary, part of the services can be provided to you directly in the room (massage or cosmetic procedures, delivery of food or the press, and more);
  • the bar is responsible for providing tourists with drinks, cocktails and light snacks;
  • You can take a dip in the clean chlorinated water pool, which also has a separate children's section;
  • the entire hotel is covered by wireless Internet coverage;
  • if necessary, it is possible to invite a professional doctor to the hotel;
  • You can arrange in advance with the reception staff that they will call a taxi for you on the appointed day and time;
  • if you need to leave the hotel, a nanny can look after your children;
  • for children in the restaurant there is a cozy corner with special comfortable furniture;
  • You can use the free secure parking for cars and other means of transport;
  • in the restaurant you can try many dishes that represent not only Indian culinary traditions, but also cuisines of other nations of the world;
  • so that you do not have worries about stereotypes associated with theft, you can use the safe, which is located at the reception.




additional information

The hotel Morjim Grande Resort 3 * has a number of rules:

  • The checkout time at the hotel is set for 11:00, and this applies to both check-in and check-out;
  • hotel guests have the opportunity to pay for received services with bank plastic cards;
  • since the apartments are characterized by sufficiently compact dimensions, it is not possible to establish additional beds in them;
  • It is strictly forbidden to bring any animals to the hotel;
  • there are no special discounts and preferential terms regarding accommodation in hotel rooms.




Positive reviews

A miniature and very pretty hotel Morjim Resort 3 * is causing a lot of pleasant impressions. Reviews about him highlight the following positive points:

  • a nice, clean and spacious pool in the courtyard of the hotel (the smell of chlorine is practically absent);
  • hotel workers take good care of the lawn and green spaces (constantly watered and maintain a neat shape);
  • sunbeds and umbrellas on the beach can be used free of charge, provided that food or drinks are ordered in the cafes to which they belong;
  • the restaurant can make freshly squeezed juice from the fruits you bought;
  • some rooms are in good repair;
  • a lot of interesting and necessary services: rent a car, massage, call a taxi and more.

Negative reviews

Speaking of comments on the work of this hotel, it is worth noting the following points:

  • the path to the sea goes along a busy roadway, where there is absolutely no sidewalk on which it would be safe to move around;
  • since the cottages are built of bamboo, there are a lot of insects, and in some rooms you can also see huge rats;
  • reception staff are indifferent to the guests' requests and treat them rather unkindly;
  • a very modest breakfast menu, which includes only scrambled eggs, some kind of toast with butter, as well as tea or coffee;
  • the rooms are very dusty and dirty, and on bedding there is a lot of hair left over from previous guests, and cleaning is rarely and not too thorough;
  • incredibly small and well-groomed territory, on which there is even nowhere to walk;
  • due to the fact that the hotel is located in a lowland area, it is very damp and there is a huge number of mosquitoes;
  • despite the fact that the brochures indicate the presence of wireless Internet, in fact, it almost never works;
  • frequent power outages and hot water;
  • there are no closets in the rooms, nowhere to put things (there are only a couple of hangers);
  • dirty linens and towels, for the replacement of which maids require money;
  • stale food in the restaurant - there are many cases of poisoning, and the prices in it are an order of magnitude higher than in the surrounding establishments.
