What is AHML? Features of the organization

Mortgages have long been considered a sought after service because they allow you to purchase your own housing. For this purpose, an Agency has been created that allows you to arrange this type of loan. It offers programs with its own terms, according to which you can profitably and reliably arrange a mortgage. Further in the article we will tell you what AHML is and what conditions are offered to homeowners here.

The concept

So what is AHML? This is the Agency for Housing Mortgage Lending. The organization was founded in 1997. The basis for its creation was the effect of a government decree of 1996. Agik shares are state-owned.





Objectives and activities of the structure

What is AHML for citizens? The organization fulfills the following objectives:

  • Creating uniform housing mortgage standards.
  • Securing affordable loans.
  • Mortgage refinancing.
  • Creating a secondary market.
  • The formation of the securities market.

And what is AHML for the country? This organization allows you to develop mortgage products, as well as provide banks with liquidity. AHML of federal significance and regionally controlled organizations operate in the country.





With the banks, the named Agency performs work according to the following scheme:

  • It determines the conditions for granting a loan and consults with the borrower.
  • A partner bank grants a loan.
  • AHML acquires the right to request a loan from a partner banking institution.
  • The Agency receives funds for obtaining mortgage claim rights by placing bonds on the stock market.
  • After changing the lender, the borrower receives a notice that the mortgage has been transferred to the Agency, as well as new details for payment. It turns out that the loan was issued by the state.

The above scheme indicates that the parameters of the terms of the contract are more attractive than in private banks. This is due to government policies that make mortgages available. But there may be additional costs from banks.

Benefits

Many turn to AHML. Helping mortgage borrowers has the following benefits:

  • This is a government agency.
  • Since the client contacts the banking organization, which is a partner of the Agency, the latter performs the function of insurance against changes in the originally agreed conditions.
  • The agency offers different mortgage programs, as different population groups are taken into account.

Minuses

But there are some nuances of cooperation with AHML. Mortgage in the organization has its drawbacks:

  • Applications are considered for longer, which is associated with a two-stage analysis of the documentation - first, the work is carried out by the Agency, and then by the bank.
  • Offers of some banks compete with the Agency and have more favorable rates.

Borrower Requirements

Banks participating in the program operate under Agency conditions and issue loans. So, in the standards created by him, there is a procedure for issuing a mortgage, there is refinancing and support. Also there are requirements for transaction individuals.





Borrowers must meet the following conditions:

  • The presence of citizenship of the Russian Federation, registration at the place of residence or stay when applying for a loan.
  • Mortgages are provided to citizens aged 18-65.
  • Under one agreement, there should not be more than 3 borrowers.
  • The borrower confirms the income with a 2NDFL certificate or fills out the form offered by AHML.

Detailed information on loans is available on the official website of AHML. The Agency monitors compliance with standards in the country, therefore, registration of a mortgage in it is considered reliable.
