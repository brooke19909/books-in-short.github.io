We make trees out of paper with our own hands: nothing is easier!

Today, there are various ways of making trees with your own hands out of paper. The simplest of them is the usual application. But there is also the possibility of using a wire to make a more natural fake. Each of them has both advantages and disadvantages. The easiest way is the first option. But the second is much more complicated. But all the same, they can be done by everyone, regardless of skill.





Applique

The first execution in this case is a 2D model. As a basis, a cardboard sheet or Whatman paper is used. Next, we cut the trunk out of brown paper, having previously marked it. Similarly, we get a green crown. In the same order, glue them to the base. After proper design, the craft is ready. But there is a more interesting way of how to make trees with your own hands out of paper in this case. To do this, you need an empty metal can of coffee without a lid. It is filled with soil or sand for stability. The outer side of the can is glued with a strip of brown paper. The top, in turn, is covered with a green circle. Next, the crown is made in the previously given way and cut out. The only difference is that it should be two-sided, and not one-sided, as in the case of application. The crown is mounted on a pre-harvested barrel and is fixed with adhesive tape. For more naturalness, 2 or 3 parts can be made. In order for them to naturally intersect in the center, they are cut in half. Moreover, one of them is cut in the lower part, and the rest in the upper.





Christmas tree

In this case, the same metal can from coffee acts as the basis. It is filled with soil or sand. Next, there are two options for how to make trees with your own hands out of paper in this case. In one of them, it is glued with brown paper on the outside. In the center of the can is set the trunk of the tree. Green leaves are put on it in order of decreasing area. Moreover, the shape of such a green beauty can be any. After all the paper leaves are put on, you need to properly decorate it, and the New Year's masterpiece is ready. In the second case, you need to make a cone out of green cardboard. He must be firmly worn on the jar - this will provide the necessary level of stability for future crafts. Next, jewelry is put on the cone. The Christmas tree is ready. This is one of the easiest ways to get wood from colored paper.

Tree of happiness

This craft has the highest level of difficulty. To create it you need to have great perseverance and patience. First, take a flower pot and fill it with soil or sand. From above all this is covered with a layer of shells or grains of coffee. Next, in its central part, a branch with a length of 20-30 cm is installed. In the next step, we will need a foam ball. It is necessary to make a hole in it so that it easily rests on a branch-trunk. If necessary, it is fixed with adhesive tape. Then the surface of the ball is covered tightly with paper cuttings. After the glue has dried, the craft is ready. Such a tree of paper with your own hands is the most difficult to make. In this case, a standard set of materials and tools can not do.





Summary

This article describes various ways in which paper-made trees can be made with their own hands. They differ in the level of complexity and the list of necessary materials. Everyone can find the one that suits him best.
