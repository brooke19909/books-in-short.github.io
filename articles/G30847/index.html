The story against liberal myths

Zhukov Yuri Nikolaevich is a historian whose books are of interest not only to ordinary citizens, but also have become material for studying the history of the Soviet period in higher educational institutions.

The chief researcher of the Russian Academy of Sciences and doctor of historical sciences graduated from the Institute of History and Archives and at first worked as a journalist. After defending his doctoral dissertation in 1976, he devoted himself to studying the problems of Russian history in the first half of the 20th century. He worked in the archives for more than 20 years and created unique works, because it was he who managed to shed light on many controversial issues of a rather difficult period and no less interesting personality of V. Series "Stalinist"

A special place in the historical works of the doctor of historical sciences is occupied by the personality of the great leader - V. I. Stalin. This man firmly entered the history of the XX century, which caused the emergence of many different theories and rumors in all levels of power. The number of books and monographs devoted to Stalin is enormous, but, oddly enough, they give him a negative characterization and show him as a cruel and unprincipled leader.

Zhukov Yuri Nikolaevich - a scientist and publicist who managed to objectively approach the coverage of the personality of a symbol of an entire era. He characterizes him as a ruler, who took into account the national interests of the new country and developed a whole course of its development. The political confrontation in the state apparatus between the ruling regime and the opposition has become the main object of research by the historian.

Jewish question

The burning topic of Jewry also did not pass over the scientific activity of the author. Soviet and Russian historian Zhukov Yuri Nikolaevich speaks of Jews in the light of the struggle for power that took place after the death of Lenin. He claims that the Stalinist repressions stopped at a time when there were no more Jews left in the NKVD. L. Trotsky and his close associates were the main rivals of Stalin. Yuri Nikolayevich Zhukov unequivocally claims that the coming to power of the Jewish proteges of Rothschild would lead to the destruction of the Soviet Union in its infancy, and history itself would take a completely different path.

As we see, Zhukov Yu.N. - The personality is ambiguous. Most Russians do not support his views on the historical events of the Great Terror. But his opinion has a right to exist.
