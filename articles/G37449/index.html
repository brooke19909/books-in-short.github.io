Helya Beach & SPA 3 * (Tunisia, Monastir): hotel description, services, reviews

Tunisia is a country that organically combines an incomparable oriental atmosphere and high-class service. And of course, there are lots of historical sights, picturesque beaches and entertainment for every taste. This is what made the country so popular - every year it receives many tourists from all over the world.

Surely every traveler knows that it is Monastir that is considered one of the most popular resorts in Tunisia, because there is everything for a truly comfortable and eventful vacation. And tourists have no problems with hotels, because there are a lot of them. And quite often when planning a vacation, people look at the large hotel complex Helya Beach Spa 3 *, which they have already managed to prove themselves on the good side. So what actually constitutes a hotel, what conditions and degree of comfort does it offer? What do the former guests say about him?

Where will the tourist live? Location





Location, as you know, can be both an advantage of the hotel and its disadvantage. So where is the Helya Beach Spa 3 * resort? Skanes is a beautiful tourist village located just 1 km from the lively Monastir. And it is here that many tourists prefer to stay.

Immediately it is worth saying that this is a calm area - most of it is occupied by hotels, cafes and shops. The distance to the city center is only 8 km, which can be overcome in a few minutes by public bus, taxi or even a comfortable train. The journey from the local airport also takes no more than 20 minutes, which is very convenient, because after the flight you would rather want to get to a comfortable room and relax. The distance to the equally famous city called Enfida is 63 km, but Tunisia is located 170 km.

In any case, tourists consider the location profitable, because you can enjoy the silence, while not feeling divorced from civilization.

Hotel Helya Beach Spa 3 * (Monastir): description of the territory

In their reviews, travelers say that the hotel complex pleasantly impresses with its well-groomed, cleanliness and original architecture. The hotel itself consists of one large five-story building with snow-white walls, built in a rather original style. Elevators work inside, and the interior itself creates a feeling of space and luxury.

The hotel area is very large - there are terraces, sports grounds, swimming pools, and even your own garden. Of course, everything here is decorated with exotic flowers, lush bushes and trees, in the shade of which it is so pleasant to relax. By the way, Helya Beach Spa 3 * hotel was built in 1994. In 2013, a complete reconstruction was carried out throughout the territory, so today guests can enjoy modern design and equipment.

What do the numbers look like? Accommodations





Helya Beach Spa Hotel 3 * is a large complex, which consists of 335 rooms. There are standard rooms, and more comfortable suites, which offer wonderful sea views, as well as spacious family apartments, where a family with children can easily live.

Reviews say that the rooms here are bright and spacious, and the decor is discreet, but pleasant. Furniture has recently been completely changed - in addition to beds and wardrobes, there are comfortable work desks and easy chairs. Of course, in every room there is a basic set of household appliances, without which comfortable living is hard to imagine. You can cool the room with air conditioning, watch your favorite TV shows on TV, use the phone and a small safe.

Of course, at your disposal will be an individual bathroom and toilet. Most rooms have a spacious shower. You can also use a hair dryer, branded shampoo and soap (stocks are replenished regularly), as well as clean towels that are delivered to the room every day. They try to keep the rooms clean - they clean here very carefully.

Meals: is it possible to eat at the hotel?





When planning a vacation, you should definitely consider the food, because on an empty stomach it is difficult to enjoy the beauty of nature. What does the hotel complex Helya Beach Spa 3 * offer its guest? Tunisia is a country in which the all-inclusive meal plan is popular - this is exactly what tourists get when they pay for accommodation in this hotel.

The hotel restaurant invites all guests three times a day. The room here is spacious and bright, there is enough space for everyone, even with maximum occupancy. Meals are held in the form of a buffet already familiar to tourists, and the menu is pretty good. In their reviews, former guests note that every day you can count on an abundance of fresh fruits and light snacks, as well as a variety of meat and fish dishes. Quite often the restaurant's menu and seafood.

In the hotel cafe, guests are offered aromatic tea and fresh coffee, as well as homemade cakes, ice cream and delicious desserts. There is a bar by the pool - polite bartenders will offer you soft drinks, cocktails and even strong drinks, however, only locally produced.

Fun and amenities on the beach





Wide beaches, soft sand - this is what Tunisia is famous for. Helya Beach Spa 3 * hotel is built almost on the seashore. The beach is not more than 100 meters away. This is a private stretch of the coast, so it is the hotel guests who rest here. Of course, you will have umbrellas and sun loungers at your disposal, which are arranged in straight, neat rows on the shore (they can be used for free). A beach towel is available at the hotel.

The reviews confirm that the coast here is very beautiful, the entrance to the sea is convenient, there is neither algae, nor garbage, as they are regularly cleaned, and the depth increases gradually. Both adult travelers and kids have a great time here.

There is a volleyball court, and no one is bored here. Indeed, in the center of sports recreation at any time of the day you can rent a catamaran or other non-motorized transport. Water skiing, banana, parachute is a favorite attraction for most tourists. And in the winter months light waves rise here, which attracts fans of windsurfing to the coast. As you can see, entertainment on the beach offers for every taste.

Should I count on additional amenities on site?





What living conditions should be hoped for at the Helya Beach Spa 3 *? Tunisia is a country famous for its high quality service and this hotel is no exception to the rule. Guests can count on all the necessary amenities. In particular, luggage storage is available here, and for a small fee you can rent a safe deposit box from the administrator. Here, without any problems, you can exchange money for local currency. Guests often use the services of dry cleaning, give clothes for washing and ironing, in order to save time and fully enjoy your vacation. No problem, you can rent a car, bike or moped - many travelers explore the island in this way. Of course, you can use the hotel parking for free.

For tourists, a business center is equipped where you can use office equipment. There is also a spacious conference room, where various celebrations and business events are often held.

What does the leisure of the guests look like?





If you want to spend time in the hotel Helya Beach Spa 3 * you can have a lot of fun. Guests often gather by the large pool, where they bathe, play water polo, relax on comfortable sun loungers and just chat over a glass of cool, delicious cocktail.

As the name indicates, the hotel provides SPA center services - this includes many types of massage, peeling, and various wellness treatments. It also has its own sauna and oriental bath, as well as a solarium. You can have a good time playing mini golf, tennis and ping pong. By the way, next to the building there is also a large golf course. And tourists who are taking care of their physical condition are invited to the fitness center.

Of course, entertainment can be found outside the walls of the Helya Beach Spa 3 * hotel. City tours can be purchased here, because Monastir, and the whole country, is an ideal place for traveling and diving into oriental culture.

Children's rest: what conditions does the hotel offer?

Helya Beach Spa 3 * hotel often becomes the venue for a family vacation, because some additional conditions for children are also available here. Tourists can choose a comfortable, spacious room in which the whole family can easily fit, and they can deliver a bed to the room upon request. The choice of dishes in the restaurant is large, the menu contains many products that are suitable for children - there are no problems with nutrition. And their services are offered by professional nannies with whom it is not scary to leave a child.

As for entertainment, the guys have fun on the beach. In the hotel itself a spacious playground with swings, slides and interesting attractions is equipped for them - here you can play, play sports and just meet new friends. In the city of entertainment for children there is much more - there are game centers, water parks, and even a large zoo, where you should definitely take the child.

Helya Beach Spa Hotel 3 *: reviews and opinions of tourists





Official information will help, of course, make up an opinion about the hotel, but only after reading the opinions of former guests can you get an accurate picture. What do tourists think about the Helya Beach Spa 3 * hotel? Travelers leave positive reviews.
