What do they bring from the UAE as a gift to themselves and their loved ones?

As you know, the UAE is an exotic Arab country for tourists who value comfort and have a high level of income. In addition, this is a great place for shopping, because here you can buy both various national goods and quality items from other countries. Let's see what they bring from the UAE?



Products

The most popular purchase among all tourists in the Emirates, of course, are oriental sweets. Delicate nougat, Turkish delight melting in your mouth, sweet sorbet and magic baklava have nothing to do with what you can buy in domestic stores. You will be surprised to learn that even dates are brought from the UAE ! Arabs have a special respect for this product. For example, try it yourself and give friends sweets or date jam. A great gift will also be a box of tea or coffee. Here you can buy real aromatic coffee, which is not easy to find in Russia. A wide selection of varieties and excellent quality will surely please you. Interestingly, even exotic fruits can be brought from the UAE . For example, mangosteen. This amazing fruit looks like garlic, very juicy and sweet in taste. And, of course, chic sets of oriental spices can be an excellent memory of your stay in the United Arab Emirates .





clothing

It is impossible to imagine the Emirates without buying a fur coat. The most interesting thing is that fashionable brand models are brought from the UAE at affordable prices. The main thing is not to buy a fake. However, the laws of this country are very severe for such acts. In general, there are quite strict rules. For example, tourists are forbidden to wear national clothing. However, fortunately, no one forbids her to buy and export abroad.

Perfumery

Cosmetics and perfumes are also considered the hallmark of the UAE. Pay attention to unique oriental perfumes, not inferior in quality to French. The only problem is that they erode very quickly. Therefore, it is better to buy them before leaving home. If you plan to bring perfume home, be sure to visit the largest duty-free store in Dubai.

Electronics

Of course, you heard that they even bring electronics from the UAE. New models of iPads or iPhones can be purchased at very competitive prices. In addition, Apple products put up for sale their new products much earlier than in Russia.





Souvenirs and jewelry

Among the souvenir products from the UAE, dishes for making oriental coffee, various bottles with sand, original figurines of camels made of gold, clay or ceramics are in demand. You can also buy incense or a national scarf - arafatka. If you want to buy jewelry made of silver or gold, it is better to do this through a travel guide that will help you not to fall into the hands of scammers.
