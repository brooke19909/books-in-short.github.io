How to get to Anapa by train?

Departure from the railway stations of Moscow and St. Petersburg





By rail from Moscow to Anapa, the distance is 1600-1900 kilometers. This significant variation is due to the fact that there are several existing routes. Of course, all vacationers are concerned about how much to travel by train from Moscow to Anapa. The minimum travel time is 1 day and 4 hours. Try to figure out what to do on a long journey in advance. A lot of vacationers come to the Krasnodar Territory from the Northern capital. From St. Petersburg to Anapa, the distance by rail is about 2500 km. Accordingly, the travel time will be about 2 days, the minimum trip duration is 1 day 20 hours. The train overcomes the Anapa-Petersburg route in such a time, taking into account the short parking at the stations. This direction serves a large number of modern comfortable cars with air conditioning, dry closets and convenient seats for passengers.

Trains from Nizhny Novgorod to Anapa





Not all cities in our country send trains to Anapa. In the summer season, residents of Nizhny Novgorod can go to the Black Sea on the passing train "Kirovsk-Anapa". Approximate travel time is about 2 days. Trains run from Nizhny Novgorod to Novorossiysk. You can also purchase tickets for the passing Vorkuta-Adler train, which makes stops in Nizhny Novgorod and Novorossiysk.

As you can see, getting to Anapa by train is not at all difficult. From Novorossiysk to this city there are intercity buses and minibuses. Travel time will be about 1 hour.

Railway platforms closest to Anapa



There is no railway station in the resort town. It is believed that the station was specially moved outside Anapa for the sake of calm tourists. The nearest platform is in the village of Upper Dzhemete. Trains from 250 cities of our country come here. If for some reason you cannot get tickets for the required date, try to book a seat on the train to the Tunnel railway station. It is still 35 kilometers from Anapa, but there is regular public transport, you can take a taxi. Do not be afraid of possible difficulties - a huge number of tourists travel this route annually. There will be no problems with luggage - there are always movers at the station, and all types of transport that goes to Anapa are equipped with luggage compartments. If it seems to you that the train to Anapa takes a long time, try to consider other options. You can also go on vacation by bus or plane. And some vacationers even prefer to go on vacation in their own car.

Useful tips for tourists

Passenger trains consist of two types of cars: reserved seats and coupes. In the first case, on the way, all passengers are located in a common space without additional partitions. The compartment car is more comfortable, it is divided into "rooms", each of which accommodates 4 people. Of course, tickets of the second type are much more expensive. But it is incredibly convenient. For example, if you go on vacation with your whole family or company, you can purchase tickets in one compartment and enjoy the company of your closest people all the way.
