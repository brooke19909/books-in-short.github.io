How to transfer contacts from Samsung to iPhone: tips and tricks

Some may find that transferring data from Android to iPhone is a very difficult task. It's no secret that the iPhone works with the iOS operating system. It does not recognize many Android formats. However, if you act correctly, the task will not cause any trouble.

Android and Google

The first step is to synchronize the Android operating system with Google. Make it easier than it sounds. Even a novice user will be able to cope with this task. Without synchronization, transferring contacts from Android to iPhone is problematic.



It is proposed to act as follows:

  1. Turn on your Samsung phone.
  2. Open the function menu. Select the "Settings" item in it.
  3. Go to the Accounts section.
  4. Select the line with the inscription Google in the window that appears.
  5. Enter data from your Google account. This is the email address and password for logging in. If synchronization is already established, just specify the appropriate profile.
  6. Click on the "Synchronize Contacts" button.
  7. Wait a while.

Done! After the procedure is completed, the contacts from the Samsung phone (Android) will be synchronized with the Google account. Typically, the duration of the operation depends on the size of the phone book of the device.

No sync

But that is not all! You can transfer data from a Samsung mobile phone (Android) in another way. It is offered by Apple. The solution was invented in 2015. Apple introduced users to a program called Move to iOS. This application is designed to quickly switch to "apple" devices from Android operating systems.





How to transfer contacts from Samsung to iPhone? The action algorithm in this case will look something like this:

  1. Download the Move to iOS program on a mobile phone with the Android operating system.
  2. Connect iPhone and Samsung to Wi-Fi.
  3. Go on an iPhone to the "Settings" - "Programs and Data."
  4. Open Move to iOS on Android. Click on the "Continue" button and accept the warning.
  5. Enter a special data transfer code on your Samsung mobile phone. It will appear on the iPhone screen after the previous steps.
  6. Click on "Next".
  7. The device will sync. After that, the smartphone owner will be asked to select the data to transfer. It is necessary to put a mark in front of the line "Contacts".
