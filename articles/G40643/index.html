Limitations

Before transferring money to Kyivstar, we will find out the restrictions that the operator imposes on this service. And the restrictions in this case are as follows:

  • The maximum number of such transfers for one number is 3.
  • The maximum transfer amount is 3000 hryvnias.

  • The minimum transfer amount is 1 hryvnia.
  • You can only transfer the whole number of hryvnias.
