How to sew a slanting inlay. Do-it-yourself slanting beiks. Neck treatment with an oblique trim

Clothing appeared in people for a long time. And to this day, many scientists, historians, archaeologists have not decided when the first time people began to put on something. Every year, the requirements for clothes only increased. For a modern person, it is already important not only to cover your body, but also to have quality things.

Professionals have created new technologies for processing fabrics, seams, sections. They also invented slanting inels. This is a very convenient way to process any slices. The finish is neat, even, and sometimes interesting. The same option allows you to make an attractive trim on any clothing.



Neck treatment with an oblique trim

If you do not need a tight or deaf collar on the dress, then you can beautifully cut the neckline. For this, oblique bakes are suitable. Make them easy. You need to take the fabric and draw segments with a width of 4 to 5 cm. It is important to place them not along the shared thread, but at an angle of 45 degrees. The length of such an edging is calculated as follows: cut length plus 2 cm for the seam. The fabric for processing can be selected according to the color of the dress, and you can contrast or satin. This will give the clothes an elegant and original look.

Slice processing

How to make a slanting inlay was discussed above. And now we‚Äôll figure out how to sew it correctly. There are several ways. For the first, the cooked strip must first be folded in half (inside with the wrong side) and ironed. To make it easier to work on and grind it more carefully, it is necessary to bend the fabric again by 0.5 mm from the cut and slightly iron. This will be the outline-line along which you need to sew everything. Then attach the prepared inlay from the front of the dress to the cut, and then take small stitches along the ironed line. The remaining ends on the collar are then swept away and stitched by car. Excess tissue needs to be cut off. Now bend the tape itself to the back of the cut and sweep it with small stitches. It is important to do this closer to the fold of the finish and so that the seam on the front side is right in the wrong side. For beginners, we will give advice: you can make a seam directly on the collar, but at a distance of no more than 1 mm from the edge. If the mark is satisfied, then you need to iron it again, and then grind it on a typewriter.



Another way

This option is slightly different from the one described above. But a similar treatment of the neckline with an oblique trim is quite acceptable. First of all, oblique beques are cut out from any fabric, their width should be from 2 to 3 cm. The fabric is not located along transverse threads, but at an angle of 45 degrees. This allows for a neat finish. Next, you need to bend both edges by five millimeters and lightly iron. The length of the fabric strip should be equal to the length of the cut and plus 2 cm per seam.



Special foot treatment

The cut oblique inlay with your own hands is sewn very quickly. This work will take just a couple of minutes. Attach the prepared strip of fabric to the cut on the front of the product and sweep it with small stitches. If necessary, grind sections at the edges of the inlay, and cut off excess tissue.

In the clothing industry , new technologies are being developed. This allows not only to facilitate the work process, but also to improve the quality of products. So there was a device for a slanting inlay. It is installed instead of the foot. With it, the fabric is sewn faster, during work creases or pinches on the fabric will not form. A similar treatment of the neck with an oblique trim will be easy and quick.

After the grinding is swept, you need to grind it. The seam is desirable to lead along the intended line. Then remove the extra threads, and throw the tape onto the wrong side of the cut. The second bent edge should be noticed again and try to make both edges of the inlay coincide in one seam. Experienced craftswomen hide the seam directly in the fold of the inlay on the front side of the product, and novice tailors can do this on the inlay. The distance from the edge should be no more than 1 mm. Then you need to grind. It is advisable that the machine has a foot for a slanting inlay. It remains only to iron, although the method of processing the neck or other cuts depends on the type of fabric.



Slice processing on knitwear

The knit fabric itself is very complex. To sew a product from it, you will need special equipment. If a slanting inlay is used to process the neck, a special foot is simply necessary. Cut a strip of length from the fabric as well as the cut to be cut plus 2 cm.The strip width should be from 1.5 to 2 cm.If the knitwear is tight, then it is advisable to cut one cut, which will be on the wrong side of the product, on an overlock or zigzag seam. Iron the other edge slightly. Now you will need to sweep the tape to the cut.

After the work done, check whether everything turned out as it should, and then you can proceed with the stitching by car. When working with knitwear, it must be borne in mind that the fabric itself is very elastic, so it‚Äôs better to adjust the stitch a little zigzag. Strongly stretching and stretching such a fabric is also not necessary, otherwise the product will lose its shape. Then remove the extra threads, and throw the tape on the wrong side. The swept section is no longer required to bend. You just need to carefully grip it, and preferably right in the seam. To grind more evenly, it is better to iron first and only then grind on a typewriter.



Neckline trim

You can make slanting inels from satin. The outfit will immediately become solemn and refined. As an option, use ready-made tapes, which you will not be able to purchase today. Only when buying, you need to pay attention to the fact that the product was not from shared threads. Slanting satin ribbons are always available. They will lie flat on the cut. Processing such an inlay is very easy.

Satin Ribbon Processing

To trim a cut with a satin slanting inlay, it is better to buy it about 2 cm wide. You can sew in two ways: with an open end and with a closed one. The edges of such a tape do not crumble, so the processing method can be chosen as you wish. In order to trim the products with an open edge, you do not even need to iron. The tape can be swept immediately at a distance of about 1 mm. Then you need to grind it and go around the cut itself. The second edge of the tape is also not required to be bent, it can immediately be swept to the product. In this case, the second seam should completely coincide with the first. If the level of skill in working with the sewing machine is not high enough, then you can make the second seam hidden or use another processing method.



A simplified version of processing a cut of a satin ribbon

You can trim the cut with a satin ribbon very quickly. Just need to make a little effort and do everything carefully. To begin, fold the tape in half with the front side to the very top and iron. And then attach one edge of the tape with the wrong side to the front of the product and sweep. Stitching is not necessary yet. In the same way, grip the second edge of the tape to the wrong side of the product. Sweep again. This is done so that there are no two lines on the finish. For those who can not make a double line, this option is ideal. Then it remains only to sew on a typewriter at a distance of 1 mm from the edge of the tape. At the end of the work, you need to iron everything.



The use of slanting pegs in the design of clothes

As soon as fashion designers and technologists decorate clothes! Take the same oblique bakes. They can not only process the neck or other sections, but also draw the bottom of the dress or suit, emphasize the line of shuttlecocks or the entire silhouette. The processing technology is the same as described above. The inlay can be from the same fabric as the dress or blouse, but you can make them contrasting.

With the help of oblique inlets, you can not only process all sections on the product, but also use them as elements of dressing. Any skilled worker will be able to complete this work. The main thing is that there is a sewing machine, needle, scissors, fabric at hand. This treatment will refresh any dress. It is only necessary to properly cut the slanting inlay and properly grind it. When cutting, it should be borne in mind that the strip is not cut along the transverse or longitudinal threads, but along an oblique line, that is, at an angle of 45 degrees. All the work can be done in different ways, the most important thing is to first decide which is best for this product. It is necessary to take into account the fabric from which the dress is sewn, and then in the end you can get a wonderful new outfit.
