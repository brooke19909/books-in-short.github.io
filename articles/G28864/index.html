What are the best hotels in Egypt for families with children?

If you decide to go with your child to rest in Egypt - you are on the right track. A huge plus is that in this country, children have almost no side effects during acclimatization. The best Egypt hotels for families with children will be listed below, but they all have exceptional service. Clean beaches, a shallow sea, a lot of exciting entertainment - all this will bring only positive emotions to young travelers. And even the most demanding parents will enjoy their holidays in Egypt. Hotels designed for the whole family have special children's pools, mini-clubs, animators and teachers work here. Let's dwell on each of them.

The best deluxe hotels in Egypt for families with children

Four seasons resort

The hotel is 15 km from Sharm El Sheikh and just 7.5 km from the international airport. In each room you will find a bathrobe and toiletries. The restaurant provides a special menu for babies; there are also high chairs and bottle holders. The smallest travelers will be delighted to stay in a mini club, where teachers look after them. Games, entertainment, contests will not leave anyone indifferent. For older children, entertainment and sporting events are held. Leaving the children as teachers, parents can enjoy free time and visit, for example, a spa.

Baron resort deluxe



This hotel is 15 km from Sharm El Sheikh and only 4 km from the airport. A wonderful place, which is located on a beautiful coral reef. Developed infrastructure, comfortable rooms, excellent food, the provision of various services for children are not much different from those described in the previous hotel. It is worth adding that a crib will be delivered to the room upon your request, and the water in the pool for babies is heated even in winter. If you plan to leave the child on a nanny, then this service will be paid here.

The best hotels of Egypt for families with children of the "Standard" class

Dana beach



This is a wonderful complex, which consists of several small buildings and the main building. The hotel welcomes guests all year round. For children there are 3 departments in outdoor pools (2 of them are heated in winter), chairs in the restaurant, playground, fast food menu. A cot will be in the room upon request.

Caribbean world soma bay



This is an excellent complex in which all kinds of services are provided for children: a swimming pool, a buffet in a restaurant, a dance, stage, football academy, a mini-disco, a sleeping room and much more.

Here you can see photos of hotels. Egypt (Hurghada, Sharm El Sheikh, Soma Bay) has a lot of complexes of this class, among which are also worth noting Albatros Palace, JazBelvedere and IberotelAquamarine.

The best hotels of Egypt for families with children of economy class

These include Ali Baba, Jasmin, Iberotel Makadi Oasis, Albatros Jungle, Montillion Grand Horizon. In each of them, little travelers will be provided with everything they need: excellent baby food, babysitting services, playgrounds, and swimming pools. The warm sea, excellent animation, developed infrastructure will give a wonderful vacation to both children and parents.
