"Indigo", a resort complex 2 * (Anapa): description, photos and reviews of tourists

Most people are very fond of spending their holidays at sea. This is a great opportunity to bask in the sun, and plenty of fun, and breathe in the fresh healing air, which is so lacking in large and even small cities. And if there are also mountains near the sea, then in such a resort you will definitely want to stay for a much longer time.

One of such places is the village of Sukko, located in the Krasnodar Territory on the Black Sea. Here you can relax and enjoy all the natural beauties of the region. A good option for living in Sukko will be "Indigo" - a resort complex 2 *, a description of which readers will find in this article.

Cozy bay with pebble beach

Before you talk about where you can stay for a vacation in Sukko, it is worth talking about this coastal village itself. It is located just 14 kilometers from the resort city of Anapa and attracts vacationers with unique natural beauties.





The beach of a small Sukko bay is covered with pebbles, respectively, the bottom in the sea near the coast is also rocky. Due to this, the water here is much more transparent and cleaner than in neighboring Anapa or Vityazevo. On both sides of the bay is fenced by mountains, which creates a wonderful climate here, pleasing visitors and residents of the village with dry and warm weather.

The mountains surrounding the village are covered with all kinds of deciduous trees, as well as junipers and pines. It is the last two types of trees that fill the air in these places with magnificent aromas throughout the summer that enhance the already healing sea air. So Sukko is a great place not only to relax, but also to improve health.

Where do tourists stay in Sukko

There are no shortages of all kinds of living quarters in Sukko: all tourists who come here will never be left without a roof over their heads.

Firstly, of course, there are a lot of small guest houses. They are located both near the sea and at a decent distance from it. There are hotels here, some of which are built directly on the slopes of the mountains, such as, for example, Indigo (2 * resort complex).

Also in Sukko there is a considerable number of children's camps, so most young tourists live in their territories.

Rest in the Indigo complex

In each resort town you can find both ordinary hotels and very interesting hotels that have, for example, an unusual location. Similar specimens are also found in the village of Sukko (Russia, Anapa).





Hotel "Indigo" is a 2 * resort complex, which is just one of such rather non-trivial places here. After all, it is located on the slope of one of the picturesque mountains that surround Sukko.

On the closed and guarded territory of the hotel, tourists are waiting for cute bright two-story cottages, in which guest rooms are located. There are also swimming pools, a private sauna and a dining room.

The Indigo Hotel (2 * resort complex) does not have its own beach, all guests can safely swim on the public beach of the village. A walk to it from the hotel takes about 15 minutes. However, tourists who choose this hotel to stay do not need to go there each time. How to get to the hotel

To be at the Indigo Hotel, the easiest way is to take a train or fly to Anapa. Already from here you can take a minibus or regular bus, which go towards the village of Sukko. It will be necessary to exit at a stop called "Electron".





You can book a meeting directly at the airport or railway station, because Indigo is a 2 * resort complex (Anapa), which provides its own transfer for an additional fee.

You can also come here by your own car, because for guests using private vehicles a special parking is provided. In this case, you need to know the exact address where Indigo is located in the Krasnodar Territory (resort complex 2 *): Anapa, Sukko, 33 Cooperative Street.

Mountainside Rooms

The nice yellow cottages of the Indigo Hotel can comfortably accommodate guests who have arrived in Succo, both large and small companies. Also, guests with different incomes can be provided with housing, because among the available rooms there are very inexpensive options.

The latter, for example, include double rooms with no private bathroom. For such rooms, amenities are provided on the floor, while two available bathrooms are designed for three rooms. In the room itself there is a TV, a wardrobe, a double and two single beds, a safe, air conditioning and additional furniture in the form of a table and chairs.





Hotel "Indigo" - a resort complex 2 *, in which, of course, there are the usual double rooms. They have a private bathroom with shower, all the necessary furniture, TV, fridge, safe, air conditioning and even a minibar. The rooms can be provided as two single beds, and one double.

The triple room has the same set of amenities as the standard double room. However, these rooms are a bit larger in size and are equipped with three single beds or a double and a single.

For large companies, vacationers are perfect for two-room four-bed rooms, some of which, in addition to all the above amenities, even have their own fireplaces. These rooms can have both single and double beds.

How much to pay vacationers

"Indigo" is a 2 * resort complex in the village of Sukko, the price of accommodation in which is quite affordable for any tourists.





The summer season at the hotel is divided into several intervals, the prices between which are slightly different. The cheapest period lasts from June 1 to June 15. A double room with conveniences on the floor costs 1100 rubles, an ordinary double - 1300 rubles, a triple - 1800 rubles, a quadruple - 2300 rubles. In the period from June 16 to 30, the numbers are 1400 rubles., 1700 rubles., 2500 rubles. and 3000 rubles. respectively.

Between July 1 and August 25, a double room with conveniences on the floor will cost guests 1800 rubles, a regular double room is 2300 rubles, a triple room is 3000 rubles, a quadruple room is 4000 rubles. From August 26 to September 30, the price of the rooms will be 1200 rubles, 1400 rubles, 1900 rubles. and 2500 rubles. respectively.

Children under 4 years old can stay at the hotel for free if they are not provided with a separate place and meals. Moreover, such a free place can be provided only to one child. An additional place will cost guests half the cost of one main place in the room.

What services are available in the hotel

For an additional fee, Indigo (2 * resort) provides guests with two or three meals a day. At the same time, when booking a room, payment of two meals a day at the rate of 500 rubles is mandatory. per person per day. Meals are at the discretion of guests.





The complex has three different pools, one of which is perfect for younger guests. Also for them there is an equipped playground and a whole children's town, consisting of three floors. Since 2017, young tourists can attend free classes in applied and fine art, which are conducted by a professional teacher.

For adults - table tennis, billiards, car parking, sauna and wireless Internet. Near the pools there are sun loungers on which everyone can sunbathe and relax.

What the former guests say about the complex

"Indigo" is a 2 * resort complex, the reviews of which are interesting to read for every tourist who planned to come to rest in Sukko. Thanks to the impressions of other vacationers, you can find out all the advantages and disadvantages of this place and make an informed decision about whether to stay here or not.





Basically, tourists who visited the "Indigo", note the friendly staff, comfortable and clean rooms, great entertainment for children. Some definitely recommend this resort for families with children.

Regarding the food in the local dining room, the guests have a double opinion. Guests note small portions, as well as a not-so-convenient schedule of dinners, lunches and breakfasts. Some tourists mention a constantly barking dog in one of the neighboring houses, which sometimes interfered with a calm and quiet rest.
