Hotel Riu Merengue 5 * (Puerto Plata, Dominican Republic): photos and reviews of tourists

Dominican Republic is a country in which the sun always shines, and the thermometer column stably keeps at around 28-29 ¬∞ C even in winter in the northern regions. Therefore, its resorts are so popular among travelers. And the Dominican Republic is rich in luxury resort hotels. And one of those is Riu Merengue 5 *. Here I would like to talk about it in more detail.





general information

Club Hotel Riu Merengue 5 *, which has 24-hour all-inclusive service, is located in the resort of Puerto Plata. This is the northern province of the Dominican Republic, which only from the end of the 90s began to develop as a tourist center. Here, amazing nature, green dense forests, stunning mountains and, of course, picturesque beaches. The presence of a place where you can sunbathe and swim is most important for a tourist. Therefore, it should be noted that the hotel has its own beach. Palm trees grow right on it, under which many tourists like to relax in the shade.

It is also worth noting that Merengue, together with the nearby Riu Bachata Hotel, forms a single stylish resort complex. And - most importantly - guests of one hotel can use the services and infrastructure of another.

About service

So, directly in Riu Merengue 5 * there is free Wi-Fi and private car parking, concierge and messenger services, laundry with dry cleaning, a beauty salon with a hairdresser, a boutique and shops, a 24-hour reception and a storage room. For an additional fee, you can visit the Internet room with computers if you do not have a laptop with you. But that is not all. It is also worth knowing that in Riu Merengue 5 * they provide wedding services.

You can also note by attention what services Riu Bachata has, once guests staying at Merengue can use them. There is a car rental service, a playroom for children and a mini club. However, baby care is also provided at Merengue.





Leisure activities

Riu Merengue 5 * has a professional animation team that organizes an interesting entertainment program not only for adults, but also for children. The smallest will definitely enjoy the RiuLand club while their parents have fun. By the way, 6 days a week a Pacha disco is organized here (admission is free). If you wish, you can go to the casino "El Millon", located in the neighboring hotel.

Also at ClubHotel Riu Merengue 5 * there is a gym and fitness center, a billiard room, three professional floodlit courts. There are several pools. By the way, there are introductory diving lessons. The first is free.

On the beach you can play volleyball, windsurf, kayak, board or pedalos, snorkel. Also, while relaxing at the hotel, you can play golf on a professional 18-hole course. Or go horse riding. In general, you definitely will not be bored here.

health and beauty

On the territory of Riu Merengue 5 * (Dominican Republic) there is an excellent SPA-center with a health center. Here, guests are offered a wide range of body, face and hair care procedures, a full list of which can be found upon arrival.

It should be noted that there are magnificent massages. A variety of types are offered. For example, a restorative massage of the shoulders, neck, back and collar zone. Or combined, popular due to its versatility. Swedish and therapeutic aroma massages are also done here. Reflexology, lymphatic drainage, shiatsu, stone therapy can also be tried here. Not to mention healing and deep muscle massage. By the way, even here they make lomi-lomi (Hawaiian technology). And a four-hand massage.

There are many procedures for the face and body. Deep cleansing, skin restoration, mud and chocolate wraps, thalasso masks are just a small list of services. And, of course, here you can go for peeling, hair removal, manicure, pedicure, haircut or makeup. A sauna and steam bath are also available.

Important Nuances

In Riu Merengue 5 * (Dominican Republic, Puerto Plata), guests can be offered connecting rooms. They are provided depending on the availability of free options, so if tourists know in advance that they need such apartments, then it is better to stipulate this nuance in your reservation request. General conclusions

In general, as people who have been vacationing here say, the hotel is perfect for a fruitful, eventful vacation. In addition, for a modest amount. For the Dominican Republic, the above-mentioned rates are really small, given that the hotel serves all-inclusive.

And the hotel takes a good place. From here you can go on the most interesting excursions. For example, on the Samana Peninsula, where you can watch a variety of whales. Even humpbacked, but this is from January to March. You can go to the cold waterfalls, jump from a height into the crystal clear water and enjoy the scenery. Or go on a sightseeing tour of Puerto Plata. In general, tourists are advised not to forget about the cultural program while in the Dominican Republic. There is also something to see.

By the way, more experienced tourists recommend taking slippers for the sea with them. The sunset here is not very convenient, sharp. In addition, sometimes you can stumble upon sea urchins, and this, to put it mildly, is very unpleasant.

In general, it is worth going here. And do not abandon this idea because of the long flight, which takes almost 12 hours. As many people who doubted precisely for this reason claim, a vacation is worth it.
