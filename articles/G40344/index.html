Providers of Yekaterinburg. Top 5 who to download

It is impossible to imagine modern life without the Internet. Chatting with friends, downloading a movie or just watching the weather - all this requires a Network. You can also get Wi-Fi equipment and use the Internet from your phone or tablet. Among the huge number of companies it is necessary to find one that will not create problems anywhere and never. Tariffs, cost of services, connection speed - all this affects the user's choice. The article presents the top 5 providers of Yekaterinburg, the reputation and quality of work of which has been verified by thousands of subscribers.

Provider with cutting-edge Internet. There are only two tariffs: one "Online 22" - 650 rubles. per month, the second "SuperOnline" - 6619 rubles. in year. Each connection has up to 100 Mbps and a wide selection of digital channels.

Among the nice features you can highlight the promised payment service and free connection.

One of the most stable providers in Yekaterinburg. Convex has its own networks for data transmission in all areas of the city, which allows you to quickly eliminate glitches or avoid them altogether.

3. "SkyNet"

SkyNet is a well-known provider of Yekaterinburg, on the market for more than 20 years.

In a word, an optimal Internet provider with a standard set of services, but high quality communications.

One of the largest providers of Yekaterinburg and Russia as a whole. For Dom.ru subscribers throughout the city, there are free access points at maximum speed.
