Festa Pomorie Resort 4 * (Bulgaria / Pomorie): photos and reviews of tourists

When planning a vacation, we all want the vacation to be perfect. And not the last role in this is played by the right hotel choice. After all, it is in it that tourists will spend most of their time. The hotel rooms should be modern and equipped with quality furniture, and the hotel must necessarily have the amenities and various entertainment for guests. Choosing a place to stay, many tourists pay attention to the hotel Festa Pomorie Resort, whose rating is quite high. But is it worth staying here?

What is attractive about the resort of Pomorie?

Pomorie resort is located on the south of the Black Sea coast of Bulgaria, and it is famous primarily for its therapeutic mud. On its territory there are two lakes with salt water, and the healing clay extracted here is considered the best in the whole country. In addition, tourists can visit the largest Bulgarian wine cellar, as well as look at the Pomorie vineyards. But there are no mountain ranges or large forests nearby.

Nevertheless, the Festa Pomorie Resort 4 * hotel is located in an ecologically clean area. Pomorie is famous for its unpolluted sea. The beach stretches for almost 4 kilometers, but the sand here is not perfectly white. Often there are small stones or even solid pebbles. In the old part of the city you can stroll along the small streets that have been preserved in their original form since the 19th century. In the new part, tourists can visit a huge number of restaurants, cafes, bars, as well as many shops.

Hotel location

The tourist complex Festa Pomorie Resort 4 * is located directly on the beach in the southern part of Pomorie. The resort is considered a great place for a health and beach holiday. You can get here from the nearest airport of the city of Burgas, the distance to which is about 15 kilometers. It takes less than an hour to get to the hotel by car. Buses also run from Burgas to Pomorie. The public beach is just 2 minutes away, and the city center can be reached in about 5-10 minutes.





Staying at this hotel is also worth visiting the nearby attractions. So, very close is a large water park (800 meters). And 2 kilometers from the complex is the famous salt museum. It is also worth visiting Nessebar and Burgas - the ancient cities of Bulgaria with many historical monuments.

Important hotel information

Hotel Festa Pomorie Resort, which is mostly positive for tourists, was opened in 2009. This is one of the newest tourist complexes in Pomorie. On the vast territory of the hotel is a six-story building, where there are more than 200 comfortable rooms. The hotel is surrounded by a forested area. High cypresses and small rose bushes grow everywhere. This place is perfect for a vacation with the whole family. Due to its therapeutic mud, the hotel also attracts the attention of older people and people with disabilities.





The complex allows holidays with children of any age. For the youngest guests there are discounts on accommodation. And it is strictly forbidden to bring animals here. Family orientation is confirmed by the ban on smoking throughout the hotel. Check-in is possible at the reception from 14:00 local time until midnight. Leave the hotel after rest before noon. Some hotel staff understand and speak Russian fluently, but most of them only know English and Bulgarian.

Housing stock

Festa Pomorie Resort 4 * is a large hotel, which has 277 different rooms and apartments. Tourists will find here rooms suitable for a whole family, as well as small rooms designed for 1-2 people. Half of the apartments have windows with sea views. The rest of the rooms overlook the city and the forested area located around the hotel. The following types of apartments are distinguished in the complex:

  • Standard room with city view. Its area is 20 square meters. m. At the same time no more than 2 people can live here.
  • Standard room with sea view. It is estimated that 2 people will live here. There is also a folding chair where a child or adult can sleep.
  • 42 sqm suite m. Accommodates 3-4 people. There are two separate beds and a sofa bed. The room is divided into a bedroom and a living room, separated by an adjacent door. Windows open to sea or city views.




Some rooms have a private small balcony, while the lower floors are equipped with terraces. The rooms are modern renovated. The walls are painted in contrasting colors and carpeted floors. Elegant lights, fresh flowers and exquisite paintings with abstract drawings decorate the room. Blackout curtains are hanging on the windows, matching the color of the bedding. Daily housekeeping and towels are provided. Sheets are also regularly updated.

What facilities are there in the rooms?

Not only exquisite design and quality repairs can please the hotel Festa Pomorie Resort. Guest reviews also note the many amenities that each room is equipped with. They not only create an atmosphere of home comfort in the room, but also help tourists relax after a hard day. We list the equipment that the hotel offers:

  • Individual air conditioning, which is in every room. With it, tourists can independently adjust the temperature in the room.
  • A TV with satellite channels. It is also possible to view Russian-language news.
  • Direct dial telephone for reception.
  • Combined bathroom, which includes a bathroom, shower, bathrobes, toiletries (shampoo, shower gel, soap), sink, large mirror and hairdryer.
  • Mini bar. Drinks are not included in the price and are paid separately.
  • The furniture in the room has a bed, coffee table, bedside table, desk and chair.
  • For an additional fee, mail, newspapers, and food and drinks can be delivered directly to your room.

Services provided to hotel guests

The developed infrastructure of the hotel is an important factor in quality rest. The more services he is ready to offer his guests, the higher the level of the tourist complex. The administration of Festa Pomorie Resort 4 * (Bulgaria) is aware of this, therefore, is trying to provide guests with all the necessary infrastructure facilities. Consider the main ones:

  • Free Wi-Fi in the lobby and public areas of the hotel. Internet connection in rooms is subject to an additional charge.
  • Paid private parking, which is guarded around the clock. You can also rent a car here to travel around the city or country.
  • Organization of a paid transfer from the airport to the hotel and back.
  • 24 hour ATM in the complex. Currency exchange is also available.
  • For storage of large bags and suitcases at the reception there is a special luggage room. Here, for a small amount, tourists can use the electronic safe and leave valuable documents, things and money.
  • Own small shopping center where guests can buy food, newspapers, tobacco and alcohol products, clothes, shoes and souvenirs.
  • Comfortable elevator designed to lift physically disabled people.
  • Dry cleaning.




All types of entertainment

Tourists vacationing at the Festa Pomorie Resort 4 * leave good reviews. Basically they celebrate quality and varied entertainment. The hotel provides various activities for both active and measured leisure, so that every vacationer will find entertainment to their liking. The complex offers the following leisure facilities:

  • 2 outdoor pools with fresh water. The hotel provides free sun umbrellas and sun loungers. Beach towels are issued upon payment of the deposit.
  • A well-kept garden where you can take leisurely walks in the shade of tall trees.
  • Public beach 50 meters from the hotel. Here sun loungers, umbrellas and mattresses are paid. You can go windsurfing and other water sports. A free beach volleyball court is provided.
  • Spa "Via Pontica". Tourists can try a tonic or relaxing massage, mud wraps. The complex also includes a Turkish bath, sauna. There is also a beauty salon that provides paid wellness services for face and body.
  • Free gym. Lesson in water aerobics in the pool.
  • Plot for playing table tennis.
  • Halls for playing billiards and darts.
  • Video game room.
  • Animation program.
  • Organization of various excursion programs.




Food, restaurants and local cuisine

Festa Pomorie Resort 4 * operates on the all-inclusive system, popular among Russians. The price includes breakfast, lunch and dinner, as well as light snacks, desserts, ice cream and drinks at the bar. Alcoholic drinks are provided free of charge, but only of Bulgarian origin. The basis of the diet includes dishes from national and European cuisine, as well as recipes that include fish and seafood. The menu always has fresh fruits, berries and vegetables. There are several catering facilities for hotel guests:

  • The main restaurant, where meals are served at the shared buffet. There is a private terrace for eating outdoors. Visitors enjoy a beautiful view of the Black Sea.
  • A la carte fish restaurant. Tourists can enjoy original fish and seafood dishes.
  • Lobby bar. Guests of the Festa Pomorie Resort 4 * hotel can have a snack here, having a cup of tea with fresh pastries. It also serves desserts, ice cream, fruits and light snacks.
  • The pool bar invites guests to enjoy cold drinks and ice cream, without being distracted from the rest.
  • Beach bar. In it, guests can cool off by drinking a cocktail, ordering alcoholic drinks, juices or mineral water.




Relax in a hotel with children

Families with children are the main part of tourists having a rest in Festa Pomorie Resort 4 * (Bulgaria). Reviews confirm the presence of well-developed children's infrastructure here. For younger guests of the hotel, high chairs are offered for feeding in the restaurant, and you can request a free cot when making a voucher. In addition, children under 12 years old are allowed free of charge if they sleep on existing beds. For young children, the hotel provides paid babysitting services. A qualified specialist will take care of the child while the parents are busy with other activities.

In one of the hotel pools there is a special shallow children's section for bathing children. It maintains a warm temperature. There is a mini club for children aged 4 to 12, and children's animation is also provided. There is also a playground in the fresh air, where children can ride on the carousel and swing, play active games.

Positive reviews

Having learned all the necessary information about the hotel, you must also read the reviews that leave tourists who have already rested here. In their opinion, the advantages of the complex are:

  • quick check-in within 5 minutes in the already prepared room of the hotel Festa Pomorie Resort 4 * (Pomorie);
  • Pomorie is a very beautiful resort city with a developed trade infrastructure, so there are many places outside the hotel where you can take a walk;
  • a lot of delicious fruits and berries on the menu (cherries, cherries, peaches, strawberries);
  • high-quality daily cleaning and towel change;
  • helpful and pleasant staff.

Negative reviews

Unfortunately, tourists who visited the Festa Pomorie Resort 4 * (Bulgaria) leave reviews not only positive. Guests believe that the hotel has many shortcomings. We list the main ones:

  • a large number of tourists, so there are often not enough sun loungers on the beach;
  • a very dirty beach, which is rarely cleaned;
  • monotonous and tasteless food;
  • the hotel is not completely populated by tourists, partly there are private traders who behave very uncivilized;
  • staff constantly begs for tips.




Conclusion

Hotel Festa Pomorie Resort 4 * (Bulgaria) reviews are mixed, but mostly good. The tourist complex has a developed infrastructure, modern rooms, a wide variety of entertainment. There are also good conditions for families with children. If you want a high-quality, but cheap to relax, then you can safely choose this hotel. He will hardly disappoint you.
