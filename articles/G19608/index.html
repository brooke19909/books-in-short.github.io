How does a pleasant trip or vacation begin? From the preliminary planning stage of this trip. Location

The complex spread out near the golden artificial beach of Al Marjan, the purity and perfection of which will conquer even the most picky tourist. From the center of the nearest city - Ras al-Khaimah - you will be separated only 20 minutes by a comfortable ride. It is also easy to get to the huge international airport in Dubai - just an hour away. The nearest airport is located in the mentioned city of Ras al-Khaimah, and it is only 30 km from the hotel.
