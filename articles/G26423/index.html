Hotel Movenpick Resort Marine SPA 5 * (Sousse, Tunisia): photos and reviews of tourists

North Africa is not such a popular travel destination, such as Turkey or Egypt. But there is such a country here that enjoys considerable popularity. And this is Tunisia. Here is one of the most attractive hotels for tourists, namely Movenpick Resort & Marine SPA 5 *. It is interesting to many, and it is worth telling about it.





Location

Movenpick Resort & Marine SPA 5 * is located in a city called Sousse. This is a port city, famous for its historical monuments and, of course, good conditions for sea holidays. After all, it is located on the Mediterranean coast. Interestingly, the old part of Sousse (known as the medina) in 1988 was recognized as a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

The hotel itself is located on the first coastline in the city center. The huge building, made in the original national style, immediately catches your eye. Well-groomed large area, numerous greenery and palm trees - all this makes the hotel attractive. But much more interesting is the fact that it has its own beach, designed for visiting guests. There is always enough space on it. And the sea always remains clean.

True, the airport is far away. It is located 135 kilometers from Sousse. But transport links are well developed, so getting to this place of work is not difficult.

restaurants and bars

On the territory of Movenpick Resort & Marine SPA 5 * there are several excellent establishments where you can eat deliciously. The main restaurant is known as Mosaique. Buffet service is served there. International dishes are served. There is everything: fish and meat treats, poultry, side dishes, several types of cheeses, sausages, jerky, kebabs, vegetables, fruits, snacks, assorted juices and sweets. And in a separate corner there are cooks who, with guests, cook the length of them fresh omelettes with different fillings and pancakes. Seafood days are often held - they serve mussels, gourmet fish, king prawns.

There is also a Spanish restaurant called "Tareo" and an institution serving Japanese cuisine. National and Moroccan cuisine can be sampled at La Villa. This restaurant is also located on site. There are also several bars here: one lobby, another by the pool, and Fresh right in it.





Sports and recreation

In Movenpick Resort & Marine SPA 5 * there is everything you need for a good rest. If the guests do not want to go to the beach, then it does not matter - they can occupy themselves with something else. On the territory there is a huge swimming pool, as well as a picturesque garden and sun terrace. The hotel also provides all the necessary equipment for water sports. Lovers of active pastime will enjoy this, as well as the presence of a tennis court, golf course, rental of catamarans, canoes, sailing boats. One of the most favorite entertainments here is parasailing (parachute + boat). Only it is available at an additional cost.

Also in this complex there is a SPA-center in which guests are offered various health and beauty treatments. They can visit the Turkish bath, massage, sauna, devote time to thalassotherapy.

The hotel also has a fitness center that will appeal to people who like to keep themselves in good physical shape. By the way, the hotel even has its own personal beauty salon with a hairdresser. This should be borne in mind by those people who are accustomed to carefully monitor their appearance.

But the main entertainment, of course, was and remains the beach. Incidentally, it has free sunbeds, umbrellas and a bar.

Service and maintenance

The hotel administration made sure that in Movenpick Resort & Marine SPA 5 * (Tunisia) there was everything necessary for the comfort of guests. This is free Wi-Fi, available both in the hotel and in the rooms, private parking, concierge and babysitting (their services sometimes help out), an ATM and a currency exchange, a tour desk, a 24-hour reception desk and much more.

In addition to daily housekeeping, dry cleaning, laundry and ironing services are also available. The hotel also has a conference room and an office with a photocopy and fax machine.

As previously mentioned, traveling far from the airport to the complex. Shuttle service is available at an extra charge. Also, guests can rent a car, request a safe (regular or for a laptop), a wake-up service, facilities for people with disabilities (if necessary) and delivery of food, newspapers, drinks to the room. By the way, on the territory of the Movenpick Resort & Marine SPA 5 * hotel (Tunisia) there are shops where you can buy anything.

It is also worth noting that the staff speaks four languages: Arabic, German, English and French.

Reservation

Movenpick Resort & Marine SPA 5 * (Sousse) is very popular among travelers, as, indeed, any other 5-star hotel. If you want to relax here, it is better to send a reservation request prematurely.

In general, the race lasts from three in the afternoon until midnight. Departure is from one in the morning until noon.

This hotel allows children of any age. If the child is under 2 years old, you do not need to pay extra for it. Moreover, the administration will arrange for a cot in the room. It will also be free. However, guests must notify the property in advance that a cot is needed. Better - at the time of booking.

In the event that the age of the child is from two to 11 years, for the night of his stay will need to pay an extra 30 euros. By the way, when booking, you will need to provide your credit card details. Hotel Movenpick Resort & Marine SPA 5 * (Tunisia, Sousse) accepts "Visa", "American Express" and "Master Card". The administration may block the amount for which the reservation is ordered. This will guarantee the arrival of tourists. And upon check-in it will be necessary to provide the same credit card, as well as an identity card.

Other features

The beach, on the shore of which the Movenpick Resort & Marine SPA 5 * hotel is located, is always impeccably clean, like the sea itself. Unless after a storm, many algae are nailed. However, when the weather is getting better, everything returns to normal.

Guests are advised to go to the hotel in early autumn, if possible. At this time, the hotel becomes much less crowded than in summer. In the event that there will be very few vacationers, guests can even give an apartment with a beautiful view even without surcharge. In any case, some say that it was exactly with them.

By the way, people traveling here with children may not worry about the child. There is an almost perfect kindergarten - the kids play, draw, eat and sleep. And parents at this time can relax and go about their business.
