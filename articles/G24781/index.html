Royalton Hotel 2 * (UAE / Dubai): reviews and photos

The Royalton Hotel 2 is a modest hotel providing a budget vacation. The choice on it is stopped by unpretentious tourists, pre-configured to relax outside the hotel. Having saved on accommodation, they have fun in the institutions of Dubai and make excursions in the UAE from early morning until nightfall.

The location of the hotel complex

Royalton Hotel 2 is located in the shopping district of Dubai, adjacent to the central part of the resort city. The center of Dubai, where important business and cultural institutions are located, is 600 meters from the hotel. It borders the famous Al Ghurair shopping center. The journey to Union metro station from the hotel takes five minutes. Overcoming five kilometers, tourists make purchases in the shopping centers Diera City Center and Burjuman Mall.





Departure from the country is possible through two international airports. One is located in Dubai, five kilometers from a budget business hotel. The second is located at a distance of 21 kilometers in the city of Sharjah.

Attractions around the hotel

A beautiful park with luxurious walking alleys and famous sights of Dubai are within walking distance of the hotel. It is adjacent to the Global Village City Museum and the zoo. The attention of tourists is attracted by events taking place at the nearby World Exhibition Center.

People visit a picturesque ethnographic village spread out nearby, find entertainment on the picturesque coast that borders the Dubai Creek. Walk along Al Nasser Square and trade with oriental sellers at the Gold Market.

Royalton Hotel 2 (Dubai) is surrounded by famous places of the resort town, which are popular with travelers. Tourists have reasons to visit the Center for Cultural Cooperation Sheikh Mohammed. There is entertainment in the Bastakia quarter. Sports enthusiasts are attracted to two clubs, Dubai Creek Golf & Yacht Club and Dubai Sports City.

Hotel infrastructure and features

A set of services at the Royalton Hotel 2 is standard. Wireless internet access is available throughout the hotel. An additional fee will be charged for it, regardless of where tourists intend to use it - in the room or in public areas. If necessary, guests who pay Wi-Fi are provided with a desktop with a computer.





The hotel is equipped with a business center and conference room. Vacationers are entitled to request concierge services, a safe for storing valuables and documents, and take advantage of assistance when booking tickets. The hotel has smoking areas, a doctor‚Äôs office, a newsstand, an air conditioning system, a left-luggage office, dry cleaning, and a laundry service. It organizes a Room-service. A reasonable transfer from the airport and shops is also available. The reception is open around the clock.

Car rental is available at the rental car. Tourists traveling in the United Arab Emirates by car do not have problems with parking - at the Royalton Hotel 2 there is a convenient place for a paid parking lot. At any time, guests can quickly exchange money at a currency exchange office. It offers free transportation to certain places, such as the beach.

Tourists are served by multilingual staff who cleverly perform imputed duties. The staff, friendly and helpful, create impeccable cleanliness and dignity in the hotel rooms. Although the hotel has only 2 stars, guests feel compliance with high standards of service. Here, a rich culture was successfully balanced with the conveniences of the present, creating an atmosphere of comfortable relaxation.





In the lower floors of the hotel complex there is a disco and night clubs. The regulars of the entertainment clubs, working until three in the morning, were Pakistanis, Indians and Filipinos. Therefore, it is worth living in apartments located not lower than the fourth floor. They do not hear the roar of music at night.

The hotel tour desk offers a variety of educational programs that introduce the history of the UAE. Rest at Royalton Hotel 2 (Dubai, Deira) is attractive for tourists doing business, who love shopping and dynamic entertainment, who know the value of cultural and historical monuments.

Supply system

To satisfy the hunger at the hotel, guests choose one of the types of food RO, BB, HB (Lunch). The restaurants have a buffet. Dishes prepared according to Asian, Arabic and European recipes are served. The Caspian Restaurant specializes in dietary dishes included in international cuisine.





CoffeeShop Cafe offers light snacks, delicious hot and cold drinks, breakfasts and lunches. The cooks at Caspain Restaurant serve Asian and European dishes. Cohiba Pub's lobby bar is dominated by traditional and original drinks. The menu at Sangam Indian Rest & Bar offers an abundance of Indian dishes. Food and drinks from bars and restaurants at Royalton Hotel (Dubai) 2 are delivered to the apartments upon request.

Beach vacation

In Dubai, many hotels are quite far from the coast. They do not even have a tiny territory and their own beaches. City business hotels line up on the third coastal line, while the distance to the beach is at least one kilometer. This is the specificity of rest in city hotels in the UAE.

Royalton Hotel 2 provides guests every morning for a trip to the public beach, to which about 1000 meters, free buses. Entrance to the comfortable Al Mamzar beach is paid. You have to pay for all beach accessories.

The municipal beach is framed by the vast Al Mamzar Peninsula and borders an incredibly popular entertainment park of the same name. Al Mamzar Park is equipped with food stalls, barbecue and picnic areas, a sports ground. Chalets are rented on its territory, where tourists live for several days, enjoying fun activities.





Visitors bathe in the pool, divided into children's and adult sections. Rescuers are on duty in the pool area, bars and cafes are working. Guests of the park make trips in the observation train, enjoy the rides. Watch musical performances in the park amphitheater. They rise to the observation platforms of a huge tower, towering in the center of the park. From a bird's eye view, stunning panoramic views of the magnificent peninsula of entertainment open to the guests. Children have fun in the comfortably furnished playgrounds.

Al Mamzar beach is the main advantage of the park. Five separate beach areas are under the vigilant control of lifeguards. All areas are equipped with toilets, showers and changing rooms. On the sand there are sun loungers, umbrellas and other necessary beach accessories.

The quiet lagoon, splashing off the coast of Dubai and Sharjah, has a perfectly smooth surface in any season. The bay, fenced from the powerful waves of the Persian Gulf, attracts tourists with tiny children and those who are not averse to ride on water transport - skiing, scooters, bananas and more.

Hotel Apartments

Hotel Royalton Hotel 2 is equipped with 85 air-conditioned apartments of two types - Standard rooms and Luxury rooms. The rooms are furnished with good furniture: beds, armchairs, a desk, wardrobes. Upon request, ironing board, iron and hairdryer are delivered to the apartment.





Rooms include safes and LCD TVs. Vacationers have the opportunity to use cable and satellite TV. The apartments are equipped with refrigerators and telephones. Bathrooms with toiletry sets function as a toilet and shower. Housekeepers daily visit the room to clean, change towels and bed linen.

Child care

Young guests under 4 years old stay at the hotel free of charge, unless a bed is requested for them. For children of other ages and adults, an extra bed is provided, but only upon prior confirmation by the hotel. Additionally, only one bed is placed in the room.

Entertainment at the hotel

Entertainment at the hotel at least. Guests are invited to visit entertainment programs, have fun at a nightclub, light up at a disco, relax in restaurants with live music, or purchase a guided tour. However, guests do not expect more, they find a lot of fun activities in parks, entertainment venues, sports clubs, museums and entertainment complexes in Dubai.

Tourists reviews





Tourists consider the Royalton Hotel 2 hotel to be quite a worthy place for spending the night. Guest reviews about its decoration, service and comfort are entirely objective. No one has any complaints about the interior and furniture. Separate plumbing, in the opinion of the guests, requires replacement. Between breakfast and lunch, vacationers recommend giving preference to the latter type of food. Lunch at restaurants is tastier, more generous and more varied than breakfast.

In order for the apartments to be cleaned, it is necessary to leave a plate with the appropriate message on the door of the room. Not every traveler likes this approach. In general, tourists note that the price-quality ratio in the economy-class business hotel is maintained at a decent level.
