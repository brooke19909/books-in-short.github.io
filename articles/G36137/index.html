What saunas does Kostroma have? Kostroma saunas: names, addresses, reviews

In the sauna you can have a wonderful rest after a working day, relieve stress or have a romantic evening and retire with your soulmate. This opportunity offers Kostroma. The saunas of this city are diverse. Now consider the good places. It is possible that they will interest you with their originality.

Kostroma: city saunas

- Hotel complex "Volga". Sauna in this complex is popular. It accommodates up to 12 people. The view of the steam room is Finnish. There is a great mid-sized deep pool. Additionally, visitors can use audio and video equipment, sing karaoke, purchase bath accessories. Separately, there is a lounge.

Meals can also be ordered at the restaurant. They will be served in the rest room. A pleasant opportunity for lovers of family vacations: children under six can relax in the sauna for free under the supervision of adults. And for hotel residents there is a 10% discount on the sauna. Of course, the advantage is a round-the-clock operation. The prices are pretty nice - just 450 rubles per hour. Visitors leave positive feedback about the service, cleanliness and atmosphere. It is very comfortable, neat and cozy. The complex is located at this address: Kostroma, st. Youth, 1. Phone: (4942) 39-42-47, 8-953-644-00-66, 8-953-661-46-13.


- Sauna "Scorpio" (Kostroma). Very famous sauna in the city. It has a large pool, but not a simple one, but with various small waterfalls and fountains. An excellent steam room and wet steam will help you get the most out of the process. Additionally, you can use the massage services, sing karaoke. There are showers. This place is suitable for families. You can also have a good time with friends. Visitors have the opportunity to order food and drinks. Sauna "Scorpio" (Kostroma) pleases with pleasant prices (from 700 rubles per hour). Many who have visited this institution leave positive reviews. Located at: Kostroma, st. Beregovaya, 45. Phone: (4942) 30-04-56.



- Sauna "Shelestov". It has several steam rooms, namely: Turkish hammam, Russian classic bath. There is a pool with hydromassage, a watercourse and lighting, a relaxation room and massage room, showers. The pool is the pride of this sauna. After all, it can be called a small water park. Safety comes first. Therefore, the water in it is filtered through ultraviolet radiation. The pool itself is shared by several zones. All of them are unique and effective in their own way. In this place you can relax with your family, loved one or friends. Russian bath provides an opportunity for visitors to feel the power of Russian temper. The interior is original: the walls are lined with Siberian cedar, the ceilings - with massive aspen. What Russian bath without a birch broom? In this, of course, they are. Brooms complement sensations and cheer up. The process itself is becoming traditional. As for the Turkish hammam, it is equipped with an aromatherapy system. This allows us to call it therapeutic. The walls are decorated with glass mosaics, which is very characteristic of the traditions of the East. This sauna is open around the clock. Prices are high, from 2000 rubles for an hour and a half. This is definitely a VIP sauna. Those who have visited here are talking about quality service and good rest. You can find a sauna at the address: Kostroma, st. Kommunarov, d. 1. Phone: (4942) 62-92-21, 62-99-99
