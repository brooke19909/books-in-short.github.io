Sound

The NICAM stereo system, which is part of this unit, includes 2 8W speakers. The total sound level of this device is 16W. Additional software solutions to improve sound quality in this case, the manufacturer is not provided.

The Haier LE42u6500TF is equipped with three 3.5 mm ports. One of them is designed to output sound to an external speaker system by wire. Another of them allows you to bring sound to a wired stereo headset. As in the first case, and in the second there is the possibility of reconfiguring the sound subsystem to the needs of the owner.

The main disadvantage of the considered multimedia center from the position of acoustics is the lack of a wireless Bluetooth connection.

Communication list

A truly excellent list of communications boasts the multimedia solution considered in this review. Using it, you can significantly improve the level of functionality of this solution. The manufacturer included the following in it:

  1. Three digital ports for receiving an HDMI signal. As a rule, with their help, a video signal is output from personal computers, modern modifications of satellite tuners or DVD-players.

  2. The presence of three universal digital USB ports can boast this solution. With their help, you can implement switching to any information storage devices and play back from them or record various multimedia information.

  3. To receive a television signal, this TV is equipped with an RF1 input. Moreover, both analog and digital television broadcasts are allowed.

  4. When receiving satellite transmissions from older tuner models, you can use either the SCART port or the RCA combo jack.

  5. A possible alternative to the HDMI port when displaying pictures from a PC is the D-Sub jack.

  6. In some cases, when decoding television programs, it is necessary to install a special module in the TV. For these purposes, this device has a CI slot.

  7. To the "global web", this solution can use either a wired RJ-45 port or a wireless Wi-Fi interface.
