Hotel Kapetanios Bay Hotel 3 *, Protaras, Cyprus: tourist reviews

Cyprus is a true paradise, which is ideal for both beach and sightseeing holidays. And tourists from all over the world annually visit the city of Protaras, located next to the sea coast and picturesque mountains. Naturally, there are many hotels of different categories, each of which is ready to offer quality service to the traveler. One of them is the Kapetanios Bay Hotel.

So where exactly is the hotel located and what is the distance to the beach? What conditions of food and accommodation can a tourist count on? How do other travelers comment on this place? The answers to these questions are interesting to many readers.

Location





Kapetanios Bay Hotel 3 * is a small cozy hotel located very close to the sea coast. Protaras city center is about 8 kilometers away. It is easily accessible both by public transport and taxi. But the nearest international airport is located in Larnaca, 75 kilometers away. Of course, a travel company will provide you with a transfer, but you should prepare for a long trip.

Meals for guests

Since food is an important issue for many tourists, it must be said right away that the Kapetanios Bay Hotel 3 * hotel offers different schemes. For example, when booking rooms, you can only pay for breakfast, and you can also have full three meals a day. In any case, even if you did not pay, for example, lunch, you can always eat in the hotel restaurant. Prices are quite affordable.





Reviews indicate that the menu here is really diverse. Breakfast includes traditional fried eggs, pancakes, cereals, several types of cheese slices. For lunch and dinner, the choice is more diverse, as it includes several side dishes, fish, seafood and at least three types of meat. Naturally, everything is complemented by an abundance of seasonal fruits and delicious desserts. Special menus are available for children and vegetarians.

The Kapetanios Bay Hotel also has two bars, open until 23.00. Here you will be offered different varieties of coffee and tea, as well as juices, alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks, interesting exotic cocktails.

In any case, there are a lot of restaurants, cafes, pizzerias and colorful taverns on the territory of the city where you can have a tasty and satisfying lunch.

Where is the beach located?

Kapetanios Bay Hotel is located on the second coastline. The distance to the beach is about 400 meters, which, you see, is not so much. A convenient road leads to the shore, so in a few minutes you can already enjoy the breathtaking view of the waves and powerful mountains.





The beach here is public, and in some months it can be really crowded. However, you can always find a free sunbed and sun umbrella. By the way, you have to pay for their use, although the prices are quite reasonable.

The traveler can count on soft, clean sand underfoot, as the beach is cleaned every day. The entrance to the sea is convenient, there is a shallow area where children can swim.

Fans of more active entertainment will also not be bored. Here you can ride a boat, banana, water skiing and motorbikes, as well as try all the charms of parasailing. Tourists can also rent a boat or catamarans. Fans of sailing and windsurfing often relax on this coast, and if you are one of them, you can count on a lot of pleasant experiences.

Additional service on site

The Kapetanios Bay Hotel tried to create all conditions for a pleasant pastime. For guests the laundry, currency exchange is constantly working. You can also rent a safe from the administrator. There is also ample parking. Upon request, staff can even help you rent a car.





There is a doctor on site who will help you in case of health problems. Internet access is almost everywhere. Also at the hotel there is a small mini market where you can buy various little things.

Protaras, Kapetanios Bay Hotel: entertainment and leisure for guests

As already mentioned, in the courtyard there is a rather large outdoor pool with sun loungers arranged around. Travelers are also offered to visit the local small cinema or have fun playing a game of table tennis. There is a spacious tennis court and a billiards room. Experienced massage therapists also offer their services. In the evenings, live music is played at the hotel, various show programs are organized that are interesting to children and adults.





By the way, for children there is also a small section of the pool and a spacious playground, so you can safely take a child here with him - he will have something to do with himself. And do not forget that one of the most interesting entertainments in Cyprus is excursions - in the city you will find a huge number of travel agencies that will help you see all the beauties of the country and get to know the local traditions more closely.
