Conditions

According to reviews, electronic registration of a transaction in Sberbank will be performed only if all documentation is available. The conditions include:

  • the presence of a document of sale or DDU;
  • Only an individual can participate in the procedure;
  • the transaction is an important part of the contract, without which electronic registration cannot be performed;
  • the share in the room is made out only with an appeal to the Rosreestr;
  • if minors are involved in the transaction, then you need to contact the Rosreestr;
  • proxy procedures are not performed;
  • You can not register a military mortgage;
  • maximum sellers and buyers - 2 people.




Subject to all requirements, registration will be successful. If there are any violations of the rules of the transaction, then they must be eliminated. As can be seen from the customer reviews, with such registration, documents arrive fairly quickly.
