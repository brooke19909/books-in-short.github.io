Hotel "Nevsky Park Inn": description and reviews

In order to get the most complete and vivid experience of visiting St. Petersburg, it is imperative that your hotel is fully consistent with the luxury and grandeur of this city. In this regard, the Nevsky Park Inn can be considered the most suitable accommodation option.

Hotel Feature

If you want to feel all the charm of St. Petersburg, then by all means stay at the chic Nevsky Park Inn hotel. This institution deservedly has 4 stars. Here, modern standards of comfort are closely intertwined with classic elements of luxury. In addition, here you will find amenities such as wireless Internet, a gym and much more that a modern traveler is used to.

Special attention should be paid to the rooms that the Nevsky Park Inn hotel offers its guests for their stay. Their design is quite bright and original, and the layout provides the convenience of guests. It is extremely important that the apartments are characterized by good sound insulation, and therefore neither the noise of the street, nor the steps and voices in the corridors will interfere with your vacation. There is also modern household appliances and electronics.

The highest praise is the work of the restaurant, which operates at the Nevsky Park Inn Hotel. A rich breakfast buffet is served here every morning. At any other time, you can order something interesting from the menu. The highlight of this institution is a huge selection of traditional German dishes and drinks. Also, you should definitely visit the bar.





Hotel Location

The Park Inn Nevsky Hotel boasts a fairly favorable location. The address of this institution is St. Petersburg, ul. Goncharnaya, 4. It is very close to the Moscow railway station (literally 5 minutes walk). Also nearby are such famous attractions as the Alexandria Theater and the Beloselsky-Belozersky Palace. Those who want to explore the whole city will certainly appreciate the fact that the metro station is just 100 meters away.

Standard rooms

Perhaps the most economical accommodation option at the Park Inn Nevsky Hotel are standard apartments. These are rather small one-room rooms, which, however, fit all the essentials so that you can relax comfortably and fully after a busy day. There is a large double bed, opposite which is a desk and a large TV. Also a pleasant moment is the availability of air conditioning.





Business Apartment

Business class rooms are specially designed for those who come for business purposes to St. Petersburg. The Park Inn Nevsky Hotel made sure that guests in these apartments could not only relax, but also work productively, and therefore there is a separate area that serves as an office. In all other respects, these apartments almost completely repeat the previous category.





Junior Suites

Feature of the Junior Suite category is that it is located on the upper floors of the Park Inn Radisson Nevsky Hotel. St. Petersburg will be at your fingertips if you look through the large panoramic windows. It is worth noting that these rooms are quite spacious and can accommodate up to three people. A pleasant moment will be the presence of a minibar and a coffee machine, which will provide you access to your favorite drinks.

The amenities provided in the apartment

In each of the rooms of the Park Inn Nevsky Hotel you will have the opportunity to take advantage of the following amenities:

  • the bathroom has a full range of bath and cosmetic accessories;
  • a bottle of drinking water will be delivered to your room daily;
  • there is a landline telephone, which serves not only for domestic, but also for long-distance and international calls;
  • there is a modern widescreen TV that broadcasts not only satellite channels, but also provides the ability to play files from external media;
  • a service is provided such as free use of wireless Internet;
  • the bathroom has a hairdryer;
  • there is an air conditioner that will allow you to adjust the temperature in your room individually;
  • there is a safe that allows you not to worry about the safety of your valuables;
  • in order for you to be comfortable in the bathroom even without slippers, heated floors are equipped there;
  • there is a shower cabin or a large bathtub with hydromassage;
  • there is a special set for self-preparation of tea and coffee;
  • an ironing board and an electric iron are hidden in the closet;
  • there is a minibar, which is filled with alcohol and soft drinks for an additional fee;
  • there is a coffee machine;
  • Each room has a work desk and a pair of chairs.




For people with disabilities

Hotel "St. Petersburg Park Inn Nevsky" does everything possible for the comfort of people with disabilities, and therefore provides for them additional amenities:

  • elevators have special braille buttons;
  • special handrails are installed in the bathroom to ensure greater security;
  • in all public areas there are special ramps and other facilities for strollers;
  • all rooms for disabled people have a sufficiently large area, and are also equipped with wide doors.

Facilities and Services Provided to Guests

Having chosen the Park Inn Nevsky Hotel for yourself, you can expect to receive the following services:

  • assistance in acquiring tickets for any type of transport, as well as in any direction;
  • delivery of dishes from the restaurant directly to the room;
  • if you want to travel the whole city on your own, you can rent a car (there is a wide selection of models);
  • for guests who suffer from allergies, there are special rooms in which all equipment is made of environmentally friendly materials;
  • 24-hour reception staff at any time of the day or night will be able to help you solve problems associated with accommodation;
  • thanks to the presence of ATMs in the lobby, you can withdraw cash from plastic cards without leaving the hotel.




Hotel Infrastructure

The Park Inn Nevsky Hotel offers its guests to use the following infrastructure facilities:

  • a modern fitness center where you will find all the necessary equipment for your workouts;
  • in the hotel restaurant you can try many international dishes, as well as enjoy live music;
  • in the bar you can watch sports matches or sing karaoke, as well as drink your favorite drinks;
  • free wireless internet is available throughout the hotel;
  • there is a private parking zone, the use of which will cost you 1000 rubles per night;
  • at the tour desk you can order a familiarization tour of the most interesting places in St. Petersburg;
  • you can leave your luggage in the storage room so that it does not take up space in the room;
  • dirty things you can hand in the laundry;
  • For business events, several conference rooms are provided;
  • if you have a festive event planned, then you will be given a banquet hall;
  • since the hotel is located in a high-rise building, an elevator functions for the convenience of guests;
  • there is a gift shop on the territory where you can buy interesting little things in memory of the hotel and the city;
  • there are special smoking areas.




Positive reviews

Every year, hundreds of guests visit the Park Inn by Radisson Nevsky hotel. Reviews about this institution contain a huge number of positive comments, among which are the following:

  • It is worth noting that the hotel is incredibly convenient, because the railway station is very close to it, there is a metro station, as well as a number of cultural attractions;
  • the staff is very attentive to all the requests and wishes of the guests;
  • a very beautiful panorama of the city opens from the windows of rooms located on the upper floors;
  • a very good breakfast is served in the restaurant;
  • the rooms are quite spacious, and thanks to the large windows they penetrate a lot of light;
  • very comfortable beds with orthopedic mattresses;
  • the rooms have excellent sound insulation, which is why you can relax very well, and no extraneous noise will wake you up;
  • most often the hotel has enough free rooms, and therefore you do not have to wait for the checkout time for check-in;
  • if you book a room in advance, the cost of living can be almost half cheaper;
  • The hotel rooms are clean enough.




Negative reviews

Despite the huge number of positive reviews, there are also negative comments regarding the Park Inn Nevsky Hotel (St. Petersburg). It is worth noting the following disadvantages in the work of this hotel:

  • the gym is very small, and therefore it is incredibly crowded and stuffy;
  • despite the fact that the televisions located in the rooms provide for the connection of external media, the connectors are closed with plugs;
  • despite the information stated in the advertisement and on the site, not all rooms have a refrigerator;
  • the restaurant in design and the proposed menu is more like a pub, and the prices in it are unreasonably high;
  • despite the fact that the rooms are equipped with metal-plastic double-glazed windows, they strongly allow cold air to pass through;
  • despite the information specified in the rules of residence, you will not be able to receive an order from the restaurant in your room before noon;
  • the cost of living is constantly increasing, but conditions are not improving;
  • the restaurant is open only until midnight, which is very inconvenient (it is better to visit neighboring 24-hour institutions);
  • the bathroom does not have a shower, but only a plastic screen that allows water to pass through.

General impression of the hotel

Having studied all the information about the hotel, as well as the reviews of travelers who have already managed to visit it, we can say that this is a pretty good option for accommodation in St. Petersburg. The biggest advantage of this hotel is its location. This is the central part of the city with good transport interchange. In addition, there are a lot of cultural attractions located within walking distance.

It is worth noting that this hotel is most often chosen by those people who arrived in the city for a business purpose. They even have a separate room category. They are characterized by the presence of a separate office, as well as by the good quality of the wireless Internet signal. And if your goal is to see the city in all its glory, then settle in a junior suite on the top floor. It is also recommended to book an orientation tour at the hotel‚Äôs tour desk.

Among the shortcomings of this hotel can be noted unreasonably high prices for both accommodation and related services. So, there are some malfunctions in the operation of the equipment, as well as the irrationality of the layout in the rooms. We can not say about the uncomfortable schedule of the restaurant. You will not receive an order for a room before noon, but you will not be able to relax in the evening either, because the establishment closes at midnight. We can say that such an overpricing is largely due to the advantageous location of the hotel. Therefore, if you want to live in the central part of the city, near transport hubs, you will have to turn a blind eye to some of the shortcomings in the hotel, as well as the unreasonably high cost of services.
