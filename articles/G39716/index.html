Pedigree

At the very beginning, it is worth noting that Demeter is the daughter of Rhea and Kronos, the sister of the almighty god Zeus and the goddess Hera, which puts her on a par with the most powerful and influential gods of Olympus.

Destination

The goddess Demeter in Greek mythology is considered the patroness of farmers, the mother of the fertility of the earth. According to legend, thanks to her and her daughter Persephone, the seasons change - only a mother and daughter can spend part of the year together, then summer comes on the ground. All other time, Persephone lives in the dungeon with her husband Hades, and at this time Demeter yearns and cries for her daughter, giving birth to rains, blizzards and bad weather. And only when the hour of meeting is approaching, the thaw comes, does Demeter have hope for an early meeting and spring comes.





Form

The goddess Demeter is very attractive, and her image is warm and pleasant. So, her hair is ears of ripe wheat, her face is pretty, and her body is magnificent, rich. At one time, it was these women who attracted men, so Demeter was always desired by the opposite sex. The goddess has a good character, she is calm and balanced, but with a painful sense of justice. She often brutally punished people who tried to trick her or their own kind.

Art

The goddess Demeter was praised by many poets, a huge number of legends are composed about her and paintings are painted. Often she appeared in the image of a wandering woman in search of her daughter, sometimes - sitting, surrounded by the fruits of the earth. Its main attributes are ears of corn, fertility symbols, and also a torch as a symbol of the search for the lost daughter. The fertility goddess Demeter considered the Snake and the Pig to be her sacred animal .
