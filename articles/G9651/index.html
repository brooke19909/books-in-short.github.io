Chalet "Swallow", Voronezh: description, rooms, features and reviews

Many residents of the region are familiar with the Swallow Camp. Voronezh is only thirty-five kilometers distant from it. Having overcome such a short distance, a person finds himself in an amazing corner, where in the oak tree you can often hear birds singing, where there is amazingly fresh air.





How to get there

A guide for tourists is the village of Rykan. At the exit from it there is a sign indicating that to the left is fifteen kilometers and the camp site "Swallow". Voronezh - the city is large enough and noisy, so the townspeople strive for a weekend in the countryside in order to relax comfortably and recharge their batteries. Many people choose budget recreation centers where, for minimal money, you can get a normal range of services. And one of these places is the Lastochka camp site (Voronezh). Around the oak forest. And the proximity of the stocked pond is another reason that many fans of fishing for a weekend with their family are perfect for the Lastochka camp site (Voronezh). The route to it is quite simple: you can get to it along federal highways in the directions to Rostov or Tambov. A dirt road leads directly to the base, passing through a dense forest.

Description

The Swallow Chalet (Voronezh) is located in New Usman, on the banks of a small river, which is considered one of the cleanest in the region. Guests can choose to stay in comfortable rooms of a two-story building, as well as in detached brick houses. You can come here with an overnight stay, or for a few hours to spend time in the fresh air, eat barbecue and just have fun. To do this, guests are offered to rent large gazebos designed for twenty to fifty people. On the brazier, which is included in the price, you can fry freshly caught fish.



A wide range of services is offered to guests by the Lastochka camp site (Voronezh). The address of the complex is Novousmansky District, the village of Volna-Shepelinovka. You can pre-book the houses by contacting the administration through the city office.

Infrastructure

The recreation center was built relatively recently. The houses and the main building are located on a well-groomed beautiful territory. The cleanest river flowing in the immediate vicinity is unlikely to impress fans of swimming and sunbathing. On a specially equipped beach there are all conditions. Guests can go fishing by renting a catamaran or boat, or play sports.

For children on the camp site there is an excellent playground. Fans of reading can use the library of the recreation center.

Free parking is available for guests. There is also a billiard room, a bar.



The camp site has a large banquet complex with several rooms. Here you can hold any event, including a wedding. A conference room is provided for meetings and seminars.

The highlight of the camp site are two bathhouses, equipped with a spacious veranda and a convenient barbecue. They are located directly on the banks of the river.

The staff of the recreation center "Swallow" takes care of everything related to the organization of holidays on its territory. Guests will be surprised not only with a variety of delicious dishes, but also with quality service.

Additional Information

The price includes breakfast. If desired, you can pay extra and get a comprehensive meal. The camp site is completely fenced. The entry and exit of cars from eleven in the evening to six in the morning is limited to ensure the peace of mind.

At the camp site there are two sauna cottages in which guests can steam on oak wood and swim in the river or in the ice hole in the winter months. They have an adjustable water tub, a large living room with leather furniture, a refrigerator, a kitchen with a set of dishes, a microwave, a TV, satellite TV, a DVD karaoke, billiards and two lounges. A swing is installed on the veranda.

If desired, tourists can set up a tent on the river bank for a fee, rent fishing rods, boats, a campfire. You need to pay for fishing separately. Only those who live in houses or cottages that the Lastochka camp site (Voronezh) can use barbecue facilities for free.

Prices

The cost of cottages starts at 11,000 rubles per day. The hostel offers rooms in a two-story building for the following prices: economy double - for 1500, VIP class - for three and a half thousand. The latter has two extra beds.



Five-bed rooms will cost four and a half thousand rubles, and designed for seven people - in 6000. The cost of a summer house starts at 1500 and ends at 4000 rubles. depending on the number of places. Renting a sauna cottage with a minimum period of three hours will cost from 1200 to 1500 rubles per hour.

The camp site has a flexible payment system. Here discounts are provided to regular customers, as well as to guests who buy vouchers for a long time.

Reviews

The vast majority of those who visited the camp site really liked it. Many of them come here not for the first time. The reviews emphasize the excellent work of the staff, its attentive attitude and prudence.

The guests here liked everything from the surrounding nature and the clean river to the menu. There is an excellent playground on site. Everywhere everything is tidy and clean.

Chalet "Swallow" is perfect for families and for spending weekends in a noisy big company. The reviews say a lot of positive words about the administration, which helps a lot in holding celebrations in the complex. There are, however, such tourists who did not like the dining room, which was a bit reminiscent of the Soviet one, but most vacationers themselves cooked their own food, grilled kebabs and prepared fish soup from the fish caught in the place.
