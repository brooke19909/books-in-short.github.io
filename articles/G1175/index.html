Holidays with a child in Montenegro: choose the right place

When planning a vacation with a child, parents study and evaluate a number of characteristics of the place where they are going to go with the baby. After all, the crumb should be fun, interesting and comfortable, then the parents themselves will receive a lot of pleasant impressions from the long-awaited trip. Holidays with a child in Montenegro, located in the southern part of the Adriatic, are becoming very popular among tourists. This country attracts with clean ecology and quite pleasant climate.



Holidays with a child in Montenegro

Usually the tourist season in this country lasts from May to October. The temperature rarely rises above thirty degrees, which is very good for children of any age. In this country, a huge number of resorts, amazingly clear sea and stunning beauty of nature. Holidays with a child in Montenegro will be held with the maximum benefit for the baby's health, as a comfortable mild climate dominates here. In addition, there are a lot of attractions in this country, so you are guaranteed positive emotions and a pleasant pastime.

Montenegro Vacation with children. Hotels

Montenegro resorts are ready to provide many places to stay with children with excellent conditions for recreation. The rooms are equipped with a baby cot. The restaurants have highchairs, a special menu for kids is offered. The hotels also have animators entertaining children and skilled babysitters. Some hotels are ready to offer discounts for children under 12 years old. The most recommended hotels are: Riviera 3 *, Iberostar Bellevue 4 *, Montenegro Star 4 *, Tara 4 *, Hotel Aruba, Galathea Hotel, Hotel del Mar.





City Petrovac

According to tourists, rest with a child in Montenegro is best done in this cozy place. It has very clean and comfortable pebble beaches, excellent climate, lots of greenery, stable water supply. A huge number of kids rest every year in Petrovac, so it will not be difficult for parents to find a fun company for their child. It is in this town that delicious buns are baked and the best sweets are prepared (fruit salads, ice cream, etc.). However, Petrovac has a small number of parks, attractions and carousels, which, of course, should be taken into account when planning a vacation.

Chan City

Great place for a relaxing holiday. This small town has a small but very beautiful and worthy beach. The water on the coast is clean and clear. The coastline is rocky on one side and sandy on the other.





Herceg Novi

This city will be preferred by lovers of a fun, active holiday with fairly adult children. It is famous for a huge number of beaches, both shallow sandy and deeper, suitable for diving from the pier. Herceg Novi has many cafes, boutiques, clubs and other entertainment for young people. Yachts, motorcycles, scooters - all this awaits guests of Montenegro.

Igalo

Many tourists come here annually, attracted by Montenegro. Holidays with children, the prices of which can be found at the tour operator, can become one of the most memorable events in life if you choose Igalo. There is a huge amusement park located on one of the beaches.
