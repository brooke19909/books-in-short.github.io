Kalithea Sun & Sky 3 * (Greece / Rhodes island) - photos, prices and reviews of tourists

The Greek island of Rhodes has long been a real magnet for travelers from all over the world. Tourists from the countries of the former Soviet Union are not an exception, the number of which is growing here from year to year. Rhodes attracts vacationers with a mild climate, excellent beaches, a variety of hotels - from the most economical to fashionable and an abundance of entertainment. Today we offer a closer look at one of the hotels in this resort called Kalithea Sun & Sky 3 *. We learn that this hotel complex is offered to our guests, find out what our compatriots think about the rest here, and also briefly go over the attractions that Rhodes offers tourists.





Where is the "Kallithea San & Sky" 3 *?

The 3-star Kalithea Sun & Sky Hotel is located six kilometers from Rhodes city center . The international airport is only half an hour from the hotel. Therefore, after arrival, you can quickly reach the hotel. Kalithea Sun & Sky hotel is located on the second coastline and does not have a private beach. Just seven hundred meters from the hotel there is Kallithea beach (you can walk to it in leisurely steps for 15-20 minutes), and two kilometers away - the famous geothermal springs.

Hotel Description

Kalithea Sun & Sky 3 * is a modern hotel offering its guests comfortable accommodation in cozy rooms, excellent service and a relaxed atmosphere. The hotel complex is located on the coast of the Greek island of Rhodes and is surrounded by olive groves. The hotel is positioning itself as a great place for a family holiday, especially since the price of accommodation here will delight you.

Hotel Policies

In accordance with the rules adopted in Kalithea Sun & Sky 3 * (Rhodes), check-in of newly arriving guests is carried out after two in the afternoon. If you arrived at the hotel earlier than this time, you can leave your luggage in the storage room and go for lunch, to the pool or straight to the beach. If your room has already been vacated by previous guests and removed by the hotel staff, then you will be settled immediately after arrival. On the day of departure, you must leave the hotel before noon. In this case, it is necessary to pay for accommodation and additional services (if you resorted to them). If you pre-paid a ticket to a travel agency, then on the day of departure you will need to pay only for additional services. Payment can be made in cash or with plastic cards.

Since Kalithea Sun & Sky 3 * positions itself as a family hotel, children of any age are allowed here. Moreover, if you place a child under two years old in your crib, then his stay will be free. Also, you do not have to pay extra for placing a child from two to twelve years old in your room on the beds already in it. If you need to place an older child or adult on an extra bed in your apartment, then you will have to pay 70% of the cost of one person per day for this. Please note that only one extra or cot can be put in each room. You should also notify Kalithea Sun & Sky hotel 3 * in advance of the need to provide such a service.

As for travelers who prefer to go on vacation in the company of their four-legged pets, we are forced to disappoint them. The fact is that at the Kalithea Sun & Sky Hotel, pets are strictly prohibited.





Number of rooms

The hotel complex Kalithea Sun & Sky 3 * (Greece) offers travelers to stay in one of 195 comfortable rooms. The hotel has 113 standard rooms located in the main building, as well as 46 bungalows built in the style of the Greek village, and 30 two-bedroom and family apartments. Each room has a balcony or terrace, private bathroom, air conditioning, refrigerator, satellite TV, telephone, radio, hairdryer. The hotel also provides the necessary toiletries. If necessary, for a fee, you can order room service. The rooms are cleaned daily, bed linen is changed three times a week.

Nutrition

Meals at Kalithea Sun and Sky (Rhodes) are all-inclusive. Three times a day in the hotel restaurant, experienced chefs serve a buffet consisting of various dishes of both Greek and international cuisine. In between main meals, you can grab a bite to eat at the lobby bar. Here and at the poolside bar you can enjoy a rich selection of different drinks and desserts.





Entertainment

Kalithea Sun & Sky hotel has an outdoor pool. Near it there is a sun terrace with sunbeds and umbrellas. Also, guests of the hotel complex have a tennis court, table tennis, billiards and karaoke. The youngest guests of the hotel can have fun at the playground or in the games room.

Sea beach

The closest beach to the Kalithea Sun & Sky 3 * hotel is 800 meters away. Here for a fee you can use sun loungers and umbrellas. The beach is small pebble, entry into the water is gentle and comfortable.

Infrastructure

Hotel Kalithea Sun & Sky, reviews of tourists only confirm this, has all the necessary infrastructure for a comfortable pastime. The reception at the hotel is open 24 hours a day. Therefore, in case of late check-in or an urgent problem, you will always find an administrator on duty here who will do everything possible to resolve all issues. In addition, the hotel staff speaks Russian and English, so you will not have problems in communication.

Ample parking is available on site. Therefore, if you decide to rent a car, then you will not have problems with parking. The hotel area, although it is not very large, but has a beautiful shady garden where you can take a walk. There is a playground for children.

There is also a TV room, an Internet cafe, a souvenir shop. Wi-Fi access is available in the lobby. For the convenience of guests, the hotel provides the possibility of ordering room service.

In addition, Kallithea San & Sky has a medical office. You can apply here if you feel unwell. A qualified doctor will quickly provide you with the necessary help.





Prices

Kalithea Sun & Sky (Greece) is an economical and comfortable hotel. So, the cost of living for one night in a double room per season is from 3 thousand rubles.

What to do in Rhodes?

As a rule, most tourists, arriving in Greece, try, in addition to relaxing on the beach, to devote time and various entertainments, as well as to see local attractions. We offer further information on what to do on the island of Rhodes. We also find out how much money should be taken with you on vacation.

Sights of Rhodes

One of the most famous attractions located on the Greek island of Rhodes is the Acropolis of Lindos. It was built over three thousand years ago. In addition to the acropolis itself, in Lindos you can see the ruins of the early Christian church and the walls of the knight's castle. Please note that in the summer it is very hot, so it is best to go here early in the morning or late in the evening, while remember to bring a supply of drinking water.

Rhodes Fortress deserves special attention. It was built during the Middle Ages and was intended to protect the capital of the island - the city of Rhodes from invaders. It is considered one of the longest in Europe: for example, the length of its walls is four kilometers.

Wildlife lovers are advised to visit the Butterfly Valley, which is a unique nature reserve. This attraction is located thirty kilometers from the city of Rhodes.

An interesting place to visit is Rodini Park. It is considered one of the oldest parks on the planet. This landscape park was erected by the ancient Romans. It is interesting that already in those days a water supply system was built here. Also on the territory of Rodini is the tomb of Ptolemy I.
