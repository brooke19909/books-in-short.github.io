Car vacuum cleaner Black & Decker: reviews

Vacuum cleaners have long been integrated into the everyday sphere of human life, so it is impossible to imagine the existence of a mistress without such a useful device. Many now do not understand how people used to do without such an undoubted assistant. Any cleaning will become much easier if a person has a vacuum cleaner. Especially when he is able to cope with inaccessible places. The dust behind furniture and carpets will be fearless. Manufacturers delight customers with dozens of new models, introducing various technologies that significantly optimize the operation of the device.

Of course, Black Decker car vacuum cleaners do not go unnoticed. If before, in order to clean cars in the cabin, a rather large amount of time and effort had to be spent, now you can use an unusual household vacuum cleaner, namely a car vacuum cleaner, to clean rugs, seats and many hard-to-reach places. It will reduce the time of cleaning the car and will please the maximum result.



Black & Decker

This company was founded at the beginning of the 20th century in the state of Maryland. Two young businessmen opened a machine shop. Then they could not even imagine that their company would become one of the most reputable and recognized leaders in the sales market. In order to achieve excellence in this activity, the manufacturer completely plunged into the work. Everyone understands that it is quite difficult to get success, trying to achieve it in several areas at once, so the founders decided to choose three main areas.

At the moment, the company is creating automobile, garden vacuum cleaners, as well as household appliances. Owners of equipment distinguish large lists of advantages. It is about functionality, small dimensions, as well as ergonomics. A car vacuum cleaner can run on battery power for about 30 minutes. This time is enough for cleaning vehicles. The company offers several fairly popular Black Decker car vacuum cleaners for cars. But the article will focus on the PAV1205, AV 1205, PAD 1200, ADV 1210/1220.

You may notice that many obsolete models are quickly discontinued, and more modern products are replacing them. These devices should be purchased in company stores or on the Internet. Service centers operate in many cities around the world. In order not to resort to their help, it is recommended that before making a transaction pay attention to the comments of those people who have long used the chosen model.

Buyers also highlight certain disadvantages that should be clarified. They indicate that all models have a small garbage tank, because of this you have to constantly clean it. The suction rates, although not bad for cleaning in the car, but when compared with household appliances, in fact they are very low. Description of models

The line of instruments for cleaning cars contains about ten relevant Black Decker car vacuum cleaners. Reviews of popular models can be read further. All of them work either from the on-board system or from the battery. Many car owners give positive feedback on the use of certain devices. Consider specific models.

Black Decker ADV1200

Car vacuum cleaner Black Decker ADV1200 is one of the most expensive in the range. You can buy it for $ 120. A car cigarette lighter is used as power. The dust collector has a volume of 0.5 l. Best of all, this device, as advised by the owners, is used for dry cleaning. In the kit, buyers say in the comments, there is a crevice nozzle and a special combined brush. The hose got a length of 1 m. In the reviews they write that this model is distinguished by good ergonomics. Also included is a bag for storage.



Black Decker PAV 1205

The next pretty good model, which sells a little cheaper, is the Black Decker PAV 1205 car vacuum cleaner. You can buy it for $ 85. Powered by a cigarette lighter. Several nozzles are included. The volume of the dust bag, according to buyers, is small. It has a capacity of 0.35 liters. It is stored in a special bag.



Black Decker ADV1220

A good model of a vacuum cleaner is the ADV1220. It is used, as buyers write, for dry cleaning. Works also from the cigarette lighter. Has an elongated nozzle. The volume of the dust bag is 0.6 liters. The comments of the owners say that the kit includes a brush, a bag, as well as a crevice nozzle. Among the advantages, a transparent flask is noted, which allows you to track how much the container is filled during cleaning. You can buy this model for $ 60.



Black Decker ADV1210

The next model in the review is the ADV1210 vacuum cleaner. He received the largest container volume, according to buyers. Tare 0.9 liters. Included is a crevice nozzle and brush. As well as the above devices, it is used for dry cleaning. You can buy for only $ 40. The Black Decker 1205 car vacuum cleaner model has approximately the same functionality, but the container volume is only 0.5 liters.
