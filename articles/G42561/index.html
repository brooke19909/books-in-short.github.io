iPhone 3G and the white screen of death

Today, Apple is still the trendsetter in mobile fashion and continues to surprise its fans with new technical developments in the field of electronic devices.

Nothing lasts forever, or the Path to Perfection



Despite the quality and high performance of Apple products, some happy iPhone owners sometimes go to service centers. The white screen of the smartphone is one of the main reasons to resort to the help of microelectronics specialists. The ergonomic design of the iPhone 3G smartphone is still unprotected from standard situations associated primarily with a fall on a hard surface and exposure to water. Let's see what is the main reason for the sometimes unexpected situation when the white screen of the iPhone hides the realism of the original palette of colors?

The white screen can tell a lot!

To understand why your favorite phone has decided that white is perfect for its 3.5-inch display, you should pay special attention to how it displays this symbolically innocent color. Having made a simple diagnosis, you can easily answer the question of why your iPhone 3G has a white screen.

So, the main typical malfunctions and causes of the appearance of white light :

  • A fairly fragile HVGA display matrix at the time of impact, critical pressure or the exerted force of the deforming property is likely to blur in the unimaginably chaotic mess of destroyed LCD pixels. In rare cases, if the impact did not have sufficient fracture strength, a luminous white screen will appear.
  • Violation of the general algorithm of the device and software failure can also be the cause of the "eternally" white display.
  • Loop elements and connector pads are most susceptible to causeless failure. The spontaneous moment of loss of the transmitted signal may be due to wear of poor-quality coating of conductive tracks.
  • Moisture and condensation are the most common reasons why a white void conquers the screen. An irreversible and sometimes fatal contact oxidation process can completely disable your phone.
  • Failure of a microcircuit, loop elements or parts of a filtering type of action, the result is a white screen of the phone. There are more than enough reasons for this, most likely the intensive operation of the device with forced battery charging.

What to do, because there are so many things ?!

Do not shout "Guard!" Most likely, you have synchronized your phone with a PC many times. Connect your smartphone to the computer and try to copy valuable information. In case of unsuccessful attempt, do not worry ahead of time, the service center will definitely help you. Moreover, the replacement of the display and the accompanying diagnosis of the iPhone with such symptoms of malfunction are urgently needed.

You should always remember: if water and its derivatives get in contact with a specialist workshop immediately. Since liquid is the worst enemy and an incredibly serious threat to your smartphone. Delaying a visit to a service center is guaranteed to create additional problems. Therefore, it is always worth listening to wise advice!
