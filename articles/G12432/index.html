Bay of the Sea of ​​Japan, Anna (bay), Primorsky Territory. Best places to stay

Anna Bay is located near the village of the same name in the Primorsky Territory. It is part of the most picturesque bay of the Sea of ​​Japan, called the Bay of Peter I. It consists of several small bays and due to the fact that the water area is half-closed, there are no big and high waves. The coastline will delight tourists with both pebble and sandy beaches. And from the vegetation, oaks and small shrubs are often found here. For vacationers with children there is no better place than Anna (bay). Primorsky Krai is a region that is located in the most favorable area for spending summer holidays. The climate here is quite mild and has a comfortable rest. The water in the bay is always clean and calm.



Housing

The village, which is located near the bay, is small, but on its territory there is a huge number of recreation centers. Those who want to save a little on accommodation can rent a room or a house with local residents. The landscapes amaze with their beauty, small hills strewn with verdure spread around the village. The rich vegetation and the absence of a large flow of cars are evidence that clean air reigns in the bay.

sights

What will surprise tourists Anna (bay)? Primorsky Krai has many interesting places. The main attraction of this region is the island of Trambetskoy, where the famous lonely lighthouse is located. Another interesting feature is that in the eastern part of the bay two sunken ships rise slightly above the water.

The Five Fingers Natural Landmark is a sharp cliff that rises in the depths of the sea.

Those who are keen on fauna can visit the Gulf of Vostok Marine Sanctuary or go to the dolphinarium. If the goal is to engage in active water recreation, then you can find it by visiting Lake Livadia. Here tourists can rent jet skis, water skis and other attractions on the water.





Recreation facilities

Among the inhabitants of Russia, Anna (Bay) is especially popular. Primorsky Krai is visited by a large number of tourists from year to year. The reason for this is the cleanest air, calm bay and, of course, the arrangement of the coastal territory. In the bay, in addition to recreation centers, there are children's camps. Lovers to organize vacations themselves set up campgrounds.

The most popular recreation center for tourists is the “27th region”. The range of numbers is presented from 2 - to 4-seater. Some rooms have sea views. To entertain guests, a team of animators works, and water equipment rental is offered. Here you can order three meals a day, characterized by a satiety of dishes.

Anna Bay (Primorsky Krai), whose photo is striking in its splendor, will delight even the most demanding tourists. They are recommended to visit the base "Hope". It offers a high level of service. On its territory there is a guarded parking, rental of barbecue areas. You can include buffet meals in the price. It organizes excursions to the main attractions of the region.

The Five Fingers recreation center of the same name with a beautiful natural attraction offers tourists a rest in houses made of natural wood. From each room you can clearly see the part of Peter I Bay, called Anna. The bay (Primorsky Territory) in this place is fully equipped. There are hotels, Internet and telephone services. Vacationing tourists can not only enjoy beautiful views, but also sunbathe on the sandy beach, go fishing or mushrooms in the neighboring forest.





Cost

The local vacation prices are quite reasonable. Both families with children and young couples can afford to travel on a bay coast. The cost of living varies from 700 to 2000 rubles. per day per person. According to the reviews of tourists, one can judge that silence, grooming and cleanliness dominate in the territories of recreation centers, beautiful flowers and fancy trees are planted everywhere. A friendly staff will provide a comfortable stay.





For those who want to combine a pleasant stay, positive emotions and magnificent landscapes, Anna Bay (Primorsky Territory), reviews of which are only positive, will be the most suitable place. After all, the services here are at the level of resorts abroad. Really worth a trip here. This is due to the fact that the cost of staying is very interesting. And children in the bay will not only be fun, but also safe, as calmness always prevails in the bay.
