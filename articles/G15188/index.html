Desire Tree - take a step towards fulfilling desires. How to make a wish tree?

It is human nature to dream and make plans for the future. Without this, it is impossible to live fully, because then there will be nothing to strive for. At all times, people have been looking for ways in which their desires will be fulfilled quickly and accurately. Tales and legends are a true confirmation of this, in them there is always a place for miracles that occur with the assistance of a magical thing. Today it has become popular to have a wish tree, which helps to fulfill all our plans. There are many options for creating it, and in this article we will consider the most popular of them.





DIY wish tree

The easiest way is to make such a tree using a simple drawing technique. To do this, prepare the following materials: paper, colored pens, felt-tip pens or pencils. Then draw a beautiful plant with large fruits or leaves that need to be left clean - do not shade. On them (leaves or fruits) you need to write all your most cherished dreams and wait for them to come true. But some lucky people are still advised to take feasible steps to implement what was conceived, then desires are fulfilled faster. You can use ready-made projects - a wish tree, the template of which is presented below, is perfect for such purposes.





Holiday tree

On holidays, one especially believes in miracles, which is why it was at this time that many traditional rituals were held to improve well-being. Today, a wonderful tree is becoming an integral attribute of any celebration. It is created for a birthday, making a frame of the simplest materials, such as paper or wire, and the leaves are replaced with rhinestones, beads or sparkling jewelry. In the New Year, such a tree is replaced with a traditional Christmas tree, also tying ribbons on it with a description of your dream. A wedding wish tree can be drawn by replacing it with a traditional guest wish book. The simplest technique for performing such a masterpiece is presented below.





Stages of creating a wedding tree

  1. It is necessary to prepare a paper or canvas, its size will depend on the number of those present at the celebration. If there are too many invited, then you can make several blanks, for example: for relatives, for friends, for colleagues, etc.
  2. Apply a bright sketch of the plant to the base, filling the rest of the space with images of the sun, flowers, birds, rainbows, etc. The picture should be very positive and kind. You can entrust such a responsible matter to professional artists, because the created exclusive masterpiece will be stored in the family archive for a long time.
  3. Prepare containers with paint (better than a smaller volume, but more units) or stamp pads, as well as wet wipes or towels. It is better to choose safe paints in order to avoid allergic reactions in guests.
  4. Ask all participants in the wedding to leave a print of their palm or finger on the sketch - in the places of the alleged leaves or fruits (depending on the theme of the picture). If guests leave a fingerprint, it is better to choose light shades of any color so that the inscription can be distinguished.
  5. On the palm print, all present write wishes, and next to the outline of the finger they leave only their names.
  6. After completing this ceremony, which should take place with the appropriate music and be accompanied by the comments of the host, the sketch is framed and given to the young.

Real instances

In many countries, there are living trees that help people - fulfill their desires. Usually these are powerful and beautiful plants located in a park or resort area. Shreds with cherished desires are tied on their branches , and money is thrown to the roots so that the plan is fulfilled faster. Many make pilgrimages to such sacred places, believing that this is a more effective way to bring the fulfillment of their desires closer. The most famous of them are described below.

  • Money oak in Scotland, on the island of Marie. In his bark, people put small coins and ask the tree to fulfill its plan more quickly.
  • Perhaps the oldest wish tree is in India. Its dimensions are so large that a dozen adult men cannot grasp it. Multi-colored ribbons are tied on the branches of this giant, and not only coins, but also small figurines can be seen in its bark!
  • A tree grows in the Maldives , to which those who wish to have a child come. It is so ancient that it is impossible to distinguish roots from branches on which colored shreds are tied.

You can create a wonderful tree in your garden or apartment, choosing the most healthy and strong plant for this. It is believed that such a tree helps and serves as a talisman for all family members.

Useful advice

The desire tree should always have roots, as they symbolize the strength of the plant and its connection with the energy of the earth. If this rule is neglected, then the fulfillment of desire may be delayed for a long time or never fulfilled. After all, if there are no roots, then where to get the power to implement the plan?
