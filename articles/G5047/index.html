Upper Dzhemete: rest in the Russian south

Upper Dzhemete is a resort village in the Anapa region. It is famous for its wide beaches with sand, as well as a gentle entrance to the water. Therefore, rest here is very popular among families who come to improve the health of young children. Its name is translated from the Adyghe language as "Golden Sands". The village is far from noisy discos and clubs. It is quiet, calm. It is suitable for lovers of relaxation in the lap of nature, especially since the lush and picturesque vegetation of the Caucasus in these places is especially pleasing to the eye. Let's see how you can spend your vacation here. You will find practical tips below.



How to get and when to go

To get to Verkhnyaya Dzhemete, you need to come to the railway station of Anapa. It starts right behind the station. The village is located on the long Pionersky Prospekt, on which buses and minibuses often go. Administratively, it is part of Anapa. It takes ten minutes to get to the city center by buses numbered 114 and 128. On the other side, seven kilometers away, there is another famous resort - Vityazevo. The climate in the village is almost the same as in neighboring Anapa. Upper Dzhemete. The temperature of air and water is almost the same - +25 and, accordingly, + 23-24 degrees Celsius, so we can assume that the velvet season reigns here constantly. But it's better to come here in June and September. Fewer people and cheaper. In July and August, prices become even higher than in Anapa, and the sea is covered with algae.



Where to live

In the village of Upper Dzhemete, the private sector is well represented, as well as guesthouses and mini-hotels. These are the main types of housing that are rented to tourists. Prices in the village are cheaper than in Anapa, and with early booking you can count on significant discounts. The first recreation centers appeared here in the middle of the last century. And since then, the popularity of the resort is only growing. Almost all housing here is a five-minute walk from the sea, no more. The private sector is mainly economy rooms and rooms. And guest houses and mini-hotels are increasingly providing standard conditions. One can also find deluxe rooms here. Housing in Upper Jamet is divided into first and second lines. It all depends on which side of Pioneer Avenue is the hotel or guest house. If between the sea and the main street of the village - this is the first line. If behind the Prospectus - the second.

Where to eat and how to have fun

Along the Pioneer Avenue in Upper Dzhemet, a number of dining rooms and cafes lined parallel to the sea. Some of them have discos in the evenings. You can eat in the dining rooms of various boarding houses and recreation centers. And also a tourist train runs around the village, with which you can go around all the most picturesque surroundings. Anapa with its boisterous entertainment is reached not only by bus. This trip itself can be turned into a pleasant walk if you go there by boat on the sea. For almost ten years now, a family-type water park "Tiki-Tak" has been opened in the village. There is a special complex of slides designed for children. And not far from it is an amusement park.
