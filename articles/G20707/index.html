Tariff "MTS Tablet" - customer reviews. Tariff Plan Features

Agree that having convenient, fast mobile Internet at your fingertips is always very nice. You can easily read your favorite news, go to e-mail, keep abreast of all updates from friends on social networks and just have fun in any way possible. You can do this by simply connecting your tablet to a reliable WiFi network.

However, the question arises of what to do if there are no wireless Internet distribution points nearby, but it is very necessary to enter the network? There is only one way out - high-speed mobile Internet, which is available in the MTS Tablet tariff plan. Feedback from users connected to this service indicates a fairly good Internet speed at this rate and, in addition, a reasonably acceptable cost of services.

What is a plan like?





So, the MTS Tablet option (reviews about it can be found on many Internet resources) is available to all subscribers of the largest Russian mobile operator of the same name. It is designed, as the name suggests, for users who are actively working with tablet computers. This is also confirmed by the characteristics that the tariff plan boasts - a large amount of traffic, the ability to watch TV in unlimited quantities, as well as a fairly high speed (with it, “MTS Tablet” - reviews confirm this repeatedly - allows you to access various multimedia entertainment without delay and in the best quality).

To connect the option to your device, it’s enough to have the “MTS” starter pack (it costs 410 rubles, according to the information on the official website) and get the settings (the operator sends them automatically). Further, using special combinations, you can connect the service to your tablet.

Tariffs and prices





As for the cost of such pleasure as the unlimited speed Internet on the tablet, then it is equal to 400 rubles per month. For this amount, the user receives a fixed quota of 4 gigabytes, as well as unlimited the ability to watch television on his tablet. At the same time, the tariff is valid throughout the country where the signal is available - this means that you can enter your favorite online game or social network page even while on the road. Imagine what level of mobility the MTS Tablet tariff can provide, reviews of which are mostly flattering.

“MTS Tablet Mini” as an alternative to a full tariff

If you are the Internet user who will have enough visits to Vkontakte once a day and check mail, and you don’t need to spend gigabytes of traffic, but are ready to be content with small ones, you may use a different tariff from MTS with the prefix “Mini”.

As the name implies, it is designed for less demanding users, since this tariff plan provides less traffic. So, for free, MTS is ready to provide its subscribers with 500 megabytes per month (up to 17 MB per day), and if it is spent, it is expected that the provided package will increase in accordance with the trend - by 10-20 rubles per day for a certain number of megabytes (50, 100, 500, 1000, etc.). Thus, it turns out this way: if you want to sit more - pay accordingly.





How to connect?

Connecting to any tariff plan is quite simple. For this, it is necessary, firstly, to have a sufficient amount of funds on the balance sheet (for the MTS Tablet plan, if you don’t lie, you must have 400 or more rubles); and secondly, you need to type the correct key combination. For the “Mini” package, this is * 885 #, while the basic “Tablet” is connected by dialing * 835 #.

After entering these commands, you will receive the appropriate SMS messages notifying you of the imminent transition to the tariff plan and requiring confirmation of this action.

Please note that not all MTS users can switch to this tariff. Those with the MTS Connect-4 package can go to “Mini”, and a much wider list of subscribers (“Onliner”, “Caring”, “Cool” and others) can go to the basic one. A lot has been written on how to switch to these tariffs on the forums. User reviews are mostly positive, since the transition is simple and in accordance with the instructions that the operator sends to everyone.

How to watch from “MTS” TV on a tablet? Reviews and recommendations





Another “feature” of this tariff is the ability to watch television for free (on the Mini package they charge 8 rubles per day for this). But you need to understand what options for viewing on the “MTS” TV on the tablet (the reviews practically do not mention this) you need to configure.

The instructions are on the official portal of the operator, which describes the step-by-step setup process. It says that first you need to install a special application, and then dial * 111 * 720 #. In the program itself, you can choose which channel you want to watch.

Another option is to buy a computer issued by a mobile operator. So, in the assortment of the company there is a tablet “MTS 1078” (reviews about it indicate that this is a budget device with all the settings for both using the Internet and watching TV). The gadget will be convenient for all those who still do not have a tablet, but who would like to get a source of entertainment at an affordable price.

.

Lifting restrictions





In order not to exceed the allocated traffic limit and not to pay 10 rubles per day for each additionally allocated package (since in the end it can be quite expensive), MTS offers the possibility of removing restrictions in large sizes by receiving large packages.

For example, the “MTS Tablet” tariff (which, as has already been emphasized, has been left by many people) implies an expansion of 500 MB and 2 GB. To do this, it is enough to pay an additional 75 or 200 rubles and make the necessary request (* 167 # and * 168 #). The package will be issued to you for a month.

So, about the MTS Tablet tariff, people’s reviews speak of an affordable and fairly high-quality product that allows us to make our life more convenient. This plan can turn your tablet into a real entertainment center for a moderate fee. Another plus is that this service is offered by the largest Russian mobile operator, whose network covers the entire territory of the country.
