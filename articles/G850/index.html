Hotel Royal Seginus Hotel 5 * Turkey, Antalya: photos, reviews

Elegant apartments, a high level of service and an amazing park ensemble from the pools, will allow each guest to plunge into the world of incredible emotions and heavenly delights.

Beach

The Royal Seginus Hotel is located on the second coastline, 250 meters from the coast. Private sand and pebble beach is equipped with a sufficient number of sun loungers with soft bedding on them. Soak up the hot sun while enjoying a cocktail from the beach bar, accessible to everyone. For lovers of shadow space there are umbrellas that can protect everyone. Towels are provided free of charge.

A gradual increase in the depth of the Mediterranean Sea allows kids to frolic and swim aground. Attentive rescuers monitor the order and safety of swimmers. The water surface is unusually transparent and crystal clear.

A wooden pontoon was built, the path to which is lined with large flowerpots with flowers. It is equipped with everything that contributes to relaxation and enjoyment: sunbeds, awnings, a bar and descent into the water in the form of steps.

Changing cabins, showers and toilets are part of the beach infrastructure.





Entertainment at the depths of the sea

For those who want to actively relax during breaks between races, it is proposed to play volleyball on the equipped platform. Every day, everyone who wants to make new acquaintances, to socialize, improve their health, and have fun gathers here.

The list of water attractions includes riding a banana and jet skis, parachuting behind a motor boat, snorkeling, windsurfing and diving. Fascinating, and sometimes extreme sports will give unforgettable emotions, enthusiastic impressions and a portion of adrenaline to everyone.
