Hotels in Voronezh: overview, addresses, reviews

Voronezh is a large Russian city, the sights of which tourists from different regions of Russia come to see. In this huge village there are several worthy hotels in which tourists staying in the city for different purposes can stay for temporary residence. Consider the following a list of the most frequently recommended places.

"Ramada Plaza"

According to tourists, the best location can boast of the Ramada Plaza Hotel. It is located in the central part of the city, in the building of the largest shopping and entertainment complex - Voronezh City Center.

All rooms offered to the attention of the guests are equipped with the best furniture, as well as modern appliances. Each apartment has a well-equipped bathroom with bath amenities. Guests of the Ramada Plaza rooms have the opportunity to use free Wi-Fi throughout the hotel.

There is a large restaurant in the Ramada Plaza building, in the hall of which the hotel guests dine. Worthy breakfasts are served from this restaurant for guests. In addition, there is a fitness room, as well as a gift shop. In the event of any problems, persons living in the hotel rooms have the opportunity to receive advice from administrators working at the 24-hour reception.

The cost of living at the Ramada Plaza Hotel is from 4,500 rubles per day for the entire room. The hotel is located at 36A Ordzhonikidze Street.

Benefit Plaza

Another upscale place for temporary residence is Benefit Plaza - a large hotel located 15 minutes from the city center. Within walking distance from the location of this hotel is Moskovsky Prospekt - a place where you can take a walk in the company of family, a loved one or friends.

The hotel building has a large restaurant, in the premises of which a buffet is served every morning for guests. This establishment and bar, which is also available at Benefit Plaza, are open around the clock. In the warmer months, the hotel has a sun terrace.

As for the apartments presented to the attention of the guests, they are all decorated in a classic style. As guests of the hotel note in their reviews, a minimal furniture set made from natural materials was used to decorate and decorate the premises. The overall picture is complemented by decor from fabrics, as well as stucco moldings and original ornaments on the walls. All types of apartments are equipped with modern climate control equipment, Wi-Fi access and satellite TV.

The cost of renting a room at the Benefit Plaza Hotel is from 3,500 rubles. Such a price by guests is considered acceptable and very appropriate. Moreover, the price includes a daily breakfast, as well as a transfer of tourists from the place of arrival to the hotel.

Benefit Plaza Hotel is located in Voronezh at 29 Vladimir Nevsky Street.

"Windmills"

When reviewing hotels in Voronezh, you should definitely pay attention to a great place to stay called "Windmills". According to the residents of the city, this boutique hotel is a rather unique place for temporary residence, staying in which you can feel like a real king. Here, the apartments are not alike - each room is decorated in its own unique style. To create a common picture, modern and only high-quality stylish furniture is used.

As for the other features of this hotel, among them it should be noted the presence of a helipad on the roof of the Vetryakov building, as well as private parking on the territory of the boutique hotel. In addition to all this, the hotel has a large and luxurious restaurant, which organizes a daily reception for guests, presented in the form of a buffet - a morning visit to this place is included in the room rental price.

The prices for the daily rent of a room in the Vetryakov boutique hotel start from 4000 rubles.

The hotel is located in Voronezh, on the Moscow highway, 62A.

Degas

Hotel "Degas" in Voronezh is a place that is designed for a relaxing and comfortable stay. It is located at 65A Pyatnitskogo Street, within walking distance of Koltsovsky Square and Lenin Square - places where you can take a walk in good company at any time of the day.

Guests of the Degas Hotel note in their comments that all of its rooms are very beautifully decorated and that they are very bright. All apartments in the hotel have a bathroom equipped with everything necessary for maintaining hygiene. In the room of any category, except for modern furniture, there is a plasma TV connected to the satellite broadcasting system, as well as a minibar and air conditioning.

With regard to additional amenities, the hotel "Degas" has a separate restaurant, a conference room, as well as a spa, which provides relaxing and beauty services.

The minimum cost for a standard room at the Degas Hotel is 3300 rubles.

Valeri Classic

Considering the list of the best hotels in Voronezh (Russia), you should definitely pay attention to the place for rest with the name Valeri Classic. According to tourists, this is where you can spend time calmly, enjoying the surrounding positive atmosphere, as well as a decent vacation.

Hotel Valeri Classic offers its guests small, but rather bright rooms, equipped with everything necessary for a high-class holiday guests. All apartments have modern appliances, a plasma TV, new furniture, and a private bathroom.

There is an Italian restaurant in the building, as well as a conference room. Valeri Classic guests can use the parking facilities located on site if necessary.

The cost of renting a room in Valeri Classic is recognized by tourists as acceptable - the price per day is 3300 rubles. According to vacationers, the conditions offered in this place are much better than those presented in other hotels in Voronezh for the same money.

Valeri Classic is located at Moscow Avenue 109A.

"Italy"

"Italy" is a hotel that is famous throughout the city for its simple but very comfortable rooms, quality service, as well as low prices for accommodation.

Guests of this hotel note in their reviews that, while staying here, you can plunge into the world of simplicity and comfort, admiring the ingenious interior of the room, represented by a combination of white and beige colors. Almost all the furniture installed in the apartments is made of wood, which gives the overall picture a certain nobility.

For the convenience of guests on the territory of the hotel in question there is a large spa, laundry, restaurant, billiard room, and also a hammam. A conference room is also available for rent.

The minimum cost for renting a room at the Italy Hotel is 3,000 rubles, which is considered an acceptable indicator for most city guests.

The Italy Hotel is located in Voronezh, at 69 Saco and Vanzetti Street.

Voronezh

All those who, having arrived in Voronezh, have a desire to relax inexpensively, but in decent conditions and with decent service, are recommended to check into the Voronezh Hotel.

Voronezh offers a small number of rooms, the cost of living in which starts from 2500 rubles. All apartments presented in it are comfortable, they have everything that is necessary for a comfortable stay for two or three. Guests note that, thanks to well-organized furniture, the rooms of the Voronezh Hotel can be productive, because each of them has a desk and Wi-Fi access.

Within walking distance from the hotel in question there is a wide selection of shops with different types of goods, as well as entertainment venues. In the building of the hotel there is a restaurant where not only hotel guests eat, but also many residents of the city.

Hotel "Voronezh" is located in Voronezh at the address: Plekhanovskaya street, 10A.

"Azimuth"

Hotel "Azimut" is one of the best places in the city intended for temporary recreation. Many residents and tourists note that from the general list of hotels in Voronezh, Azimut is famous for its great popularity. The hotel provides decent conditions for spending different types of guests.

So, in the apartments of the hotel in question, everything is decorated in a traditional classical style using modern furniture, as well as expensive fabrics and accessories. The overall design is done in warm colors, which is very popular with guests. Each apartment has a large plasma screen, a desk, a bed, a pair of nightstands, a wardrobe, and air conditioning. Moreover, all guests of this hotel are entitled to free Wi-Fi.

There is a large restaurant in the building of the Azimut Hotel. Within walking distance from the hotel there are shops, as well as entertainment venues.

The cost of daily rent of a room starts from 3000 rubles. Located "Azimuth" at the address: Plekhanovskaya street, 9.

Hampton by hilton

Hampton by Hilton is one of the best representatives of hotels in Voronezh. The description of this building proves that it is one of the best places to relax for both tourists and local residents. Staying here, you can enjoy the comfort created in bright rooms, as well as comfort. All apartments have modern appliances, as well as a bathroom with bath amenities and a hairdryer.

There is a bar in the building, guests can also visit the local restaurant, which organizes daily breakfasts for guests. In addition, there is a gym on one floor of the building, which hotel guests can also visit.

The cost of living at the Hampton by Hilton hotel is from 4000 rubles. This vacation spot is located at: Voronezh, Donbasskaya street, 12B.

Mercure

What do tourists say in their reviews of hotels in Voronezh? Many say that Mercure is one of the best places to stay. Tourists who stay here have the opportunity to easily get to anywhere in the city using public transport.

The room capacity of Mercure is quite diverse, it consists of bright and well-equipped apartments. Each of them is decorated in a marine style. The decoration has a beautiful stucco, as well as original accessories. All apartments are equipped with plasma TVs, minibars, bath amenities and hairdryers.

In the building of the hotel there are two restaurants, as well as a bar. The daily cost of living in a hotel room starts from 4200 rubles.

Located Mercure at the address: Kirova street, 6A.

Premier

Inexpensive, but decent rest can be organized at the Premier Hotel, located in Voronezh, on Bakuninsky Lane.

The room fund of this place for rest offers guests well-equipped and equipped apartments, the interior of which is decorated in bright colors. Each room, regardless of category, is air-conditioned and has a private bathroom. If desired, guests can use a plasma TV and refrigerator.

The hotel has its own restaurant, which serves a daily continental breakfast, which is included in the room rate.
