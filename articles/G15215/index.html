What are the different types of insurance services. How much do you know?

In addition, a considerable number of organizations in this industry contribute to the integration of such services in all service sectors. What does it give? Different types of insurance services are getting closer to the end customer.

Consider the most popular of them, having previously divided all types into groups.





Personal insurance

The first group includes:

  1. Life insurance. A person can conclude a contract for a certain amount. With this form, the most popular is cumulative insurance. Using this principle, the policyholder makes deductions monthly. Gradually, therefore, a good amount accumulates. If stipulated by the contract, then the company can still charge interest on this amount, that is, it is a kind of deposit that will be paid to the family of the insured person in case of his death.

  2. From accidents and illnesses. If you are asked, name the various types of insurance services that you know. And do not forget about insurance, which is used by all large organizations, trying to reduce the risk of loss, due to absenteeism or an accident at work. This species is popular both for personal needs and commercial. People try to protect themselves in the case when, due to circumstances beyond their control, they cease to be capable and make money. There are such types of professions when it will be difficult for a person to protect himself from accidents and diseases: miners, aircraft pilots, military and others. The most important thing is that before signing the contract, undergo a medical examination and indicate its results in the questionnaire. Otherwise, in the event of an insurance event, the company may simply refuse to pay you, referring to the fact that you have hidden your illnesses from it.

  3. Health insurance. Considering the types of insurance services in Russia, we can conclude that specifically this is not popular in the Russian Federation. Moreover, it is mandatory in some countries of the world. Monthly deductions in the form of insurance payments are implied.

Property insurance

In the second group, the following types are distinguished:

  • Ground transportation insurance. The most popular of this type are full and partial hull insurance. With the design of CASCO, people can have problems. Firstly, it is very expensive, and not all cars will do, since insurance companies set their age and cost limits for a car. If the vehicle is older (mostly not taken if more than 7 years old) or too expensive, then not all insurers will want to conclude an agreement with you on their services.

  • Air transport protection. Since air transport is not so common, not all insurers undertake to give financial guarantees for such a vehicle. The amount of payments under air transport insurance contracts is much higher than in the case of automobile insurance.

  • Water transport insurance. Basically, this type is made out by ship owners who want to protect themselves in the event of its collapse.

  • Cargo protection. It is a very popular type of service that is actively used in freight transportation. It is very important to be sure that in case of unsuccessful transportation of valuable cargo, the insurance company will assume obligations and pay compensation.
