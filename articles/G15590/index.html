Insurance company "Renaissance": reviews on CASCO

Renaissance is one of the leaders in the Russian insurance market. The most popular services are property protection, health and car insurance. Reviews about the Renaissance insurance company are left by both satisfied customers and those who do not like the services of the insurer.

About company

The Renaissance began its activities in 1997. The company immediately became one of the most significant structures in the insurance business. At the beginning of its activities, its assets amounted to 2.2 billion rubles, and 20 years later, in 2017, their size doubled and amounted to 4.4 billion rubles.

During the existence of the company, several subsidiary members of the Renaissance Insurance group were created:

  1. Medical insurance company "Medkorp".
  2. "Renaissance Life" is an insurer specializing in protecting the life and health of clients.
  3. Foundation of NPF Renaissance Life & Pensions. The fund was later sold.

According to reviews of the Renaissance insurance company, the insurer in 2017 merged with one of the largest non-state pension funds in Russia - NPF Blagosostoyanie. They created a joint project, Baring Vostok also acted as a partner.

How to buy a hull with a 50% discount?

According to reviews of CASCO in the Renaissance insurance company, it follows that the policy is available for customers to order with a discount of up to 50%. Buy a policy at a special price can only drivers who do not risk driving. This requires the installation of the unique SafeDrive app.

The application allows you to track the nature of the customer‚Äôs driving in order to take into account the possibilities of obtaining a discount. Before installing the application, the policyholder must purchase a CASCO policy with a franchise of 15 thousand rubles. A deductible is the amount by which the insurance company reduces the payment in the event of an insured event.

After purchasing a policy and installing SafeDrive, the client must earn the trust of Renaissance. Based on the results of driving, the app will evaluate the driver‚Äôs skills using a 10-point system.

The assessment will reduce the deductible to 100%. If the driver received from 7.5 to 10 points, a deductible of 15 thousand rubles will be completely canceled. With a ride rating of 6.5-7.4 points, the deductible will be reduced by 50%.

Additional savings on CASCO

More than 2/3 of drivers are insured under CASCO with a 5% discount. These are those who do not include more than 3 clients in their insurance. The reviews about the Renaissance insurance company describe other ways to save.

Neat drivers deserve a 10% discount. Prior to the sale of the CASCO policy, the company studies the insurance history of customers under the CTP policy. Those who have not been the initiator of an accident for 1 year or more are given a discount.

For drivers who have previously entered into a CASCO insurance contract with another company, discounts of 20 or 30% are provided. Features of car insurance in the Renaissance

According to reviews of the Renaissance Insurance insurance company, the client can independently regulate the terms of insurance under CASCO. This is very convenient, since there is no need to pay for options that are not of interest to the insured. In addition, the choice of risks allows to reduce the cost of hull insurance up to 70%.

What can be included in CASCO from Renaissance:

  • Damage protection. A feature of insurance is not just payment for technical repairs, but the ability to receive a refund in cash if the car is significantly damaged. This applies to cases when as a result of an accident the car cannot be restored. Money is paid in an amount not exceeding insurance coverage.
  • Payment as a result of damage to the car without the possibility of restoring the vehicle. If the damage exceeds 75% of the amount of insurance coverage, the client receives a refund. In case of minor damage insurance is not paid.
  • Vehicle theft insurance.
  • Protection of additional equipment. Paid for parts, devices that do not belong to the standard set of vehicle release from the manufacturer. It can be a radio or a video recorder, for example.
  • Liability insurance. Unlike CTP, CASCO in Renaissance allows you to protect a client in the amount of up to 2.5 million rubles.
  • GAP insurance. The service is available only for cars no later than the previous year of release. In the event of an insured event, the client receives a reimbursement of 100% of the insured value of the vehicle.
  • Protection of health and life. If the driver or passengers were injured, disabled or died as a result of an accident, the payment will be transferred to the beneficiary.

CTP insurance in the Renaissance

Unlike CASCO CTP, it is compulsory insurance for each driver. Without it, the client does not have the right to drive a vehicle. Therefore, the existence of a valid OSAGO insurance is a necessity, not a privilege.

In the Renaissance, customers can order eOSAGO - an electronic version of the document. It is no different from the classic driver insurance, but it saves the client time. MTPL is available online on the Renaissance website.

According to customer reviews of compulsory motor third-party liability insurance in the Renaissance insurance company, the insurer offers a favorable rate and fully complies with legal requirements. According to the law, compensation is carried out within 400 thousand rubles if the damage is caused to the vehicle, and 500 thousand rubles if the other driver or his passengers were injured.

CTP from Renaissance allows you to get paid if the insured is the culprit. In this case, it is not the client himself who seeks the compensation, but the victim.

Conditions for the purchase of a policy of compulsory motor liability insurance in the Renaissance

When reading reviews on compulsory motor third-party liability insurance in the Renaissance insurance company, customers want to know how much it will cost them to buy or extend compulsory insurance. The following factors affect the cost of a policy:

  • who concludes the contract: private individual or organization;
  • car model and power;
  • the life of the car;
  • number of drivers included in the policy, length of service and age of clients;
  • the city of residence of the policyholder;
  • accuracy of the driver (whether there were accidents with his participation).

The policy can be purchased by presenting the required documents:

  1. Passport (if there are several drivers, each will need documents).
  2. PTS and STS.
  3. Driver's license.
  4. Diagnostic card (only for cars older than 3 years).

Legal entities are also required to provide a certificate of registration of the organization.

When buying an insurance policy, it is worth considering the new changes in the law. This is relevant only for the electronic version of OSAGO. As stated in paragraph 7 of the Central Bank of the Russian Federation Directive 4190-U dated 11/14/2016, issued on the website of the insurance company OSAGO will begin to operate no earlier than on the 3rd day after the acquisition.

When buying eOSAGO, the driver must print a copy of the policy and always have it with him when he is driving.
