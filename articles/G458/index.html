Xiaomi MiPad 16Gb: reviews, reviews and opinions of users, specifications, advantages and disadvantages of the tablet

Currently, the range of tablets is striking in variety. However, most recently, no one could even think that the tasks of a bulky computer could be performed by small gadgets. The benefits of tablets are undeniable. First of all, they are mobile. The owner of such a device can go online anywhere, work with documents, and just play games.

Modern tablets are presented in all price segments. A striking representative of the flagship devices is Apple products. But in the budget class, the Chinese manufacturer Xiaomi has established itself. It has recently appeared on the domestic market. However, in such a short period of time quickly took a leading position. Its products are popular among Russian buyers due to the fact that the balance between cost, quality and, of course, functionality is optimally maintained in devices.

As you might have guessed, in this article we will focus on the characteristics of the MiPad line of tablets. There are three of them. According to the reviews of the owners, we can conclude that they are serious competitors to the "apple" iPads.

Alternative Apple iPad Replacement

The Chinese company Xiaomi is engaged in the production of various mobile equipment. Many smartphones, navigators, modems, tablets and other devices are produced annually. Demand for these products began around 2011-2012. During this time, Xiaomi's popularity is only growing, and sales are also increasing with it. On the Web, users have even given the nickname to this manufacturer - "Chinese Apple." And it is worth noting that it is completely justified. This is confirmed by reviews of the tablet Xiaomi MiPad 16Gb. However, unlike "apple" devices, Chinese ones are sold at a rather attractive price, but at the same time they are equipped with up-to-date applications. There are also no comments on quality. All functions work fine, the assembly is good, as are the materials.

Packaging and equipment

The manufacturer supplies their gadgets in simple cardboard boxes. Their design is concise, which is fully consistent with modern fashion trends. Retreating from the topic, we note that Apple was the first to abandon the colorful boxes. And the other manufacturers simply adopted this experience, which, as time has shown, was successful.

But packaging is just a box, and most importantly, what lies inside. The review of accessories will not be long. The fact is that unlike other Chinese manufacturers, this one provides a minimal set of resources. This is the documentation, adapter and USB cable. Some reviews of the first-generation Xiaomi MiPad 16Gb say that the kit also had a stylus, but not all. Therefore, this item can be considered a bonus from the seller.

Saving on components is understandable. The manufacturer simply used the minimum that could be offered without overstating the cost of the gadget.

Appearance

Having figured out the packaging and packaging, it's time to move on to the description of the appearance. All three generations of tablets are very similar to the "apple" gadgets. For example, Xiaomi MiPad 16Gb (White, Silver, etc.) of the first generation is very similar to the enlarged version of the iPhone 5c. The case of these models is made of plastic. The colors of the "apple" device are duplicated in the MiPad line. But the second version of the tablet is not so bright. Devices with a golden, gray and pink case went on sale. But this is also understandable - in such color schemes new gadgets from Apple began to be released. Xiaomi MiPad 2 (16Gb) looks very much like an iPad mini. The second generation tablet already has a metal case. The panels are made of thin aluminum sheet. The aspect ratio of the screen is 4: 3, like the iPad. The same resolution is also used.

The overall design looks strict, but not original, as it is borrowed from another manufacturer. However, this can not be called a disadvantage. But still there are differences. The rear aluminum panel is matte and rougher than the iPad mini. The third generation of the MiPad series looks like the second.

On the front panel of the tablets flaunts a 7.9 display. At the top are a selfie camera lens, an alert indicator and a sensor. At the bottom is a standard control panel, consisting of three touch-type buttons. What do users say in reviews of the Xiaomi MiPad 16Gb? All owners of this gadget praise the manufacturer for the build quality. It is at the highest level - there are no gaps, creak and play are absent. Many also liked the elegant design, streamlined body shape, as well as the absence of sharp corners.

Xiaomi MiPad 16Gb. Dimensions

Given that the tablet is a mobile device, its size is important for each user. The first model of the line weighed 360 g. With this mass, the height of the gadget was 202 mm and the width was 135 mm. The users were very pleased with the thickness indicator. It is 8.5 mm. Such a thin tablet is everyone's dream.

The second and third generation are slightly different in size from the first. True, quite a bit. These two models have reduced weight - 322 g (Mi Pad 2) and 328 mm (Mi Pad 3). The same thing happened with height and width. These indicators became 200.4 mm and 123.6 mm, respectively. Given that the MiPad 1 tablet was already quite thin, manufacturers were able to make it even narrower. In the second and third generation, the thickness became 7 mm.

Specifications of the screen and camera Xiaomi MiPad 1 (16Gb)

It's time to move on to a review of technical specifications. We begin their description from the screen. The tablet has a high-quality display made using IPS technology. The operating system of the first tablet of the MiPad series

The Xiaomi MiPad 2 / 16Gb Silver tablet is controlled by Android 4.4.2. Naturally, there is a proprietary MIUI shell, which is installed on all devices of this manufacturer. The tablet version of the OS is Russified, Google Play is installed. All applications are already on the desktop. It is not possible to combine them into one list. What do users say about the system in reviews? Xiaomi MiPad 16Gb, unfortunately, is unstable. Often error messages are displayed on the screen. It was also noted that not all menu items are translated into Russian, there are not only English inscriptions, but also hieroglyphs.

The Xiaomi MiPad 16Gb firmware is optimized perfectly. Due to this, in terms of autonomy, the Chinese tablet can compete with the "apple" gadget. The manufacturer installed a fairly capacious battery. Her resource is 6700 mAh. Definitely, this figure is impressive, but you should not count on several days of work. The fact is that to assess the autonomy it is necessary to take into account the power of the hardware platform, and it is rather big in this device.

  • Watching videos online - up to 9 hours

  • High Performance is recommended for gaming.

The tablet is charged both from a wall outlet and from a computer via a USB cable. If you use the original charger, it will take 3-4 hours to restore the battery life.

Performance MiPad 1

As mentioned above, the Xiaomi MiPad (16Gb) tablet has powerful hardware specifications. And it is true. The "heart" of the gadget was the Nvidia Tegra K1 processor. Paired with the main chipset, the Nvidia GK20A video card is installed. Manufacturers sold two gigabytes of RAM. The lineup has a modification with 16 GB of internal memory and 64 GB.
