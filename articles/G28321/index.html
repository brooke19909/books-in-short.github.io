Image Specifications

This Samsung TV is equipped with a large and high-quality screen . 32 inches is its diagonal. It was made using LED technology, and its resolution is 1920 X 1080. That is, in this case, the picture is displayed in FullHD format. At the same time, its refresh rate is 200 Hz, and the aspect ratio is 16: 9. The surface of the screen is glossy. The image quality in this case is excellent and it has no drawbacks. At least this is indicated by the reviews of the owners of such a multimedia center.

Acoustic system

The presence of a good built-in sound system can certainly boast a Samsung UE32M5000AK TV . It consists of two speakers. The power of each of them is 5 watts, and in total this allows you to get already 10 watts, which is more than enough for high-quality sound accompaniment of the watched television programs. The disadvantages of the speaker system include the lack of a 3.5 mm audio port. With the help of the latter, you can connect an external acoustic system for television broadcasts. Therefore, if the embedded system is not enough for any reason to output sound, then somehow this problem cannot be eliminated.





Power consumption

As noted earlier, the rather large screen size in this Samsung TV is 32 inches. In addition to this, it is manufactured using outdated LED technology. Already two of these factors indicate the high power consumption of this multimedia center. It is also necessary to note the outdated element base. As a result, during the operation of such a TV, the power consumption can reach 59 watts in maximum load mode. In the nominal mode, this value is 41 watts. In standby mode, the level of power consumption drops to 0.5 watts.

Communications

A good list of built-in communication ports is equipped with the Samsung UE32M5000AK. Characteristics indicate the presence of these:

  • 2 ports of HDMI format revision 2.0. With their help, you can display images from satellite tuners or DVD-players.

  • 1 antenna input, to which you can only connect a local antenna or cable provider equipment. But the satellite TV kit for this device cannot be directly switched. The limitation is on the part of the built-in tuner, which can process local (both digital and analog) or cable signals.

  • 1 USB port, to which a flash drive can be connected to play various multimedia content.

  • 1 RCA jack for connecting to legacy satellite receivers.

  • A set of Toslink ports that can both receive and output.

  • 2 DVB CAM format connectors.

In the above list, as noted earlier, there are not enough 3.5 mm audio jacks. They are indispensable in the case of outputting a sound signal to an external speaker system. Also on this list is no RJ-45. Using the latter, you can implement a connection to a computer network and directly output a video signal from a computer. In addition to this, the list of supported interfaces does not include Wi-Fi, which functions similarly to the RJ-45, but only without the use of wires.





Setting procedure

Setting up Samsung UE32M5000AK consists of the following actions:

  1. Removing the TV in question from the box. Also, it is necessary to get the rest of the delivery components from it.

  2. Build a multimedia center. Its mounting and surface mounting.

  3. Execution of switching. Cable entry from an antenna or a signal supply system from a cable operator. Connection to a power supply network.

  4. Turn on the device.

  5. The next step is to go to the "Settings" menu and search for available television channels. In this case, you must specify the type of signal source: cable or local television.

  6. In the end, you need to save the result.

After that, the Samsung UE32M5000AK is fully and completely ready for use . Reviews at the same time indicate that the setup procedure for this TV is very simple and there are really no problems with its implementation.
