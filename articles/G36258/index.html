The best recreation centers and camp sites of the Nizhny Novgorod region

Nizhny Novgorod region is one of the most attractive centers of Russia. A huge number of interesting sights and the nature of indescribable beauty do not leave anyone indifferent. Almost half of the region is occupied by coniferous forests and birch groves. There are also many ponds near which you can have a great time with friends. The buildings are located almost on the banks of the Oka River, surrounded by a beautiful pine forest. Visitors are offered comfortable apartments. It can be accommodated in a single or double room. The health resort offers a wide range of medical services. Here you can undergo a full examination of the body, work out in the gym or get a dietitian consultation.



Not all camp sites and recreation facilities in the Nizhny Novgorod region have the necessary equipment for postoperative rehabilitation.
