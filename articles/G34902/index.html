Calypso Beach Turunc Hotel (Turkey, Marmaris): photos and reviews

Holidays in Turkey can be pleasant and unforgettable if you pay enough attention to the choice of hotel. Each of them is distinguished by a certain specialization, a list of services. To decide on the possibility of booking a room, you should consider the reviews of tourists who have been here.

One of the most popular hotels is Calypso Beach Hotel Turunc (Turkey, Marmaris). He receives a lot of positive reviews. What kind of vacation a four-star hotel offers its visitors will be considered in the article.

Description

To conclude on the quality of rest in the selected hotel, it is necessary to consider its description. Calypso Beach Hotel Turunc is located near the resort town of Marmaris. It is built in the village of Turunch. From Dalaman International Airport you need to drive 120 km to get to the hotel.



The city beach is just a short walk away. Until December 2016, the hotel was called Dora Mymeric Turunc. It was built in 2006. The last restoration was carried out here in 2011.

This is a country hotel, which is 20 km from Marmaris. The hotel organizes a quality beach holiday. The hotel is built on the first line from the coast. It occupies a small area (5400 m¬≤). The main building has 10 floors. There is also a separate building with family-type rooms. This building has 3 floors.

Specialization

Calypso Beach Turunc Hotel organizes a wonderful vacation for tourists with different needs and preferences. Families with children, adults and youth groups feel comfortable at this hotel. Also newlyweds often come here.



The hotel offers a quality beach holiday. It is built close to the sea coast. You can walk to the city beach. Visitors from different countries relax at the hotel. Many Russian-speaking tourists. However, according to reviews, the staff understands this language poorly. However, the hotel staff do not speak English well. However, they show hospitality, warmly welcome all visitors.

This hotel is not suitable for organizing business conferences, trainings and trainings. If guests want to relax quietly, calmly, it is better to book rooms that do not overlook the pool. Youth companies here spend their holidays cheerfully. All tourists can go on excursions, take a walk in the center of the village or go to Marmaris. Each visitor of the hotel can organize leisure activities in accordance with their taste preferences.

Nutrition

The Calypso Beach Hotel Turunc in Marmaris provides all-inclusive meals. The hotel has a restaurant. It serves breakfast, brunch, lunch, dinner and late dinner. You can also visit the bar. Here you can take free local alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks, snacks.



Please note that for a fee, you can order fresh juices, ice cream and Turkish coffee in the bar. Foreign alcohol, energy can also be purchased here for a fee. The bar is located in the lobby of the hotel. Here you can relax on comfortable soft sofas, order drinks, cocktails and light snacks.

The restaurant serves international dishes. Some vacationers note that the choice of products is quite modest: chicken, potatoes, a large selection of vegetables, fruits, cheeses and more. Here you can choose for yourself delicious, healthy dishes.

Beach vacation

Calypso Beach Turunc Hotel (Turitsya , Marmaris ) is focused on a beach holiday. A road leads to the coast. The city beach has an area where visitors can relax. They can take free sun loungers, mattresses, towels and umbrellas. There is no bar on the beach.



The beach is sandy and pebble. The entrance to the sea is quite convenient. In season, the water temperature reaches +23 ... +25 ¬∫. A wide range of marine, beach activities allows children and adults to have fun.

The hotel has an 80 m¬≤ pool. It does not heat water. Near the pool you can lie on sun loungers. Umbrellas are placed here. There is a bar near the pool. Children and adults can ride a roller coaster. For kids built a special shallow pool. Water recreation at the hotel is varied. Additional water sports classes are available for a fee.

Service and placement features

Considering reviews of Calypso Beach Hotel Turunc , it is worth noting some features of accommodation, as well as service. The hotel does not accommodate vacationers with pets. Non-smoking rooms are also prohibited. There are no special conditions for people with disabilities. There are also no connecting rooms.

Guestrooms provide complimentary 24-hour room service. Among the services that are paid separately, it should be noted a left-luggage office at the reception, room service. Also at the hotel you can change the currency, pay with a plastic card.

Free parking is available on site. You can also rent vehicles here. If necessary, you can call a doctor or go to his appointment. Laundry and dry cleaning services are available.

The staff is responsive, ready to help resolve issues.

Entertainment

Calypso Beach Hotel Turunc organizes interesting leisure activities for its vacationers. The hotel has a room for watching movies, TV shows. If you need to organize a banquet, celebration of an important event, you can rent a banquet hall. For children there is a games room, a kids club.



Animation works day and night. Game programs are organized from June to August. At other times, the animation is missing. Programs are conducted for adults and children. The animation team consists of 3 people.

On the beach you can play beach volleyball, do water sports. The hotel rents equipment for table tennis, billiards, darts. Everyone can find an activity for themselves. The hotel area is small. If you want to take a walk, it is recommended to go to the center of the tourist village. There is a playground for children, outdoor games and competitions are held.

Excursions

Relaxing at Calypso Beach Hotel Turunc , you can go on various excursions. Popular attractions are located in Pamukkale, Ephesus, Istanbul. However, please note that they are removed from the hotel. The trip takes a lot of time.

In the immediate vicinity of the hotel there are natural attractions, bars, restaurants. You can take a walk to Turunch Bay, visit the ancient city of Amos. Within a radius of 3 km from the hotel there are many picturesque beaches. A walk along the coast will be unforgettable and enjoyable.

Close to the hotel there is a bar where young people like to relax. In the evening you can stroll through the territory of the tourist village. Amos is famous for its extraordinary beauty. Here you can observe the crystal clear water of the sea, light sand. If you wish, you can rent a car, go to Marmaris. There are also many interesting sights, places to relax.

Negative reviews

Reviews of Calypso Beach Hotel Turunc (Marmaris ) are mostly positive. The hotel receives a rating of 8 on a 10-point scale. It is a good place to relax.

Negative reviews also occur. Vacationers note that on the side of the hotel, where balconies overlook the pool, it is noisy. The room is unbearably hot on warm days. If the temperature drops at night, it gets cold. If you turn on the air conditioner, dust flies out of it.

In the corridors in the morning you can smell burning. Also, the staff does not speak English and Russian. Communicating with them is quite difficult. The administrator in the dining room receives a lot of negative reviews. The restaurant staff controls whether visitors take out fruits and other products with them.

The bar serves drinks in small portions. The food is not varied enough. During the afternoon pastries are not put on the bar. This is necessary to ask employees.

Room cleaning also gets negative reviews. Maids do not wipe the dust, do not wash the floor. They only bring new towels, cleaning products.

Positive reviews

It should be noted that most visitors to the hotel leave positive feedback. The hotel area is clean and well maintained. The rooms offer beautiful views of the sea, mountains. This is a quiet village where you can relax, enjoy the tranquility and natural beauty.

The rooms are spacious. They have everything you need. Furniture, decoration new. Plumbing also does not receive any comments. The staff, although it does not speak Russian, is quite responsive, smiling.

When a group of vacationers arrives at the hotel, they will be fed. After that, visitors are accommodated in prepared rooms. If the flight is delayed on the day of departure, you can also count on receiving everything you need while waiting for departure.

Having considered the features of your stay at Calypso Beach Hotel Turunc , you can say that this is a good hotel. It has certain disadvantages. However, the benefits are much greater. They overshadow all the flaws.
