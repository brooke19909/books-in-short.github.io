Christmas costume Ice floes: how to make

At the New Year's masquerade, all the girls want to look stunning. And in a suit of Snowflakes or Snow Maiden, this is difficult to do. Indeed, such images will be in the majority. So you need to come up with something original, for example, an Ice suit. How to make it? Read about it below.





Costume of Ice for the matinee

Creating such an outfit for a daughter is very simple. The New Year's costume of the Ice will consist of a tutu skirt and a topic like a corset. So that the outfit does not look boring, you need to give it originality. For example, to draw snowflakes or ice on a skirt. It is better to choose a topic summer, with an abundance of folds. As decorative elements, you can use fluffy pompons, which will symbolize snow. They can decorate a belt, shoes and even a hairstyle. On the head you need to pin a bow. It must be tough so that from afar it can resemble ice floes or stalactites.

Costume for the girl

It‚Äôs easy to assemble a little daughter for a matinee, but it can be problematic for a teenage girl to come up with an ice costume for the New Year. Why? Yes, because at a school disco the costume will need to be removed to change into ordinary clothes. Therefore, taking into account the needs of the child, the outfit must be formed so that, if necessary, it was easy to throw it off.





The Ice suit will consist of a crown and a cape. It is advisable to choose white clothing or a white blouse as the bottom layer. We will make the crown of ice snowflakes. You can buy them at the store, or you can make them yourself. For this we need a glue silicone gun. We warm it up and draw a snowflake on paper . While the silicone is not dry, sprinkle it with sparkles. When the snowflakes harden, they must be removed from the paper and glued to a pre-made cardboard blank. She will serve as the basis of the crown. It remains to sew a cloak. To do this, we need a large section of white fabric. We turn it diagonally and cut off two formed corners. Fur can be sewn along the edges of the fabric. We will fix the cape on the chest with the help of a decorative pin.

Gothic ice

To create such an image, we need a white dress. It should be long. We also need a piece of fabric from which we will form a wrap. If you are making an Ice suit, in which you plan to move around in a clean room, such as a house, then you will need to make a long wrap with a train. And if you plan to attend a party in a club in a festive outfit, then it is better not to make a train.





We will do the simplest cloak. We sew a ribbon to the upper side of the fabric cut. With it, the upper element of the outfit will be fixed on the neck. And cut the opposite edge in a semicircle. So that the fabric does not sprinkle and does not go, it is better to overcast it around the edge. It remains to make a hat and shoulder pads. We will make them from plastic icicles. You can buy this element of decor in a store where all the festive paraphernalia is sold. Let's start with the cap. For this purpose, it is better to take a cotton hat. To keep the icicles tight, you need to glue a wide bezel under it and fix the plastic decor on it. To make the headdress look prettier, you can embroider it with beads, rhinestones and beads.

Getting to the production of shoulder pads. We will make them from the branches of hawthorn. Tear them off and paint them white. We fix them with a glue gun on cartons. When the structure dries, the shoulder pads can be sewn or glued to the cloak.





Ice-ballerina

The basis of the image will be a swimsuit and a skirt. To make the Ice suit look elegant, it‚Äôs better to take a lace skirt or with some kind of shiny pattern. The neck of the swimsuit (which can be replaced with a turtleneck) must be embroidered with rhinestones or beads. Pointe shoes should be worn on the legs, if there are no such houses, then they can be replaced with ballet shoes. The main highlight of the image is, of course, the crown. We will do it on the principle of kokoshnik. First, we form a framework of thick wire, on which we then stretch a silk fabric. Brilliant paint, silver acrylic is well suited for this purpose, we draw snowflakes or stars all over the kokoshnik.

Next you need to glue the ice to it. Such decor is sold in abundance in stores. Ice can be of different lengths. The final stage is the manufacture of a beak. To do this, we again need to make a wire frame. We make the form not only of the base, but also in the middle we lay 5 thin strips. They will be simultaneously stiffeners for our beak, and it will also be convenient to attach decor to them later. When the frame is ready, we attach it to the kokoshnik. It remains only to decorate the beak. We will do this with a glue gun. We draw a cobweb between the stiffeners, sprinkle the silicone with sparkles and allow the material to cool. Our crown is ready.





Romantic ice

To make this look, we need a white dress with a wide skirt. Nobody knows what an Ice suit should be like, so we will fantasize. On the shoulders you need to throw something warm and fleecy. It can be a scarf or stole. The main accent of the costume is the headgear and hairstyle. We make a fancy hat. It is best based on a plastic semicircle hollow inside. It will be easy to attach decor. First of all, we glue large plastic icicles to the base. And then you can stick a couple of branches into the crown for entourage, to which artificial snow or polystyrene must be glued.

As small parts you should use small ice floes and pebbles. When you finish the composition, it will need to be painted. This is easiest to do with a spray can. Moreover, you only need to paint the foam base, and not the entire headgear. Do not worry that paint can get on ice and branches, this will give the headdress a charm. It will seem that the whole decor is a little skidded with snow.
