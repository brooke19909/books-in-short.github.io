How a chair cover can transform your home

Furniture in the house plays a leading role and occupies most of the space. Its main role is to provide convenience and comfort. The need for cabinet structures, such as cabinets, walls, due to the need for storage of clothes, books, kitchen utensils. Upholstered furniture is designed to provide a comfortable stay.

All these elements fill the space and make its appearance complete. If the apartment is finished in a certain style, then the furniture is purchased according to the general concept of the room.

A huge role, of course, is played by chairs, armchairs, sofas. This, perhaps, is the furniture that is definitely in every apartment. In the old days, the range of this industry was very scarce, which led to the appearance of all kinds of accessories. The desire to decorate furniture has survived to this day. In addition to the fact that the chair cover is an excellent accessory, it also protects the upholstery from abrasion and pollution. Covers for furniture - just a salvation for families who have pets. Wool, dirt - all this remains on the cloak, which can be easily removed and sent to the washing machine.

Covers for furniture can be made in several sets: for everyday use and for receiving guests. For each day, it is advisable to use something simple and more practical, and for the holiday you can decorate furniture with elegant designer covers.

The more various furniture accessories, the wider the list of options for original interior solutions. Furniture covers can make furniture not only more beautiful, but also comfortable. For example, a foam cape on a chair can provide extra softness. It can be made with your own hands. The packing for the cover should be cut out of foam rubber in the shape and size of the chair or stool. The fabric is selected dense, wear-resistant. Flax or twill is perfect for such purposes. From this material it is necessary to cut 2 pieces of fabric in size larger than the foam piece, thereby taking into account the width of the foam and allowances for the seams. It is also necessary to provide straps for attaching to a chair. Zipper is sewn to one side of the cover. When the cover is ready, you need to fill it with foam rubber and decorate it as desired. As a finish, ordinary buttons are perfect. You can also complement the cover with embroidery.


Upholstered furniture can also be decorated with various things. Cape on the chair will protect the upholstery and give the furniture originality and brightness. Knitted accessories look very advantageous. Beautiful lace covers are obtained by crocheting. There are a lot of ideas for creating original furniture covers. For example, a satin fabric makes an elegant chair cover. As a complement to such a cover, a satin bow of a contrasting color, which can be placed on the back, is perfect. Or come up with your own unique option. Do-it-yourself furniture covers can revive any boring chair or old chair, hide flaws and highlight dignity. Furniture covers will give cosiness and originality to any interior.
