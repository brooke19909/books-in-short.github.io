Dessole Seti Sharm Resort 4, Sharm El Sheikh, Egypt

Description: Hotel complex Dessole Seti Sharm Resort 4 is located in the resort town of Egypt, the youth capital - Sharm al-Sheikh. The trip from the airport takes 15-20 minutes.





The cozy bay in which the hotel is built attracts the attention of many tourists, and the close proximity of the Old Market and the main street of Sharm El Sheikh - Naam Bay - makes it even more attractive. The complex was built in 1995 in the form of one-story bungalows and two-story buildings. The last overhaul was in 2009, but redecoration is done every year. Rooms: The Dessole Seti Sharm Resort 4 hotel complex boasts not only a large territory, but also its spaciousness.





It has 312 rooms with different sizes: Standart Room (37 sq.m.), Superior Room (44 sq.m.), Suite (50-90 sq.m.). Each room is equipped with modern and comfortable furniture in the European style. The view from the Standart and Superior rooms is on the hotel courtyard, and from the Suite rooms - on the sea coast. The room itself has a minibar, constantly replenished with fresh mineral water, a free safe, air conditioning, a TV with Russian channels, a telephone, a bathroom with necessary accessories (in the superior rooms there is a bidet). Each room has its own terrace or balcony with a table and armchairs. Cleaning takes place daily. The rest of the room-service is round-the-clock (paid service).

Meals: The main restaurant Dessole Seti-Sharm Resort 4 seats 300 people in the hall and 80 people on the summer terrace. All inclusive meals . Breakfast is a bit monotonous and includes a huge amount of various pastries, eggs, toasts, sausages, yoghurts and more. Lunches are represented by a much larger selection of dishes, and for dinner you can always choose from several varieties of meat and fish exactly the one that you like most. It is noteworthy that reviews about the hotel complex Dessole Seti Sharm Resort 4 unanimously say that this hotel has a children's menu: mini-sandwiches, cereal with milk, low-fat boiled meat, mashed potatoes. The variety of pastries served at breakfast and dinner is amazing. But since there is a huge market near the hotel, none of the guests also lack vitamins. Alcohol is represented by local drinks, free wine is served exclusively for lunch and dinner. For those who feel hungry during the day, there are various snacks at the beach and hotel bars.

Beach: The hotel complex Dessole Seti Sharm Resort 4 * has its own sandy beach in a cozy and quiet bay.





All beach items (umbrellas, sun loungers, towels) are provided free of charge. Sunset at sea is convenient, but, unfortunately, there are no famous corals. To look at them enough, you need to stock up on special shoes and go to the wild beach located nearby.

Additional information: The hotel complex Dessole Seti Sharm Resort 4, a photo of which can be found on the Internet, boasts a large territory, on which there are two main pools with slides. Also on site is a conference room for business meetings, accommodating up to 60 people. In addition, it should be noted that the animation in Dessole Seti Sharm Resort 4 * is not only children's, but also adult. Every evening, the amphitheater hosts a variety of contests and entertainment.

Reviews: It can be concluded that Dessole Seti Sharm Resort 4 is suitable for both youth and family vacations. In favor of choosing this hotel can speak comfortable rooms, a huge territory, a beautiful beach, varied and tasty food and the proximity of the Old Market.
