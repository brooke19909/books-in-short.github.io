Using payment services

Today, almost everyone has their own wallet of one of the payment systems (Yandex, Qiwi or Webmoney). Thanks to these resources, you can instantly pay for the Internet and services of satellite companies. How to pay for a telecard in this way? Very simple:

  • Through Yandex.Money. In this case, you must first create a wallet and confirm your personal data. After that, you can attach any bank card to your profile. This procedure usually takes a few seconds. At the next stage, go to the "Products and Services" section and select "Television" there. After that, a list of all companies whose services can be paid through this system will automatically appear.
  • Via Webmoney. In this service, you also need to register and go to the service payment section. Next, you need to find the name of the company you need and transfer funds, confirming the operation by phone. In this case, the commission of the system will be withheld, which will be about 0.8% of the total payment amount.
