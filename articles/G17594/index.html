Manos Maria Hotel & Apartments 4 * (Greece, Crete): hotel description, services, reviews

Crete is a picturesque majestic island, a favorite place of tourist pilgrimage for millions of travelers. The history of this small but legendary island has more than 8 thousand years. According to the myths of ancient Hellas, it was Crete that was once the birthplace of the great Zeus, Olympus, Minotaur and Icarus. A lot of really religious monuments of ancient Hellas have survived to this day. But not only the fact that Crete was at the very beginnings of the birth of modern European civilization is today the reason for such a frenzied tourist popularity of this island. Of course, travelers are also attracted by a pleasant climate, beautiful landscapes, paradise beaches, fine cuisine, breathtakingly profitable shopping opportunities, decent hotels and much more.

Many guidebooks and travel services note that the most friendly and hospitable people live in Crete, they are always ready to tasty feed the traveler, show the way, suggest interesting places. Naturally, who else should be so welcoming, happy and good-natured than not the inhabitants of such an original and beautiful island.

Rest on the breathtaking Crete can be overshadowed by some bureaucratic formalities, or rather, by one formality. Her name is a Schengen entry visa. This beast is not at all scary if you buy a tour from the operator and get a visa from him. But two or three days to collect documents and 35 euros for the visa itself must be spent.

This hotel complex has been open since 2000, and in 2013 it was overhauled and reconstructed.

The hotel consists of one four-story building. The building has an elevator. In the summer season, the hotel is often "on foot". So you need to book a room in advance. In cases where all rooms in the hotel are occupied, guests are offered an upgrade in the neighboring five-star hotel.

The hotel accepts credit cards. No deposit will be charged upon check-in.

There are no facilities for disabled guests.

Hotel location

Hotel Manos Maria Hotel & Apartments 4 * has a very good location almost in the center of Hersonissos - this is an active, vibrant resort in the north of Crete. Developed tourist infrastructure - restaurants, cafes, night clubs, shopping centers - is within walking distance from the hotel.

The municipal municipal beach is 50 meters away.





Crete's largest international airport , Nikos Kazantzakis, is only 23 km away.

Crete is a small island, therefore, the location of the hotel is important, but not critical. Against the background of cheaper and visa-free destinations (Egypt, Tunisia, Turkey) Crete, of course, compares favorably with the presence of unique attractions. The protagonist of the excursion programs on the island is the legendary Knossos Palace - a magnificent monumental complex, on the territory of which, according to myths, was the mystical Labyrinth of the Minotaur.

The Arcadia Monastery, the ancient city of Falasarna, the Frangokastello fortress, Mount Dikti (where Zeus was born according to legend) are only the most famous archaeological sites of Crete. But there are also natural ones: a cape of three seas (yes, the island is really washed by three seas), a huge botanical park in Chania, Lake Vulismeni and the famous, largest in Europe, Samaria Gorge.

Classification and description of numbers

In total, the Manos Maria Hotel & Apartments 4 * (Standard) has 41 rooms. All have a standard category. Room size is 20 square meters. m. Each room has a balcony, shower, toilet, air conditioning, telephone, safe (paid), hairdryer, mini fridge, TV, bath accessories.





Rooms are cleaned daily. Each room has free internet, regardless of the number of devices.

Hotel Dining Options

Following the example of many hotels in Crete, Manos Maria Hotel & Apartments 4 * keeps up with the cult of modern daily gastronomic marathons under the auspices of the all-inclusive system. The time frame for the all inclusive system is from eight in the morning until ten in the evening.

For guests who are not available at the hotel until 22:00 (due to an excursion or late check-in), a free late dinner is offered.

For guests who plan to be absent from the hotel for breakfast, it is possible to visit an early breakfast (until 7:00).

On the territory of the hotel Manos Maria Hotel & Apartments 4 * there is a pool bar. Under the all inclusive system, guests are provided with the following drinks: coffee, juice, local beer, homemade red and white wine, local alcoholic drinks: ouzo, crayfish, brandy.

Ice cream - in the main restaurant after lunch and dinner.





The all-inclusive system does not include champagne, branded spirits, as well as premium drinks.

Naturally, on the municipal beach for the rental of sun loungers and umbrellas you need to pay extra. Beach towels are available at the hotel at an additional cost.

Hotel service

Manos Maria Hotel & Apartments 4 * - island resort hotel. Even despite very reasonable prices, the set of services in a hotel of this category should take into account some mandatory options. Such a resort hotel can not afford to provide guests with only the basic service - accommodation, like many ordinary European hotels and hostels.





Free services for guests of Manos Maria Hotel & Apartments 4 *:

Facilities for children at the hotel

Greece and the Greek islands are a favorite vacation spot with children. A high level of tourists' confidence in the European quality of service leads to the fact that often travelers do not mind staying with children, even in budget hotels such as Manos Maria Hotel & Apartments 4 *.

So the hotels introduce several options for small tourists in their service policy, for example, this hotel has a children's section in the pool, highchairs in the restaurant, and a cot is provided for free on request.





As practice shows, tourists often stay at this hotel with older children (from 6 years old). They are interested in going on excursions, traveling around the island, and trying sports activities. But for the smallest tourists, the smallest tourists should nevertheless settle in hotels with more elaborate children's services (animation, clubs, playgrounds, etc.).
