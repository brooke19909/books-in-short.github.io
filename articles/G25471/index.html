How to get from Vladimir to Ivanovo?

The path from Vladimir to Ivanovo is quite long. However, many people decide to overcome this distance. Why are they doing this? There may be several reasons!

Five reasons to visit the city of Ivanovo

People are ready to overcome the distance from Vladimir to Ivanovo for the following reasons:





  • in this city the cheapest and highest quality textiles. For several decades in a row, tours have been organized for women in this "fabric kingdom". It has three large shopping centers where you can buy towels, bedding and clothes at the lowest prices;




  • in Ivanovo there is a beautiful children's park with figures from favorite children's cartoons;
  • many people come to this place in order to visit the legendary chintz Museum;
  • The real decoration of the city is a creative square, on the territory of which bright large-scale events take place;
  • Each visitor should be photographed with a bronze dachshund, which is a symbol of the city.

Also in the region there are several ancient monuments, there are beautiful chapels and noteworthy architectural buildings.

Five reasons to visit the city of Vladimir

No less popular route is Ivanovo-Vladimir. In this city, you should pay attention to the following objects:

  • The Assumption Cathedral is one of the most beautiful temples in Russia, it amazes with its splendor and elegance of decoration;
  • In one of the parks there is a high ferris wheel. It raises people to the height of a 17-storey building. From this point the city is seen in full view;
  • the museum complex Vladimir Central is a very gloomy, but at the same time incredibly interesting place;
  • in the very center of the city is located the Lipki park with a beautiful landscape and an amazing fountain; young people like to gather in this place;
  • art connoisseurs will surely like the Old Vladimir Museum. A huge number of valuable artifacts are concentrated in it: objects from the time of the war, ancient photographs of the city, historical monuments and much more. The design of this building cannot but attract.

Vladimir region is a very beautiful place with amazing nature, architecture and traditions.

Train travel

As it turned out, to visit one of these cities is definitely for many reasons. Now we need to talk separately about which vehicle is more convenient to cross the road from Vladimir to Ivanovo. It could be a train. It is most comfortable to overcome long distances on it, since you can sleep, walk around wagons, and take a comfortable position.





Unfortunately, there is no direct train between these two cities. You will have to take the connecting route in the city of Kovrov. The total cost for two tickets will be about 1200 rubles, you can buy them at the local railway station. The path will not be close, in general it will take more than 17 hours. But there will be a wonderful opportunity to get acquainted with new places!

Driving by car

The distance from Ivanovo to Vladimir is about 117 kilometers. And the whole road is one straight line. Therefore, it is most reasonable to travel by car. Less than 10 liters of gasoline will be needed all the way, which will be about 500 rubles. You can overcome this distance in just an hour and a half.

Travelers will pass by one of the most beautiful cities in Russia - Suzdal. It is famous for ancient temples. It is mandatory to look into this fabulous corner.

Travel by bus

In the center of Ivanovo and Vladimir there are bus stations. From these points go both direct and transit buses. The interval between cities by public transport is easily overcome. It is worth noting that the first departure is carried out quite early - at 7:00 in the morning, and the last - at 21:00.

Total travel time is 1 hour 50 minutes. A ticket can be bought immediately before boarding for 250 rubles. Throughout the road, transport makes several stops, which is very convenient, especially for passengers with children.





We should also talk about textile tours that are provided by many companies in the city of Vladimir. Several times a month, collective trips are organized from different points of the city to the largest shopping malls - Rio, Tekstil-Profi or Tekstilshchik. You can buy such a trip at a bargain price, approximately it will be about 300 rubles in both directions. However, this tour is not suitable for someone who came to the city of brides not for shopping. Since it is allotted for shopping all the time, without leaving tourists the opportunity to enjoy the local sights.

Flight by plane

Not far from Vladimir and Ivanovo are airports. Despite this, there are no direct flights between the two cities. This is due to the small distance. If you wish, you can try to overcome the route with transfers on a private flight through the city of St. Petersburg. But in this way, almost no one uses it. Firstly, the flight is carried out only several times a month, secondly, it will take you about five hours to spend the whole trip, and thirdly, the whole trip cost will exceed 10 thousand rubles.





How to get from Vladimir to Ivanovo or vice versa? There are several ways. It is most sensible to use a bus or personal vehicle. This will save time and money.
