About hotel

Rooms

If we talk about hotels in Tunisia, then the assessment of rooms must be approached more carefully and demanding. Modern climatic equipment is responsible for maintaining a comfortable microclimate in the room. If you read almost any review of the Smartline Hammamet Regency Hotel, you will surely find information about satellite TV and a telephone in your room. Tunisia, as a Muslim state, is considered a country with a high level of security. Using it is included in the price of the hotel at the Smartline Hammamet Regency 4 * hotel.

The rooms in the hotel are spacious and bright, there is not much furniture, but all of it is convenient and comfortable to use. All plumbing in the bathroom is in good condition, problems and interruptions in the supply of hot water are not noticed.

Service

If you value first-class service and quality of room service, Smartline Hammamet Regency 4 * hotel is definitely suitable for you. Reviews confirm daily room cleaning.
