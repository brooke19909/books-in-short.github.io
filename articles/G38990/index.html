Major Kuznetsov Mikhail Borisovich

Among the servicemen who died while the terrorists held school No. 1 in Beslan. was Kuznetsov Mikhail Borisovich. His feat should not be forgotten, so in this article we will talk about what kind of person he was and how he completed his life's journey.





Salvation at the cost of life

On September 3, 2004, the advance team launched an assault on the school. Some of its participants managed to get into the school canteen. Among them was Major Kuznetsov Mikhail Borisovich. The terrorists continued to fire, but he did not stop the hero from taking out 20 hostages from the premises. Terrorists blew up the gym and continued to fire at it from grenade launchers, as well as small arms. The assault continued. Kuznetsov Mikhail Borisovich when repelling an enemy attack received multiple wounds, from them he later died in the hospital in Vladikavkaz.

No coincidence

How can one characterize a person who laid down his life for the sake of others? Strong, courageous, courageous. It is hardly possible to describe in simple words those qualities that are inherent in such heroes as Kuznetsov Mikhail Borisovich. But their exploits do not go unnoticed. The hero of our article participated in other military operations and always showed his best male qualities. So it was during the storming of Grozny, and also during the liberation of the hostages on Dubrovka and in other hot spots, where he had to visit. It is not surprising that Mikhail Borisovich Kuznetsov received many awards, including the Order of Merit for the Fatherland, I and II degrees. He also received the Order of the Red Star, For Military Merit, and For Courage.





Beginning of life

Major died at the age of 39. His short life journey began in the village of Yurovo (Ramensky district). Kuznetsov Mikhail Borisovich was born on August 21, 1965 in a family of ordinary workers. Having received a secondary education in 1980, the young man entered the SSPTU, where he studied until he was drafted into the army in 1984. Even then, he was able to excel. On duty, Kuznetsov Mikhail Borisovich ended up in Beslan, where his life path was tragically cut short.
