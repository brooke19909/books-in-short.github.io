Hotel Viet Sky Hotel 2 * (Nha Trang): photos and reviews of tourists

If you dream of an exotic and original Vietnam, but you cannot afford to stay in expensive hotels, then Viet Sky Hotel 2 * is exactly what you need.





Short hotel description

Resort Viet Sky Hotel 2 * is a unique institution of its kind, which harmoniously combines budget accommodation and high quality service, typical of titled hotels. One of its important features is the provision of round-the-clock access to free Internet, which is not found in all low-cost hotels.

Viet Sky Hotel 2 * cares not only about the comfort of guests, but also about their leisure. There is hardly a tourist who, after a busy day, would not want to relax in a bar and sing a couple of songs in karaoke. Here you can also enjoy delicious drinks, and in the restaurant guests will learn all the delights of local culinary dishes.

An important point in any hotel is the condition of the rooms and the level of comfort in them. The design decision deserves special attention, because the entire interior is permeated with traditional Vietnamese decorative elements. In addition, it provides all kinds of amenities that will provide guests with home comfort.





Where is the hotel situated

The location of the Viet Sky Hotel 2 * is the popular Vietnamese city of Nha Trang, which is located in the central part of the country. The airport from this hotel is approximately 30 km away.

It is worth noting the incredibly favorable location of the hotel. Within walking distance is everything that can only interest a tourist. Nearby there is a public beach, and a large Vietnamese market, and many attractions, among which the Tram Huong tower deserves special attention.

Hotel Rooms

Recently, such a destination among travelers as Vietnam has become increasingly popular. Viet Sky Hotel 2 * is a fairly small resort hotel, because it has only 44 guest rooms.

The most budget accommodation option is a double standard room, in which guests are invited to stay in a double bed. The same conditions are offered in the superior apartments, with the exception that there are more pieces of furniture installed on a larger area.

You can get closer to the level of "star" hotels by staying in a deluxe room. For a romantic getaway, double apartments are ideal, and for a family vacation rooms are designed for three guests.





What is provided in the rooms

Speaking about the amenities that are available in the rooms of the Viet Sky Hotel 2 * resort (Vietnam, Nha Trang), it is worth mentioning the following points:

  • a small TV that broadcasts many cable channels, among which you can find Russian programs;
  • both in the hotel as a whole and in the room in particular, the use of the wireless Internet is absolutely free;
  • You can call the reception or your loved ones located in other countries by phone;
  • there is a small refrigerator, which can also act as a minibar, but with the payment of the appropriate amount;
  • A bathroom is a toilet combined with a bath.




Guest Services

The guests who have chosen to spend their holidays at the Viet Sky Hotel 2 * are offered the following list of services:

  • it is very convenient to get to the upper floors of the hotel by high-speed elevator;
  • for business meetings, seminars and negotiations, you can rent a conference room, for the use of which a fee is charged for each hour;
  • You can leave your personal or rented transport in a guarded parking lot;
  • in all areas of the hotel you can use high-speed Wi-Fi absolutely free;
  • You can get acquainted with local attractions using the services of employees of a tour and tourism company;
  • for the servicing of guests and the solution of pressing problems, the employees of the reception are responsible;
  • you can remove stains from soiled things, as well as iron your wrinkled clothes on the road, by contacting the laundry service at the hotel;
  • some of the services can be ordered directly in the rooms (newspapers, food delivery, massage, etc.);
  • in the restaurant you can eat delicious and hearty;
  • in the backyard of the hotel there is a small but very beautiful and well-groomed garden with exotic plants;
  • tourists have the opportunity to rent vehicles (moped, car, mountain bike);
  • the reception staff can exchange currency for a small commission ;
  • there is a special room for celebrations, celebrations and banquets;
  • a safe is provided at the reception desk to ensure the safety of your valuables during your absence in the room;
  • office services such as photocopying, scanning, and faxing are provided to guests;
  • the bar provides you with a huge selection of drinks, as well as the opportunity to sing karaoke.




Hotel Accommodation Information

Going to the Viet Sky Hotel 2 * resort (Nha Trang), be sure to check out the list of rules that govern accommodation in this resort complex:

  • one of the most important moments is check-out hours, which are set at 14:00 (check-in and check-in) at this hotel and 12:00 (the time by which the guests agree to leave the room);
  • children under 8 years old without extra bed stay in the room for free, and a bed for them will cost $ 8 per night;
  • more than one extra bed is not allowed in the apartment, but cots are not provided at all;
  • You cannot come to the hotel with pets, regardless of their size and breed;
  • if desired, all payments to the hotel can be made in a convenient non-cash form.




Positive reviews

Inexpensive and comfortable rest can be offered to travelers by Viet Sky Hotel 2 * resort. Reviews of tourists contain the full gamut of positive impressions that were received during your stay at this hotel:

  • due to the good location of the hotel, street noise almost does not reach him;
  • in all areas of the hotel a very comfortable atmosphere, and also quite clean;
  • there are rooms with a picturesque and bewitching sea view (a particularly spectacular landscape opens from the windows of apartments on the upper floors);
  • close enough to the airport;
  • There are many cafes and shops near the hotel;
  • it is especially worth noting the work of the maids, as well as the daily replacement of towels;
  • spacious and bright rooms, where there are all necessary amenities for life;
  • two plastic bottles of purified drinking water are provided daily free of charge;
  • Nice breakfast buffet.

Negative reviews

Despite the general positive picture of impressions about the hotel, one can still highlight some comments regarding its work:

  • the hotel is quite far from the beach, and you have to go to it along a noisy and busy street;
  • the hotel staff do not speak foreign languages, with the exception of the reception desk staff who speak some English;
  • old furniture.

We can say that this hotel, in spite of a small number of stars, is doing its best to provide high-level services. The price of the tour is consistent with the quality of services and living conditions.
