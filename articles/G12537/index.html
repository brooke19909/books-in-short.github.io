The very first generation iPad, the comparison of models of which is our main task, was very successful and laid the foundation for a whole "dynasty" of smart tablets. The iPad was released in April 2010. The first version did not support 3G, only Wi-Fi was available to users (the built-in modem appeared in the gadget a bit later, when the model was re-released). And there were only three options for the amount of memory: 16, 32 and 64 GB. For 2010, this was quite enough, given the fact that the applications did not take up much space. The color of the case was only silver, but this suited many buyers. The latest version of the operating system that is supported by the iPad is iOs 5.1.1. There was no front camera or main camera on the iPad. This flaw was fixed in later versions. Today it is impossible to purchase a completely new iPad of the first generation, since it was discontinued in 2011. On sale there are only previously used devices, the cost of which is very low. However, it does not make sense to spend money on them, because they are very outdated in terms of hardware and the software installed on them.

First mini version

In 2012, immediately after the start of sales of the third iPad, the sale of a reduced version of the "apple" tablet started in November. He got the name iPad Mini. And the size - 7.9 inches became the hallmark. A pocket option, so to speak. The RAM on the tablet, it would seem, is a bit - only 512 MB. But even with such a "brain" the gadget just "flies." If we compare the iPad Mini with a full-sized cousin, then it looks exactly like the iPad 2. However, it has a better camera - 5 megapixels, and the front camera - 1.2 megapixels. Therefore, the gadget, despite its relatively low cost today (about 10-13 thousand rubles), is indeed a worthy representative in its category. The modern operating system iOS 9.2 supports without lags and brakes.





Second mini version

iPad Mini and iPad Mini 2 (the comparison in this section will be carried out exclusively in relation to these two models), it would seem, are very similar to each other. However, the mini-version of the second generation has become more perfect, despite the fact that the resolution of both cameras remains exactly the same. RAM doubled - 1024 MB, and for such a "baby" this is a serious claim to the speed of the device. In addition, the gadget received a more capacious battery - instead of 4400 mAh of the first generation, as many as 6471 mAh. This is enough to use the tablet all day when fully loaded, and even not the fact that you have to recharge at night. And, of course, the choice of internal memory has increased. If in the iPad mini of the first generation the choice was limited to 64 GB (16, 32 and 64), then the second generation also found a version of 128 GB so that all the necessary files were always at hand.

Fourth mini version

There is no need to talk about the third, since it differs little from the second according to the main criteria. But the fourth iPad Mini was really a breakthrough in the field of "apple" mini-gadgets, sales of which started in September 2015. The first thing that catches your eye is the internal memory of the device. The buyer is somewhat limited in choice: there are options for 16, 64 and 128 GB. But the usual 32-gigabyte version does not exist. It is worth noting that 16 GB is negligible for a tablet of this level. The battery capacity has become a little less - 5124 mAh. However, the manufacturer claims that the charge will be spent more economically, and therefore sacrificed the treasured mAh. The tablet has become thinner - only 6.1 mm instead of 7.5 mm in the second and third mini-ipads. The amount of RAM increased - 2048 MB. This is an unrivaled result for the pocket option! The rear main camera acquired 8 megapixels for pictures. But the front remained unchanged (1.2 megapixels).
