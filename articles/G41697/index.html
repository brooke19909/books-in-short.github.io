Romanian poet Eminescu Mihai: biography, creativity, poems and interesting facts

Eminescu Mihai in ordinary life had the surname Emnovich. He was born on January 15, 1850 in Botosani. Died June 15, 1889 in Bucharest. The poet became the pride of literary Romania, he was recognized as a classic. After his death, he was awarded the title of member of the country's Academy of Sciences.

Life path

In a very large family, Mihai Eminescu was born. His biography contains information about his father, who traded in agriculture. As for the mother, between her and her son from the youngest nails there was a special tenderness and love.



Mihai Eminescu wrote a lot about her. Poems such as "Mom." reflect all the charm and proximity of their relationship. The boy studied at the gymnasium in Chernivtsi, where teaching was conducted in German. Then this area was under the leadership of Austria-Hungary. Speaking at the lessons was difficult for him. And in the future, the poems of Mihai Eminescu in Romanian are much more famous .

Interesting Facts

At school, the guy had a friendly relationship with Aron Pumnul, who participated in the revolutionary actions of 1848 and taught the Romanian language. It was thanks to him that Eminescu Mihai became a patriot, drawing from the doctrine many strong ideas. He dedicated the first verse to his mentor. At that moment, a poetic biography begins. The semantic load of the work consists in a call to grief, which was supposed to disperse throughout Bukovina, because one of the country's best teachers died.

The publication of the first famous work, which was written by Eminescu Mihai, took place in 1866. Then he managed to create "Corruption of a Young Man", after which several more of his creations were brought to the attention of the public in the journal "Family". For his creative achievements and patriotism, the appearance of the poet is depicted in national currency. The banknote with his portrait "walks" under the face value of 500 monetary units.





Change of place of education

Education in Chernivtsi was not yet completed, but the young man was forced to leave the gymnasium. He entered another educational institution located in Vienna. That was his father's wish. There Eminescu Mihai acquired the status of a volunteer with the right to study philology, the history of philosophy, as well as jurisprudence. Then his creative activity does not slow down, but, on the contrary, is gaining new momentum. What poetry was written by Mihai Eminescu, it becomes clear if you get acquainted with the numerous creations of that time. One of them is the wonderful poem Epigones.





Bias in thoughts

With the onset of autumn 1872, he moves to Berlin. Within the walls of the local university, he attended lectures ending in September 1874. He was engaged in translation activities, working with the works of Confucius and Kant. Patriotic ideas captured his mind, permeating creativity. Such a character is the work of "The Angel and the Demon", as well as "The Emperor and the Proletarian." Thanks to the Paris Commune , fundamental changes took place in his thinking and attitude. In search of funds

In 1874, the poet moved to Iasi, where he planned to make money. He found a job as a teacher and a librarian in a gymnasium. He also takes on the duties of a school inspector. During this period, the poem "Calin" was completed. Allegorically, unity with the motherland is glorified here. Some time after the move, the poet created works bearing a philosophical load. In 1877, he received an invitation from the newspaper Vremya, which was published by the Conservative Party. The poet moves to the territory of Bucharest. From this, of course, it does not become easier for him financially, he has to earn extra money.

The fading of the mind and the dawn of a career

This creator really was a genius, of whom there are few. So his lyrical hero did not have enough space for himself on earth. The main value in the lines of this work proclaims peace. However, his searches take a lot of energy against the backdrop of a worrying and noisy external world. From this, fatigue arises, which can be understood by reading the text of the verse. Notes of atheistic views are present in the work "I do not believe ...". However, against the background of this, a demonic image is used in a separate poem. The poet considers the world from different angles, makes assumptions, reflects and allows the reader to think with him.

Eminescu's life was clouded by a mental illness that developed in 1883. The treatment gave some improvements, however, it was still not possible to completely eradicate the disease; he pursued the creator to death. During his lifetime, Mikhail paid little tribute. But in the same year, the only book published while he was alive. He was recognized and loved, he became a respected man, but it happened too late. The poet's mind was clouded by ailment. The death occurred on a bed in a psychiatric hospital in 1889 in Bucharest.



In a sense, it is a pity that such people are noticed after death. However, their feat can be called all the more magnificent. After all, this poet held fast to his views, not staggering from the blows of fate. For all his sensuality and creative outlook on life, he kept a fire in himself to go through all the barriers. And only at the end of his life he gave slack and allowed the disease to overcome himself. He is worthy of eternal memory and respect. Today, grateful descendants honor him.
