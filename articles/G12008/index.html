Hotel Moskovskaya

Number of rooms

At this stage of its development, the Moskovskaya Hotel (Kazan) offers its guests 63 excellent rooms, each of which has its own advantages. Here you can stay in a standard room or another room, representing a slightly higher level of comfort, which offers a gorgeous view of the capital of Tatarstan.





In addition, it should be noted that in addition to this project there is a not very large hostel with a capacity of 58 beds. Nearby Attractions

Have you already wondered why tourists come to cities of other countries for a vacation? Of course, they are interested in local attractions. Hotels that are located near the most important historical buildings of a particular locality are in greater demand than similar establishments located at a great distance from attractions.





If you stay in a city like Kazan, the Moskovskaya Hotel, the prices of which we will discuss a little later in this article, you should definitely be suitable. Hostel prices

As you remember, it was previously noted that the Moskovskaya Hotel (Kazan) has a small hostel for additional accommodation. In this case, 58 places are available for order, which is 12 rooms. It is possible to rent the only standard room, which can accommodate 8 people, at a price of 800 rubles for 1 place. There is a toilet and shower, fan, TV, refrigerator and telephone.





In turn, the remaining 11 rooms can accommodate a total of 50 people. The cost of renting one place is 500 rubles, and the rooms have a sanitary unit with shower, refrigerator, TV, fan and telephone. Please note that the price does not include breakfast!
