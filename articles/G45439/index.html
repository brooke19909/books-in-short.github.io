Samsung 5250: review, specifications, photos

Excellent from a technical point of view and very functional in terms of equipment, the device is the Samsung 5250. The main disadvantage of this device is the system software. But he has much more advantages. But first things first. A detailed review of the device will be given in this material.





The niche of this smartphone

Initially, the Samsung 5250 was aimed at the niche of the most affordable, budget gadgets in 2010. The characteristics of the technical plan indicate this. His cost was also appropriate. The key advantage of this model was the system software, which was its own development of the South Korean smartphone manufacturer - Bada. This OS combined the best features of the two leading operating systems of that time - Android and iOS. The disadvantage of this platform was that the set of application software was very modest. As a result, it was this shortcoming that led to the fact that even the manufacturer itself ceased to support this software platform. Otherwise, this device was one of the most affordable gadgets of the time. At the same time, he had very good technical specifications. And its drawback was in system software and a small amount of additional application software.

The last component is a memory card. It will significantly increase the amount of stored information on a mobile device.
