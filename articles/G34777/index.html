Recreation center “Togot”, Baikal: location, services and reviews of tourists

The majestic Lake Baikal, its amazing and sacred beauty attracts many tourists to the Irkutsk region. In these protected places there is a unity of nature and man, here you can enjoy beautiful Baikal views, clean air and silence.

Baikal and its Small Sea

On the coast of Lake Baikal, in its middle part, between the island of Olkhon and its coastline, there is the Small (or Narrow) Sea. The landscape of the coast allows you to equip convenient parking for tourists and tourist centers - in these places, nature has created many shallow bays and bays. Mukhorsky is the warmest of them. In these places, the lake is rather shallow in its coastal zone, therefore, in the summer, the water warms up, and its temperature becomes very comfortable.





The natural gentle slope of the bottom of the lake ensures safety when children swim in it. Fishing lovers at any time of the year in these places receive positive emotions from the quantity and uniqueness of the catch. The beautiful nature reserve of the surrounding places allows its fans to enjoy peace and quiet. On the coast of the Gulf of Mukhor is the recreation center Togot. Baikal and its natural sophistication are attractive for guests who are willing to book rooms here regardless of the season.

Recreation center Togot, Baikal: how to get there?

Mukhorsky Bay is located at a sufficient distance from Irkutsk, the distance is less than four hundred kilometers. Recreation center Togot (Baikal) has convenient transport links with this city - daily bus or minibus flights are organized, the fare for one person is set within one thousand rubles. The time spent on the journey in one direction will be about four hours by bus, tourists using their own car will get here faster.





For the convenience of guests with private cars, there is a guarded parking lot near the recreation center. The rules of residence at the base prohibit the use of a car on its territory.

Togot - recreation center, Baikal: living conditions

Guests of the recreation center are accommodated in comfortable wooden cottages. In each of them there are ten rooms of the category "standard" and four rooms of the class "luxury", which have a separate entrance. Eco-style was used in the design of rooms and other rooms, all elements - ceiling, walls, floor - are made of natural wood. In the interior of each of the forty-five guest rooms there is the necessary furniture, its set depends on the room category.



In each room there is the possibility of organizing an extra bed upon prior request of the guest. Bathrooms of triple apartments are located in a section designed for three rooms, the rest have their own toilet rooms. The family room has its own kitchen, you can cook yourself here, all the equipment and utensils for this are available. Satellite TV is available to all guests.

Recreation center Togot (Baikal) and its territory have good conditions for guests to spend active holidays. There is a beach equipped with sunbeds, there is a private pier. Vacationers can rent boats and catamarans, they will be offered boat trips on the lake and organize fishing. Togot Plus is a recreation center (Baikal), which offers guests sports entertainment. Table tennis, equipped volleyball court, billiards, air hockey and a disco will allow you to actively spend your free time.





A visit to the Russian bath, cozy gazebos and barbecue facilities on the territory diversify the rest. The leisure of children is thought out: for them there is a playroom and a playground on the territory of the Togot complex. The recreation center (Baikal) is proud of the cuisine of its restaurant.

Guest reviews about the rest

Popular on the Small Sea is Togot - a recreation center (Baikal). Reviews of numerous tourists indicate that the owners of the base provide coziness and comfort to guests. Magnificent views of Lake Baikal and its environs, peace and quiet - all this is found here by travelers. Bad access roads to the base disturb guests. There are some comments on the work of the restaurant - tourists would like to see a greater variety in the menu of fresh fruits and vegetables, and small portions are also not happy with them.
