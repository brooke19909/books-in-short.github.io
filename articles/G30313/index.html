Resorts in Germany: choice of holidays, the best resorts, description of hotels, procedures, reviews and recommendations

Wellness German spas have long been famous for their strict standards of preventive and therapeutic procedures. Medicine in this country occupies one of the first positions in the accuracy of the diagnosis of diseases, which is why many tourists trust their doctors in Germany.

Germany is a country in which a variety of types of tourism and leisure are developed. For example, the famous resorts of Bad Kissingen and Baden-Baden offer a wide range of medical activities. The article presents the best resorts in Germany, where you can not only relax, but also improve your health.

The advantage of German resorts is that most of them are located in amazing areas, surrounded on all sides by beautiful forests. And this, as you know, has a very beneficial effect on the restoration of the body and health. In addition, here you can see interesting ancient sights that are closely intertwined with modernity.

General information about German health resorts

Thermal resorts in Germany with current sources have gained particular fame among the inhabitants of Western Europe. They successfully combine beneficial natural factors and German-quality medical care. All this in a complex allows spa treatment in Germany to be called one of the best along with the resorts of Switzerland and Hungary.

Premium sanatoriums built in areas of mineral springs are a real attraction that Germany can rightfully be proud of. There are many geothermal treatment centers in this country; in Bavaria alone there are approximately 50 mineral springs. Medical resorts in Germany are available in all regions of this country.

Treatment features

Therapeutic spa thermal complexes have swimming and thermal pools with water of different temperatures. Physiotherapeutic and mud procedures are carried out in them. However, such spa treatment is carried out at the bases of sanatoriums and clinics of the resort.

In clinics, you can undergo a full range of body tests (Check Up), as well as inpatient and outpatient treatment. It should be noted that in Baden-Baden there are the best private clinics in Germany with powerful and modern diagnostic equipment. Many German resorts have drinking mineral water, which effectively treats diseases associated with metabolic and gastrointestinal disorders. For example, in Bad Kissingen there are both mineral and thermal waters. The largest source temperature is 75 degrees.

Additionally, in addition to the variety of proposed procedures, one can note moderate climatic conditions, which also favorably affect the body. As for the weather, in both winter and summer, it is soft and pleasant. And the price range here is quite diverse, everyone can choose a place of treatment according to their capabilities.

Balneological resorts in Germany

German thermal resorts are rightly considered the most elite in Europe. Many resort areas are located in ecologically clean regions where there is no industrial production.

The presence of thermal springs with water of various chemical composition and therapeutic mud allows the treatment of a wide range of diseases. Sanatoriums specialize in the treatment of the gastrointestinal tract, musculoskeletal system, genitourinary, nervous and cardiovascular systems, and here you can also normalize the metabolism and treat respiratory diseases.

The following tourist resorts in Germany are most popular with tourists: Bad Kissingen, Bad Reichenhall and Baden-Baden.

Baden Baden

Many Europeans prefer rest and treatment in Germany in Baden-Baden. This is the oldest and most famous resort city in Europe. In his sanatoriums, you can go through the prevention and treatment of a variety of diseases. In total, there are 20 thermal springs in the territory of this resort, around which picturesque parks stretch. Among this beauty are cozy hotels.

The German resort of Baden-Baden is located west of Stuttgart. In the summer, lovers of active leisure and ecotourism lovers prefer to come here. People who want to just relax from the bustle of the city and improve their health with the help of the famous hot and mineral springs are here.

This resort has been known since the time of the Roman Empire, as evidenced by the remains of thermal Roman baths. These places were once visited by many poets and writers of Russia. Representatives of famous noble families were treated here.

There are theaters and museums in this small town, as well as modern resort hotels, including the Radisson Blu Badischer Hof 5 *, Leonardo Royal Hotel, Dorint Maison Messmer, Heliopark Bad Hotel zum Hirsch and many others.

Bad Kissingen

This balneological resort is located in a surprisingly picturesque river valley. It offers vacationers salt and mud curative healing programs.

Water sources and healing mud are the main natural healing factors of this German resort. Mineral waters are carbon dioxide, ferrous chloride-sodium. Today, there are seven sources, the waters of which are used for baths, drinking treatment and inhalation.

Hot mud treatments in Bad Kissingen have been under way since the beginning of the 19th century. This local healing product, containing many healing biological substances, uniformly and slowly transfers its heat to the human body, so that the healing properties of this mud reach the deepest muscles and internal organs. Especially successful mud wraps and baths are used for joint diseases, rheumatism, gynecological diseases, as well as general stress conditions. Resort hotels: Hotel Weisses Haus 4 *, Hotel Sonnenhügel, Hotel Frankenland 4 * and many others other

Bad Reichenhall

This wonderful German resort is located in an alpine valley, stretching between the picturesque pine-covered mountain ranges. This area is located on the border of Germany and Austria, 120 kilometers from the city of Munich.

In the spa park is the famous Atlas-sole Brunnen fountain. Healing water that has the properties of a natural inhaler. To conduct medical procedures around the perimeter of this fountain, sun loungers are placed on which people suffering from asthma, allergies and other respiratory diseases are arranged.

There is another inhalation nearby - Gradiverwerk, surrounded by hedges of thorns and hawthorn bushes. Its length is about 160 meters, height - 13 m. According to doctors, a stay of about two hours a day at the healing fountains can replace a daily stay at sea. There are many hotels in the town, among which the best are Galerie-Hotel Bad, Galerie-Hotel Bad, Hotel Bayern Vital, etc.
