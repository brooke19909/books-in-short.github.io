A trip to Omsk on vacation. Recreation centers in Omsk: description, photo

Where to spend your holidays? Recreation centers in Omsk are in demand, and most of the reviews from tourists praise all the options available in this territory.

This article will consider the complexes that really deserve to spend the time of the long-awaited vacation here.

"Rainbow"

Any vacation should be unforgettable. Recreation facilities in Omsk amaze service and the surrounding nature. "Rainbow" is located in an area where the climate is notable for its purity, the local atmosphere is really healing.

There is a pool, parking, sports and children's playgrounds. None of the guests will definitely be able to get bored while being here.



White Sail

Spent a vacation on the banks of the Irtysh - the best vacation. Recreation facilities in Omsk allow you to improve your positive emotions from being here. "White Sail" receives guests every year. Unfortunately, the complex is small (up to 40 people at a time), but this does not prevent it from being in demand and famous. The residential building includes rooms for 2, 3, 4 residents. There is a sauna, billiard room, pool, sports ground. You can order a banquet or a celebration.

Prices for services vary quite a lot, especially due to the changing seasons. Therefore, everyone, from any segment of the population, can afford entertainment at this base. It is worth noting that none of those who visited this complex did not regret that he decided to stay here.

Base of rest them. Stelnikova

Everyone wants to spend an unforgettable long-awaited vacation. Recreation facilities in Omsk are shocking in their quantity, therefore it is necessary to determine the living conditions, the services offered and, of course, the prices. It is recommended to pay attention to the complex to them. Stelnikova. It is located just 50 km from Omsk. Due to the unique climate of this region, many people come here every year who want to improve their health.

Among the rooms where guests can be accommodated, there are both economy class options and suites. Most vacationers like the fact that near the base there is a place for spending time together with a company where no one will interfere. For family vacations, special houses for 2-6 people are designed. If you need to do wellness, then you can visit the pool, the water in which is saturated with minerals.

"Fairy tale"

30 km from Omsk there is a recreation center "Fairy Tale". It is on it that you can find peace and tranquility. Here, the peace of vacationers is rarely disturbed, and the singing of birds and the noise of the nearest body of water only have a calming effect on the nervous system.

"Fairy Tale" (recreation center in Omsk) consists of 7 buildings, where there are a sufficient number of rooms. They differ in their price category. There are family rooms, wedding, children, economy, luxury. You can stay in a house or a cottage. In 2013, a modern restaurant was opened. According to the menu, it is allowed to choose individual dishes for breakfast, lunch and dinner. Nearby there is a cheap cafe where you can send children to eat ice cream.

"Fairy Tale" - a recreation center in Omsk, which has become popular because of the water park. For his sake, tourists stay in this complex. The interiors of rooms and banquet halls make even true aesthetes marvel. It is really pretty enough here.



Political Department

The "Political Department" in Omsk is a recreation center that has become quite popular quite recently. Most often, students, schoolchildren, outdoor enthusiasts come here. The complex welcomes guests every year. On its territory there is a water park. Guests are accommodated in a large building, which consists of a huge number of rooms. There is a billiard room, gyms for sports, a tennis court, restaurants, a dining room, several spas, a sauna, etc.

For those who are nonresident, a shuttle service can be provided that will meet the guest at the station and take him directly to the recreation center. Those arriving by car may leave their vehicle in the local parking lot. It is well guarded, so do not worry.



"Petrel"

"Petrel" is a recreation center in Chernuchuchye. Omsk is only 30 km from it. The complex is located in the forest, which allows people who arrived to improve their health. It takes guests all year round.

The conditions in which the guests will live are chosen directly by them. There are comfortable economy options, as well as cozy and modern houses with all the amenities inside. It is possible to choose a package that guarantees food already included in the price. However, if there is a desire, food can be prepared independently in the kitchen.



The base has such nice extras as a banquet hall, a bathhouse, a sauna, a swimming pool, a relaxation room, a sports ground, and gazebos. In winter, you can rent skates or skis and experience the local mountain trail.
