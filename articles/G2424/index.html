Personal finance: how to withdraw money with Webmoney?

Today, virtual money is widespread due to many advantages. So, for example, you can reduce the time for registration and payment of the purchase, while it is not necessary to be present on the spot, and you can purchase goods from anywhere in the world. This is very convenient and does not cause much trouble with the account and storage of cash, compared with cash. There are several electronic payment systems that allow you to carry out various operations, such as exchange for other types of currencies, transfer and others. However, there are often situations in which you need to have cash, and then the question arises, for example: how to withdraw money from Webmoney? Consider this case in more detail.





Withdrawal methods

Before you withdraw money with Webmoney, you need to choose how you want to receive these virtual funds. The following output methods can be distinguished:

  • in cash;
  • to a bank account;
  • to an account in another electronic system.

It may be noted that for all three withdrawal methods, you must have a Webmoney certificate no lower than formal, that is, provide copies of documents (passports and other identification documents).

It can also be noted that the methods of selling virtual funds may differ from the type of currency in this system. And this can be influenced by such a factor as residence status, that is, confirmation of residence in the territory of a given country. So, for local residents of the Russian Federation, the sale of WMR can be carried out by several methods, while for representatives of other countries there are some restrictions.





How to withdraw money with Webmoney in cash?

There are several methods or ways to sell electronic amounts, the main of which can be represented in the following list:

  • Postal transfer . At the same time, a fee for services is charged in the amount of 1.2% of the total amount, the payment term takes 2-3 days.
  • Through Western Union , it should be borne in mind that you can withdraw only WMZ and the fee is withdrawn in the amount of 3.5%. The operation time is about 48 hours.
  • Money transfer . Type of currency - Russian rubles. Remuneration - 0.5%, transaction time - 0.5-24 hours.
  • Dealers and Webmoney exchange points . Currency type - WME, WMU, WMZ, WMG and WMR. The operation is instantaneous, a reward of 1% and higher.
  • Through an office partner or bank branch . In this case, you can exchange WMZ, WME and WMB, with a fee depending on the established conditions of a particular bank - 2.5% and higher.
  • Through the exchange exchanger.ru . Currency type - all types of virtual money Webmoney. Compensation - 0% and above, operation time - about 1 day.
  • Getting gold bullion . Type of currency - WMG. Operation time - up to 10 days, reward - 5% and more.




How to withdraw money from Webmoney to a bank account?

Electronic funds can also be transferred to a credit or debit card issued by a commercial branch of a financial institution. This can be done in the following ways:

  • To the bank card account . At the same time, you can transfer dollars, Russian and Belarusian rubles, Ukrainian hryvnia. The operation can take up to 48 hours, the commission is 0%.
  • Transfer through a bank . All types of currencies Webmoney. Term - 1-24 hours, remuneration - 0%.
  • Through online banking . Translation is possible only for WMR. Commission - 1% and above, term - no more than 0.5 hours.
  • To the map of the Ukrainian electronic system (NSMEP) . Type of currency - WMU. Commission - up to 2.4%, completion date - no more than 0.5 hours.

Withdraw money from Webmoney to other electronic systems

This operation can be carried out by the following methods:

  • Electronic money . All types of currencies, reward 0% and higher, depending on the selected service. You can select an exchange office in the side menu on the Webmoney website page (for each type of currency, the lists may vary).
  • Yandex system wallet . Type of currency - WMR. Commission - 4.5%, the operation takes no more than 30 minutes.




For non-residents of the Russian Federation, you can choose the withdrawal methods on the Webmoney website by entering the name of your city. The system will automatically display the possible options for exchanging virtual money for cash in the desired region. One of the important points should be noted - when deciding how to withdraw money through Webmoney, you need to follow simple precautions. For example, do not enter the personal page of the payment system from someone else's computer, allow the browser to remember passwords, and so on. It is also good to install a good antivirus program, in this case you can protect yourself from illegal hacking by intruders.
