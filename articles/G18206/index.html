Mortgage for private entrepreneurs in Sberbank: conditions, documents, interest rate

The number of individual entrepreneurs in our country is increasing every year. The government develops and implements benefits and subsidies to support small and medium-sized businesses. But any commercial activity requires investment of material resources. For its expansion, it is most often necessary to purchase real estate. Not always in the hands of the entrepreneur is such a large amount of money, and he is forced to apply for a loan in a banking institution. In 2019, Sberbank offers several mortgage programs for businessmen at very attractive interest rates. Let us consider in more detail all these proposals, find out how it is possible to get a loan, and what documents are needed for that.

Mortgage for individual entrepreneurs in PJSC Sberbank

A bank can receive a mortgage both an individual and a legal entity, in particular, an individual entrepreneur. In order to protect itself from an unreliable and insolvent client, Sberbank examines more carefully the application and documents from the entrepreneur, who, in turn, needs to prove his solvency. Therefore, the list of documents for obtaining a mortgage for an individual entrepreneur at Sberbank necessarily includes documents to confirm the financial condition.

First of all, you need to decide who the mortgage will be drawn up for: an individual or an individual entrepreneur. The conditions and terms of issue will depend on this.

Sberbank PJSC requires an individual entrepreneur to have a business life of six months and have a satisfactory credit history. In some cases, the bank has the right to demand a guarantee from a family member of the entrepreneur or a third party.

Factors contributing to the approval of mortgages for entrepreneurs in Sberbank

These factors include:

  • If an entrepreneur works on a common taxation system, under such conditions the client is more attractive.
  • There is a positive credit history in Sberbank, and information is taken into account not only on a mortgage loan, but also on any loans.
  • The business of the potential borrower has no seasonal dependence.
  • The company has been operating since registration for at least one year.
  • Absolutely transparent income of the entrepreneur, confirmed by the relevant securities.

From the above we can conclude: in order to approve a mortgage loan, an individual entrepreneur must confirm its financial stability for timely repayment of a mortgage debt and provide a full package of documents.

Conditions of mortgage programs for entrepreneurs

Sberbank offers its customers, individual entrepreneurs, the purchase of housing both for their own needs and for expanding the business. But the mortgage conditions for private equity in Sberbank are a little tougher than for conventional mortgage programs. Typically, the difference lies in the interest rate, which is 1-2% higher, as well as in the term of the loan, which is reduced to 15-20 years. In some programs, the down payment amount is increased to 25%.

Residential Property Acquisition

Residential properties acquired under a mortgage loan include the following objects: a residential building, an apartment (both in a new building and in the secondary market), housing construction, suburban real estate, as well as a garage and a parking place.

Individual program selection

The selection of a loan program is discussed individually. The loan amount may depend on the activities of the entrepreneur and the entity being located.

It is worth paying attention to the fact that mortgage rates for individuals in Sberbank start at 11% per annum, and for individuals they are lower. The loan rate may increase by another 1% in the event of an entrepreneur‚Äôs refusal to insure his life for the period of execution of the mortgage transaction.

How to calculate repayment?

For a preliminary calculation of a mortgage loan, you can use the online calculator, with which you can get the following information:

  • what will be the sum of the total overpayment on the loan;
  • what should be the minimum income level;
  • what will be the size of the monthly payment.

To obtain such information, you must specify the amount, term, interest rate of the loan, date of issue of the loan and type of payment. As a result, the user receives information that he can study, evaluate his solvency.

Documents required for a mortgage

After you have decided on a lending program at Sberbank, you need to collect certificates. The list of documents from the IP for a mortgage in Sberbank is as follows:

  • Passport of a citizen of the Russian Federation with permanent registration. If there is a temporary certificate confirming this.
  • Extract on state registration of individual entrepreneurs.
  • Documents confirming the introduction of certain activities.
  • Tax reporting: for ONS it is necessary to provide a declaration for the last year, for DOS - information for 2 years, for personal income tax for the previous reporting period.
  • Documentation of property that goes under the security of the bank: ownership, contract of sale.

additional information

After reviewing the submitted documents, the banking institution has the right to require additional information from you on the financial reliability of the client. Therefore, you must be prepared for what you will be asked about marital status, the availability of children, asked for data on the income of the spouse, etc. You can indicate your additional earnings in the mortgage application form without proof of income.

If before filing an application for a mortgage for an individual entrepreneur at Sberbank, you have not decided on the final version of the acquired property, then after obtaining approval, you must give the bank a full package of documentation.

Order of receipt

The process of obtaining a loan by an individual entrepreneur is given below:

  • Mandatory study of all conditions under the current program; To get a mortgage in Sberbank, the calculator will help you make the choice in favor of the most favorable conditions.
  • Preparation of relevant documents and certificates, with a validity period of not more than 30 days from the date of signing.
  • Filling out the application and submitting a loan application.
  • Sberbank analyzes the borrower and makes a final decision.
  • Searches for premises to purchase.
  • A preliminary sales contract is concluded with the seller, where the point of purchase with the help of borrowed funds is necessarily indicated, and the procedure and timing of settlement between the parties to the transaction are also specified.
  • A property insurance contract is concluded, and health if desired.
  • A loan agreement and a mortgage agreement are signed.
  • An initial payment is made if it is provided for by the selected program of the bank.
  • The transaction is state-registered at Rosreestr and executed in favor of Sberbank.
  • The remaining funds are transferred to the seller‚Äôs account.

After the transaction is completed, the borrower must fulfill the executed obligations according to the schedule of monthly mortgage payments for the individual entrepreneur in the bank.

conclusions

From the above it can be concluded: each solvent individual entrepreneur can get a mortgage loan at Sberbank PJSC for the purchase of any type of real estate. And on quite favorable terms.
