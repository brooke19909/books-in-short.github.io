Hotel parks in Barnaul: rating, description, features and reviews

Barnaul is a city with a special atmosphere. People come here for various reasons. There is something to see and you can even have a good time. Barnaul hotels are designed for a different contingent of tourists. There are budget options and expensive hotels here. Park hotels are a special category in the accommodation sector . There are several such establishments in Barnaul . The most popular among tourists and locals will be discussed in this article.

The data will be presented on the basis of the rating, the first place in which is occupied by an excellent institution called "The Seagull".

Park Hotel "The Seagull"





A rather unusual place with a special atmosphere is the Chaika Hotel. Park Hotel (Barnaul is proud of it) is located in a very picturesque corner and has a modern design. The attentive administration monitors all the fashionable news and tries to improve its institution. This paradise is a 20-minute drive from the outskirts of Barnaul. Park Hotel is located in a pine forest, almost on the banks of the Barnaulka River. Old pine trees grow here, which in the heat create a beautiful shade, so it does not happen hot here. But the water in the river is always cool and pleasant for swimming. The Seagull (park-hotel) will definitely save you from the summer heat. Barnaul in the summer is quite hot in the weather.

The territory of the institution is divided into two zones - for adults and for children. This is done with the aim of convenience and increase the comfort of guests.

Numerous customer reviews of the institution indicate that the park hotel took care of the quality of the services provided, the range of which is very wide. Living conditions allow you to organize not only leisure, but also special occasions, such as a wedding or anniversary. You can also arrange for business meetings and other programs.

Barnaul hotels in the lap of nature - it is not only coziness and comfort, but also clean air, beautiful landscapes and a good atmosphere.

Infrastructure





On the territory there are a large number of facilities for recreation and sports. There is a restaurant for 50 people, where you can always eat deliciously with exclusively fresh dishes. Banquet hall for special events is designed for 250 people. According to the taste preferences of customers, the room can be decorated and even thematically decorated dishes. There is also a large and bright conference room for up to 250 people. It will be convenient to conduct any kind of business negotiations or social events.

For sports guests, a gym is equipped with showers, towels and changing rooms. Beautifully equipped outdoor pool of impressive size. Around it is a relaxation area with sun loungers and umbrellas. There is a wooden Russian bath, which is built in accordance with all the rules.

Colorful and functional playgrounds are arranged for children, on which everyone will enjoy playing. There is also a small sports ground where you can try your hand. All this is on the street, so during the game the children breathe fresh coniferous air. The room has a special children's room with toys, a TV and computers.

For newlyweds, there is an incredibly beautiful area for registering marriage and an exit ceremony. And all this splendor is far from city noise, fuss and dust.

Rooms

Here you can find a room for every taste and pocket. There are budget options, as well as more expensive rooms with all amenities.

  • Economy double Twin. This room has the necessary set of furniture, consisting of two single beds, bedside tables, wardrobe, desk and chair. There is a small toilet room, but the washbasin and shower are on the floor. The room has a balcony that connects all the other apartments of this category.
  • Economy + Double. This number is more suitable for young couples. As in the previous one, there is everything you need, only a double bed, so the spouses will be familiar, the hotel took care of such trifles.
  • Standard. This superior room. There is a bathroom with toilet, sink and shower. Towels and mini cosmetics are provided.
  • Junior Suite The room has a double bed, desk, bedside tables. There is also a soft and comfortable sofa with two armchairs, a small coffee table, a flat-screen TV and a minibar with everything you need. There is a bathroom with a shower, towels, slippers and bathrobes.

Description

In second place in the ranking of hotel complexes in nature is Park Hotel Solnechny (Barnaul).





This place is located in a clean corner of nature. It is easy to get to the hotel, it is located just 15 km from Barnaul. This place can be called exclusive. The nature here is clean, untouched. The hotel offers its guests comfortable and beautiful rooms. It is interesting that each room is individually decorated, and there is no second one. Designers have tried their best.

Leisure

On the territory there is a wonderful fitness center where a professional instructor will help to solve any problem. The highlight of the hotel is a cozy restaurant in the style of the 19th century. Nearby is the beach, which pleases not only with cool water and clean sand, but also with picturesque landscapes. Each guest takes with him only good impressions and unforgettable memories. The staff annually takes advanced training courses and knows their job very well.

Number of rooms





The hotel offers rooms of different categories, namely:

  • Single room equipped with standard furniture. Private bathroom with shower, toilet and sink.
  • Double room (superior). In addition to a standard set of furniture and a bathroom, there is a TV on which more channels are broadcast.
  • Double room (connected). The room is quite spacious. It includes two bedrooms and a guest room. The bedrooms are decorated in a classic style and are equipped with the necessary furniture. The guest has a comfortable sofa, armchairs. There is a minibar, TV, air conditioning. The bathroom has a jacuzzi and hairdryer.
  • Suite Very comfortable and spacious, equipped with comfortable furniture, a spacious bathroom with a jacuzzi.

The honorable third place in the ranking takes a unique place for recreation, presented below.

Park Hotel Lesnie Dali





This hotel is located quite close to Barnaul. It takes only 20-30 minutes to get to the city center. Therefore, all the necessary institutions, pharmacies, restaurants and shops are always at hand.

This is not just a hotel. Park Hotel Lesnie Dali (Barnaul) is a whole complex that includes the hotel itself, a grill bar, a spacious entertainment center that can offer a huge range of services. There is also a large outdoor pool. This is not a complete list of services offered. Not all Barnaul hotels can be compared with this institution in terms of comfort.





Hotel guests live in a conservation area. To build a building, not a single tree was destroyed. Therefore, guests are not allowed to harm the environment in any way. The rooms have everything you need, they are comfortably furnished with new furniture. Hotel "Forest Dali" in Barnaul is especially popular not only among tourists, but also local residents. Reviews of the complex testify to its uniqueness and good value for money. The cost of living here is relatively low, and the quality of service is up to par.

Regarding nutrition, there are several wonderful establishments that will delight you with a delicious and varied cuisine.

The staff is very concerned about the leisure of their guests, therefore arranges a variety of activities, trips and other interesting programs.

A visit to a real Russian bath is offered.

Here they are very responsible for organizing all kinds of events. For this purpose, a whole team of professionals was hired to help solve any problem at the highest level.

Having visited the hotels of Barnaul described above, you can feel closer to nature and at the same time not feel a lack of comfort.
