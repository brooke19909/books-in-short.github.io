Hotel Holiday Village 4 * (Bulgaria): photos and reviews of tourists

The winter months are a great time to take care of the upcoming summer holidays. Choosing a place to stay, you should pay attention to the resorts of Bulgaria. High quality service, friendly staff, a clean sea and a large number of attractions are what make a vacation here unforgettable. Arriving here, many tourists stay in the Holiday Village 4 * complex (Bulgaria). Tours here are especially popular with tourists with children. But does the price of the voucher correspond to the quality of the provided vacation?

Features of rest in Bulgaria

Bulgaria, located on the Black Sea coast, offers all kinds of leisure options. The country has built a lot of hotels for a quiet family and youth holidays. People come here for the excursion program. Each city has a rich cultural heritage, which includes many architectural monuments. Recently, religious tourism is also gaining popularity. In Bulgaria there are many ancient temples and sacred places that are worth visiting for any believer. And, of course, the medical direction, which offers tourists to visit salt and mud springs with healing power.





It is best to come here in the summer. Mid-June is traditionally considered the beginning of the tourist season. Warm weather has been around since the beginning of May, but the sea warms up only by the beginning of summer. The most popular months are July and August. At this time, the air temperature rises to +30, and the sea warms up to +28 degrees. It is worth paying attention to September, when the velvet season begins in Bulgaria. Most tourists leave the country's resorts, but the weather is still warm. It is best to go in early September, when you can still swim in the sea.

Basic hotel information

Holiday Village 4 * (Bulgaria) is part of a huge tourist complex located in a picturesque park. The hotel is a few small villas located nearby from each other. The complex was built in 1986, and the last update of all rooms here ended in 2008. Spacious two-story villas offer about 300 rooms for tourists. There are rooms for single travelers and couples. Unlike many other hotels in the country, the staff does not speak Russian, providing services only in their native and English. The hotel is often confused with the complex Santa Marina Holiday Village 4 * (Bulgaria), which has a similar name.





Check-in starts at 14:00. Tourists can come with children of any age. At the same time, children under two years of age are given significant discounts on accommodation. To come here with pets does not work, because their presence here is prohibited. When servicing, bank cards Visa and MasterCard are accepted. At the end of the holiday, guests must leave the hotel no later than noon.

Hotel location: where to go and what sights to see

Holiday Village 4 * villas (Bulgaria) are part of the Duni Royal Resort tourist complex, which includes several more hotels. It is located in the picturesque part of the resort of Dunes, which was built only a quarter century ago. The modern village is located in the nature reserve, therefore it is considered an environmentally friendly place. In the evenings, live music is played in the amphitheater. Tourists can also visit a yacht club, play tennis on free courts, and take mini-golf lessons.

You can get to the Holiday Village 4 * hotel (Bulgaria, Duni) from Burgas Airport, which is 50 km from the village. Tourists can rent a car, order a taxi or buy a ticket for the intercity bus. The road to the villas will take a little more than an hour. 7 km from the complex is Sozopol, which is one of the most beautiful cities in Bulgaria. The beach is 300 meters away. The journey time to it is approximately 10 minutes through the pine park, which surrounds the complex. Nearby points of interest also include:

  • Amphitheater "Apolonia" (5 km);
  • port of Sozopol (5 km);
  • Rapotamo Nature Reserve (6 km);
  • water park "Aqua Planets" (12 km).

Housing stock

The room complex of the Holiday Village complex (Bulgaria) offers 297 rooms located in the villas. Each building is surrounded by dense vegetation. The building has been renovated in a classic style. The walls are painted in beige tones and ceramic tiles. The rooms have a private balcony and the first floor has a terrace. The furniture here has beds, a wardrobe, bedside tables. The rooms are also decorated with curtains, lamps and paintings. The room is cleaned daily, and linen and towels are changed 2 times a week. For guests are offered several types of rooms:

  • Standard. It offers a view of the park or partially the sea. The room has two separate beds and a folding chair. 2-3 people can live here at the same time. Rooms are 26 square meters. m
  • Apartments. Their area varies from 33 to 36 square meters. m. The room has a separate bedroom and living room. Equipped with a double bed and a sofa bed. It is assumed that a family of two adults and two children will live here.

  • Electric kettle. Tourists can make tea or coffee directly in the room. Accessories for these drinks are not provided, so you should take care of their availability in advance.

  • Central air conditioning that keeps the room cool during the hot summer months. It works from mid-June to early September.
  • Paid safe for storing valuables and documents.
  • Telephone. In addition to regular calls, guests can order paid room service: newspapers, drinks and food.
  • Separate bathroom for each room. There is a bath or shower, a washbasin with a large mirror, a hairdryer, towels, and toiletries.
  • Wi-Fi. Provided for a fee.
  • There is a clothes dryer on the balcony.

Children's holiday

Villas Holiday Village 4 * (Bulgaria) are considered a good option for families. A cradle is provided free of charge for children, and restaurants are equipped with high chairs. Good discounts on accommodation are provided depending on the age of the children. There is also a shallow pool designed for bathing babies.

Daily children's animation works. For children of all ages there is a mini-club, which has a department for teenagers. Children's discos are regularly organized, games, tournaments, quizzes and other competitions are held. An outdoor playground is available.





Positive reviews

Many tourists who visited Holiday Village 4 * (Bulgaria) leave good reviews. In their opinion, the advantages of the hotel are:

  • Very varied food. There are many types of meat (chicken, pork, turkey), as well as fish. Great menu for kids. The food does not have time to get bored, everything is very tasty.

Negative reviews

Positive reviews create a pleasant impression of the tourist complex. But do not forget about negative opinions. Tourists note the following shortcomings with which they were surprised by the Holiday Village 4 * hotel (Bulgaria):

  • Hotel description is not true. The phone did not work in the room. For the entire time of rest, it was not repaired. Refrigerators are old and do not cool food well. There is no electric kettle.
  • Dirty dishes are found in the main restaurant.
  • The rooms are not cleaned very carefully, there is a lot of dust on the cabinets and cabinets.
  • The poor quality of alcoholic drinks, it seems that they are diluted with water to save.
  • Mold in the closet.
  • Internet does not work in the evenings.

Should I relax at this hotel

Without a doubt, this complex will be a great place to relax with children. Let it have some drawbacks, but they are not significant. If you want to relax in an ecologically clean and quiet area surrounded by trees and shrubs, you can choose this hotel without hesitation. Youth companies that love outdoor activities may find it boring here. There are no huge nightclubs and bars in the area. Do not confuse the hotel with Santa Marina Holiday Village 4 * (Bulgaria). The complex has a similar name, but is a completely different place to relax.
