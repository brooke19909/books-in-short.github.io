Hurghada Marriott Red Sea Resort 5 * hotel in Hurghada: overview, description and reviews of tourists

Hurghada is one of the most popular tourist areas of Egypt. Travelers staying here want to relax on the beach, surrounded by exotic oriental nature. Of course, more and more hotel complexes open on the coast every year. One of them is the Hurghada Marriott Red Sea Resort. This is a wonderful place where guests can have fun. Naturally, for a start it is worth finding out more about this hotel. So is it worth it to come here?

Description of location, photo of the territory





The large hotel complex Hurghada Marriott Red Sea Resort 5 * is located in a calm area, almost on the seashore. On the other hand, the distance to the vibrant center of Hurghada with all its entertainment, night clubs and historical sights is 5 km. It is easy to overcome by using public transport or taxi services. The international airport is 7 km away, which is also convenient: you do not have to spend several hours on the bus.

Hurghada Marriott Red Sea Resort is a large six-story building dating back to 1995. By the way, the last thorough reconstruction was carried out in 2010, so do not worry - the hotel meets all standards. The territory is spacious, generously decorated with greenery. Its area is 15 thousand square meters, so you will have a place to walk.

Hotel Hurghada Marriott Red Sea Resort 5 * (Hurghada): room description





This hotel can be considered relatively large, as in its territory there are 283 rooms. For the most part, these are standard rooms, although there are 25 suites, as well as 3 specially equipped rooms that are suitable for the comfortable living of people with disabilities.

According to reviews, the rooms here are clean, spacious and stand out in a stylish, contemporary setting. Naturally, you will be provided with a large bed and other comfortable furniture, as well as modern appliances. In particular, the rooms are equipped with air conditioning, which can be controlled using a convenient remote control. There is a large-screen TV, a telephone, a convenient small safe and constant access to the Internet (the connection speed, by the way, is quite good).

There is also a private bathroom. There is a shower, washbasin, mirror, modern plumbing, a set of towels, cosmetics and, of course, a hair dryer.

Meal plan for guests





Like most hotels, Hurghada Marriott Red Sea Resort provides its guests with full meals. Buffet receptions are organized three times a day in the main restaurant. According to reviews, the choice of food here is very large, there is a special diet menu, as well as dishes for children.

The hotel has several other restaurants where you can have a good time. For example, a gourmet dinner awaits you in an Italian restaurant. You should definitely visit the cafe-pastry shop, which offers fresh pastries, desserts and aromatic coffee. There are several bars where guests can get drinks of local production (for everything else you need to pay). There is also a restaurant on a picturesque island - this is a great place for a romantic evening.

Are there any conditions for relaxing on the beach?





Naturally, tourists choose Egypt as their vacation destination, wanting to relax on the beach. What can the hotel offer Hurghada Marriott Red Sea Resort 5 *? As already mentioned, the hotel is located on the first coastline - the distance is only a few meters. The hotel owns its own 150 m long beach area. There is a convenient pontoon here, and the bottom off the coast is cleared for swimming. Nearby is a small, picturesque islet with a wooden bridge leading to it.

Guests can use sun loungers and parasols free of charge. Here you can get a clean beach towel. And in the local bar you can have a snack and cool off with a delicious drink. Naturally, for an additional fee you can go water-skiing or boating, take training courses with professional windsurfers and divers, enjoy the beauty of the coral reef, play beach volleyball and much more.

Additional services offered by the hotel





At the Hurghada Marriott Red Sea Resort you will find everything you need for a truly comfortable pastime. Guests are free to use the local ample parking. By the way, in the city you can easily and quickly rent a car or some other means of transport.

Laundry and dry cleaning services are offered - the prices here are quite affordable. Almost the entire territory has access to the Internet. And the Internet cafe is constantly working, where the connection is fast, there is computer equipment. Thanks to a well-equipped business center, the hotel often becomes the venue for business and holiday events. There is a small conference room. The local amphitheater, designed for 120 people, can also be used for meetings, seminars, holidays.

There is also a shopping center at the hotel where you can buy literally everything, from household trifles and beach slippers to clothes, jewelry and original souvenirs.

Entertainment: how do guests spend their time?





At the Hurghada Marriott Red Sea Resort you will not be bored. You can have a great time by the pool - here travelers swim, arrange fun competitions, relax on comfortable sunbeds, arranged around. At the pool, group classes in yoga and aerobics are also held. People who prefer to keep fit can visit the gym, where they will find all the necessary equipment.

You should definitely visit the wellness center. Here you will find a sauna, a jacuzzi with an excellent hydromassage system, an "ice" pool, massage rooms. There are good tennis courts, next to which you can rent rackets, balls and even take a few lessons and professional coaches. You can have fun playing squash, table tennis and billiards. And you will be entertained by a team of experienced animators.

Hotel Hurghada Marriott Red Sea Resort 5 *: reviews

Inquiring about the opinions of people who have already managed to visit the hotel, you can get a lot of interesting and relevant information. What do travelers think of the Hurghada Marriott Red Sea Resort? Reviews are mostly positive. The hotel is large, the rooms are spacious and comfortable, the furniture and appliances are in good condition - spending time here is always a pleasure. Special thanks to the tourists tell the chefs who really try every day to please the guests with new delicious masterpieces. Having your own clean beach, convenient access to the sea and a living coral reef are also an advantage. The hotel is conveniently located; you can go to the city or visit a night club at any time. This place will give you a lot of pleasant impressions and leave only positive memories.
