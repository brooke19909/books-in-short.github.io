Hotel Bravo Djerba 4 *: reviews, description of the hotel. Tours in Tunisia

Tunisia is one of the most popular resort countries. Every year, thousands of tourists from all over the world come here in search of clean beaches and a warm sea. Of course, resort towns are located along the country's coast. But one of the most beautiful places is the island of Djerba.

Of course, many hotels of different sizes were built on the island, offering good living conditions. One of the good enough places to spend a vacation is the hotel complex Hotel Bravo Djerba 4. The reviews about it are mostly positive - people really have a great time at this hotel.

Of course, travelers during the travel planning period are looking for all the possible information about the chosen stopping place. Where exactly is the hotel located and how far will you have to go to the beach? What conditions of accommodation and food can tourists expect? Can I bring a child with me? How do guests have fun? This information will be useful to many tourists planning to relax in Tunisia.

Where to look for a hotel? Location Description





Naturally, many travelers are primarily looking for information about the location of a hotel, because this is an important factor. So where to look for the hotel complex Hotel Bravo Djerba? Tunisia, the picturesque island of Djerba, namely, the region of the resort village of Midoun - it is here, on the very shore of the sea, that a luxury hotel is located.

It is worth saying that this is a calm area, which is currently at the stage of restoration and reconstruction. There are few tourists here, so this hotel is chosen as a refuge by families with children, older couples, newlyweds, in short, travelers who prefer peace and quiet.

By the way, the distance to the airport is only 25 km. Convenient transfers are usually organized by the agencies from which you buy tours. Hotel Bravo Djerba 4, by the way, can also organize a similar trip. Within walking distance are cafes, small shops and one large shopping center. In short, travelers do not feel divorced from the world.

What does the territory of the hotel complex look like?

What awaits people who have purchased a tour? The Bravo Djerba Hotel has recently been completely renovated both from the inside and the outside. The hotel area is quite large, and the courtyard smoothly passes into the beach. The hotel itself consists of a large, white, four-story building, built in a traditionally oriental style.

The interior decoration will also please with originality, as the hotel is decorated in a marine style. Reviews say that the hotel courtyard is also striking in cleanliness and accuracy. There is a pool and relaxation terraces. The territory is beautifully decorated with palm trees and bright exotic flowers, and very close by there is a small park for walking and relaxing in the shade of trees.

By the way, the hotel was built not so long ago - they have been hosting tourists since 2002. But not so long ago, a complete reconstruction was carried out, after which the hotel can satisfy the needs of even the most demanding traveler.

Hotel complex Hotel Bravo Djerba: description and photos of rooms





What living conditions should travelers expect when choosing Hotel Bravo Djerba 4 as a temporary haven ? Reviews say that the rooms here are clean, tidy and, most importantly, comfortable.

The hotel complex is considered relatively large - on its territory there are 310 rooms of different capacities. They are all decorated in the same marine style, equipped with quality furniture and modern appliances. There is access to a private balcony, and almost all rooms have views of the seashore.

Convenient air conditioning allows you to create pleasant temperature conditions in the room. You can shorten the time watching satellite channels. There is a telephone with the ability to dial directly. Guests can also use the small safe. There is a minibar with a cooling system, but it is filled with drinks only by order of the guests.

The room comes with a bathroom with shower (some rooms have a large bathtub). There is a set of hygiene items, including shower gels and shampoos (they are replenished from time to time). Also, guests are entitled to a set of clean towels, which are changed during cleaning or on request. You can count on a stationary hair dryer, which is also convenient.

What are the dining conditions for guests?





Tours to Tunisia, as a rule, include not only hotel accommodation, but also good food. Rest in this hotel is no exception - here guests can count on a full three meals a day. In addition, during the day in the hotel bars you can get chilled drinks, desserts and light snacks, which, of course, is very convenient.

The main meals are held in the spacious, elegantly decorated restaurant of Hotel Bravo Djerba 4. Reviews say that the guests are well fed. Breakfast, lunch and dinner are held in the form of a self-service buffet, although, of course, the restaurant has waiters who are always ready to help you.

What menu can I expect at Djerba's restaurant? Tunisia is a country famous for its hospitality and interesting cuisine. Guests can count on both familiar international dishes and delicious masterpieces prepared according to local recipes. Of course, the menu includes vegetable salads and fresh fruits, delicious desserts and homemade ice cream. The bar serves light snacks, coffee, aromatic tea and, of course, strong drinks (some of them are already included in the price).

In any case, do not worry about food for tourists who choose the island of Djerba as a stop. Hotels here are not the only places to eat. Near the hotel there are shops where you can buy groceries, as well as numerous cafes, restaurants and taverns where you can taste delicious food, completely immersed in the local atmosphere.

Beach holiday





Clean beaches and warm sea waves - this is exactly what tourists who decide to rest in Tunisia expect. The Bravo Djerba Hotel, as already mentioned, is located almost on the seashore - its territory faces directly to the beach. It is worth saying that the coast here is covered with soft sand. Moreover, the entrance to the sea is daily cleared of algae and stones, so it is comfortable to spend time here. There is also a long pontoon, on which tourists love to walk.

As for the children, they have a lot of fun here. The depth is shallow near the shore, and therefore the water is always warmed up - the kids can dabble in complete safety, under the supervision of parents and watchful members of the rescue team.

The beach, of course, is private - outsiders will not bother you, since only guests spend time here. Reviews indicate that the coast is always calm. There is a sufficient number of sun loungers with new comfortable mattresses, as well as large straw umbrellas, under which you can hide in the dining heat.

Water fun for travelers

Tours to Tunisia promise a bright, unforgettable pastime. Of course, travelers can relax on the beach, swimming in the gentle sea waves. But what do lovers of more active rest do?

Naturally, you are unlikely to be bored. An excellent volleyball court is equipped on the shore, where guests from time to time hold humorous competitions. Nearby on the shore for a small fee you can rent a pedalo, boat or canoe. You will have the opportunity to go water skiing, jet skiing, or even take a short boat trip. It is possible to join a boat trip on a yacht. Those interested can go sailing, scuba diving, windsurfing. A full range of entertainment for tourists is guaranteed.

Additional service for tourists





Can guests of Hotel Bravo count on additional conditions? Tunisia is a hospitable country that is famous for its luxurious hotels. Therefore, travelers can count on a service that will save you from small household problems and make your vacation as comfortable as possible.

Laundry and dry cleaning services are available on site, there is also ample parking (by the way, transport can be quickly rented in the city). Here you can exchange currency at a favorable rate. If necessary, you can get a safe deposit box from the administrator for free - here your things will be completely safe. There is also a luggage storage room, which is convenient for travelers who are used to frequently and actively moving around the country.

Almost throughout the territory there is access to the Internet (decent speed here). For guests, a good business center is equipped where you can use a computer, fax, printer and other equipment. There is also a large conference room where you can conduct seminars, business meetings, holidays. Of course, for a reasonable fee, hotel staff themselves organize events.

Tunisia, Djerba island, Hotel Bravo Djerba: entertainment and leisure for travelers





What can be done at the hotel? This question is interesting to many tourists. So what does the Hotel Bravo Djerba 4 offer? Reviews say that you can have a good time here. As already mentioned, in the courtyard there is a large outdoor pool with fresh water. By the way, water is cleaned here regularly. Every day, water aerobics classes are held in the pool - this is not only fun, but also a useful event.

Nearby is a large terrace with comfortable sun loungers and thatched umbrellas, where you can lie, take sun baths and chat with other guests. There is an indoor pool with a water heating system, where tourists relax in bad weather.

You should definitely visit the SPA center, which offers a wide range of wellness treatments. You can visit the hammam, sign up for several sessions of relaxing massage. It offers hydromassage, thalassotherapy. Nearby there is a fitness center with a good gym - this will please people who prefer to keep fit.

You can have a good time playing darts or table tennis. On the hotel territory there is also a tennis court, next to which you can rent rackets, balls and other equipment.

We should also mention the team of animators who daily entertain guests at the hotel complex. The reviews indicate that incredibly cheerful and energetic guys work here. For guests, various performances, shows, contests and competitions are held. You can do yoga, aerobics, modern and ballroom dancing, in a word, have a great time in the company of new friends.

And, of course, do not forget about excursion vacations. There are many interesting places that the island of Djerba can offer tourists. Hotel Bravo Djerba organizes a variety of excursions, the price of which is quite affordable. Here you will always be helped to choose the most interesting route.

Can I take children with me? Conditions for relaxing with a child

Many tourists choose the Hotel Bravo Djerba hotel, its rating is quite high according to polls on different sites. But if you travel with a child, comfort issues come to the fore. So is the hotel suitable for families with children?

In fact, this place is focused specifically on a quiet pastime. The presence of shallows near the shore allows children to have fun while swimming in the sea. As for amenities, guests can count on an extra bed, which will immediately be delivered to your room. Naturally, in a restaurant, the parents of a small child can get a highchair. There is no special children's buffet here, but the menu is quite varied - you can always find something healthy and tasty. By the way, the guys just love homemade ice cream.

Do young tourists have fun at the Hotel Bravo Djerba? Reviews say that children really have something to do. The guys have fun splashing in a special shallow pool with warm water. A mini club is open daily, where experienced educators and animators work with children. By the way, parents confirm that animators do their job well, find an approach to a child of any age, organize fun classes, dance classes, theater performances and other exciting events.
