Pension Zvezdochka in Anapa: overview, features and reviews of tourists

A comfortable and fun holiday is not only a vacation abroad. Excellent conditions were created in our country in the Zvyozdochka boarding house in Anapa. Zvezdochka is a wellness center where visitors get a high level of service at an affordable cost.

Where is it located and how does it work

Pension "Zvezdochka" in Anapa is located 10 minutes from the center, on Pionersky Avenue 9. It can be reached by taxi or public transport from the train station and airport. The journey takes about 20 minutes.





You can book a room by phone listed on the official website or at travel agencies that work with the company. The health complex operates year-round.

Accommodations

On the territory there are 5 small buildings for guests and a hotel. They are equipped with rooms with different levels of comfort. In 3-5 buildings and the hotel rooms are economy class. They can accommodate from 1 to 5 people. The rooms have:

  • single beds;
  • bedside tables;
  • wardrobe;
  • shared shower, toilet and fridge on the floor.

Bed linen and towels are changed every 5 days.





In 1 and 2 buildings there are superior rooms. They are equipped with:

  • double beds and soft headsets;
  • split systems;
  • bath and toilet in the room;
  • wardrobes;
  • TV

The two-room rooms have a small kitchen with household appliances.

Service in the boarding house for mothers and children "Zvezdochka" in Anapa

The health complex has a fairly large staff. Governesses timely change of linen and cleaned efficiently in the rooms. With any household matter, you can contact the administrator, who is trying to quickly solve the problem.

The complex has a spacious parking lot for customers who have arrived by car.

Nutrition is compiled by an experienced nutritionist at the Zvyozdochka boarding house in Anapa. Zvezdochka has its own dining room, where vacationers can fully eat throughout the rest.

Breakfast consists of:

  • snacks
  • first course (porridge in assortment);
  • second course (side dish with meat or fish);
  • tea or coffee.

For lunch are served:

  • snack;
  • assorted soups;
  • meat or fish with a side dish;
  • drink;
  • fruit;
  • bakery products.

If the client is not satisfied with the menu for the day, he can use replacement dishes.

For dinner, guests are also provided with a multi-course menu with the mandatory use of meat or fish. Sour-milk products are served as a dessert.

Entertainment in the complex

On the territory of the boarding house "Zvezdochka" in Anapa (South) there is an excursion club. With it, you can make small trips around the Krasnodar Territory and get acquainted with its culture and traditions. There are a lot of places in this region, visiting of which will surely leave pleasant memories for many years:

  • Anapa;
  • Peninsula Abrau;
  • waterfalls (Pshada);
  • Gelendzhik;
  • Lotus valley;
  • Novorossiysk;
  • Holy sources;
  • Taman.

All these attractions will be available to every vacationer of the complex, who will visit excursions organized by the club at the boarding house.

Guests can also go to the dolphinarium, to the jousting tournament or relax on horseback riding.

The wellness center supports an active lifestyle. It has a modern gym and morning exercises in the fresh air. In the boarding house there is an instructor who will teach Nordic walking. There is the necessary equipment.

Bike rental is available on site. Everyone can take a walk on this transport through the beautiful places of the circle.





The Zvezdochot club is constantly working for children at the Zvyozdochka boarding house in Anapa. Zvezdochka is interested in productive vacations of little vacationers. Various competitive programs and educational events are held here daily:

  • master classes related to creative activities;
  • talent contests;
  • educational games;
  • mobile and sporting events;
  • discos;
  • cartoon broadcasts.

Animators deal with children for several hours 3 times a day (11.30, 16.00, 20.00). The territory is equipped with a bright and modern, safe playground for children of different ages.

Beach & Wellness

Pension has its own furnished beach. It has dressing cabins, showers and shade canopies. The beach mode is set here. vacationers can visit it daily from 8.00 to 12.00 and in the evening from 16.00 to 22.00.

There is a medical center and a rescue service on the beach. This recreation area has its own characteristics. In this region, the coastline is covered with seaweed, which in its composition have a large amount of iodine.





Therefore, staying on the beach during these hours will help get rid of many chronic diseases and strengthen immunity, both for children and adults.

In a boarding house, vacationers can not only relax, but also undergo treatment. It uses general strengthening procedures for the treatment and prevention of various diseases:

  • mini-sauna "Siberian health resort" (application of medicinal properties of ancient cedars);
  • turmanium mats;
  • massage bed;
  • salt room;
  • inhalation;
  • massotherapy;
  • aromatherapy;
  • ultraviolet irradiator (irradiation of the upper respiratory tract and ear);
  • electric sleep.

Modern methods of prevention and treatment of various diseases are also successfully applied:

  • psammotherapy - the use of heated sand for taking general and local baths;
  • ampelotherapy - grape therapy;
  • enotherapy - application for the improvement of wine;
  • terrenkur - dosed climbing along marked routes.

All these methods are successfully used in the health of vacationers at the Zvyozdochka boarding house in Anapa. Prices for these procedures are available to almost everyone. They are not included in the price and must be paid separately.

Pension "Zvezdochka" in Anapa: reviews

On various sites you can find conflicting opinions about the work of the complex. Many vacationers liked it here and they seek to return here again. According to reviews in buildings with economy rooms, the interior is quite simple, but the rooms are cozy and clean.

The territory is always well-groomed and landscaped in a boarding house "Zvezdochka" in Anapa. The asterisk has a good dining room with a balanced diet. Some guests are unhappy that they removed the buffet and you have to use a specific menu for the day.





Parents are satisfied that the pension is under the supervision of a guard. Therefore, children from 6 years old can safely move on it independently and they will not be able to leave the territory without adults.
