How to make a cardboard crown? Needlework lessons for dads and mothers

Children's costumed matinees are always fun and beautiful. Of course, in order for the child to be happy contemplating his carnival image, mom and dad have to work hard to make the outfit and attributes for it. In the article, we will talk about how to make a cardboard crown. Sooner or later this information will be needed by each parent. Almost no matinee is complete without a prince and princess. And if your child fell out to play one of these roles, then our tips will become just a "find". Here you can read a description of two ways of making such an element of a carnival costume as a cardboard crown. Photos of similar products and patterns are also presented to your attention.



We master the main attribute of the royal costume. Preparation of materials

For work you will need:

  • cardboard;
  • wrapping or corrugated paper;
  • scissors;
  • ruler;
  • pencil;
  • PVA glue or clerical;
  • stapler;
  • decorative elements (sequins, lace, beads).

How to make a cardboard crown for little princesses and princes?

Paste a sheet of A4 paper on both sides with wrapping (corrugated) paper. On a separate notebook sheet, complete the product template. The photo shows the options for possible patterns. The length of the template should be 2-3 centimeters longer than the circumference of the child‚Äôs head. Transfer the pattern to a cardboard blank and cut it out. Fasten the edges with a stapler. Glue the bottom of the product with lace, decorate the teeth with beads and sequins. Let the craft dry for several hours. Puncture with an awl or a thick needle in the crown two holes at the same distance from each other and stretch a thin rubber band. When putting on the accessory, wind it by the chin, thus securing the product to the baby's head.



We make a kokoshnik with our own hands. What is needed for this?

This option of a headdress is more suitable for the image of the Snow Maiden or the Young Lady. It is performed in the form of a kokoshnik. We prepare the following materials for work:

  • wide hair hoop ;
  • color cardboard;
  • Double-sided tape;
  • foil;
  • satin ribbon - 2 meters;
  • thermal gun;
  • landscape sheet of paper;
  • pencil;
  • decorative elements.

How to make a cardboard crown in the form of a kokoshnik: stages of manufacturing a product

Fold the paper in half and sketch on it a half of the accessory. Without unrolling the sheet, cut out the template. As a result, you get a crown pattern with symmetrical sides. Attach a hoop to the template and circle it in the right place. But do not rush to cut along this line. Draw ‚Äúwings‚Äù to it, with the help of which the kokoshnik will be attached to the hoop. Transfer the pattern to cardboard and cut it out.

Apply hot-melt glue to the ‚Äúwings‚Äù and attach the blank to the hoop. Make several cuts on top of the product, which will allow it to take a convex shape. How to make a cardboard crown beautiful and elegant? Let's execute its decoration. Attach two bands to the ends of the hoop. With the help of them, the kokoshnik will be mounted on the head. Decorate the front side of the product with sequins, rhinestones, small glass beads.
