How to make an owl from disks

Sometimes a child wants to make an original craft with his own hand. Invite him to create something from the discs. Shiny round circles, which are almost never used today, can be left to the craft with a clear conscience. In this article we will tell you how to make an owl from disks.

Brilliant bird



Owl from the disc is very simple. You need to find unnecessary shiny circles and glue them together. We will decorate the owl with felt. From the yellow material we cut out two large circles, and from the red - a little smaller. Glue the felt on one side of the disk. Craft can be done and bilateral. Then you need to make duplicates of all the details of the face. On the red circles we glue small pupils that need to be cut out of black fabric. From green felt you need to make a triangle. The beak should be glued just below the eyes. It remains to make ears and paws. We cut them out of pink felt and fix between two disks. To make the bird more shiny, you can stick a trapezoid from any material on its stomach and decorate it with rhinestones.

Surprised owl



To make this craft you will need 8 disks. One of them needs to be cut in half. All shiny circles need to be made at the edges of the fringe. When the blanks are ready, proceed to the manufacture of an owl. From the disks we collect the body. We glue two of them together and glue another half between them. Disks in the next row will be denser to each other. The third row will again consist of two circles and one semicircle. We fasten everything together with glue or liquid silicone. It remains to supplement the owl with details. From the eighth disk, we cut out oval-shaped wings and eyebrows that resemble semicircles. We attach all the spare parts to the finished body. From colored paper, cut out the eyes.

Owl mask

This craft can be used as a room decor, or can be worn on a masquerade. The head of an owl from disks is made as follows. Cut out a circle with two triangles on top of the cardboard. It will be the head. In the middle we glue two discs. From the brown cardboard we cut out the trapezoid, which we fasten between the eyes so that its wide end ends somewhere between the ears. We make a beak from black paper. It remains to cut holes in the center of the discs so that you can look through them.

Big owl



To make such a craft, you have to work hard. Do-it-yourself Owl from disks is going to be quite simple, but the work is quite painstaking.

So, cut the disk in half. Glue a half to the middle of the whole circle. Now take another disk and divide it into 4 parts. ¬º glue to the workpiece, slightly higher than the already glued half. Two more quarters will become paws. Glue them to the bottom of our blank. Divide the new disk into two parts. It will be wings. Now we make a head from the whole disk. You need to glue two small disks to it. We collect the blanks into a single whole. It remains to stick the details. We supplement the eyes with pupil buttons, cut out beak, ears and eyebrows from the disc scraps.

Owl from "Smesharikov"



Children are always interested in making a figurine of their favorite character from a fairy tale or cartoon.

We offer you to learn how to make an owl from disks from Smeshariki. It is quite simple. To do this, you need two shiny circles and colored paper. We glue the wheels together. Two white circles and an orange oval are cut out of paper. Glue the eyes and beak. Now we make the pupils. These will be two small circles of black or dark blue paper. The ears will be in the shape of triangles. From purple paper, we cut out these two parts, and at the same time two semicircles that will be centuries old. We glue everything.

From the rectangles we collect the paws, we attach the brushes to the ears. Cut the wings out of the printed paper. It remains to make a hat. It can be made from yellow and orange rectangles. The cap will be crowned balabon. It is made of a circle that is notched around the entire perimeter.

Handmade bird



Such a craft can be made from the disk and all that is at hand. It can be felt, pieces of leather or colored paper.

Consider how to make an owl out of the discs with your own hands step by step. First we glue two discs. Now we cut out circles from fabric (paper, leather or felt) - eyes, drops - wings, triangles - ears, rhombus - beak and crowns - paws. All this is glued to the disk. For liveliness, we supplement the eyes with a few more circles - yellow, white and black. Now we arm ourselves with a marker and paint our craft. We put feathers on the disk, decorate the paws with dashes, and wings with waves.

Winged Owl



We glue two disks together. We take two small lids from the jar and fasten them to the base with screws. From an unnecessary spoon we make a beak. We cut the handle from the cutlery, and make a cut in the head. We attach the beak to the base also with self-tapping screws. From the disks we cut long strips. These will be the feathers of the wings. Glue them to the back of the craft with 5 strips on each side. Three small triangles indicate paws.
