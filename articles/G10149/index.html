Hotel "Zhoekvara" (Abkhazia, Gagra): overview, description and reviews of tourists

Several decades ago, every Soviet citizen dreamed of relaxing in the famous resorts of sunny Abkhazia. Trips to pensions and resorts Gagra, Pitsunda, Sukhumi were very popular. Currently, this exotic corner with picturesque nature and extraordinary vegetation is slowly being revived, and the resorts of the small but proud Abkhazia regain their former glory.

Holidays in Gagra

The most famous and most beloved by the Russians resort of colorful Abkhazia is the city of Gagra. Its infrastructure is most developed in comparison with other cities of this colorful country. Since Soviet times there have been many sanatoriums and boarding houses that still operate here.





The beauty of Gagra is indescribable. The subtropical climate is perfect for exotic vegetation, which is abundant here. Palm trees, cypress trees, evergreen laurels, oranges, tangerines, almonds, eucalyptus trees. The variety of flora of Abkhazia is amazing.

And what is the beauty of natural landscapes! Not far from Gagra are some of the most picturesque places on the planet - Lake Ritsa, the majestic Gegsky waterfall and mountains.

In the post-Soviet era, due to the political situation in the country, Abkhazia as a resort was somewhat forgotten. The infrastructure did not develop, the situation became dull. However, every year the Gagra resort is gaining momentum. In this amazing place new modern hotels, hotels, holiday homes of international level are being built.

Hotel Joecquara: Overview

One of the newest holiday destinations in Gagra is the hotel "Zhoekvara" (Abkhazia). This modern resort building was built and commissioned only this season.





This hotel is located in the center of the sunny city of Gagra, near the Black Sea. Hotel "Zhoekvara" (Abkhazia) is immersed in the greenery of the surrounding park. The entire territory of the hotel is fenced and planted with exotic trees and shrubs. Gorgeous palm trees grow everywhere.

Hotel "Zhoekvara" (Abkhazia) has a class of 4 stars, that is, it is a high-level hotel with a fairly extensive infrastructure. How much this hotel meets the stated standard can be judged by the reviews of vacationers, but more on that later.

How to get there

Tourists from Russia to get to the hotel "Joekvara" is not difficult. The distance from Adler to the 4 * Zhoekvara hotel (Gagra, Abkhazia) is only thirty-five kilometers.

You can get to Adler by any means of transport - by plane, train, bus. From there to the border with Abkhazia - about fifteen kilometers. Often, tourists get to the Russian border with Abkhazia by taxi or bus, cross the border on the bridge over the Psou river on their own. Crossing the border zone may take some time, which depends on the queue. Customs inspection, verification of documents - and you are in Abkhazia. Next, you should use the services of a minibus. From the border you can get to Gagra in fifteen minutes. Only twenty kilometers - and you are there.

From Adler to Abkhazia, you can also take a scheduled bus, which is not always convenient.

Two currencies are relevant in Abkhazia, while the Russian ruble remains the most popular.

The address of the Hotel Zhoekvara is Abkhazia, the city of Gagra, 1 Apsha Leon Street (Old Gagra, Zhoekvara district).

Accommodation for vacationers

The hotel is a modern four-story building, which houses the entire number of rooms.

The rooms of the Zhoekvara Hotel (Abkhazia) are equipped with all the necessary benefits of civilization. Each room has comfortable modern furniture, fresh repairs, necessary household appliances, a bathroom, all toiletries - shampoos, gels, a hairdryer, a set of towels. All rooms have a safe. The beds in some rooms are double, in others - single. Guests will be able to choose the best option.





The category of rooms at the Zhoekvara Hotel (Abkhazia) ranges from standard to luxury. Standard rooms are mostly one-room, designed for two or three people with the possibility of installing additional space in the form of a folding bed.

Suites have a large area, two rooms. Furniture and repairs in the rooms are more expensive and refined.

Also available are four-bed family two- and three-room apartments.

Half of the rooms have balconies with sea views.

Distance to the sea, beach

"Zhoekvara" - 4 * hotel (Gagra, Abkhazia), located on the first coastline, twenty meters from the beach. The road to the sea will take only a couple of minutes.





The beach is wide, as in the whole of Gagra - pebble. The climate in Gagra is one of the warmest on the entire Black Sea coast. The purest water warms up quickly and by the end of May, tourists will find azure Abkhazia (Gagra).
