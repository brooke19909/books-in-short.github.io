Nebug - the "Mediterranean" of the Krasnodar Territory

Nebug is a wonderful place with beautiful nature, away from the noise of big cities, calm and safe. This small village is located 15 km northwest of Tuapse. Nebug was built at the mouth of a small river of the same name, dividing the settlement into two parts. The half-kilometer coastal strip of the village includes wide convenient beaches and recreation areas: attractions, cafes, shops and local hotels.

‚ÄúThe place where the Russian subtropics begin‚Äù is the name of the village of Nebug. The Krasnodar Territory has always been famous for its unique climatic conditions: the location of the Nebug between the sea and high mountains, together with the unique landscape of the area, creates a gentle subtropical microclimate that has a beneficial effect on human health. Warm sea, picturesque mountains and centuries-old forests, clean beaches with fine pebbles and sand - the holiday season in this amazing place lasts from May to October. But not only in the summer the resort of Nebug is open for tourists on the Black Sea coast (Krasnodar Territory). Rest here is also possible in winter - in Nebug several modern heated swimming pools with sea water function.





Hotel "Tortuga", Nebug

The Tortuga hotel complex is built in the coastal area, within walking distance of the sea. The modern three-story hotel building is located on a well-groomed landscaped area.

"Tortuga" is ideal for a romantic vacation, and for active family vacations. The cleanliness and comfort of the rooms, polite friendly staff, a large number of flowering plants - all this immediately makes a good impression. For a comfortable sea holiday in the surrounding area there is a well-equipped beach.

Particular attention is paid to the safety of guests arriving at the Tortuga hotel complex (Nebug): the guarded territory of the hotel is equipped with a reliable high fence. At the request of guests, the administration can arrange a meeting at the bus station. For motorists living in a hotel complex, the Tortuga Hotel (Nebug) provides free 24-hour parking.

The convenient location of the hotel and the beauty of the surrounding landscapes, the views of which are opened from the windows of any hotel room, will not leave anyone indifferent. The unusual architecture of the building of the Tortuga Hotel and its territory, decorated in the spirit of an old pirate fort, will create a mood and give your vacation an unforgettable atmosphere of adventure and long journeys.





Posting Rules

1. For an adult guest, in order to settle in a hotel, it is necessary to present the following documents: passport, ticket.

2. For parents arriving at the hotel with children, there are a number of living conditions:

  • The hotel room can accommodate guests with children of any age. Children 0-3 years old can stay for free, without an extra bed.
  • When checking in a child under the age of 14, a birth certificate and a medical policy are presented.
  • A child over 3 years old is provided with an extra bed (folding chair) in the room. Extra bed cost: 500 rubles per day.

3. Accommodation of any pets in the hotel is strictly prohibited.

4. Checkout time - daily, at 12-00.

Entertainment, leisure

The rest in the Tortuga Hotel (Nebug) will be remembered by the guests not only for the interior of the rooms or the exquisite cuisine of the hotel restaurant. Vacationers do not have to get bored in Tortug - hotel animators constantly arrange holidays for children and adults. The hotel has a modern playground, a barbecue for a picnic, in the evenings there is a summer dance floor.

But in the resort village for tourists there is a lot of interesting things - the village of Nebug (Tuapse district) can be called a real center of entertainment. Here is the "Dolphin" - one of the largest water park in the Krasnodar Territory. Nebug, in addition, is known as the first city in the Krasnodar Territory in which a water park was built. There is also a dolphinarium in Nebug, Luna - park, Ice Palace and a huge number of water attractions for any age category. The dolphinarium and the famous water park (Nebug) are only 100 meters from the Tortuga hotel complex.

In addition, vacationers can feel like real seekers of underwater treasures: a diving club operates on the coast, offering dives of any difficulty level, under the guidance of experienced instructors. Fans of outdoor activities will surely appreciate such an extreme attraction as riding on a bed of a dried river on ATVs.

Since 2002, in the village of Nebug (Krasnodar Territory) there has been a holiday for music lovers - the summer annual festival "Aquajaz". The time of this event is usually the end of summer or the beginning of September, and the Dolphin water park serves as a platform for musicians.





Excursions, sights

For the convenience of vacationers, walks and excursions are organized at the hotel for an additional fee. Unfortunately, the village of Nebug, Tuapse district and the nearest resort villages cannot boast of a large number of interesting cultural sites. Excursion routes are very popular, including one of the main attractions of the Tuapse district - a group of 13 dolmens, ancient religious buildings made of stone slabs. These ancient monuments are located just 13 km from the village of Nebug. Nearby, in the Hoseps Gorge, is a picturesque mountain waterfall, whose height is about eight meters. In addition, tourists can take a fascinating voyage to the Kiselev rock - the place where the film ‚ÄúThe Diamond Arm‚Äù was filmed.

How can I get to?

It is easiest to get to the village of Nebug (Tuapse district of Krasnodar Territory) along the Dzhubga-Sochi highway, by personal car or tourist transport. There is also a railway line running along the resort village - the railway station is located in the district center of Tuapse. Small difficulties may arise if you plan to fly on vacation by plane: there are no direct flights to Nebug. The two nearest airports are in the cities of Krasnodar and Gelendzhik. Regular buses and fixed-route taxis regularly run from these cities to Nebug.
