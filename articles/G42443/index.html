Simferopol, hotel "Ukraine": rooms, photos, prices and how to get

Simferopol is the capital of the Republic of Crimea, the largest (after Sevastopol) city on the peninsula. It is located on the border of the foothills and the steppe and has no access to the sea. Simferopol Airport is the main air gate of the peninsula. Airplanes come here from almost all major cities of the Russian Federation and other CIS countries. From here, tourists travel by car to resorts located on the Crimean coast. In a word, Simferopol is the main transport hub on the peninsula. The infrastructure is well developed here. It has many cinemas, shopping malls, restaurants and hotels of various levels, for example, the Hotel Rezhans, the Mini-Hotel Old Quay, the Hotel Ukraine.
