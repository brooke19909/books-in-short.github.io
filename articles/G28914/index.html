Factors important to determining share value

Before contacting the agency, try to evaluate your own share in the apartment. Despite the complexity of the process, an approximate cost can still be determined.

Assessment of the share in the apartment depends a lot on what it is:

  • with an uncertain order of use;

Access to the apartment

If there are no problems with neighbors, they do not object to the change of one of the tenants, then this is already + 30% of the cost when evaluating the share in the apartment for sale. That is, the buyer does not need to worry about free access to new housing, nor does he need to contact the police or the court. Yes, and to go on the acquisition of a share in the housing, where the aggressive-minded tenants, except perhaps a desperate person or pursuing other selfish goals.

Number of shares and rooms

To increase the cost of housing allows a situation in which the number of shares is equal to the number of rooms. This fact adds to the cost of 50%.

Large total area

No one will dispute the fact that an apartment of 80 square meters is much easier to exchange for two with a smaller area. This principle also works in the case of shared ownership. If 2 co-owners decide to leave, then it‚Äôs easier for them to exchange an apartment of 100 sq.m for two, even in a remote area of ‚Äã‚Äãthe city, but at no extra cost. Empty apartment

When assessing a share in an apartment, it is of great importance whether someone lives in it. If not, then the prospect of living in empty housing even for a short time is a huge plus.

Juvenile Solicitors

The presence of interest holders under the age of majority reduces the cost by about 50%. Such residents spoil the prospect of traveling. Most likely, new residents who move in without children will have to squeeze. The same reason for reducing the cost of housing, if the apartment has incompetent people.

Queues

In some ads, realtors give the advantage of the fact that some co-owners are in the queue for housing. However, such co-owners will never agree to leave, even if the line for new housing comes only after death. So it takes about 30% of the cost.

In addition to these factors, the condition of the apartment, communications and location are of great importance. And the biggest minus, if the apartment is very expensive is in shared ownership, it is almost impossible to sell such housing.





Assessment for Trial

Very often, the division of housing between co-owners or heirs occurs through the courts. In this case, an assessment of the share in the apartment for the court will be required. An independent opinion in this case will be the main document by which the judge will be guided in the distribution of shares. In light of this, if one of the parties to the process assumes the obligation to evaluate, the selection of an appraiser should be approached seriously and not save on its services. And if you still attract two appraisers, then achieving better results is much easier.





Hereditary mass

And if there are many heirs, it is better to immediately determine the size of your share. It is in these cases that it is advisable to preliminarily evaluate the share in the apartment for inheritance. It should also be remembered that the acceptance of an inheritance is not only the receipt of material wealth, but also the acceptance of obligations. If the house has an encumbrance in connection with the purchase of other property, then obligations under the loan agreement will also have to be inherited.

In cases where the heirs manage to agree, the section will require an assessment of the share in the apartment for a notary. Then the report will serve as the basis for the distribution of shares with full satisfaction of the interests of all the heirs.

In addition, it should be remembered that tax liabilities arise after a transaction is completed or an inheritance is received. The tax will be paid precisely from the cost indicated in the assessment, so if possible it is better to reduce it, although there are risks.
