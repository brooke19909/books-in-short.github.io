Sani Resort, Halkidiki: description, reviews

Halkidiki is the peninsula that is most popular with tourists who prefer tours to mainland Greece. Why? There are several explanations for this phenomenon. Firstly, even in summer heat, Halkidiki is buried in lush greenery, which can not be said about the islands of Greece scorched by the sun and dried by the salty wind. Secondly, the proximity to the ancient heritage of ancient Greece. Relaxing here, you can join the sacrum, making a pilgrimage to Mount Athos, or just sailing on a ship around the peninsula-monastery, closed to women. And finally, the offer of housing. In Halkidiki you can find both expensive five-star hotels and budget hotels. In this article we will consider the resort complex Sani Resort (Halkidiki). Where is it located, what are its rooms and services, is it possible to reserve a place there yourself, or is the hotel available only for package tourists - we will describe all this below. An informative basis for writing this article to a large extent served as traveler reviews.



Halkidiki: what rest is there?

Before we begin the description of the hotel complex Sani Resort (Halkidiki), let's describe the place in which it is located. The peninsula from a height resembles the trident of Neptune. Three long capes stretched into the sea: Agion Oros, Sithonia and Kassandra. For tourists, only the last two peninsulas are available. Agion Oros, otherwise St. Athos, is a monastic republic. Cassandra and Sithonia are different. The last peninsula is quieter, respectable. It is suitable for families with small children, lovers who come to Greece for a honeymoon, wealthy pensioners from Western Europe. The average check at Sithonia is two times higher than at Kassandra. It should be said that the resort complex is located just on this last peninsula. Address Sani Resort (Halkidiki) - Kassandra. It is always fun here, as young people like to relax in these places. But violent (and sometimes loud) fun is concentrated on the beaches of the towns, because there is cheap housing.

How to get to the peninsula

If you purchased a ticket to one of the hotels Sani Resort (Halkidiki, Greece), then you have nothing to worry about. An airport transfer is included in the package. Halkidiki is a large island, but it does not have its own airport. The nearest touchdown point is Thessaloniki. Direct flights of Aegean Airlines, Aeroflot, Ellineyr and Es Seven fly to Moscow from the airport of this city. The first offer is more attractive in terms of price and travel time (150 euros round trip, three hours in the air). Residents of St. Petersburg will have to pay twice as much for the flight. The most budget option is offered by Turkish Airlines - 270 euros, and the fastest (six hours) - Lufthansa (five hundred euros).



How to get to the hotel complex

An independent traveler should be patient before he reaches the Sani Resort. Halkidiki is a very large peninsula. Its three prongs extended into the Aegean Sea for one hundred and fifty kilometers. First you need to get from the airport to the bus station of Thessaloniki. By taxi, such a trip will cost 25 euros. The cheapest option to get to Halkidiki and Kassandra in particular is KTEL buses. They specialize in delivering from the capital of the region passengers settlements of the peninsula (and there are a total of thirty-two of them). The road will take about an hour and a half and will cost ten euros. By taxi it will be faster, but the cost of the trip with a breeze will increase to one hundred euros. The hotel complex occupies Cape Sani - hence its name. The territory has its own helipad, so customers can be delivered directly from Thessaloniki Airport in ten minutes. The bus arrives in the nearest village, from which the hotel has a free shuttle service for guests.

Discounts, promotions and offers

This hotel complex has its own loyalty program. Special offers Sani Resort (Halkidiki) relate primarily to those who live in resort hotels for a long time or often. On their first visit to the complex, all guests are given a Blue Card. If you come to the hotel for the second time, they will exchange it for Gold. This is a higher membership card. Well, when you are a regular guest of the hotel - come there for the tenth time or spend at least 100 nights in the same season - you will receive a special prize. This is a platinum card. They give the right to various discounts on accommodation, as well as on food and drinks in the bars and restaurants of the complex. For example, the holders of the Golden Card on the day of arrival bring a basket of fruits, a bottle of Greek wine and water. They also receive a ten percent discount not only in bars and restaurants, but also in stores in the resort. Platinum card holders are entitled to upgrade the room category, free tickets to the Sani Festival, dinner in a restaurant with a bottle of exclusive wine, champagne on the day of arrival. They have a 15 percent discount on bars and boutiques. Promotions vary by season. Every year new discounts are introduced, which you will learn about by presenting membership cards at the reception. It is convenient to travel both on a tour package and on an individual reservation of accommodation in the Sani Resort complex (Halkidiki).
