Ajman hotels will help to relax from the bustle of the city

All travelers who want to escape from the bustle of the city and spend their weekend on the Persian Gulf, surrounded by beautiful landscapes, basking in the sun and enjoying the leisurely flow of life, should stay at Ajman hotels and spend a wonderful vacation in this emirate.

Family holidays in Ajman

In this city you can relax with the whole family. Adults will be interested in wandering the streets, especially because of the small size of the city, visiting a museum, getting to know the culture of this country.

Where to go with children

Despite the fact that Ajman is much smaller than Dubai and Abu Dhabi, there are several places where you can entertain young children. This is the case with the "Magic Planet" in the center. This is the place where young children can play in the playground. Quite conveniently, next to this park you will find a food court to order food while the kids enjoy the rides.

Another great place to take your children if you find yourself in Ajman is the Fun City Safeer Mall with an area full of fun video games, with a playground with balls, slides and attractions. This mall is great for kids and teens.

Ajman Beach

Do not miss the chance to sunbathe. The beach in Ajman is truly beautiful. The sand is white, the waters are clear. You will not see too many tourists here and can alone admire the beauty of this place. The beach is secluded and not crowded, unlike the artificial beaches of Dubai or the sometimes overly expensive beaches of Abu Dhabi (most of which can only be accessed through the hotel).

Where to stay

Ajman hotels will please any traveler. Room prices range from $ 99 per day, depending on the rating of the hotel, proximity to the water and the services provided.

The most famous place in the UAE is the Ajman Hotel. It boasts its own private beautiful beach and an ideal location close to many emirate attractions. You will relax in your own world with wonderful amenities and your stay will be unforgettable.

This Ajman hotel in the Emirates is a half hour drive from Dubai and 20 minutes from the airport. The complex has one of the most beautiful beaches, a swimming pool. Vacationers are offered many water sports such as water skiing, surfing and sailing. This resort will satisfy even the most demanding travelers.

Ajman Hotel has 168 rooms and 14 apartments decorated in Arabic style. The final mark of distinction and sophistication is the royal suite, which is located on the 8th floor. Its area is 250 m 2 . The suite has five separate balconies from which you can admire the Persian Gulf, as well as a separate living and dining area for six guests. The suite also includes a kitchenette and butler. Double room will cost 1465 AED (25050 rubles) per night. Breakfast included.

Ajman Beach Hotel

Imagine a sandy paradise, charming and picturesque, from where you can admire the superb Persian Gulf. The hotel is famous for its helpful staff, good service, hospitality, excellent cuisine and breathtaking ocean views in the emirate.

Room features

All 72 rooms are equipped with Wi-Fi, cable TV. Additional amenities include bedding, safes (accommodate laptops), ironing boards, and hair dryers.

Reception is at your service 24 hours a day. On the territory there are shops, a restaurant, a bar, you can buy souvenirs, a fresh water pool. Free access to shopping centers is provided. Barbecue tools can be rented for an extra charge. There are sun loungers and parasols by the pool. Clean beach towels are provided every day. There is a Russian-speaking staff.

Tourists who are looking for a budget vacation can consider staying here. This Ajman hotel not only offers quality accommodation at a reasonable price, but also an ideal location near the beach.

For shopping and lunch, guests can reach the nearby city center of Ajman or Sharjah, go to Dubai, where they will find many attractions such as the Deira Clock Tower, the Emirates Mall, Jumeirah Beach and Dubai World Trade Center. The hotel has sports tables and playgrounds, as well as an outdoor pool.
