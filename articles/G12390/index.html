Fusion TV: manufacturer, models, specifications

Fusion televisions appeared on the Russian market relatively recently and have already managed to conquer consumers with decent quality and affordable price. The Fusion TV is an excellent choice for those who wish to purchase an inexpensive TV for high-quality everyday TV viewing and recording from external media. An intuitive menu, stylish modern design and decent image quality are the main advantages of the products of the New Zealand brand, which has conquered many large markets in developed countries.

The owner of such a TV for a moderate fee receives a modern device that is equipped with the necessary interface for connecting removable external media and the ability to play media files from them.

Manufacturer

The main products of the Fusion brand are:

  • Electronics for home and car;
  • small household appliances.

Combining the production of entertainment and security electronic systems for cars under a single brand, Fusion has existed since 1998. Surprisingly, the Fusion brand, having been born in New Zealand, has long won the hearts of buyers in the UK, USA, Russia, Canada, Australia and Germany.

The Fusion brand appeared on the Russian market in 2002. Thanks to the memorable marketing concept, high quality and wide assortment, the products of this brand occupy a fair share of the Russian car audio market. Today, after the machines, Fusion comes to our homes.

Consider some models of Fusion TVs - modern, reliable and practical.
