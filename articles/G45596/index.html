How to overcome the Gelendzhik-Sochi route: the best options

Many people, in order to come on vacation or just on business, overcome the Gelendzhik-Sochi route. How to get there This is the first question that arises in connection with this. It should be noted that there are a lot of options.





By sea

And one of those is the passenger catamaran, whose name is Sochi-1. Flights run every other day - on odd numbers, the cost of a ticket for this type of transport is as follows: for adults - 1200 rubles, for children under 12 years old - 600 rubles, and for very young passengers (up to three years) - for free. In general, it takes four and a half hours to get across the sea from Gelendzhik to Sochi. On the way, the catamaran passes Tuapse (arrival two hours after departure) and Lazarevskoye - three hours later. By the way, if a person immediately takes a round-trip ticket, then he is given a discount. And, I must say, this is one of the most picturesque ways to overcome the path from Sochi to Gelendzhik or vice versa.

Self planning





If a person decided to cover the distance along the Gelendzhik-Sochi route, then there are a lot of options for this. Anyone wants to get to the necessary city as quickly as possible. And one of the best ways to achieve this goal is to carefully think through the shortest Gelendzhik-Sochi route. The first thing you need to know about the distance that separates the departure point and the end point of the route. This can be seen on the map. Then, knowing the average speed of the vehicle, it is easy to calculate the time that will need to be spent on the road. So, the distance "Gelendzhik-Sochi" is 246 kilometers. You can overcome it in about four hours. And now about working with the map. The route search system itself will find the most optimal option and offer it to a person. Then he will see a diagram on which the settlements encountered along the way are clearly marked. Information will also be provided regarding traffic police posts located along this route - this way it will be possible to better navigate the terrain that the driver is unfamiliar with. Driving along a route well thought out in advance is the best way to minimize the possibility of problems that often arise in unfamiliar areas.

Intercity Bus Flights





If there is no opportunity to drive your own car, then for such cases there is another option to overcome the Sochi-Gelendzhik route - the bus! A popular way and very simple. You just need to come to the ticket office and, having voiced the desired direction, buy a ticket. Transit flights on this route are carried out four times a day: at half past ten in the morning, at noon, at eight in the evening and at eleven. Time may vary, so it should be checked at the box office. The number of hours that will need to be spent on the journey also varies - it all depends on the route that the bus follows. Route

So, the shortest route "Gelendzhik-Sochi" - directly through several points that it is desirable to identify for yourself. These are Dzhubga, Novomikhailovsky, Tuapse, Lazarevsky, Golovinka, Big Sochi and already the final stop - Sochi. Along the way, there are a lot of small villages like Vishnevka, Volkonka or Vardana, but they usually pass in transit (meaning a shuttle bus).

Costs

If you go by yourself, then in addition to the route you still need to calculate the fare. This refers to gas mileage. So, the approximate calculations are as follows: a passenger car spends eight hundred liters of gasoline per hundred kilometers. Taking into account the fact that the distance from Sochi to Gelendzhik is 246 km, approximately twenty liters will be required. Add to this more fuel consumption for small crossings, as well as what will leave during idle time in traffic jams (which may well be on the outskirts of the city), you should stock up on another five liters from the top. Many go to Gelendzhik from Krasnodar, as this is one of the best and most convenient resort options for residents of the capital of the region. And the most popular route runs through the federal highway "Don" (M-4). And those tourists who travel, for example, from Rostov, may encounter some difficulties. When approaching the capital from Rostov-on-Don, you need to be careful, because if you miss the turn to Dzhubga, you can get into Krasnodar itself and lose a lot of valuable time. But in reality, everything is not so complicated. The road to Dzhubgu, passing through the overpass, leads to the left, directly towards the coast. Here it is necessary to move along it and not turn anywhere, until the moment when a fork in Novorossiysk or Sochi appears in the field of vision. So, before you go on vacation or on business, you must carefully consider your route to get to your destination calmly and without problems.
