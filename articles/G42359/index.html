How to make a piggy bank with your own hands - we combine business with pleasure

Today the piggy bank is a fairly popular gift for any occasion. After all, such a present is not only pleasant in itself, but can also bring practical benefit to its owner, especially if a person does not have enough willpower not to spend money deferred on anything.



The most common and commonplace option is a traditional pig. You can probably find one in the nearest souvenir shop. There are more original piggy banks, but they will have to be searched. But the most unexpected and pleasant gift will be a thing made by hand, especially if it is made in the form of a coveted object, for which funds are raised.

So how to make a piggy bank with your own hands? The answer to this question depends on what parameters and appearance the finished product should have. If the gift should be breakable and solid, it would be reasonable to decorate a jar or a bottle with a narrow neck. To do this, you need a glass base and various elements for its decoration: fabric, shells, paints, sparkles and more.
