Ryazan circus: program, photo

For a very long time, the circus has been a favorite pastime for both children and adults. From there you always go out in a good mood, there you get a full charge of gaiety and vivacity. Ryazan circus previously worked in the big top mode. Clowns, jugglers, acrobats, wildlife tamers and comedians came here to perform.

How the circus appeared in Ryazan

Walking through the city at the beginning of the twentieth century, one could see that posters were pasted on the walls, in all the brightest colors anticipating the upcoming performance that the Ryazan circus would give the audience. Tickets flew instantly, because the performances were really high-quality and interesting. It was interesting to watch them, because every time the viewer was excited by a new sensation. The Ryazan circus was represented by world-class artists.





History of improvement and development

At the beginning of the twentieth century, with the light hand of the Nikitin brothers, residents of Ryazan were presented with a priceless jewel of circus art. This trend came from countries that were part of the Old World.

Thanks to the work of Dmitry, Akim and Peter, the Ryazan State Circus became the stage for the most famous circus artists of that time. At the beginning of the twentieth century, A. Durov showed his number here, which at that time was not yet known as widely as happened later. The performance was dedicated to dog training. It was distinguished by cheerfulness, originality and was infinitely sweet.

G. Yakovlev also passed through the Ryazan circus in the twenties of the last century. He was the holder of competitions in the French wrestling, was engaged in the construction of a summer boardwalk circus. In the late twenties and early thirties this institution was rented by artists from a wide variety of groups. The Ryazan circus also received participants from the Spartak labor collective.

With the advent of this entertainment institution, its popularity, as well as that of the whole sphere of art, grew by leaps and bounds. In winter, in the early seventies, a completely renewed entertainment complex was opened, the foundation was laid, a building was built for 1.7 thousand spectators. The Ryazan State Circus is part of the Rosgosirc Circus, which is owned by a company of state art institutions.

Tickets to the Ryazan circus quickly sold out in order to have time to admire the most talented circus artists: Yuri Nikulin, Zapashny brothers, Irina Burgimova, Margarita Nazarova , Pencil. And many more talented people performed on this wonderful stage.

Enumerate the names of worthy masters of circus work indefinitely, because so many pleased the audience here. The color of the artistic light shone here. The program of the Ryazan circus was full of amazing and fascinating performances that appealed to both children and adults. With each new performance, the locals all with great zeal and pleasure joined the art of skillful circus performers.





Today the circus continues to be popular

Circus art appeared long before many others, but it has not become a thing of the past, but is standing firmly on its feet, attracting an ever new audience from small to large. For more than forty years, the conductor of the Ryazan Circus has been immersing the minds of joyful people in a light and carefree atmosphere, helping to enjoy life and have fun.

Not only the local population, but also visiting tourists do not deny themselves the pleasure of visiting this wonderful place, getting unchanging vivid impressions and taking them home with enthusiastic stories and memories. The most famous circus artists of our time gladly accept invitations to work in the circus, considering this chance to be an honor.

The numbers are constantly changing, and the programs are being improved, keeping up with the times, becoming better and more interesting day by day. Performances differ from each other and correspond to the most delicate requests of sophisticated connoisseurs of circus art.

Fire

At the end of November 2015, the circus building caught fire, the city center was filled with smoke. Firefighters bravely extinguished the flame, saving the pride of Ryazan. Rescuers opened the roof covering with saws, other tools were also used that helped in an important mission. Thanks to the quadrocopter, it was easier for firefighters to work against a background of dense smoke.





About five hours it took to localize the flame, by seven o'clock the fire was completely extinguished. Three dozen diverse technical devices and a hundred people were involved in putting out the fire. The state fire service quickly coped with the task. The city administration was not at a loss, a major overhaul was carried out, after which this beautiful place earned as usual.

Alleged handling by workers in contact with the burner is considered to be the alleged cause of fire.

The insulation roof was the first to light up. Two types of certified insulation were used. In the course of the work, the craftsmen were engaged in arranging the adjoining of a metal roof to a soft layer. For installation, a burner was used. The heater was accidentally set on fire.

First, the material smoldered, and then ignited. The fire covered three hundred square meters. According to the head of the Ministry of Emergency Situations of the Ryazan region, everything that ends well is good. Fortunately for the audience, today the Ryazan circus is working at full strength and continues to delight the grateful audience with the quality and energy of their performances.
