What is an event? The meaning and role in our lives. Important events in 2016

Everyone in life had memorable events: joyful and sad, long-awaited and unexpected, memorable and ordinary, social and personal. What do they mean to us? What is their role in our life?

The word "event" came from the Old Slavonic "come true", which appeared in Russian in the XI century. Its meaning is obvious: it is "execution", "implementation". In other words, this is a fact that happened, happened. The synonyms of a word are: a phenomenon, an incident, a fact, a case, etc.

What is an event scientifically? We will try to answer this question. The word "event" has different interpretations:

  • This is a separate case;
  • psychobiographical, natural phenomenon;
  • a fact of historical, world significance.

As a separate case

What is an event? It is often regarded as a separate case. Philosophy gives the following interpretation of this word: an event is any incident that happened, has uniqueness, is determined by some entity.

From the modern point of view, an event is a major fact, an incident in personal or public life. Such a phenomenon may be the anniversary of the mother, the opening of the exhibition of a famous artist, etc.





What events took place in Europe in 2016? Important facts include: Figure Skating Championship in Bratislava, State Duma elections in Russia, World Forum in Davos, denomination in Belarus , etc.

Psychobiographical phenomenon

An event may be a psychobiographical phenomenon. What is this event? Philosophy defines: a psychobiographical phenomenon is a system of significant descriptions taken by the unit of analysis and research of a separate life path.

The modern interpretation of this word is as follows: a psychobiographical phenomenon is a collection of cases that occur in the "history of life." This includes studying the biography of famous personalities.





Important psychobiographical phenomena in Europe in 2016 are: celebration of the 150th anniversary of the birth of Romain Roland in France, the 400th anniversary of the death of William Shakespeare in the UK, the 150th anniversary of the birth of Vyacheslav Ivanov in Russia, etc.

A natural phenomenon

What is an event? An event is considered a natural phenomenon. From a philosophical point of view, the unit of a natural phenomenon is the natural process. In nature, everything is a chain of events that interact and mutually limit each other.

In the modern interpretation, a natural phenomenon is the result of the action of natural processes that in a certain way affect people, the economy, and the environment. It can be biological, geological, physical, cosmological, etc.





What important events that took place in Europe in 2016 are worth noting? Such natural phenomena include: the largest super moon in the last 68 years in Belarus, an exploding meteorite over Baikal in Russia, a total solar eclipse in Indonesia, etc.

Historical fact

An event is often seen as a fact of historical significance. What is a historical event? The philosophical point of view suggests that there is no real time. Facts should be seen as an area saturated with instants of the past and future.

From a modern point of view, a historical fact is an incident that is of great importance and influences the course of history. Such phenomena may include: presidential elections, signing of a decree of interstate significance, etc.





The major historical facts that occurred in Europe in 2016 include: an attempted military coup in Turkey, terrorist attacks in Europe, a UK referendum on leaving the EU, etc.

World Fact

An event is considered a fact of world significance. What is an event of world significance? The philosophical point of view defines some facts as phenomena that occur at some point and are considered as a process of changing the world.

The modern interpretation of this word is as follows: an event of world significance is a combination of social facts connected among themselves that changed the world. Phenomena of this magnitude can be disasters, epidemics, wars.





Such incidents in Europe in 2016 include: a plane crash in Rostov-on-Don, an earthquake in Italy. The armed conflict in Syria is an event of the century that occurred in 2016.

Events occupy an important place in our lives. They are personal and public. Some of them are forgotten, others are remembered. They make life diverse, saturated, affect the plans of people. The course of history, economics, and politics depends on some phenomena. Modern events are the engine of progress and evolution.
