Seaside Beach Hotel 2: description, photos and reviews of tourists

Vietnam is a relatively exotic country for travelers. Nevertheless, more and more tourists come here every year. After all, here you can enjoy not only the exotic nature and warmth of the sea, but also an interesting culture and sincere hospitality. One of the popular hotels is the Seaside Beach Hotel. So is it worth staying at this hotel?

Description of location and territory





The small cozy hotel Seaside Beach Hotel (formerly Pho Bien Hotel) is located in the famous resort town of Nha Trang, almost on the seashore. Despite the fact that the place is busy, the area in which the hotel is built is relatively quiet and calm. The hotel itself consists of a modern high-rise building and a small courtyard with a garden.

By the way, the distance to the main station of Nyaang is only 3 km, which is convenient for travelers who are going to explore the country. But the international airport is located in Kamran, which is 35 km. On the other hand, tourists, as a rule, are provided with a transfer, so do not worry about the distance.

Seaside Beach Hotel (Nha Trang): what do the rooms look like?





The hotel can rightfully be considered small, since there are only 19 rooms on its territory. There are standard rooms, apartments with a high level of comfort, as well as the so-called common rooms with bunk beds, which are popular among young people.

Regardless of which room you choose, you can count on convenience, silence and comfort. From the window or balcony you can enjoy a beautiful view of the seashore. The rooms are bright, with modern finishes and the necessary amount of equipment. Cable and some satellite channels can be watched on TV. There is a climate control system, which is also convenient. A small refrigerator is ideal for cooling drinks and storing food.

Each room comes with a bathroom with modern fixtures. Here you will find a spacious shower, a constant supply of water, toiletries and, of course, clean towels.

Can I eat at the hotel?





Continental breakfasts are what Vietnam hotels traditionally offer tourists. Seaside Beach Hotel guests can count on this service. In the morning you can visit the local restaurant, where light snacks, soups, fresh pastries and, of course, exotic fruits and aromatic tea are waiting for you. Reviews say that you can really have a delicious breakfast here. The rest of the day in the restaurant you can eat, ordering dishes on the menu and paying a separate bill.

In any case, the search for a catering establishment will not be a problem, since in the cities, even near the hotel, there are several dozen cafes and restaurants offering dishes at very reasonable prices.

Beach vacation travelers





Vietnam is famous all over the world for its pristine snow-white beaches. Seaside Beach Hotel is built almost on the coast - the distance to the sea is only 30 meters. The beach here is public, but not too crowded, so if you wish, you can relax, enjoying the sound of the sea.

For a very small fee, you can rent a deck chair, comfortable mattress and umbrella for the whole day. Nearby is a bar that offers vacationers delicious cocktails, chilled drinks, as well as lunches and dinners. Like on any other beach, here you can ride on water transport, have fun doing parasailing or try other water activities. By the way, almost all year round the sea here is calm and warm.

Leisure and additional service

Despite its modest size, the hotel can still offer something. For example, almost throughout the territory there is free access to the Internet, which is important for a modern tourist. You can also take things to the laundry. And for an additional fee, staff will ensure ironing. On the territory of the hotel you can help rent a transport, including bicycles, cars, scooters - this helps travelers to open new horizons, and the prices are quite affordable.

As for leisure, there are many entertainment complexes in the city. In the hotel itself there is a permanent desk where you can purchase an interesting excursion.
