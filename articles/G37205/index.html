Holidays in Sochi in June: weather, photos, reviews

Why Sochi

It is not for nothing that this city is called the summer or resort capital of Russia. Throughout the Soviet period, this particular city was a favorite for all residents. Due to its favorable location between the Caucasus Mountains and the Black Sea, in the zone of humid subtropics, Sochi is not only a place amazingly suitable for a beach holiday, but also an excellent balneological resort. That is why, in addition to hotels, there are a huge number of resorts. Thousands of Russians and guests from neighboring countries are treated here annually. Unique natural conditions make it possible not only to have a good rest, but also to restore the resources of your body.



Just imagine the prospect. Here you will find a stunning beach vacation. Basically, the coating is pebble, but areas with golden sand are also found. At the same time, in June you can still relax in the mountain resort - Krasnaya Polyana. One of the most popular sites is the Olympic Park. A colorful show of singing fountains will impress any tourist, even having visited the best European resorts. Sochi has preserved a huge number of archaeological sites, among which mysterious dolmens can be found. It is worth taking an excursion to Mount Akhun, where from the observation deck you can admire the panorama of the city, the emerald sea and mountains covered with forests.
