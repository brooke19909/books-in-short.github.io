Guest House "Sea Star" (Arkhipo-Osipovka): conditions, reviews

Where to go south? By the way, the beach season of 2017 is still open! Some go to sunny Greece, Turkey or Spain, others choose the Russian south. Equally important for relaxation is the accommodation.

Those who argue that the main thing in the south is the sea and the beach are mistaken. If your accommodation will be a barn, on the second day of your stay in it you will be waiting for departure, as a demobilization. Another thing when you are accommodated in a spacious room with amenities. And if the hotel also has a swimming pool, it becomes unimportant that the sea is stormy or the water in it is cold. We present to your attention the guest house "Sea Star".

Arkhipo-Osipovka, in whose territory it is located, is a remote suburb of Gelendzhik. In this article, we will tell not only about the mini-hotel, but also about the features of relaxation in this resort.





How to get to the village of Arkhipo-Osipovka

The Sea Star Hotel is located at 10. Orekhovaya Street, 10. Arkhipo-Osipovka was in the past an Adyghe village, but after the Caucasian War it was renamed to erase all references to whose lands it is. Aul was called Vulanskaya - after the name of the river that flows here.

In 1889, the king ordered to give the village a Slavic sound at all. The village was named after the soldier Arkhip Osipov, who died defending the fortress from the local population. Now the village is administratively owned by the Big Gelendzhik. Although the city from Arkhipo-Osipovka - as much as 56 kilometers. But do not worry about how to get to the "Starfish". Minibuses run to Arkhipo-Osipovka from Gelendzhik. For a reasonable fee, the inn owners will arrange a personal transfer for you. The hotel is very well located in relation to the infrastructure of the village: the central market is nearby, there are shops very close by. To the sea from the hotel - 700 meters, that is, five to seven minutes walk. And notice, on flat terrain, without descents and ascents.



Territory

Guest House "Sea Star" in Arkhipo-Osipovka consists of two buildings. The first has three floors, and the second has four. The courtyard, covered with paving slabs, is beautifully landscaped. Mediterranean vegetation is pleasing to the eye everywhere.

Between the buildings there is a chic swimming pool with a jacuzzi function. Sun beds are installed in sufficient quantities. The fresh water pool is heated and highlighted in the evenings, which gives the hotel an additional charm. For those who came to Arkhipo-Osipovka on their own vehicles, there is a guarded parking lot. For guests, it is free.

A small playground is equipped on site.

For those who want to treat themselves to barbecue or grilled dishes, several barbecue facilities and a wooden arbor are installed.

Nutrition

Guests of the Morskaya Zvezda guest house in Arkhipo-Osipovka are eager to visit cafes and restaurants, which are numerous in the resort, especially during the tourist season. But why go somewhere if you can pay for meals on the spot? On the ground floor of one of the buildings of the hotel there is a cafe where guests will be served home-cooked meals. Soups, meatballs, pancakes, pickles and compotes - all this is repeatedly mentioned by reviews. Guests add that everything is delicious, made with fresh local produce. The cafe hall is very cozy, with air conditioning. Portions are decent in size. If you are an ardent supporter of self-nutrition, the owners will not be offended by you. You can use the barbecue to prepare your own grilled dish or barbecue.



Beach

Guest House Sea Star in Arkhipo-Osipovka is 700 meters from the city beach. The coast at this resort is covered with small pebbles, and the bottom smoothly goes into the depths. In addition, Arkhipo-Osipovka beach is closed on two sides by capes that protrude far into the Black Sea. Therefore, there are no winds, there are rarely storms. The beach in Arkhipo-Osipovka fully meets the requirements of a modern resort. There are entertainments on the water, changing cabins, a stationary canopy from the sun. No less lively is the promenade. For children and adults, the Malibu Park operates with various attractions and a large Ferris wheel. There is a dolphinarium on the promenade.
