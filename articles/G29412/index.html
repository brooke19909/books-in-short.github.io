Everyone has the right to rest. What he will be, it is up to him to decide. Agree, the first thing that probably comes to mind at the mere mention of rest is the sea and the beach. Unfortunately, it is far from always possible to get to the coast in the summer, when the season lasts in many countries. But this is not a reason to be upset, because in the arsenal of a modern tourist and traveler there are enough directions, including for winter holidays. One of these is Vietnam. If you have already visited Thailand or Goa, Vietnam will definitely appeal to you.
