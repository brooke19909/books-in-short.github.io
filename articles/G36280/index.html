Barbara Tourist Apartments (Cyprus, Ayia Napa): room description, service, reviews

Practically all Russian tourists wish to relax inexpensively. Therefore, with the beginning of the summer season, our compatriots begin to look for all kinds of options to go to foreign resorts and not spend all their savings at the same time. If the search for a solution to this problem is also relevant for you, then consider for yourself the opportunity to relax in Cyprus by booking apartments in Barbara Tourist Apartments. This hotel complex cannot boast of a large number of stars, however it will be quite comfortable for unassuming tourists who are not inclined to spend a long period of time in the room.





Hotel Description

Barbara Tourist Apartments is a fairly small hotel complex, designed for youth companies and families with children. Most of the guests spend whole days at sea and return to their rooms only in the evening. Therefore, during the day on the hotel there is practically not a single person.

Barbara Tourist Apartments 2 * consists of several low-rise buildings that form a courtyard with a pool and a well-kept garden. The hotel complex can be reached through several entrances. They are not guarded, however, like the hotel itself. It is worth noting that this is a hallmark of Cyprus as a whole and in particular Ayia Napa. There are very few police officers, because theft on the island is not common. Even merchants calmly leave their goods on street stalls, going about their business. This feature is very surprising for tourists.

The hotel complex provides accommodation of guests in thirty-nine rooms. Guests can choose from two categories, which we will tell readers about in the next section of the article.

Location

Located Barbara Tourist Apartments in Ayia Napa. This resort is considered one of the most vibrant and youthful in Cyprus. However, the hotel is located in a fairly quiet area. Tourists claim that the residential area where the hotel complex is built is one of the most remote from the sea. Therefore, you will have to go to the beach for at least twenty-five to thirty minutes. Many guests claim that the distance to the sea is at least one thousand meters. However, rest does not spoil it. After all, the road runs through picturesque streets planted with olive, orange and other trees. And bright small houses complement the incredibly beautiful landscape.

The beach closest to the hotel is Agia Thekla. It does not belong to the most famous in the resort, but nevertheless it is very popular among vacationers. Not only hotel guests gather here, but also locals who are attracted by clear water and crystal white sand.

It is noteworthy that very close to the hotel there are numerous clubs and bars. You need to go to them no more than ten minutes, this allows partygoers to save on a taxi during their nightly adventures.





Accommodations

The service at Barbara Tourist Apartments matches the two stars that it has. Therefore, going here, do not expect a high level of service and a huge number of infrastructure facilities.

The hotel complex has a large swimming pool with sun loungers. Most of the time it is empty and only in the evening there are vacationers returning from the beach. There is a bar by the pool where you can buy soft drinks and alcoholic cocktails.

The reception has a luggage storage room where you can leave your things at an additional cost. Since it is customary to move around in rented vehicles in Cyprus, convenient parking is available near the hotel. There are always free places for car guests.

There is one restaurant in the hotel complex that provides guests with meals according to different schemes.

If necessary, tourists can arrange transfers with staff to anywhere in the island. However, please note that the hotel staff does not speak Russian. All questions you have to solve in English.

In addition to cars, bicycles can be rented at a special point. Ayia Napa is great for lovers of this pastime. Bicycle paths are equipped here, and the views that open to the gaze of tourists can not leave anyone indifferent.





Entertainment

If you do not plan to leave the hotel often, it is better to look for another option for yourself. Indeed, in Barbara Tourist Apartments there is practically no entertainment. Of the available options, guests can choose to swim in the pool or play a game of billiards. Moreover, the cost of the latter is not included in the price.

I especially want to say about Wi-Fi. In some months it is free, while in others it will have to be paid separately. Moreover, each time the conditions for using the Internet change. It is interesting that tourists who rested in Barbara Tourist Apartments in one season write different information about the rules for using Wi-Fi. Therefore, be prepared for the fact that you will have to pay for this service.





Barbara Tourist Apartments: Food

The hotel provides guests with several catering systems. When booking a room, travelers can specify only breakfast or any other scheme up to all-inclusive. However, even with the latter option, you will not see the buffet so beloved by the Russians. The all-inclusive concept of meals includes √† la carte breakfast, lunch and dinner. Therefore, our compatriots often prefer not to eat at the hotel. They love to go to local restaurants for food and cook in the fully equipped kitchen, which is available in every room.





Barbara Tourist Apartments: room description

Of course, the level of equipment of the apartments is far from five-star hotel complexes. Visibly shabby furniture needs to be updated, and the rooms themselves "scream" about the need for repairs. Here everything is arranged very simply and resembles an ordinary rented apartment. However, everything you need for a comfortable stay in the room is still there.

Guests can use cable TV, and this technology is quite modern. But the guests have a lot of complaints about the air conditioner, it is old and noisy. In addition, quite often it is difficult to adjust it to the desired temperature.

Problems and arise with the discharge of water in the shower. Often during washing, it stops passing into the drain and completely floods the floors in the bathroom. In some rooms, water even seeps into the room. There are also problems with hot water. It begins to flow only six minutes after turning on the tap.

Each room has a small balcony and a well-equipped kitchen. It is often prepared by those guests who are trying to save on food. The reviews provide information that some tourists take to these hotels some stock of products from home, and then just buy what they need at local shops. Kitchen equipment allows you to cook a wide variety of dishes.

The rooms are divided into two categories. Some are one-room apartments, designed for four guests. And others are more like a stylish studio that can accommodate three guests.

Please note that some rooms also have a coffee machine. This allows you to enjoy a morning cup of fine coffee without leaving your room.





Cost of living

The hotel owes great popularity among Russians for its low cost. A ticket here can be purchased for very little money. For example, a tour for four with a departure from Moscow will cost no more than forty thousand rubles.

Additional information for guests

Before arriving at the hotel, it will be useful to examine some useful information. For example, keep in mind that pets are not allowed in the hotel complex.

Guests are checked in at two in the afternoon, and check-out is from seven in the morning until noon.

You can call the hotel with children of any age, and payment for additional services is made using credit cards.





Hotel reviews

Reviews on Barbara Tourist Apartments are quite numerous. Among them there are positive and negative, therefore, analyzing them, we identified the advantages and disadvantages of this hotel.

The following factors are among the minuses of our compatriots:

  • not quite clean cooking utensils;
  • occurring insects;
  • simplicity of interiors;
  • lack of security;
  • poor sound insulation;
  • a problem with a drain in the shower;
  • remoteness from the beach;
  • low level of service.

If you are not scared by this list, then you can go to the advantages of the hotel complex:

  • convenient location relative to the city center;
  • delicious food at the hotel;
  • spacious rooms;
  • calm and silence on the territory;
  • quick check-in;
  • a small number of Russian tourists;
  • luxurious beach;
  • low cost of living.

In general, the hotel complex is recommended by its former guests to their friends and acquaintances. However, do not forget that it is an economical option, so you should not expect any frills from the hotel. If you plan to come to your room just to refresh yourself in the shower and sleep, then you can safely book the apartments in Barbara Tourist Apartments 2 * and prepare for a wonderful trip to Cyprus.
