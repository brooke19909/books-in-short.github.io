Tsokkos Sun Garden Apts 4 - a great stay in Cyprus

Description: The amazing Tsokkos Sun Garden Apts 4 hotel gives young people, the elderly and families with children the opportunity to spend unforgettable precious days on vacation or vacation. The staff of this hotel takes first-class care of their customers vacationing in beautiful sunny Cyprus. It is impossible not to mention the comfortable location of the hotel. It is only ten minutes away by car from the city of Protaras, where you will find a huge number of clubs, restaurants, as well as bars. It takes less than an hour to get to the international airport.





Rooms: The five-story building has sixty-eight rooms for guests. The rooms have all the amenities needed for a memorable stay: very spacious and soft beds, air conditioning, satellite TV, a large reliable safe, telephone, refrigerator, desk, radio, kettle, free minibar, and a furnished balcony. In the bathroom, which is also in the hotel room, you will find a toilet, shower, washbasin, hairdryer and hygiene items. Cots are available upon request. Rooms at Tsokkos Sun Garden Apts 4 are cleaned daily.





Meals: "All inclusive" is a system operating in this hotel. Within Tsokkos Sun Garden Apts 4 there is a restaurant called Demetra Restaurant, a cocktail bar, and a cafeteria. In these establishments, caring and friendly staff invites tourists to enjoy international and local cuisine, advises many delicious snacks prepared with the freshest and highest quality products, as well as soft and alcoholic drinks, which travelers enthusiastically respond to.

Beach: Near the hotel there are a large number of public beaches, the most famous of which are Constantinos the Great and Konnos. Golden sands and clear water of Marilta Bay guarantee a pleasant pastime in any part of the coast. For a fee, any vacationer can use umbrellas and sun loungers on the beaches.

Information for travelers: Everyone living in Tsokkos Sun Garden Apts 4 has the opportunity to visit two swimming pools (in winter, one of them is heated), a sauna, a jacuzzi, a fitness center, and a steam room. At the hotel you can freely do all kinds of water sports, get quick access to Wi-Fi Internet, park your car, and exchange currency. The services of a doctor, courier, massage therapist, nanny, taxi, laundry and dry cleaning services are offered to all clients upon request. Nearby are various historical sites - museums and churches. The staff at Tsokkos Sun Gardens Apartments 4 answers any questions you may have.





Reviews: The wonderful Tsokkos Sun Garden Apts 4 hotel, reviews of which are extremely positive, does not leave indifferent any client.

Travelers are attracted by the cozy and relaxed atmosphere prevailing in the spacious and well-lit hotel rooms, the convenient location of the hotel and very affordable prices.

All customers are delighted with the variety of dishes that are prepared with fresh products, they give a lot of energy for a memorable and active holiday.
