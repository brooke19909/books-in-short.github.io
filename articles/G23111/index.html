Prototypes of Vasily Zalessky

Creating the image of a sort of Russian musketeer, a handsome daredevil, secretly in love with the Grand Duchess, the creators of the series used real facts from the life of historical figures. For example, an episode with the prince setting fire to his house in order to divert the attention of Peter III from Catherine II, who at that time gave birth to an illegitimate child, did occur. However, in fact, this fire was arranged by the valet of the Empress Vasily Shkurin. Knowing the love of Peter III for the spectacle of fire, he set fire to his house so that the emperor would not be present at the permission of the burden of his wife.

The appearance of Zalessky surrounded by Catherine

The young prince Vasily Zalessky, whose biography was the subject of our review, begins his service with the all-powerful chancellor Elizaveta Petrovna - Bestuzhev - and immediately becomes involved in palace intrigues, finding himself in their center. This is the time when, at the invitation of Empress Elizabeth, the young German princess arrives in Russia as the bride of the heir to the throne. Bestuzhev sharply opposes this union, believing that marriage will not benefit the state. Zalessky obtains evidence of the participation in the conspiracy against the interests of Russia of the mother of Princess Fike. However, Sophia-Charlotte herself had nothing to do with the conspiracy, later it was completely justified in the eyes of Bestuzhev and Elizaveta Petrovna.

The participation of Zalessky in the fate of Catherine

After a serious illness, Princess Fike accepts Orthodoxy, having received the name Catherine at baptism. Bestuzhev, although he ceased to suspect the German princess of hostile actions, nevertheless left in her immediate circle his favorite, Prince Vasily. When on the first wedding night a drunken Peter III began to insult a young wife, Zalessky intervened for her. The infuriated heir to the throne orders to send the insolent to exile in Siberia. However, Bestuzhev did not offend his nominee, and the case was limited to demolishing Zalessky into soldiers. In the future, Catherine in relations with her husband was largely guided by Bestuzhev's advice. The natural charm, intelligence and beauty of the wife of the heir to the throne attracted a large number of people close to the royal family. All this time, the prince of Zalessky, not claiming special attention of the Grand Duchess, as the title of Catherine was called at that time, was always nearby, ready to come to the rescue at any moment. So, when Grigory Orlov, who was close to the court of the heir to the throne, while in a drunk state, began to insult Catherine the Great, Prince Vasily Zalessky challenged him to a duel.





Further milestones of the biography of the prince

And now Prince Zalessky Vasily Alekseevich under Catherine. He voluntarily and involuntarily has a decisive influence on the development of historical events. So, after Russia declared war on the Prussian emperor Frederick, Zalessky was eager to join the battle. Therefore, he independently undertook a sortie against Prussian officers. Such adventurism gave a positive result, since it was possible to obtain documents important for Russia from the Dresden archive. The birth of Catherine the heir to the throne, which was immediately taken away by Elizabeth Petrovna, led to increased isolation. The empress does not allow Catherine to her son, and only thanks to the help of Bestuzhev, who provided her through Zalessky, the mother was able to see the child. At this time, Zalessky married, and he had a daughter, whom he named Katya.

The serious illness of Empress Elizabeth Petrovna to the limit exacerbated the question of who will become her successor. Peter III was not at all popular, and many did not want to see him on the throne after the death of Elizabeth Petrovna. At the same time, Orlov and Prince Vasily forged close ties with Catherine's closest friend, Princess Dashkova.





Vasily Zalessky at a turning point

With the proclamation of Peter III as the new Russian emperor, the danger of personal reprisal against Ekaterina Alekseevna is much greater, especially since it is no secret to anyone that she is pregnant from Grigory Orlov.
