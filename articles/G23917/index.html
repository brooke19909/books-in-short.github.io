Valset Apartments by HELIOPARK, Russia: overview, description and reviews

Rosa Khutor is one of the most popular destinations for domestic tourism, where you can have a good rest not only in the summer, but also in the winter. Valset Apartments by Heliopark will make your stay especially enjoyable.





Location

In one of the most beautiful places in the Krasnodar Territory, the Valset Apartments by Heliopark Resort is located. Rosa Khutor, Esto-Sadok, Kamenka street, 1 - the address where you can find this resort. It is just a minute walk from the beach. 5 minutes from the hotel is the main street of the resort with many cafes, shops and entertainment venues. Sochi International Airport is 50 km away.

A rich excursion program prepared for guests of Valset Apartments by Heliopark. Rosa Khutor will delight travelers with natural and cultural attractions, among which are the following:

  • ski resort (1 km);
  • sledding center (1 km);
  • cable car (1 km);
  • sports complex "Laura" (3 km).




Accommodation

Offers travelers accommodation at the Valset Apartments by Heliopark. Guests are available rooms of the following categories:

  • Studio is a spacious apartment for 2 guests. The large room has a double bed, as well as a soft sofa. Separately allocated kitchen area with household appliances, utensils and a dining table. A roomy wardrobe is provided. It has a private balcony.
  • One-room apartments are equipped with twin beds, as well as upholstered furniture for relaxation. The kitchen area is located in a niche and is equipped with a hob and a small dining table.
  • Two-room apartments include a living room and a bedroom, separated by an internal door. There is also a zone with a full kitchen, which includes a hob, sink, electrical appliances and a dining table for 3 people.
  • Three-room apartments resemble the previous category. The main difference is the presence of an additional bedroom, which is equipped with its own toilet. One of the rooms provides a workplace.
  • Four-room apartments consist of two bedrooms, as well as a spacious living room. Unlike the previous categories, the kitchen and dining room are a separate room, and not part of the living room.
  • Presidential apartments are often chosen by business people because of the availability of their own office with the ability to connect cable Internet. There are also 2 bedrooms and a living room. The hallway has a fitted wardrobe.
  • Duplex apartment is a luxury accommodation option on the top floor, which provides a beautiful view from the window. On the first level there is a living room with a kitchen, and on the second - two separate bedrooms, each of which has its own bathroom and a balcony.

Vacation with children

For those who love traveling with their children, Valset Apartments by Heliopark is a great option. Everything necessary for organizing leisure for young tourists is provided here. Guests can leave their kids in the children's room, where there are all the resources for a fun pastime - toys, educational activities, creativity and more. It also organizes walks around the territory and provides rental of children's sports equipment.

It is worth noting that there is a playground in the courtyard of the Valset Apartments Heliopark 3 * hotel. It provides modern attractions that are suitable for active games, as well as for sports. On the ground there is a safe rubberized coating that will protect the fidget from injuries and bruises.





Hotel infrastructure

Everything you need for a good rest, you can find on site Valset Apartments by Heliopark. The infrastructure of the institution can be described as follows:

  • specially equipped smoking areas, both outdoors and indoors;
  • elevator for the comfort of guests living on the upper floors;
  • special amenities for a comfortable stay for guests with disabilities;
  • convenience store with groceries and other essential goods;
  • souvenir shop;
  • vending machines with drinks, sandwiches and chocolate bars;
  • free wifi in public areas;
  • public parking, the use of which is provided for 200 rubles per day;
  • 24-hour reception desk;
  • luggage room for guests luggage;
  • modern conference room for business negotiations.

Entertainment and sports

The administration of Valset Apartments by Heliopark Rosa Khutor 3 * pays great attention to the organization of leisure guests. So, here you will have the following opportunities for entertainment and outdoor activities:

  • hotel guests are provided with coupons for using the cableway and ski slopes;
  • a tour desk will provide you with the opportunity to explore the local natural and cultural attractions;
  • Guests can rent bicycles, skis and other sports equipment;
  • an animation program that includes daytime sports and evening shows;
  • several sports grounds for lovers of an active lifestyle;
  • Guests can use the billiards room at a nearby hotel.

Hotel service

A host of services are available to guests at Valset Apartments by Heliopark (Rosa Khutor). Guests can use the following service:

  • apartment cleaning, including regular replacement of bed linen and bath towels;
  • taxi call at the appointed time;
  • the possibility of using cable internet for an additional fee;
  • delivery of orders from the restaurant to the rooms;
  • providing a transfer to the railway station or airport;
  • safe rental at the reception;
  • laundry and shoe washing services;
  • use of office documents.




Additional Information

Some information you should know before traveling to Valset Apartments Heliopark. The hotel operates in accordance with the following rules:

  • Guests can check into their room after 14:00, and return the keys to the administrator on the day of departure until noon;
  • children under 6 years old stay in apartments free of charge subject to placement on existing beds;
  • Cots are available in the room for 400 rubles per day;
  • placing children on extra beds costs 700 rubles per day, and for adults this service will cost 1000 rubles;
  • only one extra bed is possible in the room;
  • for an additional fee and by prior arrangement, pets are allowed;
  • it is possible to pay for accommodation and received services in non-cash form;
  • upon registration, guests are required to present a passport, as well as a birth certificate for each child;
  • Guests are required to pay a security deposit of 5,000 rubles, which is refunded upon eviction if no material damage has been caused to the hotel.

Negative reviews

Valset Apartments by Heliopark (Rosa Khutor) is characterized by a number of negative points. From the reviews you can find out the following:

  • there are no microwave ovens in the rooms (you have to reheat the food on the hob);
  • there are no coolers with drinking water in the halls;
  • Too small refrigerators in which practically nothing fits;
  • the tiles in the bathroom are moldy;
  • there are no signs on the way to the hotel, which makes it difficult to get to it;
  • an intricate system of buildings and the lack of information plates on the territory;
  • there is no canopy above the balcony, and therefore all the rain falls on the floor;
  • on the double bed there are two mattresses, which are constantly moving apart;
  • there is no private parking, and the one located nearby can be used only for an additional fee;
  • in the kitchen area there is neither a towel nor a sponge for washing dishes;
  • despite the ban on smoking, the administration almost never fines violators, and therefore this rule is not respected;
  • there is no coffee table on the balcony;
  • paid cribs;
  • the hood in the kitchen area does not work well.

This accommodation option is perfect for lovers of sea holidays, because the hotel is located near the beach. Also, the institution will welcome fans of skiing, because there are several ski lifts and mountain slopes very close by.
