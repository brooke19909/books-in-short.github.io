The best recreation center. Shamora, Vladivostok, recreation centers: photos and reviews of tourists

Want to relax in a stunning bay, lie on the waves and bask in the sun? Then you will find one of the most beautiful places in the Far Eastern Territory, Shamora Bay. On its coast there are a number of diverse settlements. Any recreation center (Shamora) is a favorite vacation spot not only for those who live nearby. People come here on vacation from all over Russia. The powerful energy of the sun and the sea gives an amazing charge of health and vitality for the whole year. Just a few years ago there were simple recreation centers, wooden houses and a sandy beach. What else is needed for a great summer vacation. Today, the situation has changed radically. Each recreation center (Shamora) is developing rapidly, so there are more and more cafes and restaurants, shops and attractions, water equipment rentals. Holiday Village "Dolphin"

This recreation center (Chamor) represents an amazing variety of options for your vacation. There are more modern, youth pensions, and there are calm, quiet corners where it is so good to enjoy a summer vacation. One of them is the recreation center "Dolphin". Cleanliness and comfort always reign here, vacationers are located in one of two wooden buildings. We must give them their due, despite the simplicity of the interior, there is everything you need. This bed and bedding, table and chair, refrigerator. Individual verandas are attached to the buildings, where it is so good to drink tea and play board games in the evenings.

The recreation center itself is very green, everywhere there are beautiful flower beds with flowers. Since many people come with children, a large playground is equipped in the center of the base, it is fenced and well visible from all sides. There is a tennis table on the covered porch, and there is also a rental of various sports equipment. Separately, there is a platform with barbecue facilities. The cost of living here is 1500 rubles. for a double room, 2000 for a triple and 2500 for a quadruple. Judging by the reviews, this is an ideal base for a family vacation.



The recreation center "Pearl", Shamora

For those who can not imagine themselves without outdoor activities, we strongly recommend this settlement. It is located on the shore of Solnechnaya Bay, Ussuri Bay. Only 25 km from the city of Vladivostok - and you find yourself in a wonderful world of sea and sun. "Pearl" is known for its hospitality, the highest service, excellent conditions for living and dining. For accommodation, guests are offered houses of various sizes and levels of comfort, from "economy" to "luxury". However, each of them is equipped with good furniture and amenities. The recreation center "Pearl" (Shamora) is one of the most comfortable places on the coast. On the territory of the base there is a real Russian bathhouse, barbecue facilities, various sports fields, as well as a stunning beach. The cost is very different depending on the selected package of services, the minimum daily cost of living with meals is 750 rubles, the maximum is 3000 rubles. From what tourists say, we can conclude that for sports and energetic tourists it is better not to find a base.



Modern "Alena"

This is probably the newest recreation center. Alena (Shamora) managed to earn reviews not only due to the novelty and external brilliance, this was facilitated by the convenient location, as well as the efforts of the staff. The base is located on the right bank of the Lazurnaya bay, that is, almost in the suburbs, on the first beach. This place has long been very popular among tourists. On the territory there are several options for accommodating tourists. The first is a hotel. A real, comfortable hotel with double and quadruple rooms. All of them have separate entrances, heated, with a shower and a bathroom. That is, if you are a lover of comfort, then this is an option for you. In addition, small houses with a heated bathroom and shower are scattered throughout the base. The territory itself is green and beautiful. The cost of rest here starts from 2500 rubles.



The largest and most beautiful base

This, of course, is the Cote d'Azur (Chamor). A recreation center that really impresses with its splendor. It is located extremely conveniently, in the most beautiful corner of Lazurnaya Bay. Here, the magnificent nature is combined with clean air and pleasant service. There are several options for accommodating tourists. This is a small hotel, small houses with amenities and bungalows without amenities. Meals are provided in the dining room, a three-day board costs 600 rubles. Accommodation in hotel rooms costs 3000 per room per day. The houses cost 1000 per day, and the bungalow will cost 700 rubles. Judging by the reviews, this recreation center is the most hospitable and comfortable.

All the houses have electricity, so you can enjoy all the benefits of civilization on vacation. The hotel and all the houses (except for the summer bungalow, there is a summer shower and a bathroom on the street) have a bathroom and shower. Additionally, there is a sauna and a Finnish sauna, a pool and a billiard room on site. This recreation center (Chamor) has four private beaches, of which two are sandy and two are sandy-pebble. The bottom is rocky, which creates some inconvenience, but the water is very clean.



Entertainment on the Cote d'Azur

This is a true paradise for a youth company. There are constantly working dance halls, including an open-air disco. You can regularly visit the bar and karaoke, billiards room. If these entertainments are boring, then a variety of excursions in the water area will bring variety. For lovers of water activities, there is a rental of boats and fishing equipment. However, in fairness, it should be noted that all recreation facilities on Shamor (Vladivostok) are a great opportunity to spend time in nature.

The recreation center "Cape Cool"

We add to your piggy bank one more excellent place so that you have something to think about when you will consider recreation centers (Shamora). Reviews about him are extremely attractive, starting with the fact that this cape is located on the most popular beach of Primorye. Only 20 km separates it from Vladivostok. This base is distinguished by a huge territory, a large number of cozy houses and relatively low prices. A double house will cost 2500 per day, and a suite of about 5500 rubles. Additionally, there is a camping site if you are attracted to romance.



To summarize

Of course, this is far from all. There are other beautiful places, such as "Aphrodite" (a recreation center in the Shamora area) and dozens of others. Even if you really want to, it will not be easy to visit each of them, because there are a lot of them. That is why we have compiled a brief overview of the most popular, so you can plan your vacation long before it arrives. The stunning nature of Vladivostok calls for rest, on the golden sand, to the gentle sea. It is especially pleasing that such beautiful places are located so close to the city.
