Where can I get cross stitch patterns for the kitchen?

Cross stitching is perhaps one of the most fascinating areas of needlework. It often happens that, having tried to make several crosses on the canvas, you cannot deny yourself the pleasure to continue embroidering.

What is embroidery for?

Embroidery itself is of great therapeutic value. She calms, arranges the running of restless thoughts, masterly helps to move away from the hustle and bustle. Sewing cute and vibrant stories can train your eyesight. It is only necessary that the lighting still be very good. Multi-colored juicy threads, a kreinik and other interesting and bright elements that are used in this type of needlework, act on the embroiderer, (or embroiderer), as they became popular today - antistress coloring pages.

Who likes to embroider?

Mostly women and girls like to embroider. But a decent percentage of the male population has already appeared in cross-stitch. It is noticed: men are able to embroider much more accurately and faster than some women. And if the female half of embroiderers do not hesitate to admit that they possess such a delicate and painstaking art, then men, for the most part, are silent about such a hobby.

Embroidery can decorate many things. And today we will consider kitchen embroidery.

Where to begin?

Cross stitch patterns for the kitchen are easy to find in stores with a handmade bias. And if you have never previously touched a canvas and a special needle for embroidery, then it is better to start with the smallest scheme. Look for yourself a printed canvas that depicts a kitchen motif. Usually, a memo is attached to such a canvas, and it will tell you which thread to buy.

When that small design is ready for you and it becomes clear that you love and know how to embroider, then it's time to go to the embroidery department of the needlework store again and buy this time a cross-stitch pattern for the kitchen is more complicated, and possibly more.

What subjects are suitable for applied embroidery in the kitchen?

Embroidery in the kitchen is used in the form of: potholders, coasters for glasses, for heating pads on a kettle, for decorating a kitchen apron and towels, and much more for what, it all depends on the embroiderer's imagination. Someone sews textile bags and decorates them with hand-embroidered plots, someone reaches the heights of excellence and is able to embroider drawings of amazing beauty and accuracy directly on kitchen tablecloths and curtains. And all sorts of napkins for the kitchen, probably, were not embroidered only by very lazy embroiderers. By the way, small plots, or rather cross stitch patterns for the kitchen, can be downloaded free of charge on the Internet.

To decorate a kitchen with embroidery, plots with the image: dishes, vegetables and fruits, kitchen utensils are most often taken.

Want to embroider a cute napkin for a breadbox? Look for openwork patterns and cross stitch patterns for the kitchen. Such ornaments are not difficult to complete for a novice embroiderer.
