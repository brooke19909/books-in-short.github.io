What washing machine to choose if a baby is born soon

There are so many devices for washing clothes that you can spend hours thinking about which washing machine to choose. Let's meditate together.



If you are two in a family and a child is expected, then you need a small vertical washing machine. A child will not crawl into the laundry loading compartment of such an aggregate, and when it starts to walk, it will not immediately figure out how to open the drum.

You can buy a unit with a horizontal load of linen. But then you should pay special attention to blocking the hatch and control panel. Such protection from children allows you to start washing only by sequential or simultaneous pressing of certain keys. One-hundred-percent guarantee of protection against children in such devices does not give any manufacturer. After all, kids are often persistent and stubborn in such a serious matter as pressing buttons.



If you are still wondering which washing machine to choose, read the news about how an American dad accidentally washed his baby.

So, the device in which the laundry is loaded from above is suitable for you. This is an excellent occasion to continue advising which washing machine to choose.

Look for a vertical unit that has child protection. So you play it safe. After all, a child can climb a stool when he grows up a little.



It should be noted that finding a vertical washing machine with child protection is not so simple. But in online stores you can find devices with a silent engine. The control panel and access to load laundry are on top. Therefore, the child will not be able to independently start the machine, affect the course of washing and open the drum for linen. In addition, the device does not take up much space in the bathroom, in the kitchen or in the combined bathroom. Judge for yourself: height - 85 cm, width - 40 cm, depth - 60 cm. With such dimensions, the drum is able to take six kilograms of dry linen and process it in intensive mode in just half an hour. Added to this is a delayed start, which can be programmed on an electronic display.

When thinking about which washing machine to choose, pay attention not only to vertical loading and child protection. Since washing is often necessary for the baby, the energy-saving class is important. The most economical units are designated "A ++". In addition to saving energy, it is desirable that the vertical machine is still equipped with complete leakage protection. In some units, this protection is only partially implemented. Otherwise, all vertical units are very similar. Their electronic control systems allow the economical use of water and spin control depending on the weight of the laundry.
