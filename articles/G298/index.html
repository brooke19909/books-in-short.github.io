Longuinhos Beach Resort 3 *, Colva, India: hotel description, reviews of vacationers

Hotel Longuinhos Beach Resort 3 * offers you to plunge into the Arabian Sea and admire one of the most beautiful Indian beaches, in the territory of which this resort is located.





Short hotel description

Resort Longuinhos Beach Resort 3 * is one of the best places to relax, because tourists will find a lot of entertainment here. In addition to being on one of the most beautiful beaches, the hotel also has a clean outdoor pool. And travelers will certainly appreciate the opportunity to spend an evening on the open-air dance floor. Guests also have the opportunity to take advantage of free parking.

Longuinhos Beach Resort 3 * offers its guests quite comfortable accommodation in beautiful and well-equipped rooms. It has everything you need for tourists to feel comfortable and at ease. The nice thing is that some apartments have a balcony from which you can enjoy the pool or the sea view.

One of the main advantages of the Longuinhos Beach Resort 3 * resort is its restaurant, where you can get acquainted with traditional Indian cuisine. In addition, it also serves international dishes and original masterpieces from local chefs. Also, arriving in Goa, you must certainly get acquainted with its natural and historical sights, in which you will be assisted by the staff of the tour desk.





Resort Location

The location of this hotel is South Goa. Reviews of tourists indicate a fairly favorable location of the hotel. This is due to the fact that the path to Colva Beach takes no more than one minute, even if you walk with a slow walking step. It is also worth noting that not far from the hotel is the Margao railway station, so you can easily go on an excursion to nearby cities. The way from the airport to the hotel will not be too tiring, because the road will take you no more than half an hour.

Description of guest apartments

Resort Longuinhos Beach Resort 3 * (Goa) offers guests to stay in one of the 50 comfortable rooms that can be divided into three subgroups:

  • Standard apartments are compact in size and simple in design. In view of their low cost, we can say that they provide travelers with the necessary minimum, which will help them to relax after intense excursions and swimming in the clear sea. There is a double bed, and there is also the option of placing on a pair of single beds.
  • "Deluxe" - these are quite luxurious rooms that differ in sophisticated interior design and a fairly large space. Like the previous category, the apartments are one-room and are also designed to accommodate two adult guests (the child can be provided with an extra bed). A distinctive feature of these rooms is the presence of a cozy balcony on which you can dry your clothes or enjoy the well-kept courtyard of the hotel and its pool.
  • The most expensive room category for this hotel is the suite. This apartment is very spacious, as it consists of two rooms. The bedroom is small enough, but quite cozy and comfortable to accommodate two adult guests. The room has upholstered furniture, which can serve as an extra bed. there is also a small kitchenette that allows you to cook in your room.




Facilities at the guest apartment

Hotel Longuinhos Beach Resort 3 * (South Goa, Colva) offers its guests to take advantage of a number of amenities and technical facilities in the rooms. It is worth highlighting the following points:

  • a cozy balcony with garden furniture from which you can admire the garden, pool or beach (there is also a rope for drying bathing suits and towels);
  • You can pass the time watching satellite channels if inclement weather makes you stay in your room;
  • so that you do not feel the Indian heat in your apartments, you are given the opportunity to use individual air conditioning;
  • To contact the reception or make an international call, the apartment has a telephone;
  • depending on which category of room you choose, you will have a bathtub or shower in the bathroom, as well as bath accessories, cosmetics and a hairdryer.




Positive reviews

Hotel Longuinhos Beach Resort 3 * is one of the most successful options if you decide to go to South Goa. Reviews of tourists contain a huge number of comments on which you can make a completely positive impression about the hotel:

  • despite the fact that the hotel is quite old, all the plumbing and electronics in the room are in good condition;
  • the resort hotel is located on the first coastline, and therefore it takes literally a couple of minutes to go to the sea (also the advantage is that you can enjoy beautiful scenery from the hotel windows);
  • in the rooms, corridors and throughout the resort complex is very clean and quite quiet.




Negative reviews

In addition to the positive aspects, there are also a number of shortcomings in the Longuinhos Beach Resort 3 * hotel. Traveler reviews include a number of comments on this resort:

  • the furniture in the room is very old and shabby, and there are stains on the walls, so it would not hurt to make at least cosmetic repairs;
  • despite the fact that the apartment has a large closet, it does not have shelves or hangers (how to put things there is not entirely clear);
  • in most rooms (with the exception of the most expensive apartments) there are no refrigerators, and therefore it is not possible to buy food for future use (apparently this was done with the aim of lodging guests in restaurants);
  • there is no safe in the apartment, so you have to use a paid cell, which is provided at the reception;
  • breakfasts are very scarce and tasteless, besides seasoned with an incredibly huge amount of hot spices.

Be that as it may, the Longuinhos Beach Resort 3 * Resort is a great place for unassuming tourists who want to plunge enough in the gentle sea, as well as get to know Indian culture and many attractions.
