Climate, geography

Guests Al Qidra Hotel Aqaba 3 * (Jordan, Aqaba) in their reviews speak very well about the climate of the resort. Indeed, the mountains do not pass to the coastal patch of land both sultry desert air and seasonal cold winds.

It is not surprising that in this climate a resort holiday on the Jordanian coast lasts a whole year. This is the most acceptable season for Europeans to relax. However, there are even more tourists in Aqaba in the high season: from May to September, when even air conditioners can not cope with the heat in hotel rooms, and the air temperature for months is rarely below 33 0 C. Location

A small four-story hotel Al Qidra Hotel Aqaba 3 * (Jordan), containing 43 hotel rooms, was built in the center of Aqaba, on Alsaada Street, on the second line. From it to the beach 7-8 minutes walk. Newly arrived tourists usually arrive at the hotel after landing at the airport named after King Hussein and located 10 kilometers away.





Within walking distance from Al Qidra Hotel Aqaba there are the main attractions of the city: the picturesque white-white mosque of Hussein Bin-Ali, excavations of the ancient Roman city of Aipa, Al-Hammah-matal boulevard, Akkaba museum, the second highest flagpole in the world (130 m) with the flag of the Great Arab revolution, a local, very remarkable and comfortable city hammam, the ruins of an ancient church.

About hotel

Externally, the hotel building looks awkward, but quite worthy. It is universal: in it the hotel itself is thoughtfully adjacent to shops, social and cultural institutions. The first floor has a ceiling height of one and a half, which makes it possible to create a stylish design in the reception area, other public places and in the hotel restaurant.

The interior design of the hotel can perhaps be called classic. The reception area was finished in combination: a spectacular, moderately presented decorative masonry of stone walls is adjacent to high-quality plaster. Warm pastel colors: yellow and orange prevail. The decoration is a lot of glass. The floor is mostly light, tiled, with harmoniously alternating simple patterns.

The design of the hotel rooms is a natural extension of the lobby's color scheme. The furniture is new, the plumbing is solid.

Infrastructure

The hotel complex Al Qidra Hotel Aqaba 3 * (Aqaba), renting rooms to its customers, has a pretty decent infrastructure. Guests are served in the hotel restaurant. The price of most tours includes "breakfast only", however, some guests book (with extra charge) half board.

The hotel has an outdoor pool with loungers and umbrellas. The hotel offers a hotel diving center, hairdresser, laundry, parking and car rental, tour desk, doctor's services, currency exchange.

Nutrition

In fairness, it is worth noting: the restaurant of the hotel Al Qidra Hotel Aqaba 3 * is one of the best in the district. And they cook there deliciously and adapted to the gastronomic tastes of Western tourists. However, as mentioned above, only breakfast is included in the tour from the hotel. Why is that? Yes, because even the best chef is unable to compete with the oversaturated market of restaurant establishments.





In addition, the prices of tens and hundreds of local restaurants and cafes are quite suitable for daily meals, their menus are quite diverse. In a word, a foodie, having flown to Jordan, will see a real gastronomic paradise.

Therefore, we ask ourselves a more pragmatic question: what should a tourist taste in Jordan from local cuisine? The answer may be quite specific:

  • side dish hummus (flavored chickpeas puree);

Do not neglect local coffee. It is amazing here with the addition of cardamom.
