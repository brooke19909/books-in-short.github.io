Hotel Tropicana Rosetta Jasmine Club (Egypt, Sharm El Sheikh): photos and reviews of tourists

Hotel Tropicana Rosetta Jasmine Club is a popular hotel complex that amazes travelers with its architecture and picturesque grounds.





Description of the hotel complex

Hotel Tropicana Rosetta Jasmine Club is a modern four-star resort hotel, which has everything that a modern traveler can dream of. This is a beautiful territory, and comfortable rooms, as well as a wide range of services that are aimed not only at ensuring that you can have a good rest, but also at getting you vivid impressions.

Hotel Tropicana Rosetta Jasmine Club boasts a developed resort infrastructure that turns the hotel into a small tourist town. There is parking, a bank office, a wellness center and many more facilities that will make your stay in the hotel incredibly comfortable.

The most important moment of relaxation is food. Hotel Tropicana Rosetta Jasmine Club boasts a high level of skill of its chefs who will delight you with the most varied and most exquisite dishes. If the buffet is not enough for you, then the hotel restaurants and bars will hospitably open their doors to you.





Where is the hotel situated

Tropicana Rosetta Jasmine Club 4 * is located near Naama Bay, which is one of the most popular areas in the vicinity of Sharm El Sheikh. It is approximately 14 km from the nearest international airport.

It is worth noting that the hotel is located about 400 meters from the beach, which corresponds to the second sea line. Nevertheless, this drawback can be compensated by the fact that a small tourist shuttle runs regularly to the coast and back.

Hotel Rooms

Tropicana Rosetta Jasmine Club 4 * has 358 double rooms, each of which can be classified as a standard category. They all have a bright and unusual design, as well as a large amount of space and light.

In all apartments, guests have the opportunity to use the following amenities and technical facilities:

  • the bathroom of all rooms is equipped with a shower or bathtub, as well as an electric hairdryer;
  • almost all apartments have a balcony, with the exception of those located on the ground floor (they have a spacious terrace, access to which is through glass sliding doors);
  • cable TV with Russian channels will not let you get bored while you are in your room;
  • using your phone you can not only call the reception, but also contact your friends and relatives who are in other countries;
  • create a comfortable microclimate in your room will help individual air conditioning;
  • You can store food bought in stores or brought from a restaurant in the refrigerator;
  • the minibar will become available to you after making the appropriate payment amount;
  • since there are some stereotypes about theft in Egyptian hotels, the safe installed in the room will help to eliminate all doubts.
