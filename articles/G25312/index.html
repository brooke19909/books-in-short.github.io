Bora Bora - islands of marvelous beauty

The island of Bora Bora (photo emphasizes its natural beauty) is known to the world. The proximity to the Pacific Ocean has turned it into a convenient place for conducting trade relations and comfortable relaxation, which attracts tourists from many parts of the globe. Now officially Bora Bora is included in the territory of the Republic of France . The islands, which are located nearby with the main one, call it satellites. With the oceanic space it is shared by small islands on which people do not live. Until the fifth century AD, people did not know about the existence of Bora Bora, but overpopulation in the Marquesas islands forced travelers to find and open it in order to have the opportunity to resettle the Polynesians, for whom the territory of their homeland was too small.





Until the eighteenth century, a tribal tribal system existed on Bora Bora, which was destroyed after the arrival of the royal authority of Tahiti. Since the eighties of the nineteenth century, Bora Bora (islands) became a colony of France. It should be noted that in general there are not too many historical events related to Bora Bora. At the end of hostilities, Polynesia was recognized as the Overseas part of the state of France, since that time the inhabitants became known as the French. The name of the main island in translation means "Coming from the world of the Gods." The Motu Islands are as if scattered on the sides around it, one of them has its own airport, the other small islands are famous for excellent hotels.

Bora Bora - the islands that are incredibly popular today, because there are conditions for the most comfortable stay. Unbelievable beauty of exotic nature, majestic mountain peaks, numerous temples created by the hands of people in the old days - affordable pleasures for tourists. To get to the village where the ancient leaders lived, you should start at Waitap and head towards the northern cape. Following the further route, you can find yourself in areas where the remains of the naval military base, which was built by the Americans for military operations with Japan, are located.



The picturesque and mysterious Bora Bora are islands that it is quite possible to cross by means of a road that was once considered military. An incredible experience can be obtained while traveling by car on the roads of the area. At the same time, an unforgettable view of the picturesque land opens, fragrant in the greenery of amazing plants. For tourists, interesting excursions were created: Coral Garden, feeding sharks and stingrays. In order to immerse yourself in the pristine atmosphere of the islands, you need to visit one of Motu.



Polynesians cook food from certain types of plants, know how to cleverly break coconuts and wear pareo. The magnificent islands of Bora Bora impress with their originality, traditional customs. Particularly interesting are the dances of the Polynesians.

If you dive in Tupitupiti, you can meet with a shark and other interesting marine fish and animals. Comfortable accommodation in a furnished bungalow on the lagoon leaves an unforgettable experience. Here you can fully gain strength, health and quality rest.
