How to upload music to 4 iPhones (iPhone 4)?

Sooner or later, any owner of a cell phone decides to upload audio files to their mobile device to listen to music or to set their favorite melody as a ringtone. It would seem that this is complicated? If this procedure is carried out with a regular mobile phone or even a smartphone of a manufacturer other than Apple, there will be no difficulties. However, the situation becomes completely different when it comes to the iPhone. How to upload music to 4 iPhones and what special programs should I use? Answers to these and other questions will be given in the article below.

ITunes app brief description

The main function of the iTunes program is the transfer of content, including music, from a personal computer to a smartphone. This app is developed by Apple. The official website of the manufacturer allows you to download the program for free.



ITunes is compatible with any iPhone, regardless of model. ITunes Library works with a variety of music and video file formats. Its contents can be transferred to the smartphone as a whole, or downloading only selected files. Below is a step-by-step description of how to download music to your iPhone 4s using iTunes.

Errors while downloading music to iPhone using iTunes

You already know how to download music to 4 iPhones using iTunes. However, during the implementation of this process errors may occur that will prevent you from achieving the desired result.

If after synchronization the audio files did not appear on the iPhone, it is recommended to check their format. Apple's smartphone supports AAC or MP3 extensions. The first option should be preferred, because audio files of this format are lighter and better.



Another common mistake might be using the old version of iTunes. To solve this problem, you just need to update the application. After that, it is recommended to repeat moving the music content again to achieve the desired result.

Setting a ringtone on a 4 iPhone

How to put music on iPhone 4 as a call? To do this, proceed as follows:

  1. Open the "Settings" section in the iPhone.
  2. Scroll down and open Sounds.
  3. In the menu that opens, select the "Ringtone" item.
  4. A window will appear in which the necessary file with the M4R extension will be visible. To set it on a call, you just need to select this music in the list.
