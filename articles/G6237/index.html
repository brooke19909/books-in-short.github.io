Anapa - Krasnodar: what is the best way to get from one city to another?

The Anapa-Krasnodar route can be overcome in several ways. The fastest is, of course, to use your own car. The most common is to buy a bus ticket. And less popular is to travel by train. You should consider each of them and find out which is the most acceptable both in price and in the time spent on the path.





The faster the better

This opinion is shared by most travelers. As a matter of fact, Anapa - Krasnodar is not such a long route. Between the cities there are only 160 kilometers, and this is really a trifle, given that they are in the same region. At an average speed of 80 km / h, you can cover the distance in just two hours.

This is not to say that this is a particularly beneficial way to travel. But it all depends on how much gas a car needs per hundred and what kind of fuel the owner of his iron friend is fueling. Suppose if a car takes 8 liters of gasoline per 100 kilometers, it will take about 13 all the way. If the fuel is cheap (30 rubles each), then the trip will take 390 rubles. In the event that the car spends 11 liters per hundred, it will be necessary to refuel at 18. At a price of 35 rubles per liter, 630 rubles will go all the way. Shuttle Buses

Well, there is another way to overcome the route Krasnodar - Anapa. Bus - it is the most popular and least expensive. A ticket for it costs about 270 rubles. Flights run regularly - the very first leaves from Anapa at five in the morning, and the latest at 19:00. Buses depart every hour.

In this way, most people overcome the route Krasnodar - Anapa. The distance between these cities by bus can be reached in 3.5 hours. This is slower than using your own car, but you need to remember that the minibus driver also stops in some localities where he is waiting for other passengers. In addition, the bus has another advantage that many people care about is its price.
