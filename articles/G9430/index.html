Pioneer DEH-X5600BT - model review, customer reviews and experts

Pioneer DEH-X5600BT is a bright representative of good sound among the numerous radio tape recorders of the company. Many experts note that, despite the small noise in the upper frequencies, the overall sound picture that the device gives out looks quite dense, voluminous and saturated, which the segment rivals and even some analogues of existing Pioneer models cannot boast of.





So, the hero of today's review is the Pioneer DEH-X5600BT car radio. Consider the positive and negative sides of the device, taking into account the opinions of experts and reviews of ordinary users.

The device has distinctive design features that are characteristic of almost all the lines of the company. The model looks a bit stricter than its counterparts - a sort of business version of the radio. Sound

Over its centuries-old history, the brand has established itself as a reliable manufacturer of high-quality audio equipment, because the consumer wants to receive precisely high-quality sound from the radio. On test stands, the device showed very good results, exceeding the reference indicators by one and a half times.

During listening, a clear saturation of the middle frequencies is felt, and due to this, the overall sound picture looks more complete, and the instruments and vocals sound quite collected and tight. The sound volume becomes fully saturated due to well-perceptible bass, but the transparency and frequency of the sound seem a bit blurry due to the higher frequencies, plus a little noise is heard at high volume.

The holistic sound picture produced by the Pioneer DEH-X5600BT looks pleasant, and long listening to music on the radio does not tire and does not cause headaches.

Ergonomics

The model has standard sizes for single-unit devices (1 DIN) - 178 by 50 mm and does not stand out from any other design distinctive feature among other models.





The front panel of the radio, as mentioned above, has a strict and at the same time stylish design, and the controls located around the perimeter are competently highlighted from an ergonomic point of view.

The front panel, when you click on the button in the lower right corner, can be freely removed and inserted with the same ease into place. It is worth noting that when you remove the panel, it will not ‚Äúshoot‚Äù forward and will not be on the floor mat, as it is securely fixed with special brackets. As for the backlight Pioneer DEH-X5600BT, it is made at a very high level. When you turn on the radio, a smooth change in color scheme fascinates and delights. According to the product specification, the device has 210,000 customizable colors, which is more than enough even for advanced gourmets.





The only thing that users note in their reviews as a defect in the model is the dim illumination of the Eject button, which stands out for its nondescriptness against the background of brightly glowing controls.

Among the other advantages of the Pioneer DEH-X5600BT (a review showed) is that the button stroke, when compared with similar models, is clearly better: soft, pleasant and supple, this can be felt especially when working with the lower control row.

The volume of the music is controlled by a convenient rotary encoder, although some users complain about the slippery area. With its functionality, it will help you navigate in memory cards: folders, files and subsequent selection.

To work with the equalizer, a special button on the control panel is provided. The scheme will offer you seven ready-made modes, and each preset can be manually configured for individual ranges.

Radio tuning is done through the navigation keys, which are also responsible for managing the tracks.

Ports and Connectivity

In the Pioneer DEH-X5600BT model, the connection scheme looks quite traditional and does not stand out for anything special. The device is equipped with an antenna output, a standard ISO-type connector, RCA speaker cables, a microphone input, a separate connector responsible for synchronization with the steering wheel control. You can mount the radio without the involvement of specialists in a few simple steps. This is another distinguishing feature of the Pioneer DEH-X5600BT.

Screen

The model is equipped with a single-line type monochrome display, due to which the viewing angle is unimportant in principle. The brightness at different angles practically does not change, remaining saturated and visually pleasing, and the information displayed on the display is completely readable.

There are no negative nuances in the user reviews, the same can be said about the opinions of experts - it works, it shows - there are no problems.

Additional features

In addition to the radio, CDs and memory cards, the gadget can receive sound from external media through USB and Aux`In-ports. The Bluetooth protocol is provided as a wireless module in the radio, through which you can easily connect both a smartphone and any other device that meets the module's audio standards.

At the stand, the synergy of the DEH-X5600BT and iPhone ‚Äùof the fifth version was tested. There were no problems: the devices immediately found each other, and after synchronization the radio tape recorder showed a list of missed, completed, and received calls. The only inconvenient moment, judging by the user reviews, was the processes of answering a call or rejecting it. Since it is necessary to hold this button to reject a call, the probability of answering a call is high, underestimating the element due to shaking the car.
