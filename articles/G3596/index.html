Powders for dishwashers - what is it ?!



Powders for dishwashers are bulk solids specifically designed to effectively remove all kinds of contaminants from dishes. Such funds, as a rule, have pronounced antibacterial properties, do not leave spots and stains. Good powders for dishwashers are also able to quickly and easily cope with any kind of pollution (including old ones). In addition, high-quality products protect machine parts from scale formation and, thus, extend their service life.

Powders for dishwashers: composition

Such products are produced, as a rule, in the form of tiny granules, which are very convenient for use. The composition of dishwasher powders traditionally includes chlorine, phosphates, bleach, dye and a number of other chemical elements. Such as, for example, sodium bicarbonate, sodium citrate, sodium sulfate (pH of the so-called neutral salt), sodium disilicate, enzymes, sodium carbonate and polypeptides.





In addition, among the most common ingredients used in the manufacture of dishwashing powders, one can distinguish a bleaching activator, a variety of surface-active elements, derivatives of sugars and alcohol. However, not all manufacturing companies use all of these substances in their products. In this case, it should be emphasized that over the past few years there has been a clear tendency to use exclusively environmentally friendly and natural substitutes for the above components in the production process. At the same time, the best powder for dishwashers will definitely not include sodium alkylbenzene sulfate, which is very harmful to the environment.

The main advantages of powders for dishwashers

The use of these funds will allow you to easily remove any, the most severe spots, as well as eliminate stains and unpleasant odors. Features of the use of dishwashing powders

People who constantly use powders for dishwashers, experts advise not to forget a few simple rules. Firstly, before you start using these tools, you should carefully read the instructions of the manufacturer contained in the instructions. Secondly, if there is hard water in the house , as a rule, it is recommended to pour powder into all compartments of the machine. Thirdly, do not use these products to wash dishes made of silver, porcelain, as well as products that have bone or wooden handles.
