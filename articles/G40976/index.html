Marmaris hotels - European level of service at the level of world standards.

At the confluence of the Aegean and the Mediterranean Sea, at the foot of the mountains, the slopes of which are covered with picturesque forests, one of the world's largest harbors, full of blue lagoons with warm clear water and a large number of small islands, among which in the past used to hide pirate ships. The name of this beautiful place is Marmaris Bay, where the city of the same name and resort are located. Nowadays, the Turkish resort of Marmaris is a famous holiday destination for tourists from the UK, Holland and Germany, and other countries where yacht racing is practiced. The fact is that the city of Marmaris is the capital of the annual May sailing regatta, which has the status of International. This swim has been held on the most elegant and beautiful yachts in the world for more than a dozen years and attracts a huge number of tourists from all over the world who want to admire these beauties with their own eyes. At their service are luxury Marmaris hotels that can satisfy the most demanding and refined tastes. Marmaris resort is among the top twenty vacation spots with the highest hotel density in relation to the area. The diverse hotels of Marmaris, from 3 to 5 star rank, are scattered here at every step. There are enough customers for everyone, because thanks to the warm tropical and subtropical climate, the holiday season in this place has a year-round duration. Especially popular among tourists are Marmaris hotels of the HV type (Holidey Villag), consisting of secluded bungalows scattered in separate corners of cool pine forests. Forest lands in some places go down to the sea itself, separated from the water by only a narrow strip of sandy beaches. Thanks to this, leaving the bungalow, which giant pines keep in the shade with their crowns, you can already be exposed to the sun in 5 minutes walk and, plunging into the warm waves of the sea, bury in the hot beach sand. So, for example, hotel HV-1 class of Grand Yazici Marmaris Palace (Grand Yaziki Marmaris Pellas) , corresponding to the level of 5 stars.
