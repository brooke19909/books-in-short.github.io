Process

Remember that an octopus has 8 legs. Therefore, the first thing to do is fill three pairs of tights with filler. Do not tamper too tightly, firstly, it will take a lot of material, and secondly, the tentacles will become too heavy and will pull the structure down.

We cut out two large circles from white fabric, and two smaller circles from black. If there was no tissue at hand, then you can simply draw and cut eyes out of paper. Attach the eyes to the cap.

If you have time and desire, you can decorate with ribbons, buttons or other things that have been lying around for a long time.
