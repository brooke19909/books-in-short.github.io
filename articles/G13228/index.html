Looking for where to relax in December? Read the answer in the article.

Many people wonder where to rest in December, not knowing whether to choose a warm resort or a ski resort that is traditional for this time. Therefore, in this article we will consider both options, and each will already for himself choose what he likes. However, it is worth noting right away that it is not the place you are going to go to that is important, but your surroundings. And if you think about the trip in advance, then it will be more than successful.



So, where to relax in December, if you want to escape from the Russian winter and plunge into the new summer? Countries located near the equator, as well as in the Southern Hemisphere, are at your disposal. It will be warmest on the shores of the Caribbean Sea, in Mexico, Guatemala or in the Dominican Republic. These countries are the most attractive for most tourists due to their national color and accessibility. Here, the traditions of European peoples, African and local, long dissolved in the Aztec settlers, uniquely blended. Local cuisine will also delight any tourist with its variety. In restaurants you can taste the most delicate delicacy, as well as ordinary beer snacks.



Also, if you are looking for hot places where you can relax in December, the Atlantic coast of South America is ideal. The metropolis of Rio de Janeiro with its Copacabana promenade is popular. During the day there is a whole swarm of tourists, and in the evening all the beaches are immersed in the neon light of entertainment. It is in December here that the air temperature rises above 30 degrees, and the water in the ocean warms up to 27-29. Also, on Christmas Eve, a famous carnival is held in Rio, which should be attended by everyone who came to Brazil at this time.



Fans of the warm sea often hesitate where it is better to relax in December - on the islands or on the southern coasts. It is worth noting that in island states, in principle, there are no cold temperatures. All of them are surrounded by coral reefs that do not allow cold currents to reach the lands. Therefore, the islands are an ideal vacation for lovers of the eternal paradise summer. But lovers to learn new things are more likely to go to countries such as India, China, Thailand. There is a warm sea, shopping, and historical treasures that can be endlessly examined.

Also looking for where to relax in December, and lovers of winter holidays. In most of Russia, frosts are still unstable at this time, so European ski resorts are provided to the attention of a domestic traveler. On Christmas Eve, many go to Finland to learn ski art in the resort of Levi. It is equipped with excellent infrastructure and has a large hotel complex, which consists of rooms and individual cottages, as well as many national restaurants. Here you can taste dishes from reindeer, partridges and other inhabitants of the Arctic Circle.

To understand where it is good to rest in December, in fact, you can only travel through a lot of countries. But while we are still at home, it is worth noting that Venice has a special charm this month. It is very cool, but there are no frosts, as well as a huge number of tourists. Therefore, in the silence and tranquility that are so unusual for this city, you can ride a gondola, visit the Palace of the Rains and visit the local carnival of masks.
