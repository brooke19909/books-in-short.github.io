Anomalous zones. The logic of the unusual

On the surface of planet Earth there are many areas that cause not only a lot of questions among ordinary people, but also put scientists in a difficult position. While human rumor ascribes to these sites the title of black spots, the concept of "anomalous zone" has been strengthened among researchers, journalists, and even some natural scientists. The general similarity of the parts of the earth's surface that fall under this definition is that for a long time various unexplained and mysterious phenomena occur on their territory that do not agree with the conclusions of official science or are uncharacteristic of this area. Anomalous zones of the Earth have such ominous nicknames as damned or damn places. Sensible people avoid them; for adventurers and researchers, they become objects of increased attention. Birds and animals bypass these places, even the vegetation in such areas is very different from endemic.

Anomalous zones have common features, by which it is already possible to rank one or another part of the terrain among them. First of all, in an unusual area, if not a violation of the course of time, then its other perception is observed. The influence of zones on emotional natures can provoke a feeling of sadness, fear, panic in the latter . Prolonged stay in such a zone can cause poor health, weakness, and weakness.

The largest anomalous zones of the world are the so-called devil's belt. Five places that have not received a very good reputation and, accordingly, ominous nicknames, have an equal distance from each other and are located along the thirtieth degree of northern latitude. Bermuda Triangle, Gibraltar Wedge, Afghan Anomalous Zone, Devil's Sea, Hawaiian Anomaly are notorious. Among the last signs characterizing the anomalous zones is the ufological activity in their area.

The vertices and centers of the edges of these figures are the so-called nodes. Among them, a certain hierarchy is observed. There is an energy theory according to which the earth's surface is covered with a grid with cell sizes of several meters. Its effect on biological organisms can be both positive and negative.
