What sewing machine to buy for home needlework

Have you moved to a new apartment or did you have a baby? Often, such changes entail other issues. Sometimes you want to hem the curtains or tablecloth yourself, make diapers, repair baby clothes, sew your favorite beautiful sundress for the summer. This is not possible without good equipment. So which sewing machine to buy for home use?



First, decide what you will do on it. If you are just an amateur and will sometimes flash a couple of straight lines, then you do not need a super modern computerized machine. It is enough to choose a simple sewing machine within 5,000 rubles.

In addition, it is worth paying attention to the shuttle for the lower thread. In the simplest models, it is located vertically. This does not affect the quality of the stitch, only you cannot control when the thread ends and the sewing machine is noisier than its counterparts with a horizontal bobbin. But if you need to sew a towel or a light dress, then such a model will be enough.

But if you want the machine, in addition to the smallest set of lines, to also perform, for example, a loop? So, what to buy a sewing machine for a more experienced craftswoman? The price range is highly dependent on the set of actions that the sewing machine can perform. The more operations available, the more expensive. Therefore, before buying, be sure to decide what lines you can not do without.



Often the cause of the breakdown of the sewing machine is the mismatch of the selected needle and thread with the type of fabric that they started to sew. Remember that most household sewing machines are not intended for sewing thick fabrics such as leather, denim , and drapes. You will sew a coat, change locks in a jacket, then choose which sewing machine to buy, given its specialization in fabrics. Replacement of the needle with a thicker one should be provided, and plastic parts should not be used in the mechanism of such a machine.

A specialized store will help you: which sewing machine is better to choose. However, you must clearly understand what you need it for. Most models in the price range from 5,000 rubles to 10,000 rubles can already bend the bottom of the trousers, process the seam with a zigzag hem and hem, but still it will not be a full-length overcast seam. Only an overlock can make such a seam. But this type of car is not cheap. Most ordinary sewing lovers rarely need an overlock machine. What sewing machine to buy: with an expanded list of overcast seams or a specialized overlock machine - do I need to spend more than 7000 rubles extra for a couple of lines per year?



More expensive models of sewing machines have a screen that shows all kinds of lines that it can sew, including a fairly large number of embroidery strings, as well as errors that occur during operation. In addition, there are models that use a special cord to connect to a computer, which opens up great opportunities for users to create their own stitches and embroideries. However, such models are very expensive.

So what kind of sewing machine to buy depends entirely on your financial capabilities, desires for the capabilities of the machine and your requirements for it. First decide on a price range, then choose the best that suits you. Create, please yourself and your loved ones with your sewing products.
