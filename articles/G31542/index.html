Kazan, hotel "Chaliapin": description, rating, reviews

If on business issues, or as part of a tourist visit, there is a need to visit Kazan, the Chaliapin Hotel can be safely considered as a hotel in which it is better to stay. Today, the capital of Tatarstan has a fairly developed infrastructure with an expanded hotel industry. Kazan hotels can offer a different level of service and a variety of services. Prices in them can also vary from quite affordable (budget option) to very high (luxury rooms).

Features of choosing a hotel in the capital of Tatarstan

Kazan hotels, as in most modern cities, offer a fairly wide selection of rooms at various prices. If this is your first trip to this city, it is recommended that guests make a reservation in advance to avoid unpleasant surprises for visitors.



During the year, large and developed cities often become a place for tourists to visit. Basically, they host business meetings, forums, conferences and other events. A sufficiently developed million-plus city is Kazan. The Chaliapin Hotel is ideal for negotiations and business meetings, as it is located in the very center of the capital. Such a location will appeal not only to ordinary tourists, but also to those who visit the city solely for business purposes.

Naturally, if there is a desire to stay in a deluxe room, this amount will be much higher. Kazan: Chaliapin Hotel

This 4-star hotel is located on Universitetskaya Street, in the very center of the city, not far from the Ring Shopping Center. Next to this hotel are the legendary central city clock, in which lovers often make first dates.



The Chaliapin Palace Hotel compares favorably with its location, as it is in close proximity to attractions such as:

  • Kazan Cathedral of the Epiphany;
  • Museum of Socialist Life;
  • complex of buildings of Kazan University (KFU).

Such proximity of these places with the hotel will help tourists save money on travel, since all these attractions can be reached on foot from the Chaliapin Hotel.

In addition, close to the hotel are the best cafes and restaurants in Kazan. Nearby you can find such famous institutions as:

  • "Tavern Mlyn";
  • "Draft";
  • "House of pancakes."

Rooms and services provided by the hotel

The Chaliapin Hotel can become a rather convenient place both for a single stay and for living with the whole family. Over the past decade, Kazan has been intensively creating the image of a developed city that is able to provide high quality services at an affordable price. The Chaliapin Hotel, deservedly awarded 4 stars, is an institution that fully meets the requirements of such an urban strategy.



The institution rents 123 rooms, which differ in their categories. Depending on the needs and wishes, the hotel can be rented:

  • Standart room;
  • Studio
  • suite;
  • apartments;
  • presidential suite.

Accordingly, the price of the room will depend on the selected category. On average, for the Standard category it will be necessary to pay from 3500-3900 p. per day. The presidential suite will cost about 26 thousand rubles.

Additional hotel services

Hotel "Chaliapin Palace Hotel" offers its customers not only comfortable rooms. On the territory of the hotel you can take advantage of additional services, including:

  • Spa;
  • sauna;
  • pool and gym;
  • Chapel Restaurant and Piano Bar, located on site.

The restaurant has a capacity of 100 seats, there is also a Vip zone with a closed room for 25 people. Depending on the wishes and personal preferences, visitors can be offered dishes of Tatar, Russian and European cuisines. The "Piano" bar on site is open around the clock.



For various business meetings and negotiations, the hotel has 4 conference rooms that can accommodate up to 130 people. For free use, they provide:

  • stationery;
  • sound equipment;
  • projectors and video screens;
  • flipcharts.

Customer reviews

As a rule, for those who visited Kazan, the Chaliapin Hotel makes a good impression. Most reviews of your stay at this hotel are positive. The courtesy of the staff and the ability to solve any problems encountered within a few minutes (if, for example, a second blanket is required) are noted. Clients of this establishment speak of perfect cleanliness both in the rooms for living and in all other rooms in the hotel. Also, many note a very high quality daily cleaning.

Hotel Disadvantages

Hotel "Chaliapin", whose reviews about the interior are positive, successfully combines the attributes of national decor and modern technology. But on some information resources you can see information that the last major overhaul of the hotel was carried out in 2009. Although all the rooms and rooms of the hotel are in almost perfect condition, some customers believe that the interior can already be updated.



The hotel is almost always fully loaded with residents, but despite this, many say that there is complete silence in the living rooms and the sounds from the neighboring rooms of the residents do not bother. The situation is slightly different with the rooms whose windows face Bauman Street (the central pedestrian street of Kazan). These rooms can be quite noisy, and at night. Those traveling to a hotel with young children are advised to choose rooms with windows facing the hotel courtyard.

Parents traveling to Kazan with their kids should pay attention to another factor. If the trip is planned for the winter, then, according to numerous reviews, it is quite possible to encounter cold in the room, especially for the attic rooms on the upper floors. Customers who managed to visit the hotel in the winter of 2016 say that they did not see the promised heaters, they were offered only additional blankets.

Another disadvantage of the hotel service is the inspection of things. As part of the fight against terrorism, hotel guards may ask you to open a suitcase and check its contents. If a person is not ready for such a demonstration of the contents, in order to avoid unpleasant situations, he should initially choose another hotel in Kazan.

Advantages of the Chaliapin Hotel

Throughout the hotel there is the possibility of using wireless Internet for free. Also free for regular customers is a visit to the pool and gym. On the territory there is quite beautiful, judging by the reviews of the residents, a winter garden, where it is possible to conduct free photo sessions. For pet owners, a pleasant fact may be the opportunity to check into a hotel with their pet. Pets are not allowed in the hotel.



Regardless of the category of the selected room, all residents are given the opportunity to use the following things at no extra charge:

  • hair dryer and bath accessories;
  • telephone;
  • air conditioning;
  • TV with cable channels;
  • slippers and bathrobes.

The room rate also includes a free breakfast, which is held in the form of a buffet in the morning (from 7:00 to 11:00).
