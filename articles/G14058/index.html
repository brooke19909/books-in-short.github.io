Northern islands of Nha Trang: tour, reviews. Monkey Island Orchid Island

The most popular Vietnamese resort among Russians is Nha Trang, where you can relax and have fun, without experiencing any special problems with communication. Russian language for local residents is understandable.

Especially popular with most tourists are excursions to the northern islands of Nha Trang (reviews of them are presented in the article) located in Nyafu Bay. These places attract not only wonderful animals and plants; tourism infrastructure is well developed on their territory.

Briefly about rest in Nha Trang

Vietnam has recently gained a reputation as a fashionable and popular tourist destination. And rest and excursions in this country, especially in Nha Trang, are already a symbol of a successful vacation.

In fact, the beach season in this country is almost all year round, not counting the rainy season. There are wonderful bays, modern hotels and restaurants for every taste and budget, fabulous picturesque islands scattered along the entire coast. Thanks to all this, the tourist destination Moscow-Nha Trang has become very popular with Russians in recent years.

South Islands:

  • Hon Mieu - the island is not for swimming, but there is a picturesque park and oceanarium;
  • Hong Moon - a paradise for diving;

Island Tours

This is one of the most popular routes at local travel agencies. This tour costs about 25 dollars. Her program includes visits to two islands of Nha Trang: Monkey Island and Orchid (or Elephant Island).

An excursion as part of a group is cheaper (taken from the hotel, taken to the islands and fed). A small minus is a too large group (14 or more people). You can independently reach the islands. To do this, drive to the northern port and buy a boat ticket to any island.

Orchid Island Attractions

He is the most developed and largest. A trip to one of the northern islands of Nha Trang is especially pleasant for families with children. She leaves a lot of vivid and unforgettable impressions. The main objects of the island:

  • beach;
  • ostrich and deer farm;
  • circus with elephants and bears;
  • orchid park;
  • waterfall;
  • stone sculpture garden.

Entertainment and activities

On Nha Trang Orchid Island there is a beautifully equipped beach with a secure gentle entrance, with sunbeds and gazebos. It is covered with pleasant fine sand. Pets of deer and ostrich farms walk freely throughout the territory. Visitors are allowed to feed and pet them. You can ride on ostriches.

Daily in a local small circus, performances are held with elephants and bears. The main character is the elephant Lena, familiar to many tourists. This good-natured animal gladly receives treats from visitors to the farm, rides (for a fee) and shakes on the trunk (for treats). Visitors are allowed to take photos with their pets (elephants, bears and pythons), however, for a fee.

On the way to the fabulous waterfall, no less wonderful landscapes open up. Exotic plants, parrots, peacocks and other animals are also found here. In the park by the stream during the flowering period of orchids, it is surprisingly beautiful. The territory becomes like a carpet of buds of various shades and shapes. In the purest water of the stream, thanks to convenient beaches, you can swim and do snorkeling (swimming in a mask with a breathing tube). The garden of stone sculptures, which are figures of animals and various characters of myths and legends, is magnificent on this northern island of Nha Trang. Near them, you can also take a photo.

Description of Monkey Island

There are many islands near the Nha Trang resort, but one of them is a full-fledged habitat of numerous monkeys. It should be noted that primates fell on him not by chance and partly thanks to the Soviet Union. During the Vietnam-Soviet friendship, there was a farm lab, which bred and used monkeys for experiments. After the war, it was closed, but the animals left here were left to their own devices and began to actively breed. The Vietnamese decided to use them as a kind of bait for tourists. So there was an attraction in Nha Trang - the northern Monkey Island.

This small island is only a couple of kilometers from the Vietnamese resort. The monkey reserve is an amazingly beautiful park with well-groomed alleys and thickets of the jungle. There is a small and cozy beach with shallow shores on the island. For a fee, you can rent a gazebo and deck chair, as well as rent water equipment. In the northern part of the island you can also go scuba diving by renting equipment at a dive station.

Funny inhabitants of the island

The main characters of this northern island of Nha Trang are funny monkeys. In extreme heat, they take refuge in the cool of the jungle. You can lure them out of there only by refreshments. Some of them, the smartest and smartest, run up on their own, only a tourist should start looking for something in his bag. There are arrogant ones among them, who can even jump on their shoulders.

It also happens that monkeys take food from visitors to the island, but this is not the worst tragedy. These animals can grab anything: sunglasses, cameras, bags, bags and other items that are unattended. There are a lot of monkeys, but there are people among tourists who leave the island a little disappointed due to the fact that not so many primates paid attention to them. In reality, these animals can be forced to run from the thickets by asking the ranger to feed them (for a fee). They immediately rush.

Circus show

On Monkey Island you can see these funny animals and, on the other hand, at work. There is a local circus here, which takes place several times a day. In addition to monkeys, dogs, bears and elephants participate in it. The show is interesting, but the methods of "training" using a stick can leave visitors with some unpleasant feeling. At the end of the show, small "artists" collect "tips" from guests.

It's nice to take a walk on the island and in a small cozy amusement park, which includes the beach.

In conclusion about the reviews and tips of tourists

It is insanely exciting and interesting to travel to this resort place. This is the opinion of many tourists who have visited the northern islands. The route Moscow - Nha Trang is incredibly long (7730 kilometers). But such a fabulous journey is worth it to see these exotic places with your own eyes. The cost of tickets in this direction is about 20,000-25,000 rubles.

According to tourist reviews, there are a lot of interesting things in Vietnam, especially on the islands. In their reviews, many tourists write important tips on how to behave with local animals.
