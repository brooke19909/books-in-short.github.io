Side Crown Palace 5 * (Turkey, Side): photos and reviews of tourists

For many years Turkey has remained the most popular and sought-after option for holidays with Russian tourists. They are attracted by the low price for accommodation in high-class hotels, brilliant service, excellent climate and the presence of many attractions throughout the country. At the same time, Turkish resorts are often crowded, which negatively affects the quality of service. If you want to relax in a less crowded place, then choose the small town of Side, located between Antalya and Alanya. One of the most luxurious hotels here is considered Side Crown Palace Hotel 5 *. We will tell you more about it in our article.

Hotel location

It is worth noting that the hotel is not located in Side, but in the small village of Kumkey , which is 7 km from the city. The location of the hotel is not the best . The private beach of the complex is 500 meters from the hotel, so it is difficult and long to get to it on foot. However, Side Crown Charm Palace 5 * prepared for its tourists free bus routes to the coast and back to compensate for the inconvenience. 300 meters from the hotel there is a small cafe with Mediterranean cuisine, and 400 meters from a large supermarket where you can buy food, souvenirs, clothes and household chemicals. If you want to explore local attractions, then head to Side. The city can be reached by taxi or bus. There are Roman ruins, an ancient city, museums and a harbor.





There is no private airport in Side, so tourists fly to Antalya, which is 50 km from here. As a rule, hotels organize transfers for tourists. They get to the hotels in comfortable buses with air conditioning and soft drinks, so travel time is pretty fast. It usually takes 2 to 3 hours. If you don‚Äôt like flying, you can buy a bus ticket. The path passes either through Greece and Bulgaria, or through Georgia. From southern Russia you can get to Side by ferry.

Beach and Pool

The Side Crown Palace 5 * complex in Side in Turkey has its own stretch of sandy beach. Its length is about 200 meters. Negative reviews of Side Crown Palace 5 *

But not without flaws. Some tourists felt that for such a price, the hotel service should be better. In their reviews, they indicate the following significant shortcomings that spoiled their impression of rest:

  • the hotel staff not only speaks Russian poorly, but also does not understand English at all;
  • employees refuse to change dirty towels for new ones even at an additional cost;
  • in the evening the hotel smells strongly of sewers, so tourists are forced to sleep with the windows closed;
  • some rooms have ventilation, which is very noisy and interferes with sleep;
  • poor sound insulation in the building - tourists complain that they can clearly hear the voices of neighbors from other rooms.

Should I come to rest in this hotel?

An inexpensive and relaxing holiday offers its guests the resort of Side. Side Crown Palace 5 * is a good option for tourists who prefer a high level of service at affordable prices. Hotel guests believe that the cost of living here is fully consistent with local service. However, it is not very convenient to relax with small children here, because during the day and evening the hotel is too noisy due to the loud entertainment program. Therefore, most often adult tourists and youth companies come here.
