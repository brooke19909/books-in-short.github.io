About hotel

Hotel Gaffinos Beach Resort 2 * (South Goa, Cavelossim) is a budget and comfortable accommodation option on the Indian Ocean. It is ideal for any category of adult tourists regardless of their nationality, oriented, first of all, to a relaxing beach holiday with elements of excursion service.





The hotel, founded in 1996, has been reconstructed several times during its operation and today it is ready to offer guests:

  • comfortable rooms, fully consistent with the stardom of the hotel, which have everything you need for a comfortable stay;
  • a restaurant and bar where you can try the culinary masterpieces of local and international cuisine.

If guests of the 2 * Gaffinos Beach Resort Hotel will need something additional, then the service staff understands perfectly in English and is ready to come to their aid at any time.

Accommodation

Hotel Gaffinos Beach Resort 2 * (South Goa, India) is quite small, with only 16 double rooms with a balcony, located on three floors of a single building. All of them belong to the category of ‚Äústandard‚Äù, as they have the same area (15 sq. M), similar interior decoration and technical equipment. But there is still a difference: in some rooms there is no air conditioning system. This important feature should be taken into account when booking the hotel, since the ceiling fan in the room at high air temperature does not always save from heat.





The room is decorated simply and without frills, fairly modest furniture is installed, satellite TV is available at an additional cost, and there is no refrigerator.

The private bathroom has a shower or bath and toiletries.

Room service is performed daily by the hotel; bed linen, including towels, are changed once a week.

Any hotel includes some services in the price. Their assortment always varies and depends on the number of stars, which, of course, affects the cost of housing in Goa. Hotel Gaffinos Beach Resort has only two stars, for this reason the range of services paid by the client along with the room is very limited:

  • continental breakfasts;
  • swimming pool at the hotel Mobor (150 m);
  • Wi-Fi at the reception (password is entered by the administrator);

Beach

A continuous 30-kilometer chain of picturesque beaches stretches along the coast of southern Goa. It is worthily completed by the amazing Mobor beach, near which the Gaffinos Beach Resort 2 * hotel (India) is located. South Goa is not considered as noisy as its northern part with numerous youth clubs and discos.

The entire coast of Mabor is covered with soft and fine sand of a light yellow or almost white hue. In some places, small cozy bays naturally formed in which you can relax in total solitude.





In the more populated part of the beach, umbrellas and sun loungers are provided for tourists at an additional cost. Free use of a sunbed and an awning from the sun is possible only in coastal cafes (sheks) when buying food and drinks.

Positive guest reviews

Focusing on a small budget trip to the sea, many tourists choose the hotel Gaffinos Beach Resort 2 * (South Goa), reviews about which in recent years are the most positive. Some vacationers who have already been here are even ready to assign the hotel another star to the existing two.

Most comments posted by tourists on the Internet are as follows:

  • Really, a cozy, clean hotel, with a convenient location.
  • The room has everything you need: a large bed, a wardrobe with shelves, a mirror, chairs and a table. Air conditioning, plumbing in working condition. The lack of a TV in the room and the pool on the territory does not affect the quality of rest, since there is a magnificent beach and an emerald warm sea nearby.
  • Beddings and towels, although not new, are spotlessly clean.
  • The restaurant at the hotel has very tasty and reasonably priced dishes.
  • The sea is not far, a tireless walk on a shopping street takes about 15 minutes.
  • Wi-Fi at the reception is free, which is rare for this area.

Cons noted in reviews

There are also negative aspects for which tourists who are sometimes not ready to choose the Gaffinos Beach Resort 2 * hotel for accommodation. Reviews indicate the following small flaws:

  • Over the past three years, several tourists have experienced a blackout of water and electricity. But a positive attitude towards a good rest helped to overcome temporary inconveniences, and the trip was not spoiled.
  • The presence of ants in the room was sometimes inconvenient, but insects, unfortunately, are almost the norm for this area.
  • Some guests lacked communication in Russian with the receptionists at the reception, but this served as an additional incentive to tighten their English.
  • Many tourists did not find shampoos, gels in the bathroom, but only one bar of soap.

Booking a 2 * Gaffinos Beach Resort hotel for a little money, you should not expect to get apartments comparable to a five-star complex.

This hotel is recommended for tourists who travel primarily to enjoy the sun, sea and nature of Goa.
