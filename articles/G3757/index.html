Hotel Fairmont Ajman 5 * (Ajman): photos and reviews of tourists

Ajman is the smallest emirate in the UAE. Its capital is the city of the same name, which is located on the shores of the Persian Gulf. Naturally, it is a fairly popular resort. And this is where Fairmont Ajman 5 * is located, a hotel that is quite popular. And it is necessary to tell about its advantages in as much detail as possible.





Briefly about the hotel

This hotel complex is brand new. After all, they opened it in 2015. Since the UAE is a resort actively visited by tourists, the hotels here are crowded during the holiday season. So the Fairmont Ajman 5 * hotel instantly attracted the attention of visitors.

It is located in a place that is known as a fishing center. And pearls are also mined in Ajman. The main attraction here is the National History Museum, which houses various manuscripts and weapons. Here, an interesting rest is provided to everyone and everyone: both lovers of attractions and those who want to plunge headlong into a beach vacation.

In the immediate vicinity of the hotel there is a beach with pure white sand, sun loungers and umbrellas. Before him - a stone's throw. Still not very far away is a noisy metropolis, known to everyone. And this is Dubai. In 45 minutes you can drive to the shopping center Dubai Mall, which is a real paradise for shoppers. After all, there are more than a thousand all kinds of galleries and boutiques! And in this center there is a zoo, ice rink, cinema, amusement park and much more.

In the immediate vicinity there are many attractions. For example, the local international college of law, the Ajman Chamber of Commerce and Industry, the city museum and much more. Yes, and to the airport of Dubai city from the hotel to go about 30 minutes. However, there is another one, closer. Sharjah Airport is a 15-minute drive away . In general, the location of the hotel, which occupies about 23,000 square meters, is very good.
