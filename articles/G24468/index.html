How to get there

The Omega shopping center is located in Arzamas at the address: Kalinina Street, 46.

In the immediate vicinity of the shopping center there is a public transport stop "-44", where 5 public transport lines stop - buses number 4, 10, 14, 14A, as well as number 9.

For guests using personal vehicles there is a ground parking lot, which is accessed from Kalinina Street.
