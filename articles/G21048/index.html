Magnetic motor: myth or reality.

A magnetic motor is one of the most likely options for a perpetual motion machine. The idea of ‚Äã‚Äãits creation was expressed a very long time ago, but so far it has not been created. There are many devices that take scientists a step or several steps closer to creating this engine, however, none of them have been brought to their logical conclusion, therefore, there is no talk of practical application. There are many myths associated with these devices.

A magnetic motor is not an ordinary unit, since it does not consume any energy. The driving force is only the magnetic properties of the elements. Of course, electric motors also use the magnetic substances of ferromagnets, but the magnets are driven by electric current, which already contradicts the main principle of the perpetual motion machine. In a magnetic motor, the influence of magnets on other objects is activated, under the influence of which they begin to move, rotating the turbine. The prototype of such an engine can be many office accessories in which various balls or planes continuously move. However, batteries ( direct current source) are also used for driving there .





Nikola Tesla was one of the first scientists to seriously engage in the creation of a magnetic motor. Its engine contained a turbine, coil, wires connecting these objects. A small magnet was inserted into the coil so that it captured at least two of its turns. After giving the turbine a slight jolt (spin), it began to move at an incredible speed. This movement will be eternal. Tesla's magnetic motor is almost perfect. Its only drawback is that the turbine must be given its original speed.

The Perendev magnetic motor is another possible option, but it is much more complex. It is a ring of dielectric material (most often wood) with magnets mounted in it, tilted at a certain angle. In the center was another magnet. Such a scheme is also imperfect, because you need a push to start the engine .




The main problem of creating such a perpetual motion machine is the tendency of magnets to constant mechanical movement. Two strong magnets will move until their opposite poles come into contact. Because of this, the magnetic motor cannot work properly. This problem cannot be solved with the modern capabilities of mankind.





The creation of an ideal magnetic motor would lead mankind to a source of eternal energy. In this case, all existing types of power plants could be easily abolished, since a magnetic motor would become not only eternal, but also the cheapest and safest option for generating energy. But one cannot definitely say whether a magnetic motor will be only a source of energy or whether it can be used not only for peaceful purposes. This question significantly changes the situation and makes you think.
