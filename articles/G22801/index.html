"Moscow City". Evolution Tower - The Pearl of the Center

Recently, the modern business center "Moscow City" in the capital has become one of the most prestigious places. Renting areas for doing business here, you can get not only a spacious beautiful room, but also a special status. The most prestigious offices are located in Moscow City. Tower "Evolution" makes it possible to rent premises according to various parameters.





Description

The Evolution tower is one of the most striking elements among all the components of the Moscow City complex. The twisted shape, resembling a DNA helix, was not in vain conceived by the architects. This symbolizes eternity, the evolution of all living things that exist on our planet. Each upper floor is rotated relative to the lower by three degrees - this forms a spiral. The skyscraper makes half a turn around its own axis at the level of 51 floors. It is thanks to this lightness with which the tower rotates that the feeling of infinity of movement, the evolution of consciousness, the search for perfection is created.

The facility was actively built in 2014, and from that time on, it began to attract a large number of curious views of not only residents, but even more guests of the capital. All visitors pay special attention to the center, and a rare businessman would refuse an office in such a chic building.

On the territory of the complex "Moscow City" there are not only business centers, offices. Here are the stores of the most famous brands. The business center has land connections with the facilities of the MIBC - this is the Bagration Bridge, the central core of the complex. It is planned to open its own exit to the metro station.





Rent in a business center

Everyone can rent any premises in the Moscow-City complex, the Evolution tower offers the following areas:

  • The room is free appointment.
  • Cafe in the business center.
  • Restaurant in the business center.
  • Shop in the business center.
  • Office in a business center.

The Evolution Tower is one of several skyscrapers in the Moscow City business center and consists of 53 floors. The roof of the business center where the rental is carried out has a beautiful tower. The multifunctional center is so unusual that it combines what would seem to be something incompatible: next to the business offices are a chic, impressive Wedding Palace, restaurants and banquet rooms. Visitors are simply fascinated by the views of the Moscow City complex. The Evolution Tower, the photo of which every tourist is trying to make, has become a symbol of the business center.

The stylobate part of the center has entertainment complexes, shopping areas, the underground part has a spacious multi-tiered parking. Most of the tower premises are rented as premium real estate: conference rooms, reception halls, meeting rooms. Some floors have a consolidated layout. Tenants are given the opportunity to rent the floor completely.
