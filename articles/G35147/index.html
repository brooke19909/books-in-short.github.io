Best resorts of Adler - photos and reviews of tourists

Adler resorts are rightfully considered the best place to spend a vacation in Russia. And there are many different reasons for this. Adler (the Greater Sochi region) has long been popular with millions of tourists, and for good reason. Ski fun

If we talk about the resorts of Adler, then the first thing you need to talk about Krasnaya Polyana. This is truly a good place to relax. Krasnaya Polyana is a paradise of the Russian corner with which other ski resorts cannot be compared. Adler gained popularity not only thanks to the picturesque embankments and the bustling nightlife, but also thanks to Krasnaya Polyana.

And it was in this place that part of the Olympic and Paralympic Games of the past, 2014 was held. Krasnaya Polyana is located 40 kilometers from the center of Adler - upstream of the river. Mzymta. You can get to the resort in different ways - by car, bus or train "Swallow". Less than an hour will be required to get to this picturesque place. By the way, the village is located at an altitude of 0.55 km above sea level. It is here that you can enjoy the mountain slopes, humid fresh air and high snow cover.

Olympic facilities

The vast majority of tourists, having arrived in the resort city of Adler, first of all go to the Olympic Park, to look at the places where the Winter Games were held. Getting to it is easy - many minibuses ply there from the center. In addition, in the Olympic Park you can admire not only the stadiums, torches and the Formula 1 track. Just a short walk away there is a calm and quiet promenade with benches and spacious beaches. Basically, all tourists go to the central beaches, but here the water is much calmer and, most importantly, quieter. Many people care about the question: "Is there an entrance fee?" The answer will please many - no, anyone can get to the beach, like to the Olympic Park, and absolutely free of charge.
