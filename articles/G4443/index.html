Hotel Ilios Malia Apartments 3 * (Malia, Greece): photos and reviews of tourists

If we talk about the most popular European resorts, then many tourists come to mind Greece, namely, its island part. After all, each island has a unique history, interesting sights and, of course, miles of beautiful beaches. Perhaps the island of Crete is considered one of the most famous, which receives thousands of tourists every year. It is here that the Ilios Malia Apartments 3 * hotel is located, which often becomes the place for travelers to stop.

Of course, when planning, it is important to study the official information about the selected hotel, as well as get acquainted with the opinions of tourists who have already managed to spend several days there.

Infrastructure and location of the hotel





A small, cozy hotel Ilios Malia Apartments 3 * is located very close to the center of the picturesque resort town of Malia. Within walking distance are public bus stops, as well as numerous shops and cafes. By the way, the seashore can also be reached in a few minutes.

The hotel itself consists of a modern three-story building, which, incidentally, was recently renovated. In the center of the courtyard is a swimming pool. The hotel area, although small, but very well equipped. And literally at every step of the eye, flowering bushes, tall trees and bright colors delight us.

Hotel Ilios Malia Apartments 3 * (Heraklion, Malia): photo and room information





The hotel consists of 54 rooms - there are standard rooms, more comfortable superior rooms, as well as apartments, which consist of a bedroom and a living room with folding furniture. The design of the rooms is discreet and pleasant. From your own balcony you can enjoy views of the garden and the surrounding area, breathe fresh air.

Each room has its own kitchenette, where there are all the dishes and, of course, appliances (toaster, microwave, electric kettle and a small stove) for cooking, which is important for many tourists. Of course, the room also has everything in order to ensure maximum comfort, including air conditioning, a safe, a TV and even a small refrigerator. The bathroom will be pleased with modern fixtures, high-quality cosmetics and towels that change really every day.

Meals: what can a guest expect?





Since food is an integral part of the vacation, this moment must be planned in advance. What can Ilios Malia Apartment Crete 3 * offer to its guests? It has its own restaurant, where guests are always welcome. Most tourists prefer to pre-pay only breakfasts, which, as is customary, are carried out in the form of a buffet. The menu here is not bad, the dishes are fresh and tasty.

The rest of the day, tourists can also eat in the hotel restaurant. You can either pre-pay the all-inclusive plan or pay the bill each meal. Dishes for lunch and dinner are already prepared to order, it offers not only standard international cuisine, but also island and Mediterranean dishes. And there is also a bar in the price list of which includes local drinks, cocktails, aromatic coffee, fresh pastries and light snacks.

Naturally, you can eat without problems outside the walls of the hotel, as there are always a lot of cafes and colorful island taverns in the resort area.

Leisure and amenities by the sea





What can a tourist who decides to stay at the Ilios Malia Apartments 3 * Hotel count on? Crete is an island that is famous for its beautiful beaches, pleasant weather and clear sea waters. A convenient path leads to the nearest public beach - the journey takes no more than 10 minutes.

Travelers speak well of this place, because here it is really beautiful and clean, and the waters are calm, and the entrance to the sea is completely cleared of stones. Since this beach is intended for public use, sometimes a lot of people gather here. The use of sun loungers and deck chairs, of course, is paid extra. And on the shore there is a tavern where you can relax in cool and eat.

Of course, you can have fun playing beach volleyball, boating, boating and water skiing, windsurfing, trying diving and sailing - a full range of entertainment is provided for tourists.

Should I count on additional services?





Having stayed in one of the rooms of Ilios Malia Apartments 3 * hotel, of course, you can count on some additional amenities. The territory has its own parking lot, which guests can use for free (in the town you can easily rent transport and go on a trip around the island).

The hotel can easily exchange currency, and the rate is almost the same as in the city. Almost everywhere in the hotel area there is Wi-Fi, so you can connect to the World Wide Web at any time of the day. By the way, there is a computer corner at your service, where you can use office equipment and a laptop.

Hotel Ilios Malia Apartments 3 * (Greece, Heraklion, Malia): entertainment and leisure for tourists





According to reviews, most tourists spend in the hotel only a small part of the day, preferring to relax on the beach and explore the island. Nevertheless, in the territory of Ilios Malia Apartments 3 * you can have a good time. As already mentioned, in the courtyard there is a spacious pool with clean, fresh water. This is a favorite place for guests where they relax, bathe, take sunbaths, socialize and simply have fun, especially if you are not in the mood to go to the beach. Wide plank beds with soft mattresses and umbrellas, in the shadow of which you can hide, are placed nearby in sufficient numbers.

Here, in the open air, there is a large jacuzzi - the round pool is equipped with a powerful hydromassage system and you can relax here for free. You can pass the time at the pool table. In the evenings, guests are invited to a bar where you can dance and even sing karaoke.

Do not forget about the sights of the island, because Crete is not only beaches and hotels. There are many museums, archaeological finds, and the remains of ancient temples. In a word, this island is an ideal place for a sightseeing vacation, and an interesting tour can be purchased right at the hotel, at the local tour desk, where experienced and friendly staff work.

Family holidays: conditions for children

Parents who are going to spend a vacation with their children are interested in slightly different issues, because when traveling with a child comfort comes to the fore. Hotel Ilios Malia Apartments 3 * often becomes a place to relax with children. As already mentioned, they provide spacious apartments that are ideal for families with a baby.

Moreover, upon request, a rollaway bed with a soft mattress is always delivered to the room. In the local restaurant you can always find something tasty for the child, and there are shops nearby that sell baby food. For a fee, you can leave the baby for a while with the nanny. There is also a pool for children in the courtyard - it is spacious enough for games, but shallow and safe, and the water always warms up well and changes regularly. The city itself also has many places where children of any age can have fun and have a lot of fun.
