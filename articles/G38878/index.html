Greece: sandy beach as a visiting card

Several seas, clear waters, developed tourist infrastructure, lots of historical and natural attractions - all this is Greece. Sandy beach is not uncommon in this country. There is an established opinion that in the mountainous region the entire coast is pebbled or even rocky. But contrary to him, in this southern country there is no shortage of sand. As the famous aphorism says, everything is in Greece. Beautiful beaches are no exception. Here we will review the sandy coasts of different parts of the country. After all, Greece is not only the mainland and peninsula of the Peloponnese, but also many islands.

Northern Greece Sandy beach of Halkidiki

The climate of this part of the country is milder, resort towns are buried in the greenery of pine trees, and the cozy bays of the Aegean Sea delight with pure water. To the southeast of the large city of Thessaloniki is the Halkidiki peninsula, shaped like a trident. All three capes have their own names - Agion Oros with Mount Athos, Sithonia and Kassandra. People like noisy young people come here to hang out on Paralia Katerini, pious pilgrims after visiting St. Athos, and shopaholics who came to northern Greece under the "fur coat" program. Soft golden sand takes all this motley audience into its soft embrace. Halkidiki's beaches boast 34 Blue Flags.



Southern Greece Sandy Loutraki Beach

This resort on the Gulf of Corinth is located just 80 km from Athens, so your vacation will not be limited to simply spending time on the beach. In addition, healing mineral keys are beaten here, as well as a hydrotherapy center. So you can at the same time and heal. But if you cannot live without sand, your path is to the island of Euboea. It is so close to the coast of the mainland that it is connected to it by a 15-meter bridge. The best beaches on the island - near the resort village of Eretria.

Peloponnese Peninsula (Greece). Sandy beach of Messinia

This is the most southern part of mainland Greece. The main attraction of this place is the lack of a large number of compatriots. Group tours from Russia do not go here, but only independent travelers enjoy here an unclouded vacation. Despite the fact that it has wonderful sandy beaches, beautiful nature and a pleasant Mediterranean climate, spending all days by the sea would be a crime against our own cultural development. After all, the Peloponnese is replete with historical attractions. At the southern end of the peninsula is a small island of Elafonisos, famous for the beaches of Sarakiniko, Simos and Panagia.



Crete

Despite the fact that the hiking trail was trodden here a long time ago, Crete managed to preserve the pristine beauty of its nature. The wide sandy beaches of Greece located on the northern tip of the island, while on the southern shore sand interspersed with small pebbles is available only to those tourists who are not too lazy to go down the multi-stage stairs from steep cliffs. In the north, it is good to relax with young children, since the entry into the sea is very gentle, and the water in shallow water warms up faster.

Dodecanese

The sandy beaches on this small group of islands are awarded 44 Blue Flags. To get here is to get a guaranteed relaxing holiday away from the hustle and bustle. And here are the best resorts in Greece with sandy beaches located on the island of Zakynthos (also known as Zakynthos). This paradise is the same visiting card of the country, as well-known Santorini with its black sand. If you already got to Zakynthos, do not miss the beautiful Navagio beach.
