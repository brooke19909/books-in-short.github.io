How to attract money: useful tips and signs

Every person who experienced certain material difficulties inevitably thought about how to attract money. But, as a rule, things did not go beyond lengthy reflections. Meanwhile, there are special signs of money and rituals aimed at attracting capital. They will be discussed in our article.



A variety of conspiracies and signs, the purpose of which is to improve the financial situation, can be combined by a common phrase “magic of money”. How to attract money through these manipulations?

First of all, it is necessary to completely reconsider your attitude to finance as such. After all, money has its own energy, the charge of which directly depends on your mood. A man who keeps notes in a pocket of his trousers in a crumpled and crumpled form is unlikely to attract wealth. Money loves respect. Fold them neatly and put them in your wallet or wooden box - the tree removes negative energy from banknotes. To attract money will also help a special talisman, designed for storage in a wallet. It can become an invariable note or Chinese coins connected by a red ribbon. In addition, clover or mint leaves can help raise finance.

How to attract money to the house. Signs

· In order for money to be kept in the house, it is necessary to shake the tablecloth onto the street after the guests leave.

· Pay attention to the dining table - it should not be bare. Cover it with a beautiful tablecloth underneath

Place a few notes.

· Place the broom up with a whisk.

· Pouring tea into a mug, drink the resulting foam - this sign will help attract money to the house.

· Put a coin in each corner of the house and leave them there.

· Hang a mirror near the table or in the kitchen.

· Get ​​a money tree - it can be either a symbolic talisman or a living plant. In the latter case, the fat woman is the most suitable - a plant that brings good luck in financial matters. You can also hang bills and coins from the branches of any other plant - such a talisman, created with your own hands, will certainly bring material prosperity to the house.

General tips for raising money

· It is recommended to give money with the right hand, and accept it with the left.

· Do not whistle in the house.

· Monitor the health of plumbing in the house. Money and water have a similar energy, and if a tap drips in the house, they can simply leak from you.

· If you want to attract money, do manicure or cut nails exclusively on Fridays and Tuesdays.

· Carry out a special ritual of luring money: put a few coins with the eagle up on the refrigerator door or under the rug in the hallway and say: “Money is money, like water is to the coast.”



· Ask the young month to raise money. When you see a thin sickle in the sky, show him a few notes and ask him to increase capital.

· Do not repay debts on the New Year, 31 or 13 of any month.

· Never leave a wallet completely empty - it should always contain at least one bill.

The above signs will help you attract money to the house and increase your net worth.
