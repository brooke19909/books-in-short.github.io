Grand Hotel Vostok, Sterlitamak: description of rooms, service, reviews

The city of Sterlitamak is a very large settlement, and usually a lot of guests come here. These are business travelers, and students, and athletes, and just tourists. And all these people, of course, require good housing during their stay in the city. There are many comfortable hotels in Sterlitamak. And one of the most popular complexes for tourists and business travelers is the Grand Hotel Vostok.

Where is the hotel

This modern hotel is located, according to most of its guests, very convenient. The Vostok Grand Hotel (Sterlitamak) is located in the historical part of the city, close to various sights and business complexes - on Komsomolskaya Street 84. Directly next to the hotel are administrative offices, banks, offices and, of course, many shopping centers and establishments Catering. Also nearby are the large market and the Russian Drama Theater .





Sterlitamak, Komsomolskaya, 84 - this has a grand hotel "Vostok" address. The telephone number of the administration of this complex can be found on its official website.

In addition, for a fee, the hotel provides tourists and business travelers with services such as airport transfers. The train station is just 5 km from this hotel. The nearest airport is in Ufa. The distance between it and the hotel is approximately 120 km.

general description

Tourists can spend time in this hotel with comfort. Grand Hotel Vostok is a modern convenient business complex of the European class. This hotel is a three-story building with an attic built in the middle of the last century. The territory of the hotel has its own. In the courtyard of the complex are small lawns and trees grow. Free parking is available in front of the main entrance.

Check-in at this hotel is at 14:00. Tourists must leave the rooms after the payment deadline at 12:00. In addition, the hotel provides everything you need for people with disabilities. Smoking is prohibited throughout this hotel.

The decoration of the corridors, hall and hotel rooms is considered by tourists to be very attractive. In the interior design of the hotel premises, mainly fashionable and modern styles were used .

The address and telephone number of the hotel should, of course, be known in advance. Reservation of rooms in this hotel, of course, is necessary in advance. The contacts of the complex are indicated, as already mentioned, on its official website. This rest center is very popular among the guests of Sterlitamak. Many business travelers who periodically visit the city prefer to stay here. Therefore, there may not be any available rooms at the hotel when you contact the reception directly.





Number of rooms

In total, the Vostok Grand Hotel complex, reviews of which are mostly good, offers Sterlitamak guests 64 well-equipped rooms. If desired, tourists or business travelers can rent here:

  • single one-room number;
  • double first category;
  • one-room studio;
  • one-room suite;
  • two-room family room;
  • two-room apartments.

Depending on the category of rented rooms, the cost of living in a hotel is 2700-7200 thousand rubles. per person per day. For a hotel of this level, it is, of course, very inexpensive .

Grand Hotel Vostok: room description

Actually in the rented rooms themselves tourists can use:

  • TVs with satellite channels;
  • beds and wardrobes;
  • safes;
  • writing and coffee tables.

The floors in all rooms are laminated and carpeted. The rooms for rent at the hotel are decorated in bright colors. Each room has an individual shower. Here, tourists, among other things, are provided with a hairdryer and a set of household chemicals intended for the adoption of water procedures, free of charge. In some rooms for the convenience of guests are provided air conditioning, couches .





In addition to the usual rooms, the hotel also has bridal apartments.

Reviews about rented rooms

The rooms of the Grand Hotel Vostok (Sterlitamak) offer tourists and business travelers, in the opinion of its residents, a good one. Many former guests of this hotel consider it even the best in the city. The hotel rooms, judging by the available reviews, are always clean and comfortable. They are decorated, according to tourists, is actually very attractive. Annoying screaming tones in the decoration of the rooms are not used.

The furniture in the rooms is beautiful and modern. Sleeping on beds and sitting at desks in the rooms of this complex, according to many guests, is very convenient. Tourists and business travelers also praise this hotel for the presence of mosquito nets on the windows. In the summer, mosquitoes do not interfere with sleeping in the rooms for rent in the complex. Of course, there are no other insects in the rooms of this hotel. Bed linens in this hotel are often changed, and therefore it always looks neat.

Bathrooms in the hotel are spacious and equipped, according to many guests of Sterlitamak, very good. In addition, in the shower there are sockets for razors, hair dryers and tongs. Bathing kits are provided in full. Residents can also use slippers and bathrobes.

Cons of the hotel

Some of the shortcomings of the hotel rooms are not very large. However, no one considers the rooms rented at the hotel to be too small. Also, many of its former residents attribute the lack of instructions for using safes to the minuses of the complex. Due to the fact that the guests do not know how to close them correctly, their doors are often blocked. In this case, you have to call the administrator for help.

The minus of the hotel, its residents believe that in fact in the rooms themselves there are too few outlets. Those tourists and business travelers who have booked rooms in this hotel should take a tee or extension cord with them on the road. Among the minuses of the hotel, many of its guests include, among other things, the lack of coolers on the floors.

Many tourists consider this hotel to be a drawback that it is quite difficult to find in the city. Most travelers are advised to use the navigator to search for a hotel .





Hotel infrastructure

Of course, many services are provided for guests and on the territory of the Vostok Grand Hotel itself. At the hotel, tourists can visit, for example:

  • a restaurant;
  • fitness center and solarium;
  • beauty saloon;
  • laundry, ironing and shoe shine facilities.

There is also an on-site cash machine and luggage storage. If desired, tourists and business travelers can have a good time in the relaxation room equipped with a TV. For business people, the hotel has a large conference room. The complex also has a banquet hall, rented out for corporate events, anniversaries and weddings. A small souvenir mini market is also available on site .





Of course, the Vostok Grand Hotel offers guests and quality food . If desired, rooms can be rented here with breakfast, as well as with a guesthouse or half board.

Tourists and business travelers can use this hotel and free Wi-Fi. Of course, for the convenience of guests who rented rooms on the upper floors, the hotel has elevators.

The opinion of tourists about infrastructure

The Vostok Grand Hotel (Sterlitamak) earned good reviews from guests not only for the convenience of its rooms. A good opinion among the residents of this hotel has developed about its infrastructure.

The staff of the complex are usually praised by its former guests. The hotel staff behave towards the residents very politely. At the same time, they treat their duties responsibly. The administration of the hotel tries to solve all the problems arising from the guests promptly. The front desk is open 24 hours a day.

Thus, most of the guests of Sterlitamak who have chosen the Vostok Grand Hotel for their stay , consider the service provided in it to be quite appropriate for its cost. Tourists and business travelers include the noise of elevators to some of the disadvantages of this center. Most of the former guests of the complex recommend renting rooms away from them. Also, many tourists especially recommend renting attic rooms in this hotel. They are in the hotel, according to residents, the quietest.

Wi-Fi in this complex is sometimes fast and stable, and sometimes, unfortunately, slow. The ATM of Sberbank in the hotel is constantly working. In addition, the terminal also has a cash deposit function.





Grand Hotel Vostok: Wellness Club

The spa at the hotel is also popular with most guests. It is called the Wellness Club. You can spend time here pleasantly and with benefit for yourself.

The ability to keep fit is also what many guests praise the Vostok Grand Hotel (Sterlitamak) for. The gym in this hotel has a large area and is well equipped. In addition, various kinds of cosmetology and massage programs are often implemented at the Wellness spa center of the complex . Some of them offer good discounts.

In addition to massages, the Wellness Spa Hotel offers treatments such as body wraps and scrubbing. You can take here all kinds of skin-friendly baths. There are some other wellness facilities at the hotel's spa.

The staff in the club, as in the hotel itself, are very polite and helpful. This complex is visited not only by city guests who have chosen the Vostok Grand Hotel for their stay. The spa of this complex is also popular among the residents of Sterlitamak . Entrance fees to the club for citizens are not too expensive. Guests of the hotel can use the gym completely free of charge.

Dining at the hotel: restaurant

The hotel restaurant serves mainly European cuisine for tourists and business travelers. Reviews of the guests of this institution have earned good. Dishes in the hotel restaurant, according to many of its residents, cook delicious. The only thing - some guests believe that the menu in this institution could be a little more diverse.

The restaurant staff considers the majority of tourists and business travelers efficient and responsible. The waiters are polite and fast. Not only for the good cuisine, many residents praise this restaurant. Grand Hotel Vostok (Sterlitamak) earned good reviews from guests, including the fact that the food here is served in beautiful new dishes, and the tables and chairs are very convenient. To some of the disadvantages of service in this institution, tourists attribute mainly the lack of sugar bowls on the tables. The prices in the restaurant are considered by most residents to be quite acceptable and affordable.
