Holiday Village "Miya", Abkhazia: description and reviews

Sukhumi is the capital of Abkhazia. Sunny and welcoming, he opens the door every year. Guests from neighboring countries spend their holidays here with pleasure, receiving a boost of energy for the whole coming year. Sukhumi is located just 110 km from the border with Russia, which is very convenient for our tourists. The city is protected from all sides by the high ridges of the Caucasus from the invasion of cold, airy masses. Therefore, the climate here is mild, optimal for restoring health.



Attraction for tourists

Holidays in Sukhumi will be interesting and useful to absolutely everyone. The city is surrounded by greenery, pleases with snow-white buildings and the turquoise sea. Sandy and pebble beaches stretch for more than 10 km, along which there are numerous recreation centers, pensions and motels. Each of them is ready to accept tourists and provide them with comfortable living conditions. After analyzing the reviews, we want to especially highlight one of them. The recreation center "Miya" (Abkhazia) is a corner of unsurpassed tranquility, warmth and comfort.

Tourists always pay attention to the location of the boarding house in which they are going to stay. Based on this, you can plan excursions. The recreation center "Miya" (Abkhazia) is located on the Black Sea, at the entrance to the city of Gudauta. Only 10 minutes of leisurely walk separates it from the central park. The city center, where all the tourist infrastructure is located, is only 15 minutes away by taxi. The hotel provides guests with a shuttle service. The whole territory is surrounded by acacias and ornamental plants, which makes it extremely attractive.
