Golden Lotus Hotel 2 * (Nha Trang): description, reviews, photos

Nha Trang can be safely called the beach center of Vietnam, because every year this small town attracts thousands of vacationers. The life of the resort is filled with fun, noise, energy. Tourists love a lot and a lot of things here.

Someone likes the incomparable landscapes of the town, snow-white sand, heavenly water, hills that play with all shades of green, without exception. Most of all, the city surprises with tropical islands in the middle of the bay. All vacationers will confirm that these beauties are real and not related to Photoshop.

Nha Trang is beautiful for its vibrant nightlife, because it is here that the best parties and shows are held in Vietnam. The city is known for first-class spa treatments and mud baths, as well as diving.





So, if you decide to visit Vietnam, the Golden Lotus Hotel 2 * hotel in Nha Trang is perfect for you for a good and budget holiday. Now more!

Description

The two-star Golden Lotus Hotel 2 * hotel in Nha Trang is located close to the sea, only fifty meters share the golden beach and the hotel. The windows offer panoramic views of Vinpearl Land, the local resort complex and the landscape of the captivating Nha Trang Bay.

Hotel guests and residents can use Wi-Fi for free. They can ask the staff at the front desk for help at any time of the day. In addition, guests of the Golden Lotus Hotel 2 * have a restaurant where they can enjoy traditional Vietnamese dishes, a cup of tea or refreshments.

There is also a bar on site. Each room is equipped with a TV with plenty of cable channels and an electronic safe. They also have en suite bathrooms with both bathtub and shower.

Benefits

Guests are always up to date with the latest news of the country and Nha Trang, because they are provided with daily fresh press. A tour desk will always help you and give you the information you need.





Here you will be offered to rent a bicycle or organize a day trip. The hotel also has a working laundry, luggage storage, fax and parking for your cars, but at an additional cost.

In addition, Golden Lotus Hotel 2 * provides a transfer service from the airport and train station, but for this, visitors need to notify the hotel in advance of their decision.

Room Feature

Due to the popularity of such a hotel establishment as Golden Lotus Hotel 2 *, Nha Trang attracts not only wealthy and wealthy travelers, but also guests with a limited budget, as well as young couples and large families.

The hotel offers a choice of 50 rooms of different levels, but all of them are comfortable and cozy. All visitors can watch television and get the latest news from daily newspapers. Each room is equipped with air conditioning, a hairdryer, a bathroom and a TV; some have refrigerators, a minibar and other additional equipment.

The hotel offers its guests six types of rooms. Standard Room is the most economical option. It is not particularly comfortable, but all basic requirements are met. The standard room consists of one room, in which there is a small (well, very small) window.





There are no balconies in standard rooms; there is also no bathroom with a refrigerator. They are equipped with air conditioning, a minibar and a TV. These rooms are designed for a maximum of a couple of adults and one baby.

Superior Room offers more expensive services, but with a view from the room to the city and neighboring buildings. Each room has a refrigerator, air conditioning, telephone, minibar and TV. There is no balcony or terrace in the room, but there is a standard window from which you can look at the city.

The best numbers

Deluxe Room is the most expensive pleasure, but worth it. The suite pleases with a view of the azure sea, has a larger area and the presence of all types of services that the hotel offers. The suites are located on high floors, above the fifth, and offer beautiful views from the balcony or terrace.

Double, Twin and Family Room are aimed at meeting the needs of different categories of guests: for lovers or newlyweds, for families with children from small to large, they are suitable. If the age of the children does not exceed nine years, payment for their stay at the hotel is not charged, but only on condition that they will be in the same room with their parents.

Places to relax nearby

If you decide to walk around the city and explore the local beauty outside the Golden Lotus Hotel 2 *, Nha Trang will prepare for you a lot of wonderful impressions from these places. Based on the ratings of hotel guests, RuTraveller rated places near the hotel on a point system.





The highest score (4.54) was received by the Thap Ba mud source, where therapeutic mineral mud and water were combined. By visiting the source, you will not only improve your health, but also rest your soul. Tropical greens, delicate aromas of plants and flowering trees will not leave you indifferent.

One of the favorite places for visitors to Nha Trang is the Grill House with a rating of 4.5. The institution is unique, because it provides the opportunity to independently prepare dishes from fresh seafood, of course, under the supervision and guidance of waiters. You will be delighted with the excellent interior and enjoy the taste of the freshest seafood.

The unique beauty of nature, clean sandy beaches, warm coastal water and the sky-blue sea made Nha Trang Bay popular with tourists, which received 4.47 points. This place will not leave indifferent even the most evil critics. Pure clear water makes it possible to see the local underwater world.

Positive reviews

About Golden Lotus Hotel 2 * (Vietnam), visitors reviews are mixed. Guests note from the advantages of the hotel beautiful views from the windows (but this applies only to those who stayed in deluxe rooms and on higher floors), the location near the sea, excellent service and excellent staff, as well as the hotel's location on the first coastline. But placement on the first line is also a definite drawback, because the hotel is very noisy, especially on the lower floors. Therefore, in Golden Lotus Hotel 2 * it is best to book rooms on the upper floors, a little overpay, but enjoy a first-class vacation.





Convenient location (two kilometers from the city center) allows you to quickly get to the sea, as well as to various parties and meetings, a variety of cafes. The hotel has good service - at a high level, friendly staff, always smiling girls at the reception, daily high-quality cleaning.
