Novopolotsk hotels: overview, description, reviews

In the small industrial city of Novopolotsk in the Vitebsk region of the Republic of Belarus, guests most often come on a business trip or stop there. Therefore, the presence of comfortable conditions for recreation in the city is relevant. The hotels of Novopolotsk will help to solve this issue, where at an affordable price you can get a wide range of services that a traveler needs.

Hotel Centaur

The Centaur Hotel is located off the coast of the Western Dvina. Novopolotsk is located on the other side of the river, the city center can be reached in five minutes. The rooms are decorated in wood and decorated in a rustic style. The in-house restaurant serves national cuisine. On the territory there are gazebos for a picnic. There are only 100 meters from two strategic highways - Riga - Oryol and St. Petersburg - Brest, so when planning tours to Belarus, you will make the right choice if you stay at the Centaur Hotel along the way. So you save time and have a wonderful rest in modern comfortable rooms. The hotel has a currency exchange office, offers ticketing services, a tour desk, sports equipment rental, a bathhouse and a sauna. Parking and Wi-Fi are free. For accommodation, guests are offered the following room categories:

  • double with one double or twin beds - 2723 rubles per day for two people, and 2042 rubles if one person is accommodated;
  • a suite with one double or two single beds - 4084 rubles per day for two people, and 3404 rubles if one person is accommodated;
  • Triple - 4084 rubles per day;
  • four-seater - 4084 rubles per day;
  • family deluxe - 6807 rubles per day.

All rooms are air-conditioned and have a TV. The Centaur Hotel in Novopolotsk is located at the following address: Koptevo district, ul. Dvinskaya, 15.

Hotel "Belarus" in Novopolotsk

The hotel has a category of two stars, has a favorable location in the city center, the distance to the bus station from here is only 400 meters. Within walking distance are a supermarket and market, less than a kilometer you need to go to get to the river. The hotel has its own restaurant and bar, a casino. Free Wi-Fi access is available in the hotel's public areas, and there is a guarded paid public parking nearby. Shuttle services from or to the airport are available at an extra charge. To accommodate guests, the following room options are offered:

  • single standard - 1144 rubles per day;
  • double with two separate beds - 1715 rubles per day for two people, and 1307 rubles if one person is accommodated;

All rooms are equipped with a TV. Superior rooms are air-conditioned. The Belarus Hotel is located at 2 Kirov Street.
