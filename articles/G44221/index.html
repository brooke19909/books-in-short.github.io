Kos island: attractions. Kos Island: holidays

Recently, Europeans have preferred to buy tours to Greece. In this vast and hospitable country, every piece of land is unique in its historical heritage and ancient architecture. The most popular resort areas of Greece are Rhodes, Corfu, Crete and the island of Kos. Sights cannot but admire with their ancient centuries-old history.

We will discuss in more detail the resort of Kos, which is located in the southeast of the Aegean Sea. It is famous for its wonderful virgin landscapes, colorful sandy beaches, thermal springs and amazing architectural objects. Only here tourists will be able to meet the Mediterranean seals swaying on the waves, or see how turtles lay their eggs and bury them in the sand.





The climatic conditions at the resort are very favorable, it is a bit cooler (unlike the same Rhodes), a lot of elite housing of excellent quality at an affordable price has been built. For this reason, tours to the island of Kos are becoming more and more popular every year. On this patch of land there is a harmonious balance between the modern world and pristine nature - this is a real find for those who prefer a measured and leisurely pastime.

It is famous for its special Greek atmosphere, which sets the tone for mood. Every little thing seems fabulous and mythical. The small Greek province is rich in picturesque bays, small chapels, rocky capes and just saturated with the aroma of jasmine. A huge advantage is the convenient territorial location in relation to nearby resorts such as Leros, Samos, Rhodes, Patmos, Kalymnos and Nissiros.

Transport accessibility

To get to the tropical resort will not be a huge difficulty, planes fly here and buses go. Excellent ferry service. Motor ships sail from its shores daily to any village and center of Greece. Highways built. As you can see, there are no problems with the transport fork.

Outstanding ancient spa facilities





One of the most environmentally friendly areas is the island of Kos. Attractions are scattered throughout the resort area. The amazing creations of mankind have survived to our times. The animal world flourishes in the province: there are delightful flamingos, huge turtles and lazy seals. Often Kos is called a natural and architectural reserve.

And how many ancient ruins are scattered on the territory! For example, in the central part of the island, archaeologists found objects of the early Christian and Mycenaean era, which were subsequently exhibited in the Archaeological Museum. Among the exhibits, a special place is occupied by a mosaic with images of Hippocrates and Asclepius. The evidence that there was life on these lands earlier is the found cemetery with buried children of different ages.

In the eastern zone, many ancient temples with amazing ornaments were found. Also found here is the altar of Hercules and the pandemic of Venus. And in the western part, the altar of Dionysus was excavated, as well as numerous structures with an unusual mosaic floor. Scientists have found the Casa Romana mansion, decorated with frescoes and ancient sculptures. Valuable buildings and expositions from different eras (Roman, Hellenistic, classical) are presented in the Archaeological Museum, which is located on Freedom Square.

Hippocrates' Plane





Acquaintance with the island, according to locals, it is better to start with a tour of the main square, on it grows the majestic tree of Hippocrates (or wish plane). According to unofficial sources, the ancient Greek doctor himself planted him about two thousand years ago in order to conduct disputes and exercises under his shady crowns with his friends and students.

Some researchers believe that this tree is no more than 500 years old, but there are no supporting facts for these assumptions yet. The plane-tree circumference is more than 14 m, it stands on a stone structure, and its bulky branches are supported by metal sticks. Locals take care of him, who consider the tree the main shrine of the province. Every year in the summer season, grandiose festivals are held on the square.

Temple complex Asklepion





The island of Kos, the photo of which is presented in the article, is proud of the ruins of the temple of the healing god Asclepius. After a powerful earthquake, the structure collapsed and small structures remained in its place. Asklepion was previously a medical sanatorium and consisted of several levels.

There was also a medical school with spring sources and majestic arches. In the 4th century, the shrine of Asclepius was erected on the third level. A few centuries later, wards for the sick and a cathedral of the Doric period were built.

Knights castle

The island of Kos is rightfully proud of its oldest buildings. Excursions around the castle leave a lasting impression on tourists. According to reliable information, the construction of the fortress began in the 14th century, and ended only in the 18th century. This was constantly hindered by attacks from the Turks. In the appearance of an unusual building, several architectural styles are visible. There are ancient sculptures, old inscriptions with altars.

The majestic castle of Antimachia





In a picturesque settlement, 3 km from the island, there is a Venetian building, which previously served as a haven for local residents. The appearance of the fortress was completely preserved, even despite numerous attacks. Before the entrance, the date 1494 is knocked out and the symbol of the knights of the Order of Malta is indicated.

The interior is unremarkable. There are two cathedrals of the 16th and 18th centuries and the dilapidated foundations of ancient buildings. But what a panorama opens from the walls of the building - pristine landscapes and the blue sea!

What else is remarkable about the island of Kos?

The resort map is presented in the article, on it you can see a small piece of land. Splendor and power strike the holy cathedrals and churches. Numerous Muslim and Orthodox churches are of great value to the city (the Defterdar and Haji Hassan Mosques, the Church of the Holy Martyr Paraskeva).





We recommend you to make an excursion to Cape Rufianos - the cleanest corner of nature with an interesting building, located behind the picturesque bay of Kefalos. An indelible impression will be left by waterfalls. In the summer, tourists visit archaeological sites in Pili. Here you will see buildings dating from the Middle Ages. You will also learn about the rich historical heritage of this place.

You can feel the atmosphere of antiquity on the "market" of Agora. It was here that the local Greeks made their purchases. On the territory of the attention of the viewer are presented the surviving remains of ancient buildings, bulky columns of original symmetry and an updated small church. In addition to the architectural and historical heritage, the island of Kos is proud of its amazing natural sites. Sights of the resort area are represented by Thermal springs, Salt Lake, Zia village, Peacock forest.





The best beaches of the province

Almost the entire coast is covered with colorful sand. Black, gray, golden and even red beaches of the island of Kos impress with their splendor. They are the pride of the country, and some coastlines simply dazzle with an unusual landscape - picturesque bays with stone structures. The environment encourages vacationers to active entertainment: windsurfing, diving, yachting and surfing. We offer you a list of the best beaches in the resort.

Paradise beach is a popular place with beautiful landscapes and white sand. The coastline is equipped for guests with various attractions, beach amenities, rental points and cozy restaurants.

Termez Bay is a small pebble and sparsely populated coast with delightful steep archipelagos. In this secluded paradise there is a salt lake formed during the mixing of sea water and underground sources.

The coast of Kardamena is one of the busiest resort areas. Extends over several kilometers. This is a very clean sandy beach with a gentle descent into the Aegean Sea. There is always a lot of people here, life is in full swing.

Where to stay?

Kos hotels are rich in comfortable hotels of different levels. Holidays at the resort will appeal to couples with children. Almost all recreational facilities are in excellent condition and operate on the ALL inclusive system. Most of them occupy leading positions in world ratings of comfort.

In addition to traditional equipment (sports fields, cafes and beaches), boarding houses are equipped with business centers, meeting rooms, water slides and water parks. Paradise living conditions and quality service are offered by the island of Kos. The hotels presented in the article are considered the best, according to people.

The chic hotel, founded in 2004, with its own pier for boats, is a real oasis in the middle of a vegetation park. Here, complete harmony reigns and luxury rules. Each vacationer will be able to spend their holidays in heavenly conditions, in separate bungalows. You do not have to think about food and leisure - experienced and attentive employees will take care of all the troubles.

Pension Marmari Palace 5 *

A huge hotel is located on the coast of the Aegean Sea. It works on an all-inclusive system. It invites discerning people who appreciate pomp, comfort and safety. Available chic two-room rooms with access to the pool. Animated entertainment for young children. A club and playgrounds have been opened so that children can fully and diversely spend time.

Summing up, I would like to note that the resorts of Greece are a corner where time pauses, here in the lap of wildlife you feel a surge of energy and forget about the worldly bustle. You can verify this only by visiting the island of Kos. Sights of the resort have been preserved since antiquity and are objects of a bygone civilization.
