How to make DIY bottle labels

Thinking about what to present to friends or acquaintances for a holiday? Present them a bottle of good alcohol. And how beautifully to present a gift? Make labels for bottles with your own hands. It will turn out not just a gift, but a very original present. See below for ideas on how to make a label.

Printed picture



The easiest way to make labels on bottles with your own hands is to decorate them with a beautiful image. You can draw a picture by hand or print it on a printer. The label must be selected depending on the occasion. If you go to a girl‚Äôs birthday, you need to decorate a bottle of wine with a picture of flowers. And if the present is made for a man, then you can print a picture of a car or a photo of a birthday person. To make such a gift for parents. For example, decorate a bottle with a picture of a grandson.

Baby gift



Do you think that you can only decorate alcohol bottles? Nothing like this. You can decorate children's champagne, and even a bottle of soda. You should choose the appropriate figure. Do-it-yourself baby label on the bottle should be made from a bright picture. Choose an image from a cartoon character or a cute drawing of a plush toy. In Photoshop, the label can be supplemented with funny inscriptions or decorative elements, such as stars or hearts. And you should also put the picture in a beautiful frame.

New year label



Going to visit empty-handed is not accepted. And on New Year's all the more. What to present to friends? Give them a bottle of good wine. Does she look not very presentable? Complete it with a bottle label. Do it yourself with your own hands. Choose a beautiful picture or print one of the options above. Carefully cut out the pattern and stick it on double-sided tape or on PVA. You can complement this decor with colored ribbons, cones and other New Year's attributes.

Volumetric label



A bottle of expensive nutmeg can be decorated in an extravagant way. A volumetric label should be made. It can consist of both cardboard and birch bark. How to make a label for a bottle with your own hands? Take the base and cut out a rectangle from it. In the center with voluminous beads we lay out a picture of a grape branch. From felt we cut a couple of green leaves and attach them to the right place. It remains to stick beautiful ribbons on top and bottom. You can tie a bow out of them. The label can be attached to the bottle using a gold fishing line or a thin thread.

Wedding label



On the most solemn day of life, I want only fine things around. Therefore, the decor is given special attention. Today it is fashionable to make beautiful wedding glasses, decorating them with rhinestones, beads and ribbons. It is worth considering the champagne label. It is very simple to do it yourself. The label on the bottle for the wedding is made as follows. The graphic program combines the drawing of flowers, rings and other wedding paraphernalia. The names of the newlyweds are placed in a beautiful frame. When the composition is arranged, it remains only to print the image. Before sticking a homemade label, it is advisable to remove the one that is already on the bottle.
