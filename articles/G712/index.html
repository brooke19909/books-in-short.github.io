Automatic washing machine: which is better? Reviews, specifications, sizes, brands

Modern housewives consider an automatic washing machine to be a must for household chores. Such a unit will allow not only washing, but also soaking, boiling, bleaching, and then drying the laundry. And for this you only need to enable the appropriate mode.





That is why every house sooner or later, but a washing machine is required. Which is better? Reviews of neighbors and acquaintances in this matter, of course, are important, but the owners themselves must competently approach this issue. This will allow them in the future not to be disappointed in the purchase.

What are washing machines

Essential household appliances are divided into:

  • frontal, in which the loading of linen is carried out through a round transparent hatch located on the side;
  • vertical, the cover of which is located on top.

As for the front-view washing machines, they are recognized as the undisputed sales leaders. They are offered in large quantities by all retail outlets that sell household appliances.



A vertical washing machine is less popular with housewives. In addition, the range of such models is not very wide. However, the machines in which top loading of things is carried out have a plus that is very attractive for many housewives. They can report the laundry during the washing process, without fear that water will flow from the unit.

Choosing a car at its location

In order for the irreplaceable assistant of the hostess to bring maximum benefit and be economically profitable, one must approach her choice with all responsibility. First of all, you need to determine in which place the automatic washing machine you bought will stand. Only after this is it possible to choose a model of full dimensions or a super-heavy device.

Typical dimensions of washing machines are within the following limits:

  • in width - from 40 to 60 cm;
  • in depth - from 32 to 60 cm;
  • in height - 85 cm.

In the event that a frontal washing machine is purchased, which is better? Customer reviews are advised to purchase units whose depth is 32 cm. These machines are just perfect. They easily fit even in the smallest corner of the corridor or bathroom, and also fit freely in the kitchen under the worktop. When choosing a place for your future indispensable assistant, it is worth remembering that her hatch door will require additional space, since it must rotate 180 degrees. If there is no such area, then you will have to consider buying a car with a vertical filling. However, it should be borne in mind that the upper part of such a machine cannot be used as a shelf.

Laundry is loaded into such super-heavy washing machines of 3 kg. A machine of this type in this connection is only suitable for small families of several people.

Choosing a car by washing volume

For this parameter, which washing machine is better? User reviews suggest that a family of 4-6 people will need a full-fledged model. Such aggregates can wash from 4.5 kg to 5 kg of dry items in one cycle.





For a very large family, you will need a washing machine, 6 kg of load for which is a normal operating characteristic. These units are often used by medium and small businesses - small hotels, cafes and restaurants.

However, a full-size automatic washing machine, 5 kg or more of dry laundry in which you can wash in one cycle, is not always located in a city apartment. Then a super-heavy device will be a good way out for a large family. It is used several times a day, giving the car some time to rest between cycles.

Tank material

For this parameter, which washing machine is better? Reviews of experts are advised to purchase such units that would not spoil the laundry that is washed in them. And this is only possible with a stainless steel drum. Such high-quality material can last more than eighty years. However, inexpensive washing machines cannot have such a part, since its cost is quite high. If the family budget does not allow you to purchase equipment that is in a high price category, then you need to consider the option of a tank made of plastic. This is a composite polymeric material that is resistant to corrosion, the action of detergents and high temperatures, capable of absorbing vibration and is silent in operation. Manufacturers claim that the life of such tanks is in the range of 20-25 years.

Technical Selection

So, the purpose of your purchase is a washing machine. Which is better? Reviews of experts recommend paying special attention to the technical characteristics of the device. Certain parameters, indicated in the unit's passport in large Latin letters, allow the models to serve their owners the optimal time. So, pay attention when buying should:

  • the class of the wash itself, indicating its quality and humidity of the laundry at the end of the process;
  • the spin class, on which the careful attitude to things depends;
  • energy class indicating the efficiency of the device;
  • the spin speed, the optimal value of which should be in the range from 400 to 1000 revolutions per minute (a larger indicator will lead to the drying of the laundry, it will be difficult to iron it).

In the passport of a household appliance, all these indicators are indicated in different letters. But keep in mind that A indicates the highest class, while G indicates the lowest.

List of required programs

A good automatic washing machine has extensive functionality. The number of programs she has can go up to sixteen. However, among them there are the main ones installed in each model of such units. This is washing linen and cotton, delicate items and wool, white and colored linen.

There are also brands of automatic washing machines, the programs of which have other names. However, it should be borne in mind that the essence of these functions does not differ from the main ones. Such an expansion of the number of programs is a successful marketing move that can attract the largest possible number of buyers who are ready to give money for expensive equipment.





All existing programs in the machine can be set either using an electronic display, or using the electromechanical path. Inexpensive washing machines have a second option. These are budget models of units. Existence of an electronic board significantly increases the cost of the car.

The need for drying

Complete removal of moisture from the washed laundry is an optional feature for a home helper. At high spin speeds, all things placed in the washing machine become almost completely ready for ironing.

It should be borne in mind that cheap washing machines do not dry clothes. This feature makes the unit more expensive by 20-30 percent.

Engine selection

The quality of an automatic washing machine depends on its reliability. And for this parameter, engine performance is of great importance. After all, it can be attributed to the most important component parts of the unit.

As the most reliable in operation, experts recognize asynchronous motors. They are installed in those machines, the speed of which in spin mode is within 600. At a higher rotational speed of the drum, the models are equipped with a collector motor. However, it is less durable due to abrasion of the brushes. The average life of such an engine is only seven years. And this despite the fact that its cost is one third of the price of the entire unit for washing.

Brand selection

Modern consumers often prefer the technology produced by the famous and popular brand. However, it should be borne in mind that there are not such common brands of automatic washing machines that are of really high quality. After all, the companies that produce them have achieved significant success in the production of high-quality devices.

So, such brands as AEG and Miele can be attributed to the elite class. You can also purchase a washing machine, the price of which is optimal for the family budget (within 350-400 cu), but the quality of the unit is very good. This is a brand of technology from Bosch or Candy, Electrolux or Siemens, as well as Whirlpool.

A wonderful option will be a washing machine, the price of which is low (250-300 cu), but the build quality is at a decent level. These are units of brands such as Ariston and Ardo, Indesit and Beko, as well as Samsung and LG.

Good brands of inexpensive cars - "Baby" and "Vyatka", "Atlant" and "Oka".

By acquiring such necessary household equipment, you should find out where in your city the washing machines of the selected company are repaired. If there are no such service centers, then any malfunctions of the unit will cause noticeable difficulties.

That the car served for a long time

Is it possible to operate the machine in such a way as to avoid its breakdown? To increase the life of the irreplaceable assistant, you should carefully study the instructions. In addition, hoses and other parts of the unit must be checked as often as possible.





Repair of washing machines will not be required for a long time, if you periodically call the wizard for diagnostics and inspection. In this case, household appliances can last a long time and will not cause you problems.
