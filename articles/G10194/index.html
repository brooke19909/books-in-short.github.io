What in-ear headphones to choose? Market review and reviews of the best models

In-ear (vacuum) headphones are sound devices that are compact and have good sound insulation. To listen to music, the model is inserted into the ear canal. In size, the headphones are quite different. Key device parameters include output frequency, reboot, and power.

Modern modifications are actively used by musicians on stage. The main types of devices include membrane, as well as connector modifications. Pads are used in various forms. There are high-quality in-ear headphones in the region of 8 thousand rubles.





How to choose earphones?

First of all, experts recommend paying attention to the shape of the seal. In some cases, manufacturers greatly save on the lining material. Good devices are usually made of silicone. Also on the market are many foam models. The rated power of the vacuum headphones fluctuates around 50 watts.

Many devices use a wave overload protection system. Micro drivers are used both operational and pulse type. Cords are most often used with a copper braid. Inserts are installed polyurethane type. Special attention should be paid to the quality of the diffuser. The output frequency must be at least 20 Hz. In order for the bass to be heard well, an amplifier is needed. In this regard, membrane vacuum headphones are very good. However, the advantage of connector devices lies in excellent sound insulation.

Dolby Sounds technology is not supported on all devices. Professionals prefer fixed coil models. Their nominal pressure is 4 N. The ultimate resistance in this case is at 60 Ohms. For reproducing low frequencies this is very good. Active noise reduction systems are rare. In-ear headphones (photos are presented in the article) of this type are more suitable for audio engineers.

Membrane Models

Membrane in-ear headphones are needed to smooth frequencies. Membrane models are used in various thicknesses. In order to choose a high-quality device, special attention should be paid to the power parameter. It averages 40 watts. Systems of protection against frequency vibrations are used in the first and second class. In terms of tightness, membrane modifications are very different.

Their output impedance is 30 ohms. Acoustic shutters are used both unipolar and bipolar type. They differ in signal conductivity. Inserts are most often made from rubber. These headphones can last about 5 years. In this case, a lot depends on the type of cord. In many models, it is used with a polyurethane braid. You can buy good membrane headphones at a price of 10 thousand rubles.

Connector Devices

Connector in-ear headphones - what is it? These devices are manufactured with fixed coils. The connector on the models allows you to cope well with low frequencies. The nominal power of the headphones is 35 watts. The average overload indicator is 2.7 A. The nozzle in devices is most often installed with a round cross section.

The sound insulation parameter lies in the region of 60%. The tightness of the connector devices is very different. Microdrivers are used only pulse type. Amplifier devices are rare. The main problem with this type of headphone is low impedance. The bass of the models is hard to hear. You can buy high-quality headphone jacks at a price of 8 thousand rubles.
