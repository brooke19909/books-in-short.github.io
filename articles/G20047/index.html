Hotel The Romeo Palace 3 * (Thailand / Pattaya): photos and reviews of tourists

Choosing places to stay, tourists are guided by different criteria. Some prefer luxury hotels, without thinking about the amount of money spent. But many people choose to spend their vacation places where for a little money you can enjoy a pleasant beach holiday and a beautiful area with many attractions. Such conditions are offered in the hotel The Romeo Palace 3 *. There are no frills, but the conditions created will allow you to have a good time.

Location

North Pattaya is one of the most developed tourist cities in Thailand. It is here that the hotel The Romeo Palace 3 * is located. It is located in a cozy quiet area, which has a pleasant pastime. The distance to the nearest airport is 120 km.





Many tourists who have visited the Hotel The Romeo Palace 3 * note that its main advantage is a very convenient location. Here it will be nice to be lovers of peace and quiet, because in the immediate vicinity there are noisy places. But adventure lovers will have something to do here, since the road from the hotel to the nearest entertainment centers takes only a few minutes.

Hotel exterior and interior

The four-story building of The Romeo Palace 3 * hotel is capable of captivating its guests at first sight. It harmoniously combines elements of modern and colonial style. The last reconstruction of the premises was carried out in 2013, so the complex has a fragrant appearance. Originality is given to it by plants that are located even on the roofs of buildings.





The interior of The Romeo Palace 3 * hotel is a little disappointing. Reviews of tourists confirm that the condition of the premises, despite recent repairs, are not in the best condition. The minimalism characteristic of the local environment does not create the impression of home comfort. The only thing that deserves praise is the purity prevailing here.

Settlement Features

Arriving at the hotel guests are not forced to wait long with check-in. At the reception there are always managers who are ready to provide the guests with the keys to their rooms as soon as possible. If delays occur, tourists can pass the time, comfortably sitting on the upholstered furniture in the lobby. Here you can use Wi-Fi or watch TV for free.

Number of rooms

Arriving guests of The Romeo Palace 3 * Hotel (Pattaya) offers 100 rooms for checking in. Among the options presented, you can give preference to the following categories:

  • Economy - in which a maximum of 4 people live;
  • Standard Room - designed for 4 people;
  • Superior Room - designed for 5 people;
  • Deluxe Room - Superior rooms that accommodate a maximum of 5 people.




All rooms are decorated in a simple Thai style. The decor is decorated with local and Indian decorative elements. The rooms have enough furniture to arrange all things. Tiled floors have carpets.

Room Amenities

Tourists who visited Romeo Palace Hotel The 3 (Pattaya) do not complain about the amenities provided in the rooms. Here you can watch programs on LSD-TV or use free Wi-Fi. The air conditioners present in each room are individual, which allows each guest to set a temperature that is comfortable for themselves. The rooms have minibars with drinks and light snacks that you have to pay for. A fridge is also provided. Valuables can be stored in a safe.

Guestrooms have views of the hotel gardens or its surroundings. You can enjoy the local scenery from the balconies that are in each room, conveniently located on the furniture present here. The bathroom comes with a shower and toiletries. Some guests note that the workers of The Romeo Palace 3 * hotel do not always replenish supplies of shower gel or soap in a timely manner. A hairdryer is in every room.

Hotel guests are unhappy that drinking water is not delivered to their rooms daily. You can take it in the minibar, but you will have to pay a lot for it. People who have been here advise buying water bottles in stores outside the hotel. Tourists note that this situation with drinking water is not observed in other hotels of a similar level located in Pattaya.

Pattaya The Romeo Palace 3 *: infrastructure

The hotel provides enough services to make guests comfortable. Tourists can contact the hotel managers at any time, as the reception is open 24 hours a day. Here you can take voluminous things in a safe so as not to clutter up the space in the rooms. In the lobby of the hotel there are representatives of the tour desk who will advise the most interesting sights to visit.

Since the Hotel The Romeo Palace 3 * (Thailand, Pattaya) is in a quiet location, its guests are provided with a transfer to the center of the tourist city of Pattaya or to the beach. There is no surcharge for such services. If you wish, before departure or when booking rooms, tourists can use the airport transfer.

In Romeo Palace Hotel The 3 * there are various shops where you can stock up on souvenirs or groceries. There is also a laundry service, for which you need to pay extra. If necessary, guests are provided with a free parking space. For business people, the opportunity is provided to use a small business center, which has a photocopy and fax machine.

Nutrition

Like most three-star hotels, The Romeo Palace 3 * (Pattaya) provides one-time meals. The main restaurant serves breakfast only. The quality of the dishes does not cause any complaints. Satisfied with the guests and the variety of food. Here you can try dishes of European and local cuisine.





At any time you can have a snack or treat yourself to drinks at the bar located in the lobby of the hotel. It works around the clock, which allows guests to place an order at any time. There is also a cafeteria and bar serving drinks, including spirits, desserts and light snacks.

Pattaya The Romeo Palace 3 *: beach vacation

The hotel is 650 meters from the wonderful Municipal Wong Amat Beach. This is a pretty beautiful and well-kept place where you can enjoy plenty of sunbathing and warm sea water. The beach is sandy, which allows you to walk barefoot. The sea is suitable for swimming with the whole family. Near the coast, it is quite small, so that children can buy enough. To swim, you need to walk at least 10 meters.

On the beach, tourists can take umbrellas, sun loungers and towels for a certain amount of money. There are enough places where you can eat. Since Russians often visit Wong Amat beach, a special menu is provided for them in local cafeterias, which allows you to easily find the right dish.





On the beach quite often local merchants offer various goods. They can buy unique souvenirs or light clothing. They also offer light snacks. Traders are not intrusive, so it will be enough to show them that they do not need their services.

Entertainment and sports

Sports enthusiasts will be a little bored at The Romeo Palace 3 * hotel, but they will still be able to find a fascinating activity. The hotel has a huge swimming pool, where you can go swimming. As confirmed by the reviews of tourists, the reservoir is kept in absolute cleanliness. Those who wish to relax by the pool are provided with umbrellas and sun loungers. It also provides free towels. The pool has a special area equipped for the youngest guests.

Guests at the hotel can play billiards. Game tables are provided free of charge. Water sports enthusiasts can have fun on the beach. Here, for an additional fee, they will teach the intricacies of water skiing, banana and other devices.

Fans of stormy parties will also have to get bored at the hotel. Local musicians sometimes give performances here, which limits the cultural program. But you can find entertainment for yourself in the nearby areas of the city.





The hotel has an excellent massage room. You must pay for his visit. Here, Thai massage specialists will carry out the whole complex of procedures, allowing you to relax and stock up on a charge of vivacity and positive.

Outdoor activities and excursions

Tourists who have come to rest at the Romeo Palace 3 * hotel often visit the city center of Pattaya. To get there, you can walk or take a ride on local transport. In the evening, cars are not allowed in the central streets of the city, which allows tourists to relax in a relaxed atmosphere. There are many institutions where discos or various show programs are held. In Pattaya there are a lot of cafes, bars and restaurants, which allows you to try international dishes and taste local specialties.

Very often tourists visit the popular beaches of the city. You can relax comfortably on Jomtien, where locals are rarely found. This beach is often visited only by hotel guests. You can also visit the islands by ferry, where there is a cleaner sea. There, tourists have the opportunity to enjoy the picturesque landscapes of local nature.

Guests of The Romeo Palace 3 * Hotel often visit the Big Buddha Temple. It is located on a hill, so it offers stunning views of the city streets and nearby territories. Tourists often climb the hill on foot, but you can also get here by taxi.

A great place to visit is the Temple of Truth. Women from all over the world who dream of becoming a mother come here. In the temple there are Russian-speaking guides who will tell amazing stories about this wonderful place.





An indelible impression will be made by visiting the Nong Nooch Botanical Garden, where you can admire the delightful tropical plants, unique sculptures and exotic animals. On the territory of the garden, guests are given the opportunity to admire the performances of the local theater or watch a show program with elephants.

Different impressions remain with people who visited the Romeo Palace 3 * hotel (Pattaya). Tourists are also pleased that in the immediate vicinity of the hotel there are many outlets, restaurants and cafes. In addition, information in Russian is provided in such institutions, which makes it easy to find everything you need.
