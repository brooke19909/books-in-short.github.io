How many km from Volgograd to Rostov: choose the most optimal way

Rostov and Volgograd are located in the south of Russia and belong to one of the cities most popular among tourists. Each of these cities has its own sights and famous places where many people dream of visiting. At the same time, many travelers would probably like to know how many kilometers from Volgograd to Rostov.

Travel by car

Today, the best way to get from one city to another is by car. Knowing how many kilometers are from Volgograd to Rostov, and the path is 474.46 kilometers, you can easily calculate the time that will be spent on the road.

If you move at an average speed of 60 kilometers per hour, then it will take about 8 hours to overcome the path from Volgograd to Rostov. If the speed of the car is 90 kilometers per hour, then the travel time will be more than 5 hours. And if the driver hurries and drives a car at a speed of 110 kilometers per hour, then the travel time will be approximately 4 hours 30 minutes.
