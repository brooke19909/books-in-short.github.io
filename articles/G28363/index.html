The Ashlee Plaza Patong Hotel & Spa 4 * (Thailand, Phuket): hotel description, services, reviews

The Ashlee Plaza Patong Hotel & Spa 4 * is a relatively new hotel, which was built and opened in 2011. But already in 2014, it was decided to carry out the restoration and completely convert the rooms. So everything inside looks beautiful and new. Looking at the photos below, you can see this. However, it is worth telling about other interesting nuances regarding this hotel.





Location

The Ashlee Plaza Patong Hotel & Spa 4 * is in a wonderful place. Firstly, from the hotel you can literally get to the airport in half an hour, because it is only 36 kilometers from it. Secondly, the hotel is only a few hundred meters from the sea. And the beach here is wonderful. One of the best in Thailand! After all, this is Patong Beach. Its length is 4 kilometers. There are a lot of people, as well as bars and restaurants serving them. Patong Beach is an ideal place not only for a beach holiday, but also for active entertainment.

In the immediate vicinity of the hotel are local attractions. This is Patong Pier, Phuket Simon Cabaret, Banana Walk Mall. A little further is the boxing arena, the go-kart track, as well as many other interesting places.

Entertainment

While at The Ashlee Plaza Patong Hotel & Spa 4 *, you can spend your leisure time in different ways. Of course, many people come here to sunbathe and swim. But the hotel has a lot of entertainment. In addition to the outdoor swimming pool and sun terrace, which are available in every self-respecting hotel, the complex has its own SPA center. Which, in principle, is clear from the name.

The SPA center has a massage room and several treatment rooms. Wellness complex So Thai Spa offers its clients to devote time to aromatherapy and cosmetology treatments for body and face. And there is also a fitness center.

Guests can also rent bicycles, play golf, go kayaking or go rafting. All this is nearby. You can also rent a scooter / moped, go snorkeling, full diving or go water skiing. In general, there is a lesson for every taste.





Service

The Ashlee Plaza Patong Hotel & Spa 4 * has everything that can make your stay at the hotel as comfortable as possible. From free Wi-Fi to concierge services.

So, there is free parking nearby, a left-luggage office, a tour desk and a 24-hour reception. Guests can use dry cleaning or laundry services, send / receive faxes or make photocopies of any documents in the office where there is equipment necessary for this.

A shuttle service can be arranged for guests. If they want personal transport, then you just need to go to a car rental (here it also is). The administration also organizes the delivery of newspapers, drinks and food to the apartments. By the way, here you can even call a doctor, if necessary.

The staff speaks not only Thai, but also English. If there is a problem with languages, it is better to bring a phrasebook with you. Nobody speaks Russian here, which, in principle, is understandable - the influx of tourists from Russia is small.

Staff

Naturally, the way the hotel treats guests is for many an important nuance. And in this regard, Ashlee Plaza shows itself worthy. Cleaning is daily, high-quality - not superficial. Towels are changed with the same regularity. Lingerie - once every few days.
