How to connect the radio in the car?



All of us, as a rule, like to listen to our favorite music while driving. But what if the car was purchased without a radio? How to connect a radio at home? This article will explain to you how and what to do so that you do not have to go to a car service.

As a rule, cars with minimal equipment are bought in order to save money, since not everyone has money for additional devices for their own entertainment. And then one day, you still bought yourself the desired device in the car. A reasonable question arises: "How to install the radio?". All we need in this situation is some tools, free time and the desire to follow our instructions below.

Action one

Naturally, the manufacturer can equip a car even of the cheapest assembly with at least a groove for this radio itself. Some even install a standard list of devices from 2 or 4 speakers and an antenna. Therefore, the first thing we need to know is whether this same kit is available. Otherwise, you have to buy it separately.

Action two

Having learned the vehicle equipment, we understand what we have and what we lack. We go to choose the radio. You can buy it at any electronics and car goods store. There you can get an initial consultation on how to connect the radio to a car. Our recommendations regarding the selection of the brand of the device are reduced to the following list of the most popular manufacturing companies:

  • Pioneer;
  • Alpine
  • Kenwood;
  • JVC;
  • Blaupunkt;
  • Nakamichi.

As a rule, the devices of these companies are not cheap, but this is fully compensated by sound quality and reliability in operation. In case you need a budget version, then the products of the following companies will suit you: Sony, Prology, Mystery. It is worth noting that the products of these companies do not differ in special quality and sound, but they successfully compensate for their shortcomings by the presence of USB, outputs for speakers and an amplifier, etc. As for the speakers, it can be devices of the brand Pioneer, JBL, Hertz, Kicx or something cheaper.

Action Three

So we are ready for the main action - the installation of stereo equipment in the car. Before starting the process, it is necessary to check the presence of connectors for connecting acoustics, power, etc., as well as metal skids. It is worth noting that the last of the list is intended for mounting the radio into the car. The most difficult thing is how to connect the radio, will find the appropriate wires. If there is a marking, then the problem is almost solved. As a rule, it remains only to connect everything correctly. Fortunately, in this situation, you can rely on a single system for marking wires and their purpose.
