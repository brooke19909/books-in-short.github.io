Adler train station: description, features and reviews

The evergreen, blooming Krasnodar Territory is the pearl of Russia. The warm Black Sea, the Caucasus mountain ranges, fresh air, rich southern vegetation - all this is located here. Thousands of tourists every year seek to visit this wonderful land. Some people drive their own cars, while others prefer to fly very quickly by plane. But there are many who like to travel by train.

Sochi - "paradise"

In the Krasnodar Territory, the most popular city is Sochi. It is located 1700 kilometers from Moscow. They sing songs about Sochi, compose poems and write poems. But he gained even more popularity after he was declared the capital of the 2014 Olympic Games. When this happened, Sochi immediately began to prepare in every possible way for such a bright event. Workers day and night were engaged in the construction of Olympic and infrastructure facilities. One of the most important tasks was also to ensure the comfort and safety of arriving passengers.

Accordingly, the restructuring of railway stations and airports has begun. Already by 2013, the construction of many facilities was completed - they grew like mushrooms in the recently empty areas. Local residents treated the construction of such a scale with understanding and patience. As a reward for this, they received the equipment of their hometown with all sorts of innovations in the sports world and an endless stream of foreign tourists. And not only the residents of Sochi were involved in all this action. The construction also affected the surrounding areas and localities.





What Adler is famous for

About half a century ago, Adler was annexed to Sochi and became its district. The main attraction of this settlement is the Olympic Park, which holds competitions in almost all ice sports.

The railway station of Adler was and remains one of the largest transport hubs on the entire Black Sea coast. Currently, it has a very large territory, which includes several buildings.

The first thing that catches your eye is the new station building, its area is more than 23,000 square meters, it is the height of a ten-story building. The main goal of building a terminal of such enormous size was to fully service the flow of passengers from all over the world who arrived in Sochi at the 2014 Olympics.

The construction of the new building lasted about four years. The project was developed by Russian and German architects under the leadership of Alexei Danilenko. The result was an ideal train station, which later became a winner in several categories of the competition held among the best facilities erected for the 2014 Olympic Games. The ceremony dedicated to the opening of this facility was attended by many famous people. Of course, the President of the Russian Federation Vladimir Vladimirovich Putin could not miss such an event.

Locals are very proud that they have such a wonderful train station. It is very pleasant to spend time here, almost like in modern shopping centers.





How many train stations in Adler

In addition to the new large terminal, the old building has been preserved in the city. It is somewhat more modest in size, but this does not detract from its architectural features. It was built in the fifties, when Adler was not yet part of Sochi. The old building has a very interesting and beautiful design: there are columns and stucco elements covered with gold paint, and the windows and entrance structure are made in the form of arches. Currently, in the old building of the railway station there are: a museum, a restaurant, a superior hall, a dining room, ticket offices, etc.





There are also small shops where you can buy something from the products on the road. But, according to tourists, the prices here are all too high. Therefore, they recommend purchasing everything you need in the shops of the city.

Description of the new Adler train station

The modern station building is a huge multifunctional structure in the form of a sail and is more like an airport. It is equipped with the latest technical innovations for passengers: escalators, elevators, flight displays, self-service terminals, electronic control.

When you enter the building, be prepared for the fact that your luggage will be inspected using special devices. Without exception, all passengers and visitors to the station pass the metal detector and personal inspection by inspectors of the relevant services. It is worth treating this with patience and understanding, because this is done for the safety of passengers.

Heat station is supplied by solar panels installed on the roof. Most of the building is located above the railway tracks, it connects two other large parts of the structure. Accordingly, you can get to the station both from the sea and from the city.

Inside the building there are many shops, an entertainment center for children, cafes and restaurants. Passengers can be accommodated in waiting rooms, there are several of them at once - superior comfort and ordinary. Another nice feature of the new building is that it has a unique viewing platform. Going out on it, you can admire the sea waves, takeoffs and landings of aircraft, as well as just breathe in the beautiful air. Stairs lead to the landing and an elevator is also provided. If you want to go down directly to the sea, there is such an opportunity. Baggage can be left in automatic lockers. Adler Train Station is also equipped with state-of-the-art devices for people with disabilities.





Train directions and timetables

From Adler you can go to many parts of Russia. Among them: Moscow, St. Petersburg, Nizhny Tagil, Omsk, Novosibirsk, Krasnoyarsk and other large cities. The detailed schedule of the Adler train station can always be clarified on the sites for the sale of train tickets or see the electronic scoreboard inside the building.

At the station, new beautiful electric trains with the name "Swallow" ply. They go to Krasnaya Polyana and Olympic Park. On the "Swallow" you can also get to the center of Sochi and to Tuapse.





Adler - Psou

Many tourists arrive in Adler in order to transfer to the train that follows to Abkhazia. This state is also popular with vacationers. One of the settlements of Abkhazia closest to Adler is the village of Psou. To get to it, the train will not be enough. You will have to transfer to the bus from the railway transport. The fare on this route is not more than 1000 rubles.

To get to this wonderful land, tourists stand in line at a customs point for many hours. On hot days it is very tiring. And yet, many find it worth it. In Abkhazia you can see the wonderful Ritsa Lake, Gegsky Falls, Alpine Meadows, Monastic Gorge and many other interesting places. And also taste delicious natural juices, tangerines and honey.





Adler - Gagra

If you want to go to this city of Abkhazia, then you can do it by electric train. It runs from Adler to Gagra 3 times a day.

It is worth noting that the electric train will significantly save travel time. Although the cars are not new, there are no dry closets and air conditioners, but you can always open the window and get there with the breeze. Travel time on the Adler-Gagra route is about two hours. The fare is two hundred rubles one way. Tickets can be purchased directly in the car.

The advantages of traveling to this city by train are such that you will not need to stand in line at the customs control. You will also avoid traffic jams.

Route: Adler Train Station - Sochi Airport

This path will not take much time. Sochi Airport is only nine kilometers from Adler Train Station. You can get in several ways: by taxi, by train or by public transport.

The first option is suitable for those who have a very late or early departure. At this time, public transport does not go, so you have to use a taxi. Such a trip will be the fastest (you can get in 6 minutes). But it will cost dearly - the cost varies around 700 rubles.





The longest way to get from Adler Train Station to the airport is by bus. Duration depends on the situation on the city roads. The cost varies from 80 to 100 rubles.

The most profitable way is to take an electric train. Travel time from Adler Train Station to the airport is approximately 9 minutes, the cost is only 65 rubles.

Railway station contacts

If you travel by your own transport and you need to get to the Adler railway station, the address in the search bar of the navigator should be entered as follows: st. Lenin 113. This is the main street of the city, and it will not be difficult to find the destination.

In order to ask any questions, you can find the phone number of the train station in Adler, call and find out everything that interests you.
