The most beautiful parks in Moscow

Every year our ancient capital is getting younger and prettier. Modern buildings, motorways and beautiful parks of Moscow appear , where you can take a walk in any season. Each season, they are beautiful in their own way. Each resident of the city chooses his own, the only square where he feels cozy and comfortable. In this article we will introduce you to the most beautiful parks in Moscow.

Boring Garden

According to the townspeople, all the beautiful parks in Moscow are ideal for photo shoots. And this can be done in summer, winter, early spring, when the square "wakes up." Beautiful parks in Moscow in the fall are covered with yellow-burgundy decoration and become even more attractive.





Neskuchny garden is amazingly picturesque. The park is located on the right bank of the Moskva River and is considered the oldest green space in the capital. This is a nature reserve. Newlyweds come here with pleasure. And everywhere here you can meet couples in love.

The romantic atmosphere of this park is created through a combination of unusually beautiful old architecture and amazing natural landscapes. There is still a legend associated with the arched bridge, which is located here. It says that in order for the union of two people to be strong and long, the young must hold hands and go under the bridge. And if you kiss here, then nothing will be able to separate the lovers. Everyone can conduct an experiment.

Beautiful parks in Moscow are very different. The Museon is located near Gorky Park. Here you can take a walk in silence, read your favorite book, comfortably sitting on special ottomans or sunbeds, visit concerts, exhibitions, master classes.

In addition, in the Museon, everyone can do hatha yoga, visit the open-air sculpture exhibition. I would especially like to say about the 60-meter fountain, whose width is 14 meters. Sparrow Hills

It is good to hide in the summer heat in the shade of sprawling trees. But many people love beautiful Moscow parks in the fall, when they are painted in a variety of shades, and fallen leaves rustle underfoot.

This park is located on top of the Sparrow Hills. It offers an unforgettable panoramic view - the Moscow River, Novodevichy and Andrievsky Monasteries, the Luzhniki Stadium. In addition, from here you can see the beautiful skyscrapers of Moscow City and the Shukhov Tower. In addition to vacationers, the park is often visited by artists who are inspired by the beauty of these places.



Walking along the promenade of the park, you will appreciate the calm and serene silence. This place is very fond of people who need privacy and appreciate nature.

Alexander Garden

Not everything, even very beautiful parks in Moscow, can be compared with the Alexander Garden. This is a real paradise located right in the center of the capital.





Neat lawns and flower beds, bizarre-shaped fountains amaze even sophisticated connoisseurs of landscape gardening art. The garden is located outside the Iversky Gate and covers the slopes of Borovitsky Hill. In addition, the park occupies the Kremlin Embankment, Revolution Square.

From the main entrance along the central avenue you can walk to the Sokolniki circle. From here, eight alleys fan out and cross the Crossroads. Thus, nine sectors are created. Various pavilions are located on this territory.

The park is famous for its rose garden and original floral design. Thousands of flowers are planted here every year and flower beds are planted. On the territory of the park you can visit an open concert stage, a library, several amusement towns, a dance veranda, a sports alley where it is easy to rent roller skates. For fans of billiards organized a room.

Catherine Park

This is an urban natural complex. The area of ‚Äã‚Äãthe park is sixteen hectares. There is a large pond in it, along its banks are cafes and eateries.





There is also a summer stage, where dance evenings and entertainment events take place. The Catherine Park has a rose garden, sports and playgrounds. A special attraction is the chapel of St. John the Warrior. Here you can see the rotunda of Catherine II, the sculpture "To the Stars" and a monument to A. V. Suvorov.

Vorontsov park

This park is located in the west of Moscow and is a very popular recreation area for Muscovites.

In the XVI century, the whole territory belonged to the boyar Voronts. Unique buildings of the 18th century have survived to our time: two outbuildings and two manor buildings, the Trinity Church, the guard tower and the gate. This place is a monument of park art of the XVI century. Numerous oaks and lindens have been preserved here since ancient times.

Not everyone knows that in the territory of Vorontsov Park there was a factory manufacturing balloons for aviation, in 1920 a biological station was opened on the territory, later a pig farm, and even later a football field appeared here. Until the end of the 20th century, there were barracks in the estate where people lived.

In the XX century, the park was in terrible condition and was brought into proper form only in 2006. Today, there are many sports and playgrounds, where rides for adults and children of different ages are equipped. There are magnificent ponds here. In summer, children frolic in a zorb ball. Horseback lovers have a great time here. They will be offered horseback riding or cute ponies. This park provides discounts to large families. Hungry in the fresh air, guests of the park can visit the cozy cafe.

Izmailovsky park

On the territory of the Izmailovsky Forest Park there is a pine forest, cozy clearings, birch groves, ponds and architectural monuments of the 18th century.

Here is located one of the most popular parks in the capital - Izmailovsky. A lot of attractions have been created on its territory, which are interesting not only for children, but also for adults: a Ferris wheel, playgrounds, a crossbow-beam shooting gallery. In the summer, the Akvaplan pier operates, there are rental bicycles and roller skates, an autodrome, and the rope town Panda Park.

In this article, we have presented to you not all the beautiful parks in Moscow where you can walk, breathe in the fresh air. Today in the capital there are new green recreation areas that everyone will definitely like.
