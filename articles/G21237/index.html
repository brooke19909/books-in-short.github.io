New Famagusta 3 * (Ayia Napa, Cyprus): hotel description, services, reviews

In the heart of the popular resort is the new Hotel New Famagusta 3 *. Ayia Napa will give you an unforgettable sea vacation and a rich entertainment program.





Location

5 minutes from the beach is the resort complex New Famagusta 3 *. Cyprus, Ayia Napa, Croy Nero, 18 - the address of this institution. The hotel is 60 km from Larnaca International Airport . Having overcome only 5 km, you will get to the famous Nissi beach. And 9 km from here is the Fig Tree Bay , which is extremely popular among tourists.

Remoteness from infrastructure and tourist facilities

A lot of infrastructure and tourist facilities can please travelers to Ayia Napa. Ayia Napa New Famagusta 3 * is removed from them at the following distances:

  • Thalassa Museum (1 km);
  • City Information Office (1 km);
  • Ayia Napa Monastery (1 km);
  • Church of St. Elijah (5 km);
  • water park (6 km);
  • Cavo Greco National Park (6 km);
  • Protaras Aquarium (7 km);
  • Botanical Park (16 km);
  • Othello Tower (17 km).

Hotel rooms

Your vacation will be not only bright, but also comfortable if you stay at New Famagusta 3 *. Ayia Napa is a modern developed resort, for the development of which travelers will need a lot of energy. You can fully relax and restore strength in the rooms of the following categories:

  • Standard room (18-21 sq. M.) Is designed to accommodate 2 people with the possibility of providing a berth for an additional guest. The room has 2 single beds, as well as a desk. There is a coffee table with a pair of comfortable chairs. The private balcony overlooks the pool and has plastic furniture.
  • Superior room (18-21 sq. M.) Means placing guests on a large double bed. There is a folding armchair for relaxation and an additional guest. The main feature of this category is the presence of a private balcony with sea views.
  • Family room (24 sq. M.) Is a spacious room. It is equipped with a French bed, as well as a pair of folding sofas. There is a lounge area with soft chairs and, of course, a private balcony with sea or city views. Some rooms have a bathtub in addition to a shower.
  • Junior (40 sq. M.) Is a spacious apartment that includes 2 bedrooms. One of them has a large bed, and the second has twin beds. Separately, a small living room with a seating area and a workplace is equipped. From the balcony, as well as from the windows of each of the rooms overlook the city.

Hotel New Famagusta 3 *: negative reviews

Any hotel has its drawbacks. New Famagusta 3 * was no exception. Tours to this place are in great demand, but not all travelers return from vacations with positive impressions. So, it is worth highlighting the following negative reviews:

  • due to the large amount of furniture in the rooms quite crowded;
  • hygienic cosmetics in the bathroom are not of the best quality (the smell of laundry soap is felt);
  • insufficient number of towels (for 2 guests - only 5);
  • not all rooms catch wireless internet;
  • the pool is too small;
  • breakfasts are the same every day;
  • the nearest beach is strewn with large pebbles;

  • wheels are screwed on the legs of the beds, which begin to ride on the floor with each movement;
  • uncomfortable hard pillows;
  • inflated prices for excursions (but, given the convenient location, you can see everything yourself);
  • To get the remote control from the air conditioner, you need to make a deposit of 20 euros

  • no room cleaning on Sundays.
