Car loan at Rusfinance Bank: reviews, registration procedure, conditions and interest rate

The car in our time has long ceased to be a luxury. At the moment, it is a means of transportation. Buy a car today is not difficult. This can be done both for cash and on credit. Buying a car on credit always raises a lot of questions. In this article, we consider car loans at Rusfinance Bank LLC, customer reviews on car loans and highlight some of the pitfalls that borrowers may face.

Who can get a car loan?

Today, taking a car loan through Rusfinance Bank is quite simple. But, despite all the simplicity, there are a number of specific requirements for the borrower. So who can get a car loan at Rusfinance Bank?

If you want to get a loan, then the following conditions must be met:

  1. The borrower must be a citizen of the Russian Federation and have permanent registration in the area where he intends to obtain a loan.
  2. Applicant must be between 23 and 65 years old.
  3. The period of official employment must be at least 4 months if a new car is purchased, and 6 months when buying a used vehicle.

Which car can I get a loan?

You can get a car loan at Rusfinance Bank for almost any car. For this, there are several lending programs with different conditions:

  1. "Fast and the Furious" - a program for the acquisition of a new foreign car.
  2. "Engine" - a loan for the purchase of a foreign car with mileage.
  3. "Great opportunity" - applies to new cars of domestic production.
  4. "Used car" - also intended for the purchase of a domestic used car.

Now consider these programs in more detail.

Credit program "Fast and the Furious"

The Fast and the Furious program was specifically designed to purchase a new foreign car. A loan agreement can be concluded for 24, 36, 48 or 60 months. The loan amount varies from 50 thousand rubles. up to 6.5 million. In addition, early repayment of the Rusfinance Bank car loan without penalties is provided. The interest rate for the program "Fast and the Furious" is from 15.5% to 17%. It will depend on the amount of the down payment. However, you can get a loan at a lower percentage by providing documents that confirm the level of monthly income of the client.

When applying for the "Fast and the Furious" credit program, it is mandatory to draw up a CASCO insurance contract.

Lending program "Engine"

The "Engine" program is designed to purchase a used foreign car. The loan is issued for a period of 24, 36, 48 and 60 months. The interest rate of the Rusfinance Bank car loan under the Engine program varies from 16% to 21.5%. It depends on many factors: the age of the car, the amount of the down payment, the cost of the car, the availability of a life insurance contract of the borrower, etc.

A definite advantage of this program is that it makes it possible to purchase a car from a private person.

Lending program "Great opportunity"

This loan applies to car brands: Izh, GAZ, IS, UAZ, VAZ, RAF, SeAZ, GURAN, BOGDAN, AZLK. As well as in the previous offers, the loan is issued for 24, 36, 48, 60 months. Interest rate - from 9.9% (GAP special offer) to 18%. When making this proposal, a CASCO contract is necessarily concluded.

Based on the reviews, the Rusfinance Bank car loan under the Great Opportunity program is one of the most popular products.

Used Car Loan Program

This car loan Rusfinance Bank is issued for the purchase of used cars of domestic production not older than 10 years. The interest rate will range from 16% to 21%. Particular attention should be paid to the fact that the maximum loan amount will depend on the down payment. With a contribution of 30% or more, the maximum loan amount will be 350 thousand rubles. for private cars and 450 thousand rubles. for commercial vehicles.

You can additionally familiarize yourself with special lending conditions for all programs at car dealerships and bank offices. It is noteworthy that absolutely all loans are issued in Russian rubles.

List of documents for a car loan

In order to apply for a car loan, you will need the following documents:

  1. Passport (required original document).
  2. One of the listed documents to choose from:
  • military ID;
  • tax identification number;
  • driver's license;
  • pension fund certificate.

In addition, the bank has the right to request additional documents: confirmation from the place of work, income certificate, etc. Also, loan programs provide preferential interest rates on a car loan if the client brings documents confirming his monthly income. This can be a certificate of wages from the place of work and other documents confirming the level of wages.

Loan processing

There are two ways to get a car loan at Rusfinance Bank: directly at a bank branch and at a car dealership.

To get a car loan at the bank, you need to contact the office, write an application and provide all the necessary documents. After the loan is approved, the money is transferred to even. An account must first be opened with Rusfinance Bank LLC. After crediting the required amount, according to the statement of the account holder, the money is transferred to the seller‚Äôs name.

The second option is to get a car loan directly at a car dealership where a bank loan officer works. After the client has chosen a car, an application and several questionnaires are filled out. Verification of documents takes about 30 minutes. If the bank makes a positive decision on the application, then the client needs to draw up a sales contract and an insurance policy for the car, make a down payment and pick up the purchase.

Car loan repayment

Now let's talk about repaying a loan to Rusfinance Bank. You can repay a loan in the following ways:

  1. With a bank card.
  2. Through payment terminals of Rusfinance Bank LLC.
  3. Through the QIWI service.
  4. Through the payment system "CYBERPLAT".
  5. You can also pay at the MTS, Beeline, Svyaznoy, and Euroset stores.
  6. Using the service "Golden Crown" (section "Loan repayment").
  7. Through Rosbank, payments on this loan are made without commission.

In addition, it is possible to make payments on credit and through the post office. But the payer should pay attention to the fact that a commission will be charged on the amount of payment.

If the payment was not made on time, the bank charges penalties in the amount of 0.1% for each day of delay. Therefore, we advise you to make payments a little in advance. Please be aware that sometimes payment processing may take several days.

We will also pay a few lines to the early repayment of the Rusfinance Bank car loan, customer reviews and repayment conditions. The bank gives its customers the opportunity to repay the loan ahead of schedule. For this, it is necessary to deposit the loan amount with interest. The exact amount of payment can be found in the bank or in the help desk.

Remember that the amount to repay the loan is written off strictly in accordance with the payment schedule. Therefore, deciding to repay the loan ahead of schedule, specify how much funds should be debited according to the schedule.

Who can make monthly loan payments?

Oddly enough, this question is very popular among bank customers. Monthly payments can be made by the person who received the loan, as well as his relatives, friends and other persons. In order to repay the loan, you must have a passport, a loan agreement (or a memo on paying the loan) and money. The whole process takes several minutes. After payment, the payer is issued a receipt with the following details:

  1. Surname, name, patronymic of the borrower.
  2. Number of loan agreement.
  3. The bank account number of the borrower opened with Rusfinance Bank LLC.
  4. Bank details.
  5. Amount of payment.

Proof of income

Some car loan programs offer a reduced interest rate if the client confirms their official monthly income. This can be done both by providing a certificate of income from work, and through the portal of public services. Confirmation through the portal does not provide for the collection of extra certificates and documents. In order to receive such confirmation, it is enough to provide the bank with the original FSZN insurance certificate, after which the bank employee can make a request.

Customer Reviews on Rusfinance Bank Loans

Today you can hear a lot of reviews on the car loan Rusfinance Bank. Unfortunately, not all of them are positive. Most disputes arise when the client wants to repay the loan in full or in part ahead of schedule. Today we will not find out who is to blame - an employee of the bank who did not explain the terms of the agreement well, or a client who did not read this agreement beforehand. We will analyze only the most delicate moments.

Payment of a car loan of Rusfinance Bank is made from a special account that the client opens when applying for a loan. That is, the client replenishes this account every month, and on a certain date money is debited from it. The account must contain the amount of the monthly installment. If the amount in the account exceeds it, then everything that is in excess of the amount of the monthly payment will be used to pay off the main debt. What to do if you need to pay in advance a few months in advance or repay a loan ahead of schedule? To do this, you need to notify the bank in advance that the amount that is deposited to the account is intended to repay the loan for subsequent months. In this case, you will have to visit the bank‚Äôs office and draw up a payment order on the account or issue it in an online account on the bank‚Äôs website.

When repaying a loan ahead of schedule, remember that the amount of debt should be calculated on the date the funds are debited from the account. For example, the agreement states that the loan is repaid on the 25th of each month. You have decided to repay the loan ahead of schedule on the 23rd. For early repayment of the loan, you need to find out the amount of debt on the 25th day and make it. If the amount of debt is calculated on the 23rd day, the loan will not be paid in full, and after some time the bank will begin to accrue interest on the debt.

Very often, bank customers who want to take a car on credit are invited to conclude a life and health insurance contract. This procedure is optional. This is only a service that a bank provides to its customers. However, when concluding an insurance contract, the interest rate can be reduced by several points. To issue this service or not - the client decides independently.

Mandatory when applying for a car loan is the CASCO policy.

But in order to avoid various unpleasant situations, before concluding a loan agreement, the client must carefully read its terms.
