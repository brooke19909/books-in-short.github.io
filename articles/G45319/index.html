How to choose an electric shaver: what is important to pay attention to

Many decide to purchase an electric shaver to replace obsolete insecure or safe machines, however, they are faced with the problem of choice. And here they need help. On the question of how to choose an electric razor, there are many important nuances that you should pay attention to, so as not to be disappointed in the step taken.



At the moment, the market is replete with a huge selection of electric shavers from various manufacturers, which differ not only in cost, but also in functionality, as they can provide the user with different shaving quality. To answer the question of how to choose an electric shaver, you need to understand their basic functions.

To begin with, you can consider the main differences that can occur in modern device models. There are razors with a mesh system, in which the process of shaving hairs is carried out due to the fact that the blades located under a thin metal mesh make vibrating movements. These devices are characterized by the fact that they shave much more safely, however, they are not very accurate. Basically, such razor systems are manufactured by Braun and Panasonic.



If we talk about how to choose an electric shaver, then it is worth mentioning the second type of systems - rotary. Shavers with this type of system shave off hairs by moving tiny vibrating knives that provide the cleanest, but not the safest, shave. The most famous manufacturer of rotor systems is Phillips.



Another important point when choosing such devices is the mobility and the number of shaving heads. And here everything is quite transparent: the more shaving heads are used, and the more mobile they are, the better the result.

When answering a question about how to choose an electric shaver, it is worth mentioning such a moment as the possibility of wet shaving. Electric shavers were originally created exclusively for dry shaving, but now there are many models that are equipped with a wet shaving system, making the process more safe, comfortable and soft. Phillips and Panasonic equip almost all of their shaving systems with wet shaving.

Another important parameter for many consumers may be the presence of a trimmer, which is a retractable blade that allows you to equal the mustache and beard to a certain length. It also allows you to cut your neck and smooth your whiskey.

Speaking about how to choose an electric shaver, you can touch on issues related to the possibility of working on battery or mains. The best option is when both methods are combined in the system, however, a high charging speed and a large battery capacity will significantly increase the cost of the device.

When choosing, it is worth paying attention to such a factor as the cost of consumables for each specific model, since often their cost is so high that buying a device loses its meaning.

So, now you know how to choose a razor, it is worth saying that you can prefer any manufacturer, the main thing is that shaving is only a pleasure.
