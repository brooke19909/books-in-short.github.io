Santorini (Greece)

If a couple in love is going to go on vacation, then there can be a huge number of options. Together they will feel good everywhere. But there is such a place on Earth where it seems that even the air itself is saturated with romance. This is magnificent Greece, about which many tales, legends and myths have been written. One option might be Santorini Island. Small in size, it is ideal for a relaxing and happy holiday. Just imagine for a moment such a picture: mountain hills, the dark blue sea, snow-white houses, colorful sand ... Do you still doubt that there is a more romantic place? We offer you to get acquainted with the most interesting places in Santorini:

  • The city of Fira. Every year a huge number of tourists come here to get acquainted with the history of this amazing city. Just imagine that you can see the individual buildings that were made in the eighth century BC. Among the attractions of particular interest is the ancient theater. Also be sure to visit the Archaeological Museum. You will have a unique opportunity to see the rarest exhibits.
  • Museum of wine. It is located in a real cave underground. Here you will not only have a fascinating tour, but also allow you to taste some local wines.

Dalat (Vietnam)

Waterfalls are one of the amazing natural phenomena. This magnificent spectacle can be observed completely forgetting about time. In the city of Dalat in Vietnam, there are several dozen. And among them there are those that you will not see anywhere else. Let's get to know them:

  • Pongur waterfall. There are several natural cascades. You can come here to not only enjoy the view of falling water, but also to take great pictures.
  • Datanla Waterfall. The peculiarity of this place is that you can get here both on foot and with the help of electric sledges.
  • Elephant Falls. It is considered the most powerful in these parts.

Kyoto (Japan)

We invite you to get acquainted with another unusual country for travel. In Japan there are a huge number of temples, ancient palaces, beautiful parks and other attractions. We suggest you take a trip to the city of Kyoto. This is one of the oldest cities in Japan. For a long time Kyoto was the capital of this country. Here is a huge number of attractions, parks, palaces, temples and entertainment centers. Among the most popular attractions that you must definitely visit are:

  • Kiyomizu Dera Temple. This is a whole complex of unique structures, some of them were built without a single nail.
  • The Imperial Palace. Its construction began in the eighth century. He burned down several times, and then restored. Around the palace there is a white wall with several gates. A magnificent park, where you can see rare trees and plants, will also be of interest.
  • Golden Pavilion. There are attractions that are worth seeing. Imagine that this structure is covered with real sheets of gold. On the ground floor there are rooms in which guests are welcomed. Paintings by famous masters of painting adorn the second floor. And the uppermost one serves as a place for religious rites. Near the pavilion is a garden and a lake. Tourists come here with pleasure even in bad weather.

Lapland (Finland)

Many of us first heard about this place from Christian Andersen's fairy tale Snow Queen. After all, it was in Lapland that the brave and loyal girl Gerda was looking for her unreasonable brother Kai. And yet - this is one of the most unusual places to travel. What is interesting for this place to an ordinary person? Finland is a country in which:

  • a huge number of protected parks;
  • favorable environmental conditions;
  • the sun does not set in summer, so the night here at this time of the year is no different from the day;
  • there are a huge number of resort areas;
  • there are the cleanest reservoirs, and you can even drink water from the tap;
  • Santa Claus was born in this country;
  • a huge number of lakes and much more.

We want to warn you right away that traveling to Lapland is an expensive pleasure, but it is really worth it. It is best to go here in winter. Be prepared for the fact that there is a huge amount of snow. Therefore, you can ski, snowboard, as well as dog sledding. And the most incredible and beautiful sight that you have to see here is the northern lights. Just imagine for a moment that the sky suddenly begins to be painted with all known colors. These amazingly beautiful flashes are constantly changing their shape and shades. Overflowing overflows can be observed for a very long time, admiring the magnificent natural phenomenon. People who at least once saw the northern lights dream again to admire a unique sight.
