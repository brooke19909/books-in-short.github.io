Rest in Ege Montana Hotel 4 * in Beldibi

Speaking about the hotel Ege Montana Hotel 4 *, it is necessary to pay attention to the village of Beldibi, in which the hotel is located. This is a beautiful resort town, representing a long street stretching along the Mediterranean coast.

A bit about Beldibi

Beldibi is a small Turkish village located near the Kemer resort, a 30-minute drive from Antalya International Airport. This is one of the large villages adjacent to the resort territorial zone of Kemer. In Beldibi, everything is designed for lovers of a relaxing, scenic holiday. There, climbing the neighboring mountains, you can observe the course of life below from above, admire the beauty of the Mediterranean Sea and banana groves. And sitting in a secluded cove, you can enjoy the tranquility, the sea and the sun.



sights

Once a small settlement Beldibi was the residence of sheep farmers and peasants engaged in the cultivation of citrus fruits and bananas. Thanks to German investors who ventured to invest heavily in a picturesque quiet place, Beldibi turned into an excellent resort. Now there are about 60 hotels of various levels, including the Ege Montana Hotel 4 *. Kemer, located 15 kilometers from the village, will provide tourists with all the delights of nightlife. Conducting quiet hours of relaxation in the village on the beach can easily be compensated for by activity on the dance floors of Kemer or on river rafting in the Taurus Mountains.

In Beldibi, hotels are divided into two categories: fashionable, with chic, loose sandy beaches and a large number of pools with an additional service structure, and budget ones, with rocky beaches and some restrictions on comfort. There is a municipal beach in Beldibi, which has deservedly received the Blue Flag award. This beach and several wild, inaccessible white sand beaches are especially popular among vacationing youth.



For fans of shopping, there is a large number of shops, shops and markets in this resort. Periodically, at the "Old Gate" there is an exit market with a huge assortment of Turkish goods. For those who are interested in major shopping, there is the opportunity to visit shopping centers in nearby cities: Kemer, Antalya, Alanya. Shopping tours are often organized there.



Relaxing in the Ege Montana Hotel 4 *, you can visit the caves discovered in 1959, where artifacts from the Paleolithic era in the form of cave paintings are preserved. And next to the resort, on Mount Takhtaly, you can observe an inexplicable phenomenon: the blaze of fire that appears unexpectedly in different places. Near Beldibi is the famous "Dinopark" - a place of entertainment for children and adults. There, on a vast territory, there is a park with Jurassic monsters moving and making wild sounds. And for fans of thrills, there is a 7D cinema.

About hotel

If we talk about the Ege Montana Hotel 4 * itself, reviews about it are different. But for the most part this is a positive objective opinion of vacationers. The hotel building was built in 1999, however, periodically reconstructed, the latter was in 2013. After that, the hotel acquired a modern look. The rooms were completely reconstructed, furniture, equipment and plumbing replaced. At the moment, Ege Montana Hotel 4 * (Kemer) fully meets the requirements of the time and the needs of the most demanding tourists. It works on the all-inclusive concept: breakfast, lunch, dinner - buffet, afternoon tea on the beach and snack bar, local alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks from 10 a.m. to 12 p.m. for free.

Service

The three-story building building consists of 113 rooms designed for the simultaneous recreation of 230 tourists. Ege Montana Hotel 4 * is located on the second coastline, 50 meters from its own small beach with sand and pebble coating, where for the convenience of hotel guests there are sun loungers, umbrellas and mattresses free of charge. The beach is small, but comfortable and cozy.

The rooms are equipped with everything necessary for a comfortable stay for a 4-star hotel. There is a fire alarm. Each room has a private balcony and air conditioning.



To serve guests in the Ege Montana Hotel 4 * there is a main restaurant, open from 7 am to 9 pm, and bars with a working hours from 10 am to 12 nights. The beach bar operates during the period of activity of beach vacationers - from 10 am to 6 pm. Great variety in nutrition. The hotel's cuisine is local and European dishes. Large assortment of desserts.



For use there is free wireless Internet throughout the hotel. For guests there is a large pool and a pool for children. In common areas, including the beach and pool, there are changing cabins, showers and bathrooms.

Animation at Ege Montana Hotel 4 * (Kemer), reviews are only positive, it is held regularly, every day. In the afternoon it is aerobics in the pool with an entertaining program, and in the evenings - comic and music shows, children's quizzes, Turkish night.

Additional services

For an additional fee, you can take riding lessons, visit a hammam, take a massage course, use the services of a hairdresser and a cosmetologist. If you wish, you can rent a cheap car directly at the hotel. There are paid services in the form of laundry, safe at the reception, filling the refrigerator with soft drinks, babysitting for a child, taxi, rent a tennis court. Any additional paid services at Ege Montana Hotel 4 * can be paid by bank cards of international standard Visa, Master, Euro. This is appreciated by tourists.

Rest in Ege Montana Hotel 4 * is a combination of optimal comfort, economical expenses and complete relaxation.
