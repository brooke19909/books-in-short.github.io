How to pay for the Faberlic order, delivery conditions

The production of Faberlic products is based on European quality standards. The company is one of the largest in Russia. This is not just a cosmetic company, it produces daily goods, clothing and medical equipment, presents its products in 24 countries, and delivers it to 42 countries.

How to complete and pay for the Faberlic order

Products can be bought in the following ways:

  • Through a consultant.
  • Pass a free registration and order products at a discount at the Faberlik online store.
  • At the point of issue of orders.

When performing any registration, independently online or through a friend, a consultant is provided to the consultant who will help in further work when fulfilling orders, etc.

Custom products

You can order products through the Faberlic online store after registration:

  • The first step is to choose a product. To do this, use the menu of the online store. You need to move the cursor over the necessary section and select the products that may be of interest (for example, "Clothing" - "Women" - "Homewear").
  • Next, select the product you like from the presented assortment. The card contains all the information about it: price, expiration dates, composition, reviews, etc. In the online store, you can use filters and sorting to quickly find what you need. If something is not in stock, you can use the "Notify about availability" function.
  • After everything is selected, you need to go to the basket and click "Login and Order" or "Register and Order".
  • The next step is Promotions. There are goods that are purchased at a special cost, at a discount. Their final price will be calculated at this step. Some promotional products can be selected directly in the tabs "Promotions". After the final selection of everything you need in the tabs, click "Apply changes" and continue to work with the site.
  • The final step is to click "Order Approval", then "Pay." If you need to return and select a few more products, you should click "Return" (on the left side of the screen).

Note! Products totaling less than 1,000 rubles. will have to redeem at catalog prices, without discounts.

Top up your personal account to pay for orders

How to pay for the Faberlic order? To do this, first of all, you need to replenish your personal account. This can be done through Sberbank of the Russian Federation.

There are two ways to perform such a function:

  • send SMS to 900;
  • through Sberbank Online.

Then login and password are entered, then SMS will be received to enter. In the menu of your personal account you need to select “Transfers and payments”, then “Payment for purchases and services” - “Goods and services” - “Network marketing”.

Choose what you need and step by step all the proposed actions. The personal account in this case will be replenished without commission.

Replenishment of the account can also be done through the bank cash desk.

Another option is bank cards.

In your Faberlic account you need to select "My Account" or "Top-up", enter the required amount, then enter the card details. The commission for the service will be calculated automatically.

In order to replenish the account via mobile, in the Faberlic personal account you need to select “My Account”, and then “Mobile Payments”.

Next, the panel "Replenishment of personal account - QIWI wallet" opens, you need to enter the individual account number Fab, then the phone number from which the required amount will be debited. Next, you need to specify the amount and click "Send".

Immediately after the operations are completed, a message with a confirmation code will be sent to the mobile.

You can pay for the Faberlic order through the terminal (payment systems).

They are installed in various stores, shopping centers, cinemas and other crowded places.

Using them is very simple, all actions are intuitive.

Anyone who wants to replenish their personal account in cash can do this through a bank, a communications salon, and some shops. You just need to know and name an individual account (registration number). A fee may be charged for such a service.

Payment for ordered goods

You can pay for the Faberlic order directly upon receipt. Of course, if there are payment acceptance points or PICKPOINT at the place of receipt.

After replenishing a personal account, it is necessary to pay for goods, because this is done only manually, the payment will not be automatically debited. So, you need to go to the page of the necessary order and select "Go to payment", then "Payment from account", then "Pay".

The Faberlic order can also be paid without replenishing the account. To do this, go to your order. Then click “Pay”, choose payment by card, enter information from the card and act in the same way as with the usual purchase of goods in online stores.

Ordering goods for unregistered buyers

How to fulfill the Faberlic order and pay for the purchase for unregistered consultants? For this, it is not necessary to go through the registration procedure, you can simply contact an existing consultant and select the necessary product from the catalog. Payment in this case is made not by the company, but by the person who is a registered user.

Product delivery

Faberlic delivery is carried out in a period of 2 to 7 days, it depends on the remoteness of the region and the method of receipt. These parameters must be selected at the beginning of the order.

Goods can be picked up at the Faberlic pick-up point, by mail, via courier delivery and PickPoint pick-up points. Upon receipt of the order, payment is required in the amount of 100%, with the exception of making the payment directly at the point of issue.
