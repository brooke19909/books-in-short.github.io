Hotel African Queen 4 (Hammamet, Tunisia)

Description: Hotel African Queen 4 was founded in 2006 in the picturesque city of Hammamet (Tunisia) on the Mediterranean coast. The six-story building of the complex, decorated in an African style, is surrounded by a tropical park, where sprawling shrubs (bougainvillea) with bright bracts, as well as fruit and palm trees grow of amazing beauty. The interior uses only natural and environmentally friendly materials - bamboo, sandalwood, rattan, which give the room coziness.



Guests will have the opportunity to independently visit interesting places of the resort, which are concentrated in the immediate vicinity of the African Queen 4 Hotel: Port Yasmine, Statue of Sirens, Hammamet Beach, Citrus Golf Club, diving center, horse riding school. You can have fun and plenty of dancing by visiting a disco located just a 10-minute walk from the hotel.

The following recreational facilities are within half an hour drive: Waterpark and Carthageland Theme Park. African Queen Villa is accessible from Tunis Airport (67 km) and Monastir (102 km). Tourists coming here will be provided with comfortable conditions for productive relaxation. The hotel can be recommended for family, romantic and business pastimes.

Rooms: The number of rooms consists of 234 rooms of various categories. Each room has a small balcony overlooking the outdoor pool, the city or the blue sea. The homely, warm atmosphere in the rooms offers a relaxing break. All rooms have a separate bathroom with a large bathtub and hygiene products.



The price includes business class bedding. For your comfort, air conditioning, satellite TV and a direct dial telephone are included. An electric hairdryer and ironing equipment are available upon request. Every day, wet cleaning is done in the rooms, and bed linen and towels are changed 3 times a week.

Meals: Meals are organized according to the BB, HB, FB systems - depending on the number of days spent. Morning meals and dinner are served in a European buffet style. On the tables there are always a variety of salads, vegetables, deli meats, fish, side dishes and fresh fruits.

Beach (own): 300 m from the complex stretches a sandy coast. On the beach, guests can use all amenities for free (sunshades, sun loungers, mattresses), except for towels - they are issued on deposit. There is a bar on the coast where you can enjoy a refreshing drink.



Additional information: The African Queen Hotel provides its visitors with an extensive and multifaceted range of services. A huge fitness club with new and modern fitness equipment has been built for sports and active tourists. You can strengthen your health and put your body in order in the SPA-salon. There is also a daily massage service, Turkish bath and Russian sauna.

In the evening, interesting entertaining shows are held with contests, jokes and dances. The building has an indoor heated pool, and a seasonal swimming pool is built in the open area . For young tourists there is a playground and a development club.

In public areas - free internet. Free parking, dry cleaning / laundry services, currency exchange, shopping galleries, travel agency and car rental are available. For business people - a conference room equipped with technical equipment.
