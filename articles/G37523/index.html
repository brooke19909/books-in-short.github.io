The technique of creating beautiful pages





Each page of the album needs to be designed so that the photo is striking, and its decorations perfectly complemented, telling the story of this photo. So, each element on the sheet needs to be positioned correctly. There are several types of techniques for creating a unique image of memories. Some scrapbookers use the so-called distressing, which means artificially aging the pages of the album. To do this, there are special emery sponges and ink. Scrapbooking Styles





Albums are mainly created for themselves. But scrapbooking can please family and friends by giving them a handmade greeting card with congratulations. Ideas for scrapbooking postcards. usually associated with a birthday, with some kind of anniversary, holiday or celebration. There are several styles for making a beautiful postcard or album.

  1. False ovary. Here, various things (brands, jewelry, floral arrangements) that were used in the chosen historical period are suitable.
  2. Shabby chic. Artificially aged objects are used here, for example, worn sheets, wrinkled pieces of fabric, and so on.
  3. Heritage. This style is used mainly in the design of old photographs.
  4. European. Its distinguishing feature is restraint in the choice of jewelry, and for the album - a large number of photos on the page.
  5. American. Here, on the contrary, jewelry is used as much as possible, the imagination is unlimited, but the balance for each page is strictly observed.
  6. Clean and simple. This style is akin to the European one, but if the first photo can be of any shape, then in the second - only square.
