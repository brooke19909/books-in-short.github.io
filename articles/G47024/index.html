How to knit front loops with knitting needles: step by step instructions, description and patterns

How to knit facial stitches with knitting needles?

The first thing a novice craftswoman must master is a set of loops. Traditionally, two knitting needles and fingers of the left hand are used for this (for right-handed people). There are a lot of recruitment methods and we will not dwell on this in detail.



The number of loops typed is usually related to the rapport of the selected pattern. For training, it‚Äôs better to simply dial an even amount and pull one knitting needle out of the loops. Now take the knitting needle in the left hand, and the second tool in the right. The thread should be wound over the index finger of the left hand and pressed with the rest of the fingers.

To better understand how to knit facial stitches with knitting needles, you need to carefully consider the following picture.





Here you can see that the first loop you just need to transfer from the left knitting needle to the right. Then bring the tip of the right under the first wall of the loop (it is indicated by the number 1). Grab the working thread thrown through the index finger of the left hand and drag through the loop. The result was a knitted facial loop. Further, the sequence must be repeated until all elements of the series are knitted.

Back looping

At the next stage, we begin to knit the purl loops. To do this, the blade should be turned. Thus, in the left hand there is a spoke with loops, and in the right hand - an empty tool. We remove the first loop, starting with the second, we knit:

  • We start the tip of the right knitting needle under the first wall of the loop (note that when making facial loops, the needle is inserted into the loop, and not under it).
  • We grab the working thread and drag it, forming a new element.
  • We repeat the algorithm to the end of the row.

Often, beginners from zeal knit too tight, so it can be difficult for them to drag the thread through the loops. In this case, they can be recommended to use thick yarn and knitting needles, one size larger than those recommended by the manufacturer (for example, No. 4 instead of No. 3).

How to knit the front "crossed" loop with knitting needles?

Often in modern literature mysterious "crossed" loops appear. They can be front and back. When the craftswoman works by this method, her canvas is more accurate and even, and there are no loosened loops. This is true if you have to knit a large fragment of a stocking viscous.

The essence of the method is that the needle is inserted not in / under the first wall of the loops, but in / under the second. This is not as convenient as the classical method and requires additional time. However, the resulting ‚Äúcrossed‚Äù loops form a tighter and more beautiful canvas.

Loop Manipulation: Expanding and Cutting the Canvas

When the basic techniques are mastered, you need to go further and get acquainted with the principles of the formation of a figured canvas. In the manufacture of almost any product, the craftswoman will have to reduce or increase the number of loops in a row.

Decrease occurs while knitting two, three or more elements. It shows how to cut one loop in the front row.

It is not always appropriate, as the abbreviated elements will be tilted in one direction. If you need to get a symmetrical sharp peak, act in another way:

  • One loop is transferred to the left knitting needle.
  • The next two are knit with one loop.
  • Pass the loop removed through two shortened.

These actions are necessary for the correct execution of some openwork patterns or for knitting armholes, necks or ridges of sleeves.

Adding loops: popular ways

Knowing how to knit front loops with knitting needles, you can add new elements anywhere in the canvas. There are three ways in which parts are expanded:

  1. New yarn loop. In the front row between adjacent elements perform the yarn.
    In the next row he is knitted (usually with the wrong loop) and a new element is obtained. However, when using this method, an openwork hole is formed in the fabric. Many patterns are built on this feature.
  2. The formation of a new element from the broach. Everything is simple here: a loop is formed when knitting a thread from the gap between the loops. The advantage of the method is the absence of holes.
    So knit raglans and other solid products.
