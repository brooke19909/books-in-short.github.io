Are you sure that you do not have a loan?

No, forgetting about it is quite difficult. Therefore, such information is most often of interest to employers and borrowers, as well as those who are worried about the machinations of fraudsters. Is it possible for you to get a loan without your knowledge? Yes, in recent years, scammers have stepped up and use any loophole. If you have lost documents at least once in your life, then it is likely that they were used for such purposes. So how do you know if a loan is hanging on a person? You just need to make a request. A printed story will show all the movements that were made by you, or on your behalf.

Where does it all start?

How is a database formed for borrowers? From the first day, when you come to fill out a loan application, all information is collected from you. Passport data, work experience, number of dependents. Now, for many years, the credit bureau will store data about each person who once took a loan. If you were a co-borrower or guarantor, you will not be an exception. Moreover, even if you did not take the money, but only made a request about this, this information will also be reflected here. As a result, it will be enough to take a printout and make sure that your story is completely clear.



What gives a credit history?

Can I find out how many loans a person has? Yes, quite if you get access to this information. A good story is the key to lending at a bank, and on favorable terms. Therefore, if you plan to take a mortgage in the future, they recommend gradually increasing the bank's loyalty. To do this, take a small loan for new shoes, household appliances. What is sanctified in history? First of all, loans. This also applies to what a person has already paid. Separately, you can get information on existing loans. Finally, you will see the dates of the appeal, as well as refusals, if any.

I would like to immediately note that these data are not subject to adjustment, especially on their own. Since it is possible to find out whether a person took a loan only with his consent, when applying for a loan, the bank will definitely let him sign a consent agreement. If you refuse to sign it, then even a bank employee has no right to look into these data. But will they approve a loan in this case?



Credit history structure

This is an ordinary document that contains comprehensive information. That is, the surname, name and patronymic of the borrower and his passport data. Tax ID and insurance number. Then follows the main part of the document, which prescribes all the information about the money taken. The dates of all messages to credit organizations and the responses of banks will also be listed there.

If an error is detected

Unfortunately, it happens. If you requested a story and found inaccurate data in it, or maybe just a typo, you need to contact to verify this data. Now the task of the operator will be to find out at what stage it was allowed. Perhaps they will require a printout from the bank in which you issued the loan. The operator will verify the data and, on the basis of the statement, will change it if it really did not correspond to reality.

Today it is the most popular among the population. Therefore, I would like to consider using an example how to find out if there are loans per person at Sberbank. There is also a privacy law. That is, only a person who has passed the identification can request information.
