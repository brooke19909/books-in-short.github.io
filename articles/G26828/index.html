My Sunny Resort (Hurghada): Detailed Hotel Features

Egypt is becoming one of the most popular holiday destinations for Russians. Moreover, tourists choose not artsy holidays with a full package of services. They prefer a solid middle peasant with complete freedom of action, without reference to the lunch-dinner and hotel programs. My Sunny Resort hotel in Hurghada deserves special attention. The resort of Hurghada, in general, is an ideal place for a diverse marine holiday. Active types of entertainment, boat trips, and easy relaxing procedures are available here. The main thing is that all this is included in the package of offers from the Sunny Resort 3 * hotel. The only thing worth remembering is that additional services are paid separately.





Sunny Resort is a family of hotels that balance Sunny Beach Resort 4 * and Sunny Beach 3 *.

Infrastructure

The hotel complex is located 3 miles from the cultural center of Hurghada. You can get to the international airport from the hotel in just 7 minutes by taxi or by requesting the organization of an individual transfer service.

The hotel is located in the first line from the sea in proximity to the sandy beach. The apart-complex has a restaurant and a hotel.

If you are going on a trip, you can rent a car.

For shoppers, there is a shopping center near the hotel with more than 15 boutiques.

Not far from Sunny Resort 3 * there is a clinic, from where you can call a doctor if necessary at any time of the day or night.

The hotel’s internal infrastructure is highly developed: the reception is open around the clock, the territory has its own secure parking for car guests, a laundry and a beauty salon, an Internet cafe and a games room, conference rooms and delegations.

Basic hotel information

In total, the hotel has 196 rooms by type:

  • single (110);
  • double (76);
  • villa (10).

Each of the rooms of My Sunny Resort is furnished to the latest standards and is fully equipped with necessary furniture and appliances.





The hotel operates as an All Inclusive. Possibility of placement on an individual package.

Detailed room features

Each apartment has an open view from the balcony or terrace to the hotel courtyard with pool.

The villas are equipped according to the euro standard. In the house: living room, absolutely functional kitchen, bathroom with a shifted bathroom.

Each room has satellite TV and air conditioning (as in the villa), a refrigerator and a minibar, a safe.

Entertainment available for tourists

Fans of extreme sports will definitely appreciate your stay in Hurghada . My Sunny Resort offers more than 20 types of outdoor activities. You can choose extreme diving or spearfishing, go sailing or water polo, visit the pool or take a dip in the jacuzzi. In the evening, the hotel hosts discos, and as groups form, day and night animation programs are not only for adults, but also for children (in the daytime).





Fitness lovers will find a lesson in the modernly equipped huge fitness center, and lovers of a calm and measured relaxation in the spa.

A team of experienced guides is ready to organize a group or individual trip to the desert, take tourists along the famous caravan route or arrange ATV races.

If you are relaxing with your family, do not worry about your child, he will not have time to mess out. While parents are resting, the children will be completely occupied with games: pirate races or treasure hunts.

Children will not be dissatisfied if you choose Sunny Days Mirette Resort as a place to relax. Here, young guests will be offered a special relaxation program and classes with professional educators and animators. Of the active leisure activities for children, swimming is available in a special children's pool.

Hotel catering

There are several restaurants and a lobby bar on site at My Sunny Resort. Even the most sophisticated gourmet will find a dish to their taste here. The main hotel restaurant serves an international menu and offers a wide selection of cuisines of different nationalities.

The lobby bar serves delicious desserts and refreshments, while the snack café offers quick snacks.





El Mahrousa - The hotel's restaurant offers a wide selection of seafood dishes.

There is also an Italian restaurant on the base of the hotel, where guests can taste pizza of any kind or replenish their strength with fresh salad.

While relaxing by the pool, you don’t have to run to the lobby for cocktails; here you have your own minibar with good drinks, and the beach bar has its own exclusive assortment of cocktails.

Beach Sunny Resort

Hurghada is a resort located on the shores of the Red Sea. The waters are quiet, and the water area is quite suitable for active types of underwater recreation. The Red Sea warms up well, its waters are calm, and the angle of inclination of the relief under the water is gradually extending into the depths. It is an ideal place for a family holiday in the warmest regions.

The beach at Beach Resort is sandy, regularly cleaned and belongs to the complex. The sand here is fine white, brought especially for the beach embankment. You can relax right by the water on a sun lounger under an umbrella.

According to the reviews of most of the guests, they have not met a better place than Beach Resort and are looking forward to a new meeting with the indescribable beauty of Arabian nights on the shores of the Red Sea, returning to Sunny Days.
