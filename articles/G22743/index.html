Children's camp "Ural gems" - the best camp in the world

Sooner or later, each child should become independent. You can do this by breaking away from your parents and going to a children's camp. The readiness of the child for his first trip is evidenced by his desire to go there. In addition, he becomes sociable, easily finds a common language with people and has many friends.

The optimal age for a trip to the camp, psychologists consider eight or nine years. But parents should carefully look at the child and evaluate its capabilities. Since Soviet times, Anapa has been considered the center of children's recreation. Here is the camp "Ural gems", which will be discussed today.





Location

In Anapa there are good beaches, a beautiful sea, healthy air, which is still necessary for children. Fifty kilometers from Yekaterinburg, on the banks of the Sysert reservoir, which is the most picturesque place in the regional center, the Ural Gems recreation camp operates all year round. Here, in a pine forest, there is everything necessary for children to relax and improve their health: pool, library, sports grounds, play houses and more. There is a camp beach on the reservoir.

Description

Here children from seven to fifteen years have a rest and are treated. Children's camp "Ural gems" ( Upper Sysert ) accommodates four buildings on its territory, each of which has rooms for six children. Each room has beds, fitted wardrobes, bedside tables and tables. In two buildings in each room there is a bathroom, washbasin and shower. In one of the buildings in each block there are showers, washbasins and bathrooms. Only in one building is the bathroom located on the floor. In addition, each building has a hall and a games room. The entire camp area has a fence, video surveillance is constantly conducted here. In the camp, linen is changed once a week, and wet cleaning is carried out every day.





Program

Children are led by counselors who organize various show programs, sports competitions, games and competitions for them. Sports competitions are held in football, basketball and volleyball, tennis, ping pong. In the camp "Ural Gems" (Anapa) there is a concert hall, where films are also often shown. Every night there are discos.

Counselors strive to make recreation for children diverse and unforgettable. Each year, they plan in advance to conduct various events that are unlike last year. Be sure to spend holidays such as Neptune Day, Russia Day and others. Children can also go for a free sightseeing tour along the Black Sea coast, as it is included in the package. For a separate amount, children have the opportunity to visit various interesting places together as counselors. It can be a dolphinarium, a waterfall, a water park or horseback riding, and much more. In order for the child to have the opportunity to realize his creative potential, there are creative studios in the camp that all children can attend, having tried their hand in different roles and activities. Highly qualified teachers and educators constantly conduct classes with children, the counselors are students of the Pedagogical Institute of Yekaterinburg.

Medical service

Children's camp "Ural gems " is a medical and recreational. Highly qualified doctors work here, a medical post is open round the clock, and there is also an insulator with wards in the form of boxes. The camp provides treatment for ENT organs, respiratory organs, gastrointestinal tract and musculoskeletal system. There are rooms for inhalation, magnetotherapy, electrophoresis, laser treatment, water and mud, massage and so on. Pediatricians have specially developed treatment programs for children using natural factors: air, sun, healing mud and sea water.

In spring and autumn, special attention is paid to the hardening of the body in treatment. To do this, conduct classes in the pool, use a contrast shower. It is not recommended to visit the camp for those who have acute diseases, as well as diseases that must be treated in a hospital, various infections, malignant tumors, tuberculosis, convulsions and amyloidosis of internal organs, as well as personality disorder.
