"Slavyansk Plaza", Montenegro: description, photos and reviews

If you want to enjoy a quality and colorful vacation, pay attention to such an option as "Slavyansk Plaza". Montenegro will delight you with its original culture, mild climate and incredibly beautiful nature.





Hotel Description

One of a kind can be considered a resort hotel "Slavyansk Plaza". Montenegro will open before you in all its glory, if you give preference to this option. The hotel is a kind of tourist village, decorated in a colorful Mediterranean style. Picturesque green gardens, laid out on the territory, organically fit into the local landscape. In addition, in the immediate vicinity is a huge beach, the length of which is more than one and a half thousand meters.

Only in the warm summer season does the hotel "Slavyansk Plaza" work. Montenegro pleases guests with warm weather from May to October. It is during this period that this hotel opens its doors to travelers. On a spacious territory, everything necessary for a good rest is collected. Among 11 buildings, interconnected by small streets, there are shops, restaurants, bars, a market and much more.

Location

The Slavyansk Plaza is very popular among travelers. Montenegro boasts many resorts, but only this one will give you a feeling of real Balkan color. Finding an institution is not difficult, because it is located almost in the center of Budva. In 10 minutes you will reach the old historical part of the city, and in 20 - to Tivat airport. From here, the hotel can be reached by taxi or by bus.

Hotel "Slavyansk Plaza" (Montenegro) is located close to the following infrastructure facilities:

  • bus station (500 m);
  • refueling (300 m);
  • City Clinic (250 m);
  • local market (100 m).

Guest Accommodation

More than one and a half thousand guest apartments the Slavyansk Plaza Hotel is ready to offer travelers. Montenegro will seem even more attractive if you have the opportunity to live in comfortable apartments in the following categories:

  • The standard room with two or one large bed has a compact area, which comfortably accommodates 2 guests. There is a dressing table, a nightstand with a TV and an easy chair. The balcony has a tea table and garden chairs.
  • The Junior Suite, unlike the previous category, has a fairly spacious area. Thanks to this, it becomes possible to install a soft sofa, which serves both for relaxation and for accommodating an additional guest.
  • Luxury rooms are ideal for families, as well as for those who are accustomed to luxury and comfort. The cozy bright bedroom has a double bed. There is also an additional bedroom for one guest. The huge living room serves at the same time as a place for relaxation, a kitchen and a dining room. On the balcony you can sit on comfortable rattan chairs.
  • Presidential apartments feature a large area, a high level of comfort, as well as an elegant design. The bedroom and living room are characterized by sophisticated design, done in bright colors, as well as diluted with bright details. The bathroom is equipped with both a shower and a bath. The terrace has elegant wrought iron furniture.

You will not remain hungry if you choose for your vacation such an option as "Slavyansk Plaza". Montenegro is famous for its interesting culinary traditions, which you can find in the institutions of this hotel. The All Inclusive system implies the following points:

Helpful information

Extremely popular is a tourist destination such as Montenegro, Budva. "Slavyansk Plaza" is characterized by some internal rules, which will be useful for travelers to get acquainted with, namely:

General impression

The resort complex "Slavyansk Plaza" 3 * makes a rather mixed impression. Montenegro is an incredibly colorful country that attracts many tourists. If you want to plunge into an authentic atmosphere, then this institution is perfect for you. The territory will remind you of a cozy Balkan town with many winding streets, greenery and other pleasant moments. In addition, this hotel is located in the very center of Budva within walking distance from the beach and the old part of the city. Thus, in terms of exploring the local flavor and attractions, the hotel is simply perfect.

Talking about negative points is always unpleasant. Nevertheless, keep in mind that if you are accustomed to comfort and quality service, then Slavyansk Plaza (4 stars, Montenegro) will not suit you. One of the main drawbacks is the quality of cleaning (or rather, its almost complete absence), as well as the terrible nutrition (do not spend money on full board). In addition, quite rarely in resort hotels you can meet the complete indifference of the staff to the guests. By the way, the number of employees does not correspond to the scale of the hotel. And the beach, along with the pools, is not able to accommodate everyone who wants to splash in the water.

Thus, if you are one of those tourists who do not pay attention to minor troubles and like to organize their own leisure, you will spend unforgettable days at this hotel. However, if you are used to a high-class Turkish or Egyptian service, you will most likely be disappointed.
