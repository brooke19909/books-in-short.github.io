Dimensions of the top-loading washing machine: overview and selection tips

A washing machine is an indispensable element in the life of a modern person. It saves time, reduces the complexity of homework. Sometimes the quality of washing in a machine is much better than with your hands. The presence of additional functions allows you to use a balcony or bathroom, not only for drying clothes. Sometimes after washing the clothes do not need to be ironed - if you have and start the program ‚Äústeam treatment‚Äù or ‚Äúeasy ironing‚Äù.

Consumers choose the right unit among a huge number of devices for washing clothes. Automatic machines can be classified according to a number of signs, one of them is the type of load. There is the following division according to the methods of laying clothes - with the frontal and vertical way.

Reasons for choosing narrow top-loading washing machines

Before buying, consumers must determine the place to install this electrical appliance. Keep in mind that when loading clothes, you must fully open access to the drum. The usual depth for machines with horizontal laying of things is 45 cm. But opening the hatch adds another 40 cm. For many modern appliances, they have a larger diameter, so additional space is required for the full opening and distribution of clothes inside.

Small-sized washing machines with vertical loading are chosen by those who do not have space on the sides of the washing unit. Usually these are small bathrooms. The opening of such devices takes place in the upward direction. Therefore, the lid should always be free, do not put powder on it, put towels or other little things.

Accommodation in apartments, houses

It is extremely rare to install top-loading washing machines in the kitchen. Usually they place electrical appliances with a horizontal way of laying clothes. In the latter case, the upper part is built into cabinets or covered with a worktop, can be used as an additional working area.

Traditionally, appliances with loading laundry through the upper part are placed in bathrooms without cabinets and shelves above the covers. They should open freely to gain full access to the drum.

general characteristics

They produce and buy devices with vertical loading less than with horizontal ones. The introduction of additional functions occurs later in comparison with machines that have a frontal way of laying things.

The maximum load weight is traditionally 6-7 kg of laundry. In a narrow machine, it is impossible to install a drum for laying 10-12 kg due to the small size.

The highest spin speed can be 1000-1200 revolutions in one minute. In such periods, you need to be prepared for noise or vibration. This is typical for any washing machine.

The noise level during the washing process can reach 51-57 dB, depending on the brand. During the spin cycle, this type of electrical appliance is capable of emitting sounds from 74 dB to 79 dB.

All models of washing machines with vertical loading are produced in a freestanding design. They can hardly be embedded in connection with the features of the opening.

Modern cars have an energy class of A or higher. Drying group - V.

Many models have built-in leakage protection. Almost everything is a child lock. There is also a delayed start timer. Some models have up to 24 hours. There is an indication of error codes.

Most models have an electronic scoreboard. It reflects the amount of time until the end of the wash cycle, the phase of the work process.

Dimensions of top-loading washing machines

With a maximum load of 6-7 kg, the dimensions of the devices are almost the same. The width is 40 cm, the depth is 60 or 61 cm. The height of the washing machine is usually 90 cm. Weight is 60 kg. For example, Candy CVFTGP384TMH-07 has a depth of 60 cm, a width of 40 cm, and a height of 88 cm.

Before buying, you should carefully study the dimensions of the washing machine with a vertical load, especially in confined spaces.

Main programs

Each model has a number of built-in washing cycles with preset temperature conditions, spin and rinse times. Most models are equipped with pre-wash programs for heavily soiled items.

Many narrow top-loading washing machines have an extra rinse. In addition to the mandatory, programmed, at the end of the washing process, you can choose an additional one - to wash the remnants of synthetic detergents. The function is required when laying baby clothes or things of family members with allergic reactions.

Selection tips

First you need to measure the space for installing the washing machine and select the type of load. With a lack of space, washing appliances with a vertical loading method are usually chosen. Further, it is necessary to take into account such moments:

  • Be sure to read reviews from other consumers or ask consultants about the built-in programs. Standard cycles can be of the order of 2 or more hours. Therefore, it is worth considering models with the possibility of manually adjusting the duration of the process.
  • For washing heavily soiled laundry, it is better to buy a machine with the functions of pre-washing or soaking. To facilitate ironing, the ‚Äúeasy ironing‚Äù or ‚Äústeaming‚Äù functions will help.
  • Convenient appliances with an electronic display, which reflects the time and stage of the washing process. Pay attention to the energy class. And to purchase devices that allow you to save money on paying for electric energy.
  • When washing and spinning, washing machines can make sounds and vibrate. It is worthwhile to carefully study the noise level indicators, so that in the future not to feel discomfort.
  • Before installation and connection, it is advisable to read the operating instructions.
