How to insert a SIM card in iPhone 4: instruction

So, your dream has finally come true: you became the happy owner of the beautiful iPhone 4 from Apple. Such smartphones really stand apart from the mobile device market and deserve special attention. Getting Started IPhone



The first step in using a brand new smartphone is, of course, to turn it on. This must be done before inserting a SIM card into the device. On iPhone 4, the on and off button is located in the upper part of the case, like any other device of this company. The key must be pressed and be sure to hold it for a few seconds until the company logo appears on the display. After that, you will see a message saying that you need to make a special gesture to turn on the device. After completing all the instructions, your iPhone is activated.

Card installation



If you just purchased an Apple device, the first question that usually arises is how to insert a SIM card into iPhone 4. These smartphones are designed in a slightly more cunning way than their other technical counterparts, more familiar to us. You probably noticed that a small metal key comes with a communicator, a kind of clip. This is the key to installing a SIM card. Take a closer look at your new smartphone. On the right side on the side you will notice a small secret hole. To understand how to insert a SIM card in iPhone 4, you need to press the hole with this metal key. After pressing, you will find the opened slot under the card. By the way, in case of loss of the key, which happens quite often, an ordinary paper clip is suitable for you. When opening the SIM card tray, be careful to avoid damage, otherwise it will be very difficult to open it later. After installing the card, push the cover back until it clicks. The display must necessarily show the search for a mobile network. This means that the SIM card for iPhone 4 is installed correctly. If the device displays a message about the absence of a card, it is possible that its installation was still incorrect.
