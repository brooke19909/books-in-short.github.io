Museum History

The Tambov Local History Museum is one of the most ancient museums in the country. It was founded as a provincial in 1879, and its discovery was timed to coincide with the centenary of the formation of governorship in Tambov. At first it was located in the building of the public library, then - in the Naryshkinsky Reading Room.





Reviews of tourists report that the Museum Fund was mainly replenished thanks to the enthusiasm of the townspeople. They presented personal items, books, photographs, documents, furniture. Then the museum was under the jurisdiction of the archival commission, so the collection was also replenished due to its research activities. The exposition base consisted of archaeological finds, ancient manuscripts, samples of clothing, weapons and household items.





By 1917, several museums operated in Tambov. Due to revolutionary upheavals, it became necessary to combine them into one complex. This process lasted until the 1920s. In 1937, the museum receives regional status. Since the 90s. last century, it occupies a historic building, made in the style of classicism. The fund has more than 120 thousand exhibits; permanent and temporary expositions operate.

Tambov region in the XVIII - early XIX centuries

The exposition is dedicated to the tsarist period of the Tambov region. The formation and heyday of the province, the war of 1812, the Decembrist uprising, as well as eminent historical figures of that time - Peter I, Ushakov, Lunin and, of course, Derzhavin, whose name is of great importance for the city. In their reviews, tourists enthusiastically describe these exhibits.





Tambov peasantry

The Museum of Local Lore Tambov pays special attention to the theme of the peasantry. In a separate exhibition you can see the village life of the XIX and XX centuries. The collection is very bright: photographs, holiday and ordinary costumes, household items, work tools. A rare exhibit is a map of the province of 1862.

Nature Reserve

Tambov is located in the forest-steppe, due to which the fauna of the region is very diverse. The exhibition presents about 400 animal species: mammals, birds, amphibians, reptiles and insects.
