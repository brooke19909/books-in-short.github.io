Vacations in winter in Russia: where to go?

For some reason, many ordinary citizens have the opinion that normal rest in Russia in winter is not possible. Everyone is waiting for the summer. Of course, this time of year is the hottest, and the possibilities are simply endless. But an unforgettable winter vacation in Russia is also possible. Take winter like a fairy tale. Believe that the shining rivers and lakes, the snow-covered forests and the northern lights are the countless possessions of the Snow Queen herself! A winter holiday in Russia can bring more emotions than a golden beach somewhere in Bulgaria. The main thing is to try!

You do not know where to go in the winter in Russia? We will show you some ideas, and you choose:

- Moscow region;
- Baikal;
- Karelia;
- Altai.



It is worth worrying about holidays in the suburbs in advance, otherwise the best options may be missed. Prices will depend on the distance from the center (Moscow), the duration of the trip, the level of the hotel (boarding house, sanatorium), requests for additional services.

A special plus for those living in the vicinity of Moscow and in the capital itself is that there is no question of a change in climate. Therefore, the body does not have to spend additional energy on acclimatization. Best of all, a person heals where he lives.

Nature herself is calling you to set off for Moscow Region. Winter tours here are not only saunas with gyms. This is ice skating, snowboarding, skiing, paintball, specially equipped winter playgrounds, horseback riding, winter fishing, hunting. Any agency will offer you several entertainment programs and scenarios. You just have to choose. Winter Moscow Region is close to virgin nature, it is clean air, an intoxicating magical feeling of freedom from endless domestic cares.

Winter crystal clear Baikal is always ready to accept those who are tired of gas soot and dirt, who are sick of the city smog, who like an invigorating fresh breeze, sky-clear sparkling ice and a piercingly joyful sun. A trip to these parts will turn even the most incredible ideas about real outdoor activities. The set of adventures includes snowmobiles and dog teams, trips, familiarity with protected places and local customs. You will certainly be taught to burhan there. This is the most famous and revered custom on Lake Baikal. And the oldest. They say that those who learn to really burhan are not afraid of trouble on the way, because the spirits of Lake Baikal will become supportive and will protect along the entire road. This is a really beautiful custom. It is only for his sake that he should come to the protected places of Lake Baikal.
Winter Karelia is a place of rainbow impressions. She is always beautiful. But it acquires a sparkling gloss in winter. Karelia in the snow rim will conquer anyone. It is not without reason that snow tours to this corner are so popular. It is here that the soul rests and thoughts fly. Karelia can be called a reserve of this northern winter. This is a snowy fluffy land, not covered by the smog of megacities, not covered with salt and sand, and always welcoming. This is a wide variety of routes, both ski and snowmobile, local attractions, familiarity with customs. It is a surprisingly low sun and, of course, an indescribable, bewitching northern lights ... Karelia will appeal to both novice travelers and experienced, confident ones



Want an unforgettable winter vacation in Russia? Visit Altai - one of the cleanest places on Earth with truly pristine nature. Many routes have been developed here that make it possible to evaluate the metamorphoses of the landscape. This is a land of surprises that allows you to evaluate your capabilities and overcome new, more complex, lines.
