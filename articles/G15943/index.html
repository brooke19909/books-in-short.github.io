Where is the beach located?





A soft beach, a warm sea and picturesque coral reefs are exactly the criteria by which tourists are looking for a suitable hotel in Egypt. "Beach Albatross Resort 4 *" owns its own beach area, because the hotel complex is located directly on the seashore.

The beach here is covered with soft sand. They clean it every day, so it is hardly necessary to complain about the presence of garbage. There is a piece of sea, cleared of corals, which is suitable for bathing children. Nevertheless, tourists are strongly recommended to stock up on special beach shoes, since swimming and barefoot can be dangerous.

Deck chairs with comfortable mattresses and sun umbrellas are on the shore in sufficient numbers. Here you can take a clean beach towel. There is a rescue point where professionals work - bathing will be safe. By the way, for the most part the sea is calm, only waves appear from time to time.

Fans of more active pastime will not be bored at the Hotel Beach Albatros Resort Hurghada 4 *.
