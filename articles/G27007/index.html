Crochet Magic Cardigans: Tips for Beginners

If you want to learn how to crochet patterned cardigans, you should first fill your hand in loops and deciphering patterns. An important aspect is the ability to calculate the product so that complex details such as armholes, sleeves

turned out okay, well adjoined to each other.

The easiest cardigan

We recommend starting with a simple model to gain experience. Only a few loops need to be knitted to crochet a cardigan. Product schemas are not needed. This cardigan is knitted without a pattern. For its implementation, 300 g (3 skeins) of Crystal acrylic yarn will be required; hook number 3,5. The novice craftswoman should know how to knit an air loop, a crochet.

We start to knit (calculation is given for 44 sizes)

We collect a chain of 75 air loops. Then it is advisable to fix it by making one single crochet in each of the 75 dialed. Now we need to raise the row. To do this, we perform three air loops. This operation will be repeated each time to raise the next row. Now we knit 13 rapports on our ribbon, consisting of two pairs of double crochets, delimited by air (2 p / n, 1 w / p, 2 p / n). The whole cardigan pattern consists of this combination. Having connected 15 rows, we collect on each side another 8 air loops (for the armhole). It turned out the upper back to the armhole.





Shelves

Starting to knit, you yourself will be surprised how easy it is to create crocheted cardigans. For the shelves from the ribbon, we knit two halves in the opposite direction from the back, starting with four rapports. Then gradually add in each row half of the main pattern until you get 7 main fragments. Having connected 15 rows on each side, we connect the entire product into one canvas. Get a little vest. It must be knitted to the hem with one fabric to the desired length. Please note that cardigans crocheted from various threads, like any knitted products, are stretched. Expect this product to become 3-5 cm longer after steaming.

Sleeves

Sleeves fit from the head of the armhole (from the shoulder line). To do this, they collect three rapports, and then in each row add loops with a crochet, connecting them to the edge of the sleeve. If the sleeves are not yet within your strength, then leave your product without them, having processed the armhole with one or two rows of columns. Crocheted cardigans made of thin threads look great in a festive ensemble. The main thing in them is a patterned drawing. This model is created to decorate your image, and not warm in the cold.





Product Finish

This simple product is processed along the edge, like so many cardigans: crochet waves consisting of ten columns with a crochet (in one loop). It turns out a lace edge. It is necessary to calculate so that this element does not constrict the product. Better let your dens be denser.

Tips for making more complex products

Having created such a model, you will be able to crochet more complex cardigans. Just do not need to pass. As they say, the road will be overpowered by the walking one. If you like crocheting cardigans , patterns for more complex patterns can be found in magazines. Then follow the recommendations, carefully analyzing the sequence of loops. And do not have time to look around, how you will show off in an exclusive outfit created with your own hands!
