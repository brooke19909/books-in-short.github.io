Porto Plakias Hotel 3 * (Greece, Crete): hotel description, services, reviews

Rest - each of us dreams of it after a busy and tiring working year. What it will be, is up to you to decide. Someone wants to go to the most quiet and peaceful place, others are looking for adventure. But whatever the taste preferences of tourists, most of them give preference to southern destinations, where they will have the opportunity to spend several days on the sea coast and enjoy the rays of the warm and gentle sun. The choice is quite multifaceted, and here everyone must decide for himself what he expects from the rest. If you plan to stay on a paradise island, count on first-class service, developed infrastructure and a rich excursion program, why not go to the Greek island of Crete?





Crete is an island washed by several seas, the center of the intersection of Europe and Asia, for which this place is considered one of the most unique on the whole planet. Gorgeous nature, an abundance of greenery and stunning landscape - this is what all tourists who have returned from Crete celebrate. Renting a car here is a great opportunity to travel around the whole island and enjoy its most beautiful places. Crete is a great place to relax with children, and curious, active tourists who are not ready to spend their whole vacation exclusively on the beach will also like it. As for hotels, there are plenty of them: there are quite modest apartments and chic villas, huge hotels with amazing service. We want to bring to the attention of all interested tourists a mid-range hotel Porto Plakias Hotel 3 *. It can often be found in offers of the largest tour operators. And the reviews of real vacationers will help to evaluate it from all sides.

About hotel

The small but quite comfortable hotel Porto Plakias Hotel 3 * (Crete, Rethymno, Plakias) was opened in 2005 and is ideal for a relaxing family vacation, including with young children. It is literally buried in greenery, located near the sea, which is why it attracts the attention of many modern tourists.





Location

The hotel is located in the village of Plakias in the province of Rethymno. This is one of the best places on the south coast of the island. Receiving every year thousands of guests from various corners of the globe, he managed to maintain his uniqueness and color. Miniature cute houses surrounded the port and the bay near it. The winding streets from each hotel and hotel lead to a great beach. And from the local taverns comes a stunning aroma of unique culinary masterpieces. Tourists note that this place is ideal for a romantic, relaxing and secluded vacation, lovers of parties and nightlife should take a closer look at other resorts.

Rooms

Of course, even those tourists who plan to spend only nights in the hotel are interested in the condition and arrangement of rooms at the Porto Plakias Hotel 3 *. Reviews of tourists only confirm that this is a very small hotel. Its room fund is represented by only 24 rooms, designed for two and three guests.





The technical equipment of the rooms is provided by air conditioning, satellite TV, mini fridge, telephone. Air conditioning perfectly copes with cooling the air in the room, which is so necessary in the conditions of suffocating heat. If you suddenly get bored and want to spend the evening in the company of a TV, there are three Russian channels at your service with an excellent signal - you will not miss your favorite shows and films. Each room has a balcony with stunning views of the local beauty. All tourists will be able to take water and hygiene procedures in a private bathroom with a hairdryer and a set of necessary bath accessories. Vacationers, of course, will appreciate the presence in the rooms of the Hotel Porto Plakias Hotel 3 * safe for storing valuables, documents and money.

The rooms are bright and airy, with stunning views of the mountains and the sea - a great start to the day. Signs of fresh repairs are noticeable: the walls are painted in pastel colors, creating an enveloping and pacifying atmosphere in the house. The furniture has wide comfortable beds, a table, chairs, a TV stand, as well as a relaxation area with comfortable armchairs. The condition of the plumbing is in good condition, there are no problems with the supply of hot water.





Room service

The hotel rooms are cleaned daily, towels and bed linen are changed every 2-3 days. Tourists note that cleaning takes place at a time convenient for tourists, most often during their absence. The cleaning is not of the highest quality, but the room is maintained in relative cleanliness and order. For an additional fee, you can order breakfast, lunch, dinner in your room or a romantic decoration.

Hotel infrastructure

What can you expect from a 3 * European hotel? Most often these are very small city-type hotels with a tiny adjoining territory and good service. But Porto Plakias Hotel 3 * will pleasantly surprise all tourists, without exception, with its developed infrastructure.

A lovely cozy and well-kept garden with a chic terrace and a veranda is a favorite place for vacationers. They gather here in the evenings, share their emotions and impressions of the rest. Interested in books? A good library awaits you, where the works of classics, as well as modern writers are stored - you will surely find something special for yourself here. Guests will appreciate the availability of parking. Car rental is a quite popular service on the island. But the hotel provides a parking place. It is under 24-hour security, so you can not worry about a rented car.





Service

This option also boasts excellent service. The reception is open around the clock, so questions are resolved in the shortest possible time. For all guests arriving at the hotel a little earlier than the deadline, the administration will offer luggage storage. High-speed wireless internet is available throughout the hotel. Using it is free for all vacationers.

Here you can rent a car, bike. Moreover, at the information desk, vacationers can get acquainted with the resort's rich excursion program.

Entertainment and sports on site

If you are looking for a hotel with an outdoor pool, this option is perfect for you. Of course, it is foolish to assume that tourists will spend all their free time by the pool, because there is a gorgeous sea nearby. But if you plan your vacation for the cooler season, the pool will be a great alternative to sea water. It is cleaned daily, for all tourists and vacationers using sunbeds and umbrellas is absolutely free.





Conditions for children

Hotel Porto Plakias Hotel 3 * is ideal for families with children. Children under two years old are provided with a separate berth for free. On the territory there is an excellent playground and a small pool.

Nutrition

All tourists and vacationers can eat in the hotel's luxurious restaurant with sea views. The pool has a bar with refreshments and light snacks. All tourists of this hotel note that food is not the strongest side of the hotel. It is not impressive in variety, and the quality is not the best. That is why, even at the stage of purchasing a voucher, it is worth choosing only room accommodation. On the coast there are quite nice and cozy taverns, a cafe where the best chefs of Crete will pamper you with their culinary masterpieces, by the way, quite inexpensively.

Beach

There is a public sandy beach just 100 meters away. Given that Placias is in a southern position, the sea here is quite warm and clean, making beach vacations as comfortable as possible.





A beach with a wide coastline, sand is fine, clean - what else do modern tourists need? Here, for an additional fee, you can rent sunbeds and umbrellas. On the beach, they sell soft drinks, ice cream, seafood, and there is also a rental point for sports equipment and water activities. Rescuers are responsible for the safety on the water of both adults and children - it is necessary to emphasize the high professionalism, attentiveness and vigilance of each of them.

To summarize

Porto Plakias Hotel 3 * (Crete, Rethymno, Greece) is a great option for a relaxing and measured vacation. Tourists will be pleased with a good location, first of all, proximity to the sea, as well as bright and spacious rooms, excellent service and developed infrastructure. A room without meals, namely these rooms are recommended to book by more experienced tourists, it will cost not at all expensive. Of course, Porto Plakias Hotel 3 * is not without some drawbacks, but it is difficult to call them critical. In general, if you are looking for an inexpensive but decent option for living in Crete, pay attention to this hotel - you will definitely not regret it if you reserve a room here.
