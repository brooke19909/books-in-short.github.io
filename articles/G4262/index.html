Nizhny Novgorod, Lake Svetloyarskoe: description, photo

In Nizhny Novgorod is the lake Svetloyarskoe. You can find it directly in the city, if you pass between the streets of Mokrousov, Gaugel and Svetloyarskaya. If we consider a more detailed location, the lake is located in the Balakhninsky lowland.



General characteristics of the reservoir

What can you tell about the beautiful pond, which is famous for Nizhny Novgorod? Lake Svetloyarsk is of anthropogenic origin. How did it come about? This is a rather interesting question. A pond appeared when materials for silicate production were mined in a quarry. They subsequently went to the factory, which previously operated in the city of Nizhny Novgorod.

Lake Svetloyarskoe has a sandy shore, although in some places there is also a muddy bottom. In one of the parts of the reservoir you can see a small island, measuring only 50x50 m.

Svetloyarskoe is used, as a rule, only in the field of tourism. Developed fishing and recreation. Popularity of the reservoir is added by the fact that there is a green zone near it. Shipping at the state level is not developed, but there is the opportunity to ride on small vehicles. They can be rented at the local boat station.

Ecology

Svetloyarsk lake is rich in crayfish, perch and pike, so we can talk about the purity of the reservoir. The water area that Nizhny Novgorod is proud of is Lake Svetloyarskoye. It can be clarified that it is included in the list of some of the cleanest reservoirs in this city.



Beaches

There are two beautiful beaches on the pond. Both comply with the standards and meet all sanitary requirements. Next to each are shops, shops, kiosks. It is worth noting that the infrastructure is well developed in the city of Nizhny Novgorod. Lake Svetloyarskoye has to rest, has all the amenities for this. The beaches are equipped with everything necessary and are in demand. Playgrounds have been built for children, and there is also access to water, where there is very little depth. Equipped for volleyball and indoor soccer.

The most favorite reservoir among tourists is Svetloyarskoye Lake (Nizhny Novgorod). Excursions, by the way, are held here quite often. The lake is located in the same reserve.
