What to wear linen sundress?

Summer linen sundresses have long been in fashion. This is not surprising, because they are very convenient and practical, they are not hot even in the midst of summer, they look feminine and attractive. Also, a sarafan is a universal thing, in it you can either just go out for a walk or put on some kind of event.

The story of sundresses





Sundresses begin their history in Russia from the 16th century. Moreover, initially it was purely men's clothing and was a dress with long sleeves, worn exclusively by the princes. The word "sarafan" itself came from the Persian language and translates as "formal attire." Then, over time, women began to wear it. There were a huge number of them: different styles, fabrics and colors. At that time, it was customary to tie sundresses with a belt that was above the waist.

In addition to everyday wear, a sundress was also worn at special occasions, such as a wedding. On such occasions, the beauties wore red sleeveless dresses, decorated with beads and various precious stones, or the so-called sandals, blue canvas dresses.

How to choose this type of clothing?

  • First of all, make sure that the sundress is light so that it is not hot in the summer.
  • Decide what you need it for. If you want to wear it every day, then choose a more classic model, and if you plan to wear it for exits, then choose a holiday option.
  • When trying on, make sure that the sundress fits well on the figure.
  • Although there aren‚Äôt any specific tips for color, you should pay attention to it. The most important thing is that the shade should be liked and suitable for you. Sundresses with a floral print are very popular, besides they look very cute and gentle.
  • If you choose a sundress for a trip to rest in a warm country, then it is best to purchase a strapless model, as they will leave ugly marks on tanning.

Why wear it comfortably

A sundress is a very light clothing, and the weather in the summer is volatile. For example, if you went outside during the day, and it was very hot, then in the evening it can become cool. What to do? With a light sundress you can beautifully combine a denim jacket, knitted sweater or bolero.

In terms of shoes, a sundress does not require anything specific. You can wear almost anything you want under it: sandals, shoes, ballet shoes, and if you want to create an image in a sports style, you can also wear sneakers. But it is best to choose shoes of a calm shade.

If the image seems too simple, you can add it with a light scarf, for example, from silk or chiffon. Heavy fabrics will look out of place.

If you want to add a sundress with accessories, then you can choose medium-sized beads of any length, lightweight earrings, some interesting summer bracelets. Sunglasses will not be superfluous. Also, hats with wide brim are beautifully combined with sundresses , making the image more interesting. But remember that your main decoration is a smile.

Since the flax sundress is very light in itself, the image itself as a whole and its individual components should not be too heavy and bright. In manicure, it is better to adhere to pastel shades or even apply a clear varnish. Makeup should not look defiant either.

What and how to wear

If you were invited to an evening walk, a date or just dinner in the summer, and you absolutely do not know what to wear, then feel free to choose a linen sundress. You can choose a model to your liking, there are many options: mini, medium long, on the floor, with a high waist, each type will look appropriate and feminine. In case your meeting drags on, you can bring along a cardigan or a light cape. If you want to make the look a bit more austere, you can wear a jacket over it. And if you want to plunge into a romantic, light and gentle style, then the lace model will help you. In addition, there are knitted sundresses made of linen threads, which also look airy and beautiful.





If you want to look bright, then choose a dress of juicy shades. It is absolutely not necessary to look at monophonic models only. Sundresses with different unusual prints and ornaments look very beautiful.

And if you laid eyes on a sundress of a gentle pastel shade, but at the same time are afraid to look faded? In this case, accessories will come to the rescue. But be careful not to overdo it with them.

Any model will suit girls with a slim figure: with sleeves-lanterns or overlays on the shoulders, in the style of Natasha Rostova or tight-fitting figure. A bright belt will help to emphasize the seductive bends.

Linen sundress goes well with leather jackets. The main thing here is not to overdo it and still look feminine.

Maxi sundresses are floor-length models. Sandals or open sandals work best with this item.

If you do not want to look shapeless, then it is better to bypass the long sundresses of a free cut. This is especially true for girls of short stature, since such models will only visually reduce growth. And sundresses are shorter without even the slightest hint of a waist look a little untidy, as they resemble a shirt. But they are ideal for a beach holiday.
