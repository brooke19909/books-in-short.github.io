How to make baubles from threads? Learning to do stylish accessories with your own hands.

Handmade woven bracelets - baubles - are very popular today among teenagers and youth. They are made of different materials: tapes, thin silicone tubes, threads. Braided bracelets made of multi-colored floss look especially beautiful and bright. The manufacture of such an accessory is dedicated to our article. Here we will tell you how to make baubles from embroidery threads. Review the guidelines below. Following them, you can weave fashionable bracelets with your own hands .



We learn to perform woven jewelry on the arm. Preparation of materials

Before we learn how to make baubles from threads, let's talk about the attributes necessary for work. In addition to the floss of different colors, prepare scissors, a thick sheet of cardboard or a thin plywood board, an office clip. If you don‚Äôt have the last one, then take a narrow tape. To decorate the product, you can use bright buttons, beads, beads.

How to make a bauble from threads? Method number 1 - simple



Pigtail bracelet - what could be simpler? Select three floss colors. Pull a double thread from each skein. Tie them all together with a knot, attach it with a clip or tape to the plank. How to make baubles from threads in the form of a pigtail? And then everything is simple. Weave according to the principle of braids on the hair of three strands. The product should be a length corresponding to the circumference of the wrist and plus a couple more centimeters. Fasten the ends of the threads with a bundle. Bauble is ready. On the arm, such a bracelet can be fastened with a knot or with the help of a special fastener. You can buy one in the hardware departments or simply remove it from the old bracelet or beads. If you want a product in the form of a volumetric braid, then use a floss folded 4-5 times.

Weave a bracelet of thread. Method number 2 (of four elements)

Now you will learn how to make baubles from a larger number of threads. Consider the technology of weaving a bracelet of 4 threads. Tie them into a knot and secure with a clip. Interlace the middle 2 threads as follows: left to the right edge, and right to the left. Now with those strings that have become central, perform similar manipulations. And so weave to the end. As a result, you get a braid of 4 elements.

Bauble "Chain"

To complete this version of the bracelet, you will also need four threads. Fasten them and weave according to the following instructions.

  1. The fourth thread is held under the third and over the second, the first - under the 2nd and over the 4th. At the end of this and each subsequent step, tighten the knot.
  2. We start the third thread under the first and put the second on it. As a result of this action, the 3rd element should be between the 2nd and 4th.

We carry the fourth thread under the second and above the third. In the end, it should be between the 1st and 2nd. Next, we continue the weaving process from step number 1.



You already know how to make baubles from threads and you can make such a decoration yourself. These bracelets are beautiful in themselves, but they can be supplemented with decorative elements: beads, rhinestones, beads, bright buttons.

According to the descriptions presented in the article, even a child will learn to weave baubles from threads. Take advantage of our master classes and delight yourself, beloved, with beautiful handmade bracelets.
