Ivanovo region, health resort Stanko: photos and reviews

The address of the health resort is Kineshma, Ivanovo Region. Sanatorium Stanko was known even in the era of the USSR, since there it was possible to undergo effective treatment for many diseases. Today this institution meets all modern requirements and there are necessary conditions for recreation both in summer and in winter.

Description

Accommodation is made in two buildings. There is also a beautiful administrative building in the neoclassical style, built in 1914 as the manor's manor house. In the buildings, single and double rooms of the 1st and 2nd categories are available for vacationers. There are also options for the "Suite" and "Studio" categories.





Separately from the residential buildings is the dining room building. In addition to the dining room and kitchen, it houses a post office, a shop, a bar and a hairdresser. Please note that in the summer, vacationers arriving by their own vehicles can use the free parking services.

Treatment

Sanatorium Stanko initially had a cardiological bias. However, there is also a treatment for diseases of the neurological, musculoskeletal, urological and gynecological systems. Among the procedures that you can go there, it is worth noting exercise therapy, Nordic walking, herbal medicine using oxygen cocktails, aromatherapy, classes in the sports and fitness pool and gym, as well as various water procedures (showers, bathing in the mineral pool, baths, sessions in "Cedar barrel", hydrocolonotherapy and much more).





Special Stanko Health Resort Programs

The sanatorium (Ivanovo region) offers vacationers several special programs ”:

  • "Antistress." This program lasting 7 days includes relaxation gymnastics, psychotherapy, spa therapy, classes in the swimming pool and a session in the magnetoturbotron. All these procedures are aimed at relieving nervous tension and preventing depression.
  • “A beautiful figure.” So that ladies can return from rest younger and prettier, the Stanko sanatorium offers a course consisting of a balanced low-calorie diet, facial massage, water aerobics, body flex, vacuum massage and spa therapy.
  • "Healthy child". This program lasting 6 days is designed to heal children who often have colds, with mild bronchial asthma, with atopic dermatitis, with foci of chronic infection.
  • "Cleansing the body." This program is aimed at normalizing the work of all organs. It includes a low-calorie, mainly vegetarian diet, a visit to the swimming pool, massage of the abdomen and / or other problem areas, sea pearl baths, herbal medicine, a session in the “Cedar barrel” with balms, a tuba with olive oil or mineral water, as well as Lensky irrigation.
  • “Silk skin”. Want to look younger and more attractive? Then the place of your rest should be the Ivanovo region. Sanatorium Stanko offers a program that includes diet therapy, consultations with a gynecologist and therapist, Fovever Yong complex facial skin care, a foam-licorice bath, a visit to the swimming pool and the intake of mineral drinking water from the pump room.

Summer fun

If you are looking for a quiet corner for a relaxing holiday in the bosom of native nature, then the best place for this is the Ivanovo region. Sanatorium Stanko in the summer offers its guests a variety of beach activities on the banks of the Volga. There they have a summer cafe, water rides, a tennis court and equipment rental. Although the beach is not wide, you can sunbathe there perfectly or lie quietly with a book in the shade of umbrellas in the fresh air.





Vacationers are waiting for beautiful fishing, as well as walks in the forest, where there are many mushroom places. In addition, in the evening they can have fun at the disco or sit in the bar.

Winter Fun

Sanatorium named after Stanko (Ivanovo region) is year-round. To ensure that guests were not bored in the winter, there was equipped with a 300-meter ski slope with a ski lift and a slope of 42 ° . Its capacity is 500 people per hour. Guests can also go ice fishing, visit the ice rink and use the tubing track and take part in the Laser Tag competition game under the guidance of an instructor.

Cultural program and excursions

Sanatorium named after Stanko (Russia, Ivanovo region) is attractive for vacationers for other reasons. In particular, they are offered an extensive cultural program. The resort hosts various events dedicated to Christian and state holidays, as well as throwing parties on the occasion of the Old New Year, March 8, Valentine's Day, etc.





In the surrounding area there are many attractions that can be visited as part of organized excursions. For example, vacationers will visit the estate and museum of A. N. Ostrovsky, take a walk around Kineshma, go to the Museum of Local History and Museum A. Tarkovsky in Yuryevets, as well as to the Palekh Museum of Lacquer Miniatures. They are waiting for a boat trip to the city of Ples.

Named in honor of the hero of the revolution, Stanko sanatorium (Ivanovo region): reviews

The impression that this medical institution makes on vacationers, first of all, depends on what level of service they are used to. So, in their reviews, people of the older generation who compare the current state of the number of rooms and the quality of services with those that were in the Soviet period, evaluate the sanatorium (Ivanovo region) named after the revolutionary as a revolutionary. As for young people, they find it boring there, which is quite natural. After all, this institution is intended primarily for treatment, and not for entertainment.





A variety of reviews can be read about nutrition, which many rate excellent, and for some menus it seems monotonous. At the same time, all vacationers note the exceptional cleanliness of the rooms and common areas, as well as the goodwill of the staff and the high level of professionalism of doctors.





How much is the pleasure?

I must say that at all times of the year vouchers to the Stanko health resort are in demand. Sanatorium (Ivanovo region) forms prices depending on the season. They depend on the room category.

You can get treatment either by buying a ticket with a full package of services, or by paying for treatment separately. Moreover, the number of days can be any: from 1-2 to the 21st, and if there is a desire, more.

Prices in the sanatorium also depend on whether treatment is included in the package or if it is only about rest. In the first case, in the summer, double rooms cost 2200 rubles per day, single rooms - 2800 rubles per day, "Lux" - 3000 rubles per day, and "Suite" or "Studio" with air conditioning - 3100 rubles per day. In addition, additional seats are provided, which are somewhat cheaper.

If the guest chooses the option of rest without treatment, then during the holiday season double rooms cost 2200 rubles per day, single rooms 2500 rubles per day, Lux - 2700 rubles per day, and Lux ​​or Studio with air conditioning - 2800 rubles per day.

Children under 14 years old when placed on the main place are given a discount of 25% of the cost of an adult permit.





How to get there

From the bus station of the city of Kineshma, which is located next to the city bus station, flights are made several times a day to the sanatorium named after Stanko. The first bus leaves at 06:25, and the last at 21:10.

Those who go on vacation by their own vehicles and who are not familiar with the Ivanovo region can find the Stanko sanatorium if they first get to Kineshma, then turn left at the direction indicator towards Navolok and travel about 20 km.
