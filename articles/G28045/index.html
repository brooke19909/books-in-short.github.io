A few secrets on how to pack flowers beautifully

Many are interested in the question of how to pack flowers beautifully. It is important to be able to do it right, because the gift should look decent. Currently, floristic salons are doing a great job of this. How you can do this yourself will be described in this publication.

Rules for registration

People who want to understand how to pack flowers beautifully to create an original gift should adhere to the following principles:

1. Packaging is an additional element of the composition, it should emphasize the advantages and hide the imperfections of colors, so it can not be brighter than themselves.

2. Spiral coating will visually make the bouquet more magnificent, richer.

3. If you want to know how to pack flowers beautifully, you should pay attention to the shape of the wrapper: it looks festive round and dense, while the triangular one is designed for mourning events.

4. Since living plants come as a gift, the material must be natural: rice cloth, corrugated paper, sisal, jute, etc.

5. Crepe material is unacceptable for making a gift , as it is intended for mourning.

6. Flowers wrapped in cellophane and decorated with an abundance of bows or ribbons from polyester look ugly.

7. It is necessary to show a sense of proportion, because excess jewelry can spoil the beauty of the composition.

How to pack flowers nicely with a net?





This decorative material is very common among florists today. The advantages of the jewelry in question are as follows:

  • ability to form and maintain shape;
  • softness;
  • flexibility.

In addition, a properly selected mesh is a wonderful decoration. There are many ways to form bouquets from it. Here is one of them: cut several identical rectangles and one long but wide strip. The elements are then distributed at right angles so that they form an acute-angled package.

An example of a bouquet of roses

Those who want to get an answer to the question of how to pack flowers beautifully will better understand everything with an example. To work, you need to collect a lush bouquet of roses in a bunch and arrange the stems one to one in such a way that they can be neatly and evenly cut. Then overlay the composition with plants with a white inflorescence. After that, it should be surrounded by decorative leaves, curled down. Then the bouquet must be tied with a spiral matte pale white ribbon. For the safety of plants impose a subtle mesh. Next, the composition is wrapped in pastel colored rice paper (before the buds). Its edges are cropped in the form of slightly bending waves. Finish the work with decoration in the form of beads or serpentine ribbons.

When creating a package, it is desirable to take into account the tastes of the person for whom the gift is intended, then the surprise will be even more pleasant. In this creative work, you can use different accessories: bows, rhinestones, beads, satin ribbons, cords, butterflies and much more. The main thing is to do everything with taste and not overdo it. So, the topic of how to pack a bouquet of flowers beautifully can be considered closed.
