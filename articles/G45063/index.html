Kirish, Turkey. Kirish, Turkey: rest. Reviews

7 km from Kemer is a cozy and sunny city Kirish (Turkey). This is a quiet secluded place where you can comfortably relax under the rays of the warm Turkish sun. The "lost" resort of the Mediterranean Sea is surrounded by the Toros Mountains, from which lush pine forests majestically descend. Clean sandy beaches, flowers and fruit plantations fill the air of the town with a unique aroma.

Geographical position



The village of Kirish (Turkey) is not a resort, but rather one of the districts of Kemer. It is 50 km from Antalya Airport (south-west). The distance from Kemer to Kirish is 7 km. The resort village is surrounded by mountains and the river Aqua, behind which you can see another cozy town - Camyuva. The coast of Kirish is characterized by geographic isolation: on the one hand it is covered by rocky mountains, and on the other - the river Aqua, resembling a creeping snake. The Antalya-Kemer highway is located on the back of the village. From the top of the Toros Mountains bordering the spa town, a magnificent panorama of Kemer opens. The relief of Kirish is diverse: the transparent sea and golden sandy beaches are surrounded by pine forests, pomegranate, citrus trees and steep cliffs.

Village History

Many do not know where the place of Kirish (Turkey) is located. The map clearly shows that the village is located between Kemer and Camyuva. A small resort town has its own history. Previously, there was a fishing village of the same name - Kirish. Turkey in the I millennium BC e. was subjected to numerous attacks by enemy states. In the beginning, there lived solims and terms, which were subsequently supplanted by the Lycians. They founded a new state called Lycia, which included about 70 cities. In the VI century. BC e. he was conquered by the Persians. In the 4th century BC e. the state came under the rule of A. the Great, and in the 1st century it became part of the Roman Empire. In the XI century, Lycia was conquered by the Turks.

The area where Kirish is now located at that time was called the Old Village. This name was not chosen by chance. It is connected with the geographical position of the settlement: there were many swamps and mountain villages. Among the local population there is a legend that in ancient times there lived a monster named Chimera, which caused suffering to the inhabitants of the Old Village. Over time, the settlement turned into a fishing village, and later into a small resort town with cozy hotels.

Climatic conditions

Good weather prevails in the village. Kirish (Turkey) is cut off from the surroundings by mountains, which ensures mild weather conditions. The Mediterranean climate prevails here. Sunny and warm weather lasts about 300 days a year; in winter, the rainy season begins. Tourists can swim in the sea for eight months. At the height of summer, the water warms up to +30 ¬∞ C. The swimming season starts from the end of March and lasts until October.



Beaches

Married couples with children, without hesitation, go to Kirish. Turkey is famous for its beautiful sandy and pebble beaches. In the resort village of Kirish, they are especially picturesque and cozy. The beaches here have a smooth entry into the sea, which is especially comfortable and safe for little travelers. Beautiful cliffs, spreading crowns of pines harmoniously complement the seascape. The coast in Kirish has a decent width, which avoids crowds of people there even in the midst of the season. Near the village is one of the most beautiful beaches of Kemer - Phaselis, as well as the picturesque Paradise Bay.

sights

Sea and mountain attractions are famous for Turkey. Kirish, whose photo rarely appears in a travel magazine, is not inferior in beauty to the largest Turkish resorts. The main attraction of the town is Mount Olympos (Tahtali). At the top of its slope, one can observe periodic fire flashes caused by the leakage of natural gas to the surface of the earth. You can get there by cable car and enjoy the stunning views of the panorama. In the center of the resort village you can see Villa Park, which includes a series of luxury villas. Not every tourist can afford to relax in such an exotic house. But the atmosphere there is truly picturesque: plants, palm trees, lagoons and pools of amazing beauty. All this is under reliable protection. Near Kirish are other attractions.



  • Paradise Bay, or Firefly Bay. This place is famous for its magnificent rocky landscape. In the dark, here one can observe on the water a soft radiant glow, which is created by marine microorganisms.
  • "Moonlight" is an amusement and leisure park located near Kemer. Here is a beautiful sandy beach, a picturesque promenade, attractions, a dolphinarium, a mini-zoo and other entertainments. You can get here from Kirish by any transport.
  • Ulupinar: a true paradise for lovers of fishing and delicious food. Locals here are breeding river trout and selling it to restaurants. Cafes Ulupinara - a favorite place for gourmets. Shopping

Kirish (Turkey) is a small resort village, so it is completely difficult to enjoy shopping here. For these purposes, the town has one kilometer-long street. Here are small shops and hotels. Tourists are happy to purchase souvenirs, oriental sweets, seasonings, textiles and jewelry in Kirish. The quality of items sold is different. Basically, these are inexpensive goods that are designed for tourists with an average income. In Kirish shops, as in any city in Turkey, you can bargain. You can pay for goods in cash and non-cash (bank card). But not every local seller likes the latter option. There are ATMs on the streets of the village. For big purchases, tourists go to Kemer or Antalya, where large shopping centers are located.





Hotels

Five-star hotels prevail in the resort village . Kirish (Turkey) is famous for its beautiful high-class hotel complexes. The territories of many hotels occupy a large area and resemble real parks. All Kirish hotels with adjoining gardens are perfectly maintained. The first coastline of hotel complexes has a very impressive cost. Hotels located along the road that divides the village into 2 parts are more attractive and affordable. In terms of level, they are no worse than hotels located on the first coastline. Most hotel complexes operate on the ALL INCLUSIVE system. In the evening, entertainment and animation programs are organized there.





Tourists reviews

Everyone who has visited the resort town wants to return there again. Beautiful nature, privacy, quiet atmosphere, high level of service - this is not a complete list of what is characterized by the village of Kirish (Turkey). Reviews of tourists are the best advertising. All the advantages and disadvantages of the resort town can be found out from people who have been there. Almost every tourist notes that in Kirish there are mountains of magnificent beauty and a clean gentle sea. The hotels provide excellent food, so the village is an ideal option for families with children. As for entertainment, there are not so many of them here as in large resorts. Kirish is more suitable for a relaxing holiday. Fans of incendiary parties should go to Kemer. But the village also has a couple of small discos.
