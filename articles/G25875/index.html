How to choose a LED lamp for home? LED lamps and fixtures for the home

LED lamps are a new word in the evolution of lighting equipment. More recently, they were rare exotic, and now they are firmly entering houses, and it will not be difficult to buy them in any enterprise trading in lighting equipment.

There is a steady trend towards the fact that LED incandescent lamps in the near future will occupy a leading position in the market.

The principle of operation of LED lamps

In electronic equipment, light emitting diodes have been used for a long time. It all started with light indicators, dashboards, information and advertising displays. The development of technology has allowed the use of LED lamps for lighting. From the incandescent lamp, the LED design is characterized by a minimum loss of heat when converting AC to DC. The luminous intensity directly depends on the strength of the current: more current - brighter light. LED lamp, 220V voltage, much more economical and safer than many other light sources.

LED lamp design

As can be seen from the diagram, the principle of LED illumination is based on the passage of an electric current through it. A special control device is placed in the device - a microcircuit.

A converter is also built into the design, which changes the current of the general power supply network to the operating parameters of the lamp components. The LED lamp (220V) differs from other alternative devices to the incandescent lamp in that in its circuit there is no energy-consuming component - a transformer. This affects the efficiency of the device. The principle of operation of the LED lamp is the same for all models, regardless of the shape of the ceiling, light and color radiation, power. Technical features. Cost-effectiveness

The main feature of the characteristics of LED lamps is a low consumption of electric energy, but at the same time a large light output, maximum light flux. The spectrum emitted by lighting devices is determined by the color temperature. This value is usually expressed in degrees on the Kelvin scale. The warmer (closer to yellow) the light, the smaller the numerical value.





A familiar, traditional lamp with a tungsten filament has a color temperature of 2700 to 3500 K. At the same time, the color temperature of the cold white glow emitted by LED fluorescent lamps reaches 6500 K. The low-temperature, yellow color is most pleasing to the eye, it is at a minimum degree blocks the secretion of melatonin. Having paid attention to this, the industry began to produce LED lamps of low color temperature, using a combination of various LEDs - LED-RGB. However, the price at the same time becomes incomparably high.

Heat generation

The characteristics of LED lamps indicate another technical parameter - the heat generated during operation. Its dispersion contributes to the radiator. This is an aluminum, graphite or ceramic part integrated into the device. In accordance with accepted standards, the operating temperature should not exceed 70 degrees. As a rule, the radiator is protected by a thermoplastic casing.

How to choose a LED lamp for the house in which dimmer devices are used? Most lamps do not respond to dimmer adjustments, but turn on and work continuously. Lamp life may be affected by the manufacturer's specified life. Usually claimed from 20 to 50 thousand hours. But practice does not confirm these figures. There is a direct relationship between the durability of the device and the quality factor of components and thorough assembly. Low-quality Chinese consumer goods breaks down in a matter of months. Well-known brands guarantee the normal operation of lamps for 3-5 years. This is due to the process of degradation of crystals, they slowly lose their brightness and completely stop the glow.

Another way to choose a LED lamp for the home is to evaluate the spectrum of its glow. Most lamps emit an unpleasant, inanimate white light. According to psychologists, it negatively affects the well-being. In the home interior, LED lamps of a soft yellow glow should be purchased.

Poor build quality causes pulsation of radiation. Such a flaw is not noticed by the human eye, but strongly affects the nervous system, provoking rapid fatigue. The presence of such a defect can be established using a mobile phone, its camera. The image of the pulsating lamp on the screen will blink.
