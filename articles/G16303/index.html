Top Dzhankoy hotels. What are they like?

The city of Dzhankoy is located on the Crimean peninsula. It operates a large railway station with a good interchange of trains, from there you can go to anywhere in the Crimea. There is a bus station nearby.

Dzhankoy - the main transport hub of the so-called "non-resort" Crimea. There are highways connecting the far north of Tauris with Russia and Ukraine. It is very easy to get from Dzhankoy to Simferopol and to various resorts of the peninsula. And not only there.

If someone still decided to get acquainted with local attractions, then you can stay in the best hotels of Dzhankoy. In almost all institutions, accommodation has a fairly low cost, while the reviews about being in this town are mostly positive.

For visiting tourists who visit the city in transit, an acute problem often arises: where to stay?

What hotels in Dzhankoy are available and where are they located?

Hotel "Chocolate"

It is considered one of the best places to rent a room. The hotel is located near the military airfield, on the outskirts of the city. Those wishing to stay for the night are offered single, double rooms, as well as a suite and a junior suite. The hotel Dzhankoy "Chocolate" is equipped with 50 beds.

When checking in, guests are provided with a bed, a chest of drawers, a chair. Equipped with individual toilet. On the ground floor there is a bar and a restaurant with various cuisines. Basically, these are Russian, Tatar and Ukrainian dishes. The hotel is also equipped with secure parking. A sauna and a pool are on site. Wellness services are provided. Room rates from 2000 rubles.

Address: Moskovskaya street, 148.

Two-star hotel "Tavria"

Hotel Dzhankoy "Tavria" is located in the former city administration building, near the railway station. The modest facade of a five-story building can easily be recognized by the national flags above the entrance. The windows overlook the Czech Church, which was built during the Civil War.

Comfortable rooms are equipped with split systems, modern furniture, shower. There is free internet. The capacity of the hotel is 80 people. Room rate - about 2000 rubles.

Location: Krymskaya street, 37.

Hotel Elbrus

Hotel Dzhankoy called "Elbrus" is located in the city. The rooms are furnished with modern furniture, climate control system and a private bathroom. There is no restaurant, so you can eat in a cafe located nearby. Room rate - from 2000 rubles per day.

Address: Lenin Street, 84.

Hotel Emerald

The hotel offers family rooms. It is possible to rent a bed in a room for several people, on the basis of a hostel.

In the rooms - TV, air conditioning, electric kettle. There is free internet. At the reception you can take an iron or a hairdryer, this is included in the room rate. There is a sauna for up to 4 people on site. It can be used for a fee. Room rate - up to 3500 rubles per day.

Address: Dzhankoy district, s. Emerald, st. Free, 31.

Hotel Solnechnyi Guest House

The hotel provides double rooms in guest houses with one bed and a flat-screen TV. The rooms are fully equipped for living. In the house there is: kettle, shower, air conditioning, iron, refrigerator, radio, kitchenette, microwave, washing machine. The toilet has all the necessary accessories, and free of charge.

For a fee, guests can use the sauna and barbecue facilities. There is also a massage chair. On the territory of the hotel complex is a year-round outdoor pool, there is free internet, a vending machine. Secure parking is equipped. On average, the cost of one house per day is from 4000 to 12000 rubles.

Address: Solnechnaya street, 4.

Hotel "Mini-Hotel"

There are single and double rooms with free internet, a private bathroom and shower. There is also a cafe-restaurant and a banquet room where you can enjoy delicious dishes and sing karaoke. A gym for classes is equipped on site, a beauty salon is functioning.

The hotel is located in the city at: st. Kutuzova, 34.

Hotel "Carpathians"

Provides its services when necessary overnight and rest. The rooms are equipped with air conditioning, TV, necessary furniture. There is free internet. This hotel has secure parking. A daily stay costs 2000 rubles.

Address: Sovetskaya street, 25.
