The most profitable roaming service: tariffs, features and reviews

When traveling to foreign countries, vacationers or business travelers have a lot of additional worries and questions: to complete all urgent business, prepare things, baggage, buy sun-protection products, arrange insurance, collect all documents, find out which operator offers the most favorable tariff in roaming.

In southern countries, the season is beginning. For example, in India, the first tourists have already arrived in the capital of Delhi for a further trip to Goa. Ski resorts, azure shores of island countries - all this attracts travelers. Therefore, the topic of choosing a tariff and a mobile operator may be more relevant than ever.

What is the "Best Profit Roaming"

The service of the same name was created by Beeline mobile operator for its subscribers. The option is valid in almost all countries. The company itself notes 3 advantages that can attract and satisfy subscribers:

  • does not require a connection;
  • simplicity of cost calculations;
  • payment upon use.

3 types of services are charged: incoming and outgoing calls, short messages and Internet traffic. At the same time, packet volumes are allocated for voice communication and data exchange.

Prices when visiting CIS countries

For travelers to Armenia, Belarus, Kazakhstan and other countries that are members of the Commonwealth of Independent States, the operator offers the following tariff conditions:

  • 20 minutes, which are included in the package for 200 rubles per day. After the end of the allotted minutes, each call will be charged at 10 rubles until the end of the day.
  • 40 MB for data exchange on the Internet, the cost is also 200 rubles. If the allocated volume is over, each 1 MB until the end of the day is paid 5 rubles.
  • The cost of outgoing SMS-messages is 30 rubles.

Tariffs for trips to Europe and some countries

The same cost is saved if you travel to such popular destinations as Bangladesh, India, Vietnam, Thailand, Germany, Greece, Italy and other European countries.

How much are calls, messages and the Internet in other countries

When visiting states such as the Bahamas, Bolivia, Virgin Islands, Haiti, Guatemala, Honduras, Liberia, Tonga, Uganda, Chad, Ecuador and others, the conditions will change:

  • outgoing short messages will be sent with subsequent write-off from the balance of 30 rubles;
  • Incoming calls are paid in the amount of 25 rubles per minute;
  • outgoing calls within the country, to Russia or other countries - 100 rubles with per-minute billing;
  • paid per megabyte and the Internet - 90 rubles / MB.

For China, Morocco, Tanzania, Tunisia, Nepal, Uruguay, an exception was made in the method of calculating for using access to the World Wide Web - as in other cases, a daily limit of 40 MB for 200 rubles is allocated. Write-off occurs on the first exit to the network. The rest (cost of calls and SMS) rates are saved in the amount of 25, 100 and 30 rubles.

Where the service does not work

A number of countries are classified as exotic.

These include Bahrain, Bermuda, Bhutan and several others.

When in these countries, tariffs will be very high:

  • incoming and outgoing calls can be made with the subsequent debiting of 200 rubles per minute of conversation;
  • sending short messages - 30 rubles;
  • Internet traffic is paid at the rate of 15 rubles for every 20 kb.

A full list of countries of a particular group can be found on the Beeline official website. In any case, it is worth noting that the rest of the minutes or the amount of data is not transferred. The days of the option expire at midnight local time.

Activate and deactivate option

For greater comfort of its subscribers, the mobile operator has provided the inclusion and deactivation of the service in automatic mode. There are no special actions for activation. When a traveler arrives at the airport of the host country, upon first registration on the network, the fact of being in international roaming is determined and the service is activated.

What should not be forgotten: the subscription fee in the territories where the daily limit is practiced is debited when making or receiving the first call within one day, and for the amount of traffic - the first time you connect to the network.

Is the service available when traveling in Russia

Not. The option "The most profitable roaming" is valid only in international roaming. If the subscriber travels through the territory of the Russian Federation, tariffing takes place on other conditions. If you are interested in the most profitable roaming in Russia, we are glad to please you: in 2018, at the insistence of the FAS, intra-network roaming was canceled. Tariffication should be checked with the operators.

How can you save on international roaming?

For those who do not want to pay for communication services of a domestic operator abroad at current prices, the following ways to optimize costs can be advised:

  1. Consider connecting a local SIM card. It is often much more profitable to purchase a mobile number of a cellular company in the host country than to pay for services at roaming tariffs. For example, in India, the monthly fee starts from 149 rupees (1 rupee = 0.89 rubles) for unlimited calls throughout the Republic and a daily package of 1 GB or more at maximum speed. The maximum cost can be 600-800 rupees for a daily limit of about 3 GB of data.
  2. If you are planning a long trip, check on the popular travel forums about the experience of connecting a local operator‚Äôs number, which documents are required and in what quantity.
  3. When planning a trip for 10-14 days, you can use the Internet via Wi-Fi in public places, a cafe or a hotel. You can get information from a tour operator, hotel administration or on thematic sites.
    Use messengers that work when connected to the global network to make calls.
  4. Disable the auto-sync settings on your smartphone or tablet, access to mobile data to avoid downloading data without your knowledge.
