Hotel Mercure Bay View Dahab 5

Description: Located in Dahab, Mercure Bay View Dahab 5 is about 85 km from Sharm El Sheikh Airport. The luxury hotel is located on the sandy beach of the Red Sea right by the water of the Gulf of Aqaba.

The multilevel complex, surrounded by greenery and exotic colors, cascades down to the sea.

The Sinai Mountains and the desert, which surround the hotel on land, create an impressive backdrop to the original architectural structure.

Rooms: Of the 217 rooms, 54 are non-smoking. Guests at Mercure Bay View 5 can choose a standard room offering a traditional range of services. Or stay at the improved version with a stone pool. Lovers of true comfort are invited to pay attention to Superior Suite apartments, which have a living room, dining room, bedroom, two bathrooms and a large terrace, from where you can admire the views of the valley, stretching from the Sinai Mountains to the sea edge.

Meals: The main restaurant View offers a buffet service with international dishes and drinks. It serves during all meals, from breakfast to dinner. Continental breakfast options can be ordered to your room.

Light food and all kinds of drinks, including coffee and alcohol, are always in the assortment of the lobby bar. Oriental drinks and an original menu are presented in the Bedouin tent. Refreshing drinks, fruits and ice creams can be enjoyed at the bar near the pool and on the beach.

Beach: 50 meters from the complex is a private hotel beach with comfortable sunbeds and umbrellas.

Additional information: The resort hotel offers a full range of services provided on the tropical seashore.

On the territory of the Mercure Bay View Dahab 5, magnificent free-form pools are equipped, one of which is indoor, and the other two are heated.



The hotel has a wellness center with various treatments, including spa, yoga, Turkish bath, massage. The gym of the fitness club is a great place to workout.

Fans of active leisure will choose beach volleyball, diving, windsurfing, aerobics or playing tennis.

Mercure Bay View Dahab 5 Hotel welcomes travelers with small children, for whom amenities such as a crib, baby food, high chair in the dining room, and babysitting services are offered . For children, a playground and a children's compartment in the pool are built. The mini club organizes educational activities for all ages.

In addition to the entertainment service, transport, household and medical services are presented. Here you can order a transfer, rent a car, use the laundry or dry cleaning service, visit a hairdresser, call a doctor.



Digest: Mercure Bay View Dahab 5, consisting of beautifully designed terraces, ledges descending to the water, offers the perfect vacation for people accustomed to active leisure. On site there is a good surf school and good facilities for water activities.

Well-groomed area with palm trees and dazzling bright colors, spectacularly shaded by views of the desert and mountains. When choosing a Bay View Hotel, it should be borne in mind that you will have to walk a lot, overcoming the levels of the hotel.

Spacious comfortable rooms - all without exception overlooking the Red Sea. Housekeeping and room service are top notch. The staff is friendly and helpful in everything.

Food can not be called very diverse and plentiful, but everything is tasty, and served in a normal volume. In the evenings, barbecue entertainment is periodically held.

The hotel does not have a large concentration of tourists, so it can be recommended to lovers of privacy.
