Marina Grand Beach 5 *. Holidays in Bulgaria - hotels

Bulgaria is especially popular with domestic tourists. Such mutual love arose even before the start of the perestroika period. This place has gained its popularity for good reason. Beautiful landscapes, the hospitality of local residents, the warm sea and a variety of excursion programs do not leave anyone indifferent.



General information

Currently, the tourism sector of the country is actively developing. Significantly improved quality of service. Many hotels have been renovated. In this regard, the prices of tours slightly increased. The city of Sofia is the capital of the country. You can easily get here with direct and charter flights.

Main advantages

The country is still considered an inexpensive destination. Rest in this place is available to the vast majority of tourists. All conditions for a comfortable family pastime are created here. Accommodation with children is quite inexpensive. In Bulgaria, you can significantly improve your health, and at reasonable prices. The combination of the above reasons makes vacation here so popular. Convenient geographical position also plays an important role (below is a map).



Golden Sands (Bulgaria): general information

The resort is located in the park of the same name. It is located several kilometers north-east from Varna (this is confirmed by the map). Golden Sands (Bulgaria) is an ecologically clean area. The resort is deservedly awarded the blue flag. It is ideal for those who want to spend their holidays dynamically. The resort boasts an abundance of various attractions and water slides for children and adults. Lovers of vibrant nightlife here will always find something to do. The resort has all the conditions for guests to relax and gain strength. However, it is important to know that mountainous terrain prevails in this area. Therefore, you will have to get to the beach on a steep descent. Accordingly, relaxing here with very young children is quite problematic.

Key Benefits

Currently, this resort area is gaining wide popularity. In this territory comfortable climatic conditions prevail. Most of the many tourists are attracted here by the developed infrastructure, picturesque mountain landscapes, the warm sea and clean beaches. Now, family vacations in Bulgaria are becoming more and more popular here. Hotels operating on the "ol inclusive" system are in great demand.
