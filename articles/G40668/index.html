How to activate additional traffic on the Beeline number

Using the Internet not only in everyday life, but also on business issues, it is difficult to imagine yourself at least for one day without it. Subscribers of the Beeline mobile operator can not worry about this because they cannot completely remain without the Internet, but getting a lower connection speed after exhausting the traffic is quite common for them. For watching news, e-mail, visiting social networks, a little speed may be enough, but for more serious actions (watching videos, listening to music, chatting, downloading files, etc.) this is not enough. How to connect additional traffic on the Beeline number and avoid a decrease in speed, we will describe in this article.

Beeline mobile operator offers its subscribers several options for speed extension. Recall that according to the terms of tariff plans, implying a certain amount of traffic after exceeding the limit, a speed limit (64 Kb / s) is included. To continue to use the Internet at normal speed, you can activate additional traffic on the Beeline number by connecting specialized options.

  1. "Prolong speed" 1000 Mb.
  2. "Prolong speed" 3000 MB.
  3. The automatic speed extension service provides an additional 200 MB.




Option Terms

The above packages that are used to add traffic are available on any tariff plan of the Highway and All series. Since the TP data provides for a certain amount of megabytes included in the subscription fee, it is necessary to connect the speed extension options only after the available limit on the Beeline number has been exhausted. Additional Internet traffic is activated on the following conditions:

  • a package of megabytes "Extend speed 1 GB" is provided for a fee of 100 rubles (one-time deducted from the balance of the number when the option is activated);
  • The megabyte package ‚ÄúExtend speed 1 GB‚Äù is connected for a fee of 200 rubles (it is debited when the option is activated).

What is important is the fact that these packages cease to be valid in cases of exhaustion of the limit established for them and the end of the billing period in which they were connected. If with the first situation everything is extremely simple - megabytes have ended - the option is disabled, then in the second situation you need to clarify: for example, on the Highway 18 GB tariff plan, on which the connected volumes of services are valid until December 21, the allowable traffic was exceeded - on the 17th. The subscriber activated the speed extension (1000 Mb package), which he needs to use in four days. If he does not have time to do this and the volume is not exhausted on December 21, then the remaining megabytes are burned up (i.e., the transition to a new period is not carried out). At the same time, if the activated package is used up before the onset of a new period, then the subscriber will most likely have to connect additional Beeline traffic in order to continue using the Internet at the maximum permissible speed. The number of connections for such options is unlimited.





Auto Renew Speed

This service will be especially relevant for those Beeline customers who do not use a large amount of traffic and do not want to constantly think about how many megabytes are left. The option of automatic speed extension is activated on the number once, and then after the main limit has been exhausted (at the tariff), additional traffic of 200 Mb will be connected on the Beeline number. The cost of this option is 20 rubles (for each volume provided). Activation of traffic and deduction of funds from the account occurs automatically (if the necessary amount is available) each time the main limit is exhausted.





How to connect additional traffic on Beeline?

Activation of speed extension packages, like any other services in the room, can be done through the user's personal account (on the company's official website). How to get additional traffic on Beeline, if there is no opportunity to use the Internet?

Use the following USSD requests and numbers:

  • 1000 Mb extension of speed - * 115 * 121 # (or a call to 067-409-32-21);
  • extension of speed by 3000 Mb - * 115 * 122 # (or call to 067-409-32-22 number);
  • auto-renewal of speed - * 115 * 23 # (or call to number 067-471-778); You can refuse the service through a request * 115 * 230 #, or by calling 067-471-77-80.

What to look for?

Before you take additional traffic to Beeline, you need to make sure that the amount in the account is enough to activate the service.

  • The above options can only be used in the home region. Of course, no one will forbid you to connect them, being outside it, only they will not act.
  • The cost of services is indicated for the Moscow region. It is necessary to check on the official website of the company or through the contact center how much it will cost to activate additional traffic for your region on the Beeline number.

Conclusion

Now you know how to connect additional traffic on Beeline. These options can be useful when the basic limit is exhausted, and you need to use the Internet at normal speed. Activate them through your mobile device or through your personal account. If this is not possible, then contact the customer support specialists - they will help to connect various packages, and absolutely free of charge.
