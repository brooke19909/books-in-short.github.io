Costa Rica hotels: rating of the best, choice of room, convenience of booking, quality of service, additional services and reviews of visitors and customers

The Republic of Costa Rica is located in Central America. Its shores are washed by the waters of the Pacific Ocean and the Caribbean Sea. Almost 30% of the country's territory is occupied by national reserves. The flight from Moscow to the capital of the country, San Jose, will take 13 hours.

Weather

The subequatorial climate appears on the Caribbean coast with heavy rainfall, usually hot and humid there. From the Pacific side 4 months a year the weather is dry, conducive to tourist vacations. It is here and in the capital that the best Costa Rica hotels are located.

Security

Many consider rest in Central America unsafe. However, it is in Costa Rica, where there is no army and the police are keeping order in the country, you can feel calm, here the lowest crime rate among Latin American countries.

Conclusion

In a small country, great attention is paid to the development of the tourism business. Both private hotels with a minimum of amenities and five-star hotels of international chains operating on an all-inclusive system are ready to accept tourists: Hilton, Holiday Inn, Hotels Group and InterContinental.

They are located mainly in the capital of San Jose, in resort towns on the coast. Those staying in the capital should be prepared for the bustling city life.

There are many volcanoes in Costa Rica, sleeping and active, those who wish can choose hotels at the foot of the mountains where they can find decent service, from there excursions to all corners of the country are offered.

There are few places on Earth with such a diversity of flora and fauna. In addition to relaxing by the ocean and walking through the rainforests, you can undergo a course of treatment in the sanatoriums, swim in the thermal springs.

The staff at Costa Rica hotels are exceptionally attentive and helpful. The food in the hotels is varied, the food is fresh, international, local and vegetarian dishes are offered.

Costa Rica is a real exotic, a jubilation of nature, a lot of new experiences.
