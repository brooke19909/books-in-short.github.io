Hotel Curium Palace Hotel 4 * (Limassol, Cyprus): photos and reviews of tourists

On the wonderful island of Cyprus, which is a popular resort, tourists leave both in winter and in summer. It is good here at any time of the year. Even there is no winter as such - the thermometer does not fall below the mark of 17-19 ° C. The same can be said about the temperature of sea water. Therefore, travel lovers come here. But before you go on vacation, they carefully choose a hotel for themselves. There are a lot of them. However, I would like to pay special attention to Curium Palace Hotel 4 *.





Location

This hotel is located in the southern part of the island - in a city called Limassol. It is the second largest metropolis in Cyprus. In addition, it is a large financial, cultural and economic center. The nearest airport is in Larnaca and in Paphos (about 70 kilometers distance in both cases). It’s more convenient to get to the second option. Indeed, an inexpensive express train runs from Limassol to the airport in Paphos - the fare costs 9 euros per person. A taxi will cost 60-70 y. e.

However, the distance from the airport does not matter when the sea is only 300 meters away. It is at such a distance from the beach that the Curium Palace Hotel 4 * is located, which cannot but rejoice. In addition, a 15-minute walk - the very center of the city! With cafes, night clubs, restaurants and shops. And the sights nearby are full. In 2-3 minutes you can walk to the local archaeological museum, zoo, Cypriot University of Technology and many other interesting places.

Service

Curium Palace Hotel 4 * provides guests with maximum comfort. Staying at this hotel, they will not need anything. Wi-Fi is available here for free - in public areas and individually in apartments. Private parking and car rental are available. If a person does not need a car, he can be offered transfer services.

In the lobby, guests are greeted by the reception, which operates around the clock. Currency exchange, a ticket service and luggage storage are also available there. And laundry with dry cleaning.

There is also a conference room and a photocopy and fax machine. Of the services, attention can be paid to the delivery of drinks and food to the room. By the way, if you need it, you can contact the administrators directly. The hotel staff is multilingual - speaks Greek, English, French and Russian, so no one should have problems understanding.





What else is available at the hotel?

In addition to the above, the Curium Palace Hotel 4 * has a standard entertainment minimum that will not let guests get bored. Naturally, this is a deep large pool and sun terrace. There is a fitness center with exercise equipment, a sauna and massage rooms. Bicycle rental, a tennis court, a jacuzzi and even a library are available.

There is also a bar and restaurant. This topic can also not be deprived of attention, since breakfast is included in the payment of the apartment.

Tourists who have been here assure: they feed well in the hotel restaurant. Everything is tasty, fresh, and there is plenty to choose from. Sausages, eggs in various forms, cheese, bacon, rolls, fragrant croissants, jams, butter, teas, coffee are served. Breakfast here is a buffet.

Lunches and dinners are on the menu. The portions are just huge. Dishes are predominantly Italian and Mediterranean, with such traditional dishes on the menu as jamon, sun-dried tomatoes, tiramisu, fish and vegetables, seafood, etc. You won’t be able to stay hungry.





About apartments

The Curium Palace Hotel 4 * (Limassol), which looks quite typical for itself, offers guests stylish, stylish rooms. You can be sure that they are really furnished with taste by looking at the photos below.

The cheapest rooms in The Curium Palace Hotel 4 * (Limassol) are the 2-seater standards. Their area is 20 square meters. m. Inside there is a large 2-sleeping bed (or two separate ones, to choose from), there is a powerful air conditioning, 43-inch plasma with satellite channels, a desk for work, as well as a minibar and a bathroom. And a week of living in this room for two people will cost about 45,000 rubles in the summer (with breakfast).

There is a similar option, but for three people. The area is slightly larger (24 sq. M.), Inside - 3 single beds. A week of accommodation with breakfast - 65 000 rubles. By the way, all rooms are soundproofed.





Upper class

In addition to standards, the hotel has more luxurious apartments. They are very popular among guests of the Curium Palace Hotel 4 *. Reviews they get appropriate.

For example, a junior suite has a king-size bed, and guests are provided with soft bathrobes and slippers. The room is spacious - 34 square meters. m. And a week of rest in it for two will cost 60,000 rubles.

But, of course, the most luxurious and expensive option is an executive suite. 45 square meters of space! And it is divided into a bedroom and living room. The balconies of the suites are much more spacious than the previously listed apartments. 7 days here are about 87,000 rubles. Children under the age of 2 years are allowed to stay for free, although this apartment is almost always occupied by the newlyweds.





Comments of tourists

Finally, a few words about what people leave comments at Curium Palace Hotel 4 * (Cyprus). Reviews are extremely positive. And here is what tourists pay attention to:

  • The hotel has a business atmosphere. Everything at the highest level is a real business class.
  • Very quiet. And not only because of sound insulation. Mostly people who come here to make noise come here: businessmen on a business trip, newlyweds on their honeymoon, spouses for leisure and single tourists.
  • Perfect cleanliness. And this applies to everything: cleaning of the territory, rooms, pool, surrounding areas.
  • The location. Really successful - next to the business and entertainment part of the city, the sea.

These are the main advantages of the hotel, according to former tourists. And if the listed criteria are priority when choosing a hotel, you definitely need to go to Curium Palace.
