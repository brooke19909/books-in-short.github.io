Who is Gia Eradze?

The "Royal Circus" by Gia Eradze is known all over the world. The tour program is so extensive that it covers every corner of the globe. Many people ask what the circus artist is so famous for.

Gia Eradze was born in Georgia in 1978. From childhood, the boy dreamed of an arena, so at the age of 11 he began working at the Tbilisi Circus. However, he did not forget about studying at school.

The artist has a lot of merits and regalia. Since 2007, Georgy Giulevich began working with Rosgoszirk, and a year later he was appointed head of this team.

It is worth noting that the artist chooses the staff personally. This applies not only to the main performers of numbers, but also extras. Each participant in the show must have a choreographic or ballet education. Stage images and costumes are agreed with the troupe, everyone can make their own adjustments and wishes.

The "Royal Circus" by Gia Eradze, reviews of which are only positive, deservedly takes prizes at various festivals. It is very difficult to win the love of the spectator, but the troupe managed to do this thanks to painstaking work and dedication.
