Phuket - an ambiguous resort with a strange name

Phuket - the largest island of Thailand, it is connected to the mainland by a bridge.

People fly to Phuket for perfect beaches, picturesque views, rich in SPA, delicious food, but this resort knows what to do for those who prefer to have fun or culturally enrich themselves. In Patong, there are many budget hotels, clubs, this is not such a gambling area as the "mecca of debauchery of Pattaya," but it is also very boring and much safer.

Family travelers and those looking for a quiet relaxing holiday should take a look at hotels on the west coast of Phuket.

Phuket is not only the most spacious, but also the most hilly and embossed island of Thailand. Tourists who fly in order to visit any specific attractions, it is advisable to look for a hotel near these same attractions. Renting a car in Phuket is not a problem, but the hilly terrain and the very difficult, ambiguous situation with the rules and traditions of driving make many tourists abandon the idea of ‚Äã‚Äãindependent autotravel around the island.

What to look for when choosing a hotel in Phuket

When choosing a hotel in Phuket, you should pay attention to its price, location, quality of the beach, reviews of tourists and a range of services. In general, housing here is more expensive than in the same Pattaya, but the rest here is much better and safer.

The rationalism of official accreditation on the "star" principle in Thailand, or rather the absence of this very rationalism, puzzles many travelers. However, the same unfortunate trend is found in Turkey, Egypt, Tunisia.

In addition, the official category of the hotel may, to put it mildly, differ from the real state of affairs, you must be prepared in advance. For example, The Blue Pearl Kata 3 hotel has a 3 * category, but in terms of quality of services it can compete with many fours. A correctly selected hotel can take any vacation to a new level, so when choosing a hotel in Thailand, you should arm yourself with suspicions of star accreditation of hotels and not be lazy when collecting reviews and useful information about hotels in this many-sided resort.

The Blue Pearl Kata Hotel 3 (Phuket)

This small budget boutique hotel, made in a nice marine style, is ideal for youth recreation. The hotel is located in the center of the tourist town of Kuta, 50 meters from the beach.

The official check-in time is 2:00 p.m.

The official departure time is 12:00.

The hotel does not accommodate tourists with pets.





The Blue Pearl Kata has non-smoking rooms. Hotel location

The Blue Pearl Kata has a very favorable location. Most of all, the benefits of such a successful hotel location can be appreciated by dynamic tourists who like to visit attractions, travel to interesting places, go shopping, etc.

The hotel is 32 km from Phuket International Airport.

Just 800 meters away is the famous Big Buddha statue. The statue is located on a hill, so that it can be seen from almost anywhere in southern Phuket. This is one of the most significant monuments of Buddhism in the whole world.

Kata Observation Deck is 3 km from the hotel.





Also known Thai markets, Kata Temple, numerous restaurants and cafes, shops, travel agencies, entertainment centers are a few meters from the hotel.

Infrastructure at the hotel

The Blue Pearl Kata 3 * hotel has two swimming pools, a restaurant with a Swedish line, a bar, parking, a lounge. The hotel area is stylish, well decorated, but very small. The two rooftop pools are very small, with sun loungers and parasols. The hotel has an elevator. The restaurant "Turtle" at the hotel is popular not only among hotel guests, but also among other tourists. Tourists accustomed to huge hotels can be disappointed, because this hotel does not have gyms, games rooms, tennis courts, or huge pools with slides. The hotel is more suitable as a clean comfortable place for night sleep and breakfast, and tourists should seek entertainment in the city or on the beach.

Kata Beach

The Blue Pearl Kata 3 Phuket Kata features an entire beach buffet.

The hotel is just 50 meters from Kata Beach. This is one of the most picturesque beaches of Phuket and the whole of Thailand. The beach is a little in the bay, so formidable waves, thrilling only inveterate surfers, are very rare here. On the contrary, Kata Beach is 1 km of a quiet, calm coastline. The entrance to the sea is gentle, suitable even for children and beginners.

Beaches Karon and Patong

A 10-15 minute leisurely walk from the hotel is Karon Beach. Directly adjacent to it are many residences and huge chain hotels. Consequently, Karon is the cleanest, pompous and comfortable beach. It is not located in a bay or bay, therefore the water is cleaner, but sometimes the ocean can be too rebellious, and the waves are too high, especially in the rainy season. In such situations, a ‚Äúred flag‚Äù is hung on the beach.

The feature of Karon Beach is its unusual quartz sand. Its texture is not like ordinary, it crunches underfoot, like snow, and under direct sunlight it heats much less than ordinary sand.





The proximity to expensive hotels contributed to the fact that on this beach the infrastructure is most developed: many shopping centers, water sports, entertainment centers, restaurants, Thai markets.

Patong Beach is a 15-minute drive from The Blue Pearl Kata Beach Hotel. This is the most crowded, noisy and depraved beach of Phuket. It is very well equipped, but always crowded, it is definitely not suitable for a peaceful vacation, but for fun and acquaintances it is an ideal place.

All these beaches are public, therefore, all the infrastructure for tourists on the beaches is paid: sunbeds, umbrellas, mattresses. Beach towels are provided on deposit at Blue Pearl Kata Phuket.

The Blue Pearl Kata. Tourists reviews

Guest reviews are a rich source of information that helps inexperienced guests form an opinion about resorts and hotels that they have not been to. One should only soberly perceive the information gleaned from such recommendations. Naturally, the opinion of most tourists is very subjective.

As for the The Blue Pearl Kata 3 * hotel, the reviews about it are mostly positive, but there are quite contradictory and frankly negative ones.

The authors of positive reviews point out a good combination of price / quality in terms of services, the availability of Russian-speaking staff, excellent location, stylish design, free internet, use of a safe, and friendly staff.





In negative reviews, travelers complain about poor food, slow service, small rooms, a tiny pool, the quality of cleaning, which is slightly improved in accordance with the size of the tip. Also, the authors of negative reviews rightly noted that the rooms of even one category and the same in price can significantly differ in quality and design.
