Georgia hotels (overview)

Georgia is a country of Transcaucasia on the east coast of the Black Sea. In this territory, as early as the 12th ‚Äì 13th centuries BC, the first states of Diaochi and Colchis were created. Over many centuries, the state and territorial division of Georgia has changed many times, wars have passed, kings and conquerors have disappeared. A stable mild climate and diverse landscape led to a variety of flora and fauna. Mineral springs of Georgia are famous for their medicinal properties, and the famous Borjomi is exported to twenty-four countries of the world. Holidays in Georgia can be organized in the mountains on excellent ski slopes and on the Black Sea, with amazing excursions to historical sites.





Mountain resorts of Georgia

The youngest ski resort is Gudauri, which was built taking into account the most modern requirements. Gudauri resembles alpine resorts and is considered more youthful, but even modern professionals like its modern slopes, and for beginners separate descents with experienced instructors are organized. Georgia hotels in mountain resorts are built to international standards and receive guests from all over the world.

Another popular Georgian resort, Bakuriani, was founded during the Soviet era and was one of the main skiing bases. Snow-capped mountain slopes were also chosen by snowboarders, freeride lovers and gliders.





Tbilisi hotels on the left bank of the Kura

In Tbilisi, hotels in Georgia are represented by a huge number of hotels, mini-hotels and apartments located in the historic city center along the embankment of the Kura River. Near the Holy Trinity Cathedral are located:

  1. Vinotel Boutigue Hotel (Elene Akhvlediani ascent, 4) is located in an old building of the last century with its wine cellar, where professional sommeliers conduct tasting tours. Thirteen rooms are equipped with everything necessary, there is a meeting room. Pets are allowed by prior arrangement.
  2. B&B Old Tbilisi (Kardenakhi Street, 7). The hotel has twelve bed and breakfast rooms. The hotel's cafe serves a buffet in the morning, you can use the shared kitchen, there is a free car park.
  3. Hotel Orion Old (Armazi Street 3/5). The hotel has 31 rooms of different comforts, with a fridge and a minibar, a satellite TV, a fully equipped bathroom with a hairdryer and free toiletries.
  4. Hotel Kopala Rikhe (Europe Square Rikhe) is located on the banks of the Kura River near the metro station. The hotel has 24 elegant, fully equipped rooms, a cafe, a restaurant, free parking, pets are allowed.
  5. The Marionn Hotel (9 Aleksandre Tsurtsumia street) and Tbilisi Laerton (Iliko Kurkhuli Street, 14) will also welcome guests with excellent service.

In a country like Georgia, all-inclusive hotels are slightly different from their usual ideas. Basically, this is breakfast, which is included in the price of the room, wine tasting and a master class in cooking meat dishes are possible.

Kabuleti Hotels

Georgia hotels in Kabuleti are developing rapidly after the creation of a free tourist zone with significant tax breaks. The subtropical climate made Kabuleti another capital of the resort of Adjara. From the city center along the coast there is a highway embankment on both sides of which are modern hotels and reconstructed Soviet sanatoriums. Closer to the center is Hotel Friendship (Megobroba street, 16). The hotel has 52 rooms of different comforts with all amenities, bike and car rental is organized, a bar and sauna, free parking are available. Kobuleti Pearl Of Sea Hotel & Spa (Tamar Mepe, 42) - a new modern hotel with a swimming pool, 44 rooms are equipped according to European resort standards with an extra bed. Other facilities include free parking, bicycle and car rental, and barbecue facilities. Leaving the city on Kabuleti Beach, you can meet more than two dozen hotels located directly on the coastline. They provide all kinds of services for a comfortable stay:

  • Guest House Smile (Rustaveli, 191);
  • Kobuleti Georgia Palace Hotel & Spa (Agmashenebeli Ave, 275);
  • Belgrade Rustaveli Hotel (Rustaveli Street, 170 g);
  • Hotel Ponto (386 Agmashenebeli Street);
  • GB Hotel (Tamar Mepe St, 36);
  • Cristal GB Hotel (9 April Street, 33).




Georgia Kvariati

Kvariati Resort has grown from a small fishing village. It is located almost next to the Turkish border, between the villages of Sarpi and Gonio. A small beach, covered with clean sand, is located at the foot of the mountain range, with magnificent, evergreen vegetation. One of the advantages of the resort is the location of the hotels and the beach away from the motorway, which allows you to fully enjoy the peace, privacy and clean air.

Hotel Kvari (Ioana Lazi, 33) is a small hotel providing 15 rooms with all amenities. Rooms with sea views are equipped with all necessary facilities, a private bathroom and free toiletries and toiletries. There is a restaurant, a bar, free parking is provided, a free transfer from Batumi Airport is provided.

Hotel Zura (Ioane Lazis 1st Ally Street, 26) The hotel building hangs over a magnificent beach of nineteen rooms with beautiful views of the sea and mountains. The outdoor pool has a sun terrace, air baths and barbecue facilities. The hotel has a restaurant, a free car park and an airport shuttle.

Lazuri Hotel (Kvariati Street, 14). Located directly on the coastline, the hotel has a beautiful beach and sun terraces. Twelve rooms are equipped with comfortable furniture, executed in warm pastel colors and equipped with everything necessary for a pleasant stay. The rooms offer beautiful views of the sea and the coast. The hotel provides free parking and Wi-Fi.

It will be no less pleasant to stay for relaxing in hotels:

  • Sunset Kvariati (Village Kvariati);
  • Hotel Velux (St. Andriapirveltsodebuli Street, 69);
  • Holiday Home on Ioane Lazi (Ioane Lazi Street, 35);
  • Eva Hotel (Kvariati, 4422);

Beautiful nature, hospitable residents and comfortable hotels in Georgia are always happy to welcome guests.
