Tourists also appreciate the location of the hotel in Limassol. Crowne Plaza Limassol 4 * stands on the first line, has its own beach, and at the same time is within walking distance to the old town and the commercial quarter. How to get to Limassol

Crowne Plaza Limassol 4 * is a hotel complex located on the border between the Old Town and the resort area, in the heart of the commercial quarter. Therefore, in the immediate vicinity of the hotel are all sorts of shops and shopping centers. The closest food department store is Debenarms. Also a lot of evening entertainment in this quarter - nightclubs and discos. There is no airport in Limassol. The most convenient way to get to the hotel is from Larnaca. The airport of this city is located only seventy-two kilometers from the hotel. Buses bring "package" tourists to the hotel (transfer is included in the price of the ticket). If you travel on your own, then you first need to get from Larnaca to Limassol. Buses run from the city center to the Crowne Plaza 4 * hotel. The stop is located right at the gates of the hotel. The Old Town is two and a half kilometers away. Many tourists walked to the famous castle of Limassol. But few know that a ten minute walk from the hotel is a park in which the September festival of young wine is held annually (the Cypriot analogue of Beaujolais Nouveau). Tourists claim that the hotel is an excellent starting point for traveling around the southern part of the island. Antique Amathus is located seven kilometers away, and the no less ancient city of Kourion is eighteen. The medieval Colossi Castle is only 14 km from the hotel.

Guest Room Features

The floors in the rooms are wooden. The reviews also mention one feature: shampoos, shower gels and soap maids put not the cheapest, but branded, from Yves Rocher. In addition, they put another jar of moisturizer.
