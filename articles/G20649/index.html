Hotel Royal Mirage Beach Resort 3 *, Goa: reviews, descriptions, ratings and features

Today Goa is one of the best resorts that India is rightly proud of. Magnificent landscapes and a riot of exotic nature - all this attracts and bewitches. It should be noted that this resort is difficult to attribute to the budget, but this does not lose its popularity among tourists who appreciate the quality of leisure.

Today in the center of our attention is Royal Mirage Beach Resort 3 * (Goa). Reviews say that this cozy hotel will allow you to fully appreciate the quality of relaxation in a paradise. Judging by the estimates of tourists, the average hotel rating is 4 on a five-point system.



Hotel location

If you think that a vacation in Goa is not available to you, then pay attention to this modest hotel. Despite the fact that it is very remote for Russian tourists, the accommodation itself is notable for its availability. Of course, everyone has different tastes and requests, but if you are looking for a relaxing family vacation, then you will definitely like Royal Mirage Beach Resort 3 * (Goa). Reviews of regular customers convince us that this modest hotel is a real find.



Relaxed relaxation and metropolitan entertainment

The hotel was built in a very good location relative to those places that are usually visited by tourists. And if you are a lover of beach holidays, then this is just a godsend. The hotel is located on Candolim Beach, you just have to leave your room and go down to the shore to enjoy the warm sand and the vast ocean. Within walking distance are restaurants, numerous shops, as well as cafes, the prices of which are very different from each other, so you will have to undertake your own investigation to find a suitable option for yourself. Despite the silence prevailing here, one should not forget that Royal Mirage Beach Resort 3 * (Goa) is located very close to the city center. Reviews emphasize that it is very convenient. At any time it is enough to catch a taxi - and all the capital's entertainment is available to you. Only 14 km away from the center of the hotel.

Short hotel description

For lovers of noisy fun and discos until the morning, we emphasize that this is a family-type hotel. Because of this, youth companies may not like the Royal Mirage Beach Resort 3 * (Goa). The reviews say that it is always very quiet here, sometimes it may seem that you are completely alone. This is not surprising, because there are only 45 rooms in the building, the guests of which for the most part will spend time on excursions, on the beach, as well as shop at exotic shops. A variety of programs allows everyone to compose an individual selection of entertainments for themselves, among which you will surely find what interests you.



Tourists meeting

The hotel organizes transfers to any destination. It can be a meeting at the airport and the departure of a tourist from the hotel door to the landing strip. You must inform the property in advance that you need a transfer service. The service is paid separately, specify the price. Experienced drivers will meet you at the port and take you to the ship. In addition, there is an internal shuttle service that will take you to any shopping center and bring you back to the hotel with purchases. If you come with small children or elderly people and it is difficult for you to get to the beach with them, then order a special transfer that will not only take you to the beach strip, but also pick it up when the hot sun tires you. Thanks to this service, reviews about the Royal Mirage Beach Resort 3 * hotel (India, Goa) are especially warm. Tourists say that the hotel staff is sincerely interested in turning a vacation into a real fairy tale for everyone.

Description of rooms

This is primarily interested in tourists. After a busy day, everyone wants to return to a comfortable room, which has everything you need for a comfortable stay. What can Royal Mirage Beach Resort 3 * offer its guests?

India (Goa) has a hot and humid climate, so each room is equipped with a climate control system so that you can set the most suitable mode for you. The rooms have comfortable furniture and a large TV, refrigerator, shower and hairdryer, telephone. Upon request, each guest can put a personal computer or laptop. You do not have to pay extra money for the opportunity to use the Internet, this service is provided for free. Private rooms have balconies. We guarantee that you will spend sunrises and sunsets only on the balcony.



Catering for tourists

When people are going to visit India for the first time, they usually imply acquaintance not only with another culture, but also with exotic cuisine. In fact, many of these dishes may be too unusual, therefore, in order not to spoil your vacation, experienced guides recommend eating at the hotel. By the way, Royal Mirage Beach Resort 3 * is quite developed in this regard. Reviews of the hotel emphasize that there is an excellent restaurant on site, as well as a cafe and bar. They do not close either day or night, so you can have a bite to eat anytime you want.



Hotel infrastructure

Royal Mirage Beach Resort 3 * in India (Goa) is a whole complex that can provide you with a huge variety of services. The hotel has a developed infrastructure. On its territory there is a beauty salon where every woman can feel like a real goddess. A wide range of services and procedures for the face, hair and body will help to solve many problems and become truly beautiful. For lovers of outdoor activities, we recommend the hottest hours in the garden. Here, shady alleys save from sunlight, and the aroma of flowers gives an excellent mood. In addition, in the evening, a night club opens on site, where you will find an entertainment program.



Beach vacation

It may be too difficult for those who are visiting the Hotel Royal Mirage 3 * (Goa) for the first time. Candolim (India) is a kingdom of hot sand and hot sun, therefore it is recommended in the first days to limit oneself only to its morning and evening visits. At other times, it is better to hide from the heat in a shady garden. By the way, there is an outdoor pool, which for many tourists perfectly replaces the ocean with its inconsistent nature. The pool for children is great; they spend all their free time with great pleasure. The hotel does not have its own beach, but only 700 meters separates it from the public.



Service staff

No matter how good the hotel is, it is still its staff who create the complete impression of it. And this is not surprising. Living people meet you, they are near you all the time while you live here, they help, provide you with everything you need for a comfortable stay. Hotel Royal Mirage Beach Resort 3 * in Candolim greatly benefits from its small size. Judging by the reviews, its employees feel personal responsibility for the common cause and try to serve customers as well as possible. As a result, guests are greeted very warmly, they are ready to help in any matter. We analyzed a large number of reviews and in none of them did we find negative assessments of the work of the staff. The hotel is very comfortable and clean, rooms carry out daily wet cleaning. Guests note that rooms always have hygiene items and clean towels.

Dissatisfied tourists

Indeed, there are such. No wonder, we are all different, respectively, and the requirements, as well as the perception of the environment, are different for different people. Why do some tourists do not like Royal Mirage Beach Resort 3 * (India, Goa)? First of all, expectations and reality may not initially coincide. This is a small three-star hotel, and 3 stars by the standards of India can be very different from European resorts. The building is no longer new, and the humid climate makes itself felt, therefore periodically swollen doors are being repaired, and spots are formed on the ceiling. Traces of the repair, of course, are noticeable, but in order to enter the shiny and shining room, you need to look for a five-star hotel in this resort.

Exotic outside the window

Indeed, everything is completely unusual for our tourist here, so when you come for the first time, you may feel discomfort. First of all, these are climatic features, so the first few days it is advisable to make only minor sorties, to undergo adaptation.

Secondly, the very exotic nature that beckons us so may shock. This applies not only to flora (abundance of fruits and unknown plants), but also to fauna. Namely, a huge number of insects, from which the nets and windows of the old hotel do not save. If you are afraid of everything crawling and flying, then stock up on repellents.

Finally, the third minus that tourists note is breakfast at the hotel. After European resorts, where breakfast at the hotel is the most hearty meal, here you can be disappointed. There will be no meat and fish dishes at all, you will find juice and fruits, pancakes and vegetable salads on the table. However, for a fee during lunch or dinner, you can order any dish of European and national cuisine.

Based on the foregoing, we can conclude that the hotel is good enough for its class. However, before making the final choice, it is necessary to compare several options.
