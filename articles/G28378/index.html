From a bird's eye view, it resembles a drop of water that is about to fall.

Mystery of the lake

Once, between the village of Old Kistrus and the ancient village of Ostrov, a white church towered. Near it were houses of peasants, outbuildings and several houses where the church clergy lived. One morning, the locals woke up from the terrifying scream of a shepherd. People rushed out into the street and saw that the majestic church disappeared along with the surrounding buildings. A water surface appeared in its place, which excited people from time to time with rising bubbles. The next day, local residents again came to this place and froze in fear. They saw the church dome slowly peep out of the water and plunge into the lake again. People decided that it was God's providence. Thus, the Almighty punished the parishioners for their sins.

Since then, the pond is considered holy. Rumor has it that every year at 12.00 a.m. (the day of the death of people) the dome of the church rises from the surface of the water. At this time, a bell ringing is heard from a reservoir. However, divers more than once examined Lake Svyatoe (Ryazan Region), sank to its bottom, but still did not find signs of a flooded church.

Shilovsky District: Holy Lake

For many years, people want to unravel the nature of the Tunguska meteorite. Fragments of iron resembling a real meteorite were found in the village of Borovoye. Here is the Holy Lake (Shilovsky district). Some scientists prescribe a meteorite origin. They call the main cause of the appearance of the lake the fall of the Tunguska meteorite. This origin of the reservoir is confirmed by its regular round shape. At the same time, the nature of the funnel should be taken into account. The fall of the meteorite occurred more than 1 thousand years ago. Unlearned people tell more interesting details about the origin of the reservoir. As in other "holy" lakes, here once was a church, which eventually fell underground. In fact, such reservoirs often arise in places of conservation of water of glacial origin and underground water reservoirs.

What secrets does the Holy Lake in Kosino hide?



The natural complex "Kosinskie ozera" is located in the northwestern region of Russia. This group includes Lake Svyatoe (Kosino). This is a historical and cultural monument of the city. The pond has a round shape. A thick layer of silt covers its bottom. The lake is surrounded by a quagmire, reeds and small trees. Water in a reservoir has healing properties. It contains a large amount of iodine, silver, bromine, and it never blooms.

One amazing legend is associated with the lake. Once there was a church, which gradually sank into the water. It is for this reason that the reservoir acquired healing properties. The locals sincerely believe in this. They say that when the temple was immersed in water, the Divine Liturgy was performed in it. And still the priest, who died that day, prays for the health of people. Religious procession is organized annually on Holy Lake. This takes place on the day of commemoration of the Kosinsk Icon of the Mother of God. Since ancient times, people have used the water of the Holy Lake to cure serious ailments, including rheumatism, skin diseases. They swam in a pond and wiped themselves with lake silt.
