Krasnodar, the farm of Lenin: description, photo. LCD "Khutorok"

For people who are not familiar with life in the Krasnodar Territory, the word "farm" is associated with a small village of several yards and people engaged in agriculture. Once upon a time it was just that. Today, the Lenin farm in Krasnodar can be called one of the most prestigious areas of the region. It is located only 22 kilometers from the city of Krasnodar. You can get here by car in 30 minutes.





Farm History

In 1910, the Kashirin Cossack family (consisting of 3 sons and father) came from the village of Starokorsunskaya and began to develop untouched lands along the banks of the Obrezka river flowing here (it was subsequently flooded by the reservoir). The lands here were fertile, but the places were rather dangerous: there was a forest very close by, in which there were numerous gangs of bandits. But despite this, good fishing places, fertile lands and other natural wealth began to attract active owners here.

Soon, these places were populated by Cossack families from the Pashkovskaya and Starokorsunskaya villages, and a settlement was formed. Since the first immigrants, and also quite wealthy, were the Kashirin family, it was decided to call this village the Kashirin farm (Kashirinsky). In 1928, the Kashirin commune was created here, and several years later a collective farm with the same name was created. In 1936, the Kashirin family was dispossessed and exiled. And the farm and the collective farm were given the name of Lenin.





Krasnodar, the farm of Lenin: photo, description

The farm is located in the Karasun district of Krasnodar, almost near (five kilometers) from the famous shopping center "OZ Mall", seven kilometers from the Krasnodar reservoir, which is also called the "Krasnodar Sea". In Krasnodar, on the Lenin farm, there are two cottage villages: Victoria and Rainbow. In the village of Victoria there are modern two- and one-story houses, as well as duplexes for two families. All communications are central, except sewerage (septic tank). This is a closed guarded area. A school bus takes the children to school. ZhK Khutorok in the Lenin farm, Krasnodar

On the territory of 1.8 hectares of land, surrounded by green spaces is located LCD "Khutorok". It is represented by three four-story houses of a monolithic frame structure. One-room apartments, their area is from 25 to 37 square meters. meters, large enough, which allows you to do your own redevelopment. Apartments are usually rented in the final finish, with central communications, but it is possible to purchase an apartment at the construction stage, which will save a lot of budget. The developer pays great importance to the improvement of the local area: trees are planted here, sports and playgrounds are installed, underground parking is provided.





Property prices

The price depends on the quadrature, the location of the house, a personal plot. The price of a small one-story house can start from 2,300,000 rubles and above. Duplexes - from 3,000,000-4,000,000 rubles. The price of a good cottage starts at 7 million rubles.

Transport

Through the Lenin farm passes the highway Krasnodar - Kropotkin. Therefore, getting here is not difficult. You can take a bus or taxi.
