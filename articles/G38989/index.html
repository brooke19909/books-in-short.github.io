Noyabrsk hotels: overview, description, addresses and reviews of tourists

The city of Noyabrsk owes its appearance to oil and gas fields located around its territory. This is a settlement, from the moment of foundation of which fifty years have not passed. It occupies a convenient geographical position between the Ob and Pura rivers and is considered to be the southern gate of the district. Located in the taiga zone, which has a continental climate and is surrounded by numerous forests and lakes, the city is interesting not only as a place of work for oil workers, but also as a place for outdoor activities. It has well-developed infrastructure, there is a railway station, airport and hotels.

Noyabrsk: attractions of the city

With a large number of higher education institutions, museums and monuments, beautiful natural landscapes and meeting international requirements for the quality of life, the city attracts many investors.

Arriving guests are always ready to welcome comfortable hotels. Noyabrsk is famous for the quality of tourist services .

In Noyabrsk there is a children's park with many attractions and a cinema, not far from the park are the Cathedral Mosque and the Church of the Archangel Michael. From the northern part of the city, the road leads to the beautiful Lake Hanto.



Hotel "Russia"

One of these hotels with well-developed technology for servicing visitors is the Rossiya Hotel (Noyabrsk, Republic Street, 12). It is located in the city center, a ten-minute drive from the train station and half an hour from the airport. Hotel phone: 8 (349) 636-98-88.

All hotels in Noyabrsk are perfect for corporate accommodation and for families. There is a beauty salon in the building of the Rossiya Hotel. Guests can enjoy the on-site restaurant, relax in the sauna and play billiards. This hotel has forty-nine comfortable rooms for every taste - from economy to luxury. All rooms have a TV and Wi-Fi. This is a hotel in which not only ordinary travelers, but also star guests of the city like to stay. Among them are many famous artists and showmen.



Hotel reviews

All visitors leave good reviews about the staff, hotel rooms and quality of service. Many write that this is not the first time they have stayed at this hotel and will be happy to come again. Visitors love the warm welcome that guests receive. Fine cooking of gourmet European dishes in the restaurant. The rooms are cleaned in the absence of visitors, no one interferes with the rest. Sheets and towels are changed frequently. It is very convenient to use dosed shampoo and soap. There are no negative reviews about the hotel.

Hotel Alaska

The hotel has the best restaurant in town. It has its own fitness center and pool. Their visit for guests is included in the price of the hotel room. Noyabrsk will always remain in the memory of tourists who stayed at this hotel.

The hotel is located at: st. Sovetskaya, 12. Hotel phones: 8 (349) 642-41-41, 8 (349) 642-59-40, 8 (349) 642-61-21.

Ideal place for business

Hotel Caesar

If you are interested in hotels that belong to the economy class, then the Caesar Hotel (Noyabrsk, 58 Shevchenko St., 58) is in the first place. Phones: 8 (349) 636-71-11, 8 (349) 631-55-99, 8 (349) 631-50-80.

Inside is being monitored.
