Children's sanatorium "Kratovo" in the Moscow region: description of how to get

In the Ramensky district of the Moscow Region, surrounded by forests, there is a children's sanatorium "Kratovo" (Moscow region), subordinate to the Ministry of Health of Russia. Despite the proximity to the capital, the distance to which is only forty kilometers, this health institution is located in an ecologically clean region. Crystal air with a rich content of atmospheric oxygen and phytoncides creates ideal conditions for treating children with respiratory problems.

Indication for sending children to the sanatorium "Kratovo" in the Moscow region is the presence of a number of chronic diseases of the bronchopulmonary system. Also often ill children are taken here for healing. However, there are contraindications for the stay of children in the sanatorium:

  • diseases in the acute stage;
  • various mental disorders;
  • bronchial asthma.

Information for Parents





In the sanatorium "Kratovo" in the Moscow region children from preschool and primary school age from four to nine years are accepted. Also, children from four to twelve years old with one of the parents can visit the health resort. For students during their stay at the sanatorium, training is provided. After completing the course of treatment, the child is issued a report card. All lessons are held according to a special schedule in the morning. Children who arrive with their parents are self-employed. If necessary, the teacher advises parents on various aspects of children's education. It is required to note that educational supplies in the sanatorium are not issued, they must be brought with you.

Leisure





In addition to rehabilitation and training, a large number of entertainment programs are provided for children. These are festive evenings, all kinds of games, theatrical and circus performances. In circles working in a sanatorium, children have the opportunity to develop creative abilities. Work with children is carried out by qualified teaching staff: educators and music workers. For swimming there is a modern indoor pool, open year-round. His visit takes place under the vigilant supervision of experienced swimming coaches and medical professionals. Up to two times a week, children can use the sauna. Cozy relaxation conditions are provided by a high level of comfort created for young guests.

Accommodation

Children are accommodated in modern heated buildings of the sanatorium department, which are divided into several residential units, consisting of a bedroom, a game room, a bathroom and a dining room. The bedrooms (separate for girls and boys) have comfortable modern beds. The rooms have linoleum flooring. Children are organized in groups of twenty people. Daily supervision of them is provided by shift staff consisting of an experienced educator, qualified nurse and attentive nurse.

The guys in the sanatorium building are provided with a daily six-diet diet, which is agreed with the doctor. For children arriving with their parents, several accommodation options are provided. They are accommodated in luxury rooms and two-room apartments, designed to accommodate four people. Four meals a day are provided in the department for children with adults, and wellness procedures are carried out as prescribed by the doctor.

Services





For a healthy stay of children and their parents, the sanatorium has a gym equipped with modern equipment, as well as billiards and a sauna. All kinds of sports and entertainment events are held for children. There is also a library in the sanatorium, there is a games room in which the guys can have fun. Safety of residence is ensured by the presence of a fenced area and 24-hour security. In the residential building, the administrator and medical staff are continuously on duty.

Treatment methods

The treatment is carried out by qualified pediatricians and specialist doctors. Physiotherapy, speleotherapy (salt cave), the Mountain Air apparatus, therapeutic baths, inhalations, several types of massage, and medical treatment are used as methods of improving children's health. Wellness procedures are carried out using modern medical equipment, which significantly increases their effectiveness.

How to get there





You can get to the sanatorium by private car through the city of Lyubertsy on the road to Ramenskoye to the sign "Children's Sanatorium Kratovo." You can also come to the health resort by rail. An electric train leaves from the Kazan station to Kratovo station. Next, you need to walk about a kilometer, following the road signs. The village of Kratovo, Moscow Region, where the sanatorium is located, is famous for its clean air and picturesque outskirts. It is nice to be treated and just relax.
