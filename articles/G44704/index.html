Review Acer Liquid M220

Appearance, materials, controls, smartphone assembly



Acer M220 Liquid Black comes with a charger, cable for connection, as well as a wired headset. The corners of the device are slightly rounded. The design is strict. Attention deserves the cover of the device. It is made of plastic, which has an interesting texture. It is by touch and outwardly very similar to paper or fabric, slightly rough. Sensations are unusual. The lid is comfortable. It is removed thanks to the groove from below. The smartphone does not slip in the hand. Under the cover are slots for a pair of SIM cards, as well as microSD, the maximum amount of which is 64 GB.

The ends of the smartphone are made of the usual matte plastic, it is complemented by a silver border, which is stylized as metal. On top is a microUSB jack and a 3.5mm headphone jack. On the right is the power button and volume keys. The controls are hard and short. A large part of the front side is occupied by a four-inch display, the speaker grid is located above it, proximity sensors and lighting, as well as a front camera, are located there. Under the screen you can see the empty space. The device is assembled perfectly. Gaps, backlashes and creaks are absent.

Dimensions



The Acer M220 Liquid Black smartphone is very compact. The small size makes it possible to use the device with one hand. You can reach anywhere on the surface of the display. The device is very comfortable in the hand.

Screen



Resolution smartphone Acer Liquid M220 has a decent. The image is high-quality, but only subject to a right angle while viewing. The screen fades in the sun. It can be argued that the Acer Liquid M220 display is suitable for reading various information. However, color reproduction is hardly natural. Viewing angles are insufficient.

operating system



Acer Liquid M220 is running Windows Phone. It is possible to customize the desktop. There is a notification curtain, switches for wireless interfaces. The platform works very quickly.

Other characteristics

Acer Liquid M220 has 512 MB of RAM, as well as 4 GB of internal memory. With casual games, the smartphone copes well. The device is able to withstand without recharging the whole day if the total activity of the display is equal to a couple of hours. A pair of cameras are installed in the smartphone - the main one at 5 MP, supplemented with autofocus, as well as the front at 2 MP. Photo quality is below average. The main problem is working with exposure. The camera software is trying to brighten the subject or darken it. Optics get dirty quickly. Shots come out slightly cloudy. Highlighted object is produced by the camera by increasing the ISO. The result is digital noise. Video is recorded in HD quality. GPS works smartly thanks to the Qualcomm chipset.

The device supports a pair of SIM cards. The radio module is one. When you call one of the numbers, the second is not available. In the Windows Phone operating system, interaction with a pair of SIM cards is organized conveniently. Each number has a separate icon for messages and calls. In the settings, you can select the SIM card that will be used to access the Internet. The device is characterized by high quality voice transmission. At the same time, mechanical notes can be distinguished in dynamics. The maximum volume is not always sufficient. So, we are talking about a smartphone with an interesting design, excellent body materials running Windows Phone 8.1. The weaknesses include the average speed and battery life. The display also cannot be called ideal. Oleophobic coating is absent. Windows Phone often runs faster than Android. The tiled interface is very pleasant. As the main competitor of the model, Highscreen WinWin can be considered.

Opinion



So we figured out the main characteristics of the Acer Liquid M220. Reviews about the device are very diverse, and now we will see what its owners say about it.

Among the strengths are the build quality, camera capabilities, high speed, support for a pair of SIM cards, an affordable price. A variety of functions have also been praised, while it is noted that not everyone is used in everyday life. Indicators of autonomy are recognized as worthy. The developers also earned praise for the provided front camera, excellent build quality, built-in LED flash. The owners note that the phone is comfortable to hold and its body is resistant to touch marks.

As disadvantages, users of the Acer Liquid M220 call the inconvenient sensor buttons, low volume of the call signal. There are complaints about problems with components, including a case and a film for the display. A number of reviews indicate that the manufacturer does not supply them. Some people have trouble locking the display when calling. It is also noted that the microphone may turn off during a conversation. The fact that a single speaker acts as both spoken and sound is also recognized as a drawback. Some users are unhappy with the color rendering of the display.

To summarize. Acer Liquid M220 is a smartphone in a classic case running the Windows Phone operating system. The case is made of plastic. Management is carried out by on-screen buttons. One regular SIM card is provided. The dimensions of the device are 64.6 x 124.9 x 9.57 millimeters with a weight of 120 grams. TFT color touch screen, displays 16.78 million colors. The diagonal of the display is 4 inches, type - touch, multi-touch. Resolution - 800 x 480 pixels. The pixel density is 233 per inch.
