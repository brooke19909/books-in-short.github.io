Do-it-yourself stylish accessories

Making stylish accessories with your own hands is easy. To do this, you need only minimal skills, a little free time and beautiful source material: beads, lace, artificial flowers, shiny chains and rhinestones. Home-made jewelry is unique and harmoniously complement the female image, emphasizing style and character.

Choker

The stylish choker is an excellent decoration that has been conquering fashion catwalks around the world for several seasons in a row. Making an accessory with your own hands is not difficult at all. To work, you need such materials:

  • lace 30-35 cm long and 1-4 cm wide;
  • a metal clasp of the same width that can be purchased at a craft store;
  • scissors, pliers.

Take a small piece of lace with a width of 1 to 4 cm. The color can be any: black, white, pink, blue. Such decoration does not require significant financial costs, so you can easily make several options for each outfit. Try lace on the neck so as not to make a mistake with the size. If necessary, shorten it slightly, the choker should fit it tightly, but not squeeze it.

There are a lot of options for fasteners in craft stores, they can be made of gold or silver metal or painted in other colors. Of course, you can do without a fastener and just tie a choker with a narrow ribbon, but this option looks bulky and less aesthetically pleasing. Insert the edges of the tape into the fastener holes and clamp with pliers. You need to act carefully so as not to overdo it and not deform the metal. The choker is ready. For the manufacture of accessories with your own hands, it is best to purchase special pliers, they are small in size, light and comfortable, designed for delicate materials.

Pompom necklace

Making such an accessory with your own hands with a photo and detailed instructions will not take much time. For work you will need:

  • skein;
  • fork;
  • scissors.

Wind the yarn over the prongs of the fork. Tie the skein exactly in the center with a few double knots. Then remove from the fork, carefully cut along the edges with scissors. If the pompom is uneven, cut the protruding threads. String the finished pompons on a cord or chain, fix with glue or thread and wear with pleasure.

If you know how to knit, you can make the necklace more interesting. Make a triangular hat, sew on top a small pompon of a suitable color and fix the jewelry on a long chain. Nobody will definitely have such an accessory (made with their own hands).

Bezel with flowers and stones

The bezel is not only a useful accessory for hair, but also a stylish decoration. To make it more interesting, use imagination and improvised materials. To make this DIY hair accessory you will need:

  • a simple metal or plastic rim;
  • artificial flowers;
  • rhinestones;
  • Super glue.

All you need to do is buy a hoop and pretty flowers, leaves and shining rhinestones, and then gently stick them on the rim with glue. If the base of the flowers is too small or the rim is too thin, then the flower must first be fixed on a piece of fabric, and then fixed on the hoop. Stick stasis in the gaps between the colors. Before starting work, you need to think over the composition and put it in front of you on the table, and only then proceed with gluing.

By the way, you can place flowers not only evenly. Unusually and creatively looks hoop with two magnificent bouquets.

Bead bracelet

Weaving a bead bracelet is not difficult, but how to make it original? Use colored beads and add a chain. Cut the fishing line into strips of equal length. Count the beads individually and lay them out in several piles with an equal amount of detail. Then gently string the beads onto the fishing line and secure to the metal ring on both sides. After that, screw the rings into the extreme links of the chain and clamp with pliers.

To make the bracelet really beautiful, carefully select color combinations. Combinations of blue, white and red, yellow and green, violet and pink, blue and golden look advantageous. The chain can be made of yellow or white metal.

Stylish accessories decorate the image and make it distinctive. Handmade products do not require large financial expenses, the creation process does not take much time and does not require specific skills, so each girl has the opportunity to complement her outfits with unusual accessories.
