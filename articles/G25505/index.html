Hotel "Ibis" (Yaroslavl) offers its guests 177 attractive soundproofed rooms of European level, 4 of which are designed for guests with disabilities. Only two people can be accommodated in each room, no extra beds. Connecting rooms are available for families of four.





Each standard room is equipped with air conditioning, a safe, a telephone, a flat-screen TV, Wi-Fi, and city views. Depending on the wishes of the guests, rooms with one double or two single beds can be provided. The private bathroom includes a hairdryer and shower.
