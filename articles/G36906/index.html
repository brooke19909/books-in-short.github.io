Banking and cash management services for legal entities

Settlement and cash services by a commercial bank of legal entities form the basis of a modern financial system. Such cases, when enterprises are calculated directly, are in the past. Now, for attempting to perform such actions in some cases, even criminal penalties are provided.

general information



To begin with, let's find out what is the settlement and cash services for legal entities in a bank. Under this is called a range of services that are provided by a financial institution and which are aimed at ensuring timely settlement of the organization with various entities. The procedure for their provision is regulated by the Law on Banks and Banking Activities, the Civil Code of the Russian Federation and the regulatory framework of the Central Bank of the Russian Federation. Special attention should be paid to the Regulations on cashless payments, the procedure for conducting cash transactions and other regulatory documents. Also, an agreement between the client and the financial institution is required for work. It should be noted that settlement and cash services for individuals and legal entities can be carried out, and not just the latter.

Basic services of cash settlement



After the contract has been concluded, the organization should receive a checkbook. She will be needed to receive cash for the wages of employees and the needs of the enterprise. There are basic and additional services. The first include:

  1. Issuance of account statements, where information on completed transactions will be kept.
  2. Cashless payments.
  3. Cash operations (the phrase "cash service" may also be used for designation).

Basic services are usually free, or a symbolic commission is taken for their implementation. To carry out any of these operations, you must use a unified form.
