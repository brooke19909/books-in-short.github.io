St. Petersburg - Sosnovy Bor. How to get there

Among the dunes and pines on the shores of the Gulf of Finland is the town of Sosnovy Bor. St. Petersburg is eighty kilometers from it. Pure sea air, infused with needles, amazing tranquility and silence make it unique. Although it may seem boring to some, it will be pleasant for those who appreciate the beauty of nature, while having at hand all the benefits of civilization.

Location of Sosnovy Bor

Until the end of 1958, on the site of Sosnovy Bor there was the village of Ustya, whose origin goes back to antiquity. Mention of her is found on maps of 1676. Its inhabitants were engaged in fishing and forestry. It was in this reserved place that a small village for workers was laid, whose name was given in honor of the beautiful pine forests that stretch along the entire Kopory Bay of the Gulf of Finland, where the future city is located. A river with picturesque banks flows through Sosnovy Bor. It is called Kovashi and flows into the Gulf of Finland.

What is the city famous for?

Atomic scientists live in Sosnovy Bor. Not far from the city is the Leningrad NPP , it is planned to build its second stage. A number of facilities related to nuclear energy are located on the territory of Sosnovy Bor, so until 2013 the city was closed. But today, citizens of Russia can pass into the city without hindrance, having on hand a document confirming the citizenship of Russia.

Part of the city, built in the Soviet era, is located in a pine forest, which is why it got that name. Many modern buildings are being built, which gives a certain flavor. The city is young and does not have ancient historical monuments, but here they create their own unique objects, which over time will become his pride.

These include "Andersengrad" - a fabulous town for children, which in style resembles a Western European city with its town hall tower. Or the temple of the icon "Burning Kupina", this you will not meet anywhere. This icon protects from disasters, destruction.

Drowning in greenery, with magnificent beaches located on the Gulf of Finland, with clean air, with well-developed infrastructure, the city of Sosnovy Bor attracts people who, having been here, return here again, at least for a day, to take a break from the gassed metropolis. How to get to the city by car

Road transport is becoming increasingly popular. First of all, by the fact that it gives the trip an arbitrary character, as you can stay anywhere, see interesting sights. The length of the route Saint Petersburg - Sosnovy Bor is 98 kilometers. If you draw a straight line between the two cities, the distance will be 67 kilometers. Travel time will be 1 hour 40 minutes. If the path begins in St. Petersburg, then it runs through cities and towns:

  • Kronstadt, the distance to it will be 44 kilometers. The duration of the journey to it is 40 minutes.
  • From Kronstadt to Lomonosov, the route will be 17 kilometers. The time will be 15 minutes.
  • We go from Lomonosov to the village of Bolshaya Izhora - 7 kilometers. The trip takes about 10 minutes.
  • From the village of Bolshaya Izhora to the village of Lebyazhye - 8 kilometers. The time is about 10 minutes.
  • From the village of Lebyazhye to the station. Kovashi - 15 kilometers. The duration of the trip is 15 minutes.
  • From Art. Kovashi to the town of Sosnovy Bor - 8 kilometers. The time will be about 10 minutes.

The cost of a trip by car with a fuel price of 39 rubles / liter will be about 315 rubles.
