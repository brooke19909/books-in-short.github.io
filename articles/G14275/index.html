IPhone does not charge: when is it worth to panic?

Let's talk about Apple gadget malfunctions. Consider a fairly common case, namely the situation when the iPhone is not charging. What can serve as the reason for such behavior of the equipment and what to do in case of detection of certain malfunctions?

Battery failure

Perhaps this problem occurs most often. Actively using the gadget, a person somehow depletes the resources of the battery. In addition, damage can occur as a result of chemical or mechanical (for example, when falling) exposure. Also, Apple batteries fail over time (on average, their life is about one year). In such a situation, you just need to purchase a new part and install it.

Charging Connector Issues

The problem may lie not only in the battery itself, but also in the recharge connector. In such a situation, the iPhone does not charge because the connector has oxidized, damaged, or simply become loose during use. Here, the help of a specialist is already required: only he will be able to diagnose the device and, if necessary, replace the part.

Loop Failures

This is another very common reason why the iPhone is not charging. When the device is connected, a message will appear on the screen stating that the smartphone does not support this accessory. The "culprit" of this state of affairs is a loop of synchronization with a computer and a charger. Replacing this element is inexpensive, and the whole procedure takes about 15-20 minutes.

No charging from computer





Many owners of Apple smartphones are faced with the fact that their device completely refuses to charge from a PC. This can happen for several reasons. Firstly, if the computer is in sleep mode or in hibernation, charging is not performed. Also, do not connect the iPhone to the secondary sockets on the front panel. Quite often, computers put these USB ports into power saving mode. In addition, if a large number of power-consuming applications (games, browsers, documents applications) are simultaneously working on the phone, Wi-Fi is turned on, charging will also not occur, because the computer simply will not have time to charge the device.

Moisture

Often after moisture gets in, when liquid has been spilled on the smartphone or it gets wet in the rain, the iPhone does not charge. What to do in such situations? Experts advise the first thing to thoroughly dry the device, after disassembling it. However, this does not always help. If water gets on the motherboard, it may oxidize. Subsequently, the repair of the gadget will cost you several times more. And therefore, if the iPhone does not charge after moisture, it is best to take it to a service center as soon as possible for professional drying and diagnostics.





Inappropriate charger

Despite the fact that manufacturers constantly warn buyers about the need to use original chargers, hundreds of people turn to workshops and service centers every year when they find that iPhone 4 does not charge precisely because these recommendations are not followed. Each specific device model has its own charging requirements. Therefore, if you connect the iPhone to the charging of Samsung or Nokia, you can disable the gadget. Do not trust the so-called "universal" cords. Most of them are assembled in China by incompetent fast workers. The reason why the iPhone is not charging from the "native" device can be either power surges in the outlet itself or a cable break.
