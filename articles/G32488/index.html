How to make a broom from a plastic bottle with your own hands

With the advent of autumn, there is a need for cleaning fallen leaves. Rakes in such situations are practically useless. In this case, you can use a broom made from plastic bottles. The ready-made fixture is ideal for gardening. Since it is very simple to make a broom from a plastic bottle , such a tool is often used for cleaning construction waste. It is convenient and practical. In addition, suitable material can be found in every home.



What is needed for this

So, how to make a broom from a plastic bottle. It is not as difficult as it might seem at first glance. To begin with, it is worth preparing all the necessary tools and material. To make a broom, you will need the following items:

  1. Plastic containers with a volume of at least two liters.
  2. Scissors.
  3. Awl.
  4. Hammer.
  5. Shank, preferably wooden.

How to make a broom from plastic bottles step by step

First you need to prepare the material. In this case, the shape as well as the color of the containers does not matter. The main thing is to find the amount of material you need. Since you want to make a broom out of plastic bottles qualitatively, you will need about seven plastic containers with a volume of two liters.

The material should be thoroughly washed, preferably in warm water. To make labels and glue residues easily move away, you should soak them for about half an hour. After this, the containers should be thoroughly washed and dried.

How to cut bottles

So, how to make a broom out of a plastic bottle needs to be neat, it is worth cutting containers with scissors. If this tool is not at hand, then it can be replaced with a very sharp knife.

One of the plastic bottles should cut off the bottom. It can be safely thrown away. You don‚Äôt need the bottom of the bottle. The lower part of the container must be cut into strips. Their width should be up to two centimeters. As for the length, it can correspond to the size of the bottle. However, this parameter should not be too large. Otherwise, the broom will not be stiff.



Also, plastic bottles should be cut off the neck. All containers should be prepared in the same way. Only one of them needs to leave a neck.

Collect the broom

So, how to make a broom from a plastic bottle neatly cut into strips. Everything is simple. Prepared blanks should be inserted one into one. At the sixth bottle, you need to cut off the neck and bottom so that you get a ring. It must be put on the workpieces assembled together. The finished broom should be gently squeezed in the sides to give it an optimal shape. Then, in the design, you need to make holes with an awl and fasten everything with wire.

In the neck of the bottle you need to insert the stalk and fix it. The broom is ready.
