Location

If in Novosibirsk there is an amazing, fabulous place, ideal for life, then it is already occupied by the cottage community "Lucky Park" (Novosibirsk). Photos clearly demonstrate the picturesque landscape among evergreen pines and curly birches. A stunning view of the beauty of Siberian nature opens from the banks of a small but clean river.





If you, living in Novosibirsk, believe that such a heavenly and picturesque place can be found only far from civilization, then you are deeply mistaken. Want to know where the cottage village "Lucky Park" (Novosibirsk) is located? Transport accessibility

There is a significant drawback in the cottage community "Lucky Park" in Novosibirsk - poorly organized transport links. Yes, it is only 11 km from the city center, while at the moment you can get here only by private car. Of course, public transport routes run in the direction of the village, but there are not so many, and they follow to the nearest settlements - it is unlikely that such a route will be convenient for those who are considering the village for a permanent life without having their own car.





All those who have purchased a cottage or a house for themselves here, strongly recommend that they carefully consider the appropriateness of the purchase and consider it only for those who do not have to use public transport. At the same time, a smooth asphalt road leads to the village.

Technology

Description of the cottage village "Lucky Park" (Novosibirsk) directly indicates that this is a comfort class project, where all the necessary conditions for a full life are created. Buying a house here, you pay not only for a quality asphalt road, but also a set of necessary communications. For each section, 11.5 kW is provided, a central water supply with already filtered water is supplied, and the uninterrupted operation of all the supplied communications is monitored by the management company. The first residents say with confidence that they do not experience any inconvenience.

Plots of the first and second stage are already connected to the power supply, which allows you to use all the necessary household appliances without any restrictions. Currently, there is a gasification of the village, which will only increase the level of comfort provided on its territory. Individual septic tanks, perfectly coping with their task, have become a modern alternative to sewerage.





In addition, the removal of municipal solid waste has already been organized. Construction waste is disposed of by the landowners on their own.

Security

The cottage village "Lucky Park" (Novosibirsk) is fenced on all sides. Its territory is under round-the-clock protection and is equipped with a video surveillance system, which guarantees the highest level of security for every resident. At the entrance, a checkpoint is organized, so strangers simply will not get into the village.

Infrastructure

Many believe that it is difficult to feel as comfortable as in the city on the territory of the cottage village "Lucky Park" (Novosibirsk). Of course, breathing here is easy, but can he provide a developed urban infrastructure?

Of course, there are no objects of social infrastructure in the village: kindergartens, schools and hospitals are located within the city. But all residents will be able to use the mini-market to make the purchase of necessary food products, as well as a modern playground, where all the necessary conditions for organizing leisure activities for children of different ages are created.





Most recently, the developer gave the first tenants another great gift: a great beach area on the lake. The shore was cleaned and strewn with river sand, two heated pools and a waterfall were built. In addition, there will be a real summer cafe, which will operate throughout the beach season. Such a gift has already found approval among the inhabitants of the village, now they can fully relax in nature, without leaving the territory of their home.
