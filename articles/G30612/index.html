How to make a cardboard airplane with your own hands. Several design options

An interesting type of creativity is the manufacture of paper and cardboard crafts. Often in kindergarten and school, children are given the task of making some paperwork with their own hands. From cardboard such products are harder, hold their shape well, do not crease. With such crafts, the child can play for a long time.

Boys love different techniques: cars, helicopters, planes, tanks. All this can be made of waste material, which is at hand in any home. This article will discuss how to make an airplane out of cardboard. Consider several different options, starting with the lightest product.

Flat cardboard plane according to the scheme

For such an air liner you need to have a sheet of corrugated cardboard. You can use any solid box of cookies or shoes. Using the diagram below, silhouettes of parts are drawn on a sheet with a simple pencil. Then, carefully cut out each element with scissors along the contour. The inner hole can be cut with a sharp knife.





Before assembly, you need to apply a drawing to each part or paste it with colored paper. In the end, you just have to put together all the elements. To do this, you do not even need to use glue or adhesive tape. Do-it-yourself cardboard wing and tail are simply inserted tightly into the main part of the structure.

Volumetric flying device

To understand how to make a plane out of cardboard, you need to carefully consider the photo of the craft. Nothing complicated is observed here. It is necessary to draw on the cardboard a drawing of all parts of the structure: a body with a hole for the pilot, a wing, two parts for the tail and two wheels.

In this case, parents can help with the scheme by drawing details with a simple pencil, especially if the child is a preschool or primary school age. Further, the boy can already make a plane out of cardboard with his own hands.





The first part of the work is cutting along the contour with scissors all the details. Then each can either be painted with gouache paints, or pasted over with colored paper. So that the paint does not stain the baby‚Äôs hands during the game, all parts on both sides can be coated with acrylic varnish. It has no smell, dries quickly, and the plane will look brighter, the colors of the colors will appear juicier.

At the end of the work, it remains to assemble the parts into a single whole. Wheels are put on a wooden or metal stick. If it doesn‚Äôt work out, you can just stick them around the edges of the case. That's it, the cardboard model is ready. You can play!

Toilet paper cylinder

Before you make a plane out of cardboard, you need to find: a solid cardboard tube left after using toilet paper, a sheet of corrugated cardboard, a simple pencil, scissors, a knife, felt-tip pen or paint, a brush, a simple pencil.

In the tube you need to cut a hole for the pilot, bending it forward. The following slots for the wings and the shank will be more convenient to make with a sharp knife. In the rear of the aircraft for the tail hole can be made with scissors.





The next step is to draw the details on a piece of cardboard. These are two oval wings: long and short for the shank. Then you need to draw a triangular tail and round off its hypotenuse. The most complex part of the whole structure is the front screw. When the drawing is made, it remains only to cut out the elements and assemble the plane. This is not difficult. The screw can be glued, or you can put it on a nail or paper clip, after gluing the nose of the liner first.

At the end of the work, the product is decorated. This can be done with paints, felt-tip pens, gluing with colored paper. This is at your discretion.

Big model

This version of the aircraft is the most laborious, and a small child is unlikely to be able to independently carry out this venture. Adult assistance will not be superfluous. If your baby is young, then such a technique can be made for games by parents or older children in the family.

First you need to have a large box of corrugated cardboard. If the parents recently purchased a refrigerator or a washing machine, then the cardboard from the packaging can be used for this craft. Before you make a plane out of cardboard, you need to consider the size of the future structure. If the box is large, and there are two children, then it is possible to complete the construction so that two pilots immediately sit down quietly.





Having cut off the excess parts of the box, they first make the body of the aircraft. You can try on by putting the baby there. It should be comfortable in the legs. Further work begins on the details. Since the presented model is a maize, on each side there are two wings located one above the other. They are fastened together by stops, representing structures in the form of the letter "AND".

In order for both the wings and the tail to hold tight, they must be glued with strips of paper both from below and from above. You can use adhesive tape by gluing each part a couple of times. The screw is made of a less thick cardboard in the shape of an ordinary windmill. The wheels can not be installed, so the baby will have a stronger base and he will not fall. If dad or older brother make such a model, the joy of the baby will exceed all expectations.
