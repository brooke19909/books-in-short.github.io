Vacuum cleaner Samsung SC4520: review, specifications, instructions, features and reviews

Vacuum cleaner Samsung SC4520 is a budget option, used for dry cleaning. It went on sale a few years ago, so I already managed to establish itself as a pretty good unit. According to the characteristics that the manufacturer offers, its power is at an average level, but in fact it sucks in dust with such force that it is sometimes difficult to move with a brush. The case is lightweight, small in size, has a nice appearance. The dust container is made of plastic, therefore, cleaning it from dirt does not have to bother.

The small price, of course, hints at the fact that the Samsung SC4520 vacuum cleaner also has disadvantages. For example, the hose is soft, so after a while with careless use it can become deformed. After each cleaning, the filters and dust collector will have to be washed immediately, because they become soiled immediately.

More details about the device, its advantages, disadvantages, price and reviews can be read below. The article also describes how to disassemble a Samsung SC4520 vacuum cleaner.



Specifications

A conventional vacuum cleaner designed for dry cleaning. Consumes 1600 watts of electricity, suction power - 350 watts. There is no bag, a 1.3 liter plastic container is used as a dust bag. There is no power regulator.

The pipe that draws in the trash is integral. Two brushes are included : for the floor and the carpet.

The device weighs 4.3 kg. The width of the vacuum cleaner is 24 cm, the depth is 40 cm, the height is 28 cm.



Functionality

The described vacuum cleaner, which is a device for cleaning dust and small debris, can not be called multifunctional. Suction power is good. This indicator is at an average level, however, for a budget option - a quite distinctive plus.

On the case of the device there are only two buttons that are responsible for turning the unit on and off. There are no indicators. To reduce power, you can open the valve on the hose.





One of the brushes that comes with the kit is suitable for both carpet and smooth flooring. However, buyers complain that the bristles are not enough in order to collect all the hair and wool, you will have to try hard. The second brush copes with its duties perfectly, thanks to it you can vacuum up behind a cupboard or refrigerator.

Equipment

Vacuum cleaner Samsung SC4520, the reviews of which are presented below, has a modest package. However, this is quite expected, given the low cost of the device. What does the manufacturer put into the box, except for the vacuum cleaner itself?

There are:

  • Three filters: pre-engine, motor and output.
  • Instruction
  • Hose.
  • Dust bag (plastic, looks like a container).
  • Two brushes: the main one for carpets and floors, the additional one for collecting dust in hard-to-reach places.
  • A pipe that is designed for suction.

Service

Vacuum cleaner Samsung SC4520, the price of which varies in the range of $ 100-110, is quite simple when it comes to maintenance. In order to wash the parts, it is necessary to disassemble the device. This is easy enough to do. The dust bag is pulled out using the handle. Moreover, it should be noted that when it is removed, garbage does not fall out on all sides - it is rammed along the walls. Under the container, you can find two filters: pre-engine and motor. The output is located on the back side behind the compartment. Disassembly of the Samsung SC4520 vacuum cleaner in a larger version (before the engine) is described a bit below.

The dust bag can easily be washed if it is very dirty. The same thing is done with pre-engine filter. Others must not be washed. But before reloading into the vacuum cleaner, you need to dry all the parts.

The difficulties in servicing the device are that the dust collector is designed for only 1.3 liters, so you will have to clean it every time after cleaning. If this is not done often, then the device will perform its functions poorly. Brushes must also be cleaned.

Quality of cleaning

Each filter for the Samsung SC4520 vacuum cleaner plays a huge role in the operation of the device. They function perfectly: direct duties, according to the owners of such an aggregate, the equipment performs well.

Due to its strong power, the vacuum cleaner easily draws in all trash. Consumers are often faced with the fact that the brush "sticks" to the carpet, and it is difficult to move. It is best to purchase an additional device with a good pile, because the "native" sometimes can not cope with the removal of hair and wool from carpets, especially from carpet.

On the panel you can see a black coating on which the name of the manufacturer flaunts and the maximum power is indicated. The power button is easy to find at the junction with the back wall.
