Divine Middle East where the Dead Sea is located

Probably every person on our planet knows for sure where the Dead Sea is. And not only knows his location, but is well aware of his insane beauty and benefits, seeks to visit here and see with his own eyes the legendary places.





This unique reservoir was described by ancient sources - from the Bible to the works of ancient and Arab authors. Even today it attracts the public with its special microclimate, healing properties and secrets. The Dead Sea has many names. In fact, this is not the sea, but a large lake that does not have access to the ocean. The Jordan, Arnon and Zarka rivers , as well as numerous mineral springs feed it with their waters.

So where is the Dead Sea? In the Middle East, on the border of three states: Israel, Jordan and partially recognized Palestine. Where the biblical prophets send us , where the life of Jesus Christ passed. Where the three great religions of the world arose: Christianity, Judaism and Islam.

If you come to Israel, the Dead Sea must be included in your excursion program along with a visit to Jerusalem and its attractions. From Tel Aviv you need to go to him ninety minutes, from the capital - half an hour. Also, Jordan offers its tours to these places. The Dead Sea, which gave man the famous manuscripts, still holds many secrets and mysteries.





The unique pearl is the lowest point on the planet with its 400 meters below sea level. The length of the lake-sea is eighty kilometers, and the width is seventeen. The sea is deep - about four hundred meters. Here all year round there is high temperature and low humidity. Amazingly clean air, ultraviolet radiation, the richest in its composition water will save you from many ailments. Minerals and dirt are part of the elite cosmetics, and hot springs will return the joy of life and good mood.

Many tourists do not wonder where the Dead Sea is. They just buy a tour to it and enjoy its benefits. The sun, minerals and air will relieve a variety of skin diseases, pulmonary diseases, and soothe the nervous system.



Needless to say, the salinity of the Dead Sea exceeds that of the Atlantic Ocean and the Mediterranean Sea. In addition to salt, water contains bromine and zinc, copper, cobalt, sulfur - only twenty-four minerals! The pH of the water is 9, and the density is so high that a person can lie on the surface, as if on a sofa. Indescribable feeling! On the shores of the planet's most mysterious lake there are also pools with warm water, containers with black mud, and thermal mineral baths. Infrastructure will please showers, hydrogen sulfide baths, special pools for children, beach chairs and tents, various spas, private beaches, cafes and restaurants.
