Review of push-button telephone Nokia 106

Nokia 106 belong to the budget low-cost phones with a fairly simple and affordable functionality. This device allows you to always be in touch, easily fits in your pocket. And most importantly, a simple classic case makes it resistant to scuffs and scratches.

The model was released in 2003. At the moment, it has already been discontinued. However, the demand for the phone is not declining. How can this be explained? First of all, quality. Compared to modern smartphones, the Nokia 106 (reviews allow you to draw similar conclusions), of course, is inferior in level of functionality, but has many other advantages. The owners claim that if you fall from a sufficiently high height, the phone will not only not crash, but will also work without failures. There were also cases when the device fell into the water and after that continued to work normally. The phone can work for about a week without recharging. And troubles like overheating or freezing are completely unfamiliar to the Nokia 106.



Appearance of Nokia 106

By type of case, the model of the phone 106 is a candy bar with a keypad. The body of the device is made of plastic. Thanks to this, Nokia 106 is reliable and easy to use. The phone has small dimensions, which are 112.9x47.5x14.9 mm. The device weighs only 74 grams. Such dimensions make it easy to place the device in your hand, it will not be an obstacle in any situation. For example, drivers say that they can talk on the phone completely unhindered while driving.

Color variety

It is worth noting that the Nokia 106 phone is presented on the market not only in classic white and black, but also in red or orange. Moreover, the rainbow variety is characteristic only of the back cover. As for the front, it remains black.

Functionality of Nokia 106

Functionally, Nokia 106 is equipped quite well. You can put only one SIM card in the phone. There is only enough memory for a contact book with a capacity of 500 contacts, built-in games, ringtones and notifications. The ability to put a memory card is missing.
