African sights: Tanzania and its main sites

Tanzania, the sights of which we will consider in our article, is one of the most interesting regions located in East Africa. Here is the highest African peak described by many authors - Kilimanjaro, here among the picturesque landscapes lies the largest lake of the continent - Victoria. Tanzania is an incredible beauty of exotic islands, nature, almost untouched by a human hand, wild animals that make blood stop in your veins. This is one of those states that you visit once and want to come back again and again.

Three-headed mountain

Lake with a romantic female name

What objects Tanzania boasts of. Attractions near Lake Victoria and the pond itself is another place worth visiting. Victoria is the second largest freshwater lake on Earth . Its northern coast crosses the Equator. The attraction was opened in 1858 by the traveler John Henning Speke.

Many different islands are scattered around the lake, which have become a haven for birds. And around the lake there are many famous reserves and national parks of the continent. The most interesting among them is the national park on the island of Rubondo. Almost four hundred different species of birds live here.

Visiting Lake Victoria is best from June to September.

Park with wild animals

Tanzania (sights, photos of which can be considered in our article are numerous) is famous for another beautiful park - Arusha. This is real Eden for the animals and birds that settled in it. The reserve is conditionally divided into three zones that are not alike. This is the Ngurdoto crater , covered with lush vegetation; Momela lakes, giving off different shades; Meru mountains, representing icy and cold cliffs.

Only in Arusha park can one observe zebras flying through pastures and leopards hiding near waterfalls.
