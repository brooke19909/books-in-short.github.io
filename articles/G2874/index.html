Hotel Abou Sofiane 4 * in Tunisia: photo, review, reviews

Having opted for the Hotel Abou Sofiane 4 * hotel, you can immerse yourself in the alluring atmosphere of the East. Exotic plants, characteristic architecture and decorative elements - all these details create a very pleasant picture for the tourist's eyes.

A bit about the hotel

The territory of the Hotel Abou Sofiane 4 * hotel occupies 50,000 square meters. m. It is worth noting that the hotel shares this area with the neighboring institution Riviera. The history of the hotel dates back to 1983. Since then, the hotel has undergone several renovations, the last of which is dated 2001. The original Moroccan architecture has been fully preserved, but the interior and technical equipment have become more modern.

Hotel Hotel Abou Sofiane 4 * is a complex of two buildings three floors high, surrounded by a picturesque tropical park. In total, the hotel provides 381 rooms of various categories. Thus, the hotel can simultaneously accommodate up to 900 guests.





Location

The resort complex Hotel Abou Sofiane 4 * is located in Sousse in the Port El Kantaoui area, which is the most popular among travelers. The hotel is 8 kilometers from the city center (approximately 15 minutes by taxi or bus). If you want to walk around Monastir, then you will reach its center in 40 minutes (30 km). Enfidha International Airport is 30 km away.

Hotel Abou Sofiane 4 *: room description

By choosing this hotel for your vacation in Tunisia , you can be sure that you will be provided with comfortable conditions for living. Guests can stay in rooms of the following categories:

  • Standard is a compact room designed to provide tourists with everything necessary for a good rest. The rooms can be equipped with both large and single beds. Perhaps two-, three- and four-seater accommodation. 10 rooms of this category are adjacent (i.e. connected by an internal door, which can be locked if necessary).
  • The suite is a spacious apartment with both a bedroom and a living room. The room is equipped with a large king-size orthopedic bed, as well as a folding soft sofa. The balcony offers marvelous sea views.
  • Special rooms for disabled guests provide absolute comfort for guests with disabilities. The doorways here are much wider than in other apartments, and there are also comfortable handrails.

Modern interior trends, as well as elements of oriental traditions are used in the design of rooms of Hotel Abou Sofiane 4 *. The photo shows a luxurious, bright apartment, which is dominated by pleasant pastel colors.

General impression

You can hear many different opinions about the hotel Hotel Abou Sofiane 4 *. The 2016 reviews contain a lot of comments, both positive and negative. Nevertheless, the overall impression largely depends on the mood with which you go on vacation. Anyone looking for flaws will certainly find them, because the hotel is quite old, and the repairs in it are not of the highest quality. But is it not better to focus on a beautiful palm garden and interesting architecture? The same can be said of the staff. If you communicate with employees politely and with a smile, then they will reciprocate.

Perhaps one of the main features of the hotel can be considered excessive loyalty to local vacationers. Of course, one can understand the respect of the Arab to the Arab. Nevertheless, turning a blind eye to attempts to go out of turn everywhere, noisy behavior and other nuances - this is not entirely correct. In general, the hotel has enough positive aspects that completely cover all the flaws and shortcomings. This is a clean warm sea, and delicious food in the restaurant, and date palms, and proximity to the port, and quality cleaning, and much more. Thus, if you go for positive impressions, then you will certainly get them.
