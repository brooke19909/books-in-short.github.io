Such a working strategy brought the company a huge demand for household appliances, because the brand began to create various devices that differ in interesting design, versatility and quality.

What can cook multicooker?

Depending on the model, the following cooking modes are distinguished:

  • "Express";
  • "Extinguishing";
  • "Milk porridge";
  • "Bakery products";
  • "Cereals";
  • "Steamed" and so on.

As you can see, if you want, you can cook anything, the main thing is to find a recipe for a particular dish. Next, we dwell in more detail on the cooking modes.

"Extinguishing"

This mode is intended for cooking dishes that require a long heat treatment. The presented program allows you to cook aspic, aspic, stew vegetables, meat and fish. Cooking time in this mode can be from 10 minutes to 20 hours, it all depends on the recipe.

"Steam"

This is the best option for those who are on a diet, lead a healthy lifestyle and count calories. In this mode, you can cook meat and fish for a couple. The user can choose the cooking time from three options or set it yourself. The shortest cooking time in this mode is five minutes, and the maximum is an hour.

"Frying"

The "Frying" mode allows you to fry vegetables, mushrooms, meat or fish. The program has three predefined options for cooking time and the possibility of manual installation. Pay attention to recipes, and then set the time yourself, if necessary.

"Paste"

This mode allows you to cook any semi-finished products - dumplings, pasta, dumplings and so on. As soon as the water boils, the multicooker beeps so that you can fill up the semi-finished products for cooking. Cooking time in this mode can be from two to twenty minutes.

"Milk porridge"

This mode is ideal for young mothers who need to quickly cook porridge with milk, but no time to monitor it. The hostess can place the ingredients in the slow cooker, turn on this mode and calmly go about their business without fear that the milk will boil away or ‚Äúrun away‚Äù.

Rice / Cereal

This mode allows you to cook buckwheat, rice, millet and other cereals. If you want to cook pilaf, then for this in the slow cooker there is a separate program. Again, this is very convenient, because you can put all the ingredients in the bowl, activate the cooking mode and go about your business.

"Soup"

This mode will help in preparing not only the first courses, but it is also ideal for creating delicious drinks. For example, fruit drinks, compote, jelly and so on. You should not buy packaged juices if you can prepare drinks easily and simply without concentrates.

"Bakery products"

This program is ideal for confectionery. You can cook various muffins, casseroles, biscuits, pies and cakes. The shortest cooking time in this mode is half an hour, and the longest is four hours.
