Hotel "Inter-Sukhum", Abkhazia: overview, description and reviews of tourists

Abkhazia is an ideal place to spend a vacation. Here everyone will find a hotel or a hotel to their taste and condition of the wallet. This hospitable corner on the planet has an ancient history, a distinctive and interesting culture and, most importantly, beautiful nature. Judging by the reviews, rest in the resorts of Abkhazia gives positive emotions and truly unforgettable impressions.



Miracle corner

In recent years, the tourist infrastructure of this Black Sea coast has begun to actively develop. New hotels, pensions and motels began to appear, offering travelers comfortable accommodation and a level of service that meets international standards. Tourists call this country a miracle because on its rather small territory there is everything that nature is rich in. This is an amazing purity of the lake, river, waterfalls, mountains, forests and, of course, the sea. Apparently, this is why holidays in Abkhazia have become so attractive to many people: every visitor searches and finds incredibly beautiful nature, comfort and friendly locals in this country.

It is difficult to find a better place for a spa holiday than Sukhum or Gagra, Gudauta, Pitsunda or New Athos. On the one hand, they are surrounded by the Black Sea, and on the other, the Caucasus Mountains, which protect the coast from the winds and contribute to the creation of a subtropical climate. In the resorts of Abkhazia, therefore, you can relax from early spring to late autumn.



Sukhum Hotels

The hotels of this famous resort are dispersed throughout the central part, as well as along the Kodori Highway. The hotels here are of two types: either they are capitally restored buildings inherited from the Soviet era, or new modern buildings. Many boarding houses, hotels and motels of the capital of Abkhazia have their history for many decades, maintaining the best traditions and at the same time constantly improving the level of service. More and more often, new objects began to appear - these are private mini-hotels that are designed for a wide variety of incomes. At the same time, prices for accommodation in Sukhumi hotels are considered more affordable than on the adjacent Russian Black Sea coast. On average, all offers range from a thousand to six to seven thousand rubles per room per day. Even the most fashionable local hotels delight tourists with their reasonable rates, and especially during the off-season, including the Inter-Sukhum Hotel. This hotel is familiar to many as a former "Intourist".

Beach

The Inter-Sukhum Hotel is located on the city central promenade of Dioskurov. The distance to the sea is only fifty meters. The beach is pebbly. For a comfortable stay here, vacationers are offered sun loungers, umbrellas and deck chairs for an additional fee. The presence of special shoes for children is necessary. The entrance to the sea is comfortable, smooth, which is important for kids. A pleasure boat periodically runs along the coast. He sets sail from the pier, next to which is located the Apra Restaurant, and there is also a yacht club.

Excursions

Arriving in this paradise, many tourists seek not only to sunbathe and swim in the sea, but also to see all the sights of this historic place. At the hotel reception you can book excursions to the Gagra mineral spring - the famous hydropathic, bus tours to New Athos to New Athos caves, the Simon-Kanonitsky Orthodox monastery, the grotto of the Apostle Simon Kanonit. Guides recommend to definitely see the museum and the temple in Pitsunda, go to Lake Ritsa, the Avadhara mineral spring.

Picnics at Gegsky waterfall and trout farm are very popular . Those who wish can go diving and rafting, go horseback riding, to the arboretum and monkey nursery, which is also close to the Inter-Sukhum (hotel).

Reviews

In the capital of Abkhazia, life in the "off season" does not stop. Here, as in the high season, all cafes and summer verandas, discos and restaurants work, and public transport functions.

In recent years, the number of tourists coming here from October to May has increased significantly. And this is not surprising, because vacationers come from a cold winter to a subtropical paradise: most of the trees growing in Abkhazia are evergreen, and some even bloom at this time.



Judging by the reviews, the Inter-Sukhum Hotel is equally comfortable to relax both in summer and at other times of the year. Many tourists note the good location of the hotel, as well as a rich excursion program.

Most Russians rate food at five points. There are no complaints about the work of the staff. Some tourists did not like the fact that in the lobby of the hotel is always smoked, and the fact that walking in the beach is not allowed.

Nevertheless, most vacationers agree on one thing: the price / quality ratio is good, and therefore Inter-Sukhum (3 * hotel) can be a great place to spend a vacation.
