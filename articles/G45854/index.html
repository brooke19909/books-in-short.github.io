Rest in Loo. Boathouses by the sea: overview

The heroine of one of the famous films said a phrase that became popular: in Sochi, everyone had a rest at least once in their life. And it's true - this southern city is incredibly popular among those who can not imagine a vacation without the sea, sun and fun entertainment. Those who come to rest in Sochi often have their favorite places in this city. One such corner is Loo.

Village Features

Despite the fact that the village is not very large, a vacation in Loo is never boring. The main entertainment facility is AquaLoo. This is a water park that operates all year round. But the main pride of the village are beaches with small pebbles mixed with sand - it is pleasant to walk on such a surface. A wide beach strip is equipped with various water activities, including water skiing, diving, jet skiing.





Since the settlement is small, within walking distance are various objects, without which rest in Loo is almost impossible:

  1. Lots of canteens.
  2. The shops.
  3. Cafe.
  4. Restaurant establishments.
  5. Bars

Choosing a place to live

Going to spend a vacation outside the house, everyone plans ahead where to spend their vacation, and tries to book a hotel room. But if you plan to relax in Loo, then besides hotels there are many other options for settlement - the private sector provides a lot of different types of housing, ranging from economy rooms to living rooms, equivalent to standard hotel rooms.

Another of the options for accommodation in Loo - boathouses by the sea. So called comfortable, cozy cottages, where you can live both together, and the whole company, spending time in a pleasant, warm, almost homely atmosphere.

Boathouses: features and differences

Earlier in Loo, part of the coast was built up with boat garages intended for storing catamarans, boats, and boats. But when the number of tourists and vacationers far exceeded the number of places in hotels and private houses of the village, the garages were demolished and boathouses were built in their place.





Cool morning sunrises, picturesque burgundy sunsets, fresh air and wonderful views from windows, terraces, balconies are an ideal accommodation option. That's why among those who want to settle in Loo, the slipways by the sea immediately began to enjoy increased popularity. It contributes to the popularity of the fact that the main tourist infrastructure is concentrated around the boathouses.

Due to the fact that in each of the institutions there are rooms of different capacities and categories, on which the price of accommodation depends, people of any income can live here, who wanted to spend time relaxing at the water's edge. In addition, almost every cottage has its own beach, playground, summer terrace, pool, water transport, terrace with tables. Therefore, to diversify the rest, it is not at all necessary to spend time searching for everything you need somewhere outside the institution.

"Albatross"

In Loo, boathouses by the sea are in high demand precisely because of the location - not only the beach is very close, but also the necessary infrastructure. One of these boathouses is the Albatross cottage. An excellent view of the sea, the waves splash almost at the very threshold - this is what every vacationer dreams of. But most importantly, not only well-off people can stay here: in addition to deluxe rooms, the cottage also has standard ones. Altogether there are 4 rooms in the Albatross. Of them:

  1. Two are designed for 4 people. One with a kitchen, the other without. The view from the balcony of each room is to the sea.
  2. One room is designed for three people. The balcony overlooks the mountains.
  3. There is also a room for 2 people. It is located on the ground floor. The balcony is located on the courtyard side.




In addition, there is a shared kitchen, parking for 4 cars. In the recreation area there is a barbecue, and next to the house there is an open gazebo. Linen is changed every 5 days, towels - every 3 days, but you will have to clean it yourself. If you need to use a washing machine, you will need to additionally pay for this service.

Other facilities:

  1. Air conditioning.
  2. Free shuttle service.
  3. Massage.
  4. Paid trips for excursions.
  5. Ironing

If you decide to book at Albatros Loo, you will need to make an advance payment. Its size is 10% of the room price.

"Chernomor"

Boathouse "Chernomor" in Loo, like other cottages, is located off the coast just 30 meters. Nearby is a supermarket "Magnet", the central market, grocery stores, various cafes, a dining room.





The number of rooms is deluxe, which can accommodate from two to five people. Each of the rooms has everything for a comfortable stay, from the split system to the bathroom with shower. There is a pleasant surprise for the guests of the establishment - a free transfer from the railway station to Loo, but meeting at Adler Airport is a paid service.

The cost of living depends on the season:

  1. Between the beginning of the season and until July 15, you must pay for accommodation at the rate of 1800 rubles. per day.
  2. From July 16 to the end of August the cost of living is 2100 rubles per day.
  3. From September 1 to the end of the season, accommodation will be slightly cheaper - 1650 rubles. per day.

Azov

If we talk about the boathouse "Azov", Loo positions him as one of the most prestigious cottages located in the center. Nearby are all the necessary facilities for vacationers.





The institution provides the following services:

  1. Meeting at the airport.
  2. Meeting at the railway station.
  3. Organization of excursions.
  4. Parking.
  5. Kitchen with dining area.
  6. Laundry.
  7. Brazier.
  8. Different types of entertainment.

You can stay at the institution both for adults themselves and families with children. But the most important thing is that you can relax here not only in summer but also in winter: in Loo, boathouses by the sea and in winter take everyone to rest.

"On the Azov"

Cottage "At Azovskaya" belongs to those institutions that can be booked only through the site, and all year round. If necessary, you can cancel the reservation at the agreed time. The minimum period of stay for which reservation can be made is 5 days.





Another feature of the boathouse is a small number of rooms: only 3 are deluxe. One of them has a kitchen, for others there is an additional kitchen. Features of the number of rooms:

  1. Two rooms are located on the second floor. Designed for one for three guests (balcony from the mountains), and the other for 4 people (balcony facing the sea).
  2. On the third floor there is one room. It has an area much larger than the rest.

The price includes transfer from the railway station, linen change.

Paid services:

  1. Nutrition.
  2. Parking.
  3. Guide services.
  4. Washer.
  5. Organization of trips to attractions.

Here, vacationers with children are happy to take, but for noisy companies and those wishing to come with animals it is better to look for another place.

Address of the institution: Loo, st. Azov, No. 6.
