Santa Marina Deluxe 3 *: description, reviews

Antalya is the capital of the Mediterranean Turkish Riviera. It was this vibrant town, filled with love of life, vibrant colors and bustle, that became the mecca of tourism in Turkey. Antalya is at the same time a starting point for tourists arriving in the country and a major resort.

Antalya - the center of the Turkish Riviera

Starting a conversation about the Santa Marina Deluxe 3 * hotel, which is conveniently located on one of the central streets of Antalya, you must definitely pay due attention to the resort. This is a place on Earth where the sun shines 300 days a year and the beach season 250 days. This is a city of contrasts where in spring you can ski in the Taurus Mountains at the same time, and go down to the sea, swim and sunbathe on the beach.

Antalya attracts tourists with its beauty, warmth and interesting features. Beautiful boulevards, green shady streets, the ancient city, a picturesque harbor, a convenient airport terminal, chic hotels and beaches - all this is Antalya.

Since its foundation, the city has been under the control of various dynasties, peoples and rulers. Antalya attracted the attention of many empires. Each ruler left a mark on the architecture and history of the city. There are a lot of towers, arches, minarets, madrassas, mosques and other buildings of different eras and times in Antalya. All of them are special attractions of the resort. Some of these architectural works of art are not mere witnesses to the history of mankind, but the cultural heritage of UNESCO.

Antalya is a cultural center. This is where most of the film, theater and art festivals take place. A lot of museums, a huge number of entertainment centers, water parks, velvet sand along the coast - all this is Antalya. And all this can be reached if you stay at the Santa Marina Deluxe 3 * hotel. Its location makes it possible for vacationers to visit on their own and with excursions in different places of the city, immerse themselves in the atmosphere of relaxation, touch the origins of human development.

Antalya Attractions

The main places to visit while relaxing in Santa Marina Deluxe 3 * are:

  1. Aquarium of Antalya, which is considered the largest institution of such a plan.
  2. Hydyrlyk tower dating back to the Hellenistic period.
  3. Gates of Hadrian.
  4. Mini-City Miniature Park, which contains mini-copies of all the attractions of Turkey.
  5. Archaeological Museum.
  6. Carain Cave.
  7. Mount Tuneklepepe, which offers a magnificent view of the city.
  8. Kaleici is an ancient region of Antalya.
  9. Park of tropical and aquatic plants.
  10. Waterfalls Duden and Kursunlu.
  11. Mosques.

When you want to take a break from the hustle and bustle, you can just wander through the old streets in the evening, sit under the canopy of trees on a park bench, enjoy the soft heat, lounging in a deck chair by the sea, listening to the whisper of the waves.
