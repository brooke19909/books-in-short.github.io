How to make a card for mom with quality and beauty?

Today in any bookstore or tent you can buy a variety of postcards. They are manufactured industrially, they have good quality and a wide range of applications. But at the same time there is such a thing as card making. It denotes the process of making cards manually. In this case, it turns out a model made in a single copy, and that is especially valuable, filled with warmth, sincerity, individuality. Such a card is suitable as a gift for mom. It will be especially valuable, because when it is made, a piece of the soul of the person who makes it will be enclosed in it. And, of course, mom will be much more pleased to receive just such a little thing.

First postcard

There are many ways to make a card for mom.





Here is the simplest example of such a product. For work you will need: pink paper - 1 sheet, squared paper - 1 sheet, a piece of white chiffon, a piece of braid, fabric flowers - 1 small, 1 larger, bead, glue, pencil, hole punch, scissors. Cut a sheet of pink paper a little smaller than a sheet in a box. It is necessary to arrange so that both sheets coincide on top, on the bottom you need to glue the braid or lace. In one bottom corner we have a large flower. Using a bead we draw the middle. Another, smaller flower is glued to the opposite corner, decorating it with chiffon. Glue a congratulatory inscription in the middle of the card. If desired, the edges and remaining corners can be punctured with a figured hole punch, and flowers can be glued to double-sided tape, then the card will look voluminous. Everything, our craft is ready.

With a tulip

And how to make a card out of paper for mom? Now we will tell. We will make the product with a yellow-white tulip. We will prepare a photocardboard or a finished card in light green. In addition, the remnants of paper are still needed: yellow, white, light green. So, first we will prepare the basis.





It will be paper folded in half. Then on the front side we cut out the outline of the tulip. On a yellow and white sheet of paper, cut 3 flowers. At the same time, we bend them in the middle. We glue on the inside of the card strictly in the slotted hole, first a yellow flower, and then two remaining ones along the fold. On the front side of the postcard we glue the stem with leaves. It remains to write congratulatory words, and the card is ready.

From semolina

How to make a card for mom? Here is another idea. This is a card with a drawing made by semolina. For her we need a sheet of cardboard - 1 piece, pencil, glue, semolina, watercolor paints. Getting started. On the inner side of a bright sheet of cardboard, bent in half, we carry out a drawing with a pencil. Then cover it with PVA glue, sprinkle with semolina on top.

We give time to dry everything, carefully turn it over to remove excess semolina. Now we take watercolor paints and colorize our drawing. On the other side of the postcard we sign congratulations. This craft can be put in a frame, if you initially choose the right size.

Interesting inscription

How to make a card for mom? Now consider another very original idea. In this case, all attention is paid to the execution of the inscription. Having made a greeting card with your own hands in any way, you can make a congratulation on it with milk or lemon juice. Then offer to bring such a secret inscription to the lamp. A surprise awaits mom: the secret will immediately become apparent, and mom will be able to read the congratulation.

Postcard application





How to make a card for mom? Consider a method of manufacturing an application. It is carried out simply. Separate parts of flowers, grass, butterflies, birds, are made in advance on colored folded cardboard. Fantasy is not limited here. You can put in front of you a drawing and then transfer it to a postcard. You can stick on double tape. So the postcard will be more voluminous. You can also decorate this craft at your own discretion: you can add corrugated cardboard or decorate with curly holes, make the corners of the postcard with scissors. Festive inscription will complete the design of such crafts.

Product with embroidery





How to make a card for mom? Embroidery on paper is an original lesson. Why not make a congratulatory inscription in such a technique. Birthday card "Fragrant flower" is made on colored cardboard in blue. On a cardboard folded in half with the color side out, make a pencil drawing. Then we begin to lace to carry out this ornament, securing the lace with another needle and thread of a similar color. Having made a congratulatory inscription, we are completing work on the product.

Conclusion

We looked at several ways to make a card for mom. In any case, the craft made by a son or daughter will definitely respond to the heart of a loved one. How nice mother will be to realize that the child tried very hard when he worked on the card. She will keep her in a conspicuous place so that every time she feels the warmth and care of the little man.
