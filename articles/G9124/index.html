Apartment tax does not come: what if there is no receipt?

More and more often, citizens began to complain that they did not receive apartment tax. What to do in this situation? What features should I look for? Should I be afraid of such a phenomenon? Understanding all this is not as difficult as it seems. In Russia, as in any country, the period of receipt of taxes is monitored by the population. Indeed, in the absence of an appropriate receipt, it will not be possible to deposit funds in time to the state treasury. How to make sure there are no problems with receipts? How dangerous is the situation in which payments with apartment tax do not come?

Hazard rating

The first step is to understand whether it is really worth panicking if tax receipts do not come to the citizen. This situation is not as rare as it seems. It requires special attention.



In fact, such incidents in Russia are not uncommon. You should not be afraid, but also to sit back - too. If you do not pay taxes (even if there is no receipt, there is a certain deadline for paying taxes), the citizen will be in arrears. Accordingly, debt will accumulate and grow daily. Problems with tax authorities are provided.

All this is not too happy. Therefore, you will have to understand how to behave if the apartment tax does not come. What to do in this case? When is there reason to panic? And is it possible to somehow influence the situation?

Not time yet

In fact, one should not immediately panic if tax payments do not come. The fact is that such payments have a certain payment deadline. It is to him that the payment documents will be ready.

To date, taxes on the apartment must be paid before December 1, 2016. Reception of receipts should occur no later than one month before the last day of payment. Accordingly, until about early November, you should not panic. It is recommended that you simply wait for the receipt of receipts. When the time comes, the tax authorities will deliver the payment document themselves.

No data

Still, taxes on the apartment do not come? What to do? If November has come and there are still no bills, you can contact the tax authorities. For example, by calling there. It is likely that the lack of receipts is a consequence of the lack of information about a particular property. Similar situations are not uncommon.



It is recommended that you simply follow the advice of the tax authorities. Sometimes they ask to come in person for a receipt, if it is supposed to enter data on the house left without payment in the near future. But most often they are advised to just wait and not panic. Especially if there is a new building. Taxes will begin to come to the population when the relevant real estate data is passed to the tax authorities to help make the calculations.

Forgetfulness

Doesn't the apartment tax come? What to do in this case? Sometimes receipts are simply forgotten to send out. The tax authorities are the most ordinary people. They may forget to send payment to certain payers. Or the system crashes, which makes it impossible to bring the idea to life.

If a citizen is concerned about the lack of payments, you can personally (with a passport and documents for an apartment) contact the district tax office. There, a receipt will be printed out without fail and immediately handed to the recipient. The process does not take much time. This method is the most effective.



The promised three years are waiting

It is worth paying attention to the fact that taxes have a limitation period. He is 3 years old. Therefore, if payment orders did not come to one or another citizen, it must be remembered that at one fine moment the owner may receive payment with tax for 3 years.

A noteworthy point is that penalties should not be charged unless receipts are actually sent. Then you have to pay only taxes. However, some say that if there are no receipts, then you do not need to pay. It is possible that tax authorities simply forgot to send out payments. No receipt - no problem.

After sale

A variety of transactions are constantly being carried out with real estate. Did you get tax on the apartment sold? What to do? It is necessary first of all to understand that payers pay in a given year for property that was sold in the past period. That is, in 2016, payments come for 2015.

Accordingly, if tax came on the apartment sold in the year the transaction was completed, you will have to pay it. And then it is recommended to report to the tax authorities about the sale of real estate. This technique will save you from unnecessary problems. It is recommended that you take a contract of sale of real estate with you.

Senior Citizens and Taxes

In Russia, pensioners are considered eternal beneficiaries. They are exempt from property taxes. But only in relation to one property. Does a pensioner receive apartment tax? What to do?

If this is the only apartment owned by a citizen, it is best to declare benefits to the tax authorities. Until the pensioner himself submits a standard application for exemption from property taxes, receipts will come again and again.

Sometimes older people do not immediately claim their rights. Then you can make a refund paid as taxes. This is done by the tax authorities.



By the way, if a pensioner has declared his rights, then all payments can be ignored. They should not be paid. It is recommended that you contact the tax authorities and report constantly harassing tax receipts.
