Where is the hotel situated?

This hotel complex is located in the famous resort area called Sharm El Sheikh, between the areas of Coral Bay and Sharks Bay. The resort complex is located in a cozy bay called Um Marikha Bay. By the way, the distance to Naama Bay, popular among travelers, is only seven kilometers. Five kilometers away is the international airport, so the hotel can be reached quickly.
