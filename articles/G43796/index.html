What to look for when choosing a project

When contacting a construction company, the client needs to choose the ideal project option from several dozen proposed in the catalog. It is advisable to determine in advance important nuances and familiarize yourself with the intricacies of the work.

The complexity of the construction and subsequent maintenance of the pool depends on its location. Initially, it is worth deciding on the goals for which the pool serves. It can be used as a resting place or for serious water sports. Near the pool, a sauna is often built, which makes it necessary to think through the ventilation and supply of the main communications when calculating the design of a house with a pool and a sauna inside.

The best option for the location of the pool is the basement or basement of the house. Projects of two-story houses with a swimming pool inside on the upper floors are very expensive. The heavy structure, which is a pool filled with water, can destroy the load-bearing beams, because their construction on the upper floors is dangerous and requires reinforcement of the load-bearing beams and the foundation of the building.

Capacity for the pool

There are two types of water tanks:

  • Stationary or solid. Represents concrete cast casting.
  • Prefabricated option purchased at the store.

Different models of bowls made of composite materials are available. Their volume varies from 6 to 180 m 3 , the length does not exceed 13 meters.

The benefits of stationary bowls

Preference is most often given to stationary tanks due to their advantages:

  1. Ability to choose sizes and shapes.
  2. A wide range of materials for decorative finishes.
  3. A wide selection of fittings and accessories.
  4. Connection of additional functions - fountains, hydromassage, wave acceleration system and others.
  5. Long term of operation.

The dimensions and shape of the pool bowl are selected based on the convenience of getting out of the water and the safety of swimmers. Volumetric structures require expensive and complex maintenance.

Installation of a stationary bowl

It is almost impossible to independently build a stationary pool capacity without technological errors. It is better to entrust the development of the project and the construction of a house with a pool to professionals.

To create stationary containers, different materials are used:

  1. The creation of a monolithic structure of hydraulic concrete. Work is carried out with special skills and knowledge.
  2. Prefabricated structure made of sheets of propylene, reinforced concrete elements, reinforced with concrete screed. Propylene can be replaced with polypropylene or fiberglass.

The tightness of the tank depends on the waterproofing. To do this, the inner and outer walls of the bowl are treated with polymer mastics and penetrating waterproofing, filling the pores and creating a polymer waterproof film on the surface.

Engineering systems

Normal operation of the pool is possible with the construction of a complex of communication systems:

  • Ventilation.
  • Water supply.
  • Heating.
  • Sewerage.
  • Lighting.

The sewer system in the project of a house with a swimming pool inside is special and includes heating, cleaning, disinfection and emergency discharge of water. At the request of the client, a drainage system can be mounted.

With the simultaneous operation of all systems, the load on the electrical network increases. At the construction stage of the house, reinforced wiring is laid, connected to a powerful electrical installation.

Microclimate

The projects of one-story houses with a swimming pool inside are subject to specific sanitary requirements. In internal hydraulic structures, optimal air humidity and temperature should be maintained. The temperature indicators of air and water should not differ by more than 2-3 o C. The construction of a pool in the house requires the use of special technologies for hydro- and thermal insulation of walls and foundations.

Mistakes made during the development of the heating system and supply and exhaust ventilation can lead to serious problems:

  • A favorable environment is formed for the propagation of harmful microorganisms.
  • Accumulation of condensate on the ceiling, which destructively affects structures, even created from materials of high strength.

Proper design and installation of communication systems affect the health of people living in the house and using the pool. Saving on such equipment is unprofitable. It is advisable to install an automatic system that supports certain functions:

  • Humidity control.
  • Providing fresh air.
  • Dew point control.
  • Prevention of condensation.

The choice of the project of houses with a swimming pool inside must be approached with all care and responsibility.
