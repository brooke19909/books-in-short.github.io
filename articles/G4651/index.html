Alcatel Idol 3 Owner Reviews

Alcatel OneTouch Idol 3 is a smartphone that does not have a sky-high price, but nevertheless, is able to please users with a high-quality assembly, a nice design and decent technical characteristics. In this article we will study the main features of Alcatel Idol 3, reviews of owners who have already purchased this device and have been using it for a certain time, as well as their opinion on the quality of its work.





Assembly and design

The buyers are delighted with the assembly: the smartphone lies perfectly in the hand, has an attractive appearance, a slim body (7.4 mm) and fairly light weight (141 grams). Also pleasing is the fact that the device has two conversational speakers located above and below. Thus, it doesn‚Äôt matter exactly how you get the phone out of your pocket, because you can communicate even by holding the case upside down.

Despite the beautiful appearance, robust design and convenient dimensions, some aesthetic flaws in the design are still present. In particular, it was noted that over time, paint from chromed plastic peels off. This is especially true for volume keys and gadget corners.





Screen

"Alcatel Idol 3" (reviews confirm this) has a very high-quality display. Although the smartphone has approximately average brightness, the device produces excellent color reproduction. Also pleased with the good viewing angles, allowing you to see the information on the screen from any angle. In the sun, the display fades, but not much. IPS-matrix with a resolution of 1080p allows you to enjoy watching movies, vivid pictures and entertain yourself with high-quality modern games.

As for the multitouch function, here the screen proved to be quite worthy. There were no serious flaws in this aspect, so we put the sensor in the asset of the gadget.

Specifications

Qualcomm Snapdragon 615 MSM8939 high-quality eight-core processor - four cores operate at a frequency of 1.5 GHz, and four at a frequency of 1 GHz - the device owners really liked it. It allows the system to work quite fast and stable. Using resource-intensive applications will not be a problem, since 2 GB of RAM is installed on board the device. Adreno 405 is responsible for the graphics. Although these characteristics are pretty average today, there have been no complaints from users regarding performance.

By default, 16 GB is available for data storage. At the first stages of using a memory card (the device supports microSD flash drives up to 128 GB), you don‚Äôt have to buy it at all, since the allocated volume of the hard drive is enough to download music, games, applications and low-resolution movies.





All means of communication, such as Wi-Fi, Bluetooth and USB, work fine and do not cause any complaints from the owners. 4G networks are excellent. Here, the Alcatel Idol 3 smartphone (reviews confirm this fact) does an excellent job.

Among the disadvantages of the device, we note a slight freezing of the launcher when unlocking.

Sound

The creators of Alcatel Idol 3 gave an excellent stereo speaker. Customer reviews claim that the sound was loud and clear, so that the phone can be used as a full MP3 player, since the device also works well with a headset.

Camera

Ambiguous reviews about the Alcatel Idol 3 phone were left by lovers of a mobile photo: the camera won an ambiguous opinion. One lacks the quality of the images (optics shoots with a resolution of 13 megapixels), others complain about the meager number of functions and settings.

We note that the gadget can shoot 1080p videos at 30 frames per second with a fairly high quality.

Very flattering reviews were awarded to the front camera of 8 megapixels. It is noted that taking selfie pictures on it is a pleasure: the picture is clear and bright. Of course, in conditions of light shortage, both cameras behave much worse than during daytime shooting.





Battery

The 2910 mAh battery lasts for 1-1.5 days of battery life, subject to moderate use of Alcatel Idol 3. User reviews say that with more active use, most likely, the device will have to be charged every night. But here we must recall the high-quality screen and pretty decent technical specifications, which consume most of the energy.

Conclusion

So, we examined some of the important features of Alcatel Idol 3, reviews of device owners and their opinions on this gadget. In its maximum configuration, Idol 3 will cost users about 14,000 rubles. Is the gadget worth the money? Let's briefly go through all the pros and cons.

Among the advantages are weight, thickness and assembly of the device, excellent screen, good technical specifications, Android 5.0 platform and high-quality sound. Of the minuses, the meager camera functionality is mentioned, as well as not the highest quality of images, an outstanding battery, the longevity of chrome on the case and some launcher glitches. Thus, 14,000 is quite a decent price for such a smartphone.
