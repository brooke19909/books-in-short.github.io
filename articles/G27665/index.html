Bataysk is a small city located near Rostov-on-Don. This village is famous for its special calm atmosphere and picturesque nature. A few years ago, the first water park in Bataisk was built and opened - Don Park. The complex is incredibly popular among residents of the region, what kind of services and entertainment does it offer its guests?

Water park in Bataisk: photos and description of the complex



The comfortable recreation area is divided into three sectors. This is a children's town with a pool, low slides and other attractions. Animation programs are held here, professional artists and presenters work daily. The main adult zone is a large pool with high slopes of varying complexity. Among them there are extreme and medium complexity, which will not be scary for those who are not looking for an extra dose of adrenaline. The third zone is designed for foam parties. At such events, the water park in Bataisk invites everyone in the evenings. In the afternoon, this place is intended for relaxation and a relaxing holiday.

Water park working hours and cost of visiting

If you decide to visit the Don Park water attraction center, do not forget that this is an open-type complex. In bad weather, it is better to abandon the trip, and in the cold season the water park completely closes. In summer, the water entertainment complex is open daily from 10.00 to 22.00. On weekends and holidays, the working hours of the water park in Bataisk may change, check with the administration for details. How much does it cost to visit a water entertainment complex? On weekdays, an adult ticket is 750 rubles, and a child ticket is 500 rubles. On weekends, respectively - 950 rubles is an entrance for adults, 700 - for children.

Feedback from those who have already visited Don Park



Many residents of Bataysk, Rostov-on-Don and other cities of the region were looking forward to the appearance of a new large water park. It is worth noting that at the time of the opening of Don-Park its only analogue was only in the regional center of the region. And then we are talking about an indoor water park, which not all outdoor enthusiasts like to visit in the summer.

Many people like Don Park, since it is a worthy alternative to hanging out on a regular beach of a wild reservoir. All young visitors to the complex are delighted after visiting the water slides and pools. The water park in Bataisk boasts a favorable location: it is conveniently accessible by public and private transport. Near the complex there is a large parking lot, there is a place on it for each guest who arrives by car. Some visitors to the water park are unhappy with the cost of services, but it is worth noting that it does not differ much from the prices for visiting the indoor water entertainment center located in Rostov-on-Don.
