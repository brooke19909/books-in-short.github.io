National Park and Mount Lovcen, Montenegro. How to get to the national park? Reviews

Montenegro is located on the Balkan coast of the Adriatic Sea and is considered a very popular holiday destination for guests from all over the world. On the territory of the state there are a large number of medieval architectural monuments, reserves and parks, beach lines and coastal recreation complexes.





Montenegro National Park

In the middle of the 20th century, in order to preserve and protect the rich and diverse flora and fauna of Montenegro, the Lovcen National Park was established here, which is located in the center of the mountain range of the same name. The air from the Adriatic Sea mixes with the mountain air, due to which we obtain incredibly comfortable and healthy climatic conditions for human health.

Sights of Lovcen Park

Lovcen National Park contains many interesting specimens of the country's cultural and historical heritage. Reviews of tourists report that among them the most magnificent is the ancient mausoleum of Peter the Great. The remains of the great ancient political figure, the famous person of Montenegro Peter II Petrovich Negosh are buried in it . The mausoleum is located at the very top of the Jeziers Mountain.





Another architectural monument located in the park is the village of Negushi. It has only fifty houses and about 500 local residents. The village is historically known for the fact that it was in this place that the famous dynasty of the rulers of Montenegro Negosha was founded.

In the reviews, tourists note that all the buildings in the village differ in national style. In particular, houses are made only of stone. Numerous tourists constantly come to Negushi to smell the old Montenegro, to study national costumes in which local residents make colorful theatrical performances every week, and also try the famous Negush cheese for the whole culinary world.





How to get to Montenegro National Park

In its Lovcen National Park, Montenegro annually receives numerous travelers, both individually and as part of organized tourist groups. You can get to the famous reserve in different ways: by private or rented car, using the services of a taxi or even on foot. Responses of tourists remind that public transport to the reserve is not allocated by the municipality.

  • On personal vehicles, the driver only needs to enter the destination in Lovcen, Montenegro into the modern navigator. How to get to the selected point, then an electronic assistant will tell you, directing the Budva - Cetinje road if traveling to the park from the south, and the Kotor - Cetinje road if traveling from the north.
  • It is easiest to get a taxi, because any driver of commercial vehicles knows the way to the park perfectly.
  • Getting to the park on foot and for a long time is tiring, but very interesting, because the eyes of travelers fully open the picturesque expanses of Montenegro. You need to know when going on a long walk to the top of the mountain, that near Lovcen Park Montenegro is replete with snakes.

The summit of Montenegro - Mount Lovcen

The main attraction of Montenegro is the Lovcen massif itself, in the center of which is the famous Mausoleum of Nyegos. The road leads to the mountain asphalt, but very narrow. Climbing along it, a small parking lot immediately appears, similar to an observation deck. Lovcen Mountain is formed of two peaks, one of which is 1,657 meters high and is called Jezerski Top, and the other, 1,749 meters high, is called Stirovnik. The weather at the top of the mountain range is very windy and cold, so in the reviews, tourists visiting it strongly recommend taking warm clothes with them.





The uniqueness of Lovcen Mountain

In Montenegro, Lovcen Mountain symbolizes the fortress of marriage. Therefore, most weddings take place precisely at its peaks. Bonding young hearts with marriage on Mount Lovcen, Montenegro gives them an unforgettable event, accompanied by a romantic surrounding landscape. Musicians and stylists, professional photographers and a well-organized wedding feast in a local cozy restaurant on top of the mountain range will leave many positive emotions and impressions in the souls of lovers for many years to come. Tourists in their reviews note the colorfulness of such a ceremony and the special atmosphere of nature around.
