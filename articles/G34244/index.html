Romantic and feminine dress with a skirt-sun

Modern dresses have completely different styles. Long, narrow, short, flared, straight - the choice is quite wide, and it is sometimes very difficult to dwell on any one model.

The arrival of spring and the onset of the summer season for any fashionista is an occasion to replenish their wardrobe with at least a couple of new original dresses. A dress with a sun skirt is especially popular with the fair sex. This model is very feminine, so it almost never goes out of style.

A dress with a skirt-sun first appeared in the Middle Ages, since then only the length of the hem has changed. For example, in the 60s of the XX century, the length of such models even reached the floor, and in the 70s the length of the mentioned skirts and dresses was reduced to the knee line, well, and later they became even shorter.





In the modern world of fashion, this style is again experiencing a peak of popularity. Of course, after all, such a model is easily sewn and perfectly fits almost all types of figures. How to make a pattern of a sun skirt can be found in almost any sewing tutorial for beginners. With the help of such a wardrobe item, you can hide full hips, balance the broad shoulders, and emphasize the waist line. Long dress, a skirt-sun made of dense opaque fabric will hide disordered legs, and owners of long legs can use short options for such models. Skinny hips can be visually enhanced with a yoke dress.





Sewing the model in question is quite simple, but the outfit looks very impressive. Over the past seasons, more and more popular models of dresses, with a double skirt-sun and special lush petticoats. This version of the outfit is suitable for special occasions.

A dress with a skirt-sun has many distinctive features: a lush hem, with two seams or without them at all; very feminine and at the same time laconic silhouette; length varies from ultrashort to maximum.

Basically, thin silk, satin or cotton fabrics (plain or printed) are used to make the mentioned models. A special effect has outfits in a cage, small peas or stripes. Models in this playful performance are perfect for theme parties or summer walks. A pleated skirt made of translucent chiffon fabric is also a success.

There are more dense fabrics - wool, velveteen, velvet and even leather; A dress with a skirt-sun, sewn from similar materials is perfect for the cool season.

Replenishing your wardrobe with such a style is only half the story. It is necessary to disassemble all the details that can create that very unique elegant image. Here's what is best to wear a dress with a skirt-sun: high-heeled shoes, a plain belt (contrasting!) Of different widths, a bolero and a short jacket, short gloves, elegant jewelry. The mentioned accessories will make the image complete and give it a special charm.





Dresses with a skirt-sun are distinguished by a variety of styles, and therefore they are universal: office work, club parties, special occasions and Sunday walks - the girl can safely put on the mentioned outfit for any of these events. For a business style, a monophonic model is perfect, the top of which has a shirt type, and you can choose any sleeve - long or short.
