Accommodation

Arco Baleno Apartments Anissaras is a complex of two-story buildings, which provide 25 cozy rooms for guests. Tourists are available such options for accommodation:

  • The standard room is a compact bedroom in which guests can sit on a pair of one and a half beds. It offers views of the luxurious surrounding gardens. The room is decorated in a traditional Cretan style with a predominance of natural materials. Thanks to light shades, the room becomes visually more spacious.
  • Superior is a luxurious spacious apartment, which is distinguished not only by its elegant design, but also by exceptional functionality. In addition to the bedroom area, there is a living room with upholstered furniture for relaxing. A kitchen corner is also highlighted.

In the rooms

The rooms of this hotel are decorated in accordance with local interior traditions, but the equipment meets international standards. In their apartments, guests can take advantage of the following amenities:

  • air conditioning system to regulate air temperature individually;
  • satellite television (there are Russian-language channels);

  • wireless internet signal;

  • a bathroom with bath amenities and a hairdryer;
  • paid safe;
  • a laptop can be provided upon request and at an additional cost.

Negative reviews

A number of negative things you might encounter while relaxing at Arco Baleno Apartments Hotel. Reviews note such significant shortcomings of this hotel:

  • in the evenings, the hotel is filled with rats;
  • dinner consists of three fixed dishes that are repeated daily (guests cannot choose their own food on their own);
  • the door to the bathroom does not close (no lock or hook);
  • old plumbing, which has long been in need of replacement;
  • due to the unsuccessful design of the shower, water spills on the floor during bathing;
  • the bathroom has a pungent smell of dampness due to poor ventilation;
  • lack of a private beach;
  • to get to the sea, you need to go almost a kilometer;
  • poor quality of cleaning (floors do not wash, but simply spread dust on them with a damp cloth);




  • there is no hygienic cosmetics in the bathroom (you need to bring everything with you or buy on the spot);
  • the bus from the airport does not drive to the hotel, but stops somewhere 300 meters from it;
  • in most cases, maids come to their rooms after dinner, when guests want to relax;
  • to get to the sea, you need to go along a busy roadway, where there are no sidewalks and passages;
  • the beach is strewn with large sharp stones.

Conclusion

This hotel is suitable for discerning travelers who do not pay attention to minor flaws. This place will certainly appeal to those who are looking for a relaxing and secluded holiday. Here you can also fully experience the local flavor. Nevertheless, it is worthwhile to be prepared for remoteness from the sea and flaws in the rooms.
