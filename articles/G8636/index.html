Hotel Enki Hotel 4 *, Turkey, Alanya: reviews

The Turkish city of Alanya as a resort has been open since 1960. It was then that the locals learned about the healing properties of the air in Damlatash Cave. But only by the 90s the influx of tourists here increased dramatically. The intensive construction of hotels, attractions, night clubs, restaurants, and beach facilities began. One of the popular modern complexes is Enki Hotel 4 *. Get acquainted with its description, services and entertainment. And we will find out what the reviews of Russian tourists about this resort are.



Description

Enki Hotel 4 * refers to beach hotels. It was built in 2005 and covers an area of ‚Äã‚Äãtwo thousand squares. The last restoration took place in 2015. The hotel is located two kilometers from the center of Alanya and just 300 meters from the beach. The complex is a 5-storey building with an adjacent protected area. The interior of the hotel is discreet, but very cozy. Four-star service allows you to relax here at a relatively low price and tasteful.

Get to Enki Hotel 4 * faster from Gazipasa city airport, which is 33 km from the hotel. To do this, you can take a bus, taxi or rent a car. The distance from Alanya International Airport is 135 km.

The picturesque surroundings, the Mediterranean climate, an abundance of entertainment and hospitable residents make this hotel a paradise and annually attract more than 50 thousand tourists.



Rooms

The rooms of Enki Hotel 4 * in Alanya boast spaciousness and comfort. In total, the hotel has 76 standard apartments, which can accommodate a maximum of three people, and 10 two-room (or family) rooms with possible accommodation for up to six people.

Standard rooms are 26 square meters. m have windows overlooking the pool and the surrounding area. They have a balcony-terrace and a shower room. Comfortable conditions include air conditioning, French beds and cable TV. Also in the rooms there is a telephone, Wi-Fi connection, a minibar, a safe for storing documents and jewelry (for a fee), bath accessories (soap, shampoo, towel) and a hairdryer. Family apartments include two bedrooms. They are equipped with the same amenities, plus a small fridge and an electric kettle.

Rooms are cleaned according to the rules of Enki Hotel 4 * daily, and bed linen and towels are replaced three times a week. Children are welcome to crib. Smoking in rooms is strictly prohibited.

Nutrition

The main restaurant is responsible for food at the hotel. It operates the All-Inclusive system from 7.30 to 23.30. It involves four meals a day with local and European dishes, Turkish drinks. Turkish coffee, European cocktails and freshly squeezed juices are paid separately. Couples with children are provided with highchairs.



In addition, Enki Hotel 4 * has a lobby bar and a poolside bar where you can enjoy refreshing fruit mixes and snacks.

You can enjoy goodies and local delicacies outside the hotel. Cafes and restaurants are open from early morning until late at night. They can be found on the road to the beach and in the surrounding area.

Entertainment

On-site activities feature billiards, darts and table tennis. Fans of a healthy lifestyle can visit the gym absolutely free. The hotel also hosts daily and evening entertainment programs twice a week. And once a week, animators arrange shows for children and adults. Their services can be used to create a bright and unforgettable celebration, of course, for a fee.

The hotel has tourist water paid entertainment (water slides, water polo). Access to the adult and children's pools and the use of umbrellas and sun loungers are free. Children can also enjoy the playground. Sessions of therapeutic massage and visits to the sauna and hammam (Turkish bath), which are included in the list of paid entertainment Alanya Enki Hotel 4 *, will help you relax and improve your health.



The city's attractions

Tourists who are bored in the hotel are pleased to welcome the resort town, which has many different attractions and attractions. Lovers of antiquity can go on an exciting journey through the historical sites of the Turkish city. For example, visit the Kyzyl-Kule watchtower, built in the early 13th century. Or take a walk along the picturesque Dim Chai Valley. Mysterious and majestic caves of Alanya will complement the program of outdoor activities and deliver a lot of pleasant experiences.

For couples with children it is best to go to the Sialanya park, where you can find many fascinating attractions and swim with dolphins. Informative will be a visit to the Archaeological Museum of the city.

Services

Enki Hotel 4 * is always glad to guests. The front desk is open 24 hours a day, and the friendly and executive staff helps with luggage transportation and room service. For travelers, free luggage storage and fax / photocopying are available. Motorists can use the services of round-the-clock parking and car rental.

Also, the list of hotel services includes dry cleaning, laundry, medical office and currency exchange. For convenient movement around the hotel, an elevator and accommodation on the floors of the Enki Hotel 4 * plan are provided. On the ground floor you can find a map of the Turkish resort. It will help you navigate the terrain and choose for yourself a route to entertainment and cultural monuments. At the reception there is free Wi-Fi and terminals for payment cards.



Beach

Sand and pebble beach from the hotel is 300 meters away. It is the property of Enki Hotel 4 * and is equipped with many amenities and attractions. Umbrellas and sun loungers on the beach are provided free of charge. The bar is open with a wide variety of snacks and original cocktails and alcoholic beverages. Here you can also walk along the pier, rent a yacht or make an amazing sea voyage with other tourists. Nearby is a municipal beach with cafes and entertainment.

Alanya is one of the hottest cities in Turkey. The warm Mediterranean Sea contributes to great sunshine here and protects the resort from the cold winds from the Taurus Mountains. The subtropical climate allows you to open the swimming season in Alanya in early spring and extend it until October. The hottest is August. In cool and rainy winters, many services are closed, including Enki Hotel 4 *. However, in the city you can still find interesting places to visit and relax with style.

Reviews

Reviews about Enki Hotel 4 * can be found mostly positive. The level of service and a range of entertainment are rated quite high. That's why the flow of tourists at the resort is growing every year. This helps to develop local infrastructure and improve the quality of services. Enki Hotel 4 * in Turkey is no exception. At the time of its foundation, it had the status of only three stars. Let's see what are the pros and cons of the service discovered by Russian tourists.



pros

  • The positive reviews about the Enki Hotel 4 * in Alanya are noted by the responsive staff, who subtly feel the wishes of tourists and are clearly aimed at improving the level of service.
  • The rooms are clean and comfortable, furnished with modern comfortable furniture. All equipment and hot water run smoothly. The rules for cleaning, changing towels and bed linen are strictly observed. As a bonus, in the refrigerator of the minibar, each guest is offered a bottle of cold mineral water upon check-in.
  • The food in the restaurant is varied. Fresh fruits, vegetables, meat and fish are plentiful. For breakfast, milk porridge and fresh pastries are served. Pleasant impressions of tourists are associated with the original menu and the friendly staff of the restaurant. The service is top notch. The buffet is open from early morning until midnight. Even if you could not get to breakfast or another meal on time, according to the schedule, it is impossible to stay hungry.
  • Very hot days are pleasant to spend by the pool or take a walk in the cool garden on site. Wi-Fi connection anywhere in the hotel and even beyond is good, however, there are restrictions on access to certain sites.
  • Before going on vacation, Russians often ask travel agents who the resort is designed for: young people or couples. Universal can be called Alanya Enki Hotel 4 *. In Alanya, historical monuments, night clubs and modern parks and attractions coexist quite harmoniously.
  • The entertainment and facilities provided for children were appreciated. Daytime entertainment programs, animation and a playground allow adults to devote some time for themselves and have a good time in the bar, billiard room, relax with a massage or bask in the hammam after the pool.
  • The road to the beach is no more than 10 minutes. The shore is clean, equipped. You can relax in the shade of an umbrella on a deck chair. Or take advantage of the attractions offered by the beach. Their prices are quite reasonable.
  • The pools at the hotel, as well as the surrounding area, are marked clean and fully equipped for a quality and comfortable stay. Picturesque landscapes and services of local photographers allow you to take vivid professional photos, preserve the memory of a wonderful vacation.
  • At night, the hotel grounds are decoratively lit, creating a romantic atmosphere for lovers. And since the service is far from a noisy entertainment center, nothing violates the peace of vacationers.

Minuses

  • Administrators work harmoniously, but not always efficiently. Registration may take up to two hours. This is terribly inconvenient for tourists who arrive at the hotel at night and want to check in faster after a long journey.
  • The staff at the hotel is recruited from the local population. He speaks only Turkish and English. Therefore, problems with mutual understanding may arise. You may need the help of a translator. He is at the hotel. But his services must be paid separately.
  • Tourists who visited the Enki Hotel 4 * in Alanya (Turkey) at the beginning of the bathing season, note the lack of variety in the menu of restaurants and service bars.
