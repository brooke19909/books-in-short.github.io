LCD "Bright Valley" (Kazan): photos and reviews

Kazan, the capital of the Republic of Tatarstan, is located in the center of the Russian state, on the left bank of the well-known Volga River. This is a city with a thousand-year history, which is often called the "third capital of Russia." In Kazan, there is a constant continuous tourist flow. And the population of the city already exceeds one million people. LCD "Bright Valley" (Kazan) is designed to solve the problem of the resettlement of residents.

Location of the residential complex





In the city, in the Soviet district, next to the Mamadyshsky tract, the developer chose a place for the construction of the residential complex "Bright Valley" (Kazan). Where the complex is located, it will not be difficult to find. If you leave the city artery from the city center, then after about 1.5 kilometers, you need to turn left onto Nathan Rokhlin Street. On the indicated street there are houses 1/11, 3.7, 10/3, 14/1, 14/2 and with other numbers. This is the LCD "Bright Valley" (Kazan).

You can get to the complex by your own vehicles. And also on public: minibus or bus. The road from the city center takes about 10-15 minutes.

Description of the complex

33 hectares of land are allotted for the construction of a residential complex. The project provides for the construction of 21 residential buildings using monolithic-panel technology, designed to settle almost 16 thousand people. LCD "Bright Valley" (Kazan) will consist of several modules separated by driveways, streets. The floors in the houses are provided from 13 to 18.





Traditionally, as it is now universally accepted among developers, the first floors of buildings, especially in the most large-scale residential complexes, are reserved for non-residential premises and objects of social infrastructure. Almost seven thousand square meters are allocated for their placement.

Owners of their own vehicles cannot but rejoice, and there are more and more of them every year. For their needs, more precisely, for the parking of their cars, specially allocated places are located located near residential buildings. As the Svetlaya Dolina residential complex (Kazan) moves in, the developer provides for the construction of multi-level multi-functional freestanding parking complexes.

As for the appearance of the complex, here the developer tried to translate the requirements of modernity into the external beauty of the project. These are French balconies, panoramic windows, elegant fences.

Infrastructure: external and internal

LCD "Svetlaya Dolina" (Kazan), reviews of which already allow us to talk about the high quality of the building, offers apartments that are primarily suitable for families with children. The developer has foreseen in the project the construction of three kindergartens in the complex. This is in addition to entertainment shopping centers, the first of which was opened five years ago, offering goods at competitive wholesale prices.

In walking distance there are four comprehensive schools, a large number of kindergartens, kindergartens and adult clinics. Not to mention what is planned in the complex itself.

The yard area is being improved, children's playgrounds and sports grounds have been built. There is an opportunity for recreation not only for children, but also for adults, as walkways are provided in the courtyards.

Reviews

LCD "Bright Valley" (Kazan) reviews of residents has the most diverse. Whenever a new apartment building is commissioned, there are both positive and negative opinions of apartment buyers. So in the case of the specified residential complex.

Many already settled apartment owners are satisfied with the location of the complex in the zone of favorable environmental conditions, silence. New children's playgrounds and arrangement of the yard territory are delighted. Many leave positive reviews due to the fact that they like the apartments themselves, their layout and quality of decoration. Most residents of the complex are satisfied with the presence of developed infrastructure: grocery stores, kindergartens, beauty salons.

Of the negative reviews, we should single out those that relate to any minor interruptions in the operation of utility networks, for example, interruptions in power supply. Some residents, on the contrary, are dissatisfied with the quality of decoration of apartments, uncomfortable balconies.

Ecological situation





An apartment in the Svetlaya Dolina residential complex (Kazan) will not bring disappointments, but will bring only positive emotions and can serve as an excellent investment option for available cash. This is partly due to the excellent ecological situation in the area where the residential complex is being built. There are no industrial enterprises. Around is a green array, which makes it possible to breathe fresh air freely.

Therefore, those who decide to purchase an apartment in the residential complex "Bright Valley" will not remain disappointed. On the contrary, life will appear in brighter colors, filled with fresh air and silence.
