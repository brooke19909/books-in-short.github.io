Chaika Resort (Bulgaria / Sunny Beach) - photos, prices and reviews of tourists from Russia

Sunny Beach is seven kilometers of the coastal strip of Bulgaria thirty kilometers north of Burgas. The resort is favorably located away from highways, railways and industrial centers. That is why its beaches are awarded the ‚Äúblue flag‚Äù every year.

sunny Beach



Sunny Beach is one of the largest tourist centers for which Bulgaria is famous. Hotels - Chaika Resort, Royal Palace Helena Sands, Tarsis Club & Spa and many others - are always crowded during the season. In total, the resort has about one hundred upscale hotels of different "stardom".

Sunny Beach is a place where many people come with children. For little travelers, all conditions are created here: a gentle entrance to the water, numerous playgrounds, pools. Since 2003, an upscale water park has been operating here.

Another advantage characteristic of this Bulgarian coast is that all its hotels are built right by the sea, so there is no need to go to the beach for a long time. Sunny Beach is also great for young people. She is attracted to modern night clubs and discos. The whole season, the "golden" youth are "hanging out" here.

Hotels



Since Sunny Beach is considered to be the seaside resort of Bulgaria, where tourists from all European countries come to rest in the "high" season, the hotel infrastructure is quite developed here. The best hotels in this north of the Bulgarian Riviera not only offer comfortable rooms, quality services, but also have fairly affordable prices. Prices

Bulgaria has always been considered a fairly inexpensive place to stay, although today for some reason many people prefer Turkey. There is no resort chaos, such as, for example, in Antalya or Alanya, while nature, beaches, and service in most hotels can give odds to many other countries with no less developed tourist infrastructure. And the confirmation is Sunny Beach.



Prices for accommodation in hotels of this coast are especially attractive for those tourists who prefer a budget vacation. For example, for a night in a three-star hotel you can pay only 30 euros, which is very inexpensive, considering the cost of an average European hostel.

As for tour operators who offer to visit Sunny Beach, they can get a ticket for seven days in a 4 * hotel with full board for two for forty thousand rubles.

The trend of many European resorts - aparthotels - is perfect for families or for large companies. Sunny Beach offers a large selection. It is not difficult to remove them, since there are many offers. And therefore, it affects their price level, which is very attractive. For example, fifty euros costs an average studio at Sunny Beach Aparthotel.

Chaika resort



A member of the Bulgarian tourist concern Victoria Group hotels & resorts, this hotel is built right on the first line. It is considered one of the most popular in the resort of Sunny Beach. Chaika Resort is a hotel complex, which includes three "wings". It is located fifty meters from the beach of Metropol and Arkadia. And in front of them, on the very shore of the sea, stands Chaika Beach, built in 2001.

The complex is considered one of the largest on the entire coast of Bulgaria. It is located in the very center of Sunny Beach, eight kilometers from Nessebar - the old city. Literally, the hotel has a bus stop where regular flights depart to Varna, Burgas, Plovdiv and Sofia. Nearby there are supermarkets and souvenir shops, many cafes and bars, discos.

Infrastructure

The territory of Chaika Resort (Bulgaria) is fully guarded. The snow-white nine-story building with a blue roof and trim stands out against the azure background of the sea.

On the ground floor, vacationers can use the laundry and dry cleaning services, visit the beauty salon, use the left-luggage office and lobby. There are also souvenir kiosks and a currency exchange.

At the round-the-clock reception you can order a taxi, buy a sightseeing tour, rent a car and arrange a transfer to the nearest airport, which is thirty kilometers away.



The Chaika Resort Hotel has a convenient secure parking, which you do not need to reserve in advance. The last major renovation in the building was carried out in 2006.

Number of rooms

There are 908 rooms in the entire hotel complex, 490 of which are located in Chaika Resort. All are decorated stylishly and elegantly, with a predominance of blue and white tones, and are equipped with comfortable furniture.

Each room has a furnished balcony. The rooms have a TV, a refrigerator, a paid minibar, a telephone, a large and comfortable bed, a coffee table with chairs, a large mirror.

Room types - standard (single and double), Suites and studios.

In combined bathrooms - new plumbing, shower or bath, bidet. Superior rooms offer bathrobes and slippers. There is a hairdryer. All hygiene items are updated daily. The maids change bedding twice a week, towels - every day. Pets are not allowed in the Chaika Resort. There are also no conditions for wheelchair users.



Nutrition

The hotel offers its guests the concept of "all inclusive" and "half board". Tables are served as a buffet in the main restaurant called Antique. There is also a pizzeria with a terrace located on the roof of the building, as well as an Irish pub.

Beach

Chaika Resort Hotel, reviews of which mainly begin with a positive impression of the sea, is built on the first line. Three minutes from the main building you can walk to a wonderful beach, the cover of which is golden pure sand.

The entrance to the sea is gentle, which is very convenient for children. There are no tides on this coast of the Black Sea. Sun loungers and umbrellas on the beach are paid, but a fifty-percent discount is made for tourists resting on the all-inclusive concept.

For children

Chaika Resort 4 * offers a small children's pool for its young residents, around which there are always a lot of special staff. There is also an interestingly organized mini club. If desired, parents can get an extra bed in the room.



Entertainment

On the territory of Chaika Resort there is animation, in the evenings shows and other entertainment programs are organized. Those who do not want to go to the beach can sunbathe by the pool, sit there in the bar.

In the mornings, water aerobics classes are organized here. For those who monitor their health on vacation, the hotel offers a fitness center and massage services, for which you need to pay extra. Guests can enjoy the sauna and jacuzzi.

Those who are interested in active types of recreation are invited to play beach volleyball, attend aerobics and water gymnastics classes. There is also the opportunity to play table tennis or billiards. The beach has a wide range of water activities.

Reviews





Many vacationers admit that they chose the Chaika Beach hotel because it is located very close to the sea. They really liked the design, which decorated the reception, halls and some corridors made in the antique style with numerous mirrors.

The location of the hotel was chosen very well: there are many entertainment venues nearby. A lot of positive reviews about nutrition. The menu has a lot of fruits, delicious salads, chicken and meat dishes.

The rooms are equipped with everything you need. The hotel has two excellent restaurants. The territory, although small, but green. There are two open, always clean pools, which have many free sunbeds and umbrellas. The Arkadia building has a SPA center.
