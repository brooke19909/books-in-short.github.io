Travel to Latvia: Sigulda. The city's attractions

In the central part of Latvia, just 53 kilometers from Riga, lies the beautiful city of Sigulda. It is located in a picturesque place on the coast of the Gauja River, surrounded by forests and mountains. There are many unique architectural monuments and structures that attract a large number of tourists from all over the world at any time of the year. What interesting places does Sigulda offer for visiting its guests? Attractions in this city are everywhere. Whatever area you look into, something interesting will surely come across.





What to see first of all, once in the city of Sigulda? Its sights can be combined into a fairly large list, one of the leading positions of which is occupied by Turaida Castle. It is located on the territory of the museum of the same name. From the railway station of the city it is separated by a distance of three kilometers. The height of the castle tower is 5 floors (38 meters). There is an observation deck on the roof with a beautiful view of the reserve.

We continue to inspect the most interesting buildings, which are famous for Sigulda. Sights of the city can not be imagined without such a historical monument as Sigulda Castle, which is considered one of the oldest in the country. Among such structures, Krimulda Castle should be noted, which can be reached with the funicular. It stands on a cliff above the Gauja Valley. The castle was built in the 19th century. It is recommended to inspect the architecture of religious buildings. It is interesting to visit, for example, the Sigulda Lutheran Church of St. Berthold, built in the 13th century.



But Sigulda is famous not only for architectural structures . Attractions include such interesting places as caves on your list. So, in the vicinity of the village, in the Gauja river valley, there are a number of famous natural formations, among which the largest is the Gutmana cave. This is a protected geological object with a depth of 18.8 meters and a height of 12 meters. At the entrance to it there is a spring, the water of which has healing power. In addition to this cave, you can visit such as Maza, Valna, Victor, Krauklu.

Getting acquainted with the most interesting places of the city, one can not help but mention such attractions as the cable car. It is thanks to her that Sigulda gained immense popularity among tourists. This cable car is the link between the hills of Sigulda and Krimulda. Its length is 1060 meters. Besides the fact that it is at an altitude of 40 meters, the road passes twice over the same highway. It is also interesting because it has no intermediate supports. Thrill-seekers are given the opportunity to jump down on a rubber cable.





What else is Sigulda famous for ? Latvia (photo confirms this) is a country with beautiful landscapes. This city is not an exception, once it is impossible to visit the Geuia National Park. The most popular here is its southeastern territory. The park has several lakes, the largest of which is Ungurs.
