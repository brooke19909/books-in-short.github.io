Reflection in toys

Of course, manufacturers of models controlled by remote controls could not stay away from such a popular form of motorsport around the world. And already in 1991 the first drift machine was released. Actually, it was from this that the successful history of radio-controlled cars began for a beautiful and at the same time very aggressive ride. Today, dozens of world famous companies are engaged in their creation. These include monsters such as Traxxas and HSP. In their model range there can be either a standard drift machine on the remote control, designed for beginners, or incredibly powerful professional cars that repeat real cars to the smallest detail. Vehicles are manufactured on a scale of 1: 10. It is he who is considered the world standard for this type of racing and competition.
