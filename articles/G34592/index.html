Cheap hotels of St. Petersburg: photos and reviews

In St. Petersburg there is a huge number of diverse hotels of all categories, of any level of service, comfort and size - from hotels with 3-4 rooms to huge complexes. Among the latter, one can distinguish a hotel such as the Baltic Park Inn, which has 1200 rooms. Almost all hotels of St. Petersburg are located in the Admiralteysky and Central districts, which ensures access to almost all historical, cultural places and entertainment venues, attractions, restaurants and cafes.



The hotels of Vasileostrovsky district are very popular, from where you can easily reach the city center in 10 minutes. Institutions located in the rest of the city are generally cheaper, but it will take more time to get to the main attractions.

Hotel Crowne Plaza St. Petersburg Airport

Crowne plaza st. Petersburg Airport is located at ul. Moscow hotel. St. Petersburg offers its guests 2 Crowne Plaza hotels. This establishment is located at Pulkovo-2 Airport. The hotel offers its guests a rich infrastructure and excellent service. This is an ideal choice for business people and tourists who have arrived to get acquainted with the many attractions of the Northern capital.

There are 294 rooms here, the categories of which are Standard, Suite and Club. All of them contain an LCD TV, modern furniture, free Wi-Fi, air conditioning, safe, telephone, minibar, refrigerator, bathroom with bathtub or shower. It also has a kettle, free drinking water, and a set for making coffee and tea. Each room has an iron and ironing board. Bathrobes and slippers are provided. There are 5 rooms for people with disabilities.

The Skylight restaurant is open here, with a capacity of 227 seats. It offers dishes of Russian and European cuisine. You can try signature cocktails in the Signature-bar, which is called "Russian Standard". Connoisseurs of Mediterranean cuisine will love Cafe Plaza illy. There is also a fitness center with a Russian steam room, sauna, solarium and gym.

Hotel "Ra Nevsky 44"

"Ra Nevsky 44" is a hotel located in the very center of the city, on the main avenue of the Northern Capital, near the Gostiny Dvor. Not far from it there are a huge number of attractions: the Russian Museum, the Church of the Savior on Spilled Blood, the Summer Garden, the Hermitage, the Field of Mars, etc. Locals also call this institution "Hotel Nevskaya".



Every year St. Petersburg receives a huge number of tourists, some of which are happy to be accommodated in this hotel. It has 32 rooms of the categories "deluxe", "comfort" and "standard". They are equipped with necessary furniture, a telephone, an LCD TV, a safe, a fridge, and a bathroom with a jacuzzi or shower. There is free internet. A continental breakfast is also included.

Hotel "Azimut", St. Petersburg

Azimut Hotel is the largest three-star hotel in the city. It is located in the historical part of St. Petersburg on the banks of the Fontanka.

"Azimut Hotel" until 2005 was called the hotel "Soviet". Currently, it belongs to the Azimut Management Group company, and is also part of a hotel chain that operates in many cities of our country.

The room stock of this hotel is very impressive - only 897 rooms. Here they are of different categories: “superior”, “standard”, “luxury superior” and “junior suite”. The rooms are equipped with everything necessary for work, comfortable living and relaxing: modern comfortable furniture, internet (Wi-Fi), telephone, TV, comfortable bathroom.

Hotel "Azimut" (St. Petersburg) for conferences, business events, seminars has 7 conference rooms in which capacity is 10-1100 people. Experienced, highly qualified hotel staff will help you organize any event at the highest level. In addition, the services of guests include a gift shop, a fitness center, a playroom for children, secure parking, a luggage room.

Hotel "Russia", St. Petersburg

Hotel "Russia" is located in the historical center of the city and at the moment is one of the best three-star hotels in the city.



The ten-story building in which it is located was built in 1962. It fits perfectly with the architecture of Chernyshevsky Square, while the convenient location of the establishment allows guests to easily and quickly get to the necessary points of the city, including the main attractions.

Hotel "Russia" (St. Petersburg) has 413 rooms of various categories, which are equipped with everything necessary for a comfortable stay and accommodation. Compared with other hotels located in the city center, the vanity and noise of the metropolis will not disturb the rest of the guests in this place. The hotel on its territory hosted conference rooms equipped with the latest technology, with a total capacity of up to 240 people, a shopping center, restaurants with European, Russian, Japanese and Italian cuisines, a fitness center and a beauty salon.

Hotel Apartments on Smolenka

Considering the hotels of St. Petersburg, it is worth highlighting "Apartments on Smolenka." The hotel is a cozy establishment in the Vasileostrovsky district of the city, located on the banks of the Smolenka River. Nearby are the passenger seaport and the largest exhibition complex Lenexpo.

There are 5 comfortable rooms that have the category of "luxury" and "standard". Each of them has an LCD TV, modern furniture, a bathroom and shower. The suites are air-conditioned. Extras include slippers and a set of towels. The rooms overlook a quiet quiet courtyard. It offers guests - free access to Wi-Fi.

Hotel "Ra Tambovskaya 11"

This St. Petersburg hotel has a very convenient location - it is located in the central area of ​​the city, near Ligovsky Avenue, 2 minutes from the Obvodnoy Canal station. Moskovsky Station is located near the hotel.



The hotel has 73 rooms, the categories of which are “comfort” and “standard”. All rooms contain a telephone (long-distance and international), necessary furniture, cable TV, wireless Internet, a refrigerator, an equipped bathroom and a safe. A continental breakfast is also included in the price.

Hotel Ra Kuznechny 19

Considering the hotels of St. Petersburg, this institution should be highlighted. This hotel is located in the city center, five minutes from Moskovsky Train Station.

The hotel has 36 rooms, which are represented by single and double rooms of the categories "standard" and "deluxe". All of them are equipped with a TV, necessary furniture, a refrigerator, a telephone, a bathroom with a bath or shower, a fan. Guests are provided with a bathrobe, slippers, shampoo, hairdryer, shower caps, and gel. The price includes a continental breakfast - it is served in a small and cozy cafe.

Guests arriving here by their own transport can use the free parking located near the hotel, as well as the guarded parking lot near the Oktyabrsky Concert Hall.

Mini-Hotel Ra Ligovsky 87

If we consider inexpensive hotels in St. Petersburg, then it is worth highlighting "Ra Ligovsky 87". This is a cozy mini-hotel located in the business and historical center of the Northern capital. The hotel is located near the Moscow station, not far from the metro stations "Ploshchad Vosstaniya" and "Ligovsky prospekt".



The hotel has 22 rooms of the categories "comfort", "standard", "luxury" and "deluxe". Each of the rooms has modern comfortable furniture, a plasma panel, air conditioning, a refrigerator, free internet, a bathroom with a jacuzzi or shower. Slippers, bathrobes, shower caps, hairdryer, gel, shampoo are provided. A continental breakfast is also included in the price.

Hotel Asteria

Asteria is a hotel located on the Fontanka Embankment, in the historical part of St. Petersburg. The main attractions of the city and Nevsky Prospect are located in the immediate vicinity. This can boast of a few inexpensive hotels in St. Petersburg.

The six-story separate building in which the hotel is located belongs to buildings of the beginning of the last century. Ten years ago it was reconstructed.

This hotel has 72 rooms of various categories. Each of them is equipped with a TV, modern furniture, shower, telephone and bathroom. At the same time, superior rooms have a minibar, air conditioning, and slippers and bathrobes.

Hotel Sputnik

Today Sputnik is a modern 3-star hotel that offers its guests the city of St. Petersburg. The hotel (reviews about it can be read in the article below) is located in the northern part of the city. The historic center and Nevsky Prospect are within a half-hour radius.



There are 220 rooms of different categories. "Sputnik" can simultaneously accommodate up to 360 guests. There is a cozy restaurant located on the ground floor, as well as a 24-hour cafe, which is located on the fifth. For guests there is a beauty salon, as well as many small but irreplaceable signs of attention and amenities that make life comfortable: a lot of dishes in the morning at the buffet and children's chairs in the restaurant, bath slippers and bathrobes, free internet, modern TVs and a machine for cleaning shoes.

Hotel Vyborg

What other hotels does St. Petersburg offer tourists? The hotel, reviews of which can be read in this section, is located in the north-west of the city.

Vyborgskaya is surrounded by the best parks of the city. Nearby are the most beautiful historical highways of the Northern capital - Bolshoi Prospect of the Petrograd Side and Kamennoostrovsky Prospect.

There are 267 rooms. There are two restaurants, one of which is decorated in an unusual design "Russian Provence". For guests, dishes of European and Russian cuisines are offered. For business events, seminars and conferences there is a conference room with a total capacity of up to 50 people. In addition, it offers guests parking, Wi-Fi zone, tour service, ATM.
