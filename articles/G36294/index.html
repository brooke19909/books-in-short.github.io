What is the tallest building in New York?

To date, the scope of construction is truly amazing. Modern buildings in different parts of the globe have a variety of shapes, sizes and soar to a huge height, literally drowning in the clouds. But the first place in the world in high-rise construction is rightfully occupied by New York. This is a very interesting city, a trip to which can be safely considered incomplete if you do not visit at least one observation deck. Many tall buildings in New York have special viewing platforms located on the upper floors or directly on the roof. Obviously, these buildings offer amazing views of the city and its surroundings. What is the tallest building in New York? You will learn about this by reading the article. Photos of these masterpiece buildings are also available.





Start of construction

New York is one of the most populous cities in the United States. However, long before receiving this title, a real race for the construction of skyscrapers began here. For a while, the city stopped participating in this unusual race, which was connected with the tragedy of September 11, 2001, when terrorists destroyed the tallest buildings in New York - the twin towers of the World Trade Center. For some time, high-rise construction was suspended, and the palm passed to the Arab countries and China. Today, the situation is beginning to change in a positive direction: several skyscrapers were built after 2014, while the city built the highest residential high-rise in the world. Typically, tall buildings are occupied by shopping centers, hotels, offices and similar establishments.

So, the story of the construction of tall buildings in New York begins in 1890, when the construction of the 106-meter "New York World Building" was completed in the city. It is worth noting that this is not the first skyscraper in the city, but it was she who overtook the Trinity Church in height, the spire of which reached 87 meters above sea level. For a long time, World Bealing remained the tallest building in the city. Only in 1899 did a higher construction appear. It is worth noting that at present this high-rise building does not exist, since in 1955 it was demolished in order to expand the approach to the Brooklyn Bridge.

After that, the city began a boom in high-rise construction, and much earlier than in other parts of the globe. Since 1980, 11 tallest buildings in the world have been built here . From 1910 to 1930, 16 skyscrapers "grew", all in all 82 buildings were built from 1890 to the present day. The Empire State Building, TrumpBuilding, Vulvor Building, as well as the Chrysler Building appeared during this period. Each of these buildings for some time held the title of the highest in the world. The first of them was leading for about 40 years.

Bank of America Tower

The Bank of America Tower is 366 meters high and has the status of the fourth tallest building in the city. When developing the project, the environmental friendliness and efficiency of the building were primarily taken into account. All structural elements are aimed at the most efficient use of natural resources. For example, double-glazed windows in a building partially absorb sunlight, but at the same time provide the necessary level of lighting. In addition, they have low heat dissipation, which can significantly save on heating a skyscraper. In addition, there is a system that collects and filters rainwater. The air that enters and exits the building is subjected to special filtration. When constructing the tower, the maximum amount of recycled materials was used.

At night, ice accumulates here, which is used to cool the building in the morning and afternoon. Even the urinals here are designed so that water is not required. They can save up to 30 million liters of water per year and reduce carbon dioxide emissions. The building has its own electricity generator, which is able to provide part of the needs of the building, thus they were able to reduce the cost of delivering energy to the building, which is typical for central power supply.

The building has 55 floors. It is the first in the world to receive a platinum certificate for energy-efficient and environmentally friendly construction.

Chrysler Building

One of the symbols of the city, which has the right to be called the tallest building in New York, more precisely, one of them. It is the property of Chrysler Corporation. It was built in 1930 and has a height of 320 meters. About 11 months after construction, it ranked first in the list of the highest in the world. Currently sold to new owners.

Interesting is the history of its construction. The project was commissioned by a former senator who lost his title before the end of work. Then the project was sold to Chrysler Corporation. The architect of the building is Craig Severance. When erecting the building, he was guided by the fact that he overtook the former partner, and after the ardent enemy of Van Ellen. To achieve the goal, literally a few feet were added to the building to get around the competitor. At some point, the goal was achieved, but Van Ellen received permission to install a 38-meter-high spire on his skyscraper. Without advertising this, he did the work and received a kind of satisfaction from the fact that he was able to still get around his former friend. However, the joy of the rivals was short-lived, because 11 months later the Empire State Building was opened, which ‚Äúovertook‚Äù both buildings.

When designing the building, the Art Deco style was used, but many elements were perplexing to eyewitnesses. So, the upper part of the building is stylized as automobile caps of Chrysler cars of that time, in addition, there are decorations in the form of soaring eagles. For a long time, the architect and owners of the building were criticized for frivolity, but now the Chrysler Building is considered the embodiment of the era of the 20s of the last century, and the building itself is nicknamed "handsome Manhattan." In 2005, the Museum of Skyscrapers awarded him the title of the most beloved architectural solution in the United States, this title was obtained after a survey of more than a thousand residents of the country related to architecture and construction.

On the upper floor for some time there was an observation deck, a little later it was converted into a restaurant.





New York Times Building

The building of 52 floors was built in 2007. The antenna height is 319 meters, which puts the New York Times Building in sixth place with the Chrysler Building. The building was completely given to the needs of the newspaper and was built by order of the owners of the publication. The Italian designer was engaged in architecture, the interior design was developed by the American bureau, and another company was engaged in the task of lighting at night. Thus, the high-rise building is the result of the work of several eminent architectural bureaus.

During the construction, energy-efficient materials were used. So, special double-glazed windows absorb part of the sun's rays and do not allow the building to heat up. Own power station serves 40% of the structure, while the heat during operation is aimed at heating floors.

Observation Decks

As mentioned above, a trip to the largest city in the United States will be incomplete if you do not visit the tallest building in New York with an observation deck with a magnificent view of the metropolis. For a long time, the most interesting panorama opened from the Rockefeller Center, as well as the Empire State Building.

You can climb these observation platforms by elevator, you must first go through several security posts and metal detectors, but when everything is settled, visitors find themselves on the platform from which a wonderful panorama opens 360 degrees. Given that the observation platforms are located on the tallest buildings, nothing blocks the view, and if the weather permits, you can see the whole of New York as the birds see it.

The first thing that catches your eye is the water towers. Almost every building in the city has its own tower, which provides it with water. In addition, on some roofs you can see divorced gardens, sunbeds for sunbathing and completely inhabited surfaces. Someone uses roofs to fit their needs, growing pigeons or other animals, and sometimes not only them. It is known for certain that on some roofs the real bee farms with houses for bees were equipped.

In addition to the inhabited roofs, you can see interesting places of the city and its surroundings - bridges over the Hudson River, skyscrapers in central Manhattan, Styvesant Town - an unusual area with houses of strange architecture that grew in 1940, and on the other side of the Hudson you can see the satellite city of Jersey. In addition, it offers an excellent view of New York's very first skyscraper, the 82-meter Flatiron Building, which was built in 1902.

An interesting fact about the majestic Empire State Building is that in 1945 the US Air Force bomber crashed into it, but did not cause any damage, and the next day the building was again opened for visitors.

The observation deck at Rockefeller Center also boasts excellent views, and it is worth a visit both at night and during the day.
