Sea and beach

Guests of Villa Anjuna remind that there is no private beach at the hotel.

In general, there are a lot of beaches in Goa. And it depends only on the guest whether he will be able to find the best of them. Sand on the beaches comes in different colors: from various shades of red to white.

The Arabian Sea, according to reviews, is very warm. The waves are not small, as one would expect from the sea, but rather large, real ocean. Seashells come into the water, inside of which real hermit crabs live, small crabs run along the sand on the beach. Waves ashore agate and white marble sand. It blows a very gentle and warm wind. You can sunbathe at any time, except for the lunch period, when it is especially hot here (from 12.00 to 15.00), tourists advise, otherwise you can get sunburn. It takes 15 minutes to walk to Anjuna Beach.

If there are big waves at sea, you can take a taxi to other beaches. Reviewers recommend Vagator Beach on the bay, where you can safely swim. Taxi to Goa is very inexpensive, you can safely move.
