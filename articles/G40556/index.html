Overview of hostels in Kaluga: prices and photos

On the banks of the Oka River is the city of Kaluga. A large number of tourists come to it annually. We invite you to learn more about hostels and hotels in Kaluga. There are cheap options, there are expensive ones. Even the most capricious tourist will be able to choose the best hotel.

Price per day - 600 rubles.

The hotel was built in 1973. Located on the picturesque banks of the Oka River, there is a park nearby. It is considered a great place to relax in the fresh air. There are 28 rooms in total. This hostel in Kaluga has a restaurant. If desired, you can order meals in the room. In the summer, there is an outdoor cafe, which has a quiet and cozy atmosphere.

Taxis can be called for free. Inhabit and foreign citizens. There is a TV. The dining room is open and you can use the kettle. For a fee, the hotel offers ironing and laundry services.

Hotel "Pier"

Price per day - 700 rubles.

Hostel in Kaluga, located in the historical district. Due to the amazing nature and clean air, rest in it is as calm as possible. The rooms are both budget and expensive, in which guests will relax with maximum comfort. The interior is made in bright colors. The furniture is stylish. The rooms have a work area. Breakfast is included. If you wish, you can not wait for a meal, but cook it yourself in a shared kitchen. You can purchase products in a nearby store or order their delivery directly to your room. Private parking is available. Ironing facilities can be used if necessary. There is access to the Internet. A bus stop is 250 meters away.

You can pre-book seats in a cinema, cafe, restaurant, bar. The shared kitchen is fully equipped.

Hostel "Rocket"

It is possible to rent a room at a hostel in Kaluga at a very inexpensive price. Due to the excellent location of the complex, guests can easily reach attractions and entertainment. The staff is friendly and kind. There are several categories of rooms: from two to eight. You can cook your own meals in the kitchen. A microwave, stove, dishes and refrigerator are included. If you wish, you can eat in the cafe.

There is parking on site. In the kitchen, guests will find a set of dishes, a kettle, and a dining table. The hostel provides tea and coffee for free. You can come with pets. Cable TV is provided. The hotel has a washing machine.

There is a cafe and a buffet. The airport is 8.4 km from the hotel.

There is a sauna, pool, billiards. Non-smoking rooms available. You can order a taxi through the hotel. The reception is open around the clock. There is a banquet room.

Guests note that the internet and furniture are modern. You can find a room to your taste. Value for money is perfect. The service is top notch. The restaurant is tasty and inexpensive.
