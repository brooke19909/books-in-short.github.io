Minamark Resort & Spa 4 * (Egypt / Hurghada): reviews and photos of tourists

Hotel Minamark Resort & Spa 4 * offers travelers an unforgettable sea vacation in the heart of the famous Hurghada.





Short description of the resort complex

Hotel Minamark Resort & Spa 4 * is a modern resort complex that provides travelers with services in accordance with the all-inclusive system, which operates around the clock. There are several wonderful restaurants, a large number of trendy bars, as well as a huge pool, a well-kept beach and much more.

Hotel Minamark Resort & Spa 4 * offers luxurious apartments for tourists. The rooms are decorated in a stylish and concise manner. The interior is dominated by wood and textiles. Here is the most modern equipment, as well as the entire list of the most necessary amenities that will give you a sense of home comfort.

Food is a subject of special pride, which is paid incredibly much attention to at the Minamark Resort & Spa 4 * hotel complex. It is worth noting that there are only two main restaurants in this hotel. There are also several thematic institutions that can give you a lot of gastronomic surprises.





Where is the hotel

The Minamark Resort & Spa 4 * Resort (Hurghada) is located in the Sakkala district - this is the very center of the city and the Red Sea coast. The airport is very close - only 8 km. And at a distance of 1 km from the hotel is a beautiful seaport. Also near the resort complex is a tourist area, where the best clubs, entertainment complexes, shops and restaurants of Hurghada are concentrated.

Apartment Feature

Perhaps the largest number of resort hotels can boast exactly Egypt. Minamark Resort & Spa 4 * belongs to the category of very popular hotels, which is not least contributed to the good number of rooms of this hotel. So, the following apartments await you:

  • Family rooms are in incredibly high demand, as most tourists prefer to relax with their loved ones. There are two bedrooms, two bathrooms and a small entrance hall. A pleasant surprise for guests will be a balcony with a view of the Red Sea.
  • One-room superior apartments offer double and triple accommodation options. Here, in addition to a king size bed, there is also a seating area with soft chairs and a desk. The windows overlook the garden, as well as a slightly noticeable sea.
  • The standard apartments are a spacious and bright bedroom. Here guests can stay both on a pair of single beds, and on a large double.
  • Deluxe is a spacious two-room apartment. The bedroom can accommodate 2 or 3 guests. It is also worth noting that the upholstered furniture of the living room can serve as an additional place.




Room facilities and amenities

In the apartments of the Minamark Resort & Spa 4 * resort hotel (Hurghada) you will not be able to feel the slightest discomfort, because all the basic amenities that a modern person needs are provided here:

  • air temperature adjustment is carried out using an individual air conditioner;
  • the bathroom has a shower or bath, one or two washbasins (depending on the category of apartments), an electric built-in hairdryer, as well as bath accessories and a set of disposable cosmetics;
  • widescreen television with broadcasting satellite channels (Russian programs are also provided);
  • a minibar with your favorite drinks;
  • a balcony overlooking the hotel’s own beach or green garden (if it is not about the first floor, that is, access to the terrace);
  • the numbers are covered by a wireless Internet signal;
  • A complete set of furniture necessary for a comfortable stay in the apartment.




Restaurants and bars of the hotel and resort complex

Food is such a factor, which, perhaps, more and more than all others, can affect the overall impression of a vacation in a particular hotel. Realizing this, Minamark Resort & Spa 4 * (Egypt, Hurghada) is trying to do everything possible to ensure that guests are satisfied with the cuisine. For this purpose, the following institutions operate on the territory of the resort complex:

  • "Water Blue" and "Sunshine" are the two main restaurants of this hotel, where you can fully have breakfast, lunch or dinner. Due to the fact that the role of the dining room is played by as many as two establishments, queues never accumulate near the buffet, and there is enough food for everyone.
  • The pool bar is an incredibly popular establishment, the rack and chairs of which are located directly in the water. The bartender will be happy to prepare unusual and vibrant cocktails for you, with or without alcohol. Also here you can taste incredibly delicious ice cream.
  • The lobby bar is a calm establishment, the interior of which is designed in a simple classical style. It works around the clock, and at any time you can treat yourself to tea, coffee, snacks and desserts. This bar offers a huge selection of local and imported spirits.
  • "Mazazek" is a bar that is decorated in accordance with national Egyptian interior and design traditions. Only local drinks are offered here. And tourists like this institution for the fact that here you can smoke a classic or Bedouin hookah.
  • Fish restaurant will introduce you to all the secrets of the underwater world, treating you to dishes only from the best and freshest seafood. The facility is open in the afternoons and evenings. Do not forget that to visit this restaurant you must first reserve a table.




In accordance with the internal rules of the resort and hotel complex, guests are not allowed to come to restaurants in open beachwear. Only casual or evening dresses are allowed. As for men, they must be in long trousers.

Positive reviews

Reflection of the positive aspects of the Minamark Resort & Spa 4 * hotel - tourist reviews about it. So, they note:

  • budget rates for accommodation and recreation;
  • very convenient location: close to the beach, and the central part of the city, and the airport, and numerous attractions;
  • compliments: birthday people are presented with a festive cake during dinner;
  • perfect cleanliness on the territory, and on the beach, and in the hotel lobby, and in the rooms;
  • round-the-clock operation of the all-inclusive system;
  • the presence of an understanding Russian manager who promptly and competently helps to resolve all emerging issues.

Negative reviews

The following negative reviews can be found about this resort:

  • weak menu, besides the same every day;
  • too sharp smell of bleach in the pool;
  • since the hotel is quite old, the rooms obviously require at least a facelift;
  • cases of theft in apartments;
  • registration process and check in too long in time;
  • Intrusive representatives of the tour desk, who constantly pester tourists on the beach.
