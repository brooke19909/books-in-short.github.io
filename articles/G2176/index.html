Bellis Deluxe Hotel 5 * (Turkey / Belek / Kadriye): photos and reviews of tourists

Turkey is a hot country, which from April to October becomes an excellent springboard for the relaxation of many tourists from Russia and the CIS countries. Russian-speaking travelers choose it for their vacation, as here you can admire luxurious exoticism for little money and in excellent living conditions.

Various hotels are very popular among guests, most of which are located on the very coast of the sea. One of the often recommended tourist destinations in Russia for recreation is the Bellis Deluxe Hotel 5 *.

Location

This Turkish hotel is located in the province of Belek, on the Mediterranean coast. From all sides it is surrounded by beautiful picturesque nature in the form of sea infinity, tropical plants, sand and endless pure heavenly expanses.

As for the proximity of the recreation area to major transport hubs, it is located near Antalya International Airport - literally 32 kilometers away. It should be noted that this location is very convenient for travelers.

In the place where the Bellis Deluxe Hotel is located, it is always hot, but the temperature here is perfectly mitigated by the Mediterranean air masses, thanks to which vacationers do not suffer from the sweltering heat.

general information

The hotel was built in 1992. Since then, repair and restoration work has been regularly carried out on its territory, thanks to which its building and territory are maintained in good condition. Currently, it has a modern and well-groomed appearance, which attracts a huge number of tourists. Bellis Deluxe Hotel 5 * is fully consistent with the status of "5 stars", which were assigned to it from the very moment of its foundation.

The hotel occupies a very large area - 135 thousand square meters. m. It housed a huge number of places for recreation, entertainment and just for a pleasant pastime.

Rooms

The room capacity of Bellis Deluxe Hotel is quite large. It consists of 557 luxury apartments, in each of which everything is arranged so that tourists can feel comfortable. Also, 4 rooms are presented in luxurious VIP houses - villas, which can afford those people who want privacy and have the opportunity to finance such a luxury. All other apartments are located in a huge six-story building, which attracts the attention of vacationers with its own view.

All rooms in the Bellis Deluxe Hotel 5 * (Turkey) are designed in such a way that luxury and comfort are ideally combined in their interior. As for the overall color scheme of the decoration, it is light. The interior used a huge amount of wood details and a lot of high-quality textiles. The apartments without fail have a bathroom, where all the necessary plumbing is installed (sink, shower, bathtub, bathroom) and furniture (cabinets, mirrors). Towels, bathrobes and slippers, bath amenities and a hairdryer are provided for all guests.

Also, individual air conditioners are installed in all rooms - with their help, residents can independently adjust the air temperature.

Living conditions for children

Since its foundation, Bellis Deluxe Hotel has established itself as a family-friendly holiday destination. Therefore, tourists with small children come here very often. For this category of guests, individual conditions are organized that will make their stay more comfortable.

First of all, separate cots are provided for babies (for those under 12 years old), as well as individual pots. In the restaurant for children there are special comfortable highchairs.

The hotel has several playgrounds where kids can actively relax. In addition, a large pool with a shallow depth has been created for them - 6 low slides are installed in it. These objects are created taking into account all safety requirements.

In the event that parents are forced to leave for a while, they can use the paid services of a nanny or send their child to a kids club - here animators can look after them.

Each guest at the Bellis Deluxe Hotel (Belek) has an all-inclusive meal plan at local restaurants. All institutions are famous for their luxury and exquisite decoration. They organize "buffet" for fans of cuisines of different peoples of the world: Turkish, European, Mediterranean, Japanese, Chinese, French, Italian and even Argentinean. Sport

Fans of a sports lifestyle, relaxing in the Bellis Deluxe Hotel (Belek), will definitely understand that here is a real paradise for them. On the territory of this recreation area there is a huge sports area, which includes a large gym with a wide selection of fitness equipment for different parts of the body - its visit is completely free. Fans of relaxation spend a huge amount of time in a wonderful spa area, which is located on 1700 square meters. m. Pool

The guests of the Bellis Deluxe Hotel 5 * (Belek) spend a lot of time near the pools, of which there are six in the hotel. Each of them is equipped in compliance with all safety standards, there is clean and fresh water everywhere. Around the perimeter of the water zone is an excellent sun terrace, which has a large number of sun loungers and beach umbrellas. There are also several bars where vacationers can have a bite or chill out with drinks and colorful cocktails.

If desired, guests can rent for themselves and the whole company separate pavilions on the beach. For guests staying in apartments located in the villas, their stay is free.

On the beach there are many water activities for vacationers. Here they can rent a yacht, boat or boat for a short trip through the vast expanses of the Mediterranean Sea - this service is often used by couples in love. Fans of extreme sports will not miss the opportunity to learn surfing, hovercraft or water skiing, and for lovers of the depths there is always the possibility of scuba diving accompanied by an instructor.

Prices

The cost of a ticket to such a truly heavenly place as the Bellis Deluxe Hotel 5 * in Belek is different for all tour operators.
