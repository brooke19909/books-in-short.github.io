Jordan hotels - luxury among the sands



Jordanian land is never empty. Tourists come here all year round to take a break from working days, improve their health, gain strength and learn a lot of new and interesting things. Jordan holds many secrets and mysteries, some of them were opened to the eyes of people, but nevertheless, not all. Why is this country so attractive? First of all, by nature. At first glance, it may seem a little boring and even dull, because most of the country's territory is desert, and the main vegetation is concentrated on the coast of the Red and Dead Seas. In fact, every corner is good in its own way: even the sands of the deserts make a lasting impression on travelers. Secondly, local attractions. In Jordan there are a large number of them, many of them were built dozens of millennia ago. All buildings and structures of the country are beautiful in their own way, in addition, they have a rich and interesting history. Thirdly, the hospitality of local people. Anyone who has already been on vacation in Jordan will say that he has never seen such friendly and welcoming people anywhere. Be sure that you will not be looked at as a stranger. For Jordanians, it doesn't matter what country you are from or what skin color you have.

If you have already decided for yourself that you are going to spend your vacation in this wonderful country, then it will not be superfluous for you to know where to stay in order to be comfortable and cozy. At your service numerous hotels of Jordan. Jordan hotels are located both in the capital, the city of Amman, and in other resort cities of the country. Most and part is located on the coast of the Dead and Red Seas.

Hotel Kempinski Ishtar Hotel Dead Sea

This hotel is one of the most popular on the Dead Sea. Around him reigns a unique, rich in history nature. Once, many years ago, a historical drama took place in this place, the participants of which were Aristotle, and Sheba, and even Cleopatra. Holidays in Jordan in this place is a quiet peaceful place where you can get acquainted with dozens of local attractions. The hotel has 117 rooms, complemented by bathrooms and verandas, with beautiful views of the coast and the sea.

Le Royal Amman Hotel 5 *

This hotel is located in the modern Amman area, from the rooms of which there are wonderful views of many of the city's attractions. The rooms comply with international standards and are equipped with a bathroom, refrigerator, minibar, Internet access and TV. Of the additional services, the hotel offers 14 restaurants and bars, many boutiques and shops, 2 swimming pools, a large spa, two conference rooms and much more.

Intercontinental Hotel 5 *

Hotels in Jordan have repeatedly been among the best hotels in the world. Such is Intercontinental 5 *, which was recognized as the best in 2005 by Conde Nast Travelers magazine. Dozens of comfortable rooms are located under the roof of the hotel, including suites, non-smoking rooms and people with disabilities. The hotel has restaurants, a swimming pool, a kids club, a gym, shops, squash court and much more.

Dead Sea Spa Hotel 4 *

Hotel reviews of Jordan can help you make the right choice. On the Internet you will find the advice of those who spent their holidays at the Dead Sea Spa 4 * hotel . Despite the fact that it is four-star and the only one in this category on the coast of the Dead Sea, hundreds of tourists choose it for their holidays. Beautiful nature around, quick access to the beach and the sea, all this makes the hotel attractive and affordable. The hotel rooms are standard, with all amenities. Among the additional services it is worth noting a tennis court, a beauty salon, car rental and much more.
