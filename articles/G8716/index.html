Sights of Kharkov: description, reviews of tourists

Kharkov is one of the most famous and large cities of Ukraine. The mild climate, a large number of entertainment centers, shops, as well as museums and other interesting places attracts a huge number of visitors. What sights of Kharkov are worth seeing if you came to this beautiful city for a short while? We suggest you start your acquaintance with the most interesting places. We will present their description in this article.

Sights, interesting places in Kharkov

If you choose this beautiful city for relaxation, you will receive a large number of positive emotions. Kharkov was founded in the middle of the 17th century, so there are ancient houses, architectural monuments and sculptures that deserve the closest attention. Parks, gardens, squares, temples, museums - can all this be seen in a few days? But visitors always want to see all the most interesting and not to miss anything. Where to start exploring the city? Based on reviews from the Internet, we offer you information about the most popular sights of Kharkov for tourists.

Freedom Square

Perhaps it is worth starting the excursion from the central place of the city. Lovers love to make dates and meet here, and Freedom Square serves as a venue for various concerts and public celebrations. It is impressive in size and several times larger than Red Square in Moscow. This place is considered one of the business cards of the city. Every person who comes to Kharkov needs to be visited here. Near the square there are a large number of shops and institutions of the city. In winter, an ice town is built and a Christmas tree is set up, and in the summer, sand sculpture exhibitions are held.

Gorky Park

We continue to review the popular sights of Kharkov. Gorky Park is the pride of all citizens. It was founded at the end of the 19th century, and today it is one of the ten best parks in the world. A huge number of different trees, shrubs, flowers grow here. In the park you can ride roller skates or a bicycle, walk along the well-groomed paths, ride the rides or admire the sculptures that are located throughout the territory. Citizens and tourists like to be photographed here. Despite the fact that there are always a huge number of visitors in the park, finding a free bench to relax is not a problem. Among the attractions of the Kharkov park is a mirror labyrinth. Finding a way out of it will be a very exciting experience.

House of State Industry

On the main square of the city is one of the tallest buildings. The offices of many industrial enterprises were located here. The first television tower was installed. The building has a huge number of windows and cabinets. It was built using particularly durable technologies and was not damaged during the Great Patriotic War. The house hosts excursions, which we recommend you to visit and learn a lot of interesting things about the history of the city.

Kharkov Art Museum

Among the large number of sights of Kharkov, this institution occupies a worthy place. The museum is located at Zhen Mironosits Street (formerly Sovnarkomovskaya), house 9. At any time of the year, various exhibitions are held here. The museum has more than twenty rooms with a large number of beautiful works of art.

Zoo

We recommend that you definitely visit one of the favorite places for adults and children. The zoo in Kharkov is a huge and diverse fauna.
