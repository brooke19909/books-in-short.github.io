Alfa Insurance - reviews and history

At the moment, AlfaStrakhovanie, which is easily found on the Internet, is one of the largest insurers in the country. It has a huge range of services, so that anyone can find a suitable offer, for example, life insurance, business, car insurance and more. If an individual wants to protect his life and health, he will be able to choose from a huge selection of offers, on any wallet and for all possible circumstances. The most popular insurance programs are for car owners, as well as comprehensive life and health insurance packages, including for the whole family or for travel.





In 1992, the insurance company Alfa Insurance opened its doors for the first time. Since then, reviews about it have not ceased to thunder in the media and on the Internet, because the company has branches in almost every locality in Russia, and the range of its offers is almost unlimited. AlfaStrakhovanie includes the Alfa Group consortium, which also includes Alfa-Bank, Vympel-Com, X5 Retail Group, TNK-BP, Megafon and other giants.

There are AlfaStrakhovanie OJSC and Alfa-Insurance-Life LLC: these two companies with similar names merged, and as a result the AlfaStrakhovanie company was created. User reviews do not single out one of the sub-companies, because the areas of their activity differ: Alfa Insurance-Life deals exclusively with various types of life insurance. Moreover, the total authorized capital of the company is more than 2.5 billion rubles, and the number of representative offices has exceeded 350.





If you are interested in Alfa Insurance, the reviews will also tell you about the highest investment security. An insurer with such a rating undertakes to pay all money due to the client, even in the case of economically difficult periods of its activity. The company is supported by many banks, for example, Raiffeisen Bank, Moskommertsbank, Trust, MDM Bank, Alfa Bank and many others.
