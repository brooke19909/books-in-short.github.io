Interesting places, Moscow region: photos and reviews. The main attractions of the Moscow region

There is a category of people traveling abroad but not knowing anything about the place where they live. Most Muscovites can be attributed to this group, but, fortunately, many still try to get out to nearby cities. So, what interesting places in Moscow and Moscow region can be visited? Where is better to go with children? What is easier to reach by car?

Some people like architecture, others will want to visit the holy springs and temples, and others - fans of outdoor activities. Therefore, to find the most interesting place in the suburbs, suitable for everyone without exception, is an impossible task. Nevertheless, the sights can be conditionally divided into several categories, so that each traveler can find something to his taste and make a route.





By the way, many of the facilities are located very close to the capital, so if you wish, you can drive to them in the summer even by bicycle - combine the pleasant with the useful physical activity. For architecture fans

Those who like ancient buildings, ancient masonry and the spirit of antiquity, it is best to visit regional centers such as Dmitrov, Serpukhov, Mozhaysk, Kolomna, Istra, Volokolamsk, Zaraysk, Gzhel, Pavlovsky Posad. Previously, it was these cities that defended approaches to Moscow, on their territory fortresses of those times, historical buildings were preserved. Here, in the center, are themed museums. Of course, modern life will not allow you to plunge into the spirit of antiquity, but it is quite possible to feel the spirit of the past. In the end, each such town has its own cozy places, nooks and places of interest. The Moscow region, by and large, is part of the capital, but life here seems to slow down its run a little - I just don‚Äôt want to rush anywhere. And ancient architecture disposes of this, casting memories of what was happening here. In addition, you can learn a lot about the old crafts, which remain famous today. Gzhel painting, Pavloposad shawls and much more - with inspiration, you can buy souvenirs to foreign friends for years to come. Many who have visited these ancient towns once say that they are now returning there again and again, they like the local atmosphere so much.





In the footsteps of poets, writers and artists

Shakhmatovo, Muranovo, Abramtsevo, Ostafyevo, Polenovo, Zakharovo, Melikhovo, Serednikovo - many of the most interesting places near Moscow are directly connected with outstanding people of their time. Is it not curious to walk along the paths of the park where Tyutchev walked, or to see the bench on which Pushkin was sitting? Is it not interesting to look out the window and understand what inspired the Wanderers, or to see the oaks that remember Karamzin? Many estates have museums with exhibits belonging to once famous people who lived here. Unfortunately, the other tribal nests of the nobles were abandoned and gradually deteriorate, forgetting the balls and famous local guests. However, they can be interesting in their own way. Visitors note that most of the expositions may be curious only to fans of certain personalities, but to walk along the paths of the parks and see how the nobles lived before, how they led their way of life - this simply cannot be boring.

Historical sights

Moscow neighborhoods remember many events that are now reflected in textbooks. Mostly it is about military history. The battlefields have long been built up by cities and inhabited by people, but the memory of them remains. The famous armored museum in Kubinka, a memorial on the Borodino field in the Mozhaisk district, including an exposition of military equipment in Chernogolovka, the Air Force in Monino, air defense in Balashikha, space forces in Krasnoznamensk, German antifascists in Krasnogorsk and many more.

If this seems not enough to someone, you can continue excursions in the capital itself. By the way, under Taganskaya Square there is a real bunker where visitors are taken. This so-called Cold War Museum is located at a depth of about 60 meters and is a real catacomb, where you can easily go in the wrong place and get lost. Some people even think that you can get into the subway from there. Girls rarely remain enthusiastic about military-themed museums, but boys often come out with literally burning eyes.

Churches and temples of the Moscow Region

Any more or less large city in the European part of Russia is an additional point on the pilgrim's journey, because there are a huge number of them. The suburbs of the capital, like itself, of course, only confirm this rule. Trinity Lavra of St. Sergius, Dmitrov, New Jerusalem, Stupinsky and Chekhov districts, etc. The oldest churches are located in Sergiev Posad, Kolomna, Zvenigorod, the village of Kamenskoye, Mozhaysk. Even just driving along the tracks, you can see beautiful buildings, shaded with golden domes. It is simply impossible to list them, there are so many of them, and each is beautiful in its own way.





As for the holy sources, the most popular are the "Gremyachiy Klyuch" near the village of Malinniki in the Yaroslavl direction and located in Radonezh. Not only places for collecting water are equipped here, but also baths. Every year, thousands of pilgrims take all the attractions of this category, excellent infrastructure has already developed, and there is no need to talk about the Lavra - it is included in the UNESCO World Heritage List. Even the photos and reviews of tourists, who are always enthusiastic, cannot reflect its stunning beauty.

Outdoor activities in winter and summer

The most interesting places near Moscow, many probably associated with sports. Well, they will also have something to do. In winter, many residents of the capital and its environs travel north - to Yakhroma and Sorochany, where good ski slopes, downhill runs for tubing, etc. are equipped. In summer, you can play beach volleyball on the Oka, arrange swims, organize bike rides and hiking in the nearby forests. and fields. In addition, there are several parachuting clubs in the Moscow Region, so adrenaline maniacs can tickle their nerves by jumping out of the plane, both in tandem with the instructor, and alone. Those who do not want to take such a risk so far can arrange a flight in a wind tunnel or do base jumping - an unforgettable experience is guaranteed. Well, giving them feedback is not an easy task, you need to experience it yourself.

For lovers of mysticism

There are a lot of people who like mysterious places with inexplicable phenomena and ghost stories. So in the suburbs, too, there is something to visit. For example, it may be the vicinity of Yakhroma, where the ancient Kindyakovsky stone is located, which, according to legend, heals diseases and fulfills cherished desires. You can also visit the mounds located near Peredelkino. In the local forest are the remains of French soldiers who came to Moscow in 1812. Many people who come to these places feel a special energy, and electrical appliances do not always behave predictably.





Not far from the capital there is another amazing place that has long been one of the most dangerous. This is the so-called "Death Road" - a highway near Lytkarino. For a short period of time on a flat area, more than two dozen major accidents occurred for obvious reasons which were not. Smerdyachye Lake is also very popular near the village of Baksheevo in the Shatursky district. It is almost perfectly round, and local residents are afraid to swim here - visitors often drown. Another interesting fact is that there are a lot of reservoirs with a very similar shape in the vicinity, scientists suspect that several fragments of a large meteorite once fell in this area. Opinions of visitors vary: it seems boring to some, others build their own theories regarding such places, so it's better to find the time and get out for a day to unravel the secrets of the Moscow region.
