Let's start with a simple: preparation for removing the glass

  • Remove the rear protective cover.
  • Disconnect the battery of the device.
  • Take a SIM card and a memory card.

No need to unscrew the screws and remove the attachments. The stiffer the design, the better.

Warming up the screen plane: a crucial moment

Repair of the Samsung Galaxy S3 device - replacing the glass separately from the module - implies the mandatory use of a hairdryer, since the protective element is firmly glued to the touchscreen of the device. However, the touch navigation buttons located at the bottom of the phone are also firmly fixed using the above fixing substance. As you know, the glue must be softened before you separate the glass from the module.

  • Turn on the hairdryer and adjust the discharge air temperature to the optimum mode (50-70 degrees Celsius).
  • In a circular motion, holding the nozzle at a distance of 3-5 cm from the glass, warm the surface to a state of noticeable heat.
  • Using a spatula or knife, carefully tuck out any of the upper edges of the protective element.
  • Insert one of the trained mediators into the open lumen.
  • Make at least three points of "undermining" the glass (in the upper part).
  • Repeat the warm-up process briefly.

This is just the beginning of the Samsung Galaxy S3. Replacing the glass further will require more painstaking efforts. Be careful, and you will succeed!

Separation of glass from the display module

  • When the protective element slightly moves away from the case and the screen, insert a bank card in the cleared gap.
  • Slowly and very carefully, move the separating tool inward of the part toward the center until the edge of the credit card comes out on the other side of the display module.
  • Warm up the adhesive systematically.
  • Gradually, and most importantly, slowly, advance the bank card down the plane to the main navigation buttons.

Attention: the sensor sensors (keys mentioned above) must be carefully separated from the glass until the dismantled part is completely removed. Otherwise, there is a high probability of damage to the connection cable of the buttons, which means that a visit to the service center simply cannot be avoided. Cleaning the display module

After you manage to remove the glass painlessly, you need to remove the adhesive residue from the surface of the touchscreen.

  • The glue can be easily removed using a conventional window cleaner. However, before using such a tool, you must make sure that its composition does not have aggressive substances.
  • Normal microfiber will help you finish the gloss.

Installing a new protective part

Glue the inner edges of the smartphone (around the perimeter of the screen) with double-sided tape. Make sure that the fixing tape does not extend beyond the borders of the case frame and does not overlap the outer areas of the display module. Position the part to be installed. Remember that in the case of incorrect position of the glass with one hundred percent probability, dust and moisture will penetrate into the screen.
