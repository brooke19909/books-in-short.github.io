How to transfer money from Webmoney to Qiwi? Now it is much easier to do!

Today, a significant part of the working population earns without leaving home. Freelance professions are becoming more diverse, and the popularity of remote earnings is growing every day. Indeed, if you have the opportunity to realize your talents and skills, doing freelance work, then this is a great success. As a rule, earning here depends solely on your abilities and hard work, and not on the number of hours spent in the office. The money for the work done comes in electronic form to the web-wallet that every freelancer has. There are many ways to turn virtual money into real ones. One of them is to withdraw through the Qiwi system. We will talk about how to transfer money from Webmoney to Qiwi in this article.





About the new relationship between Webmoney and Qiwi

Until recently, everything was much more problematic and complicated than today. Users puzzled over how to transfer money from Webmoney to Qiwi with minimal losses (sometimes they reached 8-10% through the use of third-party services). At the moment, transfers between these two systems, and hence the withdrawal of funds, have become easier. Now there is no need to use third-party exchangers or pre-crediting web-money to your mobile phone account. The two services came together and offered their customers a wonderful thing - replenishing Qiwi through Webmoney and vice versa by linking wallets to each other.

In order to easily make money transfers between the two services, you need to link these electronic wallets. This is possible with the appropriate WM certificate. Many have it, but in case of its absence, take care of receiving using the information on the official Webmoney resource. Next, you can proceed to the binding procedure.





How to connect two wallets among themselves: instruction

Go to the Qiwi website and go through the authorization process. In the column on the right, find the "Webmoney" item and select the "Bind" action. A page opens with information that it is now possible to easily withdraw money from Webmoney through Qiwi, as well as about the transfer fee (3%). After clicking on the "Link Webmoney" button at the bottom of the page, you will be asked to fill out a questionnaire. It must indicate the full name, wallet number, passport data and indicate the limit on the transfer of funds.

Next, a window will appear with a link to the Webmoney website. Follow it and go to your WM-wallet. In the "Accounts" section, attach a Qiwi wallet and fill out another form in which you need to specify the code (it will come in the form of an SMS message). The linked accounts will be depicted as a rectangle, which should turn green from gray (this indicates that the binding has taken place). You need to confirm confidence in the Webmoney service by clicking the "S" button in this box. You should also do this on the Qiwi website (in the "Security" section in the settings). Now your wallets on two services are connected, and you don‚Äôt have to worry about how to transfer money from Webmoney to Qiwi.





The procedure for transferring money between wallets

In order for funds to go to the Qiwi wallet, you need to go to your personal account and find the "Webmoney" block. Information about all active WM-wallets will be displayed there. Follow the link to manage your accounts and select the desired action (there are items such as "Transfer money from Webmoney to Qiwi" or "Top up Webmoney wallet"). Fill in the appropriate transfer form, indicating the required amount. You can also leave a comment (below the line with the amount) in order to quickly find payment information in reports.

Here is such a simple and understandable procedure. By tying wallets created on two services once, you can regularly make transfers to one and the other side quickly and conveniently. By the way, it is possible to bind to the Qiwi wallet not one, but several Webmoney purses.
