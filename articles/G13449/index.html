What is remarkable about the Orekhovo metro station?

Among the numerous stations of the Moscow Metro there are those that were built relatively recently. And although some of them are not of particular interest from a historical point of view, residents of the capital still consider them to be relatives. Take, for example, the Orekhovo metro station, which is located on the Zamoskvoretskaya branch. People living in the Orekhovo-Borisovo area most likely consider it the best. Hearing the familiar name from the mouth of the speaker in the train car, they understand that in a few minutes they will be at home.

History of Orekhovo metro station



As part of the Kashirskaya - Orekhovo section, on the eve of the New Year 1985, a metro station started operating. But literally after a short time period, an accident occurred on the Tsaritsyno - Orekhovo segment. Due to a violation of insulation, the tunnel in this section of the train run was flooded. It took more than a month to eliminate the consequences of the accident and recover. So the passengers were able to step on the platform of the Orekhovo station again only in early February 1985.

Of course, each building has its own history of the origin of the name. Where did the name of the Orekhovo metro station come from? A map of the capital will help answer this question. It turns out that in the territory where the underground was laid today, there used to be a village of the same name, and nearby there was also Orekhovy Boulevard.

General Description and Specifications





Orekhovo metro station is a typical shallow station, its depth is only 9 m. The lobby is decorated in the style of themes related to environmental protection. It immediately catches the eye when looking at the bronze sculptures adorning the station.

The walls and columns along the paths are faced with white and gray marble. But the central hall is constructed from a monolithic structure. In the center of the ceiling are xenon lights. In the hall you can count 52 columns located in 2 rows, the distance between which is 6.5 m.

Behind the station there are dead ends, which in the past were used to turn trains. And after the Zamoskvoretskaya line was extended, a service point and night parking for trains were located here. But during the morning hours, due to the heavy loading of the station, the trains still turn around at this place.

At the Orekhovo station there is only one lobby. And you can get out of the subway through the underpass directly to Bazhenova street or Shipilovsky passage.





Every day, the station begins its work at 5:35, and stops exactly at 1:00. Fans of talking on the phone can do this while waiting for the train, since the well-known mobile operators MTS, Beeline and Megafon work here.

Features

What is remarkable about the sleeping area of the Orekhovo metro station? The local infrastructure in this place is quite developed. Near the station there are many cafes and shops, there are several medical institutions, including a dental center. And there is a comprehensive school, a hotel management college, a whole hotel complex "Orekhovo" and a hotel.

And most importantly, from the Orekhovo metro station it is very easy to get to the famous Tsaritsyno Park, the palace of the times of Catherine II.
