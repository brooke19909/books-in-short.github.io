Snowman crochet - diagram and description, instructions for beginners

It is now very popular to give hand-made souvenirs. There are a lot of types of needlework: some are more, others are less expensive both in materials and in time. For example, a snowman crochets easily. The scheme and description of the work are given below.



New Year's snowman souvenirs

The eve of the winter holidays is always a pleasant time to prepare gifts and surprises for relatives and friends. For those who like to needlework or just starting to master this direction, there is a great opportunity to make cute winter ornaments crocheted in the form of snowmen. You can make such options:

  • A flat figure made of circles as a Christmas tree toy, an element of a garland or a magnet.
  • Functional items for the kitchen (potholder, heating pad on the kettle or warm decor for the cup).
  • Volume figures - from miniature size to full interior soft toys.

Choose your favorite option, follow the instructions, and you will get a very beautiful crochet snowman. The scheme and description are quite simple, so understanding the sequence of work will not be difficult. And a gift made in the form of a cute winter character will surely take its rightful place among your friends and relatives' home decorations.





Simple souvenir (flat object)

The most basic snowman with a crochet can be made of ordinary circles (one or several). To make the circle not tightened and without corners, it is important to correctly and evenly distribute the increase. Any circle fits in a spiral.

Work like this:

- Perform 6 chain stitches.
- Connect the element to the ring.
- 1 row: Complete 6 single crochet stitches.
- 2 row: In each loop of the previous row, knit two single crochet (= 12).
- 3 row: Evenly circle around the increase of 6 single crochet (= 18).
- 4 row: Knit with single crochet, also distributing a uniform increase in the form of 6 columns (= 24).

Work continues in the same way to the desired circle diameter. So that the figure does not work out with corners, the increases in each row should not go perfectly in one place.



For a snowman, you can knit two or three circles of the same or different sizes, and then sew them into a single object, which is easily decorated with an embroidered face and additional details in the form of a scarf and hat.

Volumetric toy: how to tie a torso

Crochet a snowman can be made on separate elements or as a whole. In the second case, it is not necessary to stitch the balls, but it will be necessary to fill the form with synthetic winterizer during the knitting process. To make the body, use hook number 2. The work will look like this:

- 1 row: Crochet a snowman begins with the implementation of the Amigurumi ring. To do this, wrap the working thread around the index finger of the left hand and hook the working thread through the loop. Knit 8 single crochet stitches and pull into a ring. Before you is a neat start to work.
- 2-7 row: knit in a circle with single crochet columns, evenly distributing the increments, so that you get an even flat, not strained circle.
- 8-15 row: knit in single crochet without increasing.
- 16-22 row: evenly reduce the loops, performing columns with a common vertex, or in any other way begin to make decreases through an equal number of columns. You should get a narrowing of the shape, a kind of "waist" of a snowman.
- 23 row: now perform the increase (a pair of columns in one loop).
- 24th row: every two columns, make increments.
- 25-28 row: performed without increase and decrease in single crochet columns.
- 29-34 row: evenly distribute the decreases in a circle to form a transition to the head, that is, the neck.
- 35 row: how did the transition in the waist area, knit a pair of columns in one loop.
- 36 row: carry out increases through 2 columns.
- 37-47 row: knit in single crochet without increases or decreases. Do not forget to fill the product with existing material;
- 48-55 row: perform decreases until the ring closes. Cut and fasten the thread.

Interior toy

If you need a big snowman, crocheting it is also easy, it will only take much more time and yarn. Depending on the desired size, you can take thicker threads and a hook or, having understood the principle of working on a small product, slightly adjust the presented scheme, increasing the number of loops, rows of increments, decreases and parts of the object without them.





How to tie the legs

The voluminous snowman should be as realistic and detailed as possible, therefore, in addition to the torso, you also need to complete the legs. Each part is knitted separately (two pieces, respectively), and then sewn to the base. Determine the location of the legs yourself. If the snowman is standing on the table, then it is better to lift them to the front plane of the lower ball. If the product is supposed to be hung on a Christmas tree, then the legs will look great and sewn below.

The work is performed as follows:

- 1 row: follow the amigurumi ring, as for the previous part. Knit 8 single crochet into it and pull it into a tight, neat piece.
- 2-3 row: knit single crochet, evenly distributing increases.
- 4-5 row: also perform without crochet, but without increments.
- 6-7 row: after two single crochet columns, make one decrease.

Fill the part with a synthetic winterizer, and simply pull the remaining small hole and sew it with a needle. Tie the second leg in the same way. Sew both legs to the body.

So, as you see, there is nothing complicated.

How to make a face

Focusing on an object created from snow in the yard, decorating his head with details that form a certain image, facial expressions and emotions, make a face. Eyes are easy to embroider or sew on small beads. Mouth is easier to make in the first way. The nose is traditionally made from carrots. To make your voluminous snowman different from the natural one only in size, take orange yarn and make the appropriate part. Hook is better to use No. 1. Work like this:

- 1 row: make 14 air loops and and fix the chain in the form of a ring.
- 2-5 row: knit in a circle without increases and decreases in single crochet columns.
- 6-8 row: Perform even decreases until a circle is closed.

Fill the resulting cone with a synthetic winterizer. Sew the item to the head.





How to knit a hat

Properly selected additional attributes, in particular, clothing elements, will also help to create a cute image of a winter hero. They can be made the same or different in color. If you want your New Year's snowman to be crocheted in a hat, work like this:

Complete the amigurumi ring and knit 10 single crochet into it:

- 1 row: perform single crochet by uniformly adding 5 columns to make 15 in a row.
- 2 row: Knit 15 single crochet stitches on the back of the loop.
- 3-4 row: perform single crochet.
- 5th row: form the hat margins by knitting two single crochet in each loop of the previous row for the front wall of the loop. It turned out 30 columns. Cut the thread and fasten.

The hat is ready.

How to make a scarf

To make a scarf, follow these steps:

  1. Dial 60 air loops and 1 lift loop.
  2. In the second loop, crochet a single crochet.
  3. Knit single crochet to the end of the row.
  4. Cut the working thread and fasten.

And this accessory is made. You can dress up a hero.

Snowman crochet: diagram and description of the manufacture of tack

This product will be 22x30 cm in size. To produce it, you will need 50 g of white and 30 g of black yarn, as well as a few apricot threads and a hook number 2.5.





The work is performed as follows:

  1. For the body, dial 8 white loops from white yarn and close 1 ring with a half crochet in the ring.
  2. Knit in a circle with double crochet, replacing 1 double crochet of each circle with 3 air loops and ending each circle with 1 single crochet in the upper replaced air loop. In addition, in the 2nd - 6th circles, enter the hook only behind the back wall of the loops.

The Snowman tack will look like this:

- 1 circle - 12 double crochet.

- 2 circle - plus evenly 6 double crochet (= 18 single crochet).

- 3 circle - plus evenly 6 columns (= 24 columns).

- 4-6 circles - plus evenly in each circle 12 columns (= 60 columns).

- 7-15 circles - plus evenly in each circle, 7 double crochets (= 123 columns).

Now knit the front and back rows:

- 16th row: only 64 columns of the previous row knit with crochet.

- 17-20 rows: double crochet.

Knit the face and cap according to the scheme with the front and wrong rows in the middle 22 loops as follows:

- Pass the first 21 stitches with half crochets, then knit along the middle 22 stitches according to the pattern.

- After the 9th row, cut the working thread.



- At the arrow, start again with black yarn and knit according to the pattern.

- For buttons and eyes dial 6 air loops and 1 half crochet shortly in a ring.

- Perform 2 stitches instead of 1 single crochet.

- Then another 7 single crochet in a circle.

- Finish 1 single crochet in the first initial air loop.

- 2nd circle: from each loop, knit 2 single crochet (= 16 single crochet). A total of 5 such motives.

- Sew eyes and three buttons.

- To embroider a mouth with a chain stitch with black threads, and a nose with a smooth surface - apricot.

So, you learned how to crochet a snowman. The diagram and description are given for several embodiments of the object. Choose your favorite. Make interesting winter souvenirs with your own hands.
