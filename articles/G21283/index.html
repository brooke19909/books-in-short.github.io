Hotel Gran Garbi Mar 4 * (Lloret de Mar, Spain): photos and reviews of tourists

Resorts in Spain have long been popular among vacationers from around the world. In our article we want to talk about the hotel Gran Garbi Mar 4 *, located in Lloret de Mar.

A bit about the hotel

Number of rooms

Gran Garbi Mar 4 * has 77 rooms, among which there are apartments of the following categories:



  1. Double: single room and single room short stay, twin room for 2 people short stay.
  2. Triple apartments.
  3. Family apartment for two children and two adults.

All rooms at the hotel are equipped with a balcony, air conditioning, hairdryer, satellite channels, TV, minibar. Most of the apartments have beautiful views of the sea coast.

Dining at the hotel

Gran Garbi Mar 4 * there is a restaurant offering its visitors a buffet. The hotel operates on an All inclusive system, however, at the request of tourists, you can take half board. The hotel chef delights guests with the best international dishes. On the tables there is always a large number of ripe fruits and seafood.

Complex infrastructure

Hotel Gran Garbi Mar 4 * has a good infrastructure. On its territory there is a reception, a left-luggage office, parking, a currency exchange office, outdoor and indoor pools, a terrace with umbrellas and sunbeds, a relaxation room, a cinema. The main pool has an area of 120 square meters, it is filled with fresh water.

The complex has a very convenient location, nearby are almost all local attractions. Near the hotel there are many supermarkets, cafes, shops, there is even a whole shopping street. A five-minute walk from the hotel there is a bus station from which buses leave in all directions. So if there is a desire to travel, then there will be no problems with transport.



Tourists can arrange a transfer in advance at the hotel, but there is one caveat. The fact is that transport does not deliver to the reception, as it should be. Tourists need to walk to the building on foot, the journey will take about three minutes, but with suitcases it is not very convenient.

The hotel does not accept pets. Estimated time and time of settlement - 12.00.

The complex has a restaurant, bar, internet cafe, car rental. The hotel building is surrounded by a beautiful garden, in your free time it is very pleasant to wander along its alleys and sit on a bench in the shade of its trees.

Children's holiday

For children in the Hotel Gran Garbi Mar 4 * in high season there is a mini-club equipped with a playground. Children under two years can stay free of charge when using existing bedding. The provision of a crib is a free service.

Sports and Entertainment

In Gran Garbi Mar 4 * (Spain) there is an evening and afternoon animation. Entertainment programs are conducted in several languages: French, Spanish, English, Italian and German. Guests can enjoy archery, darts and table tennis. The complex has a representative of a travel agency that offers vacationers to visit the most interesting sights.

Beach

Hotel Gran Garbi Mar 4 * was built in close proximity to the coast. It does not have a private beach. Guests at the property have access to the municipal coast. This nuance cannot be attributed to the shortcomings of the hotel, since in Europe almost all the beaches are urban. A bit about the resort

Hotel Gran Garbi Mar 4 * (Lloret de Mar) is located, as we already mentioned, in the town of Lloret de Mar, which is the center of the tourist Costa Brava and the main place for parties of the Mediterranean coast. Crowds of young people from different countries come here in search of entertainment. The city has an incredible number of discos with laser shows and dance programs, restaurants, night clubs, bars. Lloret de Mar has a long history. From a small fishing village, the city has turned into one of the largest tourist centers in Europe and the most beautiful city on the coast of Spain.



In Lloret de Mar, as on the entire Costa Brava, the climate is milder and less hot than in other Spanish resorts. In the summer, the temperature rises to +28 degrees, and in winter it does not fall below +10. The beach-sea season lasts from May to the end of October.

Beaches of Lloret de Mar

Within Lloret de Mar, there is the most famous and popular beach of the coast - Lloret. Next to it are other equally beautifully equipped sections of the coast. The resort also has a water park called Water World, which is incredibly popular among tourists. Local beaches are beautiful in that they are incredibly wide and well protected from northern winds by rocky cliffs and bays. Because of this, they are considered the best on the entire Costa Brava. Beautiful scenic spots, numerous bays and a clear sea attract connoisseurs of similar beauty from around the world.

Local entertainment

Relaxing in Hotel Gran Garbi Mar 4 * (Spain), you should definitely see all the most interesting places in Lloret de Mar, visit the sea attractions and attend all the events. Since the city has all the conditions for outdoor activities on land and water, this is certainly worth using. On the beach you can ride a "banana", rent a scooter or catamaran, go for a walk on a boat along the coast. The city has many diving centers, the services of which can be used. In addition, you can ride horses, play golf or bowling. Tourists love to visit Water Park. A bus leaves from the central square of the city, which brings everyone to it for free. The city has another Marineland water park and a small Aqualeon zoo. Lloret de Mar is a beautiful place. On its streets it‚Äôs just nice to walk and look at shops and shops.



After sunset, the resort does not freeze, but, on the contrary, transforms, plunged into multi-colored lights. At this time, night clubs and discos begin to work, at which active party-goers from all over the world gather.

The city has numerous equipment rental points for sports, fishing and snorkeling. Every year, festivals and holidays are held right in the open air. Many of them are devoted to the history of the city.

Attractions Lloret de Mar

Lloret de Mar has a long history, as evidenced by relics that have survived to this day. They can be seen in the center of Verdaguer. Some of them date back to 250 BC. In addition, the city has a maritime museum. The cost of visiting is only 3-4 EUR.

In Lloret de Mar, on the seaside boulevard, there is a copy of the unique Canale Fountain (the original is in Barcelona). The legend says that the fountain is magic. If you drink water from it, you will certainly stay in Barcelona or return to it.



At the end of Lloret Beach you can see a statue (bronze) of Donna Mariner. She is a symbol of this resort. The image symbolizes a woman looking at the sea, who meets or escorts her husband. The sculpture was made back in 1966 by the famous artist Ernest Maragalla. Another symbol of the city is the castle on the beach. Once it was the residence of the industrialist of their Girona. Isodor Bosch (architect) began construction of the building in 1935, but the civil war prevented the completion of the project. The castle was completed only in the late forties.

Hotel Gran Garbi Mar 4 *: reviews

Speaking about the hotel, I want to refer to the reviews of tourists who visited it. The complex is located in the center of the town, but at the same time away from the hustle and bustle.
