Izola Paradise 4 * (Bulgaria, Sunny Beach): hotel description, services, reviews

Planning an unforgettable vacation in Bulgaria? Considering the resort of Sunny Beach? Do you want to stay in a comfortable, but not expensive hotel with excellent infrastructure? In this case, pay attention to the "Isola Paradise" (Izola Paradise) 4 *.



Review, photo

The hotel we are considering is located in the very center of the Bulgarian resort of Sunny Beach. This place is very popular among young people and people who love partying. After all, the resort has a lot of entertainment facilities - clubs, bars, discos, etc.

The nearest airport in Burgas is 30 kilometers from Isola Paradise. The city of Nessebar is 8 kilometers away. The distance to the beach is 600 meters.

Izola Paradise 4 *: traveler reviews

Every person who at least once had a vacation abroad knows how important it is to plan a trip in advance, taking into account the maximum possible number of details. After all, even if you like adventure, it still makes sense to prepare in advance for a vacation. In addition, the impression of a holiday is largely influenced by how comfortable the selected hotel will be for you. Of course, if you plan to travel around the country, every day staying in a new place and a new hotel, then this item will not be so relevant. But if you plan to mainly spend time on the beach and stay in a hotel working on an all-inclusive system, then it is very important not to make a mistake with the choice. Indeed, in this case, dissatisfaction with the hotel can negatively affect the overall impression of the vacation. Therefore, experienced travelers recommend taking care of the hotel selection in advance. So, you will have time to compare options and choose from them the most suitable for you both in price and in other parameters. Also not to be mistaken will help to study the reviews left by people who have already visited the place where you are going to spend your vacation. As a rule, former guests share objective information not only about the advantages of a particular hotel, but also about the shortcomings that they had to face during their stay in it. Therefore, we suggest that you familiarize yourself with the opinion that has developed among our compatriots during their stay in Izola Paradise 4 * (Sunny Beach, Bulgaria).



Rating

Immediately, it can be noted that for the most part the guests were quite satisfied that they opted for Isola Paradise. Confirmation of these words can serve as the rating of the hotel, which is calculated based on the ratings of its guests. So, for Izola Paradise 4 * (Sunny Beach) it is 4.3 out of the maximum possible five points. The highest ratings were given to hotel infrastructure, room equipment, service, infrastructure and convenient location. Not without some complaints. However, this situation is typical for all hotels of different categories. Well, now we offer to understand everything in more detail.



Location

In general, our compatriots were satisfied with the location of the hotel. So, according to them, in the vicinity of Isola Paradise there are many entertainment places, as well as cafes, bars and restaurants. There is no shortage of shops, supermarkets, markets, kiosks, tour agencies and car rental companies. So there will be something to do here. The only thing, some tourists were not particularly pleased with the fact that it takes about 20 minutes to go to the beach from the hotel. However, most vacationers did not confuse this fact. According to them, the path goes along a busy street with a bunch of shops, souvenir shops, etc., so that, looking around, you don‚Äôt even notice how you are already at the sea.

Territory

Izola Paradise 4 * (Sunny Beach), according to many guests, is very well designed. So, despite the fact that he does not have a large territory of his own, being here is very comfortable. This is largely achieved due to the fact that residential buildings are located around the perimeter. Thus, the territory of the hotel is closed from the carriageway. Travelers who have been here note that not all hotels in the resort of Sunny Beach can boast of this. So, according to them, there are a lot of hotels, the pools on the territory of which are separated from the roadway with just a flimsy fence. Of course, this does not provide vacationers with comfort.



Housing stock

Judging by the comments of the guests, they were mostly satisfied with the equipment of the Isola Paradise rooms. So, according to our compatriots, the apartments here are not very large in area, but they can always be comfortably accommodated. It is also noticeable that the rooms are regularly redecorated. Therefore, being in them is very nice. The guests were also pleased with the spacious balconies on which you can sit in the evening, watching what is happening in the vicinity or on the hotel territory, with a glass of wine or a cup of tea. As for the furniture in the rooms, it can not be called new. However, it is of high quality and is in excellent condition. The same applies to technology. Judging by the comments of the guests, most of them did not have any problems or breakdowns in the rooms. However, even if something fails, immediately report it to the reception. The malfunction will be fixed as soon as possible.

the Internet

The question of the availability of Wi-Fi in a hotel today, as a rule, worries many tourists. After all, we are all used to having the opportunity at any time to get in touch with our family, friends, work colleagues. As for Izola Paradise 4 * (Sunny Beach), then wireless Internet is provided to guests here. Its quality, according to vacationers, is very good. However, you can use it only for an additional fee. It is about 5 euros per day. Many travelers found this price too high.



Hotel Policies

Check-in at the hotel should be done after two in the afternoon. However, as tourists who have been here note, in practice this rule is rarely applied. So, even if you arrive at the hotel in the morning, if there are available rooms for the category you have reserved, the keys to your apartments will be issued to you almost immediately. And there‚Äôs no need to pay anything extra for this. In addition, guests were pleased with the fact that even in case of early arrival at the hotel they could go for breakfast, although according to the rules of the hotel, this is not provided. However, with regard to the day of departure from Izola Paradise 4 * (Bulgaria), here on the part of holidaymakers you can meet a number of complaints. So, to vacate the occupied room should be up to 11 hours of the day. However, experienced tourists warn that if your flight is in the afternoon or evening and you want to extend your stay at the hotel, then you are unlikely to succeed. The fact is that at the height of the holiday season, the hotel is usually 100 percent full. Therefore, administrators, even with all their desire, will not be able to find a free number for you. Keep this in mind. However, for the vast majority of these circumstances were not particularly difficult. So, they handed over the keys to the room, left their things in the left-luggage office at the reception and spent time until they left by the pool or at sea.

Nutrition

Judging by the comments of the guests, they were mostly satisfied with this item. So, the food at Izola Paradise is organized according to the ol inclusive system. Of course, you should not count on the same variety of dishes and drinks, as, for example, in Turkey, beloved by our compatriots, but in general everything is organized quite well. Travelers note that they were satisfied with a large number of meat and fish dishes. They also liked local vegetables and fruits, pastries. As for the weaknesses, their choice here is not particularly great. However, this did not become a particular problem. Indeed, in the vicinity of the hotel there are lots of shops, supermarkets, cooking, where you can always buy something tasty.

In general, tourists note that, choosing the Izola Paradise 4 * hotel with an all-inclusive food system, they were not mistaken at all. After all, according to them, prices at resorts in Bulgaria are not at all low. And dinners with dinners outside the hotel cost travelers a tidy sum.



Service

According to many of our compatriots vacationing at Isola Paradise, they were very pleased with the hotel staff. So, judging by their reviews, the staff here are very welcoming, attentive and coping with their duties. This applies to both reception staff and waiters, maids and other employees. In addition, many hotel employees understand the Russian language, so you will not have any special difficulties in communicating here.

Sea

Since most tourists come to the Bulgarian resort of Sunny Beach for a beach holiday, this question excites them in the first place. Our compatriots staying at Isola Paradise claim that they really liked the sea here. The only drawback was the fact that you need to walk about 20 minutes in measured steps to the beach from the hotel. However, some travelers were even happy about this, since such a walk allowed them to stretch after long hours on the beach or by the pool and plentiful food. The sea here, as our compatriots note, is very clean and warm. There are no special waves, so it will be comfortable for adults and children to swim. The beaches here are large, sandy. Sun loungers and parasols are available at an additional cost. Many tourists were unhappy with this fact, especially since the price of this servant is considerable. So, daily use of a chaise lounge with an umbrella in high season will cost you about 10 euros! A significant part of vacationers prefers not to spend money on renting sunbeds, but to settle down on special free sections of the beach, laying a towel or mat directly on the sand.

Infrastructure, entertainment

According to tourists, Izola Paradise 4 * (Bulgaria, Sunny Beach) can not boast of a special animation program. So, for adult vacationers, it consists only in evening discos and periodically held Bulgarian evenings with national dishes, songs, dances, etc. During the same day, tourists can spend time by the pool, visit the spa, beauty salon, gym, go to the beach and go on excursions. As for the youngest travelers, for them the hotel administration tried to diversify leisure. So, although the children's playground at the hotel is small, there are animators working all day, offering kids to draw, make funny crafts, play, etc. So you can easily leave your child here for several hours.
