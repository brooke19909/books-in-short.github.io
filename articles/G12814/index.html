Origami fox: assembly workshop

Origami came to us from Japan. This is a way to fold paper nicely to make different objects. Engage your children and friends in this fun activity. We offer you to learn how to make an origami fox.

Muzzle assembly diagram





Little children will be very interested to learn how origami fox is made. So that the lesson does not turn into flour, start with simple schemes.

Instructions on how to make a fox face:

  1. Take a two-color square sheet of paper: it should be white on one side and orange on the other. If not, connect the two sheets together.
  2. Fold the sheet diagonally with the white side inward (Figure 1).
  3. Fold the top together (Figure 2).
  4. Bend both sides of the figure and turn it over (illustrations 3 and 4).
  5. Draw the elements of the face with a marker or pen.

The muzzle is ready!

The second version of the origami fox





If you take a square leaf, you can get another model of the chanterelle :

  1. Fold the sheet diagonally (Figure 1).
  2. Expand it and fold it again diagonally, only to the other side (Figure 2).
  3. Wrap the left and right sides of the resulting triangle (Figure 3).
  4. Fold the resulting shape in half (Figure 4).
  5. Wrap the right side of the upper triangle, as in picture 5.
