Abkhazia, Gagra, "Colchis." Pension "Colchis": photos and reviews of tourists

Resorts along the Black Sea coast have always been popular. These include many Abkhaz cities, which, being relatively young, these days began to gain popularity among Russians as the best tourist corners of the region. And among them is the city of Gagra, which is one of the most beautiful places in Abkhazia.



Great place to stay

This famous health resort is located on a hill. There are many relict trees around which the Seaside Park is so famous. From the rooms of the numerous guest houses of the city of Gagra there are magnificent views of both the mountains and the expanses of the Black Sea. Gagra resort is considered a true paradise for lovers of beach holidays, combined with educational tourism.

A huge number of historical monuments dating from different eras have been preserved here. Among them are the Abaata fortress, an urban hydropathic institution, which was founded at the beginning of the last century, a magnificent castle belonging to the Prince of Oldenburg and many others. Not far from the city limits is located Gegsky Falls. It was here that some scenes of the movie about Sherlock Holmes and Dr. Watson were shot. Tourists will be interested in visiting the Bzyb fortress, visiting numerous lakes and protected places, which are surrounded by a lot of Gagra.

Beach

Pension "Colchis" (Abkhazia, Gagra) is surrounded by a picturesque park, which cascades down to the very shore of the sea. On its well-groomed territory there are many floral arrangements and elements of landscape design. You can go to the sand and pebble beach by elevator, and then walk along the underpass. The bathing area is located just two hundred and fifty meters from the main building. It is fully landscaped: chaise lounges and sun loungers are placed everywhere, there are many umbrellas that protect against the summer heat.

The beach strip is covered with fine pebbles and sand, so the presence of special shoes for swimming, especially for children, is welcome. The entrance to the sea is gentle. The swimming season in Gagra begins in mid-May and lasts until the end of October. It has a warm subtropical climate with lots of sunny days. There is a summer cafe on the beach, there is a rental of sports equipment and an outdoor dance floor.
