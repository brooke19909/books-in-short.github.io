Restaurant "Tower" in Tula: interior, menu, own brewery

The green city of Tula attracts a large number of tourists from all over the world. If you want to have a good rest and try hopping beer and delicious dishes, you should visit the Bashnya Brasserie in Tula, which is located in Sovetsky, on Krasnoarmeysky Prospect, 14.





History of the city

Initially, Tula was the southern fortress of Moscow and protected it from conquerors for many decades. One of the sights of the city at the beginning of the 16th century was the Tula Kremlin, around which the modern Tula began to develop. Gradually, the city lost its defensive function and became a center of trade and crafts. New squares, streets and districts were created rapidly, and a beautiful promenade was formed on the banks of the Upa River .

Modern Tula is a developed industrial center of the country, which is famous for its historical sights and architecture. The city has nine unique towers that have their own history. Despite the unique historical heritage, in Tula there are many cozy gastronomic establishments that cordially open their doors to all guests. One of these is the Tower Brewery Restaurant.





Interior

The Tower Brewery restaurant in Tula was designed in the classic English style, namely: oak tables, leather green sofas, straight lines of shades and dark wooden panels on the walls. Subdued light makes the restaurant's atmosphere soft and even a bit homely. Wooden shelves with original beer mugs remind that the restaurant is focused on beer cuisine.

The brewery is located on the ground floor of the building. The brewery restaurant has its own amazing architectural entrance in the form of a tower, creating a special atmosphere. The institution has its own summer terrace, which opens with the onset of warm weather. The terrace is furnished with red soft sofas and wooden tables.





Brewery

The Tower restaurant in Tula has its own modernly equipped brewery. It is here that delicious and high-quality beer is brewed according to all the rules and traditions of brewing. In the institution you can find such types of beer as: white (unfiltered wheat), dark, light and red, four types of hop beer. Thanks to this diversity, even the most demanding guest will be able to find the desired drink. There is an opportunity to try a beer set, which consists of 5 different beer drinks.

A recent novelty of the institution was the Indian bright ale in the American style. A sweet and tasty drink combines bitter and sweet tastes.

Kitchen

The Tower restaurant in Tula has opened relatively recently and has already established itself as one of the best beer establishments. Guests can enjoy European dishes at the establishment. Large and satisfying servings will be a good complement to beer. The restaurant menu includes dishes based on recipes of old Russian cuisine.

In the menu of the Tower restaurant in Tula you can find:

  • snacks (tiger prawns in beer batter and with spicy sauce, beef tails, pickled pork ears) ;
  • soups (chicken, onion, creamy fish, hodgepodge with different smoked meats, cheese soup, tomato on mutton ribs, meat Eintopf, porcini mushroom broth);
  • hot and cold salads;
  • main dishes (duck liver and zucchini pancakes, pot roast, turkey heart, meat steak, grilled shank).

As drinks, you can try the tincture of horseradish and lemon, fruit drinks, wines, coffee drinks, juices. The Tower Restaurant in Tula, whose menu is tailored to guests with different tastes, is famous for its attentive service.





Events and service

The Tower Restaurant in Tula is great for holding a birthday, business lunch, or even a romantic date. Celebratory banquets and celebrations are held here at the highest level. In the restaurant, visitors are greeted by polite waiters who can tell you the ingredients of the dishes, clarify all the nuances of interest and just help you feel comfortable. Guests of the establishment can enjoy live music with a glass of hop beer.

The gastronomic institution offers the Home Delivery service, so you can try restaurant dishes in a homely setting. Reservations are made by telephone. Prices in the institution are moderate and affordable, payment is made in cash and by bank transfer. For visitors to the restaurant, who arrived in their own car, they offer secure parking spaces.

The Tower Restaurant in Tula welcomes guests from 12:00 to 01:00 (Friday and Saturday from 12:00 to 03:00). The institution is quite easy to find and get to it by any means of transport.
