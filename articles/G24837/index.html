Pension "Edem": infrastructure, location, prices, reviews

Pension "Edem" (Sochi, Chernomorskaya St., 14) is located in the center of Sochi within walking distance from the sea (40 m). Here you can not only have a wonderful vacation, but also have medical treatment (the base of the Resort Polyclinic No. 1). The guest complex has a rich infrastructure to meet any needs of tourists. Do not confuse the boarding house "Eden" (Crimea, Sudak, Aivazovsky St., 17, building 1) with the hotel complex under consideration.



Number of rooms

To check into a hotel you will need a passport, a birth certificate (for traveling with children) and a ticket. The rules in the complex are standard, but pets are not allowed.

Pension "Edem" is located in a modern three-story building with 135 comfortable rooms that can accommodate up to 300 people.

Treatment in a boarding house

Pension "Eden" provides the treatment and prevention of the following diseases:

  • pathology of the organs of the cardiovascular system;
  • diseases of the central nervous system;
  • pathology of the musculoskeletal system;
  • diseases of the musculoskeletal system;
  • skin diseases.


Treatment is carried out on the basis of a spa card, which must first be issued in the clinic at the place of residence. The standard treatment time is 21 days. It may include:

  • examination and diagnosis of a doctor;
  • various medical procedures (herbal, iodine-bromine, ion-exchange baths, etc.);
  • massage;
  • physiotherapy (ultrasound, electrophoresis, etc.);
  • various analyzes (for an additional fee).

Pension "Eden": prices

Prices for accommodation and treatment vary depending on the time of year. From July to September, the price for rooms is the highest. For example, a double suite in 2016 cost 7,900 rubles, and in November-April it surrendered for 5,800 rubles. The standard is estimated from 1500 to 2900 rubles.

The amount paid includes the following:

  • 1 person per day;
  • three meals a day (complex);
  • pool;
  • beach;
  • sports and playgrounds.


Discounts and promotions in the Eden complex (Sochi):

  • from 2 to 7 years - 70%;
  • from 7 to 14 years - 40%;
  • from 14 to 18 years - 30%.

The action "Mother and Child" provides for resettlement at a discount, depending on the age of the child.

Pension "Eden": reviews

Guests of the boarding house appreciate first of all its location. Indeed, even in the hotel complex the sound of the sea is heard, and you can walk to it in 5 minutes. The only thing that does not suit some tourists is an uncomfortable descent into the water. Those who do not know how to swim, have to swim on the children's beach.

Dining at the hotel is praised. It is solid, but without frills.



From the pension you can easily get to many interesting places on the Black Sea coast. To do this, use the tour desk on site.

Pension "Eden" is a great place for relaxation and treatment. Convenient location, affordable prices and excellent service will allow tourists to spend their holidays with dignity and interest.
