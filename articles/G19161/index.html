Hotel Atlantica Thalassaki 4 * (Ayia Napa): reviews

Cyprus has attracted the attention of tourists for decades. And this country really has something to offer, because the tourist infrastructure here is developing and improving every year. Here, travelers are offered white beaches and excellent hotels, and, of course, a lot of attractions and interesting stories.

When it comes to Cyprus, many tourists consider it an ideal option for a Ayia Napu stop. This is a large resort town located on the seafront, which is able to provide travelers with all possible entertainment. Of course, there are innumerable hotels here. And one of the really good is the Atlantica Thalassaki complex. Of course, first you need to find out everything about this hotel in order to understand whether it is worth booking a room.

Hotel Location





Many tourists evaluate a hotel based primarily on its location. Indeed, for some, silence and peace are important, but someone wants to plunge headlong into the city noise and vanity. The hotel complex is located very close to the sea ​​coast, in a rather prestigious area of ​​Ayia Napa resort. By the way, the distance to the center is only 600 m - in a few minutes guests can reach both the beach and the lively central part with all its entertainment. A bus stop, cafes, shops and souvenir shops are also within walking distance.

The nearest airport is in Larnaca (by the way, that’s where most of the tourists land). The distance is only 45 km. On a convenient transfer you will reach the hotel in less than half an hour.

Atlantica Thalassaki Hotel (Ayia Napa): infrastructure features





In the reviews, tourists say that the hotel complex is very beautiful - it is really pleasant to spend time here. The hotel consists of several two-story bungalows, so tourists can enjoy peace and quiet. Not so long ago, restoration was carried out here - the buildings are blinded with cleanliness and freshness.

The territory is very large and green. There are convenient walking paths, terraces for recreation and sports, swimming pools, places for leisure and entertainment. The whole courtyard resembles an exotic garden - you can enjoy the bright colors of nature. By the way, they clean the territory every day.

What numbers to count on? Photos and additional information





Of course, every tourist is interested in the exact conditions under which he will have to spend his vacation in the territory of Atlanyica Thalassaki 4 * hotel. The hotel complex consists of 121 rooms. Mostly tourists are offered standard rooms for 1-3 people, although there are more spacious rooms with a private living room and a sofa bed. By the way, for the child, at the request of the parents, the employees bring an extra bed.

The rooms are simply but tastefully furnished. At your disposal will be a private balcony or porch with furniture. Do not worry about the availability of household appliances - here it is. Guests can use the air conditioning, spend evenings watching TV (there are several Russian-language channels). There is a telephone, and for an additional fee you can use the safe. Many tourists are pleased with the presence of a kitchenette with a dining table - there is a stove, microwave, electric kettle, small refrigerator and, of course, the necessary utensils.

The bathroom also has everything you need: shower, toilet, washbasin, clean towels, hair dryer, soap and other hygiene items. Please note that the rooms are not cleaned every day, but four times a week. However, guests do not complain about the mess - the rooms are always clean and tidy.

Do guests provide food?





As is customary in the country, the Atlantica Thalassaki 4 * hotel complex operates according to the all-inclusive scheme already familiar to our compatriots. The main meals are served as a buffet in the spacious Aphrodite Restaurant. By the way, breakfast, lunch and dinner is allotted for 2 hours. As for the menu, in the reviews tourists speak favorably about local cuisine - the dishes are given in sufficient quantities, the food is always tasty and freshly prepared.

Two local bars offer drinks or chilled cocktails. It should be said right away that only products from Tunisian manufacturers are given out to tourists for free. The hotel also has the Okeanos Restaurant, which serves gourmet international cuisine, as well as fish and seafood. Lunch at this establishment must be paid separately.

Relax on the beach: what to do? Convenience and entertainment





Is it possible to count on a water holiday while staying at this hotel? After all, picturesque beaches are just the most interesting sights of the city of Ayia Napa. Atlantica Thalassaki is located just 200 meters from the sea - you can get there in a few minutes.

In the reviews, tourists say that the beach here is very beautiful and clean. The entrance to the sea is completely cleared, there is even a fairly large area of ​​shallows for bathing children. For a small fee, travelers can get a comfortable mattress and sunbed, as well as an umbrella. Near the beach there are several cafes, as well as a water sports center where you can rent a boat, book a jet ski or a banana ride, take a diving course, and rent a surfboard. Leisure of tourists on the beach is saturated with bright emotions.

Hotel Atlantica Thalassaki (Cyprus): Additional amenities for guests

Of course, hotel guests can also hope for a number of additional amenities, without which living in another country might not be so pleasant. To begin with, it is worth saying that one of the employees is always on duty at the reception (even at night). Here you will exchange currency for local bills. There is also a spacious parking lot, next to which you can easily rent a bicycle, moped or car (the prices here are the same as in the city).

Internet access is available at Atlantica Thalassaki, but this service is charged separately. By the way, a small quiet computer corner is equipped for guests.

How do tourists spend time? Leisure at the hotel





Of course, travelers arrive in Tunisia, wanting to relax on the beach. But you can diversify your leisure by staying at the Atlantic Thalassaki Hotel . For example, many guests spend time at one of the pools (there are two of them). The water here is clean and cool - you can refresh yourself, play water polo or take part in a group lesson in water aerobics.

There is a jacuzzi and a sauna where you can completely relax. There is also a gym with state-of-the-art equipment. At leisure, tourists like to play billiards or ping pong. And in the courtyard there are tennis courts (here, by the way, you can rent racquets). In the evenings, various entertainment events are often organized for guests, so tourists will enjoy a fun life at the hotel.

Opinion and reviews: what do guests think?

As you know, a complete picture of the upcoming vacation can be made only by talking with former guests of the Atlantica Thalassaki. The reviews that tourists leave are positive. The hotel is really perfect for a beach holiday. Moreover, there is enough entertainment on the territory - many guests spend most of their time in the courtyard.

The hotel is very clean, this applies not only to rooms, but also to all interior spaces. The equipment in the rooms works, the atmosphere is pleasant, even cozy. It is also pleasing that the cooks sincerely worry about the impressions of the guests and feed everyone very tasty. Former guests definitely recommend the Atlantica Thalassaki 4 * hotel.
