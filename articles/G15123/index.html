Vesyoloye village (Adler) - the main attractions of the picturesque corner of Sochi

The village of Vesyoloye (Adler) is a rather small, compact, but incredibly picturesque corner of Sochi. This is the most extreme point of the south of Russia, further only Abkhazia.





Main attraction

Perhaps the most visited place here is the beach of Imereti Bay. This is not surprising, since most tourists come to Sochi from May to September, when the holiday season is in full swing. A vacation in the South involves, of course, beach and sea pleasures. So, the village of Vesyoloye (Adler) is a great place to relax, if you want to swim enough and sunbathe on the spacious beaches.

This pebble beach can be considered a real visiting card of the village. And, by the way, in addition to swimming, there are many other interesting activities. For example, fishing enthusiasts can rent a boat and settle on the shore in anticipation of a catch. Others will like entertainment such as water skiing and hydrobikes. Adherents of a more relaxing holiday will like sailing on a yacht or scooter, which are regularly held in these places.

Variety of rest

In addition to the above, being in a place such as the village of Vesyoloye (Adler), you can go on some excursion. For example, visit Krasnaya Polyana and admire the majestic Caucasus Mountains or go to the Republic of Abkhazia. You can also visit the monkey nursery - it is located there, on the territory of the village itself. But this is an entertainment for an amateur.

It is worth noting that the village of Vesyoloye (Adler) is a place without any clubs, discos and entertainment venues. More precisely, they are, but they are few. In the summer, there are only a few small bars and cafes. For this reason, this place has become a favorite for adherents of a quiet and relaxing holiday.





Where to stay?

Many people think that it is better to rent a separate apartment or stay in some hotel before arriving somewhere? Someone loves complete freedom, someone likes to have the appropriate service. But the attention I would like to note such a place as the recreation center "Adler" (Vesyoloye settlement). This is a large luxury cottage complex located on its own, well-equipped pebble beach. By the way, he is one of the best not only in Sochi - but on the entire coast. Of course, it is not very large (about 50 meters), but it is very pleasant to relax on it - there are sun loungers, umbrellas, sun loungers, as well as a rental point for beach equipment.

There is also a lot of everything else necessary for a good pastime and comfortable living. Well-furnished rooms, new furniture, sports fields, a gym and a sauna, a dance floor and much more. In general, everyone will find something to their taste.





What is interesting to know

Many tourists go to Adler (Vesyoloye village, private sector). And there are a lot of reasons for this. Why not choose this resort as a place for your vacation? There is excellent service and maintenance, there is a sea full of picturesque places and attractions, entertainment and shopping centers, nightlife, and besides, the city of Sochi itself is incredibly beautiful. So it is not surprising that tens of thousands of tourists come here every year, even if they have the opportunity to go to Egypt, Spain or Turkey.

The climate in Vesyol is the same as in the whole Adler - from May to October, summer reigns here, and the average temperature does not drop below 25 degrees. Sea water also warms up to good indicators - up to 21-23 degrees. By the way, the surroundings are rich in healing mud, as well as mineral waters. So, being in these places on vacation, you can not only have fun, but also improve your health. Therefore, if there is still doubt about whether to go to Adler or not, you should get rid of them and safely go to this resort in the summer in order to enjoy all the joys of life.
