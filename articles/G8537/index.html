Villa Mirenza 3 * (Montenegro / Budvanska Riviera): photos, prices and reviews of tourists

The Budva Riviera - one of the resort regions in Montenegro - stretches along the coast for as many as thirty-five kilometers, more than half of which are picturesque coarse sand beaches. Over the past few years, more Russians have expressed a desire to spend their vacations here, coming as families on beach vacations and on excursions in order to get to know this amazing country more closely.



Holidays in Budva

Due to its diversity, the resort is suitable for all travelers, without exception. Lovers of noisy relaxation are quite suitable hotels in Budva - a kind of Mecca for tourists in Montenegro. Life in this town does not stop even at night, and numerous water and land entertainments, excursion programs, cafes, restaurants and shops, discos and a water park will allow you to spend your vacation fun and without worries.

On this part of the coast, Budva is considered the main tourist center. Of its advantages, it is worth adding an excellent tourist infrastructure and very good beaches, for example, Mogren, on which there are fine pebbles and sand. The city has many expensive hotels offering their own entertainment program and a wide range of services. There are also a sufficient number of small economy houses, for example, such as Villa Mirenza 3.

general information

The hotel, located in the resort town of Budva, is built on the second line from the sea, six hundred meters from the beach. Around it there are shops and a restaurant. The nearest airport is twenty kilometers away. To the bus station, where intercity buses depart - only four hundred meters. The historical part of Budva is only half a kilometer away, so many people prefer to study the monuments of the region on their own.



Two hundred meters from the hotel there is an excellent sports center, there are also tennis courts. Mirenza 3 has its own parking area. Advance booking is not required.

Infrastructure

The hotel offers a full range of services that corresponds to its level. 24-hour front desk with fax and photocopying, ATM in the lobby, lobby, souvenir shop. The territory of Mirenza 3 is framed as a garden. Pets are not allowed according to the rules, there are rooms for smokers.

Judging by the reviews, the check-in is almost instantaneous: five minutes - and the keys to the apartments are handed to tourists. You can rent a car from the registrar, which, in fact, many Russians do, preferring to explore local attractions on their own. Here you can arrange a return transfer, book tours or use the services of a Russian-speaking guide.

Housing stock

Mirenza 3 (Montenegro) is small: it has a total of thirty rooms. Twenty four are standard and six are suites. The housing stock is equipped in one large three-story villa.



In addition to a refrigerator and a TV, the rooms feature a minibar, individually adjustable split air conditioning, a work desk and a large makeup mirror.

The apartment has two bedrooms and a living room. In addition, they have a kitchen area with a fridge, a sink, a stovetop and a full set of dishes and pans.

In the combined bathrooms showers, there is a hairdryer. According to the rules of the Mirenza 3 hotel (Budva), maids clean the rooms daily. Bed sheets with towels are changed every other day, hygiene items are updated with the same frequency.

All rooms have small balconies. They are equipped with plastic sets of tables with chairs, as well as a clothes dryer. In the daytime, balconies can be shaded with sun awnings.

Nutrition

Hotel Mirenza 3 offers half board meals. A buffet restaurant is located on the ground floor of the villa. Since the hotel only offers breakfast and dinner, guests can dine in one of two restaurants located near the old town, for example, in Porto. Residents of Mirenza 3 have a discount here. Judging by the reviews, the restaurants located near the hotel offer excellent Mediterranean cuisine, freshly caught fish on charcoal is especially tasty. By paying fifteen to seventeen euros, you can fully eat together, albeit without alcohol.

rules

The price for accommodation at this hotel does not include tourist tax. Check-in time, which is provided for in Mirenza 3, is eleven in the morning, however, tourists who arrive by transfer during unplanned hours are instantly served. After filling out all the papers, which can last a maximum of twenty minutes, since a Russian-speaking girl works at the registration desk, the keys to the apartments are handed to tourists.



Departure is scheduled for twelve in the morning. After checking in, you can leave your luggage in the lobby, and wait for the set transfer time yourself in one of the nearby cafes or on the beach.

Additional services and entertainment

An iron with ironing equipment, including a board, is available on request. If desired, residents can get breakfast in the room. Free dry cleaning and laundry services are available.

In addition, the hotel has an excellent spa, sauna and massage room. There are also outdoor outdoor and small indoor pools.

Beach

For those who come to Budva, Villa Mirenza 3 is an excellent choice. Although it does not have its own beach, vacationers can use the nearest urban bathing area, located six hundred meters from the villa. Coverage on the beach is semi-pebble - semi-sand, so children will definitely need special rubber bathing slippers. You need to pay for umbrellas, sunbeds and trestle beds.

Reviews and prices



The vast majority of Russians are delighted with their holidays in Montenegro. Those tourists who stayed at Villa Mirenza 3 also had a lot of positive impressions. Reviews from many indicate that they are completely satisfied with the complete lack of animation, as well as the fact that you can relax in the hotel, going for an active pastime in center of Budva, where there are many night clubs.

Residents are satisfied with everything: the delicious menu in the restaurant, unobtrusive service, and the excellent location of the villa. Around the villa there is a small garden, it is also full of restaurants and shops.



The only drawback the Russians consider the lack of an elevator, which is especially felt at the entrance and exit. Nevertheless, many believe that in Mirenza 3 in Montenegro you can relax perfectly for a small cost.

For example, in May, for seven nights in a double standard room with breakfast, alcohol-free and leaving Moscow for two, you will need to pay a little more than seventy thousand rubles. At the same time, prices vary depending on the season and the availability of a flight operating charter flights.
