What determines the interest rate on loans

In banking, there is a single principle for the formation of interest rates. First, there is a summation of the refinancing rate from the state and the profit of a particular banking organization. This parameter is determined by the Bank of Russia. Next, the permissible risks are calculated, which are added to the already existing amount.

Quite often, the profit made by the bank is estimated at 5%, and the refinancing amount is about 8%. Based on this, we get the usual 13%, which the bank's client pays during the year as a loan fee.

Nevertheless, it is worth considering that this is considered an ideal calculation, under which the borrower makes payments without delay. Then the interest rate will not fluctuate from year to year. In fact, it turns out that the bank's customers arrange loans, but are in no hurry with payments. Others regularly pay monthly payments. But, despite this, the bank is forced to take into account the share of non-payment risk and to remove an additional percentage from more diligent payers. Only in this case can balance be achieved.





If we talk about commercial credit organizations, they deliberately raise credit rates in order to obtain net profit and nothing more. That is why before applying for a microloan, you should study everything several times. Perhaps it is easier and more profitable to collect the necessary paper and obtain a loan from a trusted bank than to get a pig in a poke.
