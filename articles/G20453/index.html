Wonder Woman costume: how to do it yourself for an adult girl or girl

The costume of Wonder Woman - a popular comic book heroine, a superhero woman - is very extravagant and will not suit modest girls at all. Such a frank outfit will emphasize beauty, courage and sexuality, but attempts to make the costume less provocative will only spoil the image.

Bodycon Red Top

Make a Wonder Woman costume with your own hands is not difficult. The outfit consists of simple things that can be found in your own wardrobe or bought in a second-hand. You will definitely need a bright red top, which hugs the body very much. Well, if you can find a shiny red T-shirt, generously decorated with sequins, but in order to match the image, the clothes must be strapless.





Of course, you can take the usual red shirt, but then the image will not quite correspond to the usual clothes of Wonder Woman. You can also wear a red bodysuit or a closed red swimsuit. If you will feel more comfortable in clothes with straps, then you can slightly modify the original costume. Ideally, the top should emphasize the shoulders and chest.

Blue shorts or skirt

Wonder Woman's costume still has very short blue shorts with stars. You can find blue knitted shorts or a skirt (remember that clothes should be really very short), wear jeans. To decorate shorts or skirts, you can use ready-made star stickers. If they could not be found, stars cut out of white duct tape, fabric, paper will also do. To glue the necessary parts, use special glue for the fabric.





Suitable shoes

In addition, you need to find tall red boots made of leather (leatherette). Shoes should reach at least the middle of the game, it is better to wear boots to the knee. If you can‚Äôt find the red boots, you can buy regular white ones in the second-hand and paint them red from a spray can with skin paint. Also, boots should be decorated with thin stripes of white duct tape. The white strip should be at the very top of the boots and extend along the entire length of the shoe in front.

To make Wonder Woman's Halloween costume or New Year's carnival party, you can use not only boots, but also leather sandals with high lacing. This image of Wonder Woman will be close to the Amazons.

Image completion

When all the details of the Wonder Woman costume are prepared, you need to decorate them accordingly. You will need a golden adhesive tape, glue-based paper or fabric. On the top, you need to make a golden emblem resembling the usual Latin letter W. To do this, simply decorate the upper edge of the swimsuit or top with gold adhesive tape. To make the pattern more interesting, you can make double W.

Similarly, you need to decorate and shorts. You will need a wide golden belt or duct tape. You can also just take a white belt (or cut it out of a fabric) and dye it in golden color. Add a buckle in the form of a letter W to the front, cut from thick cardboard, if desired. You can attach this part to the belt using fabric glue.





The Wonder Woman costume will be incomplete without accessories. You need to find at least gold (made of plastic painted in golden color) thick bracelets. On them, you can additionally attach one bright red star from the outside. If you don‚Äôt have such gold bracelets, you can simply wrap your hands in gold foil, wrapping paper or a dressing of a suitable color.

The Wonder Woman costume and the golden tiara will complement the costume. A comic book character wears a tiara adorned with a star. Instead of a classic accessory, you can use a golden bandage or a cardboard wrapped in gold foil or wrapping paper. On the tiara you need to stick a sticker in the form of a red star. A star can also be made from fabric or duct tape.

Another attribute of Wonder Woman is the lasso. A traditional lasso can be replaced with an ordinary rope painted yellow or gold. At the ends of a piece of rope you need to tie knots, roll it up and hang it on your belt.

Makeup and other tips

The Wonder Woman costume does not require extravagant makeup. It is enough to draw attention to the lips, applying them with bright red lipstick, to the eyes. They should be black and alluring, so that a neat eyeliner and several layers of mascara are suitable. Shadows can not be used at all. The skin should be smooth and well-groomed, so a suitable foundation is required. Wonder Woman has black hair, so it‚Äôs better for blondes to wear a long wig.





Girls who are too uncomfortable in such a revealing costume can wear flesh- colored tights under shorts or a skirt. A good solution is to use a very short blue skirt-shorts. Then you can not be afraid to show something superfluous.

Option for girls

Wonder Woman's costume for girls, of course, should not be too provocative. Such an image for children is suitable for costume summer parties, but certainly not for the New Year's party. You need to buy a red closed swimsuit, decorate it with gold stripes at the top. Instead of boots, sandals will do. Wonder Woman costume for baby is ready!
