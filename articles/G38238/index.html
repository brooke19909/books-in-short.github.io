Hotel Alaiye Kleopatra Hotel 4 * (Turkey, Alanya): description and reviews of tourists

Turkey is the non-replaceable, overthrown leader of the tourism market for the reception of tourists from Russia, Ukraine, Belarus, the most attractive mass tourist destination.

Almost the entire territory of the Turkish state is surrounded by the sea coast, but not only natural resources contributed to the development of the tourism industry in Turkey. It is difficult to find a country where natural tourism potential is exploited so intensively, successfully and productively.

Statistics say that 65% of all CIS tourists choose Turkey for their first overseas holiday and more than 50% of them return to this hospitable country again.

Features and Benefits of Alanya Resort

Alanya is the most frisky, youngest and most budget resort area on the Mediterranean coast of Turkey. Due to its democratic nature, Alanya is in demand among Russian tourists. On the coast there are many inexpensive hotels that work on an "all inclusive" and offer tourists the opportunity to relax "in the Turkish style" for little money.

Alanya can be of interest to tourists in terms of excursion. The area in the region is very picturesque: pine forests, olive and tangerine groves, national parks, mountain landscapes. In the area there is something to watch for lovers of natural interests.





Alanya also has great cultural potential, on its territory there are many historical attractions. This colorful area is rich in reminiscences of the pirate past. Bays, castles, underground grottoes - corsairs' treasures attract crowds of tourists with their beauty and spirit of adventurism. In the city itself, travelers usually tend to visit attractions such as Kyzyl-Kule, or the Red Tower, the ancient city shipyard, a large eastern market, the mosque of Sultan Aladdin and so on.

Alaiye Kleopatra Hotel 4 * (Turkey Alanya, Alanya). general information

This classic resort-style hotel is located in the center of the large youth resort of Alanya, 100 meters from Cleopatra Beach, which has the Blue Flag Award for cleanliness and comfort. The hotel was built in 1987, a large-scale reconstruction was carried out in 2013. The hotel complex consists of a six-story main building and three apartment buildings.

Check-in is after 14:00, check-out is before 12:00. No early or express check-in services.





Smoking is prohibited in rooms and public areas on site. The hotel accepts credit cards.

Hotel location

Hotel Alaiye Kleopatra Hotel 4 * has a very good location. It is located in Turkler just a 5-minute walk from the center of Alanya (1000 m.) And 60 km from the city of Manavgat. Also 100 meters from the hotel is the most beautiful, convenient and clean beach of the eastern Mediterranean coast of Turkey - Cleopatra Beach.

The only inconvenient moment in the location of the hotel may be its remoteness from Antalya International Airport - 120 km. In the conditions of a group transfer with arrivals to other hotels, it is about 3-4 hours on the road by bus after the flight. Not too comfortable.

In Alanya, the local Gazipasa airport is being completed, which is located 45 km from the hotel, but so far it does not accept charter flights from Russian tour operators.





Alanya is a decent distance from most of the main attractions of Turkey. So for those who have not been to Pamukkale, Istanbul, Ephesus, Cappadocia, but want to visit these places of worship, Alaiye Kleopatra Hotel Apart 4 * will not be the best choice, as well as any other hotel in the region. Such tourists should better look at the resorts of the west coast: Marmaris or Bodrum.

But in Alanya, you can also satisfy cognitive and cultural hunger, there are enough attractions in the area: mosques, eastern markets, pirate memorabilia, museums.

Cleopatra Beach in Alanya

Hotel Alaiye Kleopatra Hotel Apart 4 *, like other city hotels in the center of Alanya, does not have its own beach. But the hotel is located less than 100 meters from the municipal city beach of Cleopatra. Cleopatra's beach today is considered the most beautiful, clean and equipped in the eastern region. It has the prestigious Blue Flag Award for cleanliness and environmental friendliness.

The length of the beach is 2000 meters.

The coast has all the necessary infrastructure for tourists: lifeguard stations, vending machines, cafes, shops, water equipment rental centers, changing cabins, toilets, showers, water slides, etc.

Sun loungers and umbrellas on the beach are paid, cost about two dollars a day. Beach towels are provided at the hotel on deposit.

There is a road and a promenade between the beach and the hotel.

Also associated with Cleopatra's beach is a myth, from which the name of this coast most likely comes from. Rumor has it that this beach was equipped many centuries ago by the well-known Mark Antony in order to accept Cleopatra, his eminent lady of the heart, with the maximum imperial scale.





They also say that sand for the beach was brought directly from Egypt. Today, this beach enjoys great prestige among tourists than the beaches of the same Hurghada. The sand here has a special structure, it is dense, mixed with small pebbles. Such coverage is more convenient for lovers of morning runs on the beach, beach sports. Also, the sea water in the region is cleaner, transparent, even taking into account rather high waves.

In the vicinity of the beach there are many diving clubs, the area is suitable for divers who like to contemplate underwater reliefs, caves, grottoes.

Alaiye Kleopatra Hotel Apart 4 *. Tourists reviews

More than half of the tourists spoke positively about the experience of their stay at the hotel. The hotel received more positive reviews due to its convenient location, proximity to Cleopatra beach and the center of Alanya.

Tourists also noted that the price-quality ratio in the hotel is very adequate.

Guests also spoke positively about food. Most reviews say that the food is tasty and fresh, there are a lot of fresh fruits and vegetables, the drinks are undiluted, but the "buffet" could only be called so with a stretch, the variety was not enough and the choice of dishes was rather meager. The staff is polite and helpful, the bartenders and waiters are nice.





The rooms are cleaned, the common areas in the hotel are also monitored. People who respect cleanliness are likely to appreciate the desire of the staff to provide it to every guest of this hotel.

Of the negative guests have repeatedly indicated that the appliances and plumbing in the rooms have seen better times, that the hotel needs reconstruction and repair, that the soundproofing in the rooms is poor.

Also, negative reviews about Alaiye Kleopatra Hotel 4 * related to the overcrowding of the hotel. The pool and the area around it are very small for so many guests. As a result, at 7 in the morning it is already difficult to find a free sunbed, for the same reasons there are lines in the restaurant.
