Mignon hat: knitting pattern

The bottom of the cap is knitted with yellow threads according to the simplest scheme with additions. In a ring of six air loops, two double crochets are knitted. Then in each next row you need to make uniform increases: one single crochet (SBN), then two SBN in one loop. In the next row there are 2 SBN, after which the next increase in RLS in one loop. Thus, in each next row, RLS will be added to each 2-3-4-5th ... loop.

So, we knit 10 rows of the bottom with additions, and so that the minion‚Äôs hat bends and takes the desired shape, you do not need to do the addition from the 11th row. The 13th and 14th rows are knitted in black - this will be the basis for future points. Further, until the 23rd row, yellow yarn is used, and the edge of the headdress is tied with ordinary crochet columns in a blue tone. You can tie small ears, but in the classic model they are not.





To make eyes, you need to knit two identical parts according to the bottom pattern and thinner crochet. You need to start with black, which will be the pupil, then go to the brown iris and finish with white.
