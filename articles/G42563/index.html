Hotel Mandrem Retreat Chillax Stay 2 * (India, Goa): photos and reviews of tourists

Holidays in the resort state of the west coast of India in North Goa have been appealing to tourists since the 70s. Then hippies discovered it. Since then, a powerful resort industry has grown here (hotels, restaurants, bars, Ayurveda centers, diving schools) and daily charter flights from Germany, France, Great Britain, Sweden operate.

Portugal, which has owned its overseas province of Goa for more than 400 years, has changed its appearance by implanting thousands and thousands of buildings of colonial architecture in the cities of the future state of India. Here little Portugal was built with numerous administrative and other buildings: railway stations, sea villas, elevators, mills, etc.





If the south of Goa offers guests measured and sedate rest at the level of classic and premium, then the glory of Northern Goa is based on large-scale youth parties, discos accompanied by psychedelic music, and trans-party youth parties.

Location

Mandrem Retreat Chillax Stay (ex Cuba Mandrem) is a small 2-star hotel built in the resort village of Mandrem, on the shores of the Arabian Sea. The hotel is safe and can be recommended for families with children and the elderly. Tours in it are in great demand, and they can really be bought only in 3-4 months.

In Mandrem, vacationers do not experience a cultural shock from simultaneous contact with crowds of people, albeit benevolent, but of a different, unusual mentality. There are no crowds, the hotel and the beach are out of the way, away from the main human flows. In the village of Mandrem, European notions of cleanliness and order are honored, and the inhabitants of Mandrem, the ubiquitous and dirty holy cows in India, dare to reject these places. The resort atmosphere of this place is determined by the proximity of the amazing Mandrem beach of the same name with the village, which is considered to be the best beach of Goa for a relaxing holiday . To him go from the hotel in question for about 5 minutes along the path between the Osaba hotel and the wall.

The state capital, the ancient colonial Portuguese city of Panaji (in India it is called differently - Panjim), is 36 kilometers from the hotel. The transfer from the airport to the hotel complex is 56 km long.

About hotel

Mandrem Retreat Chillax Stay Hotel (India) provides its residents with quality economy-class spa services. The price of the tour for 7 nights for accommodation in a double comfortable room is 65 953 rubles. The best vacation here is from October to March (in the dry season).





This modern three-story building, built on a small green area, outwardly imitates the old colonial, secular utilitarian, Portuguese style of pombalino. Its features: an enlarged gable roof casting a shadow; long (on the whole floor) loggias with numerous exits to them; straightforwardness, symmetry and harmonious repetition of straight lines of the facade.

The Mandrem Retreat Chillax Stay is ideally and organically integrated into the environment, which is facilitated by the compact surroundings surrounding it with royal palm trees and decorative tropical shrubs. Its long, spacious large loggias are ideal for evening contemplation of the surroundings and meditation.

Infrastructure

Hotel Mandrem Retreat Chillax Stay (India) is in essence a comfortable and cozy portal to the secluded Mandrem Beach. From the side of its facade it is decorated with an avenue of royal palm trees, while it has a convenient entrance and a small parking lot for vehicles. From the back, the territory of the hotel complex resembles a landscaped small summer cafe. Hotel guests in the evenings here like to while away time before going to bed.

The reception and the hotel restaurant are excellent. The guests are given attention, their wishes are taken into account, and the requests are fulfilled. The nearest exchange office is located on the same street as the hotel complex considered in the article. Near the hotel there is an inexpensive taxi rank.

Rooms

The accommodations at Mandrem Retreat Chillax Stay are positive. On the one hand, it is predictable that the economy class hotel complex will not offer its guests apartments with a maid, helipad services, and the opportunity to become members of a golf club.

But, on the other hand, the hotel complex has a special romantic, warm, family-friendly good-natured atmosphere inherent in good small hotels. The efforts of conscientious staff in all 16 spacious (area - about 30 m 2 ) rooms maintain order and comfort.





Mandrem Retreat Chillax Stay's rooms are spacious, clean and cleaned daily. The design is dominated by soft pastel colors. The walls and ceiling are plastered, the floor is tiled.

The rooms themselves are differentiated by floors: on each of the three levels of the hotel are located the same type, characteristic only of him rooms. On the ground floor there are standard rooms. Their furniture is inexpensive, but neat: a small wardrobe, two single or one double bed. The second floor contains deluxe rooms with a balcony overlooking the back of the building. The deluxe family rooms are located on the third floor; they are larger in area, however, lack balconies (a feature of the architecture of the hotel complex.

Each of them has air conditioning, fan, satellite TV.

Facilities and service

Standard and quality services for its clients are provided by the Mandrem Retreat Chillax Stay 2 * hotel. The reviews of its guests positively evaluate the work of the hotel reception. There is a bulletin board, tour desk, bike rental. There are no complaints about the quality of cleaning the rooms.

The guests are provided with paid laundry, safe, and doctor services. In the rooms of families who have come to rest with children, a cot is provided free of charge and babysitting services are provided.

Beach vacation

The Mandrem Retreat Chillax Stay Resort hotel complex is located near the relatively sparsely populated and calm Mandrem Beach with white flour-like, fine sand and original bridges. Hotel guests celebrate the fascination and comfort of a beach holiday. The beach is not crowded and landscaped with palm trees casting a shadow. Near the coast are cafes - sheki. Tourists, having come to them with companies, buy a couple of bottles of beer and then use the sun loungers belonging to the cafe for free all day.





If you go along the coast to the left relative to the hotel, then in 10 minutes you can go to another beach - Ashvem, if you follow half an hour to the right - to the crowded and party Arambol.

Leisure

Guests of Mandrem Retreat Chillax Stay 2 * do not enjoy a single beach holiday. The most dynamic rent scooters for rent at a price of 500 rupees for two days (the closest rental is at the Shiva Beach Hotel) and go to explore nearby attractions.

Excursions

Guests at the Mandrem Retreat Chillax Stay 2 * hotel buy excursions, and sometimes organize them themselves, in agreement with taxi drivers who are constantly working at the hotel. Mandrem has no attractions on the most secluded beach. But near it you can visit a coconut grove, Alex Church, Friday Bazaar. An important local sightseeing site is the famous Aguada Fort. This ancient formidable battle fortification with thick walls and arches, defended by 79 cannons, withstood many battles.

Tourists in Goa with guides working from travel agencies are traditionally offered a list of excursions. Guest reviews of Mandrem Retreat Chillax Stay generally rate these:
