How to learn to weave baubles from threads

Nowadays, there are a lot of types of needlework, these are origami, quilling, knitting, embroidery and so on. One of the most popular today is weaving baubles.

If you immediately have a question: "How to learn how to weave baubles?" - then read the following information. They can be of different lengths and thicknesses. And yet, how to learn how to weave baubles from threads? Perhaps, many people are interested in this issue, despite the fact that such jewelry is today a little less popular than it was a few years ago. This article describes some weaving techniques and presents patterns for weaving baubles from threads.

So:

1. The size of the threads should be four times the size of the bracelet. The number of threads in a wicker bauble should be at least eight. Having decided on the size and quantity, with the help of a tailor's pin, fix the future bracelet so that it does not twist.

2. Next, tie two knots on the adjacent thread on the left with the extreme thread located on the right. Thus, the threads will be intertwined. Next, with this thread, knot the knot on the next. And so to the edge of the bracelet. As you can see, a row has formed in the form of a diagonal strip of the same color. Repeat the procedure first, just tie the two knots to the right. Follow these steps to the end of the bracelet, and at the end of the work, carefully knot the knots, and the bauble you have woven is ready.





This type of weaving is the simplest. We present to your attention a different type of weaving. We prepare the threads and fix everything with a pin. Now start weaving in the opposite direction, and you will have a different pattern.

The third type of weaving baubles. As in the first method of weaving, prepare the threads, fasten with a pin. Weave through the middle in the classic way described above, and then - mirror. It will turn out very original and unusual. Gently tie those threads that meet in the middle of the baubles. Thanks to this type of weaving, the pattern will turn out in the form of arrows.

After a little familiarization with the technique, you can take on more complex options. Here is one of them.

How to learn to weave baubles in a country style oblique weaving?

Accessory with oblique weaving is worth weaving in rows. Thus, you can weave from right to left, and from left to right. We divide an even number of already fixed threads into pairs and tie them together alternately.
