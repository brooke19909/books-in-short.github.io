Holiday Village "White Sail": overview, features and reviews of tourists

On the banks of the Verkhnesysert reservoir, the Bely Sail recreation center welcomes its guests with cozy houses . People come here to take a break from city noise, breathe in the forest air, enjoy the silence and birdsong.





Location

Within the Bazhovskiye Mesti park, near the village of Verkhnyaya Sysert, the White Sail is located - a recreation center. Yekaterinburg from this fabulous place is at a distance of 45 kilometers.

Park "Bazhovskie places" is considered one of the most beautiful places in the Urals. The hotel staff took care of the convenience of tourists arriving by car. The road from Yekaterinburg is accompanied by signs, you can use the navigator. There is a recreation center all year round. 300 meters of its own beach along the lake allows guests to relax and enjoy plenty of exercise.





Accommodation in houses of category "Housing"

The recreation center "White Sail" offers vacationers comfortable wooden houses of three categories.

  • Category "Case". An 11-bed house is designed for relaxation by a large company. It consists of two rooms, one of which 5, and the other 6 single beds. The floor is carpeted. There is a kitchen with everything you need (fridge, dishes, kettle, table and chairs). The bathroom has a toilet, sink, cold and hot water. Near the house you will find everything you need for outdoor recreation - barbecue and a table with benches.
  • Category "Corps" for 8-16 people. The house has three rooms, 2-3 beds in each, some have two tiers. There is a kitchen and a bathroom.




Accommodation in houses of the category "Comfort"

The "Comfort" category house with year-round living is designed for 4 people. The houses are equipped with household appliances, a bathroom with a shower.

  • The category "Comfort" - 4-seater, with a large kitchen. The kitchen has a fridge and microwave. Room is equipped with a double bed and a sofa bed, satellite TV. The floors are carpeted. A chest of drawers is installed to store things. The bathroom has a shower cabin, equipped with hydromassage and rain shower, heating, hot and cold water, toilet and sink. Near the house is a cozy gazebo and barbecue.
  • The category "Comfort" - 4-seater with one room. Living conditions are the same as in a house with a large kitchen area.
  • The category of "Comfort" - 4-seater with single beds. The room has four beds, carpet. The kitchen has a fridge, kettle, necessary utensils and kitchen furniture. In the bathroom there is a sink, toilet, hot and cold water. Near the house there is a barbecue area - a barbecue and a table with benches.




Accommodation in houses of the category "Comfort +"

These houses are designed for families. The recreation center "White Sail" has three houses of this category.

  • "Comfort +" - 8-seater, three-room. Equipped with two double beds and two double sofas. It is possible to place another person for an additional fee - 500 rubles per day. He is provided with a folding bed with bedding. The house has a TV, fridge, microwave, the bathroom has a shower. In the courtyard barbecue.
  • "Comfort +" - 6-seater, two-room. There is a double bed and two double sofas. It is possible to place another person for an additional fee - 500 rubles per day. In the summer, the house accommodates eight people, two twin beds are added. Living conditions are the same as in a three-room house.
  • "Comfort +" - 6-seater, two rooms, without the possibility of placing two more people in the house in the summer. Living conditions are the same as in the previous two options.

Services and entertainment

The recreation center "White Sail" offers guests a variety of services and entertainment.

  • Of course, this is fishing. The fish lives anywhere in the pond; both lovers of fishing in deep places and in shallow water can choose a place for themselves.
  • The large territory of the institution includes not only the forest, but also the man-made sea - a large beautiful pond. The hotel is located on the very shore of the "sea". "White Sail" offers vacationers boating and catamarans, which guests are happy to rent, especially in the evenings.
  • Horseback riding in picturesque Bazhov places is especially popular.
  • On the territory of the recreation center is equipped with a spacious enclosed space where billiards and a table tennis table are located. Here, lovers of these sports spend their battles.
  • Who does not love the Russian bathhouse! This is a good company, and a great mood. At the recreation center "White Sail" you can take a steam bath in a Russian bath with a font. The cost of two hours in a bath is 3000 rubles. If at the same time there are more than six visitors in the bath, each person pays 100 rubles per hour.
  • For children in the health resort there is a cozy playground in the shade of pines and deciduous trees. In the summer, an inflatable trampoline is installed here. There is a children's area in the cafe.
  • There is a rental point at the base. You can rent football and volleyballs, bicycles. The administration is trying to constantly update the inventory to make your stay as comfortable as possible.
  • Near the houses (except summer) have their own mangal zones. All that is needed for the fire, as well as for the preparation of barbecue, you can purchase from the administrator, and products in the nearby supermarket Sysert.
  • At the recreation center there is a cafe and a summer terrace, where everyone can spend a beautiful wedding, corporate event, anniversary or any other holiday. It is only necessary in advance about your desire to inform the administration 1-2 months before the meeting. In a forest hotel you are not limited in time, as in a city, so you can celebrate your holiday at least a few days. If you want, they will help you organize a theme holiday, provide the services of a photographer and videographer, which will give you the opportunity to conduct an unforgettable photo shoot by the lake, on horses, in the shade of sprawling trees. The holiday program can include various games and competitions.




"White Sail", recreation center: reviews

Reviews about the rest in the "White Sail" for the most part, of course, are the best.

  • On the territory of the base during the day soft soft music is played.
  • There is a well-equipped beach with sun loungers and benches. The beach has clean white sand.
  • There is an opportunity to play beach volleyball.
  • For small children, a bed with bed linen and a highchair are provided.
  • The chefs cook perfectly, the food is very tasty.
  • The territory reserved for the recreation center, although large, but very clean, is constantly cleaned here.
  • Vacationers note that the houses have modern soft beds, clean linen.
  • Tourists who come in a large company, especially like sports equipment rental.
  • The guests really like the complex dinners in the cafe. This is especially convenient for those tourists who came to relax for just one day.

Well, one review of the White Sail base is in a slightly different vein: unfortunately, satellite TV does not always catch in the woods. In general, the guests have only the most positive impressions and the desire to come here again.





Conclusion

If you are interested in the recreation center "White Sail", its location will delight you. In the middle of the forest you will find small cozy houses. A camp site is not only a place where you can spend your vacation and organize a vacation in the country on a weekend. Come to the address: Verkhnyaya Sysert, from Yekaterinburg 45 kilometers along the Chelyabinsk highway. Here you will always be welcome!
