Rules for choosing a technical video endoscope

Technical video endoscope is a portable device designed for non-destructive testing of hidden entrances with different diameters of the inlet.


Features of the use of technical video endoscopes

The main advantages that it has technical video endoscope must include:
1) The versatility of the device and a wide range of applications.
2) High reliability. Video endoscopes are produced from modern high-tech materials, using the latest and most innovative technologies.
3) Technical video endoscopes are convenient and easy to use and do not require any special knowledge and skills.
4) Reasonable price.

The main criteria for choosing a technical video endoscope

When choosing a technical video endoscope, it is important to be guided by the following criteria:
1) The diameter of the chamber.
2) Autonomy of a technical video endoscope.
3) The length and diameter of the flexible cable.
4) The ability to display images on the display and other devices.
5) Additional features.

Technical video endoscope device

A technical video endoscope consists of a camera, a flexible cable, a main module and a control panel. In the main module of the device there is an electronic board, thanks to which data are processed, including video, and also they are output directly to the display via cable. Some models of technical video endoscopes are equipped with a video recording function or an SD card. Using the buttons located on the control panel, the object is deleted-zoomed (zoom). Also, using the buttons, navigation is carried out on the menu of the device.

The camera of a technical video endoscope is connected on a flexible cable, the diameter of which can be of various sizes. The most common are 9.50 mm, 8.00 mm, 5.50 mm, 10.00 mm and 5.00 mm. Typically, the camera has a diameter similar to the diameter of the cable. In most models, the camera and cable are moisture-proof, which allows for research and observation of the video underwater.
