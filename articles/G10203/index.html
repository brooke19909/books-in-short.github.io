Maui island: recreation, attractions, photos, reviews

Every lover of exotic relaxation knows exactly where the island of Maui is. According to the occupied area, this is the second section belonging to the Hawaiian archipelago. Maui Island is a magical corner with incredible landscapes, where the coolest modern resorts are located. It attracts tourists with its varied vacation, stunning flora and fauna, interesting adventures.

Aloha on Maui: reviews

You can spend your vacation on the shores of a quiet harbor in the current port or visit the city with its incredible sights. You have the opportunity to visit an exotic restaurant, go shopping, which clearly express the flavor of the area.





Tourists claim that everyone will be satisfied: someone can be alone with nature and enjoy its beauty, someone will want to taste the bright, full of passions, night life of a small city. Everything on the island awakens the most incredible feats in the imagination of tourists. Maui Island combines beauty, sophistication and exoticism. Here you can learn to play golf - there are several working schools in the city for this. The island of Maui, the photo of which must be in every tourist who has been here, is an unforgettable fairy tale.

If you make a trip to the city of Khan, you will fall into another dimension. You will find yourself somewhere in the past - here you can see incredible picturesque landscapes, touch the pristine nature, marvel at the beauty of powerful waterfalls and appreciate all the delights of true exotic.

The island of Maui is one of the most popular resorts. Millions of tourists from different countries visit it annually. Big fans of Hawaiian beauties are Americans and Japanese, who are fascinated by the rainforest in the Igla Valley. The cactus desert, the beautiful slopes of the volcano, which are densely overgrown with berries and various trees, amazes with its extraordinary nature. Near the foot of the volcano Haleakala there is a beach with white sand and coconut palms. The whole territory is dotted with a large number of waterfalls, streams, rivers, and the archipelago itself is surrounded by an endless ocean, in which the bright rays of the sun play.





Windsurfers, athletes, students of oriental masters, gymnasts, yoga fans rush to visit the island. It is believed that it is on this Hawaiian island that you can experience the tides of calm, peaceful and inspiring energy. This is notable for the island of Maui. The photo clearly demonstrates all the beauty of this place.

Climate

Maui has a tropical climate; it is very comfortable for both locals and vacationers. The average annual temperature is from twenty-three degrees of heat to twenty-nine. The southern coast of the island is always drier than the northern, on which precipitation often occurs.

Features of the archipelago

Quite often, Maui is called a valley due to its large area with lush green vegetation that connects two volcanoes. The terrain is very diverse: within the island are lowlands, steep climbs, gentle and high mountains.

At a bird's eye view, when you climb nine hundred and fourteen meters above the ground, you feel dry air and lower temperatures. All this is due to the intermittent features of the island - weather conditions, cloudiness and rainfall. This is a unique and amazing difference between the climate of Maui. So, for example, the green areas of Haleakala Park are located in seven different climatic zones.

Garden of eden

Travelers claim that the island of Maui is the Garden of Eden of the Hawaiian Islands. On this charming archipelago you can admire the green canyons, stormy and strong waterfalls that fall from the mountain slopes, marvel at the contrast of white and black beaches, visit the impenetrable, riotous greenery jungle. But the most unforgettable sight of Maui is a magnificent, incredibly beautiful botanical garden, where you can see more than five hundred exotic representatives of the flora. In this park, the famous colorful film "Jurassic Park" was filmed.





Rare trees grow on twenty-six hectares of green land, there are more than six hundred species of palm trees, more than five hundred varieties of flowers. In this wonderful place you can hide from the life of noisy cities, from people and civilization, enjoy the peace, tranquility and pristine nature of the wild jungle. And what is the fragrance of exotic flowers that fill this paradise!

It is not for nothing that the locals gave the botanical garden the name "Eden" - this is the most bewitching, beautiful corner of the islands. People who have already been here fully agree with this.

Khan City: reviews

Tourists say that the island of Maui has the most diverse attractions. So, visiting the archipelago, you must make a trip to the city of Khan, which is located on the eastern shore and is the most beautiful and picturesque place of the island. This small town isolated from civilization is filled with mysticism; its unspoiled natural beauty fascinates and attracts to itself. Every tourist who rested here can imagine what the island of Maui was many years ago. For travelers in the city there is only one hotel and several cottages that can be rented.





The indisputable advantages of the city are lush green vegetation, as well as mountains with their grandeur and arrogance, stunning beaches strewn with white and black sand, where you can soak under the warm sun.

Tourists can go kayaking, go snorkelling, brave holidaymakers can hang glider, romantics can ride horses or walk to local attractions.

Road to the city

Before you get into the city, you need to drive eighty-eight kilometers, which will quietly disappear under the wheels, because at this time you can enjoy the magnificent and diverse landscape of the archipelago. You are lucky enough to see unusual bridges along a bamboo grove, climb up the slopes, drive fruit trees and beautiful gardens with flowers, whose aroma spreads over a long distance. And here you are in a dream city in which a wonderful vacation begins.

An interesting attraction of the island is the Haleakala Crater - this is the top of a sleeping volcano, which is located at an altitude of three thousand fifty-five meters. At the top you can discover an unforgettable view of the sunset, which will seem to you something unusual, magical, just wonderful. When you are in Maui, you should definitely find time to visit this sleeping volcano.

Myths of Maui

Resting on Maui, you should visit the mythical attraction of the island - Kaanapali - a beach area in the western part of the coast. This is a historical and cultural place, here is the Black Mountain. According to the traditions of the natives, this is a sacred place. According to the myths of Hawaii, it is here that the souls of the dead pass from the world of the living to the world of the dead.





Nearby, nearby is the village of Whalers Village, which houses an open-air market, as well as many shops and restaurants.

Crescent in the ocean: reviews

Molokini Island is next to Maui. Nature created it in the form of a crescent, its origin is volcanic, but the underwater volcano has been idle for decades. This attraction of Hawaii is well developed by connoisseurs of relaxation under water, the bright representatives of the ocean have been studied.

Travelers say that the unique attraction of the Hawaiian island is the Maui Ocean Center - an interesting place for lovers of underwater ocean life. Here, tourists can see thousands of different types of fish. The inhabitants of the ocean settled in a huge aquarium, which includes three million liters of water. Young guests are incredibly amazed to see this spectacular sight, and watch the sea animals that live their lives behind thick glass.





Few tourists know that, having flown to Hawaii, they find themselves in a favorite place of whales, since the space around the archipelago is the refuge of the hunchbacked giants. They appear in ocean waters from December to April. Whales migrate to the Hawaiian Islands for breeding; in the Kaanapali Park you can watch these amazing huge creatures of nature.

What awaits tourists on Maui?

The island of Maui for its visitors opens up an unforgettable vacation with a wide variety of adventures. Guests of the island will be able to learn how to play golf; there are about twenty special fields in the archipelago. Tourists have the opportunity to rent cars and sweep through the green thickets, exploring local attractions.

Fans of unusual and extreme relaxation can take a boat trip, explore the beauty of the ocean world, ride a jet ski or go fishing in the depths.

What could be more interesting than flying a warm day by parachute or conquering ocean waves on a surfboard? If you relax with your family and you have children, you can ride a catamaran. The island's unique offer is snorkeling with turtles.

Tours Maui: reviews

Travelers note that the island of Maui tours offers the most interesting and unique. Locals organize the following excursions: on the granulated sugar of Halekala beach, to the volcano, horseback riding, cycling and an unforgettable hang glider flight. A helicopter tour is popular, which involves a trip to the city of Khan and a visit to the western part of the island. Excursions are very diverse and interesting, so no matter what you choose, there will be a lot of emotions.



Tours for honeymooners are very popular, which involve romantic evenings on a yacht with dancing, cocktails and Hawaiian music. Sailing trips will not leave you indifferent. In the evenings, guests can organize stylistic contests, entertainments, magic shows and treats with traditional dishes.

Kaihalulu

There is another amazing place - the red Kaihalulu beach on the island of Maui. The coast got its name thanks to the sand of dark red color, which in some places turns into a red color. This color was formed due to long-standing eruptions of the volcano, which is now already destroyed. In addition, rust is part of the sand - it is she who gives an unusual copper tint.

Imagine the bright greenery of the local jungle, which contrasts with the red sand and the sky-blue color of the sea. All together turns into an unusual, bewitching landscape. The beach is far away, so getting there is quite difficult. Thanks to this, the coast has become a favorite place for nudists.

Maui Island has only positive reviews from visitors. Everyone who has ever visited this place wants to spend their next vacation there. The Hawaiian island is suitable for everyone - romance, adventurers, travelers, couples with children will find here for themselves relaxation. Everyone will be able to choose a vacation to their liking. Holidays will be varied, amazing and comfortable. Skillfully combines the capabilities of a megalopolis and secluded natural zone on the island of Maui. Hawaii, whose photos are mesmerizing, is the favorite vacation spot of wealthy people. So put off all your affairs - and go, towards the dream!
