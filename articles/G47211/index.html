Sights of Rio de Janeiro: photos and descriptions, overview of the most interesting places, tips before visiting and reviews of tourists

Tired of familiar Europe? Wanted more exotic, but Asia is no longer attracted? The best solution in this situation is to go to South America. There are many great places on this continent that are definitely worth a visit, but the main treasury and goddess of football is Brazil with the wonderful administrative center of the state of the same name Rio de Janeiro!

Rio de Janeiro, located off the coast of a fabulous picturesque bay, is one of the leaders in the list of the most beautiful places on the planet. This is a vibrant and at the same time colorful city that is able to fall in love with itself at first sight.

The streets are buried in tropical greenery, the famous favelas (slums) shimmer with all the colors of the rainbow under the rays of the scorching Brazilian sun, ultra-modern buildings fascinate tourists! And what are their bright carnival processions worth! Such as here, no longer find.

No city in the world can do without iconic places that are definitely worth a visit to a tourist. Sights of Rio de Janeiro - a wide variety of cultural and natural monuments of art. The quantity is simply amazing. For about 200 years, the city served as at least temporary, but the capital of Brazil. The history of Rio de Janeiro in Brazil is interesting and unique, but we will not focus on this. Let's get acquainted with the main attractions.

Christ statue in Brazil

The world-famous statue of Christ the Redeemer, located at the highest point of Rio de Janeiro, is a kind of calling card not only of the city, but of the whole country. Not a single modern film shot on the territory of a wonderful city can do without the famous panorama of Mount Corcovado and a statue of Jesus Christ with outstretched arms.

The construction of the statue began at the beginning of the XX century, the main occasion was the anniversary of the independence of Brazil. The best craftsmen from different countries worked on the future construction, since it was not possible to manufacture parts of such impressive sizes at that time in Brazil.

The main attraction of the city of Rio de Janeiro is striking in its size, the building is about 38 meters in height. In comparison, this is the height of a modern twelve-story building. The statue of Christ the Redeemer is visible from anywhere in the city, because in addition to its impressive size, the height of the hill on which it is located is more than 700 meters! In addition, this attraction of Rio de Janeiro is listed as New Wonders of the World.

Copacabana Beach

Copacabana Beach is a very popular holiday destination in all of Brazil. Driving the name of a city in a search engine, you automatically get thousands of different photos with the image of this attraction. Its uniqueness lies in its length. It stretches along the Atlantic Ocean for as many as four kilometers. It is always crowded, because it is not only a hot tourist spot, but also a favorite place for many locals. The history of the region begins from the moment when wealthy people began to come to Rio de Janeiro to relax and have fun, now it is an accessible beach for all with all its charms.

During the day, you can watch life in full swing in Rio - there are football and volleyball courts, outdoor exercise machines, diving and much more at the service of vacationers.

Many world-famous celebrities have performed on Brazil's main beach , including Elton John and the legendary Rolling Stones.

Sugarloaf Mountain

If there are exotic attractions in Rio de Janeiro in Brazil, then this place is just one of them! Sugarloaf Mountain is also the hallmark of the city and the most recognizable place in all of Latin America. It got its name as a result of its similarity to the shape of cone-shaped clay containers that the Portuguese used to transport sugar.

Separately, it is worthwhile to pay attention to the fact that the mountain, despite its small height, offers tourists a beautiful and picturesque view of the whole city. In particular, you can take gorgeous pictures during sunset. Having visited one of the restaurants located on the top of the Sugarloaf, you can enjoy not only the local cuisine, but also the bewitching panorama of the whole of Rio de Janeiro.

Tijuca National Park

The uniqueness of Tijuca Park lies in the fact that it is the largest forest in the world, located within the city territory. In addition, all that is located in the Tijuca National Park was completely created by the hand of man. At one time in Brazil, work was carried out to destroy trees to create coffee and sugar plantations. As a result, the residents of the city decided to create an ecologically clean zone, which to this day is the Tizhuk park. Today it is one of the most popular places in Rio de Janeiro, where you can admire not only the abundance of tropical plants and wildlife, but also picturesque waterfalls and cozy local restaurants.

Staircase of celaron

Among the popular attractions of Rio de Janeiro, it is worth noting the famous motley staircase of Celaron. This place serves as a kind of proof that even an ordinary design can be turned into a real work of art. The story of the staircase is very interesting. A true professional in his field, ceramics artist Jose Selaron acquired multi-colored tiles and adorned some of the stairs, located near his house. Maracana Stadium

It's no secret that all Hispanics are a sporting people, they devote most of their free time to football. On this basis, the local football arena with the fancy name "Maracana" has gained immense popularity. It is simply impossible to pass by the city stadium; many tourists, fans of this game, can admire it for hours. After all, everything is connected with the names of famous stars of world football. As for the external component, the Maracan stadium looks quite impressive and majestic. Once 200 thousand fans gathered here for the match between Brazil and Uruguay in the framework of the 1950 World Cup, since then this record has not been able to beat any of the football arenas. If you want to see the legend with your own eyes, then you are definitely in Rio!

Conclusion

Brazil is quite popular among tourists from different countries. Many rush here for a mass of new experiences, wonderful relaxation, exotic. Even though the flight is expensive, it's still worth a trip to Rio de Janeiro. This is a great opportunity to see a huge open-air museum.
