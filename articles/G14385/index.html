Boguslavsky Leonid - a successful Internet investor and triathlete

Leonid Borisovich Boguslavsky is the largest Russian investor. Actively invests in IT companies and the Internet. Head of the international company ru-Net. In 2012, Boguslavsky was recognized as the Forbes investor of the year. And in 2014, the same magazine put him on the 162nd place of the annual rating of the richest businessmen of the Russian Federation. This article will describe a brief biography of the entrepreneur.

In 1989, Boguslavsky Leonid, together with Boris Berezovsky, began to engage in a number of business projects in the LogoVAZ joint venture. Internet projects

In 1998, interest in the World Wide Web grew significantly. And since Boguslavsky already had good experience in the field of computer networks, PwC added him a leadership function in the countries of Eastern and Central Europe. After some time, Leonid Borisovich began to independently invest in various Internet projects. At the end of 1999, the hero of this article met Charlie Ryan (UFG), David Mixer (Rex Capital) and Mike Calvey (Baring Vostok). At that time, these three were planning to create a company that would specialize in investing on the Internet.

Holding ru-net

Boguslavsky Leonid founded it in 2000 with the participation of two funds - UFG and Baring Vostok. ru-Net became the first holding company to invest in Yandex (35% of shares for $ 5.27 million) and Ozon.ru ($ 3 million for a controlling stake). In 2006, all assets (except for the share in the search engine) were transferred to ru-Net investors. At the moment, Boguslavsky owns 20% of Ozon.ru.

Own company

In 2006, Leonid Boguslavsky, whose family is described below, founded his company under the name ru-Net Limited. Immediately after that, negotiations began with startups Vkontakte and Odnoklassniki. But no agreements were reached. But Leonid Borisovich invested in HeadHunter and iConText.

Leaving Yandex

In 2008, Boguslavsky left the Board of Directors of this company. This happened because the businessman began to invest in companies that could potentially create a conflict of interest with the search engine. The entrepreneur also bought the company Digital Akcess from Leonard Blavatnik, creating on its basis, together with Oleg Tumanov, the largest online movie theater ivi.ru in Russia.

international market

In 2011, Boguslavsky Leonid significantly expanded the scope of his activities. He began to actively invest in international markets. For these purposes, the businessman created a subsidiary of ru-Net in the USA.





Family and hobbies

Leonid Boguslavsky, whose biography is presented above, is married. The investor has three children. In his free time, the entrepreneur enjoys extreme travel, kite surfing and skiing. Leonid is also involved in charity work and actively supports our poets.

In 2013, Boguslavsky was seriously carried away by the triathlon and took part in the Ironman competitions. In just six months of training, Leonid Borisovich has gone from a beginner to a prize podium. Well, after another 1.5 years, the investor qualified for the World Cup, which was held in Hawaii. In early 2016, Boguslavsky founded the Angry Boys Sport commercial club. The main focus of his activity was the preparation of Russian amateur athletes for standard triathlon and other long-distance competitions (cyclic).
