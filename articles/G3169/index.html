LCD "Ankudinovsky Park": photos and reviews

For residents of such a large industrial city as Nizhny Novgorod, the opportunity to purchase apartments in a comfortable, environmentally friendly area is a real gift. Residential complex "Ankudinovsky Park" is a vivid representative of a modern, thought-out complex for life, where all the necessary conditions are created. The project attracts a lot of attention, therefore, it definitely deserves our detailed review.





about the project

Residential complex "Ankudinovsky Park" (Nizhny Novgorod) is a large-scale development project of a whole microdistrict, represented by 27 monolithic buildings of varying number of storeys. All of them are designed in the same architectural style, perfectly combined with the greenery surrounding the complex. The developer offers a variety of planning solutions, a spacious landscaped area and access to all necessary infrastructure. A new format of quality and affordable housing for modern families - all this is Ankudinovsky Park.

Developer

Residential complex Ankudinovsky Park is a project of the Karkas Monolit group of companies. For 17 years of existence in the real estate market, the company was able to gain the necessary experience. Currently, it specializes in the design and construction of residential buildings using monolithic technology, provides residents of the city with economy class apartments.





Location

For the construction of a new complex, an ecologically clean area of ‚Äã‚Äãthe city, freed from harmful industries, was chosen. A plot of 50 hectares outside Nizhny Novgorod was allocated for construction. The attention of the developer was attracted by the Kstovsky district, the village of Ankudinovka. Clean air, an abundance of greenery, excellent opportunities for organizing leisure in the fresh air - this is exactly what Ankudinovsky Park residential complex is now imagining. The developer has developed a plan for a new neighborhood, trying to preserve the beauty of the natural landscape as much as possible. The result is a modern, high-quality complex, surrounded on all sides by a forest belt. The city is very close - it is only 6 km away.

Transport accessibility

The remoteness about the city scares potential buyers, because not every family has a car with which you can get to work, take children to kindergarten and school. To ensure the necessary transport accessibility, the developer provided for the construction of bus stops, additional public transport routes will be launched in the direction of the new microdistrict. Getting to anywhere in the city will be quick and easy, even for those who do not use personal transport.





Ankudinovka railway station is a 10-minute walk from the new buildings. From it, you can reach the Moscow railway station in an hour or so. Car owners in just 20 minutes in the absence of traffic jams will be able to get to the city center.

Ecology

The apartments of Ankudinovsky Park residential complex have, perhaps, the main advantage that will distinguish them from the rest - the environmental friendliness of the place where they are built. Next to the complex is a huge Ankudinovsky park, after which the project was named, as well as the Botanical Garden with rare plant species. In this regard, the residents of the complex will have an advantage over those who live in a dusty and crowded city.

Near the complex there are clean forest lakes, a forest park. All major highways with heavy traffic are located at a sufficient distance from the complex, due to which they will not disturb the peace of the inhabitants.





Landscaping

All car owners will be able to use the underground parking: you can go down to it directly from the floor of your apartment using a high-speed elevator. Due to the underground parking, it was possible to isolate the yards from the accumulation of cars and arrange modern playgrounds and sports grounds for children and adults. The project provides for the creation of walking areas, bike paths.

Infrastructure

When planning the construction of a distant, albeit not essential, from the city of the microdistrict, it is important to consider access to all necessary infrastructure facilities. The own infrastructure of the microdistrict is represented by several kindergartens, a comprehensive school, a business center, a fitness center and a shopping and entertainment center. The first floors of the houses will be reserved for commercial infrastructure: as the object is commissioned, bank branches, beauty salons, cafes and shops will appear here.

The village of Ankudinovka is very small, infrastructure is not represented in abundance and diversity, but in 10 minutes you can drive to the village of Fedyakovo, where the largest shopping and entertainment center Mega operates .

Apartments, layouts

How comfortable was the property in Ankudinovsky Park residential complex? Reviews of apartments, of course, primarily cover a variety of planning decisions. The developer offers buyers apartments from 1 to 4 rooms ranging from 18 to 89 square meters. The apartments are not impressive in spaciousness, quite compact, but due to modern and rational layouts, it is possible to provide comfort to each member of the family.





All apartments are rented turnkey. It is represented by a laminate on the floor, washable wallpaper on the walls, entrance and interior doors, balcony glazing, high-quality European-made sanitary ware, porcelain tiles in the bathroom and the bathroom. In addition, the kitchen of all residents is waiting for a modern kitchen set with built-in appliances.

Price policy

Surely many are interested to know what the price of comfort and clean air is. The cost of one-bedroom apartments with decoration starts at 1.6 million rubles, a four-room apartment for a large and friendly family can be purchased for 4 million rubles. For an apartment ready to receive tenants - this is a great price.

To summarize

Residential complex "Ankudinovsky Park" is an example of a modern complex where all living conditions are created. The project will definitely attract the attention of families with small children and those who pay great attention to the quality of their life, want to hide from the hustle and bustle of the metropolis without losing access to the necessary infrastructure.

Take a closer look at the project, its final commissioning is planned for the 4th quarter of 2017, while there is still the opportunity to purchase an apartment on favorable terms.
