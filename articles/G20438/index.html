Prestigio - next generation tablets

Modern computers have become much more compact and now take up very little space. From now on, PC is not a bulky box. It is time for tablet computers that fit easily not only on the desktop, but also in the bag. Moreover, in terms of functionality, they are absolutely not inferior to larger

models. Compared to all other companies, Prestigio stands out. The tablets of this manufacturer have already become popular all over the world and have acquired a sufficient number of enthusiastic fans. What is their main difference from other high-tech devices?

The flagship unit was rightfully recognized as the Prestigio Multipad tablet. The line of laptop computers with this name instantly went into the hands of buyers. The "filling" of this tablet impresses with its power. Inside the 7100C, a gigahertz processor was hidden, the platform for which was an option called ARM Cortex A8. This content allows you to simultaneously run several full-size applications. None of them will treacherously slow down.



What else is Prestigio ready to offer its customers? Tablets released for retail sale under this brand can boast of high-quality displays. As a rule, they have a high resolution. In addition, all tablets are almost omnivorous in terms of video and audio file formats. For these devices, there is no need to additionally download a large number of players. The screens are of a good size, which allows you to easily read books from your tablet, as well as enjoy various games.

The Prestigio 3G tablet is another version of this brand's portable computer. A distinctive feature of this particular model is the ability to connect to networks of the specified format. This significantly increases the data transfer rate. 3G format communication is a huge plus for those who want to always be able to view their email and log in to their account on social networks.

It is worth noting a good battery. Thanks to it, tablets can please their owners with quite a long continuous operation. Of course, all models are equipped with Bluetooth and Wi-Fi adapters . Therefore, Prestigio, tablets

which is increasingly becoming the choice of customers, is considered a company that keeps up with its closest competitors.

The main advantage of all models is, of course, price. Compared to the more well-known brands, Prestigio tablets are sold almost one and a half, or even two times cheaper. In this case, the product can not be called base. A lot of positive reviews for the products of this company indicate the integrity of the manufacturer.

As for compactness, in this case we are talking about light and small models. The company Prestigio (tablets were developed for active people) focused on light weight (approximately 500 g) and small external dimensions. You can easily take your new purchase with you on a trip, to work, or lend it to a friend for temporary use. The tablet of this company will be an excellent assistant in the field of business, and will also allow you to relax while watching your favorite movie.
