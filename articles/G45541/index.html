Travel reviews. Sharjah: the charm and attractiveness of a Muslim resort

A huge number of people who have visited the United Arab Emirates mainly write about Dubai or Abu Dhabi. But there is another resort about which tourists leave very positive reviews. Sharjah is also the capital, only the emirate, which is not so far from Dubai. Go there no more than a quarter of an hour. This is a very interesting place, as tourists say. People claim that in Sharjah the European feels himself in the very heart of the civilized world, and at the same time understands that he is in the Muslim East. This is the main attraction of this resort. At least, such a conclusion can be made by reading reviews.



Sharjah is only forty years old. This is a completely new city in the desert, where skyscrapers, ultra-modern hotels, green parks and beautiful manicured promenades are adjacent to Bedouin-style ornaments and Islamic-inspired floral motifs. There are various hotels in the city, but tourists prefer a budget vacation. They write different reviews about these categories of hotels. Sharjah has such an arrangement that a good hotel should be close to the promenade, promenade, canal and inexpensive cafes and bistros. After all, as a rule, hotels include only breakfast, and you have to take care of the rest of the food yourself.



From the sights of the city, tourists unanimously mark a fifty-meter fountain, beating from Khor Khalid Bay and Al Majaz Park. The last place is also the most popular for recreation of local residents. Therefore, only good reviews can be seen about this green zone . Sharjah, like many cities in the Emirates, needs such beautiful and shady places to relax, especially since the park has playgrounds and picnic areas (although most people prefer to sit directly on the grass on the bedspreads), and special items for hygiene, and treadmills. There are even park mosques where you can pray orthodox. However, the most beautiful Islamic temples are located on the other side of the bay. They especially look spectacular at night, because they stand out against the background of skyscrapers.



Many, even very inexpensive, Sharjah hotels have a free shuttle service to the beach area. Tourists emphasize that hotel management is generally excellent. After all, there are almost never any queues or flecks either in restaurants or when boarding buses to the sea. The same transfer is provided to Dubai, and it is carried out in the evenings. Similar rules exist in many hotels in the UAE.

Sharjah, reviews of which are increasingly appearing on many travel sites, provides many opportunities for vacationers to eat on their own. Most supermarkets have culinary departments where they cook ready-made meals. There are restaurants with different cuisines and prices that are lower than in major Russian cities - Indian, Chinese, Arab.
