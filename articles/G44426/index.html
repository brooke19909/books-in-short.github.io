Hotel-park "Nemchinovka": reviews and photos

Brief description of the hotel

Hotel-Park "Nemchinovka" is based in the chateau format, which can often be found in European countries. In small cottages there are 76 rooms that offer guests comfortable living conditions. It is worth noting the restrained classic design in warm colors, which allows guests to feel the comfort of home. In addition, all apartments offer a wide range of amenities.

Despite the fact that the hotel-park "Nemchinovka" is designed in the style of medieval castles, the services provided in it are quite consistent with the spirit of modernity. Here you can hold business events, as well as any celebrations. In addition, many guests come here just to relax and have fun: swim in the pool, take a steam in the sauna, join an active lifestyle, and more.

Special attention deserves the restaurant, which has a hotel-park "Nemchinovka." Here you will be met by a beautifully decorated room, and in the menu you will find the best European dishes. It is also worth noting the lobby bar, where in a relaxed atmosphere you can drink coffee with desserts or enjoy alcoholic beverages.





Park-hotel "Nemchinovka": address

This conference hotel is located just a few kilometers from Moscow, which makes it an ideal option for a country vacation or outbound business and special occasions. The local train station is just 20 minutes from the hotel. Vnukovo Airport is 22 kilometers away from this establishment. The hotel address is Nemchinovka village, 2nd Zaprudnaya street, 36.





Hotel Rooms

An ideal option for a rural holiday is the village of Nemchinovka. The park hotel of the same name offers the following accommodation options:

  • Standard apartments are a spacious bright room with an orthopedic double bed. There is also a small hallway area through which you can get to the bathroom equipped with a shower.
  • The club room gives you a feeling of comfort and luxury. There is a lot of living space, as well as a convenient layout. Within one room, the combined areas of the bedroom and living room, which are separated by a bar.
  • Executive apartments consist of two rooms. The first is a luxurious bedroom with a huge bed and many decorative elements. The living room is equipped with folding soft furniture, which can act as a bed for additional guests. There is also a large dining table.
  • Townhouses are small two-story cottages that can be considered the best option for accommodation in this hotel. On the second floor there is a bedroom and a bathroom, and on the first floor there is a luxurious living room with a fireplace.

It is worth noting the predominance of the classical style in the design of the rooms that the hotel-park Nemchinovka offers its guests. Photos taken by tourists demonstrate restraint in color schemes and decor.





The amenities provided in the apartment

Chateau-hotel "Nemchinovka Park" offers guests to stay in apartments that are equipped with the following list of amenities:

  • landline telephone, which serves not only to communicate with the administration and reception, but also has access to long-distance and international lines;

  • signal of free wireless internet;

Positive reviews

Many tourists for leisure, as well as visiting business and special occasions choose the hotel-park "Nemchinovka". Reviews about this place contain a huge number of positive comments:

  • the rooms are very beautiful, and also a lot of living space;
  • if you settle in two-room rooms, there will be a TV in the living room and in the bedroom;
  • excellent wireless internet signal quality;
  • incredibly beautiful view from the window;
  • large well-groomed territory;
  • good infrastructure.

Negative reviews

Of the negative aspects, it is worth noting the following:

  • despite the fact that the hotel is located in a park area, its territory is quite noisy (and even closed windows do not save);
  • there are no refrigerators in the rooms, and therefore you have to eat in the restaurant (which is quite expensive);
  • in the bathroom there are no shelves for cosmetic and other accessories;
  • the rooms have a smell of tobacco smoke (airing does not help, since it has already soaked into the furniture);
  • very uncomfortable mattresses on the beds (after them the back is terribly sore);
  • very weak pressure of water in the shower;
  • double beds have too small blankets;
  • breakfast is very modest for its price;
  • the waiters in the restaurant are slow and, moreover, are not very polite to the guests;
  • in many rooms, instead of a double bed, you are offered 2 shifted beds;
  • there is no duvet cover for an extra blanket;
  • drinking water is not provided;
  • there are no utensils in the rooms (not even cups or ordinary glasses).
