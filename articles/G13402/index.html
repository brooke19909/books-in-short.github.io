Kyzyl Hotels: where to stay in the city?

Those who have never been to Kyzyl consider it only a less important point on the path leading to the most beautiful national parks, picturesque lakes and mountain passes. But it is worth coming here at least for a few days to make sure that this opinion is erroneous: there are many corners in the city worthy of the attention of even experienced tourists.

One of the most important significant objects is the prayer drum, which has no analogues yet. The house shelters the structure from the weather and the sun. In the drum itself, made of copper, there are leaves. There are a lot of them - 115 million. Each leaflet has a specific mantra. Already one prayer drum is worthy to come to the city of Kyzyl, and there are other sights besides it. To get acquainted with them, it will take several days, so be sure to take care of the place of residence.

Kyzyl hotels are quite diverse both in terms of services and cost, so you can choose the most suitable option for any wallet.

"Cottage"

Although the institution is located in the city center, it is almost immersed in the greenery of trees. Thanks to this, even in the summer heat in many rooms the hotel is cool. Not far from the establishment and the picturesque Yenisei coast is one of the best places for walking. The hotel belongs to economy class establishments: there are only 12 rooms. Modern appliances and necessary furniture, homeliness and cleanliness are its distinctive features. The range of services is quite wide, able to satisfy the needs of even the most demanding guest.

Buyan Badyrgy



The establishment is a modern hotel complex with a good location: 10 km to the railway station, 40 to the airport. 75 people can stay at the hotel at the same time. It offers apartments, standard rooms and suites. Each of them has everything that a guest may require:

  • Air conditioning.
  • Kettle.
  • Refrigerator.
  • Telephone.
  • TV
  • The Internet.

In addition, the Buyan Badyrgy hotel has a conference center, which is considered an ideal venue for a variety of business events. For exhibitions and presentations, celebrations and conferences, seminars and business meetings, the institution always provides decent decoration and provision.

"Mongulek"



For those who are interested in Kyzyl hotels located near the bus station, the Mongulek hotel is more suitable than others because it is located almost next to it. Nearby is the city center, so it will be convenient to get to administrative institutions and attractions. In the hotel building on the ground floor, in addition to the laundry room, there is a video salon, a buffet, a dental office, a hairdresser, a beauty salon, a sushi bar. The number of rooms is quite diverse, ranging from economy rooms to luxury rooms.

Strike

Not only large Kyzyl hotels are in demand, but also small establishments with several rooms. One of these hotels is a mini-hotel "Strike", among 5 rooms there is an opportunity to choose apartments of comfort. Those traveling with pets are always welcome here. In addition to free toiletries and slippers, guests are offered the opportunity to use an LCD TV, a kettle, and a private bathroom. On the territory of the institution there is a billiard room, a hairdresser.

Odugen



The facility is located in the city center. Nearby is the bank of the Yenisei. Not all Kyzyl hotels can boast of such a placement.

In the same building as the hotel are:

  • Souvenir shop.
  • Hair Salon.
  • Buffet.
  • A restaurant.
  • Beauty salon (pedicure, manicure).
  • Domestic services room.
  • Xerox and other services.

The 27 rooms of the hotel can accommodate more than four dozen guests.
