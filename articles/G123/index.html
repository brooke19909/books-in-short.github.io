Parsley is a favorite hero of booth fair performances. A cheerful, sharp-tongued buffoon who is not afraid to ridicule either law enforcement officers, or royal boyars, or the Tsar-priest himself. The doll Petrushka was necessarily in Russia in every wandering theater, and the genre scenes played with her participation invariably gathered crowds of peasants and townspeople in any locality. The appearance of the toy is easily recognizable, and your child in this image at the New Year's party will have undeniable success!

Parts and equipment



The costume of Parsley must necessarily meet two requirements: the brightness of the design and the preservation of Russian folk color. We emphasize: this is precisely the Russian folk hero. Unknown authors from the people and then professional writers created their plays about him, his leprosy.

The outfit consists of a cap, a shirt with a sash belt, wide trousers and boots. Make it easy from improvised means, having additionally some accessories.

Hat cap

Parsley suit can be with a single cap, or with a two-horn. The first is made of flexible cardboard and painted to match the clothes. Or it is covered with fabric, and multi-colored circles, shreds, etc. are glued onto it. Be sure to decorate the confetti hat! After all, this is an outfit for the holiday! The cone of the cap ends with a bell (you can buy a small sample in the gift shop) or a pompom-bell - it is easy to make it from a piece of fabric and cotton wool. You can diversify the costume of Parsley, giving it a fabulous shade, and instead of a pompom, make a brush from a sparkling Christmas tree "rain" or tinsel.



A two-arm hat is a little more difficult to make - you need to sew a hat from two caps. The material is worth taking multi-colored. Decorate as the first option. In order for the "horns" to retain their shape, they need to fill a little cotton wool or foam.
