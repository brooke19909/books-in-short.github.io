Popular baths in Ozerki

Baths in Ozerki ( St. Petersburg) are ready to offer their guests a full range of bath services. It is here that every client will be able to truly relax, forget about the bustle of the city and restore the body.

The healing properties of the bath

A bath is a great way to improve health and well-being, prolong youth and even get rid of extra pounds.

The bath improves immunity, improves blood circulation, and stimulates the work of the heart. Due to increased sweating, toxins are removed from the body, the pores open and the skin begins to breathe, and after the bath it looks more healthy and elastic. Bath procedures increase emotional and mental stability, help relieve stress and fatigue. If you add useful poultices and herbs to the heat, then you get free inhalation at the time of exacerbation of colds. When using a broom, sick joints, rheumatism, osteochondrosis, arthritis and radiculitis are treated.





All the peoples of the world have long used the bath to get rid of various ailments, and modern medicine only confirms the benefits of bath procedures and recommends them for therapeutic purposes.

In the northern capital of Russia, many companies provide services in the field of bathing business, but baths in Ozerki are considered more popular .

Baths on Suzdal Lakes

Ozerki district is located in a picturesque place on the shore of three lakes, a chain of which is called Suzdal lakes. Now this place is very popular among vacationers. In summer, people come here to swim in the clear water on comfortable beaches or go fishing, and in winter there is an increased demand for bath complexes in this area. The most famous of them are:

  • " Makarov baths" in the Lakes.
  • "Suzdal manor."
  • "Shuvalovsky Compound".
  • ParPalace.
  • FOK "Ozerki".

"Makarov baths"

The complex is located in a forest recreation area, away from the noisy city, in a closed area. For guests' relaxation, a log house is offered in which there is a sauna with soft steam, a white sauna , the only black sauna in the city, the best steam men, restaurant menu, massage.

"Makarov baths" is a place where you can gather a large company in the hall for banquets or arrange a romantic date by the fireplace. The mansion is equipped with a year-round terrace and a summer kitchen, where guests can cook something on their own, there is also a professional chef who can offer a menu of different cuisines of the world.





In addition, the Makarovskaya Banya in Ozerki on the Suzdal Lakes offers a massage room, therapeutic shower-vichy and a street font that does not freeze even in winter.

The complex is located at Bolshaya Ozernaya, building 77.

"Suzdal manor"

A place that is ideal for parties, weddings and corporate parties. For recreation, the complex offers three isolated comfortable houses with their own territory. The cottage is located on the lake, has an outdoor pool, several terraces. On the ground floor there is a Russian steam room, on the second floor there is a pool table and several rooms.





The estate offers services for organizing holidays, a complex of bath procedures, massage and more. Located on Vyborg highway, house 186.

"Shuvalovsky Compound"

Another complex from the list of popular baths in Ozerki, which offers the opportunity to organize a holiday in nature. The compound is located on the street. Bolshaya Ozernaya, building 29/2. Here, three cottages are at guests' service, each has its own steam room with an aqua zone, an equipped relaxation room, a banquet hall. If desired, you can invite an experienced bath attendant or massage therapist, purchase brooms and necessary bath accessories.

ParPalace

This is not just a bath in Ozerki , but a whole SPA complex offering premium services. High-class specialists of Balinese and Thai massage work here, there is a luxurious steam room, hammam, swimming pools and a jacuzzi.





In "ParPalace" often held private parties, lavish celebrations, original show programs. Each zone in the complex has a unique interior and modern equipment. Separate rooms are available for massages, beauty treatments and spas. If you are a fan of VIP-rest, undoubtedly, come to ParPalas on Esenina, 1. It is here that you will be taught to rest in a royal way.

FOK "Ozerki"

The fitness center offers bath relaxation in rooms with different class categories. It offers a cozy cafe, spacious banquet areas and living rooms, swimming pools, hot steam rooms, hammam, experienced attendants, billiards, karaoke, gym and much more. The complex is located in close proximity to the lake on Bolshaya Ozernaya Street, house 84a. In winter there is the opportunity to plunge into the hole.

Suzdal baths in Ozerki are always waiting for guests who wish to be in the world of comfort, serenity and relaxation. The complexes organize the best relaxation in which both body and soul enjoy.
