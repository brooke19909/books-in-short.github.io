Such different birds near Moscow

In Moscow and the Moscow region you can find many species of birds. Neighborhood with the big city is not in vain, but these are their native places. Birds of the Moscow Region are different, and not every local resident suspects their existence.





Plush feathered

There are birds that delight in their beauty. Some are on the verge of extinction.

The white stork is not a small bird, their beak is long, and the feathers are painted white. He builds nests near human housing, and flies to the African continent or towards India for the winter. These birds of the Moscow region are seen in quiet private sectors, where there are small ponds.





The golden eagle is the largest predator in the region, its wingspan reaches 250 cm. It hunts small mammals, but can attack lambs or young deer.

Big Bittern is a medium-sized bird that is under guard. It is listed in the Red Book of the Moscow Region. The plumage color is yellow-black. You can meet her near the swamps, the basis of the diet is fish.

Everyone saw them

The names of the birds of the Moscow Region are not known to everyone. Although they meet these birds often. Here are the most familiar guests of the Moscow region:

  • Raven is a large bird with black feathers and a large beak.
  • Capercaillie - representative of the chicken squad. Sexual dimorphism is pronounced in the color of plumage and body size. Flies, as a rule, no higher than trees. During bubbling, it loses the ability to hear. These birds near Moscow attract hunters.
  • Rook somehow resembles a crow. Almost omnivorous.
  • The thrush is a small inconspicuous bird.
  • The house owl has the appearance of a small owl. Often settled at the dwelling of a person.
  • The yellow wagtail is the tiniest bird of yellow-green color. Her tail is constantly moving.
  • Green woodpecker is a fearful inhabitant of broad-leaved forests with olive-green plumage.
  • Kingfisher is a short-bodied bird with bright plumage and a large beak. It lives near water bodies.

Other birds

Birds of the Moscow region are very different. Each has its own character and special appearance. Sometimes birds become victims of human activity. But it is very important to preserve the population of each species and recognize birds by plumage. Here are the birds that live in the Moscow region:

  • Chaffinch is a small bird with a bright color: a red breast and a green back.
  • The Oriole has yellow-black plumage and flute-like singing.
  • Mallard - the ancestor of domestic duck, is an object of hunting. Sexual dimorphism is developed.
  • Kobchik is a small falcon. Predator hunting small rodents.
  • Corostel has a length of about twenty centimeters. Migratory bird, however, prefers to flee.
  • Gyrfalcon is another falcon species. Hunts small birds.
  • Mute swan - a bird with a white plumage and an orange beak.
  • Coot is an object of black fishing and a white beak. Forehead without plumage.
  • The burial ground is a large predator. It flies and runs well.
  • Moskovka is an inhabitant of coniferous forests, a small titmouse of black color with a white belly.




This is not a complete list of feathered inhabitants of the Moscow region. But each of them has its own unique features. Take a look, maybe you will meet them. Birds of the Moscow Region are very beautiful . Photos of feathered neighbors can be a wonderful decoration for country houses or themed interiors.
