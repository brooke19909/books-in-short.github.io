Vyshny Volochek Hotels: description and reviews

Vyshny Volochyok is a city with a unique history. Thanks to the amazing "water decorations" (an abundance of streams and canals crossing the entire city) it is called "Russian Venice".





In order to see this amazing city, hundreds of tourists come here every year. They are all happy to host the numerous Vyshny Volochyok hotels . Some of them will be discussed in this article.

Hotel "Birch"





The hotel is one of the best in the city. It offers tourists comfortable rooms equipped with necessary furniture. For newlyweds and lovers of comfort there are luxury apartments. Each room has a modern TV and a bathroom with shower. Guests have the opportunity to use ironing facilities and access the Internet. In the hotel there is the possibility of checking into the room with pets. There is no additional charge for their accommodation. Vyshny Volochyok hotels offer not only comfortable living conditions, but also high-quality food.

The hotel has a restaurant and bar. At the request of the guests, it is possible to deliver food directly to the room, as well as prepare diet meals by special order. The hotel has a children's playground, which is convenient for guests who come with children. The hotel also has a tour desk, whose staff will help plan your vacation. For motorists free parking is provided. Shuttle service to and from the hotel is available at an extra charge. For corporate clients there are conference rooms. Gala events are also held at the banquet hall. Among the services include the ability to use office equipment. This is one of the best accommodation options for those who went on vacation to Vyshny Volochek. Hotels here are always welcome new guests.

Posting Rules





Check-in is from 14:00, and check-out is until 12:00. Room reservation conditions depend on their category. Hotel guests can use the individual check-in service. Children under seven years stay free of charge when using existing bedding. Extra beds for children of any age are provided at the rate of five hundred rubles per night. This service is not included in the total price, but is paid separately. Hotel "Birch" (Vyshny Volochek) has repeatedly become the subject of positive reviews from tourists.

Reviews about "Birch"

Most tourists who visit the hotel appreciate the level of comfort. They talk about the skilled work of the staff. The employees are responsive and do their job efficiently. The convenient location of the hotel is also pleasing. Especially memorable are the entertainment programs held at the hotel for any occasion. A varied menu in the hotel restaurant will not leave indifferent even the most fastidious gourmet. Rooms with excellent soundproofing and modern renovation will also please guests. All equipment (air conditioning, TV, refrigerator, plumbing) works perfectly. Visitors also positively evaluate the quality of food and the level of service in the Podberezovik restaurant. The food included in the price, in their opinion, was varied. All dishes were very tasty.

Hotel Baltia





Another option for accommodation is the Baltiya Hotel (Vyshny Volochek). The hotel is distinguished by the atmosphere of home comfort created by the efforts of a highly professional team of employees.

The Noah's Ark restaurant has a varied menu composed of dishes of Russian, Caucasian and Armenian cuisine. In the daytime, a great business lunch awaits guests. By special order, a special diet menu can be prepared. The restaurant is a great place for celebrations. Almost all Vyshny Volochyok hotels can offer their guests wellness treatments.
The hotel offers a sauna and massage services. They also have the opportunity to spend time for the benefit of themselves in the fitness room.

Hit Park Hotel





With special hospitality, the wonderful Hit Park Hotel welcomes guests. The Highest Volochek is worth visiting at least once to see all of its beauty. You can stay in this beautiful hotel. The hotel is located at Bauman street, house 16. The room fund includes double rooms with one or two beds, as well as deluxe and junior suites. All of them are equipped with modern TVs with a set of numerous satellite channels, DVD-players. Settlement is carried out around the clock. For guests there is secure parking and a hairdresser.

Hotel services "Hit Park"

Hotel guests have a wonderful opportunity to spend time on the terrace, in the fresh air. It even has karaoke. You can safely practice singing. Guests can also exercise in the outdoor areas or in the fitness room. The hotel offers guests a variety of sports equipment. Throughout the territory, Internet access is provided. The list of services includes individual check-in and check-out, the possibility of celebrations in the conference hall or banquet hall.

Hit Park reviews

Hotel guests celebrate quality service, excellent cuisine in the restaurant. They definitely would like such hotels to be as many as possible. Guests who leave negative reviews note the overpriced, in their opinion, cost of living in hotel rooms and slow service in the restaurant. At the same time, some visitors say that the hotel restaurant is comparable in level with the best metropolitan institutions. The friendliness of the staff, quick check-in and check-out, quality cleaning in the rooms are also noted.

Park Hotel Laguna





A great place to accommodate guests who come on an excursion to Vyshny Volochek is the Hotel Laguna. The Park Hotel is located on the banks of the Tsna River at 22a Egorova Street and is a three-star hotel for budget holidays, located next to the reservoir and picturesque lakes and forests. The hotel is three hundred kilometers from the capital and four hundred kilometers from St. Petersburg. You can get to it both by car and by train Moscow - St. Petersburg to the station Vyshny Volochek and then by bus to the stop "Ulitsa Severnaya". The number of rooms consists of forty-four rooms of luxury and junior suites and standard. Guests will be pleasantly surprised by the cost of living, because the hotel is the cheapest in Vyshny Volochyok.

The list of services and entertainment for vacationers includes horse riding, a sauna and a small pool, as well as a gym. For guests provides the necessary sports equipment. Three meals a day are provided in the cafe-bar. An undoubted advantage of living in a park hotel is the possibility for guests to enjoy the clean air and admire the chic lake and forest scenery.

Thus, in Vyshny Volochyok, tourism infrastructure has been created and is successfully functioning. People arriving in the city have the opportunity to choose a place to stay in accordance with their tastes and financial capabilities. Vyshny Volochek hotels deserve the most flattering reviews.
