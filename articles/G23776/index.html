Club Insula hv-1 (Turkey, Alanya) - photos, prices and reviews of tourists from Russia

One of the most popular Turkish resorts for many years is Alanya. Residents of the Russian Federation and other CIS countries fell in love with this region for its excellent climate, excellent beaches, warm and clear sea, as well as a wide selection of hotels and a lot of entertainment for every taste.

So, if the destination of your trip to Turkey is Alanya, Club Insula hv-1 will gladly open its doors in front of you. This first-class Turkish hotel is very popular, so it is advisable to reserve rooms in it in advance. We offer today to learn more about what he offers to his guests, and also to find out what impression our compatriots left in this hotel complex.





Where is the "Club Insula hv-1"

Club Insula hv-1 is located near the first coastline of Konakli. The city of Alanya is only 13 kilometers away. The nearest airport is in Antalya and is 110 kilometers away from the hotel. Thus, upon arrival to get to the hotel you will have about one and a half to two hours.

Hotel Rooms

The Club Insula hv-1 room fund consists of 312 rooms, which in total are designed to receive seven hundred guests. Of the total number of rooms, 274 are standard rooms, 36 are family rooms, and two more are rooms for disabled guests.

All rooms in the hotel are stylishly decorated and furnished with comfortable and beautiful furniture. Each room has a private bathroom, as well as a balcony with scenic views of the sea or the well-kept territory of the hotel complex. All rooms are equipped with the necessary amenities for an excellent stay: air conditioning, satellite TV, wireless Internet access, hairdryer, telephone, safe and minibar. Room service is available at an additional cost. The rooms are cleaned daily, bed linen is changed three times a week.

Entertainment

Staying at the Club Insula hv-1 hotel will allow you to have a great vacation not only for beach lovers, but also for people who prefer to swim in the pool. So, on the territory of the hotel complex there are two large swimming pools with clear water, one of which is indoor. Also here you will find a whole water park with a mass of diverse slides and a pool designed for the youngest guests of the Club Insula.

Throughout the day and until late evening in the territory of Club Insula hv-1 tireless animators work. These people love their work and are always ready to bring variety to the rest of the hotel guests. In the evenings, interesting colorful shows and discos await you.

The hotel administration takes care of the smallest guests. So, in addition to a variety of water activities, there is a mini club for children. Also in the territory of "Club Insula" there is a large playground.

Fans of outdoor activities and sports can go to the gym, attend aerobics classes, play volleyball, tennis, basketball. Also on the beach for guests there is equipment for a variety of recreation and sports on the water.

If you want to relax and take care of your beauty, then be sure to visit the luxurious spa located in the Club Insula. It offers a variety of beauty treatments, a massage service, and there is also a sauna, hammam and jacuzzi.





Sea beach

Hotel Club Insula is located on the first coastline and has at its disposal a large and clean beach. Here, vacationers are provided with free sunbeds, umbrellas, mattresses and beach towels. There is a snack bar on the beach where you can not only order a variety of drinks, but also refresh yourself in between main meals.

Infrastructure

The five-star Hotel Insula Club has all the necessary infrastructure for a comfortable stay. So, the reception desk is open around the clock. Therefore, if you check into the hotel at an inopportune hour, or if you have an urgent question, you can always contact the on-duty administrator who will solve all problems.

The hotel has its own spacious parking, the use of which is free for guests. Therefore, if you decide to come to rest in your own car or rent a car already in Turkey, then you will not have problems with parking.

Also in the Club Insula you can use the luggage storage, dry cleaning and laundry services, exchange currency, rent a car, book an excursion that interests you, call a taxi. There is also a whole series of shops where you can buy souvenirs, various things necessary for relaxation and even jewelry. The hotel also has a medical office, where guests can contact in case of illness.

Club Insula also offers services for organizing business meetings, conferences, symposia and other similar events. For these purposes, two conference rooms are equipped here, equipped with all necessary modern equipment.





Cost of living

The price level for accommodation in the rooms of the Club Insula hotel is consistent with the five-star category of the hotel. So, a five-day vacation for two in this hotel complex will cost you an average of $ 1,200.

Reviews - Club Insula hv-1

We bring to your attention the generalized comments of our compatriots who vacationed this summer at the Club Insula Hotel in Alanya. So, most travelers were satisfied with their vacation. So, in their reviews, tourists note the good condition of the hotel rooms and beautiful well-groomed territory. The work of local animators, who prepared new shows every day, made an extremely favorable impression on many people.

As for nutrition, the majority of vacationers were not quite happy with the choice of dishes. In their opinion, a five-star hotel should pay more attention to the variety of menus.

Our compatriots also express their dissatisfaction with the constant lack of sun loungers both near the pool and on the beach. Although this problem is more than relevant for most hotels in Turkey with different stars.

In general, tourists note that the hotel is fully consistent with its value and is suitable for a good vacation.
