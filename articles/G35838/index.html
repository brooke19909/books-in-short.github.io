Where is the residential complex located?

For the construction of the residential complex "Opaliha O3" the city of Krasnogorsk (Moscow region) was chosen. The complex is located 4 kilometers from the Volokolamsk highway, the nearest metro stations are Volokolamskaya and Tushinskaya, and the train station can be easily reached on foot (1 kilometer distance). The building area is surrounded by dense coniferous forest, which has become a kind of filter between the capital and the Moscow region. Its length from north to south is about 10 kilometers. In the Opaliha forest park there is a glacial hollow (East Anikeevskaya), which is of great scientific interest. Together with foresters and environmentalists, the UG developer is conducting a large-scale environmental program to conserve forests, rare species of plants and animals that live here (snow-white water lily, May lily of the valley, etc.).





In the immediate vicinity of the residential complex is the famous Arkhangelskoye State Museum-Estate, where it is pleasant and interesting to walk with the whole family: numerous palaces and pavilions, a church and theater, small tea houses and gazebos. Large jazz festivals are held here every summer, all year round you can come to enjoy art: collections of sculpture and painting, rare books are presented here. There are other estates nearby: Novonikolskoye, Ilyinskoye, Znamenskoye-Gubailovo and others.

Architectural solutions in the residential complex "Opaliha O3"

This complex is built according to the principles of quarterly development, characteristic of the old cities of Europe: all houses here are located around one main street. This picturesque boulevard is ideal for morning jogging or leisurely evening walks.

The main idea, which directly influenced the architectural decisions of the residential complex, is attention and respect for the natural landscape. So, the entire residential complex "Opaliha O3" is characterized by a low height of houses (no more than 8 floors), which is commensurate with the height of the forest. Moreover, the closer the house is located to the forest, the lower the number of storeys. Thanks to this decision, green panoramas are opened to residents of the complex of amazing beauty, and a specially designed format of apartments with panoramic windows and terraces allows you to fully enjoy the view from there.





The houses located directly along the main street of the complex are distinguished by great facade expressiveness and interesting architectural accents. They form a unique architectural panorama. As for the color scheme, for the Opaliha O3 LCD, soft, soft shades with interspersed with saturated colors were selected: dark brown with red, pearl gray with orange, etc. At the same time, color integrity is maintained that fits perfectly into the surrounding natural space.
