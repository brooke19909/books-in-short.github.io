Seychelles: attractions, description of the republic

The paradise of our Earth, which was practically not touched by the hand of man and which is located thousands of kilometers from human civilization, is the Seychelles (attractions are described in our article). In the Seychelles , palm-shaded beaches, unsurpassed diving, snorkeling, areas completely covered by forests in which wild life teems are at your service. Today, the Seychelles archipelago is one of those places that tourists visit more than others.



Attractive island features

Seychelles, whose attractions surprise even the most sophisticated tourists, have unique beaches, a unique flora and underwater world. There are no such beauties anywhere else on the planet. Here you can meet unusual birds and animals.

There is practically no agriculture in the republic, but no industry at all. The local government protects nature, given to him by God, and is very sensitive to this issue. The Republic of Seychelles prohibits any expansion of the tourism business, nor can new hotels be built here. All this is done so that the number of tourists does not increase.

Only in this area there is something that simply fascinates a person - these are magnetizing stones that have lain on the island for many thousands of years. It seems that here they were left by aliens from outer space. Since ancient times, stones have been used by white sorcerers. With their help, they plunged into a trance and predicted the future.

Mahe, La Digue and Praslin are the granite central islands of the republic. All other islands are coral atolls.



The largest island of the Seychelles archipelago

The largest and most visited is the island of Mahe. In the eyes of tourists, dozens of elite resort hotels, tropical impassable forests, beaches with their pristine charm and historical incredibly interesting sights make this area irresistible. The island offers its guests an exciting excursion program.

The capital of the island is the city of Victoria - this is the largest city of Mahe and the smallest capital of the planet. Here at every step are the sights: a copy of Big Ben on the main square of Victoria, the Cathedral of the Immaculate Conception, which is the most important religious attraction of Mahe.

Mahe Island invites art lovers to visit the Kraft Village area. This is such a village of artisans, in which the best craftsmen present their products. Also, travelers should try the Mon Fleury Garden, which has a huge collection of palm trees. Among them there are very rare species of trees.

Tourist Attractions

Seychelles, the sights of which every inhabitant of the Earth wants to see, have some more interesting places that all tourists visiting the republic should see. So, one of such places is the gallery of the famous artist Michael Adams. It is housed in a classic chalet with a small shop and workshop. Here you can buy your favorite canvas, postcards and copyright calendars.

The Royal Spice Garden is another interesting attraction. It was founded in 1772. Today, the estate has a small restaurant serving national cuisine, a museum and a souvenir shop. The museum exposition is dedicated to the life of planters of the XVIII-XIX centuries.

In the northern part of the archipelago you can find the one of a kind nature reserve Seychelles Morne. Tourists walk around the reserve are extremely popular. Rainforests occupy a significant part of the Seychelles Morne, and during the tour you can admire rare species of trees.
