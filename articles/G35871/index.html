Crocheting booties: for beginners needlewomen a simple master class

Warm, soft and comfortable booties, made by yourself, will definitely warm the baby's legs. This first shoe is used from birth, preventing overcooling crumbs. It is comfortable and beautiful, and most importantly - practical.

In this article, we'll talk about crocheting booties for beginners. Our master class is simple, and therefore even an inexperienced needlewoman can cope with the production of shoes. The work is quick and easy. Let's get started!

Crocheting booties (for beginners): workshop

For the manufacture of this openwork product, you need to prepare a hook number 2.5 and yarn in two colors - turquoise and white. We used children's threads "Pekhorka". It is desirable that the yarn consist of wool and acrylic. Crocheting booties for newborns will start with the sole. As a rule, almost all shoes for crumbs are made, starting with the sole. So, we will knit the first three rows according to the following pattern.

First, pick up fifteen air loops (aka VP) and skip three loops. In the fourth, we will perform five double crochets (CH). In the next loop we knit one CH. Repeat the operation nine more times. In the last - we will make six double crochets. Now we turn over our knitting and continue to work according to the scheme, that is, we make ten CH. We complete the row with a connecting column in the last lifting VP. You should get this kind of blank.


We knit the sole of the booties for the newborn

We start the second row with three VPs, and then in the first lifting loop we knit a double crochet. In the next six loops, we perform two CHs, and then at ten - one at a time. Be extremely careful when knitting all the loops. Count their number. In the next six loops, make two CHs. Turn the knitting over and continue with the pattern. Complete the second row with a connecting loop. Here is what you should get (see photo).

Third row: after knitting three lifting loops, perform one double crochet in the first of them, and then another in the next loop of the base. The final stage of work: we knit the "toe" of shoes

Take the white thread and perform the initial loop. Mark the middle of the boot, insert the hook into the loop (behind the back wall of the "bump"). Pull the initial loop.
