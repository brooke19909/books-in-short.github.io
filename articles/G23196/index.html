Visitors reviews

Every year there are many who wish to visit the guest house "Olga" (Lazarevskoye). Reviews of those who were fortunate enough to visit it are usually positive. All of them unanimously note the ideal cleanliness, which the owners (Vladimir and Olga) are trying to maintain in the rooms, in the kitchen and the territory adjacent to the house.



Bed linen is changed periodically, but at least once a week, and towels - every two to three days. Liquid soap, toilet paper and even washing powder are provided free of charge for residents. For those who are used to cleanliness, this is very helpful. Each room has access to a shared balcony with amazing sea views. Hospitable and friendly hosts always warmly welcome guests. Their care and conscientious attitude to their work allows visitors to really relax. Perhaps that is why most of them, after leaving, express a desire to visit this friendly institution again.

"Olga" on Shevchenko street

Oddly enough, but an institution called "Olga" in the village of Lazarevskoye is not the only one. Sometimes this is misleading to some tourists. It turns out there is another guest house, Olga. Lazarevskoye, Shevchenko, 2 - this is the address where tourists can also contact to rent a room for the duration of the vacation. Visitors are offered four types of rooms:

  • double room of standard type;
  • triple room of the same type;
  • penthouse located on two floors;
  • two bedroom apartments with separate kitchen.

The institution is mainly focused on family vacations. The rooms are located in two buildings, which are located on a quiet street, there are no nightclubs or noisy discos nearby.



In addition to accommodation, visitors are additionally offered:

  • organization of excursions;
  • BBQ for personal events;
  • iron and ironing board;
  • Transfer to the airport or train station.

In order not to disturb the rest of the residents, a separate smoking area is allocated on the territory. The hosts do not provide food. But the house has a kitchen where you can cook your own meals. Accommodation in such an institution at the beginning of the season costs from 800 to 2000 rubles per day, depending on the selected room.
