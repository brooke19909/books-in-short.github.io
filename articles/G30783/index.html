Hotel Cronwell Resort Sermilia 5 * (Greece, Halkidiki): overview, description, rooms and reviews

Greece is a very popular country in terms of tourism. Every year 18 million travelers go here - and this is an average figure. Naturally, there are countless hotels here. And with special attention I would like to note the Cronwell Resort Sermilia.





general information

This modern hotel is located in Psakoudia. This is the name of a small but extremely attractive resort on Cape Sithonia. Interestingly, "Psakoudia" is translated as "little pines." And everything is logical, because the resort is all buried in pine groves, acacias, flowers and shrubs. Sithonia itself is located on the territory of the nome of Halkidiki.

The distance to the nearest airport is 1.5-2 hours, as it is located in Thessaloniki. It is about 80 kilometers from Psakoudia.

But in the immediate vicinity of Cronwell Resort Sermilia there is a closed sandy beach where only guests staying in the complex are allowed to enter. And just around the corner from the hotel is a bus stop. You can take a minibus and at no cost to get anywhere - even to Thessaloniki. So the location at the hotel is extremely good. And the distance from the airport is compensated by the few people and comparative silence.

Service and Facilities

At Cronwell Resort Sermilia, the level of service is what it should be in a 5-star hotel. There is everything you need for the comfort of guests. There is free Wi-Fi, private car parking, a shared lounge, and dry cleaning laundry. Also, a shuttle service is provided here. However, to be honest, it is better to rent a car. Traveling around Halkidiki by personal transport is much more convenient and interesting than using public transport.

The hotel also has a 24-hour reception and postal services. There is a conference room with a capacity of 40 people, which is often rented for various events. And there is Bucephalus Hall. All kinds of exhibitions and meetings are regularly held there.

It is also important to note that Cronwell Resort Sermilia has apartments in which everything (from the bathroom to the furniture) is adapted for people with disabilities.

But one of the most important nuances regarding services is the level of staff training. Responsible people work here. And they cannot but rejoice at their knowledge of five languages. After all, the staff speaks German, Russian, Italian, English and Greek. So no one should have communication problems.





Leisure

Cronwell Sermilia Resort 5 * is not only a great place to stay, but also a full-fledged entertainment complex. On a private sandy beach marked with the EU Blue Flag, a team of animators works all day to amuse the guests. In addition, there are sun loungers with umbrellas and a bar.

In general, several animated platforms simultaneously work on the territory of the complex. Exercise, aerobics, dancing, gymnastics, water games - here you can participate in a variety of ventures. And in the afternoon they conduct various master classes. Many especially like to learn cooking. During this master class, guests will be able to learn the secrets of cooking traditional Greek dishes. In the evening, they organize themed holidays and parties. For children organize a mini-disco.

By the way, even guests staying at Cronwell Resort Sermilia 5 * (Halkidiki) will be invited to try themselves as a rider. Here, due to the features of the relief, horse breeding has been flourishing for a long time. If guests have a desire, they can enroll in individual lessons (both theoretical and practical).
