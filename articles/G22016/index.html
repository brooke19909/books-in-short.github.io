Camp Nou Stadium - the pearl of Barcelona

There have been matches with Madrid Real Madrid more than once. Translated, the name of this place means "new field". The stadium, among other things, is one of the ten most capacious sports arenas in the world.

The reason for this was the competition with the football club Real Madrid, because he then, in turn, managed to build the Santiago Bernabeu.
