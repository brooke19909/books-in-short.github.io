Rhodes Island: Holiday Features

Do you know how attractive Greece is? Rhodes Islands - that's what attracts tourists there. There are many islands around Greece, and Rhodes is the fourth largest among them. He "swims" in two seas: from the west - in the Aegean, from the east - in the Mediterranean.

The administrative center is named after the island, it is the most populated city on the island. Many call it the "Mediterranean Pearl." The island of Rhodes is endowed with natural beauty, glorious history, which left magnificent architectural monuments. The historical part of the capital, by decision of UNESCO, was included in the list of world cultural heritage. Rhodes is an ideal holiday destination. It has everything: bays, cliffs and beaches, villages located in green plains and beautiful mountains. All year round the sun and the gentle sea caress here, everything is saturated with greenery and flowers. What else can Rhodes Island offer?

sights

Previously, the island had one miracle of the world left from the ancient world - the Colossus of Rhodes. This is the main asset of the capital was located in the port of Mandraki. Outwardly, he depicted a young man who held a bowl on an arm extended upwards, and a bow hung behind his back. But time has not preserved this statue. In its place, two bronze deers are installed on the columns - this is the emblem of the modern city. But the Colossus of Rhodes is not forgotten, his images are found everywhere, on souvenirs, stamps, engravings. The island of Rhodes will provide an exciting walk through the old city. From all sides it is surrounded by medieval walls. There is the famous Masters Palace, Ionite Knights Street, Ippolins Tower.





Northern part

This coast is considered the center of the tourist area. Restaurants with national and European cuisine, taverns, bars, shows, variety shows, casinos - this is what Greece, Rhodes Island can offer . Hotels that can be chosen for every taste with a high level of service will leave a lasting impression. The ruins of Kamiros ("Pompeii of Rhodes") also attract many archeology buffs.

East island

The legendary city of Lindos, located near the capital, will also surprise. This tour is simply breathtaking. For example, the peak of the rock, on which is located His Majesty the Acropolis, is striking. He preserved the ruins of a temple built in honor of Athena of Lindos.





Sushi west

A trip to the western part of the island will be remembered for "the valley of the butterflies." It is a national park and nature reserve. The trees growing there emit tar, the aroma of which attracts more than a thousand insects from all over Europe. But first of all, it is worth to climb the hill and visit the monastery "Panayia Filerimu". The view from there will open the island in natural beauty. But not only the historical heritage can please Rhodes. Life on it does not stop for a minute. During the day you can sunbathe, visit the mineral springs. There are places for privacy, where in peace they enjoy the silence and beauty of nature. Athletes on the island can play golf, volleyball or tennis. Fans of the water element are offered: scuba diving, windsurfing, racing on boats.
