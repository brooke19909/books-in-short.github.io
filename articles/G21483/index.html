Novia White Lilyum Hotel, Turkey, Kemer: Travel Photos and Reviews

If you plan to spend an excellent vacation in the vicinity of Kemer and are looking for a comfortable inexpensive hotel, then Novia White Lilium 4 * can be an excellent option for living.



Location

This four-star hotel is located in the resort village of Camyuva, seven kilometers from Kemer. The nearest airport is Antalya Airport, 60 km away. Thus, upon arrival, the path to the hotel will take you about one and a half to two hours (since the bus also calls in other hotels). Distance to the sea is 200 meters.

Basic information, photo

The hotel itself was built in 2009. Since 2014, he entered the Novia hotel chain. Own area of ‚Äã‚Äãthe hotel is about 10 thousand square meters. meters. The housing stock is represented by 184 rooms located in a four-story building. Novia White Lilyum Hotel offers its guests to stay in apartments of the following categories: standard (24 square meters, maximum 3 guests), economy (the same standard rooms, but located on the lowest and highest floors), superior rooms (31-36 square meters, accommodation 3 + 1 people.).

Regardless of the type, all rooms are equipped with the necessary furniture, as well as a TV, air conditioning, safe (extra charge), refrigerator, telephone, bathroom with hairdryer. All apartments have a balcony. On the floor - carpet. Wi-Fi access and room service are available at an additional cost. Cleaning is done every day, towels and linen are updated several times a week.

Dining at Novia White Lilium is organized according to the all-inclusive concept. The hotel has a main restaurant, a snack bar and several bars with drinks (including on the beach).

The hotel has its own small pebble beach. Guests can use sun loungers and umbrellas for free.
The hotel has several swimming pools, sports fields, a hammam, sauna, massage room, hairdresser, shop, gym, conference room. Near the reception you can use free Wi-Fi. For children there is a separate pool with slides, a playground, a mini club. A babysitting service is also available (surcharge). Hotel guests are entertained by a team of professional animators throughout the day. In the evenings, spectacular performances are held. Also, animators organize trips to Kemer to a disco for everyone.

Population

In accordance with the internal rules of the Novia White Lilyum Hotel, the accommodation of newly arrived guests in the rooms of the category they booked is carried out after two in the afternoon. As travelers say in their reviews, in the low season you can count on an earlier check-in. However, this does not mean that you will have to get bored for hours at the reception. Cleaning

Our compatriots did not have any particular complaints about this issue. So, according to tourists who lived in Novia White Lilyum (Kemer, Turkey), cleaning was carried out every day and quite qualitatively.
