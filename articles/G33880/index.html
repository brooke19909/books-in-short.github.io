PCT - what is this note? What does PCT mean on Iphone

When we buy any product (whatever it is), as a rule, we rarely look at its labeling. This is normal, because all that interests us is the product itself, its functionality and capabilities.

Even if we buy an iPhone, we are not very interested in what designations can be found on its back cover - we immediately turn on the device, go to the menu, launch applications and so on.





In today's article, we talk about a single marking mark, which is very important for any buyer. We are talking about the PCT sign. What is it and why does the manufacturer put it on the device - more on that later.

Certification procedure

Let's start with general information about why certification of goods is required, what it means. This is a mandatory procedure that is carried out to confirm the quality of products. With it, you can certainly say that a particular product belongs to a certain class of quality if it was certified.

This stage is mandatory according to the legislation of the Russian Federation. For example, a product category such as machinery is subject to certification. Of course, this also applies to iPhones - they must also undergo such a procedure if they are sold legally. And on each of the products that have passed it successfully, a symbol is placed indicating that this PCT phone corresponds. What this means, read on.





Electronics Certification

The procedure is carried out by a state body authorized by law. It is called RosTest. It is this reduction that contains the icon that is applied to your iPhone. The PCT (what this means, you already know) indicates the possibility of using the model in the domestic market. Thus, specialists guarantee that the phone is safe, properly adapted and configured for our conditions. In addition, an iPhone with such a symbol will last longer, and this is again guaranteed by RosTest.





What does this sign mean on the iPhone?

The meaning of the logo that we describe in the article, you know. It indicates the device has passed certification. But you must also understand that there are a number of other points that affect the phone with and without a sign.

For example, the price. The cost of certified products is 20-35 percent higher than that of a simple device imported illegally. This is because the procedure itself costs money and takes time. Consequently, the distributor (shops) transfers these and other costs to the buyer. He pays for the PCT symbol. That this is the right to choose (pay more and be sure or save but take risks) is clear to everyone. Products that are certified will be precisely adapted to work in our market, have high quality and function in the networks of mobile operators in the Russian Federation. For this we pay more. Of course, with phones without a PCT (what kind of models it is, it does not matter), no one can guarantee this.
