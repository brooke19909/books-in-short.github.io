How to make a Christmas tree from corrugated paper with your own hands: step-by-step instructions, a master class and ideas

You can decorate the apartment for the New Year using the simplest materials. We bring to your attention a selection of the best ideas and workshops on creating interior Christmas trees from corrugated paper. It will take you very little time to create such crafts, and finished souvenirs will be an excellent interior decoration or gifts for loved ones. How to make a Christmas tree from corrugated paper with your own hands? We offer you some interesting ideas.

What materials will be needed for crafts?



Before embarking on creativity, make sure you have everything you need at your fingertips. To make this craft, you will need cardboard and corrugated paper, glue, adhesive tape, a stapler with staples, as well as some decorative elements for decoration. These can be beads of various colors and sizes, sequins, pieces of tinsel and rain. You can try to dress up the Christmas tree with something non-standard: specially made for her mini-toys, ribbons, curly buttons. The main material is corrugated paper. It is sold in office supply stores. It can be replaced with paper napkins for table setting or suitable packaging from sweets. When all the materials are collected, the main question arises: how to make a Christmas tree from corrugated paper?

Ideas for children's creativity



If you have very little time, or you decide to make an original craft together with the children, we offer you a master class on creating a simple Christmas tree:

  • Make a cardboard cone of the required size.
  • Wrap it in green corrugated paper and lock it. Already at this stage, the Christmas tree can be decorated with garlands - vertically glue several strips of shiny ribbon or colored paper, one of them will help to hide the connection of the sheet covering the base.
  • You can make the Christmas tree more interesting if you add several levels of branches to it. To do this, cut off the strips of corrugated paper used to decorate the base. On one side of the resulting workpiece, make incisions, turning the strip into a fringe. Now glue this element to the cone so that it girdles the tree horizontally. Make several of these tiers, after which you can begin to decorate the product.

An interesting idea: the tree will turn out very fluffy if you glue it with horizontal strips of paper fringe over the entire height.

Paper pompom item

We bring to your attention another interesting way how to make a Christmas tree from corrugated paper:

  1. Make a blank of cardboard in the form of a cone in the same way as for the previous craft.
  2. Cut corrugated paper into strips 2 cm wide and 20 cm long. Carefully stretch one edge of each paper tape.
  3. From the stripes we form pom-poms. To create them, the tape must be folded with an accordion and fixed closer to one edge. Collect a lot of these pomponoms or flowers.

How to make a Christmas tree from corrugated paper in this technique? Everything is very simple: glue the blank cone with finished pompons. Place the corrugated paper elements tightly together so that the base material does not stick out. The finished product does not need additional decorations, but you can make it even more beautiful if you randomly paste beads, rhinestones or bows made of bright ribbons.

Flower tree



A very beautiful Christmas tree can be made of paper roses. Do not know how to create such flowers, how to make a Christmas tree from corrugated paper? Step by step we describe the process of creating crafts specifically for you:

  • From corrugated paper, cut tapes 4 cm wide.
  • Fold it in half, start twisting the strip, pulling the edges slightly outward, forming a rose.
  • A flower will turn out prettier if individual petals are pulled randomly, rather than in even layers. Roses need to be done quite a lot.
  • Make a cardboard cone separately and glue it with flower blanks.

Especially original such Christmas trees look if you make them out of bright paper. Try to make a blue or red tree. Such a Christmas tree does not require additional decorations, but you can always add a few sparkles or some small decorative elements.

"Petal" technique



Corrugated paper is a material that is easy to model, giving it the most unusual shapes. We offer another interesting idea for creativity, which tells us how to make a Christmas tree from corrugated paper.

So, we will collect the New Year tree from separate small petals. Let's start, as always, with their manufacture. To do this, cut the paper into strips 10 centimeters long and 2.5 cm wide. We pick up one blank and twist it in the middle. Fold the blank in half. You should get a leaf with a sharp tip. We begin to paste the cardboard cone with such elements from below. It is most convenient to mount the petals on the tape. Start the second row by staggering the blanks. In this way, decorate the whole Christmas tree. A cardboard star or a large bow can be attached to the top of such a tree.

Sweet tree



Candy gifts are incredibly popular today. In fact, making a Christmas tree yourself from corrugated paper and pastry is not difficult. Choose sweets in shiny wrappers similar to Christmas toys. As a basis for the craft, make a cardboard cone, as in all the options described above. Wrap it in green paper, cut strips from it. An interesting Christmas tree will turn out if you alternate paper ribbons and rows of confectionery products, gradually filling out the whole craft.

You can make a Christmas tree from corrugated paper and sweets in any technique described above. Use sweets as a decoration or place them on the tree itself, diluting the rows of paper elements.
