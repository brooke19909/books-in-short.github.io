Multicooker power: which one to choose?

You are faced with the choice of such an indispensable and multifunctional device as a multicooker, and do not know which one to choose? What is a multicooker and why is it needed?

The popularity of using multicookers in the kitchen is growing every year. This is facilitated by the relatively affordable price of the device, depending on the functionality, as well as the ability to prepare tasty dishes without significant time costs. In addition, this device can easily be taken with you when traveling, and then cooking on vacation does not take much time. Its advantages are ease of use, compactness, low weight and a diverse set of operations for cooking delicious dishes.

A slow cooker is a mix of several household appliances at once: a pressure cooker, a bread machine, a yogurt maker, a cooker, a double boiler and others. Its options greatly facilitate the cooking process. Depending on the choice of device, it can have not only different cooking functions, but also power. Ask what the power of the multicooker influences. First of all, the speed of cooking, as well as the cost of electricity. This must be considered before buying. Let's consider what other functions this device has.

Main characteristics of the multicooker

Before deciding how much power the multicooker should be, it is worth familiarizing yourself with the other functions that the hostess will need.

The main characteristics of the device:

  • The volume of the bowl.

It varies from 2.5 liters to 5 liters. It all depends on the composition of the family, that is, how many people will prepare the food. For a family of 2-3 people, the smallest bowl size is enough, which will significantly affect the cost of the device. You can not determine the size of the bowl, then take the average (3.5-4.5 liters). If guests suddenly arrive, you can cook something quickly.

Most often, the internal coating of the device has a non-stick property. It has several layers, it is convenient to wash them, and food will not burn. Although conventional coating is also common, which reduces the cost of the product. It is also easy and convenient to use.

Most often, the case is made of plastic or metal. Metal multicookers are durable and quality, while plastic counterparts do not heat up during cooking.

  • Power.

There is no such thing as a better multicooker power. It all depends on the needs of the family. On average, it ranges from 600 to 800 watts, but can be from 200 to 2000 watts. The more powerful the multicooker, the faster the cooking process. In this case, the appliance is called a pressure cooker.   • Size and weight.

The more compact and lighter the device, the easier it is to transport if necessary, and also takes up less space in the kitchen. Its weight and size depends primarily on the volume of the bowl, as well as the availability of functions.

  • Manufacturers

It can be a device of both domestic and foreign production. Russian devices are much cheaper, regardless of functions and other indicators. For the most part, crock-pots are not difficult to maintain. You just need to properly care for the bowl and choose the right cooking function.

Power of multicookers - which one to choose?

For appliances that help significantly reduce the time spent in the kitchen, power is an important feature. But to think that the higher this indicator, the faster the cooking process, is not entirely correct.

By choosing a device with less power, you can reduce energy consumption, while this will affect the cooking time slightly. In addition, the cork will not be knocked out, since in most apartments there are too many electrical appliances.

The high power consumption of the multicooker can be important for a large family. Especially when there is no time for cooking. Also to those who are constantly on the move. Here the power should be high, and the device itself - with the function of a pressure cooker. In this case, the porridge will be ready during the time a person takes a shower, which greatly simplifies and speeds up the process. On average, this indicator varies from 500 watts to 1,500 watts.

An ideal option for a family of 3-4 people will be the selected device with power from 700 watts to 900 watts. Such devices are quite roomy, they can have a sufficient number of programs and the cooking speed is good. In addition, such a device will not hit the family budget much.

Multi Cooker Selection Criteria

Having a little understanding of the functions and the required power of the device, we proceed to its final selection.

What to look for when choosing a multicooker?

  • Size - depends on the composition of the family, that is, on how many people will regularly cook food. Also, this indicator should be considered when eating. For example, if you prefer to pamper your home with culinary masterpieces, you should choose a larger device with more functions that will affect the price.
  • Manual mode - this allows you to cook even dishes for which the appliance is not designed. Do you like to experiment with food? Then this function must be present in the selected device.
  • We pay attention to the components - they must be of high quality. Not only the quality of the device, but also the service life depends on this. The spoon and spatula must be made of wood or silicone, so that the surface of the bowl is not damaged.
  • Appearance - a crock-pot can be of a very different design and, if necessary, fit into the overall interior of the kitchen (everything here depends on personal preferences).
  • Safety is an important indicator. Before buying, inspect the cable, it should not move away from the device.

How to care for the device?

Regardless of how much power the multicooker has, the rules for caring for appliances are almost the same.

Basic rules for caring for the device:

  • Do not wash the bowl in the dishwasher. This can damage the coating, which will affect the quality of the cooking.
  • The first valve must be rinsed regularly.
  • Use only silicone and wooden spoons for the bowl. They are safe and cannot damage the coating.
  • If there is a need to wipe the dust on the outside of the appliance, use a damp cloth, but make sure that the multicooker is unplugged.
