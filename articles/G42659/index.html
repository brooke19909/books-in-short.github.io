The list of Alfa-Bank ATMs in Tula

Alfa-Bank is one of the largest commercial banks. There are several offices in the city of Tula, and for the convenience of customers, more than 10 ATMs are installed in stores, credit and cash offices of the bank and other organizations. Some terminals operate without interruption.

Installed in KCO

Alfa-Bank ATM in Tula is located in the Tula-Oruzheyny credit and cash office at the following address: 25 Krasnoarmeysky Avenue. The terminal operates around the clock. What services are available? Clients can withdraw cash in rubles, as well as deposit funds in several currencies to the card balance.

Alfa-Bank ATM in Tula with the function of accepting cash has been installed at the Yasnaya Polyana credit and cash department. In addition to making cash, it is also possible to make payments, make transfers, and also withdraw funds.

The address of Alfa-Bank ATM in Tula with serial number 401630, established in KCO Yasnaya Polyana: 32 Krasnoarmeysky Avenue. Clients can use the following services:

  • Account replenishment by number without the need to use a card.
  • Repayment of a loan.
  • Transfer between accounts.
  • Reception of bills in batches.
  • Use of cards of the Alpha-Cash line.

It is worth noting that in this terminal, customers can withdraw cash not only in rubles, but also in euros. Depositing is allowed in rubles, euros and dollars.

Installed in stores

Alfa-Bank ATM in Tula is also installed in the Pyaterochka store, located on Kaluga Highway. The exact address of the location: Kaluzhskoe highway, house 2. The terminal is available daily from 8 am to 23 hours. Of the available services - withdrawals in rubles. Alfa-Bank ATM in Tula is also located in the Pyaterochka store at 1 Demonstration Street, letter A. The banking device is open from 8 a.m. to 11 p.m. daily.

Alfa-Bank ATM in Tula was also installed in the Spar supermarket at 7, Maksim Gorky Street, Building A. Schedule: from 9 a.m. to 9 p.m. daily. There is a withdrawal in rubles, depositing funds in several currencies, both in national and foreign.

Installed in the shopping center

Alfa-Bank ATM is also installed on the 1st floor of the Demidovsky shopping center. Location address: 62 Metallurgov Street. The terminal is assigned serial number 501216. The schedule for the banking self-service device is daily from 10 a.m. to 8 p.m.

One of the bank's terminals is installed on the territory of the Maxi shopping center. Location address: Proletarskaya street, house 2. Schedule: daily from 10 am to 9 pm. Available both cash and cash. It is worth considering that the deposit is available both in dollars, rubles, and in euros. Withdrawals are in rubles. Clients can also transfer money to another client of the bank, pay utility bills or replenish the balance of mobile without commission.

A self-service device is also installed on the territory of the Rio shopping center at 22 Proletarskaya Street, Building A. It operates daily from 10 a.m. to 10 p.m.

Work around the clock

Alfa-Bank ATM in Tula with the function of accepting cash was installed at the entrance to the Spar supermarket at Lenin Avenue, 122. Of the available services: withdrawing funds in rubles and dollars, depositing funds in rubles, dollars and euros. A self-service banking device is available around the clock. The terminal is installed in the supermarket "Spar" at the address: Odoevskoye Shosse, 98. It operates 24/7.

The terminal operates under round-the-clock mode under serial number 502726, installed on the territory of the Kirovsky shopping center. Location address: 13 Kutuzova street.

It is worth noting that Alfa Bank has agreements with other banks, for example, Rosselkhozbank. Cash withdrawals and deposits at ATMs of partner banks are carried out without commission. A full list of partner banks is published on the bank's website. There you can also find a complete list of ATM addresses of these banks.

It is important to consider that the operating schedule of a terminal can change during the holidays. Also, the terminals installed in shopping centers operate according to the work schedule of this shopping center. To get additional information about the operation of a particular terminal, you must contact any bank branch. Addresses can be found on the official website of Alfa Bank in the "Branches" section.
