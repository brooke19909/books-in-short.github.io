Just 10 minutes drive from the center of Kazan to the Vesna residential complex - a new one that attracts the attention of hundreds of potential property buyers in the city. Living in an apartment located in the residential complex "Vesna" (Kazan), you will feel easy, comfortable and at ease.

Building and project features

Modern, built according to the latest construction technologies, Vesna residential complex is located in the Soviet district of Kazan and consists of two multi-section buildings.





House No. 1 is a 10-storey, 15-section building, with 529 apartments, of which 259 are 1-room, 192 are 2-room, 78 are 3-room. Commercial premises will be placed on the ground floor of the building, a place for offices will be allocated.

House No. 2 is a 19-story building with 198 residential apartments, of which 108 are 1-room, 72 are 2-room, and 18 are 3-room.

Each of the houses has a private parking lot for 258 places each. According to the development plan, it is planned to ennoble and green the territory of the residential complex.

An apartment in Kazan in the residential complex "Spring" will cost from 47,500 to 58,000 rubles per square meter.
