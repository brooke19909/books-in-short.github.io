DIY pineapple made of sweets. Master class with step by step photos



Isn't it nice to give original and beautiful things made by yourself ?! Such presentations always make a strong impression. If you want to surprise and please a loved one - make an exclusive craft in the now fashionable technique called "sweet design". It involves the manufacture of gifts, bouquets and interesting decor elements from confectionery. In this article, we will share several completely simple master classes on creating a beautiful "sweet" craft - "Pineapple made of sweets." With our own hands, we offer you to make an original product that can become an excellent gift for any occasion. Made with love, it will surely please the person who is gifted. It is also important that any master who does not even have specific skills and who first works in the technique of "sweet design" will be able to perform such a "fruit". You will need to stock up on a good mood and prepare all the necessary working materials.

Making pineapple from sweets. Workshop with step by step photos

Next, let's talk about how to make pineapple from sweets and a bottle of champagne. This original and very beautiful craft can be performed for any holiday. It will be an excellent gift for both man and woman. To make a sweet present you will need a bottle of sparkling wine, a yellow organza, a glue gun, a decorative ribbon (aspidistra leaf). The last "ingredient" can be bought at any flower shop. You will also need sweets (about 700 g). We used delicious chocolates called Golden Lily. They have a rounded shape, so convenient for work, and a bright golden wrap. Pineapple from Golden Lily candies looks very beautiful and presentable. Although you can pick up any other sweets, the main thing is that they be round.

DIY technology

First, prepare the organza - cut it into small squares (6 cm * 6 cm). Next, proceed to the preparation of sweets. Gently bend the "tails" of the wrapper and glue them with a hot-melt gun. The result is a neat rounded billet, without protruding "parts". In our case, it took 75 candies to make a bottle of champagne look beautiful and elegant. We continue to make pineapple from sweets as follows: apply glue to the candy tails, attach the sweetness to a piece of organza and immediately glue the candy to the bottle. You need to make champagne in rows, moving from the bottom (from the bottom) up.



Remember that sweets should be glued in a checkerboard pattern, avoiding gaps and stacking them evenly. At the first row of sweets, it is necessary to tuck the lower corners of the organza and fix them with a hot glue gun. This is necessary so that the champagne is stable. By analogy with the first, we draw up six more rows.

Making leaves for our pineapple

After the entire surface of the bottle is decorated with sweets, you can proceed to decorate the crafts. In order to make leaves, take a decorative ribbon that simulates a leaf of an aspidistra. If you cannot purchase it, use colored paper. Cut from the tape a few wide leaves with pointed tips. We make them of different sizes to make the pineapple look more natural. Now glue the leaves to the bottle. That's all, we got a chic candy pineapple! The step-by-step photo presented in the article demonstrates the manufacturing technology of this magnificent craft. If you wish, you can decorate your craft with sparkles, beads and any other decorative elements.

Another simple workshop is pineapple from a plastic bottle.



In order to complete this interesting craft, you will need some supplies and tools, including:

  • plastic bottle in the form of a barrel with a capacity of 1 liter;
  • Double-sided tape;
  • bead wire;
  • screwdriver;
  • chocolate wrappers in a yellow wrapper - 700 g.

How to make a beautiful present - pineapple from sweets? The instruction is as follows: first we prepare a plastic bottle - wash it well, remove the labels and let it dry. Tip: if you can‚Äôt find a barrel-shaped plastic container for sale, you can use a regular two-liter container for lemonade. But it will need to be prepared in advance, that is, to give the shape of a pineapple. It is not difficult to do this: with a sharp knife, cut the container into three parts. Put the middle aside - we will not use it. But leave the top and bottom. Align them by tucking the top with the neck into the bottom. You should get the necessary form.

We attach sweets to a plastic container



After the preparatory work, we proceed to decorating: we paste over the bottom of the container with double-sided tape. We take five sweets and fasten them into a ring, wrapping the tails with bead wire. Now we fix the sweets at the bottom of the bottle. We close the hole in the center with two more candies. The bottom of the pineapple is ready, go to the design of the sides. Glue a strip of adhesive tape in a circle. We take eight candies and fasten them with each other with wire, without having to form a ring. Glue the sweets on a strip of scotch tape, pressing them denser to the front row. By analogy, we draw up the entire plastic bottle to the very neck. Each row can have a different amount of sweets - vary it as you wish. Remember that sweets must be glued to the packaging in a checkerboard pattern. DIY pineapple made of sweets is almost done.

We continue to perform the "sweet" crafts





After filling out the entire bottle, you need to take an awl or a screwdriver and fill in the "tails" of sweets inside, closing the "gaps". Next, proceed to the manufacture of foliage of our fruit. We take green paper and cut leaves from it. It is advisable to make them different in size. How to make pineapple from sweets? On double-sided tape, glue the upper two leaves inside the neck, and the rest on the outside. Fully cover the neck of the bottle with leaves. The remaining free space between leaves and sweets can be decorated with a sisal or twine winding. If desired, decorate the pineapple with a beautiful bow made of silver fabric. Ready-made crafts can be issued in a beautiful transparent package. Such an exclusive gift is sure to appeal to anyone!
