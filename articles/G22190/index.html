Correct connection - how to connect a laptop to a TV

In general, if for some reason you want to know how to connect a laptop to a TV, this article will help in this matter.

Exit availability



Scart

The first thing you need to figure out is the availability of the necessary connectors on both the laptop and the TV. Therefore, before wondering how to connect a laptop to a TV, you need to check for the following outputs: VGA, HDMI or DVi. The presence of one of them already gives certain chances for the laptop to be connected to the TV. The TV must also have one of these connectors, or the SCART output. It is universal, that is, with the help of the necessary adapter, you can connect almost any device that supports this function to the TV. SCART is present on almost all TVs: new and outdated, digital and conventional. All you need to do is to get yourself the appropriate adapter, for example, VGA-SCART (the first name of the input depends on the connector on the laptop).

RCA



I think this type of connector does not need to be presented. This is the oldest way to connect devices to the TV, nicknamed the ‚Äútulip‚Äù. If you are thinking how to connect a laptop to an old TV, then this option is one of the solutions to this issue. Yes, such a connector is sometimes found on laptops. This option is now considered quite outdated.

Vga

A well-established and most common way to connect devices to a TV. In this way, you can transfer the picture from the laptop to the TV (well, the PC can also be connected in this way). This output is used to connect a laptop video card (personal computer) to the TV. A distinctive feature is that it is not possible to transmit sound. To receive audio, you must use other outputs.

DVI



The characteristics are not far from VGA, but still the outputs differ. On laptops is extremely rare, much more often it can be found on a PC. When considering how to connect a laptop to a TV using a DVI connector, you must be guided by the presence of an adapter, for example, DVI-BGA or DVI-SCART. Such a connector does not transmit sound, which makes the issue of acquiring additional adapters relevant.

HDMI

The most modern and effective way to connect the "books" to the TV. Very convenient and common outlet. You can talk about how to connect a laptop to a TV using HDMI in two sentences. All you need to do is to purchase (if one is not available) an HDMI cable and connect the corresponding outputs on the laptop and TV to them. Then, in the TV settings, set the image output mode to HDMI output. This option transmits both a picture and sound, and even in high quality. With it, you can even watch videos in 3D!

In general, answering the question of how to connect a laptop to a TV, you can say with almost 100% probability that any laptop can be connected to any TV. Of course, it happens that a very rare model comes across both a laptop and a TV, which are simply impossible to combine. But this happens extremely rarely.
