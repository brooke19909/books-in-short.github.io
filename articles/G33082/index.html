Zakopane, Poland. Tours to Poland. Zakopane - ski resort

One of the most beautiful, modern and affordable ski resorts in Europe is considered Zakopane. Poland is a beautiful country that boasts high mountains, deep forests, clear lakes, fast rivers, medieval castles and many other cultural and historical sights. There are all conditions for both relaxing and active recreation. Clean, healthy air, impeccable ski slopes, picturesque landscapes - all this attracts thousands of tourists to Zakopane. Ski lovers come here every year to enjoy great skiing .

Weather

Climatic conditions allow you to relax in Zakopane year-round. Of course, the largest influx of tourists here is observed in the winter, because this is primarily a ski resort. From May to autumn, travelers come to go on mountain trips. In the summer in the south of Poland it is sunny and warm, so the guests of the country have a rest near the lake, do climbing, walk or ride the bicycles to the mountains. In autumn, in most cases, warm and dry weather in Zakopane remains. Poland opens the ski season in December.

Ski slopes in Zakopane



At an altitude of 860 m to 2000 m, 6 ski areas are equipped according to European standards. The first of them is called Kasprowy Top - it is the largest. It is suitable mainly for experienced athletes, has two descent valleys. Kasprowy Upper is located on the territory of the Tatra National Park, so while the lift will lift 2000 m in height, tourists will have something to see. Ski resorts in Poland are striking in their thoughtfulness, organization. A striking example is the most popular ski area in Zakopane Gubalowka. It is suitable for both experienced athletes and beginners. The funicular is located in the city center, so getting to the tracks is not difficult.

Shimoshkovaya glade is equipped on the ridge Gulabuvki. The beginning of the descent of the route begins at an altitude of 840 m, the zone is fenced with nets, the slope is almost 2 km long. Suitable for beginners, ski masters and snowboarders.

Nosal Zone is located on the mountain of the same name. The slope is small, only 650 m, but the road is steep and difficult, therefore it is suitable only for experienced athletes.

Kozinets, Buturovsky Top, Parduluvka are suitable for both masters and beginners. There are high slopes and a yoke.

Wellness in Zakopane

People come here not only to relax, but also to improve their body. Zakopane is a ski resort that is rich in invigorating air, clean water, thermal springs, green areas. Nature itself promotes purification and healing. The city has a powerful medical base, not inferior to the most famous resorts in Europe. A mild climate, coniferous forests, clean water - all this is suitable for patients with tuberculosis and anemia. In Zakopane, people with allergies and respiratory problems can be cured. Residents of big cities come to the resort to improve their health, relax, relieve stress, and restore active processes in the body.

The main attractions

Rest in Zakopane involves not only a daily descent from the mountains, but also an examination of local beauties, historical and cultural monuments of the country. To get acquainted with the traditions and customs of Poles, you should visit the Tatra Museum, where a lot of exhibits are stored. Not far from the city is the village of Chocholow, where a little more than 1000 people live. The peculiarity of the village is that it consists of wooden upland huts that were built in the 19th century.



Zakopane resort has truly picturesque corners, one of such places is the Marine Eye reserve. This is the name of the huge lake located in the Tatra Mountains. A horse-drawn carriage brings visitors to the pond. The sea eye of anyone will conquer with its beauty: a transparent turquoise surface stretches below, and proud and impregnable mountain peaks, covered with snow caps, rise above. Near the lake, vacationers sunbathe, fish, have picnics and just relax.

Not far from Zakopane is the temple of Our Lady of Fatima, built in honor of Pope John II. Visitors stroll through the territory, examine the sculptures, admire the altar and decoration of the building. The atmosphere of the temple inspires reflection, calms and pacifies. It offers breathtaking mountain views. Zakopane is rich in thermal springs, ancient churches, knight's castles.





Many will be interested to see a museum dedicated to the Polish composer Karol Szymanowski. He spent 6 years at Atma Villa, where his personal belongings and furnishings were preserved. Visitors get the impression that Shimanovsky still lives there. Fans of avant-garde should visit the theater named after Vitkevich. He is visited by many famous people, cultural figures. A lot of unique historical places are in Zakopane. Poland is a mysterious country, opening the veil of secrets only to the most curious travelers.

Entertainment

What to do in Zakopane? Here you can relax, improve your health and, of course, have fun. Poland's ski resorts are famous for their developed infrastructure, so no one will be bored for sure. Cycling, ice rink, excursions, hang glider, swimming pool, hiking, trips to the lake - this is not a complete list of entertainment that Zakopane offers.





In the Antuluvka zone, there is a water park - this is a favorite vacation spot for children and adults. It is interesting in that one of the walls is completely glass, which provides a complete overview of local beauties. The water park has slides of varying complexity, there is also a Finnish sauna, a jacuzzi, a salt room, a steam room, a pool with hydromassage. The water here is thermal, so visitors will not only have a good time, but also get better.

The resort has a lot of restaurants, cafes, bars, no one will remain hungry. They are fed with tasty and satisfying dishes, so after a hearty dinner it is sometimes difficult to force yourself to go on an excursion or ski down from the mountain top. There are 13 entertainment centers, 18 night clubs in Zakopane, everyone can find their own music: DJs play songs for both young people and the older generation. Well, of course, do not forget about excursions. The most popular is considered "on the coolers" - a sleigh ride. Excursions to the valleys are mysterious and mysterious, because there are a lot of caves. It is also worth visiting the Peninsky Park to enjoy the beauty of the local nature, to swim on rafts past the canyons. Entertainment in Zakopane is enough for everyone.

When is it better to come to the resort?

Rest in Zakopane is good at any time of the year. In winter, lovers of skiing come here to have a great New Year's vacation. Spring, summer and autumn are great times for hiking in the mountains, when you can rent bicycles and arrange bike rides around the city. The warm season is good for exploring local attractions, visiting picturesque places such as forests and lakes. Therefore, when to go to Zakopane, everyone should decide for themselves, based on their own preferences.
