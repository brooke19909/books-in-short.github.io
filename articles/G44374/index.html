What attracts Berdyansk? Zoo city: description

The article will be interesting to those who are going to visit Berdyansk. The zoo in this city is very nice and interesting. Created by Igor Kalchenko. In the territory of the private zoo there is a lot of space for parents and children to walk. Once in the wonderful world of exotic animals and plants, walking along the paths of the zoo, you will feel like real discoverers who show the world of wild animals in Africa, Asia and America.





This park is a unique place. Here a large number of various animals are collected, both domestic and wild. Everyone who visits this place will be able to find the beast to their temperament.

Zoo: description

This is the very first zoo in Berdyansk (a photo of it is presented above), which was built in Ukraine during its independence. Initially, it was located on a mountainside in a small area. For residents of the city the appearance of the zoo was a real surprise. Indeed, only in this place it was possible to see more than thirty species of animals that are listed in the International Red Book and in the Red Book of Ukraine.





Also here it was possible to get acquainted in detail with the collection (consisting of 130 copies) of exotic animals. In 2004, the Safari Zoo (Berdyansk) was visited by more than fifty thousand people. Much attention in this place is paid to the promotion of environmental and environmental issues.

A real celebration for the guests of Berdyansk and its residents was Open Day. On campaign days, people have the opportunity to watch animals for free.
From the very beginning, the staff of this park began work on reproducing endangered animals and preserving the gene pool.





During the existence of this zoo, six babies of the Far Eastern leopard were born, although in the world there are only forty individuals of this species. Far Eastern leopards are born only in Canada and the Berdyansk Zoo.

In the period from 2005 to the present, six lion cubs were born. The birth of white cubs caused a sensation. Lion cubs were born in October 2008. White lions are unique animals, and not only for menageries of the whole world, but also for wildlife. There are no more than seventy individuals on Earth in total. White lions were never born on the territory of the former USSR .

Four Ussuri tiger cubs were born between 2007 and 2008. This species is listed in the International Red Book. There are no more than two hundred Ussuri tigers left in the world.





Also born in the zoo were ten nosuhs, twenty raccoon raccoons, four monkeys, two camel, European doe, four porcupines, four Himalayan teddy bears and a llama.

All these kids, of course, delighted those who came to Berdyansk, visited the zoo. Also, these data allow us to say that favorable conditions were created in this place for the birth of animals and their lives.

New Safari

In 2009, a new zoo opened. It created comfortable living conditions for animals and birds. Spacious enclosures and cages were installed. Also in the zoo there are artificial ponds with islands of sand. On the territory of "Safari" there are green spaces.

Rare and exotic shrubs and trees are represented here. Plants such as mountain lavender, blue juniper, alpine cloves and Canadian spruce can be seen at the zoo . There is also a wonderful rose garden where twenty kinds of roses are represented.

The new park presents new exhibits. Also, scientific, cultural and educational work is being carried out here. There is also the Zoological Garden Junior Club. There are more than forty kids involved. In the future, it is planned to expand the club to one hundred and fifty people. The management also plans to make additional special sections.





The creation of the zoo has great social significance, its work is aimed at developing the spirituality of the people and supporting the image of the country. Note that for children with disabilities and children from the boarding school the doors are always open in this zoo.

The existence of this safari park is known in more than twenty countries of the world.

Zoo (Berdyansk): opening hours, ticket price

It works seven days a week - from nine in the morning until eight in the evening. The cost of an adult ticket is 150 hryvnias (about 375 rubles). Children's price - 100 hryvnias (250 rubles). A child under three years old can visit the Safari for free. Sometimes the zoo holds promotions, then tickets are cheaper. For large groups discounts are possible. They should be found out from the Safari administration.

Zoo (Berdyansk): address. How can I get to?

The menagerie is located at: Berdyansk city, Zhemchuzhnaya street, 5. How to get to the place? You can get on minibuses No. 17, 15. You need to get off at the stop of the Krasnaya Gvozdika recreation center.





You can also take the minibus number 5/2 to the stop "Zoo". You can also get by private car or taxi.

Visitors reviews

People who visited Berdyansk, the zoo, were satisfied. They claim that the menagerie is very good. The animals here are beautiful, clean and well maintained.





In this place you will not meet thin animals. Park guests like to feed animals. You can buy goodies at the entrance. The cost of one bag of food is about ten to fifteen hryvnia (25-40 rubles). Although there is more expensive food for animals. As visitors say, kids are very pleased to feed animals.

Little conclusion

Now you know why Berdyansk is interesting. The zoo in this city is truly beautiful. It must be seen by every person. Kids will be especially interested here. Adults, too, are not bored here.
