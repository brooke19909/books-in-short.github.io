Airplane model A319: scheme of the cabin and its variants

Airbus A319 is a passenger airliner designed for mid-range flights. This aircraft belongs to the A320 family, which also includes A318, A320, A321. The Airbus A319 aircraft differs from its ‚Äúbrothers‚Äù in the cabin layout. By reducing the length by 4 meters, the number of rows was reduced by 2. Nevertheless, with a sufficiently wide fuselage, among the other narrow-body aircraft, the Airbus A319 is adapted to install wide seats for passengers or to increase the width of the aisle between the rows.





Initially, in 1990, Airbus SAS conducted research on the manufacture of an airliner based on an Airbus A320 aircraft with a shortened fuselage. The construction program was signed by early 1992 amid two competitors - the Boeing 737-300, as well as the Boeing 737-500. The first flight of the A319 was made in 1995; in April 1996, Swiss Airlines received the first purchased model.

Production of the A319 does not end to this day, as the demand for this model does not fall. The most important reason for success is the wide choice of seats in the A319 cabin. The standard scheme of the A319 is 3 on 3 in two rows on the sides. The capacity of the cabin varies from 120 to 156 seats.

Consider the various options for the schemes of the Airbus A319 cabin, which are used in the fleets of popular Russian airlines.

Varieties of Airbus A319

In addition to the possibility of expanding the number of seats in the cabin, other varieties of Airbus A319 are also offered to customers:

  • A319CJ - corporate aircraft with increased flight distance;
  • A319LR - passenger modification of the aircraft with additional fuel compartments, which allows you to increase the flight distance to 8300 kilometers;
  • A319ACJ is a corporate aircraft that can carry 39 people on board over a distance of more than 10 thousand kilometers.

Summary

This factor plays a significant role in shaping the popularity of the A319 both among Russian airlines and among foreign ones, and also allows you to compete with Boeing in this class.
