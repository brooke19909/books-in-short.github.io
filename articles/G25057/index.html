JP Villa Pattaya 3 *: hotel description and reviews

Holidays in Thailand with travelers are associated with an abundance of exotic: tropics, fruits, an extraordinary emerald color by the sea, festivals and holidays. This is not surprising: the Kingdom of Thailand is a country of smiles, one of the ten states with the largest number of holidays and weekends.

Rest in the country does not imply a limitation of hotel infrastructure. Therefore, most often the choice of accommodation stops at a three-star hotel with quality service at a reasonable price. JP Villa Pattaya 3 * belongs to this category, which will be discussed.

Category 3 * for Thailand - these are spacious and comfortable rooms. The "star" of the hotel is determined by the operator, so the same hotel can be offered in different evaluation options.

Description of JP Villa Pattaya 3 *

The hotel was opened in 2004. In 2011, it was renovated. It is 119 kilometers from Suwanapum International Airport in Bangkok and 60 kilometers from U-Tapao Airport. This small and comfortable hotel is located in the northern part of Pattaya. The city center is 2.5 kilometers. There is a bus stop near the hotel , so it‚Äôs easy to get to any part of the city.

At JP Villa 3 * total 59 rooms located in a five-story building. This is a small, quiet hotel for youth recreation, it is also suitable for business trips.

Pattaya North, where the hotel is located, is notable for its few tourists and silence. It is remote from the center of nightlife and is suitable for those who prefer peace and quiet.
