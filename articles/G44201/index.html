Beloyarsk reservoir. We are looking for a place for fishing and recreation

In 1960, the Beloyarsk reservoir was formed 50 kilometers from Yekaterinburg, the need for which appeared in connection with the construction of the Beloyarsk nuclear power plant. This reservoir is located on the Pyshma River and is the largest in the Sverdlovsk Region, it also has the function of water supply. The length of the reservoir is 12 kilometers, the width is about 4. The total area is 39 square meters. m. The average depth is 4.5 m, the greatest - 20.



Rest on the Beloyarsk reservoir

This place is famous for its birch and pine forests, which often attract vacationers. Many come here for the picturesque beauties of nature - the rugged edges of the coast, small islands, several flowing rivers. These amazing landscapes will not leave anyone indifferent.

For fishermen, these places are just paradise. The Beloyarsk reservoir is rich in a variety of fish: perch, ruff, roach, burbot, crucian carp, tench, bream, pike perch and many others - all can not be listed. There was also a single recorded case of a piranha catching a hook to a fisherman, but this happened purely due to the negligence of the employee who released predatory fish into the reservoir. Fishing is possible both from the shore and by boat, both methods are easily feasible in these conditions.

This place also will not leave hunters indifferent: in the autumn the hunting season for waterfowl opens. Mushroom pickers and berry pickers visit this place in order to collect a large number of berries and mushrooms, which are full of forests surrounding the Beloyarsk reservoir. It is also nice to just take a walk in them, enjoying the clean air and birdsong. And what else do residents of big cities need for happiness?

Every year, various events are held at the Beloyarsky reservoir: music festivals, sport fishing, sailing competitions. But in the summer, a competition is held here - ice racing. It is worth noting that both children and adults are interested in him. All this helps to increase the interest of tourists who often visit this reservoir.



Beloyarsk reservoir: recreation centers

Over the years, the reservoir is becoming more and more landscaped: boat stations are being built, the parking lots of boats and access roads to the reservoir are being improved, good beaches and rental sites for catamarans, skis, sledges and other arsenals for spending time are useful.

Also, more and more recreation centers appear: Emerald, Beloyarka, Zeleniy Bor, Forget-Me-Not, Svetlaya and Laguna. At any time, day or night, they are ready to open their doors to new travelers and lovers of the Ural nature. The cost of their services is quite acceptable: the minimum - 1300 rubles., The maximum - 6 thousand rubles. The price varies depending on the choice of house.

On the territory of these guest complexes there are sports grounds for various games, board games, sports equipment, saunas, swings, ice slides and gazebos. It is such an infrastructure that will allow you to spend your holidays as comfortably as possible, visiting the Beloyarsk reservoir. If you want to create a feeling of unity with nature, you can use the tent. There are great camping spots on the coastline.



Neighborhood

Nearby are the Reftinskoye reservoir and the Asbestov quarry, which will certainly also interest tourists. If you need to receive any benefits of urban life, you can visit the town of Zarechny, near which the Beloyarsk reservoir is located.

To summarize

It is worth noting that the reservoir is popular not only among tourists, but also among the local population of all ages: children here enjoy clean air and sandy beaches, adults enjoy fishing and hunting, while older people just relax from city noise and dust.
