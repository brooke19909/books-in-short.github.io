Sochi hotels with heated seawater pool: reviews

The alluring Black Sea coast and hotels in Sochi with a pool and a beach during the hot season are happy to welcome tourists, promising the soft warmth of salt water and a relaxing break under the scorching sun. But what if the long-awaited vacation fell in the winter? Or is the unpredictable Russian climate not conducive to visiting the coast, but really want to swim? It was with the aim of satisfying all the wishes of tourists that hotels were built in Sochi with a swimming pool with sea water and heating. The most attractive of them will be discussed in this article.

Grand Hotel Pearl

The hotel has an excellent location - not far from the main promenade of the city, in its very center, close to many shops and entertainment centers. The hotel is a 19-story building, which is located in an oasis in a beautifully landscaped area. Its infrastructure is ahead in its multifunctionality of many other hotels in Sochi on the shore with a pool: it has its own equipped beach and 2 pools, one of which is heated and filled with sea water. For younger tourists, water slides are provided.



In numerous reviews, users positively assess the proximity to the sea, well-groomed hotel grounds, the presence of heating in the pool, excellent food and beautiful views from the windows of the room. Tourists respond negatively to the entrance to the sea - it is gentle, but there are large stones that cause discomfort.

Sanatorium "Actor"

Surrounded by a park of subtropical plants, the Actor sanatorium supplements the list with the name "Recommended Sochi Hotels with a Pool". The sanatorium offers not only relaxation and rest from the bustle of soul and body, but also a specialized treatment for many diseases. The hotel building is located 30 meters from a private beach, equipped with awnings, sunbeds and changing cabins. Several bars and cafes are open to the coast.





Particularly noteworthy is the SPA-center of the sanatorium, which offers programs for weight loss, body wraps, procedures with herbal balms and relaxing massages. Guests are invited to use the sauna, gym, heated pool with sea water, solarium.

Most tourists respond positively about the resort. They pay attention to its convenient location relative to the beach and the city center, modern repair in the rooms, good food.

Atlant Hotel

Along with foreign resorts, hotels in Sochi are gaining more and more popularity among Russian tourists. With a saltwater pool and a heated pool, they are usually a priority, especially when choosing a vacation with children or in the winter. Atlant Hotel will attract the attention of the most avid and picky travelers. The architecture of the building and the interior are more reminiscent of European-class hotels, and its courtyard is decorated with a fountain and quaint flower beds.





Reviews of tourists about this hotel pay attention to the modern territory with original fountains, note the presence of a heated pool on the roof of the Lux building and small but cozy rooms. It is confusing for hotel guests that the panoramic windows in the room do not open for security reasons.

Atmosphere Hotel

Comfortable Sochi hotels with a swimming pool are increasingly appearing on the Black Sea coast. There are few pools with sea water and heated; one of them is located on the territory of the Atmosphere hotel. In winter, swimming is still not possible, because the hotel's pool is open.

The building is located on a mountain and fully lives up to its name - the atmosphere here is really wonderful. What is the panoramic view of the coast from the 3 floors of the hotel, on which the heated pool is located. During the day, they comfortably take relaxing sunbathing, receiving aesthetic pleasure. Late in the evening, you can spend a romantic time sipping a cocktail and watching the southern sun hiding behind the horizon.





Tourists visiting the hotel note the location of the hotel - it is located on a mountain. For some, this factor relates to the pluses, for someone - to the minuses. They appreciate the view from the hotel terrace, housekeeping and delicious food.

Hotel & SPA Dovil 5 *

An exclusive hotel that is ahead of many Sochi hotels with a salt-water heated pool. All inclusive - the concept of food, which is followed by units of hotels on the Black Sea coast, but in Hotel & SPA Dovil 5 * only this system is used. Guests have the right to use the services of all components of the hotel infrastructure - a candy store, a pancake shop, a pizzeria, restaurants and various bars. On the large territory of the hotel there is a place for those who want to relax in the shade from the scorching sun. Plots for relaxation are decorated with flower beds and bewitching flower beds.





A whole complex of large pools on the territory will take both adults and children - water slides are provided for guests of all ages.

Almost all tourists highly value the hotel, comparing it with similar hotels in Turkey and Egypt. Most often, couples with children of all ages choose it for themselves, noting a well-groomed beach and a high level of children's animation.

Hotel "Comfort"

The hotel is located in the private sector and is designed for those who want to relax from the city noise and escape from everyday worries. An equipped private beach is a couple of minutes walk away. For those who want to plunge into the pristine beauty of the Black Sea coast, the hotel offers several wild beaches nearby. Sochi-like hotels with a swimming pool with sea water and heating in winter can be visited freely in Sochi, as they have an indoor pool. In addition to the large pool is a jacuzzi and a waterfall. For younger visitors there is a playground and animation, and for the most active guests of all ages, there is a water park, a dolphinarium and the largest aquarium in Russia nearby.

In their reviews, hotel guests note its convenient location relative to entertainment centers and the beach. In addition, many are pleasantly surprised by the presence of an indoor heated pool and an extensive selection of rooms - from small to spacious.

Rus sanatorium"

Small buildings and villas of the sanatorium are conveniently located on a hillside, on the very shore of the Black Sea. "Rus" is considered one of the most prestigious resorts of the resort and is ahead of many hotels in Sochi with a swimming pool. Around the hotel is a healing arboretum, which is very pleasant to stroll on a cool evening after relaxing in the azure sea.





Meals are offered in buffet style at one of the restaurants on site. The resort's infrastructure is represented by several pools with sea and fresh water, a fitness room, a Finnish sauna, a Turkish bath, sports and playgrounds, restaurants and bars. Young guests of the sanatorium will be attracted by a games room and children's animation.

For those who want to improve their health, there is their own wellness base. Prevention and treatment are provided by specialists in several areas of the disease.

In their reviews, users note the beautifully maintained territory of the hotel, the high quality of treatment and the desire of the staff to quickly clean the room.
