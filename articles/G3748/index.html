Cheap hotels in Vologda: overview of city hotels, room types, standard services, photos, guest reviews

Vologda is a large city in Russia, which is famous for its oil, nature and culture. Hundreds of tourists come here every year to get acquainted with the art and sights of the city.

Cheap hotels in Vologda can be found both in the center and on the outskirts. Below are a few popular tourist accommodation options in the city.

Hotel "Sputnik"

Address: Puteyskaya street, 14a. The rooms in the hotel offer different comforts and costs. Triple room - from 1500 rubles. Here you will see three separate beds, a table, a TV, a couple of chairs. Amenities are shared on the floor. A single room of the same plan will cost 850 rubles per day.

Rooms

Inexpensive hotel in Vologda "Sputnik" offers rooms of different categories. The hotel offers standard rooms from 2200 rubles per day with their own shower and toilet.

Hotel "Sputnik" (Vologda) offers guests accommodation - this is a room with a large bed, a refrigerator, a TV, a mirror and a working area. The room has everything you need for a comfortable stay. The cost for one person is 2740, and for two - 3540 rubles per day.

The hotel has its own restaurant, where you can order breakfast, lunch and dinner or just a snack. The hotel also offers car parking, wake up service and a safe. You can drop by the gift shop or hairdresser. Also on the ground floor of the hotel building there is a salon where you can order a photo shoot.

Guests have positive comments about the hotel:

  • The optimum ratio of price and quality.
  • Updated plumbing.
  • The rooms are clean.
  • Breakfast suits most of the guests.

In their reviews, some guests noted the presence of some shortcomings. The inconvenient location of the outlets in the room surprised most. Guests believe that the audibility at the hotel is too strong. Sounds are heard from the station (located nearby) and from neighboring rooms.

In their reviews, visitors note that the hotel is clean and comfortable. The decor and design are not rich, but the price range is moderate. Many will advise this hotel to stay.

Hotel "Atrium"

Address: 27, Herzen Street. The hotel opened at the end of 2008 and since then has established itself as a place of elite relaxation. The rooms have a pleasant interior and classic design. All in warm, non-aggressive colors. There are apartments for people with disabilities.

In total, the hotel has 97 rooms with all necessary amenities. On the ground floor there is a convenient and 24-hour reception desk, where you can clarify any information. For many, the lobby bar with a comfortable sofa is becoming a favorite place. Here you can enjoy a cup of invigorating coffee or aromatic tea.

Hotel "Atrium" (Vologda) is located in the city center, so getting to any attractions from here is very convenient. The restaurant in the hotel itself offers a delicious and varied buffet breakfast, as well as set lunches and dinners. A menu has been developed for guests, in which there are both traditional European dishes and national Russians.

The hotel has a small gift shop, as well as a beauty center and dentistry. After a hard day or a long journey, you can plunge into the world of relaxation. Cheap hotels in Vologda can also be very comfortable.

In addition to the restaurant and the lobby bar, the hotel has a gym, laundry service, parking and luggage storage. You can order an alarm clock or call a taxi at the reception, almost all cheap hotels in Vologda provide such services.

Room rates

Three-room apartments for up to 5 people will cost 11,000 rubles, and a suite with a double bed - 5,000 rubles. The rooms of the first category with single and double occupancy - from 4000 rubles per day.

Guests speak well of the hotel. In their reviews they talk about friendly staff, cleanliness and convenience. The rooms of any class have everything necessary for living. The restaurant has great food. The location is also above all praise - the city center. Any point can be reached by public transport from a stop right outside the hotel. Opposite there is a supermarket.

Hotel "Palisad" (Vologda)

Address: Vologda, st. Torgovaya ploshchad, d. 17 (Orlova st., 7). In addition to accommodation, the hotel offers guests a sauna and billiards (the cost is paid separately). Sauna - 600 rubles per hour, billiards - 250 rubles per hour. The hotel also has a conference room that can accommodate up to 50 people. Recently, the Mont Blanc restaurant has opened in the hotel building.

According to the rules of the hotel, smoking in rooms and on the territory is prohibited. Children under 4 years stay free of charge. The price includes breakfast, which is organized in the restaurant from 7.00 to 10.00 on weekdays and from 8.00 to 11.00 on weekends.

Double economy class room will cost 3100 rubles per day. This is a room with a double bed, a TV, a refrigerator and all the necessary attributes for living.

A single room with all amenities costs 2100 rubles per day. Standard class rooms - from 3000 rubles per day, and business - 3800 rubles per day. Suite and junior suite - from 4500 rubles, and a two-room family room - from 4500 rubles.

Internet is available throughout the hotel. Laundry and taxi services are available.

Most guest reviews about the hotel are positive. Everyone notes its excellent location in the historic city center. Walk to the nearest stop and store a couple of minutes. A big plus is that the hotel has its own entertainment infrastructure. The restaurant serves delicious food. The rooms are large and spacious, and most importantly - bright. And all this - at a moderate cost.

Some guests were dissatisfied with the hotel and the city as a whole. They say that the room smelled of mold and damp. Bedspreads and blankets are not washed, the shower stall is dirty.

Hotel "History" (Vologda)

Address: Vologda, st. Vorovskogo 28. The mini-hotel has 50 rooms to accommodate guests. Convenient location and reasonable prices - this is what makes the hotel attractive for tourists. The interior is made in the old classic style. At the entrance is a fountain and columns. Wooden cladding and carved furniture inside give this place a special atmosphere.
