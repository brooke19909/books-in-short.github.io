Cross stitch pattern of Teddy bears, selection of materials, tips

Embroidery is considered one of the most popular embroidery techniques. It not only gives you the opportunity to relax in the process, but also allows you to create a unique picture. Cross stitch patterns of Teddy bears can be used both for creating pictures in the nursery, and for making metrics. Small motifs can be applied in applied embroidery.

Options

Embroidered cross stitch patterns of Teddy bears can be either a separate motive or an element of a picture. Usually, children's simple stories are used to create metrics, but can also be placed on clothes or decor elements.

Embroiderers believe that starting work on certain days can promise fulfillment of desire. Most often, bears are embroidered to create a family or improve family union. Recently, a family of bears has become popular, which symbolizes family well-being, comfort and warmth of home life.

Popular stories and technology

The most famous is the bear family, which shows two adult bears and small bear cubs. But a variety of options are possible! The cross-stitch pattern of Teddy bears includes not only standard crosses, but also other elements, which in total should create a beautiful and original plot.

Half-crosses can be used for background sewing, if you embroider them in different directions, you will get the clarity of the transition from one element to another. The back-needle stitch adds embroidery to the outlines and makes it more realistic.

Cross stitching Teddy Bears includes embroidered elements, it can be decorative charms, beads or stones. Embroidery in several folds of thread adds volume and also allows you to separate some elements from others.

Even simple and unpretentious plots can be original with such decoration. Cross-stitching Teddy bears can be a fun experience if you prepare in advance for the process.
