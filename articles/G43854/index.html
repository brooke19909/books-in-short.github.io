Melissa Residence Kemer 3 - family holidays

Description: Melissa Residence Kemer 3 is an excellent hotel occupying an area in one of the most popular resort areas of Turkey in the city of Kemer. It is famous for its snow-white beaches, the azure Mediterranean Sea and the beauty of the surrounding mountains and tropical gardens. The hotel is 50 kilometers from Antalya International Airport . Nearby hotels include Magic Otel and Viking Star Hotel.



Melissa Residence Kemer 3 consists of one four-story building, which was built in 2005. In 2011, major repairs were made both external and internal. For the convenience of guests there is an elevator. The territory belonging to the hotel has an area of ‚Äã‚Äã1850 sq.m.



Rooms: The Melissa Residence 3 Foundation has 61 standard rooms. They are quite spacious and comfortable, designed to accommodate from two to three people. All are air-conditioned and equipped with comfortable furniture. The bathroom has a shower, toilet, washbasin. There is a hairdryer. All toiletries are provided free of charge during your stay. All rooms have an equipped balcony where you can enjoy the surrounding beauty and excellent sea air.

The rooms are carpeted. Satellite TV has been installed, where there are also Russian channels (ORT, RTR). A telephone is installed, a safe (for a fee). Room cleaning is done once every 2 days, linen is changed once every seven days. Internet is available only in those rooms that are located on the ground floor.



Meals: Melissa Residence Kemer 3 provides all-inclusive meals to its guests, which are produced in the main hotel restaurant. It has 150 seats in the inner room and 200 seats on the terrace. High chairs are provided for little vacationers. Near the pool there is a cozy bar for 200 people. He works around the clock. Here, in a relaxing environment, you can appreciate the huge selection of high-quality drinks.

In addition, outside the hotel there are many other cafes and restaurants.

Beach: Nearby is a sandy and pebble beach area allocated on the territory of a public beach 750 meters from the hotel. For hotel guests, umbrellas and sun loungers are provided free of charge. The entrance to the sea is very convenient, the bottom is flat. A bus runs to the beach 8 times a day.

Additional information: For the convenience of guests, a parking lot is provided. For those who are on vacation without a car, but want to ride on it and explore the beauty of the surrounding area, there is a car rental, scooter (for a fee). The hotel has a swimming pool, children's pool, water slides. Among the water activities on the beach, windsurfing (for a fee), water skiing (for a fee), diving (for a fee), catamaran (for a fee) can be noted. Those who wish can visit the yacht club.



Melissa Residence Kemer 3 has a special room for luggage storage. All public areas have wireless internet. There is a room for watching movies, currency exchange. Among the paid additional services are a hairdresser, laundry, beauty salon, dry cleaning. After an active day, it is recommended to relax in the sauna or Turkish hammam. There is a qualified masseur. If necessary, the services of a doctor (paid). Among sports activities there are darts (paid), table tennis (paid), chess (paid).



For those who come to rest with children a stroller is provided (for a fee). There is an excellent playground, a cot, babysitting services, daytime animation. In the evening, guests will find an unobtrusive animation program provided by a team of 4 people.

Reviews: The main advantage is the location of Melissa Residence Kemer 3. Reviews show that all vacationers in this hotel are satisfied with the rooms, the beach, and the hotel itself. The rooms are always kept in perfect cleanliness. A similar picture exists throughout the hotel. The rooms are quite bright and comfortable. Plus is given to this by the attendants, who are always attentive to their guests. Huge selection of additional services and entertainment.
