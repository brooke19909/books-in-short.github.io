Beautiful Sicily. Resorts & Attractions

Sicily is one of the largest islands of the Mediterranean Sea. A narrow strait separates it from mainland Italy. The island is washed by the waters of three seas and is surrounded by smaller islands on all sides.

The mild climate, stunning nature, ancient architecture, unforgettable local cuisine attract travelers from all over the world to the resorts of Sicily. On the map of the island there are a lot of places worth visiting, having been here.



Sicily is known throughout the world as the homeland of the mafia. Despite the fact that this is a long time ago, the locals are happy to support the necessary atmosphere. Many restaurants, cafes or eateries in their names mention belonging to the mafia. Sicily, whose resorts are famous all over the world, is becoming even more mysterious and alluring. The small town of Corleone is considered the main place where the mafia is based.

It is unlikely that there will be a man who will not be struck by Sicily. The resorts of this island attract beautiful beaches where you can spend your whole vacation. When choosing a hotel, you need to pay attention to the view of the coast. In some places, sand prevails, somewhere pebbles or stones. Hotels in Sicily are mostly homely, comfortable and very welcoming.

In addition to a beach holiday, the best resorts of Sicily are ready to offer travelers a rich excursion program. Depending on the range of interests and preferences, you can choose an active holiday or visiting attractions. The main cities that you need to see on the island are Palermo, Syracuse, Agrigento. The city of Taormina is perfect for young people and companies. This place is considered the most fun and noisy, which cannot but please the active and cheerful youth. For families with children, more suitable options are Agrigento, Syracuse.



Sicily, whose resorts do not leave anyone indifferent, is also famous for the volcano Etna. This magnificent construction of nature is one of the pearls of the island. Some travel agencies offer guided tours of the volcano. When planning such a pastime, special attention should be paid to safety and the necessary level of training.

Many magnificent things attract tourists to Sicily. Resorts, architecture, nature and one of the main features - local cuisine. There are legends about the excellent food of Italians. Not a single trip here will take place without gastronomic delights and tastings. Local cuisine is very diverse. It is supplemented by various products and plants that were brought here from nearby countries: Greece, Spain, mainland Italy and Africa.





The climate of the island allows you to grow tangerines, spices, melons, eggplant and sugarcane. Sicily, whose resorts are surrounded by three seas, is famous for seafood and fish. One of the most beloved and popular is the swordfish. Sicilian cuisine is also rich in a variety of pastas, meat dishes, a variety of vegetables, fruits, mushrooms, sweets. In Sicily, you should definitely try some great local ice cream.
