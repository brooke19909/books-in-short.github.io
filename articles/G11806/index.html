Rest in Topar: recreation and entertainment areas

Topar is a small village in Kazakhstan. This settlement is located on the shore of the Toparovsky (Sherobaynurinsky) reservoir, only 50 km from the regional city of Karaganda. The size of the village is quite modest, a little more than 9 thousand people live in it. The founding years of Topar date back to the post-war period, therefore this place does not have a rich history on the map. Despite this, with the opening of the bathing season, the village comes to life - hundreds of tourists come to rest in Topar. There are recreation areas for every taste.

Bakhyt

This complex is located near the village on the very shore of the reservoir. Thanks to the gentle entrance to the water and the sand and pebble beach, you can safely go here with the whole family. The beach is located within walking distance, the road will take only a couple of minutes.
