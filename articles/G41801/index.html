Oceanarium on Dmitrov highway in Moscow

The oceanarium today is one of the popular places for entertainment and relaxation, not only for kids. Adults consider it necessary to visit the magical water world, to admire its inhabitants. The oceanarium on Dmitrovskoye Shosse opened several years ago. But he was immediately declared as one of the largest in our country. About twenty million dollars were invested in its construction.

Moscow Aquarium on Dmitrovskoe highway: about ownership

Today, on the territory of 3.5 thousand square meters, aquariums and green areas are equipped with about 300 species of animals and 10 thousand waterfowl. Every day, hundreds of visitors go through fascinating excursions, enjoy various show programs, where the main artists are representatives of the animal world, who settled on the Dmitrovskoye Shosse Aquarium. For the convenience of guests, the largest entertainment venue is divided into thematic zones.





And at the very entrance to the entertainment center, visitors are met by two stone warriors with spears that guard the peace of local residents. In the spacious lobby there is a large bowling hall, a nice cafe where you can have a bite to eat before a long trip, as well as numerous slot machines and even a 7D movie theater.

Transparent tunnel

The central artery on which the aquarium on Dmitrovskoye Shosse lives is a transparent tunnel leading to a large aquarium. To fill it requires a whole million liters of water. Visitors walk along a round corridor, as if plunging to the bottom of the sea. Exotic fish swim overhead, corals sway.





The first to meet visitors in the aquarium are the penguins. In the territory, which was called the "Polar zone", cute animals of different species feel at home and happily communicate with each guest. The interior of the aquarium is arranged as an exact imitation of ice polar caves.

Seven more zones with inhabitants from different mysterious corners of the globe are waiting for curious visitors. The Amazon is an outdoor pool where pacs, Aravans, and colorful astronotuses frolic. You can tickle your nerves in a large aquarium with piranhas. They became one of the first inhabitants, which the aquarium on Dmitrovskoye Shosse took.

Tropical zone

The tropical zone of the aquarium has gathered on its territory many inhabitants of hot places. The water in the aquarium becomes motley from the diversity of its inhabitants. In addition, near the aquatic pets are the land inhabitants of the tropics. Amusing monkeys make faces with their faces, paradise birds and parrots chirp.

Mysterious Caves

Do you like to open the dark curtains? Be sure to visit the "Mysterious Caves." Here, the human eye will be accessible to those inhabitants of the depths that you will not see in free water in the daytime. And you can also get acquainted with blind "Mexicans" and Egyptian wings.

The oceanarium on Dmitrovskoye Shosse is fraught with many surprises, even for sophisticated visitors. What a visit to the terrarium corner of the center is worth! The large anaconda is dangerous in freedom, but here it can make a terrifying impression. Crocodiles and large turtles lurk here, chameleons, geckos, monitor lizards, numerous snakes hide among thickets.

In the aquarium, where the inhabitants of the Red Sea are gathered, beautiful corals delight the eye, frolic clown fish , surgeon fish, crabs, starfish, hedgehogs, bodies, shrimps and even underwater angels.





Guests can watch raccoons and pheasants in various parts of the aquarium. Here you can tickle your nerves while watching sharks feed. From time to time, these dangerous marine creatures will cut through the water above the heads of visitors in a large tunnel.

One of the central points of the center was the room, exactly copying the hold of a sunken ship owned by pirates. Barrels of rum, large chests with secrets, ropes, hooks - all this recreates the atmosphere of long journeys, mysterious adventures. The beautiful "starry sky" above your head shines and shimmers with all colors.

Aquarium staff offer dozens of fun programs for guests of all ages. Pupils - fascinating interactive sightseeing tours.
