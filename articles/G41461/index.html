Getting ready for the Bright Resurrection. DIY Easter Egg Basket

The Bright Christ of the Resurrection is one of the main and ancient holidays of Christians. The Orthodox have many customs associated with the so-called Great Week. For example, on Clean Thursday, the day when it is customary to clean the apartment and cleanse spiritually, they begin to prepare for the festive table. Traditionally, from this day they paint and paint the main symbol of Easter - eggs. In antiquity, it was customary to lay colored eggs on fresh greens of oats or wheat. From Thursday we prepared curd Easter, Easter cakes and Easter cakes, which were baked from the best flour and differed from the usual ones in the form of eggs, doves, cockerels and bunnies.

For several decades, only a few of all customs were observed in Russia, namely the tradition of painting eggs and making Easter cakes. However, lately more and more often at the festive tables of Russians there are curd Easter, gingerbread cookies and other Easter decorations.



Original DIY Easter Egg Basket

The variety of products for decorating eggs and Easter cakes is simply amazing, but some craftswomen still prefer to decorate holiday tables, so exchanging Easter and painted eggs, we no longer just hand each other a colorful bag ... Today, it is being replaced by a basket for Easter eggs. Do it yourself with your own hands is quite easy, just strain your imagination a little.

For its manufacture will not require anything special. An Easter basket for Easter eggs is made of dense colored cardboard. It is better to fix the cardboard base with stapler brackets, since there is a chance that the glue will not fix the joints properly, and the basket will simply fall apart under the weight of the eggs.

The staples can be covered with a second layer of cardboard decor or other decorations, such as artificial flowers, ribbons or colored paper appliqu√©s.

Here we have this basket for Easter eggs. With your own hands, you can make a semblance of straw, with which you fill the basket. It is made even simpler - we cut the yellow paper into thin strips - the straw is ready.



Well, in the center of this composition, of course, you need to place the main symbol of Easter - krashenka. You can issue them in a variety of ways. You can simply paint it in different colors, you can paint it with patterns, which will be especially exciting if you have little children. You can make original birds out of eggs - for this you need to color them first, and then draw eyes and glue feathers, which will serve our chick as a tail. In order for the bird to stand firmly on the table, you can use paper packaging in which eggs are sold. And so it turns out the original stand for Easter eggs with your own hands.
