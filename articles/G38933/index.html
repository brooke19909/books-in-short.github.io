Hotel Alba Resort Hotel 5 * (Turkey, Side): description and reviews of tourists

Choosing a place to travel, many tourists prefer Turkey with its many small resort towns. Each of them is a separate kingdom of developed infrastructure, which has its own focus. Side is a cozy and calm town that attracts tourists from all over the world. The five-star Alba Resort Hotel offers its guests quality service, comfortable accommodation and an unforgettable pastime.

general characteristics

The walls of the hotel complex were erected in 1999, at the same time the first guests were settled. The last renovation was carried out in 2013. This institution is notable for its surprisingly large area, which reaches the figure of 110 thousand square meters. On the territory there is an amazingly beautiful green garden with many well-groomed lawns, bright flower beds and tropical palm trees. Walking in this area will improve the body and will charge fresh energy to every vacationer.

Four-story buildings in the amount of ten pieces make up the original architectural ensemble. The distance to the nearby international airport is 55 km. The absence of long trips is one of the characteristics that make us opt for this hotel.

Pets are not allowed. Smoking in rooms and public areas is not permitted. There are four standard class rooms for people with disabilities.

Equipment

The apartments of Alba Resort Hotel 5 * (Colakli) are equipped with everything necessary for a serene and unforgettable stay. Central air conditioning maintains a favorable microclimate. The presence of an LCD TV with Russian channels allows you to view your favorite TV show or news. Using the phone to communicate with relatives is a paid service, as well as wi-fi - they are available after paying the corresponding fee. The safe will save important documents and electronic gadgets safe and sound, allowing you to spend carefree time in the walls of the Alba Resort Hotel.

The minibar is replenished daily with drinking water and various soft drinks. There is an electric kettle and tea set that allows you to meet the first rays of the sun with a cup of hot drink.

The bathroom has a chic ceramic finish. It is equipped with a bath or shower, hairdryer, snow-white towels, cosmetic set and personal hygiene products.





Supply system

According to the current All Inclusive concept, five meals a day are organized according to the buffet system according to a predetermined schedule. There is a diet menu.

The main restaurant has a spacious lounge and an outdoor terrace. Its interior has a delightful arrangement, allowing you to feel yourself at a meal in the palace. Marble floors, ubiquitous spotlights, majestic columns and multi-level ceilings together create a fairy tale for travelers. Buffet-style dishes are international. Alba Resort Hotel 5 * reviews for catering gets to some extent even enthusiastic. As guests note, excellent cooks are happy to try to satisfy the gastronomic preferences of each individual visitor.

Bars and Restaurants

In addition to the main one, there are two restaurants, the work of which is organized according to a custom menu:

  • Turkish.
  • Fish.

Both establishments are open for visiting in the evening and require the observance of a dress code. A single visit to each restaurant is included in the price of the ticket, however, a preliminary reservation is required.

In the daytime, to quench a feeling of slight hunger nearby snacks are available in the pool bar or on the beach. There is also a lobby bar, disco bar and Agora Bar. The consumption of all alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks of local production is included in the All Inclusive system. Freshly squeezed juices and imported alcohol require additional payment.





Beach

The hotel is located on the first coastline, it is not difficult to get to the sandy beach. It is necessary to overcome a distance of 300 meters. The entrance to the water is gentle, which ensures the safety of the game and bathing babies in the waters of the Mediterranean Sea. Numerous sun loungers with soft and comfortable mattresses allow you to enjoy sunbathing, getting amazingly dark-skinned tan skin. However, not everyone likes to bask under the sultry rays. For this category of people, the coastal zone is equipped with awnings.

The infrastructure includes showers for flushing salt, a towel dispenser and a rescue station, whose employees constantly watch for the safety of bathing for every spa person.

Equipped volleyball court allows you to diversify monotonous swimming, gathering friendly teams fighting for victory. New activists are rewarded with new acquaintances and pleasant communication.

Water sports will saturate everyone with extreme sports and new experiences. These include: banana riding, water skiing, canoeing, diving, parasailing and surfing.





Aquazone

The swimming area includes two outdoor swimming pools with fresh water without heating, an indoor heated pool during the cold season and a mini water park. In the center of the main pool there is a giant branchy palm tree, which can be accessed using a wooden bridge. Diving and jumping from it is prohibited. Multiple sunbeds and umbrellas, anti-slip paths surround the pools.

The water park, represented by four interesting and fascinating slides of various modifications, allows adults and adolescents to experience new emotions, vivid impressions and get a great mood.

Variety of monotonous swimming is available in aqua aerobics classes: experienced instructors offer guests a range of simple exercises that will help maintain beautiful body shapes and increase immunity.

The nearby children's pool is equipped with small slides, which will also lead young guests to an unprecedented delight.

Children leisure

For the organization of children's leisure Alba Resort Hotel 5 * (Turkey) receives extremely positive reviews. Parents' relaxation is excellent when their child is busy and happy. For this, the hotel administration organized a children's club for the age group from 4 to 12 years. Under the supervision of a qualified teacher, the children are given the opportunity to play enough toys, collect amazing castles from the designer, make creative crafts or just draw. Tolocars, horses and jumpers - all for little guests. The interior of the room is incredibly colorful, which is favorably perceived by children.

In order to diversify their pastime, cartoons are organized. Placed in a soft chair, which modifies its shape for each child, the kids fly to the country of animated films.

Fresh air is incredibly important for health. For the purpose of studying outside the club, gaming complexes with many labyrinths, houses, slides and swings were built.

Cots are available on request. Baby stroller rental and babysitting - paid services. The restaurants have highchairs.

Two tennis courts with a hard surface require additional payment for lighting in the dark, rent of rackets and balls.

Pastime adults

A visit to the spa will give you a sense of unprecedented relaxation and relaxation. Amazing skin rejuvenation and restoration procedures will improve the appearance and restore the harmony of body and soul. Massage, sauna and hammam - everyone will choose what is pleasing to his soul.

The animation team tirelessly entertains travelers daily with a new program. It consists of 6 people, and there are Russian speakers. A fun show with talented dancers and artists, acrobats and clowns will cheer up and amuse every vacationer, regardless of age.

The evening ends with a visit to the disco, which is free to enter.





Additional services

Grocery stores and souvenir shops allow you to make purchases without leaving the territory of the hotel complex.
