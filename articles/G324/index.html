How and where to find out TIN debts?





Where can I find out TIN debts? Currently, everyone knows about the existence of a tax system. Most of the population, moreover, faithfully and timely pay the due amount, but sometimes it happens that some debt obligations arise. In this case, you need to know your arrears of TIN. You can do this online by visiting the website of the Federal Tax Service. Next, you need to proceed to the section entitled "Electronic Services", and then select the appropriate item - "Find out your debt by the TIN number." In addition, it is useful to note that such an official resource also allows you to print the corresponding payment document. This makes it possible to reduce the time of the procedure and significantly increase its effectiveness.

How to find out your TIN?





This question often arises among those who first encounter such actions. In addition, a situation may occur when the user for some reason does not remember his number. In this case, the TIN debt should be found out in the way described below. To get started, you must also go to the official website of the Federal Tax Service, after which you need to fill out a special form where you need to enter the details of the taxpayer: his name, date and place of birth, an identification document of the individual, and such data as number, series and date of issue. Then you should send a request, and after a while the visitor will be able to find out your TIN. However, we must remember that all the information received at this stage is not a tax secret. Nevertheless, the so-called personal account of the taxpayer allows you to determine the existing debt (transport tax) by TIN. Following a similar technique, the visitor gets access to property information and land debt obligations.





Benefits of your personal account

A taxpayer using such electronic resources has the ability to monitor the status of operations over the existing budget. In addition, at any time you can find out TIN arrears, about the amounts of payments already paid and newly accrued, about overpayments, and it is also easy to directly monitor all kinds of real estate and movable property. In addition, it becomes possible to contact the tax authorities in the absence of a personal visit to the tax office.

Be careful!

When using electronic resources, one should proceed with caution. You must use either the official website of the Federal Tax Service, or sources that link to the above resource.
