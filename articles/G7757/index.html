Hotel Rhodian Rose Hotel 3 *, Faliraki: overview, features and reviews

Faliraki is the busiest resort of Rhodes. This town is located on the shores of the Mediterranean Sea and is famous for its clean sandy beaches and vibrant nightlife. It is possible to rent comfortable housing at a relatively low cost in Faliraki without any problems. For example, guests of the resort can stay in the Rhodian Rose Hotel 3 * complex.

Hotel Location Features

This complex is located directly in Faliraki, about 400 meters from the beach. Its guests can reach the center of the town in just 5 minutes on foot. The distance from the Rhodian Rose Hotel 3 * to the nearest airport is only about 19 km. That is, tourists who rented rooms here, arrived by plane, can be reached by a tour operator‚Äôs transfer bus in about 15 minutes.





It will not be difficult to get to the place and those vacationers who booked rooms in this hotel on their own. It is most convenient for such tourists to call a taxi at the airport. Such a service will cost about 75 euros. You can also first take a taxi for $ 10 to Rhodes, and then transfer to the bus that goes to Faliraki (the cost of the trip is 2 euros). This flight runs from East Side Bus Station every 20 minutes.

If you wish, for a surcharge, tourists who have rented rooms in this hotel can also book a transfer from the airport to the hotel itself.

General description of the complex

The Rhodian Rose Hotel 3 * (Rhodes) welcomes guests in Faliraki in two modern three-story buildings. The yard of this complex is covered with paving slabs, convenient for movement. Palm trees are planted next to the buildings. This hotel was built not too long ago - in 2013. Despite the fact that this hotel can be attributed to low-rise, elevators are provided in its buildings.





In the evenings, residents of the hotel have the opportunity to take a walk in a small beautiful tropical garden. Pets are not allowed in this hotel. The same goes for smoking. It is prohibited here.

On the sites of some tour operators this hotel is presented as a two-star. But most companies sell trips here indicating the class of three stars. Travelers in this hotel at 14:00. However, guests who arrive in the morning can be provided early.

Hotel Rooms

In total, the Rhodian Rose Hotel 3 * hotel offers vacationers who have chosen Faliraki to relax, 42 comfortable standard rooms. In all rooms, tourists can use:

  • air conditioners;

  • refrigerators;

  • TVs

  • telephones.

Unfortunately, air conditioning in this complex is not available in all rooms. Therefore, the presence of such a useful addition when buying a tour is worth clarifying. If necessary, residents can use the apartments of the Rhodian Rose Hotel 3 * (Greece), including Wi-Fi. But for this convenience you have to pay separately. Extras in the hotel rooms include a kettle and stovetop.





Hotel room reviews

Opinion about renting a hotel accommodation from tourists was actually quite good. The rooms here are spacious and clean. Hotel maids are cleaned in rooms daily. Bedding at this hotel is also changed quite often.

The rooms of the Rhodian Rose Hotel 3 * have earned good reviews, including for the beautiful views from the windows. The balconies in the rooms at this hotel overlook either the garden or the sea.

Showers in the hotel are also praised by many guests. Installed here are not cabins, as in many other recreation complexes of Rhodes, but baths. So, taking water procedures at this hotel is quite convenient and pleasant. The only thing - in the bathtubs of the hotel there are no curtains. Household appliances, including stoves and kettles, in this complex are working properly.

Hotel infrastructure

Despite the fact that Rhodian Rose Hotel is just a three-star class, there are many amenities on its territory. If desired, the hotel residents can use:

  • pools;

  • Laundry

  • safe at reception.

Residents of this recreation center can also visit the fitness center. There is a jacuzzi and a billiard room. There is also a luggage storage room and a bicycle rental service.





Infrastructure Reviews

Like the rooms of the Rhodian Rose Hotel 3 * hotel (Greece, Faliraki), tourists consider its territory quite comfortable for recreation. The hotel pool, for example, has earned very good reviews from vacationers. The water in it is usually clean and has a comfortable bathing temperature. Foreigners staying at the hotel spend most of their time by the pool. Our domestic tourists still prefer to swim in the sea.

About the hotel staff on the Network, there are also mostly only good reviews. The hotel staff are responsible for their duties. At the same time, they do not forget about courtesy towards guests.

Nutrition Reviews

Breakfast at this hotel must be purchased separately. For an additional fee, Rhodian Rose Hotel 3 * (Greece, Faliraki, Rhodes) can also go for dinner. The food in this recreation center, judging by the reviews of tourists, is pretty good. The menu here is not particularly diverse. However, portions are always served large. The food itself in the hotel hotel, according to many of its guests, quite tasty. In any case, the dishes here are fresh.





Rhodian Rose Hotel 3 *: beach holiday reviews

The sea, as already mentioned, is very close to the hotel. To go to the beach, guests of the Rhodian Rose Hotel 3 * hotel need no more than 5 minutes on the paved path. Most tourists do not consider the route to the sea too tedious. And some even believe that such a short daily easy walk can be very healthy.

The beach of the hotel itself, judging by the reviews, is very convenient for relaxation. It is covered with soft sand. There are no stones on the beach near the hotel, even when entering the water. The sea in this place of Faliraki is always clean and usually quite calm. Swimming in it is very nice. Moreover, no dangerous animals, like sea urchins, do not swim in the water here. Tourists can walk along the beach and swim simply in shales or even barefoot. You don‚Äôt need to buy any special shoes for taking water procedures. Unfortunately, they do not give out beach towels at the hotel. This accessory for tourists who rented accommodation in this hotel is worth taking with you on a trip. You can also buy towels or rugs in one of the shops in the city.





One of the features of the beach of the Rhodian Rose Hotel 3 * hotel is that excursion boats periodically come here. That is, the guests of this hotel at any time can buy some informative mini-tour. Boat trips to Faliraki are not very expensive.

Services for children

Most tourists believe that the best hotel Rhodian Rose 3 * (Greece, Rhodes, Faliraki Island) is suitable for a quiet family vacation. In addition, the complex provides many services for parents with children. Available here, for example:

  • playground;

  • paddling pool.

If desired, parents can also ask to bring a cot to the room. Additionally, you do not need to pay for it. The paddling pool at this hotel is clean, comfortable to swim and shallow, and therefore safe.

Infrastructure near the hotel

In the immediate vicinity of this complex are all kinds of cafes, restaurants and shops. In any case, have a delicious lunch, purchase beach items, food, etc. tourists can absolutely no problem.





sights
