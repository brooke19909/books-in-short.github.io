Knitting from yarn (section yarn). Types and Models

The variety of yarn allows craftswomen to experiment and create unique things. Modern types of threads have many items that differ in composition, color and properties.

Sectional dyeing yarn can be classified as fantasy due to interesting, and sometimes unexpected color solutions. Such yarn is good in that you do not need to select and combine colors for the product yourself.

Sections dyed in the same color can be short or long, which allows you to control knitting. From yarn (sectional yarn) with short sections, a bright, slightly mottled web is obtained. This thread is ideal for knitting children's things or accessories - scarves and hats. Large sections lie in the product wide, well-matched stripes.

Knitting from section dyeing yarn can be done either crochet or knitting. Depending on the chosen tool and the technique of work, the web connected with the same thread may look different.

Knit Bactus





It is better to start knitting from yarn (section yarn) with a simple product, for example, bactus that is very fashionable now. On it, colored sections will unfold in the most advantageous way due to its triangular shape.

Bactus can be knitted or crocheted. Crochet an original product in the style of Bosnian knitting. The scarf begins to be knitted from one edge, gradually expanding, and then shortening the rows where the bactus narrows.

With knitting needles, you can start work from the center, gradually expanding each "wing" of bactus. The finished product can be decorated with tassels or fringe.
