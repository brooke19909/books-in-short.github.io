Samsung Wireless Charging - A Step Forward

Modern technologies do not stand in the spot, and therefore the logical development of various gadgets and other electronic devices seems quite logical. At the same time, in parallel with such progress, the demands of active users are growing, which are literally pushing manufacturers of electronic equipment in the back. So, one of the novelties of today is Samsung Wireless Charging. This mobile accessory has received widespread demand in the consumer environment and therefore deserves the closest attention on our part.

New standard

Manufacturers, following the lead of numerous users, completely focused on the production of devices that can be charged from elements that do not have wires. The transfer of energy from such a stand directly to the telephone is based on the principle of movement of electromagnetic waves. So that each of the manufacturers did not try to rise above the others, a special Qi standard was developed, on the basis of which Samsung wireless charging is also made.



Important Nuances

The Korean manufacturer makes it possible for its customers to purchase a new generation charger both with the phone and separately, which is very convenient, especially when several such accessories are required (for example, one for the home, the second for the office, and the third for the garden). Samsung wireless charging does not have any specific features in the form of its own connector or non-standard voltage. Everything is simple and clear in this matter: the network connection is made using regular Micro-USB.

Exploitation

In order for the phone (smartphone) to charge its battery, you must put it directly on the stand of the described device. Samsung Wireless Charging is in direct contact with the communications tool, and no intermediate links or parts are required to transfer the charge. In addition, a very important point: the phone will charge exactly the same, regardless of its spatial position on the charger.



Negative point

Wireless charging for the Samsung phone has one annoying negative feature, which is that the gadget can slightly damage the passage of the radio signal at a time when the smartphone is lying and charging. Yes, the direct transmission of energy occurs at other frequencies, but nevertheless, some overlap may well be, which is necessarily mentioned even in the instruction manual. Also, if the phone does not catch well in the room, there is a possibility that at the time of charging it will not receive a signal at all.
