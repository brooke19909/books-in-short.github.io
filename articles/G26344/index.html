Should I go to Tunisia hotels with water slides?

If on your next vacation you want to not just spend your money, but really relax in a comfortable hotel, then head to Tunisia. Of course, you should not go to the first tour, rejoiced at its cheapness. Choose a place to stay in advance, see what the best hotels in Tunisia offer tourists .





Most of the complexes built or renovated over the past few years are designed to receive guests from many countries. They are able to provide coziness and comfort to people of all nationalities with different needs. Of course, if you consider yourself to be a demanding customer, then when choosing, focus on the reviews and the number of stars that were awarded to the hotels of Tunisia you considered. 5 stars guarantee that the complex is built of quality materials, and the interior will be real works of art. All the doors of the complex should be opened with magnetic cards, and in the toilet, which is located in the room, you will find a complete set of personal hygiene products. By the way, for every person by international standards in such a hotel has at least 13 m 2 .

However, if you have other criteria when choosing a place, and you are looking primarily for entertainment, then pay attention to Tunisia hotels with water slides. In some complexes there are even small water parks. So, if you go to the Caribbean World Borj Cedria hotel, which is located a 30-minute drive from the Tunisian international airport and 15 from the capital, you will find yourself in a luxury complex. You can settle in the main building or in separate bungalows. Despite the fact that the hotel is located along the coastline, tourists are more attracted to the small water park located there.





Those who pay attention only to cheap hotels in Tunisia with water slides will definitely notice the three-star Caribbean World Hammamet complex. Many tourist operators recommend it as a place where you can have an excellent rest with your whole family. By the way, the water park in it is free.

Complex Caribbean World Mahdia, located on one of the most luxurious beaches in the country, near the ancient resort town of Mahdia, offers vacationers to stay in one of 462 rooms. You can visit the water park without additional expenses, access to the slides will not have to be paid separately.

However, do not think that hotels in Tunisia with water slides can not otherwise attract visitors. Believe me, this is just an additional service aimed at attracting more guests. The level of service, comfort and the range of services provided in them is no worse than in other complexes. For example, even in the three-star Hotel Samira Club you will have all the necessary furniture in the room, a bathroom, a minibar, air conditioning. On its territory you will find a tennis court, swimming pool, soccer field. And the entertainers working there are always taken care of by the entertainers.





These are not all Tunisia hotels with water slides. Magic Life Skanes, a place for family vacations, and the Houda Golf & Beach Club, can offer you such entertainment. Both young families and friendly companies, Eden Club, and many other complexes will feel comfortable in it. If you are looking for a cozy, peaceful place, and the presence of this entertainment is not the main criterion for you to choose, but at the same time you would not refuse to ride a roller coaster, then you can safely go to El Kantaoui Center. This complex is famous as the best place for a relaxing family vacation with an original design, and only 400 meters from it is a local water park.
