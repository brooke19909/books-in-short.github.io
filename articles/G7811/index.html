Mistral Bay Hotel 4: Luxury Holidays

Mistral Bay Hotel 4 * is a luxury hotel located in a bright and colorful town called Agios Nikolaos on the island of Crete. This place is loved and appreciated by many tourists from different cities and countries, because only here you can admire the unique scenery and enjoy the incredible atmosphere of a Mediterranean resort.





Where is the hotel situated?

The first feature of the Mistral Bay Hotel 4 * is its location. The hotel is in close proximity to the cleanest sandy beach - only 80 meters away from the sea. The hotel is located in a comfortable place, far enough from the airport: to get to it, you need to spend an hour and a half on the road. This is not everyone considers a plus. However, it can be stated with confidence: but here it is quiet, and nothing can interfere with a relaxing holiday.

Near the hotel there are numerous bars, cafes and shops. Although all this is on site. In general, I must say, Mistral Bay is located in a very favorable location - in the very center of the city, so it‚Äôs literally within easy reach of the sights and entertainment venues.





Things to do?

Staying on vacation, tourists are so tired of excursions and pastime on the beach that they want to stay at the hotel for at least one day and not go anywhere. However, the Mistral Bay Hotel 4 * always has something to do. So, here there is an outdoor pool, open year-round, table tennis, billiards and a fitness center. In these places lovers of outdoor activities and people leading a sporting lifestyle prefer to spend time.

The hotel also has a restaurant (serving its guests according to the buffet), as well as two bars where you can order any drinks and try a variety of snacks and treats. Wi-Fi is also available for guests, but you need to pay extra for this.

And, of course, the hotel has a clean and spacious beach, where the vast majority of tourists spend their leisure time basking in the gentle sunshine and swimming in the warm Mediterranean Sea.





Rooms and rates

Hotel Mistral Bay Hotel 4 * offers guests a choice of six types of rooms. Of these, 3 triple and 2 - for two people. There are apartments with city and sea views. They also differ in the type of beds that they have. It can be two single beds and one wide double (if considering double rooms).

The cost of accommodation should be discussed separately. It should be noted right away that for a luxury four-star hotel located on the very shore of the sea, prices are very low, and even with the all-inclusive service. So, for example, 7 days for two in a comfortable room will cost only 30.5 thousand rubles! This is only 15,000 per person! If the trip is planned for a trip of three, then 3-bed apartments with the same conditions will come out in 41,000 rubles. The cost is really low for such a resort. Unsurprisingly, the available rooms quickly become busy. So, if you plan to go here on vacation, then it is advisable to take care of the reservation early, at least a month before the date of arrival.

By the way, it should be noted that all rooms are decorated in an interesting modern style. Designers used calm relaxing tones and created a very cozy, as if home-like atmosphere. All the amenities in the apartments are: a bath, a hydromassage shower, a TV, a fridge, a hairdryer and even a terrace.
