In what place on the Black Sea will be the best vacation? Blagoveshchenskaya: recreation center, living conditions, prices

What place on the Black Sea coast should I choose so that my vacation is as complete as possible? Annunciation! The recreation center is a place that is popular among visiting tourists. There are about 20 such institutions in the village. All of them, as they say, are for every taste and color. Recreation facilities provide a large list of entertainment, comfortable living conditions on the Black Sea, equipped beaches, tasty and wholesome food.

And now a little information about the village itself. On the peninsula, located between the Vityazevsky estuary and Kizilstatsky, there is a heavenly place - Art. Blagoveshchensk. Recreation centers “Carnation”, “Dawn”, “Azure”, “Stavropol”, “Signal” and others hospitably open their doors at the beginning of the holiday season. In the Krasnodar Territory, the village is recognized as one of the most calm and cozy places on the Black Sea coast. But Blagoveshchenskaya is known not only for beach holidays. Since the beginning of October, the hunting season for aquatic representatives opens here. The village does not lack in the number of tourists, therefore it is recommended to book accommodation a month before the trip.





"Dawn"

Not far from Anapa in the Coastal zone there is an excellent recreation center with the beautiful name "Dawn". It covers an area of ​​1.2 hectares, is surrounded by a fence around the entire perimeter. Unauthorized entry is prohibited, the order is monitored by employees of the security department. Each tourist who comes to these parts will be able to fully appreciate the unique, clean and healing air. Just imagine: on the one hand - the Black Sea, and on the other - the healing mud of the Vityazevsky estuary, enriched with hydrogen sulfide.

Cottages of the Junior Suite class and standard houses of the Rassvet recreation center (Blagoveshchensk stanitsa) for a vacation will become a real home for visitors. It is possible to rent two-, three- and four-bed accommodation. Standard houses are equipped with air conditioning or a split system, a TV and a refrigerator. The advantage of this accommodation is the availability of a toilet and shower. The cost of living per day starts at 1100 rubles. (with food - from 1700 rubles.).

Guests have access to the pools. There are only two of them on the territory: large (10.6x4.20 m, depth up to 2 m) and small (4x5 m, depth up to 60 cm). The administration of the recreation center "Dawn" (Blagoveshchenskaya) took care of the leisure of the guests. They can spend time playing active games: tennis or volleyball. Those who wish are offered a sports complex with exercise machines in the open air, designed for both adults and children. The beach also has a wide range of activities. This is riding on catamarans, jet skis, banana, diving, windsurfing and more.



Carnation

The famous resort is Anapa. The recreation center (Blagoveshchenskaya village), which is located just 30 km from the city, is popular with tourists. It is called "Carnation." For living tourists are offered two types of houses: switchboards and brick. You can rent housing for 2, 3 and 4 people. For houses without amenities per day you need to pay 720 rubles. Those who want to have more comfortable conditions are invited to settle in buildings where there are full or partial amenities. The last option involves the use of the bathroom and shower by residents of several rooms. Tourists who do not limit their vacations to swimming and sunbathing can actively spend leisure time. They are invited to play football, basketball, volleyball. Also at the base there is a billiard room and table tennis.





"Azure"

Where on the Black Sea will there be a calm but complete rest? Annunciation! The recreation center, which is located at: Lazurny lane, 15, will allow you to fully enjoy clean air, warm water, pleasant sunshine. She bears a symbolic name - "Azure". Cottages designed for living are built on the territory. They provide everything for a comfortable pastime. Each room has a bathroom. The daily cost varies from 1500 to 2200 rubles. Guests can cook in the summer kitchen. Sports equipment rental, a kiting school, and a cafe work at the base.



"Stavropol Territory"

Are you looking for a place where relaxation is best? Blagoveshchensk, base "Stavropol"! There is everything you can only dream about! It is located in an ecologically clean area of ​​the Black Sea coast, far from noisy resorts and polluted highways. In order to get to the beach, you have to overcome only 50 meters. Guests are accommodated in wooden cottages, which are divided into double and triple rooms. The cost of living for one person in June will cost 900 rubles, and at the height of the season - 1100 rubles. You can eat on your own or in the dining room. From entertainment there is a bathhouse, tennis, excursion programs, billiards, horseback riding.



"Signal"

Arriving in Anapa district, everyone is looking for the best place where the rest will be unforgettable. Blagoveshchenskaya, the recreation center "Signal" in which it is located, is popular among families with small children. It is located on the coast. Get to the beach on foot for 2-4 minutes (100 meters). It is equipped with umbrellas and sunbeds, there are water rides. For the rest you can stay in single, double, triple and quadruple rooms. The minimum cost of living is 1200 rubles. This price includes breakfast. On the territory there is a dance floor, a library, bowling and billiards halls.
