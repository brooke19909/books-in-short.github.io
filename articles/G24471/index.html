"Pearl of Sochi", pool: reviews, description

Sochi is one of the most popular Russian resorts. Many tourists visit this Russian city every year, and they are faced with the problem of choosing a suitable and comfortable hotel. Such is the four-star Sochi Pearl Hotel with a swimming pool, which is worthy of special attention.

About hotel

Hotel "Pearl" is located in the heart of Sochi. The hotel guests will enjoy an amazing Black Sea panorama surrounded by the majestic and so famous Caucasus Mountains. They can also enjoy the incredibly clean sea air.

The hotel provides a range of services: from stylish and luxurious rooms to a huge pool available at any time of the year. Everyone can choose a room from standard to presidential suite.

The swimming pool at the Pearl of Sochi Hotel, 50 meters in size, is located in the open air and contains regularly heated sea water inside. At the disposal of guests are 8 tracks of different depths: from 1.80 to 4.70 meters.

Also on site is a swimming pool, which regularly holds classes in water (aqua) aerobics. Taking care of the figure becomes much easier with such a service included in the stay of guests.

For children, their own pool of 20 by 20 meters is designed, and its depth is from 0.8 to 1.2 meters. There is also a small children's slide.

Rules for visiting the pool in the "Pearl of Sochi"

The mode of visiting the pool is from 8:00 to 22:00. Both guests and hotel guests can use this service.

A visit to the Sochi Pearl pool is included in the price. You can visit the presented service at any time of the year. The exceptions are periods when the pool is being cleaned or repaired. In winter, the water temperature is +28 degrees.

For those who do not live in the hotel, but want to use the Pearl of Sochi pool, there is a certain price:

  • In the morning hours from 8:00 to 11:00 the ticket price for adults is 500 rubles, for children - 250 rubles;
  • In the daytime from 11:00 to 19:00 the ticket price for adults is 800 rubles, for children - 400 rubles;
  • In the evening hours from 19:00 to 22:00 the ticket price for adults is 600 rubles, for children - 300 rubles.

For children under 5 years of age there is a special free visit system.

Also for visitors and guests of the hotel in the pool there are sun loungers, a Finnish sauna, children's animation, SPA-procedures, a shower, a bar.

Visitors reviews

Most visitors were satisfied with the pool at the Pearl of Sochi Hotel and the full range of services provided.

For people who love to swim, this heated pool during non-tourist seasons is a salvation. In their reviews, they argue that in winter the water is very comfortable for swimming. For vacationers in the summer, the hotel and the pool area in particular are very clean and well maintained. Visitors and guests also note the scale of the Sochi Pearl pool, there are always empty seats and no crush in the water.

The negative points include a rather high price for visitors, but it is justified by the quality of the services provided.
