What sights of Zhlobin in Belarus are worth seeing?

For tourists traveling in Belarus, a good idea would be to call in the city of Zhlobin, which is famous for a large number of sights of the war years, cultural monuments and architectural masterpieces.

This article provides a description and photos of the attractions of Zhlobin, a must-see.

How to get there

Getting to the city of Zhlobin is not difficult. Every day, passenger trains from different cities of Belarus arrive at the railway station.

The distance from Minsk to Zhlobin by car is 220 km, you can overcome it in 2.5 hours on the highway.

Historical reference

The history of the city of Zhlobin is rooted in the distant past. Archaeological excavations carried out on the site of the modern city indicate that the first settlements appeared here in the Bronze Age. For the first time, the settlement of Zhlobin became known in 1654. The settlement received the status of a city in 1925. In 1938, the city of Zhlobin became part of the Gomel region.

sights

Despite the small size of the city, there are a huge number of places that are of interest to tourists. In order to choose where to go first of all, you need to familiarize yourself with the description of the attractions of Zhlobin.

Due to the fact that active military operations were carried out in the city, a large number of significant places are represented by monuments of World War II.

Each guest of the city is simply obliged to see the memorial to children - victims of fascism. The monument is dedicated to Belarusian children aged 8 to 14 years, from whom all blood was taken for officer hospitals of the Nazi army. Truly, the most terrible place in Belarus.

A must-see is the palace and park ensemble - Gatovsky's estate. The complex includes a beautiful palace, an outbuilding, outbuildings, a factory for the production of alcohol, as well as a magnificent park with pedestrian alleys. This place is especially beautiful in the fall. Here you get unsurpassed beauty photos of Zhlobin sights.

An interesting place of Zhlobin is the Museum of Local History. Here, six permanent and nine temporary exhibitions are open for visitors to the city. Museum visitors can learn a lot from the history of the city, about the life of local residents, as well as about the terrible war years.

Tourists with children must visit the Zhlobin Zoo, which opened its doors on June 1, 1991. Now the zoo has about 70 species of animals and birds, in total more than 250 individuals. The pride of the zoo are two former circus bears, who annually become the parents of several cubs. In the zoo you can see deer, wolves, foxes, raccoons and a large number of other animals, including exotic ones. Children simply love this landmark of Zhlobin.

The modern building of Zhlobin is a water park, opened in 2006 according to the European project. There is a zone with a children's pool and slides, as well as a zone for adults with various water activities: a waterfall, a jacuzzi, underwater currents and geysers. The pride of the water park is a 76 meter long spiral slide.

Churches and temples

On the banks of the Dnieper in the highest place of the city built the Holy Trinity Church. The temple operated until 1932, and after that was used as an archive. Unfortunately, the war years did not spare the magnificent building, and in the summer of 1941 the church was destroyed.

Now, at the place where the white-stone temple once stood, a new church has been erected to welcome guests with a bell chime.

One of the oldest attractions in Zhlobin is the Intercession Church, the construction of which began in 1807. The temple is built in the shape of a cross. Due to the fact that the Intercession Church is an architectural monument of Classicism, it was untouched during the years of the persecution of the Orthodox Church.

During the Soviet era, a salt warehouse, a restaurant and even a stable operated in the church. In 1989, the temple began to be restored, in 1991 - it was consecrated. Today, every guest of the city can pray in the Intercession Church.

A must-see is the Pirevichi Church of All Saints. Its area is more than 500 m 2 . The temple is considered the most magnificent in Belarus and is a monument of architecture. The interior of the church amazes the imagination of everyone who was lucky enough to visit it.

Where to stay

One day to explore all the significant sights of Zhlobin will not be enough. Tourists can spend the night in one of the hotels in the city.

A good option for accommodation is the Slavinasport hotel, located on the banks of the Dnieper. The hotel complex is located in a large sports center with an Ice Palace, an aqua park, a swimming pool and a gym. The hotel has 31 rooms, which can accommodate 76 guests. The rooms have everything you need for a comfortable stay. The complex has a free guarded parking lot, as well as a cafe-pizzeria.
