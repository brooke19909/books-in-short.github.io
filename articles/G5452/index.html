Olonets hotels: description and reviews

Olonets is one of the oldest cities in Karelia and Northern Russia as a whole. Archaeologists have discovered here the sites of ancient people that date back to the III-II millennia BC. The city is located at the confluence of the Megregi and Olonka rivers, it attracts with its beautiful nature and cultural attractions. If you are interested in Olonets, hotels will provide you with a warm welcome and comfortable accommodation.

Olonia

In the very center of Olonets at 5 Svir Divisions Street, the Oloniya Hotel is located. It was designed by the leading architect of Karelia Elena Itsekson, who was inspired by the former Olonets Fortress.

It offers travelers 59 rooms, corresponding to the following categories:

  • single standard - from 1400 rubles;
  • double apartments - from 2200 rubles;
  • triple room will cost - from 1950 rubles;
  • four-seater standard - from 2000 rubles;
  • Luxury - from 2700 rub.

The institution also provides the following services:

  • restaurant and bar;
  • parking;
  • sauna;
  • beauty saloon;
  • banqueting hall;
  • safe;
  • taxi call.

Reviews about the hotel "Olonia"

You can hear such positive comments about the place in question:

  • friendly and helpful staff;
  • convenient location in the city center;
  • the rooms are well heated;
  • excellent quality of cleaning;
  • hearty enough breakfasts (albeit without frills).

And such criticisms:

  • TV broadcasts few channels (and those in poor quality);
  • not all rooms have hot water (only those equipped with boilers);
  • outdated equipment;
  • uncomfortable beds (too soft mattresses).

Guest house "Ladoga"

In search of a comfortable option for accommodation in Olonets, pay attention to the Ladoga Hotel. The guest house has 7 rooms with a total capacity of 17 beds. The cost of accommodation is from 450 rubles per day. It is also worth paying attention to a number of additional advantages:

  • Cafe with a banquet hall with a capacity of 50 people;
  • shared kitchen area with fridge, microwave and dishes;
  • use of a washing machine and clothes dryer;
  • use of ironing facilities;
  • wireless Internet;
  • Private parking
  • shared bathroom.

Hostel "Lingonberry"

Among the hotels of Olonets the hostel "Lingonberry" is especially popular. This place is located in the very center of the city at 21 Lenin Street. The place is designed for the simultaneous accommodation of 20 guests. For this, the following room categories are provided:

  • double room with twin beds - from 1800 rubles;
  • four-bedded room with two bunk beds - from 700 rubles per bed;
  • six-bedded room with three bunk beds - from 500 rubles per bed;
  • eight-bedded room with four bunk beds - from 500 rubles per bed.

The price includes the following services:

  • use of a shared bathroom;
  • the ability to cook in the shared kitchen;
  • Access to a common lounge area with a TV and Wi-Fi.

A number of additional services are also provided to guests:

  • transfer organization;
  • washing and drying clothes;
  • visa support for foreign guests;
  • nutrition;
  • organization of excursions in Olonets and Karelia;
  • use of a hairdryer;
  • ironing;
