Online credit card issuance with instant solution

Today, Russians are increasingly resorting to bank cards to pay for services in terminals or via the Internet. This tool allows you to quickly pay off debt without leaving home. To further increase the demand for this service, banks offer to issue a credit card online and receive additional bonuses.

Design scheme

To get a credit card, you need to fill out an application on the bank's website, indicating your full name and contact information. The decision to grant a loan is made within 24 hours. The courier brings the card. To get it, you need to take a picture with the original passport in hand. The courier sends this picture to the bank. Immediately after activation, an SMS with a PIN code and password is sent to the specified phone number. This is how credit cards are processed online.

Terms of Use

Filling out an online application for a credit card promises a lot of advantages:

  • Applications are processed with only a passport.
  • A courier brings a credit card at a convenient time.
  • The cardholder gets access to borrowed funds. If he repays the debt in 55-60 days, he can use the money for free.
  • After the specified period, 12-24% per annum are charged. Monthly payment is 5-7% of the principal amount.
  • The cardholder automatically becomes a member of the cashback program. For payment of purchases in certain stores 3-5% of the cost is returned to the card account.

Limit

The maximum amount of borrowed funds is set individually for each client and depends on a number of factors. Usually, after consideration of the application, 50-80 thousand rubles are issued. If the borrower is officially employed and has a good credit history, then he can ask immediately for 250-300 thousand rubles. Separately, a limit may be set on operations carried out abroad. To quickly pay for your purchases in any country, you should immediately order a personalized card with a chip, since plastics of a different format are not accepted in all countries of the world.

What else should you pay attention to?

Before you start issuing a credit card online at Tinkoff Bank or at any other institution, it is important to understand how much money you will have to pay for issuing and using it. Bank employees immediately warn of a one-time commission. Immediately after activation, 500-700 rubles are debited from the account. Additionally, one-time annual payments in the amount of 200-350 rubles may be provided.

How much do you really have to pay?

Issuing a credit card online without reference entails a lot of risks for a banking institution. All of them are laid at an increased interest rate (12-24% per annum). If the borrower does not have time to pay off the debt on time, then the monthly payment includes 5-7% of the loan body and a commission for using the funds. In addition, the interest rate may be increased during the term of the contract. And not always the bank reports this to the client. Therefore, the borrower should periodically monitor information on the official website of the bank.

Example. An operation in the amount of 5 thousand rubles was carried out on the card. September 10, 2018. The borrower returned to the account 2 thousand rubles. October 1 and another 3 thousand rubles. October 30. However, the balance on the card on November 1 was -360 rubles. Why it happens?

The grace period in most cases is calculated not from the moment of purchase, but from the first day of the month. So it turns out that 50 days ended on October 21. For this period, the debt amounted to 5 - 2 = 3 thousand rubles. Since the debt was not paid on time, the bank wrote off: 3,000 x 0.12 = 360 rubles. at the expense of interest. And subsequent receipts did not cover the entire amount of the debt.

Credit history

Before deciding on a loan, the bank analyzes the credit history of the client. This is a database that collects information about all the loans that a person has used throughout his life, with a detailed breakdown: when, on what terms, in what volume the loan was taken, timely payments. A delay in payment of a monthly card payment of 1-2 days can significantly worsen a client‚Äôs credit history. These are the worst consequences that illiterate use of a payment instrument may entail.

Withdraw funds

Although obtaining a credit card online at Sberbank or any other credit institution is very quick, it should be understood that such a payment tool is intended exclusively for non-cash payments. For withdrawal operations, an overestimated rate (fixed amount + percentage of withdrawal) is provided, and the grace period is 30 days. But banks welcome the desire of customers to keep their own funds on plastic.
