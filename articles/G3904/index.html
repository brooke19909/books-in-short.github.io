Popular bowling in St. Petersburg

Bowling is an exciting and interesting game, especially if a good company is selected. In large cities, there are many institutions that offer the most diverse range of services attached to the main occupation. For example, in St. Petersburg there are more than 30 bowling clubs. In which of them you can not be afraid to get bored? Where to find professional rivals?

Bowling show

If you want to organize a children's bowling in St. Petersburg, then you should choose the Bowling Show organization on the embankment of the Obvodny Canal, 118.

To create a festive atmosphere are provided:

  • safe children's paths with sides;
  • soap bubbles show, etc.
  • host animator;
  • festive treats, cakes to order;
  • room decoration;
  • memorable gifts and prizes.

In addition, the Bowling Show has karaoke, billiards, a comfortable sitting area where you can eat, drink coffee, a cocktail or smoke a hookah. The organization will happily hold a corporate party, stag or hen party. Opening hours: Monday to Friday from 15:00 to 06:00, on weekends - from 12:00 to 06:00.

Bowling federation

For those who want to develop in a professional bowling game, participate in tournaments, receive awards, the official sports association - the Bowling Federation, opened in 1998, works.

The main goal of the organization is the development of existing athletes, the attraction of new players, the popularization of bowling. Classes and tournaments are held among both adults and children.

Those interested can attend special outdoor training every Saturday. Classes are led by master of sports Alexei Yakovlev. Address of the St. Petersburg Bowling Federation: Sheremetyevskaya street, 15.
