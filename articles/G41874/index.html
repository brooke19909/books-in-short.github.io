Excursions

I would like to note that the island of Kos is not just a beautiful place, first of all, it is a point of concentration of archaeological sites. For this reason, there is something to see. Therefore, all tourists will be interested in visiting a number of excursions. It is certainly worth seeing Asklepion - this is the most serious attraction of the whole island, since it is a sacred center dedicated to the deity Asclepius (the patron saint of medicine). The entire territory of the complex is divided into three levels. On the lower level are the famous Roman baths, and on the second - the temples of Asclepius and Apollo, on the third level there is another large temple of Asclepius, from which a beautiful view of the town of Kos and the Turkish coast opens.



I must say that the island is in its own way a unique place in terms of organizing leisure. The fact is that you can visit not only local attractions, but also stay on neighboring islands. In addition, at a distance of half an hour on a ship by sea is Turkey. You can also go on a day trip to the island of Rhodes, known worldwide for its medieval fortress and Acropolis in the city of Lindos. You can also see the Monastery of Panoramity (Symi Island), visit Patmos Island, on which the Prophet John saw the visions later described in the Bible (Apocalypse). On Kalymnos you can see the church of St. Sava. But on the island of Nisyros, which is of volcanic origin, you can sink into the mouth of a real volcano. In general, the list of attractions is simply endless ... Any tourist can find something interesting for himself in it.
