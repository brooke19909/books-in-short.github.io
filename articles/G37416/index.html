Hotel "Geologist" (Usinsk): description, photos, reviews

This town with a population of 40 thousand people is located in the north of the Komi Republic, just some 60 km from the Arctic Circle. They get here mainly by plane, train or river. In summer, the city is completely cut off from the mainland. There are several hotels here to accommodate visitors - mainly shift workers and business travelers. One of the most popular places of residence in Usinsk is the Geolog Hotel. The article provides information about this hotel.

Hotel "Geologist" (Usinsk): rooms

To accommodate visitors to the hotel there are cozy and comfortable rooms (1-, 2-, 3-seater, as well as VIP-rooms). On floors 1-4, those wishing to stay at this hotel are provided with superior rooms, designed for a long stay (more than a month). On the 5th floor there are rooms for short stays. It usually houses shift workers.

Impressions of guests about staying at the hotel

According to reviews, at the Geolog Hotel (Usinsk), guests will learn the most mixed impressions of their stay at this hotel. Mostly in rooms positioned as superior rooms, guests can use: refrigerator, TV (plasma), table, wardrobe, 2 chairs, three outlets. Towels are provided by the administrator at the request of the guest. This is explained by the fact that they are often stolen at the hotel, so towels in the rooms are forced not to leave. An iron is also available upon request. The hotel has its own boiler. This, according to the residents, is very convenient: guests can use hot water, even if it is not in the whole city (this happens). Toilets are located on the floors, each of them also has a kitchen - common to all residents. It is equipped with a stove, fridge, TV and microwave. Shower can be taken on the 1st floor. He is one for the whole hotel.

Mostly guests are satisfied with the level of service at this hotel, they are especially impressed by the cleanliness of the rooms and throughout. But many visitors are not satisfied with such conditions. They call the Geolog hotel an ordinary hostel with an exorbitantly high price tag. The hotel staff is called rude and uncouth, and the rooms are terrible (after all, even in the suite there is no shower and towels, not to mention breakfast). It often happens that visitors, looking around, choose another hotel to stay.
