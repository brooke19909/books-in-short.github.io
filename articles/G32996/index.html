Hotel Golden Sunrise 3 * (Rhodes, Greece): description, services, reviews

Rhodes is one of the most beautiful and most popular islands for tourists in Greece. At this resort, vacationers are waiting for the most comfortable sandy beaches, calm warm sea, many ancient sights and, of course, the opportunity to rent inexpensive comfortable housing. For example, quite comfortable apartments are offered to guests of Rhodes by the budget hotel Golden Sunrise 3 * .

Where is the hotel

This complex is located in one of the most picturesque and convenient places of Rhodes - in the village of Gennadi, right on the beach. About 43 km from it is the famous resort of Faliraki, including Russian tourists.





The island's international airport is 55 km from Gennadi. This, of course, is far enough away. Getting to a place by booking rooms at the Golden Sunrise 3 * Hotel (Rhodes), travel by public transport will, of course, not be particularly convenient. Taxis in this case can be very expensive. However, hotel guests usually do not feel much discomfort in this regard. Most vacationers get to the hotel on a shuttle bus provided by the tour operator.

Car rental

Also, experienced travelers on specialized forums on the Internet often advise newcomers to take a car to Rhodes directly at the airport. Car rental in Greece is very inexpensive. At the same time, a car can be useful not only in order to actually get to the hotel, but also for exploring local attractions. Self-guided tours at this resort are much cheaper than purchased. In addition, time in this case takes much less to travel. And therefore, and attractions in Rhodes, you can see much more.

General description of the hotel

The Golden Sunrise 3 * Hotel (Greece) is a modern low-rise building with facades, balconies and terraces painted in snow-white. With the green grass of mown lawns and the blue water of the pool, the hotel building blends in perfectly. Thanks to such a design solution, this complex, like many others in Greece, looks truly fabulous.

Of course, the territory of this three-star hotel is well equipped and landscaped. In the courtyard of the hotel has everything you need for a comfortable stay guests. On the territory of the hotel, olive, lemon, pomegranate trees, pears and all kinds of flowering shrubs are pleasing to the tourists.





The reception at this hotel is open 24 hours a day. Hotel check-in is from 14:00. Check out is fixed at 12:00. Smoking in hotel rooms is, of course, not allowed. There are no rooms for pets in this complex.

Number of rooms

If necessary, tourists who choose Rhodes as their vacation destination can rent a hotel from 45 comfortable rooms at Golden Sunrise 3 * Hotel (Rhodes) . The hotel offers vacationers single room and double room class . In the hotel rooms, in addition to comfortable beds, bedside tables, mirrors and hangers, there are:

  • mini fridges to (not in all rooms) ;

  • air conditioning;

  • safe;

  • tile and sink;

  • balcony or terrace.

Showers in the Golden Sunrise 3 * Hotel (Rhodes) , reviews of which tourists have not bad, as in any other three-star hotels in Greece, each room has individual. If desired, guests can use the standard set of household chemicals, designed for the adoption of water procedures. Of course, all hotel rooms are periodically serviced. Many balconies in the rooms of the complex offer wonderful views of the sea. Upon request, a cot can be brought to the apartment, among other things.





Reviews about hotel room stock

Opinion about the rooms rented out at the Golden Sunrise Hotel 3 * has been mixed among tourists . There are guests who consider the rooms of this hotel to be quite tolerable, but there are those who think that living here is very inconvenient .

The advantages of this hotel are its residents primarily the convenience of beds and other furniture. In most cases , the hotel rooms look quite comfortable and neat. At least there are no creeping insects that spread the infection .

The main disadvantage of the rooms in this hotel, many tourists consider an uncomfortable shower. The toilet of the hotel is criticized by almost all tourists. Guests should take water procedures with the help of a usual not too new watering can, standing in an not particularly convenient pallet. At the same time in the hotel showers there is not even any curtain. As a result, water, of course, is sprayed throughout the room. Toilet paper near the toilets in this complex is also not observed. Therefore, it is better to take it with you. Well, or buy somewhere in the village itself.

The furniture in the rooms of the Golden Sunrise 3 * Hotel (Rhodes) is quite old and shabby, but not loose and relatively comfortable. Household appliances in most cases work quite well. Even if there are any problems with her, the hotel staff does everything possible to eliminate them. Sometimes residents are accommodated in rooms with burnt out bulbs. But, of course, a similar problem can be eliminated in just a few minutes by contacting the reception.

The mattresses and pillows in this hotel are very soft. Therefore, sleeping on them, according to most tourists, is convenient. Actually, the bed linen in the hotel itself, although not too new, is always clean. Maids change it quite often .

Hotel Food Reviews

Rooms at Golden Sunrise 3 * Hotel (Gennadi) are rented only with full board. Some tourists even attribute this to its shortcomings. The fact is that in hotels in Greece they cook mostly standard European food. At the same time, in the taverns and cafes operating at the country's resorts, delicious local dishes are also served. Without having tried them, you simply cannot feel all the charm and all the color of Greece.

Thus, hotel guests eat in the morning, at lunch, and in the evening in his dining room. They are fed at the Golden Sunrise 3 * hotel (Rhodes) , reviews of which are not bad, and therefore, according to many guests, is relatively good. The dishes in the hotel's dining room are quite tasty. If desired, tourists can order buns with cheese and sausage, sausages with mustard, boiled eggs, goulash, fried chicken, scrambled eggs, bacon, etc. Also in the dining room of the hotel guests are offered grapes, plums, pears with cheese and other fruits.

About tourists about the sea in the village of Gennadi, there are only excellent ones. Many believe that for the sake of it it is possible to endure an uncomfortable shower and toilet. and the pool of the Golden Sunrise Hotel . Reviews of tourists the beach of the village has earned excellent. Sunset at sea here is pretty steep. Therefore, a long walk in order to achieve a comfortable depth is not necessary.
