Shores Amphoras holiday Resort 5 - an unforgettable vacation

Description: Shores Amphoras holiday Resort 5 - a cozy hotel with a beautiful green area. It is a great option for relaxing youth companies and families. There is a magnificent coral reef.

In addition, this place was chosen by connoisseurs of snorkeling. The hotel is located in the picturesque bay of Amphoras, which got its name in honor of a Greek ship that sank 200 years ago. It also gave the name to the complex Shores Amphoras holiday Resort 5. Thanks to this legend, in the area there is a spirit of mystery and romanticism.

Nearby, just 8 kilometers away, is Naama Bay, and 4 km away is the Old City. The village of Sharm el-Sheikh is separated by a distance of 4 km from the hotel, and to get to the airport, you must overcome the distance of 20 km.



The hotel complex has a main building of 2 floors, a building of 1 floor and a number of two-story chalets.
