Muong Thanh Grand Nha Trang Hotel 4 *: all the most interesting about the budget 4-star Vietnamese hotel

Muong Thanh Grand Nha Trang Hotel 4 * is a good Vietnamese hotel, which offers potential guests very modest prices and modern rooms with new furniture. Since this area has recently become quite popular with tourists, certain hotels have also gained fame. And this complex is no exception.





Location

Muong Thanh Grand Nha Trang Hotel 4 * is located in close proximity to Nha Trang Bus Station. Namely - 500 meters from it. It is very comfortable. Anyway, well-developed transport links have always been appreciated, especially by tourists who come to unfamiliar places.

Although many visitors from Russia have understandable associations: if not far from the station, then you should not go there. But this complex is by no means “forecourt”. This can be understood even by its appearance. The building looks very stylish and fits extremely well into the surrounding landscape. And the service with service is decent here. Therefore, you should not adhere to stereotypes, but it is better to consider all the advantages of the hotel in more detail.

In addition, the hotel is also located five kilometers from the center of Nha Trang. And there are the best night clubs, shops, shopping centers, restaurants, cafes and other entertainment establishments. The train station, by the way, is also nearby. Camran Airport is only 50 kilometers away. A lot, of course, but not 100 km, in addition, a significant plus is that the noise of the aircraft will not interfere with the rest of the guests. In general, the location of the hotel is not bad. But this is just one of many benefits.





Nutrition

Muong Thanh Grand Nha Trang Hotel 4 * has two good restaurants where guests can always try delicious and fresh dishes. So, the first is located on the 1st floor of the hotel complex. And it is designed for two hundred visitors. The restaurant is known as Ngoc Thao, and guests can treat themselves to aromatic delicacies made from fresh seafood.

The second institution is located on the second floor, and it can accommodate a lot more visitors (namely 370). The restaurant, called Yang Bay, serves traditional Asian recipes.

In addition to these places, the Muong Thanh Grand Nha Trang Hotel 4 * hotel also has a cozy comfortable bar. Its name is Ea Nrang. There guests can treat themselves to a variety of cocktails and alcoholic beverages, and very original ones.





Accommodation options

So, the most popular category of rooms in Muong Thanh Grand Nha Trang Hotel (a 5 * hotel also exists with the same name, do not confuse them) are the so-called deluxe suites, 2-bed apartments, from whose windows a beautiful view of the mountains opens. These rooms are designed for two people. And you can book an apartment for 50 thousand rubles for two (the price is indicated with breakfast and with all fees). And this is for 15 days of good rest! Pretty modest price, I must say. This, incidentally, was the name of the Muong Thanh Grand Nha Trang Hotel complex. Room reviews get positive. The guests who stayed here assure us that the apartments are probably the best for this price.

Firstly, the spaciousness is pleasing - 32 residential square meters. Secondly - a bed. Declared as a double, but its size is much larger. The furniture is new and modern. The style of the apartment is classic. It is very pleasant to be in such rooms, you feel home coziness and comfort.

The conditions also include a telephone, a flat-screen LCD TV (both satellite and cable channels), a safe, a work desk, and a seating area. The bathroom comes with a shower, hairdryer, towels, slippers and bathrobes. Needless to say, inside there is even an electric kettle with a minibar. In general, the conditions are excellent - also for that price.





Suites

Also a very popular option at the Muong Thanh Grand Nha Trang Hotel. The reviews of the luxury apartments get excellent - and you can understand why. The main reason is the extremely low price. Only more than 60 thousand for 2 weeks of rest for three (with breakfast). It turns out 20 thousand rubles. per person. This is a really modest board. But just to be able to get to such a price, you need to keep track of promotions and offers that occasionally suits the hotel. And so the price may be higher. It also varies depending on the season.

So, the area of ​​the room is 62 square meters. Inside there is a very large 2 double bed, and one ordinary. There are also armchairs, a sofa, a coffee table, and other amenities. The TV is equipped with cable channels and there is also free Wi-Fi. Amenities include a safe, air conditioning, shower in the bathroom, and a variety of toiletries. Hairdryer, bathrobe, slippers, alarm clock, kettle - all this is also offered to guests. In general, the conditions are comfortable - just like at home. By the way, there is another category of suites - for two. There, a couple will be greeted with a special decoration in a romantic style.

Leisure activities

There are many things to do while staying in such an interesting place as Vietnam. Muong Thanh Grand Nha Trang 4 * also offers its guests entertainment. Indeed, many guests are pleased to relax in the hotel, after a busy day full of excursions and walks. So, for example, you can relax in the sauna, or go to some procedure in the SPA salon. All this is on site. Or visit the massage room. Lovers of an active lifestyle will definitely like a fitness center with various exercise machines. In general, you won’t be bored even if the guests decide to stay at the hotel.





Service

Muong Thanh Grand Nha Trang Hotel pleases its guests not only with beautiful rooms, but also with a high level of service. The hotel cherishes guests and offers them everything - from food delivery to the room, ending with the services of a beauty salon and hairdresser.

The hotel even has private parking, and if a person (for obvious reasons) arrived on vacation without a car, they can provide him with a rental car. If you do not want to be “on wheels” at this time, then you can use the transfer services (both at the airport and around). The complex has a currency exchange and a tour desk. There is also luggage storage, dry cleaning, laundry and meeting rooms for banquets and conferences. Needless to say, the level of the hotel, even if there is a gift shop in it.

Well, if a person decided to relax in Vietnam and find a hotel on a budget, then this is the best option. Thousands of tourists have already proven this.
