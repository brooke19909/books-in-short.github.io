Types of letters. Types of Service Letters

Since ancient times, people have used various types of letters to share information with their own kind. Archaeologists have found such messages on clay tablets, pieces of birch bark, or on parchment. This is a clear confirmation that a person through writing has always tried to express his own thoughts and aspirations.

Definition of the term

One of the forms of communication between people using language constructs is speech activity. It represents some human efforts that are aimed at achieving a specific goal. At the same time, speech activity has such forms as speaking, reading, translating, writing, etc. All of them are means of communication between people.



Writing as a type of speech activity is an auxiliary sign system. It is used to fix the sound language. But at the same time, writing is an independent communication system that has a number of functions inherent only to it. In addition to fixing speech activity, it provides humanity with the opportunity to absorb the knowledge of previous generations and expand the scope of communication. In other words, writing connects people when there is no possibility of direct communication in connection with their separation of time or space.

This term was interpreted somewhat differently by Russian literary language of the 18th century. In those days, the word "letter" in addition to a postal item with text on paper was also understood as a composition or work written by the authors. This interpretation was given to this term in connection with the significant influence of the Polish language on Russian, which took place at that time.

Today, the letter retains its main - information function. However, the list of methods for transmitting a particular message has expanded significantly. So, letters can be received today by e-mail, as well as using a variety of multimedia elements (pictures, audio or video clips).

Message Classification

Today, there are various types of letters. First of all, they are divided into paper, which are traditional, and electronic.

The first type of letters is correspondence of various nature, sealed in an envelope. Such messages go a long way from their sender to the addressee.



Different types of emails transmitted using modern means of communication (via e-mail, chat, video, etc.), in essence, do not differ from traditional ones. They, like their predecessors, have the address of the sender and recipient, the main information text, signature, date, etc. However, electronic types of letters are a special means of communication. With their help, people can communicate in the mode of dialogue, newsgroups, etc. In other words, e-mail provides mankind with great opportunities, transmitting any information in a matter of seconds when you press only one enter button.

There is another classification, according to which the main types of letters are as follows:

  1. Friendly They are written to familiar people living, as a rule, in another village.
  2. Amorous. They are written by romantics who have not yet been extinct in the modern world. Such letters, overwhelmed with feelings, as in the old days, continue to fly from house to house, crossing the borders of regions, countries and continents.
  3. Promotional. Their main purpose is related to sales. Today in Russia such letters are just beginning to be popular, increasing volumes and developing from year to year. This can include various types of commercial offers, announcements of promotions, etc.
  4. Business. These are letters intended for clients and partners, officials and officials. Sending such correspondence is under strict control. The letter must reach its addressee in time to inform him of important information.

Need for business correspondence

When compiling an official letter, a person hardly thinks that the methods of its design and presentation were polished over many centuries. Business messages, as in previous times, are an integral means of communication between various institutions. At the same time, they all bear a single generalized name - "service letter". This concept includes documents of various contents, drawn up in accordance with the existing GOST and sent by fax, mail or otherwise. The text of such correspondence must be correct, competent and accurate. The solution of this or that issue, and consequently, the success of the enterprise as a whole, will directly depend on this.

Types of business letters

Currently, not a single organization can do without official correspondence. Official correspondence is used in solving many operational issues that arise in commercial and managerial activities.

Any business letter is nothing more than an informational message that is of a protocol nature. That is why such correspondence is subject to registration, and verbal communication to partners does not eliminate the need to send it by mail.





There are various types of service letters. Moreover, their classification depends on those characteristics that are inherent in them. So, allocate:

  • Service letters that need to be answered. These are letters of request, letters of questions, letters of appeal , etc.
  • Business correspondence that does not require a response. It includes warning letters, reminder letters, etc.

Business correspondence can be classified according to structural features. In accordance with them, there are such types of official letters as standard and unregulated. The first of them are designed to address issues related to regular economic and legal situations. The submission of information in such a message is made in the form of syntactic constructions of a standard type. Unregulated business letters contain the author's formal-logical narrative written in the framework of accepted etiquette.

The subject line of official correspondence distinguishes between business and commercial correspondence. So, there are letters covering the legal, economic, financial and other forms of activity of the enterprise. They are classified as business correspondence. If the text of the letter contains sales or logistical issues, then it is considered commercial.

According to the characteristics of the addressee, business correspondence is divided into circular and regular. The second type of letters differs in that from the same source the same text is sent to several addresses.

Single-aspect and multi-aspect business correspondence are also distinguished. The first of these two types addresses only one question or problem. A multidimensional text identifies several directions at once (messages, suggestions, requests).

Writing business letters

All official correspondence includes several structural elements. At the very top of the sheet is the header area. In its left part is placed a corner stamp of the enterprise with the name, as well as postal and other details of the company. Here, the number of the outgoing document and the date of registration are affixed.





A little lower than this corner stamp is the heading of the letter text, and then the text itself follows. When writing such a document, the following rules must be observed:

  • the text should address either one question or several interrelated ones;
  • in the first part of the text, the reason for its writing should be stated, and in the second - proposals, conclusions, decisions, etc .;
  • correspondence should be placed on sheets of format A5 or A4;
  • if there are applications, they are indicated below the main text.

Below is the signature area. The position of the person who signed the document is indicated here, as well as his surname and initials.

If you want to write different types of letters in English, German and other languages, then when you design them you need to take into account the peculiarities of compiling official letters of the partner country. So, in different states there are separate rules for writing dates, addresses and other specific points.

Unusual classification

There are 5 types of letters that should not be forwarded to your audience. These include:

  1. Sheet letters covering more than one monitor screen.
  2. "Letters zadolbashki", sent with an insistent frequency.
  3. "Referral letters" in which the author gives advice to "familiarize yourself" with something useful. To obtain such information, the reader follows the named link and sees there another lesson or training. Such attraction to a third-party site is rapidly reducing the size of the original audience.
  4. "Partisan letters" sent by the author of various mailings to the same client base.
  5. "Horror letters." These include correspondence, which is unsightly when you go to e-mail from a mobile phone or tablet. That is why when preparing any text, you need to make sure that it will be equally well read from various devices.

Writing in the process of human development

People begin to learn the basic concepts of transferring graphic symbols to paper when they are in school age. It was during these years that writing, as an activity, assumes particular significance. The desire to clearly and quickly reflect your thoughts on paper indicates that a person improves his literacy. In addition, many psychologists note the fact that the peculiarity of speech, transferred to paper, lies in the fact that it is not only a habit of fingers and hands. Writing is a means of active cognitive activity, and its assimilation is closely related to the process of personality formation.

Unusual composition

At school lessons in Russian language and literature in high school, a very interesting task is sometimes offered. The teacher encourages the children to write an essay in the form of a letter. Using such an epistolary genre, you can avoid template phrases and strict frames. At the same time, the student is given the opportunity to give a flight of his imagination, while remaining within the framework of a given topic.





Of course, writing is, first and foremost, freedom of thought. However, before compiling this essay, it will not hurt to think over his plan. This will allow you to clearly state your thoughts on paper and not get confused in the theses. Such a letter will become clear not only to the person to whom it is intended. Any reader will understand his main idea.
