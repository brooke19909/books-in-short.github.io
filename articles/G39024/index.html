Chalet "Rainbow" (Samara): description, reviews

If you are looking for an option for a year-round vacation with the whole family, stop at the Rainbow camp site in Samara (photos are presented in the article). It is located just 60 km from the city center, in a magnificent pine forest. You can have a great time here by choosing the appropriate method of settlement. So, more details.





Chalet "Rainbow" (Samara): description

With a history of over 30 years, the recreation center has recently had a different name - "Alps of Samara". This is symbolic, because one of its main advantages is the picturesque hillside covered with young pine trees. It offers a wonderful view of the opposite bank of the Volga and the Zhiguli Mountains. On the territory of the base there is a special observation deck, where it is nice to observe the nature of the Middle Strip.

175 people can simultaneously relax on the beach, choosing one of the proposed methods of settlement:

  • A room in a brick building with amenities on the floor (No. 2) or in the territory (No. 1).
  • A comfortable room in a two-story hotel.
  • Separate cottage. These cozy two-story houses, functioning year-round, there are 16.
  • A room in a three-section house with amenities on site.

Nutrition

Vacationers camping "Rainbow" (Samara) are provided with three meals a day. The dining room can accommodate 175 people at a time. The assortment of dishes is the most diverse: without fail, baked goods, vegetables and fruits are included in the diet. A special menu is offered for children. The dining room is easily transformed into a banquet hall, where you can hold any special occasion.

There are four restaurants on the territory of the town. The largest is called "Royal" and is able to accommodate 350 guests. "Italian" can accept 50 people at a time, "Basic" - 150, "Knight" - 60. Recently, establishments are in demand for weddings, as their marriage in nature has become common.

How to get there

Where is the Rainbow Camp in Samara located? Coastal - a village located within the city on the territory of the Krasnoglinsky district - the closest residential facility to the "Samara Alps". It can be reached both by train (station "125 km"), and by buses of the 79th and 447th routes. If necessary, you can visit a pharmacy, post office or market in Coastal. The further route is presented on the map.





To overcome several kilometers to the recreation center, it is better to use a fixed-route taxi.

Leisure

What conditions for an interesting pastime does the Rainbow camp site offer? In Samara, you can not always find something similar from what is in the pine forest "Alps of Samara". An exclusive is, of course, a husky club and a contact zoo. This entertainment is suitable for both adults and children. Communicating with a friendly northern breed of sled dogs will help relieve stress and make a great photo shoot. For an additional fee, in winter you can participate in Malamute and Husky skating.



Without leaving the Volga territory, it is easy to mentally find yourself in the Far North. And animals living in the base will help in this. Even at the Moscow Zoo it is impossible to meet a reindeer, but here there are two of them: Semyon and Lesha. Vacationing animals are allowed to feed, but be sure to reindeer moss, which is sold in a local bar.

On the territory there is a platform, in the evening there are dances, competitions and other recreational activities. Lovers of the bath can take a steam bath in the sauna, which has a font and a relaxation room.

The administration has well thought out leisure for kids. The play area (kids club) includes slides, a dry pool, a bright labyrinth. Here you can leave the child under the supervision of a caregiver or spend time with him. The club has the opportunity to organize children's parties and birthdays.

Sports facilities

A huge sports complex, whose area is 900 square meters. meters, provides outdoor enthusiasts the opportunity not only to compete in table tennis or game views, but also to visit the gym. Moreover, free for vacationers. Its doors are open from early morning until 22:00.

Chalet "Rainbow" (Samara) as an additional service provides the opportunity to play billiards and tennis. On its territory there are two outdoor pools (for kids and adults) with equipped sunbathing areas and modern methods of water treatment. The length of the main track is 25 meters.

The recreation center practices target races for students and work collectives. Provides the opportunity for corporate events, including sports (relay races, laser tag, paintball). It is impossible to imagine its activities without regular customers: the Argos company, the Vita pharmacy chain, the Samara cable company, and the Auchan foundation.



Chalet "Rainbow" (Samara): reviews

"VKontakte" created an open group where vacationers share their impressions of their stay on the beach. Reviews are exclusively positive. There is comfort for relaxation at any time of the year, as the housing stock is heated. In winter, skiing is fun in the pine forest. In summer, you can swim both in the nearby lake and in the pool at the Rainbow camp site.

Samara is located in an area with a favorable climate (temperate continental) for people with various diseases. The aroma of pines and the ability to communicate with animals also have a healing effect. Vacationers note the courtesy and friendliness of the staff. Sanitation of the territory is carried out regularly, so many are pleasantly surprised by the lack of mosquitoes.

Special words of gratitude are heard to the b / o staff regarding the organization of leisure activities and assistance in holding celebrations. The hostel is ready to fully take care of them.

Single dissatisfied reviews relate to food and the need to replace outdated furniture in one-story houses.
