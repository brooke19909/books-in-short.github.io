Best ring flash: rating and reviews

Almost any person who was fond of photography, both those who photographed everything in a row, and those who tried to take an art photo, had the opportunity, and had the desire to take a photo of a completely small object. Make a macro photo. And here the amateur photographer faced certain difficulties, the main one of which was the correct lighting of the model or subject.

Importance of light

After all, the final result depends on how to deliver the light, how to illuminate the object. Lighting is especially important for macro photography. After all, it is difficult to find the right shooting angle for a small object: it can either be fully or partially in the shadow, and sometimes when choosing a photography angle it turns out that when the camera is in the best position, the object is completely in the shadow. In such cases, a ring flash comes to the rescue.

Ring flash story

Surprisingly, this type of outbreak owes its appearance precisely to medicine. Dentists were the first to use such an outbreak in the early 50s. It was used to photograph the oral cavity, the light from the flash was supposed to illuminate it. An ordinary flash did not cope with this task, and a special device was developed that had an annular shape and was attached directly to the camera lens. It is this form and this method of attachment that made it possible to make high-quality clear pictures, which were then printed in textbooks.

It is completely indispensable in those cases when the subject of filming is flowers, insects or decorations. It is thanks to the ring flash that lighting of macro objects turns out to be improved and the camera lens is brought as close to the object as possible. It is such a flash that allows you to brightly illuminate the subject and leave the background dark. As a rule, in most cases, ring flashes are used to illuminate a small object and take a macro photo. But in some cases, they can also be used in portraiture and in the production of promotional photographs. With the development of technology in the production of flashes, modern technologies have been applied and modern materials have been used. And therefore, LED ring flashes are currently used.

Device Parameters

As in any technique, flashes have their own parameters by which you can make your choice. They are characterized by many quantities, among which several main ones can be distinguished. These include a guide number, lighting angle, power adjustment range, color temperature, second-curtain flash synchronization, recharge time and overall dimensions. The leading number characterizes the power of the flash itself. It is designated as GN and is measured in meters. In this case, ISO-100 is considered and the actual f / 1 aperture is accepted. The presence of various rings for connecting flashes with lenses will not be superfluous.

Features of devices for macro photography

Macro and portrait flash have differences. A macro ring flash is worn directly on the lens, which provides soft, non-directional light that does not give shade. It is the combination of many light sources concentrated around the camera lens that gives the desired lighting effect used in macro photography. Due to the fact that this mode is quite specific, for it the use of conventional or built-in flashes is undesirable. Just such devices can give a shadow from the lens to the photographed object. A ring macro-flash is devoid of such a drawback: its light softly illuminates the subject. Such a flash gives balanced, uniform illumination of the subject, allows you to show the true beauty in detail.

Ring for a portrait

Perhaps the most controversial use of ring flash is portraiture. This application has its supporters and opponents. Indeed, such a device makes the subject flat, and in order to highlight the model, it is necessary to use other types of lighting (for example, backlight or modeling light). When using an annular flash for portraiture, it should be used as a filling light source. This will avoid unnecessary glare. However, it can add additional glare that will give the photo a certain charm. Particularly memorable are the photos of models with the original glare effect from the ring flash in the eyes.

Currently, you can easily find the right device for almost any model of camera.

Do it yourself

Of course, flashes are currently available and you can always buy them both on online sites and in ordinary stores. But you can make a ring flash with your own hands. There are many such examples on the World Wide Web. Moreover, you can make both stationary - for work in the studio, and portable. In the manufacture of such a flash, you can use lamps with an E27 cartridge (that is, ordinary). Then in this case an impressive ring is obtained, which will be stationary in the studio. Or you can apply modern technology and use LEDs. In this case, you will have to bother with the heat removal system and the flash voltage supply system.
