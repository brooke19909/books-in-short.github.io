Vacations in the camp "Orange mood"

Each time before the school holidays begin, a dilemma arises for parents: what to do with the child - to go on vacation to grandma or take a vacation so as not to leave him unattended? But what to do for those who do not have such an opportunity? There is a way out - for example, you can send your child to the Orange Mood camp, located near Perm. For many years now, every shift has been offered a new cultural and entertainment program that will not allow your child to get bored alone.

Infrastructure camp "Orange mood"

The camp infrastructure meets all standards. There are sports grounds for playing soccer, basketball and volleyball, a dance floor, a swimming pool and even a bathhouse. Cozy rooms are designed for 2-5 children, which allows you to maintain optimal order and respect personal space. Camp Cultural Program

The cultural program of the camp "Orange mood" in Perm is very rich, here you can master acting, media creation, learn art design, dance, drawing and attend master classes that teach needlework. The daily schedule is so tight that the child simply will not have time to be bored alone.

A camp for a shift can take no more than 50-55 children, in the future they are divided into teams of 10-12 people. This allows children to more easily adapt in the team and express themselves creatively.

Guest reviews and tips

Judging by the reviews, the Orange mood camp (Perm) is very popular among children aged 7 to 16 years, and no wonder, because experienced staff can find an individual approach to each child. By the way, in addition to the summer shift, in the camp "Orange mood" there is also a winter one, dedicated to the winter holidays.

You can book a ticket either directly at the address: Perm, Gagarin Boulevard 30 B, or on the official site of the orange-perm.ru camp. When you make a voucher, you should always have a medical certificate with you (form 079 / y) and a copy of the medical policy.

Children should definitely bring along casual clothes and shoes, a tracksuit, as well as elegant clothes for special occasions. By the way, in order to capture the brightest moments, you can take a camera or camcorder with you, but a tablet or computer is definitely not needed here.

Are you planning your next vacation? Go for it! Buy a ticket to the Orange Mood camp for children and your child will remember such a vacation for a long time.
