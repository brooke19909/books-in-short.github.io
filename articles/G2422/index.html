Boguchar hotels: overview, visitor reviews

On the right tributary of the Don is the city of Boguchar. It is located 220 kilometers south of Voronezh and is the administrative center of the region of the same name. Near Boguchar passes the federal highway called M-4 "Don" with a length of 1,589 kilometers, connecting six regions of Russia.

History of Boguchar

The settlement of the steppe regions on the Bogucharka River began at the end of the 16th century. In 1704, the Cossacks laid on its left bank the prison and settlement Boguchar. The region began to develop in Peter's times, after the Azov campaigns. The settlement received the status of a city in 1779 and was territorially subordinate to the Voronezh province. Currently, Boguchar is a small beautiful city with an 11,000-strong population. Boguchar hotels are ready to accept travelers and passing by along the federal highway "Don" and along the road M-29 "Caucasus". The Bogucharsky region is known for its lakes and bayrack forests, and the Belaya Gorka mineral water is not inferior to world analogues.





Boguchar Hotels

Boguchar hotels are represented by four middle-class hotels that can shelter travelers and drivers traveling from Moscow to Krasnodar and further to the Caucasus. It is worth paying attention to the mini-hotel "Zodiac" (Dzerzhinsky street, 241a) which is located directly on the highway "Don". The mini-hotel has six economy rooms with shared facilities, the cafe has a good assortment of drinks and dishes, so you can have a tasty and inexpensive lunch here. According to visitors' reviews, the institution is always clean, comfortable, you can take a shower and refresh yourself, feeling the quality services of an economy class. The advantage of the mini-hotel is the presence of a car service, where you can make repairs of any complexity.





Mini-hotels "Olga"

Boguchar hotels are also represented by two hotels under the same name "Olga".

  1. The hotel, located on Vinogradova street, d. 50, provides several rooms with home furnishings, shower, bathroom, toilet. If you believe the reviews, then this is a good place for an inexpensive overnight stay.
  2. The hotel, located on Prospect 50th Victory Avenue, 5, can offer double rooms, as well as a three-room suite for a large family or company. Guests have at their disposal a shower, toilet, shared kitchen with all necessary equipment and utensils. The car can be put on the estate. According to the reviews of the guests, the friendly hosts cordially welcome guests, hospitably drink tea, provide the necessary reporting (there is a cash register).

Hotel Slavyanka (Boguchar): reviews

Guests note that this is a Soviet-style hotel with all the ensuing consequences: not all fresh repairs and modern plumbing, poor attendant services and the like, but overall the price / quality ratio is acceptable. Visitors recommend this place for an overnight stay and a short stay. Near the hotel there are branches of several banks, including Sberbank of Russia, a good grocery store, and opposite the city hospital and pharmacy. The guests believe that this is an important moment, allowing you not to worry, there are always professionals nearby who will assist in emergency cases.

Guest House "White Hill"

In the village of Belaya Gorka, not far from the city of Boguchara, directly on the banks of the Don River, a guest house (40 Pervaya Oktyabrskaya Street) is located. It offers - comfortable accommodation in deluxe and junior suites, a sauna, free parking, free high-speed Wi-Fi, a mini-restaurant. The advantage is the location in the immediate vicinity of the sanatorium and the source of mineral water.





Travelers and those who wish to improve their health with the help of the healing mineral waters of this picturesque region will be warmly welcomed by the residents of the city and the Boguchar hotel, who will provide comfortable and affordable accommodation.
