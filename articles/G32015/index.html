Russian Standard Bank: deposits





A financial institution provides its customers with many ways to save or increase their cash. Especially for them, several groups of deposits were developed. Deposits in one group allow you to get high interest for long-term investments. The main feature of such deposits is that money cannot be withdrawn from an account before the expiration of the contract. In any other case, the client will receive virtually no profit. There are other deposit programs offered by Russian Standard Bank. Deposits, the interest on which are credited to the account monthly, are also convenient for customers. They can either be capitalized - added to the deposit account and receive more money in the next reporting period - or taken as income. There is a third group, in which the deposit rate is low, but the client is free to replenish the account and withdraw money from him when he wants to.
