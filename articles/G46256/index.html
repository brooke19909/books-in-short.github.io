Hotel Daisy Spa 3 * (Nha Trang / Vietnam): photos and reviews of tourists

In recent years, Vietnam has become one of the most popular tourist destinations among domestic holidaymakers. And perhaps his best resort, undoubtedly, is the city of Nha Trang. The infrastructure in this southern village is developed remarkably. Tourists have the opportunity not only to have a good time on the beach, but also have a good fun. Of course, there are good budget hotels in Nha Trang. For example, quite comfortable accommodation is offered to guests of Vietnam by the Daisy Spa 3 * hotel. Reviews of this hotel on the web are relatively good.

Location Features

This hotel is, in the opinion of many tourists, in a rather convenient location. The distance to the square to them. April 2 from it is only 650 meters. The popular Sailing Club Nha Trang is located 800 meters from the hotel.





Unfortunately, the Daisy Spa 3 * beach is quite far away. The distance from this hotel to the coastline is about 700 meters. Hotel guests have to go to the sea for about 10-15 minutes. To some, such walks do not seem very tiring. Other tourists (mostly elderly) find this not very convenient. After all, you usually have to go to the beach in the heat. But if you wish, you can also get to the sea from the hotel by public transport. His stop is in the immediate vicinity of the hotel.

The distance to the nearest airport (Nha Trang) from the hotel is only about 30 km. Most often, tour operators selling trips to Vietnam with accommodation at this hotel offer their customers a service such as a shuttle bus. But, of course, if necessary, you can get to the hotel from the airport on your own. Many tourists hire a taxi for this purpose. The cost of transporting people in Vietnam is relatively inexpensive.

Although a taxi is a good way to get to the city, most of the former guests of the Daisy Spa 3 * are advised to order a shuttle service directly to the hotel if necessary. This service in the hotel, of course, is paid. But it costs a transfer to a hotel, judging by the reviews of tourists, cheaper than a taxi. In addition, guests in this case are met by a hotel representative. And this can be very convenient for someone who came to rest in Vietnam for the first time.
