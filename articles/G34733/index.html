Satellite TV Set: Composition

Satellite TV is ideal for those who want to enjoy watching various programs and movies. It provides high-quality image and sound. Services may include different channels: sports, educational, scientific, children's and humorous. Many buyers are interested in which satellite TV package to choose. To do this, read the information below.





The use of satellite dishes is common in large cities, as well as small towns. In small settlements, the antenna is considered the only broadcasting medium, since the signal reception level is low, and the installation of such a device is considered ideal for watching your favorite movies.

Kit Composition

Satellite TV package includes:

  • antenna (plates);
  • converters;
  • receiver;
  • remote control.

The converter is connected with a coaxial cable to the receiver, which is located near the TV. All providers have free channels, which do not require a monthly fee. Each broadcaster also has coded channels for which you have to pay. To view them, you need a special map.





A satellite television kit allows you to access a large number of channels at any time. An important part of the system are the receiver and satellite dish. All the necessary elements are needed for the operation of television. Many companies provide their equipment, while others need to purchase it additionally. The help of a specialist will be required in the correct system configuration.

Receiver

Many receivers are on sale now. Among them, there are those that recognize only one encoding. And there are devices with a connector for additional modules. There are also devices with a built-in hard drive. The price of receivers is in the range of 3500-15000 rubles. The number of additional functions determines the cost of equipment.

Satellite dish

A satellite TV set includes a dish. Its diameter is from 50 cm to 3 meters. The capabilities of the equipment depend on this indicator. The diameter determines the image quality, the number of channels. But this does not mean that you should buy a plate with a large diameter, it all depends on the destination.





For "NTS plus" and "Tricolor TV", you can choose an antenna with a diameter of 60 cm. Equipment of 1.2 meters is used for areas of poor reception and low-quality signal. Now selling devices that allow you to view high-quality programs in HD and Full HD. To do this, you need to have an appropriate TV.

Payment

A set of satellite television, access cards can be purchased in mobile phone stores. There, the receiver is activated and payment is made. You can deposit funds via the Internet and terminals. Each provider sets their prices for services. If the client is satisfied with the fee, he can conclude an agreement to provide the necessary services. Payment can be made by bank transfer, card or via ATM. For this there is a payment system Webmoney.

NTV Plus

Today, many users are buying just such sets of satellite television. Reviews about this system can be found different. But many users are satisfied with the quality of the equipment and the presence of a large number of programs. The signal is transmitted from the EUTELSAT W4 satellite. The advantages of this provider include a universal and bulk package of channels. The downside is considered a high fee for services.

"Tricolor"

Many citizens of our country choose the Tricolor TV satellite TV set. The company has become popular in a short period. As can be seen from the reviews, users value the company for a large number of channels. When there was only NTV +, Tricolor offered similar services, but only for a smaller fee - for 600 rubles a year. And now the company takes a small monthly fee, so the feedback on the work of the company is only positive.





If you divide the amount into months, you get 50 rubles. The tuner is quick and easy to set up, as there is a convenient menu. A set of satellite television "Tricolor" allows you to watch many channels. Broadcasting is from the EUTELSAT W4 satellite.

NTV + Light

Recently, a television of this kind has been popular. Such a package includes federal channels. You can use additional packages. The advantages of this provider include two-year payment during the connection, temporary blocking with saving the account. This is indispensable for those who go to the country in the summer. The disadvantages include the inconvenient HUMAX VA 4-SD receiver. Broadcasting takes place thanks to the EUTELSAT W4 satellite.

"Rainbow TV"

This is an entertainment satellite television. With it you can connect a wide variety of channels for watching movies, which is why many users have connected to it. According to reviews, people liked the variety of thematic channels.





The advantages of "Rainbow TV" include the low cost of equipment. Registering the receiver is very convenient and simple. Deficiencies users have not identified. The signal comes from satellite ABS 1.

"Continent TV"

The main feature of the provider is flexibility. Clients are provided with 10 federal channels for free. At will, a package of the necessary subjects is selected. For example, for 100 rubles there is an opportunity to connect 33 channels. Broadcasting is performed from the INTELSAT 15 satellite.

Satellite television is now in great demand. You can connect any desired channels at a bargain price. They will be available at any time. And which company to contact depends on the desire.
