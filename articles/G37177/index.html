Kustur Club Holiday Village 5 * (Turkey, Kusadasi): room description, service, reviews

When choosing a Turkish resort, travelers often stop at the sunny city of Kusadasi, which is located on the coast of the Aegean Sea. Its centuries-old history has turned a stone fortress into a large-scale tourist zone with developed infrastructure. The mild climate, long summers and hot sun are favorable for youth, couples with children and older people. The Kustur Club Holiday Village hotel located here offers first-class service. It demonstrates comfortable conditions to vacationers, gives unforgettable memories and enthusiastic emotions.





general characteristics

The hotel complex was erected in 1966. The last renovation was carried out in 2014. It is represented by cottage houses, one- and two-story, located in a pine grove and drowning in bright spring colors. Total area - 170 thousand square meters. m. The distance to the nearest airport is 70 km, and to the center of the village - 4 km. The number of buildings on the territory is 80, among which 20 are one-story and 60 are two-story. A delightful green garden, numerous palm trees, clean fresh air and evening walks in the public gardens delight guests of all ages.

Accommodation for new arrivals

The room fund of the hotel Kustur Club Holiday Village HV-1 5 * is represented by more than 400 apartments, combined in the following categories:

  • Superior Room.
  • Bungalow Room.
  • Family Room.

All chambers have a modern design, unique decorative elements. They are made in a classic style. Bright lighting achieved using sconces and floor lamps, unique paintings by local artists and designer curtains - all this gives an unprecedented coziness and comfort of living. The floors of the first floors are covered with ceramic tiles, the second - carpet. All rooms on the second floor have a balcony with a wicker table and chairs, and the first - a terrace. Smoke detectors and an automatic fire alarm system are designed to ensure the safety of spa guests.





Kustur Club Holiday Village: room description

Each room has its own color scheme. The furniture is presented as a standard set: beds, chairs, wardrobe, table and bedside tables. An LCD TV with Russian channels, a telephone, a safe and a hairdryer are present in every room. A favorable temperature helps maintain an individual split system.

The presence of a mini-bar allows you to quench your thirst without leaving the room. It is replenished once upon arrival with drinking water, soft drinks and beer.

The bathroom is equipped with a bath or shower, finished with high quality ceramic tiles. The availability of all necessary hygiene products and toiletries contributes to a favorable stay.

All apartments of the Kustur Club Holiday Village 5 * hotel (Kusadasi) have free Wi-Fi. This is another advantage. Cleanliness is maintained thanks to the conscientious daily cleaning of tidy maids. Bed linen is changed three times a week.





Supply system

The food system of guests at the Kustur Club Holiday Village 5 * hotel is based on the All Inclusive concept and is organized as a buffet. Here you can enjoy dishes from the extended breakfast, set lunch and hearty dinner at the main restaurant. Vacationers independently choose and put on the plate what they want.

During the day, tourists are provided with a wide selection of soft drinks, local alcoholic drinks, as well as a large assortment of various snacks. The presence of A'la Carte restaurants, working on a custom, additionally paid menu, allows you to enjoy culinary masterpieces, starting with Italian and Turkish cuisine and ending with Far Eastern delicacies.

On the territory of the complex Kustur Club Holiday Village HV-1 5 * there are 6 bars. For ice cream, desserts and pastries, certain hours of providing such treats are reserved. In the snack bar, in addition to drinks, you can enjoy light soups and snacks.

Imported alcoholic drinks and freshly squeezed juices are paid extra.





Toddler Leisure Outdoors

About Kustur Club Holiday Village Positive feedback is provided by grateful parents. Younger guests often prefer to frolic in the fresh air, namely to splash around in the water. Children's aquazone is represented by two pools, one of which is equipped with ladders and slides in the form of an octopus. The presence of watering fungi delights the kids. After long baths, they are happy to run to the sports field, equipped with slides, labyrinths, swings and carousels made of high-quality plastic.

Kids club

Among the possible entertainments, the appropriate place is occupied by the Kushtur children's club, which works in the morning and in the afternoon. Here they will enjoy the professional program of animators who speak English, German and French. The entertainment program includes face painting, sand drawings, drawing, children's films and a unique fairy tale show. Organized competitions and sports games motivate to fight for primacy, develop team spirit and thought processes. The children's buffet will replenish the wasted energy of the kids, and they, full of vigor and new energy, will go to enjoy their vacation.





Adult aquazone

One of the main entertainments of vacationers on hot days is the pools. The presence of five slopes diversifies monotonous swimming. Among them are direct, twisted and closed gutters. The pool area is equipped with deckchairs with soft mattresses, umbrellas and awnings. Towels are provided free of charge. You can quench your thirst and enjoy unique cocktails and punches in the pool bar.

The proximity of the adult and children's pools in the neighborhood provides parents with the opportunity to watch their children. Experienced sports instructors regularly conduct water gymnastics, which heals the body, supports the body in good shape and strengthens the muscles.

Kustur Club Holiday Village: Beach

The coast of the Aegean Sea is in close proximity - less than 100 m. The path passes through the underpass and takes no more than 3 minutes. A gentle descent into warm clear water, velvety hot sand, a light breeze create an amazing atmosphere of a fairy tale. The beach is equipped with comfortable sun loungers with pillows, allowing you to relax and enjoy the sun baths, giving an excellent tan. Towels are provided free of charge. Umbrellas and awnings will save lovers to relax in the shade.

The beach bar is included in the All Inlusive system and gives you the opportunity to taste the author's cocktails of bartenders and delicious drinks to spa guests of all ages.

The beach is rich in various water attractions and leisure activities. Among them, free are:

  • Catamaran.
  • Volleyball Court.
  • Canoe.
  • Surfing, with permission.

However, the list of additionally paid entertainment options is no less diverse: riding a banana, scuba diving, massage services and skydiving.





Types of outdoor activities

A well-equipped gym is open for everyone, where qualified instructors will help you create an individual training program. Collective aerobic exercises keep the body in good shape and tone up the muscles.

Table tennis with rackets and balls, futsal, mini-golf, archery, basketball and darts will surely appeal to lovers to compete for victory. Billiards and table football, scuba diving lessons, spa treatments and video games are additionally paid upon delivery.

Sauna and Turkish bath, tennis court with hard surface are available to everyone.

Every evening, an organized show program with the participation of professional groups of dancers, acrobats and artists is to the liking of any vacationer.





Additional services

The list of additionally paid services includes: ironing, laundry and car rental. There is an opportunity to use the services of a qualified nanny, who will give the child an entertaining pastime.

Arrivals by car are provided with a free parking space. Check-in with pets is strictly prohibited.

The complex has a mini market, souvenir shops and a photo studio. The opportunity to order an unforgettable photo shoot, filling your album with unique pictures from the resort, will delight every guest.

The hotel has a conference room for businessmen, where you can conduct business meetings, negotiations and other events.





Reviews experienced

A large number of positive reviews leave tourists who previously rested in the Kustur Club Holiday Village 5 * hotel. According to the guests, the live music that accompanies the entire period of stay pleases with its peculiar compositions. The friendly and helpful staff, speaking four languages, solves the questions within a few minutes.

The vast territory of the hotel complex creates a feeling of freedom and ease, as many vacationers note. The water park on the territory is one of the main hotel assets. Having enjoyed a wonderful and unforgettable vacation, travelers gain new strength, stock up on energy and patience to reach the unconquered peaks. As many former guests of the hotel note, it is definitely worth considering as an option for a good vacation during your vacation.
