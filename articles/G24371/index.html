Ekavi Hotel 3 (Crete, Greece) - photos, prices and reviews of tourists from Russia

The Greek island of Crete has long been chosen by both tourists from European countries and our compatriots. And not only family people with children come here, as well as pensioners in search of a calm and measured rest on the shores of the gentle sea and surrounded by beautiful nature, but also cheerful youth who will also find here entertainment to their taste. If you plan to spend your vacation in a calm place, relaxing away from city noise, then Ekavi Hotel 3 * (Rethymno, Crete) will be a good economical option for accommodation . Learn more about this hotel.





Where is

This hotel is located in a small resort village called Sfakaki. The large tourist town of Rethymnon is only 10 kilometers away. You can get here both by bus and taxi. The hotel is located on the very shore of the Aegean Sea. So its guests can be on the beach simply by going down the stairs from the terrace. Near the hotel there are a number of attractions: Museum of History and Folk Art, Museum of Archeology, Fortezza Fortress and others. In the vicinity of Ekavi Hotel 3 * you will easily find shops, taverns, souvenir shops, car rental offices and tour desks.

As for the airports, there are two of them on the island of Crete. One is in Heraklion (69 kilometers from the hotel), and the other is in Chania (78 kilometers from the hotel). So on arrival on the way to the hotel will take about an hour and a half.





Ekavi Hotel 3 * (Greece, Crete): description, photo

This small hotel, located in the small resort village of Sfakaki, invites everyone to stay in one of 64 comfortable rooms. They are located in two three-story residential buildings. The rooms here are presented in three categories: standard doubles, studios and two-bedroom apartments. All rooms have air conditioning, safe, refrigerator, TV. Each room also has its own bathroom. Some apartments have a kitchenette. The use of air conditioning and a safe is paid. Wi-Fi is also available at an additional cost. Pets are not allowed in this hotel, in accordance with internal rules.

On a small private territory of the hotel there is a pool, bar, restaurant, sun terrace. When booking a room, you can choose the type of food that suits you: breakfast only or half board. Throughout the day, √† la carte dishes (Greek and Mediterranean cuisine) can be ordered at the on-site tavern. Drinks at the restaurant and bar are available at an additional cost.





Prices

As for the cost of living in Ekavi Hotel 3 * (Crete), then, according to most tourists, it is quite consistent with the level of service and accommodation conditions here. So, a day in this hotel from June 10 to September 15 will cost you 55 euros for a studio, 70 euros for a standard room and 100 euros for a two-bedroom apartment. In the lower season, the hotel's pricing policy is as follows: 40 euros - a studio, 50 euros - a standard, and 80 - two-bedroom apartments. For stays of more than seven days, a discount can be provided. Prices shown include breakfast.

Ekavi Hotel 3 *: reviews of Russian tourists

Since today more and more tourists are in the process of planning a trip to a particular country, they are trying to choose a hotel, focusing not only on its official description and pricing policy, but also on the impressions of other vacationers, we decided to make your task a little easier and introduce our opinions compatriots who recently spent their vacations at Ekavi Hotel (Crete, Greece). Just note that many tourists liked this hotel, and they are ready to return here again. But we will understand everything in detail.





Opinion about the location of the hotel

This hotel, according to vacationers, is located near Rethymno. So, by bus (the stop is a short walk from the hotel) you can get here in a quarter of an hour. Near the hotel there are shops and taverns where you can buy everything you need and eat delicious food at very reasonable prices. Ekavi Hotel 3 * is located in a calm and quiet place on the beach. Here you can relax away from the hustle and bustle of the city.

The hotel area is small. So, on it there is a fairly spacious pool with a bridge (one part is deeper and the other is smaller), a lawn with sunbeds and umbrellas, a bar, a residential building and a terrace where you can have a great time watching the sea and the sunset.





Hotel room reviews

As for the rooms, all of them, according to our compatriots, are located in a three-story residential building. The guests found the apartments spacious enough. Furniture and appliances are in good and working condition. According to tourists, hot water has always been. Air conditioning is available at an additional cost. However, according to many travelers, it is not particularly necessary, since there is no special heat in this place of Crete. For a fee, you can also use the in-room safe and Internet access. In general, judging by the reviews of the guests, the Ekavi Hotel 3 * apartments have everything you need. There are also rooms equipped with kitchenettes.

The cleaning of the rooms, according to our compatriots, is carried out every day. Towels were changed once every two days, and linen once a week.

Impressions of tourists about food and staff

In general, the guests were satisfied with the level of food in this hotel, judging by their comments. So, many people considered breakfasts not very diverse. However, no one was left hungry. For lunch and dinner, they cook something new every day. All dishes are not just edible, but very tasty. According to tourists, they were offered meat and fish dishes, salads, various snacks and desserts. Drinks were available at an additional cost.

As for the staff of Ekavi Hotel Apartments 3 *, then he left a pleasant impression with the guests. According to them, the hotel belongs to a large Greek family, whose members make up the bulk of the staff. Our compatriots pay attention to the fact that, due to the fact that the main contingent of tourists is represented here by Europeans (Germany, England, etc.), none of the staff speak Russian. Therefore, most likely, you will have to recall a little the school curriculum of German or English.





Reviews on beach vacations and activities

As for the beach, it is literally next to the hotel: you just have to go down the stairs from the terrace and you will find yourself a few meters from the water's edge. According to travelers, entering the sea near the hotel is not very convenient. So, there are a lot of sharp stones, and swimming without special shoes is not recommended. However, the majority of vacationers, going ashore, turned left and went to the beach of the nearest five-star hotel. Here the coast is sandy and pebble. Water is warm and clear everywhere. A big plus of local beaches, many guests considered the fact that due to the remoteness from large settlements, there are few people here.

Speaking of entertainment, tourists note that you should not expect animations or any other entertainment events at Ekavi Hotel 3 *. Most travelers choosing this hotel prefer to combine beach vacations and poolside activities with trips to interesting places of Crete. To do this, you can either rent a car at one of the many rental points, or buy a tour.
