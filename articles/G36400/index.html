Lazarevskoye - Moscow: travel by car

A large number of people who wish to spend their leisure time in the micro-district of Sochi, optimal for recreation, overcome the Moscow-Lazarevskoye route by private car. These people are not afraid of the great distance and the prospect of spending more than a day on the road, because this vehicle provides a certain freedom that a train, plane or bus cannot provide. This article will highlight the positive and negative aspects of this trip.

Resort Summary

Lazarevskoye is a unique territory of the subtropics in our country. In this village, a warm and humid climate dominates. In the summer months, the air temperature is usually 26-27 degrees. In the daytime, the heat in these places is more easily tolerated due to the effects of sea breezes; at night, the elusive breath of the breeze from the mountains brings a pleasant coolness.





Lazarevsky has pebble beaches. A shallow bottom does not cause inconvenience when entering the sea. The resort offers entertainment for every taste. Each vacationer can find the most convenient option for your place of residence.

The village is not distinguished by fuss, but it is also difficult to call it full of silence. A large number of restaurants, cafes, discos located on the coast, work at night. A railway runs through Lazarevskoye, in this resort town there is a bus station and a marina. The nearest airport is Adler.

Pros of traveling by car

Tourists who overcome the Moscow -Lazarevskoye road by car find many significant advantages in their trip.

  • Mobility. Travelers can make adjustments to their route at any time, because they do not depend on a specific schedule.
  • A lot of new experiences. A trip to the south by car provides a chance to fully see what a native country looks like, full of amazing beauty by natural corners. Vacationers can stop and relax in any picturesque place.
  • Saving money. To overcome the distance Moscow - Lazarevskoye - Moscow with your own car is really much cheaper than with another type of transport.
  • A chance to take all the necessary things with you on a trip by placing them in the trunk of a car.




Cons of a car trip

  • Great responsibility. Travelers are not immune from surprises and force majeure that may occur on the highway.
  • Additional expenses (car repair, parking fees, paid sections of the road) can be expected by tourists on the road.
  • Traffic jams that can be difficult to avoid.
  • Huge effort. Long-term driving requires maximum concentration and can deplete the supply of power.




Useful Travel Tips

  • It is recommended to prepare the car for a long trip with special care.
  • It is necessary to consider places for spending the night and food.
  • You need to study your route in detail.
  • A detailed list of things that will come in handy on the road should be made.
  • When embarking on a journey in a big company, discuss all the details of your trip. This will help to avoid disagreements and disputes along the way.
