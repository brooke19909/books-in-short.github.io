Installation requirements

Before installing the Samsung WF8590NLW8 washing machine, make sure that the power source has a voltage of 220 V or 50 Hz, and that there is grounding in the outlet. During operation of the device, water must flow continuously, and the pressure in the pipes must be 50-800 kPa. If the water pressure is below 50 kPa, then the water valve will malfunction. It will not close completely. At low water pressures, it will take longer to fill the machine, which may cause the machine to shut down.

If the length of the hose was enough to connect the unit to the water supply, you need to install the washing machine at a distance not exceeding 122 cm from the water tap. If the hose length is not enough, then you can purchase an inlet analog of any length in the store and attach it to the one that comes with the kit.

In order to minimize possible damage that may occur due to leakage during machine operation:

  • Provide consolidated access to the water tap.
  • Turn off taps when the appliance is not in use.
  • Check all hoses and connections regularly for leaks.

For a good drain, the drain hose is pulled through a special clamp located on the back of the device. The drain pipe must be of such a diameter that the drain hose can fit in it.

The appliance is installed on a hard and even surface. To minimize vibration, the wooden floor is pre-strengthened. Carpets and tiles stimulate the processes of vibration and on such a coating there is a slight movement of the unit during the spin cycle.

Do not install the washing machine in places where water can freeze, as there is always a little liquid remaining in the hose and in the pump.

If the machine is installed in a cabinet or a niche, then 25 mm gaps are left on the sides and top. The clearance should be 51 mm at the rear and 465 mm at the front.

Instrument Care

Caring for a washing machine is not particularly difficult. To remove all water from the machine, you need to use the emergency drain. To do this, the device is disconnected from the power supply. Open the filter cover. This can be done using the key included in the kit. Turn out the filter plug by turning left. Take the plug, which is located at the end of the tube, and slowly pull the hose. Its end is placed in a prepared container and allowed to drain water. Next, the emergency drain tube is refilled and the filter cover is returned to its place.

Periodically, you need to clean the outside of the unit. The washing machine is served with a soft cloth without the use of chemical and abrasive agents.

Each time, after washing, clean the detergent drawer. To do this, it is removed from the machine by pressing the special lever. They take out a partition for liquid products from it, and the part is well washed under running water. The compartment niche is cleaned with an old toothbrush. After these manipulations, the detergent drawer is returned.

With the proper care of the Samsung WF8590NLW8, device repair can be avoided and its service life significantly increased.
