How to make DIY birthday cards - features, interesting ideas and recommendations

A handmade gift or a postcard will be much more pleasant to receive for a loved one on a gala day than to hold a standard purchase option in your hands. Self-made things are unique and inimitable, and even if it is accompanied by a warm wish, then such a gift will be remembered for a long time.

Most people believe that it’s impossible to make birthday cards beautifully if there are no expensive materials at hand, but this opinion is erroneous. It is enough to have simple skills in needlework to create a unique masterpiece.

How to make a card with butterflies

These creatures are gentle, very beautiful and graceful, and therefore quite deserve to become a worthy decoration of any postcard.

To make a postcard for the holiday, you will need to acquire the following materials:

  • white paper in the amount of two sheets;
  • multi-colored watercolor paints;
  • tassels;
  • special hole punch;
  • hot glue;
  • a simple pencil, if you want to create a preliminary sketch.

Manufacturing instruction

You can make a birthday card with your own hands in the following way:

  1. An abstract pattern in various colors is applied to white paper. If there is a child in the house, then such work can be safely entrusted to him.
  2. The drawing is laid aside until it dries completely.
  3. During this time, you can do other useful work. The remaining white sheet folds in half. The outer side is decorated with a blue background, for which blue watercolor paint is used.
  4. Then, after the background has dried, you need to draw a tree branch. Here you will need a thin brush and watercolor. If you are not sure that everything will turn out beautifully the first time, then you can create a sketch of a branch using a simple pencil.
  5. Using a hole punch, butterflies are created from a multi-colored pattern in the quantity in which they are needed.
  6. There comes a turn of hot glue which will fix butterflies on a tree. It is applied on the back of the workpiece, in the center.

DIY birthday card is finished.

Original gift with flowers

Many women like flowers, so why not please your loved one with an original gift? In particular, it will be appreciated if a favorite flower variety decorates a card.

Such an unusual sign of appreciation will surely appeal to mom as a present for any special occasion. So, how to make a birthday card for mom?

It is necessary to stock up on the following materials:

  • thick multi-colored paper or cardboard;
  • hole punch, which will create the contours of the selected flower;
  • sheets of colored paper;
  • adhesive pencil;
  • antique paper;
  • hot glue;
  • paint;
  • marker.

Step-by-step instruction

  1. Using a hole punch, blanks are cut from sheets of multi-colored paper. You can always replace the hole punch with a pattern and then cut flowers along its contours. The paper is used in the same color from which the flower itself is planned.
  2. Cardboard is needed here, its surface (with the exception of the edges) is processed using an adhesive pencil. Then antique paper is applied on top and ironed for good bonding.
  3. Using hot glue, they fix flowers on a card in a random order, as prompted by imagination.
  4. A marker or paints are needed at the very last stage of work to create a core for each flower.

There should not be more questions about how to make a birthday card for mom.

What else can be done

This version of the original postcard is suitable to please representatives of the strong half of humanity. Most stores offer a standard set of cards decorated with flowers. If this option is good as a gift to a woman, then for a man it is unlikely to be suitable.

But to make a birthday card out of paper in the form of a paper shirt will not be so difficult.

Before creating an original memorable masterpiece, you must have at hand sheets of thin cardboard of different colors. The first is chosen as the basis, and that is exactly what the shirt will be. The second is given the role of a tie, which must be made eye-catching, bright. You will also need a couple of small buttons to decorate the collar, as well as scissors and glue. It is recommended to use glue stick, because he leaves no traces on paper.

Next will be described in detail how to make a beautiful birthday card.

Working process

  1. The double-sided sheet of cardboard is folded in half. It will serve as a blank for the postcard. If for some reason the size or shape does not suit you, then you can use the scissors to work on the edges.
  2. Stepping back from the top edge two centimeters, it is necessary to make two cuts, they will serve as the boundaries indicating the collar of the shirt.
  3. On the second sheet, which is the back of the card, a strip having a collar width is cut off from above so that this part can stand out against the main background.
  4. The incised strips must be folded to the central part and glue applied to the fold line so that the corners remain intact.
  5. After completing work on the base of the postcard, it remains to make a tie. It is recommended to do it in contrast.
  6. The required length is measured on the selected sheet of paper, and then a rectangular shape is cut out.
  7. The resulting workpiece is fixed under the collar so that the lower part remains free.
  8. At the last stage, you need to attach the buttons, and the card is ready.

There are a huge number of options and instructions on how to make birthday cards with your own hands. This article presented only the most interesting and original ideas suitable for any occasion. As you can see from the text, all options are quite simple, and making them very easy. By the way, children can also help make a postcard; there is work for them too. The child is quite capable of helping to make the background of the postcard or gluing various decorative elements to the base. In addition, the presented crafts will not take much time for manufacturing, and the resulting gift will surely please the birthday boy on this bright day.
