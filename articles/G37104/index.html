Vikingen Infinity Resort SPA 5 * (Alanya): photos and reviews of tourists

Alanya, also called Alanya by tourists, is famous, first of all, as one of the most budget resorts on the Antalya coast. Therefore, those who are limited in means, can choose a very good option for a vacation here, having an ideal price-quality ratio.

In Alanya there are many hotels of various categories. Most of them are designed for families with children. The districts of Alanya - Avsallar, Konakli, Incekum, Mahmutlar, Karaburun and Okurcalar - are completely built up with hotels and guest houses. They have a fairly developed infrastructure, which has made the region a favorite place for many. Here, well-groomed sandy and mixed pebble beaches, a large selection of entertainment, while each area is beautiful in its own way.





Alanya is the southernmost Mediterranean resort on the entire coast of Turkey. The temperature of water and air here is always slightly higher than in neighboring tourist centers. The high season, when you can already swim, in Alanya begins in mid-March and ends in late autumn. The hottest are July and August, so it is better to come here with young children either in early June or in September until the first half of October.

Vikingen Infinity Resort 5 *

This chic hotel is located in the vicinity of the small village of Okurcalar, on the very shores of the Mediterranean Sea. It was built in 2012. According to the architects' plan, its two two-story buildings form together a curved horseshoe, which is especially beautifully seen from above.

Its vast territory - forty thousand square meters - is fully equipped. Lawns, paths, lights, numerous palm trees - all this contributes to a comfortable stay. The territory is decorated like a terrace, which gradually descends to the sea. The hotel is separated from the beach by a highway, there is an underpass.

The city of Alanya is fifteen kilometers from Vikingen Infinity Resort 5 *. The airport is an hour and a half away. There are many shops and restaurants near the hotel, there is a bus stop right at the entrance, from which buses run to Antalya every half hour.

Infrastructure

The territory of Vikingen Infinity Resort 5 * is thought out to the smallest detail. There is where to walk and play sports, sit in the shade, admiring the sea. The infrastructure of this five-star complex corresponds to its declared category. The administrative building has a cozy lobby, there is a beauty salon, a games room and Internet cafes, ATMs, souvenir and grocery stores. Concierge services are available. For the convenience of residents there is an exchange office and a left-luggage office.





A 24-hour check-in counter is available for check-in regardless of arrival time. If guests arrived earlier or later than the hours set by the hotel rules, then if they are available, they will be accepted immediately. Here you can order a taxi, if necessary, arrange a transfer to the airport.

After completing the documents, tourists are presented with a booklet with a full list of services provided in this hotel, as well as the schedule of restaurants and bars. Residents can use the photocopier at the registration desk or receive and send faxes. Those who prefer to independently move around the resort are offered to rent any vehicle, including bicycles.

On the ground floor of the main building there is also a tour desk. You can use the services of a guide to explore the many attractions of this region. Free Wi-Fi is available in the lobby. The hotel has convenient parking for cars. Large tour shuttles also stop there. No parking is required in advance.

Bathrooms

Bathrooms in Vikingen Infinity Resort SPA 5 * are combined. They have a shower, some apartments have a jacuzzi, washbasin, hair dryer, bathrobes and slippers. All necessary maid accessories are updated daily. All plumbing in bathrooms is new and modern. On the floor - ceramic non-slip tiles.

For children

Vikingen Infinity Resort SPA 5 * by tour operators is positioned as a family, therefore for small guests the administration provides a lot of services and entertainment. As well as for adults, a pool is also provided for babies, which always has special staff that monitors the safety of small customers. In addition, in the evenings, a disco is organized for them in the amphitheater, there is also a junior club. In the restaurant, for convenience of feeding, you can get a high chair, as well as dishes from a special menu by prior arrangement.

Free entertainment

The hotel is equipped with everything so that guests can combine relaxation with an active pastime. This is especially true for the pool with slides. There are comfortable tennis courts, a sauna, a Turkish hammam. Those who wish can play basketball, darts, ping pong.

Tourists reviews
