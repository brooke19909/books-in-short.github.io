The recreation center "Tatra", Nizhnevartovsk

Today, almost everyone can afford a vacation at a camp site. Nature, fresh air, barbecue ... It's inexpensive, and even the busiest city dweller can afford to relax in nature at least once a month in comfortable conditions.

Holiday Village "Tatra", Nizhnevartovsk

One of the most popular holiday destinations in the city of Nizhnevartovsk and its environs is the Tatra camp site. It is located on the lake and is surrounded by a picturesque forest. On the territory of the Nizhnevartovsk recreation center "Tatra" there are 15 houses with a capacity of 10 to 20 people and two large cottages for companies up to 40 people. All houses are furnished and equipped with the necessary equipment for a comfortable stay. Dishes are provided disposable sets for the number of guests. For special occasions, it is better to take your dishes. Also, visitors can use the sauna (electric sauna). Each of the 6 baths is equipped with a steam room and a cozy relaxation room.

Additional services at the base

In addition to the possibility of living in comfortable houses, guests can use additional services. On the territory of the recreation center "Tatra" (Nizhnevartovsk) there is a wide beach equipped with changing rooms and toilets. In addition, the recreation center provides additional services for the rental of sports equipment, playgrounds for paintball and volleyball. For fans of strength training there is a gym.

For winter recreation, not far from the base there is a ski track with a length of 2 kilometers and slides for sledding.

Prices for accommodation and services

A visit to the beach on the camp site without accommodation will cost 100 rubles per person. Everyone can visit it. Renting houses will be a little more expensive. From 5,000 rubles per day (a house for 10 people) to 15,000 rubles (a large house with a sauna, with a capacity of 20 people). The price will depend on the house itself, and the time spent at the base.

For a full set of paintball equipment will have to pay 1,600 rubles. Ski rental will cost 200 rubles for 2 hours. Children's skis will cost 100 rubles.

Reviews about the recreation center "Tatra" in Nizhnevartovsk

The city has a large number of tourist centers. But the Nizhnevartovsk recreation center "Tatra" is considered one of the most comfortable. At any time of the year there is something to do and even the smallest visitors will be satisfied.

However, nowhere is it without nuances. The main complaints of visitors relate to the attendants. Untimely cleaning of public toilets and houses spoils the impression of rest. In such situations, neither a landscaped area nor an additional range of services saves. This is especially true for holidays, when many guests come to the camp site. In addition, the Nizhnevartovsk recreation center "Tatra" does not poison midges on the beach. Therefore, going there for the weekend, stock up on insect repellent.

Having visited the Tatra tourist base in Nizhnevartovsk, you will be surprised how comfortable outdoor recreation can be. This is a great solution for those who can not afford to leave the city for a long time or just want to relax from the daily routine.
