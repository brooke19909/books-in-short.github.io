Can I find out the owner by card number: recommendations

Can I recognize the owner by card number? We have to find the answer to this question further. In general, sometimes such information may come in handy. For example, if a person found someone else's plastic. Returning it under similar circumstances is not difficult. But even without the name and surname of the card holder, you can return it to its owner. We will talk about this later. In fact, everything is not as difficult as it seems.





Chances of success

Can I find out the cardholder by card number? Or is it worth abandoning this venture?

It is impossible to unequivocally answer a similar question. The thing is that the chances of success directly depend on the specific situation. In some cases, obtaining information about the owner of the plastic is a simple task. And it happens that you can return the card without the data of a citizen.

Failed translations

Consider several different situations in which the above information is required. Is it possible to recognize the owner of the plastic by the card number, to which the funds were transferred unsuccessfully (by mistake)?

Yes, but not fully. For example, Sberbank can display only the name and patronymic of a citizen, as well as the first letter of a surname. That is, to understand 100% to whom the funds were transferred, will not work.

To get at least such information, you have to:

  1. Log in to Sberbank Online. If you use a card of another bank, you must select the appropriate banking.
  2. Start the operation to repeat the transfer of funds.
  3. Look at the column "Recipient".

There is no need to complete the transaction. Thus, a person will be able to indirectly recognize the middle name and name of the recipient of funds by the card number.





Banks and personal data

Is it possible to recognize the owner of plastic by the card number by contacting the bank directly? After all, it is in financial organizations that customer information is stored.

Under current law, last names, first names, patronymics and other data on citizens cooperating with banks are not disclosed. This is confidential information. And if you ask an employee of a financial institution to name the data of the plastic holder, the applicant will be denied legally.

The exception is law enforcement. They are provided with information about citizens by card numbers exclusively during certain investigations. Just like that, this information was not disclosed.

Card Types and Data

How to find out the owner by Sberbank card number? We have already met one rather interesting trick. But it is not exhaustive.

It is worth paying attention to the fact that the answer to the question under study directly depends on the type of card with which the client works. There is a nominal plastic and instant. In the first case, it is easier to understand who owns the card than in the second. After all, instant plastic reflects only technical information.

What can be found on the map

How to find out the full name of the owner of a plastic card? In fact, you cannot completely deal with this problem. It is impossible and illegal. There are some tricks, but about them later.





First, consider the information that can still be found by the number of bank plastic. She is not a secret. However, some information is encrypted in the card number itself.

So, by plastic you can find out:

  • payment system - the first digit (4 - "Visa", 5 - "MasterCard" and so on);
  • BIN of a financial institution - figures for the 6th inclusive;
  • Bank program, which issued plastic - 7 and 8 digits;
  • owner identifier - 9-15 digits (full name does not contain);
  • the check digit for checking the card is the last digit of the number.

That's all. As you can see, the plastic number can show detailed information about the issuing bank. But the data of the owner of the plastic is problematic to find out.

For name cards

Can I find out the owner through the card number? Name cards allow you to see only the name and surname of a citizen. That is, full information about the plastic is still not disclosed. And the card number has nothing to do with it.

The surname and name of the plastic holder are written in Latin on the front of the plastic card. Just look at the map and read what is written on it. This is the only way to somehow get a tip on whoever lost the plastic.





Instant cards, as we have said, instead of the name and surname contain only technical information. And therefore, the described technique will not help. On such products, there is simply no first name, last name, or middle name of the owner.
