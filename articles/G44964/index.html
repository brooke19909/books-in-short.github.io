Hotel Anna Hotel 2 * (Hersonissos, Greece): photos and reviews

Crete is the resort center of the Mediterranean, an island fond of the attention of travelers, a popular place for mass tourist pilgrimage in the summer.

This island has a very rich, eventful history with a length of more than 8 millennia. According to the myths of ancient Greece, it was he who stood at the origins of ancient European civilization, Olympus rose here, Zeus was born, flew Icarus, the monster lived the Minotaur. In subsequent eras, Crete was conquered by Arabs, Turks, Byzantines, Greeks again, the island retained the cultural imprint of each era. But even if you imagine that tourists in Crete are not interested in all this cultural diversity, it does not cease to be attractive as a resort. This is a very beautiful, flowering island with a pleasant climate, clear sea, good hotels, friendly people, delicious wines and the cleanest beaches in Europe.

Cretan Heraklion Resort

Heraklion is the capital of Crete and the center of the resort of the same name. He was named after the mythical hero Heracles, who, according to legend, performed one of his exploits in this city. This is a beautiful and colorful resort, which is also popular among tourists. Despite all its authenticity and island identity, it is very modern and dynamic, has a developed infrastructure. The warm sea, natural beauty, olive and grape groves, a rich historical past, an interesting cultural heritage, very friendly locals contribute to the revitalization of tourist flows to this popular resort.

Hotel Orientation

Anna Hotel 2 *, like most budget hotels in Crete, is ideal for tourists who prefer excursions and active entertainment in a place of rest, round-the-clock pastime in the walls of the hotel. For those travelers who perceive rest, first of all as absolute idleness, it is better to choose higher category hotels with all inclusive, animation, large rooms, SPA, etc. Such hotels also exist in Crete, but they are not so in demand among Russian tourists. Naturally, if someone is soberly aware that on vacation he wants to break away from work, rest and relax, he will most likely choose a hotel in Turkey, Thailand or Egypt. Unlike Greece and the Greek Islands, these countries do not require visas, they are cheaper, closer, and the range of tour offers in these areas is much wider.



But for those tourists who receive positive emotions from the knowledge of unfamiliar places, they like to visit attractions, unique places, find out how people live in different parts of the Planet, Crete is simply an ideal holiday destination. The excursion program on this island is unique, one of the most interesting in the world. On the island, many cult memorials of Ancient Hellas have been preserved, among them the Knossos Palace, the Arcadia Monastir, Mount Dikti, the Samali Gorge. And, in addition to high-profile recognizable sights, there are still many natural beauties in Crete, picturesque settlements with friendly residents, breathtaking local cuisine and many places of interest for productive and exciting shopping.



It is for tourists who do not plan to spend all their vacation time in the hotel, the Anna Hotel 2 * hotel is ideally suited, it has a very good location, different food systems, offers decent rooms at affordable prices. And for those tourists who have strong confidence that they want to spend their holidays cruising along the route beach-hotel-restaurant-hotel, they need to be more careful about choosing a hotel.

Beach

All beaches in Crete are municipal. Not a single stretch of beach belongs to a hotel. Even the most expensive hotels can offer their customers only access to the city beach. Each tourist can relax on any beach that he likes.

The nearest beach is 150 meters from the Anna Hotel 2 *. Photos on this beach are amazing. Unfortunately, according to tourists, this is where the benefits of it end. This is a public, pebble-stone beach strip, very narrow, rather wild. The place is very picturesque, but swimming is not always safe. More suitable for photo shoots on the background of cliffs, the sea and sunset, for barbecue, picnics and walks.

At 500 m. Anna Hotel 2 * (Hersonissos) from the hotel there is a city sandy beach - Star Beach. It is a comfortable, clean and well-equipped beach. Changing cabins, showers, toilets, lifeguard stations, shops, bars, pharmacies are present on the beach in sufficient quantities. Star Beach can be divided into several zones.
