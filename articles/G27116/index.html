Traditional Dymkovo toy: how to make plasticine with your own hands?

In the old days, in every city and large village of our immense homeland there was some special craft, and sometimes several. Having come up with something original and having achieved high-quality production in large enough volumes, artisans kept the secrets of their craftsmanship secretly and transmitted them exclusively by inheritance. Some ancient crafts have survived to our days, for example, everyone knows what a Dymkovo toy looks like. How to make plasticine figures reminiscent of the real products of masters from the village of Dymkovo?

Craft history



Clay is a material characterized by ductility in its raw form and acquiring a sufficiently high strength after firing. Due to the combination of such characteristics, clay products are incredibly popular from ancient times to the present day. In the old days, dishes and clay figures were made in almost all regions of our country. At the same time, in each locality, their own forms and copyright traditions of painting and decoration appeared. The village of Dymkovo (Kirov region) became famous throughout the country thanks to its craftsmen who make small bright figures that can make a variety of sounds. Curiously, the traditional Dymkovo toy was invented not for the fun of children, but for protection from evil spirits. In ancient times, it was believed that evil is afraid of loud noises. That is why in ancient times, all Dymkovo toys could rattle or whistle, they were traditionally made for the holiday of Whistle, during which it was supposed to expel evil spirits and have fun with the whole world.

Features of toys made by craftsmen from Dymkovo



What is the difference from all the other Dymkovo toy, how to make the most similar figure out of plasticine? Today, even in the village of Dymkovo, not only whistles and rattles are made, but also ordinary figurines, figurines that are not able to make loud noises. A distinctive feature of these toys can be considered a relatively small size. Masters, faithful to ancient traditions, usually make figures of animals, birds, young ladies, less often - men. However, quite often there are also difficult-to-form products - women holding a loaf in their hands, or good fellows on horseback. A simple pattern and the use of a small number of flowers in painting is what distinguishes a Dymkovo toy. How to make a beautiful do-it-yourself horse out of plasticine in the style of this folk craft? Everything is quite simple - the shape of the figure should be simple, and simple lines and dots are used to paint finished products. Note: in the traditional version, the background should be white, but today more and more often you can see toys painted in full with multi-colored paints.

Materials for home creativity

Creating a Dymkovo toy at home is a great idea for the creativity of children in collaboration with their parents. The most affordable and inexpensive material for modeling is clay. For the manufacture of figures in the traditions of the village of Dymkovo it would be more logical to use white material. But since plasticine is often sold in sets consisting of different colors, you can mix everything together and sculpt from the resulting mass. It is good to cover the finished figure with a primer, and only after it has dried, proceed to painting. Traditionally, in Dymkovo, toys are not varnished, leaving them with a matte texture. But if you use ordinary unstable paints, it makes sense to fix them additionally.

Useful tips for beginners



If the theme of your today's creative lesson is a Dymkovo toy made of plasticine, a photo of a finished figure, or even she herself as a model should always be before my eyes. It is especially important to have a sample for children, do not forget that babies do not have such a great life experience. If you want to decorate a finished toy with complex ornaments, be sure to try to make them on paper. In fact, combining circles and stripes you can achieve a variety of interesting effects, the main thing is to choose the right colors. Red, yellow, blue, green, orange - all these are the colors in which the Dymkovo toy is traditionally painted. How to make such a figure out of plasticine at home, you already know. If desired, in the process of painting, you can also use orange or pink paint, and highlight individual elements with gilding. Do not forget that you do not need to carefully draw a face, just outline it schematically.
