Season in Egypt. Reviews When the season begins in Egypt

You must admit that for many Egypt is an amazing place where you can have a pleasant and comfortable rest. This African country is one of the most popular points on the planet, a dream for tourists. Egypt is spread over the vast expanses of North Africa, so the climate here is very diverse. You can come to rest
almost at any time of the year, just choose which season in Egypt you prefer.





On the coast of the beautiful Red Sea there are a lot of comfortable hotels that are waiting for their guests all year round. The holiday season in Egypt can give not only fabulous memories of beach vacations, but also provide an opportunity to plunge into history. It was on the territory of this state that the great pharaohs ruled thousands of years ago, pyramids were erected and many interesting events took place. What could be more informative than acquaintance with the traditions and culture of one of the oldest civilizations in the world?

Many people wonder when the season begins in Egypt. In fact, as already mentioned, you can have a decent rest here at any time of the year, you just need to find out what exactly you want from the rest.





What to see

  1. One of the main highlights of Egypt is the Red Sea. Tourists not only swim and sunbathe on the beaches. For many, relaxing on the coast is an opportunity to go diving. The Red Sea is one of the best places on the planet for practicing this exciting sport. Luxurious corals, numerous schools of fish of incredible color, amazing mollusks ... Time under the water flies unnoticed. For those who do not want to dive under water, there is the opportunity to admire the exotic flora and fauna from above from a boat or boat. On board such a vessel you can not only sunbathe, but enjoy a meal of freshly caught seafood. Many tourists go on a boat trip in boats with a transparent bottom. The impressions of such a trip are simply stunning! And the most liked pictures can be fixed on a camera or camcorder.
  2. Egypt is rich in history. The first place in popularity, of course, is the Valley of the Pharaohs, where tourists can see firsthand the temple of Queen Hatshepsut, the colossi of Memnon and the monuments of the city of the dead. After that, you can go to the city of the living - a huge museum, spread out under the open sky.
  3. Vacationers do not disregard the capital of Egypt, the largest city in the African mainland - Cairo. How many names he has! This is the Gate of the East, and the city of a thousand minarets. Here you can see the magnificent pyramids, numerous treasures of the Egyptian Museum, the Great Sphinx. Those interested can visit the enchanting Perfume Museum, an exotic artisan bazaar and much more.

Climatic features

After the tourist has decided on a holiday program, you need to plan the time of departure. The beach season in Egypt lasts all year long. It remains to understand the nuances of climate for the months of the year.

December

Of all the winter months, December should be preferred. From December 1 to December 15, off-season takes place in Egypt, which means that the hotels are not crowded, the air temperature is about 28 degrees, the sea is warm. However, from the 20th day, residents of the Old World begin to come here for Catholic Christmas. Traditionally, the greatest demand for the period of the New Year holidays. The price of tickets is growing with their approach.

January

The season in Egypt, which begins immediately after the New Year, January 2-3, is becoming increasingly popular. From the beginning of the month until January 10-12, the most scarce tours are booked. After that, until the 20th day, one of the most inexpensive seasons of the year comes. The sea during this period has a temperature of about 22 degrees, air in the afternoon - about 25. There are few people in hotels, however, recently, due to the low price, this period has become more popular. A beautiful tan in the middle of winter among vacationers, even with a not too hot sun, is provided.

February

One of the lowest prices per year for this season in Egypt is due to the lowest (about 15 degrees) air temperature. Although many Russians continue to sunbathe and even swim. With the weather it may be unlucky, and the temperature drops to 10 degrees. However, it also happens that in February the air warms up to a comfortable 25 degrees.





March

Air temperature during this period rises, and with it the price of tickets rises every week. Strong winds often blow this month, sandstorms happen. The water cools completely, you won‚Äôt be able to get a special tan, however some tourists come here during the spring break with their children and on March 8th.

April

The air warms up to 30 degrees, the sea becomes much warmer. This helps to increase the number of visitors, hotels are filling, prices this season in Egypt are increasing significantly.

May

The weather in May is a real fairy tale! This is rightfully the best season in Egypt. However, during this period one of the highest prices of the year. May is not only a time of Russian holidays, but also of Arab holidays. The hotels are crowded, it is advisable to take care of the reservation in advance for this period. Last minute vouchers at this time will not succeed. However, in the second half of the month, prices begin to fall. The fact is that it was then that the rival of Egypt - Turkey - begins its season. This country is pulling a certain number of tourists, which affects the reduction in the price of tours. The number of flights is also significantly reduced, so you should familiarize yourself with the flight schedule in advance.





Summer (June, July, August)

Air temperature reaches 40 degrees. The Red Sea warms up in the summer to 28 degrees. If you decide to come to Egypt at this time, Sharm-El-Sheikh, the season in which lasts year-round, you will not lose. The refreshing sea breeze makes it easy to carry the heat. This resort is always very popular among tourists. Other areas of the country during the summer are not particularly popular. Far from the sea, the air temperature can jump up to 50 degrees! For thrifty travelers it will be interesting to know that at this time the cost of tours is significantly reduced. The same low prices are only in February and the end of May.

September

The weather is moderately hot, the water resembles fresh milk. The tourist season in Egypt at this time is gradually gaining momentum, prices are starting to rise. There are virtually no winds at this time. Many vacationers prefer to come to local resorts in September because of lower prices compared to Turkey.

October

Are you wondering when the season starts in Egypt? Come here in October - this is really the best month to relax in this country. Ideal temperatures, both water and air, contribute to an excellent mood. However, the prices for the tours are quite high.

November

In Europe, the sea is already cold, so those who want to sunbathe and swim rush to the Egyptian resorts. At this time, excellent weather persists here, the air is heated to 32 degrees, the sea to 25. Many vacationers come here during the autumn holidays with their children. At this time, beach holidays are gaining popularity, hotels are quite full. By the end of November, prices are starting to decline slightly.





Summing up, we can say that almost any month of the year is a season for Egypt. This amazing country is always ready to provide sea and sun for its vacationers, as well as a lot of all kinds of entertainment for every taste and size of the wallet. Arriving here at different times of the year, tourists can not only sunbathe and relax, but also replenish their luggage of knowledge about world culture, see with their own eyes the pyramids covered with ancient legends.
