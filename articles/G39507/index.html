Hammamet Serail, Tunisia

Description

Located in the tourist area, Hammamet Serail Hotel is located on the second line of the beach, five kilometers from the center of Hammamet. The large village of Sousse can be reached in forty minutes.

The territory on which the hotel is built is decorated in the form of a terrace, completely covered by a beautiful garden. Nearby there are many cafes and eateries, not far away is a distinctive market where fruits and vegetables are sold. Hammamet Serail infrastructure includes a lobby, a shopping center, a left-luggage office. There is also a laundry with dry cleaning, a souvenir shop, several boutiques, a conference room with the most modern equipment, a beauty salon, and a doctor's office. The hotel has its own parking, which must be reserved in advance.
The 24-hour reception desk has a safe, a rental car. Here you can arrange a transfer, including to the train station. The nearby airport terminal is 60 kilometers away, and Monatyr Airport can be reached in an hour.

Rooms

The housing stock of the hotel is equipped with 192 rooms of the categories "standard", "suite" and "family". In front of each of them there are balconies or terraces with comfortable furniture for relaxing. The rooms have a work desk, satellite channels broadcast by a flat-screen TV, a telephone, a safe, and a minibar for a fee. The combined bathrooms, decorated with modern tiles, are equipped with bathtubs; there is everything necessary for maintaining hygiene. On the floor - ceramic coating. Hammamet Serail has rooms for smokers as well as wheelchair users.

The hotel territory is air-conditioned, self-adjusting is possible in the rooms. An internet connection is available everywhere. For accommodation with pets you need to arrange in advance.

Nutrition

Hammamet Serail offers its guests meals based on the concepts of "full board" and "half board", offered as a buffet in the main restaurant "SOFRA". There is also a poolside snack bar, a Moorish cafe, a poolside snack bar and a beachfront bar. Local-made drinks for holidaymakers of the all-inclusive type are free of charge. In the lobby there is an impressive bar "DOKKANA", which offers expensive cocktails. Once a week, guests are offered a free barbecue party, held by the pool or on the beach.

Beach

The sandy beach is only four hundred meters from the Hammamet Serail. Those who wish can get on horse drawn carts, imbued with the exotic of local traditions. The beach is municipal, but the hotel has its own sector forty meters long with convenient access to the water. Sun loungers, umbrellas and mattresses are provided free of charge.

Additional Information



Hammamet Serail 4 * offers children a comfortable pool, an outdoor playground for children, as well as an extra crib in the room and babysitting services on request. Adults are presented with a wide variety of entertainment not only on site, but also on the beach. In addition to the pool, around which there are many sun loungers, there is a fitness center, jacuzzi, massage room, gym, hammam, wellness center, slot machines. You can play mini-football and golf, beach volleyball and table tennis for free. For riding, renting a pool table and water activities on the seashore you need to pay extra.



Guest reviews

All who stayed at this hotel are unanimous in their opinion: Hammamet Serail (Tunisia) is a comfortable family-run hotel with good service, quality food and nice staff. The only drawback, many consider the lack of a hair dryer in the room.
