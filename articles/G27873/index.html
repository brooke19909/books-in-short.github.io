Tinkoff Credit Systems: reviews and information



One of the well-known Russian banks - Tinkoff Credit Systems - has been working for the benefit of our citizens since 1994. The company got its current name thanks to the main shareholder Oleg Tinkov, who owns 68% of the shares. Until 2006, the bank was called Khimmashbank.

Tinkoff Credit Systems, a review of which an increasing number of people are interested in, works with its customers using advanced technologies, that is, through the Internet. At the moment, there are no branches of the Tinkoff Credit Systems bank. Service takes place exclusively via the Internet or by phone. It is worth noting that this method of service significantly facilitates contacting the bank. However, this system is not clear to everyone, for example, many middle-aged people still consider service via the Internet to be very unsteady.
