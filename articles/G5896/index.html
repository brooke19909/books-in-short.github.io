Prestige House 3 * (Bulgaria, Sunny Beach): hotel description, services, reviews

Sunny Beach is one of the largest and fastest growing resorts in Bulgaria. It is located near Burgas (35 km) and ancient Nessebar (3 km). Sunny Beach is recommended for families with children, as well as for active youth recreation.





For the convenience of vacationers, the resort offers more than 200 hotels and apart-hotels, many pizzerias, restaurants, taverns, discos and night clubs, cafes and casinos. One of the most popular accommodation options is the Prestige House 3 * hotel.

About the resort

  • The length of the beach strip is more than 10 km. It is covered with golden sand and natural dunes. Vacationers note a clean coast and the absence of dangerous fish; entry into the sea is convenient, even.
  • The landscape is flat, also very comfortable, there is no need to climb up or down.
  • The resort has more than 200 hotels and apart-hotels, many restaurants, taverns, pizzerias, discos, night clubs, cafes and casinos.
  • There are a variety of attractions: bowling, hydrojets, water parks, trampolines, bike rental, jeep safari, surfing, parachutes, yachting, diving.
  • Discotheques and clubs in Sunny Beach are famous all over the world, often there are popular DJs and performers in the world.
  • The resort features equipped playgrounds, corners for entertainment and games, children's pools.
  • There is a developed trading network - bazaars, shops representing art and craft products.
  • Buses run regularly to Burgas, Varna (regional centers) and Nessebar (ancient city).

Prestige House Hotel 3 * (Sunny Beach): meeting

The hotel is located in the central part of Sunny Beach, 200 meters from the beach and 100 meters from the central alley. Nearby are some of the most famous clubs ("Condor", "Gianni"), entertainment venues, water park. Small and positioned as homely, Prestige House 3 * hotel has well-decorated rooms and offers guests good conditions for relaxation and quality service.





Description

The year of construction of the Prestige House Hotel 3 * - 2006. The last repair was carried out in 2015. The area is 500 square meters. m. Placed in one 5-story building.

On the territory there is a swimming pool (outdoor), which includes an adult and a children's section. The rooms are air-conditioned. There is a playground for children.

Prestige House 3 * is fully consistent with the reputation of the resort as one of the best places for tourists. The hotel boasts a convenient location with superb service and modern in-room amenities. An airport shuttle and 24-hour room service are available. In Prestige House 3 * (Bulgaria) there is a garden that allows guests to enjoy relaxing under the canopy of trees. The efforts of the staff here provided all the conditions for creating an indelible impression on guests of their stay at the hotel.

Prestige House 3 *: reviews

Guests are very conflicted about the hotel. Some tourists positively characterize the service provided at Prestige House, but only in comparison with the conditions in Indian hotels, which, as they acknowledge, they do not want to remember. In general, the hotel is called a small clean place, with friendly and helpful staff. They note that the rooms have a dryer, a refrigerator and even a wash basin. Food is characterized as completely edible, no worse than in other hotels on this coast.

But basically, even the most unpretentious of the authors of the reviews believe that this hotel can be used exclusively for overnight. Why it is classified as family, many do not understand.

Population

Settlement occurs without delay. At the reception they immediately give out keys, a Wi-Fi password and a bag of water, sandwiches, an apple and an orange.

Territory

According to reviews, the territory of the hotel is small, with a very small pool. Some guests believe that the hotel has no territory at all. For children, a small playground is built in the backyard.

The hotel is located on the third street parallel to the sea. According to the reviews of some guests, in rooms where the windows face the road, you often feel the unpleasant smell of sewers and utility rooms from a neighboring hotel.

Town

Streets for walking in Sunny Beach, according to reviews, no. There is very little greenery, cars are parked without any order.

Rooms

According to reviews, the hotel rooms are spacious, bright. In the equipment: air conditioning, refrigerator, TV with Russian channels (free). The beds are large, but the mattresses are so old that they feel "all the ropes."





In general, the guest rooms are characterized as good. But still, the authors of the reviews call the many shortcomings that exist in them. All rooms feature stubborn dirt. Workers do not clean under sofas and beds, mattress covers do not change new guests upon arrival. Towels are changed after 3 days. Changing the bed often needs to be reminded.

The balconies in the rooms are very small, lovers to sit in the morning or in the evening with coffee or beer will be uncomfortable. There are no ropes, like clothes dryers, on the balcony.

The rooms have telephones, but, as reported by the reviewers, non-working. For two weeks, the master may not come to repair them.

The quality of the Internet in the rooms, according to reviews, is simply excellent. Wi-Fi catches even outside the hotel. Guests can watch movies online without any problems.

Nutrition

The authors of the reviews characterize the food as very meager, but fresh and tasty. From fruits only melons and watermelons. Cheese of one kind (similar to our sausage), several types of sausages. From two to five hours ice cream is offered.



A breakfast + dinner system is organized for meals (lunch is not provided). Breakfast is a standard set of products: omelet, fried eggs, tomatoes, sausages, cucumbers, breakfast cereal, jams, honey, yogurt, milk, apricots or cherries. For drinking there is: a cooler with water, a coffee machine. For dinner, meat (several types), vegetables, sausage, cheese, desserts, melon and watermelon are served.

Staff

The positive reviews especially noted the staff at the reception. They are always sincerely friendly and welcoming.

But guests express an absolutely opposite opinion about the owner of the hotel. So, some guests say that the owner can arrange a family holiday at the hotel with a lavish feast, his own guests, loud toasts, music, dancing and even karaoke.

Sea and beach

To the sea you need to walk 10 minutes on a pedestrian street. Some guests believe that with a child this is quite a long distance to overcome is not easy.

On the beach, one lounger costs 10 leva (about 330 rubles).

Conclusion

Many tourists believe that the Prestige Hotel is not suitable for families. Only the most unpretentious vacationers can settle here, and that is only to just spend the night.
