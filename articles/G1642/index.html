The idea for a photo shoot in the forest. Photoshoot in the forest in summer and autumn - beautiful ideas for inspiration

The forest is one of the wonders of nature and a wonderful canvas for the photographer. Within a few hours, he can change his appearance - from the mysterious and intimidating to the majestic and poetic. Need an idea for a photo shoot in the forest? We have a lot of them - look and be inspired to create your own masterpieces.

Forest Nymph

Immerse yourself in the atmosphere of fairy tales and magic. This idea for a photo shoot in the forest requires training and a professional level of skill with the photographer. So, you will need not only a detailed study of the image of the model - makeup, hairstyles, clothes, accessories, but also a difficult choice of a good location and time of day to achieve the desired lighting.

Of course, editing a picture in Photoshop will give effects that cannot be obtained simply during the shooting process, but the better the source material, the better the result.

Your fairy or nymph can be both a good-natured keeper of the forests, and their furious protector, gentle and carefree or saddened. Try to work not only at the foot of the trees, but experiment with frames on the branches.

Real magic

A fabulous photo shoot in the forest looks very interesting. Photos (ideas for creating which can be very diverse) will help you:

  • Little Red Riding Hood - not only little girls, but also adult girls are happy to try on this image. Often the components for him are already in the wardrobe, and finding a basket and filling it with yummy pastries is also easy. An interesting addition to the image is a gray dog. The modern Little Red Riding Hood is not afraid of the wolf, but drives on a leash.
  • Snow Queen - in winter it is a sin not to repeat this image. White furs, a crown, snowy eyelashes and a cold look are his mandatory attributes.
  • Snow White - a significant part of the action of the tale takes place in the forest. To more accurately recreate the image, you will have to spend money on a costume, however, you can simplify the idea and use just an elegant dress. The main thing is to leave red apples.

Natural beauty

The forest is conducive to sincerity. What could be more appropriate than pure, genuine beauty in the wild? Pay attention to this frame if you have a photo shoot planned in the forest in summer. The idea is quite simple to realize and the model in light dresses will feel more comfortable in the warm season.

You will need a minimum of props, so as not to distract attention from the environment. Makeup and hair model should be as natural as possible, no bright colors and intricate hairstyles.



Photosession in the summer forest

Ideas for such a shooting can be different - from simple ones to those that require professionalism and preparation:

  • A picnic on the lawn is always bright and beautiful. Especially if you bring along a large, juicy watermelon and a good mood.
  • Balloons are an easy way to add vibrant colors to your photo. But be careful with them, do not bring them close to coniferous trees and do not put them on the grass, otherwise your props will quickly burst.
  • Do you think that only comfortable and practical clothes will be appropriate in a fox? Fashion photographers, on the contrary, make nature the backdrop for exquisite outfits. If you have a beautiful airy dress, then you can do such an elegant photo shoot in the summer forest. Photo ideas can be different. A model can become a romantic princess or a forest fairy.

Autumn photoshoot in the forest

Ideas for shooting in this colorful season can be extremely simple. Use the rich colors of the season as a background for your masterpieces:

  • It blew cold? Look forward to the approaching winter in elegant warm clothes.
    This can be not only a coat with fur, but also a beautiful thick sweater or just a bright hat and scarf. The main thing is that the photographs should blow warmth and comfort.
  • The leaves thrown into the air is a classic of autumn photos. Interesting processing of the frame will allow us to present it in a new light.
  • Have you tried decorating your hair with leaves or weaving wreaths? The lush golden crown looks bright and elegant, and a small, woven from twigs - mysteriously and magically.

For lovers

Love story is a great idea for a photo shoot in the forest. Feel yourself away from the bustle of the city and show the sincerity of your feelings. Natural beauties will be an excellent background for them:

Blankets and umbrellas will be useful and bright props.
If you are lucky enough to find a beautiful broken tree, then try to beat him.
