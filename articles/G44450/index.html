Cap pattern for a newborn: to help mom and grandmother

Clothing for a baby who has just been born should be special. A pattern of a cap for a newborn will help to sew the much-needed wardrobe of the crumbs with their own hands.

The baby, born recently, needs constant care from adults. The baby needs to be fed, taken care of and hygiene on time, dressed in comfortable things. For example, caps and hats for crumbs have their own characteristics. They are sewn and knitted from hypoallergenic materials - cotton, linen, viscose or acrylic. The pattern of the cap for the newborn should be as close as possible to the size of the head so that unnecessary folds can form on the product that can damage the delicate skin of the baby. By the way, it is for the same reason that all the things that the child wears and which directly touch his body are sewn outwards on the front side. A bonnet knitted with knitting needles may have no seams at all, since knitting allows you to get the necessary configuration of the product without them by adding and adding loops, as well as knitting several loops into one.





Traditional bonnet

Patterns of children's bonnets may be different, but a traditional bonnet is sewn from four parts:

  • occipital part;
  • front lane;
  • two ties.

You can measure the baby‚Äôs head with a tailor‚Äôs meter. To build the occipital part, you need to measure from behind the distance from the ear to the ear and the height along the head from the top of the head to the beginning of the neck. According to these sizes, a pattern should be built on a piece of paper by rounding the upper corners so that the bonnet lies on the head. The anterior stripe is measured from the neck of the baby on the head to the neck on the other side. You should also measure the distance from the crown to the forehead. Based on these sizes, a pattern of the front strip of the bonnet is built, but small bevels are made on both sides for landing along the front line. Variations on the theme of bonnet

A cap pattern for a newborn is not necessarily a cap that ties to the head. Many parents prefer to dress their baby in knitted hats and caps. For such hats, cotton knitwear is suitable.





The pattern of such bonnet caps is the simplest. You can build it like this:

  • measure the circumference of the baby's head;
  • measure the distance from the eyebrows through the crown to the neck;
  • divide the head circumference in half - this will be the width of the pattern;
  • divide the size of the second in half - this will be the height of the pattern;
  • based on the data obtained, construct a rectangle, adding 1.5-2 centimeters to the freedom of fitting;
  • rounded corners for landing caps on the head;
  • add 5-7 centimeters in length - for the turn, which will help regulate the size of the cap.

Such a cap is sewn of two layers of knitwear for softness. If the cap is two-layer, then the seams are hidden between the layers, and if it is single-layer, then if the baby is very tiny, then the seams are best done outside. And if the child is already holding the head confidently enough, and his skin is already less tender, then you can sew a hat according to all the rules - seams must be made from the inside out. The pattern should be positioned so that the product stretches wide.

For the cap, which will look nice on the crumb, after the beginning of the rounding of the corners of the pattern, an elongated tail is drawn. It allows you to adjust the size of the cap, if you tie it, not too tightening, knot.

Do not stitch, so knit

Many mothers and grandmothers with the appearance of crumbs are wondering how to sew a bonnet. But after all, the first hats of a baby do not have to be sewn at all, they can be knitted. A bonnet knitted with knitting needles can be thin and delicate, or it can be a little thicker for cool days. Toddlers most likely need a bonnet with a so-called cut penguin. It consists of three parts. The middle toe of the cap protrudes on the forehead, and the other two cover the ears, protecting the baby from drafts. The knitting pattern of such a hat is unique, it is easily transformed in size and will serve to create hats for a child of any age.

Required:

  • children's yarn (in a skein of 50 grams should contain 216 meters of thread);
  • knitting needles number 2.

Dial 109 stitches. 1 row knit with facial loops. Further knit as desired - with the front or wrong side of the surface, hosiery or garter stitch - anyway. But knit each even row as follows: 1 edge loop, 1 add crossed from the broach, * 16 loops of the main knit, 3 loops together with one double broach, 16 loops of the main knit, 1 add crossed from the broach, 1 loop of the main knit, 1 add crossed from the broach *, from * to * should be repeated one more time, then knit 16 stitches of the main knit, 3 decrease, 16 stitches of the main, 1 add twisted from the broach. Close all loops simultaneously 15 centimeters from the edge of knitting. Sew the back seam, then assemble all the seams into a single whole, like the petals of a flower. From the lateral toes, you can collect loops, for example, 3 and 3, and knit, according to the principle of knitting, caps of a tie 14 centimeters long.

Such a cap can be knitted from colored yarn - you get stripes.
