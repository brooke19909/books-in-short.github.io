Hotel "Laguna" in Lipetsk: location, description of rooms, photos, visitor reviews

Hotel "Laguna" in Lipetsk is a modern institution that will satisfy any needs of city guests. In addition to comfortable living conditions, there are also opportunities for banquets and conferences. Guests can enjoy their time in the restaurant or relax in the sauna after a busy day.

Location

The address of the Hotel Laguna in Lipetsk is 1D Mira Square. It is on the banks of the Voronezh River, just a 4-minute walk from the beach. A walk to the city center takes about 10 minutes. You can get to the hotel as follows:

  • from the railway station - by buses No. 17, 317, 356;
  • from the bus station - by buses No. 25, 27, 30, 308, 308k, 351, 353;
  • from the airport - by taxi.

It is also worth noting a convenient location regarding such attractions:

  • Peter the Great Square (2 km);
  • Lipetsk pump room (2 km);
  • Metallurg Stadium (2 km);
  • Cathedral Square (2 km);
  • Drama Theater (2 km);
  • sports complex "Spartak" (3 km);
  • Bykhanov Garden (4 km);
  • Palace of Culture "Falcon" (6 km).

Phones of the Hotel Laguna in Lipetsk can be found on the official website.

Restaurant and Bar

Delicious food and quality drinks are provided by the Laguna Hotel in Lipetsk. This function is assigned to such institutions:

  • The restaurant complex includes several dining rooms for relaxing and banquets (from 30 to 100 people). Breakfast is served from 06:00 in the restaurant, and a business lunch is served from noon until 16:00. In the evening, the restaurant has a cozy atmosphere with pleasant music and subdued light, which is ideal for a romantic dinner or friendly gatherings.
  • Sky Bar - a panoramic restaurant on the top floor of the hotel. Through the glass walls, guests can enjoy the beautiful day and night views of the city. Here you can enjoy popular international dishes, as well as watch tepan shows. The facility is open from noon to midnight.
  • The lobby bar is a cozy establishment, divided into several zones. Here you can drink your favorite alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks, as well as a hearty snack.
