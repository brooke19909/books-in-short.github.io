Cesars Resort Bodrum 4 * (Turkey / Bodrum) - photos, prices and reviews of tourists from Russia

As you know, for over two decades, Turkey has been a favorite vacation spot of our compatriots. One of the most popular resorts in this country is Bodrum. After all, here a magnificent beach holiday goes well with a mild and healthy climate and a lot of interesting sights. So, if you decide to go on vacation to Bodrum, Cesars Resort Bodrum 4 * is exactly the hotel where you should stay. We offer today to learn more about this hotel and the services provided in it, as well as find out what impressions he left with tourists from Russia.





Where is the hotel

Hotel Cesars Resort Bodrum 4 * is located in Gumbet Bay. It is two kilometers from Bodrum. Milas Airport is 32 km away. Thus, you can get to the hotel in less than an hour after arrival, which is a big plus, especially if you travel with small children. Literally a hundred meters from the hotel there is an excellent sandy beach, where there is everything you need for a relaxing beach holiday, and for active pastime.





Cesars Resort Bodrum 4 *: photo, description

The hotel was opened in 1993. The last large-scale reconstruction in Cesars Resort Bodrum 4 * was carried out in 2008. The area is one and a half thousand square meters. The hotel itself is positioning itself as a great vacation spot for tourists who value quality, comfort and a relaxed atmosphere and prefer active entertainment.

Hotel Policies

In accordance with the internal rules of Cesars Resort Bodrum 4 * (Turkey), the settlement of newly arriving tourists is carried out from three in the afternoon. But if you arrive earlier, and the booked room will be already vacated by the previous guests and removed, then you will be settled in it immediately. On the day of departure, you must leave the hotel before 12 noon. Payment for accommodation and additional services should be made upon departure. You can pay in cash or with a plastic card of Visa or Mastercard systems. If you have already paid for your stay with a travel agency in Russia, at check out you will only need to pay for using additional paid services (if you needed them).

Since Cesars Resort Bodrum 4 * is a family hotel, children of any age are allowed here. As for tourists traveling with their four-legged pets, pets are not allowed at the hotel.

Sea beach

Hotel Cesars Resort Bodrum 4 * (Bodrum, Gumbet) has its own beach with fine and clean sand. Here, guests can use towels, sunbeds and umbrellas for free. If you want to take a mattress, then you will have to pay extra for it. The beach is very well maintained and clean. The entrance to the sea is gentle.

Cesars Resort Bodrum 4 * has a developed infrastructure designed to make the vacation of tourists as convenient, interesting and varied as possible. The hotel has ample parking for guests. Entertainment

On-site leisure facilities include an outdoor pool. Sunbeds and umbrellas are located near it, the use of which is free for guests. There is also a children's and indoor pool (the latter is seasonal).

People who prefer outdoor activities can play tennis or table tennis, basketball, beach volleyball, billiards, visit the gym. On the beach you can enjoy surfing, diving and other water sports. Cesars Resort Bodrum 4 *: tourist reviews

Overall comments from travelers staying at this hotel are positive. So, many praise the food at Caesars Resort. It is very diverse and tasty. Most tourists appreciated the fact that local chefs are not addicted to adding pepper and other seasonings to dishes, which is a sin in many Turkish hotels. In short, it‚Äôs impossible to stay hungry here. But experienced tourists recommend, whenever possible, to come at the beginning of lunch or dinner, so that later you do not stand in line for food.

The location of the hotel also made a good impression on vacationers - near streets with lots of shops, bars and nightclubs. If you do not want to spend the evening in your room, there is where to go. Pleased many and the hotel's own beach. Here you can always find free sunbeds. It is wonderful that the beach is sandy and always clean.





As for the shortcomings, the complaints of our compatriots were caused by not very high-quality cleaning of the rooms and a rare change of bed linen and towels. And this despite the regularly abandoned tip.

Also, some tourists from Russia and other CIS countries, especially those who had previously vacationed in Alanya and Antalya, were a little bewildered due to the fact that the hotel staff did not speak Russian. However, in English it is quite possible to explain. This is due to the fact that the main contingent of holidaymakers at the hotel is represented by local residents, while there are few Russian-speaking tourists here. Therefore, when explaining with staff, certain difficulties may arise.

The rest of the hotel made a favorable impression on vacationers. Many of those who visited here would recommend the hotel to their friends and relatives.
