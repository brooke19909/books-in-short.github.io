Park "Gardens of Dreams" in Krasnoyarsk: description and reviews

On June 28, 2014, the Dream Gardens park was opened in Krasnoyarsk. This was a great gift to the city, where there are so few squares, alleys and park areas. Now parents have a place to go with children in Krasnoyarsk . Yes, and the expanse of the children: they can ride on a variety of attractions, see beautiful animals, just walk along the alley for the handle with mom or dad.

Description of the park

If you still think where to go with children in Krasnoyarsk, be sure to visit the "Gardens of Dreams". Its organizers managed to place alpine slides, fountains, all kinds of attractions, a Japanese garden, pancake houses and two catering facilities in a small area. In addition, there are numerous arbors, swings, a shooting gallery, a statue of Buddha and a pond with real carps. There are even pens with rabbits, geese and goats. Bunnies and birds can even be fed. For this, feed is sold at the entrance for 40-50 rubles.

In addition to real animals in the landscape park "Gardens of Dreams" there are also all kinds of sculptures: Krasnoyarsk and Thai geese, female figures (though for some reason, bare). And also small houses equipped with a barbecue area. However, even trees and plants look so beautiful and wonderful here that you can admire them every day.





About the Garden of Dreams Park in Krasnoyarsk, reviews say that there are many different entertainments for children: free bicycles, toys, cars, trampolines, swings, special playgrounds for games. Adults will surely enjoy the huge lake in which koi carps splash in the thickets of water lilies.

You can see the fish from a wooden gazebo located on the shore. Only they are frightened by the children who throw coins and pebbles into the water, so they tend to hide somehow. Try not to offend carps and other animals, teach your children to be careful about the nature around them.

Why come here?

Park "Gardens of Dreams" in Krasnoyarsk is suitable not only for families with children. You should come here to spend a romantic evening with your loved one, to celebrate family holidays, to realize creative ideas, hiding in a secluded place, to take a picture against the background of the surrounding beauty, to conduct a business meeting. For all this, separate places are provided here, there is wi-fi.





Advantages and disadvantages

Park "Gardens of Dreams" in Krasnoyarsk is considered the best in the city. Everything here is very beautiful and cultural: a large number of flowers, including in pots, clean paths, ponds, streams, carved benches. Local flower beds are somewhat reminiscent of Thai. Everything here is well-groomed and pleasing to the eye. But this does not count all the advantages of a new park attraction, they can also be reckoned with:

  • the presence of both paid and free children's attractions;
  • the possibility of holding a fiesta in a hammock to quiet music;
  • feed animals (except goats, they apparently have weak stomachs);
  • arrange a gorgeous photo shoot on the background of the photo zone and the beautiful landscape;
  • the presence of cozy cafes in which you can always eat;
  • all kinds of promotions and discounts on paid services for regular customers, groups of 10 people, senior citizens and disabled people.

In addition, there is a free library in the Gardens of Dreams park in Krasnoyarsk, where you can read books on landscape design and gardening, as well as the market for garden plants. And all would be well, but without a "fly in the ointment" was also not complete. So, according to reviews of Krasnoyarsk residents, they do not like:

  • a small assortment of local cafes;
  • a small area for ordinary walks;
  • a fairly high price for tickets;
  • lack of shady places (planted trees have not yet grown).

Where is

The park is located at: st. Volunteer Brigade, 14. To get there, you need to drive to the supermarket "Commander", and turn left. Getting lost is hard. And any people you meet will definitely tell tourists where it is. As the famous proverb says: "Language will bring to Kiev."

As for the time of visit, to come to the "Gardens of Dreams" is any day from 9 to 21 hours. In the evening, of course, it is already dark, especially in winter, but you can look at an interesting illumination that creates a particularly mysterious and romantic atmosphere in the park, breathe fresh air, stay with your beloved man away from everyone, enjoying delicious dishes and the surrounding beauty.





Visit Prices

Free admission to the "Dream Gardens" is provided only for children who are under 3 years old. For older children, as well as senior citizens, it will cost 100 rubles. For everyone else - 220 rubles. The time of stay is not limited to anyone, the main thing is not to lose a ticket so that you do not have to pay a new visit fee.

In case you get hungry, you can always visit the pancake house, here the most expensive pancake is 200 rubles, the cheapest - 60 rubles. You will have to pay about 90 rubles for drinks. You can eat both inside the cafe and outside. Visitors often choose the second option: warm, fresh, beautiful and good, what else is needed? Guests of the Krasnoyarsk Territory must visit the park. The special atmosphere of coziness and hospitality created by the park employees will be remembered by vacationers for a long time.
