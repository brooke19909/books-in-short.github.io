Hotels (Dragobrat): description and reviews

Dragobrat is an actively developing ski resort in Transcarpathia (Ukraine). It is surrounded by the Svidovets ridge, which creates a kind of trap for snow, which often lingers on the slopes of the mountains until May. The elevation difference is approximately 500 meters with the highest point on Mount Bliznitsa (1,880 meters). It offers a magnificent view of the Carpathians and the highest point of Ukraine - Mount Goverla.

The resort is international, this status corresponds to its hotels. Dragobrat has more than ten ski slopes, which, according to international qualifications, are divided into red, blue and black. They are equipped with lifts, there are also freestyle platforms. The main routes are located on the slopes of Mount Stog.





Hotels in the tract Dragobrat

Hotels (Dragobrat) harmoniously fit into the Carpathian landscape among coniferous forests and alpine lawns.

  • Hotel "Adrenaline" is a modern, new hotel, located at the foot of the Stog Mountain. The four-story buildings have 70 rooms with the possibility of settling more than 200 people. Two meals a day are made in the hotel restaurant and are included in the price. After an intense day, you can relax in the sauna with pool, play billiards or table tennis.
  • The hotel and restaurant complex "Dryada" has 28 double rooms of increased comfort with the possibility of sharing (there are folding sofas). The chic restaurant for 110 people offers delicious Ukrainian and Hungarian dishes. The hotel has a modern nightclub with a DJ, and the bar serves drinks and cocktails to suit all tastes.

According to the guests, the presence of the club is not only a plus, lovers of silence should take this into account.



The recreation center "Dragobrat"

The recreation center "Dragobrat" is a large complex consisting of a five-story hotel and two cottages with eight rooms. It is located on an alpine lawn and is surrounded by a pine forest, four towing lifts of different lengths rise from here. Two meals a day are included in the room rate, in addition, there is a restaurant of Hutsul cuisine, a wooden hut and a cafe in the open air. In the universal sports hall you can enjoy playing sports, there is a billiard room and a sauna with a pool. It also organizes recreational activities and discos. The highlight of the recreation center is a ski training center, where, under the guidance of experienced instructors, you can undergo individual and group training. Children of almost any age can be put on skis, and there is always appropriate equipment for them at the box office.

Hotel Marina

Hotel Marina (Dragobrat) is located in the very center of the tract in the immediate vicinity of the main ski lift. In the five-story building, 31 rooms are designed to accommodate 83 people. This allows you to accept small tourist groups. The hotel provides rental of all equipment and equipment, such as sleighs and snowboards, as well as organizes drying and storage. The spacious restaurant "Marina" offers a comprehensive menu of dishes of national and European cuisine, you can also order children's menus and dietary dishes. The bar serves natural coffee, herbal tea and a variety of cocktails.

According to vacationers, the excellent location of the hotel, thoughtful shuttle service and the cordiality of the staff allow you to fully enjoy your vacation.



Mini-hotels of Dragobrat resort

Small hotels and hotels (Dragobrat has enough of them) are mostly wooden cottages with all amenities. This is usually a family business, and the owners put their whole soul into it. Especially lovers of a calm, measured rest and home comfort will like them.

  • Mini-hotel "Smerekova hata" is a two-story building with six rooms with all amenities. There is a banquet room with a fireplace on the ground floor, a barbecue area and a parking lot. Near a Russian wood-fired sauna with a pool.
  • Hotel "Gendarmes" (Dragobrat) is located a little off to the side, at the foot of Mount Gendarme, from which it got its name. In two new three-story buildings - 15 rooms for double and quadruple accommodation. Some rooms have shared facilities. On the ground floor of one building is a cozy dining room with fireplace. In another building there is a wood-fired bathhouse with a relaxation room. The nearest ski lift is 150 meters away, all the rest can be reached by car. The hotel also offers ski equipment rental, a small library, and organization of excursions.

According to guest reviews, this is a good middle class hotel with good food and attentive staff.



Dragobrat hotels in Yaseni

This is the nearest village near a ski resort with developed infrastructure. It is from here that all transfers to hotels and lifts in the tract Dragobrat are made. In the hotels of the village you can live much cheaper, and well-organized transport quickly get to the ski slopes.

  • Forward Hotel offers comfortable rooms with balconies for two, three and four people. It features a restaurant and lounge lounge with a bar. A big plus is the presence of an indoor swimming pool and sauna with the possibility of ordering massage. For active recreation there is a rental of sports equipment and fishing is organized.
  • Five Fireplaces Apartments is ideal for families or small groups of up to 8 people. Kitchens are equipped with necessary appliances and utensils. For entertainment, billiards and table tennis are provided. The children are provided with a games area with board games and a children's playground on the street.




Dragobrat Resort (hotels): reviews

Reviews of vacationers are mostly enthusiastic. The magnificent nature and natural snow allow you to relax and ski. Depending on the class of the hotel, there are complaints about the availability and pressure of hot water, as well as minor troubles in service. The food and dishes of national cuisine of different countries amaze with their assortment and will appeal to almost everyone. Professional skiers and snowboarders highly appreciate the complexity and preparedness of the tracks, as well as the ski jumps for freestyle.

In the resort of Dragobrat and its environs more than 60 hotels and mini-hotels offering their services, which gives a wide range for choice in terms of comfort and price. The cost of living in a double standard can range from 400 to a thousand hryvnia (860-2100 Russian rubles), depending on the season and the filling of the resort.

Fans of winter holidays should definitely come to the Carpathians (Dragobrat). Hotels made of ecological material with national colors, snow-covered ski slopes, beautiful nature - all will bring a lot of positive emotions and give a boost of vivacity for a whole year.
