National Children's Wellness Center "Zubrenok": description, photos, reviews

In the beautiful country of Belarus there is one wonderful place for children - this is the Zubrenok National Children's Educational and Health Center. It is located in a wonderful picturesque place, among lakes and forests, and over the years has been pleasing its young visitors with unforgettable impressions, bright and bright moments, as well as the comfort and tenderness with which they are completely surrounded there.

History of creation

In 1967, the Council of Ministers of the BSSR decided to create a pioneer camp, which was planned to be exploited very soon and assign it the status of a republican.





Thus, in 1969, a wellness center was solemnly opened to the sounds of pioneer horns, which is a Komsomol asset of all Belarusian schools and, since those days, has become a real happy country called "Childhood". In those days, it was considered a great reward for diligent behavior and good grades to receive a ticket as a reward to this camp.

This wellness center has come a long way in its formation and over many years of work has received universal approval from the public, has won the hearts of many girls and boys. Its territory and buildings underwent many transformations and reconstructions, and several more buildings were opened.

In 2010, by order of the president, he received a new status and was renamed the Zubrenok National Children's Health Center. But its territory continued to expand, and a little later began the construction of a new camp complex "Rainbow", consisting of six cottages. After that, the medical building and the pavilion were also redone, where the guys can play a variety of games. Last winter, a complete reconstruction of this place and all its structures was completed.

Description

The Zubrenok National Children's Health Center is located in a beautiful pine forest on the shores of a magical lake called Naroch, which is the largest in all of Belarus and has crystal clear water. Such unsurpassed surrounding nature makes it possible to make amazing walks every day on a variety of routes.





This camp is famous for its interesting health and educational programs for schoolchildren, so children's and youth vacations here will be just perfect.

The camp area occupies about 80 hectares of pine forest, so the children who have come here are surrounded by a healing climate that has a beneficial effect on overall well-being and health.

Children's Wellness Center "Zubrenok" has 56 modern facilities. It consists of: five dormitories, a game pavilion, a medical center, an administrative building, a house of creativity, a school, a canteen and other outbuildings.

Here, children from 6 to 17 years old are happy to relax. On the territory of this center will find for themselves entertainment and useful activities, without exception, from babies to teens.

What can be found in the center?

The Zubrenok National Children's Health Camp has a large pool, a bath complex, halls for concerts and dance competitions, a lounge where you can watch a variety of informative and interesting films, as well as a place for playing table tennis. In addition to all this, there are a couple of gyms on its territory, a room with computers, a special place where a full set of beautiful outfits for various theater productions is provided, and a stadium.

The Zubrenok Educational and Health Center, instead of its department with books, created the Intellectual Complex, where interesting training and competitions are held. Also in the summer, for a variety of interesting events, such zones as the “Friendship Square” and the “Green Theater” are used. They show a lot of productions and scenes.





Accommodations

At the same time, the Zubrenok National Children's Health Center on its territory can accept about 1060 children in the summer and about 650 people in the fall or spring.

Young visitors are divided into groups of twenty or thirty children. Throughout the entire time they are there, they become the sole responsibility of the teacher who has the appropriate qualifications and special training for working in institutions of this kind.

The Zubrenok Center provides an opportunity to rest and undergo wellness procedures on its territory for children from large or low-income families, orphans, toddlers and adolescents living in areas with poor ecology, as well as creatively gifted children and winners of various festivals. His rooms are in buildings with a block system. Each room is equipped with four beds and modern furniture, and for the convenience of holidaymakers there are household rooms where you can store food, iron clothes or warm food.

Six meals a day are organized in the camp: these are the first and second breakfasts, lunch, afternoon tea, dinner and food shortly before bedtime. Children eat in two cozy and comfortable dining rooms.





Wellness

Medical care for children takes place in a large special complex, as shown in many of his photos. The Zubrenok National Children's Health Center provides its vacationers with a full range of medical services. They can use the dental or physiotherapy room, as well as the hospital and clinic located there. A variety of massages and therapy will be provided to children, aimed at improving well-being and strengthening the child’s immunity.

The duties of the personnel of the health-improving department also include carrying out various preventive measures and monitoring the quality of food for children.

Sports and cultural activities

Over the many years of its existence, the Zubrenok National Health Center managed to achieve great success in many activities. He constantly interacts with a variety of creative teams and youth sports organizations. Complex-targeted and social programs are held here, such as: Young Talents, Patriot, Leader, and many others.

Children from different countries take part in international gatherings, competitions and forums held on the territory of this camp. The children's home of this center has about thirty different circles that can develop their young visitors in different creative directions, depending on the child’s favorite activities.

A fascinating excursion center was created for children, dedicated to the rural ancient culture and life of the Nesluch farm.





Learning process

If the visit to this camp falls on the school year, then for such cases, so as not to interrupt the education, there is a school where qualified personnel work and educates children according to general basic state standards. Lessons are held in special classes equipped with everything necessary for the implementation of this process.

The school week, as in regular schools, consists of five days, but the guys are not given homework. Various courses and electives are organized for everyone. "Zubrenok" independently provides books and all the necessary educational material to its students.





Reviews

According to all the children and their parents who were previously resting there, there is not a single drawback of Zubrenok (national children's health center). Reviews in his address come exclusively positive.

Children like that on its territory there are many different sights and interesting places, and their parents - that their child undergoes a variety of recreational activities, breathes fresh air and does not break away from the educational process. It is important that this camp, unlike many others, is open to its visitors almost all year round. Those who have visited there at least once, after this try to get there in the following seasons, as there is no better place for a child.
