Making an Autumn Crown

Autumn with its bright colors simply attracts you to go out. I would like to collect a bouquet of beautiful leaves and late flowers and build a herbarium. But you can make not only a bouquet of natural gifts, but also decoration. For example, the autumn crown. How to make such an unusual accessory, read below.

Rowan Crown



To build such a hat is not difficult. We will need a bezel, a glue gun, flowers, mountain ash and dry leaves. If you prepare the autumn crown in advance and want it to be preserved for a long time, then you will have to prepare the leaves in advance. They need to be ironed and allowed to dry thoroughly, preferably under pressure.

Getting to the manufacture of headgear. First, glue the branches of mountain ash. They are heavy and voluminous. Under them will be easy to hide the rest of the flowers and leaves. Now we put the main emphasis. We will do them with the help of large asters. They can be easily replaced with chrysanthemums or other flowers that grow in your country house or garden. When the accents are placed, we begin to stick the leaves. The main thing here is not to overdo it. 3-4 large sheets and 3 small ones are quite enough. The final effect is the gluing of small dried flowers. Autumn crown is ready.

Decoration on the head of small flowers



Such a simple and elegant decoration can serve as a crown at the autumn ball. With your own hands to create such an accessory is quite simple. We will again need a bezel, but this time - thin, wire. Our crown will be made of orchid flowers, barberry berries and willow branches. The picture shows a sample made of artificial flowers. You can create your headpiece in the same way or decorate it with living plants. We select flowers and berries in one color scheme. If you make a craft from artificial material, then it will be quite simple to paint.

We begin to manufacture. Glue flowers on the rim. They are large in the center and small on the sides. The remaining space needs to be filled with willow branches, on the ends of which we will tie with a wire berries. The interweaving can be chaotic and asymmetrical. The main thing is that in the end, flowers and berries should be evenly distributed.

Maple leaf crown



This is a pretty non-trivial way to make a hat. The autumn crown with us will consist of a cardboard rim, maple leaves and gold beads. The first step is to prepare the material. Dry the leaves and make a cardboard rim 4 cm wide. First, we glue the front row of leaves. We go around the rim and fasten them with a glue gun. Thus, a cardboard strip on both sides turned out to be surrounded by leaves. Next, glue the second row. Now we fix the leaves on the back side of the rim. Thus, we make the inside out beautiful. The final touch is gluing gold beads. It is advisable not to fasten them around the entire perimeter of the crown, it is better to make such an emphasis on the front.

Crown hat



Such a hat is simply adored by children. He replaces them with summer wreaths. Even a child can make a crown for an autumn ball of maple leaves. We will describe two methods for its manufacture. The first method is similar to weaving wreaths of dandelions. Only here, instead of legs of flowers, we will bend and add leaf branches.

The second method is more time consuming. For such a crown, you will have to make a cardboard base in the form of a circle. We will tie ribbons at its ends, which will make it easy to remove and put on a hat. Maple leaves will need to be ironed and dried first. And then, using a glue gun, fix on a paper rim. It is advisable to make the crown magnificent. Therefore, it is necessary to glue the leaves not in one, but in at least two rows.

Gothic crown



Such a hat looks pretty intimidating. But some girls achieve exactly this effect. It is very simple to make an autumn crown with your own hands in this style. We need thick wire, branches, electrical tape, paint, chains, beads and autumn leaves. Let's start manufacturing. To begin, twist the rim of wire. Then wrap it with a branch. For this purpose, soft and supple willow twigs can be used. Now, using electrical tape, we attach branches to the crown. It can be branched branches of an apple tree or mountain ash. When everything is ready, we paint our headpiece in black. It is advisable to use paint in the spray can for this purpose. So it turns out to evenly distribute the color pigment. After the crown has dried, we attach chains and beads to it. You can repeat the version from our sample, where one bead is in the middle, above the forehead, and the other two are located near the ears, or you can come up with your own version of the decor. The final effect is the stringing of dried leaves on the crown.

Crown of flowers, leaves of butterflies and ribbons



Such a hat is reminiscent of the one we talked about in the first paragraph. But only this version of the crown is more complex. Here we will use both live and artificial materials. How to make an autumn crown in this way? Let's get started. First, take a wide plastic rim, it will serve as the basis. Glue asters and chrysanthemums to it on hot glue. We will supplement the crown with both dried golden leaves and green plants. A special role in our headdress will play branches of grapes. We glue them on the back so that they fall beautifully on the back of the head. They need to weave beads and real berries. You need to decorate the finished crown with butterflies, dragonflies and colored ribbons. And remember that such a hat requires catchy makeup.
