The paradise of the reservoir Hamilton. Dream lake

Residents of Texas rightly consider the picturesque lake located in the Hamilton Pool Preserve to be one of the most fabulous views on earth, and thousands of tourists travel the long way to enjoy the beautiful pictures of a unique natural phenomenon.

Enchanting landscape

A half-open beautiful pond with a 15-meter waterfall was formed thousands of years ago after the collapse of the calcareous vault of a small river that made its way underground. It is as if the amazing lake consists of two parts: it lies under the open sky and is covered by a part of the preserved stone dome. The locals and visiting tourists are amazed by the natural, natural beauty of the Hamilton Pool. A small lake that emerges from the grotto always has an unusual bright green color, and a never-drying waterfall fills it with powerful streams.



The natural splendor of the lake

Incredible beauty, wild orchids with a bewitching aroma and thickets of ferns surround the fabulous reservoir of Hamilton. The lake makes you freeze with the admiration of even experienced travelers who watch how small fish and small turtles frolic in clear water. This magnificent place was noticed by Hollywood directors, and several episodes of popular films were filmed in this setting.

Virgin Cave View

Hamilton Pool Lake is surrounded by towering stone slabs, closely approaching it. This is a favorite place for swallows howling their nests in a lime grotto. The transverse rings of the walls of the ancient cave keep the memory of the level of once oozing water, huge stalactites adorn the ceiling of the grotto. The inner dome and walls are covered with eternally green moss, giving a virgin look, not touched by civilization. A truly fabulous view of the sunlit shore opens from the side of the dungeon.

National Park History

In the 19th century, a scenic plot of land was bought by an American whose brother was elected governor of Texas. And the name of the magical pond was given in honor of the Hamilton family. The lake, however, gained its fame with immigrants from Germany, who bought the territory from their brothers and tried to earn money on arranging a spa holiday.





Only 30 years ago, the state authorities thought about preserving the national heritage, because because of the huge number of people visiting the picturesque places, the ecological system of the park began to suffer. The incredibly popular Hamilton Lake was taken under the protection of the local administration, and now there is a reserve on the territory, the entrance to which costs only ten dollars.

Rules for visiting the reserve

Now there are strict rules, violations of which are severely punished by the authorities. It is forbidden to bring glass bottles to the lake, to make fire, to put up tents and even ride mountain bikes. Local youth, who chose the reservoir as their favorite vacation spot, liked to jump into the lake directly from the top of the waterfall, but now it is also not allowed. The use of raw water from the Hamilton basin is not allowed: the lake contains an increased number of microorganisms, as swallows make nests right under the arch of the underground grotto. A dedicated service takes samples daily for health-threatening bacteria.

Floods due to rains may not allow entry to a local attraction. Therefore, it is recommended that all tourists preliminary determine whether the lake is open for visits and swimming. The park is equipped with numerous lounges, open until 18 hours, where you can relax after a walk to the lake and watch the unique beauty created by mother nature.





By the way, everyone who visited this unique place notes that no ultra-clear photographs convey the extraordinary beauty of this miracle of nature.
