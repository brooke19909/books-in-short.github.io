Olenevka, Crimea, hotels near the sea: description and reviews

The beauties of Crimea are unique and diverse. Completely dissimilar landscapes are concentrated on a rather small peninsula. The exotic nature of the southern coast of Crimea is intertwined with the volcanic rocks of Karadag in the east and the endless steppe and sandy beaches in the west.

Western Crimea, pos. Deer

The westernmost point of Crimea is Tarkhankut. The peculiarity of these places is crystal clear water, white cliffs and clean air. This part of the peninsula is the least populated and wild.

The village of Olenevka is located closest to the cape, only five kilometers away. It is in the vicinity of this village that all the beauties that the Tarkhankut Peninsula is known for are located . It is an ideal holiday destination for those who love silence and solitude with nature. Due to the lack of industrial enterprises and large highways, this area is considered environmentally friendly.

This cozy village was chosen by divers, surfers and other amateurs and professionals. In 2016, the Crimea-Extreme festival was held for these sports in Olenevka. The sea on Tarhankut is incredibly clear, the entire underwater world is clearly visible.





In addition to the sea, Olenevka is washed by an estuary with salt water and healing mud. If you look at the map of the area from above, you can see that the village is located just on the shore of this estuary. A lane of the cleanest sandy beaches separates the estuary from the sea. The sand here, like in the Maldives, is snow-white. The beauty is incredible. The road to the sea beach from Olenevka will take about 10-15 minutes.

Olenevka, Crimea, hotels near the sea

In this wonderful village there are no large sanatoriums or boarding houses. The development of the resort began not very long ago, but every year it is being transformed. Infrastructure for vacationers is being formed, new hotels and holiday homes are being built, various entertainments are appearing.





If you like a quiet vacation in an ecologically clean place, then its amazing Olenevka (Crimea) will provide you. Hotels near the sea offer their guests comfortable accommodation at affordable prices. Accommodation here is for every taste and pocket - from cozy suites to affordable rooms in mini-hotels.

On the banks of the estuary are modern buildings - the buildings of hotels, hotels and holiday homes. Housing here is somewhat more expensive than in the private sector of the village of Olenevka (Crimea). The best hotels near the sea were built recently, but are popular, so it is advisable to book accommodation in advance.

Olenevka (Crimea), hotels near the sea, reviews of vacationers

There are a lot of housing in the village, you can list for a long time. But how to choose housing to your liking? To do this, read the reviews of vacationers. Tourists celebrate positive and negative points and thereby help determine the choice of a place to stay.

As for Olenevka herself, there are simply no negative reviews about her. The cleanliness of beaches, crystal water and the unique steppe air of these places amaze everyone without exception. Almost everyone notes the special cordiality of local residents and staff of all hotels, and this is important. Hospitality means a lot on vacation. After a decent welcome, I want to return again and again to places where you are welcome.
