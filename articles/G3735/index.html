The many faces of Haifa. Israel - a country combining Jewish traditions and European culture

There are a lot of beautiful cities in Israel, interesting not only for their amazing landscapes and favorable climate, but also for their cultural heritage and historical past. No wonder millions of tourists and pilgrims from all over the world come to the country every year. The cultural and industrial center of the region is considered the largest northern city of Haifa. Israel has prepared many unusual sights for foreigners, and some of them are located in this place. The city has its long history: it was founded by the Romans, and in the XIX century it became one of the largest ports in the country.



Haifa is divided into several parts: office, business and shopping areas. It has a very good location. Being not far from Tel Aviv, it is connected by rail with Jerusalem, Netanya, and Nahariya.
