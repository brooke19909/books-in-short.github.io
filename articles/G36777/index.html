Montenegro, Budva - Adriatic Travel Reviews

Favorable climatic conditions, rich cultural heritage, interesting historical and architectural monuments, picturesque nature attract travelers Montenegro. Budva receives only positive reviews from tourists, because it is one of the most popular European resorts that meets the requirements of the most capricious and demanding vacationers. The city is located in one of the most beautiful places in the country - on the Adriatic coast.



Montenegro boasts numerous seaside resorts . Budva receives enthusiastic reviews from guests for good reason, because this is one of the best resorts in the Adriatic. In the summer more than 200 thousand tourists come here. The coastline is a little over 11 thousand meters. The most popular beaches are Jaz, located outside the city, Mogren, Slavic beach. Many will love the trip to the island of St. Nicholas, which is also called Montenegrin Hawaii.

Montenegro is famous for many ancient cities . Budva ( traveler reviews are grateful for the pleasant moments of rest given) has interesting historical and architectural sights. This is one of the oldest cities on the entire Adriatic coast, the first memories of it date back to the 5th century BC. To learn more about the historical past of Budva, you should go to the Old part, where the largest number of interesting structures are located. Many will be interested to see the old churches, the seaside citadel of the 15th century, the fortress gates with the wall and towers that Montenegro has been guarding for many centuries.



Budva, reviews of which are full of stories about the beauty of the city, has retained its rich culture. In museums you can see the work of many famous artists, unique expositions. Of interest is the Orthodox Church of the Holy Trinity, built in the Byzantine style, as well as the church of St. Mary, built in 840 on the basis of the ancient monastery. Separate city buildings also look very unusual, they are built of hewn stone and decorated with elements of the Baroque style. The church of St. Ivan, built in the 7th century, is known for its bell tower.

From April to October, the weather here pleases the guests of the city. Montenegro, Budva in particular, stands out for its many sunny days. Various cultural and entertainment events are held here annually. In summer, poetry and theater festivals are held, as well as a festival of classical music. For almost all tourists, regardless of age, nature and personal preferences, Budva (Montenegro) is suitable. Prices for housing, food and entertainment are not sky-high, so everyone with an average income can afford to relax on the Adriatic coast.



Young people will enjoy the rich nightlife, the city has many nightclubs, discos with incendiary music. You can spend the evening in one of the cozy restaurants in Budva to enjoy delicious wines and local dishes. Cultural lovers are advised to visit literary or music evenings.
