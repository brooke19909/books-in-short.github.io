Pyatigorsk hotels: addresses, description of rooms, reviews

The largest city located on the territory of the Caucasian Mineral Waters is the picturesque Pyatigorsk, which has long been known as a wonderful medical resort. The city received its name in honor of Mount Beshtau. It translates as "five mountains." An unusual view opens from its top - on a clear day from here you can see the snowy peaks of the Caucasus Range, almost all resort towns, blue mirrors of lakes.

Of all resorts, KMV Pyatigorsk has a wide variety of healing natural resources. There are more than forty mineral springs, which have different water temperatures and their chemical composition.



Geographical position

The picturesque and hospitable Pyatigorsk is located in the Stavropol Territory, in the center of the CMS, on the banks of the Podkumka River and on the slopes of the Goryachaya and Mashuk mountains. The highest point in the vicinity of the city is Mount Beshtau (1401.2 m.). The main spa facilities are located in the eastern part of the city.

Where to stay?

All travelers must face this question. The only exceptions are city guests who have come to rest or receive a treatment package. Just want to reassure tourists traveling to this glorious resort on their own - you will not have problems with accommodation.

Along with a huge selection of offers from private owners, guests can choose hotels in the city that will meet the most stringent requirements of travelers. Here you can choose housing of different levels of comfort and at different prices.

Beshtau (Boulevard, 17)

Most of the hotels in Pyatigorsk are concentrated in the city center, while the cozy 4-star Beshtau is located in the business district. It is five kilometers from the city center and a short drive from Mineralnye Vody Airport.

Hotel "Beshtau" (Pyatigorsk) offers rooms of any category of comfort. They have individual air conditioning, balconies or verandas, safes, minibars and fireplaces. The deluxe rooms have bathrooms, with all toiletries, the remaining rooms have showers.



Hotel "Beshtau" (Pyatigorsk) offers vacationers a 24-hour restaurant that offers delicious Russian cuisine. The bar offers light snacks, fruits, and coffee. Guests are provided with an alarm service, for convenience - a 24-hour reception, free parking and a garage.

Intourist (Lenin Square, 13)

In recent years, numerous new hotels in Pyatigorsk have appeared. The city is actively developing. Nevertheless, many tourists prefer to stay in time-tested hotels that have already proven themselves. These include the Intourist Congress Hotel (Pyatigorsk). The hotel is located at the foot of the magnificent Mount Mashuk, in the business, commercial and administrative center of the city.

Its infrastructure is universal - it allows not only good rest, but also fruitful work. Hotel Intourist (Pyatigorsk) offers guests accommodation in one of one hundred and fifty comfortable rooms. Of these, 52 - two-room suites, 32 single rooms, 64 - double. They are all equipped with functional, modern furniture. The deluxe rooms have a comfortable bathroom with a jacuzzi, while the rest have ordinary bathtubs or showers.



All living quarters are equipped with satellite television, a telephone with internal and long distance communications, and free Internet. Going to the window in the room, you can see the gray-haired Elbrus - the highest mountain in Europe - and the Caucasus Range, as well as a panorama of Pyatigorsk from here.

"Sport" (Dunaevsky, 5)

Hotel Sport has 45 comfortable rooms. It is located in the center of the spa town. All hotel rooms (regardless of price category) are spacious and bright, equipped with modern appliances and everything you need for a good rest.



Guests who have arrived in the city on business can use the business center with a small but comfortable meeting room equipped with technical equipment. Hotel Sport on its territory has a restaurant that serves dishes of both Russian and European cuisine. In the banquet room you can hold special events, mark important dates. Connoisseurs of a healthy lifestyle can go to the modern gym, do fitness, visit the Russian sauna.

Apart-hotel Compliment (Partizanskaya, 4)

Many hotels in Pyatigorsk are convenient for people who came for treatment without a ticket. For example, the compliment apartment hotel, located in the resort area, at the foot of Mount Mashuk. Nearby are the Lower Radon Hospital, Narzan Gallery.

The living areas are air-conditioned. Each room has an individual kitchen, which has functional furniture, a microwave, a refrigerator, cutlery. Guests arriving by car can park it for free.



Hotel "Lost Paradise" (Lermontov Razdez, 2)

The Lost Paradise Hotel is located near a historical place - the infamous duel of M. Yu. Lermontov at the foot of Mount Mashuk. The hotel is located in a beautiful forest, near thermal springs. Despite the apparent remoteness, the city center can be reached in 5 minutes.

The territory of the hotel is ennobled. In summer, guests can visit the outdoor pool, relax in the English garden. All rooms are modernly decorated and equipped with everything necessary for living.



Hotel "Gallery Palace" (Gagarina Ave., 2)

This hotel is very favorably located - in the center of the park area, on the slope of the picturesque Mount Mashuk. The hotel occupies an impressive territory (one hectare). A pump room with healing mineral drinking water functions on it.

Guests are invited to settle in a three-story building. In addition to the rooms, there are a dining room, massage rooms, a Turkish bath, a sauna, and a gym. In addition, there is a meeting room and a conference room.



On each floor there are cozy rooms. All are connected to central air conditioning, equipped with LCD TVs, individual safes, refrigerators, etc. All rooms have comfortable furniture. The hotel has a guarded parking lot, its own park area with a playground and gazebos. Visitor security is provided by a 24-hour security service.

Tourists reviews

All the hotels in Pyatigorsk listed above receive very different reviews. Mostly guests are satisfied with the accommodation. Most of all kind words are addressed to the teams of the Intourist, Beshtau, Hayat and Gallery Palace hotels. Ideal conditions for living are created here - beautiful rooms, attentive and friendly staff, excellent cuisine in restaurants and cafes.

But the reviews about the Sport Hotel are not so positive. There are many complaints about room service - cleaning once a week, a rare change of bedding. According to guests, in this hotel you can stay for one or two nights, no longer. We hope that the hotel administration will hear the opinion of its guests and eliminate the existing shortcomings.
