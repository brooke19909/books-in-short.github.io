Holiday Village "Lebyazhye" (Kazan): description and reviews

The country club Lebyazhye is located in the Kirov region, not far from Kazan. This place is quite popular among both the local population and visitors. Just twenty minutes from the city center - and you find yourself among the stunning nature and lots of entertainment.

Country Club Description





The territory of the Lebyazhye recreation center extends between two lakes: Lebyazhye and Glubokoe. Around extends a protected natural area. The territory provides everything for a comfortable stay in the family circle, with friends and colleagues.

Facilities and service

On the territory of the Lebyazhye recreation center (Kazan), the following types of services are available to vacationers:

  • various types of outdoor activities;
  • visit to the bath and sauna;
  • rental of the bath complex (the bath on the ground floor accommodates up to 8 people, and the whole complex - up to 18 guests);
  • organization of various types of events;
  • rent a large banquet hall (can accommodate up to 250 people);
  • rent of tent areas;
  • conference room rental (for up to 50 people);
  • a playground for the smallest with a lot of attractions;
  • guarded parking lot (50 rubles per day);
  • security throughout;
  • wireless Internet;
  • transfer (a comfortable minibus can pick up a group of vacationers from anywhere in the city and take it back).

Meals are provided in two cafes. Guests are offered a menu with a wide variety of dishes. There is even vegetarian cuisine. For vacationers, the organization of two and three meals a day is provided. For those who want to spend all time outdoor recreation, a spacious terrace "Solar" is provided. Here, in the fresh air, you can enjoy barbecue, drink soft drinks, listen to pleasant music. After lunch, karaoke is open and DJs work.

Overnight options





On the territory of the Lebyazhye base you can book a hotel room or rent a whole house. Cottages from a log house are designed for up to 9 people. The houses are equipped with everything necessary for a comfortable stay: a set of furniture (double beds, bedside tables, wardrobes, tables and chairs, sofas), a TV, a fridge, a microwave, an electric kettle, dishes and a tea set. The cottages have a small kitchen area, as well as a bathroom with a shower. The interior is in an authentic style, and the windows offer stunning views of the surrounding nature.

The hotel can stay in single and double rooms. One apartment can accommodate a maximum of six people. A set of furniture and appliances is the same as in individual houses. Some rooms have bunk beds for children.

Prices at the Lebyazhye recreation center (Kazan) for accommodation in houses start from 3,000 rubles per day from two people. A stay in hotel rooms costs the same.

Leisure activities





On a large territory of the recreation center, everyone can find something for themselves to their liking. Here they offer:

  • swim in the outdoor pool;
  • play football, volleyball, tennis on specially equipped venues;
  • try your hand at paintball;
  • feel the adrenaline rush in the rope park;
  • rent a catamaran.

In winter, there are opportunities to skate on a prepared skating rink, to travel around the territory on skis or a snowmobile, to play real hockey.

You can dance and enjoy local cuisine at the caf√© and tent. Also, vacationers have the opportunity to rent a personal gazebo with everything necessary for cooking barbecue.

Guest reviews about the recreation center





Every year Lebyazhye is visited by a large number of guests. Some people come here not only for the weekend, others live here for weeks, and someone prefers to celebrate holidays in a country club. Naturally, many different comments have accumulated over the entire time. If we analyze all the reviews about the recreation center "Lebyazhye" (Kazan), then we can highlight such positive points:

  • The territory is very large, well-groomed, clean. Garbage bins are provided everywhere.
  • Very convenient location, the base is located near the city.
  • Each room has shower sets (gels, shampoos, soap, etc.), bathrobes, slippers, and even dishes.
  • Garbage is taken out of the houses daily.
  • Housekeeping and the porch take place every two days.
  • Hot and cold water in the rooms is uninterrupted.
  • The food is hearty and very tasty, the dishes are at home.
  • A very good entertainment program for children. There are playgrounds.
  • Large selection of outdoor activities. I am glad that you can not only fry kebabs and steam in the bath.
  • Barbecue areas are equipped with everything necessary.
  • Great place to spend your holidays. The organizers work here at the highest level, think through each item of the entertainment program. This is especially true for the New Year holidays.
  • From time to time, various interesting seminars and master classes are held at the base.

Among the negative aspects, vacationers note the following:

  • In some rooms, a bad smell emanates from the bathroom.
  • Security can confuse with a simple passerby, scold and shout.
  • Wireless internet is available throughout the base. In most cases, you can access the Network in a cafe or near the reception.
  • Sometimes even booked rooms can be occupied by other vacationers.

Country Club Location





The address of the Lebyazhye recreation center: Kazan, Kirovsky district, Lake Lebyazhye street, 3. You can get out of the city both by personal transport and public transport. The distance is only 12 km. By car, drive along the Gorky Highway towards the Kirov District. Then turn right onto Lake Lebyazhye. If you go by bus, you need to take the route number 36, 46 or 72.
