Golden 5 Topaz Suites Hotel 4 *: reviews, photos

Many tourists dream of visiting the Red Sea. And their desire is well founded. After all, here you can not only bask in the hot rays of the sun, but also enjoy the amazing world of indescribable flora and fauna that hides in the depths of this sea. It is worth noting how wonderful and hospitable there are, for example, in Hurghada (Egypt) hotels. Golden 5 Topaz Suites Hotel is one of such wonderful places where tourists will be provided with everything necessary for an excellent comfortable stay.

This hotel is very popular among tourists from around the world, including from the Russian Federation. And many, of course, are interested in as much as possible detailed information about where the Golden 5 Topaz hotel is located, what is on its territory and what rooms are offered to guests. A large number of people are often interested in whether there is an opportunity and conditions for relaxing with kids. All this information about Golden 5 * Topaz, reviews about it and its photos can be easily found in this article.

Chain Egyptian Hotels Golden 5 City

The story about the Golden 5 Topaz hotel should certainly start with the fact that it is part of a huge hotel complex called Golden 5 City, which is owned by the Egyptian company Princess Egypt Hotels.





This is really a real city, on the territory of which there are as many as seven hotels of the highest star categories. There is a 5 * hotel (deluxe), as well as simpler hotels: 4 and 5 stars. Thanks to this, tourists of all kinds of incomes can be in this huge complex.

Where is the hotel Golden 5 Topaz located

Very close to the Egyptian resort city of Hurghada, on the very coast of the Red Sea, there are a large number of hotels that flock to tourists from all over the world. Among them was built a modern hotel called Golden 5 Topaz Hotel 4 *. It is located at a distance of about 17 km from the city center and its various shops, shops and cafes. So, it will not be difficult for vacationers to get out of the hotel territory one day, take a taxi or public transport and visit Hurghada itself.

The path to the hotel from the international airport will take literally 20 or 30 minutes, because you will need to drive only 10 km. Such a short trip is especially relevant for those guests of the resort who got off the plane after a very long flight and want to quickly be in a comfortable room.

Golden 5 Topaz 4 * is located across the street from the main entrance to the entire Golden 5 City. Next to it is also another hotel of this chain. This is Golden 5 Sapphire Suites Hotel 4 *. Thus, if you are guided by generally accepted characteristics, these two hotels are built on the second coastline.





In the immediate vicinity of Golden 5 Topaz Suites, there is a street with small shops, a variety of bars and restaurants. And five minutes away by transport there is a shopping and entertainment center. So everything you need for relaxation can be found not only in the vast territory of the hotel itself, but also next to it.

Overview of the hotel, the territory and its photo

Before the gaze of all the tourists who came to rest in Egypt, Golden 5 Topaz appears in the form of two luxurious snow-white buildings in two and three floors. They were built in 2009, and therefore still look just like new. The central part of the two-story building and a small part of the central facade of the three-story building are completely covered with glass, due to which there is a tremendous effect of flicker and glow when the sun's rays fall on these numerous glasses. To make sure the buildings are beautiful, such a description will be enough for some of the potential guests of the Golden 5 Topaz Suites Hotel. Photos of the hotel will allow everyone to personally see all its originality.





The territory, which is directly adjacent to the hotel, creates a luxurious and unique oasis around it, filled with rich green colors, numerous shrubs and palm trees. There are tennis courts and a volleyball court, a special club for children and a playground, a conference room and a place for playing billiards with equipped tables. There are also three restaurants at the Golden 5 Topaz Hotel, a lobby bar and a poolside bar.

There is a large circular pool, near which there are umbrellas and a large number of sun loungers. In the winter, it warms up, so that even when it is cool outside, guests will still be able to swim in it. There is also a special children's pool in which all the young visitors of the Golden 5 Topaz 4 * hotel will feel great.





All the entertainment and services, restaurants and bars located on the main territory of the Golden 5 City complex are also available to vacationers at any time. It even has a large water park with many diverse slides.

What number of rooms is offered to guests

Since both buildings of the Golden 5 Topaz are not very large, the number of rooms here is also small. There are 226 of them, among which there are the following categories: family, suites and standard apartments.

Regardless of the type chosen, any room will be bright and spacious, furnished with new furniture and appliances. Be sure to have spacious comfortable beds, bedside tables, wardrobes, tables with chairs, armchairs. The bathroom, as in many hotels, is combined with a toilet, but it necessarily has a spacious bathtub, hairdryer and a set of necessary cosmetic accessories. From the balcony, which is present in any of the rooms, offers a wonderful view of the pool or hotel garden.





Of technology, vacationers are provided with a TV with many satellite channels, a modern split system, thanks to which the room can not only be cooled, but also heated. This is especially valuable in the winter months, because this time in Egypt is very cold evenings and nights. There is always a minibar, which is filled with drinking water, juices and soda before each guest enters the room. The rooms of Golden 5 Topaz Suites 4 * also have a telephone for communication with the outside world and a reliable safe in which you can store something valuable and important.

Guests of any type of room can make their own tea or coffee at any time convenient for them, thanks to the special kits available in their apartments and an electric kettle. But you can use the 24-hour room service or laundry services at an additional cost.

What food is available for vacationers?

Any potential vacationer will certainly have a question about food. This problem is especially relevant when traveling to a distant country in which the food may not be the same as what we are used to at home. However, tourists may not worry about this if they decide to go on vacation to Egypt. Golden 5 Topaz, like almost most hotels of this country on the Red Sea, provides its guests with fresh and tasty products on an all-inclusive basis. Thanks to this service, anyone will not only be full throughout the day, but will also be able to use any drinks, including alcoholic drinks, but only locally produced for free. Of course, there is also imported alcohol in the hotel, but you will have to pay extra for it separately.

For three main meals, tourists are invited to a restaurant called La Perla, which is the main one at this hotel. It is designed for 320 people at the same time and offers a variety of dishes of international cuisine, so no one will definitely leave the hungry. By the way, any of the hotel guests can also eat in the main restaurants of other hotels in the complex.

On the territory of Golden 5 Topaz there are also two specialized restaurants - La Luna (Italian cuisine) and Mumbai (Indian cuisine). There are five more similar establishments in the entire hotel complex. Any guest has the opportunity to visit each of them for free once, but according to the preliminary reserve of the table. But in the a la carte restaurant called Sea Food, which is located in the Golden 5 Emerald hotel, you will have to go only at your own expense and also by a prior appointment.





Guests can enjoy light snacks and a drink in the pool bar called La Brasseri. He works from 10 a.m. to 5 p.m. In the lobby, the bar works much longer: from 10 am to 2 nights, but in the billiard room - only from 18:30 to 23:00. But these two bars serve exceptionally varied drinks.

However, this is by no means the entire list of bars that are available to guests of the Golden 5 Topaz Hotel (Hurghada). The beach, owned by the hotel complex, has its own institution of a similar plan, in which you can taste delicious pizza or pasta, as well as a variety of drinks with or without alcohol. There are also two bars near the water park and others, scattered throughout the complex. They work from morning to sunset and daily offer light refreshments and drinks to vacationers.

What is a hotel beach?

You should talk about the beach at Golden 5 City separately. This is a spacious sandy beach, the length of which is as much as one and a half kilometers. It has everything for a comfortable stay by the water: umbrellas, sun loungers and towels are provided for free. Right next to the sea you can play volleyball or squash, as well as spend time watching animation programs. The sand on the beach is clean and soft, but still have to get special slippers. After all, there are small coral reefs here, to admire which is one great pleasure for many vacationers.





Golden 5 Topaz is approximately 800 meters from the beach. You can easily overcome this distance on foot, enjoying beautiful views of the rich territory of the complex, or use special free transport that runs quite often between the hotel and the beach.

Entertainment at the Golden 5 Topaz Hotel

On such a large territory it is very difficult to get bored, especially since there are plenty of various entertainments and activities for leisure. The complex has its own water sports center, where for a fee you can go windsurfing, snorkelling or take an exciting boat trip on a yacht or other swimming facility. It is possible to completely plunge into the underwater world, using the services of a diving center.

For fans of entertainment on land, horseback riding, biking or typewriting are also offered for some money. You can even rent a limo here. A visit to the wellness center is also paid, but here you can get a quality full body massage, relax in the jacuzzi and go to the sauna or steam room.

You can also visit the gym, where there is first-class equipment so that any tourist can stay in top shape. And near one of the hotel's pools there are 5 water slides from which you can freely ride for both children and adults.

Be sure to go to the water park

Great entertainment for guests of all ages will be the Aqua Planet water park, which recently opened its doors to all vacationers in the Golden 5 City hotel complex.

Its total area is just over 26 thousand square meters, and it can accommodate about 3000 visitors at a time. Here everyone will find entertainment to their liking. After all, the local slides of different heights and complexity are suitable for both extreme lovers and those who like to relax more peacefully. And in the waters of a slowly flowing special river, you can slowly ride along the entire perimeter of the water park. There is also a simple recreation area near the pool, restaurants and fast food bars, and, of course, a special town created especially for young guests of the complex.





Is it possible to relax with children in the hotel?

Since now so many are sent to foreign countries with their kids, this question can not be left aside. The Golden 5 Topaz Hotel has all the conditions for families with children of all ages to feel comfortable throughout their stay.

One crib can be put in each room for free, the subsequent ones will be at an additional cost. In the restaurants you will surely find highchairs, as well as a special menu and even a corner for children. A variety of playgrounds, children's pools and slides will not leave indifferent any child who came here. And so that the rest of a child aged 4 to 12 years was even more fun, you can bring him to a special kids club with animation. For babies for a fee, you can always invite a nanny.

Impressions of tourists about the rest in the hotel

For those who are just planning to come to the Golden 5 Topaz for the first time, reviews about it will be very helpful. Indeed, thanks to the impressions of others, you can add the most complete picture of the hotel.

Most of our compatriots are satisfied with the choice of hotel. Many liked the spacious and bright rooms, but often guests talk about the need for repairs and more frequent cleaning. The highest praise from tourists goes to a well-groomed and beautiful area. The food in the restaurants is very diverse, according to our compatriots, so anyone here will find dishes to their liking.
