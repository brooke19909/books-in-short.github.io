Sunrise Royal Makadi - welcome home!

Sunrise loudly announced itself in 2003, opening its first three hotels in Egypt. The network is quite actively developing in the local market both due to the construction of new facilities and by taking over existing hotels under its control.

Today it has more than ten complexes that are located in the main resorts of the country. In Hurghada, near the Makadi Bay area, the Sunrise Select Royal Makadi Resort is located.

Description

Sunrise Royal Makadi - a beautiful hotel, built in the national style and offering everything you need for a wonderful holiday, received the first travelers in 2005. The territory of the complex is located a short distance from Hurghada (about 30 km), but at the same time, shuttle buses and taxis make it easy to get there.



The complex was built on a grand scale. Water slides, as well as five pools for adults and three for children that are heated in the winter, of course, not only decorate the territory, but also provide additional opportunities for recreation.

Number of rooms

The number of hotel rooms did not reach 700 a little. Fortunately, the territory allowed them to be placed in small two-story buildings, so there is no overcrowding. Even the most modest rooms in Sunrise Royal Makadi are quite impressive in size. But for the convenience of guests, there are rooms of various types - from standard to royal suites, whose area is more than 100 square meters.

Dining at the hotel

Guests at Sunrise Royal Makadi are provided with AI UAI meals. For the convenience of residents, the already traditional buffet is organized in two restaurants at once.



Well, if traditional food is tired of during the holidays, you can eat in one of the many establishments located directly on the territory of the complex. The hotel has several restaurants where guests can taste Italian, Greek, Mexican, Asian and, notably, Bedouin cuisine. In addition to them there are several bars and cafes.

Beach

The hotel is located on the sea coast, so you can get to the beach by taking a walk through its territory. It is worth noting that it is well equipped (rental of sunbeds, umbrellas, towels, sports equipment).

Additional Information

As in most Egyptian hotels, Sunrise Royal Makadi has a team of animators who entertain guests from morning to evening with sporting events and organizing and showing various performances.

For a fee, you can visit the beauty salon and spa.

For those who are used to relaxing actively, and not just basking in the sun, on the territory you can do the following sports: tennis, billiards, beach volleyball and football and, of course, table tennis. The Red Sea is a favorite place for diving and snorkeling, and water sports and scuba diving centers make these and other activities available to hotel guests. True, such entertainment will have to pay extra.



The complex is positioned as a universal place for recreation, where you can have a great vacation for the newlyweds, older people and, of course, families with children. What is prepared for young travelers should be said separately.

When booking a room, you can request a cot. The restaurants for the youngest guests have high chairs. The already described pools, a playground and a club for kids from 4 years old, where Russian-speaking specialists work, will not let the child get bored.

We should also say that the Dolphin World Dolphinarium is not far from the hotel, so here you can always find something interesting for both adults and children.

Sunrise Royal Makadi. Guest reviews

This hotel is probably one of the few in Egypt that boasts consistently positive ratings of its work. There are minor complaints about the work of the waiters, but the quality of accommodation, food and other services is maintained at a decent level, which is confirmed by numerous reviews of its guests.
