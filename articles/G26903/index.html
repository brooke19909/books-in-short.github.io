Hotels Langkawi - overview, rating, features and reviews

The Langkawi archipelago, consisting of 140 islands, is located in the north-west of Malaysia, washed by the waters of the Strait of Malacca, the largest island included in it is also called. The magnificent nature, warm climate, a large number of historical attractions and a developed tourism network, modern hotels of the island of Langkawi in Malaysia have made it a popular tourist destination. Here you can stay in luxury five-star hotels, choose affordable accommodation, find great food at affordable prices.

Climate on the archipelago

The islands are dominated by the equatorial climate. The average annual temperature is 27 degrees Celsius. The least precipitation falls from November to March, and the preceding months, September and October, are considered the rainy season, which occurs almost daily, and the sky can be cloudy for a long time.

The time from April to August is the average season in which you can relax at reasonable prices, in good hotels you can stay at significant discounts. High air humidity is maintained throughout the year.

Exotic relaxation in the wild jungle

The best hotel of Langkawi 5 stars is Datai Langkawi 5 *, which is a 20-minute drive from the city. The international airport is half an hour away.

Tourists, approaching a hotel, find themselves far from civilization, in the pristine jungle on snow-white beaches.

Guided tours are held here at night, which show various exotic animals and insects.

The hotel in Langkawi with the best beach is located on the 1st line, 200 m from the sea. It is problematic to leave the hotel for excursions.

The rooms are equipped with individual air conditioning, ceiling fans. There is satellite TV, international telephone communications, and a safe. Breakfast and drinks can be ordered.

During leisure, you can play sports. The hotel has tennis courts, an 18-hole golf course, and a fitness center. Those who wish can take cooking classes, go diving, and there are other water activities.

The hotel Langkawi offers the organization and holding of weddings.

Meals are held in four restaurants, where you can enjoy dishes of spicy Malaysian and European cuisine, served delicious desserts, exotic fruits, freshly squeezed juices.

According to reviews, the hotel in Langkawi Datai Langkawi 5 * is suitable for family and romantic vacations in the wild jungle, remoteness from civilization. The divine nature and high-level service will help you to experience real relaxation, forget about stresses and problems for a while. Comfortable hotel 4 *

Adya Hotel Langkawi 4 * is built in the Moroccan style. It will be especially comfortable for observing Muslims, since there is a family pool, as well as a separate one for women, a special transfer takes tourists to pray in a mosque.

Spacious rooms with a view of the city or the sea are equipped with air conditioning, flat-screen TVs, minibars and have a good design. There are prayer mats.

The restaurant serves Malaysian and European dishes, which can be ordered to your room.

Guests can use the services of the spa, work out in the gym.

The reviews indicate that Wi-Fi is available on site, and there is a free private parking.

Beautiful hotel with 4 * villas

Local Attractions

During their stay at the hotel in Langkawi, tourists can go on exciting excursions. Here is the world famous Sky Bridge, which can be reached by cable car. There are transparent glass sections on the bridge that are scary to step on, adrenaline and an unforgettable experience are guaranteed. From it you can see the beauty of Langkawi from a height of 750 m.

Going to Kilim Karst Geoforest Park, tourists ride along the canals in the mangroves, get acquainted with the local nature and fauna, visit the caves, dine at the fish restaurant.

You can go from a hotel in Langkawi to Tanjung Rhu Beach, one of the most beautiful snow-white beaches in the world.

Conclusion

Tourists who choose a vacation in Malaysia are guaranteed comfort and excellent service in the lap of exotic nature. Someone can retire to a quiet hotel, and for lovers of night parties there are numerous discos, bars and nightclubs.
