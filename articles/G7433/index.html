Best Los Angeles 3. Description, reviews

Description: Best Los Angeles 3 is located in the center of the vibrant Spanish resort of Salou, close to the city beach of Capellans and 100 kilometers from Barcelona.

The hotel complex, owned by the Best Hotels chain, consists of two six-story buildings that were built in 1973 and last reconstructed in 2007.



Located along the main shopping streets, numerous bars, restaurants and nightclubs, Best Los Angeles is a popular holiday destination for youth companies and anyone interested in outdoor activities and the vibrant nightlife of the seaside resort.

Rooms: Among the 264 rooms, there are 5 single rooms. The rest are designed to accommodate from two to four people. Standard, superior and deluxe rooms are available.

All rooms at Best Los Angeles 3 have a furnished balcony, air conditioning and a minibar. In the bathroom - shower, towels, bath accessories. There is a TV with cable channels, a direct dial telephone and a chargeable electronic safe.



The apartments are serviced and cleaned daily. Towels and linen are regularly changed. One child is provided with free accommodation. Pets are not allowed in the hotel.

Food: Several types of food are offered . You can choose a room without meals, with breakfast only, with two or three meals a day, as well as all-inclusive. The hotel's restaurant, which specializes in European and world cuisine, serves √† la carte and buffet meals.

Beach: Guests at Best Los Angeles Hotels are invited to the city's sandy beach with free entry and paid sunbeds. There are locker rooms and showers on the shore, there is a pier for pleasure boats.

Additional Information: Service at the hotel is represented by a variety of paid and free services.



Best Los Angeles 3 has a sun terrace, a lounge with a lobby, a safe, a TV room, parking, a left-luggage office. It is possible to call a doctor, rent a car, exchange currency, send a fax.

There is an outdoor pool with a children's section on the territory, next to which there are free awnings and sun loungers.

In the afternoon, guests will find fun on the beach: diving, water skiing, catamaran, pleasure boats. You can play billiards and table tennis at the hotel. In the evenings, video games and music performances are organized.

It is possible to alternate beach vacations with excursions, during which you can see not only the sights of Spain, but also visit the southern resorts of France, such as Nice or Cannes.



Digest: According to tourist reviews, the Best Los Angeles 3 hotel is a worthy vacation in the center of a popular resort at a reasonable price. A big plus is the proximity to the sea and the city center. Near the hotel there is a supermarket and many restaurants.

The comfortable, clean rooms feature a cozy balcony with plastic chairs and a table, where you can relax in the evenings with drinks and snacks. Towels are cleaned and changed every day. Food is diverse, a large number of dishes of meat, fresh fish and seafood, an abundance of vegetables and drinks. For breakfast, champagne and delicious coffee were served daily.

According to many guests, for its three-star category, the hotel deserves an excellent rating.
