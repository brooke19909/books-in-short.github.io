Mosoblbank: reviews of employees and customers

Mosoblbank, reviews of which make it possible to form a wide variety of opinions, was registered 22 years ago, in 1992. During its existence, this financial organization often changed owners. First, it was acquired by bankers Victor and Roman Krestins, who moved the main office to the town of Fryazino. By the way, the bank owes its name to the mentioned brothers. For some time, the organization developed rapidly, but (for unknown reasons) in 2005 was sold to a group of private individuals. In 2006, the composition of shareholders of the financial institution changed again: now it includes managers of OTB Bank.



Currently, the institution is functioning perfectly and bears the same name - Mosoblbank. The reviews on the numerous information and economic portals make it possible to form a fairly positive opinion about the mentioned organization, which, by the way, is almost completely part of the Republican Financial Corporation. Its share in it is about 94%. Since 2009, the chairman of the board of the financial organization is the former speaker of the State Duma Gennady Seleznev.

Advantages of the Mosoblbank financial institution. Customer reviews



Since 2007, the financial institution has made its main focus on the development of a sales network and retail business. At the moment, bank branches can be found in more than 40 cities of the Russian Federation. Today, the organization has over four hundred different branches in the largest cities of the country. Moreover, most of the offices are located in the metropolitan area, which justifies the name of the organization. Mosoblbank, whose customer reviews are, as often happens, the most diverse, currently has a staff of more than five thousand highly qualified employees. In addition, the financial institution issued about forty thousand plastic cards, and 300 ATMs and 1300 terminals were installed for their owners. Bank customers can use their services at any time, they will not be charged any additional fees for such operations.

Such a bank policy attracts a huge number of users. Especially for them, mortgage programs and consumer and car loans programs have been developed. In addition, each client of the bank can use the services of safe deposit boxes and international money transfers.
