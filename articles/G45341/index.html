Titanic Deluxe Golf Belek - Belek's Pearl

A hotel with a high level of service and an all-inclusive system. The hotel offers standard and superior rooms, family room, deluxe and villa accommodation. Each villa has its own pool, one of which has a semi-Olympic size.

Hotel Titanic has a huge selection of restaurants and bars. Guests can visit 11 restaurants offering European, Mediterranean, Italian and Turkish cuisines. Poolside bars, an Irish pub, a vitamin bar, a bowling bar, a patisserie and an ice cream parlor offer refreshing drinks, fresh Turkish coffee and fragrant pastries.

The spa offers a range of beauty treatments, a jacuzzi, sauna and steam room, hammam and salt-mineral water pool.

Mini-clubs for children are ready to entertain young guests throughout the day, and for young gourmets there is a restaurant where children try dishes prepared in accordance with their preferences and desires.

The beach is located among untouched nature and is a unique "highlight" of the Titanic Deluxe Golf Belek. You can get to him on foot through the vast territory of the hotel or take a boat ride on the Beshgez river.
