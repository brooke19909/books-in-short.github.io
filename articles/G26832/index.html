Vacuum cleaner with water filter "Thomas" - fast and high-quality cleaning

Any woman and any man, whether they are thrice absorbed in their careers or home improvement, pay special attention to the cleanliness and comfort of their homes. An apartment or a cottage is always a place where a person wants to feel calm and confident. Comfortable only where cleanliness and order reign. Stains on carpeting, stains on tiles or laminate flooring are always the headache of the owner. However, do not worry. Indeed, with the modern world there are a huge number of accessories and devices that "at once" cope with these problems. A vacuum cleaner with an aquafilter also belongs to them . Thomas, Bissell, Bosch and many other companies offer consumers high-quality products designed for quick and thorough cleaning.





Vacuum cleaner company "Thomas" - the best assistant in everyday life

Any dust exhauster, equipped with a washing function, will significantly save the time spent on bringing the home in a decent look. This device has a number of attractive features that other models do not have. The main and most important advantage is the ability to carry out both dry and wet cleaning. Moreover, these processes can be carried out simultaneously. Almost every high-quality vacuum cleaner with an aquafilter is equipped with a liquid collection function. Thomas offers a wide range of vacuum "dust absorbers" that meet the tastes and needs of the most demanding customers. The price category of goods sold is also quite different. Moreover, the quality of the product offered to the buyer will not depend on its value. Only the availability of additional functions and accessories is included in the price differential. By purchasing a vacuum cleaner with a Thomas aqua-filter, the family becomes the owner of an excellent assistant who can cope with stains of any complexity.





The first generation of "dust absorbers"

Depending on the model, the presence of certain functional features is different. Consider a vacuum cleaner "Thomas Twin", whose aquafilter is equipped with additional cleaning functions. The company produces this model in several versions. The first to see the light was a compact vacuum cleaner with a Thomas Twin T1 water filter. This vacuum apparatus is equipped with a specially developed system for cleaning the room from dust, various microbes, bacteria and substances that can cause allergies. During the operation of this "aspirator", along with impurities, air is also drawn into the device, which undergoes a two-filter fine treatment and then returns to the room. The vacuum cleaner is equipped with a clean water tank with a capacity of 2.5 liters. It is convenient to wash carpets, floors and upholstery with it.





Additional features of the new option

The next advanced model is the Thomas Twin T2 vacuum cleaner. This equipment is equipped with a more complete set of nozzles for cleaning. There are also water and air filtration systems, thanks to which the air in the apartment becomes more clean and humidified. The vacuum cleaner is equipped with dust and water level indicators, which allows you to remove excess liquid and carry out dry cleaning. Thanks to advanced technology, this device does an excellent job of cleaning not only carpets, but also tiles, laminate, parquet, furniture and even curtains.





Say no to allergies and yes to technology

The second generation of the vacuum device has a 3 liter water tank. The Thomas Twin TT vacuum cleaner also boasts a similar volume. The aquafilter of this dust exhauster is an improved version of the previous model. With a capacity of 1 liter, it is equipped with additional cleaning devices. The latter include HEPA and MCA filters. They are designed for highly efficient collection and retention of particles of various nature and size. It is noteworthy that in this generation of Thomas vacuum cleaners, a sensory system for regulating the power of equipment operation is involved. This model has received many awards both in innovation and in the medical field.
