Hotel Memento Kassiopi Resort 4 * (Corfu, Greece): photos and reviews of tourists

Corfu is one of the most picturesque and romantic cities in Greece. Thousands of tourists flock here every year to immerse themselves in the mysterious atmosphere of ancient myths and just have a good time on one of the picturesque beaches, enjoying the breathtaking scenery. And if you have chosen an island for your vacation, then it is worth considering the hotel Memento Kassiopi Resort 4 * as an option for a stopping place.

Of course, before booking, you should learn more about the hotel complex. For example, you need to familiarize yourself with the general information, study the location, ask what conditions of living the traveler offers here and what kind of reviews tourists leave.

Hotel Memento Kassiopi Resort 4 * (Corfu, Greece): What do the rooms look like?





The hotel complex consists of 120 rooms. It offers tourists double and triple standard rooms. There are more spacious suites, as well as large apartments that are suitable for a family of 4-5 people.

Regardless of the room you choose, you can count on modern comfort, homeliness and cleanliness. The furniture is modern and amenities. You can relax in a large bed, use bedside tables, spacious cabinets and a comfortable work desk.

Naturally, there is also a necessary equipment for comfort, including an air conditioning system, as well as a large flat-screen TV and a telephone. Tourists can use the Internet for free. There is a minibar, although filling it is paid separately.

The bathroom is small but very clean. Here you will find a modern shower, toilet, washbasin, mirror and, of course, round-the-clock hot water. By the way, towels are changed daily, and cleaning is carried out efficiently. For the convenience of tourists, a hairdryer is left in the bathroom.

Food: what do tourists offer?





Guests of the Memento Kassiopi Resort 4 * hotel can choose their own meal plan, whether it is only breakfast, or a full three meals a day. By the way, meals are held in the spacious restaurant of the neighboring hotel.

Reviews from tourists suggest that the menu here is not bad. Breakfast includes toast, freshly baked rolls, hot appetizers, salads, fruits and yoghurts. But lunches and dinners are more diverse, include dishes of poultry, meat, fish and seafood. Of course, guests will also enjoy delicious desserts.

By the way, the island has many cafes and traditional taverns, where you can completely immerse yourself in the color of the Ionian islands. Prices for lunch and dinner are quite affordable, and the dishes are delicious, especially when it comes to traditional cuisine and seafood.

Entertainment and amenities by the sea





Most tourists expect to spend at least a few days on the beach, especially when it comes to Corfu. Corfu Memento Kassiopi Resort 4 * is located very close to the sea - the distance is no more than 100 meters. The coast here is pebbled, and the entrance to the sea is cleared of stones, although special shoes still will not hurt. Tourists can count on comfortable sunbeds with umbrellas and, of course, incredibly beautiful landscapes.

A approximately 10-minute drive away is a spacious public beach. A bus runs to him several times a day, which is very convenient. Here, travelers are waiting for all possible entertainments, including boat trips, boating and water skiing, snorkeling, diving and windsurfing.

Entertainment and additional amenities for tourists





Of course, guests of the Memento Kassiopi Resort 4 * hotel can count on additional service. Currency exchange and laundry services are available on site. The reception is open around the clock, and the staff here are understanding and pleasant in communication. You can rent a safe from the administrator.

Throughout the hotel there is Internet access, which is extremely important for many tourists. Also, guests can use the conference room. On the territory there is a small parking lot, and it is not difficult to rent transport at the hotel.

As for entertainment, many guests spend part of the day at the hotel. For example, in the courtyard there are four pools of different sizes, around which there are sun loungers and umbrellas. In their free time, guests can enjoy playing badminton, table tennis and billiards. There is a small but fully equipped gym, as well as a sauna. In the evenings, live music is often played, and guests have a chance to sing karaoke.

Do not forget about excursions, because Corfu is an incredible island with breathtaking landscapes, a mysterious and romantic atmosphere and a lot of interesting sights.

Hotel Memento Kassiopi Resort 4 * (Corfu): conditions for a family holiday with a child





For people who are planning a vacation with their children, comfort issues always come to the fore. As already mentioned, the Corfu Memento Kassiopi Resort 4 * hotel provides spacious rooms in which the family can comfortably live. In the restaurant you can also always find something tasty and really healthy for the child.

The hotel can offer a little traveler some entertainment. For example, babies can splash around in a specially equipped, shallow and safe pool with warm water. And for the children there is a playground with swings, sandboxes and some other attractions. Young travelers can also enjoy sports areas, in particular, a basketball court.

How do tourists respond?

Often, a few minutes of communication with a person who has already visited a hotel can shed light on the big picture. What do they say about the hotel Memento Kassiopi Resort 4 *? The reviews are mostly positive, because the hotel complex is really clean and comfortable. The undoubted advantage is the proximity to the beach.

The hotel area is beautiful and clean, as are the rooms that tourists offer. The rooms are quite spacious and offer a view of the flowering garden. Household appliances work, and the repair in the premises is relatively fresh. Breakfast is delicious, and the service is really high, because the employees are always polite and attentive.

Memento Kassiopi Resort 4 * hotel is perfect for a beach, family and romantic getaway. By the way, the prices for accommodation here are quite affordable.
