Hanioti Melathron 4 * (Halkidiki, Kassandra): description, photos and reviews of tourists

Hanioti Melathron 4 * is a small hotel with a fund of 50 rooms, which is very loved by lovers of nature and the clean sea. In addition, relatively low prices for accommodation are set here, when compared with other hotels in Greece. However, all the advantages should be described in more detail.





general information

Hanioti Melathron 4 * is located on the Kassandra Peninsula, which, in turn, is part of the more massive Halkidiki Peninsula. And to be more precise, the hotel is located in a village called Hanioti. It is about 100 kilometers from Thessaloniki's Macedonia Airport. By the way, tourists can find a bus stop 300 meters from the hotel. Shuttle taxis from almost all directions go from there.

The most important thing for which everyone is coming here is the beach. It is less than 100 meters from the hotel. Another 6 kilometers from here are the famous healing springs of Loutra (the village of Agia Paraskevi). And in 15 minutes by car you can get to Kallithea, which has long been considered synonymous with the phrase "a good vacation in Greece." The cleanest sandy beach, protected from the scorching sun by tall pine trees, archaeological sites, the remains of the ancient temple of Ammon Zeus, night clubs, restaurants - there are many interesting places here, so any tourist will find something to do. Hanioti is an ideal resort for water sports. It is worth considering this when going here on vacation.
