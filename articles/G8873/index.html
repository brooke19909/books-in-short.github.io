Rio de Janeiro tours - the dream of even the most avid travelers

Rio de Janeiro Tours ... Are you dreaming of spending at least a week in Brazil? Then you definitely will like the incredibly beautiful city located on the shores of the Gulf of Guanabara, Rio de Janeiro. Nature was extremely generous to the area. Tourists will be able to enjoy the greenery of the forests, the beauty of the rivers, admire the view from the tops of the mountains, sunbathe on the sand of the famous beaches of Copacabana, Leblon and others.

Section 1. Tours of Rio de Janeiro. general description

Climatic conditions allow you to relax in Rio de Janeiro all year round. The most successful period begins in November and lasts until the end of March. But from April to September it is somewhat cool here, it often rains.

Rio is a city of contrasts, where every day you stay in this place is comparable to a real holiday. You begin to understand this especially sharply during the colorful carnival, which is held annually at the end of February. At other times of the year, you should visit the famous samba show or just take a walk in Quinta da Boa Vista park. However, vacationers always with great pleasure visit not only prestigious areas, but also poor favelas.

Section 2. Tours of Rio de Janeiro. sights

On Mount Corcovado there is one of the greatest monuments - the Statue of Christ the Redeemer. At the same time, this work of art, erected in 1931, is considered a symbol of Rio. As conceived by architects, Christ blesses the city and its inhabitants. It is best to look at the monument from Mount Pan de Asucar. And at night a huge statue is illuminated, and it is clearly visible from afar.

Brazil ... Rio de Janeiro ... Tours that cannot be forgotten. What does the National Stadium of Maracana cost? It is a symbol of Brazilian football, some even call it the temple of the country's second religion. The project became a reality thanks to journalist Mario Filho, his name became the official name of the stadium. Until a certain moment, Maracana was considered the largest stadium in the world, now it takes second place. Absolutely every football fan dreams of getting here, not to mention sports fans.

If you think that in a city where some residents play football, while others dance samba, there is no cultural entertainment, then urgently drop such thoughts. In Rio de Janeiro, tours, the prices of which are able to surprise, will be able to interest even the most inveterate and curious tourists.

Piazza Floriano is decorated with the Municipal Theater, built in the style of the Paris Opera. The walls inside the building are decorated with mosaics and ancient paintings. The theater has a symphony orchestra, choir and ballet troupe.

Section 3. Tours of Rio de Janeiro. Beaches

Along the coast of Rio de Janeiro, an endless strip of sandy beaches extends, the picture is complemented by an endless body of water and majestic hills.

The famous Copacabana Beach is located in the southern part of the city. White sand, a transparent ocean, hotels with a high level of service and other amenities of a comfortable stay - this is what attracts tourists here. Two historic fleets located at opposite ends of the beach make a special flavor.

Proponents of romance, early sunrises and spectacular sunsets will appreciate the beauty of Ipanema Beach. Service here is no lower than on the coast of Copacabana. The fundamental difference is only in the surrounding local landscape.

The most picturesque place is located in the south of Rio between the beaches of Ipanema and Copacabana. Coming to rest in the lagoon of Rodrigo de Freitas, you seem to find yourself in a real fairy tale. The road to the ocean or lake lies through a park in which vacationers walk and cyclists ride. When night lights turn on, the lagoon is transformed beyond recognition.
