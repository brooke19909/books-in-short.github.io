Londa Hotel 5 * (Cyprus, Limassol): hotel description, services, reviews

Many business people and ordinary tourists have been staying at the wonderful Londa Hotel 5 * hotel complex for several decades now. It was built back in the 80s. But "age" does not make this hotel worse. During all this time, several restorations were carried out, so the hotel is modern and very stylish. However, first things first.





Location

Londa Hotel 5 * is located in the sunny Greek city of Limassol, which is located in Cyprus. To be more precise, in the area known as Germasogeia. Its difference from the rest of Limassol is silence, a relaxed atmosphere and the presence of a large number of cozy cafeterias. Due to these advantages, the hotel is popular among couples who come to rest in Greece.

This hotel is also good for its close proximity to the beach. You can reach it in just a minute. The hotel also has a rather favorable location in terms of transport links. The airports of Paphos, Larnaca and Nicosia can be reached in approximately the same amount of time.

By the way, the hotel is immediately visible. She is distinguished by a unique style. This is the first boutique hotel in Cyprus, which Italian architects performed in a traditional island style.





Service and Facilities

Londa Hotel 5 * has various amenities. Since the hotel is focused not only on tourists, but also on business people, the building has a business center, halls for conferences and banquets. Also on site there are shops, a currency exchange office, laundry with dry cleaning and, of course, parking. In addition, shuttle services, concierge, doctor and babysitting are available (for a fee), equipment for people with disabilities, non-smoking apartments.

There is a tour desk, 24-hour reception, secretarial and receptionist services, an office with a fax and photocopy machine, separate smoking areas and even exhibition areas. The staff speaks English and Greek, so people who have problems with this should bring a phrasebook with them.

By the way, it was said above that the hotel has babysitting services. This is true, and if you call this specialist, he will be with the child and deal with him in the apartment, and not in the mini-club. It's comfortable.

Entertainment

At Londa Hotel 5 * guests can have a good time to brighten up their leisure time. Firstly, the hotel has its own beach with pebbles and sand. There are comfortable sun loungers and umbrellas (all for free). However, if the trip is planned in high season, you must be prepared to either sunbathe on a towel spread out in the sand, or take a seat in advance. For children, the hotel has a separate playground, a shallow pool and a mini club.

Adults can enjoy table tennis, play billiards or use the fitness center. A gym and water sports equipment are also available. In the evening, a disco and various entertainment shows are organized at the hotel.

And of course, Londa Beach Hotel 5 *, like any other self-respecting hotel, has a swimming pool and a sun terrace.





For body and soul

Hotel Londa is very prestigious and presentable. On its territory, in addition to all of the above, there is a hairdresser, a beauty salon, as well as a SPA salon. Here you can talk about the latter in more detail.

Sauna, massage, bath, jacuzzi - all this is available here. But still this SPA-salon has several rooms for procedures. Including there are those that are designed for couples in love. Massage, by the way, is available not only here, but also on the beach. Here you can also order treatment procedures, as well as devote time to reflexology or aromatherapy. Local experts also offer Swedish and sports massages. You can also sign up for stone therapy here. It is commonly known as hot stone massage.

However, there is a wide selection of various procedures and services. Cellular restoration, face cleansing, collagen enzyme enhancer, exotic body wrap, salt peeling with ginger and lime, Elemis-therapy for hands, nails and legs, waxing and much more.

Just remember one important nuance - you must sign up for procedures in advance. It is advisable even when making an application during remote booking.





restaurants and bars

Guests arriving at the Londa Hotel 5 * (Limassol) are worried about whether the food is good in this place. If you believe the numerous reviews of guests here, the answer is unequivocally affirmative. The hotel has a restaurant called Caprice and a bar next door to it.

This establishment offers a buffet breakfast. Lunch is also served here. The restaurant has an excellent Italian menu in which everyone will find what he likes. The excellent Iceberg Chicken Salad and hearty spaghetti in a sauce of juicy cherry tomatoes with beef fillet, arugula and aromatic Parmesan are prepared here. These are the two most popular dishes here.

And on Saturdays, a bar is located near the pool, and barbecue is prepared. By the way, here is a very wide selection of different exotic cocktails, drinks and snacks.

The best apartments

But there is also the so-called executive suite. He has his own characteristics. Firstly, it is designed for two adult guests. The stylish, spacious bedroom has a huge king-size bed. There is a living room with a stunning panoramic view of the sea. There are also two bathrooms. One is ordinary, as in the rest of the rooms, and the other is with a round whirlpool.

Guests staying in executive apartments receive a plasma TV with 200 satellite channels, a cordless telephone with an answering machine (you can order services from the reception with it), high-speed Internet, and all without exception, as described earlier.

7 days in such apartments will cost about 285,000 rubles for two with breakfast. If you book a full board, you will need to pay 350,000 rubles. Of course, the prices are high, but the hotel is 5-star, so it is designed for a certain contingent. Although even for executive rooms there are good discounts.





Reviews

Finally, a few words about how impressed the rest in this hotel is by those people who visited Limassol. Limassol Londa Hotel 5 * receives mostly good reviews from guests. The guests pay special attention to the staff. All attentive and friendly. The apartments are always clean, they are cleaned daily, in the morning - when guests are on the beach. At the arrival of each guest, a bottle of purified water awaits, as well as replenished supplies of cookies, fruits, tea, sugar and coffee. Many are happy and at the same time surprised by the presence of a Wi-Fi router in each room.

Seasoned tourists are advised to borrow sun loungers on the beach in advance, and come early for breakfast to take places on the terrace, from where you can enjoy a wonderful panoramic view of the sea during a meal.

In general, this is a wonderful hotel for people who want to get everything from their holidays, and even more. High-quality food, an upscale level of service, excellent apartments - all this and much more await guests at the Londa Hotel.
