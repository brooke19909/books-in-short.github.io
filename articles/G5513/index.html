"Diamond of Uvildy" (recreation center): description, prices, reviews

In the Urals, on the shore of one of the most beautiful lakes, there is a recreation center "Diamond Uvildy". The reviews of those who have already rested there are very positive. The hotel complex is located in the south-west of the lake, 95 kilometers from Chelyabinsk. Tourists note the excellent service, several accommodation options and thoughtful entertainment. The Almaz company, to which the recreation center belongs, is located in Chelyabinsk.

Base location

Lake Uvildy, on the shore of which a complex for visitors is built, is considered one of the most beautiful in the Urals. The pond is famous for its healing waters, which never bloom. The lake contains many useful minerals: magnesium, iron, radon and several others. The water in Uvild is weakly mineralized and saturated with oxygen. The territory of the base is very beautiful. The recreation complex is surrounded by dense vegetation. Among it are rare species. And on the shore of the lake is a clean sandy beach.



Accommodation

Diamond Uvildy (recreation center) offers several accommodation options. Summer houses are designed for several beds: 3, 4, 5 and 6. Each bungalow has a refrigerator, electric stove, table and chairs. On arrival day, bedding and a minimum set of dishes are provided. If you wish, you can rent a house with a veranda. Toilet and water are on the street. The approximate rental price is from 1350 to 3000 rubles.

“Diamond Uvildy”, a recreation center that is suitable for large companies. It offers rental two-story cottage. It is designed for eight people. At the entrance, bedding and a set of dishes are provided. The approximate rental cost is 7000 rubles. In the House there is:

  • separate kitchen with oven, electric stove, table and chairs;
  • refrigerator;
  • kitchen set;
  • separate living room with armchairs and a sofa;
  • restroom;
  • shower cabin with hydromassage;
  • playpen for children;
  • three separate bedrooms.




You can rent a large wooden house with two separate residential halves. Bed linen and a set of dishes are issued upon entry. Approximate rental - from 2400 to 3200 rubles. In each residential half there are:

  • kitchen with electric stove;
  • refrigerator;
  • Kitchen Cabinet;
  • sink;
  • table and chairs;
  • a bathroom;
  • bedroom.

On the base you can rent and just a double room. It has an ottoman and a sofa. There is a small refrigerator and electric stove. There is a table, chairs and a wardrobe. Upon entry, issued a set of bed linen and utensils. The bathroom is on the floor.

Base infrastructure

“Diamond of Uvildy” is a recreation center that has an internal small infrastructure. There is a Russian bathhouse for guests. Near the lake are healing mud and healing radon springs. Those who wish to sunbathe on the beach can use the sun loungers. For lovers of outdoor activities, certain entertainments are provided: volleyball court, football and badminton, table tennis.



One of the most popular entertainments is fishing. It attracts not only in the summer, but also in the winter. The lake is home to perch, chebak, crucian carp, pike and other species. You can fish sitting on the shore or from a boat.

For children there is a separate playground and a games room. The sandy beach is fully equipped and is cleaned daily. There are several brazier zones. Before a trip to the recreation center, you do not need to purchase products in advance, since there is a small shop on the territory of the complex. Parking spaces for cars are free. There are 70 cars in the parking lot.

Visiting Rules

“Diamond Uvildy” (recreation center) has certain periods of arrival and departure for guests. Arrivals are accommodated only until 18:00. And you need to leave before 16:00. You can rent a house or a cottage only by providing a passport or other identification document. It can be a military card or a driver’s license.

Payment for rest in the base is made only in cash. Or the guest is placed upon presentation of a previously purchased ticket. You can pay by bank transfer at the following address: city of Chelyabinsk, Lesoparkovaya street, 15. It is forbidden to bring animals with you on vacation. Children under the age of six can live with their parents without providing a separate bed.



Special offers

Like many hotel complexes, for guests has special offers and recreation center "Almaz". Lake Uvildy has healing waters. A long stay at the base contributes to a more complete recovery of the body. And for guests staying in a hotel complex for a long time, discounts are provided (accommodation is indicated in days):

  • from 5 to 7-15%;
  • from 8 to 14–20%;
  • from 15 to 24-30%;
  • over 25-50%.

If several special offers coincide on one application at once, then the discounts are not cumulative. Only one is provided, but the most profitable. Renting a house for a month has its advantages. The cost of living is reduced from 40,500 to 18,000 rubles. Permanent availability is guaranteed. The territory of the cottage is guarded around the clock. Showers are free to use.

How to get there

The recreation center "Diamond Uvildy" is located near Chelyabinsk. A lot of transport leaves from the city along this route. The base can be reached by minibuses. Get off at the Lesoparkovaya street stop. From there, walk to the base - five minutes. Buses No. 15 and 16 also go to the Lesoparkovaya Street stop. You can take a trolleybus and get off at the Gagarin PKiO stop. The base will be ten minutes on foot.

If you go by your own car, then from the city you need to get to Argayash. And from the turn to Karabash to the hotel complex there will be only 12 kilometers. If you go from Yekaterinburg, you first need to get to the turn to Kuznetsk. At 12 kilometers from it will be the base.
