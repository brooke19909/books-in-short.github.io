Let's try to recall what the origami scheme looked like (clapperboard). You can make this harmless paper toy with your children!

Origami clapperboard does not require you a lot of costs. From the materials we need only one ordinary piece of office paper for the printer or a piece taken out from the spread of the most ordinary school notebook. Recall that the notebook sheet must be carefully removed from the middle of the notebook, unbending paper clips and not tearing it.

A further scheme of origami (clapperboard) will look like this:

  1. the prepared piece of paper needs to be folded in half
  2. bend the corners of the sheet from two sides to the center
  3. bend the workpiece inward in half
  4. bend the sheet in half again, and then bend it, getting clearly defined fold lines.
  5. bend the edges to the existing bend line, getting oblique bend lines from the sides of the workpiece
  6. Now gently fold our workpiece along the formed fold lines

An origami clapper pops if you take it by its free ends so that the inside of the craft looks straight ahead, while the hand must make a sharp forward movement. If everything is done correctly, then the inside of the cracker will fly out and emit a loud pop.

We can make a double or double-barreled origami clapperboard that makes a louder clapping sound. To do this, the corners of our sheet must be bent on both sides.
