  • double superior comfort (23 m 2 ) with lake view, two rooms and a large bed;
  • Suite (25 m 2 ) with a view of the mountains and Lake Baikal, two bedrooms and a mini-fridge;
  • family (14 m 2 ) with mountain views, double and single beds.

All rooms are well furnished, have an LCD satellite TV, a private bathroom, and a shower. The hotel specializes in corporate events of various sizes and themes, like many other hotels located in this area.
