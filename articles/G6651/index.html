Choose the best hotels in Tunisia for families with children

The Islamic country of Tunisia, which has its own coast on the Mediterranean Sea, has long been popular with tourists. The nature here is surprisingly contrasting: the southern part is occupied by the Sahara desert, the northern territory is subtropics, and the eastern part is the spurs of the mountains. This country is famous all over the world for its therapeutic thalassotherapy. And the local culture carries the flavor of an oriental tale: mysterious palaces, beautiful gardens, colorful bazaars, high minarets. The local beaches are gently sloping sand spits that make it possible for even the smallest vacationers to swim. Tunisia hotels for families with children are located in almost every resort city in the country. Which one to stay in?



Choosing the best hotels in Tunisia for families with children, you should pay attention to many details. Firstly, what rooms the hotel provides for its guests (family, studio, apartments), since not all will be comfortable for the child. In addition, it is very important that the room has air conditioning, a refrigerator and a TV. The first is necessary to maintain the appropriate temperature, and the last - so that the child does not get bored. An important factor is the distance to the sea. After all, if it is quite large, then the path to it for a small tourist will be too tiring. It is necessary to take into account the location of the hotel (central part of the city, park area, proximity to shops).



Tunisia hotels for families with children must have a developed infrastructure. You need to know if there are playgrounds and pools for babies in the hotel complex, whether there are babysitting services, whether a cot is provided in the room, whether there are chairs for little vacationers. When choosing a hotel, it is recommended to take an interest in landscaping. This is necessary in order to always have the opportunity to hide with the child in the shade. It is very important to know some nuances about the hotel's beach: whether it is urban or private, pebble or sand, whether there are umbrellas and sunbeds on it. Do not forget to consider the type of food: is there a children's menu, what dishes are offered for the child.



There are similar hotels in every resort town. Therefore, it is important to decide where it will be best when traveling to Tunisia with children. The hotels most suitable for this are mainly concentrated in the city of Hammamet. Here, all family-type hotel complexes are simply surrounded by greenery. The city has a lot of children's entertainment. In addition, it is quiet and calm. In addition, hotels such as Hotel Samira Club, Hotel Patadis Beach, Club Hotel Omar Khayam, Hotel Dar Khayam have small water parks on their territory. And Club El Bousten 3 * even has its own mini-zoo. Each of the listed hotels has vivid and memorable animation.

Beautiful hotels in Tunisia for families with children are also in the city of Sousse. There are hotels on the coast of this resort that can satisfy even the most capricious tourist. For a quiet and relaxing family vacation, it is recommended to pay attention to the Tour Khalef Marhaba Thalasso & Spa, which is located in the central part of the city. The hotel has its own beach, children's pool and playground. Babysitting services are available. It is recommended to pay attention to the hotel Marabout 3 *. It is perfect for families with children aged 3 to 7 years. There is a well-equipped playground, a pool with slides, a disco for the little ones and excellent animators.



It is also recommended to take into account Tunisia hotels for children in Mehdia. So, Iberostar Royal El Mansour 5 * is perfect for relaxing with children of different ages. Babysitting services are provided here for younger tourists, and a mini club is available for older children. Those families who wish to go to Monastir should pay attention to the Primasol Golden Beach 4 * hotel, aimed at children under the age of 7 years. No less suitable for children will be the Caribbean World Monastir 4 *. There is a small water park, a pool with toboggan and waterfalls.
