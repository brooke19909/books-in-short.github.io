The best hotels in Moscow: review, rating, description and reviews

The capital of Russia attracts not only tourists, but also businessmen, politicians, artists and other personalities who arrived in Moscow for leisure, study or work. On the territory of the city, guests will find hundreds of hotels offering excellent service and comfortable rooms. These hotels can easily compete with their western competitors. In this article we will tell you about the best hotels in Moscow, including not only 5-star grandees, but also cheaper hotels with a wonderful price / quality ratio. Where is better to stay? Rating of the best hotels in Moscow, see below. For greater completeness of information, we will talk about the best hotels of different stars - from 5 * to 3 *. The rating is based on reviews from tourists and independent expert ratings.

No. 1 among 5 * hotels - National

The magnificent facade of the building resembles, rather, a theater rather than a hotel, bewitching with a wonderful architectural style. The National was built in 1903 according to the design of Alexander Ivanov, the famous Russian architect in those years. It is located in the heart of Moscow, a couple of hundred meters from Red Square. The hotel rooms are real works of art, pleasing with exquisite design and antique furniture, creating an extraordinary atmosphere of comfort and tranquility. Needless to say that the functionality is at the highest level? Flat-screen televisions, minibars, refrigerators - there is everything that only the soul desires.

A wide range of services is another pride of the institution. Dear guests are the most important value of National, helping it to improve. A wonderful SPA center, swimming pool, restaurant, sauna, fitness center - this is not a complete list of services provided by the hotel. Choosing the best hotels in Moscow, you should definitely pay attention to the "National".

Customer reviews can tell more about the hotel. For the most part, they are full of flattering comments, only occasionally there are negative comments. Briefly telling about the impressions of tourists, you can as follows describe the emotions after a vacation at this hotel. Tourists believe that the chic location and landscapes that open from the windows of the rooms are perhaps the main advantages of the hotel. Calmness and comfort are two more important components that determine the popularity of National. The design of the rooms, their convenience and practicality confirm the status of the hotel, and delicious and satisfying breakfasts accompanied by pleasant music are truly wonderful moments that are remembered for many years. Rest in the "National" gives only a pleasant feeling, and negative reviews about this hotel are very rare.

No. 2 - Sheraton Palace

This hotel is located in the heart of Moscow - on Tverskaya Street. Spacious and modern rooms await guests. The hotel has bars and restaurants, as well as a 24-hour fitness center. The hotel rooms are equipped with all necessary equipment: air conditioning, televisions, minibars and much more. What can I say, a rating of 5 stars is the highest level for such institutions. Customers can order food and drinks in the room around the clock, which, of course, is very pleasing. 300 meters from the hotel building is the Belorusskaya metro station.

Travelers vacationing at the Sheraton Palace note the friendly and helpful staff, comfortable rooms, first-class services and a good location. The disadvantages are sometimes attributed to insufficient room service.

No. 3 - Metropol

Impeccable hotel located opposite the Bolshoi Theater, it delights with a grand exterior and no less impressive interior. The hotel began its life journey in 1905. Since then, she has received many guests who held business meetings, bright celebrations and other events. The list of "Best Hotels in Moscow" cannot do without the "Metropol", whose numbers are regularly received by officials, world-class actors, businessmen and others. A walk to the famous Kremlin takes only 5 minutes, and in the immediate vicinity of the hotel is the Central Department Store - the famous shopping center of Moscow. The number of rooms reaches 388, each of them is decorated in the style of Art Nouveau. In them, guests will find antique furniture, large windows and modern appliances. The list of services is also very extensive: a cozy restaurant, a fitness center, free Wi-Fi and much more.

Most tourists praise the hotel, calling it a masterpiece of architecture. Praised and wonderful location, close to the main attractions of Moscow. Design decoration of rooms, equipment, breakfasts served in the restaurant - all this causes only positive emotions among vacationers. Sometimes they complain about the operation of air conditioners, slightly cooling in hot weather, but such a minor flaw is easily resolved at the reception.

No. 4 - Savoy

This hotel must be mentioned, if we talk about the best hotels in Moscow (5 stars). The appearance of the hotel is very remarkable, embodying the classicism of the early twentieth century. Near the hotel building are the most popular attractions of the capital, including the Bolshoi and Maly theaters, Red Square and others. The hotel harmoniously combines centuries-old traditions of Russian hospitality, room comfort and a number of other elements inherent in respectable hotels of the highest class. All this puts her in the TOP of the best hotels in Moscow. The first visitors appreciated the level of the establishment in 1913, which marked the beginning of the excellent reputation of the Savoy Hotel. The number of rooms is small, it has 67 rooms. Residents are invited to visit the gym, indoor swimming pool, restaurant and more.

Hotel guests note the ideal location of the hotel, because there are a lot of restaurants with cafes nearby as well as popular architectural monuments. All rooms are impressive with exquisite interiors and impeccable equipment. They also praise good noise isolation and an excellent bathroom, which has plenty of towels and toiletries. Rarely recall shortcomings, because it is very difficult to find them. Some tourists complain that the image on the TV is obscured by obscure black stripes, the sinks in the bathroom are not too comfortable, and the phone often blinks bright light all night. But these are trifles. In general, if you are looking for the best hotels in Moscow, be sure to pay attention to the Savoy.

The best hotels of Moscow 4 *

Moscow is replete with chic 5-star hotels that can compete with Western institutions. Nevertheless, there are enough more modest analogues, offering a wonderful quality of service, comfortable rooms and a wide range of services.

The first in the list of the best is the Raikin Plaza, located within walking distance of the Marina Grove. The business center of the capital is a few minutes by car, which is important for tourists and business people. Not far from the hotel building is the Satyricon Theater, as well as the KVN Youth Center. The number of rooms has 54 rooms, each of which shines with a design decoration and wonderful equipment. Extras include bathrobes, slippers and a hairdryer.

Next in line is the Park Inn by Radisson Sadu. Perhaps one of the most popular hotel chains in the world. The hotel is located in the historical center of Moscow, with its windows offering breathtaking views of the city and the Cathedral of Christ the Savior. The central square is 15 minutes on foot. The Park Inn rooms boast stylish furniture and impeccable functionality. The quality of service will also not disappoint anyone.

Finally, the last hotel on our list is the Radisson Slavyanskaya. It is located on the banks of the Moscow River, delighting visitors with views from panoramic windows. The cozy rooms are decorated in a classic style, and their equipment includes all the necessary household items. For the convenience of customers, the hotel has a restaurant and a fitness center.

Cheap hotels

Not all city guests can afford upscale hotels. If you prefer budget options, we will indicate the best hotels in Moscow. You can relax inexpensively, you just need to know the place. Next, we will talk about hotels with a rating of 3 *: Warsaw, Sputnik, SkyPoint. Accommodation in a standard single room of these hotels will cost from $ 40 to $ 70 per day. Prices can change, so before checking in, it is better to check the cost with hotel employees.

No. 1 among 3 * hotels - SkyPoint

The hotel is located 800 meters from Sheremetyevo International Airport. The hotel catches the eye with a beautiful modern building. In many reviews, guests note spacious rooms, furnished with quality furniture, good appliances, and friendly staff. A buffet breakfast is also served every morning. Disadvantages include rare interruptions in the water supply, as well as the fact that some rooms are windowless.

No. 2 - Warsaw

The hotel, named after the Polish capital, is located in the heart of Moscow, not far from the famous park named after Gorky. Here guests and residents of the city like to relax, strolling along the shady alleys and enjoying the fresh air. The hotel itself has 136 rooms equipped with panoramic windows. Travelers speak well of no, noting a good location near the Oktyabrskaya metro station, friendly and unobtrusive staff, excellent breakfasts and an atmosphere reminiscent of Soviet times. The minuses include the bathroom, in which there is a very slippery bottom, as well as unconfigured channels on the TV. In general, a great choice if you are looking for good hotels in Moscow.

No. 3 - Sputnik

The 15-storey hotel building is a real decoration of the area. Not far from it are the metro stations Vorobevy Gory and Leninsky Prospekt. There are 349 rooms equipped with large beds, TVs, air conditioning, private showers and minibars. Guests can enjoy a tasty meal in the hotel's wonderful restaurant, which serves dishes of various cuisines of the world. Wi-Fi is free throughout. Near Sputnik there are parks, playgrounds and entertainment facilities. Consider this institution if you are looking for good hotels in Moscow. It is a pleasure to relax here with children because of the excellent location and proximity to cultural places.

Finally

Choosing good hotels in Moscow, everyone will be lost in an insane amount of such establishments scattered throughout the city. In this article we have listed some of the most popular hotels in the capital with a rating of 5 *, 4 * and 3 *. We hope the information was helpful.
