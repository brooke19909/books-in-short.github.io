The Byke Resort Goa (India, Goa): description, photo, reviews

Goa is famous all over the world for its snow-white beaches and picturesque nature. Therefore, it is not strange that more and more travelers visit this place every year. In connection with the active development of the tourism business on the coast, the number of hotels of various types is increasing. One of them is the hotel The Byke Resort Goa, which is famous for its hospitality and extraordinary design. So what can a guest expect and is it worth choosing this place for a vacation?

Description of location and territory





Among other hotel complexes, The Byke Resort Goa stands out for its convenient location. It is located in the cozy resort village of Cavelossim, in the south of the island. The picturesque coast is about 180 meters away. By the way, on the one hand, the hotel territory is washed by the waters of the Arabian Sea, and on the other, there is a backwater of the famous Sal river. Nature here is truly amazing. Dabolim Airport is about 36 km away. Madgaon Train Station is 15 km away.

The hotel was built in 1989, but not so long ago there was a complete reconstruction. The main building is built in the shape of a ship. Residential rooms are located in small two-story bungalows. There is a garden that pleases the eye with the exotic beauty of plants, and the territory itself is clean, well-groomed and decorated with greenery.

Goa, The Byke Resort Resort Goa: what do the rooms look like?





The hotel can be considered relatively large, as on its territory there are 196 fully equipped rooms. Basically, these are standard rooms of different capacities, but there are also deluxe rooms. By the way, all living quarters are located in small cozy two-story bungalows, and each room has an individual entrance from the street.

The rooms are spacious and characterized by a modern decor and pleasant design. You can count on comfortable beds, upholstered furniture, a spacious living area with large windows. Naturally, there is a set of equipment, without which a truly comfortable stay for a modern tourist is impossible. For example, you can relax watching satellite channels. At your service - a telephone, a small safe, a set for making tea and coffee (replenish it every day). With the help of a convenient air conditioning system, you can create the most comfortable temperature conditions in the room.

In the small bathroom you can refresh yourself, take a shower. By the way, there is water constantly. There is also a wall mirror, a hair dryer, a set of soaps and shampoos, a set of clean towels that change every day. Reviews of The Byke Resort Goa Old Anchor 3 * hotel suggest that the living conditions here are quite decent.

Dining





Naturally, the issue of nutrition is important for every guest. What can the hotel complex The Byke Resort Goa 3 * offer in this regard? Paying for accommodation, guests are entitled to free breakfasts, which are held in the hotel restaurant in the form of receptions. According to reviews, the menu here is quite diverse and includes light snacks, pancakes and pancakes, delicious soups, freshly baked buns and, of course, aromatic tea. By the way, some of the dishes are served with traditional Indian spices.

You can have lunch or dinner in the same restaurant, but already ordering dishes on the menu and paying their cost, which, incidentally, is quite affordable. In addition, you can eat in one of the cafes, which are many in the vicinity. Yes, and almost every beach has cafes that offer snacks.

How close is the beach? Water activities for tourists





The main purpose of the visit of most tourists is to relax on one of the picturesque beaches of Goa. The Byke Resort Goa is located just meters from the seafront with a well-kept path. The beaches here are public, and therefore you will have to pay for the use of sun loungers and umbrellas. There are lively coastal areas where you can enjoy water sports, and secluded, unspoiled beaches where you can relax while enjoying peace and unity with nature.

Entertainment, leisure and additional services





Despite the fact that the hotel is intended for a beach holiday, you can find something to do on its territory. For example, you can have a good time by the pool, where guests swim, sunbathe and make new friends. You can also visit the gym and Ayurvedic center, where, among other things, you will be offered massage. You can have a good evening playing billiards.

Guests can also use local parking. There is a small business center, as well as Internet access throughout the hotel. Despite the fact that there is no constant animation at the hotel, from time to time there are organized performances of local groups, discos and other entertainment events.

India, Goa, Hotel The Byke Resort Goa: Traveler Reviews

Having got acquainted with the opinion of travelers who have already managed to visit one place or another, you can get a huge amount of valuable and really relevant information. So what do tourists say about The Byke Resort Goa?

For most people, the hotel leaves a pleasant impression on itself. The undoubted advantage is the convenient location and proximity to the beach. The rooms are quite comfortable and kept clean. Special thanks to the tourists say the hotel staff, as they are without exception polite and helpful. Food is also at a decent level, especially for this region.





Naturally, it is worthwhile to understand that this is India, and therefore the conditions here are slightly different than, say, in five-star Egyptian resorts. Nevertheless, the hotel is perfect for lovers of a fun beach holiday. Even families with children often stop here.
