Tablet Sony Xperia Z2 Tablet: reviews, specifications

Tablets have become part of the life of modern man. But choosing a good gadget is problematic. Manufacturers annually produce a variety of phones and tablets that can attract certain characteristics. For example, today we have to get acquainted with the tablet Sony Xperia Z2 Tablet. Reviews about this device, review and specifications will be presented to your attention. Is it worth paying attention to this device? How can he pleasantly surprise users?

Short description

Sony Experiment is a modern tablet with support for LTE-communication and original design. New-fashioned device for work, study and entertainment.



The gadget is endowed with a standard list of features. For example, you can use it to work on the Internet, chat on Skype, listen to music and even shoot videos! Sony Xperia Z2 Tablet 16 GB does not perform any essentially new functions.

Equipment

It should be noted that the delivery set of the device also does not stand out. It can be called standard.

In the box with Sony Experiment you can find:

  • the device itself;
  • Charger;
  • USB cable for connecting to a PC;
  • headphones (not in all assemblies);
  • user's manual.

Additional accessories for the Sony Xperia Z2 Tablet must be purchased independently. The good news is that finding the right devices is not difficult.

About design and construction

The Sony Z2 Series tablet is a device that, according to manufacturers, is protected from moisture and dust. The high level of protection of the device allows you not to worry about its integrity.

Reviews on the Sony Xperia Z2 Tablet indicate that it is generally light - weighing about 430 grams. But the size of the gadget is not encouraging. It is often called bulky - 172 by 266 by 6.4 millimeters. Holding this tablet in a long time is inconvenient. This is indicated by many of its owners.



The front side of the device is primarily a screen. But it has a fairly wide frame. The back panel is made of matte plastic, on which fingerprints are very noticeable. At the back of the Sony Xperia Tablet Z2 LTE 16GB is a camera. The end of the device is equipped with a power button and connectors for USB-cable. There are compartments for the "SIM card" and "flash drive". Also on the case there is an infrared port, which is used to control the Smart Home function.

Reviews of the Sony Xperia Z2 Tablet indicate that the device bends a little. This fact must be taken into account if the gadget is planned to be carried in a bag with a lot of things. It is advisable to immediately buy a tablet case so that you can not worry about this feature.

Screen

Sony Xperia Z2 Tablet SGP521 tablets are devices with a fairly large screen. The display is designed for 10.1 inches with an extension of up to 1 920 by 1 200 points. The tablet uses IPS technology. It provides the device with a good viewing angle and brightness.

According to users, the screen is perfect for viewing photos, films and videos. Among the main disadvantages of the display, the owners highlight a yellowish tint of white. Sometimes the screen gives yellow, sometimes pink. The aspect ratio of the device is called by some strange.

Power

A huge role for modern mobile devices is performance. Some phones and tablets, despite their characteristics, are slow. Therefore, users refuse them.

Reviews on the Sony Xperia Z2 Tablet emphasize the ambiguous attitude of the owners to the performance of the device. For example, some say that the device works quickly, but over time, brakes appear. And someone immediately assures the slow execution of requests.

The Sony Experiment Z2 tablet has a quad-core processor with a resolution of each 2.3 GHz. RAM in it is only 3 GB. For a modern tablet, this is not as much as we would like. Some gaming innovations will not start on this device. But basically all the programs and games on the Sony Xperia Z2 Tablet work without brakes.



Memory

That's not all! Specifications Sony Xperia Z2 Tablet emphasize the presence of several assemblies of the device. Especially in terms of memory. Operational, as already mentioned, provides only 3 GB. There may be several built-in spaces. Namely:

  • 16 GB;
  • 32 GB

In addition, the device supports working with additional memory cards. Some say that you can insert a USB flash drive up to 64 GB in the device under study, and someone says that you can expand the space to 128 GB.

Assembly

Tablet Sony Xperia Z2 Tablet 10.1 16GB is a device that comes in different assemblies. And not only in relation to the built-in space. The thing is that this tablet is available in 2 versions. It is about supporting communication.

There is a model that supports only LTE-connection. It does not have the ability to work with a wireless network. Internet access will be carried out using the SIM card inserted in the device.

But there is also a Sony Xperia Z2 Tablet Wi-Fi + LTE. It is easy to guess that this device works with a wireless network. It is these tablets that are most in demand in Russia today.

Features of modifications

Based on the proposed information, we can conclude that the device has 3 modifications. Namely:

  • Wi-Fi - on 16 and 32 GB;
  • Wi-Fi + LTE - 16 GB.

There is a headset in the kit with the LTE tablet, it is missing in all other assemblies. Accessories for the device also have some features. For example, among them, a Bluetooth speaker with a stand, a special stand for watching movies and videos, and also a fastened keyboard are often distinguished.

The Sony Xperia Z2 Tablet, whose characteristics can be called very powerful, allows you to connect joysticks from the PlayStation 3. Therefore, the device sometimes surprises with its capabilities!



Battery

Reviews on the tablet emphasize the presence of a good battery in the device. It uses Quick Charge version 2.0 technology. Manufacturers claim that the battery life of the device is increased by 75%.

The battery is rated at 6,000 mAh. The tablet can work up to 10 hours with active use and up to 100 hours in standby mode. These are very good indicators for a modern mobile device.

In fact, the tablet presented to attention can be compared in power with the iPad Air. It is noted that the device under study actually discharges quite quickly. At the same time, charging takes a lot of time (the battery charges about 5 hours from a standard power supply). If you use a special magnetic docking station, you can reduce the battery charging time by a third.

Cameras

Sony Xperia Z2 Tablet 16GB 4G has several cameras. Namely, the front and rear. The first is located on the front panel of the device, the second is on the back.

The front camera is designed for shooting with a quality of 8.1 megapixels, and the rear - 2.2 megapixels. Flash is not provided for any of the components. Sony Expert Tablet has built-in Sony's standard picture and video editing features.

Users do not respond best to the operation of the carmer of the device under study. Some complain about the poor quality of images, some are not satisfied with the lack of flash. At night, shooting with Sony Experia will not work. With poor lighting, videos and photos also leave much to be desired.

operating system

There are practically no complaints about the operating system and its capabilities. Reviews on the Sony Xperia Z2 Tablet tablet emphasize that Sony in this device went the common way - it used the Android 4.4 operating system.



This OS is known to many modern people. Therefore, many say that working with the Sony Xperia Z2 Tablet is simple and pleasant. There are no obscure changes and features. Any person who has ever worked with Android can easily control the device.

Externally, the OS design has some changes. For example, the Sony Xperia Z2 Tablet has new standard wallpapers and themes, as well as program icons changed. But in general, the device offers a clear and simple interface that makes it easy to manage any gadget features.

What do owners think

Sony Xperia Z2 Tablet is a new pretty interesting gadget. It pleases and slightly surprises users with its appearance. Like any other device, this tablet has several disadvantages and advantages.

Among the strengths of the device, users usually distinguish:

  • performance;
  • a lot of space on the device;
  • multitasking;
  • beautiful and intuitive interface;
  • stylish design;
  • noises;
  • lightness of the device;
  • screen clarity;
  • good viewing angle.

Despite this, the tablet cannot be called ideal. This device still has flaws. They are distinguished by different ones.

For example, many say that when you select a black tablet on its back panel, fingerprints will be noticeable. Have to either put up with this phenomenon, or constantly clean the device. On white panels there is no such problem.

In addition, the disadvantages of the tablet are its following features:

  • slow battery charging;
  • cost (tablet will cost about 25,000 rubles);
  • the high cost of accessories and repairs;
  • poor camera quality and no flash;
  • quiet speakers;
  • yellowness of the display;
  • fast overheating of the device.

Nevertheless, this does not mean at all that Sony Experia Tablet is a bad tablet. Rather, it is a decent model from Sony, but with its drawbacks. If you believe the reviews, then the tablet overheats only when shooting in FullHD format. Also, some claim that the device screen may crack due to deflections. But with careful handling of the tablet, this event will be avoided.
