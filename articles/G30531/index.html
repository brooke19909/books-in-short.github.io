Chalet "Golden Sands". Irkutsk region has a real gem

An increasing number of compatriots who appreciate the beauty of their native land are attracting pastime on the coast of local reservoirs. It is interesting and financially not too burdensome to get together with the company and wave for the weekend to the recreation area or to the camp site. How to find a suitable place from among the offers? The choice is huge, and finding a decent option among tourist sites is not difficult. So, the Irkutsk Region is proud of the Golden Sands camp site. The photo clearly demonstrates the advantages of this place.

Thousands of vacationers seek to get to the coast of the Bratsk reservoir with families, friends or colleagues.

Dreaming to relax in nature is better not to find a place!

The harsh Irkutsk Territory is rich in picturesque corners that attract tourists. One of such places is the Golden Sands camp site. It attracts every year an increasing number of vacationers. A luxurious coastline, a comfortable sandy bottom, clear water that warms up to 25 degrees in the summer. Yellow sand plays in the sun, raising the mood for vacationers. A shallow shore of impressive size is framed by a birch grove, a thin strip stretching along the reservoir.

After the construction of the dam in 1967, the so-called Bratsk Sea was spread among the people, the banks of which became a favorite vacation spot for both local residents and visitors. An ideal place for relaxation and active pastime. Here everyone will find a corner to their liking.

Today, a quality vacation does not have to go far. But the level of claims of tourists is diverse. It is foolish to hope on the coast of the fraternal sea to meet a fashionable 5 star hotel! But on the territory of 52 hectares there is where to turn around. It offers vacationers 16 cozy one-story summer houses, cafes. The Golden Sands camp site is designed for 48 places. Irkutsk region is rapidly developing a variety of tourist destinations, and the ecological type of recreation is one of them. The territory of the base is designed for camping, which they bring with them or rent on site. The cost of services is justified by the clean coast, the absence of landfills, equipped toilets and security facility. Reasonable prices and magnificent nature attract every year an increasing number of people who want to come here.

Having approached the barrier, from each car you need to pay 400 rubles (per day), but if you are more than 24 hours, the price is 350 rubles for each day. Those wishing to rent a 3-4-person tent with a sleeping bag will cost from 500 rubles per day. You need to book a house in advance, leaving an advance payment, because there are plenty of people who want to relax in comfort here. Rent a house is from 900 rubles per day, the cost of entering a car is not charged. At the crossing of the fence, vacationers are provided with garbage bags, thus stimulating tourists to clean up after themselves.

The benefits of a holiday on the Bratsk Sea

The name of the camp site was born involuntarily and arose due to the bright yellow sandy coating of the beach. Fresh air, crystal clear water, a gently sloping bottom make Golden Sands an attractive place. Irkutsk region, having such a recreation base, can in the future compete with well-known foreign counterparts. By developing an infrastructure that will make full use of the natural resources of this region, everyone will benefit: vacationers, owners of tourist facilities and tenants who can make money out of clean air and golden sand.

Today, relaxation on the beach of the Bratsk reservoir will easily be allowed to those who are a bit cold on Lake Baikal, and a little expensive to go abroad. Affordable prices, luxurious nature guarantee an unforgettable vacation.
