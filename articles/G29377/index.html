Ligurian Sea in Italy: tourist reviews and interesting facts

The coast of the Ligurian Sea annually becomes a resting place for many tourists. People from all over the world come here to enjoy the warm and mild climate, relax at the resort and just have a good time.

Location

Geographically, for the most part, the Ligurian Sea belongs to Italy, it partially bathes its administrative region Liguria, as well as France and Monaco. The approximate width of the coast is thirty kilometers and a length of about two hundred, it runs along the Gulf of Genoa.



Conventionally, it is divided into two parts - the Riviera di Ponente and Riviera di Levante, located from the border with France to Genoa and from Genoa to Tuscany, respectively. Just as Italy looks like a women's boot, the coast resembles a horseshoe. The sea is limited to three points: the islands of Corsica, Elba and the coast of the Gulf of Genoa.

Famous Resorts

The pleasant mild climate and tropical plants of this region have long attracted tourists here. In the 19th century the whole European nobility was resting here, only representatives of this layer of society could afford it. Here is one of the most famous areas for recreation - the Riviera. The most popular resorts are San Remo and Allacio, for more economical trips Diano Marina is suitable.

Interesting Facts

  • The waters of the southeastern part of the Ligurian Sea pass into the Tyrrhenian Sea, and the south into the Mediterranean.




  • Many rivers flow into the Ligurian Sea, which begin mainly in the Apennine mountains. Among them: Arno, Roya, Lavagna.
  • The largest bay of the Ligurian Sea is Genoese.
  • The Italian naval base, Livorno, is also located in this area.
  • The coast of this area is considered one of the most beautiful areas of all of Italy.

The coast

Despite the fact that this coastal region of Italy is popular with tourists, in fact a considerable part of the coast is unsuitable for recreation. The rocky terrain is sometimes interrupted by access to the sea and bays, there are sandy beaches. In addition to the aforementioned administrative region, the Ligurian Sea is also washed by the French Riviera .

A favorable climate, incredible beauty of landscapes, clean Mediterranean air - all this contributes to a good rest from everyday life. Although not everyone can afford to stay here.



According to tourists, the prices of resorts in two regions - the western and eastern - Ligurian coasts are significantly different. The eastern region offers its tourists plenty of entertainment and more modern and well-groomed beaches than the western. There are also several resort towns, which, despite their popularity, have not lost their color. The Ligurian Sea (Italy can truly be proud of it) often becomes a resting place for very wealthy people. Reviews confirm that there are even special parking lots for private yachts, where new visitors come to resorts.

Popular resorts. Ligurian sea

Photos of local hotels and beaches show only a fraction of how varied you can spend your vacation. Often, tourists who find themselves in this region decide to visit Genoa. In this city you can see local attractions and admire the magnificent Italian architecture, which has been honed here for many centuries and takes on new forms.



After that, a huge selection of resorts of the Riviera di Ponenta opens up for tourists. One of the most popular is Arenzano. It is located near Genoa and is characterized by democratic prices. Here you can find entertainment on any wallet. A little further away are the resorts of Varazze and Spotorno. They delight their visitors with well-groomed sandy beaches and a calm, measured rhythm of life.

One of the most popular resorts on the entire Ligurian coast is Alassio. Despite the fact that vacation prices are far from the lowest, many tourists come here every year. Already in the last century, the rich and famous gathered in Alassio, since then this resort has become known as one of the most respectable in the Italian Riviera. Celebrities such as Winston Churchill, Sophia Loren and Ernest Hemingway have visited here.

Not far from Alassio is another resort, which recently was a small village - Laigueglia. These two cities are located very close, but the second attracts lovers of secluded relaxation to a greater extent, because Laiguelia still has the charm of a small town, although now you can spend time on comfortable beaches or go to the mountains.

Riviera di Levante





Next, mention should be made of the resorts of the Riviera di Levante, which are more accessible for recreation. Camogli, Portofino and Rapallo, small coastal towns, stand out especially here. They are all alike, but still distinct from each other. Small cozy resorts are worth visiting for those who want to stay in seclusion and get rid of the daily bustle.
