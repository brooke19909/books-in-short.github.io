Intercontinental Taba Heights 5 * (Egypt / Taba): photos, prices and reviews of tourists

The Intercontinental Taba Heights Resort Hotel is an Egyptian Five, which is part of the Intercontinental line of hotels. It is located on the Sinai Peninsula, and possibly embodies all the charms with which the ancient land of the pharaohs can attract tourists. Here are beautiful white beaches with clean sand, and mountains, and the clean warm waters of the Red Sea. As befits a hotel of this class, Intercontinental Taba 5 * provides its guests with the maximum number of services of very high quality, as evidenced by the numerous responses of tourists. In this hotel you can not only relax on the sea (especially since this resort complex is on the first line of the beach), but also make profitable purchases and travel to local attractions. The Pearl Bay of Sinai, on the banks of which the hotel is located, is even called the local fjord for its rugged beaches and magnificent landscapes, as well as turquoise colored water. The hotel is open year round and is considered to be the best in Taba.



How convenient is the hotel

It can be said that the Intercontinental Taba Heights Resort is in an ideal location. Of course, if you come here from Cairo or Sharm el-Sheikh, then this is far away. The capital of Egypt is five hundred kilometers from the hotel, and the main city of the Sinai resort is two hundred. Therefore, getting here from Sharm is quite a long time. However, Taba itself has an international airport, where direct flights fly from Russia, in particular from Moscow. From there, go to the hotel a little more than thirty kilometers, so the transfer does not seem tedious to you. Moreover, the hotel has a good fleet of vehicles - air-conditioned Toyota cars.

Not very far from the hotel and to the center of Taba - seventeen kilometers. But most importantly, the hotel is located in such a place that the coasts of three neighboring countries - Israel, Jordan and Saudi Arabia - are visible from its territory. Guests say that special contemplation is their contemplation at night. The shores are buried in lights, and you can‚Äôt literally take your eyes off them. In addition, to the border with Israel - only thirty kilometers.

Gourmet Hotel

Intercontinental Taba Heights Resort is a real celebration for lovers of good cuisine! It has four restaurants, and each of them is good in its own way. Let's start with El Mar. This institution, as its name implies, specializes in seafood and fish. It is located right on the beach and is open exclusively for dinner. This is one of the most romantic places in the hotel. Enjoying the amazing taste of freshly caught clams, crayfish or grilled fish, you can watch the sunset over Aqaba Bay.

The Lagoon Bar and Restaurant mainly offers guests dishes of Spanish and Arabian cuisine. It seems to be in harmony with its menu architecture Intercontinental Taba Heights. Its terrace also offers views of the sea, and even on the shores of neighboring Saudi Arabia.

Another restaurant where you can contemplate the expanse of water is the Bay View. It is located in the main building and is open all day. In it you can have breakfast, and lunch, and dinner. Dishes of different nations are popular here, but sometimes there are thematic days, such as Italian, French, Cuban, Greek and so on. The main restaurant is famous for grilling - here you can try lamb, barbecue, squid, oysters.

And finally, Bird Nest introduces tourists to the delights of Thai cuisine. Of the bars, visitors especially recommend Splash near the main pool. They mainly make good hot grilled snacks and serve various drinks. The Reef Bar has good ice cream and English tea with pastries. A cafe "18 Hole" in the SPA center specializes in light and healthy food and fresh, which is especially good for dieters. Tourists say that local food often depends on the day of the week. For example, the best pastries on Tuesdays, and on Wednesdays - the most delicious seafood for dinner, in particular crabs.

It allows you to get to any of the four hotels of the Iintercontinental Taba Heights 5 * resort. She also carries those who wish to the local El Vecala market.

Sea, beach and pools

Intercontinental Taba Heights has its own beach, which is located near the main building. It is sandy, with a convenient entrance to the water. However, there is not a very diverse underwater world - the beach is actually only suitable for swimming, as, for example, the Mediterranean coast. There are many corals along the coast, as elsewhere in Egypt, but a special path has been cut through them into the water. Although the presence of special shoes would still be useful. The sea itself is very clean. Equipped beach - there are sun loungers, deck chairs, umbrellas. Towels are issued without deposit.

On the territory of the neighboring Sofitel Hotel, which is allowed to be used by guests of the Intercontinental Taba, there are beautiful corals and beautiful fish that you can watch for hours. There are good corals near the beach of the hotel itself, it‚Äôs only worth a little distance from the places of mass bathing and swim along the shelf. Therefore, you will not even need to book a special tour - you can explore the underwater world yourself. On the territory of Sofitel there is also a more convenient bottom for entry, there is a small pier.

Near the beach of Intercontinental there is a water entertainment center where you can go diving, go skiing and so on. In addition, on the main territory of the hotel there is an indoor pool and several open (in one of them the water is always warm because it is heated). One of the ponds has the shape of a fountain in which you can sunbathe. All pools are very clean, they are constantly cleaned and the temperature is checked. Two of them are connected in cascade. The water is pleasant for swimming and does not smell like bleach. For children built two special pools.



Excursions

One of the bonuses of how the Intercontinental Taba hotel is located is the opportunity to go on excursions to neighboring countries, namely Israel and Jordan. Some vacationers return to this hotel precisely because it is the most convenient for visiting these states. They visit Jerusalem and the Dead Sea in Israel, and Petra in Jordan. From the hotel it is very convenient to go to Mount Moses (about an hour). However, climbing there is only possible at night, and it is very difficult. Therefore, comfortable shoes for lifting are simply necessary. Twenty minutes from here to get to the island of the Pharaohs.

It is also worth a trip to the monastery of St. Catherine - one of the oldest Christian monasteries in the world. Here you can see the Transfiguration Basilica, a variety of chapels (including the Burning Cupids), as well as the well of Moses. All are included in the UNESCO list. The famous desert ATVs, a trip to the picturesque Colored Canyon and swimming in the Blue Lagoon are also very popular. All this is also within walking distance. They also recommend visiting an artificial salt cave in the city of Taba, where you can get a forty-five-minute rehabilitation session that has a healing effect on the body.

Prices

Of course, we always count money to figure out how to organize a budget when traveling to Egypt. Intercontinental Taba is one of the most popular hotels, and prices for travel packages for various tour operators start at forty-two thousand rubles for two for a week (with the all inclusive system). Eleven to twelve nights with half board will cost 56 thousand at least. To smoke a hookah, you have to pay three dollars or fifteen pounds.

As for the purchase of souvenirs and products, tourists recommend not buying them in a shopping complex at the hotel, but taking a free shuttle bus to the local market, where the state supermarket is located. The prices there are very low, since mainly the goods are intended for local residents. If you want to play golf, then it costs about thirty-five euros for two hours, along with the services of an instructor. Do you prefer to pay for everything with Egyptian pounds? Then change money in the hotel bank - everywhere they consider the dollar as one in five, and the local exchange rate is 5.5. The cost of excursions to Israel and Jordan at the hotel is about one hundred ninety to two hundred dollars per person. A trip to the Colored Canyon by jeep - $ 45. Excursion to the island of the Pharaohs - about fifty, if you sail on a yacht.
