Hotel 3 * BelleVue Dominican Bay: reviews, description. Dominican Republic, Boca Chica

Dominican Republic is a sunny country, which pleases tourists with picturesque beaches, always sunny weather, interesting culture and unhurried pace of life. Naturally, many travelers choose this country for their holidays. In resort towns there are many hotels of different sizes and categories. One of them is Hotel 3 * Bellevue Dominican Bay. Reviews of tourists about him are very positive, because this hotel can offer its customers a lot of entertainment along with sincere hospitality and excellent service.

Where is the hotel complex located?

Of course, first of all, people who are going on a trip are interested in the question of exactly where the Bellevue Dominican Bay 3 * hotel is located. Boca Chica is a cozy resort town on the sea coast, which consists almost entirely of hotels, cottages and guest houses. According to reviews, this is a real paradise for tourists - picturesque streets, a relaxed atmosphere, a sufficient number of entertainment and shopping complexes and, of course, picturesque beaches. By the way, the hotel is located on the third coastline. Las Americas Airport, which, incidentally, is mostly used by tourists, is 15 km away. Do not worry about distances if the Dominican Republic becomes your temporary home. Vouchers already include a transfer to the hotel.

Another good news is the distance to the vibrant city of Santo Domingo is only 30 km. This distance is easily overcome by public transport or by a rented car.

What does the hotel look like?





What is Hotel 3 Bellevue Dominican Bay? Reviews, as well as official information, state that the hotel occupies a rather large territory and consists of a complex of two- and three-story cottages. There are swimming pools, walking paths, terraces, secluded areas for relaxation and, of course, a large picturesque garden where you can fully enjoy the beauty of exotic plants. Moreover, the entire hotel area resembles a tropical garden - everywhere palm trees, bright flowers and flower beds.

Dominican Republic, Hotel Bellevue Dominican Bay 3: photo of rooms and a brief description





The hotel is considered relatively large, as there are 437 rooms of different capacities. As already mentioned, they are located in small cottages, and therefore you can count on peace, quiet and a small number of neighbors. What do the rooms at Hotel 3 * Bellevue Dominican Bay look like? Guest reviews suggest that the rooms are spacious with large windows and tiled floors. Almost every room adjoins a covered terrace or a spacious balcony, where you will find comfortable furniture and amazing views of the garden.

Naturally, guests can rely on a standard set of household appliances, which includes an air conditioning system, a large-screen TV (there are the most popular satellite channels), a telephone and a minibar with a cooling system. For a small fee, you can rent a safe in which tourists, as a rule, store documents and other valuables.

A spacious bathroom with modern fixtures and a shower will delight you. By the way, water is supplied constantly. Guests can count on a selection of quality cosmetics and clean towels that change every day. Cleaning is carried out regularly, and reviews indicate that the maids are doing their job well - the rooms are always fresh and clean. An iron and ironing board, as well as a hair dryer, can be delivered to the room upon request. Tourists with children can rely on a free extra bed.

Beach and water recreation: what awaits the traveler?





What conditions for a beach holiday does the Bellevue Dominican Bay 3 * hotel offer? Boca Chica is a town located on the seashore, and therefore there are really many beaches here. By the way, the hotel itself does not own a private section of the coast. Nevertheless, the nearest public beach is about 300 meters away - a convenient road leads to it. According to reviews, the coast is clean, the water is warm and calm, and the entrance to the sea is convenient, because the bottom is covered with soft sand. As for the sun loungers and umbrellas, they are, of course, available, but you can rent them for a fee, albeit a small one.

If simple swimming and sunbathing do not appeal to you, then there is the opportunity to do water sports, for example, do parasailing, go skiing and banana. There are excellent conditions for windsurfing on the coast - you can even hire an instructor if necessary. There are also scuba diving courses , because nearby the underwater world is bright and incredibly diverse.

Additional services offered by the hotel





What else can guests of Bellevue Dominican Bay 3 * count on? Dominican Republic is a country famous for its hospitality and high level of service. Therefore, even in a three-star hotel, tourists can count on a rather impressive range of additional services.

For example, almost the entire territory has access to the Internet, which is important for modern people traveling with various devices. There is a store where you can buy food and other household items, as well as a cozy and colorful souvenir shop with a large selection of interesting things that you should definitely bring from the Dominican Republic.

Currency exchange is constantly working. There is a spacious luggage storage room, and if desired, you can rent a safe at the reception. Guests can use the parking lot. By the way, here, in the hotel, tourists can rent a car or some other means of transport. Since the hotel is often used for corporate leisure and business, it offers a business center with all the necessary equipment, as well as a conference room for 400 people.

Leisure: how are the guests having fun?





Any tourist can find something to do on the hotel. For example, there are two spacious pools with cool clear water. Around are comfortable sun loungers with soft mattresses, as well as sun umbrellas. There is a rather large golf course (18 holes), and tennis courts are located nearby, where you can rent rackets, balls and other accessories. Guests can have fun playing billiards or table tennis.

There is a volleyball court, and group aerobics classes are held in the mornings. And anyone can sign up for dance lessons. Fans of fishing and horse riding will not have to be bored, as the hotel has all the conditions for this. Naturally, this is not all that the Dominican Republic has to offer. Vouchers, sightseeing tours, travel routes can be picked up and purchased at the hotel office or in the city. This is a country with a unique nature, interesting traditions and culture, and therefore travelers always have something to do.
