Negative hotel reviews

Thousands of guests visit the Park Inn Hotel . Sochi, Rosa Khutor is a popular tourist destination. But, unfortunately, some of the shortcomings of the hotel can overshadow the rest. The following negative comments should be especially noted:

  • ventilation is poor in the hotel corridors, which is why it is incredibly stuffy and has a stale smell;
  • the first shuttle to the sea goes at a quarter past nine in the morning, which is quite late;
  • on the beach there are very few sunbeds under sunshades;
  • the bathroom in the room is very cramped (there is nowhere to turn around);
  • hygiene products are provided only upon arrival, after which the maids do not report them (you have to call the reception desk and make a comment);
