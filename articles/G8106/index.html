Chrysalis Hotel 4 * (Greece, Crete, Hersonissos): description, service, reviews

One of the tourist destinations, which simply can not leave you indifferent, is the island of Crete. A trip to Crete is a luxurious beach vacation, unforgettable landscapes, long festivals and holidays, friendly people, many attractions and a huge number of establishments for a vibrant nightlife. In other words, no one will be bored here. In addition, this tourist destination is suitable for those who are considering the question of where it is better to go to Greece in September.

The advantage of a holiday in Crete also lies in the fact that there are both luxury hotels and hotels with more affordable prices. In this case, the quality of service will please in any case.

In the list of small hotels that are ready to give tourists their warmth and homeliness, it is necessary to mention the Chrysalis Hotel 4 *.

General Hotel Information

The building of the Chrysalis Hotel 4 * was built in 1996. The last time major repairs were carried out here only a few years ago (in 2013). This hotel belongs to mini-hotels. The building is a 2-story building with 3 buildings without an elevator, there are only 32 rooms. Today Chrysalis Hotel 4 * is a modern building, clean and comfortable. Design rooms and stylish furniture will make your stay here as comfortable and enjoyable as possible.





The hotel grounds are well thought out. There are green spaces, and places to relax, and paved paths.

Where is the hotel located

Hotel Chrysalis Hotel 4 * is located in the city of Hersonissos - the city center is only 2 km away. Due to such a favorable location, the hotel can offer its guests accommodation in a green quiet corner, while all the entertainment (day and night) will be within walking distance.

At a distance of 25 km from here is the city of Heraklion and the airport of the same name. You can take a taxi directly from here, rent a car or catch a bus (a bus stop is 1.5 km from the hotel).

Chrysalis Hotel 4 *: service at the hotel

This small hotel is ready to offer its guests high-quality service and a full range of services so that your holiday in Crete seems truly wonderful:



  • Reseption. You can use her services around the clock.
  • Parking. Private parking is available here (right on site). This service is paid.
  • The Internet. In public areas and hotel rooms you can use free WI-FI.
  • Air conditioners. Air conditioning is installed both in public areas and in rooms.
  • Concierge service.
  • Laundry service. Guests can take their things to the laundry at any time - you need to pay for the service separately.
  • Luggage storage. Luggage storage is available at the front desk.
  • Safe. All valuables of tourists will be under reliable protection.
  • Doctor's services.
  • Currency exchange. Here you can always change money, however, in local stores, restaurants and other institutions, they are willing to accept euros and dollars.

Business people can always use specialized services:

  • fax machine;
  • Xerox;
  • a printer.

In addition, for convenient travel around the neighborhood and a good pastime, tourists are offered additional services:

  • Transfer. If necessary, travelers can always order delivery from the airport to the hotel and back.
  • Car rental. This option is suitable for active tourists who are going to travel around the island on their own.
  • Excursions. The hotel will always help with purchasing tickets and organizing excursions to local attractions.

Rooms

Tourists staying here are offered a choice of several types of rooms:

  • Double rooms (double). This option involves 2 types of accommodation: with 1 double bed or 2 single beds. It offers garden views.
  • Family Room It has a double bed and 2 single beds. These rooms have sea views.
  • Superior double with sea view. This room has a double bed.




Room Amenities

The rooms in the Chrysalis Hotel 4 * are cozy rooms with a fresh repair, designer finishes and pretty good furniture. The design is made in neutral restrained shades with the addition of brown, orange and green accents.

Each apartment has a living and sleeping area, a bathroom, and a furnished balcony from where you can enjoy the view. The apartments have tiled floors.

Room service allows you to always maintain cleanliness and freshness:

  • room cleaning is carried out every day;
  • towels are changed in the bathroom every other day (3 times a week);
  • change of bed linen - 1 time in 7 days.

In the room there is everything that a vacationer may need during a stay in a hotel:

  • air conditioning (this is a central air conditioner with individual control);
  • safe - it is located right in the room and you can use it for free;
  • telephone;
  • TV - there are many channels;
  • shower - in the bathroom there is only a shower;
  • hair dryer;
  • toiletries are given (among them toilet paper, soap, shampoo and others);
  • The rooms are equipped with mini-refrigerators;
  • mini-bar - filled with bottled water on the day of arrival of guests.

Entertainment at the hotel

This small hotel cannot offer a lot of entertainment, as it is designed mainly for quiet living in a green corner of Hersonissos. Vacationers can entertain themselves:

  • billiards;
  • swimming in the outdoor pool - there is fresh water, and the size of the pool allows you to spend time here with pleasure (area 96 sq. m, and depth 2 m).




Those who took a ticket to Crete for a more active holiday, Hersonissos can offer a lot of options. Everything is within walking distance:

  • noisy cafes, restaurants, night clubs;
  • well equipped beaches;
  • attractions;
  • beautiful places in the city for walking;
  • shops, bazaars, souvenir shops.

Dining at the hotel

The hotel has:

  • main restaurant;
  • bar.

Located at the Chrysalis Hotel 4 *, tourists receive an all-inclusive service based on breakfast. There is a restaurant on site serving international cuisine. Delicious dishes made exclusively from fresh ingredients are served as a buffet. There is always a good selection of meat, salads, fresh fruits, so both vegetarians and meat-eaters will be satisfied.

In the hotel bar you can have a light snack and drink soft and alcoholic drinks - here they are presented for every taste. In the evenings, barbecue and theme parties are held here.

A wider range of dishes can be found in every city restaurant. Here, every tourist will find an institution with their favorite cuisine, however, having arrived on the island of Crete, you should definitely try the local cuisine.

Conditions for children

Couples with children often stay at the hotel, so the staff offers the creation of comfortable conditions for the youngest travelers:

  • baby cots in the room;
  • garden;
  • playground.

Things to do in the area

Very few tourists prefer to spend their entire vacation exclusively on the beach. Usually, after several days of swimming, there is a desire to walk through the streets of the city and see the surroundings. For active travelers it is recommended to choose not too hot time. For excursions, it is better to go to Greece in September, where hundreds of seekers of new experiences seek every year.

It should be noted that Hersonissos offers a wide selection of entertainment. Getting to them from the Chrysalis Hotel 4 * hotel is as easy as shelling pears:

  1. The fountain of Sarakino. Lovers of antiquities will surely appreciate this attraction. An ancient Roman fountain is located right on the city street. It is quite well preserved, but has not been working for a long time. It is a 20-minute walk from the hotel.
  2. Church of St. Paraskeva. This small building with a very interesting and long history is located near the port. You can reach it in 20 minutes.
  3. Lychnostatis. This is the Ethnographic Museum of Crete, located directly in the open. Traveling here will allow you to get acquainted with the history, life and life of the local population. Located 3.8 km from the hotel.
  4. Aquaworld Aquarium Hersonissos has a wonderful aquarium. Here, adults and children will greatly enjoy exploring turtles, fish, pythons, and iguanas. The peculiarity of this aquarium is that it was originally created to save animals in distress. It will take about 20 minutes to get there.
  5. Water parks Star Beach and Acqua Plus. These establishments are perfect for families with children.




Young people (and not very young) who prefer club life, parties and other nightly entertainment will certainly be satisfied. With the onset of darkness, dozens of restaurants, cafes, and popular night clubs begin to work. Many of them close only at 6-7 in the morning.

Beaches

The quality of beaches is of interest to every second tourist, and this is understandable, because it is precisely after the sea waves and a good tan that hundreds of tourists come here.

The hotel is located on the first coastline - to the beach of Chrysalis Hotel 4 * only 50 meters. Here it is sand and pebble. Those who wish can use the sun loungers and umbrellas, however, these services will need to be paid. For a comfortable stay there are changing cabins, a toilet and a shower, so you can spend all day here. You can have a little refreshment right here - beach bars with snacks, fast food and cold drinks are at your service.





This beach, located near the hotel, is well suited for a quiet holiday. Surf lovers can go to the central beach of the city (it is located just west of the port). It is here that the ideal conditions are to catch the wave.

Near the water park there is Star Beach, here you can lie quietly under an umbrella, and jump from a bungee, and dive.

additional information

Check-in at 14:00.

Check-out is at noon.

Pets are strictly prohibited on site.

Smoking is allowed in strictly limited areas.

Chrysalis Hotel 4 *: reviews

Among the reviews of vacationers there are both positive and negative. Those guests who were not completely satisfied with the accommodation indicated:

  • old plumbing;
  • insufficiently varied food (according to many, the hotel does not correspond to 4 stars in the quality of the cuisine).

However, the positive descriptions of the Chrysalis Hotel 4 * are much more. Moreover, even those who pointed out the shortcomings of the hotel, nevertheless noted:

  • Responsive Russian-speaking staff;
  • the cleanliness of the rooms;
  • good beaches, a sufficient number of sunbeds and umbrellas;
  • a large number of attractions in the area;
  • reasonable prices for accommodation.




In other words, in Greece Chrysalis Hotel 4 * is a small cozy hotel offering tourists quite comfortable accommodation in a picturesque corner of Crete. Accommodation here is suitable for couples, vacationers with children, business travelers, young people (do not forget that Hersonissos is famous for its vibrant nightlife). When booking a room here, you need to remember that entertainment and basic food will need to be sought in urban establishments.
