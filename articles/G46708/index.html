The greatest tank battles in the past?

Tanks are not created to fight tanks. They have a different task, and it is concluded (depending on the class of the car) either in rapid advancement behind enemy lines to cover defensive areas, or in direct hacking of enemy fortifications. In the first case, light tanks are used, in the second - medium and heavy.





The greatest tank battles in the history of this kind of troops took place in the 20th century, during the Second World War. Each of these battles was the result of the erroneous actions of the General Staffs, which necessitated the adoption of emergency measures to prevent a breakthrough of the front. However, such situations arose repeatedly in the last century.

If we evaluate the greatest tank battles by the number of military vehicles that took part in them, then in such super-battles several historical episodes can be considered, in which hundreds of armored vehicles fought simultaneously.

The small North African village of El Alamein became world famous due to the fact that in 1944 in the desert not far from it, six hundred German and Italian tanks met with the British, the number of which was 1,200 vehicles.
