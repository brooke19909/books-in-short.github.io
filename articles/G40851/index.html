Advantages and disadvantages of plastic carbines

The main advantages of plastic carbines:

  • lightness, wear resistance and strength;
  • small dimensions;
  • simplicity and reliability of fixation, accessible even to a child;
  • operating comfort: a plastic carbine will not make it harder to wear, it will not ring when moving, but it will add comfort to the process of using a backpack or bag, and will add style and originality;
  • low cost;
  • resistance to chemical and mechanical influences;
  • retains its original color;
  • Does not affect the properties and color of the fabric.

One drawback is that plastic carabiners for backpacks and bags have low tensile strength. They can not be seriously overloaded and used in the manufacture of sports climbing equipment. An exception is the fittings made of high-strength polyamide.
