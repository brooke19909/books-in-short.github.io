Spain in November: rest, weather, reviews

In November in Spain, weather conditions resemble early autumn in our country. The heat is rapidly disappearing, and the sun begins to warm less and less. Those who love beach vacations do not have to go to Spain for this. However, those tourists who want to visit historical sights, various cultural events and take a walk along the streets with a unique color and atmosphere can well enjoy the beauties of this country at this time of year. Spain in November is an ideal place for a romantic pastime. In this publication, we will talk about what a tourist should do in the fall in Spain and how to plan his vacation.

Weather in Spain in November

There are very few sunny days this month. It rains more and more throughout the country. If you believe meteorologists, then November can please travelers with only nine to ten sunny days. In addition to precipitation, a strong wind blows on the seashore. Moreover, the latter is the main characteristic of Spanish late autumn. This is especially true for coastal cities. Thus, the weather in Spain in November is relatively cold (of course, when compared with the usual heat in summer). Reviews of tourists indicate that due to low temperatures and cold water on the beach, it becomes impossible to relax at this time of the year. The air warms up slightly.

You can significantly save your money on hotel and hotel services. According to tourists' reviews, food at this time will also be cheap. What to see in Spain in November?

Spain in November is not boring, and confirmation of this is the reviews of tourists who visited here. At this time of the year a large number of young people and pensioners come here, for whom prices for accommodation and comfortable conditions for walking are important. Various pastimes, museums and concerts remain a popular pastime. Granada is the venue for the traditional Jazz Festival, and Seville is the venue for the International Film Festival. In addition, it is the beginning of November in Spain - this is the time of the Olive Day holiday , which every tourist arriving at this time should visit.





Feast of olives

November 9 in Spain begin to celebrate Olive Day. This is a traditional event, in particular for the Andalusian region and the city of Baen. The next few days become the time for festivals, exhibitions and fairs for these places. This region is one of the leaders in the production of olive products. It will be difficult for you to refrain from tasting oil and participating in various festivals. If tourists go on excursions, they are told there how olives are grown, harvested and cooked. You can learn the secrets of how oil is made. After the holiday of olives you will not remain indifferent to this product. For the real Spaniard, this is one of the national symbols that feeds a significant part of the country.





Madrid in late fall

As you can see from the above, tourists who enjoy a cultural pastime will enjoy Spain in November. Prices have a budget holiday, which you can not forget for a long time. Madrid is no exception. It is here that you can appreciate all the sights (of which there are quite a lot) in a relaxed atmosphere without suffering from the summer heat.

Late in the fall, Madrid becomes one of the centers of European culture. The capital is the venue for the Autumn Art Festival, where you can enjoy the performances of dance, theater, circus and music groups from different parts of the Earth. Performances and concerts are mainly held at city venues. Most often these are theatrical scenes. Guests at the festival also attend various performances and go to galleries, in addition, there are meetings with celebrities and artists.
