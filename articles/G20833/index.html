Where to spend an unforgettable vacation in the Ryazan region

When planning their vacation, everyone wants to spend time in the best way to relax both body and soul. If you are attracted by the unique nature, rivers, fishing, a Russian bath, then the best place for this is Ryazan region. Holidays with children or a youth company will not be boring. But, before you go to these places, it is important to decide on a specific direction. To begin with, it is worth finding out what recreation centers are in the Ryazan region.





Chalet "Meshchera"

This base is located 73 km from Ryazan. She settled in the Meshchersky protected areas, on the river Pra. You can come here to rest at any time of the year. The hotel complex is designed for 200 guests. In the summer, you can go to the beach located on the river, in the winter stroll through the picturesque surroundings. The base has infrastructure, which includes a sauna, bar, club, tennis, billiards, gazebos with barbecue facilities and more. A playground is provided for children. You can visit the mushroom house, and if you wish, go along the mushroom route. River trips and excursions around the Meshchera park are also organized.

The base has access to the boat station. You can rent fishing or camping equipment. In winter frosts, boots and skis are offered. Such a vacation in the Ryazan region allows you to plunge into a real Russian fairy tale.





"Meshchersky Gorodets"

Another base that allows you to touch the Russian nature. The peculiarity of this place is that it is possible to rent for a whole month a separate furnished cottage made of timber. There are extensive hunting grounds on the territory of the base, along which the picturesque rivers of Unzha, Unkura, Ksengzha flow. In the summer, many go on forest routes in search of herbs, berries and mushrooms. In winter, skiing or snowmobiling are possible. On a frosty evening, you are invited to visit the bathhouse. Usually recreation centers in the Ryazan region offer to try real Russian cuisine.

Terekhovo

This place is chosen by people who want to do good fishing or to hunt. The territory itself, which belongs to the base, is small, but in its possessions there are rooms, a two-story bathhouse, a cafe, billiards, a banquet hall, rest rooms, places for barbecue facilities and a picturesque pond. The whole territory is fenced. The village itself Terekhovo stands on the banks of the Oka.





"Assumption gave"

This recreation center is fully consistent with its beautiful name. It is located in the Skopinsky district, in the center of picturesque nature. On the shore of a fabulous pond there is a village of cottages, which can be rented in full, or you can choose one of the most liked houses. Each mansion has its own summer kitchen, bathhouse. You can order lunch in your room so as not to waste time cooking. At the disposal of vacationers there are boats and the opportunity to go fishing. Enjoy the walk along the pond and through the lush groves.

When it snows in winter, vacationers take skis or snowmobiles and go for a walk. Not far from the base there is an entertainment center where you can go in the evening. There is billiards, bowling, massage, a huge bathhouse and more. Such a vacation in the Ryazan region will appeal to every traveler.

Chulis

On the territory of the Meshchersky National Park there is another base - Chulis. This area is surrounded by forests and lakes. If you visit "Chulis", the rest in the Ryazan region becomes unforgettable, since the mixed forest and tranquility prevailing there are beneficial for the nervous system. Also of great importance is clean air.





In addition to the standard infrastructure (cafes, billiards, barbecue, fishing, etc.), here you can walk on horseback or rent bicycles. You can enjoy riding a jeep.

So that vacationers feel unity with nature, they are lodged in wooden houses. If desired, you can order individual meals from ecological products that are grown in this area.

Holidays in the Ryazan region fell in love with many city dwellers, so the area offers many tourist centers where you can fully enjoy nature and relaxation. For example, you can consider as an option such places: "Friendship", "Dolgovskoye", "Barsky fun", "Sumbulovo", "Lake", "Pine forest" and many others.
