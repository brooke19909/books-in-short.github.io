Kolyvan, Altai Territory - an original vacation that will leave a vivid impression

The modern tourist has a huge selection of countries and places for active and exciting holidays. Often preference is given to the vastness of Russia. And this is not at all surprising, because each strip has its own characteristics. Rest on the beach, sightseeing, hiking, river rafting - all this is feasible without going abroad.

So you will get acquainted with historical facts and see natural beauty, staying in the village of Kolyvan. Altai Krai is generally rich in tourist routes, including mountain passes, river valleys, ancient tracts and much more.



History

The foundation time of this settlement is considered to be 1727. It was then that Akinfy Demidov built a plant for the smelting of copper and silver in the northwestern foothills of the ridges on the Belaya River. This production was included in the vast complex of Altai metal manufacturing. And the main control point of the organization was Kolyvan. Altai Territory was the backbone of the country's metallurgy.

Stone carving art

At the beginning of the 19th century, metal mining ceased, and stone began to be processed in the walls of the plant. The products of the new masters became so popular that product samples were sent to different parts of the world and decorated the palaces of the ruling persons. The stone processing plant has been operating for two centuries. The company survived the difficult years after the collapse of the USSR, making tiles for paving sidewalks. Now its products are jewelry, art products, elements for technical equipment. A small museum in the village is dedicated to the extraction and processing of stone. There are collected natural samples and products that reflect the development of the industry.



Places to visit

As a reference point for the study of beauties untouched by man, Kolyvan is well suited. Altai Territory has many points for a relaxing pastime and outdoor activities. All this is in relative proximity to each other.

Mount Sinokha is a place for pilgrims and lovers of hiking. On the slopes beat sources that are considered holy. From a height of 1210 m above the sea surface, panoramas of Zmeinogorsk and other elevations and reservoirs open. Paragliders found that it is very convenient to fly from an elevated position, which is why the place has become a favorite extreme point.

Jealous - one of the peaks of the ridge. It was in it in the 19th century that huge deposits of green jasper were discovered, from which columns were made in the Cathedral of Christ the Savior. Taiga vegetation, spaciousness and extraordinary views will appear for those who go to conquer the heights.



Ponds

Another platform where everyone will find entertainment to their liking is Lake Kolyvan. The Altai Territory here offers not only ascetic walks along the plains and hills, but also beach activities and relaxation. The banks of the reservoir are covered with coarse sand, the water is transparent, there are no drops in depth. Tourists rent special houses, stop in camping areas or set up a tent separately and live in "savages." The water saucer is surrounded by rocky formations, along the perimeter an ancient chilli nut grows . All this for those who do not like to bask in the sun or ride a "banana".

One of the favorite tourist routes is Kolyvan (Altai Territory) - Lake Beloe. To stay, they stop at a camp site, which is located nearby. It is also possible to put up a tent in a campsite or in a public parking lot. In the pond you can swim and fish.
