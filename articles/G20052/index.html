Rest in Samara. Chalets of Tsarevshchina

Between the two tributaries of the Volga, the Samarka and Sok rivers, is a million-plus city. Since ancient times, it is not for nothing that people settled here. A wonderful mild climate, beautiful river banks, one of the pearls of Russian nature - the Samara onion, as well as the Zhiguli mountains, the old adits, Tsarev Kurgan and many others attract many tourists. The region has potential for a good rest. The article describes the camp sites in Tsarevshchina in Samara along the banks of the Sok River, not far from the estuary and the Volga.

Along the banks of the Sok River

The coast of Sok has long been a favorite vacation spot of many Samaritans. Throughout the river has preserved almost virgin coast, you can enjoy the unity with nature. The main tourist routes for lovers of rafting begin in the spring, along the big water from the village of Sokolinka. A week you can admire beautiful places. Of course, these are not sports routes along mountain rivers. More suitable for leisurely walks with the family. Along the tributary of the Kondurcha river, routes for kayaking enthusiasts are laid. A weekend weekend in May can be very exciting.

In summer, the river attracts beach lovers. It is pleasant to spend a day at calm, well-warmed water with family or close friends, and visit camp sites in the Tsarevshchina. In Samara, this place is famous not only for its good beaches and beautiful views, but also for the availability of public transport.

Village Tsarevshchina

Tsarevschina has been known since the beginning of the 18th century. Many noble boyars and members of the royal family owned lands here. That there are only such names as Dolgorukovs, Bashkovs, Panchulidze. Today, Tsarev Kurgan has tourist camps, among which are the camp sites "Islet", "Pine", "Marina of the Sun" and others.

There are legends that Tamerlan feasted on the top of this mound covered in golden brocade after the victory over Tokhtamysh. Hence the name of the mound. According to another legend, much later during the Azov campaign, Peter the Great personally crowned the summit with the Orthodox cross.

Chalet "Islet"

But this is yesterday. And where to spend time for modern residents and guests in Samara in the Tsarevshchina? Chalets provide the opportunity for a good rest.

Between the lakes Black and Krivoye cozy houses of the Ostrovok camp site are located. The size of the cottage allows even a large company to spend time comfortably. During the day, guests have the opportunity to relax on the beach, engage in sports games - volleyball, football or basketball. For lovers of billiards provided excellent tables. In the evening, you can sit near the grill with aromatic barbecue, enjoy a meal. At this time, there are always held discos. You can relax comfortably, fun.

For everyone, whether it's a couple in love or a family with children, a young company or the elderly, there is something to do.

Rest on the Volzhanka

On the other hand, near Lake Krivoy, closer to Lake Beloe, in a picturesque place are the buildings of the Volzhanka camp site in Tsarevshchina.

Samara is well aware of this place. It offers guests a football field, tennis courts, bowling, a sauna with a pool. On a rainy day, you can visit the indoor sports complex. In addition to a two-story building, a motel, 6-8 local cottages, summer houses are provided for guests. For families there is the opportunity to leave the child in the children's room, play with their peers. According to reviews, the base provides all services to vacationers at the highest level. Celebratory and sporting events are held regularly. Children under 5 years old can be taken with them for free, up to 13 years old a discount is provided.

Features of Kalinka

Closer to Tolyatti, on the shore of Lake Kuprino, the Kalinka camp site was located in the Tsarevshchina. Samara is famous for its Volga shores and coastal lakes, picturesque oak groves and the mighty Zhiguli mountains. You can enjoy all this by visiting Kalinka.

Comfortable cottages with summer houses are located on the grove in such a way that create the coziness of solitude and tranquility. A bar and a modern dining room are provided for guests to relax. On the territory there is a bathhouse, a well-equipped sports town. There is a playground. Fans of outdoor activities can ride on the Volga in a comfortable boat, rent a boat or catamaran, jet ski. You can fight with a big company at a paintball training ground. Horse riding is also available. For fishing lovers - cozy quiet places with a good bite. In the evenings you can relax in the disco.

Above are not all camp sites in Tsarevshchina in Samara. You can truly appreciate the quality of relaxation by visiting the place. The choice is provided for every taste, with any income level.
