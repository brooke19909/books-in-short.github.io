"Gloria Golf" (Turkey / Belek): hotel description and reviews

Russian tourists come to Turkish Belek quite often. They are attracted here by magnificent sandy beaches and amazing healing air, saturated with the scents of pine forests that surround almost every hotel complex in this resort. Add to this the unique local flavor and oriental hospitality with which all tourists are welcomed here, and you will come across a formula for excellent relaxation, which is well known to representatives of the host country in Turkey. Of the many Belek hotels, our compatriots often stay at the Gloria Golf hotel complex. It is one of three chain hotels and is considered the best of them. If you also think about purchasing a tour to this particular hotel, but cannot find the necessary information about it, then we are ready to help you with this. From our article you will find out where the Gloria Golf Hotel is located, as well as what are the features of living within its walls.





Brief description of the hotel complex

Hotel Gloria Golf (Turkey) is a luxurious complex surrounded by a park of pine trees. And its entire territory is literally buried in verdure and luxurious exotic colors.

The hotel complex was built in 1997, in the same period of time he received his first guests. Since the hotel is popular with tourists, it is not surprising that after several years of work, it began to need serious repairs, which was carried out about nine years ago. The hotel complex is assigned five stars, which it fully confirms. This is evidenced by his guests, who happened to relax in such a wonderful place.

It is noteworthy that guests of the Gloria Golf Resort Hotel can use all the services in the Gloria Verde hotel complex. This makes it possible to visit almost both hotels at the same time and compare their level of service and design.

The Gloria Golf Hotel has several buildings built in different parts of a vast territory. The main building consists of four floors and it houses restaurants, lobbies and cafes. In addition to the main building, guests are accommodated in two two-story blocks, as well as villas with different floors and comfort. In general, the hotel complex has five hundred and twenty-five rooms of several categories.





Hotel Location

The hotel complex "Gloria Golf" is located in Belek. This resort has a very mild climate, here you will not suffer from suffocating heat and you can enjoy long walks. Many of our compatriots claim that the air in Belek has healing power and after resting here you can forget about bronchitis and other diseases associated with the respiratory system for a long time.

The Gloria Golf Hotel is located approximately seven kilometers from the center of spa life. Vacationers overcome this distance by public transport or a rented car. The road takes no more than five to seven minutes.

The village of Serik, where the Gloria Golf Hotel is located directly, belongs to small settlements that develop thanks to the presence of tourists. A few years ago, Serik could not boast of a rich infrastructure, but now there is everything necessary for a good and memorable vacation.

The nearest airport to the hotel complex is in Antalya. To get to the hotel from it, you have to drive at least forty kilometers. Tourists usually overcome this way by transport belonging to the host side. All hotels provide their guests with a shuttle service to their accommodation.





Hotel Pools

The hotel complex occupies a vast territory, so it can afford a large number of infrastructure facilities. There are at least fourteen pools here. Several of them are equipped for children and are located on the territory of the game club. The largest is the outdoor pool, whose area is one thousand two hundred and eighty squares. It is often called Olympic. The Gloria Golf Resort Hotel (Belek) also has several indoor pools. Those that have the function of heating the water only work in the winter. The rest function in the summer season. It is very convenient for them to swim in those who are afraid of sunburn under the hot rays of the Turkish sun.

Hotel spa

Judging by the reviews of the guests of the Gloria Golf Hotel (Turkey), here is one of the best spa centers in the country. In La Source (the name of this luxurious salon) you will find an indescribable aroma of fragrant herbs and sparkle of candles. Each room has its own sound system to tune clients to the desired mood.

The spa offers several types of massage, a sauna, a Turkish bath and other treatments that will turn any woman into a real oriental princess. The most popular are the following services:

  • Balinese massage with aromatic oils and a cleansing effect.
  • Ayurveda massage helps relieve nervous and physical stress, and also promotes cell regeneration and pacification of the mind.
  • Body peeling is carried out with the help of products created on the extract of grapes and pomegranate. The procedure turns any skin into a kind of delicate silk.
  • Nepali massage. It is not carried out with the help of hands, but through sound. For the procedure, special bowls with a deep and low-frequency sound are used. After such a massage, a person feels refreshed and healthy.

It is pleasant that in the spa each client is found to have a special approach and individually select procedures.

Hotel kids club

Guests leave the maximum number of positive reviews about this place. Gloria Golf does not have its own children's club, but small guests spend time in a single club belonging to this hotel chain. It is called Gogi and accepts children of almost any age. Here they are waiting for exciting contests and competitions, as well as incredible show programs. To make all young guests interested, the club is divided into three subgroups, based on the age category of children:

  • From a year to three years. Here you can safely leave the kids and not worry that they will miss their parents. This club has its own swimming pool, a games room and playgrounds, a cook who cooks according to a special menu, and a cozy bedroom.
  • From four to seven years. Entertainment is selected according to the age of the children. A trampoline was prepared for them on the beach, radio-controlled cars, fairs, pirate parties and so on. Children are completely delighted with the day spent in the club.
  • From eight to twelve years old. Teenagers will be interested in sports, a climbing wall, trampolines, rifle shooting and the like.

At the kids club, guests can rent a baby monitor, stroller or other items for the baby. Upon request, a nanny will sit with your child, but this service is also charged extra.

Sports activities in the hotel complex

Some vacationers believe that the concept of the hotel has a certain sports component. Therefore, on its territory there is absolutely everything in order to maintain and even improve your athletic form.

From the earliest morning until late evening, the hotel complex has a gym. It is equipped with modern equipment, so classes turn into a real pleasure.

Hotel guests can play tennis, football, volleyball and other team games. Not the last place in the ranking of popular sports is water sports. These include diving, water skiing and water gymnastics. You can also try parasailing and windsurfing.
