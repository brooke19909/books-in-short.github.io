general information

A green corner of nature with a relict park was conveniently located on the territory of the former estate of the princes Baryatinsky (1811-1820). The small village of Maryino (Kursk region), where the health resort is located, is a palace complex of a family estate with an original architectural and landscape design. When you get here, you instantly feel the special atmosphere that charges with healing energy.

The pearl of this place is a pure oval pond with artificial islands and elegant bridges. There is a boat station, equipped places for swimming and sunbathing.





The preventive maintenance has preserved the medieval spirit of Russian culture, which makes it unlike any other. He began to receive guests from 1923, and to this day, guests from different parts of the Russian Federation come here for clean air and treatment. A special advantage of this place is its remoteness from the highway and factories. The health resort is considered one of the best in the area.

Children from the age of five are accepted for recovery. The cost of the tour includes full meals, insurance, transfer to the hotel and sightseeing tours to the Yusupov estate, Maryinsky park and the Museum of Local Lore.

Number of rooms

They accommodate guests in three-story buildings, which are interconnected by a wide glass corridor. All rooms of the sanatorium "Maryino" (Kursk region) are decorated with ancient stucco, wall paintings and bas-reliefs made of plaster. All this gives the room pomp and noble gloss.





Apartments of all categories are equipped with bath and toilet rooms. There is a soft large bed with bedside tables and wardrobes. Hot drinks facilities are available. There is a TV and a fridge. Superior rooms have seating areas and an entrance hall. Daily maids wet cleaning and changing beds.

Nutrition

The Maryino Prevention Center (Kursk Region), a photo of which can be seen in the article, has its own subsidiary farm on the territory, where organic and natural vegetables and fruits are grown. It also produces meat, dairy products and even honey. The hotel is treated with special care: there is always a lot of greens, pastry and bakery products on the tables. The menu abounds with meat and fish delicacies, as well as delicious desserts.

Wellness Services





Various specialists work in the staff of the Maryino health resort (Kursk region), including neuropathologists, dentists, psychotherapists, otolaryngologists, urologists, gynecologists, and pediatricians. The task of the sanatorium is to restore the lost health of patients and raise seriously ill people to their feet. Specialists take people with circulatory pathologies, diseases of the musculoskeletal and digestive systems. It helps patients with respiratory, genitourinary, skin and nervous diseases.

The medical center conducts microbiological, biochemical, hormonal and radiation studies. The treatment base includes balneological methods of therapy, apply mud in the form of applications and hydrotherapy. Treatment is not complete without non-pharmacological (hardware massage, reflexology, exercise therapy) and physiotherapeutic procedures (magnetotherapy, laser therapy, ultrasound treatment, etc.).

Cultural and leisure activities





Sanatorium "Maryino" (Kursk region) is a multifunctional health resort with a rich internal infrastructure. Within walking distance is a well-maintained beach. There is a rescue team on the coast. There are places for children. The beach is equipped with sun loungers and showers. There is a water entertainment center.

The building itself has a swimming pool with water slides. Water aerobics classes are regularly held here. Ski equipment is available for hire in winter. The hotel always has something to do: year-round sports grounds for active games are open, comfortable and safe conditions for children (play area and playground) are organized.

Book lovers will be delighted with a good collection of books in the library. It features a professional beauty salon with beauty treatments and massage rooms. In the evenings, you can visit the concert hall or disco. For children and adults, the territory of the Maryino Hotel (Kursk Region) constantly hosts musical events, animated shows and competitions.
