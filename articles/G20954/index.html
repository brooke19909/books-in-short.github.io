Turkey, Bodrum Hotel - what are the best hotels bearing this name?

Tourists who are going on a trip abroad to sunbathe often think about what to choose - the island of Kos or a vacation in Turkey. Bodrum, whose hotels are often located on the shores of the Aegean Sea, can offer many attractive places. This is a town with several beautiful bays, in which there are resort villages that are part of a larger settlement. In the center of it there are also many beaches, but they are quite "populated", and often there is nowhere for an apple to fall.



All hotels are located near the town or on its territory. Of course, small family hotels are located in the center, and large luxurious palaces are located on the outskirts of Bodrum resort (Turkey). 4 and 5 star hotels in this region offer almost the same services, but tourists believe that the local Quartet is much better than the Antalyan Quintuple. The owners of the best hotels in this region are patriots. Not without reason, many of these hotels have the name of the city in their name. Let us briefly review some of them.

Turkey, Bodrum Magic Life Hotel

This "five" is now called the "Imperium", but many remember it under the old name. The hotel is built as a chic palace in the style of the Ottoman Empire. Vacationers seem to be transferred to the series "The Magnificent Century," so everything here shines with luxury. Elegant three-story buildings, the cleanest sand and pebble beach (blue flag), the amazing nature around ... Turkey, Bodrum Rixos Premium Hotel

This hotel is located in a very prestigious area - Zeytinkave Bay. The hotel stands along the Cleopatra beach and occupies a vast territory of 187 hectares. The hotel is designed for very demanding tourists. Therefore, there are a myriad of different types of rooms - Deluxe, Premium, Superior, Royal Suite, as well as luxurious villas - some with panoramic views. This is a club hotel for vacationers who value comfort and tranquility. At the same time, a club car will help to get out into the village. Internet and water activities, iPods and in-room movies, as well as much more, are included in the list of free services.



Turkey, Bodrum Marmara Hotel

Designer boutique hotel. Its architecture and layout was developed by Turkish and French specialists. Although the hotel is not located on the beach, but on a hill, the stunning views that open from above cover all the inconvenience. Moreover, an excellent shuttle service is organized to the sea. And from the bizarre pool, located on a hill, even the Greek island of Kos is visible. Beautiful and comfortable wooden furniture, excellent location (near the marina, and the famous Bodrum castle a kilometer from the hotel), numerous services and excellent staff are bearing fruit. Turkey, Bodrum Holiday Resort & Spa Hotel

Winner of a gold certificate for the purity of water and the quality of food, this hotel is located closest to the city center. It is the oldest of the fives described above, but was completely renovated in 2004. The hotel is located in Icmeler Bay. There are standard, two-room rooms, as well as suites with living rooms and several bedrooms. The hotel is very good for people who value outdoor activities - it has a small water park, as well as many sports and water activities. The hotel has several buildings - the usual, "club" and for VIP-s. The hotel is surrounded by a beautiful olive grove.
