Bus route No. 125 Sochi - Adler

Adler is the southernmost of the four inner-city districts of the resort city of Sochi. Here is located the international airport, railway station, Olympic Park. Therefore, a good road connection between Adler-Sochi and Adler was established along the route of bus No. 125 to Sochi.

End stops and distances

The distance between Adler and Sochi is 35 kilometers.

Bus route number 125 in Sochi has four types of route:

  1. No. 125 - "Moremall" - Hosta - Kudepsta - agricultural "Rossiya" It combines the most stops, as the bus runs along the route with a stop in the resort villages Khosta and Kudepsta and stops at each bus stop.
  2. No. 125 (fast) - Sochi railway station - Adler railway station - Olympic Park. The shortest and fastest route, as the bus stops at only six stops.
  3. No. 125c (fast) - Sochi railway station - Adler railway station - Rossiya state farm. One of the most optimal bus routes No. 125 in Sochi, as it also attracts with the speed of travel to the final destination and the demand for stops.
  4. No. 125p - stop "4th city hospital" - Psou checkpoint. The longest route, which ends at a checkpoint on the Russian-Abkhaz border.

Interval of movement and fare

Buses run at intervals of 15-30 minutes. A clear schedule is not respected due to unforeseen difficulties in driving vehicles on the roads.

Suburban transport runs along the Adler-Sochi route along the only highway along the coast. The time for a trip along the route of bus No. 125 to Sochi with stops will be from 1 hour to 1 hour 40 minutes. The understudy of Kurortny Prospekt and the Adler-Dagomys bypass road have now been commissioned, but city and suburban vehicles do not drive them. Therefore, the main highway is very heavy.

The fare on the 125 Sochi bus route depends on the carrier. The ticket price range from the initial station to the final station is from 86 rubles to 120 rubles.
