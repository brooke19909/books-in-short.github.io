Routes "Lugansk - Moscow": what types of transport can be reached?

The situation in the LPR and DPR is currently such that it is rather dangerous to be there for civilians. Therefore, many citizens of this republic are moving to live in Russia. And some of them go to Moscow. Of course, many migrants from the LPR who came to the Russian Federation from the LNR also suffered greatly during the hostilities of Lugansk. To get to the capital of Russia to the residents of this city, if necessary, is not difficult. Next, we consider what routes exist from Lugansk to Moscow.

Bus service

The easiest way to get to the capital of Russia from the LPR is on this type of transport. Today, several companies carry passengers from Lugansk to Moscow. For example, Neoplan, Setra , Man, Scania . Tickets for the route can be purchased, including online, on the official website of the selected carrier. Travel time on the route " Lugansk - Moscow" is 19-20 hours. The cost of a ticket, depending on the carrier, can vary between 2-2.5 thousand rubles. for one person.





You can get information about routes for this particular day on the official website of the Lugansk bus station. Flights from this city arrive at the Krasnogvardeiskaya bus station in Moscow. The subway entrance is about 50 meters from it.

Also, the bus "Pervomaisk - Moscow" runs through this city. He arrives in the capital of the Russian Federation at a stop located near the entrance to the metro - at the Novoyasenevskaya station. Another flight goes to Moscow via Lugansk from Stakhanov. This bus arrives in the capital of the Russian Federation to the South Gate.

Bus routes "Moscow - Lugansk" do not pass through Ukraine . In the Belovodsk area, buses turn east - into Russia. All flights go further through Voronezh.





Most companies send quite comfortable buses to Russia. Therefore, a 20-hour trip to Moscow for a resident of Lugansk will certainly not be too tiring. If desired, a bus ticket for the route connecting the two cities can be purchased on any day of the week.

Is there a train "Moscow - Lugansk"?

Unfortunately, there is no railway connection between Russia and the LPR, as well as the DPR, for 2017. Until recently , a high-speed train ran between these two cities . However, this flight was canceled due to known events.

So, the Lugansk-Moscow train for 2017 does not exist. Therefore, in order to get to the capital of the Russian Federation by rail, the residents of Lugansk will first need to take a bus to a Russian city, for example, Millerovo, and here you can catch a train to Moscow. However, this option may not be very convenient. A train ticket from Millerovo to the capital of the Russian Federation costs around 1800-2000 rubles. For a trip by bus to this village from Lugansk, you will have to pay about 300 rubles. In terms of finance, such a trip will cost about the same amount as for Moscow by bus. But the road will take much more time.





Can I fly on an airplane?

The airport in Lugansk was destroyed during the hostilities. So the route by air, as well as by rail, from Lugansk to Moscow is not growing.

In principle, you can get from this city to the capital of the Russian Federation by plane. But for this, you first need to get to some locality with an existing airport. The closest city to Lugansk is Rostov-on-Don u . A trip here takes approximately 3.5 hours. A bus ticket costs around 8 00 rubles. The price of the flight "Rostov-on-Don - Moscow" with the company "Aeroflot" is approximately 9-12 thousand rubles. But if you wish, you can find an option and cheaper from another carrier. The flight to Moscow from Rostov-on-Don will last approximately 2 hours. The road here from Lugansk will take 5 hours. So, by plane, to Moscow from this city, even taking into account transfers, in principle, it will be possible to get faster than by bus. However, the departure time of the flight from Lugansk and the plane from Rostov-on-Don will have to be carefully selected.

If desired, residents of Lugansk can fly to Moscow and from Taganrog. The conditions in this case will be almost the same.

How to get there by car?

The route of the train "Lugansk - Moscow" was once convenient. At the same time, the trip was relatively inexpensive. However, now the inhabitants of the LPR do not have the opportunity to travel with this mode of transport to Russia. A bus ride is not as comfortable as a train ride. Therefore, many residents of the capital of the LPR prefer to go to the Russian Federation in their car.

By private car to get to Moscow from Lugansk, of course, is not difficult. To do this, you must first move along the highway T1314 (P22). In Belovodsk, you should turn onto the P07 highway (go past the traffic police). On this road you need to get to Chertkovo. Here you should turn right - onto the T1307 highway ( Peoples' Friendship Street ). After a few hundred meters you can see the customs point.

Further, upon reaching the Northern Lane, you need to turn right, and then continue along K. Marx - Petrovsky Street - Pochtovy Ave. On this road you should get to the station. TNCs. Here you need to turn left - onto the Don highway E115 (M4). In Pavlovsk in a circle you need to turn to the second exit onto ul. East / M4 and again move along the M4. This route goes all the way to Moscow.





How can I get there yet?

The automobile route "Moscow - Lugansk", as you see, is uncomplicated. By bus to the capital of the Russian Federation from this city can also be reached without any problems. However, if desired, residents of Lugansk who do not have a car can get to Moscow faster than on a flight from any company, and even spend less on this. All that is needed is to become someone's fellow traveler. You can find such a service today via the Internet.
