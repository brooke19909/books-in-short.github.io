We knit snood with knitting needles. Tips for Beginners

Almost all of the loans are made with a smooth cloth. You can knit this accessory on two knitting needles in straight and reverse rows. But in this case, the product will then need to be sewn. Ideal are snoods made in circular rows. This can be done on two knitting needles connected by a fishing line, or on five separate tools. Then the product is solid, it will not need to be stitched.

The width of the snood may be different. This accessory can be tied to the size of the neck in the form of a stand-up collar. Such a product will be narrow and neat, it does not need to be wrapped around the neck several times. If snud will also serve as a headdress, then such a thing should be wide.

What patterns to use in order to knit snood with knitting needles? Looks very good, except for "infinity", drawings of rhombs, squares, plaits, braids. These patterns add volume to the product.

How to knit snood with knitting needles?

We invite beginners in knitting to make the simplest model of this accessory. Prepare knitting needles No. 5, thick wool / acrylic yarn (50 g / 45 m). Dial 70 loops and knit with an elastic band 1x1 or 2x2. If you want to get a more voluminous product, then use the English gum as the main pattern . Perform work with a direct cloth. The length of the product can be different, usually up to 60 centimeters. After knitting the desired number of rows, close the loops, cut the thread, fasten and hide the tip of it. If you performed a snood on two knitting needles, make a seam. Everything, the product is ready.

Experienced knitters will make this accessory in just a couple of hours. Beginners will need an evening or two. But the result is worth it.
