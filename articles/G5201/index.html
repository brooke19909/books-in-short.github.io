Lotte Hotel ST Petersburg: description and reviews

It belongs to one of the largest luxury networks in South Korea. Hotels of this line combine resort and spa hotels. It includes 15 top-class tourist complexes in different countries of the world. The company invested 140 million dollars in the construction of Lotte Petersburg. How to get to the hotel, what is around, history

So, where is the Lotte Hotel ST Petersburg located, how to get to it? The hotel is located on St. Isaac's. It is very close to such famous sights of Northern Venice as Palace Square, Nevsky Prospekt and the Mariinsky Theater. Many rooms offer views directly to St. Isaac's Cathedral and the main streets of the city. The nearest metro stations are Admiralteyskaya (purple line), Sennaya Square and Spasskaya. Before each of them walk about ten minutes. The hotel opened in a restored building of the nineteenth century, which representatives of the Lotte company managed to bring to life. This mansion was built in 1851, but then fell into disrepair. The building is historical, at one time Gogol lived here and one of the US presidents stayed. It was built by the French architect Robin Hadrian. The railway station (Moskovsky) is less than two kilometers from the hotel, and the distance to Pulkovo Airport is 14 km.



Guest Accommodation

Hotel Lotte Hotel ST Petersburg has 154 rooms. All of them are united conceptually, created from unique and environmentally friendly materials. The interior of the premises is very aesthetic and goes well with color and lighting. Bathrooms are faced with black and white Italian marble. The ceilings are high, up to 4 meters. The rooms have individual styles - from classic to modern. The rooms have both air conditioning and heating. All guestrooms have LCD televisions, refrigerators (minibars), cosmetics, bathrobes and slippers. Each room has two telephones. The most budget rooms are located in the attic. Their area is from 34 to 50 square meters, and the windows are in a sloping ceiling. The modern superior rooms open onto the courtyard and street, or have city views. Premier and Deluxe have individual smart home type touch panels to control all the devices in the room. There, guests can enjoy tea or coffee using special kits. Lotte Hotel ST Petersburg: service description

The hotel offers guests the whole range of services that is necessary for a hotel of this class. This 24-hour registration and reception, and free Wi-Fi, valid throughout the territory, and underground parking for residents. The hotel can provide a shuttle service to the train station or airport. A special club lounge is open for VIP clients. All guests are provided with free morning newspapers and two bottles of drinking water per day. The rooms are equipped with luxurious bathrobes, slippers and toiletries of well-known companies. Smoking is not allowed throughout the hotel, in accordance with Russian law. But there are many devices so that people with disabilities can stay at the hotel and feel that all the necessary services are available for them. At the reception you can order tickets and leave your luggage in case of late departure. Check-in at 14:00, and checkout time, as elsewhere, at noon.
