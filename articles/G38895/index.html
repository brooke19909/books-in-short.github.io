DIY scarf dresses - stylish and original

Have you ever seen dresses made of scarves sewn with your own hands? If not, we dare to assure you that these are original and unique models that can well compete with dresses from famous designers. The question immediately arises: "Why?" Firstly, you choose exactly the scarf ornament that suits you best.

Secondly, you yourself create the style that you dreamed about. And you can be 100% sure that at no party you will meet a stranger in the same outfit. In addition, it is not at all difficult to sew an original and effective dress from scarves with your own hands.

From two silk or chiffon shawls you will get a chic dress. If its neckline and hem line go along the oblique, then your wardrobe will be replenished with an exclusive extra-class model. Spaghetti straps in such a dress can serve as ordinary thin graceful chains. We assure you, even without sufficient experience in tailoring, you can sew yourself a new and original outfit in two hours. What do you need?

  • 2 scarves made of thin silk or chiffon 90x90;
  • 2 thin chains 40 and 80 cm long;
  • 2 small loops.

Are you ready to go? Then proceed. Fold both handkerchiefs with their faces and draw the lines of the side seams. Cut off excess tissue. Stitch the side cuts to a width of 1 cm. Seam the seam allowances and iron them towards the back. Sew loops on the back of the back, and chains to the front of the neckline. Your dress is done!



You can sew dresses from scarves with your own hands in different ways. This option is quite interesting: take two identical scarves, connect their sides. You will need a braid to collect all the dress. Scarves can be overlapped. In this case, the corners of the scarves will cover the chest, and a ribbon or silk belt is sewn under the chest.

Hand-made dresses made of scarves look amazing! Light, airy and incredibly pleasant to the touch. This is the best option for a hot summer. In addition to light fabrics, scarves made of denser silk or crepe de chine can be used for their manufacture.

Our compatriots learned to sew dresses from scarves with their own hands during times of total shortage. In those days, clothes in stores were quite tight, but scarves did not fall into the category of scarce goods. IN

in fact, not every designer was ready to cut ready-made shawls, and some considered such actions blasphemous - because each shawl was a work of applied art.

The first designer to decide to sew a dress from scarves was Vyacheslav Zaitsev. He used Pavlovsky Posad shawls. These were worthy models, but they did not become a trend. Rather, they were perceived as a whim of a fashion designer.

Naturally, the sewing industry could not make such clothes widespread, so only those who knew how to sew and, in addition to everything, had good taste could sew dresses from scarves on their own.

Original patterns from silk scarves are indispensable in the resort. If you are interested in this idea, remember that the larger the perimeter of the scarf, the longer and freer the finished product will be. In addition, pay close attention to the color and texture of the selected products: they must match each other.
