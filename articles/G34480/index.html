Armeria Hotel 3 *. Armeria Hotel, Turkey: photos, prices and reviews of tourists from Russia

Kemer is one of the most picturesque and interesting resorts in Turkey. There you can relax unforgettably, enjoying the beach and the sea, as well as recharge your batteries with positive energy until your next vacation. Russians are quite frequent guests in Kemer, mostly they stay in mid-range hotels. A striking example of the location of tourists from Russia is the 3 * Hotel Armeria Hotel.





About the resort

Kemer annually welcomes guests from Russia, and both people for a quiet holiday and lovers of night discos and noisy parties come here.

The beach season captures the end of spring and the beginning of autumn, but it is best to relax in the resort in the summer. Most Kemer hotels are located along the coastline and have pebble beaches. Many are attracted by the historical and architectural heritage of the town, all excursions to local attractions are exciting and interesting.





Hotel Description

Hotel Armeria Hotel 3 * was built in 1998. The building is not new, but the facade and interior of the hotel are in fair condition. The date of the last restoration is 2000. The hotel consists of three three-story hotel buildings, the main building is painted in burgundy and has a large and bright sign, so it will not be difficult to find it.

Where is?

Any tourist, planning his vacation, has some idea how to get to the hotel. Like many 3 * hotels, Armeria Hotel located near the airport. The nearest aircraft arrival point is 55 kilometers from Kemer.





Description of rooms

Armeria Hotel 3 * (Turkey) belongs to the category of medium-sized hotels. It is designed for a small number of vacationers. About 120 people can stay there at a time. In total, the hotel has 51 rooms. All rooms are divided into three categories.

  1. Twin Room.
  2. Double Room.
  3. Triple Room.

15 rooms have single beds, 28 rooms (Double Room) - double. The rest (Triple Room) are designed for a larger number of residents - they are equipped with 1 single and 1 double bed.

Each room has a private shower or bath. It has everything you need - clean towels, bath accessories, a wash basin, etc. In addition, the rooms have air conditioning, a minibar, satellite TV, and a telephone. The rooms are small but comfortable. Each room has its own balcony, from where you can enjoy the "Kemer" sunsets. Of all the rooms - 31 rooms have a normal balcony, the rest - French.

Linen is changed and the room is cleaned constantly, several times a week. However, Russian guests are always advised to remind the maid that you need her services.





Beach description

Hotel Armeria Hotel 3 * (Turkey) is located on the 2nd coastline, and the beach, as they say, is just a stone's throw away - only 200-300 meters. This is very convenient, since tourists most often come to relax closer to the sea, especially for Russians. After all, everyone knows that Russian travelers appreciate and love the Mediterranean coast of pebbles and sand.

The hotel has a shared beach strip of 100 meters. Private beach is located near the city. You can get to the beach both on foot and by transport. Buses run to the place several times a day.

The beach has everything you need for the comfort of vacationers - sun loungers, umbrellas, beach towels.

Hotel Infrastructure

Hotel 3 * Armeria Hotel is designed for tourists, "average people", so the hotel infrastructure is not diverse enough, as we would like better-off people. On the territory there is a restaurant, several bars. If necessary, vacationers can use the car rental service or a safe at the reception.

Visitors can count on the fact that cleaning and changing towels in the rooms will be just like that. You will not have to pay for television, as well as for the use of sun loungers and other beach accessories by the pool. This ends the list of free services. Entertainment / Leisure

Despite the fact that the hotel complex is suitable mainly for a calm and measured rest, the leisure program is quite wide. There are several pools (adult and children). The pool for adult tourists is open, its area is 65 m 2 . Vacationers can sit in the open-air restaurant and try national dishes, as well as some European and even Asian products.

There is a bar by the pool where you want to have a cool cocktail or beer. In addition to entertainment at the hotel, the hotel has a sauna and Turkish steam bath. Guests can book massages in their rooms, this service is paid separately. Lovers of outdoor activities can have fun on the beach by the sea, playing beach volleyball. There you ride water attractions - skiing, catamarans, etc.

The hotel building has a hall where you can play table tennis, as well as a TV lounge where travelers can relax and watch TV in the company. For the female half of vacationers there is a SPA center where ladies can take care of their appearance and put themselves in order.





Children's holidays at the Armeria Hotel 3 * (Kemer)

Frequent guests of the hotel are families with kids. Come here with children of all ages. For young travelers there is everything you need: a children's pool of 2 m2, fenced with sun loungers and umbrellas. The restaurant has a children's menu and special furniture. Unfortunately, there are no prams for rent at the hotel. For tourists, the hotel does not provide babysitting services.

Cost of rest

Prices for tours in Armeria Hotel, as in other hotels in Turkey, depend on the season, number of guests and type of room. The cost of the trip is also affected by the duration of the trip and additional services, such as excursions, transfers from the airport to the hotel, etc.

The average price for a standard double room starts at 22 thousand rubles per week of relaxation. Tours during the velvet season increase in price, and Russian tourists who decide to travel in late August or early autumn will have to pay a bit more. The cost of such a ticket varies between 30-40 thousand rubles.

Many tourists from Russia spend their whole vacation in Kemer, and tours for 9, 12 or 14 nights are very popular. Their cost is 45,000-48,000, 49,000-52,000 and 53,000-58,000 rubles, respectively.

Today you can buy a ticket both in a travel agency and virtually. Modern services provide travelers with various ways to pay for tours. The main thing to do before buying a ticket to Armeria Hotel 3 * (photo of which can be found here) is to study in detail information about the hotel, its location and the services provided. This will help to avoid conflicts and unpleasant incidents during the holidays.

Reviews and recommendations

Tourists who are counting on a budget vacation option should understand that in a hotel of this category there will be no "royal" dishes and luxurious furniture. Unfortunately, most Russian travelers are accustomed to something else. For the quality of service you need to pay a higher price, our citizens do not want to put up with this at all. When expectations do not coincide with the real picture, disappointment appears, followed by negative reviews. Hotel Armeria Hotel 3 * is an example of the discrepancy between expectations and reality. Most often, this contradiction arises due to unreasonable requirements of guests to the staff or the administration of the hotel. Of course, it happens that the tour operator is also to blame for the negative opinion about the rest when he gives incorrect information about the hotel. There can be many reasons. Unfortunately, Turkey, the hotel Armeria Hotel 3 in particular, when receiving Russian tourists, does not always receive a flattering opinion about the rest. However, this does not affect the number of visitors from Russia.

As for the 3 * Armeria Hotel, it has both fans and opponents. Most tourists give the hotel complex a rating of "3". Someone did not like the local cuisine. According to them, turmeric is added to all dishes to hide the smell of expired products. The sizes of portions seemed small to others; the third was bored with the lines in the dining room.

Most often, guests complain about spoiled furniture or broken plumbing in the rooms. Unfortunately, this is sometimes found. But this can be avoided, and it can be done quite simply - you need to invite a representative of the hotel administration to the room who will be required to provide a new room.

Depending on the time of year, different categories of tourists relax in the hotel. Sometimes noisy youth interferes with a relaxing holiday for families with children, this also happens quite often. In this case, you can only advise one thing - pre-select a hotel on the outskirts, closer to nature. Lovers of rough rest often stay in central hotels. As for the Armeria Hotel, it is better to go there with children in spring or autumn, summer is the time for students to relax. Therefore, measured your vacation in perfect silence is unlikely to succeed.

Most often, tourists who knew what they were counting on were satisfied with the rest at Armeria Hotel 3 *. This category of guests includes undemanding tourists and students. Most often they leave only good opinions about Armeria Hotel 3 *, their reviews are usually positive.

As for young people, these tourists note the convenient location of the hotel - it is very close to the entertainment facilities of the city (bars, discos and restaurants). Many people like the pebble beach and water attractions where you can have fun and have a good time. Nearby there is a pier where you can stroll along the sea. Most people like a gentle entrance to the sea or free access to the beach by bus, etc.

Also an indisputable advantage of this hotel is the availability of a diverse excursion program. Numerous walks and trips to interesting places of Kemer and its outskirts are the main advantage of Armeria Hotel 3 *. Reviews on the work of guides and guides are extremely positive. The same can be said of the hotel staff. Despite the controversial situations during the holidays, the hotel staff is always friendly, good-natured and intelligent. This is noted by all guests.

Armeria Hotel 3 * is a wonderful hotel in the center of Kemer, designed for an economical and modest vacation. If you plan to spend time on the beach, walking around the city or on excursions, then this hotel will suit you perfectly.
