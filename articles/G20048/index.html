general information

The full name of this hotel is Marco Polo Presnya. It is located within walking distance of many Moscow attractions, including the famous Patriarch's Ponds. Red Square with the Kremlin, the Bolshoi Theater, the Maly, a synagogue, several ancient churches, a church - all of them are only a few blocks away from Marco Polo.

The hotel with its convenient location - inside the Garden Ring, - transport accessibility, a wide range of services and an individual approach to each guest has become an ideal place for business travelers and those travelers who come to get acquainted with the Russian capital. A significant role in choosing this particular hotel is played by very affordable prices.

Marco Polo is a hotel whose building was built back in 1904. Created by the English architect W. Walcot, today it is striking in its grace. Before the October Revolution, the British and American elites came here, in the Soviet years, the Krasnopresnenskaya hotel was a closed hotel intended for party leaders. In the nineties of the last century, the hotel passed into the management of an Austrian company.



Infrastructure

Marco Polo is a hotel that provides a wide range of services for conferences and business meetings, wedding events. It has two conference rooms. In one of them, which is located on the ground floor, it is possible to hold events without the use of microphones. The lights in this room are adjustable. Equipment, including a projector with a screen, is mobile: it can be installed anywhere at the request of the client. Another conference room, called "Moscow", is decorated in pastel beige colors. Judging by the reviews, it is an excellent option for plenary meetings, negotiations, seminars and other business events.

For a thousand rubles a day you can get a place in a public parking lot right next to Marco Polo. The hotel also provides concierge services, an ATM and luggage storage. Reception is open around the clock. It will help to call a taxi, buy tickets for performances, arrange a transfer, send or receive a fax.

Housing stock

"Marco Polo" (hotel, Moscow) offers seventy-three rooms of the categories "standard", "luxury" and "deluxe" for living. They offer beautiful views of the narrow Moscow streets that preserve the charm of the old city. Each room has its own unique design. All of them are equipped with the necessary furniture for a comfortable stay. Engravings and paintings by contemporary artists hang in rooms. Two-room apartments of the "deluxe" category - "Presidential" room, decorated in art deco style, can accommodate four adults at the same time. They have two balconies.



All rooms have LCD televisions with satellite TV with sixty channels, a telephone and a minibar, a safe, all coffee / tea making facilities and an iron with ironing board. The entire space of the hotel is air-conditioned and heated. On the doors are electronic locks. The cost of the presidential suite starts at 48,890 rubles.

Nutrition

Marco Polo (Hotel, Moscow) offers its customers only a continental breakfast, which is included in the price. In the mornings, the buffet offers assorted meats and cheeses, eggs, butter, pastries, fruits, cereals, jam, tea and coffee. You can have lunch and dinner at restaurants, which are two on the hotel territory. In addition to them, there is also a lobby bar and a summer outdoor terrace.
