Hotel Anemos Luxury Grand Resort 5 * (Greece, Crete): photos, reviews

Anemos Luxury Grand Resort opened just a few months ago. In May 2016, to be more precise. Since Crete, where the hotel is located, is very popular with travelers, many went here this year. And in its first season the hotel was not empty. In general, it is worth listing all its features, as well as touching upon the reviews left by the first guests of the complex.





Location

This is the first point to talk about. Anemos Luxury Grand Resort is located in Georgioupolis. This is such a small fishing village. Not far from it is the port city of Rethymnon (one of the most beautiful places in Crete) and Chania - the second largest metropolis on the island. The main feature of the fishing village is the local flavor that has been preserved, despite the booming tourism business. And the main attraction - clean many kilometers of beaches, exotic greenery and whole forests of eucalyptus trees. Ioannis Daskaloyannis Airport can be reached from here in half an hour by car.

But, returning to the discussion of the Anemos Luxury Grand Resort, it is worth noting that its main highlight is its private beach. In fact, it was thanks to its presence that the vast majority of tourists paid attention to the hotel.

Sports and recreation

On the territory of the brand new hotel there is everything that will help guests brighten up their leisure time. Naturally, there is an outdoor pool, a terrace and a picturesque garden. But besides this, there are other ways to have fun at Anemos Luxury Grand Resort.

In the evening, for example, various animated show programs are held here. For children there is a mini club with educators. You can also swim in the indoor pool, go horse riding or diving, rent a bike for a walk, visit the fitness center or play table tennis.

Also in Anemos Luxury Grand Resort 5 * (Greece, Georgioupolis) there is karaoke, a full-fledged court, as well as such entertainment for the soul and body, as a sauna, SPA-procedures, Turkish bath and massage.

Anemos Luxury Grand Resort 5 * (Crete) has all the conditions for a comfortable stay, from free Wi-Fi to free parking next to the hotel.
