Homework lessons: how to make a DIY bag

After each shopping trip, we bring many plastic bags to the house . Those that are whole and clean are a pity to throw in trash. And then we begin to think where to put the packages so that they do not bother anyone and at the same time are always at hand. We will show you a good solution to this issue. To store plastic bags, we suggest you get a device such as a bag. Do it yourself, anyone can do this from improvised materials. The following workshops describe how to make a bag of cloth and a plastic bottle. We invite you to familiarize yourself with them.

We sew a cover for saving cellophane handbags



We will make this version of the product from textiles. Such a DIY bag will be made of the following materials:

  • the cloth;
  • elastic;
  • wide braid or tape;
  • decorative elements (lace, buttons, beads).

How to make a bag with your own hands? We study the instructions.

Its length should be half the width of the textile part. Tie or sew the ends of the elastic. Attach a piece of tape or tape to the top of the bag in the form of a loop that will serve as a handle. If desired, you can decorate the product with lace. embroidery, beads, bright buttons.



Packages are folded into such a cover through the upper hole, and are pulled through the lower edge, gathered on an elastic band. Handbags do not fall out of this device, but at the same time, if necessary, they can be easily taken out of there.

Do-it-yourself bag-holder made of PET containers. What could be easier?

We invite you to study the description of the process of performing such a product from a plastic bottle and polymer clay. It will have the shape of a house with us. For work, we prepare such materials and tools:

  • 1-1.5 liter PET bottle;
  • polymer clay of different colors;
  • textile lace;
  • scissors.

Want to know how to make such a bag yourself? The master class will help you.

Cut the neck and bottom of the bottle. The narrow part of the container will be the bottom of the product, and the wide part will be on top. Having retreated a few centimeters from the place where the bottom of the tank was, pierce the two holes at the same distance from each other. Pull the cord through them later. Roll out light-colored polymer clay into a thin layer and apply it on the entire surface of the plastic container. Thus, you designed the basis of the crafts.

Next, we proceed to decorate it. Blind clay of various elements (windows, doors, fence, flowers, leaves) and attach them to the product. When the plastic hardens, stretch the ends of the lace into each hole and secure them with knots. Hang the bag in the chosen place in the house. Fold the bags in it through the upper wide hole from above, and get through the narrow one from below.

Conclusion

A do-it-yourself pouch made according to one of the master classes presented in this article will become not only a practical thing in your home, but also a worthy decoration.
