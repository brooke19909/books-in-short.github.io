The best hotels of Sharm El Sheikh: photos, reviews

Hotels in Sharm El Sheikh (Egypt) are famous for their first-class service, developed infrastructure of entertainment and recreation. Here every year thousands of tourists from different parts of the world relax. This resort has many attractions, cultural monuments, beautiful hotels. Hotels are famous for their comfort, high-quality service, which will leave an unforgettable impression of time spent near the sea.

For completeness of true relaxation it is worth to book a room in hotels of Sharm El Sheikh (5 stars). Here are wonderful conditions and many different types of entertainment. The best hotels are located on the first line with direct access to the coast.

Monte Carlo Sharm Rerost & Spa 5 * (ex. The Ritz-Carlton 58)

Everyone who likes to snorkel and flippers is welcome to this hotel! In the sea near the hotel there are stunning coral reefs. The underwater world has the opportunity to admire almost in seclusion. There is everything for a quiet, leisurely vacation. You can dive into the sea with a pontoon.

Concorde El Salam Sport Area

Concorde El Salam Sport Area has many nightclubs, restaurants, bars with excellent service. Here you can spend time doing snorkeling or other sports. The hotel has charming architecture and beautiful surroundings. The beach is literally within reach. Also, tourists can enjoy horse riding, canoeing. Beauty and originality skillfully complemented by oriental hospitality and a special attitude towards hotel guests.

Sultan Gardens Resort

The Sultan Gardens Resort impresses with its pomp. It is not only beautiful, but also quiet, calm. The whole territory is fenced, there is a lot of greenery around, the pools are equipped with sun loungers with curtains. Children are entertained by professional animators, and there are also children's clubs. Nearby is the beach, scuba diving courses are held. Since there are many corals in the sea, where, while diving, you can see the beautiful underwater world. This is one of the most luxurious hotels in Sharm El Sheikh first line, according to tourists.

Dreams beach resort

Dreams Beach Resort offers its guests to spend an unforgettable, fun, fantastic vacation. The hotel complex is located by the sea. This is a special beach. Around it there are rocky cliffs, which gives a feeling of solitude and peace. The local landscapes are unusually beautiful. And romantic evening walks create a special mood. The sea is shallow, but those who want to see the underwater beauty need to use the pontoon, to overcome a short distance. The hotel offers all services for diving and snorkeling. In addition, you can indulge yourself with delicious food in the restaurant, visit a nightclub, spa treatments, gym and much more.

Jaz Mirabel Park 5 *

A stunning hotel located in the resort of Sharm El Sheikh, on the beach. This is one of the new buildings with a unique design. The hotel welcomes its guests in 196 spacious, comfortable rooms, which have everything you need for a comfortable stay. The five-star Jaz Mirabel Park Hotel has parking, provides car rental, for events you can order a conference room for up to 100 people, or use the banquet room. There are many interesting entertainments for children. An extensive program is offered for adults: an outdoor amphitheater, a private beach, a sauna, massage, spa treatments, a jacuzzi, a pool, a billiard room, restaurants, bars, night clubs. For those who love sports, you can use the gym, tennis court. Do yoga, aqua aerobics.

The best hotels in Sharm El Sheikh (5 stars) are represented by another wonderful hotel complex.

Premier Le Reve Hotel & Spa 5 *

The hotel has five floors, all-inclusive meals. Also, guests are provided with a huge territory with various entertainments. There is everything for every taste: bars, restaurants, night clubs, internet, satellite TV, a swimming pool, spa treatments, massages, a jacuzzi sauna, parking and washing. And also to keep fit there is a fitness center.

Old Palace Resort 5 *

A wonderful five-star hotel is located near the sea. The hotel offers its guests a huge list of all kinds of entertainments for a unique holiday. Here you can spend time in the spa, gym, disco, night bar, restaurant, sauna. You can also play billiards, tennis, water polo, volleyball. For various events, a conference and banquet room is provided. Round-the-clock porters, taxis, parking and more.

AM Royal Hotel 5 *

The hotel is near El Gouna. It has 395 rooms with the possibility of admiring the seascape. There are places for various events in the conference room, banquets. Parking or car rental, a bar, restaurants, outdoor pools are also provided for tourists. In stores you can buy souvenirs for memory. Also, guests can use the diving, jacuzzi, billiard room, windsurfing. You can relax in the sauna, bath, massage. For those who prefer active forms of entertainment, there is a fitness center, the possibility of playing table tennis, beach soccer or volleyball. There is a kids club for children.

Stella Di Mare Beach Hotel & Spa 5 *

This is a five star hotel in Sharm El Sheikh. It has 258 spacious, comfortable rooms of various categories. There is also a conference room, a venue for banquets, a disco, a pub, a restaurant and a bar. You can relax by ordering services in the spa, there are 3 excellent pools, a diving center, a court and tennis tables, a billiard room. You can also play volleyball on the beach. In addition, there is everything for people with disabilities. Lots of fun for the kids.

Iberotel Aquamarine 5 *

Favorite tourists hotel complex. It is located on the seashore. The hotel is ready to accommodate guests in 1001 rooms, offers charming landscapes, a swimming pool, a beautiful garden. The room has everything you need for relaxation. The hotel includes 20 outdoor pools, three water parks for adults, one for children, a Jacuzzi on the beach, a fitness center, and a spa complex. Also, lovers of active entertainment can do beach volleyball, tennis and table tennis, darts. There are also restaurants, night clubs, bars, shops. For those traveling with children, you can rent prams, chairs and cribs. There is also a special playground for kids, a mini club, a swimming pool and more.
