Uralsib Bank: customer reviews, service overview, loans and mortgages

Uralsib Bank is a recognizable brand in the Russian market. It is included in the TOP-30 of the largest banks in the country. The business organization covers 7 federal districts and 46 regions of the country. Customer reviews of Uralsib Bank allow us to judge the quality of service and the convenience of services.

PJSC Uralsib Bank is a licensed commercial bank of Russia. The license was issued by the Central Bank in 2015 (unlimited). Since November 2015, V.I. became the head of a commercial bank. Kogan, a famous Russian banker, statesman.

For 3 years, Uralsib has been operating at the expense of long-term development loans. Loans to the bank in the total amount of 81 billion rubles. Previously, the company was called "Bashkreditbank" and offered services only in the Republic of Bashkortostan (in 1993). Until 1997, he was one of the leaders in the region (together with the largest bank in the country).

The name change took place in 2002, when Bashkreditbank was renamed Ural-Siberian Bank. As a result of the merger with 10 domestic companies, the modern brand Uralsib was founded. Subsidiaries included:

Mortgage Terms

From the beginning of its activity, Uralsib has been one of the country's leaders in terms of mortgage lending. Now he is in the TOP 10 companies by the number of open mortgage contracts in 2018.

Mortgage Bank Uralsib, according to reviews, can apply for not only the owners of salary cards. A housing loan is available to all clients with official earnings, but the conditions for concluding a contract for regular borrowers, depositors and other persons are different.

The bank can arrange a mortgage for new (under construction) housing and real estate in the secondary market. The interest rate, down payment differ from the type of loan and the status of the borrower. The most favorable conditions are provided to regular customers (cardholders, depositors, borrowers, including former) and employees of subsidiaries or partner companies.

According to customer reviews, a mortgage at Uralsib Bank is beneficial for borrowers. This is confirmed by the loan conditions:

  1. General: mortgages up to 30 years, interest rate from 9.9% per annum, minimum installment - 15%. Issued in the amount of up to 50 million rubles. The program is relevant for both under construction and finished housing in the secondary market.
  2. A loan secured by real estate without a down payment. Issued in amounts of up to 20 million rubles. The rate is from 12.5% ‚Äã‚Äãper annum. The maximum contract size as a percentage of the value of collateral housing is 60%. Issued up to 30 years.
  3. A military mortgage is issued for a period of up to 20 years in the amount of up to 2.6 million rubles (the size of a mortgage loan minus own funds + ZHZZ). The minimum rate is 10.9% per annum.
  4. Mortgage loan from partners of the bank. Under the terms of the borrower, a loan of up to 50 million rubles is available at 9.9% per annum (minimum) for a period of up to 30 years.
  5. A loan secured by property. This can be a mortgage or consumer lending for any purpose. The volume of a mortgage loan cannot exceed 6 million rubles, and the minimum rate is 13.5% per annum. It is issued for a period of up to 15 years.
  6. Mortgage Holidays. This is a loan for those who purchase housing under construction in a new house. It allows you to pay 50% of the monthly payment for up to 2 years. After the "vacation" the client must make 100% payment before repayment of the loan. It is issued for a period of up to 30 years, the maximum amount is 50 million rubles. The interest rate starts at 10.9% per annum.
  7. Mortgage refinancing of other banks. Clients can apply for a home loan by paying off a debt at another financial institution. Terms of the contract: rate from 10.5% per annum, term - up to 30 years. Provided in the amount of up to 50 million rubles. The object of the pledge is the acquired property.

Car loan processing at the bank

Clients of Uralsib Bank can become the owner of their own vehicle using borrowed funds under the Car Loans program. The bank provides 2 loan options for future motorists:

  1. "Auto partner". Under the agreement, the borrower buys a car worth up to 3 million rubles with a loan term of up to 5 years. The minimum loan installment is 20%. The rate is from 10.9% per annum.
  2. "A fairy tale without hull insurance." This is a car loan in the amount of up to 3 million rubles for a period of up to 5 years. The borrower must provide a down payment of 20% of the cost of the loan. Bank interest - from 14.8% per annum. The bonus is the lack of compulsory insurance under the CASCO program (which is required in 9 out of 10 banks when buying a car with car loans).

Customers can include life insurance in their credit or refuse the service. In case of refusal, the interest rate increases by 2 points.

Customer reviews about the car loan at Uralsib Bank are positive. Borrowers write that the lender strictly complies with the terms of the loan. Interest suits more than 67% of car owners.

Early repayment of car loans is carried out without commission. Clients can fill out an application at any office, through an online bank or by calling a support service. When applying for a loan, attention is paid to the car brand, the borrower's credit history and documents confirming solvency.

Consumer loans at Uralsib

Loans for any purpose at Uralsib Bank allow borrowers to solve financial problems within 1-2 days. Consumer loans are issued to working citizens and retirees. Types of loan products of Uralsib:

  1. A special loan is provided for public sector employees with a fixed interest rate of 15.9% per annum. It is issued in the amount of up to 1.5 million rubles for a period of up to 7 years.
  2. For retirees. The product "Honorary Senior Citizen" is issued to those who retired early or in old age. Amounts up to 500 thousand rubles are available for a period of up to 7 years.
  3. Refinancing of loans of other banks. Uralsib Bank customers can obtain a consumer loan on favorable terms in the amount of up to 2 million rubles. Loan term should not exceed 7 years. The interest rate starts at 11.4% per annum.
  4. Cash loan. This is a standard lending option. You can draw up a contract for up to 7 years, the amount is limited to 1.5 million rubles. Interest ranges from 11.9% per annum.

Positive feedback on Uralsib Bank. The lender offers competitive rates, convenient repayment periods. Clients can receive borrowed funds in cash or by transfer to a bank card, Uralsib account.

Loans are issued to employees or those who receive a pension. Minimum references for obtaining a loan include the provision of a passport and a second document of your choice. This may be a passport, driver's license, SNILS, certificate 2-personal income tax, military ID and others.

Information on refinancing at Uralsib Bank

Refinancing is gaining popularity in all Russian banks, and Uralsib is no exception. The service allows you to improve the conditions on mortgages and consumer loans by concluding an agreement with the recalculation of interest and payment of debt to the previous creditor.

Reviews on refinancing Uralsib Bank are of interest to everyone who wants to reduce the overpayment. You can get borrowed funds only after providing information about the availability of a valid loan at another financial institution.

The client must receive refinancing certificates from the lender within 1-2 days before submitting an application to Uralsib Bank. A month after the issuance of certificates lose their relevance, as interest is accrued on the balance of funds. Feedback on refinancing loans from other banks of Uralsib Bank is positive. Customers are pleased with the opportunity to receive funds to repay loans of other banks at 11.4%.

But rates are not the lowest. Some banks in Russia offer to get a loan at 9.9% per annum or less. In addition, interest rates are set individually for each borrower. This means that customers can receive borrowed funds at 12.5% ‚Äã‚Äãper annum or more.
