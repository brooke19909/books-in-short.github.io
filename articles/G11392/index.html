Vacations offered by Janhot hotels

In the Krasnodar Territory is the resort village of Janhot. It is located at the mouth of the Hotetsay River on the Black Sea. Clear water and comfortable beaches, combined with local nature, attract many tourists who love a holiday near the coast.

Village

Dzhanhot - a small village, its indigenous, permanent resident population totals only three hundred people. It is located near the popular resort of Gelendzhik in the Krasnodar Territory. The Black Sea coast here near the village is considered one of its most ecologically clean areas. The uniqueness of the nature of this place lies in the combination of pine forest, clean mountain air and the sea. In the holiday season, such amazing natural conditions do not go unnoticed by potential guests, and the population of the village of Dzhanhot increases hundreds of times.



Relaxation

You can get to the village through Gelendzhik, the distance between them is about 20 kilometers. This can be done by ordering a taxi or using the services of passenger shuttle services. In recent years, resort infrastructure has been developing in the village of Dzhanhot. There is:

  • small market;
  • the shops;
  • several cafes.

Guest houses, cottages, recreation centers, boathouses, hotels are comfortably located on the seashore and in the pine forest. Janhot is a hospitable village. Its residents willingly accept tourists in the private sector. They organize quite decent accommodation and meals for guests in their houses and country houses. But more comfortable, organized and even economical holidays will be offered in the village of Janhot by hotels. The price range per day for accommodation is different - from 900 to 10,500 rubles. The conditions for accommodating guests offered by the hotels also vary. Janhot is a place where everyone can relax according to their financial situation.

Tourists reviews

Hundreds of tourists are attracted by the resort village of Janhot. Hotels here are developing and improving every year, so there are no negative reviews about the organization of work of local hotels.

The guests of this resort village note that the rest here is a magnificent nature, wonderful climate and warm sea, as well as good living conditions and quality service that suits vacationers.
