Where is the hotel located?





Kos Island is a fabulous place. And the hotel complex Kipriotis Village 4 is located on the east coast, very close to the beaches of the Aegean Sea. By the way, the village on whose territory the hotel is located is called Psalidi.

The undoubted advantage of the hotel is that it is located near the large resort town of Kos. The distance to the city center is only three kilometers - it is easy to get there by taxi, bus, car or even bicycle. By the way, a public transport stop is located just a few minutes walk from the main hotel building. But the international airport of Kos is a little further - 25 kilometers.

Short description

The hotel complex Kipriotis Village Resort 4 has a rather large private territory - its area is about 135 thousand square meters. In the center is the main building with rooms and a reception desk, and around it are small two-story buildings. It is worth noting that the architecture of the complex perfectly combines the national flavor and original modern styles.

The hotel was opened in 1994. The last thorough restoration was carried out in 2008, so today the resort complex is equipped with all the necessary innovations.

By the way, the territory is beautifully arranged: there are wide tiled paths for walking, and palm trees, in the shade of which you can have a great time, as well as secluded arbors, bright flower beds and real art compositions from exotic plants. This is what the Kipriotis Village 4 hotel looks like.

Psalidi is a great place for a beach holiday. Every year couples with children come here, as well as youth companies who want to have fun. In addition, an unforgettable romantic vacation can be organized at the hotel - often the hotel becomes a place for a honeymoon.

Kos island, Greece: map, attractions and sightseeing holidays





In fact, this island is a great place for excursion tourism. And you will always have the opportunity to use the services of a hotel tour desk. So what can the island of Kos (Greece) offer? It is said that Hippocrates himself worked on the island of Kos for a long time.
