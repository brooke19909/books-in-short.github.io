Safa Resort Aquapark 3 * (Tunisia, Hammamet): description, reviews

If you plan to spend an unforgettable vacation in Tunisia, but do not want to spend money on expensive four- and five-star hotels, then the Safa Resort Aquapark 3 * hotel located in Hammamet will be a good option for accommodation. Today we offer a closer acquaintance with this institution, as well as find out what impression was left on tourists from Russia staying here.

Housing stock

Each room has comfortable furniture, a TV with satellite channels, a telephone, a safe, air conditioning and a private bathroom. A minibar and room service are also available at an extra charge.
