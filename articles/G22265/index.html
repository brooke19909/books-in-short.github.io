The history of the rink in Victory Park (St. Petersburg)

This skating rink is located in the picturesque place of the city, which is sometimes abbreviated as St. Petersburg State Unitary Enterprise Moscow Victory Park. In St. Petersburg, this place has been held sacred since the end of World War II. The park was laid down on October 7, 1945 by residents of Leningrad who survived the most severe blockade. Very soon, he became a favorite vacation spot for citizens. How to get there

From history, we will move on to the exact location of the object of interest to us, where the ice rink works in the winter. Victory Park is located at the intersection of Basseynaya Street and Moskovsky Prospekt. Geographically, it belongs to the Moscow region of St. Petersburg. The rink itself is located inside the park on the territory of the Gagarin Park attraction complex.

Near the park, on the side of Moskovsky Prospekt, is the Victory Park metro station, which belongs to the second line of the St. Petersburg metro. There is a bus stop No. 26, 39E, 3, 50, 2M, 2MA. There is a bus stop No. 36, 72, 63 from the side of Basseynaya Street next to the park. If you prefer a taxi or your own car, then access to Victory Park is quite convenient. Thus, you can use both public and private transport to visit Victory Park and the ice rink.

In what mode does the ice rink in Victory Park (St. Petersburg) work?

This information is useful to those who value their time. The ice rink in St. Petersburg in Victory Park is open from December to February. What is the price?

What rink in Victory Park (St. Petersburg) sets the price of services in season? If a person is not a pensioner, schoolchild or teenager under 18, then he cannot consider himself a beneficiary. For such people, one skating session (the time is indicated in the article above) on a weekday and weekend will cost 300 rubles.

You can rent skates for ordinary citizens who do not have benefits, for 150 rubles per hour. But you need to remember that a guarantee is required, which is a passport, car license or 1000 rubles. If you are not a beneficiary and you need to sharpen your skates, then this service will cost 200 rubles, but you will go to the rink in St. Petersburg fully prepared.
