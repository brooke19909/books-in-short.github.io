Hotel: Atelika Nebug. Reviews: advantages and disadvantages

The Black Sea will give tourists unforgettable moments of relaxation, and the Atelika Nebug resort hotel is responsible for comfort and service. Reviews about this institution will help you find out all the necessary information and decide where you want to spend your vacation.





Brief description of the resort hotel

Tourists consider the resort hotel "Atelika Nebug" cozy and comfortable. Guest reviews especially note the well-organized infrastructure. Hotel guests will not have to be bored for a second, because on the territory there is a large open-air pool, a modern tennis court for sports enthusiasts, and its own shop, and a sauna, and a fitness center, and a playground with attractions for children, and many more opportunities for active and vibrant relaxation.

Special attention should be paid to the comfortable apartments that the Atelika Nebug hotel offers its guests. Reviews, photos made by tourists demonstrate an incredibly attractive interior, decorated in a classic laconic style. Since the local climate is hot enough, air conditioning is installed in the rooms. There are also such amenities as a TV, refrigerator and other equipment that will allow hotel guests to feel no less comfortable than at home.

Sea activities, as well as clean forest air, contribute to a good appetite, which you can quench in the restaurant of the Atelika Nebug hotel. Guest reviews suggest that the menu contains the widest selection of traditional Russian dishes. There is also a small cafe on the terrace where you can enjoy coffee and desserts while admiring the incredibly beautiful nature. There are also barbecue facilities that allow you to cook your own kebab.





Resort Location

One of the main characteristics of any hotel is its location. So, if we talk about the hotel "Atelika Nebug", the reviews suggest that its deployment is quite successful. The facility is surrounded by trees in a park area, which is about 18 kilometers from the resort town of Tuapse, namely in the village of Nebug (where the name of the hotel comes from). It is worth noting that the hotel is located on the first coastline, and therefore the sea is visible from the windows of most apartments. If you arrived in Tuapse by train, then you will have to travel about 20 kilometers from the train station to the hotel. But the nearest international airport Gelendzhik is removed from this area by as much as 110 kilometers.





Hotel Guest Apartments

The Atelika Nebug resort complex (Tuapse) consists of three beautiful buildings of different storeys . Reviews indicate the presence of the following categories of apartments:

  • There are two variations of standard rooms, which are designed to receive two or three adult guests. These rather miniature rooms in addition to beds can accommodate a whole range of necessary amenities.
  • Superior apartments, like standard, have a double and triple modification. From the previous category of rooms they are distinguished by the presence of air conditioning. Also, some of the rooms have a sea view from the windows (although this option of accommodation costs a little more).
  • Two-room rooms are among the best in this hotel. The compact bedroom, furnished in a luxurious classic style, has a large bed and a plasma flat-screen TV. The living room with upholstered furniture and a coffee table is very spacious, and also has access to the balcony, which allows you to visually expand the space. It is worth noting that an extra bed can be placed here.




Amenities at Resort Apartments

If you want your room to be as comfortable as at home, then pay attention to a place like Atelika Nebug. Guest reviews suggest that the apartment has the following amenities:

  • a widescreen plasma TV connected to a satellite dish (you will be able to watch more than a hundred news, entertainment and music channels);
  • orthopedic twin or double beds, which will provide you with a full night's sleep;
  • in each of the rooms there is a refrigerator, which will allow you to preserve the freshness of your favorite products or to cool bottles with drinks;
  • the bathroom is a toilet combined with a shower, where there are also all the necessary bath accessories and disposable cosmetics;
  • a fairly spacious balcony, which can serve you not only as a place for drying towels, but also as an excellent corner for relaxing in the fresh air with a view of the sea or beautiful natural landscapes;
  • most of the apartments of the Atelika Nebug hotel are equipped with air conditioning (reviews from last year indicate that this option is not available in standard rooms).

Entertainment

An ideal vacation is simply impossible to imagine without entertainment and active pastime. An excellent opportunity is provided by the resort hotel "Atelika Nebug". Reviews of vacationers talk about the following features:

  • in the open air there is a huge sports ground that combines a tennis court, a field for football, volleyball and basketball;

  • in the warm season, animators will work for you all day, in the evenings you will have concerts with invited artists;

Useful information about the resort hotel

The hotel "Atelika Nebug" has a number of distinctive features. Reviews focus on the following points:

  • Check-in time for hotel rooms starts at noon, and you need to leave your apartment on the day of departure no later than at 10 a.m.
  • Children under 6 years stay free of charge when using existing or extra beds;
  • if, contrary to the rules, you bring an animal to the hotel, you may be denied check-in and canceled your reservation;
  • the hotel is guarded around the clock;
  • meals for children under 6 years of age are provided free of charge;
  • check-in at the hotel is not possible without a passport, as well as a birth certificate (for children);
  • hotel staff provide services for the purchase of tickets for all types of transport;
  • When planning your vacation in advance, you can make a reservation (Atelika Nebug, reviews of which are mostly positive, has a special website where you can reserve a room).

Hotel "Atelika Nebug": reviews. disadvantages

Since there are no ideal hotels, this institution was no exception. Tourists have the following comments and complaints about the Atelika Nebug hotel:

  • dishes with cracks and chips spoil the whole impression of the restaurant;
  • Until 11 a.m. loud music plays in the courtyard, which will not appeal to those who prefer to go to bed early;
  • dishes are laid out in very small portions on the buffet, so visitors are in a hurry to get as much as possible, fearing that they will not get it;
  • due to the fact that there is no partition between the balconies, there is no feeling of privacy, and it is somehow uncomfortable to sleep with the door open;
  • the stairs that you need to go down to the sea are in poor condition and require urgent repairs, otherwise tourists risk being injured;
  • the operation of air conditioners both in the lobby and in the rooms is regulated by the administration, and therefore, in the most intense heat, even in a room it is not always possible to escape from stuffiness;
  • Hotel rooms are very cramped, especially in standard rooms;
  • the plumbing in the bathroom is old and constantly dripping;
  • since there are no mosquito nets on the windows, mosquitoes will constantly pester you (stock up on fumigators or special insect sprays;
  • the dining room is very crowded, and the number of tables and chairs does not match the number of guests;
  • in the dining room, quite often there is a situation where guests do not have enough plates and cutlery;
  • if you arrive before checkout time, then even for an extra charge you will not be settled ahead of time (even small children will not pay attention);
  • in order to have a satisfying breakfast, you need to come to the dining room as early as possible, otherwise there will simply not be any normal food at the distribution.

General impression of the hotel

A great place to relax is Atelika Nebug (Tuapse). Reviews note that it will especially appeal to those who are not indifferent to nature, because the hotel is located in a park area, in the midst of dense coniferous plantings. It is not only beautiful and pleasant, but also incredibly useful for those people who have problems with the health of the respiratory system. Very close to the sea. The beach is big enough and clean. Whenever you decide to swim, you will never have problems with the availability of free sunbeds. But the descent to the coast leaves much to be desired: the stairs are very dilapidated, they can even be called emergency. Needless to say, to overcome more than 200 steps when returning to the hotel is quite problematic, especially for older people.



Atelika Nebug is positioning itself as an all-inclusive hotel. But such an assessment is not entirely fair. Three meals a day on the buffet system - this is not the whole list of services that are necessary for tourists. It is worth noting that the food in the restaurant is quite high quality, varied and tasty. But the catering of guests leaves much to be desired. It seems that, in an effort to save on products, distribution workers lay out just microscopic portions. Finally spoils the impression that there are not always enough empty seats and cutlery, queues are constantly being created.

As for the rest, judging by the words of the guests of the complex, this hotel is ideal for active people who want to spend time away from the hustle and bustle of a big city, are not afraid of physical exertion (during a trip to the sea) and are nature lovers. The city infrastructure is far enough from here, but because the rest will be quiet and quite calm.
