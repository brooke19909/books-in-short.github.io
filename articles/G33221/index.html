Crystal Family Resort & Spa 5 * (Belek, Turkey): description and reviews

Turkey is well known to all for its chic hotels and the high-class service provided in them. Some hotel complexes are more impressive, others a little less. Now we will talk about a hotel belonging to the first category. And this is Crystal Family Resort & Spa 5 *.





Location

The five-star complex is located in a village called Bogazkent, located near Belek (Antalya province). This is a popular resort, it began to develop relatively recently. The advantage of Belek is its favorable geographical position, which allows you to make numerous excursions to the sights of all of Antalya.

From Crystal Family Resort & Spa 5 * you can reach many interesting places in 7-10 minutes. These include numerous golf clubs (among which there is one with a national status), the ancient city of Aspendos and the amphitheater located in it, the Roman Aqueduct, the Garden of Tolerance and much more. What about transport links? Antalya Airport is located about 35 kilometers away, so getting to it is not a problem.

More importantly, the hotel is located right on the seafront, on a spacious sand and pebble beach, where it is always clean and has free sun loungers (even at the height of the swimming season).

Service

The Crystal Family Resort & Spa 5 * has everything that should be in any 5-star hotel. This is free Wi-Fi, private parking, luggage storage, currency exchange, 24-hour reception, child care services (personal baby sitter), dry cleaning, laundry, ironing, several shops (including a souvenir shop), car rental, and a separate business center.

Also in Crystal Family Resort & Spa 5 * (Belek) there is a hall for congresses and balls, designed for 430 people (there is an opportunity to divide it into two parts). The interior is decorated in an interesting design, and the hall itself is equipped in accordance with all modern requirements for premises with a business concept.

What about equipment? The hall has everything that you might need: demonstration screens (stationary and portable), sound system (wired and wireless), a multimedia projector, microphones (wired and radio), a DVD player, a stage, wireless high-speed Internet, a plasma TV and lighting systems. Of course, there are also such trifles as a fax, pointers, air conditioning, laser pointer, etc.

SPA salon

The very name of the hotel Crystal Family Resort & Spa 5 * clearly indicates that this hotel complex has a beauty and health center. This is a professionally equipped salon in which anyone can pamper themselves with various programs for body and skin care.

It includes a separate SPA garden with a relaxation zone, two pools (one of them indoor and heated), a sauna, a rain shower, a steam room, a jacuzzi, several treatment and massage rooms.

Everyone can familiarize themselves with the full list of services on an individual basis, but at least some of them should be listed.

In the SPA-salon of the hotel, treatment with flowers and plants, algal baths, hydro massage, aromatherapy are popular. Naturally, numerous types of massage are also practiced. Among them are soothing, relaxing, Thai, Ottoman, Indian, headache, shiatsu, using hot stones and essential oils, as well as reflexology.

In addition, many are enrolled in algal and mud therapy, peeling and anti-cellulite programs. But facial programs are especially popular. The most frequently ordered procedures are color, flower and magnetic therapy, lymphatic drainage and long-term makeup.

Leisure activities

Crystal Family Resort & Spa 5 * (Turkey) is a resort hotel, which is intended not only for temporary accommodation of tourists in it, but also for entertainment. Without leaving its territory, you can well brighten up your leisure time.

In addition to numerous pools (including several for children), there are water slides, a fitness center, the possibility of water sports (canoeing, windsurfing), bowling, billiards, bike rental, table tennis, mini-golf and a full-fledged professional field nearby .

And, since Crystal Family Resort & Spa 5 * (Turkey) is a hotel for the whole family, there will be entertainment for the smallest here. A mini club with a buffet, a playground, several slides, a pool and a disco is organized for them here.

It is important to note that professional educators who speak English, Turkish, and Russian are engaged in teaching with the kids. Children are taught the basics of cooking and the basics of confectionery, they are attracted to developing games, they conduct classes in drawing, music and choreography. Reservation and complex rules

If people have planned a trip to Belek and decided to stay at Crystal Family Resort & Spa 5 *, then the reservation process is better not to postpone. This hotel complex is very popular, and rooms are booked for weeks or even months in advance (both by ordinary travelers and tour operators).

By the way, if circumstances allow, then it is better to go on vacation not in the summer. The climate here is good for relaxing in almost any month of the year, but prices in autumn and spring (and even more so in winter) fall almost twice. This is worth considering for people who want to save money.

What about check in? Registration of new guests here starts at 13:00. Accordingly, the departure of previous guests lasts until noon. We must remember this when choosing a flight. If guests arrive earlier than 13:00, they will, of course, be settled without waiting - but only if there are free and cleaned rooms of the reserved category. But, as already mentioned earlier, Crystal Hotels Family Resort & Spa 5 * is popular, and it is rarely empty.

By the way, when placing on extra beds all children under the age of 12 do not need to pay for them. This is the policy of the hotel. But if you need an extra bed - this should be reported in advance. You must contact the administration and request this service.

And yet, making a remote reservation, you will need to indicate in the application the details of the credit card ("Visa" or "Master Card"). When checking in, the administrator at the reception will ask you to provide it with your passport, therefore you only need to use your own, and not borrowed from someone.
