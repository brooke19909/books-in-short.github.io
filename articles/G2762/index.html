Guest House "Niagara" (Kabardinka): description, photos, reviews

Every year, a huge number of Russians from all over our vast country come to the resorts of the Black Sea coast of the Krasnodar Territory. Holidays in the south of Russia are the best and most memorable for many tourists, because a trip to these parts does not require registration of passports and visas, and magnificent landscapes and the warm sea are not inferior to foreign resorts.

Rest in Kabardinka

Between Novorossiysk and Gelendzhik, in a wide intermountain valley, there is a cozy village of Kabardinka. Mountains surround this small resort from all sides, thereby protecting it from cold winds and bad weather.

Kabardinka is conveniently located in the vast Tsemess Bay. The beaches in the village are all pebble, there are wild and comfortable, but they are all free.





The village has a landscaped promenade with beautiful alleys, flower beds and fountains. Walking along it, you can enjoy the magnificent seascape. Right there, on the waterfront, there are numerous cafes and restaurants where you can have a good time.

The air in the village is simply divine. The fact is that juniper forests grow on the slopes of the mountains surrounding Kabardinka, which create unique climatic conditions, saturating the air with antiseptic properties. It creates an atmosphere saturated with aromas of sea breeze and coniferous phytoncides, which favorably affects human health.

Where to stay?

The village as a resort has been developing since the days of the Soviet Union, so there is a huge selection of places to stay. There are children's camps, large pensions and recreation centers in Kabardinka.

Among the numerous boarding houses, a large five-star sanatorium-resort complex "Nadezhda" stands out, which is one of the best on the entire coast. There are other sanatoriums of various profiles where tourists come to rest and be treated.

Perhaps, the most popular among holidaymakers are private guest houses, of which there are a great many in the village. The private sector of Kabardinka will provide relaxation for every taste and pocket. Guest houses offer tourists a comfortable stay in wonderful home conditions at affordable prices. Many tourists come back to the same places from year to year, as welcoming owners greet them as dear and welcome guests.

Guest House Niagara (Kabardinka): hotel description

If you decide to spend your vacation in Kabardinka and want to relax in a decent place at an affordable price, then you should pay attention to the Niagara guest house, which is located in the center of the village.

The building of the guest house is a chic modern five-story cottage with balconies and an attic. The first floor is a basement in which there is a kitchen-dining room for vacationers. Here you can order a comprehensive meal or cook your own meals.





The guest house "Niagara" (Kabardinka) is located near the sea, at the address: st. Partizanskaya, 41a. The road to the sea, as the owners of the hotel promise, will take only 10-15 minutes by walking. The terrain here is flat, the road to the sea goes through the park, a walk will give you a pleasure.

Accommodation for vacationers

Guesthouse Niagara (Kabardinka) has twenty spacious comfortable rooms in its fund with excellent renovation and all amenities.

The rooms can accommodate two, three and four people. There are rooms with and without balconies. Prices for holidays depend on the season, the availability of a balcony and the number of people in the room.





Prices for holidays in 2017 (indicated for the entire room per day):

  • Double room without a balcony - from 1000 to 3000 rubles.
  • Double room with a balcony - from 1000 to 3500 rubles.
  • Room for 3 people (without balcony) - from 1000 to 3500 rubles.
  • Room for 3 people (with balcony) - from 1000 to 4000 rubles.
  • Quadruple room - from 1000 to 4500 rubles.

The rooms that the Niagara Guest House (Kabardinka) offers are very beautifully decorated, each of them has been renovated in a European style. The furniture is very high quality, each room has air conditioning and satellite TV.

On the balcony there is a table and chairs where you can have a wonderful time.

Hotel infrastructure

Guest House Niagara (Kabardinka) has its own spacious courtyard. In the courtyard there is parking for cars, there is an equipped place for cooking barbecue, benches and gazebos.

The highlight of the hotel is its chic spacious pool, which is located in the courtyard at the entrance to the hotel. The pool is perfectly clean, divided into adult and children's areas. The water in it is heated. Around the pool are comfortable sunbeds. If there is a storm or cold water at sea, then you and your children will be most welcome in the pool.





In the courtyard opposite the pool there is a small summer cafe. The cafe sells a variety of drinks, it is very beautiful and cozy here, plasma TVs add chic.

At the entrance to the hotel there is a large hall with a marble white floor, upholstered furniture and a huge TV.

The hotel offers a gorgeous view of the sights of the village of Kabardinka.

The guest house "Niagara", the photo of which you see, is fully consistent with all European standards.
