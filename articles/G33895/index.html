Hotel "Riviera" (Sochi): address, description, reviews. Holidays in Sochi

Comfortable living conditions are offered to you by the resort hotel "Riviera". Sochi attracts millions of tourists, and therefore there are a great many hotels here. This institution can be considered one of the best for a budget holiday.

Brief description of the hotel

An unforgettable holiday experience will give you the Riviera Resort Hotel. Sochi is a sea city, and therefore tourists arriving here want to live near the beach. This hotel provides you with such an opportunity. In addition, many services and services have been prepared for you here that will give you a feeling of comfort. It has its own beach, and a walking area, and much, much more.

It is worth paying special attention to the apartments that the Riviera Hotel (Sochi) offers you. The rooms here, although modest enough, but in them you will find everything you need to feel no less comfortable than at home. There is a minimum necessary set of furniture, as well as a bathroom and other pleasant things.

It is worth noting that the Riviera Hotel (Sochi) does not have its own restaurant. But this is not necessary, because there are a lot of catering establishments nearby. Nevertheless, there is a private dining room where you can order delicious home-style dishes for a low price.





Hotel Location

The hotel is located in one of the most popular resort areas of Sochi. The city center is only a few minutes away. As for the beach, you will have to walk no more than 30 meters to it. Around - there are many cafes, restaurants and shops, as well as in the immediate vicinity there is a large park with walking alleys and attractions.





Hotel Rooms

Hotel "Riviera" is quite small by local standards. There are only 23 rooms, so it is better to book apartments in advance. So, the following accommodation options are available to you:

  • Standard rooms consist of one room with two single beds. In a small area there are all the necessary amenities for a comfortable stay. There is a small fridge, as well as a TV. The bathroom is a separate toilet and shower room.
  • Superior apartments consist of two rooms. The first is a bedroom with a large bed. There is also a small living room, which can serve as a dining room. A folding soft sofa will serve as an extra bed.
  • The family room is the largest and most comfortable in this hotel. There is only one room, but it is very spacious. There is a large bed, as well as a double folding sofa. There is also a kitchen area, which is separated from the main part of the room with a bar.




What facilities are provided in the apartment?

Hotel "Riviera" offers you to use the following amenities in guest apartments:

  • individual air conditioning, which allows you to keep cool in your room, despite the summer heat;
  • if you want to relax in this hotel in spring or autumn, then you will be pleased with the availability of a heating system;
  • a color TV connected to a satellite dish broadcasts several dozen channels, and therefore you will have something to fill your leisure with while in the room;
  • the bathroom includes bath towels, as well as disposable soap;
  • there is a small refrigerator for storing your products, as well as cooling drinks;
  • Each room has a telephone with which you can contact the administration and also call in another city.

One of the budget options for accommodation in Sochi is the Riviera Hotel. Prices for accommodation in the high season start from 2500 rubles.





Resort Infrastructure

Tours in Sochi are very popular due to the fact that the city has a developed resort infrastructure. Nevertheless, it is important, without leaving the hotel, to receive a full range of services. So, in this institution the following infrastructure facilities are available to you:

  • in the rooms, as well as in the area where the reception is located, you can use the wireless Internet absolutely free;
  • the hotel has its own parking lot, which can be used by all guests for an additional fee;
  • for guests who do not tolerate tobacco smoke, special rooms are provided;
  • there is a safe at the reception where you can hand over your valuables if you fear for their safety in the room;
  • special places for smoking;
  • Riviera beach, which is located in close proximity to the hotel.




Services and facilities for guests

Hotel "Riviera" provides its guests with the following services:

  • The reception is open around the clock, and therefore, if you have any problems with your accommodation, you can count on them being resolved as soon as possible;
  • in the laundry room, they are not only engaged in washing bedding from rooms, but can also tidy up your soiled or rumpled clothes;
  • if you want to relax in Sochi in the cold season, then you will be pleased with the heating system in the rooms and other rooms of the hotel;
  • Hotel staff can help you organize sightseeing tours.




Useful information about the hotel

Before you go on vacation to this resort hotel, be sure to read some of the rules that apply to accommodation and recreation:

  • check-in of guests is strictly after 13:00, and check-out is before noon (if there are free rooms, then this rule can be changed individually for an additional fee);
  • if children under 5 years old do not need an extra bed, then no fee will be charged for their accommodation; Babies who are under two years old are provided with free cots;
  • guests are not allowed to bring pets with them (otherwise, they may refuse to register you);
  • to pay for the services you received, you can use a bank plastic card;
  • When booking a room, keep in mind that in the rooms on the upper floors you can hear music from the restaurant.




Positive reviews

It is worth noting that many tourists prefer the hotel "Riviera" (Sochi) for their sea holidays. Reviews about this institution contain such positive comments:

  • well-equipped access to the hotel;
  • pleased that the hotel is located in close proximity to the sea;
  • It is located near the city center, surrounded by cafes and restaurants;
  • friendly and polite staff; the rooms are very well cleaned;
  • I am glad that the room has a refrigerator;
  • next to the hotel is a large, beautiful park;
  • exit from the rooms located on the ground floor leads directly to the street;
  • maids do not clean rooms when it is convenient for them, but only when you leave the keys with the corresponding request at the reception;
  • fairly budget prices for accommodation in rooms;
  • from the windows of the rooms a beautiful view of the sea;
  • Superior rooms have new plumbing.

Negative reviews

It is worth noting that the guests of the Riviera hotel highlight the following shortcomings in the work of this institution:

  • plumbing is leaking into the shower room, and due to poor ventilation, a very stale smell and mold;
  • on the walls of some rooms hang carpets that not only spoil the appearance, but also smell disgusting;
  • there is no makeup in the bathroom, and even paper is not put on the toilet;
  • the hotel always has some kind of confusion with the reservation, and therefore you can’t be sure that you will check into the reserved room;
  • the soundproofing in the rooms is simply terrible, and therefore not only noise from the restaurant, but also voices from neighboring rooms will interfere with your sleep;
  • TV shows just disgusting: all channels hiss;
  • despite the fact that free wireless internet is claimed, it almost never works;
  • on the territory of the hotel for some reason it very poorly picks up the signal of a mobile phone;
  • most outlets in the room do not work;
  • the rooms have bare wires under voltage, and therefore it is quite dangerous to be here for children;
  • a drain is clogged in the shower, and therefore, while you are swimming, a large puddle forms, which can flow into the room;
  • in the rooms that are located on the ground floor, the windows do not open, and therefore it is very stuffy there;
  • the towels provided by the guests are not only old but incredibly small;
  • on the windows of some rooms - cracked glass, which is quite traumatic.

General impression

Fairly high cost of living are characterized by hotels near the beach. Riviera (Sochi) is a kind of exception. Prices by local standards are quite affordable, and the sea is visible from the windows of the rooms. Nevertheless, for a reduced price you will have to pay in comfort. The rooms are absolutely minimalistic in that there is a fairly small set of furniture and amenities. But given that tours to Sochi are mainly bought by lovers of sea holidays, who spend little time in the rooms, then these conditions are quite suitable for an overnight stay.

The main disadvantage of this institution can be considered that there is a club with a disco on its roof. Here music plays loudly until the morning, and therefore guests here are simply impossible to fall asleep. That is why it is not recommended to come here with young children. But young people who like bright entertainment, but can not afford an expensive hotel, have a good time here. It is also worth noting the advantageous location near the beach and a huge amusement park. In the immediate vicinity there are a huge number of cafes, shops and other infrastructure entertainment facilities.
