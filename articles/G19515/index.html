Dolphinarium in Koktebel - an interesting place

For the first time, the Koktebel Dolphinarium opened its doors to the public in the summer of 2008. Now it is one of the most interesting tourist attractions of Crimea.

Description

The dolphinarium complex includes outdoor stands and pools, the surrounding area is surrounded by flowers and greenery. The stands are protected from the sun by deep canopies to provide comfort to the audience. The wide stage provides a great overview. In winter, the dolphinarium closes the dome. On the territory there is a small zoo, which can be visited for free.





The show program is shown from May to October. The rest of the time the dolphinarium in Koktebel is closed to visitors, and the animals rest from performances under the strict supervision of experienced specialists.

Artists

Very nice and smart artists take part in the performances - the Black Sea bottlenose dolphins and northern fur seals. During the show, they demonstrate curiosity, quick wit and excellent learning ability.

A trio of dolphins, Dania, Zoya and Marishka, are ready to entertain the audience before the start of the performance. Zoya is the fastest, most energetic and unpredictable. She is a big prankster, loves to splash water and knock out buckets of fish from the hands of trainers. He loves to play with the ball, constantly comes up with new tricks and never gets bored. Marishka is thorough and serious, and at the same time gentle and easily wounded. Most of all she likes to play with an air mattress, demonstrating the wonders of ingenuity. Danya is the oldest dolphin, he is already more than 30 years old. Harsh, but very kind. But the descent does not give offenders. It is respected by colleagues - both animals and people.

Vasya, Petya and Zhorik are strong and agile fur seals. The oldest of them is Petya. He has a huge experience of performing, he loves to draw and sing. Zhorik perfectly catches rings, is fond of sports and is always active. The youngest cat is Vasya. Every morning he does exercises in order to grow strong and powerful, like his older comrades.

Experienced trainers enjoy the indisputable authority of marine artists, animals trust them and gladly carry out all the teams, vividly responding to the reaction of the public. During the performance, artists play with balls, dance, train coaches in an inflatable boat, perform intricate acrobatic stunts. A colorful show with well-chosen musical accompaniment will appeal to both children and adults. The program is designed for 1 hour.

Additional services and ticket prices

You can take pictures with animal artists: a photo with a dolphin costs 700 rubles, with a cat - 300 rubles.

Those who wish can swim with the dolphin. This pleasure will cost 3,500 rubles.

Location





Where is the Dolphinarium in Koktebel? His address: Crimea, Koktebel, st. Marine, d. 77.

Koktebel Dolphinarium is located in the resort village of Koktebel in the eastern part of Crimea, 20 km from Feodosia. You can get from Feodosia on the highway P29 Feodosia - Alushta by car or by minibus "Feodosia - Biostation" or "Feodosia - Koktebel". Also, the village can be reached by bus from Simferopol, Sudak and Kerch. The facility is located on the promenade behind the water park 40 meters from the sea.

Conclusion

Dolphinarium in Koktebel is a very interesting place. A visit to it is a wonderful opportunity for children and adults to enjoy a fascinating sight and come into contact with the amazing world of marine animals.
