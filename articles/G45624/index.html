Country of contrasts

Many people think that in a country with such a developed tourist infrastructure and a huge network of hotels of various categories, the population should live happily and well off. However, this is not at all true. At first glance, you can understand that Thailand is a country of incredible contrasts. Near the magnificent palaces, the shacks of the poor can be huddled; next to the most modern shopping centers (malls), you can find street markets where ordinary people sell fruits and vegetables, etc. The same applies to hotels - next to luxury deluxe hotels you can find a completely dilapidated two-star hotel or hostel.

However, the best option - the golden mean - these are hotels and mid-level complexes, for example, Ambassador City Jomtien 3 *. As already noted, such hotels in terms of service cannot be compared with European ones. They are quite respectable and are quite consistent with higher class hotels.

How to get there?

Every tourist, knowing that Thailand is located in the very east of the continent of Eurasia, having bought a tour to the Ambassador City Jomtien Inn 3 * hotel in Pattaya, of course, begins to wonder how to get there. From Moscow and other major cities of Russia there are regular and charter flights to the capital of Thailand - Bangkok. After that, tourists change to a bus and get to the resort within two hours.

Pattaya is a city located on the shores of the Gulf of Thailand. To get to it, tourists usually fly to Bangkok and then go by bus for about 2 hours (185 km). Usually a transfer from the airport to the hotel is included in the price of the tour. By the way, in the past, Utapao Airport operated near Pattaya, but today it is closed.

Ambassador City Jomtien Inn 3 *: General Description

Despite the fact that Pattaya is not very suitable for families with children, the administration nevertheless does everything to attract couples with offspring here.
