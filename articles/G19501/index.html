Under what conditions give a mortgage: documents, features and recommendations

The question regarding the conditions under which a mortgage is granted is of interest to many people nowadays. And each person who needs his own roof over his head at least once thought about this issue. Many are scared that they may not approve a mortgage. Well, such cases do happen, albeit rarely. But it is also true that a mortgage loan is extremely beneficial to banks. Therefore, they are issued to many people who have applied for financial assistance. However, knowing about the conditions does not hurt.





Income

The extent to which a potential borrower is solvent is most interesting for bank employees. Under what conditions do they give a mortgage? For those in which the bank's client makes good money and can afford to give part of his income (half, as a rule) as repayment of the debt.

In the eyes of the bank, a person can appear in a good light if he works according to the Labor Code of the Russian Federation, has a higher education and works in one place for at least a year. Entrepreneurs are ‚Äúwelcomed‚Äù reluctantly, considering an individual business a risky activity.

Words about your income will have to be confirmed. There are four documents accepted by the bank. These are certificates in the form of an employer, 2-personal income tax, proof of income in the form of a bank and a statement of declared monthly received funds. ‚ÄúTraditionally‚Äù, clients pass 2-NDFL. This is a familiar document, which indicates income for a certain period - you can track the dynamics or stability that is valued by the bank.





General requirements

Talking about the conditions under which they give a mortgage for an apartment, one cannot fail to note the general provisions. So, here are the requirements that a person who wants to get a home loan must meet:

  • He is required to have citizenship of the Russian Federation.
  • The minimum age of the borrower is 21 years.
  • Maximum - 65 at the time of repayment. That is, if a person intends to obtain a housing loan in 60 years, then they will give it only for 5 years. These are the requirements of most banks. Although Sovcombank, for example, has a minimum age of 20 years and a maximum age of 85.
  • Also, the client must be registered in the region in which he draws up a housing loan.
  • Total work experience - at least a year. At the last workplace - from 6 months.

That's, in principle, and everything regarding the conditions under which banks give a mortgage. In some cases, there may be exceptions. As with Sovcombank, for example. Or with "Gazprombank" and "VTB 24", where the experience counts from 4 months. But in general, the requirements for borrowers are the same.





Documents

Even the person who does not understand anything about the topic of loans can assume that 2-NDFL is far from the only paper necessary for obtaining a housing loan. Well, under what conditions give a mortgage? Documents for a person should be as follows:

  • statement;
  • copy and original passport;
  • SNILS (photocopy is also needed);
  • TIN certificate (original + copy);
  • military ID or registration - for males under the age of 27 years;
  • documents on education, marriage, divorce and marriage contract (if any);
  • in the presence of children - a birth certificate;
  • a copy of the work book, mandatory certified by the employer.

This is the main package of documents. They must be prepared if a person is seriously interested in the question of how to get a mortgage for an apartment.

Who is given a mortgage? Almost everything, and that is why for older people, their retirement certificate is an obligatory document for presentation. And for people with a dubious past - a certificate from the addiction / psychoneurological clinic. However, the bank may request any other documents - but which ones, this will be specified individually.





How much do you need to earn?

The bigger, the better. Paradox! After all, it would seem that a mortgage was created for people in need of financial services. But you really need to earn enough - with the expectation of paying off debt and living.

It is better, however, to give an example. Many go for a mortgage at VTB 24. The conditions are especially favorable there for the bank's customers. So, let's say a person came to arrange a mortgage. He looked after an apartment in a new house for 2,000,000 rubles. He has a down payment of 15% (this is 300,000 p.). And he wants to get a housing loan for 5 years. In this case, his monthly income should be about 63,000 rubles. Because ~ 37,900 he will have to repay as a debt for five years.

Given an interest rate of 12.1% per annum, for this period the client will pay 2,274,120 rubles as debt. Overpayment - 574 120 p. A rather big amount. So, as you can see, the shorter the term and the greater the salary, the less costly the loan will be.

Payment past

It also needs to be noted with attention, talking about under what conditions they give a mortgage. A good credit history is no less important than a good income. And so what, and her banks are checked carefully. Before approving the service to the client, they find out if he has debts under existing contracts, whether he paid the amount in time as payments. In general, as he showed himself as a client. If there is no credit history, then everything will depend on how complete the package of documents submitted by the potential borrower is.
