Hotel Mira Olimpos Beach Hotel 3 * (Turkey / Kemer): description, reviews and photos

Turkey, despite everything, is the most popular mass tourist destination for travelers from all over the world. The country, in the present territory of which great civilizations once flourished, offers tourists not only a chic sea vacation, but also a rich excursion program, many leisure opportunities, as well as complete relaxation and wellness treatments. Nevertheless, in the minds of Russians this country is primarily associated with the Antalya coast, comfortable modern all-inclusive hotels, a huge variety of delicious dishes and drinks, and the gentle sea. At the same time, Turkey offers various, more than affordable types of accommodation in 2 and 3 star hotels, such as Mira Olimpos Beach Hotel 3 * (Kemer / Turkey) , etc. The most important thing here is that regardless of class hotel, tourists are greeted by a friendly welcome and upscale service. They feel themselves welcome guests of this country.

Kemer: leisure features

This resort has a very favorable location: on the one hand - the turquoise boundless expanses of the Mediterranean Sea, and on the other - a 2000-meter wall of picturesque mountains that not only add beauty to the local landscape, but also protect Kemer from winds and bad weather. The resort area stretches for several hundred kilometers. Here next to each other are the following tourist areas with a fairly developed infrastructure: Beldibi, Kirish, Goynuk, Camyuva and others. In all of them, as well as in the city of Kemer, there are hotels with Russian-speaking staff. After all, it is important for the administration that the guests from Russia do not experience dixomfort when communicating with the hotel staff. Such hotels include Mira Olimpos Beach Hotel 3 * (Kemer Center). That is why this hotel is so popular among our compatriots.

How to get there?

If you want to visit the Turkish coast, and for this you have chosen the Kemer resort, you should know that you have to fly to Antalya, since there is only one airport on the Mediterranean coast of the country, and it is located 42 kilometers from the resort you need. From some large cities of Russia, both direct and charter flights are carried out to Antalya. Arriving at the airport, you will first need to get by bus or taxi to the bus station, and then, transferring to another bus, go to Kemer. However, if you buy a tour to Mira Olimpos Beach Hotel 3 *, which includes a transfer, then you will be met right at the airport and delivered with comfort to the hotel. This option is very convenient. In less than half an hour, you will already receive keys at the reception, and a quick employee will guide you to the door. There is another good option - rent a car. Moreover, if this is done in advance, before arrival, the car will be waiting for you at the airport. Take the D400 on the highway to your hotel in minutes. Number of rooms

The hotel has a total of 94 rooms of the category "standard" ("single", "double" and "triple"). All are renovated and equipped with modern furniture. All rooms have air conditioning, refrigerator, satellite TV, telephone. The beds are very comfortable. There is a comfortable sofa, which can become an extra bed. The bathroom includes a shower, hairdryer, towels and toiletries. All rooms, except those located on the ground floor, have balconies with a plastic table and chairs, as well as an umbrella. There is also a clothes dryer. These conditions can be found in the rooms of the Mira Olimpos Beach Hotel 3 * Standard category. By the way, they are cleaned once a week, and towels are changed every other day.

Relax

You must have heard of the Turkish bath. Most tourists go to this country to certainly take a steam bath in it. This type of relaxation is famous for Turkey (Kemer). Mira Olimpos Beach Hotel 3 * can offer tourists hammam services (the so-called Turkish bath). Mira Olimpos Beach is just 50 meters from its own stretch of coast, which is located inside a public beach.
