How to make cookies in Minecraft and how to use it

How to make cookies in Minecraft? In earlier versions of the game, when this type of food just appeared, cookies were a rare and completely ineffective pleasure. The reason for this was that the cocoa beans, which are necessary for crafting cookies, could only be found in the treasures. However, from version 1.3.1, cocoa beans began to appear on trees in the jungle, and due to the fact that they could now be grown on their own, this type of food completely lost its value.

Minecraft Cookies

One cookie recovers 2 units of hunger and only 0.4 units of saturation, which is the smallest indicator among all types of food in Minecraft. So a player eating only cookies quickly gets hungry. The same bread has as many as 6 units, not to mention another meal, for example, fried beef or pork have as much as 12.8 units of saturation.

Craft cookie





Well, let's look at how to make cookies in Minecraft and what you need to craft it. As you can see in the image, there is nothing tricky in the production of this delicacy, all materials do not need pre-treatment, moreover, they can be found in nature if you find the village of NPC and get wheat there. Only two bunches of wheat and a handful of cocoa beans are needed for crafting. We place the cocoa beans between two units of wheat - and our cookies are ready. In its manufacture, the player receives eight cookies for one craft. If you eat them all at once, then after satisfying the hunger they will be equivalent to two pieces of fried pork or beef. This type of food will be a good substitute for bread if the player lacks wheat, as it is easier and faster to grow cocoa beans. In addition, cookies will restore more units of hunger than bread. You can also buy our delicacy from the villagers: for just one emerald, you will get from 6 to 8 pieces.

Application





All the most detailed information can be easily found on the Minecraft Wiki website. How to make cookies and where to get the ingredients for it is described in sufficient detail there. Keep in mind that cookies have a very low saturation rate. This means that the player is almost immediately hungry again after taking it as food. Everyone playing Minecraft is not recommended to eat cookies often and in large quantities, it is best to use it when it is necessary to pay off no more than two or four units of hunger, for example, in order to restore health. I hope that after reading this article you will not have any more questions about how to make cookies in Minecraft.
