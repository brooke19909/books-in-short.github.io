Golovinka. Private sector: living conditions

The Golovinka microdistrict, located in the Lazarevsky district of Greater Sochi, stretches for several kilometers along the Black Sea coast. The mountain peaks of the Caucasus, overgrown with forest thickets, surround it on three sides, and the Shahe River divides into two parts. Through it lies a road leading from Tuapse to Loo and Adler.

Mini-hotels occupy the first line of the village of Golovinka. The private sector is represented by economy class housing. A few steps from the beach runs a single-track railway, on which the "Swallow" trains run regularly. The main attraction is the lyrodendron, which rises one hundred meters from the building of the church of St. Nina.





Left Coast

Be extremely careful when choosing a private sector in the village of Golovinka. The most inexpensive and budget offers are concentrated on the left bank. This is due to the fact that guest houses are removed from the main recreation areas and retail outlets of the microdistrict. To get to the center, you need to use a car or public transport, which runs once every thirty minutes. A walk takes at least an hour.

Center

It is here, on the right side of the mountain river bed, that the most comfortable and convenient guest houses of the village of Golovinka are represented. The private sector occupies the area between Lineyna and Shturmansky Lane.

The most sought-after residential facilities are located on Kommunarov Street, which abuts against the A-147 highway. This is the very heart of the neighborhood. On its territory there are a secondary school No. 78, a Magnit supermarket, a food market, manufactured goods and household chemical goods stores, a bakery and butcher shops. A ten minute walk - dining rooms and the railway station "Golovinka".





Coastline

The beach stretches along the Sturmansky and Central lanes. It is a coastal strip, the width of which in some places reaches ten meters. There are no typical boathouses towering at the very edge of the water. The first line of the microdistrict. Golovinka - a private sector of low floors, cottages and houses.

Until the middle of June, the village beach is empty. The first sunbeds and water activities here appear only in the twentieth numbers. Then the bars and eateries begin their work.

Service

Many private households, like mini-hotels, offer their guests a wide range of additional services. These include connecting to a high-speed Internet access network, access to your own beach, the provision of umbrellas and sun loungers, the use of adult and children's pools. Perhaps the presence of a game zone, barbecue for self-cooking meat and fish on charcoal, tennis or pool tables.





When choosing housing, focus not only on colorful descriptions compiled by the owners of the objects, but also reviews of tourists who have already had a chance to relax in these parts.
