Tablet: specifications, device selection, tips

Today, tablets occupy one of the first places among portable gadgets. Such popularity is easily explained: a large selection of the most diverse models allows you to purchase the right device, always be in touch and up to date with all the news.





The choice

Of course, with such a variety of these devices, the question of choosing the right model can cause some inconvenience. But you do not need to panic, a tablet whose characteristics will completely satisfy all your requirements is not so difficult to choose.

To get started, just decide what you need from a gadget like a tablet. It may be difficult for most users to answer even such a question. Then let's take a closer look at what a tablet is.



The characteristics of this gadget primarily provide for its use for such purposes as watching movies (both video files and directly on the Internet), working with electronic documents, reading electronic books, games, using as a GPS navigator, and working with maps.

These are the technical specifications of tablets of almost all models. Depending on the price and manufacturer, tablet computers may contain many additional features and applications.

Internet tablets, for example, allow you to be on the World Wide Web almost around the clock. If you are a fan of Internet surfing, then you should definitely opt for gadgets in which a SIM card for working with a network is embedded inside.

The fact is that most inexpensive, but high-quality tablets do not allow you to use the built-in SIM card, you need to connect a modem. Agree that this is very inconvenient. Of course, you can use Wi-Fi, but access points are not everywhere.

Internet tablet: specifications

1. CPU

As for personal computers, the processor is the main component of the entire device. Application speed, stability - the main function of the processor. So, if you do not need to deal with graphics processing and solve other complex problems, then a processor with a frequency of 600 MHz will be enough.





But for 3D games with a "heavy" interface, such a processor is not suitable for watching movies in FullHD mode. In this case, it is advisable to choose a frequency of more than 1 GHz.

size 2

Also an important detail of such a gadget as a tablet. Screen size specifications range from 7 to 10 inches. With a large screen, it is more convenient to watch movies, play, read the same books. But there is one significant minus. Such tablets lose in terms of compactness, and therefore wearing this gadget always and everywhere with you becomes not very convenient.

Therefore, if you need a tablet, which should always be at hand, which is easy to use on the go, then the optimal size is a screen of 7 or 8 inches.

Not bad have proved themselves the Lenovo tablet. Prices and specifications (the budget option costs no more than $ 130) these gadgets are pleasantly combined with each other, plus users note the very high quality of the device models of this company.
