Hotel Korona 3 * (Sunny Beach, Bulgaria): photos and reviews of tourists

Sunny Beach is the most lively, fun, noisy resort on the entire Black Sea coast of Bulgaria. By the way, they even want to assign the status of a city to this place because of the large number of people who want to visit such a beautiful place and relax, basking in the rays of the gentle sun.

Sunny Beach itself is located near Varna, only 90 kilometers away, and Burgas - 36 kilometers is still very close. So everyone can go on an excursion to these places and enjoy the sights, expand their horizons.

In Sunny Beach, a large number of houses, bungalows, villas, hotels, waiting in their arms for vacationers and tourists. Since the days of the USSR, this Bulgarian resort has been unheard of in popularity in comparison with other towns. And all because of the fact that it is rich in diverse vegetation and the price of living here is much lower, in comparison with other places to stay. The length of the beach in this paradise is more than 7 kilometers.





Corner of comfort and coziness

Among the luxury of landscapes and a riot of bright colors, the beautiful Korona 3 * hotel (Sunny Beach, Bulgaria) is located. Its location can be called more than successful. And all because it is literally at hand to numerous entertainments. Luxurious beaches, restaurants, karaoke bars, discos, a water park - all within walking distance.

Hotel Korona 3 * (Sunny Beach, Bulgaria) is oriented both to active youth who are thirsting for adventure and entertainment, and to families with children, people who simply want to relax in peace and quiet from the noisy city bustle.

In general, there is a place for coziness, comfort, excellent service.

Pool

It is worth telling about it additionally. On the territory of the hotel Korona 3 * (Bulgaria, Sunny Beach) there is a swimming pool for adults and for the smallest. Children can have fun in warm water, frolic with a variety of toys and enjoy chatting with their peers.

Visitors reviews

Hotel Korona 3 * (Bulgaria, Sunny Beach) receives numerous reviews from tourists, and the vast majority of them are positive. According to vacationers, the hotel has a great location, where from the balcony you can see the courtyard with flower beds and a pool. The reviews also noted that the room has a clothes dryer, the balcony is very spacious, it has a table and chairs. Every day cleaning is carried out, toiletries are replenished regularly.

Excellent soundproofing, no audible discos and songs of cheerful tourists. The hotel staff is polite, polite, not rude, always ready to help, answer questions.

Tourists noted another significant plus: the staff knows Russian well, which simplifies communication.

Breakfast is always plentiful, hearty. The pool is clean, there is a gym and SPA-salon. The hotel has Wi-Fi, it is absolutely free. Themed parties are held for everyone.

Korona 3 * (Sunny Beach) reviews are also negative. Among the shortcomings, vacationers note the absence of a children's menu item, there are no cereals. Also, the prices of sun loungers and sun loungers are slightly overpriced.

But these annoying little things do not spoil the overall pleasant picture that the hotel creates.

Tourists note that they were cordially greeted by staff, a free luggage storage room was provided. The beach is very close, it is clean, the entrance to the sea is not sharp and slippery, but flat, gentle.

But among the shortcomings, vacationers noted a small amount of entertainment, but they can be found near the hotel.

In general, we can say that the rest at the Korona 3 * hotel (Sunny Beach, Bulgaria) is magnificent. Everyone who visits this place at least once will be satisfied and will certainly return to this cozy, welcoming place, where he will be met by sunny weather, warm sea and always friendly staff.
