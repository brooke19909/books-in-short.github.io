The best recreation centers on Lake Sugoyak: description and reviews

A large number of people living in cities dream of relaxing in nature near a reservoir. Residents of the Sverdlovsk and Chelyabinsk regions constantly visit the local "sea", which is Lake Sugoyak. The area of ​​its water mirror is about 13 km 2 . Depth varies within 4 m, and the largest indicator is a mark of 7 m. The reservoir is rich in fish. Fishing lovers come here every year. This entertainment is paid here - 100 rubles. All visitors are hospitably greeted by recreation centers.

There are good sandy beaches on Lake Sugoyak that will appeal to tourists. The depth grows gradually, so here you can relax with young children. The water is clean, warms up quickly. Around the reservoir grows a beautiful Ural forest. In summer, mushrooms and berries are harvested here.

Tourists during their holidays can stay in hotels and camp sites. The latter option is ideal for those people who want to save on accommodation. Let's look at the most popular recreation centers on Lake Sugoyak.

"Cheryomushki"

The Cheryomushki complex is focused on improving and cultural rest. It is located on the shores of Lake. Sugoyak. Next to it are two settlements - Azure and Miass. Visitors are accommodated in two-story buildings built of brick. In winter, all buildings are heated. Six hectares are occupied by the Cheryomushki complex. The whole territory is equipped, there are paved paths. A capital fence runs along the perimeter, the base is guarded around the clock.

Here you can not only have a good time, but also improve your health. A wide range of programs aimed at weight loss, getting rid of allergies, improving heart function and others is offered. A two-story dining room, dance floor, conference room, sauna, beach, barbecue areas, sports grounds - all this is on the territory of the recreation center on the Sugoyak Lake “Cheryomushki”. Reviews about this place are only positive. All visitors were satisfied and come here with pleasure every year.



"Sunrise"

In the village of Lazurny, Chelyabinsk region, there is an inexpensive recreation center “Voskhod”. It is located right on the shore of Lake Sugoyak. Annually the administration conducts anti-mite treatment in the territory. Due to this, there are always many vacationers who come here with young children. The cost of living for one person will cost from 150 to 300 rubles. You can rent houses designed for 3, 4, 6 people. They are equipped with water supply, there are household appliances and furniture. Bed linen and utensils are issued by the administration of the recreation center on Lake Sugoyak “Voskhod”.

Guest reviews are different. Many are satisfied with the rest, but there are those who do not like certain points. For example, a toilet on the street, the lack of a dining room and the Internet. But, in fairness, it is worth noting that the living conditions and prices have an ideal ratio.



"South coast"

Tourist base "South Coast" - one of the inexpensive institutions in the village of Lazurny. It is located 50 meters from Lake Sugoyak. It is comfortable to rest here both in summer and in winter. Many visitors noted that a lot of people come here on weekends and holidays, which leads to a noise that does not subside even at night. However, in general, to spend a full vacation here is quite possible. The necessary furniture, refrigerator, electric stove, kettle - all this is in the rooms of the two-story building and summer houses of the recreation center on the Sugoyak Lake "South Coast".

Visitor reviews are mixed, but in most cases positive. Some tourists believe that repairs are needed in summer houses. The bathroom is only in superior rooms. Separately, it is worth noting that a discount system operates on the basis of. Children under 6 years old are accommodated free of charge, and for a child under 12 years old parents will pay only 20% of the total cost of the room.



"Aquarius"

Just 50 meters from the shore of the beautiful lake Sugoyak is the recreation center "Aquarius". Only two minutes to the beach. A mixed forest (birch, pine) grows on the territory; shrubs are found closer to the water. From Chelyabinsk to "Aquarius" the distance is about 40 km. The base only works in the summer. One night will cost at least 800 rubles.

All guests are accommodated in wooden houses. There are two-, three-, four- and five-seater. On weekdays, accommodation will be cheaper, and on weekends and holidays, prices increase by about 200-400 rubles. The houses of the recreation center on Lake Sugoyak "Aquarius" are equipped with everything necessary. There is furniture, electric stove, fridge and kettle. There is no organized food. All houses with amenities.

Tourists are invited to go with a ranger for a walk in the surrounding area. On the territory there is a playground for children, tennis and billiard rooms. Fishing enthusiasts can go fishing in the lake. All necessary equipment can be rented.



"Birch"

Describing recreation centers on Lake Sugoyak, it is necessary to talk about the "Birch". It belongs to the category of economy. The minimum cost of living starts at 300 rubles. It works all year long. The base is located in a birch grove. On the territory there are tables with benches, wooden houses, there is a playground for children's games. Visitors are resettled in four-seater houses. You can rent housing for any period of time.

The administration organizes training courses in spearfishing, as well as entertainment programs: water rides (banana, skiing), boat rides and bicycles. Tourists can visit the cafe and karaoke bar, enjoying live music. There are also equipped barbecue areas.
