Hotel Pinero Tal 3 * (Spain, Mallorca): description, service, reviews

Choosing a place for an exotic weekend with a loved one or family vacations with your family, many people stay in hot Spain with its colorful temperamental population and year-round favorable climate. The largest island of the Balearic archipelago is Mallorca, where you can spend time varied and enjoy the beauties of amazing nature. Climb to the top of the mountains, sink to the bottom of the Mediterranean Sea or enjoy riding on the waves - something that is available to absolutely everyone, regardless of age.

Tours to Mallorca are popular among citizens of different states and continents. Here everyone can find housing in their own pockets. The run of prices will please both lovers of budget holidays and those who wish to live in luxury apartments. Tours to Mallorca are acquired by families with children, as well as noisy companies. Everyone will find something to their liking. Where can I stay? There are many options. I would like to bring to your attention the Pinero Tal hotel complex, which rightfully has three stars. It offers European standards of service and comfortable apartments to everyone who decided to settle in its walls.

general characteristics

The history of this hotel dates back to 1969. Over the years of its existence, it has been repeatedly modernized; cosmetic repairs are regularly carried out. Global reconstruction took place in 2006. On a compact territory there is one nine-story building, which is equipped with two elevators for the convenience of guests. In addition, there is a swimming pool, surrounded by palm trees and fragrant greenery.

Location

One of the significant reasons in favor of this institution is its favorable location in relation to the airport. The road to it will take less than 15 minutes, because the distance is about 6 km. A short transfer does not bother tourists after a long flight to the island. You can reach Palma city center within 20 minutes. The nearest bus stop is 50 meters from the hotel.

General Provisions

According to the rules of the internal regulations, check-in with pets is unacceptable. Smoking is prohibited throughout. One room is equipped with everything necessary for people with disabilities. The language barrier is eliminated thanks to staff who speak many languages: English, Russian, French and Spanish. Payment for services is possible through cards of international banking systems. Check-in time is standard: 2 p.m.

Number of rooms

For accommodation, 198 superior apartments are offered. All of them belong to the Standard category. The area is 17 square meters and is designed for the simultaneous residence of three people. Upon prior request, it is possible to install an extra bed in the form of a folding bed.

The rooms of the Pinero Tal 3 * hotel complex convey a sense of home comfort and warmth. They have a modern design and are equipped with everything necessary for a comfortable stay. The walls are decorated with paintings by local painters. The floors are covered with ceramic tiles. White walls visually expand the space, filling the room with freshness and tranquility.

There is access to the balcony, where the plastic table and chairs are located. Here you can spend sunset and enjoy the beautiful starry sky. The romantic atmosphere allows you to enjoy a full stay. French glazing reveals the chic landscapes of the sea, and allows the first rays of the sun to penetrate and wake sleeping travelers with its warmth.

The duty of the maids is to clean up the rooms every day, which they, as experienced specialists note, can easily cope with. Cleanliness is one of the main advantages of this establishment. Linen is changed twice a week. Towel change every day. For those who like to sleep, a timely wake-up service is available, according to which the responsible reception staff will call the guests at the appointed time. Room Service is paid additionally and is sold 24 hours a day.

Equipment

Living here, as experienced in their many reviews of Pinero Tal, is a pleasure. The furniture set is primitive, but incredibly convenient. Soft beds allow you to relax and sleep in the morning. There are bedside tables, a table, a wardrobe and an armchair.

The modern central split system allows you to create a favorable microclimate as weather conditions change. There is a TV for watching your favorite TV shows and news in Russian. There is a telephone, the purpose of which is an instant solution to problems through communication with the reception staff. To keep important documents and gadgets safe and sound, a safe will help. However, the use of it is paid extra. Wireless internet access is free everywhere.

The bathroom is decorated with light tones. It is equipped with a sink, a bathroom, a shower and a hairdryer. The number of towels complies with international standards. Here you can take advantage of the proposed set of personal care products, which is updated daily.

Supply system

For its guests, the Pinero Tal hotel complex organizes meals according to the All Inclusive system. In accordance with it, a three-time meal is offered in the main restaurant. The meal is served as a buffet, according to which each guest, having independently assessed the dishes visually, chooses what he likes and puts it on his plate. According to experienced reviews, the abundance of products on offer, cold and hot appetizers, desserts and fruits is varied and incredibly nutritious. Once a week, a themed dinner is served. The consumption of all locally produced drinks, namely beer, wine, coffee and tea, is available at the bar until 23:00. After - paid extra.

The interior of the main restaurant wraps every visitor in a friendly atmosphere and is conducive to eating and easy communication. Talented chefs invest a piece of their soul in culinary masterpieces.

Beach

Guests at Pinero Tal can visit Arenal Beach, 300 meters from the property. The width of the beach area reaches 35 meters. In order to find yourself in the waters of the Mediterranean, you must cross the roadway. There is no underpass.

The infrastructure is well developed. There are changing cabins and a rescue service. You can wash off sea salts in showers. The shoreline is dotted with sun loungers for sunbathing and equipped with shady canopies for those who wish to hide from the scorching sun. Thanks to the gentle entrance to the sea, the children have the opportunity to frolic in shallow water under the supervision of their parents.

Among the large list of beach activities, the most popular is diving. Acquaintance with the picturesque reefs, unique flora and vibrant inhabitants impress both beginners and experienced divers. Riding on the waves - windsurfing - perfect for lovers of extreme relaxation. Here you can also ride on water scooters and go on an excursion to the open sea.
