LG Refrigerators

How can I give feedback on LG refrigerators? This technique is a great example of introducing progress into everyday life. Refrigerator LG GA-B409SVQA

Refrigerator LG GA-B409SVQA (customer reviews indicate this) is perfect for a family of 3-4 people. It is quite high (190 cm) and roomy, with a 87 liter freezer. The entire volume of the freezer is divided into three deep drawers. Convenient handles are another feature that the LG GA-B409SVQA refrigerator boasts. Reviews indicate that continuous recesses inside the door are much more convenient than screwed on the outside. Such handles cannot be broken, torn off or touched when moving. The display allows you to monitor and adjust the temperature of each camera separately, in addition, it gives the model a more modern look. However, it cannot be said that it is equipped with some unique options or provides any additional features compared to other similar models. This refrigerator comes with the No Frost system, which allows you to operate it without defrosting. On the one hand, this technology facilitates the maintenance of a household appliance, on the other hand, a working fan increases the noise level. However, this problem concerns not only this model, but also others with a similar defrosting system.

Summary

In general, refrigerators under the LG brand can be described as reliable, modern, requiring minimal maintenance. All units are characterized by approximately the same drawbacks: a high noise level, amplified when the door is open, a low-informative display that cannot be used to track the current temperature, and a non-standard egg stand. At the same time, an extensive lineup can be noted, which allows you to choose equipment to your liking, including choosing a refrigerator for the interior of the kitchen. LG produces both compact single-chamber units and huge capacious Side-by-Side. Refrigerators under this brand are available to middle-class customers, but they cannot be called cheap.
