HTC One M7: specs and features

Over the past years, one of the largest manufacturers of mobile phones HTC has partly lost its leading position. Its latest models were losing to peers from competitors Apple and Samsung, and the level of popularity in the market became lower every year. In order to regain credibility among consumers, the company begins to develop a new model, which will be the completion of the One line and will embody all the advantages of previous communicators. Thus, the HTC One M7 was created, the characteristics of which are really impressive. All the shortcomings of previous models were taken into account by the manufacturer so that they would not be repeated in the new smartphone.

Review of HTC One M7, characteristic of HTC One and some features



At the initial inspection of the new items from the famous brand, a solid metal case immediately catches your eye. Unlike previous models, the manufacturer excluded almost all plastic parts, leaving outside only a molded frame and glass. In general, it should be noted that before the development of HTC One, devices with a 100% metal body were not yet produced in principle, since this kind of basis has certain features. The surface shields the radio waves, so previously had to embed parts from other materials. However, developers from HTC were able to take a different approach to solving this problem - to transfer all the necessary transmitters to the outside with the help of small holes in the smartphone case. All trace elements are located inside these holes and are fixed with a composite. Negative sides



One of the main shortcomings of the developers, experts consider certain metal features. So, for example, with a strong impact, the external case can easily change its shape (since the metal does not absorb shock well) and damage the internal parts of the smartphone, and, unlike other materials, it is not very practical to use. The weight of this model is quite large due to the aluminum back cover. In addition, many consumers complain about the inconvenient placement of the lock key, given the large dimensions of the device. In general, HTC One M7, the characteristics of which we examined, also has a number of other shortcomings that users pay attention to, namely, the location of the volume control and changing the standard set of touch keys.

HTC One screen



It should be noted separately the quality of the display of this device - the image performance is truly impressive. Smartphone HTC One M7, the screen characteristics of which can be considered one of the best, compared with competitors, also has an amazing multi-touch function, the ability to respond to 10 simultaneous touches. The internal matrix of the model provides a chic viewing angle and wonderful color reproduction. The screen perfectly shows even in bright sunlight, in contrast to the competitive models of the iPhone 5 and Galaxy S4. Such a nice bonus will be appreciated by all consumers, especially those who like to use the phone while driving. The touchscreen of the screen quickly responds to all commands executed with the touch of a finger or stylus.
