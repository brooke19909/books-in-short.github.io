The game "Scrabble". Rules of the game, detailed instructions.

Scrabble is a board game that is very popular among intellectuals. In the article we will tell you in detail what this entertainment is that you can play with friends, describe all the rules and tell who will be able to become the winner of the linguistic game "Scrabble". The rules of the game are simple.

To learn, you first need to buy a box with a set of letters and the main "board", drawn by squares. You can do this even on the Internet.

What is included?

There are small magnetic travel chips and boards on sale, but you can buy a simple set of a paper box and a set of plastic chips.

The magnetic kit consists of a plastic box, one part of which is a playing field, drawn into squares of different colors. Most of them are black or white. But there are prize squares of bright colors. There are two magnetic strips on which are placed 7 letters, relying on each player.

The markup value of the playing field

The main cells of the field have the main colors - white or black, depending on the model of the game "Scrabble". The rules of the game indicate that if the letter is on a simple cell, then its value is indicated on the chip with the letter.

Colored cells are considered premium. There are squares that increase the prize pool of only letters. So, the points of the chip located on the green cell are doubled. If it falls on the yellow square, then the points increase three times. In order for them to count towards one of the players, he must place the word on this square before his opponent in the Scrabble game.





The rules of the game say that bonus points can also increase for the whole word at once. This happens when the letters fall on the blue and red cells. If the words pass through the territory of the blue square, then all the meanings of each letter are doubled. If the word turned out to be one of the letters on the red cell, then all the glasses written on each letter triple. This is the best result.

If the letters of one word hit both the bonus cells for the letter and the cells for the word, then the bonus points are added up.

There are sets of games with a field on which its meaning is written in letters on each cell. These are mostly large desktops. In small road complexes, detailed printed instructions are given with all the rules.

Scrabble: Board Game Rules

The game set includes 128 chips with letters and three chips with a painted star. This is the best detail, since it can easily replace absolutely any letter in the game at the request of its owner.

They start the game by turning all the chips upside down so that the players do not see their value. Then each player blindly collects for himself 7 chips, from which it will be necessary to make some kind of word.



If the player does not go first, he has the right to use one of the letters of another player, but only one. Who is the winner of the game?

Laying out words from his seven chips, the player calculates the points received. To do this, add all the numbers written on each letter. If a player places a letter or a word on the bonus square, then the value accordingly increases two or three times. All points are recorded in a notebook opposite the name of the player.





After the words of these seven letters are made up, and the player cannot think of anything else, the move is passed to the next player, and the player who wins gets the required number of missing letters to the number seven. So, in turn, all the players write the words. When there is already no free space on the field, players begin the total score. Whoever scored more points, the winner of the game "Scrabble" with friends. The rules are easy, so quickly memorized.
