Dzhemete, Anapa: tourist reviews, best places to stay

Tourists who have repeatedly rested in Anapa, of course, fell in love with this cute resort. It may not be possible to find such a level of service as abroad, but our native sea can also please vacationers. This article will focus on the village of Dzhemete (Anapa). Reviews of him as a beach resort make you pay attention to this place. But we will tell you more about everything in our article.

A little about Dzhemete

Positive reviews about Dzhemete in Anapa probably inspired many to visit this cute corner of the domestic resort. The village should be considered exclusively as a place for a wonderful beach holiday. In general, according to reviews, the village of Dzhemete (Anapa) is a youth and family resort. Here there is a fairly large selection of pensions, resorts, hotels and housing in the private sector. In principle, tourists have the opportunity to choose the suitable option for their holidays in Dzhemete (Anapa), the reviews confirm this.

Conducting a conversation about the village, it is worth mentioning that at present the infrastructure is already well developed in it, so vacationers will always find where to go and what to do. The village has a water park, numerous water activities. You can not only relax on the coast, but also go on an excursion to Anapa, which is located five minutes by shuttle from Dzhemete.



Speaking about the advantages of the village, it is worth noting that it is located very close to the airport and the railway station, so traveling from far away is quite convenient. And a taxi can deliver you to the resting place.

Pension Dzhemete (Anapa)

Pension "Dzhemete" (Anapa) - and reviews confirm this - is located on the first line of the famous Pioneer Avenue. Its territory occupies about five hectares. It has four-story buildings with rooms of different categories. The hotel's park area is an ideal place to relax.

Three meals a day in the form of a buffet are organized for tourists. On the territory there are two pools for children and adults. A playground is equipped for the smallest, a playroom with a tutor works.

Near the boarding house operates a water park "Tik-Tak", nearby there are restaurants and cafes. Reviews about the pension "Dzhemete" (Anapa) suggest that the institution is focused on family vacations with children of any age. For this, all conditions are created.

The territory of the complex is surrounded by coniferous and deciduous trees and is equipped with flower beds. Near each building there are benches for rest.



Near each pool there are equipped relaxation areas with sunshades and sun loungers. The hotel also offers bike rental, scooters, rollers and prams for babies.

But adults can visit the Finnish sauna, massage room, pool, play table tennis or volleyball. There is a private sandy beach 200 meters from the hotel. It is equipped with sun loungers and umbrellas, there is also a first-aid post and lifeguards. The guest house offers free parking. The hotel always has a representative of a travel company offering to make exciting trips to local attractions.

Pioneer Avenue

Discussing reviews of Dzhemete (Anapa), it is worth recalling the main attraction of the resort, which is known to all tourists - this is Pioneer Avenue. It is considered the longest street of Anapa. Avenue begins at the Anapka River and stretches along the coast. It built a considerable number of boarding houses, sanatoriums. Most of them have been here since Soviet times. The name of the street was due to the fact that the first on it began to build pioneer camps. Since then, the prospectus has not changed its name. It runs along a long beach with a length of about ten kilometers, for the sake of which tourists come here. In recent decades, the number of hotels on Pionersky Prospekt has increased significantly. Anapa Lazurnaya

Good reviews of Anapa Lazurnaya in Dzhemete give reason to recommend it as one of the worthy options. The complex consists of a hotel and a boarding house. It is located on Pioneer Avenue. Beach Vacation Reviews in Dzhemete

Summing up the conversation, it is worth discussing the reviews about the village of Dzhemete (Anapa). According to tourists, the resort is incredibly convenient for families with children, because there is a very shallow sea, which warms up quickly and makes it possible for kids to flounder in the water. To some extent, Jamet is a little calmer than in the center of Anapa.



However, do not hope that the beach will be free and comfortable. The local coast is pretty busy with tourists. There are always a lot of people here. Of course, as in any resort, there are a lot of water activities and other beach services. The coast is equipped with sunbeds and changing rooms. In general, the beach itself is very good. However, in August, algae appear in the sea, there are a lot of them and, of course, they spoil the impression.

Benefits of a children's resort

As for Dzhemete itself, the village is a good alternative to larger resort towns. The infrastructure is well developed here. There are all kinds of entertainment, including a water park and a dolphinarium.



An indisputable advantage is the proximity of Anapa, which makes it possible at any time by minibus to go to the resort center. However, it is worth emphasizing that Dzhemete is a great place for a family vacation with children, but active young people may need to choose something else.

Instead of an afterword

If you plan to go on vacation to one of the domestic resorts with your child, then the village of Dzhemete near Anapapa can be a great place. A shallow warm sea, good infrastructure and sun - all this awaits you on the coast of Dzhemete.
