Smartphone HTC U11: specifications, description, reviews

HTC is one of the first companies to introduce an Android smartphone. Most users were interested in the Sense proprietary shell. Its interface was the most convenient in comparison with other devices. But time goes on, and developers are constantly improving Android, so HTC smartphones have gradually lost their uniqueness. Demand for them began to decline. The fact is that the manufacturer kept a rather high cost when competitors regularly produced functional devices with decent characteristics and, most importantly, with an affordable price.

In the spring of 2017, HTC introduced a new flagship. Many believe that the manufacturer using this model wants to regain its former glory. Of course, time will tell what will happen next, and now the novelty has definitely interested advanced users. And it is worth noting that the gadget can really surprise. When choosing equipment for the device, the manufacturer did not stint. What is really worth attention in the HTC U11?

  • Price. After studying the characteristics, the cost of 45-50 thousand rubles looks quite adequate.
  • Design. The company abandoned the usual style, preferring an experimental appearance.
  • Camera. Excellent optics with top performance.
  • Performance. Heavy duty processor in conjunction with a large amount of RAM.




Innovative gadget look

The HTC U11 review needs to start with the design. The company has radically changed its style. This model is experimental with a completely new design direction. The flagship has a glass case iridescent with different colors. This effect was achieved due to the reflective mirror surface.

Have buyers been surprised? Definitely yes. But whether this decision was successful, eloquently described in the reviews. All owners claim that the beauty of the case can be enjoyed until it is in the hands. In just a few minutes, the mirror surface of the glass becomes completely blurred. It was not so easy to wipe fingerprints and greasy stains, which is why it is better to carry the HTC U11 smartphone in a case. More easily soiled options are blue and black. In addition to these colors, the manufacturer also introduced copies with a white, blue and red body. As for the latter, he really liked the weak half of humanity. It looks fresh, bright and original.

The shape of the phone is elongated. With a width of 75 mm, its height reaches almost 154 mm. Unfortunately, the developers did not abandon the screen frames, which are quite thick. According to many users, they are the "fat" minus. Bearing in mind that the screen has a width of only 68 mm, the thickness of the frames was 3.5 mm. The manufacturer tried to do everything possible in order to avoid similarities with other flagships.

Using a smartphone was not very convenient. Because of the slippery body, you only have to operate with two hands. If you take a chance, then the probability of dropping the device increases significantly.

There is one more point worth paying attention to. The case of the device is moisture resistant. Here, the manufacturer implemented the IP67 standard. What does this mean? Theoretically, a smartphone can be immersed in water to a depth of one meter for no more than 30 minutes. He is not afraid of dust. Therefore, if you suddenly find yourself in the sand, then you should not worry that small particles penetrate inside.

Performance

According to the performance criterion, HTC U11 has top-end specifications. It runs on the Qualcomm Snapdragon 835 powerful 8-core chipset. The process technology is 10 nm. The set of commands is carried out according to the architecture of ARMv8-A. This processor in one second will perform a huge number of actions, which confirms the clock frequency of 2450 MHz. The system is 64-bit, capable of processing 8 bytes per cycle.

Software

Continuing to describe the characteristics of HTC U11, you need to talk about the operating system. Without it, a gadget is just a "brick." The device is based on the current version of Android. We are talking about the "seven" (Nougat). Of course, the proprietary Sense UI shell is installed on top of the system.

Those buyers who constantly use the phones of this brand will not notice any changes. The design is simple, the settings are flexible. All owners liked this.

Separately, the reviews highlighted the presence of three assistants: Amazon Alexa, HTC Companion and Google Assistant. A special Boost + application is also preinstalled, which allows you to optimize the system and clear the memory. Under Armor service appealed to those who like to actively spend time doing fitness.





Edge sense

The review of HTC U11 will be incomplete, if not to talk about one proprietary "chip". It's about Edge Sense. The side faces of the phone are equipped with special sensors that respond to clicks. For example, in order to activate the flashlight, you need to press the edges once. You can turn it off by repeating this step. Configuring this feature is very easy. The manufacturer provided for the adjustment of the degree of pressure, the installation of any applications and functions. Definitely, users liked this solution.

Autonomy

HTC U11 (128Gb / 64Gb), unfortunately, was not pleased with the battery life. The model has a 3000 mAh battery. In principle, the battery life is enough for 20 hours of operation of the gadget. It is worth highlighting the possibility of fast charging, using which you can charge the phone to 100% in just 1.5 hours.
