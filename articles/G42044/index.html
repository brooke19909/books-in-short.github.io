Hostels in Adler: addresses, description of rooms, reviews

The city of Adler is a traditional vacation spot for Russians. There is a developed infrastructure, which includes many hotels and pensions, the comfort of accommodation in which is combined with the high cost of living.





Inexpensive segment is represented by hostels, which are economy class hotels. Hostels in Adler are a great option for a company vacation (rooms are usually designed for four people). Despite the low cost of living, the hostels have renovated, equipped with all necessary rooms, and the staff is always ready to assist in organizing recreation. In the midst of the tourist season, it is almost impossible to find a hostel with free rooms, so you need to book a place in advance. This article will cover the most popular hostels in Adler.

"Olympus"





Located in the Olympic Park area (13 Kamyshovaya St.) next to the Formula 1 highway, the hostel offers its guests accommodation in separate (equipped with air conditioning, TV and tea making facilities) and dormitory rooms. Vacationers are offered dinners for a relatively small fee. Guests can also cook in the shared kitchen or visit the many cafes and bars located within a 15-minute walk. Many people think that the Olympus (hostel) is the best offer on the low-cost housing market. Adler is a rather big city. But the convenient location of the hostel allows tourists to easily get to the most interesting places.

Its distance from the railway station is six kilometers, to the airport - seven kilometers. To visit the Olympic Park will be enough to cross the road. The nearest beach is one and a half kilometers away.

Reviews about "Olympus"

Olympus is a great budget hostel (Adler). Prices - from 600 rubles. per day. Visitors stay at the hostel remembered for delicious food for a small price. Also, guests note the cleanliness and friendliness of the hostess, the proximity of the main attractions of the city. Most tourists are delighted to stay in comfortable, clean rooms. They like that the hostel is located in a beautiful location, next to the park. For economical living, many recommend the Olympus hostel. Adler is not an expensive city, but at least a little for housing, but you can save. As a drawback, some note a small number of stores in the district.

Hotel Anita





The hotel is located in the old city center and is a guest house with twelve rooms. The rooms are decorated in warm colors and equipped with necessary furniture, a TV and air conditioning. Each room has a safety deposit box. For self-catering, there is a shared kitchen, which also has a washing machine. It offers sea views. Such good conditions have attracted Anita travelers for many years. The hotel (21/2, Mira St.) is famous for its affordable prices and decent conditions.

The hotel is located close to city attractions (dolphinarium, Olympic facilities). For an additional fee, excursions to Abkhazia and Krasnaya Polyana are organized, as well as a shuttle service. Almost all hostels in Adler offer travelers good accommodation for a small fee.

Traveler opinions

Tourists note comfortable rooms, friendliness and impeccable courtesy of the staff. Without exception, everyone enjoys staying at a hotel. Vacationers note the cleanliness of the room and the responsiveness of the administrator.

Some people like the proximity of the hotel to the sea and the presence of a refrigerator and a safe in the room. Particularly pleased with the speed and quality of cleaning in the room. According to numerous tourists, decent housing for little money can be found in the Anita Hotel. The hotel (21/2, Mira St., Adler) is waiting for guests throughout the holiday season.

Hostel "Marine"





The hostel is located in the central part of Adler, just a seven-minute walk from the sea coast (27 Pavlik Morozov St.). Vacationers are offered dormitory rooms, a desk, wardrobe and air conditioning. There is also a shared bathroom. The hotel has a shared kitchen for cooking. Also, vacationers have the opportunity to use wireless Internet and free parking. In the evenings, guests can relax on the terrace. And you can even read something interesting in the library. The Morskoy Hostel (Adler) has earned a lot of positive reviews. The guests are satisfied with the prices, conditions and quality of service.

"Tower"



The hostel is located just five kilometers from the famous Sochi Aquarium, on the street. Ulyanova, 80-1. Guests stay in comfortable rooms equipped with furniture, air conditioning and a TV. The rooms are non-smoking. Pets are also welcome. Vacationers use a shared bathroom.

The hostel provides 24-hour check-in and check-out. Photocopying and faxing services are available upon request. The hotel has free parking. Shuttle service and organization of excursions are possible at an additional cost. Also, guests can use the free wireless Internet access service.

Hostel "HSE" is located in close proximity to the largest shopping centers. Two kilometers away is the airport.
