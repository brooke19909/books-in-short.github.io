Hotels in the center of Saratov: addresses, description, reviews and photos

Saratov is located on the right high bank of the Volgograd reservoir of the Volga River, is the administrative center of the Saratov region. This is a city with its own history, there are many modern industrial enterprises, as well as interesting architectural objects, so both business people and tourists come here. Hotels in Saratov in the city center will offer you comfortable conditions for relaxation, and you can feel at home.

Hotel "Pearl"

Staying at this hotel you can admire the unusual views of the Volga. Its location is the very center of Saratov, nearby is the beautiful park of the city "Lipki". You can get to the railway station and bus station from here in just 15 minutes, many attractions are very close by. Hotel "Pearl" can be called one of the most beautiful hotels in Saratov in the center (photo below).

If you come with your own car, you can leave it in the free indoor car park. The hotel's restaurant serves European cuisine. An ATM is installed on the territory, and an elevator is available for access to the upper floors. Internet is free of charge. If necessary, an airport shuttle will be arranged for you. The hotel offers a choice of rooms such as:

  • single standard - 3 700 rubles per day;
  • double standard - 5,400 rubles per day for two people and 3,700 rubles for one guest;
  • double deluxe - 8,400 rubles per day for three people, 6,700 rubles for two and 6,400 rubles per day for single occupancy;
  • standard triple - 8,400 rubles per day for three people, 6,700 rubles for two and 6,400 rubles per day when placing one guest;
  • Suite - 8 900 rubles per day for a three-person stay, 7 200 rubles for two and 5 200 rubles per day for single occupancy.

The above prices include bed and breakfast. Upon request, guests can pay for the room without meals. The hotel "Pearl" is located at the address: Cathedral Square, house 2.

Hotel Volga

If you are interested in Saratov hotels in the city center on the street. Kirov, and this is a favorite place for walks of citizens, the Volga Hotel invites you. The hotel is located in a beautiful building with almost a century of history. Nearby are several theaters, the city circus, the Lipki park, and luxury shops. The train station can be reached from here in six minutes.

The hotel's own restaurant serves traditional Russian cuisine. Parking for guests is free, and guests can freely use the wireless Internet. At the hotel there is an ATM, shops. Here you can live with pets at no extra cost, which requires a preliminary request. The hotel has an elevator. For accommodation, guests are offered the following rooms:

  • single standard - 2 400 rubles per day;
  • double standard - 2 800 rubles per day for two, and 2 400 rubles for one person;
  • Suite - 3,900 rubles a day for two and 3,500 rubles for single occupancy.

The price includes a continental breakfast. The Volga Hotel is located at 34 Kirova Avenue.

Hotel on Kuznechnaya

If you want to spend the night inexpensively, hotels in the center of the city of Saratov will please with their variety. A good option would be the Kuznechnaya Hotel. Walk to the central Theater Square in just ten minutes, and to the park "Lipki" - fifteen. Guests of the hotel can use the Internet freely, but a private parking space nearby is paid. If you wish, they will arrange a transfer to the airport or you will be met upon arrival. The hotel has a shared kitchen, equipped with everything necessary. Several cafes are within walking distance. The following room options are offered for accommodation:

  • single with shared shower and toilet - 1,000 rubles per day;
  • single with a bathroom - 1,500 rubles per day;
  • single standard - 1 900 rubles per day;
  • double room with shared bathroom and toilet - 1,500 rubles per day;
  • double standard - 2 000 rubles per day.

All air-conditioned rooms are equipped with a TV and fridge. The hotel is located in a residential area at the address: Kuznechnaya street, 28/42.
