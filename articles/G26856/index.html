Camp "Oblomov", Voronezh: description, how to get, reviews

Recently, vacationers are increasingly choosing places to spend vacations in the vastness of their native country. Some go to the Black Sea, others to the Far East or the Volga region, but there are also those who prefer to go to one of the regions of the Black Earth region, for example, to Voronezh. The Oblomov camp, located near the city, is no worse than any other place and, according to the leaders, is quite capable of providing high-quality and inexpensive vacation. However, is this really so, it remains to be understood.

Where is

The Oblomov camp site is located in Voronezh, more precisely, 30 km from the city, which, in fact, is a suburb. The place was chosen perfectly - this is Levoberezhnoye forestry, with many trees and plants. The air around is very clean, and there is also a pine forest nearby. Also nearby is the local river Usmanka.

The shore near the river is sandy, so in the warm season you can sunbathe on the beach and swim.

How to get there

How to get to the camp "Oblomov" in Voronezh? First you need to get into the city itself. You can do this in different ways and in any convenient way, so you should not particularly focus on this. Another question: what are the ways to get to the base? And looking ahead, I want to note that there are only two of them.

So, in order to get to the Oblomov camp in Voronezh, you need to use a taxi or your own car. No buses go in this direction, and no one carries anyone from the central office of the base, which is located in the city itself. The "transfer" service is not provided, and it‚Äôs very in vain, because it‚Äôs difficult for a person who first arrives in Voronezh to navigate. This nuance is often reflected in negative reviews.





The hostel is located at: Zheleznodorozhny district, Cordon Lighthouses, the bank of the Usmanka River. Actually, there is only one road to this place - a toll circuit M-4. Following the road, you need to get to the turn to Kozhevnoye, and there you can already navigate by the appropriate signs.

Not too convenient, but what to do, there are no other options. The task is further complicated by the fact that the navigator does not always correctly indicate the direction, which is why some vacationers circled the forest for some time in search of the correct route.

To avoid such cases, you must:

  1. At the first fork, in front of the burned forest, turn right.
  2. Upon reaching the second fork, you will see two roads - one can go to the lighthouse, and the other to the base. So, you need to go straight to the second fork.

And one more important point. It is recommended to drive carefully in the forest, as at any moment a roe deer or any other animal can jump out on the road and simply do not have time to press the brake in time.





As for the option with a taxi, you can consider it, but such a trip will be expensive, after all, the distance is 30 km from the city.

Description

It's time to move on to the description of the Oblomov camp in Voronezh. As already mentioned, due to its location, namely due to the large number of trees and other vegetation, the air in the district is very clean.

For accommodation of guests both separate houses, and various numbers are provided. Prices for them depend primarily on the class. More on this below.





The hostel offers the organization and holding of weddings, events and other holidays. It has a banquet hall for 120 people and even a small amphitheater with a capacity of up to 60 people.

Also has its own cafe, which serves European cuisine.

There is a separate billiard room, a playground, and equipment rental is available for people involved in sports. It is worth noting the presence of a Russian bath and Internet in all rooms.

Since the camp site is open all year round, including in winter, here you can rent skis and ride through the forest and the countryside.





In the warm season, you can swim in the river Usmanka and sunbathe on the sandy beach. Comfortable deckchairs are provided for everyone.

In addition, there are several boats and kayaks on the shore, where you can swim and enjoy the local beauties directly from the water.

It‚Äôs impossible to call the Oblomov camp in Voronezh boring, there is definitely something to do here. But vacationers would like something more. For example, the same water skiing or riding a jet ski here obviously would not hurt. This is what tourists say in their reviews.

As for the rest itself, everything is extremely simple here. Walking in the woods, admiring nature, barbecue, swimming in the river, sunbathing on the beach. A standard set, but do not forget that nearby, just 30 km from the base, is the glorious city of Voronezh, where there are attractions and interesting places.

Reviews

According to reviews of the Oblomov camp in Voronezh, tourists like it here, but not all. Complaints are mainly related to garbage, which is sometimes not cleaned up at the right time, with not very friendly staff, whose mood is constantly changing, and the fact that the "wild" vacation bothers pretty quickly.

Well, from the little things, it is worth noting that the "special" room check for the theft of towels, soap and other accessories and the lack of a grocery store are too special. I have to travel to the city, and this is 30 km one way.

Positive feedback is left by true lovers of nature, solitude. In this forest you can forget about the bustle of the city, enjoy the clean air and feel like a piece of the universe.
