Advantages and disadvantages of passbooks

A document such as a passbook gives citizens a sense of security, as it is associated with a reliable state structure. A bank card does not provide the opportunity to see the completed transactions, while the passbook clearly shows the cash flow. Regardless of the type of transactions performed, all entries that are recorded by the operator are reflected in the passbook. Among the shortcomings can be highlighted the inconvenience of using this document. This is due to the fact that to complete the operation, you must personally visit the office of a banking organization and have a passport with you.

Varieties of accounts

Sberbank offers the following types of accounts: deposits, current accounts and card accounts. Cash on deposit can be placed for a strictly defined period. With a certain periodicity, interest will be charged on the balance. Their size directly depends on the specifics of the bank offer.
