Marins Hotel-Park, Sochi: overview, description, rooms and reviews of tourists

The central part of Sochi is a place where many seek to come on a long-awaited vacation. After all, here you can not only get plenty of fun in the waters of the Black Sea and get a great chocolate tan under the rays of the gentle sun. Here you can also visit many excursions and see various interesting places. Some of the guests for their vacation choose in Sochi "Marins Park Hotel" 4 *. It has long established itself as a luxurious, comfortable and welcoming place.

Short story

It housed the Leningrad Hotel, which opened its doors to tourists and guests of the city on November 5 of that year. Only in 2007 did the hotel acquire its modern name.

General Description and Overview

The Marins Hotel-Park (Sochi) occupies a long eleven-story building, which looks very fashionable, stylish and modern both externally and internally. Outside, almost the entire hotel is covered with glass, which gives the building a special shine. It beautifully reflects the sun's rays. The hotel has practically no territory - it stands right next to the road.

The hotel has its own restaurant and lobby bar, a wellness center and a large number of conference rooms are organized. An airport shuttle can be provided upon request. Also near the hotel there is parking for personal vehicles.





In the hotel you can live with your animals, which is quite rare. Moreover, this service is completely free for pets up to 5 kg. If the animal weighs more, then you will have to pay 600 rubles per day for it.

Where is the hotel situated

For those who decide to visit Sochi Central, the Marins Park Hotel 4 * will be an excellent choice due to its very convenient location. The hotel stands in the very center of the city, just a five-minute walk from the beach. Also within walking distance are the Festivalny concert hall, the Art Museum, the Winter Theater, the seaport and the water park.





The city's railway station is approximately 3 kilometers from the hotel. Sochi International Airport is about 26 km away. This will take a maximum of 20 minutes if you go by public or private transport. That is why it will be very convenient for any guest of the city to come to the Marins Park Hotel (Sochi). Hotel address: 2 Morskoy Lane, Sochi, Krasnodar Territory, postal code: 354,000. To contact the hotel, you can use the following numbers: +7 (862) 271-30-00 and +7 (495) 139-10 -52.

How do tourists get to the hotel?

The Marins Park Hotel (Sochi), as mentioned above, is located very close to the airport and railway station. Guests intending to reach the hotel from these public areas can take advantage of the large number of shuttles and buses that go to the Sochi-Magnolia stop. From the station, vacationers should choose the direction towards Adler, and from the international airport - towards the Sochi station.

What rooms will the guests live in

At the Marins Park Hotel (Sochi), tourist rooms are of different categories, there are more than 400 in the building. Among them are simple and improved standard rooms, suites, Studio and Apartments.

In any of the rooms, vacationers will find all the necessary comfortable furniture, beautiful views from the balconies and windows of the city parks and the Black Sea. Single and double beds are equipped with comfortable orthopedic mattresses, soft hypoallergenic pillows and blankets with swan feathers, as well as cotton sheets.





Mandatory attributes of any room are a large TV with the ability to play USB and DVD media and a wide selection of high-quality channels, air conditioning, a telephone with international and long distance communication, high-speed Wi-Fi, a fridge or freezer, a hairdryer, slippers and bathrobes for each guest.

Terms of accommodation

The price of all rooms includes breakfast, which is organized in the hotel restaurant according to the buffet. In addition, dinners can be included in the price. Also the price of accommodation in any room includes laundry and ironing in the hotel laundry, minor repairs of clothes, use of an iron with an ironing board, luggage storage, room service and a visit to the wellness center from 9 am to two in the afternoon.

"Marins Hotel-Park" (Sochi) carries out check-in of guests in rooms from 14-00 on Sochi time. If early check-in is required, then a fee equal to half the cost of the day is taken. Guests who intend to stay at the hotel less than a day will still have to pay the full price of the room, since hourly payment is not provided here.





Guests must check out of the rooms before 12 noon. If an extension is suddenly required, you must inform the reception and accommodation service about this. The requirement will, of course, be satisfied with the availability of rooms. At the same time, the stay is extended up to 14 hours for free, and until 23:59 - for half the cost of the day (with dinner included in the "buffet" system).

Children under 4 years old on extra bed are free of charge with breakfast. Guests over 5 years old can be accommodated in extra beds only in suites for an additional fee.
