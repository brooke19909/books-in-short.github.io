Fishing base "Success": description, photos, reviews

It is known that fishing was once one of the available ways of survival, the ability to feed yourself and your family. Today it is a favorite, pleasing to the soul occupation of millions of people around the world. Of course, the Russians are no exception. We can say with confidence that in almost every family there will certainly be at least one admirer of this fascinating old occupation.

In the Astrakhan region, fishing is an essential part of the culture of the local people. It is simply impossible to live in these parts, famous for fishing, and not to pick up a fishing rod.



Especially popular among Russian fishermen is the Uspekh fishing base created in 2000 on Akhtuba. A webcam installed on the territory captures the leisure of fans of fishing and family vacations among beautiful nature.

Location

The fishing base "Success" is located on the banks of the river. Akhtuba in the Kharabalinsky district of the Astrakhan region and represents a truly unique place both for lovers of fishing and hunting, as well as for family leisure surrounded by beautiful nature, as well as rich opportunities for active recreation on the water. The address of the fishing base "Success": the village of Sasykoli, Kharabalinsky district, Astrakhan region.

Accommodation

The fishing base "Success" on Akhtuba has a rich number of rooms, consisting of several comfortable cottages with different capacities and cozy rooms equipped with necessary amenities. In all accommodation options, the presence of modern comfortable furniture, air conditioning, TVs, microwave, refrigerators, a bathroom with shower is provided.

What is included in the price?

The price of accommodation in the rooms of the fishing base "Success" includes:

  • breakfast and lunch;
  • breakfasts and dinners;
  • boat mooring at the pier;
  • parking.

Nutrition

The fishing base "Success" in the Astrakhan region provides guests with two meals a day in the cafe complex. The menu offers a variety of drinks and dishes. Guests can cook for themselves. At the request of guests in the banquet room organize celebratory or corporate events.





Infrastructure, services

The convenience of guests of the Uspekh fishing base (Kharabalinsky district) is ensured by the presence of a transfer, parking, gazebos, barbecue, billiards, rental boats, boats and fishing tackle. The service of the huntsman is provided. Payment is made in cash or by bank card.

Guest Impressions

Fishing on Akhtuba is a favorite hobby for people of various social groups, professions, interests. Fans of her name, although not as productive in terms of the number of fish caught, as in the Delta region, but, according to many, it is more interesting here in terms of the size of specimens supposed to be captured. According to the authors of numerous reviews: those who manage to have patience, the necessary gear and jig-spinning and trolling skills , fishing on Akhtuba can really give their dream trophies.

Fishermen urge all guests to release caught and undamaged fish back into the river. In recent years, more and more amateur athletes have been joining this noble cause of preserving the river fauna. Many transmit their photographs with the captured trophies for the local museum - specimens of zander, catfish, asp, common carp.

Akhtuba and the Lower Volga are considered by many to be unique places both for true anglers and for lovers of active water recreation. The guests of the fishing base call the first visit to this region by the degree of emotional perception comparable only to the first trip to Africa. Once fishing here, many tend to come back here again.





Vacationers call the nature of the Astrakhan region truly charming. The mood is promoted by the wonderful local climate, characterized by an abundance of sunny days.

It is known that in recent years there has been a tendency to come to these places for recreation with the whole family. According to reviews, the base "Success" offers many accommodation options, from which you can easily choose the one suitable for comfort and affordable cost. Many guests note that the quality of accommodation and the services provided allows you not to be distracted by domestic issues.

The guests here are directly involved in what they came to Akhtuba for - rest, hunting and fishing. According to reviews, on the territory of the base "Success" there are shops, cafes. Boat and boat rental services are also available. As the reviews of regular visitors to the base assure, there is everything that you may need while fishing and relaxing.
