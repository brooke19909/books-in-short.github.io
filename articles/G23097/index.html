The village of Mirny in different regions of Russia

Studying the map of Russia, among more than 170 thousand settlements, one can repeatedly stumble upon the village of Mirny. Almost anywhere in the country there is a place with that name. Of course, it is not original. But it‚Äôs immediately clear how the name came about.

Why did the villages call the peace word

There are 2 reasons why villages were given such a friendly name:

  1. Perhaps the village was built in the XIX-XX centuries by the combined forces of all residents. As they said before, the whole world. So they gave the village built by joint efforts the name Mirny.
  2. Another option - the village was restored or built after 1945. The horrors of war left an imprint on the consciousness of people who wanted to see around them only joyful, peaceful, flourishing.

Mirny settlement on the map of Russia

Among the many diverse villages with a peace-loving name, there are those that have become famous, despite the small territory and small number of inhabitants. It is worth telling more about such settlements.



Peaceful in the Arkhangelsk region

On the shore of Lake Plesetsk in the Arkhangelsk region is the village of Mirny, better known as Plesetsk.

Probably, only the lazy did not hear about the Russian spaceport . It is from here that unmanned spacecraft soar into space, and scientific, applied, and defense activities are carried out outside the earth. In the 70-80 years of the last century, almost half of all space launches were made from the cosmodrome. Therefore, in 1966, Mirny received city status. The village is a closed territory.

About 32 thousand people live in the village.

Near Kazan



In the Volga region of Kazan, there is another village called Mirny, and its name is fully consistent with the nature of the inhabitants. Indeed, literally in the neighborhood in the village of Mirny (Kazan) is the Tynichlyk mosque and the Orthodox Church of the Blessed Virgin Mary.

What is remarkable Kazan Mirny?

  1. The annual republican national holiday Sabantuy, which is visited by tens of thousands of people, takes place in the Birch Grove Park. During the celebration, athletes compete, creative teams perform, masters demonstrate their skills and teach those who wish ancient crafts. Dmitry Medvedev, being the president of Russia, also attended this big holiday.
  2. The village hosts an international festival of Turkic theaters.
  3. At the sports base located here, not only republican, but also all-Russian competitions are held.

The population of the village is about 4 thousand people.



In the Samara region

45 km from Samara, thanks to a large oil field discovered in the 50s of the last century, the village of Mirny appeared on the map, which then surpassed even Baku, the recognized leader in the oil field, in terms of the amount of hydrocarbons produced. In the history of the village there were terrible fires and heroic labor, estimated by the state of victory.

Now in the village of Mirny, Samara Region, about 7 thousand people live, for whom all the necessary infrastructure has been established. There is a comprehensive and music school for children, a sports complex, shops, pharmacies, and regular transport links with Samara.

In Yakutia

The diamond capital is called the village of Mirny in Yakutia, in the Far North.

The name of the village, which later became a city, was given by the Mir kimberlite pipe, discovered in 1955. The richest diamond deposit caused the development of this desert region. Now in the village there are several factories for the extraction and processing of diamonds. It even built its own airport. Unique is the kimberlite museum opened in the village.

About 37 thousand people live in northern Mirny.

Peaceful in Crimea

Probably in the Crimea is the friendliest village of Mirny. Yevpatoriya - the famous children's resort, which is located next door: 25 km.



The sea, sandy beaches, healing steppe air, peace and quiet - this is how you can characterize this settlement. In summer, the village becomes crowded, and in winter only local residents remain: about 4 thousand people.

Nearby is a lot of interesting things:

  • Donuzlav lake, which was chosen by fans of surfing;
  • wind power station;
  • dolphinarium;
  • therapeutic mud lake Oyburskoe.

In the village, the Center for Long-Range Space Communications is based. Its huge antennas are visible from afar. Thanks to these installations, communication was maintained with Yuri Gagarin, the work of the lunar rovers was coordinated, and broadcasts from the Olympics-80 were broadcast throughout the country.

And other Peace

In the Volgograd and Rostov regions, in the Stavropol Territory, Ulyanovsk and Bryansk Regions, Krasnodar Territory, as well as near Saratov and Chelyabinsk, you can find the village of Mirny. But that is not all. Near the capital, in Ramensky, Noginsky district and near Serpukhov, there are small settlements with the same name.

So, when you are going to send a letter to Mirny, you must carefully indicate in which area and region it is located in order to avoid confusion.
