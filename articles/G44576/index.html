Which smartphone with the best camera? Top 10

The year 2016 is drawing to a close, which means that most of the flagship presentations of various IT companies have already died down. Apple introduced the iPhone 7 and 7 Plus, Samsung - the long-suffering Galaxy Note 7, LG showed the V20, Meizu presented the Pro 6.

And if there are no more new flagships that can excite the minds of the public and specialized publications, then we can distinguish leaders in different categories from all the new products in 2016. For example, in cameras. Shooting modules over the past two years have stepped up significantly in color rendering, image quality and smoothness. Therefore, you can try to answer the question: "What is the best camera on a smartphone in 2016?"

TOP 10 best camera phones 2016

In the sections that follow, ten smartphones of this and last year, which can rightfully be called flagship camera phones, will be highlighted, their main features and advantages over competitors will be highlighted. Opinion may not coincide with generally accepted, because people evaluate the quality of photos and videos. And how many people, so many opinions. So, we present to your attention the top 10 smartphones with the best camera in 2016.

Tenth place

Sony Xperia Z5. Despite the fact that the line of flagship Z smartphones from Sony has already ceased to exist, last year's flagship Z5 still shows a good photo and video shooting result in picture quality that is not much inferior to what the 2016 flagships can offer. This can be confirmed by the high ratings of the profile publication DXOMark. The numbers speak for themselves: the auto focus time is 0.03 seconds, and this is on a 23-megapixel matrix! We can say that the Xperia Z5 is a smartphone with the best camera, however, in 2015.

Ninth place

Meizu MX5. The MX line, once considered flagship, lost this title to the Pro-series, and, accordingly, the technical equipment became slightly worse. Nevertheless, the Meizu MX6 with its Sony IMX386 camera module with excellent software optimization is still able to deliver excellent photo and video quality in all conditions.
