Name history

Previously, this shelter for tired tourists was called Koh Phangan. He is now so often called. He owes his name to the word "Ko", which is translated into the familiar language as "island", and another word from the vocabulary of the inhabitants of the southern provinces - "ngan", this word means "sand barrier" or "strip".

A telling name, because the coastal part of the resort is replete with many sandy islands and sediments. They protect the island from waves of aggressive times and strong monsoons.

Geographical position

The small islet of Phangan (Thailand) is located in Surat Thani - one of the provinces of the country. It is separated from the capital by 500 km. It is part of the famous Samui archipelago, which includes about 40 islands. For a small country like Thailand, this is a lot. Koh Phangan is located at a distance of 15 km from the main island of the Samui archipelago . The main port of the town is Thong Sala. He is also part of the capital.

History tour

We rarely think about the past of the settlements we visit while traveling. Meanwhile, some of them store in the caskets of their cities whole placers of legends and interesting stories.



Phangan (Thailand) is notable for its archaeological site. It was they who confirmed his solid age. As it turned out, the existence of Phangan Island dates back 2000 years. In the 19th century, the Chinese began to emigrate, due to which population growth was formed. At the very beginning of the 20th century, about 300 families lived here. Lovely, kind Thais did not pretend to European charms. They quietly enjoyed the eternal sun and the generous gifts of the sea. Mainly engaged in agriculture and fishing. But since the mid-80s, the situation has changed dramatically. Thailand decided to go into the tourism business. And crowds of tourists from all over the world poured into this paradise country. Phangan (Thailand): how to get



If you are in the country of eternal summer for the first time, do not worry: in Thailand it is impossible to get lost. It is so compact and intuitive. In the case when your goal is the island of Phangan, you have to get to it on the mess. Of course, you will fly from Europe by plane. It remains only to choose the primary destination of your destination. There are several options.

  1. Bangkok. It is located near Phangan. However, in this cozy country everything is at hand. A bus runs from Bangkok to the island. Such pleasure will cost from 500 to 1500 baht. There are both tourist and local buses to Phangan. The first of them leave from the street, which is designed specifically for guests of the country, the second - from the usual bus station. You cannot give preference to any one type of transport. Both are equally comfortable and suitable for movement.
  2. Surat Thani. This place also has a local airport called Surat Thani Airport. You can get to Phangan by traveling by bus, and then by ferry. The trip will take about 2.5 hours.
  3. Samui Here you will be met by the airport of the same name. From this city, getting to Phangan is easy. Ferries depart in this direction several times a day. The fare is from 5 to 8 baht. Travel time is about 40 minutes.

Phangan beaches



Of course, many of us are attracted to holidays in Thailand. The beaches of Phangan Island represent a whole kaleidoscope of popular and not-so-small places where you can swim in the sea waves, meditate, lying on the beach and absorbing in every cell the generous warmth of the Asian sun.

What kind of beaches does it make sense to visit when you arrive in Koh Phangan (Thailand)?

  1. Malibu. This place was named for the pleasant to the touch white sand. There are swings on the beach right under the palm tree.
  2. Mae Had. Another place that can not be ignored. It is characterized by crystal water and coral reefs. Decoration can be called an alley located in the middle of the beach. It consists of 2 rows of trees on which flowers stunning in their beauty grow.
  3. Coma. The island, which will be a discovery for those who are engaged in diving. You can get on it with Mae Had, going through a sand spit. Here is an unforgettable snorkeling (scuba diving). There you can admire a huge variety of marine life.
  4. Haad Salad. One of the most beautiful beaches that Phangan boasts. Thailand is famous for such places where it takes your breath away from the miraculous charms of the surrounding nature. Nearby is a village with developed infrastructure. So here you will not be divorced from civilization.
  5. Had Yao. This is a vacation for those who want to be alone, who are tired of the daily crazy rhythm of life. There are no Thai noisy markets, no cafes, but there is an amazing opportunity to enjoy the sunsets and enjoy the ringing silence.
  6. Had Reed. If you want to look here, you should remember that the road to it is really dangerous, many steep turns will appear on your way. Therefore, it is better to give preference to a taxi. Had Rin is famous for its amazing Full moon party. This is a great place if you are attracted to a club holiday. But there are all conditions for doing nothing on the beach.
