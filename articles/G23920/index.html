Club "Mask" in Lipetsk: address, menu, reviews

Youth clubs have long been popular holiday destinations in different cities of the country. Here guests can relax, dance and eat. The Mask Club in Lipetsk updates its show programs every year and brings original highlights to the menu.

Where is it located and how does it work?

The institution is located on the street. Sovetskaya, 4 on the ground floor of the building. Mask Club in Lipetsk occupies a small room, but it is designed so that guests feel comfortable and spacious.





The institution works daily. From Monday to Wednesday, guests are expected from 11.00 to 21.00, on Thursday the club closes at 03.00. On Friday and Saturday, customers can visit it from 11.00 to 05.00 and on Sunday the institution is open from 16.00 to 03.00.

Description

Club "Mask" in Lipetsk does not differ in the original interior. The design is dominated by warm colors. A fairly spacious dance floor makes it possible to dance almost all guests at the same time.





Recently, this institution has received the status of the City Center. Such a reward indicates that here you can not only "light up" on the dance floor, but also spend a quiet evening in a cozy restaurant and even organize business negotiations in the VIP-hall.

In the karaoke club "Mask" (Lipetsk), vocal lovers can feel like stars of show business on stage. A modern system for singing with texts of almost all Russian and foreign hits is installed here.

Two restaurants will be able to boast of their menu of Eastern, European and Caucasian cuisines. In the halls it is possible to hold celebrations and corporate parties.

Menu and drinks

The Mask club in Lipetsk has an excellent assortment of beer snacks and snacks. Almost all of them are cooked by the chef. Here you can order pizza with various fillings or several types of sushi.





Interesting seafood snacks or hot steaks can spoil the taste of any guest. The Mask club in Lipetsk also has exquisite European dishes on the menu. For their preparation, modern cooking technologies and exotic products are used.

The club's restaurants serve many varieties of meat and fish dishes. And the amazing desserts of our own baking will enchant with the delicate taste of each guest who ordered them.

Entertainment

Every weekend at the Mask night club in Lipetsk, an interesting thematic program takes place or a guest DJ performs. Often the leaders of the institution throw foam parties or costume dances here in honor of different eras of humanity.





The club regularly hosts beauty contests where they choose the most beautiful and artistic client. According to the results, the winner receives prizes from the establishment and good discounts on the menus of both restaurants.

Artists of various genres are invited to the night club. They spend interesting evenings with visitors, speaking and arranging all kinds of competitions. On New Year's Eve, a grandiose show program is organized in which celebrities and, of course, Santa Claus and Snegurochka take part.

Reviews about the institution

Comments on the work of the nightclub are left by customers who have already visited it. Online reviews about the institution a large number. Guests mainly note that recently the club has noticeably revived and the work of the maintenance staff has become better.

Regarding entertainment, vacationers have virtually no complaints. More often they are unhappy with the menu in the institution and the lack of free space on the dance floor on weekends. Clients indicate that spirits and a variety of cocktails are of normal quality, if they are made by the rules of the bartender. Often, he clearly does not add the declared alcohol in the portion and thus deceives the vacationers.

Guests are also outraged by the fact that the administration of the Mask club in Lipetsk allows visitors to go to the karaoke room, like to a smoking room. Because of this, all the tablecloths on the table are burned in it and it stinks of cigarette smoke very much. The karaoke installation has been updated and has a great sound. But constantly coming smokers do not allow guests to relax and sing without interference.

Visitors note that security passes everyone here without face-checking. Therefore, after a few hours of walking on the floor, you can see pretty drunk customers who can no longer stand on their feet. The same guards in the middle of the entertainment program begin to display such visitors.

A lot of negative reviews about the boorish attitude of the staff towards the club guests. Customers claim that the waiters inattentively listen to orders and after comments begin to openly be rude to them. The administration practically does not respond to complaints of this type.

And also visitors warn that you need to carefully read the check at the time of payment. Often incomprehensible amounts appear in it that are not listed on the menu. Clients note that the club staff brazenly deceives the guests and tries to cheat.
