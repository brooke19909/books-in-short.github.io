How to make flowers from paper: schemes, templates, master classes for beginners

To create any beautiful card you will need the ability to make flowers from different types of materials. There are simple options that even children can handle, but there are complex ones that consist of several stages of manufacturing. Any material can be used - fabric and satin ribbons, felt sheets. How to make flowers out of paper so that they turn out beautiful, neat and effective, read on in the article.

The technique for making each type of crafts is different. Consider the easiest ways for those masters who only try their hand at this art form. Flowers made of paper for beginners are made according to schemes and patterns. Striped Sunflowers

Such light flowers from paper can be made even by preschoolers. Each flower is made up of thinly cut strips of yellow double-sided paper. They add up with the intersection at a central point, like a sun. You need to distribute the petals at an equal distance, the more there are, the more magnificent the sunflower will be. A black circle is glued in the middle. Marker draws vertical and horizontal lines on it. These are sunflower seeds.

Similarly, work is being done on the remaining colors. Then, cocktail tubes are installed that act as stems. It remains to draw a vase on a white cardboard and cut it along the contours. It is not glued completely, but only along the edges. It turns out the effect of a three-dimensional image. You already know how to make flowers out of paper in an easy way; now we will consider more complex options.

Hyacinths

A very original bouquet can be made of double-sided colored paper in bright colors. Sticks for stems can be made from a variety of materials - plastic tubes, wooden skewers or simple straight twigs. They will not be visible, since they are wrapped in green paper. How to make flowers from paper?

Stripe Patterns

In the photo below, you can see several types of cutting strips of paper. You can use double-sided color or corrugated paper. How to make flowers from paper? First cut a strip of paper. The width is taken different. The wider the strip, the longer the petals. The longer the strip, the more magnificent the flower is.

The shape of the petal depends on the method of cutting the edges with scissors. You can make them round, wide or narrow, or you can cut into sharp corners. After preparing the strips, take a base, for example, a toothpick, and begin to wind the paper around it. The movements are carried out with tension so that all the turns are tightly in contact with each other. Such flowers from paper according to schemes (patterns) can be made from corrugated paper with various centers. Looks beautiful flowers made up of several shades. To create such a multi-color bouquet, you need to glue paper of a different color after the strip of one color ends. After attaching, winding continues again. So you can add several shades of the same color. For example, start with white, add pink, then raspberry, finish with maroon. Aster

A master class of large flowers made of paper will help to create a beautiful magnificent craft, as in the photo. Make it from crepe paper. For the middle, you need to take a narrow bright yellow strip and cut it as often as possible so that the "noodles" are thin and thick. Wrap it on a thin stick or toothpick. The edge is attached with PVA glue to the last turn. Then the same manipulation is also made with a yellow stripe, only the width is taken more. It turns out a magnificent middle.

Further work continues on the flower itself. Crepe paper flower petals are long and pointed. Therefore, a strip of cream paper is cut into long and sharp corners. If desired, each can be rounded with a round pencil. It is applied to the petal and lightly pressed with two fingers. Crepe paper flowers will turn out to be large if you make each of several glued strips. Since a real aster in nature is large, then a flower from paper should be made magnificent.

Two-tone flower

To make such a beautiful flower out of paper, read the step-by-step instructions later in the article. You will need double-sided white and purple paper.

Instruction:

Step number 1. First, the middle is made of chopped "noodles" thin strips.

Step number 2. Another part is glued onto the prepared cylinder, only it is already purple. Make it loops. To do this, cut a wide strip of paper, fold in half and finely cut. Then you do not need to deploy the part, and you need to double it in the middle.

Step number 3. When the base for the petals is prepared, work continues on the manufacture of the flower itself. Petals are cut according to the pattern. Purple details make a couple of millimeters more white.

Step number 4. When pairs of petals are prepared, they begin to attach to the central cylinder from twisted strips. Place them around the perimeter. You can place them side by side, but you can overlap. It all depends on the desire of the master. The flower turns out to be bright and very effective, although it is not difficult to make it.
