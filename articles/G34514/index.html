Hotel Valsami Hotel Apartments 4 * (Greece, Rhodes): description, reviews

Holidays on the island of Rhodes - this is just a great opportunity to make your vacation bright and memorable. This famous Greek resort has literally everything that is necessary for a pleasant pastime: comfortable beaches, a gentle sea, interesting sights. Well, of course, there are comfortable hotels in Rhodes where you can rent inexpensive accommodation. For example, Valsami Hotel Apartments 4 * offers excellent service to tourists .

Location Features

The Valsami Apartments complex is located on an island in the small village of Kremasti. This southern village is very popular, including among Russian vacationers. Kremasti is located about halfway between the airport of Rhodes and its capital. Tourists arriving to the island by plane take no more than 10-15 minutes to get to the hotel. The distance from the airport to Kremasti is only about 8 km.

Vacationers arriving by plane can get to the hotel by bus or taxi. Also, if you wish, you can order a transfer in the hotel itself.





General description of the hotel

It is a hotel V alsami Hotel Apartments 4 * complex, consisting of several buildings of original light architecture (Greek style) . Outwardly, this hotel center looks, according to tourists, very attractive. The facades of its buildings are painted in snow white and pink, perfectly harmonizing with each other colors. The tiles covering the hotel courtyard and walkways have a beautiful light peach hue. Actually, the hotel territory itself is a real blooming southern garden. In the courtyard of the hotel there are flowerbeds everywhere and exotic trees and an arnica bush planted .

The reception at this hotel is open around the clock, which is certainly very convenient for guests. According to many tourists, most of all the hotel Valsami Hotel Apartments 4 * is suitable for lovers of beach holidays. The coastline is located just 150 meters from it.

Number of rooms

In total, the Valsami Hotel Apartments 4 * hotel offers Kremasti 56 comfortable rooms. All rooms in the hotel are equipped with the necessary furniture and appliances to create a comfortable environment. The rooms have:

  • TV;

  • air conditioning;

  • refrigerator;

  • a kitchenette;

  • kettle;

  • radio;

  • telephone.





The television at this hotel is satellite. Each room has a well-equipped shower room. For your convenience, the bathroom comes with toiletries and a hairdryer.

All hotel rooms are class apartments. Valsami Hotel 4 * family apartment (family rooms) and rooms for friends with individual beds are very popular with tourists . Among other things? the hotel offers tourists who have chosen Rhodes as their vacation destination, and specially equipped apartments for people with disabilities.

Room Reviews

In general, tourists respond well to the rooms rented at Valsami Hotel Apartments 4 * (Rhodes) . The main advantage of the hotel apartments guests consider their large area. Appliances in rooms of all categories, judging by the reviews of tourists, are working properly. Thanks to the presence of air conditioning, the air temperature in the rooms is always comfortable.





Unfortunately, there are not very good reviews about cleaning in the apartments of this hotel. Maids at the hotel wipe the dust and mop the floors quite often, but not particularly carefully. Bedding may also not be too new. The same goes for towels in the shower. They look washed.

Infrastructure

Of course, guests can enjoy all sorts of amenities on the territory of the hotel itself. The Valsami Hotel 4 * hotel with its residents offers the following services:

  • car and bicycle rental;

  • room service;

  • currency exchange at a pretty favorable rate;

  • photocopying, faxing.





On-site facilities include a library, a sun terrace and an outdoor pool. Safety deposit boxes and luggage storage are available at the front desk. For children, the hotel provides a playroom and a specially equipped playground. Lovers of outdoor activities can visit the billiards room at this hotel.

Hotel guests can buy souvenirs in memory of visiting the island of Rhodes right on its territory. The hotel has a small gift shop. The hotel also has a mini market.

At the same time, according to most former residents of the hotel, only low room rates save the situation. The cost of renting rooms in this hotel is slightly lower than in many other four-star Rhodes. According to most tourists, this hotel is well suited only for lowly vacationers.

As for the services themselves, in most cases vacationers speak about them well. The pool, for example, in the hotel is quite large and, according to many tourists, is convenient for swimming. Hotel staff often wash the pool, and therefore the water in it is always clean. Its temperature is also comfortable for taking water procedures.

Dining at the hotel

The hotel also has a snack bar and restaurant. Also, guests can optionally visit the bar.

As rooms at this hotel have kitchenettes, most residents prefer to cook their own meals. Products in stores located next to the hotel are usually sold fresh and at not too high cost. You can buy meat, vegetables, etc., for example, in the inexpensive Anastasia supermarket.

Guests can enjoy a variety of drinks and snacks at the bar. Reviews about this place on the web are not bad. The hotel's restaurant serves Mediterranean cuisine.

Beach Hotels

The coastline from this hotel can be reached in just three minutes. The beach of the hotel, most of the guests find it convenient enough for sunbathing . The coastline in this place of the sea is covered with soft sand mixed with fine gray pebbles . If desired, you can rent a sun lounger on Kremasti beach . It does not cost too much - about 3 euros.

Many tourists consider the Kremasti beach to be a disadvantage because it is located in that part of the island where strong winds often blow. Therefore, the sea here is usually not too calm. Swimming at Kremasti beach is not particularly convenient. But it is very popular among fans of all kinds of water sports.

Service Reviews

About the attitude of the hotel staff to their guests on the Web, there are basically only good reviews. Hotel staff behave politely. Administrators and maids are trying to solve any problems that arise with residents promptly.





Cost and living conditions

Rooms at this hotel are for non-smokers only. Pets are welcome here, but only for a fee (50 euros). Breakfast is not included in the room rates at this hotel. You need to pay for them separately (about 6 euros per day per person). If necessary, tourists can book an extra bed in the hotel room. In this case, the new tenant is provided with a comfortable folding bed. The cost of her rental is 20 euros per day.

Actually, the prices for accommodation in this hotel themselves, as already mentioned, are not too high. The cost of renting rooms at the Valsami Hotel Apartments 4 * (Rhodes, Kremasti) starts from 45 euros per person per day.

Available Attractions

There are many interesting places near Valsami Apartments that are worth a visit to its residents. For example, such sights of the island as the Acropolis of Rhodes, Filerimos can be very interesting .

The Acropolis of Rhodes is located approximately 5 kilometers from the hotel. This attraction on the island is perhaps the most interesting and popular with tourists. The local acropolis is known primarily for the ruins of the Apollo Pythian temple and the stadium built in the II century BC. e. Also, many tourists are attracted here by the marble amphitheater of oratory.





Filerimos is a mountain located in the north of the island of Rhodes. Its height is 276 m above sea level. This place is very beautiful, and here, among other things, you can see the ancient monastery.
