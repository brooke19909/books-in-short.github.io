Plan: how to sew a sundress for the summer

Even though the summer has passed, the demand for light things has not diminished. Of course, because the south is always warm, which means that the relevance of summer dresses and sundresses is very high. For those who know how to create clothes on their own, this is another reason to get a sewing machine and make a real masterpiece for the beach. Those who are wondering about how to sew a sundress for the summer, it is time to stock up on patterns and threads.





Of course, you can seek help from a professional seamstress. But an outfit made independently is not only clothes, but also pride in oneself. Sewing any thing begins with taking measurements. For a sundress, they need different ones, depending on the model. If the attractive style is fitted, then you need to take measurements of the waist, if free, then no. Breast volume should be measured in any case. Whatever the style, it must be emphasized. Before you sew a sundress for the summer, you need to decide on the fabric. It is better if it is light, airy. But then it is worth remembering that a lining is also required. She also needs to take measurements. And, of course, a pattern will be required. You can find it in the magazine for sewing or in the bins of relatives. Each pattern can be "customized" for yourself or slightly adjust the style. According to your taste and desire.

The pattern of a summer sundress on the floor is very simple. It is important to take not only all the basic measurements, but also determine the length of the future outfit. It starts either from the line of the chest, or from the shoulder. All elements of the sarafan are sewn in turn. The straps are either made separately, or are included in the drawing immediately. Then the corset pattern for the outfit should be with them. If not, they can be done separately. This will help reduce the number of stitches.



Before you sew a sundress for the summer, you need to decide on the method of fastening it. There are no problems with buttons, just like with Velcro. But a sundress with a zipper is better to think about right away. Ideally, if it is secret, somewhere on the side. This is useful when fastening in the future.

Sundress pattern is free in many magazines. It will not be possible to print it, but transferring it to graph paper step by step is easy. In addition, you can simply draw your own version with your own parameters. In the last stage, all decorative elements are sewn: ruffles, bows, ribbons. It is advisable to have a sewing machine with an overlock, which will help to hide all the seams securely. In addition, all patterns are indented. It is necessary so that the sundress does not creep along the edges, the threads do not stick out. If desired, the straps can be replaced with a braid, on which the entire outfit will subsequently be held.





Before you sew a sundress for the summer, choose a model, style, fabric and suitable decorative elements. Finding the right pattern is easy. All elements of the outfit are sewn first "on rough" with simple threads with your hands. Tailoring by tailor pins is allowed until the final fitting. The golden rule of home seamstresses: more - no less, you can trim. Indentation should not be too large or too small. About 3-5 mm. This is enough to ultimately fit the outfit perfectly.
