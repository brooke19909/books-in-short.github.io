Hotel "Plaza" (Vityazevo): description, photos, reviews

Vityazevo over the past few years has evolved from a nondescript village into a beautiful resort town. Here the infrastructure is developing successfully and new modern hotels are being built. The Green Plaza Hotel in Vityazevo opened in 2012 and has since been hosting guests at any time of the year.

Where is

The hotel is located a 10-minute drive from Anapa Airport. The road from the railway station takes no more than 15 minutes. Directly the hotel "Plaza" in Vityazevo is located on the Passage, Beautiful, 3.





The hotel is located in proximity to the promenade "Paralia", where the whole cultural life of the village is concentrated. The complex is located on the first coastline.

The hotel provides transfers for a fee from the place of arrival of guests in the city to the complex.

Description

The hotel occupies a small territory. The hotel has 5 floors and an unusual angular shape. The building has two separate entrances. On the ground floor there is a reception desk. Staff work here around the clock.

All common areas are made in an aristocratic classic style. A lot of shine and fabrics in the decor. The lobby bar has simple, classic furniture.





There is absolutely no place for walking on the territory, there is no playground. This is due to the location of the hotel and the lack of additional land around the building. One of the entrances to the Plaza Hotel in Vityazevo is located directly from the street that overlooks the embankment.

Terms of Accommodation

The complex offers rooms with different levels of comfort and, accordingly, differing in cost:

  • double and triple rooms - double and single beds, bedside tables, table and chair, wardrobe, mirror, TV, refrigerator, air conditioning, telephone, safe, shower and toilet, balcony with furniture;
  • double and triple junior suites - double bed and folding sofa, carpet, safe, TV, wardrobe, desk and chair, refrigerator, telephone, air conditioning, hairdryer, Internet, shower, bathroom, balcony with furniture;
  • Suite - a two-room suite, a double bed and a large sofa bed, each room has its own bathroom and shower, TV, air conditioning, wardrobe, desk and chair, safe, hairdryer, furnished balcony, Internet.




In the "Plaza" (Vityazevo) there are identical rooms for arrangement and decoration, only without balconies. In total, the hotel has 30 rooms.

Food in the complex

On the ground floor of the Plaza Hotel in Vityazevo, there is a spacious cafe-pizzeria. It prepares dishes according to Italian recipes using original products from this country.

The cafe has a summer terrace, which is also decorated in the style of Italy. The pizzeria is famous not only for everything Vityazevo, but also considered one of the best in Anapa. Here, customers are offered a menu of European cuisine:

  • lasagna;
  • fettuccine paste;
  • pasta bolognese;
  • caprese;
  • pizza;
  • risotto;
  • salad "Nicoise" and others.




In the lobby of the Plaza Hotel in Vityazevo there is a lobby bar. In it, on comfortable classic armchairs at the tables, guests can enjoy aromatic coffee or tea. There is a modern coffee machine and several types of drinks are presented. The assortment of tea - about 10 types - impresses the guests of the complex.

A variety of desserts and pastries are also on display at special racks. Meals in the lobby bar are included in the room rate.

Pool and beach

The hotel has a small pool. The water in it is heated and passes through multi-level purification filters. In the pool there is a special ladder for descent, small fountains and a hydromassage device.

A special anti-slip tile is laid around. Sun loungers and sun protection umbrellas are installed in this area. There is also an outdoor shower for washing your feet before entering the pool.





The city beach is 300 meters from Green Plaza Hotel. Hotel "Green Plaza" in Vityazevo is located on the first coastline, so the road to the sea takes no more than 10 minutes.

It has a sandy beach. There are changing cabins on it. You can rent sun loungers and parasols. A variety of water activities operate on the shore:

  • slides;
  • riding a banana and a bun;
  • trampolines;
  • shooting gallery.

The beach has small cafes and bars. You can grab a bite to eat and enjoy cold drinks.

There are souvenir and fresh fruit stalls on the way to the beach. Here you can also purchase the necessary goods for a comfortable stay.

Entertainment

In the immediate vicinity of the complex are all kinds of centers for recreation. Here you can visit the dolphinarium and the aquarium. Vivid show programs with animals will not leave indifferent both children and adults.

An amusement park is located in the area. Visitors can ride on modern carousels, get a boost of emotions in a variety of attractions. In the evening, the park is lit by thousands of lights and bulbs, so it can be seen from afar.

Tourists love to walk along the promenade with its many cafes and restaurants. They can have a delicious dinner, enjoy live music, dance. In the evening and at night, clubs and discos open. Tourists can attend a foam party.

Additional services

At the Plaza Hotel in the village of Vityazevo, vacationers have the opportunity to take advantage of:

  • laundry service (extra charge);
  • excursions (you need to arrange in advance at the reception);
  • transfer (order at the time of booking);
  • ordering air and railway tickets;
  • taxi call;
  • car parking.

Hotel workers are trained to provide first aid in various situations.





Upon request, guests are provided with an ironing board and an iron. Room service from the pizzeria is also available.

Near the hotel there is a sanatorium "Dune". In it, guests of the complex can undergo medical and preventive procedures for an additional fee. Aquamarine Spa Hotel is also located in the area. It is possible to undergo a comprehensive examination and sign up for wellness events. These services are paid separately.

Throughout the hotel complex, guests can use free internet. Its speed and quality are sufficient to satisfy workers and personal needs.

Hotel Plaza in Vityazevo: reviews

On the Internet on various sites there are a lot of comments about the work of the complex. Mostly visitors are satisfied with the service and note the friendliness of the staff. Reception staff deserve special attention. According to hotel guests, they are always smiling and welcoming.

Regarding the arrangement of rooms, vacationers are not so optimistic. They note in their comments that the rooms have a lot of dust and furniture has numerous chips. There are also many negative reviews about the cleanliness of the carpet.

Vacationers are unhappy with the noise that comes daily from the territory of a neighboring hotel. They include very loud music and can be heard until 23:00. Thus, in the room you have to constantly keep the windows closed.





At the expense of the pool, guests have practically no complaints, except for its small size and the lack of a zone for bathing children. The beach, according to tourists, is a 20-minute walk, not the declared 10. They also say that often on the shore in the morning there is no garbage collected.

Tourists are surprised that an institution of this level did not include the cost of breakfast in accommodation, as is done in other hotels. According to them, the food in the restaurant is delicious, but only the prices on the menu are quite high.

Also, vacationers are surprised that instead of one double bed in the rooms two twin beds move. In this case, the couple is not very comfortable to rest because of the seam in the middle.

Also, guests are outraged by the fact that you can not bring your own food and drinks to the pool. According to their comments, it is clear that the fruits bought had to be eaten in the rooms. But there are also positive aspects - the hotel often holds promotions with good discounts on accommodation in any season. Also, vacationers note that such a situation with comfort in the rooms is not everywhere. The main thing is to competently book a room with windows that do not overlook the neighboring hotel. In this case, guests can sleep in silence.
