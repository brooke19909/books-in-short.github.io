Hotel Club Elephant Almyros Natura 4 * (Greece, Corfu): overview, description, rooms and reviews

The total length of the coastline of. Corfu is 217 km. Its northern coast, originating from the bay of St. Catherine, is famous for its sandy white beaches and crystal clear sea. It is a picturesque coastal plain on which vineyards and almond trees grow in abundance. Along the beachfront of northern Corfu, many huge hotel complexes and small cozy hotels have been built. Most of these recreation centers have earned just excellent reviews from tourists. For example, it is believed that very good rooms can be rented at Club Elephant Almyros Natura 4 *.

Where is the hotel situated

This comfortable hotel is located directly on the Ionian coast, 40 km from Corfu. From all sides , Club Elephant Almyros Natura 4 * hotel is surrounded by picturesque green fields, and hills overgrown with southern vegetation flaunt it. The nearest town, Acharavi, is about 2 km from the hotel. From the airport, located near the city of Corfu, the easiest way for tourists to get to the hotel, of course, is by taxi. Also, in most cases, tour operators provide vacationers who have bought permits to Corfu with accommodation in this hotel, a shuttle bus.

Beach Hotel Reviews

The sea, as already mentioned, is in the immediate vicinity of the Club Elephant Almyros Natura 4 * hotel . Guests can get to the beach directly from the complex. Here, among other things, hotel residents have the opportunity to use sun loungers and umbrellas. It costs them to rent is not particularly expensive - about 2.5 euros.





The beach of the hotel is covered with small pebbles (similar to coarse sand), on which it is very convenient to sunbathe. If you want to walk along the coastline at the Hotel Club Elephant Almyros Natura 4 * you can even without shale. A small pebble beach is what, among other things, this hotel has earned good reviews. Sunbathing in fine sand, of course, is a little more convenient than pebbles. However, in this case, tourists are sometimes forced to experience certain inconveniences. After all, sand has the property of cramming literally everywhere and getting rid of it is often very difficult.

The water in the sea on the beach of the Club Elephant Almyros Natura (Acharavi) hotel , with fishing reviews by its guests, is simply wonderful - always clean and warm. However, the northern coast of Corfu itself, unfortunately, is a pretty windy place. Therefore, the sea is never particularly calm on the beach of the hotel. For tourists who know how to swim well, the hotel's beach is a great place to relax. For children, swimming here may not be too convenient. But waves at sea in this place are, of course, not always. Actually, the entrance to the water on the beach of the hotel is not particularly gentle. The depth of the sea increases quite sharply.

Akhalavi village

There are no shops or cafes in the immediate vicinity of the hotel, since it is, in fact, located in a field. However, guests of the Club Elephant Almyros Natura 4 * hotel, if necessary, at any time can take advantage of the amenities offered to guests of the island by the village of Acharavi. To reach this settlement, if desired, you can even just walk. In this case, the road will take about 30 minutes. Also, many tourists go to Acharavi from the hotel on rented bicycles. Using this technique, you can literally get to the village in 5-10 minutes.

Actually in Acharavi itself there are many souvenir shops, cafes and restaurants. The residents of this village welcome the guests very friendly. If necessary, in Acharavi you can not only try delicious local dishes or purchase beautiful trinkets to commemorate a visit to Corfu, but also rent a car. Also, many tourists visit the water park in this village. It is located on the territory of the Gelina Village hotel .

Available Attractions

The Club Elephant Almyros Natura 4 * hotel , among other things, also has a tour manager. You can get some good excursion from him, or you can turn to him for help for planning independent trips.

Of course, most hotel guests in the first place acquires an extensive sightseeing tour of northern Corfu. The organizers of this trip usually introduce visitors to the island with such sights as the Mavromatis factory, Achilleo Palace, Paleokastritsa Bay , etc. Also, tourists who buy such an excursion are offered a boat trip with a transparent bottom along the coast.





On their own, residents of the hotel can go, for example, to the city of Kerkyra. Here is a very beautiful Greek church of St. Spyridon and many inexpensive cozy cafes.
