Organized leisure

What entertainment does the Sibiryak Sanatorium (Berdsk) offer? Reviews of tourists are filled mainly with positive impressions of staying here. Changes that have occurred

Sanatorium "Sibiryak" in Berdsk began to function in 1982. Its first guests were printing workers from a local printing company and members of their families. This institution existed as a subsidiary of the publishing house until 2006. Since this period, the health resort has become a joint stock company. Over the years, she has managed to significantly expand the range of medical services, and since 2011, the sanatorium began to rapidly carry out the modernization process. This was made possible thanks to an infusion of investments. As a result, to date, it has been possible to completely change all the equipment in the boiler room, as well as to ennoble the territory of the "health factory", to improve the laundry, and so on. Hotel rooms have been redesigned to meet European standards. Air conditioners appeared in them; they were equipped with modern furniture. The building was equipped with a lobby bar and free internet. Do not ignore the dining area. As a result, the restaurant began to surpass the quality of dishes and the interior of many well-known local restaurants.
