Skirt-shorts: a pattern from the best couturiers

A skirt-shorts is perhaps one of the most versatile and inventive ideas in fashion. It combines the best qualities of both separate elements of clothing and remains an excellent soil for the flight of imagination in its sewing and decoration.



A short skirt, this pattern and manual sewing, or the result of mass production, is always a relevant element, because in it you will always feel confident, not afraid of strong gusts of wind or the irrelevance of your image, since this year light materials are in fashion. Volatile and airy, they are romantic, elegant and elegant. In addition, in order to get such a treasure in your wardrobe, it is not necessary to scour the best boutiques of the city or online stores, because independent sewing of skirts has never lost its relevance and value. Made exactly according to your standards, according to your preferences and your favorite material, such a short skirt will be your best friend in the summer season.

If you decide to sew such an element of clothing yourself, then it is better to first decide what kind of skirt-shorts you need. Photos in magazines and patterns will come in handy in this case. In addition, based on the information found, you can always improve the future product by experimenting with decorative elements and combining several materials. And this is an absolute guarantee that the fruit of your imagination and manual labor will be the only instance in the world.





Variations

So, if you want to look feminine and still not feel pinched, then you should come in handy for a short skirt. The pattern of this product can be made even on the basis of a simple model of "sun". The most relevant this year are considered two-layer pieces in which shorts are covered with a skirt on top. Moreover, it can close them both completely and only at the back and on the sides. For the manufacture of such a product, two types of material are required: for the inner shorts - breathable, but opaque, and for the outer skirt - flying air silk, cambric or chiffon. Fold the first cut in half. We apply our short home shorts and draw a contour first of the front side and then the back, not forgetting the allowances. Sew everything on a typewriter. Then we make a skirt in the form of a three-level shuttlecock so that the future product does not shine through. To do this, cut out three strips of the same width (equal to the waist + 10 cm) and lengths of 10, 20 and 30 cm. We pre-process all the edges on the overlock. Next, we apply strips to each other so that we get a three-layer shuttlecock, and sew on an elastic band. It remains only to make the back seam and sew in shorts.
