Hostels of Kirov: cheap accommodation

Hostels of Kirov enjoy unprecedented success. This is due to the excellent living conditions, which are here at the highest level, low prices and a good location. It is often visited by tourists, travelers and just those who need to spend a few nights. The article will help determine the choice of the best option. Here you can find basic information about the institutions:

  1. Addresses of institutions.
  2. Accommodations.
  3. Additional services.

Hostel Dostoevsky

Hostel Dostoevsky (Kirov) is located in the center of the village. The complex has Internet access, which is provided for free. Guests have a shared bathroom. You can register at the hostel database at any time of the day. Parking, like the Internet, is free of charge for customers. The main thing is to have time to take a place on it. Pets are not allowed. A library is equipped, as well as a sports section. At the request of customers, the administration organizes hiking and other outdoor activities for those who wish. There are lockers where you can put luggage under the lock. The rooms are designed for non-smokers. There is a laundry service, you can order a transfer service, but you need to pay for it separately. If necessary, a fax is available on site. Housekeeping is provided daily in each room. In winter, heating is provided. Not far from the hostel there are several shops where you can buy souvenirs as a keepsake, as well as essential items.

Address of the institution: Karl Liebknecht street, 93.



Hotel "Vyatka"

Vyatka is a hostel hotel (Kirov, Volodarsky St., 209), which is located in the city center. From the bus station, the journey will take more than twenty minutes. Among the services provided can be noted a cafe, bar, fitness club and the distribution of free internet.

All rooms are decorated in a contemporary style using pastel shades that help you relax as much as possible. From their windows you can see the city, which at night becomes beautiful and bewitching. In the rooms, guests will find a work desk, refrigerator, TV. More expensive accommodation options include a spa bath in the bathroom. A hairdryer is provided for free. The reception is open 24 hours a day and there is also a snack bar. With pets do not populate anyone. You can order the service of shoe shine, additional cleaning in the room, visit the laundry if necessary.

Hotel "Selena"

Speaking about cheap hostels in Kirov, you need to mention that there are great options that are affordable for hostels, but the quality of hotels. It's about the Selena. A ten minute walk from the establishment there are several shopping centers where you can buy essentials, food, souvenirs. The hotel provides free internet and parking, where you can leave your car.

The rooms are tastefully decorated, light colors prevail in the interior. The style can be said to dominate the classic. Each room has a TV, refrigerator. The bathroom has a hairdryer, slippers, toiletries. Near the hostel there are cafes and restaurants.

Stations (bus and train) are just two kilometers away. The bus stop where city transport passes is not much removed. You can reach her in five minutes.



Hostel Alaska

Hostel "Alaska" (Kirov, Zavodskaya street, 41) differs from many institutions because it is allowed to live with pets here. On its territory there is Internet.

Guests have access to shared bathroom facilities. You can register your arrival or departure at any time of the day. Alaska also offers table tennis, which will definitely appeal to people who prefer outdoor activities rather than passive ones. There is no parking, therefore it is better not to come here on your cars or to look for parking in advance. Rooms are cleaned daily and laundry facilities are also available. Such services are provided by many hostels in Kirov. Products can be delivered directly to rooms, which is pleasantly pleasing. There is a common living room where guests can watch TV with their neighbors in comfort. Heating is provided in each room if necessary. If you want, you can order a special indoor lunch - a packed lunch. For smokers, the doors to this hostel are closed, as cigarettes are not allowed here.





Hotel-Hostel "Old Vyatka"

The institution is located in the center of Kirov (Gorky St., 57). Free internet and parking are offered by this hostel. The rooms (Kirov is a beautiful city, so you can count on magnificent views from the windows) are kept clean. Some have a seating area, but this is only in expensive options. There is also a TV in the rooms, which is connected to cable TV.

You can register at any time of the day, as the counter is open around the clock. Pets are not allowed. The entrance is closed for smokers, as all rooms are equipped in such a way that the tobacco smoke in them is a taboo. There is a laundry service, cleaning is done once a day, but if desired, you can order additional. Also on the territory there is a fax and photocopy machine, which allows you to print any documents.
