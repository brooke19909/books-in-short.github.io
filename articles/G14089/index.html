Monastic Gorge in Abkhazia - a wonderful hidden corner

Mountain air, citrus plantations, fishing, young wine tasting , hospitable hosts - you can immediately understand that this is a resort. Despite the general characteristics, all resorts have their own characteristics and hidden corners. For example, in Abkhazia there are a lot of unique places that you will not see on standard excursions.

Abkhazia - another world

As soon as you enter the territory of Abkhazia, you immediately realize that you are in a completely different world. There is a lot of free space around, on the one hand - the sea, and on the other - mountains with snowy peaks. There are very beautiful sunrises and sunsets, and frolic dolphins can be seen in the turquoise waves.

The city of Pitsunda in Abkhazia is very popular among tourists. Famous pensions are located in Pitsunda, they have been functioning since the times of the Soviet Union. The coastline is sometimes full of vacationers. There are a lot of sculptures and attractions, as well as all kinds of entertainment for children and adults. If you want a secluded and relaxing holiday, then not far from Pitsunda there is a secret corner - the Monastic Gorge.





History of the Monastic Gorge in Abkhazia

The gorge has been called Monastic since ancient times. Once upon a time in this beautiful place a group of monks settled. The gorge met all the requirements - there was a source of fresh water, a lot of fruit trees and a cave where you can hide from the weather. The source of water was replenished due to the cleanest mountain rivers, the taste of the water was amazing.





The monks left this corner a long time ago, and the spring is still full of delicious water - they began to consider it holy. The cave also remained, where you can enter and look around, imagine how the monks once warmed themselves in it by the fire.

How to get there

Almost every resident of Pitsunda knows about the Monastic Gorge. A reference point in the search for this object can serve as the Pitsunda fish factory. Also in the region of the Monastic Gorge is the Armenian village of Agaraki. It has very hospitable owners, they will gladly rent a house or a room at a reasonable price. The private sector in the Monastic Gorge in Abkhazia is populated year-round.

To get to the Monastic Gorge, you can use the services of a private cab or get on foot if you are light. Walk about eight kilometers along the coastline, along the way there will be reserved zones and groves with unique flora. Some of the reserves are fenced with a high fence, and some are completely free to enter. A walk will give you unforgettable pleasure.

Housing

In the region of the Monastic Gorge (Abkhazia), housing is not a problem: there are usually very few tourists here. This is due to the fact that there are no mention of comfortable hotels with three meals a day in this region. The apartments you rent here are small outbuildings or rooms on a common area. As a rule, bathrooms are shared. There will also be a shared kitchen with a large terrace, where everyone has lunch and chat together. Guests prepare their own meals on their own, while you can use common utensils and kitchen utensils. Fresh produce can be bought in a small market right in the village.

As a reward for their humility, tourists living in such conditions get the opportunity to swim and sunbathe on the only sandy beach in all of Abkhazia. Silence, peace, pacification - only you and nature.





The hosts will give you enough attention, surrounded by care and love. If you want to see dolphins, you need to get up early in the morning and go to the coast. Most likely, there you will find at least a couple of these cute creatures - their children like to watch their games in the water so much!

It is very interesting to dive here, as the underwater life is rich, and the water is clear and clean. Some tourists like to swim very far into the sea.

Leisure

If you like various ancient artifacts, make time and be sure to visit the Monastic Gorge in Abkhazia. Rest in it will be remembered for a lifetime. There is something to do here, in addition to the contemplation of picturesque landscapes. You can go on a journey along the coast and wander away into the thickets, overcoming stone blockages. Near the gorge you can see ancient buildings, various caves and traces of ancient civilizations. Also here is the legendary Golden Bay, where at the entrance to the sea you will feel a gentle sandy bottom under your feet. Here you can go diving or just diving with a mask.





There is an opinion that hermits live behind the gorge; Of course, few people meet them.

There is a lot of greenery, various animals, as well as the Relic Grove nearby, it is cool even in the hottest weather. As for the local air, it's just a fairy tale! Mostly conifers grow here, so the air is literally filled with volatile products. After a walk along the coast, you will immediately feel a surge of strength and vigor. The sea here, as already mentioned, is very clean and less salty than in other areas, since a lot of rivers flow into it, which originate high in the mountains.

If you want to go on an excursion, you need to get to the center of Pitsunda - from there go various sightseeing buses or jeeps. It organizes walks to Lake Ritsa, Gegsky Falls, Alpine meadows, etc. A picnic in nature is always included in any excursion. The stories of the guide who will go with you are very interesting. Also keep in mind that you will need to walk a lot, so the night before, have a good night's sleep and rest.





Rafting is very developed in Abkhazia - this is a seasonal hobby. Fans of this sport monitor the full flow of mountain rivers, then they go rafting on special boats, overcoming a strong current.

Reviews on the Monastic Gorge in Abkhazia

Abkhazia is a very beautiful region, always welcoming guests. What do tourists say about holidays in this paradise?

The village of Monastic Gorge in Abkhazia differs from all well-known resorts in that it is a very calm place, located away from city noise and bustle. If you want to enjoy the sandy beach and the crystal clear sea, surrounded by beautiful nature and healthy air, you are welcome here, because here you will relax both your soul and body.

Real reviews of tourists are very contradictory, as many travel to the gorge, not knowing in advance what awaits them. There are people who are simply delighted with this wonderful corner and come here every year, settling in the same houses.
