Accommodation in Abrau-Durso: options for a budget and comfortable stay

Abrau-Durso is a famous resort of the Krasnodar Territory. Its name is famous thanks to the champagne wine factory, which produces the best brands in Russia, using grapes grown here.

Features of the resort area

This resort is located 14 kilometers from Novorossiysk and actually consists of two villages. Abrau is located on the shores of the lake of the same name, the largest in the Western Caucasus. You can drive to Durso on the road. This village is located 7 kilometers from Abrau. Durso got its name from a mountain river. Both villages form the resort area of ‚Äã‚ÄãAbrau-Durso, housing in which not only tourists are interested. In the village of Abrau is a winery.





Besides him, in the village you can find shops, cafes, and a market. There are not many guest houses here, as nearby is Durso, which is located on the Black Sea. Nevertheless, the flow of tourists to Abrau does not stop, mainly due to the champagne wine factory, built in the 19th century.

In addition to the plant, the attention of visitors is attracted by the picturesque and deep lake Abrau, in which there are:

  • trout;
  • bream;
  • silver carp.

The flora and fauna of the lake is recognized as unique, therefore it is under protection. There is a small beach on the shore of the reservoir, it is believed that swimming in Abrau is good for health. A legend is connected with the lake that it was formed from the tears of a girl who mourned her dead parents. On the Black Sea coast, almost every beautiful corner is covered in legends.

Rest in houses

Housing in Abrau-Durso by the sea is not difficult to find. Right on the seashore there are many quiet guest houses that can be rented for a small price:

  • "Rainbow in Durso."
  • "Eden".
  • "Chocolate".
  • "Call of the sea."
  • "Valeria".

This is not a complete list of possible guest house options. All buildings are modern, equipped with parking lots, the rooms are homely and cozy.





Holidays in Abrau-Durso are characterized by peace and quiet. This is not worth going to those who crave noisy parties and discos. But this is a great place for families, for those who are tired of the hustle and bustle. Homemade barbecue prepared on the grill, wine from the company store at the champagne factory will decorate the evening of tourists.

Where to relax on your own

In Durso there is a small lake Sweet Limanchik, the fresh water of which is separated from the sea by a small rocky spit. Not far from the lake, in the mountains covered with forest, there is a tent city for lovers of leisure in the style of savages. The mild Black Sea summer contributes to the fact that the tent city can become a home in Abrau-Durso. Of course, there are no amenities, but this kind of relaxation has its own romance.

In guest houses you can rent a house in Abrau-Durso for daily rent. This option is convenient for tourists who travel by car along the coast to cover all the beautiful places of the region:

  • Novorossiysk;
  • Gelendzhik;
  • adjacent villages.

Travelers will certainly stay in Abrau-Durso to visit the champagne wine factory, buy drinks and explore unique natural beauties. The resort area is attractive for lovers of eco-tourism, as the local mountains, forests and lakes are still untouched in their virgin purity. For the Black Sea coast is a rarity.





Private housing in Abrau-Durso is also available for lovers of economical vacations. Tourists will be provided with rooms and the opportunity to do their own cooking in the host kitchens. Locals are hospitable and welcoming. If there is no desire to cook, then guest houses and cafes will inexpensively and tastyly feed all visitors.

For lovers of comfort

It so happens that you want to relax as comfortably as possible in the domestic plan. Despite the fact that the resort occupies a small territory, you can find fashionable hotels here. In Abrau-Durso, similar accommodation plans are represented by the following hotels:

  1. Imperial & Champange SPA.
  2. Hotel Abrau.
  3. Hotel "Laguna".
  4. Club Hotel Durso.
  5. The farmstead "Round Lake".

Among them, the most comfortable is Imperial. The hotel offers beautiful views of Lake Abrau. The number of rooms is very diverse. You can find an economizer or rent a presidential suite. The hotel has a SPA center and a swimming pool, which is becoming especially important due to the distance from the sea.





The most convenient location is at the club-hotel "Durso", from it to the sea only 80 meters. This is a small hotel, but comfortable. It has deluxe rooms, a pool, a sauna.

Recreation facilities

In Abrau-Durso, housing can be found in recreation centers, which are not famous for their special comfort, but offer affordable prices. Usually in such places of residence the bathroom is designed for the floor. But there are some among them that can offer room amenities and additional entertainment. For example, the recreation center "Energetik", located on the coastline, offers guests:

  • pool;
  • billiards;
  • tennis;
  • the bathhouse.

We talked about the possibilities of living during a summer vacation in a picturesque place on the Black Sea. Abrau-Durso is waiting for travelers, hospitably providing any accommodation options.
