Shopping in Prague: Traveler Reviews

The capital of the Czech Republic attracts tourists with its beauty and color. Vacationers can count on an interesting cultural and entertainment program, because there is something to see here. But a pleasant bonus to excursions to the main attractions attached shopping in Prague. Travelers' reviews of local stores and shopping centers are only positive, if you get into the sales season, you can purchase branded goods with significant discounts.



Tourists usually buy shoes, clothes, porcelain and glass, jewelry, authentic drinks and food. Over the past decade, shopping in Prague has come as close to world standards as possible. Guest reviews of the Czech capital allow you to choose the best quality and most interesting products, as well as find out when and where it is most profitable to shop. The authorities do not set exact sales dates; each shopping center decides on its own when to make discounts. Typically, prices drop after the New Year, when new collections arrive in stores, in the middle of summer, in April and October.

You can find discounts up to 70% during the season, but you need to hurry, because not only local residents, but also Europeans, and even Americans do not miss shopping in Prague. Reviews of tourists allow us to understand what there are crowds in stores and shopping centers during the peak season. It is impossible to bring many things from the Czech Republic, so you should pay attention to those goods that are not at home, or they cost much more. Women will certainly enjoy the jewelry made of Czech garnet, which is sold framed in silver or gold. A chic gift will be a vase, chandelier or glasses of Bohemian crystal.



Just because of the Yablonex jewelry, absolutely all the girls will enjoy shopping in Prague. Reviews about products manufactured in Jablonec nad Nisou are only positive. Ladies will love the handmade buttons and Tonac hats worn by the Queen of Great Britain. Natural cosmetics will not leave indifferent any representative of the fair sex either. There are a lot of shops for children's toys and goods in Prague. Here you can buy the popular cartoon mole, porcelain dolls in beautiful outfits, wooden puppet dolls for the puppet theater. You can also buy interesting wall clocks, paints and pencils of the famous Kokh-i-nur brand.

For real men, the assortment of goods is no less than for women, prepared by the Czech Republic. Prague takes shopping to a new, modern level, here you can buy absolutely all things of high quality and at the best price. Men will like smoking pipes manufactured at Velke Losiny factory, writing sets, natural cosmetics, beer mugs, tinctures and beer.



In the pre-Christmas period, shopping in Prague is especially appreciated. Prices for goods at this time usually fall, there is a huge variety of holiday gifts, Christmas toys. Tourists also purchase souvenirs related to Karlovy Vary. A rose, covered with salt from a mineral spring, is considered to be a particularly beautiful and unique souvenir.
