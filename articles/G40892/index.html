Suspension bridge on Ai-Petri: photos, description and reviews of tourists

Mount Ai-Petri is one of the most famous peaks of the Crimea. Its height is 1234 meters. Every year, a huge number of tourists rise to this peak who want to get their dose of thrills and enjoy excellent panoramas. There is also a unique tourist attraction - a suspension bridge on Ai-Petri.

The most extreme entertainment in Crimea



In fact, there are two suspension bridges on Ai-Petri. They are located at an altitude of more than 1200 meters above sea level. The distance from the steps of the bridges to the rocks is about 50-60 meters. These numbers are breathtaking. Tourists who decide on such an extreme excursion will have to overcome two suspension bridges over the abyss. The length of each is 45 meters. The first suspension bridge on Ai-Petri is straight and level. However, even a passage through it will give a lot of new sensations. The steps are located at a solid distance from each other, and the railing looks symbolic. The second bridge is popularly called the "Road to Heaven." It has a noticeable slope upward, climbing up it, a person "climbs" uphill. Many tourists admit that they decided not to overcome such a route at all the first time. In the photo, the Ai-Petri suspension bridge looks reliable and not too long. In fact, a strong wind almost always blows at the top. Suspension bridges are driven by several tourists at once. Easy rocking of the construction is ensured!

Are Ai-Petri Suspension Bridges Safe?



The construction of the most extreme Crimean mountain attraction was carried out by the local public organization "Freerith" and the emergency rescue squad from Yalta "Crimea-Spas". Before opening the bridges, thorough calculations and tests were carried out. Steel cables were used to strengthen the visually fragile structure. Thanks to them, each suspension bridge on Ai-Petri is able to withstand a load exceeding the permissible norm by 10 times. During the operation of the attraction, representatives of the rescue service are constantly here. They provide brief instruction for tourists, as well as help prepare for the passage over the bridge. Admission to the attraction is carried out only after securing the insurance. In addition, every tourist should wear a safety helmet. For the entire duration of the attraction, no special incidents or accidents were recorded.

Interesting Facts



Ay-Petri is an ancient Greek name. Historians translate it into Russian as "St. Peter". According to experts, the mountain in Crimea was named after an ancient monastery that once functioned here. This peak is not the highest on the peninsula. It is famous for its geographical location and beauty. This mountain is considered the most foggy and windiest peak in the Crimea. For those who want to visit the suspension bridge on Ai-Petri, these are only pluses. During the tour, you can often observe the clouds directly under their own feet. How much is a trip over the abyss?

The most extreme attraction of Crimea has a democratic cost. Pass on suspension bridges on Ai-Petri is possible for only 500 rubles. The main requirements for thrill-seekers: self-confidence, well-being and mood. Attention: it is forbidden to visit this attraction in a state of intoxication. Pregnant women, people with disabilities, as well as everyone who doubts their own physical fitness and general stamina should also refrain from walking over the precipice. Small children are also not allowed on the bridge.

How to get to the suspension bridges on Ai-Petri?



Ai-Petri Mountain looks very impressive from below. Its top has several teeth, making it look like a crown from afar. Suspension bridges are stretched between adjacent teeth, which have become popular tourist attractions. There are two ways to get to them. The most extreme is to climb the Miskhor Ai-Petri cable car. Alternative option: take a private car, public transport or taxi. Be prepared for the start of a hiking trail at the end of the mountain serpentine. Entrance to it costs one hundred rubles. The climb is simple, very often this excursion is chosen by novice tourists who first visit the Crimea. The Ai-Petri suspension bridge is located on the top of the mountain, its exact coordinates are: 44.448009, 34.055987.

Extreme Entertainment Reviews



A suspension bridge is not just an entertainment facility. This is a real test, the opportunity to feel like a pioneer and adventurer. Suspension bridge Ai-Petri positive reviews due to its high safety features. The attraction works daily, its schedule coincides with the schedule of the cable car. The construction of the bridge is simple and reliable. For all the time of its existence, not a single tourist was injured during a walk over the abyss. What does a person feel when stepping on a bridge? Of course, this is a tremendous excitement and fear. But, having already taken the first few steps, you can gain self-control and enjoy the views. This tour will bring much more new experiences and emotions than a standard trip to the water park or zoo. You will definitely want to tell your friends and relatives about such an adventure. If you love the thrill, your goal is Ai-Petri Mountain. The suspension bridge is a unique attraction in the Crimea, a visit to which simply cannot leave anyone indifferent.
