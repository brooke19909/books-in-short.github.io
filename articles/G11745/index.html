Panwa Beach Resort 4 * (Thailand, Phuket, Cape Panwa): description, service, reviews

About 10 km from the Thai city of Phuket is the picturesque cape Panwa. Here, tourists who decide to relax near the sea, can not only swim and sunbathe, but also visit the famous Phuket Aquarium with several hundred colorful sea fish and all kinds of interesting sea creatures.

Cape Panwa is very popular among tourists. And of course, this resort has all the conditions for a comfortable holiday for vacationers. There are also many hotels of different price categories. For example, on the cape you can stay at the Panwa Beach Resort 4 * hotel.





Features of the location of the complex

This hotel is built on a cape in Chalong Bay. Here is, among other things, the main port of Phuket. In relation to city infrastructure, the Panwa Beach Resort 4 * hotel is located very well. In Chalong Bay, several large shopping centers popular with tourists and many shops specializing in the sale of utensils, clothes, souvenirs, etc., have been built.

There are many shopping centers in Panva, but, unfortunately, there are no catering establishments on this cape. Vacationers staying at a hotel can only eat in other areas of Phuket.

Chalong Bay is located on the first coastline. However, unfortunately, this does not give special advantages to lovers of sunbathing and swimming in the sea, staying at this hotel. The fact is that the sea in Chalong Bay is very muddy. At the same time, a truly huge number of fishing boats are sailing here. That is, swimming in this place is simply uncomfortable. But Chalong Bay is very popular among scuba diving enthusiasts. In the sea, the sea lives a lot of outlandish fish and other marine life.





In relation to the airport, Panwa Beach Resort 4 *, according to many vacationers, is located quite well. The distance to the nearest transport air hub is only about 42 km. That is, tourists who have booked rooms here can reach the hotel in just an hour.

When is the best time to come to a hotel?

The beach season in Phuket lasts all year round. In principle, you can visit this resort whenever you want. But the best thing, of course, is to come here in high season. This period lasts in Thailand from November to May. At this time, the country stops raining, and the temperature of the water in the sea becomes as comfortable as possible.

But in any case, vacationers, of course, can visit Phuket in the low season. This will save them money. For example, in October, the weather in Phuket usually already costs very good. It rains here infrequently at this time. The prices for accommodation during this period in resort hotels, including Panwa Beach, are slightly lower than in high season.

The weather in Phuket in October, therefore, is usually sunny. The average temperature during the day is 28 ¬∞ C. The water in the sea warms up to 29 ¬∞ C.
