How to make a ball of flowers for the holiday

Sometimes there is an urgent need for room decor. Whether it is a personal wish for cozy beauty, a solemn event that must be held at the highest level, a charming ball of flowers will always be appropriate. This is a simple and at the same time elegant solution.

How to make a ball of flowers, what is the secret of the technique for making this product? Of course, you can use fresh flowers - this will give the holiday special beauty and freshness. Basically, carnations are chosen for this - steady, solemn flowers. How to make a ball of carnations so that they do not wilt after three days? Use a floral sponge soaked in water. Insert flowers into it, the stems can be cut. You can decorate such a ball with beads, ribbons, spangles. It can be hung on a beautiful rope or mounted on a stand, after leaving an empty space for this.





Such a decor item can be made not only from fresh flowers. But also from textile, paper, plastic. How to make a ball of flowers from bright paper? Firstly, instead of a floristic ball, it is necessary to take foam. Secondly, for flowers made of paper, it is better to use corrugated paper - it takes a better shape and looks much more interesting than ordinary offset sheets. Do not forget to buy satin ribbons and needles with beautiful hats.

Carefully wrap the ball in satin ribbons, pre-lubricating it with glue. Make paper flowers in your favorite technique and attach them one at a time to the ball. You can fix it both with needles and glue.





Balloons have been and will be a very popular decor item for various events at all times. Several years ago, decorators began to actively use them for making flowers. Flowers from balloons are very interesting, pleasing to the eye of both a child and an adult, reminding the latter of the happy days of his life.





It is absolutely not difficult to complete this decorated item, and only two balls for one flower may be needed. Determine the required number of products and buy the right number of balls. Inflate two oblong balls: a green stem, bright, your favorite color - an inflorescence. In a bright ball, leave the tip at 3 cm and tie the beginning to the end. Having divided into three equal parts, scroll the ball in these places, and boldly fold it into an accordion. After taking the resulting composition at the bend, scroll through the petals again.

Proceed to the stalk. The green ball must be twisted about ten centimeters from the beginning and bent in half. Tie the end to the fold and insert the stem into the petals. Bend the stem again in half and scroll around perpendicularly. With such simple manipulations, you will get a flower from balloons with a bright bud, stem and leaves. It looks beautiful and graceful.

So we decided how to make a ball of flowers. In this event you can maximize your imagination and design abilities. After all, it is only your vision of beauty that limits you, and it can be infinitely large.
