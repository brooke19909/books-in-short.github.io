Hotel Populus 3 * (Prague, Czech Republic): description, photos, reviews

Hotel Populus 3 * (Prague, Czech Republic) is able to offer really comfortable and incredibly affordable housing. The hotel complex is located in one of the quiet residential areas of Prague, 6 km from the center. From here it is not hard to get to the bus and tram stop. The hotel has only 77 rooms, but the guest can choose absolutely any apartment. In total, these rooms can accommodate 220 people, in addition, it is possible to deliver an extra bed. On the territory there are several places where you can have a tasty meal, a summer terrace, as well as many other infrastructure facilities.





EA Hotel Populus 3 * (Prague) offers rental of premises for business events, where you can accommodate all your guests. The rooms are equipped with everything necessary, equipped with the latest technology. Guests can use the free parking area, where all cars will be under reliable protection.
