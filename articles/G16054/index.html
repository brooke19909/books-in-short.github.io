DIY mobile from felt

The desire to create for your baby the most comfortable, sweet and enchanting atmosphere has been overwhelming zealous mothers from the moment they find out about their situation. Future mothers and fathers begin to look after the baby's clothes, shoes, personal items and, of course, toys. One of the first funs for children is the mobile. With your own hands, you can do it by investing in it all the care and love for a small miracle, but the purchased mobile is no worse.





Who really needs a mobile?

Many parents sincerely wonder why clutter up the crib with unnecessary accessories. The older generation, not used to modern diversity, takes such a toy especially sharply. The problem can be solved if you ask the future granny to make a mobile phone with her own hands. Having penetrated the task and creating it in a cooperative way, she will immediately change her point of view and take the side of her parents.

Of course, first of all, the mother sets the mobile on the bed not for the child, but to a greater extent for herself. The kid will do fine without him, because he is so small that he does not immediately understand what it is and why different figures flicker before his eyes. Many generations of children have grown and developed perfectly without such a device, but there is absolutely no harm from it.

Using a mobile phone allows you to draw the attention of a child to colors and shapes, various textures. Having made a children's mobile with her own hands, mom herself can pick up the appearance of the toys.

What to choose from?

Children's product range pleases with its wide selection. For mobile phones, manufacturers pay no less attention than other toys. What are they, and what is the difference between children's mobile phones, made with their own hands, from purchased analogues?

Industrial products are very diverse. The most popular are plastic toys equipped with a mechanism that rotates toys. Most often, they are musical, while the volume and melody can be changed as desired. There are mobiles with small nightlights and even projectors.





For lovers of eco-goods there are wooden and textile mobiles. Ready-made mobiles are equipped with a special mount, which is fixed to the side of the crib with the help of a clamping bolt or clamp. What is felt, what kind to choose for a children's toy?

This material is loved by many needlewomen. He gives unlimited opportunities for creativity. There are different felt, differing in composition, thickness and color. It can be made of natural wool and made of artificial material, mainly polyester. The colors of felt are very diverse. At the same time, in the palette there are not only primary colors, but also many shades. To make a mobile phone on a bed with your own hands, or rather toys for it, it is very important to choose the right felt for crafts. For small parts, it is best to use thin material, this will greatly facilitate the work with it. Thick felt, more than 2 mm in thickness, is used for large products requiring stiffness.

For the manufacture of felt, different compositions are used, often synthetic threads are mixed with natural raw materials to give the material strength. Woolen and half-woolen species are better not to use in the manufacture of children's toys, because they can cause allergic reactions. It is best to stay on felt from eco-polyester, experienced needlewomen advise taking Korean-made material, it works very well, pleasant to the touch and safe.

How to make a mobile felt?

Making a mobile home from felt is really very simple. This material does not require special sewing skills, special equipment and skills. Felt is absolutely loose, so products from it do not need to be processed in slices.





In order to make a mobile phone with your own hands, you need to think about what style the toy will be, develop templates for simple drawings and draw them on paper. You should not use complex sketches. The kid can tire of bright and overly artsy crafts. On the mobile, various geometric shapes, flowers, stars, hearts, clouds will look great.

When the patterns are ready, you need to use chalk to transfer them to a double folded fabric and cut along the contour. It is customary to sew felt crafts on top, with straight stitches, using a contrasting thread for this. To add volume to the toy, you can put a little cotton or filler from the pillow.

When stitching parts, it is important not to forget to insert a thread that will be attached to the base of the mobile. The length of the thread or cord must be measured and cut to the required number, depending on the height at which the mobile will be attached.
