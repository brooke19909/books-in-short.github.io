Holidays in Bodrum, at the L'ambiance Resort Hotel

Bodrum is considered the capital of the azure coast of Turkey. It is located on the picturesque Anatolian Peninsula and is washed by two seas: the Aegean and the Mediterranean. Here, people from Western Europe prefer to spend their holidays. Beautiful landscapes and a unique shade of the sea can inspire the creation of new cultural masterpieces. Therefore, this resort is also considered the capital of the artistic bohemia of Turkey.





Imagine this picture: snow-white villas buried in the greenery of tropical trees, beautiful bays with numerous sailing yachts, the sparkling starry dome of the southern sky on hot nights. Bodrum has the largest disco club in Europe, Halicarnassus, which is named after one of the most ancient Greek cities, destroyed by Alexander the Great.

Bodrum Hotels

The city has many cozy hotels of all categories; Daphnis Hotel 2 *, Golden sunset 3 *, Crystal Hotel 4 *, L ambiance Resort Hotel 5 * and others. All of them, regardless of the number of stars, provide high-quality service for vacationers. There are also hotels that belong to the same chain, but in different categories. For example, hotels of the L'ambiance Resort Hotel chain . Corporate ethics requires employees to be extremely attentive to customers and provide service at the highest level. If you want to relax in Bodrum with the greatest comfort, but want to save a little, then choose hotels of this particular chain, for example, the four-star L'ambiance Resort Hotel. It is practically no different from a five-star one, only the prices are slightly more affordable. In addition, it is perfect for couples with children, and for seekers of peace and quiet.



Description and services at L ' ambiance Resort Hotel 4 *

L ambiance Resort Hotel 4 * is a hotel complex, which consists of several two-story bungalows surrounded by verdure truly verdant gardens. The complex is located just a kilometer from the modernly equipped beach and 3 kilometers from the center of Bodrum. The hotel has 162 cozy and comfortable rooms of various categories, 10 of which are luxury apartments. All bungalows are equipped with central air conditioning, showers, satellite communications and television, minibars, safes, etc.

Nutrition





Meals at the L'ambiance Resort Hotel complex are based on the ALL INCLUSIVE and SELECTED system. This means that 3 times a day - for breakfast, lunch and dinner - you can use the buffet, the variety of which will appeal to even the most capricious vacationers. In addition, all day until midnight in a separate room you will be offered various snacks, fresh pastries, tea, coffee, etc. From 10 a.m. to 1 a.m. you can also use the services of a bar where you can choose from alcoholic drinks Turkey, as well as local juices and other drinks. In addition to the buffet, the L'ambiance Resort Hotel has 2 a la carte restaurants and one Italian. The services of these restaurants are not included in the price of the tour.

Entertainment

The complex has: 1 outdoor pool for adults and 1 for children, a sauna and Turkish bath, fitness equipment, fitness, spa, table tennis and other board games, as well as darts, several water activities (canoe, catamaran, etc.), children's playroom, evening entertainment. For the smallest there are babysitting services.
