Hotel Sol Y Mar Paradise Beach Resort (Egypt / Safaga): tourists photos and reviews

Egypt is a wonderful country to relax. Here at any time of the year you can enjoy swimming in the sea, sunbathe under the tropical sun and get acquainted with the history and life of this truly fabulous area. Also, a well-spent vacation is facilitated by the living conditions that are offered by the selected hotel. Sol Y Mar Paradise Beach Resort welcomes all its guests and provides a comfortable stay for every guest.

Hotel location

For many tourists who have come to rest in Egypt, the remoteness of the hotel from the sea is of great importance. Sol Y Mar Paradise Beach Resort will not disappoint its guests, because it is located on the picturesque coast of the Red Sea. Just a few minutes to walk to the beach and plunge into the warm waves.



The hotel is in a quiet and peaceful area, as all the vibrant settlements are at a respectful distance. The nearest city is Safaga. The hotel is 5 km away. The international airport is 50 km away, but tourists are quickly delivered to their place of residence. Moreover, on the way to the hotel, vacationers talk about the sights encountered on the way. Hurghada is 65 km from the hotel. Guests can get there by public transport. You can use the services of local taxi drivers.

What are the reviews of tourists talking about?

Hotel information about Sol Y Mar Paradise Beach Resort can be found on numerous travel agency sites. But to find out what the actual conditions for living are offered, will allow the reviews of tourists who have been here. Vacationers in most cases share their positive impressions. The hotel has a very friendly and friendly staff who can take care of every guest who arrives at them. Here, at a meeting, they will always ask whether everything is fine with a person and wish him a pleasant stay.



According to tourists, the hotel service is up to standard. Satisfies almost everything:

  • condition of rooms;
  • quality of cleaning;
  • nutrition;
  • relaxing on the beach;
  • entertainment.

The only thing that sometimes upsets vacationers is the orientation of the hotel on tourists from European countries. Many Germans are resting here, and therefore themed parties and various programs are held in a foreign language, incomprehensible to Russian-speaking travelers. But otherwise, no discrimination is manifested.

Hotel Description

For people who want to relax in a relaxed atmosphere and fully see what kind of Egypt really is, Sol Y Mar Paradise Beach Resort is perfect. The complex itself is made in the Arabic style, which allows you to enjoy the features of oriental architecture from the first days of your stay.



Guests here are very welcome. No one complains about a long settlement. If there are any delays, tourists can comfortably spend time in a cozy lobby, furnished with luxurious and comfortable wicker furniture. There is a lot of greenery in the hotel lobby, and the light passing through a peculiar dome of the ceiling fills the room with a magical atmosphere.

Guests are offered refreshments upon arrival. At the reception there are always administrators who speak Russian, so there are no communication problems.

Room Features

Sol Y Mar Paradise Beach Resort consists of a four-story building, two-story chalets and separate bungalows. All rooms have rooms. In particular, guests are provided with 236 rooms, from which you can choose:

  • standard rooms;
  • Family rooms
  • villas.

Each room has everything you need during your stay. Leisure can be brightened up by watching TV on which Russian channels are broadcast. You can use the phone. Calls to the reception are free, but you need to pay extra for international calls. Guests can use the electronic safes available in each room to store valuables. There are also minibars with various drinks, for which you will have to pay extra.

The bathroom in the rooms is quite comfortable. Here you can take a shower, use a hairdryer. Toiletry stock is systematically updated.

Guests will appreciate the quality of cleaning in the rooms. The staff of the Sol Y Mar Paradise Beach Resort 4 * hotel does its job perfectly, and after wet cleaning the room is very clean. Do not forget here about the daily change of linen and towels.

What will the food please?

Very often, tourists are disappointed with the quality of food in hotels, which cannot be said about Sol Y Mar Paradise Beach Resort. Guest reviews confirm that they feed here very well. The main restaurant seats 350 people. You can eat in the beautiful, well-conditioned room or on the terrace. The menu is quite varied. Here you can choose diet meals. Do not forget about the young guests of the hotel. For them, you can choose dishes from the children's menu.

Locally-made drinks are offered to guests living on an all-inclusive basis for free, you can only buy imported drinks. Many guests like that in the restaurant you can always make yourself coffee or tea. As for freshly squeezed juices and ice cream, they are provided only for money.

Infrastructure

The Sol Y Mar Paradise Beach Resort provides many different services that allow guests to spend their holidays in fairly comfortable conditions. Near the hotel there are two swimming pools, one of which is designed for children. Everyone can sunbathe or relax at these ponds on the sun loungers, which, like towels, are provided free of charge.

Since the hotel is located at a considerable distance from the cities, many vacationers prefer to rent a car to drive around the area on their own, admiring its beauty. Car rental is charged a certain fee. Local laundry and dry cleaning services are available. For a certain amount, they will quickly put their clothes in order.



Hotel guests looking at their appearance will enjoy the spa facilities. Vacationers here are invited to a relaxing and wellness massage, in the jacuzzi or steam room. At the hairdresser you can tidy your hair.

The hotel has experienced doctors who provide medical care on call. Their services must be paid. To leave the children for a while, parents can order the services of a qualified nanny.

Relax on the hotel beach

Many tourists praise the beach at the Hotel Sol Y Mar Paradise Beach Resort. Photos of this paradise will make sure that this is truly a fascinating place. The beach area is quite vast, and tourists do not have to be crowded. Entrance to the water is very comfortable. For swimming there is no need to use aqua-jets.



Vacationers at the beach are provided with free sun loungers and parasols. Since there are always plenty of them, hotel guests can find a free place at any time. In the water you can meet marine life, who sometimes swim to the shore itself. The view of bright tropical fish is especially popular with children. Some were lucky to even see a sea turtle.

How can I have fun at the hotel?

Guests at Sol Y Mar Paradise Beach Resort will not be bored. Here you have the opportunity to entertain yourself with various games and sports. Free vacationers can always play table tennis, beach volleyball or basketball. Animators constantly organize various competitions and contests, which contributes to communication between guests. For an additional fee you can learn scuba diving. Experienced instructors teach at a diving school. There is an opportunity to swim on a boat, windsurfing. But you have to pay extra for this.

Classes in the gym at the hotel are paid. For money, you can play mini-golf or billiards.

Quite fascinating for tourists will be excursions that allow you to see many different attractions. Experienced guides will tell very informative stories about the origin of historical monuments or other interesting places.

Safaga, Sol Y Mar Paradise Beach Resort: price of tours

Despite the fact that the hotel offers quite comfortable living conditions, the cost of tours can be considered budgetary. A weekly tour will cost one person only 15,075 rubles. But this price is relevant only for the winter months. At the height of the season, the cost rises slightly.

Hotel Sol Y Mar Paradise Beach Resort will leave only positive impressions, if you remember that at any such institutions you can find any shortcomings. A positive attitude to rest will allow you to enjoy the sea and the beautiful beach, stock up on pleasant memories for a long time.
