Some tips on when and where to relax in Egypt

Every vacation we plan, based on our financial capabilities. And in this sense, the question of when is the best time to relax in Egypt plays a key role. Since the price of tickets is very dependent on the season. The most favorable climatic characteristics are April-May and October-November. There is still no suffocating heat, and the water has already managed to warm up (or has not yet cooled down). Also, prices soar fabulously during the holidays: starting from Catholic to Orthodox Christmas. It is also expensive during the period of March 8, for the May weekend. The hottest month in the resorts of Egypt is September.

Based on what time you plan to come, you should choose where it is better to relax in Egypt. The coast of Hurghada is too open to the winds, and the sea is shallow. Therefore, December (when the water still stores summer heat), as well as the summer months and September (when the breeze from the sea softens the heat) will be optimal for relaxation here. Sharm El Sheikh, protected from the north by the mountains, will give winter vacationers quite comfortable temperature indicators of water and air. But we should not forget that this resort is considered the most fashionable, and the prices here are not very much and fall out of season.



"Big Hurghada" is a huge coast, and each individual village has its own special microclimate. In January, when sandstorms fly from the desert, Domina Bay is quiet and calm.
