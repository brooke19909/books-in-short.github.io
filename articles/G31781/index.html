Sunrise Diamond Beach Resort 5 *, Egypt, Sharm El Sheikh: photos, description, prices and reviews

Club Hotel Sunrise Diamond Beach Resort 5 * provides a high-quality and affordable guesthouse for those who wish to relax in the resort city of Sharm El Sheikh. It is not far from the airport: the road by car takes only 15 minutes. The price of a stay in this hotel for Russians, depending on the season, is $ 650-750 per stay for one vacationer for 7 nights in a double room. Connoisseurs of spa holidays prefer the velvet season, when prices decline and the heat subsides.





Sharm el Sheikh ... Both this resort and its hotels are modeled on the best European resorts. Beach

Since the soil is stony (the hotel is located on a cliff, and the terraces from it cascade down to the sea, which is typical for the coast of Ras Um Sid), the hotel beach has an unnatural origin. It is loose, sandy, very neat and well-groomed. Fully equipped with mattresses, umbrellas, sun loungers. To get the full pleasure of swimming, it is advisable to take fins, a mask and a camera with you. It will also suit a regular, digital, but in a special waterproof case. (The latter can be bought or rented.) The entrance to the water is from a hotel plastic pontoon. We strongly recommend that vacationers visiting the Sunrise Diamond Beach Resort 5 * hotel pre-take a few diving lessons. A hotel instructor is a truly knowledgeable and qualified person. The underwater world of the Red Sea





Contemplation of what residents of the Red Sea can a vacationer enjoy at Sunrise Diamond Beach Resort 5 * (Sharm El Sheikh)? The photo posted in the article will be the answer to this question. We can not help but tell about some of them. First of all, these are corals. There are more than 250 species of these exotic living organisms in the Red Sea! They are colorful and beautiful.



Resorts pay much attention to sea vacations in their reviews. They are amazed that the depth in the near water of the beach is up to 30 meters. You can, of course, look at the underwater world from the surface of the water. However, if you have scuba diving skills, then the vacationer will have more powerful sensations obtained from the direct contemplation of coral and fish. And if the resort in the Red Sea takes a real underwater photo shoot, then the memory will remain even longer.





How do hotel guests describe their impressions of the dive? Adults seem to be turning into children. They write with enthusiasm about colored fish, as if from an aquarium, only, of course, more. And the underwater world is really colorful. Reminds us familiar scalar fish Platax. Flocks of photogenic zebras having the color of an electrician float every now and then , and for some of them, the tail and fins are for some reason bright yellow. Such contrasts can only be seen in the Red Sea. Multi-colored parrot fish scurry about on coral. The surgeon fish is very beautiful (do not be afraid, it is not aggressive, just its tail and fins are really sharp; the upper fin and tail are colored in the color of an electrician, and the side fins are yellow). Indeed, a lot of impressions are associated with the resort with the beach of the hotel Sunrise Diamond Beach Resort 5 * (Sharm El Sheikh). Their reviews are enthusiastic and interesting, because the corals here are one of the most tempting in the world even for qualified divers.
