We leave for Turkey

We will begin our acquaintance with the mentioned hotel chain with Flamingo hotel 4 *, Marmaris. Situated in a place where the Mediterranean Sea merges with the Aegean, the cozy Turkish city of Marmaris is a fabulous corner filled with green foliage and blue coastal waves.



Thanks to the mild Mediterranean climate, the swimming season lasts until winter. And coniferous and plane trees make the air unusually clean and healthy.

The courteous hotel staff, the friendly local population, beautiful nature, affordable prices, clean water and a relaxed, out-of-town atmosphere make your vacation here unforgettable, as evidenced by the huge number of most enthusiastic reviews from Russian tourists who visited Marmaris.

A beach in an area with a warm sea (the fact is that in Marmaris there are many cold currents and springs along the coast), the absence of high waves, jellyfish and hedgehogs, small pebbles, palm trees, a gentle entrance are just some of the advantages of the Flamingo hotel.

Hotel Flamingo in Marmaris

Hotel Flamingo 4 * (Marmaris) is located three kilometers from the city center on the sea coast. From your own comfortable beach for vacationers offers stunning views of the colorful bay. In addition, a large clean pool is provided for adults, and a separate pool for children.



Rooms with sea views in a modern five-story building are cleaned daily and very diligently, which confirms many satisfied vacationers. The rooms are equipped with a split system, guests are given the opportunity to use the Internet, as well as a bathtub, shower, minibar, satellite TV (with Russian channels), a safe (additional charge).

The hotel has its own excellent restaurant and bar, there are show programs and day trips to Dalyan Kaunos, Ephesus, Turtle Beach, Datca Knidos, Pamukkale and small beautiful bays.

And now a little about spending

This type of hotel offers free breakfast and dinner for tourists. As a rule, in the morning it is cereal, various egg dishes, vegetables, fruits, coffee and tea. For dinner, chicken or fish with various side dishes, as well as ice cream and sweets are served. Drinks must be purchased separately.



For those who want to have lunch, there is a large selection of inexpensive restaurants, you can order food in the hotel itself, which will not be much more expensive.

You can order excursions from tour operators (their price will fluctuate around $ 60 per person) or arrange a ride on bicycles or ATVs ($ 40 each). A visit to the water park will cost $ 25, and a Turkish bath will cost vacationers the same price. Come to Turkey!

Who did not dream at one time to visit the famous resort Sunny Beach! Sandy beaches along the warm sea, for the purity of which they are awarded the Blue Flag of the European Union every year, excellent wines, juicy fruits watered by the sun, a friendly population - these are what make the Bulgarian coast especially attractive for tourists.

In the northern part of the resort, just 250 meters from the sea, the Flamingo 4 * hotel is located. It is located almost in the center, close to shops, restaurants, cafes and night clubs. What the Flamingo Hotel has to offer

Hotel Flamingo 4 * (Sunny Beach) is located on a large well-groomed area, surrounded by green trees. The six-story modern building is equipped with two elevators.

For your stay, you will find comfortable rooms with an additional folding sofa. Each has air conditioning as well as showers. There are rooms for people with disabilities.
