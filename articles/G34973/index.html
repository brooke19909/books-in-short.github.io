Side, Turkey: description of the resort. Photos, prices, reviews

Side is a rather popular and lively resort on the shores of the Mediterranean Sea, which is conveniently located 75 km from Antalya. "Side" in translation into Russian means "pomegranate". According to one version, the city received this name in honor of the goddess Artemis. Despite the decent distance from large ports, the infrastructure is quite well developed in Side (Turkey). Description of the resort, hotel prices and many other useful information you can find out more.



Climate features

This resort is characterized by a Mediterranean climate. Summer in the region is sultry and rather arid, and winters are mild, rainy and cool. The most significant rainfall is observed in December and January. The temperature of the winter months is + 14-16, rarely drops below +10. In the summer, the air temperature reaches more than 30, while the water warms up to +25. The most comfortable time to relax in Side is from May to October, at that time, as a rule, cloudless, hot and calm weather sets in.

Side Resorts

Tourists have the opportunity to relax in the following tourist centers: Manavgat, Kumkey, Side, Sogun, Kyzylach, Cholakra, Titreyengel, Evrenseki. For a detailed study of this region, you will need a map of resorts.

Local beaches

The nature of the resort is the majestic Toros Mountains, which smoothly pass into the golden sandy beaches. There is very little vegetation in this area, but from year to year Side is becoming greener and more comfortable thanks to the efforts of volunteers who plant trees and flowers in the city.

The most famous natural attraction is Manavgat Falls. It reaches 2 meters in height and 40 m in width. The picturesque waterfall is rapidly falling directly into the river.

The gentle Mediterranean Sea is what Turkey beckons. Photos of beaches that stretched along the old city, you can see below. In high season, the coast is literally crowded with tourists. There are two main beaches in the city: western and eastern. They are awarded the Blue Flag. The entrance to the sea is quite gentle and convenient. There are usually fewer people on the east beach, since a lot of water activities are offered on the west beach: sailing, water skiing, diving, boat excursions, snorkeling. 3 km from the city there is a secluded bay with azure water and a golden sand. Here you can retire from a huge stream of holidaymakers.



Historical sights

Side Resort (Turkey) is, without exaggeration, an open-air museum and the country's most important archaeological zone. Here, for example, a market square has been preserved since antiquity, where not only various goods were sold, but also slaves.

Side gem is an antique amphitheater, which was founded in the II century. In those days, the theater could accommodate up to 15 thousand people, gladiatorial battles were often held here. In the period from V to VI century, this building was used as a church. Now it periodically holds various festivals.

We continue to explore the amazing city of Side (Turkey). The description of the resort would be incomplete if we did not recall the temple of Apollo, which was built in the II century. The temple had a rectangular shape, and on all sides it was surrounded by a colonnade in a row. True, only 5 columns survived, since the temple was badly damaged during the earthquake that occurred in the 10th century.

This resort is also famous for the fact that, according to legend, it was here that Cleopatra made a romantic date with Marc Anthony. Since then, Side has been very popular with couples in love.



Amazing places around

Not only the Side resort in Turkey (whose photos are simply mesmerizing), but also its surroundings can boast of unique architectural objects. You can visit the ancient ruins of Perge, in Bujakshihil are the ruins of the mausoleum, theater, agora, church, Roman baths, the temple. The Altynbeshik Cave National Park, which is located north of Manavgat, is very popular.

Side - a resort for the whole family

You can go to this resort even with the smallest tourists. For children, all conditions are provided. And sandy beaches with convenient descent are also perfectly adapted for bathing kids. For high-quality pastime, the following villages are perfect: Sogun, Kizilot, Kyzylach and Titreengel.

When choosing a hotel, it is better to give preference to large hotels of well-known chains, here usually the children's infrastructure is quite well developed. Such hotels can offer many resorts in Side. 5 stars, however, will cost a lot. Therefore, with a limited budget, you can stay in economy class hotels. Among them there are also many worthy options with good service.
