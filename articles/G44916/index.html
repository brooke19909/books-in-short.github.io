Resort Hotel - one of the greatest hotels in Egypt.

Famous throughout Europe, Egypt has not lost its popularity at present and is considered the best resort among all inexpensive European tours. Now the resorts in Egypt combine all the most necessary types of entertainment and outdoor activities for the traveler: here is the warm coast of the Red Sea, and beautiful sandy beaches loved by all vacationers, as well as many interesting events and celebrations. From ancient times Egypt was appreciated not only for its cordiality, but also for its low prices for modern service. Here you will be offered for a small amount luxurious beaches and modern hotels.

It is worth noting that in Egypt there is a whole set of entertainment for the traveler. These are world famous sights, and attractive 5-star hotels and restaurants of various cuisines of the world. In general, absolutely everything in this attractive country is intended to delight the views of all vacationing tourists. But in order to rest in the resorts of Egypt was remembered only from the best side, it is necessary to choose the right hotel for check-in.

Hurghada hotels are in great demand among tourists in the summer. This resort is considered the most attractive in terms of price and quality, and the hotels here offer the vacationer all the conditions for a pleasant pastime. Only positive reviews about the service and the quality of service has the 5-star hotel Grand Resort Hurghada or Grand Resort. The resort hotel is very close to another famous resort hotel - the Grand Hotel. They are separated from each other only by the street and the coastline of the sandy beach.

The resort hotel is under the absolute management of German-Egyptian companies, which supply it with regular customers. It is worth noting the unique location of this hotel - the Grand Mall is built directly opposite it, where every tourist can hear daily live music, as well as the performance of folklore programs by local groups.

In the city of Hurghada, the resort hotel is represented by modern, comfortable beaches, the presence of many sites for outdoor activities, from the gym to the pool, as well as the original design, executed in oriental style. All tourists noted the high level of service provided. It should be said that the resort hotel is suitable for both family and youth recreation. Here, with the same success, you can spend an active holiday with participation in beach parties, as well as a relaxing sightseeing holiday with trips to the famous attractions of the resort.

The second most popular tourist destination is a 4-star hotel called Rehana Sharm Resort or Sharm Resort Hotel. It is located in the business center of Nabq Bay, and is located 18 km from the center of Sharm El Sheikh. Here, 393 comfortable rooms are designed for the attention of tourists, which are designed for both one person and a large family. For vacationers are provided free use of various sports facilities, as well as some spa-procedures. The main advantage of the hotel are restaurants with international cuisine.

Restaurant "Ala carte" is considered the best place in Egypt, where you will be offered wonderful dishes of fish and seafood. The famous Italian restaurant is considered to be the legislator of Italian cuisine, and they will offer their visitors the famous appetizers and desserts of Italian cuisine.
