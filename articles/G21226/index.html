Hotel "Kamenka", Yeysk: address, photos and reviews

Yeysk is a universal resort of the Azov coast. Thanks to the legendary salt and mud springs, he acquired the status of a health resort in Soviet times. Thousands of vacationers from different parts of the country and neighboring countries came to local sanatoriums and boarding houses in order to improve their health.

After the collapse of the Soviet Union, Yeisk was forgotten for a while. Modern tourists have found a worthy alternative - foreign resorts that can offer first-class service. Nevertheless, this place is still ideal for a therapeutic, beach holiday. There is shallow water and well-developed infrastructure, so families with young children will really like it.

Where to settle down? This is a matter of concern to all vacationers. There are enough boarding houses, hotels and hotels in the city. In addition, local residents rent rooms in the private sector. Sometimes you can even rent a whole house. In short, the choice is not so simple. One of the options is the Kamenka Hotel, Yeysk - its location. Of course, it is difficult to understand what conditions it provides for vacationers. Therefore, we propose to evaluate this option based on reviews from real vacationers.





About the hotel

On the Seaside Boulevard, just a 2-minute walk from the coast, the Kamenka Hotel (Yeysk) is located. Outwardly, it looks very presentable, and therefore attracts the attention of tourists. A favorable geographical position is something that all guests will appreciate. Dolphinarium, water park, children's entertainment center - all this is within walking distance from the hotel. In the evening, you can stroll along the promenade, which offers stunning views of the picturesque beauty of the resort.





Rooms

Mini-hotel "Kamenka" (Yeysk) - this name can be found in various tourist booklets. This status is justified by a small number of rooms. Vacationers are invited to stay in one of the 24 comfortable rooms of the Standard and Comfort categories. Some of them overlook the coast, due to which vacationers can enjoy the sunrises and see off the sunsets, sitting on the balcony.

The rooms are small - only 19 square meters. m. But due to the presence of exclusively functional pieces of furniture, a feeling of clutter is not created. Each room has a private bathroom, hot water is supplied around the clock. From the equipment in the room you can see satellite TV, a mini fridge and air conditioning, without which a sea vacation is unthinkable.

If you are used to relaxing in fashionable resorts and living in the best hotels, the atmosphere of the apartments may seem too simple. But, believe me, there is everything that is necessary for relaxation. Of course, equipment breakdowns periodically happen, for example, in some rooms, if you believe the reviews of tourists, sometimes the air conditioning buzzes. Nevertheless, he continues to cool the air. Sometimes there are interruptions in the supply of hot water, but they are also short-lived. If necessary, the hotel administration can provide an extra bed in the form of a small but quite comfortable folding bed.

Service

Hotel "Kamenka", which Yeysk can be proud of, offers good service. The price includes the use of secure parking, which will delight guests who have arrived at the resort by car. In season, all vacationers can count on free sun loungers. Agree, in the presence of a sand and pebble beach, sunbathing on it is much more pleasant.

In addition, the hotel has wireless high-speed Internet. The rooms are kept clean due to daily cleaning. Towels are changed in a day, bed linen - every 2-3 days. If it becomes necessary to replace it ahead of schedule, tourists will not be refused.





Hotel Territory

Hotel "Kamenka" (Yeysk) is ideal for families with kids. There is a good playground. In general, the territory is small, but well-maintained and comfortable. There is a recreation area with barbecue facilities and a gazebo. There are a lot of greenery on the territory. The balconies on the ground floor are entwined with a vine that creates a pleasant shadow. Some tourists may be frustrated by the lack of a swimming pool, but for hotels and hotels in Yeisk, located near the sea, this is the norm.

Beach and sea

The hotel and the beach have the same name. Most likely, the hotel received this name because of its proximity to this place of rest. All hotels on the beach "Kamenka" (Yeysk) are popular among tourists, especially with young children. And all because the best conditions on the coast are created here: a gentle entry into the water, a shallow and quickly warmed up sea, entertainment for children, showers and changing rooms. Unfortunately, the Kamenka Hotel (Yeysk) cannot offer its own fenced beach. Reviews of tourists from this do not become less positive. After all, the local city beach is quite spacious, clean (it is cleaned daily).

On the one hand, the coast is washed by the Yeisk estuary, on the other - the Taganrog Bay. That is why here you will definitely not encounter great depths and huge waves. If you are fond of water sports, such as surfing or diving, you should look for another place for your vacation. But for children there is a real expanse. When entering the water, small stones can be a little embarrassing, but after a few meters you will find an amazing sandy bottom. The sea here warms up in May. Therefore, parents definitely should not worry about the health of their children. Sports equipment rental is available on the beach at an additional cost.





To summarize

If you are interested in Yeysk hotels by the sea, Kamenka will be a good option. This hotel is ideal for families. The rooms are simple but clean and comfortable. Proximity to the sea is perhaps the main and undeniable advantage of this option.
