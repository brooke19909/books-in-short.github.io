Imereti Bay is a resort area. It includes the villages of Mirny, State Farm Russia, Fun and others. Imereti Bay is located near Sochi (thirty kilometers). It was in this area that the main competitions of the 2014 Olympics took place. And it was thanks to the fact that the International Competitions were held that the Imereti Bay was transformed, received a new excellent infrastructure. The updated resort area is ready to receive a large number of tourists.

Relaxation

Tourists who would like to spend a vacation in the Imereti Bay are primarily concerned about the issue of accommodation. To date, two segments can be noted. Beaches

The coast of Imereti Bay is a wide pebble strip. Note that pebbles are sometimes so small that it can be difficult to distinguish from sand. It is for the sake of the beaches that many tourists go to this resort area. The main advantage of the coast is few people. In addition, there is no railway track over the beach (as, for example, in other areas).

How to get to the complex

On a personal car

From the airport of Sochi should move along the street. Peace to the southwest 1.3 km. Drive to the junction where the Olympic rings are located. From the adler railway station you need to move along the street. Lenin to the southeast. After 1.2 km, the road will lead roundabout to the overpass. It should go along the same roundabout with the rings. From it it is necessary to turn in the direction of Vesyoloye and then go for about 6 km. Behind the Olympic Park railway station, you need to turn right along the sign toward Str. Champions. At the traffic light, after 530 m there will be a left turn. Then after another 600 m, at the T-junction, turn right. After 200 m, the entrance to the "Alexander Garden" will appear.

By public transport

Bus number 57 follows from the Adler railway station. It will take you to any block through the Olympic Park. You can take bus number 125 (125 "s"). He will take to the stop. "State Farm Russia". From there you can walk or take the bus number 57. To the stop "Olympic Park" bus number 124 "c". Then you can again transfer to route number 57. From the railway station also goes "Lastochka" (electric train) to the station. "Olympic Park".
