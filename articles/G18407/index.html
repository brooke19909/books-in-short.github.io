Hotels of Leninogorsk. Description. Reviews

Leninogorsk is a small city in the Republic of Tatarstan, relatively young by modern standards. Well maintained and green, has a developed infrastructure. The industry here is represented by enterprises in the oil industry. If you have to come here on a business trip, you will find comfortable living conditions in Leninogorsk hotels.

Hotel Aquamarine

You need to drive 2.6 kilometers from the hotel to the city center. Within walking distance are a supermarket, cafe, market. Car owners can benefit from free public parking. Wireless internet in the rooms is free. Upon request, an airport shuttle can be arranged for an additional fee. The hotel has a bar, gift shop, and a vending machine with drinks. If you are considering hotels in Leninogorsk where you can stay with pets, the Aquamarine hotel offers such a service. To do this, make a preliminary request. To accommodate guests, several categories of rooms are offered:

  • small single - 1900 rubles per day;
  • standard double - 1900 rubles a day;
  • double with a double bed - 2300 rubles per day;
  • Luxury - 2600 rubles per day.

The room rate is based on a full English breakfast. The hotel "Aquamarine" is located in Leninogorsk at the address: Avenue 50 years of Victory, house 17A. Reception is open 24 hours a day.

Hotel "ARS"

The location of the hotel is a quiet sleeping area. You just need a few minutes to walk to the forest park with attractions, the Ice Palace, the stadium. The hotel has a wood-burning bath, comfortable saunas with pools, a bar. Meals are organized in your own cafe. By prior request, they will help you with the organization of corporate parties, anniversaries. The territory of the hotel is guarded. There is free parking, a car wash. There is access to free internet. Each room has air conditioning, a minibar, a cooler with tea and coffee making facilities, and free toiletries. The hotel guests can use the laundry service, wake-up at the specified time. The number of rooms is small, only five rooms. The following options are offered for accommodation:

  • standard room - 2000 rubles per day;
  • Junior Suite Room - 2500 rubles per day;
  • luxury room - 3000 rubles per day.

The hotel "ARS" is located in Leninogorsk at the following address: Tukaya street, 15a.

Hotel "Oilman"

If you are considering Leninogorsk hotels in the city center, the Neftyanik Hotel is at your service. The hotel has its own cafe, sauna, gift shop. Here, for guests, a service is available for ordering air and railway tickets. Free private parking is available for guests with their own car. For accommodation, guests are offered rooms of various levels of comfort. Each is equipped with a fridge and TV. There are accommodation options without meals, with breakfast and full board. The cost of living in a standard twin room:

  • without food - 2831 rubles per day;
  • taking into account breakfast according to the "buffet" system - 2300 rubles per day;
  • full board - 4308 rubles per day.

The Neftyanik Hotel is located in Leninogorsk at the address: 49 Leningradskaya Street.

Hotel "Comfort"

From the mini-hotel Uyut to the city center is only one and a half kilometers. Guests enjoy free parking and free Wi-Fi. Shuttle services from or to the airport are available at an extra charge. A gift shop is located on site. There is a laundry, at your request the staff will iron clothes. Pets allowed. Upon request, breakfast will be delivered to your room. The cost of hotel accommodation starts at 1,500 rubles per day. There are four rooms in the mini-hotel; the following options are offered:

  • double with a double bed and fan;
  • This suite has air conditioning and seating area

Each room has a TV. Complimentary toiletries are provided. The Uyut Hotel is located in Leninogorsk at the address: 2 Suvorova Street.
