Ufa, residential complex "Dubrava": reviews, location

In order to provide comfort to every resident of such a metropolis as Ufa, the apartment must meet the basic requirements: good location, well-developed infrastructure, clean air, fenced area, own parking, recreation and leisure facilities. And all this can be offered by the new buildings of Ufa, the residential complex "Dubrava" in particular. If you are faced with a difficult choice of apartments, we strongly recommend that you take a closer look at the project. As part of this material, we will evaluate the complex, identify the advantages and disadvantages.





about the project

The group of companies "Dawn" proposed an excellent project - "Dubrava" (Ufa). The residential complex, reviews of which real interest holders do not stop leaving, combines the advantages of suburban and urban life. The developer paid attention to every detail, attracted qualified specialists to work on the project, which helped to create such a comfortable complex.

Dubrava in Ufa is a residential complex of 40 three-story buildings located in an ecologically clean area, only 25 km from the city. The developer of the project was inspired by modern European complexes with a fully developed infrastructure and all the necessary communications.

Construction Features

Low-rise construction is currently common and in demand. Moreover, it is supported by the state. It is important that the Dubrava residential complex in Ufa participates in the Affordable Housing for Young Families program, which is why all the necessary conditions for modern life and good rest for young families with children are created here: playgrounds and sports grounds, well-equipped areas for rest and walking in the fresh air, kindergarten, shops.





All houses are built from environmentally friendly Porikam bricks using a unique patented technology. Due to this, it is possible to achieve impeccable quality, reliability and provide the necessary heat and sound insulation properties.

Location

Surely you want to know where the Dubrava residential complex is located in Ufa. So, the developer chose a place remote from the hustle and bustle of the metropolis. The village of Suuk-Chishma is located in the Karmaskalinsky district, only 25 km from the city.

Many equity holders note in their reviews that they fell in love with the project at first sight. The place is picturesque, the complex is surrounded by greenery, which certainly can not be found in Ufa itself. The residential complex "Dubrava" is a great option for those who are thinking about their future, the future of their children.





Security

When choosing an apartment outside the city, it is important to pay special attention to the security system. In this case, the Dubrava residential complex (Ufa) was no exception. Its location provides for remoteness from the metropolis, which is why the developer not only fenced the territory of the complex, but also equipped it with a 24-hour video surveillance system, as well as a throughput system. Residents note that they quietly let the children go to the playground, knowing that nothing threatens their safety.

Transport accessibility

Do not be afraid of the distance of 25 km from Ufa. The residential complex "Dubrava" is perfectly located, so it will be comfortable to live here not only for families with a personal car. The city can be reached by public transport. Residents of the complex emphasize that a choice of more than 7 bus routes and minibuses running with minimal time intervals. A 10-minute drive from the complex has everything you need: large shopping and entertainment complexes, shops, social infrastructure.





Infrastructure

The developer on the territory of his complex tried to recreate all the infrastructure facilities in Ufa. The residential complex "Dubrava" offers residents a kindergarten, a mini market, a clinic, a bank branch, a pharmacy. Moreover, in the near future it is planned to open its own bakery, cafe and fitness center. All courtyards of the complex are gated, without through traffic - for the safety of children. And for car owners in the complex there will be a separate multi-level parking.





Apartments, layouts

All apartments in the Dubrava complex are rented with a fine and semi-finished finish. When purchasing housing, the owner has the right to choose the option of decoration. Moreover, if necessary, qualified craftsmen will translate into reality the design project of your apartment, help in the selection of building and finishing materials.

Owners who managed to visit the construction site note that work is being done quite quickly, the developer does not save on materials and has ensured clear quality control. That is why the funds invested in the project are fully justified.





A variety of planning decisions is what all owners note. One-room apartments ranging in size from 33 to 42 square meters are represented by a small kitchen, a separate room, a combined bathroom and a balcony. One bedroom apartments are a great solution for a modern family. The developer notes that this is the most popular option, especially among young families. Price policy

At the construction stage, it is possible to purchase a one-room apartment with decoration for only 930,000 rubles, the cost of the finished option starts from 1,400,000 rubles. Cooperation with the largest banks allows you to purchase real estate on favorable terms.
