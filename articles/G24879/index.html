Lake Akakul: recreation centers, prices, services

In the Chelyabinsk region, about 80 km from the city of Chelyabinsk, there is a natural lake Akakul. The silence and heady air of the coniferous forest, the purest water of the reservoir contributed to the opening of numerous recreation centers in these places. "Forest estate"

On the shore of the lake there is a large recreation area, which belongs to the complex "Forest Estate". Clean well-groomed area around the buildings, friendly staff will welcome visitors. Fully equipped cottages, buildings, gazebos will help vacationers feel as comfortable as possible on Lake Akakul. Recreation facilities of this type belong to the expensive price category. Of course, a lot depends on the chosen housing and length of stay. For example, a one-room apartment per day will cost 1850 rubles. It has a double bed, a bedside table, a TV and a shower. For double apartments you will have to pay more than 2000 rubles per day. This room is cozy, small in size, but there is everything you need to relax. Very light, pleasant classic interior, warm pastel colors. The administration makes discounts on long stays.



The overall impression that is created from visiting Lake Akakul (recreation centers operating in this territory monitor their reputation) is generally very good. In many respects, it was possible to achieve such reviews thanks to cozy comfortable rooms. Also in great demand here is a chic restaurant with delicious food and polite waiters. There is a coffee shop with aromatic coffee for every taste, a sauna, a swimming pool, a games room with billiards and a bowling alley. For guests with children, a children's room is provided. A massage service is available.

"Shore"

Tourists are advised to pay attention to the southern shore of Lake Akakul. Recreation facilities in the area are open all year. One of the most popular is the "Beach". The surrounding landscapes are insanely beautiful. All houses are designed for family vacations. They have heating installed, so even in severe frosts there will be no discomfort. The capacity of the houses is 2-4 people. On the territory of the built summer kitchen, which is equipped with everything necessary. However, organized meals are not provided, everyone prepares at their discretion. If you want to enjoy barbecue in the fresh air, then you can cook them in a designated area where the barbecue is installed. As a rule, it is designed for two adjacent houses. Some of them are covered with a canopy. There is impeccable order throughout, there is a Russian bathhouse, a volleyball court, a children's area with benches and tables. Large selection of playing material: checkers, backgammon, chess, cards. A wonderful beach. Like many other recreation centers on Lake Akakul, "Beach" provides rental of catamarans, boats. Fans of fishing are always with a catch. The bite in these places is excellent. The daily cost of one place is 350 rubles in the summer. You can get there by car, following the signs on the Kuznetskoye - Kyshtym road. And, importantly, each house has parking.



Rest house "Star"

At the very foot of the Ural Mountains there is an excellent Zvezdny holiday home. The complex itself consists of several brick buildings in 2 floors and small wooden houses. Vacationers here are waiting for comfortable rooms for 2-3 people. The rooms have a refrigerator and kettle, chairs, heater, table. The only inconvenience is the bathroom on the street. Meals are included in the price. The base is designed for 120 vacationers.

Tourists will appreciate the equipped territory. There is a wonderful bathhouse, you can rent a boat or a catamaran. For outdoor enthusiasts, a volleyball court and table tennis are available. Free barbecue area near the rooms, hot shower. There is a bar and cafe on site. The beach area is well equipped: umbrellas, sunbeds. Accommodation is quite expensive, the maximum cost reaches almost 3 thousand rubles per day.



"Berendeev Forest"

Virgin nature, birdsong, the cleanest air surrounds Lake Akakul. The recreation center "Berendeev Forest" is located on the northern coast of the reservoir. People come here who want to take a break from civilization. The cottages are ideal for families, as well as for events (birthday, wedding, etc.). The base is open all year. Depending on the season, they offer different types of entertainment:

  • In the summer - rental of catamarans and boats, a children's town with wonderful attractions, numerous excursions, walks in the woods, fishing, equipped playgrounds for active games (volleyball, football).
  • In winter, there is a skating rink, winter fishing, downhill skiing, skiing, snowboarding, and field trips (preliminary application).
