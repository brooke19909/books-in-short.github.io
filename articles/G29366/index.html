Construction technology

Taking into account the peculiarities of the region, the residential complex ‚ÄúI Am Romantic‚Äù is being built. Residents' comments note worries about the frequent and powerful winds in the Gulf of Finland and the occurrence of possible flooding in the mud because of the proximity of the water's edge. Builders, in order to eliminate problems associated with alluvial soils, scored piles 28 meters long under the foundation (with the current norm of 25 meters), and the foundation plates themselves were laid with a thickness of 700 mm. Therefore, the houses of the complex ‚ÄúI am romantic‚Äù should be centuries old.

For those who doubt, it is possible, as an argument, to cite examples of the fact that on Vasilievsky Island they have been washing soil and building buildings on them for a long time, and so far not a single house has been destroyed. They are erected in monolithic, as well as bearing 200 mm walls. External walls are made of aerated concrete. Their thickness is also 200 mm. Interroom walls in the project are 130 mm thick, therefore, sound insulation should be good. And only interior partitions are made of concrete panels 70 mm thick.

It is planned to insulate the facades in the buildings with a special 150-mm layer of insulation, decorate with decorative tiles, and the facade of the "Dreams" will be completely lined with wood.
