Val Gardena Ski Resort, Italy: Overview

Val Gardena ski resort is located in Italy, in a picturesque valley among the Dolomites. This is a famous place that attracts avid skiers and tourists. Here is the peak of an unusual form, which is called Sassolungo, which in Italian sounds like a "long stone."

Val Gardena (Italy) attracts thousands of tourists every year. He has been repeatedly awarded the title "the best resort of the Italian Alps", so you should definitely go there. And how to get there, what to visit and what to try, we will tell in this publication.

The spa towns of Val Gardena

If you are interested in what are the ski resorts of Italy, Val Gardena is what you need. There are many wonderful opportunities to have a great vacation. In the valley you can settle in one of the small towns - Selva Gardena, Santa Cristina and Ortisei.





Santa Cristina is located at an altitude of 1466 meters. This is a small city in which the World Cup is held every year at some stages. The most athletic is considered to be Selva Gardena. History and population of Val Gardena

Many in Italy and abroad know the ski resort of Val Gardena. Where is this place located? In the Dolomites where tourists from all over the world go.

Back in the 70s of the last century, Val Gardena became the venue for the world championship in skiing, so the World Cup is still held here at some stages. If you see with your own eyes the mighty peaks and their slopes, then in any case you want to go down the highway. And it doesn‚Äôt matter - you are a beginner or a professional in this matter. Until 1918, the valley was attributed to Austrian possessions, however, at the end of World War I, it became Italian property. Despite this, local traditions have survived to this day, and the local population is difficult to strictly attribute to Austria or Italy.

Val Gardena ski resort is located in the ethnographic region of Tyrol, and the culture of the locals is quite different from both Italian and Austrian. Since the city traditionally belongs to one of the oldest and most famous resorts in the Dolomites, the locals have long adapted to the constant neighborhood of tourists. Due to this, local residents have long developed a unique hospitality and cordiality. They speak here in the Tyrolean dialect, which can be described as a mixture of Italian and German. All signs and signs in the towns of Selva Garden, Santa Cristina and Ortisei are made simultaneously in two languages ‚Äã‚Äã- Italian, the official state language, and German, the use of which has long been fixed since Austrian times.

What about the Val Gardena Valley itself? The ski resort is located in the valley of the same name, the length of which reaches twenty kilometers. Equipment rental, the help of experienced instructors - all this will help you to safely and with great interest learn the basics of climbing art. For skiers, several special tracks are equipped for classic jogging. How to get by without a skating rink at a ski resort? Figure skating on a large ice space is also an advantage of the resort. Fans of extreme sports and beautiful scenery will love paragliding, paragliding. Ortisei also has a large water complex for water sports enthusiasts.

How to get to Val Gardena?

Val Gardena (Italy) is a popular ski resort; getting to it will not be difficult for you. Closest to Val Gardena are large cities such as Bolzano (at a distance of 40 kilometers) and Innsbruck (110 kilometers), in whose territory there are international airports. If you prefer to travel by train, then you need the Verona - Brennero - Innsbruck - Munich railway line. Your stop is Brunico Station, just thirteen kilometers from the resort. In both cases, both from the plane and from the train, you can get to the final destination of the trip by bus or get off at Autobahn 22 and catch a car there.
