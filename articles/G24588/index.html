Palace Hotel

When choosing Athens to relax, hotels by the sea can be divided into those that do not have a park area and those that are surrounded by gardens. One of these hotels is the Palace Hotel, which is located in the middle of a palm grove on the seashore.

Standard rooms are equipped with a bathroom, upholstered furniture, air conditioning, TV and telephone. Reviews of tourists note a cozy relaxation area near the pool and an excellent menu in the restaurant, striking a variety of dishes.

The hotel is located 30 minutes by tram from the center of Athens, the stop of which is right next to the entrance. 10 minutes to the nearest store, so there is silence around, allowing you to fully relax.
