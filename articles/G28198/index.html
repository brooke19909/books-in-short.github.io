Cheap hostels in Minsk: addresses, contacts, room descriptions, photos and reviews

If you are in the capital of Belarus on a business trip or just want to get to know this city and its sights better, there is a large selection of cheap hostels in Minsk at your service. A description of hotels and reviews of tourists who were able to appreciate the hospitality of the Belarusian capital and its residents will help you navigate and find the suitable accommodation option.

The cheapest hostels in Minsk are not located in the city center, but convenient transportation allows you to easily get anywhere in the city.

Happy Hostel

This small but very cozy hotel is located on the street. Mozyrskaya, 37A, which is 4 km from the center of the Belarusian capital. For budget accommodation, guests are provided with rooms for 12 people, the cost of living per day is 420 rubles.

Guests can use the shared bathroom and shower, as well as a kitchen with a very cheerful and optimistic interior. It is executed in greenish-yellow tones and in the morning it charges with positive for the whole coming day.

For a fee, guests can enjoy airport transfer, room service and ironing. Throughout the hostel there is free Wi-Fi, a barbecue area. The nearest bus stop is a 5-minute walk away. Of all the hotels and hostels in Minsk, this option of accommodation is referred to cheap and decent tourists.

Dreamy castle

The hostel in the center of Minsk is really somewhat reminiscent of a medieval castle, and it is located almost in the center of the city. His exact address: Independence Ave., 31.

8-bed rooms are available for settlement, the price of a daily stay is 500 rubles per person. Travelers who choose this hotel need to keep in mind that they can only pay in cash for their stay at the hostel, credit cards are not accepted.

And although the bathroom is shared on the floor, many guests noted that the bathroom has good ventilation and almost new plumbing. There is a laundry, parking for cars, and a fully equipped kitchen.

Former guests in their reviews of this hostel note its convenient location (3 minutes to the nearest metro station) and the friendliness of the staff.

Guest house Andrew Loft

If you are looking for a cheap hostel in Minsk near the central part, we advise you to pay attention to this guest house. One of its advantages, according to former guests, is its small size and silence. It is located at: Independence Ave., 43, apt. 12, which is a 6-minute walk from the metro station "Victory Square".

For 620 rubles, tourists are provided with a berth in a bunk bed with a soft and comfortable mattress. Despite the fact that this guest house belongs to the budget category, each guest is given free slippers and toiletries. Bathroom and toilet are on the floor, there is a shared kitchen where you can prepare food.

Visitors to this hostel in their reviews note another positive feature of the guest house - bike rental. Of the minuses, they pay attention to the fact that the building where the hostel is located does not have a sign, as well as the lack of parking for private vehicles.

Your hostel

Among inexpensive hostels in Minsk, tourists respond positively to this option. This guest house is perfect for those who do not want to settle in the noisy central part of the city, but are looking for a quieter place.

For 430 rubles, a tourist will have a separate bed in a room for 10 people, a shared bathroom and a kitchen. Guests can pay for accommodation only in cash, credit cards are not accepted.

Having a good time in the fresh air, vacationers can on the terrace or in the garden, there is a special barbecue area.

Many guests note the well-coordinated work of the staff, a 24-hour reception desk and the ability to rent a car. The pluses include the presence of a spacious living room with fireplace and board games, for those who like to read there is a library.

Your Hostel is located at: First Lane Skoriny, 28, apt. 7. The staff speaks Russian, German and English.
