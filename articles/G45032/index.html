Beeline: All Inclusive. All Inclusive L Rate: reviews

The Beeline company is one of the leaders in the Russian cellular market . Tariff offers from this operator are presented in a wide range. One of the most popular lines is All Inclusive. What are its features? What are the specifics of the evolution of tariffs in this line? What services within the framework of this offer is it particularly beneficial to use?

Features of the tariff line

The features of the Beeline All-Inclusive tariff line are that the operator provides subscribers with the opportunity to use communication services in a comprehensive format as part of a monthly fee. That is, the services included in the tariff (voice, SMS, mobile Internet) would be more expensive if each subscriber bought separately. For example, the Beeline tariff ‚Äú All Inclusive L‚Äù in one of the very first versions suggested that for a monthly fee of 500 rubles per month (Moscow region), a user would be able, firstly, to talk 300 minutes when calling any operator‚Äôs numbers, firstly secondly, write as many SMS and MMS as you like and, thirdly, download up to 600 megabytes of data from the Internet - without speed limits. The only thing that perhaps should be noted is that there is not too much of the specified amount of Internet traffic, even if you use the network from a mobile device. Therefore, this type of tariff is calculated more likely for users who do not have a special need for online resources.





Our thesis regarding the limited adaptability of Beeline All-inclusive tariffs to the Internet can confirm the following fact. Another offer of the line - XXL - suggests that for 1000 rubles of subscription (Moscow region) a person will receive 900 minutes of calls, an unlimited number of messages, however, the amount of prepaid online traffic at full access speed will not change - 600 megabytes.

Transition is not free

It is also worth noting that switching to Beeline All Inclusive from other operator tariffs may not be free (although it is possible that the company's policy may change over time). In this case, the amount on the balance sheet must exceed that which is required to be paid upon transition.

The goal is optimization

Top managers of the operator also spoke about the features of tariffs of the Beeline line of All Inclusive. In particular, in one of the statements it was noted: the goal of introducing the tariff is to provide customers with the opportunity to use several services at the same time within the framework of optimal prices. At the same time, the proposals were allegedly arranged in such a way that, by default, clients should not experience the need to connect any additional options. To the tariffs of the line in question, both individuals and organizations can connect.





The specifics of the monthly fee of the type "All inclusive": the amount is debited by the day (with a prepaid system, in other cases - monthly). In some configurations (for example, when concluding an annual contract), subscribers can receive a significant discount on the use of services. But! If a person did not spend some minutes during a billing period (say, a month), then they will not be carried over to the next.

Tariff: prepaid or postpaid?

We said above that subscribers have two options for connecting to line tariffs. The first is prepayment. Its essence is that communication services are provided by the operator with a positive balance in the account. The second option is postpay. When using this format, the availability of funds plays a secondary role - all expenses can be compensated later, based on the invoice issued by the company. Which of the two options for applying the tariff for the All-Inclusive line can be more profitable?

Imaginary cheapness

The opinions of experts on this subject are mixed. However, analysts say - everything is very individual. As for the benefits in absolute terms (that is, calculated on the basis of the ratio of costs to received services), postpaid tariffs, as a rule, look preferable. Actually, the Beeline company, as we already noted above, is inclined to configure its offers in this way - at a discount in case of postpaid registration. There is another side to the benefits. The fact is that not all subscribers manage to accurately monitor their expenses on cellular communications. And therefore, the invoice sent by the company at the end of the month can be quite impressive, but at the same time in many cases smaller than if a person used the prepaid system. An unexpected "overspending" often occurs, for example, when using the Internet - when a subscriber simply forgets to leave a mobile browser, and data continues to be downloaded from online.





Some prefer a compromise option - use "auto payment" with a prepaid system. That is, if the funds on the balance run out, replenishment occurs automatically from a bank card or electronic wallet - most operators allow this to be done, and Beeline tariffs are no exception: reviews by many subscribers confirm that such a system works without failures.

However, there are a large number of users who prefer the postpaid option for various reasons. Beeline tariffs "All inclusive XL, XXL" and more "younger" of their "brothers" allow you to use this format of interaction of the subscriber with the operator.
