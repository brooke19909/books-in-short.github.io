Nam Hong Hotel 2 * (Vietnam, Nha Trang): description and reviews

Travelers choosing Vietnam for a beach holiday usually always come to Nha Trang. It is rightfully considered the local tourist capital. There are all conditions for a relaxing and active holiday. Nha Trang is famous for the most noisy discos in Vietnam, and also has amazing sandy beaches with clear sea water. Inexpensive restaurants, bars and cafes open all over the place, serving unique local cuisine. And Vietnamese markets are sure to appeal to shoppers. Coming here, tourists can stay at the Nam Hong Hotel 2 * hotel (Nha Trang). What does he offer his guests?

Vacation in Vietnam: hotel location

This small hotel is located on one of the lively city streets of Nha Trang, just 25 meters from the public beach. To get to it, tourists need to cross a small road. The beach here is sandy, and the entrance to the sea is gentle. It is worth knowing that sun loungers and umbrellas are not given out for free. They can be bought in a store or right on the coast. The hotel is located in the northern part of the city, which is remote from large night clubs. The tourist infrastructure in this area is less developed, but at night it is much quieter and calmer. Here you can order a course of wellness services or just swim in the indoor pool if the water in the sea is cold. The ancient temple of the sun, Hon Do, built in the 16th century, is 1.5 km from the hotel.





Near the Nam Hong Hotel 2 * complex (Vietnam) a large number of shops are open, and a large market is 3 km from here. The nearest airport is 42 km away. Tourists, buying a ticket, often pay for the transfer to the hotel. The average travel time is about 1 hour. You can also get to the hotel by taxi, but in this case the trip will be more expensive. Economy travelers can get to Nha Trang by minibus. They run directly from the airport, and the ticket price does not exceed 200 rubles.

basic information

This small hotel was built in 1982. Its appearance is almost no different from most Asian hotels. In a single building there are 5 floors, which house a total of 29 rooms. The hotel does not have its own territory. Next to it are also several hotel complexes. Registration of tourists starts here from 13:00. As a rule, it is customary to leave a passport here as a deposit. He will be returned to you after the eviction. No cash deposit will be charged. Noon is the checkout time at Nam Hong Hotel 2 * (Nha Trang). If desired, tourists can extend their stay until the evening, if the hotel has free rooms. The service is provided for a fee.





Settling with children is not prohibited, but there are no special conditions for relaxing with them. Guests under 7 years old will not be charged for accommodation if they sleep with their parents in the same bed. Cots for babies are not provided. It is also forbidden to check in with animals of any species. Hotel administrators do not speak Russian. Therefore, before the trip, you should take along a phrasebook in Vietnamese or English. The hotel accepts MasterCard and Visa credit cards, as well as cash in local currency.

Number of rooms

Nam Hong Hotel 2 * can not be called a large tourist complex. It offers a total of 29 rooms. They cannot boast luxurious decoration or a large area. This apartment is suitable for people who are comfortable with minimalism. They all have their own bathroom. Some rooms have a balcony. You can enjoy sea, mountain, or city view from the room. Maids clean the living rooms every day. Towels and linen are usually changed twice a week.

Guests making a tour here can choose one of the following types of apartments:

  • Single Room. Its area is 20 square meters. m. The living room has a bedroom and living area. There is a double bed. A tile is laid on the floor. Superior rooms also have a balcony with a dining set.
  • Quadruple Room. It consists of a spacious bedroom with two double beds. The floor is also tiled. It offers sea views. There are apartments with a balcony. Room size is 30 square meters. m

Details of equipment

The hotel provides a minimum set of amenities in the room. Nevertheless, these apartments are perfect for a comfortable overnight stay. So, in hot weather, air conditioning is turned on in all living rooms. It is central, therefore regulated by the hotel administrator. A flat-screen TV is usually located opposite the bed. It broadcasts cable channels, most of which are intended for Asian viewers. There are in the hotel rooms Nam Hong Hotel 2 * (Nha Trang) and a direct dial telephone. With it, you can call the reception. International calls are made at an additional cost.





Free Wi-Fi is available in each of the rooms. Its speed is not too high, but it is enough to send letters or view social networks. You can leave your things and clothes in a spacious closet and bedside tables. An empty minibar is also provided free of charge, but it is not included in the price. The bathroom does not have a set of cosmetics, but there are slippers, disposable toothbrushes and towels. The bathroom is equipped with a shower, toilet and washbasin. A hairdryer and ironing facilities are not provided.

Hotel Infrastructure

The hotel provides a small selection of services. They are mainly designed for those tourists who use the Nam Hong Hotel 2 * complex as an overnight stay. We list the main infrastructure facilities that hotel guests can use:

  • Free car parking. Pre-ordering a seat is not required. Car and motorcycle rentals are also available.
  • Wireless Internet. It is provided in all public areas of the hotel for free. The password for connection can be requested from the administrator.
  • Lockers for each room. They can leave clothes, bags and even valuables. You do not need to pay for their use.
  • Ironing service. The hotel can clean your clothes upon request. However, you cannot wash clothes in a hotel.
  • Sale of bus tickets and arranging airport transfers.
  • 24 hour currency exchange.
  • A modern elevator that can always be used by tourists living on the top floors.

Catering

The cost of accommodation at the Nam Hong Hotel 2 * usually includes breakfast only. However, tourists who rested here earlier are advised to take a ticket without food. The hotel's small restaurant does not offer a buffet. Food is available on special coupons. Visitors can choose scrambled eggs, scrambled eggs, garlic bread and soup. There are no fruits and desserts on the menu. Drinks are also not included, but tea or coffee can be purchased at breakfast for a fee. The quality of the food is not very good, so most guests eat outside the hotel.

Approximate cost of living

The cost of living at the Nam Hong Hotel 2 * hotel (Vietnam, Nha Trang) primarily depends on the selected room. The cheapest are single apartments. Their price is approximately 1300 rubles per night. Two adults can stay in the rooms at the same time. Thus, a week of living in such a room will cost 9,100 rubles for two. Family apartments are a little expensive. Their minimum price starts from 2000 rubles. Given that 5 people can live in them at once, we can say that this is also a fairly economical option for settling. A week of relaxation in a family room will cost only 14,000 rubles, excluding meals and other additional services.





Positive reviews

Guests who previously had a rest at the Nam Hong Hotel 2 * hotel (Vietnam) leave mixed reviews. Stay here was to the liking of tourists who love outdoor activities outside the hotel. In their opinion, the price here corresponds to the service offered. In their reviews, they describe the following advantages of the complex:

  • Good location. Nearby is the beach, several cafes and a grocery store.
  • At the reception are very polite girls who understand English well.
  • The rooms are clean. It is held daily. Towels are changed on time if put on the floor.
  • Large and comfortable beds in the room. The furniture is in good condition.
  • Wifi works around the clock . The Internet supports good speed even in the evening.




Negative reviews

Being a two-star hotel, Nam Hong Hotel 2 * reviews often get negative. According to tourists, the administration of the complex should pay attention to the following disadvantages:

  • The hotel restaurant is often closed, so breakfast can not be taken, although it is included in the price.
  • The portions in the restaurant are very small, so you often have to have additional breakfast in the cafe. A limited selection of dishes, and the food itself is not very tasty.
  • Hot water is not always available. Instead, they serve a little warm, which is rather cold to wash in.
  • Air conditioning in some rooms does not work. The administration did not even try to fix it or relocate guests to other apartments.
  • Close shower, water from which constantly flows to the floor, pouring everything around.

Instead of an afterword

After reading the description and reviews of the Nam Hong Hotel 2 * hotel, we can say that it will be a good option for an inexpensive vacation in Vietnam. The distance from the center provides a quiet sleep, but it forces tourists to spend money on daily trips by taxi or bus. The hotel is suitable for beach and sightseeing holidays. Most often, youth companies and adult couples without children stop here. The national composition of the guests is mixed. The quality of the services provided here is not too high, but the price is considered one of the lowest in Nha Trang.
