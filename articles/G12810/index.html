A brief overview of the cafe "Sea buckthorn" in Kazan: menu, reviews, interior

Cafe "Sea buckthorn" in Kazan - an institution with a simple name and a friendly atmosphere inside. It is often visited by whole companies - here you can meet friends, have a family dinner or have a romantic date with your loved one.





Interior

Inside the cafe "Sea buckthorn" (Kazan) is made very original, in its interior there are a lot of fascinating details.

The institution welcomes its guests in a huge and very comfortable room, which is designed for a total of 141 people at a time. At the entrance of each visitor there is a reception desk, behind which there is an administrator - he can guide each guest to the ordered table.





The first original interior detail can be seen right at the entrance - on a white wall decorated with masonry, there is a green bicycle. Going into the main hall, you can notice another feature - a green lawn on the ceiling. It also has several inverted baskets with fruits, berries and mushrooms - almost all guests note the originality of such an idea by an interior designer.

Guests of the cafe "Sea buckthorn" in Kazan can stay in any zone - they are all different and are designed for every taste of the visitor. Here you can meet a corner with a glass table, the base for which are wooden bars, and you can also sit between bright bookcases against the backdrop of a huge British flag. All places for guests are very comfortable, they are presented mainly in the form of sofas and soft chairs.

Guests at Sea Buckthorn can watch music videos or sports programs broadcast on TV in the gym.

Kitchen





The menu of the Sea Buckthorn cafe (Kazan) invites guests to taste the dishes of European cuisine that visitors really like. Here they can offer very original appetizers, among which there are both cold (garlic croutons, herring with potatoes and beetroot salad, salt-trio with Borodino bread, mini-sandwiches carousel, salmon sliced) and hot (zucchini baked with porcini mushrooms , calf tongue with sauce "Hunter", "Cocktail" with crayfish and salmon, wings "Fiery" with homemade adjika). In addition to appetizers, salads are presented from light dishes (chicken breast with bacon and avocado, with veal roast beef and vegetables, pike perch and cream sauce, chicken and porcini mushrooms, and fresh vegetables).

For hot, the Sea-buckthorn cafe (Kazan) serves branded dumplings (Siberian, with beef, salmon, chicken) and dumplings (with cottage cheese, cabbage, potatoes, cherries). In addition, there is a large assortment of meat (veal medallions, lamb skewers, pork escalope, Skoblyanka with chicken offal, chopped chicken schnitzel) and fish dishes (salmon steak, home-made pike perch, rainbow trout on a spit). The first one presents several types of soups ("Ukrainian" borsch with donuts, tomato with turkey, cream cheese with veal, solyanka with chicken giblets, mushroom).





For lovers of sweets there is a decent selection of desserts (signature cheesecake "Sea buckthorn", sorbet with nuts and dried fruits, pies, halva with berry jelly).

Entertainment for guests

In the cafe "Sea Buckthorn" (Kazan), new conditions are regularly created in order to make guests interested and comfortable in its walls.

Background music is constantly playing in the hall, which allows you to relax and tune in to a positive mood. It also often hosts entertainment shows and culinary master classes.





Visitors to the cafe have the opportunity to read books from the cupboards while preparing the dish they ordered, or play board games ("Mafia", "Monopoly", "Jenga"). There is also a hookah bar where guests can enjoy a delicious smoky smoke.

Visitors reviews

The guests of the institution leave their reviews about the cafe "Sea buckthorn" in Kazan. They can be seen on the pages of social networks or on special sites. Often in their comments they mention deliciously cooked food. According to the guests, "Sea buckthorn" is one of the best establishments in all of Kazan. Also, a lot of comments are devoted to the original interior of the institution. According to guests, it is very bright and at the same time comfortable.





Visitors pay special attention to the prices indicated on the menu - they are very low. On average, the bill per person does not exceed even 800 rubles, which is good news.

Address of the institution and mode of operation

Cafe "Sea buckthorn" is ready to receive guests in its hall any day of the week from 11 a.m. to midnight. Every Friday and Saturday, the establishment closes at one in the morning.





Cafe "Sea buckthorn": Kazan, Pushkin street 1/55, it is located on the second floor of the building. It can be easily reached by any form of public transport, including the metro (the nearest station is Tukaya Square).
