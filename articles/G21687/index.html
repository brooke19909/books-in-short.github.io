Welna Eco Spa Resort, Tarusa: overview, description, features and reviews

Welna Eco Spa Resort offers modern comfortable conditions for relaxation in an atmosphere of Russian traditions and untouched nature.





Hotel Description

Welna Eco Spa Resort is a modern hotel complex for those who are a fan of a quiet and relaxing holiday surrounded by nature. The remoteness of the hotel from the city limits does not mean the absence of modern conveniences and benefits of civilization. The hotel has everything you need for a comfortable and full stay and relaxation.

Here, in the lap of nature, you can not only relax away from city noise, but also improve your health, because Welna Eco Spa Resort has a modern spa and a large wellness center. Also, you will certainly enjoy beautiful modern rooms of various categories.





Hotel location

Welna Eco Spa Resort is located in Tarusa, Kaluga Region, 2 km from its historic center. Once in the city, you can touch the beautiful by visiting the museum dedicated to the poetess Marina Tsvetaeva, as well as a large art gallery. Also, after driving 2 km, you can swim or go fishing in the Oka.

Hotel Welna Eco Spa Resort (Tarusa) is located at a distance from the main transportation hubs, which can be described by the following numbers:

  • bus station - 2.5 km;
  • railway station - 30 km;
  • Domodedovo International Airport - 120 km.




Hotel Rooms

Welna Eco Spa Resort Hotel consists of several buildings and cottages decorated in the style of old Russian towers, inside which are comfortable modern rooms. Guests are offered the following accommodation options:

  • Standard rooms are available in 19 apartments, 13 of which have a balcony. All rooms are located in a new freestanding building. The rooms are very small (25 sq. M.), Therefore, from the furniture in them there is only a double bed or a pair of single.
  • The hotel has 15 Studio apartments, which occupy two buildings. The rooms are 36 square meters. m, which, thanks to the original interior solution, is divided into a bedroom and living room area. It also has a private balcony.
  • The rooms of the "Suite" category (number - 8) are spacious two-room apartments of 50 square meters. m. This is perhaps the best option for a relaxing family vacation.
  • The size of the rooms of the category "Apart" can vary from 39 to 59 square meters. m, and all of them in the hotel 4. They occupy a separate cottage. These numbers consist of two (and one - of three) rooms. The living room has a small and comfortable kitchen area. Also, guests staying in apartments of this category have access to a spacious terrace of 35 square meters. m
  • The hotel complex has three family cottages, the main feature of which is the presence of a full kitchen with a microwave, refrigerator, electric kettle and the necessary set of dishes. Also a pleasant moment is the availability of your own relaxation area.
  • In a quiet and secluded place is a cottage of the "Romantic" category, designed specifically for newlyweds or couples in love. It has two rooms, each of which consists of a bedroom and a living room.
  • Two cottages of the "Grand" category are characterized by a large area and, as a result, good capacity, which makes it a great option for relaxing large companies and families. Each of them has several bedrooms, a living room, a kitchen, as well as its own courtyard.




Room Amenities

Eco-hotel Welna Eco Spa Resort aims to ensure that its guests can enjoy a leisurely and relaxed vacation in the lap of nature and, at the same time, without feeling a lack of urban comfort. That is why they make sure that the rooms have everything they need, so that their inhabitants feel at home. So, in the apartments of the hotel there are the following amenities:

  • the bathroom combines a toilet and a shower, as well as a hairdryer, slippers and a disposable cosmetic set;
  • in each room there is a tea set in the form of a service, an electric heater for water, as well as a large assortment of flavoring variations of a fragrant drink;
  • Hotel staff will replenish your minibar if necessary;
  • almost all apartments have a balcony or terrace;
  • modern widescreen satellite TV;
  • free high-speed wifi;
  • telephone for calls inside the hotel, within the city, as well as outside it.

Hotel infrastructure

If you want to relax and unwind, then you should definitely spend your weekend or vacation at the Velna Hotel. Welna Eco Spa Resort has planned its infrastructure so that here you can completely escape from the worries, city noise and worldly bustle. So, hotel guests can take advantage of the following features:

  • several cozy fireplace rooms where you can bask in the fire and drink a glass of wine for a friendly conversation;
  • banquet room for celebrations or business events;
  • a cozy forest park, which belongs to an ecologically clean conservation area;
  • special venues for weddings and other special occasions in the open air;
  • small contact zoo;
  • the possibility of paid fishing;
  • special areas for picnics equipped with cozy gazebos and barbecue facilities;
  • guarded parking area.




Entertainment program and opportunities for healing

Welna Eco Spa Resort (Tarusa) offers its guests many opportunities for entertainment, as well as to restore their health and well-being:

  • Wellness program developed by professional doctors;
  • You can relax and become more beautiful by regularly visiting the spa;
  • observe Russian traditions, as well as cleanse your soul and body in a wooden bath;
  • You can take a steam bath in a Finnish or salt sauna;
  • you can bring your figure into shape in the gym;
  • You can make your body more flexible, and you can also come to a state of harmony through yoga classes;
  • You can actively spend time playing paintball;
  • You can explore the local natural beauty during a boat trip on the river;
  • animators work at the hotel;
  • on weekends the restaurant hosts an entertainment show;
  • for children there is a playground in the open air, as well as a special room in a warm room;
  • a chic restaurant complex that has a large elegant dining room, as well as a cozy outdoor terrace.




Positive reviews

Fans of eco-friendly relaxation should definitely go to Welna Eco Spa Resort. Reviews about him, posted on specialized resources, contain a lot of thanks and positive opinions:

  • all furniture, electronics and plumbing in the rooms are brand new and serviceable;
  • good breakfasts, the menu of which corresponds to the concept of a healthy diet;
  • pleasant atmosphere in the spa: everything is very clean, sterile, comfortable, and the work of specialists is simply at the highest level;
  • beautiful, well-groomed, green territory;
  • calm atmosphere, as well as traditional Russian colorful design;
  • An excellent entertainment for both children and adults is the contact zoo, in which animals are well-groomed and well-fed;
  • good opportunities for families: spacious rooms, fishing, a picnic area.




Negative reviews

In the hotel business, unfortunately, everything is not perfect, which is confirmed by the example of the Welna Eco Spa Resort. The reviews of tourists contain some comments and negative comments that are related both to the quality of service and to the personal preferences of the guests:

  • lunch dishes are served almost cold;
  • food in the restaurant is unreasonably expensive (the prices are clearly too high due to the remote location of the hotel);
  • despite the high cost of food in the restaurant, the portions are incredibly small (it is simply impossible to eat them);
  • the design of the rooms does not match the one stated in the brochures;
  • entertainment is practically absent (those that are, are designed mainly for children);
  • poor quality of cleaning;
  • Towels and bed linen are not changed during your stay (except when guests strongly demand this).
