An original bouquet of sweets for beginners

Birthdays, New Year's holidays, corporate events and other memorable days are complete without gifts. The most commonplace is a bouquet of flowers, a bottle of champagne and a luxurious box of chocolates. But if we want to surprise someone, then we choose a unique or original gift. For example, to impress a woman, you can buy a bouquet of sweets. Chocolate roses are not only beautiful, but fresh, original and bright. You can be sure that the woman will be simply shocked and will not forget such a gift. After all, you can admire a chocolate bouquet for a long time or enjoy its taste.



Even ordinary caramels or chocolates, originally packaged in a beautiful wrapper and brightly decorated with fresh flowers, ribbons or toys, can form a luxurious bouquet of sweets. Photos of such bouquets, giving a whole firework of pleasant emotions, can be seen in the catalogs of companies involved in the preparation of candy bouquets or compositions.

We will show all our imagination and try to make the simplest gift. Let it be a wonderful bouquet of sweets for beginners, but made with love and desire to bring joy to loved ones or loved ones. After all, getting manual work will be much more pleasant.

The process of creating a candy bouquet is real creativity. Before you make a bouquet of sweets for beginners, you need to stock up on everything you need. This can be a vase, flower pot, basket or some original vessel for a bouquet, wrapping paper of different colors, ribbons, greens, scotch tape, long and short wooden sticks, an oasis, scissors and glue.

Depending on who the bouquet is being created for, or on a sketch invented, various other decorative elements may be needed: decorative butterflies, feathers, small soft toys. Well and, of course, flowers and sweets suitable for composition in beautiful wrappers.



Creating a bouquet of sweets for beginners is quite simple:

1. We glue a flower pot or some other vessel with wrapping paper. Cut an oasis of size suitable for the pot and paste it. The oasis should be tightly attached to the pot.

2. From multi-colored packaging paper, cut small squares. Putting them in layers, we pierce, insert the skewer and wrap it with adhesive tape at the bottom. This is the decor for our bouquet.

3. Wrap a wide variety of sweets in wrapping paper, then string it on a wooden stick and decorate it with a decor made on top. You can wrap the candies already packed in paper with a flower grid and tie a ribbon at the bottom.

4. You can decorate sweets in another way: we make a cone out of colored paper, then put the candy on a stick in it. We glue it with tape below, and wrap the stick with green paper.



5. Cooked decor and beautifully decorated sweets are inserted into a vase with an oasis. You can arrange sweets arbitrarily, as your fantasy tells you. Empty spaces can be filled with paper decor.

6. When the candies are in their places, you can additionally decorate the pot, for example, with a net, elegant bows, translucent taffeta or beautiful ribbons. And in the finished bouquet, insert green leaves or fresh flowers. This will give the sweet masterpiece a special charm and even greater resemblance to a real bouquet.

As you can see, it is very simple to make such a bouquet of sweets for beginners. It will not fade for a long time, and will delight a loved one for a long time.
