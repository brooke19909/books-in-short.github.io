sights

The city welcomes the guests of the Amursky Bay hotel with the sights worthy of their attention:

  • Arch Tsesarevich. It was built in 1891, after the Vladivostok City Council decided to perpetuate the memory of the visit of Vladivostok by the future Emperor Nicholas II. The author of the project was a military specialist Konovalov. In 1927-1930, during the Soviet era, the arch was destroyed. It was recreated in 2003.
  • Vladivostok fortress. This complex of defensive structures was built at the turn of the 19-20th centuries. The construction of the fortress was not completed due to the outbreak of the First World War and then the October Revolution.
  • "Monument to the fighters for Soviet power in the Far East" (located in the central square). On a high pedestal (30 m) there is a figure of a fighter holding a waving banner in his right hand. The figure is facing the sea, it is a symbol of the liberation of the native land from Japanese interventionists.




  • Sports embankment. This object is one of the oldest and most beloved places of recreation for citizens and guests of the city. Before the October Revolution, there were baths; after the war, the largest Dynamo stadium in the city was built here. In 1953, the beach and sports grounds were equipped at this place.
  • Church of the Protection of the Holy Virgin. Located in Pokrovsky park. In September 1902, he was consecrated by Bishop Eusebius. At the beginning of the XX century. the church was famous as the second largest temple in Vladivostok. The five-domed Church of the Intercession accommodates 700 people; on holidays, a thousand parishioners also fit in the temple.
  • The Millionaire. This is the customary name for the spontaneous "Chinatown" that arose at the end of the 19th century. To this day, the legendary quarters have reached from that time almost unchanged.

  • When booking, a floating price system is used: the cost of rooms may change, depending on the degree of occupancy of the hotel.

Check-in / out terms

In case of a guaranteed early check-in (before 09:00), a surcharge is paid - the full price of the room, from 09:00 to 14:00 - half the cost of the room is paid, with an unguaranteed early check-in, if there are free rooms, a surcharge is not required. For late check-out (from 12 to 18) you need to pay half the price of the stay, for late check-out after 18:00 you must pay the full price of the room.

The price for an extra bed is 600 rubles / day (the price includes accommodation and breakfast).

Children under 6 years stay free with parents (breakfast is included, no berth is provided). Children from 6 to 12 years old are accommodated with their parents for free (no bed is available). Payment for food at an adult rate is paid at a 50% discount.

Room rates include breakfast. Room reservation is free of charge. Prices include VAT.

Reviews

Guests note neat rooms with modern, functional furniture. The technique, they said, is on par.
