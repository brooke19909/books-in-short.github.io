Where is?

This 3-star hotel is located in the village of Germasoya, close to the major resort town of Limassol. Other nearby major towns, Larnaca and Paphos, are around a 40-minute drive away. "M. How to get there

Since the resort town of Limassol does not have its own airport, most travelers arriving here have to travel from the air harbors of Larnaca or Paphos. The distance to both is about 70 kilometers. Thus, in the way you have to spend about 45-60 minutes. If you travel with the assistance of a travel agency, you will most likely be provided with a transfer from the airport to the hotel. In this case, along the way you can not only admire the beauties of Cyprus, but also listen to an interesting guide's story about this country and its features. If you plan an independent trip to Cyprus, then from the airport to the hotel M. Moniatis Hotel 3 * can be reached by taxi, intercity bus or minibus. In addition, keep in mind that there are regular ferries from Rhodes, Crete, Haifa, Ashdod, Beirut and other cities to Limassol. Hotel Policies

In accordance with the internal rules of the hotel M. Moniatis Hotel 3 * (Limassol), guests are checked into their rooms after two in the afternoon. If you arrive earlier than this time, then, if there are free rooms, they will try to accommodate you immediately. Otherwise, you will be asked to wait for the checkout time. At the same time, you can leave things in the storage room and have a good time by the pool or take a walk in the surroundings. On the day of departure, release your room no later than noon. In this case, you will need to pay for the entire stay at the hotel. You can pay here both in cash and with plastic cards of popular payment systems of the world (Mastercard, Visa, Maestro, American Express and Diners Club).

Special conditions

If you come to M. Moniatis Hotel 3 * (Cyprus) with the whole family, then for accommodating the smallest tourists, a children's or extra bed can be provided. At the same time, you do not have to pay extra for a child under two years old in your room. As for tourists traveling with pets, their accommodation is prohibited in this hotel.





Number of rooms

M. Moniatis Hotel has at its disposal 30 comfortable standard double and triple rooms. They are all equipped with air conditioning, heating system, private bathroom, TV, telephone and balcony or terrace. Cleaning is done daily, bed linen and towels are changed three times a week.

Nutrition

The cost of accommodation in M. Moniatis Hotel 3 * (Limassol) includes breakfast. They are served in the hotel restaurant in buffet style. You can also have lunch and dinner here by ordering a meal from the menu. In addition, the hotel has a bar with a wide selection of drinks for every taste.





Entertainment

Guests of M. Moniatis Hotel 3 *, who decide to spend time on site, can relax by the outdoor pool. It has a sun terrace with sun loungers and parasols. The hotel also has billiards.

M. Moniatis Hotel 3 *: reviews of tourists from Russia

As for the hotel rooms, in general, travelers were satisfied with them. So, according to them, the situation here is quite simple, but at the same time comfortable. Experienced travelers recommend, if possible, asking the administrator to put you in their rooms with mountain views. No complaints were noticed about the cleaning, as well as changing clothes and towels. The maids carry out their duties regularly and efficiently. Due to the fact that the hotel has only 30 rooms, and it is located in a quiet place surrounded by beautiful nature, our compatriots thought it was very cozy and conducive to a calm and peaceful rest.





Some negative tourist reviews related to the location of M. Moniatis Hotel. However, those who previously carefully read the hotel description and reviews about it were satisfied.
