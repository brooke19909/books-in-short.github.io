Dubai's best areas - overview, features and attractions

The second largest, but leading in popularity, Dubai emirate is well known throughout the world. Anyone who wants to relax on the shores of the Persian Gulf, experience the atmosphere of luxury, walk through the giant shopping centers, visit festivals and incendiary parties should go to Dubai.

Often, this name means the city itself, but it is only part of the large area that makes up the emirate of the same name. Before you go on vacation, we will offer you an overview of the areas of Dubai, tell you about the features of each of them.

This is a residential seaside area, where prestigious hotels, luxurious villas of wealthy citizens with private pools, beaches, excellent restaurants are located. In Jumeirah, you will be offered a VIP-vacation by the sea, a family vacation with children, a vacation for active youth. There are many popular night clubs and restaurants.

The areas of Dubai are constantly expanding and landscaping. Al Barsha is a new area of ‚Äã‚ÄãDubai. The place is quite quiet, one might say, family friendly. There are many inexpensive but comfortable hotels located near many of Dubai's attractions. Near the Dubai Mall, a large ski center, Ski Dubai.

The mere thought that a unique ski resort is located in the middle of the desert leads tourists to indescribable delight, although this structure is more popular among the local population and visitors from neighboring countries. At Ski Dubai you can rent everything you need: snowboards and skis, sledges, here you will even be offered winter clothes. For the local population, the ski resort has become a real miracle - now they can play snowballs and ski and sled. This is, without exaggeration, the amazing attraction of Dubai.



Deira

It's no secret that many tourists like to visit the eastern regions of Dubai - here are the famous markets, which you can wander around for hours. For example, the Gold Market. In Dubai, there has long been no own field. Therefore, sellers here work with gold brought from South Africa, Russia and China. You are unlikely to see price tags in such markets; the seller will tell you the price, but you must bargain.

The area is an alternative to budget vacations. There is a huge selection of hotels of any category, in comparison with the old tourist area of ‚Äã‚ÄãDeira. They are separated by Creek Bay. The area is intended for shopping, beach vacations, business trips. The vast majority of hotels offer a free shuttle service to the equipped municipal beach of Al Mamzar Beach, the entrance to which is paid. There is also a free beach.

Among the attractions we recommend visiting the Fort Museum, the fabric market, Creek Park with a dolphinarium, go for a walk along the bay by boat, walk through the Burjuman and Wafi City shopping centers.



Jebel

The area is suitable for a secluded relaxing holiday in beachfront hotels with their own beaches. Here, experienced tourists recommend choosing an all-inclusive option in five-star hotels. Outside the hotel infrastructure is poorly developed. But the area is developing rapidly: there is already a horse and go-kart club, a golf club.

Bar dubai

We walked through the main areas of Dubai and finally got into the historical center, where historical and cultural attractions are located. Inspecting them, connoisseurs of Arab culture and art, history buffs will be delighted. At the local textile market, you can buy Oriental and European fabrics, pillows, carpets, handmade linens.

Al Fahidi Museum

The exposition of the cultural and historical national museum of Al Fahidi is dedicated to the history of the country. The entrance to it is located from the side of the ancient fort. Here you can get acquainted with the life of the local population in antiquity, see reed huts, learn all the stages of the historical development of the UAE.



The exposition is divided into three parts: modern, medieval and prehistoric Dubai. Tours are conducted by experienced professional guides who speak several languages, including Russian. In the museum you can see many interesting exhibits that open the veil of secrets of the history of the emirates.

Sheikh Said's House

This is a special attraction of Dubai. The building was built by the grandfather of the current ruler of the emirate, later it housed the residence of Sheikh Saeed, and business meetings and meetings began to be held. Today, everyone can visit this house. This monument is especially popular among foreign tourists. In addition, this museum is included in the list of cultural monuments of the country. The official status of the museum was assigned to him thirty years ago, after restoration work was carried out in the building. Interestingly, the inspection of the House-Museum is carried out according to the rules of the eastern countries - from right to left.
