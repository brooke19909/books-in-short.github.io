Natural features, climate. Greece awaits tourists at any time of the year

Greece is located in the south of Europe. The country is washed by the Ionian, Mediterranean, Aegean seas and has a subtropical Mediterranean climate, characterized by hot, dry summers and warm, rainy winters. Depending on the specific area, weather conditions can vary greatly. Thus, the climate on the islands of Greece and in the southern parts of the country is milder than in the northern and central regions. In the north in winter, even freezing temperatures can be observed, especially in November-January. The coolness is accompanied by rainy weather. The driest period in this area is from July to September. The islands and southern zones have a warmer climate.
