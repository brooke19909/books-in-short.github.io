Royal Alhambra Palace 5 *: description, reviews of tourists

The popular resort of Side is the cleanest sea, spacious sandy beaches, a picturesque green peninsula, pine forests, orange groves and lots of interesting sights. Arriving in this city, people ride snow-white yachts, visit numerous cafes and restaurants, go to souvenir shops, admire the architecture - in general, they spend time with pleasure. In addition, there are the best resort hotels in Turkey, which is good news. You can come here at any time of the year, but it is especially beautiful here in the early autumn and late spring. And this period is considered the most successful time in terms of climate.





Sea holiday

The vast majority of holidaymakers like the seaside resorts. And this is understandable, because a person who has been sitting in a noisy metropolis almost all year, worked in the office or just did the same thing, regularly observing the movement of cars on highways and high-rise buildings, I want to radically change the situation. Go, say, to the Mediterranean Sea in Turkey, spend a couple of unforgettable days, enjoying the sea, the gentle sun and exotic food. Therefore, many do so. Having decided to relax, having gone to the Mediterranean Sea in Turkey, you should first choose a resort city. Side is perfect; it is located 75 kilometers from Antalya, and it is one of the most popular. It is not only a place where you can enjoy the delights of beaches, it is also an open-air museum. It is here that the archaeological zone is located, which is a real treasure for history buffs. In general, here everyone will find something to their liking. After all, in this resort you can enjoy delicious drinks, and lie on the beach and attend excursions.

The best complex in Turkey

Whatever goals a tourist pursues, coming to Turkey, he needs to stop somewhere. Royal Alhambra Palace 5 * is one of the most popular places in the entire resort where visitors prefer to stay. It is located on the seafront, this hotel has its own beach. And this is just one of the many benefits that have yet to be listed. SPA-salon, outdoor and indoor pools, incredibly comfortable rooms - Royal Alhambra Palace Hotel 5 * has everything necessary for tourists not only to have a good time, but to remember their vacation forever.





This hotel can be seen right away: the complex, made in a unique and unique style, harmoniously fits into the overall landscape design. It was built only two years ago, in 2012, but thousands of tourists have already managed to evaluate the quality of service in this place and even return here again.

Apartments

Hotel Royal Alhambra Palace 5 * is a six-story building, made in the original style. The complex offers a variety of accommodation options: a total of 414 standard rooms, of which six are with special conditions, for guests with disabilities. There are also 83 family apartments, which are divided into two types. Of these, 48 are two-level. On the first - a bathroom with a shower and a bedroom, on the second - the same, only the shower is replaced by a bath. There are also 35 two-room apartments - two bedrooms and one beautifully equipped bathroom. The hotel also offers 60 one-room rooms with one spacious bedroom. If the listed options do not suit you - no problem. There are 20 two bedroom apartments with sea views, a luxurious living room, two bathrooms and a bedroom with a jacuzzi. And the last accommodation option is a two-level room, equipped with everything necessary. This is a living room, bedroom, bathroom on the first level and the same thing on the second, only with a jacuzzi.

Accommodation cost

Now we should talk about how much it will cost to spend an unforgettable vacation in one of the rooms of Royal Alhambra Palace 5 *. Prices, it should be said, are not as high as it might seem. The hotel regularly provides discounts, and very substantial ones - up to 50 percent! You can save half! So, for example, a standard double room for two with all amenities for seven days will cost 55 thousand rubles - and this is for the all-inclusive service! Junior Suite will cost 116 thousand rubles. This room is 70 square meters. m. It is equipped with everything necessary for life and relaxation, plus everything has a picturesque view from the window on the sea surface. Family apartments for four will cost exactly the same. In general, as you can see, the price is quite a compromise - and this is for all the advantages offered by the hotel.





Situation

Of course, such rooms are equipped with everything necessary. The first thing I would like to note is the traditional Turkish style, in which the apartments were originally made. It is very pleasant to relax in such rooms, as the atmosphere is really comfortable and sets up for a pleasant pastime. All apartments of Royal Alhambra Palace 5 * visitors are offered modern furniture, a safe. The floor is covered with soft carpet. The standard room, for example, has a balcony, a telephone, a plasma TV with satellite channels, air conditioning, and a bathroom. Of course, such trifles as a hairdryer, slippers, a minibar, linen and a towel are all included. When booking, you can also indicate preferences regarding furniture: it can be one double bed or two single beds.





Nutrition

The most important aspect of a good rest is nutrition. A person needs to gain strength, and this is achieved not only in the process of sleep. A good standard of nutrition is also needed. However, staying at Royal Alhambra Palace 5 * (Side) you can not worry about this. In the main restaurant of the hotel complex the buffet service is available, therefore a chic breakfast, lunch and dinner are provided here. In addition, the hotel has its own patisserie, where guests can try a variety of goodies - upscale chefs serve fresh pastries and delicious cakes. If you want to please yourself with national sweets, then you can go into a Turkish tent, there is a huge selection of different goodies. And, of course, there are lots of different buffets, bars and snack restaurants offering drinks and other treats.

For body and soul

A SPA salon offering a wide variety of cosmetic services will definitely attract the attention of representatives of a beautiful part of humanity who have come here on vacation. Sauna, Turkish bath (known as a hammam), a fitness room, which has everything for full training and classes, a highly professional massage therapist - this and much more is offered by the hotel. If you want to change something in your appearance, then a hairdresser will help. By the way, you can also do water aerobics, work out in the gym or simply sunbathe by the pool. Umbrellas, sunbeds - no need to worry about this, everything is provided by the hotel. In general, even if you do not want to leave the hotel, the guests will always have something to do.

Service

It should also be noted such an aspect of relaxation as service and maintenance. This is very important because the impression of the guests about the hotel and the rest in general also depends on it. Royal Alhambra Palace 5 * employs exclusively highly qualified service professionals who speak three languages: German, Turkish and English. Each guest is treated with respect and understanding. There is an accelerated procedure for check-out and check-in, by the way, if the guest wishes, then breakfast delivery to the room can be arranged. And, of course, there is daily housekeeping - not superficial, as is the case in some hotels, but thorough. In addition, linen and towels are regularly changed.





Services for little guests

It should be noted that many people come to the hotel with children. This is the right choice, because this resort is really an ideal option for families. If adults want to relax together or retire - this is not a problem. The hotel has everything that is necessary for a fun pastime for children: a children's playground, a pool with slides, animation staff that will not let you get bored.
