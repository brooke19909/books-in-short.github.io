Lesson for the soul: knit napkins with knitting needles

Almost every person has their favorite pastime. Men go fishing, hunt, or disappear for days in the garage. Women visit beauty salons, shop, embroider with a cross, knit clothes, scarves, napkins with knitting needles or crochet.

Let's consider in more detail such a hobby as knitting.

Knitting calms the nerves (provided that you succeed). You can create real masterpieces with your own hands. This activity does not require large investments. All you need is threads and knitting tools.

Knitting is divided into three types, depending on the tools:

  1. Knitting. They come in different sizes and types - the size corresponds to the thickness. There are knitting needles with and without coating (for example, Teflon provides better gliding of the thread). They are also divided according to their purpose - for example, socks, circular (knitting in a circle with knitting needles allows you to get seamless clothes).
  2. Knitting on a mechanical or electric machine. This equipment is used to speed up the work process, but does not provide such a wide selection of patterns as when manually knitting.
  3. Crochet. Hooks are separated by size and the material from which they are made. Metal is more reliable, but they are much smaller than those made of plastic.


Plastic hooks are large (for example, for knitting with thick threads), but they are less reliable (often break).

Knitting is more widespread than using a hook. Beginners need to first learn how to knit satin and elastic (English and French), and then you can try to create napkins with knitting needles or some other small product. At this stage, you should stay longer.

Special literature and the Internet present materials that describe in detail the process of knitting napkins with knitting needles for beginners. In them you can find all the information about what tools and threads you need. Hand knitted napkins with knitting needles will be a good decoration for your home and great gifts for friends and loved ones.



After gaining the first experience in knitting, you can move on to more complex patterns and products. Start with small things and gradually master the implementation of larger and more laborious ones. Any complex pattern on the blouse will emphasize your perseverance and the ability to create exclusive and original items of clothing. When knitting children's things, it is best to use a seamless method. This will avoid rubbing the seams of children's skin, and the baby will be comfortable and warm. A seamless method is used when knitting hats, berets, sleeves on clothes, children's pants. It provides not only comfort when wearing clothes, but also a beautiful appearance of the product.

Whatever you do - knit napkins with knitting needles, cross stitch, go fishing - remember that your favorite hobby should be fun. And if your hobby gives you not only satisfaction, but also tangible profits, then this is doubly pleasant.
