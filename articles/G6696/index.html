Hotel Rooms

A feature of this hotel is the availability of special family rooms. Of the one hundred forty-five rooms, sixteen are adjoining, which allows them to accommodate large families with children or companies consisting of two families.

All rooms are non-smoking and feature an unusual design. Upon check-in, a deposit of one hundred dollars is taken, upon departure it is returned. Please note that pets are strictly prohibited in the hotel.

The number of rooms is represented by several categories. We will talk about them in more detail.

Family rooms

The family suite is designed for a different number of guests. On average, these are two, three and four people. Each similar room has access to the pool, it must have a hairdryer, air conditioning, satellite TV, a minibar and a safe.

Family suites have an unusual design, a group consisting of well-known European and Thai designers worked on each room. These rooms are over eighty square meters.

Suites

This room can accommodate up to five people. Its area is close to one hundred square meters. Room filling is standard, as well as family suites, it has access to the pool.

Double rooms

These rooms are suitable for families of two or three people. They have a queen-size bed and sea views. Each of these rooms has everything you need for the life of a whole family. If you need an extra bed for a child, the hotel staff will provide you with it upon request.





Single rooms

These rooms also often have a queen-size bed, so they can be freely accommodated together. Each room has an excellent sea view. The area of ‚Äã‚Äãsingle rooms ranges from thirty to fifty square meters.

Beach area

Like many other hotels in Thailand, Veranda Resort Pattaya 5 * (na Jomtien) does not have its own beach area. You will have to go to the city beach along with all the other guests of Pattaya. But nevertheless, you will notice some differences: the hotel staff daily clean the sand related to the hotel and carefully clean the coastal area. As a result, you will rest on the white sand without unpleasant surprises in the form of wrappers from chips or cigarette butts. In addition, the water in this place is surprisingly clean, so you will definitely enjoy swimming on the beach.

We have already specified that the hotel is located on the first coastline, which greatly facilitates the road to the beach. It will take you no more than five minutes, which is very important if you are relaxing in Pattaya with young children.





The cost of living in a hotel

Here it is worth clarifying that this hotel complex, despite frequent promotions, will never be available to the general mass of vacationers. It is designed for a special category of tourists who are ready to give a lot of money for comfort and excellent service.

The minimum price per night at the hotel is equal to six thousand rubles. This is very expensive for most Russians. Especially if you add flight and transfer to this price. As a result, the average cost of a tour for eleven to twelve days can approach two hundred thousand rubles.
