Hotel Marbas Beach Hotel 3 *, Turkey, Marmaris: reviews

Hotel Marbas Beach Hotel 3 * is located on the coast of a beautiful bay, surrounded on all sides by mountains. This is the southwestern part of Turkey, where two seas meet - the Aegean and the Mediterranean. The windows of the hotel on one side overlook the picturesque green mountains, and on the other - the azure waters of the sea.

Location

Although the Marbas Beach Hotel 3 * (Marmaris) is located far from the nearest Dalaman airport, 110 km, you can order a transfer from the hotel and you will be transferred to the place of stay in two hours. The road is picturesque, so it will be boring. But the hotel is very close to the beautiful city of Marmaris, the center of sailing in Turkey. There are both historical interesting sights and modern entertainment. You can go with children to one of several city water parks and have fun all day.





And just 300 meters away is the small town of Icmeler. There are all conditions for evening relaxation: nightclubs, bars, karaoke and restaurants. There are many shops and an oriental bazaar where you can buy Turkish spices and sweets, which this wonderful region is famous for.

Beach area

The photograph of the beach area shows that it is located just 50 meters from the Marbas Beach Hotel 3 *. This is a small pebble strip with a wooden pier. The sea is warm and not very clean, as many tourists write. Bed trestles, towels and umbrellas are free, but to take a seat in the sun, you have to hurry. Many people write that, coming after breakfast, they made the following picture: there were already towels on the laid out sun loungers, that is, people in the morning fussed and took their places.





So be careful. Some write that the sea is calm and there are no big waves, since the bay is surrounded by mountains, and there is no strong wind. Conditions for relaxing on the beach, in principle, are comfortable. You can ride a banana or a pedalo for a fee, on small boats along the bay. The places are still very beautiful. Fans of natural attractions will be satisfied.

Some tourists complain about the garbage on the beach, they say that it is full of cigarette butts, no one collects them, although there are staff on the beach, and there are garbage bins. But there is a way out. Some tourists write that the beach area is conditionally divided into free and paid beaches. On a paid - sand cover, and you just need to buy at least one cocktail from the owner in his bar - and sunbathe as much as you like.

Also near the sea there is a free shower and comfortable changing rooms. Sunset at sea is quite gentle, so it is convenient for both children and those who cannot swim.

Rooms

In total, Marbas Beach Hotel 3 * (Turkey, Marmaris) has 88 rooms. These are mostly standard rooms for two people, but there are 4 single rooms. One room is for people with disabilities. There are special facilities for the disabled.





Almost all rooms have balconies with plastic furniture, where you can sit, smoke, drink tea or coffee. But there are no dryers, there is no place to hang linen, experienced tourists advise taking ropes with them.

The rooms are decorated in bright colors, the furniture is presented by bedside tables near the bed, a table with a mirror, a wardrobe. There is a minibar but empty. There is also a small fridge and satellite TV.

You can ask for a crib for a small child, it should be given free of charge. The rooms have an intercom phone where you can order room service. Should be cleaned every day, but about the Marbas Beach Hotel 3 * hotel reviews of tourists say that this is not always the case. They do not clean very diligently and not every day.

Bathrooms

Each room has its own bathroom, which has everything you need: towels, toiletries, a hairdryer, a mirror. There is a toilet. There are rooms in which there is a bathtub, in the rest - showers.





Towels are changed not every day, but three times a week. There were no complaints from tourists regarding the bathrooms.

The hotel has a 24-hour reception, although check-in is only after 14.00. But if you arrive earlier, you will be allowed to use the pools and relax in the garden. They can feed dinner. Things will temporarily be recommended to you to put in a left-luggage office.

Excursions

The Marbas Beach Hotel 3 * in Marmaris has tour services. Some tourists recommend taking trips in the city, as they will cost more at the hotel.

First of all, you will want to see the nearest places - Marmaris and Icmeler. The small Icmeler can be reached on the 1.5-kilometer promenade. This strip stretches along the sea, it is surrounded by pine trees, cedars and eucalyptus trees.

Marmaris is next. Can be reached on orange dolmushi. It will be interesting to ride a water taxi there.

Tourists advise that if you come to rest at the Marbas Beach Hotel 3 * in Turkey (Marmaris), then be sure to go on an excursion to Pamukkale. This will give you an unforgettable experience. It can be called the eighth wonder of the world. Not only is this a very beautiful place, but also healing. They say that one hour spent in the Cleopatra basin will heal you of all diseases. Our tourists, according to reviews, did not feel it very much, but received the maximum pleasure from the tour. We drove for a long time, 5 hours, through the mountain passes along steep serpentines, but were not tired at all on the road. Such an excursion will take a whole day. Its duration is 15 hours.





Many more are advised to go to Turtle Island and the Dalyan River. In addition to the beauty and natural attractions, tourists have seen enough on giant turtles listed in the Red Book. The tour also includes fishing for blue crabs with their subsequent eating. One serving will cost 450 rubles. People complain that the crabs are very tasty, but the portions are small. To eat plenty, you need to take a few. I also liked the trip to the island of turtles along the Dalyan River. Rocky banks surround the river, on which the famous Lycian tombs are located.
