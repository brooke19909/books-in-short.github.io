Hotel Location





The modern and new complex is located in an ecologically clean zone of the Moscow region, only 70 km from the capital itself and 15 km from the center of Russian Orthodoxy - the city of Sergiev Posad. The hotel is multidisciplinary and is ready to offer services for organizing historical and cultural tours, sports events, as well as hold any celebration, such as a wedding. One of the main advantages of its location is the forest around, clean air and remoteness from city bustle and noise. If you chose "Peresvet" (hotel) as a vacation spot, the address will be easy to remember: Moscow Region, Peresvet, 9 Pionerskaya Street.
