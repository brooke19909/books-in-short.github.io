Pensions Kuban. Rest in pleasure

Kuban Territory is a resort center on the Black Sea coast. Adler, Sochi, Gelendzhik, Anapa - the list of the best resorts of the Krasnodar Territory can be continued for a long time. Every year crowds of holidaymakers come here, as these are the best places for a summer vacation at sea. Numerous boarding houses of the Kuban are ready to receive more and more tourists every year. Some of them even work year-round. In the summer - the sea and the sun, and in winter - the mountain slopes.

The heart of the Kuban is the beautiful city of Gelendzhik. It is considered one of the best resorts in the Krasnodar Territory. Everything here is intended for the convenience and enjoyment of tourists: a lot of holiday homes, equipped beaches, incredible entertainment and unsurpassed natural wealth. Pensions Kuban, located in Gelendzhik, are happy to receive guests, offering them the best conditions for a comfortable stay. Even the most sophisticated travelers will be delighted with local attractions. There is no problem with housing here either.





Pension "Kuban" (Gelendzhik) is located in the heart of the resort town.

Description

The guesthouse is a five-minute walk from the Hippopotamus water park and the central promenade with lots of entertainment.

The recreation center has two new buildings, an outdoor pool, a children's area and a playground, a parking lot, a tour desk, a shop and a restaurant. The pension is guarded around the clock.





Services

A five-minute walk away is a free pebble beach. For an additional fee, you can rent an umbrella, sunbed or deck chair.

Pensions Kuban are distinguished by unsurpassed service and professionalism of staff.

For those who do not want to spend time on vacation cooking, the Kuban boarding house offers 3 meals a day. The menu can be ordered independently. For eating in the boarding house provides a new cozy room. Chefs prepare healthy, varied food exclusively from fresh ingredients.

The tour desk offers the best known routes for visiting local attractions. The opening of the season is accompanied by a carnival procession, which can be accessed by pre-ordering a ticket to the bureau. The main attraction of Gelendzhik is the promenade. Not a single Russian resort has such a unique architectural structure. Pension "Kuban" in relation to the embankment is closer than all other rest houses and sanatoriums.
