Mogilev, sanatorium "Sosny": photos and reviews of tourists

Fortunately, there are many places left on the Belarusian land where nature has preserved its originality, where it is not difficult to get lost in the forests, meadows shine with a variety of colors, and rivers slowly carry their waters to the sea.



Location

The city of Mogilev has one of such corners. Sanatorium "Pines" is located among a dense forest filled with the aroma of needles and fresh foliage. Nearby, no more than 400 meters from residential buildings, a full-flowing Dnieper flows proudly along the coast of which a long terrain path passes. All guests, without exception, who come to the Sosny sanatorium at any time of the year, are engaged in its development. Mogilev is located only ten kilometers from the health resort.

A bit of history

Back in the distant Soviet 1973, construction began on a sanatorium designed for 300 vacationers. The health resort is able to accommodate the same number of visitors today, of which 130 places can be allocated to children. For almost 40 years, the health resort has not undergone a full-scale repair. Only in 2011, the reconstruction of residential and medical buildings was completed, and the buildings that make up the Sosny sanatorium (Mogilev region) found their modern, finished look.

Residential base

Currently, the health resort includes a medical and 3 bedroom buildings, a club and a hydropathic center. The six-story building offers double accommodation in a block system. In addition to new furniture, the rooms have a TV, amenities are provided on the unit.

The two-story building is ready to accommodate its guests in apartment-type rooms, which for the convenience of holidaymakers are equipped with a full kitchen with a spacious refrigerator. They can accommodate from 2 to 4 people, also in the room there is enough space for an extra bed. The four-story building has two-room superior rooms.


During the reconstruction, all outdated furniture was replaced with modern furniture; natural materials were used to the maximum extent possible in the decoration of the premises. The presence of a TV with a wide selection of channels will allow you not to get bored before going to bed.

Medical base

One of the best hospitals Mogilev possesses, the Sosny sanatorium, has been specializing in the prevention and treatment of diseases of the respiratory and digestive organs, problems of the musculoskeletal system since its founding. But the health resort received the greatest fame thanks to its gynecological profile. Experienced doctors successfully cope with the treatment of female inflammatory diseases and even infertility. Health resorts are widely used:

  • Electrotherapy.
  • Heat treatment.
  • Mud and electrotherapy.
  • Laser Therapy
  • Halotherapy and inhalation.
  • Massage.

If necessary, doctors conduct additional diagnostic tests on the spot, then medication is prescribed. Often they are combined with traditional methods, such as phyto- and aromatherapy, the intake of mineral water.

From the very first day after arrival at the Sosny sanatorium (Mogilev), the level of physical activity necessary for him is selected for each patient and a group or individual form of physical therapy is prescribed . In accordance with the recommendations of the therapist, diet food is selected.

Water treatments

For everyone who comes to rest and restore their health in Mogilev, the Sosny sanatorium offers various types of hydrotherapeutic procedures. And this is not only drinking highly mineralized water according to the individual scheme prescribed by the doctor. This and measured swimming in the pool with mineral water, intensive underwater massage, as well as various types of showers: Charcot, circulating and ascending.



Given the profile that the Sosny sanatorium (Mogilev region) has been following for many years, the variety of bathtubs offered by doctors for their patients becomes not surprising. These are mineral and coniferous, pearl and turpentine, iodine-bromine and coniferous-mineral.

To prevent the occurrence of respiratory diseases, a visit to the sauna is of great importance.

Catering

For vacationers, five meals a day are provided in the dining room of the sanatorium. Some rooms have a kitchen that allows you to cook your favorite meals without any restrictions.

All the necessary products for this can be purchased in a store in the health resort, or by going to Mogilev. Sanatorium "Sosny" does not differ, according to vacationers, with a special variety of dishes prepared by cooks. Therefore, a small supply of provisions will not be superfluous. Guest reviews often regret the lack of any fruit on the menu or in the free sale at the sanatorium.

Prices for rest and treatment

One of the most budgetary health resorts in Belarus offering excellent quality of rest and treatment is the Sosny sanatorium (Mogilev). Prices for adult vouchers with accommodation in a double unit start from 1900 rubles per day. The cost of living in an apartment type room is 2090 rubles. For accommodation of children under 7 years old a fee of 1650 rubles is charged, for children of older age 1740 rubles.

The price includes five meals a day, accommodation, as well as treatment selected by an experienced doctor.



Holiday packages are in great demand, thanks to which enjoying the unique nature and restoring health has become affordable for those who prefer to leave Mogilev only on weekends. Sanatorium "Sosny" offers such a vacation at a price of 1570 rubles. Guest reviews do not give the opportunity to doubt the correctness of such a choice.

Additional services

For those who want to feel the impact of a procedure or undergo an additional examination, and the doctor did not prescribe them for any reason, it is worth knowing that any research and treatment procedure can be carried out for a fee. Additional charges apply for access to the pool and sauna.

Leisure activities

On the territory of the sanatorium there are excellent sports grounds where teams can deploy an exciting game, having received all the necessary equipment from the administration. In winter, there is a ski base. In the open air there is also a wonderful playground for children who have been admitted to the sanatorium from three years old.

In the first building, the library doors are hospitably open, where an interesting book can be found both for the child and for lovers of female novels. For the guests of the sanatorium, fascinating excursions are organized in the Mogilev region and memorable places in Belarus.

Despite regular discos and creative evenings, reviews about the Sosny sanatorium often criticize their uniformity and lack of additional entertainment. Especially this question excites visitors to the health resort with children who would be happy to take part in a variety of animation programs.

But the treatment of the dissatisfied practically does not remain. The reviews page is full of words of gratitude to the attending physicians, and to the maintenance staff who help to cope with ailments and make every day a pleasant one.
