Wireless lamp: types, characteristics, advantages

A wide variety of lighting devices are used today to create high-quality and stylish lighting. At times, customers are obviously confused, because the choice of fixtures on the market is very large. Recently, wireless luminaires have become increasingly popular. We will understand the differences, technical specifications. Define the disadvantages and significant advantages.



Lighting design

When creating high-quality lighting in an apartment, country house or on the street, several important factors must be taken into account. The following options are important:

  • The place where the lighting devices will be located.
  • Protection of wiring and the devices themselves from high humidity. Is there any?
  • Indicators of the level of electric current.
  • Protection against mechanical damage and external negative influences. Is it provided?

No wires better

Using a wireless lamp in the house, a person loses numerous problems associated with lighting. Such lighting devices have one significant feature - they are completely devoid of wires. Therefore, even a beginner can cope with their installation. No experience or wiring knowledge is required.



Battery- powered cordless lights work , which is very convenient. No dependence on the presence of current in the mains. Even if, for technical reasons, the electricity in the house is turned off, the lamps continue to work and perfectly perform the function assigned to them.

Such lighting can be controlled either using a button or touch panel, or using a remote control.

Advantages

Why are wireless lights (photo below) so in demand? The popularity of this type of lighting is associated with a number of significant advantages.

  • Fully autonomous work. The absence of dependence on the electric network allows wireless light sources to function in any conditions, which is very convenient. If you choose the right and high-quality reliable batteries (batteries), then the duration of the working cycle for wireless fixtures will be about a year.
  • Mounting. Simple and fast, no wiring required. No wires and time-consuming dangerous processes of "communication" with the electrical network. No additional installation of sockets. Lack of dust and dirt. A few minutes, double-sided tape - and the installation of a wireless lamp is completed.
  • The choice of any place. You can install such a lighting device anywhere. It can be a bathroom or a pantry, a dressing room (closet) or a flight of stairs, a gazebo or a fence in a summer cottage. There are a lot of options.
  • Variety of choices. Very pleased with the fact that the wireless lamp you can choose any color, configuration, shape, size and style.
  • An opportunity to buy a lamp equipped with a motion sensor.
  • Affordable cost. Depending on the size, material and type of lamp, a wireless light source will cost from 250 rubles to two thousand.




Types of luminaires with motion sensors

There are four main types of lamps with motion sensors: ultrasonic, microwave, infrared and combined.

For the street, a wireless lamp with an ultrasonic motion sensor is ideal. Sound is transmitted to the receiver when the signal is interrupted - the lamp gives light. This lighting option is also suitable for large spacious apartments.

For residential and industrial premises, a wireless lamp with an infrared motion sensor is most often used. Such devices respond to temperature changes in the environment. A photo relay installed in such a device reacts to infrared radiation coming from a person. Experts consider this lamp to be an ideal indoor lighting fixture.

Microwave options are very similar to ultrasonic. The only difference is that the inclusion of light does not involve sound, but a radio wave. Combined luminaires are convenient in that they combine several types of sensors.



type of instalation

Wireless light sources differ in the type of installation. Ceiling lights are used for ceiling installations. They are great for stretch ceilings, as they are lightweight, do not heat up and at the same time give a good bright light beam.

Most often in apartments a wall lamp is used wirelessly. Devices that are mounted on a vertical surface, the easiest to find in the store. Very convenient desktop light sources. Wireless lamps can be moved from place to place. No wires or finding the nearest outlet. This is ideal for the school desk in the children's room. Often such lighting fixtures are used in exhibition halls, working as additional illumination. But to illuminate a picture or a mirror in an apartment, a wireless lamp will do just fine.
