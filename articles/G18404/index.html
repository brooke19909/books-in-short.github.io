A little bit about the resort ...

As we already mentioned, the Catamaran Resort Hotel 5 * (Beldibi), the photo of which is posted in the article, is located in the resort village of Beldibi, near Kemer. The village is located in a very picturesque place, which is why it is popular. Beautiful mountains, fabulous sea and sunny sky - all this is in Beldibi.





As a rule, the village is considered as a single resort area of ‚Äã‚ÄãKemer. However, couples and those who prefer a more relaxing vacation will enjoy Beldibi more. Young people usually prefer the bustling Kemer with its many discos and clubs. In addition, Beldibi is interesting for lovers of active tourism and mountaineering, as well as rafting. On the slopes of the Taurus Mountains, you can make an amazing photo shoot.

The heyday of a small village began not so long ago, in the 1980s, and, moreover, completely unexpectedly. Local residents involved in the cultivation of citrus fruits and sheep breeding could not even think that Beldibi would quickly turn into a popular resort in a matter of years. The Germans were the first to notice a wonderful place. They built the first hotels here. Now the resort has more than 60 tourist complexes, among which there are institutions of various levels.

The main attraction of the village is its stunningly beautiful nature. The village is adjacent to the Taurus Mountains and the alluring Mediterranean Sea. Such a unique location makes the resort's climate special. Beldibi is characterized by hot summers and warm, but wet winters. The holiday season lasts from May to mid-October.

Arriving in Turkey, all tourists flock to the sea coast, because it is for the sake of a beach holiday that they come here. Local beaches, of course, cannot be called ideal, but they compensate for the shortcomings with picturesque views. The coast of Beldibi is characterized by rocky sunsets and pebble beaches. In some places, sand is also found. In order to avoid injuries due to pebbles, holidaymakers buy special swimming shoes. The most fashionable hotels sand their own beaches, so when you buy a tour, take a look at the coast if this issue is important for you.





Beldibi is a paradise place for those people who appreciate natural beauty. Walking along the coast, here you can see wild coves of tremendous beauty, where stone blocks of rocks border azure sea waters and white sand. The village has not only the beaches of hotels, but also the municipal beach, which has been awarded such a high award as the Blue Flag.

Local Attractions

Beldibi is not much different from similar villages built on the Mediterranean coast. On both sides of the main street of the town, named after the famous hero Ataturk, there are hotels. There is no familiar promenade that is characteristic of any resort. Walking around the village, you must definitely admire the local mosque. It was built not so long ago and has no historical value, however, it is executed according to all the canons of oriental craftsmanship. There are no special attractions in Beldibi. The main values ‚Äã‚Äãare the sea and mountains. Walking near the village, you can contemplate the stunning landscapes of coniferous plantings and gray mountains. Local guides offer to go to the mountain gorge, up the riverbed. During the trip you can see a lot of interesting things and enjoy the stunning beauty of nature.





Near Beldibi in 1959 caves were found in which the drawings of ancient people were most surprisingly preserved. Excavations of primitive settlements have now become a real attraction. Not far from them is a beautiful waterfall.

Resting in Beldibi with children, you should definitely visit Dino Park. This amazing place was created by human hands. On the territory of the park you can see stunning dummy dinosaurs. The unique experience of visiting this place is guaranteed, since the prehistoric animals are made to their full height, besides they not only emit loud roars, but also move their paws.

But lovers of antiquity should go to inspect the ancient ruins of Termessos. The canyon on the Goynuk River is very interesting, near which you can admire the unique waterfalls. Local guides also offer a ride on the mountains on ATVs, an amazing adventure will appeal to fans of extreme sports.

The Kemer Beldibi resort area is in demand among diving enthusiasts. There are many interesting places and beautiful bays that divers will appreciate. Near Kemer you can see the sunken ship, which for many years rests at the bottom of the sea. It is worth noting that diving in the Mediterranean is gaining more and more popularity. The local clear waters have a lot to offer. As soon as you move away from the noisy resorts, you immediately see stunning bays with their secrets and beauties.





Beldibi is only thirty kilometers from Antalya, so there is a great opportunity to travel and see something new. Reviews about hotel Catamaran Resort Hotel 5 * (Beldibi)

Many tourists generally believe that the institution does not correspond to the five-star status, since it has significant shortcomings. Let's try to figure out what is the reason for such diametrically different opinions.

Hotel Catamaran Resort Hotel 5 * (Beldibi) is located in a stunningly beautiful place, for which many holidaymakers like it. The sea, mountains, sun and relaxation - this is what the resort village is famous for. The complex is located right on the first coastline, near the sea. The territory of the institution is at least very small, but very beautiful and well-groomed. The gardener daily irrigates the plants and looks after the flowers, which is why it is so green and beautiful. But at the same time take a walk in the Catamaran Resort Hotel 5 * (Beldibi), photos will confirm this thesis, there is simply nowhere to go, since the territory is really quite small.





The number of rooms of the hotel is the moment that causes a lot of complaints from the side of the spa. General impression of the hotel ...

In general, the hotel makes a very good impression, despite the pros and cons. A picturesque place and beautiful territory are the main advantages of the institution. The hotel is located near the coast, but the pebble beach is far from ideal. Sunset at sea is covered with sharp pebbles, and then in the water there are larger stones. However, tourists get out of the situation by purchasing special shoes. But the sea pleases tourists with warm and clean water. For convenience, a pontoon was built on the coast, where you can relax, as well as go down into the water, bypassing stones.

The hotel has a wonderful friendly staff and good animators. In the evenings, guests can enjoy the entertainment activities of the activity program. In the daytime, tourists can attend water aerobics classes. According to vacationers, the range of animation events could be expanded, since there are not so many entertainments.

As for the children's club, it does not have a separate room. Children are entertained in the open air playground. In addition, the staff of the mini-club speaks only Turkish, which the children do not understand. Although, most of the guests of the institution are representatives of the post-Soviet space. It is simply impossible to meet Europeans here, as evidenced by reviews of the Catamaran Resort Hotel 5 * (Beldibi, Kemer).

According to guests, the complex is suitable for relaxing unpretentious tourists. But if you want to get a real five-star service and the corresponding rooms, then you should think about the choice so that you do not suffer disappointment during your vacation. Beldibi can be a great place to relax if there is a desire to find solitude and find harmony in the lap of nature. There are all conditions for this. But for the noisy youth it is worth looking for a hotel in Kemer itself.
