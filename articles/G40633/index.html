Hotels in Zelenogorsk (Leningrad Oblast): overview, addresses, reviews

Zelenogorsk is a small town on the shores of the Gulf of Finland, 40 km from St. Petersburg.

Zelenogorsk (Teriyoki) from the very beginning developed as a climatic resort. Numerous Zelenogorsk hotels provide comfortable living conditions.

What attracts Zelenogorsk

Vacationers and tourists come to Zelenogorsk in winter and summer to breathe in the air, saturated with the scents of coniferous forest and the sea, to visit the golden beach of the Gulf of Finland or Lake Simaginsky, go fishing, go sailing, walk along the sand dunes. Even a day or two spent in Zelenogorsk, restore the nervous system and restore the taste for life and good mood.

Corresponds to the status of a climatic resort and the variety of hotel services.

For the most part, Zelenogorsk hotels are located along the Primorsky highway, which runs along the coastline of the Gulf of Finland.

Hotels on the coast

But most of the hotels in Zelenogorsk are located on the coast of the Gulf of Finland. Proximity to beaches and attractions becomes the main criterion for tourists choosing a place to spend the night and stay.

A true luxury vacation is offered to guests at the President Hotel (Primorskoye Shosse, 572). 20 elegant rooms and 4 separate cottages with terraces overlooking the Gulf of Finland, the Birds and Berries restaurant, spa and summer pool, as well as sports grounds - all create a relaxed atmosphere of respectable comfort.

48 rooms are available for guests at the Teriyoki Hotel (1 Gavannaya St.).

The highlight of the hotel is the opportunity to sail on the waters of the Gulf of Finland on a yacht. Water enthusiasts can go to the outdoor or indoor pool, or rent a bicycle at the spa with various types of saunas. There is a sunbathing terrace, a wellness center and a restaurant.

The hotel offers rooms of various categories (from standard to presidential suite).

Park Hotel

Those who chose the Park Hotel in Zelenogorsk note that each guest is surrounded by the attention and unobtrusive care of the staff, and the homely atmosphere is relaxing.

Hotel Park (Primorskoye Shosse, 551/2) is located in a quiet green area near the Gulf of Finland. The complex is a closed territory, in which two-storey stylish townhouses are located around the perimeter. It is nice to relax and work comfortably. Wi-Fi and office equipment are available for workaholics, attractions for children, adults will like sports grounds, gazebos, where you can have a barbecue and play board games.

Rooms

Hotel Park in Zelenogorsk provides a variety of room categories, some of which accommodate pets with bowls and food. The wooden elements in the spacious rooms create a feeling of comfort. The hotel has only 2 types of rooms (each has a bathroom):

  • standard;
  • Junior Suite - duplex, bedroom on the 2nd floor.

According to visitors' reviews, Park-Hotel is characterized by the optimal combination of cost and comfort.

"Aquamarine"

Hotel "Aquamarine" in Zelenogorsk is located on Primorsky highway, 593 N.

This is one of the few hotels where you can stay with animals. Vacationers celebrate the modern hotel infrastructure, thanks to which each guest will find something to do. The hotel has:

  • gym room;
  • court, sports grounds;
  • bath complex;

Guests are treated to the Pearl Restaurant and Bar.

Hotel G9

One of the most popular in Zelenogorsk is the G9 Hotel (Primorskoye Shosse, 551/2). After all, the hotel is located in the center of the resort, if the plans include sightseeing, then there is no better place to choose. And the ski resort "Puhtolova Gora" is only 7 km away. In addition, guests are satisfied with the variety of room rates.

The hotel has a restaurant and bar, you can get lunch in the lunch box, relax on the terrace with city views or in the common living room. A feature of the hotel is board games, which guests play with enthusiasm, regardless of age.

Hotel G9 (Zelenogorsk) works at a high level, so the staff communicates in English.

Particular attention is paid to people with disabilities at the hotel, they provide additional amenities in the bathrooms and on the stairs.

Of the shortcomings that tourists note - the lack of parking.

Rooms

Hotel G9 (Zelenogorsk) provides the following room types:

  • Suite, there is a balcony;
  • family;
  • double room (option - superior).

Cozy, stylishly decorated rooms are equipped with everything you need, including air conditioning, TV, minibar, kettle. Visitors to the hotel note good soundproofing of the rooms.

Despite the fact that Zelenogorsk is a small city, tourists who come here can choose a hotel for their comfort and cost.
