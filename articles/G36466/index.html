Hotels in Velsk: photos, review and reviews

Velsk is a city located in the Arkhangelsk region, famous for its history. It is quite ancient. In this regard, there are numerous historical sights and cultural monuments. Guests of the city can have a wonderful time and feel the atmosphere of this place.





Hotels in Velsk offer their visitors comfortable accommodation and first-class service.

Shopping and hotel complex "Wheel"



5 km from the city of Velsk, in the village of Shilovskaya, is located the hotel complex "Wheel". The railway station is 8 km from the hotel, and Vologda Airport is 240 km away.

The hotel has 10 rooms decorated in warm pastel colors and equipped with satellite and flat-screen TVs. Housekeeping is provided daily. The estimated time for arrival at the hotel is considered to be 14:00, and the check-out time is 12:00. At the same time, the reception is open 24 hours a day, which allows guests to stay at a convenient time. Velsk hotels are happy to welcome guests day and night.

Terms of accommodation

Also a big plus is the ability to accommodate guests with pets, a separate fee for this is not provided. The hotel offers free indoor parking, without the need to reserve a parking space in advance. Visitors can also relax in the fresh air, have a great time on the terrace.

Hotel guests can use the Internet, Wi-Fi access is available absolutely free throughout the complex.

Local entertainment

On the territory of the complex there is a bar where you can relax and unwind, enjoy refreshing drinks, and a restaurant where you can dine with the whole family. Russian and European cuisines are presented, so any visitor will find their favorite dish here.

It offers guests a comfortable Finnish sauna and hammam, which will give a feeling of gentle deep relaxation. Shuttle services, laundry services, and fax or photocopying services can be provided at an extra charge.

Accommodation with children

Hotel "Wheel" (Velsk) accepts all guests, children of all ages are welcome. For an additional fee, on request, cots are available, as well as extra beds for children under 16 years old at a price of 600 rubles per night (1 extra bed is provided for 1 room). It should be remembered that additional services are not immediately included in the bill, but are paid separately during the stay.

Hotel "Yurievo Compound"

The city of Velsk is famous for its unique architectural structures. The Yurievo Podvorye Hotel is located in one of these buildings in the very center of the city.

The 15 standard rooms of the hotel combine the atmosphere of an old house with modern amenities. It includes LCD cable TVs, a fridge, and a minibar. The flooring is environmentally friendly: parquet or wooden floor.

Guests who visit the hotel with their own cars can use the parking lot. Until late in the evening, there is a bar where guests can have a good time. In the summer, there is an outdoor cafe in the open-air courtyard where grilled dishes are prepared. There is also a restaurant that offers a variety of dishes of Russian, Japanese and European cuisines.





Upon request, the chef can prepare meals from the diet menu, and the waiters can serve breakfast in the room. All standard rooms have a private bathroom with toiletries and a hairdryer.

For the convenience of customers, all rooms are equipped with all necessary household items, such as slippers, iron, electric kettle, desk. The hotel offers its guests a wake-up service, a visitor just needs to ask the administrator to wake him up at the right time.

Hotel Podvorye (Velsk) has luxury rooms. These are spacious and comfortable rooms with air conditioning and a sofa. Natural materials were used in the interior decoration. The room is characterized by increased comfort. There is also the possibility of designing a suite in a romantic style for honeymooners.

The hotel rooms are non-smoking, but there is a smoking area on site. A huge plus is the 24-hour front desk offering individual check-in and check-out services. A distinctive feature is the presence of a gift shop and library. Wi-Fi is free throughout.

The hotel also offers business meetings and banquets.
