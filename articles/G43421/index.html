Mini-oven VES SK-A20: owner reviews, user manual, advantages and disadvantages

A modern kitchen, whether in an apartment or in a country house, is unthinkable without various electrical appliances. This technique greatly facilitates the process of cooking for housewives and saves time. In the market of such products, the mini-oven with the function of a bread machine VES SK A20 is popular. It is multifunctional, easy to manage, does not take up much space in the kitchen, affordable. We offer detailed information about this model. The article will present the characteristics of the mini-furnace, instructions for its operation, and consider the disadvantages and advantages.

General information

The mini-oven VES SK A20 in the reviews, many housewives consider it an ideal choice. Cooking with it can be easy, but this miracle of kitchen appliances costs only 5,900 rubles. For comparison, we inform that prices for similar stoves from other manufacturers start from 25,000 rubles. Some sales representatives position this model as a product made in Spain. In fact, VES SK A20 furnaces are made in China. Perhaps this explains its affordable price. However, this does not affect the quality of work.





The VES SK A20 mini-oven is powered by 220-240 Volts. Its power is 1500 watts, electronic control. To facilitate cooking, it is equipped with a non-stick coating.

You can purchase this product in online stores that sell household appliances. Therefore, the cost of delivery must be added to the price. In large cities, a mini-oven of this model can be bought in ordinary electrical stores for the home.

VES SK A20: Description

This mini-oven is fully consistent with its name. It is compact, so the kitchen does not take up much space. Its width is only 49 cm and its height is 42 cm. Furthermore, the volume of the oven is 20 liters. The furnace body is metallic, black. The front door is made of heat-resistant glass. It opens with a convenient handle. Nearby are a control panel with a list of programs and a display. Inside the oven is equipped with 2 upper and 2 lower heating elements. Their power, respectively, 350 and 400 watts.

VES SK A20: instruction manual

We have already noted that this technique is produced in China. Since Russia is the customer, all accompanying documents for the furnace are printed in Russian. It‚Äôs not difficult to understand the instructions, as all actions are scheduled step by step. In addition to using the oven for cooking, you need to take care of your assistant and at the end of the process, since fat can accumulate on its internal surfaces, plus the oven is always saturated with various odors.

After use, it must be washed from the inside. To do this, you can use the classical method (detergent, sponge), or you can do steam cleaning. It lies in the fact that water is poured into a baking sheet and the oven is heated to 100 or slightly more degrees. The resulting hot vapors melt the fats, which are removed very easily after this procedure.

The instruction warns that you can only use a working stove. If, after turning it on, strange odors or sounds are noticed, as well as if there are problems with installing programs or operating buttons, the unit must be turned off and a service center should be contacted.

It is important to remember that after the start of the work of the bread machine, it is no longer possible to change the installed program. If you try to do this by clicking on the buttons or disconnecting the unit from the network, you can cause it to break.

Manufacturer defects

There are no negative reviews for the VES SK A20 furnace. Deficiencies reported by some respondents, unfortunately, are still present. Although they are not very significant and do not affect the quality of dishes.





In their reviews of the VES SK A20 furnace, customers note the following disadvantages of the device:

  • There is no backlight, although it is stated in the description attached to the miracle oven.
  • The convector is too noisy. To reduce noise, a pallet is required.
  • Uneven heat (you have to turn the baking sheet over the products), since the heating element located closer to the back wall works more intensively.
  • Some respondents in their reviews report that the factory assembly of the furnace may not be of high quality (there are not enough bolts). But this defect is fixed without problems.

Some buyers wish that the VES SK A20 model should be available not in one color (black), but in several. This does not affect the operation of the oven, but it is important for the design of the kitchen.

Another wish of the housewives is that it would be great if there was a device in the kit that protects against splattering of fat during cooking of some dishes. Without this accessory, the entire interior of the oven is very dirty.

Some housewives like the compact size of the stove, which is why it is mini. But among the respondents there are those who consider this to be a flaw, since too few products fit on the baking sheet, which increases the cooking time, for example, of pies.
