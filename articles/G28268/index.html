The best hotels in Prague in the city center: overview, description, rating and reviews

Prague is the capital of the Czech Republic, one of the most beautiful cities not only in Europe, but also in the world. Every year this city gains popularity as a tourist destination. Here you can walk for days on end, but never see at least a quarter of the sights. As Kafka said: “Prague never lets go.” So it is, having come here once, you strive to return again and again in order to inspect what you did not manage to or once again get to where you already managed to visit. Here is such a wonderful city!

In view of the growing interest from tourists in the city, more and more hotels are appearing to develop the region, and the old ones, built decades ago, are undergoing reconstruction to meet the requirements of the modern tourist market. The best Prague hotels in the city center are located. After all, most of the guests come here just to explore the old town, which is located in the Prague 1 area.

Description

Once Prague was the capital of Bohemia. This is a typical European city: with squares, a promenade and bridges over the Vltava River, golden-domed cathedrals and towers, red-tiled roofs, numerous monuments, etc. Despite the fact that the Czech Republic was also involved in the Second World War, the city was practically not damaged and retained its historical value. The modern part of the city is also interesting. There are many modern buildings, restaurants and cafes. Nevertheless, tourists are drawn to the historical center, which is located in the Prague 1 area. It is here that the best hotels in Prague are located. In the city center, living is very convenient for tourists. You can wander late into the old streets and not worry about how, and on what to get to the right place.

Old Prague

That is the name of the district number 1 of the Czech capital. This is the historic city center. Here at every step you can meet the attraction. Moreover, one that can take your breath away. Each kind of old Prague seemed to have come off a postcard. Hotels in Prague in the city center are also located in beautiful old buildings, and having settled in them, it seems to tourists that they traveled by time machine and are in the Middle Ages.

Device

Since 2001, Prague has been conditionally divided into 22 administrative districts (districts), but for tourists there are only 10, and here the old city is called Prague 1. Of course, the Czech capital is not among the most ancient cities in Europe, but it has a special color, a kind of aura that makes it attractive to millions of tourists. Charles Bridge is especially interesting for them . The rulers of the city, Czech rulers liked to walk on it. Nearby there is their residence - Prague Castle - well, a very grandiose building. It is interesting to know that this is the largest castle in the world. In Prague 1, of particular interest is the Josefov quarter, where the Jews lived. Oddly enough, the German invaders did not want to destroy it, but decided to leave it in memory of the fact that representatives of the biblical people or, as they called them, the "lower race" once lived here. In the same area is only part of the "small country", consisting of baroque castles. Hotels in Prague in the city center often look at one or another attraction. And this is another plus. Prague 2 is also considered one of the important districts of the capital. True, it is located on the other side of the Vltava. This district is the cultural center of the city, because there are many monuments, many of which are included in the UNESCO cultural heritage list. And, nevertheless, when buying a tour, tourists choose hotels in Prague in the city center, that is, in area 1. Of course, they are more expensive than those located in the periphery. But their guests rarely use transport, walk a lot, if only the shoes were comfortable. So, which hotel is better to stay at? Further in the article we give the rating of Prague hotels in the city center.

general information

In Prague, it is customary to evaluate hotel service on a five-point scale. And most importantly, here you will find full compliance with the characteristics. So, hotels in Prague in the center of the city 3 * completely correspond to their category. By the way, in this country, even in 1 and 2-star hotels, tourists are perfectly served, they just do not have a large number of services. You also need to consider that in the center even “treshki” are not cheap, but in distant areas from the center you can find a hotel with a wide range of services and much cheaper. In a three-star hotel, prices start at CZK 2000 . In almost all hotels, the staff, besides Czech, also speaks English, and if you're lucky, they are also Russian.

The best five-star hotels (5 *)

Three Storks. Already at the entrance to the design hotel “Three Storks”, you are breathtaking from luxury. It is located in Malaya Prague, in the building of the old monastery brewery dating back to the 14th century, and next to it is the famous Charles Bridge, a 5-minute walk away. The feeling that you will spend your night in a building that has historical value is incredible. The biggest advantage of the hotel is its high location, with views of the red roofs of the houses. Despite the fact that the building is very old, the furniture in it is new, designer, but with an imitation of antiquity. All appliances in the rooms are ultra modern.

Occidental Praha Wilson. This hotel is within close proximity of the National Museum. The service here is at a very high level and is fully consistent with European standards of hospitality. Tourists are provided with bathrobes and slippers, a hairdryer and a set of towels. Internet throughout the hotel is wireless and free.

Merchants Crown. On the main square of Prague, Wenceslas, this magnificent hotel is located. Merchants Crown provides tourists with gigantic-sized rooms that include kitchenettes with appliances and utensils.

Prague: 4 star hotels in the city center

Oasis Prague Apartments. This 4-star apartment hotel is located on Petrska Street. The building was last reconstructed in 2013. It is also located next to Wenceslas Square, which means in the very center of Prague. As in most Czech hotels, the rooms have kitchens, therefore, tourists can make coffee or tea, and sometimes scrambled eggs. The rooms have balconies or terraces with magnificent views of the cityscapes. Room service is just wonderful. The staff is helpful and caring.

Emporio Prague Apartments 4 * is another hotel located in an old mansion house built in the neoclassical style. Old Town Square is 700 meters away . A Powder Tower is 5 minutes from the hotel.

Prague: 3 Star Hotels in the City Center

Hotel Kampa Garden 3 *. Despite the fact that this is a budget hotel, it is located in the old city, and therefore it is very popular. Charles Bridge is 200 meters away. By the way, from the airport you can get here in 10 minutes. The hotel has a mini-bakery and guests can enjoy hot pastries in the morning. There are many options for leisure: mini golf, billiards, a casino, a massage room, etc. There is a playground in front of the hotel for kids.

City Partner Hotel Atos3 *. Another economical hotel. It is located in the heart of the city. Most of the attractions can be reached on foot. These are Children's Island, Kampa Park, Prague Castle and Old Town Square.

Three Star Hotel Ibis Old Town

And this hotel, included in the ranking of the best hotels in the city, is located in an old district in the very center of Prague. It offers a restaurant with delicious cuisine and a bar where you can enjoy the best drinks. The hotel also has a heated pool, sauna and solarium. All these procedures are paid.
