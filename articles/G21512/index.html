How many fly to the Bahamas from Moscow, what are the options?

Before sending to the famous beaches of the Bahamas archipelago, a lot to think about. For example, how much time will have to fly. Choosing a route is a responsible event. It is important not only to successfully determine the time of departure and return, but also to consider the services of a reliable airline. You can only leave from Moscow to the Bahamas with a transfer, since the distance is too great for a non-stop flight. If you are traveling with children or elderly people, then every extra hour in the air matters, as does the length of time you wait for transfers at transit points.

What airports are there in the Bahamas?

In total, the country has 60 runways. But not all of them accept international flights, most cooperate with domestic airlines. Given how popular the local beaches are with the powerful, there is nothing surprising in the fact that it is much easier to see a private yacht or plane than a car.

There are two options for the Russians:

  1. The Linden Pindling Airport on New Providence Island in Nassau, the capital of the Bahamas. It is the largest and well-equipped with everything that guests may require.
  2. Airport on the island of Greater Bahama in the city of Freeport. It is inferior to its predecessor only in size, but otherwise no less comfortable.

You can go to any of these islands with transit stops in the USA or Europe. How long to fly from Moscow to the Bahamas depends on the decision made. It should be borne in mind that Russians do not need a visa to visit local luxurious beaches. In 2018, you can freely travel to the islands by reserving hotel rooms. But since you cannot do without intermediate stops, you will need either a Schengen or an American transit visa.
