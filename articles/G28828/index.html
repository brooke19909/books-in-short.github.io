Turkey, Holiday Garden Resort 5 *: hotel reviews

Hot and dry summers, the warm Mediterranean Sea, quality service in hotels where all-inclusive, a lot of interesting excursions and developed tourist infrastructure - all this can be provided by sunny Turkey. "Holiday Garden Resort" is the name of the five-star hotel, which will be the focus of our article today. This hotel is chosen by our compatriots. There are many reviews about her, mostly positive. We grouped them and analyzed. The result is a detailed description of the hotel, which, we hope, will be useful to you. The hotel is located in the village of Okurcalar, the most western satellite resort of Alanya. This location makes it close to another Turkish city - Side. About what to do in Okurcalar and what to see, also read in our article.





Where is it and what is it

The Anatolian Riviera is what Turkey attracts our compatriots in the summer. Holiday Garden Resort is located in its far west. 35 km from the city of Alanya, and 85 km from Antalya International Airport even further. You can get to the hotel (if your transfer package does not include a transfer) by bus to Side. Hotel Holiday Garden Resort is located on the first line of the sea. Immediately after it passes the road connecting the resort towns of Incekum and Avsallar. On it with a small interval dolmushi, local minibuses ply. So, despite the location remote from Alanya, tourists in Okurcalar do not feel cut off from the world. "Holiday Garden Resort" is a small hotel across the territory, consisting of one six-story building. It was built in 2005, and the last reconstruction took place in 2010. This is a chain hotel. Her management is part of the Alba Resort group. Although the territory of the hotel is small, but, according to reviews, very beautiful and well-groomed. The building stands near the road. It adjoins the magnificent open terrace of the restaurant. Immediately behind the body are pools with water slides. And finally, the territory of the hotel ends with its own beach.





Where do they live

Most of the rooms in the hotel are classified as standard. Each room has a spacious balcony. It offers amazing views of the sea or the green hills that the Alanya coast (Turkey) is so famous for. Holiday Garden Resort has specially equipped two rooms for disabled guests so that each guest feels comfortable and comfortable. The bathroom has a shower. And the suites are equipped with a jacuzzi. What pleasantly surprised the guests was the carpeted floor. The rooms have everything that relies on the Turkish "five": hairdryer, flat-screen TV with cable channels, individual air conditioning and the like. What the reviews say is a very thorough cleaning. It is done without any tips. And if you leave a couple of lire for the maids, they will create different shapes for you from towels: swans, hearts and the like. A minibar and safety deposit box - warn reviews - are available for a fee.

Pools and beach

Of course, not a single "all inclusive" person lives. We are attracted by the hot sun and the warm Mediterranean Sea - what summer Turkey is rich in. Services for the smallest

In a republic such as Turkey (the Holiday Garden Resort hotel is no exception), they are especially kind to children.
