Lucerne (Switzerland) - a resort rich in architectural and natural attractions

An ancient city with a huge number of attractions, a real gem of the country and the heart of its central part - all this is Lucerne. Switzerland is a very beautiful country with many crystal clear lakes, high mountains with snowfields, rocky shores and interesting architectural structures. No wonder hundreds of thousands of tourists come here every year, because most of the local resorts are of the highest class. Lucerne stands out with a special flavor, only here you can see painted houses, old wooden bridges, fortress walls. Lovers of outdoor activities will enjoy hiking in the mountains, skiing and sledding. Couples in love can stroll along the promenade or sit in local restaurants. Both children and adults will enjoy cruises on the picturesque lakes that Lucerne is rich in.



Switzerland will provide guests with a varied vacation, so you will definitely not be bored here. Lucerne attracts with its picturesque location. It is located on the banks of the Royce River and Lake Firvaldsteterze, on all sides it is surrounded by high mountains with snowy peaks, it is they who fill the air with purity and freshness. The town is small, so exploring the main attractions will not take much time. During the day you can visit museums and admire the architectural structures that Lucerne has.

Switzerland is especially beautiful in the dark, so you should buy tickets for an evening cruise to enjoy the bewitching spectacle of the illuminated banks of canals and lakes. The hallmark of the city is the 170-meter wooden bridge, which is considered the oldest in all of Europe. It is indoor, under the roof are paintings by Heinrich Wagmann. Not all of them are in good condition, since in 1993 there was a fire that charred some items, they did not begin to restore them in order to maintain originality.
