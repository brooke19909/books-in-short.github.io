Tours to Germany or how to choose hotels in Berlin.

Tourists of different age groups and social backgrounds are now very popular tours to Berlin. The unique spirit of this city is created by numerous architectural monuments, museums, lush greenery, the very rhythm of life. Guests of Berlin can easily alternate exploring local attractions with exciting shopping. After all, the prices here please not only those who are looking for the best hotels in Berlin, but also those who shop at numerous shops, salons, boutiques and shops. Local cuisine will surely appeal to travelers who have always wanted to try traditional German dishes. Everyone will find something to their liking here.

In Berlin you can not only relax, but also improve your health. There are many wellness and spa facilities. Professional doctors conduct treatment programs, and in general, medical care here is at the highest level.

Visitors love to visit the beach, which is located on Lake Wannsee. And the Berliners themselves are happy to go for a walk in Grunwald - this is a huge forest, which is famous far beyond the city limits.

Berlin hotels are modern and diverse, they are known throughout the world. International, exclusive, elegant, wellness, affordable and luxurious - any tourist will be able to choose the most suitable place to stay. Reviews of Berlin hotels are mostly positive, although sometimes there are some misunderstandings such as a lack of traditional cuisine or rude staff at the hotel. In this case, the human factor must be blamed for everything .

In the center of Berlin is the aristocratic hotel Adlon Kempinski Berlin 5 *. Each room in it has a unique interior and designers paid great attention to details. Among 328 rooms there are 2 specially equipped for disabled people, as well as 6 for those who suffer from allergic diseases. The local elevator has been preserved since the 30s, and the luxurious lobby with a beautiful fountain create a cozy atmosphere. For guests there are two winter gardens, 12 boutiques, a restaurant, 5 conference rooms, a library, halls for special occasions, as well as parking.

Hotels in Berlin is represented by another five-star hotel Concorde Berlin. It is located in a 17-story building in the heart of the city. So tourists can easily get to the local zoo, shops, restaurants and nightclubs. The hotel has 311 rooms, and some even have a kitchenette and a guest toilet. There is also an extensive infrastructure: a business center, a beauty salon, an elite club, a dance hall, as many as 16 conference rooms and a concert hall.

Berlin hotels that have 4 stars are also quite popular with tourists. The family-run Hotel Artim was recently built, but is already in great demand. Here guests can book interesting excursions, buy tickets for a concert, to an opera or to a theater. Vacationers will undoubtedly appreciate the tastefully furnished rooms, among which there are also for the disabled and non-smokers. The hotel has a large conference room equipped with modern facilities.

Guests of the city who want to save even more on accommodation can safely go to the Hotel Brandies 3 *. It is located in a building built in 1904, but recently it was reconstructed. The hotel has 37 comfortable rooms, a tour desk and laundry facilities.

Among the hotels in Berlin you can find decent hotels with a category of 2 stars. For example, Hotel Abendstern 2 * has a very convenient location and 42 rooms that are equipped with air conditioning, TV and even Internet access. Guests have access to a business center, safe and luggage storage.

In any case, Berlin will leave only pleasant memories. No problems with accommodation. After all, more than 100 hotels of various categories are at the disposal of tourists, among which one can choose the most worthy.
