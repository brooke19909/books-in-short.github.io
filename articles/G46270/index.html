Recreation facilities in Vladivostok

Legendary chamor

All of Russia became aware of this word after the release of the album "Mummy Troll", which was called "Shamora". This is the city's most popular and beloved by all residents. On its shore there is any recreation center. Shamora, Vladivostok and its amazing surroundings attract both tourists and local residents. There really is plenty to choose from: comfortable pensions, cozy cottages, guest houses and much more for every taste. At the height of the summer season there are rides, barbecue, restaurants. Vacationers, if you wish, can ride a water bike, fly a glider, go windsurfing, and there are open rental points for sports equipment. The huge beaches of the bay are equipped with sun loungers and gazebos. The recreation area is fenced off the road, there are large parking lots.





The recreation center "Sadko"

Primorye offers relaxation for people with different incomes, everyone will find an option here to their liking. The recreation center "Sadko" is very popular. Vladivostok provides a full range of leisure services, all bases are located in ecologically clean areas. This base is located in a picturesque suburb on the territory of the bay Lazurnaya (Shamora). There is all the necessary infrastructure: parking, cafe, volleyball court, gazebos. Also a sauna, a bathhouse, barbecue places, rental of beach equipment, boats and catamarans. Discos and excursions are regularly held. There is a private beach. The houses are quite comfortable, they have the necessary furniture and kitchen utensils.

Where else to relax

In Primorye there are many unique places for a beach holiday. Most people choose islands for vacation : Popova, Reiniki, Petrova, Antipenko, Rikorda, Zmeiny and many others. For outdoor enthusiasts, the northern part of the region is excellent. Here you can go rafting on the river, go fishing, take part in tours and excursions. In the south of Primorye, in the Vostok Bay, the village of Livadia is located - a favorite place for many tourists. This is where the best beach vacation (Vladivostok) is. Recreation facilities here offer magnificent beaches of white sand. The swimming season begins in mid-June, the water warms up to +24 degrees. The recreation centers of the village are located on the shore of Reef Bay. In these places there are many beautiful landscapes that leave an indelible impression. Private accommodation, cottages are provided for vacationers, any recreation center of your choice . Shamora, Vladivostok, Russian Island, Rynda, Zerkalnaya Bay are the main and favorite vacation spots of most Primorye residents.





How to plan a vacation

The climate of Vladivostok has its own characteristics. Summer here does not start strictly on the calendar. It comes in the second half of June. The best time for a vacation is the end of July and August. These days the heat is usually hot and lasts until the end of September. This is the best period, the so-called "velvet season." Want to organize a great vacation? Vladivostok, recreation centers of the city will completely satisfy the needs of even the most demanding travelers. This is the best place for lovers of water sports, extreme sports, boat trips, eco-tourism. Having visited Primorye, you will be charged with energy for a long time and bring a lot of impressions and vivid memories!
