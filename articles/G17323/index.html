LCD "Park European" Volgograd: description and reviews

The well-known place of the Battle of Stalingrad, the hero city of Stalingrad, is now renamed Volgograd. It is located in the southern part of the state, on the banks of the wide Volga River. This is a very promising city for developers, as its population has crossed the line of one million people. Among the new buildings in Volgograd, "Park Evropeisky" stands out.





City Volgograd

The former outpost of the Volga Way, now Volgograd is a large million-plus city. Its convenient location (on the banks of the Volga River), warm climate, good ecological situation attract many immigrants from the Far North, from Siberia, the Far East. Residential "European Park" in Volgograd is pleased to offer customers comfortable apartments.





Today, the city maintains a very favorable environmental situation, which is very important for those who move here for permanent residence from other regions. Mostly only motor transport pollutes the atmosphere, as there are few industrial enterprises in Volgograd.

Location of the complex

The complex is located on the border of the Kirov and Soviet districts of Volgograd. His specific address is 64 Army Street, next to Lyachin and Sanatorna Streets. This area is considered not only environmentally friendly, but also dynamically developing. Here are the largest shopping centers, leading educational institutions, all objects of social infrastructure.

LCD "Park Evropeisky" in Volgograd is located in a zone of excellent transport accessibility. From the city center, you can get directly to the residential complex on your own car along Universitetskiy Prospect, which smoothly turns into 64 Army Street. The road will take no more than 15-20 minutes. A large number of fixed-route taxis also go to the complex.





Ecological situation

For the construction of his project in Volgograd - "European Park" - the developer chose the most favorable region from the point of view of the environmental situation. The complex is located at a distance of about 15 km from the city center. There are no industrial enterprises near it. On the contrary, it is surrounded by a green mass of trees. And at a distance of about 1 km, the wide Volga River flows, on the banks of which it is so good to relax on a hot day or take a walk in the evening.
