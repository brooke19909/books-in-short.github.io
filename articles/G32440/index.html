Synosse Hotel 4 * (Turkey, Kemer): description, photos and reviews of tourists

The relatively small town of Kemer is known to the Russians as a resort with beautiful beaches and excellent climate. Good weather, mountains, coniferous forests - all this attracts tourists here. In addition, Kemer is famous for its hotels, which have a fairly developed infrastructure.





general information

The high season at this Turkish resort begins in May and lasts until November. First of all, those who choose a beach vacation come to Kemer. Many tourists combine it with a visit to local historical and cultural monuments. Today, many Russian travel agencies offer tours to this Turkish resort. In Kemer, there are about one hundred and seventy hotels with different categories of "stars". Here, any tourist can find for himself an accommodation option for every taste, from budget economy hotels to luxurious expensive apartments. Hotels in Kemer can be divided into two types: the former are more suitable for outdoor activities, young people travel here more often, and the latter are suitable for regular family vacations with children. Almost all hotels have their own entertainment program. One of these hotels is Synosse Hotel (ex. Marinem Diana Hotel 4 *).

Description and photo

It was built in 2004 on the first line of the Mediterranean. The hotel has a rather convenient location: it is located in the center of the resort area, in the village of Beldibi, only two hundred and thirty meters from the coast and a kilometer from the harbor. Bright flowers, convenient walkways, clean well-groomed territory - all this immediately catches the eye of tourists arriving at the Synosse Hotel 4 *. Antalya Airport is only thirty-five kilometers away, which is very important for the parents of those children who do not tolerate a long transfer. There is a bus stop near the entrance to the hotel. Numerous shops and cafes are located nearby, where you can enjoy delicious Turkish coffee. The last major renovation in this hotel was carried out in 2014. It was then that its name changed. Previously, the hotel was called Marinem Diana Hotel, and today - Synosse Hotel 4 *. The total area is six and a half thousand square meters, mainly a park area. By travel agencies, the hotel is positioned as an option for families. And this is true: it provides all the conditions for young children. The convenient location of the hotel, in particular in terms of exploring local attractions, is another of its advantages.





Infrastructure

Being a four-star, Synosse Hotel (ex. Marinem Diana Hotel 4 *) has a fairly developed infrastructure. Even some of the five-star hotels often cannot boast of such a range of services offered to customers. Its infrastructure includes: a hairdresser and a paid office for emergency medical assistance, a conference center with one room, laundry, concierge services, and dry cleaning. The hotel has two small shops, including a souvenir. On the ground floor of the administrative building there is a coffee machine, a juice machine, and there is also an ATM. The point where the currency is exchanged works right there. The hotel has its own outdoor parking, provided to customers free of charge and without reservation. The registration desk works round the clock, without a break. Here you can fax or duplicate documents.

Hotel Policies

Check-in at the Synosse Hotel 4 * (Kemer, Beldibi) is fast enough. If there is currently a free number of visitors, you have to wait only fifteen minutes. At the registration desk, residents will be given a listing of all the services offered at this hotel. In all public areas, residents have the opportunity to connect to the Internet for free.

Upon departure, tourists, according to the rules in force at the hotel, vacate the rooms they occupy at noon. For the maximum convenience of customers, they are allowed to pay the bill not only in cash, but also with a plastic card. Those who prefer this calculation option are recommended to discuss this issue with the administration in order to avoid misunderstandings.

Housing stock

Synosse Hotel 4 * (Beldibi) is considered to be an average hotel in size. It has a total of one hundred and forty-two numbers. The hotel can accommodate a maximum of three hundred and eighty people at a time. The rooms are located in four-story buildings. The rooms are designed according to the category of hotel. They are decorated in light colors. The interior uses many decorative elements made of wood. Comfortable furniture, lighting to promote complete relaxation, curtains with bedspreads as a continuation of the overall color scheme - all this is thought out in order to make tourists as comfortable as possible in the rooms of the Synosse Hotel 4 * in Kemer.





Room furnishings

In addition to comfortable beds and nightstands, a wardrobe and a desk with a chair, the rooms also have a TV (with one Russian channel), a paid safe and a minibar. All rooms have a standard category. They feature small furnished balconies with pool or patio views. The rooms have laminate flooring.

Bathrooms combined. Bathrooms include a shower and washbasin with nightstands. On the floor are ceramic non-slip tiles. The rooms are cleaned daily, with the same frequency, the maids update all the necessary accessories in the bathroom: shampoo, liquid soap, etc. Bed linen is changed two or three times a week.

Nutrition

Synosse Hotel 4 * (Turkey, Beldibi) operates only on an all-inclusive basis. This concept is valid for hotel guests from morning until eleven in the evening. Meals include not only regular, but also brunch, as well as lunch and, of course, dinner. Residents of this hotel on an all-inclusive basis have the right to receive snacks during the entire time set by the administration, as well as hardware coffee, tea and certain sweets. Refreshments and alcohol from a local producer, such as beer, wine, crayfish, gin, vodka, etc., can also be taken for free. However, for ice cream, freshly squeezed juices, whiskey, champagne, etc., you already need to pay extra.

Breakfast is served at eight in the morning, it lasts two hours, lunch at noon, and dinner at seven in the evening. The tables are served as a buffet in the main indoor restaurant, designed for three hundred people, and one hundred in the open room located on the terrace. The institution also works by reservation.

In addition, on the territory of Synosse Hotel 4 * there are also two bars. One of them is by the pool, where the set time for tea is from half past four to five thirty in the evening, and the second in the lobby.





Beach

The hotel is located on the first line from the shores of the Mediterranean Sea. According to the reviews of Russians who have already visited the Synosse Hotel 4 *, it has an excellent location: its own beach is only a hundred and fifty meters away from the buildings. You have to go to it across the road, which, however, is not particularly busy with automobile traffic. The entrance to the water is sand and pebble, the coating on the beach is the same, so the presence of rubber special slippers for swimming is simply a must. Those who did not bring them with them should not be upset, because they can be purchased at any nearby store, including the hotel. The length of the strip for swimming and tanning is one hundred and fifty meters. There is no need to pay for the use of umbrellas, deck chairs and mattresses. Paid are only all types of water activities.

For children

Synosse Hotel 4 * (Turkey, Kemer, Beldibi) is positioned more as a family, so many Russians come here with their children. And this is the right decision. Excellent conditions have been created for young guests at the Synosse Hotel 4 * (Turkey). Here, children can play safely in the playground or splash around in the shallow pool area under the supervision of a special worker.

In addition, the hotel restaurant for young customers provides a diet table and high chairs, convenient for feeding, and the rooms provide the opportunity to get an extra bed. On the territory there are comfortable swings, as well as a children's playroom.





Additional Information

On the territory of Synosse Hotel (Beldibi, 4 *) there is an outdoor pool with a low slide, open from eleven in the morning until four in the afternoon. For those tourists who do not want to swim in the sea, it will be convenient to swim in it and sunbathe on the trestle beds located around. For a comfortable stay at the pool in the hotel all conditions are created. There are a lot of umbrellas and sunbeds, as well as a bar. To comply with the rules of hygiene and safety of tourists, drinks are dispensed in disposable glasses. Throughout the hotel there is wireless Wi-Fi (free service), a boutique works, and there is also a TV lounge.

Lovers of active pastimes and those who prefer to lead a healthy lifestyle can visit the sauna and Turkish hammam for free. You must pay extra for a whirlpool and massage course. In the evenings, the territory of Synosse Hotel 4 * hosts discos and shows.

Paid and free entertainment at the hotel

Positioned more by Russian tour operators as a place for families, the hotel also offers aerobics and aqua aerobics classes, here you can play darts and table tennis, chess. Reviews on the quality of fitness center services are mostly positive. There is also a modern gym. All of these activities, as well as volleyball on the beach are free. However, for billiards, the services of a photographer and a diving center, which provides an instructor and related equipment, as well as for all kinds of sports on the beach, you have to pay.





Excursions

Turkey, in particular Kemer, among Russians is primarily associated with holidays on the Mediterranean beach, however, having arrived here, many want to get acquainted with the ancient history of the region, to visit the centuries-old ruins of ancient cities and temples. Fifteen kilometers from Kemer, you can see the once flourishing ancient Olympos, in which, according to legend, the gods lived. Here you can see the classical theater and thermal baths, lined with beautiful mosaics. In the vicinity of Kemer, another no less surprising place is Yanartash, or the "Fire Mountain". Here you can observe how gas clots that burst from the ground ignite when they come in contact with air. Judging by the reviews of our tourists, this is an incredibly bewitching sight, especially in the dark. At the foot of Mount Yanartash is also a dilapidated temple of Hephaestus.

To the west of Kemer there is an open-air ethnographic museum. Having visited it, tourists will be able to learn all about the life of many nomadic peoples, get acquainted with ancient crafts and dishes of different eras.

The cost of excursions depends on the pricing policy of the agency in which they are purchased. For example, a boat trip to Kekova will cost an average of forty-five dollars. Judging by the reviews, many tourists bought sightseeing tours in the office, which is located on the ground floor of the administrative block.





Synosse Hotel 4 *: reviews

The vast majority of our compatriots who have already visited this hotel were satisfied with their choice. They liked the convenient location: a three-minute walk from the sea and the proximity of many shops and cafes. Tourists leave a lot of positive reviews about the work of the staff, politely and prudently solving all the problems that arise. Receptionists also received praise: check-in is very fast.

As for the quality of food, it is, as stated in the reviews, quite diverse, the main thing is tasty. A lot of vegetables, salads, fruits, there is something to choose from meat and fish. Some even separately thank the chef for the excellent food, although there are some who complain about the stuffiness in the closed part of the restaurant and the scarcity of the menu with a low occupancy of the hotel.

The sea in this part of the Turkish coast is clean, and although there are always a lot of people on the beach in high season, sun loungers can be found at any time. The nature around the hotel is simply amazing. Around the mountains and greenery, so from the balcony you can spend hours admiring this beauty. Many residents of Kemer have a good understanding of Russian. On the streets of the city you can find signs in the native language of the Russians. In addition, the Turks are unobtrusive people, they behave quite correctly and always try to help.

As a result, we can say that only five percent of Russians who visited the Synosse Hotel 4 * were unsatisfied with the choice of the hotel, the rest liked it so much that they intend to return again.
