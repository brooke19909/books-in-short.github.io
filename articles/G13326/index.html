Russian smartphone YotaPhone 2: owner reviews, specifications

The YotaPhone story has many facets. They belong to both the political sphere and public relations at the state level. The device was discussed by many experts, but none of them could fail to note that the history of the device has some political feature, inextricably linked with it.

As you know, Yota Devices has a co-owner. This is a state-owned corporation called Rostec. Its leader is currently Sergei Chemezov. Back in 2010, he at one of the presentations showed what the YotaPhone will look like. Moreover, this presentation was held among the first persons of our state. From this moment on, the attention of government officials to the project becomes very intense. And it cannot be said that every year it weakened. At least it remained the same, and this is the position of skeptics. Realists, on the basis of facts, say that every year the attention paid to the project of the first Russian smartphone only increased.

Speaking of the case, the creation of YotaPhone is the very case in which the result is not only proportional to the funds invested in the project, but equal to it. There are no weakening coefficients, and in front of us is a really working product that will be suitable for use, and not just as another decoration for exhibitions. Such cases, judging by statistics, are actually rare.

Only a year passes, and the second generation of the YotaPhone smartphone is ready. In it, unlike the first trial (and nevertheless quite decently working) device, a whole range of the latest features is implemented. Technical equipment allows you to push the device to a new level, and also creates the ability to bookmark "technical chips" in it, which are inherent only to the second generation of YotaPhone.

And be that as it may, the original turning point was not the moment of the presentation. The hype of the tangent generation of the Russian smartphone YotaPhone 2, the reviews of which we will consider in this article, has intensified even earlier.

This happened due to inappropriate screen sizes.

Dimensions





In all three planes, the dimensions of the device are as follows: height is 144 mm, width is 69.5 mm, and thickness is 8.9 mm. The weight of the device is 145 grams.

Strength





A very good feature in this regard is given by YotaPhone 2 owner reviews about it. The smartphone case is made of matte plastic. Coverage of screens on both sides of the device - Corning Gorilla Glass. Immediately it is worth saying that the quality is quite good, the screen is durable. It is also coated with an oleophobic coating that effectively masks fingerprints.

Ease of use





Thanks to well-selected dimensions, the phone is quite comfortable in the hand. With comfort during operation, no problems should arise. In this regard, a lot of devices are left behind YotaPhone 2. Performance





The Qualcomm family chipset was used as the basis for the design of the device, or more precisely, the Snapdragon 800 model. By today's standards, this is not the most powerful chipset, but when the device began to be developed, it was the best option.

The YotaPhone 2 smartphone, the design reviews of which were disassembled earlier, boasts 4 processor cores that operate at 2.25 GHz. Time passed, and the chipset was finalized, new variations were created, allowing some adjustments in terms of a balance of performance, in terms of optimizing power consumption.
