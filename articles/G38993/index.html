Hotel Accommodation Policies

Check-in and check-out at Dionis Hotel Resort & SPA 5 * (Bogazkent) is carried out in the same way as in most hotels around the world. Check-in of the rooms of the category reserved by them is carried out from 14:00. However, despite this rule, the hotel complex administration is trying to do everything possible to immediately populate tourists arriving earlier than the specified time. Unfortunately, sometimes this is not possible due to the fact that in the midst of the season the hotel is almost 100 percent full. Therefore, newly staying guests have to wait until the previous guests have left the room and the maids have prepared it. In this case, you will be asked to leave things in the luggage storage room and stroll through the territory of Dionis Hotel Resort Bogazkent 5 *, have lunch, swim in the pool or immediately go to the beach. Check-out should be done before noon. If your plane leaves only in the evening, then for an additional amount you can extend your stay at the hotel. For payment of the hotel complex "Dionysus Resort & SPA" (Belek, Turkey), both cash and plastic cards are accepted.

Accommodation with children and pets

Since Dionis Hotel Resort & Spa 5 * is positioned as a family hotel, guests with children of all ages are welcome here. At the same time, if you place a child up to twelve years old on the existing sleeping places in your room, then you will not have to pay extra. For children under two years old a cot is provided free of charge. Please note that in each room you can install only one extra bed for a child. Adult beds are not available upon request at the hotel.

For tourists who do not want to leave their four-legged pets at home on vacation, the following information will be important. So, unfortunately, pets are not allowed at the Dionis Hotel Resort & SPA (Belek). Therefore, you will either have to leave your furry pet at home, or choose another hotel from the Pets Friend category.
