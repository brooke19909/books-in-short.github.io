Where better to relax on the Sea of ​​Azov? Of course, in Berdyansk!

The Sea of ​​Azov is one of the smallest seas on the planet. Shallow depth is both a plus and minus of the Sea of ​​Azov. Of course, not everyone wants to go 100 or 200 meters to swim normally. Although it is precisely because of the small depth that the Sea of ​​Azov manages to warm up quickly, which attracts families with small children.

Where to relax on the Sea of ​​Azov with minimal cost, but at the same time get a pleasant experience? Of all resorts, the port city of Berdyansk is the place where you can spend your summer vacation with benefit and pleasure.

What attracts tourists to Berdyansk

Berdyansk is a symbiosis of an industrial and resort city. As in other resorts of the Sea of ​​Azov, there is no such a huge influx of tourists as in Ukrainian or Russian Black Sea resorts. The city has a well-developed infrastructure, which attracts not only families with children, but also young people. There is a water park, a dolphinarium, a zoo and a lot of cafes and nightlife.





In Berdyansk, you can look at the original monuments of popularly popular characters. No, not Lenin or Marx, but Plumbing, Toads and Goby-breadwinner. Speaking of the latter, you can also go fishing and catch a couple of kilograms of an Azov bull, which is just a dime a dozen in the Berdyansk region. After all, where is it better to relax on the Sea of ​​Azov and really relax? Definitely fishing!



Due to the fact that in the center of Berdyansk there is an industrial zone, swimming on the central beaches is not recommended. So where better to relax on the Sea of ​​Azov? Vacationers mainly prefer to swim on the sandy beaches of the Berdyansk spit. The water on the spit is much cleaner than in the city center. Compared to Kirillovka or Genichesky, on the Berdyansk spit you can really swim in the sea, and not wander.

In Berdyansk, all conditions for extreme tourism are created. The city has a windsurfing club and a yacht club. You can also ride a jet ski, hang glider or an inflatable slide.





The healing air, warm sea and many types of entertainment make Berdyansk a very attractive resort. Where better to relax on the Sea of ​​Azov, now you know.

Housing and food

Housing in Berdyansk is much cheaper than in the Black Sea resorts. And this is another argument that attracts middle-class tourists to go to the Sea of ​​Azov. Where better to relax: in a boarding house or in the private sector? It all depends, of course, on your wallet. The most budget option is a room in the private sector. So, a double room with amenities on the street will cost between 50-75 hryvnia. The minimum cost of a room in local pensions and hotels without meals is 200 hryvnias.

However, there will be no problems with nutrition either. Berdyansk has many cafes and dining rooms with fairly affordable prices.

Holidays on the spit will be much more expensive than in the city. Here you have to pay at least 250 hryvnias per person for a room. Luxury rooms with all amenities cost from 500 hryvnia per person. On the spit is the main part of recreation centers, resorts and health camps.

So, if your budget is strictly limited, but at the same time you do not want to lose all the benefits of civilization, Berdyansk is the most acceptable place where it is better to relax on the Sea of ​​Azov.
