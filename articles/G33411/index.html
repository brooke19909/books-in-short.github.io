Bowling in the "Cuba" in Chelyabinsk: a great vacation for the whole family

TRC "CUBA" is located in the center of Chelyabinsk and is very successful. 100 meters to the north lies the most popular pedestrian street named after S. M. Kirova is a local Arbat, 4 public transport stops are located nearby and there is enough space for personal cars in the underground parking lot.

KUBA is an excellent multi-story shopping and entertainment complex, created on the basis of the reconstructed Chelyabinsk watch factory Molniya. On the 3 first floors there are a large number of different shops and cafes. There is almost everything that the soul desires.

Bowling in the family park

And on the 4th floor in the entertainment center "Planet Smiles" (the complete motown singles) there is an excellent bowling alley with 12 modern tracks.

Up to 6 people can play on one track. There is a sports zone and a zone of class "Comfort". Here you can not only have a great time playing, but also just relax in company, sitting on soft sofas.

For those who feel hungry during the game, the menu has a variety of dishes and drinks from the Asabi Restaurant.

Discounts, promotions, gifts

As in any self-respecting institution, there are a variety of motivational discounts and promotions in the bowling of the Chelyabinsk shopping and entertainment center KUBA. For example, those who give birthdays give 10% upon presentation of a passport. If the birthday boy came with friends to a banquet, then the discount is already rising to 30% and it can be used within 7 days after the date of birth.

Especially in the summer days, promotions for students and schoolchildren held in the morning and afternoon are popular. By presenting a passport, birth certificate or student card, you can get a discount of 30% of the cost of the game.

Discounts up to 50% on the game of bowling in the "CUBE" (Chelyabinsk) can be obtained by purchasing a certificate for services or the purchase of goods in specialized online stores. But we must remember that each proposal has a limited duration and clear conditions for participation.
