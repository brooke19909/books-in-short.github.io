Hotel "Ibis Krasnodar Center": address, photos and reviews

The capital of the Kuban, the city of Krasnodar, is one of the largest settlements in Russia. The distance from it to the Black Sea is approximately 150 km. Traditionally, Krasnodar is considered the "gateway to the Caucasus." Thousands of people come every year to this big city - business travelers, students, tourists. And of course, the first thing guests of Krasnodar try to take care of is renting good temporary housing for little money.

There are many hotels in the capital of the Kuban. Most of them offer their guests quite convenient service. For example, many visitors choose the Ibis Krasnodar hotel to stay in this city. Reviews about this hotel are mostly good.





Hotel address and telephone

The Ibis Krasnodar Hotel is located in the very center of the city. Judging by the reviews of many residents of the hotel, it is really located in a very convenient location. Within walking distance of the hotel are many interesting historical sights of Krasnodar. The ability to visit a variety of stores without spending a lot of time is also what the Ibis Krasnodar Hotel is praised on the network. Address of this hotel: st. 128, Krasnoarmeyskaya Street. Its postal code is 350015 and the telephone number is (861) 259-64-52.

How to get there

The easiest way to get from Pashkovsky airport to "Ibis Krasnodar" is by minibus No. 15. In this case, you should leave at the stop at ul. Hakurate. Next you need to go along the street. Red (about 200 m) before turning on Golovatovo. By car, you should go (focusing on signs) to the city center. The last street you need to turn onto is also Golovatovo.

If necessary, a shuttle service can be ordered at the hotel. There is a meeting at the airport and delivery to the territory at this hotel is inexpensive. Many tourists and business travelers who have rented rooms at this hotel use it. The presence of a transfer, of course, greatly adds to the hotel's attractiveness in the eyes of guests of the capital of the Kuban.





Ibis Krasnodar: general hotel description

This hotel is a huge multi-storey modern building with vertical architecture. The facades of the house are tiled. The overall color scheme of the building is blue. It looks, therefore, "Ibis Krasnodar" against the backdrop of urban high-rise buildings is very good. This hotel belongs to the three-star group. Her lobby has comfortable sofas and a TV. In the rooms located on the upper floors, tourists have the opportunity to climb the elevator.

Room Reviews

Accommodations

Check-in at the ibis Krasnodar hotel is at 14.00. Guests must leave their rooms no later than 12 hours on the last paid day. The reception desk at the Ibis Krasnodar Center is open around the clock. If desired, the hotel can order an accelerated check-in / check-out.

Smoking is not allowed in the rooms of this hotel. Pets are also not allowed in the hotel rooms. The hotel staff speaks both Russian and English. Which, of course, is quite convenient.

Business center

For business people, the hotel rents a conference room if necessary. You can also send faxes from the hotel or make photocopies of documents here. There is a large banquet room in the hotel.

Infrastructure Reviews

The hotel staff considers most tourists polite and helpful. Not only the administrators, but also the maids, as well as the technicians, treat their duties at the hotel well. The hotel rooms are carefully cleaned, and bed linen is always fresh and snow-white.

In addition to the restaurant, the hotel also has a bar where you can order any drinks. The institution is quite comfortable. Juices, tea, coffee, etc. can be ordered here without any problems. But many guests are somewhat unhappy that there is no alcohol in the hotel bar.





Instead of a conclusion
