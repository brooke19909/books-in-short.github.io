Stamps: types of stamps, rare collectibles

Since the mid-19th century, stamps have become one of the collectibles. Types of stamps have always reflected the social and political life of states, as well as the history of world mail.

Sign of postage

The first postage stamp appeared in London in 1840. It was an image of Queen Victoria on a black background worth one penny. "Black penny" - under this name is known this invention of Roland Hill, later a postal worker in England. This brand performed the role of the sign of the postage.





Industry and trade in Europe developed at that time at a rapid pace and demanded speed from the postal service. The volume of mail forwarding was large, and the fee for mail services was high. Therefore, only rich people could use the services of mail. The correspondence was paid by the recipient.

Hill's mother worked at the post office for a long time, so he knew about these shortcomings. And then a wonderful thought occurred to him: the delivery of the letter must be cheapened, and the sender must pay for it, who will receive a small receipt for paying for the service. The receipt is glued to the letter and is extinguished with a stamp that prevents its reuse.

So there were postage stamps.

Specialized stores offer postage stamps, prices for which start from tens of rubles to several hundred thousand dollars. In addition, an indicator of the culture of the people is the collection of items such as stamps. Types of brands, for example, unite people interested in an era, serve as a source of information for them, and form their aesthetic tastes.

Profitable investment

Investing in postage stamps is reliable, safe and profitable. They are always in demand. Especially if an instance falls into the category of rare ones. Rare stamps are copies that exist in a certain quantity. The fewer of them, the, accordingly, the price for such a brand will be higher.

This uniqueness is most often explained by oversight of printers. The sheet will not get the color you need, and now a rare brand is ready. Or the image is printed upside down. Or the size is the largest. Or there are no teeth along the edges.





Here are the most famous rare stamps:

  • Perm brand. It was released in St. Petersburg in 1879 on paper containing the watermark "honeycombs." And then there was an incident. Redeemed in Perm, it became the basis of the myth according to which the city became the birthplace of the brand. Today, only two such specimens are known.
  • "Tiflis brand". Issued in 1857 in Tiflis, printed on yellowish-white paper in strips of five pieces. Three instances are known;
  • "British black penny." It was released in 1840 in Britain. The very first brand.
  • "Saint Mauritius". Released in 1847, has an error in the inscription.
  • The Holy Grail. The rarity is due to the fact that only two copies are known.

The cost of these brands does not get smaller over time, and, therefore, buying a rarity of philately, a person makes a profitable investment. Any copy can be a collector's dream.

Collections of Soviet philatelists

Philately in the USSR was very popular. Numerous private collections are known that are for sale today. In general, stamps of the Soviet Union are expensive, but since demand is less than the number of collections themselves, their price is low.



The cost of USSR stamps today is this: everything that was released in the second half of the 20th century costs on average 5 rubles per copy, and the stamp must be in perfect condition and without any stamps; earlier cost 10-20 rubles.

Low prices are explained by the fact that material for collections is freely available by mail, the circulation of stamps is large.

Usually stamps are resold in albums consisting of several hundred pieces. The price of such a purchase is not very high, the album is intended for another purpose: to familiarize the owner with the world of collecting, to appreciate this hobby.

The most expensive brand of the USSR is the so-called "Cardboard". This is a souvenir sheet for each participant of the First All-Union Exhibition of Philately. Four stamps are printed on the sheet, some bear the inscription "Best Drummer." These sheets became valuable after one of them was sold at auction in New York for $ 766,000.
