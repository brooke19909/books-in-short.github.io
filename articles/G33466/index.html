How is Crochet applique made?





The photo clearly shows how you can decorate knitted children's things. Since it will be rather problematic to sew a fabric boat over dense material, the application is made in the form of individual elements with a hook. Suppose the bottom is blue. Select the thread of the appropriate shade and the right size knitting device. Dial 15-20 stitches and complete the first row with single crochet stitches. To expand the application around the edges at the beginning and end of the second row, knit several elements in one loop. So, when the base of the boat is ready, you can begin to manufacture sails. They look like two white triangles. In order for the parts to narrow from the base, the middle two or three columns of the previous row are knitted in the middle of the next together. Sew the completed parts to the product with hidden stitches, and decorate the boat with a flag (from several stitches). The application is additionally made out in waves.
