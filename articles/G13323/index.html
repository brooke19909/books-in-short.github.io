LCD "Vysokovo", Elektrostal: reviews

If you are looking for an apartment in Moscow, you are limited in means, but you are not ready to lose access to the capital's infrastructure, pay attention to the residential complexes of the Moscow Region. Currently, they are becoming more popular and in demand. Due to their own infrastructure and excellent transport accessibility, residents of such complexes will never feel remoteness from the capital. We want to bring to your attention LCD "Vysokovo" in Elektrostal. This is an excellent project that meets all the requirements of modern residents. Based on the feedback of real residents, we will give the project the most objective assessment.





about the project

The project of the residential complex "Vysokovo" is represented by three 17-story high-rise towers, erected using monolithic-brick technology, as well as a multi-sectional house of varying number of storeys. A multi-sectional house will be erected as part of the state program on the site of demolition of dilapidated housing. The unique architecture, the use of modern materials and innovative technologies is what sets the project apart from others and makes it so attractive.

The houses turned out to be beautiful, spectacular, many even call them decoration and a landmark of a small town near Moscow. Location

LCD "Vysokovo" (Elektrostal) occupies the eastern part of the city. For the construction of the facility, the intersection of Trudovaya and Zakharchenko streets was chosen. Many forums cite the proximity of industrial zones, the rumble of trains. But real residents who already managed to move into the apartments of the rented house did not notice these shortcomings. Moreover, the developer foresaw this in advance, so he used high-quality glazing to minimize noise. An area with good ecology, fairly clean air - this is how real estate buyers choose the place to build. Interest holders had the opportunity to observe all stages of construction through a cycle of videos. So, they noted that the concept of the maximum possible conservation of green spaces was laid in the project.

Transport accessibility

All those who preferred the Vysokovo residential complex referred to the relative proximity to the capital and transport accessibility. In order to get from the complex to Moscow, you should move in the direction of the Gorky highway. The traffic here is heavy, traffic jams are not uncommon, but due to the reconstruction of the highway and the appearance of new road junctions, it will be possible to improve the situation in the near future. From the Partizanskaya metro station to the new building, residents get on bus number 399, departing every 15-20 minutes. Moreover, you can get to the complex by train to the Mashinostroitel station, from it to the house only 5 minutes in a calm step.





Infrastructure

On the territory of the residential complex "Vysokovo" (RF, Elektrostal) a multi-level parking will appear. Within walking distance from it are the necessary objects of social infrastructure: kindergartens, schools, clinics, as well as shopping and entertainment centers, cafes, restaurants. As for parking: each resident of the complex can buy an additional car space and forget about the difficulties of finding free space upon returning home. Its cost is quite high, but you can pay for the comfort and safety of your car.

On the territory of the residential complex "Vysokovo" there is a children's and sports playground, flowerbeds and flower beds are broken. The developer took care of the improvement of the territory, organized platforms for walking and recreation. Near the new buildings there is a forest belt with a huge picturesque lake: the first inhabitants have already chosen it. If you want to live away from the hustle and bustle, plan to educate your children in the fresh air, you will definitely like the project. And do not be alarmed by the remoteness from the capital: at any time you can get to the center of Moscow both by personal and public transport.





Apartments, layouts

Surely you are worried about the apartment fund of the residential complex Vysokovo (Elektrostal). Reviews of the first residents confirm the variety of planning decisions. The choice of buyers are offered studios, one-, two- and three-bedroom apartments. We are used to seeing studios very tiny, but the builder started from the comfort of every resident, so even the studio has a full bathroom, toilet, wardrobe, spacious kitchen-living room and a balcony. Two-room and three-room apartments are presented with options with combined and separate bathrooms, isolated and adjacent rooms - everyone will find a great option for themselves, starting from their taste preferences and financial capabilities.





The houses were comfortable, the developer provided excellent heat and sound insulation properties of each apartment, so even the noisiest neighbors will not disturb your peace. At the same time, apartments are rented without finishing.

Price policy

The affordable price of apartments in the residential complex "Vysokovo" (Elektrostal) is what attracted the attention of potential buyers. At the moment, the complex has already been commissioned, put into operation, most of the apartments are populated, but the sale continues. You can still become a happy property owner. The cost of apartments is 1.6-3.3 million rubles. The cost primarily depends on the area that the apartment occupies. The cooperation of the developer with the largest banks allows you to obtain a mortgage loan on favorable terms.

In general, the Vysokovo LCD (Elektrostal) turned out to be excellent. The layout of the apartments, the successful location of the complex, well-thought-out infrastructure, focused on the needs of every modern resident of the metropolis - this is what all residents note. We strongly recommend paying special attention to the complex, be sure to visit it. Many, after examining the territory and the apartment, make a positive decision.
