Smartphone review Fly IQ4405 quad. Characteristics and reviews

Fly does not particularly boast of the number of advanced models. However, inexpensive devices are obtained from the company not bad. One of the interesting representatives of the company's public sector employees is the 4405 model.

Design





Appearance Fly IQ4405 quad can not boast of anything special. The design is quite familiar for low-cost devices. The case of the device is made of plastic, but a successful decision of the choice of material gives similarities with metal. The screen of the device is made of glass with a protective coating.

Front display, touch buttons, speaker, sensors and camera. The front side does not have any special frills. Behind the device is a speaker, a main camera and, of course, a flash.

On the side, on the left, is the volume control, and on the right is the power button. The bottom end got only a microphone, but the top located on itself jacks for headphones and usb.

In general, the design of the Fly IQ4405 quad came out pretty solid. Plastic, similar to metal, and rounded shapes give some zest to the device.

Screen





He got the Fly IQ4405 quad black with a diagonal of only 4.5 inches. Although such a display is enough for a budget employee, the impression spoils the resolution. The device has a total of 854 by 480 pixels, respectively, there is a noticeable grain size.

The quality of the IPS technology used slightly improves. The high viewing angles of the Fly IQ4405 quad and good brightness make working with the screen more enjoyable.

In principle, the display turned out to be quite good for a budget phone. Although in comparison with state employees of eminent companies it does not look particularly attractive.

Camera





Smartphone Fly IQ4405 quad black takes pictures of very poor quality. This situation is due to the installed camera of only 5 megapixels. For a device released in 2014, this is a huge miscalculation. The photo is obtained without detail and with the presence of a lot of noise.

The front camera, which has an eyelet of 0.3 megapixels, will not leave a good impression either. Such a camera is suitable only for video calls, and you can forget about the pictures.

Installation of such characteristics would be relevant in 2012, but for not the oldest device, this is just a minimum. Of course, the desire of the company to save and lower the cost of the device is understandable, but other parameters could be cut.

Filling





Surprisingly, the Fly IQ4405 quad processor with as many as four cores. Each of them has a frequency of 1.3 GHz. Such characteristics are wonderful and should please, but the overall picture is not the most pleasant.

Having equipped the device with a powerful processor, the company once again decided to save money and installed only 512 MB of RAM. This parameter is the minimum for modern devices and does not provide a special speed of operation.

There is little pleasant in the native memory, which is only 4 GB. But the installed "Android" takes away most of it. As a result, the user only needs 1.4 GB. This is not enough even to install the most necessary applications.

One gets the impression that the company simply cannot create the optimal balance. As a result of such miscalculations, both performance and the impression of the smartphone fall.

System

The device operates under the guidance of Android version 4.2.2. A pretty old system, but perfect for a 4405. Along with Android there is a familiar set of programs. A system update is possible, but it doesn‚Äôt make much sense, since the version will differ only in details.

Battery

The device got a battery with a capacity of only 1800 maH. Such a battery is quite enough for the device for continuous operation. Since the main consumers are display and filling, the charge will last for two days with economical use. With active operation, the battery lasts for 5-6 hours.

Price

The cost of the device looks somewhat overpriced. The price ranges from 7 to 8 thousand rubles, and this is a lot for the Fly IQ4405 quad. Customer reviews also tend to believe that the cost does not match the characteristics.

Equipment

The phone comes with a familiar set of instructions, a headset, an adapter and a usb cord. Headphones have a call acceptance button, but they do not stand out with special quality.

Advantages

The advantages of the device can be reliably attributed to a pleasant appearance. The smartphone looks pretty stylish and is not bad for women.

The main advantage of the device is its powerful processor. A small number of phones from the budget category can compare with its performance.

The selected battery looks quite acceptable in a smartphone. The small battery capacity is quite capable of ensuring the long-term operation of the device.

disadvantages

Significantly more negative aspects in the device. The biggest disadvantage is the small amount of internal memory. Only 1.4 GB will not satisfy even the most modest requests. The owner will be forced to additionally equip the smartphone with a flash drive.

A less noticeable drawback of the device is its RAM. Installed 512 MB is the minimum for Android phones.

Also, the Fly IQ4405 quad camera is not happy. Owner reviews indicate poor quality images even in the daytime.

Cost also causes discontent. Even market leaders have many more successful models in the same price category.

Reviews





The final verdict can only be drawn up using the Fly IQ4405 quad smartphone. Reviews will undoubtedly help to build up the initial impression, but the final conclusions remain with the buyer.

Total

After studying the characteristics of the device, there is a feeling that the company saved on all the necessary details exclusively for the processor. Installed four cores look like a black sheep on a general background.

Compared to its competitors, 4405 looks inconspicuous. It will be enough for the device to work, but you will probably have to forget about entertainment.
