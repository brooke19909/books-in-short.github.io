Where to go in Naberezhnye Chelny? Attractions, cultural monuments, amusement parks and recreation places

A visit to the beautiful city of the Republic of Tatarstan - Naberezhnye Chelny - will definitely leave memorable memories. The picturesque modern city will delight the tourist with a large number of attractions and interesting places.

In the article you will find the answer to the question of where to go and what to see in Naberezhnye Chelny.

general information

Due to the fact that the city was built from scratch and there was no need for architects to put houses in the space of old streets, the layout of the city was made on a special scale. Residential neighborhoods are separated by wide avenues, green alleys and footpaths.

There is a huge number of places where you can go to Naberezhnye Chelny, here everyone will find something to occupy their free time.

Two religions coexist on the territory of the city: Orthodox and Muslim. All signs and signs are written in two languages, and residents of the city, as a rule, speak both Russian and Tatar.

History of the city

According to historical data, the first settlements appeared on the territory of the modern city about 5 thousand years ago. The first mention of the settlement of Naberezhnye Chelny can be found in chronicles dating back to 1626. The status of the city was assigned to Naberezhnye Chelny only in 1930.

How to get there

You can get to Naberezhnye Chelny by plane from major Russian cities. So, for example, from Moscow every day on average five flights are made to Begishevo airport. You can get to the center by regular bus or taxi, the journey will take no more than 30 minutes.

At the entrance to the city there is a railway station, which daily receives a large number of trains from Moscow, Krasnodar and other cities.

Major Attractions

The first place to go to Naberezhnye Chelny is the embankment of the Kama River. A beautiful pedestrian zone stretches along the coastal park to the New Town. If you ask any local resident where to go for a tourist in Naberezhnye Chelny, the first thing you will hear is on the embankment. There are several free and paid beaches, there are swimming pools for children, you can rent a catamaran or a boat. There are a large number of cafes and clubs on the promenade. In addition, several bike and roller rental points are organized.

An equally popular pedestrian zone in the city is Enthusiast Boulevard. The boulevard is decorated with beautiful flower beds and a fountain. An annual festival dedicated to flowers is held here, those who were lucky enough to take part in this festival say that the spectacle was so beautiful that it could not be described in words.

Another interesting place to go to Naberezhnye Chelny is the business center 2/18. This is a round tower with a height of 25 floors. On the roof of the building is an observation deck with panoramic views of the city.

Anyone who does not know where to go in Naberezhnye Chelny must definitely go to Tsentralnaya Street. City authorities have restored part of the old village of Naberezhnye Chelny. In this place of the city you can see the life and life of people who lived here many years ago. This place is a cultural monument of the city.

Naberezhnye Chelny is famous for its area named after Vysotsky. It was here that a monument to the bard was erected. The monument is a 9-meter part of the guitar, on which you can read the poetry of the poet. A guitar weighs over 10 tons.

Cultural activities

Where can I go to Naberezhnye Chelny in the evening? The answer to this question is the Artisans Theater. The theater itself has an unusual architecture. It seems that the tree trunk sprouts directly through the walls and windows of the theater and surrounds the facade with its branches. Tickets for performances should be purchased in advance, as the performances are very popular with both local residents and city guests.

You can get into the atmosphere of culture and art in the art gallery of the city, here you can inspect artworks at exhibitions and attend a literary and musical evening. In addition, next to the art gallery, experienced guides organize small excursions for everyone, during which they talk about the monuments of the city on Enthusiast Boulevard.

Entertainment for children and adults

Among the places where you can go in Naberezhnye Chelny in order to have a good time, you should highlight the extreme park "Grenada". A beautiful rope park is organized here. Passing the maze will be interesting for both children and adults. In addition, the park has various outdoor fitness equipment. The park is open around the clock seven days a week. Entrance to the territory is free.

A favorite place for children is the Kid Space space. This is a children's town where a child can try himself in a particular profession. The territory of the game complex has a large number of stations. There is a hospital, a construction site, a school, a fire and police department, a veterinary clinic, and many other workplaces. Children are given a special uniform, and under the guidance of adults, they perform a task that is an integral part of the chosen profession. The time spent in the children's town is not only entertainment, it helps the child understand what profession he likes best.

Another attraction that will be interesting for tourists with children is the dolphinarium. Each show is an excellent program with the performance of dolphins.

Cafe

You can have a good time at the VineGret time cafe. The meaning of the cafe is that the guests of the restaurant pay only for the minutes they spent in the restaurant. During the purchased time you can drink hot drinks and lemonade in any quantity, play board games, watch movies and just chat with friends. The cafe is especially popular in the cold season. The cost of one minute in the first hour of visit is 2 rubles. The cafe is located at: Prospect Mira, 24p.

Summing up, it should be said that it is very easy to find a place to go to Naberezhnye Chelny, and sights to see. In the city there are cultural and entertainment events for every taste and age. The impressions of visiting Naberezhnye Chelny will be remembered for a lifetime.
