Mallorca (Spain) - paradise on earth

Mallorca (Spain) is a world-famous island, a favorite leisure destination for wealthy people who prefer to buy their property here in order to be able to relax at any time. The island is known for impeccably clean resorts, white sand beaches and the turquoise sea, magnificent nature, rich historical and cultural history, which, combined with entertainment programs, make the resort attractive to a large number of vacationers. Mallorca (Spain) is fascinated by the location of large cities side by side with small settlements that have completely preserved their usual way of life. The island is striking in its diverse and variable nature, mountains, plains and hilly landscapes.

Mallorca gained popularity due to its mild Mediterranean climate. The period from May to October is considered to be an excellent time of the year for relaxation. A huge number of beaches and cultural attractions can make your vacation unforgettable and interesting. The south side is very popular with the city of Palma, which is the capital of the island of Mallorca (Spain). Rainfall is very rare here, and this makes it possible to rest comfortably on the beaches. The northern part of the island is covered with green olive and orange trees.





Mallorca History

The island is inhabited by people from the Paleolithic era and for many years has been a strategic point controlled by Carthage. After his defeat, these beautiful places became the base of pirates, and in 123 BC they were conquered by the Roman troops, which gave rise to an era of prosperity. After the fall of the Roman Empire, the island was included in the kingdom of Vandals, and then Byzantium, which brought Christianity with it, but did not save such beautiful lands, which were soon captured by the Cordoban caliphate. And only in 1229, Mallorca (Spain), freed from Muslim oppression, was annexed to the kingdom of Aragon and included in the autonomous region of Spain. Until the 50s of the last century, it was considered a Spanish province, until the invasion of tourists began. The tourist boom has transformed this paradise into a popular Mediterranean resort offering both democratic and luxurious vacations. Spain (Mallorca) is considered a true paradise.

An interesting resort with a rich architectural heritage and a large number of fashionable shops. You can visit the church, built at the end of the 12th century, take a walk along the many narrow streets leading from the square on which it is located. Not far from this church you can admire the Cathedral, in the construction and design of which Antonio Gaudi participated. The castle known on the island is the Castell de Belver, built in the Gothic style. It has an observation deck from which it is good to enjoy wonderful views of Palma. And the most memorable trip will be a visit to the picturesque village of Valldemossa in the mountains, where you can see stone buildings, winding streets, paved and decorated with flower pots. It will be useful to visit in those parts of the beautifully preserved majestic monastery. Adventure lovers will love visiting the five caves, which are carefully guarded by Spain (Mallorca). A map for excursions on them is issued to tourists. Caves are popular and interesting for their underground lakes, spectacularly decorated with light lighting, thanks to which you can see the dawn underground. Tourists who prefer a club vacation should go to one of the many casinos or show in restaurants. It will also be interesting to relax in water parks, theme parks, go sailing, windsurfing, scuba diving and other activities presented in the fabulous and luxurious Mallorca.
