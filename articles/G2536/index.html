Grenada 4 Sunny Beach: reviews and photos of tourists

Grenada 4 Sunny Beach is a four-star hotel located in the south of the popular Bulgarian resort Sunny Beach. The hotel is directly created for a comfortable stay on the golden sands of the Black Sea coastal zone. After all, the hotel is located right on the first line of the beach, 100 meters from the water surface.

Hotel Description

Grenada 4 Sunny Beach was built relatively recently, in 2006. It is convenient to get to the hotel, it is only 35 km from the airport. Nearby are historical sights: the ancient city of Nessebar, protected by UNESCO, with a huge number of medieval buildings and ancient churches. In your free time you can plunge into the history of the country, learn a lot of informative about the life of the people of Bulgaria.





The Grenada 4 Sunny Beach Hotel itself is a modern building with an unusual design. The six-story building has interesting carved balconies. The hotel is clean and bright, has two spacious restaurants and two bars. During your stay in such a place you can not only relax, swim in the warm sea and sunbathe under the rays of the southern warm sun, but also take a course of wellness treatments in the hotel itself.

Throughout the hotel there is free Wi-Fi via wi-fi, so that settlers can contact relatives and friends at any time of the day and share their impressions of their vacation in Sunny Beach.

Staff

The hotel staff proved themselves cultural and well-mannered. One minus - they do not know Russian, but they speak English well. Most tourists come from Western and Eastern Europe. About 15% of Russians, many guests from Ukraine.





According to vacationers, the staff carefully clean the rooms every day, towels are changed twice a week. But there are reviews that say that maids clean even too cleanly, after which things disappear in the rooms. So you need to always be alert. In hotels, quite often, funny things happen, you need to carry your valuables and money with you or leave in a safe. Each room has its own, and the receptionist has a hotel safe.

And tourists are advised to give the maids a tip, at least 2 leva, and the room will shine, and the workers will smile with all their heart, trying to please the guests.

Room Categories

In total, the Grenada 4 hotel (Bulgaria, Sunny Beach) has 175 rooms of various categories of comfort. There are single standard rooms, if you wish, you can ask for an extra bed (1 + 1). Double rooms can also accommodate a maximum of three people (2 + 1). Another category is luxury one-bedroom apartments.





Each room has a refrigerator, a TV with cable channels, a telephone, a balcony, air conditioning, wireless Internet via Wi-Fi, and a minibar. Each living room has its own private bathroom with modern fixtures: toilet, beautiful stylish washbasin, shower. There is a hairdryer.

All hotel rooms, regardless of category, are furnished with modern comfortable furniture, beds have good orthopedic mattresses. Many tourists in their reviews have repeatedly noted that sleeps in the hotel perfectly. Even restless children get enough sleep.

Nutrition

The Bulgarian hotel Grenada 4 Sunny Beach (AI) recently has this food system. A - all (all), I - inclusive (included). But over the years, the hotel managed to establish itself as a hotel with very good food, including both Bulgarian and international cuisine. Breakfast is a buffet, between breakfast and lunch - a snack with pizza and sandwiches.

All vacationers note a large amount of fresh fruit. There are cherries, kiwi, apples, oranges. Ice cream is served for lunch, and you can also collect it by the pool with several horns. For dinner - meat in all kinds of variations, portioned fish.





Our tourists like the food of Bulgarian cuisine more, as it is somewhat similar to ours.

In addition to the main restaurants, the hotel also has a pizzeria, a cafe and a bar where you can drink soft drinks, a snack between the main meals. And you can drink coffee or tea both in the fresh air and in any cafe inside the hotel. On the territory near the pool there is also a bar with soft drinks and snacks for every taste.

All unanimously in their comments confirm that no one has ever left the table hungry. Everyone really liked the food of the hotel, so the hotel lives up to its name: Grenada 4 Sunny Beach - all inclusive.

Additional services

The hotel has its own parking lot. It is possible to order a transfer from the airport: there and back.

For outdoor enthusiasts, the hotel has a large gym with a sufficient number of shells and cardio machines. After classes, you can take a steam bath in the Finnish sauna and get a massage, of course, at an additional cost.





Well, visitors write about the disco bar available at the Grenada 4 Sunny Beach Hotel, where you can spend an evening with a glass of delicious cocktail. Music is only the latest hits, modern and beautiful, light music is also impressive.

For lovers of a beautiful uniform tan there is a solarium at the hotel. By the way, it is recommended to sunbathe a bit in the solarium before the first visit to the beach, so as not to burn out in the open sun.

Every day animators work for children, various events, competitions, contests are held.

You can play billiards at a surcharge. Water pool tournaments are organized in the pool. Animators from Poland work, so their speech is quite understandable.

After the procedures, sunbathe in a solarium and dance at a disco in a nightclub. After all, a tourist has been preparing for such a vacation for a whole year, so why not break away in full, having received all the pleasures and impressions?
