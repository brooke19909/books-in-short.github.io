Hotel Golden Beach 4 *, Turkey - photos, prices and reviews of tourists from Russia

Winter and summer are the seasons when residents of megalopolises dream of a break from the bustle of the city on the sea coast. Why do Russians prefer to endure a many-hour flight and spend time on a visa when the same benefits can be obtained without leaving the territory of their native country? Often, relaxing in a four-star hotel in Egypt or Turkey, such as Hotel Golden Beach 4 *, is much cheaper than a ticket to a small cottage on the coast of Sochi or Anapa. The Russians prefer to go to countries with excellent, but inexpensive service - these are Thailand, Turkey, Greece and Crete, Egypt, the UAE.

Top 5 countries to relax

Southeast Asia is explored by tourists who are not afraid of long flights and exploring a new culture. Thailand is an exotic country where residents of Russia, the USA and China fly in during the winter holidays, so you will have to take care of booking a room in advance. The capital, Bangkok, is usually less interested in travelers than resorts located on sandy beaches washed by the warm waters of the Indian Ocean. In Thailand, every tourist will feel like a welcome guest: smiling Thais will gladly share the history of their country and offer national dishes and exotic fruits at the lowest price.





Families with children or youth go to Turkey to rest with all the noisy company. A huge number of nightclubs and other entertainment venues await visitors in Bodrum, while Antalya with open arms awaits lovers of the urban type of relaxation. Tourists come to Turkey not only to get a beautiful tan, but also in order to buy inexpensive gold jewelry. Shells and corals, unfortunately, are not allowed to be transported to the airport, since seafood is considered the property of the republic.

Mediterranean countries attract tourists with their unique culture and warm climate. In Greece, ancient art was born over several hundred years, and travelers will be able to see firsthand the ancient temples and other colossal buildings. Service in Crete will certainly please lovers of relaxation with comfort - Golden Beach Hotel Hersonissos 4 *, which is located in the north of the island, will be the best choice for single travelers or families with children.

To fly to the United Arab Emirates from Moscow is a little longer than to Turkey or Egypt, but for patience this country will reward tourists with a varied vacation. In luxury hotels, visitors can instantly feel like shahs and princes. A fascinating desert safari and helicopter flying over the capital are just a small fraction of the adventures that Russians are waiting for upon arrival in the UAE.

Egypt attracts tourists from all over the world with its beauties. The passion for studying the ancient artifacts and tombs of the pharaohs began more than a hundred years ago and continues to this day, and travelers stay in the best hotels such as Calimera Golden Beach Hotel 4 * not only for beach activities, but also to devote themselves to studying the history of this extraordinary country .

Egypt or Tunisia?

Russians rarely prefer African countries to relax - tropical heat, insects and fever attract archaeologists rather than prosperous Europeans. There are two states in the north of the continent, in which thousands of tourists with different levels of income each year rest - Egypt and Tunisia. Russians, who have never had to visit African resorts, often can not decide where to go on vacation again. In Egypt, you can have a great time at Crystal Sharm Hotel 4 * (Sharm El Sheikh), and in Tunisia at Hotel Golden Beach 4 * (Skanes).





A small North African state is famous for its magnificent sandy beaches, which is why the prices for tours to this country are slightly more expensive. The lack of Russian animation in Tunisia sometimes scares tourists who come with the goal of not only sunbathing on the beach, but also culturally relaxing. Considering that the second official language in the country is French, in many hotels the staff does not speak English. Tunisian resorts are focused on spa vacations, while in Egypt diving can become the main hobby of vacationers. The bottom of the Red Sea literally beckons with its amazing corals, shells, jellyfish and fish. In Egypt, even in the most expensive hotels, tourists are treated as a source of profit, so they can feel false hospitality, which can not be said about the Republic of Tunisia. Hotel Golden Beach 4 * is cordially waiting for its visitors and is ready to provide everything that is necessary for a quality and interesting vacation: archery, tennis, minigolf and other entertainment that guests can do in their free time from the beach. Going on vacation with your family, you will not lose by choosing Tunisia, while active youth will be more interesting to spend time in the bustling resorts of Egypt.

A package of documents required for a flight to Turkey, the UAE, Egypt and Greece

Turkey attracts Russians by the fact that they will not need to apply for a visa to relax in this country. At the border, it is enough to present a passport and a return ticket or the necessary amount for its purchase. Given the annual multimillion-dollar flow of Russians to this country, there are rarely cases when border guards send a vacationer back home - most often tourists successfully register and have no problems with leaving. The government strictly ensures that guests of the country do not violate the terms of their stay at the resort. If in 2012, Russian citizens could stay in Turkey for no more than two months, today the permissible period is halved.
