Professional photographer Elena Korneeva

Photography has become a familiar part of our lives. Some perceive the process of taking pictures as entertainment, others try to reflect something individual, while others regard photos as art. But for some, photography is the subject of a hobby, and work, and creative activity.

Professional photographer Elena Korneeva

In the modern world, everything is changing very quickly, in this life turmoil I want to keep the best memories. Photography is the best way to capture the best that usually goes unnoticed or is forgotten after a year.

"Using photographs, you can reflect and permanently preserve the individuality of a person," - says the eminent photographer Elena Korneeva, who is engaged in professional photography of children. For her, in the first place is the creation of a beautiful, memorable image - an eternal gift that can be considered again and again for many years.

The inspiration that the photographer experiences during work and which is designed to make his work unique is another feature of Elena's photographs. All of them are created under the influence of a beautiful muse. Moreover, Elena believes that she should share her inspiration with everyone on the set, the reciprocity of the author and the character helps the photographer to be insightful and, as a result, successful.

Elena Korneeva is a photographer who prefers aesthetics in everything: on the set, in life, in food, in leisure. She is engaged in charity work, arranges creative meetings at which she shares the secrets of her craft, and helps to reveal wonderful qualities in the soul of a person.





Master classes

Photography is akin to playing the piano, but instead of the keys, the photographer catches the only moment for which his work is conceived. You need diligence and training, this is the guarantee of professionalism that Elena Korneeva possesses. Reviews from an audience enthusiastic about her publications prove this, and those who were lucky enough to get to her for a photo shoot remain satisfied for a long time.

Elena will gladly share her skills to enable beginners to find themselves in the art of photography. To do this, she organizes master classes in which she talks about the intricacies of the photographer, shares her experience and gives advice, helps to analyze the work of other authors.

One of these master classes was held on November 14, 2015, places were taken a week before the event. Participants speak warmly and enthusiastically about a photographer like Elena Korneeva.





Children's and family photos

The main type of photographs that Elena deals with are family photographs and portraits of children. She believes that every child is unique, and her originality is emphasized in her photographs.

A wonderful example of photo art - photographs of expectant mothers, in them the master reveals perhaps the most beautiful phenomenon on Earth - the love of mother and baby, their infinitely strong bonds.





Especially vivid images of children and mothers look against the backdrop of nature changing with the seasons. Elena Korneeva offers a series of projects dedicated to the seasons and children. On them, she expresses the uniqueness of not only the child himself, but also his mood at one time or another.

There is no place for sadness and boredom in Elena's photographs, all the pictures are endowed with meaning, permeated with children's simplicity, sincerity and playfulness. In addition, the author creates and uses costumes that emphasize the images of children.
