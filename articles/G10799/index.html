Hotel location





One of the main advantages of the modest and cozy three-star hotel Simplitel Phuket 3 * (Phuket, Karon) is the convenience of its location relative to the main objects of the island. Firstly, it is located on the first coastline, just 300 meters from the sandy beach, where you can enjoy the beauty and serenity of the Land of Smiles.

Secondly, the quiet daytime streets at night turn into a carnival of colors, sounds and smells. Street food, souvenirs and restaurants, bars and clubs are all nearby. From the international airport can be reached in 40-50 minutes (42 km).

Thirdly, you can quickly get by taxi or rented transport to two neighboring famous beaches - the noisy and explosive Patong or the calm and peaceful Kat. Relatively close to the island's zoo (21 km), Central Festival (19 km), golf park (3.3 km).

Terms and Conditions

Prices for accommodation at the Simplitel Phuket 3 * hotel depend mainly on the season. In total, there are three of them. The high season proceeds in 3 stages. The first begins in November and ends on the eve of Catholic Christmas - December 20. The second stage is from January 21 to February 5 (again on the eve of the holidays). Well, the final stage is from February 15 to March 31. During this period, cancellation is possible only 14 days in advance, otherwise a 50% commission will be charged. An extra bed will cost tourists 1000 baht.

The peak of the season falls on the periods from December 21 to January 20, from February 6 to 14. At this time, free cancellation is allowed only for 21 days, or the cost is not refundable. At the same time, the length of stay must be at least 5 nights, and the price of an extra bed is 1200 baht.

From April 1 to the end of October, the whole of Phuket plunges into the low season. Simplitel Phuket 3 * at this time allows cancellation of the reservation seven days without penalty and with 30% sanction at a later date. An extra bed in the room will cost guests 700 baht.

Registration of arriving tourists is carried out after two in the afternoon, and departure before noon. Smoking is prohibited throughout the hotel.

About Karon Beach





If you are a big fan of relaxed beach holidays and water activities, then the Simplitel Phuket 3 * hotel will suit you fully. Karon Beach is one of the best in Phuket and has a developed infrastructure. Like mushrooms after the rain, luxurious luxury hotels have grown along it, and this, of course, requires cleanliness, safety and comfort.

The six-kilometer beach is completely open, so the waves in the rainy season are very impressive in size. Be careful and carefully monitor the signal flag, without neglecting caution. Sand on the beach is crystalline, quartz. One of its characteristic features is a creak, as if walking in the snow on a frosty day. But here everything is exactly the opposite - the heat, the scorching sun and high humidity. The water temperature does not fall below +27 degrees during the year.

Everyone will find entertainment on Karon Beach to their own taste. Water species: bananas, motorbikes, skiing, diving, boat trips, etc. Gourmets will surely like numerous restaurants specializing in the preparation of freshly caught seafood and fish.

And of course, where without Thai massage and SPA procedures. They have long been the hallmark of the country. Numerous salons offer services at the most varied prices, depending on the type of establishment.
