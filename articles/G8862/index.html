Quadcopter Syma X8W: reviews and specifications

Power and load capacity

The drone is equipped with collector engines. However, these are unusual motors. The increased size, increased power and efficiency allow them to accelerate the quadrocopter to a speed of 8 m / s for horizontal movement and 3 m / s when climbing. The device literally soars up and quickly overcomes tens of kilometers, you just have to press on the direction stick. At the same time, powerful engines provide not only high speed, but also load capacity. So, the quadrocopter can be used with attachments - for example, action cameras in aquaboxes.

First-Person View (FPV)

Another advantage that users often mention in their reviews of the Syma X8W quadrocopter is its real-time broadcast support. The device is equipped with a Wi-Fi module and transfers the image in HD resolution to the smartphone screen. By controlling the drone, you can observe everything that falls into the camera lens in real time. With an average price of the device up to $ 100, the presence of such a function is not typical.

Huge selection of spare parts

The manufacturer offers a large number of accessories and spare parts. All elements of the case and the electronic system are in free sale: replaceable batteries and covers, propeller protection and the screws themselves, the chassis and parts of the case, cameras and receivers. Any part in the event of a breakdown can be replaced, and this is another advantage that users note in their reviews of the Syma X8W quadrocopter with a camera.

Additional functions

The presence of Headless mode and a 6-axis gyroscope greatly simplifies interaction with the quadrocopter. By the way, directly on the remote control there is a key that is responsible for automatic flips.

Complete equipment and more

In positive reviews of the quadcopter Syma X8W, users often note the rich content of the set. Unlike most manufacturers, Sima provides not only protection for propellers, but also a phone holder, spare screws and even a screwdriver. Also in the package there is a battery, charging, control panel, USB cable for connecting a third-party camera, instructions and a card reader for connecting MicroSD to a computer or laptop. The only thing you need to buy is a set of four AA batteries.

Lack of GPS

In reviews of the quadcopter Syma X8W, owners note dissatisfaction with the lack of geolocation. Of course, this is a necessary thing, which allows you not to lose a drone. However, the range of the control panel specifically in this model is small - up to 150 meters, so the quadrocopter will always remain within sight. However, GPS is not limited to finding a lost device. Navigation allows the drone to return to the user when the battery is low or the signal from the remote control is lost, as well as fly along a predetermined path.

Poor shooting quality

Quadcopter Syma X8W with FPV-camera, reviews of which are mostly positive, still scolded for unsatisfactory recording. A matrix with a resolution of 0.3 megapixels formally shoots HD-video, but the color rendition and detail of distant objects really leave much to be desired. As noted above, the quadrocopter has enough power to lift almost any action camera in the aquabox, so the problem of poor quality shooting is solved. It is important to consider that with extra weight the flight time is reduced: up to 12 minutes without load and up to 8 with equipment.
