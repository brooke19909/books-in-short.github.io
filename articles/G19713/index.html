Irreplaceable circuit breaker



Let's see why it is so called. The word "switch" is known to everyone - it is a device that provides the ability to switch an electrical circuit. Clicking the toggle switch - and the light in the room lights up, a second click - and everything goes out. When the light is on, the switch passes current through itself. In part, it can be compared to a ball valve in a water supply system.

But the word ‚Äúautomatic‚Äù means that in some cases the device can turn itself on or off. Normally, the circuit opens. Inside the case there are two mechanisms that constantly monitor the magnitude of the current and, when it is exaggerated, turn off the circuit breaker. The first is a thermal release. It is represented by a bimetallic plate, which, when heated, bends and discards the return mechanism from the latch. The second is magnetic. It is made in the form of an inductor, which breaks the circuit in the device with its magnetic field (if the current is too high).

Types of "machines"

Depending on the number of switched lines, single and multipolar devices are distinguished. That is, with the movement of one toggle switch-tongue, you can use several chains at once.

For example, a two-pole circuit breaker installed in a residential electrical panel protects the connected branches from overload and heating (thermal release), as well as from a short circuit (magnetic setting).

The choice

Thus, the selection of the "machine" is performed at the rated current. This value is always indicated on the housing. It should not be less than in the line that this device will switch and protect. By the way, this is why it is impossible to install simple toggle switches on powerful equipment, since the magnitude of the flowing current is too high.

The number of poles is selected individually. Here you need to take as much as necessary. Some models allow you to combine cases into a single unit, increasing the number of switched lines.
