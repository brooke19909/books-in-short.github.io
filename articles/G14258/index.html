Mineral Caucasian Waters: photos and reviews of tourists. Sights and sanatoriums of the Caucasian Mineral Waters

Mineral Caucasian Waters are one of the most visited places in Russia. They include several cities of the North Caucasus. The unusual nature, clean air allows each guest to plunge into the unusual world of this region. Many come here for recreation and just have a good time on their weekend or vacation. Here is the Tambukan lake, which is famous for its reserves of silt mud. In 1992, it was decided to give this resort a special status - a protected ecological and resort region of Russia. Mineral Caucasian Waters first appeared in Schober's notes. It happened back in the XVII century. Schober himself was the doctor of Peter the Great. It is he who is considered the discoverer of this medical field. Caucasian Mineral Waters consist of the cities: Essentuki, Kislovodsk, Zheleznovodsk and Pyatigorsk. The relief here is predominantly mountainous, which is why many extreme people, not only from Russia but also from all over the world, seek this resort. This place has more than 90 mineral springs, about 120 sanatoriums and pensions, which work not only in summer but also in winter. Sights of the Caucasian Mineral Waters





Everyone who came to these places should enjoy such a chance, because there are many unique places to visit. The sights of the Caucasian Mineral Waters are distinguished by their individual history. Each place has its own legend and history. One of them is the Cathedral of the Protection of the Holy Virgin. This building was built in the 90s of the outgoing century. It should be noted that the construction process was carried out in a very short time. An equal cross was chosen as the basis of the temple. It serves as a symbol of salvation. As for the interior decoration, it was carried out by the best masters who painted the Moscow Cathedral of Christ the Savior. There is another church, the older one is St. Nicholas. It was erected after the end of World War II. Inside the church you can see the most ancient icon “The Naughty One”. It was written by monks who lived on Mount Athos. You also need to pay attention to the Mineralovodsk Museum of Local Lore. There are exhibits that cover a large time period. In addition, there are the Museum of Aviation Engineering and the Museum of the writer A.P. Bibikova. Many tourists visit the magmatic mountain Snake. The people call it Zhlan-tau (translation into Turkic). Well, it’s worth a visit to the mud bath building. It was built at the end of the 19th century at the railway station. The interior of the room resembles the ancient Roman baths. Nearby is a real engine, which has been standing since 1985. Capital Pyatigorsk





The cities of the Caucasian Mineral Waters are similar to each other. The main settlements are Pyatigorsk, Zheleznovodsk, Essentuki and Kislovodsk. Pyatigorsk is the capital of this region. It is located at the foot of Mount Mashuk. Its height above sea level is 680 m. It is unique in that it is the only place in the CIS countries where there is natural radon and mud from Lake Tambukan. In some properties, they surpass even the mud of the Dead Sea. On the slopes of Mount Mashuk lies an old forest, which includes mainly broadleaf trees. If you walk through the forest, you can see paths, arbors, small fountains that delight with fresh water. Pyatigorsk has a good medical base, composed of favorable factors of geographical location. Take, for example, a radon hospital. It is the largest in Europe. Here it is possible to accept 15 types of procedures that are aimed at treating various diseases, including spinal diseases. In Pyatigorsk they will be able to cure diseases of the digestive system, nervous system, carry out the treatment of infertility, gynecological diseases, etc.
