Car receiver PIONEER AVH-170: owner reviews

In this article we will talk about the radio from "Pioneer". The Pioneer AVH-170 G is powered by a Bluetooth wireless module. With their sister model AVH-170 they are completely similar. The release took place in 2015.



Car Features

The power amplifier of the device is 50 W * 4. There is an audio and video input (output), so it is possible to work with DVDs and CDs. Built-in display. Its size is 6.2 inches. USB connector available. Screen resolution is 320 * 240 pixels. In addition, you can pay attention to the fact that the radio has a graphic equalizer for 5 bands. The main color of the backlight is green. The body of the device is black. The warranty is valid for up to 12 months. A more detailed review of the Pioneer AVH-170 is later in the article.



Additional device parameters

The package includes two speakers, a frame, an ISO connector, a USB extension cable. You will not find anything superfluous. The new model received a CD-DVD drive, on the back there is a USB connector. The screen is created using WVGA video technology. Next to the screen on the left side there is a panel where the control buttons are located. At first glance it may seem that they are sensory, but this is not so. They are mechanical. In general, the management is convenient. Instructions for the Pioneer AVH-170 are included.



Rear panel device

In order to understand how good the Pioneer AVH-170 radio tape recorder (reviews on this issue are controversial), you need to look at the rear panel. Here on the left you can see: USB, then the video output, under it is the input for the camera. As a rule, rear-view matrixes are used, there is an audio input, it is a standard mini-jack. Next to it is an output for the rear subwoofer, a linear output is slightly to the right. Next, you can notice the main connector for connecting the radio, the input for the adapter to control the buttons on the steering wheel, as well as a special port for the antenna.



Menu

It is necessary to say a few words about the menu. According to reviews, the Pioneer AVH-170 does not change the color of the backlight (if we talk about the buttons), but in the menu you can independently change the image theme. Three design options are available: green, red and blue.

In the Russian translation of the software, you can notice some inaccuracies, which is completely uncharacteristic for the equipment from the Pioneer company. When choosing loudness, a choice is made between low, medium and high frequencies - LF, MF, HF. Many may not understand what the section is responsible for. There is a problem with the translation. This is not a frequency, but a level - LOW, MID, HIGH. This is a trifle, but people who do not understand and have not previously worked with such devices can be confused. In general, the menu is clear and intuitive. You can add individual items to your Favorites.

Sound characteristic

According to reviews, the Pioneer AVH-170 has a very good sound, like other models from the manufacturer "Pioneer". Equalizer Powerfull ("Powerful") is included by default. This gives the unit brightness of sound, which contributes to better sales when demonstrating its capabilities by managers. When using the radio in a car, it is recommended to change the equalizer settings if the car also has normal acoustics and a subwoofer. Therefore, it is recommended to use the equalizer "gently" to avoid excessive sharpness of sound.
