The second generation of Air, as mentioned above, looks almost the same as the first. The company very often uses successful developments in terms of design, as they say, to the last - but why give up what still works great?

Performance

Performance characteristics were tested on the Apple iPad Air 2 64Gb, and, according to AnTuTu, the increase in capacity compared to the previous generation Air is quite significant, about one and a half times.
