Unforgettable vacation in Tuapse: reviews, attractions, climate

Tuapse is one of the most popular resorts in the Krasnodar Territory. Warm velvet sea, sunny beaches, good cafes and restaurants - there is everything you need for a good rest.

If you have already sunbathed in plenty, soaked in the warm sand, then you can safely proceed to sightseeing in Tuapse. Reviews say that local monuments of nature and culture are of great interest. What is popular in the city?
1. Dolmens. These mysterious formations cause active debate among scientists. Their origin is still not clear. Locals call them the houses of heroes, but from a scientific point of view, these are either tribal sanctuaries or ancient tombs. Scientists agree on one thing - dolmens are at least 4 thousand years old. To see these amazing structures, you do not have to travel outside of Tuapse. Reviews of tourists confirm the extraordinary energy emanating from dolmens.
2. Tulip tree - this is perhaps the most popular natural monument. According to legend, the age of this tree will soon reach 200 years. This is the largest representative of the species in Russia. The diameter of the trunk reaches 2 meters and a height of 35 m. The leaves of the tree are lyre-shaped, and the flowers look like tulips. Adyghs consider this tree sacred, and among tourists there is a belief. You need to make a wish and go around the tree, and then tie a piece of fabric on the fence, and the desire will certainly come true.


3. The Lagonaki Highlands. This excursion will take two days, but it's worth it. Getting to the highlands, you can take many beautiful photos against the backdrop of the majestic Caucasus Mountains. On the first day, tourists can enjoy views of the Hadjok gorge. This natural landmark of Tuapse, reviews of which you often hear from tourists, clearly proves that a drop is sharpening a stone. For centuries, the river made its way through the rocks, forming a gutter. Now tourists can enjoy this miracle of nature. At night you can stay at the camp site. There are special viewing platforms from which stunning views of the gorge illuminated by floodlights open up. On the second day, tourists leave for the Lagonaki plateau. The long winding road that goes along the mountain slopes at an altitude of 2000 meters above sea level will be remembered for its bright colors, picturesque cliffs, various plants.

The culmination of the tour - Azizh cave, the pearl of Tuapse. Reviews of tourists talk about unusual sensations that arise when immersed in a real underground kingdom. Here you can drink crystal clear water from a spring, inhale fresh air. Not only the sights will be remembered for a vacation in Tuapse. Reviews of tourists confirm that the infrastructure is well developed here, there is where to relax and have fun.


The climate in Tuapse is temperate, strong, sweltering heat is almost never, the sea temperature reaches 25 degrees. There is also an activity for thrill-seekers. Rafting is carried out on a mountain river with rapids. Those who have already spent their holidays in Tuapse in 2013 leave reviews exclusively positive. The city has its own water park, bowling centers, casinos and clubs.
