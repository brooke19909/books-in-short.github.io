Where is Holland located on the world map and what should be paid attention to

Where Holland is located on the world map, far from everything is clearly imagined. Moreover, most people, even those who have never been there, have in their minds the image of this territorially small country that has already formed under the influence of the media. The most amazing thing is that it is not so much at odds with reality.



Holland on a map of Europe

The official name of this country is the Netherlands. And for many, the correct answer to the question of where the Netherlands is can be quite unexpected. The fact is that only two provinces of the Netherlands officially bear this name. They have historically been the most developed and famous in Europe and far beyond its borders. And there is nothing surprising in the fact that people are used to referring their name to the whole country as a whole. But whatever the name of this country, you should look for it on the map of Europe on the coast of the North Sea. Its inhabitants have been known for centuries both on the continent and far beyond its borders. They have traditionally been renowned as magnificent mariners, prudent merchants, and skilled craftsmen. This territorially small country played a more than appreciable role in the culture of Europe. Art lovers do not need to explain where Holland is located on the world map. The canvases of the Dutch masters are decorations of the collections of all significant museums on both sides of the ocean.



On a tourist map of the world

Due to its geographical location, this country has historically been at the crossroads of trade routes. From this situation, the country benefited greatly. Holland welcomed people from all over the world and was open to any influences. Today it is a free country with a fairly high standard of living. People on many continents do not need to explain where Holland is located on the world map. They know this well, and they come to it with pleasure. Tourism is one of the most significant sectors of the Dutch economy. And the country has created excellent infrastructure for receiving guests. A lot is being done so that tourists from all over the world can see Holland exactly the way they used to imagine it - with endless fields of colorful tulips, with rotating wings of windmills, with numerous canals and drawbridges. In the cities and towns of Holland, they are accustomed to respect the rich architectural and cultural heritage. All historical monuments are carefully restored and presented to the attention of tourists.
