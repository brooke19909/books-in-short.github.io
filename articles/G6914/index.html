Invites the shopping center "Suvorovsky" in Penza

Penza is a regional center located in the Volga region on the Sura River. According to the latest census, nearly five hundred twenty-four thousand people live in Penza. The city was founded in 1663. At this time, a fortress was laid on the banks of the Penza River, from where the name of the settlement went. Currently, Penza is the industrial and cultural center of the region. The city has registered more than fifteen thousand business entities of different directions and forms of ownership. Five theaters, more than ten museums, various cultural centers of various directions, a network of children's music and art schools provide cultural life. Trade in the city is carried out by five thousand objects of wholesale and retail trade, public catering enterprises, markets, chain stores and shopping centers.

Shopping center "Suvorovsky" in Penza

The shopping complex is located in the city center at the following address: 144 Suvorova Street (working hours from 10.00 to 20.00). The new ultramodern building has five floors, 25,000 m 2 of space is provided for business, leisure and entertainment. The shopping center can be reached by all means of public transport. And also there is a private parking for five hundred places for private cars, which leads through the entrance to the premises. The management of the complex is constantly changing the format of promotions and discounts to attract more visitors.

Layout of the complex

In the shopping center "Suvorovsky" in Penza on the ground floor there are: a food supermarket, several cafes, a restaurant, communication salons, leather goods stores, jewelry, cosmetics and perfumes. Here are: a jewelry salon and a tobacco boutique, a pharmacy, optics, sports nutrition stores, for fishers and hunters, a pet store. The second floor is occupied by a travel agency, a real estate agency, a beauty salon, clothing stores, including sports shops, a souvenir and gift shop, vending machines with coffee and ATMs. The third floor is fully occupied by clothes and shoes, including for children and pregnant women. There is also a fur salon and a clothing repair studio where you can customize your favorite item. The fourth floor is a zone of comfort and entertainment. A modern cinema with comfortable seats and a peculiar design allows you to get the effect of maximum presence during a movie show.

Ground floor

Those who do not like shopping may not even go up to the trading floors. In Penza, the Suvorovsky shopping center offers a billiard room with fourteen tables, a nine-lane bowling alley and an arcade. For lovers of relaxation, there is a sauna and a tanning studio. Tired, you can relax in the cafe for one hundred visitors, sushi bar or in a karaoke restaurant.
