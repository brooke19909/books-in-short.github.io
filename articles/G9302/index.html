Hotel Santa Marina Holiday Village 4 * (Sozopol, Bulgaria): description, services, reviews

In our article, we want to talk about the Santa Marina Holiday Village hotel, which is located in the resort town of Santa Marina near Sozoplya.

Resort Location

Before proceeding to the description of the rooms, I want to talk a little about the town itself. The resort complex of Santa Marina with an amphitheater is located on the first line near the sea. Very pleased is the famous royal beach, known for its white sand. The resort town is surrounded by mixed forests, turning this place into a cozy oasis, protected from the whole world with its problems.



The territory of the complex has a well-developed infrastructure. There are children's entertainment centers, bars, restaurants, swimming pools and many other amenities that make tourists a real holiday.

Complex structure

The whole village consists of four-story cottages with different apartments, many of which have wonderful sea views. The resort is surrounded by greenery. The architecture of the buildings is made in the style of the Bulgarian Renaissance of the nineteenth century. The town was built in such a way that seventy percent of its territory remained under green spaces and park alleys. The best place for a relaxing holiday is simply not to be found.

A bit about the hotel

Santa Marina Holiday Village is located two kilometers from Sozoplya. It has direct access to motorways. The complex was founded in 2006, and since then it has hosted lovers of sea resorts. In 2013, the hotel was completely renovated. Currently, it is a completely modern complex with all the necessary infrastructure.

Hotel Santa Marina Holiday Village is located at the following address: Bulgaria, Sozopol, 8130, HV Santa Marina, ap 13, 407. In fact, it is a closed type hotel complex. It consists of eighty villas and cottages (two- and three-story), which are built along the Black Sea coast. Externally, the resort resembles an ordinary Bulgarian village on the shores of Sozopol Bay.

Hotel Features

Santa Marina Holiday Village is a town living its own resort life. On its territory, you can rent not only apartments with several rooms, but also entire attics. The interiors of all apartments fit perfectly into the general atmosphere of this resort: the walls are painted in sand tones, natural wooden furniture in the inimitable country style, highlighted by unobtrusive, but original accessories.





The layout of the apartments makes them very convenient for everyday relaxation. They have kitchen units, washing machines, ironing facilities, and, of course, the Internet.

Local Attractions

Spending a vacation at the hotel, it is impossible not to visit Sozopol. This wonderful old town is filled with beautiful places in which you can take unique pictures for memory. It was founded in the sixth century BC by the brave Greek colonists on the rocky coast of the peninsula. In ancient times, the city was called Apollonia.

Currently, this place has become a favorite among Bulgarian bohemians. Numerous musicians, artists, artists are attracted here by the very special atmosphere of the town. There really is something to see: beautiful old churches, white houses under red tiles, narrow cobblestone streets, stone alleys ... All this is buried in the greenery of figs and vineyards. And the unique sandy beaches and the beautiful harbor are the pride of Sozopol.

Once a year, the city hosts an art festival dedicated to the god Apollo. At this time, Sozopol is filled with an artistic atmosphere. The festival lasts ten days. If you happen to be resting here at this time, then you should definitely wander around the city. Here you can see theatrical performances, photo exhibitions, artwork. The festival attracts foreign and Bulgarian artists, singers, actors, dancers and ordinary spectators.





Of greatest interest to tourists is the old part of the city, which is an architectural reserve and is protected by UNESCO. The church of the Bulgarian Renaissance is especially beautiful in it, old houses, the remains of the fortress walls, restaurants serving national cuisine with panoramic views of the sea coast.
