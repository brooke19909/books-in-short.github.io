general information

The hotel is located on the island of Crete, Greece. Its location offers a quiet and relaxing holiday, as the Minos Mare Hotel is located 75 km from the airport of Heraklion, away from the main roads of the island. Within walking distance from the hotel is the Aegean Sea, the beauty of which is difficult to describe in words.

Minos Mare Hotel 4 * (Crete) - n a small hotel, which is located on the 1st coastline, a few steps from the Aegean Sea. The main guests of the hotel are the British, Germans and Greeks. Tourists of our country are also not uncommon in a hotel; for convenience, a Russian-speaking guide is always waiting for them at the reception. Service

One of the advantages of this hotel, vacationers consider the courtesy and hospitality of the staff. Minos Mare Hotel employees are always ready to help in difficult situations.

The staff at the reception at any time is ready to answer all questions. There are usually no problems with settlement. Guests are invited to the room as soon as it is ready.





Rooms

The hotel has more than 120 rooms. Some of them overlook the pool, others - on the green lawn with a wide variety of colors.

The room has everything you need: a small plasma TV, air conditioning, minibar, safe, shower and balcony. For guests at the tables in the room there is always a kettle, cups, as well as tea and coffee.

Bed linen and towels are changed every day, which is good news.

On the balcony there is a table and chairs: you can relax in the evening, admiring the sunset. The presence of clothes dryers on the balcony is, of course, a great advantage, because in the hotel, which is located by the sea, this part of the interior is very necessary.

The minuses of the hotel visitors include the operation of air conditioning. It is designed in such a way that it can be turned on if there is a key card in a special unit, that is, it is impossible to cool a room without being present in it. The air conditioner works only for a strictly defined time, mainly in the morning and afternoon hours.





Nutrition

The hotel has two food systems (breakfast and breakfast-dinner). Breakfast includes a variety of cereals and fruits. Snacks and snacks are always available.

For dinner, the option of dishes is extensive, here anyone can find food to their taste. The hotel has such entertainment as a master class from the chef. The chief chef of the hotel invites everyone to cook Greek cuisine with him. There are several advantages for tourists in this action: entertainment and the opportunity to get to know the cuisine of another country.

At Minos Mare Hotel, all vacationers are offered to try real Greek wines. The tasting takes place every day, everyone can get on it.

  • For lovers of evening gatherings, the hotel provides themed evenings, mainly a Greek dinner.
  • At the Minos Mare Hotel 4 * (Platanias, Rethymnon) animators work, their work is unobtrusive. If you want, you can participate in contests, if not, relax in peace.

Beach

Hotel Minos Mare Hotel 4 *, as mentioned above, is on the 1st line, so the sea is visible even from the hotel rooms. The Aegean Sea is rarely calm, high and fast waves can be found here much more often than calm. Wave lovers will love this place. The rest of the guests, in order to calmly swim in the sea, will have to wait for calm weather.

Many tourists in their reviews notice that the beach itself is sandy, but the entrance to the sea is rocky. This distance lasts about 3 meters, so it is better to stock up on special shoes for entering the sea.

On the beach there is a restaurant that is always glad to its customers. If you need the ‚Äúsimpler‚Äù option - within walking distance from the beach there are a couple of small mini-markets and local cafes. There are always free places on the beach. Sun loungers and umbrellas are paid. Beach towels are issued on deposit, they can be obtained on the ground floor of the hotel.





City of Rethymnon

Minos Mare Hotel is 5 km from Rethymnon. This is a small tourist town located on the west coast of the Aegean. Rethymnon is very popular among tourists because of its architecture, which mixed Muslim and Venetian traditions. Locals always welcome tourists, and it doesn‚Äôt matter at all whether you are staying in a luxury hotel or in a small guesthouse.

On the streets of the city, a large number of cafes and dining rooms are warmly welcomed by all passers-by. Each of them has its own style and a variety of menus. That is why they are not alike. Even a true foodie will appreciate the quality of the dishes served in these cafes. And the atmosphere and friendly service will certainly make a lasting impression on a long memory.

Where to go to Retmino? sights

Venetian fortress

This building was erected in the 16th century. They built a fortress with the whole city. Inside there are houses, hospitals, barracks and even a church. It was believed that if disaster struck, the inhabitants would be able to hide there. It is impossible to get lost when going to the fortress, because because of its height it can be seen from any part of the city.

Arkadi Monastery

The monastery is huge, its area is more than 5200 square meters. m. During the siege of the Ottoman Empire in the monastery was the headquarters, which developed a plan to save the island. Currently, the monastery contains exhibits that tell about the history of the island. The monastery garden became very popular among tourists. Luxurious flowers grow on its territory, in the company of which you can relax and enjoy nature.

Melidoni Cave

Charming and mysterious place. In past centuries, the cave was sacred. During the pressure of the Ottoman Empire, the inhabitants of Crete were hiding in a cave. The story ended tragically, all residents suffocated from smoke. In memory of this event, a sarcophagus with fragments of the bodies of the dead was installed in the cave.

Venetian embankment

This place seems to be specially created for romantic meetings. Lovers of the whole city date on it. Venetian-style buildings, the promenade and the atmosphere of relaxation attract all tourists here. On the Venetian Embankment, you can take a leisurely stroll and watch the sunset or sit comfortably at a table in one of the cafes.





Hotel Vacation Tips

  • It is better to choose the type of breakfast-dinner meal, because it is for dinner that delicious and varied dishes of Greek cuisine are served. In addition, thanks to the master class, which sets the hotel chef, it is possible to try to cook a new dish yourself.
  • If you like to sleep in the morning, it is better to choose a room away from a farm located nearby, as the morning singing of roosters will wake you at dawn.
  • If you are going to visit Minos Mare Hotel 4 * (Greece, Platanias, Rethymnon) with young children - be extremely careful when swimming in the sea, since it is mostly not calm on the coast.
  • Evening show programs on the territory of the hotel are not held every day, so if you want to have fun, you will have to travel outside the hotel.
  • Umbrellas and sun loungers on the beach are paid, but if you come to the sea in the afternoon you can avoid these fees.
