Summarizing

If you have several mobile gadgets at your disposal (tablet, smartphone, laptop, etc.), then it is better to dwell on external batteries of a universal type, which, by the way, are the vast majority on the market.

The principle of operation of such devices is quite simple - the greater the capacity of the device, the more gadgets it is able to charge. In general, if your mobile device works in comfortable conditions, somewhere in a cozy pocket or on a table in the room, and you do not plan to take it out to the wild, then taking external batteries with a capacity of more than 10,000 mAh simply does not make sense, because you yourself they will charge much longer than simpler models.
