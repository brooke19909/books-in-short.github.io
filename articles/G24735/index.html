Varvara Lopukhina: biography. Barbara Lopukhin in the life and work of Mikhail Lermontov

The deepest cordial affection of the great Russian poet Mikhail Yurievich Lermontov - Varvar Lopukhin, the younger sister of his friend Alexei. In the spring, before Easter of 1832, a company of secular young ladies and young people went to the Vigil to the Simon Monastery.





Love

Six horses slowly moved along Moscow streets - from Povarskaya to Molchanovka, then to another Molchanovka, and then to where Avtozavodskaya metro station is now located . Young people enjoyed the spring evening and a fun company, so they were in no hurry. Was it by chance that young Varvara Lopukhina ended up in the lineup next to an equally young poet who was in love with her peer? This question can hardly be answered reliably. But one thing is known for certain: Varvara Lopukhina remained in the role of a muse almost until the poet's death.

She only turned one winter in the light, taken out of the village to the "brides' fair", was innocent, natural, did not lose her rural blush and could not yet count every gesture, pose and word, like seasoned young ladies from Moscow.

Varvara Lopukhina had a passionate, enthusiastic, and poetic nature: far from the capitals, solitude and reading novels greatly affect the development of girlish daydreaming, without diminishing natural liveliness, cheerfulness, and sociability.





Through the eyes of contemporaries and the poet

Varvara Aleksandrovna Lopukhina was extraordinary in appearance: she was a blonde, which, of course, was not uncommon, but with moving and absolutely black eyes, eyebrows and eyelashes. This gave her a special charm - all mood changes were reflected on her face instantly and clearly. It was impossible to make a portrait of Varvara Lopukhina once and for all, so differently people saw her in changing circumstances.

Sometimes an uncontrolled facial expression made her almost a fool, and sometimes - almost a beauty. Love is defense

Both vanity and proud thoughts left the poet when this love appeared to his soul. Although from the very beginning it was clear that Varvara Lopukhina and Lermontov were not a couple, since they are of the same age. At her sixteen years, one could be a full member of society, even get married (it was for this purpose that she appeared in the capital), but the poet ...

In his very sixteen he was still a child in the eyes of all. Youthful maximalism forced him to exaggerate his physical disabilities: short stature, stoop, and ugliness. The youth story "Vadim" was never finished, but it was in Vadim that he saw himself, and in the beautiful Olga, her, Varvara.

The last time they saw each other in 1838 was fleeting when Varvara Lopukhin and Lermontov, it would seem, were supposed to completely forget about each other. Despite the rare and short meetings, everything managed to be in their relationship: friendships, crazy love, hot passions, and killing jealousy, even hostility. Life is short but roomy

Varvara Lopukhin was always present in Lermontov‚Äôs work, sometimes invisibly, as if dissolving in the motley of his life, but never leaving her. The only feeling that accompanied him all his life was his love for Varenka Lopukhina. But there was no understanding between them.
