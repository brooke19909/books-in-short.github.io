Discount for regular customers

The discount card "Red and White" will be offered to the visitor when buying in a network store. It is only necessary that the purchase amount exceeds five hundred rubles.

On the back of the card is a barcode. Use his number to activate the discount card.

Here's how the bonuses on the Red and White discount card are calculated:

  • purchase of more than three thousand rubles - 2% discount;
  • if the store spent eight thousand rubles - a 4% discount;

The discount in the stores "Red and White" is cumulative. The amount of discount is determined by the total amount of purchases in stores in the network.
