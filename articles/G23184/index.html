Sanatorium "Blue Lakes", Pskov region - tourist reviews and photos

Of course, every resident of our country knows perfectly well what a sanatorium is. In the USSR, it was quite a natural phenomenon when parents specifically took permits to relax with their children in the Caucasian Mineral Waters or the Crimea. Why was this kind of holiday popular? Firstly, here man, being in harmony with nature, restored his health. Secondly, a trip to a sanatorium is one of the opportunities to change the situation at least temporarily. Now many of these institutions are equipped with modern health centers where you can swim in the pool, work out in the gym, play tennis, soccer, volleyball, ride a horse and so on.

For these and other reasons, a huge number of Russians prefer to relax in the sanatorium "Blue Lakes" (Pskov region). What is remarkable for relaxing in this unique corner of nature? Let's consider this question in more detail.

Description

The sanatorium "Blue Lakes" (Pskov region) was built specifically for people to restore their health.





It is located in the southern part of the region, in an amazingly beautiful pine park, directly on the shores of Lake Small Ivan. The town of Velikiye Luki is 50 kilometers from the recreation complex, and the city of Nevel is 25 kilometers away.

There is clean air and the most favorable environment, since the territory is located away from industrial facilities.

Supply system

The administration of the health complex provided for its guests three meals a day, which is included in the price of the ticket. Diets prescribed to patients by physicians are also taken into account when drawing up the menu.

Number of rooms

The sanatorium "Blue Lakes" consists of three buildings, in which rest and treatment of people is carried out.

Buildings No. 1 and 1a are three-story, and in building No. 2 there are six floors. The dining room and the club are located in the same block with three-story buildings, but the diagnostic and treatment complex occupies a separate building. It offers guests - rooms of various categories of comfort.

You can rent a double room with a toilet, shower, washbasin and TV. Such apartments will cost 1600 rubles per day.





There is also a single room with a refrigerator, TV, toilet, shower and wash basin at a price of 1700 rubles per day.

It is also possible to rent a two-room double room, which has a bedroom, living room, toilet, shower, sink, refrigerator and TV. For such housing, vacationers will have to fork out for 2600 rubles per day.
