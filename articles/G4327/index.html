Hotel "Rhodes Palladium", Greece. Rodos Palladium Leisure & Wellness 5 *: photos and reviews of tourists

If we talk about which of the Greek hotel complexes is truly impressive, then, perhaps, many will mark the first thing the hotel "Rhodes Palladium". And he really can be considered as such. Why? Here you can tell a little more about this.





general information

Rhodes Palladium Hotel has been around for 18 years. But, despite his considerable "age", he looks very modern and beautiful. The snow-white six-story building, made in the traditional Greek style, rises in a green, well-groomed area with numerous pools and palm trees. Next to the main building there are five more buildings, with which the hotel forms a large and beautiful complex.

And there is a hotel on the island of Rhodes, which can be understood from its name. To be more precise - in the town of Faliraki. It is a very busy resort. He is very loved by youth. It takes 10 minutes to get to Rhodes from here, as well as to the airport. In the immediate vicinity there is a clean and spacious beach with sun loungers and umbrellas. In general, the location is good - everything a tourist needs is nearby.





What is available in the hotel?

Rhodes Palladium Hotel is not without reason declared as a five-star hotel. Here is absolutely everything that a newcomer may require. And even more. All basic services are here. These include free wireless Internet, parking, dry cleaning and laundry, 24-hour reception, etc. It is worth talking about other, more "elite" services.

There is a mini market, a souvenir shop, as well as several specialized boutiques. One sells jewelry, the second sells leather clothing and accessories. Even the Italian fashion boutique Benetton is. And our own car rental office is also available.

But the most important thing is that the business structure is well developed here. It is officially recognized that this hotel is the best place in Rhodes for conferences and other important events. After all, it is here that the round modern Academia Hall is equipped, in which the chandeliers are made of Venetian glass. There are also three conference rooms with modern technology, the total capacity of which is 750 people. By the way, weddings, anniversaries, banquets are often held here.

Naturally, such a luxurious hotel as Rodos Palladium Leisure & Wellness 5 * has facilities for people with disabilities, babysitting services, transfers, food delivery, newspapers and much more. Needless to say, here the staff speaks six languages. Among which, by the way, there is Russian.





For leisure

Rhodes Palladium Hotel is a resort complex. And this means that you can have a good time here. There is everything for this. Fitness center with trainers, sauna, massage rooms, indoor and outdoor pools (among which there is a separate one for children), two tennis courts with lighting and artificial grass, a basketball and volleyball court, mini-golf, as well as everything necessary for playing bocce , football, aerobics and water gymnastics.

Every day there are shows and various performances. Here live music of different genres plays, local groups perform, theme nights are held and traditional dances are held.

And, of course, Rodos Palladium Leisure & Wellness 5 * has its own professional SPA-center, which employs a group of highly qualified therapists. It offers globally recognized body and face care treatments, as well as the practice of using the French company Thalgo, which was the first in the world to use algae in cosmetics.





SPA salon services

The hotel has its own health and beauty center, so you can not say a few words about it separately.

This SPA center has several treatment rooms. Among them there are those in which couples are served. Many people like to attend procedures together, it is quite romantic.

Stone therapy, deep muscle, sports and Swedish massages are especially popular . Many also like hydro and aromatherapy and Ayurvedic treatments. SPA bathtubs are also very popular. In general, the list of services is very wide. Hair care, pedicure, manicure, stylish haircuts, soft hair coloring and styling, peeling and body scrubs, body wraps, relaxing baths, narrow-profile massage (arms, legs, neck, head, whole body) - here everyone will find that procedure, who wants to devote his time. And you should definitely do it. After all, these are unforgettable impressions.





Bars and Restaurants

Rodos Palladium has several presentable establishments where guests can enjoy the delights and skip a couple of glasses of quality alcohol. Symposium's main restaurant serves American breakfast and dinner with local and international dishes. They also often have theme nights here. Thalassa Restaurant serves surprisingly delicious seafood and original desserts. And you need to come to these institutions, observing the dress code. Restaurants correspond to a certain level, and guests should remember this.

There are also three bars on the territory of Rodos Palladium. One - on the beach - is called Poseidon. They serve great snacks, alcohol and refreshments. The second is by the pool. There is also the Mythos lobby, which always has live music. Such an atmosphere is the best for a pleasant conversation over a cup of coffee.

Guest Comments

Many tourists come to Rhodes. Hotel "Palladium" prices are quite low (as for a 5-star hotel), so a certain part decides to stay here. And there are simply no negative reviews about the hotel. Everyone praises her, which, in principle, is logical.

After all, this hotel has absolutely everything for everyone. Babysitting, a small pool and a mini-club - for children, special rooms - for people with disabilities, excellent noise isolation - for those who appreciate silence, great food - for gourmets. And the list goes on.

Many are happy to talk about staff. Very polite and smiling people work here. Administrators are warmly welcomed, even early check-in is possible if guests arrived in the morning. A welcome surprise awaits you in the room - a bottle of quality wine and juicy seasonal fruits.

However, even if early check-in is impossible (for example, the room has not been vacated), then the luggage of the guests will be taken to a left-luggage office, and breakfast will be arranged for them, after which they will be taken to a room where you can change clothes. After that, guests usually spend time on the beach.

Daily cleaning is here, as well as linen replacement. New towels are brought here twice - in the morning and in the evening. Cosmetics in sets are also replenished.

And the location is extremely successful. An excellent beach nearby (marked with the EU Blue Flag, by the way), numerous bars, taverns and restaurants (for those who do not want to take a full board), a bus stop directly in front of the building.

Summarizing, we can say with confidence: this is one of the best hotels in all of Rhodes in terms of price and quality. If you want to enjoy the excellent service, comfortable rooms, excellent food and have fun from the heart - it is worth going here.
