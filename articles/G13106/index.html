Grand Okan Hotel 4 * (Turkey, Alanya): description and reviews

Near the Toros Mountains, almost at their very foot, the town of Alanya is actively visited by tourists. Back in the mid-2000s, it was more like a pretty village with several hotels. But today it is a popular resort with a constantly evolving infrastructure. Many good hotels are concentrated here, but now we will talk about the Grand Okan Hotel 4 *.





Location Features

The hotel complex is located on the notorious Cleopatra beach, which extends for about two kilometers along the coast. He was awarded the Blue Flag, a sign of the international environmental certificate.

It got its name in connection with a beautiful legend: they say that the great Cleopatra often visited the resort of Alanya, and especially loved this beach. Mark Anthony, in a sign of his love, decided to give it to her. That's just the queen did not like the sand - so he ordered that he be replaced with another, delivered straight from the Nile River delta. We will never know whether this is a fairy tale or a truth. But on Cleopatra's beach, the sand is really different from what others are covered with.

Now about the landmarks nearby. At 500-900 meters from the hotel there is Alanya water park, cultural center, archaeological museum, Damlatas cave. A little further - the fortress, amusement park, Ataturk Square, Red Tower. If you want to ride, then in 10 minutes you can get to the river and the cave Dim.

As you can see, the location at the hotel is more than successful. Needless to say, even to the airport go less than 40 kilometers.





Business service

Grand Okan Hotel 4 *, the photo of which is presented above, is the place that many business people often choose for official events. In fact, there is everything for this!

The hotel has a spacious, stylish, well-equipped conference room. If the client wishes to be prepared for the event, then the staff with maximum responsibility will approach all, even the smallest details. So the guest may not worry - everything will be done in the best possible way by an experienced and professional team of organizers.

The hall itself can accommodate 80 people. Inside, there is all the necessary equipment - a DVD player, an LCD projector, flipcharts, a projection screen, cable and wireless microphones, wired Internet and Wi-Fi, and much more. By the way, next to the conference room there is a business corner - with a photocopy machine, fax machine, computers and network access.

restaurants and bars

On the territory of Grand Okan Hotel 4 * there are several institutions in which guests can get a real gastronomic pleasure. Here is all you need to know about them:

  • Mahperi. This restaurant is located in the harbor of Alanya, but you just can not help but mention it. After all, it was opened in 1947! In addition, it is here that prepare excellent dishes of fish and seafood.

Important Nuances

Before proceeding to the reviews of the Grand Okan Hotel 4 *, I would like to note a few points regarding the reservation.

This hotel is allowed to stay with children. They are even provided with free cots, but only upon request. If you need them, you must inform the administration before your trip. The hotel must send a confirmation.

If the guests are sent with older children or someone else is traveling with him older than 18, then you must be prepared to pay 28 y. e. per day per person. By the way, an adult bed will be provided for adults traveling with guests who have booked an apartment.

And the last important nuance. When making a reservation, you must provide your credit card details. If it is not, you have to start it. The object accepts "Visa", "MasterCard", "Maestro" and "American Express".
