Best hotels in Ayia Napa, Cyprus: photos, reviews, rating

Ayia Napa is a thriving resort on the east coast of Cyprus. The water temperature here reaches 27 degrees, and a huge amount of day and night entertainment attracts tourists of all ages in Ayia Napa. There are several popular beaches on the coast where the youth party and large families with children get along well. All beaches are well equipped, the entrance to the sea is gentle, and the water is clean and clear. In numerous photos of Ayia Napa hotels you can see how the resort's infrastructure is highly developed.

Ayia Napa has a huge selection of hotels for every taste and budget. The basis of the hotel fund are three- and four-star hotels, but there are also luxurious fives. Many hotels include breakfast, some have half board. Ayia Napa all-inclusive hotels are not so common.

This hotel was designed by one of the world's modern designers and features exquisite decoration in the style of "snow-white island."

The recently renovated hotel consists of thirty-five fully equipped one-bedroom apartments with a studio. There is a restaurant and pool on site. Its ideal location allows you to quickly reach the restaurants, cafes, shops and supermarkets of the city, as well as visit the main attractions of Ayia Napa. Near the apartments there are souvenir shops and convenience stores.

Deluxe apartments. The apartments at the Khaos Hotel offer comfortable rooms for up to 5 people. The rooms have two single beds, one double, chest of drawers, wardrobe, luggage wardrobe, balcony with table and chairs. The living room has two large sofas, a TV, a coffee table, a dining table and an open-plan kitchen with an oven, electric stove, kettle and a large refrigerator.
