Several ways to get from Kirov to Moscow

For several decades now, Moscow has been a city of opportunity. For this reason, many people are interested in the question of how to get to the capital from their hometown. For example, there are several ways to overcome the distance Kirov - Moscow.





Most popular way

The most popular way to get from Kirov to Moscow is by train. It is on it that a large part of the population moves. There are several reasons for this.





Firstly, there are always free tickets, and some of them can be purchased at a fairly low cost.

Secondly, departure takes place every day.

Thirdly, the train is one of the most convenient modes of transport. There is no need to sit all the way in one place, you can walk, sleep and even have a full meal.





All the way will be about 14 hours. In total, several trains run, they depart with a time difference of several hours (the first at midnight, the last at 6.00). The cheapest ticket costs about 900 rubles. You can buy a luxury place at a price of 7 thousand rubles. It is worth noting that all trains from Kirov arrive at the Yaroslavsky station. Each carriage is equipped at the highest level: it has air conditioning, dry closet and convenient service.

The hardest way

The most difficult way to move from one point to another is to use your own vehicle. The difficulties are that the driver has to constantly concentrate on the road, you can confuse the direction, and the new track always causes excitement. However, if you wish, you can try to cover the distance from Kirov to Moscow by car. It will be approximately 950 kilometers. It will take at least 12 hours to go all the way.





It is recommended to start your journey from the route from Kirov to Kostroma, then you need to continue driving to Nizhny Novgorod, after which you can go to Moscow. Along the way there is a smooth, wide and fairly free road. You will have to drive through two magnificent cities famous for their attractions. If you wish, you can stop during the trip in order to admire these beauties. It is not recommended to move from Moscow to Kirov and vice versa in the winter season, since almost the entire route during this period is noticeable and there may be difficulties with driving.





We should also talk about the cost. To overcome this distance, you will need to spend about 4000 rubles on gasoline.

Fastest way

There is the fastest way that will allow you to get from Kirov to Moscow in the shortest possible time. That is the plane. Travel time is only 1.5 hours. However, almost no one uses this route due to the fact that there are a lot of difficulties. First of all, they are connected with the fact that there is no direct flight that would follow a clear schedule. A private flight is organized several times a month, which departs from Kirov Airport to Vnukovo, and flies in the opposite direction. Tickets cannot be bought in advance, but only immediately before boarding. Approximately the cost per person will be 3,500 rubles.





There are several flight options with transfers. In this case, the total travel time with all stops and crossings will take at least 15 hours. The minimum ticket price is about 7,000 rubles. Of course, such a situation will not please many. Therefore, it is better to choose the most convenient way.

The most problematic way

You can also get from Kirov to Moscow by bus. But, unfortunately, there is no direct flight either. First of all, you need to buy a ticket at the Kirov International Station to the city of Cheboksary. Further from this point you can get to Moscow. It is recommended that you book your ticket online in advance so that there are no unforeseen situations. Such a move will cost the traveler only 1,500 rubles. However, it will take 24 hours to spend the entire journey, of course, not every person can sustain such a trip.

other methods

There are several other ways that will allow you to get from Kirov to Moscow. Through a social network you can find travel companions who plan to make a similar move. This will save money on fuel consumption, get a good company and have fun.

You can also use the train with several transfers: in the cities of Uren, Nizhny Novgorod and Vladimir. The maximum cost of each ticket will be only 250 rubles.

In summer, travel companies offer city cruises on the boat, you can use this wonderful vehicle.

It is recommended that the train be preferred from the whole variety of options. This is the most convenient transport that will allow you to get as comfortable as possible from one point to another. You do not need to make any transfers.
