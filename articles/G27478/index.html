Beanie hat - what is it? How to knit a fashionable beanie hat on your own?

This is an ordinary knitted hat that fits tightly on the head. It is not the first year that she has firmly taken a leading position among hats.

Where did this trendy accessory come from

It is believed that the first such hats began to be worn by London workers in 1920: loaders, mechanics, joiners, welders and so on. This thing was part of their work uniform. It was warm, comfortable, and most importantly, under her hair could be hidden.

Then in the 40s, a beanie hat became an indispensable element in the form of first-year students of English and American colleges. The emblem of the educational institution was sewn onto the headgear.



In recent years, this accessory has been modernized, has acquired new forms. In the 90s, the beanie entered the fashionable stage. Until recently, it was an inconspicuous hat, part of a work and student uniform, and now it is a bright accessory that is often found in the images of fashionistas and fashionistas. Representatives of the star elite, for example, Rihanna, Mary-Kate Olsen, Ashley Simpson and many others adore this kind of hats .



Now the trend is a beanie hat with various inscriptions, brand names, plain, with a pattern, ears, pompons, crystals and so on.

Variety of models

The following types are distinguished:

  • Baggy. It is made of textured fabric. This type of hat goes well with loose fit. You can complement the image with ankle boots and a huge bag.
  • In oversized style. Coarse thing. Thanks to this headdress, a playful image is created. Complement it with a dress made from light fabrics of a delicate shade.
  • High. They are made of various materials (fleece, cotton and so on). This view is fixed on the street-bows of bloggers.
  • Hat with a pompom. The latter can be made of large viscous or English rubber. The main element is the pompom. Such a cap will add a little frivolity to your image. Better wear it with things in bright colors.

Surely in the wardrobe, many have a similar cut hat. This accessory is ideal in the autumn-winter period, but you should not forget about it in the summer.

How to choose a model

The beanie hat has a loose fit, and many believe that it fits any type of face. But still pay attention to our advice: first of all, pay attention to mating. A hat made of thick threads and having a large pattern is best suited to a person with expressive facial features. And people with fine features are best suited for knitwear models. You can also choose a monochromatic cap with a soft print.





About the color scheme. We recommend giving preference to pastel colors, shades of green, orange and, of course, classic colors - white and black. All this is very relevant today. But in no case do not forget about your own preferences, choose your favorite model and wear with pleasure!

What material is made of

A hat for the cold season is usually knitted from natural wool, angora, cashmere or mohair. Caps are also sewn of dense knitted material.

For the manufacture of summer options use cotton threads, fleece or water-repellent fabrics. Recently, new materials have been used, such as bamboo yarn, soybean silk.

Next, we learn how to wear a hat and with what it can be combined.

What is the best way to wear

With long hair, this headpiece looks perfect and stylish. Try to close your forehead with a hat, and you can loose the hair on your shoulders or make romantic waves and lay them on one side or just collect the hair in a low tail. This is your choice.



If you have short hair, then wear a hat so that the curls peek out slightly from under the headgear. To do this, slightly lower the cap on the back of your head and open your forehead. If you have a bang, then lay it on its side.

Using the cap, you can adjust the presence of a high forehead or an elongated face. To do this, simply slide the accessory over your eyebrows.

This headpiece can be worn as you like: pull on the crown of the head, and on the forehead, and wear straight, and move a little to the side. Experiment!

What to wear

A large knit hat goes well with down jackets, leather jackets, pullovers, jeans and leggings, as well as with tracksuits. And if there is a pompom on the hat, then it will add some zest to your image.

The knitted model is suitable for a dress made of the same material, for a denim skirt, cardigan, blazer and skinny trousers.



A cashmere hat will look great with a coat, pencil skirt or with strict trousers and a blouse.

If you purchased a bright accessory, then do not oversaturate your image with a lot of other colors. Do not combine more than three colors, otherwise your image will be too ridiculous and variegated.

The hat and scarf look good with each other. They can be of different shades, it is not necessary that they are combined in color and texture.

Such an trendy accessory can be made by yourself. Usually, a beanie hat is knitted or crocheted. This article will present several work options.

How to knit a beanie hat with knitting needles (for beginners)

To work, you need 150 grams of yarn and knitting needles No. 5 and No. 6. Head circumference: 56 centimeters.





Type 68 stitches on knitting needles # 5. Knit eight rows in a circle with an elastic band. Then go to the number 6 knitting needles and knit further with a pearl pattern. That is, in each odd row, change the front loops to the wrong ones. Dial twenty-nine rows this way. Then reduce four loops at the beginning of each row until there are twelve loops left. Then finish the job. Thread the remaining stitches and tighten them tightly. Tie the ends of the thread and hide. So, the beanie hat is ready. A beginner can knit such a model. To make it, one evening is enough. Indulge yourself with such a new thing!

In specialized knitting magazines you can find a huge number of workshops on how to start knitting a beanie hat. Next, we offer another option that is suitable for more experienced knitters.

How to tie a baggy beanie hat

It will take 150 grams of a light shade of yarn and stocking knitting needles No. 7 and No. 8. Head circumference: 58 centimeters.





On the needles number 7, dial 78 loops. Then follow the diagram:

1. Knit ten rows of elastic. Further, for beginners there will be incomprehensible actions - the creation of shortened rows (that is, it is not necessary to knit several loops). We advise beginners to read carefully, if you still choose this model.

2. Take the number 8 knitting needle. In the eleventh row, knit 74 loops and leave them on the right knitting needle, but do not knit four and leave on the left.

3. Turn the job over. Sew 74 loops of garter stitch .

4. Dial the next row again in a shortened way.

5. Knit two rows of garter stitch.

6. Repeat steps two through five.

7. Thus, knit forty centimeters. At will, you can do more.

Pay attention to the product and you will see that one side is narrower than the other. The narrow part will be the top of the head.

8. When the product is completely connected, tighten the loops with a thread, and tighten its end well and thread it. All is ready!

To date, a beanie hat is the most popular accessory. Already for some season she remains at the peak of popularity. This headpiece is perfect for a variety of clothes. For any look you will find the right model. Do not be afraid to experiment. Good luck
