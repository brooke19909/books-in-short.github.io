Overview of some models

ASUS Memo Pad HD

It should be noted right away that this model is widely known and popular not least because of the cost, which is about 4.5 thousand rubles. In this regard, the Asus Memo Pad tablet is the most affordable and high-quality gadget in the lowest price category.

It is built on the basis of the MediaTek MT8125 processor, operating at a frequency of 1.2 GHz. The chip is far from the newest and most productive, but it is enough for everyday tasks. On board there is a gigabyte of RAM. The display is seven-inch, made using TFT technology (LED-backlight). The internal memory is 16 GB, there is also a slot for Micro SD memory cards.

Unlike other cheap models of its competitors, the Asus Memo Pad tablet has both a front and a rear camera. Customer reviews say that the choice of this model is largely due to this particular feature: thanks to the front camera, you can use Skype!

Users note that the Asus Memo tablet is definitely worth it: you can conveniently browse web pages, use some office programs, watch movies and listen to music on it. What is the Asus Nexus tablet? This is a compact device with a seven-inch display. Available in two versions: 16 and 32 GB (internal memory). This is important, since there is no card slot in it. Alas, in this case, the manufacturer followed the path of Apple. However, almost all users believe that a tablet with 32 GB of memory for 11 thousand (and 7 thousand for 16 GB) is a reasonable price, and this amount of internal storage is enough for almost everything.

In addition, experienced tablet enthusiasts testify that in this case it is possible to completely get rid of the unpleasant features of Android: there are always problems with this OS when you need to install applications on a removable memory card. Unlike other tablets, this gadget is supported by the manufacturer (in terms of updates) much longer, so it makes sense to purchase it for those users who need a simple and durable tool to work.
