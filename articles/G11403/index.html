The best Bluetooth headphones for sports: an overview of the best models and reviews about them

Musical accompaniment has long been a natural attribute in the process of sports. Only approaches to the organization of the acoustic background change. And if earlier it was stationary speakers and music centers installed in gyms, today even diving into the pool is not an obstacle to using a sound source. At the moment, the best Bluetooth headphones not only make it possible to listen to your favorite songs in the classroom, but also completely save the owner from unnecessary manipulation of settings and connections. Of course, this segment also has its own gradation of models in terms of characteristics and functional capabilities, which makes us carefully approach the choice of the optimal solution.





What are the features of headphones with a bluetooth module?

The use of systems that provide wireless data transmission has long been practiced in the electronics industry. And if before radio modules were mainly used for exchanging files between gadgets or communicating with a computer, today the developers of audio equipment began to master the idea of ‚Äã‚Äãstreaming audio playback, the source of which is located at a distance. That is, the signal is received, processed and reproduced without acoustic wires.

At the moment, the best inexpensive Bluetooth headphones with price tags of the order of 3-5 thousand are able to work with sources at a distance of about 8-10 m and this distance is constantly increasing as new models appear. Of course, the sound quality as a whole loses to devices that are directly connected to the basic equipment serving as a signal source, but this drawback is gradually minimized.

Philips SHQ7300

Philips is famous for its high-quality portable acoustics and, of course, the compact headphone segment with the ability to wirelessly receive music is not without its products. The model is part of the ActionFit line, which indicates its belonging to the sports line. However, in terms of the implementation of the form factor, this version has several differences from the established fashion. The developers used more massive cups than in the standard, and also supplemented them with ergonomic clips.

It turned out to be a cross between overhead models and gags, but in any case, the device sits securely in your ears and the risk of losing it when running, for example, is low. Therefore, in terms of physical ergonomics, these are the best Bluetooth in-ear headphones, complemented by clips. Of course, the mounting system made the construction a little heavier, but this is an insignificant addition and is hardly noticed during wearing.

As for user reviews, they include the advantages of the model with the presence of effective protection against splashes, convenient control and a fairly long time on a single charge - 5.5 hours. There are no complaints about the sound quality. In this regard, Philips' richest experience in creating a small but well-thought-out audio equipment is precisely what affects it. But there are also negative reviews about the model, which note the inability to start the virtual assistant from the headset.

How to choose the best bluetooth headphones?

The once narrow and specific segment of sports headphones has undergone several cardinal changes associated with increased protective qualities, ergonomic properties and communication abilities. Today it is a wide niche in which both professional athletes and amateurs find the best options. In the forefront in this choice, experts advise to make precisely the structural parameters. The form and method of adjustment are paramount factors, since the convenience of interacting with the gadget depends on them. So, familiar overhead models are gradually becoming a thing of the past, but for lovers of this configuration there are still suggestions.

For example, the best Bluetooth on-ear headphones are featured in the Philips lineup. This is the SHQ7300 model, which, however, is more likely to be combined. The choice of designs with gags focuses on ergonomics and a secure fit - it is advisable to physically test the device before purchase. Do not ignore the operational characteristics, including battery life, communication channels with the sound source, the possibility of settings, etc.

Conclusion





In the latest models of sports wearable electronics, manufacturers rely on high technology to ensure data transfer, although at the dawn of the formation of this segment, questions of reliability and durability were primary. The equipment is subjected to physical stress, contact with water, strong vibrations and temperature effects. Therefore, in the question of which Bluetooth headphones are better for use in sports, it is advisable to take into account the specific scope of their application.

Large full-size models, for example, are not suitable for a wide range of sports disciplines - from running to swimming. A universal solution will be earplugs, but even in this class not every model will be able to withstand the same water environment. These and other operational nuances should be calculated, as well as the playback parameters of musical compositions.

Also, the cost may influence the choice. As practice shows, high-quality wireless headphones for sports can cost 5-7 thousand. Lowering this bar will result in poor quality sound, and an increase can indicate the use of high-tech element base.
