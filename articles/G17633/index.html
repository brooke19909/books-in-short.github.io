Base of rest (Samara). Amazing places of Russia

Every year, the popularity of domestic tourism is growing rapidly. And this can be connected not only with the economic crisis. People are increasingly beginning to appreciate the local natural assets that Russia is so generously sharing.





Samara region is one of the most picturesque in Russia. There are many tourist camps, rest on which will bring a lot of pleasure. Residents of big cities quickly get tired of the constant fuss. Therefore, I want to go on vacation to paradise at least for the weekend. An excellent option would be a recreation center. Samara is famous for its favorable geographical position. The greatest Volga River flows through the entire region.

All the advantages of domestic tourism will show the recreation center "Upper Bor" Samara.

Description





The recreation center is located in the Samara region in a small distance from the city. The environment of the Zhiguli Mountains, pine forest and the Volga River create a unique atmosphere for relaxation.

The complex welcomes guests all year round. Minimum stay is 2 days. Three meals a day are provided here in summer. It is possible to rent sports equipment. The local places are simply designed for outdoor activities. Entertainment

The recreation center (Samara, "Upper Bor") is happy to receive small visitors. There is a playground for them. Activists can engage in various sports. There is a place for playing badminton, table tennis, billiards. You can go for a walk in the picturesque places.

For an additional fee, you can use the services of the host and videographer.

"Rook" is an equally popular recreation center (Samara), located in the city.

Description

The base is located in an ecologically clean area on the right bank of the Volga. Guests can stay in comfortable log cabins. There is also a hotel and two-story hotel houses.

In cottages, the ground floor is dedicated to the living room. There is a TV, a sofa (as an extra bed), a fully equipped kitchen, a bathroom and a shower. On the second floor there are bedrooms.

The three-story cottage can accommodate 8 people. On the attic are single beds with bedside tables and chests of drawers.

The hotel complex has several triple and double rooms. More expensive rooms have amenities inside, while economy rooms are on the floor.
