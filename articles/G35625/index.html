Holiday “hand made”: how to make a wedding invitation do it yourself?

Such an important celebration, like a wedding, is not organized in a hurry. Every detail of the holiday is carefully thought out and prepared. Even such an element as invitations deserves considerable attention, because this is the visiting card of the happiest event in your life. Learn how to make a wedding invitation with your own hands, so that their design is bright and original.





Wedding invitation etiquette

We will start the conversation about holiday cards not with a wrapper, but with the internal content. After admiring the beautiful flowers and beads, the guest opens your message and reads the text, which must be written according to certain rules. It is also worth noting a few more important nuances of wedding etiquette:

  • invitations are awarded a few weeks before the celebration. Guests need time to pick up a gift for you, and holiday dresses for yourself. Well, if the invitees travel from far away, then they must be notified of the joyful event at least a month in advance;
  • Before you make wedding invitations with your own hands, think over the text. It can be different for each guest. For example, when inviting work colleagues, limit yourself to official phrases. For close relatives, a more soulful tone is suitable. Lovers of humor will appreciate the playful mood of the invitation. But the older generation will like poetry;
  • the card must indicate the place and time of the celebration. If you are planning a wedding in a certain style, then mark the requirements for the dress code in the invitation;
  • Recipient names should be written by hand;
  • inviting guests to a wedding in person or by phone does not preclude the need for postcards.

How to make a wedding invitation with your own hands in the technique of quilling?

This style is insanely popular today, because it allows you to create real masterpieces from improvised means. Quilling - the art of composing amazing patterns from thin strips of paper. A kit for this technique can be purchased at the store or made independently. For postcards you will need:





  1. White thick paper.
  2. Contrast color paper, embossed.
  3. Thin paper for strips.
  4. Glue.
  5. Scissors.
  6. Beads.
  7. Tape.

From a white paper, cut a small rectangle and fold it in half. A rectangle (or other shape), slightly smaller than half the previous one, will become the background for our picture. It must be fixed with glue on a white base. Next, from thin sheets we cut many long strips half a centimeter wide. Using a special quilling stick, we twist the spiral from each strip. Lightly crushing paper snails, you can form petals of future flowers that will decorate the card. Thus, do-it-yourself wedding invitations are performed. A workshop on quilling techniques can be found in specialized publications. The center of the flowers is decorated with a bead. An invitation is printed inside, printed in beautiful font or written by hand.

How to make DIY wedding invitations using scrapbooking technique?





This is another popular design style for postcards, commemorative albums, notebooks, diaries. This is an applique made of various materials that is perfect for wedding invitations. You will need rectangles made of thick white paper and special embossed paper for scrapbooking. Also use tape and beads. Stick colored paper on white so that a frame remains around the perimeter. Between the layers it is convenient to place a ribbon, the ends of which are tied in a bow. We decorate the middle of the bow with a bead. You can also use dried flowers, lace and rhinestones in the decor.

The invitation itself is printed on white paper from the back or inserted into a pocket that is easy to make on your own. Scrapbooking is perfect for those who don’t know how to make DIY birthday invitations .
