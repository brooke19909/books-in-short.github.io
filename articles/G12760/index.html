Medhufushi Island Resort 5 * (Maldives): description, service, food, reviews

Small islands in the Indian Ocean are a true paradise on Earth. Here, dazzling white sand and clear white water caress the deserted coast, lush exotic greenery captivates with its unusualness, rainbow birds cheerfully wake up in the mornings with their singing, and hotels - the embodiment of a dream of perfect relaxation and peace. Medhufushi Island Resort is one of the most beautiful places to enjoy a paradise vacation.

Features of rest in the Maldives

Maldives is a small archipelago consisting of many small islands in the Indian Ocean. Their area is so small that the airport is located on a separate stretch of land, and the capital of Male - on the other. All hotels are scattered in the sea, like robinsons, each on its own island. Wealthy tourists use seaplanes to get from the airport to their hotel, and those who choose a budget vacation use speed boats. Between the main cities of the country ferries run.

A rich vacation away from noisy cities begins already in a taxi plane. The service in them is top notch. Tourists are offered refreshments, wet towels and light snacks. The windows of the windows open breathtaking views of the ocean and the small rows of bungalows surrounded by bright coral reefs. In many hotels, small individual houses are located directly in the water, and they are connected to the land by small wooden houses.

When is it best to go to the Maldives

The islands are located near the equator, and all year round the air temperature here does not drop below 28-30 ¬∞ . In the climate there is no pronounced seasonality, as we have here: winter, spring, summer and autumn. But there are months with rains and months without rains. The season in the Maldives begins when it gets colder and autumn begins, that is, somewhere from October-November, and ends in April. All the summer months of the island are influenced by the southwest monsoon, which raises waves and brings frequent rains.

If you are flying to the Indian Ocean to merge with nature: diving, cycling around the islands, admiring crystal clear transparent water, then choose the peak of the hottest period. The season in the Maldives without rains, wind and an abundance of shallow plankton off the coast is January-February and a little March. It is during these months that the lulls of the island become an example of peace and tranquility. Only here you can completely isolate yourself from worldly problems and be alone with yourself and nature.

Prices of tickets to the Maldives

As you know, holidays on the distant islands are very expensive. The flight is about 8 hours with a change in Dubai. The price of a ticket to the Maldives per person for ten days (8 nights) starts at 70,000 rubles. The price includes: flight, transfer to the hotel, accommodation, breakfast.

Another feature of holidays on the paradise islands is that almost all hotels include only breakfast in the price. This is a type of BB food. This is slightly different from the usual All Inclusive service in Turkish and Egyptian resorts. Type of food BB means literally bed and breakfest, which means "bed and breakfast." Hotels have their own restaurants and it is assumed that you need to order food there yourself. And to get a boost of energy for the whole day, a light breakfast is enough.

The price of rest at the Medhufushi Island Resort hotel per person per 10 days is 165,000 rubles. The hotel has a category of 5 stars and boasts excellent service and beautiful rooms.

Description

Medhufushi Island Resort is located on Miimu Atoll. The size of this patch of land is very small. In length - about one kilometer, in width - 2 kilometers. By the standards of the Maldives, this hotel is not very far from the airport - only 130 kilometers away. The seaplane takes about 40 minutes.

According to tourists, hotel representatives are greeted right at the airport. Baggage is sent immediately, but tourists themselves still need to get to the plane to begin a short trip.

The shores are clean. The west beach of Medhufushi Island Resort is shallow, suitable for swimming with children. On the eastern side of the island there is a lagoon, which is nice to swim and dive. In the depths there are colorful fish, stingrays, reef sharks. Herons walk along the shores and crows nest.

Service

There are several restaurants on the island of the hotel where you can taste dishes of different cuisines of the world:

  • Makaafaiy Restaurant - a large restaurant, it is considered the main on the island. Located on the terrace by the pool. It has a beautifully decorated room, and the windows overlook the lagoon. It offers buffet meals.
  • Al Fresco is another restaurant, but it is located in stilt houses right on the ocean. Delicious food and a wide selection of dishes delight tourists.
  • Vilu bar is the center of the resort's life. All parties and noisy discos are held only here. In the evenings, you can take part in the billiard tournament. At the bar you can buy an exotic cocktail, an alcoholic drink or enjoy a light dessert.

The Medhufushi SPA Center offers peace and tranquility. Located on a snow-white smooth surface in the very center of the lagoon. Here you can use the services of a beautician and undergo various face and body treatments. There are several types of massage. Medhufushi SPA - complete relaxation on the island of dreams.

Service Medhufushi Island Resort includes a doctor, room service, laundry, library, gym, billiards, beach volleyball, many different water activities. The hotel has Wi-Fi.

Opinion of tourists

According to those who have already rested, the hotel has more marks of "excellent" and "good" than "bad" and "terrible." Excellent service, beautiful views, delicious food and interesting entertainments make this hotel a favorite among tourists. Reviews Medhufushi Island Resort has both positive and negative.

pros

Almost all vacationers say that the food here is really excellent. A variety of dishes, there is fish, meat, many seafood. An abundance of fruits, many grow right on the island. Alcohol can be purchased separately. Tourists do not recommend choosing the All Inclusive system, because, apart from breakfast and free cocktails, nothing else is included.

The hotel has a lot of staff ready to fulfill any request. Sometimes it seems that there are even more of them than vacationers themselves. There are special people who make a bed and put swans from towels. Men work separately to clean the beach, remove coconuts from palm trees and cut leaves from trees so that they always have the same shape. Even women work who feed the goldfish all day long.

Pleasing entertainment. Cool diving, only expensive. On average, one hour will cost 9,000-12,000 rubles. A lot of water activities, including boat rental. He will take you to the open ocean to enjoy dolphins.

Very beautiful area: spacious, green, everything is neat and tidy. The rooms are simply and tastefully furnished. Everything is made of natural materials: wood, metal, vines. They constantly clean and make sure that unpleasant neighbors in the form of ants or other insects get into your room.

Minuses

The hotel has very poor internet. All tourists are advised to buy a local SIM card at the airport and use the Internet services through it. Maldives is a multinational resort, but most of all the Chinese like to relax there. According to tourists, there are many at any time of the year.

But the big plus of the hotel is that all the houses are separate from each other and the chance to meet their neighbors is small.

Tourists also note that, in general, the resorts of the Maldives are quite boring. In fact, you are attached to your island, where you have everything to relax. You can go to the capital or any other city in the country, but for this you need to rent a boat or a seaplane separately.

Overall, the hotel rating is very good. Small cons they are everywhere, but there are still more pluses. Beautiful vacation on a paradise island! Come to the Maldives at any time of the year and enjoy impeccable service.
