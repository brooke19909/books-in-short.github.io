"Veterok" (recreation center), Dolzhanskaya village, Yeisk district: photos and reviews of tourists

Krasnodar Territory is certainly rich in hotels, pensions and guest houses. Today we want to get to know a resort area such as Dolzhanka. Recreation facilities on the territory of the Yeisk district of the Krasnodar Territory are growing every day, old ones are being improved and new ones are being built. We will briefly talk about the most popular and visited places on this sunny coast so that you can make your choice.



A bit of history

As you already understood, Dolzhanka is one of the villages of the Yeisk district of the Krasnodar Territory. It was founded by Cossacks back in the 19th century and is located on the shores of the Sea of ‚Äã‚ÄãAzov, at the base of the Long Spit. About 40 km the city of Yeysk developed. Today the Long Spit is a kind of nature monument of the Krasnodar Territory. Its unique shell beaches, freshwater lakes and a diverse fauna and flora each year bring here new tourists. Nearby is the Veterok, a recreation center visited by hundreds of Russians every year.

Base Description

Initially, it was built as a resort and sanatorium base for rest and treatment of Russians. The Azov coast of Russia was right for these purposes. "Veterok" is a recreation center located in the village of Dolzhanskaya Yeisk district, on the shore of the Taganrog Bay, in a park zone. This is a very good place for parents with young children. Wonderful shallow beaches with golden sand stretch to the horizon, the children are ready to splash around in warm water for days on end.



Sports tourism

Veterok is a recreation center that has long been a favorite for windsurfers and kitesurfers. This is facilitated in the first place by the very successful geographical location of the spit, which makes it possible to practice this difficult and wonderful sport for all, without exception. A beginner will be able to try his hand, and the master will enjoy the process. Spit Dolgaya is a unique reserve, so it is doubly interesting to relax here. The stunning beaches of all, without exception, return to childhood. And what could be better than sculpting a sand sculpture with the whole family? The air here is simply amazing, the smells of taiga dissolve in the fresh sea breeze, turning into a wonderful mix.



Infrastructure

All lovers of beach holidays are waiting for the welcoming "Veterok". The recreation center makes it possible to spend a lot of time in the fresh air, hiking in beautiful places. However, if you worry that you will be bored in the evenings, then it is completely in vain. There is a market and shops, cafes and bars, cinemas, as well as a luxury tour block. That is, you will find here everything you need for an amazing vacation. In 2004, the Veterok base was reconstructed for the last time. Dolzhanskaya village offers a number of boarding houses, sports and health centers to choose from, but today we will focus only on this.



Number of rooms

Since the flow of tourists here is quite large, they need to be comfortably accommodated. The village Dolzhanskaya succeeded in this. The recreation center "Veterok" offers accommodation 81 rooms. These are double, triple, quadruple, junior suites and suites. Some rooms are located in detached cottages.

Now a little more detail. On the base there are a number of wooden houses designed for 2, 3 or 4 people. These are ordinary summer buildings without amenities. In each such house there is a refrigerator and a set of dishes, and a toilet and shower are located on the territory. In addition, there are brick and wooden houses of a higher class, there is already a refrigerator and a TV, air conditioning. The toilet, shower and washbasin are one in 2 rooms. These are considered junior suites. The suites are still slightly different from them, they are located in brick houses, equipped with air conditioning and a TV, fridge and bathroom.



Cost of rest

I must say that this is another point for which the village Dolzhanka is so appreciated by tourists. The prices here are quite affordable, so a vacation on the coast of the Sea of ‚Äã‚ÄãAzov will not affect your budget too much. Renting a double house without amenities without meals - from 288 rubles per day, a triple house without amenities costs 438 rubles. per day. Quadruple without amenities will cost you 563 rubles.

For two people they cost 875 rubles, for three or four - from 1250 rubles.



Catering for tourists

Meals are not included in the package price, so you can go to dinner in cafes or restaurants in a city like Yeysk. Dolzhanka also invites you to any of the local canteens, and the easiest way is to dine right at the recreation center. There is an excellent dining room in which you will be offered three meals a day at a modest price - only 400 rubles per day. Judging by the reviews of tourists, the cuisine here is excellent, the menu contains all kinds of pastries, fresh vegetables and fruits, wines and beer for every taste, which any gourmet will appreciate. I must say that even sophisticated connoisseurs give these drinks high marks.

Tourists reviews

Vacationers very warmly remember the time spent here. All guests emphasize that the base is a huge amount of entertainment. You can play billiards or go horse riding. Others like the opportunity to go to the sauna, the third all nights spend all night at discos.

Separately, there are reviews of the cinema hall, in which older guests like to spend time. Those who come with a cheerful, friendly company say that there are excellent conditions for picnics. There are gazebos equipped with barbecue facilities, as well as a sports ground and tennis court to get in shape after evening gatherings.

And finally, without exception, everyone likes the opportunity to travel around the protected area. The tour desk offers dozens of route options of varying lengths and complexity, but Dolgaya Spit is the most popular.
