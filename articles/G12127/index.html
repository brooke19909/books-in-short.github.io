Solnechnogorsk Hotels: Review and Reviews

Solnechnogorsk, founded in the XVIII century. And he received the status of the city in 1931. Solnechnogorsk is a city with a developed industry, which arose on the basis of a working settlement. But, despite the large number of manufacturing enterprises, it is one of the cleanest regions of the immediate suburbs. And it is here that tourists come with great pleasure to relax from the noisy metropolis.

Environmentally friendly region

Cozy hotels are mainly concentrated in the Solnechnogorsk city center, since the main attraction of the city is the picturesque Lake Senezh, located in the same place. Here you can often meet lovers of spearfishing, windsurfing and fishing. And both in the hot and in the cool months of the year. The lake is home to a huge number of different fish.



The city has a very convenient location and is located in the Moscow region, only 44 km from MKAD. Here you can always find the right hotels or hotels for yourself. Solnechnogorsk can offer a variety of living conditions and the number of stars. And each visitor will be able to find something that suits him personally. And judging by the numerous reviews of tourists, the Solnechnogorsk hotel (Moscow region) is an excellent option for vacationing residents of a huge metropolis at affordable prices.

Hotel "Alice"

Hotel "Sun City"

The cozy hotel "Sun City" is located in the city center. It is small, located in a shopping center. The interior is decorated in a contemporary style, and free Wi-Fi is available throughout the hotel.



Sun City offers its guests seven rooms: double, with one double bed or studio rooms. Each has its own shower. Meals - only breakfasts that deliver to the room. And a huge number of bars and restaurants located near the hotel, a 5-7-minute walk, will help the guest solve the problem with lunch and dinner. The train station and bus station are 300 meters away.

The cost of living is 3500 rubles. per day. Check in at 13.00.

The reviews of tourists staying here note the interesting interior of the rooms, the friendliness and friendliness of the staff, as well as their convenient location. The disadvantages of the hotel include the lack of cashless payments and the location in the shopping center.

Hotel "Sunny"

In the small city of Solnechnogorsk, hotels are divided into establishments located in the city center for business trips and intended for a short stay and those where you can spend your long-awaited vacation or holiday weekend.

One of these hotels is "Sunny" - a whole suburban complex. It is surrounded on all sides by picturesque views: Zhigansky ponds are located on one side, and on the other hand a coniferous array covers it from prying eyes. This hotel, by the way, can be attributed to the section "Solnechnogorsk Hotels - Moscow Region" , as it is located outside the city.

"Sunny" offers its guests 90 rooms:

  • standard;
  • one-room and two-room "junior suite" and "luxury",
  • cottages;
  • three-room apartments.

The hotel has an outdoor pool, SPA, sports and playgrounds, ice rink. The hotel operates on an all-inclusive basis, there are two restaurants and three bars. Free Wi-Fi and internet are available on site.

Tourists, having a rest here, give him a solid 4+, noting the excellent cuisine, good alcohol for the all-inclusive system, the friendliness of the staff, the large territory and the high quality of the services provided in the SPA. Vacationers pay special attention to free services: bike rental and all sports fields.





Boutique Hotel Seneschal

"Seneschal" refers to one of the most visited hotels in Solnechnogorsk, which are located on the lake. The journey here takes only a few hours, as the distance is only 55 km from Moscow to the hotel. Solnechnogorsk is proud of this hotel.

It has only 11 rooms, but they are all furnished with antique furniture that gives a certain charm. The staff speaks two languages. Guests have access to a private beach on the lake. Each room is equipped with disposable slippers, toiletries and bathrobes.

It is worth noting that the hotel is expensive; a day of living in it costs 12 thousand rubles.

Reviews of tourists about the hotel are ambiguous - everyone notes a somewhat unfriendly attitude of the staff, a small territory and high prices, but the view from the room more than pays for the shortcomings.



Hotel Heliopark Lesnoy

The hotel is located in a relict forest, ideal for youth recreation, as well as for couples with children. The hotel has three stars.

The hotel has two saunas, a swimming pool, sports grounds for football, basketball and volleyball. For children there is a children's animation group, there is a specially equipped room and an outdoor playground.

The hotel operates on an all-inclusive basis: for lunch, breakfast and dinner, a buffet with free alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks.

All the furniture in the rooms is modern, the design of the rooms is very stylish. Each room has a private bathroom with free toiletries. The staff speaks two languages.

Check-in time is 17 hours and check-out time is 15 hours.

The hotel has its own website where you can find all the necessary information about the hotel and the services provided.

Visitors' feedback on Heliopark Lesnoy is very good - the hotel is perfect for large corporate parties. Almost every visitor puts him a solid five. They note the excellent work of the staff of the whole complex, the cleanliness of the pool, quality food and reasonable prices.





Rest house "Senezh"

Rest house "Senezh" is a great place for family vacations. The international airport is only 40 km from the hotel. Solnechnogorsk is ready to receive guests not only from Russia.

All rooms located in the hotel (and there are 38) have a balcony with stunning views of the forest or lake. Each room has en suite bathrooms with free toiletries. Check-in is expected from 14 hours, the staff speaks two languages. A feature of the holiday home is the presence of its own beach on the lake.

Reviews of tourists about the hotel are only positive, most of all they rated the quality of service and food, which, according to reviews, is simple, but tasty and fresh. The visitors were impressed by the spacious and multifunctional area and hiking trails. The rooms are large, spacious, however, the repair is not very new.
