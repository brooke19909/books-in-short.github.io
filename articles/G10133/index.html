DIY puzzle for the mind and relaxation

When a large company gathers, the question immediately arises about what to do so that everyone has fun. The best way to usefully spend time is to occupy yourself with something necessary and interesting. Free time can be spent on solving puzzles or on a board game in which several people can take part simultaneously. But such entertainment is a rather expensive thing, so not everyone can afford to buy them. But this does not matter, since there is a great way out: a puzzle that everyone can create with his own hands. To do this, you need only a little patience and free time, as well as an original idea for the game, so that a large company likes to participate in it. To make a game you need a wooden board 50x30x2.5 cm (width, length, height) and a jigsaw. If you do not have such professional equipment, use a conventional hacksaw. Each piece of your future tower will be 2.5 x 10 x 2.5 cm (width, length, height). Mark the prepared board according to the specified parameters and cut it. You should get 60 exactly the same wooden blocks. Here‚Äôs a do-it-yourself puzzle, and it's ready.



The rules of the game are very simple. First, we build the resulting elements in the form of a tower. We place four small blocks next to each other on the floor or on any other stable surface. Suppose they are vertical to you. Then you must put the next four blocks on top of them, but horizontally. Then again vertically and again horizontally. And so on all the figures. Now each player in turn pulls out any one block from the resulting structure. The essence of the game is to pull out the tower details as accurately as possible, otherwise the whole structure will collapse. Accordingly, the loser is the one on whose move the tower will fall apart. As you can see, a do-it-yourself puzzle can also be interesting and exciting.



Another universal type of puzzle that you can do at home is puzzles. But this mosaic will not be ordinary, but unique. So, we take a small thin wooden board about 0.5 cm thick. First you need to decide what shape the puzzle will be. It is better to make it rectangular or round so that it is easier to assemble. So, using a jigsaw, we saw the board of the right size into small parts with a curved outline. Be sure to make a frame for our puzzle to make it easier to assemble. Your task in this game will be to combine the resulting pieces in their original form. This is another puzzle that you can do with your own hands simply and quickly.

Now you can solve puzzles for free at any time, because you do not have to spend money on them, because you can build each of them yourself at home. Most other wood games are made similarly. Thus, you and your friends will never be bored, having at hand a lot of wonderful puzzles!
