Hotel "Cosmos", Yaroslavl: description of rooms, reviews

A quite popular accommodation option is the Cosmos Hotel. Yaroslavl will welcome you with hospitable smiles, quality service and a high level of comfort.





Brief description of the hotel

On the street Volodarsky in Yaroslavl is the economy hotel "Cosmos". Here guests can enjoy services such as wireless Internet, parking area, special non-smoking rooms, use of a shared kitchen, games room and much more. The 24-hour reception staff will come to your aid at any time.

The rooms that the Cosmos Hotel provides its guests deserve special attention. Yaroslavl will open before you in all its glory from the windows of your apartments. All apartments have their own bathroom with shower, as well as other necessary amenities. Maids carry out daily cleaning, as well as regular replacement of bed linen and towels.

The Cosmos Hotel has its own cozy cafe. Yaroslavl is rich in catering establishments, but it is extremely important to be able to have a hearty meal without leaving the hotel. Waiters can deliver your order directly to your room. You will burn breakfast or three meals a day for meals.





Hotel Location

The address of the Cosmos Hotel in Yaroslavl is ul. Volodarsky, 99. It is approximately 2 kilometers from the city railway station. From here to the hotel you can get to the hotel by shuttle buses No. 9, 11, 30 and 52. Also, trolleybus No. 3 runs from the station. You need to get off at the Karl Marx Square stop, and then walk a little. In the immediate vicinity of the hotel are such infrastructure facilities as a large park, Atlant sports complex, as well as Arena 2000.

Cosmos Hotel: description of rooms

This hotel has 51 rooms that can accommodate 110 people. Standard and superior rooms are offered to guests by the Cosmos Hotel (Yaroslavl). Accommodation prices range from 1800 to 2500 rubles. So, you can choose one of the following accommodation options:

  • Single rooms are ideal for those tourists who are used to traveling on their own, as well as for business people. There are all the necessary conditions for a comfortable stay.
  • Double rooms are equipped with a pair of one and a half beds, as well as bedside tables. The room has a desk, chairs and a mirror.

Services included in the price

Choosing for accommodation such an option as the Cosmos Hotel (Yaroslavl), you can be sure that the following services will be included in the price:

  • all guests can use the first-aid kit, which is located at the reception;
  • if you feel unwell, the hotel will call a doctor for you;
  • all correspondence sent to your name will be delivered directly to your room;
  • if you are afraid to oversleep an important event, tour or train, use the "alarm" service;
  • you can have fun playing dominoes, checkers or chess; you can take boiling water at a cafe at any time;
  • use the fully equipped shared kitchen for cooking;
  • if necessary, you can use the sewing kit (needles, threads, and so on).

Service for an additional fee

For an additional fee, you can use the following list of services:

  • if you have too large and heavy suitcases, the hotel staff will help you bring them to your room or to a taxi;
  • storage of bulky or valuable luggage in a specially equipped room;
  • if necessary, the reception staff will call a taxi for you;
  • change of bed linen outside the schedule;
  • at the reception there is a safe in which each of the guests can rent a cell;
  • You can make a copy of your documents or send a fax.




Hotel "Cosmos" (Yaroslavl), reviews: advantages

About this hotel, guests leave the following positive comments:

  • quite convenient location near the central part of the city and the railway station;
  • the hotel is very quiet, due to the fact that it is in a quiet area;
  • a bed, a table, chairs, a wardrobe - all the furniture in the rooms is quite new and designed in the same style;
  • on each floor there is a cooler where you can get both cold water and boiling water;
  • very clean linens, as well as towels;
  • nearby is a public transport stop, from where it is very convenient to get to the city center;
  • fairly fresh repair;
  • the bathroom has a new shower; own secure parking;
  • there is hot water in the hotel even when it is turned off throughout the city;
  • there is a refrigerator on each floor;
  • happy with the availability of a shared kitchen, so you can not spend money on food in cafes or restaurants;
  • good breakfasts;
  • very friendly staff who do their job well;
  • there are many sockets in the room, so you can turn on all household appliances and charge the gadgets.




Negative reviews

If we talk about the negative moments of rest at the Cosmos Hotel, the most significant are the following:

  • the hotel is located next to the courtyard of a residential building, and therefore, if your windows do not look outside, the room will be quite noisy;
  • guests are allowed only by appointment and for a strictly limited time;
  • linen is changed quite rarely - about once a week;
  • there is no way to pay by credit card;
  • in summer, there is an acute shortage of air conditioning in the room;
  • despite a fairly fresh repair, there are small cracks on the plaster;
  • the room does not have a hairdryer or large bath towels (all this must be requested at the reception);
  • the bathroom has very poor lighting and ventilation;
  • the rooms have very poor soundproofing, and therefore noise from the kitchen and the corridor will interfere with you;
  • the cafe has a very uncomfortable schedule, and there are no similar establishments nearby.




General impression

Cosmos Hotel in Yaroslavl is an excellent option for those who are looking for affordable accommodation near the city center. This hotel is often chosen by those guests of the city who stay for a long period. The hotel is located in a quiet residential quarter, and therefore there is a fairly calm atmosphere. The city center can be reached on foot or by bus. I am glad that on each floor there is a shared kitchen where you can cook for yourself. But the cafe here leaves much to be desired. It is located on the ground floor, and only breakfasts deserve attention here.

It is worth noting that the rooms in this hotel are quite comfortable, despite the simple atmosphere. It has everything you need for a full stay and relaxation. Pleased with fresh repairs, as well as the fact that the furniture in the room is new and designed in the same style. There is also a good shower, which is a rarity for budget rooms. But there are also shortcomings. For example, cracks in the walls, as well as poor ventilation. There is no electric kettle, hairdryer, iron and large bath towels. In general, the living conditions and related services are fully consistent with low cost.
