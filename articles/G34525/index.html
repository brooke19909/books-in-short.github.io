Pine Ribbon Burs

These are forest shelter belts reaching many kilometers across and located along river banks. There are a lot of them in the south of the region. They grow for a reason, their function is to protect soils from weathering and sand storms from the Republic of Kazakhstan.

These forests also create a microclimate favorable for humans and animals. The names of pine forests often come from the rivers in which they are located. The main enemy of the tape forests is currently forest fires. Forest protection is not able to provide them with protection from this threat.





Pillars Preserve

It is located in the northwest of the Eastern Sayan. It has about a hundred rock outcrops, whose height reaches 600 meters. They were born thanks to the activity of volcanoes. About 450 million years ago, hot magma, breaking through to the surface of the earth, pushed piles of limestone and sandstone. Over time, rains and wind destroyed soft rocks. So above the ground appeared these huge pillars, consisting of solid rocks.

Each pillar was named after its own name. These places have gained popularity among climbers and proponents of hanging out in the lap of nature.

Sayano-Shushenskaya hydroelectric power station

It is the most powerful hydroelectric power station in the Russian Federation and the seventh in the world. It has the highest dam in our country. The construction of the facility began in 1963, and after 15 years (in the year of launch), 1,700 people have already successfully worked on it. The dam of the station has a height of 245 meters and a length of 1074 meters.

A hydroelectric power station was built on the Yenisei River near Sayanogorsk. After the accident in 2009, the station was completely restored. The cities of Siberia fascinate with their power, because such important Russian objects are located there.
