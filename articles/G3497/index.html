Eco farms in the suburbs: options for recreation

In the age of high technology, rest on ecological farms is gaining more and more popularity, where you can not only get acquainted with the way of rural life, but also improve your health through walks in the fresh air and natural food. In the suburbs there are also such farms.

Cheese Factory in Istra

If you move along Novorizhskoe highway, then after about 60 km you can reach the village of Dubrovka. It was there that in August 2015, one young man, Oleg Sirota, a specialist in the field of IT technology, opened his own cheese production farm. Oleg‚Äôs dream is simple - to bring his production to the federal level, to deliver products to all nearby and distant stores of the country. Cheeses are very diverse: hard and soft, special attention is paid to the production of domestic analogues of parmesan and emmentaler.

Ecofarm in the suburbs is always glad to new visitors. Both children and adults will be able to stroll through the cheese factory, which has large panoramic windows, peering through which you can see many cheese heads that each ripen peacefully on their shelf: "Beer", "Wine", "Governor", "Istra", "Peasant". If you arrive in the afternoon and you are lucky enough to find the owner of the production, Oleg Sirota himself, he will gladly conduct a tour for you and your children, tell you about the main stages of production, as well as interesting stories related to the appearance of cheese in the world.

After the tour, you can independently taste the products that are available for sale in the store, which is located right at the farm. However, if you come here solely for the purchase of cheese, you should call in advance and check availability, because some varieties may end. The plans of the owner of the cheese factory are grandiose: start your own herd, because now milk is purchased exclusively from farmers, as well as open a museum and conduct master classes for children and adults on the primary production of cheese. By the way, in the store you can also buy natural yogurts with berry additives, which children love so much.

Agrofarm "Eco-village"

On the 110th kilometer along the Novoryazanskoye shosse, next to other eco-farms, in the Moscow Region is located "Eco-village". The complex is famous for its unusual excursions. Where else can you find a real crocodile farm or see a breeding of grape snails!

The grape snail has been known since ancient times. Eastern Europe is considered its homeland, from where it eventually spread throughout the territory, except for the northern regions. Interestingly, grape snails were not considered a delicacy before and they were eaten by all segments of the population. It was an ordinary tasty and healthy protein meal. Snails fall into hibernation, so the farm has all the conditions for their year-round wakefulness. Various packings are offered for sale: 500 grams each or on plates in 200 g.

Rest on an eco-farm in the suburbs will be remembered forever if you choose to visit a crocodile nursery, which is also available in the Eco-Village. Several species of reptiles are represented here, but the caiman, which reaches two to three meters in length, is worthy of special attention. For crocodiles, this is not very much. Both adults and children will be able to take pictures against the background of reptiles and feed them under the supervision of a specialist.

In addition to snails and crocodiles, you can diversify your leisure with horseback riding. The specialists of the Eco-Village will introduce you to the basic rules, help you climb the saddle, which is not easy for beginners, and will accompany you on the trip. Communication with horses is one of the strongest antidepressants. And, of course, a real Russian bathhouse. At the eco-farm, it is also available. Between excursions, you should definitely enjoy a lunch of natural products, which are prepared for guests every day.

"Vankovo"

In the Moscow region, eco-houses with accommodation are in great demand. Especially in the summer, when the forests are full of mushrooms and berries, the sun warms up and you can walk until sunset. One of these farms is located 116 kilometers along the Mozhaisk highway.

The farm has a wide selection of entertainment. In addition to the fact that it contains various types of birds, such as chickens, geese, turkeys and ducks, cattle, large and small, also live. You can stroke and feed animals and birds, for example, a goat, which, with soft lips, gently with pleasure will take a piece of bread from you. Some can even be milked. But this is a job for those who can. From the milk obtained, you can cook sour cream or cottage cheese yourself. Production specialists will be happy to help in this interesting undertaking.

Ecological farms of the Moscow Region with accommodation are famous for the fact that you can explore the surroundings. In the summer, for example, you can go to the forest for mushrooms and berries, in winter rent a snowmobile or ski and go on a snow expedition, and after an eventful day, return to a wooden house and relax for a delicious dinner. Farm in Potapovo

Rest on an eco-farm in the suburbs can be very comfortable, contrary to ideas about the countryside. The farm in Potapovo, for example, has four stars and full hotel functionality, as in the best hotels of the famous sea resorts. The rooms have air conditioning, free Wi-Fi, luggage storage and dry cleaning services. If you come to rest with children, you can bring them to the games room, and do the essentials yourself. There is parking and a pool, and a restaurant on an eco-farm in the Moscow region will delight you with delicious dishes made from fresh products for a reasonable price.

Communication with horses will bring special pleasure, because it is here that one of the large stud farms works. Trainers will tell and show how to properly approach horses, stay in the saddle, introduce interesting facts from animal life. This is one of the best eco-farms in the Moscow region for children. The cost of living in a family room is about five thousand rubles a day.

Agricultural complex "Bogdarnya"

Holidays with children at the eco-farms of the Moscow Region are always associated with a large number of animals. But the villages closest to the region boast the presence of animals with whom you can communicate throughout your vacation. Such a place is the eco-farm "Bogdarnya", located in the village of Krutovo, Vladimir Region. Here for the entertainment of children there is a mini zoo and horseback riding both in the saddle and in the harness.

A large and friendly company can be accommodated in guest houses, extreme inns near the stables, as well as in cottages on the shores of lakes with a full range of attributes of village life. The cost of housing for a day with breakfast starts from 3500 rubles.

Teterin Ecofarm

If you are looking for an eco-farm in the Moscow Region for children with parents, the Teterin complex is definitely suitable for you. It is located 100 kilometers from Moscow, in the village of Ryzhevo. Here is an extensive economy: several species of poultry, horse, goat, cow. In summer and winter there is something to do. In the warmer months, you can ride horses in a saddle and harness, in winter to harness animals in a sled and with a breeze to explore the surrounding area. This will appeal to both children and adults. Heads of families can relax while sitting with a fishing rod on the shore of a calm lake. All the catch caught is left to the fisherman and can be prepared in the local cuisine.

Guests can rent bicycles and snowmobiles in winter. For accommodation, you can choose comfortable eco-houses or rooms in a Russian estate. The cost of accommodation per day in the houses is 10 thousand rubles, in the estate - 20 000 rubles. This price includes breakfast and lunch, Internet, children's room and excursions.

Farm "Olgino"

In the village of Fedtsovo in the Volokolamsk district, a surprisingly comfortable farm "Olgino" is located. If you are looking for an option that is not at all like an eco-hotel in the Moscow region, then Olgino will definitely suit you. All houses are located at a respectful distance from each other, with their own access roads and parking. So you can safely come here in your car and leave it here.

Such infrastructure allows you to feel not in a rented house outside the city, but in your own cottage somewhere near a small lake. In the courtyard of each house there is a barbecue. You can take out some chairs into the courtyard in the evening, lay a barbecue in the brazier and enjoy the beautiful summer sunsets. Ducks, geese, chickens, ponies and horses, goats live on the farm. Communication with them is available at any time of the day. Active guests can rent bicycles and fishing equipment.

You can also while away the time on the lake, swim and sunbathe, and in the evening bask in a real bath. All dishes are prepared in Russian style. The products at the eco-farm in the suburbs of our own production, so you can eat as much as your heart desires without fear of an unexpected allergy. All food is simple and healthy. Two-story houses for living, you can come in a big cheerful company, there is enough room for everyone. The cost is from 3500 rubles.

"Forest Guinea Fowl"

Nineteen kilometers from MKAD, and you are in the Solnechnogorsk district, in the village of Lunevo, where the lovely cozy farm "Forest Guinea Fowl" is located.
