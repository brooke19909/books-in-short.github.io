Villa Neno 3 - economy class hotel

“Montenegrin Miami” - this is what Budva is called, a seaside resort and the main tourist center of the country. It has everything from discos and casinos, restaurants and bars, casinos to sports fields. Budva is great for active and youth recreation. In the high season, life in the resort does not freeze for a minute, and the hotels in the city are filled to the last number.

In the city and its environs, you can count about thirty-five beaches with clear water. Eight of them have been awarded the Blue Flag. Budva - the center of mass tourism - today many Russians choose. There are about a hundred hotels with different price categories: from luxury five-star to three-star economy class hotels. Among them, Villa Neno 3 occupies a worthy place.



general information

The hotel is conveniently located in the heart of the resort of Budva. Only three hundred meters to the sea, the Slovenian Beach tourist complex, popular in the region, is almost as far from it. The territory of Villa Neno 3, although small, but completely turned into a green garden. There is also a comfortable terrace. The old city, which has many entertainment venues, is located almost nearby, half a kilometer away. There is also a grocery market and supermarkets.

The hotel has many restaurants and cafes. The nearest airport is only twenty-three kilometers away, so in just half an hour, tourists get to Villa Neno 3 (Budva).

Infrastructure

The hotel is assigned the category of “three stars”, therefore it does not have a very well-developed infrastructure, however, residents can receive all the services necessary for a comfortable stay. On the ground floor of the only building there is a lobby where guests can safely wait for their check-in, a safe deposit box, and a laundry room. The hotel has a small free parking that does not require prior reservation.

The registration desk operates around the clock. Here you can order a return transfer, photocopy documents, rent a car, which is especially convenient for those who combine relaxation with self-examination of local attractions.



Housing stock

Villa Neno 3 (Montenegro) - the hotel is very small. It has only twenty one numbers. This is a big plus for many tourists, since the hotel is always quiet, which is especially true for lovers of a calm measured rest.

Standard rooms are designed for a maximum of two or three people at a time. They have a small area of ​​15 or 19 square meters, but they have everything you need for a comfortable stay.

Hotel policies do not include pets. Maids clean the apartments daily, and change sheets with towels every other day.

The rooms are equipped in one four-story building, built in a modern style. The rooms have new furniture: one double or two single comfortable beds, depending on the type of placement, wardrobe, desk and bedside tables. Apart-rooms, in addition to the kitchen area, where there is all the utensils, individually controlled split systems are installed, satellite TV is available. On the floor - ceramic tiles.

Each Villa Neno 3 room has a small, furnished balcony. A clothes dryer, plastic chairs are installed here . Bathrooms - combined, there is a hairdryer.

Nutrition



On the ground floor of the hotel ’s villa, a small, but very cozy à la carte restaurant is open for lunch and evening. The hotel serves a continental breakfast. Here, almost at home, you can taste fresh fish and a glass of amazing wine.

Judging by the reviews, you can dine cheaply in the restaurants located near the hotel: the price of a hearty dinner for two without alcohol is an average of fifteen euros.

Beach

To the sea from Villa Neno 3 - ten minutes walk. The beach is public, so everything you need is available at an additional cost. Coverage - pebble and sand, the entrance to the sea is convenient. Those who do not want to sunbathe on the beach can take a seat on the comfortable terrace in the garden of Villa Neno 3.

Reviews and prices

The Russians have very different impressions of being in Montenegro. Spoiled by the noisy resorts of Egypt and Turkey, a domestic tourist sometimes expresses dissatisfaction with a measured vacation in Budva. However, couples and elderly people here really like it.



Those who stayed at the Villa Neno 3 Hotel also have many positive reviews. Many are satisfied with the lack of a swimming pool and noisy animation. In a calm environment, you can stroll through the garden, take walks in the Old Town.

At a hotel, Russians are satisfied primarily with the price-quality ratio of services. The cost of the tour in July for two with departure from Moscow and accommodation in a double room with meals only “continental breakfast” - from forty-seven and a half thousand rubles. The price may be changed depending on the charter flight.

The only drawback, according to some, is the slightly small rooms in Villa Neno 3. Nevertheless, the vast majority of Russians believe that in this three-star hotel you can relax normally, and on a budget, enjoying the sea and the gentle rays of the sun.
