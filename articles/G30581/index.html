The best hotels of Morocco: photos, reviews

More recently, the Kingdom of Morocco has become an open tourist destination. Previously, to visit here, you had to fly with transfers, but now everything is different. Charter flights operate from time to time.

Morocco is one of the western states of the Arab countries. On the one hand it is washed by the Mediterranean Sea, on the other - the Atlantic Ocean. In the country there are mountains. Among the inhabitants of the wild there are gazelles, panthers, baboons, foxes. In Morocco there is a holiday for every taste: you can just lie on the beach, take a walk through the ancient cities of Berbers, or you can even go skiing. There are many hotels of different levels of service: from 1 * to 5 *. But this classification is different from accepted European standards. For example, a 5 * hotel may not have a hair dryer or shampoo, but a 3 * hotel has it all. Service also has its own differences. Everything is much simpler here. There are many campsites on the coastline.

The best hotels of Morocco 5 *

Almost all hotels are located on the seashore or ocean. High-quality service and national hospitality provide the most unforgettable vacation. All-inclusive Morocco hotels have all the conditions for holding business meetings and conferences. For this purpose, special meeting rooms are equipped, and you can also order the services of an interpreter or secretary. Therefore, there is no concern for organizing business meetings. Having booked a room in a hotel of the capital itself, you can enjoy a unique view of the sights of the city. In addition, you can use the services of various entertainment and sports programs: massage, spa, jacuzzi, hammam, tennis courts, fitness rooms, swimming pool, minigolf, as well as all kinds of excursions. Very diverse and exciting can be a vacation in Morocco. Hotels that have taken the lead in the ranking of holidaymakers' reviews will be presented in this article.

Royal Altas 5 *

Royal Altas 5 * is a high-quality hotel with 335 rooms. The architecture of the building will leave a good impression. Everything is there for the most fastidious traveler. It has its own fenced beach with free umbrellas and sunbeds. For entertainment, there is a program for children and adults, as well as everything you need for sports, a spa. Having booked a room in this hotel, wealthy guests will be able to get real pleasure from relaxation and excellent service.

Atlantic Palace Agadir Golf Thalasso Casino Resort

Located on the beachfront of Agadir Beach, this hotel is close to Souk El Had of Agadir. This small four-story hotel complex provides luxurious Art Nouveau rooms. Built in 2000, the hotel was renovated in 2011. The city center is within walking distance from the complex. Near it are surrounded by beautiful parks and gardens. Agadir Almassira Airport is a 25-minute drive away. The rooms of the hotel complex have free Wi-Fi, air conditioning, a balcony for contemplating the surrounding beauty. In addition, windows overlooking the sea. Also, guests can enjoy private huge bathtubs with showers, a bidet and fresh bathrobes. An in-room dishwasher and coffee maker are also provided. The hotel has a safe, ATM, hairdresser. The restaurants offer European cuisine and delicious, refreshing drinks. It is unlikely that anyone can refuse the services of a spa, Ayurvedic therapy, massage. Relax in the jacuzzi, sauna or just maintain your health and shape by visiting the gym, water aerobics classes. There is also the opportunity to ride a mountain bike, windsurf and sail.

Baech Albatros Agadir 4 *

The hotel is near a gorgeous long sandy beach. But at the same time, the city center is at hand, there you can see unique historical cultural monuments. Guests are accommodated in bungalows or in comfortable rooms of a 6-story building. There is a pool for children and adults, a beautiful garden, places for tennis. Gym, pool, spa - this is not the whole list of what they can offer for guests. In addition, there are animators with interesting programs. And in the restaurants of the Baech Albatros Agadir 4 * hotel you can taste delicious Mediterranean cuisine.

Hotel Sofitel Agadir Royal Bay Resort 5 *

The hotel is located on the ocean with a wide coastline. From the center the hotel is a five minute walk. The architecture of the hotel resembles a fortress decorated with luxurious decorations. The hotel complex is distinguished by sophistication and style. There is a nightclub, conference room, business center, car parking, shops, car rental and currency exchange. Each room has a telephone, satellite TV (including the Russian channel), a refrigerator, the ability to use the Internet, a huge bathroom, climate control equipment, a safe, and a hairdryer. Guests can use the huge pool, places for beach volleyball, football, gymnastics, water aerobics. There are also two golf courses, a water sports station, you can organize horseback riding, camel riding, quad biking, boat trips on a yacht and, of course, fishing. In the evening, there are various events, discos, casinos. The presence of bars and restaurants contributes to the complete harmony of relaxation. Having looked at the spa center, you can order the following services: massage in the salon or near the pool, clay wrap, various aesthetic procedures. You can also look into the sauna, hammam, participate in a tea ceremony. Fenced sandy beach with sunbeds and umbrellas. BB means that the price includes breakfast buffet. Lunch and dinner are available at an additional cost in the restaurant.

Restaurants and bars:

Comments vacationers

Reviews for Morocco hotels are mostly positive. People like exotic and oriental flavor. They come to Morocco with pleasure with the sea, sun and sweets. The hotels have excellent service and delicious Moroccan food. Most hotel complexes have their own beach, many offer interesting excursions and wonderful leisure. Travelers definitely recommend that Morocco be included in the list of countries to visit. Impressions from the trip will be remembered for a long time.
