How to crochet children's dresses

Of all the variety of clothes created with your own hands, it is perhaps most interesting to crochet children's dresses. The first is fast. Even a novice craftswoman will need no more than a week to create a work of design art, the likes of which cannot be found in stores. Secondly, light summer and warm winter knitted dresses always look in a special elegant way and distinguish their owner from other girls.

Crocheted children's dresses can be knitted from individual elements. Choose a theme and create a real picture: a meadow with flowers and butterflies, the seabed with the inhabitants of the depths, the sky with clouds and the sun, etc. Then sketch the layout of the elements on a paper pattern and start their production: a bright terry flower knits no more than half an hour, the same amount of time will be spent on a butterfly or fish, a bush of algae or a starfish. Put the finished elements on the pattern and attach them with pins in those places where they will look best on the finished product. When all the space is occupied by figures, begin to fasten them together.

The easiest way to sew. At the end, grind the side and shoulder seams and go along the contour of all the armholes with one or two rows of single crochet posts. Secondly, playing with the choice of texture and coloring of the fabric, you can make sarafanas completely different in mood, being able to knit only one and only kind of top.
