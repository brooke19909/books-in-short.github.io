Monkey pillow. DIY pillow for the New Year: patterns

If you like to do needlework, and now prepare for the holiday and are looking for ideas for an unusual gift, make a functional decoration of the interior. For example, a DIY pillow monkey can be made in several ways: very simple and more complicated. Follow the recommendations and try to make an original gift yourself.





DIY fabric pillow

Any pillow can combine a functional purpose and be an element of decor. There are several options for making such a souvenir:

  • A square or rectangular pillowcase is decorated with applique or embroidery in the form of an animal (realistic or cartoon).




  • A product that repeats the shape of the head with a flat or volumetric muzzle and separately sewn ears.
  • Also, a do-it-yourself monkey pillow can be made in the form of an entire animal figure with paws and tail.

As you can see, there are several options. The former is the simplest, the latter will require more time and experience.

Tools and materials

In order for you to get a beautiful monkey pillow, you need to cook all the required materials with your own hands. You will need the following:

  • Pattern or pattern of the product.
  • Paper for patterns, pencil, eraser.
  • Scissors.
  • Fabric of suitable quality and color (faux fur, fleece, plush).
  • Pins
  • Tailor's chalk.
  • Filler (synthetic winterizer, holofiber).
  • Threads with a needle.
  • Sewing machine.
  • Additional details (plastic eyes, nose, satin ribbons, buttons, fabric for making clothes).

In a word, you need everything that is required for sewing any voluminous decorative product.



Work technology

Any DIY pillow-cushion for the New Year or another holiday is performed according to a similar technique, only the number of manufactured parts will differ, and the complexity of the work will increase. The sequence for creating the product is as follows:

  1. Find a finished pattern or build the parts yourself on graph paper or thin paper.
  2. Cut out the details.
  3. Lay the elements on the wrong side of the fabric and pin them with pins.
  4. Circle the contours with chalk.
  5. Make allowances for the seams, if they are not provided for in the pattern.
  6. Cut out the details.
  7. Fold the paired items with the sides inward.
  8. Fasten them with pins.
  9. Sweep and then sew on a typewriter along the seam line, leaving a hole for turning out (do not close the seam in the ring).
  10. Fasten the threads and twist the workpiece.
  11. Fill with a padding polyester or holofiber.
  12. Gently sew in the hole manually.
  13. If the pillow consists of several parts (separately made ears, tail, muzzle elements are provided), perform all parts using the same technology. It is better to leave openings for filling the workpieces at or near the joints of the parts to make the manual seam less noticeable.
  14. Sew or glue plastic eyes. If there are no ready-made ones, you can embroider them.
  15. If desired, decorate the monkey with additional details, for example, a bright bow.

How to make a pattern

So, you have probably already decided what your monkey pillow will be . With your own hands for the New Year, you can sew such a souvenir both according to the finished template, and according to your own design. The first way is done as follows:

  1. Choose a suitable workpiece.
  2. Print it on the right scale. If the sheet size is not enough for the whole part, print on several and glue it.
  3. Cut out all the elements and use for work.

If the printer is not at hand, and the pattern is urgently needed, you can redraw it from the screen, and in the case when the shape of the parts is complex or several, transfer the contours to glass or transparent plastic with a marker, and then to paper.

If the creative abilities in you are well developed and you want to create an exclusive thing, draw what your pillow will look like, and then draw all the details.

How to sew a simple pillow

The lightest (for beginners) DIY pillow-monkey can be made of fleece. This material is pleasant to the touch, soft, and most importantly - it allows you to sew parts even on the front side, as it does not crumble. Felt is also good.



Work like this:

  1. Choose a simple pattern of the product, which will consist of two parts (squares, rectangles, circles, heads with ears).
  2. Cut both parts according to technology.
  3. Fold the elements with their faces outward and sew them with a decorative seam manually or on a typewriter. Before closing the seam, fill the product with synthetic winterizer.
