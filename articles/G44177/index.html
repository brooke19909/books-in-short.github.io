Hotels of Feodosia: prices, reviews. Private hotels in Feodosia

Feodosia is a resort city located on the southeastern coast of Crimea. Here the climate is a little cooler than in the same Yalta, while it is drier and sunny, which makes Theodosius a great place for a summer vacation. The history of the city has more than 2500 years. It is rich in the sharpest turns: initially the city was founded by the Greeks, then it was owned by the Ottoman Turks, Alans, Khazars, Genoese and Tatars. With the most recent, the city was called Kefe. In the XVIII century, he entered the Russian Empire, after which he received his current name, meaning "given by God."



During the Great Patriotic War in Feodosia, many architectural monuments were completely destroyed, although today there is something to see in this place. Of course, this is the Genoese fortress of the XIV century, the fountain "Good Genius" in the Jubilee Park, Pushkin's Grotto, a monument to the marine painter and the Aivazovsky Art Gallery, as well as the Alexander Green Museum . Attention, as in every coastal city, deserves a promenade with souvenir shops and cafes. In this place it is especially good to walk in quiet evenings to all those who have decided that Feodosia is suitable for relaxation.

Hotels (prices for accommodation here are low by Russian standards) are ready to receive guests throughout the year. Experienced tourists recommend the hotel "Scarlet Sails" in the center, and "Theodosius" 150 meters from the promenade. In this article we will consider the most popular resort hotels.
