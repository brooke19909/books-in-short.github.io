Specifications

First, let's study the main characteristics of the device. The phone in question has:

- communication modules operating in the GSM, GPRS, EDGE, 3G standard;

- micro-USB connector for connecting other devices;

- Built-in flash memory of 8 GB;

- a connector for connecting audio devices 3.5 mm;

- Android operating system in version 4.4.2;

- GPS navigator;

- battery capacity of 2 thousand mAh;

- housing with a height of 139.8 mm, a width of 69.8 mm, and a thickness of 9.9 mm.
