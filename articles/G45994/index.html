Rwanda hotels and various ways to have a great vacation in Africa

Rwanda is a country in eastern Africa that attracts tourists more and more every year.

I must say that the hotels of Rwanda are not as numerous as we would like. However, each of the tourists will be able to stay in a comfortable room and have a good vacation. Rwanda hotels can provide both modest but comfortable accommodation and luxury apartments.

Below we will talk about some places where you can check in upon arrival.

The most expensive and chic is Kigali Serena Hotel. This is a five-star hotel, which means there are not only beautifully decorated rooms, but also the corresponding service.

Kigali Serena Hotel is located near the Kigali Memorial Center. The rooms are nicely furnished. Guests will enjoy a beautiful interior, comfortable beds, comfortable showers and bathrooms with free toiletries, a bathroom, air conditioning. In addition, there are minibars and convenient reliable safes.

Hotel guests have the opportunity to use the telephone located in each room. For an additional fee, you can access the Internet. But breakfast is free here. Among other things, the hotel can relax in one of the bars, dine in the good restaurant and swim in the spacious pool.



You can book a hotel in advance: many travel agencies and sites provide this opportunity today.

The 4-star Hotel des Mille Collines also allows guests to relax in luxurious and very spacious rooms. It is located in the center of Kigali. The hotel offers stunning views of the countryside. Each room has very comfortable bathrooms and showers, a bathroom, air conditioning, a minibar, a safe, and a modern flat-screen TV.

You can relax here in the outdoor pool. In the evening it will be nice to relax in the bars. And if you want to taste delicious dishes, the restaurant on site will provide you with such an opportunity. The cost of living here is from 50 y. e. per day.



Other Rwanda hotels primarily provide 2 or 3 star service.

All tourists need to know: it is better to take care of check-in in advance, since it may happen that all rooms are already occupied by the time you arrive. Fortunately, booking hotels around the world is available today. This allows you to make the rest as comfortable as possible.

If during your vacation you want to completely immerse yourself in the atmosphere of this African country, then it is not necessary to check into hotels in Rwanda. All tourists have the opportunity to live in lodges - special wooden houses. They are grouped into camps and are located in protected areas.

And for those who want to get as close as possible not only to nature, but also to local residents, a good option would be to check into a house to one of the local families. You will live in separate rooms, but you will be able to chat with the natives.

As you can see, there are many options. Rwanda is a great place for those who do not like standard and in many respects boring holidays in luxury hotels in popular resorts.
