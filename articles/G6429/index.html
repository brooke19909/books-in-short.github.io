Types of Inkjet Printer Paper

How much time you can spend on choosing an inkjet printer is unknown. Among all the variety, one can watch for hours the appearance of new models.

But with the choice of paper for an inkjet printer, you have to fool, because we have unusual equipment. He has his own requirements for the paints used, and for the right material.

Device

Let's start with the printer itself. String is a relatively "experienced" type of device. It has a low print speed relative to the laser, but it effectively conveys picture quality, halftone, saturation and color.



Before you select paper for printing on an inkjet printer, you need to understand the principle of operation of this device. It was adopted from the matrix device. A picture on a sheet or any other medium is created thanks to the dots.

Previously, heads with needles were used for this, but now devices have acquired matrices with liquid dyes.

Sometimes cartridges with printheads are used, sometimes with a replaceable matrix. It all depends on the decision of the manufacturer and the design of the equipment.

Diversity

Inkjet paper can be very different. In addition to the material recommended by the manufacturer, the carrier can be varied. So, in addition to ordinary paper, there is watercolor, with and without coating, a luster film and for lighting, textiles, canvas and many others. other

Naturally, not all materials can be used in home devices. By doing such experiments, you can say goodbye to the printer forever. To understand what can and cannot be used, you need to know about the quality and behavior of drives during the printing process.



Composition

Inkjet paper consists of:

  • the base that forms the structure determines the mass and density;
  • glue, which is introduced into the middle of the fibers or applied on top to bind or collect particles;
  • the coating that most brands have and affects the quality of the picture, especially with photo printing.

Types

As you already understood, paper has many types, on which its use in a particular field depends. There is paper:

  • for inkjet printing;

  • art.

You can also highlight the types of coverage. It happens:

The properties

In addition to structure, any, even glossy inkjet paper, has its own properties. These include: size, color, weight and thickness.

Regarding the color, everything is also clear. Most often it is white. In this case, the black ink is not distorted. Different grades of paper have their own white tone. If the sheet is blue, then the picture will be cold, if with yellowness - dull.

Now let's take a closer look at each type of paper.

Top grade

This option can be used in inkjet and laser printers. It contains wood and cellulose fibers. Lignin is the glue that binds the fibers. This type of drive is usually impregnated with resin to increase its density.

Some do not recommend this type of inkjet paper. Solid ink devices may be an exception. Ink absorption may affect quality. The paper itself is quickly destroyed by tar glue, which oxidizes and hardens.

For inkjet printing

Such a drive is better than the previous one. This first-class paper received an outer coating. Therefore, its surface is ‚Äúpolished‚Äù, white and accepts ink well.



It is important to understand that regardless of the size of the paper for an inkjet printer, this type is used for letters and diagrams. As a photographic storage device, it can lose to other species.

Coated

Coated inkjet paper was the most sought after. The surface is specially adapted for certain inks and creating high-quality images. For coating, alumina, quartz, clay, polymers, etc. can be used.

The result may be matte or glossy A4 inkjet paper.

Art

This is paper that has been used by artists for decades. It can be printed using inkjet equipment, but it is better to use water-soluble ink.

Art paper is also different: rough, cold or hot pressing. This affects its surface. Such material is made from cellulose, which is obtained from wood or cotton.

The disadvantage of such paper is that it is not easy to use in an inkjet device. A lot of dust and foreign particles collect in the fibers, which can clog the ink supply mechanism. Also, the paper itself is a little uneven, so it can damage the print heads in the process.
