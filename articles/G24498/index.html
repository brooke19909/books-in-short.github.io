Egypt Airports - Sky Gate to the Land of the Pharaohs

If you want to touch the culture of ancient civilization, as well as relax on the beautiful beaches, then your option is Egypt. The airports of this country, in turn, are the gateway to the wonderful world. There are 10 international airports in the country of pharaohs. Going on a journey to the "cradle of human civilization", simply choose the nearest "heavenly harbor" to your destination.



Cairo

Cairo International Airport is the busiest airport in Egypt and the second most important in all of Africa. It consists of two terminals, between which buses run round the clock and every half hour. In Cairo itself there are a lot of attractions, and in the suburbs there are unique historical monuments - the pyramids.

Sharm El Sheikh

Another popular tourist destination is Egypt, Sharm El Sheikh. The airport serves the famous resort town of the Sinai Peninsula, which attracts tourists from all over the world due to its beaches. In addition, in this region for Russian travelers there is a visa-free regime.

Hurghada Airport

Egypt is an opportunity to relax in the unique resorts of the Red Sea, the best of which are in Hurghada. Hurghada International Airport is located just 5 kilometers from the city center.

Alexandria

This city is served by two airports. It is the international airport of Alexandria and Borg al-Arab. While the first is temporarily under reconstruction, the second serves all arrivals. If your travel destination is the country's largest seaport and the world famous resort, then buy tickets to Alexandria (Egypt). Airports will help you with this!

Other airports in Egypt

If you plan to visit Egypt, airports that are located in other parts of the country are always waiting for guests from all over the world.



  • El Arish - This international airport serves the resort town on the Mediterranean coast.
  • Aswan. Located 16 kilometers from the city - an important tourist center of the country.
  • Luxor is the main airport that serves the open-air museum city.
  • Mars Allam - the "heavenly haven" of international importance, was built in connection with the growing popularity of local resorts of the Red Sea.
  • Sohag - serves the city of the same name on the banks of the Nile, which is famous for its historical monuments and mosques.
  • Airport of St. Catherine. Located on the Sinai Peninsula and serves the city, which is full of historical and cultural attractions.
  • Taba is an international airport located on the border of Egypt and Israel. The resort city of the same name is the northernmost tourist city of the country of the pharaohs, which is also called the Riviera of the Red Sea.

Sandy beaches, clear sea, amazing history and unique sights - these are all Egypt. Airports of international importance are always waiting for their foreign guests. At the same time, less popular tourist centers of the country can be reached using local airports. Among them, the "heavenly gates" stand out, such as Ras Garib, Port Said, New Valley and others.
