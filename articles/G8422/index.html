Yarn hair for textile doll

Each craftswoman has her own secrets, but the principle of creating hair at the root is not different. So, we make the hair of the doll from yarn.



Method one

First, the head of a rag doll must be embroidered with the same threads, from which there will be ponytails or pigtails. The stitch after stitch fills the entire surface, starting from the extreme line of growth to the crown. After that, ‚Äúhairs‚Äù are implanted in a checkerboard pattern all over the head about half a centimeter apart.

Second way

Hair for a doll can be made in another way. The yarn is cut into segments equal to double the length of the hair, laid in the middle on a strip of paper tightly to each other and stitched on a sewing machine. After the base is removed, and the stitched locks are attached to the head. To make a hairstyle for a girl, you will need several such strips with threads that are placed evenly throughout the head. If you need to update the head of the doll-boy, then one strip with small hairs, which is placed from ear to ear through the crown, will be enough.



Restoration of a doll using yarn

If this is an ordinary Barbie doll whose hair has become unusable, then there are two ways to create a new hairstyle from yarn:

  • The head of the pupa is measured and cut from the fabric to the color of the skin to the size of a circle of fabric on which hairs are strung, tying a knot from the inside. Then this peculiar wig is glued with hot glue.
  • The second option is more painstaking, but it looks more aesthetically pleasing. To begin with, all old hair is pulled out, leaving traces of firmware clean. The next stage is filling with new strands. How to sew a doll's hair in the wake of an old hairstyle is not at all difficult to guess. To do this, the head is removed and, having threaded a strand into a needle, from the front side they enter it into one hole and bring it to the nearby one. At the same time, the desired length of curls from each point is left. After all the strands are pulled off, inside the head is poured with glue so that when combing the hairs do not fall out.

It is worth noting that thin silk or cotton knitting threads are best suited for Barbie.

Satin Ribbon Hair for Rag Doll

This material is one of the most affordable. And it is from him that it is easy to make interesting curls and curls. The next step is sewing or gluing blanks to the base of the head. How to sew doll hair from satin locks? Start work from the back of the head, gradually moving towards the temples and the crown.



Satin ribbons for restoration

Mouline thread hair for a rag doll

Replacing matted hair on a mouline thread

A doll whose hair is very tangled and unusable can find a second life. To do this, you need to make hair for the doll with your own hands. So, how to make hair for a doll with your own hands? First, all old hair needs to be removed by cleaning the holes. Felted wool hair

A material like wool is perfect for making hair for a doll. With your own hands, using this natural fiber, you can easily create fancy hairstyles. The big shark at the top with a small bright bow will look simply charming. Also, it is easy to create cute pigtails and cute ponytails from it. However, you should know a few basic rules about the principle of felting. To work, you need to stock up with wool, a special needle and a pencil for marking the hairstyle on the head. Better use a vanishing tailor marker. First draw a hairline and parting, after a small strand of material is folded, leaving one edge of 1 cm and the other of the desired length. They put it with a fold to the head and begin to work with a needle, driving wool in the middle. For the second side, do a similar procedure. In the same way drive all the details of the hairstyle. Further from them you can make a big beautiful bunch or braid a braid. How to make a doll hair from wool with curls? There is nothing complicated here, as it might seem at first glance. For beautiful curls, strands of wool are wound on toothpicks and left for some time so that they are fixed. After a couple of hours, you can untwist them and give the hairstyle the desired shape.

Despite the lightness of this method of creating hair, unfortunately, it is not suitable for a play doll, because they cannot be combed and woven.



Other options
