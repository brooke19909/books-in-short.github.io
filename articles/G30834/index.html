Hotel "October" (Pskov): address, photo, reviews

Pskov land is a land with an incredible history and unique culture.

City Information

Pskov is an ancient Russian city, which is considered one of the most beautiful in our country. It is located in the North-Western part of the Russian Federation, on the coast of two rivers: Pskov and Great. Pskov was first mentioned in the annals of 903, but archaeologists believe that the city was founded earlier: in 862. Locals still consider the famous Princess Princess Olga to be the patroness of the capital of the Pskov Region (the Tale of the Temporary Years mentions her).

Pskov has a rich history and a large number of incredible tales. During the Great Patriotic War, the capital of the Pskov region showed itself as a real hero: for this Pskov was awarded the honorary title - "City of Military Glory."

Hotel "Oktyabrskaya" (Pskov): guest reviews

Tourists staying at the Oktyabrskaya Hotel distinguish the hotel as a good location (in the historical part of the city, right on the main avenue of the city of Pskov), excellent furniture in the rooms and decent condition of bedding (bedding and towels), TVs in all rooms and satellite TV, new plumbing and inexpensive secure parking (only 120 rubles per day).

The disadvantages include a small room area and a small refrigerator: there is not much to put in such a refrigerator. However, all these shortcomings are covered by the fact that the hotel is a fifteen minute walk from the famous Pskov Kremlin. Near the hotel there are a large number of different restaurants, cafes and a not very large, but insanely beautiful park.

In addition, guests are noted by friendly, friendly staff, ready to listen carefully to any wishes and immediately fulfill any request. The hotel has a separate room with a washing machine: washing will cost the guest 250 rubles. There is also a place for drying clothes and an iron with ironing board. There are coolers on each floor, which is also very convenient.

Some negative reviews of guests about the hotel were related to the lack of hot water in the room (guests encountered such a problem on the 4th floor of the hotel), or to the presence of a slightly warm stream of water instead of hot water (such a problem arose on the 3rd floor). Guests also noted an unpleasant pungent smell of paint. However, such a problem arose only once.

Many tourists admire the small souvenir stall located directly in the lobby of the hotel, where you can buy various gifts for relatives, friends and even yourself at low prices. In general, guests respond positively about the hotel: clean, tidy, comfortable, without any frills. The hotel is quiet and peaceful: guests can relax and enjoy the silence both day and night.
