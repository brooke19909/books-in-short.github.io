Treating children

general information

Permits to this medical institution are provided both within the framework of the state assignment, and under contracts drawn up with legal entities and individuals. Built right on the seashore in Sochi, the Yunost sanatorium has been operating since 1982. He works year-round. It treats diseases of the musculoskeletal and nervous systems, diseases of the connective tissue, blood circulation, mastoid and ear.



The successful location of the sanatorium, two hundred meters from the beach, a unique combination of such natural factors as the abundance of green spaces, the sea and the sun, as well as clean air became the reason that children from all regions of Russia come here.

The treatment base, which has a sanatorium "Youth" (Sochi), consists of three pediatric departments and one medical and diagnostic. The staff employs the most experienced doctors, most of whom are highly qualified.

A clinical and biochemical laboratory operates in which modern equipment is installed. Children are examined in the office of ultrasound, functional diagnostics, and consultations are also carried out by specialists such as an orthopedist, neurologist and cardiologist. A psychologist works with children in the sanatorium, conducting both individual and group classes.

Accommodation

The sanatorium "Youth" (Sochi) accepts children from seven to fourteen years old. They are placed in four-, five- and six-seater chambers. Facilities are located on the floor.

Sanatorium rules

If the child is not accompanied by his parents, then they need to provide a notarized power of attorney confirming the authority of the person. According to the rules by which the Yunost sanatorium (Sochi) operates, admission is allowed up to five days late, but only for a good reason and with the provision of a document justifying it. The administration of the Yunost sanatorium reserves the right not to accept treatment if any of the required documents is missing or it is drawn up incorrectly, as well as if the child has contraindications for spa treatment.

Check in

Reception of children in the sanatorium "Youth" (Sochi) is carried out in the ward from eight in the morning. All the children who come for treatment are immediately examined by the doctors who work here and are immediately distributed among departments and units. The formation of the latter is carried out by age.





In the sanatorium there are junior, senior and middle groups. Each of them is assigned two educators who are constantly next to the children. In academic time, advisory lessons are organized here in three main subjects - mathematics, the Russian language and literature. Every day, with the exception of Sunday, two lessons are held for thirty-five minutes each.

Prices

To obtain permits that are provided from the federal budget, parents need to collect a package of required documents and submit them to the local health department.
