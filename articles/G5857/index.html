How interest is calculated on a Sberbank pension card: benefits, conditions and interest rate

Not so long ago, the majority of financial institutions of the Russian Federation replaced their social pension cards with a single payment system Mir. The most famous Sberbank in Russia was no exception. How interest is calculated on the pension card of this financial institution, we will describe in the publication.

What are cards?

Pension card - a plastic carrier issued by Sberbank of Russia. The product contains a bank logo, a black magnetic line at the back, a picture on the front side and a three-digit code on the other.

The only distinguishing feature of such plastic is the accession to a single domestic payment system. It is with her that Sberbank has been working recently. Are interest accrued on the retirement card? Of course yes.

Briefly about the reasons for the transition to a unified payment system

The main reason for the transition of most financial organizations to a single domestic payment system is a disruption in working with giants such as Visa and MasterCard.

The presence of regular failures is directly related to the inclusion of new sanctions. At the same time, dependence on such payment systems made customers of all ages nervous and worried about their savings or accumulations. Many at that time were interested in the question, how many percent are charged on the pension card of Sberbank? And most importantly, will it be possible later to withdraw the dividends earned?

In 2014, the domestic payment system Mir was launched. And the published law on the Russian payment system of a single sample forced all banks to connect to it. Sberbank did this in 2016, transferring all its customers to the domestic payment system Mir.

How many percent are credited to the card?

According to the developers of retirement plastic, getting money for it is very profitable. Indeed, the interest is accrued on the balance of a Sberbank pension card. According to representatives of a credit institution, 3.5% per annum is added to the residual amount.

It is noteworthy that interest is accrued only on the balance of the amount transferred by the state pension fund. If receipts from other sources go to your card, the amounts received in the bonus and accumulation systems do not participate.

On what conditions are interest calculated?

In order to have the opportunity to accumulate additional funds for a pensioner, certain conditions must be observed. Interest is accrued on the pension card of Sberbank, as the owners themselves say, subject to an open current account.

To do this, you must personally visit the nearest branch of Sberbank with a passport, SNILS and a certificate of the type and amount of pension, other municipal payments of the Pension Fund. This is one of the most important conditions.

The second important point is the presence on the account of money credited from the Pension Fund of the Russian Federation. Accordingly, the larger the amount, the more substantial your reward will be. The countdown for calculating interest is from the very first day that the card was opened. Interest is charged once per quarter. Consequently, the nearest interest rate for each new cardholder will be credited exactly three months later at the end of the quarter.

How to be retired with old cards?

All the above conditions apply to the holders of the new plastic, connected to the unified domestic payment system Mir. But how is interest accrued on the pension card of Maestro Sberbank?

According to the current rules of the bank, the transition from the old payment system to the new one takes place automatically. As soon as the card expires, a transition occurs. After transferring to a new payment system, all previous accumulations and amounts are transferred to the issued Mir pension card.
