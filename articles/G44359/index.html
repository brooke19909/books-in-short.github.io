Cyprus is an island nation in the middle of the Mediterranean Sea. The resort area is almost the entire coast of the island. Many tourists consider rest in Cyprus fabulous.

Cyprus

Cyprus is a mysterious island that has been the object of attention of various rulers at all times. Power on the island passed from hand to hand for a long period. All this has left its mark on traditions, architecture and historical values. Cyprus is reflected in legends and myths. It is on this island, according to ancient Greek legends, Aphrodite was born. In Cyprus there are baths of Aphrodite, in which she spent a lot of time. There, according to legend, the goddess indulged in love joys.





For many vacationers, Cyprus is an island of romance, rich history, beautiful architecture and great wine. Relaxing at the Jacaranda Hotel Apts Class B 3 *, you can feel all this. The location of the hotel in the resort town of Protaras allows you to spend time, not only lazily roasting in the sun. Anyone who wants to actively spend their holidays can independently or with a guide take an excursion to the sights of the beautiful islands of the Mediterranean.

Protaras

The resort town of Protaras was once a place for growing figs and was a sad sight from fig trees and dry shrubs. Now, thanks to the human factor - the work of people and their imagination, you can enjoy a comfortable vacation with a developed tourist infrastructure. The chic promenade with a promenade along the sea about 15 kilometers long turns evening walks into a kind of action: a trip to shops, cafes, admiring the picturesque scenery.





This area is famous for its pristine beauty and cleanliness. Protaras is an ideal holiday destination for lovers of peace, order and comfort. It is there that the best beaches are: the sandy coast and the clean coastal zone. The sea there is calm, transparent, warm. A cult resting place is the Bay of Fig Tree, a picturesque bay where there are no high waves, hustle and bustle.
