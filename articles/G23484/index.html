Rest in Moscow region: "Malibu" (beach, pools, entertainment center)

So the summer heat came to the territory of Russia. The soul is torn from a gassed metropolis to the bosom of nature, preferably to water. Do you still think that you can swim and sunbathe only in the southern resorts? You are mistaken! No less developed industry of outdoor recreation is in the suburbs.

If weather conditions permit, head to the Pirogovskoye reservoir. Malibu Beach is not just a strip of sand. This is a whole entertainment complex. Many call Malibu a resort. So it is, because there are three hotels, two of which are floating. And what is the recreation infrastructure in the Malibu resort near Moscow? How to get there? How much does it cost to stay there for the weekend? What other services does the administration of the Malibu hotel and entertainment complex provide? We will talk about all this in our article.



Where is the Malibu hotel and entertainment complex located

You don‚Äôt have to go for a long trip to bring to the sandy shore of the beach and cool the hot bodies in the waters of the Pirogovsky reservoir. This paradise is located just twenty one kilometers from the Moscow Ring Road. Turn off from the Moscow Ring Road to Ostashkovskoe highway. It stretches to the northeast.

After driving seven kilometers along the highway, you will reach an intersection in the village of Belyaninovo, at which you should turn right (along the main road). Next you will pass the settlements of Boltino and Pirogovo. After the last village you need to turn left and follow the signs for Sorokino. The road will lead you to the barrier of the MLO "Bay of Joy." Having passed it, you need to turn on the second turn to the right.

Entrance to the territory is free. From public transport, only the Rocket and Moscow motor ships from the capital's River Station go there. They are mooring at the marina of the Bay of Joy. And the Malibu beach is already two hundred meters from there.

The hotel and entertainment complex occupies the northern shore of the reservoir. His exact address: Moscow region, Mytyshchi district, the village of Sorokino. It is part of the municipal recreation area "Bay of Joy."



Malibu Beach in Pirogovo: Description

The hotel and entertainment complex operates year-round. But it is especially popular in the summer months, when you can swim and sunbathe. There are two wonderful sandy beaches and a two-story VIP zone located on pontoons. In addition, on the surface of the water there are several hookah houses that can be rented for recreation. VIP - ‚ÄúMalibu‚Äù - a beach, divided into two zones. One of them is called the Lounge. It houses a pool with a waterfall and a glass bar. The second zone is Nemo. It has three swimming pools (one of which is a children's pool) and a round bar.

The entrance fee includes the use of a sun lounger and an umbrella. All beaches have the necessary infrastructure: air-conditioned showers and toilets, changing cabins. On the shore are two life-size sculptures of elephants. This is an extra shower.

Many summer vacationers are in no hurry to leave the Malibu beach. Moscow is not so far away, but still many residents of the capital prefer to stay in one of the three hotels of the complex in order to catch the first rays of the sun and make their weekends more eventful. Malibu (beach): prices

The cost of admission depends on the season, days of the week and on the zone. All of June, as well as from August 16 to September on weekdays and weekends you can get to one of the sandy beaches for 500 rubles. "High season" in the suburbs lasts one and a half months - from July 1 to August 15. During this period, a ticket on weekdays costs 600 rubles. This price includes the use of sun loungers, beach infrastructure and attractions. Additionally, such visitors pay for towels (250 rubles). An entrance ticket to the VIP zone costs a thousand rubles in the low season and 1200 rubles in the high season. It should be said that for residents of any hotel in the complex use of the beaches is free.
