King Town Grand Hotel & Wedding Center 4 * (Vietnam, Nha Trang): description, service, reviews

Vietnam is a country full of unusual historical monuments and cultural objects. The objects of architecture that are built here have a unique style that is unusual for Russian tourists. That is why many visitors from the CIS and, of course, Russia come to the country.

Almost the most favorite tourist city in Vietnam is Nha Trang. There is not only something to see, but also several places where you can stay and spend a pleasant time. The rooms in local hotels are relatively inexpensive, and the service in them is very decent. One of these is called King Town Grand Hotel & Wedding Center 4 *.

general information

This place for rest has a certain peculiarity - it is often chosen for wedding ceremonies, and newlyweds often spend their honeymoon here. All this is due to the fact that it is built in a romantic style, light shades prevail in the interior, which adjust to the desired mood.

King Town Grand Hotel Wedding Center 4 * was built recently - in 2016. Its huge 19-story building has a modern look, it houses the entire hotel room fund. In a small area adjacent to the hotel, there are a number of entertainment and places to relax, some of which are built in the building of the building itself.





Location

The hotel is located near the central part of Nha Trang - five kilometers from it, almost on the coastline. Its territory is a private garden, which was created according to the best ideas of landscape design. Tourists who relax here have the opportunity to enjoy all the charms of plants that are planted here - they are all unusual for Russian tourists, as guests themselves note.

As for transport links, the nearest international airport is just over 30 km from King Town Grand Hotel & Wedding Center 4 * (Vietnam / Nha Trang) - absolutely all vacationers who immediately pick up a comfortable car with air conditioning sent by the hotel arrive here as part of the provision of a transfer service.

Rooms

In total, the hotel guests are offered a choice of 105 apartments, which are conditionally divided into five groups: Deluxe, Executive, Executive Suite, Family and Connection Room. All of them offer increased comfort conditions, provided with a beautiful interior, modern electronics, as well as high-quality furniture and beautiful views that can be seen from the windows.

All apartments without fail have a bathroom, which offers a bathing place, a bathroom, a washbasin, as well as several cabinets where personal belongings of guests are stored, as well as bathrobes, bath towels and slippers. The hotel provides each guest with a set of bath accessories for bathing. The bathroom also comes with a hairdryer.

Air-conditioning is installed in rooms of any class, with the help of which guests can struggle with the sometimes debilitating heat standing on the street - at the touch of a button you can create optimal climatic conditions.

When is the best time to relax in Vietnam?

The tourism season in this country traditionally opens in December, when snow revenge begins in Russia. Here at this time real summer begins: the air becomes more humid and the sun is hot. From this moment, housing prices and, accordingly, for tourist trips to the country begin to increase sharply. The holiday season ends here in April, during which the weather continues to be warm, but the air is already drier - this is how the hot season in Vietnam ends.

When is the best time to relax in the central part of the country? For this, a short period of time is allotted. In particular, a trip made between the end of April and June may be the best option - at this time, warm air flows still prevail in this region.
