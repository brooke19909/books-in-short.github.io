Shopping center "Marmalade" in Taganrog: a brief overview

The marmalade shopping center in Taganrog is the largest complex of this type in the whole city. In its stores there is a large number of entertainment for every taste and for visitors of all age categories. In addition, on the floors of the center there are many stores that present on their windows products manufactured by world famous brands.



general information

Shopping center "Marmalade" in Taganrog is a place that is perfect for family shopping and walking. Many of its visitors note that people with disabilities feel comfortable on the territory of the shopping and entertainment center, since each floor has a whole range of conditions designed for them: from comfortable escalators to cabins in toilets that are fully equipped for such a group visitors.

Location

The shopping center "Marmalade" is located in Taganrog, on the territory of Peace Square. You can enter this building from two sides: Chekhov Street or 16th Lane. Not far from here there is a public transport stop through which buses and minibuses passing from different parts of the city pass. Many guests of the complex get on such modes of transport.

As for the location of the shopping center, it is located in the middle of large residential areas that are populated by a large number of people - many of them make their purchases in the shops of the shopping center "Marmelad" (Taganrog). Opposite the building of the shopping center is a large city school No. 27.

Services

Visitors to the shopping center very often use the services that are provided at various points located on its territory. Among them, lockers are very popular, a point with which can be found on the ground floor of the building. In addition, near the entrance there is a number of ATMs where you can get cash from plastic cards, according to visitors, this is quite convenient.

On the floors of the shopping center there are two beauty salons, which are also very often visited by residents of Taganrog: Plastylin and TWIGGY. It offers a wide range of cosmetic hairdressers and other services.

On the floors of the mall there are catering establishments, which guests can visit at any time within the schedule of the "Marmalade". Among them, the Sakura restaurant, as well as the Oliva cafe, is widely known.





Entertainment

Guests of the mall often admit that they love Marmalade for the wonderful cheerful atmosphere prevailing in places for entertainment. There is a large billiard club, a huge playground with attractions, as well as clubs - including karaoke.

The most frequently visited place in the Taganrog marmalade shopping center is the Charlie movie theater. The schedule of sessions going in it can be specified on the poster located in its hall. Here guests are offered 7 rooms equipped with the latest technology.

Mode of operation

The shopping center works every day according to one schedule: from 10 a.m. to 10 p.m. However, some institutions have their own working hours. In particular, this applies to some cafes, restaurants, as well as a cinema. The SEC "Marmelad" (Taganrog) has a 24-hour security service that monitors the safety of visitors during the day and the safety of goods in stores at night.

You can easily get to its location by public transport - many citizens come by buses No. 31, 30, 58 or trolleybus No. 5. Address of the Marmalade shopping center: Taganrog, 7 Mira Square.
