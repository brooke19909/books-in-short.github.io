Hotel "Central", Izhevsk: reviews, prices, description

Izhevsk is a large center of republican significance. On the territory of the city there are many industrial and industrial associations that have gained federal fame: the industrial associations Izhmash, Izhstal, Machine - Building Plant, and the Metallurgical Plant for the production of high-quality steel. This is not only a large industrial center, the city accumulates a large number of cultural institutions.



Every year, the capital of Udmurtia is visited by a large number of guests. To solve business issues and get acquainted with the sights of the city it will be convenient to book rooms that the Central Hotel kindly offers its guests. Izhevsk from the windows of the hotel opens at its best - a panoramic view of the central square of the city.

Hotel Description

The AMAKS complex, the Tsentralnaya Hotel, personifies the developed leisure infrastructure. The institution is located on the Central square of the capital of the Udmurt Republic. This place is the center of the most important commercial and administrative resources. Literally within walking distance is the Government House of the Republic.

For those who come to solve business problems, the most convenient option would be to use the services provided by the Central Hotel, Izhevsk (address: 233 Pushkinskaya St.). The transfer from the city airport to the hotel takes less than half an hour (17 km). The train station is 7 kilometers from the hotel. The location is convenient in every sense (both accommodation and accessibility of transport hubs).
