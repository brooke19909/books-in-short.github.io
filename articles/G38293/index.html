Do not back down and do not give up! HTC: a lineup of new smartphones

HTC is a Taiwanese corporation founded in 1997. First, the company specialized in the production of laptops and personal computers, then in the manufacture and sale of communicators. Nowadays, HTC is one of the members of the Open Handset Alliance business community, a community of smartphone makers that are promoting the Android platform.





The crisis in the corporation

From 2008 to 2014, HTC held a good position in the global market for the sale of equipment and gadgets. The lineup was presented by various smartphones, which were popular with buyers. But in 2015, the company faced a number of financial problems. Rumors began to circulate that the company would soon go bankrupt. The press reported on the forecasts of analysts who claimed that in 2016 HTC would own about 1% of the global smartphone market.

However, in the same year, Sher Wong (HTC CEO) said that the Taiwanese corporation did not think to leave the arena of the world market. Moreover, the company management has a strategic plan to bring the company into the leading smartphone manufacturers. In 2016, the release of new HTC phone models was announced. The range of smartphones replenished with high-quality modern smartphones. They will compete with similar products of other companies.

In November 2016, it was announced that 3 new HTC smartphones were on sale. The lineup of phones released by the company fully meets the requirements for modern communication devices. Their names are: Desire 650, Desire 10 and 10 Evo. Below are detailed descriptions of the new HTC models.
