Tenerife hotels: a fairytale vacation

A high-quality vacation is a vacation in Tenerife, which offers its guests a magnificent resort life in a marvelous Mediterranean climate and European entertainment. There is everything that the soul of a whimsical tourist can only wish for: the sea and the sun, a cultural day program and a rich evening, turning into night life. You can spend several days on this extraordinary island, visiting flamenco shows, numerous casinos and exotic shows, or you can spend a relaxing vacation as Tenerife hotels offer - relaxed, lazy and with pleasure.

An excellent vacation is guaranteed by visitors from different countries at the Sol Tenerife 4 * hotel, located on the coast, a few meters from the cleanest and most well-kept beach. From here you can start the journey around the island - stop, go for a walk on the sea boulevard, and then go to the resort and entertainment area, since it starts right next to the hotel. Gaming lounges and discos, restaurants and bars - this is what awaits the guests of this hotel. Perhaps, only pet owners who are accustomed to rest with their pets will be dissatisfied: this animal, as well as many other Tenerife hotels, is strictly prohibited.

For those who do not like to be in a crowd and appreciate a quiet seclusion, the H10 Las Palmeras 4 * hotel is perfect. Here you can spend your vacation by the large pools, play tennis or billiards, and also take unique walks in the huge subtropical park, which occupies a huge territory around the hotel (more than 14,000 m2).

Many Tenerife hotels offer visitors services such as babysitting, which allows parents to devote some time to themselves. In addition, there is an excellent evening program for adults and a well-equipped playground. In general, there is everything you need for the common relaxation of parents and children.
