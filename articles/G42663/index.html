Svetlana Shchelokova: biography

This article will tell about the life of Svetlana Shchelokova, the wife of the Minister of the Interior, who was accused of abuse of power. The couple could not stand the shame that fell upon them and the deprivation of all privileges. Shchelokova loved to live in luxury, this ruined her and the minister.

Biography of Svetlana Vladimirovna Shchelokova

Shchelokova, nee Popova, was born in the city of Krasnodar in a poor working family. Svetlana Vladimirovna Shchelokova's date of birth is February 2, 1927. Svetlana met her future husband during the Great Patriotic War.

Shchelokova was a field nurse. Both were front-line soldiers. In 1944, they officially got married.

Svetlana was an otolaryngologist by training, and also taught various disciplines at a medical institute. She was by nature a responsive and very sociable person. And to this day, former classmates at the Kiev Medical Institute warmly remember her. In that difficult post-war period, Svetlana Popova, the daughter of a modest Krasnodar worker, was the soul of the course.

Having married the young deputy minister of industry of the Ukrainian SSR, also a front-line soldier Shchelokov, at first she was even confused. Having drowned in difficulties in childhood, and at the front, and in the hungry school years, she did not immediately accept her high status as a "high-ranking" wife.

And before graduating, she fed her classmates with wonderful dishes that she could get by pulling. Her husband, who soon became the head of the industry department of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Ukraine, who loved his young wife crazy, was not against it. But then, in those young years, she could not dream of that unimaginable luxury that would eventually fall upon her.

The couple were pleased with their family life. Shchelokova was very worried when her husband was transferred to a high position in Moscow from his native city of Chisinau (Moldova), where he held a high state post. This was a turning point in the biography of Svetlana Shchelokova.

Friendship and business with Galina Brezhneva

The biography of the wife of Minister Svetlana Shchelokova is also significant for her acquaintance and close communication with the daughter of the Secretary General.

Svetlana Shchelokova was very close friends with Galina Brezhneva. They not only hunted together for the best diamonds, but also came up with a very profitable business. From their sources, they learned about the upcoming increase in jewelry prices, and could buy them in advance in jewelry stores, then sell them at a higher cost. Under Brezhnev, prices for gold jewelry and jewelry with precious stones increased regularly. Svetlana Vladimirovna Shchelokova and Galina Leonidovna Brezhneva always managed to get whole lots of jewelry to increase the cost, so that it would be profitable to resell them later. A possible raise in prices could be reported by one of the influential officials. The business turnover of a diamond business woman was hundreds of thousands of rubles. These two women were untouchable, law enforcement agencies could not even get close to them.





Acquaintance with Vishnevskaya and Rastropovich

In the late 60s, the Shchelokovs got acquainted with the famous married couple - singer, soloist of the Bolshoi Theater Galina Vishnevskaya and famous musician Mstislav Rostropovich. Then they were one of the most respected artists. They were watched by the Soviet and foreign public. Friendship with them, of course, flattered yesterday's provincials.

Soon, the Shchelokovs became an influential couple that belonged to the highest Soviet caste. And the famous artists, on the contrary, entered into an acute conflict with the authorities, old friends and colleagues moved away from them, their further fate is unknown and alarming. However, the Shchelokovs did not stop communicating closely with Vishnevskaya and Rostropovich without concealing these relations. Vishnevskaya to this day believes that Svetlana was eliminated as an extra witness.

Crash of Shchelokov

On November 10, 1982, Brezhnev died. All the compromising evidence on Shchelokov was long ready. Andropov receives the post of General Secretary, and nothing prevents the destruction of the enemy. A month after the death of Brezhnev, he was relieved of his post as minister. Every day there was interrogation after interrogation; his life became unbearable. The life of the Shchelokovs went downhill. The former minister understands that the worst may be ahead - the Soviet prison.

Receptions in the country, trips to guests and restaurants and the Bolshoi Theater are a thing of the past. They could no longer appear in society. All of Moscow knew that the ousted interior minister was suspected of abuse.

On November 6, 1984, without trial, Shchelokov was stripped of the rank of general. On November 10, on Police Day, he was also deprived of the title of the hero of social labor, and then they called and offered to voluntarily surrender all front-line orders and medals. Shchelokov invited them to take everything themselves. On November 12, they searched him with a search and seized 124 paintings worthy of the Tretyakov Gallery. Money and jewelry were also discovered. Investigators noted that crystal chandeliers were even in the bathroom and toilet. Shchelokov was confused and answered all questions that he did not know where all this came from.

In December 1984, millions of Soviet citizens were told in the newspapers that the Minister of Internal Affairs of the USSR Nikolai Shchelokov was deprived of the rank of army general, although he had held this position for sixteen years.

It was an irreparable blow to him. Then others followed: expulsion from the Central Committee, expulsion from the party. The daughter and son of the disgraced spouses Shchelokovs were kicked out of work, for a long time they could not get a new place.

Death of the minister

A year later, the minister himself decided to commit suicide. On December 10, 1984, he wrote a letter to Konstantin Chernenko. He asked me not to allow slander against him and apologized in every way. On December 12, he visited relatives and friends. On December 13, he put on his full uniform, drank coffee with cognac and shot himself in the head.

He realized after the call that soon they would come to seize orders and medals, and then there would be an arrest. The minister was already powerless to change the course of affairs. The operatives who arrived at the place of suicide found a suicide note of the once powerful minister, where it was written that the order was not removed from the dead. His relatives and friends believe that the Shchelokovs suicide is the result of systematic bullying.

Rumors of the murder of Andropov

The case was gaining momentum; rumors circulating throughout Moscow were rumors that an attempt had been made on Yuri Andropov. Allegedly, he was shot in the stomach and he died on the spot. This was reported by the cleaner of the famous house number 26 on Kutuzovsky Prospekt, where all the main people of the country lived. Raisa Starostina testified at the police station and told that she saw with her own eyes how the wife of the Minister of Internal Affairs Svetlana Shchelokova tried to kill Yuri Andropov. Allegedly, avenging her husband, she was face to face in an elevator with Andropov and shot him. The hearing was not confirmed later. People vigorously discussed the death of the wife of the former Minister of Internal Affairs, someone gloated, someone empathized.
