Display

The screen, unfortunately, is not the strongest side of the Alcatel 918D. This model uses a TFT matrix that is not of high quality. The display size reached only 3.2 inches (takes up about half the space). The capacitive screen of the smartphone has a resolution of 480 by 320 dpi (PPI value is 180), which, of course, affects the quality of the picture. It turns out grainy, pixels are easily noticeable even at a great distance, breaking the image into pieces.

The color rendition and display saturation suffer, the image is faded, unrealistic, dull. There is also not enough margin of brightness: even on a sunny day (not to mention the direct hit of light), the display fades. No glare protection, which clogs the last nail in the coffin of the Alcatel 918D display. The touchscreen, being capacitive, distinguishes touches quite clearly when it comes to large display elements, but when typing on the keyboard, the touch panel is poorly calibrated.
