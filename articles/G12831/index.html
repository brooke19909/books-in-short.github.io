How to make Christmas trees for the New Year with your own hands?

You need to develop a child in all possible ways. And various crafts from improvised materials are an excellent way out of the situation. So what can be done for the upcoming holiday? Various homemade Christmas trees!





Cardboard

If you want to make Christmas trees on your own with your own hands, you can use material such as cardboard for this. There are already opportunities for action - a huge amount. The easiest way - to make a cone out of cardboard - this will be the tree itself. The edges can be fixed with glue or a stapler, so for sure. Now the forest beauty needs only to be decorated. To do this, you need colored paper from which toys will be cut and pasted onto the Christmas tree. You can use the rain, which also decorates the tree.

Kitchen

You can climb into the kitchen and there look for material for making Christmas trees for the New Year with your own hands. What might be needed? Pasta! You need to take spaghetti, break them into equal pieces and stick to a pre-cooked cone from the same cardboard at a certain angle. Further, the product can be painted with a spray can in green. Decorations for such a Christmas tree can also be pasta - shell pasta or bow-pasta are suitable. In the kitchen, you can also take disposable forks, from which, according to the same principle (by gluing to a finished cone), you can make a very original Christmas tree. And from plastic cups you can make an unusual snowman (the cups are glued bottom to the already prepared ball).





the cloth

There may also be fabric Christmas trees sewn with their own hands . To do this, you will need a green fabric for the forest beauty, threads and a needle (preferably a sewing machine). A Christmas tree can be in the form of a cone, in the form of three (or more) parts that lie on a stick-base ... There are actually a huge number of options, you just need to find the right one for yourself. You can also make a Christmas tree, which is attached to a specific frame. It will turn out a voluminous postcard.

Felt

Modern needlewomen have already made great friends with material such as felt. Therefore, it is quite easy to prepare a felt Christmas tree ornaments sewn with their own hands. It can be in the form of a Christmas toy - a small one, or maybe an ordinary Christmas tree. To do this, you can glue felt shreds onto the base (cone) - one after the other. And so another interesting Christmas tree will be ready. The decoration of such a beauty is a personal matter. But it is worth noting that even without toys, the felt Christmas tree looks very elegant.





Knitting

People who are friends with knitting needles can knit the Christmas tree. This is pretty easy to do. Such a Christmas tree is decorated with beads or beads - depending on the size of the finished product.

Beads

There is also a more laborious way to create a Christmas tree for your New Year with your own hands - it can be woven from beads. However, this is a rather time-consuming process that requires, among other things, special skills.

Feathers

Another option how to make a Christmas tree with your own hands: use feathers for this. They need to be glued to a pre-prepared base (again, best of all - to cardboard). You can decorate it at will, because in itself it looks great.
