USB lighter: overview, specifications, description, types and reviews

USB-lighter is a modern accessory designed not only for smokers, but also for people who like outdoor activities. The gadget is a new class of electronic devices that have several advantages.

Features of USB Cigarette Lighter

Electronic gadgets work on the principle of a conventional heating element, because they receive alternating current from the battery. For conservative people, lighters are designed that look similar to ordinary products. In the market you can find modern options with an original design. Such a gadget does not require additional liquid refueling, as it receives power from the computer. This is much more convenient than resorting to the classic refueling method. Therefore, there are many grateful reviews from real buyers who appreciated the merits of this product.

The product will never go out from the wind, so a lighter with usb charging can be safely used in all weather conditions. A fully charged device allows you to make about 100-150 lighting. By purchasing a quality model, you can save a personal budget. Smokers do not have to spend money on standard products and their constant refueling.

Popular models of electronic lighters

Products differ from each other in material quality and price. Many budget models work for a long time and quite well:

  1. Tesla Coil Lighters. The electronic device is powered by a special battery, which operates on the principle of an electric arc. The product is presented in a variety of colors.
  2. USB Lighter - SPPARX Arc Lighter. The product has a double arc, which allows you to quickly light a cigarette. Users can choose black, white, gold or silver model.
  3. BOLT Lighter. One of the most budgetary options, because the product is made of cheap material. The device allows you to light about 200 cigarettes. The product is suitable for those people who do not believe that an electronic lighter with usb is part of the image.
  4. Mantello Coil Lighters. The device is made in the style of minimalism, so there are no unnecessary design solutions. A lighter is perfect for people who value practicality and simplicity.
  5. OBON Mini Portble. The product is a futuristic cigarette lighter, because it has an unusual shape. The device is powered by a filament, which provides the process of lighting. The device allows you to set fire only cigarettes.
  6. Carteret Collections Flameless. The device works from an incandescent filament, therefore it is aimed at those users who wish to purchase an ordinary product, but without a cigarette lighter.
  7. Tiger usb lighter. The gadget runs on a small battery. By pressing a button, the device can be activated. An electric discharge quickly occurs between contacts that are energized. Customer reviews say that the device looks like a small stun gun. The lighter can be used for lighting, burning paper and other flammable materials. These lighters combine superior quality and great design. Tiger products are premium.

Potential buyers should be aware that there are many types of models of lighters, among which there are high-quality samples and not very safe models of low quality.

Specifications

The USB lighter works great in all weather conditions. The gadget is convenient to use both in everyday life, and on a hike, fishing or hunting. The products are absolutely safe, since they charge from USB, do not require additional refueling with gasoline or gas. The average battery power is 200 mAh, and the voltage is about 3.7 V.

Gadget benefits

The device has many advantages, among which are the following:

  • high degree of reliability;
  • long term of operation;
  • excellent fire safety;
  • convenient use in any weather;
  • easy care;
  • profitability;
  • stylish design;
  • low price category.

The best quality electronic lighters with USB charging have a rhodium or palladium coating, which is highly resistant to any mechanical damage.

Potential customers are advised to consider the appearance, size, shape and type of product that should best meet their needs. Included with the products are: mini- USB, battery and manual. An extensive lineup includes gadgets made in the form of watches and other products. Some lighters are equipped with more advanced functionality - flash drives, flashlights, openers. Praise about this device speaks not only about the versatility of the product, but also the ability to place it in your pocket or small bag. This is especially important if the owner of the gadget is going to relax in nature. In the reviews, buyers note that a lighter can be a wonderful gift that will come in handy in everyday life.

Operating principle

The product is charging from a mobile device or USB port. The presence of a computer in each house makes it convenient to use electrical energy to charge the gadget.

The product works like a lighter with a piezoelectric element, so when you press the button, the voltage of the electronic circuit increases. It takes thirty minutes to one hour to fully recharge. Customer reviews suggest that different models require an individual recharging mode.

How to choose the right one?

Potential buyers may be confused when choosing an electronic device. Among the many original, bright and stylish gadgets it is difficult to make the right choice. When buying, you should pay attention not only to the attractive appearance, but also to the technical component.

It is important to consider the expected intensity of use of the electronic lighter, type, size of the device, workmanship, weight, completeness and the ability to replace parts. Compliance with recommendations for the proper use of a lighter will allow the device to last a long period of time. Reviews of electronic gadgets will make the right choice.
