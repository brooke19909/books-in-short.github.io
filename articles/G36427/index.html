Zheleznovodsk hotels: list, description, reviews

Zheleznovodsk hotels receive thousands of tourists from all over the world year-round. Why? The climate of these places is reminiscent of the middle Alps and is favorable for improving health. Near the city is the only forest park in the area, surrounded by mountains. The entire economy of Zheleznovodsk is focused on servicing the tourist infrastructure, which includes numerous motels, boarding houses and hotels.





List of the best hotels, pensions and motels of the city

  1. Hotel "Pearl of the Caucasus".
  2. Apart-hotel "Slavyanovsky source".
  3. Sanatorium Plaza".

Each establishment is described in more detail below.

The best hotel of the city

Hotel "Pearl of the Caucasus" is one of the most famous in the city. The number of rooms is represented by apartments of various categories (standard, junior suite, suite). Single and double rooms are decorated in pastel colors. Each of them has its own unique design. The apartments are equipped with furniture, air conditioning, modern appliances. Each room has a bathroom equipped with modern fixtures. Free toiletries are provided. Vacationers have the opportunity to use the services of wireless Internet access. The hotel is designed for simultaneous accommodation of ninety-seven people.

"Pearl of the Caucasus" - all that is needed for a good rest



Dining is organized in the cafe-bar of the hotel on the basis of the buffet. Guests are offered a varied menu, which includes dishes of European and Caucasian cuisines. Cafe-bar is not only a place of eating, but also a center for communication of guests, making new acquaintances. A pleasant, soft-sounding music contributes to creating a special atmosphere. Sitting at the table and indulging in a pleasant conversation, you can relieve emotional stress. During the day and in the evening, you can have a good time sitting on one of the many sun loungers on the hotel terrace. For vacationers, entertainment events are held: discos, competitions, sports competitions.

Business and leisure





Zheleznovodsk hotels can offer their guests a wide range of services. The hotel has all the conditions not only for relaxation, but also for all kinds of celebrations and corporate events. The conference room can accommodate forty people and is equipped with the necessary modern technical equipment. Various accommodation options for event participants contribute to the optimal use of the conference room area. At the request of customers during the break, coffee or tea with sandwiches can be offered to participants in the events. Also for a fee, the service is provided by waiters. For corporate clients, office equipment and communications are provided.

The hotel is an ideal venue for celebrations and banquets. The presence of modern audio and video equipment allows you to hold any event at a high level.

The city of Zheleznovodsk is quite beautiful. Having visited him on a business visit, you should not deny yourself the pleasure of walking along the picturesque streets. But before that, you can have a great time without even leaving the hotel.

Other services

The hotel has a Health and Beauty Center, using the services of which you can transform your appearance and get a boost of energy. The center employs qualified employees with specialized education and extensive experience. For guests working sauna and solarium. There is a playground for children and a games room.

Not far from the hotel, just a few minutes away are the famous mineral springs and the Pushkin Gallery, which the whole city of Zheleznovodsk is famous for.

Numerous positive reviews of the hotel guests indicate a highly qualified staff and a decent level of service. The rooms are cleaned at an incredible speed, they are always clean. Food deserves special attention. The portions are large and serve them very quickly.

Apart-Hotel Slavyanovsky Istok





Pension "Slavyanovsky Istok" (Zheleznovodsk) is a modern hotel, named after the world famous brand of mineral water "Slavyanovskaya". On the territory is one of its sources. The hotel is located in the city center and offers comfortable rooms of various categories (standard, deluxe).

Services and entertainment



Almost all the hotels in Zheleznovodsk are designed for the rest of the family. Ample opportunities for this exist in the "Slavyanovsky source". For children there is a children's playground with the Highlander complex, which is designed for ten playgrounds. Also, during the rest of the parents, children can play games under the supervision of specialists in the children's room. The hotel provides rental services for high chairs and baby cots.

Guests have the opportunity to improve their health in the nearby sanatorium "Rus", which has modern medical equipment. Also, vacationers can leisurely stroll into the quiet alleys, enjoying peace, or go in for sports. Free parking is available on site for motorists. Wi-Fi access is available throughout the complex.

Pensions Zheleznovodsk really deserve good words addressed to them. This is confirmed by the reviews of those who have ever been to the "Slavyanovsky source". Vacationers noted the special friendliness of the staff and excellent conditions for families with children.

Sanatorium Plaza"



Located in the central resort area, the Plaza sanatorium (Zheleznovodsk) is a six-story building, inside which there is a whole health complex, which includes a diagnostic center, spa, sauna, hammam, gym and a beautiful pool. For physiotherapy exercises there is a separate specialized gym. A separate block has been allocated for diagnostics and laboratory research and medical procedures. The complex is equipped with modern high-speed elevators for the convenience of guests.





According to the reviews of those who visited the city more than once, it can be concluded that almost all Zheleznovodsk hotels are intended for a resort holiday. The living conditions and the services provided are at the highest level. What does the Plaza sanatorium offer its guests?

Residence

The number of rooms is one hundred thirty-four rooms. It offers vacationers apartments of various categories (double one-room standard, deluxe, studio and double two-room). The deluxe rooms have a jacuzzi, while the standard rooms are equipped with a bathroom with a shower. All apartments are equipped with a ventilation system. The rooms are equipped with modern furniture, safes for storing valuables, TVs and kitchenware. Some rooms have a loggia. A hairdryer and bath accessories are provided free of charge. Those who want to not only relax, but also improve their health, should pay attention to the health-improving complex "Plaza" (Zheleznovodsk).

Sanatorium-based treatment

The complex is designed for the simultaneous residence of two hundred sixty-eight people. The safety of vacationers is ensured by round-the-clock security. The territory is surrounded by a fence. Just a few steps from the sanatorium are mineral springs.

The complex specializes in the treatment of diseases of the digestive system, metabolic disorders and female diseases. It is also possible to treat diseases of the nervous and cardiovascular systems. Great opportunities are available for the prevention of diseases and the overall strengthening of the body. The list of services provided includes a visit to the sensory room, physiotherapy courses and emotional discharge procedures. Climatotherapy and bioresonance therapy are also available .

Since the sanatorium is located in a unique place famous for its mineral springs, great importance is given to various types of treatment using mineral water. The complex is equipped with modern diagnostic and medical equipment (magnetotherapy unit "Magnetoturbotron", ECG devices, for monitoring blood pressure).

The city of Zheleznovodsk is a unique spa and wellness center on an all-Russian scale. The mild healing climate, the presence of mineral springs make it attractive to tourists. The popularity of the resort is maintained through the continuous improvement of tourism infrastructure.

Numerous Zheleznovodsk hotels and sanatoriums of the city fully provide the opportunity for vacationers to receive a full range of services to improve the body.
