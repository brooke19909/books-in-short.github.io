Hotel Malin Patong ex Mussee Patong 3 * (Thailand, Phuket): overview, description and reviews

Thailand is an incredibly beautiful country that attracts tourists from all over the world with its exotic and original culture. Spectacular landscapes, mild climate, interesting history and traditions - this is exactly what creates the conditions for an unforgettable vacation.

If we are talking about a holiday on the beach, the most popular place is the island of Phuket. Of course, here the traveler is offered a huge selection of hotels and pensions of various sizes and categories. One of the most popular places is considered to be the Malin Patong ex Mussee Patong 3 * hotel complex.

What does the hotel look like and is its location convenient? Are there any facilities for families with children? Will it be comfortable and interesting for a domestic tourist here? The answers to these questions inevitably arise from a person planning a vacation on Thai soil.

Description of location and territory





First you need to find out the location of the hotel, because for tourists this is an important point. Hotel Malin Patong ex Mussee Patong 3 * is located in a quiet beach area, not far from the center of Phuket. Within walking distance are supermarkets, salons, cafes, shopping centers and interesting historical sights. By the way, only 200 m is the world famous Patong Beach. The distance to the international airport is 37 km (the road takes about 45 minutes, hotel guests are provided with a shuttle service).

The hotel itself consists of a four-story building, built in a discreet style. But the interior decoration will delight you, because here it is full of traditional Thai decorations, and the whole territory is literally buried in greenery. The yard area is small, but well-equipped - there is a place for recreation and for sports.

How many hotel rooms?





The hotel complex Malin Patong ex Mussee Patong 3 * is considered small - there are 60 rooms on its territory, each of the rooms is characterized by comfort and a unique design. Guests can choose a room of a suitable category:

  • Cozy superior rooms with one king size bed or two single beds.
  • Deluxe rooms, which provide guests with the maximum level of comfort.
  • Spacious suites, which consist of two separate rooms, are a bedroom and a living room with soft chairs and a large, comfortable sofa.

Hotel Malin Patong Hotel (Patong Beach, Thailand): What do the rooms look like?





Regardless of which room you are accommodated in, you can count on comfort with a calm heart. The rooms are quite spacious, and large windows overlook the hotel. Guests are satisfied with the set of furniture - in addition to the bed, bedside tables and cabinets, there are also chairs and a large desk.

As for household appliances, it is new here, without breakdowns. It offers a large flat-screen TV, because in the evening, before going to bed, you sometimes want to watch something interesting and relax. There is also air conditioning and a telephone. Some rooms also have a safe with an electronic lock and a minibar (it will be filled upon your first request, but at an additional cost).

The guests of the Malin Patong ex Mussee Patong 3 * hotel note the advantages of a private bathroom with modern, new fixtures. There is a fairly spacious shower, toilet, sink, mirror and stationary hair dryer. You can count on a daily change of towels and the availability of personal hygiene products. Reviews say that the maids at the hotel work tirelessly in order to maintain cleanliness and comfort not only in the rooms, but throughout the territory.

What kind of food should a tourist count on?





When paying for a room, you yourself can choose a convenient power scheme. Of course, all guests are invited to the cozy restaurant of the Malin Patong ex Mussee Patong 3 * hotel. Reviews say that this place is really good - stylish design, talented cooks and impeccable service.

As a rule, in the mornings there are breakfasts in the form of a buffet. The food here is varied, serving traditional Thai and European dishes, so you can choose to taste. There are also seafood, fish, fresh fruits and vegetable dishes. The rest of the day, you can also eat at the restaurant, but food will be prepared for you by order and, accordingly, will be paid separately for the bill.

Nearby there are enough cafes and restaurants with different menus and prices, so every traveler will be able to choose a place to taste.

Where is the beach located? Convenience and entertainment on the beach





Of course, tourists arriving in Phuket crave beach activities. What can the hotel Malin Patong ex Mussee Patong 3 * offer? Patong Beach, the most famous of the beaches on the island, is located not so far away - the distance is about a kilometer. Many tourists prefer to get to the shore on foot, while exploring the surroundings. But if you do not like long walks, you can use the shuttle, which runs from the hotel regularly, or even public transport.

Patong is a public beach, which means you will need to pay to rent sunbeds, umbrellas and some other services. On the other hand, the prices here are moderate, and the fun hits over the edge, because various contests, competitions, parties are constantly held. According to reviews, the beach is clean enough, and the entrance to the sea is convenient. Nearby there are several bars with a wide selection of drinks for every taste.

Of course, tourists have the opportunity to engage in more active sports, including boating, boats, water skiing, windsurfing, parasailing, diving and even sailing. Prices at the local sports center are not very high.

Should I count on additional services?





According to the guests, the hotel has all the conditions for a comfortable stay. Here you can exchange currency at a favorable rate, rent a safe at the reception, use the laundry services. Also, tourists are offered to rent vehicles at competitive prices - this is how most travelers travel around the island. Nearby there is a small but roomy parking where you can leave a car or motobike.

The staff will be happy to arrange a transfer to the airport for you or order a taxi. If you feel unwell or sick, a qualified doctor will be called to your room, whose services, by the way, are not included in the price.

For most modern tourists, the availability of the Internet is important. You can connect to Wi-Fi throughout the hotel, and for free. But it is worthwhile to understand that when the number of guests accumulates, the connection speed leaves much to be desired.

Conditions for a vacation with the whole family?

What can parents expect when they come to Malin Patong ex Mussee Patong 3 * with their children? Reviews say that by and large the hotel is adapted for family vacations - you can get a rollaway bed, and the restaurant's menu has dishes that are quite suitable for children. By the way, kids who do not occupy a separate bed in the room can stay for free.

As such, there is no entertainment on site. There is a small section in the pool where children can swim. Nevertheless, entertainment for children of any age can be found on Patong Beach, as well as in the city center, where there are many children's play centers, a water park and other institutions.
