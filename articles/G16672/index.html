Hotel "Azaria" in Gelendzhik: description and reviews

Gelendzhik is the legendary sunny resort town in the Krasnodar Territory, spread out in a picturesque bay of the Black Sea coast, at the foot of the Markotkh mountain slope. The pristine beauty of the oval bay and the Caucasus Range, between which there are hotels and guest houses with tiled roofs, has long attracted travelers.

The resort is conditionally divided into three parts: in the north guests are invited to the Thin Cape beach and private mini-hotels; in the central part of the city there are sanatoriums, pensions and hotels of the highest category, in the south Tolstoy Cape offers accommodation in a vast private sector, guest houses and mini-hotels. Climate

The climate in Gelendzhik is called very comfortable (dry subtropical, in winter there is little rainfall). The uniqueness of the microclimate is due to the fact that cold air from the plains is delayed by the Greater Caucasus Range. Clear dry sunny weather lasts from March to mid-January, then until the very middle of March, it rains insignificantly. Gelendzhik is recommended to come to rest in the most favorable season - from June to September, swimming here is best from May to October: during this period the water warms up to + 18-24 ¬∞ C.
