Sol Marina Palace 4 * (Bulgaria / Nessebar): photos, prices, description, traveler reviews

The city of Nessebar is a real gem of Bulgaria, known for its unique architecture and annually attracting tens of thousands of tourists from all over the world. In addition to acquaintance with one of the oldest cities in Europe (its age is estimated at three thousand years), travelers will find a wonderful beach holiday, wide shopping opportunities, as well as developed tourist infrastructure. If you decide to spend your vacation in Nessebar and at the same time you are looking for a good comfortable hotel, then we recommend choosing Sol Marina Palace 4 *. We suggest that you take a closer look at this hotel and find out what our compatriots remember here.





Location

Sol Marina Palace is located between Old and New Nessebar. Thus, its guests can successfully combine all the modern resort entertainment with the romantic moods of one of the oldest settlements in Europe. Just a couple of hundred meters from the hotel there are magnificent Black Sea sandy beaches. As for the nearest airport, it is located in the city of Burgas, 25 kilometers from the hotel. So, upon arrival in the air harbor, you can literally get to the hotel in half an hour.

Sol Marina Palace 4 *: photo and description

This hotel was built in 2006. It consists of one seven-story building, erected in a modern style and in shape resembling a ship. In 2012, a complete renovation was held here, so today guests are expected to be beautifully decorated and equipped with everything necessary for comfortable accommodation. In total, the hotel's housing stock is represented by 126 standard-type apartments. An interesting feature of Sol Marina Palace is the presence on its roof of a large outdoor pool with a jacuzzi and a bar. It offers a magnificent view of the Black Sea and Old Nessebar.





Hotel Policies

As in most other hotels in Bulgaria and other countries, Sol Marina Palace 4 * (Nessebar) has a certain time for arrival and departure of guests. So, check-in is done after two in the afternoon. On the day of departure, it is necessary to vacate the occupied apartment before noon. You can pay for your stay here both in cash and with cards of MasterCard and Visa payment systems. This hotel is always happy for guests with children. For babies up to two years old free cots are provided. As for pets, hotel guests with their four-legged pets are not allowed.

Housing stock

As already mentioned, Sol Marina Palace 4 * (Bulgaria) offers guests to stay in one of 126 rooms of a standard type. All are decorated in a pleasant style and are spacious and comfortable. Most apartments offer magnificent views of the sea and the Old Town. Each room is equipped with its own bathroom, TV, telephone, air conditioning, safe, minibar. Laminate flooring. Hotel guests have the opportunity to use Wi-Fi.





Nutrition

Sol Marina Palace 4 * hotel offers its guests a choice of two food systems : all inclusive and only breakfast. At your service there are several restaurants and bars with a wide selection of dishes and drinks.

Accommodation prices

The cost of accommodation in Sol Marina Palace 4 * (Bulgaria), according to tourists, is more than adequate for the level of service and living conditions that are offered to guests here. So, a seven-day stay for two in July and August will cost you in the amount of 1120 euros (breakfast is included only) and 1720 (all inclusive meals).
