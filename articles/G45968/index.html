Underwater restaurant in the Maldives: photos

Maldives is a very popular resort. If you want to spend your vacation in a paradise, enjoy the peace and quiet, get a beautiful even tan and great photos for your family album, go to the Maldives. The newlyweds have chosen this resort for a long time, it is in such a heavenly place with white sand, numerous islands and a rich flora, far from civilization I want to spend my honeymoon. Many believe that it is quite boring here, so avid travelers who prefer an active vacation are unlikely to like it here. But we are in a hurry to dispel the prevailing stereotype.

The Maldives is an amazing world of everything unusual, mysterious, it is here that you can visit amazing places, visit attractions that are unrivaled in any other point on the globe. An underwater restaurant in the Maldives - it is about this attraction of the resort that will be discussed in this material. We picked up the best photos, as well as the impressions of those who managed to visit this truly amazing place.

Where is?

If you are relaxing on the islands, be sure to visit the hotel in the Maldives with the underwater restaurant Ithaa. Today it is the only restaurant under water that attracts the attention of tourists and reporters. The design of the restaurant itself is unique, gives rise to a lot of controversy and doubt, but does not become less attractive from this.





The restaurant is part of the Conrad Maldives Rangali Island Hotel, located on Rangali Island. It is really located under water, it is not a ground structure, as many believe. The restaurant opened to visitors back in 2004, and by 2010 it had become a full-fledged attraction and legend of the resort.

Concept

The New Zealand company worked on the concept of creating the world's first underwater restaurant (Maldives). Initially, the project planned vertical walls with huge panoramic windows. But chief architect May Murphy offered his vision - an underground tunnel with a panoramic roof made of heavy-duty acrylic. About the depth

The largest underwater restaurant in the Maldives is located at a depth of 5 meters and is a small room just 5 x 9 meters. The transparent dome of the restaurant just allows you to enjoy the beauty and richness of the underwater world of the island. All those who had long dreamed of diving, but were afraid of self-diving with equipment under water, had a unique opportunity to enjoy the beauty and perfection of the underwater world in the company of relatives and friends.





The restaurant has already become the hallmark of the resort, one of the most beautiful and romantic places on the planet. If you are looking for an opportunity to surprise your soul mate, admit to her your feelings, surrounded by amazing underwater beauty, feel free to book a table in this restaurant.

Security

How safe is it to be in an underwater restaurant in the Maldives? Of course, this question excites many. It is unlikely that even among the most courageous and brave tourists there are those who want to pay for stunning photos and unforgettable impressions of their own lives. For the construction of a transparent dome, an innovative material was used - acrylic, it is not afraid of water, water mass pressure, durable and reliable, while it is endowed with transparency so necessary for an underwater restaurant.

Construction difficulties

Of course, with just one look at the photo of an underwater restaurant in the Maldives, which has been available for visiting for many years, it seems that its construction took place without the slightest difficulty. But this is far from the case. The pavilion with a panoramic roof was built on land, after which the whole structure had to be placed under water, to a depth of 5 meters.





The construction of the tunnel was carried out in Singapore in 2004, it took only six months. At the exit, the weight of the pavilion with all the necessary structural elements amounted to 175 tons. In November 2004, the tunnel was delivered to the Maldives and submerged using 85-ton ballast. To fix the underwater restaurant, steel supports were used, which were driven into the seabed for 45 meters.

Then, the authors of the project faced a difficult task - to connect a restaurant with land. To do this, a spiral staircase was built, starting from a small thatched house right on the pier.





Reservation

If you looked at a photo of an underwater restaurant in the Maldives and decided to visit it during your vacation, remember that this is a rather small, chamber establishment, which is very popular among tourists. It is ready to receive no more than 14 visitors, so it is better to reserve a table in the restaurant in advance. During the season, a whole queue is created to visit such an interesting attraction.

It should be remembered that the operating life of an underwater restaurant in the Maldives is only 20 years, so you need to have time to visit it before the end of 2024-2025. The restaurant is open from 11 to 00 hours. Children are not allowed for dinner, if you have come to rest with your whole family and want to visit this place, feel free to go to lunch.
