Club Bamboo Boutique Resort & SPA 3 * (Thailand, Patong Beach): description, service, reviews

Tourists from Russia call Patong little Pattaya. It is a large and densely populated region. Its coast is occupied by premium and mid-range hotels. The abundance of bars and restaurants, shopping centers and shops brings diversity to the measured life of vacationers.

Luxury Jungle





Bangla Road is the epicenter of the night and cultural life of the island. A trip to these parts is chosen by the young and energetic. The doors of night clubs and discos are open for them. Patong coast is equipped with everything necessary for a flax pastime. Sun beds and umbrellas, awnings and mattresses are for rent. The sea is always noisy and crowded. There are practically no secluded places.

If you compare the cost of holidays on the island with the prices of Phuket, then in Patong it is higher. This shortcoming more than compensates for the abundance of all kinds of entertainment. At the disposal of tourists are beauty salons, massage rooms, medical centers.

The nearest international airport is in Pattaya. He is stung from the resort for forty kilometers. To get to Club Bamboo Boutique Resort SPA yourself, you need to use a taxi. The trip will cost 900 rubles. By bus you can drive for 300.

The route runs along Bangla Road. Landing is carried out on demand. The parking lot is the southern tip of the central beach.

Reviews

At Club Bamboo Boutique Resort SPA, shared services are provided free of charge. The words of vacationers confirm this. The maid regularly cleaned the room. Shampoo and liquid soap were replenished as needed. The ATM, which is located in the lobby of the hotel near the reception, worked properly. It was always possible to withdraw cash.

Internet speed was sufficient for downloading files and sending emails. The low cost of living compensated for the inconvenience associated with faulty appliances and shabby furniture. Due to its location away from noisy highways, the nights in it were quiet. Nothing disturbed the sensitive sleep of young children and their parents. Dining at Club Bamboo Boutique Resort SPA is available at the restaurant.

Not without cons. The guests complained about insects that ran right in the rooms. Many did not like the steep steps. It was difficult to climb them. There were complaints about the diner menu. The dishes seemed too sharp and salty. I had to go to the center in search of budget European canteens or bistros.

If you got the old number, then in compensation the hotel administration offered substantial discounts on cocktails in the bar.
