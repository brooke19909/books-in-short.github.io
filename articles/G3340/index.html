Things to do in Kabardinka

Kabardinka is a resort village in the Krasnodar Territory, located just 15 kilometers from the large city of Gelendzhik. This place is popular due to its favorable climate, favorable location and developed tourist infrastructure. There are no ancient monuments and any unique objects, however Kabardinka offers vacationers entertainment for every taste and budget.
