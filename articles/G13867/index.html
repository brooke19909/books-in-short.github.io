Aurora (hotel, Tyumen) operates in a popular youth hostel format. This allows not only to lower prices for accommodation, but also to create unique comfortable conditions in which you can make new friends.





Brief description of the hotel

If you are looking for an inexpensive and comfortable accommodation option, then you should definitely pay attention to such an option as Aurora (hotel). Tyumen mainly attracts business people who want to have the opportunity to have a good rest after a busy day. It has everything that a modern tourist needs - wireless Internet, comfortable furniture, as well as a common lounge area where you can watch TV while sitting on a soft sofa.

It is worth noting that the Aurora (hotel, Tyumen) offers fairly budget accommodation. However, you do not have to live in a dormitory room. You have the opportunity to order individual apartments. All the furniture in the rooms is new and comfortable. In addition, you will have a choice between a private or shared bathroom.

As for food in an institution such as Aurora (hotel, Tyumen), here you can cook it yourself in the shared kitchen. It provides all the necessary household appliances, as well as dishes. Tea, coffee and spices are provided free of charge. The only condition is compliance with the rules of cleanliness. Breakfast is included.

Hotel "Aurora" (Tyumen): address

This hotel is located in the city center at the address: 50 years of the Komsomol, 13. The hostel is located in a building of an apartment building. Having walked for about 20 minutes in an unhurried step, you can reach the main promenade street and the central square. Also very close is a large garden and a popular theater. The road to the train station will take you no more than 20 minutes. There is also a bus station nearby, so you can quickly get anywhere in the city.





Guest rooms

Sufficiently comfortable conditions for accommodation are offered to tourists by the Aurora Hotel. Tyumen will seem even more hospitable if you stay in one of the comfortable rooms:

  • A multi-bed room involves one and a half beds. All rooms have a TV and wardrobe. Each guest has their own bedside table. The bathroom and toilet are shared by several rooms.
  • Individual rooms can be single or double. They are ideal for those who love solitude, peace and silence. This is a full-fledged hotel room with its own bathroom.
  • Executive apartments are a bit of an apartment. On a huge area there was not only a bed, but also upholstered furniture, a dining table. It also has its own full kitchen.

General impression

We can say that the Aurora hotel is an attractive option for accommodation in Tyumen. This is a budget hostel that compares favorably with institutions of this type. Here, new and beautiful furniture, a full shared kitchen, as well as absolute cleanliness. You can also relax in a pleasant friendly atmosphere. It is important that smoking and drinking alcohol, as well as loud music in the hostel are strictly prohibited. There is a full kitchen with all necessary appliances and utensils. You will also enjoy unlimited tea and coffee.

If we talk about the negative aspects of this hotel, then this is, of course, the location on the 11th floor of an apartment building. If this is your first time in the city, then it will be difficult for you to find this place among the yards. Another inconvenience is that the reception is in a completely different house. And, of course, the biggest drawback is the breakfast. It would be better if it were not included in the price at all. For this money, each guest can afford something more than porridge and a bland pancake. And the surcharge for the bath set for the second guest is completely beyond the scope of any hotel rules.
