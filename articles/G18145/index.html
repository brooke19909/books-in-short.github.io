Amaris Complex Hotel 3 *, Bulgaria: reviews and photos

Sunny Beach is a resort that will appeal to all travelers who value a developed tourist infrastructure. This complex is fully focused on the needs of vacationers. Near the embankment, which runs throughout the village along the coast, there are a huge number of hotels of various categories. So, the Amaris Complex 3 * hotel (Sunny Beach) can become a budget accommodation option. What services does she offer tourists?

Location

This small beach hotel is located in the heart of the Sunny Beach resort. It is located on the second beach line, and it is about 300 meters from the sea. Typically, tourists spend only 10 minutes on the road to it. Next to it is the main road, so sometimes it can be a little noisy from passing vehicles. In the immediate vicinity of the hotel there are many infrastructure facilities that travelers may need. There is a large supermarket with low prices, a bank branch, a medical center, a taxi rank and a night club.

At 200 meters from Amaris Complex 3 * (Bulgaria, Sunny Beach) is a public transport stop. From here, you can take a bus to Old Nessebar and Varna, which are worth visiting for tourists who love excursion vacations. In the territory of Sunny Beach there is also an amusement park and a water park, which are often visited by vacationers with children. The nearest airport is near the city of Burgas, which is 30 km away from here. Transfer to the hotel on average takes about an hour. Tourists can pay for it when issuing vouchers. You can also come here on your own in a rented car or order a taxi.

Important information

The hotel opened in 2006. Its main building attracts tourists with its original design and bright colors. In total, there are 7 floors in the building, on which 130 budget rooms are located. Travelers from Russia and other CIS countries who prefer an inexpensive vacation stay here. Near the Amaris Complex 3 * hotel (Bulgaria) there is also an outdoor pool and a miniature terrace where you can sunbathe enough. The building is equipped with elevators and wheelchair ramps. Guests will be able to enter their rooms no earlier than noon. In addition, they should leave them at the end of the rest also before 12:00.





The hotel allows children of all ages. Pets are not allowed. Small children are provided with a cradle in the room for a fee. An extra bed can also be delivered to the room, and a discount is offered to guests sleeping on it. The hotel administrator is working around the clock. Some employees speak Russian well, although most of them prefer to speak English or Bulgarian. You can pay for your vacation and additional services here with a Visa or MasterCard bank card.

Number of rooms and its equipment

Spacious and comfortable rooms are offered for its guests by the Amaris Complex 3 * hotel. They are unlikely to surprise you with a luxurious setting, but they are perfect for budget living. All apartments here have a small cozy balcony with a table and several chairs. From the windows of the last floors offers a magnificent view of the Black Sea. Residents of the first floors can get rooms that overlook the garden or pool. Carpeted floors. According to European service standards, maids carefully clean their living rooms every day. Bed linen is changed once a week, and towels are usually renewed every other day.





Most of the rooms in the Amaris Complex 3 * hotel are standard apartments designed for two adults and a child. They are a small living room and bathroom, which houses a shower, toilet, washbasin and clothes dryer. When checking in, the option of single occupancy is also possible. Some of the rooms are spacious studios, which are complemented by a small seating area. Here on the sofa bed another adult can sleep.

All rooms have a color TV, which broadcasts satellite TV. In hot weather, tourists can always use individual air conditioning. Drinks can be chilled in the minibar, which is provided free of charge upon request. However, you still have to pay for drinks separately. All apartments have a telephone, and for an additional amount you can connect wireless Internet.





Local infrastructure

A good selection of additional services offers guests Amaris Complex 3 * (Sunny Beach). It is not very diverse, but there are all the necessary infrastructure facilities that may be needed by picky tourists during their holidays. We list the main ones:

  • Own parking for cars. Hotel guests can leave their car here absolutely free at any time. Tourists can also rent a car by filling out the necessary documents at the reception.
  • Internet cafe. Here you can pay for using a modern computer and high-speed Internet.
  • Currency exchange, which is located at the reception. There is also a 24-hour cash machine near the hotel.
  • Upon request, the administrator can call tourists to the room of a qualified general practitioner. His services are provided for a fee.
  • Laundry and ironing. Employees can also clean shoes and leather goods.
  • Safe at reception. You can leave your valuables in it if you are afraid that they might be stolen in the room.




Food concept

When buying a tour to this hotel, it is worth knowing that Amaris Complex 3 * does not offer tourists the all-inclusive concept. You can issue here only breakfasts or breakfasts and dinners. Therefore, tourists are forced to independently organize their meals. It is not difficult to do this, because there are many cafes specializing in Bulgarian and European cuisine near the hotel.

Breakfast and dinner are held in the hotel's own restaurant. He has an outdoor terrace. It offers amazing sea views. If the hotel is full, then, as a rule, a common buffet is served. Otherwise, tourists are individually served √† la carte. Continental breakfast is provided. It includes sandwiches, fried sausages, bacon, cereals and dry cereal, as well as pastries and vegetable slices. For dinner, quality meals are served, and the menu must have meat, fresh fruits and vegetables. Sometimes fish and seafood are served.





Entertainment program for adults and children

The hotel does not offer its guests a wide selection of entertainment. Amaris Complex 3 * is focused on beach holidays. In good weather, a swimming pool and sun deck are open for tourists. Sun loungers, sun umbrellas and mattresses are provided on it for free. There is also a separate shallow pool for young children. You can relax after a tiring day or wait for the check-in time in the cozy lobby of the hotel, which is equipped with soft chairs and sofas. It has air conditioning and a TV. Positive reviews

Tourists who have read the opinions of guests know that Amaris Complex 3 * reviews are not bad. They note the advantages and disadvantages of relaxation here. First, consider the advantages of the hotel:

  • Great location. Near the hotel there is a bus stop with which you can inexpensively get to the beach. This option is suitable for lazy tourists who do not want to walk to it on foot.
  • Well-chosen staff. They always smile, talk politely. The administrator will always help with solving problems, will meet the requests and wishes.
  • Clean and deep pool. Ideal for tall people. There are many free sunbeds on the terrace.
  • All equipment in the room is in good condition. The shower cabin is spacious, water from it does not spill onto the floor.
  • The hotel is in a quiet area, so at night you will not hear noise from discos and nightclubs.




Negative reviews

Unfortunately, guests of Amaris Complex 3 * (Bulgaria) sometimes leave extremely negative reviews. In them, they describe the shortcomings that greatly spoiled the vacation experience. So that other tourists do not repeat their fate, they recommend choosing another hotel for relaxation. We list the main disadvantages of the hotel:

  • The room is a lot of dust. Carpet and blackout curtains do not vacuum, therefore, tourists with respiratory diseases are not recommended to relax here.
  • Dishes in the restaurant do not update. If you come to breakfast at 9:00, that is, there will already be nothing. The menu did not change throughout the rest.
  • The restaurant has rude staff who do not comply with sanitation rules.
  • Internet is available at the hotel for a fee, but its speed is terribly low. The administrator refuses to return money for it.
  • The windows offer an ugly view of the wasteland, where there is a lot of construction waste.

Conclusion

Is it worth it to relax in the Amaris Complex 3 * hotel (Sunny Beach)? Reviews say that it is better to choose another hotel. The level of this complex, unfortunately, does not reach the other three-star hotels of the resort. However, it can be recommended to picky tourists who want to save a lot on vacation.
