Hotel "Emerald", Anapa: description, reviews and photos

In our article we want to talk about one of the hotel complexes of the village of Vityazevo near Anapa. Hotel "Emerald" (Anapa) was built fifteen kilometers from the famous resort.

Village Vityazevo

The village of Vityazevo is part of Anapa, which has long won the title of one of the most popular and largest tourist destinations on the Black Sea coast of the Krasnodar Territory. The village is washed not only by the sea, but also by the waters of the Vityazevsky estuary. This place has become famous for its first-class large beaches, which are of considerable length.

Shallow warm sea, gentle relief, dry and mild Mediterranean climate made the village a popular resort among Russians. In Vityazevo , mainly couples with children and young people rest . An interesting fact is that most of the local population are Greeks. This gives a special charm to the village, because even in architecture, traditions, dishes there are peculiarities. Indigenous people call Vityazevo little Athens. It is worth visiting at least once to change prevailing stereotypes about the Black Sea coast.



A wonderful area that Anapa - Vityazevo can boast of. The Emerald Hotel is not the only one here, but one of the best. In the village there is really a large selection of housing: hotels, motels, health-improving complexes, private households and even campsites on the wild shore.

Hotel Location

Clean air, the sun, the sea, bewitching landscapes - all this gives its guests Anapa (Vityazevo). Hotel "Emerald" is an excellent accommodation option for those who come here to relax from everyday worries and gain strength and energy.

The hotel is located in the center of the village, on the famous Paralia Boulevard, which is the focus of resort life. A ten minute walk from the hotel is a wonderful sandy beach. The complex has a territory of 0.8 hectares, which is sufficiently landscaped and is under protection.

Healing procedures

Do not forget that Vityazevo is not just a seaside resort, but also a medical center with sanatoriums. In this unique region there are healing springs that have beneficial effects on the cardiovascular system, biliary tract and gastrointestinal tract. The doctor schedules the intake of water, as a rule, the treatment course is two to three weeks.

In addition, mud volcanoes are of particular interest. Homer also mentioned them in his Odyssey. Not only are the volcanoes themselves - this is an unusual sight, but also the mud in them is healing. They can treat many diseases: from infertility to skin ailments. In Vityazevsky district, in the crater of the Tizdar volcano, up to two dozen people take mud baths at the same time.
