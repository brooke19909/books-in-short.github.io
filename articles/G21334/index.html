A trip to the Novotroitskoe reservoir: recreation facilities. Which one to choose?

Have the opportunity to come to the Novotroitskoe reservoir? Rest in these places is unforgettable. However, so that nothing could spoil it, you must correctly choose a camp site. This article will consider the best options where you can stay on vacation. Their choice should be taken seriously, because the quality of rest depends not only on the presence of a forest and a reservoir, but also on living conditions, service, and organized leisure. Many tourists prefer recreation centers for one important reason - cost. Among these institutions you can always find an interesting inexpensive option.

"Shore of the Sun"

A trip to the Novotroitskoe reservoir promises to be amazing and interesting. Recreation centers in the district are trying to organize a variety of entertainment programs. This pond has gained distribution due to its shallow water and rich wildlife. Families with children often relax here, as the depth increases only a few meters from land. "Sunshine" is more likely to meet fishermen at its doorstep. Most of them are in July-August. It was at this time that pike and catfish were especially active. There are other fish species, but their populations are small.

Guests are accommodated in cottages that differ in their conditions. There are options for economy, comfort, and there are VIP seats. The average price is 3500 rubles. The price always includes breakfast. Lunch and dinner will have to be purchased for separate funds.

The Novotroitsk reservoir of the Stavropol Territory attracts many vacationers to its shores due to its beautiful landscapes. Fishing on it is not prohibited, moreover, on the described base they will even always give for temporary use all the necessary accessories.



"Gazovik"

The recreation center "Gazovik" offers accommodation in comfortable rooms, which are designed for 2-5 people. The minimum cost per day is 500 rubles.

The complex has a cafe, restaurant, piers, barbecue facilities, and a bathhouse. It is possible to rent some sports equipment and more. The beach is private. You can fish from the pier for free at any time of the day. Of the minuses of this recreation center can only be called the fact that in the summer there are too many guests. This is due to such an excellent water resource of the Stavropol Territory as the Novotroitsk Reservoir. Recreation facilities located on its shore in summer are in high demand among all residents not only of this region, but also of neighboring regions.

From the beach "Gazovika" you can catch pike, carp, catfish, roach, carp. However, fishing is permitted only with authorized gear.



"Gull"

What options to choose for accommodation, coming on vacation to the Novotroitskoe reservoir? Recreation facilities! "The Seagull" is a good institution, which is a boarding house. It is located very close to the coastline. Travelers and fishermen constantly settle here. The living conditions in the boarding house are quite good, as the staff tries to keep the level. The administration is friendly and will always help if you turn to it with a question or request.



"Exclusive fishing"

Guests at the recreation center "Exclusive Fishing" lodged in houses for 2-4 people. The price does not include meals. The minimum price per day is 1 thousand rubles.

The base has everything you need for entertainment: cafes, hammocks, barbecue facilities. It is possible to rent boats, fishing tackle, boats, etc. There is also a parking lot in case the guest arrives by car.

You will have to pay for the catch. Price depends on weight. Moreover, the cost will also vary from time to time: 800 rubles will have to be paid during the day, and 1 thousand rubles at night. From year to year, many come to fishing at the Novotroitskoe reservoir. Recreation facilities, in particular "Exclusive Fishing", offer rental equipment and gear. There is a high probability of catching a trophy catch.



Six Lakes

The recreation center "Six Lakes" is a bit unusual. Guests are accommodated at a local hotel. The minimum price is 500 rubles. Meals are not included.

The Novotroitskoe reservoir is artificially stocked in this place. Recreation facilities, which are located on its coast, offer organized fishing. For example, at the Six Lakes complex, fishing is permitted only from the shore. In the summer, there are quite a lot of vacationers. A fisherman can always rent fishing rods and other equipment. You can cook your own catch in the kitchen if you wish.
