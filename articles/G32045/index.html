What sights of Corfu is worth seeing?





Corfu is the common name for the Greek island of Kerkyra. He is the most beautiful in the Ionian Sea. Lush vegetation, magnificent beaches and developed infrastructure attract many tourists there. At the same time, Greek settlements, almost untouched by civilization, remained high in the mountains.

What are the sights of Corfu you need to see? Most often, tourists come here because of the beautiful beaches. Most vacation spots are on the east coast of the island. They are great for families and lovers.

The sights of Corfu are not only beaches. Many tourists go there to see historical monuments. Stormy life flowed in these places many hundreds of years ago. Different peoples and countries tried to conquer the island, power changed there many times. And everyone left there the monuments of their culture. Only in the capital you can see the Old and New Sea Fortresses, Esplanade Square, Liston Street, the Museum of Asian Art, the Mon Repos Palace and many other attractions of Corfu. The city is interesting not only for its historical monuments, but also for its many cathedrals, monasteries and churches. Many pilgrims go to bow to the Cathedral of the Virgin Mary of Spiliotissa and to the church of St. Spyridon, the patron saint of the island. It is also interesting to see the unusual church of St. George, which was built in the Doric style, and the most beautiful church from the time of Byzantium - Saints Jason and Sosipatra.





A place that has preserved many magnificent monuments of antiquity is Greece, the island of Corfu. The map is full of ancient names that attract tourists. This is a small island of Pontikonisi, about which many legends tell, for example, that this is the ship of Odyssey, which Poseidon turned into a piece of land. Achilleion Palace is very beautiful , surrounded by a beautiful park. And not far from the capital is the resort of Paleokastritsa with very picturesque nature and the ancient monastery of the Virgin Mary. Next to it is the fortress of Angelokastro or in another way the castle of Angel.

The sights of Corfu are also modern leisure. Tourists are waited by comfortable hotels, pools, tasty food and various entertainments. If you come to Corfu with children, then you should visit the water park. This is a whole amusement town, where there are small arbors for kids, and a huge pool, and twisted slides, and even a jacuzzi. In addition, the mild climate of Corfu is ideal for families with children.

And for those who love modern and comfortable rest, and those who prefer solitude and enjoy the views of nature, Corfu island (Greece) is suitable for relaxation . Its attractions will not only allow you to enjoy your vacation, but also spend time with benefit.
