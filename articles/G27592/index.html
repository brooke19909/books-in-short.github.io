Hotel Landmark Hotel Baniyas 3 * UAE, Dubai - overview, features and reviews

Choosing a destination for a long-awaited trip, tourists pay attention to different characteristics: for some, climate is important, for someone in the first place is the stellarness of the hotel, someone prefers a variety of entertainment. Dubai resort combines a number of positive aspects, and everyone can enjoy a long-awaited vacation here: a range of amenities and pastimes are designed for different income levels. Picturesque natural landscapes, good-natured residents and luminous skyscrapers are what impress from the first minutes of stay on these lands.

The tourist season opens in September and lasts until May. It is during these months that the temperature is less sultry and easily tolerated by unadapted vacationers. The 3-star Landmark Hotel Baniyas offers top-level service and comfortable accommodation.





general characteristics

Hotel Landmark Hotel Baniyas 3 * (UAE) was built and received its first guests at the turn of the millennium. Over the years, it has been subjected to a number of transformations, the last of which was carried out in 2014. From this moment, the hotel complex shone with new colors, attracting an increasing number of travelers. The main and only building is represented by eleven floors with windows reflecting like mirrors. It is equipped with three elevators. The first floors are occupied by boutiques and shops in the amount of more than fifty units. Such a symbiosis of a shopping center and a place to stay is to the liking of guests who note this fact in their reviews as a positive characteristic.

A distinctive feature is the absence of any alcohol in the restaurant and bar. No deposit is taken at check-in. There are staff who speak Russian at a conversational level. Pets are not allowed in the rooms. There are facilities for people with disabilities. Check-in time is standard - 14:00. Leave the walls of your apartment before noon. Smoking is not allowed throughout the building.





Location

The convenient location of the Landmark Hotel Baniyas 3 * (Deira) is characterized by being in the heart of the business and shopping center of this city, which explains its wide popularity and demand among visitors. To the international airport - 7 km and exactly the same to the beach. The largest shopping centers are within walking distance. You can reach the Dubai Creek offshore in minutes.

Equipment

If we talk about a long stay, then there is everything that is necessary for the feeling of being at home. The furniture set includes a large wardrobe, a luggage stand, a desk with a chair, double or single beds, an armchair, a coffee table and bedside tables. Near the berths there are many sockets. There is a mirror.

Split system - individual, allows each guest to set the temperature required for him. A multi-channel flat-screen satellite TV contains Russian-language channels. A telephone is required for quick communication with the reception.

A tea set is a combination of everything necessary for tea drinking. There is a set of dishes. An electric kettle will help you make fresh coffee and tea. The minibar is stocked at an additional cost with a wide range of soft drinks and snacks. Drinking water is provided upon check-in.

In the rooms there is a safe, hangers and bathrobes with slippers. High speed internet is free and available throughout. The bathroom is decorated with modern ceramic tiles, equipped with a hairdryer, bidet, shower or bath. The mirror has personal hygiene products: soap, shampoo and shower gel. The types and quantity of snow-white towels comply with international standards.





Nutrition

To satisfy the hunger and thirst of vacationers, the Landmark Hotel Baniyas 3 * (Dubai) has two restaurants: Palms and Ko Chin Spices. The first of them specializes in the preparation of international cuisine and operates around the clock. The second pleases guests exclusively with dishes, the homeland of which is India. Their visit and meals are not included in the price.

Shamoana's main restaurant provides breakfast and lunch options. The organization of the price includes a buffet. At predetermined hours, tourists can choose any dish that they liked, in an unlimited amount. The interior of the hall is cozy and has a meal and a friendly conversation with neighbors on the table.

The interior of the institution is decorated in a classic style: multi-level ceilings, numerous spotlights, paintings and ceramic tiles on the floor. Serving and furniture are pleasing to the eye and create comfort for hungry visitors. The abundance of the dishes on offer is very large. Unique masterpieces are incredibly tasty and satisfying. According to many guests, even the most sophisticated tourist can choose a dish to his taste. Guests who come to rest with children note the presence of a special children's menu in the restaurant.





Aquazone

One of the goals of those arriving at the resort is swimming and sunbathing and air bathing. At the Landmark Hotel Baniyas 3 * (UAE, Dubai), the aquazone is located on the roof of the building. Amazing views that are visible through the fence are fascinating: the city is in full swing, shining and all in motion.

The swimming pool has a gradual increase in depth and is surrounded by a few sun loungers. They are covered with soft striped mattresses, which allows you to sunbathe and relax without leaving the walls of the hotel.

Under the sun loungers is an artificial lawn. A lifeguard is on duty nearby. A table with chairs allows you to dine in the warm sunshine or to enjoy a romantic dinner in the fresh air by candlelight.

Entertainment

The main and easily realized entertainment is a shopping trip on the first floors. However, this opportunity is more to the liking of the female half. At this time, men can play a game of billiards. Most guests prefer to go on excursions to local attractions. These include the legendary fountains of Dubai and the architectural highlight of Burj Khalifa. Boat tours are impressive and in great demand among vacationers.





Sport and beauty

For lovers of outdoor activities and preferring constant physical activity, the doors of the gym are open. It is equipped with cardiovascular equipment, weight training equipment, treadmills and other numerous quality equipment. Here you can lose a couple of extra pounds, gaining elegant shapes and a slender waist. Professional trainers will help you plan your workouts and optimal exercises.

A visit to the sauna is paid upon. The opportunity to take a steam bath in a friendly company for travelers. This procedure has a positive effect on all systems of the human body: cleanses the skin, removes toxins, improves blood circulation, relieves irritation and improves mood. Massage is not included in the sauna program, but is available for a fee.





Beach

Due to such a considerable distance from the beach, the management of the Landmark Plaza Hotel Baniyas 3 * took care of the comfort of its guests. A free bus runs daily to the Persian Gulf in both directions. The beach cover is exclusively sandy, which will allow young children to customize sand castles and pasochniki. The entrance to the sea is gentle, which is safe for bathing children.

The infrastructure of this zone is developed at the highest level. It has everything you need to enjoy the hot sunny weather and get a shiny dark brown tan. Comfortable deckchairs with soft bedding, umbrellas for shade and the presence of beach bars allow you to spend a carefree time, forgetting about the problems. Towels are provided free of charge. Rescuers and the security service tirelessly watch for safety and order.

A number of water activities are available at an additional cost. You can have fun not only for children, but also for adults: laughter, smiles, joy and fun are integral companions of every Dubai guest.

Additional services

In an effort to satisfy the needs of tourists, over the years of its existence, the Landmark Hotel Baniyas hotel complex has created the optimal package of related services. The main one is the presence of a conference room. For those who have arrived not only to relax, but also to work, here you can implement a scheduled meeting, conference or presentation. To do this, there is all the necessary equipment, which includes projectors, a screen, a laptop, microphones and much more.

Within the walls of the hotel complex, you can also hold a planned celebration or celebrate an anniversary. The decoration of the banquet hall, with a capacity of 50 people, fully corresponds to the format of such an event.

There is a currency exchange office. Access to the Internet corner is possible for a fee. Laundry operates on a fee basis. A doctor is called upon request and is paid upon. At the reception you can use the personal safe.

For travel with the aim of exploring the surroundings, you can rent a car. The tour desk can assist with a selection of exciting excursions to local attractions. Transfer service is paid extra: in a matter of minutes, guests will be delivered to the intended point safely and comfortably.
