Real work has a lot of flaws. You have to wake up early, and endure the stampede in public transport, and listen to the discontent of the authorities. Such a life does not please at all. Internet features

All modern people are actively using the Internet. For each person, a worldwide network plays a role. For many, the Internet is primarily entertainment. People watch movies, music videos, interesting videos, listen to music, turn on the radio, play online games.

For some, the Internet is an opportunity for education and self-education. A large number of books are posted on the Internet in electronic form; there are distance learning programs offered by higher education institutions.

And not everyone knows that the Internet is also an opportunity to make money. At the same time, finding a job or source of income is not so simple. Beginners have a very difficult time, because they often encounter scammers, deceivers who talk about thousands of earnings in 1 day. When asked how a girl can make money, you can give a few verified answers.

Rewriting and copywriting

If you know the rules of the Russian language well, have perseverance and patience, then pay attention to such a way of earning as rewriting and copywriting. These terms indicate the writing of texts for various sites. Rewriting is the rewriting in your own words of an existing article, and copywriting is the creation of a completely original text.

If you decide to try yourself in rewriting and copywriting for the first time, then you should register on some specialized exchange. She acts as an intermediary between the contractor and the customer, serves as a guarantor of all obligations. There are several well-known exchanges - Advego, Text.ru, Etxt.ru.

Recommendations for future rewriters and copywriters

Exchanges themselves do not deceive their performers, but fraud is still possible on these online resources from unscrupulous users. You can avoid cheating. Here are some recommendations on how a girl can earn money on rewriting and copywriting, while not encountering scammers:

  • Do not start writing any article at the request of another user until you receive an order to work with the correct cost indication;
  • Before starting cooperation, always read the reviews written on the user (customer) page;
  • Do not write free trial articles, even if the customer promises you higher paying orders in the future.

How much can you earn on rewriting and copywriting?

Earnings on rewriting and copywriting depends on several factors - on the volume of executed orders, the cost set for 1 thousand characters, the availability of free time. At the very beginning, you can try to fulfill several inexpensive orders. As a rule, when writing the first articles, beginners will learn about all the requirements, rules, programs necessary for checking texts.

In the future, look for more expensive orders, try to work with third-party customers, requiring an advance payment. As a result, you can reach a good level of income, but again, it all depends on the volume of articles performed. Some performers write very little, and their earnings are a couple of thousand rubles a month. Others cope with large volumes without problems. Their earnings reach 30 thousand rubles a month, and sometimes even the amount is greater.

Income from Instagram

Instagram today is a popular social network. It has registered approximately 25 million users in our country. The platform attracts not only by the presence of photographs of various people, but also by the opportunity to generate income. However, in this social network, a girl can either earn a large sum of money or not get anything at all. What to do for a girl to earn money on Instagram? Earnings in a social network are presented in several ways:

The latter method is the simplest, but it is suitable for promoted profiles with an average of 10 thousand subscribers or more. For those girls who do not have start-up capital and relevant knowledge to create a website, an online store and who are far from copywriting, do not use Instagram and are not interested in computer games, there is a simple way to make money - working as an online consultant or operator.

On sites with job advertisements, large companies sometimes look for assistants for remote work. The average income can be about 15 thousand rubles.

Remote Work Example

One of the companies offering remote work is Tinkoff Bank. The available vacancy is an operator. Operators fulfill their duties during business hours from 7 am to 10 pm. Each employee independently plans his schedule. The payment is decent. With effective work, good results, the bank pays from 20 thousand rubles. In order to earn a lot of money for a girl in this way, as a rule, you need to try very hard, not be distracted by social networks, etc.

Another advantage of remote work from Tinkoff Bank is that it is official. From the income of each operator, contributions are made to the Pension Fund of the Russian Federation.
