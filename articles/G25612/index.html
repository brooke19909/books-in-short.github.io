It is recommended to take any pattern for cross stitching as its basis. It is clear that the figure should correspond to the subject. It can be a Easter cake or a willow twig, a candle or a painted egg, or possibly a cross. Then you get an Easter napkin (crochet).

Round motif napkin

For her, you need to complete one central circle and seven smaller ones around. They will then need to be filled with patterns suitable for the occasion. It can be the letters X and B, as well as a cross and flowers. This approach allows you to get unique Easter napkins. It‚Äôs easy to make them with your own hands, the main thing is to remember the technique by which you need to connect the base of the circle.

Its beginning is in a set of five loops. The first row is formed by 5 air, two columns with two double crochets, air and two more columns, with only one double crochet. The second is similar to the first, only in the reverse order. First, 5 aerial and two double crochet, aerial and two double crochet.

The central circle is made of 42 similar rows; Easter napkins (crocheted) are based on it. The schemes of small circles are identical to him, only there will be 38 rows.

It is recommended to fill the center circle with a pattern in the form of a regular heptagon. You can start it from the center by typing a ring of 8 loops. Its first row begins with three aerial, then two double crochets with a common top. 10 aerial and three double crochet with one top. Repeat until seven elements are formed from columns separated by 10 air loops.

Second row: 3 air loops, another (*) 4 air loops, single crochet in the middle of the chain of the previous row, air, another single crochet. 4 aerial, single crochet to the top of the triple column. Continue the pattern as described after marking (*).

Connect the pattern with the circle using two double crochet stitches. They should begin in an arch from one airway and be attached to two adjacent arches of a circle.

Crocheted chicken

Not only an Easter napkin (crochet) turns out to be a decoration at home for the holiday. As an alternative, it is recommended to tie a bulky chicken.

Start knitting relies on the head. For her, dial a ring of 5-7 loops. The first row consists of 10 double crochet posts alternating with an air loop. Second: two double crochet in each arch of the previous row.

There are two such circles. Tie them in single crochet with each other. Leave free on seven columns in both circles. From them the knitting of the body will begin.





The first row of the calf: columns with two double crochets, alternating with an air loop. In the second, two double crochet posts go after one air. Moreover, the columns are executed in arches.

Third row: already three columns with two double crochets alternate with two air loops. The fourth begins the difference in the breast and wings of a bird.

Half of the fourth row, which will be the breast, repeats what was in the third. And the second part consists of such triple columns connected by two in each arch and separated by two air loops. So continue two more rows.

It remains to tie the tail of the chicken. At first two rows are arches from air loops. The next row - 8 columns with two double crochets, pairwise divided by picot.

Finish the Easter chicken by knitting a scallop and a beak.
