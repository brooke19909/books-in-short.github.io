Overview of the best hotels in Lisbon

If you are thinking about your next trip to Europe, do not miss the opportunity to visit Lisbon. This historic city, which is always comfortable to be in, the sun shines here 290 days a year, and the temperature rarely drops below 15 degrees Celsius.

Lisbon - 2017 Travelers' Choice

Lisbon is a city where you want to walk until night, it is full of stories, here you can easily get gastronomic ecstasy from local dishes or wine. This is the city where you want to stay.

Travel tip: bring comfortable shoes with you. Lisbon is located on a hilly area, and you need to walk a lot to avoid injuries, we do not recommend wearing shoes with heels or wedges.

Lisbon was the leader in attendance in 2017 among other European resorts, so do not hesitate, the Portuguese know a lot about service and hotel business, the hotels of Lisbon are proof of this. The city center is rich in various hotels, there are chic ones that are breathtaking in view, there are more modest ones. More often, tourists prefer to stay in hotels in the city center, and they will be discussed later.

Valverde hotel

Thanks to its great location on the famous Avenida da Liberdad, it is easy to see why the Valverde TripAdvisor ranks as the best hotel in Lisbon.

This stylish and welcoming hotel pleases from the first minutes of your stay. The work of the designer is felt in everything, the combination of modern works of art, antique furniture and rich fabrics delight.

Each room is unique in its own way. Perfectly sustained colors, wonderful views, a private balcony, TV and free Wi-Fi.

Breakfast is included in the room rate, allowing you to start the day with fine Portuguese cuisine at S√≠tiode Valverde Restaurant. A variety of dishes are offered each morning, and jazz sounds accompany the weekly Sunday brunch. If you prefer a meal outdoors, it is recommended to visit Pateo. The territory, filled with greenery, will allow you to conduct a business conversation, so relax with a drink by the pool.

Advice to the hotel guest: the hotel offers its guests massage services or a yacht tour of the Atlantic Ocean, take advantage of this wonderful addition!

Martinhal Lisbon Chiado Family Suites

Traveling with kids? For families there is an ideal option for cohabitation. The hotel offers studio apartments or rooms with two different rooms for parents and children. Of course, there is free Wi-Fi throughout the hotel. At the request of guests can be accommodated in a room with a living room.

Another feature of the hotel, for those who prefer to cook their own food while traveling, is their own kitchen, which is in every room. The small but cozy kitchen has everything you need: a stove, dishwasher, coffee maker, refrigerator and other kitchen utensils. When cooking, there is no desire, you can go to an excellent cafe in the hotel M Bar Family Caf√©.

This hotel option is well suited for those who need family apartments and the possibility of privacy from children. Parents will be absolutely calm, as their children are in safe hands.

Travel Parent Tip: The hotel offers a childcare service while their parents explore the city. Kids club is ready to entertain children until late at night and even organizes pajama children's parties.

Memmo Alfama - Design Hotels

If you want to stay in the heart of the old center of Lisbon, Memmo Alfama is one of the great places in this part of the city. Its peculiarity lies in the fact that the building in which the hotel is located was built in the 19th century. The highlight of the hotel is the panoramic terrace, which is located above terracotta roofs overlooking the Tagus River and the famous cathedral of the city.

Room design is a great combination of sophisticated furniture and modern technology. The room rate includes breakfast, in principle, this is offered by almost all Lisbon hotels. The hotel reviews are excellent, and you can often find food comments. Buffet breakfast with a wide choice of dishes, in the evening the restaurant serves local dishes.

In summer, when the temperature warms to maximum, the hotel invites guests to refresh themselves in the pool. This is a rather rare find in Lisbon, especially in combination with the view. On cooler winter nights, the pool is warmed to a comfortable temperature.

Advice to the guest: in reviews, tourists often mention small rooms. For two, we recommend booking rooms with a terrace.

Four Seasons Hotel Ritz Lisbon

This hotel has long won the magnificent reputation of one of the best hotels in Portugal. Lisbon is magnificent from the top of the hill, and it beckons to get acquainted with its unique architecture and nearby attractions.

Most rooms have a private veranda overlooking Edward VII Park, St. George's Castle, the Old Town, the Tagus River or the April 25 bridge. The design combination of historical charm and modern comfort creates a special atmosphere of tranquility, peace.

The daily breakfast buffet offers the stylish and chic Varanda Restaurant, combined with a view of the park. Superb dishes from chef Pascal Maynard, whose track record is full of prestigious Michelin-starred restaurants, once again confirms how good the hotel is.

Do not miss the Ritz summer bar with a terrace decorated with a magnificent tapestry created by the famous Portuguese artist Pedro Leitao. The main highlight of the bar is the piano with autographs of world celebrities, which gives the atmosphere a special, bohemian charm.

Delicate aromas and harmonious music create a special calm atmosphere in the Ritz SPA-salon, which sets the body and mind to rest and relaxation.

Advice for guests: when booking four nights, 5 nights - as a gift. Take advantage of the nice bonus!

Each room is individually decorated, has air conditioning, a minibar, good soundproofing, a flat-screen TV with cable channels. Featuring a shower, private bathroom also comes with a hairdryer and toiletries.
