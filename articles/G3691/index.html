How to find out the balance of a Sberbank card via SMS?

Many people use non-cash payment when paying for goods and services. Therefore, it is important to understand how to find out the balance of a Sberbank card. We will deal with this issue further. In fact, getting the information we are interested in is not difficult. Especially with proper preparation.

Solution methods

How to find out the balance of a Sberbank card? A similar problem has several solutions. And each person decides what to do. In any case, almost always the Sberbank client will receive an SMS report on the mobile device.

To request a plastic balance, you can:

  • make a purchase;
  • replenish card account;
  • request information at an ATM or payment terminal;
  • use the Mobile Bank application;
  • send an SMS request from a mobile phone;
  • send a USSD request;
  • Take advantage of online banking.

In any case, to solve the problem, it is recommended to pre-connect the option "Mobile Bank". With it, a person will be able to obtain information about the status of the plastic account via a mobile phone.

ATMs and terminals

First, find out how to find out the balance of a Sberbank card through an ATM or payment terminal. This is a fairly common trick. It does not require a Mobile Bank connection.

Instructions for sending a request will be as follows:

  1. Find a payment terminal or an Sberbank ATM.
  2. Insert a card into the device and enter the PIN code from it.
  3. Select "Card Transactions". This item is located in the ATM main menu.
  4. Go to "Request / Check Balance".
  5. Indicate the method of obtaining information. For example, ‚ÄúDisplay on screen‚Äù or ‚ÄúPrint receipt‚Äù.
  6. To get acquainted with the received data. In the first case, the balance will be displayed on the display of the device, in the second - it will be printed on the receipt.

Internet banking and verification

For example, you can connect to the Sberbank Online service. This resource will allow you to manage a bank card without leaving your home. You can activate your profile on Sberbank Online:

  • in any branch of "Sberra";
  • through the terminals or ATMs of the company;
  • on the official page of Internet banking.

After a person has a profile on Sberbank Online, he will just have to go to the service with his username and password. Information on available cards and their balance will appear on the main page of the portal. If this does not happen, it is recommended to look in the "My Account" section.

SMS request and check

How to find out the balance of a Sberbank card via SMS? A similar function will be available only after the activation of Mobile Bank. Suppose this step is completed. What's next?

To send an SMS request, the user will need:

  1. Take the phone tied to plastic and open the "New message" function in it.
  2. Write in the text: "Balance last_4_digits_maps". You do not need to put quotation marks.
  3. Send a request to number 900.

That's all. It remains to wait a few minutes. A message will be sent to the mobile device from which the request was made with the current status of the account.

Important: as a code word, you can use Balans, Balance, Balance, Ostatok.

Digital Query and Messages

How to find out the balance of a Sberbank card via phone? You can use another SMS request. It is not much different from the previous form.

The thing is that to quickly query the status of the card account, you can write "01" instead of code words and plastic numbers. You do not need to put quotation marks. Send the resulting message to 900 short number. You can wait for the request to complete processing. After that, the client will receive a message about how much money he has on the card.

USSD requests

How to find out the balance of a Sberbank card by phone? The next approach to solving this problem is to send a USSD request. The operation is extremely simple, even a schoolboy can handle it.

The algorithm of actions for translating ideas into reality will have the following form:

  1. Open the dialing mode on your mobile phone.
  2. Print the command * 900 * 01 #.
  3. Press the button responsible for calling the subscriber.

In response, the person will receive an SMS in which the current status of the account will be written. If a citizen has several Sberra cards, the request will be changed. In this case, you will have to use the combination * 900 * 01 * 4_final_digits_plastic #.

The program "Mobile Bank"

But this is not all the available options for the development of events. The thing is that customers can request a balance through the Mobile Bank program.

The step-by-step process will look as follows:

  1. Install Mobile Bank application on your phone.
  2. Log in to the program.
  3. Select "Card Transactions".
  4. Click on the "Balance" button.
  5. Confirm procedure.

In fact, everything is not as difficult as it seems. But such a technique is rarely used in practice. Therefore, few people know about it.

Make purchases

How to find out the balance of a Sberbank card? A simple and fairly common technique is to make a purchase with plastic. It is necessary to act on condition that the Mobile Bank is connected.

The thing is that after passing the payment, the client will receive a notification about the amount of the transaction, as well as with the balance of the account. If the Mobile Bank option is disabled, the notification simply will not come.

We replenish the account

Another method is replenishment of bank plastic. Then a person can easily answer how to find out the balance of a Sberbank card. Consider the procedure for the transfer of funds through an ATM.

To do this, you must:

  1. Insert the plastic into the ATM and indicate the PIN from it.
  2. Select the function "Card operations" - "Deposit" in the main menu of the ATM.
  3. Deposit money into the receiver. You can put several bills into it at once.
  4. Confirm money transfer.

As soon as funds are credited to the account, the cardholder will receive an SMS notification. It will contain information about the amount of replenishment of plastic and the current status of the account.
