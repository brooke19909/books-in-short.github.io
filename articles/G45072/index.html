Hotel Description Fujairah Rotana Resort 5 *

Description: The cozy Fujairah Rotana Resort 5 hotel is an embodiment of oriental flavor and modern comfort. Located along the hillside of Khojar, it is located off the coast of the Indian Ocean. The building is literally immersed in the greenery of tropical vegetation, rare shrubs and flowers.



The hotel was built in 2007 and decorated in a luxurious colonial style. The overall design of the hotel includes nine comfortable 5 and 2-story buildings. The main four-story building is the center of the cultural and entertainment life of vacationers, all events are held here, there are playgrounds, gyms, a club, etc.

Near the Fujairah Rotana Resort 5 is located the picturesque bay of Al Aqah, which makes this vacation spot attractive for naturalists - lovers of the unique beauty of wildlife. Also, the hotel will appeal to couples with children. The complex is located 45 km from Fujairah and 140 km from the airport located in Dubai.



Rooms: Fujairah Rotana Resort 5 has 250 apartments with sea views. Their interiors, combining Arabic motifs and colonial style, are made using warm colors, natural materials, elegant furniture and exquisite decorations. The rooms are equipped with telephone, air conditioning, balcony, Internet, mini-bar, television, safe, folding bed. Cleaning and linen change is daily.

Meals: Fujairah Rotana Resort 5 offers its guests a 1, 2-time visit to the restaurant (to choose from). All Inclusive mode meals are also possible . The main restaurant, designed for 315 people, offers international dishes. There is a bar on the beach, in the lobby and around the pool. Beach: Fujairah Rotana Resort has a sandy beach with a length of 300 meters. Vacationers can use mattresses, umbrellas, towels and sun loungers for free.

Additional information: The complex has an outdoor pool, two elevators, a tennis court, four conference rooms, currency exchange, parking, a business center, hairdresser, laundry, fitness club, sauna. Tourists can enjoy the following activities: beach volleyball, shuttle bass, table tennis, squash. The hotel has a doctor. The resort complex has a base of water sports, you can do sailing, windsurfing, kayaking, parasailing, bananasailing, deep sea fishing, safari, diving. For younger travelers, there is a children's pool, a club, animation, a playground. Cots are available upon request. Babysitting service is available.

The staff is friendly, attentive and helpful, constantly ready to help and resolve any issues. The spa is chic, you can take a subscription at a discount. A magnificent playground, there is something to keep the child busy all day. The food is not bad, fruits were served constantly, pleased with seafood dishes, drinks and ice cream. Due to the fact that the hotel is located away from the bustle of the city, the rest turned out to be magnificent, nothing distracted, did not interfere.
