Hotel Possidi Holidays Resort Hotel 5 * (Greece, Halkidiki): description of the hotel and leisure, reviews

In our article we want to talk about one of the Greek hotels on the peninsula of Halkidiki. Possidi Holidays Resort Hotel 5 * is a wonderful complex for a good and carefree holiday on the beach.

Location

Possidi Holidays Resort Hotel 5 * is located in the Possidi area on the sea coast, 105 kilometers from the Macedonia Airport, 22 kilometers from Kallithea. The hotel is a complex of buildings: a two-story main building and eight two-story bungalows. It was built in 1992, the last update was made in 2002. The complex offers its guests a variety of rooms for a comfortable and carefree holiday.

Halkidiki in September

In late August, the velvet season begins. It is even possible that this is the most favorable period for visiting these beautiful places, since the weather is still warm, but there is no too extreme heat, and the sea is very warm.

Halkidiki in September is conducive not only to relaxation, but also to sightseeing. This is made possible thanks to a pleasant and mild temperature. During this period, it is within
+25 - +29, and at night it drops to eighteen degrees. In the evenings, warm sweaters or light jackets are required. They are certainly worth taking with you from home. In September, there are no strong winds here, as in Rhodes or Crete, sometimes there are only light breezes, making it much easier to breathe than in summer. The sea temperature in September also drops slightly. At the beginning of the month it is equal to 25 degrees, and then approaches 23 degrees.

What is interesting on the peninsula in September?

In the summer, the main entertainment on the peninsula is swimming, games, relaxing on the beach, water activities on the most beautiful sandy beaches. In September, excursions can be added to all this. Halkidiki is a rather large peninsula in area, and there are a lot of different sights here, therefore, going to the resort in the fall, think over the cultural program in advance. And upon arrival at the Possidi Holidays Resort Suites Hotel 5 * consult with local guides which of the most interesting places are nearby.



Of the huge number of attractions, you can safely select the highest point of the entire peninsula - the famous Mount Athos, the cave of Petralons with amazingly stalagmites and stalactites in shape, Meteors, which are considered the eighth wonder of the world.

sights

Possidi is located in the picturesque region of Greece. It is believed that he got his name from Poseidon. Once there was a temple to this god. Its remains can now be seen on Cape Possidi. Currently, the village is a wonderful place to relax with long sandy beaches extending beyond the horizon, which are awarded the Blue Flag.

Possidi Holidays Resort Hotel 5 * (Halkidiki, Kassandra) is located at some distance from the village. This makes the place quiet and relaxing. To see the nearest attractions of Halkidiki, you can take excursion tours of local guides or rent a car at the hotel, which greatly simplifies the movement.

One of the noteworthy places is the Meteora monasteries, which are a whole complex of 24 buildings, reaching a height of 600 meters above sea level, located at the very top of steep cliffs. It is difficult to convey the stunning beauty of this place with words. No wonder it is listed as a UNESCO World Heritage Site. It is difficult to imagine now, but the hermits conquered these rocks in search of solitude long before the tenth century. Each of the 24 monasteries is a work of art that has its own amazing history, unique architecture, frescoes and murals, icons.



Of no less interest to tourists is Mount Athos, located in the eastern part of the peninsula, washed by the Aegean Sea. Orthodox people revere this place as the earthly abode of the Virgin. The mountain can only be visited by men with a special visa, and women are generally prohibited from entering. However, monasteries can be seen from the sea on a pleasure boat.

Many tourists are interested in Mount Olympus. And this is not surprising. It is a national park of Greece. Beautiful views of these places are fascinating. Each vacationer considers it his duty to visit this mountain. Guides organize hiking trips here.

A lot of pilgrims flock to the Tempi Valley, who seek to see the cave Temple of St. Paraskeva, located at the foot of Olympus. Near the temple there is an underground lake with a source. The whole Tempi Valley is simply buried in the greenery of centennial plane trees, there are many sources with healing properties.

Tourists will be interested in visiting the thermal resort of Loutraki with springs, the temperature of which is 37 degrees year-round. It is surrounded by green forests, rocky cliffs and deep gorges. Loutraki spa resort is very famous; on its territory, pools and baths with thermal water are open 24 hours a day.

In addition, vacationers can visit the cave of Petralion, the city of Thessaloniki, the Byzantine Museum, the city and lake Orchid, Ancient Dion, the Platamonas Fortress.
