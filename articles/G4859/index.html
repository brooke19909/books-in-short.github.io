How to make crafts for Monster High dolls with your own hands?

Recently, Monster High dolls stand out among the toys for girls. These characters are also the heroes of a series of books, cartoons that girls around the world like so much.

Like any other doll, these also need to have their own house, their own room, their own furniture. Let's try to make crafts for dolls. Photos of various items are presented below. There are many variations of such products. We have selected several.

Cupboard

So, how to make crafts for dolls? Like any doll, Monster High has many outfits. Therefore, you just need a wardrobe. To make it you will need:

  • white cardboard - 1 sheet;
  • color paper (red and black) - 2 sheets;
  • drawing accessories;
  • scissors;
  • glue.

On a sheet of cardboard we measure 2 cm above and below, draw a line, on it we put 6 cm on the sides, draw another line. Through vertical lines from above, draw a line at a distance of 4 cm. On it, from the center to the edges, lay 3 cm.





Then we connect this point with the lower and upper points on this vertical line. We wipe all auxiliary strokes from the sides, mark the places for gluing the shelves and strips for gluing.

Now we will prepare the cabinet doors. To do this, draw a hexagon similar to the first. Gluing strips will only be on the side. Then divide the hexagon in half. Next, we carry out a drawing of the sides, the upper part of the cabinet and the bottom also with gluing strips. It remains to cut out the details along the intended lines and glue them.

Before gluing the doors, you must first "install" the shelves. We fix two ribbons on the doors that will act as a lock. Now the cabinet needs to be decorated: inside we glue it with black paper, and outside - red.

High chair

How to create DIY crafts for Monster High dolls? Let's look at an example of a highchair. Now let's make it for the doll. To make it, you need to prepare:

  • ice cream sticks;
  • a piece of wood;
  • sticks for legs and back;
  • carpentry and drawing accessories.

You will also need white paint and a Black Rose sticker.

Grind a piece of wood with sandpaper. Next, we outline the holes for the legs and insert the sticks, pre-processing them with sandpaper. On the other hand, we will make two holes for the back, insert ice cream sticks, glue it. We paint the chair with white paint, and when it dries, we glue the rose.

Bed

What else can you make crafts for dolls "Monster High" with your own hands? Now we will make a bed and bed linen.

For crafts, prepare:

  • fabric (black and pink);
  • black lace;
  • black beads;
  • synthetic winterizer;
  • cardboard;
  • cardboard box;
  • sewing supplies.

First, we cut out a blank for the back from cardboard, tighten it with a pink cloth, decorate with beads. Now we will make a pillow: we sew a pillowcase from pink fabric, we stuff with a synthetic winterizer, we sew it up.

We begin to make the bed: we tighten the box with a pink cloth. Then we sew lace on the sides, we stuff the top a little with padding polyester. After we tighten the fabric from the bottom, fasten, attach the back.

We sew a blanket of pink fabric, stuff it with a padding polyester, sew it up, decorate it at our discretion with lace. The berth for the toy is ready.

Armchair

What else can I do for the Monster High dolls with my own hands? For example, a chair. For work we take:

  • fleece of different colors;
  • synthetic winterizer;
  • threads
  • cardboard;
  • sewing supplies;
  • glue;
  • plastic ball;
  • white beads.

First, the ball is divided into two parts. We take one part, put the synthetic winterizer inside. We fit on top with a green cloth, inside - pink. The junction is decorated with beads. From a cardboard we cut out a circle, we tighten it with a green cloth, decorate along the edge with a green thread. We connect the parts of the chair, decorate the junction.

That's all, DIY Monster High dolls are ready-made. You can use them during games with friends.

Conclusion

Now you know how to make crafts for dolls, we have provided you with photos of some products. Such products, assembled from improvised means with their own hands, are very popular. The connection of these dolls with the other world requires an appropriate design of everything that is connected with them. Therefore, all the crafts will be quite real if they are decorated with small skulls, coffins, black roses.

"School of monsters," as the name of the dolls is translated, will allow the child to plunge into the world of good "horror", where mysticism and a certain mystery rule. This is the new modern world of a child of the 21st century. The characters in this school are eccentric and have many fans. With them, the child embarks on incredible, mystical travels. In addition, the outward pallor and bizarreness does not diminish the kindness and compassion of these characters. Therefore, for girls, these dolls are in great demand.
