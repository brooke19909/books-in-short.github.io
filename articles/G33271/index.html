Ozer Park Hotel 3 * (Turkey / Kemer / Beldibi): description, photos and reviews of tourists

The history of the hotel began in 2000, when the first tourists spent their holidays in it. Since then, minor repairs were carried out on its territory and in the building, aimed at improving the technical and furniture equipment. Location

Ozer Park Hotel 3 * is located in Kemer, near the sea coast. According to the guests, the Mediterranean coast is clean, and the water in it is warm for a rather long period. The central part of Kemer, where there is a huge amount of entertainment designed for tourists of different ages, is fifteen kilometers from this place. Many experienced tourists recommend newly-made hotel guests to rent a car or bicycle, which is possible at Ozer Park Hotel 3 *, and go on them on a short trip to entertainment places and attractions.

Rooms

The hotel has only fifty apartments, which are presented in one category - standard. As guests in the Kemer Ozer Park Hotel 3 * note in each of the reviews, in each of them the interior is decorated in bright colors that allow you to relax and perfectly relax in a relaxed atmosphere.

All apartments have modern air conditioning, which in case of hot air temperature outdoors cools it indoors and vice versa. In addition, the rooms have a bathroom, where in addition to the place for bathing, there is a bathroom and a washbasin. If desired, tourists can use the bath accessories that are provided here for each guest.

All apartments have a private balcony, where there is a small set of furniture - a pair of chairs and a small table. Travelers who rested here note the beautiful view of the sea and gardens that opens from here. You can also have intimate conversations and drink your favorite hot drinks in the evening.



Interior

Each room is equipped with a set of modern furniture, which is designed for a couple of guests. If necessary, a third bed can be provided for a third guest, presented in the form of a folding bed of a European type. The main bed, which is available here, is a double bed, it is also made in accordance with European standards and is covered with cotton white and brown linen. Here, a pair of nightstands is attached to the bed, a dressing table with a mirror and a large wardrobe.

In the room you can order the installation of a safe, minibar or telephone. There is a small TV on which vacationers can watch their favorite shows.



Living conditions for children

Many tourists recommend having a rest with children in the Ozer Park Hotel 3 * hotel. Such recommendations are due to the presence of a number of conditions designed for a comfortable pastime for both kids and their parents.

On request, a cot can be accommodated in any room for guests between the ages of four and twelve.

In the open area there is a small niche with water, equipped in accordance with all safety requirements. The restaurant has small chairs designed for a comfortable stay for children.



Nutrition

A huge number of reviews of guests of the hotel Ozer Park Hotel 3 * (Beldibi / Kemer) relates to food. The concept of food in this place provides for a visit to a buffet, which is covered in the main restaurant of the hotel. Guests talk about the amazing taste of dishes prepared by chefs working in the kitchen. Culinary specialists here can surprise gourmets with food that is traditional for Turkish and European cuisines.

If desired, guests can visit the bar, which is located near the pool. As a rule, its clients are tourists resting in the sun terrace. They can offer a wide selection of snacks and desserts, as well as a considerable list of soft drinks, among which there are both alcoholic and non-alcoholic.



Sport

Lovers of an active lifestyle can afford to monitor their body, even while relaxing on holiday at the Ozer Park Hotel 3 *. For such purposes, a small gym is arranged in which several simulators are installed, which are designed to work on different parts of the body.

Table tennis fans often gather in rooms specially equipped for such a game. On the beach there is an excellent volleyball court, where teams of guests often compete.

If desired, guests of Ozer Park Hotel 3 * (Turkey) can learn extreme water sports, among which surfing and water skiing are very popular. For such purposes, several professionally trained instructors work here, who for an additional fee will help in acquiring the necessary skills.

One of them is the provision of vehicles for rent. Using this opportunity, guests have the right to a free parking space for their car, which is allocated in the open, but guarded territory of the hotel.

On a free basis, all guests can use access to the Internet, and throughout the Ozer Park Hotel 3 *. Aquazone

A very popular place among guests is the aquazone, which occupies a rather impressive area throughout the hotel. It is represented by a combination of a large sun terrace, pool and bar.

On the terrace, everything is arranged for a more comfortable stay for guests. Here, a whole series of sun loungers with beach umbrellas is presented for them, where you can sit quite well and enjoy the sun's rays. If necessary, during breaks between water procedures, guests have the opportunity to visit the bar, which offers a large selection of soft drinks.

In the very center of the terrace there is a medium-sized swimming pool with one rather high water slide installed - guests of different ages like to frolic on it. The water here is heated only under the influence of sunlight, in connection with which, in winter, this site is closed to visitors - the water in this period is cold for swimming.



Beach

The hotel has its own plot along the coastline, which is very well decorated with everything you need for a good holiday. For comfortable accommodation of tourists, a number of deck chairs and an equal number of umbrellas are exhibited here. Tourists recommend taking towels when leaving the hotel and, if desired, mattresses, since they cannot be handed out on the beach under any circumstances.

In their reviews of the hotel, tourists note that in order to get to the beach, it is necessary to overcome a certain distance - about 300 meters. The shore here, they say, is quite clean and gentle, and the water remains warm for a long period.

Hotel Policies

All tourists who relax in the Ozer Park Hotel 3 *, as in the case of staying in other places of this type, must follow the rules binding on all. First of all, they relate to the accommodation of tourists with animals - this is strictly prohibited here. Otherwise, the tourist, even if he has permits, may be denied accommodation.

In addition, all apartments are non-smoking. Violators are subject to financial recovery in accordance with the legislation established in Turkey.

The hotel does not provide facilities for people who have some physical disabilities - rooms for this category are simply not provided here.



Prices

The pricing policy at the hotel pleases many travelers. Here, according to guests from Russia, you can relax pretty well, acquiring a ticket, the cost of which is in the range of 40-50 thousand rubles. At this price, a tour is provided for a weekly stay of two guests in standard apartments. This price without fail includes a two-way flight for two. Upon arrival at Turkey Airport, a modern car that is equipped with air conditioning will pick up guests. According to them, a rather long road runs quickly and in comfortable conditions.

Prices for additional services are also in great demand. In particular, to store a small amount of valuables here you can rent a safe at a rate of 120 rubles per day of use.
