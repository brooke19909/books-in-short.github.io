October Square of Yekaterinburg. From the Exile Sloboda to Yekaterinburg City

Oktyabrskaya Square in Yekaterinburg, which can rightfully be called the embankment, is a favorite place for youth to spend time. The spacious territory allows extreme sportsmen to gather here. Rollers, skateboards and bicycles are the favorite means of transportation along the city pond. But the area is interesting for both less-speed citizens and for city guests.

The exile settlement

That is what this place was once called. Outskirts, where people settled, who came to work in the Ural factories. Maybe they were immigrants, and possibly convicts. Factories needed any workforce.

Yekaterinburg developed rapidly, and soon, already at the beginning of the 19th century, the Exiled settlement became not the outskirts, but the center of the city. The area was already considered prestigious, wealthy merchants and industrialists now settled here. Constructed shops and benches, opened various workshops.

October Square in Yekaterinburg

In the early 1970s, a draft of the new square of the city was developed. According to the architects, she had to carry a cultural and administrative burden. It was decided to place a complex of structures on the shore of the pond, for which it was necessary to demolish the dilapidated housing stock. The model prepared for the demonstration of the complex displays the building of the Regional Committee of the CPSU, the Drama Theater, and a well-equipped embankment.

Today, all structures take their places not on project documentation, but on October Square in Yekaterinburg.

The building of the Sverdlovsk Drama Theater balances the composition of the square. It appeared only twenty years later, but harmoniously blended into the surrounding landscape.

A typical design was taken as the basis, but the architects finalized it taking into account local requirements. Very quickly, the townspeople accepted the fact that the drama theater is located in the same place as the October Square in Yekaterinburg, and gave it a second name - Theater.

In the 80s of the last century, a square was set up here. The dominant was the fountain "Shar" - the work of V. Loskutov. They completed construction in the late eighties, and he immediately revived the entire territory of the square and the embankment. The work was awarded the prize of G.V. de Gennin, the founder of Yekaterinburg. The fountain is a round platform - a bowl located in the recess. In its center is a ball, to which wide steps descend. The design as a whole is a bit like an arena. A ball is the Earth washed by clean jets of water.

Not surprisingly, the square has become the most visited holiday destination. Here, sports enthusiasts, theater-goers, and those who want to sit by the cool water, and children jumping at the fountain find classes for themselves.

Yekaterinburg City. The future of the city

The development of the exile settlement continues at the present time. In the process of transforming Oktyabrskaya Square in Yekaterinburg, a design of the business center of the city was developed. It is being built at the site of the demolition of the remaining dilapidated houses. It will consist of five skyscrapers, the construction of one of them is being completed. The good news is that with such grandiose plans, the architects managed to preserve several historical monuments of the Exiled Settlement.
