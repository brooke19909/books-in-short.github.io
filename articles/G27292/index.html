The stadium "Labor" in Togliatti: address and mode of operation

New sports centers are opened regularly in the cities of the country and old ones are being reconstructed. Thanks to this, the population gets the opportunity to lead a healthy lifestyle, and athletes engage in and prepare in modern stadiums. After the reconstruction, the Trud in Togliatti is visited by many citizens. Both sports fans and professionals are trained here. Many young athletes begin their careers here. The updated complex inspires, giving desire, strives for better results.

General information

The stadium "Trud" (Tolyatti) boasts a rich history. It appeared in 1955, and began to receive visitors from 1965. Its main purpose was to provide sports services to the population, as well as to conduct various sports events. As far back as the last century, a large-scale construction became one of the most significant in the country. There were classes and training in hockey, basketball, volleyball, football, figure skating. The sections were in great demand. Among them were rare types of dispute - javelin and nucleus throwing.

Already in our time, the complex decided to upgrade. The reconstruction made the Trud stadium in Tolyatti even better. The center provides citizens with new, modern services. It has become a venue for sporting events.

A couple of years ago, a grand opening took place after the reconstruction of a sports facility, which was timed to coincide with an important day - Athlete's Day. The event brought together not only the townspeople - honorary figures and honored sports veterans honored the opening of the center with their presence. The townspeople blew up the opening with genuine applause. The new complex received a platform with a visor for 500 spectators, a volleyball, basketball and other active sports field. A new football field was opened, six tracks for athletes, areas for jumping and shot put.

Stadium "Trud" (Tolyatti), address

The sports complex is well known to many people in the city. The construction is located on Karl Marx Street, Building 37. Next to it is the Sports Palace and Freedom Square. Nearest bus stop "Labor Stadium". A minibus number 141 goes to it. The complex can be reached differently - get off at the Freedom Square stop, at which the following stops:

  • Buses 22 *, 22, 73.
  • Minibuses 96, 108, 328.

Mode of operation

The stadium "Labor" (Togliatti) is open every day. It is open from 6.00 in the morning until 00.00. On the official pages on social networks and by phone, you can find out in more detail all the necessary information.

Additional features

In the modern sports area there is not only an open football field and treadmills, but also an indoor complex. Athletes enjoy sports at any time of the year, maintaining their health.

The pool at the stadium "Trud" (Togliatti) is very popular. At the disposal of visitors are 6 swimming lanes 25 meters long. One visit costs 120 rubles. When buying a subscription, visits cost from 80 to 150 rubles - check the cost at the box office.

In the center, professional trainers teach figure skating, taekwondo, freestyle wrestling, rowing, and scuba diving. You can enroll in the children's section through a school or center at the beginning of the school year. Children are taught by masters with extensive experience, professional athletes.
