Train station Anapa: address, tickets, timetable

Anapa Railway Station is one of the dead-end stations on the North Caucasus Railway. Most of the trains run here only in the summertime, when tourists from all over the country head south to enjoy a wonderful vacation. There is no suburban communication as such, since there is a well-developed bus network, and it is generally possible to walk to some settlements.

Anapa





The railway station in Anapa is one of the most important infrastructure facilities in the city; 3-4 million passengers pass through it annually. Most tourists come here, of course, in the summer, when everyone wants to relax and forget about the difficulties accumulated over the year. The Krasnodar Territory is very picturesque in the summer, and Anapa is especially beautiful in the evening, when the sound of the sea and a little wind create a romantic atmosphere here.

The city is located on the Black Sea and is one of the most popular resorts in Russia. About 70 thousand people permanently live here, all of them are somehow engaged in the tourism sector: some rent housing to vacationers, others organize vacations on the beach, and others are engaged in transportation. Prices for food and housing here are quite reasonable, the main thing is to reach an agreement with the owners on time, because during the tourist season private houses, rooms in hotels and mini-hotels are bought up quite quickly.

Railway station





Railway station "Anapa", whose address Privokzalnaya street, 1, works around the clock, receiving and sending trains. The station is a dead end: trains don't go anywhere further, so at the landing you can take your time and calmly get your things out of the train. The station and the station were opened in 1978, in 2005 they underwent partial reconstruction and acquired a completely new look.

The building of the station provides all the services necessary for the passenger: a hotel, a cafe, a relaxation room are operating, waiting rooms operate around the clock. Pay attention to the fact that there is a room for mother and child, so if you suddenly need to swaddle a baby, you can use it.

Train station schedule





Anapa waits for tourists all year round, so the station works without breaks and weekends. There are three ticket offices in the building where you can purchase a ticket, as well as vending machines for printing travel documents. One box office operates around the clock with lunch breaks, the other two only in the daytime. However, many tourists have long begun to purchase electronic tickets on the Russian Railways website, where you can even choose a convenient carriage and location.

You should pay attention to the fact that the station is located at a distance of 7 kilometers from the city itself and you will need to get there by public transport or taxi. Therefore, try to choose a train with such an arrival time so that you can leave for the city without any problems.

Public transport

If you do not want to use taxi services, pay attention to public transport that connects Anapa Train Station with the city. We are talking about bus routes 100,113,120,127 and 129. Most of them are transit, the 120th route runs between the railway and the bus station of the city. Using route No. 113, you can get to the airport from the train station, bypassing the paid transfers offered by travel companies.

You can also use the services of a fixed-route taxi, which will take you anywhere, however, this option is not suitable for everyone, because it is not cheap. Some locals are engaged in private carriage and offer to use their vehicles. This is cheaper than regular taxi services, but if the traffic police stop the car, it may violate your plans.

Where can I go from here?





Anapa railway station is the starting point of routes operating year-round and connecting the city with Moscow, Tomsk, Krasnoyarsk. There is also the Anapa-Minsk route, running all year round, it is actively used by residents of Belarus who want to relax on the Black Sea.

There are a number of trains that run only in the summertime according to a special schedule. Additional flights are assigned to Moscow, Severobaykalsk, Irkutsk, St. Petersburg, Tynda, Samara, Kirov, Smolensk and many other cities of Russia. The exact schedule can be clarified at any railway ticket office in any region.
