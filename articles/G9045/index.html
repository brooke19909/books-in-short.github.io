Smart watches for children - reviews, models, specifications and manufacturers

Parents, letting the child go for a walk, to school or to the section, constantly come up with some terrible things that can happen to him. Fears for the most dear little man entail the emergence of a desire to know where he is and what is happening to him at the moment. Due to the fact that it is impossible to constantly be near your child, you can solve the problem with the help of modern gadgets that provide control that the child himself does not even know about.





Smart Watch Features

The independence of young children brings great joy to their parents, but it often borders on emotions and anxiety. Today, many devices have been invented that help maintain communication between parents and the child. These include smart watches for children, reviews of which are different. This miracle of technology has many interesting functions for the baby and makes it possible to reliably control your child to moms and dads.

Now many buyers are looking at reviews which smart watches are better for children. The opinion of other parents, of course, is important, but because if some child liked this model, this does not mean that it is perfect and others will like it. Before choosing the right model, you should understand the capabilities of these gadgets.

Smart watches are a real find for caring and loving parents. In some models, the functions may differ, but absolutely all of them allow you to do such things:

  • track the location of their owner;
  • Receive notifications when the watch is removed from the hand;
  • find out the route of movement of the baby;
  • mark several points on the map (school, section, house) and receive alerts when a child leaves the borders of these places;
  • make a secret call, during which a microphone is automatically connected to the device, and parents can hear what is happening around it.

Gadgets are beneficial not only to mothers and fathers, but also to the children themselves. For example, the owner of such a watch can at any time find out information about the time, see and answer incoming messages, make and receive calls, and also press the alarm button in case of danger. Thanks to this, positive reviews about smart watches for children come not only from parents, but also from kids.



Why are they better than a smartphone?

Any modern phone can handle more than just calls. And even the cheapest device has a GPS module. Therefore, you can turn a gadget into a tracker in a matter of minutes. Many reviews of smart watches for children suggest that they have several advantages over modern smartphones. The main ones are:

  • the watch is harder to lose, because it is firmly fixed on the hand, but the phone always accidentally disappears from the pocket of your trousers or backpack;
  • on the watch, the charge lasts much longer than on a smartphone, and you will need to charge them no more than once every 5 days;
  • the creators of the device did not foresee the presence of games in them, so the child will spend less time in the virtual world.

All these advantages do not mean at all that smart watches can completely replace the phone. Although it should be noted that for a child of primary school age they will be a more profitable option.



Principle of operation

Reviews of smart watches for children with GPS are often received about their work. In fact, they function quite simply. In a small case, there is a board, a battery for power, as well as a GPS module, which is responsible for determining the location through communication with satellites. There are models on the market that catch the signals of GLONASS satellites or specify the location via Wi-Fi networks and data from mobile communication towers. In addition, the watch provides a slot for a SIM card, which is the main means of transmitting information to a computer or smartphone of parents.

In order to get the maximum benefit and leave only positive reviews about smart watches for children, you should pay attention to the following points when choosing:

  • Accuracy of determination of coordinates. It is advisable to choose the tracker with the highest rate. Advanced devices can determine location with an accuracy of only 3-5 meters. Some models show the location of the baby with an accuracy of 15 meters.
  • The quality of signal reception from satellites. This parameter is very important, since anything can block the transmission. Of course, the manufacturer himself does not indicate this in the description, but customer reviews of smart watches for children will help to understand better.
  • The presence of a panic button. This feature is necessary for hyper-caring parents. When a button is pressed, a notification is sent to their device indicating the location coordinates of the child.
  • Record the route of movement. Many models automatically fill out the "walk log". This feature allows parents to check if they have missed something during the day.
  • The speed of the sensor. Its definition is far from necessary in all cases, although sometimes it can even become indispensable.
  • The presence of a microphone and speakers. They help make and receive calls from strictly defined numbers.
  • Impact resistance and moisture resistance. A significant advantage of the watch is especially necessary for overly active children who can easily fall from any height or catch on their hands, damaging the device.
  • Battery capacity. This nuance determines the frequency of charging the device.




The best models

In the market of children's watches, interesting models are presented in a rather wide assortment. All of them have similar functionality, but differ in the untwisted manufacturer and advanced features. Below are the leading products, as well as parental reviews of smart watches for children.

Q50

The absolute leader in sales is especially popular, which is why you can find many fakes on the market. Therefore, in order not to run into a fake device, it is recommended to purchase it in the official store. The cost of the device is $ 30.

This model is on sale in various color options. It is made of high-quality plastic, equipped with a telephone function, and weighs only 40 g. Parents can easily call their child for hours, however paradoxical it sounds, and he can accept this call and talk via speakerphone.

On the side panel there are buttons: SOS, receiving an incoming call, as well as a couple of keys for dialing pre-entered numbers. The device provides the ability to secretly call parents to the child.



The device is equipped with a hand-held sensor, due to which a notification is received on the devices of the parents. At the same time, the watch has a pedometer, an alarm clock, a function for tracking the phase of sleep, as well as recording the history of the baby's movements.

The backlit monochrome screen has a diagonal of 0.96 inches. Thanks to the 400 mAh battery, the device can function for about 100 hours in standby mode.

Reviews

Positive reviews about the Q50 smartwatch for children can be found quite often. Parents like the ability to control their child at any time, and the children themselves are attracted by the appearance of the device. In addition, customers respond positively to the function of making calls, so you can always keep in touch and remind each other of any important events.

Of the shortcomings, difficulties in tuning and an insufficiently capacious battery are sometimes noted. Although, despite this, the model suits parents and children, therefore, due to these minor drawbacks, it does not lose its popularity.

Dokiwatch

An advanced version of a watch worth about $ 180 pleasantly surprises customers with its capabilities. In this case, full compliance of price and quality is noted, so there are practically no complaints about the device.

The device is equipped with a color touch screen. With it, you can make voice as well as video calls. In addition, if desired, the owner of the device can configure safe zones and, when crossing their borders, receive notifications.

The main advantage of the model is a thoughtful fitness application. It enables parents to monitor the physical form of their child.



The model is perfect for children 7-12 years old. This watch is considered a simplified version of the device for adults, because it has a number of additional functions.

What do children like?

Reviews of the smartwatch for kids indicate the following benefits:

  • correspondence of cost and quality;
  • the ability to search for a contact in the phone book using touch control;
  • interesting appearance;
  • wide range of colors.

No shortcomings were found for this device, therefore, negative reviews are not being received in its direction.

Smart Baby Watch Q80

A model with a color touch screen will cost customers about $ 45-50. The watch has an alarm button, an alarm clock, as well as a promotion function. Along with this, they allow the child to receive and send messages. The device is also equipped with a hand-held sensor and anti-loss function. The diagonal of the screen is 1.22 inches, which greatly simplifies management and further improves the appearance of the watch.

The device independently records all the movements of the child and stores this information for a month. If the baby leaves a certain area, then parents will be notified about this.



Comments

Among the reviews about the smart watches Smart Watch for children you can find both positive and negative opinions. First of all, buyers indicate the quality of the device, its smart work, as well as the accuracy of determining the coordinates.

Unfortunately, in addition to advantages, parents often point out disadvantages. Their number, of course, is less, but still they are. These include: poor sound quality and not too good autonomy. Otherwise, there are no complaints about the device, and the price is quite consistent with the functions performed.
