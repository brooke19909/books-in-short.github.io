Erisson TVs: manufacturer and reviews

The Erisson trademark is owned by the Kaliningrad company Telebalt. In 1999, this young enterprise began with the assembly of televisions for the well-known world brands Samsung, Toshiba, Philips, Hyundai. Today, almost one third of all Russian televisions are produced at the Telebalt plants.

Manufacturer

The company is one of the leading Russian trading brands of consumer electronics. Its main specialization is TV-technology, however, the assortment also includes such household goods as coffee makers, microwave ovens, air conditioners, air grills, mixers, kettles, vacuum cleaners, heaters, meat grinders and much more. Only high-quality imported components from Matsushita, Sanyo, Philips, Thomson and others are used in the production. Thanks to this, the range of LCD and plasma TVs Erisson is constantly updated.

The buyer is offered a choice of models with a diagonal from 15 to 60 inches, which support Full HD or HD ready resolution, with different functionality and in different designs.

Why choose an Erisson TV?

The manufacturer has a number of advantages that provide the brand leadership positions in the Russian market:

  • extensive experience in the production of reliable modern TVs;
  • careful control at numerous stages of production;
  • high quality of components;
  • access to advanced technologies through established contacts with leading global manufacturers;
  • use of modern equipment on assembly lines;

The company regularly releases new models of TVs, so there are products in the assortment that meet the latest standards in the television market.

With a wide range and quality, Erisson TVs, according to customer reviews, please with their moderate cost. Many buyers note the company's operational services.
