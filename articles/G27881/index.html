"RosMoney": reviews. RosMoney - microfinance organization

The activities of microfinance institutions in Russia are aimed at providing urgent financial assistance to Russian citizens within the limited amount of funds for a short period of time. As a rule, applications for a microloan in MFIs do not have refusals, since the high interest rate fully compensates for unforeseen delays in loans. One of these credit services is RosMoney. Customer reviews indicate the trouble-free issuance of short-term loans and loyal conditions that allow you to issue a microloan to citizens with a bad credit history.

What is a microfinance organization

The concept of "microfinance structure" has appeared on the modern financial market for a long time. The first MFI was founded in 1944. At that time, the profitability of microcrediting was evaluated by borrowers and accepted in society. Today, the majority of the country's population uses the services of microfinance organizations. According to the register of MFIs, which was organized by the Ministry of Finance, there are approximately 350 such institutions. Microcredit services are in the stage of intensive growth, and by the end of the year their number may increase to 450.





A high degree of demand among citizens of the Russian Federation is due to the loyal conditions for obtaining a loan and the ability to take an urgent loan without additional income certificates and attracting guarantors. As a rule, microcrediting is carried out online, that is, without the need to personally visit the office of the company. Therefore, the client base of MFIs is constantly expanding due to new users, which creates healthy competition for loans in large banking institutions.

The similarity of a bank loan with a short-term loan

Both types of lending represent the receipt of borrowed funds in order to improve the financial situation and solve urgent financial problems. From the moment of receiving the money, the borrower has certain debt obligations to the organization, which he must fulfill upon the expiration of the established period. The lending period is indicated in the contract. Together with the principal amount of the debt, the client is required to pay a fixed interest rate accrued for the use of cash.

Unfulfilled debt obligations to a bank or an MFI can lead to serious consequences, including criminal liability. The imposition of penalties and interest on debt is also a common feature of both types of lending. Do not think that microfinancing with its simplified design system is a frivolous type of lending. Just like large financial institutions, MFIs take measures and collect debt from the borrower in the same way as banks.

Credit or microloan - what's the difference?

According to the opinions of borrowers, credit and microcredit are significantly different from each other.

  • First of all, making a microloan in an MFI is much easier and faster than a bank loan. The bank carefully checks the identity of the client, solvency, and, as a rule, 50% of applications are refused. Microfinance institutions are more loyal to their customers and give out money even with a bad credit history. 9 out of 10 applications are approved by the credit service.
  • The size of the loan amount in the bank is many times higher than the possible amount of the microloan. MFIs do not give out clients more than 30,000 rubles, and the bank is able to lend to citizens for millions.
  • The loan maturity is several years. A microloan must be repaid within a short period of time. According to reviews, "RosMoney" requires borrowers to return money in 17 days.
  • To apply for a loan, it is necessary to collect a voluminous package of documents: passport, TIN, certificate of employment, certificate of average monthly income, medical insurance, a pension certificate, military ID, documents for property or motor vehicles, etc. A microloan is issued only according to the passport of a citizen of the Russian Federation .
  • You can get a loan only at a bank branch. Microfinancing is carried out by transferring the requested amount to the client‚Äôs bank card. The identity of the borrower is verified by a scanned copy of the passport.
  • Most large banking institutions issue loans in 5-7 days from the date of application. You can get an urgent loan on the same day.
  • An urgent microloan can be issued at any time of the day, since credit services work around the clock. Loans are issued at the bank branch and only at the established working hours.

MFI Interest Rates: Myths and Reality

Among the comments of those who use the services of microfinance organizations, such as RosMoney, interest rate reviews are common. The annual percentage of loans in numerical terms is quite high and, compared with bank charges, is not profitable. The company set a fixed interest rate of 2% of the loan amount per day. That is, for each day of using the money, the borrower must pay the organization part of the funds. The shorter the loan term, the less money will be overpaid.





However, if we analyze and compare the annual interest rates of banks and MFIs, it turns out that there is practically no difference. The high ready rate is offset by a short loan term and a small loan amount. Instant bank loans that financial institutions issue on the day of circulation have an interest rate not much lower than microloans in MFIs. Reviews about the company "RosMoney" indicate the fact that it is quite realistic to lend money to a salary or pension, as well as to pay interest on the loan. However, do not forget that in case of untimely repayment of the debt to the borrower a penalty is charged every day in the amount of 2% of the amount owed.
