Knitting is unusual, but beautiful. Creative needlework ideas.

  • Combine different types of materials and yarn.

  • Apply tools or objects that have never been used before to work.

What is creativity, and with what sauce is it served

The word "creative" is translated from English as "creativity". That is, something created (created) by man should be called that. By and large, both completely new ideas and a fresh combination of familiar elements are creative.

When it comes to knitting, it‚Äôs hard to find anything completely new, because the basic elements remain the same: front and back loops, double crochet and without crochet. But the original cut of clothes, the use of interesting materials and the game with the scale of canvases are all modern knitting.

Unusual ideas are sometimes so amazing that it becomes interesting how the designer came to his discovery. For example, who first thought of tying a tree? And why? Probably out of love for the beautiful.





Beautiful knitwear that completely duplicates other patterns is not creative. But changing the cut, applying other schemes and adding your own flavor makes the copying process a creative creation.

Huge little soft toys made by crochet or knitting have gained immense popularity. Such knitting, unusual and very funny, takes a little time and does not require large stocks of yarn. The main thing here is the accurate execution of all elements (loops and posts), as well as compliance with geometric accuracy.

In recent years, a rather specific type of knitting has gained popularity - work with very thick, even, twisted yarns. Initially, merino wool was used , but such a thread is very expensive. Interesting Jacquards

Who would have thought how varied and original jacquard patterns can be ! The history of this technique has several hundred years, but new approaches to its implementation are constantly appearing.

Jacquards knitted with melange yarn look extremely interesting. Even though the ornaments look the most traditional way, this knitting is unusual and original due to the color scheme. To form a pattern, the craftswomen choose material for section dyeing with very long areas of color transition. For example, there is such a yarn where the areas dyed with a certain color are several tens of meters. As a result, the finished product has a very smooth and beautiful transition from one color to another.





As a background, monophonic yarn of a suitable color or another melange yarn can be used. Traditionally, the background is knitted with a darker color, and the ornaments are made lighter.

Hats, caps and hats

Wishing to entertain themselves and their loved ones, craftsmen with crochet and knitting needles create creative and unimaginable hats. A variety of funny hats amazes the most daring imagination:

  • Different types of helmets.
  • Hats with beards.
  • Gas masks.
  • Wigs made of yarn.
  • Caps stylized as food (muffins, hamburger, chicken leg).

Such knitting is unusual from the point of view of people who have a traditional and conservative view of knitting skills. Of course, these hats are not worn for going to work, they are used for photo shoots or as original gifts.





Schemes are developed not only by bored masters, but also by designers who want to get fame on social networks. First of all, they rely on the creativity and unusualness of their models.

Freeform - the ‚Äúmagic‚Äù crochet technique

In search of inspiration and new ideas, masters often invent completely new approaches and techniques. The top of knitting art can be called a technique such as freeform.



Its peculiarity is that the canvas is composed of individual motifs. Unlike Irish lace or just a typesetting pattern, freeform motifs have the following features:

  • High density. Often the canvas is so continuous that it is suitable for making a coat.
  • Custom shape. There are no rectangular, triangular or hexagonal fragments. Typically, motifs with round outlines, curls and asymmetrical protrusions are knitted for the freeform.
  • The presence of volumetric elements. Almost always, lush, postal and twisted columns are used, as well as voluminous scars and stitches laid over the finished canvas.

Freeform is suitable for the manufacture of clothing, accessories and interior items.



Among the craftswomen, models of cardigans and coats made up of color motifs in such a way as to obtain a gradient transition were popular. Usually darker fragments are placed below, and lighter motifs are placed higher. In the most contrasting models, one can observe a transition from a rather dark color (lower edge of the back and shelves) to very light (shoulder line, collar).

Variety of materials

Creative masters often combines unusual crochet with knitwear. Also quite often they are combined with fragments of tissue and skin.

As accessories, metal, wooden and plastic buckles, rings, clips, chains, buttons, buttons and other items are used.

Many designers combine their passion for knitting with a love of creating jewelry. This is how knitted beads, necklaces, brooches, hairpins and other jewelry are born.

Knitted jewelry can be large and created in the style of "folk" or can be quite elegant accessories that are quite capable of decorating an evening outfit.

Knitted elements combine with beads made of wood, plastic, metal, glass, with precious stones and Austrian crystals.

Freedom of thought is the main quality of a creative designer

Many knitters, looking at photos of original items created with a hook or knitting needles, sigh heavily and think that they will not be able to do anything like this. However, such work does not require compliance with any rules and cannot be subject to stringent requirements.

The beauty of creativity lies in creating something that did not exist before. Therefore, do not be upset because of the inability to knit hats. Today, individuality is in price, so maybe you need to think about your own unusual bags, mittens or slippers that no one has ever seen before.
