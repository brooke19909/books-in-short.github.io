Entertainment and the beach

Lovers of outdoor activities can visit the tennis court, fitness center, billiards room, sauna, play bocca, beach volleyball and squash. Water activities will also please: diving, windsurfing. You can ride a banana, water skiing. Children will also not be bored at the Hilton Hurghada Resort. For them there is a mini club, a playground. In the outdoor pools there are children's departments.
