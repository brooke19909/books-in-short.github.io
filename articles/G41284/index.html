"Elykaevskaya Sloboda": rest for everyone

The recreation center "Elykaevskaya Sloboda" is a great place for those who want to relax from the daily routine and spend time in a large company of loved ones. Guests are offered 14 summer houses and 5 comfortable cottages, as well as plenty of entertainment, without which any vacation is complete. The camp site is located on a cliff of the shore, and a staircase leads to the river, along which vacationers can go down at any time.

Leisure

Entertainment at the Kemerovo camp site is more than enough for both children and adults. Here, even a lazy person will find a fascinating activity for himself. On the territory of the base there is a playground with sports equipment, a play area for children, two saunas for up to 10 people, barbecue facilities, gazebos, as well as a zoo with wild animals. The houses are surrounded by a pine forest, perfect air and great views will not give up on foot. You can also ride a pony. But the most interesting thing is that deer and deer walk freely around the territory of the Elykaevskaya Sloboda camp site, which can be hand fed and stroked.

House rental

"Elykaevskaya Sloboda" offers guests several types of houses. Their main difference from each other is not only in the number of seats, but also in the level of comfort. The accommodation is equipped with everything necessary: â€‹â€‹a TV, a microwave, a refrigerator, furniture, double and single beds, bedding, dishes, a modern bathroom with a shower. You can also rent summer houses for up to three people. Each room is connected to electric heating, there is hot and cold water. The houses are located at a great distance from each other, so none of the neighbors will interfere.

Summer houses are equipped with infrared heaters, two beds, a bedside table, a refrigerator, a hanger.

The superior cottage is designed for two people. The house has a refrigerator, kettle, microwave, dishes, LCD TV. In addition, guests are also given individual slippers, hygiene items.

The six-bed house is equipped with one double bed, a sofa bed, an armchair bed. The kitchen is equipped with everything you need, including household appliances.

Eight-person house - housing designed for a large company. They can provide two additional beds in the form of folding beds. The house has all the necessary amenities: a bathroom, a TV, mirrors, wardrobes, consumer electronics, benches, a dining table.

Prices

The rental price varies from 2500 to 13000 rubles per day. Summer houses can be rented for three people, and comfortable options are designed for a number of from two to ten guests. All entertainment is paid separately, prices can be found on the website or at the administrator.
