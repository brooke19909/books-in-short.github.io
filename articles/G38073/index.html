How to choose and buy a radio

Radio was discovered in 1895 by the scientist A.S. Popov, and since then has firmly entered our lives. Despite the fact that the sunset of wired broadcasting is taking place, there are still many supporters of the radio, which is capable of receiving wireless signals. Radio broadcasts on short, medium and long waves. The most popular is the FM-band of ultra-short waves, which broadcast entertainment, music radio stations. Analog receivers allow you to manually adjust the wave reception mechanically. In contrast, digital radios provide high accuracy signal processing and tuning to the wave.





Modern radios allow high-quality reproduction of stereo sound, listen to radio broadcasts from all over the world. Radio is in demand by motorists, summer residents, housewives, office workers, as it allows you to receive information, and does not distract from work.

If you decide to buy a radio, then you should get acquainted with the models that the market offers. First of all, these are traditional analog devices and digital radio, where modern computer technologies are concentrated.





If you need a radio for long trips, trips to nature, to the country, then you will need a radio that is equipped with a digital tuner and can catch medium and long waves using an antenna. The receiver will help you find out the weather forecast, will cheer you up with a pleasant melody. For a hike, fairly affordable all-wave radios are suitable, because there can be breakdowns and losses along the way. A convenient option may be a built-in dynamo or replaceable batteries as batteries.

An important part of an all-wave receiver is the antenna. It should be long enough to catch waves well. It is necessary to choose models of radios with the ability to connect an external antenna, which contributes to high-quality reception of signals. In order to make it convenient to operate the radio in a dark tent, it must be equipped with a backlight. To buy a radio for trips to nature, you can choose from a wide range of online stores. The Tecsun model meets all the above requirements. In addition to the characteristics described above, it has a built-in MP3 player and can play files from an external SD card. The radios of this model are representatives of a new generation. They have a large number of memory cells, have a stereo headphone jack, and a clock. Digital receivers are equipped with LCD screen, telescopic antenna.





If you are going to buy a radio receiver that accepts a wide range of waves, pay attention to the Degen model. This is a digital radio that has gained popularity in the Russian market. The radio is affordable, compact, lightweight and durable. It is equipped with automatic backlight display and buttons, has electronic volume control and a network adapter.
