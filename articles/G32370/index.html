Bath "Vivacity" in Kemerovo: description and reviews

The Bodrost bathhouse in Kemerovo is a unique complex equipped with general departments for male and female visitors. There are also twelve rooms with steam rooms, saunas, a jacuzzi, pools, lounges. Premises vary in interior and capacity. The services of the bath complex are described in sections of the article.

Facility Features

Bodrost bathhouse in Kemerovo is located at the address: Oktyabrsky Prospekt, building 65.

Clients of the organization are given the opportunity to relax and recover after tiring working days. For visitors are provided:

  1. General bath sections designed for male and female visitors.
  2. Twelve rooms are equipped with modern facilities.
  3. Beauty saloon.
  4. Sport Club.
  5. Cafe.

The opening hours of the Bodrost bathhouse in Kemerovo are as follows:

  1. General offices are open from Tuesday to Friday from 12-00 to 22-00. On Saturday and Sunday, they operate from 11 am to 10 pm.
  2. Room services can be used from 10-00 to midnight. This schedule is valid from Tuesday to Thursday and Sunday. On Fridays and Saturdays, they work from ten in the morning until 2-00.

Visitor Lounge

On the territory of the Bodrost bathhouse in Kemerovo, there are separate rooms designed for a comfortable pastime with family and friends. There are rooms intended for washing, comfortable saunas.

Guests can use brooms and hairdryers. The rooms have different sizes and are designed for a large number of persons (up to 12 people). The rooms are equipped with spa baths and a jacuzzi. Each room has a TV.

other services

For customers of the Bodrost bath in Kemerovo, a beauty salon is provided. It is located on the ground floor of the complex. Guests can use the services of professional masters, enjoy the pleasant and useful procedures. The salon is called Cloudberry. This berry has an excellent composition, it is used for the manufacture of various body care products, facial skin and hair.

Visitors to the complex can also use the services of a cafe. People come here to have a snack after visiting the baths, spend time in the company of friends and loved ones, and organize a gala event. The menu is diverse and includes salads, snacks and hot dishes, pastries. The cafe has a summer terrace.

Visitors to the Bodrost bath in Kemerovo are offered a sports complex. The fitness club provides classes for adults and small clients.

Training is provided to reduce body weight, build muscle, develop flexibility, and prevent pathologies of the spinal column. Special programs are offered to enhance the health of expectant mothers.
