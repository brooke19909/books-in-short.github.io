Hotel Tal Hotel 3

Description: Tal Hotel 3 is located in the Arenal - resort town of Mallorca. The hotel was built in 1969 on the Mediterranean coast, 14 kilometers from the city of Palma and a short drive from the airport.



The hotel has an advantageous location near the beach, water park, numerous shops, cafes, discos, transport stops. The city hotel consists of a nine-story building and a small landscaped area with a swimming pool.

Rooms: Guests at the Tal Hotel 3 have 198 standard double rooms with furnished terraces and city or sea views. The interiors are dominated by natural shades in the spirit of the natural colors of the Mediterranean. The rooms are unobtrusive and feature light wooden furniture.

Housekeeping and towel change are provided daily. Linen is changed every three days.

Meal: There are several food options and service in the restaurant with the buffet system. The restaurant's cuisine will satisfy people with a variety of passions, including vegetarians. On the table there are many desserts, dishes of vegetables, fish and seafood. Free alcoholic drinks from local producers are served.



In the hotel bar, located in the lobby, you can not only have a snack and a cocktail, but also play a game of billiards.

Beach: The road to the beach runs through the city street and takes no more than 10 minutes. Municipal sandy beach is equipped with comfortable sun loungers and tents, for which a fee is charged.

Additional information: Hotel Tal 3 offers its guests a carefree holiday on the beach and numerous entertainments. Mallorca is an unforgettable place with natural and historical sights to go on an excursion to.

At the hotel, tourists have the opportunity to exchange currency, deposit their luggage, put valuables in a safe, rent a car, use the laundry service, call a doctor, visit the sauna and solarium. Internet access is available on site for an extra charge.

Without leaving the hotel you can find a lot of activities. There are tennis and billiard tables, darts targets, a gym, an Internet cafe, a games room and a television room.

Near the Tal Hotel 3 there is a sun terrace with sun loungers and two outdoor fresh pools, one of which is for young tourists. In cold weather, a winter indoor pool is available.



Children are invited to the mini club and to the playground. They have a special diet in the restaurant and small cots in the room.

Digest: Tal Hotel 3 is perfect for a decent vacation in Mallorca . Reviews prove that the hotel deserves attention when choosing an economical option with good living conditions.

The hotel is close to the beach, shopping and entertainment. It offers fairly spacious, well-kept rooms with balconies. In rooms with simple furnishings, all appliances and electrical appliances are working. A thorough wet cleaning and towel change is carried out daily. The staff is helpful and attentive to each guest.

The food is varied and tasty: there is always a lot of greens, fruits, vegetables and seafood. They serve mainly local cuisine and typical international food.

Tal Hotel 3 has daily animators who conduct contests, games, competitions, quizzes. In the evenings, they organize music shows with artists.
