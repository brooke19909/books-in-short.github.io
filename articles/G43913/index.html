SEC Lipetsk "Armada": description, shops

Lipetsk is a Russian city, which was founded in 1703. Currently, 510 thousand people live here.

For the state, this city has the most important industrial and transport significance. However, in Lipetsk there are not only enterprises in metallurgy and engineering. Residents and guests of the city are also given ample opportunities for recreation and entertainment. In Lipetsk, there are several theaters, concert halls, museums, cinemas, as well as more than a dozen shopping and entertainment centers, and Armada is one of them.

SEC Lipetsk "Armada". Photo and location

Armada is a large 6-tier shopping and entertainment center with more than a hundred different shops: boutiques of popular brands of clothes and shoes, supermarkets, pharmacies and many others. In total, the area of ‚Äã‚Äãall six floors of the center is about 25 thousand m 2 .

Address of the SEC "Armada" in Lipetsk: st. Petra Smorodina, 13 A. The nearest public transport stop is the 20th Microdistrict, which can be reached by buses under numbers 27, 28, 306, 308k, 352 and others. Also nearby are the Oktyabrsky Market and Lilac Passage stops.

For the convenience of visitors arriving in their own car, on-site parking is available at the Armada shopping center.

The shops

In Armada, there are about 50 stores for men's and women's clothing of well-known mass-market brands: Zolla, TVOE, BROADWAY, FI More, Familia and others. Clothing and toys for children of preschool and primary school age are presented in "Daughters-sons", "Fashionable Schoolboy" and "Hippo."

Shoes and accessories boutiques - Rieker, Alvista, Renet, RESPECT. Jewelry and jewelry can be purchased at stores such as Sunlight, Crystal, Love Line, Silver.

Also in the Armada shopping and entertainment center in Lipetsk there is a supermarket of the popular OKEY chain, the Four Paws pet products store, a pharmacy, the Clavier musical instrument salon, and the Chita-gorod bookstore.

It is easy to get lost in a huge 6-story building, especially when you are there for the first time, therefore, for the convenience of orientation, visitors are advised to use the mall cards located on each floor of the Armada. Also, an interactive map can be found on the official website of the shopping and entertainment center.

other services

SEC Lipetsk "Armada" provides its guests with a wide range of services. In addition to various stores, here are located:

  • fitness club "Krypton", which includes a gym, cardio zone and a room for group classes;
  • a beauty salon providing manicure and pedicure services, eyebrow correction, eyelash extensions and depilation;
  • children's hairdresser;
  • solarium.

On the territory of the shopping and entertainment center also operates a cinema "Kinomir."

While parents go shopping, children can spend time on the playground with many attractions: ride a slide, jump on a trampoline, swing on a swing. In addition, for the youngest visitors, the Armada shopping and entertainment center also has a Karuselkino and Knight's Castle slot machine hall, an entertainment center with a two-story maze, a dry pool with balls and a professional animation team.
