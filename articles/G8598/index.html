Knitted men's scarf: a description of several simple patterns

To be a stylish and fashionable man, it is not necessary to have a chic wardrobe, consisting of many things. Some accessories will help to easily refresh the image, giving it a touch of chic and extravagance. Such items include, for example, a tie or a knitted men's scarf. How to make this little thing special using thread, knitting needles or a hook? You will find several different options in this article.





The choice of drawing, model and method of execution

What patterns are most often used to get a knitted men's scarf? Using knitting needles, for beginner needlewomen it is easiest to complete it, perhaps, with a gum pattern. For this, it is necessary to alternate front and back loops. On the reverse side of the work, the technology is similar. If you use a hook, you can "refresh" a plain canvas with an original ornament. His knitting occurs according to the schemes according to which one cell is equal to one single crochet. In this case, an unused thread is usually laid along a row, hiding inside the knit. The ornament usually adorns the scarf on both sides. Another finish is the use of multi-color knitting. Consider, for example, what a product from a thread of several shades can be. About how to make a knitted men's scarf, alternating transverse and longitudinal stripes, is described below. It is worth considering that the technology varies depending on the method of work used.





How to knit strips with knitting needles?

The alternation of multi-colored transverse segments is very simple. Dial the desired number of loops and work until the color changes. Then use a different ball to continue knitting. If the strips are very wide, the thread is torn off, and the free end is woven into the fabric, hiding between the loops. When alternating colors in the longitudinal direction, several coils are used simultaneously. We will describe how to make such men's knitted scarves. Schemes provide for the conduct of the type of jacquard weaving. So, for example, to knit a blue striped scarf, shown in the photo, the following color change order is used: hem, eight to ten blue loops, three to five blue, three to five white, three to five blue, eight to ten blue, hem. In order for the canvas to turn out solid and not have random holes when changing colors, change the threads, crossing each other. Finishing brushes also make different. They are a visual continuation of a multi-colored canvas.





Ways to make men's crochet striped scarves

Female models are distinguished by a huge variety of options. Among them, the most popular are scarves and stoles, assembled from small lace fragments, crocheted. Or alternate transverse or longitudinal strips of two or three shades. Similarly, you can get men's products, crocheted. Scarves made in this way have some features. Use one of two options:

1. Cross strips. With this technology, it is easiest to make knitted scarves. Men's patterns provide for the change of different threads several times. Having knitted several rows in one color, you need to continue to work using another. A thread of unused tone usually stretches along the edge or breaks. To "hide" then all the errors, the canvas can be tied in a circle in one or two rows of single crochets.

How to knit a scarf snood?

This model is something between a hat, a cape and a hood. Scarf snood made of thick threads looks more like a large collar. Models made of thin material make it much wider and more voluminous. This involves wrapping them around the neck in a double loop. It is more practical to carry out such a knitted men's scarf in a circle. Using a hook to work, draw a chain of air loops and lock it in a circle. Then, move only in one direction, performing a suitable ornament. Spiral knitting somewhat modifies the pattern, making it special and unique. The greatest benefit from a practical point of view is manifested in the absence of seams, which is especially convenient when using thick threads. The technology for manufacturing a product with knitting needles, in principle, is no different from that described above. Use a circular tool and spiral in the same direction in only one direction. Having tied the cylindrical cloth of the desired height, finish the job by closing the hinges with a hook. The original scarf snood is ready!
