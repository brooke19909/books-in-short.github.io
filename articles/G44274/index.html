Hotel Green Hill Resort and SPA 3 * (Vietnam, Phan Thiet): photos and reviews of tourists

Going on an exotic trip to Vietnam, it is important not to make a mistake in choosing a hotel. Comfort, quality service and aesthetic pleasure will give you Green Hill Resort and SPA . Here you will find harmony with nature and get acquainted with the local culture.





Short hotel description

Green Hill Resort and SPA was built in 2011. Given the small number of rooms (21 in total), it is almost always quiet, calm and not crowded. The entire territory, free from buildings, densely planted with a garden of tropical palm trees and other exotic plants. Artificial ponds, beautiful landscapes, as well as themed decor create a unique atmosphere of tranquility.





Location

In one of the most interesting tourist places is the resort Green Hill Resort and SPA 3 *. Vietnam, Mui Ne, Nguyen Dinh Chieu, 121 - address of the institution. It is only 1 minute walk from the beach. The hotel is 700 meters from the center of Mui Ne. Phan Thiet Train Station is 17 km away.

By choosing Green Hill Resort and SPA for your relaxation, you can visit the following nearby facilities:

  • kitesurfing center (0.1 km);
  • diving center (0.1 km);
  • fairy stream (3 km);
  • sand dunes (7 km);
  • Poshanu Cham Tower (8 km);
  • local market (8 km);
  • Fairy Springs Waterfall (10 km);
  • Mount Takou (37 km);
  • Ke Ga lighthouse (37 km).

Description of rooms and prices

If you want to live in comfortable conditions, this opportunity will be provided to you at the Green Hill Resort and SPA 3 * hotel. The following options are available for placement:

  • The standard is equipped with a pair of single beds and a dressing table with a mirror. It offers scenic views of the palm garden. The cost of double occupancy is 20 euros per day.
  • Superior room is different from the standard more spacious area. Due to this, it became possible to equip a small corner for relaxation with soft chairs. The cost is 26 euros.
  • Family rooms can accommodate up to 4 people, and also allow the installation of extra beds. There is one large and a pair of twin beds. The cost is 40 euros.
  • Bungalows with lake views are very popular. The interior resembles an improved room. There is a private terrace with sun loungers, as well as access to the pool. The cost is 43 euros.




Room Amenities

A feeling of comfort and homeliness will accompany you during your stay in this resort building. Green Hill Resort and SPA provides guests with the following in-room amenities:

  • individually controlled air conditioning system;
  • wardrobe for your clothes;
  • a small TV connected to a cable antenna;
  • mini-bar, which can be filled for an additional fee, and also serves as a refrigerator;
  • sleep lovers will appreciate the presence of dense curtains on the windows, which provide complete dimming of the room;
  • a bathroom with necessary plumbing, a hairdryer and bath accessories.




Hotel infrastructure

Everything that a modern tourist needs for a good rest can be found on the territory of the Green Hill Resort and SPA 3 * resort complex. So, travelers can use the following infrastructure facilities and services:

  • round-the-clock reception, the staff of which will answer all your questions and help solve problems associated with accommodation;
  • free wireless internet throughout;
  • Private parking, the use of which is included in the price;
  • a left-luggage office, where you can leave things before check-in and in the interval between eviction and departure;
  • a large palm garden, under the shadow of which you can hide from the Vietnamese heat;
  • a restaurant serving local and international dishes;
  • terminals for payment of accommodation and related services through plastic cards.

Spa Services

Beauty and health will be presented to its visitors by the Green Hill Resort and SPA spa. Vietnam is famous for its effective treatments. Here you will be provided with the following services:

  • healing mud baths;

  • a full range of facial skin care procedures;

  • body scrubbing with coconut or coffee;
  • aromatherapy sessions.




Additional Information

Before you go to the Green Hill Resort and SPA, pay attention to the rules of accommodation. The most significant are the following points:

  • a foreign guest cannot stay in the same room with a Vietnamese citizen of the opposite sex without presenting a marriage certificate (legal requirement);
  • one child under 11 years old stays in a room free of charge without extra bed;
  • only registered guests can be in the rooms (outsiders are not allowed);
  • Check-in is done no earlier than 14:00, and leaving the room on the day of departure is before noon;
  • in the period from December to October, newly arrived guests are treated to a glass of fruit juice;
  • if you book a room for the New Year holidays, then you must pay extra for participation in the gala dinner ($ 45 for each guest);
  • upon registration, the guest is required to pay a deposit by credit card or in cash (if you do not cause the hotel material damage, upon eviction it will be returned in full);
  • new arrivals are required to provide photo identification;
  • self-registration is possible only from the age of 18;
  • pets are strictly prohibited (including service animals and guide dogs).

  • good breakfasts that delight not only with their taste, but also with the beauty of the design;

  • a huge amount of greenery in the territory (thanks to the shade, it is cool here even in the most intense heat);
  • the bathroom is practically open-air;
  • the breakfast menu has a lot of fresh fruit;
  • There is one Russian-language channel (you can always be up to date with the latest news from the homeland);
  • sometimes you can hear domestic music in a restaurant;
  • the host speaks Russian;
  • in the territory you can find ripe coconuts that the restaurant staff will kindly help you clean;
  • at night, the pool is not closed, and therefore you can swim under the moon;
  • the staff greets guests very cordially, with a sincere smile (even towards the most fastidious guests are not sent dissatisfied glances and a sharp tone);
  • waiters at breakfast tell guests about each dish and help put food on a plate;
  • the staff quickly responds to all comments and requests of guests;
  • the territory is kept in perfect cleanliness (workers constantly clean, wash, water something);
  • warm water in the pool (especially for those who like to swim at night);
  • daily bottles of drinking water are provided in the rooms (they are brought by the maids during cleaning);
  • Vietnamese cuisine in the restaurant is adapted for European tourists (the number of hot spices is significantly reduced in it);
  • the pool always has free sunbeds; Considering that the hotel has a lot of tourists from the CIS countries, you can always find yourself a fun company;
  • there are a lot of squirrels on palm trees, which are very interesting to watch;
  • Considering that the bungalows are located in a secluded part of the hotel, it is quite quiet and calm;
  • the room is cleaned only at your personal request, and therefore you can not worry that during your absence someone will enter the room;
  • original design of rooms (sustained in local traditions);
  • there is a power outlet on the veranda in the bungalow, so you can connect music equipment or a laptop.




Negative reviews

Particular attention should be paid to the disadvantages of the Green Hill Resort and SPA 3 * hotel. Reviews say the following:

  • not the most convenient road to the sea;
  • there are a lot of jellyfish in the sea, and therefore it is not always possible to swim;
  • there is no animation, and therefore it can sometimes be boring in a hotel;
  • despite the fact that the hotel is located in close proximity to the sea, almost all the beaches are the property of neighboring hotels, and therefore it takes a long time to look for a wild area where you can swim;
  • upon registration, the administrator picks up the passports that are stored in his safe until the time of eviction;
  • on the way to the beach there is a busy track, which is not so easy to cross, given that drivers do not particularly stand on ceremony with pedestrians;
  • standard rooms are just tiny; aka relocation to the best apartments requires an extra charge;
  • throughout the hotel (except for the area around the pool) wireless Internet is not working well;
  • right during the guests' rest, the hotel is undergoing renovations (it would be more logical to temporarily close the institution or reduce the cost of living);
  • the institution needs a complete reconstruction;
  • the bathroom is very dark (need more lighting);
  • it is alarming that the water in the pool does not smell like bleach at all (the suspicion creeps in that it is not disinfected at all);
  • the hotel looks rather inconspicuous from the outside, making it difficult to find upon arrival;
  • use of the safe in the room is paid ($ 5);
  • hygiene items have to be requested several times at the reception;
  • the area is too small for walkers;
  • upon check-in, you may find that there was no cleaning in the room (the situation is corrected by the reception);
  • the trip from the airport to the hotel takes about 5 hours, which is very tiring after a long flight;
  • there are no curtains on the bathroom windows, but on the contrary are residential buildings and a construction site.

General impression

Despite its many shortcomings, the Green Hill Resort SPA makes a pretty good impression. Reviews mark a cozy green area, being on which you can feel complete unity with nature. It is also worth noting a hospitable meeting by the staff and the Russian-speaking hotel owner. Here guests will find a cozy overnight stay and delicious food.

The greatest number of complaints is the lack of a private beach. Official sources indicated that the sea can be reached in a minute, and in fact it takes at least a quarter of an hour to find a place to swim. And, of course, the unpleasant moment is the need to hand over passports to the administrator. But persistent tourists manage to defend their rights and avoid this procedure.
