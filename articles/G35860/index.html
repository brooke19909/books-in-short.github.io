How does the program "Thank you from Sberbank"





The “Thank you from Sberbank” program works in stages: while you purchase the goods you need in stores, the card credits half the percentage of the purchase amount to the card. As you probably already understood, the longer you use the card and the higher the amount of purchases, the greater the chance of accumulating huge bonuses.

How to pay with "Thank you" bonuses from Sberbank MTS?

Absolutely any subscriber can replenish his balance with the help of bonuses, you only need to connect to the program. Subscribers are given the opportunity to connect the service in different ways.

Connection Methods





1. Through the Internet. So the option “Bonus payment“ Thank you from Sberbank “MTS” is quite simply connected. The Internet makes it possible to do this without leaving home. You just need to be registered in your account. We enter our page, find the “Thank you” tab, select “Activation”. Next, fill out information about yourself, read the rules and agree with them. A message will come with the code that needs to be confirmed.

2. At any ATM. We insert the card into the ATM, enter the PIN code. In the main menu, click on the item “Bonus Program”. Following the prompts, indicate your number and wait for the message.

3. Using a mobile bank. To do this, we need a phone. We send a short message to the number 900: the word THANKS and - after the space - the last four digits of your card number (note: we write the word in capital letters). You will receive a code that we copy and send to the same number. It is interesting that payment for MTS services with "Thank you from Sberbank" bonuses is carried out by any card.

4. Through an online call. You apply to a special unit of the organization, where each call and each appeal is processed. We dial 900, press the send call key, wait for the operator’s response and notify him that we want to activate the service. The assistant will tell you how long it will take to connect the service and from when you can use the service.

Important information: unfortunately, it is impossible to top up your phone balance completely free of charge. Bonuses can be paid only ninety-nine percent, and the rest is debited from the card account. Example: we transfer a thousand rubles - ten rubles leaves in the form of a commission, and 990 is credited to the account.

Now you know how to pay with MTS "Thanks from Sberbank" bonuses.
