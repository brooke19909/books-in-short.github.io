Yas Island Rotana Hotel (Abu Dhabi, United Arab Emirates): traveler reviews

Yas Island is an incredibly colorful place. Everything is fascinating here - nature, people, events. Yas Island Rotana Abu Dhabi (photos, descriptions and reviews of tourists about this complex will be presented in this article) is one of the most fashionable hotels in the city, which every tourist dreams of settling in.

general information





The hotel is assigned 4 stars, the right to which it deserves in full. All rooms of the establishment are very spacious, the designers have tried and embodied a magnificent European style that will appeal to even the most demanding tourists. The highlight of the place is the incredibly beautiful view from the windows. No less remarkable is the service and attitude towards the guest. The staff will gladly fulfill any whims, having done it at the highest level. It is an individual approach to vacationers that makes this place pleasant and comfortable for living.

The complex has a rather favorable location. Everything you need is within walking distance. Nearby is a park where you can have a picnic or just take a walk. A ten minute walk is the club, which brings together true golf enthusiasts. Another plus is the proximity of the airport. It only takes 20 minutes to get to it.

Accommodation

The Centro Yas Island Abu Dhabi By Rotana hotel complex offers its guests 308 cozy rooms. Designers equipped them primarily for a comfortable stay. Therefore, each guest will be able to fully feel the atmosphere of relaxation and rest. The living rooms are very comfortable. Of particular note is the bathroom, which features an incredibly beautiful finish in natural marble. Bath amenities, good cosmetics and soft bathrobes will also please you.

Hotel Rooms




Each room at Centro Yas Island Rotana 3 Abu Dhabi is equipped with air conditioning, satellite TV, Internet access, and a telephone (even in the bathroom). You can use the safe, minibar, special equipment for making tea and coffee. There is an iron and ironing board, but it is not necessary to put things in order yourself; you can use the laundry services. A hairdryer, bathrobes, slippers and other necessary things are also provided.

The hotel has the following room types:

- Classic room. A small room with a large bed, a spacious bathroom and everything necessary for comfort.

- Premium room. The room has two spacious rooms, a large bathroom with a jacuzzi and shower. There is also a private balcony with armchairs where you can enjoy morning coffee.

- Rotana club room. Guest room with special privileges.

- Rotana club classic suite. The room includes several rooms, a very spacious bathroom.

- Suite 2-bedroom. Room is a high level of comfort. It has a large area, two bedrooms, a living room, a huge bathroom. Guests also have privileges.

Room filling

Regardless of the category, the rooms are equipped with air conditioning, all household and toiletries, tea and coffee making facilities. In the minibar you can always find something tasty. The room has a TV and even a set-top box, so children can always have fun. There are several telephones (in the living room and bathroom). There is free water, fresh newspapers are delivered to your room every morning.

Even the simplest room looks decent, with a large bathroom, ironing board, hairdryer and air conditioning. An extra bed for a child is provided for a fee, but most often there is no need for it, as the rooms have very large beds. The bedding is of good quality, the mattress and pillows are comfortable. The floor is carpeted. Towels, bathrobes and linen are changed frequently.

Guest meals

The hotel has several restaurants and cafes. There is an international restaurant. Rangoli Restaurant serves traditional Indian cuisine. There is also a grill restaurant where you can always enjoy delicious steaks, especially men like this place. There is also a sports bar with a huge TV for the stronger sex. You can enjoy delicious cocktails at the poolside bar.

Service service




This hotel has a huge network around the world. Any other hotel complex can envy the service here. Yas Island Rotana Abu Dhabi 4 * offers a huge range of services, which include banking, support, tour desk. It is possible to use the services of a secretary and translator.

There is free parking on site for those who have arrived in their own car, there is also an ATM and a currency exchange area. You can rent any equipment, for example, a mobile phone or laptop. In the lobby of the hotel there are souvenir and newsagents. The good news is that the staff is multilingual, so everyone will feel at home.

Entertainment and sports



The complex has several pools, there is also a children's pool with heating. You can use the services of the SPA center. In addition, you can visit a beauty salon, try a variety of treatments and more.
The hotel is a unique place, because it provides high quality services at a budget price. Therefore, even a poor family with children can relax here. You can also take a business trip.

The best advertisements for the Yas Island Rotana Abu Dhabi hotel are guest reviews.

What vacationers say

It is best to plan a vacation in Abu Dhabi in mid-March. Especially if you decide to travel with children. The weather is warm, but there is no scorching heat.

The first thing that gives the impression of high quality service is an individual transfer. Even if you arrived at the hotel alone, you will still be met by the hotel staff. The road from Sharjah Airport takes about 2 hours. Time flies unnoticed, because the windows await you with magnificent landscapes. The surroundings and the service fully justify the fact that the hotel complex Yas Island Rotana Abu Dhabi is magnificent.

At the reception work Russian-speaking administrators. If the guest arrives too late, the administration will make sure that people get to dinner.

Upon check-in, a Wi-Fi password is immediately issued. Employees configure access themselves. The Internet works well not only in the room, but also near the pool. The administration resolves any requests instantly. The staff is very friendly, always smiling and interested in your affairs and problems. There is a special care for every guest.

The rooms are superbly equipped. They have everything you need: bathrobes, slippers, tea accessories, soap and other body care cosmetics. All this is replenished daily.

About location

The island itself, on which the hotel is located, is only a few km away from Abu Dhabi. 15 minutes drive - and you are in the capital. On the island itself, the infrastructure is not particularly developed. There are several more hotel complexes, a beach, a water park, a stadium and a Ferrari park. For entertainment it is worth choosing places more interesting. All hotels offer excellent facilities. It is better not to go to the water park and Ferrari park with young children, there simply is nothing to occupy them. Although in both of these places it is very interesting for adults. In the store at Ferrari Park you can buy unique souvenir products with logos. Prices are relatively inexpensive compared to similar products elsewhere.

The stadium is intended for concerts and races. The noise and roar from the fireballs is heard every day, but it does not bother at all. In the room all this is not heard.

The island's infrastructure is improved annually, so after some time there will be a real paradise. But the prices, of course, will not remain the same.

Sea and beach





The island has one beach, which is used by guests of all hotels. Customers at Yas Island Rotana Abu Dhabi 4 can visit it for free. For strangers, the entrance costs about 500 rubles. You can also relax in cozy tents with awnings, which cost 2500 rubles.

Beach towels are available at the entrance. The coastal zone is equipped with comfortable sun loungers, tables, benches, mattresses, ottomans and umbrellas. The beach is very clean, lifeguards are on duty, there is a toilet and shower. There is a playground nearby. True, there are no entertainments on the beach for children, only sea and sand.

The road to the beach takes only 15 minutes. For those who do not like to walk, buses go. Schedules are issued at the reception.

About hotel services

For children, the complex has a shallow pool with a canopy. There is also an aquazone with a jacuzzi for adults. Towels are provided upon entry. For children there is a playground. Sauna and gym are free of charge.

Nutrition is full. Many recommend taking breakfast only, so as not to become attached to time. Breakfast includes fresh croissants, eggs, stuffed omelettes, gravy pancakes and buns. A lot of dairy dishes, cheeses, vegetables and fruits.

If you wish, you can dine in the hotel restaurant. Themed dishes are served at dinner. Drinks are available for a fee. Everything is very tasty, but for children this menu is completely inappropriate: the dishes are quite spicy. You can diversify the diet in the nearest restaurants. Although the hotel has an excellent assortment, especially fruits and desserts.

Excursions

Sightseeing tours are offered by the Yas Island Rotana Abu Dhabi hotel itself. The tours that most agencies offer already include several excursion programs. But you can independently go on an interesting journey. For example, many guests recommend a trip to Dubai or to Jumeirah on the coast. You can also go to the Bourge Khalif or visit the aquarium. Independent trips are much cheaper than excursion programs. For young children, admission to almost all establishments is free.

A free shuttle bus runs several times a day from the hotel to the main shopping centers in Abu Dhabi, but shopping is best done in Dubai. There are cheaper and more choices. You should definitely visit the singing fountains. This spectacle for a long time leaves in memory indescribable sensations. In addition, this entertainment is completely free. Entrance to the aquarium is also free of charge. The outside is also very interesting and there is something to see.

Yas Island Rotana Abu Dhabi is a great place to relax. Everything here is at the highest level (food, service, accommodation), but with small children it is better not to go here. However, practically nothing is adapted for children's recreation here. No children's menu, playrooms, entertainment on the beach.
