The best hotels of Montenegro: reviews and photos of tourists

Montenegro, or as they call it in the West - Montenegro, invites you to summer vacations from May to mid-autumn. The average summer temperature is about 28 degrees. The cuisine of Montenegro is represented by a large number of vegetables, meat and spices. Fish goulash is very common. Holidays in Montenegro hotels are unforgettable emotions and a huge selection of inexpensive tours.

The most popular resorts in Montenegro: Budva, Becici, Herceg Novi, St. Stephen, Ulcinj, Petrovac, Sutomore.

Tourists love to visit Montenegro, because it is an inexpensive and quality vacation. Based on reviews and photos of tourists, our small rating of the best hotels in Montenegro has been compiled.

Budva

Budva resort is an old small town with small narrow streets. It is surrounded by an ancient fortress wall. Budva is a bustling city: in the summer, various music festivals are held here. During the holiday season, discos and casinos are open around the clock.

There are many other resort attractions in the city. The magnificent pebble Slavic beach and Magren beach invite tourists to relax on the seashore.

Budva hotels in Montenegro offer relaxation for every taste: it can be accommodation in an expensive hotel, or an independent trip to one of the many villas.

Admiral Club Hotel 5 *

Admiral is a comfortable hotel, which is located 200 meters from the beach. The hotel is known for quality service. In 1998, he was awarded a special hospitality prize. Impeccable European cuisine and luxurious interiors attract many celebrities.

In the hotel: 30 rooms, outdoor pool, restaurant, cafe, playground for children, car parking.

Blue Star Hotel 4 *

Cozy hotel with an interesting design was opened in 2004. It is located 300 meters from the sea, not far from the Slavic Beach Hotel in the central part of Budva.

In the hotel: 20 double rooms, two studios, a restaurant with 70 seats, an aperitif bar, wellness center, French bistro, jazz club, Sirius conference hall with 30 seats, Turkish bath.

Hotel Aquamarine 4 *

This is a city hotel 300 meters from the beach. Near the hotel is the football stadium "Magren".

In the hotel: 6 rooms, 18 apartments, restaurant, aperitif bar.

Hotel Avala Grand Hotel & Villas 3 *

The hotel is located close to the sights of the Old Town, close to restaurants, souvenir shops, cafes. The comfortable Magren beach is just 150 meters away.

In the hotel: 7 floors, 227 rooms, a restaurant, cafes and bars, a casino, a spacious terrace overlooking the beach and the old part of the city, an outdoor and indoor pool, a disco, a beauty salon, three conference rooms, shops, an art gallery.

Hotel "Slavic Beach" 3 *

The huge tourist complex on the Montenegrin coast is built in the Mediterranean style and is located 300 meters from the Old Town, surrounded by a green park.

In the hotel: three-story houses, 703 rooms, a shopping center, restaurants, bars, cafes, several outdoor pools, including a children's pool.

In the rooms: balcony or terrace, shower, toilet.

Hotel Alexander 3 *

The hotel occupies part of the territory of the Slavic Beach complex.

In the hotel: three floors, a restaurant, cafe, bar, conference room, parking.

Hotel "Alexander" refers to the all-inclusive Montenegro hotels.

Park Hotel 3 *

The hotel is built on the seafront, surrounded by a beautiful green park and has its own beach.

Meals: breakfast and dinner - buffet, lunch - menu.

Park Villas 3 *

The complex of villas consists of seventeen detached villas with eight rooms. The complex has a restaurant working according to the "buffet" system.

Beach: fine sand pebbles. Tourists living in villas use the territory and beach of the Park Hotel.

Villa "Dimich"

This is a cozy small building near the government villa "Goritsa". The villa has a restaurant with a terrace. The sea is only a few meters away, and the center of Budva can be reached on foot.

Villa "Boskovich"

The villa consists of two houses: the main three-story and a mini-house. The territory is green and well-groomed, there are cafes and restaurants nearby, 250 meters to the beach.

Becici

Becici is a modern resort located near Budva. A small train constantly carries tourists from Becici to Budva. The resort is a European tourist complex with shops, restaurants and bars. The equipped sandy beaches and azure water create excellent conditions for relaxation and sports.

Splendid Hotel 5 *

Hotel "Splendid" is one of the best hotels in Montenegro. This is the first hotel on the Adriatic where a wellness center was opened.

In the hotel: suites, superior, penthouse and presidential apartments. On the territory there are several restaurants with an outdoor terrace and scenic sea views, a restaurant-bar on the beach, a lobby bar decorated with a winter garden, an aperitif bar. Room service works around the clock.

Hotel Montenegro 4 *

One of the best hotels in Montenegro is located on the banks of the beautiful Becici beach.

In the hotel: 165 rooms, 9 apartments, a conference room, a modern medical center, bars, a beauty salon, a fitness center, swimming pools, a pastry shop, a restaurant, Gallia express restaurant, a beach bar, a night club.

Queen Montenegro Hotel 4 *

The hotel is located in a picturesque part of the city among the beauties of Mediterranean nature on a hill. The beach is just 100 meters away, a pedestrian bridge is built over the road. The luxurious terrace overlooks the Adriatic coast. The large lobby is decorated with a fountain and conservatory.

In the hotel: 236 rooms, restaurants, aperitif bar, indoor and outdoor pools, sports grounds, a conference room, a tavern, a fitness center, a night club.

Hotel "Tara" 3 *

The hotel is next to Becici Beach and the Montenegro Hotel.

In the hotel: outdoor pool, central air conditioning, four elevators, a restaurant for 480 people, a Russian-style bar "Romanov", shops. The conference hall is equipped with specialized equipment for holding seminars, it takes 500 people. It consists of a stall for 400 people and a gallery for 100 people. The large stage is suitable for various events. A sports field for indoor soccer, volleyball and tennis invites guests to play sports.

Villa Magnolia

The complex consists of several two and three-story villas and is located next to the Tara Hotel in a green park. All rooms have a balcony and are equipped with new furniture.

Meals: guests vacationing in villas are served in the restaurant of the Tara hotel according to the "buffet" system (breakfast and dinner).

Villa Tamara

This is a new villa, which is located on the second line behind the hotel complexes. The villa has a large terrace, all rooms are very comfortable with new furniture.

Villa "Bel Mar" 4 *

The villa consists of three buildings, each of which is completely individual. The villa has a swimming pool, sunbeds and umbrellas for residents free of charge. The rooms have air conditioning and a balcony. Most rooms have sea views.

Today on the island there are hotels of the highest category, which can provide the opportunity for complete privacy and a quiet, relaxing holiday in a great location. Crystal clear water, hiking in the mountains, delicious dishes of Balkan cuisine - all this is wonderful Montenegro!

Tourists in their reviews note the absence of a language barrier and the special kindness of people.

Another main question worries travelers of Montenegro - what is the best choice: hotel or villa? Studying reviews of hotels in Montenegro, you can find out what tourists recommend villas. After all, living in a villa is like you came to European friends to the country. And there are not so many hotels on the coast, and you can always choose a room in a villa. This country is not for lazy hotel rest, but for those who like to travel. Good hotels are expensive and often do not include meals. It would be better to rent a car, and drive around the country on your own, stopping for the night in cozy little villas.

Great views, clean mountain air, a clear sea, cheap and tasty food, the kindness of the local population - this is what awaits travelers to Montenegro!
