Flagman Beach (Pirogovo): rest, prices, reviews, how to get

Summer is the time when you want a bright mood, an excellent tan, soft drinks, sports games in the fresh air, swimming. Absolutely everyone wants to plunge into the atmosphere of this season. There are various options for summer holidays. Some choose trips to hot countries, for example, to Turkey, Egypt or Cyprus. Others just leave for Europe. However, many people remain in their homeland. There can be many reasons, which is why in Russia there are many recreation centers, hotels, and rest houses. But what to do to people who are not going to go there? Visit beaches and places that are more or less nearby, since there are a lot of them on the territory of the Russian Federation. Today I would like to talk about the "Flagship" (beach). Mytishchi, where this resting place is located, is located near Moscow. Therefore, it is loved by many tourists.





Pirogovskoe Reservoir

This reservoir extends from Chivereva to the Pirogov dam. It is connected to two other reservoirs; a dam has been built on its eastern part . This area is a great place for lovers of active and extreme type of relaxation, as it offers:

  • jet skiing;
  • boat trips;
  • water skiing;
  • diving from the tower and other water sports.

The place is also noteworthy for boaters. The reservoir itself was built on the territory of the urban-type settlement Pirogovskoye, in which about 10 thousand people live. In its own way, a charming place has its own interesting history and culture. There is a woodworking plant and a power station. On the territory of the Pirogovsky reservoir there are many recreation centers and beaches, one of which is the flagman beach.

Tourists reviews

Simple, inexpensive, but at the same time the most beautiful vacation gives the beach "Flagman". Reviews of tourists, both regular and casual guests, are mostly positive. Visitors enthusiastically respond to the magnificent nature and beautiful views of the Pirogovsky reservoir. Useful Tips

A few tips will help you spend your holidays with benefit and comfort, and eliminate minor troubles.

  • Since this place is popular in autumn, it is better to leave the house early and arrive at the beach no later than 11, otherwise you risk being left without a place ‚Äúunder the sun‚Äù.
  • It is better to buy food and soft drinks in advance, as the cafe and shop, which are located on the beach, offer them at inflated prices.
  • Do not forget to bring along various skin care products and to protect it, in order to protect yourself from exposure to sunlight.
  • Do not stay long under the sun, there is the opportunity to receive a sunstroke; be sure to wear a hat.
  • If possible, after exposure to the sun for about half an hour, plunge into the water. When in water, be extremely careful.
  • Do not swim where there is too much depth.
  • Do not immerse yourself in water while intoxicated! Unfortunately, the rescue team does not work on some beaches and all responsibility for your own life rests solely with you.




Follow these simple rules - and you will get the most out of your vacation and be safe. But do not forget about other people who, like the rest, came to rest. Do not loudly turn on your music, scream, litter on the beach, especially leave glass bottles that are dangerous objects in such a place. Be polite with people, treat them the way you want to be treated. Do not make a fire on the beach and think about the safety of those people who are with you.
