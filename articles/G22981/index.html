How to make a rabbit out of paper: master classes

Unusual paper crafts will interest both children and adults. We offer you a choice of master classes: how to make a rabbit out of paper. Take interest in this craft of your kids and make it together. It will be interesting and informative.

Master class 1: how to make a rabbit out of paper





To create an eared one, you need a square sheet of paper. We add it according to the following instructions. On the table, place the sheet like a rhombus. Then wrap the edges on both sides in the form of triangles. Then scissor cut a thin strip at the top in the middle of the product. Fold the left edge of the sheet at the top. This corner will be the ear of our hare. Do the same with the right side. Ears are ready.
You can bend them. Proceed to the paws. Bend the back of the craft inside. Everything - a paper hare made with his own hands. Draw his eyes. If you still paint a paper friend, it will look realistic.

Workshop 2: how to make eraser from paper modules

Before you learn how to make a rabbit out of paper, you need to collect 520 triangles. To make them, you need a rectangular sheet of paper (A4). Modules should be two centimeters in width and height. A hare can be made of white paper or color, and you can also use notes for notes. They are great for assembling triangular modules. So, take a rectangular piece of paper and fold it in half. Bend the workpiece, as if plotting the middle. Flatten back. Fold the two corners in the middle. Turn the workpiece over and lift the edges up. Next, bend the corners by bending through a triangle.

Tuck in the edges. Fold the blank in half. Now proceed to the assembly process.

DIY assembly scheme

Take two triangles and insert into the third. In this way, connect other modules in a chain. Collect two rows of 48 pieces. In the third put on in a checkerboard pattern. Turn out a product accurately. Take 24 modules and do five rows in the same way. Proceed to the head. Wear the same number of triangles with the long side inward and, accordingly, the short side outward. Increase the next row by six. That is, put on two modules for every fourth. Collect your head like this: eight rows of 30 modules. In the latter, bring the triangles together. Give the head a ball shape. To assemble the ears, take six pieces of modules and fold them as the first row. Then put on five modular triangles, then six. Perform so seven rows. And in the eighth, narrow down to three. Do the other ear in a similar fashion. The craft is almost ready. Make decorative details. Cut the collar, eyes, nose and smile out of colored paper. Glue everything. Handles are best made of thick cardboard, put them between the modules and glue for strength. Next, take a rectangular sheet, make cuts and curl with scissors. The result is a bang. All is ready!

Master class 3: how to make a hare from paper

Take a square sheet of paper, bend it diagonally. Measure two centimeters at the bottom. Bend along the line. Turn the product over. Measure this width at the top of the triangle. Bend the corner and bottom edges. Turn over the workpiece. Bring the appearance of the bunny in order. Draw a black felt-tip pen eyes, nose and teeth. Such a nice craft will be a great gift or table decoration for Easter.

How interesting and exciting it is to collect paper crafts. The hare turns out almost alive and looks spectacular.
