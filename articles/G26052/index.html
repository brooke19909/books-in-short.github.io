Faros Hotel 4 * (Cyprus, Ayia Napa): hotel description, services, reviews

Greece is a wonderful place to relax. Many people come here to enjoy the sights, the warm sea and clean beaches. And each person, before buying tickets, picks up a hotel in which he will live, while in Greece. And Faros Hotel 4 * is becoming a very popular choice.





Location

There is a hotel complex in the resort of Ayia Napa. This is a resort town located in the eastern part of Cyprus, a 5-minute drive from Protaras. It is very attractive for tourists, and all thanks to its amazing beaches with clean golden sand. In addition, it is here that you can go windsurfing, go water skiing and try diving. By the way, all the beaches of the resort have a very important award. Namely, the EU Blue Flag.

Here is the Faros Hotel 4 *. It is located in a picturesque area. It is surrounded by a beautiful garden with lush vegetation. And in the immediate vicinity of the building is the sea. And the promenade, with many bars, shops and nightlife. Therefore, this hotel complex is attractive to young people.

By the way, it is worth noting that the airport of Larnaca is also relatively close. To go to him for 30-40 minutes. But very close to the hotel there is the Talassa Museum, the monastery of Agia Napa and many other interesting places.
