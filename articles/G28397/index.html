Waterpark "Caribia" in Perovo (Moscow)

More than a year has passed since the Karibia water park opened in Perovo, in the east of Moscow. Complexes of water attractions of this kind are very popular all over the world. And Moscow is no exception. This circumstance puts their creators and developers in the conditions of fierce competition. They are forced to constantly invent and invent something new to attract the attention of the public and constantly look back at what has already been achieved in the field of design and construction of objects of this purpose.



Water park "Caribia": reviews of experts and impressions of visitors

After a year of successful operation of the water attraction complex in Perovo, it is quite possible to draw some preliminary results of the project. The main thing that distinguishes the Caribia water park in a favorable way is the design of the project, designed in the same style, combining the artistic image of which is the exotic of the Caribbean. The creators of the water park took into account world experience in designing such complexes, and the best is taken from it. Everyone here can find entertainment according to their taste. For lovers of thrills, the Caribia water park has several slides of great height and complex spatial configuration. The very names of some slides promise those who climb them a good dose of adrenaline ("Free Fall", "Black Hole"). The central element of the entire composition of the complex of water attractions is a large wave pool, in which the trajectories of descents from all slides end.

It features a spacious pool with hydromassage equipment and large jacuzzi bathtubs. Of course, the youngest guests are not forgotten, for them the Caribia water park has a separate pool-paddling pool with a small slide and a variety of floating toys. On the second floor there is a bath complex. It has several branches that correspond to different national traditions in this area. These are Russian, Finnish, Roman and Japanese baths. At the water park you can find additional services and entertainment: bowling, billiards, rooms with cosmetic and massage treatments. There are several catering facilities with restaurant-level cuisine. You can rent a banquet hall for family and corporate events. In the summer, this can also be done on the roof of the complex.


"Caribia", water park: prices and opening hours

There is a complex of water attractions in Perovo without days off and holidays. It is available to visitors from ten in the morning until ten in the evening. The pricing policy is rather complicated, and tariffs vary quite significantly. A full adult ticket for the entire twelve-hour day costs 2150 rubles on weekdays and 3040 rubles on weekends. For children up to one and a half meters tall - 1100 rubles on weekdays and 2000 rubles on weekends. You can take a two-hour ticket, it will cost 800 rubles for adults and 465 rubles for children on weekdays. And, accordingly, at 1130 rubles for adults and 800 rubles for children in the morning. Billing from six in the evening on Friday occurs at weekend rates. Children taller than 120 centimeters have the opportunity to go to the water park for free.
