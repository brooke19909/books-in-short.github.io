Zantina Hotel 2 * (Crete, Greece): description of the hotel, services, reviews

Crete is one of the largest islands in Greece, the largest in fifth place in the Mediterranean. It is located in its eastern part and is surrounded by three seas: Cretan, Libyan and Ionian.

In ancient times, the island was called Kaftor, but today it is popular under the more modern name of Crete. The infrastructure of the island is more developed in the tourist direction. This is one of the most popular European resorts. Companies, selecting a tour for customers, if possible, always advise Greece for a comfortable vacation. Crete is one of the most beautiful and romantic islands in Europe, which can offer tourists an exciting vacation. This is a great place on the map, attracting with its sights and extravagance.

In addition to a wide variety of museums and unusual castles, art exhibitions, elite restaurants and bars, on the island of Crete you need to see the main thing, or rather feel. An unforgettable vacation on the Mediterranean coast, a variety of excursions, many tastings will help to feel the full flavor of a truly interesting and memorable trip.

Hotels of the island comply with European quality standards. It has cozy and beautiful rooms, a continental breakfast, and a number of additional services and entertainment. Staying in one of the complexes, tourists will be pleasantly surprised by the attention and hospitality of the staff. Those who dream of a fascinating beach vacation will be attracted by the hotels near the sea. Holidays in Crete will be unforgettable.

Weather in Crete in winter



The island is dominated by a Mediterranean and North African climate. But the first one still dominates, and this means that it rains in winter and dry in summer. Basically, the average temperature in winter does not exceed +16 degrees with high humidity of 75%. Little snow falls, mostly it remains lying in the mountains, it is practically absent on the plains. The cold winds of boron and Euroclidon bring abundant cloud cover, but then beautiful weather sets in.

In the winter months, the weather prevails unstable. Most of January it rains and it is very cold. But there are such days, people call them quiet, then there is warm, dry weather. February is usually much warmer and hotter than January. During this period, the buds even begin to swell in the trees. The winter months are considered the low season for the tourism business. For those who love a warm beach holiday, in principle, at this time in Crete there is nothing to do. But for fans to admire the ancient historical buildings - this is an ideal period. Since the weather is changeable, it can be warm or very cold.

December

In December, there is a decrease in daylight hours by about 6.5 hours. The low sun slightly warms the earth, and in the highlands, in general, snow lies. In the mountains, frosts are very common. Water warms up to about 18-19 degrees. In the absence of wind, a beautiful natural phenomenon occurs that looks like swirling steam.

January on the island

This month, the longitude of the day increases, this significantly affects the heating of the air. At night the temperature is from + 7-12, during the day + 11-16 degrees, sometimes it is cooler. The cyclones coming from the western side of the island significantly spoil the weather, they bring with them coolness and rainy gloomy weather conditions.

February

The weather in Crete in February is more unstable. This month, daylight hours increase, the probability of heavy rains slightly decreases. But the dominant northerly winds (borras and mesoborras) continue to cool the troposphere of Crete: at night the temperature is + 7-12, during the day - plus 12-17 degrees. In certain periods, cold air currents from Europe can cool the surface layer of the troposphere to zero, but the sea remains warm: plus 15-16 degrees. February is the coldest month.

High season on the island

To this season travel companies include all the summer months. Hot weather on the island is from the very beginning of summer until late August. Air temperature ranges from about 28 to 30 degrees. And sometimes 40 degrees can be seen on the thermometer. It can also be noted that the island is not very crowded with tourists. True, this is observed in cities that are located far from the center and offer minimal living conditions for a fairly small fee. Also in the midst of the high season, you should go to the northern part of the island, on the shores of the Aegean Sea.

Swimming season in Crete

The bathing season on the island, as a rule, is opened in the second half of April, because at that time the water in the Mediterranean warms up to about 21 degrees. More comfortable only at the end of May. This month the sea is more pleasant and warm. The maximum temperature of the water can be observed somewhere from the middle or the end of summer, when there is sultry heat. In summer, it is advisable to go out to sea only in the morning or in the evening, as in the daytime the sun is very harmful and dangerous. In general, those who want to relax comfortably and lie on the beach should come to the island in September. Indeed, at this time the heat is falling, the sun is not dangerous, and the sea is more pleasant.
