Modes and Functions

Additional modes in no way affect the production of bread, sauces and main dishes. However, they greatly simplify the entire cooking process. So what modes does the Redmond bread machine boast of?

  1. Maintaining heat for an hour.
  2. "Delayed start" of cooking for up to 15 hours.
  3. Weight / color selection of bread crust.
  4. Non-volatile memory for 10 minutes.

If you expect guests, but they are delayed, then the mode of maintaining heat will not allow the bread, pilaf or other dishes to cool. As the temperature decreases, hot air begins to circulate inside the multicooker.

The mode of delaying the start of cooking will help to bake bread at the right time. For example, if you put ingredients into the oven in the evening, then in the morning the aroma of freshly baked bread will wake the whole family.

The weight selection function is set depending on the amount of ingredients to be filled in, which are written in the Redmond cookbook, it also describes which weight is preferable to choose: 500, 750 or 1000 g. A crust of bread can be made dark, medium or light.





When it becomes necessary to free the outlet or the power is turned off, the memory mode is simply irreplaceable. The REDMOND RBM-M1907 bread machine in this case remembers all the settings and after switching on continues to work from the moment of interruption.

How to use?

Before using the bread machine, all components must be washed with a soft sponge and dried. After that, you can choose your favorite recipe from the book, insert the mixing paddle and use the measuring spoon and glass to fill the ingredients into the mold. If you need to bake bread, then the bucket should be greased with butter first - this will not allow the product to stick to the walls. By the way, in order for the bread to rise, you need to fill the yeast and salt not in one place, but distribute it on the entire surface of the flour or in different corners of the form.

After these manipulations, you again need to look into the book, there it is described in detail how to choose the weight, color of the crust and the baking mode. Press the "Start" button and cook until the end of the mode. A loud signal will signal the end of cooking.

Some Tips Before Buying

So that the bread maker does not fail prematurely, when choosing, you need to take into account several tips that will help save time and money.

  1. Be sure to look at the warranty period in the documents. On the Redmond model, he must be at least 2 years old.
  2. Pay attention to the coating of the bucket form and the blades. Teflon should not be scratched, uneven or have chips, roughness. Check how the bucket is inserted into the case.
  3. Inspect the box that the oven was in. Dents on it say that the equipment was dropped or hit. Check for cracks in the case.
  4. Be sure to ask a consultant to demonstrate the operation of the furnace. Press the "Start" button and listen to how the mechanism works. There should be no extraneous noise, squeaks and jolts. First, the blade should rotate slowly, and then its speed increases.
  5. Check the operation of the heating element by selecting the "Baking" program, and the mixer operator - with the "Dough" mode.

In addition, you must provide a warranty card for the bread maker. The instruction should also be, as well as the check. After payment, also look in the box, it should have all the accessories.
