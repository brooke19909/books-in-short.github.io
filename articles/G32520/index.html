Three ways to make a rose bud out of paper

The most beautiful flower is, of course, a rose. She is loved by many women. It looks very attractive and its paper copy. Everyone can create a rose bud from paper on their own, because there are different ways to do it. Let's talk about the three most interesting of them.





The ancient myth of the rose

Flowers cannot but please the eye. They decorate the interior of the room, making it natural and vibrant. But, for example, real roses wither quickly, lose their attractiveness. Now popular are flowers made from corrugated paper, from which you can collect a beautiful bouquet.

There is a legend associated with the rose. A goddess named Aphrodite bathed in the sea when she emerged from the water, then foam flowed from her body, turning into beautiful snow-white flowers.

They acquired a red color when Aphrodite ran through a thorny thicket to help her beloved, and blood flowed from the wounds received from the thorns. Of course, this is just a legend. Now everyone can make a rosebud of any color from plain or corrugated paper.

Simplified version





So how to make a rose out of paper? First, we need the following materials:

  1. One sheet of thick colored paper of the desired color.
  2. Any pencil.
  3. Scissors.
  4. PVA.

detailed instructions

  1. From a sheet of paper we make a square and draw a spiral on it. Ideal lines are difficult to achieve, so do not try hard. Freehand drawing is a creative version of a flower. On the specified line, cut a strip.
  2. Turn the strip from the center of the spiral. In order for the rosebud to be fixed, it is necessary to add glue to the base.
  3. The paper flower is ready. You can add leaves of green color or collect a bouquet of several roses.

Medium difficulty

Making a rose bud from corrugated paper is not as difficult as it might seem at first glance.





The following materials must be prepared:

  1. Corrugated paper.
  2. Scissors.
  3. A wire or twig for the stalk.
  4. Duct tape.

Instructions for execution

  1. From corrugated paper, you need to cut 15 parts in shape resembling a heart, and 5 teardrop-shaped.
  2. Each part or petal is stretched from the middle to the edge to give natural bends.
  3. Using a pencil, bend the edges of the petals. To get a rosebud, it is advisable to bend the edges harder.
  4. The stem can be wrapped with duct tape for naturalness.
  5. Assembly is carried out in layers with drop-shaped parts. All elements are attached to each other with adhesive tape, and the first is worn on a wire.

The bud can be any size. Original and interesting photos with rosebuds made of corrugated paper are obtained.

The option is harder - with a surprise

If an ordinary paper rose is too simple for you, then try to make a bud with a surprise inside. Prepare the following materials:

  • thicker corrugated paper;
  • scissors;
  • gun with glue;
  • wooden sticks or skewers;
  • Ferrero or Raffaello sweets.

You can take corrugated paper in different colors to make original buds. Two rectangular parts with a width of 7 cm and a height of 10 cm must be cut out of paper. Two strips are obtained, which must be folded so that the corrugated strips become horizontal. The edges need to be rounded. Swap the petals and collapse. They need to be stretched.

Pull the first petal up. We stretch two folded fingers and make a small hole for the candy. Inside our bud we put candy. On the lower edge of the petal, distribute the glue. Wrap the candy and insert the stick or skewer.

Clay does not hold candy, but paper, so getting a treat is not difficult. The rosebud keeps its shape due to the density of the paper.





Now it's time to make sepals for our flower. From corrugated paper of green color, cut a rectangle 4 cm wide and 10 cm long. We make two sepals. From the edges, it is necessary to make flagella, and the lower edge to stretch. On the lower edge we distribute the glue in a circle and fasten the sepals to the base of the bud. Wrap the skewer or stalk from bottom to top with green paper.

In fact, creating a bud is not as easy as making a rose. But everyone can do it, if you understand the technique. Each time, paper flowers will turn out better and better.

The perfect decor

Paper rose fits well into the interior of the room, is the subject of decor, which can be done independently. Paper roses make the room interesting and original. A bouquet of pink buds, and even with sweets inside, will be an unusual gift for a loved one.

Paper analogs of fresh flowers fit perfectly into the interior of any room. Most often, compositions are placed in vases of various shapes. Paper counterparts are made very tiny or gigantic, it all depends on desire and imagination.

In a private or country house, a rose bud can be useful for decorating flights of stairs or railings. The festive table also looks original, in the serving of which roses from paper are used.
