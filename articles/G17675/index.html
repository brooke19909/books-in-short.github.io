Centro Sharjah Rotana 3: description, rooms, services, reviews

Description: Centro Sharjah Rotana 3 is a stylish, modern and affordable business class hotel. It is designed by one of the most successful hotel groups in the region and is designed for a new generation of businessmen who prefer elegance and functionality at a reasonable price. Designed for this purpose - from scratch, Centro Sharjah Rotana 3, represents a new genre of executive hospitality. The chic appearance of the hotel is an unprecedented landmark for thrifty business-class travelers. Its distinguishing feature is modern architecture and a uniquely organized living space. The exterior of the hotel, its clear and modern interiors meet the necessary requirements and advanced technologies. Centro Sharjah Rotana 3 is located in close proximity to Sharjah Airport, not far from the business districts of Sharjah, only a 15-20 minute taxi ride from the city center. Historically, this city was one of the richest in this region with a settlement that has existed for more than 5000 years.





Rooms: Centro Sharjah Rotana 3 was built in 2011. The number of floors in the hotel is 4, the rooms are 306, of which single / double are 238, the triple are 68. The number of rooms with several rooms is 32. All rooms are carefully designed to maximize space and light, providing hotel guests with the latest technology, and modern furniture in classic style creates comfort and coziness. Enhanced business opportunities are built into every room: Wi-Fi Internet access. In addition, all rooms are equipped with panoramic windows from the ceiling to the floor, LCD TV, mini fridge, safe. Tea and coffee are provided free of charge.

Meals: The price includes three meals a day, which is provided in the hotel restaurant. Also on site there is a grocery store where you can buy everything you need.

Beach: no.





Additional information: Creating unique living quarters in modern conditions, Centro Sharjah by Rotana 3 offers amenities that are specifically focused on the needs of business class representatives, including three meals a day, a restaurant, a salon, a grocery store, a well-equipped business center, a swimming pool, 2 conferences hall and gym. The color, location, lighting and equipment have been carefully selected in order to ensure maximum performance during meetings, seminars and various business meetings. So, each conference room is fully equipped with all the tools and technologies necessary for effective business. In addition, the executive centers of the hotel provide additional services, including Internet access anywhere in the hotel and other modern amenities. And those who wish can get a boost of energy from a workout in the gym or refresh themselves in the stunning pool from 7 am to 10 pm His services as a whole can be described in six words: convenience, style, quality, the latest technology, attention to detail and an individual approach. In short, for those who value effective solutions and efficient services, the best choice is Centro Sharjah Rotana 3.





Reviews: This is one of the best 3 star hotels in Dubai. The hotel staff is very welcoming and polite. The rooms are spacious and modern. Breakfast is excellent and has many options. The pool is also nice, clean and large. It is recommended to all those who make business trips, as well as those who are looking for maximum comfort at an affordable price.
