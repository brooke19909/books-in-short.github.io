How to make weapons from the "Lego" -constructor?

The world-famous Lego constructor, which first appeared in 1958, has long been loved by Russian children. From plastic parts that are easily attached to each other, you can assemble a robot, tank or gun. An understandable and accessible instruction is attached to any Lego set, following which you can make any crafts yourself. The advantage of this designer is also that, combining parts from different sets, it is easy to come up with and create unusual and interesting toys. Today we understand how to make a "Lego" weapon.





Instruction manual

If you collect crafts with your children, start by explaining safety precautions to your child. The difficulty lies in the fact that the details of the designer are very small, they are not recommended for children under 3 years of age, as babies can accidentally put plastic in their mouth or nose. This is fraught with serious danger to the health of the child. Starting a game in Lego is better with large parts and simple designs.

Material

To understand how to make weapons from Lego, you first need to prepare the designer himself. For the manufacture of the gun you need the basic parts. These are thin and thick plates. In the absence of the necessary parts, they can easily be exchanged for other parts. The main thing is to understand the essence of the assembly.





How to make weapons from Lego?

We start making crafts with the handle. To do this, take 6 bricks (with eight spikes), connect them in a checkerboard pattern, two in a row. Next, fix two parts from the top (with six spikes). Insert two bricks into the empty spikes (with 12 spikes). This will be part of the future trigger design. Now we attach two plates (with 4 spikes) to the very edge of the thin part. Next, we install two thin parts without spikes. And we continue to "grow" the handle. We lay from a wide edge along 3 bricks (with eight spikes), next two more of the same. In the remaining spikes, insert two more bricks perpendicular to adjacent parts.





Now we will move on to the design of the barrel of the future pistol. We attach a thin plate (with 36 spikes) to the extreme parts of the trigger mechanism from above. We lay it in such a way that it rests only on two extreme spikes. On both sides of the future trunk from the muzzle side we install two thin plates (with 4 spikes) on top of each other, next two long plates (with 8 spikes) next.

To understand how to make a weapon out of Lego, you need to carefully look at the picture offered here. To connect the handle and the upper part of the barrel, insert two thin plates (with 4 spikes) from the bottom of the trigger (you should have four spikes under the upper part of the last brick of the handle). Now between the barrel and the handle you should have an empty field the size of 12 spikes. Fix the structure by inserting bricks with 8 and 4 spikes into this space.

The next level of crafts - parts with cavities, and between them - plates without spikes. It is almost clear how to make weapons from Lego. It remains to install a few details. On top of the crafts, we lay the plates without spikes, filling the entire upper part of the trunk. If you want to make a more powerful gun, then build up a few more layers of the structure. Now your craft is ready, and you know how to make weapons from Lego!
