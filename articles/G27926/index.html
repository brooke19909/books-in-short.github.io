Waterpark "Limpopo" - the first in the Urals

The Limpopo water park (its photo below) received its first visitors in 2005 (the first such complex opened in the Urals). Under its roof there are several sections:

  • aquazone;
  • places of special (increased) comfort;
  • pools;
  • food court.

Here you can simply relax, and if you want to get a thrill of sensations, and improve your health. The complex has water attractions (adults, children), a variety of slides (17 species), swimming pools (including hydromassage), waterfalls, coffee houses and cafes.

In one day, the Limpopo water park can serve 1000 people (at a time). Thanks to modern equipment, the most comfortable conditions are created here: along with an integrated water purification system and supply and exhaust ventilation, constant air and water temperatures (+30 and +28 , respectively) are maintained at a humidity of 60%. In the aquazone are constantly representatives of the rescue service.





Waterpark "Limpopo". Water rides (adults only)

  1. "Anaconda" - the length of the closed pipe is 146 m, the inner diameter is 1.4 m. The descent is carried out in a circle with a height difference of 16 m.
  2. "Orange River" - descent into the gutter, performed "head back", length - 67.4 m, drop - 8 m).
  3. "Free Fall" - the descent is similar to the previous attraction. Length - 44 m, drop - 16.3 m.
  4. "Black hole" - descent in a closed pipe with light effects (93 m, with a vertical drop of 12.5 m).
  5. "Multislide" - a three-lane slide (open, 75.4 m, with a difference of 14 m).

Waterpark "Limpopo". Children's water rides

  • "Red River" - a gutter with a difference of 4 m and a length of 33.5 m (open).
  • "Ship" - consists of five slides with a difference of 95 cm, length - 1.7 m.
  • "Cliff" - three slides with a difference of 2.6 m, length 7 m.
  • "Hydrotube" - two elements with a total length of 6.8 m, with a height difference of 2.7 m.
  • "Slow river" is a basin encircling an aquazone (with a light course).
  • The wave pool (area 400 sq. M).
  • Swimming pool (25 x 11 m).




High comfort zone

This is a private area with an individual Finnish sauna with a font. Here you can enjoy drinks, a hot dinner or light snacks. The maximum number of guests is 10 people.

Bars, cafes

The territory of the bath complex has several bars and cafes where you can order juices, tea, cocktails, pizza, french fries, barbecue, fish cooked on charcoal. Children's cafe has its own special menu. Prices are quite reasonable.

Waterpark "Limpopo" takes on the organization of holidays for children

There are discounts for a birthday person in the form of a free visit (you need to have a birth certificate with you). Your child and guests will be provided with an individual entrance, a dressing room, a playroom (soft), a reception room. If you wish, you can easily agree on the order of corporate invitations to the holiday. Children can be accompanied by adults (up to 3 people).

An order is placed under the following conditions:

  • number of children - up to 18 people (one room) or up to 12 (another type of room);
  • the minimum amount is 1000 rubles .;
  • the maximum age of the birthday person and guests is 15 years;
  • obligatory presence of the birthday man himself with a document confirming the date of birth.

At an additional cost, you can use the address mailing of invitations, the work of animators, the services of a photographer. Reception is possible.

Discounts are provided for regular group visits (non-holiday) with complex lunches served.

Waterpark "Limpopo", prices:

  • 2-hour rest - from 100 rubles (children) to 650 rubles (adult);
  • 4-hour stay - from 100 rubles to 850, respectively;
  • evening rest (19.00-22.00) - 3 hours for the price of 2 (from 100 rubles to 850).

If the entrance ticket is purchased between 10.00-11.00, then a full day at the water park will cost you only 600 rubles (adult ticket). The cost of a children's ticket is from 100 rubles.

On weekends, prices rise slightly.
