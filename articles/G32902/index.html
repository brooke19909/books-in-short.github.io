Tips for users: how to activate a Beeline SIM card

In most cases, when buying a mobile phone, an SIM card is additionally attached to it. To start using the device, it must be activated. If you are an experienced user, then for you this will be a matter of just a few minutes. How to be a beginner? This article will help them. It describes in detail how to activate a Beeline SIM card.



If you purchased a new cell phone, you should know that without activating the start balance you will not be able to make calls and send SMS messages. What then to do? How to activate a Beeline SIM card correctly? We offer you step-by-step instructions.

Step number 1. So, you have an envelope in your hands, in which there is a SIM card. Carefully open it and get a SIM card. Separate it from the plastic base. Sometimes it can be quite difficult to do this, so you can not do without something sharp. It can be manicure scissors or a pocket knife.

Step number 2. Separating the SIM card from the base, insert it into the mobile phone. To do this, the device must be turned off and its back cover removed. Certain cell phone models also mean removing the battery. The SIM card should take place in a slot designed specifically for it. If you cannot find it in any way, then refer to the instructions that came with your mobile phone. Before activating a Beeline SIM card, make sure that it is correctly located in the slot. The yellow contact strips should be on the bottom. If so, then you can put the battery back in place and close the back cover.



Step No. 3. Turn on the mobile phone by pressing the appropriate button and holding it for several seconds. How to activate a Beeline SIM card? To do this, you need to enter a PIN code. You will find it under a protective layer deposited on a plastic base. Carefully erase it and get the coveted pin code.

Step number 4. When answering the question of how to activate a Beeline SIM card, it is worth noting that during this operation it is important not to damage the pin code. If you enter it incorrectly 3 times in a row, then the SIM card will simply be blocked. However, do not worry if this happened to you. You can unlock the SIM card by using the PUK code, which is also hidden under the protective layer. If this does not help you, then contact the nearest communication salon to restore the Beeline SIM card.

If the activation of the SIM card was successful, then you must check the personal account for crediting the starting amount. You can do this in one of three ways:

1) Send a USSD request to * 102 #.

2) Call 0697.

3) Send a balance request using the Beeline SIM menu.
