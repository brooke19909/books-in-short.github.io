The recreation center "Rainbow". Taganrog: reviews and description

The Rainbow recreation center located near Taganrog is a favorite place for relaxation for many travelers. The administration invites you to visit a small resort for all those who wish to have a fun weekend with family or in the company of friends. You can learn more about the institution and read reviews in this article.

Infrastructure

The recreation center "Rainbow" arranged near Taganrog offers all visitors to enjoy nature and swimming in the Miussky estuary. It is located directly on the Sedykh farm and occupies a fairly vast territory. Visitors are offered a choice of rooms of different levels of comfort. Two to four people can live in a room at the same time. The houses are surrounded by deciduous and coniferous trees, which contributes to a healthy vacation. In the summer season, people can not only swim, but also entertain themselves with a game of soccer or volleyball on equipped sites. Especially for children, the administration has created a playground where they can find mates and have fun.

Positive reviews

The location near the Taganrog recreation center "Rainbow" attracts a large number of visitors. Some of them shared their impressions of the time spent. The houses are quite comfortable and equipped in accordance with the standard requirements of visitors. Near each housing there are barbecue facilities where you can cook on fire for large companies. Also available space for logging firewood. The pond is located just a hundred meters from the recreation center "Rainbow". Taganrog is located nearby, and therefore you can go shopping quickly if you have a car. Green spaces create a feeling of relaxation in the middle of the natural environment, which significantly helps to gain strength for future work. Morning and evening fishing will please fans of this lesson. Views of the estuary fascinate the eyes of visitors, but because the rest takes place in one breath. These are the main advantages of this institution, which were noted by visitors.

Negative in the comments

Among the reviews about the recreation center "Rainbow" in Taganrog sounded negative thoughts. Some users did not like the price, which they consider overpriced for such an institution. This point can be considered controversial, because other users have noted a good combination of cost-quality. For a family holiday, nightlife can become a problem, when young people want to have fun. Music plays loudly and sounds are heard in the houses. Holidays with children can also be a problem due to the characteristics of the estuary. There are bulk beaches, but the coastal stripes are small. The entrance to the water is too steep, in a few steps the adult average person will get the liquid to the chest or even the neck. Stairs to enter the reservoir appeared not so long ago. Another significant drawback is the presence of snakes in the water, which can be a big barrier for lovers to swim a lot. The audience cannot be called quiet, although each visitor has his own fenced house.

Summary

It is worth noting that the recreation center "Rainbow" located near Taganrog can be a great place for a weekend trip. This is notified by all visitors who managed to stay at the institution. In terms of price and quality, the option is quite acceptable, in the rooms the furniture is not old, care and cleanliness are visible. In summer, noisy companies can interfere for a quiet family time, but this is not a very significant minus. Parents should definitely watch the children on the beach and keep them out of the water without supervision. The choice of rooms will delight visitors with a variety, because here you can settle in low-cost housing or book a lux room. For most people, the rest left a pleasant impression and the recreation center "Rainbow" is recommended for visiting.
