Cheap Hotels in Lyubertsy: An Overview Of The Popular

Lyubertsy is a small city in the Moscow region, the administrative center of the Lyubertsy district. Many people working in the capital prefer to rent housing here because of reduced prices. Inexpensive hotels in Lyubertsy are suitable for short overnight stays or long stays. Consider the most popular options.

Hostel "cityOK"

A small hotel is located on the street 3rd Post Office, house 29, just a half kilometers from the city center. The hostel offers comfortable rooms, as well as women's and men's rooms with shared accommodation. For a bed here you will have to pay about 300 rubles per day. Many workers who are decorated at Moscow construction sites prefer to settle here. The price does not include meals. However, guests can use the kitchen and utensils provided free of charge. Near the hostel there is a round-the-clock guarded parking. Everyone can also use the wireless Internet.

If you need a more comfortable hotel in Lyubertsy inexpensively for a day, you should pay attention to the double superior rooms of the GorodOK hostel. For living in such a room you will have to pay more than 1000 rubles. The room has a TV and a private bathroom with shower.

"Star"

If you need a hotel in Lyubertsy inexpensively for the night, you can pay attention to the Zvezda hostel. For living here you have to pay only 250 rubles. The economy class hotel is located next to the GorodOk hostel. The exact address is 3rd Post Office, 29A Street. For a small fee, male and female eight-bed rooms with bunk beds can be accommodated. A bed in a six-bedded room will cost a little more (from 300 rubles per night).

Good reviews can be heard about the family room with a large double bed. For the daily rent of such a room you will have to pay 1,500 rubles. The room has a TV and fridge. Amenities are provided on the floor.

Hostel Zvezda offers its guests additional services. Nearby there is a guarded parking lot with 24-hour video surveillance. The hotel has a wood-fired bathhouse. For an additional fee, guests can relax after a hard day at work.

Hotel De Art 13

This hotel chain is popular among Muscovites and visitors. There is a representative office in Lyubertsy. It is located on the street Natasha Kucherevskaya (house 4). You can consider this option if you need an inexpensive hotel for an hour in Lyubertsy. For a stay in a standard room you will have to pay from 330 rubles. A day in such apartments will cost 2000 rubles.

For a romantic evening, the Wedding Deluxe Room is perfect. For an hour in such apartments you have to pay 750 rubles. A night will cost 3500 rubles. Guests have the opportunity to spend time in an atmosphere of luxury. The highlight is the large marble bathroom. Guests have the opportunity to also use the jacuzzi.

Hotel "Moscow Nights"

This place is worth considering for those who are looking for cheap hotels in Lyubertsy. Hourly pay is also possible here.
