We select material

If you want to make beach bags with your own hands, then first decide on the material. Designers from developed countries have long envied the craftsmanship and creativity of our needlewomen. What material is not allowed into the important business of creating bags for the beach is our beautiful half. For most of the fabrics do not have to run to the store. Of course, you can purchase them at any accessories store. But do not spend money on beach bags. You can sew them with your own hands in just a couple of hours. We sew two do-it-yourself beach bags

Very simple

An old T-shirt, a calico pillowcase, thread, scissors and a sewing machine - that‚Äôs all we need. The good news is that you don‚Äôt have to rip anything. Put the t-shirt on the pillowcase and draw a rectangle so that the picture does not fall under the cut. Secure with studs and cut. Fold the knitted and calico parts with the front sides. Get off. Turn and lay the finishing seam. Fold the processed parts - the front and back seamy sides and sew, departing from the edge of 0.7-1 cm. Turn and sew again, departing 1.2-1.5 cm. Cut a rectangle from the pillowcase rests, glue it with glue and sew a long handle. Sew it to the side seams with double stitches.

Sew on the top of the bag;

Fold the almost-assembled bag with its front sides. Look, you might want to make curly sides. Trim to your liking. A bag can either expand to the bottom or narrow;

Stitch the parts of the bag fixed with pins. Treat the stock for beauty and reliability with an oblique trim.
