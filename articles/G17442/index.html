Tropicana Grand Azure 5

Description: Tropicana Grand Azure 5 beach complex is located in the famous Red Sea resort of Sharm El Sheikh. You can drive from the airport to the hotel in a few minutes. The coast of Naama Bay is 20 km from the hotel.



The hotel complex welcomes guests since 2000. Tropicana Grand Azure Hotel consists of several Andalusian-style buildings and landscaped grounds with exotic vegetation and dazzling pools. From the windows of the rooms, guests can enjoy the Sinai Mountains, the sea, the pool and the tropical park.

Tropicana Grand Azure 5 Resort is suitable for youth companies and for families to relax.

Rooms: The large hotel has 820 rooms, including standard, superior and junior suites. The whole family offers spacious rooms with a bedroom and an extra small room. The suites consist of a cozy bedroom and a spacious living room.

Rooms are cleaned daily and sheets are changed every other day.

The apartments with ceramic floors have a balcony, air conditioning, safe, TV, telephone, alarm clock, minibar, satellite TV, internet, desk, refrigerator. The bathroom has a shower, modern hairdryer and bath amenities. Guests have the opportunity to make coffee or tea without leaving their room.

Beach: The hotel has its own sandy beach with a pontoon and coral reefs off the coast. Sun beds, towels, mattresses and umbrellas are provided free of charge.



Meals: The hotel operates on an all-inclusive basis. Meals are provided in the form of self-service. A la carte late dinner is served by waiters. The bars offer freshly squeezed juices, soft drinks and spirits from local producers all day.

Tropicana Grand Azure 5 has six restaurants to suit all tastes. The main restaurant serves international dishes. In Chinese, dishes of different provinces of the country are presented. In Italian - a large selection of Mediterranean cuisine. The fish restaurant offers a selection of seafood delicacies. Refined oriental dishes are served in the Arabian restaurant. Fans of international dishes are invited to the Tropicana restaurant.

Tourist information: Tropicana Grand Azure 5 has a developed infrastructure and offers vacationers an impressive range of services.



On the territory there are several pools, a small water park and two tennis courts. Guests are invited to relax in the sauna, jacuzzi, spa, massage room. There are many water activities at sea: diving, snorkeling, water skiing. Diving courses and equipment rental are available.

For business travelers equipped with a meeting room and office center. Car rental, laundry service, medical assistance are offered. The hotel has parking, a hairdresser, a left-luggage office, an Internet cafe.

The hotel has a well-developed children's service: children's pools and water slides, high chairs in the dining room and cots in the room, children's animation and a mini club.

Reviews: LTI Tropicana Grand Azure is a large beautiful area with pools, waterfalls and fountains. Friendly staff and good cleaning. Varied food and a large selection of restaurants. The rooms have comfortable furniture and a good shower. There is a minibar, a kettle, coffee bags, sugar and cream. Animators work all day . Large selection of excursions and evening entertainment. Pontoon beach due to the abundance of corals off the coast. It is possible to dive and see the amazing underwater life.
