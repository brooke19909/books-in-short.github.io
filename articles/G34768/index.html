Tossa Beach-Tossa Center 4 * is a small hotel located in the heart of the Spanish resort town of Tossa de Mar. Most often, budget tourists who seek a relaxing beach or sightseeing vacation stop there. Proximity to the sea and attractions, low cost of living and high quality service make it an attractive option for families with children and youth. Let us consider in more detail in this article the location and internal structure of the hotel, as well as the reviews of former guests.

Hotel Location

Tossa de Mar is located in northeastern Spain near the border with France. Its coast is considered one of the most beautiful in this region. Hotel Tossa Beach-Tossa Center 4 * is built on a busy street of the city. The distance to the historical center is approximately 200 meters, which means that tourists can easily overcome it on foot. The beach is also only 150 meters away. Near the hotel you can find many restaurants and cafes that serve Spanish dishes for a small amount. There are shops and nightclubs here. Of the attractions, you should definitely visit the rocky coast of the Costa Brava and the 12th-century fortress Villa Vella. The large city of Lloret de Mar is 8 km away.

Tossa Beach-Tossa Center 4 *: hotel description

This resort was built in 1989. It receives tourists almost all year round, closing from mid-March to the end of April for repairs. The last renovation of all residential premises here ended in 2013. In a small area of ‚Äã‚Äã4000 square meters. m. there are 2 four-story buildings. In between, there is an outdoor pool and a sun terrace. The hotel has 190 rooms, designed for two adults. Check-in starts here from 13:00 and lasts until midnight. If you plan to stay in the hotel for less than four nights, you will have to pay an additional 18 euros per day per person. If you check in for 2 days, the surcharge will increase to 24 euros. No security deposit is required upon entry. Release your rooms after the rest is necessary until 10:30. Number of rooms and its equipment

190 comfortable rooms prepared for travelers by the Tossa Beach-Tossa Center 4 * hotel. Tours to this hotel are notable for their low cost, so here you will not see spacious apartments with a luxurious interior. All rooms are standard and are designed for two adults. If desired, tourists can choose rooms with one double or two single beds. Some of the rooms have a small balcony with comfortable armchairs. The hotel overlooks nearby buildings or a patio. On the ground floor there are 4 rooms, equipped for disabled tourists . It is worth noting that extra beds are not available in the room.





A standard suite of amenities is offered by this hotel. In the room tourists will find a small TV with cable channels (1 Russian). For an additional fee, an electronic safe for storing valuables and a mini-refrigerator without filling are provided . To use the phone, tourists must make a deposit to open the line. From June to September, the hotel has a central cooling system, and in winter, on the contrary, heating is turned on. In the bathroom from amenities there is a mirror for makeup or shaving, a built-in hairdryer, bath accessories. An iron and ironing board are available upon request at a surcharge. Tourists can also connect to the room wireless Internet.

Hotel staff tidies up all rooms daily and replaces towels. Bed linen is changed 2 times a week or at the request of residents.

Hotel infrastructure

The Tossa Beach-Tossa Center 4 * hotel (Spain) does not offer a developed infrastructure, but there is everything you need for a comfortable stay. For the needs of tourists, there is a 24-hour reception desk, which also provides currency exchange and car rental. You can leave your car in a private parking lot. Parking fees are charged daily. The lobby has a luggage room for storing large bags and luggage. At the reception, tourists can make a paid call home or send a fax.

Beach and Pool

The Tossa Beach-Tossa Center 4 * complex does not have its own beach area. Tourists usually visit the city coast on their own. 150 meters from the hotel is the municipal beach Playa de Tossa. It is completely sandy, and the entrance to the sea is flat and gentle. Guests can also buy sun loungers and parasols or a beach towel. There is a water entertainment center on the coast.





The hotel also has two outdoor pools. The first is located between the hotel buildings. Its area is 87 square meters. m. Next to it there is a sunbathing area with sunbeds. They are provided free of charge. A second pool is located behind the hotel. It is much smaller - its area is 41 square meters. m. Both pools are filled with fresh water and work only in good weather. In winter, they are closed for reconstruction.

Entertainment and sports

Most tourists prefer to spend time outside the hotel, so the Tossa Beach Center 4 * complex (Tossa de Mar) does not offer guests a large selection of entertainment. Near the pool is an unheated hot tub. For guests there are entertainment programs and evening discos. At special venues, tourists can play volleyball or basketball. For a fee, access to the billiard room is available in the evening. In addition, at the reception guests can purchase tickets for excursions to the sights of Catalonia, including a sightseeing tour of Barcelona.

Nutrition Details

Meals at the Tossa Beach Center 4 * hotel (Tossa-de-Mar) are provided only on the all-inclusive system. It is valid daily from 10:00 to 23:00. It includes breakfast, lunch and dinner, as well as coffee, pastries, ice cream or light snacks between meals. Tourists can order non-alcoholic and alcoholic drinks of local production without restriction. They are poured once in glasses. For breakfast, a European set of dishes is offered, which usually includes omelets, fried eggs, sausages and cheese slices, light salads. For lunch and dinner, tourists can try meat, fish and seafood, fresh vegetables and fruits.





The hotel has one restaurant, which serves a common buffet. There is a bar by the pool where you can enjoy cocktails and refreshments. There is a cafe in the lobby serving light snacks, salads, ice cream and pastries.

Conditions for relaxing with children

Often in Tossa Beach Center 4 * (Spain) tourists with children stop. When they check in, they may ask you to deliver a cradle to their room. The fee for it is 6 euros per day. The restaurant has high chairs for feeding.

The large pool has a shallow bathing area for children. Parents can send their kids to the mini club , which is open daily from 10:00 to 20:00. For children from Russia, a Russian-speaking animator works here. In the evening, a disco is held for children. There is also a playground in the open air where anyone can have fun.
