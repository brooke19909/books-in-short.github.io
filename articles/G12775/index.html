Washing machine with dryer: reviews. The best washer-dryer

Drying washed laundry is often a problem. This question is especially acute in the cool season. That is why near the washing machine some owners put special clothes dryers. These devices are characterized by a number of advantages. However, not every unit can simultaneously accommodate two units at once.

A washing machine with dryer will solve this problem . User reviews indicate the practicality of the purchased device. However, one must be prepared for the fact that the performance of such a machine will be slightly lower than that of both units separately. First of all, it concerns the loading of linen. In a combined machine, this figure is usually two times lower than in a conventional dryer.

Need to purchase

The machine, capable of washing and then drying the laundry laid in it, is an electronic-mechanical unit. According to the creators, such a device should greatly simplify the work of the hostess, as it will relieve her of the need to hang and remove clothes.



Who especially needs a washing machine with dryer? Customer reviews indicate that the unit attracts the owners of those apartments in which there are no balconies and loggias. These machines are convenient for mothers with young children, as well as for those people who have to wash frequently for some reason.

Principle of operation

In machines that not only wash but also dry the laundry, a second heating device is installed. This is TEN. It heats the air, which is then transferred through the air duct to the unit tank using a fan. How is the drying process? Hot air penetrates the wet laundry and as if absorbs the moisture in it, which then enters the drainage system in the form of condensate. All things are dried evenly. This is possible due to the fact that the process does not depend on the speed of rotation of the container with the laundry, but on the flow of hot air. The uniformity of drying is achieved by periodically changing the direction of movement of the drum during operation of the machine.



It is known that for each type of fabric there are special washing conditions. The same applies to drying. This moment is considered by manufacturers in the models released by them. For example, an Electrolux washing machine with dryer is equipped immediately with two heating elements that heat the air. If there is cotton underwear in the drum, then both are turned on. For processing synthetic fabrics, only one TEN functions.

What is convenient for owners of a washing machine with dryer? User reviews talk about significant space savings. Owners of unique equipment receive, at the end of the process, lingerie that is already wearable. There is no need to hang things on the balcony or in the apartment, where humidity will not be increased. A washing machine with a drying function spreads the laundry. The mistress will not even have to pet him.

Types of Drying

In the bulk of the combined units, the main function is still washing. She focuses on the manufacturer. Drying is considered an additional option. That is why in many models it is still set using a timer.

However, there are more advanced units. In them, the drying process lasts up to a certain indicator of residual moisture. In such models, a sensor-thermometer is installed. With its help, the ‚Äúsmart‚Äù Fuzzy Logic system determines the humidity of the laundry. When the indicator matches the specified drying stops. User reviews indicate the convenience of such a system. It allows you to bring the laundry to the desired state without leaving it wet and not overdrying.

Weight of things

Drying in washing machines has one drawback. It is able to process only half of the device‚Äôs capacity. For example, the Electrolux EWW 16781 W model is capable of washing seven kilograms of laundry at once. For drying, it should be no more than 3.5 kg. That is why this process will stretch in two stages. In this regard, many users talk about the inconvenience of combined units, since all the manipulations take extra time and increase energy consumption. In addition, it should be borne in mind that machines with combined washing and drying functions are less economical than conventional ones. The energy class of combined models is not higher than B. There are units and more expensive. They belong to classes C and D.

Manufacturers seek to rectify the situation. A good example of this is the Hotpoint-Ariston ARMXX D 129. It can wash 7 kg of clothes and dry up to 5 kg depending on the type of fabric.

Reliability

On forums on the Internet, users have different opinions about combination washing machines. Some people think these devices are less reliable than conventional devices. A similar statement is based on the argument that they are more complicated in their technical equipment. At first glance, this idea is quite logical. However, it would be very strange if manufacturers offered their customers deliberately unreliable devices.



According to specialists of repair shops, the main cause of breakdowns of any devices, including combined washing machines, is the improper handling of them by users. Non-observance of the operating rules and neglect of the recommendations that are set out in the manual, as a rule, lead to negative consequences. Types of devices

Models of units equipped with built-in dryer are developed in two versions. The first of them provides a frontal way of laying clothes. The second version of the models is with vertical loading.

The front-view device is best suited for those rooms where there is enough free space. A vertical washer / dryer is recommended for small rooms. Such models are the best suited for installation in small apartments. Machines with vertical placement of linen have a depth of fifteen centimeters less than that of units with a front tab. In addition, such devices are more comfortable in operation. The hostess does not have to bend over to load the laundry and choose the right program. It is also convenient that the control panel of a vertical machine is out of the reach of curious kids.

Depending on the size of the bathroom, a standard, compact or narrow washing machine with dryer can be purchased. Take into account when buying and type of load. For those owners who have every centimeter of space on their account, it is best to purchase a narrow model of a washing machine. You can put it anywhere, even in a small room.



A narrow washer- dryer is the best option for any home. According to user reviews, its main advantage lies in the small size. Such a machine will compactly fit in a small kitchen or in the bathroom.

When choosing an aggregate, it is worth considering that its size affects capacity. Typically, such machines are designed for washing from 3.5 to 7 kg of laundry. It is believed that a family of four is quite happy with the model, which houses five kilograms of things.

Each manufacturer of combined washing machines has special models in stock. These are built-in units. Their main task is to save space and solve design problems. To buy or not?

Is a washing machine with dryer so necessary? User reviews on this issue are mixed. If there is free housing, buyers still recommend purchasing a separate appliance for drying clothes. Its dimensions are almost the same as that of the washing machine. You can choose both narrow models and full-size ones. At one time, such a device is able to dry the same amount of laundry as the washing machine does. In addition, the number of programs for such units is certainly greater than for combined devices. The disadvantage of such a solution lies only in spatial and financial factors. Two devices will take up 2 times more space, and you will have to pay almost as much for a good tumble dryer as for a washing machine.



However, those owners who do not want to hang clothes around the apartment, but at the same time seek to save space and money, still prefer combined devices.

How much does a washer / dryer cost? Its price is one and a half times higher than conventional washing units. At the same time, offers start with fifteen thousand rubles.



What is the best washer / dryer? There are only two main technical criteria when choosing a suitable model. This is not a drying timer, but the residual moisture, and the maximum weight of the clothes that the machine can handle. With proper operation, the combined device is able to serve the owners for many years.
