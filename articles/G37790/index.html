Plot in the village or I want to nature!

Oh, this great dream, which has become almost an obsession for many Russians, is a dream of your own country house! Almost every resident of a large city wants to buy a plot in the village today. The craving for land, laid down in our compatriots at the genetic level, has acquired gigantic proportions to date. Allotment prices are rising, their value is calculated by an incredible amount of zeros. Especially appreciated is the site in the suburbs. This, in principle, is quite explainable by the proximity of the land to the capital. And this is also explained by the number of people who want to buy a plot in the cottage village, of which there are a great many in the Moscow Region today, or in a village located in the immediate vicinity of the metropolis. The advantages and disadvantages of land allotments in the Russian province will be discussed in this article.





What a good plot in the village

Own cottage or even a comfortable country house - what could be better? Here you can relax from the gassed metropolis, the frantic pace of his life, join the splendor of nature or even settle down for permanent residence. True, not everyone can afford such an expensive purchase as a ready-made mansion, but here many people can afford to buy a plot in the village and gradually build on it a small but own house.



Today, the village is not a dozen kosoboky houses in which old people live their lives, but a very modern settlement, which is fully consistent with ideas about civilized living conditions. The houses here are equipped with all necessary communications, comfortable and cozy. Therefore, buying a plot in the village, you can be sure that the construction will not cause any difficulties - the ability to connect to gas, water and electricity is provided. In addition, in the countryside there will certainly be its own, and quite good infrastructure. Shops with a full range of products, outpatient clinics, or even hospitals, pharmacies, schools, cafes, post offices, and households - all this is available today in almost all small towns. And if you buy a plot in the village, taking into account the prospects (changing the status of a city dweller to a provincial status), then when choosing land it is best to pay attention to those settlements that are located in close proximity to highways. Thanks to this location, you can live outside the city without losing touch with the capital, in which many have a job, friends and relatives remain.


However, today man is not alone in comfort. A house in the village is a great opportunity to live and relax in a clean and healthy environment, breathe fresh air, and pamper yourself with good products without various nitrates and pesticides. And fishing, hunting, swimming in the clear water of the rivers near Moscow, hiking in the forest for berries and mushrooms! A magnificent view from the window, the ability to wake up to the singing of birds, walk barefoot in the dew and much, much more! What could be better for a city dweller tormented by vanity and constant stress? Only life in paradise!
