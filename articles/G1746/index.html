Wyndham Dubai Marina 4 * (Dubai, United Arab Emirates): hotel description, services, reviews

Dubai is the most amazing and popular emirate in the UAE, famous for its skyscrapers, infrastructure, snow-white multi-kilometer coast and upscale hotels. The tourism business plays an important role here. In this regard, most of the hotels are located on the beach, including W yndham Dubai Marina 4 *. The swimming period lasts a whole year, so you can have a great vacation or vacation in Dubai both in summer and in winter.





Description of the area

The city has many buildings and structures that attract attention with its uniqueness, but most of it is concentrated in the Dubai Marina. Local attractions, white sand beaches, the luxury of the Emirate's life, comfortable apartments, popular nightclubs, skyscrapers and architecturally amazing structures are concentrated here. Number of rooms

Wyndham Dubai Marina 4 *: guest reviews

According to tourists, the hotel at first sight impresses with its luxury, size, friendly staff, cozy and friendly atmosphere. The rooms are spacious, bright and, most importantly, clean. There were also enthusiastic reviews about the hotel's location: a transfer to the beach (although you can walk to it in 10 minutes), shopping centers, souvenir shops, attractions, skyscrapers, business centers and a water park.

Also, guests were satisfied with a varied and tasty food. But one drawback was found: there is a construction site nearby, where constant noise comes from. Therefore, tourists recommend checking into the upper floors and requesting a room with a view of the Arabian Sea when booking.





Hotel guests appreciated the work of the staff, which pays due attention to each guest.

Couples were delighted with the availability of cots (upon request) and babysitting services, which, for a certain hourly fee, looked after their child when their parents went away on business.
