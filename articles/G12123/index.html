Interesting crafts on the topic of "Fire Safety"

All pre-school and school institutions pay great attention to fire safety. They conduct trainings, organize competitions of thematic drawings and posters, exhibitions of children's art. Children's crafts on the topic "Fire Safety" always look interesting. Such events are very important for every child and cannot be wasted.

Fire safety. DIY crafts

Children learn to behave properly in case of fire, handle matches, gas and electrical appliances. Because before you do something with your hands, you need to try to understand it with the mind. Parents should not be aloof in preparing such events. Moms and dads should not only help to choose and make a sketch of crafts, but also talk about how to use what the child will produce in miniature, for example, a fire truck.

Application in facing technology





Crafts on the theme of "Fire Safety" can be made in a wide variety of techniques. Parents own some of them. Everyone will choose for themselves and their child what they like best.

The cutting technique looks very beautiful and modern . The manufacturing process is simple, under the power of a kindergarten kid.

You need to prepare corrugated paper. It is sold in rolls and comes in different colors. This material is useful for other crafts, if in this all will not be used. You will need another sheet of cardboard, glue, a pencil and scissors.

A future applique drawing requires a sketch. Therefore, it must be applied on a sheet of cardboard. The picture should be on the topic of fire safety: you can draw a fireman next to the machine. Now that the sketch is ready, glue should be applied to the drawing, but not to the entire sketch at once. Take the corrugated paper of the desired color (in accordance with the figure), cut out a small square from it, twist it around a wooden rod or pencil and wrinkle it with your fingers. Now it remains to glue the workpiece in its place on the cardboard.

From semolina

Crafts on fire safety can play not only a good educational role, but also help to develop fine motor skills of the fingers of kids. Hard work and perseverance requires work with semolina. It is also often used when creating children's crafts on fire safety. On the topic under study, you can perform a fire truck in a hurry to prevent disaster. As always, a pencil with glue, a sheet of cardboard or thick paper, gouache and semolina will come to the rescue. Semolina is pre-stained in the desired colors. Then it should be dried on a newspaper or film. Semka is ready, laid out in colors in separate containers. Now you need to draw a contour of the machine, apply glue on them and fill them with semolina painted in red. Then fill in all parts of the picture with the necessary colors.
