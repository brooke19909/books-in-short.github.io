Apple Music: Unsubscribe. How to disconnect subscription to Apple Music?

Many scolded Apple for the quality of its web products, but the Apple Music streaming service turned out to be extremely successful. The service is developing, growing, and now it is the best on the market among similar services. This is evidenced by numerous positive reviews from Web users. However, you should immediately clarify that the service is paid.

Many are not satisfied with this fact, they use a trial subscription, and then they want to cancel it, but they do not know how to do it. Some believe that this is impossible to do. But anything is possible. You just have to dig into the settings a bit, but you can cancel your subscription at any time.

Apple offers each new user to test their service for free for three months, however, you still need to provide credit card information. If you do not cancel your subscription, then after three months it will be renewed automatically. Therefore, you should know that you need to cancel your Apple Music subscription one day before the renewal date, otherwise your funds will be debited.

What devices can be canceled?

You can unsubscribe from any device: iPhone, iPad or iPod, and even using the fourth-generation Apple TV. If you have iTunes on Windows, macOS or Android, you can also cancel your Apple Music subscription from these devices.

What are Apple Music subscriptions?

To have access to work with the service, you will need an Apple ID registered in your name. The presented identifier makes it possible to use absolutely all products of the company. After the subscription has been issued, the user is given full access to the service. Such a user has no restrictions in choosing and listening to musical compositions.

If you take into account the free trial subscription, then there are the following types of subscriptions:

  • student;
  • family;
  • individual annual;
  • personal;
  • free.

There are countries where a free trial subscription is replaced with a preferential paid one.

How to unsubscribe from iOS devices?

Interested in how to cancel your Apple Music subscription on your Apple device? Do the following:

  1. Go to "Settings". Next, go to the iTunes Store and App Store.
  2. Select your Apple ID in the upper display field.
  3. Next, click on "View Apple ID." Now you need to enter your own password or Touch ID.
  4. Click on the ‚ÄúSubscriptions‚Äù column.
  5. Find Apple Music and unsubscribe.

As you can see, everything is simple. Some people wonder: what will happen to your Apple Music after canceling your subscription? The service will continue to function until the end of the period you paid.

How to cancel via iTunes on Mac or PC?

Wondering how to unsubscribe from Apple Music without having a mobile device on hand? You can do this both from a macbook and from a regular personal computer. You will need to do the following:

  1. Open iTunes on your MacBook or other computer.
  2. Log in, enter your Apple ID.

How do I cancel my subscription using the fourth-generation Apple TV?

Make it simple:

  1. Select "Settings", then go to "Accounts", and then select "Manage Subscriptions".
  2. Search the list for Apple Music and unsubscribe.

An important nuance: cancellation of Apple Music is only possible on the fourth-generation Apple TV. Third-generation Apple TVs and others support this service, but they do not provide account management.

What do people do with Android smartphones?

Apple made an excellent marketing move, allowing all users to use their music service. Many, using their application, after they switch to the "apple" products. But this, of course, is not all. It happens that users do not buy products from Apple, but they continue to use their music service very actively. All due to the fact that it is very convenient, new items quickly appear there, and a subscription is inexpensive. True, there are users who just want to use the service for three free months. But after that, the question necessarily arises of how to unsubscribe from Apple Music on Android.

Of course, users of Android smartphones are also given the option of disabling a subscription to the service. You can do it this way:

  1. On the main page of the program, you need to go to the menu to open your account settings.
  2. Next, find the subscription management section.
  3. Turn off auto-resume.

As you can see, canceling your Apple Music subscription is simple, without any problems, even on Android smartphones. As soon as you cancel your subscription, know that it will work until the end of the paid term.

What did users like about Apple Music?

As soon as Apple Music was introduced and the company began to give a three-month free term for using the service, many decided to try out all the advantages and disadvantages of the service. In this case, it is important to know how to unsubscribe from Apple Music, because not everyone wants to pay money to listen to music from their own pockets, even for a quality product.

Judging by the reviews, many are satisfied with the service and note that during this time they managed to find much more interesting music than during the use of analogues from competitors. It is also convenient that Apple Music often offers collected interesting playlists, which are divided thematically, as indicated in the headings. In addition, the cost of subscription is more than adequate in today's market.
