Boutique Hotel Tatiana Provence (Zvenigorod, Moscow Oblast): description of rooms, service, reviews

In choosing the perfect hotel, it is important to consider many factors. And the location, and comfort, and the concept, and the range of services, and the quality of service - all have a certain weight. The Boutique Hotel Tatiana Provence in Zvenigorod is a paradise where you can relax your body and soul from worries and fuss.

Location

Boutique Hotel Tatiana Provence is located in the very center of the ancient city of Zvenigorod at 12 Moskovskaya Street. To get here by car, enter the coordinates 55.73014, 36.85316 in the navigator. It can also be reached by rail. The station is just 4 km from the hotel.

Zvenigorod is a beautiful old city that is rich in attractions. So, within walking distance from the hotel is the Savvino-Storozhevsky monastery. Also just a few steps away is the embankment of the Moscow River.
