How to register in the personal account of a taxpayer: legal entities, individuals and individual entrepreneurs

The life of individuals and legal entities of the country is directly related to the constant payment of taxes. Now many services are trying to maximally computerize all types of services. The tax inspection is not far behind. They created a portal that helps every citizen to monitor their property, to determine how much they are supposed to pay. How to register in the personal account of the taxpayer? Simple tips and algorithms will help.

What is a personal account? Why is it needed?

What are the advantages of a personal account for an ordinary citizen? What is this service? Before you understand how to register in the personal account of a taxpayer, you need to understand what it is.

In fact, this is a service that is provided by the Federal Tax Service, abbreviated by the Federal Tax Service. It allows you to quickly receive information about the presence of tax arrears, and also provides data on available real estate.





Who can register in your account?

There are three types of cabinets for different categories of citizens.

  • Personal account of the taxpayer IP. How to register in it? It is enough to follow clear instructions from the service website.
  • Personal account for legal entities
  • Personal account for individuals.

Registration in the account of an individual

How to register in the personal account of the taxpayer? There are two ways to authorize on the site. The first is associated with a username and password. You can get the latter at the tax office. It is worth noting that it is not necessary to contact the place of registration. You can provide a password in absolutely any department. To do this, you must have a passport and TIN with you. Copies of documents will not be required.

How to register in the personal account of the taxpayer for individuals? With a password it's easy. It must be entered in the appropriate box on the website of the Federal Tax Service. Login is the TIN number.

After the password is generated, which is located on paper and is issued to the FTS by the taxpayer, it must be changed. This is given a month. It is noteworthy that this is necessary in order to enhance security.

The second option to enter your personal account is the presence of an electronic signature, which, in turn, can be provided at a center accredited by the Ministry of Communications of the Russian Federation.

This service helps to check your tax assessments, ask questions to relevant services, and also pay taxes directly through the site.

How to register in the personal account of a taxpayer of a legal entity

What are the differences between these services? In some ways they are similar. However, the personal account for legal entities has its own characteristics:

  • You can get timely and accurate information about tax arrears to the budget, as well as possible penalties for not repaying them.
  • There is the possibility of extracts from the Unified State Register of Legal Entities and the USRN for the enterprise itself.
  • Send requests for any tax certificates and statements.
  • To observe and control the process of making decisions or preparing documents of various tax authorities.

To register on the service, you need to make sure that all the conditions published on the website of the Federal Tax Service are met. This is directly related to the technical capabilities of the organization. Also, one cannot do without an electronic signature received in the corresponding organization.





Personal account of an individual entrepreneur

Why do you need another service? How to register in the personal account of a taxpayer of this category of persons?

The advantages of this service, as well as for the others described above, are obvious. It allows you to reduce time for standing in queues, and also eliminates the pile of papers, which in the future may not be useful.

One of the advantages of a personal account directly for an individual entrepreneur is the availability of a calendar. He tells when the deadlines for the delivery of a particular reporting approach. You can also note a special calculator IP. It helps to calculate in a visual form what is more profitable for the entrepreneur to choose which form of accounting and taxation.

You can also remotely send requests for certain statements and inquiries.

How to register in the personal account of the taxpayer? The tax office will come to the rescue. You can use the same login information as for an individual, but at the entrance you will have to additionally specify a number of parameters, namely PSRN and TIN. After verification, the IP will receive a confirmation letter to its email address. If the data was initially entered incorrectly, then the input will not be carried out.

If there is no password and login, you will have to contact the tax office. You will need a PSRN, TIN and passport. The entrepreneur will immediately be given a password, which will also have to be changed soon. It is noteworthy that there is also a need for an electronic signature. Otherwise, a number of functions will not be supported.





What to do if the password is lost?

If the password for your personal account has been lost or forgotten, do not panic. You can contact the tax office again. You can approach both the same compartment and any other. You should also have a TIN and passport with you. It is also worth saying that the password has been forgotten.





In the future, the citizen performs the same actions, that is, comes in with new data, and then changes the password to his own, invented. It is also worth doing if there is a suspicion that the password has been stolen and other persons have access to it.
