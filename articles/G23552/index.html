As after a long illness, the country slowly returns to normal. It seems that everything is already destroyed, everything is destroyed. Only through the efforts of some enthusiasts was it possible to maintain the centers of social support for the population. Especially for the most needy - the elderly. The market is interested in serving young, able to pay well. Those who survived the collapse of a great country remember well - not everything is measured in rubles.

Sanatorium "Volga open spaces"

There was a time when each large enterprise had a sanatorium or a rest house for its employees. People could relax for free, improve their health. Ulyanovsk cement plant on the banks of the Volga in Novoulyanovsk had its own recreation center. The factory worked, produced high-quality cement, enterprises were built around. Workers could rest at their base. The new owners felt that they did not need a social program. The plant should not just produce products, but bring them profit. All non-core assets were transferred to the balance of impoverished municipalities. The sanatorium, successfully working for more than 10 years, began to die. He stood for 5 years without a rest. Only thanks to the governor of the region and the persistence of the veterans, the enterprise was revived. The center for the rehabilitation of veterans ‚ÄúVolga open spaces‚Äù was born.

Silver Holidays

Maybe the Ulyanovsk region has not learned how to build ceremonial facades over the years of timelessness, but managed to maintain good relations between people. Thousands of pensioners can relax in the camps and sanatoriums of Ulyanovsk, Novoulyanovsk, and the districts of the region - Cherkalsky, Veshkaysky, Melekessky.
