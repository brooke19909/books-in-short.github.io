Sanatorium "Green Town", Ivanovo region. Sanatoriums of the Ivanovo region

Improving rest is popular today. And a rather famous place is the sanatorium "Green Town". The Ivanovo region is famous for its hospitality, beautiful nature and, of course, wellness centers. So where to go to the traveler?

Location of the sanatorium





Of course, the location of this or that institution is the first question that interests the traveler. And this health resort is located 15 kilometers from the city of Ivanovo. Sanatorium "Zeleny Gorodok" is a complex of residential buildings and individual cottages, which are surrounded on all sides by a forest. Here, in the lap of nature, in peace and quiet, you will have a wonderful vacation. By the way, the nearest village is the village of Loma, which is 2.5 km from the recreation complex.

What services does the health resort offer?



Sanatorium "Zeleny Gorodok" (Ivanovo region) is a diversified center. It uses a variety of treatment methods, which, of course, can solve a lot of problems. The health resort works all year round and gladly accepts both children and adults. By the way, in the summer there is a recreation camp for children aged 7 to 14 years.

What services does the sanatorium "Zeleny Gorodok" offer? Ivanovo Oblast is a territory rich in mineral waters that are saturated with sodium, sulfur, magnesium and calcium ions, therapeutic muds (peat and sapropelic). It is these resources that are primarily used in the health resort.

To begin with, it's worth talking about a balneotherapy center, which offers a lot of wellness treatments. Most patients take therapeutic baths (oxygen, mineral, medicinal, pearl). Irrigation of the intestine, as well as gynecological procedures, are also carried out here. The shower section is also quite popular, where they offer a circular, rising shower and hydromassage.

As for mud therapy, patients are often prescribed applications, baths, or even intracavitary procedures. There are also inhalation rooms for people suffering from problems with the respiratory system.

Various methods of physiotherapy are widely used, including electrophoresis, laser and ultrasound procedures, electrosleep, darsonvalization, galvanization and electrical stimulation. Some vacationers are shown ozocerite or paraffin applications. In addition, phytotherapy, hirudotherapy and mineralotherapy methods are widely used in the hospital. There are also specially designed systems of physical and mental recovery, including yoga and aromatherapy.

Living conditions for guests: a description of living rooms and catering





On the territory of the sanatorium there are rooms of various categories, including both standard and luxury rooms. Regardless of the number you choose here, you will find comfort, peace and cleanliness here. Of course, each room is furnished with the necessary amount of furniture, including beds, wardrobes and bedside tables. There is also a private bathroom, as well as a bathroom. Of household appliances there is a TV, and in the suites there is also a microwave, a small refrigerator and a set of tableware.

As for nutrition, he is given special attention here. The diet is selected individually by the attending physician, depending on the patient's abnormalities. The spacious dining room can simultaneously accommodate all guests of the health resort. And in the kitchen work exclusively experienced and talented chefs.

Leisure and entertainment for guests of the sanatorium





In fact, the health resort organizes not only treatment, but also the rest of its guests. To begin with, it is worth noting that the Vostra River flows just 250 meters from the recreation complex. This gives patients the opportunity not only to walk along the coast, enjoying the beauty of nature, but also to sunbathe and ride on boats.

On the other hand, there are plenty of opportunities to have a great time on the territory of the sanatorium itself - these are board games, and a library with an impressive collection of books, as well as regular cultural events, as well as excursions in the surroundings and hiking.

Sanatorium "Green Town": reviews

The health resort leaves itself exclusively positive impressions. The guests of the sanatorium first of all note comfortable rooms, which are kept in exceptional cleanliness. Naturally, the treatment and quality of the procedures are also at a decent level. Patients also thank the chefs, who every day delight with a delicious and varied menu. The undoubted advantage of the wellness center is its proximity to the river, which opens up opportunities for water recreation.

Other resorts in Ivanovo region

Unfortunately, not everyone can relax in such a wonderful health resort. What to do if you were not able to get to the resort "Green Town"? The Ivanovo region is rich in health centers, so you can easily find another option.





For example, in the Teykovsky district there is a wide-profile health resort Oboslunovo, which offers its guests a lot of wellness treatments, including mud therapy, galvanization, apparatus physiotherapy, ozokeritotherapy, etc.

No less popular is the resort "Sunny Beach", located on the outskirts of the city of Kokhma. Here you will also be offered a complete diagnosis, a course of treatment, as well as a lot of cosmetic procedures. Health Center "Green Town" in the suburbs

If for one reason or another you cannot go to the Ivanovo region, then do not forget that there are always other options.
