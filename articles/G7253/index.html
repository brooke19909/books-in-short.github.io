Instruction manual

If you have not already done so, then download iTunes from the official Apple website, install it on your computer and launch it. How to add music to iPhone through iTunes? Download music to iTunes

Apple has figured out how to add music to an iPhone through iTunes, buying licensed songs and downloading existing ones on your computer. iTunes is not only a program for synchronizing iPhone with a computer, but also a multifunctional music player, as well as a huge store of music and movies.

In order to download music from the iTunes store, you need to log in to the program under your Apple ID, and then select your favorite songs and purchase them. After the download is complete, the purchased music will appear in the program window in the "Music" section. It can be found on the left, in the "Media Library" section.

In order for iTunes to get the music you already have, you need to drag the files you need into the program window. They will be displayed there, in the "Media Library".

In the same way, you can upload ringtone files to Aytuns: purchase them in a store or download them on the Internet and drag them into the program. They should be sought in the "Media Library", in the subsection "Sounds".



Download music to iPhone

Now, when the music tracks are prepared, you can return to the iPhone. By going to the "Music" tab in the central part of the program window, you need to tick "Sync music". Note what exactly you want to upload to your iPhone - the entire media library or songs of certain artists or genres. Do the same with the Sounds tab if you want to download ringtones to your smartphone.

Now that everything is ready, it remains to click "Apply" or "Synchronize" at the bottom of the window.
