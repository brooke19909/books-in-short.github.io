"Nalchik" - sanatorium of the Ministry of Internal Affairs of Russia

Nalchik is a foothill resort, as it is located at an altitude of 550 m above sea level. The sanatorium opened in 1973, then it was a health resort of the Office of the Central Committee of the CPSU. In 1992, he was transferred to the Ministry of Internal Affairs of Russia - the Ministry of the Interior.





The sanatorium has a large territory - 20 hectares. Most of it has a park area. It is nice to take a walk here, go in for therapeutic walking.

At the same time, 300 people can rest in the health resort, I am glad to receive all of them at my Nalchik (sanatorium). The Caucasus is known for its hospitality, and this health resort is no exception.

In addition to excellent living conditions, there are all conditions to strengthen your health. In the sanatorium they treat:

  • musculoskeletal system;
  • cardiovascular system;
  • nervous system;
  • respiratory system;
  • metabolism;
  • Diseases associated with gynecology, urology.
