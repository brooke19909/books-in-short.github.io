What can be done from the tire with your own hands and without much effort?

Recently, old tires have moved from the category of garbage to the category of material for crafts. Different figures can increasingly be seen not only in the country, but also next to ordinary residential buildings. Suppose you don‚Äôt have any skills, you still can control this art of transforming an unnecessary thing. Look around. How much waste material can be adapted to decorate a small area in front of windows.



What can you do from a tire with your own hands? This question is no longer relevant. How many articles were devoted to this topic, how many ideas were implemented, what is more reasonable to ask: "What did you like more and what physical efforts are you willing to spend on it?"

Consider once again, for the purpose of reminder, various crafts so that you can decide what to make from old tires. The simplest and most affordable option is a small flowerbed. Simply put the wheels and pour them into the ground. Then plant flowers or seedlings. Everything, your contribution to the decoration of the city, you can say, has been made. If you also paint the tire, you get a bright colorful spot near the gray houses.



We continue to consider further options for what can be made of tires. Now we will analyze already complicated tasks for which it is necessary to have several wheels, and it does not matter what size they are. They need to be laid in any order on top of each other. You can make a camomile of 6 tires. We lay one in the center, and lay out the future petals around. If you can arrange more circles next to it, great! So your daisy will have more beautiful petals. And if you also plant flowers wisely, for example, in the center - yellow, and along the edges - white, you get a colorful composition.



Knowing what you can do from a tire with your own hands, you will create a real fairy-tale world around you. The same flower beds will look very good if you place them vertically in several tiers. The same dimensions must be laid on top of each other, laying out with a wall or in a circle. If the tires are all different, then you can get a great slide, similar to a children's toy. Remember how kids were taught? First, put on a stick a large circle, then - a smaller circle, then - even less, and so on. Remember children's skills and make a multi-tiered floral Christmas tree.

If you like different little animals, then choosing from what you can do from a tire with your own hands will not be difficult. The simplest options are a frog, a hedgehog and a turtle. These are lying varieties, therefore it is enough to lay one wheel, and make paws and a muzzle from an empty plastic bottle. You can also make a ladybug. If you managed to pick up bottles of different colors, then you will not need to paint anything.



A teddy bear or a hare is excellent if you fix two wheels vertically. Here you will need to apply some materials and additional efforts. First of all, you need a metal rod to connect the two tires to each other. Then you need a dense material (oilcloth or linoleum) to sheathe the figure. On the upper part you should draw a face, and on the bottom - the trunk. Ears get great from the bottle.

All the rest of what you can do from a tire with your own hands already requires effort and skills. Sometimes a strong male hand is even required. However, what excellent swans, parrots, snails turn out! Many more animals can be made from unnecessary tires. But the main thing is that you wanted to do this, and you decided to make your small contribution to decorating the city.
