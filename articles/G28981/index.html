Thick yarn for knitting. Hat made of thick yarn with knitting needles, crochet

Thick yarn is ideal for quick and easy knitting. It is perfect for beginners, as the result will not be long in coming, and you can immediately notice and correct errors. In addition, thick yarn is now in fashion: cozy, voluminous products appear in glossy magazines, they are worn by celebrities, and even eminent designers include such things in their collections. Try it and you create your own unique and fashionable accessories. Crochet or knitting - the choice is yours.

Materials

First, let's decide which thread is right for us. Look carefully at the label. There should be a box with a skein drawn on it and a number on top of it, from 0 to 6. It shows the thickness of the fiber and the recommended size of the tools. Thick yarn is marked 5, knitted with needles of size 6-8 mm or crochet 6.5-9 mm (in English it is called bulky yarn). Each skein has its own recommended values, you only need to experiment with what size of the tools will give the desired density and appearance of the fabric with your style of knitting. There is also a very thick yarn (super bulky yarn) - number 6 in the designation. To work with it you will need tools from 9 mm in size.



Crochet thick yarn hat

Such a project can be made from one skein. The hat looks unusual because of its asymmetric shape: it can be worn as a cap or as a helmet.

A very thick woolen thread and a size 17 hook were used. If you doubt whether its size is correctly selected, the check is done like this. The tip of the tool with which he grabs the thread is called the beard. Its height should be equal to the height of the thread slightly tensioned on the hook. Then knitting will be moderately dense and not loose.



Description of work

5 air loops close in a ringlet. Numbering points corresponds to the number of the series:

  1. Do 3 lift loops. This is equal to one double crochet (hereinafter referred to as art. S / n.), So we begin each row. We knit 8 tbsp. s / n and close them in a circle with a half-column.
  2. Doing 18 tbsp. s / n (2 in each loop).
  3. Evenly perform the additions. For this we knit two tbsp. s / n into a separate loop, then two into one, so continue to the end, total should be 24 columns.
  4. The top of the head is ready, now we are not making additions so that the edges of the cap begin to fall over the head. To do this, just knit a circle with double crochets.
  5. Give the cap a cap shape. Three lifting loops, 17 tbsp. s / n into each loop of the warp.
  6. Turn the work over and repeat row 5.
  7. Again we changed the side and we tie the whole cap with a "crustacean step".
  8. Cut the thread and hide the tails.

Accessories

For a complete set, crochet a scarf and mitts from thick yarn. They are made even easier than a hat.

Mitts

  1. Make a chain of 12 air loops. But leave the tail from its beginning longer than usual.
  2. In the fourth loop from the hook, knit one st. s / n (the first three loops are again equal to one column) and continue to the end (total 10 tbsp. s / n.). With a half-column, connect the connected strip in a circle, do not twist it during operation.
  3. Now we work in a circle with double crochet, and so two rows.
  4. Sew the separate part of the first row with the tail from the beginning of the thread, hide the free ends.

Scarf

It is made extremely easy with a crochet of thick yarn, but you will need one large button (diameter 4 cm). Description of work:

  1. Chain of 6 air loops.
  2. As in the mitts, in the fourth loop from the hook, make a double crochet, and so on to the end. Total 4 tbsp. s / n
  3. Also tie another 13 rows, turning over the work at the end of each of them.
  4. Cut the thread and hide its ends.
  5. Sew on the button. Since thick yarn is used for knitting, the button can creep between the posts. Then it is better to fix it with a pin and optionally change it.

Knitting hat made of thick yarn

Frost hit, and you don‚Äôt have a fashionable headgear? Or does it not match the color of the new jacket? From thick yarn with knitting needles you can knit a hat in a few hours. In addition, this is a great way to use one extra skein of yarn.



For the project you need circular knitting needles, size 11, fishing line length 40 cm.

  1. Dial 45 loops in any way you like and knit the first and last together, closing the knitting in a circle.
  2. The first 5 rows are made with 1 x 1 elastic: one front loop, one wrong side.
  3. Then we proceed to the formation of a striped pattern. After the elastic, we knit rows 1-4, 6-9, 11-14 with facial loops, rows 5, 10 - with the wrong ones.
  4. 15th row - purl knitting, start and end with decrease: we knit the first two and last two loops in pairs together. Total 42 loops.
  5. Round off the cap on the top of the head. To do this, we knit three rows of facial.
  6. Then a row in this sequence: * two loops together, five faces * and repeat from * to * in a circle.
  7. In subsequent rows, the number of loops between decreases decreases: four, three, two, one. And so on until the whole row consists of loops knitted together. On this knitting is over. Then cut the thread, thread it into the remaining loops and hide the end.
  8. Make a big pompom - and the project is ready.
