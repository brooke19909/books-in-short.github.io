Romanza Mare 3 (Greece, Rhodes) is a 20-minute drive from the airport of the fourth largest island of the ancient Mediterranean country. Excursions

Tourists staying in Romanza Mare 3 (Rhodes), thanks to the bus service get the opportunity to see the sights of the island of ancient civilization.

They can see the place at the entrance to the main bay of the island, where in ancient times one of the wonders of the world - the Colossus of Rhodes. Rhodes was considered the largest trading port of the Mediterranean. The local school of sculpture in the ancient world was considered one of the classic.

Of greatest interest to tourists is an excursion to the capital of the island. It is noteworthy that in the city of Rhodes there are well-known historical architectural objects of the XV-XVI centuries: the Quarter of the Knights (Kollakium), the Palace of Masters, the fortress. In the Middle Ages, there were powerful strategic strongholds of the Order of Hospitallers.

Now the formidable structures have turned into museums, mosaics are kept in the Palace of Masters and exhibitions dedicated to the ancient and medieval period of the history of Rhodes are held.

The second most attractive city on the island is Lindos. It is famous for its acropolis, which in the world is considered the second in historical value after the Athenian. Here is the legendary bay of Paul - the place where the apostle, following his mission, went ashore.

Vacationers of the resort of Kallithea primarily seek to explore the local baths - antique baths, restored during the time of Mussolini. They are happy to stroll through the park, decorated with intricate arches and rotundas. On the beach of Kallithea, the remains of the temple of Zeus are visible, they were discovered by chance during construction in 1969.
