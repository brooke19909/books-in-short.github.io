Crochet shawls: schemes, description

Wardrobe is the most vulnerable place of every woman. At the same time, there are always a lot of things, but not everyone can look beautiful. There can be many reasons for this. However, the basic seems to be the wrong selection of accessories. To transform your wardrobe, thereby changing your appearance, it is enough to add one or more details. Especially crochet shawls can help with this ; patterns for this can be easily found in various print media.



Such a product will certainly adorn a woman, attracting gaze to her. In addition, in the case of the selection of a dense pattern with a minimum number of air loops and arches, it will warm your shoulders during a walk on a cool summer evening.

Many who are interested in crocheting shawls are looking for patterns only in order to create for themselves a beautiful accessory that will help protect themselves from the cold at work. Especially if you have to work in a cold office, where the temperature is constantly kept below acceptable levels. In this case, a woman will not only be able to fulfill her duties professionally, but at the same time she will feel quite comfortable.





Pay closer attention to crocheting shawls. Schemes most often suggest starting work from below. And it is right. In this case, the woman will be able to control the thread consumption, and will also be able to determine in time that the product is ready and it is time to stop working.

At the same time, if crocheting shawls is carried out, the schemes provide the opportunity to start it from above. In this case, the product is most often formed not a characteristic triangular shape, but semicircular. Such a shawl is even somewhat close in appearance to an ordinary cloak. However, it may vary in size.

Shawls also knit on top with the help of knitting needles. If you want this accessory to retain its shape for a long time, choose 100% wool as the yarn. The presence in the composition of a large number of synthetic fibers can cause the finished shawl to be easily modified during socks, smoothly transitioning from triangular to rectangular. Moreover, the thinner the thread, the more tender the created product will look and deform more strongly.



Crocheting a shawl, a scheme for which there is always a free access, is a unique opportunity to diversify your wardrobe, making it more beautiful and stylish. So is it worth it to miss? Moreover, for its manufacture, depending on the scheme, a maximum of 500 grams of yarn is required. For individual models, no more than 0.1 kg is required. True, and its length will be appropriate.

If you have found crochet patterns for shawls, but you cannot figure them out, read the literature for beginners or ask experienced craftswomen for advice. With them you can always discuss the problem you are interested in, as well as get a lot of other useful information. Feel free to ask questions or ask for a scheme. Experienced knitters will be happy to share their knowledge with you. It is possible that for some of them, knitting is not only a hobby, but also a means for additional income. Perhaps you can increase your income in this way.
