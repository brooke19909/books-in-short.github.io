Smartphone Xiaomi Redmi 4X 32Gb: specifications, description, reviews

The announcement of the first smartphone of Xiaomi Corporation took place in 2011. For six years of titanic work, the company has gained worldwide fame and can compete with Samsung and Apple, companies recognized around the world, not to mention less famous rivals. Xiaomi's model range in recent years has expanded significantly due to the number of announced devices. Sometimes it is not even clear how some Xiaomi smartphones differ from each other. Let's get to know more closely one of the representatives of the budget line of the company - the Xiaomi Redmi 4X 32gb smartphone.

Design and materials

Alas, the smartphone can not boast of any new design decisions in the execution of the case and the appearance. Xiaomi Redmi 4X 32Gb, according to reviews, its appearance is very similar to the model of the third generation Redmi line. The very design of the case and the quality of performance do not cause any complaints. The case of the gadget is metal, only the lower and upper ends of the device are made of plastic. All faces are neatly rounded and smoothly transition into 2.5D glass on the front panel. The device is very convenient to hold in your hand. If desired, you can use Xiaomi Redmi 4X 32Gb with a case.

On the back of the device are a fingerprint scanner and the main camera with a flash. The scanner has a convenient location. If you take the phone in your hand, then the index finger just hits the scanner window. The camera perched at the upper edge of the case and, together with the LED flash, is shifted to the left edge.





On the side faces are the lock and volume buttons (right) and the slot for SIM-cards and microSD (left). At the bottom, in the center, between the same speaker and microphone grilles, a USB connector is sheltered. On the upper edge of the device are a second microphone, an infrared eye and a headphone jack. Almost the entire front panel of the device is occupied by a screen protected by glass. At Xiaomi Redmi 4X 32Gb under the screen are three standard touch-sensitive control buttons, deprived of backlight. Under the center button is an LED indicator for notifications. Above the display sheltered the earpiece, front camera, as well as light and proximity sensors.

Screen

The display on the smartphone has a diagonal of five inches with HD resolution. The IPS matrix is ‚Äã‚Äãused, the screen does not have an air gap. Ten touches are supported simultaneously. The Xiaomi Redmi 4X 32Gb screen, according to reviews, gives out rich colors, bright and pleasing to the eye. With strong sun at maximum brightness, the image remains easily perceptible. Fans of reading in the dark are also not offended: at the minimum brightness, information is perceived comfortably, the eyes do not strain. An oleophobic coating avoids screen contamination. Fingerprints still remain, but are easily cleaned. For its price, the screen is very decent. Some models of smartphones at a higher price have a worse quality screen.

CPU and performance

The Xiaomi Redmi 4X 32Gb mobile phone received an 8-core Shapdragon 435 processor and an Adreno 505 graphics accelerator. One should not forget that the smartphone belongs to the class of budget devices. Waiting for some superpowers from him is not worth it. Heavy games at maximum settings on the device will most likely not function normally. However, with average specifications on Xiaomi Redmi 4X 32Gb, any modern gaming application will launch.





According to synthetic tests, the hero of the review has very good performance and, thanks to a more modern processor, looks more productive than the gadgets of its competitors, for example, the same company Meizu. The device left a pleasant impression on itself. The performance of its processor is enough to perform any everyday tasks. Perhaps the device will not suit fans of serious mobile games, but it does not strive to be ‚Äúahead of the rest‚Äù. The device fulfills its price by 100 percent.

Cameras

The main optical module of the device with a resolution of 13 megapixels, as expected after reading reviews of Xiaomi Redmi 4X 32Gb, did not bring any surprises. With good lighting or in clear weather on the street, photos of good enough quality are obtained. Naturally, when the conditions for shooting deteriorate, the quality of the resulting photographs decreases. Not surprising for a budget-grade gadget camera. The advantages of the camera include fast focusing and a convenient application for working with the optical module. The main camera allows you to shoot video with FullHD resolution. The front camera Xiaomi Redmi 4X 32Gb, according to the characteristics, is ordinary, has a resolution of 5 megapixels and is no different from competitors.

Wireless Modules, Navigation and Communications

Of the wireless interfaces, the Xiaomi Redmi 4X 32Gb smartphone has Bluetooth 4.2 and Wi-Fi 802.11n. NFC, unfortunately, is not supported by the device. Wireless modules are standard here, while working with them there were no nuances. The device supports navigation on GPS, GLONASS and Chinese BeiDou systems. The device very quickly finds satellites and does not lose connection. Everything works at the highest level.





Of the features can also be noted support for 4G networks (LTE). The communication quality is good, maybe the sound from the speaker is a little distorted. The MIUI application built into the shell allows you to record all calls.

Autonomy of the device

With this option, according to reviews, Xiaomi Redmi 4X 32Gb is all right. The device received a very good battery with a capacity of 4100 mAh, which is a good indicator for a representative of the budget segment. Of course, the device did not set a record for autonomy, but it proved to be a strong decent middle peasant in terms of time.

With the usual intensity of use, the Xiaomi Redmi 4X 32Gb mobile phone will live peacefully for a day, and if you save a little, then two. This result is possible, first of all, thanks to the capacious battery, as well as the screen resolution. Also, do not forget about the many energy-saving applications of Xiaomi's proprietary software shell - MIUI. However, with a ‚Äúheavy‚Äù game, a smartphone can be set in 6-7 hours. With continuous video viewing, the device will last a little longer - about 13-15 hours. But given that the device is positioned as a budget device, these indicators can be called quite acceptable.

The "chips" of the gadget, unique to Xiaomi

The most important feature of the device, the pride of Xiaomi, is the software shell of its own production MIUI. This firmware has many built-in useful applications. But there is a slight nuance. A person who previously used a smartphone with the usual Android system, at first it will not be easy to deal with the MIUI shell. The usual menu items on Android may be in the most unexpected place, or even completely absent.





Also, the device boasts the presence of an infrared port. At first, many are perplexed why this anachronism was even stuck in a modern smartphone? This is actually a well-thought-out marketing move. Using a special MIUI shell program and an infrared port, you can turn a smartphone into a control panel for almost any TV or set-top box. Very interesting thing.

I would also like to talk about the built-in program for managing sound reproduction through headphones. You can choose the type of headphones from a rather extensive list, as well as adjust the equalizer to give the sound the necessary frequencies. When playing through the headphones, a very good and deep sound is obtained. Of course, a music lover with a musical ear may not like the sound, but the average user should like the sound.
