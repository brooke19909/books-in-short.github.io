Sanatorium "Rosinka": photos and reviews. Treatment and relaxation at the Rosinka sanatorium in the Vitebsk region of Belarus

Belarus rightfully bears the title of a lake region. Tourists are attracted to these places by the oldest forest in Europe - Belovezhskaya Pushcha, the famous Braslav Lakes, biosphere reserve. The health resorts of Belarus are no less interesting. Sanatorium on the peninsula





On the peninsula, framed by three lakes, is located the picturesque health resort for children ‚ÄúRosinka‚Äù. The healthy climate of the surrounding nature is complemented by numerous coniferous and deciduous trees. The sanatorium began its history in the 80s of the last century. It was then that in the Vitebsk region the Rosinka children's sanatorium received its first vacationers, since then all year round thousands of adults and children undergo a preventive treatment course in this unique place. In the summer season, a well-equipped beach on the lake contributes to the full relaxation of parents with toddlers and older children. In the cold season, the wellness pool, located in the indoor building, is open for water treatments.

The range of medical services of the sanatorium

The medical profile of a health institution is the treatment of respiratory, circulatory, renal and urinary systems. Vacationers are given the opportunity to get expert advice from specialists who can also adjust wellness procedures. The medical base of this popular health institution is quite diverse. Ultrasound therapy, EHF-therapy, water procedures in the healing shower, baths are practiced.

Artificially created conditions of salt caves are practiced for the prevention and treatment of bronchopulmonary problems. On the territory of the sanatorium there are several wells with healing mineral water, which allows the use of treatment with mineral baths. Specialists working in this health institution are ready to provide additional wellness services for a fee.

Improvement is contraindicated ...

Having made the decision to purchase permits to the sanatorium, vacationers should take into account the fact that there are a number of diseases, the presence of which may block access to rehabilitation at Rosinka. First of all, the ban concerns diseases related to alcohol and drug addiction of patients, as well as sexually transmitted diseases. Otherwise, the Rosinka sanatorium adheres to the same contraindications as the other sanatoriums in Belarus. "Dewdrop" accepts for the recovery of adults and children from the age of three years. At the same time, wellness services are provided to all categories of vacationers.





Organization of children's leisure

Considering the age category of vacationers, the sanatorium pays special attention to the organization of leisure and entertainment. The staff of the institution tries to make the stay of children in Rosinka not only health-improving, but also interesting, filled with positive emotions. In the library, children will be offered an assortment of cognitive works for reading, in the cinema, regularly watching videos and TV shows. Interest groups work all year long: those who wish to beadwork or learn the origami technique will be able to devote their free time to these classes.

An interesting pastime for children of primary school and senior preschool age will be classes in the circle of testoplasty. Those who do not like to do manual labor are invited to participate in an intellectual club or a poetry studio. Depending on the time of year, children are offered various types of entertainment and games on the street. In winter, seasonal sports equipment is rented, in summer you can ride bicycles.

Organization of leisure and leisure for adults

For a comfortable and unforgettable vacation for adults and older children, Rosinka provides a number of entertainment programs, as well as the use of a certain infrastructure of the object. On the territory of the sanatorium there is a bathhouse and sauna, a visit to which is provided in conjunction with the pool. For outdoor activities there is an outdoor tennis court, several tables for table tennis. Sports ground for soccer games, volleyball awaits those who wish to relax and stretch a little to keep fit. There is also a professional table for lovers of Russian billiards on the territory of the sanatorium.

Catering

Vacationers are offered five meals a day, which is held in one shift. Cafeteria staff offer four types of diet food, depending on the disease that worries the vacationer. The menu is as close as possible to the home, in the assortment all year round fruits, juices, sour-milk products, fresh vegetables, pastries. The staff of the Rosinka sanatorium is also ready to provide adults with food options depending on taste preferences, for this, from the second day of the vacationer's stay, a custom-made menu system begins to work. In this regard, there are no cafes in the territory of this health-improving institution.



Accommodation for vacationers

Sanatorium "Rosinka" is a health-improving and preventive institution for children, therefore accommodation is provided according to the age category of vacationers. Two three-story buildings are equipped with maximum comfort for children. Facilities are located on the floor, in the lobby there is a TV and a refrigerator. Lounge rooms are characterized by a cozy atmosphere, designed for two or four residents. For the older category of vacationers, guest houses and cottages have been built to accommodate parents with children.

Additional entertaining and educational services

For those who, having arrived for recovery, want to expand their horizons and enrich them with new impressions and knowledge, additional services are offered - excursions. Like many health resorts in Belarus, this offers vacationers to get acquainted with local attractions and nearby historical monuments. Several of the proposed excursions have a spiritual focus - visiting the Church of St. Anne, St. Sophia Cathedral, the convent. Sightseeing tours are also offered in the nearby cities of Braslav, Miory, Polotsk.

"Two to three weeks ..."

Standard trips to the Rosinka sanatorium are designed for a 21-day stay with meals and a complex of medical and health procedures. Vouchers with a shorter stay in a health institution are also offered, in this case treatment is also adjusted, taking into account new terms. The Rosinka sanatorium differs from many medical institutions of this type in that it has the conditions for climatotherapy.

Already a stay in this institution brings a healing and cleansing effect for the body. For those who appreciate not only a comfortable stay, but also try to get the most out of their stay in nature, the Rosinka Sanatorium will be an excellent option for recovery. Vitebsk region is the keeper of unique natural and ecological zones, this region is ready to generously share its resources with people.

Reviews about rest and recreation in the sanatorium

You can also get acquainted with the reviews of those who have already spent their holidays in this unforgettable place with an easy name - the Rosinka sanatorium.

Reviews of adults and children are mostly positive. Many parents note that a decent rest and recovery of children in the sanatorium was accompanied by the friendly attitude of the staff and the professional performance of their duties. The professionalism of the treatment and maintenance staff is also noted by adults who spent their holidays in this sanatorium. The quality of the services provided is of a sufficiently high level, while the prices for leisure and additional paid services are affordable. Some of the disadvantages noted in the reviews mainly boil down to the fact that the dance floor is too close to the guest houses, which is why the noise from the disco in the evenings interferes with the early relaxation of the clients of the sanatorium. Food in this health institution, according to reviews, suits all categories of vacationers. A well-equipped beach is noted by all participants in the discussion on the forum, and the pure nature of the coast is added to this.
