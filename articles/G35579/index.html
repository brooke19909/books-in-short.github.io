Short review of the shopping center "June" (Krasnoyarsk). Address, how to get there

Fashion shops, entertainment and a cinema - all this is located in the shopping center "June". Krasnoyarsk daily invites all fashionistas and women of fashion, parents with children, as well as avid moviegoers to visit this center in order to have a good time, for the benefit of themselves.

Cinema

In the shopping and entertainment center "June" is a huge and quite comfortable cinema Moni Crema, which consists of seven halls. It is located on the third floor and has its own bar, where there is the opportunity not only to drink tea, coffee or cool with a cocktail, but also to taste sushi and rolls.

The cinema of the shopping center "June" (Krasnoyarsk) constantly shows the most anticipated news of foreign and domestic cinema. Each room has very comfortable soft wide chairs. Watching movies is provided with the best audio and video equipment.

As for the size of the halls, that is, two large ones with 217 seats, one hall with 140 seats, three with 134 seats, as well as a small VIP hall designed for a company of 24 people - here you can gather a large company to watch the long-awaited premiere or some old movie beloved by all. In total, the Moni Crema Cinema is able to simultaneously accommodate 1,005 guests within its walls.

Entertainment

First of all, it is worth noting the presence in the shopping center "June" (Krasnoyarsk) the presence of a good fitness room - Well Fit, where residents of the city constantly come to maintain and adjust their figure, as well as to normalize their health. This fitness center is located on the fourth floor of the building.

The TacticCon club is very popular among visitors to the June shopping center, where you can go with a big noisy company to compete in sports and tactical games for airsoft and laser tag. It is located on the ground floor of the center. Some choose this place to celebrate a birthday and other significant.

For young guests of the shopping center there is also an entertainment complex Fancy Fox ("Fancy Fox"), where there is a huge number of all kinds of carousels, toys, labyrinths. There are large inflatable slides with a pool. Watchful animators can keep an eye on your little ones while you shop. This is a true paradise island of entertainment for any child!





Restaurants and Cafes

After long trips along the four floors of the shopping center, it is necessary to restore the energy expended. The guests are helped by restaurants and cafes located on the territory of the June shopping center (Krasnoyarsk). Here is a list of some of them:

  • Cafe Burger Club ("Burger Club") - the menu offers a huge assortment of burgers with cutlets of natural beef.
  • Bakery Cinnabon is a world famous confectionery that will simply conquer lovers of rich bakery products.
  • Cream Bar is a place where you can drink coffee and taste fabulous desserts.
  • Cafe Terrasa - an institution where you are offered to try dishes of European cuisine or drink a cup of coffee.
  • Restaurant "Sbarro" - here you can taste exquisite hits of Italian cuisine, prepared by professional chefs.
  • Sushi Terra is a Japanese bar serving delicious rolls and other traditional oriental dishes.

This incomplete list involuntarily suggests the idea that an institution with a kitchen for every taste can be found in the shopping center "June" (Krasnoyarsk).

How to get there

The shopping and entertainment complex is located near the Oktyabrskaya Square stop, which can be reached by bus - the following routes are suitable for you: 27, 60, 7, 160.

If you intend to arrive by personal car, you have the opportunity to use the services of an open parking lot, which is available near the shopping center "June" (Krasnoyarsk).





Address: Krasnoyarsk, Partizan Zheleznyak St., 23.

The shopping and entertainment complex "June" is open every day from 10 am to 10 pm.
