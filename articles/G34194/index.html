How to use the service from Beeline - Locator?

  1. To determine where the desired subscriber is now located, use the "Where [Subscriber Number]" command by sending this word to 5166. Or go to the control menu and select "Find Subscriber".

Service activation

Subscription to "Locator" "Beeline" paid. The cost of a subscription fee is three rubles, which are debited for each day of use. At the same time, all commands and requests (both location and search for the necessary objects) are not paid extra. Also free are calls to number 09853 and SMS messages to number 5166 about connecting and disconnecting services.

By the way, new users are given a free period of 7 days, during which you can familiarize yourself with the service and decide on its use.

To begin, consider how to connect a Beeline Latitude. It is very easy to do this: just call 09853 or send an empty SMS message to 5166. The service will be connected. You will use the same number (5166) to send the corresponding commands.

Service Management Commands

Here is a list of the most popular query commands:

  • where number / where name - searches for the coordinates of the subscriber;
  • number / name_number - adds the coordinates of the subscriber with the assignment of a name;
  • delete number / name - deletes from the subscriber list;
  • stop - removes one of the observers;
  • list - receiving a list of subscribers being monitored;
  • who - getting a list of those who monitor you;
  • start - subscription to this service;
  • yes / no - permission or ban on the request for coordinates.

If you decide to deactivate the Beeline Locator service, then this is also simple: send an SMS message with the Off command to 5166. The service will be disabled.

However, the way out of the situation is as follows: remove one number from your subscribers list and add another in its place (you can do this regularly).
  3. The third caveat - you can determine the location no more often than with an interval of five minutes. However, this is not such a long period of time - you can wait.
