Hotel Rodos Blue 3 * (Afantou, Greece): photos and reviews of tourists

The Greek resort of Afantou, located in the northeast of Fr. Rhodes, offers tourists not only clean comfortable beaches and a warm sea, but also a well-developed infrastructure. There are plenty of convenient hotels in this village. And one of the best, according to many vacationers, is Rodos Blue 3 *.

Location Features

This hotel is located in the picturesque surroundings of Afantou, on a hill. The distance from it to the beach is approximately 1 km.

Almost all tourists fly to Greece, of course, by plane. Rhodes International Airport is approximately 20 km from the hotel. Typically, tour operators provide their customers who have booked a room at this hotel, a shuttle bus. If you wish, you can also get to the hotel by taxi or public transport. Tourists, however, almost never use the latter method. It should be a bus ride, of course, much cheaper than a taxi. However, in this case, tourists have to make transfers, which, of course, is very inconvenient.





Some tourists get to the hotel from the airport by car. If you wish, you can rent a car right in the waiting room. This service is not very expensive in Greece.

The island's capital, Rhodes, is only about 18 km from the hotel. You can also get here by taxi or bus. Near the hotel there is another island resort, very popular with tourists, Faliraki.

General description of the hotel

This hotel accepts guests in a multi-storey building of modern architecture. In 2015, a large-scale reconstruction was carried out at the Rodos Blue 3 * hotel (Rhodes) . Therefore, the decoration of the building and all equipment are relatively new. The courtyard and terrace of the hotel are not tiled, as in many other hotels in Greece, but with stone. On the territory of the complex, lawns are everywhere broken and spectacular southern ornamental plants planted.

Number of rooms

If desired, tourists can book one of the 58 comfortable rooms at this hotel. All rooms of the hotel are designed for a maximum of 2-3 people. Children under the age of two years stay at the hotel for free. Actually the rooms in the hotel are equipped with:

  • a refrigerator;
  • telephone
  • air conditioning.

All rooms have private showers. Here, tourists can use free toiletries and towels. In addition to the shower itself, the bathroom has a bathtub, toilet and sink. This hotel features rooms with balconies. The view from most of the hotel rooms is quite attractive . Air conditioning at this hotel, unfortunately, is paid. Their connection costs about 5 euros. Hotel beach

The hotel Rodos Blue Resort 3 * (Rhodes) is located quite far from the beach, and also on a hill. Therefore, to get to the sea on foot to its guests, of course, is not particularly convenient. However, many vacationers do not consider such a hotel location too big a problem. The fact is that a comfortable bus belonging to the hotel goes to the sea three times a day.

Wide long beaches - this is what, among other things, offers tourists Greece. Rodos Blue 3 * many guests praise including the fact that you can really relax on the coastline located opposite it, with great comfort. Due to the size of the beach, there are never too many people here.

Actually, the hotel's beach itself earned only good reviews from vacationers. The coastline in this place of the Aegean is very clean. All the beaches of the resort of Afantou for lack of dirt awarded the Blue Flag of UNESCO. Located opposite the hotel Rodos Blue Resort 3 * (Rhodes, Greece) is one of them.

Available Attractions

In the immediate vicinity of the Rodos Blue Resort 3 * hotel there are several very interesting places that experienced tourists advise beginners to visit. For example, the ancient city of Kamiros is a very popular attraction among vacationers. You can buy an excursion here at a not too high price. At the same time, tourists always have a lot of impressions after visiting this attraction.

The ancient city of Kamiros is located on the Aegean Sea, on a hill. It was founded, according to historians, by the Dorians. Once Kamiros was really a very large and significant settlement. Among other things, there even existed its own small mint.

In addition to Kamiros, many hotel guests visit Rhodes and its famous Acropolis. Here you can see, for example, the stadium where races were once held in honor of the sun god. Also in the Acropolis there is a very interesting Antique Theater, reconstructed by Italian architects from ruins. This is perhaps the most famous landmark of Rhodes near the resort village of Lindos, about 24 km from Afantou.
