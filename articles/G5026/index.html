Centara Kata Resort Phuket (Phuket), or Perfect family vacation

The Centara hotel chain is one of the most recognizable chains in the world. It is famous for the quality of service, family concept and most importantly in our economic situation - affordability.

Centara in Phuket

At the moment, there are six hotels of this chain on the favorite island of Thailand's tourists: three - on the snow-white Karon beach, one - on the elite Maikao, one - on the frantic Patong and one - on the beautiful Kat, which will be discussed.

Kata Beach is one of the three most popular resort destinations in Phuket. Travelers looking for tranquility, magnificent nature and at the same time well-developed infrastructure go here.

Centara Kata Resort Phuket (Phuket) is a quality four-star hotel that fully justifies its value.

The whole complex was built in 2002 and completely renovated in 2012 (a complete redecoration of the entire room stock was carried out), which indicates its freshness and willingness to accept small and large tourists.

The number of rooms of the complex "Centara Kata Resort"

The hotel has a small number of rooms - only 158 (which is why you can relax there as much as possible - none of the tourists observe large crowds, there are always free sunbeds by the pool and tables in the restaurant).

Moreover, despite this fact, Centara Kata Resort Phuket (Phuket) offers a fairly diverse category of rooms, which will allow any family to stay comfortably:

  • Deluxe A fairly spacious (30 square meters) one-room room with a view of the garden or pool to choose from (when booking a trip, you can put a note about the desire for a particular panorama from the window, and the hotel management will necessarily take into account the tourists' opinion when checking in). There are large double and single beds. An electronic safe, air conditioning, tea and coffee sets are also provided free of charge. For an additional fee, you can use the telephone, internet or a minibar. The bathroom must have bath accessories, shower gel and shampoo, hair dryer. It is worth noting that not all rooms at the Centara Kata Resort Phuket 4 * (Phuket ) will have a balcony (it is better to say in advance when booking if you want to get a room with it or, conversely, without it).
  • The family room with one room is ideal for families up to four people. This category of rooms differs from the previous one only in the presence of an extra double bed in the room.
  • Suite with one bedroom. Tourists can stay in a free (60 square meters) room with a bedroom and a living room with an interior door. Also, suites can please travelers with the presence of mini-kitchens, which will be very convenient when traveling with young children or if you want to save a little on food in local cafes.
  • Suite with two bedrooms. 90 square meters will be provided to a large family or a whole group of tourists. A separate living room, a kitchenette with all the necessary utensils for cooking, two bathrooms with a shower will be the ideal place to relax for a special category of travelers.

In all categories of rooms an extra bed is a modern folding bed.

Infrastructure of the complex Centara Kata Resort Phuket 4 *

Thailand (Phuket) mainly hosts active tourists, so most of the hotels on the island do not have a huge amount of entertainment on their territory, like in Turkey or Egypt.

However, Centara Kata Resort Phuket (Phuket) will not let anyone get bored.

Elements of the free infrastructure include three huge outdoor pools, sports games (volleyball and table tennis), a mini club for children aged 4 to 9 years, and a playground with different swings and horizontal bars.

Hotel Service

The management of the Centara Kata Resort Phuket (Phuket) hotel cares about the convenience of its tourists:

  • Free Wi-Fi is available throughout the entire complex.
  • The children's restaurant provides high chairs for meals.
  • For a small fee (about 350 baht per hour), you can hire a qualified nanny to look after your children.
  • Also for corporate clients there is a conference room for up to 30 people with the latest equipment.
