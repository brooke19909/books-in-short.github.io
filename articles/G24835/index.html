Tutaev hotels: description and reviews of tourists

In the ancient city of Tutaev, located twenty-five kilometers from Yaroslavl, many ancient churches have been preserved. Tourists and pilgrims come here, so the question of their placement is quite relevant. If you decide to stay in the city for a few days to get acquainted with all the sights, Tutaev hotels will offer you inexpensive and comfortable living conditions.

Hotel Imperium

The hotel is located near the business and cultural center of the city. Within walking distance from the hotel there is a supermarket, market, cafes and restaurants. If you want to walk to the Volga, you need to walk only one kilometer, two kilometers from here is a ski lift. Unlike other Tutaev hotels, the Imperium Hotel is a restaurant and hotel complex with a cozy restaurant on the ground floor. Upon request, the ordered dishes will be delivered to your room. Guests leave their vehicles in a paid guarded parking lot. Wireless internet is available for free. Sauna services are available at an extra charge. Guests are offered the following room options:

  • single standard - 1500 rubles per day;
  • single superior - 1700 rubles per day;
  • double economy - 1400 rubles per day;
  • double standard - 1500 rubles per day;
  • family classic - 2500 rubles per day.

Each room is equipped with a TV and free toiletries. The Imperium Hotel is located in Tutaev at the address: st. Roses Luxembourg, house 44.

Hotel Imperium: reviews of tourists

Guests of the city share their impressions of their stay in Tutaev hotels in the Yaroslavl region in their reviews:

  • Hotel Imperium liked tourists a convenient location near city attractions and crossing to the other side of the Volga.
  • Near the hotel there is a bus stop to Rybinsk and Yaroslavl, a supermarket "Magnet".
  • On the ground floor of the hotel there is a small restaurant in which deliciously prepared for an affordable price.
  • The rooms are spacious, the decor is quite basic, the bed is clean.
  • The staff is friendly, populate quickly.
  • Among the shortcomings, guests note poor soundproofing, some rooms are smoky.
  • In one of the rooms, the sockets did not fit the charger.
  • Some rooms have no bedside rugs.
  • Music plays until late at night in the restaurant.

Tutaev Hotels: Ekaterina Hotel

The Resurrection Cathedral is only four hundred meters from the hotel. In ten minutes you will reach the market, shops and cafes. Like other hotels in Tutaev, the hotel is located near the Volga. Parking and Wi-Fi are free for guests. A picnic area is available on site and barbecue facilities are available. If you want to cook yourself, a shared kitchen is at your service. For a fee, you can relax in the sauna. Laundry service is available. For accommodation, guests are offered the following rooms:

  • double with two separate beds - from 4900 rubles per day;
  • double with one big bed - from 5300 rubles per day.

The price is based on full board. All rooms are equipped with a TV, electric kettle and free toiletries. Hotel "Ekaterina" is located in Tutaev at the address: st. Sobornaya, house 53.

Hotel reviews

At the Ekaterina Hotel, guests liked her location not on a noisy street, but in a quiet side street.

In addition, tourists note the following:

  • Near the hotel there is a ferry and a museum.
  • It is very convenient that the parking lot is located in a fenced area, there is enough space for everyone.
  • The rooms are rather modest but clean.
  • The staff is friendly and welcoming. They will tell you what you can see and tell how to get through.
  • For accommodation of three people in a double room for a third person, an additional fee was not taken.
  • Tourists also appreciated the availability of a shared kitchen at the hotel.
  • You can use the barbecue for cooking barbecue.
  • Internet is working well.

Among the shortcomings, guests note poor sound insulation between rooms, poor ventilation.

When compared with other Tutaev hotels, the guests consider the cost of living in this hotel to be overestimated.

Hostel "CPC Volga open spaces"

If you are interested in hotels in Tutaev of the Yaroslavl Region with budget accommodation, then the KTK Volzhsky Prostory hostel would be a good option. From here you can also walk to the Volga, which is three hundred meters from the hotel. Within walking distance are supermarkets, several cafes. Guests can park their car in free public parking. WIFI is also free throughout the hostel. The hotel offers a tour desk. The hostel has eleven rooms, including:

  • the total six-seater for men and women - 600 rubles per day per person;
  • single - 950 rubles per day;
  • double - 1400 rubles per day for two and 700 rubles per day for one person;
  • double with a bathroom - 2600 rubles per day for two and 1300 rubles per day for one person.

The hostel is located at: Yaroslavskaya street, 36a.

How tourists respond about the hostel

The guests left the following reviews:

  • Guests enjoyed the hostel's location very close to the crossing.

  • Internet works great.
  • The rooms have comfortable beds.
