Crocheted slippers: diagram, description. Crochet slippers from squares: scheme

Home slippers always attract attention. They can be from a variety of materials. Original products that are made by oneself always look original. In this article we will talk about how to crochet slippers. A diagram of some products will also be presented. You are sure to pick up something for your family members.

Women's slippers

At home, girls want to stay fashionable and stylish. Why put on your feet worn slippers or ugly rubber slates? You can always pick up a hook and create a real work of art. Not sure where to start crocheting? Slippers (the scheme is presented below) are done in one evening. The pattern is simple, but it looks feminine and sophisticated.





First you need to determine the size. To do this, take your old sock. You will be guided by it. First of all, the sock of the product is knitted. It consists of a set of half columns. First dial the ring. Wrap two full rings of yarn on your finger. Through it, knit four loops and close the resulting circle by pulling the thread. After that, the product knits in a circle. Every two rows, it is necessary to make additions of loops.





After the sock is ready, we proceed to the most difficult part. The base will be in the form of patterns. The scheme is presented above. As you can see, double crochet and air loops will be used. The bootleg is knitted with half columns. Buttons are sewn along the edges as decoration.

Men's slippers

Guys also like to keep their feet warm. We also offer them to crochet home slippers. Product layout is very simple. But the appearance is attractive and stylish. A teenager will definitely appreciate such a creative approach to slippers.





To knit, you need two colors. The pattern is the simplest available. We use double crochet. The product knits in a circle. First, we collect a ring of light threads and knit several rows with the addition of loops. From light wool turned out the nose of the future slipper.





Next, we take a dark shade and make several rows. It is better to try on the foot to understand how long it is worth to take rows. To crochet such slippers, the scheme is not needed.





After that we knit the language of the future sneaker. The length depends on the size of the feet of the owner of the slippers. Next, proceed to create the bottom. Knitted fabric with the addition of loops at the edges. It should be a triangle. Do not forget to put your masterpiece on your foot. We connect the product in a single design and decorate with decorative elements. In the place of formation of the tongue, we make lacing from the tape.

Square motifs

If it seems to you that it is difficult to crochet such slippers, the pattern also seems incomprehensible, then try using square motifs. This method is the simplest of all available. Geometric shapes are created according to the scheme. You can use a variety of patterns and colors.





Crochet slippers from squares look interesting. The motive scheme is very simple. Women and men can wear such a product. Slippers for children are made from small cubes. One pair will need six original figures. They are sewn on the outside with a neat seam.





For the smallest

It is very simple to crochet baby slippers. The circuit is usually not needed. First you type a chain of air loops. Its length is equal to the size of the legs of your child. After that, a full-sleeper is tied in a circle. Additions are always made along the edges, knitting two double crochet in one loop. Better to use tight threads. We add new rows until the heel of the baby completely stands on the blank.





Next, the sides are knitted. You knit in a circle, but do not make additions. You can knit a variety of patterns. If you use only half-columns or double loops, decorate the finished product with a decorative element. In this case, a flower with a bead was used.

Funny animals

You can crochet funny slippers . The scheme is usually very simple. You can always fantasize and invent your own animals. For example, bright owls will attract the attention of everyone who comes to visit you. It will be fun for you to walk around in such slippers around the house. The brighter the threads, the more interesting the product looks. First, a base of any color is knitted. Choose a dense coat so that the service life is longer. After the slippers are ready, we sew decorative elements. We determine the nose, eyes and beak. You can make a bow or attach a flower.





Harder to make chanterelles. In this option, you need to guess where the white spots are located. If you find it difficult to mix colors, just wash your cheeks by tying them separately. The base is made of orange threads . Ears are sewn onto the crown. A cute tail is attached behind. Of course, knitting them is much more difficult than slippers crocheting from squares (the scheme in such products is improvised).





It's all about fish

Especially popular are the inhabitants of the deep sea. Shark slippers look a lot of fun. There is a feeling that the fish swallowed the entire human leg. Of course, who practices regular crochet, slippers (a pattern is not even needed) can create in one evening. Others need to exercise more, and only then you will get the desired result.

Back to our fish. They can be made in two versions. The first involves knitting a standard gray base. After that, fins, tail and eyes are sewn to a simple slipper. Do not forget about the characteristic part of predatory fish - teeth. All this is assembled in a single design and looks attractive.





The second option begins with the nose of the toe. A simple circle turns into a sock, the heel is better to do white. It will be the belly of our fish. Near the ankle, each side begins to knit separately. The nose of the shark is pointed, take this into account and give it the right shape. Next, we lengthen the backdrop and go to the front with a white thread. We make teeth to our predator. We knit the fins separately. The tail is missing, as it will interfere with walking. Eyes can be made from buttons or beads.





These simple slippers for the home can be knitted by any needlewoman. It is always pleasant when the feet are warm.
