Polotsk: hotels and its attractions

Polotsk is the oldest city of the Vitebsk region in Belarus. This city is located on the banks of the Western Dvina River. The first mention of Polotsk dates back to the distant 862.



Historical reference

Now in the city there are only about 90 thousand inhabitants. Once upon a time, 60% of the population were Jewish families. However, times and customs changed. Most of the locals left for permanent residence on the "promised land."

For centuries, the city absorbed the culture of various civilizations. The mentality of the Polovtsy formed the influence of different cultures: Scandinavians, Balts, Russians, Jews. Hotel "Dvina"

Hotel "Dvina" is part of the largest hotel complex "Slavic". The windows of the building offer a gorgeous view of the coast of the Western Dvina and part of the city of Polotsk. Hotel "Dvina" is the oldest post-war building, decorated with bas-reliefs and columns. It opened in December 1954. Of course, during this period the building underwent renovations and reconstruction several times. However, outwardly it did not change.



The room stock of this hotel is 111 rooms of various categories. There are standards, quins, suites, and suites.

Each of these rooms is equipped with everything necessary for relaxation and work. The rooms are equipped with modern appliances and ergonomic furniture. Each has a shower and a bathroom, a fridge and a telephone, a work desk and a TV. Personal hygiene items and a hairdryer are always available in the bathroom. Superior rooms and suites have a dining area with a fully equipped kitchenette.

Breakfast is included in the room rate, which is served in the hotel restaurant until 10 a.m. According to the guests of this hotel, the restaurant at the hotel has good cuisine, which serves national and European dishes.

The Dvina hotel has a sauna, a restaurant, a cafeteria, a hairdresser, a bathhouse. In the courtyard of the hotel complex there are free parking spaces.

Near the hotel there are such attractions as monuments to F. Skorina and S. Polotsky. Nearby is a memorial sign reminding everyone that Polotsk is the geographical center of all of Europe.

"Sail" - the best hotel of the city

The second largest and most popular hotel is the Sail. It is located in the building of a new sports complex in the city center. There is a base for training athletes of the highest qualification in rowing. The intricate building resembles a ship. It consists of 3 blocks of various heights and sizes. The Parus Hotel (Polotsk) is part of the rowing center system. Guests can use all the halls located in the building: sauna, gyms, games room, pool, bathhouse. The hotel offers fitness, yoga, strip plastic services.



The hotel is designed for simultaneous accommodation of 100 people. The number base is 59 rooms of various categories and sizes. These are cozy single and double rooms equipped with everything necessary for a comfortable stay.



Each room has a shower room and a bathroom, where good plumbing is installed. Furniture and appliances are modern. There is free Wi-Fi. Cleaning and linen change are regularly carried out.



The hotel offers a wonderful view: from one side to the Western Dvina, and from the other - to the Holy Protection Church.

For an additional nominal fee, you can use an iron and an electric kettle. No smoking at the hotel.

You can have lunch and a meal in the Volna cafe at the sports complex. Nearby there are restaurants and nightclubs. The warm atmosphere and the friendly attitude of the Parus Hotel staff will delight city guests.
