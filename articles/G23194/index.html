Waterpark (Gelendzhik) "Dolphin": address, reviews

Gelendzhik is one of the most popular resorts of the Black Sea. Attract tourists to this city the gentle sea, exotic nature and, of course, all kinds of entertainment. There are three water parks in Gelendzhik alone. One of them - "Dolphin" is located right on the shore of the bay, in the city beach.

general description

Waterpark (Gelendzhik) "Dolphin" - the oldest and smallest water park in the city, opened back in 2002. A very convenient location makes it the most popular establishment of this kind in this resort. Both adults and children can have a good time. Dolphin was initially oriented towards family vacations. Its popularity is explained, among other things, by very low prices for attractions, as well as a well-established work schedule. Since the territory of the water park "Dolphin" is very small, it is convenient for parents to watch the children.





The staff of the water park, judging by the reviews of tourists, is very friendly and constantly monitors the state of slides and attractions. The territory of the park is periodically cleaned. Visitors can count on the help of employees at any time. Very attractive to tourists in this water park and the lack of lines.

Rides

Amusement water park (Gelendzhik) "Dolphin", despite its small size, provides many vacationers. If you wish, you can visit, for example, attractions:

  • "Kamikaze-Abyss". It is intended for thrill-seekers and has a length of 26 meters and a height of 15 m.
  • "Multifom." This attraction consists of four tracks with a length of 37 and a height of 8 meters and is just perfect for racing enthusiasts.
  • Kamikaze-Zero Gravity. The length of this slide is 32 meters, height - 15 m.

In addition to the water park "Dolphin" there are:

  • Two slides "Tabogan". One of them has a length of 98 m and a height of 11 m, the second - a length of 60 m and a height of 7 m.
  • Family slide "Superslide". Parents can ride it with their children. This is a very fun and also completely safe ride. The length of the slide is 12 m, the height is 7 m.
  • Hydrotube "Corkscrew". The length of this slide is as much as 105 meters. Its height is 15 meters.

Pools

Slides are not the only entertainment provided by the park to the attention of vacationers. You can also have a good time plunging into the warm water of the local pools. The largest are:

  • The Jacuzzi . This pond 60 cm deep has a very interesting teardrop shape. It is equipped with geysers, cascades and air jets. This pool is just the perfect place for hydromassage lovers.
  • "Children's." This is a very shallow pond, specially designed for bathing the little guests of the water park. Its depth is only 20-40 cm. In the center is an interesting cascade in the form of a fungus.

In addition to them, the park has several more small children's pools.

Cafes and Bars

The Dolphin water park (Gelendzhik) provides an opportunity for visitors not only to have fun and relax, but also to eat deliciously. It offers guests on its territory a cozy cafe with the same name. Here, visitors are served mostly familiar dishes of European cuisine. Polite and quick waiters serve guests. The food in this cafe is not only delicious, but also inexpensive. Queues in an institution never happen.

Souvenirs and entertainment

On the territory of the water park "Dolphin" you can also purchase original trinkets, which in the future will remind you of a pleasant vacation in the resort town of Gelendzhik. If you wish, you can order a mug, keychain or magnet with your photo. To do this, you need to contact a small kiosk located in the complex.





"Dolphin" - an aqua park (the city of Gelendzhik), where you can not only ride the slides and swim in the pools. For example, professional actors give very interesting representations here. They are held daily. In the evening you can visit an interesting water-light show. Within its framework, pools are illuminated by multi-colored lights that create completely unique effects in the water. At the same time, fireworks are launched into the air.

In the daytime for the kids in the pools are carried out animated rides. Children can also have fun while riding from the small slides with which the pond intended for them is equipped. A large bucket hangs above the pool itself. Overflowing with water, it overturns and splashes little bathers, whose delight there is no limit.

Ticket price

Relax in the water park "Dolphin" can be very inexpensive. Adult tickets cost only about 500 rubles. For a child from 3 to 10 years old you will have to pay a little less - 300 rubles. For very young children, admission to the water park is absolutely free. For the convenience of visitors, locker rooms, a toilet and a shower are provided on site. If desired, things can be left in the storage room.

The exact prices are easy to find by visiting the official website of this complex. There, Internet users are presented with a large photo gallery and a diagram of the park.

Water bike rental is available in the immediate vicinity . Outdoor enthusiasts can also visit the beach volleyball court.

For a not too big fee in this park you can stay long enough to try all the entertainment and dine. Of course, vacationers also like the fact that the complex is located right on the beach. After all, the resort usually spend time here. Thus, thanks to the presence of this water park, on the Gelendzhik beach you can not only sunbathe and swim, but also have good fun. Other water parks in Gelendzhik

In addition to "Dolphin" in this resort town there are two more such complexes - "Hippo" and "Golden Bay". The first is located right in the center of Gelendzhik and accepts vacationers without breaks and weekends. In this water park you can swim in three pools and ride from seventeen different slides. Also on its territory is equipped with a playground.

The largest water park in the country, Golden Bay, is a huge complex built in a unique style (a mixture of eras). This is a complex of European level and the amount of entertainment is appropriate.
