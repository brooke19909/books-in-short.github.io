How to clean the headphones from sulfur? IPhone Headphones: Care Rules

How to clean the headphones from sulfur? The more often you wear such a miracle of modern technology, the sooner you notice that the sound changes over time: it becomes quieter, the quality is not the same as it was before, the bass is noticeably reduced. There are two obvious reasons for this: the wires are damaged or the speakers are clogged. If most of your life, headphones do not leave your ears, then they become clogged faster. What can be a source of pollution? Of course you are!





The ear tirelessly produces earwax, which enters the ear canal. This viscous viscous substance has a yellowish tint. It is this vital product that clogs up in dynamics, making the sound so bad. A few simple and practical tips will give them a second life, and you will save on buying new ones.

Types

Firstly, you need to remember that not all headphones are the same. There are many different types: buttons, intra-channel, wired, wireless, overhead, full-sized, closed, open. Naturally, they require different care. The article will focus on the most common today. The most used models are vacuum and those that come with, for example, an iPhone - Apple EarPods.

We clean the vacuum headphones. What do you need?

How to clean vacuum headphones from sulfur? Such models always have rubber pads that can be easily removed. This is their plus.





List of tools for work:

  1. Hydrogen peroxide.
  2. Cotton pads.
  3. Alcohol.
  4. Ear sticks.
  5. Paper napkins (in extreme cases, can be replaced with toilet paper).
  6. Scotch.
  7. Capacity for liquids.

There is no need to take a basin as a container. Any cap with small sides will be just right. It should be clean inside, so wipe it before use. Medical alcohol is not always in the medicine cabinet, so you can use vodka instead.

How to clean your headphones from sulfur: action plan

First, prepare the bath: in the selected container, pour water, a few drops will be enough. More is not better, the main task is to clean the net.

Remove rubber pads and hide away, most importantly, do not forget where they were put, because losing plans is not included in our plans.





After that, we gently and slowly immerse the headphones in peroxide. Do not dunk the device as if you are brewing a tea bag. Only the mesh should be in the liquid! In this position, they will soak for about 15 minutes. Holding hands is not an option. It is most correct to fix the structure with pre-prepared adhesive tape and do other things.

Remember where the rubber bands were removed, take them out and start cleaning with a cotton pad moistened with alcohol in advance . Alternative: alcohol is poured into a glass and linings are sent there. Well, nothing will happen to them, soak as much as you want. Here you can use cotton swabs. After all, dirt accumulates in inaccessible places. After that, they must be wiped dry.

The time allotted for the adoption of water procedures is over. Remove the tape and remove (as carefully as put) from a solution of hydrogen peroxide. It is contraindicated to turn upside down, if the remaining liquid gets inside, it will be possible to throw out the accessory. Slowly, pat them with a napkin and leave for an hour and a half to dry completely.

Then you put on everything that was filmed, and then enjoy your favorite tracks.

Headphones from an iPhone. What is needed for cleaning?

How to clean the headphones from the iPhone? Note that they are slightly different from vacuum ones. There are no inserts in the kit, so the mesh is clogged with dust and earwax much stronger. It is real to clean iPhone headphones from sulfur, however, it takes a little perseverance and effort.

To conscientiously clean the grid, it is better to remove it. If there is such an opportunity, then use a needle or tweezers, carefully picking up the net on one side. If you do not risk touching it, you will only have to wipe it from the outside.





What needs to be prepared for this operation:

  1. Alcohol or vodka.
  2. Toothpicks or needles - 2 pcs.
  3. Cotton buds - 2 pcs.
  4. Paper towel.

Earphone tips and tricks

Any item will get dirty sooner or later. Headphones are no exception. This is a natural process; you do not store them on a shelf under a glass dome. Proper and constant care of the device is very important. Dirt accumulating on the surface is a real threat. It can become a breeding ground for pathogens.





Dirty headphones look completely unaesthetic. To listen to good quality music for as long as possible, to save your money and health, adhere to three rules:

  1. Clean regularly (1-2 times per month).
  2. Take care of ear cleanliness (daily cleaning is not necessary).
  3. Headphones are an individual thing, like a toothbrush. Do not allow anyone to use them, observe the rules of hygiene.
