How to transfer money from "Life" to "Life": detailed instructions

If you, as is usually the case, run out of money on the balance of the Life operator at the wrong time, then you can take advantage of special services from this mobile provider. In the article, we will consider all the ways how to transfer money from Life to Life. But since this operator serves two countries - Ukraine and Belarus, then different options for the same operation will be considered.





Transfer using a USSD request (first method)

This translation option is the most common, although many may not know about it. A USSD request allows you to quickly transfer funds from one Life balance to another, and all you need to do is dial the required key combination on your phone. Now we will discuss a way how to transfer money from Life to Life using a USSD request.





Please note that this transfer method is possible only for subscribers residing in Ukraine. Here is what you will need to do:

  1. You need to dial a USSD request in the following format: * 111 * recipient number * transfer amount #. Take into account that between the filled data there should not be unnecessary information. The number must be entered in the national format, that is, with the number 3. Here is an example of this request: * 111 * 380631234567 * 10 #.
  2. After that, press the call key.
  3. Once the operation is complete, you will receive a message with a report on your phone.

By the way, for this service you will be charged 1 hryvnia commission from your account. With one transfer you can send a maximum of 500 hryvnia. A transfer will be successful only if after it you have more than 10 hryvnias left in your account.

Above, the first method was presented, how to transfer money from Life to Life. But not the last. If the first option seemed complicated to you, then read the article to the end, in which case you will find 100 percent a more suitable way.

Transfer using a USSD request (second method)

In the second option, we will also use the USSD request, but it is completely different.

Dial the following key combination on your phone: * 124 #. Press the call key. As soon as you do this, a pop-up window will appear on your screen in which the service menu is located. Since we need to transfer money from our account to another, we select the corresponding line "Balance Transfer". To select it, click "Reply" and enter the number denoting "Balance Transfer".





In the next window you will need to indicate the number of the subscriber to whom the money will be sent. The number must be entered starting from the three. Now you need to specify the amount in hryvnias. After the operations done, in case everything went well, you will receive an SMS notification on your phone.

This was the second way how to transfer money from Life to Life. By the way, this method will also deduct 1 commission from the account from your account, and you can also send a maximum of 500 hryvnias. Roughly speaking, you send the same request as in the first case, only here everything is explained in more detail.

SMS translation

We continue to talk about how to transfer money from Life to Life. Number for transfer in another way, see below. We have already figured out how to do this using USSD-requests, but there is a high probability that these methods will seem complicated to someone. That is why you can use SMS translation.





To transfer money from your balance to another, enter the option of mobile - SMS. You will see two fields: 1 - a field for entering a number, 2 - a field for entering text. In the first field, you will need to enter the following number: 124. For the operation to be completed in the second field, you need to enter the following: "PEREVOD_ number of the subscriber receiving the money_ transfer amount in numbers." Note that instead of the "_" sign you need to put spaces, otherwise the operation will not be successful.

To make it more clear, you should give an example: PEREVOD 380631234567 25. After filling in all the fields, click on the "Submit" button. If the operation is successful, you will receive a message with a detailed report. By the way, unlike the previous methods, there is no commission in this, that is, you will be able to send money without spending your money.

So you have learned the third way how you can transfer money from Life to Life, and, in fact, it is the last, but the fact is that all these methods apply to subscribers residing in Ukraine.
