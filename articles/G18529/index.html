Provider "Ecotelecom": organization reviews, description and contacts

Ecotelecom is an Internet, telephony and television provider. This organization operates in Moscow and the Moscow region. It works with residents of new residential complexes, cottages and, in general, with those who can afford much more than the average Russian. Despite this, Ecotelecom, reviews about which are often unflattering, which the company itself did not fail to report on the official website and pages on social networks, enjoys considerable popularity.





About provider

The provider is positioned as a company providing high-speed Internet. This statement is based on the use of the latest technology. But Ecotelecom, according to reviews from Odintsovo, for example, is slightly exaggerating. Most people complain about the low speed of the Internet - about 40-60 Mb / s., While the stated 100 Mb / s.

But the company has many tariffs and offers for customers. And Ecotelecom is trying to build its service on a level. The company offers beneficial and generally diverse and optimal cable television options not only to customers individually, but also to groups and corporate clients. The provider works in almost all of Moscow and in the region.

Coverage map

The company operates in such districts of the city of Moscow as the Western, Novomoskovsky, Northern, North-Western, Central, Southern, South-Eastern and South-Western administrative districts.

In the region, Ecotelecom is present in such cities: Balashikha, Lyubertsy, Zheleznodorozhny, Krasnogorsk, Mytishchi, Odintsovo, Podolsk, Khimki and Chekhov. This is also the areas of Solnechnogorsk, Odintsovo, Lyubertsy, Leninsky and Krasnogorsk.





Depending on the area, customers leave diametrically opposed reviews. For example, in Moscow, in some places, people complain about the low speed of the Internet. Conversely, Ecotelecom, according to reviews in Podolsk, is the best provider of the city.

Internet "Ecotelecom"

The data transfer rate when using the Internet, according to the provider, ranges from 30 to 300 Mb / s. Such a pleasure is relatively inexpensive - from 399 to 749 rubles / month. Ecotelecom adds a lot of positive feedback about the Internet to a large extent to the free service and the ability to create a car payment or ask for a deferred payment from the provider.
