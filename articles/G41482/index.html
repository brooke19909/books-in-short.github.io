Cottage Village "South Cape" - the best place for a quiet life

Residents of Moscow and the Moscow region are not surprised by the variety of cottage villages, the proposed land for development. But among them stands out the cottage village "South Cape", located 35 kilometers from the Moscow Ring Road.





Location

How to get to the village? In the complete absence of traffic lights, the Kashirskoye highway will lead directly to the cottage village. By your own car, it‚Äôs just forty minutes on the road along the almost always free highway, and you drive into one of the most beautiful places in the Moscow Region. A place in which the whole family will live comfortably in their own new home.

Good transport accessibility is an important criterion when choosing a future residence. You can get to the cottage village "Southern Cape" not only by personal motor transport, but also by train, leaving from the Paveletsky station. There is a public transport stop near the village . A fixed-route taxi will easily and quickly deliver residents not only to Domodedovo, but also to Moscow to the Domodedovo metro station.





What is offered for sale

The cottage village "Southern Cape" Domodedovo offers to purchase more than 150 land plots ranging from six to twenty-five hundred parts. Their minimum cost is about three hundred seventy thousand rubles. The maximum is one million six hundred thousand rubles. The price varies depending on the location of the site. The status of all the land that the Cape Town "Cape" offers to purchase is IZHS (for individual residential construction).

In addition to the value of the land allotment, it is also necessary to pay for all communications and infrastructure. The owner of the village does not offer a building contract so that the owners can choose for themselves which house to build, do it on their own or contact any existing construction company. Further development of the territory will be carried out by decision of the general meeting of owners.

Infrastructure

The cottage village "South Cape", or rather, its owner, will not ignore the small future residents. For them, the project provides for the construction of a playground. To address emerging issues, an administrative building is being erected.

Very successfully the cottage village "South Cape" is located. Around untouched nature, beautiful large ponds in which you can swim in the hot summer.





Communications

All communications necessary for a comfortable life for the owners of the site are provided. Electricity lines of 15 kW are allocated to all houses, which is quite enough for housekeeping. The houses will be separated by wide roads, the surface of which is made of rubble and sand. The streets of the village will brighten the way home.

One of the great advantages that the cottage village offers is a closed guarded area. Along the perimeter of the village there will be a continuous metal fence through which no one can penetrate. The fence will create additional security, and you can not worry about the safety of your property and take evening walks. In addition, the entrance to the village is guarded by a checkpoint, where a security guard is located around the clock, checking all those entering and leaving.

The territory of the village is cleared of snow and debris by special economic services.

Moving will not break the usual way of life. The cottage village "South Cape" reviews has only positive. Both from those who have already managed to build housing on its territory, and from those who are just planning to build.





Proximity to the metropolis of the capital, but at the same time living in the fresh air in an ecologically clean area - what more could you want ?! By purchasing a plot of land in this cottage village, you are buying truly paradise life.

The village is included in the urban district of Domodedovo, which automatically gives its residents access to the entire infrastructure of this district. And these are schools, kindergartens, and hospitals with polyclinics. That is, its inhabitants will have the same comfortable life to which they are accustomed, but only with the advantage of fresh air.

The nearby forest is rich in gifts of nature, such as mushrooms and berries. In wonderful summer weather, it will be nice to take a walk there and at the same time pick up a bucket of mushrooms or a basket of wild berries. This will be appreciated by avid mushroom pickers and berry-growers.
