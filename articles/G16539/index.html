Park Husky in Pervouralsk. Description

Many interesting entertainments can be found in Pervouralsky "Husky park". In this place you can try yourself in the role of a blacksmith by making a metal souvenir under the supervision of a professional. There is also the opportunity to stay Tarzan and walk through the rope park. This is quite an interesting and popular place. Further, you can learn more about the city park of culture and rest.

About the park

In such a place, residents and guests of the city have the opportunity to chat with Samoyed huskies and huskies. The former are considered some of the kindest dogs. And about huskies it is known that representatives of this breed will never bite a person. Therefore, not only adults, but even small children can communicate with them. Puppies

You can meet puppies in the park. For example, last July, Samoyed babies were born here. Then they were born six pieces.

Note that puppies are usually engaged in their children's affairs, for example, they can nibble something stubbornly. The smallest puppies are kept in a special enclosure. You can only watch them. It is forbidden to touch babies, as their immune system is still poorly developed.

By the way, in addition to the fact that you can chat with dogs in the park, you can also buy a Samoyed or Husky puppy here. Regarding the acquisition, it is worth talking to the owners of the park.

How to get to the park?

Between the Water Sports Palace and the Ice Palace is the entrance to the city park.

You should drive through the square first. Further on the left will be the stadium and behind it the Ice Palace. Before the pedestrian crossing, you should turn left, there is parking. After you enter the park, go straight ahead. There you will see further wooden buildings. They are a mark of Husky Park.
