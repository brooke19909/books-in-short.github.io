Hotel "Intercontinental Moscow Tverskaya"

According to the latest statistics, approximately 500 thousand people come to the capital of Russia annually. This, in turn, means that the hotel industry has great prospects for further development.

Moscow

The capital of the Russian Federation - the city of Moscow - annually receives thousands of tourists from different countries and corners of Russia. Excursions leave bright unforgettable emotions for city guests. People come here to see the giant glass "Moscow City", take a walk along the quiet Zamoskvorechye, take memorable photos against the backdrop of the Kremlin on Red Square. Moscow is considered to be the "third Rome": the greatness of this million-plus city has already spread far beyond the borders of the Russian Federation.

In order to make their stay in the capital as comfortable as possible, tourists should take care of accommodation facilities in advance. One of the popular Moscow hotels is the Intercontinental Moscow Tverskaya Hotel. The official name of the hotel is "InterContinental Moscow-Tverskaya".

Hotel Description

The hotel complex "Intercontinental Moscow Tverskaya" is a symbol of comfortable luxury and sophistication. The rooms of the five-star hotel are a fusion of modern design and Soviet-era architecture. The best designers of the English company AlexKravetzDesign put their hand and creative look to the exclusive hotel interior .

Hotel Location

Hotel "Intercontinental Moscow Tverskaya" is located in Moscow, at the address: Tverskaya street, house 22.

Towering in the heart of the capital, the hotel will provide a comfortable stay to anyone who gives it their preference. Moscow guests will be pleasantly surprised by the proximity of several metro stations and the walking distance of such city attractions as Pushkinskaya Square, various exhibition centers and the Cathedral of Christ the Savior.





Hotel rooms

The total number of rooms at Intercontinental Moscow Tverskaya Hotel is 203. All of them differ in the level of service and type of accommodation, half of them are for non-smoking guests. One thing unites them: each room belongs to the category of superior comfort and corresponds to the level of service offered to a five-star hotel. Any of them is equipped with:

  • one or two beds
  • bathroom and toilet
  • 42-inch LCD TV with satellite channels
  • free internet
  • a safe
  • iPhone Dock
  • mini-bar
  • air conditioning
  • wardrobe
  • telephone
  • work table.

It also has bath amenities, a hairdryer, a kettle and an ironing board with iron. It should be noted that the Intercontinental Moscow Tverskaya Hotel (guest reviews confirm this) provides good quality service and clean rooms.





Features of the services provided

On the second floor of the hotel complex, guests can enjoy modern options of long-loved dishes of Russian traditional cuisine in the Chekhonte restaurant. They will be prepared in the open kitchen, while guests will spend time waiting, enjoying the bewitching view from the panoramic windows to Tverskaya Square. In the P-Square bar, you can indulge in small talk or simply relax among the marble columns under the light of chic chandeliers, ordering one of the delicious desserts and a variety of cocktails.





At the hotel guests are offered a wide selection of amenities and services.

  • Guests can relax in the sauna or bath, and then use the services of a massage therapist and cosmetologist.
  • If desired, food and drinks can be ordered directly to your room.
  • Car rental is available; shuttle services and a guide are provided. The hotel staff will also undertake the organization of any celebrations and holidays.
  • For those who prefer to keep fit everywhere and always, there is a fitness room and a fitness center.
  • For business people who came to the capital to negotiate, the second floor has a conference room.
  • The hotel offers babysitting services with children .
  • Paid parking, an ATM and dry cleaning / laundry services are also on site.
  • For people with disabilities, all conditions are created.
  • Pets are not allowed in the hotel, with the exception of guide dogs.
