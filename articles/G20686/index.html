Such an insidious gazebo

Almost always, the marine junction was especially popular. As everyone knows, it has several varieties, among which the gazebo should be noted . It is also called "bowline" - a nasal rope. It belongs to the main types of nodes since ancient times. For its simplicity and versatility in use, it is sometimes called the king of nodes. In addition, he has no obvious flaws.



As history has shown, the gazebo was used in ancient Egypt and Phenicia about five thousand years ago. For the first time, mention was made of him in one of J. Smith's textbooks on maritime affairs. This textbook was published back in 1629.

The following was written verbatim about how to knit a marine knot of this kind: it knits in such a way as to get a hose on a rope through which the free end is subsequently passed up and down. Subsequently, it will be necessary to skip it again into the peg, rounding the running section of the lappar around the main one. After that, the peg must be tightened as tight as possible. In addition, it will be necessary to tighten the undercarriage of the lapper's rope.



The gazebo site got its name thanks to the marine term "gazebo", which means a small wooden board with which a person was raised or lowered. A bowline (and this knot was called in England in this way) is a tackle that is necessary in order to pull the lower edge of the sail. It does not untie, but also does not drag out under load, such as a noose-knot. It also allows you to easily untie yourself at a time when the load is already removed.

The gazebo is often criticized because of its cunning. Even tied up incorrectly, he may not give himself away until they begin to lift a load with his help. It is at such a moment that he is unleashed. The pancake will work correctly only if the same load is applied to all lateral ends of the rope. In addition, the loop of such a node does not allow the presence of side loading, except on the main rope.



In addition, a control unit is also needed, which will not allow the gazebo to stretch. All these errors lead to the fact that they try to replace the pancake if possible with any other similar nodes.
