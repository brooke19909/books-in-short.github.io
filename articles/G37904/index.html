Small sea: recreation facilities. Photos and reviews

Probably, many of our compatriots have never heard of the existence of the Small Sea in our country, and even more so did not know about the availability of recreation centers in this area. Today we will try to fill this gap.



Where is the Small Sea?

This is part of Lake Baikal, which is separated from it by the island of Olkhon. Experts consider Small Lake a strait or a bay. Baikal connects the Olkhon Gate Strait with the Small Sea, and Mukhor Bay is located to the north. This is an ecologically clean area, so those who appreciate the pristine nature and crystal clear air tend here.

Origin of the name and general information

Buryats call this area Narin-Dalai. This name literally means ‚Äúnarrow sea‚Äù. Its width does not exceed 16 km, the length is more than 70 km. In the northern part, the depth reaches 210 meters. The shore is mountainous. The vegetation is steppe, mountain taiga. On the coast are settlements - Kharantsy, Khuzhir, Kurma, Peschanaya.

The Small Sea is perhaps one of the most visited regions on Lake Baikal. He is considered a place of spiritual and mental strength. Adherents of yoga and other meditative practices come here. It is often called "Little Tibet." Tourists from all over the world come to admire the beauty of these places.



Small sea: recreation centers

Everyone who wants to see the pristine northern nature of the famous Baikal should go on vacation to these places. It created quite comfortable living conditions, built numerous resort areas.

Recreation center Ulirba (Small Sea)

This charming place is located on Cape Ulirba of Mukhor Bay, 250 km from Irkutsk, 12 km from the village of Chernorud. "Ulirba" accommodates one hundred vacationers. Wooden houses with rooms for two to five people have been built on the territory. In addition, you can settle in a tent city, or use the novelty - to live in a yurt. Washbasins, shower, toilet are in designated areas.

On the territory of the base there is a refectory, in the form of a yurt, where you can eat three times a day if you wish. Rare dishes of national cuisine are offered - fish caught from Baikal in various forms, homemade cakes. For those who prefer to cook on their own, there are gas stoves and kitchen utensils in the houses. Here you can do volleyball, tennis, various water sports. In the evening, tourists are attracted to the water bar.



Reviews of tourists about this base are ambiguous. "The Seagull" - recreation center

This is a famous holiday destination on Baikal located on the shore of the Gulf of Kurkut. On the territory there are beautiful houses made of golden pine. They very harmoniously fit into the stunning landscape - deciduous woodlands with edelweiss and bells and other beautiful flowers.

Today, many Russian and foreign tourists tend to get to the Small Sea. Recreation facilities are being built the most diverse, designed for any financial income. The Seagull has been hosting guests all year. Those who wish can settle in a comfortable two-story building or in comfortable 2-3-seater houses. Guests will be shown Buryat rites. Here, in your presence, a ram can be killed and butchered (unless, of course, you have a desire to look at this procedure). In the cafe a delicious dish will be prepared from it according to the recipes of the Buryat cuisine.

The recreation center "The Seagull" (Small Sea) attracts tourists with its excellent service. Reviews of tourists suggest that in addition to a completely stunning nature, guests are impressed by the friendly attitude of the staff. In addition, national rites, especially vivid ones like the Buryat ones, cannot leave anyone indifferent. Many tourists note the conformity of price and quality of services provided. The rooms are quite comfortable and allow you to have a good rest.

"Star" - a camp site on the Small Sea

It is located in Khuzhir-Nute Bay. It works only in the summer. Free walking tours of the picturesque bays are provided. Many people believe that the Zvezda recreation center (Small Sea) is a leader in conducting discos in the area.

Here you can do different types of water sports, extreme sportsmen use a water parachute, by the way, the only one on the Small Sea. Guests of the base can stay in the house in two rooms (double). The price is 500 rubles per day. A double room in an individual house will cost 600 rubles per day, a similar room in the building costs 800 rubles. Children under five stay free. Up to 12 years old children are paid at the rate of 20% of the total cost. The reviews of tourists vacationing at this base make it possible to consider it an ideal place for families with children. In addition, quite affordable prices are attracted.

Each of them has a bedroom, a relaxation room equipped with satellite TV. In addition, there are 5 detached houses, with two triple rooms in each. Three meals a day are offered, it is possible to order an individual menu.

"Altan" - fishing paradise

For everyone who wants to see the Small Sea, recreation centers are an excellent choice that allows you to realize your dream. There are a lot of them now, so you need to take care of the choice in advance.

The recreation center "Altan" (Small Sea) is located on the shore of Khuzhur-Nuuga Bay. Its unique location, the proximity of the holy Baikal places attract tourists here at all times of the year. You can stay in double rooms of a two-story cottage or in detached houses with verandas, designed to accommodate from 2 to 4 guests. All rooms have a view of Lake Baikal.

Meals are provided in the dining room with a spacious terrace and beautiful views of Lake Baikal. Tourists speak of ‚ÄúAltan‚Äù as a very cozy place where you can truly relax and unwind with both soul and body. According to visitors to the base, this is a real paradise for anglers. Here you can rent the necessary gear and motor boats, take a fishing tour with an experienced instructor who will show you the cherished places where the nibble is especially good. And after fishing, you can take a steam bath in the fireplace room.



In conclusion, I would like to advise everyone: come to the Small Sea. Recreation facilities of various comfort levels are waiting for you. If you are tired of the city, dream of visiting nature, breathing in truly clean air and seeing such beauties that neither a photo nor a TV can transmit, then come to Baikal - it is waiting for you!
