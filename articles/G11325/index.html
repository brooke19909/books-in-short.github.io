Cameras

The optics used in the BQ-4072 Strike Mini, reviews were not very flattering. The main camera is based on a 5 megapixel sensor. The developers supplemented its characteristics with autofocus and flash, which allow you to get a more or less high-quality frame. You can change the settings in the application. There are several additional modes. The clearest pictures are taken in the daytime. In poor lighting conditions, blurring and noise are visible in the photo.

The front-facing module with a resolution of 2 megapixels is mainly used for video communication. Selfies, of course, can be done, but the frame is obtained, frankly, not very. Detailing is low, color rendering is weak.

Autonomy

The maximum number of negative reviews BQ-4072 Strike Mini received due to a weak battery. Autonomy is provided by a 1500 mAh battery. Definitely, this is not enough for a smartphone that will be used not only for calls. Unfortunately, the phone does not have a special economic mode, so in order not to be left without a connection, you will have to purchase Power Bank.
