Sony offer

In March 2015, a big presentation from Sony was held at MWC in Barcelona. The waterproof phone Xperia M4 Aqua was the opening of this event. Its main distinguishing feature is the level of protection of the case, which is determined by the IP68 standard. This unit can stay under water at a depth of one and a half meters for half an hour. Background

Sony's first waterproof phone came out in 1999. It was a Ericsson R250 model. The company's next attempt to create a secure device was in 2008. But it was not successful due to the fact that the developers did not plug the communication port with a stub. This actually reduced the tightness of the housing to zero.

In June 2011, in Singapore, the world saw the Sony Ericsson Xperia Active. This time, the mistakes of the engineers were fixed. This unit was equipped with a double case. Its upper part performed a decorative function, and the inside - protective. The phone could withstand immersion by 1 meter.



In 2014, the Xperia M2 Aqua model was released in black and white. It was equipped with an 8 megapixel camera and an Exmor RS sensor. Thanks to a special application, Social Live, users have the opportunity to broadcast moments of their lives in real time to social networks. This smartphone could be connected to any device for listening to music - from headphones to the music center and speakers.
