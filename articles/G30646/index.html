Hotel Palm Beach Resort SPA Sanya 4 * (China, Hainan): photos and reviews of tourists

In our article we want to talk about the hotel Palm Beach Resort Spa Sanya, which is located in the territory of the best resort in the Middle Kingdom. Hainan Island is considered the best Chinese health resort, it is also called East Hawaii. Once upon a time, the island was a place of exile nobles. And now about this paradise in the South China Sea have heard tourists around the world. Residents of the Far East and Siberia have long been recovering here, treating ailments, and at the same time basking in the warm sunshine and in clear sea water.

Hotel infrastructure

Palm Beach Resort Spa (Sanya) 4 * has two pools on the territory. One of them is open to guests until late at night, there is a bar near it. When it gets dark, people move from the beach to the sunbeds near the reservoir, and continue their rest there. Swimming in the pool by the light of the lanterns is incredibly romantic. A wonderful tree grows above the water, the flowers of which constantly fall into the pool. Swimming amidst such beauty is something amazing.

In addition, the complex has tennis courts, a massage parlor, a golf course, a beauty salon, a fitness center, five conference rooms and a business center. On the coast, active tourists can always go in for water sports, try out new hobbies. There are all conditions for this.

Near the territory of Palm Beach Resort Spa Hotel 4 * (Sanya) there is a cemetery. Currently, it is not operational, and from afar resembles a square. However, not all vacationers like this neighborhood. Still, people come to relax and distract from problems.

Sea beach

Palm Beach Resort Spa Hotel 4 * (Sanya) has its own beach. In general, the location of the complex is its highlight, since not every hotel is on the first line. A small inconvenience is that you must cross the road to get to the shore, but it is not so difficult. The hotel's sandy beach, there are never waves on the sea. Umbrellas and sunbeds on the coast are completely free.

Speaking about the hotel Palm Beach Resort Spa (Sanya), reviews of which, by the way, are rather contradictory, it is worth noting the presence of small midges on the shore. According to vacationers, insects live in the grass on the beach and behave quite aggressively, because of which many bites appear on the bodies of tourists. Unfortunately, no local creams completely influence the midge, it is impossible to get rid of it. Apparently, there are periods when insects appear and then disappear somewhere. Otherwise, the coast is very clean and beautiful. The water is warm and clear.



There are always many fishermen on the shore waiting for their catch. Swimming in the sea, you can see flocks of shrimp, small fish, squid. There are small jellyfish in the water, but they are fearless, because they do not sting at all.

Dining at the hotel

I would like to pay special attention to nutrition. When you buy a tour at the Palm Beach Resort Spa (Sanya Bay), you only get breakfast. As for dinner and lunch, they can also be ordered at the hotel. And you can have good and inexpensive refreshments in local cafes, of which there are many in the vicinity of the hotel. The food there is quite normal and tasty, so you can safely eat it. Due to the fact that the resort is located near the sea, all establishments prepare a lot of seafood dishes. This is definitely worth using. Tourists advise careful attention to the choice of dishes, since the Chinese are preparing all sorts of marine reptiles that we are not used to eating. In the variety of local food it is quite possible to choose something to taste.

For breakfast, the hotel serves European and Chinese dishes, as well as seafood. If you do not like local food, then you can choose for yourself more acceptable options. Some tourists believe that morning food is very monotonous, others emphasize that they are quite happy with the food. Apparently, individual preferences are affected.

The standard breakfast at the hotel includes: sausages, duck (cooked according to the local recipe), vegetables, fruits, meat dishes, porridge, yogurt.



In general, vacationers are happy with the food. It is wonderful that the Chinese people carefully prepare not only their traditional dishes, but also European ones, since not all guests will master the local gastronomic delights. Still, European food is more familiar to our compatriots.

Quality of service

The Chinese are very interesting people. They are very sensitive to smiles and kind attitude. It is for this reason that they relate better to those guests who are not aggressive. This point is worth considering. Unfortunately, in a place like Palm Beach Resort Spa (Sanya, China), there is staff who speak only Chinese. But this is not a hotel problem, but rather, local customs. In China, it is not customary to use English at all. However, our compatriots do not have communication problems, because among the service staff of the complex there are people who perfectly understand the Russian language.

By the way, in China, quite a lot of local residents speak and understand our language.

Check in at the hotel is fast. Overall the service is very good. Apart from the fact that the rooms should be repaired for a long time, the Palm Beach Resort Spa (Sanya), the reviews of which we are analyzing, is a pretty decent place to stay. Staff clean the rooms daily and change towels. It is very nice that the apartments have special beach sets: bags with towels. It is very convenient and practical.



In Palm Beach Resort Spa (Sanya) 5 * it is customary to take a deposit for the room upon check-in. They then return the money when people are going to leave, if the apartments are rented in full order, and all services are paid.

sights

Not far from the city (23 kilometers), on the very shore there is a park with the beautiful name "End of the World". It attracts tourists with beautiful landscapes. On a sandy beach lumps of bizarre shapes rise. Each stone has its own name: "a stone that supports the heavens", "stone - the refuge of a bachelor", "broken heart". "Boulder - wealth" is even depicted on the local banknote (2 yuan). And on one ten-meter stone there is even an inscription saying that it is in this place that the extreme southern point of China is located - ‚ÄúEnd of the World‚Äù.

No less interesting for tourists is the oceanarium, in which you can get acquainted with the life of two hundred varieties of exotic inhabitants of the deep sea. Here you can see a show of crocodiles and sea lions, trained parrots. The bravest guests can feed the reptiles and take pictures with them. On the territory of the oceanarium live all species of birds of southern China. Here you can see unique turtles, whose age is more than six hundred years.

Eighty kilometers from the city there is an island of monkeys, on which more than two thousand primates live. A cable car stretches across the strait to the island. Tourists from the cabins of the funicular can enjoy a bird's eye view of the sea. On the island you can see not only animals, but also see the monkey circus.

Near the city (about forty kilometers) is the largest Buddhism Center in Asia at the foot of Nanshan Mountain. It was opened in 1997, its area is more than fifty kilometers square. Tourists will certainly enjoy not only the oldest temple (it was restored), but also a large landscape park.



The ethnic settlement of the Li and Miao ethnic groups is very popular among holidaymakers. These aborigines lived here long before the Chinese. Even now they live in thickets of tropical forests in the very center of the island, keeping their foundations and way of life unchanged.

Near the thermal radon keys is the famous botanical garden, which contains all the tropical plants of China. Their total number exceeds a thousand copies.

Thirty kilometers from Sanya is a butterfly museum, which is more like a piece of rainforest, in which many different insects flutter. The sight is simply amazing.

List all the attractions within the article is simply impossible. There really are an infinite number of them on the island, and many are located just near such a resort as Sanya.
