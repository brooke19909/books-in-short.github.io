Hostel (Korolev): various options, reviews, addresses

Korolev is a city in the Moscow region. The first information about it dates back to the twelfth century. Here are important rocket and space enterprises, there is a unique museum, as well as other interesting sights. If you want to visit the city and discover a lot of new things, then you can stay in hostels. This will be the most inexpensive and convenient option for living! There are plenty of them in the Queen. Information on the most worthy of them is presented below.

"Good cat"

This hostel (Korolev) takes a significant place among the various options for living. His very name - "Good Cat" - raises the mood and causes a smile on his face. Another of the many advantages of the institution is its location - in the very center of the city. For visitors it is very convenient. After all, having walked in the best places of the Queen and getting acquainted with its sights, you can rent a house there.

The rooms at the Kind Cat Hostel can accommodate from two to eight people. It has convenient parking, luggage storage, as well as a swimming pool. At your service are numbers from several options:

  • Single. There is a bed, a bathroom, as well as all the necessary hygiene items.
  • Double. It features a washing machine, a TV with a large selection of entertainment programs.
  • Triple The room has a comfortable wide bed for two, as well as a bed for another person.

Address: Ordzhonikidze street, 5.

Forest Inn

We continue to review hostels in Korolev. Forest Inn is a small but very cozy place. The hotel occupies two floors of the administrative center. It offers customers about thirty comfortable rooms. They have sleeping places, a TV, a wardrobe, a table and much more.

Address: Cosmonauts Avenue, 17 A.

Holiday Village "Sputnik"

This hostel in Korolev is homely. "Sputnik" - always quick placement and comfortable living. Cozy rooms with all the necessary household items will make your stay especially enjoyable and carefree. Additional amenities include: free Wi-Fi, parking area, ironing facilities.

Hostel address in Korolev: Pervomaisky microdistrict, Krasnooktyabrskaya street, 40.

"Amur"

If you come on vacation or on a business trip with your pet, you can be sure that you can definitely be accommodated in this institution. Non-smoking rooms are also available. The hotel has free Wi-Fi, there is a convenient parking zone. Among the additional services: left-luggage offices, washing machines, refrigerators.

The institution is located in the Yubileiny microdistrict, Komitetskaya street, 13.
