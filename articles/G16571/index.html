What threads and tools are suitable for a summer beret

To tie a good summer crochet for a girl, initially it is worth choosing the right thread. Since the item is intended for use in the warm season, the material must meet the needs.

The easiest crochet pattern for a summer beret

To create a beret for a girl of 1.5-2 years old, you will need 100 grams of yarn. For such a model, 100% cotton would be an ideal option. To make the pattern smooth and accurate, you should choose a hook number 1.5.

How to crochet a girl for a girl? A description of the sequence of actions is presented below. The result is a product with simple motifs. The work consists of the following steps:

  1. Dial a chain of 4 air loops (VP) and close in a circle.
  2. The 2nd row is knitted with crochets (CCH), making 2 stitches in one.
  3. 3rd row knit 2 CCH in one loop of the previous row.
  4. The entire 4th row is knitted as follows: 1 magnificent column (PS) with 3 air loops alternately.
  5. Next, 9 wedges are formed: 2 PS in the arch of 3 VP, 2 VP are knitted between the PS; 2 CCHs are knitted in the next arch. This alternation is carried out to the end of the row.
  6. To increase the number of CCH, you need to knit 2 PS in the PS, and knit the arch of 2 VP into the arch of 2 VP.
  7. In all subsequent rows, the number of CCHs increases by 2 loops in each of the wedges. So 8 rows are knitted, until in each of the wedges 18 SSN are obtained.
  8. The next three rows knit without adding to the wedges.
  9. From the 17th row, the decrease begins. It is necessary to knit together 2 CCH, which are located near the PS.
  10. 2 other rows knit without reduction.
  11. Until the 28th row, there is an alternation of decreases and ordinary knitting, as described above.
  12. From the 29th to the 36th rows, you need to knit in single crochet columns (RLS).
