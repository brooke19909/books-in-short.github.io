Holiday Village "Vesyegonsky Island": description, overview

Everyone likes to relax, but not many really do it fully and efficiently. If you want to spend time with your loved ones away from the hustle and bustle of the city, breathe in clean, fresh air, head to the recreation center "Vesyegonsky Island". As part of this material, we will try to evaluate the option from all sides and determine what kind of vacation it offers to modern visitors.





About the recreation center

All residents of the Tver region were incredibly lucky, because they have the opportunity to regularly visit the recreation center "Vesyegonsky Island". The reviews of those who spend their weekends here, celebrate holidays and important events, once again confirm the uniqueness of this place.

Location

For the recreation center "Vesyegonsky Island" the Rybinsk Reservoir was not chosen by chance. This is a picturesque corner with stunning views. Right at the confluence of the Mologa and Kesma rivers, there is a surprisingly quiet and peaceful place where time literally freezes and silence is broken only by the singing of birds.





It is impossible to convey the emotions of those who first visit the Rybinsk reservoir. Coming out of a motor boat and taking the first step along the island, you feel that you are in a different, other world: it is so quiet, cozy and atmospheric here. The place is amazing, mysterious and enchanting, I want to come back here again and again.

Features of rest

What is the recreation center "Vesyegonsky island"? Photos clearly demonstrate its basic concept. This is outdoor recreation for the benefit of the body. A true paradise for those who want to be alone with themselves, to relax from the busy city, endless problems and life's troubles. At the same time, here everyone will find for themselves entertainment to their liking, as much as they wish.

Entertainment

The recreation center "Vesyegonsky Island" offers an excellent program for organizing leisure activities: fishing, motor boat trips along the reservoir, in winter - extreme snowmobile rides and much more. There are mushroom and berry places, so all lovers of forest gifts can get tremendous pleasure from the process, breathe fresh air.





Sports equipment and equipment rental is open on the territory of the recreation center: a set for badminton, volleyball, bicycles and much more. Be sure to take a steam bath in a real Russian bathhouse.

Fishing should be given special attention. If you are an avid fisherman, this place will definitely be remembered and you will like it. The catch here is always good, and the opportunity to spend time alone with nature, to be in silence, to enjoy the light breeze and unforgettable sunrises is truly priceless. On the territory of the recreation center there are gazebos, places for picnics, barbecue grills.

Accommodations

All guests of the recreation center "Vesyegonsky Island" are invited to rent detached houses for 2-4 residents. The house can be rented both for a day and for a longer period. A separate entrance to each room provides comfort to all its residents. The toilet and washbasin are on the street, which should be prepared in advance. The rooms have a bedroom and kitchen with a set of necessary equipment and utensils. In addition, a veranda is allocated for each room, which will become an excellent place for friendly gatherings and feasts.





Count on comfort? Pay attention to enclosures with amenities. Toilet, shower, kitchen with household appliances and electronics, a set of dishes, several bedrooms, a common living room - this is what you can count on. Such houses are popular among large companies, as they can accommodate 6-8 guests at a time. Book a house with amenities in advance, especially if you plan to celebrate an important event here.

Nutrition

The recreation center "Vesyegonsky Island" has all the conditions for a comfortable stay. Each room has everything you need for food storage and cooking. Nevertheless, if you wish, you can eat in a restaurant operating in the base. The menu is varied, represented by dishes of traditional European cuisine.

Price of rest

So, the daily stay in a double room is 700 rubles per person, subject to placement in an economy house with amenities on the street. The cost of living in a room with amenities starts from 100 rubles per person per day. Checkout time, operating on the territory of the recreation center - 12:00.

Boat rental costs 250 rubles per day, boats with a motor - 400 rubles. You can rent a set for badminton, table tennis, volleyball for only 50 rubles per day. In addition, if you are passionate about fishing, you don‚Äôt have to bring all the gear with you, because for only 200 rubles a day you can rent a spinning reel and a set of necessary gear.

Reviews

If you read the reviews of those who at least once spent their vacation here, you will want to immediately collect the bags and go on the road. An excellent combination of quality of rest and affordable cost is what vacationers attribute to the main advantages of a recreation center. It is unlikely to satisfy the requirements of discerning people who prefer fashionable resorts, because in most buildings there is only a minimal set of conditions necessary for living: furniture, a refrigerator, a set of dishes. In economy buildings there is no toilet and shower - all amenities are on the street. At the same time, it is difficult to exaggerate the importance of the right place: the air here is amazing, the views are picturesque. Many of those who spent even a week here noted improvement in well-being, normalization of sleep.

To summarize

The recreation center "Vesyegonsky island" in the Tver region is a great option for a weekend or vacation. Here you can spend time with family and friends, celebrate important and solemn events. Be sure to visit this amazing place at least once to personally enjoy all the benefits of a country vacation.
