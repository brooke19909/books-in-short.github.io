Royal Paradise Resort 4 *, Sharm El Sheikh, Egypt: hotel description, reviews

Due to its convenient location, tropical climate, multifaceted underwater world, resorts of Egypt on the coast of the Sinai Peninsula are included in the list of the most popular tourist places. All conditions have been created for holidaymakers and many comfortable hotel complexes have been built, including Royal Paradise Resort 4 *, Sharm El Sheikh (photos can be seen in almost all tourist catalogs), pleases tourists.

General information

Royal Paradise Resort 4 * (Sharm El Sheikh) is an economical hotel with white buildings that look nice against the green vegetation of the picturesque Ras Om Elsid Bay, 20 kilometers from the airport. The hallmarks of the hotel are its vast territory (3200 square meters), magnificent sea and stunning coral reef.





On the square occupied by the hotel complex are the main building, several two-story villas, a huge pool with two compartments (it does not heat up in winter), restaurants and bars.

Hotel Royal Paradise Resort 4 * (Sharm El Sheikh) was built in 1999 on the first line from the sea and has its own beach, which is about 150 meters long. On the sandy shore, vacationers are invited to use free sunbeds with mattresses and sun umbrellas. Very close is a wonderful coral reef, where you can disappear for hours while watching the underwater kingdom.

Housing stock

Royal Paradise Resort is ready to offer its customers rooms of various sizes (from 32 to 64 square meters) and comfort. The interior decoration is quite simple but elegant. In addition, for a comfortable stay in the room there is:

  • television (reception of Russian-language channels);
  • air conditioning with individual control panel;
  • mini-bar (for a fee);
  • telephone line;
  • private bathroom with hygiene items and a hairdryer;
  • spacious terrace or balcony;
  • safe.




Cleaning is done once a day, bedding is changed 3 times a week. Possible round-the-clock individual room service, which is provided by a paid hotel room service.

Food on vacation

Royal Paradise Resort 4 * (Sharm El Sheikh) has restaurants and bars operating according to the All Inclusive formula. Guests are offered a buffet in the main restaurant (air-conditioned indoor area and summer terrace), light snacks and drinks in the bars.





Two restaurants serve guests on a la carte basis. In one of them they serve fish dishes and seafood, in the other - cuisine of sunny Italy. Meals in these establishments are not included in All Inclusive.

Sporting events and other pleasures

Royal Paradise Resort 4 * (Sharm El Sheikh) provides everyone with the opportunity to diversify their leisure time by taking part in active events:

  • table tennis tournaments;
  • aerobics on land and in water;
  • Beach volleyball;
  • exercises in the gym;
  • darts;
  • billiards.




Diving enthusiasts are given the opportunity to rent equipment and to improve their immersion skills on their own or with the help of an experienced instructor.

With the onset of darkness, evening entertainment shows and a real night club & Disco disco await vacationers, at which omnipotent DJs put on hit songs of the nineties along with modern compositions.

Another advantage of the hotel complex is its proximity to one of the most famous places in Egypt - the entertainment center "1000 and 1 night" and numerous Arabian shops.

Positive reviews

When choosing a budget hotel on the sea coast, most tourists love to get acquainted not with the brochures of tour operators, but with the comments of real people who have already visited the resort. Preferring the Royal Paradise Resort 4 * (Sharm El Sheikh), reviews of which can be read on the Internet, future hotel guests are guided primarily by a favorable location.

Indeed, all tourists who have been here give the highest rating to its location and beautiful green territory. They write that depending on the hull to the sea, it takes three to seven minutes to go. The beach is worthy of praise, many free umbrellas and sun loungers. For bathing, it is advisable to purchase special slippers.

But the main thing here is the unusually beautiful sea with a lively coral reef, where you can snorkel for hours, watching the diversity of the underwater world.

Negative reviews

Do not expect anything special from a budget hotel. Everything is quite modest: good animation, monotonous food in the main restaurant, at times tasteless drinks, shabby furniture in the rooms. The cleanliness and freshness of the premises also have complaints from guests.

Many vacationers are indignant that a hotel with such a beautiful territory and ideal location is not given due attention, no one is engaged in its reconstruction. And the beautiful hotel complex is slowly acquiring a deplorable state.

Buying a tour package Royal Paradise Resort 4 * (Sharm El Sheikh) at an attractive price, you need to be prepared for possible domestic turmoil and not to find fault with trifles. Only in this case of exceptional beauty the sea and the magnificent coral reef will bring tourists real pleasure.
