The Yellow Sea in China. Yellow Sea on the map

The Chinese call the Yellow Sea Huanghai. It belongs to the basin of the world's largest ocean - the Pacific. This sea, bearing such a strange name, is located on the eastern coast of the Eurasian continent, washed by the western coast of the Korean Peninsula.



World Map Location

So, the Yellow Sea is located in the northeastern hemisphere off the coast of mainland Eurasia. From the south, it borders the East China Sea. Only from this side the sea is not limited by land. On the other hand, it washes the shores of three peninsulas: Korean, Liaodong and Shandong, i.e., the coast of three countries: China and two Koreas. Why is the Yellow Sea?

So why is this natural reservoir called in this way? Its waters have a strange greenish-yellow color. The reason for this is the sediment brought by the Chinese rivers (Yellow River, Haihe, Luanhe, Liaohe, Yalujiang), which flow into the Yellow Sea. China is rich in water resources, and there are a great many similar rivers. In some of them, the water has not just yellow, but a buffy hue. However, merging with the water of the sea, they acquire a golden green color, especially in sunny weather. One of the largest is the Yellow River, the river that gave the name to the Yellow Sea. Another reason for yellowness is spring powerful dust storms, which subsequently settle on the water and paint it in an atypical shade for the sea.



A little about the Yellow River

The Yellow River is one of the largest waterways on the planet. She got this name due to the huge number of suspended particles that give the water a yellowish-ocher hue. The Yellow River originates in the Tibetan Plateau, at an altitude of 4 and a half thousand meters. Its channel is winding, it constantly winds in its path, constantly changing direction. At the end of its journey, the Yellow River flows into the sea.

Climate

The Huanghai Sea coast is characterized by rather cold winters, when the water temperature drops to zero, and hot summers (water temperature - up to + 27-28 degrees). This is facilitated by the fact that the Yellow Sea on the map is located in the temperate zone. In winter, ice floes may form on the pond. And in the summer, despite the warmth of air and water, the sea can not be called affectionate and pleasant for a long vacation. From time to time there are dust storms, heavy rains and a typhoon.



Water movement and currents

The temperature of sea waters, as well as their movement are influenced by the warm current from the East China Sea and the cold from the north-west. Therefore, the water temperature can constantly change. The flow on the surface is directed counterclockwise and forms a cycle. Depending on the coast, the magnitude of the tides changes, and if in the west they do not exceed the level of 1 meter, then in the southeast, especially in narrow bays, they can reach up to 9 meters.

Flora

In its flora, the Yellow Sea is similar to the Japanese. In the waters and on the coast you can see thickets of red and brown algae, as well as kelp. Coastal vegetation is widely used for industrial purposes.



Fauna

It follows from the foregoing that the flora of the Huanghai Sea is rather scarce, which cannot be said about the animal world, that is, about marine fauna. It is much richer and more diverse and has a huge number of species of marine animals and microorganisms.
