Reviews about hotels in Egypt: what to expect from a holiday in hotels of this country

For several centuries, European travelers discovered Egypt. And still they do not cease to be amazed at its mysterious deserts and mountains, the beauty and scope of temples, the luxury of civilization, the theological depths. And what is the sea here - is it possible somewhere else to meet such colorful fish, similar to tropical flowers, flickering among corals? In order for tourists to enjoy all this in full, there are chic resorts on the Red Sea, where hotels are built for holidays in Egypt. What tourists say about them, you will find out if you read the information below.

Hotels of the Red Sea

The popularity of Egypt as a resort is due to many factors. Not the last place is occupied by the fact that it is in this country, like in no other, that you can combine rest on the beach and acquaintance with amazing historical monuments. Reviews of hotels in Egypt are very diverse and often ambivalent, but one can draw such a conclusion. Here, a rich man and a man with a very modest budget can easily find a place to have a great time. And how many various excursions do tourists offer in Egyptian hotels? And a desert safari in jeeps or ATVs, and a sunset meeting in the mountains with dinner and dancing by the Bedouins, and a boat trip on the Nile, and a trip to Luxor, Cairo or Alexandria. But experienced people are advised not to succumb to the entreaties of the hotel guide. All organized trips are best bought at travel agencies on the street. You will be provided with the same services, but much cheaper. In addition, from the hotels of Hurghada it is easiest to get to the famous pyramids and temples of ancient Egypt. And Sharm El Sheikh will provide you with a great opportunity to visit Israel and Jordan inexpensively.
