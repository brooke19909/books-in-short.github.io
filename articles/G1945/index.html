How to calculate the power consumption of an electrical appliance?

Let's talk about how to calculate power consumption. To carry out the calculations, elementary knowledge from the school course of electrodynamics, related to voltage, current, power, will be required.

To calculate the power consumption by current, you need to know its strength, as well as the magnitude of the voltage source. Power (P) is calculated by multiplying the current by an electric voltage.

Rules for calculating power consumption
