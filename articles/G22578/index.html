Elephone G7 Phone: Owner Reviews

Chinese mobile devices have managed to fill the electronics market around the world. Already even in the USA, one can find many cheap and, admittedly, fairly high-quality phones and tablets presented by Chinese brands, not to mention less wealthy countries. Yes, many of these phones are made in a case that copies the design of other models released by Samsung, HTC, Sony or Apple. Despite this, the quality of the devices can be called good, despite the fact that their cost is really much lower.

Today's material will be devoted to one of these phones - Elephone G7. Customer reviews, as well as the official characteristics of the device will be given in this article in order to learn more about it.

Elephone Company

We will begin our small review, perhaps, with the general characteristics of the manufacturer, which introduced this model. Most of us probably have not even heard about a brand like Elephone. Although if you take a little time searching for information on the Internet, you can find a lot of sites that respond to these devices in Chinese. On this basis, we can conclude that in their homeland, these gadgets are quite famous.





Guesses are confirmed when we go to the page of some major online auction from the Middle Kingdom, for example, on Aliexpress. Here you can really find offers from several hundred suppliers from which you can buy one of the Elephone models. What is noteworthy is the really close similarity between the appearance of the phone and some of the flagship models of previous generations from Samsung and other "top" companies. For example, the device to which this review is devoted is similar to the Galaxy Note 4. Obviously, the company's designers did not bother too much about developing something original.

Smartphone Elephone G7

The model we are talking about today is a typical budget Chinese device, made in the style copied from another flagship device. Like many similar devices, G7 has a fairly serious declared technical equipment, which, however, is not so noticeable in the direct operation of the device. The phone is presented, like many of its "colleagues", smartphones-copies from China - on the Android operating system. To ensure multifunctionality, the model is equipped with support for two SIM cards.





Housing

We will start our description with the main one - the external shell of the phone, its body. As many customers testify about the Elephone G7, the device is made in an elegant style, which is provided by a thin case consisting of metal and plastic inserts. Due to this, the phone looks quite stylish, and sits securely in the hand. Although this elegance effect collapses very quickly after the device falls into the hands, very noticeable fingerprints still remain on its plastic back panel. On the Elephone G7 model, owner reviews indicate that having bought such a smartphone, you will need to take care either to cover it with a protective film, or to be able to erase ugly marks with a napkin.

Although, as for the film, it is in the base kit and glues to the screen of the Elephone G7. Reviews report that its quality is such that the first thing most buyers are forced to get rid of it.

The metal plate is used around the circumference of the phone - it will reliably protect against vertical drops.

For more practical use, the Elephone G7 black (user reviews confirm this) has a protective glass. Therefore, we can say that the phone display justifies the cost of the entire device.

Reviews

You can find a ton of information about how the Elephone G7 behaves in practice immediately after purchase. Reviews about the phone are mostly positive - users praise the price of the device, its appearance, powerful processor, camera and overall performance. They also mention the battery, colorful display, the ability to expand memory through microSD.

True, not without negative characteristics. Many buyers complain about the poor quality of the "native" charger. As the phone is put on recharge, its screen begins to carry out some independent actions - these or other areas of the display are pressed in random order, applications are opened, and text is printed. This causes serious inconvenience and eliminates the possibility of working with the phone while it is in a state connected to the network. The solution to the problem is elementary - you need to replace the cord and adapter for charging with any other, universal kit. However, the mere fact of having such a problem does not please.

The second point is the assembly. On some models, the display glass is not tightly glued to the case - so that there is a small gap between it and the phone itself. Since this can cause a fatal ingress of moisture or dust, for such a flaw obviously a serious minus should be set.

The third thing buyers of the model most often complained about is the sound. Neither a standard headset nor an external speaker can provide high-quality sound transmission. This fact must simply be accepted as is.





Cost

The price of the phone varies depending on where to make its purchase. For some reason, on the vaunted Chinese sites, where, in theory, the phone should be cheaper, it costs all 10-12 thousand rubles (depending on which seller to buy). The advantage of working with Chinese stores is, firstly, free delivery by mail, and secondly, the ability to order accessories for the phone at a lower price from the same seller. True, the main minus of delivery from such stores is the term. About 3-4 weeks will have to wait until the G7 is in your hands.

In Moscow, the device can be found cheaper - for 8,200 rubles. True, the largest retail chains of the Elephone G7 smartphone (reviews also emphasize this) do not offer - you can order them in online electronics stores. Delivery in this case is carried out by courier or mail. In rare cases, such outlets have offices in which you can pick up the phone in person.
