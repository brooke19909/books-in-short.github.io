Bedspreads on the bed with your own hands: exclusive and comfort in the house

With the words "cozy bedroom", soft light, beautiful curtains, a fluffy carpet on the floor, and a comfortable sleeping place come to mind first of all. The bare walls and the bedspread on the double bed, preserved from Soviet times, cause antipathy. Nevertheless, adding coziness is not at all difficult. It is only necessary to show imagination and give occupation to the hands for a while.

Central to any bedroom is the bed. She is the first to be seen when you enter the room. Good enough bedspreads to make the room look cozy and stylish, or a crumpled bed, so that the sensation of a mess does not leave you, even if everything is cleaned around. Here's how much a piece of cloth tossed onto a bed means.



Unfortunately, it can be difficult to choose a blanket of non-standard sizes or just an original and stylish thing that would fit the bedroom interior. You can go around all the shops in the city, review a bunch of catalogs and still not find a suitable option. Nevertheless, there is a solution. If you can‚Äôt find anything suitable, you can sew the bedspreads on the bed with your own hands. Patchwork, knitted, with applique, with or without frills, from any fabric you like, yarn and other materials. Fantasy is not limited when we get to work ourselves. And do not think that it is too difficult. Believe me, anyone who can even handle a sewing machine or hold a hook or knitting needle in their hands can sew or knit bedspreads on a bed with their own hands.

What fabric should I use for sewing? Tight weave. It will be quite simple to work with it, moreover, the dense fabric will hide the bed more reliably, and bed linen will not be visible from under it. In order to sew bedspreads on the bed with your own hands, you need to take into account some points.



If the bed has one or two headboards, you have to think about ruffles. You can make one solid lush (measure out more material so that it is enough for folds) or assemble it from separate sections so that the cover fits the corners beautifully. Decide on the length. You can make it to the floor or leave the legs open. When cutting, pay attention to the direction of the pattern and the outflow of fabric. And don't forget about seam allowances. The edge can be trimmed with edging from scraps or specially selected contrast material.

Original look bedspreads on the bed with your own hands, made using the patchwork technique, that is, patchwork. Usually used multi-colored pieces of fabric in the form of identical squares or triangles. They are stitched together until the desired size is reached. Such products will suit the bedroom in a rustic style or for the nursery. Individual fragments can be linked and then joined together.



If you want luxury and wealth in the decoration of the room, choose a fur cover on the bed, but it will not add originality to the bedroom if it is chosen from the same type of store products. It is quite another matter if you sew it yourself using artificial material of different colors or textures.

As we have seen, it is not so difficult to decorate your bedroom with an exclusive thing. A little zeal, and everything will work out!
