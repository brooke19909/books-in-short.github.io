We give a definition

According to the definition of explanatory dictionaries, a multimillionaire is the owner of multimillion-dollar capital. In simple terms, one can be called a person whose condition is in the gap between a billion and a million. That is, this is not a millionaire, but not a billionaire yet.

Several million ... why?

According to public opinion, a huge fortune should imply wealth. A multimillionaire is a person whose fortune is estimated at several million monetary units of hard currency, for example, US dollars or euros. Also, the condition can be stored in Swiss francs or pounds sterling.





It is known that the assets of wealthy people are not always money. A multimillionaire is a person who has property in the form of metal accounts, securities, real estate and other assets, which in monetary terms are valued at several million dollars or other monetary units of hard currency.

Statistics data

According to experts, more than a third of multimillionaires in the world live in the United States. Of these, 4/5 reached their status on their own, the rest was inherited from a wealthy relative. Some also gained wealth at one point by winning the lottery, show, or sports.

In terms of the number of multimillionaires, Russia is ahead of most developed countries, second only to the United States and Germany. There you go!
