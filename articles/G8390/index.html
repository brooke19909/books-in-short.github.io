Types of elastic with knitting needles, schemes. Knitting English and hollow gum

How to process the edge of a knitted fabric? The most common option is gum. Depending on the choice of the thickness of the thread and the combination of loops, it can be completely different. Let's look at the types of elastic with knitting needles. The schemes presented in this article will help you easily master the basic techniques for creating simple patterns. Be patient and get started.





The main types of elastic knitting needles

The canvas is corrugated due to a certain alternation of loops. Let's consider how the main types of elastic bands are carried out and what are the types ( it is not difficult to knit them with knitting needles ).

  1. Simple. The front and back loops are knitted alternately , on the reverse side - according to the figure.
  2. Grouped. The principle of operation is similar to the previous one. But the main difference is that the same type of loop alternates between each other, for example, two front loops and two wrong ones.
  3. Transverse. Scars on the canvas are not located in the vertical direction, but in the horizontal. There is an alternation not of various loops, but of rows.
  4. English The technology is improved through the use of yarn in the ornament.
  5. Volumetric. Its peculiarity is in the distinctive features of the execution of the canvas from the front and the wrong side.
  6. Hollow. It is used to trim the edges of the product and the neck. It can be two-color on both sides of the fabric due to the features of the knitting.




How does a simple elastic band fit?

Very often in this way almost any knitting begins or ends . Types of gum can alternate with each other. Usually simple goes into English and vice versa (photo 1). With a fairly dense knitting, it may seem that the fabric consists only of facial loops. But in fact, it is worth stretching it a little to the sides, and the wrong loops will appear. We will analyze in more detail how to perform the simplest gum, guided by the scheme (photo 2). Type an odd number of loops on the knitting needles, taking into account the total number of edge loops. Start the row with the wrong loop and end with it. Edge knit only at the end of the row, and in the beginning just take off the knitting needle. Alternate front loops with wrong ones. The reverse side fits in the picture. Other types of gum, knitting according to the description, will allow you to get slightly different in appearance ornaments. Check out some of them, starting with the simplest.





Cross "corrugation"

Knitting gum in this way gives a slightly different result. Cross elastic, as a rule, is not used to design, for example, the bottom of the sleeves of blouses or jumpers. Most often, scarves, hats or skirts are knitted with such an ornament. Perform it even easier than regular gum. To do this, knit an even number of rows with the front surface - two or four. Then change the pattern to the wrong one. Similarly, knit the same number of rows, getting an equal width of a piece of canvas. English gum

The second distinctive feature of the ornament is the same appearance both on the front side of the product and on the wrong side. How is knitting English gum knitting? There are some options for performing English gum, in which the work is somewhat simplified. For example, you can get the canvas on the front side is the same, but from the inside it will already be another knitting, without crochets. Other loops fit in the pattern. Try to make the fabric using a technology similar to knitting a simple elastic band (photo 3). To do this, dial the number of loops multiple of two (excluding the edge). How to process the edge

If you are performing a product according to a finished pattern and in addition you have photos of models, you can not think about the problem of design at the end of knitting. With improvisation, things are completely different. Therefore, you need to know some secrets. Let's talk about the main one - a combination of different bindings with each other. When moving from one pattern, the density can change significantly. For example, a canvas with a front smooth surface, ‚Äútransformed‚Äù into a dense elastic band with loops removed, tapers off by almost a quarter in width. There may be a reverse situation - to obtain a sample with multiple braids and weaves, you need a larger number of loops than with ordinary denticles. Knitting English gum with knitting needles, as a rule, expands the fabric. This means that to create a beautiful transition to the gum, you must definitely complete the sample. One of the commonly used edge design options is a hollow (double) elastic band.

How does a hollow elastic band fit with knitting needles (photo 6)?

What is the main feature of the work? If you look at it, it actually turns out not a completely bound gum, but two canvases with a common base and free sides from the inside of the work. Dial the number of loops, a multiple of two, and perform the first row of the type of regular gum. The second and subsequent fit as follows: knit the front, and remove the wrong side by stretching the thread in front of it. Having reached the end, beautifully shape the edge. Keep in mind that the hem at the beginning of the row does not knit. Thanks to this, the edge is neat. In fact, two canvases are knitted at the same time. Using this property, you can design, for example, the bar on the blouse is very original. Knit on the front side with yarn of one color, and on the inside of the other. Then the hem will be hollow not only inside, but also from the edges. A drawstring can be pulled into this makeshift belt. Often the throats and edges of hoods are shaped like this, especially on children's products.





Features of the work in a circle

Double knitting with elastic needles will look different if, for example, the neck is trimmed. In this case, it is important not to confuse the procedure. To do this, mark with a pin or contrasting thread the joints of the rows. When knitting the first circle, make all the loops face, and the rest, alternating, take off the knitting needle. Having reached the mark, go to the second wall of the gum and do the wrong side. When loops are removed, always let the thread inside the work. Having knitted the desired distance (usually 1.5-2 cm), close all the loops in turn - the front, the wrong side, etc. Using the properties of a hollow gum, you can not only make out the edge of the product, but also specifically make strips for processing pockets and other details. For example, try to knit a narrow (4-5 cm), but long (10-12 cm) fabric using this technology. You will get a great phone case. As you can see, ordinary ornaments can be used in completely different ways, getting practical and original things!
