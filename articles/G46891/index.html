How to untie a number from a Sberbank card: all methods

The Mobile Bank service allows customers to make transactions with cards online and be aware of the movement of funds in accounts. SMS-informing is a paid service: depending on the type of card, a user is charged 30 or 60 rubles per month. If a customer has changed a SIM card or wants to refuse a Mobile Bank, he must know how to untie the number from the Sberbank card.

Methods for deactivating SMS informing service

"Mobile Bank" is available to disable in the following ways:

  1. When you visit the bank office.
  2. When calling the Sberbank Contact Center.

Any of the options involves cancellation of information from the number "900". If the user of the bank card decides to use the service again, he can reconnect the service at the office or terminal of Sberbank.

Mobile Bank Cancellation Terms

Customers who already know how to untie a phone number from a Sberbank card must be aware of the conditions for canceling the service. As with connecting SMS-informing, when the service is deactivated, the bank does not charge a commission. The exception is cases when the monthly fee was not debited from the client during the current period.

For example, the cardholder used the newsletter for 12 days and decided to cancel the service. In this case, when disconnecting, a fixed amount will be debited from the client, according to the bank's tariff.

Visit to the bank office

A proven and affordable way to untie a number from a Sberbank card is to report to a branch of a financial institution. If the owner uses several plastic products, it is required to inform the manager of the type and last 4 digits of the credit card.

When you visit the additional office of Sberbank, the service is disabled within 24 hours. A copy of the application for cancellation of SMS informing is issued to the client as confirmation. Suspension of service or change of tariff plan

An alternative option, how to untie the number from the Sberbank card, is to block the newsletter using the USSD command or switch to the "Economy" tariff.
