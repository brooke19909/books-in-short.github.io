Autocentre "Olympic": reviews, services

Buying a car is an event that needs to be taken seriously, having spent some time preparing. It is not limited to choosing the most suitable car. Equally important is the method and timing of payment. Regarding where the purchase is planned, it is important first of all to collect information about the car dealership, its reputation. Since the process of acquiring a car is not a matter of minutes, comfortable conditions during registration will not be superfluous.

Next, we will focus on the auto center "Olympic", reviews and other information about it.





About salon

Which car would not be planned to purchase - new or with mileage, it makes sense to look towards this car dealership. After looking at reviews about the Olympic auto center, you can make sure that this seller has a lot to choose from. And the fact that at the same time more than 10 budget cars of different brands are put up for sale shows that there is a choice. Car dealership is an authorized dealer. Recently, the salon has been renamed and is now called Auto Premium.

All related services are also present. Including this:

  • Credit. Application for a loan directly in the cabin. You can fill out an application form and send it to the bank or leave an application by phone.
  • Insurance. Collaboration with leading insurance companies avoids additional problems.
  • Service "trade-in". Car dealership can evaluate the old car and change it to a new one. In this case, only the difference must be paid.

Services

  • Consultation. The car dealership staff will advise on the advantages and disadvantages of certain cars, help you compare different models and tell you about the features of the assembly at manufacturers.
  • Delivery. Different options are possible. Everyone can choose the most suitable option based on cost and urgency. Urgent transportation is carried out through airlines. Delivery is possible to any place within the Russian Federation and the CIS.




  • Decor. During customs clearance, company employees provide assistance (for example, upon receipt of TCP and other documents).
  • Lending and leasing. A wide range of conditions and rates. To clarify the details, you must contact the company employees.
  • Auto insurance. Available types such as CTP, DSAGO, CASCO (partial / full). Corporate rates are provided.
  • Technical service. As part of the car dealership, there is a service center that provides services both for installing additional equipment and tuning work. Specialists carry out all types of repair work and maintenance.

Cars available for order can be found in the catalog on the car dealership website. It is necessary to select the car brand, model, and equipment in the search parameters. To clarify the information, you can contact the specialists of the car dealership.

Car loans

The fact that the car has long been not a luxury, but a real necessity, no one doubts. But sometimes its cost is significantly high, and it is not always possible to buy it right away. If you do not need a car urgently, and there is time for the accumulation of the necessary amount, then, of course, it‚Äôs worth it. But what if the car is needed now, but there is not enough time?

In this situation, buying a used car on credit will be a compromise. This will allow you to use all the advantages of a personal vehicle, and pay for it in stages, in amounts much less than the total.





A frequent reason for the need to purchase a car in installments is damage resulting from breakdowns or accidents. It is impossible to predict such situations, but a way out of them is needed. Quite a good opportunity to take a car on credit without a down payment. This gives some time to improve their financial situation.

If there are no such troubles, and there are enough funds, you can take a regular loan with a down payment. Moscow dealerships offer an extensive range of loan conditions, you just need to study the options and choose the most suitable.
