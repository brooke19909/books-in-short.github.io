Rubi Hotel 5 * (Avsallar, Turkey): hotel description, photos and reviews of tourists

Just 20 km from one of the most popular resorts in Turkey, Alanya, is a small cozy southern village of Avsallar. You can go from one end of this settlement to the other in just a few minutes. The tourist infrastructure in this village, however, is simply superbly developed. There are many comfortable hotels in Avsallar, of course. For example, the hotel Rubi Hotel 5 * just deserved excellent reviews from tourists at this resort.

Location Features

This comfortable five-star hotel is located in the village of Avsallar. Alanya can be reached from this hotel in just 30-40 minutes by public transport. The Rubi Hotel 5 * was built on the first coastline and from the windows of many of its rooms you can see the sea. Guests of this complex can literally get to the beach in 4-5 minutes.





The nearest airport is Alanya Gazipas Airport, approximately 60 km from it. Antalya's transport air hub is 100 km from this complex. In any case, tourists arriving from Russia will have to, unfortunately, take quite a long time to get to the hotel. Moreover, on the way to this complex, tour operator buses usually call in several more hotels.

Rubi Hotel 5 * (Avsallar): description

This hotel was built already a long time ago - in 1992. The last reconstruction here was carried out in 2001. That is, the hotel is relatively old. But minor cosmetic repairs here from time to time, of course, in past years were still carried out. Therefore, his bodies do not look too shabby. This is also evidenced by the reviews of tourists existing at specialized sites on the Internet. And the furniture in the rooms here is not new, but quite comfortable and not loose.

The Rubi complex consists of two five-story buildings, one of which is located on the first coastline (A), the second - across the road from the beach (B). The area of ​​this hotel is relatively large. The courtyards and buildings of the Rubi Hotel 5 * in Avsallar (and the photo above shows it) look quite neat and beautiful. This fact is noted on the Internet by many travelers who have ever rested here. Some tourists even note the fact that upon arrival it seemed to them that they were in paradise. The hotel grounds are well landscaped, and all paths and paths here are decorated with paving tiles in a very pleasant color. The total area occupied by this complex is 8000 m 2 . This, of course, is quite a lot.

Number of rooms

If necessary, Avsallar guests can rent standard rooms, two-room or family suites at the Rubi Hotel 5 * hotel. The area rented by the hotel apartments are quite large - 22-37 m 2 . In each room there are:

  • TV and telephone;
  • mini fridge and safe;
  • air conditioning.




The minibar in the hotel is free. Residents need to pay a certain amount for using the safe. Air conditioning at this hotel is centralized. Tourists can watch on TV, including one Russian-language channel. In the B block, the floors in the hotel are carpeted, in A block - with ceramic tiles. All rooms at the complex include balconies.

Beach

Rubi Hotel residents can walk to the sea in just a few minutes. Building B in this hotel, as already mentioned, is separated from the beach by a highway (D400). But the residents of the complex do not need to overcome this rather busy road with a danger to life. The fact is that buildings A and B in this hotel are connected by a tunnel. The tenants of Block B need to go to the sea, of course, a little longer than A. But in this case, too, the guests of the hotel will not be particularly tiring to get to the beach.

The sea itself near the Rubi Hotel 5 * hotel, if judged by the reviews of guests, is never dirty. The same goes for the local beach.
