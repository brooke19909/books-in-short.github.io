SMS (MTS) do not come to the phone: reasons, solution to the problem and recommendations

Recently, you can find many complaints of subscribers that they do not receive SMS (MTS) on the phone. What to do in such a situation? Why can these events occur? Are there reasons for panic if you suddenly have messages stopped coming to your mobile device? About all this further!



Overload

Mobile communication is a mystery that makes many people panic at the slightest glitches. Why do not you receive SMS (MTS) on the phone? The reasons for this may be a huge amount. And the first one is network congestion.

Usually this phenomenon is observed during the holidays. Overloading the operator‚Äôs network does not allow calls and does not allow sending / receiving messages. Crash

It is easy to guess that this is just the beginning. Any network is not immune from a variety of failures and malfunctions. During such periods, problems with calls and SMS messages from operators are usually observed. Most often, no one can predict when the next failure will occur.

In other words, if you do not receive SMS (MTS) on your phone, especially when it comes to incoming letters from different operators, you can only complain about this reason. Just wait a while. When the problem is fixed, everything will return to normal.

Take note: network outages are most often observed in adverse weather conditions. For example, in heat or severe frosts. Try not to panic if you notice a problem with the telephone connection of the MTS operator. This company is considered one of the most stable. Therefore, her problems do not happen too often. And if this happens, then they try to fix all the problems as soon as possible.



Crash

I wonder why SMS do not come to the MTS phone ? The cause of this phenomenon may be a banal accident that occurred at the station. Also a fairly common reason, which is impossible to predict.

To find out exactly what happened, call the MTS office or personally go to the nearest mobile phone salon of this operator. Ask why you are having problems receiving SMS messages. If the reason is precisely what happened on the line of the accident, you will be informed about this. In this situation, you will not need anything. Innovation and Testing

But the next reason can still be predicted. Do not receive SMS on the MTS phone? Having problems making calls on your mobile device? Be sure to contact your mobile operator in some way. He will help you understand the causes of what is happening.



For example, a new service is being tested or some innovations are being conducted. In this case, the network usually fails. But MTS is always trying to convey to its subscribers all the news about the planned work or about testing services. Typically, the operator or publishes such news on the official website, or simply sends you a warning in advance about possible malfunctions in cellular communications in a given period. It is necessary to wait until the end of testing or scheduled work. Then the calls will again be carried out without problems, and you can receive messages.

Virus

Typically, the phone "infection" simply pumps money from a SIM card, and also blocks access to the network. Tracking the virus is not as easy as it sounds. It is recommended that you take the phone to a service center, express your suspicions there, and then wait for the diagnosis to complete. Lock

Do not receive SMS on the MTS phone (Russia)? As you can see, there are a lot of scenarios. And to predict what kind of problem you have is almost impossible. If all of the above situations do not work, try checking the balance of the SIM card that is having problems.

Sometimes it turns out that a person simply does not have money on the balance sheet. Moreover, there is a lot of debt. If it is about 300 rubles, the number is blocked. MTS usually notifies about this by message. It will say that on a certain day (the date is indicated) your number will be blocked if you do not replenish the balance.

Due to blocking, you lose the ability to make and receive calls, as well as send messages. Their receipt is also included here. The only solution to the problem is to replenish the balance. As soon as the number is unlocked, all the capabilities of the cellular network will return to you.

Mobile device issues

You do not receive SMS on the MTS phone? Then try to insert another SIM card into your mobile device. You can even from the same operator. Check if communication is working properly. Not?

Then the reason for the inability to receive messages is a malfunction of the phone. How to proceed? Either take the mobile phone for repair, or buy a new one. All problems will disappear when the malfunctions are resolved.
