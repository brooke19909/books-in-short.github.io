Eilat, Israel. Red sea

This ancient city is mentioned in the Old Testament. It existed another thousand years before the birth of Christ. This city is very peculiar and is located quite far from the densely populated areas of the country. Only overcoming a vast stretch of desert, you can get to Eilat. Israel in this place has access to the Red Sea and the sea route in the direction of the Indian Ocean. This circumstance is decisive in his economy in general and in the tourism sector in particular. And its tourism potential is quite significant.



Eilat, Israel. Natural and geographical features

In certain circles, this place is almost a cult. From very remote places, often from other continents, people rush to this edge of the desert to rest. "Israel, Eilat" - these two words say a lot to diving fans. Fans of deep diving have well studied the way here, and many return to the shore of the Gulf of Eilat more than once. The unique underwater world of the Red Sea does not let go of those who saw it. This is authoritatively testified by those who have the opportunity to compare who have ever gone under water in other seas, on the shores of other continents and islands. And these natural features are generously supported by the highly developed tourist infrastructure of the city.

Residents of Israel know how to do business and make a profit. And they did everything in their power to make tourists and diver athletes feel good and comfortable on this narrow stretch of coast. And this was largely possible - from a little-known settlement on the edge of the desert to create the world-famous diving center - Eilat. Israel on this coast does not end for many, but only begins. After all, not everyone has a desire to get acquainted with the underwater world. But Eilat is also a world famous resort with a beach holiday of the highest level. This is a hot place, the temperature of air and water here is little dependent on the season of the year. If the question arose of where to fly to bask in the endless Russian winter, then this is, of course, Eilat, Israel. It is possible that the price level here does not differ in excessive availability, but the level of the proposed service fully corresponds to these prices.


Israel, Eilat. Excursions and cultural program

The city of Eilat itself is not distinguished by an abundance of historical relics and architectural monuments. The infrastructure of this city is built to provide a comfortable beach holiday. But his natural environment deserves attention for its expressiveness and dissimilarity to anything else. Mountains and rocks in this place are found in the desert. Their coloring and shape with their unusualness make us recall the scenery for science fiction films about extraterrestrial civilizations.

And in order to join the cultural and religious sights of the host country, there have never been any special problems. Israel is territorially not such a big country. And in Eilat, it will not be difficult to find a suitable tour on an individually composed route. There are plenty of options for such excursions here.
