The recreation center "Summer" (Kirillovka), or what awaits tourists in the summer

This year, most lovers of sea holidays can not afford to pay for foreign trips. Therefore, it is worth considering real alternatives to the usual vacation in the southern latitudes. After all, a vacation is drawn to spend near the sun and the sea.

Recreation base "Summer" (Kirillovka) - one of these options.

Type of rest and number of rooms

Such a cozy base would be an ideal place for families.

All guests live in eight separate two-story buildings.

  • Standard rooms (16 square meters). Small rooms with double or triple bedding, air conditioning, satellite TV, balcony, small refrigerator and electric kettle.
  • Junior Suite (18 square meters). This category of the room differs from the standard ones by the presence of its own sanitary unit with shower and room area.
  • Suite (32 square meters). Up to four people can live here. The room has two rooms with an interior door and a microwave for quick heating of food.
  • Suite (45 square meters). In other words, we can say that this is a separate small apartment with three rooms and a sanitary unit. It can accommodate five adults in the main places. The recreation center "Summer" (Kirillovka, Fedotova spit) offers guests in deluxe and junior suites bathrooms with hot and cold water.
    Residents of standard rooms can take a hot shower from 5 to 9 pm in the complex.

Complex Features

The main advantage and huge advantage of the popular resort institution is its location on the very shore of the Sea of ​​Azov. The beach here is with fine white sand and a shallow entrance to the sea, which is very important for a safe holiday with small children.

The recreation center "Summer" (Kirillovka, Peresyp Spit) is also famous for its good location relative to the main infrastructure of the village: next to the hotel you can find many cafes, souvenir shops and bars for relaxation. Also nearby is a huge market and an amusement park for children with a wide variety of attractions (dolphinarium, ferris wheel, water slides, a center for training in kiting, surfing and diving).

In the closed and round-the-clock guarded territory, the recreation center "Summer" (Kirillovka) has a free parking lot for guests.

Nutrition

The recreation center "Summer" (Kirillovka) has on its territory a small, but very pleasant cafe for food, in which in the evening you can have fun with friends, singing in karaoke, light music, and in the afternoon watching television on satellite TV. In the cafe you can taste both alcoholic and popular soft drinks.

From tourists

The recreation center "Summer" (Kirillovka) can safely brag and be proud of exclusively positive reviews, regular travelers and awards for the development of tourism in the region.
