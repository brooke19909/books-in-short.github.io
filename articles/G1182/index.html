Hotel Rainbow Castle Hotel 3 * (Side, Turkey): photos and reviews of tourists

Turkey today is one of the most popular, vibrant and interesting resort countries in Europe. After all, here a traveler can count on modern comfort, interesting entertainment, high-class service and relatively low prices. The country is famous for its large resort cities where you can spend a real dream vacation.

One of the rather interesting places for tourists is the city of Side, located on the Mediterranean coast. Of course, travelers planning to spend a few days here have a large selection of hotels. One of the popular hotel complexes is the Rainbow Castle Hotel 3 *.

When choosing a place where a vacation will be spent, a person tries to get the maximum amount of valuable information. For example, how far is the hotel from the coast and how long does it take to get to it from the airport? What do the rooms look like and are they equipped with the necessary equipment? Is Rainbow Castle Hotel 3 * suitable for families with a child? How do travelers respond about it? This information will be useful when choosing a hotel.

Where to look for a hotel? Location





Where to look for the hotel complex Rainbow Castle Hotel 3 *? Side, a picturesque resort village by the sea - it is here, in a quiet area, that the hotel is located. By the way, the distance to the city center is only 3 km (here you will find entertainment and, of course, historical sights). At 7 km is the no less interesting city of Manavgat. By the way, just a few meters from the hotel there is a public transport stop - guests can easily and quickly reach almost anywhere in the city.

The nearest international airport is located in Antalya and is approximately 63 km away. The road does not take so much time, and the hotel (or travel agencies) provides travelers with a convenient transfer.

General information

Rainbow Castle Hotel 3 * is a modern hotel complex that pleases its guests with an excellent design, decent living conditions and quality service. It consists of several five-story buildings built in a modern style and painted in all colors of the rainbow (which, in fact, the name says). The hotel area is relatively large: there is a park area, and the whole courtyard is decorated with palm trees and bright colors.

The courtyard is created for the most comfortable rest and walks. By the way, the last reconstruction here was carried out in 2015, and today the hotel has everything that is needed for a comfortable stay of a modern demanding traveler.

After an eventful day, every traveler wants to return to a clean comfortable room where you can relax and unwind. So what can the Rainbow Castle Hotel 3 * offer? Feedback from tourists suggests that it is very comfortable to spend time here.

The rooms are large and bright, and the windows offer wonderful views of the courtyard and garden. There are balconies with garden furniture, here you can breathe in the healing sea air and enjoy the panorama.

Fitted with tiled flooring, rooms are discreet in a contemporary setting. There is the necessary furniture, including not only a comfortable bed with an orthopedic mattress, but also cabinets, bedside tables, a large table and chairs.

The life of a modern traveler is difficult to imagine without technology that can provide maximum comfort. Each room has a modern air conditioning system, with which you can create pleasant temperature conditions and cope with the summer heat. You spend your free evening watching satellite and local channels. For a small fee, you can rent a safe with a combination lock, where it is safe to leave valuables. It offers guests a telephone with the ability to dial directly, as well as a minibar with drinking water (the remaining drinks will be delivered to you at an additional cost).

The room has a private bathroom. It is small but clean and equipped with modern fixtures. Water is supplied to the room around the clock. In addition, there is a toilet, a large wall mirror and a washbasin. Clean towels are changed every day (sometimes every two days) during cleaning. By the way, the rooms are really clean, the maids are doing a good job.

Dining for tourists: what does the hotel offer?





The issue of nutrition is important for almost every traveler, because during the vacation you want to not only have fun, but also get healthy and treat yourself to delicious delicacies. What does the Rainbow Castle Hotel 3 * offer its guests? Turkey, Side in particular, are those tourist places where hotels operate mainly on an all-inclusive basis, which is the best option for many people.

Paying for this service, hotel guests have the opportunity to fully eat in the local restaurant, where buffet is served three times a day. Reviews indicate that the cuisine here is really at a high level - guests can count on various snacks and salads (including fresh vegetables), dishes from different varieties of fish and meat, several types of side dishes and, of course, homemade ice cream, desserts and pastries. The all inclusive system also provides free tea, cafes, soft drinks and spirits from local manufacturers.

By the way, you can refuse from good nutrition. In such cases, guests can still from time to time eat in the hotel restaurant, but paying for meals. Since this is a resort area, there are a large number of cafes, bistros and restaurants nearby, where you can not only eat, but also have a good time.

Where is the beach located? Water recreation





In most cases, tourists decide to visit Turkey precisely because of the picturesque coast, mild climate and warm waters of the Mediterranean Sea. What can the Rainbow Castle Hotel 3 * hotel complex offer in this regard?

The beach is located about 500 meters from the hotel. Of course, this is not the first coastline, but in 10-15 minutes, travelers can easily reach the coast. Buses run between hotels and the coast, but they go on schedule, which is not always convenient.

The beach here is public, which means that many tourists and townspeople have a rest here. There are umbrellas and sun loungers on the shore, but their rent is paid. By the way, for a small price you can take a clean beach towel for a while . And on the shore there is a bar where guests are offered drinks and snacks.

According to reviews, the entrance to the sea is convenient. The shallow area is popular among children - the kids here splash merrily in the company of new friends and under the supervision of their parents. A rescue team is working on the shore, there is a toilet and changing rooms.

A sports center has been created for travelers, where they offer to engage in more active types of recreation. You can rent a boat or a catamaran, go water-skiing or a boat, go windsurfing and sailing, play beach volleyball, in a word, really have fun.

The list of additional services

Of course, for maximum comfort, travelers need some additional conditions. What can the hotel offer? To begin with, it is worth saying that the reception is open around the clock. This means that guests at any time of the day have the opportunity to ask questions, check in (if the room is free), solve some problems of a domestic nature. In addition, there is room service, however, this is paid separately.

A laundry service is available on site, giving guests the opportunity to not worry about the state of their belongings. If necessary, you can use the luggage storage room. You can exchange the local currency at an acceptable rate. In case of health problems, you need to inform the staff about this - they will immediately arrange an examination with a qualified doctor.

In public areas there is free access to the Internet, although sometimes the connection speed is not too good. There is a well-equipped business center where you can send a fax or use computer equipment. There is also a small conference room, which is used for various business and educational events.

Leisure of guests: how to spend time at the hotel?





Entertainment is an important part of the vacation, so for many tourists it is important that the hotel has conditions for interesting leisure. Naturally, the courtyard has a terrace for relaxation and a rather large swimming pool with clean warm water - here travelers relax, sunbathe, swim, have fun competitions and simply communicate with each other.

The hotel has a wellness center where guests are offered an impressive selection of wholesome treatments, including various types of massage. By the way, you should visit a real Turkish hammam or relax your muscles in a more familiar sauna.

As for entertainment, guests have a good time playing billiards and table tennis. And on the territory of the complex there is a team of groovy and talented animators who create a cheerful and festive mood among the guests, organizing various games, contests, competitions, shows and discos.

Entertainment can be found outside the Rainbow Castle Hotel 3 * (Side). The city has an interesting history and culture, so it is perfect for excursions. Right at the hotel you can buy an interesting tour of the surroundings or even make a route for independent travel, with which the hotel staff will be happy to help you.

Vacation with a child: are there any conditions?





Many people who are going on vacation with their child are interested in information of a different plan, because for them it is important not only their own comfort, but also the presence of certain conditions for children. What can the hotel complex Rainbow Castle Hotel 3 * offer?

Turkey is a country that is the best suited for family vacations, because in almost every hotel, employees try to organize the most active and useful leisure for the child. This hotel is no exception. In terms of amenities, parents and children are offered special spacious rooms. In the restaurant, kids can count on a high stool for feeding, and among the abundance of food you can always choose something useful for the child.

In terms of entertainment, the hotel complex will also not disappoint. For example, a spacious section for children was created in the pool - the depth is small, and the water is always warm, so the guys just love to spend time here. Entertainment awaits the kids in the open area with a variety of swings and slides. There is also a daily mini-club where children can have fun in the company of peers and animators, doing dance, art, sports, participating in interesting group games. In the evenings, fun discos are organized for young tourists.

By the way, parents can find entertainment for their child outside the Rainbow Castle Hotel 3 *. Side is an interesting city with large shopping centers, water parks and other entertainment venues. Here, not only small tourists, but also their parents can have a great time.
