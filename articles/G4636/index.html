Hotel Golden Coast 4 * (Protaras, Cyprus): description, photos and reviews of tourists

Tourists love Cyprus for a quiet and relaxing holiday on the pristine beaches of the island. The hotels offer modern rooms, the highest quality of service and an extensive amount of entertainment. The only minus of the island, perhaps, are only high prices. If you are looking for a place for a respectable vacation, then pay attention to the Golden Coast 4 * hotel (Cyprus, Protaras), which is considered one of the best complexes in the resort.

Hotel location

The tourist complex is located right on the beach. Tourists who live here can walk to the beach in just two minutes. The territory of the Golden Coast 4 * Hotel (Protaras, Cyprus) is surrounded by a lush garden, and a beautiful palm grove leads to the shore. In the immediate vicinity of the hotel there are numerous restaurants where vacationers can try national dishes. Shopping centers and an ATM are also within walking distance.

The hotel is located in a quiet part of Protaras. It is remote from nightclubs and bars, so it is quite quiet day and night. The resort center is 4 km away. From Larnaca Airport, you can reach the hotel in about half an hour. For tourists organize transfers. For independent travel, you can order a taxi or rent a car. Protaras is a place for a relaxing holiday, but lovers of vibrant nightlife can go to Ayia Napa. The path to it will take about 10 minutes by car. You can also get to the city by bus. A free itinerary daily runs for tourists.

What rooms does the hotel offer?

The hotel offers guests luxurious rooms, designed in a modern style. All apartments have a private balcony with a dining table and chairs. In total, the Golden Coast complex (Cyprus) has 228 rooms. About half of them have windows facing the sea. The rest is in the forest zone and mountains. Daily cleaning is included in the price. Also in all rooms there is a regular change of bed linen and towels. Tourists can stay in the following rooms:

  • Standard room (25 sq. M.). It consists of a bedroom and a bathroom. It provides accommodation for no more than three people.
  • Junior Suite (27 sq. M.). These rooms are located on the ground floor and overlook the sea. Two adults and one child can stay here.
  • Family room (31 sq. M.). There is a bedroom, separated by a partition, and a bathroom. A rollaway bed is provided for the child. A family of two adults and three children or three adults and two children can live.
  • Children's Suite (26 sq. M.). For children there is a bunk bed. Designed for a family of two adults and two children.
  • Large suite (41 sq. M.). There is a bedroom and a spacious living room, in which there is a folding sofa, as well as a dining area. The bathroom is additionally equipped with a jacuzzi. Includes accommodation for two adults and a child.




All about the equipment and amenities of the rooms

All rooms of the Golden Coast 4 * hotel are equipped with numerous amenities that make your stay at home comfortable. Checking into the apartment, all guests can use the following equipment:

  • TV. A modern flat model is offered. It has satellite and cable TV. There is one Russian-language channel.
  • Central air conditioning. It keeps the room cool. It can be regulated by guests independently.
  • Set for tea or coffee. Includes an electric kettle, several mugs and saucers, teaspoons.
  • Minibar and mini fridge. Available at extra cost. Filling the minibar is also paid separately.
  • Radio. Only local radio stations work. It can be useful for listening to music.
  • Direct telephone. On it you can contact the administrator or order in the room delivery of food, drinks and newspapers. Some rooms have two: in the bedroom and in the bathroom.
  • Electronic safe. Designed for storing small items, such as a phone, jewelry or money. Paid by the day.
  • Hair dryer. It can be used for free.
  • Cosmetic accessories in the bathroom.

How can tourists entertain themselves?

For its guests, the Golden Coast Hotel (Cyprus) can offer a wide selection of different types of entertainment. For tourists are available:

  • Open pool. It works in fine weather. There is a separate sun terrace, equipped with sun loungers and parasols.
  • Indoor pool. Additionally equipped with a jacuzzi.

  • Fitness Centre. Aerobics classes are regularly held for everyone.
  • Day and evening animation. Greek guests are organized for the guests on the history and culture of this country. Dance classes and workshops on cooking local dishes are held. These events are accompanied by live music and karaoke contests.
  • Bicycles for rent. The hotel also has a garden with bike paths.
  • Equipment for playing darts, squash, table and classic tennis, volleyball, as well as billiards.




Children's holiday

Tourist complex Golden Coast 4 * (Protaras, Cyprus) is ideal for families with children. A quiet and environmentally friendly environment is what guests value most. For children under two years old, a cradle is provided in the room. Restaurants always have high chairs for feeding, and a children's menu can also be developed upon request. Also, tourists can always hire a qualified nanny.

From May to October, the hotel has a mini-club, which offers children not only entertainment, but also a development program. Together with experienced animators, they play on the beach, attend excursions, participate in sports and recreational activities. Also for them shows of educational films and discos are held. In addition, a children's room equipped with toys and books, as well as an outdoor area, is always open for visitors. There is a children's pool equipped with water slides.
