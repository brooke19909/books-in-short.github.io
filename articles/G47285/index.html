Krasny Sulin, Rostov Region

Each place on the map of our vast and blessed country of Russia has its own unique history. And some cities and towns are named after great people. One of these cities is Krasny Sulin, which is located in the Rostov region on the river. Rotten and Kundryuchya. This is an administrative center with a population of 43 thousand souls. And now some interesting information about the Krasnosnosulinsky region.

Biography of the district center





The history of the city began in the 18th century, on the day of the foundation of the small farm Sulin. What remains of the former settlement? An elevated plain with stone hills, dissected by beams and streams. Sulinskie lands on its territory have numerous picturesque villages and regional cities (Zverevo and Gukovo). Today, there are wide highways and railways of international importance connecting the Donetsk region with the central part of Russia, as well as Ukraine and the Caucasus.

But it was not always so. Previously, the territory had four different volosts, in each of which there were small settlements. Life in them was measured and boring, until the railway station Voronezh-Rostov-on-Don was formed. The quiet and calm rural way has radically changed. In a peasant settlement, the construction of a metallurgical plant began on the initiative of the industrialist D. Pastukhov

Workshops and enterprises (mechanical, blacksmith, boiler, crutch, rolling, model) were built like mushrooms after rain. The economic situation in the region has skyrocketed. Blast-furnace smelting brought enormous income. About four million tons of various metals were produced annually at the plant, more than three million tons of pig iron was smelted.

But the First World War brought with it tremendous upheaval and destruction.

The city of Krasny Sulin (Rostov Region) did not fall under the onslaught of the Nazis





After the region was granted official status in 1926, its revival began after chaos. Initially, a metallurgical plant was reconstructed, squares were gradually built, parks and recreation areas were built. But happiness did not last long - the Great Patriotic War came.

And again, the city of Red Sulin has been devastated. Local residents stood up to defend their homeland, having taken military weapons and desperately fighting for the honor of the region. The plant worked in heavy duty, it produced anti-tank hedgehogs. A little less than a year the administrative center was in German occupation. In February 1943 the city was liberated by the army under the command of Vatutin N.F.

Achievements and attractions





Neither war nor revolution could break the Russian spirit. Even after significant losses, Red Sulin "rose" from the ashes, spread its "wings" and grew even more. Adversity and destruction made him more beautiful, stronger and more civilized. Today it is a developed industrial center with 14 metallurgical, processing, coal and energy enterprises on its vast land. Grammar schools, vocational schools, lyceums, universities and schools of Krasniy Sulin take first places in terms of education. The area has the best healthcare systems.

Pokrovsky Temple





The old church built in 1874 is the most important object of the city. It is always crowded here. When you are inside the temple, breathtaking from its beauty and decoration. This is the only church preserved from that time. In 1942, it underwent significant destruction. Even after a global reconstruction, traces of machine-gun fire are visible on the walls through a thick layer of plaster. The temple is truly unique and conveys the atmosphere of the Russian spirit. Today the territory of the church is being ennobled; at the moment, a Sunday school has been built.

Repeater





The highest construction of the city (80 m). It was founded in 1981 on the "Pock Mount", which offers a breathtaking panorama of the local surroundings. The tower is the hallmark of the area and one of the main attractions. Guests who come to the region first of all inspect the repeater, climb to the site and enjoy the lights of nearby cities: Zverevo, Novoshakhtinsk and Gukovo.

Nature Object and Recreation Park - Canyon Lake

A unique, naturally created reservoir (in 1970) with rocky shores, located almost in the center of the city. The water in it is so clean and transparent that the contours of stones located at 10 meters depth are visible. This is not an easy natural treasure of the Krasnosulinsky district, but also a favorite vacation spot of the local population. In summer, people gather near the canyon to swim, sunbathe and dive. There is very little silt and overgrown.

A lot of mysterious and outstanding objects are located in this region and to see with their own eyes enough, visit the historical city of Red Sulin. Getting here is very simple: by plane, by train or by bus to Rostov, and then transfer to a minibus or taxi. You can stay in comfortable hotels "Sulin" or "Caspian".
