The best hotels of Protaras: interesting collections

Protaras (Cyprus) is a fairly young resort with an actively developing tourism industry. The town is famous for its picturesque sandy beaches, unique nature, rocky bays, crystal clear sea and numerous modern hotel complexes. Protaras hotels are aimed at a wide audience of tourists. All these conditions are ideal for families with children.





The resort has a warm friendly atmosphere, so most vacationers come here. Unlike the city of Ayia Napa, which is famous for its entertainment venues and vibrant nightlife, Protaras is always calm, comfortable and quiet. There is no noise, fuss, during the day there is always something to do and where to go, and at night you can have fun at the disco or take a walk around the town and admire the singing fountain that delights the eyes with a colorful performance.

All year round the resort is warm and there is practically no wind, as the town is protected by picturesque cliffs. Even in winter, the temperature does not drop below +17 ¬∞ C. Protaras hotels are very popular among Russian tourists. Every second hotel is located on the seafront, which is a huge advantage. It is worthy and comfortable to stay in a chic 5-star complex or in a modest 3-star guesthouse - the choice is huge. We bring to your attention the best hotels of Protaras of various categories.





5 star complex "Constantinis Great Beach"

Luxurious hotel located in a beautiful bay on the Mediterranean coast. The complex is famous for its high service, sophisticated atmosphere and convenient location close to a variety of entertainment and rich infrastructure.

The hotel offers its guests truly royal conditions and a chic selection of services. When buying a tour, you can choose for yourself a comfortable room in a 4-storey building, and apartments in a 2-storey cottage with a personal kitchen. Near the hotel there is a wide beach with the necessary amenities.





All Protaras hotels will offer tourists interesting water activities: riding a banana, donuts, a yacht, a catamaran, parachuting, scuba diving, water skiing and windsurfing. Despite the measured and unhurried flow of rest, Protaras also has a vibrant nightlife with numerous clubs, discos, karaoke and pubs. Each vacationer will discover new experiences.

Complex "Golden Coast 4 *"

A paradise for a vacation with the whole family. The resort center is located on the first beach line and is surrounded by tropical vegetation on all sides. On a large well-groomed territory there are swimming pools, playgrounds and development clubs for the smallest.





Rooms with a comfortable layout and modern technology are characterized by increased comfort. There is a Greek chapel at the hotel where couples in love marry. At the reception work Russian-speaking staff, always ready to help guests.

Hotel "Pernera Beach Horel 3 *"

If you do not have large funds, you can always find economy-class Protaras hotels with good conditions. For example, the 3-star Pernera Beach Horel Hotel is one of the best in the declared category. The guesthouse is located 2 km from the central part of the city. Perfect for family and youth pastime.





Lush shrubs and tall palm trees grow all around the hotel. Children's leisure is well developed here: playgrounds, centers, pools, animation. Nearby lies a beautiful promenade and a magnificent beach with fine sand. Fun, active, relaxed you will spend time in this wonderful complex at an affordable cost.

This is only a small part of the tourist centers where you can have a great rest. If you are planning a trip, we recommend that you select Protaras hotels in advance. Prices range from 700 to 6500 rubles. per day. A colorful, rich experience will be provided to you.
