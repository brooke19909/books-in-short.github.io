King Tut Aqua Park 4 * (Egypt, Hurghada): photos and reviews of tourists

Today in our article we will talk about the King Tut Aqua Park 4 * hotel. What you need to know about him, going on vacation to Egypt? Hotel King Tut Aqua Park 4 *, located in Hurghada, we will consider from different positions, based on the reviews of tourists who stayed in it.

Where is?

Hotel King Tut Aqua Park 4 * (Hurghada, Dahar) is located 5 km from the center of the old city. At 6 kilometers is the eponymous airport of this developing resort.

Hotel Description

King Tut Aqua Park Beach Resort 4 * hotel was opened in 2004. In 2011, furniture and plumbing were updated. The hotel consists of a main building (height - 6 floors) and three one-story bungalows.

This is a low-cost hotel that is suitable for an economical vacation, and windsurfing enthusiasts often stop here.

Description of rooms

In total, King Tut Aqua Park Beach Hotel has 4 * 135 rooms: 97 standard double rooms (garden / pool / city view, area - 30 sq. M, two extra beds are possible); 5 family two-room rooms (sea view, accommodation for up to 4 people, room size - about 50 sq. M); 30 superior rooms with sea view (30 sq. M, maximum 4 people); There are also 3 one-story detached bungalows. The hotel has 26 non-smoking rooms.





Bathroom, telephone, air conditioning, satellite TV (with one Russian channel) - in each room. A hairdryer and a safe - in the room or at the reception (free of charge), a minibar is paid, and one two-liter bottle of mineral water is provided for free daily. Most of the rooms have a balcony or terrace. Cleaning is done daily, bed linen and towels are changed every two days. Some room service is available at an additional cost.

Nutrition

King Tut Aqua Park 4 * hotel (Hurghada, Dahar) accepts guests on an all-inclusive basis, but it should be remembered that only local spirits are free, all imported alcohol is at extra cost. It offers guests - several restaurants and bars.

Restaurants

  • Cleopatra (free). The restaurant is open buffet style. You can have breakfast from 7 to 10 in the morning, lunch is served from 13 to 15 hours, dinner - from 7 to 9.30 in the evening. Going on a tour, you can order a late dinner, he will be waiting for you from 22.30 to 00.30. According to tourists, hot dishes are tasty and varied, it is better not to take ready-made salads (cut too large, a lot of mayonnaise and not always fresh vegetables), but you can independently prepare a salad of fresh vegetables. Dairy products are tasteless. There are also complaints about the dirty room.
  • Memphis (free). The restaurant is open daily from 7 to 9 pm, you should pre-reserve places on the day of dinner. Themed evenings are organized here, and the choice of dishes is on the menu. Travelers note interesting show programs of this institution, but it is better to reserve places in the morning, as otherwise there is a risk of not getting into this restaurant for dinner.
  • Restaurant "Love Bot" (paid). If you want to organize a romantic dinner, you can go to this restaurant, which is open on Tuesdays from 7 to 9 pm. It serves a variety of fish and seafood dishes. Advance reservation is required. According to hotel guests, the fish menu is tasty and fresh, the dishes are varied, beautifully served, the atmosphere in the establishment is relaxing and relaxed, the terrace offers stunning views of the Red Sea.
  • Restaurant "Isis" (paid). You can have a fun Sunday evening in the Isis Restaurant, which is open from 7 to 9 in the evening. Here, a wonderful barbecue menu, guests are waiting for an African show program. Reservation is required on the day of dinner. The guests of the King Tut Aqua Park 4 * hotel will appreciate the meat and vegetables cooked on the grill, but they warn about very loud music in this restaurant and about the overly intrusive animators.




From June 1 to September 30, all restaurants offer oriental dishes.

Bars

To services of visitors - 4 bars:

  • The lobby bar , located in the lobby of the hotel, is open around the clock, from 16 to 18 you can drink tea or coffee with small cupcakes. A light dinner is also served here.
  • Isis Bar is open from 10 am to midnight. From 3 to 5 days a light buffet is served here.
  • The beach bar is open from 10am until sunset. It serves refreshments, juices, beer, light cocktails, ice cream, fruits and delicious desserts, which are highly praised by hotel guests.
  • Cafe "Oasis". The name of the cafe fully describes its interior: among the flowers and trees around the beautiful fountain in the fresh air are cozy tables. The cafe serves juices, fruits, ice cream, desserts and light snacks.




Hotel Services

The hotel has paid and free services.

They provide free room service, cots, sun loungers and umbrellas on the beach and near the pool, a bottle of drinking water daily and the ability to use a safe and a left-luggage office at the reception. Throughout the hotel guests are offered free Wi-Fi (including on the beach). During lunch in the Cleopatra restaurant you can enjoy ice cream, and from 3 to 5 pm smoke a hookah in the beach bar. Also, at no extra charge, you can visit the hotel's water park, bask in the sauna or go to a disco (for drinks - a fee).

Do not count on visiting the jacuzzi in King Tut Aqua Park 4 *, a photo of which is posted in almost every hotel brochure. More precisely, there is a jacuzzi here, but far from being so attractive: located on the roof of the hotel, with dirty water and weak streams.

For a fee, you can use the laundry, hairdresser and beauty salon, call a doctor, order a transfer to the airport, rent a car to travel around the resort, and even order a limousine. You can also purchase the necessary things for yourself and souvenirs for friends in small shops and shops that the King Tut Aqua Park 4 * hotel has located on its territory. Guest reviews warn of poor quality hotel laundry. When ironing, things are often spoiled here, without compensating for the cost of damaged clothes.





Beach, pool

King Tut Aqua Park Beach Resort 4 * hotel is located on the first coastline, less than 200 meters from the sea. The hotel has its own small sand and pebble beach with a comfortable gentle entry into the water. The sea forms here a small lagoon, in which children and vacationers who are poorly kept on water can calmly frolic. However, you should take care of special shoes for entering the water, since there are many sharp pebbles and sea urchins. A live coral reef is located not far from the beach, and another hundred meters from the shore, with many bright fish, sometimes found barracudas and turtles. For those who like to dive from the beach to the sea, a pier separates the territories of two hotels.

The beach of a neighboring hotel (use is free for guests of King Tut Aqua Park 4 *) tourist reviews recommend for people who know how to swim well: the sea is immediately deep (three meters from the coast, the depth is more than five meters). Sunset at sea - on paved steps from a stone slab. The bottom here is ugly, a lot of construction debris, the reef is dead.





The beaches are equipped with sun loungers with mattresses and umbrellas, beach towels are provided (once a day). The waiters from the bar serve guests of the hotel, offering drinks and fruits.

Located in the city of Hurghada, King Tut Aqua Park 4 * offers its guests two large outdoor pools, in one of them from December 1, water is heated. The water in the pools is fresh, around there are sun loungers with mattresses and umbrellas.

Also, guests of the King Tut Aqua Park 4 * hotel can use the pools, beach and bars of the neighboring Sphinx Aqua Park Beach Resort 4 *.

Aquapark

In addition to swimming pools and beaches, tourists can have fun in the water park of a nearby hotel. It is small, with heated water in the winter. For children there are three slides and a children's pool. For adults, 4 slides are installed here: one is low, gentle, double, the second is straight, rather steep, the third is spiral, open, the fourth is closed, spiral. When visiting the water park, you should be careful: the joints on the slides can be poorly fitted, and there is a high risk of getting hematomas. Also, reviews note the inconvenience of climbing up for a descent from the hills - for these purposes only one staircase is provided, which is rarely cleaned.

Leisure for adults

Regarding the animation at the King Tut Aqua Park 4 * hotel, the reviews were divided. Some guests complain about the boring monotonous animation, the lack of a good evening program, while others, on the contrary, are completely satisfied with their excellent leisure and colorful shows.





The hotel has a Russian-speaking animation team. During the day you can go to belly dance classes or try your hand at windsurfing, play water polo or do gymnastics, conduct water aerobics classes twice a day in the pool of the water park. Daytime animation is unobtrusive, not too noisy, for the most part these are activities in various sports. Twice a week, the hotel organizes holidays.

There are two tables on the territory of the Isis bar: a billiard table and a table tennis table. There are no declared basketball and volleyball courts, as well as a tennis court at King Tut Aqua Park 4 * hotel. There is a ball court at the nearby Sphinx Hotel, but the net and balls are not always there.

Evening animation at the hotel is represented by a disco, which hosts a variety of comic games and contests. Sometimes you can watch fire shows, fakir numbers or oriental dances.

Children's animation

For children, the King Tut Aqua Park 4 * hotel has a good playground with a large selection of swings and horizontal bars, there is a shallow pool and children's slides in the water park. During the day, animators deal with children, hold competitions, arrange active and calm (attentiveness, erudition, dexterity) games. Those who want to make crafts or draw pictures can go to the kids club. In the evening, a mini-disco with fun children's songs and contests is held for young guests of the hotel, once a week they choose a mini-miss and Mr. Hotel.

Excursions

Those who want to diversify their beach holidays can go on various excursions, which can be purchased at the reception or on the beach, with local guides (the second offer will be cheaper and more interesting). The most popular city tours, fishing and a trip to Cairo.

A sightseeing tour of Hurghada will help guests of the resort to get to know this city, which 18 years ago was a small fishing village. The guide will tell about the history of this region, show two sides of this city - the bright and beautiful tourist and poor real life of local residents (the second part of the tour is carried out at the request of the group). In addition to the Coptic Church and the mosque of St. Abdulhasan Elshazi (the main Muslim temple of the city), there are no attractions here, the trip is more likely to be interesting for those who want to walk around the colorful oriental bazaar with a lot of merchants and wander around the shops veiled under the name of the museum of aromatic oils, the museum papyrus, hibiscus factories and gold factories, where you can learn something new about manufacturing processes and product properties and purchase small souvenirs.





If you love the sea and want to participate in the fishing process, then, of course, you will be interested in sea fishing. Usually the tour starts at 9 am: group members are taken from hotels and taken to the pier with many boats. Further, as elsewhere - access to the sea, the issuance of equipment and fishing itself. Fishing rods here are a small stick on which you need to reel the fishing line, using chopped squid carcasses or pieces of the same fish as profit. The nibble is excellent, the fish is caught differently: from a small inedible colorful picasso to impressive sea bass. Those who wish can swim in the sea on the other side of the boat. At the end of fishing, a delicious lunch prepared from freshly caught fish awaits everyone.

Those who want to see one of the wonders of the world - the Egyptian pyramids - can go on an excursion to the capital of the country - Cairo. This is a long tour, which will take a whole day (some companies offer two-day options with a stop in Alexandria). After midnight, a comfortable bus will pick you up from the hotel to deliver you to the modern metropolis of Cairo in the morning. A large number of museums, architectural monuments and other attractions have been collected on the territory of the city, which are impossible to see in one day. Therefore, as part of the tour, you will visit the most ambitious and famous beauties of this country: the national museum, which contains a huge number of treasures of the pharaohs, jewelry, statues, various artifacts, unique sarcophagi and mummies, the pyramids of Giza, whose monumentalism amazes and delights, and the Great Sphinx, whose lion's body is crowned by the head of the pharaoh. In addition to the main attractions, you will visit the Museum of Papyrus and the factory of essential oils, where they will tell and show all the technology of their manufacture and offer to buy a small souvenir in memory of visiting museums.





On the road, it is advisable to take a small pillow with you to sleep comfortably on the bus, drinking water, money for additional expenses (visiting the pyramids and museums, buying small souvenirs). Tourists who have already visited this tour caution to give photographic equipment into the hands of the local population, ride camels near the pyramids (a well-known local joke: you will climb a camel for free, but you will have to fork out to go down to the ground) and advise you to avoid too annoying sellers, which offer free postcards and souvenirs.

Rest at the King Tut Aqua Park 4 * hotel, located in Hurghada, on the Red Sea coast, is suitable for those who love travel and excursions and are looking for affordable accommodation in Egypt. A beautiful sea reef and its own water park are the undoubted advantages of the hotel, which help to turn a blind eye to some of its shortcomings.
