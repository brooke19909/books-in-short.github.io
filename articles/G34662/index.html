Review Supra M727G. Features, reviews, unlock

Some devices are available adapted only for a specific operator. That was the "budget" Supra M727G. The user receives not only a cheap device, but also a connection to the MTS network.

Design





It is difficult to find a highlight in an inexpensive tablet. Unfortunately, the Supra M727G turned out to be just like all the "state employees". Among other devices, it can only be identified by the logo on the back.

The body of the device is made of plastic. Given the low cost, it is not worth hoping for good material. Small scratches quickly appear on the case, which does not add to the device's attractiveness. In addition, squeaks are heard when pressed.

External details took their usual places on the tablet Supra M727G. On the front part, a display, sensors, a speaker and a front camera were sheltered. The power button and volume control are on the right sidewall. At the back of the tablet is the main camera, speaker and, of course, the logo. The upper end was taken under the connectors for USB and headset.

An unattractive appearance is complemented by a lack of colors. The device is available exclusively in black version.

Display





Tablet Supra M727G received a standard diagonal of seven inches. The manufacturer paid attention and resolution. It is 1024 by 600 pixels. Naturally, such a characteristic does not look particularly attractive, but for a "state employee" this is an excellent solution.

Hardware





The "filling" of the device looks a little better. The tablet received the MTK processor, a favorite of "budget employees", which is able to cope with many tasks. The device will be pleased with the presence of two cores with a frequency of 1.3 GHz each.

The hardware was also not without flaws. The manufacturer saved on RAM, and as a result, the device received its minimum characteristic of 512 megabytes. Such memory significantly reduces overall performance.

There are other shortcomings. For example, the device received only 4 GB of native memory. Fortunately, it is possible to expand its flash drive to 32 GB.

System

The tablet operates under the guidance of a good Android 4.4. Naturally, this is not the latest version, but for the "state employee" it is enough. A shell on top of the system is not installed, so the user will work with a clean version.

Some users have encountered difficulties with flashing the device. After installing a more recent version of the system, the device refuses to start. Perhaps a similar problem will be fixed soon.

Price

The manufacturer is asking for a very affordable price for the Supra M727G. The price fluctuates around 5 thousand rubles, which is quite acceptable. Accessibility and functionality make this device very attractive.

Autonomy

The M727G, like most tablets, has a lame battery. The device received a battery of only 2000 mAch. Accordingly, with the active operation of the device, charging is enough for only 3.5 hours.

Unlock





The tablet accepts only MTS cards, but a similar problem is easy to fix. You can contact the experts, or you can independently eliminate the defect in the Supra M727G. Unlocking begins with finding out IMEI in the device settings.

Having determined the numbers for each device slot, add the first and last six digits of the code. Thus, a six-digit number should be obtained, it is the unlock password. Each slot has its own code.

Then, in the "dialer" you need to dial the number * # * # 29305652 # * # *. Thus the engineering menu is called up. Then we find the Simme Lock tab, and in it - Network Personalization. Next, click Unlock and enter the code. For each slot, a password is entered separately. After all, we restart the device.
