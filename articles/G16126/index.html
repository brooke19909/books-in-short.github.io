Hotel St. Constantin 5 * (Greece / Crete): photos, descriptions, prices and reviews of tourists

Greece is a country in which a bright sun shines year-round, warming its inhabitants with its warmth. The mild climate and favorable atmosphere invariably attract travelers who come here from all over the world.

Economy Premium Hotel





St. Constantin 5 * is a five-star hotel that serves its guests at the highest level, and does not take that much money for it, when compared with other hotels of this class. However, it is worth talking about pricing later. For starters, some information about the hotel itself.

Its first advantage is transport accessibility. Not only are there public transport stops nearby, but also the airport is within 20-30 kilometers. This ideal distance is far enough so that the noise of the landing planes does not interfere with relaxation, but is quite close to the hotel.

People do not like to spend extra hours to their temporary residence, given that they have just left the ramp and want to quickly settle down and begin to relax. Another worth noting is the fact that St. Constantin 5 * is located on the first coastline - literally two minutes walk from the beach.





Number of rooms

The hotel offers guests rooms of various categories - each guest will find the option of apartments that suits him the most.

In St. Constantin 5 * only 132 rooms - the best designers worked on their design, so being inside is a pleasure. The apartments are made taking into account Cretan style traditions - this can be seen both in the design and in the architecture of the entire hotel.

The rooms are perfect for a romantic getaway or for a family pastime. At least in these places youth companies stop, often elderly couples also act as guests. So St. Constantin hotel 5 * can be called a universal hotel. But one very important point should be noted. Despite the fact that there are many rooms in the hotel, it is worth to book apartments in advance - the hotel is popular, and at the height of the season it can be simply clogged.

Accommodation options

Guests are offered a fairly wide selection in terms of rooms. There are standard, double - two people inside will sit comfortably. This is the most popular type of rooms, since couples most often come to rest.

Next are the standard family apartments with a separate bedroom - for 2-4 people. You can book with two separate bedrooms - these rooms usually accommodate five people. Guests who value comfort will love the Superior Double. But in these rooms they stop for three - booking options allow this. And finally, superior family rooms, that is, superior apartments.

Room features





Apartment St. Constantin Village 5 * is designed for a comfortable stay - they have everything you need so that a person can get enough sleep, relax and just have a good time in a relaxed and cozy atmosphere.

The standard rooms have air conditioning and heating (even heating is thought out, as it is sometimes cold for some guests), a refrigerator, a plasma TV with satellite and music channels, modern comfortable furniture, a terrace (or balcony). It is also worth noting the attention of such trifles as a hairdryer, safe, telephone, kettle and even an outlet designed for a razor. In general, everything that may be needed.

Superior rooms are distinguished by the provision of additional services that are included in the price of the reservation. Entertainment and additional services

In St. Constantin Village hotel 5 * service is developed at the highest level - the administration made sure that the guests feel as comfortable as possible. The reception is open 24 hours a day, Wi-Fi is provided in public areas (naturally, free of charge), sports and active lifestyle lovers will definitely like the hotel fitness center and gym.

Those people who like to relax in body and soul can visit the sauna or the garden located on the roof of the hotel - there are also sun loungers. Even reading lovers will find something to do - the hotel has its own library. Hotel guests
