Diamond Beach Hotel Spa (Turkey / Side): description, reviews of tourists

Colakli is a city in Turkey, which is located next to the Side entertainment center. In all of Turkey, these are the most popular resort areas where guests come from the different regions of the world every year, including from Russia.

One of the most popular hotels in the town of Colakli - Diamond Beach Hotel & Spa 5 *. Here, according to vacationers, almost the best conditions for living and recreation are provided, and, as tourists themselves note, representatives of any generation are comfortable here, due to which the hotel is often referred to as family.

Location and General Information

Diamond Beach Hotel & Spa is located in the coastal part of Colakli. Literally 12 kilometers from it is one of the zones most visited by Russian tourists - the village of Manavgat and the city center of Side. As for the air routes of transport, the Antalya International Airport is literally 50 km from the hotel, which is very convenient for foreign tourists - their travel time will not be long.

Hotel Diamond Beach Hotel & Spa 5 * was built in 2004, its final restoration was carried out in 2010 - it was at this time that its buildings were perfected, they were given a modern look. Currently, the total area of ‚Äã‚Äãthis place for recreation is 10,000 square meters. m, which is a lot.



Rooms

Several categories of apartments are presented to the attention of guests, the total number of which is 288. All rooms in the hotel are divided into two categories: Standard and Family. All apartments have exclusively modern furniture, which, as noted by residents, is not only beautiful, but also very comfortable.

All rooms have individual air conditioning, with which guests can set the desired temperature in their room, regardless of the weather, which is on the street. Each apartment also has modern bathrooms with a bathroom and shower.

The apartments are cleaned three times a week, as well as linen and towels. Tourists note that the room service at the hotel works great.

"Standard"

In the rooms of the Standard group there is only one room, designed for a couple of guests. Often they are chosen by tourists who come with their soulmate in order to have a good time together.

The apartments of this group have everything you need for a comfortable stay. The interior is simple, but at the same time elegant. Comfortable large beds for two are provided here as sleeping places. Some apartments have a large sleeping place replaced with two single beds. If necessary, a folding seat of a European type can be installed in the room. The Standard furniture set also includes a large mirror with a dressing table, a desk and a chair that you may need for work, as well as a wardrobe for personal items. Near the beds there is a pair of nightstands. The room is beautifully lit by various devices, and the window offers beautiful views of the flowering gardens and pool.

In a number of rooms of the Standard group there are two of those that are designed for people with disabilities. Everything is arranged in them in such a way that people with disabilities feel comfortable living, despite their characteristics. The furniture installed here was made to order, and plumbing was brought from Europe. These rooms are located on the ground floor of the main building.



"Family"

The apartments of the "Family" group are designed to accommodate guests who come to rest with small children. Each of them has a good crib made of light wood - it is designed for guests aged 4 to 12 years.

Rooms of this level are designed to accommodate four adult guests. Two rooms are provided for them, in each of which two beds are installed. As for the rest of the furniture, its set coincides with that which is available in the "Standard". In their free time, guests can watch their favorite TV shows on a plasma TV that broadcasts satellite channels, many of them in Russian. The room has a minibar, which is filled with non-alcoholic drinks upon arrival of tourists - their range is updated every day. The apartment has a telephone, and if necessary, you can install a safe in the room to store a small amount of valuables, but its use is paid and is charged daily.

Conditions for a comfortable stay for children

For the administration of the Diamond Beach Hotel & Spa, the safety of guests plays an important role, special attention is paid to children in this matter. In order to ensure that their pastime was not only fun, comfortable, but also safe, there is a small, built by European standards, children's pool in the open area, in which an automatic water heating system works. Another niche with water, which is also intended for babies, is located in the main building, in the spa area.

On the summer terrace, surrounded by a flowering garden, there is a large and well-equipped playground, where young guests can actively spend their time communicating with other children in parallel.

Parents themselves in their reviews of Diamond Beach Hotel & Spa note that their children can always be sent to a special club, which operates on time. His visit is paid, but the kids really like to spend time here, because in the game rooms with them are funny animators who not only look after the child, but also do everything to ensure that time is not wasted, - the entire show program , which is held here, is aimed at the development of this category of guests. If desired, the hotel can order babysitting services, but they must be booked in advance, approximately one day in advance.

Nutrition

Vacationers often in their reviews of Diamond Beach Hotel & Spa 5 * note that the food at the hotel is provided at the highest level. This is due to the fact that in local restaurants where the buffet is organized, exclusively professional chefs work, whose culinary creations are able to win the hearts of even the most sophisticated gourmets.



Guests at the hotel can be fed as part of the all-inclusive concept at three restaurants serving international cuisine. In reviews, it is often noted that food made from seafood is most popular - according to vacationers, it is in the restaurants of Diamond Beach Hotel & Spa 5 * (Side, Cholakli) that any seafood can be prepared in such a way that it opens as unusual as possible.

In addition to gourmet establishments, the hotel has three bars where it is always possible to taste snacks, as well as drinks, among which there are both alcoholic and non-alcoholic ones.

Entertainment

Guests at Diamond Beach Hotel & Spa have the opportunity every day, starting in the evening and throughout almost the whole night, to participate in a disco, where you can really relax under the sounding hits of foreign pop music, mixed by talented DJs. Such events are held outdoors, near the aquazone.

In the daytime, all fans of games and recreation can visit the billiard room, horseback riding center or have the opportunity to just go to the beach, which also presents a lot of water activities. For fans of games such as darts, chess and checkers, a separate room is equipped, which tourists visit quite often.



Spa area

Fans of relaxation, staying at the Diamond Beach Hotel & Spa (Cholakli), will not miss the opportunity to look into the spa area, which occupies a rather impressive area. It consists of an aquazone and a beautiful thermal complex, in which there is a massage room.

The structure of the thermal zone includes a classic Turkish bath (hammam), as well as a sauna. Upon exiting it, guests can plunge into the pool, where the automatic heating of the contained water is installed.

Only professionals in their field work in the massage room, who know exactly how to give the client pleasure and immerse him in a state of complete relaxation. If desired, the guest can use a number of cosmetic procedures designed for the body and skin, but they are paid - their cost can be clarified with the hotel.



Pool

All lovers of relaxation near the water in the daytime gather in the aquazone, which is in the open. It is fully furnished with comfortable sun loungers and beach umbrellas, which are able to save from sunlight. If desired, guests can borrow towels and air mattresses for temporary use.

As for the pool, there are three for adults. In one of them there is a water park consisting of two high slides. Among the guests of Diamond Beach Hotel & Spa 5 * (Side), it is incredibly popular - that you can have fun and unforgettable time on the slides, it is often written in the comments left by tourists on the results of their holidays.

Sport

All the guests of this place for recreation, who prefer to lead an active lifestyle, gather in the gym, where many decent devices are installed, which, as tourists say, perfectly affect different parts of the body.

According to the comments left, it can also be concluded that the female half of the guests prefer to visit the fitness room, where you can do aerobics under the guidance of a professional instructor.

In the open air there are also several sports areas, among which volleyball and basketball courts, as well as a football field, are very popular. For an extra fee, you can play tennis at the professionally equipped court or visit the horse riding center.

Beach

The coastline, which is 22 meters long, is located at a distance of 250 meters from the main building. In order to get to it, guests of the Diamond Beach Hotel & Spa 5 * (Turkey) need to pass the promenade.

The beach area is perfectly ennobled for a comfortable stay - it offers a beautiful view of the nature. The recreation area is completely covered with fine sand, it has a small area for playing beach volleyball. If desired, guests can stay on the established sun loungers, which are located under huge umbrellas - using them is completely free. Towels are available at a surcharge.

As for entertainment, while on the beach, you can ride a boat or boat, hovercraft, as well as water skiing. The most extreme tourists will not miss the opportunity to dive into the depths with scuba diving.



Prices and reviews

Diamond Beach Hotel & Spa (Side) is a hotel where you can relax together for a week for 40-45 thousand rubles. Tourists are sure that this is one of the best offers that are possible.



In the process of rest and immediately after it, almost every traveler shares his impressions from staying at Diamond Beach Hotel & Spa 5 *. According to tourists, a truly high level of service is provided here. This translates into a thorough cleaning of rooms, excellently prepared food, excellent childcare, as well as interesting entertainments at any time of the day. Guests also note the excellent arrangement of rooms - according to them, comfort and harmony reign in the Diamond Beach Hotel & Spa apartments, which contributes to a pleasant stay.

Most tourists plan to return to this place more than once, and also recommend resting here with their friends and relatives, mentioning in their best words the time spent here. At Diamond Beach Hotel & Spa, according to vacationers, for a small fee, you can get expensive conditions that will definitely appeal to you. These words are often supported by vivid and cheerful photographs of vacationers made at the hotel and on its territory.
