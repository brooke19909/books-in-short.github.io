Hotel 3 * Longuinhos Beach Resort (India / Goa): reviews and photos of tourists

Hotel 3 * Longuinhos Beach Resort is a quiet and tranquil resort located near one of the best beaches on the entire south coast of Goa.

Hotel Description

3 * Longuinhos Beach Resort will help you plunge into the atmosphere of a carefree and incredibly comfortable stay. Your attention is presented to a beautiful beach, a clean and well-kept pool, a dance floor and many other interesting and useful services.

Hotel category 3 * Longuinhos Beach Resort boasts its cozy and comfortable rooms that can satisfy the needs of even the most demanding tourists. It offers many amenities, modern appliances, as well as scenic views, a courtyard and a swimming pool.

Of great interest is Indian cuisine with a variety of flavors and spicy spices. Hotel 3 * Longuinhos Beach Resort has its own restaurant, which serves the most delicious local and international dishes. You will also be invited to visit the restaurant, where the menu has a lot of Indian and imported drinks, as well as original cocktails.





Hotel Location

Hotel Longuinhos Beach Resort 3 * is located on one of the best beaches in all of Goa - Colva. Also, after making a 10-minute journey by bus, you can find yourself in a huge shopping center where you can buy a wide variety of goods at affordable prices. Here is the Margao railway station, from which you can quickly get to other cities and get acquainted with their attractions. Half an hour away is Dabolim Airport, and after 45 minutes, you will be taken to the village of Panaji.





Guest apartments

To accommodate guests in Goa, Longuinhos Beach Resort 3 * hotel has 50 rooms, which can be divided into the following groups:

  • Apartments of the "standard" category are the most numerous. They can be characterized by a fairly modest interior and compact size. This is just one room with a bathroom.
  • Guests are invited to stay in suites. Here you are equipped with two comfortable rooms, one of which is a bedroom, and the second is a living room.
  • The best apartments in this hotel are deluxe rooms. They differ not only in a huge space, but also in an elegant interior, which is dominated by wooden pieces of furniture, as well as traditional national decor.




Facilities in the hotel apartments

In all apartments of Longuinhos Beach Resort 3 * resort (India, Colva) at your disposal are the following amenities:

  • each room has a balcony, from which, without leaving your room, you can admire the green tropical garden or the surf and breathe in the fresh air;
  • large modern TV: you can watch many satellite channels, as well as video from external media;
  • individual air conditioning is responsible for ensuring a comfortable microclimate in your room;
  • for round-the-clock communication with the reception, as well as making direct international calls, there is a telephone in the room;
  • the bathroom adjacent to the apartment has a shower, hairdryer and a full range of bath and cosmetic accessories.




The infrastructure of the resort

The hotel Longuinhos Beach Resort 3 * (Colva) for guests developed the following infrastructure:

  • the hotel has a huge green garden where you can admire exotic plants and flowers, as well as listen to the singing of tropical birds;
  • a large and clean pool is open year round due to the hot Indian climate;
  • You can taste the best Indian dishes in the restaurant that operates in this hotel;
  • You can enjoy refreshing drinks and tropical cocktails in the cozy bar, where you can also watch sports matches;
  • the reception is open around the clock, so you can contact the hotel staff at any time with your questions and emerging problems;
  • there is a currency exchange office in the lobby (a fee is charged);
  • if you want to see the surrounding attractions, then you should definitely use the services of a tour desk;
  • You can hand over your luggage to the left luggage room so that it does not occupy the free space of your apartments;
  • if you got your clothes dirty or wrinkled on the way, then the laundry staff will help you quickly solve this problem;
  • the hotel has several rooms that are specially equipped for banquets, as well as business events;
  • at the reception you can deposit your valuables in a safe;
  • You can buy souvenirs in a small shop on the hotel territory;
  • tourists have the opportunity to rent a vehicle;
  • The hotel has wireless internet, but use of it is possible only at an additional cost.




Positive reviews

We can say that tourists on the positive side characterize the Longuinhos Beach Resort 3 * hotel (Goa). Reviews are full of friendly comments and vivid impressions:

  • the whole appearance of the hotel, as well as the work of the staff shows the desire of the hotel to earn the love of tourists and be awarded a higher status;
  • the territory is very clean and green: employees clean daily, and also cut and water the plants;
  • the hotel is located very close to the beach (it is literally a couple of minutes of leisurely walking);
  • the staff is very friendly and polite towards tourists, and also responds with lightning speed to all comments, complaints and requests;
  • hotel staff carefully monitor the cleanliness: they wash not only the floors, but even the walls;
  • daily maids bring two small bottles of drinking water, even if previous supplies have not been exhausted;
  • three times a week musicians and singers who give live concerts are invited to the hotel restaurant;
  • in the evening and at night the hotel territory and its garden are very beautifully illuminated;
  • if two bottles of drinking water are not enough, then at the first request they will bring you more (absolutely free).




Negative comments

Of the negative comments and comments of tourists, the following points are especially worth noting:

  • the interior of the rooms is already quite worn, so it would not hurt to carry out at least cosmetic repairs;
  • in the closet there are no hangers, no hooks, no shelves, so it is better to take the trempels with you;
  • all hotel rooms, regardless of category, are almost the same both externally and in terms of equipment (cost increases are mainly due to sea views);
  • the hotel does not give out beach towels;
  • the food is quite monotonous, in addition, too many spices are put in the dishes;
  • the cafe plays music very loudly even at night, so the rooms are just a terrible noise;
  • in the vicinity of the hotel everything is very dirty and groomed, and every morning everything is smoky due to the fact that the locals burn dry branches and palm leaves.
