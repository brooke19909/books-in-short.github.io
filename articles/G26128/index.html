Smartpolis coupon

This product is distinguished by the ability to generate annual income when all the conditions of the contract are met. Choosing a "Smartpolis" coupon, the client invests funds for 5 years. In the event that an increase in the value of the shares did not occur in the first year, the client can receive coupons. The program provides for two of their types:

  • investment, which is provided to the client after a year with an increase in the value of shares;
  • guaranteed, which is payable regardless of external factors (income is 0.1% of the deposit amount).

If the value of the shares has not increased over the year, and in the next period there has been a sharp increase, the client may receive a double investment coupon.

Advantages of the coupon "Smartpolis"

If the value of the shares has risen, the investor receives benefits, for example:

  • The client can expect a 100% deposit return.
  • Throughout the entire period of the investment agreement, a tax privilege on deduction is applied.
  • At the time of conclusion of the contract, the percentage of the investment coupon is established.
  • If the client dies during the term of the contract, then the beneficiary can receive all the money. At the same time, the final payments are not subject to taxation.
