Paradise Beach Resort 4 (Tanzania / Zanzibar Island): description and reviews

If you want to relax in paradise, where you can bask under the warm and gentle rays of the sun, splash around in the turquoise waters of the ocean, fall asleep under the whisper of palm twigs, and wake up from the cheerful twittering of strange birds, you should definitely come to Zanzibar. There are a lot of hotels here, from very cheap guest houses to chic spa complexes. We offer you to stay in a very comfortable hotel of medium price category Paradise Beach Resort 4 *. An interesting fact - in Zanzibar, the stars themselves award the hotels to the owners, so their number does not always correspond to the quality. Regarding Paradise Beach Resort, you can be absolutely sure that he is given four stars honestly. Here, tourists are waiting for excellent rooms, quality food, excellent service. The owner of this establishment, Senor Alejandro lives in one of the rooms, eats what the cook prepares for the guests, so there is always control over the taste and freshness of dishes. With tourists, Senor Alejandro is friendly and sociable. You can always turn to him and quickly resolve any issue.

Location

In a picturesque place of Zanzibar Island , where there is white sand, palm trees and turquoise waters of the ocean, there is Paradise Beach Resort 4 *. Tanzania, more precisely, its coastline from the island is located only 40 km. If you look at the map, you can see that Zanzibar rises from the water almost parallel to the continent, stretching from the southeast to northwest. Its western side is washed by the Zanzibar Strait, and the eastern Indian Ocean. Approximately in the center of this part of the coast there is a large bay, on the banks of which the relatively large city of Kwaka Town has grown. 7 km from it, Uroa Beach stretches with a wide hundred-kilometer ribbon. This is where the hotel we are describing is located. In walking distance from it there are only small fishing villages, so the rest of the guests is perfectly calm and measured. Here you will find a special life, completely unlike the mainland, untouched by man natural massifs, beauty, purity and serenity.



How to get there

You can arrive in different ways at Paradise Beach Resort 4 *. Zanzibar has its own international airport named after Amani Karume. His second name is Kisonuni. Unfortunately, not all airlines have flights here from Russia. If you fly with Flydubai, there will be only one connection in Dubai, with Qatar Airways there is also one connection in Doha. If you use the services of other airlines flying to Tanzania, there will be two transfers - in Dubai and in Dar es Salaam or in Nairobi. In all cases, you will have to wait from 5 to 12 hours to expect the desired flight at the airports. Arriving at Zanzibar, getting to the hotel is already easy. It is most convenient to take advantage of the transfer provided by travel agencies where tickets were purchased. Independent tourists can take a taxi. You will have to pay about $ 80 for it. Drive from the airport to the hotel a little less than 40 km. In time it takes half an hour.

Description of the hotel and territory

Paradise Beach Resort 4 * began to function in 2009. Its name "Paradise Coast" is fully true. The territory it occupies is not very large, only 2 hectares, but it is unusually beautiful, lawns, flowerbeds are broken all around, benches are installed, hammocks are suspended. In the shade of palm trees and surrounded by flowers unusual for the Russian eye, there are pretty buildings and bungalows under reed roofs.

Near the houses there are two swimming pools, one large, the other small, a hydromassage bathtub, which can be used for free, is installed slightly under the African-style canopy. In the public area there is a bar stylized as an exotic gazebo and a small shop. Free parking is available at the main entrance. Please note that there are several hotels in Zanzibar whose name includes the words "Paradise Beach", so sometimes there are confusion in the description. Our hotel is located almost on the shore of the vast ocean. From some of his bungalows to the sand line is only a couple of meters.

Infrastructure

In the central building of the hotel in the spacious lobby, decorated with exotic elements, Paradise Beach Resort 4 * is the reception. All the staff speak excellent English. Here you can rent a car, exchange currency, call a doctor, buy an excursion tour, hand over things for washing, exchange a beach towel, get unlimited drinking water for free, solve all current issues. In the hotel you can not only have a good rest, but also work fruitfully. To do this, there is a conference room equipped with modern equipment. And for those who decide to hold a wedding in this fabulous corner of the Earth or celebrate a family celebration, a chic banquet hall is provided.

Leisure for adults

The Paradise Beach Resort 4 * hotel is focused on a quiet harmonious vacation. Guests here can spend time at the pools around which sun loungers and umbrellas are installed. The water in the pools is always clean, beach towels are changed by the reception staff upon request. There is a bar nearby.



For sports fans, there is a small fitness room on site, which can be practiced for free. There is a massage parlor nearby for relaxation lovers.

In the evenings in the restaurant and on the veranda of the hotel entertainment programs, performances by artists, interesting shows are held. According to the reviews of our tourists, you need to pay for them in the course of action.

Leisure for children

Not a lot of services provides Paradise Beach Resort 4 * for its little tourists. For them, a children's section is equipped in the pool, and in the rooms, at the request of the parents, the cots are installed for the kids. For children under 1 year they are free, for the rest you need to pay 27.5 cu per day. In the evenings for kids, a mini-disco and a short entertainment program are held. There is no playground in the territory yet; there is no children's menu in the restaurant.

Beach

Holidays in Zanzibar largely depend on which side of the island you chose the hotel. In the west, in view of the fact that there is only 40 km to the mainland of the water surface, there are almost never tangible tides. On the east, "looking" into the vast ocean, the ebbs and flows are very significant. They are up to 4 times a day, with water leaving the coast for 2 km. Especially tangible water movements in the area of ‚Äã‚ÄãUroa beach. For this reason, it cannot be said that a beach holiday at the Paradise Beach Resort (Uroa) 4 stars is very successful, although the hotel is located almost on the beach. It is very good to sunbathe here, since the sand on the beach is white and very soft, like flour, and umbrellas and sunbeds are always available, and they are free. If the tide is caught, the entry into the water on the beach at the hotel is very even, the depth does not start from the shore itself, which is especially appreciated by vacationers with young children.

Adults and during high tide are not very comfortable relaxing on this beach, because there are too many algae. Some tourists think that they are being brought in from the ocean, but this is only partially true. In fact, in the Uroa region, the locals have whole plantations of these algae. They grow them and sell them in restaurants. Our tourists can even organize excursions to these underwater plantations.

During low tide, the hotel provides free transport to the nearby beach, 3.5 km from Uroa. Here, water level fluctuations are weaker, and the ocean is a little cleaner.

Those who rented a car do not depend on the quirks of nature, because any beaches of Zanzibar are accessible to them.



Leisure outside the hotel

To diversify your vacation, there are a lot of opportunities for the guests of Paradise Beach Resort 4 *. Zanzibar is an unusually beautiful and distinctive island. The most popular tourist destination here is Stone Town (Stone Town) and its so-called House of Miracles. This is the palace of the former sultan of the country and at the same time a museum. The unusual carved doors standing in almost every house attract attention in this city. In addition to Stone Town, tourists are always invited to visit the famous Josani forest with its unique plants and animals, spice plantations, the Tomb island, where flying dogs live, the Prison island to stare there on huge turtles. It is very interesting to do snorkeling and diving in Zanzibar, because the underwater inhabitants of coastal waters are unusually beautiful.

In addition, tourists can rent a car or order a taxi and go to the west coast, where there are dozens of cafes, discos, bars and nightclubs. There, a noisy fun life boils almost until the morning.

Additional Information

Paradise Beach Resort 4 * is perfect for those who dream of a relaxing holiday, away from the noise of tourist centers, night clubs and discos. On the coast where it is located, there are not even shops, only small kiosks with a limited selection of goods.

The hotel rooms are cozy and comfortable, but in them, as on the entire coast, they often turn off the lights. This entails turning off the air conditioners and the water supply.

The hotel allows pets.

Payment for services here is accepted in cash. Reservations can be paid by credit card.
