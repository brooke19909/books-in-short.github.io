Big headphones: pros and cons, the best models

Headphones and music are the best way to pass the time on the road and relax after a hard day. Someone prefers vacuum ("plugs"), and someone chooses full-size models. Given their abundance in the market, it is very difficult for the average user to decide. In addition, most buyers want to get a good sound, giving their money. Of course, no one wants to pay money, but in the end "enjoy" a low-quality product. Therefore, in the article we will analyze the best large headphones and their advantages over the "gags".

An excellent model of headphones from a well-known manufacturer that will definitely satisfy the demands of audiophiles. FIDELIO X2 fixes the flaws of the previous version, adds the ability to change the ear pads. The main plus of the headphones is a deep, clear sound. They cope with almost any frequency, give a good bass and reliable sound of musical instruments. It is better to listen from a personal computer or a high-quality player in order to feel all their power.

We used high-quality manufacturing materials, which is not surprising for the flagship model. Thanks to them, these large headphones sit well, do not crush. The only negative that can be distinguished is the open type. You hear others well, and they know about your musical preferences.

BEATS STUDIO 2

Great model from a well-known brand. They can be connected via a wireless interface, which makes them a great option for use with a smartphone.

Of course, the first thing you pay attention to is appearance. And he is amazing. In principle, as with other Beats models, Studio 2 is distinguished by its versatility - they can be used without and with a wire. Attention is drawn to the generous equipment, which makes it clear that the user bought an expensive thing. Have these big earphones and good noise reduction.

Of the minuses - the price. Despite all the advantages, the cost of the model is too high, which is facilitated by the brand and proper marketing of the company.

The named company is able to please music lovers by releasing cheap headphones with good sound and design. They are made full-size, with a closed design. Despite the budget, they sound good.
