Shockproof smartphones: reviews, review. The best shockproof and waterproof smartphone on Android

We are used to the fact that smartphones are fragile graceful everyday helpers that require careful attitude and additional protection in some situations. For example, you should use such a device in a case so that in case of a fall its internal components are not damaged; and in order to maintain the integrity of the fragile touch screen, you need to use a protective film, which serves as another layer, taking all the scratches onto you. So, most users manage to maintain both the appearance of the device and its functionality at the factory level.

However, there is a category of smartphones for which all these and other measures are not required. These are telephones, which, in essence, are protected, protected from any possible damage. They are also called ‚Äúshockproof smartphones‚Äù, because such a phone, despite its functionality, is not afraid of falls from a height or any other mechanical influences. In today's article, we will consider just a few of these devices so that the interested reader knows what they are and what they are.

Design

Immediately I want to describe the concept that manufacturers who manufacture shockproof smartphones adhere to. Typically, such devices are equipped with a high degree of protection, represented by a powerful glass, reinforced housing, as well as a rubberized shell that can prevent moisture from entering the housing. Due to this, most phones in this category have a somewhat bulky and rather large appearance. For example, using them in everyday life will be somewhat inconvenient (especially for girls).

However, not all shockproof smartphones are like that. There are also gadgets with a sophisticated design and easily fit in your pocket. True, their degree of protection without a dense layer of plastic or rubber is seriously reduced.





Functionality

As a rule, shockproof smartphones (reviews of them confirm this) do not have such a productive technical stuffing as the usual devices that we use in everyday life. The reason for this, apparently, is the purpose of the phone: as a rule, protected devices are needed in ‚Äúfield conditions‚Äù, where high-speed processor operation or the presence of a high-precision camera are not required. Such phones are required, for example, to be able to determine the location using GPS, to communicate via the network in areas where the signal is weak; support for the work of two SIM cards, the presence of memory cards and other things will not hurt.

In view of this, protected shockproof smartphones are equipped with not the fastest processor, a small amount of RAM, not a new version of the operating system.

Battery

An important aspect of the functioning of any such device is the availability of a volume battery. This follows from the needs that stand in front of a phone of this class - the need to hold a charge as long as possible in the absence of any power sources. This is logical, because in nature (camping, hunting or traveling) there is no way to connect the phone to a charger, which requires a powerful battery. As a rule, developers take this into account when bundling their shockproof smartphones.
