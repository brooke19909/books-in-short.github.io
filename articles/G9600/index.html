Guest House "Bright" Adler: overview, features and reviews

Adler is a dream for many Russians. Warm sea, golden sand, ahead of vacation and pleasure. But before you go on vacation, you need to choose a good hotel and book a ticket. Adler is part of Sochi resort, which opens up a lot of opportunities for a pleasant pastime. Here you will find a well-developed infrastructure and many options for rental housing. One of the popular guest houses is the Svetly Guesthouse in Adler.



Where is?

Experienced travelers note that this is a cozy and very beautiful corner of the Black Sea coast, where you want to spend every vacation. You are waiting for a compact, clean and picturesque Adler. Svetly Guest House is just a two-minute walk to the beach. I would also like to note the proximity of such facilities as parks and playgrounds. This is especially true for those who are going to rest with children. And to the Olympic facilities literally at hand. Of course, it’s taking too long to walk, but you can get by bus or trolley quickly and with minimal inconvenience.



In the territory

Svetly Guest House in Adler is completely fenced, which is convenient for families with children. For the convenience of guests, there are relaxation areas with a gazebo and sets of summer furniture. The yard itself is buried in greenery and flowers, which pleases the eye. The second plus of the abundance of greenery is salvation from the summer heat. At the height of July, the air temperature here exceeds 30 degrees. For younger guests there is a playground.

Modern technologies did not pass by the Svetly guest house in Adler. The entire territory is served by wireless Internet, so you can take laptops and tablets with you. For lovers of barbecue on the territory there is a barbecue and skewers. Most tourists prefer to go to cafes or dining rooms, or dine on excursions.



Main advantages

Analyzing information and reviews, you can highlight the main points that speak for the choice of this hotel as a place to spend a vacation.

  • Svetly Guest House in Adler has been operating since 2010. This company is not a newbie in its business and knows exactly how to make your vacation unforgettable.
  • Guests will find comfortable accommodation and a pleasant stay, as well as affordable prices. Compared to other hotels in the region, they are considered low.
  • Professionalism. Those who have been here once are ready to come again and again.
  • Friendly service, good-natured atmosphere and peace.

Agree, this is quite a lot.



Number of rooms

First of all, a person is interested in the conditions in which he will spend his leisure time. To appreciate its comfort, we need feedback. Svetly Guest House in Adler is ready to offer comfortable and spacious rooms to accommodate others. Each of them has modern furniture and a fan, air conditioning, internet, satellite TV, refrigerator and balcony, toilet and shower. All rooms are constantly supplied with hot and cold water, as evidenced by reviews.

Types of rooms

To calculate your family budget, you need to know the prices in advance. Guest House "Svetly" in Adler offers its customers double and triple rooms. This is a room of 20 m2, in which there are main places and one additional, it is most often used for children. The room has a TV and refrigerator, Wi-Fi. The cost of living in apartments per day per person is 1000 rubles in the low season, and 1800 rubles. - in high season. Prices need to be checked with the administrator.

For a large family, a standard that is designed for four tourists is suitable. This is one room with an area of ​​22 m2, with air conditioning, shower and toilet. The cost of renting a room is from 1000 to 2800 rubles, depending on the season.



Reviews

You can find quite a few reviews that describe a vacation on the Black Sea. Most emphasize that you need to book a room in advance. At the height of the season, places are usually not free. Having booked a room and leaving an advance payment, you can be sure that they will be waiting for you on the appointed day. Even from the gate you can note almost perfect cleanliness.

Hotel address: 180/1 Enlightenment Street. On the map and in reality, it is the first from the sea. It turns out that the beach is located right around the bend. On the way to the beach, various excursion offers await you. There are many entertainments in the resort town, but the prices for everything are quite tangible, especially in the high season. The hotel has helpful staff, whose care is felt every day. Delicious breakfasts, fruit in the room, a car called on time - all this forms great memories. Most of the guests who have been here several times have noted that they are happy to return here again next year.

Reason for early booking

Guest House Svetly is one of the best guest houses on the territory. Small, clean, comfortable and bright, most guests enjoy it. People note that this is an opportunity for a comfortable stay and quality service at affordable prices. Discounts can be obtained individually, there are separate options for the group. Particularly beneficial is the long-term reservation of the entire hotel. Terms are negotiated directly with the administrator. To do this, you can call him.

The hotel is located in a place with well-developed infrastructure. Walking distance has everything you need. And in the guest house, friendly hosts are waiting for you, who will try to do everything possible to make your vacation unforgettable.

Negative reviews

Svetly Guest House in Adler occupies a leading position in the rating, there are never any empty seats, so finding them was very difficult. However, there were cases that the hotel closed at the end of the season, but the administrator forgot to remove the advertisement from the site. As a result, people came and were faced with the fact that the hotel was closed. But in this case, you can easily return your money, and to place an emergency to find another option. Fortunately, there are plenty of them out of season. Such cases are very rare; during the seven years of the hotel's operation, a similar story happened once. Of course, people were upset and justifiably considered the administrator guilty of a spoiled vacation. But if you analyze all the reviews, it turns out that there are few negative ones. Therefore, you can safely book a ticket and hit the road.
