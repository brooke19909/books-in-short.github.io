What is a remote switch?



This is the name of a device that can pick up signals sent from the remote control without using conventional conductors. A remote switch allows you to control operating modes without having to approach a specific point in the home - that is, from anywhere.

Principle of operation



Depending on what the functioning of such devices is based on, there are two main schemes for mounting a light switch with remote control:

  1. Having motion sensors. Such devices are based on infrared ports and turn on only if someone is present in the room.
  2. Possessing sound perception. They react to certain mechanical vibrations of the air (a specific word, click, pop and the like).

Since there are many different implementation schemes, exclusively general features of the construction and operation of the device in general will be considered. The mechanism includes a steel core located inside the coil. Thanks to it, a special contact device is activated by means of which the connection and opening of the power circuit is carried out. Efficiency does not depend on the state of the start button - after all, the mechanical component reliably supports the power supply of the circuit.

As two control links, a radio signal receiver and a mobile remote control are used, with which commands are given (for convenience, you can make or buy a special holder for it). The radius of their interaction can reach 20 meters in closed rooms, and in the open air - generally 100 meters. If we have a factory remote switch, then we should not be surprised if it has the ability to control the brightness of lighting (which positively affects the life of the lamps).

Often, it may also be provided to reproduce the last set system parameters. Also, many models include the ability to periodically turn on / off the light to create the effect of presence. This feature will be extremely convenient and useful when leaving home for a long time. Developed and marketed devices that have a touch control type. But the disadvantage of such remotes (as well as phones and tablets, which may act in their role) is the rather low sensitivity. Another important advantage that almost any remote switch has is the ability to control several power grids at the same time.

Advantages of remote switches



It is worth focusing on the advantages of these devices:

  1. Convenience of operation. The remote switch that works on infrared ports can be controlled from any remote control.
  2. Easy to install. Any person who has had successful experience with the network can carry out the installation of this device.
  3. Extensive control and adjustment of the illumination of individual rooms, as well as the ability to turn on devices automatically at the right time. Remote lighting switches are not complex devices, and with experience and dexterity, they can be seriously modified.
  4. A wide variety of patterns. You can choose a device that will satisfy its technical and price characteristics.
  5. Power saving Many models provide automatic shutdown during continuous burning for more than 12 hours. Therefore, when traveling for a long time, a solid account will not run for a randomly left light.
