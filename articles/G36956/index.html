We choose mobile air conditioners. Bork is a great option



Choosing a system for cooling a room, one cannot but pay attention to mobile air conditioners. Bork, DeLonghi, Samsung, Daewoo, Ballu and many other large companies have mastered the production of such units for air cooling. Each corporation has released a line of devices in order to satisfy the needs of everyone who wants to get such a device in their home.

Operating principle

Mobile air conditioners (Bork, Daewoo, Ballu, etc.) work on the same principle as conventional air conditioners (air cooling with refrigerant), but are considered not stationary, since they do not need to be installed. That is, at any time, you can easily roll the device to a new place or take it with you to the country.



The peculiarity of the operation of any of these devices is that during cooling the working engine emits heat, which undoubtedly reduces all efforts to zero. For the removal of warm air from the engine, manufacturers have provided a special air vent, which is a banal corrugated sleeve.

Using

The next point in the conversation is the practical side of the issue, namely use. It is quite simple: turn it on and enjoy. You can enable it in different ways. Some mobile air conditioners (Bork, for example) have remote controls, and there are those samples that are equipped only with a control panel. The degree of cooling is different for everyone, but in principle, if you correctly calculate the power depending on the volume of the future room, then you will be provided with coolness. The only thing that can really obscure the opportunity to enjoy is the noise level. The working engine of the device makes itself felt. We can say that this is the only serious minus. The plus is a small price (relative to split systems that perform, in fact, the same functions).





Design

The design of the units is diverse, manufacturers have developed more than one project, allowing to satisfy different tastes. For example, the Bork-Y500 mobile air conditioner has an attractive appearance, is able to cool a rather large room (up to 26 sq. M). During operation, the room air is cleaned by means of a built-in ionizer. There are functions to drain or humidify the room. Undoubtedly, heating mode and remote control will delight future owners.

This unit is considered an improved model of the previous generation. Neem was the air conditioner mobile Bork-Y501. The new model tried to eradicate the errors of the previous version. In particular, a mode appeared that made the device run quieter, and Heat Pump mode allows you not to worry about accumulated condensate, the unit uses this water for internal needs. These representatives of the company's products will be an excellent ally in the struggle for comfortable living in any room.
