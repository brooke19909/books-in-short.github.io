La Vie Hotel 3 is the best place to stay!

Description : La Vie Hotel 3 belongs to the category of small but very cozy Turkish hotels. Its location is quite successful - it is the central part of the village of Kemer, preferred by many tourists.





It has everything you need for a full beach holiday. Namely, the maximum proximity to both the main airport of the country (55 km) and the center of the famous coastal city of Antalya (45 km), the distance to the sea is only some 150 meters, which means that even the most exhausting tourist is no problem can cross the road in a matter of minutes and end up on the beach.

Rooms : The last major overhaul of the building was carried out relatively recently, in 2004, and today La Vie Hotel 3 is represented by one three-story building and adjacent territory, whose area is about 1000 m2. The hotel is currently represented by 25 rooms of a standard type, which are designed for two adult travelers.

Each living room is equipped with powerful air conditioning, a modern TV with the possibility of free viewing of Russian-language television channels and a telephone. A small safe for storing especially valuable personal belongings and documents is available at an additional cost.

The bathroom is equipped with a modern shower, so the pressure of the water flow and the direction of the stream can be individually adjusted.

The floor has a warm and safe ceramic coating.

Meals : The hotel operates on different systems of providing meals. It depends on the conditions of the purchased tour.

During breakfast, lunch and dinner, tourists are waiting for superbly prepared dishes of both local and overseas cuisines. And throughout the day, you can grab a bite to eat and have a drink in the pool bar.

Beach : La Vie Hotel 3 is located on the second coastline. However, this in no way can serve as the basis for the occurrence of any problems or inconveniences.

The thing is that in Turkey, and especially in the more hilly Kemer, hotels are located at a fairly close distance to each other, so the distance from the beach area is a little more than 150 meters.

The beach is paid. Urban. Presented by the sandy and pebble coast.

Additional information : Towels at La Vie Hotel are changed daily during cleaning, and linen is changed upon request of the guests or once every two to three days.

The ability to use mattresses, sun loungers and parasols by the pool is provided free of charge.

A team of professional animators is ready to entertain guests throughout the day (on the beach and by the pool), and in the evening directly at the hotel. Dances, sports games and competitions, contests and classes in fitness and aerobics - everyone will be able to choose something for themselves.



Reviews : La Vie Hotel 3 ... Reviews for this hotel are the warmest and most enthusiastic. All this is largely due to both a good location and the excellent work of the staff.

Former guests note the coziness and comfort of living at La Vie Hotel 3. For example, the fact that each room has a spacious balcony cannot but please the vacationers. In the evening, tired of the beach and dizzying shopping, it is recommended to enjoy a cup of tea or coffee sitting on the balcony overlooking the city or the sea.
