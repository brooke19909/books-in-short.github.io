Residential complex "Nevskaya Zvezda", St. Petersburg: description

Active construction of new houses is ongoing in the Nevsky District of St. Petersburg. Among the new buildings is the Nevskaya Zvezda residential complex. About the developer

The IPS company has been operating in the real estate market for quite some time and has an excellent reputation. The developer submits all its facilities on time, as a rule, no claims are received from customers. In addition, the company uses an integrated approach in its activities, in connection with which all work, from the creation of the project to the commissioning of houses, is carried out without involving third-party organizations. This explains the high pace of construction and the absence of shortcomings in the delivery of facilities.



Unfortunately, the exception was the LCD "Nevskaya Zvezda". The IPS developer began to erect the residential complex literally before the economic crisis began, in connection with which the deadlines for commissioning the facilities had to be reviewed. However, work on construction sites did not stop for a minute, today their progress is fully consistent with the schedule, and potential buyers hope that all houses will be completed on time.

About the complex

Nevskaya Zvezda is a residential complex that will include five multi-sectional houses of different floors. In this regard, the construction progress was decided to be carried out in stages. To date, one house for 404 apartments has been commissioned, work is underway on the construction of buildings of the second and third stage. Construction is carried out using frame-block technology. In parallel with residential buildings, infrastructure facilities are being built. The construction of the second-stage high-rise building is planned to be completed by the end of 2016, and the third-stage house will be commissioned in 2019.



Already today it is possible to purchase on an equity basis one of the apartments that have attracted attention in the building that is being prepared for delivery. Which is very beneficial for potential customers, since at this stage the apartments are sold at the lowest cost.

Infrastructure and ecology of the area

Nevskaya Zvezda is a residential complex characterized by an extremely attractive location. It is being built almost on the border with the suburbs, in connection with which a forest and a river are located not far from the buildings. What today is very valuable for a resident of a metropolis. In addition, in the region where the construction of buildings is underway, there is no industrial production, so from the point of view of the environmental situation, the situation in the Nevsky district is very favorable.

Not far from the residential complex is located the metro station "Proletarskaya", which can be reached in just five minutes, which is another undoubted advantage. Near the houses there are bus stops from which buses and fixed-route taxis leave regularly, so residents of the complex can easily reach any area of ‚Äã‚Äãthe city. Motorists, on the other hand, can use Obukhovskaya Oblast Avenue to get to the center, or take the exits to the roundabout to go onto the autobahns of the Leningrad Region.



The Nevskaya Zvezda residential complex is located in an area with an already existing social, shopping and entertainment infrastructure, so residents of high-rise buildings will definitely not experience a shortage of its facilities. Near the houses there are schools, and gardens, and supermarkets and much more. In addition, commercial buildings will also be located in the buildings on the first floors, so that you can get all the necessary services after the construction is completed literally without leaving home.

Since the Nevskaya Zvezda residential complex belongs to the economy class segment, it mainly consists of studios and one-room apartments. Prices

The Nevskaya Zvezda residential complex offers potential buyers to purchase housing at fairly affordable prices. The cost of one square meter is about 77 thousand rubles.
