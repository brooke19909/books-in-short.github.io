Hotel VDB Nha Trang Hotel 4 *, Vietnam, Nha Trang: description, features and reviews

If you are planning a vacation you want to spend in Nha Trang (Vietnam), the VDB Nha Trang Hotel 4 * hotel can be a very suitable option for a comfortable and affordable stay.



Location

This 4-star hotel is located in the Nha Trang residential area, across the road from Pham Van Dong Municipal Beach. The distance to the city center is only three kilometers. You can get here by public transport or on foot. The nearest airport is 40 kilometers from Nha Trang. Therefore, after arriving on the road to the hotel, it will take you no more than forty minutes.

Hotel description, photo

The hotel in question was opened just a few years ago. It consists of a large 16-story building, which houses residential rooms and infrastructure. The hotel has practically no territory of its own.

As for the housing stock, it is represented by 150 rooms of the categories Superior, Deluxe, Senior Deluxe, Executive Suite and Presidential Suite. Regardless of the type, all apartments have a modern TV, air conditioning, telephone, electric kettle and tea and coffee making facilities, a minibar, a safe, a bathroom with a hairdryer. In-room guests can use Wi-Fi for free.





Also, hotel guests are provided with bathrobes, slippers and necessary bath accessories (toothpaste and brush, cotton swabs, gels, shampoos, soap, etc.). Their stocks are replenished as necessary. Rooms are cleaned every day. The maids renew their towels and bed linen several times a week.

Infrastructure

Reception at VDB Nha Trang Hotel 4 * is open 24 hours a day. So at any time, day or night, you can contact the administrator on the issue you are interested in. There is also wireless internet access throughout the hotel. The hotel has a conference room, laundry service, luggage storage, outdoor pool, spa and gym. For the convenience of guests there is an elevator and parking.

The hotel has two restaurants. One of them, ‚ÄúBlue Ocean‚Äù, is located on the second floor and offers everyone to taste Asian and European dishes. Another restaurant is located on the 14th floor. It offers stunning views of the surroundings. The hotel also has three bars: a lobby bar, a poolside bar and a Sky Bar, located on the 15th floor with panoramic sea views. Visiting restaurants and bars of the hotel is not included in the price (with the exception of breakfasts organized in buffet style).

VDB Nha Trang Hotel 4 * (Vietnam): reviews of tourists from Russia

Many modern travelers know very well how important it is to be responsible in choosing a hotel to stay during your holidays in a particular country. After all, impressions from the rest depend on this for the most part. Therefore, tourists today, when planning a trip, try to collect the maximum information about a particular hotel in the place of interest to them. At the same time, they not only get acquainted with the official description of the hotel, but also look for reviews of real people who happened to be here recently.





Such an approach allows you to make as close as possible to the real state of affairs the idea of ‚Äã‚Äãwhat awaits tourists here. Therefore, we bring to your attention the generalized comments of our compatriots regarding their stay at VDB Nha Trang Hotel 4 *. Reviews about the hotel, I must say, the vast majority are positive. So, Russian tourists were satisfied with the living conditions, service and price. But we learn about everything in more detail.

Number of rooms

In general, our compatriots were satisfied with the accommodation in this hotel. According to them, the rooms here are freshly renovated, bright and comfortable. They are small, but there was enough space for everyone, especially since most tourists come to their apartments only to change clothes, as well as in the evening before going to bed. Guests note that the room has everything you need for a comfortable stay - bathrobes, slippers, a hairdryer, toothbrushes, paste, soap, shower gel, shampoo and much more. At the same time, stocks of bathroom accessories are replenished as they are used.





According to tourists, the use of a minibar is paid. However, maids bring several bottles of drinking water every day. It's free. Drinking tap water is not recommended here. Many guests are advised to choose rooms located on the upper floors, if possible. So you will be provided with a magnificent view from the window. The only minus of some guests considered the lack of a balcony in the rooms where they could dry things. However, this did not become any serious problem for the guests.

Check-in, cleaning

Tourists leaving their reviews regarding VDB Nha Trang Hotel 4 * were very pleased with the quick check-in. So, according to them, upon arrival at the hotel they were offered a soft drink and invited to sit in comfortable armchairs near the reception. The key to the apartment could be obtained literally in 10-15 minutes. Moreover, the hotel employee escorts guests who have just arrived in the room designed for them and carries the luggage. Also, some time after checking in, they will bring you a plate of fruit. By the way, maids leave fruits in the room a couple of times a week. In addition, if your birthday falls during your stay at this hotel, then you can count on congratulations and a cake from the administration.





As for cleaning, in general, there were no complaints about its quality. According to some of our compatriots, it would be possible to carry out wet cleaning more often. But most of the guests were quite satisfied with the work of the maids.

Nutrition

According to travelers, the price of accommodation at the VDB Nha Trang Hotel 4 * (Nha Trang, Vietnam) includes breakfast only. In general, according to tourists, this approach is typical for hotels in this country. Our compatriots were very pleased with the breakfasts. Judging by their comments, it was always possible to choose food to your taste - differently prepared eggs, delicious pastries, fruits, vegetables, sausages, cereals, soups and much more. However, some guests did not really like the fact that instant coffee is served here, and tea is brewed from bags.

During the day it was possible to eat both in the hotel restaurant and in cafes located outside. According to our compatriots, in the vicinity of the hotel there are a couple of excellent places where you can try delicious dishes of national cuisine at a very reasonable price.

Sea

Vacationers note that the beach from the hotel can be reached in just five minutes. However, you must cross a busy road along the way. According to tourists, a hotel employee helps to do this. As for the beach itself, it is public. However, on its part VDB Nha Trang Hotel 4 * places its sunbeds and umbrellas, which guests can use for free. According to travelers, it is advisable to go to the beach no later than nine in the morning. Since at a later time all the sun loungers of the hotel may be busy. Here, on the card, towels are given out, which must be passed upon leaving the beach. Taking them to the room is not allowed. So, as our compatriots note, if you plan to visit other beaches during your holidays, you need to grab towels from your home or purchase them already in Vietnam.





As for the sea, most tourists were satisfied with it. According to them, there are often waves, but everyone really enjoyed swinging at them. The beach itself is sandy, quite clean.

Entertainment at the hotel

Since VDB Nha Trang Hotel 4 * (Vietnam) is a city-style hotel, it does not have its own large territory and, therefore, cannot offer any special entertainment to its guests. Nevertheless, on the third floor of the hotel building is equipped with an outdoor pool, where you can swim in fine weather. The hotel also has a gym, spa and massage services. As some guests note, they also gladly went up to the roof of the hotel, which offers stunning views of this area of ‚Äã‚ÄãNha Trang, the beach and the sea.





Travelers also recommend that you definitely go for at least a few excursions, especially if you have not been to Vietnam before.

Staff

The work of the staff as a whole, our compatriots were satisfied. According to them, the staff here (however, like almost all Vietnamese) are very friendly, smiling, always ready to listen and help. True, almost none of the hotel staff speaks Russian. Therefore, it will have to be explained in English. As a rule, for the vast majority of travelers this is not a problem.
