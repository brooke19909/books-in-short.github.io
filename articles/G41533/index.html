Overview of residential real estate in Samara: primary and secondary

The implementation of new housing in Samara has not yet been able to gain sufficient momentum. Nevertheless, secondary housing here is in much greater demand. For those who want to buy an apartment in Samara, www.bn.ru offers to understand the situation on the Samara real estate market.

Secondary housing market

The secondary market is the set of owners who want to sell their own residential real estate.

Advantages

These apartments interest potential buyers in that the transaction is conducted without intermediaries. In this case, you can always evaluate the location of the house and the area, examine the apartment carefully from the point of view of the repair carried out in it, the safety and reliability of communications, and the like. You can even talk with your neighbors to find out if the current owners had any difficulties with housing.

disadvantages
