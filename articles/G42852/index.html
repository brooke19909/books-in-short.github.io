Rope town Sky Park





In general, it should be noted that in this city there are many places that you can visit in company with small children, while receiving great and, importantly, mutual pleasure from the process itself.

So, where to go with children in Kazan? One of such charming places for spending unforgettable and vivid leisure time is the rope park Sky Park at the Lebyazhye recreation center. Here you can get all kinds of memorable impressions, completely without jeopardizing either life or health.

Immediately, we note that, despite the fact that in which company - youth, friendly or children's - you find yourself in the park, you will immediately begin to feel like real adventurers lost in the wild jungle, then climbers, and even climbers.

Kazan for children is actually a fascinating fairy tale. Rope crossings, high jumps, tightrope walking, trolling flights and so on will help to lose the feeling of reality. An exciting adventure at the very beginning can be a little frightening, but then the trials become so entertaining that with excitement you overcome puzzle after puzzle - anything to get to the very end.

The crossing is carried out at a level of 9 meters above the ground and consists of 23 stages of varying complexity. All routes are absolutely safe, so almost any person in the end will be able to pass the test, regardless of age and level of physical fitness.

During the trip, the life of visitors is constantly protected by certified safety systems of the world French brand - PETZI, and indecisive experienced instructors will come to the aid at any time.

Kazan Museum of Natural History

What else can Kazan provide for recreation? Sights, photos of which can be found in almost any guide to Russia, of course, include the Museum of Natural History.

It should be noted that it is here that almost all the information about the plant and animal world, fossils, mineral resources of the region is collected.

It was created in 2005, and its initial goal was to promote and preserve the geological heritage of the republic.

A trip to the museum will be interesting and informative for people of different ages and levels of knowledge.

On the ground floor, tourists will get acquainted with the basic concepts of astronomy and the history of our planet, and on the second floor there is a paleontological exhibition dedicated to the evolution of vertebrates.

By the way, the museum runs the Pilgrim Science Club.

Where to go with children in Kazan? To the puppet theater, of course!





The Tatar State Puppet Theater "Ekiyat" is considered the leading in the country and occupies a significant place in the cultural life of the Russian Federation. His repertoire consists of 50 productions, which are held both in Tatar and in Russian. Today, the company has 100 puppeteers.

The theater will tell the youngest spectators a variety of fairy tales and wonderful stories from the fascinating works of children's authors. Among them there are adventures of both world-famous children's heroes and completely unfamiliar.

Many different dolls are involved in the productions, from classic to the most modern.

Everyone who gets to the theater is guaranteed to be transferred to the magical world of miracles and magic.

Amusement park "Klay"





Planning a trip to the amazing city of Kazan? What to see with the children will have to be planned in advance. We would like to give you very valuable advice. On the banks of the Kazanka River there is a modern amusement park "Klay" - a place that cannot be missed.

It consists of Italian-made attractions for both family and purely children's vacations.

In the center of the park there is a 55 m high ferris wheel, which makes it possible to examine the view of the Volga River and the panorama of the city.

A cafe is constantly working on the territory of this recreation area, in which a children's menu with delicious pastries, cotton candy and corn, drinks is specially compiled for kids. For adults, the cafe offers a choice of Armenian dishes.

Every weekend there is an entertainment program with contests, playrooms for children and prize draws.

Unfortunately, the park does not work in the winter, but on any day from March to October you can have a great time.

Touching Zoo of Kazan





Planning a trip to Kazan? Attractions for children are very diverse. For example, a zoo has recently opened in a new shopping and entertainment complex.

The unusual thing is that in it you can not only watch funny animals. Without problems and absolutely free of charge, it is allowed to photograph their babies in the company of representatives of the world fauna. By the way, children will be able to touch the animals.

The zoo is presented in the form of a small village farm, where clean and well-groomed animals live: Angora and dwarf rabbits, hedgehogs, turtles, guinea pigs, pheasants, peacocks, geese, ducks, hens and Georgette's pig. At the entrance of the zoo guests are greeted by a friendly goat.

However, it is worth paying attention to the fact that visitors are allowed to feed all animals only with special food, which is sold here.

As a rule, a special paddock with tiny yellow chickens causes special admiration for the kids.

Kazan Botanical Garden

One of the favorite vacation spots of citizens and guests of Kazan is the garden, which consists of a botanical and zoological garden.

It was founded by K. F. Fuchs over 200 years ago on Lake Kaban. Interested in biology? Then you will definitely like this place! Now there are 170-year-old palm trees, rare trees and shrubs are grown, exotic plants from around the world are studied, more than a hundred species of cacti are collected and scientific research is constantly being conducted.

It is safe to say that entertainment in Kazan for children is impossible to imagine without visiting this place. Intelligent adventure games and entertainment programs are regularly held at the zoo. In this place you can ride horses or ponies, watch plenty of reptiles, mammals, birds. Zootherapy classes are constantly held, which, according to scientists, helps to treat mentally retarded children.

Recently, a contact zoo for the smallest ones, Lukomorye, has been opened in the garden.

Circus! Circus! Circus!





What else is Kazan known for? Sights, photos of which are quite common, it is impossible to imagine without a circus that has been operating for more than 100 years.

This is perhaps the most popular leisure place in the city for both kids and adults. Performances are organized year-round. Funny clowns, gymnasts, balancers, acrobats, dogs, bears, parrots, etc.

Since 1996, a circus school has been operating at the circus, which during this time has trained many world-class artists.
