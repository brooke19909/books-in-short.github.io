Yekaterinburg developers: demolition housing or fair play?





Regardless of the situation in the financial market of a country or the world, a person has always sought to acquire housing. The desire to find a "roof over your head" gave rise to the creation of a huge number of different companies offering housing services that people dream of. The cost per square meter is growing steadily, so most often we pay attention not to dreams, but to the fact that the desires as closely as possible correspond to the possibilities. The volume of the individual housing market is growing in every city. Developers in Yekaterinburg, Perm and Saratov, for example, offer interested parties to purchase both finished housing and consider projects for future buildings. Most companies around the world work on this technology.

Times and manners

Developers in Yekaterinburg and other cities of Russia are going through hard times. New laws in the field of land acquisition confuse even large and “adult” companies. So, if in Yekaterinburg a few months ago, municipal authorities dealt with the issues of delimiting land on the territory of the city, then already in May this paragraph of urban planning has been regulated by the Ministry of State Property Management. It is because of the intervention of outside forces in the process of erecting new housing that has been worked out for years, the developers of Yekaterinburg spontaneously refuse to be active at auctions.





The scandal of individual housing construction in Yekaterinburg

In 2012, many companies in this city were involved in a scandal. The project of individual housing construction (IZHS) hit the table in the prosecutor's office. The thing is that the developers of Yekaterinburg, handing over finished houses, did not commit themselves to verification of compliance with safety standards. As a result, several high-rise buildings were demolished. Part recognized as unsuitable for housing. All construction companies were divided into two camps. Some by all means strive to legalize their "offspring." Others, using sharply falling prices, are buying up and seizing hectares of space for housing, which again will be demolished. It is worth noting that the erected houses did not pass even half of the registration steps, so the city administration declared them illegal.





Affordable demolition apartments

However, numerous apartments from the developer in Yekaterinburg continue to be born at a steadily growing pace. With the same speed, the prosecutor’s office receives statements about unauthorized land seizures. Representatives of some companies agree that most of the buildings were built by negligent speculators who sought to save on materials, work and design procedures. However, there is a percentage of those developers whose buildings meet all the requirements. However, the presence of buildings in the IZH zone equates companies with violators. This issue is the fault of both the administration and construction organizations.

Moreover, the scandal continues to gain momentum. Some bold holdings perform public projects such as "Affordable New Buildings in Yekaterinburg from Developer N". However, they pay little attention to the fact that in such apartments, pre-programmed for demolition, it is unlikely that anyone will live happily ever after.
