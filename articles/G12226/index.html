Turkmen Hotel 3 - rest in one of the most picturesque Turkish areas

Description: Turkmen Hotel 3 is an excellent hotel located in one of the most picturesque Turkish areas. It is perfect for those who want to relax closer to the sea with minimal cost. An asphalt road and a small sandy strip separate it from the sea. This hotel occupies an area in the village of Obagel, which is located two kilometers from Alanya. It is separated from Antalya International Airport by a distance of 132 kilometers, which can be overcome by taxi or by intercity bus.



The Turkmen Hotel 3 building was built in 1996 and consists of one five-story building. The last repair took place recently, in 2012. The territory belonging to the hotel spreads over 3000 sq.m. It has a magnificent garden where you can take a walk before bedtime and enjoy the beauty and aroma of trees and flowers.





Rooms: The number of rooms of the Turkmen Hotel 3 is 94 standard rooms, 50 of which have views of the sea coast. The rooms are spacious, bright, decorated in soft colors. Each has a furnished balcony with beautiful views of the surrounding area. The air-conditioned rooms have a shower. All the necessary toiletries for the entire stay are free of charge. Tap water has a low salt content. There is satellite TV (1 Russian channel), a hairdryer, a minibar (empty), and a work desk. There is no telephone. A cot is available on request, and an iron is provided. There is a service such as waking up by phone.

Pets are not allowed. Disabled rooms are not available.



Meals: Turkmen Hotel 3 provides AI-type meals to its guests. A table is served in the main restaurant of the hotel, which seats 150 people in a closed room and the same number on the terrace. The all-inclusive system is relevant from 10 a.m. to 11 p.m. All local drinks are provided free of charge during this period. There is also a bar located by the pool, the capacity of which is 100 people.

Beach: The hotel has its own adjacent sandy and pebble beach, which stretches for 85 meters. Sun loungers, mattresses and umbrellas are paid. The hotel is separated from the beach by a road that can be crossed through an underpass.

Additional Information: For a wonderful stay at Yeni Turkmen Hotel 3 There is an outdoor pool of 120 square meters. meters. For adults, a slide is installed. Wi-Fi is free in the lobby bar. There is a safe at the reception in which guests can store their valuables (for a fee). A doctor is available around the clock upon request. There is a laundry room.





Among the sports activities, a sauna, table tennis, a beach volleyball court and chess are available free of charge. Among the paid services are a Turkish bath, massage, billiards. You can visit the diving center, learn to windsurf. There are various water activities (water skiing, pedalo, parasail, canoe).

For children there is a mini-club, an outdoor playground, an outdoor children's pool.

The hotel hosts light animation 3 times a week (English, German and Russian).

Reviews: The main advantage of the hotel is its proximity to the sea. But not only this is famous for Turkmen Hotel 3. Reviews show that those who prefer an inexpensive, but comfortable rest, go here on vacation. The rooms here are not very large, but bright and comfortable. The beach is clean, there are many kinds of entertainment that do not let anyone get bored.
