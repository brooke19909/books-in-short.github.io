It is here, on a picturesque seashore, that the small Himeros Beach Hotel 3 * hotel is located. So what does this hotel look like and is it worth spending a vacation here?

Where is the hotel situated? Short description





Where exactly to look for the hotel complex Himeros Beach Hotel 3 *? Kemer, namely, the city center, almost the coast itself - this is where tourists relax. Very close to the hotel is a marina where you can watch yachts or even get an excursion. Yes, and in the city center there are lots of interesting places, ranging from markets and restaurants to water parks and museums.

Anlai Airport is about 60 km away. The hotel was opened in 2004, but not so long ago, a thorough reconstruction and repair was carried out here. Its area is relatively small and amounts to 2.8 thousand square meters. The hotel is a large building, which consists of a new and an old wing. The territory is green, everywhere you can see palm trees, curly bushes and exotic flowers. There is a swimming pool, playgrounds for sports and recreation.

Hotel Himeros Beach Hotel 3 * (Turkey, Kemer): description and photos of rooms





This hotel, by the standards of Turkey, can be considered small. On its territory there are 87 rooms, namely 71 standard rooms and 16 spacious apartments (such rooms consist of a bedroom, a well-equipped kitchen, a living room and a bathroom). Most rooms have access to a private balcony where you can relax while admiring views of the sea and surroundings.

In the room you will find a pleasant design and comfortable furniture, including a large bed. From household appliances in the room there is a TV (shows some Russian satellite channels), a telephone (it is possible to dial a number directly), as well as air conditioning (simple control system). Naturally, there is a fully equipped bathroom. Here guests can take a shower. There is a hair dryer, a large mirror, a set of towels and necessary toiletries.

Room service is always available, including night time. But this service is paid separately. Cleaning is carried out either every day, or at the request of guests (some tourists refuse daily maid services). This is exactly what your stay in the territory of Himeros Beach Hotel 3 * will be. The reviews confirm that the conditions here are not bad, especially considering the cost.

Dining at the hotel





Like most Turkish hotels, Himeros Beach Hotel 3 * offers its guests a convenient "all inclusive" system, which provides full three meals a day, as well as drinks throughout the day, but only from a local manufacturer. Three meals a day are served in the main restaurant. The choice of dishes is quite large - slices and cold snacks, meat and fish dishes, delicious desserts and fresh pastries, fruits and other desserts.

There is a stylish bar nearby. Here you can get a chilled drink or ask the bartender to make a delicious cocktail for you. By the way, imported booze is also available, but you have to pay for it separately. And you can eat in the city center - there are dozens of cafes and restaurants for every taste.

Water recreation: where is the beach located?





What quality of a beach holiday can Himeros Beach Hotel 3 * offer? Kemer is a city famous for its picturesque coast. And the hotel has its own private beach area, which is only 100 meters away. Here you will find soft sand, comfortable entry into the sea and, of course, gentle waves. A rescue team is working on the shore. Here you can not only swim, but also relax, relaxing on a comfortable sun lounger with a mattress.

Naturally, the fun is not limited to this. On the shore there is a well-equipped volleyball court. Here, for a small fee, you can ride a boat, water ski, do parasailing, windsurfing or sailing. And since the marina is located nearby, you can buy a tour of the coast on a yacht. In Kemer itself there are several public beaches that can also be visited.

Leisure of guests and additional services





For customers of the Himeros Beach Hotel 3 * hotel tried to create maximum comfort. To begin with, it's worth talking about an additional service. As in most hotels, there is a private laundry room, the services of which are quite affordable. Temporarily unnecessary things can be taken to the storage room for greater convenience. The hotel will also help you rent a car or other form of transport, but for this you need the appropriate documents. Here, guests can change the currency at a favorable rate.

In the courtyard you will find a large pool, around which there are sun loungers and umbrellas. Travelers can freshen up, sunbathe and chat with each other. In the daytime, music is played here, various contests and competitions are organized, and in the evenings, guests are entertained and dancing. You can have a good time playing a game of table tennis or billiards.

Of course, this is not all that Himeros Beach Hotel 3 * offers. Near the hotel there are many shops, shopping and entertainment centers, night clubs, restaurants, museums and historical attractions, so no one will be bored here.

Hotel Himeros Beach Hotel 3 *: reviews

Turkey is a great place for people looking for an interesting beach holiday. Travelers speak favorably about this place. Its main advantage is a favorable location, since within walking distance not only the beach, but also all the attractions of the resort center.

For what rest is the hotel Himeros Beach Hotel 3 * suitable? Reviews say that the hotel is primarily designed for young people. There is music and noise everywhere, city bustle, and night clubs are located nearby. Fans of excursion vacations, who need a hotel only for sleep, will feel good. But families with children here may not be too convenient. By the way, the cost of living in Himeros Beach Hotel 3 * is much lower than in nearby hotels, which makes it so popular among tourists looking for budget vacation options.
