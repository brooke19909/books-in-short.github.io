Homemade monkeys: DIY crafts

The symbol of 2016 is the monkey. Therefore, for any holiday homemade monkeys will be in place. A handmade gift was always valued more than what was bought. Think about which material you work the easiest, and get started.

How to make a clay monkey

1. Take the brown polymer clay and make an oval for the body and a circle for the head. These are the main details. Press the top of the oval with your fingers so that you can easily stick a circle there.

2. Connect the parts together.

7. Eyes can be made of black clay. If you have black beads, then use it.

8. Make a small brown triangle - this is the nose.

9. Stack draw a monkey smile.

10. Using a needle, depict the monkey's hair, drawing strokes.

Beads

Beadwork is a simple and very entertaining task. Basic knowledge will help you create not only figures, but also jewelry, decoration elements. A bead monkey can be made even without using fishing line. Draw a picture of the animal on thick paper. Calibrate the beads of the desired color. This is important because all the details will be visible. Now you just glue the necessary colors on the cardboard. A good option for those who can draw.

To weave a monkey from beads, you will need:

‚Ä¢ Beads of red, black, orange.

‚Ä¢ Fishing line.

‚Ä¢ Scissors.

Making a bead monkey

1. String 13 red beads on the wire.

2. The second end passes approximately through the center of all beads. The ends of the wire should be directed towards each other.

3. Now string one orange, five red and another orange bead. The second end of the wire goes through them.

4. The fourth row of weaves in this order: red, yellow, black, yellow, black, yellow, red bead.

5. Fifth row: red, two yellow, black, two yellow, red.

6. At one end we put on eight beads. We fix between the second and third row, then we pass the wire down. Same thing with the second end. It turned out ears.

7. Now, by the same principle, we continue to weave the torso. First comes a row with six red beads, then with eight.

8. On one end string 11 beads and pass through them back without grabbing the last. Same thing with the other hand.

9. Then follows two rows of eight red beads.

10. The next row is four red, one black and four more red beads.

11. Then nine red.

12. On one end, string 19 red beads and do the same as with the paws.

13. Now, on the end on which the tail was, we string two beads, and six on the other. The first end we fix four beads of the second.

14. It should turn out that there are two beads on each side to the wire.

15. String 15 beads on each end and return without grabbing the last. The legs turned out.

Homemade monkeys from beads can serve as a keychain, which will always remind of the symbol of the New Year or the giver. The color scheme can be changed.

Salty dough

We make a monkey from the dough called salty. This is a special mixture that can replace gypsum or clay. You can create an animal in the same way as it was made from polymer clay. Only in the end will have to be painted manually.

You will need:

‚Ä¢ Salty dough.

‚Ä¢ Paints.

‚Ä¢ Stack.

‚Ä¢ Thick thread.

‚Ä¢ Glue.

Working process:

1. Blind a small oval, which will play the role of the body.

2. Now make an oval for the head and another smaller one for the face.

3. From two circles the size of a muzzle make ears.

4. Correct the muzzle and make an heart out of an oval.

5. Roll up three small balls for the eyes and nose.

6. Stack out the mouth.

7. Make a tummy in the shape of a circle. In size, it is equal to the ears. And glue a circle on it.

8. Make a hairstyle.

9. Draw a muzzle, creating a strip between the nose and mouth.

10. We depict the wool in stacks, drawing strokes.

11. We sculpt a butterfly.

12. For the paws on the legs, form a triangle, slightly bend the edges, make the fingers a match. For the paws on the hands - the same thing, only smaller.

13. Paint the details.

Now connect the legs to the body. But it will be much more interesting if the thread plays the role of legs and arms. Homemade monkeys from salt dough are ready. It remains to wait until the work hardens.
