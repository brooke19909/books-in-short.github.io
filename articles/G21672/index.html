Hotel Charm Beach Hotel 4 * (Turkey / Bodrum): description, photos and reviews of tourists

Bodrum among tourists from Europe enjoys the fame of the most fun Turkish resort, where a truly record number of entertainment venues - beer and restaurants, discos, nightclubs, etc.



general information

Located on the shores of the Aegean Sea, the incredibly beautiful Bodrum district includes not only the city of the same name, but also fourteen bays. This Turkish resort has originality, amazing attractiveness and originality. It is surrounded on three sides by low mountains, overgrown with orange, tangerine and pine groves. The beaches in Bodrum are usually part of the hotel complexes and are their property. The most prestigious and expensive hotels are built near the sea. Most of them operate on an all-inclusive basis. They are distinguished by excellent service and well-developed infrastructure. The resort is famous not only for its chic hotel complexes, but also for relatively cheaper hotels, many of which are located not only in the city itself, but also in nearby settlements. One of them is Charm Beach Hotel 4 *. Reviews about the number of rooms, infrastructure, location, services provided and photos of this hotel are presented in this article.

Description

Every year, the number of Russians choosing holidays in Turkish resorts is increasing. This is explained, first of all, by democratic prices for accommodation in local hotels, as well as excellent service. More and more vacationers from our country buy tours to the Charm Beach Hotel 4 *. Bodrum, in particular the village of Turgutreis, in which this truly remarkable hotel is located, receives thousands of tourists from our country every year. It has a great location. When describing the hotel, many Russians separately note the picturesque panoramas around. On the practical side, the location of Charm Beach Hotel 4 * is also very advantageous. It is located in the village of Turgutreis, six kilometers from the center of Bodrum. This allows tourists away from the bustling large village to enjoy peace, while not giving up the benefits of civilization. From the airport in Bodrum to the hotel - fifty-eight kilometers. Charm Beach Hotel was built in 1990. The latest renovation with many innovations in it was carried out in 2015. The hotel area is not very large - about four thousand square meters. Nevertheless, all the facilities necessary for a good rest fit on it.



Infrastructure

Management at Charm Beach Hotel 4 * is aimed solely at making clients feel as comfortable as possible. The hotel has a grocery and hardware stores, a souvenir kiosk. If vacationers come by car or rent vehicles on the spot, they have the right to park in a guarded parking lot for free. The hotel also has a conference room for one hundred and fifty people at a time, laundry service, shoe shine service and an office for medical assistance. At the always open reception there is a paid depository safe. Residents of this hotel may not worry about the safety of things, since they can be left in the left-luggage office.

Housing stock

Since the contingent of vacationers in the Charm Beach Hotel 4 * (Turkey, Bodrum) is quite different, the administration is trying to cater to each client. And first of all it concerns the requests and wishes related to the housing stock. The hotel consists of several two- or three-story buildings, which contain one hundred and twelve rooms of different categories.



In total, in Charm Beach Hotel 4 * one hundred and six Standard Rooms measuring twenty-one square meters. These rooms can accommodate a maximum of three people at a time. The housing stock also includes two-room Family Rooms. There are only six of them. Family rooms are thirty-two square meters. meter. They are designed for four adult vacationers.

The rooms are decorated in light colors with a bright emphasis on curtains and bedspreads. Depending on their category and content, the cost of living also changes. The rooms are bright and spacious with a simple but comfortable interior. The rooms have a small paid built-in safe, manually adjustable air conditioning, a telephone with a direct line, all the necessary furniture, a TV, and a minibar.

Beach

Well-equipped rooms, quality service, entertainment, the presence of a pool are, of course, advantages, but when tourists go to the resort to the sea, they definitely need a good swimming area - sand cover, convenient access to the water, etc. The beach at the hotel Charm Beach Hotel 4 * (Bodrum) own. It is fifty meters from the administrative building. The beach has it all: free sunbeds and umbrellas, a bar, a toilet and a shower. The staff monitors the cleanliness of the territory constantly, therefore, judging by the reviews, it is impossible to see cigarette butts or candy wrappers here.



Nutrition

Many Russians put the issue of quality food in the first place - above the number of rooms and the service offered. When a person buys a tour with the concept of "all inclusive" in order to distract from all the hardships of everyday life, he wants the food to be of the highest quality. In Charm Beach Hotel 4 * it is. The hotel operates according to the incredibly popular all-inclusive concept among Russians. The price of the tours includes not only a three-time buffet, but also aromatic coffee with delicious cakes from five to six in the evening, as well as night snacks and an unlimited number of drinks from a local producer. In addition to the main one, the hotel also has a pre-order restaurant. Once during the stay tourists can visit it for free. There are also two bars - in the lobby and by the pool.

Services for children

When checking into rooms at Charm Beach Hotel 4 *, parents can request an extra cot upon request. For a fee, babysitting services are provided. The hotel also has an equipped playground for children and a shallow pool. There is also a games room in the administrative building.



Additional Information

Rest is always useful for the body, but if a person does not deny himself the adoption of various wellness procedures on vacation, this is simply excellent. At the Charm Beach Hotel 4 *, customers can train in the gym, and after a bike ride on mountain streamers or long excursions, take a massage course. For a fee, guests are invited to visit the hammam or sauna. Judging by the reviews, everything is thought out in the hotel. During the day you can swim and sunbathe on the beach or by the pool, walk, and have fun in the evening. Once a week, Turkish Night is organized on site, in the restaurant you can see a show program or listen to live music.

Charm Beach Hotel 4 * (Bodrum): traveler reviews

The most accurate and specific information about the hotel can be found out from the impressions of real visitors. Reviews for the Charm Beach Hotel 4 * in Bodrum are mostly good. Most of our compatriots are ready to come here again and again. The territory pleases the eye with well-groomed and clean, there is a lot of greenery. In general, some in their reviews say that in this hotel they were satisfied with almost everything - their location, room, and food. Walking distance to the beach, proximity to the city center and a short transfer to the airport save people from many difficulties.



Of course, the Russians have some complaints. And first of all, they relate to the scarcity of entertainment in the territory and not very well-established work of the staff. According to the recollections of some tourists, bed linen for the entire stay was changed only once. And another minus is the proximity of the mosque, as a result of which in some rooms a call to prayer was heard every morning.
