Club Fontana Life 4 * (Hotel "Club Fountain Life"), Kemer, Turkey - photos, prices and reviews of tourists

The popular Turkish resort Kemer is conveniently located on the coastal strip between the picturesque mountains and the azure sea. Here tourists expect a wide hotel base, well-developed infrastructure, a lot of world-famous discos, as well as magnificent nature and excellent beach vacations. If you want to comfortably relax in a quiet place in the vicinity of Kemer, then we recommend that you consider the four-star Club Fontana Life Hotel as an accommodation option. We offer further to learn more about this hotel, as well as to find out what impression was formed from our compatriots staying in it.





Location

"Fountain Life Club" is located in a resort village called Kirish. The distance to the main city of the entire resort region - Kemer - is only six kilometers. You can overcome it if you wish on foot or by bus and taxi. The tourist capital of Turkey - the city of Antalya - is 55 kilometers away. In the vicinity there is also the airport closest to the hotel. The path to it is about 65 kilometers. So after landing in Antalya's air harbor, you can get to Club Fontana Life (Kemer) in about 1 hour. As for the distance from the sea, the distance to the hotel‚Äôs own beach is only 250 meters. So literally in 5-7 minutes at a slow pace you can reach the shore.

Rooms

Club Fontana Life offers everyone to stay in one of the 155 comfortable rooms of a standard type. There is also one room specially prepared for people with disabilities. All apartments have a private bathroom with a hairdryer, air conditioning, TV, telephone, refrigerator and a balcony. An in-room safe is available at an extra charge. Club Fontana Life 4 *: reviews of Russian travelers

Since today many tourists, when choosing a hotel, rely heavily on the impressions of other people who have already been here, we decided to bring to your attention the generalized comments of our compatriots about their vacation in Fountain Life Club. Looking ahead a bit, we note that most travelers were very pleased with their choice. According to them, this hotel more than meets its category and price, as well as the quality of accommodation and the level of service. But we offer to understand everything in more detail.





Comments of tourists about the rooms and the hotel area

The rooms in this hotel seemed quite spacious, clean, comfortable and pleasantly decorated to our compatriots. Furniture and appliances are in good condition. Pleased with the availability of a hairdryer in the bathroom. Club Fontana Life Apartments is cleaned every day. Towels are changed if dirty toss on the floor. Housekeeping maids update twice a week. As for the hotel‚Äôs own territory, its travelers found quite large. In addition to three residential buildings, a reception, a restaurant and bars, there are also three swimming pools, a water slide, a hammam, a sauna, a stage and a volleyball court. The area is very green and well-groomed.





Reviews of our compatriots about the location of the hotel

Fountain Life Club has a very good location. So, close to it there are all objects of tourist infrastructure (shops, restaurants, shops, excursion bureaus, car rental points and much more). In addition, in many places they accept not only Turkish lira, but also dollars, euros and even Russian rubles.

The village of Kirish itself is very green. It is located near orchards and pine forests. So in its vicinity there are plenty of places where you can take a pleasant walk.

Tourists comments on food and hotel staff

Now we offer a little focus on the topic of nutrition at the Club Fontana Life hotel . The reviews of our compatriots on this issue basically boil down to the fact that the restaurant offers guests a very diverse and well-prepared dishes. The menu here, of course, cannot be compared with luxurious five-star hotels, but everyone will be satisfied with the quality of food. In addition, in the restaurant you can often see the chef, who here also has lunch and dinner. Guests have the opportunity to contact him, he will be happy to answer all questions and tell you in detail about a particular dish. The guests also liked the work of the waiters, who almost instantly clean the dirty dishes and are always ready to fulfill any request of visitors.

As for the hotel staff as a whole, all the staff here are friendly, attentive and helpful people. Almost every hotel employee speaks Russian well, so you will not have any communication problems. Upon arrival, the staff will happily bring your items to the room. Also, upon arrival, administrators always try to check in the guests immediately, without condemning them to waiting for the checkout time.





Reviews of tourists about beach vacations and entertainment at Fountain Life Club

Club Fontana Life Hotel's own beach, according to guests, can be reached in just five to seven minutes at a leisurely pace. At the same time, the road to the sea passes through a nice shady square. The beach here is pebbly. You can calmly walk barefoot, it doesn‚Äôt hurt your legs. The entry into the sea is gentle, the water is very clean. As for entertainment, the hotel has three pools. All of them are open: one main, the second with water slides and the third - for children. The pools here are clean and spacious. Closer to the night, hotel guests can go to the nightclubs with the animators.
