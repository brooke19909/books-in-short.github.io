Anapa, Vityazevo: holidays with children. Hotel overview, features and recommendations

In winter it is a small and cozy village, and in summer it is a popular and lively resort located ten kilometers from Anapa. In Vityazevo, relaxing with children is simply magnificent. Families from all over the country come here to have a good rest on magnificent beaches, to breathe in unusual air, to enjoy magnificent nature.



What is attractive about holidays in Vityazevo with children?

To begin with, the village of Vityazevo is very conveniently located. Holidays with children (parents are well aware of this) always tire of long journeys and flights. From Anapa Airport you can take a bus to the village in twenty minutes. In a matter of hours, you can get here on the highway by car.

Tourists are attracted by this quiet place with sandy beaches. Children of any age are happy to build sand castles and palaces here, prepare funny cakes and bury each other in soft and warm sand. These classes are quite suitable for parents: the child is busy with the game, communicates with peers, and parents calmly take sun baths at this time.





In this sense, it is difficult for mothers and fathers to find a more peaceful place than the village of Vityazevo. Rest with children here is fully consistent with the dream of a family vacation.

I must say that no one will have problems with living here. In the village you can choose a comfortable hotel, guest house, villa or housing in the private sector. Guests choose options according to their desires and financial capabilities. Vityazevo hotels for families with children are equipped with everything necessary for kids. There is special furniture and utensils, swimming pools and playgrounds, in some of them you can use the services of a nanny.

But there is one advantage that differs from other resorts in the village of Vityazevo. Rest with children here is not connected with a long and tiring journey from the place of residence to the beach. You can go to the sea from anywhere in the village in a few minutes.





When planning a vacation with children, all parents must take into account the quality of water and the coastal zone. And on this indicator, many are satisfied with Vityazevo. Holidays with children suggest that the water and the beach should be as clean as possible, such as in this resort village. It is important that the coastal line here is quite shallow - the first two to three meters at the edge are convenient for swimming even for the smallest travelers.

The village has a modern water park. We think that there is no need to comment on this item.

Sea and beach

Many compare the water temperature in the sea near the village in the summer with fresh milk. You can swim here from May to October. The bottom is smooth and even, so you can not worry about the safety of children. The beaches are well equipped: for children there are many slides and water attractions, and for adults you can choose something more extreme. It depends on your preference: diving or flying a hang glider, a light-engine plane or parachuting.



Entertainment

It should be noted that the Vityazevo resort is becoming more beautiful and developing every year. Rest with children today is not only the beach and the sea. You can visit two excellent parks with dizzying and at the same time safe rides. We are sure that your children will enjoy this walk.

Hotels

There are many hotels and hotels in the village. Most of them provide for the rest of parents with children. Meet the most popular ones.

"Watercolor"

The hotel is 550 meters from the wonderful sandy beach. Within walking distance are the central promenade, the sports club "Volleygrad", a dolphinarium, a water park, bowling, cafes and shops.

Rest in "Watercolor" will appeal to both adults and children. Talented animators work here who conduct fascinating contests and entertainment programs. The hotel can arrange tour services upon request.

Types of rooms "Watercolors" consists of comfortable, cozy and bright rooms with all amenities and wireless internet. Almost all have balconies. The hotel has regular customers who have been choosing Vityazevo (families with children) for years. The on-site swimming pool with water attractions, a wonderful playground, a special menu for young guests - all this attracts tourists and makes their stay as comfortable as possible.





Pontos

Guests of the village can choose a vacation with children in Vityazevo with and without meals. This service is provided by the Pontos Hotel. Rates are offered:

  • without food;
  • half board (breakfast and lunch);
  • full board.

The hotel consists of two buildings, which are located opposite each other. They have the names Alpha and Beta. This hotel features original interior solutions.

In the room stock one hundred and twelve comfortable and modern rooms. On the territory there is a children's playground, swimming pools, a summer cinema and sports grounds. The territory is under round-the-clock protection.

In addition to hotels, Vityazevo also has boarding houses for families with children. We recommend that you pay attention to the best of them - the "Golden Line", which is intended for families. It offers guests comfortable living conditions.

Throughout the year, you can afford to spend time with children in Vityazevo. The heated pool located on site will brighten up even gloomy autumn days. Other facilities include a tennis court and fitness room. It offers open parking, an excellent massage room. For children, a children's room and a playground are created here, which operate year-round.





Adult vacationers can play table tennis, visit the outdoor basketball courts and tennis court with excellent coverage.

Pension "Black Sea"

On the territory of this boarding house there are four-story buildings with a building for one hundred and twenty places. Guests are offered double and triple rooms with all amenities. "Black Sea" is literally immersed in greenery. Mostly pine trees grow here.

Cots are provided for younger guests; nutritionists have developed a special menu for them. All their free time from visiting the beach, kids enjoy playing in the playground.

Vityazevo: cheap vacations with children

Not all parents can pay for accommodation in a hotel or boarding house. But this does not mean that they will not be able to relax in Vityazevo. Guest houses located in the village will provide good living conditions for a moderate fee.

Glories

Wonderful guest house located in the center of the village. It attracts many parents with a cozy gated courtyard and comfortable rooms with all amenities. Each of them has a bathroom and a TV.

This guest house also has a dining room and a kitchen where you can cook yourself. Very close is the Paralia attraction complex.





Green Yard

This is one of the few guest houses that have allergy-free and non-smoking rooms. Agree, this is important for living with a child. Cozy rooms for families with children allow guests to relax in comfort. For those who have not quit smoking, there are special places where they will not disturb anyone.

Peace of mind is ensured by good soundproofing of the rooms, which are also equipped with air conditioning, and the heating system will create a comfortable atmosphere in the cold season.

Vityazevo, holidays with children: reviews

According to vacationers, this picturesque resort village is an ideal place for families. Beautiful beaches, smooth access to the sea, a huge selection of options for living with children - these are the basic requirements that parents make for a resort convenient for kids.

Many note the great work of the resort administration in organizing the relaxation of young guests. A lot of attractions and playgrounds have been created for them here. It is important that families with different levels of financial income can relax in the village of Vityazevo.
