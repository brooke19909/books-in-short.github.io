Nha Trang in October: weather, attractions, entertainment

Nha Trang in October is an opportunity to spend time in an exotic country and have a good rest, despite the changing weather, since for a real tourist who likes to learn new countries, any circumstances are on the shoulder. A beautiful city in the center of Vietnam always welcomes tourists. But what should one be prepared for? What is the weather like in Nha Trang in October? Travelers who have been there will provide answers to these questions. They will also talk about what you can see during this period.





Weather in October

In October, Vietnamese autumn is felt in Nha Trang. Of course, for our latitudes this is an unusual definition of a given season, since here you will not see yellowed foliage. In Vietnam, the fall season means more frequent rains and cloudy weather. The end of the month will give only a few sunny days.

However, Nha Trang in early October, as in the last days of this month, is rains in the evenings or in the afternoon. Do not be scared: most often the precipitation is short-term. Also, one should not think that it is cold here, as the average temperature in the daytime reaches thirty degrees of heat. So, we can say that the weather is pretty good in Nha Trang in October. Reviews of tourists claim that it is warm here at this time. The climate is suitable for relaxation.





Beaches in Nha Trang

The coast in this part of Vietnam is washed by the waters of the South China Sea. In fact, the water here is beautiful, it is transparent. The sand is pleasant to the touch and most often yellow. Traveler reviews indicate that on the beaches of Nha Trang you rarely find dangerous marine animals, jellyfish, etc. There are also no stones and large shells.

The coast will appreciate the opportunity for a budget holiday. If you do not need paid sun loungers, then you can simply sunbathe in the sand and swim. Most tourists visit the city beach in the central part of Nha Trang, as it can easily be reached from any hotel due to its convenient location. Despite the popular stereotypes that it is dirty in such recreation areas, Nha Trang is an exception. It's pretty clean here.





Holidays in Nha Trang in October

Weather conditions this month make it possible for tourists to choose the conditions for spending time. If you still do not decide to relax on the beach because of the frequent rains and strong winds, you can replace this leisure with another, based on your own preferences. However, if you have surfing skills, you can afford it because of the weather.

Nha Trang in October is also a great opportunity to visit the famous Winperl Park in Vietnam. To do this, you need to get to the island where the destination is located. You can do this with a boat, but, as a rule, tourists prefer to use the cable car. This mode of transportation does not mean anything dangerous or too unusual. Of course, it should be noted that the road is at an altitude of about seventy meters (in case you are afraid of heights).

Three kilometers away there is a park called Winperl. There are many attractions that will appeal to both children and adults. Usually trips here last from morning to evening. Therefore, you will need to have a snack somewhere on the island. Fortunately, there are many catering establishments in which you can not only satisfy your hunger, but also learn something new for yourself, namely try the dishes of Vietnamese cuisine.





Attractions Nha Trang

Nha Trang (Vietnam) in October promotes a closer acquaintance with local culture, because instead of a beach holiday you can visit attractions near the resort and experience a unique Asian flavor. The building of the Catholic Cathedral, which is distinguished by its special architecture, is considered to be a famous monument.

However, more tourists are still attracted by local religious shrines. For example, the towers of Po Nagar, where worshipers of Hindu gods send their services. You must also visit Long Sean Pagoda, otherwise you will not feel the local atmosphere to the end. Travelers are usually remembered most of all by a large Buddha statue, which is located on a hilltop.

If you are interested in the life of the Vietnamese emperor, then you can find such information in the villa of the last ruler of this country - Bao Dai. Nha Trang in October is a great opportunity to find an activity for the child. For example, take him to the aquarium of the local oceanographic institute, where he can look at the animals living in the underwater world.





Excursions in Nha Trang

As a rule, vacationers in Nha Trang prefer to attend a variety of excursions. One of the most common and popular trips is considered to be excursions to islands that are nearby. You will definitely like the picturesque Khon Tam (its name in translation corresponds to the meaning of "Silk Island"). There are ethnic villages where you can learn more about local traditions. Tourists also like Monkey Island, where more than fifteen hundred of these animals live.

October prices

This season, the prices are quite affordable. If you want to go to Nha Trang, in October the cost of rest will be quite acceptable due to weather changes. About a week this month will cost from two thousand dollars (including air travel there), if you live in a hotel with three stars. Of course, you will need additional funds for purchases, excursions and other recreational activities.
