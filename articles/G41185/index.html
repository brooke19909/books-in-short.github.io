Hotel "Palma" (Adler): photos and reviews of visitors

One of the most crucial moments when planning a vacation is choosing a hotel. To guarantee your comfort and vivid experience, pay attention to the hotel "Palma". Adler will leave only pleasant memories about himself if you stay at this establishment. And thanks to the beautiful design of the hotel and the surrounding area, you can bring a lot of interesting photos from your vacation.





Resort Location

On the very shore of the Black Sea, literally a minute walk from the city beach, this hotel is located. Her address is Adler, ul. Alleynaya 11. Hotel "Palma" is 4 km from the railway station, and 6. from the international airport. If you decide to get to the hotel by bus, you need to get off at the "Knowledge" stop, and then walk a bit. 10 minutes from the hotel there is a large shopping and entertainment center and a water park. And if you want to visit the central part of the city, then you have to do a 20-minute journey by bus. The road to Krasnaya Polyana takes about 40 minutes.

Hotel Rooms

The atmosphere of comfort and homeliness is given to its guests by the resort hotel "Palma". Adler offers you many opportunities for relaxation, and to take full advantage of them you need to regain strength. You can do this in one of 52 guest rooms of the following categories:

  • "Standard" is a compact one-room room, where there is everything that is necessary for a comfortable stay. An improved variation is also provided with its own exit to the balcony.
  • Superior is a superior double room. In addition to the bedroom area, there is also a workplace and a small sitting area with upholstered furniture, a floor lamp and a tea table. An improved version also has a balcony.
  • The family room consists of two rooms. The bedroom has a large bed, and the living room has folding upholstered furniture. In total, it can accommodate up to 4 people. You can choose the option with or without a balcony, which will affect the cost of living.

Special attention should be paid to the interior of the rooms that the Palma Hotel (Adler) offers its guests. Photos taken by tourists demonstrate a homely, cozy style in design.





Guest Room Amenities

Comfortable living conditions are offered to guests by the hotel "Palma". Adler will seem even more hospitable if you stay in comfortable rooms with a full range of necessary amenities, namely:

  • comfortable orthopedic beds (double or twin);
  • capacious wardrobe, where you can put all your things;
  • bedside tables for storing personal belongings; dressing table with a large mirror;
  • folding sofas and armchairs as extra beds;
  • a small refrigerator for storing food;
  • Plasma TV with cable or satellite channels;
  • split system to create a comfortable air temperature in the apartment;
  • combined bathroom with shower, trouble and electric hairdryer.




Pricing and Reservation

In the "hot season" accommodation in a resort such as the Palma Hotel (Adler) will cost you from 3,500 to 6,000 rubles, depending on the room category. This amount also includes breakfast. As for the placement of additional guests, its cost is 500 rubles per day.

Booking is carried out through the official website, specialized resources, and also by phone +7 (988) 158-09-2. The following conditions apply:

  • confirmation of the reservation is an advance payment in the amount of the cost of the room per day;
  • any changes to the reservation (room category, length of stay, etc.) can be made no later than a week before the planned arrival date;
  • Refund of the deposit is possible only if you refuse to reserve the apartment at least 20 days before the specified date.

What is included in the price

Choosing such an option as a mini-hotel "palm" (Adler), you can use the following services absolutely free:

  • swimming in the outdoor pool (in cold weather, it maintains a constant water temperature of 28 degrees);
  • use of a gazebo with barbecue facilities for barbecue and picnics;
  • Parking space in the parking lot, equipped with alarm and video surveillance;
  • use of high-speed wireless Internet;
  • buffet breakfast in the hotel dining room;
  • daily housekeeping;
  • once every 3 days, towels and bed linen are replaced;
  • use of a common iron and ironing board;
  • visit to the gym with modern sports equipment;
  • safe box rental at the administrator.




At extra charge

For an additional fee, the hotel "Palma" (Adler) will provide you with the following opportunities for a good rest:

  • You can leave your children under the supervision of a qualified nanny (the service must be ordered no later than 24 hours);
  • if you have dirty things, then in the laundry they will quickly be put in order;
  • at the hotel you can be provided with a service for minor repairs of clothes;
  • a steam sauna will positively affect your health and emotional state;
  • You can relax after a busy day in a massage chair with several modes;
  • visit to the billiard room (located next to the pool);

  • Transfer to the airport, train station or neighboring settlements.




Hotel Infrastructure

If you dream of a bright and unforgettable vacation, go to Adler. Hotel "Palma" (Alleynaya, 11) has the following infrastructure:

  • a large garden with an original landscape design (flowers and green spaces, artificial ponds, bridges and all kinds of decor);
  • large terrace with sunbeds;
  • rental of bicycles and roller skates;
  • a playground for the youngest tourists;
  • 24-hour reception, where you can get advice on accommodation and solve problems;
  • luggage room for valuable or too bulky luggage;
  • a common living room where you can watch TV in the company of your neighbors;
  • special places for smoking.




Useful information for guests

It will be useful for tourists to know the following:

  • check-in starts after 14:00;
  • return keys for reception on the day of departure until noon;
  • children under 5 stay free in this hotel;
  • throughout the hotel, except for designated areas, smoking is prohibited;

Positive reviews about the hotel "Palma" (Adler)

Travelers note the following advantages of this resort:

  • fairly new bedding and towels;
  • the hotel is located in close proximity to the sea;
  • incredibly beautiful area with lots of greenery and decor;

General impression

At first glance, the Palma Hotel in Adler has all the necessary conditions for a comfortable and budget vacation at sea. Very close is a public pebble beach and urban infrastructure. There is a dining room on site where you can eat tasty and inexpensively. The territory is very well-groomed and creates the most pleasant impression. Nevertheless, the main thing is missing - service. Most tourists note that the hotel staff is extremely inattentive and even dismissive of the requests of guests. And the owners, in general, perceive any remark as a personal insult. The hotel has constant problems with cleaning, changing bed linen and towels, which, in principle, should not be. If you take these things calmly, then you can safely choose this option for accommodation in Adler.
