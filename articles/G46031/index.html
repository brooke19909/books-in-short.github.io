Pattern for children's t-shirts, sewing tips

If you suddenly decided to update the wardrobe of your little son or daughter, and you want to do something original, have free time and desire, then try to sew a T-shirt yourself. The article will provide you with patterns for children's t-shirts for the boy. But that's not all. When you understand how to build patterns correctly, you can easily remake them for a pattern of children's t-shirts for girls. Enjoy the results of yourself and your child!

What do you need?

To sew a t-shirt on your own, you need to prepare:

  1. The cloth. Well choose not only color, but also material. It should be convenient in the first place, let air through, and not deteriorate when washing.
  2. Threads in tone of fabric.
  3. Scissors for cutting. They should cut the fabric well, not tear it.
  4. Pins
  5. Needles.

Necessary measurements

For the correct construction of the pattern, carefully take the necessary measurements:

  • POG - half chest;
  • NAP - half neck circumference;
  • Depth and width of the armhole;
  • DI - product in length;
  • DR - sleeves in length;

Of course, you can use universal (standard) measurements. You can find them in the table of children's measurements. But it will be better if you take measurements from your son or daughter. You can build a pattern yourself, it is not as difficult as it might seem.

What to do next

The pattern of the sleeve of a children's T-shirt is also easy to do. Draw a vertical line and put a dot at the top of the sheet. Three quarters (3/4) of the depth of the armhole and the length of the sleeve according to the measurements taken by you, set aside from the point down. You got two points. From them, draw two horizontal lines of arbitrary length.

On the front of the t-shirt, measure the length of the armhole. We transfer the value that we measured to the pattern from the side of the sleeve - we get a segment. We call it VO. From point O, lay to the right the segment OV 1 = VO.

Next, we divide the obtained segments BO and OB 1 into 4 equal parts. We construct the armhole of the sleeve as shown in the pattern. You can narrow the sleeves along the bottom by about 2‚Äì2.5 centimeters on each side.

Sewing products from patterns of children's t-shirts

Getting to sewing:

  1. Cut out all the necessary details from the fabric, according to your pattern of a children's t-shirt. Before cutting out the elements, be sure to check the drawing again. As the saying goes "measure seven times - cut once."
  2. Need to stitch the sleeves to the front of our t-shirt. Face the sleeves, attach the sleeves and connect the pieces of fabric with pins.
  3. Attach the sleeves to the front of the product.
  4. We connect the free back of the sleeves in the same way with the back of the T-shirt and stitch it.
  5. We fix the free lateral edges of the T-shirt and the edges of the sleeves with the help of pins. Firmware.

We process the edges. From all edges, bend first by 0.5 cm, then another 1 cm and stitch. If you wish, you can trim the edges with a ribbon or oblique trim.

Shutdown

Carefully inspect the finished work and add the finishing touches. Check that the threads do not hang loose. If necessary, cut off the excess. You can decorate the finished t-shirt. For this, you can use all kinds of stripes or thermal stickers. Are you done? Now you can try on a T-shirt.

Sewing Tricks

  • You can use the line direction table. It is very comfortable. In addition, it looks beautiful and neat, especially on thin fabrics.
