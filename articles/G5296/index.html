What kind of payment?

What kind of payment are you talking about? This is the first question to focus on. After all, taxes in Russia can be different. And not everyone receives certain receipts. Apartment tax is the same as property tax. These accruals come to all property owners. This is an annual payment that will have to be paid without fail.

Typically, taxes come for apartments, rooms, cottages, houses, buildings, shares in the specified property. If the property does not have anything from the previously listed list, then it is not necessary to think about why the apartment tax does not come. This is a normal occurrence.

No time

Why don't taxes on the apartment come? The next scenario is a situation that is more common. It has already been said that property tax is a mandatory payment, moreover, annual. If there is no receipt for payment , it is likely that there is still no time to receive a payment.

As a rule, tax receipts for property to citizens come in the fall: from September to November. Terms of payment

From now on, it is clear why the tax on a privatized apartment does not come . Moreover, several tips for resolving such an unpleasant situation are also known. But we do not yet know how long it is required to pay property tax. Everything is also simple here.

In 2016, all property owners must pay for their property until December 1, 2016. Moreover, inclusive. This is the deadline set in Russia for property tax fees. No exceptions and concessions. Temporary restrictions apply to all citizens.





New rules

Why doesn‚Äôt the apartment tax come in? 2016 is the period in which the new rules regarding tax payments came into effect. What is it about? The thing is that not all property owners will receive receipts from the tax authorities. If a citizen has a profile on the State Services portal, then it will be in electronic form that a payment will come. Mailings in this case are excluded.

Thus, since 2016, receipts on the payment of property tax have been issued only to those who do not have an account on the State Services website. They can wait for the appropriate payment or apply for it to the tax. All others will receive a notification about the need to deposit funds in the state treasury for their property in electronic format.





New Year - New Calculations

Another question that worries citizens: why big taxes come? The privatized apartment, even the smallest, according to the rules established in Russia, will have to be evaluated. According to the new calculation rules, payments for property will be formed on the basis of the cadastral value of real estate. And with a certain coefficient. Therefore, it is likely that the payment received in 2016 will be with a lower amount. And then the payment will increase. True, gradually.

Why did the big apartment tax come? Probably the cadastral value of real estate is high. And therefore, payments require quite large amounts. The cadastral value of housing can be found in Rosreestr. And the amount of property tax for individuals is calculated on the website of the Federal Tax Service using a specific calculator.

Accordingly, it is not necessary to be surprised at high taxes. The final amount, as already mentioned, is added up based on the cadastral value of the property. Therefore, an apartment of the same size in different areas of the city and in different regions of the country will have to pay not the same amount.
