Hotel Matina Rodos 3 * (Greece / Rhodes): overview, description, rooms and reviews

Faliraki is the busiest resort on the Greek island of Rhodes. This popular southern tourist destination is famous for its golden beach, coniferous forests, vibrant nightlife and, of course, cozy hotels. Guests of Rhodes in Faliraki can rent inexpensively comfortable accommodation without problems. And one of the best hotels in this resort is Matina Rodos 3 *.

Where is the hotel

This hotel is located about 10 kilometers from the international airport of Rhodes. You can get here by taxi or transfer bus in just 15-10 minutes. The capital of the island, Rhodes, is 14 km from Faliraki. If necessary, you can easily get here by public transport.

What is a hotel

This hotel was built a long time ago - in 1990. However, in 2013, reconstruction was carried out here. Therefore, the decoration in the lobby, corridors and hotel rooms is relatively new. The same goes for furniture and household appliances.

The hotel Matina 3 * ( Rhodes island, Greece) is a three-story concrete building of modern construction with a rather large courtyard. On the territory of the complex there is a huge pool, and flower beds with exotic plants are broken. A five-minute walk from the hotel is a small church in which peacocks live. If desired, tourists can feed them directly with their hands.





Some vacationers note the fact that upon arrival at the Matina hotel guests may be denied check-in, explaining this by the lack of available rooms. Of course, tourists are offered to stay in another hotel until some room is vacated. However, the result is often populated in a hotel of a lower category and very uncomfortable. If this happens, experienced tourists are advised to return to Matina Rodos 3 * and request for a reserved room. Usually, after this, the hotel administration will meet you and find a free room.

Number of rooms

Guests of Faliraki hotel Matina Rodos 3 * provides 86 standard rooms. In the rooms rented at the hotel, tourists can watch TV or listen to the radio. Each room has a shower and kitchenette (stove and sink). For a fee, tourists can use the mechanical safe. Also at this hotel you need to pay a certain amount for air conditioning and a refrigerator. In the shower room, in addition to the standard set of toiletries, there is a hairdryer. All rooms have spacious loggias. The views from them, judging by the reviews of tourists, are not bad.

Guest opinions on hotel rooms

The rooms rented out at the Matina Rodos 3 * hotel (Rhodes), according to many tourists, are quite suitable for living. They have a large area, and they look cozy. The beds in the rooms are set outwardly unsightly, but quite comfortable. Air conditioners and televisions in the rooms in most cases work properly.

Among the pluses of the rooms of this hotel, many tourists, among other things, include the fact that there is always hot water in the showers. Also, the hotel is considered a daily cleaning service. Hotel maids take their responsibilities responsibly, and therefore the rooms here are always clean. Quite often, bed linen with towels is also changed in this hotel.
