Preparatory stage

To knit a summer pink dress of a straight cut of size 46-48 with a small neckline and lowered sleeves, you need to buy circular and straight knitting needles No. 3.5, as well as 500 grams of Miro yarn (polyacryl and cotton 50/50, 140 m / 50 g ) By the way, knitting a warm dress with knitting needles requires the same thread consumption if the thickness of the yarn matches the specified brand.

The model is convenient in that it is knitted with the main openwork pattern, similar to the stems of plants, and tied with a simple garter stitch. Therefore, the density of knitting is compared with the main pattern on a 10-centimeter pattern, consisting of 30 rows and 22 loops.

For the main picture, you need to dial the number of loops, a multiple of 11, and add another 3 loops, two of which will be edge. All seamy rows along with yarn-stitches are sewn with purl stitches. The front rows are repeated from the first to the 11th according to the scheme excluding the edge ones (rapport is indicated by asterisks). Please note that the upper, lower broaches and the two knitted front loops go under each other without bias in all rows. Due to the crochet, a drawing on the dress is obtained.

Knitting for women: stages of work

For a pattern of this size, it is enough to make a pattern of the back and front halves:

  • 29.5 cm - width of the top of the product: 11 cm for the cutout and 19.5 cm for the shoulder;
  • 30.5 cm - the width of the bottom of the product;
  • 91.5 cm - dress length: 24 cm for sleeves, 65.5 cm for the main length and 2 cm for shawl; neck side: 17.5 cm V-neck, 72 cm base of dress and 2 cm for shawl.

Start any crochet and knitting dresses from the bottom of the products. For a back you collect 135 loops, knit 7 rows of garter stitch and 268 openwork rows. When closing the loops, mark 43 loops on the shoulders and 49 loops for the neck.
