Les Quatre Saisons (Tunisia, Djerba): tourists photos and reviews

Djerba is a small island in the Mediterranean Sea (Gabes Bay in eastern Tunisia). A fabulous seaside resort, with a milder and warmer climate than on the mainland, since the sultry Sahara is nearby. The island has the longest swimming season in all of Tunisia - lasting from April to October. Here you can splash around in the sea and bask in the sun all year round. Djerba is called a real oasis of enchanting landscapes, "floating" in the turquoise waters of the Mediterranean. The resort is known not only for its magnificent fine sandy beaches, fantastic warm climate, amazingly clear sea water, but also for modern hotels. One of the most popular guest accommodation options in Djerba is Les Quatre Saisons.
