Resort and recreation center "Dolphin" (Koblevo)

Recently, Odessa, Kherson and Nikolaev regions have become increasingly attractive for Ukrainians to relax . The resort village called Koblevo is vivid evidence of this. Here is everything why people go somewhere to Bulgaria, for example. Sandy beaches, wonderful climate, healing air, safe sea, suitable for children. These places are removed not only from enterprises, but also motorways and gas-polluted megacities. An excellent option for both youth recreation and families with children will be the recreation centers in Koblevo. "Dolphin" is one of them. In fact, this is a modern hotel with excellent service and well-trained staff.



Where is it and how to get there

In order to get here, you should first get to one of the nearby regional centers. It can be Nikolaev or Odessa. If you were without a car and reached the sea capital of Ukraine by train, then it‚Äôs easy to go further. Half a kilometer from the railway station is the famous Privoz market, and next to it is a small bus station, from where minibuses leave to Koblevo. You can also use any public transport that goes in the direction of Nikolaev. And from this city they get to Koblevo in two ways - either by minibus, which goes directly to the resort area, or by bus, going to Odessa. Any of them will take you to Koblevo. Base "Dolphin", of course, is located in the resort area. But from the bus station of the village every half hour there are small minibuses that make stops at all hotels and pensions. The hotel provides a paid transfer from the railway stations of both cities and Odessa Airport.



Rooms & Facilities

Most tourists who visit here note the convenient location of the Dolphin Hotel. Koblevo is a rather large village, and this base is located in its very south, right on the seashore. Only ten meters to the beach from some rooms! The hotel has its own well-groomed territory, three buildings and a cottage. A total of one hundred and ninety-three rooms. All of them are cozy, equipped with modern furniture, plumbing, comfortable. There are standards, junior suites, two-three bedroom apartments, suites and VIP rooms. The service staff speaks Ukrainian, Russian and English. The rooms have free Wi-Fi. When checking in, the administration conducts a tour of the hotel. The rooms offer fantastic sea views.

Beach

The coast and the Hotel Dolphin (Koblevo) are very wide, clean, with fine sand, and extend for two whole miles. There are all conditions for children, there are many sites, and in addition, the village itself is famous for its attractions and a water park in the immediate vicinity of the beach. The recreation area itself is well-maintained, sunbeds and sun loungers are placed everywhere. Shallow water is an added bonus. After all, tourists can put a deck chair directly in the water and indulge in relaxation, as if somewhere in the Maldives. There is another place on the territory of the Dolphin Hotel (Koblevo), where you can sunbathe and swim. This is an outdoor pool. The water in it is always heated, and it is open in any weather. The pool has a special children's area with a depth of one meter. There you can swim even at night, arrange competitions, book parties. There is a jacuzzi in the pool.
