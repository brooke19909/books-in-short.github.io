Turkey, Belek (Belek) - Belek Beach Resort Hotel 5 *: description and reviews

Having a favorable geographical position on the coast of the warm Mediterranean Sea, the resort town of Belek in Turkey annually attracts the attention of tourists not only from Russia but also from around the world. Reasonable prices and quality service allow you to come here on vacation with the whole family, and not only for a couple of days, but even for several weeks. In addition to the beaches, the relatively young resort is famous for its golf courses. At the moment, one of the most famous centers of this sport, and not only in Turkey, namely Belek. Belek Beach Resort Hotel 5 * is an accommodation option that certainly deserves the attention of travelers.

Hotel location and description





The resort complex is located on the first line of the coast (200 m from the sea), 35 km and half an hour from the international airport of Antalya and 45 km from its center. The hotel is located not in Belek itself, but in a small village 15 km from it - Bogazkent. The complex was commissioned in March 2005. Consists of separate 4- and 5-storey buildings. Graceful walking paths on the territory of Belek Beach Resort Hotel 5 *, winding between palm trees, will lead you directly to a sandy beach or a spacious pool with water slides. restaurants and bars





Turkish resorts are famous for their hospitality, affordability and luxury all-inclusive system. In this regard, Antalya and Alanya are in no way inferior to Belek. Belek Beach Resort Hotel 5 * offers its guests not only a simple all inclusive, but also the Ultra system. However, they are limited in time and are valid from ten in the morning until midnight. Entertainment and sports





The hotel has its own pebble-sand beach with free sun loungers, towels, umbrellas and mattresses. The nearby bar serves refreshing drinks, ice cream and light snacks. The hotel has a spacious swimming pool, as well as with water slides and one indoor (for children). The following types of entertainment services are provided for free: water aerobics, mini-golf, discos, amphitheater, daytime animation, an entertainment center and a library, beach and simple volleyball, basketball, squash, table tennis. Billiards, a scooter, a catamaran, a games room, water activities, including skiing, horse riding, are paid separately. If you relax with your children, then quiet and calm Belek (Belek) is quite suitable for you.
