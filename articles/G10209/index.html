Alova fabric: description and advantages

Today, outerwear made of membrane material has gained great popularity. Its advantages are that it removes moisture from the inside and does not let it pass from the outside. Protective membrane materials include Alova fabric. It has an artificial origin, it has a knitted base, a membrane coating and has a water-repellent effect.

Description

Matter "Alova" belongs to the group of artificial fabrics, it is presented in the form of knitwear made of 100% polyester on the front side, and on the inside has a membrane coating. It is this fabric that protects and does not allow moisture and wind to penetrate from the outside, but can pass body and air vapor from the inside.





The layers of this fabric are tightly interconnected. Because of this structure of the material, it is classified as breathable, and the outer surface of the knitwear is quite resistant to natural phenomena, as well as to wear.

Fabric advantages





It should be noted that the “Alova” fabric does not rustle, it was specially developed for the manufacture of outerwear for special occasions when it is important to move silently. It can be designed for lovers of hiking, skiing, as well as for athletes who engage in sports such as mountain climbing, mountaineering and for people who are interested in fishing and hunting.

Production and raw materials

Matter called Alova is made from two-layer synthetic raw materials. The first layer is made of knitted polyester, and the second is made of synthetic polymers with a membrane coating. Under pressure, a membrane film, heated to a certain temperature, is applied to the main fabric and adheres tightly to it, forming an adhesive compound.





The characteristics of the Alova fabric are as follows. Its outer surface may consist of different types of bases, it depends on their purpose. Options are presented from knitted, umbrella, raincoat material; polyester fabric with camouflage colors is in great demand. The purpose of this surface is to provide the finished product with high strength, sufficient thickness and beautiful appearance.

The excellent properties that the Alova fabric possesses are due to the inner layer. Such special material was made for people who work in harsh conditions. Numerous tests and reviews on the Alova fabric confirm that it reliably provides protection in harsh climatic conditions.





Fabric Features

Membrane matter has the following characteristics:

  • Water resistant. It has the ability to withstand moisture of 8000 mm water column, the membrane tissue does not allow moisture to penetrate. Sewn clothes made of such a protective material will not get wet in the pouring rain.
  • Breathability. Thanks to this fabric, the skin breathes and is not blown. The value of this indicator is 1.5 dm 3 / m 2 sec.
  • Vapor permeable fabric. This property means the ability to remove body vapor. This indicator is 1000 g / m 2/24 hours.
  • Thermal insulation. Keeps fabric warm perfectly.
  • Hypoallergenic properties. It does not cause allergies and protects against the spread of dust mites and microorganisms, due to which such reactions can occur.
  • Wear resistant material. If you follow the proper care of the product, it can last a long time and at the same time not lose its qualities.
  • Frost resistance. Membrane fabric "Alova" is resistant to cold.
  • Dirt-resistant matter. The fabric has a dirt-repellent property.
  • Ease of care. Products from it can be washed both in the washing machine and manually, they are not subject to deformation and hood, do not shrink.

Care Rules

The fabric is unpretentious in the care. It is practically not contaminated, but to maintain its properties you need regular washing. In order for matter not to deform and not to lose properties, it is necessary to observe simple rules:

  • You should familiarize yourself with recommended care methods.
  • Membrane materials must be washed in warm water. For this, manual and machine modes are used, but clothes should not be soaked in advance.
  • Special detergents or regular liquid soap will be required.
  • After that, the thing is air dried.
  • Ironing is done from the outside. The surface of the iron should not be heated above 110 degrees.

So that matter does not lose its excellent properties, you can not use bleaches and chlorine products. Not suitable for her ordinary washing powder. Due to such means, the material will quickly lose its neat appearance. You can’t dry such clothes near heat sources, it is advisable to hang them on the street. If you follow the rules of care, you will be able to maintain the attractive appearance and properties of this durable material.

What is it used for?

Such membrane material is designed specifically to make special-purpose clothes, therefore, products made from it are intended for those who are in difficult climatic conditions.

Pea jackets, jackets, windcheaters, overalls, trousers are sewn from fabric. "Alova" is used to receive various items of equipment: tents, tents, backpacks, bags. The material is often used in the creation of clothing for fishermen, hunters, tourists. Fans of the military style will also find wonderful kits for themselves.





Thus, the suit made of Alova fabric will reliably protect against frost and due to its heat-insulating properties it will retain heat. A product made of a membrane sheet will create maximum comfort for its owner, it will not be electrified, and with good proper care can last a long time.
