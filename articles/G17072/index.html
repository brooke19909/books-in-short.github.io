Hotel The Chilli Salza Patong 3 * (Thailand / Phuket): photos and reviews of tourists

Phuket Island is one of the most respectable resorts in Thailand. He is very popular among Russian tourists. This is explained, among other things, by the ability to rent inexpensive and at the same time quite comfortable housing. For example, many domestic vacationers book rooms in Phuket at the Wynn Chilli Salza Patong 3 * hotel. Reviews of tourists this hotel has earned good.

Where is the hotel situated

This hotel is located, according to many tourists, very convenient. It is located almost in the center of the small city of Patong. The beach from its territory can be reached in just 10 minutes. Five minutes walk from the hotel is one of Patong's busiest streets - Bangla Road - with the large Jungceylon Shopping Center.





The nearest airport is Phuket Airport, 45 km from The Chilli Salza Patong 3 * Hotel. Tourists arriving to the hotel by plane arriving on the island take about an hour. In most cases, vacationers who have booked rooms at this hotel get to their place on the transfer bus of their operator. You can also get to the hotel and take a taxi.

General description of the hotel

The hotel The Chilli Salza Patong 3 * is a modern three-story building with rather original architecture. This hotel looks, according to many tourists, pretty pretty. Most of the guests also like the way its territory is framed. The courtyard is tiled and landscaped.

The front desk at this hotel is open 24 hours a day. In order to check into the hotel, guests who have rented a room in it need to give the administrator a copy of the passport and a deposit of $ 100. Check-in at this hotel is at 14 hours, check-out is at 12 hours. If necessary, guests can book a transfer to the airport here.

Room Reviews

The opinion of Patong guests about this hotel was mostly positive. The rooms at The Chilli Salza Patong 3 * Hotel are always clean and comfortable. The maids here are very responsible and also polite to the guests. The beds in the hotel rooms are comfortable, and the bedding is not washed, new and clean.

In particular, this hotel deserved good reviews of its guests for the presence of balconies in the rooms. Here, if necessary, you can quickly dry your beach accessories. On the balconies, among other things, light tables with chairs are installed. So in the evening, hotel guests have the opportunity to sit here with some sort of drink. But the views from the balconies of the hotel open, unfortunately, not too beautiful.

Many tourists attribute the advantages of rooms rented at this hotel, including good noise isolation. No sounds from the corridor or from the street to the guests of The Chilli Salza Patong Hotel (Phuket) 3 * hotel do not interfere. The residents of the hotel are mainly residents of European countries and Russians. Noisy Chinese and Koreans do not rent rooms here.

The apartments in this hotel are equipped, according to tourists, unfortunately, not too good. For example, a balcony door can independently open in a room, tables wobble, etc. The furniture in the rooms is quite old and even slightly cracked.

Showers in the hotel

The rooms intended for the adoption of water procedures in this hotel, according to many guests, are equipped relatively well. Plumbing in showers is working properly. It looks quite new at the same time. Maids in the hotel wash the showers and toilets quite often. They periodically take out the garbage.

Some tourists think that many tourists consider the lack of bathroom accessories designed for brushing their teeth. Unfortunately, there are no glasses for toothbrushes in the showers of The Chilli Salza Patong 3 * hotel. Putting such accessories just on the sink, of course, is extremely unhygienic. Therefore, tourists who have booked a room in this hotel should take a glass with them from home.

Also, many vacationers cause certain complaints about the hotel due to frequent interruptions with hot water. Having come to the room from the beach, many, of course, would like to take a shower and wash off the salt. But this succeeds, unfortunately, by no means always.

The hotel, for example, has free Wi-Fi (with good speed). In addition, hotel guests can use the outdoor pool. Another rather convenient service provided to the residents of the hotel is a barbecue. If desired, guests can cook their own barbecue. Also on the territory of the hotel are:

This property also offers room service, such as breakfast and drinks.

Hotel infrastructure reviews

According to most guests, living at the Chilli Salza Patong 3 * hotel (Phuket) is quite convenient. Politely to the guests are not only the maids, but also other hotel employees. Some complaints from tourists is caused only by the behavior of the waiters. Sometimes they can be rude.





This hotel has earned good reviews, including for the availability of parking near it. It does not belong to the hotel itself, but to the city. But the car on it can be set absolutely free.

A good opinion about the service at this hotel among its guests was formed, among other things, because all the respected employees here know how to speak English. Unfortunately, none of them knows the Russian language. In any case, all hotel guests' problems try to solve quickly.

Dining at the hotel

Rooms at The Chilli Salza Patong 3 * (Phuket Island) are rented with breakfast. On the food in this hotel, its guests respond not too well. In the mornings, only sausages, fried eggs, bread, tea and juice are usually served here. Therefore, many hotel guests prefer to have breakfast, lunch and dinner in the cafes and restaurants working nearby. Cooks of such establishments in Patong are usually prepared very well. The dishes here are always fresh, warmed up and tasty. In addition, they are very inexpensive.





In addition to the restaurant, the hotel also has a small bar. Here, if desired, guests can order all kinds of snacks and drinks. Reviews about this institution on the network are mostly positive.

Hotel beach

The coastline is located at a distance of approximately 600 meters from this hotel. In most cases, guests of the Wynn Chilli Salza Patong 3 * hotel (Phuket, Patong) visit Patong Beach. The place is for recreation, according to many tourists, very suitable. The total length of Pathog Beach is 4 km. It is covered with light fine sand. The entrance to the sea on this beach is gentle. Therefore, it is very convenient to relax with children here. The water in the sea at this place during the holiday season is always clean. Moreover, it is also very warm (like fresh milk).

Some disadvantage of Patong Beach, according to tourists, is that it is rarely cleaned. Cigarette butts, algae, and paper can be littered on the shoreline. There are always quite a lot of people on this beach. But at the same time, the infrastructure here is developed just fine. On the coastline of Patong Beach, there are many cafes, restaurants and various entertainment venues.

Unfortunately, there is no natural shadow on this beach. But tourists at any time can rent sun loungers and umbrellas. They stand on Patong Beach not too expensive.





City infrastructure near the hotel

The tourist service in Patong itself near the hotel is developed just fine. Near the hotel there are many shops of various specializations. Hotel guests have the opportunity to purchase food, beach accessories, souvenirs, etc. There is also a scooter rental point next to this hotel, which many guests find actually very convenient.

sights

Works at The Chilli Salza Patong 3 * hotel (Thailand), among other things, and a tour desk. If desired, hotel guests can contact its staff for help in organizing leisure activities on the island. In fact, there are a lot of attractions in Patong and Phuket. If desired, hotel guests can visit, for example:

  • National Park "Khao Lak";

  • Museum "Talang";

  • Temple of the Big Buddha.

Khao Lak National Park is a beautiful area that stretches along the sea. Conventionally, it is divided into mountain and coastal parts. In this park you can go boating, spend the night at a campsite, and look at exotic animals living in a special nursery.

The Talang Museum is located in the western part of the island. It was opened in 1985. The basis of its exposition is objects related to the sisters Muk and Chan, who saved Phuket from the Burmese invasion in 1785. Also in this museum you can look at samples of stone rocks of the island, a sculpture of the 9th century, preserved in full growth, etc.





Big Buddha is a huge statue located in the capital of the island - the city of Phuket. It is located on Mount Nakked at an altitude of 400 meters above sea level. Next to it is a temple where you can receive the blessing of local monks.

What you should know about

The staff at this hotel is polite and attentive to their duties. But sometimes the staff of this hotel can still be inattentive to their guests. Some vacationers note, for example, the fact that hotel maids may not close the door in the room after cleaning. Thais - the majority of people are honest and do not commit thefts. However, guests from other countries also live in the hotel. Therefore, an open door left by a maid can cause serious trouble. Most likely, you should not leave money, valuables and documents in your room unattended at this hotel.

Many tourists advise beginners who decide to book this hotel in Patong to take some mosquito repellent with them on a trip. There are really a lot of mosquitoes in Thailand, including hotel rooms. In the absence of protection, they can ruin the whole impression of relaxation.
