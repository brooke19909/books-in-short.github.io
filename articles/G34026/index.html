Vladivostok, recreation center "Mayak": description, reviews, prices

Everyone wishing to have a pleasant and carefree time upon arrival in Vladivostok, the Mayak recreation center offers excellent conditions for living, dining and entertainment. The base is located just half an hour from the city. Pleasantly please tourists and the local prices. And probably many Russians know about the magnificent natural park, clean air and landscaping of the territory on which the recreation center is located.





Location and description of the recreation center "Mayak"

In a very picturesque and convenient location is the recreation center "Mayak" - Vladivostok, Shamora (not far from Lazurnaya Bay). Nearby is Cape Vilkova. The complex has a vast territory - more than 6 hectares. There is also an outdoor pool, a well-kept beach, a Balinese SPA and everything you need for a comfortable and fun stay with children. The complex has excellent mobile communications, as the MTS tower is located here.

In addition, Mayak has its own well, water from which is supplied to all rooms and facilities of the complex, an autonomous sewage system and the possibility of uninterrupted power supply, which even Vladivostok often cannot please its residents. The Mayak recreation center is also safe against ticks, because twice a year the entire territory is treated with special means.

The Mayak complex is completely fenced and is guarded and has 24-hour video surveillance. Vehicles rarely travel across the territory of the recreation center, which also cannot but please its guests. Tourists can park their car in a secure parking lot.

Conditions for children

Children under 12 years old can stay in Mayak with adults for free. All rooms are equipped with folding beds for the youngest guests who arrived on vacation in Vladivostok. The recreation center "Mayak" took care of the leisure of children. So, a game room of 80 square meters is provided for them. It has a huge number of toys, houses, pillows, kits for creativity and designers. It is worth noting that visiting the game room for guests of the Mayak is completely free. In addition, there are several color tables where children can play relaxing games, draw or read a book. There is also the opportunity to watch the Carousel TV channel. There is something to do in the hotel and older children. Air hockey and slot machines, as well as a game labyrinth, are at their disposal.

In one of the cottages, located on the territory of the complex, there are creative workshops in which children, under the guidance of an experienced master, can learn how to make crafts with their own hands or sculpt from clay.





Adult Activities

While children are engaged in a variety of games, adults can enjoy walks in the park, equipped with decorative lighting and having a thoughtful landscape design. It is worth noting that the Mayak recreation center (Vladivostok, Shamora) has three gardens (rhododendrons, continuous flowering, Japanese), in each of which vacationers can arrange for themselves a magnificent photo shoot.

Fans of outdoor activities are provided with opportunities for playing futsal, basketball or volleyball at sports fields with specialized equipment.
