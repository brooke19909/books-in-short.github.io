Overview of the Zanussi ZWY 180 washing machine

Polish washing machine Zanussi ZWY 180 appealed to many domestic customers. Unfortunately, the issue of square meters is still relevant. It is for this reason that it is not always possible to choose what you want. However, at present this is no longer a disaster. Manufacturers of washing machines have developed compact models with vertical loading. They can be installed even in an inaccessible corner, the main thing is that it is convenient to open the lid. If you choose Zanussi ZWY 180, then you do not have to give up other furnishings. Model dimensions: 60 √ó 45 cm, with a height of 85 cm. She will be an excellent assistant to every housewife, working for a long time without breakdowns. Let's get to know this model in more detail. We will understand what its advantages and disadvantages are.



Characteristic

This model belongs to the category of devices with vertical loading. You can load a drum with a maximum of 5 kg of laundry. Belongs to economical models - washing and energy consumption class - A. Control panel - electronic-mechanical, buttons and knob are conveniently located. The laundry is wrung out with the help of 800 rpm, corresponds to the class - C. After the end of the regimen it needs to be dried, since the residual humidity is within 72%. There are 20 modes for washing. They are divided by type of tissue. Additional rinsing, water adjustment, incomplete loading, fast program - all these functions are equipped with the Zanussi ZWY 180 model. Repair in case of minor breakdowns can be carried out independently. For example, clean the drain hose or filters. However, if the problem is something more serious, it is recommended to contact only the service center.

The device has leakage protection, adjustment of the imbalance of the drum. The consumption for one washing cycle is: water - 49 liters, electricity - 0.95 kW / h.



Benefits

In order to make a list of the advantages of the Zanussi ZWY 180, many real reviews have been studied. Thanks to the owners, it became possible to appreciate this model. So let's get to know the benefits.

  • Vertical loading allows you to install the device in a remote place.
  • Excellent value for money and quality.
  • Large selection of programs (not all models can boast of such a quantity).
  • The control panel is convenient and intuitive.
  • The main modes are performed perfectly (washing, rinsing, spinning).
  • There is no vibration, it does not slip on the floor.
  • The design is concise, it looks simple and neat in appearance.
  • The presence of an economical quick wash mode.
  • Reliability, quality at a high level.
  • Long term of operation.

disadvantages

Even with such a large list of advantages, Zanussi ZWY 180 has some disadvantages. It is worth noting that there are not many of them.

  • There is no lid lock (critical for families with small children).
  • The hatch opens quite hard.
  • There is no display, because of this it is impossible to accurately determine the residual washing time.
  • It is noisy during spinning.
  • The load loading hatch is small, it is difficult to pack overall things.
  • Short drain hose.

Zanussi ZWY 180: reviews

What is your opinion on this model? Many buyers were satisfied with the quality of the Polish device. Sufficient loading with compact sizes is especially distinguished. The design is certainly rustic, but the device fits perfectly into the bathroom. The buttons and controls are located on the raised panel, which allows you to use them without any problems. It is important that for about 10 years, many devices worked without breakdowns. Of course, for such a period it is necessary not to violate the rules of operation and to do scheduled maintenance. But the comments made by the owners are already described above.



Malfunctions

Like any other appliance, the Zanussi ZWY 180 washing machine has minor breakdowns.

  • The device does not turn on. The reason for this may be improper connection, not tightly closed lid, lack of water supply.
  • The collected water is drained immediately - the drain hose is not installed correctly.
  • The spin and drain mode does not turn on. It is necessary to check the condition of the drain hose, imbalance of the drum may be disturbed.
  • Strong vibration. Such a problem is caused by unmet transportation bolts, improper installation, the location of the washing machine close to the wall, and the uniform distribution of the laundry is disturbed.
