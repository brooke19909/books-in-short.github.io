Afkos Grammos Luxury Collection Boutique Hotel: tourists photos and reviews

Afkos Grammos Luxury Collection Boutique Hotel is a very beautiful hotel, the atmosphere and conditions of which are the best for relaxation. That is why people come here who want to relax and take a break from the daily rush.





general information

Afkos Grammos Luxury Collection Boutique Hotel is located in the northwestern part of Greece, in the village of Nestorio, right at the foot of Mount Grammos. It is an ideal place to reunite with nature. Here everything was overgrown with spruce, pine, cedar, beech and oak forests. And Grammos organize mountain hiking, accompanied by experienced instructors. And from the windows of the hotel you can see the river Aliakmonas. Also, this place is widely known thanks to Lake Orestiada.

The nearest airport to the hotel is Aristotle, 5 kilometers south of Kastoria, which is about 25 kilometers from Nestorio.

Service

Afkos Grammos Luxury Collection Boutique Hotel has everything you need for your relaxation. There is free wireless Internet and private car parking, in the lobby there is a 24-hour reception with a left-luggage office, and here you can also find a rental car. Inside there is a souvenir shop, a small conference room equipped with modern technology, a business center and a meeting room. An airport shuttle and room service are also available. The hotel also has apartments that are fully adapted to the requirements of guests with disabilities.

As for the staff, the people working here speak English and Greek, so if you have problems communicating in a foreign language, it is better to take a phrasebook or dictionary with you on the road.





Leisure

Despite the fact that Afkos Grammos Luxury Collection Boutique Hotel is a modest in size hotel, guests can still find here entertainment to their taste. There is no pool, but there is a small wellness center. He has a modern gym in which guests can not only work out on their own, but also undergo a fitness program under the guidance of an experienced trainer. After that, you can enjoy relaxing in the jacuzzi or sauna.

There is also a playground for indoor soccer and golf. So guests have the opportunity to stretch their legs and diversify their leisure, if you do not want to leave the hotel.

Nutrition

Afkos Grammos Luxury Collection Boutique Hotel 3 * has two restaurants. One offers breakfast, lunch and dinner, paid with accommodation. And the second one can be visited by anyone. But you need to pre-book a table.

Service here is based on a buffet. There are plenty of European breakfasts, for lunch and dinner - meat, fish, vegetables, side dishes, etc. If you want something unusual and original, you can walk around the village and look into some restaurant or tavern - there are plenty of them . And Greek national cuisine, while relaxing in this beautiful country, is definitely worth a try.
