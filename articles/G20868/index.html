Definition

What is a transport tax (Samara region or any other - does not play a role)? You should always know what we pay for. Especially when you consider that many drivers recently are not happy with the huge bills for their vehicles.

This tax is the annual registration fee for your vehicle equipped with an engine. It is important. Anyway, what region are we talking about - about Samara, Moscow or Kaliningrad. The fact remains. The main feature of this payment is its regionality. What does it mean? The transport tax in the Samara region and in any other locality will differ from each other. Its calculation, beneficiaries, as well as the terms and procedures for payment in each subject are established individually. Therefore, we have to take this issue very seriously.

The fact is that here, as in most cases, many are exempted from paying this payment. Yes, there are fewer applicants, but they are still there. Who exactly?
