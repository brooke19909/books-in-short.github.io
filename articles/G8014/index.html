Auckland Natural Attractions





Auckland (California) is located on a plain and rich in natural attractions, which include:

  • Middle Harbor Schorline Park, located on the shore of the bay. Here you can sit and enjoy the water.
  • Joaquin Millet Park, located right in the city center. Here you can wander along the many paths lost among tall trees.
  • Leon Heights Park.
  • Anthony Chabot Regional Park with a wonderful lake.
  • Lake Merritt, located in the city and bearing the title of the largest salt lake in the United States of America.
  • Other small parks and squares.

In all these places you can not only walk and enjoy the fresh air, sit on benches and enjoy the view, but also get acquainted with the local vegetation, some species of which are several hundred years old.
