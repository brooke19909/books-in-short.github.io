Hotel Original Sokos Hotel Olympia Garden 4 *, St. Petersburg: photos, reviews

Guests of St. Petersburg will certainly be interested to know more about the hotel complexes of the city. Hotel Sokos Hotel Olympia Garden is just one of the fantastic options for travelers. The hotel is located in the historical part and has convenient access to the Moscow station and the airport.

A bit about the hotel ...

Hotel Sokos Hotel Olympia Garden is the founder of the Finnish quality of service of the Sokos network in St. Petersburg. The institution has been pleasing guests with a homely atmosphere for more than a year. The hotel is located in the beautiful Olipmiya Park. The hotel is convenient primarily because it is located near Moscow Avenue, as well as a metro station called the Technological Institute.



Hotel Sokos Hotel Olympia Garden is located in a fairly quiet area and at the same time very close to the city center. The Olympic Park protects the complex from external noise and fuss. Moscow Avenue is a very busy highway that connects Pulkovo with the center. The distance from the airport to the hotel is only ten kilometers, so getting to it is not difficult.

Number of rooms

Hotel Sokos Hotel Olympia Garden has at its disposal 348 rooms. All apartments have a very thoughtful and functional layout. The rooms are equipped with modern Scandinavian-style furniture. Classical European interiors give a special charm and comfort.

Hotel guests, regardless of the selected room category, are waiting for a consistently high service. All rooms are equipped with individually adjustable air conditioning, safes, internet, satellite channels. All apartments have natural wood floors. In addition, the rooms are equipped with all ironing facilities. There is also a minibar, and dishes can be provided upon request. The bathrooms are equally well equipped. High-quality plumbing, a hairdryer and other accessories will please the inhabitants of the rooms.

Restaurant complex

The Fransmanni Restaurant at Sokos Hotel Olympia Garden invites visitors to experience authentic French cuisine. The menu of the institution contains real Provencal dishes and a variety of varieties of French wines. The hotel offers a daily buffet breakfast, served in the Fransmanni restaurant.



For private dinners or meetings, private rooms are available. In the evenings, guests can relax by the fireplace. The restaurant also has an outdoor terrace.

The Original Sokos Hotel Olympia Garden also has a Sports Bar 84. This place is intended for joint viewing of sports broadcasts in a relaxed atmosphere. By the way, the bar was named after the events of 1984, when the Zenit city team won the USSR championship. The facility has spacious dimensions. It occupies most of the first floor. Its menu contains not only snacks, but also main dishes.
