Mitsis Rinela Beach Resort 5 * (Greece, Crete, Kokkini Hani): description of rooms, service, reviews

Prices for holidays in Greece annually please thousands of Russian tourists who wish to spend their holidays abroad. Often, to select a destination, vacationers turn to professionals in this matter - to travel agencies, where they can choose the perfect hotel based on the individual preferences of the client.

Many tourist business sharks recommend that Russian travelers visit the Mitsis Rinela Beach Resort & Spa 5 * in Greece. It is here that rest is offered at the highest level with the best conditions.



Location of the hotel Mitsis Rinela Beach Resort & Spa 5 *

This hotel is located on the famous island of Crete, near Heraklion Airport - literally 10 kilometers from it. The coastal village in which it is located is called Kokkini Hani.

In this area, beautiful weather and a mild climate always reigns. Resting here, you can observe a huge number of sunny days, which means that every day spent here will certainly be saturated. On the one hand, the territory of Mitsis Rinela Beach Resort & Spa 5 * overlooks the warm and clean Mediterranean Sea.

general information

This wonderful vacation spot was built back in 1989. After the overhaul made in 2010, Mitsis Rinela Beach Resort 5 * gained a more modern look. At this moment, the buildings were reconstructed, and additional conditions were created for a more comfortable stay for each guest. In 2017, work was also done to improve entertainment on the beach and in the pool area.

In total, the hotel occupies a considerable territory - 50 thousand square meters. m. On it are housings for guests, as well as a huge number of places for entertainment.



Hotel rooms

The hotel consists of 441 apartments, in which everything is arranged, as it should for five stars. There are several buildings for living. Guests can stay in rooms of different categories: this is a family VIP, VIP on the beach, as well as a suite. The interior of each of them was designed by professional designers who took into account all the moments in the organization of maximum comfort for guests. All furniture installed in the hotel is made of expensive wood; each room has a beautiful marble bathroom. All apartments have a climate system that allows you to set the optimum air temperature, regardless of weather conditions.

All available apartments are located in a huge main building, as well as in single bungalows, which are located near the coastline.

Family VIP

The rooms of this category are divided into two types: some of them offer beautiful views of the pool, while others have a view of the surrounding nature. However, both of them are decorated very stylishly and conveniently.

The furniture here has the necessary number of beds - beds with orthopedic mattresses. Also on their area are installed cabinets for storing things, bedside tables. In addition to sleeping areas, family-type rooms have individual living areas equipped with comfortable upholstered furniture, plasma TVs with the ability to view satellite channels, as well as personal telephones. The apartments are equipped with safes for valuables.

VIP on the seashore

When describing the rooms of Mitsis Rinela Beach Resort & Spa 5 *, located on the seashore, one cannot but mention that they are all located in luxurious bungalows, which are located at some distance from the main building.

All apartments of this class are decorated exclusively in warm colors, which allows guests to truly relax and immerse themselves in an atmosphere of warmth and homeliness. The bungalows also have individual air conditioning, which residents can use. The rooms have private bathrooms, in which there are a lot of mirrors and expensive finishes in marble and glass.

For the convenience of guests there is a minibar, which is replenished every day with fresh soft drinks.

Conditions for children

Travel agents often mention that a vacation in Mitsis Rinela Beach Resort 5 * is ideal for couples with small children. It is here that for kids there is a whole range of amenities that will certainly make their stay more comfortable.

In particular, caring parents can pay for babysitting services for their child - a professional in the upbringing will look after the baby and surround him with proper attention. Throughout the day, the hotel has a group of animators who often arrange show programs, participation in which will also bring positive emotions to the children.

In Mitsis Rinela Beach Resort & Spa 5 * (Crete) there is a large children's club in which parents can leave small guests for a fee. There are also animators who can amuse its visitors. Toddlers, being in it, can get acquainted with their peers, participate in game programs, and also just relax actively.

Cafes and restaurants have separate menus for small gourmets, and for their convenience, special highchairs are installed. The summer terrace is equipped with a good playground, as well as a small pool with two water slides, designed for fun descents into the water.



Entertainment for adults

Guests of the hotel will never have to be bored while being on its territory, as there is a wide range of entertainment. In particular, in the evening, there are discos in the club zone where tourists can dance to fiery foreign hits. Throughout the day, they are entertained by a group of animators, most of whom, by the way, speak Russian.

For lovers of cinema novelties at Mitsis Rinela Beach Resort 5 * there is a small cinema equipped with spacious halls, equipped with comfortable seats and high-quality sound and video equipment.

Tourists can use free Internet access throughout the lobby bar, which allows them to stay in touch with their families and friends during the whole vacation time. If desired, Wi-Fi can also be used in your room for a fee.

Beach

Mitsis Rinela Beach Resort & Spa 5 * has its own beach, which is located directly on the hotel. It is completely covered with sand and has a gentle slope into the sea, which is very convenient for young children.

The territory of the beach recreation area is fully equipped with everything necessary for a comfortable stay on it. In particular, there are several locker rooms, cheap cabins, as well as toilets. Comfortable sun loungers and umbrellas are displayed along the coastline, and you can rent a towel and an air mattress.

There are bars on the sandy zone that vacationers can visit at any time.



As for beach activities at Mitsis Rinela Beach Resort & Spa 5 *, they are quite diverse: there are classes for everyone who loves extreme relaxation and more quiet ones. In particular, here you can ride on a yacht, air cushions, water puddles, parachute into the sky, rent a jet ski or go surfing. Tourists who like to explore the underwater world can go down to the depths with scuba diving, which is carried out under the guidance of an experienced instructor.

Pool

In Mitsis Rinela Beach Resort 5 * there are several five large outdoor pools, two of which are filled with fresh water, and three are marine. Each of them is regularly cleaned, if necessary, the water is heated.

Having a swim in the pool, guests can relax on the sun terrace, which is furnished with a large number of comfortable white sun loungers with beach umbrellas. There are slides for outdoor activities here - both adults and children can frolic on them.



Sport

Fans of a healthy lifestyle, once in the territory of the Mitsis Rinela Beach Resort & Spa 5 *, will definitely not be bored, because they are offered a wide range of opportunities to take care of their health, while maintaining their body in shape. The main building is equipped with a large sports hall, in which exercise machines are designed for different muscle groups. Aerobics classes and a fitness room are very popular among women.

Fans of team games can go with their friends to the soccer field or play volleyball on the beach. The hotel also has a well-equipped tennis court with rental equipment for the game.



SPA-center

The hotel has a large spa area, the structure of which includes an excellent Finnish bath, as well as a sauna - their free entry is included in the package. Guests have the opportunity to visit a large massage room with professional equipment and a beauty salon where real specialists work.

In the SPA area there is a large indoor pool in which heating is possible. It is incredibly popular among fans of visiting the steam room. Here, good background music plays, which contributes to complete relaxation, and the interior is able to amaze anyone with its style and beauty.

Services

Guests can use the whole range of services that are provided by hotel staff both on a paid and free basis. In particular, guests have the opportunity to rent game equipment, items for outdoor activities and even cars, without leaving the hotel. For cars, there is an excellent guarded parking lot, the services of which can be used free of charge during the entire period of residence.

Service in Mitsis Rinela Beach Resort & Spa 5 * provides daily room cleaning and linen change every three days. Guests can take their laundry and dry cleaning services.
