RC Quadcopter Walkera QR X350. Model overview and reviews

Choosing a good and inexpensive drone for a beginner can be difficult. By releasing a quadrocopter Walkera QR X350, the manufacturing company has relied on the conquest of this particular market. It doesn‚Äôt matter if there is many years of management experience or not, this quadrocopter will suit everyone. In the base model for $ 390, the user receives GPS, the ability to install an action camera, decent flight time and a DEVO7 transmitter.

Model benefits

The quadcopter Walkera QR X350 is quite small, measuring 290 x 290 x 205 mm and weighing 2.81 kg. It also has a homecoming function. When it is turned on, the drone uses the built-in GPS to automatically land at a given location. This feature is very convenient, especially for beginners. You can also switch the control to manual mode, which will restore full control over the device.

Build quality is normal. Plastic seems cheap, so this is not the most durable quadcopter, but for such a price this is normal. Anti-vibration mount of the camera allows you to get the best quality image in adverse conditions.

Quadrocopter Walkera QR X350 flies easily, quickly gaining height and moderately balanced. It is not difficult to dismantle a light design, and adjustable propellers are not prone to breakage. If problems arise, the blades can be replaced. The LED backlight system allows you to find out if the Walkera QR X350 GPS quadcopter is calibrated correctly, as well as the number of satellites ready to help during the flight.





Design flaws

Quadrocopter Walkera QR X350 RC is very sensitive and requires more than a general understanding of the aircraft model. It seems that experienced operators have a tradition of changing conventional radio transmitters to much more advanced systems. But it depends on the correct determination of satellites whether the numerous functions will work well or, possibly, with lesser performance. Users do not like the need to recalibrate the quadrocopter for the GoHome function to work accurately, as well as regular software updates.





Modifications

The device is made in many options, for example, such as the quadrocopter Walkera QR X350 PRO and X350 Premium. Since the manufacturer advertises the drone as an ideal solution for shooting from the air, there are modifications related to the choice of a video camera. For example, the Walkera QR X350 PRO FPV quadrocopter allows you to get a first-person image. Wi-Fi options are also available. A typical R / C control consists of an integrated screen for viewing video broadcast from a drone. The model was created to compete with Phantom, and repeats many of its functionality.

Quadrocopter Walkera QR X350 GPS has settings that are suitable for users of all levels of training. An experienced pilot who is preparing to record an aerial video using an alternative GoPro camera, or perhaps a beginner who needs help to return the drone to the launch site, just press one button.

Quadrocopter Walkera QR X350 PRO FPV supports the mode of balancing, GPS hold, return home, smart flight, trouble-free return, and also has a height sensor and voltage safety system.

In addition, there are modifications with various transmitters. For example, the Walkera QR X350 PRO DEVO 10 quadrocopter is equipped with a transmitter with ten channels, DEVO 7 with seven, and DEVO F12E with 32 and detachable antennas.





Pro-modification

The Walkera QR X350 PRO quadrocopter is also equipped with a height sensor. In combination with GPS, this allows you to set the drone at a certain distance from the ground. He will remain there until the pilot decides where to move him. This is very useful when photographing or filming.

In addition, the model is characterized by more durable plastic, a higher landing gear, twice the battery capacity (5200 mAh) and the presence of the APM ArduCopter flight controller.

Quadrocopter Walkera QR X350 Pro DEVO 10 features a control panel with 10 radio channels. The remote control can have levers of G-2D or G-3D systems. The first of them allows you to rotate the quadrocopter in two axes, and the last - in three. Users do not recommend buying the 3D version if there is no separate operator to control the camera or if video quality is of great importance.





Premium Modification

This drone features an extremely robust construction with an integrated front FPV camera. Users who conduct critical aerial photography sessions will be able to count on this device. You can install a GoPro camera and 3D control on it.

On the other hand, the innovative and one-of-a-kind dual GPS system gives the quadrocopter the ability to overcome geolocation errors. And besides, the model offers great redundancy and accuracy. Since the drone is powered by a 3 Ah Li-Po battery, it is able to survive in the air for up to 25 minutes of flight time. Users like this model because of its OSD, which displays various flight information or its updates on a TV, monitor or LCD. The drone's on-screen menu reports its current position, displays warnings, satellite signals, and even calculates battery consumption. In addition, there is a reminder of the quadrocopter flight mode - failure-free or on autopilot.





Design features

The quadrocopter implements many good features: returning home with the touch of a button, an altitude sensor, an integrated GoPro camera mount that has a Walkera QR X350 FPV quadcopter, a lithium-polymer battery for brushless motors, as well as a trouble-free 2.4-GHz transceiver that looks as a spacecraft control device from early science fiction films.

Manual flight mode offers 100% freedom of movement that experienced pilots will welcome. The hover mode allows you to stabilize the drone in its current position and hold the specified GPS position. The satellite tracking system simultaneously focuses on several of them, which ensures accurate and reliable positioning. There is a low voltage warning system. The drone is ready to take off right out of the box, so you don‚Äôt have to bother with tools in order to assemble it.





Camera

The advantage of the QR X350 is its willingness to take photos or shoot videos from the moment the device is unpacked. It is enough to put on propellers and a chassis, charge a LiPo-battery or change it to an already charged one. The quadcopter flies well, so the desired image can be obtained quite quickly. As soon as the user gets used to the control, he will be able to use the drone for any type of aerial photography, photographing and first-person viewing, from sightseeing and observation of sporting events to shooting land ownership. It is enough for the user to adapt his schedule to perform all types of tasks, as soon as he learns to fluently control the quadrocopter according to different scenarios.
