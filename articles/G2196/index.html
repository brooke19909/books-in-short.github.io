The recreation center invites you - there are enough nuts and pleasures for everyone

The Krasnodar Territory or the Kuban is one of the precious pearls of southern Russia. A favorable climate, an abundance of solar heat, powerful oaks and centuries-old beech trees, broad-footed firs and firs, spreading alpine meadows, picturesque rivers and lakes - it is hard to imagine a better place to relax. And if guests are greeted by a cozy recreation center - you want to return there again and again.

The recreation center, nuts, flowers, pleasure

The recreation center "Nut Grove" is rightfully recognized as one of the most picturesque corners of the Krasnodar Territory. Tourists are invited to relax in several settlements - the location of the base, one of which is Guamka Farm. Recreation facilities "Nut Grove" comfortably nestled among the spread reserve, famous for its beautiful nature and an abundance of animals and birds, among relic boxwoods and yews, beech and fir, surrounded by majestic cliffs. This place is also famous for its rivers, the waters of which are saturated with silver ions. The water of the Kurdzhips and Matuzka rivers is crystal clear, you can drink it raw. A Matuz waterfalls will certainly amaze with a rainbow-colored game of drops in the golden rays of the gentle sun. Playgrounds, a bathhouse, a cafe, spring pools, mountain grass tea, oak brooms, Caucasian and Kuban cuisine, picnics in nature - vacationers will have something to remember.

We stop at Arkhipo-Osipovka

This place is constantly attracting new visitors. The recreation center "Orekhov" (as its regular guests call it) in the village of Arkhipo-Osipovka has its own attractive feature - it is here that the beautiful and picturesque Volcano River flows into the Black Sea. This is a unique opportunity not only to enjoy the gentle waves of the sea every day, but also to spend a single hour with a fishing rod, so that everyone can have a picnic with a fragrant ear or baked in coals.





And the base itself can only please - a small town with beautiful lanterns that illuminate it at night. Summer houses, comfortable rooms, spacious verandas, delicious food and, of course, the rich nature of the Krasnodar Territory Arkhipo-Osipovka offers all this. The recreation center "Nut Grove" is waiting for guests at any time.

What is the attractiveness of the recreation center?

Of course, in the first place - this is a unique nature, fresh air and a lot of sun. In the Apsheron district (Guamka village) there is the famous Guam gorge. The Orekhov recreation center located here invites guests to stroll along the Lagonaki plateau, whose narrow passages sometimes shrink to just two meters wide. It will be memorable to ride on a small train, which is assembled from only four trailers. The gorge has such a broken path that the locomotive often disappears from the eyes behind the next ledge. But every time before strolling new, stunning paintings are opened. The mountainous and swift Kurdzhips river, crashing into the rocky shores, surges in dangerous waves shrouded in white foam.





And quite the opposite, the calm looks like numerous small waterfalls, trickling down from above.

Come to the symposium

The recreation center "Nut Grove" in each of the above areas is also equipped with special rooms for various events. Here you can celebrate a personal holiday - anniversary, birthday, wedding, or you can take part in a symposium, conference or other important event.





It is noteworthy that the base works all year round, which means that gathering with friends or colleagues away from noisy cities (not without reason all the bases are located in villages - places remote from the hustle and bustle of megacities) will be possible at any time - whether it's New Year's holidays, May holidays ( By the way, from May 1, the pools in the territories of the bases begin to be filled with the purest spring water) or the velvet season rich in fruits.

Don't forget

Do not forget that the guests of the Orekhov recreation center have developed an interesting cultural program. These are all kinds of excursions (such as the Guam Gorge), jeep trips along mountain and forest routes, boat trips by sea and even a trip to a wild beach, where the pristine beauty of nature has been preserved to this day. This is the choice of rest for the taste of the tourist - daily relaxation in sun loungers by the pool, sitting with a fishing rod away from other vacationers, endless trips to nearby districts or swimming in the sea. This is Caucasian hospitality and the cordiality of the staff, who will make every effort so that only positive memories remain about the rest. And of course, the most delicious cuisine - because the food here is prepared using healing water, organic products and with great love.
