How to get to the Crimea by car. Travel Reviews

Fairytale Crimea has always attracted tourists, pilgrims, travelers and adventure lovers. Today, the history of Crimea has replenished with another page, which makes it even more interesting for guests. However, politics and international relations had not only a positive, but also a detrimental effect on the infrastructure of the region ... First of all, this affected the transport links with the peninsula. Today, getting to the sunny Crimean coast is not so easy. The question of how to get to Crimea by car is especially acute today.





Recent routes to Crimea

A year ago, it was possible to get to the blessed land of the peninsula in several ways: by plane, by train, by road. Often, tourists entered the Crimea through Chongar or Krasnoperekopsk. There were no checkpoints in these parts; night trains flew a thin isthmus without stopping. And only stopping at Dzhankoy, sleepy passengers realized that they had already left the mainland. To get to the Crimea by car was also possible without any problems. And only the drivers of heavy trucks passed several control procedures, which were quite ordinary and did not take much time. Crimea airports took flights from all over the world and flights from Moscow, for example, took only a couple of hours.

How to get to Crimea today? Choose a route

After recent events in the international arena, when Crimea became part of the Russian Federation again, and war broke out in the Donbass, most of the previous routes to the peninsula have lost their relevance. For example, getting there by a passenger train became dangerous - you have to go through the territory covered by the war. In addition, most flights are simply canceled. For the same reasons, passenger vehicles lose their relevance. It was also impossible to fly to Simferopol airport by plane at one time. Today flights from Moscow and many other cities of Russia are resumed. Ukrainian and most international airlines do not fly to Crimea yet. You can get to the peninsula by plane rather quickly, but the price can not be called budget. It remains a personal vehicle. You can get to the Crimea by car from the side of Kerch, using the services of the ferry.

The issue of ferry crossing is very acute today. The authorities are doing everything possible to reduce the waiting time for passengers, increase safety in a troubled area, and organize maximum comfort. In the areas of the waiting points there is free internet, doctors and law enforcement officers are on duty. Unfortunately, a long wait aggravates the relationship, and from time to time they heat up. So, in August 2014, while waiting for the queue for the ferry, a verbal skirmish arose between passengers, as a result of which a fight broke out. The search for solutions to the problems continues. Perhaps in the near future pre-registration will be introduced, which will reduce the waiting time. New bridge





The land road to the Crimea is needed by tourists, and business, and the authorities. The bridge can make it possible to significantly reduce material and time costs for the transportation of goods. During the holiday season, he will relieve stress at the entrances to the Crimea. The route "Moscow - Crimea" by car will become easy and affordable. However, no one wants a repeat of the 1944 situation. Plans for the construction of a bridge in Ukraine and Russia were long ago, relevant agreements and treaties were signed long before Crimea became part of the Russian Federation. Today, the state government is engaged in the construction of the bridge closely. All the nuances are taken into account - the bridge must be strong enough to withstand a possible ice attack and a significant traffic flow. And he should in no way interfere with shipping. For the implementation of the new project, the past negative experience, the latest technologies, and urgent needs are taken into account. According to the plans of the authorities of the Russian Federation, the construction of a bridge connecting the Caucasus and Crimea will begin in the near future.
