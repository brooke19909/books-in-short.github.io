Ocean Blue Ocean Sand 5 * (Punta Cana, Dominican Republic): room description, service, reviews

Many tourists make a travel plan in such a way as to be able to not only relax on the beach, but also fully enjoy the exotic. And the Dominican Republic is considered a great place for an unforgettable pastime. This country is famous for its luxurious resorts - one of them is the city of Punta Cana. The weather here allows you to relax on the beach even in the winter months.

There are a huge number of hotels on the Dominican coast - the traveler is faced with the problem of choice, because each hotel has its own advantages and disadvantages. A good place to spend a vacation in Punta Cana is the Ocean Blue Ocean Sand 5 * hotel complex. Of course, before booking a room, you should familiarize yourself with the available information about this place. What is a hotel and is there anything on its territory for a comfortable stay?

Where to look for a hotel? Location Information





Of course, an important criterion for evaluating a hotel is its location. Where to look for the hotel complex Ocean Blue Sand 5 *? The Dominican Republic, namely the lively resort of Punta Cana, is here, just a few minutes walk from the beach, many travelers stop.

By the way, the large shopping center Plaza Bavaro is located very close, which will please fans of shopping. In a few minutes you can reach the largest casino on the coast, in which gambling tourists also have a good time. Punta Cana Airport is only 35 minutes away, which is very convenient.

Description of the hotel complex

Hotel Ocean Blue Ocean Sand 5 * - a relatively new resort complex, which was opened in 2005. The hotel occupies a vast territory and consists of several small buildings and dozens of separate villas built in a unique colonial style. The ancient architecture blends perfectly with the picturesque garden, neat lawns and colorful flower beds.

By the way, not so long ago, all buildings were completely renovated. For the most part, young people from Canada and the United States rest here, less often tourists from Europe and Asia stay at the hotel.

The hotel rooms are furnished with new comfortable furniture. It offers a private terrace or balcony, and offers wonderful views of the sea or green areas.

Hotel Ocean Blue Ocean Sand 5 *: food





This is a hotel with a huge territory and many places where you can eat. The hotel has nine main restaurants - here guests have breakfast, lunch and dinner daily. Each institution stands out with its original interior.

Guests do not complain about the menu - dishes are served in sufficient quantities, there are cold and hot snacks, vegetable salads, fresh fruits and desserts. According to reviews, fish and seafood are regularly served to the table. Drinks are also available for free.

Where is the beach?





What entertainment can the hotel offer Ocean Blue Ocean Sand 5 *? The beach is just a few minutes away. The coastal strip is not too wide, but covered with soft white sand and belongs to the hotel - only guests rest here. Of course, on the beach there are sun loungers and umbrellas, which guests can use for free. Large beach towels are also issued at the hotel - you just need to contact the reception.

If you wish, you can relax on other beaches, which are so rich in Punta Cana. The weather here allows you to swim and sunbathe almost all year round, which makes this country so popular among travelers.

Water leisure and sporting activities for tourists

What can you count on when staying at the Ocean Blue Ocean Sand 5 * hotel? Entertainment is here for every taste - for free you can take a boat or a catamaran, go kayaking. Many travelers love to swim with a mask, because the underwater world here is bright and interesting. By the way, for an additional fee, you can visit the local diving center, undergo training or rent equipment (subject to availability of certificates).

The list of entertainment does not end there - tourists are offered to ride a boat, banana, water skiing and even a luxury yacht. For a small fee, guests can organize exciting fishing.

Additional service





What to count on when staying at the Ocean Blue Ocean Sand 5 * hotel? The service here is good, which is confirmed by the reviews of former guests. The hotel has a small shopping center and a souvenir shop where you can buy almost everything you need, including household items. Guests can use laundry services and ironing facilities if desired.

There is no medical office, but if there are problems, employees call a doctor who is ready to help. Many tourists spend time in an Internet cafe, where you can use computer equipment and access to the Web.

Motorcycles or cars are available for rent. Bicycles are provided free of charge. Several experienced photographers work on the territory, ready to capture the most interesting moments of the guests' relaxation.

Business Events

Many tourists skillfully combine leisure and business. For business travelers, the Ocean Blue Sand Hotel is also suitable. It offers guests a business center where you can use a computer, printer, fax and other office equipment. There is also a large conference room, which is suitable for organizing various events, including seminars, business meetings, training courses, etc.

Internet is available at the hotel, but for an additional fee - this fact should be taken into account when calculating the budget.

Leisure of guests: how to spend time at the hotel?





The hotel provides tourists with all possible ways to have fun and usefully. In the yard there are several pools with sun loungers and umbrellas around. In the mornings, classes are held in gymnastics and water aerobics, and at lunchtime guests are entertained by a team of cheerful animators.

There are basketball, volleyball and indoor soccer courts. There are tennis courts and a golf course - here you can not only rent equipment, but also take a short training course, using the services of experienced instructors. You can have fun playing billiards or bowling. Tourists with pleasure try themselves in archery, go in for sports (there is a fully equipped gym) and horse riding.

Many guests respond positively to the work of the local spa, where you can not only sign up for massages and beauty treatments, but also relax in the jacuzzi and warm up the muscles in the sauna.

In the evenings, hotel guests gather at the theater, where they organize interesting performances, vivid shows and even small fashion shows. At night, you can have some good fun at the disco club - admission is free for guests, but drinks are paid separately.

Of course, do not miss the opportunity to get to know the country and its unique culture. The hotel has its own tour desk, where tourists are helped to plan an exciting trip through the expanses of the Dominican Republic.

Vacation with a child: additional conditions for small tourists

If you are going on vacation with a child, then you are probably interested in the availability of appropriate conditions and additional amenities. Is Ocean Blue Sand 5 * hotel suitable for family vacations? Punta Cana is a true paradise in which not only adults, but also small tourists can relax.

There is a kids club on site that accepts children under 12 years old. Here the guys play, study, engage in art and get to know each other, constantly being under the supervision of educators. Small guests splash in their own section of the pool, and a spacious playground opens up a lot of opportunities for a pleasant pastime. In the restaurant, the waiters offer kids high chairs for feeding. By the way, parents will appreciate the presence of a special menu for children. Babysitters are available on site and can be used for a small fee.

Features of the all-inclusive system

It's no secret that many modern tourists prefer to relax on the all inclusive system. Of course, each hotel offering such a service has its own conditions. Paying the all-inclusive scheme at the territory of the hotel complex Ocean Blue Ocean Sand 5 *, the traveler receives the right to a full three meals a day. This does not apply to all hotel restaurants - there are also such establishments where you need to reserve a place in advance and pay for lunch or dinner on the bill.

In hotel bars, guests can order drinks (non-alcoholic and alcoholic) in unlimited quantities, but only those that are listed on the menu. And in the rooms of the guests are waiting for full minibars.

Tourists also visit the local gym free of charge, practice on sports fields and tennis courts. Non-motorized sports (boating, catamarans, etc.) are not paid extra by guests of the Ocean Blue & Sand 5 * hotel. Punta Cana is a good place to spend a vacation, and thanks to this hotel, a few days in the Dominican Republic will turn into a real adventure full of pleasant and vivid impressions.
