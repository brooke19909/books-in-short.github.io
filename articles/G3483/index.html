How is lens alignment performed?

The alignment of the lens can be disturbed for various reasons. What to do in this case? How to return images to their former sharpness and clarity?

Types of problems





One of the most common causes is a violation of autofocus. It can be the result of active or prolonged use of technology, but it also occurs on new devices.

Another possible reason is a violation in the autofocus of the lens itself. This is found on many models, especially for cameras with a zoom function. Moreover, violations can be different at large and small focal lengths.

Reasons for adjusting the lens:

  • Incorrect factory adjustment.
  • "Unleashing" of the lens, increasing gaps and backlash during long-term operation.
  • Drops and bumps.

It is worth noting that Canon lens alignment is not always required. Sometimes there is a "glitch" due to the specific type of artificial lighting. That is, autofocus goes into flight or undershot. More often, such features are noticeable when the energy - saving lamps are turned on .

To verify this, place the camera on a tripod, then aim at a ruler at a 45-degree angle. Pictures need to be taken with autofocus. Only energy-saving lamps must be on. Then let only incandescent lamps give lighting. With a high probability you will see a flight in the pictures with energy-saving devices.

Service centers say that modern models are less prone to this phenomenon. Fighting this defect is useless and pointless. In addition, it usually does not create tangible difficulties when shooting.





How can lens alignment be restored?

The most reliable and correct way is to contact a specialized service center. Moreover, they will justify both the "carcass" and the lens. If your camera is still under warranty, this procedure will not cost you anything. If not, you will pay a relatively small amount.

Usually this manipulation takes a week. But financial incentives for servicemen can bear fruit. It is real that they can handle the day.

The second option of how the lens alignment can be performed is adjustment in the "carcass" setting. But in some new cameras, this option is no longer there. The likelihood that you will be able to change something is. But to do this in practice is very difficult. Not to mention the cases when you need to adjust the zoom lens, which has various focus disturbances at different distances. In such cases, this method will not help for sure.



But if you need a Sigma, Tamron or Tokina lens alignment, this is an acceptable way. After all, there are no sensible specialists working with such devices in Russia. Or they are extremely difficult to find.

But remember that this process is laborious. In addition, autofocus is largely influenced by the temperature and type of target. It is unlikely that you will be able to recreate those laboratory conditions that are in the service center.

Of course, you can configure it yourself. But this is not recommended. You are more likely to damage the equipment than to tune something.
