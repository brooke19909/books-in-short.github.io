Knitted hat-stocking with knitting needles - dress fashionably!

The stocking cap is very fashionable, it is comfortable to wear. To knit such a product is also not difficult at all, since the hat-stocking is knitted with knitting needles in a straight line. It is not necessary to create rounded lines that require accurate following the pattern and adding / decreasing loops. Another undoubted plus of products created with one's own hand is financial savings. And no one else will have such a thing as yours, unlike the purchased one.





A bit about combining colors

Creating this product, you can show imagination and connect it based on your preferences. Girls and extravagant women can create such a hat from threads of different colors, alternating them. Thus, the problem will be solved, if you do not know where to put the remains of the threads, then make a headdress out of them, called a stocking cap. Knitting first knits the desired number of rows in one color. Then knitting is turned on the front side, the second ball is taken. The preceding and subsequent threads are twisted on the first loop of the row. She still will not be visible, since when stitching the part she will go into the seam. But you get a funny hat-stocking (female). Knit it quickly with knitting needles, so you can start now.

We make calculations and start knitting





First of all, you need to make the necessary calculations. First, measure the volume of the head and record this figure. Let's say it turned out 58 cm. Now you need to dial 30 loops and knit 5 cm with an elastic band, the same number with the main pattern. Simple calculations will help to understand how many loops there will be in 1 centimeter. It is important to warn those who, when they see exactly the same yarn and knitting needles in a magazine or other publication, gain as many loops as indicated in the publication. After all, everyone has a different knitting density. Someone will pick up 100 loops, and another will need to increase them to 110 so that the product is not small or large.

Let's say 1.8 cm turned out to be 1 loop. We solve a simple example 1.8x58 = 104.4. Then you should dial 104 or 105 loops for the main product. Before knitting the cap-stocking with knitting needles, we made the necessary calculations and realized how much to loop. Now you can be sure that at the end of the knitting process there will be no disappointment due to the fact that the product was not sized.

We knit a hat

After the desired number of loops is typed, you can start knitting with an elastic band. This pattern will help the hat sit firmly and not slip. The 2x2 elastic band looks most spectacular, where 2 loops fit together on the wrong side, then the front, then again the wrong side. In the next row, we change the loops. Where there were purl, we knit facial and vice versa. We always remove the first loop, and we sew the last loop on the wrong side. Then the edges will be smooth, without knots. The width of the gum depends on your desire. Further, the fabric is knitted with the selected pattern or facial smoothness, it is easier.

Determine the length





Now you should decide how long the stocking cap will be. You can knit it with knitting needles so that it is slightly or much lower than the back of the head. If it is slightly lower than it, then after the fabric is connected to the Techka line, we begin to knit 2 together in each facial row through the same number of loops. Next, close the loops on both the front and back rows. When there are 7 loops left, we knit together and close the last loop.

If you want a long hat-stocking, like Pinocchio, then you must first draw on the paper this descending part of the hat, and then through a certain number of rows, following the pattern, knit 2 loops together on each side. Finish the product the same way.

How to knit a stocking cap with knitting needles so that a picture flaunts on it

Related things reveal the creative abilities of the craftswoman. Romantic natures may well want to tie a heart like that in the photo on the side of the hat. Then, as a result, we get with a pattern a hat-stocking with knitting needles. Knitting pattern simplifies the task of creating a pattern. By the way, a needlewoman can create any pattern, even tear out a portrait of her beloved. To do this, you need to take a photograph of it and spread it with a pencil and a ruler into horizontal and vertical stripes to make small squares with a side of about 0.5 cm. Each square is a loop. Start from the bottom row, changing the glomeruli, according to the color of the square in the photo.





As for the heart, it fits in a single color, and the cap itself - in another. White and pink tones look beautiful. For example, the canvas itself is white. We knit this thread, we approach the bottom row - the tip of the heart. As you can see in the diagram, at first (for example, with a pink thread) one loop is knitted. We knit a pink thread once with white, and then knit to the end with white yarn. With a thread of the same color, we knit the next row with purl stitches. When we come to the drawing, we knit 3 loops with a pink thread, symmetrical to the one that we tied on the front side. In the next row, 5 loops of pink yarn are already knitted, in the other - 7. All this is clearly visible in this diagram.
