Hot spring Avan in Tyumen: detailed information, location, prices

To see the snow-covered landscapes, while basking in one bathing suit in a hot thermal spring, it is not necessary to go far, far abroad in the winter. You can also get indescribable emotions in the Northern Urals by visiting one of the hot springs near the city of Tyumen - Avan. We will tell in detail about the unique suburban complex in the article.

Hot spring Avan - what is it?

The thermal spring Avan is unique in that year-round temperature in it does not drop below +45 degrees. And this means that you can swim here year-round, even in the most severe frosts. It is also distinguished by a high level of mineralization - 75 g / liter of water.





The waters of the hot spring Avan in Tyumen are rich in elements such as bromine, iodine, sodium chloride. Bathing here will be an excellent preventive measure for people with diseases affecting the motor apparatus, vascular, nervous systems. Surprisingly, you can also drink water from the source - it will have a beneficial effect on the digestive and genitourinary system.

About the country club "Avan"

Avan spring is located on the territory of the country club of the same name. It differs from similar recreation centers in that it functions year-round. A considerable advantage will be the fact that the pools in Avan are open. In a bowl with water from a hot spring, hydromassage installations, a waterfall, attractions for small visitors are installed.





Additionally, in Avan (Tyumen - the closest city to the resort) there are three swimming pools:

  • Massage round with nozzles. Its depth is 0.9 m, diameter - 6 meters.
  • Large rectangular pool with settings like Charcot's shower. Parameters - 9 x 16 meters, depth - 1.3-1.7 meters.
  • Small children's pool with a geyser and a waterfall. Diameter - 4 meters, depth - 0.8 m.

Cost of services

During the summer period, the Avan suburban complex has a discount system, so the prices at this time are the lowest:

  • The cost of an adult admission ticket on weekdays is 400 rubles.
  • Entrance adult ticket on weekends and holidays - 500 rubles.
  • Children under 10 years old have a rest with accompanying people for free.
  • From Monday to Wednesday, an entrance ticket for students and senior citizens costs 240 rubles.

In winter, visiting a hot spring will cost a little more:

  • A ticket for an adult from Monday to Thursday is 500 rubles.
  • A ticket for an adult from Friday to Sunday, as well as on holidays - 700 rubles.
  • Children under 10 years old also have a rest without entrance fees.

Contact Information

Hot spring Avan is located just 31 km along the Irbitsky highway from Tyumen. His exact address: Tyumen district, Kamenka village, st. Mira, d. 4, p. 2. Coordinates on the map: 57 ¬∞ 14'28.2 "N, 65 ¬∞ 07'52.7" E.





There are two ways to get to the suburban recreation center "Avan":

  • By private car along the Irbitsky tract. The driving directions are as follows: follow from Tyumen in the direction of Roshchino airport, on the ring near this point, turn towards Kulakova-Kamenka-Irbit direction. As soon as you reach the village of Kamenka, you will see a sign of a turn to the country club.
  • On a public vehicle. Every hour, regular buses and fixed-route taxis from Tyumen run through the village of Kamenka. There are no direct flights, but all vehicles make a stop near this point at the request of the passenger.
