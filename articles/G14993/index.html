How many rooms are there in the hotel? Description of rooms

  • 50 type C rooms (two bedroom suites) - these rooms consist of two separate bedrooms, a kitchen bathroom and a sitting area (dining room combined with a living room).

How far is the beach?

The Sigma Resort Jomtien hotel complex owns its own section of Jomtien Beach, which is located across the road, just 50 meters from the hotel building. This is a small, cozy stretch of coast with convenient access to the sea and a shallow area for bathing children. Tourists are advised to acquire appropriate shoes. On the shore there are comfortable sun loungers with parasols. Beach towels are available free of charge in the hotel building.
