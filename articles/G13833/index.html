Popular ski resorts in the Krasnodar Territory

Each person presents rest in his own way. Someone likes to sunbathe under the hot sun on the shores of the southern sea, while someone prefers to play sports and overcome all kinds of difficulties. Ski resorts of the Krasnodar Territory invite professional athletes, beginner skiers, as well as those who prefer to spend time with their families. In this article we will tell you about the most popular ski centers located in the south of our country.



Krasnaya Polyana

This is perhaps the most popular and most expensive ski resort in our country. It is located near Sochi, surrounded by the beautiful Caucasus Mountains and dense forests. Vacationers expect a wonderful stay in comfortable hotels, unforgettable skiing from the steep slopes of skiing and snowboarding, as well as numerous excursions and entertainment. They appreciated the clean mountain air, which has a beneficial effect on the body, the beauty of the local nature, as well as a high level of service. Experts say that the advantages of this resort can argue with the best European counterparts. Currently, the resort has several ski services, and Alpika-Service is considered one of the best.



Ski resorts of the Krasnodar Territory. Lagonaki

In this unique natural region, everyone will find an interesting activity for themselves. Skiers and snowboarders enjoy riding the steep slopes throughout the winter season, climbers are attracted by inaccessible peaks, and rafting enthusiasts are attracted by the raging streams of the Belaya River. Those who do not like extreme sports can appreciate the beauty of the local nature and relax in silence from the stresses of a big city. On the territory of Lagonaki there are many tourist complexes that are ready to meet tourists all year round. Snowboarders and fans of alpine skiing often stop at the Azish-Tau ski complex, which is considered one of the best for sports and recreation. You can also choose one of the tourist centers for your vacation, such as Edelweiss, Alpika or Silver Key.



Ski resorts of the Krasnodar Territory. Dombai

This beautiful resort is one of the best tourist centers of our country. Alibek, Dombay-Ulgen and Amanauz are the three main gorges of Dombay, which form the famous Dombay glade, spread at an altitude of one and a half thousand meters above sea level. The resort has many modern centers of sports recreation, where skiers, snowboarders and climbers from all over the country come together every year. Like many ski resorts in the Krasnodar Territory, the Dombai resort boasts slopes of varying difficulty, modern lifts, as well as numerous hiking trails and routes. It often hosts Russian and international competitions in alpine skiing, giant slalom and snowboarding. This resort has long been chosen by professional athletes of various levels and experienced sports enthusiasts. But for beginners it is not so comfortable here, since the complexity of some routes is not available to them.

Conclusion

Modern ski resorts in the Krasnodar Territory attract more and more Russian tourists. Many vacationers appreciate the local service, hotels and infrastructure. They argue that Russian resorts are not inferior to European counterparts and increasingly prefer to spend winter holidays in Russia.
