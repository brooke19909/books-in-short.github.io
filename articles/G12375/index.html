Basic rules for choosing a nettop

It is known that the first computers had huge system units, which required a separate room. Due to their specialized purpose, they were used only in certain areas of science. With technological development, the electronic components of computers have decreased significantly, which allowed manufacturers to make computers for domestic use. At the same time, a variety of form-factors of the hulls that exist to this day arose:

  • Desktop;
  • Nettop
  • A laptop.

For home use, users often choose nettops. They are a kind of golden mean. From desktop personal computer nettop It features a compact size and low power consumption, and from a laptop - increased computing power. The appearance of the nettop resembles a small plastic candy bar on a stand. It is installed on the desktop and peripheral devices such as a monitor, speakers, keyboard and mouse are connected to it. Then begin to use.





How to choose the right nettop

If you want to purchase a miniature computer, it is not advisable to rush. It is necessary to take into account the basic rules in order for the device to fully satisfy its potential user capabilities.   1. Pay attention to the characteristics, since the productivity of working with a nettop depends on them. Of great importance is the processor frequency and the amount of internal RAM. Also pay attention to the hard drive if you plan to store data.
  2. Consider attaching a nettop. The vast majority of miniature computers are located on the table, using a special stand. However, there are models that are mounted on the back of the LCD monitor.
  3. Check if the nettop makes a lot of noise during use. Manufacturers of some computers equip them with loud coolers, which irritate users' nerves. It is necessary to take this aspect into account so that the device does not have to be changed.
