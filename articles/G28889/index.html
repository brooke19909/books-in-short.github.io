Hotel Sentido Tara 4 * (Becici, Montenegro): description, services, reviews

Montenegro has long gained popularity among our compatriots as a seaside resort. And the resort town of Becici is known for its beautiful beaches, which are considered one of their best in Europe. In our article we want to talk about one of the resort hotels - Sentido Tara 4 * (Becici, Montenegro).

A bit about the hotel ...

"Centido Tara" (Montenegro) is located in Becici, next to one of the oldest cities on the Adriatic coast - Budva. The hotel is located on the beach, a two-minute walk from the beach 80 (meters). You can get to it quite quickly and simply, since it is only 26 kilometers from Tivat airport.



The complex was completely renovated in 2012. It consists of the Main building (main building) and Magnolia Villas, Mimosa, Palma, Camellia (a complex of two-story buildings). The hotel is very attractive, with a beautiful and well-groomed area, which is nice to walk on summer evenings.

Number of rooms

Sentido Tara 4 * (Becici, Montenegro) offers its guests 227 rooms:

  1. Double apartments - 143 pcs. Almost all of them have a French balcony.
  2. Triple - 54 pcs.
  3. Suites - 28 pcs.
  4. Apartments - 2 pcs.

All are equipped with wireless Internet, television with international channels, safes. In addition, the price of the apartment includes one umbrella and two sun loungers, which can be used on the beach. Most of the rooms have a balcony. Some of them even have a beautiful view of the Adriatic Sea.

The most popular option for tourists purchasing a tour in Sentido Tara 4 * - Standard. This category of apartments is located in the main building and in the villas.

Double and triple rooms are standard. But in family rooms - two rooms: a bedroom and a living room.

Dining at the hotel

Sentido Tara (Montenegro) offers its guests meals in two systems: all-inclusive and half-board. It includes: continental breakfast, breakfast, lunch and dinner. During the day, tourists can eat sandwiches and snacks in the hotel bars. In addition, drinks, coffee, pastries and alcohol are included in the price.



Breakfast, lunch and dinner are served in the hotel's main restaurant. The buffet allows guests to present a large number of a wide variety of dishes. For example, the main restaurant serves the best of Mediterranean cuisine. It is designed for 400 people.

The hotel also has an a la carte restaurant, Gauchos, offering Argentinean dishes to its visitors. It can accommodate sixty people.

The complex also has a beach bar, a poolside bar and a lobby bar.

Complex infrastructure

Sentido Tara 4 * (Becici, Montenegro) has a large conference room for negotiations and business meetings, which can accommodate up to four hundred people. There is also a small meeting room in mini format for 25 guests.

The hotel has an outdoor pool for children and adults with a relaxation area equipped with sun loungers and parasols. In addition, the territory has a hairdresser, beauty salon, gift shop, parking, massage parlor. Laundry facilities are available for a fee. The hotel has a car rental point.

Sports and Entertainment

In Sentido Tara 4 * (Becici, Montenegro), tourists can visit the gym, tennis court, play football, minigolf, billiards, bocce, table tennis, darts, football.

There is practically no animation at the hotel. If you wish, you can arrange any trip for yourself. In the hotel you can rent a car, which will always be at your fingertips.

Hotel beach

"Sentido Tara" is a hotel (Montenegro), which has its own beach with sand and pebble coating. It is located 80 meters from the complex; a leisurely step does not take more than three minutes. On the shore there are sun loungers and sunbeds, they are free, because the use of them is included in the price. The beach itself is covered with sand, but in the sea the bottom is made of small pebbles. The water is always warm and clean.

Resort Becici

Becici is a small provincial town of the Budva Riviera. This part of Montenegro has gained great popularity among holidaymakers due to its wide sandy beaches. Here you have everything you need for a good rest: hotels, restaurants, cafes , entertainment. Becici is an ideal place for a relaxing holiday with the whole family. But for active entertainment it is better to visit Budva. In Becici, most vacationers are pensioners from Europe. In the town they prepare wonderful dishes of fresh fish, from ancient times they were engaged in fishing. Becici is buried in bright greenery surrounded by the sea. Here you can not only swim and sunbathe, but also make exciting and enjoyable walks. The beaches of the resort stretch almost two thousand kilometers along the entire coast.



From Becici you can easily reach all the most interesting places in Montenegro.

Tours and activities

If you have purchased a ticket to "Sentido Tara" (hotel, Montenegro), then your vacation will be very calm and comfortable. However, if you like not only to lie on the sand, but also to travel, then you certainly need to consider an excursion route. The hotel, of course, has representatives (guides) of the host country, but you can book a trip from any agency. Going out on the promenade by the sea, you will find a considerable number of representatives of travel companies offering vying with their excursions. By the way, the cost of their trips is cheaper than in a hotel.

From "Sentido Tara" (hotel, Montenegro) you can independently get to Budva (bus fare - 1.5 euros), and there you can see a wide selection of local attractions.

According to the residents of Budva, in the summer in the city there are ten Russians per inhabitant. How true such accurate data is, is unknown, but the fact that in the summer a large number of our compatriots come to the resort is true. What attracts crowds of vacationers here? Probably, the secret of popularity lies not only in the beautiful beaches and landscapes, but also in the very atmosphere of the city with its historical monuments. It cannot be said that in the city there are historical places at every step, but nevertheless a harmonious combination of some factors creates a certain charm of the resort.



In terms of architecture, the Old Town of Budva is of interest. Charming old streets with Venetian buildings are surrounded by greenery and flowers and surrounded by 15th-century ramparts. Walking around this area, you can feel the spirit of ancient Budva. It has its own attraction - the citadel fortress. Thousands of tourists climb to its observation deck in search of beautiful sea views. There are many cathedrals in the city worthy of attention. Among them - the church of St. John, the church of the Holy Trinity.

Museum lovers in Budva must visit the Budva Library, the Gallery of Modern Art and the Archaeological Museum. In these places you can not only get acquainted with the most ancient archaeological finds, but also plunge into the world of art of Montenegro.

The Budva library is amazing for those who visit it for the first time. And there are reasons for that. The fact is that it is housed in an old Scandinavian-style fortress. In 1979, the building was damaged during the earthquake, but after a couple of years it was rebuilt and strengthened. Currently, the fortress is more like a fairy-tale palace. However, inside it the library interiors themselves amaze, they do not at all coincide with our stereotypes about dusty shelves. The reading room, for example, is a truly luxurious living room with numerous glass cabinets.

For several centuries, the town was as if in hibernation, its borders did not change. And only in recent decades, the resort has grown and gained a new look. And in the old district, as before, the same atmosphere prevails, and its inhabitants live in ancient houses. To get into this spirit, you just need to slowly walk through the quiet Budva streets.

Sentido Tara 4 * (Montenegro): hotel reviews

Speaking about the hotel, I want to turn to the reviews of tourists who shared their impressions of the rest. Can I recommend it to people planning a vacation in Montenegro? According to tourists, Sentido Tara Hotel 4 * (Montenegro) is a good place for a quiet and relaxing family vacation. If you want to really relax and forget about all the problems, then you need it here. Confirmation of the words of tourists is the fact that ninety percent of vacationers are pensioners.



Hotel Sentido Tara Hotel 4 * (Montenegro) has a small territory. Its buildings are located near the sea. Only a cobbled promenade separates the hotel from the beach. The hotel rooms are spacious and beautiful, with good furniture and large, comfortable double beds. Very often it is impossible to sleep in hotels, because everyone gets used to his bed and the slightest inconvenience takes off his sleep as if by hand. But in the Sentido Tara Hotel 4 * good beds and a cozy atmosphere set up for relaxation. The rooms have air conditioning, very well located in the hallways, and not overhead. All plumbing in good, good condition. The bathroom has cosmetics and detergents, replenished as necessary. The rooms are cleaned every day, there are no complaints. As for towels, they are regularly changed, as expected. Unfortunately, beach towels are not provided, if you want to take them, then an extra replacement will cost you money.

The only minor drawback of the rooms is the French balcony. By the way, in our understanding, this can not be called a balcony. The door just opens, and there you will find a surprise from the bar for drying clothes. Of course, this is not enough for a sea resort hotel. However, such balconies are only on the second and third floors of the villas. On the ground floor and in the main building there are full balconies.

Service: reviews

General impression of the hotel

Undoubtedly, the complex has many advantages. Therefore, those who plan to relax in Montenegro can safely recommend Sentido Tara 4 *.
