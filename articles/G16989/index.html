Smartphone Overview HTC Desire 700

The HTC Desire 700 smartphone model, an overview of which will be presented below, is a very controversial novelty of the Taiwanese manufacturer of high-tech phones. Those people who prefer a large screen diagonal and sound quality can fall in love with this device. But if you need a good camera in combination with a high-quality case for the best price, then this phone from a Taiwanese company may not suit you.





Ergonomic features of the model

Smartphone HTC Desire 700 in its design resembles other models of the company. Like them, this phone boasts aluminum inserts, which is a positive criterion in terms of protecting the gadget. However, most of the body is made of glossy plastic, which is both a plus and a minus. The advantage of this HTC Desire 700 phone cover is that it is smooth and easy to clean. Its disadvantage lies in the susceptibility of the phone to scratches, which will be clearly visible on it. The display glass of the model is very high quality, however, due to the fact that it is flush with the case, the smartphone should not be placed with the screen down. Speakers, headphone and charger holes, sound control buttons are installed in accordance with company standards and are located quite convenient for the user.





Smartphone software

Oddly enough, the operating system and shell are not a strong point of the HTC Desire 700 smartphone. The review shows that, despite the relatively recent appearance of the phone on the market, the Taiwanese model does not have the latest version of the operating system, but runs on Android 4.1. Most likely, this is due to the fact that the company does not use the latest models as the processor, but the old Qualcomm Snapdragon S2 MSM8255. Many users may wonder about the need to install new versions of the operating system. Updating Android to later versions is necessary if only because new features of the legendary HTC Sense appear in them.



What is the telephone equipped with

Another model of the Taiwanese company HTC Desire 700 stands out from other phones of this company in that: firstly, it has an old version of Android; secondly, cannot but oppress the people who liked her. The phone has a five-inch screen, the resolution of which is only 960 x 540 pixels. You might think that for such a large display, the resolution is extremely small, but there are no problems with poor rendering of the picture.

The camera of the phone is characterized by eight megapixels, as on many other models of the company from Taiwan. However, unlike other phones, the pictures taken on the HTC Desire 700 smartphone turn out to be somehow faded, and at times blurry. The phone has 1 GB of RAM and 8 GB of internal memory, which is enough to be able to play small-sized toys. But if you want to install games with good graphics, then get ready for freezes and braking. The smartphone battery is rated at 2100 mAh. A full battery charge can easily last for a day and a half with moderate phone load.





Smartphone display

The five-inch diagonal of the phone, of course, attracts attention, but there is also a moment that can push away the buyer. It consists in the resolution of the phone, which is only 960 x 540 pixels. When looking at a smartphone, you will not notice all the disadvantages of such a small resolution. But if there is an opportunity for the same money to buy another model, whose resolution is 1280 x 720 pixels, then why not take this opportunity?

HTC Desire 700. Reviews

As noted above, this model of smartphone from a Taiwanese manufacturer is very controversial. For this reason, you can not give accurate advice regarding the purchase of a model such as the HTC Desire 700. Customer reviews suggest that the main disadvantage of the phone is its outdated operating system, which, among other things, does not have the ability to update. Also, the dissatisfaction of ordinary buyers was caused by the design of the phone. They were not happy with the excessively glossy surface of the gadget, which is why scratches quickly appear on it.





But, perhaps, there will be a slightly different opinion of knowledgeable people about the HTC Desire 700. The reviews that experts left about this phone were more meaningful. They not only appreciated the characteristics of the phone, but also compared it with other models of the same price segment. The responsiveness of gadgets such as the Huawei Ascend G700 and ALCATEL OT Idol X was much higher. However, the phone in question has undoubtedly its advantages. For example, ordinary users and experts note the navigation system of the Taiwanese model. It works perfectly, so in this regard, the phone can give odds even to specialized gadgets. Therefore, of course, this model has its fans.

Phone rates

From three hundred to three hundred and fifty dollars will have to be spent to purchase HTC Desire 700. The price may vary depending on what kind of flash memory the buyer wants to buy as an addition to his phone. Also, different prices can be observed in various dealer stores. However, if it is possible to order a new phone via the Internet, then the cost in a specialized store in China will not exceed $ 320.

The HTC Desire 700 phone, the review of which is presented above, can find its fan among consumers, especially if the most important thing for them is sound quality and a good navigation system.
