Club "Cabaret" (St. Petersburg)

The club began its existence twenty years ago and, perhaps, is considered the oldest, most visited and largest (about 1,500 "squares") gay club in the northern capital of Russia. The institution is part of the Cabaret Art Center located in a two-story building (Ligovsky Prospekt).

On the ground floor there is a large wardrobe (for 500 people), a cafe, restrooms, a theater area (130 seats) with a transforming stage and an isolated dance floor. On the second floor there is a bar, a zone of karaoke and relaxation.
