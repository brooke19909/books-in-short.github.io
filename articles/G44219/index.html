Making a message and a list of textbooks

The envelope insert consists of 2 sheets, one of which has a black and white coat of arms of the school and an appeal to the student, and the second has a list of school supplies. Before you make a letter from Hogwarts, you need to decide on the method of applying the text. Thanks to the "book canon," it is known that the message to future freshmen is written in ink in calligraphic handwriting with curls. But in the modern world, very few have the ability to use a goose or even an iron feather. However, there is a way to make a letter from Hogwarts with your own handwritten effect, for which you need to do the following:

1. Type the text using LinoScript, LegendeC Plain, Ariadna script or any other beautiful font with Russification. Minerva McGonagall's signature should be different from other parts of the letter, and for her it is better to find an imitation of handwriting.

Before you make a letter from Hogwarts, it is recommended to decide on the procedure. It will be optimal to first apply the text and only then cut the sheets.
