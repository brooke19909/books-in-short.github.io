Satellite "Tricolor" - high-quality television in every home

One of the most famous and widespread television operators in Russia broadcasting channels in high quality is the Tricolor TV satellite. According to the company, it has more than 10 million users.

Plate selection

At present, the operator Tricolor broadcasts to the central part of Russia and to Siberia. For these purposes, a total of three satellites are used. With the right equipment and settings in any weather, you can enjoy a clear picture on the screen of your TV.



For high-quality reception of channels, you must install the included dish. But the choice of diameter must be approached with all responsibility, because it is tied to the region of residence. The 55 cm diameter antenna included is not suitable for everyone. It is better if you decide to catch the signal that the Tricolor satellite gives, using a plate with a diameter of 90 cm. This will ensure reliable signal reception in any weather in all regions of the country.

Equipment

If you decide to tune in to the Tricolor satellite, keep in mind that the manufacturer includes a plate with a diameter of 55 cm, 10 meters of cable, 2 connectors, a Ku converter, and a digital receiver in the standard set.
