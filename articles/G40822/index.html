Goa, 3 *: Highland Beach Resort. Description and photos of the hotel, reviews of tourists

North Goa attracts tourists with its snow-white, vibrant beaches, beautiful weather and interesting culture. Naturally, there are many hotels on the coast, ready to offer the traveler comfort, coziness and hospitality. One of them is the 3 * Highland Beach Resort hotel, which is famous for its good living conditions. So what do tourists say about this place and is it worth it to come here?

Description of the territory and location of the hotel





Where exactly to look for the Highland Beach Resort 3 * hotel ? Candolim (Candolim) is a cozy resort village in the northern part of Goa, which is located right on the beach. It was here, among the greenery of palm trees, that the small but incredibly beautiful and comfortable Highland Hotel was built. The international airport is about 50 km away. Naturally, the hotel provides its guests with transportation, but it is still worth preparing for the move. By the way, about 22 km is a railway station.

The hotel is located away from the bustling main street. Nevertheless, there are many shops, cafes, restaurants and even two large supermarkets nearby, which is very convenient. The hotel itself consists of small four-story buildings built in the colonial style. Almost the entire courtyard is reserved for gardens and small pools. Reviews say that the territory here is beautiful, well-groomed and very clean, which is important for India.

Hotel Highland Beach Resort 3 * (Goa): what do the living rooms look like?





The hotel is quite large and consists of 180 apartments - these are standard double rooms, rooms with a high level of comfort (consist of a separate living room and bedroom), and ambassador rooms that are perfect for a large family or company (consist of a living room and two separate bedrooms )

The rooms are not too big, but comfortable and well furnished. Of household appliances there is air conditioning, a fan, a TV (no Russian channels), a telephone. There is also a minibar with a cooling system, but it is filled for an additional fee.

The bathroom is also clean enough, equipped with working plumbing. There is a shower, sink and toilet. Hair dryers are available upon request. Of course, you can count on a daily change of towels.

Food and restaurants on site





For many guests, the issue of nutrition is extremely important. What does the 3 * Highland Beach Resort offer? The room rate includes daily breakfast, which is held on the large poolside terrace. The choice of food here is quite normal - there are toasts, rolls, cereals, granola, fresh pastries, soups, cereals. And of course, you can enjoy fragrant Indian tea. Here you can eat during the day, but for an additional fee.

In addition, it is worth visiting the cozy Gulzar restaurant at least once, which serves popular Indian dishes. And at Cheers Sports Bar, guests are always offered healthy food, freshly squeezed juices and other vitamin drinks.

In any case, food here will not be a problem, since there are many bars, cafes and restaurants with different menus on the territory of the village, and even with relatively affordable prices. In addition, food can be bought at one of the supermarkets.

How close is the beach?





Naturally, tourists choose Goa as a vacation destination precisely because of the opportunity to have fun on the beach. The 3 * Highland Beach Resort Hotel is located about 500 meters from the clean, comfortable Candolim Beach. Despite the fact that this stretch of coast is intended for public use, it is not too crowded. The sea is calm and warm, and the approach is quite comfortable. For a small fee, you can eat at one of the beach cafes, as well as get a comfortable deck chair with a mattress. Naturally, lovers of more active entertainments will not be bored, as tourists are offered to do almost all kinds of water sports, from water skiing and speedboats to swimming with dolphins.

Hotel Highland Beach Resort 3 * (India): entertainment, leisure and additional service for guests





The hotel provides its guests with comfortable living conditions. You can use the parking lot, visit the local shops, relax on the terraces surrounded by palm trees. There is constant access to the Internet, although the speed is sometimes not too good. Hotel 3 * Highland Beach Resort also provides a business center with the necessary equipment, plus a spacious conference room for 200 people.

In the courtyard there are several small pools with sections for bathing children. Guests can use the gym, jacuzzi, sauna, massage rooms. There is an Ayurveda center where various events are regularly held, including yoga classes. There is also a travel agency where you can buy an interesting tour or more information about the sights of the island.

Traveler Reviews for This Hotel

Naturally, many people are interested in what other travelers say about the Highland Beach Resort 3 * hotel. Reviews about this place are almost completely positive, as the hotel leaves only pleasant memories. The territory here, although small, is incredibly beautiful and clean. The undoubted advantage is the large number of pools - you can always find a secluded place. The living conditions in the rooms are also decent. Despite the fact that some furniture and appliances are not too new, everything is in good condition. There is always water in the shower, and cleaning is available on request.

The hotel staff are pleasant in communication, although they do not speak Russian. You can walk to the beach in 15 minutes, which can also be considered a virtue. Indeed, the hotel is ideal for a rich beach holiday on the picturesque seashore.
