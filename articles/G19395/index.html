Turkey, Atlas Hotel Alanya 4 *: photos, prices and reviews of tourists from Russia

Of course, Turkey can be considered one of the most popular tourist countries. And many tourists are interested in additional information about the Atlas Hotel Alanya 4 * hotel. Where is it? What conditions does it offer its customers? Who is this hotel suitable for?

Where is the Atlas Hotel Alanya 4 * located?





The hotel complex is located in the popular resort town of Alanya. The frustration to the city center is only 1.5 kilometers - you can even walk there. The city of Antalya, no less famous among tourists, and, accordingly, the international airport, is 125 km from the hotel.

By the way, the hotel was built in 1988. Despite the small territory, there is everything you need to relax. And just next door is another popular hotel from this line - Atlas Beach Hotel 4 *.

Alanya is a great place for a beach holiday. The resort town offers you a host of entertainment, including bustling markets, historical sites, leisure parks and signature boutiques.

What do the rooms on the hotel look like?





Atlas Hotel Alanya 4 * is considered relatively small - on its territory there are 76 standard rooms and 10 spacious connected family rooms. Of course, every room is furnished with modern furniture. Most rooms have a private balcony with tables and chairs - it is always a pleasure to spend time here, watching life on the hotel grounds or admiring beautiful views of the coast.

As for the set of household appliances, there is air conditioning and a telephone. You can watch satellite channels on TV, including some Russians. There is also a minibar, which employees fill for a fee. Also paid is a safe for storing documents and other personal items.

The room has a toilet and a bathroom where there is everything you need, including a washbasin, shower, wall mirror, hair dryer and a set of clean towels. Maids clean the rooms every day, and linen on the bed is changed three times a week.

The scheme of food on site





The hotel provides its customers with three meals a day, as well as light snacks and delicious drinks throughout the day. The spacious hotel restaurant serves tables for tenants three times a day. Guests are offered cold snacks, a variety of side dishes, meat and fish dishes and, of course, delicious desserts and local ice cream. Fresh fruit is served at the table.

The hotel also has two bars - one is located in the lobby, and the second is serving guests by the pool. Here you will be offered a large selection of local alcoholic beverages. The menu, of course, has light snacks, chilled juices and exotic cocktails.

Beach and water recreation





Atlas Hotel Alanya 4 * has its own beach, which is located about 600 meters from the hotel complex. You can get there in just 5 minutes - you just need to cross the road. On the beach you will find clean soft sand. Sea water is also clean, and in the coastal area there are shallow areas where children can swim. Comfortable sunbeds with mattresses and umbrellas are placed along the beach - you can use them for a small fee. It is worth noting that in most cases there are enough deck chairs for everyone.

If you are interested in a more active vacation, then in this case you can find a lot of interesting entertainments. For example, there are sports grounds where hotel guests often organize beach volleyball competitions. Of course, you will have the opportunity to ride a boat, kayak, boat and water ski. Near the beach there is a bar with a huge selection of cooling drinks and cocktails.

Family holidays with children

Of course, traveling with children requires some additional conditions. And the Atlas Hotel Alanya 4 * hotel offers them. In the local restaurant you can easily find the right dishes for a child of any age. In addition, cots and highchairs are available for caring parents.

As for entertainment, then there are enough of them. Of course, kids can have fun on the beach. And at the hotel there is a special shallow children's pool, where your children can have fun. Children of different ages can also visit the mini-club - here they can safely be left to the care of responsible teachers. And in the evening, children will find a rich show program and fun entertainment with the participation of a group of talented animators.

Additional services for foreign travelers

Many travelers are interested in the availability of some additional services at the Atlas Hotel Alanya 4 *. Turkey is a hospitable country that provides tourists with opportunities for recreation for many decades. And, of course, in high-class hotels, your vacation will not be overshadowed by small household problems.

The hotel complex has its own spacious parking with a reliable security system, which is convenient if you are traveling by car. For those tourists who want to explore the surroundings on their own, there is a car rental point where you can rent vehicles for a small fee.

The hotel also has its own laundry and dry cleaning services, which can be used at an additional cost. Near the hotel complex there are lots of shops where you can buy everything you need. By the way, you will have the opportunity to visit the shopping center - many travelers come here during the discount season.

There is a currency exchange office at the hotel. There is wireless internet access in the lobby. If necessary, you can visit the Internet cafe. And if you have health problems, the hotel staff will definitely call you a qualified doctor.

Entertainment at the hotel





The hotel has its own large swimming pool with water slides, where both children and their parents can have a great time. Around are umbrellas and sunbeds, which can be used for free.

In addition, the hotel offers its customers to use the services of the gym or fitness center. There is also a jacuzzi where you can relax. And you can have fun with friends at a pool table or a game of table tennis. And in the evening the guests are entertained by a team of animators.

Of course, this is not all that Atlas Hotel Alanya 4 * has to offer. Alanya is a city in which there is something to see. A tour desk is located on site to help you find the right trip for you.

Hotel Atlas Hotel Alanya 4 *: reviews of Russian tourists





Alanya is one of the most popular resort areas in Turkey. And, despite the abundance of hotels and resorts, many Russian tourists choose Atlas Hotel Alanya 4 *. Reviews for this hotel are mostly positive.

Of course, all the rooms here are equipped to all standards - the rooms are kept in order and clean. The food is also commendable, as guests are always waiting for an abundance of dishes of traditional international and national Turkish cuisine, including fresh vegetables, desserts, meat and fish. The hotel also has all the conditions for a fun pastime, and in the evenings you can always enjoy breathtaking performances, including oriental dances. All hotel staff are friendly and helpful, always ready to help with advice or come to the rescue. By the way, some of them speak good Russian, while the rest speak English, so you are unlikely to have difficulty communicating.
