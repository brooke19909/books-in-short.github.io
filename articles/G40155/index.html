The first and most important meal in the hotel is from 8 to 10 in the morning. Breakfast is held in the form of a buffet, so you can choose any dish you like among a huge number of truly chic culinary masterpieces.

No healthy breakfast can do without dried apricots, raisins or prunes (the client adds these ingredients to the dish of his own free will). In addition, yogurt, milk, kefir or fermented baked milk will be an ideal complement to the main meal.

If you are a person accustomed to a rich breakfast, then be aware that Hotel Venera Resort (Hotel Venera Resort, Vityazevo) additionally offers you to order a classic European breakfast consisting of an omelet or a classic scrambled egg with sausages, bacon or other ingredients.





Here, too, sweet tooths will find something tasty for themselves, because the main dish card contains a variety of spring rolls, cottage cheese pancakes, dumplings with cottage cheese, and much more. Corn flakes, granola and chocolate balls are also available.

Number of rooms

Hotel Venera Resort Hotel offers its guests to rent rooms of 7 different categories. So, each visitor has the opportunity to stay in the economy for 1200-1800 rubles, the standard without a balcony - for 1450-2650 rubles, the classic standard - for 1600-2800 rubles, comfort - for 1900-3000 rubles, triple comfort - for 1800 -2800 rubles., Studios - for 2050-3150 rubles or a suite - for 2350-3450 rubles.





And now let's discuss in more detail each of the rooms available for booking at Venera Resort Hotel.

Economy

In rooms of this category you will find everything you need for an excellent holiday for friends or couples. The interior of the room is made in bright light tones, which simply makes you relax and have a good time.

In economy, upholstered furniture and climatic equipment are installed, and the room itself is on the fifth floor of the hotel. Rooms are 15 square meters and can accommodate a maximum of two adults and two children.





The house-keeper is equipped with a folding armchair, a double bed, a dressing table, a wardrobe, an ottoman, a fridge, a TV and other amenities.

Standard without and with a balcony

The Venera Resort Hotel (Vityazevo), reviews of which we will discuss in more detail later in this article, includes classic standard rooms, some of which have a balcony, while others, unfortunately, are not.

In both cases, the standards are decorated in bright colors, and their rooms have the same area - 18 square meters. In addition, a maximum of 2 children and as many adults can also stay in them.





In the room you can use a double or two twin beds, a dressing table, a folding armchair, an ottoman, a wardrobe, a mirror, bedside tables, a TV, air conditioning, a cot (upon prior request), and other amenities.
