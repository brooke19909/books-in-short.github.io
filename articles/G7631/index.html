Park Hotel "SDL": reviews and photos of tourists

For many, Seliger has long been one of the most beloved holiday destinations in our country. And this is not surprising: after all, the nature of this region - an amazing combination of forests and sparkling surface of water - is rightly called one of the most beautiful in Russia. The local places are as if created for relaxation and detachment from the city bustle and hassle. In this region there are a lot of stunning lakes, the air around which is filled with the aroma of trees. It is no coincidence that many cottage boarding houses were built on their shores for recreation, for example, in the Petrikovo park-hotel "SDL".

Seliger and Sig



The system of glacial lakes in the Tver region has become quite popular in the country as a place for car, water and even bicycle tourism. The uniqueness of nature around the lakes Seliger, Peno, Sig, Velig, Sterzh, etc. It gives an opportunity to fully relax for the residents of Russia who come here from many large cities. After all, here is a real paradise for those who like to hunt, fish, pick berries and mushrooms, swim in kayaks, etc.

Another reason for the great popularity of this region was its small distance from the largest Russian cities. For example, Ostashkov, Lake Sig, Park Hotel SDL are located just 400 kilometers from Moscow, and 450 from the northern capital. In addition, there is the possibility of coming here by rail.

general information

Park Hotel "SDL" is located on the shores of Lake Sig. Its territory is twenty hectares of protected area. Forest and meadows, as well as its own beach area and sports complex, a convenient helipad ... This is not a complete list of the infrastructure of this complex.



Vacationers can not only settle in comfortable apartments. They also have cottages by the lake and VIP mansions with a separate territory. Walking in the vast territory, a family picnic in nature, fishing and horseback riding, relaxing in a Russian bath or sauna, as well as bathing and boating and catamarans await those guests who come to the SDL (park hotel) in the summer. New Year's Eve, Easter, any holiday or just a weekend can be spent here, combining relaxation with active pastime - skating, skiing, games on sports fields and much more.

Infrastructure

Park Hotel "SDL" is considered an ideal place for team building. On its vast protected area several business centers are equipped, there are meeting rooms. Recreation areas are designed for sporting events, entertaining picnics and business trainings. The hotel staff helps guests organize banquets for corporate parties. Those who wish are provided with all the necessary equipment for barbecue.

The administrative building has a medical office and a beauty salon. Laundry facilities are also available.



Accommodation

On the territory of the park-hotel there are many options for settlement. Vacationers can choose to accommodate both hunting lodges and cottages designed for large and noisy companies.

For hunters and fishing enthusiasts on the territory there are special log cabins at the near estate. Houses No. 1 and No. 2 are located on the shore of a small lake far from the main buildings. They have everything that you might need for relaxation. There is also equipment to prepare for a successful hunt and fishing. In the houses there are LCD TVs with DVD players, there is a fan, a refrigerator. The combined bathrooms have showers, a hairdryer and all the necessary accessories for personal hygiene.

There are only three hunting lodges at the far end. They are a wonderful option for a vacation with the whole family or in a small company of like-minded people, those who seek peace and unity with nature.

All three houses are designed for a maximum of six people. They are two story. On the first level there is an entrance hall with a kitchen and a bathroom. On the second floor there are two more berths and the possibility of adding two more. The houses are decorated in the appropriate country style: the log walls of the log house are decorated with animal skins, cast metal lamps are hung everywhere. In addition, the kitchen area has everything necessary for a comfortable stay - dining furniture, dishes, a refrigerator, an electric kettle, a stove and a microwave. This circumstance is especially to the liking of those who like to cook from their own fish, as well as for mushroom pickers and hunters.

The rooms are equipped with LCD TVs with DVD players, there is a connection to satellite channels, wireless Internet, refrigerators.

Additional Information



Park Hotel "SDL" is a place where you can combine relaxation with active pastime. It offers a huge range of services. For lovers of bath procedures, a Turkish hammam, a Russian steam room, a Japanese sento and a sauna are provided. There is also a solarium and a gym.

For sports entertainment, basketball and five-a-side football courts, tennis courts and a golf course are equipped.

Many city dwellers come to the SDL Park Hotel located on Seliger to play paintball and ride wonderful horses. And the indoor pool with three lanes at any time of the day or night is ready to welcome those who wish to swim.

There is also a shooting club and an impressive fleet of vehicles, where under the supervision of experienced instructors you can ride ATVs, buggies, snowmobiles, SUVs. Those who prefer aquasport can rent a boat, catamaran and jet ski, a pleasure boat and a kayak. There is also a shooting club, billiard room, bowling, hookah and the opportunity to play air hockey.

For children

Park Hotel "SDL" offers its little tourists an inflatable football field, an excellent playground, children's jeeps and electric cars. In the restaurant, they can order a separate menu and use a high highchair. The territory has its own zoo.



Nutrition

Park Hotel "SDL" is designed to delight and surprise its customers. The restaurant, which is located in the very center of the complex, is no exception. This is a two-story log cabin, furnished in the style of a real hunting lodge. From its windows, visitors can watch the beautiful landscapes of Lake Sig. The chef of the restaurant offers a wide range of delicious dishes from the Russian and European menus, ranging from local Ostashkov to exclusive exotic ones. There is the possibility of organizing holiday banquets. The restaurant room seats about one hundred and fifty people.

Prices

Accommodation in cottages in a double room per day in winter will cost two thousand four hundred, in summer - six and a half thousand rubles. The price includes: accommodation, two meals a day, buffet type, secure parking. With a small filling of the recreation center - less than thirty people - a comprehensive menu is offered. The cost of additional space for an adult client with the same nutrition is one thousand rubles per day, for children under three years old - free of charge.

Accommodation in quadrodoms in the summer months will cost eight and a half thousand for two. The entire cottage, which can accommodate up to eight people, costs thirty-five thousand rubles a day with meals.
