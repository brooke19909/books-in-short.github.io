Veretennikov Oleg: biography, career, photo

Veretennikov Oleg is one of the few football players who played in all Russian championships as part of only one team - Rotor (Volgograd). For eight years of the game, he managed to become a leader, as well as a certain symbol of the hero city. Confessions Oleg achieved his stable results. The constancy with which he hit the gates of rivals in each season can be envied. This article will describe a brief biography of the athlete.

In 1992, Veretennikov arrived in Volgograd and ended up in Rotor. National team

Despite the successes in Rotor, Oleg was not called to play for the country. For this, there were any excuses: there was no experience in performing at international competitions, did not fit into the team game, etc. Of course, everything would have been different if Oleg Veretennikov, whose career was going up, played for one of the Moscow clubs. From the capital to go to the team was not difficult. It was almost impossible for a peripheral soccer player to join the national team. In Soviet times, any athlete from the hinterland knew that there were only two ways for growth: to go either to Kiev or to Moscow. The exception was the Georgian players choosing Tbilisi Dynamo. Oleg remained loyal to Rotor, so the shortcut to the national team was closed for him. But even when he joined the national team a few years later, he could not stay there for long.
