Hotel Hawthorn Suites By Wyndham 4 * (UAE / Dubai): description, photos and reviews

Hawthorn Suites By Wyndham 4 * is an elite hotel located in the heart of Dubai, the largest city in the UAE. It is only 400 meters from the Persian Gulf coast. And the Dallas / Fort Worth International Airport is 5 minutes away. A favorable location, of course, is one of the advantages of this complex. But not the only one. This hotel has a lot of features, and everyone deserves special attention.





Service

In Hawthorn Suites By Wyndham 4 * there is everything that should be in a hotel of this level. The hotel has free Wi-Fi, distributed throughout its territory, parking, 24-hour reception, currency exchange, luggage storage, ATM. There is also a dry cleaning service with laundry, where they also clean shoes, press trousers and provide ironing services.

In addition, the hotel has a business center, several large conference rooms, a computer complex, and another 6 rooms for negotiations and meetings. Hawthorn Suites By Wyndham 4 * is an ideal venue for large-scale business events, as there is everything you need for this.

It is also worth noting that the hotel has several shops (including a souvenir), a large market, a beauty salon, and a hairdresser. There is a delivery of food, drinks, packed lunches on the road and newspapers, a shuttle service is provided. Guests can also be offered VIP services. There are even apartments of special categories - for family people and for guests with disabilities. It is important to mention that smoking is not allowed in this hotel, but there are places reserved for fans to smoke.

Sports and Entertainment

No one will be bored while being in the Hawthorn Suites By Wyndham 4 *. Even though most tourists come here in order to enjoy a beach holiday or attractions, there is still something to occupy yourself without leaving the hotel.

You can spend time sunbathing on the terrace or swimming in the pools. There are several of them, there is even one with heating. Also on the territory of the complex is a SPA-center. It has several treatment rooms, massage rooms, hydromassage baths, a Turkish bath, and a sauna. There is also a modern fitness center, equipped with all the simulators necessary for a full workout. So lovers of an active lifestyle will definitely like it.





Bars and Restaurants

Hawthorn Suites By Wyndham JBR Dubai 4 * has several establishments in which guests can have breakfast, order dinner or have a snack in the afternoon.

The main restaurant specializing in international cuisine is known as Flavors. It is open for breakfast, brunch, lunch and dinner. In this institution, in addition to the standard menu, guests are offered a diet. All dishes are delicious and fresh. People relaxing at the hotel notice amazing pastries, a variety of cheeses, unusual salads, Moroccan soups, lasagna according to a special recipe, and delicate tiramisu. If guests come with a small child, then upon their individual request they will cook what is required: milk porridges or light soups for example.

If you want to treat yourself to something especially delicious, then you should visit the French cafe La Maison Du Pain. They serve excellent coffee, tea, fresh pastries and delicious pastries. But the establishment of Pan Asia will have a soul lovers of Asian cuisine. Thai, Japanese and Chinese dishes are served there.

Service

Talking about the Hawthorn Suites By Wyndham JBR Dhubai 4 *, one can not help but pay attention to the topic relating to staff. Indeed, the hotel employs truly highly qualified professionals.

First of all, it should be noted that the staff speaks 8 different languages. In addition to Arabic, this list includes Urdu, Ukrainian, Filipino, Hindi, French, English and, importantly, Russian. So even in the UAE, our compatriots should not have problems with the linguistic barrier.

What about cleaning? Here it is carried out daily. The apartments are tidied up quickly and efficiently, without being imposed on vacationers. Guests, by the way, themselves can tell the maids what time it is advisable to clean the room. Towels are also changed regularly, remembering to leave tea, coffee and bottled water in the apartment.

The territory of the hotel is also closely monitored. She is neat and well-groomed. The same can be said about the pools. It's nice to swim in them. Water, of course, is chlorinated, but in moderation - there is no pungent odor. By the way, guests are given separate towels for the pool. If they are going to the beach, then they will also hand over a bag and umbrellas. All, of course, is free.

Helpful information

Before planning a trip to Hawthorn Suites By Wyndham 4 * (Dubai Marina), you should also note a few important facts.

If, for example, a person goes on vacation with two children under 12 years old, then for their accommodation you do not need to pay extra. But they will have to stay in those sleeping places that are already in the room. However, for babies under the age of 2 years, the administration can provide a cot. Also free. But for guests over 12 years old you have to pay extra (150 AED per day). But then an extra bed will be installed in the room. True, only one - these are the rules. And if a person needs to provide an extra bed (full-size or children's), he needs to contact the hotel in advance and inform about it.

When booking a room at Hawthorn Suites By Wyndham 4 * (Dubai), you must provide your own credit card details. The hotel accepts American Express, Visa, Master Card and Diners Club. It is forbidden to use someone else's card, because upon arrival you will need to present it with an identity card (passport).
