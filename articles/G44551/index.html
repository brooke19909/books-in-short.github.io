Scorpio - a constellation of bright stars and unexpected finds

Scorpio - the constellation is the most wrong, in terms of the zodiac circle. According to the boundaries of the constellations that were finally approved in 1935, it only accounts for a small piece of the ecliptic on November 23-29, after which the Sun goes into the constellation Ophiuchus, which is not considered to be the zodiac. Because of this, the astrological community is constantly debating about the truth or falsity of horoscopes associated with Scorpio. However, this article will focus on the knowledge gained by those who are patronized by Urania.





General excursion

There are not so many constellations in the sky that are really similar to the object they represent. Heavenly Scorpio actually resembles the earthly arthropod. It is no coincidence that the Aztecs quite independently of the Greeks called him by the same name. The most interesting objects

Antares . The brightest star in the constellation Scorpio, is included in the host of the most conspicuous stars in the earth's sky. A red supergiant very similar to Mars in its color and brightness (0.86 m ). It is a visual double star, and in the blood-red glow, the bluish-white Antares appears green.





Beta Scorpio (Akrab) . This star is like a nesting doll. At first it was assumed that this is a classic double luminary. However, careful research has shown that in the Akrab system there are at least five stars orbiting their companions. It is assumed that fivefold is not the limit.

Scorpio X-1 . The strongest source of radiation in the x-ray range, second only to the sun. In its place, the hot blue variable V818 Scorpio was discovered. A binary system with a neutron star is suspected.
