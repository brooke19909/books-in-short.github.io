Aktibeach Village Resort (Cat. A) - Family-friendly Resort

Aktibeach Village Resort is a beautiful family vacation destination in Cyprus. Set in beautiful gardens on the shores of the Mediterranean Sea, this resort will give you relaxation in the shade of olive trees and palm trees. The air here is filled with aromas of roses, jasmine and lemon. And at sunset, the sea romantically changes the turquoise color to purple.





On the beach you can take a swim, dive, or just take a walk along the seashore. A variety of entertainment awaits you here for you and your children.

No less interesting are the historical sights and ancient Greek myths of the city of Paphos. This and the Royal Tombs, carved into the rock, and the magnificent castle of Paphos. You might be interested in a mosaic museum or a winery. You will be pleased with the personalized service at Aktibeach Village Resort (Cat. A means that this 4-star vacation spot is a favorite among tourists).

You will be offered to stay in one-bedroom apartments or in the studio. The studios at Aktibeach Village Resort (Cat. A) are spacious apartments for a family of two or three, and are 39 square meters in size. For large families, rooms with one or two bedrooms are provided. All rooms have air conditioning, TV, sofa, bed. And also - a kitchen and a bathroom.

You are offered to purchase the half-board or self-service packages. All inclusive service is also provided at Aktibeach Village Resort. Cat. A suggests that the list of services depends on whether in the winter or summer season you decide to visit the resort. Pafos Restaurant, Poolside Restaurant and Serina Cocktail Bar are located on site. Room service is available. You will be pleasantly surprised by the variety of menus, delicious barbecue and buffet.



At Aktibeach Village Resort 4 stars are at your service: a tennis court, a playground, two large adult pools and one children's small pool, a conference room, a small shop, a fitness center and Kariadis Health Spa. The latter includes a jacuzzi, a heated indoor pool, a steam bath, a sauna and massage services. A special team creates unique entertainment events for adults and children. Theme nights, exciting shows and excursions to local attractions, children's discos and holidays. For beautiful ladies, and not only, Spa-procedures, pedicures, facial skin care procedures and others are presented.



Children's mini club operates on the basis of Aktibeach. Village Resort (Cat. A) provides you with the opportunity to leave your children under the supervision of qualified staff. Knowing that your children have fun and enjoy their stay at the Nemo Club, you can relax and take time for yourself, which is so necessary for each of us.

Additional services for holding various celebrations are also offered. You can celebrate the wedding in the Paphos Restaurant. On this occasion, you will be provided with fruit and wine in your room, champagne breakfast and a romantic candlelight dinner, 10% discount from the bar, as well as preferential prices for additional services. If you have a birthday, you will be provided with cake and wine in the room.





There are two international airports in the city. You can get to them by public transport, or by renting a car or bike at the resort.

Have a nice rest!
