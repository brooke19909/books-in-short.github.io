Resort Riccione (Italy). Short review

Fans of an unforgettable Italian holiday on the Adriatic coast with golden sand and crystal clear waters spend their holidays in the resort of Riccione (Italy). A green pearl - as the locals call this resort - a great place for summer leisure surrounded by numerous villas and flowering gardens. Entertainment for every taste, sun, sea and reasonable prices - what else is needed for relaxation? From this article you can learn in detail about this resort, its main beaches and hotels.





Riccione (Italy). Location

The city of Riccione is located on the Adriatic Sea, a little south of Rimini. Despite its small size, the resort is very popular among tourists from different countries.

It is easy to get to Riccione, a few kilometers from it is the Federico Fellini International Airport. By the way, in the summer season a large number of Russian charter flights fly here. 3.5 hours - and you are already on the Adriatic coast. Hotels

The main resort hotels have an average level of "star rating", most often there are hotels of the level of three or four stars. It is the affordability of accommodation prices that attracts tourists Italy (Riccione). The hotels are located on the seashore, some are literally nearby - on the next street. In any case, the beach is not far, a maximum of 100 meters.

In local hotels there are no swimming pools, and if you meet it, then for sure it will turn out to be a "paddling pool" 5x6 m in size. All tourists swim in the sea.

The level of service can be assessed as high, but the location of the hotel most often affects the quality of service. The closer to the city center, the more diverse the services and the higher the service.





Beaches

The Adriatic coast is famous for its wide beaches with fine and clean sand. The coastline stretches for more than 7 kilometers. The entrance to the water is safe, and the slope is gentle, so you can often meet tourists with young children here. Sun loungers and sun loungers on the beach can be rented for money, the average cost is 4 euros per day. In the middle of the season, rents can reach 10 euros.

On the beaches of Riccione you can engage in active sports, play tennis, billiards or volleyball. The entire coast is equipped for a comfortable pastime of vacationers - there are toilets, showers, bars, pools and other entertainments. For the smallest on all beaches, play areas and playgrounds are provided.





The most popular beaches in Riccione (Italy) are La Playa and Spiaggia. They have existed for more than 50 years, and every year attract more and more tourists.

Resort Features

In addition to beach vacations and excursions, many visitors are interested in the so-called Health Centers, because Riccione (Italy) is known to many as the main place of thermal therapy in Europe. There are several natural springs in the city, the most famous being Riccione Terme. In special dispensaries, you can perfectly heal. In the local "capital of health" inhalation, mud and water treatments are done, a beauty and aesthetic-thermal medicine center is also open there. High-quality service, as well as fresh and favorable sea air, not only help get rid of ailments and diseases, but also contribute to a good mood and a positive state of mind.





Night Riccione

The resort annually receives more and more young people from all over the world. This is directly related to the exhibition of achievements in the field of dance music. Discos, bars and beach parties until the morning - this is what attracts students and young tourists. In addition, very often Italian stars come on tour in Riccione.

The most famous local club is Pere Nero, which is visited by thousands of nightlife lovers from year to year, the Hakuna Matata disco bar, Piter Pan and Prince disco are no less popular.

The resort of Riccione (Italy), reviews of which are mostly only positive, is a great place for an unforgettable vacation and interesting pastime. Tourists who have already been here are happy to come to this small and cozy town again. The charm of this Italian resort can be felt only when you walk along the quiet green streets or sunbathe on the shores of the Adriatic Sea.
