What is sound and, in particular, warm tube sound

Many of us have repeatedly thought about what sound is. In physical terminology, this value is described as the wave formation of air pressure. Simply put, without air, we would not have heard anything. The ability to perceive sounds is due to the sensitivity of our ears to sound waves. We sense changes in air pressure.





What is sound can be understood with a little experiment. Clap your hands near your face. In addition to the sound, you will feel a slight breath of air. The distribution of this airflow is similar to the distribution of sound. A sound wave is created by expelling air through the clap of your hands.

The ability to hear makes people interested and explore acoustics. This science describes the laws of sound propagation and provides a general understanding of what sound is. Tube sound has such a name due to the use of transistor amplifiers, which were rich in odd harmonics, which allowed us to denote the timbre of the sound. This effect was obtained due to reproduction on electronic tubes, hence the name itself.

Only in contrast to the old days, when transistors were in short supply, today they are in abundance. Basically, this hobby is typical for people who consider themselves lovers of vintage style.
