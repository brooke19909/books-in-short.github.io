Ulan-Ude hotels are inexpensive. Addresses, description, reviews

The city of Ulan-Ude is the capital of Buryatia. More than four hundred thousand people live here. Clean air, a large number of parks and other attractions attract the attention of visitors to this city. Today we will tell you about cheap hotels in Ulan-Ude. We will give their description, address, as well as provide feedback from visitors.



Guest house "Kuzya"

What if you came to Ulan-Ude with a pet? After all, as you know, in hotels they are not populated with them. Fortunately, among the inexpensive hotels in Ulan-Ude, there is a great option for you and your pet. Guest house "Kuzya" offers its visitors other services:

  • Free private parking
  • Internet access
  • round-the-clock registration of visitors;
  • accommodation in the city center;
  • Free toiletries
  • ironing service;

The rooms are clean and tidy, there is everything you need for your stay. Reading reviews of visitors, you can make sure that it is quite comfortable and cozy. Cost per day - 500 rubles.

The address of the guest house "Kuzya" is Schmidt street, 24.

Hotel "Altan"

If you do not want to pay extra money for accommodation, then why not choose this option? The hotel has nine-bed and six-bed rooms. The cost for one day starts from 550 rubles. Also for visitors are offered other options:

  • Double standard room. There is a comfortable bed with clean and ironed linen, bedside tables, a TV, a toilet and a bathroom. The price is from 2000 rubles. The price includes delicious and nutritious breakfasts.
  • Double economy. Breakfast is not included. On the floor there is a kitchen with everything necessary for cooking. Many guests note that it is very convenient. The cost of this option is 1500 rubles.
  • Triple room. Great choice for couples with a child. The room has everything you need. Price - 3000 rubles per day. Is breakfast included.
  • Suite If you are accustomed to luxury, here you will be cozy and comfortable. You will have a great time watching cable TV shows. Price - from 3500 rubles.

The address of the Altan Hotel is 67 A. Trubacheeva Street.



"Ayan"

Another inexpensive hotel in Ulan-Ude. Guests are pleasantly surprised by the combination of affordable accommodation prices and high quality service at the "Ayan" hotel. It is homely warm and cozy. The rooms are bright and comfortable. There is a cafe where you will be offered the most delicious dishes of national cuisine. Among the additional services for guests:

  • Laundry room where you can tidy your clothes.
  • Sauna. Many visitors spend their time here with great pleasure.
  • If you need to withdraw money, then you do not need to run around the city in search of an ATM. It is located on site.

In their reviews of visiting the hotel, many guests note quite affordable prices and a cozy atmosphere.

The address of the hotel "Ayan" is Babushkina street, 164.



Hotel "Buryatia"

In the city center there is an excellent option for visitors. Inexpensive hotel in Ulan-Ude attracts the attention of a large number of tourists. What are its features? Studying the reviews of guests, we can distinguish the following:

  • hospitable and polite staff;
  • cozy and comfortable rooms;
  • a large number of additional services;
  • the proximity of various shops and entertainment venues;
  • beautiful, panoramic views from the windows of the tallest hotel in the city.

The cost of living is from 1500 rubles.

The address of the Buryatia Hotel is Kommunisticheskaya Street, 47 A.

Finally

Prices in hotels of Ulan-Ude are quite affordable and affordable even for people with a very modest income level. The quality of service and living conditions correspond to the most requests of tourists from different cities of vast Russia.
