How many animals are in the Moscow zoo?

The zoo holds more than 8 thousand animals and breeds rare and endangered species. By the 150th anniversary, in 2014, a tropical greenhouse was opened, which contains more than 100 species of exotic fauna.





The animals of the Moscow Zoo are mammals, birds, fish, amphibians and invertebrates. Some species are kept in the nursery near Volokolamsk. The zoo constantly updates its nurseries and makes exchanges with other zoo nurseries around the world, so the number of animals in the Moscow zoo is constantly changing.

In addition, mammals of the Moscow Zoo can be taken under guardianship and taken care of as a volunteer.

On the territory it is allowed to take photos of animals. The Moscow Zoo is very large, there are many pets that cause affection and delight among the children.
