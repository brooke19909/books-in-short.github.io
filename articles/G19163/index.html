Ozgurhan Hotel 3 * (Turkey / Side) - photos, prices and reviews of tourists

How many people would like to relax in such a stunning Turkish resort town like Side! This is not strange, because it is here that a large number of archaeological sites are located that attract tourists from all over the world. The Turkish government carefully takes care of its historical heritage and tries to develop the tourism sector in Side. That is why a large number of hotels are collected here - from ordinary and inconspicuous to chic and majestic. Just one kilometer from the stunning beach, the three-star Ozgurhan Hotel 3 * is located, which has 37 rooms for temporary residence.





A little bit about the hotel building

The hotel was built in 2001, since then it has been renovated several times. Therefore, today she appears before her guests in an updated form. The building itself is built in the shape of an accordion, consists of three sections. The managers chose mustard as the main color for their hotel. In general, Ozgurhan Hotel 3 * looks quite attractive. In the backyard of the building is a large outdoor pool. The hotel is located somewhere three kilometers from the city center. But many monuments and museums of the city are in close proximity to it. To reach the seashore, you must overcome the distance of one and a half kilometers from the hotel.

The road to the hotel

Ozgurhan Hotel Side 3 * is located in the most popular resort and historical city of Turkey - Side. But there is no airport, so you can get to the hotel only through Antalya. Airplanes to Antalya fly from most cities of Russia. In particular, a round-trip ticket from the capital of the Russian Federation will cost approximately $ 300. Before flying to Antalya, you can order a transfer from the airport to the hotel. The service is paid and is provided directly by Ozgurhan Hotel 3 *. If you do not want to use this service and decide to go to Side on your own, you need to know that between it and Antalya it is 75 km. A bus leaves the bus station every hour and takes you to the city you need in 1.5 hours. By taxi, the same path can be done in an hour, but the cost of such a trip will be higher.





Hotel Service

Ozgurhan Hotel 3 * Side tries to take care of its guests, so it offers a fairly wide range of services. Among them there are both free and paid. Among the free ones you can name a parking place for a car that can be rented, as well as a free shuttle service to the hotel's bus in the city of Side. All others are paid. Even in order to use the Internet, you need to pay 5 euros per day. This can be called a minus, because in almost every Turkish hotel, wireless Internet is free absolutely anywhere. A local laundry service and luggage storage are also available at an additional cost. If a safe is needed, then you must pay for its rent. But it is worth noting that the service in Ozgurhan Hotel 3 * (Antalya) is at a very good level. Every day a conscientious cleaning of the rooms is carried out, and in case of any questions you can always turn to the manager for help.





Rooms

In total, the hotel has 37 rooms. All are standard. But at the same time, if you enter the room alone, living in it will cost $ 34. For a couple, one night in this hotel will cost $ 45. The price also includes a complimentary breakfast, while the all-inclusive option is not available at this hotel. To use the minibar in the room, you will need to pay a certain amount. Each room is 25 square meters. Here you can find one bed (single or double, depending on the number of visitors), air conditioning, refrigerator and TV equipped with satellite TV. There is also a bathroom, which is combined with a toilet. It provides for a towel and a hairdryer for drying hair. There is also a balcony with a beautiful view of the sea.





Recreation and entertainment

The only place where you can at least have some fun in the hotel is the large outdoor pool, which is located in the backyard of the Ozgurhan Hotel 3 *. The water in it is always clean and clear thanks to a systematic change (every day). In order to entertain yourself not only with sunbathing around the pool, you can go to the sea, which is located 1.5 kilometers from the hotel. This distance is easily overcome in 15-20 minutes of uneasy walking. On the seashore, you can both admire the beautiful views of the Mediterranean, and engage in interesting water sports. For example, scuba dive to the bottom of the sea and see all the amazing wildlife that lives there. Extreme lovers can always go to Side Bay and go water-skiing or banana there. As an additional entertainment, you can go to the city and look at the many archaeological sites that have been preserved here since ancient Greece.

Food and drink

Ozgurhan Hotel 3 * (Turkey) offers a free breakfast as an additional service upon arrival. However, all other meals at this hotel are paid. The food in the hotel restaurant is very tasty. Here you can find a large assortment of both cold snacks and hot dishes, which are usually served in the third. The hotel bar offers a large number of alcoholic drinks. Fans of drinks will be glad to know that the bar contains various types of vodka, wine, cognac, as well as beer. Among non-alcoholic drinks, freshly squeezed orange juices are popular, the prices of which are low. Outside the hotel you can always find a large number of cafes that offer their guests no less delicious dishes.





What to see in Side

Side is a very beautiful and interesting city. A large number of museums, historical monuments are collected here, and there are simply amazing beauties that nature itself has created. Ozgurhan Hotel 3 * hotel is located in such a place that you can go from it in any direction and get to one or another place. You can visit the ancient ruins of Side, where parts of buildings built many millennia ago are preserved.





If you go to the Side Museum, then you can find a large number of artifacts that preserve the history of the city, found in the twentieth century. Visiting Manavgat waterfall, you can immediately understand how beautiful the nature of this city is, because the view around it is simply mesmerizing. Well, if the whole Side is circumvented, you can always take a bus and go to nearby Antalya to continue your excursion to the historical places of Turkey.

Ozgurhan Hotel 3 *, guest reviews

On numerous tourist resources you can find a large number of reviews about this hotel. Many people who happened to stay in this hotel noted that for its class it is very decent. They liked the level of service and the quality of the food, but there were also disadvantages that were noted by almost all tourists. The first is paid Internet, while everywhere it is free, and the second is that the distance to the sea is more than one kilometer, and people had to spend quite a lot of time, from their point of view, on the way to relax on the beach.

Based on the foregoing, we can conclude that this hotel is suitable for those who want to save on accommodation and get a completely high-quality service.
