Ambassador Ocean Hotel (Pattaya, Thailand): reviews

Every year, tourists increasingly prefer to travel to Thailand. And here the question arises: where does it stop so that the vacation is not spoiled by unpleasant nuances? In order to make the right choice, you should analyze the reviews of former guests of a hotel complex. Today we will talk about the Ambassador Ocean Hotel.

Location

The Ambassador Ocean reviews are mostly positive. This is one of the four hotels that are part of the Ambassador City tourist complex. On a vast territory of more than 160 thousand square meters. There are 5 buildings, 8 restaurants, 4 swimming pools, several bars and night clubs, as well as a fitness center.

The hotel is located on the first coastline and has its own beach. Ambassador Ocean is located off the coast of Jomtien. The distance from Bangkok Airport to the hotel is 130 km, to the city center - 12 km.

The advantage of this hotel is its remoteness from the city, there is neither a large number of people, nor dust and smog, as in Pattaya itself. It is perfect for relaxing with children or a romantic trip.





Hotel

"Ambassador Ocean" reviews in 2016 are called a cozy, great option for accommodation. It is an 18-story building. It was built almost 30 years ago, but thanks to major repairs in 2013, it is not inferior in comfort and equipment to new hotels. There are more than 900 rooms of different configurations in the building: standard with one or two beds, family rooms with two rooms and one corridor and superior rooms.

8 elevators provide fast delivery of all guests to their destination. The hotel windows face both the street and the territory.

"Ambassador Ocean" is located on the first coastline, a few meters from the sea.

Service

All visitors to the hotel note that the staff does not speak Russian well. I had to explain using gestures or in another language. Only at the reception there is always an administrator who understands Russian-speaking tourists, ready to help as soon as possible.

Housekeeping is provided daily. If you leave a little tip to the maid, then upon arrival in your room you will find not only a clean room, but also amazing creations from towels or bedspreads on the bed.





Rooms

  • Standart - a room in which there are one or two beds.
  • Superior Suites - several rooms connected by a common living room.
  • Deluxe Suite - the room has three bedrooms and a shared living room.
  • VIP Thai Suite - a large room located on the 17th floor of the building, has three bedrooms and a common living room.

All rooms at the Ambassador Ocean Hotel have balconies, which is a salvation for tobacco lovers, as smoking is strictly prohibited in the hotel and rooms.

The hotel rooms have everything you need:

  • furniture;
  • TV;
  • air conditioning;
  • mini bar;
  • safe;
  • kettle;
  • refrigerator;
  • telephone;
  • bathrobe and slippers;
  • bathroom with hygiene items such as toothpaste, brush, soap, shampoo, shower gel.




Nutrition

Ambassador Ocean Hotel only includes breakfast. Every morning, the hotel restaurant opens its doors to guests at exactly 6 in the morning. It offers a buffet with a large number of snacks, slices, fresh salads and fruits. Toasts and hot pastries are always available.

Dining at the hotel is as follows: on the day of entry at the reception, tourists are issued coupons. Each of them shows the date and room number. One person is given one ticket per day. Before 10 in the morning you need to have time to come for breakfast. If for some reason it was not possible to visit the dining room - throw out the ticket, money for it will not be returned. Food cannot be taken out of the premises; the fine is 280 baht.

For the most part, visitors to the Ambassador Ocean Hotel (Pattaya) leave positive reviews, they agree that breakfasts are diverse in content, there is always something new. Looking at so many dishes, it‚Äôs hard to stay hungry.

From drinks there is tea, coffee and juices with pieces of fruit.

Lunch and dinner can also be spent in the dining room, but for a fee. Many guests eat only at the hotel, because it is close, tasty and not very expensive.





Dining outside the hotel

For those who want to have lunch or dinner in other establishments, on the street near the hotel there are a large number of different cafes. Prices in such places are lower than in a restaurant, but the service can be very different.

In general, many tourists note that even in street cafes, the food is fresh and tasty, and the portions are large.

Within walking distance from the hotel there are small shops where you can buy products, as well as basic necessities.

For lovers of fresh fruit, a market is located directly opposite the hotel. The variety of products and their prices are pleasing to the eye and attract many tourists.

Hotel Territory

The Ambassador Ocean Wing Hotel (Pattaya) has a common territory with other Ambassador City buildings. Tourists note the following:

  • 4 pools, which are located near the building;
  • cafes and restaurants (Japanese, Chinese and Italian), where delicious and inexpensive you can have lunch or dinner;
  • a small green garden with exotic flowers;
  • a gift shop in which you can inexpensively buy gifts for loved ones with the symbols of Thailand;
  • grocery and grocery stores (you can find everything in them: products, clothes, shoes and accessories);
  • conference room equipped with all necessary equipment, which is ideal for meetings, conferences and presentations;
  • a business center where you can use a computer with Internet access, a printer and fax;
  • banquet room where you can order events for a large number of people.

For those who are accustomed to monitor their appearance, the hotel has a hairdresser, beauty salon, spa with several types of massage, sauna and jacuzzi.

For sports enthusiasts, the hotel offers several services at once, such as a gym, jogging track and tennis court. It offers athletes professional instructors.

For those who like to have fun, a night club and a karaoke bar are open on site. They can have fun at any time of the day.

If you come to rest with children, then on the hotel territory they will like a specially equipped games room and a mini club. There is also a "childcare" service.

For fun companies, a bowling center is located on site. Convenient location and quality of the tracks will pleasantly surprise vacationers.





Beach

Ambassador Ocean Hotel (Pattaya) is within walking distance of the sea. The white sandy beach and the azure sea - this is something that attracts tourists. The hotel is 12 km from the city center, and thanks to this, the water in the sea on the beach is much cleaner.

Sun beds and umbrellas are free. On the beach there are several bars and cafes. If desired, you can have a bite to eat and buy drinks.

All hotel guests note the good condition of the beach, clean sand and clear sea. The only thing worth remembering if you fall into the rainy season is the clouding of the water.





Upcoming Activities

On Jomtien Beach, not far from the hotel, you can not only soak up the sun, but also have fun. Crowds of tourists gather in this place to watch and participate in local entertainment. The most popular of them:

  • Waterpark Pattaya Park and Tower - an unusual and exciting place, with lots of paths, slides and pools. Spray of water and complete delight to all tourists will be provided.
  • Jomtien Bowl - Bowling Center. In it you can have fun in a big company, because there is enough space for everyone.
  • Jomtien Fishing Park - fishing. If you are a fishing rod lover, then you will definitely like it here. For 400 baht you will be offered a table on the beach, where spectators can relax and enjoy nature. The main fisherman is given a fishing rod and bait. Next is a matter of technology. In the pond there are specimens of large sizes that are sure to peck at the bait they offer. After the fish is caught, you can take a picture with it and release it back. It is not forbidden to catch fish here, but you cannot leave it to yourself.




Guest Tips

  • All rooms have a remote control near the bed. It regulates the lighting in the room and the operation of the air conditioner. You can set an alarm on it.
  • On the balcony there is a small metal clothes dryer. When you decide to use it, do not forget about clothespins, otherwise your clothes run the risk of being on the balconies of other rooms.
  • The hotel is non-smoking, smoke sensors are installed. You can smoke on the balcony, using an ashtray for this, which you need to purchase yourself.
  • Every day, 2 bottles of clean water per person are brought to the room. It is free, but it is not recommended to take it out of the room, as this is returnable packaging, and if the maid does not find the bottle, they will charge you a fine.
  • You cannot drink tap water at the hotel! Also, be careful when swimming in the pool or the sea, try to prevent water from entering the stomach. Watch carefully for children who accidentally swallow part of the water when bathing. Sewage treatment facilities in Thailand do not work at full strength, even native Thais do not drink such water. Therefore, always have activated eel in the medicine cabinet, as an ambulance leaves for the vacationers only for a very serious case.
  • If you store food in your room, you need to do this in the refrigerator, otherwise it will be eaten by ants, which are present in almost all hotels in Thailand.
  • Want to exchange currency? Go to the exchanger or ATM. At the reception, the exchange rate is always disadvantageous for tourists.
  • Decided to go on excursions? Do not take tours from official representatives at the hotel. Opposite the main entrance there are a large number of local companies that will provide you with the same excursions for little money. And, as tourists assure, the quality of service at such companies is much better than that of official companies.
  • The city center can be reached by white tuk-tuk. Its cost is about 20 baht.
  • If you decide to stay late in the city until late, it is worth remembering that after 10 p.m. tuk-tuks become taxis and their prices take off several times.




Finally

Ambassador Ocean Hotel (Thailand) is a great option for those who decide to choose Pattaya as their vacation destination. The hotel is far from the noise of the big city, smog and the strong smells of Thai spices and cheap food. Thanks to the extensive entertainment program on site, no one will be bored, and those who come to Thailand for shopping will be pleasantly surprised that you can find everything at once and inexpensively in the shops at the Ambassador Ocean Hotel.
