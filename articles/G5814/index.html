Best pensions Kabardinka: photos and reviews of tourists

The village of Kabardinka was founded only in 1836 as an auxiliary building of the Russian troops. Initially, this place was called the Alexandria Fort. The war ended long ago, and the village began to be popular among tourists. Today, a huge number of sea lovers from Russia and other countries come here. The best pensions Kabardinka welcome guests all year round. Below is a list of the most popular holiday destinations.

Rest house "Kabardinka"

Beautiful landscape, clean sea air, well-developed infrastructure - all this is collected in one place. The most popular among tourists is the boarding house "Kabardinka". Photos of the area impress with their extraordinary beauty. If you believe the reviews of tourists, this holiday home is perfect for a vacation with a small child. The rooms are thought out for little things. Toddlers under 5 stay free with parents. It is possible to install an extra bed.



In the immediate vicinity (only 20 meters from the buildings) begins a beautiful sandy beach. Everyone has the opportunity to rent a sun lounger and parasol. Entrance to the beach is free for residents of the boarding house. Outsiders are not allowed here. Vacationers are offered a wide range of water attractions. Everyone can ride a catamaran or slide downhill directly into the saltwater pool. The beach of the Kabardinka boarding house is a place of carefree fun and quality relaxation without thinking about problems.

Rest house "Victoria"

On the Black Sea coast in a picturesque place meets tourists comfortable boarding house "Victoria". You can book a room here throughout the year. But most tourists prefer to visit the boarding house in the summer months. Those planning a vacation in June-August, it is advisable to book apartments for accommodation in advance. Pension Victoria (Kabardinka) offers its guests comfortable double and triple rooms. Additional services such as laundry and dry cleaning are available.



Pension "Victoria" (Kabardinka) is not only a high-quality service, but also the opportunity to improve your body. Guests note that high-quality medical services are provided here. Each vacationer has the opportunity to visit a physiotherapy room, pass basic tests and get consulted by qualified specialists.

Hotel complex "Nadezhda"

For those who are used to relaxing at a high level, not sparing money for their own comfort, it is worth booking a room in the hotel complex "Nadezhda". The guests here are met by helpful staff who do their job really high quality. Due to the level of service, the hotel has a five-star status. Only people with good financial capabilities can plan a vacation in the Nadezhda complex. Those who decided to save money should consider other boarding houses in Kabardinka.

For accommodation, guests are offered double and triple rooms with shower, toilet, TV, Internet. The apartments have personal hygiene products, an iron, a hairdryer, a minibar and a refrigerator. Most rooms are air-conditioned. The hotel complex "Nadezhda" offers a wide range of additional services. On the territory there is a gym, sauna, indoor pool. In the evening, everyone can spend time at the disco or play billiards. Only the best resorts in Kabardinka can boast of such a service.

Pension "Laguna"

For those who are accustomed to relax away from the hustle and bustle, it is worth paying attention to the Laguna Hotel. The highlight of this place is given by a large courtyard with an original landscape design. Each guest can have a great time without even leaving the hotel. The three-star Hotel Laguna offers comfortable rooms with sea views. The apartment can accommodate a maximum of three adults. If desired, you can install an extra bed for the child.



Pension has a good location. The city beach is just a 10-minute walk away. Near the complex there is a local water park. You can have fun with friends in the amusement park. Safe trampolines and attractions are offered to children.

Pension "Laguna" is suitable for recreation in the winter. On the territory there is an indoor pool, sauna, billiards, restaurant, banquet hall. Many locals prefer to hold weddings, anniversaries, proms here. Like other guest houses in Kabardinka, Laguna offers additional services. These include round-the-clock guarded parking, laundry, dry cleaning.

The holiday house offers accommodation in a cozy double or triple room. Children under three years stay free with parents.

Hotel Kamelot

This place is perfect for tourists with a minimum number of requirements. The two-star hotel offers comfortable rooms with Internet access. A bathroom and shower are on the floor. Personal hygiene products are not provided. Therefore, all the necessary accessories will have to be taken care of in advance.

The hotel has a comfortable cafe, in which everyone has the opportunity to enjoy delicious dishes of Caucasian and Russian cuisine. Here you can rent a banquet hall for 50 people for any event.



Tourists who come to rest in their own car can leave it in a 24-hour guarded parking lot. In the evening, guests can spend time in the sauna or visit the billiards club.

Pension "Angelina"

This hotel is relatively young. He began his work only in 2009. But today there are many regular customers who annually come to rest in Kabardinka. Attracts tourists with a landscaped courtyard with all amenities. There are several gazebos here. You can rent barbecue and skewers. A small pension is perfect for a quiet holiday with your family. Very often tourists stay here with young children.

Like other boarding houses in Kabardinka, Angelina offers comfortable rooms for two, three or four beds. An extra bed is available. The buildings are a 10-minute walk from the city beach and just 400 meters from the M4 Don highway. In the village there are signs with which you can easily get to the pension.



Hotel Angelina offers its guests a number of additional services. Those who come to relax in their own car can use the 24-hour guarded parking lot. Securities, decorations have the opportunity to hide in a safe. The guesthouse has a laundry room and there is a special room for drying things.

Mini-Hotel Emerald

Those who have planned a quiet family vacation, Kabardinka pensions offers the most suitable. One of them is the small hotel "Emerald", designed for simultaneous accommodation of no more than 50 bangs. The building is located just a 3-minute walk from the beach.

In the pension / complex there is a small pool, near which you are invited to relax on a hot summer evening. Each guest can leave their car in the parking lot belonging to the hotel.

Tourist base "Iskra"

A hotel-type holiday home is perfect for a quiet family holiday, or for fun companies. In the buildings there are double and triple apartments with the possibility of installing additional beds. Each room has an iron, an electric kettle, a hairdryer, a TV, and Internet. The territory of the tourist base is an unusually beautiful arboretum, in which there are more than 30 different types of plants. Even on a hot day, pleasant coolness remains in the courtyard of the holiday home.



The tourist base "Iskra" has its own beach with a length of more than 100 meters. Vacationers have the opportunity to rent sun loungers and parasols. Young tourists are offered a huge range of beach activities - from water slides to vibrant trampolines. Extreme lovers can parachute right into the sea.

Sanatorium "Sunny"

Tourists who seek to combine a quality vacation with wellness treatments should pay attention to the sanatorium "Solnechny". Like other resorts in Kabardinka, this institution offers comfortable accommodation, quality service and many additional services. The resort has an excellent indoor pool, which allows you to have a great time even in winter.

Resting on the Black Sea, everyone can go through a program of healing the body. Initially, you will have to pass a number of tests and get a consultation with a therapist who will offer to undergo several physiotherapeutic procedures.

Reviews of tourists about the rest in Kabardinka

Throughout the year, the village of Kabardinka meets tourists. Pensions, hotels work even in winter. The basis of the tourist infrastructure here is precisely a private hotel business. Vacationers note that the owners of boarding houses are good-natured people who strive to please their guests in everything. Pleasing are also low prices. Without much difficulty, it will be possible to find a room costing no more than 500 rubles per day.

For those who prefer to relax without too much fuss, inveterate tourists recommend visiting Kabardinka in May or September. At this time, it will be possible to rent a comfortable room with minimal financial costs.
