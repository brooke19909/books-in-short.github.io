Alfa-Bank loans: types, conditions, interest and reviews

Alfa-Bank, operating in the territory of our country, is the basis of the group of the same name, which also includes banking business in neighboring countries (Ukraine, Belarus and Kazakhstan) and the Netherlands.

Modern positions of the bank

Alfa-Bank is a universal credit institution that currently serves more than three hundred thousand corporate customers and more than fourteen million retail customers. According to last year, more than 700 offices were opened at the bank, and the number of active employees was more than 21,000 people. Alfa-Bank has a huge ATM network, supplemented by ATMs of banks that are partners of a credit institution, including Binbank, Gazprombank, Moscow Credit Bank, UBRIR.

Alfa-Bank is developing as a universal financial institution in the following areas: corporate, small and medium-sized businesses, retail business (cash loans and credit cards, special-purpose loans, accounts and deposits).

According to the results of 2016, this institution took the fourth place in the card market with a credit limit with a portfolio volume of more than one billion US dollars.

Types of lending

An application for a loan at Alfa Bank can be given for the following types of loans:

  • mortgage;
  • car loans;
  • customer credit;
  • credit cards.

Credit Limit Cards

In addition to loans to individuals, Alfa-Bank offers on very attractive terms to issue credit cards:

  • "100 days without%." The one-day interest-free period starts from the moment the debt arises and resumes after it has been fully paid. Credit limit up to 300,000 rubles. Ability to withdraw up to 50,000 rubles per month without paying a commission.
  • "Installment card instead of money." On this card you can buy in installments goods in any stores around the world. The installment plan for partners is up to two years, in other stores - four months.
  • Aeroflot, Alfa-Miles, and Russian Railways Credit Card cards have been developed for travelers. With the Aeroflot card you can exchange miles for flights, hotels and car rental.
  • For shopping enthusiasts, Cash Back, M.Video-Bonus, and Crossroads cards have been developed.
  • "Map of the 2018 FIFA World Cup." The main opportunity given by the card dedicated to the football championship is to receive winning tickets or valuable prizes.
  • "Twins". The peculiarity of the card is a combination of debit and credit cards.

To apply for a credit card you must:

  • Contact the bank branch or fill out an application on the site.
  • Wait for the decision of the bank.
  • Get the card delivered by the courier.

Mortgage

Thinking about the possibility of acquiring an apartment or a house, citizens are forced to believe in the usefulness of mortgage loans for the purchase of real estate, because interest rates are becoming more acceptable every day, and payments are feasible.

According to reviews of loans at Alfa Bank, the institution offers convenient lending programs secured by real estate. The client has the opportunity to arrange a loan for the purchase of finished or under construction housing. The bank has the following mortgage programs:

  • Mortgages for finished housing at a minimum rate of 9.29 percent. The maximum loan amount is fifty million rubles. The down payment must be at least fifteen percent of the loan amount. The maximum loan term is 30 years.
  • Mortgage on housing under construction. The conditions are similar to the first program. Reviews of loans at Alfa Bank confirm that a rate of 9.29 percent applies to bank customers participating in a payroll project, taking into account insurance for all of the above risks.
  • A loan secured by housing at a minimum rate of 13.29%. The maximum loan amount is six hundred thousand rubles. The loan term is not more than thirty years. The ratio of loan amount to property value is up to sixty percent for targeted and up to fifty percent for loans for consumer purposes.

  • When applying for a mortgage loan, it is mandatory to insure the loss of real estate and the life of the borrower. Borrower insurance

When applying for loans at Alfa Bank, individuals can take advantage of a special offer for comprehensive insurance of their income, health and life. By arranging insurance, the borrower protects himself from the fact that he is applying for a loan and until it is fully repaid:

  • will lose his job due to a reduction in the number of employees of the enterprise or liquidation of the organization;
  • very ill;
  • will receive disability of the first group;
  • will die.

If the current borrower loses his job, he will be paid three monthly salaries. If the client receives a disability or dies, the insurance company will pay off the loan debt to the bank.

To apply for insurance:

  • Contact any nearest Alfa-Bank branch.
  • Get all the necessary insurance information.
  • When applying for a loan at Alfa-Bank, put a mark on the authorization of insurance.
  • Sign an insurance contract.
