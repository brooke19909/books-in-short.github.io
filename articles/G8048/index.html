Hotel Sentido Rosa Beach 4 * (Tunisia, Monastir): photos, reviews

Tunisia has been a leader among Russian tourists for many years. They are attracted by low prices, modern hotels and the good quality of the services offered. Travelers will appreciate the sandy beaches, the clear blue sea and the many attractions of this country. Hotel Sentido Rosa Beach 4 * (Tunisia) is located in the inexpensive resort of Monastir . It is chosen by tourists who want to relax on the best beaches of the country at an affordable price.

General information about the complex: opening date, location

The hotel was completed in 1995. Since that time, he annually takes vacationers in the winter and summer. It is located in the northern part of the tourist area of Skanes , which is part of Monastir . Sentido Rosa Beach 4 * is located on its own section of the beach, so the road to the sea from tourists takes only a few minutes. The nearest airport is also nearby. The distance is approximately 2 km. It only takes half an hour to get to the hotel. A large shopping center is 200 meters away, and the center of Monastir is 3 km away. You can either move around the city by bus or taxi.





The four-story building, built in oriental style, is located on a vast territory of 47,000 square meters. m. It is ideal for a comfortable stay with the whole family. The hotel also has facilities for wheelchair users. Tourists with animals are not accommodated here. In 2009, Sentido Rosa Beach 4 * (Tunisia, Monastir) was updated. Renovated all hotel rooms, as well as a common lounge, restaurants and bars.

Number of rooms: amenities and equipment

The hotel is considered a large tourist complex, because it offers tourists about 400 comfortable rooms. They are suitable for single occupancy, and for the whole family. 3 rooms have facilities for physically disabled people: wide doorways, a special shower, sinks, located at a low level. Hotel staff provides housekeeping every day. They take out the trash, vacuum the floors, wipe the dust, and also change their laundry once a week. The apartments have large windows and balconies that overlook the sea, pool or garden surrounding the hotel.





Room equipment is also given great attention. They always have a flat-screen TV that broadcasts many channels of the best quality. Drinks can be chilled in the minibar , which is provided free of charge. But you can use the safe only at an additional cost. It is useful to those tourists who are afraid to leave valuables in their rooms. For round-the-clock communication with the administration of the Sentido Rosa Beach 4 * hotel (Monastir), as well as for calls home, a direct dial telephone is placed in the apartment. In the season when the weather is hot in Tunisia, air conditioning is included in the rooms. They maintain a cool temperature, suitable for a comfortable sleep and rest. The bathroom must have a replenished set of cosmetic accessories, as well as a hairdryer.

Dining at the hotel: restaurants, bars, personalized menu

Like most other hotels in Tunisia, Sentido Rosa Beach 4 * ( Monastir ) offers tourists the popular all-inclusive concept. It includes not only basic meals, but also brunch, light snacks in bars and cafes, as well as soft drinks and spirits produced in Tunisia. Also, vacationers once a week can visit the restaurant, serving its visitors √† la carte. They work daily from 10:00 until midnight. If desired, guests can purchase a drink later than this period, but only for a fee. A children's and diet menus are also available upon request.





Tourists, without leaving the hotel, can try not only local, but also Italian and international cuisine. In addition to the main restaurant, the following catering establishments operate in the complex:

Services provided: local infrastructure and services

The complex offers its guests not only comfortable accommodation, varied and tasty food, but also developed infrastructure. The Sentido Rosa Beach 4 * hotel has everything you need for a quality vacation. Tourists will appreciate the following infrastructure facilities and services:

  • Internet, which can be connected for a fee. You can also take advantage of the free connection, which is located in the common lobby.
  • A small shopping center located on the territory of the hotel. It sells jewelry, clothes, shoes, local and imported alcoholic beverages, souvenirs, food and the press.
  • Secure parking, where guests can leave their car for free. Car rental can be arranged upon request.
  • An ATM for withdrawing cash from credit cards, as well as a currency exchange office.
  • 3 conference rooms where presentations, business meetings, negotiations and banquets can be organized. The largest of them accommodates about 500 people.
  • Doctor's office.
  • Smoking area. It is strictly forbidden to use tobacco products on site or in rooms.
  • Laundry. Here you can take your laundry for free.
  • Barber and beauty salon.




Organization of leisure: beach, pools and entertainment

The organization of leisure at the hotel is presented at the highest level. Guests are offered a wide private section of the beach, which has convenient access to the sea. Comfortable depth allows both adults and children to swim in it. You can also freshen up in hot weather in the outdoor pool, which is filled with fresh water. Free umbrellas and mattresses are located nearby on the terrace. Beach towels are issued as a deposit only. In bad weather, the Sentido Rosa Beach Thalasso and Spa 4 * hotel has an indoor pool. There is a water entertainment center on the beach where tourists can rent windsurfing equipment, motor boats, catamarans and even yachts. For an additional fee you can ride a banana, a hydro scooter , do parasailing . Animators organize massive water polo and volleyball games.





An amphitheater is located on site. Here in the evening entertaining events are held. Children and adults will enjoy non-gambling slot machines and a computer games salon. For sports enthusiasts, the hotel has billiards, futsal, tennis, and aerobics classes.

Rest in a hotel with children

It is believed that the orientation of the Sentido Rosa Beach 4 * hotel is a relaxing family vacation. For tourists with young children, a good discount on accommodation is provided here. A cot can be delivered to the room for an extra charge. All restaurants and cafes are equipped with feeding chairs, which guests can use for free.

There are a lot of entertainments for kids in the hotel. There is a children's "paddling pool" - a shallow pool with fresh water. Parents can also work with their child in the playroom, which has a bookcase, entertaining and intellectual games, paints, pencils and felt-tip pens. For children over 5 years old, a mini club is open daily . While the guys play it, parents can go about their business. The nanny also offers her services.

Guest reviews describing the benefits

On the territory of Monastir there are many hotels. How to choose the best of them? First of all, it is worth reading the impressions of tourists about the rest. Sentido Rosa Beach 4 * reviews get excellent. Guests note the following hotel benefits:

  • The staff is very friendly and friendly. There is no neglect of Russian-speaking tourists. The complex has a guide who will always tell you where to go, where to buy cheap souvenirs, etc.
  • A varied diet. The restaurant prepares new dishes every day, including baked meat, fish, and local specialties. There is no desire to visit paid institutions, because there is enough food at the hotel.
  • The hotel has an excellent team of animators who organize funny exercises, volleyball games, evening shows . However, they are not imposed.
  • There are many free sun loungers on the beach, so there is no need to take a seat in the early morning.
  • Mostly couples from England and Germany rest here, so the hotel is quite quiet, there are no drunk youth companies.




Guest Reviews Describing Deficiencies

Unfortunately, perfect hotels do not exist. So, in the hotel Sentido Rosa Beach 4 *, according to tourists, there are small flaws. In their comments, guests describe such disadvantages of the hotel:

  • The airport is very close to the hotel, so sometimes flying planes can make a lot of noise.
  • In the morning on the beach there are a lot of algae from the sea, which are removed by noon by the hotel staff. But in the early morning because of them there is no sunbathing.
  • When exchanging currency, the administrator does not issue a receipt, which must be presented at the airport when leaving the country.
  • Maids can steal money and valuables, so you need to leave them in a safe.
  • The rooms are tidy poorly. There is a lot of dust on the furniture, and the balcony is in disrepair.
  • Waiters in the restaurant constantly beg for tips for any service.




Is it worth it to relax here?

Complex Sentido Rosa Beach 4 *, the photo of which can be seen in our article, receives a lot of praise. Therefore, it can be recommended for relaxation. The hotel can not be called a budget, but the cost of the trip justifies the quality of service. It will appeal to tourists with young children, lovers of the clean and shallow sea, as well as older people who are looking for a relaxing place to relax.
