Samui Laguna Resort 3 * (Thailand, Koh Samui): address, location, description of rooms, hotel infrastructure, photos and reviews

Thailand in recent years has been very popular among domestic tourists. Despite the long flight, you can relax in this country at a relatively low price, because a lot of budget hotels have been built at its resorts. For example, on Samui, the second largest island of the state, the inexpensive Samui Laguna Resort complex is popular. More information about its infrastructure and housing is described in this article, as well as reviews of former guests.

Location and address

According to tourists, the Samui Laguna Resort 3 * complex (Thailand, Lamai) has a very favorable location for a budget hotel. The fact is that it was built directly on the sandy beach, so guests can walk to the sea in just a couple of minutes. On the other side of the hotel is a fairly large river. The complex is located in a secluded place, so it is suitable primarily for a relaxing holiday. At the same time, the central part of the resort is only 400 meters away. Therefore, restaurants, shopping centers and nightclubs can be reached on foot in 5-10 minutes if desired. And in the immediate vicinity of the hotel itself there is an amusement theme park and a yoga studio.

The exact address of this hotel: 124/103 Moo 3, Tambon Marret, Amphoe Koh Samui, Surat Thani, 84310 Lamai Beach, Thailand.

At 17 km from the hotel there is an international airport, which accepts flights from Russia. Tourists can pay for the transfer or get to the place of rest by public transport, a rented car or taxi. The journey to the hotel usually takes about 1 hour.

Basic hotel information

Hotel Samui Laguna Resort 3 * in Samui offers its guests an inexpensive beach holiday right on the beach. The complex was built in 1990 and during this time it was repaired several times. So, the last renovation of rooms and public spaces was held here in 2010.

This hotel cannot be called great. It occupies a relatively small area of ‚Äã‚Äãonly 5600 square meters. m. On it is the main residential building and a complex of individual bungalows. There are 64 rooms in the hotel, which means that usually around 150 tourists live on its territory. There are no Russian-speaking staff at the hotel, and most of the staff speak Thai and English.

The hotel adheres to a standard policy of settlement, forbidding its guests to stay in rooms with pets. But the children are welcome here, so you can come here even with babies. In addition, upon arrival, guests are not charged a deposit, which is positively evaluated by travelers. Check-in for all guests starts at 14:00 local time and ends at midnight. After the rest, tourists must leave their rooms before noon.

Number of rooms of Samui Laguna Resort 3 * hotel and its equipment

It should be noted right away that the rooms of this hotel do not differ in large sizes, good equipment and exquisite decoration, since the complex provides budget accommodation. Tourists can rent apartments both in the main building and in a separate bungalow. As a rule, the second option will cost a little more. Also in the complex there are no amenities for people with disabilities. Interconnected apartments are not provided here.

In the main building of the Samui Laguna Resort 3 * hotel in Thailand, the rooms of the Building Room category are located. They consist of a living room, a combined bathroom and a balcony. Their area is 31 square meters. The windows overlook the tropical garden, river or surroundings. Some apartments have partial sea views. They can accommodate two adults and a child. The living room has wooden floors. Choose between single or double beds.

Bungalow Rooms are located in separate beachfront bungalows. This is also a one-room apartment, but their area is slightly larger - 38 square meters. The balcony overlooks the garden and the river. Tiled flooring.

The equipment in both categories of rooms is completely the same. The apartments have individual air conditioning and TVs. For a fee, you can use the phone or request a minibar with filling in the room. Wi-Fi is free. The bathroom is equipped with a shower. Upon arrival, tourists are given a set of bath accessories and towels. All rooms are cleaned daily by staff. They change bed linen several times a week.

Hotel infrastructure

On a small territory of the Samui Laguna Resort 3 * hotel (Lamai), a good infrastructure for a budget complex was created. Guests can seek help from one of the receptionists, who are located around the clock at the reception. They will not only help with the solution of the problems, but they will also exchange currency, arrange car or bicycle rental. The latter service is provided only for a fee. Tourists can leave their cars free of charge in a public car park located nearby.

Lockers are also located next to the reception, where you can leave your valuables. A tour desk and ATMs are on site.

Wi-Fi is free in public areas. If you need high-speed network access, you can visit the paid Internet corner with computers.

Upon request, the administrator can call a general practitioner in the room, but his services are not included in the price. Laundry and dry cleaning services are also available at a charge.

Catering

The Samui Laguna Resort does not have the all-inclusive concept, which is popular with tourists from Russia, as many guests prefer to have lunch and dinner at inexpensive restaurants and cafes specializing in local cuisine. Therefore, when making a voucher, guests can pay one of the following systems:

  • BB - breakfast is included in the price;
  • HB - tourists pay breakfast and lunch / dinner;
  • FB - full board, the price of which includes three meals a day and some drinks.

The main Sunshine Restaurant is located on site and serves breakfast. They are served in buffet style. A separate children's menu is available on request. For lunch and dinner, the √† la carte menu includes local and intercontinental dishes. Guests can enjoy refreshing drinks at the bar, which is open daily until 23:00. It also serves desserts, fresh fruit, cocktails and ice cream.

Entertainment

First of all, Samui Laguna Resort is focused on beach holidays, so most of the entertainment here is somehow connected with the sea. The hotel owns a separate section on the city coast, which is not allowed traders and local residents. The beach here is completely sandy, there is no road between it and the hotel, which is appreciated by tourists with young children. Its length is approximately 150 meters. Sun loungers with mattresses and sun umbrellas are installed in the sand. Tourists can borrow them for free.

The complex also has a small outdoor pool with a depth of 2.8 meters. It is filled with fresh water. Its area is only 84 square meters. m. Next to it is also a sun terrace, equipped with sunbeds with mattresses.

In their free time from swimming and sunbathing, tourists can play table tennis or visit the massage room. True, all services are provided for a fee. The beach has a volleyball court. There is also a water entertainment center. For a fee, tourists can go windsurfing, water skiing, parachuting or canoeing.

Conditions for accommodating children

Samui Laguna Resort is a beachfront hotel in a peaceful location, making it suitable for an inexpensive family vacation. Tourists with children of all ages are welcome here, but guests under 12 years old receive discounts on accommodation. Guests can also count on an extra baby cradle if staying with babies under 2 years old. For a fee, guests can hire a nanny, but she will not speak Russian. The children's restaurant has high chairs for feeding.

But there is no large selection of entertainment for children at the hotel. There is also no separate pool for kids. At the same time, there is no shallow compartment in the main pool either. But the territory has a playground.

Positive feedback about Samui Laguna Resort

This hotel receives mixed reviews from tourists, but most of the guests enjoyed their stay in this place. They believe that not the best quality of service is justified by the low cost of living. In reviews, hotel guests describe many of its advantages, we list the most basic of them:

  • clean hotel beach with convenient gradual access to the sea;
  • good location - next to the complex there is a cafe and a market;
  • Cozy and well-groomed area surrounded by a tropical garden;
  • Friendly staff, all employees, without exception, always greet guests;
  • the prices in the restaurant and massage room are not very high, but the service at the hotel is better than in similar infrastructure facilities in the resort center.

Hotel criticism

Like other budget hotels, this complex receives a lot of negative feedback from tourists in whom they criticize the local service. According to the guests, the hotel staff and guests should pay attention to the following disadvantages:

  • regular but poor housekeeping - dust remains on the furniture, and dirty spots can be seen on the floor;
  • tourists saw large mice in the bungalow;
  • the hotel has many cats that beg for food and make noise in the evenings;
  • rooms need repair and replacement of furniture;
  • in the evenings, a huge number of mosquitoes and midges appear on the street, so it‚Äôs better to immediately buy means to protect against them.
