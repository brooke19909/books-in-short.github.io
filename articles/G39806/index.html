Hotel Elegance Hotel (Turkey / Marmaris / Marmaris Center): description and reviews

Kemer is a resort town in Turkey, where every year at the height of the tourist season a huge number of travelers from different parts of the world arrive. It is not surprising that its infrastructure is simply superbly developed. Hotels in this region also offer tourists the best conditions along the coast. A huge number of guests of the village pays special attention to the Elegance Hotel - a hotel located in one of the best places in the resort.

Location

The hotel is located in the Turkish region of Kemer, in one of its small towns - Marmaris, which is also known for its achievements in the tourism business. Not far from the Elegance Hotel is the main market of the city, where tourists tend to get in order to make purchases and choose unusual souvenirs for their relatives and friends. Literally a couple of kilometers from this place is the central part of the city, where fun is in full swing around the clock.

The nearest air traffic hub - Dalaman Airport - is at a fairly impressive distance - 95 kilometers from here.

general information

The construction of the hotel continued for several years, after which it was opened in 1992. Since then, various reconstructions have been carried out on its territory, innovations have been introduced in the interior, arrangement of the territory, technical equipment of rooms, as well as in the facade of the building and at the moment it is a modern building with a bright and large territory, lined with gardens. "Standard"

The hotel provides only 161 rooms of the category "standard". Guests in their reviews note that apartments of this category in Elegance Hotel are made in light color, which creates an even more laid-back atmosphere. They are small in size and consist of one room. In the "standard" can accommodate a couple of guests, as the main berth is designed for such a number of people. If you need to check in another person, an additional place to relax can be a comfortable folding sofa, which is also available in the room. Guests can put their things in one common cupboard, the surface of which serves as a table. In one of the corners is a small coffee table and a pair of armchairs, which together constitute an excellent relaxation area.

If desired, guests can watch the TV, which is available in the room: it is connected to international channels, many of which are Russian-speaking. A safe, telephone and minibar can also be installed here, however, for the use of such interior details a separate rental fee is required.

The rooms offer excellent views of the flowering gardens, and some offer breathtaking views of the Mediterranean coast.



"Family"

Hotel guests who come to rest with young children or just a mass train often prefer apartments of the "family" category. According to tourists, they can very comfortably accommodate with the kids, and at the request of the guests, a cot will be installed in the apartments for free.

With regard to furniture, the number of beds here is designed for 4 people (2 double beds). The room has a pair of wardrobes, as well as one TV for the whole room. Here, as in the "standard", there is also a relaxation area, however, it provides for a larger number of chairs - 4.



Superior Apartment

Elegance Hotel has a total of 23 rooms, which are equipped with the highest standard of comfort. The territory of such apartments is quite large, it is divided into two rooms, one of which is designed for sleeping and relaxing, and the second is a living room.

In the sleeping area there is a large double bed, on which lies an orthopedic mattress, as well as hypoallergenic linen. If desired, tourists can unpack their personal belongings and put them in a large closet, which stands along the wall. The living area has a furniture set. It includes a large soft sofa and a pair of armchairs, next to them there is a coffee table where you can drink a cup of tea or coffee, for the preparation of which the room has a special substation with utensils. If desired, vacationers can watch TV, which is on the wall.



Conditions for children

In many tourist reviews and official descriptions, the Elegance Hotel is positioned as a place ideal for families. That is why conditions are created here that are ideal for small children and teenagers.

First of all, this applies to sleeping places for this category of guests. The family rooms are equipped with a cot as standard. It can also be installed in any other apartment, however this is done on prior request, but also free of charge.

A special club has been created for the kids at the hotel, where a team of professional animators works, who can do everything so that little guests have fun and enjoy being in it. If you do not want to send your child to this place, parents who previously rested at Elegance Hotel International recommend using a babysitting service.

In an open area, not far from the aquazone, there is a swimming pool equipped specifically for children. It has a shallow depth and a special coating that prevents slipping. Near it there is also a large playground, organized in such a way that children can actively spend their time on it.

Nutrition

Guests who are staying at the hotel on an all-inclusive basis are offered a buffet, which is organized in the main restaurant, located in one of three buildings. Here, service starts from the time of breakfast (8 a.m.) and ends with a late dinner (9 p.m.). Experienced tourists recommend that newcomers to the restaurant pay more attention to dishes prepared with seafood - supposedly they have a rich and unusual taste, which you definitely need to familiarize yourself with.

In addition to the restaurant, there are also several bars where you can not only have a drink, but also have a snack. One of them is located on the beach, the second - in the water zone, and the third can be found in the hotel building. Visitors to these places with a flow of laudatory comments note the taste and technology of cooking grilled dishes, which are served in the lobby bar. The bar menu has a large selection of tea and Turkish coffee, which are especially in demand in the evening.



Entertainment

Tourists who are on vacation at the Elegance Hotel (Marmaris) are not bored. All this is because on its territory various events are regularly held and show programs are organized with the participation of a team of talented animators. Every evening, guests can visit the disco, which is held in the bar or on the outdoor terrace by the pool. In the daytime, sports programs are provided for guests.

Fans of quiet relaxation can visit the halls, which are designed for playing billiards, darts, chess, backgammon and table tennis.

Sport

Lovers of an active lifestyle can visit the separate sports sections, which are located in the hotel. One of them is a gym, where a small number of units are installed, with which you can act on different parts of the body. The female half of the guests prefer to attend aerobics classes, which are held in a large hall, under the guidance of an instructor.

If a desire arises, guests can learn skills in water sports, but this activity is only suitable for lovers of extreme sports. At Elegance Hotels International Marmaris, you can use the paid services of instructors who can teach surfing, water skiing, jet skiing or diving.

SPA

Fans of relaxation prefer to visit the huge spa area, which is equipped in the hotel. It consists of a sauna and hammam, near which there is a pool with a jacuzzi, where guests can swim after hot procedures. In the steam room, thermal services can be provided by parmeisters, whose work is paid hourly.

If desired, vacationers can visit the massage room located next to the sauna - experienced massage therapists work in it, who know a lot about their business. A number of cosmetic procedures are also provided here, such as wrapping, peeling, masking, etc.



Additional services

The hotel provides a number of additional services that are paid. In particular, one of the most popular is the rental of cars and bicycles. On them, travelers prefer to travel around Marmaris, go to the central market in order to buy interesting goods, and also go to see local sights and historical monuments, of which there are many.

If desired, guests can take their belongings to dry cleaning or laundry service - in this case, the cost of the service is calculated based on the amount of work. You can also visit the Internet room and use the Network for free.

Hotel guests are completely free of charge at any time of the day to contact the administrator, who is located at the reception 24 hours a day, and ask all questions of interest regarding accommodation and the provision of services. It should be noted that many of the administration team speak Russian.

Prices

The cost of a ticket to this hotel is relatively small. According to statistics, a tour to the Elegance Hotel (Kemer) costs Muscovites an average of 70 thousand rubles per two people. This price includes standard items, which include accommodation during the week, round-trip flight, and also upon arrival of tourists delivered from the airport to the hotel. If necessary, the package can include various paid services or sightseeing tours, but experienced tourists recommend paying for all this upon arrival at the Elegance Hotel (Marmaris).



Reviews

Hotel guests often share their impressions gained during the holiday. So, most of them talk about the excellent service at the hotel. This is especially true for administrators and the animation team. According to tourists, these groups of employees are very responsible for their activities and are attentive to each guest. Almost every one of them knows the Russian language, which greatly facilitates communication with tourists from Russia and the CIS countries.

Reviews of Elegance Hotel often say that the hotel rooms have a warm atmosphere, it is cozy and calm. Linen is changed and room cleaning is done every day, which is also a worthy indicator of service.



Special attention is often paid to the level of cooking in the restaurant and the organization of leisure activities for guests. Great admiration for the surrounding nature, which can be seen on the Elegance Hotel photos taken by tourists during their holidays.

Many guests are ready to recommend this place to their friends and relatives as the most suitable for a vacation, since it is here, in their opinion, that high service and low prices are ideally combined.
