This application works on all modern operating systems: Android, Windows, iOS. For devices with Android OS, you need to download AnTuTu in the Google Play games and applications store.

Test results

After analysis, the program will show how many points your device "earned" and the number of other devices that were able to be circumvented by the test results.

If after testing your device received an estimate of about 100 to 140 thousand, it means that it is a representative of mid-range smartphones: the device will be able to perform all standard tasks, launch most games at medium and high settings and have multitasking in dual-window mode. If approximately 70-100 thousand points, then the phone is a budget option. He will not be able to run most modern games, given the growing requirements for new applications, his performance will last for a year or two. If after the test you see an estimate from 140,000 to 200,000, then you have a top-end smartphone that can view and record 4K video, as well as run all games taking into account the maximum graphics settings.
