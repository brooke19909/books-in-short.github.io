The tablet slows down: causes, settings, antivirus, programs

Sometimes users complain that their tablet is slow. Often this gadget is slow for one reason or another. Some scenarios do not require serious action and are not dangerous. But there are exceptions, and under certain circumstances complex manipulations may be required. Why does this occur? How to fix the situation? Numerous user and specialist tips will help sort this out.





Device overheating

Does your tablet slow down? Then it's time to check how much this gadget heats up during operation. As practice shows, it is the overheating of any computer device that causes braking. In some cases, a spontaneous shutdown or reboot is possible.

If you notice that at first the tablet is working fine, and later it heats up and slows down, therefore, you need to provide a good cooling system. First, do not use the gadget for too long. Secondly, run a minimum of applications at the same time (running in the background are also considered). Thirdly, use a special tablet stand for support.

These simple rules will help the owner solve the problem. True, not always. Overheating is not very common. Other options are more common, but they are also not a reason for panic.

Many programs

The fact is that there are not so many really serious reasons for this problem. Does your tablet work slowly? Check the number of running programs on the gadget system. They can cause certain problems in the operation of the device.





Do not confuse this problem with overheating - now we are talking about loading the processor. This usually happens when a large number of applications and games are launched at the same time. Sometimes just one ‚Äúweighty‚Äù and demanding utility is enough to disrupt the tablet.

What will help normalize the situation? Close all unnecessary programs and applications, not forgetting about games, and continue to run only those utilities that are needed at the moment. Do not bring the gadget to processor overload, because this phenomenon can cause not only braking, but also mechanical damage to the components of the device.

Demanding application

Does your tablet slow down? The reasons may be different. It is worth paying attention to the circumstances in which this malfunction occurs.





Sometimes the demanding application can become the reason of braking. But in this situation, the gadget starts to work slowly only when the program starts. The rest of the time, he performs his functions at the proper speed.

There are two ways out: either abandon the braking application, or put up with the slow operation of the device. How to proceed, it's up to you. Usually, users try to choose the first scenario. After all, most programs are replaceable, and games are not so important.

Therefore, it is recommended to reconfigure Android. Usually, this is about disabling the autorun of most existing programs and applications. You can do this in the "Settings" on your gadget.

Full reset

Sometimes such manipulations are useless. What to do in this case, and what can help establish the work of Android? How to clean the tablet so that it does not slow down anymore when working? It is recommended to make a complete reset. This process is called Hard Reset. During it, all user settings are reset and returned to the factory state.

How exactly on the tablet to carry out this procedure? To begin with, try to save your personal data in any way (the slightest system failure - and you will lose the information stored on the tablet). Do not be alarmed if the tablet restarts - this is absolutely normal. It remains only to wait for the system to start. Then check how well the gadget works. Does it slow down again? Then the reasons are not at all in the settings. In general, Hard Reset is a radical solution to most problems with Android. Therefore, resetting the settings should be done only in the most extreme case - do not rush, resorting to this method.

Memory

Instead, you should first consider other causes of braking the gadget. How to increase tablet speed ? To do this, you will have to monitor the remaining space on your device, as well as on the memory card. The problem is that sometimes the memory of any gadget is clogged with either system files or user files. When the memory card or the built-in space is full, the tablet starts to slow down. This is quite normal - the device reduces its performance due to lack of resources.

The only way out is to clean the gadget from ‚Äúgarbage‚Äù. Manually delete old photos, videos and programs, that is, everything that has not been used for a long time. Only in this way can the operational system work. A good option would be to purchase a memory card for a larger amount of space. But this will not solve the problem completely - you can only delay the inevitable.





A great piece of advice that will help to maintain the tablet‚Äôs normal performance is to conduct timely cleaning of equipment from unnecessary, old and already unnecessary documents.

Viruses

Sometimes computer equipment is hit by a variety of viruses. They seriously affect the performance of any tablet or phone. To verify infection, you must install a special antivirus for Android and check it. This is done in the same way as on a computer.

All that is required of you is to launch the application, check the system for viruses, and then treat potentially dangerous objects. Remove everything that the antivirus program detects. After these steps, it is advisable to reboot the device. Does the tablet slow down again? If the reason lay in the infection of the gadget, there should no longer be any slowdown.

The choice of antivirus

Which anti-virus systems are suitable? The most successful is Dr.Web. It quickly finds malicious files, carefully and carefully cures potentially dangerous objects. Often, a trial version of the application is offered immediately after purchasing the tablet.

Also worth paying attention to Avast Mobile. Unlike the Doctor, it does not have a firewall. Otherwise, it works just as well. A significant advantage of Avast is noted - its free distribution. Dr.Web provides only a trial version of the application, after which the antivirus for Android should be purchased.

Kaspersky Internet Security is the next paid comprehensive anti-virus for tablets and phones. Users note that Kaspersky removes most viruses, but some of their types infiltrate the system anyway. Like Dr.Web, this program also requires payment.





The latest popular antivirus for tablets and phones is 360 Security. It is a free mobile anti-virus application that allows not only to get rid of malicious content, but also to clean the device registry no worse than Kaspersky or Doctor.

Which antivirus to choose? You must decide this yourself. It all depends on your personal preferences: someone will want to pay for an antivirus program, and some will choose free versions.

Time

Does the tablet slow down? Another reason for this phenomenon may be the banal wear of equipment. Over time, even the best gadget becomes worthless. If your tablet has worked for about 3-4 years, do not be surprised at the braking that has appeared. This is a signal to replace the device.

Also, among the main reasons for the slow operation of any device, one can distinguish such a point as the breakdown of any component. If you suspect that you have suffered a similar fate, take the tablet for repair. In the service center you will receive a consultation with recommendations for further actions.
