La Vaiencia Beach Resort (India, Goa): hotel description, traveler reviews

An increasingly popular destination among travelers is India, namely Goa. This island can offer tourists a lot, from long white beaches and warm sea waves to delicious food, good service and the opportunity to have fun on excursions. Naturally, over the past few years, the number of hotels on the coast has increased significantly. One of the pretty popular destinations is La Vaiencia Beach Resort.

Of course, when planning your vacation, a person will be interested in all possible information. So what does the hotel look like and at what point in Goa do you look for it? How do people who have already managed to relax here speak of him? Should I choose a hotel as a temporary refuge?

Description of territory and exact location





The cozy La Vaiencia Beach Resort is located in the northern part of Goa, almost on the coast - you can reach Morjim Beach in a few minutes. Dabolim International Airport is about 62 km away. But do not worry about this, as the hotel offers a shuttle service to its customers.

It’s worth mentioning right away that the hotel is quite small - it covers an area of ​​1200 square meters and consists of two two-story buildings built in an interesting coastal style. The hotel opened its doors for tourists not so long ago, namely in 2005. The last thorough repair was carried out in 2010, so the hotel is well equipped and equipped with all necessary modern equipment.

Hotel La Vaiencia Beach Resort: photo and description of rooms





As already mentioned, the hotel is quite small, on its territory there are 24 rooms:

  • 19 Deluxe rooms;
  • 3 Suites;
  • 2 Standard rooms.

The rooms are quite spacious, with large windows and access to a small but completely private balcony, where you can breathe fresh air and enjoy the garden. The rooms are furnished in European style, the furniture is new and comfortable. You can count on a basic set of household appliances. There is an air conditioning system that is simply indispensable in hot weather. Cable and some satellite channels can be watched on TV. There is a safe in the closet, which you can use for free. Tourists are also provided with a hot drink set. There is also a small fridge and minibar (filled only upon request).

The bathroom will also please with cleanliness and convenience. There is a toilet, shower, mirror, wash basin, modern plumbing. You can count on shampoos, soap and other hygiene products. Towels are changed to clean regularly, and the rooms are cleaned well.

Can I eat at the hotel? Eating places





Paying for accommodation at the hotel La Vaiencia Beach Resort 2 *, tourists are entitled to free breakfast. Immediately it is worth saying that since for the most part the food in India is vegetarian, the choice of meat dishes is unlikely to be large. Nevertheless, in the morning you can enjoy fresh fruits, vegetable dishes, fresh pastries, toasts, pancakes, etc.

At lunchtime and in the evening you can eat in the same restaurant, however, already at an additional cost. Work here on the menu, dishes are prepared to order, offer a large number of masterpieces of international, Indian and Asian cuisine. In addition, you can eat near the hotel - along the street there are many cafes, restaurants and bars that offer delicious dishes at an affordable price.

Water recreation: where is the beach located?





Goa is primarily famous for its beaches. What can the hotel La Vaiencia Beach Resort 3 * offer? As already mentioned, the hotel was built just off the coast - the distance is about 200 meters. According to reviews, the beach is incredibly beautiful and picturesque. Fresh sea air, warm water, gentle sun and soft sand underfoot - every traveler can count on this. By the way, there is a small bar on the shore serving chilled drinks and snacks.

Tourists accustomed to a more active pastime will also not be bored. Here you can go sailing, scuba diving, windsurfing (there are special training centers in the city), as well as rent a canoe, a boat, go water skiing and boats.

Does the hotel offer an additional service?





Despite its relatively modest size, La Vaiencia Beach Resort 2 * hotel has something to offer its guests. To begin with, it is worth saying that the reception desk and room service are working around the clock - you can get help at any time.

There is a laundry service, which is quite inexpensive. Wireless access to the World Wide Web is available throughout the hotel. Reviews indicate that the connection speed is not bad, and Wi-Fi is available even in some rooms.

There is no medical office at the hotel, but in case of deterioration in well-being, a qualified doctor will be called to you.

Guest Leisure: Entertainment

There is not so much entertainment here, because the hotel is primarily designed for a calm, measured rest. In the courtyard there is a fairly spacious pool with sections of different depths. Here guests bathe, relax, have fun and just chat with each other. By the way, there are plenty of sun loungers and towels around.

Guests can also use the local sauna. Of course, it is difficult to imagine India without traditional massage rooms. Here you can take a course of wellness or relaxing massage, as only professional masters work at the hotel. Pleased with the presence of a beauty salon, where tourists can use the services of talented stylists.

And do not forget that India is just perfect for a rich excursion vacation - you can enjoy not only historical and cultural attractions, but also the incredible beauty of nature. Guided tours will also be available at La Vaiencia Beach Resort (Goa). The staff will tell you where to go in order to get to know the local culture.

Hotel complex La Vaiencia Beach Resort: reviews





Experienced travelers know that a lot of useful information can be obtained by talking with those people who have already managed to visit a particular place. Immediately, we note that tourists for the most part leave good reviews about the hotel La Vaiencia Beach Resort 3 *. This is a really calm, small hotel located in a quiet area. Here you are surrounded by care and sincere hospitality, and therefore you will feel at home.

The rooms here are spacious and clean. The furniture is serviceable, the equipment also works. There is always cold and hot water in the bathroom. Maids clean every day, and they really cope with their work. As for nutrition, the breakfast is hearty, although not always varied. Many tourists prefer to dine in the hotel restaurant, on the summer terrace, which offers beautiful views of the surroundings and the garden. By the way, in cafes located on the beach, you can also eat, and the prices are very affordable. The staff are polite and pleasant in communication.

In general, La Vaiencia Beach Resort is perfect for tourists looking for a relaxing holiday by the sea. Fans of crowded cities, nightclubs and fiery parties here can be a little boring.
