Hotel Caribbean World Nabeul 4 * in Tunisia

Hotel description: luxury hotel Caribbean World Nabeul 4 * is very popular among foreign tourists. This place is intended for an excellent beach holiday, and here both youth companies and families with children stay.





Hotel Caribbean World Nabeul 4 * is a large complex located just a few meters from the beautiful golden beaches. From all sides the hotel is surrounded by flowering exotic gardens. Just one kilometer from it is the ancient city of Nabeul. The airport is 60 kilometers away. Huge golf courses are located nearby, which will please both beginners and professional players.

Description of rooms : the hotel consists of the main residential building, which houses 181 rooms, as well as 92 separate spacious bungalows. The rooms have different capacities, so guests can choose exactly what they like. By the way, the room has a secluded loggia, where it is always a pleasure to spend time.





All rooms are decorated in an exotic oriental style. Guests will find here all the necessary household appliances. The rooms are equipped with air conditioning and heating, as well as a telephone, safe and TV. There is also a private bathroom, equipped with modern fixtures.

There are also two separate luxurious restaurants, where guests are offered the most interesting delicacies of Tunisia and Italy. There are two bars, one of which is located in the middle of a picturesque garden and is covered with palm leaves. Here you can enjoy carbonated drinks, excellent wines and locally made beer.
