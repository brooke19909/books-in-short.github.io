Popular restaurants in Murmansk

The indigenous population of the city of Murmansk is the descendants of Russians and Pomors, who have long lived on these lands. Of course, this also left its mark on the national cuisine.

In the 18th century, the local population had fish and the most common bread as their main food. Given the severe weather conditions, wheat was not grown here, therefore, for the most part, the bread was rye. They ate vegetables that were grown in such conditions: turnips, peas, garlic, carrots, swede and onions.

Today it is a modern city, which means that in most institutions culinary specialists cook the same as in the rest of Russia. In this article, we consider the popular restaurants of Murmansk. Moreover, there are plenty of them here today.

Pie "Stolle"

Consider the restaurants of Murmansk should start with the pie "Stolle." It is located in a quiet city center in a separate building on the avenue to them. Lenin. Nearby is the city administration and museum of local lore.



The Stolle company appeared in 2002. It has a reputation for being the best pie in all of Russia. They tried to recreate the taste of pastries and the atmosphere of the early twentieth century.

Judging by the reviews, the internal interior resonates well with the interiors of various Russian cities of the century before last: solid doors, oak flooring, cast brass chandeliers, leather sofas. The aroma of freshly baked pies and melodic music fill the cafe with homely hospitality and soul.

Restaurant "Forest"

We continue to consider the restaurants of Murmansk. Forest invites residents of the city and its guests to relax in a pleasant atmosphere and taste amazing dishes cooked in tandoor and over an open fire. Here, artesian water produced outside this city is used in cooking.

Here guests are served dishes in handmade dishes, which are made in the Czech Republic by special order, drinks in spiegelau glasses, which are both beautiful and safe, as evidenced by numerous reviews.

Iceberg Restaurant

Looking for restaurants in Murmansk with live music and a dance floor, it is worth highlighting the Iceberg complex. This is a great place for anniversaries, weddings, corporate and graduation parties, romantic dates, children's parties and other celebrations, as evidenced by the reviews.



This is a classic restaurant with excellent European cuisine and a warm atmosphere. The interior is thought out in such a way that you will feel privacy and comfort at any table. The institution includes four rooms, two of which are on the 2nd floor, and the other 2 are on the third.

Good drinks, an original menu, live music, comfortable atmosphere, excellent service, friendly welcome and reasonable prices - this is exactly what is necessary for a normal stay.

Restaurant "Alan"

Looking for restaurants in Murmansk with live music, pay attention to the "Alan". In this place you can have a great time and get wonderful impressions of Caucasian, Eastern, European and Georgian cuisine, the aroma of hookah and heady music.



The menu includes classic dishes: hichina, dolma, manti, kharcho. Judging by the reviews, here is a rich wine list, all kinds of non-alcoholic and alcoholic drinks.

Restaurant Dekante

Of course, the restaurants of Murmansk could not do without French cuisine. Dekante hospitably welcomes its visitors every day. The sophistication of European, French, Pan-Asian cuisine, children's and Lenten menus is at the head of local culinary delights, as evidenced by numerous reviews.

All dishes are expertly prepared in the spirit of contrast and clarity - this is what real European gourmets prefer most of all. Always in a huge assortment, an excellent selection of fine wines, as well as pleasant background music.



The guests of the restaurant are fascinated by the filigree of its interior, gallantry of service and luxurious cuisine.

Cafe "Retro City"

We continue to consider restaurants in Murmansk with live music. Reviews of the cafe “Retro City” suggest that you can spend an unforgettable evening with friends watching various sports broadcasts, banquets and corporate evenings, theme parties, birthday parties, children's parties, weddings, anniversaries.

The cafe seats 60 people. At the same time, a huge panoramic window offers stunning views of the city. Here guests will be offered classic European dishes and meat from the grill, while the assortment of alcoholic beverages will delight quite pleasant tasters.

You can enjoy live music here on weekends.
