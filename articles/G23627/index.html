Pension "Nightingale" in Anapa: description, photos, reviews

If you are going to spend your vacation in Anapa, the Pension "Nightingale" will be a great option. It offers comfortable accommodation, many types of entertainment, a beach at 350 meters and all at affordable prices.

Brief information about the boarding house





Pension "Nightingale" (Anapa), whose photo is located above, is located in an excellent area with a clear sea and good beaches. It is also conveniently located near the city infrastructure (bus stop, shops, cafes and restaurants).

On the territory of 1.14 hectares is a complex of small buildings and cottages. Around them are many green spaces. Everything here is conducive to family vacations: there are playgrounds, cultural events are held.

Number of recreation facilities





In total in the boarding house "Nightingale" (Anapa) there are three residential buildings and nine cottages. Can accommodate up to 250 people at a time.

The following room categories are available in the buildings:

  • double "standards";
  • triple one-room "standards";
  • triple two-room "standards";
  • triple "block 3 + 3".

All cottages have a standard set of furniture, a small refrigerator, fan, TV. Two rooms have two separate exits.

List of Services





In the boarding house "Nightingale" vacationers are offered:

  • four meals a day in the dining room;
  • playground with swings and slides;
  • paid guarded parking;
  • tour desk services;
  • billiard room;
  • library
  • rental of sports and beach equipment (umbrellas and deck chairs);
  • transfer services from the airport or railway station and back;
  • first aid and call specialists;
  • taxi call;
  • use of the camera at the administrator;
  • Wellness services.

Also on the territory of the boarding house are constantly held cultural and entertainment events for adults and children. Hot and cold water is supplied to the rooms without interruption. The price includes only accommodation and four meals a day.

Organization of leisure in the boarding house "Nightingale"





The boarding house "Nightingale" offers a comfortable stay for the whole family. Therefore, there is a large comfortable playground with new attractions, swings and slides. There is also a small library where you can take read as children's literature, as well as applied, contemporary works and much more.

Among the activities for parents at the guesthouse is billiards and table tennis. You can also rent sports equipment (balls, rackets). There is also a video room in the boarding house where various cartoons and films are shown.

For those who wish to improve their health, the Nightingale offers several options for medical services: therapeutic massage and magnetotherapy. You can pass the session in a separate building.

There is no pool in the guesthouse, but all guests can visit the neighboring recreation center "Ryabinushka" for an additional fee. The beach at the Nightingale has its own sandy beach, located just 350 meters from the gate. The shore is clean, the water is clear, the entrance is gentle, which is very good for families with children. There is a public bar and cafe, there are showers, toilets, changing cabins. Sun loungers, parasols and sun loungers can be rented on site. Also on the beach there are many different entertainment attractions: scooters, boats, banana riding, scuba diving, water skiing, boat trips, parachuting and much more.

When tourists get bored with beach vacations, the administration of the Solovey boarding house offers excursion services. From here you can visit the famous dolphinarium at Cape Utrish, the resort of Gelendzhik, water parks in Anapa, Tuapse and Gelendzhik, the cities of Novorossiysk, Abrau-Durso, Tsemess Bay, mud volcanoes, mysterious ancient dolmens, a beautiful lotus plantation and much more. Also, in the guesthouse, guests organize magnificent sea and horse rides, regular and underwater fishing, diving.

Reviews for the pension "Nightingale" (Anapa)





The Nightingale has been open to its guests since 1976. During this time, many families with children and single tourists visited here who took away positive memories of their holidays. Indeed, despite the relatively modest conditions, the boarding house administration is trying to offer its guests all the best. Therefore, it is not surprising that guests leave mostly positive reviews. They write that:

  • The territory of the boarding house is landscaped and landscaped. Everything is done wisely. There are paths and benches, garbage cans are installed, a children's playground is in the shade of trees.
  • Very often, various events are held: in the evening for adults, and in the afternoon for children.
  • The dining room serves delicious homemade dishes. The portions are large. The food is diverse, there are many fresh vegetables and fruits, herbs, homemade cakes. Dishes are immediately set on the table, no need to stand in lines for extradition.
  • The rooms are not very spacious, but renovated, clean, there was no dust and debris anywhere.
  • The staff is attentive, friendly.
  • The territory is under round-the-clock protection, protected from strangers. It is noteworthy that children are not allowed out of the boarding house after seven in the evening without adult permission.
  • Hot water is uninterruptedly supplied, only the pressure is not very strong.
  • The beach is very nice, sandy.
  • Near the boarding house there is a market where you can buy almost everything you need: food, fresh fish, personal hygiene items, medicines, various leisure products.

The location of the boarding house

Pension Solovey is located in Anapa, Dzhemete, on Pionersky Prospekt 74. You can get from Anapa airport or railway station by public transport in 15 minutes. On your own car you need to go along Simferopol highway to the Guest Avenue, and then turn left.
