Movie Gate Hotel 4 * (Egypt, Hurghada): reviews and photos

During a vacation in Egypt, many tourists from Russia and other countries of the former USSR today choose 4 star hotels in Hurghada . And this is not surprising. After all, this city offers the opportunity for a wonderful holiday on the Red Sea coast. As for hotels, the local institutions of the four-star category offer guests comfortable accommodation and excellent service at an affordable cost. Today we offer a closer look at one of these hotels called Movie Gate Hotel 4 *.

This hotel is perfect for a relaxing family vacation, or for a fun pastime for young people.



Hotel Gate Hotel (Hurghada, Egypt): room description

The housing stock of the hotel complex under consideration is represented by 456 comfortable apartments located in several buildings. There are rooms of the following categories: standard double (with sea view and without), family, economy, superior. All apartments have the necessary furniture, TV, air conditioning, telephone, bathroom with hairdryer, balcony. Wireless internet access is available at an additional cost. There are no safes in the apartment. Valuables can be deposited at the reception (surcharge). Rooms are cleaned every day. Towels and bed linen are regularly changed.



Nutrition

Hotel Movie Gate Hotel 4 * operates on the "ol inclusive" system. Three times a day, hotel guests can eat in the main buffet restaurant. You can also visit one of the four √† la carte restaurants. Here you will be offered to choose from the menu dishes of oriental, traditional Egyptian, Italian cuisine, as well as superbly prepared seafood. In addition, during the daytime, guests can eat fast food in the snack bar and enjoy their favorite refreshments and alcoholic drinks in one of the eight bars of the hotel.



Entertainment and infrastructure

The Movie Gate Hotel has its own sandy beach, which is 350 meters long. There is a sufficient number of sun loungers and parasols. Near the coast, snorkeling enthusiasts can admire a variety of underwater inhabitants. All the necessary equipment is offered to be purchased in a shop on the hotel premises.

In addition, guests can have a great time at one of several pools. It has sun terraces and bars. During the day and evening, the guests are entertained by a team of animators.

As for the children, several small shallow pools are equipped for them at the hotel, a mini-club works, and in the evenings there is a mini-disco.

Movie Gate Hotel 4 *: reviews of tourists from Russia

Many modern travelers, in the process of choosing a hotel in one country or another in the world, try, besides other information, to study the impressions of people who have already been here. After all, this allows you to make a picture closer to reality. In order to facilitate your task, we bring to your attention the generalized reviews of our compatriots regarding the Movie Gate Hotel, located in such a wonderful Egyptian city like Hurghada. 4 stars, according to travelers, is a justifiable category for this hotel. It corresponds to the cost of living. Thus, our compatriots were generally satisfied with their choice of hotel. However, we offer to understand everything in more detail.



Hotel itself

The overwhelming number of guests note that the rooms in the "Movie Gate" are spacious, with a fresh renovation. The household appliances here are modern, the plumbing is in excellent condition, the apartments have everything you need. Stocks in the minibar are constantly replenished. According to tourists, the cleaning here is of high quality and regular. Towels and linen are changed several times a week. As for bath accessories, the guests say that there is only liquid soap. So shampoos, shower gels , etc. will have to be taken with you from home or purchased on the spot. Some of our compatriots considered a small minus the lack of a safe in the apartments. So, you can leave things for storage only at the reception.

With regard to the territory of the hotel "Movie Gate" (Hurghada), tourists leave reviews about it positive. So, according to them, it is very spacious, green and clean, which, according to many, is rare for Egypt. The guests say that all day the hotel workers are cleaning, washing, cutting, replanting plants, etc.



Kitchen

Regardless of which hotels in Hurghada are being discussed - 4 stars, 5 stars or cheaper options, disputes among guests about food are commonplace. The Movie Gate Hotel was no exception. So, in their reviews, some travelers claim that they did not like the food here. In their opinion, cooks are overused with hot spices. However, the vast majority of guests claim that the food is consistent with the four-star category of the hotel. In the main restaurant you can always choose a wide variety of dishes from meat, poultry, seafood. In addition, there are always plenty of side dishes, salads, fruits and sweets for guests. In addition, many tourists enjoyed visiting √† la carte restaurants. But as for drinks, according to our compatriots, there are no natural juices here. An alternative is powder drinks. Regarding alcohol, guests had no complaints.



Entertainment

Since most tourists come to Egypt to relax on the beach, the proximity and quality of the beach play one of the most important roles in choosing a hotel. So, as for the Movie Gate Hotel 4 * (Hurghada), it is located on the very shore of the Red Sea. According to travelers, the hotel's beach is quite large, there is a sufficient number of sunbeds and umbrellas for everyone. You can enter the water either directly from the shore or from the pier. In the first case, you will have to walk about 200 meters to a depth. Many guests did not like this, and they preferred to swim from the pier. Tourists who have been here also note that it is recommended to enter the water only in special shoes. Otherwise, you risk injuring your legs. You can buy such shoes in the shop at the hotel. Here you can buy a mask for snorkeling, flippers and other equipment.

Many hotel guests also enjoyed spending time by the pools, of which there are several on the territory of the Movie Gate Hotel 4 * (Hurghada, Egypt). It has spacious sunbathing terraces with sun loungers, bars where you can not only get a drink, but also, if you wish, have a snack.

As for animation, the opinions of our compatriots on this issue were divided. So, some of the guests claim that they were quite satisfied with the unobtrusive entertainment program offered here. According to them, during the daytime animators invite all those who wish to do aerobics, stretching, water aerobics, play water polo or volleyball. At the same time, they act unobtrusively and do not try to convince vacationers, causing irritation, as is the case in some other hotels. As for the second group of tourists, they were not very pleased with the weak evening entertainment program. According to them, the hotel could take care of more diverse shows and performances, so that people had something to occupy themselves after dinner.

Experienced travelers also recommend that those who come to rest in Egypt be sure to go for a vacation at least for a couple of excursions in order to diversify their pastime. At the same time, they advise not to buy tours from hotel guides, but to order them at local travel agencies, focusing on reviews of other vacationers. So, according to them, the quality will be offered to you the same, but if such a trip costs one and a half, or even two times cheaper than a hotel guide.

Staff

As for the staff at the Movie Gate Hotel (Egypt, Hurghada), the reviews of which we are reviewing, our compatriots were generally satisfied. So, according to them, the hotel staff are mostly friendly and attentive. They do their job well. A big plus for our compatriots was the fact that many hotel workers speak Russian well. And this despite the fact that the main contingent of the hotel consists of Germans and local residents.
