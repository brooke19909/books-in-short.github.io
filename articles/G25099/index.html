What are the bus stations of Cherepovets

Of course, every vacationer who first arrived in the city is interested in the question of where the bus stations of Cherepovets are located.

Bus station location

In fact, Cherepovets bus stations are represented by only one station, which is located in the city center on 44 Maxim Gorky Street. For visitors, the bus station is very convenient because it is only a hundred meters from the railway station, so if you need to continue your journey, the city guests There are problems with the search.

The appearance of the bus station





Cherepovets bus stations are always like a magnet for every tourist who wants to plunge into the romance of travel. This small two-story building is striking in the simplicity of its construction, because during the construction in the 70s of the last century no one even thought about highlighting the bus station among the other gray buildings. Cherepovets is famous for its smoke screens and buildings of the same type, built during the active development of the metallurgical industry. This building, almost completely made of glass, fits into the gloomy atmosphere.

Here you can appreciate the simplicity of Soviet construction, when everyone was chasing budget savings, rather than originality. This small building can hardly accommodate a large number of people who want to shelter from rain and snow in bad weather. This cannot be done on the street, as there are no canopies.

Guests of the city can have a meal here in the buffet, existing since the time when the bus stations of Cherepovets opened. No alternative food points are provided here, unless you can walk to the nearest cafe or train station.
