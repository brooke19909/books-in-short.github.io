Hotel Grecian Park Hotel 5 * (Cyprus / Protaras): photos and reviews

If you want to spend your vacation in some picturesque resort village, then you should go to Greece. There are plenty of such places. They, as well as the hotels located there, are very popular among tourists. And one of these options is Grecian Park Hotel 5 *.





Briefly about the hotel

A beautiful and modern hotel complex is located in a picturesque place of Protaras. This is a resort village located in the southeastern part of the island of Cyprus. Just a short drive from Protaras is a local attraction. It is the southeastern tip of Cyprus. The nearest airport is about 50 minutes from here, as it is in Larnaca.

Grecian Park Hotel 5 * itself is located in close proximity to Konnos beach. The Cavo Greco National Park is a minute's drive from the hotel, and Ayia Napa Sea Caves are three kilometers away. By the way, it takes about 10-15 minutes to get to this popular resort. And you should definitely visit Ayia Napa. There is a museum of Thalassa, a local monastery, a water park and many other places of interest for tourists. Fortunately, there is a bus stop a hundred meters from the hotel, so you can leave almost anywhere.

Each person, arriving at the resort, dreams that his vacation would be carefree and easy. At Grecian Park Hotel 5 * they understand this. So there is everything that the guests may need.

There is free Wi-Fi and parking nearby, a 24-hour reception and currency exchange office, as well as laundry with dry cleaning and car rental. However, this is not the entire list of services.

A mini club and a playground are organized for the children whom their parents brought with them. And if you need childcare, you can call the nanny. Fortunately, there is such an opportunity.

In addition to the above, Grecian Park Hotel 5 * has various shops, a hairdresser, a beauty salon and the provision of such basic services as delivery of food, drinks, newspapers and packed lunches. And for business travelers, there are all conditions. There are several modern, well-equipped meeting rooms. The first is called George Seferis. It boasts parquet floors and high ceilings. Entertainment

Bored, being in Grecian Park 5 * (Protaras), will not work. Here, in addition to a huge list of services, there are many more ways to have fun. Of course, there is a huge pool and sun terrace, table tennis, a billiard room and a full court.

But that is not all. There is an indoor pool and an Olympic Health Club. Floor simulators, cardioprograms, barbells, dumbbells, treadmills - this is just a small list of what is here. Each person who prefers a healthy lifestyle, going inside the hall, will forever fall in love with him. But there is also a jacuzzi, sauna and hammam. You can purchase a membership card at the club or order several fitness programs. Professional trainers work in the hall, under the guidance of which many here are happy to train. And as a result, they receive a fitness assessment, which includes everything related to the body and human health - body mass index, amount of fat, flexibility, blood pressure and much more.

For beauty and health

The Grecian Park Hotel, like any other 5-star complex, has its own SPA salon, which employs experienced specialists with a medical background. It offers treatments for both women and men. And their list is quite large. There is everything - from nail treatment and waxing to aromatherapy and make-up (wedding make-up, by the way, is also done).

They also make magnificent Indian, Asian and Thai massages, chocolate wraps, peels, masks. And, of course, they carry out stone therapy. However, again, this is not the entire list of services. A full range is available upon arrival (guests are given brochures).

And by the way, there is a hairdresser. High-class stylists are well aware of their job - it will not be difficult for them to make a spectacular haircut, hairstyle or styling.





Bars and Restaurants

Being in Grecian Park Hotel 5 * (Protaras, Cyprus), it is impossible to stay hungry. There are several excellent establishments where guests are treated to haute cuisine. Take, for example, the Charisma restaurant. Here, the buffet system serves breakfast, lunch, as well as light snacks for lunch. Le Grand Foyer offers coffee, tea and sweets in the lobby.

For dinner, you should visit Antaeus Restaurant. You just have to dress up - the restaurant has a dress code. And treat here dishes of international and Cypriot cuisine. Themed nights are also regularly held. Dinner at this restaurant will be remembered for a long time, thanks to live music and an indescribable atmosphere.

On the fourth floor of the hotel is an institution called UMI Japanese & Sushi Bar Restaurant. Lovers of Asian cuisine and beautiful landscapes will definitely like it. Indeed, from there an amazing view of the sea surface opens.

There is also an Italian restaurant, the exotic Cliff lounge bar with a rich selection of alcohol and cocktails, as well as the Xasteria Bar and a poolside drink shop.

Finally, a few words about the reviews that leave about staying at the Grecian Park Hotel 5 * (Protaras). If you look at the comments of travelers who have already been here, you can see that the hotel is really as good as its description.
