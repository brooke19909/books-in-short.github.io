Children's Health Camp "Sunrise" (Anapa): reviews, photos

As summer approaches, more and more parents think about the relaxation and health of their children. After a busy academic year, I want so much that my son or daughter relax and immerse themselves in an atmosphere of fun and joy. Children's health camps can help. There are a lot of them in our country. Especially kids and teenagers are interested in institutions located on the sea coast.





Bathing conditions

Camp "Sunrise" (Anapa) has its own sandy beach. The clean air of the Krasnodar Territory, as well as the mild and warm climate will provide a wonderful holiday for your children. In Anapa, the weather is mostly sunny in summer. You can swim in May. The hot summer is tempered by fresh sea breezes. And wooded hills prevent the air from being too dry.

Anapa has unique beaches - they are covered with golden fine sand. The entrance to the water is smooth and gentle. Near the coast, the sea is shallow, so it warms up well. Sandy beaches and warm coastal shallow water make this region ideal for children.





Accommodation and meals

Camp "Sunrise" (Anapa) welcomes guests aged seven to fifteen years. They will live in one-story buildings. The rooms accommodate several people. There is one multi-story building in the camp; children of primary school age live in it. There are no amenities in the rooms - they are on the floor. Washbasins, showers and toilets are in good condition. The rooms and the dining room are renovated.





Camp "Sunrise" (Anapa) is distinguished by good nutrition: children are fed five times a day. The menu has fresh vegetables and fruits, children are given meat and fish, they prepare the first dishes - soups and borsch. Often tasty pastries. The last two meals (afternoon snack and dream book) include a light meal. Children are given cookies with kefir or yogurt, as well as fresh fruits. Breakfast, lunch and dinner fully satisfy the nutrient requirements.

Institutional Infrastructure

Camp "Sunrise" (Anapa) has a large territory, decorated with shady trees. It is nice to relax in the summer heat. In the camp are located:

  • stadium;
  • areas for sports;
  • playgrounds;
  • indoor cinema;
  • places for discos;
  • cozy arbors.

There are flower beds, as well as tables for drawing and sculpting. The camp has a creative workshop. Here experienced teachers conduct master classes. There are also interest groups in the camp.



Camp "Sunrise" (Anapa), whose photo shows a cozy corner of the Black Sea coast, is famous for its beach. It is equipped with its own rescue service. There are sunshades, umbrellas and changing cabins on the beach. Coastal shallow water is safe even for babies who cannot swim. The beach is close to the camp - at a distance of about a hundred meters. Rescuers are on duty all the time. The beach is separated from the city center by sand dunes covered with rare greenery. Therefore, car exhaust and noise will not interfere with rest.

What to do

Children's Health Camp "Sunrise" (Anapa) offers its young guests daily fun activities. Sports are held every shift. Units compete in the ability to dance, sing and make a wall newspaper. All children participate in the sports game "Zarnitsa", as well as in the sports contest "Strong, agile, courageous."

Every evening, discos are held at the camp. Concerts are held at the dance floors by children and counselors. Not only hobby groups work in the camp, but also sports sections. Often in the evenings, guys watch movies in the indoor cinema. At a high level in the camp is medical care. There is an insulator on the territory. All children undergo a medical examination immediately after arrival. Children often go on excursions - in Anapa there are many interesting places, for example, a dolphinarium, an archaeological museum, an ostrich farm and the Golden Beach water park.





Paradise for water lovers

There are various sights in the city. But children usually like the water park the most. It is in the open. Several pools surround the structure of numerous water slides - there are more than twenty of them in the water park. Separately, there is a real children's town. In a special reservoir, the effect of waves of different sizes is created. But the main focus of the water park are slides. Every lover of water entertainment will find here a design to their taste. There are slides straight, spiral, extreme and comfortable. All of them are safe. No one will be bored at the water park! Children especially like the Black Hole slide. It completely absorbs light and seems completely dark. This slide is classified as extreme. Kids will be pleased with a children's town with low, gentle slides and a plunge pool.



Events organized by the teaching staff

Recreation camp "Sunrise" (Anapa) children visit with pleasure. The staff provides not only the safety, care and entertainment of the children, but also a strict daily routine. Children go to the sea twice - after breakfast and lunch. They bathe only in warm and sunny weather. When it is cloudy and cool outside, the guys hold concerts, organize competitions and watch movies. On the beach, children not only swim and sunbathe, they also play outdoor games, such as volleyball. If the weather is too hot, the children are in the shade, under awnings and awnings.





Children's Camp "Sunrise" (Anapa) has on its territory a library with a large book fund. Counselors organize joint events for children. For example, the holiday of the opening of the camp shift promotes the acquaintance of children. And in small Olympic competitions, the guys compete not only in outdoor games, but also in board games (checkers, chess, dominoes).

Guests often come to the camp: musicians, actors, circus performers. Children enjoy concerts and performances. Original project - thematic events. Each shift has days of sport, business, surprises and much more.





The sunny city of Anapa, the camp "Sunrise" - the pearl of this beautiful resort. A professional teaching staff works here. Counselors are students of specialized institutes and universities. The leaders of the circles are experienced teachers, experts in their field, who worked for many years in the palaces of children's creativity. In addition, the institution has a methodologist, physical education instructor and other specialists, as well as qualified medical personnel.





Reviews and comments

This is the camp "Sunrise" (Anapa). The reviews of the children who visited the institution are full of enthusiasm. Especially children enjoy the sea. The guys respond very well about the teaching staff - counselors become real friends for them. At the beginning of the shift, children quickly get to know each other and begin to communicate, time flies by unnoticed. And at the end of the shift, new friends dream of returning to Voskhod again. There is a great merit of the teaching staff.

The pearl of the Krasnodar Territory is beautiful - the city of Anapa. Camp "Sunrise", reviews of which celebrate a cheerful and vibrant vacation, is located almost in the center, on Pioneer Avenue. Of course, not everything in this institution is perfect. Some visitors note that the sea is dirty and covered with mud. In addition, many are scared by the abundance of jellyfish. The guys write that in the evenings mosquitoes fly in the camp. And in the afternoon flies often annoy.





Sometimes the guys are not happy that in multi-bed rooms there is only one outlet. The institution was built in Soviet times. There were no mobile phones then, it was believed that sockets were not needed. Today, the inability to charge a mobile phone can be a problem. At the same time, as visitors write, there were no interruptions in cold and hot water.

Are cooks good or bad?

Children camp "Sunrise" (Anapa), reviews of which note the friendly attitude of the staff, guests characterize on the positive side. But the guys do not always like food. Many complain that they have to eat fast food from the store, because the dishes in the dining room are not always well prepared. However, this is a matter of individual addiction. For many young guests, the food did not leave unpleasant memories.





The camp has a very strict discipline. For smoking, teenagers are expelled from the squad. Older guys write that the sea is shallow, waist-high. But for kids it fits perfectly. Some children who love a relaxed lifestyle are not happy with the abundance of daily activities. But these are isolated opinions, there are few such reviews.
