Balashov hotels: an overview of the best options

Balashov is one of the largest city in the Saratov region. There are many flowering alleys and wonderful parks, a well-developed cultural sphere (there are theaters, cinema centers, museums). Also, the city administration is trying to provide residents and guests of Balashov with all the conditions for a comfortable stay: they support the work of old factories, open a large number of shopping and entertainment centers, cafes and restaurants, as well as hotels that can comfortably accommodate tourists from other cities. The main thing to remember, even if you arrived on a business trip for a couple of days, you need to book a room in advance. The article will provide a brief overview of the best hotels in Balashov (Saratov region).

Hotel "Comfort"

The first option that we want to draw your attention to is the Uyut Hotel. A wonderful place where you can live a couple of days, enjoying the tranquility and comfort. The reception is open around the clock, the girl administrator is always ready to help you. Near the hotel there is a parking designed for a large number of cars. The guard carefully monitors the cars, not allowing suspicious persons.

The hotel is located in one of the central parts of the city. In walking distance are: a large vegetable market, cafes and restaurants, nightly entertainment. The hotel has a hairdresser, stalls with pies and coffee, gift shops, steam rooms, a jacuzzi, a sauna and billiards. Another plus: free internet access.

All rooms are freshly renovated: they are equipped with modern appliances and furniture. Cleaning is carried out regularly. Some rooms have a small but cozy kitchen with a microwave. The cost per night is from 1,500 rubles, it all depends on the category of the room. The hotel offers a choice of 4 accommodation options: double, triple, quadruple rooms and family suites (up to 5 people). Feel free to book a room in this Balashov hotel, you will not regret it. Address: st. Kommunarov, 155.

Simple but tasteful

Hotel Balashova "Trump" is popular with most visitors. They are happy to come back here again. This is a wonderful restaurant and hotel complex. Here, with warmth and attention to each guest. Also among the advantages of the Kozyr hotel are: the location in the very center of Balashov, on the territory there is a restaurant with delicious food, breakfast (buffet) is included in the price, parking and free Internet access.

The price is consistent with the quality. For only 2,000 rubles (per day) you can rent a standard standard single room. It has all the necessary furniture and appliances. The rooms are equipped with a security and fire alarm system. Doors are closed and opened using special electronic cards.

The hotel in Balashov is a wonderful place to relax. It is located at: st. Karl Marx, 23/2.

One of the best hotels in the city of Balashov

Hotel Lexx is the best option for businessmen who come to solve important issues. After all, these people really appreciate the chic living conditions and are willing to pay decently. In addition, the hotel has the largest conference hall in the city, which is ideal for business meetings. Each room has a huge plasma TV with many channels. There is also a seating area and a modern desk.

Another plus: excellent sound insulation, this allows you to relax with maximum comfort. The rooms are mostly luxury (price per day: 3,600 rubles), but there are a couple of single standards (from 2,400 rubles a day). The hotel has a billiard room. There is a very friendly staff who are always ready to help you.

Address: st. 30 Years of Victory, 15 b. This Balashova hotel (Saratov region) is popular with locals and visitors.

Hoper

Among the hotels of Balashov there is another good option - "Khoper". The hotel received its unusual name in honor of the local river, which flows through the city. Number of rooms: 38. All of them are from the Standard category. You can rent a room for one, two people or a group of friends. Average price per day: 1,400 rubles. It is better to book in advance, otherwise there may not be any empty seats. The rooms are simply furnished, but they are always clean and tidy. Toilet and shower are shared (on the corridor).

On the ground floor there is a cafe where you can eat tasty and inexpensive. It serves the best dishes of national cuisine. The hotel area in Balashov is ideal for walking. Its main decoration is fountains ("King of the Seas" and "River Nymph"). Address: st. Karl Marx, 43
