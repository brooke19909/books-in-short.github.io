Do-it-yourself backpack for a doll: glue, sew, knit

Most girls love to play with dolls. And each seeks to provide his darling with everything necessary. And sometimes not only. However, in this regard, the girl or her mother has a lot of questions. For example, how to make a backpack for a doll with your own hands? After all, a purchased item does not always satisfy the requirements. In addition, every girl wants her doll to be the most beautiful, unique.

Therefore, we offer the reader a simple and understandable guide that will help you craft an interesting backpack in a few minutes.

Elementary model

It‚Äôs good when mom is a creative person who has an almost limitless imagination and knows how to sew, knit and cross-stitch. And if she can barely hold a needle or a needle in her hands?

In this case, we propose to make a backpack for a doll from a matchbox with our own hands. To do this, you must:

  • stationery glue;
  • scissors;
  • colored paper;
  • satin ribbon;
  • various spangles, beads, stickers and other decorative elements;
  • Matchbox.

When all that is required is at hand, you can begin to work:

  1. Take out the inner box.
  2. Tape the base with colored paper.
  3. Cut out the valve.
  4. Glue webbing straps.
  5. Decorate the product as you wish.

Simple fabric option

If mother knows how to sew - well, she can make a backpack for a doll made of fabric with her own hands. For this, you can use any flap. The main thing is to take into account the size of the pet. And the material to choose is dense - drape, jeans and the like. You will also need:

  • scissors;
  • a simple pencil;
  • needle with thread in the color of fabric or contrast;
  • stencil album sheet - if necessary.

Have you prepared? Then you can proceed! The principle of action is quite simple:

  • Draw a wide strip on paper or directly onto the fabric.
  • Cut out.
  • Transfer to flap or immediately sew the ends.
  • Place the structure on the fabric, form an oval and mark 4 points, as shown in the photo below.
  • Connect by drawing an oval.
  • Now sew the bottom to the base.
  • Add flap, webbing and button closure.

Knitted doll backpack

With your own hands, you can create many variations of the studied product. However, if you know how to crochet, you can perform another original model. To do this, you need:

  • Knitting thread;
  • hook;
  • needle with thread.

The idea is implemented as follows:

  • Tie a chain of 4 loops.
  • Lock in the ring, connecting the first and last.
  • Then move in a circle. At the same time, in each row 2 times, knit out 3 loops 3, strictly on the same line, through an equal number of loops. As shown in the photo below.
  • Having tied the bottom of the desired size, lift the walls. To do this, just knit in a circle without increases.
  • Tear the thread and tie another oval detail - the valve. Sew to the base.
  • Finish the straps and clasp.
