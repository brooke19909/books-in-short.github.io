Sumela Garden Hotel 3 * (Turkey, Kemer): description, reviews

Turkey is an incredibly popular tourist destination. This country annually attracts millions of our compatriots. And this is not surprising, because in Turkey they are waiting for a great inexpensive vacation. There are several large cities (Istanbul, Izmir, Ankara) and many small towns and villages (Kemer, Antalya, Beldibi), where you can relax and have a lot to see.





Beldibi is a small resort village on the outskirts of Antalya, located on the shores of the Mediterranean Sea. In recent years, the village began to develop rapidly, there appeared a lot of hotels with pools, slides, children's and sports grounds, as well as various parks, shopping complexes and much more, which is necessary for a pleasant vacation and pastime.

The village is very beautiful, surrounded by greenery, surrounded by mountains with coniferous forests. The streets are filled with a variety of shops with numerous goods for every taste and restaurants where you can taste the national dishes of Turkish cuisine.

The climate here is Mediterranean, soft and pleasant. The swimming season begins in May and lasts through October.

The village is not very youthful, more suitable for a vacation with the whole family.

There are a total of 64 hotels in Beldibi. One of which will be discussed in the review presented below.

Sumela Garden Hotel (Turkey)

A nice small 3 * beach hotel, which is ideal for an economical and quiet holiday. It has everything that is needed to make the vacation go well and be remembered for a long time. The hotel has a small green area, which fit children's and adult pools, a playground, a cozy amphitheater. The hotel is located on the first coastline, the entrance to the sea is pebbles. Among the employees there is a Russian-speaking staff, the animation is in Russian.





Location

Sumela Garden Hotel 3 * is located ten kilometers from the resort center of Turkey in Kemer, in a village called Beldibi, on the first coastline. From the hotel to the airport about 45 kilometers. The journey takes about an hour.
