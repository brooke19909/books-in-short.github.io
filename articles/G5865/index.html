Superb stay at Grand Plaza Resort (Hurghada)

If you have not yet decided where to stay, having gone on vacation to the city of Hurghada, Grand Plaza Resort will be an excellent place for economical living. The hotel has a four-star category and is located on the second coastline. Across the street from it is the Grand Plaza Hotel, the free services of which are available to all guests at the Grand Plaza Resort (excluding restaurants). The hotel opened in 2008. It includes in its complex the main one-story building where the reception is located, and a number of two-story and three-story buildings decorated in a traditional Arabic style. The hotel grounds are well-groomed, with numerous green spaces. A distance of 2.5 kilometers separates it from the airport, and in order to get to the city center, it is necessary to overcome a distance of 5.5 kilometers. The hotel is a great place to relax for both young people and families with children.



Rooms

The Grand Plaza Resort (Hurghada) offers its guests 199 standard rooms, each area is 36 square meters. m. Up to three people are allowed in one. The rooms are quite comfortable, the floor is covered with ceramic tiles. There is a bathroom, hairdryer, individual air conditioning, satellite TV, telephone. There is no safe in the room, if you need to store valuables, you can use the safe installed at the reception. There is a bar, which is filled at an additional cost, a kettle. Each room has a balcony or access to the terrace. A cot is available upon prior reservation.



Nutrition

To its guests "Grand Plaza Resort" (Hurghada) provides AI (all-inclusive) meals, served in the hotel‚Äôs main restaurant. Upon reservation, breakfast can be served in the room. In addition, the hotel has another restaurant and 3 bars with soft drinks and light snacks. For small guests are provided highchairs.

Beach

The beach is sandy, located near the Grand Plaza Resort. There are corals, so it is recommended to wear special shoes for swimming. Beach equipment is provided free of charge.

Additional Information

On the territory of the "Grand Plaza Resort" (Hurghada) there are all conditions for a great vacation. There is an outdoor pool, divided into three parts and having a gentle entrance. One of the parts is heated in the winter. For younger guests, the pool is built separately. Set 3 water slides. A number of services are provided for guests. So, for an additional fee, you can use the services of laundry, dry cleaning, a doctor, order a taxi or exchange money.



The hotel "Grand Plaza Resort" (Hurghada) provides its guests with a number of various entertainments, including sports. So, while relaxing in this hotel, you can do aerobics, water aerobics, volleyball, play darts or table football. On request, you can visit the gym. In addition, animated shows are regularly held. There are a number of activities for an additional fee, including billiards, tennis, windsurfing, banana riding, diving, camel riding. After an active day, a steam room or a sauna will help you relax before a disco, you can use the services of a professional masseur. The hotel management did not forget about the little guests. A special playground for them is reserved for them, a kids club is working, and babysitting services are provided . In the evening, all vacationers gather at a disco.
