Probably, all needlewomen who knit, like openwork patterns: airy, light, beautiful. Openwork knitting is used not only to create shawls, stoles and scarves, but also cardigans, jumpers, dresses. The products are very light, refined, and perfectly show the skill of the knitter.

Yarn and tools for openwork patterns

You always need to know where knitting starts. Pattern "Leaves" is no exception. It starts with a selection of yarn and knitting needles. The most beautiful openwork patterns look in a thin cloth, that is, knitted from fine yarn. Summer items can be knitted from cotton or linen yarn. Products are light and not ‚Äúhot‚Äù. The lace looks very beautiful and spectacular from silk thread, the canvas acquires a light noble shine. From yarn with the addition of silk, you can associate a product that belongs to an evening group of dresses, for example, a black openwork bolero for an evening dress or a low-necked top for a female tuxedo.

In winter and autumn, the pattern with knitting needles ‚ÄúLeaves‚Äù (the scheme to which will be described later) is best done from woolen and semi-woolen yarn. It is warm and, despite the lace, will warm you. For such patterns, mohair is perfect. This is a yarn of goat hair, very soft, fluffy, airy. There is no pure mohair yarn, so it always comes in a mixed composition, for example, with acrylic or silk. From mohair beautiful shawls, stoles, dresses with fluffy skirts are obtained.

Knitting needles for knitting openwork patterns suitable for yarn. The manufacturer always writes the recommended number on the label. But if you want a lighter and more airy canvas, take one or two more knitting needles. Be sure to tie a sample to know how the product will behave during wear and after wet-heat treatment.





The principle of knitting "Leaves"

Patterns for knitting with leaflets are obtained on the canvas by alternating yarn and two or three loops knitted together with a slope in one direction or another. First comes the expansion of the picture, then the narrowing. It is usually symmetrical about the middle. The pattern rapport is usually 10 to 15 rows high. That is how many of them will be needed to knit out a full leaf.

Knitting pattern ‚ÄúLeaves‚Äù with a description and patterns you will see below. Read the description carefully and everything will work out! Do not tighten the loops too much so that the ornament does not deform, but also do not knit too loosely so that the product does not lose shape during wear.

What to bind?

Now that your collection has been replenished with schemes with leaflets, the question remains: what to do using these patterns for knitting?

Most often, these openwork ornaments are used in knitting stoles or scarves. Due to the fact that the pattern goes from top to bottom, it is more suitable for elongated products. A knee-length dress will also look very beautiful. A vertical drawing will visually stretch the figure, which means to slim. For a long summer skirt, the pattern is also well suited. It will turn out a thing in a peasant style, which becomes relevant every summer.
