The beautiful island of Mallorca. Tourist tip - best recommendation!

Mallorca is the pearl of the Mediterranean and the largest of the islands of the Balearic archipelago. Here you will be greeted by a mild favorable climate and picturesque landscapes. If you want to spend an unforgettable vacation surrounded by majestic castles surrounded by lush greenery, turquoise bays, hot white sand and fine cuisine, then you will definitely like Mallorca, a review of which you can leave immediately after your arrival in the travel company that offered you this island as a vacation spot.





The capital of Mallorca is the cultural center of the island

The capital of the island is Palma de Mallorca, combining two different lifestyles: modern urban and provincial, rustic. The population of the city is more than 50% of the inhabitants of all of Mallorca. Here are the main historical monuments made in the Gothic style, which are the Almudain Palace, Belver Castle, the Cathedral. The Museum of Mallorca has exhibits - the richest heritage of the island in the field of archeology. There is also an old quarter in Palm, in which there are aristocratic courtyards of Can Vivot, churches of St. Francis, Santa Eulalia, as well as the Arab Baths.





Things to do in Mallorca

The geographical position and climate of Mallorca allows it to be a great place for water sports such as sailing, scuba diving, windsurfing, fishing and water skiing, which are very popular among tourists visiting the island and the local population. And all this Mallorca offers! Feedback from any tourist about this beautiful island will be the best confirmation of this. In addition, you can visit yacht clubs, casinos, golf courses, water parks, museums, racecourses, theaters and national parks.

Transport

Buses are the main form of transport on the island. Their routes connect the capital with other resort centers. The fare is approximately 1 euro. There are also 2 train directions on the island. The cost of travel in this form of transport can be from 3 to 6 euros. It must be remembered that at night all traffic stops. Another way to get around is to rent a car. But this is not a very convenient option, given that in Mallorca a large number of people have cars in their property, so it can be very difficult to find a parking place.
