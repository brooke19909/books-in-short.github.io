The hospitable banks of the Neva

It's no secret that St. Petersburg is a magnificent city that is hard not to fall in love with. In his history of more than 300 years, he managed to visit the capital of the Russian Empire and gain the glory of one of the most beautiful places in his country. Here you can stroll along the embankments of the majestic Neva River, which is the main waterway of the city, and ride it on trams or boats. It will take more than one week to see all the sights of this city of Peter's times, because there are a lot of them. It will take a lot of time to study the Hermitage, whose expositions are presented in numerous halls (their number exceeds 350).

There is a very large number of various architectural monuments, some of which are true masterpieces of architectural thought. All kinds of temples, mansions and palaces appeared here thanks to the best craftsmen who came here from different parts of the world.





Many tourists come to St. Petersburg to enjoy the amazing natural phenomenon called the White Nights and watch how the bridges over the Neva River are being built.
