Tarusa Hotels: addresses, descriptions, reviews

Tarusa is a city that is the administrative center of the Kaluga Region, located at the confluence of the Tarusa River into the Oka River. The first mention of settlements along the Tarusa riverbed dates back to the middle of the 13th century. But the city of Tarusa gained great fame in the XIV-XV centuries, during the battles against the Horde yoke and the attacks of the Lithuanians, when the troops of the Tarusian princes were part of the Russian army.

The modern history of the city

A small town with a population of ten thousand due to the lack of a railway and industrial enterprises seemed to freeze at the crossroads of the 19th and 20th centuries. The untouched nature, patriarchal silence and measured life of the city attracted many figures of culture and art. At different times, the Polenovs, Tsvetaevs, Vatagin and Vinogradovs came and lived in Tarusa. Such outstanding personalities as Roerich, Paustovsky, Brodsky, Ginzburg and Solzhenitsyn lived and worked in the city. To receive famous guests and travelers began to build cottages, inns and hotels. Tarusa has become a favorite vacation spot for people of art.

Reviews

At any time of the year and in any weather, travelers and vacationers will be welcomed by hotels in Tarusa. Guests unanimously note the decent service and courtesy of the staff. Picturesque nature is conducive to a relaxing holiday. Almost all hotels organize fishing, it is magnificent on the Oka River, in the Tarusa region. Guests of the city will enjoy cycling and horse riding, which are also offered. Vacationers note a diverse range of services for all age categories: from older people to restless kids. For lovers of not only an idle holiday, there is the opportunity to visit numerous historical sites. Museums of outstanding artists and writers, for whom has become the favorite city of Tarusa. Hotels prices are quite affordable, so travel and leisure do not overshadow.
