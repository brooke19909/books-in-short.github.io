Resort Location

The name of the Costa Brava northeast coast of Catalonia received at the dawn of the XX century. The journalist Ferrand Agulio-i-Vidal with such an expression figuratively entitled a local history article about these places - rocky, wild and beautiful. Until the middle of the last century, when the general attractiveness of Spain was marked by Caissillo of Spain by Generalissimo Franco, the unhurried life of the Spanish heartland continued on the coast. The first tourist invasion of a Mediterranean resort by holidaymakers from Great Britain and Western Germany came in the 70s. In the coastal strip began the rapid development of leisure infrastructure. In 1972, in the town of Lloret de Mar, the Gran hotel Don Juan 3 was built. Spain sought to take a place along with the main tourist destinations in the world.

The city of Lloret de Mar is famous for its ecology. There is not a single industrial enterprise in it, and even the railway passes far from the resort streets. The surrounding Navarre Mountains prevent the penetration of winds into the coastal zone, and this affects the formation of a mild, warm climate in the resort enclave. The development of tourism in the last 70 years has become the main direction of business development in Lloret de Mar. Hotel complexes, vineyards appeared on the site of aristocratic mansions, and the fields gave way to amusement and gaming establishments. Hotel Don Juan 3 * is one of hundreds scattered in picturesque neighborhoods, among souvenir shops, shops and restaurants located on narrow winding streets.

Hotel





The hotel complex is located in three buildings (6-7 floors). The reconstruction of buildings carried out in 2014 improved the internal conveniences and comfort of the Don Juan Lloret 3 hotel (Spain). Guest reviews indicate full compliance of the complex with the level of 3 stars. 850 rooms of the standard double room category (16 m 2 ) and 14 single rooms (14 m 2 ) at the height of the tourist season accept up to 3 thousand guests. In addition, 9 well-appointed rooms are designed for people with disabilities. Conveniences and furnishings comply with generally accepted standards of hotel services:

  • Sleeping places. Linen is changed twice a week, and towels are changed daily.
  • Central air conditioning.
  • Bath or shower.
  • Heating.
  • Telephone and satellite TV.
  • Balcony.
  • For tourists with children, a cot is provided for free in the room.

The complex operates a paid access to the Internet in a wired and wireless (wi-fi) connection configuration. In the room, at the request of residents, the installation of a safe is provided.

It offers guests - 2 outdoor fresh water pools and a heated indoor pool, designed for use from October to May. For business meetings, you can rent one of three conference rooms. There is a reading room for guests, car rental, a lounge for relaxation, a television room and a shopping gallery. For a fee, the Don Juan 3 * hotel offers guests to use the parking lot and qualified medical assistance, as well as massage services.

Hotel buildings are interconnected by internal passages, which increases the convenience in using the services provided at the hotel.

Sea and beach





The story of the Catalan beaches is a separate topic in the description of holidays in Don Juan 3. Costa Brava is famous for its clean coastal recreation areas and crystal-pearl water. The coastline of the central city beach of Lloret de Mar is 1,500 meters. Locals tell legends about the caravans of ships of the ancient Romans and Iberians, who stopped on the hospitable shore to enjoy the peace and quiet of these places. The equipment of the city beach corresponds to the prevailing ideas about the organization of sea holidays: rental of sun loungers and umbrellas, catamarans and yachts, fast water skiing and diving. At the height of the high tourist season, the beach is noisy and crowded, the Costa Brava is a familiar holiday destination for Spanish and European youth.

For older people, another beach is better - Fenals, which is not visited by noisy youth companies. It is surrounded by cliffs overgrown with pine trees, which provide protection from the wind. The sea, like the rest of the coast, is clean. Entrance to the water on a 700-meter stretch of Fenals Beach is steep, without shallow water. Deep water begins already 2-3 meters after the coastline. There is also a pier for sightseeing boats that run between the nearest cities.

For parents and children, Sa Boadella beach is preferable - 250 meters of coastline surrounded by pristine forests. There are no noisy companies and dangerous entertainment. A gentle entry into clean water allows parents not to worry about children frolic in shallow water. Some minus can be considered the presence on the outskirts of the beach of nudists who have chosen a secluded and picturesque stretch of coast.

A distance of 400 meters separating the Don Juan Lloret 3 (Costa Brava) hotel from the nearest beach, vacationers walk along the picturesque shady street of medieval Lloret de Mar among souvenir shops, bars and small shops selling goods necessary for a vacation.

sights





Of interest is the history of the coast of Catalonia and the city of Lloret de Mar, in which the Don Juan 3 hotel is located. Costa Brava was inhabited in ancient times by Iberians, who appeared in these parts from nowhere. The history of the city‚Äôs origin is still unclear and covered in a veil of secrecy. For the first time the name Lloret was mentioned in chronicles dated 966. Until the XVIII century, Lloret de Mar lived in the rank of a secondary coastal settlement. In 1778, after a royal decree allowing free trade, the inhabitants of the city established shipping routes from America to Europe, and later succeeded in shipbuilding. At this time, rich mansions appeared, destroyed during the restructuring of the city in the XX century. But the tourism infrastructure did not absorb the millennial architectural and cultural heritage of the proud Catalans.

The postcard image of the city of Lloret de Mar - built in 1509-1522, the Church of St. Roman, located a few blocks from the Don Juan 3. Hotel guests always focus on visiting this monument of Catalan Gothic art.

From the windows of the hotel complex you can see another attraction of the city - a chapel in the Romanesque style de les Alegres, built in the XI century. In its original form, only the bell tower has survived to our time.

The charm of small cities with an ancient history is that during a walk a tourist plunges into the atmosphere of genuine antiquity. Walking along the narrow streets of Lloret de Mar, it is difficult to walk past the Can Zaragoza - the oldest building in the city. Formerly the home of the poor (built in 1317), it was later rebuilt into a luxurious summer residence. Today, an exposition telling about the history of the Iberian people is demonstrated here.

Active life





Architectural monuments are not the only thing that can interest the guests of the Don Juan 3 * hotel in Lloret de Mar. A variety of entertainments and excursions will satisfy any claims of vacationers.

  1. The Botanical Garden of Pigna de Rosa. It was founded in 1945. The collection includes 7 thousand species of plants. The collection of prickly pears (the cactus family) unites 600 species - this is a unique, best in the world collection. Visiting Pigna de Rosa from February to October, tourists become witnesses to the pore of flowering cacti.
  2. WaterWorld Waterpark. He opened the door to the first visitors in 1985. WaterWorld was a pioneer in the field of water parks in Spain. 15 attractions are located on 140 thousand m2, some of which are not represented in other water parks on the Mediterranean coast of Spain.
  3. A 15-minute drive from Lloret de Mar is another water park - Marineland. In addition to water activities, it has a dolphinarium, which hosts performances of trained sea lions and dolphins.
  4. For lovers of fishing, trips to sea or river fishing are organized.

Da Paolo is a culinary island of Italy, which has found shelter on the Spanish coast of the Mediterranean Sea. Traditional pasta, pizza and truffles.
