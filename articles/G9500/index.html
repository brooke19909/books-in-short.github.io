What it is?

Call forwarding is a service with which you can transfer incoming calls from the old to the new number. This service can be connected to any mobile phone using its menu. The offer is presented in several versions. Widely used by subscribers of Megafon, Beeline, Tele2, MTS forwarding to another number is unconditional. This means that any incoming call is redirected to the number of your choice. The service is valid even when the phone is off - just change the old SIM card. This offer is free for company customers. If you are within the home network, call forwarding is charged as outgoing calls at your rates.

Call forwarding and SMS in "Tele-2"

This company has its own classification of types of forwarding.
