"White Nights" - a hotel in St. Petersburg. Description and feedback

White Nights is a hotel in St. Petersburg that offers rooms of various categories for visitors with any budget. It is considered the oldest hotel in the city. Currently has 3 stars. Number of rooms

The best option for those who need an inexpensive hotel is White Nights. The hotel has 350 rooms designed for one, two or three guests. Several accommodation options are available:

  1. Room of the category "Economy". The approximate cost is 1,500 rubles. These are small rooms with two single beds, in which there is no modern repair and additional furniture. Everything is quite simple. Ideal for tourists who need a room just to spend the night and take a shower.
  2. Double room with one large or two small beds. The price is 2000 rubles. The rooms of this category have their own bathroom, as well as the necessary equipment and objects, for example, iron, ironing board and TV.
  3. Category "standard". Price - 2100. The room is improved, but the arrangement - as the previous option.
  4. Junior Suite The cost of living is 2550 rubles. In addition, the rooms in this category have additional furniture and a bathtub.
  5. "Classic family." The price is 2700 rubles. These are 36 square meters rooms with a double bed and a sofa bed as an extra bed.

White Nights (hotel): services for guests

The hotel area is guarded around the clock, as is the parking lot. Valuables can be left in the safe, which is in the room, or at the administrator. There is a telephone for guests if you need to make a long-distance or international call.

Those who are tired of wandering around beautiful St. Petersburg all day can relax in the massage room. There is also a jacuzzi and a sauna. If you plan to attend any event, the doors of the beauty salon are always open.

There is a tourist and tour desk, a taxi call is possible. In the evening, guests are invited to play billiards. For business customers, a conference room with the necessary equipment is equipped.





Nutrition

White Nights is a hotel that has a bar and cafe for 100 people. When paying for a room, a person receives a free breakfast, which is served every morning. It is called "continental." And for a serious event, it is proposed to rent a banquet hall.
