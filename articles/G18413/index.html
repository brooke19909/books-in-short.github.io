LCD "Argo": features of the residential complex

RC "Argo" consists of three sectional towers of a business class 26 floors high, 2 of which are reserved for technical rooms. The development project is carried out at the address: Samara, rn Oktyabrsky, ul. Novo-Sadovaya, a 5-minute walk to the Mega City shopping center. The company responsible for the sale of apartments in the real estate market is Business Garant.

Features of the construction of the residential complex "Argo" on Novo-Sadovaya

The technology for the construction of residential buildings is monolithic, the external decoration is performed using elements of the Leningrad NPP system.

Options with a typical layout are available for sale in the Argo residential complex: studio apartments, one-, two- and three-room apartments ranging in size from 30 to 99 m 2 . The level of housing prices is formed depending on the chosen floor - the higher the apartment is located, the higher the cost of 1 m 2 .





Apartments with completed finishing work are being commissioned. The layout of the Argo residential complex is rational - all apartments are clearly zoned, and separately divided apartments allow you to create an exclusive interior design in each room.

The windows of the residential complex offer spectacular views of the landscapes surrounding the neighborhood: the Volga River, Novo-Sadovaya Street, temples, the Botanical Garden. With this in mind, the developer included panoramic windows and balconies / loggias in the project in order to give residents the full sensation while enjoying the morning sunrise and evening sunset.
