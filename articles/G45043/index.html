General characteristics of the resort Jermuk

Jermuk is a unique environmentally friendly resort. It is located in the southeast of Armenia, 170 km from Yerevan (RA capital). This town is located high in the mountains (2080 m above sea level) in the upper reaches of the Arpa River, which divides it into two parts. From all sides Jermuk is surrounded by mountain ranges, on the slopes of which coniferous and mixed forests grow. They protect it from strong winds and bad weather, and also contribute to the formation of their own microclimate.
