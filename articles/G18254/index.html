Thermal springs Balfa

The Alps, Lake Ferte, the proximity of Sopron (7 km) and Vienna (75 km) and numerous healing springs - these are the main advantages of the Hungarian resort of Balf. The local thermal waters were known during the time of the Roman Empire. True, at that time they belonged to Sopron. An interesting fact is that it was the first bathhouse in Hungary, the entrance to which was paid.
