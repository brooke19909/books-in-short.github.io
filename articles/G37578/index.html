"Morozovka", boarding house of Gazprom: reviews

Morozovka is a boarding house of RAO Gazprom. The administration of the institution offers visitors a vacation in the suburbs, in one of the most beautiful places on the territory of an old noble estate. The guesthouse, surrounded by relict forests, is located on the banks of the Klyazma. Currently, there are one hundred and sixty varieties of shrubs and trees in the park area.
