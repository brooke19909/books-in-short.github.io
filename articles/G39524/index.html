Best mini-hotels, Anapa: description and reviews of tourists

When choosing options for living, many lose a lot of time. All hotels located in Anapa have an excellent reputation. Thanks to this article, you can easily choose a hotel where you have to live a certain time. If you need to select elite housing, then you need to pay attention to the institutions described below.

Among the hotels reviewed there are no those that people would not like. All reviews about them are as positive as possible, and practically no hotel has any minuses. Moreover, they are all budgetary and will not require a particularly large amount.

"Palanga"

Popular among tourists are mini-hotels. Anapa is one of the most famous places for visitors. "Palanga" is widespread, and all because of its good location. Near it there are shops, parks, a market, places for entertainment. The beaches, which are located very close, are covered with sand and pebbles.

The hotel has a developed infrastructure. On its territory there is a sauna, pools, bars, cafes. There is a laundry service, there is a telephone connection. Internet is available throughout the hotel.

The pool area is conveniently equipped for guests. Here are the sunbeds. You can order a cocktail or coffee. If you wish, you can arrange a romantic dinner in this room. In winter, the water in the pool is slightly warmed up. Saunas are open around the clock. There is Finnish and Russian.



Sunmarin

Like other Krasnodar mini-hotels (Anapa), "Sanmarin" is located on the Black Sea coast. There are clean sandy beaches and gentle sun. It offers comfortable rooms with TVs and balconies. The guests eat on the buffet system. This hotel has the largest Anapa pool.

There is a restaurant for meals. Its design is quite simple, the dominant colors are warm and not straining the eye. Meals are carried out three times a day. The assortment of dishes is wide, among them there are both traditional and European. For dinner, guests are offered a glass of Kuban wine.

The beach where guests rest is the property of Sanmarinna. The coastline is 400 meters from the building.



Resort

Few people do not know Anapa. "Resort" is a mini-hotel that is known to all tourists. On the territory of the institution there is a bar, pool, etc.

The rooms where guests are accommodated are equipped with a large number of appliances. There is an iron, a kettle, a TV, and air conditioning. Plumbing is all new, there will be no problems with the bathroom. The beds are excellent, sofas and beds have a soft base. The hotel is quiet enough; nobody will disturb the peace of guests. The photo shows how cozy and comfortable it is to be in this institution.



ViTa

ViTa, like other mini-hotels (Anapa), is located in the city center. This place is quite popular among guests, as well as among indigenous people. The building is located very close to the beach, the zoo and other beautiful places of nature.

Hotel guests, settling in the institution, fall into the courtyard with a beautiful design. The main element of the exterior is the fountain. A good mood will give tables placed on a fresh vacation. Free Wi-Fi is available on the territory of ViTa.

The sauna is open from 7am to 1am. It has a swimming pool, steam room and relaxation area. Staff are available around the clock. The service is at a high level. Guests are accommodated in homes. Room categories range from economy to suites.



Ritsa

Mini-hotels are in great demand. Anapa is a resort city that brings large funds to the country's budget. Hotel "Ritsa" is located in the park, 10 minutes. from the market and 10 min. from the beach.

The hotel has been operating since 2015. The interior and furniture of the rooms really impresses the guests. A store located on the territory of Ritsa serves people around the clock. There is a pharmacy and a sauna.

The rooms that accommodate visitors are designed for a maximum of 4 people. All amenities are located in the rooms. The bathroom has soap, a towel and a hairdryer. Satellite TV, air conditioning and refrigerator.

Meals are provided in the dining room. It is three times a day. If there is a desire, you can refuse it and eat on your own.



Flamingo

Mini-hotel Flamingo (Anapa) is located just 9 minutes from the beach. Parking is private and free, internet is free too. There is a kitchen in every room.

All rooms are made in a classic interior. Dominant colors are warm and bright. The bathroom has a shower and free toiletries.

If you wish, you can organize a small picnic. All necessary accessories are rented from the administration.



"Patio"

The hotel is located in the resort area, like other mini-hotels. Anapa, reviews of which are amazing, impressed with its Patio Hotel. At 300 m from it there is a beach and a water park, at 100 m - train stations, a market.

The hotel has a fairly scenic view. There are waterfalls, a courtyard with beautiful landscaping. The staff is friendly and will always help guests.

On the ground floor there is a shop, bar, cafe, beauty salon. You will have to pay for parking. It is located just 100 meters from the hotel. By the way, there is a shopping center opposite it, a little further - a cinema, an amusement park.

Accommodation is provided in standard, deluxe rooms. They have a hairdryer, internet, refrigerator, air conditioning.

Hayat

Hayat is a guest house located in the center of Anapa. The market is only 10 minutes. walk to the beach - 15 min. On the territory of the building there is a parking and a pool. Such services are provided by almost all mini-hotels (Anapa). The year 2016 has become quite profitable for many institutions. This is due to the migration of people from neighboring countries and republics.

The hotel has at its disposal 30 rooms. There are only two categories: standard and luxury. All rooms have a balcony, TV, refrigerator, split system.

You will have to eat on your own, for these purposes a kitchen is specially equipped. There is a restaurant near the hotel if someone is not happy with the prospect of cooking.

"Diana"

Considering accommodation options in a city such as Anapa, mini-hotels and guest houses take first place among existing complexes. Just a few minutes Diana is located from the center. Next to it is also a theater square.

The mini-hotel has rooms for 2-4 people. Each is equipped with new and comfortable interior items. Among the appliances here are a TV, air conditioning, refrigerator. The bathroom is in the room.

On the ground floor of the complex there is a hall where there is a table, corner, plasma, mini-bar. The latter is filled with various drinks, from alcoholic to non-alcoholic.
