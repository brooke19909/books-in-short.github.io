Metropolis

And now let's try to plunge into the atmosphere of Sharm el-Sheikh. It is located in the coastal zone of the Red Sea - it is an Egyptian resort. The South Sinai Governorate owns several district centers, one of which is Sharm.

During the years of the capture of Sinai by the Israelis (1967-1982), the city was called Ophira, but in general it has been known since the formation of the Ottoman kingdom. It was a small village, but due to its convenient location, excellent climate, chic natural world, rapid development, the metropolis is becoming increasingly popular among tourists.

In this fabulous area, Regency Plaza Aqua Park 5 * is located. It should be noted that Sharm el-Sheikh does not look like typical Egyptian cities: it resembles Mediterranean European resorts.
