How many parks are in Moscow and which are the most popular?

Moscow is a well-maintained city with a large number of parks. In total, there are 120 recreation areas that are dispersed throughout the capital. However, the question of how many parks in Moscow there is no definite answer, since many recreation areas can be classified as parks only conditionally. Some parks are very popular and attract many visitors. Among them, the Central Park of Culture. Gorky, Zaryadye, Filevsky and some other recreation areas.





How many parks in Moscow?

The city has a large number of parks of various types. However, answering the question of how many parks are in Moscow is not easy. It all depends on what to rank them. The total number of parks is dozens, and if you include small green corners of rest, they will be more than a hundred. And how many large parks in Moscow ? There are only a few such parks. Still, the city is quite densely built up, and there are not so many empty seats.





Zaryadye Park

Zaryadye Park is sure to become the most popular in the capital and one of the best parks in the world. It is also the newest park in the capital. The year of its foundation is 2017. The territory of the park is a kind of zoo, where all the main natural areas of Russia are represented, for each of which the corresponding microclimate is artificially maintained. Each zone has its own piece of territory.

The project also includes a philharmonic society under a huge dome with the highest quality acoustics. It will have a capacity designed for one and a half thousand people.

There will also be a viewing platform with a view of the center of the capital, including the Kremlin. In addition, visitors will be able to see ancient temples, unique archaeological finds dating back several centuries.

The park will be available for visiting around the clock and year-round. Estimated number of visitors is 12 million people per year.

Gorky Culture Park

The park is located in the central areas of the city, and despite this, it is a cozy corner, designed for a relaxing holiday. The park adjoins the Moscow River and visitors can use the boat, or take a walk along the quiet and green alleys.





Culture Park in Moscow is one of the oldest in the territory of the former USSR. It was formed in 1928 on the site of the famous Trubetskoy estate, near which the botanical garden was located.

The total length of the park zone is 7 kilometers along the river. The main entrance is located on the side of the Crimean shaft. There are also 2 other (secondary) inputs.

The park is famous for its fountains and fountains with drinking water. There are ponds, one of which is inhabited by swans and ducks. The size of this reservoir is large enough, which allows it to be used for riding on water modes of transport. The park has many flower beds and flower beds.

Among other objects are the Green Theater built in 1928, playgrounds, bicycle paths, a rope town, a winter skating rink, an observatory, a summer cinema, cafes and restaurants.

Filevsky park in Moscow

Filevsky park is located in the western part of the capital. It is impressive in size and 90% covered with forests. The influx of visitors is observed on weekends, while on weekdays there are few people.





The park is located on the high bank of the Moskva River. This is a real piece of wood in the middle of a huge city. Maples, lindens, oaks, birches, and pines grow here. Under the trees there is a dense grass cover.

The area is crossed by walking and cycling paths and trails. Bicycles can be rented right at the entrance. Equipped picnic areas are available. Littering directly in the forest is prohibited.

The beach area also did not go unnoticed by the designers of the park. Directly on the river beach are pools with blue water and a heating system. There are dressing cabins, showers, sunbeds, exercise machines and cafes, as well as a small sports ground. On the shore you can meet fishermen. According to them, catfish, perch, bream, roach, and rudd are found in the river.

Also, the ancient Naryshkin manor has been preserved on the territory of the park, and nearby - a pond with exotic trees. A fish is also found in the pond.

Alexander Garden

Alexander Garden is located in the center of Moscow, directly at the Kremlin. The park was founded in 1812 on the occasion of the victory in the war with Napoleon. This is a very popular place with residents of Moscow. It hosts concerts, exhibitions, and festive evenings. And all this in the open. The main attraction is the Tomb of the Unknown Soldier, which burns eternal flame.

Hermitage Garden

This is a small-sized recreation area, located among multi-storey buildings. The park appeared in 1894. There is everything for youth recreation, with the exception of the carousels. In addition to the three theaters, there is a playground, a fountain, clubs, cafes, restaurants, a cinema. There are almost always a lot of people. The main attraction of this park is the large Silver Heart, near which there are flower beds and benches. This is a traditional meeting place for young people.

Izmailovsky park

The park is located in the eastern part of the capital. This is the most extensive recreation area in Moscow. The park has attractions, sports grounds, a swimming pool. It is suitable for those who prefer active leisure activities, and those who come with their families for a walk in a park in Moscow.
