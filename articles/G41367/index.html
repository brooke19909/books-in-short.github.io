What to bring from the UAE as a gift to friends and relatives?

I always want to bring home something to commemorate a vacation in a particular country. Even an ordinary souvenir will bring pleasant memories of the trip. In the United Arab Emirates, a wide selection of various commemorative products. The state is located at the crossroads of trade routes, so goods from other eastern states are added to local souvenirs. If you are interested in the question of what to bring from the UAE, you should go shopping and markets, take a closer look at things and ask the price.



Various magnets, figurines and figures are always brought as a souvenir. Desert ships - camels will always remind of the Emirates. A huge number of figures of these animals are sold here, they are made of different materials: glass, wood, plastic, metal, leather. What to bring from the UAE as a gift to women? Of course, incense (bahur) made in the form of figures made of wood or soft balls. They are mounted on appliances powered by electricity, and when heated they spread aromas.

Arabs use oily perfumes that are not inferior in smell to the best Parisian perfume. They are applied only to the skin, because they leave stains on clothing. Prices for perfumes are very diverse, but you should not save too much, because the smell will be cheap. What souvenirs to bring from the UAE to remind you of your vacation? Since the state consists of seven emirates, a bottle of sand from different beaches is very popular. It is very different in colors and shades, so it creates unique patterns inside the container.



What to bring from the UAE, if not spices! The markets offer a huge variety of them. It is very important not to get lost in the ocean of aromas emanating from each counter. In the Emirates, coffee is considered a traditional drink, so you can buy everything for coffee drinking. Choose copper kettles very carefully, because among them there are those that are suitable only for interior decoration. They drink coffee with cardamom, although it is a little bit bitter, it has its own unique taste.

Oriental sweets - this is what sweet tooths will order when you ask them what to bring from the UAE. Reviews about sorbet, Turkish delight, halva are only the most positive, because such yummy can not be found anywhere else. Sweets should also include dates; they have a special place in the Emirates. Fruits are sold fresh, in honey, chocolate, with almonds inside, etc.

What to bring from the UAE, if not smoking appliances! A wide selection of hookahs, pipes, and tobacco are offered in markets and shops.



Many will be interested to look at antique edged weapons and firearms. Before buying, you should learn about the rules for importing goods to your homeland. Perhaps in no country there is such a variety of jewelry as in the UAE. There are a lot of gold items and precious stones, you can also purchase very beautiful jewelry storage boxes. Of course, this list of souvenirs does not end there, in the UAE there are a lot of quality things. In this rich country, everyone will find something necessary and interesting for themselves!
