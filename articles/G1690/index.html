Michael Freeman and his work

Who is he - photography guru Michael Freeman, who has written a huge number of excellent books? What stands out among other authors?

Biography

Very little is known about him. He was born in 1945 in Britain. He wrote a lot of books (over 100) that help young photographers in their professional growth. He became the winner of prestigious awards at the international level. His books are real creations; they cannot be called simple textbooks or manuals.



Traveling and constant self-development helped Michael Freeman write his wonderful books. His unique photographs are published by the most famous editions. In his books, he uses his own photographs, which is what distinguishes him from other writers. Combining the two directions, he helped other amateurs and professionals to master the intricacies of digital photography, to learn all its nuances.

Book "Photographer's View"

This edition is a real find for photography lovers. She is recognized as the best in her category. Tells an easy and understandable language about the history and development of the art of photography.

It reveals the secrets and secrets of success. It will help to understand the soul of the photographer, his creative techniques. The best collection of creative secrets, styles of artistry and genres has been compiled. This book will push for new incredible pictures and ideas.

"School of Photography"

Michael Freeman has written many books. "School of Photography" is one of the most popular. The book shows the essence of street style. Street photography is a bit like journalism. The book will teach you how to go beyond everyday life, express your emotions. Michael Freeman introduces her best students in it and shows their work. His books support and motivate young photographers.



The book is written in easy and simple language. The reader will understand how to create perfect pictures with a simple camera, understand the lighting, learn about the combination of compositions. In fact, this book is a guide to action for experienced photographers and beginners. Taking pictures is a real creative process that requires certain skills and knowledge.
