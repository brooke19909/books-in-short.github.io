Universal network charger: review, specifications, types and reviews. USB Charger

Virtually any digital gadget requires periodic replenishment of the consumed energy. When buying a phone, camera or tablet, you do not need to purchase a special device for such purposes, since it is always included in the kit. Nevertheless, in the process of active operation of several units of such equipment, confusion may arise. In addition, taking a gadget with you on a trip with a service headset is quite expensive and inconvenient. The solution to the problem may be a universal network charger that can replenish the energy supply of various types of digital technology. Of course, each product has its own range of devices suitable for servicing, but even standard models are quite suitable for most portable devices.





Main characteristics

The main operational parameter that you should pay attention to when choosing such devices is the current strength. The standard range varies from 1000 to 3000 mA. So, for small mobile devices, players and certain categories of digital cameras, a network charger with a current strength of 1000 mA is suitable. For tablets, laptops and other types of equipment that use a larger amount of resources, you should purchase such accessories with a potential of at least 2000 mA. It is important to consider that the charge speed depends on the number of amperes. Other specifications relate to the weight, dimensions and configuration of the interface for connection. These parameters are quite diverse, so every user can find the best option on the market for the needs of their portable gadgets.

Types of devices

There are no special classifications, but it is possible to conditionally divide the devices according to their purpose. The most common are network chargers for phones, the features of which include the presence of a micro-USB interface. The user in this case receives the connector and the connector itself. It is important to note that charging time also depends on connecting cables and adapters. The better the wire, the faster the process of replenishing the feed element.

Also worth noting are chargers for more energy-demanding equipment. This is a segment in which tablets, powerful cameras, some laptop models, etc. are presented. In terms of design, they do not stand out, but the current in this case can reach 3000 mA. However, the network charger for the tablet may be limited to 2000 mA - another thing is that the process will take longer.

Car Chargers





To begin with, it is worth saying that the very principle of an alternative charger arose from the need to free the user from a conventional power supply. Most efficiently, solar batteries and external batteries perform this task . Network devices focus on another aspect of ergonomics, but car models of this type combine two concepts. The fact is that the network charger can work from on-board wiring 12V directly in the car. Of course, compared to 220V, this is not much, and the process efficiency may be minimal, but as an additional feature this option suits many. Especially when it comes to emergency situations.

Conclusion





Despite the outwardly modest function that the chargers perform, every little thing in their design has significant practical value. Replenishment of the energy of the battery today is not complete without special devices, so manufacturers strive to make them as convenient as possible. For this reason, the network charger is sometimes supplemented with auxiliary functions. For example, few of the users of modern gadgets can be surprised at the possibility of communication between a mobile device and a computer through communication systems of chargers. In addition, protection and energy conservation tools are being developed. Many manufacturers strive not only to give charging devices optimal indicators of voltage and current strength, but also to make them universal. As a result, the USB interface itself is developing, as evidenced by the spread of its modern configurations, in particular the micro version.
