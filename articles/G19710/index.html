The easiest way to make a men's hat





If you are just starting to learn how to work with a crochet hook, take yourself into service for this simple and understandable master class, which describes how you can crochet a man's hat with ordinary crochet posts. Performing such a product is quite easy.
The manufacture of any product begins with the selection of consumables and tools. Of course, to make a beautiful and comfortable men's hat you need to choose a good, high-quality yarn. In this case, it is desirable to give preference to knitting threads of medium thickness. For example, in our work we used knitting threads, consisting of acrylic and wool, with a density of 50 g / 150 m. The color of the yarn can be any, but, of course, you need to know the preferences of the future owner of the thing. As a rule, men's hats are made of dark, muted shades. You can purchase threads of gray, dark green, blue, purple and black. It is important to remember that the color of the headgear should be in harmony with other details of the wardrobe.
