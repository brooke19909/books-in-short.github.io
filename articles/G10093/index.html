Hotel "Alpha", Kirov: address, photos and reviews

Business people, like no one else, value comfort, because for productive work it is very important to have a good rest on the eve. This opportunity will provide the hotel "Alpha". Kirov will open up wide business opportunities, and this hotel will give comfort and coziness.





What the hotel offers

Convenient location and quality service will delight its guests the hotel "Alpha". Kirov will open up wide tourist and business opportunities for you. This hotel, in turn, will please you with a service corresponding to a high international level. The hotel offers the following:

  • accommodation in one of 48 comfortable rooms;
  • 2 conference rooms with professional equipment for conducting full-fledged business negotiations;
  • beautifully decorated banquet room for holidays and celebrations;
  • a cozy cafe with delicious dishes at an affordable price;
  • use of wireless Internet throughout the room and in the rooms;
  • private parking with round-the-clock security and video surveillance;
  • transfer services;
  • visit to the sauna;
  • uninterrupted supply of cold and hot water.




Hotel Alpha (Kirov): address

This institution boasts a favorable location. This is the business center of such a wonderful city as Kirov. Hotel Alpha, 129a Moskovskaya Street - exact address. The nearest major supermarket is just a 3-minute leisurely walk away. If you want to take a walk in Victory Park, you will have to overcome no more than 2 km. The city center is 4 minutes away.

Hotel "Alpha" (Kirov): how to get

Thanks to the good transport interchange, it will not be difficult for you to quickly reach the hotel. If you arrive at the railway station, you should go to the public transport stop and take bus number 37. After 12 minutes, you will reach the Avtosalon stop, from where you need to walk 120 meters along Moskovskaya Street. By the way, minibus number 37 also runs through the bus station.

Hotel Restaurant

Has a luxurious restaurant "Alpha" business hotel. Kirov will be in front of you at a glance, if you look at him through the panoramic windows of this institution. Here, in addition to the dining room, there are also two rooms for banquets. Thanks to the cozy interior and pleasant music, a favorable, relaxed atmosphere is created here.

The institution is open from 7:00 to 23:00. At the same time, guests of the hotel can enjoy a delicious breakfast in the buffet format. The cost of this service is 350 rubles per person. From noon to 5 p.m. business lunches are held. The cost per person is 300 rubles. It is also possible to order food in the room.





For business people

A lot of opportunities for business negotiations provides a business hotel "Alpha" (Kirov). Description of the conference rooms should start with the smaller of them. The stylish room is decorated in red and white colors and has large panoramic windows (a beautiful view opens from the 4th floor). It can accommodate up to 30 people. The hourly rental price is 600 rubles. However, it is impossible to rent a room for less than 4 hours. The room is equipped with a plasma screen, modern projector and sound system.

A large conference room for 70 people is located on the ground floor. The decoration is done in pastel shades. The rental price is 700 rubles per hour on the same conditions as in the previous case. The technical equipment is the same as in the small hall.

Negative reviews

This hotel is not without flaws. Based on the opinions of guests, it is worth noting the following flaws:

  • even if you book a transfer and a meeting at the station in advance, there is a risk that this service will not be provided (guests are not warned about force majeure circumstances);
  • salted food in a restaurant;
  • the staff is polite and friendly only until the guest has any comments and complaints;
  • substandard coffee served for breakfast;
  • during the cleaning, the maids shift things of the guests from place to place (of course, nothing disappears, but the impression is unpleasant);
  • guests' special wishes for beds are not fulfilled (if you book separately, you can get a bigger one, and vice versa);
  • standard rooms do not have refrigerators;
  • shaving kit is not put in the bathroom, it has to be requested additionally;
  • there are no information booklets in the rooms that contain data on the rules of accommodation and the cost of services;
  • hygiene kits are provided only on the day of arrival (then you have to ask yourself at the reception);
  • during breakfast, waiters are in no hurry to replenish the buffet, and do not respond to comments;
  • if you book a room not through the official website, but through third-party resources, it is possible that he will be busy upon arrival;
  • too narrow single beds;
  • air conditioning is noisy;
  • the hotel is far from city attractions;
  • inflated prices for dishes in the restaurant;
  • breakfast is not included in the price;
  • Some rooms do not open windows.

General impression

Alpha is a good hotel in Kirov. Given the remoteness from the main tourist attractions, this option is often chosen by business people (which is why the prefix "business" is used in the name). It provides all the opportunities for business negotiations, as well as special occasions. The organization provides all kinds of assistance.

As for the living conditions, guests note fresh repairs, new furniture and high-quality sanitary ware in the rooms. Nevertheless, the guests consider the price too high (apparently, you have to pay extra for the four-star status of the hotel). There are also some questions for the staff and the reservation system. Nevertheless, this accommodation option is considered one of the most worthy in Kirov.
