Hotels in Sovetskiy (Khanty-Mansi Autonomous Okrug): description and reviews

Sovetsky is a small town in the Tyumen region. This is the working town of the Far North, which is mainly engaged in logging, wood processing and furniture production. Despite the modest size of the village, there are many attractions. Well and, of course, the taiga nature attracts. If you are interested in Soviet in Khanty-Mansi Autonomous Okrug, the hotels of the city will provide you with a decent welcome.

Reviews about the hotel "Olymp"

Travelers respond quite positively about this hotel in Sovetskiy in the Khanty-Mansi Autonomous Okrug. Namely:

  • fresh modern renovation and pleasant interior;
  • absolutely clean and new bedding;
  • Friendly and helpful staff.

But not without comments and criticism:

  • no air conditioning in the rooms;
  • lack of sound insulation;
  • cramped bathroom and inconvenient location of plumbing;
  • for two guests, only one set of towels is provided;
  • Breakfast is not served in the restaurant, but in containers.

  • parking;

Reviews about the hotel "Alexandria"

About this hotel in the city of Sovetskiy in the Khanty-Mansi Autonomous Okrug guests leave such approving comments:

  • polite and helpful staff;
  • pleasant cozy atmosphere and cleanliness of the rooms;
