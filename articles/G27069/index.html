Simple dressing "Solokha"

In a woman’s wardrobe, fashion accessories occupy a special niche. They firmly entered her life, making a woman unique and romantic. Or vice versa, they create the image of a business and strict lady.

The headband of Solokha has been known for a long time. At some time, she came into oblivion, she was dubbed the "old woman" jewelry. However, recently this unpretentious little thing is gaining more and more popularity. She is back in fashion, complementing various looks.





A look into the past

It turns out that many women and girls do not know what kind of accessory. However, the bandage is very popular among different age categories of women. And the star of French cinema Brigitte Bardot often used such dressings, demonstrating love for this thing.

Nothing stands still, fashion is a changeable thing. What is fashionable today, tomorrow will be forgotten forever. But retro stuff is always available at any time. Unpretentious at first glance, the bandage gains unexpected accents.

What is Solokha

The Solokha dressing is a small piece of fabric that is wrapped around the head and tied in front with a bow or knot. How to choose the width of the fabric, the fashionista decides, it all depends on personal preference and taste. Many women themselves make such a jewelry on their heads.

Previously, it was a large scarf that folded in a certain order. Now this is a finished thing. Sometimes a wire is inserted in the middle. This allows you to conveniently fix the bandage on the head, and twist the ends. Everything is simple and convenient!





Unique charm

Thanks to such an unpretentious little thing, fashionistas create various images. It can be worn with office clothes, diluting the rigor of this style. She is also suitable for a date, making the image of a girl romantic and airy. And in the street style, the headband of Solokh will add ease and liberty.

The uniformity of office style can be diluted with the original bandage. Just take into account several rules for wearing it in this way. Dressing "Solokha" should be pastel colors or two-tone. One color must be chosen bright, and the other black. With a two-color bandage, it is worth highlighting with eyes or lips make-up. The bow is located on the side, the ends are bent.

If a romantic image lacks some details, then perhaps the headband of Solokh will fix the matter. The accessory is matched to the color of the dress with a similar color. The color scheme is better to choose delicate: blue, pink, salad, turquoise. The bow is located on the side. To give the image a touch of romance, bend one end and leave the other as it is.





How to wear "Solokha"

There are various ways to wear and tie a bandage. Tie-up of fabric over the hair, taken in a bun, the most common option. A gentle, cute and reverent look is created, and the hair gathered in a bun reminds of a forgotten retro style.

A fashionable little thing on her loose hair looks original and original. This is a universal method of wearing the Solokha. It looks equally beautiful in an ensemble with a romantic dress, acceptable for a festive setting and everyday wear.

Trendy sunglasses accentuate a glamorous look. A beautiful bundle of bandages looks piquant and cocky. He will give the girl a charm and charm peculiar only to her.





Do-it-yourself Soloch dressing

Even a novice needlewoman can wear the Solokha dressing. The pattern for this straightforward little thing is simple and easy. In each house there is a piece of chiffon or cotton fabric of the desired color and size. You will also need scissors, a pencil, colored threads, a tight elastic band 3 cm wide and 50 cm long, a sewing machine.

Work begins with a head measurement. Then an elongated rectangular part is drawn on the material. A tube is sewn from this rectangle, into which an elastic band is subsequently inserted and sewn with a false seam. After this, the ends of the tube are sewn with hidden stitches.

Next, a bow is sewn. A rectangle is drawn exactly the same, only a smaller one. Optionally, you can narrow the ends of the bow or leave it as it is. If the needlewoman is inexperienced, it is better to make a pattern first on paper, and then transfer it to the fabric.



In order for the bow or the ends of the bandage to keep in shape, a metal wire is inserted into it. The bow is sewn to the tube manually. That's all, a beautiful and fashionable little thing is ready.

Young mothers can sew such a little thing for their little princesses. Mom and daughter in identical dressings look so touching and gentle! And besides, Solokha favorably emphasizes the unity of mother and daughter, their external similarity and spiritual kinship.





Girls and women, wear Solokha, this uncomplicated accessory that gives your image a unique, charming and attractive appearance. Experiment with what hairstyle to wear, what outfit she will complement and transform. Do not be afraid of changes in your external and internal image.
