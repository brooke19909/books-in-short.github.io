Android Pay: how it works and how to use it?

Most recently, in May of this year, Russians were able to get acquainted with the technology of contactless payments on phones running the Android operating system. The main competitors Apple Pay and Samsung Pay started half a year earlier, but the huge reach of devices that support Android Pay can play into the hands of Google and help it win a new audience. In this article, we will consider a new application for Android smartphones. How Android Pay works, how it differs from its competitors, is it safe to use it, and what difficulties did the users of the service face?





System requirements

To get started, it's worth figuring out what devices Android Pay runs on. The requirements that Google makes are not too high. An NFC chip (for making payments) and Android version 4.4 (for installing the Android Pay application) must be installed on your phone. It seems to be simple, but in fact there are a number of restrictions that may interfere with the inclusion of Android Pay:

  • Firstly, the service works only on those gadgets that operate under the official firmware (developer versions and less popular assemblies are not supported).
  • Secondly, there is a list of smartphones on which it is impossible to enable Android Pay. These are Nexus 7, Elephone P9000, Samsung Galaxy Note 3, Galaxy Light and S3.

As for the terminals, then everything is quite simple. For payment, any terminal that supports PayPass or PayWave technology is suitable. Such terminals are installed in almost any, not even the most prestigious store or point of sale.





What banks and cards does it work with?

Just like in the case of other payment systems, Android Pay started only with a part of banks operating in Russia. Fortunately, among them are all the most popular institutions in great demand: Raiffeisen Bank, Russian Standard, Rocketbank, Otkritie, Sberbank, Tinkoff, a number of other less well-known organizations and a payment service from Yandex " With shops, the situation is no worse. Almost all popular retail chains showed interest in the new technology and pledged to support its work. This is quite natural, since these same networks already work with Apple and Samsung.

How to connect?

What phones Android Pay works with, figured out, now you need to connect this service. If you have already paid for any of the Google services and linked your bank card to your Google account, then installing the Android Pay application, immediately find them in the list. If there are no linked cards, you will have to enter all the details yourself. You can use the built-in credit card scanner, but it is often mistaken with the number (it is not clear why Google could not bring the technology to mind).

Before you add a card, do not forget to set a password on your device, otherwise Android Pay will respond with an error and prohibit paying anything. After adding the card, you will need to confirm it. You can confirm the card using the SMS code or by calling the technical support service of the bank and verifying that you are connecting your card to the mobile payment system. You will be charged 30 rubles for confirmation, but will be returned after some time.





Security

Your card data is stored on Google‚Äôs servers and is securely encrypted on them. Not your real details are used for payment, but specially formed sets of numbers - tokens. This does not mean that a permanent connection to the servers is required to work. No, tokens are created on servers, but then uploaded to each individual device and stored there until a payment is made. Apple and Samsung devices have a separate physical space for storing tokens, which significantly increases the level of security. Plus, Android Pay, one way or another, will ask for access to the Internet when tokens run out in the device, which is a significant drawback.

To make each purchase, you will have to enter a password, a key code or put your finger on the fingerprint scanner (it all depends on what protection method is used on your phone). If you disable any of the blocking methods, then all data regarding your bank cards will be destroyed. If the gadget has been lost or stolen, then you can remotely erase information about attached cards. In general, do not worry about security.





How does Android Pay work?

When working with terminals and paying for a purchase of 1,000 rubles or less, just turn on the gadget's display and attach it to the terminal. In the case of larger amounts, you will need to enter a password or put your finger on the fingerprint sensor. You can also use smart watches for payment.

Android Pay works both in physical retail outlets and online. Many do not go to stores at all and make purchases on websites or in applications, so Google took care to introduce technology there. For Android Pay to work on the site, technology support from the resource owner is required. The buyer needs to find a button with a green robot and the inscription Pay and click on it. Immediately after that, it will be redirected to the application itself, where in the same way as in the case of payment in real life, you will need to remove the lock and confirm the operation. That's all, the site or application will instantly understand that the order has been paid and will issue it.

Another problem that users face is the return of goods. The fact is that the token that hides your details will be saved for only one terminal, and in order to make a return, you will have to look for exactly that terminal.

Promotions and discounts

The launch of each payment system is accompanied by discounts and promotions, which are designed to popularize services among the masses and make people try it at least once. Of the stocks known today, it is worth allocating a 50% discount on travel in the Moscow metro. 50% discount on paying for an Aeroexpress ticket and the same discount on buying any burger on the Burger King fast food chain. It all works as follows - you pay the full price for a ticket or any other product, and for some time half the amount is returned to your card. This means that in the absence of the necessary amount, it will not work to use the promotion.





How to enable Android Pay on a jailbroken phone?

Owners of some devices that have an unstable or hacked version of the operating system installed have encountered a number of problems when entering bank card information. The fact is that Google (for security reasons) prohibits the use of financial applications in any Android-systems other than the original. The problem can be solved by deceiving the Android Pay program. To do this, you need to hide from her information about hacking the system. So, first you need the Magisk Manager utility, which will install and update the Magisk program. With Magisk open, find the Magisk Hide entry in it and activate it. Reboot the gadget and re-enable Magisk Manager. A list of programs from which you can hide the fact of hacking will appear. We find Android Pay and disconnect. Once you do this, reboot the device and try using the payment system again. Just in case, check if Android Pay works with your phone (maybe Magisk will not help).





Instead of a conclusion

So what do we have? Another payment system that was launched too late or a really worthy product that people will like? Rather, the second, because with its army of fans Google can compete on equal terms with Apple and Samsung without any problems (given the fact that Android Pay works both on devices of Google itself and on devices from Samsung). And people themselves are ready for a new step in this direction. Bank cards are convenient, but the younger generation more often holds a smartphone in their hands, which means they will be more willing to pay with it. Discounts also played an important role, perhaps promotions will be repeated, and user interest will be actively heated for a long time.

Service advantages:

  • A huge number of devices running Android Pay.
  • Different payment protection options.
  • Discounts and promotions.

Cons of the service:

  • Doesn't work on jailbroken devices.
  • Tokens for payment are stored only on Google servers.
