Where is

Tourists find the location of this hotel quite convenient. The Park Avrupa hotel is located in Kemer near its center. There are many cafes, restaurants and shops near this complex. What is Park Avrupa Hotel 3 *: general description

Rooms for rent in this hotel in one three-story building. The hotel yard is well landscaped and fenced. This complex was built in 1996. The last renovation for 2017 was carried out in 2013.
