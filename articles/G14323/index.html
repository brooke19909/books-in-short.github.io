Flower made of leather. Workshop on manufacturing

Many needlewomen leave old unpaired gloves, cut off the shafts of obsolete leather boots, and all trimmings of genuine leather are put into a special box, so that over time they can be used as trim on jackets, sweaters or denim jackets. However, over time, trimmings still remain, which seem to have nowhere else to use. But you can create a flower of leather. The master class of this process is very simple, and the manufacturing itself also does not take much time. As a result, you get a beautiful, practical flower that can be used as a decorative decoration.

Flower of leather: a master class. Materials

To create another masterpiece, you will need, of course, the skin itself. The more different pieces you have, the more fantasies you can realize. The skin can be thin, taken from women's demi-season gloves. And maybe a thick one, cut off from the top of the boots. Perfect leftover bag or leather wallet. The only condition is that the material must be natural. Dermatin is not suitable, since products from it are not durable enough, easily lose their shape. Coloring also does not matter. Classic black, lilac, green or pale gray ... they will all be equally in demand if you decide to make a flower from leather. The master class also provides for the presence of a pencil, scissors, threads, needles, thermal gun and candles. If all the materials are laid out on the table - you can proceed.

Flower of leather: a master class. Description of work

Select a few pieces of leather from which you plan to make the product. Then you need to decide on the estimated size. Draw a future flower on a life-sized piece of paper. In order to make flowers from leather, patterns can be done independently. It is enough to draw on the sheet of paper the petals of the size that is needed. A rose needs three sizes of petals. Approximately 5 cm, 3.5 cm and 2 cm. Cutting out 6-8 pieces of each size from the skin, we begin to form a bud. Roses look the most vibrant, given a certain volume, characteristic of a living flower. This can be achieved using a candle flame. Bring one petal to the flame at a distance of about 2-4 cm. The skin begins to melt and take on a bizarre outline. If you practice trimming, then very soon you will be able to give the petals a beautiful shape. A flower forms starting in the middle. Attaching the petals is very easy using a heat gun. The most important thing is to let the product cool.

Leather flower: master class and application

At first glance, it seems that the scope of use of this jewelry is very limited. In fact, this is far from the case! We offer some photos. Leather flowers can be fixed on a new handbag or on a boot, on a rim or on a hair clip. The main thing is that it is perfectly combined in color and style with the rest of the way. A carefully executed flower will be a great addition to both a business suit and an evening outfit.
