Water holiday

Holidays in Cyprus in June are tempting in that during this period the biggest and most pathos event is held there - the festival of water. Locals call it Cataclysmos. On this day, in the temples of the island, solemn services are held, which end with a procession to the embankments. There, priests read a prayer and throw a cross into the sea. After the men are offered to get it from the seabed. And the most desperate agree to participate in the competition. Anyone who manages to get a cross from the depths of the sea, is proclaimed king of water triumph. At the water festival, none of those present remains dry. Water on this day is a symbol of purification, therefore, every participant in the ceremony should take a bath. After the "water procedures" walks continue on the coast. The festive program includes concerts, fun contests, dances. Guests are offered to try Cypriot sweets and delicious national dishes.

June Island Prices



The cost of tours to Cyprus at the beginning of summer is low. As a rule, vacation prices begin to rise at the end of the month.
