Hotel "White Mountain" in Noyabrsk

Winter in the Yamal-Nenets Autonomous Okrug is beautiful and snowy. Therefore, much attention is paid to the organization of recreation in the cold season. Hotel "Belaya Gora" in Noyabrsk, in addition to modern comfortable rooms, offers its guests a wide range of winter activities. This is one of their favorite places for city dwellers, where they like to spend their weekends and holidays.

Description

The location of the White Mountain Hotel in Noyabrsk is a forested area. The distance from the hotel to the city center is 2.3 kilometers, to the train station - 3.5 kilometers, and to the airport - 11 kilometers. A grocery store is within walking distance.

The ground floor of the three-story hotel building is occupied by a bar and restaurant. In the summer, a cafe is open-air. In the morning, a continental breakfast is served at the restaurant. On the second and third floors are hotel rooms of various levels of comfort.

The hotel is an entertainment complex where you can not only skate and ski, but also relax in saunas with pools, in cedar barrels, and visit a phytobar. Indoors, guests have the opportunity to play billiards or table tennis.

Guests arriving by car are provided with a free, private, guarded parking space. You can use the wireless Internet services for free anywhere in the hotel.

Thanks to the convenient transport interchange near the entertainment complex, from here you can easily get anywhere in the city in a few minutes. The White Mountain Hotel in Noyabrsk is located at 141 Shevchenko Street.

Accommodation

For accommodation in the hotel "White Mountain" guests are offered several categories of rooms:

  • single with a single bed - 2200 rubles per day;
  • double with two single beds - 3000 rubles per day for two and 1500 rubles per person;
  • double with a large double bed - 3000 rubles per day for two and 2500 rubles per person;
  • double "Deluxe" with a large double bed - 3800 rubles per day for two and 3300 rubles for one person;
  • double "Deluxe" with two double beds - 4200 rubles per day for two, 4700 rubles for three, 4950 rubles for four, and 3500 rubles for one person.

Prices are for accommodation only. You can pay for the room including breakfast served in the restaurant. At the Belaya Gora Hotel in Noyabrsk, breakfast can also be delivered to your room at the request of guests.

Room Amenities

All rooms in the hotel are equipped with the following amenities:

  • TV
  • telephone
  • an electric kettle;
  • a hairdryer;
  • cooking utensils;
  • dining area;
  • own bathroom;
  • free toiletries.

Superior rooms at the Belaya Gora hotel complex in Noyabrsk have a refrigerator and a seating area. Ironing facilities are available. All rooms have mountain views. Cleaning is done daily.

Posting Rules

When checking into the Belaya Gora hotel in Noyabrsk (Shevchenko str., 141), it is advisable to first familiarize yourself with the rules of accommodation:

  1. Children can stay at the hotel from any age.
  2. For extra beds, both a child and an adult will be charged separately in the amount of 1,100 rubles per day.
  3. This room can only accommodate one extra bed. This requires a prior request and permission from the hotel.
  4. The cost of all additional services is paid separately from the cost of living.
  5. Pets are not allowed in the hotel.
  6. Free cancellation is possible at least two days before the expected date of arrival.

If you want to receive more detailed information about staying at the Belaya Gora Hotel in Noyabrsk by phone, the number can be found on the booking sites.

Additional services

In order to make your stay in the entertainment complex "White Mountain" as comfortable as possible, there are many additional services for guests:

  • Meals are organized in the hotel's own restaurant. A continental breakfast is served here in the morning. A la carte meals are available. Room service is also available.
  • In the evenings, karaoke is available at the restaurant.
  • Guests can also relax in the cozy bar.
  • In the conference room / banquet hall you can help organize a business meeting or hold a festive celebration.
  • You can use the services of fax and photocopying, extra charge.
  • For guests, the hotel has a sauna with a swimming pool and a solarium.
  • The phytobar offers a health tea or oxygen cocktail.
  • Laundry and ironing services are available for a fee.
  • The hotel has its own free secure parking.
  • Wi-Fi is free throughout.
  • Upon request, a shuttle service from or to the airport will be arranged for you (paid).
  • Wake up service is provided.
  • Reception is open 24 hours a day.

Additional services for sports

Since guests generally come to the Belaya Gora Hotel in Noyabrsk for winter sports, various services and amenities are also provided for them:

  1. In winter, a good skating rink is poured near the hotel.
  2. There is a ski slope with a lift.
  3. Here you can rent sports equipment - skiing, snowboarding, snow tubing, ice skating.
  4. Equipped ski storage.
  5. A clothes dryer is provided.
  6. The hotel sells ski passes.
  7. Hiking trails are organized for holidaymakers.
  8. The hotel also has a pool table and table tennis table.

  • There are very few channels on TV to watch.
  • If the restaurant serves a banquet, then you will not be able to have dinner.
  • There are poor access roads to the complex.
  • Some dishes in the restaurant are more expensive than in a cafe in the city.
  • There are complaints that the price at check-in was higher than when booking.
  • The bar plays the music that is heard in the rooms loudly until late.
