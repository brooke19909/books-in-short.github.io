The best hotels of Thailand: photos, rating and reviews

Today, many tourists from Russia prefer to spend their holidays in Thailand. This decision is fully justified. This amazing country is located on the territory of two peninsulas - Malacca and Indochina. Thailand is located in several climatic zones, and therefore the tourist season in the country continues year-round.





The magnificent nature, clean and well-groomed beaches, many comfortable hotels designed for vacationers with any level of income - all this guarantees a great vacation. In Thailand, guests are happy to receive numerous hotels, inns, villas. Regardless of the level of their comfort, the service in them is at the highest level.

To facilitate the choice of those who want to relax in this fabulous country, we will present you a rating of the best hotels in Thailand, divided into several categories.

In phuket

Experienced travelers recommend starting exploring the country from this island. We will follow their advice and introduce you to the best hotels in Thailand in Phuket. They are usually distinguished not only by luxurious interiors, but also by their location. Magnificent views of the ocean, pools and bars around them, soft white sand beaches, spas, fitness centers, spacious rooms with huge beds - all this is characteristic of the best hotels in Thailand. “All inclusive” is a system that has found widespread worldwide, and Phuket was no exception.

Baan Yin Di Boutique

A luxurious, very comfortable hotel, built of wood and rattan, with a splendid finish in marble and granite. The cozy and very comfortable rooms have internet, cable TV, mini-bars and refrigerators. The spacious rooms are cleaned and linen changed daily. The bathrooms are equipped with all the necessary toiletries and cosmetics.





As in most of the best 5 * hotels in Thailand, guests have the opportunity to visit the outdoor pool, where you can swim at any time of the day. And on a well-kept sandy beach you can sunbathe and take a Thai massage here. This hotel will be comfortable for both young people and older travelers.

Avista Resort Phuket SPA 5 *

A comfortable hotel with luxurious rooms, nestled among the green hills of Kata. All rooms have balconies with stunning sea or garden views. Guests can use the refrigerator, minibar, bathroom with all necessary toiletries. All vacationers at any time can visit the infinity pool with a bar, sauna, spa. The hotel's restaurant serves buffet meals, and if you wish, you can order breakfast in your room.





The hotel welcomes guests with children. A club has been created for them, in which experienced educators work. The hotel's restaurants offer a special menu for younger travelers.

The millennium resort of patong

The hotel is located in the center of Phuket's vibrant entertainment, surrounded by tropical vegetation. On its territory there are several outdoor pools, a spa, restaurants and bars. Amazing rooms equipped with modern appliances, furnished with comfortable furniture made of wood. One of the restaurants serves traditional Russian cuisine.

A large shopping center is located next door. If you want to explore local attractions, you can hire a car. The popular Patong Beach is a ten minute walk from the hotel.

Kalima SPA Resort

Experienced travelers believe that Kalima Spa is one of the best hotels in Thailand in Phuket. It is located in the west of the island, near Patong Beach. To check in, guests are offered to choose one of two hundred rooms and spacious villas with a TV, functional furniture, a minibar, a safe.

Each villa has its own pool, living room, dining room and terrace. An American breakfast is served in the rooms in the morning. In this hotel you can order a wedding ceremony, by the way, there is also a very beautiful room for newlyweds.





3 and 4 star hotels

I would not want our readers to get the impression that the best hotels in Thailand are 5-star exclusively. Hotels below the rank differ only in the absence of luxury. These are middle class hotels. Their rating is slightly lower, but they provide the highest level of service, comfortable accommodation, which can satisfy the requirements of the most fastidious tourists.

Eastin Easy Patong Phuket

This hotel was appreciated by everyone who comes to the country not only for leisure, but also for business purposes. In addition to the necessary furniture, household appliances, the rooms include a work desk with a computer and a daily fresh press. The cozy rooms are cleaned daily. We want to warn you that this is a non-smoking hotel, so if you have not yet got rid of a bad habit, you need to consider another option.

The best Thailand hotels in Pattaya

And now we offer to move to the youth resort of Thailand. In Pattaya there are almost a thousand hotels of different levels of comfort and price category. Most of them are located along the coast and have their own beaches.

Garden cliff resort

A luxurious 5 * full-board hotel where families with children can stay. Those who wish can hold a wedding ceremony here. Magnificent rooms equipped with the most modern appliances and comfortable functional furniture, have a good rest.

Vacationers can use the fitness center, indoor and outdoor pools, and restaurants serving traditional Thai cuisine.





Royal cliff beach

Tourists from around the world book rooms in various hotels in Thailand. Which one is better? It is not easy to answer this question, since it largely depends on your preferences, wishes, financial capabilities. And there is plenty to choose from here.

Royal cliff

Lovers of both family and romantic vacations will appreciate it. The rooms here are decorated in a contemporary style. Lovers can get married and celebrate their wedding in a chic banquet room. I must say that this ceremony is common in the best hotels in Thailand. It passes very solemnly and unusually beautifully.

Near the hotel there is a beautiful white sand beach where you can enjoy your favorite water sports.

Woodlands suites

Many travelers admit that the best 5 * hotels in Thailand are simply amazing with luxury. A striking example is the posh Woodlands Suites. The spacious rooms are equipped with unusually beautiful handmade furniture and modern appliances. The private bathroom, artfully decorated with marble and granite, is equipped with all necessary toiletries.

In the rooms, bed sheets embroidered with silk are changed daily. It offers guests an indoor and outdoor pool, several restaurants serving Thai and European cuisine, spa, non-smoking rooms, parking.

Hotels on Koh Samui

The advantage of relaxing on the island can be considered the location of hotels and villas. The best hotels of Thailand for a holiday in Samui are located on the coastline, regardless of whether the villa is in front of you or a simple bungalow. The cleanest private beaches, amazing sea views attract here lovers of solitude, peace and silence. Well-off tourists mostly rest on the island, because the comfort of the local hotels is amazing, and the cost of living in them is not available to everyone.

Banyan tree samui

The luxury hotel consists of detached landscaped villas. Each of them has its own pool, dining room, living room and garden. The cozy rooms are furnished with magnificent wooden furniture. In the bathrooms you can use various oils and fragrances, which are rarely found in other hotels of the country.





The hotel's restaurants offer gourmet Thai and Asian dishes. According to vacationers, in the evening you can invite the cook and the waiter, who will prepare only for you the desired dishes.

Tango Luxe Beach Villa

Not all the best hotels in Thailand can boast a view from the windows of the main attraction of the island - the Buddha statue. This is one of the features of the Tango Luxe Hotel, which is located on the north side of the island. Each villa has a pool, spa, bars. Everything here has to a comfortable rest and relaxation. Surprisingly, a Russian bath was even built here, which was highly appreciated by our compatriots.





The restaurants mainly offer Thai cuisine, although European food can also be prepared on request.

Paradise island estate

The villas of this hotel are located in a fenced area with a huge swimming pool and fitness center. A ten minute walk from it is the beach. The rooms are decorated in Thai style. All villas have a spacious bathroom or rain shower, all necessary appliances.

Muang Samui Villas Suites

One of the best hotels in Thailand offers 64 comfortable rooms decorated in Thai style. The hotel is surrounded by tropical gardens. On the territory there is a huge pool. Snow-white sand and crystal clear water will make your stay at this hotel truly fabulous.

In restaurants you can taste original dishes for every taste. Spa treatments, massages will help you to truly relax and forget about the daily bustle.





Hotels in Krabi

Krabi is the southern region of Thailand. The tourism industry is still developing here, but I must say, at a very fast pace. In recent years, hotels of the highest level have appeared on the coast, and the number of tourists has increased significantly. Krabi has an unusually beautiful nature, which you will not see either in the center of the country or in the north.

Dee Andaman Hotel

This is a non-smoking hotel. Its rooms are decorated in a contemporary style. On the territory there is a large pool, on the roof - a restaurant. Guests can rent a car to drive to the city and explore local attractions. The hotel has a massage room where professional craftsmen can help you relax.

Krabi Tipa Resort

All rooms at this hotel are decorated with natural wood furniture. Here guests are offered only double rooms of various comforts. They are equipped with modern appliances. The balconies offer stunning views of the surroundings. The hotel has a large outdoor pool, a restaurant where you can order almost any dish.

Hotels for families with children

I must say that in Thailand a lot of attention is paid to families with children. Especially for young travelers, the hotels host various entertainment programs, water parks that kids really like, built children's rooms and clubs, special meals. The best hotels of Thailand for families with children we will present below.

Bella villa cabana

Very nice new boutique hotel, which is located in a secluded place on the seashore in the north of Pattaya. It has a high level of service and a pleasant homely atmosphere. The hotel offers comfortable rooms and a well-kept private beach with a gentle entrance to the water, which is especially important for families with children. Guests with babies can invite a professional babysitter.





Grand Pacific Sovereign Resort & SPA

The five-star hotel is located on the outskirts of Cha-Am Beach, in the southwest of the Gulf of Thailand. The influx of vacationers here falls on the weekend. The rest of the time the hotel is a calm and quiet place without a noisy nightlife. This is ideal for families with children. Younger guests under 12 stay free with parents.

When staying with children at this hotel, pay attention to the rooms with access to the pool with a children's section, located in two-story buildings and two-level rooms. Some of these apartments have kitchenettes, and there is the Italian restaurant Celestino, open from 11 a.m. non-stop, which offers a special children's menu.

For younger guests, the pool, kids club, games room can be diversified with other entertainments. It can be excursions to elephant farms, to natural national parks.

Tourists reviews

According to everyone who has already visited Thailand, a vacation here is like an immersion in a special fairy-tale world: magnificent nature, well-groomed beaches, excellent service. Vacationers address many warm words to hotel and villa administrations. Everything here is aimed at a comfortable stay for guests. Travelers note that the service and maintenance in hotels in no way depend on the level of their comfort. The staff in all hotels is helpful and attentive to the wishes of the guests. It is important that in Thailand you can choose a hotel according to your requirements and financial capabilities. And thanks to this article, you could get acquainted with the list of the best hotels in Thailand.
