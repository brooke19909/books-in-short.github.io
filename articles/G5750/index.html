Naithon Beach (Phuket): beach photos , advantages and disadvantages

The sandy and cozy shore is located in the north of the island. A distinctive feature is a large number of tourists, regardless of the season. The difference is even greater when compared to Karon Beach or Patong beaches.

Naithon Beach is not wild: you can rent a sun lounger and an umbrella in a Thai cafe. The services do not end there: across the road there are massage parlors, hotels, restaurants where you can escape from the scorching sun on the veranda of the establishment while having a cool cocktail. There are no cafes on the beach.

The main advantage of Knighton is its incredible beauty, the absence of noisy discos that prevent us from spending thoughtful and slow rest on the sand in the company of a refreshing Mojito, poured in half a coconut.

How to get there

You can get to Naithon Beach (Phuket) as follows: drive 4 km south along the road called Thep Krasat Tri Nai Yang. Then at the intersection, turn right. Then, following the signs, after 3.5 km to be in the right place.

Despite the fact that there is no fee for a sunbed at the entrance, you will have to pay 100 THB per day for using an umbrella.

Naithon Beach (photo below) is a pale yellow hue, and the depth of the water begins 20 meters from the coast. In the rainy season, Nai Thon may close for several days. The sea at this time becomes troubled, so it is advisable to postpone the visit to the beach until the high season.

Ideal time to visit

An ideal period for a vacation in Phuket is considered the time from the end of October to mid-April. During these months, the sea is calm and the temperature is not so high (at night +20, during the day +26).

The optimal period for outdoor activities is considered to be the beginning of May and the end of October. The southwestern monsoons cause tropical downpours and turbulent sea waves.

Despite this, the distribution of precipitation for the island of Phuket is the same as for the marine subtropical regions. For example, it began to rain in the evening without a break, but after a day everything is dry. This weather lasts 2-3 days, until the next showers. Therefore, making predictions for Phuket is quite difficult.

The beaches of Naithon, Nye Young and Layan

Perhaps the most beautiful beach in Phuket is Bang Tao, where Layan is located in the north. The area is considered a nature reserve, so the construction of buildings is under strict control. Layan is ideal for day trips: the beach is secluded and very calm. Tourists recommend it to couples with children and elderly people who want to stay away from noisy parties and resort entertainment.





In the northern part of Phuket, near the airport, Nai Yang is located. The beach is considered part of the Sirinat National Park. In the period from November to February for several centuries turtles sailed for laying eggs to the shore of the Nai Yang. But, despite the good security of the territory, in recent years, marine inhabitants appear here less and less.

Tourism infrastructure is poorly developed. On the beach there is only one restaurant, several hotels and guest houses for rent.





Nai Yang is suitable for a relaxing holiday. A big plus is the proximity to the airport (5 minutes by car), which is very convenient for families with children.

How not to spoil your vacation in Thailand: 10 rules from experienced travelers

Like it or not, tourism is a delicate matter. Arriving in a particular country, you need to know the basic rules of conduct and etiquette of the visited place. Especially when it comes to Asia.

Thailand is considered to be a country of smiles, sweet bananas and a warm sea. At first glance, everything seems simple: I paid a ticket, arrived and went to rest. But even here, in order not to get into trouble, you need to follow the principles of behavior. Here is a list of rules that will help not spoil your planned vacation:

  1. Always carry a copy of the insurance policy and identification document.
  2. Respect the royal family.
  3. Be attentive to the national religion.
  4. Take care of your health in advance and buy all the necessary medicines.
  5. In no case do not take out the image of the Buddha from the country - it is forbidden.
  6. Be vigilant and pay attention to the safety of documents.
  7. Left-hand traffic. Therefore, crossing the road, you need to look first to the right, then to the left.
  8. Be careful with strangers.
  9. When purchasing tourist goods (excursions), check the quality certificate.
  10. Be as knowledgeable as possible. Before going to Thailand (applies to any Asian country), study the rules of passport control, exchange rate, rules of conduct and etiquette.

Following the above rules, relax without incident and negative emotions.
