Oasis Hotel, Adler: description, services, reviews

Everything necessary for the pleasure and relaxation of tourists has the hotel "Oasis". Adler, as one of the most popular domestic resorts, will delight travelers with the hot sun and quality service.





Location

This institution is located near Sochi. Oasis Hotel (Adler) is located on the beach, just a 1-minute walk from the beach. The main promenade with its many cafes and shops can be reached in just 5 minutes. The distance from the bus station is 8 km. Also, while relaxing in Hotel Oasis, you can visit the following facilities:

  • aquarium (600 meters);
  • dolphinarium (1 km);
  • water park (2 km);
  • natural biosphere reserve (7 km);
  • Green Theater (19 km);
  • Riviera Park (19 km).




Accommodation

Comfortable living conditions are provided by the guests of the Oasis Hotel. Adler will provide you with a warm welcome, and in this hotel you can choose these options for accommodation:

  • The standard room is compact in size and has a cozy atmosphere. The rooms can be equipped with large or twin beds. There is also a desk and a wardrobe.
  • Comfort is a spacious room decorated in a luxurious classic style. This is a large bedroom with a French bed, a workplace, a wardrobe and a seating area. In the hallway there is a hanger for outerwear and a stand for suitcases. There is access to a large terrace with a magnificent view of the mountains and the sea coast.
  • The suite consists of two spacious rooms. The bedroom has a luxurious French bed, and the living room is equipped with soft leather furniture. There is a private terrace with a beautiful sea view. The bathroom is equipped with both a bath and a shower.
