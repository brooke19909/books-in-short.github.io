Fujairah hotels: a paradise for a relaxing holiday

The Principality of Fujairah is mainly a mountainous region, and it stretches from the city of Dibba to Fujairah, stretching its beaches along the coast for 50 kilometers. In the resorts, almost all hotels in Fujairah work on the principle of All Inclusive, that is, accommodation plus food.





Who needs a vacation in Fujairah

Experienced travel agents recommend families with children to go on vacation to hotels in Fujairah, because there is very little noisy entertainment that lasts all night and they all move indoors after midnight. An unforgettable vacation will be for tourists who can not imagine a day without diving. Ocean waters are a great place for diving and snorkeling, because here you can see sunken ships, corals and bright representatives of marine fauna.

Top Hotels

The top five, according to vacationers, include the best hotels in Fujairah: five-star hotels - Fujairah Rotana Resort & Spa, Le Meridien Al Aqah Beach Resort, The Radisson Blu Fujairah Resort, Iberotel Miramar Al Aqah Beach Resort, and the three-star Sandy Beach Resort. Of course, not all reviews are only good, but that's how they perceive it.





For example, Radisson Blu is a small hotel that offers a relaxing holiday, although there are not enough conditions for very young children (there is no kids club where you could leave a child). There is a diving club with rental equipment for diving and snorkeling. You can watch the life of sea turtles, stingrays, cuttlefish and small sharks.

Miramar is a fairly new hotel, operating since 2007, located near Dibba. The hotel itself is built in Moroccan style. A pleasant surprise - a great Italian restaurant. Large and clean beach. There are places here that are especially popular with divers (Dibba Rock).

Next, the top three beach hotels, all the entertainment in which is concentrated only on the territory. "Rotana Resort" is distinguished by good animation, the presence of a large number of Russians among staff. There is also live music, a kids club, a mini-zoo and a SPA center. There is a deposit - about $ 30 per day.





Le Meridien is a very large hotel, the deposit here is much higher - up to $ 100 per day. The hotel has a volleyball court, tennis courts. For lovers of diving, the area near Turtle Island will be interesting. Wonderful kids club.

Many hotels in Fujairah organize a free shuttle bus to Dubai twice a week. You shouldn‚Äôt be late for the return trip, because then you will have to get to the hotel on your own, and taxi services are not cheap.

Sandy Beach is a no deposit hotel, ideal for families with children.

Cost of rest

Hotels in Fujairah, accommodation prices are set depending on the "star" and location. There are about ten 4-5 * hotels in the emirate , several good three-star hotels, and there are motels and hostels.

In the hostel, the place will cost $ 25-30 dollars. But to relax here on your own is no more profitable than a package trip. A trip for a week with a stay at a 3 * hotel in Fujairah will cost from $ 650. The price includes airfare, transfer, hotel stay and meals.

The average cost of a ticket to the resorts of the emirates: Sharjah - $ 900-950, Abu Dhabi - $ 1000-1100, Dubai - $ 1400-1500, Fujairah - $ 900-1000.
