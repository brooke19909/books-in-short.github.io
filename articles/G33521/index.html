Time difference Moscow-Khabarovsk. Road from west to east

Khabarovsk is a large industrial center in the east of our country. The time difference between Moscow and Khabarovsk is very significant, therefore those organizations for which contacts with the capital are important should take it into account in order to be able to plan their work schedule correctly.





About city

Khabarovsk is located 6144 kilometers from the capital of our country. This is a huge distance, therefore it is not at all surprising that there is such a big time difference between Moscow and Khabarovsk. Several time zones are shared by these large settlements, each of which is unique.

The time difference between Moscow and Khabarovsk is huge, as is the distance between these cities. That is why Khabarovsk was built very late - only in 1858, after the question of the border with China was finally resolved. A military post was erected on the right bank of the majestic Amur River, named after the first governor of the city. Erofei Pavlovich so skillfully managed the village that in 1893 he received city status. By the end of the century, in just three decades, a small military post had become a city with a population of 15.5 thousand people. It was much more than in many old provincial cities.

At present, this settlement, although the time difference between Moscow and Khabarovsk is many hours, is a very important transport hub. It connects various flows of air, river and rail transportation. Khabarovsk takes the first place in the entire Far East in the total volume of all transport passing through the city.





How does the Khabarovsk Territory live?

The list of key industries for which the Khabarovsk Territory is famous is quite rich. First of all, it is non-ferrous metallurgy, which is a leading industry. As part of this fishery, tin, lead-zinc ores and tungsten are mined. The time difference between Moscow and the Khabarovsk Territory is not an obstacle for diamond mining. Not the last place is occupied by the fishing, woodworking and forest industries. Far beyond the borders of the region, shipbuilding and aircraft-building enterprises are known that form the basis of machine building in the Khabarovsk Territory.

The problems are the undeveloped reserves of raw materials, exorbitantly high prices for transport services, a shortage of energy resources, territorial remoteness, leading to the breakdown of economic ties, and some other similar difficulties.





Difference in time

So, the time difference between Moscow and Khabarovsk is seven hours. This means that when the population is going to work in Khabarovsk , clubs full of lovers of fun have not yet closed in the capital. And only when dinner ends in the Far East, Muscovites drink morning coffee, going to work.

The hourly difference Moscow-Khabarovsk makes it possible for the Far East to celebrate the holidays seven hours earlier. So the New Year comes to Khabarovsk when not everyone in the capital has cut the salad. It is in the Far East that one of the first salutes in the country begins to be launched.





Travel time

To get to the industrial center of the Far East, although the time difference between Moscow and Khabarovsk is seven hours, there are different ways.

The fastest of them is the plane. In this situation, the road (without the necessary delay at the airports) will take from 7 to 8 hours, depending on the type of aircraft. Thus, if you fly from Khabarovsk to the capital, you will have to leave at the Moscow airport at the same time as you landed on the plane.
