Entry-level smartphone LG E612: feature software and hardware capabilities

A stylish smartphone for every day with a full range of communication interfaces is the LG E612. The characteristics of its software and hardware filling, as well as the capabilities of the device from the perspective of the end user will be discussed in detail later in the text.





Design

A typical representative of the first-generation L-Series device line is the LG E612. The characteristics of this device from a design perspective clearly indicate this. The second code name for this smartphone model is L5. Although the device was positioned as a mid-level gadget, its case is completely made of plastic. Glossy front panel simply attracts dust and dirt. Yes, and damage it is quite simple. To protect the front of the smart phone, you need to glue a special protective film.

The side faces are covered with a special coating that looks like metal. The back cover is made of corrugated plastic. Externally, the smartphone looks larger than its actual size. A similar effect is achieved due to the fact that all the angles are not smoothed, but straight. A 4-inch display is displayed on the front panel. Above it is the earpiece and sensors, below - the usual button control panel. The volume control buttons are displayed on the right side of the device, and the lock is carried out on the upper edge of the gadget. On the back cover is the main camera (top) and a loud speaker (bottom).

Hardware stuffing

A very modest hardware filling is used in the LG E612. The characteristics of his CPU prove it once again. One core, which operates at a frequency of only 0.8 GHz, speaks for itself. More specifically, this device uses Qualcomm‚Äôs Snapdragon S1 chip, which is based on the outdated A5 processor architecture. The RAM in it is 512 MB, and the capacity of the internal drive is 2 GB (about half of them are occupied by the operating system and pre-installed software). The developers did not forget to equip the device with an expansion slot for installing an external flash card. Its maximum volume in this case can be 32 GB.





Screen, camera and graphics accelerator

The diagonal of the display installed in this unit is 4 inches. It is based on a typical TFT matrix. The stock of brightness and contrast is not satisfactory. But the characteristics of the phone LG E612 in terms of display resolution cause a lot of criticism. Only 320 pixels by 480 pixels. As a result, the picture on the screen will be grainy, it is not difficult to distinguish a separate pixel on its surface.

The Adreno 200 is used as a graphic adapter in this smartphone. A modest video accelerator that will perfectly cope with most everyday tasks, even today. A modest 5 megapixel camera is used in the LG E612. There is no autofocus and image stabilization system, but it is perfect for panoramic shooting . It is also possible to record video in VGA format.

Autonomy

Not bad with the autonomy of the LG E612. The characteristic of its battery is impressive - 1500 mAh. Add to this a single-core CPU (moreover based on an energy-efficient architecture) and a modest 4-inch display diagonal with a resolution of 320x480. At minimum loads, this device can last 4-5 days on a single battery charge. With an average level of device use, this value will be reduced to 2-3 days. But with the maximum load of the smartphone, the battery life will decrease to 1-2 days.





Characteristics of the device by the owners and results

An excellent entry-level smartphone with good features is the LG E612. The characteristics of its hardware and software parameters clearly indicate this. At the same time, his autonomy is at an acceptable level. This is a great device for those who need a budget class smartphone to solve the most simple tasks for every day.
