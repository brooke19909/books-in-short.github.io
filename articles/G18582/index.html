How to make roses from napkins beautifully?

If guests are coming to you and you want to surprise them with a beautiful table setting, this master class will be 100% useful. If you want to charm a girl you like with an original act, then this lesson is also for you. Well, it's simple, if you are bored, and have nothing to take your hands on, let's learn together how to make roses from napkins.

This is one of the tricks in the arsenal of a novice illusionist, an indispensable way to meet a girl in a cafe or simply decorate your house for the holiday. How to make roses from napkins, and what is needed for this? No matter how trite - just two things: a napkin and your hands. Well, let's get started?



To get a rose from a napkin, a diagram or picture is not needed. Just take a napkin, the best of those that are denser, and unfold it. Then we bend its upper edge a couple of centimeters in a straight line and slightly smooth the bend. We take the resulting workpiece with the left hand by the left corner and clamp it between the index and middle fingers. We begin to make a rose bud. Wrap with your right hand, holding the upper right corner, a napkin counterclockwise around the fingers of your left hand. In this case, the curved edge should be on the outside. We do this a few turns until the napkin runs out. You get a tube, in the upper part of which there will be a bend. We take the free tip of the napkin, which was formed from the bend, and fold it diagonally to get the right triangle. This will be the outer bud of the bud.



We begin the next stage of the lesson on the topic "How to make roses from napkins." We retreat from the edge of the tube with a bend down by 3-4 centimeters. Everything that remains above will be a bud, and everything below will turn into a stem of a rose. At the indentation, we tightly squeeze the napkin with the index and middle fingers. Below the compression point, the napkin needs to start twisting more tightly. The denser the twist, the stronger the stem of the rose. But you should not overdo it either, the napkin is a delicate material, it can tear from too much pressure. Having finished the stem to the middle, we will take up the leaf.

We find the free end of the napkin from below and lift it upward vertically just above the place where the twisting stopped. Now the napkin needs to be squeezed again in the same place, and continue to twist to the end. At the exit, we get a ready-made pretty rose.



Roses from paper napkins can become a decor item on the festive table. Take for their production the same napkins that will be used in serving, or vice versa - of the same quality, but in a contrasting shade and wrap more flowers. You can make a whole multi-colored bouquet - for a children's birthday, for example, this will be a good solution to the central flower arrangement on the table, and at the end of the holiday you can give little guests a rose. And if the celebration will be for adults, it is enough to put such a flower on each plate for each guest when serving, or to wrap a fork or knife with a rose. In general, knowing how to make roses from napkins, you can add a twist to any laid table.
