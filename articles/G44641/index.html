Sardinia (Italy): description, location on a map of Italy, rest and reviews of tourists

In the heart of the Mediterranean Sea lies the beautiful Sardinia. Italy has many beautiful resort sites, but this island is special. Sardinia is isolated from the whole world. This secluded area, in which pristine cleanliness reigns, attracts more and more guests every year. The atmosphere of the island is completely different from the Italian flavor, it is special. Sardinia is an enchanting landscape, rich culture and history, a sea of ​​entertainment, a great place to relax.



Resort Location

Sardinia (Italy) is located in the very center of the Mediterranean Sea, between Corsica and Sicily. The contours of the island resemble a huge sandal, so its name is consonant with the ancient Greek word "sandaliotis". In size, Sardinia is second only to Sicily. It is located only 12 km from Corsica (France), west of mainland Italy. From the Apennine Peninsula to Sardinia, a distance of 200 km.

Climatic features

Every year, about 50 million tourists from all over the world hospitably welcome Italy. The island of Sardinia attracts travelers with a subtropical climate. You can relax here from May to mid-November, during this period excellent conditions for swimming remain. In spring, the temperature is in the range + 17-25 ° , in the beginning of summer - + 20–26 ° , in July the thermometer rises to + 30 ° . August is considered the hottest month, on the island the temperature rises to + 34 ° . In September and October in Sardinia up to + 25 ° .





A bit of island history

Sardinia (Italy) boasts not only snow-white beaches, a transparent emerald sea, bewitching landscapes, but also ancient history. On this earth there are traces of ancient civilizations that lived here 17 thousand years ago. After them, the nuragi — constructions of huge cobblestones — are still preserved. During this period, Europeans still lived in caves and dressed in skins, and the island's masters were excellent engineers, made complex mathematical calculations.

Scientists have not been able to solve the mystery of the nurag, who built them and how. The island is a crossroads of the most ancient civilizations, for centuries experience and knowledge of different peoples have been accumulating here.

Sardinia lives its own life, guarding itself from the whole world. A patriarchal way still reigns here, residents honor traditions, take pride in their culture. Each village boasts its own national costume. But do not think that this is the most abandoned and old-fashioned patch of land that Italy can offer. The island of Sardinia has luxury hotels with a developed infrastructure, the highest service. There are fashion boutiques, incendiary discos, expensive restaurants, and entertainment programs. Nobody will be bored on the island.



South of sardinia

Fans of a peaceful, relaxing holiday are advised to go to the southern part of the island. There are very good long beaches with golden and white fine sand, a turquoise transparent sea. Mostly, couples with children, elderly people, as well as couples in love who seek solitude, mainly go to the south. Sardinia offers luxury 5-star hotels with excellent service and entertainment. Here you can economically and comfortably relax in private apartments, enjoy the beautiful scenery, see local attractions.

The most popular tourists are Cagliari, Santa Margarita di Pula, Villasimus, Kia, Costa Ray and Teulada. Cagliari is suitable for lovers of antiquity, those who are interested in history, archeology. The island in Italy Sardinia boasts many attractions. The administrative center of Cagliari has medieval towers and a castle, narrow streets, ancient ruins, Carthaginian fortifications, an archaeological museum, a Roman amphitheater, a huge number of palaces and churches. And at this resort you can see pink flamingos on the beach.



Santa Margarita di Pula is famous for its sandy beaches, pine and palm groves, clear emerald sea. This resort is suitable for lovers of outdoor activities, because it provides all the conditions for golf, tennis, horse riding and water sports. Kia, Costa Ray, Villasimus, Teulada are suitable for divers, here you can see the sunken ships, get acquainted with the underwater world.

Northern part of the island

Sardinia on a map of Italy takes a decent place, it is the second largest island of the Mediterranean Sea, so there are so many resort places. In the north, a patriarchal way of life has been preserved, but at the same time there are a lot of entertainment centers, developed tourist infrastructure. In this part of the island there is a very beautiful nature, travelers are attracted by the mountain landscape, the aquamarine transparent sea, clean beaches with white fine sand. In the north of Sardinia, the most beautiful and richest resort towns are concentrated.



Isola Rossa is suitable for fishing enthusiasts. The town is located near the fishing village of ancient times. The buildings here are built of red granite. In Isola Rossa, you can rent a boat and go fishing all day. Santa Teresa Gallura is located near Corsica. It is very beautiful here, the azure sea, rocks reminiscent of animal silhouettes amaze the imagination. The resort offers many tours and excursions, here you can rent a yacht, visit the diving school.

The area from Castelsardo to Stintino is dotted with grottoes, caves, snow-white beaches, rocky cliffs, cozy bays. These are incredibly romantic places that attract the largest number of tourists. There are both interesting historical and cultural attractions, as well as entertainment programs, developed infrastructure.



Costa Smeralda

The Emerald Coast is a paradise that Sardinia can offer holidaymakers. Italy (a photo of these places makes you fall in love with the resort at first sight) does everything so that guests feel comfortable, have a good rest, gain strength and new experiences. The Costa Smeralda attracts with luxury, impeccable service, and an excellent entertainment program. It is here that politicians, oligarchs, pop stars, cinema and sports come to rest. Discos, nightclubs, snow-white yachts, expensive boutiques will not let you get bored, and the pristine nature will contribute to relaxation and the resolution of all problems.

Sardinia (Italy) offers all comers a holiday in the most elite resorts in Europe. For example, Porto Cervo is suitable for casual meetings with business partners. In this resort center, you can solve all important matters at cocktail parties in a relaxing environment. Snow-white beaches, a clear sea, a huge number of boutiques, restaurants and shops will not let you get bored. Porto Rotondo is the most fashionable and expensive coastal resort. This is a great place for divers, as well as lovers of luxury hotels. In Bahia Sardinia there is a lot of entertainment, there is a developed tourist area, many restaurants and cafes, there is a water park.

What to do on the island?

Sardinia (Italy) receives positive reviews from travelers. And how else, because the island is almost a paradise on earth. Breathtaking landscapes, clear azure sea, white beaches with fine silky sand, developed infrastructure, high level of service - all this contributes to the fact that people who came here once, come back again and again. Sardinia offers a lot of entertainment for every taste. Active people will be interested in water and horse riding, playing tennis, golf. There are diving schools, yachts and boats for rent. The island has water parks, discos, night clubs, a huge number of excursions are offered. For shoppers, fashion boutiques, souvenir shops, and supermarkets are open. Restaurants and cafes offer a huge number of dishes. Nobody will miss the island for sure.



Sardinian cuisine

Roasted meat with spices, bread, pasta, seafood, cheese, sweets, wines - these are the main dishes that the island is famous for. Sardinia (Italy) for many centuries produced cheeses, there are many types of them. The main role in the kitchen is given to fried meat on fire, usually it is baked whole on a spit with the addition of bay leaf and myrtle. It is definitely worth a trip to the regions of the island and try bread, almost every village has its own recipe for this product.

Sardinia is very fond of sweets, they are usually made from flour, honey, eggs and almonds. Restaurants indulge visitors with sumptuous seafood dishes, and Italians also love pasta seasoned with oysters or sea urchins. While relaxing on the island, you should definitely try the local wines - they are divine.
