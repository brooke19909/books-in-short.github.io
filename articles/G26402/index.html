It should be noted that there is a very rich vegetation, but not only this attracts tourists from all over the world. The fact is that here is an incredibly clean, emerald sea, which attracts all who come. White sand beaches are striking in their cleanliness and well-groomed, but rather, it is a merit of the government, which carefully monitors its resorts. There are also mountains here where you can safely travel. In the mountains are small villages, the locals of which are happy to receive tourists. The coast itself is noteworthy in that there are a large number of cliffs that store many caves. Everyone dreams of visiting them, and everyone is happy to fulfill this desire, having arrived on the island of Zakynthos. The coastline here is about 120 kilometers, so you can safely say that you will find yourself in a quiet paradise on planet Earth, where you can choose a calm and measured rest, as well as more youthful, with discos and dances until the morning. Zakynthos Island - a unique place

These are not big words, but the real truth. Here is a huge marine park, which was created specifically to protect the endangered species of the Mediterranean seal and sea turtles, which are found here almost everywhere. As a tourist, you have a great opportunity to watch these amazing creatures and find turtle masonry on the shore. If you are lucky, then you will meet the Carriage-Carriage - a rare species of turtles. The individuals reach incredibly large sizes, about 3 meters in length and originate from ancient times. The creatures are indeed thousands of years old. This place is perfect for outdoor enthusiasts. After all, everything is provided for here: windsurfing, diving, various water attractions and much more. In the resort of Lagana, an active life is very developed. How to get to about. Zakynthos. And a little about prices

As a rule, most tourists come directly from the capital of Greece - Athens. You will be expected to take about 6 hours by bus, although you need to take a ferry for some time. In total, you will have to pay about 20-25 euros for the road, which, in principle, is not much, but it is only one way. You will arrive at the central bus station, which is located in Zakynthos. From here you will begin your independent journey through the city. In principle, no one forbids you to go around the coastline of the island, which lasts only 120 kilometers. As for the cost of food, they are average for this region and are unlikely to cause you problems.
