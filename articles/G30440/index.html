Why iPhone does not connect to WiFi

So, today we will consider with you situations in which the iPhone does not connect to WiFi. This problem has become quite relevant recently. The reason for this is a lot of reasons that we will meet today. The main thing - do not panic and do not think that the gadget should be thrown away and buy a new one. Let's find out why the iPhone does not connect to WiFi.



Out of range

Let's start with the most common cause of the problem. This is nothing more than finding the gadget out of range of the network. After all, any Wi-Fi has its own radius of "defeat." It is worth deviating from it - and you can lose the Internet. Therefore, users note that the iPhone does not connect to WiFi.

Fortunately, here you can easily and simply fix everything. Let's start by getting closer to the Wi-Fi source. Moreover, so that we are in the zone of "defeat". After that, you need to turn on the Wi-Fi function on the iPhone, and then find the network to which we will connect. Remember: if you are in range, you will soon find the corresponding Wi-Fi name. Click on it and wait a while. That's all, the problems are solved. Now you know what to do if the iPhone does not connect to WiFi. But this is not all possible scenarios. There are a number of more difficult situations. And now we will get to know them all.

Wrong data

Does your iPhone not connect to home WiFi? Then it makes sense to check how accurately and correctly you entered the password for the connection. This scenario, as you might guess, applies only to secure Wi-Fi networks. After all, to join them you have to enter a special secret combination, or simply a password.



Here you can get into two positions. The first is when you yourself own Wi-Fi. In this case, it is enough to double-check the correctness of the entered data, correct all errors and try to connect again. After typos are eliminated, everything will work fine. Well, good. In cases when you are trying to secretly connect to someone else's network, you will have to give up attempts. Anyway, it is very, very difficult to guess the correct password. That's all, the problems are solved. Thus, if the iPhone 4S does not connect to WiFi (or any other model of it), you can work a little with the data entered when connecting. Very often this option works great.

Only not always the situation depends on the password and user login. More often, people face more serious problems when trying to connect any gadget to Wai-Fay. Which ones? Let's try to figure this out.

System crashes

For example, if the iPhone does not connect to WiFi, you can try restarting both gadgets - both the phone and the modem. Sometimes the cause of this behavior can be the most common system failure. This is not the most dangerous, but a very unpleasant situation.



Fortunately, it is eliminated quite easily, quickly and simply. In truth, if you have problems connecting to the Internet, then it is from this point that you can begin to combat the situation. Most users, as a rule, do not attach importance to system crashes and reboots. And in vain. Sometimes only this move can help you fix a situation in which iPhone 4 does not connect to WiFi (or any other gadget model).

But do not think that all the problems will end there. On the contrary, the most acute and large-scale causes of this kind of behavior are just beginning. And now we will get to know them, and also learn to deal with the lack of an Internet connection.

But do not dwell on this point. We have another option in which the iPhone does not connect to WiFi.

Viruses

Of course, with the development of technology, so-called viruses also began to develop. Now they apply not only to computers, but also to smartphones. And it is this type of infection that becomes the source of so many problems with gadgets. Of course, this applies to those models that work with the network, and also have documents downloaded from sites.

In general, it is very easy to diagnose a virus now. For this, as a rule, there are special anti-virus telephone programs. It is possible to eliminate this option of events independently, but it is very difficult. Sometimes such steps are not justified. If you suspect that the iPhone is not connected to WiFi precisely because of virus attacks, then it is better to take your gadget to professionals and explain the situation. They, as a rule, will cope with your problem faster and better.
