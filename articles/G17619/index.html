Appearance

The device looks noticeably attractive to its competitors. "Meizu M5s" is just the case when the company did not touch the premium design, but changed only the set of materials used. In our case, rather high-quality plastic is used instead of metal.

Design Features

Despite the fact that the device has a five-inch screen, it cannot be called large. The available dimensions (144 x 71 x 8 mm) allow you to comfortably place your smartphone in any pocket or small handbag.

The ergonomics of the device also did not disappoint: the gadget fits comfortably in the palm of your hand and does not try to slip out of your hands due to the convenient matte finish. Spraying the case, although high-quality, but users in their reviews about the Meizu M5c smartphone still complain about fingerprints and dust. Of course, to the gloss, which like a vacuum cleaner collects everything and everything is far away, but you will have to wipe the device quite often, especially if you choose dark tones.

Interfaces

From its more noble counterparts, the model is distinguished by the absence of some key controls. Here, alas, you will not see a fingerprint sensor, a duplicate microphone for the noise reduction system and other "chips" from the premium class. But the whole standard set is present.





At the bottom you can see the mirror slots for the speakers, and between them there is a universal USB port. There are no complaints to the latter: it connects all the peripherals, which, in principle, should work with smartphones. But the grilles turned out to be not quite practical. Users in their reviews of the Meizu M5c smartphone have repeatedly complained that dust and dirt are clogging in the holes. At the same time, you can get it out of there only with the help of a needle or small tweezers. Therefore, a "pocket" life for a gadget is not the best option, because the grates will be clogged with small debris.

Interface Features

On the upper edge is a standard 3.5 mm mini-jack audio jack. At the side there is a hybrid interface for working with cards. Their hot swap is not provided, so you have to reboot the device.

The volume rocker and the power key are located on the right side. The buttons move clearly, and they themselves are large and stiff, so they are no problem to the touch. Judging by the reviews on the Meizu M5c smartphone, users did not notice accidental clicks, even in the back pockets of pants and other tight clothing.

Above, on the front side, you can see the usual speaker grill, sensors and the front camera eye. The latter, of course, does not have a flash, and in its place is an LED event indicator.

Under the screen there is an oval-shaped hardware key familiar to Meizu without a fingerprint sensor. It has a touch pad, so gestures and other functionality are available to the user, which is usually the case with other phones with three buttons ("Home", "Back", "Menu"). Screen

The device received an ordinary IPS-matrix with a resolution of 1280 x 720 pixels and a pixel density of 293 ppi. On a five-inch screen, you can see the pixelation, but if you only specifically look closely.





The picture on the output is normal, without critical flaws. Viewing angles have a maximum degree for the IPS matrix, so there will be no problems with viewing content in the company of one or two friends. The data on the screen is more or less readable in the sun, thanks to the anti-reflective coating, but you can‚Äôt talk about comfortable work.

Performance

Smartphone chipsets are based on the proven Mediatek MT6737 series with four cores. The graphic part fell on the shoulders of the Mali-T-720 accelerator.





2 GB of RAM is more than enough for trouble-free operation of the interface, as well as the volume of the internal drive of 16 GB. It is possible to expand the last parameter to 128 GB using third-party SD-cards.

Judging by the reviews on the Meizu M5c smartphone (16 GB), 2 GB RAM is not enough for modern and "heavy" toys, so the graphic part has to be reset to medium or even minimal settings.

In the combined mode (calls, the Internet, messages, games and videos) the device will last all day, but by the evening it will need recharging. If you properly load the device with toys and video in high resolution, then the battery lasts for three hours.
