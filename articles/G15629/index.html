Travel Moscow - Vologda

Many people have never been to Vologda. This is not surprising, since it is not a very popular tourism destination. Nevertheless, a trip to this cozy and sweet city will be interesting to most people. The Moscow-Vologda route may seem very fascinating from the point of view of traveling around Russia. Interesting nature, combined with many cultural monuments and attractions, few can leave anyone indifferent. The article will discuss how to get to Vologda, what type of transport is better to choose for the trip, the Moscow-Vologda train will be described in detail, and some interesting features of the city will be highlighted.





A bit about the city

To begin with, it's worth talking about the city itself and giving its general description. Vologda is a wonderful city, which is the administrative center of the region of the same name. However, his functions do not end there, since he performs not only an important managerial role, but is also a cultural and major scientific center of the region.

The city is located near the capital, which makes the route Moscow - Vologda very convenient for travel. The distance between cities is only about 450 kilometers.





It is truly an ancient city. The first mention occurs in 1147 in one of the annals. It is interesting that now Vologda has the status of one of the most important transport hubs in the north-west of Russia.

It is worth talking about the population of the city. In recent years, it has grown slightly, now it is about 311 thousand people.

Also Vologda is a city in which a large number of unique historical cultural monuments have been preserved. There are more than 200 objects, most of which are under special protection.

Moscow - Vologda: how to get there?

Many people want to go to this wonderful city. After a trip decision has already been made, the following question usually arises: how do you get there? Undoubtedly, everything here depends on a person‚Äôs personal preferences. There are several options for getting to Vologda.

For those who prefer to travel by their own transport, a great option would be traveling on the route Moscow - Vologda by car. The distance is about 465 kilometers. In time it will take a little more than 6 hours. Driving by car is a really good option, as the road passes through other cities, where there is also something to see.





Another way to travel the route Moscow - Vologda is by bus. There are several buses that depart from different points of Moscow: from the metro station Domodedovo, Komsomolskaya and VDNKh. Travel time by bus is about 7-8 hours.

Is it convenient to travel by train?

Another good way to get to this wonderful city is the Vologda - Moscow train. This trip is preferred by most people, as this is one of the most reliable options. Usually trains follow from the Yaroslavl station, run daily several times a day.

The Vologda - Moscow train follows on average from 8 to 9 hours. Such a trip will bring a lot of positive impressions, so many choose this particular mode of transportation. In addition, rail transport is recognized as one of the safest transportation options and is less tiring.





Signature trains - an interesting ride in comfort

Branded trains run in many directions. The Moscow-Vologda direction (a distance of about 465 km) was no exception. The difference between branded trains is that they have a lot of additional services and convenient little things. The time of their departure and arrival is selected in accordance with the most convenient timetable for passengers.

So, now it‚Äôs worth discussing how exactly the branded train Moscow - Vologda differs. Its full name is Vologda Dawns. This train has been carrying passengers for many years. For his long existence, he fell in love with many people and earned their trust due to the comfort and quality of the services provided. You can even say that he is a kind of symbol of Vologda. Recently, many innovations have been added, for example, the composition has increased by 13 new cars, including several reserved seats, several transformer cars, and also there is one SV car.





Train timetable

Now it‚Äôs worth knowing what time he leaves and arrives. As already mentioned, this time in branded trains is selected so as to best meet the needs of people. Therefore, the Vologda Dawns train leaves daily in the evening. At 20:20, he begins his journey from the Yaroslavl station and arrives in Vologda early in the morning, at 05:13.

Of course, you can choose another option - a regular train, not a branded one. In this case, the trip may not be as comfortable, however, the timing becomes a little wider. The first train usually leaves at around 10 a.m., the last at 21:50. On average, 5-6 trains leave from Moscow to Vologda per day.

Ticket categories and their price

After deciding to hit the road, the question usually arises: which ticket to choose? When traveling from Moscow to Vologda, it is possible to choose the most suitable option. There are several categories of tickets: common carriage, reserved seat, coupe and luxury. At the beginning of 2016, the fare in a common carriage is 600-800 rubles, in a reserved seat - 1100-1700 rubles, in a compartment - 1700-3000 rubles, in a suite - 3900-7000 rubles. Thus, everyone can choose for themselves the most convenient way to travel.
