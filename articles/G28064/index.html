Georgia in November: rest, weather, reviews

Nobody needs to talk about the hospitality of Georgians. Therefore, tourists come to this country with pleasure. But some travelers are concerned about the weather factor, they doubt whether they can fully relax in different seasons. Let's talk about what to expect when planning a vacation in Georgia in November, what you can do at this time and what is worth seeing.



Geographical position

Thinking about what Georgia is good or bad in November, it is worth remembering its location on the world map. The country is located in the very center and in the west of Transcaucasia. The state stretched from the Black Sea to the peaks of the Caucasus Mountains. The area of ​​Georgia is slightly less than 70 thousand square meters. km, the coastline is 308 km. Geographical location leads to a wide variety of terrain. From the plateau and low-lying shores of the Black Sea, the territory passes into a hilly area, and then gives way to a mid-mountain and high-mountainous terrain. This affects a wide variety of flora and fauna in the country, where, despite the relatively small size, you can see representatives of various natural zones.



Climate

Fencing off the negative influence of the northern winds by the Greater Caucasus Range creates a comfortable microclimate in Georgia. According to its characteristics, it is close to subtropical and Mediterranean. In the upper reaches of the mountains there is a sharp alpine climate. All the same mountain ranges are the reason that in Georgia there is less rainfall per year than in other countries of the region - an average of about 2000 mm. But in the west there is less rain, and on the Batumi coast - more. The climate of Georgia is characterized by long, warm summers and short mild winters. Winter begins in December and lasts until February, only in mountainous areas does the season last until the end of March, when snow is falling. The average temperature in the Tbilisi region is +7 degrees, in the mountains - about zero. In the highlands in winter, some passes are often closed due to snow. Spring in Georgia is stormy and warm; in March, gardens begin to bloom. The average temperature at this time is +15 degrees. This summer already begins in May, the most heat falls on July-August, when the thermometer on the coast stably holds above the +25 mark. Autumn comes at the end of October and lasts 2 months. Georgia in November is similar to Moscow in early October. Of course, this applies to coastal and central areas. In the mountains, autumn begins at the end of September, and already in November, snow begins to fall here.



Weather in November

Tourists going on a trip are often worried about what weather awaits them at their destination. What is the weather like in Georgia in November? Different. This is one of the most volatile seasons. Here in one day you can visit "at a party" in the summer, winter and autumn. However, in general this is a pleasant and comfortable time, if only because there is very little rainfall. The worst that a tourist can expect is a little rain, fog or wind. The average air temperature during this period is 12-14 degrees Celsius, by the end of the month there may be frosts at night. If you are going to Georgia at this time, you should take care of warm, windproof clothes and shoes - and then any weather will not be scary.



Features of rest

Georgia is famous for the fact that it offers various types of recreation. For curious tourists, the country has a lot of interesting places and attractions. Those who wish to improve their health are offered treatment in the sanatoriums and health centers of the famous Borjomi resort. In addition, there are excellent opportunities for ecological and gastronomic tourism. Therefore, a trip to Georgia in November can be unforgettable, because this country is beautiful. The tourist infrastructure here is well developed; a diverse hotel base at affordable prices is at the disposal of tourists. And the hospitality and hospitality of this people have long become a legend.



sights

Organized tours to Georgia in November allow you to see the most important places in the country. But this region is very convenient for independent trips. In this case, you need to make a route to the main attractions, which include:

1. Old Tbilisi. Usually all trips start from the capital, and here you need to find time for a leisurely walk along the old streets and quarters, where the spirit of antiquity has been preserved, here you must definitely see the Abanotubani complex.

2. New Tbilisi. The uniqueness of the Georgian capital lies in the combination of the past and the future. Modern architecture in the area of ​​the Ministry of Justice is striking in its futurism.

3. Jvari Monastery. This ancient building recalls the poem by M. Lermontov “Mtsyri”. The building stands on top of a mountain and fits very effectively into a unique landscape.

4. Cave monastery Vardzia. The amazing place in which the monks used to live impresses with its grandeur and scope.

Georgia has many ancient castles and cathedrals worthy of attention, including the ancient city of Mtskheta, which is almost 2000 years old.



What to do in November

In the country at any season there is always something to do. Georgia in November is ideal for walking, tasting the famous Georgian wines and dishes of national cuisine. For tourists, tours to the wineries are specially arranged, where you can get acquainted with the features of the production of drinks and try different varieties. In Tbilisi, you must definitely choose a time to visit the sulfur baths, where you can take baths from hot mineral springs.

What to see

Georgia in November is truly beautiful. This is an excellent time to explore the many natural attractions of the region. First of all, it is Kazbek, against which tourists are always photographed. Or the Makhuntseti waterfall, in which you must wash your face, and then admire the views, the bridge and the power of water pouring from a 20-meter height. In the mountains of Georgia there are many caves, some of which are open for tourists to visit. You can also visit the mineral springs, which are located in the famous resort cities of the country.

Tourists reviews

Recently, the country has become increasingly popular among Russian tourists. They talk about the fact that the weather in Georgia in November is ideal for exploring the history and traditions of the country. There is already no heat, there is not a lot of rainfall, and you can safely travel through small towns, sightseeing. Travelers note that at this time the number of tourists decreases, so the price of accommodation and meals are falling. And of course, people are grateful to the locals for their cordiality, friendliness and hospitality.
