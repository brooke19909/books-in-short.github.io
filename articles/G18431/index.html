Hotel Iberostar Safira Palms 4 * (Tunisia, Djerba): photos and reviews of tourists

The popularity of Tunisia among Russian tourists is growing every year. It has become a great alternative to Egypt, because the country is not far from each other. In Tunisia, of course, there are no pyramids, but it can offer travelers the monuments of ancient Carthage. Choosing an inexpensive, but high-quality hotel, tourists often choose the Iberostar Safira Palms 4 * complex (Djerba). What can he offer vacationers?

general information

The tourist complex was built in 2005. It consists of three low buildings, which are located near a large pool. The rooms are designed for small families, adult couples or the elderly. In total, about 300 apartments are located in the buildings. The hotel accepts children of all ages. Check-in with animals, on the contrary, is prohibited even for a fee. It is worth knowing that the staff of the Iberostar Safira Palms 4 * hotel (Tunisia) does not speak Russian well. Communication in English or French is much more preferable for them. Check-in here, as in other hotels, starts at 14:00. Guests should leave their rooms after a rest no later than noon.





The favorable location of the complex attracts many tourists. It is built next to a public beach. You can reach it in about 5 minutes. The nearest village, the resort of Zarzis, is 12 km from the hotel. Guests usually go there by taxi, private car or public transport. There you can visit the oriental market, book an excursion, eat at local restaurants and bars. Djerba Airport is 50 km away. It takes 1 hour to get to the hotel. The complex organizes a shuttle service for its guests, but only at an additional cost.

Number of rooms

Hotel Iberostar Safira Palms 4 * (Tunisia, Djerba) offers its guests 302 comfortable rooms. Standard apartments are designed for two adults and a child. Most of them consist of a bedroom and a separate bathroom. The room has not only a bed, but also a small living area. All rooms have a private small balcony. It has a dining table and chairs, as well as a clothes dryer. The hotel overlooks the pool and the garden surrounding it. Instead of balconies, on the ground floor there are terraces of a similar size. Guests can choose between single or double beds.





The complex welcomes tourists with disabilities. They are allocated 3 special rooms that are equipped with all necessary amenities. The doorways here are wider than in ordinary rooms, and the bathroom has a special shower and washbasin. For large families, the hotel offers 22 connecting rooms connected by an internal door. The standard apartments are 37 square meters. m

Room features

Each room of the Iberostar Safira Palms 4 * hotel (Tunisia, Djerba) is equipped with all the amenities that guests may need throughout their vacation. In addition to a set of furniture, the room has a small flat-screen TV. Many satellite channels are connected to it, some of which are intended for Russian tourists. All rooms are connected to a common central air conditioner. He works here from June to October, when the hottest weather is in Tunisia. Room temperature is regulated by hotel staff.

Tourists can leave their valuables in a safe, which is available for a fee. To contact the administration, you can use a direct phone. It can also be used to make paid international calls. The guest bathroom has clean towels, a hairdryer and a set of household chemicals. The bedroom also has a small fridge. An electric kettle is available upon request. It is worth knowing that the rooms do not have internet. Wi-Fi cannot be connected even for an additional fee.

Supply system

For its guests, the complex offers an all-inclusive concept. It includes not only basic meals, but also local alcoholic beverages. The system works every day until 23:00. Get alcohol after this time can only be paid. Breakfast, lunch and dinner are served in the main restaurant of the Iberostar Safira Palms 4 * hotel (Tunisia, Djerba). All meals are served at the buffet. The menu includes both European and local food.





In addition to the main restaurant, the hotel also has other catering facilities. So, light snacks, alcohol, cocktails and ice cream tourists can take in the pool bar. The beach cafe serves only non-alcoholic drinks (juices and mineral water), as well as fruits, pizza and pastries. Guests can have a snack in the lobby bar between lunch and dinner. It offers tea, coffee, soft drinks, sandwiches and desserts. During discos, there is an American bar in which visitors can purchase alcohol, snacks and tobacco products for a fee.

Infrastructure

A variety of infrastructure facilities can be offered to its guests by the Iberostar Safira Palms 4 * hotel. Reviews about the services tourists most often leave positive. For example, they note that there is a car park on the territory of the complex. Here, guests can leave their car for free without prior reservation. For independent travel around the country, tourists can rent a car. Local doctor services are available upon request. It is worth knowing that his visit is usually not included in the insurance. Wi-Fi is available in the lobby. You can connect to it for free.





Reception is open 24 hours a day. The hotel administrator will help you quickly solve the problems. Here you can exchange currency or take your things to the laundry. Luggage storage is at the reception. Tourists leave their suitcases here so that they do not take up space in the room. For an additional fee, a small beauty salon and a hairdresser are open for guests.

Entertainment Organization

Leisure activities at the Iberostar Safira Palms 4 * hotel (Tunisia, Djerba) are also given great attention. For tourists, both active and calm entertainment are presented here. A shared freshwater pool is open daily until 18:00. For everyone, it also provides water aerobics classes. Sun beds and umbrellas on the terrace are free of charge. In bad weather, tourists can visit the small heated indoor pool. A team of animators works day and night. They hold entertainment and sports events, as well as thematic programs dedicated to the culture of Tunisia.





There is a water entertainment center on the beach. Here guests can rent windsurfing equipment, ride a catamaran, canoe and water ski. Animators organize common volleyball and water polo games. A motor boat can be rented for a fee. The hotel has a spa center that offers tourists to undergo wellness treatments, visit the massage room, jacuzzi, sauna or Turkish bath. You can play sports in the gym or play basketball or tennis on specially equipped playgrounds. Archery lessons are also available for a fee.

Children's holiday

The tourist complex Iberostar Safira Palms 4 * (Tunisia, Djerba) is focused on a relaxing holiday for the whole family. For children there is a private shallow pool. You can also leave the kids in the mini-club, which is open daily from morning to evening. Experienced animators conduct sports contests, quizzes and entertainment events for children. For the youngest guests you can call a nanny. She works around the clock and takes an hourly rate. Tourists can always ask for a baby crib in their room, as well as use the high chairs for feeding in the hotel restaurant.





Positive reviews

Often, tourists, choosing a hotel, turn their attention to the opinions of its previous guests. About the complex Iberostar Safira Palms 4 * reviews often leave good. Its guests note the following advantages of relaxation here:

  • Pleasant and well-mannered staff. Animators are not imposed, and the administrator is always trying to help.
  • The hotel mainly rests elderly tourists from Europe, so in the evening and at night it is always quiet and calm.
  • Small but clean and well maintained hotel area. In the evening, it is pleasant to stroll through the cozy garden.
  • The cleaners are regularly carefully cleaned in the rooms. Towels and linen are changed in a timely manner.
  • Good food. The restaurant always serves a lot of fresh fruit, several types of meat (beef, chicken, lamb), sometimes there is fish. For children, a special children's menu has been developed, which includes cereals and yoghurts.
  • The sandy beach is tidy, and the entrance to the sea is smooth and comfortable without stones at the bottom.




Negative reviews

A small number of negative opinions are received by the Iberostar Safira Palms 4 * hotel. Reviews in 2016 note that the service of guests here has noticeably worsened. Tourists noted the following disadvantages of the complex:

  • The hotel flies a huge number of flies, but most of them in the restaurant. Guests recommend buying an insect repellent in advance.
  • The restaurant serves food on poorly washed dishes. Teaspoons give out and at all plastic. The room smells unpleasant.
  • Guides constantly impose visits to excursions, but do not help with the solution of problems.
  • The rooms smell damp. The cleaners do not wash the floors, so there is a lot of sand left on them.
  • The hotel does not have elevators, so tourists with sore feet are best to choose the rooms on the ground floor.
  • There is no infrastructure near the complex. Behind its territory, a wasteland begins, where a lot of garbage is lying.
  • There are jellyfish in the sea. Sometimes floating algae or other debris can be found in the water.

conclusions

Iberostar Safira Palms 4 * hotel can be recommended to tourists who are looking for a place for a relaxing holiday. Most often, elderly people or families with children stay here. There are no nightclubs and bars nearby, so youth companies should look for another complex that is closer to the central part of the resort. Tourists note that the cost of living here is consistent with local service. The hotel itself deserves its four-star status.
