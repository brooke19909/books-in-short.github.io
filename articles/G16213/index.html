Cheap hotels in Yekaterinburg: overview, description and reviews of tourists

In many cities, hotels are widely represented by both budget and luxury rooms, which, by the way, are approximately equally popular. Yekaterinburg was no exception.



Basically, the budget for living is determined by various factors. For example, stardom. Inexpensive hotels in Yekaterinburg start from three stars or less. These are hotels that offer their guests a minimal range of services.

Cheap hotels in Yekaterinburg also suggest that you sacrifice a central location. Although, on the other hand, in the capital of the Urals there is a well-developed transport network and metro, therefore, you will not lose much.

And the third option, which is possible for those who are interested in cheap hotels in Yekaterinburg, is hostels and mini-hotels. The latter are either in small rooms, or in former apartments and in the list of services have the necessary minimum set.
