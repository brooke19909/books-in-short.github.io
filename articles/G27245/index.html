Setun River Valley: description and features

Setun River is one of the few nature reserves in the Moscow Region. Its length is so great that in some part the river captures the capital itself. It is worth noting that most of the reserve is located in the city. This fact became the reason that on its outskirts there are all kinds of buildings, both residential and domestic. The Setun River Valley, a photo of which can be seen in this article, will be described below.



Nature reserve

The territory of the reserve is quite diverse for the presence of not only the plant, but also the animal world. The forest belt has at its disposal both ordinary plants and those that, due to extinction, were listed in the Red Book. Unfortunately, these plants were able to survive only on the territory of the reserve itself and were able to continue to exist only along the banks of the river.

The variety of tree species, which the Setun River Valley is so rich in, cannot but impress. Oak, ash, maple and many others that can be found both in the city itself and in the reserve. But there are also those who cannot be seen anywhere else. The reserve also has architectural and historical buildings.

Special views of the plant world

Do not forget about the herbs located on the territory of the Setun River Valley. Here you can find a sufficient variety of plants, which are often used in folk medicine. The herbs used in the treatment of cardiovascular diseases are especially popular. The total number of plant species is more than 380. And most of the vegetation belongs to the category of healing.



Animal world

The wildlife of the Setun River Valley is also diverse. All kinds of creatures living on earth could themselves safely find shelter here. Here you can meet an ermine, affection, a ferret. Closer to the water live muskrat and kutor. You can also meet other representatives here, of which about 40 are considered rare and are protected by the state. If you try to count all the species that live in the reserve, this figure will almost reach 100.

Amphibians, birds, herbivores - there is no reliable information about the exact amount. But in order to understand the scale - there are more than 69 species of birds alone, not to mention all the others. Do not forget about the vertebrates, which became the Setun River Valley home. There are more than 5 species. Due to the fact that hunting in the reserve is prohibited, many animals were able to maintain their appearance only here.

sights

The uniqueness of this place is not only in the plant and animal world. Here you can admire the remains of ancient settlements and the monuments of various cultures and peoples. By studying them, many new facts and historical events are discovered. The buildings themselves, which are not only historical, but also spiritual, can transfer vacationers to a completely different era.

In fine weather, local residents and guests of the city often stroll through the old manor parks: Troekurovo, Spasskoye and Troetsk-Golenishchevo. Various excursions are organized here, thereby allowing you to plunge into centuries-old memories.

Setun River Valley also has various ancient-built churches. This natural park is very popular and famous among people of any age categories, and not only representatives of the local population like to be here. Both children and older people come on excursions and vacations.



Relaxation

Tourists here expect massive places for recreation, sports grounds, as well as equipped beaches with the ability to take water treatments and sunbathe. All these conditions are unlikely to let vacationers get bored from idleness, being in a place like the Setun River Valley.
