iPad Mini: can I make calls with FaceTime?

Without a doubt, FaceTime is the easiest way to make a phone call using the video conferencing software that comes with your iPad. This application uses your Apple-ID to make a phone call for everyone who also has the specified identifier. Thus, you can communicate with any owners of the iPhone, iPad, iPod Touch or Mac. These calls are free, so even if you use your iPhone, you won‚Äôt waste your minutes of conversation. You can even make FaceTime calls with only the email address associated with your Apple ID.

What is needed for this

When answering the question of whether it is possible to make calls from the iPad Mini, you should remember the basic FaceTime requirement. In order to contact someone, you must have either his ID or email in the contact list. The program uses these contacts in the same way as standard numbers use telephone numbers.

After you launch the FaceTime application, a screen will appear showing the front camera in operation . On the right side of the screen is a contact list that you will use to select who to contact. If the person you want to call is not in the list, you can add him there by clicking the plus sign button located in the upper right corner.





Everyone knows Skype

Skype is the most popular way to make calls over the Internet, and unlike FaceTime, it does not limit people to using the device on iOS. Therefore, speaking of whether it is possible to make calls from the iPad Mini using this application, it should be noted that this is the most universal service.

Installing Skype on an iPad is a relatively simple process. First you need to download this program to your device. Unlike FaceTime, there is a payment associated with making calls via Skype, but calls to users of the program and from them are free. Thus, you will only pay for a call to a subscriber who does not use Skype.

Following the instructions on how to make calls from the iPad Mini, remember that your gadget must be prepared to install the application. You have to check two things: firstly, your input and output speaker - you can use the built-in equipment or connect a headset via Bluetooth to it. Secondly, you need to provide a good Internet connection.



Talkatone - a little-known way

FaceTime and Skype are the main services because they offer significant benefits. However, FaceTime works only with other users of this application, while Skype can make a call to any subscriber (however, a free call is possible for other Skype users). Can I make calls with the iPad Mini in other ways? Theoretically, there is such an opportunity.

So, to use this method, you need to download and install two applications on the iPad - Talkatone and Google Voice. In the future, you will need to follow the Talkatone instructions in order to create your Google Voice and subsequently make calls from your iPad Mini. As a bonus, this app can also connect with friends on Facebook.
