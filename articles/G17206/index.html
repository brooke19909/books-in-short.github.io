A fabulous vacation in Jamaica: reviews, climate, cuisine, attractions

Caribbean islands attract a huge number of tourists. One of the largest islands of the archipelago is Jamaica. He is familiar to us from numerous songs, legendary football matches, fascinating novels ... And adventure lovers this island is also known as one of the most interesting and amazing places of recreation. The nature of Jamaica is very beautiful. Rocky mountains, flat rainforests, golden and white sandy beaches - all this will remember the rest in Jamaica. Reviews about it are extremely positive. However, you yourself will see this if you read our article. So what can this ancient haven of pirates have to offer us?



Rest in Jamaica: prices 2013

Holidays here are not cheap, but the quality justifies the price. The currency is the Jamaican dollar (by the way, it is much cheaper than the US dollar). In large stores located in cities, prices are fixed, but in shops, small pavilions and markets you can safely bargain. Local cuisine is a mixture of several traditions. Particularly popular are pork, fish and vegetables. As for poultry, almost every corner sells a special grilled chicken. It is pickled in a special hot sauce and fried on the coals of the Jamaican tree. You should definitely try local dishes if you have chosen a vacation in Jamaica. Reviews show that the local cuisine is pleased with the tasty, rich, albeit unusual because of the abundance of crouching.



Local resorts

The island welcomes tourists with three resorts. The first of these, Negril, offers, so to speak, a "loose" vacation in Jamaica. Reviews from tourists indicate that Negril is a place for parties, rum and unbridled fun. The beaches of the resort are covered with white sands. Here you can see the ruins of old forts, which have existed since Jamaica was an English colony.

Ocho Rios is truly a paradise. Many villages scattered along the azure coast, where local residents will gladly feed you fresh fish and offer beautiful souvenirs. The capes and bays make the beaches especially attractive, and the footage shot in this picturesque area will remind you of a wonderful vacation for a long time. The entire resort is surrounded by a chain of rivers and mountains, and the waterfalls here are beautiful.

Montego Bay is not so big, but very lively. Here, a gorgeous selection of bars and restaurants, the nightlife is in full swing. But the main thing is a luxurious underwater park for diving enthusiasts. If you are not a passive lounger, visit Montego Bay. This will be a truly active vacation in Jamaica, reviews of which set in a positive mood.

We relax culturally, or What sights does the famous island boast of?

Are you interested in a cultural holiday in Jamaica? Reviews of tourists relate mainly to such attractions of the island as the Bob Marley Museum, St. Catherine's Cathedral, and the National Dance Theater.

All these cultural activities are concentrated in the capital - Kingston. This city has existed not so long ago, but it gives the impression of, if not old, then just old. And all due to the fact that the streets of the city are quite poor and groomed. Only one part of it is like a piece of any European capital, but the whole south is ancient, unreconstructed houses.

Climatic conditions

The climate of Jamaica fully disposes to rest. It is hot in summer and winter, the average air temperature is 26 degrees, the water is about the same. In mountainous areas it is slightly cooler: in summer, the average temperature is 20 ¬∞ C, and in winter it drops to 15 ¬∞ C. The wettest months are May and October, but even heavy tropical rains will not interfere with relaxation, since they are not too long. On the contrary, such weather even helps withstand the heat.
