Renaissance Phuket Resort & Spa 5 *, Thailand, Phuket: hotel description, reviews

Renaissance Phuket Resort & Spa 5 * refers to the well-known chain of resort hotels, which can be considered a guarantee of an unforgettable vacation and quality service.





Short description of the resort complex

Resort Renaissance Phuket Resort & Spa 5 * specializes in providing spa services, but this is not limited to the list of its services for tourists. In addition to the large and clean beach, you can also use the well-kept pool to swim in plenty in the refreshing water. Your attention is presented with many opportunities for sports, including a modern gym. You can eat and taste delicious dishes in one of 5 luxurious restaurants.

Visiting the Renaissance Phuket Resort & Spa 5 * hotel, be sure to use the services of its spa center. Here you can order the best cosmetic procedures for both the face and the whole body. In addition, you can undergo a massage session that aims to relieve physical stress or to improve the health of the body or its individual systems. Complete the set of procedures is in the hot tub with aromatic salts.

Restaurants - this is one of the main advantages of the resort complex Renaissance Phuket Resort & Spa 5 *. To be sure that your dishes are prepared only from the best products, you have the opportunity to choose them yourself. You will be offered not only local dishes, but also culinary masterpieces of other nations of the world. If you follow the figure, you can order a light snack in one of the hotel bars, where a wide range of drinks is also presented.





Resort Location

The hotel is located on the shores of a pristine beach, which is called Macau Beach. This place is located in a fairly remote area from the city center, which guarantees you peace and quiet atmosphere. The liner, the next flight from Moscow to Phuket, arrives at the international airport, which is located at a distance of about 16 km from this resort and hotel complex.





Hotel guest rooms

Having made a tiring flight on a flight Moscow - Phuket, tourists, quite naturally, want to quickly get into their room and relax. For resettlement, travelers can be provided with the following comfortable options:

  • The majority of the rooms in this hotel are standard rooms, which differ in compact territory, which does no harm to comfort. There is a double or a pair of single beds, as well as many amenities to make guests feel comfortable.
  • Suite apartments offer you two spacious rooms. The bedroom will comfortably accommodate two adult guests. A soft folding sofa located in the living room may well serve as an extra bed for a child or another tourist.
  • Deluxe rooms can offer travelers double and triple occupancy. In their characteristics, they are similar to apartments of the "standard" category, but the interior of these rooms is more refined and has many decorative elements in a traditional style.
  • The villas are located in close proximity to the beach. It is possible to settle in a small one-room chalet, which has its own pool. For family vacations or large companies, a three-bedroom villa is ideal, in which each bedroom has a separate bathroom.




What is provided in the rooms

The Renaissance Phuket Resort & Spa 5 * hotel provides for the following amenities in the guest rooms:

  • a complete set of modern orthopedic furniture made of hypoallergenic materials;
  • a modern TV with a wide and flat screen, to which a large package of cable channels is connected;
  • it is possible to play discs through the player;
  • free wireless internet is available in all rooms, as well as in every room;
  • the apartments have a refrigerator, in which a separate compartment is provided for a minibar;
  • individual air conditioning will make it possible to bring the microclimate in the room in full accordance with your preferences;
  • the phone will allow you to call the reception at any time, as well as use the international telephone line;
  • You can always make hot drinks thanks to the electric kettle and coffee maker in your room;
  • the bathroom is equipped with a toilet, bath or shower, as well as a hairdryer;
  • the bathroom provides a full range of cosmetics and bath accessories.




Positive hotel reviews

The resort hotel Renaissance Phuket Resort & Spa 5 * is well-deservedly used by tourists. Reviews about this institution are full of thanks, vivid impressions and the most positive comments:

  • the hotel has a huge clean and well-kept beach with a huge number of sun loungers, and palm trees and other tropical trees create a shadow on it;
  • so that jellyfish do not interfere with hotel guests enjoying a swim in the sea, employees are caught daily with special equipment;
  • all rooms are very clean, with new furniture, good repair, as well as good plumbing and lots of electronics;
  • food at the hotel is beyond praise - a huge selection of dishes and products, there is even a special menu for those who adhere to vegetarianism (by the way, the food is no less tasty and nutritious);
  • very friendly staff who know their duties well and do an excellent job of fulfilling them;
  • one of the main advantages of the hotel is its spa: the choice of procedures is quite solid, and the masters work at the highest level.

Negative hotel reviews

Renaissance Phuket Resort & Spa 5 * (Phuket), in addition to positive feedback, also received some comments from guests related to the quality of service and some shortcomings in the organization of the hotel complex:

  • despite the fact that the hotel positions itself as quiet, it is quite noisy here, especially in the pool area;
  • in some rooms, the sounds of ventilation work greatly interfere;
  • the children's pool, for unknown reasons, is deep enough, which gives parents a lot of inconvenience;
  • The hotel is located far from urban civilization.
