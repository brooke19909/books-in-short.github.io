Amphora Beach Resort Suites 4: services offered



The city of Paphos is one of the favorite and most popular resort areas in Cyprus. Its distinctive features are the abundance of architectural and cultural-historical structures. The resort is famous for its sandy beaches, the emerald Mediterranean Sea, numerous cafeterias and, of course, luxurious hotel complexes for various incomes.

Constantly at the resort, "growing" all new hotels offering the best value for money. One of these guest houses is the modern Amphora Beach Resort Suites 4, built in 2013. It is located half an hour from the international airport. Upon arrival in the city, you will be met by the hotel staff and delivered absolutely free of charge right to the door of the complex.

The bride and groom will enjoy a pleasant surprise in the room in the form of sweets and flowers. In this place couples with small children and elderly people will like to relax. Due to the fairly close location of the beach and external infrastructure, you can quickly get to the desired object.

It should be noted that the Amphora Beach Resort Suites was not so long ago built, so the territory is not as planted with vegetation as we would like. But the landscape design of the house territory is simply designed perfectly - there is nothing to complain about. Everywhere is clean, well-groomed, there are paths to residential buildings. Another significant advantage of the hotel is the availability of free internet and parking.

Number of rooms





Amphora Beach Resort Suites 4 has several Greek-style three-story buildings, complete with balconies overlooking the blue sea. They have tidy rooms with private facilities, decorated in soothing colors. Even the standard rooms have air conditioning and LCD TVs with cable channels.

A direct dial telephone and safe are included. On the day of check-in, a set of towels and hygiene products (shampoo, gel, washcloth) is issued. For the discerning tourist - superior apartments. This room includes an hour-long excursion program (hiking and horseback riding).

Nutrition





The payment includes breakfast and lunch or dinner, if desired (you can choose). In the mornings, a varied American breakfast awaits you, while lunch is served in buffet style. Dinner (of course, for a fee) can be in the bar or in one of the five restaurants of the Amphora Beach Resort 4 hotel (Cyprus).

According to holidaymakers, all dishes are fresh, high-quality, prepared by skilled hands of professional chefs. Even there is always something to feed the children. For those on a diet, a separate menu with lean foods is offered. If you want to enjoy the delicacies of national or international cuisine - come to the restaurant. Admirers of elite alcohol are advised to visit the lounge bar. The second bar is open seasonally by the pool.

Coast

Amphora Beach Resort Suites 4 is steps away from a private beach. The coastline is still being ennobled. There you will find a complete set of beach accessories, including towels, all for free. There is a water entertainment center. You can rent fishing equipment and go fishing. There is a municipal beach nearby.

Sports and Entertainment





Near the residential buildings there are several outdoor pools, in one of them there is a children's section and a hydromassage. In cold weather, there is an indoor pool (heated in winter). Comfortable sunbeds are built for sunbathing.

Those who are tired of passive relaxation are invited to go to the golf course, which is a 20-minute drive from Amphora Beach Resort 4. You can visit the gym or sports grounds. And you can rent a bike and just ride along the promenade, breathe in the fresh sea air and enjoy the local scenery.

Beauty and hairdressing salons are available. There is a rental car, exchange office and a conference room with a banquet room. For kids and teens, Amphora Beach Resort Suites 4 offers a playground and a development club. Very young visitors are given high chairs in a restaurant. Cots are available upon request.

In conclusion, I want to say only positive words about this hotel. There are no old buildings and dilapidated rooms, everything is new and beautiful. At the reception work Russian-speaking staff, ready to answer any questions with a smile and respect.
