All about the best skating rink in Krasnodar - the Oz Molla skating rink (Krasnodar)

Ice skating is one of the most common outdoor activities. Both children and adults will agree to glide on ice. Fortunately, in most cities of our country a large selection of ice rinks. Krasnodar is no exception. It is considered to be one of the best in the city of Krasnodar skating rink "Oz Molla" (Krasnodar).

About SkyIce Rink

This skating rink, whose area is about 1 thousand square meters. m, located on the first floor of the Oz Mall shopping and entertainment center. It differs in irregular shape - not oval, like usual rollers. Directly on the ice there is a zone with a place for sitting, where you can relax a bit without taking off your skates and without stepping off the ice. The skating rink is located a little in the recess and is surrounded by a glass fence, near which, with elbows, spectators of the shopping center often gather. By the way, you can watch from any floor, as the skating rink is visible from each floor of the shopping center. SkyIce operates year-round, not just in the winter season.

Skating rink services

Unlike other skating rinks in Krasnodar, the Oz Molla skating rink (Krasnodar) has certain features - you can save money by skating on your own. It also provides special supportive simulators for children who are still uncertainly skating, and you can also pay for classes with experienced trainers.

Contact Information

Schedule of skating rink in the Oz Mall in Krasnodar:

  • works from ten in the morning until ten in the evening;
  • weekdays there is one technical break, the beginning of which is at 17:00, a break of 40 minutes lasts;
