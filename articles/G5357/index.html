Chrispy World Hotel & Resort 4 * (Greece, Kolimvari): description, service, reviews

This resort village is conveniently located on the shore of a spacious bay at the foot of Cape Rodopos, 24 km from Chania (the westernmost point of the bay of the same name). The population of Kolimbari (Crete) is 5500 people. Many travelers are amazed by the fact that, despite the rapid development of tourism on the north coast, the resort managed to maintain its special charm.

Kolymbari is famous for its fish restaurants and a beautiful spacious pebble beach with crystal clear water. In the vicinity of the village there are endless olive groves, in this area the production of first-class wine and olive oil is established. Tourists who have chosen Kolymbari as their vacation spot fall under the charm of the local cozy streets and magnificent architecture, original cafenions, a bay with many fishing boats and a small port. In the village you will not find noisy nightclubs, there are no crowds of tourists, those who value relaxation and silence choose this place for relaxation.

The village, characterized by a wealth of attractions and cultural monuments, annually attracts thousands of tourists from all over the world, including from Russia (the time difference between Moscow and Greece is 1 hour). In the hotels of Kolymbari, guests are plunged into the atmosphere of first-class service, a developed infrastructure of entertainment and quality relaxation. One of the popular accommodation options for tourists is the Chrispy World Hotel Resort 4 * hotel.

Acquaintance

Chrispy World Hotel Resort 4 * is a hotel complex located 150 meters from the sea and consists of 3 *, 4 * and 5 * establishments. The institution invites tourists to the world of the sun and cordial hospitality. According to the advertisement, here you can choose the accommodation option that fully matches the preferences of each of the tourists. Friendly and experienced staff always responds to any requests and is ready to make the stay of hotel guests even more enjoyable. The advertising and appeals of the administration to potential guests contain a tempting promise: a stay in this hotel will be truly unforgettable for everyone.

Location

Chrispy World Hotel Resort 4 * is located on the sea coast in one of the most picturesque regions of Crete. Address: Greece - 73006, Crete, Rapaniana, Kolimbari, Chania. The city of Chania is located just half an hour from the hotel, a few minutes. drive from here is the resort village of Platanias. At a distance of 300 m from the hotel is the resort of Rapanyan. The surrounding area has regular transport links. Near the hotel there is a bus station (100m). The hotel is located on the 1st beach line. The center of Kolymbari is one and a half kilometers away. Chania city center is 25 km away. Chania Airport is 35 km, and Heraklion Airport is 162 km.

Room Selection Guidelines

Tourists in their posts on social networks report that at this hotel, at night, vacationers who have had to live by the pool are annoyed by the noise of the pump motor. Therefore, future guests are advised to choose rooms away from the pools. In addition, the reviewers warn that guests may be offered accommodation in semi-basement rooms, in which it is dark, the windows are small, opening halfway, the coolness and humidity are constantly kept. In addition, mosquitoes annoy those living in these rooms. Those who do not want to feel like they are in prison on vacation are not recommended to settle in the basement.

Chrispy World Hotel Resort 4 *: reviews

Hotel guests willingly share their impressions of their stay here. They note the presence of a spacious well-groomed territory, freshly renovated, swimming pools, spaciousness of all rooms. Tourists like good room decoration, quality service, beach, convenient location of the hotel. The authors of the reviews consider that in general the hotel corresponds to its category. Impressions from vacationers about the time spent in the hotel are mostly positive. The time difference between Moscow and Greece (at 1 hour), according to many tourists, is not felt.

As a drawback, guests note the lack of a hairdryer and bathtub in some rooms, security systems (there are no surveillance cameras, each room has a separate entrance from the street), the small size of the bathroom, and poor Internet performance. Claims of vacationers also relate to the work of some hotel employees accused by guests of impoliteness, insufficient quality of children's animation programs. However, many tourists consider these shortcomings insignificant and recommend the hotel as a worthy option for living on vacation.
