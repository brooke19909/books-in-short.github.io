Kabardinka hotels: Castro. Sea Holidays - Kabardinka

Kabardinka is a small cozy resort village of the Black Sea coast of Russia, located 15 kilometers northwest of the city of Gelendzhik. It has been chosen since Soviet times, and today Kabardinka hotels (Castro, Nadezhda, Starfish and others) have gained great popularity among Russians and foreign guests.





Spring in Kabardinka does not stop with the arrival, even in February the temperature sometimes reaches up to +23 degrees Celsius. Accordingly, the swimming season opens early. And the clear advantage that many Kabardinka hotels (Castro, Golden Antelope, Siesta, Kristina) can boast is its proximity to the sea.

Castro

Hotel "CASTRO", built in 2012, is located 150 meters from the beach. This is a modern six-story complex, reviews about which all as one were left by tourists in a positive way. Despite the fact that the hotel is chic, equipped with the latest technology and has many suites, it also has the opportunity to relax in economy class.

The hotel is located 5 minutes from the center of the village. At the height of the season, the cost of daily living in a double room is from 1,500 to 3,000 rubles., For a triple room you will have to pay from 2,300 to 3,300 rubles. For a four-seater "junior suite" - from 2,600 to 3,000 rubles. And the room of the category "luxury" will cost the guest from 2600 to 5000 rubles.

Holidays offered by Kabardinka hotels (Castro, Nadezhda and others) are suitable not only for healthy people, but also for guests with various types of diseases, for example, bronchial diseases. The climate here is the driest among the southern regions of the Black Sea, there are no adverse conditions for the lungs in the form of high humidity.

The guests of the Castro Hotel liked the quality of service at the hotel, the availability of free Wi-Fi, the proximity to the location of the Sberbank branch and many shops.

"Hope"





Talking about resort hotels, one cannot fail to mention the Nadezhda Hotel - Kabardinka have the right to be proud of this chic resort complex. Its exact address is the city of Gelendzhik, the resort of Kabardinka, ul. Mira, 3, the distance to the city center is 13 kilometers.

The hotel has a private beach and indoor and outdoor pools. Guests can enjoy international cuisine in one of the four restaurants, and a buffet breakfast is served in the mornings.

Holidays at sea Kabardinka with its hotels can provide the highest level. This is proved by the Nadezhda hotel with its chic villa rooms. In them, guests will receive the best VIP service. There are rooms and more modest, but no less convenient and comfortable. They are all equipped with air conditioning, balconies, most rooms have a fully equipped kitchen, and from the windows you can enjoy beautiful views of the Markotch Mountains.

Pool Hotels

Pearls of the Kabardinka resort - hotels with a pool: Villa Jasmine, Cosmos, guest houses Golden Pegasus, Fandorin and many others. They have long found their regular visitors who come from year to year to their favorite vacation spot. Let us consider in more detail some hotel complexes, some of the most worthy.

"Achilleion Park"

This is a new four-star hotel, which began its operation in June 2013. It is located a bit away from the hustle and bustle of spa life, next to it a mountain river carries its waters to the Black Sea, and half a kilometer away there is a promenade with a pebble beach. Nearby is a beautiful green park, ideal for walking with children, as it is equipped with playgrounds.

The hotel will appeal to families, young couples with children, and will also serve as a great place for a romantic getaway for couples.

The hotel building consists of six floors, there is an atrium, an elevator for ten, a spacious hall equipped with a wonderful waterfall and an aquarium with goldfish. The panoramic restaurant serves a variety of cuisines.





If you do not take into account food, the cost of living per day is from 2000 to 8000 rubles.

The rooms have beds with orthopedic mattresses, deluxe rooms are equipped with king size beds. There are bedside tables, television broadcasting tuned to Russian-language channels, a fridge, air conditioning and a safe, as well as a direct dial telephone.

Rooms are cleaned daily and linen and towels are changed every other day. Hotel guests have the opportunity to use free Wi-Fi.

The hotel has two freshwater heated pools - for adults and children. Sun loungers and parasols are available free of charge.

An undoubted advantage and convenience for guests is that the hotel accepts children of any age for placement.

"Starfish"

The hotel, located in the center of the village, just a 7-minute walk from the sea coast, operates year-round. Three hotel buildings are interconnected.

Minimum amenities are provided for all guests, regardless of the occupied rooms (there are double, triple rooms, as well as apartments): television, refrigerators, air conditioning and bathrooms.





The cost of placing one person per day in the midst of the holiday season is from 650 to 3500 rubles, depending on the category of room. Prices do not include meals.

"Emerald"

Among the mini-hotels can be distinguished "Platan" and "Emerald", we dwell on the last one, which in 2013 in the nomination "Best mini-hotels of the Krasnodar Territory" took first place.

This hotel is pretty compact, but it has everything you need for a great comfortable stay. The rooms here are cozy, there is a small courtyard with parking. You can use the sauna and swim in the 5x6 m summer pool.

If you are waiting for some privacy from a holiday in the resort of Kabardinka, mini-hotels are the best option for lovers of peace. Hotels have a maximum of 5-10 rooms, and the level of accommodation in them is not inferior to ordinary hotels.





In "Emerald" cinema lovers and Internet users will not be bored, there is a library for guests, as well as an extensive excursion program.
Walking distance to the center is a 10-minute walk.

Guest houses

Not only Kabardinka hotels (Castro and others listed above) can shelter guests, you should pay attention to Siesta, Rainbow, Natalya, Nadezhda and Mercury. This is not a complete list of guest houses that annually glad to welcome their guests. The average price of daily accommodation per season per person is from 1700 to 2200 rubles. Meals are paid separately.
