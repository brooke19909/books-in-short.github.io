How to knit a hat-helmet for a boy with knitting needles? Even novice craftswomen will be able to do this.

Warm clothes are necessary for all kids. This is especially true for boys who prefer noisy and active fun with catch-ups, friendly fights, the construction of snow houses and figures, and sleigh rides over quiet and calm walks. Therefore, the mothers of such tricks need to know how to knit a hat-helmet for a boy. This will allow them not to worry that the child will freeze during a long winter walk.

Oh, these boys ...

Little angels kids look only on leaflets or pictures. There they together with their parents walk by the handle to a park or store. They are always very neatly dressed. But in ordinary life, everything happens completely wrong. After active games on the street, the kids come home in a hat, which has rolled to its side, with an unwound scarf and a collar wide open from running, behind which there may be a couple of handfuls of snow. Of course, sore throats, colds, bronchitis are possible ...





One cannot but agree with the fact that every child needs movement. But how to combine the pleasure of games and warm clothes that would not interfere while walking and at the same time be comfortable and warm? And here, an interesting combination of an uncomfortable hat and a reeling scarf will come to the rescue. Such an unusual and at the same time very convenient hybrid of two simple and familiar things from childhood. From this article, you can understand how to knit a hat-helmet for a boy with knitting needles.

Why is such a thing good?

For a long time, a hat-cap is not considered an accessory worn exclusively by skiing professionals or those who love skiing. Now such a hat is worn not only by children, but also by some adults who are convinced that no other can compare with it both in terms of comfort and warmth.





Moms usually buy these hats for their little ones because they feel confident that only such a hat will protect their children in winter frosts. But if you buy it in a store, then it will cost quite expensive. Unfortunately, not everyone can afford such expenses. Therefore, it will be much cheaper to knit yourself using knitting needles. From this article you can learn how to knit a hat-cap with knitting needles. The scheme and description are quite simple, even a novice craftswoman can connect. And it will not be difficult for her.

What do you need for knitting?

How to knit a hat-helmet for a boy with knitting needles, we will learn further. What tools and materials will be needed to make it?

So, for such a headdress, approximately 160 g of yarn of any color at the choice of the craftswoman will be enough. If mother knitted something earlier, it will be easy for her to combine two or more colors in this product.





Knitting needles are better to take No. 3: a pair of straight and a pair of circular (better connected by fishing line). If Mom plans to tie the edge of the product, then she will need a hook. Its thickness should be the same as that of the spokes. Conventional scissors are also useful to cut the threads.

Knitting pattern for hat-cap pretty simple. Actually, there is no scheme itself, because the usual gum pattern is used, which will not be difficult to knit.

The direction from the front to the wrong side should be changed after every two loops, and then vice versa. You need to dial 152 loops and tie the elastic in a circle.

We form a cutout

When the length of the prepared fabric will be 14 cm, you must close the first fifty-four loops. This is where the neckline will be formed.

After these actions, knitting will not continue in a circle, because now the direction will be first forward and then back. It will be necessary to tie another 12 cm and collect exactly the same number of loops as you typed at the beginning. Now knitting continues with knitting needles in a circle. Tie another 8 cm of fabric, reset the loops and leave only seventy-eight pieces.





It is not very difficult to knit such caps with a description with the description, the main thing is to do everything in order.

From the remaining loops, ten equal segments should be formed and the facial surface should be connected. This stage of work requires reducing the loops by one piece in every second row. When the number of loops will be ten pieces, it is necessary to tighten them with a thread, the end of which should be hidden from the wrong side of the helmet-cap. If you want to decorate his neckline, you can crochet him. All is ready.

We knit a neck zone

To knit such caps with a description is quite simple. Now we will understand in more detail how to make a collar. On circular knitting needles, loop around the edge. Now, at every second level, it is necessary to make an increase in the center of two loops. We knit a hosiery cloth using only front loops.

So that mom could understand how long the shirt-front should be, it is better to try on it directly on the son.





Now the headgear - a children's hat-helmet knitted with needles - is ready. Before you start wearing it, you must not forget it, wash it well and dry it on a horizontal surface. When the hat dries, it can be used for its intended purpose.
