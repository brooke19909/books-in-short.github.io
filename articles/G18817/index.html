Hotel Costa Bitezhan Hotel 4 * Turkey: overview, description and reviews of tourists

The Turkish resort of Bodrum, located along the coast of the sea, is a relatively small town. Its population barely reaches thirty thousand people. However, this did not prevent Bodrum from gaining the fame of one of the fun ‚Äúparty‚Äù resorts in Turkey. Representatives of golden youth come here very often, including from many European countries.





general information

In recent years, many entertainment centers have been built in the city. Apparently, due to this fact, Bodrum has become an international resort, perfectly suitable for outdoor activities of tourists. Its picturesque nature, the bay for yachts, the mass of places where a cheerful noisy life passes at night - all this made Bodrum incredibly popular. The weather at the resort strongly resembles the climatic conditions of Croatian and Italian cities located on the shores of the Mediterranean Sea. The temperature here in the summer ranges from twenty-seven to thirty degrees, and the water warms up to a comfortable twenty-five.

The beach season in Bodrum begins at the end of May and lasts until about the twentieth of September. However, inveterate tourists come here in March, to the period of almond blossom. The excellent climate is another circumstance that has made this corner one of the most popular tourist destinations. There are many hotels for every wealth and taste. One of them is Costa Bitezhan Hotel 4 * (Turkey). Bitez, and this is the name of the bay in which it is built, is very popular among beach lovers from around the world. It is approximately seven kilometers distant from the center of Bodrum. On the map, Bitez looks like an even crescent. The bay is fully built up with hotels, restaurants and cafes. This article will focus on the Costa Bitezhan Hotel 4 * (Bodrum).

Description

The hotel is located directly on the seafront. The international airport in Bodrum is forty-two kilometers away. This is an excellent indicator for those who can not stand a long-term transfer. From the windows of the rooms, judging by the reviews, a magnificent seascape opens, as well as a view of the interior. The resort of Bodrum, which has many entertainment venues and popular tourist shops, hotel guests can reach a maximum of twenty minutes by public transport. The bus stop is located right at the entrance to the Costa Bitezhan Hotel 4 *. The hotel is urban. He has no private parking.





Built in 1997, this hotel has been remodeled several times. The last time it was repaired was in 2014.

Overview of Services

Costa Bitezhan Hotel is a four star hotel. Its infrastructure is fully consistent with the category assigned to it. The hotel area is almost five thousand square meters. meters. It is equipped and landscaped.

Services such as transfer to the airport or in any other direction, dry cleaning, car rental and excursions, tourists should be paid separately, since they are not included in the price. Free are Wi-Fi, a depository safe at the receptionist, as well as the use of the pool and sun loungers on the beach.

The paper desk is open around the clock. Despite the rules of the hotel, receptionists do not adhere to a strictly specified hour for check-in. If there are currently available numbers, keys are issued within a maximum of twenty minutes. There is also the opportunity to send a fax and rent a cell in a left-luggage office.

Housing stock

Costa Bitezhan Beach Hotel 4 *, and this is how this hotel was called until 2014, is a two-story Main Building building. It is equipped with eighty-one rooms of economy classes and standard with views of the sea or a beautiful garden. The interiors of the rooms are made in European style with some oriental elements. All rooms are eighteen square meters. A maximum of three people can live in them (2 + 1).



The rooms have beds two meters twenty centimeters long, wardrobe, luggage rack, refrigerator. For the built-in safe you need to pay separately, at the rate of three dollars per day. There is also a flat-screen TV with one Russian satellite channel, a telephone and air conditioning (split system). There are small balconies in front of the rooms. Judging by the reviews left by the Russians, it is very convenient that, in addition to plastic furniture, they also have a dryer and a mosquito net on the opening.

Bathrooms in all rooms are combined. They have showers, a hairdryer and hygiene items.

The rooms are carpeted. Internet connection is not available in the rooms.

Nutrition

The all-inclusive Costa Bitezhan Beach Hotel tours also include a three-time buffet in the main restaurant, as well as the opportunity to receive local drinks throughout the day at the bar.

Meals at the hotel are provided strictly according to the schedule established by the rules. Opening hours of the restaurant can be seen both on the stand and on the memo, which is handed to guests upon check-in. The price does not include delicacies, alcohol and elite bottled drinks. During the day, guests can enjoy sandwiches and cookies at the on-site snack bar. The restriction on free access also applies to wine produced in other countries, and not in Turkey.





Beach

Judging by the reviews, it is he who is the pride of the Costa Bitezhan Beach Hotel 4 * hotel. In just two minutes, guests reach the bathing area. The beach of this hotel is considered one of the best in all of Bodrum. The coverage of the bathing area is sand, although in some places it is pebble. In any case, for children, the presence of rubber slippers will be relevant. You can buy them both in the store operating on the hotel premises, and in the nearest large shopping centers.

Umbrellas and sun loungers are provided free of charge on the beach for all-inclusive guests. Free bath towels are also available.

Services for little guests

Starting from the year of its construction, Costa Bitezhan Beach Hotel 4 * (Bodrum) has been advertised by tour operators as a hotel suitable for family and youth recreation. For the youngest customers, certain conditions are provided here to make them comfortable on site. Children can play on the playground, ride a slide or play in the sandbox, attend a mini club in which animators work with children from five to eleven years old.

Upon request, parents can get a cot and bed linen in the room, high chairs in the restaurant are freely available. A baby tray is not provided.

Hotel Policies

More than half of the Russians who had already rested at Costa Bitezhan Hotel were satisfied with their choice. According to the rules of the hotel, check-in is scheduled for two in the afternoon. Nevertheless, receptionists rarely adhere to this time if there are available rooms, especially in cases with guests arriving with children. Departure is scheduled for noon.

According to the rules, pets are not allowed in Costa Bitezhan Hotel. Smoking is strictly prohibited throughout the hotel.





Please contact the property in advance to receive transfer service from the airport.

Additional Information

Costa Bitezhan Hotel, reviews of which our compatriots are mostly positive, has a rather convenient location. This is especially true for those who combine relaxation with sightseeing. The hotel has a tour desk. Here you can buy tours to the Bodrum windmills, the castle, the gates of Mindos, the Museum of Underwater Archeology, the Halicarnassus Mausoleum and the ruins of Pedasa. Children will especially enjoy visiting the Bodrum Dedeman Waterpark.

Free entertainment

An outdoor pool is located on site. The sun loungers and umbrellas installed around it are in the public domain. There is also a bar where, according to the all-inclusive concept, local juices, coffee and beer are provided free of charge.

Judging by the reviews, the hotel has a good animation. Our tourists expressed many good words about the work of the team of young guys.





There is no need to pay for a visit to the sauna and Turkish hammam. Table tennis and darts are also free.

Paid services

Guests must pay a fee for access to the hairdresser, spa and massage parlor. The same service is a consultation with a doctor.

All water activities on the beach are also paid - riding a catamaran, banana, scooter and boat rental. Guests can rent snorkeling equipment.
