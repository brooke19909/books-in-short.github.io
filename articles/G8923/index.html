A large number of pixels makes the photo bright and clear, it can be enlarged to almost any size, for example, to make the format 21 to 30.

The quality of the photo and its size depend on the maximum resolution of the camera‚Äôs matrix. To date, there are almost no devices that take low-quality pictures. Since even the most ordinary phones have in their arsenal a camera with two pixels.
