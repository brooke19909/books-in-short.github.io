How to sew to sew a tunic with your own hands without a pattern quickly: features and recommendations

A huge number of women fell in love with such a piece of clothing as a tunic. It is ideal for trips to the sea, to hide from the scorching sun or gracefully defile from the beach to the house, without carrying a heap of clothes. Also, many beautiful people prefer to wear a tunic as home clothes. She does not constrain movements and allows you to look feminine and fashionable. That's just on the shelves of the store you can not always find the goods of the desired style and color. Therefore, in the current article we will talk about how to sew a tunic with your own hands. The master class is ideal for beginner needlewomen, and even those who absolutely do not have the skills of cutting and sewing.

Where to begin

Sewing a tunic is not at all difficult. But only if competent training is carried out. First of all, you should think over the design of the desired thing. In the material presented, we explore the technology of performing the simplest tunic. But after all, if desired, it can easily be decorated with bows, beads, appliques and other details. Professionals recommend sketching their idea on paper in order to look at it from the side and finally approve the design.

Material purchase

Experienced needlewomen, telling how to sew a tunic with their own hands quickly and without a pattern, say that special attention should be paid to the choice of fabric. Beginners should not buy loose or crumbling. It will be extremely inconvenient to work with it, because such material needs processing. And overcasting the edge with a sewing needle will not be enough; an overlock is required.

Stretch fabric should also not be used for sewing tunics. It is very difficult to sew it both manually and on a typewriter. Experts are convinced that the best option for beginners is cotton or linen. It‚Äôs good to cut it, and then sew it together.

Taking measurements

The next step in the instructions on how to sew a tunic with your own hands without a pattern will be the measurement of the model for which we are preparing the intended product. Therefore, we take a centimeter tape, a piece of paper and a pen. A beautiful lady should undress to underwear. Only in this case will it be possible to obtain the correct parameters.

So, we need to find out the following parameters:

  • estimated tunic length;
  • distance from the lower edge to the armpit;
  • girth of the chest or hips (we take a larger value);
  • sleeves length;
  • girth of the neck.

Building patterns

Professional experts say that you can sew a tunic with your own hands on a pattern made by yourself. This is not difficult! To do this, buy a piece of fabric on which we can fit two rectangles. The length of each should be equal to the estimated length of the tunic. Since the intended product should be free, we calculate the width of each rectangle as follows: add 15-20 centimeters to the circumference of the hips or chest and divide the resulting number into two.

Now we turn to the main part of the master class, which will help to sew the tunic yourself. We fold the purchased piece of material in half and put it on a flat smooth surface, ideally on a comfortable table. We fasten the segment with pins or pins so that it does not fidget when constructing a pattern and cutting.

Using a large ruler and soap or chalk, set aside the desired length and width. Then draw a rectangle at the given points. We outline on it the distance from the lower edge to the armpit and collar, equal to half the circumference of the neck. Then cut out two rectangles at once. Next, draw the goal line. On the front, it should be a little deeper, but you can do the same.

Separately, cut out two smaller rectangles. The length of each is equal to the estimated length of the sleeves, the width is measured on the main pattern of the tunic - from the shoulder seam to the armpit, we multiply the final value by two.
