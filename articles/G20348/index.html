Insurance company "47 Trust"

The construction company "47 Trust" was founded back in 1963, and therefore has vast experience and high authority among other companies in the field of construction of houses. According to available merits, construction companies of St. Petersburg place this organization among the main leaders in the real estate market in this region. The company operates in the northern capital and in the Leningrad region. Moreover, it performs the functions of an investor, a general contractor, a developer, and a customer.

47 Trest CJSC erects brick, panel, monolithic brick houses, carries out reconstruction of both individual houses and entire areas. An example of such an excellent job is the quarter on Turbina Street. The contest "Builder of the Year 2009", which was attended by almost all construction companies in St. Petersburg, was won by this company.

Among the facilities that were commissioned by 47 Trust, there are many truly significant.
