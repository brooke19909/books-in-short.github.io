Morjim our compatriots who settled in it, called the "Indian Ruble" in Goa. Recently, the number of Russian tourists here is more than eighty percent of the total. And this is not surprising.



Holidays in Morjim

This magnificent secluded Indian resort enjoys a tranquil location on Goa, surrounded by dense vegetation. From the shore, tourists enjoy a breathtaking panorama of the Arabian Sea. Basking in the golden sand of the famous Morjim beach and swimming in the sunshine to the soothing sound of the waves, a person will relax his soul, completely forgetting about his worries. There are many restaurants and shacks at this resort where you can have a delicious lunch at ridiculously low prices. There are many hotels and small guesthouses designed for traveling in economy class. Many come here for relaxation. And the amazing beauty and tranquility that reigns on Morjim Beach in the morning hours made it an ideal place for meditation.

Reviews
