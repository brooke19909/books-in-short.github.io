Top 10 vacuum cleaners: an overview of the best models, specifications, reviews

Models and types of vacuum cleaner may be different. Many owners acquire this technique for specific types of cleaning. However, this technique is the fastest and easiest way to keep the house clean. It is important to buy the right type of vacuum cleaner, choosing a model that is suitable for many parameters.

Before proceeding to the main reviews, you should familiarize yourself with buying tips that will help you choose the right type of household appliance. The main key questions that users most often ask before buying such equipment will be considered. After that, the top 10 vacuum cleaners will be presented, which are considered the best, according to users. There are both wired and wireless models on the list.

How to choose the best technique

The list of top 10 vacuum cleaners includes models of various types. Basically, they are distinguished by functionality. There are two key options here. First, you need to choose a vacuum cleaner with or without a bag. Secondly, to figure out which type of equipment is better for cleaning - cylindrical or vertical.

But there is another option - wireless vacuum cleaners. The convenience of the non-contact connection they offer is a growing trend in the market and worth considering. Most of them are not as powerful as wired vacuum cleaners, but they compensate for this with versatility and simplicity. Getting rid of the cable makes cleaning the house a lot easier, so they are a great alternative if the user already has a decent wired vacuum cleaner to cope with more severe types of dirt if necessary.

Some owners of houses and apartments have several devices. In the list of top 10 vacuum cleaners, it will be possible to find wired and wireless models that are responsible for specific tasks during cleaning. Thanks to the latest technology, wireless outperforms wired models. In fact, Dyson announced that it would no longer develop new wired models, focusing on wireless. Existing wired Dyson models will still be sold, but for contactless future.

Dyson popularized bagless vacuum cleaners, but both types have clear advantages and disadvantages. The main advantage of a bagless vacuum cleaner is the absence of loss of suction power. Productivity varies from brand to brand depending on the quality of their systems, but these are key indicators. In the list of top 10 vacuum cleaners, models for each type of cleaning will be described. This will allow you to make a choice and understand for what purposes a specific device is used.

The problem with bagless vacuum cleaners is that they can send dust back to the room after emptying if the container is not carefully removed. This is where bagged vacuum cleaners are best suited, especially from the tough materials used by companies like Miele. A vacuum cleaner with disposable assembly bags is the best option if the user is allergic.

Another advantage of bagless vacuum cleaners is the following: no need to buy replacement bags, which saves money in the long run. However, the cleaning systems in a vacuum cleaner with a container must rinse the filters, and then they must dry for at least 24 hours. The only exceptions are some of the new Dysons, which are some of the first to have no built-in filters.

Vertical vs. Cylindrical

The choice of a vertical or cylindrical vacuum cleaner, largely depends on the cleaning style that the user prefers. The latest models are generally easier to store, but their large size and long hose are not always convenient for putting things in order. Moreover, these are not the best types of devices for people with a sore back, because it will be necessary to constantly bend down to lift them. The list of the top 10 best vacuum cleaners will not list bulky industrial models. For the house, more compact devices with good mobility are suitable.

Vertical models have no such problems. Usually they have wider cleaning heads that cover a large area in one pass. However, with some vacuum cleaners it will be difficult to get under the furniture, but there are modifications of devices designed to carry out cleaning even in hard-to-reach places.

What properties are worth paying attention to

When choosing the best of the top 10 vacuum cleaners, you should pay attention to the following points:

  1. Maneuverability. It is necessary to check how easy it is to control, pull and lift equipment. It is important that both the wheels on the vacuum cleaner and the nozzles have good adhesion to the surface.
  2. Carpet cleaning. How many cleaning cycles are needed to completely clean the fleecy surface.
  3. Cleaning in hard to reach places. The nozzle must be bent. It is important that the brush has the ability to collect dust and dirt from the wall.
  4. Cleaning on the wooden floor. Vacuum cleaners may not always be able to deal equally with dirt on the carpet and laminate. It is important that the nozzle has a tight grip on the surface.
  5. Cleaning pet hair. Is the nozzle or power of the vacuum cleaner able to collect all the hair from pets?
  6. Cleaning on the stairs. Does the nozzle allow cleaning on the stairs of the house? Are there any additional devices for collecting larger debris?
  7. Noise. What is the decibel level at maximum load of the device?

It is also worth checking when buying a vacuum cleaner which accessories are included, how well they work and how versatile the equipment is. For example, some models show themselves excellently in certain jobs, while others have many additional nozzles with which you can increase the functionality of the device. Do not forget to check the cord length in wired devices, battery life in wireless models and how easy it is to empty bagless models.
