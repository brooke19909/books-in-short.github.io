An operational day is part of the working day of a banking institution. Bank working hours

During the day, banks and other organizations (members of the payment system) accept documents for payment and transfer of funds. During this period, operations can be completed without delay. All actions taken should be reflected in the accounting for off-balance and balance accounts through the preparation of a daily report. The operational day is the period during which there is a transition from one financial condition recorded in accounting to another. This is part of the working day of a financial institution, which is allocated for the provision of services to customers and is associated with the reception, execution, control of accounting documents of the bank and customers, their reflection in accounting, completion of information processing and the formation of a daily balance.

Operating time

Operational time is the period during which bank employees make banking operations and other transactions. Also under this concept is the time of workflow and information processing. The duration of the operating time in most cases is determined based on the operating mode of the bank.

What is a trading day in simple words

How can I convey the essence of this concept in simple words? Most often, a business day means a business day of the bank plus the time required to complete the basic procedures. In most organizations, this period is pretty standard - from 9 to 18 pm. But for some actions (for example, currency exchange), it is limited to 9-15 hours of the day. The start and end time of a banking day is set by the bank management. All information is necessarily communicated to customers. If a person comes to the company after the end of the trading day, then the transaction or transfer he needs will be carried out only on the next day (if he is a worker).

The beginning of the day

The start and end time of a business day in a bank is established by order or order of the head of a financial institution. In the first working hours, the chief or his deputy gives the employees keys that allow them to perform various operations. Then the bank employees prepare their jobs: receive cash, put their signatures in the control book, connect and start up the necessary technical equipment.

Daily Balance Dates

The daily balance is up to 12 hours of the next business day. The consolidated balance sheet must be made up to 12 hours on the same day after processing the balance of operations performed. All documentation received during operating hours is subject to execution and reflection on accounts on the same day. The same operations that came to be executed after its completion are carried out the next day.

The end of the day

The end of the trading day in the bank is characterized by the following operations:

  1. Issuance of all necessary reports (daily, monthly, ten-day, quarterly reports).
  2. Conducting relevant documentation in payment.
  3. Fixing the state of the infobase, creating a copy of it, transferring it to the server.

Postoperative time - what is it?

Postoperative time is considered the period from the end of the operational banking day and customer service until the end of the working hours of the bank branch.
