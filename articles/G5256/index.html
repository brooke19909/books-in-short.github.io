Hotel "Sport", Rzhev: description, reviews

Rzhev, although a small, but rich in monuments and attractions of the city. So, if you came here on a trip or are on a business trip, you can find out a lot of interesting things for yourself. A comfortable stay will be provided by the Sport Hotel in Rzhev.

Description

The building of the Sport Hotel is two-story and comfortable, built in 2008. Near the hotel there is a park area and the banks of the Volga, where guests can take a walk and relax in the fresh air. Rzhev city center is just 300 meters away. Within walking distance is the Magnit supermarket. The train station will need to walk 2.3 kilometers. Free private parking is available for guests with their own vehicles. The territory is under video surveillance. Meals are provided in the shared kitchen, where guests cook at will, on request, and a continental breakfast is served here in the mornings. Hotel rooms are offered on any wallet. In each, regardless of the level of comfort, there is access to free wireless Internet. Administrators will be happy to tell you which city attractions you can visit, in which cafes you can eat deliciously. Check-in time is 14:00, checkout time is 12:00. The Sport Hotel is located in Rzhev at 2/85 Voloskovskaya Gorka Street.

Accommodation

The hotel offers the following room options for guests:

  • economy twin room with single beds - 1570 rubles per day;
  • triple economy with single beds - 2400 rubles per day;
  • standard double with single beds - 2250 rubles per day;
  • business class number - 2460 rubles per day;
  • Junior suite with a large double bed - 2700 rubles per day;
  • deluxe room with a large double bed - 3950 rubles per day.

At the Sport Hotel (Rzhev), you can use the continental breakfast, which costs 220 rubles.

Room Amenities & Services

All rooms are equipped with TVs, kettles and free internet. The economy class rooms have a shared bathroom. Superior rooms have a refrigerator, toiletries, and a private bathroom. This suite has a microwave and comfortable desk. Also, guests of the Sport Hotel (Rzhev) can use the services of:
