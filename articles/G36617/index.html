Priozerye, cottage village (Kaluga Oblast): reviews

Many residents of the capital prefer to purchase land and houses with cottages not only in Moscow, but also in neighboring regions, since the cost of the desired real estate there is much lower. It is these favorable conditions that Priozerye offers (cottage village, Kaluga region).





The location of the village

Kaluga region borders on Moscow. You can get to the village from the metropolis by two highways: Kievsky or Kaluga. On the paid section of the M3 highway, you need to turn onto Golovteevo. And then the road will lead directly to the village Priozerye. The distance to it is a little over a hundred kilometers. A freeway can be reached in approximately one hour.

Surprisingly convenient location has Priozerye, a cottage village in the Kaluga region. How to get to it, you can easily find out by looking at the map. A direct federal highway will lead directly to it. It almost never has traffic jams, and this is an important fact.

Once you leave the capital, you will find yourself in an ecologically healthy environment in which Priozerye is comfortably located. The cottage village in the Kaluga region is surrounded by beautiful landscapes that make life in it especially attractive, regardless of whether it is seasonal or year-round.

Not far from the cottage village is a large reservoir with clean water. On its shores there are sandy beaches. You can get to it in about seven minutes at a leisurely pace.
