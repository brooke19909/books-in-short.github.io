Several ways to deposit money on a Sberbank card



The banking system in our country is developed enough, an indicator of this is a huge number of both the banks themselves and their offices. But despite this, Sberbank of Russia OJSC has been the most popular and well-developed for several decades now. According to 2011 data, the corporation received 1st place for the number of ATMs serving offices issued by plastic cards. Its services are easy and simple to use.

Sberbank plastic card: is it convenient?

Using "live" money is becoming less and less attractive, more precisely, more and more people have appreciated the convenience of plastic cards. For example, to take with you on a long trip it is more convenient to have a small piece of plastic than a supply of cash for the entire period. Paying for purchases, loans and utility bills is more convenient and faster through Internet systems or terminals than standing in line at the cashier (this will also help the card). Most often, money comes automatically to the card (transferred salaries, pensions, compensations, etc.), but what should I do when I need to replenish it?





Through the cashier

You can put money on a Sberbank card through the cashier of any servicing banking office. To do this, you will need a passport and a card (or account number and bank details), of course, a margin of time. This service can be used both in Sberbank itself and in any commercial institution. The only caveat is that a third-party bank will require a commission for the service.

Through an ATM

You can put money on a Sberbank card through an ATM. Please note: not all ATMs are equipped with a function of receiving money. The location of these devices in your city can be found on the bank's website or at the operators of the hotline. These devices are available around the clock, which undoubtedly speaks of their dignity.

Through a payment terminal

You can put money on a Sberbank card through payment terminals. The pluses are their availability, and the minuses are the commission charged automatically, and the fact that the transaction takes a lot of time. Via Russian Post

You can put money on a Sberbank card using the services of the Russian Post. All that is required of you is to fill out the form, indicate the bank details and account number. The operation may take several days. This should be taken into account if money in the account is needed immediately.





Via wire transfer

You can put money on a Sberbank card using the services of electronic systems Yandex.Money, Webmoney, etc. The account is replenished by transferring from an electronic wallet. The service is also paid, and the deposit procedure will take from 1 to 3 days.

Through personal account

You can put money on a Sberbank credit card without leaving your home, having carried out the procedure in the personal account of the electronic system of the bank, having executed a transfer from one account to another in a matter of minutes. The online system is the most convenient and efficient compared to the above.
