Knitted cap: scheme. Crochet a hat cap

Always quite relevant (and especially with the onset of cold weather) remains such an accessory as a hat. In addition to the fact that it has a warming effect, it must correspond to the chosen style: everyone wants to look fashionable. Therefore, any fashionista tries to choose both a style and a model that would suit only her. Hat cap It is a headpiece that is suitable for both respectable adults and non-intelligent babies. And to whom it is more to face, it is still necessary to understand.

Getting ready seriously

From the very beginning, the cap was intended for young men and youths. But it so happened that the girls adapted this headdress for themselves. Already this season, these hats remain popular for both sexes.

Even beginners can tie this headdress. If they know how to first dial a few loops, and then knit them face or wrong, then it is quite possible to start work. Knitting a hat-cap with knitting needles for beginners will not be a problem. In just a couple of evenings, a cute hat, made by yourself, will be ready.





If English gum is selected, then the number of loops should be a multiple of two. In the first row, you just need to alternate the front and back loops. In the second, knit a little differently: first, front, then double crochet, remove the wrong side, without knitting. Knit the third row as follows: knit the front loop and the yarn from the previous row together with the front, then make the yarn and remove the wrong side again.

If you knit a hat-cap is supposed for a girl, it should be fun, therefore, as a rule, different colors of yarn are chosen. You can even make it striped by combining two or three matching in color or tone of a ball of thread.

Choose a pattern

It is most preferable to knit a hat-cap with simple patterns - a simple or English rubber band, front or back smooth surface. If it is decided that the thing will be striped, then the very connection of a couple of three colors will give originality, so the simplest pattern will be needed.

If the cap-cap, the scheme of which will be given below, is connected by an elastic band, then it will comfortably fit your head due to its elasticity.





To get a hat very fashionable and exclusive, you need to use thick knitting needles. Thanks to them, you can not come up with some kind of complex pattern, because in this case even the simplest knitting will look great. Those ladies who want to quickly cope with the task and quickly finish their work, you need to remember that it is with thick knitting needles that you can get as close as possible.

A crocheted hat will also look pretty, but this work will take a little longer than knitting. And the principle of operation is the same as in the first case. In the figure, you can see the schemes that will be needed for work.





How to start work?

When the model and color of the yarn is already defined, you can start knitting. As a rule, the first step is to knit a sample and calculate the proportions. It is necessary to calculate how many loops are obtained in the finished sample, to measure its size. The volume of your head is known. Now you can calculate the number of loops needed for the header, given the number needed for rapporting the pattern (when needed).

So, in our plans - a cap-cap knitted with needles . How to do it? You can choose one of two options: work either on two knitting needles, or on circular. The latter method is more convenient, because in this case it will not be necessary to make a seam at the back of the product. If you don‚Äôt have the habit of working with circular knitting needles, you can knit using the five usual straight lines.





If such a solution is chosen, then the entire number of loops must be divided into 4 spokes equally. If the pattern is not elastic, then first you need to tie at least two or three centimeters with an elastic band so that then the finished product does not hang on your head, gradually stretching.

And now you can knit a hat with the main pattern. Its length can be about a quarter meter. But if you really want a hat that is more authentic, then you need to knit about 35 cm directly, and then do the reduction. It is quite easy to complete them: knit two loops together. Reductions should be distributed moderately. When only four loops remain on the knitting needles, you need to stretch the thread through them and tighten it, then gently hide it inside the caps.

For example, take the cap size 56. When folded, the width will be 28 cm and the length 48 cm. It is best to use acrylic yarn - 100 g with a length of 400 meters - and knitting needles No. 3,5.

Cap with a pompom. Description of work

It is necessary to dial 75 stitches on the knitting needles and knit a pattern of "tangle" eleven centimeters. Then knit with a viscous fourteen centimeters of canvas. But now you can gradually reduce the loops at the beginning and end of every sixth row - one at a time.

Having knitted a total of 48 cm from the beginning of work, you can begin to reduce the loops. At the very end, pull off the few remaining stitches and make a neat seam. If you want the cap to look more elegant, you can independently make a decorative pompom and attach it to the crown of the cap.

And the kids too

Children's photographers love to capture tiny little ones in these hats. They are suitable not only for photo shoots, but also for every day. How to tie such a hat on a one-year-old baby whose head circumference is approximately 43-48 cm? This will require two skeins of acrylic yarn (150 m per 50 g), knitting needles No. 2.5.

With a blue thread, you need to dial 108 loops, which then are distributed into 4 knitting needles: each 27 loops. Using a 1 x 1 gum pattern, knit fourteen rows. Next, you need to knit the front surface. Now attach the thread of white color and knit with it 6 rows, then 4 rows - blue. Throughout the work, you need to alternate the colored stripes to get symmetrical stripes. Knit until the height of the cap is sixteen centimeters.





The ‚Äútail‚Äù of the cap can be done like this: on the left and on the right in each fourth row, make a decrease in two loops. It is important to make them neat and symmetrical with respect to the sides of the cap.

When the fabric is knitted to a level of 30-32 cm, decreases can be made a little less often - in every eighth row. So knit ten centimeters. Decreases do now in every tenth row. When a pair of dozens of loops remains on the knitting needles, it is necessary to tighten with a thread. Sew a pompom if desired.
