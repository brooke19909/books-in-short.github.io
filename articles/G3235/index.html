New buildings of Krasnodar from the builder of economy class

The list of "New buildings in Krasnodar" is constantly updated. The city is growing, expanding its borders every year. This is due to the constant influx of people from various parts of Russia to the southern capital.





How to choose apartments in new buildings of Krasnodar

This matter should be taken with wisdom and careful attention. Real estate in the city is in great demand not only among the indigenous people of Krasnodar and the region, but also among visitors from other regions. There are several reasons for this influx of residents.

Firstly, Krasnodar is one of the cities in which large expansion of both the residential sector and infrastructure is observed. Many move to open their own business, because the region often receives public investment.

Secondly, Krasnodar is a warm city. Therefore, northerners often move here for permanent residence.

Thirdly, to increase the influx of population into the region, the state is implementing a number of affordable housing programs for young families, in which new buildings in Krasnodar are also participating.

The advantages of real estate in this city

High demand is observed for new economy class buildings in Krasnodar. As a rule, buyers prefer to buy apartments at the construction stage (then the price is several times lower than after putting the house into operation), and then rent the apartment (pay the loan on the proceeds if the apartment is mortgaged). Or buy a studio apartment. The latter type of apartments is often preferred by young families, moving to Krasnodar.

New buildings from an economy class builder are characterized by a modern European layout of apartments. A number of banks offer mortgages at the lowest interest rate - therefore, housing in Krasnodar is considered affordable.





Floor plans

Many realtors cooperate with construction companies and provide assistance in finding an apartment, having everything at hand to search and verify the new building in Krasnodar. The layout of the building from state-owned construction companies includes the availability of convenient access roads, parking lots, and playgrounds. It also includes convenient stairwells, elevators (both freight and passenger), porches with special mechanical lifts for wheelchair users. Apartments in new buildings of Krasnodar are equipped with modern European-style heating systems, high-quality drainage systems, modern fire and security systems. Construction companies provide a wide range of real estate not only economy class. Thus, you can easily pick up an apartment for every taste, moving to Krasnodar.

Negative sides

As mentioned above, when choosing new economy class buildings in Krasnodar, buyers prefer either an apartment at the construction stage (extension) or a studio.

When choosing an apartment at the construction stage, the price for it is several times lower, but there is a certain risk: even a state developer may have problems that will lead to freezing of the construction.

Studio apartments in most cases are offered by private developers on the highest floors of low-rise cottages or townhouses. Most private developers have documentation problems. Such construction projects are periodically frozen by a court decision, then resumed again (and most often frozen again). Or even demolished. Therefore, if there is a desire to cooperate with a private developer, first of all, you should contact the Department of Urban Planning of the Krasnodar Territory and find out information about this house.

Signs of self-construction

Since the adoption of the 285 federal law of September 1, 2015, any structure that has the following features is recognized as self-building:

  • erected on a land plot that is not intended for this;
  • construction is carried out or has already been completed without obtaining permission from the relevant authorities;
  • the construction was erected in violation of the construction rules and established urban standards.

Of course, when choosing a new building in Krasnodar, it is very difficult to distinguish an unscrupulous developer from an unscrupulous one. In this case, it is better to contact the already mentioned department of urban planning and find out information.

This problem is relevant not only for Krasnodar and the region. In fact, not a single region of Russia is immune from such a phenomenon as self-construction. And to reveal the fact of violation in the bud is not always possible for the relevant structures.

In addition, many unscrupulous developers go to tricks, erecting five-story cottages, design them according to the documentation as a four-story building, the top floor is designed as an attic and sold as a studio apartment, which, with due knowledge of jurisprudence, sometimes rolls. But with a change in law, tenants may fall into disrepute due to the sudden recognition of the building as illegal.

Real estate of Krasnodar. New buildings. Apartment purchase

If a decision to purchase an apartment has already been made, an appropriate developer has been found, the documentation of which has been verified by the buyer, the department gives an answer about the absence of problems for this company and that the construction is carried out on completely legal grounds, the issue of purchasing an apartment should be considered.

If the buyer has funds in an amount equal to the price of the apartment, you can immediately go to the sales department.

If you are considering buying a home on a mortgage, you first need to go to a bank and talk with a loan officer for loans.

After resolving the issue of granting a loan, the specialist will issue a list of documents to the credited person that must be submitted to the bank to purchase an apartment for a mortgage.





As a rule, if the developer is accredited with this bank, then some of the documents of this construction organization are already in the bank. It remains to convey only a few. It should be noted that the presence of accreditation in a bank (for example, in such large ones as VTB or Sberbank) is a sign of a bona fide company. Especially if accreditation is obtained at both competing banks, which in itself is not so simple.

Different banks have different lists of documents. As a rule, those are issued free of charge (some in the sales department, some in the accounting department at the place of work), except for a conclusion on the appraisal of the acquired housing. The cost varies from 4000 to 6000 rubles. There are many appraisal companies in the city.

Documents are provided to the loan officer. After that, it is necessary to wait a certain period during which the documentation will be checked. During this period, the borrower (the person who received the loan) and the co-borrower (the one who vouched for the person to whom the loan is issued) can be called up by the bank's experts and ask questions of a more precise nature regarding work, acquired housing, the amount for which the loan is granted, and loan terms.

If there are problems identified during the analysis of the documentation provided, the bank may refuse to grant a loan without explanation. This is another reason not to mess with unscrupulous developers.

After approval of the loan, the required amount is transferred to the account of the developer, the buyer is given details on the loan repayment, conditions and payment schedules.

The developer (or the representative of the developer) is sent to the nearest multifunctional center to obtain a certificate of ownership by the buyer.

After receiving this document, you can go to the sales department or the housing and communal services to get the keys to the apartment.
