VIK Arena Blanca 4 * (Dominican Republic, Punta Cana): hotel infrastructure, room description, service, reviews

The Dominican Republic is an interesting, but so far only growing destination among tourists from Russia. Unfortunately, due to an expensive flight, she loses to the more budgetary resort countries - Turkey and Thailand. But in vain, because the Dominican Republic boasts whitewashed sandy beaches, a warm sea and luxurious hotels. And history buffs will be interested to look at Santo Domingo - the former colonial capital and the oldest city in the Caribbean.

We will tell in this article about one of the most popular options for accommodation in the Dominican Republic - VIK Arena Blanca 4 * hotel.

Hotel location

This beach resort is located in the heart of one of the most popular destinations in the Dominican Republic - the resort of Punta Cana. First of all, it is famous for its snow-white sandy coast and excellent ecology. There are 3 national parks around this small town, so the air here is good for health. Punta Cana will appeal to tourists who love a relaxing holiday. The resort has almost no nightclubs, but diving centers and restaurants specializing in seafood are located everywhere. It is not surprising that most often families come here with young children and newlyweds.

The VIK Hotel Arena Blanca 4 * complex is located directly on the sandy coast on the first line of the sea. Shopping lovers will also enjoy its location. A large shopping center is open just a 5-minute walk, where you can buy not only food and souvenirs, but also clothes, shoes and jewelry. A diving shop and several restaurants are also located next to the hotel. Punta Cana Airport, which receives flights from Russia, is 18 km from the hotel. Therefore, tourists can get to it after arrival in just half an hour.

Basic hotel information

The resort of Punta Cana is hardly a budget vacation spot. The entire coast here is built up with luxurious five-star complexes. Among them, this hotel stands out, which is popular with tourists because it offers more budget accommodation.

It should be noted that in fact two complexes are located on the same territory at once. Close to the four-star hotel is bordered by his five-star counterpart - VIK Hotel Cayena Beach. This is a small exclusive beach complex on the territory of which other guests cannot.

Additional room facilities

Since the VIK Arena Blanca 4 * hotel (Dominican Republic, Punta Cana) is focused on inexpensive holidays, they cannot boast of a large area and rich equipment. But there is still a standard set of amenities.

Checking in the room, tourists will find in it a plasma TV, wired telephone, hairdryer and minibar. On the wall is a ceiling fan. All of these amenities are free of charge. Every day, the maids bring drinking water to the guests during cleaning. Bed linens and towels are changed according to the rules at least 3 times a week.

The air conditioning at the hotel is central. The guests themselves cannot change the cooling temperature - it is regulated by the employees of the complex. For a fee, an electronic safe is provided where you can leave your documents and money. You can make free phone calls only at the reception. The rest is subject to an additional charge. There is no wireless internet in the rooms.

Infrastructure and service

As a rule, tourists spend almost their entire vacation in the complex. Therefore, the infrastructure of the VIK Arena Blanca 4 * hotel is well developed. Guests are invited to:

  • 24-hour reception, where you can arrange car rental or exchange currency;
  • jewelry store, flower boutique and convenience store;
  • ATM in the hotel lobby;
  • car parking;
  • conference hall;
  • laundry;
  • barbershop;
  • gym.

Most of these infrastructure facilities provide their services for a fee. The administrator at your request can call a doctor in the room. A wedding service is also available on site. Wireless internet is only available in the lobby.

Catering

The price of accommodation at the VIK Arena Blanca 4 * hotel in the Dominican Republic includes an all-inclusive food system. This means that all tourists during the holidays can count on a full three meals a day, as well as snacks and drinks in between. Also available alcohol and desserts. Vegetarian and diet menus are provided separately.

The hotel has several catering establishments:

  • Mexican, Italian and international restaurants;
  • lounge bar;
  • Lobby bar;
  • two bars on the beach;
  • night club.

All restaurants have a dress code. Visitors in beachwear are not allowed in them. Tourists can dine in restaurants for free, the number of visits is not limited.

Beach holidays at the hotel

The VIK Arena Blanca 4 * hotel in the Dominican Republic has its own coastal stretch of about 400 meters. It is completely sandy. All towels, sun loungers and sun umbrellas are provided free of charge. The beach has its own water entertainment center. Guests can go diving, diving with a mask or ride on motorless boats and catamarans.

Conditions for placing with small children

According to tourists, the VIK Arena Blanca 4 * complex in the Dominican Republic is an excellent option for families with children. Children up to one and a half years old are provided with a separate cradle for free. Upon request, the administrator will call a nanny for your child directly in the room. However, her services will not be included in the price. Babysitters also speak English only.

The hotel has a mini club, which accepts children from 4 to 12 years. Kids can also swim in a separate shallow pool or play in the open area with swings and a sandbox.

Positive feedback about VIK Hotel Arena Blanca 4 * in the Dominican Republic

If you are in doubt about choosing a hotel, be sure to read the reviews of tourists. As a rule, guests in this resort complex were satisfied. They are ready to recommend this place to their friends and relatives. Accordingly, such tourists leave mostly positive reviews.

What did the guests of VIK Arena Blanca 4 * in the Dominican Republic like most? In their opinion, this hotel has the following advantages:

  • clean rooms with a new renovation, in the living rooms there is no smell of moisture and insects;
