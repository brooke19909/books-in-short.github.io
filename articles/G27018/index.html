Hotel Sui Resort Hotel 5 * Turkey, Alanya: traveler reviews

Okurcalar village is an ideal holiday destination for people who dream of a peaceful vacation, which is planned only for spending time on the beach, swimming in the sea, relaxing walks and pleasant evenings in restaurants. And in order for the weekend in Turkey to succeed, you need to choose a good hotel with a decent level of service. That is what Sui Resort Hotel 5 * is. However, a little more can be said about its merits.





Location

Okurcalar, which is located Sui Resort Hotel 5 *, is 90 kilometers from Antalya Airport. And about 30 minutes drive from Alanya - a major local resort and seaport.

There are practically no entertainments, and attractions too. Is that the ancient defensive fortress of Alarahan, located 6 kilometers from the hotel complex. Here it is worth a trip. It is located on a high mountain, from where an amazing view of the surroundings opens, for the sake of which many come here.

In addition, after visiting the fortress, you can go to nearby Side. It is not only a popular resort, but also a place where interesting cultural and historical sights are concentrated - the ancient theater, the Manavgata fountain, the basilica at the Roman Theater, the ancient city of Lirbe and much more.





What is in the hotel?

On the territory of Sui Resort Hotel 5 * there is everything that should be in a hotel of this level - from high-speed public Wi-Fi to free private parking. The reception, of course, works here 24/7.

There are several pools (including an indoor one), a real Turkish bath, a sauna, a fitness center with a gym, a sports ground, a nightclub, and also a SPA salon where guests can order a variety of procedures, whether massage, body wraps, peeling or something else. All the same, at first the guests will be introduced to the list of services offered.

It is worth noting that there is a free shuttle service to the beach. True, it is only a five-minute walk away. By the way, guests of Sui Resort Hotel 5 * are offered free access to the water park.

Nutrition

In Sui Resort Hotel 5 *, like in many other Turkish hotels, the all-inclusive system operates.

There are three restaurants for guests. The first is the main one, and it serves a daily buffet for breakfast, lunch and dinner.

The second restaurant is themed and specializes in Mediterranean cuisine. True gourmets will definitely like it, as it offers a huge assortment of a wide variety of seafood. But this institution is open only for dinner. To visit it, you need to book a table in advance, and also come in accordance with the evening dress code.

The third restaurant is also themed. Specialization - Turkish cuisine. Like the previous establishment, this restaurant is open only for dinner. Need a reservation table and matching dress code.

Accommodation options

In Sui Resort Hotel 5 * (Turkey, Alanya) only 225 rooms. In terms of furnishings and amenities, they are all almost the same.

All apartments have a plasma TV with satellite channels, a safe, a climate system (air conditioning + heating), a wardrobe, a dryer and a clothes rack, a minibar, a bathroom with hygiene products, a balcony and a living area. Rooms differ only in size and price. And here is the approximate cost of living for 7 days, including VAT and payment "all inclusive":

  • Economy room with a large 2 double bed and one standard, 25 square meters. m. About 75-80 thousand rubles. for two people. 100-105 thousand rubles - for three. The only option without a balcony.
  • Standard room, 25 square meters. m. By sleeping places - similar to the previous version. 80-85 thousand rubles. for two vacationers, one child can stay for free. For three adults, the price will be 105-115 thousand rubles.
  • Family Room, 45 Sq. m. Inside there is a large 2 double bed and two standard. Price - 120-130 thousand rubles for 2 adults and 2 children. The same price is set if 3 guests over 18 years old (with or without a child) are accommodated in this apartment. But for four adults, the price of living will be 145-160 thousand rubles.

The latter option, by the way, is very profitable. In the best case, each of the vacationers will pay only about 35,000 rubles per week in a 5-star hotel on an all-inclusive basis.





Guests on the pros of the hotel

It will not be superfluous to pay attention to reviews of Sui Resort Hotel 5 *. Here are the features that people who have already rested here relate to its obvious advantages:

Tips from experienced tourists

Travelers who have already been to Alanya at Sui Resort Hotel 5 * love to share recommendations that can help other people who are just going here. Here's what they say:

  • It is better not to book apartments located on the minus floors. Since they have a view of the concrete wall from the outside, and from the other, the pool, where it is always noisy, because the animation takes place there.
  • If possible, it is better to plan a vacation in mid-late spring or early fall. But at least - not in July-August. At this time, the hotel has a lot of vacationers with children, and you can only dream of silence, especially during breakfast / lunch / dinner.
  • If you suddenly needed to replace a towel, linen or something to remove - just find a maid in the hallway and ask her about it.
