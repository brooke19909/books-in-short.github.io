Camera

The camera in the Lenovo K6 Power Gold turned out to be a good solution. Users left good reviews, especially regarding the front module. It works with a resolution of 8 megapixels, has a portrait mode with face editing.

The main camera works with a resolution of 13 megapixels. It produces medium quality pictures. This is not enough to create photo masterpieces, but enough for social networks. Better to shoot in the afternoon. At night, the photos are very bad.

Offline work

This version has a 4000 mAh battery for Lenovo K6 Power. She received good reviews, although the manufacturer's statements did not materialize. The company claims that in standby mode, the smartphone will last 11 days. If you talk, then two days.





In fact, these figures are slightly less. With moderate load, it can withstand two days. Up to 6 hours can last an active screen. The conversation will drag on for 2 hours.

Reviews

That turned out to be such a good budget smartphone Lenovo K6 Power 16Gb. He received good reviews on average. Anyone who understood that in front of him was the middle segment, and did not expect anything supernatural, in return received a solid device.

The users liked the screen, quick system update, good camera, beautiful appearance. The stereo speakers turned out to be quite good paired with conversational. The battery also paid off.

But the negative emotions were caused by the sound in the headphones, the lack of fast charging, the combined slot and the incomprehensible operation of the additional microphone.

If you close your eyes to some improvements, you can safely agree that the manufacturer managed to create a balanced budget device with the best price / quality ratio.
