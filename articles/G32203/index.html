Cottage village Shishkino (Berezovsky): description and photo

Those who are guided by the choice of housing by two important criteria, namely: favorable environmental conditions and proximity to urban civilization, should pay attention to the cottage village Shishkino (Berezovsky). Choosing this project, you find yourself in a measured and calm rhythm of life, which optimally contrasts with the developed infrastructure available here. The village is located twelve kilometers from Yekaterinburg, on the territory of the Berezovsky administrative district.

The cottage village Shishkino (Berezovsky) is under construction. At the same time, housing is being built at the expense of the buyer himself (when he acquires the land) and by the forces of the construction company, which is EkaterinburgGorStroy.
