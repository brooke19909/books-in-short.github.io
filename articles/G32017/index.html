Smartline Protaras 3 * (Cyprus, Protaras): hotel description, services, reviews

Hotel Smartline Protaras 3 * (Cyprus, Protaras) is located in a picturesque corner of our Earth. Its location is very favorable, since the complex is located near the city. To get to the center, you need to overcome 2 kilometers. You can walk this distance or use the services of a taxi or bus. You can easily reach all the necessary attractions and shops, but the noise of the city is far from the hotel, and you can enjoy a truly relaxing holiday.





To get to the beach, you need to walk a few minutes, and before your eyes a picturesque seascape opens. And lovers of spending time without leaving the territory of the complex can use the pool, which is located in the courtyard of the hotel. There are also sun loungers for those who want to sunbathe.

On the territory of the Smartline Protaras 3 * complex (Protaras) there is a wonderful tennis court. Here you can have a great time, while actively relaxing and, possibly, making new acquaintances. Throughout the hotel there is free Wi-Fi, the password from which will be reported upon arrival at the reception.

Number of rooms

The hotel rooms are simply furnished. All furniture made of wood has clear geometric shapes. Such an interior adds some zest to the hotel.

Each room has a TV with many channels, air conditioning, a small refrigerator and even a tea set with several cups, saucers, spoons and a kettle.

Guests are offered tea bags, coffee and cocoa. The presence of a tea set is very convenient, because you can prepare a cup of hot drink right in your room while enjoying the view that opens from the windows. There is a balcony in almost every room. From it you can see incredible beauty views of the Mediterranean Sea.

Nutrition





On the territory of the Smartline Protaras 3 * hotel (Cyprus, Protaras) there is a cozy restaurant called Pavilion, where you can try national dishes. And for those who do not like experiments, the menu includes dishes of all the peoples of the world.

Near the pool there is a cozy garden where the Windmill tavern stands. It is here that you can order refreshing cocktails or enjoy a cup of coffee. Meals are included in the price.

Entertainment





For guests, all conditions are created for the most comfortable stay. Missing this hotel is simply impossible. You can entertain yourself by playing squash. It also offers relaxing massages and a relaxing atmosphere. For those who, even during the holidays, take care of their uniforms, there is a well-equipped gymnasium where a professional trainer helps each client.

It is recommended to visit the sauna for complete relaxation and soothing. For fans of more active pastimes, it is suggested to visit the games room, where you can play table tennis or billiards. As an evening entertainment, the Smartline Protaras Hotel 3 * hotel comes up with special programs, and several times a week in the evenings there is live music.

The administration also took care of the little guests, for whom a wonderful game room was created.

About rooms





The standard rooms have a balcony with scenic sea views. But these apartments are slightly more expensive. Each room has an old but working air conditioning, a hairdryer, an electric kettle, a bottle of water, coffee, tea, a TV and a paid safe. On the balcony there is a table with chairs.

Pleases guests and the availability of new plumbing. Electricity and other equipment, according to their reviews, works without interruptions. A slight minus is a small window opposite the bed, the light from which prevents sleep in the morning.

About nutrition





Those who choose the All Inclusive system will not regret it at all. Meals include breakfast, lunch and dinner. You can also drink juices and alcoholic beverages for free at any time. Snacks at the bar are served with sandwiches and ice cream. At each meal, you must say the room number.

Breakfast consists of dry cereal with milk, sausages, eggs in various variations and delicious fried bacon.

Lunch and dinner consists of meat or fish dishes - to choose from, a side dish (pasta or potatoes) and fresh vegetables. The food is not very diverse, but, according to tourists, everything is very well and deliciously prepared.

Service

The hotel staff is very friendly. Many speak Russian. Bar workers even remember the rooms of regular customers, and there is no need to constantly tell them.

Even without tip guests are served very well. Bed linen and towels are changed once a week, but more often on request. Cleaning is carried out daily when there is no one in the room. If necessary, they can issue a spare key.

About entertainment

During the day, guests are entertained by animators. Little but fun. Of the active events, water aerobics in the pool and water polo can be noted. You can also play table tennis. In the evenings, children can go to the disco. Adults have the opportunity to enjoy beautiful live music or to show their talents in karaoke.

Mostly in the evenings, people gather at the bar. Parents enjoy drinks and socializing, and children play. Children's daytime activities include a pool, playground and games room.

Territory





The hotel is located in a small area. This is rather a plus for those who come with children. The complex is surrounded on all sides by green spaces. There is even a small waterfall. The pool is spacious, and for lovers of swimming there is where to take a walk. The right part of the water zone (when viewed from the side of the hotel) is intended for children. But for them there is also a separate round pool. Kids can play here at their pleasure, splashing out of water pistols and diving under water in masks.

Unfortunately, this hotel does not have its own mini water park. But in the complex "Anastasia", located on the road to the sea, there are wonderful water attractions. On a special flyer, you can even get a discount of several euros. Children will definitely like it here. And adults will find a lot of extreme entertainment.

Beach vacation

As experienced travelers warn, depending on the time of year, water in the sea can change the temperature. Sometimes in the evenings it cools down very much, and swimming is almost impossible if you are not a seasoned person. The beach here is sandy, which is ideal for families with children. The road to the sea takes about 10 minutes of a leisurely step. Along the way, you can peek into local retail stores. The beach itself is equipped with changing rooms, and for an additional fee, you can use sun loungers and umbrellas.

If desired, you can go to the central beach with a sandy bottom and a gentle entrance to the water. Within walking distance are many other beaches and there are even wild places with chic nature. But the sea is almost equally beautiful everywhere. The beach is covered with soft sand. But many children still prefer to spend time in the hotel pool.
