The best tablet on Android: a review of models, specifications and reviews

Today's market for mobile devices is filled with various devices, many of which run on Android. As a rule, all manufacturers offer several models of tablet PCs, which are not always large, but still significant differences. How to choose the best tablet on Android, what properties should it have?

Key selection criteria

In order to determine which tablet on Android is better, you need to check the gadget for several basic parameters:





  1. Diagonal display. The size that will become optimal for the buyer can only be decided by him, based on the tasks for which the device is purchased.
  2. Screen resolution. The higher it is, the seemingly it should be better, only there is one thing: the processor must comply with this resolution, since with a weak processor the picture will slow down and be viewed intermittently.
  3. Battery capacity. The tablet computer‚Äôs operating time will depend on it, the more it is, the longer the device works. Do not trust the indicated hours of operation too much, this parameter depends on too many factors to be calculated up to a minute.
  4. Version of the operating system. Not only the gadget interface should change, but also the OS, today it should be at least 4.3, 4.4

What kind of memory should a tablet have

To identify the best tablet on Android, you need to pay special attention to its memory. Given that Android is an OS, which allows you to easily download programs using the market or other sources, this option is of great importance.





It is necessary to pay attention to:

  1. RAM More precisely, its size, the minimum value should not be lower than 1 GB. With less RAM, the tablet‚Äôs performance is unlikely to please.
  2. The presence of a slot for an SD card. A tablet computer without the ability to connect additional memory does not deserve attention.

CPU Recommendations

As a rule, a tablet is not purchased for production needs. More often the reason for the purchase is entertainment and leisure activities. Therefore, the best tablet on Android should have an excellent processor so that there are no problems with various games and applications stuffed with graphics.

So, a good engine should have:

  1. Four cores, then it will cope with all applications, and fans of modern computer games should choose eight-core models in general.
  2. Clock frequency more than 1 GHz. Ideally, if it is in the range of 1.8-2.5 GHz.

Having studied all the characteristics, it is not at all difficult to choose the best android (tablet) for yourself.

What else will facilitate the search

The best advisor in choosing gadgets can be experience. Advanced users who are looking for themselves is not the first tablet computer, and who are familiar with its standard functionality, will be guided by choosing, first of all, what tasks are set for the device.





It is known that the minimum screen diagonal of 7 inches is not enough to fully enjoy games or movies, for these purposes it is better to choose gadgets with a screen of 9.7 inches or more. And for clarity, a high resolution is required. Also of particular note is the camera. Two megapixels is enough for video communication, but you can get high-quality photos only with a 5-megapixel camera and higher.

Before buying, it will not be superfluous to read customer reviews about the model you like.

Which operating system is better

Along with Android tablet computers, sellers are rushing to offer Windows devices. Which tablet is better - "Android" or "Windows"?

The first and very important criterion for buyers when buying any device is price. Android wins here, not everyone decides to overpay for a new system.

"Windows" have proven themselves in smartphones, but have not yet achieved the same reputation in the work of tablets. The proven Android is still better. Windows is significantly behind in terms of interface and functionality.

"Android" is being improved with each new version. In terms of touch capabilities, Windows does not meet specified standards, and the main drawback is the incompatibility of this OS with the ARM architecture common in tablets.

Speaking about whether to purchase a tablet on Windows or Android (which is better to choose), it is worth noting the uselessness of Intel processors in tablet PCs. Therefore, to date, the arguments in favor of Android more.

Antiviruses for Android tablets

The majority of viruses that exist today are Trojans. First of all, they get information about the phone number, personal data and credit cards, while taking the form of useful applications.

Given that tablet computers are used, as a rule, to access the Internet, it is simply necessary to protect them from any network muck.

Choosing the best antivirus for the Android tablet is quite difficult. The fact is that there are a large number of high-quality, and at the same time, free antiviruses for Android. Among others, you can find proven and well-known anti-virus programs: Dr.Web, Kaspersky, AVG, Norton, McAfee.





Most tablet owners do not even think about installing antivirus programs, considering them completely useless. But it is desirable that at least one protective product be installed on the device. This will help to avoid most of the problems that arise when working on the network and downloading programs and files.

In addition, you will not have to pay for them, which is pleasantly pleasing and allows you to choose the product that is ideal for a particular user.
