Azalea Apart Hotel 4 *. Turkey, Alanya, 4 star hotels

Alanya is considered the warmest Turkish city. Perhaps this feature made the resort so popular. In addition, the city has such a developed infrastructure that even the most avid sybarites come to rest here.

A busy motorway provides quick and easy access to another major Turkish resort - the capital of this tourist region - Antalya.

Where to stay



Turkey has long been considered neighboring countries for Russians. Mostly those who are attracted to beach vacations and low prices come here to relax. I must say that, judging by the reviews of tourists, stardom in this region does not matter much. After all, Alanya is one of the most inexpensive Turkish resorts. And his luxury apartments are not much different from hotels with a lower level of comfort.

The best hotels in Alanya, with four or five star categories, are located mainly on the first line from the sea. They are distinguished not only by a high level of service, but also by reasonable prices and an excellent combination of low cost and quality. Among them, Azalea Apart Hotel 4 * is not the last in the popularity rating.

general information

The hotel is located right in the center of Alanya, a five-minute walk from the famous Cleopatra Beach in this resort. It was built relatively recently, however, two years ago there was a major overhaul with a change in plumbing, furniture and furnishings in the rooms. Since this hotel is considered a city, it has a relatively small area. Nevertheless, it is fully landscaped and arranged so that residents are comfortable and cozy.



The airport is one hundred and twenty kilometers distant. Many local attractions are located nearby, there are cafes, restaurants and shops that can be reached on foot while walking along the resort's main promenade.

Infrastructure

The level of Azalea Apart Hotel (Turkey, Alanya) is 4 stars, and its hotel justifies a wide range of services offered. The hotel has a paid medical office, ATMs, laundry. The reception is open around the clock. At the counter you can use the fax, arrange a transfer, as well as rent a car, bike or taxi. The hotel has a hairdresser, there is a paid parking lot for cars, which must be reserved in advance. The whole territory is guarded, and modern elevators work between floors in the building.

Number of rooms

The Azalea Apart Hotel 4 is not great. In its only five-story building, there are only ninety rooms in which two adults and one child can live at the same time, for which upon request you can get an extra bed - a cot.

The rooms have everything you need so that vacationers spend their holidays without problems - new furniture, a wardrobe, a TV, bedside tables, comfortable single or double beds with comfortable mattresses, there is also a large makeup mirror. Azalea Apart Hotel 4 is positioned as an apart-hotel, therefore its family apartments have a kitchen area with a fridge, stove, sink and dining table. In addition, there are upholstered furniture and a coffee table.



Bathrooms - combined with a bathtub and flexible shower. Everything that is necessary for personal hygiene, including towels, is updated daily. Room cleaning is carried out in good faith and unobtrusively - when there are no guests.

Beach



To the beach "Cleopatra" vacationers reach a maximum of five minutes. Everything you need here must be purchased at an additional cost. But the beach infrastructure is very developed, there are toilets, changing rooms, showers. Coating - sand with small pebbles. Entrance to the water here is convenient even for children. Special shoes are not required.

Hotel Policies

Check-in at Azalea Apart Hotel (Alanya 4) is quick. Judging by the reviews, it lasts a maximum of fifteen minutes. Each settling tourist is provided with a printout of information about the services offered by the hotel. In all areas, even by the pool, there is an opportunity for free Internet connection.

Upon departure, vacationers are required to vacate the room they occupy before noon. When returning the keys, residents are required to finally pay not only for accommodation (if this is not done in advance), but also for those services that they used, but which were not included in the price.

For convenience, the account can be closed both in cash and with plastic cards from many systems. Moreover, for those who prefer cashless payments, it is better to discuss the possibility of this type of payment with the hotel administration in advance to avoid misunderstandings.

For children



Azalea Apart Hotel 4, reviews of tourists which indicate that this is a great place for a relaxing holiday, is usually crowded. Vacationers from the Scandinavian countries, Germans and French come here. Here you can rarely meet Russians. Nevertheless, such an international composition of vacationers is not an obstacle for the children to feel comfortable and communicate well with each other on the playground, which is specially equipped for them.

For younger guests there is also a children's section in the pool, a high chair for feeding in the restaurant and, upon request, a special menu in the restaurant.

Entertainment

Those who are not willing to go to the sea can spend no less comfort and pleasure sunbathing and swimming directly on its territory by the pool. There is also a bar where you can refresh yourself quite well.



In addition to swimming in the pool or relaxing on the beach, the hotel has the opportunity to visit the sauna or hammam - Turkish bath. Guests can also enjoy massages or beauty treatments. In addition, the hotel has an excellent beauty salon: here you can do a manicure or a new hairstyle.

Reviews

In general, Azalea Apart Hotel 4 (Turkey) met the expectations of those few Russian tourists who rested in it.



The beach is very clean and large, there are comfortable sun loungers, umbrellas, sand cover. Some vacationers, however, do not like that you have to pay separately for amenities, but this is because the Cleopatra bathing area is municipal and not the property of the hotel.

As for the number of rooms, then, according to some reviews, it is compared even with five-star hotels in Alanya. Most vacationers prefer Azalea Apart Hotel 4, finding that the conditions for living in this four-star hotel are very comfortable for young children.

A lot of good words have been said about the staff. The food, according to tourists, is very tasty. In the restaurant you can always order dishes to your taste. Some, of course, do not like the lack of animation, but this is a fixable thing. For a more active pastime, you can always go to the city center.
