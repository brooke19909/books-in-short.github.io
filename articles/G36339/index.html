How to sew a butterfly on the neck: sequence of work

It often happens that a family is invited to an official event, but the husband does not have a tie or does not like to wear it. In this case, it can be replaced by a butterfly. And you can do it yourself. Before stitching a butterfly on the neck, you should definitely choose the right fabric and various accessories: lining, hooks for fastening.



For work, you need the main fabric (satin or another), threads of a suitable shade, chalk or thin soap for marking the material, pattern and pins with which it will be attached. Naturally, you will need a sewing machine, although such a thing can be done manually, however, the procedure will need to be carried out very carefully.

Before stitching the butterfly on the neck, make a pattern on the main and lining fabric. Now we put the cut out pattern on the back side of the main material and draw a contour with chalk. During the cutting of parts of the product, it is necessary to observe a slight indentation, which subsequently will need to be overcast. The allowance is usually up to 0.5 cm. Naturally, you need to make two parts of the future butterfly, which will be stitched.



Before stitching the butterfly with your own hands, it is necessary to sweep the non-woven fabric to the main fabric. Naturally, all parts must be smooth. The main line should go along the edges of the product, and then the workpiece should be twisted and stitched on the sides. After this, the tie should be ironed again. Next, you need to fix the butterfly on that strip of fabric with which it will be tied around the neck.

It should be noted that the allowance should be cut as close to the seam as possible so that it does not spoil the shape of the product. During eversion, you should also pay attention to the corners.
