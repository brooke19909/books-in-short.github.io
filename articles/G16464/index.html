Services

The list of services that guests of the Blue Crane Hotel APTS 3 * (Limassol) can use is quite wide for a three-star hotel. First of all, the hotel has its own medical office, which allows vacationers to use medical care if necessary, without leaving the room. Wi-Fi is free, but only by the pool and in the hotel lobby. Room service consists of regular cleaning and changing of bed linen and towels approximately every three days. In addition, the wake up call service operates.

Agios Tihanos

Near the ruins of ancient Amathus is located the much less ancient Agios Tihanos, once a small village named after Bishop Limassol - St. Tikhon, now a full-fledged resort town with sandy beaches, hotels and the most famous diving club in Cyprus.

One of the main attractions of the city is Agios Nikolaos - a church in the name of St. Nicholas, located in the oldest part of the village. It was built relatively recently, in two thousand and seven, by the efforts of local fishermen and, in addition to its direct purpose, serves as a kind of lighthouse for those who are from the sea. Icons are stored here, the writing of which dates back to the 19th century. Every Sunday in Agios Nikolaos there are services in Russian.

Tourists who find themselves in Agios Tihanos, are also eager to visit the ruins of the temple of St. Tikhon, which, according to legend, contains healing power.
