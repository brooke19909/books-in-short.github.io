Energetik, a camp site in Tolyatti. Description, services, reviews

The cozy and spacious Energetik camp site was created specifically for corporate parties, as well as for relaxing couples and families with small children. This complex was built on the territory of the coastal zone of the Volga River, surrounded by beautiful coniferous and deciduous forests. The accommodation fund consists mainly of log cottages, which have everything for a comfortable stay.





For weddings, the Energetik camp site has a special gazebo and two restaurants with a chic and varied menu. For various forms of active pastime, special fields are equipped, and on the beach there is any necessary equipment (umbrellas, deck chairs, sun loungers, etc.).
For those tourists who prefer a vacation with a truly Russian scale, at the disposal of the tourist base there is a Russian bath with a pool and a jacuzzi function.

As for families with small children, for such groups of vacationers are offered qualified babysitting services, a games room and a kids club.
