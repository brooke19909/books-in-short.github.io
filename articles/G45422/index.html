Kata Happy House Resort 3: overview, description and reviews of tourists

When planning a vacation in Thailand, many tourists choose Phuket as their vacation spot. After all, there is a mass of hotels for every taste, excellent beaches, as well as plenty of entertainment. If you plan to spend your vacation in this resort and are looking for a budget hotel, we suggest that you consider the three-star Kata Happy House Resort hotel as an option for your stay.



Location

This hotel is a 20-minute walk from the popular Kata Beach. Near it there are many restaurants and entertainment venues. The journey from the nearest airport to the hotel takes about 50 minutes.

Description, hotel photo

Kata Happy House Resort 3 * (Phuket, Thailand) is a small cozy hotel. The housing stock of the hotel consists of only 30 rooms of the categories "standard" and "deluxe". All apartments have a TV (with Russian-language channels), a refrigerator, an electric kettle, tea and coffee making facilities, air conditioning, a safe, a bathroom with a hairdryer and a furnished balcony. Free internet access is also available. Rooms are cleaned every day. Linen and towels are updated several times a week.





The hotel area is small but very nicely decorated. It has an outdoor pool, sauna, jacuzzi, and a gym. The hotel's restaurant serves breakfast only. You can always eat during the day in one of the restaurants in the vicinity of Kata Happy House. At the hotel you can use the dry cleaning and laundry services, order a transfer to the airport, rent a car or a moped, buy a tour in the direction you are interested in. There is a small car park on site. Therefore, if you rent a car, you will not have problems with parking.

The hotel does not have a private beach. Every morning a shuttle service is organized to the shore from here.

Kata Happy House Resort 3 *: reviews of Russian tourists

As you know, modern travelers are becoming more demanding. So, when choosing a hotel, it is not enough for them to simply study the official description and listen to the recommendations of the travel agency. Today, many people are trying to get acquainted with the reviews of those who have already happened to stay in a particular hotel. This helps to create a picture closer to reality and make the right choice.





In this regard, we decided to save you some time and give generalized comments from our compatriots regarding their vacation at Kata Happy House Resort. It should be noted that most reviews are positive. But we will understand in more detail.

Hotel and location

Accommodation at Kata Happy House Resort 3 * (Phuket) all tourists were satisfied. So, according to them, the rooms here are small, but quite spacious. There is all necessary furniture, as well as a terrace with a table and chairs. Here, vacationers enjoyed spending time with a cup of coffee or a glass of wine while admiring the surroundings. All equipment is in working condition. The guests liked that the rooms have an electric kettle. The cleaning of the rooms, according to the assurances of our compatriots, was carried out regularly and was carried out quite qualitatively. In this regard, the guests had no complaints. Travelers note that the hotel has free Wi-Fi. Communication sometimes breaks, but in general, the Internet works well.





As for the location of the hotel, according to most tourists, it will appeal to those who plan to spend time actively and not sit all day in the hotel and on the beach. So, to the coast from the "Kata Happy House" walk about half an hour. The hotel provides a shuttle service to the beach in the morning. But most of the guests preferred to rent mopeds. This can be done both in the hotel itself and in one of the many rental offices located in the surrounding area. By the way, experienced travelers are sure to recommend taking this opportunity, as this will allow you to go around all the nearby attractions and beaches yourself.

In the vicinity of the hotel in question there are a lot of restaurants, cafes, bars, currency exchange points, shops, travel agencies, rental agencies, etc. So everything you need is within walking distance.

Food Impressions

The cost of living at Kata Happy House includes breakfast only. They, according to most tourists, are quite acceptable. So, you will be offered eggs in different forms, sausages, rice, chicken, jam, sausages and a number of simple products. For lunch and dinner you can in one of the many restaurants located in the vicinity of the hotel. Travelers recommend going to different places, as this will allow you to try new interesting dishes of local cuisine.

Entertainment Comments

Of course, the vast majority of tourists stay in Thailand for a beach holiday. Therefore, the issue of remoteness of the hotel from the sea plays an important role. The beach closest to the hotel in question is Kata Beach. Kata Happy House Resort 3 * is removed from it at a distance of about a kilometer. However, according to tourists, walking in the heat this way can be very tiring. Therefore, they recommend using a transfer from the hotel, calling a taxi or renting a moped. As for the beach itself, it is big and clean. There are enough places for everyone. On the beach there are lots of cafes and restaurants where you can eat during the day without having to return to the city. By the way, guests note that at the Kata Happy House hotel guests are provided with beach towels. So you do not need to take them from home or buy directly in Phuket.





As for entertainment directly at the hotel, here guests have a pool and a sauna.

Personnel Opinion

Many tourists note in their reviews the friendly and excellent staff. So, according to them, the employees here are always ready to help and answer any questions. Check in the rooms almost immediately after arrival, without condemning them to useless waiting. In case of any problems in the room, the problems are fixed very quickly. It is only necessary to inform the receptionist about the incident.
