Step-by-step lesson: how to make a box of paper

It’s not always that we have at hand a box of a completely certain size for specific needs. For example, it would be nice to carefully put small treasures into it, divide gloves, socks, hairpins, scarves into compartments, or make a gift. Therefore, knowing how to make a box of paper is worth a lot!

Step 1

Take a square piece of paper. Carefully fold it exactly diagonally in half. First, on the one hand, then on the other. Expand the sheet.





Step 2

Fold the sheet in half horizontally. Expand it again. To achieve the best results in solving the simple problem of how to make a box of paper, you need to fold the blank carefully, each time carefully aligning the edges from corner to corner.



Step 3

Repeat the folding action, but vertically.



Step 4

At this stage of the lesson, how to make a box of paper, you should get a sheet with crossed lines from the folds exactly in the center.





Step 5

Fold in turn all 4 corners exactly to the center of the square where the lines from the folds converge.



Step 6

The upper half of the resulting workpiece must be folded in half.



Step 7

Repeat the same action, but with the bottom half.



Step 8

Fold the halves and release the two opposite corners, as shown in the illustration:



Step 9

Rotate the folding sheet 90 degrees.



Step 10

Fold half the workpiece from the top edge to the center.



Step 11

Fold the bottom edge to the center, as in the previous step.



Step 12

So far, the origami class and the description of the process of how to make a box of paper seemed rather far from the final result. But at this stage of the lesson, finally, the man-made box gains "sides". To do this, open the just-folded pieces of paper 90 degrees.



Step 13

Raise the open end while pressing the corners as shown in the illustration until you get another “side” of the box.



Step 14

Lower the top corner so that it fits exactly in the center of the base of the paper product.



Step 15

Repeat on the other side of steps nos. 13 and 14.



Step 16

This step is not necessary, but if desired, you can glue the rising corners at the bottom of the box. Done!



To make a lid for a box, you need to take a paper square 4 millimeters larger than the previous one and fold it in the same way. Then connect both parts, and you will have a full box with a lid on your hands. Now you know how to make a gift box. Of cardboard, of course, it will be stronger. Presentable box should be colored. Bright or in restrained tones - it depends on the event.

Perhaps making the box will prompt you to learn how to make a vase out of paper with your own hands, a lampshade, or other no less interesting things. Do not forget to involve children in the manufacture of such gizmos. General classes are not only training useful and interesting, but also unforgettable moments of communication.
