Alanya Klas Hotel (Turkey / Alanya / Mahmutlar): hotel description and reviews

Near Alanya, in neighboring villages, a vibrant tourist life boils all season. We invite you to the small but unusually picturesque Mahmutlar, near which - almost on the seashore - is located the excellent 4-star Alanya Klas Hotel. It belongs to a single hotel chain with a positive and promising name Klas. First, visit Alanya Klas Hotel 4 *. It is located a stone's throw from First Class Hotel 5 *, therefore guests of one and the second hotel can use many infrastructure facilities without the slightest obstacle. With the location of these two hotels, as well as other similar establishments of the Klas chain, there is little confusion. Tour operators report that they are in the village of Mahmutlar. In fact this is not true. To clarify the situation. In 2014, Mahmutlar officially became a district of Alanya, one might say, its suburb. From it to the city center a little over 11 km. An excellent asphalt road connects these settlements , along which minibuses ‚Äúrun‚Äù all day. You can get to the city in 20 minutes. On the seashore, you can also get to Mahmutlar from Alanya (walk), since beaches stretch along the coast with an endless ribbon. This former village, and now the urban area itself is small, there are only three central streets in it, but its infrastructure is well developed. There are many supermarkets, bars, restaurants, pharmacies, the Russian Cultural Center, discos, beauty and health salons, and a market. From the eastern side (farthest from Alanya), Mahmutlar smoothly passes into the village called Kargichak, the border between which as such is not. This is the farthest resort in the region. It is remarkable only for its natural beauties - the Taurus Mountains, the sea coast and orange gardens. The infrastructure here is poorly developed, but hotel prices are the cheapest in all of Alanya. It is in this place that those who choose tours to the Alanya Klas Hotel will have a rest. General information

Description of the hotel Alanya Klas Hotel we will start with its territory. Although there is another Klas hotel in the neighborhood, the territory of this hotel has its own territory. It is small, only 10,000 m 2 , but it has two swimming pools, restaurants, bars, a shop and a spa center. There was a place for a playground. The whole territory is very clean and green, exotic palm trees grow around, pine trees more familiar to our eyes, as well as oranges and olives.

The hotel was built in 2005, and in 2015 there was a total reconstruction, so that so far all the equipment in it is new and functional. The building is five-story, quite attractive from the outside, although there are no special decor elements in it. For the guests this, perhaps, is not so important, it matters to them that here each room has its own spacious balcony with views of the sea or the mountains surrounding the village.

The reception at Alanya Klas Hotel has friendly staff who understand Russian and are able to speak Russian, which helps a lot in solving current issues. Here you can not only get room keys, but also exchange money, buy a tour, call a doctor, taxi, rent things, rent a vehicle (car, motorcycle, bicycle). In the lobby, where the reception is located, there are comfortable sofas, armchairs, tables, a bar, and free wireless internet. By the way, you can use it only here, since nowhere else, including numbers, the Internet does not work, although theoretically it is.



Accommodation

There are exactly 100 rooms in Alanya Klas Hotel, the description of which we will give as much as possible. According to tourists, their design is simple, fully consistent with the concept of minimalism. The rooms have the most necessary set of furniture, a mirror, a flat-screen TV with a diagonal of 55 cm (there are three Russian channels), a refrigerator, air conditioning. The hygiene room has a shower with a curtain, a toilet and a washbasin. Hygiene products are provided in bottles with a dispenser. The soundproofing in the rooms is average, there are no insects (ants and others), cleaning is done every other day. The maids, by their kindness or sincere tip, twist the figures on the beds from towels. Their responsibilities also include mopping, taking out garbage, replenishing ending soaps, shampoo, toilet paper, changing bed linen (2 times a week) and towels (every other day). There are only two categories of rooms in Alanya Klas Hotel:

1. Standard area from 14 to 28 m 2 .

2. Family 40 m2.

The difference is only in their size and in the number of beds installed. The standard can accommodate 2 people (adults) and 1 child (in a crib). The family can accommodate 4 people, plus 1 child in a crib.
