Hotel rating of Tunisia 3 *, 4 *, 5 *

Tunisia is located on the African coast of the Mediterranean Sea. Due to the subtropical marine climate, it is almost always warm and sunny. The landscape of these places is beautiful: blooming jasmine, mountainsides covered with cedar forests, beautiful sandy beaches, emerald sea. Holidays on the Tunisian coast became popular during the years of French colonization. It was at this time that the branches of the French centers of thalassotherapy, a wellness technique using sea water, seaweed and the sun, settled on the coast. Many Tunisian resorts are designed for a quiet measured rest, therefore people of retirement age, mainly from Western Europe, prefer to come here. However, there are also resorts for young people in which there are entertainment centers, discos, bars, water parks, etc.

Tunisia hotels comply with European standards. Below we present to your attention the rating of the best hotels of Tunisia 3 *, 4 *, 5 *. Regardless of the number of stars, they provide tourists with a full range of services of the highest quality. The staff of each hotel with their friendly and helpful attitude each time proves the veracity of the legends of the famous oriental hospitality.
