Nizhny Novgorod: attractions and the charming spirit of antiquity

One of the brightest cities in Russia is Nizhny Novgorod. It is located at the confluence of two rivers: the Oka and the Volga. Due to the fact that the city was formed in the thirteenth century, it retained the spirit of antiquity, architectural monuments and historical museums. Arriving in Nizhny Novgorod, sights should be examined starting with a sightseeing tour. Without visiting it, every tourist will lose a lot. After all, such landscapes as there are almost nowhere else to be found. In fact, wherever a person comes, he will no longer find a similar place. Speaking of Nizhny Novgorod, one cannot fail to mention the bewitching landscapes, the spirit of history and the great Russian people. There are many interesting legends about this land, and traveling on it is a pleasure.





So, consider the wealth that Nizhny Novgorod has - sights. After a sightseeing tour, you can safely go to St. Michael the Archangel Cathedral. This is one of the most ancient temples in Russia, which was built in 1221. Of course, over the years it was rebuilt and restored several times (the original structure was made of wood), and in 1628 it acquired the finished look that can be seen today. Nizhny Novgorod, whose sights can be studied for months, is itself a small city, but with a huge and great history. In order not to miscalculate with the planned excursions, it is best to choose the most visited. One of them is a trip to Minin and Pozharsky Square. This, in fact, is the social and cultural center of the city. It is here that the main events, holidays and celebrations are celebrated. Nizhny Novgorod, whose attractions are famous throughout Russia, has one of the best universities in the country. Tourists are even taken on excursions to some universities. In addition, the city had the famous church of St. Alexius the Metropolitan, built in 1641. Unfortunately, in 1930 it was demolished, but the street that has survived to our time was named in her honor. On the territory of the city, the Annunciation Cathedral was once located, which very often was attacked and burned. It consisted of eight churches. The government also decided to demolish the building in 1930.

The city of Nizhny Novgorod, whose attractions are filled with historical spirit, always attracts crowds of tourists.
