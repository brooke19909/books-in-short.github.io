Muine Paradise Resort 3 *, Vietnam: hotel description, traveler reviews

The resort complex Muine Paradise Resort 3 * provides many opportunities for an unforgettable exotic vacation in Vietnam.





Brief information about the hotel

The Muine Paradise Resort 3 * hotel has a huge advantage over other similar institutions, as it has its own beach, as well as a clean outdoor pool in case the weather turns bad and the sea is too restless. In addition, in the modern world, it is important that tourists have unhindered access to wireless Internet.

Particular attention in the hotel Muine Paradise Resort 3 * is paid to ensure that guests feel in the rooms cozy and comfortable. For this purpose, the rooms are equipped with comfortable furniture, as well as household appliances. In addition, it is worth noting the laconic interior design, which traces the traditional Asian notes.

The hotel Muine Paradise Resort 3 * provides many infrastructure facilities that make travelers relax comfortable and unforgettable. Of particular note is the green garden, as well as a large terrace where you can sunbathe. You can go on a fascinating sightseeing trip or you can travel around the surroundings on a rented bike.





Resort Location

Muine Paradise Resort 3 * hotel (Vietnam) is located on the seafront, in the popular resort city of Mui Ne, among travelers. One of the main attractions of this village are the most beautiful sand dunes, which are located at a distance of about 5 km from the hotel. Also, after driving 20 km, you can visit the city of Phan Thiet.

Resort Hotel Rooms

Resort Muine Paradise Beach Resort 3 * has 35 rooms for guests. Tourists can choose for themselves apartments from several categories that differ in size, equipment and price:

  • Superior Double Apartment is a small room where guests can stay in a king bed or two single beds. This room offers scenic garden views.
  • Deluxe rooms, like the previous ones, are equipped with a large or a pair of small beds. In addition to the luxurious interior, it is also worth noting the picturesque view from the windows. So, some of them overlook the pool, and those rooms that are more expensive will offer you a beautiful view of the ocean.
  • One of the best accommodation options are small comfortable villas. They differ not only in luxurious decoration, but also in location. These small chalets are located in close proximity to the beach, so you will quickly get to the sea, as well as always have the opportunity to enjoy the ocean scenery.




Guest apartment features

In order to ensure the comfort of guests, the rooms include the following amenities:

  • given the hot climate of Vietnam, one of the most important attributes of the room is an individual air conditioning;
  • thanks to the TV with a large package of cable channels, you will not have to be bored if you want to stay in your room in bad weather;
  • in each room there is a minibar that can be filled with your favorite drinks for an additional fee (you can use it like a regular refrigerator);
  • depending on the class of your apartment, the bathroom will be equipped with a shower or a large bath;
  • There is a set of bath accessories and cosmetics, as well as a hairdryer.




Positive reviews

Vietnam in November is one of the most popular tourist destinations due to the warm and mild climate. Accordingly, the flow of travelers at this time sharply increases, and many prefer to stay in this hotel. Reviews about him are mostly positive, but this is expressed in the following comments:

  • check-in and check-in of guests is carried out quickly enough, without unnecessary formalities and delays;
  • the pleasant moment is that each guest in the apartment will be greeted by a welcome note addressed to him personally from the hotel administration;
  • despite the fact that the decoration of the hotel rooms is concise and modest, it has everything you need to create a comfortable environment, as well as very high-quality cleaning, which is carried out daily;
  • maids every day replenish in their rooms supplies of drinking water, as well as report bags of tea and coffee;
  • replacement of the bed is done as often as you want (just leave a special plate on the bed with the requirement to provide this service);
  • Breakfasts are quite substantial, and they are held on the outdoor terrace, which allows guests to enjoy the panoramas of the surroundings, as well as pleasant live music;
  • despite the fact that the territory of the resort complex is very small, it is very beautiful and quite densely planted with greenery and all kinds of exotic plants;
  • the pool is in good condition - it is cleaned regularly, and the water practically does not smell like bleach or other chemicals;
  • one of the main advantages can be considered that the territory of the hotel is closed, and therefore you can not worry that someone else will come into it;
  • the restaurant is very tasty and the prices are quite affordable.




Negative reviews

In addition to the positive aspects, there are also negative aspects of relaxation in the Muine Paradise Resort 3 *. Reviews about this resort hotel contain a number of negative comments, comments, and complaints:

  • the hotel is located very far from the international airport (approximately 6 hours drive), which is very tiring after a long flight;
  • the curtains in the apartments, apparently, did not wash for a very long time, or they are too old, because there are terrible spots on them;
  • to the beach you need to go down the steep descent along the stairs, which creates quite serious difficulties when returning to the hotel;
  • since the sea is quite turbulent, the waves constantly bring to the shore a lot of algae and jellyfish, which sting painfully;
  • despite the fact that the beach is private, local fishermen very often come to it with boats and all their equipment, which is why there is practically nowhere to swim and sunbathe;
  • if the key is lost, a very high fee is charged - as much as $ 35.
