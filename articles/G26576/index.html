Zheleznovodsk, sanatorium "Rus": description, services and reviews

This resort region has long been known for its wellness resources. In Soviet times, there were health resorts of national importance, but even today they are no less in demand. Today we would like to tell you about the resort city of Zheleznovodsk. It is located in the Stavropol Territory, in the foothills of the Greater Caucasus. The unique mountain-forest climate, similar to the Alpine one, makes this small town one of the best in its area.



Rus sanatorium"

This is a great health resort, which is located in the center of Zheleznovodsk. Sanatorium "Rus" is not in vain so famous throughout the post-Soviet space. The unique natural conditions create ideal conditions for healing and restoration of strength. Zheleznovodsk is the only KMV resort that boasts a huge and very clean forest. Clean air, drunk with the scents of the forest, provides a speedy recovery.

Short description

Already looking at the photo of this health resort, I want to be there at least once in my life. The city of Zheleznovodsk itself is very beautiful. Sanatorium "Rus" is a snow-white architectural ensemble among the boundless emerald green field. Mount Zheleznaya rises very close by, and literally a stone's throw from the buildings there is a well-known drinking well of the "Slavic" spring.

It does not matter if you have a chronic illness, or if you just want to relax in silence - everyone will be happy to welcome Zheleznovodsk. Sanatorium "Rus" accommodates up to 210 people. On its territory there are sports fields and a swimming pool. It treats diseases of almost all organs and systems. Children are accepted from 14 years old. Three meals a day, ordered.



The main wealth of the sanatorium

These are natural healing factors that Zheleznovodsk generously endowed. Sanatorium "Rus" is located in a narrow green valley between the picturesque mountains. But the uniqueness of the landscape and landscape is half the battle, the most important thing is the mineral springs of the resort. They are approximately the same in composition, they are waters of low salinity. There are cold and hot springs here, which are good in their own way. The resort is multidisciplinary, so you can come here as a family.



Medical profile

People from all over the country come here, just to relax and thoroughly heal. Sanatorium "Rus" (Zheleznovodsk) is a health resort of the most widely specialized profile. We will very briefly list the main areas of treatment:

  • Gastrointestinal diseases, in particular gastritis.
  • Stomach ulcer.
  • Colitis and enterocolitis.
  • Chronic hepatitis
  • Cholecystitis and cholangitis.
  • Chronic pyelonephritis and other kidney diseases.

The best diagnostic and treatment base

To effectively cure a person, you must first determine what is wrong with his body. Moreover, the effectiveness of treatment depends entirely on the quality of the diagnosis. There are all conditions, equipment and laboratories for this.

The sanatorium "Rus" (Stavropol Territory, Zheleznovodsk) is a small but very cozy health resort where a close-knit team of doctors works. 15 doctors work here, each of them is a specialist in his field. But the most important thing is the attitude of the staff to their work. Each incoming patient is greeted with great attention and warmth, which is so lacking in ordinary clinics.

Despite the abundance of procedures, the main thing here is mineral water. Moreover, the treatment is comprehensive, which is why the Rus resort (Zheleznovodsk) is so valued. Reviews emphasize that recreation plays a very important role. You do not have to stand in lines, your doctor is always ready to give you time. Rest in a relaxed atmosphere, in the fresh air in the most positive way affects the course of treatment.

Dietary nutrition and a special daily routine complete the picture, and climatotherapy also plays an important role. All the factors of the balneo-mud resort are used here. This is a drinking treatment and mineral baths, mud therapy and inhalation. These wonderful treatments are complemented by physiotherapy and hydropathy, as well as various massages.



Resort Accommodation

Designed health resort for 210 places. Here you can relax and undergo treatment all year round. The sanatorium complex includes sleeping and medical buildings, as well as well-developed tourist infrastructure. Each room is equipped with a TV and fridge. There are single and double rooms in which cosiness and comfort always reign. The maintenance of cleanliness is constantly taken care of by the maids. For lovers of a comfortable stay, there are deluxe rooms. Here you can find luxurious, superior rooms that will appeal to even the most demanding guests. Particular attention is paid to the nutrition of tourists, the menu is replete with various options.

Entertainment

People come here very often in order to have a good time and relax, such prevention is very important against a number of diseases. So that people do not get bored, there is an excellent sports and recreation center here. Huge pool, tennis court, fitness and gymnasiums, choose entertainment to your liking. In order to spend the evening in a pleasant atmosphere, there is a concert hall and a library, as well as a fun disco every evening. Large billiard room, beauty salon and tour desk.

We calculate the budget

Of course, everyone, without exception, worries about the question of whether you can afford a trip to Zheleznovodsk. Sanatorium Rus offers reasonable prices for the middle class. Judge for yourself: the cost of living in a single room is 3400 rubles. The price is per day and includes accommodation and treatment.

Double standard room is a great option for a small family. Cost - 2600 per person per day. Double superior room is a little more expensive, 2900 rubles. Double two-room suite costs 6800 rubles per day, and three-room suite - 7800.

Judging by the reviews, this resort is one of the best places for a wonderful holiday, diagnosis and preventive treatment. Tourists note the excellent service, courtesy and courtesy of medical staff. Without exception, everyone praises the atmosphere in the rooms, the cosiness and comfort reigning in them.
