Capsule coffee machines - choose the right one

It's no secret that there are a lot of coffee lovers, and most people prefer to enjoy this drink at home or at work, because it is so much more convenient and, of course, cheaper than in any cafe. Available capsule coffee machines allow you to make high-quality coffee at home. Therefore, this equipment is in high demand among lovers of this drink.



Capsule coffee machines are very easy to maintain, with their help you can quickly prepare aromatic and tasty coffee, while the machine itself will heat the water to the required temperature and select the optimal ratio of the amount of water to coffee. With this device you can prepare a variety of coffee drinks, such as espresso, which will have a thick foam, or ristretto, and, you want, just hot chocolate, it all depends on your preferences.



If you decide to purchase equipment of this type, you need to decide on the following questions:

- what productivity of the car is necessary for you;

- what kind of drinks will you prepare with its help;

- in what conditions this equipment will work.



There are universal machines that can prepare not only coffee, but also many other drinks, such as tea or hot chocolate, they are usually used in large companies or companies where a lot of staff work. If you are the only coffee lover in your family, then a coffee machine that makes 1-2 cups of coffee is enough for you. It can be, for example, Cremesso Uno or a coffee maker SK-207A.



Capsule coffee machine - instructions for her choice

There are many types of this equipment at various prices. The main parameters that you need to pay attention to are:

- the power of this equipment, on which the speed of preparation of the drink depends. The most optimal is a power of about 1200W. This can be, for example, Gaggia Nautilus White or Gaggia K-111D SILVER. If you do not like to wait long, buy more powerful cars, for example Saeco - Talea Ring Plus Black RI 9826/11;

- the level of noise generated during operation of the equipment. The lower it is, the more pleasant and comfortable will be the use of a coffee machine in an apartment or office;

- to obtain a high-quality drink, high-pressure water passes through existing coffee. This pressure must not be less than 15 bar. Machines that create less pressure are not able to prepare a tasty and aromatic drink;

- the size of the tank, which should be at least one liter, also it should be easy to remove and install, as the coffee machine will be cleaned daily. It is necessary to clean not only the tank, but also other parts of the machine, for which it is necessary to use special tablets or powder for reading the coffee machine. The use of ordinary dishwashing detergents is prohibited;

- the presence of additional functions, for example, automatic shutdown of the machine, as well as a function that controls the volume of coffee prepared, will greatly facilitate and secure the work with this equipment;

- Pay attention to the water heating system. It is better to have a descaling function, as well as special filters and cleaning programs that are especially needed when using hard water. With these functions, the capsule coffee machines last much longer.



Thus, the choice of this equipment must be approached very responsibly. Not every person has enough free time to choose capsule coffee machines in the store, so online stores are a good alternative to them. They work around the clock and, being at home, in a relaxed atmosphere, you can slowly study the characteristics of the offered goods and make your choice. A big plus is that capsule coffee machines in online stores are much cheaper.
