Coniferous wreath

To make such a Christmas decoration for your home, you will need: wire, branches of living coniferous trees (or artificial), satin or nylon ribbons, Christmas tree decorations, threads. To make a Christmas wreath from branches, you need to twist from the wire the base of an oval or round shape. Next, attach branches to it. When tying them, try to move from bottom to top. The next step in the manufacture of the product is decoration. Wrap a wreath with satin ribbon, fasten the ends between the branches. Tie up Christmas toys with threads. From the kapron ribbon, make small bows and glue them with a hot gun or with any glue for textiles. From another tape, make a loop to hang the jewelry. Do-it-yourself traditional Christmas coniferous wreath made by yourself. Now he is ready to decorate your home.



Do-it-yourself Christmas wreath from improvised material

Such a Christmas decoration will cost you a penny, and the result will exceed all your expectations. To make a wreath, you will need a piece of foam sheet, twine, braid, paper napkins, beads. Cut the ring from the foam and wrap it tightly with twine. Glue flowers or snowflakes from paper napkins on the entire surface of the wreath. Everything is clear with snowflakes, and the flowers are made as follows: each napkin is folded four times, in the middle they are fastened with a stapler. The corners of the workpiece are cut to form a circle. It turns out an element consisting of 8 layers of paper with a bracket from the stapler in the center. Now, with fingers, we bend each layer in the middle, forming petals. As a result, we get a magnificent flower. We decorate the voids between paper flowers with beads. Such a wreath can be hung inside the house or on the street in dry weather.

Christmas ring of twigs

A wreath of twigs of deciduous trees looks very impressive and interesting. In this case, all the beauty depends on the selected decor. Long thin rods are twisted into a ring and secured with kapron thread. A satin ribbon is wrapped around the ring and fixed on the wrong side of the product. Glue gun or glue "Dragon", "Titan" on a wreath fasten decorative elements. Cones and walnuts, previously painted from a spray can in golden or silver color, natural bunches of mountain ash and rose hips, dried flowers look especially good on such a wreath. You can use Christmas garlands and toys as decorations.
