The first lake of Chelyabinsk: fishing, bathhouse, barbecue

The Southern Urals is richly endowed with natural resources and has rightly earned the fame of the "Lake District". Many different reservoirs are located throughout the region far and close to Chelyabinsk, but some formed almost on its outskirts and even inside the city itself.

Not all lakes are of natural origin, there are also artificial water spaces formed from industrial human intervention. Each has its own story and unique name.

First Lake, Second and others

On the whole planet there are karst lakes, they are formed in landscape soil dips, where rain, melt water collects, these voids can also be filled with underground spring water, fresh and even salty. Just once, industrialists decided to use four small karst lakes located on the eastern outskirts of the city as waste tanks. Here is a story of the origin in Chelyabinsk of the First Lake, then the Second Lake, the Third Lake, the Fourth Lake and Shelyugino.

Unromantic names in former water sumps for technical effluents of local plants ChTZ, CHPP-2, etc. Although they are all connected by drain channels, over time the water in two such reservoirs became more safe for use, but only a year after the cessation of discharge into them industrial waters. And in 1957, a young fish factory began to stock up the First Lake of Chelyabinsk. Recreation center

The modern First Lake of Chelyabinsk is one of the favorite vacation spots of citizens. Since there is a reservoir within the city, you can get there by public transport. Buses, trolleybus routes or fixed-route taxis go here. But you still have to walk a little on foot to get to the recreation center by the Lake Baths on the First in Chelyabinsk. The road will go along the "Teacher" gardening association, so you can not be afraid of excesses.

Here are cozy cottages with amenities and real Russian baths. Hotel in 2 floors with rooms of various capacities and comforts. Banquet rooms, summer terraces, secure parking, sports grounds for adults and children.

It would be a great idea to hold a wedding ceremony on the First Lake of Chelyabinsk. There is definitely enough space for any number of guests. But you need to reserve places here in advance. There are many holidays in the country, but it is necessary to celebrate them somewhere.

City Beach

Here is a well-kept sandy beach, which you can get to just by paying 100 rubles for an admission ticket. Moreover, the beach on the First Lake of Chelyabinsk is also visited year-round.

Especially for kebab lovers - equipped barbecue areas. It lacks only the barbecue itself, a delicious feast and a cheerful company of friends.

For lovers of winter forms of entertainment - horseback riding, tubing center. An ideal place to relax your body and soul at any time of the year.
