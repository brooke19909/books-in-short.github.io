Rest in Egypt. Hurghada. Albatross Beach Hotel

The luxurious Beach Albatros Hotel, owned by the Pickalbatros Group, is located on the seafront, just twenty minutes from the center of the popular tourist resort of Hurghada. Albatross Beach, built in 1992 and renovated in 2005, is decorated in a traditional European style.



It consists of a main four-story building, a three-story building and a complex of two-story bungalows. The hotel is surrounded by magnificent fragrant gardens, wonderful pools and clear sea water. For couples with children who have chosen the resort city of Hurghada for their vacation, Albatross Beach can be an ideal option.
