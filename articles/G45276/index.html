Crocheted baby scarves: patterns. How to crochet a baby scarf

Nothing gives a wide scope for creativity, as do-it-yourself children's scarves (crocheted). They can be tied under each new jacket or hat.





Set of hats and scarves

The pattern for them, you can choose the simplest. It will be described a bit below. The highlight of this set is a combination of three similar in intensity shades.

Crochet children's scarves and hats is best knitted from yarn, which includes wool and acrylic. And for the winter you will need another inner hat. For her, threads of their cotton and acrylic will come in handy.

A drawing requires a chain of so many loops to divide into four. In the first row make a climb of three loops. In the second, tie three columns with a crochet, in the fourth - only one such column. Continue this knitting to the end of the row or circle.

The second row needs to be lifted out of two loops. At the top of one column, connect three, and where there were three, there should be only one. All subsequent rows repeat this pattern.

To get an unusual effect, you need to knit each row with a new color, that is, alternate all three selected shades.

It remains only to connect the inner hat from a simple set of columns with a crochet. Then sew them together and tie a lace strip in a circle.

After you crocheted a children's scarf, it is also better to arrange it with an openwork strip. Moreover, it will look more spectacular if this strip is made around the perimeter.





Inclined Square Scarf

Despite the apparent complexity, these are still simple children's scarves (crocheted). Schemes (the product is intended for a boy or girl - it is not so important) consist of air loops, connecting loops and posts with two double crochets.

The pattern repeats every four rows. Its scheme is located in the figure below.





Spring scarf

The direction of knitting goes from the center in a circle, so the chain of loops should be long. Since ready-made children's scarves (crocheted) according to this scheme will be slightly longer than the set chain, this should be taken into account. The pattern is repeated every 6 loops, so their total number should be divided by six.

Yarn on such children's scarves crocheted (with patterns) should choose a thin one, for example, having a length of 370 meters and weighing 100 grams. The hook for her will have to be taken at number 1.75.





The first row consists of an alternation of five air loops and a connecting column connected to every sixth of the initial chain.

In the second row, after three lifting loops in the first arch, connect 8 double crochets, in the second arch - one connecting column, 5 aerial, another connecting to the third arch, continue knitting the same pattern, only the double crochets in each first arch should be 9. Knit like this in a circle. In the corners, you need to knit five air loops for rounding.

The third row (rise - 3 loops) consists of double crochet columns alternating with one air loop; in the arch from the chain of air loops there will be a connecting column.

In the fourth row at each vertex of the double crochet, make a connecting column, between them connect 5 air loops.

Fifth row: connect the first two arches the same, in the third arch, connect 9 columns with a crochet. Continue the arch and fan pattern to the end of the circle.

The last is the sixth row: over each fan, connect 9 columns alternating with one air loop, in the arch of the air loops make one connecting.

You can add another row, consisting of single crochet columns that alternate with one air loop.





Bactus Scarf

The described technique allows you to get an unusual serrated edge. It resembles the open mouth of a dragon. To make the product large enough and light, you need a thin thread. Well, if it has such characteristics: weight - 60 grams, length - 260 meters. It is better to choose a hook at number 5 or 6.

The beginning of each row is a climb of five air loops. They are equal to one column with three crochets.

The basis of knitting is a chain of 5 loops. In the first row, execute a column with three crochets in the very first loop of the set chain. A row is completed. It turns out that it consists of two large columns.

Second: after lifting, tie 2 columns with three crochets to the top of the second column of the previous row.

In the third and each subsequent odd row to the center of the scarf, knit the first column with three crochets at the base of the lift, and then on such a column in each loop.

In all subsequent even rows, a double column with three crochets must be knitted from the last loop.

From the middle of the scarf, make a decrease. Instead of a double column from one base, knit two with a common top.

Scarf scarf

Children's scarves (crocheted) can be crocheted in different techniques. One of them is Bosnian knitting.

It is based on knitting with simple connecting posts. Most importantly, always carry them out for the same loop wall. For example, only the front or only the back. The pattern of the front side is identical to the wrong one.

But the pattern in these techniques varies greatly. It is recommended to tie test squares in order to understand which one more wants to tie a children's scarf-scarf.





Scarf with a simple pattern for a boy or girl

You need to start their knitting with a chain of so many loops so that its length is equal to the width of the future scarf. But at the same time, the total number of air loops should be even. Because the pattern has a repeating part based on two loops.

Then repeat the rows that repeat the same element of the pattern - an arch of two double crochets and an air loop between them. This scheme can be viewed in more detail in the figure.





Scarf snood

These are very simple crocheted children's scarves. There are usually no problems with circuits. Because they are formed by connecting, half-columns, columns with one or two crochets. Each needlewoman will easily reproduce these elements.

If an air loop is indicated in the diagram, then when knitting, you need to skip one loop in the bottom row to get a gap.

The beginning of knitting in a set of chains of loops, the number of which is divided by 16. On them, perform a series of connecting posts.

The first row of the pattern: aerial, three connecting, aerial, half-column, aerial, double crochet, aerial, double crochet, aerial, then the columns go in reverse order to one connecting. Between fragments with increasing column height, three connectives should form. Continue these slides to the end of the row.

The second and third rows of the pattern consist of connecting posts. In the fifth row, knit the same slide, but so that its top falls on the hollow, and on the top of the mountain of the previous rows - three connecting ones.

Sixth and seventh - repeat the second and third. And so on to the desired width of the snud.





Simple fringed scarf

Boys and girls can knit a scarf in which all the rows are the same and consist of repeating the same columns. For example, half columns or single crochet columns.

The main decoration of such children's scarves is yarn, which can be colored in sections. At the ends they can be tied with a long fringe. To do this, it is enough to carry out the necessary length of the chain and fix them along the edge of the scarf. Very high arches will be obtained, each of which will play the role of a single fringe rope.
