Hotel Description

The hotel-resort complex "Ambassador Pattaya In Wing" is a division of the hotel family "Ambassador City" and represents its budget wing. This is one of the largest institutions of its kind in all of Asia. The hotel also boasts that it has the largest beach among the hotels in Pattaya.

Due to the fact that the Ambassador Pattaya In Wing is combined with several other hotels, guests have access to a huge territory for a lot of tourist infrastructure. There are several pools, many restaurants, and even its own market, the prices of which are much lower than in the surrounding retail outlets.





Price policy

The hotel has a small building. The apartments in it are quite compact and modest, which allows tourists to take advantage of a fairly budget vacation in the resort city of Pattaya. Ambassador In Wing offers accommodation prices quite affordable. A week stay in a double room, taking into account transfer and flight, as well as medical insurance, will cost you about $ 1,300.

Hotel Location

Ambassador In Wing Resort is located in Thailand, about 15 km from Pattaya city center. This is the Na Jomtien area, which is located right on the ocean. You can get here from Bangkok Airport (180 km) or U-Tapao (30 km), which is much closer.





Guest Apartments

The Ambassador City Jomtien In Wing building has standard rooms, which, although characterized by compact territory and a minimum set of amenities, nevertheless provide guests with a decent level of comfort. The only drawback is the lack of access to the balcony.





Room Amenities

So, in all apartments of the Ambassador In Wing 3 * hotel, tourists will find the following set of amenities:

  • a small TV with a wide range of cable and satellite channels;
  • air conditioning, which allows guests to individually adjust the air temperature;
  • shower cabin, as well as a full range of bath and hygiene items;
  • international telephone, which is also associated with the reception.




Infrastructure facilities

For the comfort of the guests of the Ambassador City Jomtien In Wing Hotel, a rich resort infrastructure has been developed. This is very important when you consider that the hotel is located far from the city center. So:

  • the business center has an Internet club, as well as a large list of office equipment for working with your documents.




Opportunities for children

Not only adults, but also young tourists visit the resort city of Pattaya. Ambassador Inn Wing has provided a number of opportunities for children to relax:

  • a children's pool, which is characterized by a shallow depth, as well as the presence of all kinds of children's attractions;
  • a playground, which is located in the shade of a cozy green garden;
  • in the kids club, kids can take part in games, entertainment, and watch cartoons;
  • if necessary, you can always use the services of a nanny who will look after your child.

Restaurants on site

Of great interest is the cuisine of a country such as Thailand. Ambassador In Wing offers gourmets to visit the following places:

  • "Atrium" - here guests are offered a huge variety of dishes that relate to Thai, Russian, Italian, French and other cuisines. There is also a small bar where the most original and most delicious cocktails are prepared.
  • Hong Tae is a Chinese restaurant that is considered one of the best not only on the coast, but throughout Pattaya. Here you can try both traditional dishes and the chef's inventions.
  • "Pasta-Pasta" is a famous Italian restaurant, which has won many international awards. Here they are not limited to template dishes, but pay attention to the culinary features of every corner of this country.
  • Espresso Terrace is a small restaurant right on the seashore, where you can enjoy a cup of coffee and taste delicious cakes.
  • Tokugawa is a popular Japanese restaurant where you can taste delicious sushi fairly inexpensively.

Service Ratings

According to guest reviews, the Ambassador Inn Wing 2 * hotel was given the following ratings for services and facilities:

  • The infrastructure of this resort complex was rated at 4.5 points. The territory has everything you need to ensure a complete and high-quality stay for hotel guests.
  • For the quality of services was rated 3.5. In general, the work of the staff is satisfactory, but the lack of animation and low quality of the Internet significantly lowered the score.
  • The hotel provides very few opportunities for children's relaxation, so the score for this item is 2.6.

Positive hotel reviews

The Ambassador In Wing Resort attracts thousands of tourists from all over the world. Among the reviews of guests of this institution, the following comments can be distinguished, which create a fairly positive picture:

  • huge beautiful territory (due to the fact that this institution is combined with several more hotels of different categories);
  • a good location of the hotel away from noisy objects and highways;
  • nice private beach (large and well-kept);
  • good breakfast menu (varied and satisfying enough);
  • there are as many as 4 swimming pools on the territory, so there is no feeling of crampedness while swimming;
  • despite the fact that the hotel is quite far removed from the city center, food prices in restaurants here are quite affordable;
  • right on the territory of the hotel there is a large market with a huge selection of fruits and other food products, as well as souvenirs;
  • apartments are cleaned daily and at a fairly high level;
  • snow-white bedding and new towels, the replacement of which, by the way, is carried out daily;
  • relatively low cost of rest;
  • There are many retail outlets near the hotel (shops, shops, stalls, markets, etc.);
  • location of the hotel on the first coastal line relative to the sea;
  • there are special terminals on the territory for replenishing a mobile phone account, which is very important due to the remoteness of the hotel from the city infrastructure;
  • Near the hotel there is a large parking of tuk-tuk, which allows you to use transport services at any time;
  • the hotel has as many as 8 restaurants, so everyone can choose the dishes to their taste;
  • some rooms have a view of the sea coast;
  • smoking is prohibited throughout the territory, and for violation of this rule fines are provided that are calculated from the deposit left upon check-in (although for some this may seem like a disadvantage);
  • there is a lot of greenery in the territory;
  • there is a large observation deck on the roof of the hotel.

Negative reviews and complaints about the hotel

The guests of the Ambassador In Wing Hotel, in addition to positive impressions, also have a number of comments on this institution, which is manifested in a number of negative reviews:

  • poor insulation from noise in the enclosure, which is why all the sounds of the street are heard too well in the rooms (especially at night and early morning);
  • hair dryer not provided;
  • in some rooms, as well as in the corridors of the building there are cockroaches;
  • the appearance of the hotel, as well as the view from the windows of some rooms, are ruined by sloppy outbuildings;
  • the staff is not very polite to the guests;
  • in most rooms there is no balcony, because of which there is nowhere to dry towels, bathing suits and other things;
  • getting to the city is very long and uncomfortable;
  • the rooms are clearly in need of repair, and some pieces of furniture would do well to replace with new ones;
  • the apartments are very crowded;
  • there are very few sun loungers near the pools for so many guests at the hotel, so constantly someone does not have enough space and unpleasant situations may arise;
  • the bathrooms do not have hooks for towels;
  • the rooms have almost no decor, and traces of time (lots of stains and dirt) are clearly visible on the white walls;
  • cases of theft from rooms are known;
  • there are no umbrellas or any other sunshades on the beach to protect from the sun;
  • very noisy air conditioning in the rooms;
  • upon check-in, a deposit of $ 100 must be left at the reception;
  • hotel staff are very indifferent both to the hotel itself and to requests or comments of guests;
  • almost no children's infrastructure;
  • on the beach there is no shower, no toilet, no changing cabins;
  • the corridors and rooms of the building are rather damp, which is why there is a lot of mold on the walls.

General impressions

One of the most popular holiday destinations is Thailand, Pattaya. Hotel "Ambassador In Wing" offers a fairly budget accommodation in a chic area that accommodates several hotels of different categories. If you do not take into account the modest apartments, as well as their small size, then guests can feel like guests of an upscale resort. Here you can use the modern gym, several swimming pools, as well as visit chic and inexpensive restaurants and many other institutions that provide leisure activities and specialize in wellness treatments. The problem of remoteness from the city can be solved using the services of a tuk-tuk.
