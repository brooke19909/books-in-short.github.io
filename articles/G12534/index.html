Rest in Krasnoyarsk. Recreation facilities for every taste and budget

Many people try to take a summer vacation. Where better to hold it? It is recommended to pay attention to rest in Krasnoyarsk. Recreation facilities must be chosen correctly in order to provide yourself with a wonderful pastime. In some establishments, it is necessary to make reservations a few weeks before entry, otherwise there will be no places left. This is especially true in the early to mid-summer. On the territory of Krasnoyarsk and the region you can find very nice options that are characterized by increased comfort and low prices.

Village enisey

Everyone will be impressed by a vacation in Krasnoyarsk. Recreation facilities here are quite comfortable and delight with a huge range of services.

Village Enisey is an inexpensive complex, where the minimum cost of rooms starts from 707 rubles. The base works all year round. For meals you need to pay separately, as it is not included in the price. If the guest arrived on a ticket from the sanatorium, then he is fed for free. Children under 10 years old will not have to give money if they do not occupy an extra bed. The rooms that are offered to guests are various: from economy class to vip.



Berendey

The recreation center "Berendey" in Krasnoyarsk is open all year round. The minimum cost per day is 550 rubles. The peculiarity of this institution is that in the winter season there is no food. It must be organized independently. In another period, the dining room works here.

There are several houses on the territory of the base where guests are accommodated. They have all the amenities for a good existence.



Green park hotel

Different for each impression may be a vacation in Krasnoyarsk. Recreation facilities, which will cost more, offer comfortable conditions and a greater number of services.

Rooms at Green Park Hotel cost from 4 thousand rubles. There is no independent food, you have to go to a local restaurant. There is a recreation center all year round. It is possible to conduct horseback riding. It offers billiards, a bath. You can just sit on a picnic. The rooms have a telephone, TV and internet.

Green Park Hotel is located 15 minutes from the center of Krasnoyarsk. The price includes accommodation. There is a river near the base where you can go fishing.



Mana

The recreation center "Mana" in Krasnoyarsk provides many different services. On its territory there is a cafe and restaurant. Minimum room rate - 2500 rubles.

In summer, you can rent bicycles, ATVs, electric motorcycles. In winter, ski slopes, snowmobiles, and skates are in demand. On the territory there is also a Russian bath.

Guests are accommodated in two- or three-story cottages. They have household appliances, shower, television. It is proposed to listen to music on professional equipment, as well as sing in karaoke. On the territory of "Mana" there is a manor, which is designed for 10 people.



"Beretskaya glade"

Many people will have a great vacation in Krasnoyarsk. Recreation facilities will only enhance a wonderful experience. The minimum daily cost for a room in Beretskaya Polyana is 900 rubles.

Meals are carried out independently. Kitchens are equipped for this, there is all the necessary equipment. Arbors where you can cook kebabs are installed. Crockery is also available for hire.

The rooms are of various levels of comfort, respectively, each will choose the one that suits him best. If someone wants to come with a tent, he should know that the administration has set up a toilet with hot and cold water for camping enthusiasts.

Here you can go to the shooting range, play soccer or volleyball, ride a bike, boat, roller skates.

"Husky Husky"

This recreation center in the Krasnoyarsk Territory is considered one of the most expensive. The minimum cost per room is 14,500 rubles. She works all year round. If we consider the sphere of nutrition, then it is independent. For cooking, equipped kitchens and barbecue facilities.

There are two cottages on the territory of the recreation center. Both are designed for 8 seats. They have fireplaces, baths, halls, dining rooms, kitchens, and bathrooms. On the second floor there is a balcony, bedrooms and soft sofas.
