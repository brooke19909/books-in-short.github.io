Waterpark in Glazov - extravaganza of splashes and emotions!

The water park in Glazov is the only high-level water attraction in the city that offers a wide range of services for everyone. All conditions for adults and children of different ages are created here. High-quality service, a sufficient number of entertainment, wellness, relaxation areas. The room fully complies with all safety requirements and SES standards. The complex operates seven days a week and receives visitors daily.

Attractions, services

The price of one ticket includes visits:

  • Large pool equipped with hydromassage. The depth of the structure reaches 1.7 m.
  • Water slides "Black Hole", "Hydrotube".
  • Russian steam room, Turkish hammam, Finnish sauna. To create a contrast, you can use a special font filled with cold water.
  • Volleyball courts.
  • Children's pool and children's steam room. It provides a comfortable, safe temperature within 50 ¬∞ C.
  • Solarium.
  • Heated outdoor pool.

You can also rent a sauna and a house, which automatically allows customers to use the services of a water park in Glazov.

In the territory of the aquacenter there is a cozy cafe and bar. Here you can have a hearty meal with hot dogs, snacks, pizza, as well as order hot dishes and a full meal. In the proposed menu, you can choose a wide selection of drinks. A play area with a trampoline, cars and other devices for active, fun, interesting pastime is organized for kids (not included in the ticket price).

Features

The first water park in Udmurtia in Glazov is a unique place where vacationers from all over the region gather. Izhevsk even organized special bus tours. The complex is divided into two parts:

  1. Covered area.
  2. Beach area.

In the first zone, water rides operate all year round. It is warm, good lighting, the most thoughtful interior, convenient location and pleasant atmosphere. The aquacenter allows you to swim at any time of the year, which positively affects the immunity and general health of not only adults, but also children.

Also, water aerobics classes organized by the best trainers of the republic are organized here.

In the summer, a beach area opens in the Crystal Crystal Center, which is located by a clean, well-kept pond of artificial origin. It is equipped with a special anti-slip bottom, seating areas with sun loungers, gazebos, slides.

Beach area

The water park in Glazov is the place where you can sunbathe and swim without leaving the countryside. In summer, this can be done outdoors, thanks to the beach area. This is a unique place where all the conditions for different age categories of visitors to the water area are provided.

There are Concord and Cascade slides on the territory. Here it is allowed to conduct various events, bring food and drinks with you, gather with companies and spend weekends with your family. During the existence of this site, a large number of weddings, anniversaries, birthdays, corporate parties were held here.

You can hide from the heat in cozy gazebos, ordering delicious food in the local grill bar, which is located on the banks of a picturesque reservoir.

Opening hours of the water park in Glazov:

  • The indoor water center receives visitors Mon-Fri - 12:00 - 20:00; Sat-Sun - 10:00 - 22:00.
  • The beach area is open from 10:00 to 21:00.
