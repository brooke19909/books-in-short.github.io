Centra Coconut Beach Resort Samui (Thailand, Samui): photos and reviews of tourists

Thailand is a popular destination among travelers. And one of the most picturesque places to relax is Koh Samui, where the large modern hotel complex Centra Coconut Beach Samui is located. When planning a trip, many tourists are looking for additional information about this place. Where exactly is it located? What living conditions can I expect? Is there anything to do here?

Where is the hotel located?





A small but cozy hotel Centra Coconut Beach Samui 4 * is located almost on the seashore, next to Thong Tatone beach, in the south-west of Koh Samui. The hotel is surrounded on all sides by a picturesque palm grove. This is a calm, secluded place where you can simply enjoy the beauty of nature, avoiding the bustle of the city. However, there are several shops and restaurants nearby.

In approximately 15-20 minutes, you can reach the famous Lamai Beach and Nachtong Marina. You will have to drive 40 minutes to the city center with all its attractions and attractions. The city's international airport is approximately 40 km away.

Description of infrastructure and hotel territory

Centra Coconut Beach Resort Samui occupies a small area near the seashore. Its entire territory is decorated with palm trees and lushly flowering plants, the beauty of which pleases the eye. In the center are long two-story residential buildings built in Thai style. In addition, in the palm grove there are seven separate villas, which are perfect for travelers who want to spend time in comfort and privacy. The courtyard is reserved for relaxation terraces and pools.

Hotel complex Centra Coconut Beach Resort Samui (Thailand): photo and description of rooms





This hotel can be considered relatively small - in its territory there are 53 rooms. For the most part, these are standard rooms with sea, garden or pool views. There are entire villas, however, small, which are perfect for living entire companies or families.

Regardless of the category of the room, the rooms are spacious and bright - their area ranges from 44 to 63 square meters. The rooms on the ground floor have a private terrace with access to the pool. From the rooms of the second floor there is access to a balcony with a view of the sea or the garden. The villa also has a large area with an individual garden where you can relax.

The room is furnished with the necessary amount of furniture, including a bed, wardrobes and bedside tables. A simple but elegant design will delight travelers. Of course, the rooms have the necessary appliances, including air conditioning, TV (satellite channels) with a DVD player. Guests can use the safe for storing personal belongings absolutely free of charge. There is also an electric kettle, tea, coffee and cups. The minibar is filled daily with drinking water - all other drinks in the room are delivered for a fee. Guests can freely use the Internet access, which is also important.

Each room has a spacious bathroom. There is a toilet, sink, separate bathroom and shower, where you can refresh yourself. There is also a hairdryer and a range of cosmetic branded products. According to reviews, the maids cleaned here every day, so the rooms are always clean.

What kind of food do tourists offer?





Upon request, when booking a room on the territory of the Centra Coconut Beach Samui 4 * hotel, tourists can pay for both full-fledged meals and only accommodation. In any case, in the main restaurant of the hotel you can always eat while enjoying a view of the picturesque sea coast. The restaurant offers its guests a huge selection of Thai and more familiar international dishes. For a fee, breakfast can be delivered directly to your room.

Nearby is a bar where guests can order delicious exotic cocktails, including alcohol, as well as sweets, desserts and light snacks. Next to the pool is another bar serving chilled drinks to visitors.

In addition, the city has many cafes, restaurants and hotels, so every tourist will be able to choose a cozy place for themselves.

Where is the beach located?

The scenic Thong Tatone Beach is located right next to the Centra Coconut Beach Resort Samui. 3-4 minutes walk - and you are already by the sea. The beach here is clean and well maintained, and since it belongs to the hotel, it is also quite secluded.





Comfortable sun loungers with mattresses and sun umbrellas are on the shore. The entrance to the sea is convenient, there is a shallow area, which is suitable for bathing children. In addition, Centra Coconut Beach Samui is easily accessible by public transport to the famous Lamai public beach, where you can already have fun, enjoy various water sports, attend all kinds of shows and even parties.

Are there any conditions for water recreation?

As already mentioned, the Centra Coconut Beach Samui hotel complex is located in a quiet area, some distance from the city center. Nevertheless, some entertainment is still there. For example, you can use boats for free or canoe. It will be possible to ride a boat and water ski. The nearby islands are very popular among divers, as the underwater world is really bright and rich. Right at the hotel you can buy an excursion to one of the islands and plunge into the warm sea waters. A favorite activity of many tourists staying here is fishing.

Additional services for travelers





Of course, the hotel tried to create all the conditions so that the guests' rest was not overshadowed by everyday turmoil. To begin with, it is worth saying that the laundry constantly works here. If desired, tourists can rent a safe from the administrator. There is internet access throughout. From here, if necessary, you can send a fax or use the services of a post office.

There is a small newsstand where you can get a fresh press. And on the territory of the hotel there is an experienced photographer who can hold a professional photo session for you, capturing the most interesting and bright moments of your vacation.

Entertainment and leisure of tourists





It is worth saying right away that there are no noisy entertainments on the territory of the hotel. Nevertheless, you can have a good time. Almost the entire courtyard is reserved for swimming, where you can swim, while enjoying views of the sea and nearby islands. Around there is a spacious terrace with sun loungers with umbrellas. Here, guests take sunbathing, relax and just chat with each other. Nearby is a small bar where you can get a delicious Thai drink at any time of the day.

If you want to pass the evening by watching an interesting movie, you can go to the local video library.

You should definitely visit the local spa. Here, tourists are offered a huge selection of various face and body treatments, including cosmetic peels, anti-aging masks, wellness massage and much more. By the way, the wellness center uses branded high-quality cosmetic products. There are other salons near the hotel where you can experience the wonders of Thai massage.

Naturally, this is not everything that the tourist offers the Centra Coconut Beach Resort Samui 4 * hotel complex. Samui is a wonderful island with many interesting sights. A tour desk is constantly operating at the hotel, where you can easily choose an interesting tour for a reasonable price or even visit neighboring islands and big cities of the country.

Hotel complex Centra Coconut Beach Resort Samui: reviews of tourists





Useful information can be obtained by reading reviews of people who have already managed to visit a particular place. So what do tourists say about the Centra Coconut Beach Resort Samui 4 * hotel? Reviews are for the most part positive. It is really beautiful here - a large clean area, uncomplicated design, a picturesque grove of coconut trees and, of course, a warm sea.

The rooms are also spacious, always cleaned - maids change towels every day. Household appliances are working, there is water constantly. Convenient and the presence of a separate shower. The food is quite varied, and the dishes are fresh. The disadvantage of the hotel, unfortunately, is the sea, since there are a lot of algae near the shore. True, the hotel administration is already taking steps to clear the coast.

Some tourists note that the Centra Coconut Beach Samui is located at some distance. Therefore, the hotel is unlikely to suit lovers of noisy entertainment and night parties. On the other hand, there is a car, motorbike and scooter rental service nearby. The presence of transport greatly facilitates movement in the vicinity. The city center can also be reached by bus, which runs regularly.

It is also worth noting that the hotel is not too suitable for children to relax. Of course, the child will be pleased to swim in the warm sea, and in the city there are many entertainments, but, unfortunately, there are no children‚Äôs animation and attractions in the resort complex itself.

In general, the hotel leaves behind a positive pleasant experience and is perfect for lovers of a relaxing holiday surrounded by nature, especially considering the relatively low cost of living in a hotel.
