Holidays in Lower Tavda

You can have a good rest and recharge with energy and optimism not only at foreign resorts, and domestic resorts are an excellent confirmation of this. Experienced tourists often choose ecologically clean areas where you can not only admire the local attractions, but also relax away from the noisy and gassed city. If you are also interested in holidays in ecologically clean areas, then you should pay attention to the Lower Tavda, located in the Tyumen region.

Location and climatic features

Lower Tavda is a small village with a population of just over 7000 people, located near Tyumen. The climate in this area is moderately cold with high rainfall. Temperatures range from -25 in winter to +30 in summer. The most favorable time for a vacation in this region is considered to be summer, although some tourists claim that even in the cold season you can have a great rest.

Sights of Lower Tavda

Despite the small size of the village, there is something to pay attention to. This is a village with a very rich history, and therefore, it is rich in historical monuments and ancient architecture. Before arriving in the Lower Tavda of the Tyumen region, you need to prepare in advance a list of attractions that you plan to visit, this will not only save time on their searches, but also carefully plan your route. Locals believe that coming here and not admiring the following attractions will be a big omission:

  1. Church of the Holy Trinity, erected in 2006. The style of the church is somewhat reminiscent of a tent building of the 16th century.
  2. Monument to the heroes of the Second World War in the form of a memorial stone.
  3. Monument to V.I. Lenin, there are a great many of them.
  4. Fountain.
  5. The local museum of local lore, where you can see not only a large number of ancient household items of the peoples of the North, but also the interior decoration of the courtyard. This museum is also significant in that its exhibits are located not only in its building, but also in its courtyard.
  6. Local registry office with an unusual monument in the form of wedding rings.
  7. Foul lake. The name of this lake, according to historical data, appeared after the bloody battles of Ermak with the Tatar princes. After the battle of the killed enemies, Yermak ordered to be drowned in the lake, as a result of which it became known as Pogany.

However, not only these attractions can attract the attention of tourists. There are a lot of cozy courtyards decorated with local craftsmen - wooden sculptures, but the nature of the village is simply mesmerizing with its virgin beauty.

Rest in the Tyumen region in Lower Tavda

It is a mistake to believe that in this small village it will be very boring. Holidays in this historical area will surely be appreciated by both fishermen and hunters, connoisseurs of cultural heritage and even the most fastidious tourists. But for the vacation to be successful, you need to take care not only of the cultural program, but also of your living. Of course, you can pitch a tent in some picturesque area, but it is best to rent a house or a hotel room with the same name "Tavda".

Rest on the river bank or a walk around the area is far from the only entertainment in Lower Tavda, as this village has its own indoor pool, dolphinarium, oceanarium and even an aqua park. Therefore, rest in this comfortable village will surely appeal to both children and the older generation.

Travel Guide

To get to the Lower Tavda of the Tyumen region, you need to overcome about 85 kilometers from Tyumen, passing settlements:

  • Tyunevo;
  • Karaganda region;
  • Velizhany;
  • Suit;
  • Cherepanovo.

You can get to the village both by car and by bus.

As you can see, it is interesting to relax even in such a small village as Nizhny Tavda, and for sure such a vacation will be appreciated even by the most fastidious tourist.
