Equipment





The smartphone comes on sale in a medium-sized box, made of thick cardboard, painted in red and white tones, which will reliably protect the device if necessary. In the box itself, you will find a BQ Strike Power smartphone with a factory film on the display, manufacturer documentation (warranty card, instructions), a USB cable and a USB to plug adapter for using the cable as a charger connected to a 220-volt network.

Housing

Front display

Screen

As for the screen, the BQ 5059 Strike Power has a five-inch work surface with support for Full HD quality and an impressive overview from many angles in relation to the screen. But get ready for the fact that in the sun it will fade due to insufficient brightness.

Power supply

BQ Strike Power Max is part of the Power line of phones. Based on the name, many people already understand how this line differs from most phones on the market. A non-removable battery of five thousand mA / hour is built into it. It is non-removable to minimize the thickness of the device. Five thousand mA is enough for a whole day of active use, and for everyday needs, for example, to call, send a message, check mail, more than enough for a longer period of time.





Insides

BQ Strike Power, the review of which is now presented to your attention, is positioned as a phone, the main advantage of which is the capacity of its battery. However, the filling of this device also deserves attention. So, the BQ Strike Power 4G has a MediaTek MT6580 quad-core microprocessor with a clock speed of up to 1.3 GHz under its cover. Unfortunately, this chipset does not support working with 4G networks, which is not good for the user. The ARM Mali-400 MP2 video processor is rather weak in terms of parameters and is hardly suitable for games. 1 GB of RAM and 8 GB of the main one with the ability to add Micro-SD up to 128 gigabytes is an excellent indicator, but today it will not surprise anyone.

RAM with active use, when you need to quickly switch applications, is not enough, and noticeable glitches appear. There are no complaints about the main memory, if there is not enough, you can insert a USB flash drive or use cloud storage. As a result, the BQ Strike Power specifications are very meager, when compared with the analogues of this year.
