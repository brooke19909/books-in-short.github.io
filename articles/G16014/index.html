Beeline Digital TV: List, Reviews and Connection

High definition television officially appeared in our country not so long ago. Only in 2009, the corresponding state standard was approved, which defines the parameters of HD. One of the operators that can broadcast channels in a suitable quality under GOST is Beeline.





Necessary equipment

If you want to watch an expanded list of television channels without being distracted by ripples on the screen and other noise, it is worth thinking about connecting a digital television. Beeline is ready to provide appropriate services to each of its customers. True, a prerequisite is the presence of a fiber optic network of this operator in your home. For example, each user of the home Internet provided by Beeline can connect to one of the offered packages of TV channels.

But for this, you must first purchase equipment. Beeline TV home digital television will become available as soon as you buy a set with a set-top box for the TV. It can be with or without a recording function.

The set, in addition to the main set-top box, includes: a control panel, a cable for connecting to a TV and 2 Ethernet cords, a switch. Of course, a device with a recording function is more expensive than usual. However, having acquired it, you will get the opportunity not only to save interesting fragments in memory, but also to pause while watching programs, rewind them forward (backward), and return to the beginning of the plot you are watching.





Beeline HD Benefits

It's no secret that the picture that a conventional analog television gives is uncomfortable to watch on large screens. Therefore, very often, along with the purchase of a new flat-screen TV, most people think about buying a satellite dish or tuner. If you already have home Internet from Beeline provider, then it will be much easier for you to connect high-quality digital television. It should be noted that the usual analog signal gives 486 lines, while HDTV - 1080. And thanks to the special perception by human eyes of the image on the TV, it turns out that the picture is 6 times clearer.

In addition to excellent quality, you get a lot of advantages. After all, Beeline digital television provides a choice. You can decide what and when to watch. Each client can choose the appropriate channel package for him. In addition, modern digital television allows you to watch the program for two weeks in advance right on the screen.

How to become a Beeline subscriber





To be able to watch broadcast programs in excellent quality, you need to connect digital television. Beeline provides the opportunity to buy the appropriate console or rent it. In addition, you will also need a Wi-Fi router (switch), through which you can connect a computer, laptop or tablet, and a TV tuner to a single network.

But the main condition is the availability of home wired Internet from this provider or the technical ability to conduct it. If there is no problem with this, then it remains only to purchase or rent the appropriate prefix. By the way, for customers who use the Internet and watch television in HD quality, special payment conditions are provided. For example, from 10.07.2014 everyone can rent a tuner absolutely free. A monthly fee for Internet and TV will be set depending on the selected software package.

Connection Features





Having bought the appropriate equipment, you can configure it yourself, or you can invite a specialist who will do all the necessary work. If you do not want to wait for a person from technical support, then information on how to connect is useful to you. This is done as follows. Initially, the switch and set-top box are connected to the network. After that, a cable of a dedicated Internet line is inserted into any of the ports of the router. The next step is to connect Ethernet cables, the so-called patch cords. The presence of a signal is checked by the illuminated indicators on the router. If this happened, then you can safely connect one patch cord to the computer, and the second to the acquired TV tuner. After that, the prefix can be connected to the TV and watch your favorite movies and shows through the tuner from the Beeline operator. Digital television, the list of channels on which the subscriber chooses at his discretion, gives access to advanced features.

Further image settings will be made depending on what kind of TV you have and what screen resolution is maximum for it: HD Ready or Full HD.
