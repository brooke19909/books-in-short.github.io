Bali Kut hotels await their guests

Not far from Denpasar, the capital of the island of Bali, is the flourishing city of Kuta - the most popular and lively resort. The wonderful coast of the island attracts tourists and vacationers from all over the Earth with its endless golden sand beaches.

Popular chic hotels in Bali Kut are always ready to receive guests. There are different categories of hotels in the beach area. Fans of excellent service are always waiting for the four-star hotels Best Westem Kuta, Alam Kulkul and others, which occupy

more than three hectares of tropical plantations near the city center. Elegant, luxurious rooms with balconies or terraces are equipped with air conditioning, a toilet, a bathroom with a hydromassage, a shower. All rooms have digital television, Internet access, a personal safe and a minibar.

Guestrooms have direct dial phones. If desired, a convenient wake-up call service can be connected. For the convenience of residents, the room always has purified bottled water. Hotel restaurants specialize in international dishes. At any time, guests can order lunch in the room.

Guarded covered parking spaces are equipped for vacationers' personal cars. Hotels Bali Kuta if necessary, provide concierge services, laundry, dry cleaning. For business meetings, halls for banquets and conferences are provided. For parents,

who arrived with the children, babysitting services are provided . Children will be supervised by experienced professionals - educators or teachers.

There are hotels in Bali Kuta, the rest of which is more affordable. On the beach there are homely comfortable hotels that have gained popularity among tourists with a decent level of service. It should be noted that three-star Hotels also provide top-level room service. The staff is polite and friendly, doing everything for a comfortable stay, foreseeing in advance the wishes of the guests.

Hotels are located Bali Kuta in the west of the island, which allows you to admire the most beautiful sunset. Many hotels are surrounded by lush vegetation of the tropics with exotic animals. Fantastic in their beauty, they are never empty. Arriving to relax, you will enjoy the aroma of the ocean surf, the atmosphere of the busy streets of the resort town even at night.



The sea in Kut is the most diverse wave size, which attracts amateurs and professionals of windsurfing and body-surfing. In many outlets you can purchase special equipment for any sport.

Bali Kuta hotels have tour desks to help guests get acquainted with the sights of the amazing Indonesian island. On the territory there are a large number of various shops, night bars and clubs, snack bars, cafes and restaurants.

Having been on the island once, many try to book the same rooms at the hotel and on their next visit to Bali Kuta. Reviews from tourists and vacationers are the most wonderful.
