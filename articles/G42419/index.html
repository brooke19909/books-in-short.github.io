How to find out at MTS where the money went from the account?

Almost every MTS subscriber had to deal with the problem of disappearance of funds from a mobile number account. Incomprehensible write-offs, unreasonable decrease in the balance in the absence of connected services and options. How to find out where the money went at MTS, how to check the availability of subscriptions and mailings that have a monthly fee, how to turn them off, whether it is possible to return money illegally withdrawn from the account - we will consider these issues in this article. We will also tell you how to control the connection of services.





MTS Account Management: all options

In order for the problem of the disappearance of funds from the account not to concern you, it is necessary to exercise full control over your number. Thanks to the popularity of the Internet and the remote provision of data, this is very easy. Firstly, there is such a thing as a personal account. This is the personal page of the subscriber, which contains detailed information on the number: starting from the tariff plan, the list of connected services, ending with data on expenses. By registering on the official portal of the mobile operator and periodically checking the status of the account, you can timely identify options that were not connected by you, and quickly get rid of them. By the way, a mobile application has been developed for smartphones and tablets that replaces your personal account and provides all the same functions that are available on the site. Secondly, in the absence of the Internet, you can use the technical support services provided by the mobile operator. The question "Where does the money go to MTS?" can be asked by a customer service specialist, while specifying the owner of the number. If there are paid services that may be the reason for the costs, the employee will inform about this and tell you how they can be deactivated.

Conclusion
