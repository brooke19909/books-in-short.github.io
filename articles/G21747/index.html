Krasnodar Territory, the city of Temryuk, hotels: addresses, descriptions, reviews

Every year thousands of tourists from all over the world come to rest in the city of Temryuk. For them, the sights of the Taman Peninsula are of primary interest. Fans of hunting and fishing will also find many interesting things for themselves. Numerous hunting and fishing bases work for them. This and much more attracts many vacationers to the city of Temryuk. The hotels here are warmly prepared to receive visitors not only in summer but also in winter.

Hotels in the village of Golubitskaya, Temryuk district

One of the leisure options for guests of Temryuk is accommodation by the sea in the village of Golubitskaya, founded in 1556. It is located near the train station, 8 km from the city center. Flamingo





Guest house "Flamingo" is located at the address: Krasnodar Territory, rn Temryuk, stanitsa Golubitskaya, st. Kurortnaya, 68. The hotel can be reached by rail or by using the transfer services at the rates on the day of booking. The guest house is literally within walking distance of the wonderful sunny beach. The four-story building easily accommodates up to 100 guests. The number of rooms is represented by four-, three- and double rooms with all amenities (bathroom, air conditioning, furniture). The kitchens are equipped with electric stoves for cooking. A hundred meters from the guest house is a beach equipped with everything necessary (sunbeds, umbrellas, sunshades). For lovers of water recreation, there are jet skis available, as well as skiing from water slides. On the beach, everyone can do beach volleyball or ride a boat. In the evening, vacationers can have fun at the disco. More and more tourists are attracted by the city of Temryuk. The hotels here are inexpensive and the service is of a high standard.

Infrastructure





Separately, you should stay on the infrastructure of the guest house. On its territory there is parking for vehicles. Guests can rent a quad bike or bicycle. There is a sauna for guests. Sports enthusiasts are given the opportunity to play table tennis, go diving or rock climbing. On the ground floor of the guest house there is a small cafe where vacationers can eat. High quality service, a wide selection of different dishes will pleasantly surprise the most demanding tourist. Vacationers can order breakfast, lunch or dinner separately. For children, the cafe has a special menu. Also, the highlight of the institution is the implementation of culinary orders of tourists. Vacationers can use free sunbeds on the beach. Tourists planning to stay in the guest house must have with them identification documents (passport or birth certificate for the child). Mandatory is a ticket. It is desirable that vacationers have health insurance. Reviews about the guest house "Flamingo" are extremely positive. Vacationers are delighted with the quality service of friendly staff, the proposed entertainment and living conditions. The city of Temryuk is rich in such places.
