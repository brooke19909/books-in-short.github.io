This hotel is located in the village of Bogazkent, five kilometers from Belek. Antalya city center is 45 km and the nearest airport is 35 km.

This hotel was built in 2014. The housing stock is located in two five-story buildings and includes 612 rooms of the following categories: standard, family and king-suite.

Food in the hotel complex is organized according to the ultra all-inclusive system. The area itself is 50 thousand square meters. meters. There are several pools, a spa, a gym, bars, restaurants (the main and a la carte), a mini club, an amphitheater, a shop and much more. The hotel has its own sandy beach.
