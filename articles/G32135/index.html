Hotels of Muravlenko. Description. Reviews

Muravlenko, a small town in the Far North, was founded in 1984 thanks to the oil fields that surround it from all sides. Oil specialists come here with business trips, therefore it is important to have conditions for their accommodation. Muravlenko hotels will provide cozy and comfortable rooms that will allow visitors to relax after a hard day.

Hotel "Rus"

The location of the hotel is the central part of the city. Hotel "Rus" in Muravlenko is within walking distance from shopping centers, restaurants, banks, administrative centers, the Central Park of Culture and Rest named after Muravlenko. For guests with private vehicles, free private parking is available. There is access to wireless internet. The hotel has its own cafe, which operates Russian cuisine. A cash machine is located on site. Laundry and ironing services are available at an extra charge. The hotel offers a wellness center, beauty salons and massage services. Here guests are allowed to stay with pets, service charges are not charged. There is no elevator. For accommodation, the hotel "Rus" offers several categories of rooms:

  • standard single - 2300 rubles per day;
  • standard double - 4000 rubles per day (2000 rubles for one place);
  • standard triple - 3900 rubles per day (1300 rubles for one place);
  • Business categories - 3200 rubles per day;
  • luxury category - 5200 rubles per day.

All rooms are equipped with cable TV, telephone, electric kettle. The hotel is located in Muravlenko (Yamal-Nenets Autonomous Okrug) at the address: st. Gubkin, house 16.

Hotel E-Hotel

The hotel is located more than one kilometer from the city center. Its distinguishing feature from other Muravlenko hotels is the ability to use free bike rental. The hotel has private paid parking, places for people with disabilities are provided. Upon request, an airport shuttle can be arranged for you. Wireless internet access is free at the hotel. For an additional fee, guests can use the laundry, iron, shoe shine. The hotel has a gift shop. In the morning a continental breakfast is served. The staff can help arrange a banquet or business meeting. E-Hotel offers the following room categories:

  • double with two single beds (shared bathroom) - 2000 rubles per day;
  • double deluxe room with a large double bed - 4000 rubles per day for two, and 3000 rubles per day for one person;
  • Suite (there is a hot tub) - 6000 rubles per day for two, 5000 rubles per day for one person.

All rooms except double with shared bathroom are inclusive of breakfast. The hotel E-Hotel is located at: st. Yamal, house 28A.

Hostel Rus Muravlenko

If you are interested in budget hotels in Muravlenko, Yamal-Nenets Autonomous Okrug, pay attention to the Rus Muravlenko hostel. Nearby are a cafe, restaurant and supermarket. It offers a shared kitchen, free parking, and free Wi-Fi in public areas. Here you can stay with your pets at no extra charge upon prior request. To host the hostel offers the following room options:

  • double with one double or two single beds - 2500 rubles per day;
  • general six-seater with bunk beds - 800 rubles per day for one person;
  • common eight-seater with bunk beds - 800 rubles per day for one person.

Room rates include breakfast. All guests have access to a shared bathroom. The Rus Muravlenko Hostel is located at: Friendship of Peoples Street, 20A.
