Rest in the private sector in Lazarevsky: features and prices

The bright sun, warm sea and wide pebble beaches - all that is needed for an unforgettable summer vacation! That is why many citizens of Russia choose Lazarevskoye for their holidays. This resort is located at a distance of 50 kilometers from Sochi and is distinguished by convenient transport links and developed infrastructure. It offers vacationers and shops, and cafes, and restaurants, and discos, and shopping centers, and a water park. And when all this is boring, you can even go on an excursion to Sochi.

But no matter how beautiful the resort may be, everything can be ruined badly

matched housing. Therefore, this issue must be resolved a few weeks before the trip, especially if you are interested in holidays in the private sector in Lazarevsky. This is due to the fact that although this area is represented by numerous mini-hotels and guest houses, it is still quite difficult to find an option that would suit the location, internal equipment and price.

When planning a vacation in the private sector in Lazarevsky, you first need to decide what kind of housing you are interested in. It can be cottages, private or guest houses, villas and, of course, mini-hotels. Next, you need to pay attention to the internal equipment of the housing and the adjacent courtyard.

After all, if you have already decided to spend your holidays in the private sector in Lazarevsky, then you will probably want to feel at home in rented housing, as well as have the opportunity to sit in the gazebo, admiring the flowers in the flower beds and other vegetation. It is good when there is a pool in the courtyard, because then in order to swim, you will not necessarily go to the sea. Take an interest in the presence of a playground if you decide to visit Lazarevskoye with your whole family. Rest, prices The private sector of this resort offers a wide variety of prices. Therefore, if you devote some time to this lesson, you will surely find a suitable option.

Vacationers who plan to spend most of their time on the beach need to make sure their accommodation is as close to the water as possible. Of course, usually such options are more expensive, but then for many years to come

such a vacation at sea will be remembered . Lazarevskoye offers to stay next to him such guest houses as Evelina, Ekaterina, U Lesa, Semerochka, Nika, or the following private houses: Hut, Homeliness, House and a lot others. All these types of housing are distinguished by well-equipped rooms and cozy courtyards, some of them offer their guests three meals a day, and some provide a fully equipped kitchen for self-cooking.

If proximity to the sea is not so important to you, but the cost of housing is more worrying, you can choose to relax in the private sector in Lazarevsky in the center or on the opposite side of the village from the sea. In this case, you will get excellent accommodation at an affordable price. Such guest houses and private houses as Adele, Rita, Antonina, Coolness, etc. deserve special attention.
