Appearance story

The introduction of this definition into circulation is associated with the continuation of the reform that began in 1996. Since that time, the establishment of individual registration of citizens in the general pension system has been phased. One of the levels of conversion is insurance experience. The Federal Law of 04/01/1996 and July 16, 1999 No. 165 give different interpretations of this concept, due to which a certain contradiction arises.





The first law does not mention labor activity at all, the second has a more precise wording, but it also leaves many questions about what insurance experience is in reality. The contradictions in the definitions make it possible to take into account even the time when a person did not officially work, and there were no transfers to the Pension Fund.

Features of the calculations

Future social benefits to Russians are formed in the mandatory pension system. The procedure for calculating the length of service is established by federal law dated 17.12.2001 No. 173 ‚Äì (amended on 11/19/2015). Payments are assigned and made in the form of an urgent, one-time or funded pension.





The calculation of the insurance period is based on calendar values, in case of coincidence of periods, the citizen has the right to choose one of them. All accumulations are recalculated into special points, their amount depends on the monthly payments by the employer in the PF. The state indexes these values ‚Äã‚Äãevery year according to the existing inflation. For persons self-sufficient in earning money, which may be members of farms, as well as representatives of small peoples of Siberia, periods of required length of service are calculated provided that they regularly contribute part of their income to the Pension Fund of the Russian Federation. The same rule applies to individual entrepreneurs.

Accounting for non-insurance periods

The legislation provides for exceptional cases in which a person was not able to make regular deductions to non-state funds for a good reason. For such persons, the state has established its own system of accruing insurance points:

  • one year of military service - 1.8;
  • caring for a disabled person, a person older than 80 years - 1.8;
  • maternity leave with the first child - 1.8;
  • with the second - 3.6;
  • with the third and fourth - 5.4.

Moreover, the period of raising the fifth child is no longer included in the total length of service.
