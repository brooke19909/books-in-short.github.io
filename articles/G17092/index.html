Is it possible to repay a mortgage in Sberbank ahead of schedule by maternity capital?

Parents with two or more children often have to deal with the question of whether it is possible to early repay the mortgage in Sberbank. It depends on the conditions for using the loan how soon the family will be allowed to use the important state subsidy - maternity capital.





Why apply for a mortgage

In cases where state aid is used to build housing, acquire square meters, pay for educational services or form the funded part of the pension, it is impossible to receive money until the child is three years old. If the maternity capital goes to pay off the mortgage, you do not need to wait until the decree expires. That is why the registration of a home loan with the right to early repayment becomes for parents the main means of gaining access to subsidies.





Pay attention to loan repayment terms

Commercial banks, as a rule, are not interested in having clients repay the loan ahead of time. Therefore, many potential borrowers are worried about whether it is possible to repay a mortgage loan at Sberbank ahead of schedule.

When obtaining a loan, it is important to read and analyze the loan documentation line by line, not succumbing to the provocation of employees who want to rush you. The terms of loan repayment are usually devoted to a whole section of the contract. Pay attention to the payment schedule - it can be taken out in a separate application. Are the dates indicated in it convenient for you? Is the monthly payment amount too large? The terms of prepayment are described in detail, although they can be found in footnotes or hidden in an abundance of text in small print.





Tricks of Lenders

Many banks try to limit the right of customers to repay a loan in whole or in part earlier than the terms specified in the contract. For this, additional conditions are often made to the loan documentation. For example, such:

  1. Early repayment is allowed no earlier than after a long time period, which may be 6 months or more.
  2. Set a minimum threshold for the amount of payment ahead of time. For example, a borrower is prohibited from sending less than 10 thousand rubles in repayment. Accordingly, the bonus that was suddenly received at work, if it is not too large, cannot be paid to reduce debt.
  3. The documentation may establish a fee for early repayment of the loan. As a rule, its size discourages the desire to pay off obligations ahead of time.
  4. Much less often in loan documentation there is an enslaving condition obliging you to pay interest and fees in full for a specified period of time (for example, a month or a quarter), despite the fact that you are repaying a loan ahead of schedule.




Is it possible to early repay a mortgage in Sberbank

Conditions restricting the borrower's right to repay obligations ahead of time become especially relevant during the financial crisis. Therefore, many customers are beginning to doubt whether it is possible to repay a mortgage loan in Sberbank ahead of schedule.

In the country's largest financial institution, any loans are allowed to repay earlier than the dates established by the agreements. No commission is required. It is permissible to deposit money even the next day after receiving a loan. The main debt will be closed or reduced subject to the simultaneous repayment of accrued interest. It is important only to inform the bank in writing no later than two business days of what date and what amount you want to return ahead of schedule. Based on this notification, responsible employees will carry out the redemption transaction. Otherwise, the funds you deposit are deposited in a special account and will be debited to the account for repayment of the loan debt on a monthly basis in accordance with the payment schedule.





A message on the early repayment of loan funds is compiled and signed by the client in person at the bank office.

Your loan also relies weekend

Many recipients of maternity capital are also interested in the question of whether it is possible to repay a mortgage in Sberbank ahead of schedule on Saturday. The operation to repay loan obligations will take place on the same day if the office in which the loan was issued works. If the branch is closed on Saturday, repayment of the mortgage will be carried out on the next weekday. No credit operations are performed on Sunday.

Applying for a subsidy

The Pension Fund of the Russian Federation also often receives requests whether it is possible to early repay a mortgage in Sberbank using maternity capital.

In 2016, the size of the parent certificate is still 453,026 rubles.

To take advantage of the subsidy, parents of two or more children will need to collect a package of documents established by law:

- identification;

- SNILS;

- TIN;

- certificate of marriage (divorce);

- a certificate from the housing department about the composition of the family;

- birth certificates of all children;

- generic certificate;

- notarial obligation of parents to allocate a share in housing to children.



Consideration by the Pension Fund of the Russian Federation of the question of whether it is possible to repay a mortgage in Sberbank ahead of schedule by maternal capital personally for you, takes 1 month. After this period, if there is no conflict with the law, a certificate will be prepared.

Next, you will need to prepare a second package of documents containing information about the loan agreement:

  1. Borrower passport repeatedly.
  2. SNILS.
  3. INN
  4. Loan agreement (original or notarized copy).
  5. Pledge agreement (mortgage) - the original or a notarized copy.
  6. Payment schedule.
  7. Statement of account movements (how much loan funds were issued and actually returned to the bank).
  8. Payment details.

If the documents are correctly prepared, after one month the Pension Fund will transfer the amount of the certificate to a special bank account.

To do this, one month after the submission of the second package of documents, you can personally appear in the mortgage office of Sberbank. By telephone, employees are not allowed to provide financial information unless a code word is entered for you.

However, it is much more convenient to control the payment of maternity capital through a personal account in the Sberbank Online system. Using your username and password, you track the flow of funds through any computer connected to the Internet. You can enter Sberbank Online through ATMs, payment terminals, and also using the mobile application.

After the transaction, the payment schedule is recalculated - the amount of the monthly payment is proportionally reduced.



To start all over from scratch?

Parents who plan to receive maternity capital in the near future are often interested in whether it is possible to repay a loan ahead of schedule in Sberbank and take a new one. According to the law, any private person can conclude several loan agreements at the same time . From this point of view, in order to get a new loan, it is not necessary to repay an existing one.

But lending services of the bank, analyzing the possibility of providing a loan, calculate your solvency. It is determined on the basis of income statements. Existing obligations are also taken into account. The level of debt burden on a person should be such that he can pay debts on time, while providing food for himself and non-working family members. There are also some loan products, which must be covered by pledges or sureties.

Having paid the mortgage ahead of schedule, you can again offer the housing free from encumbrance to the bank as security for new loans. Secured by financial institutions, loans are much easier and more willing.

However, if your loan is mortgage, do not rush to repay it ahead of schedule if you assume that funds may soon be needed again. Housing loans are usually the cheapest to maintain, and the financing terms for them are the longest. Mortgage interest rate is from 11% per annum. On consumer loans, it increases to a level of 14.9 to 21.9% per annum. For housing loans, the maximum financing term is 30 years, for consumer loans - only 5 years. Mortgage loan repayment is possible only upon the purchase of a new property.





Travel Information

Families who have to change their place of residence are worried: is it possible to repay a loan ahead of schedule in Sberbank in another region? Current loan payments can be made as usual. To do this, it is sufficient to transfer the required amount to the account specified in the contract in a timely manner. But early repayment from another region, the borrower will not be able to personally carry out. Indeed, to carry out this operation, it is necessary to come to the bank office, in which the housing loan was issued , to sign the notice.

In this regard, for parents preparing to move, the question of whether it is possible to repay a loan ahead of schedule by proxy early becomes relevant. After all, it depends on whether it will be possible to instruct any of the relatives or friends to return the housing loan.

Why go to a notary

In order to deposit money into a special account designed to accumulate funds, a power of attorney is not needed. However, in order for the operation to repay the loan ahead of schedule, a notice signed by the borrower or another person authorized to act on his behalf is required.

The right to represent the interests of the client in the bank is certified by a notarized power of attorney. Its text must contain phrases that mean that your friend or relative is allowed to repay the loan obligations ahead of schedule.

So, is it possible to repay a mortgage in Sberbank ahead of schedule by means of maternity capital? Yes, if you are legally entitled to receive a subsidy. Know your opportunities and take advantage of them.
