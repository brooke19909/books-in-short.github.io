Dadonghai Bay (China, Hainan): description, beaches, reviews

Hainan Island (China) is considered to be one of the best resorts in the country. Centuries ago, it was positioned as an island for nobles. The warm sea, a large number of wide beaches and stunning landscapes do not leave indifferent even the most sophisticated tourists. Hainan became famous all over the world as the "Eastern Harbor" and became the main place for recreation in China.

One of the most famous places on the island is Dadonghai Bay, the infrastructure of which has grown to a full-fledged resort. The azure sea, comfortable beaches and fascinating diving - these are the main reasons to come here on vacation. In this article you will learn more about what Dadonghai resort is and what features a vacation in this place has.





Geographic location

Dadonghai Bay is located on the southwestern side of Sanya, which is also very popular among tourists. Nevertheless, tourists more often choose a bay for recreation, since in cities the prices for accommodation and entertainment are much higher. The distance from the bay to the city is about three kilometers. At 15 kilometers from the city of Sanya Phoenix International Airport is located.

How to get to the resort?

Getting to the resort from the airport is not at all difficult. Here, tourists have three options: regular bus, taxi or transfer (if the hotel provides such a service). Buses run regularly from Dadudhaya to Sanya, so traveling to the city is easy. And besides, the distance of three kilometers can easily be overcome on foot, admiring the scenic landscapes along the way. A bus from Sanya to Dadonghai will cost a tourist 1 yuan, and a taxi 10-15 yuan (1 yuan - 8.95 rubles). Local buses can travel around the entire island.





Climate

Since Hainan is the southernmost island of China, it has a tropical climate. Almost 300 days a year, Dadonghai resort welcomes its guests with warm sea and sunny weather. Since the island is constantly warm, only two seasons are distinguished here - rainy and dry. In the period from April to November, heavy rains reign on the island along with intense heat. At this time, the cost of rest falls.

The average annual air temperature in Dadonghai is 24 ¬∞ , and in the South China Sea washing it - 22 ¬∞ . During the year, a sharp temperature difference does not occur. However, the island is obviously cooler in winter than in summer, especially in the evening. In autumn, the island is subject to the influence of typhoons. Nevertheless, the tourist season lasts all year round.

Such a concept as a high season in Dadonghai is very arbitrary. Since weather conditions allow you to comfortably rest here all year round, the influx of tourists usually depends on the holiday dates. Nevertheless, in the summer, due to showers and heat, the popularity of the resort falls. Most tourists come here in December and January. And in September and October, mainly Chinese people rest at the resort. Swimming on the beaches of Dadonghai Bay is best in November and from March to June.





Beaches

The beaches of Dadonghai resemble a young moon in shape. In principle, this is one huge beach with a length of almost two kilometers. Among the clean white sand you can find oases with palm trees, which create a pleasant coolness. Thanks to the excellent wind rose, this place is famous among surf lovers. The disadvantage of this resort is the abundance of algae, which after typhoons and storms strongly pollute the water and the coast. There are always a lot of people on the municipal beaches of the resort. It goes to rest the local population.

Particularly noteworthy are beaches with coral reefs. They can be found here more than 100 species. Corals serve as a habitat for 300 species of fish. Due to the fact that in the season when there are no storms, the water of the South China Sea is very clean, divers and lovers of boat trips can fully appreciate all the maritime beauties of the harbor.

Entertainment

The resort offers its guests all the conditions for a first-class pastime. Lovers of outdoor activities can go diving, surfing, fishing, riding scooters and ATVs, snorkeling or go on a boat trip. Those wishing to relax in a more relaxed atmosphere can experience local cuisine and music in bars, restaurants or clubs. In addition, there are many shops in Dadonghai Bay (Hainan) that offer great shopping. The largest shopping center of the resort is Summer Mall.





Kitchen

Hainan Island and Dadonghai Bay in particular are famous for quite interesting exotic cuisine, which can surprise even real gourmets. In local restaurants and bars, they serve dishes not only of Chinese cuisine, but also cuisines of several other countries: India, Thailand, Italy, Japan and even Russia. Due to the abundance of seafood and tropical fruits, the menu of local restaurants is very diverse. In it you can meet such delicacies as shark fin soup, coconut dishes and more.

The main dishes of the local cuisine are dishes from chicken, fed with sesame seeds, and soups, which are used at the end of the meal. A feature of all local food is the use of fermented soybeans and rice. Also in restaurants you can find a lot of meat and vegetable dishes.

The resort offers inexpensive, but very tasty fruit fresh fruits everywhere.





Dadonghai Bay Hotels

Dadonghai is considered a relatively inexpensive resort. At the same time, many local hotels belong to the high categories (4-5 stars), due to cleanliness, quality service, wide range of services and excellent location. The local population is very responsible and working, so there are practically no problems with the service here. Most hotels have Russian-speaking staff, a private area with a beach, a tour desk and an entertainment program.

When choosing a hotel, you should pay attention to such aspects:

  1. Having your own beach.
  2. Supply system.
  3. The comfort of the rooms.
  4. The presence of entertainment and sports events.
  5. Availability of additional services.

The best hotels to be highlighted are: 4-star Royal Garden Resort, 5-star Marina Spa Hotel, 5-star Shanhaitian Hotel Sanya, 4-star Linda Hotel - Linda Seaview Hotel. If you wish, in the Dadonghai Bay you can find budget hotels with a minimum number of additional services and an acceptable level of comfort.





Attractions of Dadonghai Bay

The bay is located between two mountains: "Turned Deer" and "Tail of the Hare." Mount Turned Deer is the main attraction of the harbor and a famous natural monument. Once upon a time, local shamans gathered here to perform their rites. Today, on the slopes of the mountain there is a beautiful landscape park where you can admire the local beauties, as well as meet monkeys, turtles, deer and other interesting animals.

According to ancient legend, once a hunter wanted to catch a golden deer on this mountain. The pursuit of the beast dragged on for nine days. In the end, the hunter managed to drive the deer off the coast. Realizing that there is no way to retreat, turn the deer into a beautiful girl who amazed the hunter with her beauty. As a result, the couple fell in love with each other, and they lived happily ever after. This is the most common variant of the origin of the unusual name of the mountain. There are other versions. All of them are displayed in numerous souvenirs that are sold in the shops of the bay.

In the vicinity of the resort, you can find other attractions: museums, ancient temples (the most famous of them is the Nanshan complex, which is one of the main centers of Buddhism in China), factories (the pearl factory in Sanya is especially popular), nature reserves (including famous park Sanya Meitianya and "Monkey Park").

Wellness

Hainan Island (China) is a great place not only for relaxation, but also for recreation. There are several popular centers of traditional medicine in the Dadonghai harbor: the Garden of Longevity, the Taiji Clinic, the Do Wen Tien Medical Center and others.





Treatment in local health facilities is carried out in accordance with traditional methods, the effectiveness of which has been confirmed over the centuries. As a rule, treatment includes: massage, breathing exercises, gymnastics, taking herbal decoctions, acupuncture, reflexology and more. All these measures can get rid of a large number of diseases against which modern drug medicine is not always effective.

Family resort

In Dadonghai it is convenient to relax with children of different ages - a healing tropical climate, clean sandy beaches, warm sea and fresh exotic fruits will help the baby get a lot of impressions from the trip. In addition, the resort can find a lot of children's entertainment and playgrounds.
