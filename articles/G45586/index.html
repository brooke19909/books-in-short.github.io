Ways to crochet a volumetric flower (diagram and description)

It is permissible for them to decorate everything: from a hat and handbag to a T-shirt and dress. The needlewomen have already guessed that this is a volumetric flower (crochet). The layout of such an accessory can vary greatly. It can be made very simple or intricate. Moreover, in each case, it will be eye-catching.





Flower with any petals

It is with anyone. Because here it is only necessary to complete the base, and then knit the petals according to the pattern that the craftswoman wants. An experienced one will choose something more difficult, and a beginner can stop on simple columns.

In all cases, they will only have a similar foundation. To get a voluminous flower (crochet), the scheme of its knitting will begin with a ring. It is recommended to make it of 6 loops.

The number of lifting loops depends on the size of the petals of the future flower. You can start with 7. Then, connect 10 air loops, make a column with four crochets - this is the first petal. Repeat the air loops and column again as many times as there will be petals. The basis for the volumetric flower is ready.

Then each petal is tied in turn. Ten stitches and the next column must be crocheted. The second row is allowed to be made with double crochets. And then knit an arbitrary openwork pattern. The end of this row must be fixed on the dial ring to avoid protruding the petal from the finished product.

The third row is usually also made openwork, and the last - fifth - with a decorative strapping, for example, alternating a connecting column with an air loop. Once again, fix the work on the dial. On the wrong side, move to the beginning of the second lobe. Repeat all rows for this item and then for others.

Alternatively, it is recommended to make the petals of different sizes, for example, alternate large and smaller. Then you get an original volumetric flower (crochet), the pattern of the petals of different sizes, of course, should vary.

Please note that the number of petals depends on their width. There should be many narrow ones so that they overlap each other and there are no gaps between them. There may even be six wide. They will still look voluminous and spectacular.





A flower with many loose petals

In this case, the crochet pattern of a volumetric flower will resemble the previous one. Only individual petals will not be fastened at the top. They will be, like daisies, long and attached to the ring with one edge.

Tie a small ring into which at least 8 petals will be attached. Each of them is formed by 10 air loops. To make a connection to the ring, you need to make a single crochet.

For knitting the first row of each petal forming a three-dimensional flower (crochet), the pattern will be the same. Its bottom is made by connecting columns, then single crochet, with one and, if necessary, with two crochets. The amount of each of these elements is determined by the needlewoman herself. For example, if there are 21 columns in total, then the first one by one, the second - two, that is, 15 will be left on the double crochet, let the middle be with two.

Knit the second row similarly to the first. Only the number of columns can be increased to 25-27 by knitting two columns from the same base at the top of the petal. To crochet this volumetric flower, the pattern does not have to be that way. It can be made more airy by adding an openwork pattern to the drawing.





Gift Idea: Knitted Bouquet

Those who have addressed this topic before have come across volumetric color schemes (crocheted) that look like real ones. The needlewoman may like to make a gift from knitted flowers in a small vase. Cute, beautiful and unique.

In order to perform such crocheting of volumetric colors, the most useful schemes are useful. That is, those in which the flowers themselves are flat. On a small ring of air loops, knit five tiny petals. The result is a violet flower. If the petals are made more authentic and three, then it will be a snowdrop or crocus.

Supplement them with single crochet legs and matching leaves. It remains only to collect the bouquet in a vase to give.
