Hotel Print Kamala Resort 4 * (Phuket, Thailand): description and photos

If you want to spend your vacation in a tropical paradise on the coast of the warm sea, but do not plan to spend money on an expensive hotel, then Print Kamala Resort 4 * hotel in Phuket (Thailand) can be a great solution for you.



Location

This hotel is located in the western part of Phuket, next to Kamala Beach. Distance to the sea is only 100 meters. So, to get to the beach, you just need to leave the room, go through the reception and cross the roadway. The hotel is nine kilometers from the Patong Beach nightlife center. You can get here by tuk-tuk or rented motorbike. Travel time is about 15 minutes. As for the Phuket airport, the distance to it is 35 kilometers. So on arrival you will not have to spend a lot of time on the road to the hotel. In general, with regard to the vicinity of the hotel, then, according to tourists resting here, the infrastructure here is well developed. So, within walking distance there are many cafes, shops, shops, markets, pharmacies, massage parlors, travel agencies, transport rental offices, etc.



General information, photo

Print Kamala Resort 4 * hotel was opened in 2004. In 2014, partial renovation was carried out here. The hotel has a not very large, but green and well-groomed territory, where, judging by the reviews of guests, you can pleasantly walk and relax. For the convenience of guests, there is a swimming pool, and next to it there is a sun terrace with comfortable sun loungers. The housing stock consists of 102 rooms, which are located in three- and four-story buildings and several one-story bungalows. The hotel has a restaurant, bar, parking, conference room. Rooms

As already mentioned, Print Kamala Resort 4 * (Phuket, Thailand) has 102 apartments. They are either in buildings or in a bungalow. The rooms here are of the following categories: deluxe, superior, family suite. The apartment has everything you need for a comfortable stay - a TV with cable channels (including in Russian), a telephone, air conditioning, a safe, a hairdryer, beds, bedside tables, a wardrobe and other furniture, a bathroom, a balcony or terrace with table, chairs and clothes dryer. In general, according to travelers who have been here, the equipment and furniture in the rooms are of high quality and sound. However, former guests note that not all bungalows were renovated, so upon arrival at the hotel, ask you to check in your recently refurbished rooms if possible.

Cleaning service

Our compatriots, leaving 4 * reviews about Print Kamala Resort, for the most part were quite satisfied with the work of the hotel maids. So, according to them, the cleaning of the rooms was carried out quite high quality and every day. True, guests note that sometimes the maids forgot to sweep on the balcony or terrace, but this was not a serious problem. As for the towels, they were updated every day. The maids changed the bed linen in the rooms twice a week.

As for the rest of the hotel staff, in general, the guests had no particular complaints about it. So, tourists say that the staff here are very friendly, friendly and always happy to help. True, it should be noted that no one speaks Russian here. In addition, not all staff speak English at a good level. However, the guests without any difficulties, according to them, managed to communicate with the employees of Print Kamala Resort.



Nutrition

The price of accommodation at Print Kamala Resort 4 * (Phuket) includes breakfast only. They pass in the hotel restaurant. According to our compatriots, they are standard for most budget hotels in Thailand. So, in the morning you will be offered to taste several dishes of local cuisine (soup, noodles, meat or fish), vegetables (both fresh and heat-treated), cereals, rice, pastries, scrambled eggs and omelettes, fruits, milk, juice, tea and coffee. As guests note, breakfasts are not very diverse, but you can always find something to your taste.

During the day, tourists can eat both in the hotel restaurant and outside. Moreover, there are a lot of various cafes in the vicinity of Print Kamala Resort and Patong.

Beach vacation

A big plus of the Print Kamala Resort hotel (Phuket island, Thailand), according to travelers, is its proximity to the sea. So, guests can walk to the beach in just a couple of minutes. The sea here is quite clean, the water is clear, calm. The beach is fine sand and well equipped. Here you can not only comfortably sit on a sun lounger, but also if you want to ride a banana or do parasailing. Also, many tourists enjoyed traveling to Patong Beach.
