Where to look for a bank card number and why does the holder need it at all?

Who today does not know where the bank card number is located? Of course, on its front side. In the event that someone doubts, we will clarify: convex figures on the front side, divided into four groups of four - this is it. By the way, they can be either embossed (embossed, as experts say), or simply printed.



It should be noted that the sixteen-digit number of a Visa bank card is characteristic not only for it, but also for the plastic of the MasterCard system. But American Express has a 15-digit mark. If someone has been using banking IPCs (international payment cards) for a long time, then he probably remembers that before Visa was equipped with a thirteen-digit number. But today you can meet such instances infrequently.

Regardless of the number of positions that this attribute is represented, it is unique.

Sometimes the bank manager forgets to tell the client that the bank card number and card account are two big differences. Firstly, the difference is in the number of characters. In the first case there are sixteen, and in the second twenty. What is characteristic, when the plastic is lost, the client receives a new one, which, of course, has a new bank card number. As for the card account, not only is it impossible to lose it, it also remains unchanged as long as it is needed.
