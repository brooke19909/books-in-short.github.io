Hotel Features

Grand Cettia Hotel 4 * (Turkey) is located on the picturesque coast of the Aegean Sea. The presented complex was built 2.5 km from the tourist city of Marmaris, in the Armutalan region. The hotel is surrounded by a pine grove. The air here is clean, helps to cleanse the respiratory tract, normalize metabolic processes in the body.

The hotel is built on the 3rd line from the beach. You will need to drive 1200 m to the sea coast. The nearest airport is located in Dalaman. From it to the hotel you need to drive 96 km.



This is a medium sized tourist complex. In total, 2 buildings were built on the territory. The first has 5 floors, and the second - 7. In total, the hotel has 244 rooms. The hotel was built in 2000. The last restoration was carried out in 2015. The total area is about 8500 m¬≤. Room decoration is new. The territory is well-groomed. There is enough space for walking.
