Hotel Louis Ledra Beach 4 * (Paphos, Cyprus): photos and reviews of tourists

Many people, going on a holiday to Cyprus, decide to stay at Louis Ledra Beach 4 *. This is an officially classified 4-star hotel with excellent customer service and accommodation. It is widely known among avid travelers. It is necessary to tell about it in more detail.





About hotel

Exclusive hotel Louis Ledra Beach 4 * is located on the seafront. It has its own sandy beach. Only five minutes away by car separates it from the very center of Paphos. Everyone knows that this city is one of the most elite resorts in all of Cyprus. In addition, during the Roman conquests, he was the capital of the island. And now it is considered a cultural and historical center. In general, it is not surprising that many people come here.

By the way, not far from Louis Ledra Beach 4 * there is a local airport. It is only 11 kilometers from the hotel.

The building of the complex is visible from afar. It fits perfectly into the overall landscape. In 2009, the overhaul of the hotel began, and in March 2010, its first phase was completed. At that time, managed to update 192 numbers. The situation was taken to a new level - stylish fittings, floors made of natural wood and white marble appeared. The wedding rooms were also repaired. They added a jacuzzi, large king-size beds, themed decor. Then, from December 2010 to March 2011, the remaining 78 apartments were renovated. The corridors with the main restaurant were also changed.

Now, when guests enter the hotel, they see a marbled lobby, luxurious sofas, sparkling decorative elements - this is what makes the whole thing luxurious.





restaurants and bars

On the territory of Louis Ledra Beach 4 * there is an institution called Akamantis. This is an indoor restaurant that serves a varied breakfast daily (buffet service), as well as lunch and dinner. Themed evenings are also held here. They are organized daily. The guests have the opportunity to try dishes of Italian, French, Cypriot, Chinese and Greek cuisines.

The hotel also has a cozy cocktail bar, known as Gazebo. There is a relaxed and cozy atmosphere. It is in this place that you can try a variety of alcoholic drinks and cocktails. Local bartenders know countless recipes. So this place is a favorite among regular guests.

There is also a restaurant-bar, known as Sundowner, on site. It offers light snacks, soft drinks and salads.

By the way, in the summer there is also a beach bar. This is a favorite place for people who can‚Äôt imagine their vacation at sea without refreshments and snacks. But it works exclusively in the season, in cold weather it is closed as unnecessary.

Sports, recreation and entertainment

Louis Ledra Beach 4 * has everything that helps guests brighten up their leisure time. If they do not want to go to the sea (although the vast majority of tourists are sent here for this), then you can do something else. For example, swim in the outdoor pool or sunbathe on the terrace, in the beautiful garden. And in the evenings there are interesting shows and animated evenings.

By the way, the hotel has an indoor, year-round, heated pool. In addition, there are other entertainment and options to brighten up leisure activities. For example, a gym with different simulators and equipment. There is everything you need in order to arrange a full-fledged training.

Lovers of an active lifestyle will love the tennis court, archery and playing French balls, or petanque. If anyone does not know, the essence of the latter is that two teams on the court take turns throwing balls of metal, trying to make them fly to a wooden ball (called a coupon) at the finish line. In general, a traditional Provencal sport. In Louis Ledra Beach Hotel 4 * you can play it.

There is also table tennis, billiard room, beach volleyball, aerobics and water polo. People who enjoy relaxing body and soul can soak in the jacuzzi or sauna.

By the way, there are conditions for children. A separate playground, pool and club - even the smallest guests will not be bored on vacation.





Services

The Louis Ledra Beach Hotel 4 * has everything you need for comfort. Free private parking, high-speed Wi-Fi, a 24-hour front desk, currency exchange and luggage storage. Many services are offered on an individual request, for example, calling a nanny to care for a child. In fact, this is a very necessary specialist, since many people who come with a baby on vacation want at least occasionally to be together. And in this case, the nanny helps out - there is someone to follow the child.

Other guests can take advantage of dry cleaning, ironing and laundry services. In addition, the complex has a fax and photocopying center, a business center and a spacious conference room.

If you need to buy something, but do not want to leave the hotel, you can not worry. After all, the territory has its own minimarket with everything you need. The hotel also provides car rental services, newspaper and food delivery to apartments, as well as a shuttle service. By the way, if you ask at the reception, then guests can pack lunch for the trip. It helps if travelers are going somewhere for the whole day.

There will be no communication problems here. In any case, our compatriots, since in Louis Ledra Beach 4 * (Paphos), most of the staff speak Russian, as well as German, Greek and English.

More about apartments

Hotel Louis Ledra Beach 4 * (Cyprus, Paphos) has a total of 269 apartments in its room stock. Among them - 2-seater and family. It is worth briefly describing the latter.

There are 33 of them at the hotel. They are larger, and inside there are four beds, not two. There are apartments with access to both a balcony and a terrace. Available central air conditioning and heating, coffee maker with kettle, minibar, direct dial telephone. In general, everything is almost the same as in double rooms. There are also 18 studio rooms. A maximum of four people can live there.

The last category is wedding numbers. They are slightly different from all of the above. Their highlight is the presence of a jacuzzi, an interior made in a romantic style, as well as a huge 2-bed bed.

In principle, there are no special differences between the rooms that the Louis Ledra Beach 4 * hotel has. Some guests sometimes write it down. But everything is stylish, neat and comfortable. This is the main thing on vacation.





Standard Apartment

As mentioned above, this 4-star hotel has several categories of rooms. It is worth talking about the most budgetary - namely, the standard. In Louis Ledra Beach 4 * (Paphos) there are most of them.

These rooms are 25 square meters. m. Inside there is a spacious bathroom, equipped with everything necessary, that is, a toilet, a bathtub, free hygiene products, a hairdryer, towels and cleaning products.

In addition, there is a telephone, a plasma TV with satellite channels, a safe, air conditioning, a seating area, a minibar, an electric kettle, a dressing room and a work desk. In general, everything you need, including such trifles as chairs, an armchair, sconces, spotlights, etc. And the guests sleep on two single beds that adjoin each other, making it seem like it's a big king bed -size.

The room itself, by the way, opens onto a furnished balcony. The view can open either on the sea or on the surroundings. The first option, of course, will be more expensive. Such apartments for two cost about forty thousand rubles per week of accommodation (with breakfast). Rooms with a different view are cheaper by about 5 thousand. True, it is worth noting that the cost sometimes varies depending on the season. At the height of summer, apartments can be more expensive.





Guests on service

As already mentioned, Louis Ledra Beach 4 * (Cyprus, Paphos) has its own restaurant and several bars. There is not much official information about these institutions, but guests are told in detail about them.

Many people pay special attention to cooks. They not only prepare delicious and varied dishes, but also are very pleasant in communication - they are happy to talk about Paphos and local attractions. The manager of the main restaurant is also happy. As the guests say, it is immediately clear that the person is in place. He finds an individual approach to each client, remembers who prefers what, and always smiles affably.

In general, tourists say that at the Louis Ledra Beach 4 * hotel (Cyprus) they feed from seven in the morning until midnight, without stopping. For breakfast (they are from 7:00 to 10:00) served omelettes, fried bacon and sausages, Viennese waffles with a variety of jams, chocolate, honey and jam, oatmeal, fruits. Huge selection of teas and juices. Of course, coffee is also served.

For lunch (from 12:30 to 14:30) you can have a meal of traditional local cuisine, enjoy wine or beer. And for dinner (from 18:30 to 21:30) treats of various cuisines are served. Between meals, you can enjoy a drink in the lobby or have a snack in the snack bar near the pool and on the beach. Varieties - more than enough.

True, people who came to rest with children were not satisfied with something. They say that little is suitable for the child, since everything is mostly fried. However, it is difficult to attribute to the disadvantages, as there are shops where you can buy anything.

About booking

The hotel has many rooms, but all of them are very quickly picked up by tourists. Therefore, it is better to apply for a reservation in advance, at least a couple of months before the trip. When applying for it, you will need to provide your credit card details. It accepts the hotel "Master Card", "Maestro", "Visa" and "American Express". By the way, the administration can block on the account the amount for which the reservation was ordered. This is a guarantee that guests really intend to come, and not just reserve an apartment just in case. Arrival, by the way, starts at 14:00, and departure continues until midnight.

After the tourists have made a reservation request, they will be sent a form to register their arrival.

In general, this hotel is perfect for those people who want to spend their holidays in a comfortable environment, with delicious food and cozy rooms. This hotel is not without reason rated four stars. It really has everything that modern tourists need for a good rest.
