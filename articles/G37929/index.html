Aspery Hotel 3 * (Phuket, Thailand): hotel description, service, reviews

What is your vacation? An opportunity to relax and be alone with nature, enjoy the sound of the surf, meet the sunrises and spend sunsets on the coast? If this is true, and you, for example, want to go to a paradise in the middle of winter, you simply cannot find a better option than Thailand. All lovers of the picturesque beauty of the coast should take a closer look at the truly paradise island of Phuket. Vacation packages to Phuket today are very popular, and all because this is a unique opportunity to get a beautiful even tan and enjoy the beauty of pristine nature, to experience real Asian exoticism, ride on elephants and take unforgettable excursions to chic islands. Yes, you have to overcome a long flight, but believe me, it is really worth it. Of course, you have to choose not only the direction of travel, but also the hotel. In such an exotic country as Thailand, problems with hotels certainly will not arise - this is the largest tourist center. Quite often you can find tours with accommodation in a modest hotel Aspery Hotel 3 * (Phuket). Surely you as a tourist would like to learn as much information about him as possible so as not to spoil your vacation in the future.





Features of Thai Hotels

If you are used to relaxing in luxury five-star hotels in Turkey and Egypt, you are probably quite demanding and picky about the hotel, the condition of the rooms and territory. Just want to note that Thailand is an Asian, quite exotic resort. The focus here is not on hotels, but on endless sandy beaches, gorgeous virgin nature and exoticism. Nevertheless, local residents try to make the stay of tourists as comfortable as possible. All the beaches of the island are municipal, so here you will not find hotels with their own beach located on the coast. But you have the opportunity as a tourist to choose a hotel located closer to the coast - so you do not have to spend time on the road. You will find friendly and welcoming staff, Thais are quite attentive to tourists and vacationers, show their hospitality and cordiality to everyone.

About hotel

If you are looking for a cheap hotel for your holiday in Phuket, pay attention to Aspery Hotel 3 * (Phuket). Reviews from real vacationers gave this hotel a good rating. That is why we can safely recommend you this option for your tropical and exotic holidays. It is believed that the hotel is located on the second coastline, that is, 500 meters from the coast. Only 3-5 minutes, and you are on the chic coast with the purest white sand. The airport is only 20 minutes away by public transport. Location can be called one of the strengths of the hotel Aspery Hotel 3 * (Phuket).





Many tourists note that the hotel, despite being close to the coast and the resort infrastructure, is located in a surprisingly calm and quiet place - therefore, an evening vacation here is more pleasant than ever.

The hotel is represented by two buildings, located 50 meters from each other. They are absolutely identical to each other.

Rooms

The hotel is quite large by local standards - it offers accommodation in one of 76 comfortable rooms. The room has a chic balcony with stunning views of the coast and local beauty.

For comfort and microclimate in the rooms of Aspery Hotel 3 * (Thailand, Patong, Phuket) air conditioning is responsible. In a tropical climate, its presence is mandatory in the hotel rooms. Moreover, all tourists and vacationers can rely on satellite TV and wireless Internet. For safety in the room, the safety of valuables, documents and money, the safe is responsible. Unfortunately, there is no refrigerator. But there are dishes, a kettle that will allow you to enjoy a fragrant drink at any time.





The rooms are quite spacious and comfortable. There is not much furniture, but enough for a pleasant stay: one or more beds (depending on the category of the chosen hotel), a wardrobe, bedside tables, a table and chairs on the balcony. There is also a dryer on the balcony where you can dry towels, beach items and some things.

In fact, if you are looking for a good place for your vacation, you will like Aspery Hotel 3 * (Phuket). The description of the rooms confirms the presence in each bathroom. It is small, but there is everything you need to receive water and hygiene procedures.

Service

For a fee, you can count on additional services of the Aspery Hotel 3 * hotel (Phuket). The service here is really impeccable. Friendly staff will tidy up your things: wash and iron them. Rooms are cleaned daily, bed linen and towels are available upon request. In principle, the hotel does not justify its 3 stars, but even surpasses the description of tour operators. Tourists note that it is very pleasant and comfortable to relax here. The staff is friendly and welcoming, after each cleaning, fancy Thai towels appear on the bed - a pleasant enough and uplifting trifle.

The hotel will kindly provide sports equipment and water sports equipment for rent at an additional cost.

Hotel Territory

The territory of the hotel Aspery Hotel 3 * (Phuket) is very tiny, but literally immersed in the greenery of tropical plants. Sometimes you want to be in a cool place. All tourists can sit on sunbeds by the outdoor pool on the fifth floor of the building for free. The pool is cleaned daily, regularly changing the water in it, so the water is crystal clear. Children love to frolic here.





Nutrition

If you choose Aspery Hotel 3 * (Phuket), breakfast will be included in the price of your stay. I must admit that breakfasts here, as in most Phuket hotels, are monotonous: rice, toasts, cheese and meat cuts, fresh vegetables, fruits, flavored coffee, tea and freshly squeezed juices. Despite this, you certainly will not stay hungry. If you have planned an early excursion, it is enough to warn the hotel administration the night before and breakfast will be prepared for you on the road.

You can have lunch and dinner at local restaurants serving national Asian, European, Russian and even Japanese cuisines. The hotel will tell you the most interesting and noteworthy places.





Beach

The beach and the sea are just 500 meters away. If you want to enjoy the sunrises and sunsets, you will definitely like the Aspery Hotel 3 * hotel (Phuket). Reviews of tourists say about the cleanliness of beaches covered with white sand. Sunset at sea is gentle, quite comfortable. In general, this is a great place for a paradise beach holiday. For a fee, a beach chair and umbrella can be rented on the beach. Prices are quite affordable. Do not want to overpay? You can always sit on the sand. And one more thing: be sure to stock up with sunscreens with a high protection factor, creams that protect you from the bites of local jellyfish - there are a huge number of them.





To summarize

So, if for your vacation you decide to buy permits to Phuket, it remains only to decide on the hotel. This option will be ideal for you: impeccable service, spacious and clean rooms, relatively low cost - what else do you need in an Asian paradise? That is why we strongly recommend that you pay particular attention to this option. We hope that we were able to provide the most comprehensive and objective review of this Phuket hotel.
