Where to relax in summer: vacation options

On the eve of the summer season, each of us thinks about a vacation. And the subconscious already draws tender sand underfoot, the gentle sea and the warm sun. To make dreams come true, it is worth considering in advance where to relax in the summer. In our article we want to talk about the best resorts in the country and abroad.

Evpatoria

The Black Sea coast offers a fairly large selection of resorts in the country. If you are thinking about where to relax in the summer with children at sea, then it is worth remembering such a recognized children's resort as Yevpatoriya. From the very beginning, the city was focused on kids, for whom nature itself created amazing conditions for swimming. If pebble beaches prevail throughout the peninsula, then in Yevpatoriya, children will appreciate fine fine sand. In addition, the city has interesting entertainment for children of all ages. An amusement park, a dolphinarium, an aquarium, a children's theater, a dino complex - and this is not the whole list.

Sochi

If the question arises, where to relax in the summer, then the first thing that comes to mind is Sochi. The most famous Russian resort can please you with interesting objects and entertainments at any time of the year. Interactive museums, Olympic venues, a race track, water parks and amusement parks, family rope parks and an extreme skypark - all this can be seen with your own eyes. The resort is of interest not only for adults, but also for children. Sochi can offer a very diverse vacation for tourists. The modern city is equipped with interesting latest facilities. In addition, the close location of the mountains allows you to go hiking to enjoy the waterfalls and gorges, ride quad bikes and horses. Even if you do not really like idle lying on the coast, here you will find a lot of interesting entertainments. And yet, most of the tourists in the summer are attracted by the beaches. The pebble coast will delight you with clean and warm water. Of course, rest in Sochi cannot be called cheap. But if you stay in the villages closest to the city, you can find quite acceptable options.

Perhaps the main attraction of Sochi is the unique subtropical climate. This is simply not anywhere else in Russia. If you are planning a family vacation and wondering where to relax with your children in the summer, Sochi can be an ideal place. Especially considering the number of children's facilities that operates on its territory.

And the special nature of the region deserves close attention of tourists. You can admire the riot of colors and flowering in the very old Southern Culture Park. In a relatively small area, you can see plants literally from different parts of the world.

Anapa

One of those places where you can relax in the summer at sea is the famous Anapa. The resort remains one of the most developed in the country. Anapa boasts the best beaches on the Black Sea. The resort is in demand not only because of the sea. They also come here to improve their health in sanatoriums, hydropathic and mud baths. Or just soak up the spa. Rest in Anapa allows you to feel the mixture of Kuban and Caucasian traditions, cuisines and cultures. The fame of local cuisine and wine has long gone beyond the region. Therefore, each guest of the region seeks to visit the local cafes and fully enjoy the local hospitality.

Gelendzhik

Sochi is not the only place on the Black Sea coast. Where to relax at sea in the summer? It is worth recalling the beautiful Gelendzhik. In addition to the warm sea, there are also sanatoriums in which mud and mineral water are used for treatment. Gelendzhik boasts a variety of beaches, among which there are not only pebble, but also sandy. The resort is located in a very picturesque place. The city has a beautiful promenade, safari park, water park and many other attractions. If you are interested not only in sea vacations, but also other attractions of the city, then you definitely will not get bored on vacation. Gelendzhik is considered the cleanest city in Russia. The city is located in a closed bay, which closes it from almost all sides. For this reason, there is always warm water and almost no waves. Local agencies offer a variety of excursions, jeeping in the mountains, fishing in the sea.

The resort has a beautiful promenade, which is the central place where tourists gather in the evenings. In Gelendzhik, it's good to relax with children. Local beaches seem created for kids. In addition, the resort has a lot of entertainment for children. No less beautiful nature of the surroundings of the resort. Here you can see the Caucasus Mountains, mountain streams and streams. Gelendzhik is one of the most interesting places where a summer vacation will be guaranteed to be rich and interesting.

Kislovodsk

If you are interested in the question of where to relax in the summer in Russia, you should pay attention to the famous resort of Kislovodsk. Caucasian mineral waters are still relevant. Modern hotels and resorts are good for the whole family. They can be rejuvenated by taking procedures and soaking up the spa. Tourists will surely enjoy walks in the Resort Park with the Valley of Roses. Guests of the city can take a cable car ride and also drink mineral water in the very old Narzan Gallery. Natural attractions of the region are no less interesting, including Honey Falls.

And the reason for this is beautiful beaches, unique nature, numerous palaces and other unique attractions. Karelian Isthmus

Where to relax in the summer in Russia, if you do not belong to the category of lovers of sea resorts. In this case, you should study the natural beauties of the country. And there are a lot of them in Russia. In the north of St. Petersburg on the Karelian Peninsula, there are stunningly beautiful places worth seeing. In June, romantics can see white nights between the Gulf of Finland and Lake Ladoga, and in July you can even swim, but with the advent of August, it is time for mushrooms and berries. In Vyborg, tourists will be interested in a medieval castle, Monrepos park and granite caves. In Priozersk you can see the Korelu fortress. The Karelian Isthmus is a great place for outdoor activities. Local guides offer river rafting, rock tours and more.

Baltic Sea

It‚Äôs hard to say where it‚Äôs best to relax in the summer. Much depends on your personal preferences. If you do not like the heat, then you can go to the Kaliningrad region. You will be pleasantly surprised by the well-groomed and clean land. A good rest is guaranteed on the Curonian Spit, which stretches from Zelenograd to Lithuania, ending near the city of Klaipeda. It is worth saying that the spit is a protected area. Here the sandy coast is surrounded by dunes and coniferous forests. Lakes of Russia

If not at sea, then where to relax inexpensively in the summer in Russia? Of course, on beautiful lakes. After all, each in its own way is unique and is surrounded by marvelous nature.

The Caspian Lake, or the sea, is located just between Asia and Europe. In the summer, it manages to warm up to 30 degrees, which means it is quite suitable for swimming. On the coast you can find quite budget places to stay. In the Caspian, you can stay in cities such as Kaspiysk, Makhachkala, Lagan, Astrakhan, Derbent. Also, small villages on the coast are suitable for recreation. Of course, the local infrastructure is not too developed, but this is not the main thing. If you go for the sun, sand and water, then all this you will certainly find here. But your vacation will definitely fall into the category of economical ones. Fans of fishing and hunting will be able to do their favorite thing. And mothers with children, meanwhile, will fully enjoy all the charms of a beach holiday. The Caspian coast is the place where it is possible to have a cheap summer vacation, unlike the Crimea and the Krasnodar Territory.

Baikal

There is no universal advice where it is better to relax in the summer: at sea or lakes. It all depends on your wishes. We suggest that you add beautiful Lake Baikal to the lists of options considered. Of course, it is not in the south, but in Siberia, but still quite acceptable for a beach holiday. By the way, hotels, camp sites and cottages have long been built on the coast.

While resting on Lake Baikal, you can go fishing, go boating, go for berries and mushrooms, try your hand at rafting down the river, ride horses. In general, guests will not be bored. On the coast you can go with the whole family. The clear water of the lake is considered unique, because there are no more such reservoirs in the world.

Lake Brosno

In the north of the Tver region, you can spend time on the shores of Lake Brosno. The reservoir has its own characteristics. The fact is that at a depth of forty meters there is a process of decay of the forest in it, which is why hydrogen sulfide is actively produced, which goes to the surface of the water. If you do not know about this, then you will certainly be surprised by the noise from the bursting bubbles. Locals tell an old legend that a dragon lives in the lake. It is he who makes noise and scares uninvited guests. Of course, the legend is just a beautiful fairy tale that attracts curious tourists here.

Numerous camp sites and recreation centers work on the shore of the reservoir. In addition, you can always settle in the private sector. But it is worth remembering that the swimming season in the pond lasts only two months. The lake is surrounded by forests, which gives tourists the opportunity to collect berries and mushrooms, ride horses along the trails. Lake Brosno is another place where it is fun and interesting to relax in the summer, especially if you like natural beauty.

Extreme vacation

Among the tourists there are many people who prefer outdoor activities, rather than idle lying on the beach. Such travelers may be advised to go rafting or mountain climbing. There are many suitable places for one and the second sport in Russia. Extreme sportsmen prefer rafting along the fast rivers of Karelia, hiking in the mountain region of Khamar-Daban, climbing the Caucasus mountains, diving in the amazing caves of the Urals and much more.

Rafting has been popular for quite some time. Among his fans there are people of completely different levels of training and even beginners. For each category of tourists their own routes are selected. As a rule, for rafting, rivers are sent to the Urals, Altai, Karelia, the Caucasus or Eastern Siberia.

The routes are designed in such a way that there are numerous camp sites on the way. In between rafting, tourists can visit excursions, see interesting places and go fishing. Such tours are good for active people.

Holidays abroad

If we sorted out domestic resorts a little, then where to relax in the summer abroad? The choice of foreign resorts is quite large.

You can, for example, go to Burgas. The mild climate and shallow sea, beautiful sandy beaches stretching for kilometers - all this you will find in the resort. It is very comfortable to relax with children. The sea seems to be created specifically for kids. In addition, the city has a zoo for young tourists, attractions, playgrounds and more. And in August you can become a member of the sand sculpture festival. In general, in Bulgaria it is good to relax with the whole family, since there are all the necessary conditions and entertainment for representatives of all ages.

In the summer, you can also go to Antalya. The beautiful coast of the resort and historical attractions will pleasantly complement your vacation if you want to get to know the history and culture of the region.

In the summer, southern Spain is no less attractive for tourists. One of the popular resorts is Malaga. However, the beautiful coast and excellent beaches are not all that Andalusia can boast of. Here, everywhere, one can observe a mixture of cultures and eras. Therefore, in Malaga, you should definitely see the ancient Roman theater, the Arab fortress, houses in the Baroque style and modern. And of course, you need to visit Seville and Granada. The beaches of the resort are simply amazing. The coast will appeal to those who like to swim. The depth here begins very quickly.

Another resort in Spain is no less attractive - Alicante. It is especially good to relax here in July. If your vacation falls on June, do not rush to be upset. Of course, in most resorts at this time it is too early to swim. But this does not apply to Sicily.
