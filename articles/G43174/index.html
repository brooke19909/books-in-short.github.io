Magnitogorsk-Moscow: travel options on the route

The distance from Magnitogorsk to Moscow is 1700 kilometers. It can be quickly flown by plane, traveled by train in 35 hours, overcome by car or bus in transit through Ufa. Since Magnitogorsk is located on the border of Europe and Asia, then during the trip you will have to cross almost the entire European part of Russia.

Flight flight route

There is an airport in the city, so it is not surprising that the planes of different airlines fly from Magnitogorsk to Moscow:

  • Aeroflot.
  • "North wind".
  • Pegas Fly.

The flight takes 2.5 hours, aircraft land at Sheremetyevo. The price of an economy class ticket starts at 4,300 rubles. Departure time can be different: from 6 am to 9 pm.

Conversely, from Moscow to Magnitogorsk, flights are operated by the same airlines (from Sheremetyev), as well as Ural Airlines (from Domodedovo). A choice of night, morning and afternoon departures.

Rail ride

From Magnitogorsk to Moscow there are only direct trailers with poisoning at 21:45. The train is in transit 35 hours. The ticket price depends on the type of car, and there are only two of them:

  • Reserved seat - from 2800 rubles.
  • Kupeyny - from 5500 rubles.

The trailed car departs from the capital from the Kazan station at 21:22 and arrives in Magnitogorsk 32 hours later at 07:30 in the morning.

It is much more interesting to get to Moscow with a change in Ufa.

To the capital of Bashkiria from Magnitogorsk, the train also leaves at 21:45 and arrives there after 9 hours. There are only two types of cars in it: reserved seats and compartment. Tickets cost, respectively, from 680 and 1200 rubles.

From Ufa to the capital, four trains depart with the following schedule:

  • 01:47. The composition of the local Ufa formation. Before his departure, you will be able to walk around the city for a day: there are enough attractions.
  • 07:50. Passing train from Chelyabinsk. It has seating cars, a ticket to Moscow in them can cost about 1400 rubles.
  • 08:53. Another composition from Chelyabinsk - branded and more expensive. It has sleeping cars. From 11 thousand rubles is a ticket to the capital.
  • 14:41. Passing train from Karaganda, formed by the railways of Kazakhstan. This affects the product range of the conductor.

All of them arrive at the Kazan station. From 1600 rubles a ticket costs in a reserved seat car and from 3000 in a compartment.

The trains depart from Moscow to Ufa according to the following schedule:

  • 12:26.
  • 3:30 p.m.
  • 21:22.
  • 22:50.

Ride from 22 to 30 hours.

When planning a trip, it is worth considering that the time difference in Magnitogorsk and Moscow is two hours.

Bus ride

There are no direct buses from Magnitogorsk to Moscow, so you need to go with a change in Ufa. Before it, a ticket costs about 800 rubles. There are 10 flights: from 3 nights to 10 pm. The trip takes 4.5 hours.

Buses also run from Ufa to Moscow, despite the long distance and the availability of trains. They depart at 10 and 11 in the morning and arrive in the capital after 20 hours, that is, at 4 and 5 in the morning, at the bus station near the VDNKh metro station or to the Kotelniki metro station. A ticket costs 2200 rubles, that is, more expensive than a seated carriage in a train and the cheapest options for a reserved seat. But you need to ride while sitting and long enough - this should also be taken into account.
