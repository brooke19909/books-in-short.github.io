Short description

In 2008, the Mansion on Teatralnaya opened its doors for tourists for the first time. Kazan is a modern developing city. To comply with the general trend, in 2013 the hotel was renovated. The institution provides guests with a good transport interchange, comfortable living conditions, as well as aesthetic pleasure from architecture and interior decoration.

In the very heart of the city is the Mansion on Teatralnaya Hotel. Kazan, st. Teatralnaya, 3 - address of this institution. It is a 40 minute drive from the airport. In the immediate vicinity of the hotel are the following attractions:

How to get there

There are several ways to get to the Mansion on Teatralnaya Hotel. Kazan is a big city where you can get lost. To prevent this, use the recommendations of the hotel administration:

  • If you arrived in Kazan by plane, you need to take an aeroexpress train to the railway station. Next, you can get to the University stop by buses No. 10, 30 or 63.
  • From the river port you also need to get to the University stop on Pushkin Street. Bus No. 54 or trolleybuses No. 3 or No. 5 will help you with this.
  • From the bus station located on Devyatayev Street, you can be reached by bus number 54. Also trolleybuses No. 3 and No. 5 run along this route.
  • If you arrive at the North Railway Station, first of all, you need to get to the Ploshchad Svoboda stop by bus number 89. Another option is to get from the Severny Vokzal metro station to the Tukaya Square station.
  • Bus No. 22 runs from the Yuzhny bus station to the Freedom Square stop.
