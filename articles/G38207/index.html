Cyprus, Seagull Hotel Apts 3 *. Tourists reviews

Holidays in Cyprus are always wonderful. The mild climate, white beaches and crystal waves are a fabulous island where Aphrodite emerged from the foam of the sea.

In Cyprus, there are many hotels for every taste in both the Greek and Turkish sectors. This article provides information about the reviews of tourists who visited the Seagull Hotel Apts 3 - a small hotel in the resort town of Protaras in the Turkish part of the island.

Protaras

In ancient times, Protaras was an ancient Greek polis (from which small ruins were preserved). In 1974, this part of Cyprus was occupied by the Turks, and the active development of the resort industry began in Protaras. Now dozens of hotel complexes, restaurants and entertainment centers have been built here.

The best beaches of Cyprus are located in Protaras, for example, the world famous Fig Tree Bay ( Fig Trees), not far from which Seagull Hotel Apts 3 * is located.

Protaras has a reputation as a resort for a relaxing and quiet holiday. In search of more active entertainment, you can go to the neighboring village of Ayia Napa (6 km), where a youth audience gathers.

Among the local attractions, one should mention the medieval Orthodox church dedicated to Elijah the Prophet (14th century) and built on a hilltop with amazing views of the sea coast.

Climate and beaches

The climate in Protaras is subtropical (as on the whole island) - dry, hot summers (25-40 degrees), warm spring and autumn (30 Celsius), not at all cold winters (15 warm). For Russian tourists accustomed to the cold, this means that you can relax here all year round - it is warm in any season.

The beaches stretch almost continuous sand strip and are the best in Cyprus. There are no high waves, the sand is fine and clean, the entrance to the sea is shallow, the water is warm and clear (and the most beautiful shades of blue and emerald color) - a paradise for families with children.





From the Seagull Hotel Apts 3 * to the municipal beach is only 2 minutes walk. This is a calm bay where you can not only swim, but also engage in diving, as well as other water sports.

There are no crowds on the beaches, you can always find a free place. The most well-equipped beach is in Ayia Napa (a neighboring resort). An excellent beach in the Bay of Figs near the Chaika Hotel (you can take a bus for one and a half euros). One of the most beautiful is Konnos Bay, which you have to go down along a magnificent path (you can walk to it from Seagull Hotel Apts, but with a child the transition will be difficult).

Near Cape Greco there is also a nice beach and Pirate Caves, where tourists have fun jumping from a cliff.

Very popular beaches in the bay of Pernera (200 meters from the "Seagull"), they are often called chamber for privacy, comfort and tranquility.

Tourists who lived in Seagull Hotel Apts 3 * report that on any beaches for a sunbed and umbrella you need to pay 5-7 euros (depending on the particular beach), and in local stores you can buy a beach umbrella for 10-11 euros.

Hotel

Seagull Hotel Apts 3 * (Cyprus) is a hotel with "home" apartments, that is, with a kitchen corner in each room, where you can cook your own meals and not pay for meals.





The hotel has excellent landscaped pools (adult and for kids), surrounded by palm trees, and a children's playground, a green well-tended garden, a sun terrace and free parking.

The hotel has an elevator (convenient for tourists with children and strollers), and free internet access is available in the lobby.

The Seagull Hotel Apts 3 * (Cyprus) provides the following services and entertainment:

  • Billiards.
  • Table tennis.
  • Equipment for water sports.
  • Bicycle, buggy and car rental.
  • Room for games.
  • Transfer to the airport (for a fee).
  • 24 hour registration.
  • Exchange office.
  • Luggage storage.
  • Laundry and dry cleaning.

On request, a baby cot can be placed in the room (no more than one). Parents do not pay for one child under 2 years of age. It is forbidden to bring animals to Seagull Hotel Apts 3 * (Protaras).

Rooms

The Chaika Hotel has 59 rooms, including a studio (one large room combined with a kitchen) and two-room apartments (bedroom and kitchen-living room).





The rooms do not have a TV, but there is a radio, a bathroom with a bathtub (soap, shampoo, gel, toilet paper are provided), a balcony, air conditioning, a hairdryer and a kitchenette with a set of dishes and household appliances.

There is no Internet in the rooms (only in the lobby). For an additional fee, you can take a safe (10 euros per week), but it is very small, only for documents, and the tablet, for example, does not fit.

Tourists living in Seagull Hotel Apts 3 * (Protaras) note that there are no insects in the hotel, the rooms are spacious, but there is nowhere to hang wet bathing suits and towels - there is no rope or clothes rack on the balcony.

Air conditioners are often installed directly opposite the bed, so some tourists moved their beds (and the cleaners put them back daily), while others changed the head and slept with their feet to the air conditioner.

All balconies of the studio apartments face Protaras Street or the parking lot (almost always empty), with views of the mountains and trees.

The furniture is upholstered with dermantine, water is pumped into the toilet with a special lever.

External irritants

Annoying noise is noted by almost all tourists who lived in Seagull Hotel Apts 3 *. The reviews are full of reports of constant music from two bars - outside on the street and inside near the pool.

In all rooms below 4 floors, many tourists could not fall asleep either at night (music plays until 2 o‚Äôclock) or during the day (noise starts from 11 a.m.). Closed windows and balconies and included air conditioning did not help.

For older people and families with children, the ability to get enough sleep is very important, so you should pay attention to this feature when choosing a hotel.

On the other hand, many tourists believe that music does not interfere very much - with the windows closed and their headphones closed, they slept quietly in their apartments. In addition, there are people who go to bed late.

Also, one should not forget that loud music in bars is characteristic not only for the Chaika Hotel, but also for most hotels in many popular resorts. Therefore, replacing a place of residence does not always help to solve the problem.

And finally, the proximity to the bar gives one advantage - a good Wi-Fi signal and the ability to calmly watch movies on the Internet in your room (in headphones), not paying attention to external irritants.

Service

The staff of the Protaras Seagull Hotel Apts 3 hotel complex is always attentive and responsive, but there are no Russian-speaking staff at the reception, although there are maids who speak Russian, and you can always ask them to help in communicating with the administrators.

The rooms are thoroughly cleaned daily (tips - 1 euro - usually left on the table). Very clean in the pool and on the playground, the territory is well looked after.

If vacationers arrived before 14.00 (when check-in begins), then they can leave their luggage in the luggage room and go to the beach.

At the reception you can ask for an iron or an adapter to charge the phone - employees will always meet you (you need to pay a deposit, which is returned upon departure).

Dining at the hotel

The price includes only a continental breakfast - the same set of products every day:

  • tea, coffee, milk, juice (2 types, diluted concentrate);
  • yogurt with jam (4 types);
  • croutons, toasts, margarine (no butter);
  • fried eggs, fried potatoes, sausages;
  • omelet (once a week);
  • canned beans, olives, fresh tomatoes and cucumbers;
  • bread, cereal (muesli 4 types);
  • processed cheese and inexpensive sausage;
  • watermelon.

It should be remembered that there is no baking, fruit, butter, milk porridge - therefore, many people (especially with children) prefer not to take breakfast at all, but to cook in the room.

Self catering

Near the hotel there are many grocery stores, as well as taverns and restaurants where you can order wonderful Cypriot dishes (the portions are very large, so you can take for two), as well as soups for children, seafood, etc.

For example, a hot meat dish with a side dish and a salad of fresh vegetables costs 6-10 euros (in institutions with live music - 11-14 euros). For regular customers who come for the second time, they give a 10% discount.

In restaurants you can have a delicious dinner for 30-40 euros for two (with alcohol).

According to reviews, the nearby stores have a very limited range of products and high prices, so you need to go to a large supermarket - it's cheaper there.

Interestingly, everywhere in Cyprus, coffee is not very good, even in cafes. Tea Lipton sachets costs 4 euros. But pizza is much better than in Russia.
