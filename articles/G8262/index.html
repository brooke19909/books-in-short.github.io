Royal Wings Hotel, Turkey: description and reviews of tourists

Why do many people prefer holidays in Turkey? Yes, because a good Turkish hotel offers a wonderful family vacation, which will be interesting to absolutely all family members. We want to talk about one of these institutions in our article. The Royal Wings Hotel (Turkey) is a five-star complex on the coast of Antalya , offering excellent food and good service.

A bit about the hotel

The Royal Wings Hotel (Turkey) was built in 2005, so it can be attributed to relatively young institutions. It has a very vast territory, about 72,000 square meters. The complex is located in the village of Lana, 15 kilometers from the airport and 17 kilometers from Antalya. Such close proximity to the airport makes it possible to quickly reach the hotel after the flight and not languish on the bus.



The Royal Wings Hotel (Turkey) was built right on the coast and is a ten-story building with a rather interesting design. The oval building has open corridors between floors overlooking the lobby and glass exterior walls. The building is built in such a way that the maximum number of rooms (60%) has a view of the coast. On the vast territory of the complex there is a lot of entertainment for children and adults. The spacious territory of the hotel is planted with greenery, so it's nice to walk and enjoy the beautiful views.

The hotel offers its guests all-inclusive meals. A popular concept is the ability to visit bars and restaurants at any time of the day without any restrictions. Complex infrastructure

The description of the Royal Wings Hotel (Turkey) would be incomplete if not to mention its sufficiently developed infrastructure. In fact, the hotel complex is a mini-city in which there is everything necessary for vacationers, from food to entertainment. It is for the good organization of leisure that our holidaymakers appreciate Turkish resorts.

The Royal Wings Hotel (Turkey), as we have already mentioned, has a fairly large territory, which is well equipped and thought out to the smallest detail. The reception desk serves guests around the clock. On the territory there is parking for the transport of guests, as well as Fantastic Park - a large water park with slides for adults and 3 for children. In addition, hotel guests have the opportunity to visit the amusement park, which has all kinds of swings and even a ferris wheel.



The hotel has its own spa, dry cleaning and laundry services. Currency exchange and car rental are available in the lobby. The hotel has several shops that sell all kinds of little things for tourists. The business center of the complex has three large rooms for business events.

Children's services

The Royal Wings Hotel (Turkey) is focused on family vacations, so a significant part of the infrastructure is designed specifically for young guests of the resort.

The complex has a good kids club that accepts children from four to twelve years old. Here, the animators are organizing leisure activities for kids. The hotel also has a children's playground and a pool with slides for children.

In the main restaurant, on request, children's chairs are provided for feeding. The range of establishments has a special children's menu. If necessary, babysitting services can be ordered at the reception.

Children's events are organized not only in the daytime, but also in the evening.

Sports and Entertainment

Royal Wings (Turkey) offers a fairly wide range of entertainment for its guests, so that the rest becomes not only comfortable, but also interesting. Tourists have the opportunity to visit the spa, visit the solarium, Turkish bath and indoor pool. It offers a good water park with all kinds of attractions. In season, the hotel employs a group of animators of thirty people, most of them speak Russian, so entertainment is understandable to our tourists. During the day, activites offer to do water aerobics, archery, play basketball and volleyball, billiards, table tennis and darts.



The hotel has tennis courts and its own bowling club. And on the coast, vacationers have the opportunity to do water sports, ride a banana scooter, do parasailing.

In the evenings, an evening entertainment show is organized for tourists, artists are invited several times a week.

Beach vacation

The Royal Wings Hotel (Turkey, Antalya) has its own large beach. The complex is built on the coast, from the hotel to the sea you need to walk a couple of minutes. The wide beach has a sand cover. There are many canopies, umbrellas and sunbeds on the coast. There is always enough space for all tourists.

A bit about the resort

Lara is considered a popular and prestigious resort of Turkey on the Mediterranean coast. The village is located near Antalya. Lara hotel complexes mainly have a five-star status, and therefore belong to respectable establishments. In general, the resort area is built along the coast in three lines, respectively, the cost of living on the second and third lines is lower than on the first. Therefore, if you wish, you can find a more budget hotel.

A huge advantage of the resort is its proximity to the airport and Antalya itself. It is not difficult to get to Lara, especially since the mass of charters arrives at the international airport every day not only from Moscow, but also from other cities of Russia. From any hotel to Antalya and Lara can be reached by dolmushes and buses. Transport links are well established here.



The resort's beach line stretches along the coast for twelve kilometers. Sand cover made the resort so popular. International recognition of Lara‚Äôs status is confirmed by the regularly awarded Blue Flag. So, the coast is clean and safe for relaxation. The resort has free city beaches and private hotel ownership. All are equipped with awnings and sun loungers.

The resort is characterized by a Mediterranean climate with dry summers and rainy, mild winters. July is considered the hottest month, at this time the air temperature rises to an average of + 29 degrees. In Lara, the humidity is quite high, so the heat is a little harder than in other regions.

Attractions Lara

On the east coast of Lara, a river flows into the sea, forming the Lower Duden Waterfall. A large and noisy stream of water flows into the Mediterranean Sea from a very high cliff. You can admire the stunning beauty for hours. A little upstream in the park area, vacationers can see a second waterfall of a more modest size called Upper Duden. Next to it there is a cafe, sitting in which you can relax and have a good time under the sound of water.

Not so long ago, a new tradition appeared at the resort. An international sand sculpture contest was held here annually. Material for the competition is brought from different parts of the country. An interesting sight attracts many tourists.



Immediately after the Lara Beach, a park zone begins, in which you can comfortably walk in the shade of trees in hot weather. The highlight of the resort is a restaurant on the very top of the Tyuntekel hill, which is located at an altitude of 800 meters, on a rotating platform. Since the institution rotates all the time, then resting in it, you can admire the sea and mountains in turn.

The advantage of Lara is, of course, her location. Antalya is just a stone's throw away, which means that all its attractions and attractions are quite accessible to vacationers from Lara. Aqualand is a famous water park, and the floating night club Meds are places that are worth a visit.
