How to knit a pullover with knitting needles for women (with patterns)?

Any woman, regardless of age, wants to attract the attention of others. One of the details that allows you to stand out from the rest is clothing. The modern fabric industry offers a wide selection of outfits, but even the most attractive of them can sometimes be repeated on another person. Therefore, many ladies begin to get involved in needlework and create exclusive models of things, focusing on individual taste. It would not be difficult for such craftsmen to knit a pullover with knitting needles for women (with patterns inspired by modern fashion trends). Literally two or three evenings will be enough to create a unique thing that you can‚Äôt buy in any store.

White classic mesh

To get a beautiful stylish thing, it is not necessary to resort to complex drawings and a riot of colors. White color and a straightforward mesh pattern in the skilled hands of a skilled craftswoman will yield a beautiful openwork pullover with knitting needles for women, knitting patterns of which are easy for even a beginner to understand.





For work you will need:

  • 9 skeins of wool yarn (175 m and 56 grams each);
  • 3 skeins of mohair (855 m and 100 grams each);
  • knitting needles number 5;
  • circular knitting needles No. 5 (length 100 cm);
  • holders for loops or pins;
  • special markers.

Particular attention should be paid to the choice of yarn. Wool is often prickly and can cause discomfort when wearing a knitted item. Therefore, the skein should be attached to the cheek and rub a little about it. In the absence of irritation, such yarn can be safely taken to work and knit the original pullover with knitting needles for women (with or without patterns). It should be noted that the product in question is knitted from a double thread.

Missing accessories and tools for work can be purchased at any needlework store.

Operating procedure

All calculations are given for size M: product length - 69.5 cm; chest circumference - 101.5 cm; sleeve length - 39.5 cm. Experienced needlewomen will not be difficult to choose the size for themselves. To do this, in order to determine the density of the model, a small sample is knitted. When using the recommended yarn, a 10 x 10 cm square will consist of 24 rows of 20 loops with an openwork pattern made in 2 threads on knitting needles 5. Do not neglect this trick, because it is easier to dissolve a small square than one finished part or the entire pullover.
In order not to be mistaken with the size of the model, it is important to try on each related part for yourself. Manual work is good because the thing can always be corrected and adjusted according to the figure. You should also correlate all the details to each other so that the back, for example, does not turn out more forward.

The difference in knitting the rest of the details

The front fits in the same way as the back. The difference is only in the deeper neckline, for which it is necessary at a height of 50 cm (after 26 decay for raglan) with a marker to mark 6 loops in the center.

Remove the marked loops on the holder or large pin. Next, perform each shoulder separately, adding another thread and a knitting needle. On the pin moves 3 times for 3 p., 2 p. 2 p. and 2 p. 1 p. Do this in sequence, previously knitting each loop. Loops do not need to be closed. The result is a round neckline. The remaining 36 p. Also do not close and re-pin.

To finish the pullover with knitting needles for women (with patterns in accordance), it remains to tie the sleeves. To do this, dial 50 p. With a double thread, start knitting from the 2nd row of the openwork pattern and continue it to 44.5 cm, adding 1 p. In each 6th p. (so 17 times). Thus, the bevel of the sleeve is made. The result is 80 loops. Then begin the formation of the raglan, as in the back, until it has 14 loops on the needles. Transfer sleeves to circular knitting needles, on either side of the back.

Assembly

At the last stage, it remains only to collect all the details together. First, transfer in front on circular knitting needles to the back and sleeves and close all raglan seams. This is usually done with a knit stitch. Then close all rows with facial loops on circular knitting needles. And, of course, make the side seams with the same knitted seam.

It is worth noting that after assembly, any thing must be washed, dried on a towel and steamed. So it will look better when finished. After the effort spent, the output will be the original pullover (knitting) for women. With and without schemes, you can knit interesting things for yourself and your family, each of which will be a small masterpiece.





Extra bonus

A pleasant addition to the finished pullover can be a scarf knitted with a regular elastic band. To get started, you need to dial 55 p. On the knitting needles and knit, alternating the wrong and front loops. Thus, knit 142 cm (the length can be changed as you wish) and close the loops. Despite the simplicity of knitting (only 1 x 1 elastic is used), the scarf will turn out to be elegant and, most importantly, warm. It can be worn both with a pullover and separately.
