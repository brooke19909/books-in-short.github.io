Roses from corrugated paper - we create unusual bouquets with our own hands

It is so easy to create roses from corrugated paper with your own hands that a child can also cope with this task. Available materials, free time and a little imagination - that’s all it takes to recreate natural floral perfection.



Materials and Tools

To create roses from corrugated paper with your own hands, a beginner should purchase special materials and tools in advance.

The first is corrugated paper. As noted by the masters of this art, it is this material that allows you to naturally recreate a masterpiece of nature. Just do not experiment with color: it is best to give your preference to natural tones: green, red, yellow and white palette.

Secondly, tools. These are stencils, teip tape, scissors, pencil, glue in the form of a pencil or a gun, wire.

The above is a minimal set that can be supplemented depending on which project is being implemented.



Unusual bouquet of roses from corrugated paper

Such a bouquet of roses can be made even by a beginner. To do this, you will need a tape roll of corrugated paper about 10 centimeters wide, a medium-sized foam ball , an adhesive gun and scissors.

The first thing to do is to cut the blanks from corrugated tape 60 centimeters long. First, prepare 10 pieces. Next, each workpiece is bent one third wide and twisted so that the bent edge is at the bottom of the flower.

Once all the roses have been created, they are mounted on the ball with a glue gun. This must be done so that between the colors the surface of the ball is not visible.

By the way, such bouquets can be used not only as a gift, but also as an element that gives the interior a gentle romantic style. To create the latter, foam balls should be taken with a small diameter, for example, 6-8 centimeters.

Bouquet for the wedding

Making roses from corrugated paper with your own hands and collecting a wedding bouquet from them is somewhat more difficult.

He will need soft pink and dark green corrugated paper, golden foil, round chocolates, golden metallized thread, scissors, green wire, tulle and a flower basket.



The roses themselves from corrugated paper are step by step performed as follows. To start, we form the pestle. To do this, each candy should be wrapped in foil and tightened with a thread.



Next, pink paper needs to cut thin petals. For each bud, two blanks are required. They are folded together with a slight shift and a pestle is wrapped in them, pulled together with a thread.

After that, a green cup is cut out of green paper. Make it simple: just cut out the “crown” from the green rectangle.

The bud is wrapped with a cup blank, fixed with glue, again tightened with a thread and the ends are cut diagonally.



Next, cut a piece that will become a stem from the wire, put a bud on it and gently paste it over with green corrugated paper.



The flower is ready.

But in order to give a more romantic and delicate look, each bud is wrapped with milky white tulle, as is done with real roses.

The number of such buds depends only on the invented design of a wedding basket of roses. To fix the received flowers in a basket is quite simple. To do this, the stems are planted on a foam or floral sponge.

As you can see, making roses from corrugated paper with your own hands is quite simple. The main thing is to master the technique of performance and show imagination.
