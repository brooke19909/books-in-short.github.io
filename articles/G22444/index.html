Where is located

165 km from Nizhny Novgorod there is a camp site "Sunflowers". It is safe to say that air and nature are ecologically clean. There are many places for fishing and hunting lovers that are definitely worth a visit.

The hostel keeps its doors open 365 days a year and is ready to receive the next guest at any time. The size of the camp site can accommodate up to 110 people.

How to get there

To get to "Podsolnuhov", it is best to use a car that you need to move along the highway N. Novgorod - Kirov. Route: N. Novgorod - the village of Vysokovka, with the passage of three settlements (Bokovaya, Kalinikha, Bogorodskoye). In Vysokovka the necessary camp site is located.

Where to live

There are several options for accommodating guests at the base: log houses of varying degrees of comfort, and rooms in the building (for avid urbanists).

Standard house

The house has one room, with toilet and cold water. There is a veranda. The room has a sofa, 3 beds (1.5-sleeping), electric kettle. It is possible to equip an extra bed. There is no heating in the house, so in the cold season, an electric fireplace is added to the list of things. There are 4 such houses.

The remaining 13 houses have a similar situation, but they do not have a sofa, an electric kettle and the possibility of additional equipment.





Superior houses

One-room houses with hot water and a shower fall into this category. There is also a veranda. The house consists of one room, which has 4 beds (1.5-sleeping), electric fireplace, TV, electric kettle. As a heating system, a wood stove is used. Houses of this category 3.

In one of the three superior houses, an extra bed can be allocated, so a sofa is added to the interior of the house.





Luxury houses

They boast two rooms, a toilet, hot and cold water, and a shower. Consist of two rooms, which accommodate 3 beds (1.5-sleeping). There is a veranda. It is possible to install an extra bed. In the house there is a refrigerator, electric kettle, TV. You can heat in the cold season with a wood stove and electric fireplace. There are 6 such houses.

There is also a one-room luxury house in which the situation is identical. There are 2 such houses.





Rooms in the building

1-2-room rooms with a number of beds from 2 to 4 are provided. The furnishings in the rooms vary from Spartan (only a double bed) to a suite with two rooms and necessary amenities (toilet, shower, hot water, desk, TV and Electric kettle).

Fishing and hunting

Near the camp site there are many small lakes and old ladies, which in the spring are poured into a single water system. There is also the opportunity to fish well in the nearby Vetluga. Anglers can fish not only from the coast, but also on the water. There is a boat rental for this. It is proposed to fish at the base according to its seasonality: from May to March.

In May, a valuable asp is caught, a small somnock can be caught.

In June - September, fishing is carried out by spinning, but, at the request of guests, fishing for a float or bottom tackle can be arranged. You can fish bersh, ide, perch, pike perch, pike.

In November - March fishing is mainly carried out on mormyshka. Pike is caught on the flags. But the most popular fish in the dead of winter is ruff.

Extreme amusement park

For those who want to test their nervous system and neighbor for a fortress, the hostel offers the following sports:

1. Climbing wall - an artificially created structure, the purpose of which is climbing. Based on the type of climbing wall and its structure, it can fully or partially imitate the relief of the rock.

2. Tarzanka - a sports and game structure of handicraft production. As a rule, it consists of a crossbeam and a strong rope or rope, which is tied to a tree branch by the principle of a swing. In most cases, they are placed above the water to sway and dive.

3. Team building programs - are active events that are designed to rally the team and solve team problems.

Nizhny Novgorod Region, "Sunflowers": prices for services

Prices for accommodation at the base vary depending on the day of the week. On weekdays, the room rate is standard (from 1000 rubles per day), and on weekends the price increases by 20-30%, based on the category of the room. This applies to all rooms.

Weekdays at the camp site begin at two in the afternoon of Sunday, and last until twelve in the afternoon of Friday. Weekends - from 14:01 Friday and, accordingly, until 12:00 Sunday.

If guests live on the basis of more than 5 days, then the cost of their room is calculated at prices on weekdays. When visiting a camp site with a company of more than 10 people, you can get a discount of 5%.

For those living in summer houses there is an opportunity to take a shower in the main building for free.

Three meals a day will cost 800 rubles per adult, children aged 5-12 have a 40% discount, food for them will cost 480 rubles.

Pets are allowed at a surcharge of 200 rubles. per day.
