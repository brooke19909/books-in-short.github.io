Myrtle Beach (South Carolina): description, attractions, reviews

When traveling around the United States, you should definitely visit Myrtle Beach in South Carolina. It will pleasantly surprise the hospitality of local residents, impressive beauties and a wide range of entertainment for tourists of all ages.

general description

The state received its name from the province of the same name. At first it was a British colony in North America, which at the beginning of the XVIII century was divided into two parts: North and South Carolina (USA). The name of the province itself comes from the name of King Charles the First. Many will think that the name is not at all in tune with Carolina, but in Latin Karl is pronounced like Carolus.

Myrtle Beach (South Carolina) is located on the Atlantic coast. Due to the climate, beauty and many entertainment venues, tourists come here not only from all around, but also from other cities. On the coast of the Atlantic, this is one of the main resorts, although only 30 thousand people live here. The service is at a high level, a well-trained friendly staff is always ready to help solve any arising problem.

Myrtle Beach Sidewalk

It was opened less than ten years ago, and it immediately became one of the favorite places for tourists and residents of the city. The sidewalk is a two-kilometer wooden flooring located along the Myrtle Beach coastline. A mild warm climate and well-thought-out infrastructure allows you to get ample opportunities for an excellent holiday.

Comfortable beaches are organized for tourists, where you can not only relax, but also engage in various water sports, including sailing. Numerous souvenir shops offer a huge selection of unusual products. Among the demanded entertainments, cultural and mass musical events are distinguished. One of the most spectacular is the launch of kites. The city's best restaurants and modern outdoor bars offer a wide range of dishes and drinks. Myrtle Beach's sidewalk is ideal for romantic strolls with stunning views of the Atlantic Ocean.

State park

It is located on the coast of the Atlantic Ocean and includes a small part of the state. Myrtle Beach Park (South Carolina) was founded in 1935, its territory stretches over 120 hectares. The subtropical climate offers vacationers a lot of opportunities for outdoor activities.

A great location in the heart of the Grand Strand makes the park one of the most visited beaches on the South Carolina coast. There is a zone for comfortable camping, picnic tables, a pier for those who wish to go fishing. For people who prefer active sports, there are places for surfing, diving under the guidance of professional instructors, there is also the opportunity to organize a sailing trip.

During tourist walks, visitors can get acquainted with the rich world of flora and fauna represented in this region. Most visitors come to Myrtle Beach with a visit to the city park. An organized sea excursion allows you to enjoy picturesque views and a pleasant ocean breeze.

Family kingdom

One of the most popular entertainment spots in Myrtle Beach (SC) is the Family Kingdom theme park. This is the only such attraction in the city. The park was opened in 1966 and immediately became popular among visiting tourists and local youth. The entire territory of the entertainment complex occupies a little more than four hectares.

Family Kingdom is an ideal place for a family pastime. A wide range of entertainment is represented by many exciting games, vibrant sights. Over 40 attractions for visitors of all ages - from the oldest generation to the youngest children. The huge ferris wheel enjoys special popularity among the guests of the park, it offers unique views of the coastal zone and the expanses of the Atlantic Ocean.

In addition, a 4D cinema has been built in the park, showing films for adults and children, a large aquarium, as well as a dolphinarium, which offers a program with the participation of marine life for watching the show. Costume parties, music festivals and other events are held on the beach adjacent to the park. For visitors, there is a pavilion for picnics for 100 seats, bars and restaurants of different cuisines of the world.

Myrtle Waves Waterpark

It is the largest in the United States and one of the largest in the world. Myrtle Waves covers over 20 hectares. The water park amazes with its size and original design concept in the Caribbean style. The model consists of models of plants and wild animals, aquariums with exotic species of marine inhabitants, an island with blue lagoons.

In the water park there are 70 varieties of water attractions, including slides, wave pool, waterfalls, all this is accompanied by sound and unique visual effects. The park has a children's area, including playgrounds and other safe entertainment.

The water park has not only water rides; here you can ride on Nascar go-kart tracks, play golf, use the football stadium and sports grounds. In memory of visiting this place, travelers can buy souvenirs in one of the shops, dine at local restaurants. Myrtle Waves Waterpark is the perfect place to relax and enjoy the locals and guests of Myrtle Beach, a city in South Carolina.

Reviews

Even if you try hard, you are unlikely to be lucky to find a negative opinion about your vacation in Myrtle Beach. Absolutely all tourists who were lucky enough to visit there note that the rest was successful.

Amusement rides, many entertainment venues and, of course, the ocean and beaches left no one indifferent. It is only noted that you need to have a sufficient amount of funds with you.

Everyone who has been here is planning to return again.
