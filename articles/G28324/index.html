Holidays for every taste and budget - Novoaleksandrovo Recreation Area

Almost within walking distance from the capital there was an amazing place to relax with friends or family. On the coast of the Klyazma reservoir , the Novoaleksandrovo Recreation Area invites guests. This is 4.5 hectares of well-developed necessary infrastructure, and only seven kilometers from the Moscow Ring Road.

Modern comfort zone

The current borders of the Klyazma reservoir exist since 1937. It was arranged for the uninterrupted supply of water to the capital, to maintain the necessary water level in the Moscow River and the work of the Skhodnensky hydroelectric power station.



Today, the banks of the Klyazma reservoir are a very famous holiday destination. There are many places organized for summer entertainment, large celebrations and fishing. In the warm season, a lot of cafes, hotel complexes, yacht clubs, restaurants, water sports stations, etc. open on the coast. There is even a small contact zoo. From the point of view of vacationers, the most popular are Khlebnikovo, the Troitskoye recreation center and the Novoaleksandrovo recreation area.

Tempting offer

A new place of rest is a celebration not only of the body, but also of the soul. It took care of the comfort and convenience of guests, regardless of age. Therefore, I want to come back here again and again.

The shore is covered with sand, the bottom is cleared, the water is clear. For families with young children, the ideal place is the Novoaleksandrovo recreation area. Reviews, by the way, are enthusiastic, leaving both lovers of lazy beach doing nothing and outdoor activities. Throughout the territory of Novoaleksandrovo, including the parking lot, round-the-clock security posts operate, and lifeguards are on duty near the water. The administration constantly monitors water quality.





Entertainment

In the warm season, guests can enjoy many water attractions: calm for children and risky for adults. For example, there is a "Wave-4". This is a descent into the water with steps and inclined gutters. Or the Aztec pyramid. This attraction is in the water and looks like a pyramid with six faces on which the slides are located.

Novoaleksandrovo Recreation Area has several rental locations for any beach equipment and sports equipment. There are two volleyball courts on the territory, a field for indoor soccer and volleyball. There are ping-pong tables, trampolines and many other structures and attractions. And also several arbors from a wooden bar for rest and cooking barbecue or barbecue for small companies.

A children's area with slides, swings, etc. is also organized. The price for a water slide ride is only 100 rubles.

There are serious funs for adults: aquabike riding (three places), a tablet (two places), water skiing and a motor boat (10 thousand rubles).



Additional services

"Novoaleksandrovo" (recreation area), the prices of which surprise even the most demanding holidaymakers, offers to diversify your vacation. For this, a restaurant is opened on the territory, in which European cuisine is predominant, but the chef pleasantly surprises with dishes of Japanese and Uzbek cuisine.

For fans of strong alcohol a bar is open.

Numerous vacationers can rent spacious tents for parties (rent about 60 thousand rubles). Most guests prefer arched tents to celebrate banquets. They are covered with a waterproof white awning, which protects not only from the sun, but also from the wind. At the request of the client, transparent walls are installed, which allows you to admire the surrounding landscape.

On big holidays, by prior arrangement, the administration allows you to bring your own alcoholic beverages and does not charge cork fees for this .

In addition to various banquets, it is possible to arrange exit registration of marriage in Novoaleksandrovo (recreation area). Prices for the event must be specified on the official website. For this, a special platform was built near the water. Also on the site you can invite the presenter, registrar and DJ.

The recreation area assists in the organization and conduct of teambuilding of any orientation. There are established contacts with professional animators and presenters.

As an additional service, rental of sound, light, video and other equipment for a memorable evening is offered.

All tents are located on the coast and have individual exits to the water. It is possible to invite qualified waiters.

The Novoaleksandrovo Recreation Area has a guarded parking lot, dry closets, showers and beach changing rooms.



Location matters

This recreation area quickly gained popularity not only due to the cleanliness of air and water, comfort and coziness. A big plus is the advantageous location of Novoaleksandrovo (recreation area). How to get there Yes, very easy! On Dmitrovskoe highway (7 km from MKAD) to the village of Novoaleksandrovo (this is the final destination of many fixed-route taxis). Further along the numerous signs to the place of rest.
