How to make carved candles

Making carved candles is a complex process that requires certain skills. To make a beautiful product, you need to have artistic taste and talent. In addition, a good knowledge of the properties of paraffin is required in order to perform complex cutting of this material with a variety of tools. Having created some original candles, many start their own business in this area. Where to start?



Create blanks

Almost everyone can make carved candles at home. The whole process of creating such products can be divided into 5 stages. Some steps should be performed in a hurry, as paraffin hardens very quickly. How to make carved candles?

To begin with, you should make the core of the future candle - a blank. To do this, use pure wax or paraffin. The blank must be in the shape of a star. It can be five- or six-pointed. The height of the core should be approximately 30 centimeters. In general, it all depends on the preferences of the author and on the desired result.

Prepare the bath

The next stage is the preparation of baths containing liquid paraffin of various shades. This will make carved candles more original. The number of such baths depends on how many colored layers the product should have. Each shade requires a separate container. Baths with liquid paraffin must be constantly heated so that the material does not freeze. The temperature of the composition also matters. This indicator should be from 75 to 90 ¬∞ C. A very high temperature will allow a thin layer of paraffin to be applied, and a low one will make it thicker. The complexity of the drawing on the slice depends on this. If paraffin was applied in thin layers, then the candle will turn out to be more interesting and original.



Putting layers of paraffin

To make original carved candles with your own hands, you need to apply several layers of multi-colored paraffin. How to do it? It is necessary to immerse the workpiece alternately in bathtubs with material of various shades. This process in itself is simple. However, the master must feel the temperature of the composition and have the knack.

The workpiece must be fixed on the wire and alternately lowered into each paraffin bath. At this stage, do not rush. After each bath it is necessary to wait a certain period of time for the material to solidify. To speed up the process, you can gently lower the workpiece into cold water. You should not wait for the paraffin to solidify completely, since later it will be simply impossible to cut the candle. The main thing is that paraffin is fixed on the core.

Cutting process

Cut candles are difficult to make without certain skills. Perhaps the first time you won‚Äôt get the perfect product. After all, after baths with paraffin only 15 minutes remain to create a beautiful picture. The complexity of the process is that such a short period of time does not give the opportunity to experiment and carefully work out the design.



For starters, you can practice on solid candles. To do this, gently heat the product with a hairdryer or microwave, and then apply a picture. This will allow not only to learn how to work quickly with the material, but also to feel paraffin.

Carved candles are almost ready. It remains to trim the bases so that the finished product is more stable. It is also necessary to make a small indentation at the very top of the candle for the wick.

In conclusion

Carved candles are an original decoration for any festive table. Such products are perfect for a gift or souvenir. The process of making such candles at first seems very complicated. However, after a few samples, everything will start to work out. After some time, it will be possible to start your own business for the production of carved candles. Do not forget that such products are in great demand.
