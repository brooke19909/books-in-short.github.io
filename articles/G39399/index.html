Philips Home Cinema: reviews, overview, specifications, choice

Philips compact home cinemas, according to consumer reviews, due to the presence of a soundbar provide high-quality surround sound. With their help, you can completely immerse yourself in the atmosphere of your favorite movie. For this, the manufacturer equips the equipment with proprietary developments Virtual Surround Sound, Ambisound, Dolby Digital.

Features and model selection

When choosing the system that suits you, pay attention to technologies for improving sound quality and the number of built-in amplifiers. Most often, the price of a Philips home theater depends on these characteristics.

The company produces acoustic panels in the formats 2.1, 3.1 and 5.1. Virtual Surround Sound technology, which creates the illusion of five-channel sound, is used in two- and three-channel models. And with 5.1-format home systems, the Ambisound option for vector processing is supported. According to user reviews, it is she who significantly improves the sound quality.

Philips soundbars can transmit audio content via mobile devices via Bluetooth. To establish such a connection, just click the NFC icon on the speaker panel housing. In addition, the systems are equipped with standard audio inputs, allowing you to connect external media using a cable. And premium home devices are equipped with optical drives that support any disc - from DVD and CD to Blu-ray.

The following is a short overview of the popular Philips home theater models and customer reviews about them.
