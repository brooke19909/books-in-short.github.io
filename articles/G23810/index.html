Greece, Paralia Katerinis: reviews, description, features of leisure and attractions

There are many places in the world that are worth visiting for every traveler. Every year, new resorts appear on the coasts of the seas, attracting the attention of tourists. For many Russians, Greece has become the "summer home". Paralia Katerini collected the most positive reviews. This young resort invites thousands of people to the shores of the Aegean . Here, in the eastern part of the prefecture of Pieria, there is a huge number of cozy hotels, restaurants, clubs and bars.

Holidays at this resort are suitable for everyone: honeymooners, large families and the elderly. You can and should come to Paralia Katerinis for the sake of shopping, clean beaches and various entertainments.

Path to the sea

Many European countries have become popular for recreation. Among them is Greece. Paralia Katerini, reviews of which are only positive, is located on the coast of the Aegean Sea. Not far from the village is located no less popular place - Soloniki. It is from here that you can get to the resort.

The distance between these two cities is 70 kilometers. Trains and buses leave from Soloniki daily to Katerini. There is a highway between them for those who travel by car.

In general, from Russia there are many flights to Greece. If suddenly you did not find tickets to Thessaloniki, you can fly to Athens. From here, by train or bus to Paralia Katerinis 350 kilometers. On average, taking an express train takes 5 hours.

What to pack in a suitcase?

This country is always the most comfortable stay. In Greece (Paralia Katerinis), climate reviews are only positive. Those who are familiar with geography will be able to guess what weather conditions to expect. For others, it is worth noting that the Mediterranean climate has always been distinguished by comfortable temperature indicators. In winter, despite the rain, the weather is mild, in the summer it is always warm and dry.



At this resort, as well as at nearby ones, the year is divided into two seasons: rainy and dry. The first begins in October and leaves with the beginning of spring. The second pleases from April to the end of September. Despite the fact that winter in many regions of Russia is associated with snow and frost, in Katerini, January and February are considered the coldest months. At this time, the temperature drops to +5 degrees.

But the warm season is as hot as possible in August. At this time, the temperature rises to +35 degrees. But since we are talking about coastal resorts, thanks to the fresh sea breeze, even on the hottest day, tourists feel comfortable. In addition, the town is surrounded by mountains, significantly affecting the weather.

Where to live?

Of course, independent travelers can also find private accommodation options, but it is best to choose safer accommodation - hotels. Paralia Katerinis (Greece) hotel reviews get the most flattering. The fact is that since the resort is young, all the hotels here are new and well-groomed. They are represented by a huge selection of categories and are located on the first coastline. It is better to book in advance, since in spite of their sufficient number in the city, there may still be no places, and even more so rooms with views of the Aegean Sea.



Of course, in a short time, the resort was able to highlight its leaders in the hotel business. The most visited hotels were Villa Sevasti, Dion, Eva Hotel, Hotel Strass and Kymata. Find information about these hotels on the Internet.

Villa Sevasti is a great place for those who value style. The exterior is very elegant and modern. The hotel is categorized as a Four Star. There is everything you need in the room. The staff is friendly and always ready to help.

Dion is a two-star hotel located directly on the shores of the Aegean Sea. Everyone who rested in this place is advised to take a room with a view of the beach. The hotel is close to all the necessary shops and restaurants. Also nearby are many attractions.

Eva Hotel is a great budget option, as rooms here start at $ 55 per night. The price includes breakfast and snacks. Inside the hotel there is an excellent tennis court and casino. It is possible to arrange a hike or fishing.

Hotel Strass is a terrific hotel overlooking the sea. Located only 80 meters from the beach. This three star hotel is a great affordable option for the whole family. The center is 2 kilometers away, and the famous Mount Olympus is 4 km away. The hotel has everything you need: restaurant, bar, spa, fitness center, etc.



Kymata is a comfortable three star hotel. Located on the beach, close to shops and restaurants. Hotel "Kumata" (Greece, Paralia Katerinis) received positive reviews. Features

Paralia Katerini is an excellent resort that will delight not only the beach, entertainment and attractions, but also shopping. It turns out that many come here for shopping. The fact is that prices here are much lower than in the CIS countries. The main thing that travelers go for in autumn is fur coats. Of course, this is not such a huge choice as in Kastoria, but the "fur business" is flourishing here and will soon become even larger.



By the way, the best time for shopping is in August and February. It is in these months, in Greece, sales begin and discounts reach up to 50%. There are a lot of shops that sell fur coats. Moreover, most of the sellers here are Russian, and therefore it is easy to bargain with them.

In addition to fur coats, tourists are also pleased with low prices for souvenirs. Unlike Spain, France, Italy, in the resorts of Greece you can inexpensively buy magnets for relatives, beautiful services, amphora vases, shells or olive oil. The local Metax alcohol is also inexpensive.
