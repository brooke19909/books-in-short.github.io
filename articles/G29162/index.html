Princess Seaview Resort & Spa 4 * (Phuket, Thailand): hotel reviews

The Thai resort of Phuket in the last decade has become one of the most popular holiday destinations for our tourists. Thai exoticism, wonderful beaches, unique attractions and excellent service attract here. Enjoy the rest in full, without spending large sums, provides the opportunity to a comfortable modern hotel Princess Seaview Resort Spa 4 *, located away from the noise of the city, surrounded by evergreen vegetation. Staying here has some features that are not popular with all guests. But most tourists consider this hotel ideal for a good budget holiday.



Location

Princess Seaview Resort Spa 4 * hotel is located in Phuket, at a distance of 16 km from its center, near Karon Beach, but in a place that some tourists consider to be successful and others unsuitable, especially for a beach holiday. Regarding the coast of the Andaman Sea, this is the third line. Hotel guests carries a free tuk-tuk to the beach. Around the life of the Thai people, leisurely shops and establishments where you can eat are working around. From Phuket Airport to the hotel 40 km, or about 40 minutes on the transfer provided by the travel agency. Princess Seaview Resort Spa 4 * guests will have to drive about 10 minutes by taxi or tuk-tuk to the famous Patong night entertainment center. It costs from 200 baht (who will agree how). But you can walk to the famous Buddha statue, as well as to the Dino park . It is about 2 km. or 15 minutes walk. About the same to the nearest market and to the temple.



Description

The territory of Princess Seaview Resort Spa 4 * is small. It houses only three residential buildings and free parking. The entire infrastructure of the hotel is concentrated inside the buildings and on the roof. Reception and concierge are available around the clock. The staff does not understand Russian, but some staff speak English. You can use the Internet (for a fee) in an exclusively decorated spacious hall furnished with beautiful furniture, and at the reception you can leave your luggage in the left luggage office, hand in laundry, dry cleaning, ironing, exchange currency, rent a bicycle, call a taxi, order a free tuk- tuk to the beach, use photocopy and fax machines.

For guests combining vacation in Phuket with work, the hotel offers a conference room and a business center.



Accommodation

Princess Seaview Resort Spa 4 * has 152 rooms. All of them are very spacious, bright and comfortable, decorated in a modern style according to the principle of minimalism. Each is equipped with a balcony and large panoramic windows, but sea views from them are only from the upper floors, starting from the fifth, and from the fourth and lower - to the city. The bathrooms have a bathtub and a transparent shower, toilet, hairdryer, washbasin. Guests are provided with a full set of personal hygiene products, slippers for rooms and streets, bathrobes.

Cleaning is done thoroughly and daily; towels, sheets and pillowcases are changed according to the schedule.

Nutrition

Regarding the catering at Princess Seaview Resort Spa 4 *, the reviews are mostly good. The hotel has 2 restaurants, one located on the ground floor, the second on the roof, the views from which are simply fantastic. Tours to this hotel are offered either with breakfast only or with half board, and during the season an additional barbecue is organized. According to the guests, the breakfast menu is varied, the dishes are delicious, the assortment is rich. Type of food - self-service. All the waiters work quickly and smoothly, tourists have no complaints about the service. The assortment of dishes includes several types of side dishes (rice in different variations, pasta, potatoes), meat, fish, ham, eggs in different types, salads and fresh vegetables, sliced, soups, dairy products, fruits, sweets, juices, coffee, tea.

Guests can enjoy drinks at the bars located in the lobby and by the pool. If the cost of breakfast is not included in the price of the tour, they are paid separately. For adults, the amount is 500 baht, for children - 250 baht per day.

Leisure

Of course, people go to other countries not only to stay in rooms, even the very best. Many are interested in beach vacations. And this opportunity gives its guests Princess Seaview Resort Spa 4 *. Phuket is primarily famous for the Andaman coast and beaches. The most calm and clean, albeit public, is Karon. It is about 2 km from the hotel. You can go there on foot along the road (about 15-20 minutes) or through the territory of other hotels (7-10 minutes) if their security misses. There is another option - use the free tuk-tuk provided by the hotel. The service is valid until 17:00. You can also get from the beach by tuk-tuk, for which you need to go to the hotel representatives on the coast and order this transport.



Karon beach is popular because of its great length, so there is always a lot of free space. The sand here is clean, creaking underfoot. The sea is warm, the entrance to it is good, but there are often waves in the area. All services on the beach are paid.

You can also have fun at the hotel by visiting the SPA lounge or the large outdoor rooftop pool. There is a bar next to it, a spacious sun terrace with sun loungers and umbrellas. This place offers fantastic views of the surroundings.

Many tourists will certainly want to try Thai massage, coming to Phuket. Princess Seaview Resort Spa 4 * hotel provides this service and invites all guests to well-equipped massage rooms. In addition, in the SPA-salon there is a beauty center, fitness, sauna. For kids, the hotel has a children's pool (also on the roof), cots are available on request in the rooms, and you can use highchairs in the restaurant.



Additional Information

You can book hotels of various quality and price for trips to Phuket. Princess Seaview Resort Spa 4 * is suitable for those tourists who do not see a problem in a location far from the beach, but want to get quality service for relatively little money.

Children to this hotel are accepted from any age. If the child or adult is accommodated in an extra bed, the service charge is 1,500 baht per day.

- Check in at the hotel from 14:00.

- Check out - until 12:00.

If you visit the hotel during the winter holidays, the mandatory payments include New Year's dinner for adults and children. The cost is 5000 and 2500 baht, respectively.

Internet throughout the hotel is paid. Price per day - 600 baht, per hour - 120 baht.

Reviews

Rating of Princess Seaview Resort Spa among different tour operators is set from 3.4 to 4.3 out of 5 points. The opinion of tourists about the rest in this hotel is ambiguous.

Noted advantages:

  • low price (double room here costs from 5800 rubles per day. with all fees and taxes);
  • spacious comfortable rooms;
  • high-quality cleaning;
  • delicious breakfasts;
  • great pool;
  • beautiful views from the roof;
  • free tuk-tuk to the beach.

Noted disadvantages:

  • poor location with respect to the beach and city infrastructure;
  • lack of territory;
  • a lot of Chinese making noise;
