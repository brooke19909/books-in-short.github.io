Offline work

Buyers note that battery life is considered the strength of the Xiaomi Redmi 4X 16Gb Black smartphone. We are not talking about records, but in general, the data received from the owners is impressive. The battery in the device is used at 4100 mAh, which speaks for itself. The battery life is also affected by the fact that the screen resolution is small, and the operating system has a special program to optimize energy consumption.

The manufacturer claims that in video mode the battery will last about 15 hours. When talking, it will last 36 hours. It will take about 13 hours to play applications without recharging. When the 4G network mode is on, the battery will last no more than 18 hours, and with GPS navigation - 15 h. However, the owners of these parameters give their data.

So, when watching video on HD quality, the phone lasts 12 hours without charging. If you constantly update sites through a browser, the battery will be discharged after 8-9 hours. With games, the phone will last no more than 6-7 hours.

With average or even passive use in the daytime and active mode at night, the smartphone performs well. Nevertheless, it will have to be charged every day. System optimization allows you to delay the moment you reach 0%, but buyers do not advise to ‚Äúwake up‚Äù the device every 20 minutes during the day.

Software

Phone Xiaomi Redmi 4X 3 / 16Gb Black runs on the "Android". The fact that the MIUI shell is installed on top does not distinguish it from previous models - this solution is rather considered standard. It should be noted right away that not all beginners will like this idea. For already experienced users, it will seem standard and simple.

The whole problem is that the shell is very different from the pure "Android". It combines the qualities of not only the "green robot", but also Apple's operating system - IOS. The developers decided to create such a shell for a long time and still have not abandoned it, trying each time to make it more comfortable for users.

It should be noted that in the model in question, the lock screen immediately after switching to the desktop erases notifications. The status bar has a minimum of notation. The list of notifications is structured in such a way that it is not clear whether it is all fit or if there are any other messages. Their so-called cards are sometimes displayed singly, sometimes in blocks. The workspace is organized strangely: there is no application menu. The clock in the corner may disappear if you take other widgets to the desktop. These are all trifles, but to a person who is not used to it, a purchase may seem unsuccessful.

If we consider competitors, then Xiaomi Redmi 4X 3 / 16Gb Black should be called an average with strong characteristics. Cost and quality combine perfectly, there are no significant drawbacks. This device may seem boring, but it performs all the work and functions perfectly.

The main rival of Xiaomi, according to buyers, should be called Meizu M3s Mini. This smartphone is famous for its balance in work, but at the moment it is already outdated. Moreover, his device is running the fifth version of Android, and the eighth in Xiaomi. Meizu M5 is another good smartphone that can be recommended as an economical option.

Those who are already tired of using Chinese models should pay attention to ASUS Zenfone 3 Max. This is another direct competitor to the smartphone described in the article. Its cost is slightly higher than the previous ones, it uses other technologies and a larger screen size. Buyers note that if you compare these two models, then ASUS has the best design, good battery life and the latest version of Android.

We have provided a detailed review of the Xiaomi Redmi 4X 16GB Black model. To buy it or not to buy - the choice is yours.
