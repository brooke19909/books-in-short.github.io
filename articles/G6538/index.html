London millennium

Continuing to explore the sights of Western Europe, do not miss the Millennium Bridge, which is located in the capital of England. Another name for the bridge is the Millennium Bridge. This place is a steel suspension made for pedestrians. The bridge is thrown over the London Thames. The Millennium connects Bankside and City.

In 1998, the construction of the Millennium Bridge began, and its official opening took place two years later. During one of the charity events, its participants had the opportunity to go over this design. They felt a slight stagger, so it was decided at the same time to close the Millennium. For two days, pedestrian access to the bridge was limited, and after that the crossing was blocked until the problem was fixed. Reconstruction of the Millennium Bridge lasted until 2002, after which it again became open for mass use. At the moment, the Millennium Bridge is not only an object that is included in the sights of Europe, but also one of the symbols of London.

The Eiffel Tower

Well, how can you describe the sights of European cities in order not to mention one of the symbols of Paris - the Eiffel Tower? Not knowing this design is simply impossible, unless you were frozen several millennia ago, and you just woke up. The author of the object was Gustav Eiffel. He created his masterpiece in honor of the centenary of the French Revolution of 1789. It was decided to celebrate this date with the Paris World Exhibition. And the 360-meter tower made of metal became its main exhibit in the nomination of the latest technical achievements.
