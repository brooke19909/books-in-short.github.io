Bear Corner: a recreation center in Altai

Green tourism is gaining in popularity. Today, spending holidays in a village or at a recreation center remote from civilization is much better than frying under the hot sun of a crowded resort, tourism experts say.

Rest in the green zone is not only more interesting, but also more useful. And to spend a vacation away from a busy city does not mean to go to a place where you will be bored.

Independent camp sites and complexes are guided by the demand for such a type of recreation as green tourism, so they try to do everything to make people interesting and comfortable.

"Bear Corner" - a recreation center, located in a picturesque place, near the village of Aya on the banks of the Katun River. Just 9 kilometers from the new Aysky bridge, near the ski resort on Veselaya Gora, there is a modern complex.





The main activity of the tourist complex is family vacations, leisure activities for newlyweds and couples in love. Banquets are held here and corporate rides are organized.

Settling in a hotel complex, you can choose one of the available types of accommodation and live in a wooden cottage with all amenities.

Rules of stay at the camp site

What you need to know about the schedule in the Bear Corner hotel complex? The recreation center has established several clear and understandable, easily enforceable rules:

  1. Smoking inside is strictly prohibited.
  2. It is forbidden to call in and check into the hotel with pets, a dog walking area is not provided, and the base's activity specializes in family vacations and involves accommodation with children.
  3. When settling in the complex from May to August and on New Year's holidays, you need to know that it is necessary to pay the cost of food.

Complex Features

All major buildings are made of cedar wood. The internal infrastructure of the recreation center is well developed. The complex has free parking for cars. The river has comfortable recreation areas with gazebos, bonfires and barbecue facilities. Here you can go fishing and even cook freshly caught fish or give it to the cook who will cook it, observing all technological processes. For children there are special playgrounds, a pool. The territory adjacent to the complex is well maintained and safe.
