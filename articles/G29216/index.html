The Sands Khao Lak By Katathani: description, photo, reviews

Having stayed at this hotel, travelers passionately fall in love with Thailand. According to reviews, The Sands Khao Lak By Katathani 5 * has absolutely no flaws. The guests respond with admiration about the high level of service and the conditions created in the hotel for a pleasant stay. About the location of the institution

The Sands Khao Lak By Katathani Hotel (photo available in the article) is located in the Southern Region of Thailand at 82190, Moo 7, Phang Nga, Kukkak Takuapa, 10/18 Petchkasem rd. The institution belongs to the category of beach, as it is located directly on the beach of Nang Tong (1st line from the sea).

Attractions nearby

The Sands Khao Lak By Katathani (Koa Lak) is located an hour's drive (60.6 km) from Phuket International Airport. The nearest attractions are:

  • Krungsri Bank, Khaolak Branch is 0.3 km away.
  • Nangthong Supermarket is 0.5 km away.
  • Tublamu Pier is 9.6 km away.
  • Tsunami Memorial is 2 km away.
  • Bangnieng Afternoon Market is 1.5 km away.
  • PTT Gas Station is 5 km away.
  • Sairung Waterfall is 11.1 km away.

What beaches are located in the vicinity?

Description

The Sands Khao Lak By Katathani is just one of the fantastic options in Khaulak. It offers comfortable rooms with Wi-Fi (free) and a balcony. Residents are offered the opportunity to swim in the pool or relax with a massage. Wi-Fi (free) is available throughout.

The modern rooms at The Sands Khao Lak By Katathani are equipped with a flat-screen cable TV, iPod dock and DVD player. En suite bathrooms come with hairdryers and free toiletries.

At the reception (24 hours), luggage storage is available at the disposal of residents. The hotel provides parking (private, free). The hotel's restaurant serves a variety of seafood dishes, as well as Thai and Western dishes. An excellent choice for anyone interested in beach walks and relaxation is Nangtong Beach, where the hotel is located.

This option is considered by many tourists to be one of the best in Khaulak. The price range is 6,270 - 25,150 rubles. (average rate of a standard room).
