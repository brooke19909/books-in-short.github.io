Hotel "Grinn" in Kursk: description, photo, address, reviews

Hotel "Grinn" in Kursk has long been recognized as one of the best in the whole city. According to vacationers, it offers a wide range of services for a moderate fee, as well as quality service and decent living conditions.

Hotel "Grinn" (Kursk) is located not in the city center, but 25 kilometers about it, where the main city attractions are concentrated. This fact is very pleasing to tourists who travel to the city in order to get acquainted with its history. Her address: the city of Kursk, Dzerzhinsky, 40.

Hotel "Grinn" is located in the place of concentration of a large number of holy sources, this place is famous for its religious history. Not far from it there are many shrines that preserve the memory of the famous Seraphim of Sarov, and also there are many places of military glory during the Great Patriotic War.

Rooms

The room fund of the hotel "Grinn" (Kursk) consists of 98 comfortable apartments, in which everything is made in a modern style. In total, they can simultaneously accommodate up to 300 guests.
