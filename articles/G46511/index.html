Important information

Check-in here is at 14:00, and check-out is at 12:00. This should be taken into account so as not to overpay the extra money. Children under three years stay free when using existing beds. If the child is more than three years old, then an extra bed can be provided. The cost of an extra bed is 500 rubles per night. Catering for tourists

A rich breakfast buffet is served each morning. Guests are offered a wide selection of cuts and sandwiches, scrambled eggs and omelettes, fruits and fresh pastries. Many tourists say that such a breakfast successfully replaces lunch and even lunch. The traditional restaurant serves European dishes. At the wine bar you can always order wine. However, despite the positive reviews, tourists emphasize that the service still does not attract five stars.

It is proposed to visit the Portofino restaurant. This is a stunning corner of Mediterranean cuisine. The chef also offers guests to taste original dishes. The design of the restaurant is very elegant and at the same time simple, so it will be an ideal option for any occasion.

Sports recreation

The hotel management advocates a healthy lifestyle and prohibits smoking on its territory. In addition, it creates all conditions for active training and deep relaxation.
