Hotel Alexandros Palace Hotel Suites 5 * in Greece: traveler reviews

If you plan to spend a vacation in Greece, then, considering the most suitable options for you, be sure to think about the Alexandros Palace Hotel Suites (Halkidiki, Athos). In our article we want to talk in detail about this complex so that you have the most complete picture of it.

Location

Alexandros Palace Hotel Suites 5 * (Greece) is considered one of the best places to relax in Halkidiki. The huge and undeniable advantage of the hotel is its location. It should be noted that the complex is located in a very picturesque place. It is surrounded by mountains drowning in greenery and a clear sea. And the hotel itself is well decorated in a modern style.



As you can already understand, the Alexandros Palace Hotel Suites is located on the Athos Peninsula, in the Tripiti area, just three kilometers from Nea Roda and five kilometers from the town of Uranupoli. There are nine kilometers from the complex to the border with the monastic republic. The distance to the Macedonia Airport in Thessaloniki is one hundred eight kilometers, so it will take some time to get to the place of rest. In principle, this is not a problem, since you can arrange a transfer. A comfortable bus will be waiting for you, which will take you to the Alexandros Palace Hotel Suites 5 * (Halkidiki, Athos). By car, the road does not take more than one and a half hours (the bus goes a little longer). Some vacationers are unhappy that they have to spend so much time on a trip.

Alexandros Palace Hotel Suites 5 * (Greece, Halkidiki) has a fairly developed infrastructure. The administration of the complex does not forget about guests with disabilities. Alexandros Palace Hotel Suites accepts guests with pets subject to prior arrangement and extra charge.

For guests Alexandros Palace Hotel Suites 5 * there is a poolside bar, snack bar, restaurant, beach bar, tavern, Classic bar. Here you can get acquainted with Mediterranean cuisine and taste international dishes. The hotel bars offer exotic cocktails and the best refreshing drinks.
