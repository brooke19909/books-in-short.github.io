Hotel Leonardo Club Eilat 4 * (Israel, Eilat): photos and reviews

The main pride of Eilat are mountains, sea and sun. It is for their sake that numerous tourists come here. No wonder that Eilat is considered the pearl of the Red Sea. In our article we want to talk about one of the resort hotels - Leonardo Club Eilat 4 *. Address: Northern Beach, 88103, Eilat.

A bit about the hotel

Leonardo Club Eilat 4 * is a visited hotel in which vacationers can find the perfect combination of comfortable apartments, excellent food and friendly service. The complex is located in the heart of the city, close to a shopping center and a ten-minute walk from restaurants and shops.

The distance from the hotel to the beach does not exceed 600 meters. Very close to the complex are such objects as the Dolphin Reef Reserve and the Red Sea Carall Reef Aquarium.



Hotel Leonardo Club Eilat 4 * was opened in 1996 and since then has been successfully hosting tourists. The last renovation of the institution was made in 2005. The complex is part of the Fattal network.

The hotel is of great interest to tourists vacationing with children.

Number of rooms

Hotel Leonardo Club Eilat 4 * has 282 comfortable rooms with beautiful design. Modern apartments provide a quality vacation for the whole family. Guests will appreciate the well-equipped rooms with balconies overlooking the pool. The number of rooms is represented by the following categories:

  1. Superior 22 sq. M. m. It is equipped with air conditioning, cable TV, safe, minibar, desk, sofa and double bed. The room can accommodate four guests (two adults and two children).
  2. Superior overlooking the pool. Such apartments can be either two single beds or one double, so it is worthwhile to specify in advance what equipment you would like.
  3. Superior overlooking the garden. These rooms are located on the ground floor and have their own private access to the garden, which means access to the pool.
  4. Junior Suite - Superior rooms.
  5. Superior Suite is ideal for large families. These apartments are more spacious. And the bathrooms in them are equipped with a jacuzzi. The rooms also have small kitchens that allow you to prepare meals.

Catering

Hotel Leonardo Club Eilat 4 * (Israel) operates on the very popular all-inclusive system. Aviv Restaurant serves a varied buffet for breakfast, lunch and dinner.



There is also a cafe in the hotel, where bartenders offer snacks and light drinks, wine and beer. In season, every evening, guests are entertained with show programs with the participation of singing waiters.

At Poolside Grill, guests can enjoy light meals, ice creams and drinks.

Hotel infrastructure

Hotel Leonardo Club Eilat 4 * (Israel) is interesting for guests, first of all, with its water activities. It offers four swimming pools and a park with water slides. In addition, the complex has a regular artificial pond and a large outdoor whirlpool. The territory adjacent to the pools is surrounded by greenery, among which waterfalls pleasantly murmur.

The hotel Eilat Leonardo (Club Fattal) 4 * has a large conference room, which can accommodate about a thousand people. The premises are rented for organizing various events: banquets, exhibitions, weddings, seminars and business meetings.



The hotel has a fairly developed infrastructure. On its territory there is a hairdresser, gift shop, library. The facility offers babysitting services, an internet room and free parking.

Organization of children's leisure

Hotel Leonardo Club Hotel Eilat 4 * (Israel, Eilat) takes care of children's leisure, and therefore the administration allocated funds to update the amusement park for young guests. Leisure for small tourists is divided into age groups:

  1. Up to four years old (Kishkushim club).
  2. From four to nine (slot machines and board games).
  3. From nine to twelve (car simulators, PlayStation consoles, dance mats).

However, the favorite place for all children is the amazing water park with attractions.

Entertainment for adults

Hotel Leonardo Club Hotel Eilat 4 * (Israel), tourists can visit the spa, where a series of wellness treatments and massages. It features a gym and sauna. On the territory there are tennis courts that vacationers can use.

Taking care of the leisure of their guests, the administration of the institution organizes entertaining evening shows. The animation team entertains tourists throughout the day, providing a charge of positive emotions.

Beach vacation

Hotel Leonardo Club Eilat 4 * (Israel, Eilat) is built on the third line, the distance to the coast is about 600 meters. Since Eilat itself is a fairly small and compact town, all objects are within walking distance, including the beach. Slowly, the coast can be reached in 15-20 minutes.



Hotel guests can relax on the beaches of the Leonardo Plasa or Herodos hotels. At the reception you should take cards in advance, the presentation of which will allow them to use sun loungers and umbrellas for free.

According to guests of the institution, the hotel is certainly worthy of attention. However, it has both advantages and disadvantages, which are worth knowing to vacationers planning to spend their holidays in Eilat. But let's talk about everything in more detail.

The Leonardo Club Hotel Eilat 4 * complex is suitable for relaxation for all categories of tourists: both young people and couples with children. As a rule, tourists from different countries have a rest in a hotel, therefore the contingent is international. The hotel has a beautifully landscaped area, surrounded by greenery and flowers.



The hotel complex has quite the same type of rooms, the difference between which is only where the windows of the apartments go. The rooms located on the ground floor have their own access to the pool. Otherwise, the equipment in all apartments is almost the same.

General impression of the hotel

If you plan to visit Eilat, Eilat Leonardo (Club Fattal) 4 * can be a good accommodation option. The main advantage of the institution should be considered its entertainment complex, consisting of pools and slides, which are always interesting to ride not only for children but also for adults. Water rides are always popular with tourists. Hotel ponds are equipped with waterfalls and greenery. In general, the recreation area is very nicely decorated and offers relaxation and a pleasant pastime.

The hotel has no heated ponds, so after the season, in cloudy weather, the water may seem a little cool. Otherwise, slides and rides are a great way to have fun.



Young tourists can use the private pool. And experienced animators will not let even the smallest holidaymakers get bored. Their entertainment programs are divided by age and allow children of various categories to be involved in games. It is the presence of animators and water attractions that makes the rest in the hotel not only fun, but also unforgettable.

Beach Vacation Reviews

As we already mentioned, the hotel is located at some distance from the coast. The nearest beach is a 20-minute walk, but it is paid, but Herodos beach is a little further. The coast is covered with fine pebbles and sand, the entry into the sea is gentle and comfortable. Tourists are very pleased with the beach-sea vacation on the Red Sea coast.

Interesting places and entertainments of the resort

Eilat is a small port town that is famous for its beaches. All are well equipped. The coast is divided into two parts: Coral beach and North coast. The first of them is located outside the city, its name speaks for itself. Divers like to gather on it to swim with scuba diving and masks. The north coast is located within the city and has a sand and pebble coating.

It is worth noting that the Coral Reef is the main attraction of the city. However, one does not have to be a diver to watch it. The reserve has an underwater observatory. Here you can see an exhibition of the fauna and flora of the Red Sea, as well as tropical animals of South America.
