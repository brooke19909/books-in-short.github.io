Where to invest a small amount of money and how to profit from it?

To be sure of your financial independence, you need to save small amounts of money, as they say, on a rainy day. An ideal option would be to invest, since the funds will not just lie somewhere in the envelope, in the box. They will start working for their owner. Only here is the question: "Where to invest a small amount of money?" After all, contributions are usually quite impressive. But small investments are also popular, and even more than large investments.





Deposits

Perhaps the most popular way to get your money to work is with deposits. In fact, this is a proven method that is considered reliable and risk-free. If the question arises of where to invest a small amount of money, then the option with a deposit investment comes to mind first. From a variety of offers put forward by the bank, one's eyes run wide. These can be non-refillable deposits, as well as those that can be increased during the deposit period. Interest is accrued either every month or quarterly. Or they can be received by the account holder only at the end of the period for which the deposit was created. By the way, it should be noted that the interest is quite substantial. Each bank offers its own, but in general, the average rate is 11% per annum. Thus, if the contribution is 100 thousand rubles, then at the end of the term a person will receive another 11,000 from above. A pretty good increase. By the way, many banks accept deposits starting with small amounts - for example, from a thousand rubles. Modern investment

These include PAMM accounts. They appeared in Russia not so long ago, only a few years ago. However, the income that can be extracted from investments of this kind is impressive. Many people who wondered where to invest a small amount of money online have come up with this. The principle is that the funds of several private clients are deposited into the general account, and the appropriate specialist (trader) manages the money. In the end, income is generated, and it is distributed among those people who invested in the PAMM account. The benefits are many. First, savings are in control. Secondly, they can be quickly withdrawn. Thirdly, you can monitor the work of the manager. And most importantly, you can make small contributions, which is an undoubted advantage, since everyone can try themselves in this matter.
