LCD "Garden Park Edalgo": apartments, photos, reviews

Moscow is a huge metropolis located in the heart of the state. Many of its residents, especially in recent years, are trying to sell their Moscow housing and move closer to nature, beyond the Moscow Ring Road. And so many developers choose precisely such areas for the construction of their projects. Garden Park Edalgo was no exception.





For its location, the developer chose the most suitable area - the village of Kommunarka, which is part of the Sosensky settlement in the Novoskovsky administrative district. It is only four kilometers from the main highway of the capital - the Moscow Ring Road.

A bit about the village of Kommunarka

About four kilometers south of Moscow along the Kaluga highway, immediately beyond the village of Gas Pipeline, is the village of Kommunarka. Previously, about 5 thousand people lived in the village. But this does not take into account the rapidly built residential complexes over the past few years: Garden Park Edalgo, Edalgo.





Currently, the infrastructure of the village is actively developing. Metro stations are being designed, the construction of which should be completed in 2019-2020. From an environmental point of view, the village is the most advantageous offer: a green massif, two lakes almost in the center of the village. Not every settlement boasts of this.

Location of the complex

LCD "Garden Park Edalgo" in Kommunarka is located in its southern part, on the street Fitarevskaya. Across the street from the complex is the administration building. You can get to the village by personal transport along Kaluga highway, turning left behind the Gas Pipeline to Kommunarka. Alexandra Monakhova’s street will lead straight to the village, where then it will be necessary to turn right into Fitarevskaya street, on which the Garden Park Edalgo residential complex is located.





The above is suitable for owners of their own vehicles. Those who do not have cars can get to the metro stations “Buninskaya Alleya”, “Ulitsa Gorchakova”, “Admiral Ushakova Boulevard”, “Teply Stan”, transfer to public transport (buses, minibus), which will bring you directly to the complex . Thus, the journey takes about 20-25 minutes.

Description of the complex

What is Garden Park Edalgo? If you purchase an apartment in this residential complex, you can be sure that you will live surrounded by beautiful nature, lakes and a park. The developer has completely preserved the existing landscape during the construction of the complex.





Eight multi-storey buildings include the Garden Park Edalgo. Garden Park Edalgo has now been fully commissioned and occupied. In total, 18 buildings, recreation and sports areas are provided. The houses are being built using monolithic technology using the most modern building materials, ensuring maximum reliability of buildings.

Internal infrastructure

What is the complex inside and what services with conveniences does it provide to its residents? Garden Park Edalgo, whose photo confirms this, is equipped with high-speed silent elevators that can lift residents to the highest floors in a matter of seconds. All houses are connected to the central heat supply provided by the village of Kommunarka. Windows are equipped with double-glazed windows with an energy-saving function.





For owners of their own vehicles, an underground parking is provided. For recreation and entertainment, Garden Park Edalgo offers a landscaped and landscaped courtyard area, equipped children's sports and playgrounds, places for walking and recreation. Child safety is provided for by a ban on cars entering the yard.

External infrastructure

Since the LCD "Garden Park Edalgo", reviews of residents about which is mostly positive, is not located near the outskirts, but directly in the village of Kommunarka, its residents have the right to use the entire infrastructure of the village. As for the children, the school for them is located very close to the complex. Another one is a little distance away, within walking distance. Next to the second indicated school is also a kindergarten for the smallest residents of the complex.

In addition, the village has a polyclinic, the Palace of Culture. There are large chain stores, such as Crossroads, Dixie. On the territory of the complex itself, the developer also designed the construction of a business park, a shopping center, and a kindergarten. Butovo Forest Park and two ponds invite you to relax across the road from the complex.

Apartments in the complex, their cost

Garden Park Edalgo offers different apartments: from one-room to three-room. Their footage is from twenty six to one hundred and eighteen square meters with a ceiling height of three meters. Any apartment "Garden Park Edalgo" is provided by the developer in draft finish. According to reviews, this allows you to significantly save on purchase and subsequently create your own interior.

The cost of one square meter of living space starts from 90 thousand rubles and reaches a maximum of 140 thousand rubles. Thus, a one-room apartment costs on average a little more than 4.5 million rubles, a three-room apartment - over 6 million rubles.





Reviews about the complex

The already fully built and commissioned Garden Park Edalgo residential complex has mostly positive reviews. The developer offers everyone in the immediate vicinity of the Moscow Ring Road business-class housing, not inferior to the capital. Therefore, everyone who decided to move from a stuffy, gas-filled metropolis to the suburbs does not feel restrained in anything. This also affects their comments on social networks and on the official website of the developer.

Kommunarka is a very promising area for further construction, the residents of which receive Moscow registration. By the way, not far from the residential complex, construction of metro stations is underway, the social infrastructure in the village is developing more and more. After all, this residential complex is not the only one, in the immediate vicinity of it many more houses of other residential complexes are being built.

Some residents complain that the Kaluga highway is very busy, especially at rush hour. And that on personal vehicles, that on public sometimes to get to the city is not easy.

Ecological situation

It is not even worth discussing the benefits of living in this particular residential complex. The distance from the metropolis, from the Moscow Ring Road, the location near Butovo Forest Park - all this makes it possible for residents of the complex to inhale clean air, not exhaust gases. Kaluga highway passes nearby, but the harm from it is incommensurably less than, for example, from millions of cars in the capital.

About the developer

The developer of LCD Garden Garden Edalgo is the KROST concern. This company has existed for a long time and was one of the first to appear on the building construction market. KROST mainly implements the city-in-city projects. In Moscow and the Moscow region in various places you can see the projects of the company already implemented. At the same time, only the most advanced technologies and all the best achievements in this area are used in construction.

The designers of the concern were the first to propose and began to implement the concept of “a courtyard without cars”, which allows you to protect the leisure of children in the yard and turns it into a truly paradise, where you can safely walk without breathing exhaust fumes. In addition, when designing, an indispensable condition is the landscape decoration of courtyards, which allows the developer to provide their future customers with maximum comfort.

The embodiment of the ideas of the developer

The developer pays special attention to the issue of parenting. Therefore, together with residential buildings, the plans of the complexes at the same time include equipping them with innovative kindergartens and schools. Moreover, the latter are built with bright facades that attract attention, with bright spacious rooms, equipped with the most modern technological devices. Together, this is intended to develop the child's interest in the educational and cognitive processes.

Over the entire period of its existence in the construction market, the KROST concern has established itself as a reliable partner, embodying highly successful projects. Every year, companies are awarded numerous prizes and awards. To date, more than 450 objects have already been commissioned. These are prestigious residential complexes, and business centers, and industrial facilities, and modern kindergartens and schools, and sports complexes, even churches of the Russian Orthodox Church.





The total number of areas sold by the company is more than 4.5 million square meters. The company operates in three areas: construction and industrial complexes and development of residential and commercial real estate. Among the partners of the concern are the world's largest architects and architectural bureaus: Rem Koolhaas (Netherlands), Zaha Hadid Architects (Great Britain), Konstantina Cusack (Great Britain), Evan K Marshall (Great Britain) and many others. The main postulate of the concern is to be the first and work ahead of the curve. Its specialists create trends and offer new solutions. A focus on innovation and international experience has enabled the company to set the bar in the construction industry for more than two and a half decades.
