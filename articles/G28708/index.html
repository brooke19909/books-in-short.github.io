Grand Troyka Hotel 3 is a good option for a budget holiday

Description: Going to rest in Turkey, you make a choice in favor of this or that hotel. In this sunny country there are many places to stay, from small and comfortable hotels to luxurious five-star villas.

Grand Troyka Hotel 3 is located near the city of Alanya, 150 km from the airport and only 90 meters from the sea. It will amaze you with its location on the mountain, where it forms a small town, and the room offers a wonderful view of the nature of Turkey.





Rooms: The Grand Troyka Hotel was opened relatively long ago, but now thousands of tourists are resting in it. It began operations in 2000, and was restored in 2008. Its area is 3200 m¬≤. The three-star hotel consists of 2 6-storey buildings and 110 standard rooms.

As for the rooms of Grand Troyka Hotel 3, in them you will find a shower, air conditioning, hairdryer, TV with two Russian channels and a telephone. For a fee, a safe and minibar are also available. Room cleaning will be done three times a week, and bed linen will be changed for vacationers twice in seven days.

Meals: As for food, Grand Troyka Hotel 3, like most hotels in Turkey, has an all-inclusive (AI) concept. The food is quite varied: guests will be offered first and second courses, as well as dessert and fruits, various drinks. On site there is a restaurant where you can have breakfast, lunch, dinner, and three incendiary bars. If something was not enough for you, you can buy everything in the store - it is located right there.



Beach: The beach is nearby. There, a bar, sun loungers are provided for vacationers, and towels, mattresses and umbrellas can be purchased for a fee.

Additional information: You can have fun at Grand Troyka Hotel 3 not only on the beach, but also near two pools, on a large water slide. For children, a playground and two small pools are intended, and in the evening kids are entertained by funny animators. For an additional fee, you can visit the Turkish bath, have a massage, go to the jacuzzi, or play volleyball, the playground for which is located on the beach. Table tennis is also available on site.

Reviews: The hotel is ideal for families. It is inexpensive and comfortable. About Grand Troyka Hotel 3 reviews you can find the most diverse, but the main thing is that for a little money you will settle in a comfortable hotel. The rooms will be clean, the food is simple but tasty, there is no need to wait for a too varied menu, but you can choose excellent dishes from what the administration offers tourists.

The only thing may be some shortcomings during the arrival at the hotel, because the flow of tourists is very large - vacationers are constantly moving in and out. But the staff will do everything possible so that you do not feel any inconvenience - the service here is at a good level.





The presented hotel is also convenient for those who want only to spend the night and have a meal, and spend all their free time at sea, go on long excursions, walk around night establishments, in general, have a rest "in full". From the hotel you can go to the water park, go to major cities such as Alanya. The main thing, as many vacationers write, is with whom you spend your vacation: in a pleasant company of friends or with your beloved family.
