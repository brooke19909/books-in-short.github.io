How to make mastic yourself?

If there are no specialized pastry shops nearby, but you really want to learn the art of decorating cakes with mastic, do not despair! You can make it yourself, but the base for this will be a marshmallow chewing souffle. The most common brand of this dessert that can be purchased in almost any grocery market is Bon Paris.





Yes, it is precisely these viscous sweet candies that we will need. A pair of sachets with a total weight of 180 grams can be purchased for 110-120 rubles, and the finished mastic as a result of this amount will turn out 900-1000 grams! As you can see, the savings are obvious. But this is not the only plus of homemade mastic. Many people consider it tastier than a purchased competitor.

So, do you need to decorate the cake with mastic with your own hands? We will tell you how to make this product yourself using marshmallows. Everything is very simple. You need to put the souffle in a bowl, melt it to a viscous state in the microwave, add 2 tablespoons of room temperature butter to it, a couple of teaspoons of natural lemon juice and mix it all well. After this, you need to add one tablespoon of powdered sugar to the mass (stock up on it, you will need a total of 500-700 grams of this product) until it acquires the consistency of batter. After this, the future mastic must be kneaded (like a regular dough). Homemade wonderful product is ready! It is necessary to store such mastic in the same way as purchased mastic - always in cellophane film and in the refrigerator.

Secrets of working with mastic

Each confectioner has his own unique and original ways of working. We will tell you about the basic and well-known. Knowing these secrets, you can even more easily create jewelry from mastic with your own hands.

  1. In order for the pre-cut parts from mastic not to dry before time, cover them with polyethylene.

  2. If you want to color the mastic, use gel dyes, not dry ones. They already have a liquid texture, so using them is easier to color the product evenly.

  3. After your decoration is ready and dried, to give it a more saturated color and shine, hold it for 10 seconds over a saucepan with steam.

  4. If you want to give the jewelry a glossy shine, arm yourself with a brush and a mixture of vodka with honey in a 1: 1 ratio. Brush your masterpiece. Do not worry, the smell of vodka will evaporate, and the mastic will look smooth and shiny.

  5. If the mastic is dry, just send it for 5-10 seconds in the microwave, and then knead thoroughly.

  6. In order not to overdo it with the color intensity when staining the product, use a toothpick. With its help, it is very easy to intervene the dye in the smallest doses and control the process.

  7. Mastic can be cut with ordinary scissors. With their help, you can, for example, cut cloves on a leaf of rose.

Do-it-yourself jewelry from mastic. Workshop using silicone molds

The easiest way is to make some figure out of mastic using silicone mold. With him, everything becomes easier and faster. Molds can be large and small. With their help, you can make 2d-decorations (in which only the "front" part is created) and 3d-figures. In the latter case, a complete product is obtained, beautiful from all sides.

Let's take a step-by-step look at how to make jewelry from mastic with your own hands using silicone molds? For example, create a colorful 2-d flower.

We will need:

  • Mastic of 3 different colors.
  • 2-d mold.
  • A confectionery tool in the form of a narrowed ‚Äúshoulder blade‚Äù (but you can do without it).

The manufacturing process is very simple.

  1. Take the mastic of the first color. Fill it with a piece of the bottom hole of the mold, pressing it with your fingers and a spatula on all sides. You need to be sure that the mastic has completely filled the entire capacity, without leaving a single mm of free space. Only under this condition will the figure have a high-quality performance.
  2. Take a piece of mastic of a different color, in our case - light green. Fill them with the middle part of the mold.
  3. Take a blue piece of mastic, for convenience it can be folded into a circle and fill the rest of the mold.
  4. Press the mastic well. If there is a surplus, they just need to be carefully cut with a knife.
  5. Place the mold in the freezer for 3-5 minutes.

  6. Very carefully remove the resulting figure from the mold, stretching its walls.
  7. Leave the decoration in the open air until it dries completely.

Just like that, within 10 minutes and with the help of silicone mold you can make a beautiful flower of mastic yourself .
