A little bit about the company. The origins

Strictly speaking, Nokia was founded back in 1865. But then she was engaged in the production of paper and cardboard. A little later, the manufacturer mastered the production of a variety of rubber products and some simple electronics. But the company's work in the direction of high technology began only in the 80s of the last century. Then Nokia decided to start producing computers. But something went wrong with them, and the production (along with patents) was sold to the Japanese giant Fujitsu. After that, the company came to grips with mobile devices. And then their business went. Proof of this is the many very popular phones from Nokia. But Nokia 216 Dual Sim, reviews of which we will analyze a little later, is the brightest representative of quality products of the Finnish manufacturer.





Continued development

I must say that the release of Nokia 216 Dual Sim, the feedback from many users about which are favored, marks a return to the glorious tradition of Nokia. The fame of her phones boomed around the world. And now the legendary manufacturer decided to return and surprise everyone again. But Nokia will succeed, because this company has behind it - solid experience in the manufacture of mobile devices and more than a century-long history of existence.
