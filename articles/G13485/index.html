Hotels in Liski: where to stay in the city

The city of Liski is located in the Voronezh region and is best known for the fact that there is a beautiful Lake Bogatoe, which is the old man of the Don. Back in the Middle Ages, a watch post was founded on the shores of this reservoir. Here is also located the nature reserve "Divnogorye" - a unique open-air museum. You can stay in Liski for the night in one of several hotels.

How many hotels in Liski

Attention arriving to the city offers a variety of hotels in Liski. Accommodation prices vary, as well as living conditions.

In Liski there are inexpensive hostels, and cozy guest houses, and comfortable hotels, and even a sanatorium. In total, in a small city with a population of only 54 thousand people (data for 2017) there are about ten accommodation options, which is a lot.

Mini-hotels of Liski

Mini-hotel "Granat" is located near the city bus station. It offers comfortable two-room apartments that can accommodate up to four guests. In a clean kitchen with modern equipment is everything you need for cooking.





One kilometer from the city center is the modern Cranes Hostel. This democratic hotel in Liski offers everything for a comfortable stay: clean rooms, Internet access, separate bathroom, pleasant service. Moreover, prices here start at 500 rubles per bed, and for 1400 you can rent a whole double room. Cranes are located in the new microdistrict of the city, where there are all the necessary grocery and other shops.

Hotels and guest houses

Barocco LV Hotel is a luxury hotel in Liski. It has beautifully decorated rooms with modern furniture and renovation. Payment by card is possible. This hotel is located just a few hundred meters from the city center next to the Don Shopping Center . Guests are offered welcome coffee at the bar, the rooms have pleasant things in the form of a bottle of water or the possibility of ordering breakfast in the room. There is free wireless internet throughout the hotel, as well as ample parking.

Hotel RaDom is located just four minutes from the beach. This modern and very stylish hotel with friendly hosts offers rooms for two and three people. Breakfast is available at an additional cost. It has its own kitchenette. The peculiarity of the hotel is that it is rather a large comfortable guest house, rather than a hotel. It offers access to a spacious garden and breakfast in the gazebo in the fresh air.





Dog and cat owners will be pleased to know that pets are allowed (and it's free). However, with regard to the minuses of "RaDom", it should be noted that despite its name, consonant with the word "nearby", this hotel in Liski for transit travelers is not very suitable, as it is located at a decent distance from the highway.

Also near the city center is the Hotel Noi. This relatively small hotel in Liski can accommodate 23 people. It is a five minute walk from the train station. Two, three, four and even six-bed rooms are available. Can accommodate a separate bed for a child. Noah has its own garden and terrace, where you can relax in the summer heat. The rooms are equipped with new furniture, LCD TVs (cable TV).





Other accommodation options in the city

In addition to hotels, Liski also has a Radon sanatorium. It offers comfortable rooms with cable TV. Buffet breakfast is available. In addition to medical and cosmetic procedures, guests are offered all kinds of entertainment, such as: ping pong, billiards, rent a bike, ski and other equipment, karaoke, bar.
