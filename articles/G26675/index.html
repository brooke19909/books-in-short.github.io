Insectarium

When traveling around Montreal, be sure to check out the insectarium. It is one of the largest insect museums in North America. The collection today has about one hundred and sixty thousand insects (living and dried artifacts). The museum has its own anthill; bumblebees and bees live in the hives. The inhabitants of the insectarium live in their aquariums, which are securely closed, so that bugs can be viewed quite calmly, without fear for their life and health.
