Medina Solaria Thalasso 5 (Tunisia, Hammamet): photos and reviews of tourists

Many people dream of a vacation on the Mediterranean Sea, in some good hotel complex, which would have reasonable prices. And there is such an option. The hotel is called Medina Solaria Thalasso 5 *. This hotel is an all-inclusive type, but this fact is not its only advantage.





Location

Medina Solaria Thalasso 5 * is located in Tunisia, to be more precise, in Hammamet. This is a popular resort city located in the same bay of the Mediterranean Sea. The beach from the hotel is close at hand, but the Enfida Airport is far away - about 75 kilometers.

But in the immediate vicinity are local attractions. This is the medina of Yasmin, a large shopping center of Costa, a religious museum, the port of Hammamet and much more. If you want to go somewhere far away, then it will not be difficult, since the bus stop is one hundred meters from the hotel building.

The hotel itself is visible from afar. The building, built in the national style, successfully fits into the surrounding landscape. The hotel is only 10 years old. And, importantly, it is part of the famous Spanish hotel chain Iberostar, widely known throughout the world. And this fact already speaks of how high the level of service is.

Service

Medina Solaria Thalasso 5 * has all the standard and additional amenities. It has free Wi-Fi, parking nearby, a currency exchange and car rental service, and a 24-hour reception. If guests come with children, they will be offered the services of a nanny and a mini-club.

There is also a laundry room, several shops on site, a business center, a conference room and five rooms for meetings and negotiations.

It is also worth noting that the hotel will be convenient for guests with disabilities. For them there are separate apartments in which everything is adapted to individual requirements.

The staff, by the way, speaks not only Arabic, but also English, French and Dutch. And although this is not stated in the description, guests resting here assure that some employees speak Russian too.

Bored, being in Medina Solaria Thalasso 5 *, it definitely will not work. Because there are many ways to have fun. Of course, there is a large swimming pool and a terrace on which guests sunbathe. There is also a tennis court and a gym with modern fitness equipment.
