Ibis Hotel (Samara) - a comfortable 3-star hotel for business people

Hotel "Ibis" (Samara) is very popular among our compatriots. People come here who want to get acquainted with the original culture of the city, see outstanding sights and spend time in a comfortable environment. The hotel was built in 2005 near the railway station, entertainment facilities and the promenade.





The road to the complex from Kurumoch Airport will not take more than half an hour, which is a huge advantage for most tourists. If desired, you can use the paid transfer. The three-star Ibis Hotel (Samara) is convenient for business travelers, couples with children and the elderly. The hotel works according to the European quality standard and offers comfortable conditions for a good rest.

Guests arriving by their own transport may not have to wonder where to put it, as the complex has a guarded parking lot (free of charge). Near the hotel there are many interesting objects of the city: the Mega shopping gallery, the Victoria water park, the Peter and Paul Church, the circus and more. Within walking distance there is a tram and bus stop. Just 2 km - the center of Samara, 9.5 km - River port.

The hotel operates year-round. The Ibis Hotel (Samara) has an official website on which you can book rooms. Arriving here in the summer, you can take a walk along the picturesque promenade, take a dip in the Volga River, enjoy water transport and sunbathe. In winter, the hotel offers no less interesting entertainments: fishing, skiing, ice skating, sleigh rides, horseback riding - an active and fiery rest is provided for you.





Number of rooms

All 205 rooms are equipped with comfortable furniture and necessary equipment. Each room has a private bathroom with hygiene products. In the presence of air conditioning. Free internet access.

An LCD TV with satellite channels is featured. There is a safe, direct dial telephone and radio. Cots are available for children. All rooms are soundproofed so that noisy neighbors will not bother you.

Nutrition





Hotel "Ibis" (Samara) offers delicious full breakfasts, which are served in the restaurant as a "buffet". The cost of food is calculated separately - at will. The hotel has 2 restaurants and a 24-hour bar. The establishments serve gourmet Mediterranean cuisine. Connoisseurs of elite alcohol will appreciate a wide range of products.

Beach

The city beach is 10 minutes from the complex. The coast is rocky, so it is recommended to wear special shoes.





Additional Information

The Ibis Hotel (Samara) has a dry cleaning and laundry department on its territory. In addition, there is an exchange office and shoe shine. Tourists who combine work with leisure can use the business center at any time. The hotel staff can help organize any event.

It will not be difficult to hold a large-scale meeting or a business meeting in any of the three conference rooms (up to 130 people). All rooms are equipped with modern equipment, wireless internet, a projector, a microphone and lighting.

You are waiting for an unforgettable vacation and a lot of fun. You can get to the tourist center by car, train or fly by plane to Samara, and then transfer by taxi or bus and get to Novo-Sadovaya street, 160 - this is a legal address. Ibis Hotel receives positive reviews from customers. Many tourists decided that they would return here again.
