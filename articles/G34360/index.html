How to get there

The nearest airport, where planes fly from Russia, is located 10 km from Antalya and 55 km from Britannia Hotel 3 *. Vacationers are delivered to the hotel by transfers of travel agencies, where vouchers were purchased.

Many tourists who have already been to Turkey more than once come to Kemer without using the services of intermediaries. Another way to get to the hotel on your own is to rent a car directly at the airport. Even if this is your first time in Turkey, getting to Kemer is very easy, as there are many signs along the road. Sea, weather in Kemer

If you are going to Turkey for a vacation, it does not hurt to know in advance what the weather is like. This country is located in the Mediterranean, and the climate here is mostly warm, especially in Kemer. The city grew on the seashore. In summer, it slightly reduces heat, and in winter it helps to keep the air warmer. Containing the flow of cold air and the Taurus Mountains, encircling Kemer from the north and covered with dense coniferous forests. Therefore, in March during the daytime in sunny weather, the thermometer can give numbers above +20, but at night it is still very cool, often near zero, and the water temperature even on the coast is not higher than +16. April is even warmer. Even at night in Kemer, the air temperature does not drop below +9, but swimming in the sea is still cold. But starting in May, the beach season can be opened, since the water by the end of the last spring month has time to warm up to +22. The beach season lasts until mid-October. The weather in Kemer in the summer is mostly hot, on some days the thermometer can fly up to the level of +40 and above, but the fresh sea breeze and the aromas of pine needles help not to notice such high temperatures.

The hotel in relation to the sea is located quite convenient. Although you need to go to the beach for a few minutes, but the guests' path does not tire, as it lies in a beautiful shady area. The Britannia has no beach. But its owners have an agreement with the Olympus Hotel, which has its own, and a very good stretch of coast. In addition, tourists can relax on the city beaches. The coating on most of them is pebbles of various fractions, but entry into the water is convenient. Sun beds and umbrellas are everywhere paid, from 1 cu for all day.

A lot of beach lovers in Turkey prefer to go to Kemer in September. The high season at this time has already been certified, so the prices for tours begin to fall significantly, and in hotels and on the beach there is not a large influx of people. The weather in the first month of autumn is still excellent, there is almost no rain, the water in the sea is up to +24, and the air warms up to +30 and even higher. Shopping lovers will especially enjoy their vacation in September, since at this time, prices for goods are reduced by 2-3 times.
