DIY Christmas masks

New Year is the most magical holiday. It is on this night that we wait for Santa Claus, make wishes and exchange gifts. So why not go on this holiday to a masquerade or arrange a costume evening on your own? After all, carnival costumes and New Year's masks with their own hands are very easy to do.

What are carnival masks



A carnival mask can cover the entire face or half of it, be fixed on the head or have a wand for carrying in hands. These accessories are flat and convex, as for design - everything depends entirely on the imagination of the manufacturer.

Simple Christmas masks for children can be made of cardboard, colored paper, foil and other materials. As a template, you can use the part from the old carnival costume, or you can find a stencil.

Another option is to measure the face of the model and arbitrarily draw your own shape. In order for the mask to hold firmly to the head, an elastic band or ties should be attached to it.

Involve your kids in the process of making new clothes. You can trust the smallest to color the cardboard and stick elements from colored paper.
