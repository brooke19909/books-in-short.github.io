Hotel "Brothers Karamazov": description and reviews of tourists

Choosing a good vacation destination is of utmost importance. Distinctive features

The Brothers Karamazov Hotel hospitably opened its doors to guests in the historical center of the city in 2004 (the last renovation was held in 2015). F. M. Dostoevsky's house-museum is very close (a 10-minute walk). The founders gave the name to the hotel, apparently inspired by the novel of the same name by the writer. Today, hotel rooms are booked with enviable regularity, and the hotel is among the ten leaders in popularity among tourists. Judging by the rating, the "Brothers Karamazov" fully justify their 4 * category.

The building itself is very modern, but the interior interior recreates the atmosphere of the second half of the 19th century, some rooms have elegant antique furniture. Impeccable service, well-trained staff, sensitive and attentive attitude towards its guests - this is what distinguishes the hotel from other numerous hotels in the Northern capital.

Convenient location

Sotsialisticheskaya Street is located in the central district of the city, it is a very convenient place to start exploring the main attractions of St. Petersburg. For example, from here it is easy to get to the arrow of Vasilyevsky Island, the Hermitage and the Winter Palace. Three metro stations are located nearby ("Vladmirskaya", "Dostoevskaya" and "Zvenigorodskaya"). The exact address of the Hotel Brothers Karamazov is well known to frequent guests and residents of the northern capital.

The road from Pulkovo airport will take no more than 10 minutes. And the distance from Vitebsky and Moskovsky stations to Socialist street is about 2 km.

Choose a number

It should be noted right away that all the rooms of the Brothers Karamazov Hotel are equipped with air conditioning, minibars, telephones, satellite TV, free Wi-Fi, modern bathrooms with showers. For young guests (up to two years old) free accommodation is provided with a cot. Children who are not yet 12 years old can also live in a room with their parents and go to breakfast without paying for an extra bed.

You can choose a standard (single or double) room or an improved category. Some of them, as mentioned above, are furnished with antique furniture in the spirit of the 19th century, heavy curtains made of expensive fabrics hang on the windows, combining in color with the bedspreads. In others, you will find a completely modern setting, made in the style of classical minimalism.

There are bridal suites that are very popular with couples. The reviews of the Brothers Karamazov Hotel note that they have a sophisticated and romantic setting by candlelight, a huge bed and great views. As a compliment, many newlyweds are brought champagne and fruit to the room before arrival. Having reserved the service "brunch", you can sleep until lunch.

Exclusive

Separately, it is worth noting the business card of the hotel - interior rooms. These are large and spacious rooms, designed in the style of the XIX century. Everything here is saturated with grace and harmony from tapestries to tea sets. The numbers are named the same as the names of the female characters of Fyodor Mikhailovich (Grushenka, Aglaya, Nastasya Filippovna, etc.).

Superior rooms are more spacious (26 m 2 ), mostly with garden views. The room is divided into a sleeping area, hotel and work. After long walks in the city, you can relax in front of a flat-screen TV. A night spent on a comfortable bed with an orthopedic mattress will give strength for a new day. The bathroom includes a hairdryer, a white bathrobe, slippers and free toiletries for guests.

Food for every taste

The restaurant of the same name has a chamber atmosphere, a large selection of dishes of Russian and European cuisine and excellent service. You can start the morning with strong coffee, which is very tasty here. For those who like to soak in bed, breakfast is delivered to your room. The reviews note that waiting for a delicious treat takes no more than 10 minutes.

Meals at the Brothers Karamazov hotel are carried out according to the Russian system of buffets, which is beloved by the Russians. There is a choice of dietary and vegetarian dishes on the menu. It is open for breakfast, lunch and dinner from early morning to late evening. Vacationers really liked meat dishes, grilled vegetables, desserts. If you do not have time to eat at the hotel, the friendly staff can pack a meal with you.

In the evening, you can pass the time in a cozy and stylish bar or on the summer terrace with a glass of wine. The range of drinks will satisfy even the most sophisticated tastes of visitors.

In the banquet hall of the restaurant often hold celebrations, celebrate significant dates. Judging by the reviews, the design and maintenance are not satisfactory.

Top level service

For the convenience of guests, the reception is open 24 hours a day, express check-in is possible. It is necessary to discuss in advance too early or later the time of your arrival in the city, so that the staff will prepare a room at the right time. Visitors with pets will be welcome here (subject to prior arrangement). Tourists traveling by car can leave it in the private secure parking of the Brothers Karamazov Hotel (200 rubles / day). If you wish, you can rent a car by filling out the necessary documents at the hotel.

The administration provides excursion services, will gladly tell you where the main attractions of the city are located, and will help you decide on a place to visit. The Brothers Karamazov Hotel in St. Petersburg offers laundry and dry cleaning services. Vacationers can use the safe and luggage storage, book the necessary tickets.

Focus on business travel

The hotel can be positioned as a business hotel that meets all international standards of service quality. Business travelers have the opportunity to work in a room at a computer desk, which is located in the working area of ‚Äã‚Äãthe room. For holding trainings, business events, meetings and negotiations, a conference room is equipped with everything necessary. Partners can conveniently sit around a round table, watch a presentation on a large screen through a DVD projector.

The business center is equipped with everything necessary for sending a fax, photocopying documents. The business part of the city is in close proximity, so you do not have to spend a lot of time on the road, getting to the right office.

What else can they offer here

The service of the Brothers Karamazov hotel includes transfer services. They pay separately and make a reservation individually with each guest. Guests are warned in advance that the area is a non-smoking area. For people with disabilities there are all conditions for living.

If you decide to book a room in this hotel for your foreign friend, then he will not have problems communicating with staff. The staff is fluent in English, French and Spanish. Visa support is provided here, administrators will help to correctly fill out all the necessary documents. The cost of the service is 900 rubles. By the way, the hotel has translation guides who can accompany foreign guests throughout the trip.

When booking a room from the hotel‚Äôs official website, discounts up to 40% apply, which is incredibly pleasing to vacationers. There is also an interesting rate "Business trip" - at a reduced cost they offer a room with half board (breakfast and dinner are included in the price).

Walk around

Experienced tourists are advised to start sightseeing with a walk along Nevsky Prospect. It is a 15-20 minute leisurely step. The road from the Admiralty to Uprising Square is remembered for a lifetime. Here, literally every building is shrouded in its own unique history. "Passage", Literary Cafe, Kazan Cathedral and much, much more will meet along the way of this main pedestrian artery of the Northern capital.

You should definitely visit the Church of the Icon of the Vladimir Mother of God, the Dostoevsky House Museum. A little further is the Alexandria Theater, St. Isaac's Cathedral, the Russian Museum. At 3 km from the hotel there is the famous cruiser Aurora, as well as the Peter and Paul Fortress, full of mysterious and dark stories. By the way, tickets to all museums, theaters, concert halls of the city can also be booked without leaving the hotel.

What hotel guests remember

People resting in the Northern capital know that it is very difficult to stay in the center and enjoy the silence. However, the creators of the hotel succeeded - the building stands in a courtyard isolated from the noisy Socialist Street. In this cozy corner among the green trees you can have a great time, protecting yourself from the hustle and bustle of the big city.

The guests were satisfied with the "museum" cleanliness of the hotel. In the reviews they write that cleaning was carried out daily, towels were changed and personal hygiene items were added as necessary. When describing the rooms of the Brothers Karamazov Hotel, guests emphasize that they create the most comfortable living conditions. All the equipment worked perfectly, climate control coped with its tasks, hot water was working properly.

A variety of breakfasts are included in the hotel, which are included in the price. Here you can enjoy delicious cereals, granola, pastries. The guests mention that all the food was very fresh and of high quality. Vacationers recommend definitely try amazing dishes on the chef's menu. The dishes are sustained in the best Russian traditions of the 19th century.

The hotel staff receives only the most flattering reviews. Tourists appreciated the attentive and friendly attitude, unobtrusive service, prompt solution of minor problems that arose during the stay.

Many guests find the acceptable cost of accommodation (approximately 2500-7500 rubles / day), noting the excellent price / quality ratio.

There are always dissatisfied

Despite the very high rating of the hotel (4.92 out of 5 possible), there were guests whose accommodation caused a number of complaints. Some visitors were unhappy with the too dark and gloomy rooms. Indeed, the interior rooms, named after the characters of the novel by Dostoevsky ("Katerina", "Netochka Nezvanova"), turn out to be an "amateur".

Not surprisingly, the business hotel has absolutely nothing to entertain children. However, couples note this as a drawback. In other reviews, they were skeptical and suspicious of breakfasts, guessing yesterday's products in them. Soundproofing in the rooms was not very good.

Summarize

If you are planning a business trip or just a trip to St. Petersburg for sightseeing, then you should pay attention to the described hotel. Convenient location in a quiet location near the historical center of the Northern capital, high-quality food, excellent number of rooms, a wide range of services are the main advantages of this accommodation facility. A small but comfortable hotel will leave only positive emotions about a trip to St. Petersburg (this is verified information compiled from numerous reviews of real hotel customers over the past 2-3 years).
