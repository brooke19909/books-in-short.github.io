Autumn crafts: do-it-yourself exclusive

A person always seeks to surround himself with beautiful things, using ready-made works of art and folk art. DIY handmade craftsmanship can also transform any room and become an exclusive element of the interior. It evokes pleasant memories associated with relaxation and nature, and serves as the embodiment of the author‚Äôs creative imagination.





Autumn crafts from natural material

Each time of the year can inspire a person to create a creative masterpiece that is performed independently or in company with children and other family members. But autumn, painted in bright colors, is perhaps the most suitable time for such activities.

Necessary materials

At this time of the year you can stock up with bright leaves, twigs, seeds, nuts, berries and fruits. Flowers and cones are also ideal for embodying the creative imagination of the author of the future composition. But for the proper use of these materials, they must be prepared: clean and dry well. Leaves can be ironed through a thin cloth or paper - this procedure will remove excess moisture and allow you to maintain its original shape. A good and seed-free pumpkin is one of the most popular elements from which autumn crafts are made. A photo of one of them, by the way, is presented in this article.





To make the composition look more impressive, it is allowed to use bright foil, varnish with sequins, rhinestones and beads. Natural fabrics and wood can serve as the basis or one of the elements of the composition. Acorns, cones and nuts are covered with acrylic paint, observing moderation. An autumn craftsmanship made by oneself should look natural, and an excessive amount of artificial materials can disrupt natural harmony.

The most common forms of compositions

Recently, wreaths of dried herbs decorated with flowers, acorns or cones have been very popular. They can be placed on the front door or walls of the room.



Do-it-yourself autumn handmade crafts are often performed in the form of a bouquet: in it colorful leaves are collected in buds and fastened with wire or hot glue to natural branches. As the basis for such a composition can serve as foam or foam. The bouquet can be placed in a ceramic, wooden or glass vase.

Frames for photographs and paintings will also become more aesthetic if they are decorated with natural materials. The panel or application of seeds, leaves and acorns looks very impressive, it can be placed in the kitchen, in the hallway or presented as a presentation to a friend.

Original candlesticks decorated with natural materials are deservedly popular among lovers of a romantic setting. Spicy herbs used in the design of such a common piece of furniture will be able to flavor the air in the room.

It should be noted that the handmade DIY autumn craft should be medium in size, since a large composition is very difficult to preserve, while small ones do not always look aesthetically pleasing.
