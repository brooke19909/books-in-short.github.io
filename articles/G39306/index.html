What is monetization of mobile traffic?

If you want to monetize traffic, it will be useful for you to learn how to do it on mobile devices. Mobile traffic makes up one third of all existing in the network, and this number is growing every day. As more and more people spend their time on smartphones, the emphasis is gradually shifting from desktop computers to mobile devices.

However, most users are still shopping on computers. Does this mean that e-commerce should focus more on sites for PC? Does this affect the monetization of mobile traffic?

First you need to see how users behave on each device. People make fewer purchases on mobile devices. According to experts, almost twice as many users make purchases and visit information sites from a PC or laptop. Despite the low level of purchases from mobile devices, the rate of transition to advertising on them is higher than on computers. And it turns out profitable if you make money on ads from your site.

What kind of advertising can be used for profit? Monetization of mobile traffic with payment may include the use of the following services.

CPI and CPA

CPA is a pricing model where the advertiser pays for a certain action, such as registering, selling in a store, etc. CPI is a more accurate version of CPA in which payment occurs when the user installs the application.

The first option of advertising is paid three to four times more than the second, depending on the region. This means that if you use only CPI offers, you will lose profits dramatically.

Despite the fact that most advertising networks out there make a lot of money in the CPI business, it is advisable to choose a niche for yourself in CPA. The main action of these links is to encourage users to subscribe to a paid service, thanks to which they will receive excellent mobile content, such as video, ringtones, games, wallpapers, etc. The peculiarity of these subscription offers is that they are designed to work on mobile devices. Thus, the placement of such ads on your site is the monetization of mobile traffic with pay-per-action.

Ad space

The traditional advertising model, which dates back to paper newspapers, is to sell advertising space and embed ads in content. The same model is used for television with 10-minute commercials for every 20 minutes of broadcasting. Since some time, even videos on various hosting companies have received income from the placement of such advertising. The Internet has been using this model for the past 15 years, and revenues from it have reached tens of billions of dollars.

This method can also be used on a mobile platform, and it can generate real income. By placing small ads at the top or bottom of the screen, the advertiser will receive referrals and traffic to their sites. It turns out monetization of mobile traffic with pay per click. However, the main drawback of this approach is that creating an attractive ad on a small screen is much more difficult. Application developers want to make money, but do not always want to sacrifice too much screen space, which can interfere with the application.

Affiliate and referral programs

Another monetization idea recently includes affiliate marketing with mobile apps. In fact, instead of selling the advertising space, the advertiser offers an affiliate program that contains links embedded in the application. They will generate revenue for the creator of the service based on the recommendation. In affiliate programs, the advertiser pays for the sales facts that are generated by the affiliate. The system can track these "clicks", and if the consumer goes to the advertiser's website and makes a purchase, the application developer receives a commission.
