Moscow Hotels 5 stars: addresses, descriptions, reviews

Moscow 5-star hotels are an important component of the tourist infrastructure of the Russian capital. On average, visits to Moscow by guests increase annually by 10-12%. In 2016, over 17.5 million guests visited Belokamennaya.

Approximately 36% of the tourist flow comes from business tourism. Guests arriving for leisure are traditionally interested in Red Square, the Kremlin, Kazan and St. Basil's Cathedrals, the Bolshoi Theater, the Tretyakov Gallery, the Moscow City complex, the zoo, the Moskvarium, interactive parks, and the Moscow circus. The ideal starting point for such excursions are fashionable hotels located in the city center.

26% of all tourists in the capital of Russia are guests from abroad who prefer 5 star hotels to stay in Moscow. The truth of these words is emphasized by one fact: during the imposition of sanctions against Turkey, the Red Hills Hotel was forced to temporarily suspend its work.

Among the annually growing flows of foreign visitors to Moscow, the largest share (about 40%) falls on guests from China. Although among the foreigners visiting Belokamennaya, so far the representatives of European states dominate: Germany, Greece, Italy, the Czech Republic and France, however, in the future, residents of Asia will dominate.

Five star hotels in Moscow. Specifications

According to the law dated 05.02.2018 on the full certification of Russian hotels until 2021, criteria for the highest level of service, which 5-star Moscow hotels must meet, are identified. The list includes:

  • 24-hour reception;
  • in all rooms - bathrooms;
  • round-the-clock cold and hot water supply;
  • the presence of an autonomous generator of electricity;
  • more than 5% of the rooms are in the highest category;
  • daily change of linen and towels, as well as cleaning;
  • international communication and Internet in rooms;
  • restaurant, cafe, night club;
  • business center;
  • swimming pool;
  • gym;
  • the choice of the type of food the guest;
  • parking;
  • dry cleaning and laundry services;
  • safekeeping services;
  • tourist services.

Hotel Metropol. History and Modernity

The Metropol Hotel is a unique decoration of the Theater Square of the capital. Moscow received it thanks to the patron and industrialist Savva Mamontov (years of construction - 1899-1905th). The architects V. Valkot, N. Shevyakov, L. Kekushev worked on the classic modern look of the building along Teatralny Proezd, 2.

The intricate interiors of the hotel were designed by V. Vesnin, I. Zholtovsky, A. Erichson. Interior decor was created by K. Korovin, V. Vasnetsov. On the main facade is a majolica panel by M. Vrubel "Princess Dreams." Currently, the rooms are carefully restored and organically supplemented with the latest equipment.

At different times, Metropol in Moscow was chosen by B. Brecht, B. Shaw, M. Zedong, M. Dietrich, D. Armani, M. Jackson, K. Deneuve, Sh. Stone for residence. Lenin spoke here, and the king of Spain received visitors.
