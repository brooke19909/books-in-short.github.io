Aochalong Resort Villa Spa 3 * (Thailand, Phuket): hotel description, reviews

Resort Aochalong Resort Villa Spa 3 * is located in a beautiful and peaceful place, near the clean beach near Chalong Bay.





Brief information about the hotel

Aochalong Resort Villa Spa 3 * is a unique place where everything is saturated with the breath of the sea. You can enjoy the view of the ocean breeze, not only being on the beach, but also looking through the windows of your rooms. Even swimming in the pool, you will have the opportunity to admire the azure sea coast. Another feature of this hotel is that it is decorated in accordance with traditional Asian interior canons.

A lot of services are provided to guests by the Aochalong Resort Villa Spa 3 * hotel. The most popular and sought after, of course, is Thai massage. You can also use the wireless Internet absolutely free. Small electric cars ply the territory of the hotel, as well as outside it, which will quickly and comfortably deliver to the desired location.

In the Aochalong Resort Villa Spa 3 * hotel you have the opportunity to book a tourist trip to the sights with a guide or to make it yourself by renting a bicycle, motorcycle and car. Do not miss the opportunity to try the national Thai dishes served at the restaurant that operates at this hotel.





Resort Location

Resort Aochalong Villa Resort Spa 3 * is located right on the beach of the quiet and picturesque Chalong Bay. Also nearby is the port where you can look at luxury yachts. To get to the center of Phuket, it is worth driving just 10 minutes by bus or using other transport. From here it is very close to attractions such as the coral island, as well as the archipelagos of Koh Khai and Phang Nga.

Hotel Rooms

The hotel complex Aochalong Villa Resort Spa 3 * is quite small and has only 45 guest rooms. Tourists are offered the following accommodation options:

  • The standard double apartments are equipped with a king size bed. Some rooms overlook the hotel‚Äôs green exotic garden, while the rest offer you a beautiful and well-kept view of the pool in the courtyard.
  • Three varieties of villas are available for your attention. They are small and inside largely repeat the equipment of standard rooms. The difference is that some of the houses are located among the green spaces of the garden, and the rest are by the pool and in close proximity to the beach.

It is worth noting that the decoration of the rooms is quite modest and made in the traditional Asian style. The emphasis is on wooden furniture and decor.





What is provided in the rooms

Each of the rooms of the hotel complex Aochalong Villa Resort Spa (Phuket) offers the following amenities:

  • a prerequisite is the availability of air conditioning, which will help you create a cool climate in the room, which will hide from the Thai heat;
  • so that you do not get bored while in the room, you can watch a huge number of satellite channels (including Russian-language programs) on a modern TV;
  • the room has a refrigerator for storing food (since the ticket provides breakfast only, you will have to buy all other products yourself);
  • the bathroom is a toilet combined with a shower, where, among other things, there is a hairdryer and bath accessories;
  • a safe will allow you not to worry about the safety of your valuables and money during your absence in the room.




Additional Information

Hotel Aochalong Villa Resort Spa 3 * operates in accordance with the following list of internal rules:

  • Check-in is possible only after 14:00, but you must leave the apartment before noon (this rule applies regardless of how much you arrived at the hotel or what time your return flight is scheduled for);
  • for stays in rooms of children under 12 years (using existing beds) will be charged 200 baht per night;
  • an extra bed for both children and adults will cost 700 baht per day;
  • You have the opportunity to use international payment systems for cashless payments for services received;
  • In no case is it allowed to come to the hotel with pets or any other animals;
  • if necessary, you can be provided with a transfer to / from the airport (for a fee and by prior arrangement).




Positive reviews

Tourists only get a delightful stay and stay at the Aochalong Villa Resort Spa. The reviews are filled with positive comments, among which the following points are especially worth noting:

  • both in the adjacent territory and in the hotel itself, a very beautiful and interesting atmosphere, made in the Asian style;
  • breakfasts, although monotonous, but quite tasty and satisfying;
  • polite, responsible, helpful and very friendly staff;
  • almost every day, bed linen and bath towels are replaced;
  • live instrumental music is played in the restaurant every evening, and sometimes guest singers also perform;
  • throughout the hotel, as well as in the rooms, it is very quiet, which makes this place simply ideal for a relaxing pastime;
  • almost the entire courtyard of the hotel is planted with greenery and trees that attract exotic birds (it is very pleasant to listen to their singing in the early morning);
  • Each room has an electric kettle, as well as coffee and tea bags;
  • every day, the maids put in the refrigerator a bottle of drinking water per guest, regardless of whether the previous supplies were used up or not;
  • I especially want to note the hotel management, which is very attentive to all the nuances of the hotel and creates a very pleasant friendly atmosphere;
  • all the newlyweds at the hotel are given a pleasant surprise in the form of a romantic room decoration with flower petals and intricate figurines of towels;
  • there are two clean and well-kept swimming pools that completely compensate for the inability to swim on your own beach;
  • The massage parlor is open almost round the clock, so there is the opportunity to walk around enough, and then go through a session of relaxing procedures.




Negative reviews

Speaking about the quality of service at Aochalong Villa Resort 3 * (Phuket, Chalong), travelers note a number of negative points that spoil the impression of a vacation:

  • despite the fact that the hotel has its own beach, it is practically unsuitable for swimming due to the large number of sharp stones and the abundance of algae (it is better to go to public bathing places for this purpose);
  • very cramped rooms in which there is literally nowhere to turn around;
  • the resort staff do not speak Russian;
  • Despite the fact that advertisements declare a hairdryer in the bathroom, it is sometimes absent in some rooms.
