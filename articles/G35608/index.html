Philips Xenium X1560: specifications, instructions, reviews

The Philips Xenium X1560 mobile phone is literally a small power station in your hand. Not only does it have superior battery performance designed for long conversations, it can also recharge other smartphones by connecting them directly with a USB connection cable. With this device, you can literally feel the possible battery power.





The phone without recharging can work up to 100 days in standby mode, which seems incredible. In addition, on a single charge, it can last up to 40 hours of talk time.

Two SIM cards

The ability to use two SIM cards simultaneously is another undoubted advantage of this device. So, you can organize your personal and work affairs much better and save the necessary contacts separately, using two different phone numbers. With two SIM cards you do not need to carry 2 phones with you.

Screen features

Despite the fact that the Philips Xenium X1560 phone does not offer many additional features, its screen looks pretty attractive. With a small size of 2.4 inches, the TFT 262K QVGA display looks very good. Viewing images is pretty quick, and the pictures are displayed bright and colorful. At the same time, the response time for loading each image is minimal. The display resolution is 240 x 320 pixels, 16 million colors are available. For the device of the budget price category, these are quite good indicators. Available image compression formats are BMP, JPEG, GIF, PNG.





FM radio

The built-in antenna and external speaker features found on the Philips Xenium X1560 mobile phone mean you don‚Äôt have to be in the headphones all the time if you want to listen to the radio. Now you can enjoy broadcasts from your favorite radio stations at any volume while listening to them indoors or outdoors. Depending on your mood or circumstances, you can easily switch the listening mode to the headphones.

Bluetooth

This unit also supports Bluetooth A2DP profile. This will allow you to enjoy your favorite tunes without using wires. With the Philips Xenium X1560, you get the opportunity to listen to high-quality stereo music with Bluetooth-tuned headphones.





MicroSD card slot

A nice point is that you can increase the amount of memory for storing multimedia files by inserting a microSD memory card into the built-in slot of the phone.

Appearance

The Philips Xenium X1560 mobile phone has the following dimensions: height - 119.6 mm, width - 51.1 mm and thickness - 16.3 mm. The device weighs only 122 grams, and its compact size allows you to place it in any pocket. The device is available in only one color - black. Also, the phone is equipped with a built-in antenna, and its form factor is a candy bar.

The phone supports several GPRS (Rx Tx +) communication formats - class 12 and class B, as well as GSM with a frequency of 900, 1800, 1900 MHz. The formats for receiving and sending messages are also different: combined SMS (long SMS), SMS CB (Cell Broadcast), SMS (Short Message Service), multi-purpose SMS, MMS, multimedia messaging service. An additional feature is a network connection using WAP 2.0 technology. Of course, the Philips Xenium X1560 has very modest specifications, but for a budget device they are very good.

Sound

The sound playback capabilities on the device are very modest - an MP3 call is available, there is polyphony (64 tones). Supported audio formats: MP3, AMR, Midi, AAC, which can also be used as a ringtone for a call. The Philips Xenium X1560 phone (which reviews are positive) carries out sound recording in AMR format. It is possible to record your own voice, the sound is quite clear and loud.





Phone memory

The device supports micro SD cards with a maximum capacity of 8 GB. Philips Xenium X1560 practically does not have its own place to store data.

Communication

The gadget has a headset jack and allows you to use the hands-free device. Wireless communication is available in BluetoothV2.1 format, the following Bluetooth profiles are supported: A2DP, AVRCP, FTP, GAVDP, HFP, HSP, IOPT, OPP.

A wired connection is possible using a USB cable and is used to transfer MicroUSB data from the phone to a computer or other devices.





Buttons

The device is controlled using physical buttons. There is a 4-way navigation key and enter key, as well as an on / off button.

Call management

The menu has an archive of incoming and outgoing calls, including unanswered ones. There are call forwarding settings for incoming calls, which can be done in various ways. There are also options for call waiting and emergency calls. The sound signal during a call can be turned off or replaced with vibro, its volume is also adjusted. A digital clock showing the current time is displayed on the screen.

Applications

There are no built-in games on the phone. There are preinstalled applications such as a reminder, agenda, alarm clock, calculator, calendar, stopwatch. Various personalized screensavers, wallpapers and melodies are presented as personal and user settings. Also, the device is equipped with a built-in flashlight, which gives good lighting.

Text can be entered as a standard alphanumeric method, or using the T9 mode. The interface language can be changed at the request of the user. Available in a choice of Russian, English, Romanian and Ukrainian.





Power

As mentioned above, the phone has an ultra-powerful battery. In standby mode without recharging, the device is able to work for more than three months. This is provided by a 2900 mAh lithium-ion battery.

Accessories

The standard kit when buying a phone is as follows - a charger, a headset, a USB data cable, and a user manual. The device comes in a thick cardboard box. For Philips Xenium X1560, the instructions are extremely simple. It is also rated by users.

Positive qualities of Philips Xenium X1560

  • Low cost.
  • A capacious battery that works for a very long time and is economical.
  • A rather big screen with a size of 2.4 inches. Its characteristics are quite good, it's just a phone without any additional features.
  • Attractive appearance. Despite its simplicity, the device looks modern.
  • Ability to use two SIM cards at the same time.
  • High-quality work and stable communication. Conversations are very comfortable.
  • The volume of the call is at a fairly high level, and the sound of the call is pleasant. The vibrating alert is good, it's hard to miss.
  • The device has a good menu that does not contain unnecessary items. There is no direct access to social networks, etc.
  • For each of the contacts, it is possible to record the place of work, email, and other important information.
  • Alarm clock in five modes with the possibility of various settings regarding the frequency.

disadvantages

  • The button located in the center is not very convenient. When you control your Philips Xenium X1560 phone, you can easily miss it, especially for a user with not-so-thin fingers.
  • The device has a fairly large size and especially large thickness. At first glance, the device is not too bulky, but compared to more advanced smartphones, this difference is felt very strongly.
  • There are no applications that synchronize the device with the computer. Perhaps this is the most significant drawback. As a result, if you need to transfer contacts somewhere, you only have to do this through a SIM card. In addition, it is impossible to create a backup copy of contacts - if the phone breaks, all the saved data will be irretrievably lost.
  • The telephone directory is not very convenient. There is no way to save multiple mobile numbers in one contact. Only one mobile number, one home number and one work number are allowed. The name must contain no more than thirty characters.
  • There is no way to save a large number of SMS, as well as store them for a long time. Almost all users will agree that a certain part of the messages must be saved, but this is not possible in the described device.
  • You cannot set the desired sound to receive SMS. There is only a choice of several predefined very simple signals.
  • The USB connector located below is almost useless. Of course, through it you can charge other gadgets, including smartphones, but in practice few users will do it. The design of the device provides for a rubber plug. However, this slightly spoils the look of the phone.

Final verdict

This device is suitable for those users who need only the functions of the phone. For them, the long battery life and the absence of additional options will be relevant.
