Traveling together! Picturesque Croatia. Croatia Hotels with Sandy Beach

Croatia is a wonderful country, which attracts tourists from all over the world every year. Some want to see the sights and cultural and historical monuments of the country, while others dream of a good outdoor recreation, as Croatia is rightfully considered one of the most environmentally friendly places. Croatia's hotels with a sandy beach are famous for being surrounded by pine forest parks, thanks to which the air remains pristine. In this amazing country Pannonia, the Mediterranean and the Alps are brought together, which greatly diversifies the holidays in Croatia.



The hotels here are comfortable, so rest will be a pleasure and will be amazing, vibrant and unforgettable. The characteristic features of each of these places are connected together, therefore they have become a wonderful harmonious and unique whole. The capital of the country is Zagreb, which is the largest city of this state. The time difference between Zagreb and Moscow is two hours.

Climate

The climate in Croatia is surprising in its diversity. So, continental is observed in northern Croatia, Mediterranean - on the Adriatic coast, mountain - in the central part of the country. If you want to relax by the sea, you will probably be interested in Croatia hotels with a sandy beach.

Transport

Croatia has excellent bus connections, so if you want to see the sights, this type of transport is at your service. The fare is 2 euros per hour (about 40 km). Croatia Hotels with Sandy Beach

If you come to relax by the sea, such hotels are just for you. Try what Croatia offers. Hotel "Laguna Materada" - one of these. It has a status of 3 stars. Its windows offer a beautiful view of the old part of the city. In total there are 400 rooms in which there is satellite TV, a balcony, a bathroom, a telephone. If you are traveling with children under the age of two years, this hotel has the opportunity to order an extra small cot in your room for 5 euros per day. For people who are accustomed to maintaining good physical shape all the time, the hotel has a fitness center and a swimming pool (including a children's one). Leisure facilities include tennis courts, minigolf, bike rental and table tennis. For children, there is a playroom and a playground, as well as animation programs that take place in July and August.
