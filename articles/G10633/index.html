Ryazan Ice Palace is always glad to guests

Despite the huge amount of controversy, disagreement and debate, the Ryazan Ice Palace was completed, albeit with a delay. In 2005, the grand opening of the Olympic Palace took place at the address: ul. Zubkova, 12, bldg. 2. A large number of disputes were related to its location in a residential area on the outskirts of the city. It was quite difficult to get to the Ice Palace, public transport routes did not pass by the newly constructed building.





Ice sport comes to town

The skepticism of some citizens about the fact that the Ice Palace of Ryazan will become another item of expenditure of the city budget did not materialize. From the moment of opening, sports sections began to function on the basis of the Olimpiysky Sports Palace, which attracted many young Ryazanians from all areas of the city. In the shortest possible time, the problem of transport accessibility was solved, and now you can get to the Ryazan Ice Palace from anywhere in the city with virtually no transfers.

Multipurpose construction

In the Ice Palace operates a youth sports school for children ()

"Olympic". Here, absolutely free can engage any child. And the choice of sections is quite large: hockey, figure skating, recreational skating, dance sports, karate, aerobics. Recording takes place in August.

Ryazan Ice Palace is perfect for holding mass shows, performances of artists, holding competitions in various disciplines: hockey, figure skating, dancing, wrestling, etc.

Since 2011, a comfortable hotel has been opened for the convenience of nonresident athletes coming to competitions. It offers various rooms: single, double and triple, as well as luxury rooms. Reasonable prices, convenient location, proximity to the city center make this facility quite popular.

Health for the whole family

Despite a large number of skeptical residents, after almost 10

years after the opening, we can safely say that the Ryazan Ice Palace has become a resting place for the whole family. Here you can not only visit competitions or concerts of famous artists. Popular with all residents of the city became free skating. Even in the cold season, when there are a lot of open skating rinks, the Ice Palace (Ryazan) does not lose its popularity. The schedule of skating can be clarified by phone 90-44-79 or at the box office of DS "Olympic". You can use the skate rental service, leaving a small deposit. Citizens love to come here as whole families, putting even kids on their skates.

The popularity of figure skating in Russia is growing every year, and Ryazan was no exception. For many years now the Ice Palace has been raising future champions, opening its doors to all comers. In the figure skating section, as well as in hockey, you can register a child who is already 4 years old. Experienced teachers will teach kids to feel confident on the ice, and training will make them stronger and healthier, because sports and diseases are completely out of the way. Now residents of the city of Ryazan can safely say that the Ice Palace is another attraction that gives people health and good mood.
