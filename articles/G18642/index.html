Specialist consultation: what is the difference between Eurotest and Rostest

Previously, consumers did not think much about which electronic device to buy. And now we have to puzzle over what to choose: Eurotest or Rostest. And although the second option is a source of pride for the seller, for the buyer - everything is far from so simple. This issue has maximum relevance in the market of smartphones, of which a huge amount is currently sold, and eyes are diverted from the choice. Let's try to figure out how the Eurotest differs from Rostest.



Faced with the need for a choice, we will quickly see that specialized stores will clearly declare the undeniable advantage of Rostest devices, but another option will be mentioned as an advantage in all other places. What is the difference for an ordinary, ordinary consumer?

Rostest-Moscow issues certificates of conformity, which confirm that the product meets Russian safety and quality standards, and also confirms that the device legally entered the country, with payment of all duties, and will be sold by an official distributor with the consent of the manufacturer.

Now we will consider how the Eurotest differs from Rostest. The main thing that you need to pay attention to is that for the user and the buyer there is practically no difference. After all, a person who is looking for a good, convenient phone does not care about taxes and customs. Sellers advertise their goods with completely opposite intonations. Some lift the PCT badge almost to heaven, others - CE. If until recently certification of electronics was strictly mandatory, now it is voluntary. Seven years ago, the Russian language was required in the menu, now it is already supported in most modern smartphones. By the way, Rostest does not guarantee this. In this matter, everything depends on the OS, and they are all friends with the great and mighty.

For the consumer, in terms of how the Eurotest differs from Rostest, it is important to have instructions in Russian and a guarantee. But the first point has lost its relevance, as on the website of any manufacturer you can download instructions in all world languages, and the Russian service is often simply disgusting. Moreover, European phones have a minimum annual guarantee of the store and their own service centers, and smartphones for Russia are serviced by branded centers.



Devices for Europe are often called "gray", and it may seem that this is either a criminal fake or just a fake. But technically they are completely identical to devices for our market, assembled from the same materials, on the same conveyors. The only difference is in the stickers and in the fact that they came to us bypassing the officials. And the requirements for European consumers cannot be milder than ours.

When considering how Eurotest differs from Rostest, pay attention to the main advantage of the first - software updates always occur earlier, while PCT may be later, or it may not be at all. And the price. In the first case, the price of the device is 15-20-30% always lower. The main drawback is the lack of a plug for charging for our electric networks, but recently, Russian sellers have put them in a box. And sometimes there are pleasant surprises - additional accessories.
