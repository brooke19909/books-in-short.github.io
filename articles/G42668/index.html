MFI "Credit Plus": reviews. Express loans. Online loan application

Very often, relatives, friends and acquaintances turn away from people who find themselves in a difficult financial situation. Trying to find a solution, those in need of money turn to different banks, microfinance organizations. Some people among the huge number of offers in the credit market are turning their attention to Credit Plus. What kind of company is this? On what terms does she lend money? What are the reviews about Credit Plus? We will consider all this.

About the service "Credit Plus"

Credit Plus is an online service. He is quite young and modern. People are invited to quickly arrange loans, receive money in the most convenient way. When registering on the site, a personal account is created. Through it, you can perform the necessary actions at any convenient time without contacting employees. In the personal account, each client is given the opportunity to extend the current loan, arrange a new one under the most suitable conditions, and spend the accumulated Cashback bonuses.

The Credit Plus service belongs to the limited liability company, the microfinance organization Ecofinance. She grants loans only to capable citizens with a constant source of income. To submit an online loan application, only a passport is required. It is worth noting that, under the existing conditions, not all people can take a loan. The company has set an age limit. For the first time, an application can be submitted at the age of 22 years.





Company benefits

The Ecofinance company declares that the Credit Plus online service belonging to it has several advantages:

  1. He is a responsible direct lender. You do not have to worry about the indicated data. All personal information is transmitted through a secure channel.
  2. With service employees, all communication is carried out through phone calls, e-mails and SMS messages. Personal meetings are not required, so you can apply for a loan at CreditPlus at home, at work, or anywhere else.
  3. Loans in the service "Credit Plus" - it is also profitable. Firstly, upon successful return, the client has a better credit history. Secondly, each subsequent loan becomes cheaper, as the interest rate is reduced. Thirdly, with a timely return of funds, Cashback is accrued up to 10% of the loan amount.




Products Offered

Online service ‚ÄúCredit Plus‚Äù offers 2 types of express loans to its current and potential customers:

  1. MicroCredit. For customers choosing this product, the company provides from 1 thousand to 30 thousand rubles. The loan term is determined by the borrower independently, but it can not be less than 5 days and more than 30 days.
  2. MiniCredit. In accordance with the terms of this product, a client can receive from 15 thousand to 50 thousand rubles. The loan term can be long - from 10 to 24 weeks.

At the first application, as a rule, a person cannot take a loan for a large amount. This is explained by the fact that the company has established customer levels: beginner, expert, partner and VIP. Beginners are those customers who first contact the company. The amount available to them is only up to 15 thousand rubles. The experts include clients who returned from 2 to 4 loans. The maximum possible amount is 20 thousand rubles. In addition to the increased amount, experts are also offered the opportunity to arrange loans using SMS messages. Partners are customers who returned from 5 to 10 loans, and the VIP group includes people who have used the service more than 10 times. For partners and for VIP clients, the amount of express loans is significantly increased.

Loan processing

Clients applying for the first time to receive money must fill out an application. It is presented on the main page of the company website. Filling out an application consists of 4 stages:

  1. At the initial stage, the client indicates his contact information in the form - last name, first name and patronymic, gender, date of birth, email address, phone number. After the transition to the next stage, the potential borrower is sent a confirmation code to the phone number.
  2. After indicating the code on the page, the passport data is entered in the appropriate fields - place of birth, document number, information about when it was issued and by whom.
  3. The following pages indicate additional information about the borrower and the customer confirms the completed online loan application.




Decision-making

An application for a loan can be sent at any time. The solution comes very quickly - within 1 minute. The company often responds positively to applications sent, but sometimes it has to refuse. Only customers who provide false information about themselves, hide information about existing and non-returning loans, receive a negative decision.

The online service takes positive decisions based on one main criterion. For the company ‚ÄúCredit Plus‚Äù it is the presence of a positive motivation for the borrower to repay the money that he plans to borrow.

An approved loan in Credit Plus can be transferred in different ways:

  • through the payment system CONTACT or "LEADER";
  • to the bank card indicated by the borrower;
  • to a bank account at the details specified by the client.




Loan repayment and extension

With the MicroCredit product, the debt is repaid in a lump sum payment on time. With "MiniCredit" a longer loan term is established, therefore, its return is carried out in a slightly different way. Equal payments are required to be made every 14 days.

If repayment on time is not possible, then you can use the service ‚ÄúRenew loan‚Äù in ‚ÄúCredit Plus‚Äù. You can activate it in your account. The company has set the maximum allowable extension period. It is equal to 15 days. It is also worth noting that the extension is not a free service. A small fee is required for it.

Even for customers, the company created a service called "Autoprolongation." It is designed for people who find themselves in a difficult financial situation and are unable to repay the debt on time. With auto-prolongation, the loan term is increased by 10 days. Renewal fees are included in the amount due.

About Cashback

According to reviews, ‚ÄúCredit Plus‚Äù offers its customers Cashback, but not all people know what it is. This foreign word denotes a cash bonus. In the service "Credit Plus" it is set as a percentage of the amount of debt. For example, a loan is 5 thousand rubles. The cash bonus is set at 5% (this figure is not accurate, for some borrowers it is more). Upon timely contact, the client will be returned 250 rubles.

Accrued bonuses can be used as follows:

  • pay the next loan;
  • extend the term of a loan;
  • transfer to your account or through a money transfer system.




Positive feedback from borrowers

Some customers leave positive feedback about the Credit Plus online service. Borrowers say that their opinion is due to the advantages of the company:

  • it takes about 10 minutes to fill out an application on the site;
  • you can find answers to all questions (they are located in a special section of the site);
  • if necessary, you can contact the service staff (through a special form on the website or by calling the support service at a toll free number);
  • all operators are real experts - they know about all the nuances of obtaining loans, can answer any question, correctly and politely explain any incomprehensible information;
  • the application is considered very quickly, it will not take several hours or days to wait for a decision, as is the case with other microfinance organizations and banks;
  • when making a positive decision on the application, the funds are sent to the client instantly, and delays in crediting occur not through the fault of the company (funds are transferred to the bank account in accordance with the terms of the bank).
