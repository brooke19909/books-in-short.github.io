Days Hotel & Suites Sanya 5 * (China, Hainan): description, service, reviews

China, of course, is not as popular as Egypt, India or Turkey, a country among Russian beach lovers. Nevertheless, you can actually relax here really well. Tourism infrastructure in the resort cities of China is well developed. In any case, quite tolerable hotels have been built here. For example, tourists who choose Sanya for a holiday in China can stay at the Days Hotel & Suites Sanya 5 * complex . Some Russian tour operators conclude agreements in Turkey with her, and she earned more or less good reviews from tourists.

Location Features

In terms of service, most hotels in China, unfortunately, are somewhat inferior to Egyptian or Turkish. Reviews on the Web about the hotel Days Hotel & Suites Sanya 5 * s are still somewhat controversial. Some tourists like this hotel. Other, more demanding vacationers, believe that the service here could be better. But in one practically all the travelers who have ever stayed at this hotel converge: it is located very well. With trips to the sea or to the city, its guests never have any problems.





This five-star hotel was built in the southwestern part of the city of Sanya, on Fr. Hainan. It is located at the very foot of beautiful mountains and its surroundings are simply magnificent. This is where all the tourists who have ever lived in this recreation center agree. Residents of this hotel can reach the sea in literally 3 minutes. This hotel is built on the first coastline.

In relation to the airport, this hotel, according to its guests, is also very well located. The distance to the nearest international air transport hub from it is only 15 km. This means, first of all, that tourists who have booked rooms in this hotel practically do not have to spend time on the way here, and shake themselves in the transfer of the tour operator. Getting to the hotel from the airport can be literally 20 minutes. At the same time, the hotel is located in such a way that rumbling planes do not fly over its buildings and do not interfere with the guests' relaxation.

Vacationers who have booked rooms at Days Hotel & Suites on their own can, if desired, book a transfer directly to the hotel itself.

Days Hotel & Suites Sanya 5 *: complex description brief

This hotel was built in 2007. That is, by hotel standards, it is quite old. The structure of this recreation complex includes one seven-story main building and three family villas.





According to many tourists, probably, initially the Days Hotel & Suites Sanya 5 * was really very stylish and comfortable. This is clearly noticeable both in its interiors and in the equipment of the rooms. When opening this complex, most likely, it was free to give exactly 5 *. But many years have passed since the construction of the hotel. A serious reconstruction in the hotel (at least for 2017) was not carried out. Therefore, at the moment, the complex, judging by the reviews of tourists, has already lost some of its gloss. The carpet in its rooms and corridors is a little worn out and covered with spots, plumbing has exhausted its resource, etc. Simply put, to date, this hotel, according to tourists, cannot be given more than 4 stars with a stretch. This is for vacationers who decide to rent rooms here, it is worth keeping in mind.

Initially, the Days Hotel & Suites Sanya 5 * hotel was built for the Chinese. Russian tourists began to settle here not too long ago. And now few Russian tour operators are working with this hotel. For 300 Chinese, no more than 15-20 Russians live in this hotel . Consequently, the service in this hotel is focused mainly on the local population.

The opinion of tourists about infrastructure

Hotel beach

This hotel is located, as already mentioned, right next to the sea. But in order to get to the beach, tourists need to cross a narrow road with not too heavy traffic. A trip to the sea to residents of this recreation center usually does not cause any particular inconvenience.

The beach of Hotel & Suites has earned just excellent reviews. It is covered with small pebbles and sand. So you can walk here even without shale. Only in the heat, when sand is heating up in the sun, guests have to wear beach shoes. Built hotel D ays Hotel Suites Sanya Resort 5 * in one of the quietest places of the city. And so there are never too many people on the beach next to it. Hotel guests on the beach can use sun loungers for free. But there are not too many sun loungers on the beach. To occupy a deck chair, residents should get up early.

In the afternoon, in order for the Chinese not to take the lounger, you need to put more things on it - a towel, toys, etc. Otherwise, local residents can simply shift the "mark" and lie down on a "strange" deck chair without any special torment of conscience.

The sea opposite the hotel, as many tourists note, is not too transparent and the color is not as beautiful as the same Red or Mediterranean. However, the water here is still more or less clean. Yes, and burning jellyfish in the sea in this place almost never occur.
