The recreation center "Lukomorye" (Oryol) invites guests to relax

Oryol region can be safely called the middle of Ancient Russia. This land has a very rich historical heritage. The ancient settlements of the Oryol region were mentioned in chronicles dating back to the 12th century.

Sights of Oryol

The region is rich in Russian folk culture and traditions, unique nature. Numerous rest houses and camp sites ( recreation center "Lukomorye" (Oryol), recreation park "Gorki" (Mertsalova), camp site "Ecoostrov") invite outdoor enthusiasts to visit. A lot of what they can show travelers.





The lands of Orel are saturated with ancient epics and fairy tales, there are ancient monasteries and churches in the region, not far from the village of Saburovo the remains of the fortress - bastions, towers, dilapidated walls. The area fascinates with natural beauties, forests. Tourists are offered a lot of excursions: to the zoo and national park, along ecological trails, to the Holy Spring.

Guest reviews

For those who want to have a good and fun weekend or vacation, the ideal place is Lukomorye (recreation center, Oryol). Reviews about him are extremely positive. A special delight is the new winter entertainment - snow tubing. Both children and adults ride the mountain with great pleasure, parents of very young children can lower them on foot to the middle of the hill and start the congress from there. Judging by the reviews, Lukomorye is an ideal option for a family vacation: it is safe and comfortable here, there are no outsiders. Also, vacationers praise the kitchen of the complex, especially pilaf and barbecue.

It features an indoor, warm gazebo with coffee, tea and bread rolls.
