Hotel Royal 4 * Golden Sands, Bulgaria: overview, description, rooms and reviews

Bulgaria is one of the most iconic tourist destinations among Russian travelers. The love of the Slavs for the Bulgarian resorts has strong, yet pre-perestroika roots. Even today, when the undoubted industry leaders in receiving mass package tourists - Turkey and Egypt, with their brilliant service policies, visa-free entry and the ubiquitous all inclusive - have lured a large portion of international tourists, Bulgarian resorts are still crowded with vacationers from Russia, Ukraine and Belarus.

Bulgaria, being an extremely low-cost tourist destination, has all the attributes of an elite, expensive resort (luxurious beaches, a pleasant climate, beautiful nature, a unique excursion program, etc.) However, as in many other post-Soviet countries, here the process of taking proud competitive positions in the world market of travel services is a little tough. The Bulgarian resorts are still old panel, exceptionally sovdepovye sanatoriums side by side with grandiose expensive hotels, and ancient minibuses interspersed with modern sightseeing buses on the streets. In fairness, it is worth noting that in recent years, Bulgaria has gradually improved the transport and hotel sector, trying to adapt them to the needs of the modern European consumer.





Russian tourists wishing to spend summer vacations in Bulgaria have to work hard when applying for a visa. The terms and list of documents are the same as when applying for Schengen. But the cost of a Bulgarian visa is one of the highest in Europe: 55 euros (35 euros costs the general Schengen consular fee and 20 euros - visa center services).

Royal Golden Sands 4 * (Bulgaria): general information

The complex is located in Golden Sands, 400 meters from the beach. The hotel is surrounded by picturesque green territory, from the windows of rooms on the upper floors a beautiful panoramic view of the sea and mountains opens.

In total, the hotel has one fifteen-story building with 2 elevators and 150 rooms. The hotel was built in 2003, and in 2013 a large-scale reconstruction was carried out in it.





Check -in time is 14:00, eviction takes place until 12:00.

The hotel does not accommodate pets.

The hotel accepts credit cards. When checking in with tourists, no deposit will be charged.

Hotel location

As already mentioned, Royal 4 * (Golden Sands) is located 400 meters from the beach. The resort center is just 1.3 km away. Golden Sands is the epicenter of youthful, active life throughout the southeast coast of Bulgaria. The city has a lot of entertainment, night clubs, shopping centers, restaurants.

The hotel is located 25 km from Varna International Airport. Nearby hotels are Tsarevets and Atlas.

Public transport is 200 meters away.

Just 250 meters from the hotel is one of the best water parks in Bulgaria - Aquapolis.

Classification and description of numbers

In total, the hotel Royal Golden Sands 4 * (Golden Sands) has 150 rooms of the Standard and Superior categories.

Standart room. Area 20 sq. M. m. The maximum number of people in a room is 3 + 1. There is a balcony. View - to the park and mountains or to the sea.

Superior room . Area 29 sq. M. m. The maximum number of people in a room is 4 + 1. There is a balcony. The view is on the mountains or the sea.





The rooms have air conditioning, bath / shower, refrigerator, hairdryer, telephone, TV with satellite channels.

Rooms are cleaned every day. Change of towels and replenishment of bath accessories daily, bed linen - every three days.

Golden Sands Beach

Hotel Royal 4 * (Golden Sands) is located on the third coastline, 400 m from the beach (approximately 10 minutes on foot).

Golden Sands Beach has such a name not only because of the tendency of local tour operators and marketers to pompous expressions. The sand in this place is really very pleasant, light, small, does not repent in the sun. The beach itself is flat and very wide. Despite this, often in high season there is a shortage of vacant seats. Back in the mid-2000s, this beach received the Blue Flag Award from the European Union for cleanliness. It is well equipped, there are changing cabins, toilets, bars, lifeguards work. There are many rental points for equipment and sports equipment for swimming, water sports.





Sun loungers and umbrellas are available at an additional cost. Beach towels are provided at the hotel free of charge.

Sun loungers, umbrellas and towels by the pool at the hotel are free.
