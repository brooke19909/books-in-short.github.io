Biserovo Lake: for fishing and health

Muscovites or Moscow guests have probably heard or been to chic places near Moscow, where the tranquil expanse of the wonderful Biserov Lake is asleep in the forests. Any resident of the metropolis at least several times a year wants to relax from the hustle and bustle of nature, which envelops in its beauty and magical sounds. If you have not looked here yet, then it's time to familiarize yourself with these places a little.

A bit of history



The lake is located just 20 kilometers from Moscow. Its history, judging by the excavations of archaeologists, stretches from the time of primitive man. There were found primitive tools made of flint, as well as the remains of dishes from the Neolithic.

Why such a name? It's simple: Catherine the Great herself turned her royal attention to the local beauties and, as it were, accidentally dropped beads into the water. So they began to call Biserovo Lake that way.

Pisces - the abyss!

Once upon a time, monks lived on its shore and bred a sterlet in it, and this fish, as you know, is a lover of pure water only. Today, Biserovo Lake is a natural monument of national importance. Thanks to the work of environmentalists, it is kept in proper cleanliness and safety for tourists and vacationers.



About twenty species of fish live and live in its waters: pike, perch, silver crucian carp, pike perch and others. In order to ensure a proper existence of the fish and maintain its population every year, the reservoir is stocked up, that is, the planting and cultivation of planting material is carried out: fry, yearlings.

The constant presence of fish attracts fishermen here. According to visitors to these places, no one leaves here without a catch. Close proximity to the fish farm and several other fish ponds provides good fishing. If the caught fish does not reach the desired size, then you can try your hand at neighboring lakes. Faithful companions of water: duckweed, vodokras, water lily and hornwort - decorate the water surface.

Relaxation

The shores here are striking in their beauty, the natural beaches washed by the water attract lovers to soak up the sun and swim. In summer, Biserovo Lake warms up well, in depth it has about 5 meters. In shallow water, children will find themselves a fun pastime: splashing around in warm lake water when their parents take ultraviolet baths and watch their offspring lying on a sand.

You can come here to relax as a savage, but decent conditions are provided for lovers of a civilized holiday. In spring and summer, visitors are waiting for Lake Biserovo - rest in tourist complexes will be held according to the most convenient program. Those who wish will be provided with fishing tackle, boats - everything that is necessary for excellent fishing. Those who arrived in their car can leave it in the car park. Here at the fishing and sports base you can buy a ticket to fish.



It is also worth noting that Biserovo Lake is a resource for health resorts, since its sapropelic mud is considered healing in its composition. It is not difficult to get to it: drive to the Kupavna station (departure from the Kursky station). From there, walk a few meters: the lake is on the left side of the road.
