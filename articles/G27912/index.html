Mareblue Beach Resort 4 * (Corfu, Greece): photos, prices and reviews of tourists

Mareblue Beach Resort 4 * - this is the name of the hotel, whose name used to sound like Blue Bay Escape. This hotel complex is very popular among people who come to rest in Greece. Mareblue Beach Resort 4 * (Corfu) receives extremely positive reviews , because a hotel with a similar level of service just a priori can not be bad.

Location



This hotel complex is located on the first coastline in the immediate vicinity of the beach. Less than 200 meters separates the hotel from the sea. Also close to it is the airport - about 20 kilometers. This is quite an important aspect, because after the flight by plane, not everyone likes to shake for two hours to the hotel. It will take only half an hour, or even less. The hotel complex is located in Ag.Spiridonas Perithiac. Mareblue Beach Resort 4 * very harmoniously fits into the general landscape, it is noticeably from afar - this is a luxurious, richly decorated in the traditional Greek style hotel, which immediately shows that wealthy people are resting here. The hotel itself consists of a main building and several bungalows located throughout the complex. The hotel serves its customers from May to October - this period of time is considered the holiday season.

Accommodation

Mareblue Beach Resort 4 * offers a choice of 200 rooms. This is both apartments and studio rooms - guests of the complex can choose the option that they like most. It should be noted that absolutely all rooms are spacious, decorated in an elegant style, furnished with comfortable and modern furniture. The apartments have many other nice additions. For example, a balcony with a magnificent view from its windows attracts attention. For guests a plasma TV (satellite channels), high-speed Internet, air conditioning, mini fridge, telephone, radio, and even a kettle with coffee maker are offered. All rooms have a private bathroom with a shower cabin and necessary toiletries. In general, in such apartments the rest will be truly full.
