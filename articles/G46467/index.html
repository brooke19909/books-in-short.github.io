Where is Grenada located and how to get there?

Once in the Soviet Union, one song was popular, in which there were such words: "Grenada, Grenada, My Grenada ...". Almost everyone knew and sang these lines. But if they asked these people if they knew where the country of Grenada was located, then many would have difficulty in answering this question. We will try to fix it.

Today we will tell you about where Grenada is located and how to get there. And also we will give useful tips to those who go on vacation to this country, and introduce its most important sights.



Where is Grenada located?

Tourists from all over the world have always been attracted by the most unusual places. Grenada is famous for its national parks, warm climate, beautiful waterfalls. We will tell you about where Grenada is located, in which country.

First of all, it is an island state. Its area covers more than three hundred square kilometers. Where is the island of Grenada located? In the southeast of the Caribbean. This state is located on two islands: Grenada and the Grenadines (southern part). You can get into the country in two ways:

  • By air. There are no direct flights from Grenada from Russia. There are two most convenient options with transfers: in London and Frankfurt. For travel, a visa is not needed, only a passport. Travel time from 12 to 14 hours.
  • By sea transport. Where the country of Grenada is located, there are a large number of shipping companies, as well as international shipping lines.

If you come here, you will spend time in one of the most beautiful corners of the earth, in which there is everything that a person might need in order to feel happy.



Climate

Since Grenada is located in the Caribbean Sea, where the climate is quite favorable, the weather on the whole island is hot and humid for a whole year. The average annual air temperature is at least 26 degrees.

Grenada: curious facts

We invite you to get acquainted with some information about this amazing country:

  • Interestingly, where the state of Grenada is located, it is forbidden to build houses above palm trees. For this reason, you will not find tall buildings here.
  • The country is washed by the Caribbean Sea and the Atlantic Ocean.
  • The flag of this state shows nutmeg, as Grenada is rightfully considered one of the largest exporters of spices.
  • There is an underwater sculpture park.
  • The number of tourists coming here on vacation significantly exceeds the level of the local population.
  • The official language in this country is English. This is due to the fact that for a long time Grenada was part of the UK.

sights

Each country has its own unique monuments of architecture and art, which are worthy of close study by tourists. We invite you to get acquainted with the most interesting places in Grenada. So, let's begin:

  • Where Grenada is located, one of the main attractions is the Seven Sisters waterfall. It is located in the center of the island, so getting to it is quite easy. During the journey, you will enjoy the beauty of the rainforest, and then you can plunge into the refreshing water of the waterfall.
  • Do you like diving or just tired of swimming in not very clear water? Then you definitely need to visit Jenny Island, where you can study the underwater world and its inhabitants.
  • St. George is the capital of Grenada. The city was built in the 17th century on the slope of an extinct volcano. It burned about a dozen times. But this fact makes the capital of the island even more interesting and attractive for tourists.
  • National Museum. If you want to not only know where Grenada is located, but also to study its history, then you should definitely visit this place. A huge number of rare and unique exhibits are stored here.
  • If you ever get to where Grenada is located, be sure to visit the underwater sculpture park. Here at a depth of three meters there are a large number of images of various people.

There are other attractions in the country that you can meet on your own.

Travel Tips:

  1. Now that you know where Grenada is located, be sure to take all the necessary vaccinations before leaving for this country. There is a high chance of contracting malaria, so be careful.
  2. Since solar activity is very high here, it is best to take sunscreens with you.
  3. The crime rate in this country, although not high, will not be out of place if you scan all the important documents before the trip. It is best to have them in two forms: printed and electronic media (tablet, laptop, etc.).
  4. Also, no matter how sad it may sound, beware of people who volunteer to help. They may turn out to be real scammers themselves. There are cases when a person offered to convey things, and then demanded a very high fee for his work.
  5. Few people know, but collecting corals from the bottom, and even more so trying to take them out of the country, is prohibited. Therefore, do not make such mistakes.
  6. If you do not have as much time to study the country as you would like, then initially make a list of the places that you want to visit. In this case, you will have time to see much more.
  7. If you really want to relax, and not solve business issues, then leave your phone at home, and tell your relatives in advance.
  8. You can fearlessly swim in the sea near the beach, because because of coral reefs sea predators cannot get here. But to swim far, we do not advise you, since this is already unsafe. There can be strong undercurrents and marine predators.
