Hotel Khalidiya Palace Rayhaan 5 * (United Arab Emirates, Abu Dhabi): description and reviews

Our article will interest those tourists who are going to spend their holidays in Abu Dhabi. The richest emirate is a sedate and respectable resort, which is significantly different from Dubai. There is no fuss and big traffic jams inherent in other cities of the country. All Abu Dhabi hotels are respectable places you always want to return to. One of such complexes is Khalidiya Palace Rayhaan 5 *. It will be discussed about him in our article.

A bit about the hotel

Khalidiya Palace Rayhaan 5 * is a good five-star complex focused on family vacations. The hotel is quite young since it was opened only in 2010. The area occupied by him is 85 thousand square meters. m. The complex consists of an eighteen-story building. The hotel is located in one of the prestigious areas of Abu Dhabi and is focused on beach vacations. It is built on the bay, and there are shopping and entertainment centers nearby.





Getting to the hotel complex is not difficult, since the international airport is located only thirty minutes from it. Khalidiya Palace Rayhaan by Rotana 5 * is the first establishment of the Rayhan Hotels and Resorts by Rotana network.

Number of rooms

The number of rooms Khalidiya Palace Rayhaan 5 * is represented by 443 apartments. All are decorated in a modern Arabic style. Hotel rooms are presented in the following categories:

  1. Classic apartment with a balcony has an area of ​​45 square meters. m. One room has a view of the neighboring hotel. All apartments in this category are equipped with king beds.
  2. Superior classic room with a balcony has an area of ​​50 square meters. m. The apartments can overlook the pool or the sea.
  3. Premium rooms with a balcony are 60 square meters. m and a partial view of the coast.
  4. Double "Premium" with a balcony has an area of ​​60 square meters. m and a view of the pool or the sea.
  5. Classic Suite has an area of ​​65 square meters. m. These apartments consist of a living room and bedroom, which are separated by a door.




All apartments are equipped with air conditioning, TV with satellite channels, and bathrooms. Some rooms even have a small kitchen with kitchenware and appliances.

Catering

Guests of Khalidiya Palace Rayhaan 5 * can use a variety of food options, paying half board, full board or breakfast. The hotel has several restaurants, each of which is worthy of the attention of tourists. Overlooking the pool, the Kamon establishment offers visitors dishes of Arabic cuisine. The Horizont Restaurant serves international cuisine. The Lounge is located in the lobby of the hotel. It offers beautiful views of the pool. It serves delicious sweets and light snacks, as well as numerous types of natural coffee.

Hotel infrastructure

Khalidiya Palace Rayhaan by Rotana 5 * has a good infrastructure. The hotel has a business center that may be needed by business people. At the hotel you can rent a car, visit the sauna, beauty salon and fitness center. The hotel offers dry cleaning, laundry, currency exchange. There is also a swimming pool with an equipped relaxation area.

Beach vacation

Khalidiya Palace Rayhaan 5 * (UAE, Abu Dhabi) has its own sandy beach, the length of which is two hundred meters. The coast is equipped with umbrellas and sunbeds, and free towels are provided.

A bit about the resort

Khalidiya Palace Rayhaan Hotel 5 * is located in one of the most respectable areas of Abu Dhabi. The city is both the capital and the resort. It is located on the coast of the Persian Gulf. The richest emirate has large oil reserves, which, of course, affects the standard of living of its inhabitants. Abu Dhabi is developing rapidly, but at the same time preserves traditions. Guests of the resort do not always understand the local order, but you need to be respectful of a foreign culture. In the city next to the ultra-modern buildings, the most beautiful oriental mosques are adjacent. Abu Dhabi is significantly different from other eastern cities. It has a lot of mosques and greenery, and this despite the fact that it is located in the desert.





The city, like the whole emirate, has a long history. For centuries, the region’s economy was based on fishing for fish and pearls. But in the sixties, significant oil deposits were found here. Since then, the flowering of the emirate began. The desert has turned into a real flourishing kingdom of peace and prosperity. Modern Abu Dhabi is a large city with skyscrapers, parks, mosques, fountains, gardens, flowerbeds and ancient markets. The city wonderfully combines modern architecture with ancient buildings and strict oriental mores. All tourists are invariably struck by beautiful parks. No wonder Abu Dhabi was called the garden city. The capital is characterized by a fusion of the future and the present. The city has a lot of attractions. One of the main is an oil exhibition, where you can learn about the history of the city. Not far from the exhibition is located the Kornichi shipyard, on which to this day the construction of Arab dhow boats is underway.

The most important attraction can be called Al-Husn Palace. It is also known as the White Fort. The complex was built at the end of the nineteenth century and is currently considered the oldest building in Abu Dhabi. Once the rulers of the city lived in it, now it serves as an archive of historical documents of the cultural center of the city.

For those tourists who are interested in the history of the country, the doors of the exhibition complex called “Cultural and Ethnographic Village” are always open. Here you can see exhibits that help to understand what life was like in different regions of the country. Over the past decades, Abu Dhabi has been very actively developing and gradually turning into one of the most luxurious and sophisticated cities in the world. It is characterized by a combination of the most diverse nations and cultures. The city is inhabited by Americans, Europeans, Indians and other peoples of Asia. All new residents are attracted by a very high standard of living.





Currently, Abu Dhabi is a real center of tourism, sports, recreation and trade. The city gained universal love as a resort thanks to the beautiful beaches on the Persian Gulf. The city welcomes tourists very hospitably, offering comfortable modern hotels.

General impression of the hotel

Khalidiya Palace Rayhaan 5 * hotel (United Arab Emirates, Abu Dhabi) offers its guests to spend an unforgettable vacation on the sea coast. One of the main advantages of the institution is its proximity to the coast. Not all city hotels can boast such a good location. The beach of the hotel is spacious, its length is about 200 meters. Even with a full load of hotels on the shore, you can always find a place to relax. The sandy beach is equipped with sunbeds and umbrellas. Tours and activities

Relaxing in a hotel that has such a favorable location, you should definitely take a moment and see the local attractions. Moreover, you can travel around the city on your own without buying excursions. Thanks to the good transport interchange, you can easily get to any point in Abu Dhabi. The colorful city is filled with architectural wonders that are worth seeing with your own eyes. The capital is often called Manhattan of the Middle East, this is due to the fact that most of it is built up with skyscrapers and skyscrapers.

In the ultramodern city there are practically no ancient buildings. The only greetings from the past can be considered Al-Husn Palace. Among locals, it is better known as the White Fort. In the city, it is rarely possible to find buildings that are more than thirty years old. But Al-Husn was built in the nineteenth century.





Tourists will also be interested in the Sheikh Zayed Mosque. It is a true shrine for all Muslims. The snow-white building is striking in its amazing beauty and enormous size. The main Muslim Mecca is built of white marble, as well as semiprecious and precious stones. The Arabs spared no money to create such a temple. It is believed that this mosque is one of the largest in the world. It is difficult to imagine, but it can simultaneously accommodate up to 40 thousand people.

The city government does not spare money on recreation areas, so new places are constantly appearing in the open. One of them is the Caliph Park, whose organization cost the authorities $ 50 million. The name he received in honor of the president of the country. The locals themselves love the amusement park no less than tourists. On its territory there is an incredible number of attractions, cultural sites and simply beautiful places. One day is not enough to see the park. But still, you can recommend vacationers to take a little time and visit it with the children.
