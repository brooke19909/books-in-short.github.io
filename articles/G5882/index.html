How many kilometers from Yekaterinburg to Moscow and the features of the trip

The distance from Yekaterinburg to Moscow is 1400 kilometers in a straight line, but it is almost impossible to drive it on a flat line due to the nature of the road, along the path of the mountain and river, so the trip will require you to cover a greater distance in kilometers.

Features of the trip by rail

It is useful to know how many kilometers there are by rail from Yekaterinburg to Moscow - about 1670. Trains can travel in a straight line through Yanaul, Kazan, Kanash and Murom, or they can make a hook to the north, that is, through Perm, Nizhny Novgorod and Vladimir. The second option is more interesting in the sense that there are international trains from Ulan Bator and Beijing to Moscow. The fastest are on the road for 25 hours, but there are also slow ones, they travel 33 hours.

A ticket to a regular reserved seat car costs about 2700 rubles, the exact price depends on the tariff regulation schedule and on the shares of Russian Railways. In a branded train, for example, in the one that follows from Vladivostok (the longest railway route in the world), tickets cost more, from 4200 rubles.

If you consider how many kilometers are from Yekaterinburg to Moscow, the tariff will be like this:

  • reserved seat - from 1.7 rubles per km;
  • compartment - from 2 rubles per km;
  • sleeping - from 6 rubles per km.

Trains arrive at Kazan and Yaroslavl stations in Moscow.

Trip through Ufa

If for some reason you plan a trip along this route by bus, you should understand how many kilometers are from Yekaterinburg to Moscow via Ufa. Buses depart from the northern bus station and from the Zvezdny Dar gift. The journey is 8 hours. A ticket costs about 1200 rubles, and in Ufa a bus makes a stop near the department store or TRK "Family".

Next, you need to transfer to the bus, which leaves at 11:00. In 20 hours and 2,000 rubles he will be taken to Moscow, to the Novogireevo and Kotelniki metro stations.

It should be remembered that you can leave from Ufa to Moscow by train. A ticket for a seated car costs 1,500 rubles, it is cheaper than a bus, but it will take 29 hours.
