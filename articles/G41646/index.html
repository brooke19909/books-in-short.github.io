A microcomputer is ... A microcomputer on Windows

A microcomputer is most often a portable computer or laptop, the only central processor of which is a microprocessor. They are used for arithmetic operations, management of complex technological processes. Such microcomputers are equipped with peripheral devices, such as displays, and analog-to-digital converters, printing devices, and others.

Basic features of a microcomputer

  • Bus connection of system devices.
  • Complete unification of hardware and software.
  • A large number of potential customers.

Types of microcomputers

  • Stationary This species is the most popular and common. It consists of a single system unit, which is located separately from external devices. After all, what is a microcomputer without a mouse, keyboard and, of course, a monitor? It is possible to connect other external devices to a separate system unit, for example: a printer, game joystick, speaker system.
  • Portable. Today, this species has also become very popular. The most famous and widespread portable microcomputer is a laptop. It differs from the previous one only in that the external and internal devices are combined in one housing. But another appearance does not take away the ability to connect other external devices to the microcomputer.
  • Network After the worldwide network rapidly began to develop in the 1990s, it became necessary to invent network microcomputers. This view is designed to work only on the network. They are much cheaper than stationary or portable, because they do not need drives and their own memory. And soon, telephone companies, schools and other educational institutions will be fully equipped with network microcomputers.
  • Pocket. Small PDAs weighing 200-400 grams that fit comfortably in the palm of your hand. Not so long ago, namely in 1990, there were PDAs with operating systems. This made it possible to exchange information with other computers and microcomputers, as well as connect to a worldwide network. Pocket microcomputers do not have a drive or hard disk. Most of these computers are touch-sensitive, but there are also models with a small keyboard.
