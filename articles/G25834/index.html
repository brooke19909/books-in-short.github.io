Hotel Ramada Plaza Jumeirah Beach 4 * (Dubai / UAE): description and photos of tourists

Ramada Plaza Jumeirah Beach is a four-star hotel located in the center of the famous Bur Dubai. The hotel is a forty-five-story building located in the Jumeirah Beach area.

Ramada Plaza Jumeirah Beach, as we already mentioned, has a great location. From the airport, the hotel can be reached in just thirty minutes. A ten minute drive from the complex is the famous shopping center called Mall Of The Emirates. The hotel is between the Jumeirah Beach Residence Promenade and the beautiful Marina area.

The hotel is located in the most beautiful area of ‚Äã‚Äãvast Dubai. Here, the lights of a night megalopolis fascinate. If you decide to visit this unusual city for the first time, then there is no better place to stay. You will certainly be impressed by this place.



The Ramada Plaza Jumeirah Beach Hotel is very beautiful and new. In real life, it looks much better than in brochures. The hotel complex positions itself as a four-star hotel, in 2012 it passed certification.

Some rooms include kitchenettes with microwaves, freezers, dishwashers, and washing machines.

Hotel beach

The Ramada Plaza Jumeirah Beach 4 * hotel does not have its own beach, like many other hotels. Vacationers have several public beaches. The coast is very close, so the road does not take more than five minutes. Before leaving the hotel, vacationers receive beach sets consisting of an umbrella and towels.

It's nice that the coast is cleaned daily. This is not to say that you will not meet cigarette butts anywhere, but in general the sand is quite clean. The beach is free for visitors, you can swim and relax at any time.

The entire coast is fine white sand, lovers will appreciate this place. And the sea is generally a separate topic for discussion. It is incredibly warm and transparent here. Of course, this is not surprising in such weather, but gradually you get so used to such a temperature of the water that the pool seems already cold. Nutrition

In the Ramada Plaza Jumeirah Beach 4 * hotel several food options are possible. Guests at the complex can order a la carte dishes or use the buffet option. In addition, by purchasing a tour, you can pay for the most suitable meal option: full board or breakfast only.

The hotel has a main restaurant that serves international cuisine. Vegetarian, baby and diet foods are also available.



Breakfast starts at 6.30 and continues until 11.00. The food in the restaurant is always fresh and tasty. At least one kind of soup is served in the morning. From the side dishes you can choose mashed potatoes, rice, potatoes. There are always meat, fish, sometimes a seafood cocktail on the tables, as well as various cold snacks. From fruits, as a rule, you can see melons, watermelons, pineapples, fruit salads.

As for drinks, they are free only at breakfast, coffee pots and teapots are put on the tables, so you can pour yourself as much as you want. In general, the food in the hotel is very good. However, it should be understood that the hotel is four-star, so there will not be any special delights, lots of seafood. At least no one remains hungry while relaxing in the Ramada Plaza Jumeirah Beach Residence 4 *. Reviews of tourists regarding food are very positive.

Those vacationers who are planning their first vacation in the UAE should know some nuances. When purchasing a tour at an agency, you need to think about the type of food. For example, if you are planning a trip in June, then be sure to take a full board, since this is Ramadan time, which means that all street cafes will be closed until evening. Having breakfast at the hotel, lunch will be problematic. Therefore, it will be much easier to eat at the hotel. At any other time, food problems do not arise. Around the hotel there is a resort area, in the territory of which there are plenty of food establishments.

Excursions

Relaxing in the Ramada Plaza Jumeirah Beach Residence 4 *, you can use the local tour desk. The UAE is an amazing country that you should definitely see. Therefore, do not limit your vacation only to visiting the beach. Experienced tourists are advised to definitely go and walk to the Dubai Mall. There you can see a fascinating show of fountains. It starts every evening at 18.30.



Usually vacationers visit the gold market. In addition, the fake market is especially popular, where you can buy a variety of branded items at bargain prices (of course, goods are skillful fakes).

Walking around the city is quite interesting and entertaining. Typically, such trips are some kind of dash between different shopping centers, which are nice and cool. In the United Arab Emirates, as you know, it‚Äôs hot, but because air conditioning works at its full capacity almost everywhere: in supermarkets, buses, shopping centers, restaurants and cafes.

Dubai has the tallest building in the world - the Burj Khalifa. The height of this skyscraper is 828 meters, a total of 163 floors. The building looks like a stalagmite, it was opened in January 2010.

The skyscraper was originally built as the largest in the world, and therefore its height was kept secret. The Dubai Tower was conceived as a "city within a city." It houses apartments, a hotel, business offices. The building was built on the basis of a special concrete mixture that withstands the heat. Glasses do not let in the rays of the sun, which makes it possible to maintain the optimum temperature. There are 57 elevators in the skyscraper. But from the ground floor to the top floor you can only go up to the service. All residents and visitors to the building move with transfers. At the very top of the Burj Khalifa there is an observation deck - a favorite place for tourists.

Another attraction is the musical fountain, which is a unique composition of sound, water and light. It is located on the territory of an artificial lake in the heart of Dubai, near the Burj Khalifa. Currently, this fountain is one of the largest in the world. Its length is 275 meters, and the height of the jets reaches 150 meters. The fountain gives out world hits in the voice of Whitney Houston, Andrea Bocelli, as well as numerous Arabic tunes. This amazing and amazing sight is definitely a must see.

Dubai is famous for its many kilometers of beaches, low prices for leather, fur and gold, but not everyone knows that there is a huge aquarium, the dimensions of which are comparable to a three-story house. It is inhabited by various marine inhabitants. The aquarium is even listed in the Guinness Book of Records. It is a must-see, especially for tourists with children. The aquarium is located in the Dubai Mall.

The most amazing thing is that in the center of it there is a completely transparent tunnel that allows you to admire the marine inhabitants and take pictures against their background. Sharks and stingrays look especially picturesque. In the aquarium, you can swim with sharks, if you have the certificate of an experienced diver, or go in a boat with a glass bottom.

Another attraction of the city is Al Mamzer Beach Park. It has a large and beautiful territory. The beach even received the Blue Flag. In total, the park has five beaches located in the coves. Each of them is equipped with showers and changing cabins.



The park has playgrounds and exercise machines, as well as swimming pools, cafes. There are few people here on weekdays. However, after the closure of another park (Jumeirah Beach) in Al-Mamzer in 2014, more people are now flocking.

Resting in Dubai, you just need to visit the Palm Islands, which are the second building of mankind, visible from space. They were created by the country's authorities precisely to attract tourists and to increase territories at the expense of the Persian Gulf.

The entire artificial archipelago consists of three islands, all of them are collected in a palm tree. The largest of them is Palm Deira, and the other two are Palm Jebel Ali and Palm Jumeirah. The islands are surrounded by barrier reefs to protect against waves.

Resting with children, you must definitely go to the zoo. This is a great place not only for children, but also for adults. Dubai Zoo is considered the oldest in the entire Arabian Peninsula. An interesting fact is that on its territory not palm trees grow, but deciduous trees familiar to us. The park is home to 230 different animals and 400 reptiles.

Dubai offers not only the world's best beaches, entertainment and shopping, but also an impressive list of attractions: from the highest skyscrapers to archaeological parks. The metropolis is simply filled with unique places. All the amazing sights are on the lands once conquered from the desert.
