Very useful activity

Where to begin?

The main advantage when creating such models is that the minimum amount of materials and devices is used. To decorate finished products, you can use markers, paints, stickers and so on.



Paper airplane: step by step instructions

  • Step 1. Take a piece of paper and bend it in the middle so that the fold line is exactly in the center. After that, deploy it back.




  • Step 2. We bend the upper corners from both sides to the line where the central fold passes.




  • Step 3. Fold the sheet horizontally so that the resulting triangle looks nose down.




  • Step 4. Bend the edges on both sides, forming the so-called ‚Äútowing flaps‚Äù.




  • Step 5. Bend the wings. To do this, add two more leaves to the center of the plane. The figure is already starting to look like a real paper airplane.




  • Step 6. We make additional folds on the wings parallel to the central fold.




  • Step 7. The airplane is ready to fly!




  • Step 8: bonus. If you want your paper airplane to fly further and longer, you need to fix a regular paper clip in front on the case. Extra weight will help him fly a greater distance.
