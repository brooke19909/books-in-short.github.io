The most expensive phone in Russia: an overview

When buying a mobile phone, most users pay attention primarily to the technical characteristics of the device, and the appearance features in the best case come down to choosing the color of the case from several standard options. But this can not be said about the rich who seek to get a unique model, albeit not with the best characteristics.

Vertu Signature Diamond

One of the most expensive mobile phones in Russia is designed for wealthy people who value sophisticated elegance. The classic case of the keypad is made of white and yellow gold and platinum. The item is encrusted with 170 rounded diamonds. Vertu supports the GSM standard. The antenna is built-in.

The lithium-ion battery lasts only five and a half hours in talk mode, and the battery has 400 hours of standby time. The phone supports Bluetooth technology, Email-client, MMS and GPRS. Despite such modest characteristics, phones from the exclusive Vertu series are repeatedly included in the top of the most expensive, elegant and luxurious.

In total, about two hundred copies of the Vertu Signature Diamond were created. Each costs about 88 thousand dollars. This is almost 6 million rubles.

iPhone Princess

Modification of the first generation iPhone was developed by world famous jeweler Peter Aloyson in 2008. Over the years, the cost of the model has not changed. For iPhone, Princess Plus will have to pay $ 176,400 (for comparison: the price of the serial iPhone is $ 400 or $ 500 - the final cost depends on the amount of internal memory of the device), that is, 12 million rubles.

Often the most expensive phones in Russia are made of gold (18 carats), in the case of this model white is chosen. The case is additionally decorated around the perimeter with 318 diamonds. Each pebble is 17.75 carats. Almost 200 gemstones are crafted using the unique Princess technique.

There is an inexpensive version of the iPhone Princess Plus smartphone, which is decorated with only 180 diamonds (classic cut). The cost of such a designer product is 66,150 dollars, that is 4.5 million rubles.

As for technical specifications, one of the most expensive Apple-branded cell phones in Russia is the most common iPhone with 8 or 16 GB of memory. A smartphone is made only by hand and by reservation. The first owner of a unique gadget was a businessman from Russia who wished to remain anonymous.

Vertu Signature Cobra

In 2017, Verty introduced to the general public a mobile phone, which is released in a very limited series. Each of the eight existing smartphones costs 360 thousand dollars (24.2 million rubles). Phones are collected manually with the assistance of specialists from the French brand Boucheron.

Each smartphone is covered with 439 rubies that adorn the body of the snake, and the cobra's eyes are made of two emeralds. Gresso Luxor Las Vegas Jackpot

In 2010, Gresso announced the release of the new Gresso Luxor Las Vegas Jackpot and the less expensive version of Luxor Las Vegas. The design of the ancient Egypt and modern Las Vegas inspired the development of the unique design of both the most expensive phones in Russia.

The case is inlaid with black diamonds, which are very rare in nature, made of gold. The thickness of the device is only 12 mm. Developments of the Gresso brand traditionally involve wood in design. The back panel of one of the most expensive models is made of a unique African ebony.

Another innovation is the keyboard. Each individual key is made of sapphire. Gemstones are polished by hand with a fine diamond tool. The letters and numbers are laser printed.

There are only three Gresso Luxor Las Vegas Jackpot phones in the world. An individual copy number is engraved on the rear panel. The cost of the model is 30 million rubles, and the budget novelty - Luxor Las Vegas - costs 650 thousand rubles.

Diamond Crypto Smartphone

The unique device is not only made of the most expensive materials, but also has cryptographic protection. All parts of the case are made of 950 platinum, the key and logo are in 18-carat pink gold, and the ends are made of black or ebony.

Buttons Diamond Crypto Smartphone made of precious metal, engraved by hand. The navigation key is pink gold with a diamond in the center. Side buttons - faceted diamonds in a natural blue hue, each of which weighs 1.2 carats. On the back and front parts, as well as on the sides of the machine, 25 diamonds are cut in the Princess cut. Of these, five are natural blue on each side.

Judging by the reviews, the most expensive phones in Russia are distinguished by special cryptographic capabilities. Manufacturers of other devices in this price category rarely think about technical equipment, which can not be said about the developers of Diamond Crypto Smartphone.

Cryptographic capabilities are provided by the TMS 320 VC 5416 processor. Encryption is based on the parameters of elliptic curves and is carried out using a key (256 bits). Messages are encrypted, including voice messages. All data in memory is also encrypted. The smartphone is running the Windows operating system.

The cost of Diamond Crypto Smartphone is 1.3 million US dollars, that is, a little more than 90 million rubles.
