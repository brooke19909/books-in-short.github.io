Description of the best hotels in Lytkarino

The city of Lytkarino has earned popularity among residents of the capital and the region thanks to its famous career, where lovers of beach holidays and active sports come together every summer.

The quarry - also known as Volkushinsky - was created in the 70s by artificial means. And at the moment it is a large pond with clear water, surrounded by five well-equipped beaches. On the beaches there are volleyball courts, gazebos and barbecue facilities for cooking barbecue, cafes and cozy gazebos with a hookah. In addition, in the summer, there are regular noisy parties.

Fans of extreme sports can enjoy water rides, water skiing or a catamaran. If you are planning to spend a weekend or vacation in this wonderful place, then you should pay attention to the list of the best 5 hotels in Lytkarino (Moscow region). Accommodation in them will allow you to have a great time in this wonderful city.
