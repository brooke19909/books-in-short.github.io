Ramayana Koh Chang Resort 4 * (Koh Chang, Thailand): reviews and photos of tourists

Ramayana Koh Chang Resort 4 * hotel is designed for a beach holiday, but it is not at all at sea. You will have to go from it to the coast, but the hotel provides a daily shuttle service, both there and back. The rooms at the hotel are private bungalows with a private patio. The hotel has a beautiful garden and pool. For those who are attracted by the calm, blissful pastime on clean beaches and solitude with the sea, this is the most suitable place to relax. We will try to describe it as objectively as possible, based on the feedback of travelers.



Where is it located, what are the infrastructure and territory

Hotel Ramayana Koh Chang Resort 4 * on the map of Thailand can be found thirty kilometers from the city of Trat, and twelve - from Klong Prao beach. Not very far from the hotel is one of the famous waterfalls. This is approximately the middle of the island, so from the hotel you can get literally to any place - both north and south. Major iconic spots are also nearby. The hotel area is very large and just surrounded by greenery and flowers. This is a kind of artificial mini jungle. At every step - comfortable benches, gazebos where you can relax, figures in the style of ancient Thai architecture. There are even several decorative water mills. A ten-minute walk from the hotel is a bank where you can change currency.



Koh Chang

This is one of the most popular Thai islands in recent times. It is rediscovered by lovers of relaxation in the wilderness. This island is located four hundred kilometers from Bangkok, very close to the border with Cambodia. To get here, you need to fly to the airport, which is located in the city of Trat (the capital of the province of the same name). There fly planes from Bangkok. And from there to Koh Chang reach by ferry (about forty minutes). Rooms

When you come here, you will first be poured delicious juice, and then taken to a bungalow on a mini-machine. The guest rooms at the Ramayana Koh Chang Resort 4 * hotel are very spacious, you can say luxurious, air-conditioned, with wooden floors. They are decorated in an interesting style. It is typical of traditional Thai homes. Each room has a seating area, kettle and coffee maker, fridge, safe, hairdryer, bathrobes, slippers, beach towels, umbrellas, lanterns. Four poster beds. There is even a foot massager. Many vacationers believe that the "five" can envy the filling of rooms. Rooms are located in private bungalows with balconies.

Services

Hotel Ramayana Koh Chang Resort 4 * throughout its territory has Wi-Fi coverage. It is free for guests, including in the rooms. You can also use the hotel library - there are books in Russian. There is a spa center on site, where you can trust the hands of experienced massage therapists and bask in the sauna. There are programs designed for the whole day. The tourists who visited them did not regret either the time spent or the money. And there is a good gym. If you want to explore the surroundings on your own, a car and bicycle rental service is at your disposal. Just do not forget that in Thailand there is left-hand traffic. A laundry service is also available, and you can also iron clothes. There is a nice playground with several swings and a sandbox. Transfer to the beach and back to the hotel is free. They carry there every half hour. You can get to the waterfall under the same conditions. But to return from an excursion to the hotel, you will have to pay extra. The hotel staff is helpful, very polite and with a sense of humor. He is constantly busy cleaning the territory.



Ramayana Koh Chang Resort 4 *: beach, sea, pool

At sea, tourists are transported to the territory of the five-star hotel "Deva". There is a great beach, as in the picture, beautifully equipped, with umbrellas and sun loungers. True, sometimes there are jellyfish in the water. But there is a wonderful entry into the water, magnificent sunsets and amazing restaurants, right on the sand, with snow-white tablecloths and cheap prices. Another beach is closer to the hotel and can be reached on foot. The distance does not exceed a kilometer. In the middle of the hotel is a cool pool, and next to it is a jacuzzi with hot water and bubbles. It is medium in size, very clean. It is pleasant to swim there, especially in the evenings. There is a compartment for children, nearby - good sun loungers.



Excursions and trips

Tourists claim that this is the most advantageous hotel in the vicinity - Ramayana Koh Chang Resort 4 *. O. Chang has several main attractions, and almost all of them are not very far from the hotel. Travelers recommend riding an elephant farm and waterfalls. In addition, if you are a lover of trekking, the hotel can advise you on many hiking trails through the jungle, mountains and waterfalls. Tourists also praise the good five-island boat trip. They take you all day by sea, give you a swim with a mask, and also conduct an individual tour of the national park. The hotel is an excellent starting point for independent travel to Cambodia. Transfer from it to Siam Riepa costs three hundred butts per person.

Ramayana Koh Chang Resort 4 *: reviews

Despite the fact that the hotel does not stand at sea, tourists really like it. They call the hotel fabulous. Here you can live in a real jungle and at the same time feel very comfortable.
