Hotel "October" (Ulyanovsk): how to get from the airport

Arriving in a new city, we want to be in our room as soon as possible and not spend much time on the road to the hotel. Hotel "Oktyabrskaya" in Ulyanovsk is located 15 kilometers from the Brataevka airport. From here, the hotel can be reached by taxi. On the road it will take you about 35-40 minutes. You can also order a transfer, which will be provided by the Oktyabrskaya Hotel (Ulyanovsk). Her phone can easily be found on the Internet. Thus, you will have the opportunity to place an order in advance, and at the same time reserve a room.

How to get from the railway station

The train station is 8 km from the hotel. In addition to taxis, the destination can be reached by public transport. Tram No. 4, buses number 40, 26, 21, 1, as well as fixed-route taxis No. 94, 91, 64, 55, 40, 38, 33, 1, run from the station in this direction.





Accommodation

Cozy and comfortable living conditions are provided by the Oktyabrskaya Hotel. Ulyanovsk is a friendly city, which is reflected in the atmosphere of the hotel. You can choose any of these accommodation options:

  • Superior room is equipped with a double orthopedic bed. The bathroom is equipped with a bath. The room has recently been refurbished and fully upgraded.
  • The superior standard has a pair of single beds, as well as a desk. A distinctive feature of the room is the thematic design, which combines the spirit of the Soviet era, as well as modern interior trends.
  • Junior Suite - Room with a double bed, decorated in soft pastel colors. In addition to the berth, there is also a sitting corner and a desk with additional spotlights.
  • Executive Junior Suite, like the previous version, is equipped with an orthopedic king size bed. The design is dominated by natural wood and leather interior elements.
  • Suite is a spacious two-room suite. The bedroom has a large orthopedic bed, a workplace and a sitting area. Behind the interior door there is a cozy living room equipped with leather upholstered furniture. The bathroom has a hydromassage.

Negative reviews

Despite all the advantages, there are still certain flaws in the Oktyabrskaya Hotel (Ulyanovsk). Guest reviews focus on the following negative points:

  • the rooms do not have air conditioning (in hot weather, guests are given fans, but they do not save the situation);
  • at the time of weddings and other events, the hotel restaurant is closed for guests;
  • the standard rooms have not been renovated for a long time, and therefore the atmosphere is rather gloomy;
  • maids do not clean the plumbing in the bathrooms;
  • constant interruptions with wireless Internet (a more or less stable signal can be caught only near the reception, reception area);
  • old creaking elevator that breaks often;
  • too narrow single beds;
  • the room does not have a list of what should be present in the room, and therefore during the check at the time of eviction you can not prove your case;
  • the refrigerator in the room is very loud;
  • the floors in the rooms do not look very clean;
  • there is no possibility of installing extra beds;
  • if you arrive for breakfast after 8 hours, the most delicious dishes will already be sorted out (food stocks at the distribution are not replenished);
  • strong audibility between numbers;
  • there are no outlets in the bathroom, and therefore you will not be able to use a hairdryer in front of the mirror (by the way, it is not in the room);
  • high prices for laundry services;
  • in the rooms the number of dishes and cutlery does not match the number of guests (this also applies to hygiene sets in the bathroom);
  • inconvenient shower holder complicates the bathing process;
  • frequent inconsistencies with the reservation (especially if ordering a room not through the official website, but through third-party resources);
  • overpriced breakfast (250 rubles per person);
  • too expensive drinks and snacks in the minibar (and the guest will find out the price already at the time of eviction, since there is no price list in the room);
  • unreliable locks on the entrance doors of the rooms (at the same time there is no safe in which the valuables can be hidden in the room);
  • there are no windows in the dining room where breakfast is served, which is why it is quite stuffy and uncomfortable there;
  • in winter the rooms are very poorly heated (fortunately, there is a heater);
  • in winter no one clears the parking lot from the snow (and this despite the fact that it is paid);
  • the restaurant is open until eleven o'clock in the evening, and therefore guests arriving at night remain hungry;
  • malfunctioning remotes from the televisions in the rooms (hotel staff do not respond to calls about this problem);
  • the furniture in the rooms is saturated with tobacco smoke;
  • it takes a long time until the water in the shower drains and becomes warm (this problem is especially relevant for the upper floors);
  • intercity telephone line does not always work;
  • of all budget rooms, only one is equipped with a full double bed, and the rest are single;
  • on the windows of some rooms there are no handles, which makes it impossible to air them;
  • there is no practice in the restaurant to write down the amount of the check to the account of the room (you have to pay on the spot);
  • when the water in the cooler ends, the bottle is not immediately changed to full (you have to look for water on other floors);
  • the facade of the hotel needs updating, and also it would not hurt to break the lawns and flower beds;
  • there is no lock on the door leading to the bathroom, which is very uncomfortable if more than one person lives in the room.
