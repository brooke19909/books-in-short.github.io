Hotel Melissa Garden Hotel 3 * (Turkey / Alanya / Konakli): photos and reviews of tourists from Russia

Melissa Garden Hotel 3 * hotel offers guests from different regions of the world a pretty decent vacation. Most tour operators recommend this particular place to spend their vacations, since the cost of tours here is very small - an average of 30-35 thousand rubles for a seven-day stay. Melissa Garden Hotel 3 * often occupies a leading position in the ratings of Turkish middle-class holiday destinations, along with another hotel - Iko Melissa Garden Hotel 3 *. Often, travelers evaluate the living conditions in it at 8 out of 10 points, which is a pretty good indicator that deserves attention.



general information

Melissa Garden Apart Hotel 3 * received its first guests in 2017. The complex immediately attracted the attention of tourists due to the modern buildings built, which are equipped with new furniture, the best climate system and have fresh repairs in the rooms, made according to all European standards.

At the moment, the territory of the hotel is 2250 square meters. m, which is enough to accommodate a small hull and aquazone on it. In the open square Melissa Garden Hotel 3 * there is a small garden that delights tourists with its abundant flowering throughout the holiday season.

Location

The hotel is located in the small village of Konakli, which is located 12 km from the largest resort city of Turkey - Alanya. The place is quite quiet, but despite this, representatives of the younger generation prefer to relax here.

The hotel is located on the first coastline, which provides walking access to its guests to the beach.

As for the location of the hotel relative to the nodes of air transport, the nearest international airport, where all foreign tourists arrive, is located 110 kilometers in the resort city of Antalya.



Rooms

Tourists are offered a small number of rooms - 69. All of them are located within the building and are divided into two categories: standard and family. There are no apartments designed for people with disabilities. Disabled people can rent a room here on a general basis.

The apartments offer tourists excellent living conditions, taking into account all the needs of a modern person. Each room has wireless Internet access, so guests can always stay in touch with their family and friends. There is a private bathroom, equipped with a full set of plumbing, represented by a shower and sink.

Of all rooms there is an individual exit to the balcony, where a minimal set of furniture designed for relaxation is installed: a pair of soft armchairs and a small round table.

Standard

Standard class rooms in Melissa Garden Hotel 3 * (Turkey) the overwhelming majority, a total of 67. All of them are decorated in a classic style, using contrasting shades in the color scheme that tourists from Russia like.

This type of room is designed for two guests. To accommodate a large bed of the European type. Several apartments have two single beds instead of a large bed. If necessary, an extra bed can be provided - a folding bed of a European type.

Large sliding wardrobes are equipped for storing personal belongings of tourists, and for small items there is a pair of side tables installed near the beds.

In their free time, guests can watch TV connected to the satellite broadcasting system. It is a medium sized pendant plasma screen.

Entertainment

Guests of the Melissa Garden Hotel 3 * (Alanya) in their reviews often talk about small entertainment programs that are held here every evening. They are represented by a disco, which can last throughout the night.

The hotel has a couple of animators involved in entertaining guests. As tourists from Russia note in their comments, each of them can find an approach to guests of different age categories, however, the negative point is that they do not speak Russian.

Nutrition

In reviews of the Melissa Garden Hotel 3 *, travelers from Russia often talk about the food they provide at the hotel during the holidays. It is provided as part of the all-inclusive concept and includes daily visits to the buffet, which is located in the main restaurant of the hotel. According to the guests, quite original and very nutritious dishes prepared in the best traditions of Turkish and European cuisines are served here. The all-inclusive system also implies free use by guests of all drinks made on the territory of the Melissa Garden Hotel 3 *.

A considerable number of tourists recommend a glimpse into a bar located on the territory of the aquazone. In it, professional bartenders will certainly surprise any gourmet with original cocktails and soft drinks, among which there are both non-alcoholic and alcoholic ones. The menu offers a large assortment of snacks, as well as oriental sweets.



Aquazone

Melissa Garden Hotel 3 * (Turkey, Alanya) offers tourists a wonderful holiday on the territory of the sun terrace, which is part of a small water zone. It has everything so that each guest has the opportunity to sit comfortably and soak under the rays of the Turkish sun. To protect against the harmful effects of ultraviolet radiation, guests can open a beach umbrella above each sunbed.

In the very center of the terrace there is a small pool in which everyone can carry out water procedures. The water in it is cleaned regularly and heated by the sun. For active pastime of guests in the pool there is one slide.



Conditions for children

A huge number of tourists from Russia recommends the Melissa Garden Hotel 3 * for family holidays. Such frequent such recommendations are associated with a considerable list of conditions intended both for the babies themselves and for the convenience of their parents.

First of all, guests in positive comments note the availability of cots in the hotel, which can be installed in the room upon request. Tourists also talk about the availability of special highchairs in a catering establishment and a special menu, which contains only the brightest dishes that can conquer small gourmets.

If desired, parents can send their child to a special club where the animator will entertain him with games, watching cartoons and educational programs, as well as just fun communication. Here the child can play and communicate with his peers, which develops his communication skills.

The aquazone has a small pool for children, and a bright playground is nearby, where small guests can have fun and actively spend free time.



Beach

Hotel Melissa Garden is located on the first coastline and is separated from it by just one road, for the intersection of which an underground passage is provided. The distance between the main building and the Mediterranean coast is only 70 meters.

On the sandy territory, according to travelers, there are all conditions for a pleasant stay. The beach here is clean, the water is clear, and the sea is rather shallow, which is especially appreciated by parents resting with children.

Everyone can sit for free on sun loungers under huge umbrellas. Towels are not available for use, so many tourists recommend taking them with them from the hotel. For entertainment, a small volleyball court is provided here, equipment for the game can be obtained at a special point.

Price policy

Especially a positive moment in vacation at the Melissa Garden hotel, many tourists consider the cost of the tour. According to vacationers, the price of a week-long trip to this hotel is about 35,000 rubles, which is quite inexpensive for a place with excellent conditions for recreation, located almost on the seashore. Moreover, this cost without fail includes transportation costs on both sides, standard room rental, as well as insurance premiums and a transfer service. The last part of the costs is included in the package without fail, as the hotel is located at a fairly large distance from the airport. If the transfer is not provided, the traveler will be forced to cover more than a hundred kilometers on his own, in a tropical climate. Many comments of vacationers say that the transfer is carried out on a modern passenger car equipped with air conditioning. Travel time is pretty fast.
