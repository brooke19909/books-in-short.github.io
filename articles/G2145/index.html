Paradise holidays: the best beaches in the world

Hot summer is at its zenith, so if you haven‚Äôt had time to go on vacation yet, now is the time to choose the paradise where you will go to enjoy the sea and the sun. Experienced travelers have already named the best beaches in the world, the photos on which are just wonderful, and the vacation turns into a fairy tale.

The American journal National Geographic, which publishes materials on nature and culture, has taken this issue seriously. So the rating was compiled, which included ten of the best places on the planet. So, according to this list, the best beaches in the world are ...

Seychelles

In the first place on the list are the magnificent beaches of the Seychelles. The azure sea, coral islands, unusually clean and fluffy sand, small but cozy coves. All this forms excellent beaches of coral origin.





Perhaps this paradise has a small problem - there are too many islands and, accordingly, beaches on them, which leads some tourists to bewilderment, because it is easy to get lost there.

In general, an ideal vacation spot than Seychelles probably does not exist. Amazing landscapes, pristine nature, amazing underwater world and endless beaches - this is a real paradise.

Maldives

The second place in the list of "The best beaches in the world" is occupied by the Maldives. The beaches of these islands are considered the most beautiful and idyllic. Here you will find turquoise lagoons with clear water, shimmering with amazing shades, beaches with white sand and a cozy atmosphere of tranquility. In the Maldives, wide beaches blend perfectly with flowers and palm groves. Holidays on the islands will turn into a paradise tale for you.





In the Maldives, in addition to the opportunity to wallow in a soft sand or on a hammock, there are many other active entertainments. You can go diving and discover the beauty of the amazing underwater world. Or take part in the hunt for crabs. Or have a romantic dinner on one of the neighboring uninhabited islands.

Bora Bora, Tahiti

Bora Bora is a huge atoll. It is surrounded on all sides by small islands and coral reefs. This place became the third in the rating of ‚ÄúThe Best Beaches in the World‚Äù thanks to the mother-of-pearl lagoon, perfect white sand and clear water. In addition, all hotels on the island are located on stilts above the water. It makes a lasting impression. The most beautiful landscapes can be observed at sunset and dawn, when the lights of hotels fade in the water. The whole island is a huge beach, so you can plunge into the water wherever you want.

Hamptons, New York

The city beaches of the Hamptons are located on the Atlantic coast. There is absolutely no civilization and service, so you have to sunbathe in the old fashioned way: on straws and under an umbrella. People come here both by car and by boat or yacht.

The beaches of the Hamptons are always different: one time they are completely full of vacationers, and the other time they are deserted, waves are raging here today, and calm tomorrow. This is an ideal place for those who decide to combine a rest on the sea and an excursion tour of the "Big Apple" of America.





Lan Kai, Hawaii

Hawaii, Hawaii - these are undoubtedly the best beaches in the world! A truly magical place where a relaxing beach holiday smoothly flows into a vibrant nightlife. Luxurious white sands and shady coconut palms are a real paradise, allowing you to plunge into the atmosphere of the Bounty advertisement.

There are tons of pleasures and entertainment for every taste. Diving, surfing, scuba diving, yachting, even turtles and ... coconut paradise.

Nantucket, Massachusetts

Nantucket is a small lonely island in the Atlantic Ocean. This is the territory of the United States. Despite the fact that this island cannot be proud of its size, it attracts tourists with its huge (!) Amount of excellent sandy beaches. This place is ideal for anyone who is picky about the temperature of the water. It is always warm here, and water at any time of the year is like fresh milk.
The infrastructure of the island is at the highest level, which is good news. Any water entertainment is available here. There is a highlight: feeding small sharks.

Fraser Island, Australia

The most beautiful beaches in the world - on the island of Fraser. Snow-white sand and crystal water will not leave anyone indifferent. And sand lakes and huge dunes will cause delight. The sea here is restless - waves are walking all the time. This attracts a huge number of windsurfing enthusiasts and professionals.

This Australian island is a place wild and untouched by civilization. Here you can feel like Robinson Crusoe. Only the main thing is not to get carried away.

St. Bartholomew Island, Caribbean

The coast of the island is an alternating cove. Indescribable beauty! Lots of vibrant corals and underwater inhabitants await you during your diving activities. And at the bar, you will be treated to the best Caribbean cocktails.





Sandy beaches - not a single pebble, soft sand, turquoise water surface and a hammock under a coconut tree. What more could you ask for in this sun-drenched paradise? Right, a bottle of rum!

Langkawi, Malaysia

This island is preferred by active tourists who do not want to bask in the snow-white sand all day and contemplate the azure sea. Nightlife is booming here. Continuous parties, loud music and a sea of ‚Äã‚Äãalcohol. Service is at a high level. All kinds of extreme water activities are offered.

And for those who are tired of the endless stream of fun, there is the opportunity to retire somewhere in a cozy and calm bay. But, if music gets you there too, then escape to the nearest uninhabited island. Fortunately, there are a lot of them.

Kauai, Hawaii

And the last in the list of the Good Beaches of the World ranking is Hawaii again. Only this time the island of Kauai.





The beaches are perhaps the only attraction here. However, they are really excellent. Most vacationers come here just to wallow in these fluffy sands. It also has great service. In the ocean you can see the famous Manta - the largest of all types of stingrays. Therefore, Kauai attracts fans to snorkel.

Well, it is impossible not to note the unforgettable sunsets and sunrises of this paradise.
