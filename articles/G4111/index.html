Retona washing machine: owner reviews

Washing is a process that cannot be dispensed with. Of course, you can constantly take things to dry cleaning, but this is not suitable for everyone. Since ancient times, people only washed clothes with their hands. A washing machine was later released, which greatly facilitated this process. Now these devices have improved as much as possible, thanks to which a person only needs to load things into the hatch, close the door and press the button. The machine will do the rest itself. Technology does not stand still. Now, ultrasound has also begun to be used for washing, although previously it was used only in the military industry. Cleaning of the tissue occurs under the influence of acoustic waves that penetrate between the fibers. Is this technology effective? Reviews will help to answer this question. "Retona" is an ultrasonic washing machine that works according to the described principle. Many people find this appliance an ideal alternative to handwashing. But there is such an audience that is extremely negative. Let's see what a Retona washing machine is. What are its advantages and disadvantages? We also consider recommendations that will help make washing with an ultrasonic machine as efficient as possible.



Description

The Retona washing machine, reviews of which will be presented below, is notable for its small size. It is equipped with a radiator and a power adapter, which are interconnected by a wire. The device itself is light, only the unit that is connected to the mains is heavy.

The case of the emitter is made of plastic. Inside is a generator (piezoceramic element). It is this part that, when an electric current passes, begins to emit ultrasound. The human ear does not hear it, so work cannot provoke a feeling of discomfort. As the manufacturer of "Retona" assures, when the device is turned on, it generates acoustic waves that simply knock out the accumulated dirt from the fabric.

Ultrasound has long been used in industry. With its help they clean any surfaces. This method is most in demand for inaccessible places, for example, inside metal pipes. Its effectiveness will depend on power. In washing machines, it does not exceed 100 kHz. In industry, ultrasound is used ten times higher, and therefore the performance is much better. If you take into account that a lot of water is poured during washing, then the power of the machine decreases markedly.



Benefits

If you believe the reviews, the Retona machine has undeniable advantages. The owners include them:

  • Save energy. Compared to standard automatic machines, Retona consumes significantly less electricity during operation.
  • Removing odors.
  • Fabric color update.
  • The appliance disinfects items during washing.
  • Noiselessness.
  • Small size and light weight.
  • Reasonable price (up to 4000 rubles).
  • Gentle washing process.
  • Things are not deformed.
  • The risks of short circuit are minimized.

Do all buyers agree with the list of advantages? There are consumers who are skeptical that the Retona device can effectively remove things from pollution. It is important to understand that the result will depend on some factors. These include the degree of contamination and the powder used.



disadvantages

Having talked about the advantages, it is worth voicing the disadvantages of Retona. The reviews of the owners say that this model is not suitable as the main machine. It is not possible to completely replace a stationary device with an ultrasonic one. Also, do not forget that "Retona" does not exempt from manual rinsing and push-ups. The manufacturer strongly recommends that you do not leave the device unattended when turned on. For greater efficiency, the emitter must be moved to different places, as well as turn over the linen.

The most significant drawback of this model is that it will not be able to wipe things with heavy pollution.

Brief characteristics

Before we find out what the Reton reviews received, let's get acquainted with its characteristics.

  • For operation, the device must be connected to a 220 V network.
  • It can be used in water, the temperature of which does not exceed 80 ¬∞ and does not fall below + 40 ¬∞.
  • The mass of the device is 300 gr.
  • The power of acoustic waves is 100 kHz.

You can turn on the machine when the emitter is immersed in water.



Retona washing machine, owner reviews

Customers who have already purchased and tested this device left comments. What do they say? First of all, if things are very dirty, then use an ultrasonic machine is useless. In such a situation, it is ineffective. Regarding stain removal, the opinions of the owners were divided. Some argue that, for example, wine can be easily removed with Retona, while others disagree. They say that stains are not completely eliminated unless special detergents are used. This machine will be ideal for cleaning blankets, curtains, pillows, rugs. Things turn out fresh, have no unpleasant odors.





Useful Tips

In the reviews of the Retona apparatus, you can read the recommendations that will help you easily achieve the desired effect when washing. Consumers advise:

  • Use any container. The material does not matter. It is possible to erase "Retona" even in a glass container.
  • Pour hot water, but not boiling water, whose temperature is above 80 ¬∞.
  • Add the amount of powder indicated on the detergent package.
  • Distribute linen evenly.
  • Place the emitter in the center of the container.
  • When connecting the device to the network, the indicator on the case should light up.
  • Stir the laundry several times during washing. Before this, be sure to disconnect the machine from power.
  • The wash cycle should not be less than one hour. If necessary, time can be increased. There are no restrictions.

When the washing is finished, you must first turn off the machine, and then begin to remove the laundry from the container. After that, rinse and squeeze it.

In the reviews of "Reton" it is said that after washing it is necessary to rinse the device from the remnants of detergent. You will also need to wipe it dry. Fold the device carefully so as not to accidentally bend the wire. After that, you need to put it in a box.

What is forbidden to do

There are a number of warnings in the instructions for the device, which must be heeded so that there are no problems when using the Reton machine. Owner reviews confirm the importance of this information.

The manufacturer forbids using the device if there are mechanical damages on the case. Also, in no case should you turn the device on / off with wet hands. While boiling the laundry, it is strictly forbidden to use Retona, since the water temperature exceeds 90 ¬∞, which can lead to deformation of the plastic case.

You can not repair the device yourself, as this can completely disable it. It is necessary to protect the machine from shock, mechanical squeezing and other forces that could harm the body or the entire device.



Who needs such a washing machine

In the end, we will review the reviews about Reton, which say who this machine can come in handy for.

  • Often traveling people.
  • Students living in a dormitory.
  • For washing in the summer at the cottage.
  • People who have a lot of clothes made of delicate fabrics or with rhinestones.
  • Allergy sufferers and those suffering from skin diseases.
