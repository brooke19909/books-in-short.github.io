Where to go to relax?

The small island of Bali offers an unforgettable vacation for young couples and those who have been married for many years. And the expensive resort of Nusa Dua with the most luxurious hotels and vibrant nightlife in the clubs seems to be intended for everyone who wants to have a real break.
