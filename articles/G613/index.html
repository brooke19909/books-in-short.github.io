Some significant changes in terms of design, compared with previous models, the new gadget did not happen. The ASUS Nexus 7 tablet is easily recognizable by its outlines, but it could not do without some additions.





The very concept of an elongated device with smooth contours and attractive side faces was not touched, but the new device is now thinner and the weight, respectively, is less by 50 g. Compared to its predecessors, the ASUS Google Nexus 7 is slightly extended up, and the width of the device has been slightly reduced.
