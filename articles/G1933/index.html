Considering the inexpensive hotels of Smolensk, the reviews of which are given in this article, it is worth highlighting the "Change". On the territory of the institution there is a bar and a cafe, which is not all the inexpensive hotels in Smolensk differ. Great cooks will always be happy to treat you with dishes of Russian and European cuisine. There is also a sports and fitness complex, which provides a huge range of services.
