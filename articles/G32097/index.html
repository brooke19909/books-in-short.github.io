Resorts on the Caspian Sea, Azerbaijan: reviews, photos

The Republic of Azerbaijan was somewhat affected by the outflow of holidaymakers in the 90s of the last century. But now the tourism industry is rapidly gaining momentum. How to get to Azerbaijan

The largest lake in the world washes the country from the east. It is there that the resorts on the Caspian Sea are located. Azerbaijan is connected with the Russian Federation by air and ground. To get to the famous resorts of the country, you must first arrive in Baku, the benefit is that this city is also located on the sea. Given the distance of two thousand three hundred kilometers between the capitals of Azerbaijan and Russia, the best transport is the plane. Every day, Aeroflot aircraft depart from Moscow Sheremetyevo-2 (Terminal E) to Baku. Flights from Domodedovo operate with the same regularity. They are carried out by S7 and Azerbaijan Airlines. Three hours in the air - and you are already in Baku.

It is not necessary to go to Belokamennaya to go to the resorts of Azerbaijan. Regular flights fly from Baku to St. Petersburg, Nizhny Novgorod and Novosibirsk. By train Moscow - Baku the journey will stretch for sixty hours. To get to the resorts of Azerbaijan by car, you need to go through Dagestan. You can follow the roundabout route - through Iran. Then you will find yourself in the southernmost resort of the country - Nakhichevan. But for such a trip, you should open an Iranian visa. But in order to enter Azerbaijan, it is enough for a Russian tourist to have only a foreign passport. These are the requirements of the law.



When to come to the resorts of the Caspian Sea

Azerbaijan lies in the same latitudes as Italy, as well as the French Cote d'Azur. But, thanks to the continental climate, there is a clearer change of seasons. Winter is pretty cold here. There are frosts up to minus 10 degrees. But the summer is very hot and dry. The thermometer can sometimes jump up to +40 degrees. But in the sea resorts, the heat is tempered by the presence of a large amount of water and a fresh breeze. The best time to visit the country is from April to October. The swimming season begins on the May holidays, and ends officially at the end of September. Although some vacationers splash in the sea in April and October.

The nature of Azerbaijan

In this mountainous country, altitudinal zonation is well defined. Therefore, here you can meet nine of the eleven existing climatic zones in the world. In Azerbaijan, you can take a mini-journey around the globe, descending from the tundra to the hot and humid subtropics. Another attraction of the country is oil. Black gold is not only the backbone of the Azerbaijani economy. Here, oil has healing qualities. Many health resorts practice therapeutic wraps of clients in black gold. Resorts on the Caspian Sea bring considerable income to the country. Azerbaijan is developing a hotel base and tourism infrastructure. Old Soviet resorts are being reconstructed according to European standards. But new ones are being built - literally from scratch. An example is the Nardaran-Kurdakhan resort. After completion of construction, this chain of tourist hotels will not be inferior to such untwisted places like Antalya and Dubai.
