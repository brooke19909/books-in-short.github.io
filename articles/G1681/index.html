Do-it-yourself paper flowers on the wall: what paper to use, stages of execution, ideas for design, photo

Recently, it has become a fashionable trend to decorate the interior of apartments, houses and banquet halls with huge paper flowers. They attract attention with their originality, bright colors and unusual manufacturing techniques. Although such products are short-lived, they are loved by masters from all over the world, because it is not difficult to make them, there is little material left, and it is inexpensive. You can update the interior every month, and in the halls for celebrations and even more often.

In the article, we will consider how to make paper flowers on a wall with your own hands, what material you need to prepare for work. We will share our knowledge about the methods and methods of manufacturing different flowers and leaflets, how to fix them on the wall and among themselves. The photographs presented will help to understand the material presented and choose something interesting for yourself.

Necessary materials

Large paper flowers do it yourself with a variety of materials. Bright and colorful, they are obtained from a thin isolon from 0.2 mm to 0.5 mm. It is purchased in construction stores, as it is considered an insulating material. You can cut it with simple scissors, since the sheets are quite soft. It comes in different colors, so it‚Äôs easy to find the right one for the job.

Simpler paper flowers are made with their own hands from a material with a density of 100 g / m 2 , rolls of corrugated paper, some products can even be made from thick napkins. They glue together the parts with either hot glue or thick and fresh PVA.

In order to collect a beautiful and lush flower, you will need: cardboard for cutting patterns of petals and leaves, a simple pencil for outlining contours, scissors for cutting elements. If you plan to make a rose, then you can bend the flower petals with a knitting needle or a round wooden stick.

To work, free up space in the room and prepare a flat surface on the table, removing all unnecessary. We begin the explanation with the manufacture of do-it-yourself flowers from paper napkins.

Dahlia from napkins

For work, buy large dense wipes. First, you need to deploy each on the surface of the table, then lay several products on top of each other and fold everything with an accordion, turning the product back and forth. When you get a thin strip, bend it in half to accurately determine the center point of the craft. It is on it that you need to tie a ribbon or a thin thread that will hold the entire flower. Some craftsmen in the middle make small triangular cuts of paper so that the bundle squeezes the napkin as little as possible.

The edges are cropped in a semicircle, as can be clearly seen in the frame under No. 3 in the photo above. When everything is cooked, carefully, so as not to tear thin sheets, straighten all layers of paper to the middle. You should get a magnificent flower-ball. At the end of a thread or thin tape, it can be hung on a wall, chandelier, curtain rod or one above the other, like a garland. The craft will also look beautiful and lying on a table or shelf.

Using corrugated paper

The principle of making such paper flowers on the wall with your own hands is similar to the ‚Äúaccordion‚Äù method of collecting it earlier, but it is composed of the folds of a whole roll of corrugated paper. Each fold is made at a distance of 6-8 cm from the previous one. The ends are rounded off with scissors, and in the center, be sure to reduce the paper size by making two triangular cuts. After tying with kapron thread, the paper layers are gently straightened in all directions.

To center the center of the contrasting color, slightly squeeze the paper, making a small dent. The middle consists of a strip 10 cm wide, one edge of it is cut into "noodles". After twisting the prepared strip around the rod (knitting needle, wooden skewer or pencil), smear the end with PVA glue and press it to the last turn. Then attach the finished part to the center point of the flower. Craft is ready!
