Kondopoga, Ice Palace: description, photo

Kondopoga is one of the largest cities in Karelia. It is an administrative district center, takes the second place in terms of population in the republic. According to the decree of the Government of the Russian Federation, it is assigned to the category of single-industry towns with a rather problematic socio-economic situation. Nevertheless, it has a considerable number of attractions that are of interest to tourists and not only.

The most famous masterpiece of modern architecture that Kondopoga is rightly proud of is the Ice Palace. The attraction is a huge building, sparkling in the sun with billions of glare reflected in metal and glass. Like most other sports facilities in the city, the palace belongs to Kondopoga OJSC. Opening date: December 2001.





Kondopoga, Ice Palace

The Ice Palace (Kondopoga) is a famous sports complex for 1850 spectators, which was opened in 2001. The huge building, sparkling with metal and mirrored glass, contrasts sharply with the architectural ensemble of the city and is one of its most popular attractions, which Kondopoga is so rich in.

The Ice Palace has two blocks. In the first there are stands for guests around the ice rink mirror, in the second there are two modern sports halls designed for universal trainings and regional and international competitions, the participants of which are hosted by Kondopoga. The ice palace has gained immense popularity among residents and visitors of the city. In any season, in any weather, you can skate here. The local ice rink is famous for its good quality ice, ensured by the use of the latest technology. In addition to sporting events, the palace hosts concerts of pop stars and all kinds of ice shows.

On the square in front of the palace is a decorative complex, consisting of fountains, umbrellas and bells, hourly satisfying the guests' ears with a pleasant gentle melody.





Kondopoga, Ice Palace: poster

Since the opening of the complex, numerous sporting events, tournaments of various levels, ice shows and concerts of pop stars have been held here. In addition, for fans of skating, there are specially provided hours of free visits: on weekdays in the evenings, on weekends during the day. Everyone who is interested in the entertainment schedule can find it in the billboard of the Ice Palace, located on the company's website, which is a kind of window into the cultural and sports world of the city of Kondopoga.

Organization of sports events

So, one of the ads containing information about the services provided by the Sports Palace of Kondopoga OJSC is of genuine interest to lovers of sports events: according to the submitted applications of individuals and organizations, the employees of the Palace provide assistance in organizing sports events, provide professional scripting services for the event and refereeing. The party conducting the event pays rent, gives prizes and gifts for delivery to the winners. Any form of payment is acceptable.

Free swimming

The following information will interest swimming enthusiasts: everyone who wants to improve their health is invited to the swimming pool of Kondopoga OJSC. The days and hours of classes of free swimming groups are defined:

  • Monday - Friday, from 12:00 to 13:30, and then from 19:30 to 21:40;
  • on Saturday: from 10:45 to 13:00, after the break - from 18:00 to 22:00;
  • Sunday: 10:45 a.m. to 10:00 p.m.

Lesson time: 40 minutes. Visiting Fee:

  • for adults - 110 rubles .;
  • for children - 40 rubles. (up to 7 years), 70 rubles. (over 7 years old);
  • for pensioners - 70 rubles.

In the poster of the Ice Palace you can find the schedule of the athletic hall, mass skating, information on updating the price list for certain services and much more.





Concerts of stars

The poster of the Ice Palace contains information on the time and conditions of concerts of popular artists. Among the guests who are planning to perform in Kondopoga in the near future, are Animal Jazz, Animation, Zero People, and others.

On February 13, a grand jubilee concert of an unforgettable and vibrant singer, which fans call the empress of the national stage, Irina Allegrova, is expected in the Ice Palace. In her richest song repertoire there are images from which each of the audience will surely draw something for themselves.

On March 31, one of the most talented and beloved singers of Russian pop music, Ani Lorak, will present her best songs in the Carolina program at the Ice Palace. The story of a girl who has traveled a difficult path, the bewitching voice of the singer, her unique energy, the vivid show with which the performer plans to perform on the stage of the palace in Kondopoga, nothing will leave the audience indifferent.
