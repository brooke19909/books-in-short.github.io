How to play 21 on the Internet or with real rivals

The card game, which has several options and is common on almost all continents of the world, is called "Twenty One." In North America, it is better known as Black Jack and is a required member of all the largest online casinos. The standard set of cards in this case is 52 or 54. In the Soviet period, such decks could be rarely found on the market, more often the option with 36 pieces was used.

Main aspects

The second name of the American version - "Three Seven Ace". It was this variation of the combination that gave one of the opponents a victory. The ace value of each variety is 11 points, while Jack, Queen and King are calculated differently.

In Blackjack they had ten full-weighted points, and playing in 21, a point or another version with 36 cards, you had to add 2, 3 or 4, respectively. Such an adjustment in the rules compensated for the absence of fives, fours, etc. In addition, the game balance was changing, because ten-point cards were much smaller.

If you answer the question of how to play 21 in the Soviet version, then the beginning is a classic development of events: the first banker is selected by lot, which changes in turn in the direction of clockwise movement. The face value of each card in Twenty One contains the following indicators:

  • Six to ten, each with its own number of points;
  • J-2, Q-3, K-4;
  • A-11.

In a situation with Black Jack it is necessary to be guided by such figures:

  • Two to ten are considered at par;
  • Jack, Queen and King add ten points each;
  • Ace always has an eleven-point value.

In the case of the 54-card deck, both jokers give 11 points.

Additional Rules

Studying the question of how to play in 21, it is necessary to pay attention to several interesting basic aspects, which are agreed among themselves by all participants. The most famous modifications include the following amendments:

  • The game is dark. When collecting a face value of 17 points, you can take the next card without looking and expect a showdown by the banker. This will make it possible not to pay a fine to the general piggy bank during the search and will exert psychological pressure on the opponent.
  • Knock. It means obligatory showing of cards by the participants to each other upon reaching two or three times the value of the initially played amount. The specific ceiling is discussed before the start of the game.
