Negative reviews

Do not forget that there are also negative points in Royal Taj Mahal Hotel 5 * . Photos and the official website are not always reliable sources of information. Reliable information is more likely to be found in reviews that indicate such institution flaws:

  • Weak animation. In the daytime, the territory is boring.
  • Many guests smoke on the beach. The staff does not take any action in this regard.
  • The two-story rooms have a very steep staircase. In addition, it is covered with slippery tiles.
  • The restaurant staff does not speak either English or Russian. Nevertheless, everyone is friendly and trying to find a common language.
  • There are no freshly squeezed juices on the menu of restaurants and bars. Instead, drinks based on berries, mint and carrots are served.
  • The hotel area is so densely built up that there is practically no room for walking.
  • Overpriced spa services. However, the quality of the procedures is not the best.
  • During breakfast, not the best quality sausage is served.
