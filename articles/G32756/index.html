Hotel Karat Hotel 3 * (Alanya, Turkey): reviews, description and features

Alanya is a large Turkish resort located on the Mediterranean coast. The water in its water area is clean and clear. Pebble beaches with sand. The lion's share of recreational areas is marked with the international sign "Blue Flag". Due to the remoteness of the region from Turkish national airports, the cost of living in local hotels is considered one of the most affordable in the country.

Welcome





Entertainment in Alanya is for every taste. The city has large shopping centers, amusement parks, stadiums and sports complexes. At night, nightclubs and bars open their doors. Noisy discos are arranged right on the beach. In the vicinity of the village are concentrated hotels focused on the reception of families with small children. This part of the resort is quiet and peaceful.

Fans of sightseeing holidays travel agencies Alanya offer a wide selection of trips. Tourists are shown the heritage of the Greek era, the reserved islands of Mediterranean nature. They invite you to visit neighboring states. It offers visitors intercity buses, planes and ferries.

Accommodations





The hotel building does not stand out against the background of the same type of structures encircling the Mediterranean coast. It occupies the second line and is about a kilometer away from the beach. The number of floors of the building is 4. The main contingent of guests is people who prefer a quiet and modest vacation.

Judging by the reviews of Karat Hotel 3 * (Alanya, Turkey), it takes five leisurely walks to the market square from the residential building. There are also catering points, shops and shopping centers. The hotel rooms have everything you need for a comfortable stay. There are 60 guest rooms. Family apartments are available. Each room has a balcony. It offers views of the roadway, garden or sea.

In the restaurant Karat Hotel 3 * (Turkey, Alanya), traveler reviews confirm this, serving fish and meat dishes, side dishes, vegetables and pastries. The menu is replete with delicacies. The price includes vodka soft and alcoholic drinks produced in Turkey. The bar serves ice cream and cocktails.

Beach





The hotel does not have its own recreational area on the Mediterranean Sea. Residents use the publicly accessible municipal part of the coast. To her about fifteen minutes walk. Transfer to the beach is not provided. Guests have to not only get on their own, but also pay for renting sun loungers and umbrellas. There is no towel collection point.

The entrance to the water is sandy. There are no boulders and sharp stones. In high season, the beach is crowded and noisy. There are street bars and eateries. Photographers obsessively advertise their services. Points of sale for excursions and sightseeing trips open. There are agents inviting for walks in the waters of Alanya. Organization of fishing is possible.

Reception staff at the Karat 3 * hotel (Alanya, Turkey) also offer similar services. But their rates are somewhat overpriced. Homeless dogs roam the beach in the evening. They clean it irregularly, therefore it is dirty. After a storm on the shore, fragments of branches and other garbage can be seen.

The hotel has bars and snack bars, a restaurant, a swimming pool, tour desk and car rental facilities. Currency exchange works. A paid safe is located at the information desk. There is a small playground for children with slides, carousels and a sandbox.

The entertainment program is carried out once a week. There is no animation command. At leisure, tourists are left to their own devices, since the mass of outlets and clubs are concentrated nearby. Hotel Karat Hotel 3 * Alanya (Alanya, Turkey) is served by a private doctor. The cost of his services is higher than the cost of treatment in a city hospital.

Laundry is provided at Karate. A parking space is provided free of charge, but you do not need to reserve it in advance. Cash and plastic cards of Visa and MasterCard systems are accepted at the cash register. Parents of young children are given high chairs in the restaurant.
