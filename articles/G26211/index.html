Over the years, this center has welcomed everyone who wanted to relax here. Tourists came here from all over the then still existing USSR, and soon the recreation center became one of the most popular places in the Soviet Union, where you could relax and heal.

The updated recreation center, equipped with the latest innovations, acquired the fashionable name "Park Hotel" and the sonorous name Porto Mare, with which the new philosophy of all relaxation came here.

Hotel beach

The hotel complex "Porto Mare" has one of the best beaches in the entire Alushta. There is everything for a comfortable beach holiday, from sun loungers to showers. Rich hotel infrastructure

If you are planning to come to Crimea (Alushta), Porto Mare is a great place for your vacation, because there is a very rich infrastructure and there is a lot of entertainment. All this will not let bored adults, let alone children, for whom the best conditions are created in the hotel complex.

Fun for kids

In order for young guests to rest bring only positive emotions, the Porto Mare Hotel has developed a whole special children's infrastructure.





There are several clubs for children of different ages. Each of them has many suitable toys, there is even a small theater and cinema. In a special quest room, children can test their logical thinking and even call adults here.

From October to the end of April, a real kindergarten operates in the hotel, in which children play, study, develop, eat and sleep - in a word, spend time in the same way as in a regular garden. At this time, adults who come on vacation or on a business trip can safely go about their business.

Any child will be happy to spend time on a variety of attractions collected in a local amusement park. There are trampolines, racing cars, archery and slingshots, fishing and other great entertainment.

Children will be able to talk with animals in a small zoo, also located on the territory of the Porto Mare Hotel. Here they will meet swans, a donkey, a pony, a goat, a sheep, a falcon, and even an eagle. By the way, any young guest will be able to ride a pony.
