Dolfin Lizamary - a fabulous vacation in Bali

Dolfin Lizamary Rethymno is an excellent three-star hotel, located 800 meters from the tourist center in the Greek village of Bali. The city of Rethymino is separated by a distance of 30 kilometers. In addition, one of the island's most visited archaeological sites , the historic city of Lindos, dating from the 5th century BC, is located 35 kilometers away. It can be reached by public transport, a stop of which is 800 meters away.





Dolfin Lizamary itself includes five buildings, which were built in the traditional Greek style in 1998. The last reconstruction of the buildings was carried out in 2010. On the territory there is free parking, shops. Guests are offered one of the 83 air-conditioned rooms. Each is equipped with a TV and telephone, has a bathroom, which is equipped with a shower, hairdryer, free Internet access. The rooms have carpet floor and tiled floors in the bathroom. Each has access to a balcony or terrace.



To your guests Dolfin Lizamary provides food All-inclusive, which is served in the hotel restaurant as a buffet. Greek cuisine prevails. Upon reservation, breakfast can be provided directly to your room. In addition to the main restaurant, the hotel has a pool bar and a snack bar. If this is not enough for someone, then there are many other restaurants near the hotel where you can try a variety of dishes and choose a glass of aromatic wine from the wine list. In front of the hotel there is an excellent tavern, which is popular not only with local residents, but also with vacationers in the area.

As for the beach, then Dolfin Lizamary does not have one. Vacationers can use the city's sand and pebble beach, which is 100 meters from the hotel. On the beach there are all kinds of water activities (for a fee). Beach towels are not provided here, and umbrellas and sun loungers are also available for a fee. Those who want to stay on site can use the fresh water pool.



For those who came not only to relax, but also to work, a conference room is provided . Also Dolfin Lizamary provides its vacationers with a number of services, including the Internet in all public places. The reception has a safe for storing valuables during your stay. There is a special room for storing bulk luggage. For those who do not want to watch movies alone, there is a special room located in the lobby of the hotel, where everyone can watch a specific movie by reservation. In addition, the hotel service includes concierge, laundry (fee), currency exchange.

In order to make your stay in Dolfin Lizamary 3 as comfortable as possible, many entertainment programs are held here. For sports there is a gym (for a fee). For those who want to see the sights and interesting places in the surrounding area, various excursion programs are organized. The hotel is suitable for any vacation, including family. For children there is a pool, a cot is provided in the room. For an additional fee, babysitting services are available.
