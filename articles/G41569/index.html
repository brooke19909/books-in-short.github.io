Self-installation and tuning of the Tricolor antenna

Recently, more and more people install and configure Tricolor. In this regard, many different organizations have appeared that offer to complete this task quickly and efficiently. But if desired, or in order to save, many themselves install this antenna. We will try to consider in more detail how to do this and the various nuances that may be interesting.

Assembly and tricolor installation . When assembling the antenna, you must follow all the instructions that are included with this device. It is rather not complicated and affordable. You will need to take the keys at 10 and 13. Then. After assembly, be sure to fasten the bracket to the wall, while making sure that there are no obstacles to the waves in the form of tall buildings or trees. This is necessary for better signal reception. If obstacles are found, it is best to consult with a specialist, he will help to do everything more reliably and efficiently.

In order to fix the bracket to the wall of brick, monolith or concrete, use a dowel made of plastic. And for the fastening itself, special anchor bolts. After you make sure that the bracket is firmly fixed, you can mount the antenna. Then, connect the converter and receiver with a cable. Remember that when connecting the cable, the receiver must be de-energized. Do not forget about your safety and precautions!

How to configure the Tricolor antenna?

In order to make settings. You need to go to Menu- Settings- Automatic Search. Where you will see two scales located below. When rotating the antenna, it is necessary to make sure that the strongest signal appears. Rotate the antenna carefully, 5 mm each, while gently fixing it in each position for 2 seconds. This is necessary so that the receiver has enough time to receive and process the incoming signal.

After you find the highest quality signal, you will need to fix the antenna. Then, in the "Automatic tuning" menu, select "Search", after which, all channels will be tuned automatically.

Of course, when setting up and installing the antenna for the first time, it may take a little longer than the wizard needs. Therefore, it is best not to save money, but to save your time, energy and nerves and invite a professional to install. Within an hour he will perform all the necessary actions and give a guarantee for his services. In addition, for all issues that arise, it is best not to engage in amateur activity, but to contact specialists in order to avoid exacerbating the problem and malfunctioning of this antenna.
