Caves Beach Resort 5 * (Hurghada): tourist reviews, description and photo

If you plan to spend an excellent vacation in Egypt and are looking for an original comfortable hotel, then as a suitable option we suggest considering the five-star Caves Beach Resort (Hurghada) 5 *.



Location, description, photo

Caves Beach Resort 5 *, tours to which today a large number of operators offer, is a new hotel with a unique design. So, it was built in the form of a mountain cave. It is located 10 kilometers from Hurghada and 8 kilometers from El Gouna. The nearest airport is 15 kilometers away.

The housing stock of the hotel consists of 360 stylishly decorated comfortable rooms of the standard, deluxe categories with sea views, a suite and a royal suite. All apartments are located in a complex of four-story residential buildings.

Another feature of the hotel is the fact that only guests over 16 years old can relax here.

Caves Beach Resort has a large territory, has its own beach. Food is organized on an all-inclusive basis.



Caves Beach Resort 5 *: price for accommodation

According to our compatriots vacationing at Caves Beach Resort, the cost of accommodation here is consistent with the level of service offered. As for the price, an average weekly stay in a standard double room will cost you in the amount of 60 thousand rubles.
