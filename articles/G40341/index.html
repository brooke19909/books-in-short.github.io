How to withdraw money from Beeline: tips and tricks

Quite often, citizens think about how to withdraw money from Beeline. This question can be answered in different ways. The main thing is that the meaning does not change - you need to transfer funds from the SIM card account either to bank plastic or to an electronic wallet, or simply cash them out. Approach the solution of the problem is allowed by various methods. Next, we will talk about all the possible options.



Methods for solving the problem

What are the scenarios in practice? There are a lot of them. The bulk of the techniques involves the payment of a commission for operations. But more on that later.

Thinking about how to withdraw money from Beeline, we can distinguish the following methods:

  • through ATMs;
  • using bank plastic;
  • through the official BeeLine page;
  • through translation systems like Contact;
  • using the "Russian Post";
  • through electronic wallets.

Everyone chooses how to act. Transfers through the Contact payment systems and contacting the Russian Post for help are not common. They require a lot of time and effort. Therefore, we will not talk about them.

ATM

How to withdraw money from Beeline? You can try to realize the idea through ATMs. The user can find the nearest suitable ATM machines on the Beeline operator‚Äôs website.



Transactions include the following conditions:

  • commission - 5.95%;
  • the minimum transfer amount is 100 rubles;
  • the maximum limit for a single operation is 5000 rubles;
  • the limit of transfers per month is 40,000 rubles.

It is important to remember that after the transaction, the client must have at least 50 rubles left on the mobile phone account. Otherwise, the operation will not go through the processing stage.

To withdraw money from a Beeline account using an ATM, you must:

  1. Generate a message of the type: name of the payment system_number plastic_number transfer_amount. For example, Mastercard XXXXXXX 100, where XXXXXXX is the 16-digit card number.
  2. Send SMS to number 7878.
  3. Confirm the operation. Instructions for this will come in a reply message.
  4. Get a PIN to withdraw funds.
  5. Find a suitable ATM.
  6. Indicate mobile phone number.
  7. Enter PIN.
  8. Get cash in cash.

This technique sometimes causes a lot of trouble. They are connected with the fact that it is not always possible to find a suitable ATM near the subscriber. In conventional ATMs, the studied procedure is not performed.

On the cards

How to withdraw money from Beeline to a Sberbank card and more? This is the second way to cash funds from a mobile phone from the BeeLine operator. It is used quite often.

Money can be transferred to cards "Visa", "MasterCard", "Maestro". The option is available to any banks. The fee is charged according to the following rules:

  • Transactions up to 1 thousand rubles - 50 rubles;
  • work with the Visa card - 5.95% and an additional 10 rubles;
  • transfers to "MasterCard" and "Maestro" - similar to the previous commission.

The minimum payment is 50 rubles, the maximum one-time per day is 14,000. More than 40 thousand rubles cannot be transferred per week. The same rule applies to monthly restrictions.



The principle of action is extremely simple. To understand how to withdraw money from Beeline, just follow the instructions:

  1. Write a message like: payment_system card_number payment_sum.
  2. Send SMS to number 7878.
  3. Wait for the transfer of funds.

That's all. There is nothing difficult or incomprehensible in the reception. Further, the funds received can be cashed by withdrawing them from an ATM using the generally accepted method.

Site and maps

It is proposed to withdraw money from Beeline to the Sberbank card on the official page of the mobile operator. A similar operation can be carried out with plastic of any Russian financial institution.

It is proposed to act according to the instructions:

  1. Open the official Beeline website.
  2. Go to the section "Payment and Payments" - "Money Transfers".
  3. Select "On the map".
  4. Indicate the details of bank plastic in specially designated fields.
  5. Prescribe the phone number from which funds will be debited.
  6. Indicate the amount of transfer.
  7. Confirm the operation.

Fast, easy and convenient. But that is not all. And withdrawing money from Beeline is proposed by various methods. There is still a conclusion to the electronic wallet.

Wallets to help

We found out how to withdraw money from Beeline to Sberbank. Some citizens prefer to work with online wallets. With their help, you can pay for purchases on the Web.



To transfer money from a Beeline SIM card to any wallet on the Internet, you will need:

  1. In the SMS message write: card_type # wallet_transaction_total.
  2. Send to the short number already known to us.
  3. Receive a response message about the successful processing of the request.

This is one of several approaches. Some subscribers bring the idea to life over the Internet. To do this, you need:

  1. Open the page beeline.ru/customers/how-to-pay/oplatit-so-scheta/#/services/category-17.
  2. Choose the type of electronic wallet.
  3. Enter the details.
  4. Indicate the number from which you want to charge money.
  5. Prescribe transaction size.
  6. Click on the "Submit Request" button.

In a few minutes, the funds will be transferred to the indicated wallet account. A rather large commission is charged for such transactions - 8.5% of the transfer amount + 10 rubles. At least at a time you can transfer 50 rubles, at most - 5000, per month - 30 000 rubles.
