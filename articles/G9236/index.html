Qiwi e-wallet - what is it?



Cash is becoming less and less popular. It is much more convenient and safer to store money in a bank and use a plastic card. How to create?

Go to the registration page, enter your data by filling in all required fields. Then it will be necessary to confirm the registration by mail. The wallet is ready to go.

Mobile applications

The site has a section of mobile applications. There you can download the program for your smartphone on Android, iPhone or phone on any other platform.

A plastic card

You can order a Visa Qiwi card online at the site. With it you can go to a regular store for shopping. It is very comfortable. You only need to come to the bank to pick up a plastic card. It will be made to your order.

How to replenish?

The creators of the site have prepared instructions for replenishment indicating all the ways. You can familiarize yourself with it on the page "Top up your account". This can be done both through the bank and from the terminal. You can indicate your city and see on the map the terminals of Qiwi, communication salons that cooperate with the electronic payment company, offices and shops.

Help

Also on the site you will find a list of digital combinations that you can dial on your phone for help or a description of the services. This is convenient - you can save a combination on your phone and use it at any time, even where there is no mobile Internet.
