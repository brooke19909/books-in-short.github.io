Rovaniemi city: attractions and photos

Rovaniemi is the municipality of Finland. This city is the largest in Europe in terms of occupied territory. The population is about 60 thousand people.

The sights of Rovaniemi (Finland) are amazing and beautiful. Here you can see with your own eyes the midnight sun and the real northern lights, visit Santa's residence and visit the world's largest wooden restaurant, built from logs.

Snow Lapland presents to its guests the most wonderful places that can only be seen in winter.

General information

The sights of Rovaniemi are amazing and unique. This is such a unique place that any tourist and traveler can spend time according to his interests. Here you can engage in active types of recreation, get some valuable knowledge about the culture of the area and plunge into the magical world of a fairy tale.

Particularly interesting are trips to Rovaniemi on New Year's holidays. This small, cozy town can provide interesting entertainment: winter sports, visiting the residence of Santa Claus and the beautiful Santa Claus theme park.

It should be noted that it is best to see and feel the sights of Rovaniemi (Finland) in winter. Photos in the article invite to admire this corner.

Zoo Ranua

Not far from Rovaniemi, in the beautiful city of Ranua, there is a zoo. This attraction of Rovaniemi is a wildlife reserve that is surrounded by a beautiful coniferous forest. About 60 species of animals and about 30 species of birds live here.

Rest in these places is not limited to acquaintance with wild animals living here. There is an opportunity to enjoy a more active holiday: snowmobiling, visiting the Lapland plague, riding sleds, etc. In the summer you can go karting and horseback riding in the equestrian center.

Sirmacco Farm Reserve

Here deer live in a natural environment. Thanks to a visit to this amazing place, you can find out a lot of interesting things about the indigenous people of the fabulous Lapland - the Sami.

The farm is home to a huge herd of deer, numbering more than 230,000 heads of these smart and beautiful animals. At the end of the holiday, guests are invited to undergo a symbolic ritual that dedicates them to the Laplanders.

Husky park

And this unique place belongs to the sights of Rovaniemi (Finland). In winter, you can ride a sleigh. In this park, 90 husky dogs found a home. They attract with their friendliness and charm. These are real friends.

Each guest of this park is invited to ride a team of dogs. For lovers of extreme travel, here is the opportunity to make a daily husky safari, thanks to which you can see amazing local beauties.

Lordi Main Square and Yatkian Kunttil Bridge

This is a popular vacation spot for citizens and tourists. Previously, this square was called "Sampo", but now it was renamed in honor of the participants who won the Eurovision Song Contest - the local rock band "Lordi".

The Yatkan Kunttilla Bridge, built in Rovaniemi, crosses the Kemiyoki River. Through this suspension bridge (its height is 47 meters) there is a highway in the direction of Kayani-Rovaniemi.

Amethyst mine

This landmark of Rovaniemi, which is the pride of Finland, is located 130 kilometers from the city. This mine is the only one in Europe producing these precious gems. This place is open for visiting.

Anyone can walk through the labyrinths of the mine and, finding amethyst, leave a memory of this place.
