Description of the hotel Vile Oliva

In the cozy town of Petrovac, located in Montenegro, in a quiet area is the hotel complex Vile Oliva. It consists of eleven modern villas, built among a park of olive trees. Rooms

The two and three-story buildings of Vile Oliva have 123 standard rooms, 45 rooms with a kitchen and 23 two-level apartments. Linen is changed and cleaned every day. The rooms have a washbasin, bidet, shower, hairdryer, bathtub, toilet. All rooms are equipped with satellite TV, telephone, TV, kettle, refrigerator, safe, air conditioning. Some of them have a kitchen table, hob, dishes. The apartment has a balcony with a view of the park or the sea. Standard rooms designed for 2 people are equipped with a French bed or two separate beds. Standart + apartments have a folding armchair. Three vacationers can live in them. The rooms with a kitchenette for 4 people consist of a bedroom and a living room. They include a pull-out sofa and a French bed. The duplex apartment also consists of a living area and a bedroom. Four people can rest in them. On the first floor there is a folding sofa, on the second - a French bed.

Nutrition



At Vile Oliva, meals are organized according to the all-inclusive system, which includes daily lunch, dinner, breakfast, afternoon tea and free drinks. Gourmet dishes and superb views can be enjoyed in the refined restaurant with terrace. In the shade of olive trees there is a cozy bar with soft drinks and light snacks. In addition, the pool has a buffet counter.

Beach

Near Vile Oliva 4, 100 meters away is a sand and pebble beach. The entrance to the sea is quite convenient. There are umbrellas and sunbeds. The famous Lucice Beach is a 10-minute walk away.

Additionally

The complex has two swimming pools: an adult (150 sq. M.) And a children's (9 sq. M.), Near which there is a sun terrace. In the evening, animation works. The guests of the complex can rent cars, parking, internet, safe. A mini market is located on site. In addition, the hotel has a laundry, sauna, wellness center and spa, massage room, children's playground, Turkish bath. Upon request, non-smoking apartments are available. Packed lunches can be delivered to your room.
