Hotel "Argo" (Abkhazia): description and reviews

The city of Gudauta is famous for its clean beaches, unsurpassed nature and warm sea. Many seek to spend a vacation in this amazing place. Going on vacation to a foreign country, it is necessary to study all the features of staying in it in advance. One of the important tasks is to find good housing. In Gudauta, the Argo Hotel (Abkhazia) will be an excellent option. Photos and description of the hotel will be given below.

Guest house "Argo"



Hotel "Argo" is located in the wonderful resort town of Gudauta on the Black Sea coast. The low-rise building, consisting of three floors, is located on Cape Bambor from Russia. Getting to the hotel is very easy. Initially, you need to get to the memorable city of New Athos, the road from the Rakushka stop to the hotel takes 20-25 minutes. The city center of Gudauta by public transport for about 6-10 minutes.

The hotel began hosting guests in 2010. The territory contains 2 buildings, a playground for children, a parking space with a security system, on the ground floor there is a cozy cafe with a summer terrace, and on the second floor there is a spacious terrace.

For those who need to always be in touch, there is free access to Wi-Fi.

Near the hotel has its own equipped beach. The coastal zone is covered with medium and small pebbles. There are comfortable sunbeds and places with sunshades. For those who need to change clothes, there are special cabins on the beach.

"Argo" (2 * hotel, Abkhazia) is located near the city, but at the same time in a very quiet corner. For those who prefer a relaxing holiday, this place will allow you to enjoy the beauties of the Black Sea.

The private hotel is well equipped for corporate races of sports groups in Abkhazia. The territory of the hotel is fenced, there is a garden and a cafe, everything is guarded, on the first and second floors there are large conference rooms.

The sea, the sun, a huge number of attractions - all this is Abkhazia, Gudauta. Hotel "Argo" is located near a high massive rock, on which is located the early medieval temple of Ailag-Abyku. Residents of the temple tried to preserve the beauty of this place as much as possible, in some parts of the shrine the remains of ancient frescoes are noticeable.

For guests of the city and the hotel there is a traditional Christian church nearby, which can be visited every day.

Number of rooms

The hotel rooms are almost all one-room, double. If necessary, you can install an extra bed. There are 47 rooms in the first building of the complex, and 11 in the second.

Each room is equipped with everything necessary for a comfortable stay: TV, fridge, working air conditioning. There is a private bathroom, quality furniture, a wardrobe for storing personal belongings and clothes. For each guest in the rooms there is a set of necessary towels.

While at the hotel, you can contemplate the picturesque landscapes. Residents of the second and third floors can enjoy views of the sea and mountains on the spacious terrace. The first floors of the complex look very impressive due to the fact that they are built of stone.

Check-out and check-out time is 12:00.

Room cleaning takes place every other day, bed linen can be changed if necessary at any time.

Hotel Service



For a comfortable stay, the Argo Hotel offers its guests:

  • three meals a day in the cafeteria;
  • affordable internet;
  • laundry service;
  • comfortable beach area;
  • safe playground
  • tour desk;
  • paid parking zone with a modern security system;
  • continuous supply of water.

Meals are not included in the price and are payable locally.

Nutrition

The complex offers its guests to choose their own diet for the day, you will not be served boring monotonous dinners. On the menu you can arrange a three-time complex (breakfast, lunch, dinner). The cost of food is about 600-800 rubles per person per day.

In order to cool off with drinks or eat delicious ice cream in the summer, the beach is equipped with a small cafe.

For tourists in the cold season from October to May, the administrators of the complex can provide for use their own kitchen for preparing personal meals.

Vacation with children

The hotel is magnificent in that there are special conditions for living with children of any age. Children under 12 years old receive discounts on extra beds in rooms. Children under 5 years stay free of charge.

Working hours

Private pension "Argo" takes tourists all year round. The rooms are equipped with a special heating system, if necessary, additional equipment is offered. In the winter, a cafe with a summer terrace does not function, so guests are allowed to use the kitchen for self-catering.

Reviews about the Argo Hotel (Abkhazia)





The hotel, although not very large, but the territory on which it is located is spacious and all planted with trees. In summer it is just paradise. In the shade you can hide from the unbearable heat.

You can safely go here with children. For them there is a playground with a swing and a sandbox. Adults can enjoy relaxing in the gazebos.

The pebble beach is 20 meters from the fence. The hotel offers sunbeds for free use. Changing cabins are also available, although a bit non-standard. The sea is just gorgeous. The first three meters the bottom is pebble, then sand. The water is clear.

Prices here are not very high, especially when compared with other similar hotels in Abkhazia. The rooms are spacious, but the only drawback is the poor soundproofing. With neighbors, you can safely communicate through the wall. The room has a TV, a small refrigerator. There is always hot water. But you need to clean the rooms yourself or ask the staff.

The food at the hotel is gorgeous. All dishes are prepared very high quality and with a soul. The main thing is that all these goodies are served on the table with lightning speed. The waitresses are incredibly nimble. Food prices are reasonable.

Among the shortcomings can be noted the lack of drinking water throughout, a small refrigerator in the room, problems with cleaning.

But in this place you can definitely have a good rest. It is quiet, calm and the sea is close.
