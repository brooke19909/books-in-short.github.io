Paphos hotels: overview, description, selection, reviews

The historic city of Paphos is ideal for lovers of the sun and nature, and the Coral Bay is considered one of the most beautiful in Cyprus. Clear water is ideal for swimming, diving and other water sports. The best hotels and restaurants serving local Cypriot delicacies are also located here.

Paphos hotels differ in their stardom and in providing comfort. Most of the coast is occupied by five and four-star hotels. Usually they have their own beach, large territory, pools, spas and restaurants.

In order not to get confused in a wide variety of local hotels, we offer a small list of Paphos hotels, a review and reviews of the best of them.

Holidays in Paphos for a small price

In Paphos (Cyprus), 4-star hotels are often no worse than five-star hotels , despite the fact that rest in them is much cheaper. These are also high-class hotels where they offer spa services, several restaurants and bars, and conference rooms. The right choice of the hotel is the key to a successful vacation, so the future vacation destination must be carefully studied, based on personal criteria, and reviews of tourists.

Akteon Holiday Village 4 - A Great Choice

This hotel is located on a cliff, right next to the promenade of Chloraka, from where a magnificent view of the sea opens. Located in a suburb of Paphos, the hotel is a short drive from the harbor and Coral Bay. Guests are offered two large pools, tennis courts, a gym, a jacuzzi and a sauna. An entertainment program for the whole family is held daily. The hotel has a buffet restaurant.

Alexander The Great Beach Hotel 4 - A Place for Cultural Rest

Hotel "Alexander" was built near the archaeological site, in the city center and overlooking the picturesque harbor and the old castle. It offers superior half board and full board, which includes meals in one of the four restaurants to choose from. The hotel offers great entertainment. The beach is within walking distance, as are shops. In 2017, the Alexander Hotel took 9th place among the best romantic hotels in Paphos, Cyprus.

Aphrodite Sands Resort - Relax

Aphrodite is a modern hotel located in the village of Mandria. The Mediterranean Sea is just a short walk away, and the center of Paphos is 10 kilometers away. This makes the resort an ideal place for a relaxing holiday away from the busy city life. Around the hotel are beautiful gardens, architecture combines traditional design elements with modern innovations. Aphrodite offers everything you need to ensure an unforgettable vacation.

Venus Beach 4 - modern and comfortable

Venus Beach is located on the first coastline just five minutes drive from the center of Paphos and the harbor. Coral Bay Beach can be reached in 15 minutes by bus. The hotel itself looks very modern, with spacious comfortable rooms. The on-site sports and entertainment center offers a fully equipped gym, table tennis, massage, sauna and steam room. The hotel has a lovely pool and a large sun terrace. There is a mini club for children. Three restaurants serve buffet meals.

King Jason 4 - silence and comfort

Built in Greek style surrounded by greenery, the hotel has recently undergone a complete renovation. It is located in a residential area and is included in the ‚ÄúSmall Elegant Hotels of the World‚Äù top. The hotel has a swimming pool, children's pool, sauna, gym, steam room, restaurant and bar. St George Hotel Spa and Golf Beach resort 4 - luxury service

The hotel "St. George" offers excellent rooms, a variety of services and gastronomic delights. The spa is completely new and ultra-modern, built in the "Roman" style, provides guests with complete relaxation and restoration of health. The hotel is a 5-minute drive from Paphos Harbor and 20 minutes from the airport. On site is the Chapel of St. George - a historical landmark of Cyprus.

Louis Ledra Beach 4 - Elegant Interior

This hotel is in an excellent location: on the one hand there are cafes and restaurants, and on the other there is a sand and pebble beach. The center of Paphos is a half hour walk along the promenade. The rooms are furnished in an ultra-modern style: pastel walls, wooden floors and dark blue linens. On the territory there are two pools.

Theo Sunset Bay Holiday Village - tourist village

It is a wonderful hotel with great views where customer service is of the utmost importance. The hotel is located 50 meters from the beach between Paphos Harbor and Coral Bay Beach. This is a family resort, which prides itself on its level of service and friendly Cypriot hospitality, the purpose of which is to provide each guest with a great holiday.

Holidays in Paphos are more aimed at adults. There are very few children's entertainments, but there are cozy bays, beautiful beaches and vibrant nightlife.
