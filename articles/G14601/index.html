This question is often asked by tourists who want to visit the Surf base for the first time (Feodosia). You can get here:

  • By train: to Feodosia (Aivazovskaya station), then change to a bus (the stop is located 30 meters from the railway station and 50 meters from the bus station). By shuttle bus No. 34, 4b, 4a, 4 go to the stop. "Chalet".

Description

Guests, once having arrived at the Surf, will certainly choose this place for their vacation in the future. Base "Surf" (Crimea, Feodosia) was built in 1990, has 2-storey and 3-storey buildings, as well as cottages. Total capacity: 300 seats. The territory of the Priboy camp site (Feodosia) is covered with numerous green spaces, in the shade of which are cozy arbors where vacationers can take refuge from the hot sun during the day, and arrange a romantic meeting in the evening and enjoy the sunset.
