The best hotels of Los Angeles: description, photo, reviews of tourists

Los Angeles is a dream city. Here you can easily meet the most famous people in the world, who can often be seen on television and movie theaters. Luxurious parties, beautiful people, the most famous beaches in the world are in this city. Also here is a major business center in America. Depending on the purpose of the trip, you need to choose the right place to live.

Los Angeles hotels offer their visitors different conditions for spending time. There are both elite five-star apartments and inexpensive rooms. Many hotels offer discounts upon fulfillment of certain booking conditions or upon check-in on certain days. To choose the best option for themselves, travelers will be interested to know the living conditions in this metropolis.

District selection

Considering the offers that Los Angeles hotels today provide their visitors, it is necessary first of all to decide on the choice of the area. The impressions of a vacation or a business meeting spent here depend on this.



If travelers want to spend a lot of time on the beach, do not settle in the city center. The road will take too much time. This area is good for business people. This will allow you to get acquainted with the main attractions of the city in your free time, and get to the meeting quickly. The central area is called Downtown. A large number of business hotels are concentrated here.

The metro interchange allows you to get to almost anywhere in the city. Wanting to enjoy their stay and fun, travelers stay at Westside. Noisy parties, shopping, excursions are available to everyone.

If, on vacation, tourists arrived with the whole family, with children, it is recommended to stay in Universal City. The proximity of the Hollywood movie sets will allow you to quickly get here by public transport.

Popular hotels

Los Angeles offers its visitors to stay in one of the 500 hotels, 43 of which are located in the city center. The remaining complexes are located within a radius of 11 km from Downtown. If you want to stay in close proximity to the airport, you can consider the offers of 29 hotels. They are located in a radius of no more than 3.5 km from it. You can choose a hotel at the Los Angeles airport among three-star and four-star complexes.

For those who love antique apartments, you can advise staying at The Los Angeles Athletic Club. It was built in 1912. The most expensive and chic hotel in the city is Tripbz Olive Suites.

The most popular among our compatriots recognized Omni Los Angeles Hotel California. This 4-star hotel is located just 0.4 km from the center.



The Westin Bonaventure Hotel provides apartments with mountain, pool and water views. There are also many budget offers. These are small rooms in which the list of amenities is quite limited.

There are also very specific offers. Existing motels, hostels, villas and hotels in Los Angeles are ready to satisfy a variety of requests from visitors.

Cost

Choosing the best hotels in Los Angeles , city guests also evaluate their budget. Room rates depend on the season and day of the week. Also affects the quality of service, location, additional hotel services.

The cheapest vacation costs in January. This month, the average price per room is only about 4.5 thousand rubles. In June, the highest cost for apartments and rooms is set. This month will have to pay for the number of about 8 thousand rubles.



If you book accommodation in advance, you can save a lot. There are discounts up to 52%. Room in this case will need to be booked per month. There are also weekend or weekday offers (depending on the hotel).

The most popular types of accommodations for city guests are apartments. There are also various thematic offers. Deluxe rooms will be quite expensive, but will give the highest service, beautiful rooms. Cheap rooms in various guesthouses, motels, hostels will save. However, one should not expect comfort from such rooms.

Reviews of luxury hotels

To feel like part of the city, imbued with its spirit, you should consider hotels in Los Angeles 5 stars. These are rooms with superior service. These hotels offer personal concierge services, limousine rental services, and access to spa and fitness centers.

Here you can enjoy a luxurious vacation. Delicious dishes of restaurants and cafes, sightseeing tours, an entertainment program will allow you to have a wonderful time. 26 hotels in Los Angeles received 5 stars.

Four Seasons Los Angeles at Beverly Hills is recognized as one of the best, according to vacationers reviews.

Here in the immediate vicinity are the most prestigious boutiques and the famous Rodeo Drive and Melrose Avenue. Some rooms feature views over Hollywood Hills and Beverly Hills. There is a beautiful Italian cafe, as well as a bar. Every detail has been designed to make guests comfortable at this hotel. The room rate is about 53 thousand rubles.

Guests who visited this hotel remain satisfied with the service, the elegant beauty of the rooms and the comfort. And both for a business trip, and for relaxing with your family, all conditions are created here.

Airport Hotels

For city guests who want to rent a room in the airport area, there are 29 different hotels. There are many comfortable, cozy rooms. One of the best recognized hotel "Hilton" (Los Angeles). This is a four star hotel with a modern interior design.



The rooms have a TV, access to wireless network, TV and radio. A coffee maker allows you to brew a cup of an invigorating hot drink. Extras include a desk and an ironing board. This allows you to relax after a long flight, prepare for a business meeting.

In cafes and restaurants you can taste delicious local dishes, as well as order food in your room. Coffee is prepared around the clock. There is also a business center and gym. Check-in is available around the clock.

With free time, visitors can head to Dockweiler Beach State Park or drive to the historic center of Santa Monica. The quality service, clean rooms and friendly hotel staff leave a good impression on visitors.

Family holiday

The hotel located in the tourist center of Salou is recognized as one of the best for beach and family vacations. The Los Angeles Best Hotel is ready to provide its visitors with rooms from 6.5 thousand rubles.

The chic beaches of Capellans and Levante are just a 10-minute walk away. A special pool has been built for guests with small children at the hotel. Well-equipped rooms make your stay comfortable. Kids can play on a specially equipped playground while adults indulge in relaxation.



For adults, a second deep pool is also provided. Here you can enjoy a beach holiday without even leaving the hotel. If you want to relax, you can soak up the outdoor hot tub.

Best Los Angeles Hotel offers its guests a visit to their restaurant. It serves Spanish and local dishes, which are served in buffet style. For people of all ages, various entertainment programs and excursions are provided.

Inexpensive, quality rest

A lot of tourists from different countries of the world travel to the resort city of California. These are Russia, France, Great Britain, Spain. The hotel (Los Angeles offers a high-class vacation for different categories of tourists) must meet the requirements of visitors. To ensure normal communication between the administration and visitors, people who speak English, Russian, Spanish and even Arabic are working in the hotels.

However, ordering a cheap hotel, you should be prepared for a limited number of additional features. If among the reviews about upscale hotels there are only positive comments, then with the cheap segment options are possible.

It should be noted that there are many thematic hotels. They stop students, young people who want to diversify their stay with thrill. However, such rooms are not suitable for families with children. In order not to spoil the vacation, but also to be able to save a little, you should carefully read the hotel reviews. One of the best budget options will be, according to tourists reviews, American Hotel, which is located in the eastern part of the city.

Hotel American Hotel

Choosing a cheap hotel in Los Angeles , it is worth paying attention to the American Hotel. For an affordable price, visitors get the opportunity to explore the main attractions of the city, as well as relax comfortably in one of the cozy rooms.

The cost of living depends on the season. In January, the room can be rented for 6.3 thousand rubles. By September, the price increases and can reach 9.5 thousand rubles.

The hotel is located in downtown Los Angeles. Here you can visit museums, restaurants, boutiques and entertainment centers.

Wireless internet access is available on site. Each room has a flat-screen TV. The bathroom is shared. This provides some inconvenience, especially for people with small children or disabilities.

According to reviews, the rooms are clean. The general atmosphere in the hotel is cozy. Concierge services are available. The airport can be reached in an hour. According to visitors' reviews, this hotel provides the best value for money.

Negative reviews about budget holidays

It should be noted that according to the reviews of tourists, a budget vacation can turn into a disappointment. One of the popular holiday destinations is the Cecile Hotel (Los Angeles) . However, visitors leave many negative comments about this hotel.



Visitors should be aware that this hotel has a rather creepy reputation. There were murders and suicides. Some of these cases have not even been resolved. Also, this place is famous as the residence of serial killers. In 1958, Richard Ramireza, known as the Night Stalker, lived here, and in 1991, Jack Anterweger (Vienna Strangler). According to tourists' reviews , the Cecile Hotel (Los Angeles) is a place with a terrible atmosphere. The rooms have no air conditioning. The musty smell, old furniture and other unseemly moments can ruin the impression of relaxation.

However, some tourists claim that the hotel is in a favorable location. From here it is easy to get to various museums, entertainment centers. A spacious lounge, friendly staff who speak several languages, as well as many additional services make this hotel quite popular among budget tourists.

Hotel Cortez

There are hotels whose terrible reputation serves to attract a certain category of tourists. Based on the events taking place at the Cecile Hotel, the fictional Cortes Hotel (Los Angeles) was created . This hotel is built in an art deco style. It is filming the series "American Horror Story." The building in which the fictional hotel was created is a historic building. In just 7 weeks, the rooms and lobby of this hotel were built.

Within the walls of this building there are special passages, moving walls and secret rooms. There is even a mine where a serial maniac dumps the bodies of his victims. The atmosphere is saturated with fear and luxury. The cinematic image is known to the viewer. Fans of the series travel to Los Angeles to plunge into this mysterious, eerie atmosphere.

A fictional hotel, rather, contributes to the rating of the Cessille Hotel. Among certain groups of tourists, there is growing interest in such scary places. Students, youth in search of thrills are ready to stay in such hotels. However, the hotels presented are not suitable for families with children. Therefore, wishing to rent a room in a popular hotel, you should study the reviews about it and its specifics. This will avoid disappointment in the future.

For a variety of categories of tourists here provide apartments and rooms with a specific list of services. For business people, family vacations or traveling with friends, you can choose the best option. Time spent in Los Angeles will be pleasant and unforgettable in this case.
