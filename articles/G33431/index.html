Connection via short commands

There are several ways to connect the service, one of them is the simplest and does not require access to the Internet - the use of short commands. In order to connect the 2 GB MTS turbo button, you need to dial the following combination: * 168 #, and then press the call button. To connect a 100 mb button, dial * 111 * 05 * 1 #, and then also the call button. Each button size has its own short command:

  • 1 GB - * 467 #;
  • 5 GB - * 169 #;
  • 20 GB - * 469 further also #.

In order not to memorize short commands, save them in the phone book.
