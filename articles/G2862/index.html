Ischia Island: attractions, excursions

Our compatriots have long been fond of sunny and hospitable Italy. Many Russians are happy to spend their holidays in the magnificent and well-groomed resorts of this country. Today we will tell you about Ischia Island.



From the history of the island

The heyday and development of Ischia falls on the period of the Roman Empire. In these distant times, the city of Aenaria appeared. Today, at the place of its foundation, researchers and scientists find various objects of historical value. In the Middle Ages, the island of Ischia was a tidbit for the invaders, so the local population had to build observation towers around the perimeter.

Until the moment when the island became a full member of the united Italy, it was the property of the Napoleon family. According to historians, J. Garibaldi came here to improve his health.

Reasons for the popularity of the island

Why do many people want to visit here? It is no exaggeration to say that the island of Ischia is one of the most visited resorts in sunny Italy. Perhaps because it is possible to spend here not only a luxurious vacation, but also an effective treatment.

Ischia Island is famous for its many radon hot geysers. Their waters mix with the sea, which gives a unique opportunity to swim here even in winter. This resort is designed for those who prefer a relaxing family vacation with children.

Resting on the island of Ischia, you can make excursions in other cities of Italy. Most often, tourists go to beautiful Naples.

Ischia Thermal Parks

This is a place that attracts all the guests of the island. Often, tourists, once in the thermal park once, buy a subscription for a week. These unique centers are aimed at restoring the human body, relieving stress, rejuvenation without the use of medications and procedures.

In the park, anyone can visit the thermal specialized treatment centers, whose specialists will do professional massage, help you take thermal baths, and talk about mud therapy.

Thermal waters

Even the ancient Romans studied well the beneficial effects of thermal waters on human health. However, their most active development occurs in the sixteenth century. It was during this period that the physician J. Yazolino was engaged in the scientific study of thermal waters. It is important that this water is suitable for oral administration.

There are sources that knock to the surface in the sea and, of course, are accessible to everyone. These waters are saturated with phosphates, mineral salts, bicarbonate, bromine, aluminum, and iron. They are effective in the treatment of neurosis, arthritis, metabolic disorders, infertility, diseases of the respiratory system. It should be remembered about the existing contraindications - water is prohibited for people with diseases of the cardiovascular system.



Volcanoes

Everyone who comes to rest in Italy is always interested to see the volcanoes located on the island - Epomeo (788 m), Monte Vezzi (395 m), Trubotti (512 m). They are all sleeping. Their last active activity dates back to the fourteenth century. Today, only hot thermal springs and steam remind of those times.

It is only natural that Italians from ancient times learned to make medicines and cosmetics. Thermal water-based medications help treat spinal diseases, neuralgia, and some types of skin ailments.



Ischia Island Attractions

We recommend that you begin familiarizing yourself with the history of the island by examining the exposition of the Pithecus Archaeological Museum. Here are samples of objects from terracotta, bronze, exquisite painted vessels, household items of people who lived on the island many centuries ago.

Locals consider the main treasury of the island of Aragonese castle. It is located on a picturesque rocky shore. Its appearance is attributed to ancient times, however, in the Middle Ages they decided to rebuild it. Since that time, the castle has been connected to the dam island.

The dimensions of the building are impressive, its area is five hundred forty-three square meters, and its height is one hundred and fifteen meters. In ancient times, on its territory there were thirteen churches, which in the nineteenth century were destroyed by the British. Despite its complex history, today the castle is still a grandiose building, a magnificent monument of history and architecture.

The Cathedral of the Assumption of Our Lady suffered no less from the destruction. Despite this, the decorative elements of a luxurious chapel decorated with frescoes of saints have been preserved in it until our time.

The exposition of the Museum Lukino Visconti is completely devoted to the work of the great director. Numerous photographs and stage costumes are exhibited here.

Two fabulous hectares of land on which three thousand of the most diverse plants grow are the Mortella Gardens. They began to be created by the wife of the composer W. Walton. The garden is set by Monte Zaro Mountain. You can visit it from April to November.

Beach vacation

There are a lot of excellent and well-maintained beaches on the island. All of them have their own characteristics. Some of them are paid. In addition, there are many public but well-equipped beaches. Almost every of them has a cafe, restaurant or pizzeria. Vacationers can rent a sunbed, an umbrella, swimming equipment.

On the island of Ischia, the beaches differ from others in the country by the presence of thermal springs. For example, on Kartaroman hot keys are beaten at the very shore, which allows even in winter to swim in warm water.

The largest beach is Marina dei Maronti. It stretches for three kilometers, also has its sources and healing mud.

Locals consider the most popular beach of Citara Beach. It is located at Mount Epomeo.



Island cuisine

The magnificent island of Ischia, a vacation on which you will remember for many years, is difficult to imagine without original national cuisine. What is its feature? The most popular and favorite dish of many vacationers is a fragrant and tender rabbit in Iskitan style. Many locals cook it on Sundays. Rabbits for this dish on the island are grown in natural conditions.

Kitchen in such places is impossible to imagine without fish and seafood. Ischia Island is no exception. It perfectly prepares sea urchin, squid, mussels, octopuses, mackerel, tuna.

For dessert, we recommend tasting an amazing rum woman. It is traditionally added Iskitan flavored lemon liquor.

Ischia wine is known outside of Italy. It is very fragrant thanks to spices - marjoram, rosemary and dill.

Holidays in Ischia

If you arrive on the island in June, then on the twenty-ninth day you can become a participant in the solemn procession, which thanks the sea for its generous gifts.

On July 26, the inhabitants of the island solemnly celebrate the feast of St. Anne. On this day, fishermen and their wives and children gather at St. Anne's Church. Since ancient times, there is a belief that she patronizes women who are expecting a baby.

In honor of the holiday, there is a parade of boats beautifully decorated with flowers, various competitions are held, people relax on picnics.

On the twenty-sixth of August, the island celebrates St. Alexander's Day. The celebration is accompanied by a magnificent costume procession through the streets of cities. There is no doubt that a person who once visited the island of Ischia for many years will save vivid impressions of this charming place, where folk traditions and national sights are carefully protected not only by the state, but also by ordinary citizens.

It is rightly called the Green Island. Extraordinary landscapes will not leave anyone indifferent, and healing thermal waters will support health and emphasize beauty.
