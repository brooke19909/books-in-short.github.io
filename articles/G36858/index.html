The best hotels of Kislovodsk: description, guest reviews

The most famous mud and mud climatic resort in Russia Kislovodsk is located at an altitude of 800-1163 meters above sea level. For more than 200 years, the mountain climate and narzan have been helping to treat diseases of the nervous system, respiratory and digestive organs, and cardiology is considered the main focus.

In Kislovodsk you can organize a varied cultural program. Young travelers are sure to be interested in the dolphinarium, water park and circus, and for adults in the city there is an exhibition devoted to the history of Kislovodsk, the unique theater-museum "Grace", the cinema "Soyuz" and the museum of A. I. Solzhenitsyn. In addition, without exception, everyone will benefit from a walk in an amazing resort park.

Treatment and rest

The heart of the resort is the source of narzan, the first detailed description of which was given in 1773 by one of the members of the St. Petersburg Academy of Sciences. Sulphate narzan waters have a beneficial effect on digestion, regulate bowel function and increase gastric secretion. Dolomite narzan improves metabolism, and the waters of the Kumskoye deposit are used both for drinking and for taking carbonic baths.



There are really a lot of indications for treatment at the resort, so Kislovodsk hotels accept tourists all year round. The complex is annually replenished with new hotels and resorts. For serious treatment, it is best to choose institutions with good equipment and qualified medical personnel, such as the Plaza sanatorium.

If you just want to relax, breathe in the magnificent air and try the famous narzans, then opt for one of the city hotels. Read about our most popular hotel complexes in our review.

Opposite Narzan Gallery

In the heart of the resort area is the Grand Hotel (Kislovodsk). It is impossible not to notice the magnificent three-story building, on the porch of which the doorman meets guests. The interior is a little surprising in the abundance of sculptural compositions and gilding.

It is worth noting that the cost of rooms is fully consistent with the luxurious interior. Wealthy tourists can afford a suite (from 6,700 to 10,000 rubles) or a presidential suite (40,000 rubles). Buffet breakfast is included in the price of the reservation.
