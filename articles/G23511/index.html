Control

The control panel in the Samsung GE83XR is touch. It is of high quality and reliability. It is thanks to her that working with a microwave is easy and simple. It is divided into two sectors. At the top are automatic programs and defrosting, at the bottom are modes, clocks and on / off buttons.

Programs

Samsung GE83XR is a microwave oven in which you can quickly defrost any product. For each semi-finished product there is a special mode, for example, "Beef", "Chicken", "Vegetables". Before starting the defrost program, you will need to enter the product weight correctly. The time is set automatically. During the process, the oven will play a signal indicating that the contents must be turned over.

The presence of the grill allows you to give the dish a beautiful golden crust. The cooking power is changed by double-clicking the button of this mode. If necessary, you can use the option "Grill + Microwave". During this process, the dish will not only be fried, but also cooked under the influence of microwaves.

An important option is a timer. In this model, you can set it for a maximum of 99 minutes.
