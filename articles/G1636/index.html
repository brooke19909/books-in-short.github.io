Smartphones "Apple" is usually divided into generations, and first we will get acquainted with the devices of the sixth generation.

iPhone 6





The manufacturer country of this device is, as you might guess, China. Most stores offer a one-year factory warranty. The smartphone form factor is presented as a monoblock. At the beginning of sales, the Six came with the eighth version of the operating system on board. For communication, a slot for a SIM card of the NanoSIM standard is provided. To ensure good performance, the apples integrated their own A8 processor into the device. The smartphone has a display such as "Retina", its diagonal is 4.7 inches. The resolution is 750 by 1334 pixels. The screen is a touch type, equipped with a zoom function - multi-touch. The device is able to work in cellular networks of both the third and fourth generations. The camera has a resolution of 8 megapixels, a flash is attached to it. The software has an auto focus function. There is also a front camera with a resolution of 1.2 megapixels. The lithium-ion battery provides up to 14 hours of talk time and 250 hours of standby time.
