LCD "Skhodnya Park": description, reviews

Construction today is on the rise: the largest metropolitan companies offer truly unique house designs. These are full-fledged residential complexes, even entire blocks and neighborhoods with their own infrastructure and excellent conditions for the most comfortable living of various groups of the population. Skhodnya Park is a modern residential complex in the north-west of Moscow. Around it there is a lot of controversy and controversy, but we will try to thoroughly examine customer reviews, as well as give our own review of the project.





about the project

LCD "Skhodnya Park" (Khimki) - a harmonious combination of comfort of a modern metropolis and the delights of suburban life. If you want to live in a forest belt without losing access to the developed metropolitan infrastructure, take a closer look at the project. Five multi-section buildings with 9 floors built using monolithic technology, their own infrastructure and a developed transport network are what every resident of Skhodnya Park residential complex will receive. The delivery of the project takes place in several stages. Finishing work was completed in the buildings of the first stage, but the commissioning of the buildings of the second stage is scheduled for the end of 2018.

Infrastructure

The remoteness from the city center in this case is compensated by objects of our own infrastructure, thought out to the smallest detail. The project provides for the construction of a new kindergarten, a shopping and entertainment center with shops, restaurants and a bowling alley. Underground parking for 316 cars, modern playgrounds and sports grounds that meet all safety requirements.





Residents of the complex will have access to all necessary infrastructure facilities of the Skhodnyi microdistrict. In walking distance there is a secondary school, several kindergartens, even higher education institutions, a clinic and all the necessary services. Shopping enthusiasts will surely appreciate the proximity to shopping and entertainment centers such as MEGA-Khimki and METRO.

Flats

Reviews of the Skhodnya Park residential complex (Khimki) confirm that a variety of comfort class apartments ranging from 35 to 82 square meters are offered. Spacious isolated rooms, a separate bathroom, wardrobe - something that even some time ago it was impossible to even dream of. The variety of planning decisions allows each family to easily choose the best option in all respects.





All apartments in the complex are rented turnkey. It is represented by a laminate on the floor, modern wallpaper on the walls, suspended ceilings, all the necessary plumbing work, including the installation of modern European-made plumbing, high-quality double-glazed windows, interior and exterior metal doors.

LCD "Skhodnya Park" is an excellent project for true connoisseurs of comfort.
