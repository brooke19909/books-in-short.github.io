How to get from Orenburg to Samara?

How to get from Orenburg to Samara? Most likely, a similar question interests many people. These are two beautiful cities famous for their architecture, attractions and other historical values. Here, every tourist will find a place to their liking.

What is Orenburg famous for?

Orenburg is a small city, its population barely exceeds 500 thousand people. Nevertheless, there are a huge number of places worth visiting. Every tourist must visit a pedestrian bridge. From its height, the city plays with new colors.





We should also talk about Sovetskaya Street. On it, each building and lane retained their original historical appearance. There is also a huge number of monuments, boulevards and a magnificent promenade.

What is famous for Samara?

The city of Samara is three times the population of Orenburg. In this place you can see a huge number of attractions. Samara Square has incredible beauty. Every day on its territory lovers of outdoor activities gather. Students and schoolchildren can often be seen near the huge Sipa fountain; it allows them to gain new strength after a hard day. The magnificent promenade, helipad, railway station and much more - all this deserves special attention.





Two cities with their own history and their beauties. It is worth visiting each of them! The Orenburg-Samara route is one continuous line, which significantly facilitates the journey by personal transport.

Car

The distance from Orenburg to Samara is only 411 kilometers, so the fastest way will be to overcome it by personal transport. It will take about five hours to travel.





It is worth noting that between these two cities almost everywhere there is a very smooth road and there are no traffic jams, which cannot but attract drivers. It is recommended to move in the morning or at night, when the track becomes almost empty. It is necessary to pass through the following settlements: Enlightenment, Domashka, Neftegorsk (territory of the Samara region), Buzuluk, Totsky, Sorochinsk (Orenburg region).

Another important advantage is the presence of a large number of gas stations, cozy cafes and repair shops. Approximately a little over 30 liters of gasoline will be required on such a path. About 1400 rubles will be required to move from one city to another, depending on the brand of liquid fuel.

Airplane

The plane has always been considered the fastest vehicle. With it, you can overcome the distance Orenburg - Samara in just one hour. As for the main advantages, in this method of movement you can highlight the speed, convenience and a high degree of security. A ticket for such a flight can be bought at affordable prices, the lowest is about 2350 rubles. Moreover, you can book a seat on the plane via the Internet.





However, there are significant drawbacks to this method. The airport is located at a fairly large distance from the city (about 27 km from Orenburg, about 15 km from the center of Samara). That is, the road will need to spend an additional hour. The second negative point is that the flight is operated only five times a week. For this reason, there is always not enough space.

A train

The longest journey from Samara to Orenburg is by train. The traveler will have to spend about 8 hours on the road. A positive quality is that this vehicle runs often enough, it is unlikely that there will be any problems with buying a ticket. It is also one of the most convenient compared to a car or bus, especially for the elderly, pregnant women and children. In the wagons you can lie down, walk around and comfortably eat. It is worth noting that both stations are located in the city center.





The ticket price is different, it depends on the degree of comfort. The minimum price is about 700 rubles. It is also convenient that the transfer is carried out at night: at three in the morning, landing, and at 10 in the morning the person is already in another city. Every day there is a train number 131, 031 or 343.

Bus

You can also buy a bus ticket at number 545 at the ticket offices of the cities of Orenburg and Samara. A distinctive feature is that the flight passes through the centers of two cities and you can enjoy all the beauties as much as possible during the trip. The path will be long, the total time takes from 8 to 10 hours. This indicator is also influenced by the type of transport, weather conditions and the degree of congestion on the road.





All buses are extremely comfortable: with a TV, air conditioning and comfortable seats. During the move, there are several stops to relax. The cost of such a trip for one person will be about 900 rubles.

Orenburg and Samara are two beautiful cities worth visiting. If you want to move quickly enough, it is best to choose a plane. If you need to save money, you need to give preference to the train or bus. The most convenient is traveling on a personal vehicle, since you can travel anywhere in the city, make the necessary stops on your own and enjoy the trip as much as possible, this trip will be especially beneficial if you take a few friends into it and, together with them, save on expenses.
