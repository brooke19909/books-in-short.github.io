What are the most beautiful New Year's tablecloths?

It is probably no secret that the New Year is the most favorite holiday for most of our compatriots. Both children and adults love him. They begin to prepare for it in advance. Thoughtfully dresses and the menu of a festive table are thought over carefully. Gifts for loved ones are eyeing. Scripts of a festive night are written. But sometimes in the general festive bustle of the hostess completely forget about one of the main attributes of the holiday - the tablecloth. Someone wave their hand and say that this is a trifle. However, a carelessly chosen tablecloth can spoil the feeling of a holiday. Not the richest, but beautifully designed and served table will leave good memories. Not only a spectacular look, but also the mood of the guests depends on the tablecloth.

What are tablecloths



This important accessory speaks above all about the taste of the owners of the house. Tablecloths are divided into dining, tea, dining, interior, banquet, and other purposes. In addition, they can be divided into festive and everyday. For especially solemn occasions, a snow-white tablecloth is most suitable. It looks great on appliances, exquisite dishes.

New Year's tablecloth

For the New Year's Eve, this serving item is a must. Choose a maxi tablecloth that softly falls downwards this evening. If you were not able to purchase an accessory specifically for this holiday, then you can independently decorate your existing elegant embroidery or applique. The most important thing is to observe the measure in everything. Place on the table transparent vases with colored balls, spruce twigs, silver rain, sprinkle confetti and sparkles.

New Year's tablecloths can be any color. You can choose them according to the color of the interior of the room in which the celebration will take place, or focus on the shade of your dishes. If you decide to choose a tablecloth taking into account the color scheme of the room, then you can go in two ways: choose an accessory that blends in with the decor, or contrast. Lovers of classics are closer to the first option.



What holiday tablecloths are made of

It can be any material, but flax is considered classical. However, cotton is more practical. Its significant drawback can be considered only significant shrinkage during washing. The product may lose up to 10% of its original size. Christmas tablecloths made of mixed, mixed fabrics - polyester and viscose are much more practical. They have a stable and durable staining and almost do not crease.

We approach the choice of tablecloths creatively

New Year in all welcomes creative and creative approach. Therefore, for example, tablecloths sewn from several large towels with the symbols of the New Year will be very helpful. If you are not using the entire table fully, you can sew several table mats and paths. They can be both independent elements, and additions to a tablecloth.

How to create a Christmas tablecloth with your own hands

The easiest way to sew such a tablecloth for a square table. Cut the square out of the fabric from the calculation: the length of one side = the length of the table plus the length of the overhang, multiplied by two. On all sides, make a hem in two centimeters and stitch it. To make your tablecloth look more original and more elegant, you should add decorative details. It can be several rows of jacquard braid. You can use ruffles, lace, sewing. New Year's tablecloths with appliques look original. If you are doing this work for the first time, then it is better to select simpler drawings - Christmas trees, hearts, balls, bells.

The tablecloth with delicate lace inserts looks elegant and sophisticated. It resembles a vintage thing, which is very popular now. The inserts can be guipure, lace fabric or independently connected fragments. They can be located in the center of the product or along its perimeter. The inserts need to be slightly starchy, ironed thoroughly and only after that should they be swabbed and stitched to the fabric. This tablecloth is well complemented by thin lace around the edge.

Other models

A round table looks very solemn if it is covered with a long tablecloth covering the legs. For such a sample, a soft, well-draped fabric is suitable. In addition, such a tablecloth may have a variety - a smooth cover of the countertop and a lush "skirt" to the floor. It can be made from one fabric or from contrasting.

Tablecloth with embroidery

We want to warn you right away that this is a difficult job, which can be done by a skilled worker who has some experience in embroidery. You will need:



- finished linen white tablecloth or linen fabric ;

- thread floss;

- tapestry needle number 24-26;

- white lace;

- beads to decorate the corners of the product.

The size of the fabric and the number of threads for embroidery depend on the size of the table and the location of the pattern. To begin with, you should choose the shape and size of the tablecloth. Before you begin, count how many repeat patterns you need in length and width. Start embroidering from the corner if the pattern is in the form of a border.



First, we recommend embroidering a corner pattern. Then, in opposite directions, repeat the rapport of the main pattern to the middle of the side. Then go to the opposite corner and repeat the same steps. If you correctly calculated the number of rapports, then in the middle of the side the pattern will match.

When embroidery is finished, work on the edges of the tablecloth. They can be hemmed or decorated with lace to match the embroidery. At the corners of the product, beads or bead brushes can be sewn. Wash the finished tablecloth, slightly starch and iron well.
