What materials and tools are needed to make embroidery with ribbons for beginners as simple and comfortable as possible? First of all, you will need a hoop corresponding to the selected size of the diameter picture. They are wooden and plastic. What material they are made of - it does not matter, the main thing is to be comfortable and so that the fabric sandwiched between their rings does not slip.





  • The base fabric on which to study is canvas or a rare weave of linen and burlap. These fabrics have a loose structure and large cells between the threads into which the silk ribbon will easily slip.
  • Set of needles. For such a basis, tapestry needles with a large eye and with a rounded end will be convenient, which will easily spread the threads without damaging them. Ordinary sewing needles for a mouline thread will also be required to fasten ribbons or to sew decorative beads and beads.
  • A set of ribbons. Stitching with ribbons for beginners should be carried out with a thinner artificial braid with a width of 3 to 12 mm. As you master the basic techniques, you can switch to the use of natural silk and chiffon ribbons and to embroidery on a more dense weave fabric.
  • For work, scissors with sharp ends and a source of fire, for example, a lighter, are also needed to melt the slices.
