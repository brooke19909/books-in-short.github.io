The essence of trust management





Trust money management is financial management in which a private person transfers his funds to another person who is a professional manager. The activity of the latter is aimed both at the preservation of funds and at their increase. The process of cooperation is strictly limited by the time frame, and the manager‚Äôs manipulations are paid for by the agreed percentage of net profit. The financial service does not imply any responsibility on the part of the manager in the event of adverse events.

Not only legal entities, but also individuals can transfer their money to management. In the person of the manager can act as an individual or an entire company. On the territory of Russia, persons offering remote control must have special documents authorizing activities in this segment.

Mutual Funds and Remote Funds





Trust management of money can be carried out in several formats. Moreover, the profitability of each partnership always exceeds the percentage of deposits with banks. The only thing: no one ever gives guarantees. No one promises fixed dividends. Financial services have some similarities with mutual funds, which over the past 20 years have become very popular. Remote control in the foreign exchange market

Money trust is widespread in the international financial and stock markets. Funds are transferred to the trader. If previously finance was given directly to an experienced player directly, which promised certain risks in connection with a person‚Äôs honesty, today the partnership is regulated by brokers. Companies have created a product like PAMM accounts. Their modernized version is PAMM 2.0., Where there is a distribution of not only profit, but also losses. Funds are transferred to the management of the trader, but he does not have direct access to them. Using them in trade, he will not be able to withdraw to his account. These are additional guarantees that reduce the risk of capital investors. Today there are a fairly large number of opportunities for money management. This is not only trading in currency pairs, but also the manipulation of options, futures and forwards.

Individual approach





An individual approach is another point that distinguishes money trust management. The partnership agreement defines not only the general aspects of the activity, but also describes the specifics of management. For example, when the stock market falls, the manager does not have the right, in accordance with the law, to sell shares, to play lower on short transactions. He can only temporarily drop his position and wait out the crisis. Loans and lending are prohibited. The interests of each investor are taken into account, which is excluded in mutual funds with equity participation of many investors. DU - according to investors - is one of the most popular services, which with minimal time costs and in the complete absence of special knowledge allows you to increase capital exponentially.
