Best Negresco 4 Salou Hotel, Spain: Travel Photos and Reviews

In the Spanish city of Salou, there are more than fifty hotels of various categories. Most of them have three or four stars. However, when choosing where to stay in this wonderful town, you need to take into account that the starryness of Spanish hotels, as well as the level of service in them, are significantly different from those we are used to in Turkey or in Egypt.





So that this feature does not come as a surprise to you, before booking a hotel you like, you should definitely be interested in the reviews of those who have already visited it. We hope that the detailed description of Best Negresco 4 * in Salou below will help you make a smart choice.

Location

This hotel is one of the inexpensive. Due to the good service, food and reasonable prices, it is in demand among tourists. During the day outside, Best Negresco 4 * looks quite democratic and restrained, but in the evening it turns on the backlight, and it turns into a fabulous palace.





The hotel is located in a tourist area by the sea. Within walking distance are supermarkets, shops, cafes, restaurants and bars. Nearby is a pine park, so there is a unique healing air and aroma. The hotel was completely renovated in 2008, after which it earned its fourth star.

Next to Best Negresco 4 * in Salou, there is a more modest hotel of the same chain, called Best Negresco 2 *. Both hotels are positioned as two parts of the same complex. Their location on the coast is very good - just 100 meters from the small beach of Playa Llarga. The center of Salou is also nearby, less than 10 minutes walk, and the nearest bus stop less than 5 minutes. There is free parking in front of the main entrance to the hotel, which will be appreciated by guests planning to rent a car.

Most tourists come to Spain by air. From Salou, the nearest airport is 15 km in Reus. To go to Barcelona much further, as much as 110 km, but there are much more flights to its airport.

Food and cuisine features

In all Spanish hotels for Russian tourists, the service in terms of food is somewhat unusual. Firstly, drinks here are not included in the standard diet, so keep in mind that you will need to pay extra for them, and the prices in the hotel restaurant are overpriced. Only if you have selected the All Inclusive food system, you will be provided with free drinks.



Secondly, the children's menu here is very different from the usual baby food for us. Among the dishes on offer you will not find milk porridges, but instead of them there will be several types of yogurt, french fries, meat in batter, pizza and other products from the fast food series. Therefore, parents with infants should take mixtures and custard cereals with them, or, alternatively, buy them at the nearest supermarkets. The quality of children's products in Italy is impeccable, and the hotel will help you with the preparation and heating of food for the child. Adult food at the Best Negresco 4 * hotel in Salou has the following features:

  • the restaurant serves a buffet with a rich selection of dishes not only Spanish but also international cuisine;
  • every day a varied selection of dishes from vegetables, meat and fish is offered;

Rooms

The design of the rooms is simple but very cozy. The design here uses warm colors that are conducive to relaxation and pacification. The rooms have:

  • bathroom with bathtub and shower;

For an additional fee it is established:

Staff at Best Negresco 4 * (Spain, Salou)

The staff of both hotels of the complex is very friendly, friendly and attentive to all guests. Receptionists speak Spanish and English. Only a few people know Russian, so you will have to solve your problems here with the help of representatives of travel agencies accredited to the hotel.

Climate features

We will describe where to look for Best Negresco 4 * and Salou and when to go here to relax. As mentioned above, this is a Spanish hotel located in the Costa Dorada region. The terrain here is mountainous, which favorably affects the climate, because the mountains, covered with pine trees and other vegetation, perfectly protect the city from cold air currents. The climate in the whole region is Mediterranean, the average air temperature in summer is 28 degrees, in winter - 17 degrees.

Water temperature in summer is 23 degrees, in winter - 8 degrees.

The rains here are short-lived and even if during the rest at the Best Negresco 4 * (Salou) hotel there will be a downpour, it will not spoil the rest and mood.
