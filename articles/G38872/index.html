The best Bali hotels - overview, features and reviews

Bali is an ideal place to forget about the grayness of ordinary everyday life and completely relax. Indeed, this is a cozy corner of the planet for heavenly enjoyment. For tourists there are many tourist complexes. The best hotels in Bali are ready to provide high-quality service and a comfortable stay. This article reviews the ideal placements in Bali. The rating of the best hotels is based on numerous reviews from tourists from around the world, as well as ratings from leading tour operators.

Melia Bali Indonesia 5 *

Royal Hotel Melia Bali Indonesia 5 * is located on the coast in Nusa Dua. The hotel is suitable for families and individuals. It offers comfortable rooms, top-level service and a varied and delicious meal. The hotel has beautiful rooms with all amenities, decorated in a modern style, with beautiful views of the amazing Garden of Eden.

The hotel has cute double rooms as well as spacious family apartments and luxurious suites. Each room has everything you need for a carefree vacation: minibars, air conditioning. Meals are served on a buffet. A variety of delicious seafood dishes, exotic fruits, refreshing drinks - all this is available in large quantities. The main advantage of this hotel are rooms that are designed for people with disabilities.

The Nusa Dua (Bali) hotels offer a luxurious beach holiday in luxurious conditions and with excellent service. In the Melia Bali Indonesia 5 * hotel, every tourist can find a restaurant to their liking. You can get acquainted with Asian cuisine in the Lotus Asian Garden restaurant, enjoy pizza (just like in Italy) at Sateria Beach, and enjoy the view of the beach, for lovers of gourmet food there is the Sorrento Mediterranean restaurant, and the best drinks are served at Sakura Sushi Bar and at the El Patio cafe.

Chic Hotel The St. Regis Bali Resort is located 2 minutes from Nusa Dua Beach. The airport is a 20-minute drive, the Collocation Nusa Dua shopping district is just 5 minutes away, and it takes just a little more than 30 minutes to get to Kuta.
