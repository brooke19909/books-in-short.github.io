Clamshell Motorola W395

This model has a clear rectangular shape, only at the edges of the case a little rounded. The upper part on which the screen is located is slightly smaller than the one where the keyboard is located. The unit can play mp3, AAC, AAC + files. Screen resolution - 160x128 px. It supports a large number of colors, has a good level of brightness and contrast. The phone is equipped with a camera with a resolution of 1.3 MPh. It is worth noting that the pictures are not very high quality. The battery type is lithium-ion.
