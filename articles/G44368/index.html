Sights of Divnomorsk and its environs

Divnomorsk is a surprisingly beautiful resort village in the Krasnodar Territory. Thousands of tourists from all over Russia and the countries of the former CIS rest here every year. Sights of Divnomorsk impress travelers with its beauty and interesting history. Founded in the 19th century, a settlement on the seashore almost immediately became famous as "Fake Gelendzhik." This is due to the fact that hundreds of years ago, stray sailors mistook Divnomorskaya Bay for Gelendzhik. The city has amazingly clean air, rich flora and fauna, which attract tourists here. In this publication you will find the most interesting information about Divnomorsk (attractions, photos, entertainment).

Natural attractions

Divnomorsk is considered an environmentally friendly village. It is located away from large routes, on the coast of the clear Black Sea. That is why, speaking about the sights of Divnomorsk, one can not help but recall the natural beauties of this region.

On the slopes of the coastal mountains is a surprisingly beautiful place - Pine Grove. Rare Pizunda pine trees grow here . Here is the largest in Europe array of these relic plants!





Divnomorsk (Krasnodar Territory) has long been famous for its beautiful landscapes. Not far from the city is the famous "Blue Abyss". Here, every tourist can swim in the cleanest waters and enjoy the incredible sea view from the high bank.

Rock Sail

Skala Parus is one of the most popular tourist destinations located near Divnomorsk. Boat trips are organized here for travelers.

A sail-shaped rock is an amazing creation of nature. It is especially beautiful here in the evening when the sun sets. At this time, every tourist will be able to observe an amazing picture of the sun's rays passing through a hole in the rock.

Pshad Falls

From Divnomorsk, excursions to Pshada waterfalls are organized. Here, tourists have a unique opportunity not only to admire the most beautiful landscapes, but also to relax in the fresh air in one of the boarding houses.

Mysterious Dolmens

In the vicinity of Divnomorsk were found the remains of ancient civilizations - dolmens. Similar stone slabs forming the houses were discovered in England, Mexico and Egypt. The age of these buildings reaches more than a thousand years! They are the keepers of the secrets of the past. Some scientists believe that there lived dwarfs that once existed on our planet. However, most researchers agree that dolmens served as burial houses of 2-3 thousand BC. e.





From Divnomorsk to the location of the mysterious stone slabs can be reached in several ways. The excursion route "Jipping" offers tourists a fascinating journey during which you can see not only the most famous sights of Divnomorsk, but also Gelendzhik. In addition, you can see stone dolmens during an equestrian walk around the city.

The city's attractions

Divnomorsk (Krasnodar Territory) is famous not only for its natural beauties, but also for architectural monuments located directly in the city.

  • Compound of the Trinity-Sergeeva Lavra is a unique temple, erected at the end of the XX century. The relics of St. Sergius of Radonezh and his image are stored here.
  • In the city is the Patriarchal and Synodal Center of the Russian Orthodox Church. Despite the fact that it was opened only a few years ago, today there are the main church authorities in the south of the country.
