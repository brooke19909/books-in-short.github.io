Syaj Hotel 2 *, Dubai: photos and reviews of tourists

Dubai is a city that is developing at an incredibly fast pace. And there is nothing strange in the fact that every year it attracts more and more travelers. Here you can relax on the beach, have a great time on excursions, go shopping, solve business problems. Over the past few years, a huge number of hotels of different categories and sizes have been opened here. One of them is the Syaj Hotel 2 * hotel.

What can this place offer for travelers? What type of vacation is the hotel most suitable for? What do tourists say about him? The answers to these questions are sought by many readers.

Hotel Syaj Hotel 2 * on the map: description of the territory and location





The modern hotel complex is located in the quite popular center of Dubai called Deira. What do hotel rooms look like?





This hotel by the standards of Dubai is quite small - there are 61 rooms on its territory. These are standard rooms, which nevertheless pleasantly amaze with freshness, modern decor and neat design. You can count on a comfortable bed, clean linen and a set of furniture is needed. The room has its own balcony, from which you can enjoy the incredible panorama of the city (especially if your room is located on one of the upper floors).

There is also a home appliance, without which comfort is hard to imagine. You can use air conditioning and a telephone, and on TV you can watch the most interesting satellite channels. For an additional fee you will be connected to the Internet, which is also important. Also, guests use the safe for free, but you will have to pay extra for drinks from the minibar.

An individual room will also please with cleanliness and, of course, modern plumbing. You can take a shower at any time of the day, as water is supplied constantly. The stocks of gels, shampoos and soaps replenish constantly. Every day, you can count on clean towels and thoroughly wet room cleaning.

Are there catering establishments on the territory?





The hotel has a cozy, beautifully decorated restaurant. By the way, the Syaj Hotel 2 * hotel offers its guests only free breakfasts - the usual all-inclusive scheme is absent here. The morning buffet will delight you with a wide selection of dishes and drinks, including fresh pastries, aromatic coffee and exotic fruits. You can also have dinner and lunch here, but already ordering dishes on the menu and paying the bill.

By the way, on the same street there are many cafes, restaurants, bars, pizzerias for every taste and at any price category, so every tourist will find a suitable place for himself.

How far is the beach?





It's no secret that many tourists visit the country in search of a beach holiday. What can the Syaj Hotel 2 * offer in this beach? Dubai is a city that is famous not only for its developed infrastructure, but also for miles of well-groomed beaches. The distance to the sea shore is about a kilometer. Immediately worth saying that the hotel does not provide a shuttle service to the beach. Nevertheless, the sea can be easily reached by bus or taxi.

The beach here is big and long. This is a public place, and quite often it is crowded. Sun loungers, umbrellas and mattresses are issued for an additional fee, but, by the way, their price is not so high. On the shore there are lots of bars and cafes where you can buy chilled drinks or a snack.

Naturally, tourists who prefer active water recreation will also not be bored. You can ride on water transport, take an exciting boat or yacht trip, go sailing, enjoy windsurfing and even join a beach volleyball team.

Does the hotel offer additional services?





Despite the relatively modest size and lack of surroundings, the Syaj Hotel 2 * can still offer some services. As already mentioned, tourists are provided with a transfer to the airport. The front desk and room service are open 24 hours a day.

Also on site there is a laundry, whose services will not cost too much. There is access to the Internet, which is important for most travelers. Here you can exchange currency at a favorable rate.

Leisure of guests: is there anything to have fun?

Hotel Syaj Hotel 2 *, as a rule, is used as an overnight stay. There are no special entertainments on the territory of the hotel itself. You can spend the evening in the local bar, enjoying original cocktails, calm music and a pleasant company of new neighbors. By the way, the roof offers breathtaking views of the city, especially at night. Here, on the terrace, next to the small pool, many guests prefer to spend their free time.





Ches else you can do for the clients of the hotel Syaj Hotel 2 *? Dubai is a city that has a lot to offer the traveler. Almost anywhere you can get an interesting tour. Also nearby is a huge shopping center, which is sure to please fans of shopping.

Syaj Hotel (Deira, Dubai) Hotel reviews

Undoubtedly, having talked with people who have already managed to visit one place or another, you can get a lot of relevant and useful information. What do tourists say about the Syaj Hotel 2 *? Reviews are mostly positive. The hotel is small, but it impresses with a pleasant atmosphere, accuracy and real coziness. The employees here are polite and helpful, always ready to prompt or help with a solution to the problem - conflicts with staff are very rare here. By the way, everyone is fluent in English, there are people who understand Russian quite well.

The rooms are also clean and tidy, the furniture is comfortable, household appliances work. Stocks of shampoos and high-quality soap are replenished daily, and cleaned well. Despite the fact that the hotel offers only breakfast, the reviews on the restaurant are also positive - in the morning you can have a good snack here, and the city can easily find an institution for every taste.

The disadvantages include the distance from the beach, but public buses run regularly, so there will be no problems with the distance. This hotel is more suitable for a relaxing holiday. Businessmen often stop here, as the business center of the capital is only a few steps away. Syaj Hotel 2 * is a good place, especially considering the quite affordable cost of living along with impeccable service.
