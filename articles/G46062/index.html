Country Club "Forest Glade" (Berezovsky): list of services, photos, reviews

The frantic rhythm of the metropolis, lack of personal time, constant fuss and everyday problems negatively affect health. The physical and psycho-emotional state is undermined. To restore vitality, stabilize the nervous system, a person tries to travel out of town more often in a circle of close and dear people.

Some strive for complete solitude - "wild" rest in tents, others prefer to spend holidays in the country, others - at sea. If you are not a fan of camping, and also do not own a country cottage, then choose a comfortable complex with all the amenities. An excellent option would be the Lesnaya Polyana camp site (Berezovsky). Here you do not have to "feed" mosquitoes.





Getting to know the hotel

The complex is famous for its convenient location in a picturesque town called Berezovsky. The Pyshma River and a luxurious forest park zone add a special flavor to the place. Country Club "Forest Glade" (Berezovsky) has a three-star category, is distinguished by high European service and excellent service.

When you get to the hotel, you get the impression that you are in a small village with developed infrastructure. The architecture of residential buildings is decorated in the national Russian style. For greater persuasiveness, berry bushes, pine trees and garden flowers are planted around the entire perimeter.

A special place is given to the safety of visitors. 24-hour video surveillance and security of the facility. For guests arriving by car, parking is provided. The camp site "Forest Glade" (Berezovsky) operates year-round. You can come here in the company of friends, loved ones.

The conditions have been created for a romantic getaway, family weekend, corporate and business meetings. Professional staff will help you relax and immerse yourself in the atmosphere of serenity. It offers incredible entertainment, fun activities, active games, delicious food and much more that a person on vacation needs.
