Hotel Rooms

The guests of the resort-hotel complex "Swissotel Resort Sochi Kamelia" 5 * are offered the following accommodation options:

  • Standart room. Despite its compact size, it gives a feeling of lightness and spaciousness. Here you will be offered to stay on a double or a pair of one and a half beds. You will have a working corner where you can do business matters, as well as a private balcony so you can enjoy the picturesque scenery.
  • Sea view room. Almost completely duplicates the previous category. The most important difference is that from the balcony you can admire the golden beach and the azure waves. Also a pleasant moment will be the presence of a coffee machine in the apartment, which will save you from having to go to a cafe for a hot aromatic drink.
  • Premium Standard. It involves double occupancy in a spacious bright room. You can choose a view from the window both on the sea and on the green park. An interesting design decision was the glass walls that separate the bathroom from the bedroom (they can be closed with a curtain if desired).




Executive apartments

Fans of comfort and luxury in the hotel "Swissotel Resort Sochi Kamelia" 5 * will be able to choose the following options for accommodation:

  • Suite is a relatively small two-level room. The ground floor has a spacious living room decorated in an elegant minimalist style. Separately allocated dining area, where you can have lunch or drink tea with your loved ones and friends. On the second level there is a bedroom with a king size bed, as well as a large bathroom.
  • Grand Suite - due to its enormous size, it is simply ideal for relaxing with a family or a large group of friends.   • Two-room suite - by its characteristics completely repeats the previous categories. The only difference is the layout, which is single-level. You also have choices regarding the view from the window (sea or garden).
  • Suites with Terrace - Located on the top floors of the hotel. They consist of two rooms (living room and bedroom), and also have a spacious bathroom. But the main feature is that to admire the scenery you will not just have a balcony, but your own huge terrace.   • Presidential Suite - similar in performance to previous apartments. Nevertheless, it is distinguished by the incredible luxury of decoration, as well as the presence of two whole bedrooms. Despite the general mass of positive comments, there are flaws in the Swissotel Resort Sochi Kamelia hotel. Guest reviews speak of some negative points. In particular:
