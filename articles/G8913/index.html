Hotel "Apricot", Krasnoyarsk. Description.Reviews

Krasnoyarsk is a large city on the banks of the Yenisei River, where guests can see structures of modern architecture next to carefully preserved old wooden houses. A large number of attractions attract many tourists. If you are traveling in Siberia, you will be interested in having an inexpensive hotel. Hotel "Apricot" in Krasnoyarsk offers city guests comfortable budget accommodation.

Description

The Apricot Hotel is aimed at people who like to travel. It can simultaneously accommodate up to three hundred people. The hotel is located in the Leninsky district of Krasnoyarsk, occupies a four-story building with modern renovation. The city center is nine kilometers away. The closest attraction is the Garden of Dreams park (6.8 km), you need to drive almost ten kilometers to the Paraskeva Pyatnitsa chapel.

If you drive your own car, free private parking is available on site. Wi-Fi is free. In bright rooms of different capacities, tourists will find everything they need, corresponding to the standard list of services. Shower and toilet are shared on each floor. The hotel has a well-equipped kitchen. Accommodation is possible both daily and for a long period. The distance to Yemelyanovo Airport is 38 kilometers from here.

Address of the hotel "Apricot" in Krasnoyarsk: Prospect named after the Newspaper Krasnoyarsky worker, d. 27, p. 39. Check-in is at 12.00, checkout time is also at 12.00.

Accommodation

To accommodate guests at the Apricot Hotel in Krasnoyarsk, rooms of several categories are offered at a cost of 350 rubles per day for one person:

  • superior with a double bed;
  • single with a double bed;
  • double room with two single beds;
  • triple with three twin beds;
  • quadruple with four twin beds;
  • six-bed with six twin beds;
  • twelve with twelve single beds.

Each room is equipped with a wardrobe, dining table and chairs. Guests are provided with bed linen. There is the necessary utensils.

Monthly payment terms

Since the hotel "Apricot" in Krasnoyarsk, students and business travelers sometimes choose to stay here for a long period, the cost of living for one month is provided here.

  • Single room with one bed - 7000 rubles per person.
  • Double room with two single beds - 4000 rubles per person.
  • Triple room with three single beds - 3300 rubles per person.
  • Quadruple room with four single beds - 3000 rubles per person.

The price includes bed linen, use of a shared kitchen, TV and internet.

Additional services

For tourists who among budget hotels in Krasnoyarsk chose budget accommodation at the Apricot Hotel, a number of additional services are provided here.

  • Guests with their own vehicles are provided with a free private parking spot located directly on the territory of the hotel.
  • Free Wi-Fi is available in public areas.
  • In the equipped kitchen, guests can use an electric kettle, a microwave, utensils and appliances.
  • In order for guests to be able to wash their belongings, the hostel has a washing machine.
  • A specially equipped room for storing the property of guests is provided.
  • Temporary registration is granted to foreign citizens for a period of days to one year.
  • Reception is open 24 hours a day.
