"Migovo" - ski resort in the Carpathians

Tourists who like outdoor activities, skiing, snowboarding and sleigh rides will be welcomed by a ski resort in Migovo. Everything will be provided for them for a comfortable pastime: trails of varying complexity (including slalom), exotic night skiing on the lighted mountainsides, excursions, delicious Carpathian cuisine and comfortable accommodation in the resort in cottages or in the private sector.

A train or train will take tourists to Chernivtsi. You can take a taxi to the ski resort "Migovo" from the railway station, or from the AC-1 by minibus or bus. Travel time will be about one and a half hours. You can come by car from any city to Chernivtsi, and from them you can already get to Migovo.

Trails in Migovo

In the Ukrainian Carpathians, on the slopes of two mountains - Stozhok and Kichera, next to the village, which gave the name of the resort, the ski resort "Migovo" is located. He is still young, but already attractive for experienced skiers with his slopes. Their total length is more than 3 km. From the Malaya Kichera mountain there are 2 runs of 1100 and 1300 m in length with a height difference of 250 m. A ski track 600 m long with a height difference of 130 m, a short ski run for beginners 150 m and the same length for snow tubing.



The advantage of the ski resort "Migovo" is that it is located between the mountains, snow falls here early and lies for a long time. Natural snow cover does not give such glaciation of the track as artificial snow. Sometimes the resort uses snow cannons and retracs. The winter season on the slopes of the mountains begins in December and lasts until April. The advantage can be called a convenient infrastructure.

Infrastructure and Services

The infrastructure of the ski resort "Migovo" has four hotel complexes, private estates, and recreation centers. The hotels have rooms of different levels of comfort. Colorful and memorable will rest in the cottage. Wooden houses for 6-8 people. There will be no problems with housing. Tourists who come to ride and relax in the ski resort "Migovo", the prices will pleasantly surprise you. For a holiday at the resort acceptable. So, a standard double room per day costs $ 15-20.

Two towing lifts with a length of 1100 and 600 m, two multi-lifts with a length of 150 m will deliver to the highest point of the route. There are several food points. These are restaurants, cafes, pizzerias. There is an ice skating platform, which in summer becomes a roller park for roller skate lovers.



There are several rental points. They have equipment not only for skiers: for hire sledges and snow scooters. A set of skis, boots and poles will cost you $ 3 per day for hire. Beginners can hire an instructor, whose services cost from $ 5 per hour.

Everything is so thoughtful by the resort's designers and compactly built that moving between hotels, catering, rental and ski lifts is very simple and fast.

Feature of the resort "Migovo"

Each ski resort has its own characteristics. There is also a highlight in Migovo - these are conversion tracked all-terrain vehicle transporters. They can deliver tourists to any place in the mountains where vehicles cannot pass. Two all-terrain vehicles carry out routes to the mountains of the Migovo resort. The ski resort reviews are only enthusiastic. It seems to be nothing special on these trips, but what is the climb along a steep slope with a traverse! Walking is safe, but, as tourists jokingly say, the adrenaline of 12 people (such a capacity of an all-terrain vehicle) is enough to refuel the all-terrain vehicle.



Newbie Schools

On the basis of the ski resort there is a ski school and a school of snowboarders from the Association of Professional Ski Instructors of Ukraine (APGI). The instructors at the school are experienced skiers and climbers. Beginners are trained by the method of APSI. Both individual and group classes are held.

What does the beginner learn and what levels of training? If the student is diligent, then after a short period of time he will also ride carving skis, and will receive freeride skills, and will hone his ability to ride freshly fallen snow, making his way along it. The safety of skiing on mountain slopes depends on the culture of skiing, that is, on skill.



Infrastructure for summer holidays

Migovo ski resort is open to tourists all year round. Infrastructure for summer holidays consists of attractions for adults and children, a cable park and a climbing wall.

At the rental point you can take climbing uniforms, roller skates, sports equipment.

There are wooden arbors on site. For tourists staying at the resort, they are provided free of charge. Barbecue and skewers can be rented. Tourists purchase firewood and meat themselves.

Volleyball, tennis and football courts, two outdoor pools, a place for swimming, a fairly large area for roller skating.

On the territory of "Migovo" a large parking lot for personal transport.

All that is needed for relaxation, the resort complex in Migovo is ready to provide tourists.
