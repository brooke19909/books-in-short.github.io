Phuket: what is an island?

Phuket is a fabulous island washed by the warm waters of the Andaman Sea and the Indian Ocean. It is the southwestern territory of Thailand. At the same time, there is a province bearing the same name. The word "phuket" is translated from Malay as "mountain": the island is mainly covered in mountains with dense green vegetation.

The island is separated from the mainland by the Pak Pra Strait. Two bridges connect the mainland of Phuket. This small island is very popular as a place for tourists from all over the world to gather, so it has its own international airport.

According to experienced travelers, striving for this island is exclusively for young people who want to get more vivid impressions and experience strong emotions. Thavorn Beach Village Resort & Spa Hotel: Location

The hotel is located in Nakalay, a secluded bay on the west coast. Its territory stretches for 500 m along the coastline. Its area is 11 hectares.

40 kilometers from the resort is Phuket International Airport. A trip to it takes 40 minutes. Hotel guests are provided with parking (free). Phuket Town is located 25 kilometers from the hotel.
