What are live photos?

Live photos are images in which the main subject remains static and the background is animated, or vice versa. In fact, any detail can move: eyes open / close, leaves flutter, water flows, hair flutters, etc. Everything else remains strictly motionless, which creates a feeling of real magic.

What is the difference between "live" photos from gifs?

We used to call the animated image consisting of several static frames as a gypsy. All GIFs, respectively, are in GIF format.

Live photos are a combination of the JPG and MOV format. Such a hybrid is only supported by iOS 9 and higher. All other gadgets, if you transfer this file, will see only a JPG picture. To show the world the original appearance of such a Live Photo, it is most often converted to GIF format.

Well, of course, the very style of live photos implies that part of the image will be static, and part will be animated, thereby creating a gap in the template and the effect of magic.
