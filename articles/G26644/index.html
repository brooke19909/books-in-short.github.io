Green World Hotel 4 *, Nha Trang: reviews

If you prefer to stay in comfortable modern hotels and plan to spend your vacation in Vietnam, then Green World Hotel 4 * (Nha Trang) may be the right option for you to stay.



Location

This four-star hotel is located almost in the center of the resort town of Nha Trang on the coast of the South China Sea. The nearest international airport is 37 kilometers away. Nha Trang port is five kilometers from the hotel, and the train station is two kilometers away. The beach is three hundred meters away.



General information, photo

The hotel consists of one 21-storey building. Green World Housing Fund includes 228 stylishly decorated apartments of the Premier Suite category, Executive Family, Senior Deluxe, Deluxe and Superior. Some rooms have no balconies. Some apartments have sea views.

Regardless of the category, all rooms are equipped with very comfortable and stylish rattan furniture, an LCD TV (there is one channel in Russian), a refrigerator, air conditioning, a bathroom, a hairdryer, a safe, a telephone, a kettle and a coffee maker. The balconies in the apartments are furnished. A minibar is available at an additional cost. All rooms have Wi-Fi access.

The hotel building is equipped with several high-speed roomy elevators. According to the guests, they do not have to wait long. On the 21st (last) floor of the hotel is a restaurant with panoramic views. Here you can taste dishes of both local Vietnamese and European cuisine.

The hotel also has parking, a garage, two conference rooms equipped with everything necessary for business events and meetings, souvenir shops, a business center, a tour desk, currency exchange, car rental, a medical room, and a laundry room. Reception is open 24 hours a day. At any time, guests can order the room service.



Green World Hotel (Nha Trang) 4 *: reviews of Russian tourists

Perhaps all experienced travelers are well aware of the importance of a careful and responsible approach to planning a trip abroad. Of particular importance is the choice of a hotel in which you spend several days, or even weeks, of your long-awaited vacation. Of course, every tourist, even if he plans to devote a lot of time to exploring the surroundings, traveling on excursions and relaxing on the beach, wants to return to a comfortable clean room, enjoy the pleasant view from the window and be able to count on good service and a friendly attitude from the staff. In search of information about hotels, travelers often spend a lot of time studying the reviews of those who have already visited this or that place. After all, the comments of former guests are ways to tell not only about the advantages of the hotel, but also about possible disadvantages. Therefore, you can make a more correct and balanced decision based on all available data. In order to save you time and energy, we suggest reading general comments from tourists from Russia and the CIS countries who recently managed to visit the Green World Hotel 4 * hotel in Nha Trang (Vietnam). The reviews of our compatriots, we immediately notice, are overwhelmingly positive, even admired. In their opinion, this hotel is worth the money spent and can become a great place to stay during your vacation in Vietnam. But we learn more about the details.



Housing stock

In their comments, our compatriots often praise the rooms of the Green World Hotel. So, according to them, the apartments here are in excellent repair, stylishly decorated, with comfortable comfortable furniture. True, guests warn that not all rooms have balconies. Therefore, if this is critical for you, then you should pay attention to this feature when booking. If possible, hotel guests recommend asking you to check in to apartments located on the upper floors. This will provide you with great views. By the way, the windows here are panoramic, to the floor, which many people really liked. An interesting feature of the tourists considered the fact that the bathroom is not separated from the rest of the room by a wall, but by a matte partition. On the inside there are blinds. A big plus for travelers was the availability of wireless Internet access in the apartments. Moreover, the connection speed here is very good. If, for any reason, Wi-Fi is weak, then report it to the reception at Green World Hotel 4 *, and hotel staff will quickly solve this problem. In addition to all of the above, some guests also note that they liked the availability of a kettle in their rooms, tea bags and coffee, stocks of which are regularly replenished, and dishes in the form of cups, glasses for wine, glasses and spoons. There is also a minibar in the apartment, but it can be used at an additional cost. On a free basis, daily maids bring a few bottles of bottled drinking water. As for the shortcomings, some guests would like to have umbrellas in their rooms.



Cleaning

The guests of the Green World maids had no particular complaints. So, according to them, cleaning is carried out daily. You can not call it especially thorough, but you will not find dirt in the apartments. Tourists also considered a pleasant moment to be the fact that often after cleaning on the beds they found pieces of paper fastened with beautiful ribbons on which interesting facts about Vietnam were printed - information about the country, traditions, people, etc. Regularly maids updated towels and bed linen , as well as replenished supplies of personal hygiene products in the bathroom and tea bags and coffee. As for tips, then, according to most tourists, they did not affect the quality of cleaning the rooms. Therefore, experienced travelers are advised from time to time to leave 1-2 dollars to the maid, but not to abuse it.

Additional service

Tourists found a pleasant addition to the fact that all guests of Green World Hotel, Nha Trang 4 * (Nha Trang, Vietnam) can count on compliments from the hotel. So, every three days a basket of fresh fruits will be brought to your room. Special birthday parties, newlyweds and couples celebrating the anniversary of marriage get special attention here. Holidaymakers of these categories will be presented with delicious cakes with wishes, fruits and champagne or wine. However, experienced travelers are advised to inform the hotel in advance that you are planning a significant date during your stay at Green World.



Check in, departure

As for arriving at the hotel, according to tourists who have already rested here, in most cases you will not have to languish waiting for the checkout time to get the keys to the room. Many guests who have just arrived are accommodated in the apartments prepared for them in just a few minutes. And you do not have to pick up luggage in the room yourself. This will be done by the hotel employee.

On the day of departure, the occupied room should be vacated before noon. If you plan to leave the hotel later, you can extend your stay in your apartment for several hours. However, it is advisable to notify the administrator at the reception in advance of such a need.

Nutrition

The cost of living in Green World Hotel, Nha Trang 4 * (Vietnam, Nha Trang) includes breakfast only. According to our compatriots, in the morning you can eat from 6 to 10 in the morning. The breakfast restaurant is located on the 21st floor. From here, according to the guests, a magnificent view of the sea and the surroundings opens. In addition, you can eat both indoors and on the outdoor terrace. A buffet breakfast is served at the restaurant. According to travelers, it is impossible to stay hungry here. So, you can taste delicious Pho soup, cheese, sausage, fried omelettes and fried eggs especially for you, boiled eggs, fresh fruits and vegetables, fresh pastries. Also, our compatriots greatly praised the offered yogurt and fruit juices.

During the day, tourists can eat in one of the cafes, in excess of those available in the vicinity of the hotel. Moreover, you can find establishments with both local cuisine (of course, most of them), and try the cuisine of other countries. In addition, travelers enjoyed visiting the local market, where they bought fresh fruits and seafood, which are prepared right in front of you.



Beach

The Green World Hotel is not located on the first line. According to the guests, it took them about 5-7 minutes to get to the shore. Many tourists were surprised by the fact that the hotel has its own plot on the beach. Sunbeds here for guests, respectively, are free. True, the sun loungers and umbrellas themselves are extremely few (no more than 20). Therefore, all vacationers living in the Green World Hotel 4 * space is not enough. However, this did not become a particular problem for anyone. After all, you can sit next to a towel or rent a lounger on any part of the beach. It costs quite inexpensively. By the way, beach towels for Green World guests are issued at the reception by card. You can exchange them for clean every day.

As for the beach itself, it is here, according to tourists, quite large and clean. True, sometimes the sea throws garbage ashore, but it is quickly removed.

Infrastructure

Since the hotel we are considering does not have its own territory, it does not offer special entertainments for its guests. However, there is a swimming pool (on the fourth floor) and a spa in its building. According to some travelers, the water in the pool seemed to them too cool. But all visitors were satisfied with the quality of service in the hotel spa. However, in their opinion, the prices for services here are very high. Therefore, experienced travelers are advised to go to one of the spa centers located near Green World, if you wish. The quality of services in them is in most cases no worse, but the cost is more acceptable.
