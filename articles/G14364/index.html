Museum of Local Lore in Krasnoyarsk: history, exhibits, opening hours, prices

One of the largest local history museums in the Russian Federation is located in the capital of the Krasnoyarsk Territory. Its scope is amazing. In Eastern Siberia and the Far East, the local history museum in Krasnoyarsk is also the oldest curator of exhibits. The idea with its discovery appeared at the beginning of 1889. The founders of the museum were representatives of the local intelligentsia - the Matveev spouse. Its history goes back over 100 years.

A bit of history

Currently, the Museum of History is located in a building that was originally built for these purposes. However, the work lasted for a long time. Construction began at the end of the 19th century. The idea was embodied by the architect L. A. Chernyshov. The museum building was erected in the Art Nouveau style, and extravagant notes added features of ancient Egyptian architecture to it.

The First World War was the reason why the construction was temporarily stopped, and in the unfinished building there were barracks and a military hospital. The Museum of Local Lore (Krasnoyarsk) was finally completed only in 1929.

From the late 80s to the beginning of 2001, large-scale reconstruction was carried out here, a new stock building was completed. Expositions

At the moment, 8 expositions operate on a permanent basis. They tell in detail about the history of the Krasnoyarsk Territory and Siberia from ancient times to the present. Among them: the history of the development of Siberia, the first civilizations and ethnic groups, the nature of this region, education and religious teachings of this region. As well as the museum of local lore in Krasnoyarsk exhibits an interesting exhibition, especially popular among the population. She talks about the Tunguska phenomenon (meteorite).

Exhibit collections

When the museum was just launched, the number of exhibits in it barely totaled 10 thousand. Currently, it stores more than 450 thousand units, of which more than 300 thousand are exhibition items, and on an ongoing basis. They are grouped in collections:

  • Archaeological - the largest, the basis of the museum.
  • Art and ethnographic collections.
  • Paleontological - the most interesting specimens in it are the skeleton of a mammoth and a stegosaurus.
  • A written collection of manuscripts, letters, documents (autograph of Rasputin, Napoleon, the heritage of the Decembrists, etc.).

Staff

There are 144 employees in the cultural institution, of which 70 have the status of scientific employees. Currently, the director is a member of the Presidium of the Union of Museums of Russia - V. M. Yaroshevskaya. All branches are located in close proximity to the main building; complex excursions are conducted.

Museum of Local Lore (Krasnoyarsk): opening hours, address, prices

The main building is located in Krasnoyarsk, on Lenin Street, No. 124. The doors of the National Education Center are open daily except Monday. By public transport, the museum can be reached by getting off at the Opera and Ballet Theater stop. Excursions are carried out both with the help of professional guides, and independently. During their time, staff members present information in 4 languages. In addition to the Russian guide, English, German and French translators are provided. Photo and video in the hall is paid.

Everyone can visit the local history museum (Krasnoyarsk) from 10.00 to 18.00. Ticket price for adults - 100 rubles. There are discounts for children, students and senior citizens.

Every year events are held there that are dedicated to memorable dates and holidays:

  • International Museum Day (May 18).
  • Thanksgiving of Siberia (October 26).
  • Opening of the museum after reconstruction (March 22) and others.
