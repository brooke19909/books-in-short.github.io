Mobile phone "Siemens M65" (Siemens M65): description, specifications

Siemens, like Nokia, produces mobile phones with a high level of protection against external influences. The line of such devices significantly increased the image of this brand. For example, the M35 and ME45 models are considered legendary, and at one time had no analogues. They were so loved by the user that the Siemens M65 phone was wildly popular from day one. In this model, all technical specifications have been retained. The case had a high level of protection against dust and dirt. The device belonged to the class of shockproof. And, most outstanding, the functionality was at the level of sophisticated gadgets.

So, let's look at the most secure Siemens M65 phone .



Model Overview

The device of the Siemens trademark with the M65 index is a monoblock. It has a fairly large size: 109x49x19 mm. In the external performance of the case, new features appeared, the manufacturer tried to give sportiness and a certain aggressiveness. Keys of the original form, an extreme combination of gray and orange, clear but sharp contours, a metal frame - a vivid confirmation of this.

Although many elements look designer, they have quite practical functions. Their purpose is to protect against moisture and shock. Siemens M65 is protected by a duralumin frame, which, when dropped, does not allow the screen or casing to break. Thanks to the gasket installed between the halves of the body, the possibility of water penetration is completely eliminated. Such an element is used in the battery compartment and under the keyboard. All connectors located on the sides are covered with plugs. However, there is a slight flaw on the part of the company - they are completely separable. As a rule, most people lose their stubs over time.

Keyboard

The keyboard of the Siemens M65 model deserves special attention. Its composition is standard. At the top of the phone (below the screen) there is a joystick. It has five positions: right, left, up, down and pressing. The digital block is standard. The buttons for accepting and resetting the call are triangular in shape, located just below the soft keys. In the manufacture of the keyboard, the manufacturer used two types of materials: plastic - for a column with the numbers "0", "8", "5", "2" and control buttons, and rubber - for external speakers. The joystick has a plastic case, but with a rubber tip, thanks to which it is easy to operate.
