Sahara Beach Hotel 4 *: photos and reviews of tourists

Planning to discover Tunisia? Looking for a hotel with a large private territory, beach and water park? At the same time you do not want to spend much on a trip? In this case, be sure to pay attention to the four-star Sahara Beach Hotel.





Sahara Beach Hotel 4 *: photo, description

Sahara Beach is located on the seashore, eight kilometers from the city of Monastir and two from the city of Sousse. Near the hotel there are taxi, bus and train stops. The nearest airport is just 4 kilometers away.

This hotel is perfect for vacationers of all categories. So, here it will be comfortable both for family tourists with children of different ages, as well as for elderly people and young people.

Sahara Beach Housing Fund is represented by 1,042 single and double rooms, as well as family rooms. All of them are equipped with the necessary furniture and appliances. There is also free Wi-Fi throughout the hotel.

Territory

Hotel Policies

Check-in of new arrivals starts after two in the afternoon. According to our compatriots, in most cases, hotel employees do not depart from this rule, as there are a lot of people staying here. However, if you arrive at the hotel in the morning and it turns out that the room intended for you has already been vacated by the previous guests and prepared by the maids, then the keys will be given to you almost immediately. Otherwise, you can leave things in the left-luggage office at the reception and go for breakfast or lunch, walk around the territory or visit the pool or the beach right away. Approaching the reception for about two hours, you can get the keys to your apartment and check in.

As for the last day at the hotel, you should leave the room before 11 a.m. If you would like to extend your stay, you should notify the administrator in advance at the reception, as it is likely that there may not be any free apartments.

In general, travelers, they said, were satisfied with the employees of Sahara Beach. So, it is noted that the staff here is attentive, friendly, helpful. True, some tourists note that the employees have a lot of work, and they, as they say, literally sew up and do not have time to do something. However, the staff is trying as quickly as possible to solve all the difficulties that arise, and there are practically no interruptions in work. As for knowledge of foreign languages, many hotel workers speak, or at least understand, Russian. So our compatriots should not worry about this. Bars and Restaurants

As for food at Sahara Beach Hotel 4 *, guest reviews indicate that it is organized at a very decent level. So, the hotel we are considering operates on an all-inclusive system. This means that at almost any time of the day or night, his guests have the opportunity to eat or treat themselves to their favorite drinks.

So, the main meals are held in the main restaurant, working in a buffet format. When you check in, a certain table will be assigned to you. As a rule, the same waiter will serve you throughout your stay. So in a couple of days he will be aware of your preferences. Many of our compatriots recommend giving a tip to the waiter. In this case, he will be very attentive to you, and by your arrival at the restaurant a table will always be served, and you will be provided with your favorite drinks.

Most non-tip tourists also did not complain about the service. But still best to thank the waiters. As for the restaurant‚Äôs overall performance, some travelers complained about long lines. Sea

Since most travelers come to Tunisia mainly for an excellent beach holiday, this item is one of the main when choosing a hotel. The Sahara Beach Hotel 4 * considered by us, according to many guests, will not disappoint tourists in this regard. So, the hotel has at its disposal a rather large sandy beach with a sufficient number of sunbeds and parasols for everyone. There is also a bar and restaurant. By the way, in the bar you can not only enjoy drinks, but also enjoy ice cream or a snack during the day.

As for the beach, according to travelers, despite the fact that at almost every step there are bins, and hotel staff regularly clean it of garbage, in the sand there are still often, for example, cigarette butts. Entertainment

According to our compatriots, no one will be bored in Sahara Beach. So, on the territory of the hotel you can play football, darts, mini-golf, tennis and table tennis, archery. For an additional fee, guests can ride a horse, rent a bicycle, and indulge in water activities (scooter, parachute, banana, etc.).

The hotel also has several excellent pools and a water park with water slides suitable for tourists of all age groups - from small to large. In addition, the team of animators works here all day and evening, which does not let vacationers get bored. In the evening, entertainment shows and performances are also held.
