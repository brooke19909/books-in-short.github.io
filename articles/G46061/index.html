Foros beaches: overview, description, features and reviews of tourists

The southernmost part of the Crimean coast has long been the most attractive holiday destination on the entire peninsula. Here you can find the excellent natural conditions and privacy that the local beaches are famous for. Foros (Crimea) is a wonderful place for healing, so people usually come here not only to sunbathe, but also to improve their health.





Geography

Foros is the most extreme settlement on the Crimean coast. Dozens of kilometers separate it from the nearest settlements: 40 from Sevastopol, 120 from Simferopol and 50 from Yalta. The relief of Foros is determined by the main ridge of the Crimean mountains, which separates the coastline from the rest of the land. The height of the coast above sea level is 32 meters. The soil in Foros is rocky, the coast is indented with rocky bays. Therefore, the beaches of Foros are mostly pebble, and the entrance to the water in places is very sharp and steep.





Climate and weather

The climate on the Crimean coast is subtropical, Mediterranean. This means that there are long and hot summers and mild, short winters. The Crimean mountains do not allow cold weather masses to enter the region, and in summer precipitation is delayed. Therefore, there are much smaller temperature differences between seasons than in most of the peninsula, only about 20 degrees. Summer begins in Foros in May and lasts until mid-October, with an average summer thermometer rising to 22 degrees. In winter, there is practically no snow cover, the average temperature is 6 degrees Celsius. In the low season, there is a lot of rainfall, mainly in the form of rain, but still winter in Foros is significantly drier than on the rest of the Crimean coast.

The number of hours of sunshine per year is about 2300, as, for example, in Nice. Foros beaches are best visited in September, when the weather is pleasant with mild warmth and minimal rainfall. The region as a whole is characterized by great aridity in summer, with precipitation at this time of the year 2-3 days a month.

In recent years, Crimea has broken all records for tourist attendance, and Foros was no exception. Several thousand vacationers come here every year, and this figure is growing steadily. The main difference of this settlement is the unique wellness conditions. The air here is saturated with beneficial phytoncides that emit cypresses, junipers, pines and cedars, and sea iodine. In total, this cocktail brings a lot of benefits to a person, not without reason even in tsarist times it was customary to be treated for tuberculosis. The local places are distinguished by special chamberness, although it can be very crowded on a city beach in high season. But the surroundings provide great opportunities for walking and sightseeing. In addition, the beaches of Foros are washed by the clear sea, which also distinguishes this village from many places in Crimea. It is not in vain that it was in Foros that the authorities of the country always liked to rest. Due to the fact that the village is quite small in size, there are not very many beaches here, and they are very different from each other.

Hotel Beaches

For those who want to relax in comfort, Foros hotels with a private beach are offered. These include Villa Reoli, guest house Chaika, Rosemary. What to do in Foros

The beach (Foros, Crimea) is the main bait for tourists. But besides sunbathing, there is something to do. The first is walking. Foros is famous for its nature, and you need to inspect it, walking along the rocky shores and coniferous groves. The best place for walking is Forsos Park. For those who crave city life, there is always the opportunity to go to Yalta or Sevastopol, where you can get the most entertainment and excursions. Curious tourists can visit the Kuznetsov Palace of the 19th century, the Museum of Julian Semenov, the Church of the Resurrection of Christ of the 19th century.
