Time difference with Cyprus. Moscow - Cyprus: time difference

Cyprus is a paradise that gave people love, because it was here that the goddess Aphrodite was born. She came out of the sea foam, illuminated by bright sunshine, under the sonorous singing of birds. Everything here seems to be filled with her presence: blue sky, fragrant plants, quiet starry nights. The cool forests beckon in their shadow, the golden beaches fill with joy and health, from the citrus orchards a delightful aroma spreads everywhere.





Everyone who goes to the island is interested in the time difference with Cyprus. Indeed, for a comfortable stay, it is important that the body adapts easily. Going on a trip, it is better to ask in advance in what time zone the resort is located.

Difference time Cyprus

Everyone knows that a day consists of 24 hours. In accordance with this, our entire planet is divided into as many time zones. The belt system also contains 24 meridians. The zero belt, from where the countdown begins, is called the Greenwich Meridian, its longitude is zero. Having studied the map of time zones, we can conclude that Cyprus has +2 hours relative to Greenwich.

So, for people traveling Moscow-Cyprus, the time difference will be only one hour. This has a very positive effect on the state of the body. Moscow tourists do not have to adapt at all. A flight in comfortable conditions takes only four hours, so on the very first day, without wasting time recovering from the road, you can plunge into this exotic world, and even the hourly difference with Cyprus does not affect your well-being.

General description of the island





Cyprus is famous for its beautiful landscapes. There are echoes of ancient Byzantine culture everywhere. Once upon a time, the island was part of Byzantium, Turkish, British and Arab conquerors dominated here in due time. The ancient castle of Colossus testifies to Byzantine rule. Left-hand traffic confirms that once the island was conquered by the British.

The vast territory of the island is occupied by mountains, they are covered with chic coniferous forests. They lost many traces of ancient monasteries and ancient cities. All tourists seek to visit these unforgettable places.

The capital of Cyprus is called Nicosia. On its territory there are many interesting historical monuments. The largest resorts of the island are the cities of Paphos, Larnaca, Ayia Napa and, of course, Protaras. All of them are located in the south of the island. The beaches of Cyprus are distinguished by a high level of ecology, have the awards of the European Union. Tourists resting here can go on an excursion to Israel, Egypt or the island of Rhodes.

Climate

Tourists are interested, of course, not only the time difference with Cyprus, but also the local natural conditions. The mild Mediterranean climate dominates here. The weather is always warm, in the summer the unobtrusive sun warms the air up to 35 degrees, but there is no sultry heat, as the sea breeze pleasantly brings coolness. In winter, the temperature does not drop below 15 degrees.





Doctors believe that Cyprus is a place with a healthy climate. Those who suffer from thyroid diseases often recover without any medication after living on this island for a year and a half. The time difference with Cyprus for residents of Central Russia is not noticeable at all, it is also an important factor for restoring health, because the body does not require additional forces to adapt.

Sea and sun

All beaches in Cyprus are municipal, most often you have to pay for beach equipment. The best beaches are in Ayia Napa. Picturesque Nissi Beach is also attractive for its snow-white sand, and a small island is nearby in the water. Popular in Cyprus Sand Bay, Golden Sands. The beautiful pebble beach of Petra tou Romio stands apart. The longest beach in Cyprus is the Ladies' Mile, it is located west of Limassol and is very popular among the local population. On Governor's beach, beach parties are often held. In Larnaca, Finikoudes and Mackenzie are popular. Often hotels in Larnaca, Paphos and Limassol provide their guests with free beach equipment, but in Ayia Napa and Protaras this is very rare.

Where to stay

The time difference with Cyprus is easily determined. Knowing that Cyprus is located +2 hours from the Greenwich meridian, being at any point, you can calculate the time of arrival to the island. It is worth noting that the island has many hotels that receive guests around the clock. Service categories are different, from two stars to five. Two-star hotels are of quite decent standard, equipped with air conditioning, although this is paid extra. Often you can meet Russian-speaking staff in hotels. Breakfasts in 2 * continental hotels, 3-5 * - a buffet is organized. Some hotels offer half board: a choice of lunch or dinner. Modern 2-4 * hotels offer an all-inclusive system. The higher the rating for Ayia Napa hotels, the greater the likelihood that the institution has its own beach.





What to see

Nicosia is considered one of the oldest cities in the world. Here you can see many architectural masterpieces that are thousands of years old. The gates of Famagusta are located in the old part of the city. Here, music evenings and exhibitions are constantly held here. Nearby, north of the gate, is the Byzantine church of Chrysaliniotissa, which houses an amazing collection of Byzantine icons. It should be noted that all historical sights are located here nearby from each other, you can get around them all on foot.

For residents of Russia, the time difference with Cyprus is completely imperceptible, so many here easily wake up early and meet the dawn. Such beauty can be seen only in these heavenly places. The rising bright red sun, the snowy peaks of the mountains and the gentle rustle of the waves, at some point it seems that the goddess Aphrodite will emerge from the sea foam. Thousands of lovers seek to arrive in Cyprus to get into this magical atmosphere and receive the blessing of the goddess of love.

Entertainment





In the resorts of Cyprus, no one is bored. On the beach during the daytime there is a lot of entertainment: paragliding, water skiing, pleasure boats, banana skiing, speed skiing, canoeing, glass bottom boat cruises. Aquariums, dolphinariums, water parks are located everywhere on the shore. In Paphos, golf courses amaze even professionals with their convenient facilities. At night, for lovers of noisy parties and discos, dozens of clubs and bars are open. Those who prefer a quiet, romantic atmosphere can also find many restaurants with a cozy atmosphere.
