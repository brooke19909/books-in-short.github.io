Samsung Plasma TVs: Features of Choice

Modern life is hard to imagine without plasma TVs. The importance of the device is comparable to cars, furniture and other products. There are many brands producing these devices.



Samsung Plasma TVs use a unique technology that is fundamentally different from its LCD and CRT counterparts. For several years on the market there are widescreen flat models made using plasma technology.

Samsung's slim plasma TVs look great and adorn any modern interior. The device is suitable even for the most sophisticated consumer. It will be appreciated by movie buffs, those who want to watch the news, and avid gamers by connecting the matrix to a computer. Today, many models are quite inexpensive, and you can choose the right option for any family. When choosing a TV, you should decide on the purpose of use. The characteristics of the device will depend on this.



First, when choosing Samsung plasma TVs, you need to consider the screen size. This is the most important parameter. Today it is difficult to find models with a small display size, since the production of such equipment is unprofitable. It is practically not realistic to purchase a model with a diagonal of less than 32 inches. In this case, it is better to pay attention to the LCD model. For home theater, you should choose a matrix size of 50 inches.

The next feature, which largely depends on the diagonal, is the Samsung screen format. 4 to 3 plasma TVs have almost sunk into oblivion and are rare. Today the format is 16 to 9. Such panels allow you to view programs in the old format. The device adapts the image, bringing it to the desired size.

When choosing a Samsung plasma TV, you need to decide on the screen resolution. The latest models support the HD option. Although the resolution will be normal from 720i to 1080p. The digital value of this characteristic indicates the number of lines on the screen, and the letter parameter indicates the type of scan (interlaced or progressive).

With other parameters being equal, preference should be given to a progressive indicator.

The main advantage of plasma over LCD TVs is the increased characteristics of brightness and contrast. Since, according to the production technology, the display pixels are independent light sources, and LCD models use a special lamp for backlighting. However, in different models, the brightness characteristics can differ significantly. The same applies to the frame refresh rate. For the plasma version, this parameter is about 400-600 hertz.

The devices are quite high quality and reliable, so Samsung plasma TVs repair may not be required for a long time. When choosing a device, you need to look at the number of ports and inputs. A modern TV should have HDMI (DVI) connectors and older: S-Video, RCA, VGA, composite and other ports. It will not be superfluous to have USB inputs.
