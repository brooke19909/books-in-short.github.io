Hotel Holiday Inn Mai Khao Beach Resort Phuket (Phuket, Thailand): description and photos

If you want to relax in a secluded place on the shores of the warm sea in Thailand and are accustomed to living in comfortable rooms with excellent service, then the four-star Holiday Inn Mai Khao Beach Resort Phuket can be a good option for you.



Location

This hotel is located in the northwest of Phuket (Thailand) next to the picturesque Mai Khao Beach. The place here is very quiet and secluded, so hotel guests will not annoy crowds of tourists. The center of Patong is 45 kilometers from here. The airport is 9 kilometers away. A number of travelers are advised to take a stroll in the direction of the air harbor along the beach and watch the planes taking off and landing. You can get back by taxi. As for the beach, it is literally a couple of hundred meters from the residential buildings.

Nutrition

When booking accommodation at the Holiday Inn Resort Phuket Mai Khao Beach in Phuket (Thailand), you can choose the right food option. According to tourists from Russia vacationing here, most of them preferred to pay only breakfasts in advance and did not regret it at all. The fact is that lunch and dinner at a hotel restaurant are very expensive. Therefore, many guests ate here only a few times during their stay. As for breakfast, almost all travelers were satisfied with them. According to them, the choice of dishes in the morning is quite wide, and everyone will find something to their taste. You can eat during the day in neighboring cafes. Near the hotel there are only two of them, however, the guests were quite enough. Moreover, judging by the reviews, they cook in them very well, and the prices are much lower than in the Holiday Inn Resort restaurant.



Entertainment

Since the Holiday Inn Phuket Mai Khao Beach Resort 4 * (Phuket) is located on the beach, its guests will be able to fully enjoy a beach holiday. Tourists were very pleased with the sea. According to them, the water here is very clean and warm, and the entry into the sea is gentle and pleasant. A huge plus is the fact that there are always very few people on the beach. Therefore, you can enjoy the sea and the sun in a calm and peaceful environment.

As for other entertainment, many enjoyed spending time at the hotel's pool. However, according to our compatriots, basically there have always been many tourists from Europe. Travelers from Russia preferred the beach.

Those who wish to warm up can play volleyball. Those who want to spend time with benefits for appearance and health can visit the local spa. Many guests note that they were very pleased with it.
