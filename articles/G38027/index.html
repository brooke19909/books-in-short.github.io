Sunrise Select Arabian Beach Resort 5: overview, description and reviews of tourists

Resort Sunrise Select Arabian Beach Resort 5 * is a chic hotel complex with a huge green area and its own sandy beach on the Red Sea.

Short hotel description

Hotel Sunrise Select Arabian Beach Resort 5 * is a modern resort complex, in the interior of which there is Arabian luxury and oriental charm. The hotel impresses not only with its design, but also with the wide range of services provided, as well as its wonderful private beach.

Sunrise Select Arabian Beach Resort 5 * can pamper its guests with exquisite culinary masterpieces. Of course, you should pay attention to local dishes, which are seasoned with exotic spices. In addition, the hotel has several more themed restaurants that specialize in international culinary.

The Sunrise Select Arabian Beach Resort 5 * hotel is an example of luxury and comfort. You can not ignore its chic rooms, which resemble palace chambers. In addition, tourists are invited to take advantage of the mass of infrastructure facilities that are responsible for entertainment and comfort.

Hotel Room Amenities

Hotel Sunrise Grand Select Arabian Beach Resort 5 * offers its guests to stay in rooms in one of 4 categories: standard, family, superior and deluxe. The following required amenities await tourists in any apartment:

  • paid wireless internet;
  • a full range of bath accessories, which, in addition to cosmetics, include toothbrushes, shower caps, and shaving accessories;
  • a bathrobe and disposable slippers are provided in the bathroom;
  • large modern TV with a connected player for playing discs;
  • a refrigerator, which also acts as a minibar;
  • the bathroom has a shower and hairdryer.




Upon prior request, you can use the iron and rent a tablet, laptop or other equipment at the reception. It is also worth noting that the cleaning of the room, according to your desire, can be carried out up to 2 times a day.

Positive reviews

Many travelers prefer the resort hotel Sunrise Select Arabian Beach Resort 5 *. Reviews about him are a combination of positive impressions and enthusiastic comments:

  • the hotel is very new, and therefore all the furniture and furniture in it are in excellent condition;
  • a lot of greenery and scenery on the territory - there is where to walk, and you can also make beautiful and original photographs;
  • hotel staff carefully monitors the cleanliness not only in the rooms, but also in the corridors, and in the hotel courtyard;
  • both the main and themed restaurants make a positive impression - everything is very beautiful, tasty and of high quality;
  • the work of the animators deserves the highest rating - the entertainment program is very rich, nevertheless, no one is imposed on those who do not want to take part in the entertainment;
  • quick check-in, which is especially important after a tiring flight;
  • almost no wind penetrates the area where the hotel is located, therefore it is very comfortable to be on the beach and by the pool;
  • reception workers, as well as maids, respond very quickly to all requests and contribute in every way to solving problems;
  • the diet has a lot of fresh herbs, as well as fruits and vegetables.

Negative reviews

Hotel Sunrise Select Arabian Beach Resort 5 * causes some complaints from guests:

  • the hotel has an insufficient number of attendants (this problem is especially acute in restaurants);
  • there are no drinks on the buffet, and waiters have to wait a very long time;
  • there is only a pontoon on the beach;
  • an inclined road leads to the sea, along which it is quite difficult to climb on the way back;
  • there is no self-service in the bars, so drinks are sometimes crowded.
