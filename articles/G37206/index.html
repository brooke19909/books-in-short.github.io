How to get to the island of Skot?

Shekota is an island in the Sea of ​​Japan, in Peter the Great Bay. It is located in the archipelago of Empress Eugenia, twenty kilometers from Vladivostok. In Primorye, this is the largest island of those that can be wade during low tides. The history and geographical features of Shkota will be discussed in today's article.

How to get to the island of Skot on foot? This can be done in the summer. But many residents of Vladivostok prefer to go hiking in the winter. Military strategic facility

Once the island was the main cover for Vladivostok. Here was the post of the Voroshilov battery. When the enemy attacked, a crushing blow was carried out. Today, only the trenches and the remains of a military base speak of the island’s military past. Silence and peace reign in these places. However, as a result of frequent visits by tourists, some areas on the island are quite contaminated.
