Briefly about the park

Just imagine: more than three hundred hectares at your disposal! There is absolutely everything that you and your children will like. Now fast forward to the past. In the nineteenth century, the park "Sunny Island" was given over to the famous Martha Golovatova, the widow of the colonel. In those days, her husband was not only an artillery officer, but also a famous Kuban writer. Time passed, slowly flowing into the new century, and in the twentieth century Rokkol Adolf Nikolaevich bought this site. Thanks to the good entrepreneurial and economic qualities of the new owner, a park soon appeared at this place, which was open to all wildlife lovers. Gradually, this area was rebuilt, reconstructed, more and more ennobled, and soon officially recognized as a place of rest.





The "Sunny Island" can rightfully be considered a national treasure. Here, between the new plantings and decorative flower beds, a long-lived - a huge oak tree - has been preserved. According to local residents, he is already more than a hundred years old. Many come only to touch him and feel all the power and greatness.
