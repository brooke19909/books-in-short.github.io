Dzerzhinsky quarry: how to get, what to see?

The Lyubertsy district of the Moscow region is known to many thanks to its picturesque careers, which are a great place for swimming. Dzerzhinsky quarry is very picturesque - steep sandy beaches, pine forest, attractive blue water, gently sloping wide beaches and comfortable swimming areas.



The length of the quarry from west to east is more than 1 km, and the width reaches 500 m. The Dzerzhinsky quarry was formed on the site of a wide foundation pit, which was gradually filled with groundwater. It is adjacent to one of the areas of the city, built up not so long ago. The clear water of a beautiful turquoise hue attracts many vacationers and residents of nearby houses to spend time on the beach and enjoy the beauty of nature. In this regard, the northern coast of the quarry, which is a steep sandy cliff covered with a pine forest, is very picturesque and attractive.





The city of Dzerzhinsky is divided into two parts - industrial and residential. For these reasons, the ecological situation of the city cannot be called very favorable, but the big plus is that there is no transit passage for cars through the city. This alleviates the situation and reduces air pollution.

Dzerzhinsky quarry is not the only attraction of the city. Dzerzhinsky often comes to see the Nikolo-Ugreshsky Monastery - one of the oldest in the Moscow region. Erected by Dmitry Donskoy after the victory in the Battle of Kulikovo, the monastery laid the foundation for the construction of the city and is still its main dominant. The city also has an interesting attraction, protected by UNESCO as a World Heritage Site - this is the Jerusalem, or Palestinian, Wall.

Dzerzhinsky quarry is located near Moscow and is a popular vacation spot for residents of the capital as well, especially in the warm season. The center of Dzerzhinsk with the capital is connected by several types of transport, including water and rail. A separate exit from the MKAD and the lack of transit traffic through the city facilitate the road to the Dzerzhinsky quarry. How to get there by rail depends on the point of departure. You can get from both Moscow and the city of Lyubertsy. Commercial and social bus routes go to the city of Dzerzhinsky from the metro Kuzminki and Lyublino, as well as from the city of Lyubertsy. By car, the Dzerzhinsky quarry can be reached via the MKAD, following the 16th km, then turn off at the Dzerzhinsky-Kapotnya junction.





By water transport, the city of Dzerzhinsky can be reached only from the beginning of May to the end of September. During this period, the ships leave on Saturday and Sunday from the pier "Kolomenskoye" and follow to the Nikolo-Ugreshsky monastery. It is better to learn about the schedule of excursions and availability of seats in advance in the sales offices of the Capital Shipping Company.

Dzerzhinsky quarry is becoming more popular among the local population and residents of Moscow. Beautiful nature, interesting sights of the city and good transport accessibility make the place lively, especially at weekends.
