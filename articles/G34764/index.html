Hotel Grand Hotel Side (Turkey Side) - overview, description and reviews of tourists

In the XIII century in the area where the tourist town of Side is now located, there was a small fishing village. Since 1895, the settlement grew due to immigrants from the island of Crete, and thus the village became officially called the city.

Now it is very popular - there are many resort complexes concentrated here, and one of the best is the Grand Hotel Side, which will be discussed.





general information

For starters, a couple of interesting facts about the city. Few people know that its name comes from the local dialect, and is translated as "pomegranate." An interesting fact is that the name of this territory was given in honor of the ancient Greek goddess of fertility (Artemis). And pomegranate is a symbol of prosperity and fertility.

Today, the city, which is located on the Mediterranean coast between Antalya and Alanya, due to the Mediterranean climate, has become one of the most popular resort towns in Turkey among young people and couples with children.

Sandy beaches equipped according to the international standard, which almost every year receive the prestigious Blue Flag award, due to clean water and safe swimming, are the main reason for the high rating of this resort area.

The resort has more than two hundred hotels, built for those who want to spend their vacation time on the shores of the Mediterranean Sea, which is the warmest in Europe.





About hotel

Among the large number of bungalows (one-story family house with a spacious veranda) that are located throughout the resort area, the Grand Hotel Side is recognized by tourists.

It is located in the resort area in the village of Cholakly far from large industrial cities (about 20 km). The hotel complex, built in 1990 at a distance of 250 m from the sea shore and 7.5 km from the city of Side, is designed for couples with children of any age. At the hotel, a child under 12 years stays free with parents if they do not occupy a separate bed.

The room capacity of Grand Hotel Side includes 340 rooms, which are located on seven floors connected by elevators.

Amenities inside

After major repairs in 2011, each room of Grand Hotel Side was equipped with new upholstered furniture, air conditioning, a telephone with international access, satellite TV, a bathroom with shower and a balcony with sea or pool views. All apartments have carpeted floors and a safe, which can be used at an additional cost.

Room cleaning is done every day, at hours convenient for guests. They are discussed at the reception with the administrator. Bed linen is changed three times a week.

restaurants and bars

This complex, like any other worthy hotel, has several establishments that serve guests.

For guests staying at the Grand Prestige Hotel Side there are two indoor restaurants and one open. In addition, the hotel has four bars: Pool Bar (pool bar), Beach Bar (on the beach), Bar Snack (with light snacks) and Lobby Bar (for meeting friends).

Meals are organized on an all-inclusive basis: a buffet is served for breakfast, lunch and dinner.

The staff of the bars and restaurants kindly offer a menu of national Turkish cuisine, which consists of different types of kebab (fried meat), Turkish soups and seafood salads. And for dessert, they will offer Turkish coffee, syrup-filled rice dough (baklava), and many other popular national dishes.
