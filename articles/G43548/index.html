The Supernatural Story of the Warren Family

In fact, for the first time, Edward was faced with unusual phenomena in childhood. When he was five years old, his family moved to a real haunted house. In the future, an adult Ed more than once recalled how at night he was afraid of rustling, sounds and strange voices, saw objects moving for no reason, and once even saw a ghost - an angry old lady.

Edward's father, a police officer, convinced the boy that every event had a rational explanation. Alas, the dad could not explain logically the oddities of their home.

After the war, Ed began to become intensely interested in paranormal phenomena. At first, Lorraine did not really believe in the existence of other worlds, but after the first few investigations, her opinion changed. Both spouses began active self-educational activity - they studied a lot of scientific and esoteric literature. Naturally, the first studies were not easy for the young couple, because they were just children whom no one took seriously. But every year their popularity and fame grew - now many families have already asked them for help.
