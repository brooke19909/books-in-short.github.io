Various issues of a banknote of 500 rubles

There are several modifications and issues of a banknote of five hundred rubles. At present, there are 4 types of such notes in circulation: a 1997 sample, and the same sample, but modifications of 2001, 2004 and 2011. All four types are accepted for payments throughout Russia. Since this bill is the most popular, the Bank of Russia is constantly increasing the level of money protection and introducing more modified options into circulation. A banknote of five hundred rubles in 2010 is considered the most secure. The prevailing color of the bill is violet-blue, size 150x65 mm. Two-tone and gray fibers, subtle embossed strokes, security threads and watermarks are embedded in the paper . When combining the reverse and front sides, their fragments coincide, forming a solid pattern. The numbers of the serial number of the banknote five hundred rubles of the 2010 modification are gradually increasing from left to right, the image has changed on the back, some of the elements of which have magnetic properties.
