Oz Hotels Sui 5 * (Turkey, Alanya): description, service, reviews

Administratively, Turkey is divided into seven regions. One of them is Alanya, located in the west of the country. It is here that the village of Okurcalar is located. This is a relatively small resort with a population of only four and a half thousand people. However, today many Russians already know this place quite well. Tours in this direction have recently become quite popular.
