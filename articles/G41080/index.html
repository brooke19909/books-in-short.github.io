Connection Instructions

Since there were many wireless models in the review, it‚Äôs worth separately to tell how to connect Beats headphones.

In general, the connection itself follows the same scenario. Often the difference can be precisely in the devices to which the headset is connected. For example, for the Beats Studio headphone model to work, you need to disconnect the audio cable from the gadget, if it is connected.

Beats headphones are connected a little differently. The instruction is included, but you can describe it in several steps:

  • in the list we are looking for a model of headphones;
  • we are connected.

If you have an iPhone, you need to unlock it and put the device next to it. Next, click on the power button of the headphones to turn them on. A notification about connecting to the headset will automatically appear on the smartphone screen. Next, follow the instructions on the phone screen.

Repairs

Unfortunately, breakdowns happen even with very expensive headphones. Repair may be required for various reasons. Well, if the device still has a warranty and you can repair or replace them for free. But what if there is no guarantee anymore?

There are not many options. You can take them to the store where you bought. And for the money you will try to repair them. You can also just throw them out and purchase new ones. Alternatively, you can also repair the device yourself.

Of course, do-it-yourself repairs need to be done only by understanding this issue. The easiest option is to replace the jack plug. You can also try cleaning ear pads. If the design of the headphones is overhead, it is better not to deal with such things yourself.
