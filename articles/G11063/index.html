Hotels in Schelkovo: description, accommodation options, reviews and photos

The small town of Shchelkovo near Moscow does not have five-star hotels, but this does not make it less hospitable. Each tourist or business traveler will be able to choose a placement option that suits him in terms of pricing and location. Hotels in Schelkovo are located both in the city center and on its outskirts.

Country Hotel "Ulitkino"

If you want to stay away from the noisy city streets, the best accommodation option is a guest house located in the village of Ulitkino, which is 6.5 km from the central part of Schelkovo. This hotel took its first guests in 2010 and since then has become one of the best guest houses in the Moscow region.

The tourist will be able to choose a number from the following:

  1. Double standard for 3500 rubles. The room has 2 single beds, TV, wardrobe, refrigerator, private bathroom.
  2. Double standard for 4000 rubles. The room is equipped with a double bed, a table and chairs, a TV and a fridge. Also in the room there is a safe for valuables.
  3. Triple room in a Finnish house for 4200 rubles. The room has 3 single beds, refrigerator, TV and sofa. There is also a bathroom.

It is especially nice that this hotel in Schelkovo provides half board, which is included in the price. Guests can have breakfast and lunch right at the hotel.

Based on enthusiastic reviews from guests, we can conclude about the excellent infrastructure of the guest house. Here you can play table tennis, swim in the pool or try your hand at billiards. Guests also note the professionalism of the staff and impeccable cleanliness in the rooms and throughout the hotel.

Hotel "City"

If a guest is looking for a hotel near the central part of the city, it is worth paying attention to this hotel in Schelkovo. Address: st. Radio center, 5, building 18, building 2.

To accommodate guests there are double rooms belonging to the categories of "standard" and "comfort". The first will cost the guest 2800 rubles, and the second will have to pay 3600 rubles. The rooms have a comfortable double bed, a desk, a refrigerator, a bathroom with all necessary accessories.

Throughout the hotel there is free Wi-Fi, parking for the transport of guests with 24-hour video surveillance.

Reviews hotel in Schelkovo earned mostly positive. Guests noted daily housekeeping, friendly administration and new furniture. Also, many consider the advantage of the hotel small size and silence. The hotel has only 12 rooms.

Apartments on Fryanovskoe highway

The exact address of this hotel in Schelkovo is 64 Fryanovskoe Shosse, Building 2. The hotel is 2.5 km from the city center and 70 km from the airport.

It offers self-catering apartments of 35 square meters. It is equipped with all necessary appliances and new utensils. There is also a fridge and a washing machine. The cost of living is 2600 rubles.

The hotel offers free internet access and parking. Perhaps a settlement with pets, only this must be warned by the administrator in advance.

The opinion of those who have already managed to evaluate this hotel in Schelkovo is positive. Spacious room, clean linens and convenient location of apartments are noted.

Silk Road Hotel

Travelers can find this hotel at the address: pr. 60 years of October, 11. This is 5 km from the central part of the city.

To accommodate guests, the hotel provides single and double rooms at a price of 2000 and 2800 rubles, respectively. The rooms have comfortable modern beds, a desk and a TV. There is a private bathroom and shower.

Meals are not included in the price. However, the local restaurant serves a delicious breakfast and lunch. Here are dishes of Japanese, European and Russian cuisine.

Those who travel by their own transport are provided with a parking space. You can also connect to the Internet for free.

Judging by the reviews, the hotel does not make much of an impression. Guests note a very modest decoration of rooms. But the hotel administrator is mentioned in the reviews as a professional and a very kind and helpful person.

Hotel "Circle"

The hotel, located on Zavodskaya street, 5a (almost in the central part of the city), has 3 stars. Guests here are accommodated in double rooms, the cost of which ranges from 2800 to 3220 rubles. It depends on the meter of the room itself. The rooms have comfortable beds, a wardrobe and a TV, as well as bedside tables. Equipped with a private bathroom, there is a hairdryer and all necessary hygiene items.

Meals are not included in the price, but for only 100 rubles a tourist will be provided with a delicious and fresh breakfast. Parking is also provided for transporting holidaymakers and free internet access.

Judging by the photo of the hotel in Schelkovo, this is a very comfortable and modern accommodation option. Positive reviews from tourists relate to daily cleaning of the rooms and the bright interior of the rooms. The disadvantages of this hotel guests attributed the lack of a restaurant on site.
