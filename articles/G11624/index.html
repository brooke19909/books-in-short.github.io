Turkey is waiting for us! Marmaris Hotels

In the south-west of Turkey there is a colorful and versatile resort of Marmaris. Here, at the meeting point of the Mediterranean and Aegean Seas, is the Marmaris Bay, which is an example of environmental cleanliness. And the city itself, because of the many discos and bars, is also called Turkish Ibiza. Here you can find hundreds of bars, whose terraces are located on the beach. Also, one of the streets of Marmaris is given to dance establishments. And the music in this city does not stop until the morning. Therefore, this resort is most suitable for lovers of fun holidays. Although it is visited by tourists of different calibers, who came to rest in a country called Turkey. Marmaris hotels can provide leisure to all comers.





There are many of them, and they are very diverse. And those who have already been here could appreciate the choice of completely different types of places of residence. Take the Elegance 5 * hotel , which is located 3 kilometers from the center of Marmaris and, naturally, on the seashore. It was built in 1992, and in 2007 the last restoration was made. And now it has three 5-story buildings with 190 rooms. There are also restaurants, bars, two conference rooms, several swimming pools, a jacuzzi and a sauna for hotel guests. Like the rest of Turkey, Marmaris hotels pamper tourists in full, as there is plenty of entertainment here. Guests at the Elegance Hotel can play squash and archery, dive and surf, visit the solarium and gym. There is a tennis court and a fitness center, video games and billiards, massage and beauty salon and much more.





But not only rich people are attracted to Turkey. Marmaris hotels can accept tourists with more modest incomes. There is a category of youth hotels that everyone can afford. But even despite the relative cheapness, they offer their guests quite decent service and food. However, the bars and discos that are built next to such affordable hotels will not allow young people to sit in the rooms. And an example of such a hotel is the Green Beach Hotel 3 *, which is located in the city center, near the sea. Its 5-story building has 65 standard rooms. This hotel also has a restaurant for 120 people, a vitamin bar and a bar on its own beach. In addition, the hotel has a small pool, a TV room, and the lobby has free Wi-Fi.





Marmaris also has club hotels. These are houses on 1-3 floors, each of which nevertheless has its own beach. All 4-5-star hotels, as well as some hotels with 2 and 3 stars on the signboard, can boast their own beaches here. But besides the beaches there are other entertainments, for example, small cruises along the turquoise bays. Here, tourists enjoy picturesque landscapes, mysterious caves and wild beaches. In a word, like the whole of Turkey, Marmaris hotels can offer an unforgettable vacation.
