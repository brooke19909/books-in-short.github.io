New York Plaza Hotel Apartments 3 * (Cyprus, Paphos): hotel and leisure description, reviews

Cyprus is called an island with a big heart, cordially inviting guests and hosting them as the closest friends. This is a land of contrasts and extraordinary beauty. The serenity and tranquility of these villages contrasts with the vibrant and colorful cities, distinguished by their cosmopolitanism. The island surprises with a significant number of long-livers inhabiting it, which is explained by the fact that the local climate is the most moderate and healthy in the world. Almost all year round the island shines bright southern sun.





Those who wish to spend leisure time in Cyprus often face a difficult task: from the rich assortment of options they need to choose the most suitable place to relax. For experienced lovers, experienced travelers recommend staying in Limassol or prefer never-sleeping Ayia Napa; Those wishing to save money are advised to opt for budget Larnaca; those who wish to relax with their family should go to Protaras; lovers of solitude and tranquility will certainly enjoy Polis, Northern Cyprus, Famagusta or Kyrenia; connoisseurs of prestige, according to experts, should prefer the elite Paphos.

The island is unusually rich in options for accommodating people who come here to relax. One of the most popular is the New York Plaza Hotel Apartments (Paphos). Information about the institution, as well as guest reviews about it, are contained in the article.





For future guests New York Plaza Hotel Apartments (Cyprus, Paphos): get to know the resort

Paphos is a resort located in the southwest of the island between the coast and the Troodos Mountains. The city has a huge number of hotels of various categories. There are both 2 * category establishments and 5 * hotels providing high quality service. Among other quite popular analogues, the New York Plaza Hotel Apartments (Cyprus) warmly welcomes visitors in Paphos, and grateful guests left a lot of positive reviews about their stay in Paphos.

Tourists, going on vacation, are always interested in the history of the place in which they have to spend leisure time, as well as other conditions of stay. Paphos is known primarily as the birthplace of Aphrodite, the famous goddess of beauty from ancient Greek mythology. The place where the goddess, according to legend, appeared from the foam, is especially noted here. Back in 1980, the city became the very first object in Cyprus, taken under the protection of UNESCO as a place teeming with historical and cultural monuments. Paphos fully justifies its name: it is considered the most elite resort on the island, where mainly wealthy people can afford to relax. According to reviews, this resort is completely non-focused on families with children; neither animation programs for children, nor playgrounds are provided for here. This should be taken into account by potential tourists considering choosing a place to stay. But in Paphos there are a lot of secluded beaches and cozy bays, first-class restaurants and other places that make up the infrastructure of a "beautiful life", which, if you believe experts, would be more than enough for the whole southern island.

More about beaches

Whatever the hotel was chosen for accommodation on vacation in Paphos - New York Plaza Hotel Apartments or any other - information about the conditions of stay on local beaches will never be superfluous. According to the reviews of tourists who have been here, the beaches in Paphos are rocky and sandy. Entrance to the sea, vacationers warn, is not always convenient: often it is complicated by a steep rocky descent. The beaches are all free, but sunbeds and umbrellas can be used for a fee. The best and longest in Paphos is the long and wide sandy beach located in the bay of Coral Bay.





Hotels

As a rule, hotels in Paphos are built on the first coastline, and the higher the "star" of the hotel, the greater the likelihood that the institution has its own beach. Tourists claim that sometimes the entrance to the sea at 3 * hotels is sometimes even better than at 5 *. Paphos hotels provide mainly breakfast, although many offer 2 meals a day. In hotels of category 3-5 * a buffet is served, in institutions 2 * the choice of dishes is usually very limited. Almost all hotels can please the presence of Russian-speaking staff. Animation in all hotels is practically absent. In winter, many hotels in Paphos close. Those that remain open attract guests with working tennis courts, pools and gyms.

As the most successful accommodation option, which pleases both the high level of service and the quality of food, tourists recommend the New York Plaza Hotel Apartments 3 *. The "star" of the hotel, according to reviews, is consistent with the service provided here.

Reviews
