Kahya Aqua Resort Hotel 5 * (Turkey, Alanya): description, service, reviews

The wonderful weather in Konakli attracts tourists every year to visit this Turkish city for a wonderful holiday. Here, for almost the whole year, the sun shines brightly and pleases all guests with its warm rays. The average air temperature in the midst of the holiday season here reaches 35-45 degrees, the air here is not hot, it is quite well softened under the influence of marine air masses.

One of the largest hotels in this village is Kahya Aqua Resort Hotel 5 *, which attracts thousands of tourists with its excellent living conditions and good leisure opportunities. There is a considerable number of places for entertainment, as well as a beach within walking distance from its territory.



general information

Kahya Aqua Resort Hotel 5 * offers tourists from different regions of the world excellent conditions, perfect for family, youth and even a solo vacation. Around it are planted a considerable number of beautiful and abundantly blooming gardens that delight the eyes of tourists, from here you can also enjoy mountain views - it is these landscapes that often attract travelers.

The hotel is quite modern, it was built in 2012, since then a small reconstruction has been made in its buildings, which happened in 2016. During its implementation, the interior was slightly modified and some technical elements were improved.

Currently, the hotel area is 36,000 square meters. m. On them, developers have posted a couple of modern and pretty beautiful buildings, a luxurious garden, as well as several places for entertainment.

Location

Kahya Aqua Resort Hotel 5 * is located in Turkey, at one of its most visited resorts - Alanya. It is located on the outskirts of the town of Konakli, which is famous for its natural landscapes, as well as excellent climatic conditions. Weather in Konakli allows tourists to relax in this city for almost the whole year.

Near the hotel there are a large number of attractions, including many historical ones. The sea line is 100 meters from the main building, across the road.

The nearest airport is 110 kilometers from this vacation spot - in Antalya, it is here that all foreign tourists arrive and here they are met by a car sent by the hotel as part of the transfer service. It is mandatory and is included in each travel package on a standard basis.



Rooms

The room fund of Kahya Aqua Resort Hotel 5 * is rather considerable, it contains 447 apartments, thanks to which the hotel is classified as a large vacation spot. All apartments are located in two modern six-story buildings, which are surrounded by gardens and water zones. For easier movement of tourists on their territory in the buildings of the buildings elevators are provided.

All presented apartments are divided into two categories: family and standard, depending on the proposed living conditions and the number of possible places for tourists to resettle. Since the last renovation, all of them have been equipped with modern air conditioners, with the help of which the temperature in the rooms is now regulated regardless of weather conditions.

Each room has a modern, well-equipped bathroom, which has absolutely all the plumbing, a hairdryer, as well as a pair of cabinets that store bath accessories and bathrobes.

Standard

The standard apartments are designed for three guests, 34 square meters are allocated for each room. m square.

In the furniture set, two beds occupy a central place, one of which is single, and the second is double. Near each of them there is a small pedestal - a great place to store small everyday items, it has a landline telephone connected to an international line. You can use it for a fee. There is a large closet with several shelves for large things of guests, and an excellent working area consisting of a large desk and a chair is arranged for employment.

In their free time, guests can watch TV, which is also in the room. It is suspended and broadcasts satellite channels, some of which, according to the guests, are Russian-speaking. The rooms are equipped with a free minibar, the assortment of which includes cool water, an electronic safe can be ordered for a fee.

Not far from the balcony a rather comfortable sitting area is equipped, it is represented by a small sofa and a table. Here guests can enjoy a cup of tea or coffee, which can be prepared in the room using the substation.



Family

Often in their comments about the rest at Kahya Aqua Resort Hotel 5 * indicate that you can stay on its territory with the whole family. First of all, the convenience of this circumstance is due to the fact that a number of apartments of the corresponding class are presented in the hotel room stock.

The family rooms are 36 square meters. m is divided into two rooms, each of which is a fairly comfortable bedroom. They have two sleeping places in the form of beds, as well as a place to store things. With regard to electronics, the apartments have one TV, a minibar, and a telephone. The safe can be installed subject to daily payment.



Conditions for children

Families who stay in this hotel with children, note in their reviews about it that on the territory of Kahya Aqua Resort Hotel 5 * (Turkey) there are all conditions for the comfort and safety of small guests. There are also several places for their active pastime, among which special attention is paid to the presence of a large playground and pool, which is equipped with all safety requirements.

If necessary, an individual crib can be installed in the apartment, this is completely free and upon prior request. With negative comments, parents note the lack of the possibility of renting baby pots and prams, which is in many other hotels in Turkey.

In one of the buildings there is an excellent children's mini club where kids can spend their time in the company of peers under the supervision of animators. Parents talk about the fact that some animators speak Russian well, which makes it even more interesting for children from Russia to be with them.

Entertainment

The hotel offers a range of entertainment, among which the most popular are evening discos. You can visit them for free, they are held every day. During parties on the venues, clockwork music performed by DJs is played. It also hosts show programs with a team of animators. In the morning and before lunch, hotel guests can take part in recreational sports events that take place within the large aquazone and outdoor areas.

One of the most favorite leisure activities, according to reviews, is a visit to the diving center. Here, for a fee, everyone can be taught the art of scuba diving. Under the guidance of a diving instructor, you can practice your skills in practice without wasting time.



Nutrition

The hotel has three large catering establishments - these are restaurants serving delicious Mediterranean, Turkish and Italian cuisine. Often in their reviews of Kahya Aqua Resort Hotel 5 *, guests talk about the excellent and very bright serving of dishes at the buffet, which is organized in these places every day. All travelers who live on the concept of "all inclusive" can take part in it.



In restaurants, if desired, you can request three additional types of menus: vegetarian, diet and children's. All of them are provided absolutely free of charge, which, of course, pleases tourists.

In addition to large establishments, the hotel also has six bars, one of which is open around the clock. There are discos throughout the night. Behind the bar there are professional and very talented bartenders who can conquer guests with their skill in preparing cocktails and drinks. Here you can also taste light salads and taste real oriental desserts.
