Hotels Phi Phi (Thailand): review, rating, reviews

Phi Phi (Thailand) is a resort that allows you to enjoy a quiet, not crowded holiday and white sand beaches. This region attracts divers and honeymooners, who are the majority here. The archipelago includes 6 islands. The inhabited Phi Phi Don, located in the north, and Phi Phi Lei, on which almost no one lives, located in the southern part, are the largest of them.

Climate

The period from November to April is the most suitable time for a visit to the archipelago. The air temperature is steadily held at around 30 degrees Celsius and is truly heavenly. Rain in the tropics is most often stormy, but short in duration, so the rainy season is absolutely not an obstacle to relaxation. In a word, Phi Phi (Thailand) is an island created for lovers of magnificent natural landscapes, wild impenetrable jungle, sun and clear sea water, exotic species of birds and animals, trees and plants, and just an amazing vacation.

Where to stay





One of the best resorts in Thailand can offer tourists a vacation in one of the many various hotel complexes: premium-level hotels, resort tourist complexes, cozy bungalows and hostels in which you can have a great vacation with your family or company. The choice depends on the available budget, but do not doubt that not very well-off tourists have the opportunity to stay in a hostel, where for a small amount they will get a bed with basic amenities. For those who prefer a more expensive and comfortable holiday, a wide selection of resort complexes and spa areas is available. The best hotels of Phi Phi are true tropical complexes surrounded by rich flora and an atmosphere of equilibrium of nature. The rooms offer magnificent, breathtaking views of the waters of the Andaman Sea. By opting for a bungalow, you can enjoy the sound of waves and the starry sky every night. Most tourists recommend choosing hotels in Phi Phi 5 stars. There are not many of them, but it is here that you can fully experience the paradise on earth.

Zeavola Resort





This 5-star hotel offers guests access to a private beach, an outdoor pool and cozy rooms. It features a fitness center and sauna. Rooms are equipped with a minibar and air conditioning, as well as coffee / tea makers and bottled water. Each has a seating area and amenities for entertainment and leisure, including a DVD player. Guests at Zeavola Resort can enjoy seafood and Thai dishes at Baxil Restaurant or enjoy international and seafood at Tacada Restaurant. Breakfast is served daily and room service is included. Guests can enjoy a wide selection of drinks at the bar, which offers scenic views of the coast. Nearly all traveler reviews on this property are positive. Tourists like excellent living conditions, developed infrastructure and excellent service.

Holiday Inn Resort Phi Phi Island

The hotel complex is located on the coast of the Andaman Sea. It is calm and quiet, there are no cars and roads. The complex is located on a quiet snow-white beach Laem Tong Beach, considered the best beach on the island of Phi Phi. Location: 42 km from Krabi and 48 km from Phuket. The hotel has two landscaped pools, two bars, newspapers, a sun terrace, concierge services, fax and photocopying, room service, a 24-hour front desk, air conditioning, luggage storage, shops, including a gift shop, and currency exchange. Pets not allowed. The cozy Cha Bah restaurant by the beach will delight guests with freshly caught grilled seafood. The Thai Rom Prao restaurant hosts entertainment every evening and offers a buffet.

Phi Phi The Beach Resort





The hotel is located on the island of Phi Phi, on the beach of Long Beach. It offers hillside apartments with stunning ocean views, an outdoor pool and kayaking and snorkelling equipment. From Krabi and Phuket airports to the hotel about 2 and a half hours drive. Transfer is possible both by sea and by land. The teak-furnished rooms at Phi Phi Beach Hotel have a private satellite TV, a balcony and tea making facilities. The bathroom has both cold and hot water for a comfortable bath. Guests can sign up for their favorite excursion at the tour desk, and after exploring the sights, order traditional massage from experienced Thai specialists. Laundry facilities are also available. A buffet breakfast is served daily in the cafe. The cozy restaurant serves delicacies of different cuisines, as well as seafood dishes, and delicious cocktails can be tasted at the beach bar. Guests are very happy with the location of the hotel and consider it one of the best in Phi Phi. Couples like the location and stay in the area especially. Value for money in Phi Phi.

Phi Phi Relax Beach Resort

A hotel complex was built in a quiet area on the amazing Pak Nam beach. The hotel apartments are equipped with all basic amenities. Around the hotel are picturesque gardens with tropical vegetation. Each room has its own private balcony. The hotel complex can be reached within 15 minutes by water from the main pier of Phi Phi. Such a transfer is paid separately. Great snorkelling spots can be reached in just a few minutes. The complex has an extremely good location between the two bays. It offers vacationers all kinds of exciting trips around the island. The bungalows are made of straw and bamboo and are simply furnished. Bathrooms equipped with bathrooms offer magnificent views of the amazingly beautiful tropical landscape or the ocean. Hotel guests can go fishing, go kayaking or book a massage right on the beach. Fans of energetic pastime will appreciate walking to the city through the dense jungle. The resort has its own restaurant, which serves local and Western dishes. At the beach bar, guests can enjoy invigorating cocktails and exotic fruit juices.
