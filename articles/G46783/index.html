Webcams with a microphone: overview, specifications, features and reviews

Video connectivity between portable devices is a science fiction novel that is being actively introduced in our life today. And for the organization of video communication between stationary personal computers, webcams were invented, the latest models of which are available with built-in microphones. What is it?





Features

Webcams with a microphone are a practical solution for mobile operation in the conditions of constant moving, as they do not need additional connection of a headset. The microphone can be made outside the webcam body and internal. Usually external microphones are characterized by a cleaner and higher-quality sound, however, in the latest models of webcams, their built-in brothers can compete in this matter.

Benefits of Built-in Microphones

The cost of such devices is quite high, but it is compensated by powerful functionality. For example, the ability to noise reduction makes it possible to talk in busy rooms, and the built-in microphone processor suppresses these noises. Such cameras vary widely in price, which will allow any buyer to find their model.

Webcams with a microphone work without drivers and additional utilities. There is a possibility in live mode to adjust the volume of the signal that is transmitted to the interlocutor (voice or sound). But initially (if the microphone does not work on the webcam), you need to check the sound settings in the program used.

The latest generation of webcams is equipped with a built-in microphone, which greatly increases their demand. Therefore, manufacturers are focusing on improving the sound quality of the microphone and its free transmission over the Internet. Let us consider in more detail the most rated representatives of this model range.
