Hotels located in Helsinki give peace.

Finland is a small state that attracts many tourists from all over the world. The only million-plus city in Finland, considering satellite cities, is Helsinki. This city is the capital of the state, but it is worth noting that Helsinki has little resemblance to the European capital, in the usual sense - everything is quite modest and differs in small size, as well as lack of bombast. Hotels located in Helsinki also do not stand out with too high a level of chic; five-star hotels can be counted on the fingers. Reviews of Helsinki hotels, as well as the city as a whole confirm that it is not luxury that attracts tourists to this place. The small territory of the city contains many amazing architectural monuments, cultural and historical sights, the examination of which involves almost all tours to Helsinki .

The history of Helsinki dates back to 1550, when the city was laid down by order of King Gustav Vas. Almost half of the millennium that Helsinki exists has brought the city many trials. The city passed from the hands of the Swedes, who captured Finland in the 12-13 centuries, to the Russians, who, in 1809, having won the Russian-Swedish war, included Finland in Russia. Only since 1812 Finland became independent and gained its own capital - the city of Helsinki.

The capital of Finland is a very green city, on its territory there are many parks, forests grow. The main attraction for tourists from all over the world is the Barents Sea.
