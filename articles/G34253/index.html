Rest houses on the Volga. Pension on the Volga: families with children

The Volga is considered one of the largest rivers on the planet. She paved her way across the European part of Russia and flows into the Caspian Sea. Unusual in their beauty of the local places have a pleasant pastime. There are beach beaches of sand, pebbles and small pebbles; farms where fish are bred; holiday homes on the Volga.

Staying here helps to restore mental and physical strength. Here you can not only have fun sunbathing on the beach or relaxing in a disco, but also get acquainted with the cultural heritage of this region by visiting historical places.



Entertainment

Due to the excellent climatic conditions, the proximity of the reservoir and the picturesque nature, many here seek to relax. One of the interesting types of recreation is a multi-day trip, a catamaran trip and swimming. You can see a lot of new and interesting things, making excursions along the river or its banks.

Also, one of the types of entertainment on the Volga is hunting for waterfowl or for a larger animal living on land. Fans of fishing are offered free and paid places on the river, each section of which is known for its kind of inhabitants. For example, the upper course is rich in bream and roach, and the lower one is rich in pikeperch, perch, pike, catfish, and asp.



Comfort

The Volga consists of four sections: the source, the upper and middle parts, as well as the lower reaches, including the delta of the watercourse. Each of these segments has individual features attractive to tourists. A favorable climate is characteristic of some areas, others are not far from large cities, and in others, environmental conditions are better. But all of them are united by one thing: regardless of the composition of the group of visitors and the chosen place, the rest houses on the Volga will welcome tourists and provide high-quality service at low prices. Tver, Yaroslavl, Ivanovo, Kostroma regions offer a calm and harmonious pastime. It is quite possible to go to a boarding house on the Volga with children. For amateur anglers, places are presented in the upper part of the stream. However, it should be said that in these areas there are few institutions with a medical profile.



Rest houses on the Volga, working in the preventive direction, are located in other areas. All of them specialize in the treatment of certain ailments. Where to find an inexpensive therapeutic and preventive holiday home on the Volga? Samara, Saratov, Ulyanovsk have all the conditions for receiving guests who want to improve their health. Institutions are mainly located in the Gorky, Ulyanovsk, and Saratov regions. In Tatarstan, similar resorts are also located.

All holiday homes on the Volga offer their guests a boat trip on the river. Such an excursion allows you to enjoy all the beauties of this magnificent region.

Rest house "Vanguard"

This health resort is located in an unusually beautiful place. Here the great Volga and the Tereshka river merge together. Going around beaches, mixed forests, forming channels and islets, they create a picture of amazing beauty. This land is attractive not only for avid fishermen, but also for simple romantics who dream to enjoy the contemplation of native Russian landscapes. And the children will be pleasantly surprised to find themselves in the very place where Tom Sawyer and Huckleberry Finn used to play Indians, crossing the raft to a desert island.



general information

This holiday home on the banks of the Volga appeared relatively recently. The base was built in accordance with modern requirements for accommodation. In the rooms of the building and in the summer houses, relatively inexpensive for their price, all the necessary comfortable conditions are created. They differ from the panel rooms that are offered on Seliger and Valdai. The choice of entertainment here is very rich. Here you can not only swim, fish, but also visit restaurants, bars with European and Russian cuisine, run for a soccer ball, ride a bicycle and roller skates, play billiards or relax in the sauna. In bad weather, you can sit by the fireplace in the room with a home theater. So, the described recreation center is one of the best and most affordable places on the Lower Volga for a good time with your family or company. It is located sixty kilometers from Saratov and covers an area of ​​nine hectares.

Characteristic

The recreation center "Vanguard" is a three-story hotel with comfortable rooms and about 40 summer houses with the necessary amenities. There are 13 rooms in one building. Each of them is designed for two people and includes a standard set of necessary: ​​shower, toilet, two beds, TV, sofa, kitchenette, refrigerator, kettle.



There are two types of summer houses:
1. In double rooms there is everything that is on the premises of the hotel. You can comfortably sit on two and a half or one double bed.
2. For a company of four, two-room housing is offered. There is an entrance hall, two bedrooms with two and one sleeping place. In the main building there are several double and triple rooms from 2800-3800 rubles per day.

Base "Samarkand"

The choice of vacationers offers standard rooms (3000 p. / Day.) And economy class (2500 p. / Day.), Designed for two people. Ordinary houses and VIP categories, designed for the same number of customers, cost from two to three thousand rubles. Four-bed apartments will cost twice as much. Children who are under seven years old can stay for free. For a child of 8-14 years old, five hundred rubles must be paid. Bed and breakfast are included in the price.

You can get to the recreation center by taxi, having paid the driver upon arrival at your destination. A trip from Yelshanka-Usovka will cost 200 rubles, and from Saratov - three times more expensive. Lunch and dinner are estimated at 700 rubles per person per day, and an additional breakfast at 200.

Rest house "Seagull"

The location of this base is unique. It is located next to Lake Rubsky, which is identified with Lake Baikal. Both adults and children can restore health here. Water containing sapropels and minerals is good for everyone. Thanks to the shallow depth of the sand spit, swimming is safe enough for kids. On a clean and well-kept beach in a calm and comfortable atmosphere you can have a great time. In the gazebo swaying on the water, there is everything for cozy gatherings with the company, and the competent staff will serve and select the necessary menu.

Meals are organized in the dining room. Each house is equipped with a kitchen for self-cooking. In the summer, the playgrounds and equipment (for rent) are presented to vacationers. Double rooms "Standard" are 2400-3000 rubles per day for two.
