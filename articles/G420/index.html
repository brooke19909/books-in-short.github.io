MFI rating on deposit reliability

Situation 1: urgently needed a small amount of money. So small that banks do not arrange loans of this size. Where to borrow to pay is not a question. If options with colleagues, friends and neighbors do not work, then microfinance organizations - MFIs will help.

Situation 2: the goal is set to increase selfless hard work earned a lot of money. Bank deposits promise 6% per annum, risky deposits guarantee 12%, debt receipts - profit not exceeding 4%. Firms will accept free finance at 20 percent per annum.

MFI rating will help limit the risks of placement and lending.





Item Definition

For transparency of the microloan market in the financial openings of Russia, it is allowed to create two types of mini-credit structures.

A microfinance organization is a company with equity of $ 70 million or more. The laws of the Regulator allow them:

  • to attract free cash funds of legal entities and citizens;
  • invest borrowed funds ;
  • lend to borrowers up to one million Russian rubles;
  • issue bonds - bonds.

At the same time, microcredit organizations with a capital of less than 70 million rubles function in the legal field. MCOs are allowed to work only on the basis of their own funds. For them, a ban has been imposed on attracting and investing funds of the population. The maximum amount of micro-loans from MCOs is 0.5 million rubles.

An MFI rating helps expand the client base and build trust in investor relations.

Parameters for calculation

When determining the level of reliability rating of MFIs, the following parameters are used:

1. The amount of the loan receivable. Pay attention to how many credit companies bear the name with the words ‚Äúborrow until payday‚Äù or ‚Äúloan week‚Äù. If it is impossible to return within the time period specified by the contract, at a rate of 1% per day, a minimum of 30% will be charged for a month, and with a loan of 5000 rubles. the consumer will be forced to give 6.5 thousand Russian money in a month .

4. A variety of ways to obtain microcredit money. Some organizations work only with bank cards, so as not to complicate the life of cash problems. Others offer a choice - cash or card. The list of third ones contains cards, bank accounts, and electronic wallets.

Look closely at the cost of each parameter. Additional costs add to the credit burden.

Investment Rules

Interest rates of deposits vary in banks depending on the size of the current rate of the Central Bank. Current figures are becoming less attractive. In these conditions, offers to place free money at an annual rate of 20, 25 and even 30 percent look attractive.

Both for lending services and for investment operations, MFI deposit ratings are calculated and published. Based on this indicator, we must remember that not all small companies are able to bear the cost of calculating comparisons with competitors.

Therefore, before transferring your own money to outsiders, you should check:

  • feedback on the quality of work;
  • OGRN number in the State Register on the site tax.ru;
  • registration number in the self-regulatory organization of microfinancing "Unity" or "World".

The population can give money in growth to microfinance offices. But here there are restrictions on the volume of investments. The law allows individuals to invest in MFIs in an amount of at least 1.5 million rubles.

Example: at a rate of 20% of investments in one and a half million Russian rubles, a profit of 300 thousand rubles is generated. Upon receipt of investment income, the client must pay income tax in the amount of 13%. Clean hands investor will receive 261 thousand rubles.

An honest MFI notifies the depositor that it assumes the duties of a tax agent, withholds the necessary funds, transfers it to the treasury, and, upon request, issues a personal income tax certificate.
