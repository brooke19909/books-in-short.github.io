Verkhnemakarovskoe reservoir: description of the reservoir

Not far from Yekaterinburg is the Verkhnemakarovskoe reservoir. Administratively, this territory belongs to the Sverdlovsk region. The reservoir was built on the Chusovaya River. Used for household needs of the city of Yekaterinburg. On its banks is the village of Verkhnemakarovo. It was she who gave the name to the reservoir. They began to fill it back in the 70s. However, the work on cleaning the territory was not carried out in full. This led to the fact that a lot of debris is found at the bottom of the reservoir. Near it there are many summer cottages, there is a farm, a fish hatchery and a sawmill.





Unfortunately, at present, the SES has banned swimming in this reservoir. The state of water did not affect the underwater animal world. There are still a lot of fish, so the Verkhnemakarovskoe reservoir is popular among fishermen.

Verkhnemakarovskoe reservoir - how to get there?

Many travelers are interested in the question: how to get to the reservoir? It goes to public transport. A regular bus travels from the city of Yekaterinburg. The final stop is the village of Kurganovo. From it you will need to walk to the reservoir. This distance is about 1 km. However, you need to be prepared for the fact that on a country road there are wetlands. But there are transitions through which you can go through these areas.





Those who travel by car must leave Yekaterinburg in the direction of Polevsky. Near the village Kurganovo driver will see the bridge. If you cross through it, you can turn into the city of Degtyarsk. Before entering the city (the village of Vyazovaya) there will be a turn to the right, it is he who leads to the reservoir itself. If you follow this route, you can go to the dam.

Fishing

As mentioned above, the Verkhnemakarovskoe reservoir, a photo of which can be seen in the article, is popular among fishermen. Access to the reservoir is free. There are a lot of fish, such as bream, carp, burbot, pike and others. You can fish both from the shore and from the boat. If you swim to the middle of the reservoir, then the catch will consist of larger individuals. For example, in order to catch a bream, you need to pre-feed the place. The depth should be at least 4 m. For bait, you can use semolina or barley. The best bite in the morning: from 4:00 to 10:00. You can catch perch all day. He pecks at a worm or bloodworm. The specimens are not very large, but in a few hours you can catch about 30-40 pieces.





It is recommended to go to a pike in a boat with a motor. You can use spinning with bright bait. Due to the fact that there are a lot of snags at the bottom of the reservoir, pike will not take long to wait. Large enough individuals are found. Despite some shortcomings, the Verkhnemakarovskoye reservoir (most of the positive reviews of fishermen about it) is ideal for a wild vacation. On its territory you can make bonfires, so many vacationers cook their own fish on an open fire.

The recreation center "Kurganovo"

In the village of Kurganovo there is an excellent recreation center. Here you can spend time both as a family and in company with friends. There are tents and gazebos on the territory where they organize wedding parties, receptions and other events. On sunny days, vacationers go to the reservoir for a swim. A football field and tennis court are also on site. Organize paintball, there is a rope town. Shooters visit the shooting range. For accommodation, rooms are equipped in accordance with European standards. They are located in several buildings.





The territory of the base is surrounded by a mixed forest. The air here is clean. This place is located far from noisy cities on the banks of such a reservoir as the Verkhnemakarovskoe reservoir. How to get to this recreation center? Given that it is located near Yekaterinburg, you can use public transport. Regular buses run from the city regularly.

Holiday Village "Trubnik"

In the village of Verkhnemakarovo there is a recreation center "Trubnik". She works all year round. Arriving here, tourists fall into the untouched nature of civilization. A dense forest grows on the territory of the base. The air here is clean, birds are constantly ringing. What else is needed for a good rest? The administration organizes fishing in the summer season. In winter, you can go skiing. On the territory of the base there is a pool table, dance floor, gazebos, a bar. In sunny weather, you can go swimming in the Verkhnemakarovskoe reservoir. The beaches are sandy. The depth increases gradually, so you can relax even with young children. Cozy houses are offered for living.





You can also rent luxury rooms. They are equipped with the necessary appliances. For holidaymakers who have arrived in a private car, there is a guarded parking lot. The recreation center provides banquet facilities for celebrations. It can be weddings or corporate parties.
