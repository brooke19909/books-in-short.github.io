Hotel Description The Three Corners Palmyra Amar el Zaman 4

Description of the hotel. The Three Corners Palmyra Amar el Zaman 4, a large modern hotel, is located very close to the sea coast, next to a small but very picturesque rocky bay. Here, guests are offered the best living conditions and plenty of opportunities to have fun. In addition, visitors here are waiting for a sincere welcome and professional, friendly service.





To begin with, it is worth noting that the hotel complex The Three Corners Palmyra Amar el Zaman was built recently, namely in 2005. It has a huge territory, decorated with palm trees, compositions from exotic plants and decorative walkways. Residential buildings are built in a simple but elegant oriental style. Getting here, tourists really feel like heroes of a beautiful oriental fairy tale.

The Three Corners Hotel Palmyra Amar el Zaman 4 is located just five kilometers from the international airport, so there will be no travel problems.

Description of rooms. The hotel is quite large and offers tourists check-in in one of 174 rooms. Here there are standard rooms designed to accommodate one or several people at once, as well as combined family rooms and suites.

It is worth noting that each room has access to a spacious balcony or terrace, from where you can enjoy views of the sea and the rocky mountains, watch the lively life by the pool. All rooms are decorated in a simple style. Here you will find ceramic floors, comfortable furniture from expensive varieties of wood and, of course, the necessary equipment. In particular, in the room you will find a TV that will allow you to watch your favorite channels, as well as a safe, telephone, air conditioning and a minibar with a small refrigerator.





Of course, there is a separate, rather large bathroom, where guests can take a shower or relax in the hot fragrant bath.

Maids clean the rooms every day. New towels, bathrobes and bed linen are provided every two days or upon request.

Nutrition. The Three Corners Hotel Palmyra Amar el Zaman 4 operates on an all-inclusive basis. Here guests are offered breakfast, lunch and dinner, and throughout the day, guests can enjoy rich buffets with fresh fruits, light snacks, some traditional oriental dishes, as well as soft drinks. From midnight to seven in the morning, sandwiches and fruits can be delivered directly to your room.

Of course, the hotel complex also has gourmet restaurants where you can fully enjoy exquisite dishes and luxurious surroundings. You can choose any of the local five restaurants, but it is worth considering that you need to reserve a table in advance. There are also several bars where you will always be treated to the best drinks, including wines, fresh draft beer, original and bright cocktails, prepared according to traditional local recipes.

And, of course, there are a lot of small fishing restaurants, taverns and eateries on the beach where you can dine, while enjoying the sound of the waves, beautiful landscapes and a warm sun.

Beach. The territory of The Three Corners Palmyra Amar el Zaman 4 overlooks a luxurious coral sandy beach with sun loungers and parasols in neat rows. Here you can get beach towels. Vacationers not only bathe and sunbathe, but also engage in water sports, including boating and catamarans. In addition, you can go scuba diving, as there are special courses for this. Here you can rent flippers, masks and diving equipment.





Additional services. Naturally, everything in this hotel complex meets international standards and is designed to guarantee vacationers the maximum level of comfort and convenience. There is a laundry and dry cleaning service, there is a small conference room and a medical aid station. There are also several shops and a wonderful souvenir shop. Parking is available. By the way, the hotel has the opportunity to rent a motorcycle, bike or car.

During your stay at this hotel you can improve your health and learn something new. To begin with, it is worth noting that there are two huge pools with umbrellas arranged around and comfortable sun loungers. You can also attend aerobics and fitness courses, learn oriental dances, and play billiards. There are also water gymnastics classes, which are held in one of the pools. And, of course, the hotel has its own group of very talented animators who practically always entertain guests, organize competitions and contests, arrange vivid, colorful performances and dances.

For children there is a separate pool, club, playground, mini-disco, as well as excellent water slides. There is also a nanny who provides excellent childcare.

Reviews Many foreign travelers choose the paradise hotel The Three Corners Palmyra Amar el Zaman 4. Reviews about it are almost completely positive. Guests praise the beautifully decorated area, delicious and varied food, and, of course, truly comfortable rooms. Parents are satisfied that the children have something to do, and the smallest tourists are simply delighted with such a variety of entertainment. Tourists really appreciate the convenience and comfort provided by this hotel, so most of the guests quickly become regular customers.
