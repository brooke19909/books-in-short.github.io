Hotel Svelton Manor 3 * India, North Goa, Calangute: photos and reviews of tourists

In India, they are able to provide tourists with excellent conditions for a memorable vacation. This is easily verified by visiting the Svelton Manor 3 * hotel. They always welcome guests and make every effort to ensure that their guests have a pleasant and unforgettable vacation.

Hotel location

The southwestern state of India Goa is famous for its amazing nature, warm climate and good-natured people. It is here, in the tourist city called Calangute, that the Svelton Manor 3 * hotel is located. The airport is only 55 km away. Therefore, arriving guests in just an hour get to their place of residence.





The hotel is located near the main attractions of the city. The beach can be reached on foot in 8-10 minutes. Literally a few steps away you will find various cafes where guests will have the opportunity to appreciate the local culinary art. Tourists who visited the hotel recommend visiting the Calangute market, where you can stock up on various exotic fruits, seafood or other goods for little money.

Features of the exterior and interior

From the first steps around the hotel, tourists fall in love with this place. Charming majestic palm trees that border a beautiful five-story building. All tracks are tiled and kept clean, which contributes to pleasant walks.

The garden at the hotel is quite small, but it is quite cozy. Lawns and ornamental shrubs are always neatly trimmed. In the shade of huge trees you can always hide from the rays of the scorching sun.





No less nice to be inside the hotel. Guests are greeted in a beautiful lobby, the interior of which combines modern and Indian style. Everything inside the building looks new and well-groomed, which indicates the excellent work of the staff.

Population

Arriving at the Svelton Manor 3 * Hotel (Calangute), tourists are immediately shown local hospitality. Vacationers are not forced to wait long and literally in a matter of minutes they are resettled by numbers. A family with a small child can be requested from managers for a cot, which is also immediately delivered to the room.

Hotel guests are advised to study the most common phrases in English before vacation. This will greatly simplify the task if you have to arrange something with the staff during the check-in, since the employees here do not speak Russian at all, although they try their best to understand the guests.

Infrastructure

Hotel Svelton Manor 3 * (Calangute) is an excellent example of combining low cost of living and good service. Here you can leave your personal transport in the parking lot, for which you do not have to pay extra.

The hotel provides the opportunity to leave valuables or other things in the luggage room. Such a service is provided for money. If the safe is reserved for the entire stay, then for its use you will have to pay only 50%. There is also an exchanger in the lobby where you can get local currency.

There are kiosks on site that offer souvenirs, food and other goods. There is also a laundry and dry cleaning service, where they will quickly put things in order, but only at an additional cost. In the lobby of the hotel there is a tour desk, which will advise the most interesting places to visit.

You can correct your hairstyle or renew your manicure at the hotel's hairdresser. There is also a massage parlor, where the masters of their craft offer visitors various procedures that allow you to relax and feel a surge of strength and vitality.

Room Features

Very good tourists respond to the rooms of the hotel Hotel Svelton Manor 3 *, a photo of one of which can be seen below. They consist of two rooms, which creates a feeling of home comfort. In total, the hotel has 27 rooms of the same type. They are comfortable to live with family or friends, as there is more than enough space.





Tourists note that the atmosphere in the rooms is very pleasant. Everything is new and in working condition. True, there are not enough closets in the rooms, and things have to be stored in a suitcase, on chairs or a bed. There are enough cabinets for placing small items in the rooms.

Sometimes guests complain about the presence of insects in the room. When dealing with a similar problem at the reception staff quickly corrects the situation, using various disinfectants.

Room conditions

Each room of the Sodder S Svelton Manor 3 * hotel has 2 balconies, which allows you to have a good time while admiring the beauty of these places. It can be accommodated on comfortable forged furniture. The rooms have air conditioning and fans, which work perfectly, creating a pleasant coolness in the rooms. The rooms also have two televisions, but Russian channels are not broadcast here. There are phones in the rooms. They can be used free of charge at the reception. Other calls require surcharge.

The rooms have kitchenettes. There is an electric kettle, there is tea leaves, coffee and sugar. The ingredients for making drinks are replenished as needed. Food can be stored in the refrigerator in every room. Drinks and snacks from the minibar come at extra costs.

In the bathrooms, the plumbing is working properly. Here you can take a shower. The guests do not complain about the lack of cold or hot water. Of personal hygiene items, only soap is provided. Hairdryers in some rooms are missing. But when contacting managers, this problem is quickly resolved.

Cleaned in rooms daily and very thorough. Sheets and towels are changed once a week. Some guests complain that the sheets here are far from new. But still, linen always smells good, which indicates its cleanliness.

Nutrition

Hotel Svelton Manor 3 * (India, North Goa, Calangute) provides its guests with one-time meals. Many tourists are not very happy with the small variety of dishes served for breakfast. Often guests here are treated to tea or coffee, sandwiches and delicious porridge. You can have breakfast by the pool or, if the weather does not allow, in the cozy restaurant hall. The hotel also has a bar and cafe. Here you can have a light snack or purchase a variety of drinks.





Lunch and dinner can be ordered at an additional cost in the hotel restaurant, which offers a selection of international and local dishes. But many tourists prefer to visit the restaurants and cafes of the city for afternoon and evening meals.

Beach holidays at the Svelton Manor 3 * hotel (India)

Calangute is known to many tourists as the most beautiful beach in North Goa. It is on it that the hotel guests have the opportunity to relax. Here you can enjoy plenty of fun in the warm waters of the Indian Ocean, get a great tan, take sunbaths, and walk up the sandy beaches, admiring the beautiful views of the local nature.





Umbrellas and sun loungers can be rented at beach cafes. Tourists note that if you buy any dishes in the neck and order drinks, then everything you need for a comfortable beach holiday is provided for free. Some cafes prepare delicious dishes, so they can have lunch, dinner and get umbrellas and sun loungers for the whole day.

The distance to the beach is about 500 m. You can get there on foot. The road will take a maximum of 10 minutes. If you wish, you can use the services of taxi drivers.

Sports and Entertainment

At the Svelton Manor 3 * hotel (North Goa, Calangute), guests can go swimming in the outdoor pool, near which there are always sunbeds, umbrellas and towels provided free of charge. This limits the ability to exercise. Children can swim in the special section of the pool, equipped for young guests. On the beach there are centers that provide the opportunity to engage in water sports, but only for a fee.





Entertainment programs at the hotel are also not conducted. But you can have fun in various institutions located nearby. Very funny holidays are arranged on the beach. Each shake hires musicians or other artists for an evening program to entertain guests until late at night.

Tours and activities outside the hotel

In Calangute, they tried to create excellent conditions for tourists to relax. There are many entertainment venues, each of which has its own unique atmosphere. Local clubs expect nightlife lovers from 22.00. It is interesting to spend time in the city's many bars and restaurants, where guests are offered to try delicious local specialties or international dishes.

Guests of the Svelton Manor 3 * hotel will be pleased to enjoy the beauties of local nature. Excursions in the jungle will allow you to see many beautiful places, meet exotic animals, admire the waterfalls. Also, tourists are offered to go to the spice factory or to the settlements of local tribes.

The real paradise will be Calangute for shopping lovers. In local markets you can find goods for every taste. The prices here are pleasantly surprising. Besides bargaining, you can significantly reduce the cost of any purchase. Here you can buy any seafood at the markets and turn to the necks to be cooked there.





Art and architecture lovers will also find something to do during their stay at the Svelton Manor 3 * hotel. Here you can visit the church of St. Alex and admire its beauty. The temple majestically rises above the surrounding area, drowning in beautiful greenery. You can see the work of local artists and sculptors in the Kerkar Gallery.

Hotel staff

Tourists vacationing in Svelton Manor 3 * note that all employees of the hotel are distinguished by courtesy and courtesy. There are no Russian-speaking employees here, but this does not greatly interfere with communication. Each employee makes every effort to understand what the guests need or what problems they have.

Vacationers at the Svelton Manor 3 * hotel note that tips here do not play a special role. Even if you do not leave a small remuneration to the cleaners or porters for their work, they still diligently perform their duties. None of the former guests of the hotel complained about the loss of things. Moreover, some tourists even trusted the staff to pack their bags, while all the valuables were neatly packed.
