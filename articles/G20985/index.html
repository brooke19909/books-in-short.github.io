Guest House "Elena" (Kabardinka): description and reviews of tourists

Kabardinka is a southern village located on the shore of the Tsemess Bay, in a picturesque valley descending to the seashore. This resort is popular with tourists primarily for very comfortable beaches. The sea in Kabardinka is almost always calm, because from the south-west the village is protected from the winds by Cape Doob, and from the north-east by the Markotkh Range. There are many good hotels in this village. And one of the best, according to many tourists, is the guest house "Elena".

Where is

This hotel is located in the center of Kabardinka on a quiet street, 300 meters from the sea. Her exact address: st. Friendly, d. 9. About 13 km from the village is the popular resort of Gelendzhik among domestic tourists.





Unfortunately, there is no train station in Kabardinka. Tourists who rent rooms at the Elena Hotel should get off the train at Novorossiysk Station. You can also get to Anapa, and then catch the intercity bus. The nearest airport to Kabardinka is in Gelendzhik.

Number of rooms

Guests of Kabardinka Hotel Elena offer two types of rooms - Economy and Standard. In the first case, amenities are provided for two blocks. That is, guests of economy rooms must share a bathroom with neighbors from an adjacent room. Standard rooms have individual showers. All rooms rented at the hotel include:

  • refrigerator;
  • air conditioning or fan;
  • TV.

The calculated apartments in this hotel can be for 2-5 people.
