TV appearances

For the first time on the screen, Gordey Kolesov appeared in April 2014, in the program "Director for himself." There he demonstrated knowledge of hieroglyphs and sang in Chinese. In January 2015, he took part in the talent show on CCTV-1 and won. This is the first foreign child to have achieved such success on Chinese television. Gordey Kolesov became famous overnight, the video with his performance hit YouTube, and over a million people watched it in a week. In April 2015, when the number of video views reached four million, the boy, along with his father, was invited to participate in the Let They Talk program. At the same time, two documentaries about Gordey and his family were shot and shown on Chinese TV. Since then, the child began to be called a child prodigy.

Passion for chess

Gordey Kolesov started playing chess in 2014, and a couple of months later he took part in the Guangzhou Championship, where he won the silver award. Since 2015, the boy began to systematically learn this game and participate in various qualification tournaments. In April 2015, Gordey Kolesov completed the first category and won the championship in Shenzhen. In May of the same year, the young chess player took part in a tournament among schoolchildren, held in Thailand, and entered the top ten.

In 2016, Gordey Kolesov took six points out of nine at the All-China Chess Tournament, and the implementation of this norm allowed him to become a candidate for master of sports.
