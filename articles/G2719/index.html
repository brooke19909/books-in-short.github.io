Water park in Zhlobin: phone, photo, reviews

The complex, located in Zhlobin on the street K. Marx, 3, began its work in 2006. The water park is part of a sports and entertainment institution called the "Olympic Reserve Center". The best qualified engineers and specialists worked on its creation, and the experience of foreign colleagues was also taken into account.

Zones





The water park in Zhlobin is conditionally divided into recreation and entertainment areas. The first includes pairs of different types, for example, Russian, Finnish and Turkish. There are also massage rooms, two tanning salons - vertical and horizontal. Fans of billiards can enjoy tables of various sizes. There is also a beauty salon on site , while parents are in which children can play in the equipped room with a computer. In the entertainment area there is a swimming pool for adults, size 30x16 m, as well as for small visitors. A 76-meter helical waterslide will help boost adrenaline. For lovers of water procedures, the water park in lobin offers a jacuzzi, pearl baths, geysers and hydromassage services. There is also a mountain river in this part of the water park. The entertainment area is about 1.5 thousand square meters. m

Additional services and places of rest

Also in the water park you can visit a cafe called “Laguna”, refresh yourself in the pizza restaurant “Madagascar” and enjoy refreshments in the “Ice Cafe”. Here, visitors will be offered a portion of the cocktail enriched with oxygen. And for movie buffs, there is a 5D-effect movie theater where you can watch movies of a diverse genre. Here everyone will find entertainment to their taste.

Reservations and Phones

The water park in Zhlobin is ready to accept everyone, whether it is a family, corporate or collective holiday. You can buy a ticket right at the entrance in case of an individual visit or a company of several people. In any other case, a reservation is required. To file a group application, you must pre-register at the water park in Zhlobin. Phones for booking seats in the complex: +375 2334 50051, +375 2334 50931, +375 2334 50048.

In case of preliminary registration, one person from the group must present his passport. One document is enough to issue tickets for the entire company.

For cashless payments, applications are executed on letterhead indicating the details of the payer organization. Documents can be sent by fax, whose number is +375 2334 50304.

What do you need to visit and what are the rules in the water park?





To visit the water park in Zhlobin, you must have an individual towel, personal hygiene products, rubber or silicone slippers, as well as shoe bags. You should also follow the rules of conduct in the entertainment complex. Calculation of the time spent on the territory is done hourly. Exceeding the limit is paid per minute. At the entrance, each visitor is given a bracelet with a magnet, with which you can open your locker in the locker room, as well as go through the turnstiles installed in the complex.
