How to use a credit card? Credit Card Overview and Terms of Use

This financial instrument is used today by people all over the world. Getting a credit card is quite simple, but not everyone knows how to use a credit card correctly and how profitably.

The credit card is a plastic card with a protective chip and a magnetic stripe. Unlike debit cards, credit cards have cash in the form of a loan. The main advantage of such a card is the possibility of issuing it online on banks' websites without visiting a bank office.

The decision to issue a credit card is received by the client within a few minutes after sending the application for receipt. If approved, card issuance may take up to three days; some financial institutions issue them to customers immediately upon request. To issue a credit card to a borrower over 18 years of age, he must provide the banking organization with his passport data, documents confirming income (reference 2 personal income tax). In some cases, proof of income is not required, and only a passport of a Russian citizen is needed. In the article we will try to find out which credit cards are the most profitable in Russia.

Credit Card Benefits

The main advantages of this method of lending are:

  • the presence of bonuses, discounts, promotions from partners of a financial institution;
  • convenient payment for goods and services;
  • cashback;
  • grace period - interest-free use of borrowed funds.

A credit card is a good alternative to a consumer lending program. The limit amount is determined by the financial institution based on the solvency of each individual client. This amount is usually renewable - after repayment of the debt, the loan becomes available again. Both the owner and the credit institution may increase the limit on a credit card.

The main disadvantage of a credit card is a high interest rate, a fee for servicing a credit card, a fee for withdrawing cash.

How to choose a credit card?

Before applying for a credit card, it is necessary to carefully analyze the information on banks and various loan programs. The choice of financial institution, its loan product should be the most profitable for the borrower. When selecting a credit card, it is necessary to take into account not only interest rates, but also the size of commissions, for example, for cash withdrawals.

To date, all credit cards have a grace period without interest, but it is calculated in various ways. Before receiving the card, the client needs to know about the method of calculating the grace period of time and its duration.

Favorable cash withdrawal from the card

Terms and conditions for the issuance of funds from the card are prescribed in the contract. Credit cards with a grace period of cash withdrawals are extremely rare. Usually a commission is charged for cash withdrawals - 3-5% of the amount or more. If funds are cashed out using the terminals of other banking organizations, a certain percentage may be withheld for withdrawal. Issuance of money may not fall under grace periods - this should be clarified with a bank employee. It is usually recommended not to withdraw cash from a credit card - this financial instrument is provided for non-cash payments. Otherwise, you will have to pay a commission.

What is a credit limit?

The credit limit amount is set by the banking institution - as a rule, for each client individually, after studying his credit history and assessing solvency. A credit limit is the maximum amount of a credit card to be used by a borrower. The definition of the limit is based on some rules - the amount of payment cannot be more than 30% of the revenue. In addition, other factors may influence the decision of the bank - the borrower's credit standing, his age, the length of his last employment, the availability of alimony and other debt obligations.

Grace period: how to choose profitably?

A grace period is a specific time period that is set by a banking institution when borrowed funds without interest can be used by a client. To do this, the borrower should pay off all debt in time before the end of the grace period. Typically, the grace period is 50-120 days.

Definition options:

  1. It is based on the settlement period (30 days), followed by a payment period (up to 20-25 days), during which you should pay off the credit card debt (Sberbank).
  2. From the first payment (this method is practiced by Alfa Bank).
  3. Given each financial procedure (Moskomprivatbank).

The borrower needs to pay off credit card debt in a timely manner before the end of the grace period, which will eliminate the likelihood of charging interest on the use of credit funds.

How to increase credit card limit?

After some time using the credit card, the limit on the card either increases automatically, as, for example, in Sberbank, or after contacting the bank. The lender can independently offer the borrower such a service if he fulfills the following conditions:

  • active use of the card;
  • systematic and timely debt repayment;
  • the borrower is not a debtor for other loans;
  • good credit history.

Where can I pay by credit card?

There are two ways to pay off credit card debt:

  1. During the grace period, a one-time payment equal to the entire amount of the debt.
  2. Mandatory minimum payments, the amount of which is established by a financial institution.

Additionally, commissions may be charged from the client, for example, a fee for servicing a credit card during the year.

Money can be deposited in various ways:

  • via online banking;
  • through an ATM;
  • using the cashier in the bank;
  • by bank transfer from card to card;
  • using a mobile bank.
