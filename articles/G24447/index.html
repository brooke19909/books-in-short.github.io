sights

Not far from the hotel for lovers and extreme sportsmen there is a water park Water World, famous for one of the tallest towers in the world (80 meters) for bungee jumping. Due to the large number of attractions it is called the "city of entertainment" in which everyone will find something for themselves: from dizzying slides to the pool with sea waves.

Santa Clotilde Gardens is located in a picturesque place on a cliff with breathtaking sea views. Terraces, streams, fountains and waterfalls are truly worth seeing. National treasure is one of the most successful examples of the Italian Renaissance, where garden architecture is in harmony with vegetation. An ideal place for memorable shots from relaxation.

The fortress of Tossa de Mar, amazing in its safety, was built on a cliff 70 meters above sea level. Beyond these walls, local residents were forced to defend themselves against the pirates who had taken possession of the coastal waters at that time. The fortress was a whole city with its church, from which today there are only ruins, and in the former residential buildings there are cozy cafes and hotels.

Sant Joan Castle, or St. John's Castle, is located on top of a hill that separates Lloret Beach and Fenals Beach. The appearance of this castle is inextricably linked with the emergence of Lloret himself. And although the historical building suffered many partial collapses, today the rebuilt symbol of the city again crowns its hill.

Amusement park Gnomo Park. The main visitors here are precisely the children who happily dive into the labyrinth with slides and dry pools. The park has rope tracks with different levels of difficulty of the route. While children are busy on slides, trampolines and swings, adults can take shelter from the sun in a shady picnic area.
