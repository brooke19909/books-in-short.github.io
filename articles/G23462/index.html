Hotel Sugar Marina Resort Fashion 3 *: overview, features and reviews

Sugar Marina Resort Fashion Hotel 3 * in Thailand is located 45 km from Phuket Airport, on the southwest coast of the island, washed by the waters of the Andaman Sea in the Indian Ocean. Three bridges connect the island to the mainland. Its length is 50 km and its width is 21 km.

The captivating island of Phuket

Most of the island is occupied by hills, separated by deep transverse passages. In the mountains there are relict tropical forests protected by the state, and on the plains there are buildings, plantations of coconut palms. The main mountains do not exceed a height of 500 m.

To the south of Patong, at a distance of 15 km, is the popular Kata Beach with Sugar Marina Resort Fashion Kata Beach 3 *.

Monsoon rains in Phuket

The island has a subequatorial climate, the year is divided into dry and wet seasons. The average annual temperature is 32 degrees Celsius. The wet season lasts from May to October. At this time, daily showers, which are sometimes lingering, fall and the sky is often overcast. Rains may occur during the dry period, but last a few minutes, after which the bright sun shines again.

Another feature of the climate of Phuket is the appearance of strong currents in the summer months. Swimming during this period becomes dangerous, as indicated by red flags on the coast.

Holidays on Kata Beach

Kata Beach is popular because of its golden sands and beauty, as well as amenities and entertainment that are organized for tourists who come to enjoy the ocean and sunbathe. Those who are looking for a calm sea will enjoy the season from November to April, and in the southwestern monsoon season, from May to October, Kata becomes a pilgrimage site for surfers trying to ride the rebellious waves. The beach consists of two parts.

Guests of the Sugar Marina Resort Fashion Kata Beach 3 * can enjoy all the events taking place on the beach, since only 150 meters are needed from it across the road. The beach is already visible at the exit of the hotel.

On the beach you can rent umbrellas, mattresses. Massage parlors work in the shade of trees, while street vendors prepare mouth-watering snacks. You can buy fruits, drinks, fried chicken.

Sellers walk along the beach, offer clothes, sunglasses, fresh fruits. In its shady part, it is convenient to relax with small children. In the restaurants you can order Thai and international dishes, seafood. During the meal, a picturesque view of the sea opens; you can enjoy magnificent views of the sunsets.

During the holidays, tourists from the Sugar Marina Resort Fashion Kata Beach can go kayaking, diving and snorkeling, enjoy local parachute corals, rent a yacht and go night and day fishing.

The fast high waves on Kata Beach are an irresistible temptation for surfers who make a pilgrimage here in the rainy season. Here you can rent boards, take a training course. On small waves off the coast, even children can practice.

Do not forget to bring sun protection with maximum and medium protection.

The Royal Sailing Regatta, one of the largest in Asia, is held here in December.

A variety of excursions in Phuket

The island has an ancient and eventful history, therefore, despite its small size, there is something to see in your free time.

Patong beach is located in the northern part of Phuket, which is 3 km long and can be reached by tuk tuk. In Kata there is an opportunity to rent bikes to travel around the entire island on your own.

Water park in phuket

The island is open water park "Jungle Spray." It takes about an hour to get to it from the hotel. This can be done at an inexpensive transfer of the water park.

It has a thematic division into zones symbolizing Asia, Africa, America, Antarctica, ancient civilizations. You can move around the territory along the riverbed. For kids there is a children's town, you can visit the sauna.

Grand Buddha statue and temples

From the southern part of the beach you can see the Buddha statue towering over the island, the distance from which is 1.79 km from Sugar Marina Resort Fashion 3 *. The statue is 45 meters high and is located on the territory of the existing Buddhist complex.

The figure of the Enlightened One is located at an altitude of 400 m above sea level. Hiking enthusiasts can make an independent ascent, which takes about two hours. You can visit the complex as part of an excursion, on a quad safari, arrive by taxi, bike or tuk-tuk. The attraction is open for free visits from 6am to 7pm. The complex began to be built with donations in 2002, and it is not finished yet. The observation deck offers charming views of the hills and the sea.

In the temple you can buy a tablet, thus making a donation; the monk will tie a knot for good luck. Dexterous monkeys feel completely safe on the territory, who can easily take away from a tourist who has looked in the blue distance, fruits and in general everything that they like.

Buddhist temples can be found everywhere on the island. The ancient Chalong temple was built in the 18th century. Here is a Buddhist shrine - a piece of Buddha bone. It is placed on top of a 60-meter pagoda. Buddhist temples sparkle brightly with gold and bright shades of red. At Sri Sunton Temple in the north, you can admire a large statue of a reclining Buddha. Ancient and modern temples are open for tourists to visit.
