LCD Olympus (Kirov) - a great solution for expanding housing

Does your family need to expand your living space? How not to make a mistake with the choice of the area and the best-priced housing? LCD "Olympus" (Kirov) proposes to solve this problem.





Kirov city

Most people choose an apartment to move in the same area where they live, where their parents or acquaintances live. This is convenient in many cases. Children do not have to change schools, and adults have the usual routes and shops. Residential complex "Olympus" from this point of view is very convenient. This is the acquisition of a new apartment, and the preservation of old habits.

LCD "Olympus" (Kirov) is conveniently located in one of the sleeping areas of the city. This has its advantages: the absence of industrial enterprises polluting the atmosphere, the ability to enjoy clean air and silence.





This sleeping area can only boast of greenery: a square, the Palace of Pioneers, a huge number of green spaces in the streets and courtyards. All this leads to unhurried walks after a busy day or on weekends with the whole family or in the silence of loneliness. Still good for those who like to play sports. Jogging in the shade of trees enhances mood and vitality.

District Infrastructure

Since the LCD Olympus (Kirov) is located almost in the heart of the city, the complex accordingly uses the entire infrastructure located around: kindergartens, schools, including the music school and the School of Arts, there are hospitals and clinics. Children can develop in different directions. To this end, the area provides many opportunities. Everyone will find a circle or section to their liking. And adults at this time will be able to watch the latest movies or enjoy a cup of coffee in coffee houses.





And so what is especially plentiful in this area, so it is shops for every taste and a purse - both huge hypermarkets, and small specialized shops.

Location

LCD Olympus (Kirov) is located in the central district of the city. Nearby lies a large road artery - Vorovsky street. To get to the center in the mornings, nothing prevents, but in the evening short traffic jams are possible.

Residential complex

LCD Olympus (the city of Kirov) offers new ten buildings of twenty-five and nine-story monolithic-frame buildings. In its size and scale, the complex leaves an unforgettable impression. From many points of the city, the buildings of the complex are visible. And, accordingly, the houses themselves serve as an excellent platform for viewing the panorama of the city. The most impressive vision is obtained at night, when the city shines and shimmers with lights. For the enjoyment of residents, the developer has provided panoramic glazing in the project. Some apartments boast windows all the way to the floor, and in others only up to three quarters of the wall.





The construction company LLC Stroysoyuz has been in the construction market for more than a year and has managed to earn the trust of residents. Other residential buildings built by him are also towering in the city. The same reliability awaits LCD Olympus (Kirov). Reviews about the developer are only positive.

Developer Offers

What does the complex offer? Here you will find apartments for your liking both single people and large families. Both small one-room apartments and huge spacious eighty-meter sites are being built.

The layout of the apartments is characterized by the greatest convenience and functionality. On the official website of the developer, you can even see the proposed options for arranging furniture, as well as inspect the apartment in reality. In addition, the developer provided for the construction on the floors of such necessary premises as pantries. For a fee, residents can buy them in their property and store all sorts of unnecessary things there, for example, bicycles, sledges.

For children in the yard provides for the construction of a children's developmental playground. Moreover, it is built not only for the smallest children, but also for those who are a little older. Kids will be able to play in the sandbox, and in the typewriter, ride on the carousel. Older children will develop strength and dexterity on rings, parallel bars, lianas, bridges and ropes.

And for very adult children (adolescents) a sports ground is provided. It is surrounded by a high fence. On its territory you can play futsal, basketball, work out on modern simulators. Even rain will not become an obstacle when playing sports, as the site is prudently equipped with a canopy.

It is not difficult to get an apartment in the Olympus residential complex. It is enough to contact the sales office of the developer. If it will be necessary to obtain borrowed funds from the bank in the form of a mortgage loan, the housing complex has been accredited by the largest banks in the city. In this regard, the loan application is considered as soon as possible. And this is despite the fact that the client will be provided with the most favorable conditions for obtaining a loan.
