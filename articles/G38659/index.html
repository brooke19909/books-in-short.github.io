Samsung Galaxy Note 4: model specifications, photos and reviews

The Galaxy Note lineup was initially positioned as a trial, experimental. Unexpectedly, even for Samsung itself, the result of this experiment showed that the line is in demand in the smartphone market. The second generation of the line was marked by the acquisition of the status of flagship devices. At the same time, the lineup is becoming the most real separate direction. The number of sales of precisely various Note models is growing.

One of the flagships is the Samsung Galaxy S. The other flagship is rightly considered Samsung Galaxy Note. The company said in 2013 that the largest percentage of technical innovations will be involved in the design of the Note range.

It is worth noting the fact that if at the beginning the Note line played the role of a kind of testing ground for various experiments, now it is a key product. As time goes on, the popularity of the Galaxy S is gradually fading, while the Galaxy Note continues to be in great demand. Perhaps the main trump card of this line, which has the buyer to purchase a product, is its filling, which provides excellent graphic characteristics. Samsung Galaxy Note 4, the characteristics of which will be given in this article, is one of the representatives of the line.

Samsung believes that the Note line should be the most expensive. In this article, we will consider the Samsung Galaxy Note 4 model, the design characteristic of which has many positive aspects.
