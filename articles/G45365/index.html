Voronezh, Admiralteyskaya Square. Ship on Admiralty Square, Voronezh

One of the most beautiful places in Voronezh is Admiralteyskaya Square. For many years, it has been a center of sports, youth and cultural recreation for citizens. It is with her that you need to start acquaintance with the city. The place where the construction of the Russian navy began, was Voronezh. Admiralty Square is its symbol and a favorite place for walks of citizens. A particularly active life goes on there in the summer, when people enjoy beautiful views of the reservoir, sit in cozy cafes or ride a boat.

History of Admiralty Square

For many years this place has been a favorite among citizens and visitors. The square was opened in 1996, on the day of the centenary of the Russian Navy. It is located on the right bank of Petrovskaya Embankment.



At the end of the 17th century, a shipyard was located in this place, there was the first Admiralty. It is because of this that the square received such a name. It has a rich history: the first Russian warship Goto Predestination was built on this site, and the symbol of the Navy, the St. Andrew's flag , was consecrated . Nearby was the Church of the Assumption of the Blessed Virgin Mary and the Virgin Mary, which is part of the Assumption Monastery. Various foreign masters settled near it in the 17th century, which is why this area was called the German settlement.

Area Description

At the entrance are air, or triumphal, arches. They are very beautiful and symbolize Russia's access to the sea. Columns connected by iron chains extend from the three arches in a semicircle. The square itself was designed by a famous architect. But by the 425th anniversary of the city, it underwent reconstruction. Now this place has the shape of a semicircle facing the convex part towards the right bank.



In the center of the square is a 28-meter raster column. It is adorned with the bow of the ships and is visible from afar. This square has now become a favorite place for recreation and walks with the townspeople. Benches, paths and lawns make it pleasant, and a magnificent view of the reservoir and the left bank attracts tourists who want to get to know the sights of which Voronezh is famous. Admiralteyskaya Square is one of the most famous. In addition, not far from it is an observation deck with a magnificent view of the city.

Ship on Admiralty Square

Voronezh is known worldwide as the cradle of the Russian fleet. It was here that the shipyards for building ships were located. And in order to perpetuate the memory of this, it was decided to build a copy of the ship of Peter's time. It took almost three years, and in the summer of 2014, on the Day of the Navy, the grand opening of the Goto Predestination ship took place. It is an exact copy of the first Russian ship built in 1700 in Voronezh. Now the ship is a museum where you can get acquainted with the everyday life of sailors of that time, with samples of clothes and weapons, with the arrangement of cabins and household items. Other attractions

In recent years, tourists are increasingly coming to Voronezh. Admiralty Square attracts them with its attractions. These include:

  • Rostral column - set in honor of the construction of ships. It is a 28-meter stella decorated with the bow of the ship.
  • The arch at the entrance symbolizes Russia's access to the sea as a naval power.
  • A unique architectural monument is located next to the square - the oldest temple in Voronezh - the Assumption Admiralty Church of the 17th century.
  • In summer, you can ride a river tram through the reservoir.
  • One of the attractions of the square is a cafe located on the ship Skedia.




Admiralteyskaya Square (Voronezh): events

The year 2014 was remarkable for the townspeople and for this place. July 27, on the Day of the Navy, the ship "Goto Predestination" was opened for visiting on the square. Now it is the most beautiful museum of the city. But not only because of this, many people gather in the square for all the holidays. Since 1996, when the Admiralteika was built by the day of the city, it has become the center of cultural, sports and youth life, which Voronezh is famous for. Admiralteyskaya Square on June 28 of every year blooms with colors, dances and sings - Youth Day is held there . Now it has become a tradition that for all the holidays in this place you can visit a concert or a disco, watch a firework or participate in contests. Many famous artists and musical groups performed at Admiralty Square, youth and sports festivals were held.
