What time of the year is the best vacation at the hotel?

To answer this question, we examined a number of reviews from hotel guests. We have revealed a certain pattern. Indeed, there is an important aspect that affects the activity of local holidaymakers. After all, the climate of the Land of the Pharaohs has its own peculiarities: it is unbearably hot in summer and windy in winter. Therefore, successfully (at the best time of the year), buying tours at the Faraana Reef Hotel, holiday-makers provide the maximum quality of relaxation. The optimal months to relax in the hotels of this resort are April, May and October. Moreover, even in these relatively non-hot months, vacationers should take with them sun protection cream level 30-50.



About hotel pools

The pools operating at the hotel complex deserve a separate description. There are 4 of them: three adults and one for children. The largest pool is located near the main building, it is multilevel, with artificial waves. At the pool there is an artificial waterfall and artificial geyser - a powerful jet of water.

The second pool is a smaller, Relax class. It is good for bathers, around it - a relaxed lounge environment. He is preferred by older spa guests.

The third pool is for active people, it has a very decent depth of 2.5 m. A diving tower rises above its water bowl. Above the center of this original pool is equipped with a jacuzzi, sheltered by a canopy.

In addition, the infrastructure of the "Farahana Reef" (Sharm el-Sheikh), due to its advantageous location, is naturally complemented by a general resort. On the coast, within walking distance from the hotel complex, on the shore of the neighboring bay of Naama Bay is the famous shopping center "Thousand and One Nights." Nearby is the famous El Mercato district of Sharm El Sheikh, as well as a dozen well-known club venues: Hard Rock Cafe, discos "Little Buddha", "Black Horse", etc.

Hotel housing

Buildings with a high ceiling on the first floor, with characteristic stylized arches and relatively narrow windows, niches, wrought iron grates. This design is aesthetic, simple, elegant and harmonious. The housing stock of the hotel complex is located in a two-story main building, sixty-four one-story cottages, ten two-story rooms and ten two-story villas.

The territory of this club hotel is quite large: 110 thousand m 2 . Diving

Ras Um al Sid Bay with its wonderful gorgon corals is one of the best diving spots in the world. The famous ocean explorer Jacques Cousteau believed that the underwater world of this bay, in its richness and uniqueness, takes pride of place in the top ten best in the world.

An interesting diving, which is written by divers - guests of the Faraana Reef Hotel, is promoted by the bottom relief of the bay, gradually lowering to a depth of 20 meters, which is extremely convenient for underwater excursions. Here is the famous wall of gorgon corals, and numerous underwater grottoes and caves.

The dream of any experienced diver is a night dive in the waters of the famous 60-meter coral pillar. The underwater world is diverse, rich and extremely beautiful.
