Mushroom costume do-it-yourself will sew the son and daughter mom

It is not necessary to buy a fancy dress, spending a lot of money on it. You can invent and make it yourself, for example, a mushroom costume. By the way, there are several different manufacturing options for this outfit.



Mushroom costume with a knitted hat

It will not be difficult for the craftswomen familiar with the crochet hook to make a creative beret hat in white peas on a white counter. And as a decoration on a white rim, you can start a voluminous application - a snail. And on the hat itself it is quite appropriate to place a bright butterfly or dragonfly, both knitted and purchased (brooch). The main part that makes up the mushroom costume, in addition to the hat, can consist of a white dress for a girl or a white pantsuit for both sexes. At the bottom of the apparel, it‚Äôs appropriate to sew an applique made of fabric, stick paper cut out or paint (there is such a technique) grass with flowers, berries and insects.



Mushroom costume with paper hat

This robe is the fastest to prepare. Indeed, the manufacture of hats made of thick paper will take no more than half an hour. On the paper, draw a circle, cut a segment out of it and glue the cone along the radii. Then you can color the hat as you wish: in red, brown, yellow (stick white circles on it if it is a fly agaric). A fringe should be made along the edge. It is necessary to glue the twine that will be tied under the chin.

How to sew a mushroom costume with a fabric hat?

For such an outfit, it is necessary to choose a bright fabric for making a hat. It‚Äôs good if the craftswoman has a red cloth in white peas at hand - you can sew a beautiful fly agaric costume. But a fabric of a different color is quite suitable, because mushrooms in the world are very different. First, you should make a pattern of paper, for example, from a newspaper. It looks like this:
1. A circle is drawn and cut, the diameter of which is equal to the diameter of the cap.
2. Folds in half.
3. 5 cm recede from the fold and draw a straight line parallel to it.
4. From the points of contact of the parallel line with the circle, they retreat in different directions 3-4 cm and connect them with an arc that touches the parallel line.
5. You should get a drawing that looks like a sample. Details should be cut along bold lines, and shaded areas should be cut. It turns out 3 parts, 2 of which are identical to each other.

Having applied the patterns to the fabric, the master carefully outlines them with dry soap or chalk, then cuts them out. Stitch the parts so that the strip is in the middle. This will be the top of the hat. The bottom is made in the form of a simple circle and sewn to the top, leaving a hole for stuffing. As a filler, you can use pieces of foam rubber, but it is best to take a synthetic winterizer - it is easier.



Costume collar

Such a detail of a suit as a collar looks original. It can be made from a highly starched fabric or kapron tape, collecting a strip on one side on a living thread and pulling it slightly. You get such a magnificent circular collar, similar to those worn in the past by aristocrats and nobles. You can make a magnificent collar out of paper by folding it with an accordion-fan and stretching a thread through one edge of the paper stack.

Honey mushrooms are funny guys

You can make a mushroom costume with your own hands under the name "Cheerful honey agaric". Then the robe itself and the hat will be made in beige, the top of the hat can be made darker. It is best to use a straight-cut tunic dress, but a trouser suit is also suitable. On the bottom of the dress or trousers you can sew small mushrooms, "kids", because honey agarics grow in groups, almost without meeting one by one. Cool huge gauntlets will complement a fancy dress.





But no matter what fungus the author of the fancy dress would choose as a character, for making the outfit he would need first of all imagination, in the second - diligence and patience, and in the third - some simple skills.
