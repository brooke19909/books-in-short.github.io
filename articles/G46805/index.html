USSD request as an alternative way

Almost all operators provide their customers with sets of ussd commands, with which you can quickly and without payment receive the required information. This is a special kind of request, which always starts with an asterisk and ends with a grid, followed by a call send button. After a few seconds, a message from the operator with the necessary information is displayed on the phone screen. So, if you are thinking how to check your number on Megafon for free and quickly, try typing the following commands:

  • For Moscow, the Volga region, the Far East and the Urals - the command * 205 # and press the send call button.

  • If your SIM card is registered in the Northwest, the command * 127 # will act for you.

  • For residents of the Caucasus, Megafon provides the USSD command - * 105 * 1 * 2 lattice and call send button.

  • If you are a subscriber from Central Russia and want to find out how to check your number on Megafon, simply dial * 105 * 2 * 0 # on the phone.

  • And finally. Residents of Siberia will have to use the command: * 105 * 1 * 6 and the lattice to get information about their number.
