How to make a rose: a guide to action

There are several ways to make flowers from notes, for some of them the origami technique is used. The proposed method is somewhat simpler, since not everyone can patiently and, most importantly, even fold a small bill into tiny figures.





So, to make a flower of money, in our case a rose, you will need banknotes of any value, preferably new ones, a cork from champagne or wine, several elastic bands, a knitting needle . As a stalk, you can use part of an artificial rose or wire wrapped in paper for decoration.



Method 1

We lay out the bills on the table, iron it if necessary. Using the knitting needles we twist the corners inward. Then we make a fold across the bill, we intercept it with wire. Similarly, we do 2 more parts, connect together. The preparation for the middle of the flower is done the same way, but the petals are twisted together until a cup is formed. We wrap together the ends of the wire with green paper, if desired, fix with glue.





Method 2

We turn bills with twisted ends in half, put on an elastic band. At an artificial rose, remove the cup with the petals, put a cork in its place. In the cork we make girdle grooves, grooves, we put on elastic bands with wound petals in several rows. This rose will look especially organic.
