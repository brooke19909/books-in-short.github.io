Waterpark "Acapulco". Opening hours and ticket prices

The water park is open from eleven in the morning until 22 from Tuesday to Friday. On Saturday, the schedule is slightly different - from 10 a.m. to 4 a.m. On Sunday, the water park is open from 10 to 22.

Children under four are free. Up to fourteen years of age, visits to the water park in Ryazan are allowed only with adults. Full-day visits for adults on weekdays - 1100 rubles, and on Saturday and Sunday - 1400.

A children's ticket (from four to 14 years) on weekends - one thousand rubles, on weekdays - eight hundred rubles.
