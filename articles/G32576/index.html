Let's save the happiest moments in life, or how to make an album for a newborn with your own hands

A newborn baby grows very fast. Literally every day you notice some changes in his appearance, behavior, development. In the first year, the child grows up and changes. Therefore, mom and dad try to capture all the most interesting moments in photographs. And it has already become a tradition to store pictures of the first year of life of a little man in a separate album. This item should be very beautiful, original and, of course, unique. Only an album for a newborn made with love can meet these requirements. We will provide you with interesting ideas and useful tips on its implementation.

To design pages, pick up thick paper or cardboard. Sheets can be made in different colors, decorated with appliqu√©s, stickers, and inscriptions. Binding

Doing an album for a newborn with your own hands, consider a reliable way to connect pages. There will be a lot of them, so the product will turn out to be voluminous. Mounting can be done as follows:

  1. Tie all the sheets with tape, previously piercing them with a hole punch.
  2. Insert the spring. It can be bought at a needlework store or removed from an old notebook. For this fastening, you first need to make holes on all sheets.
  3. Connect pages with rings. They can also be removed from the file folder.
