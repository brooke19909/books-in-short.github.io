Hotel "October", Novomoskovsk: description, reviews

Novomoskovsk - the second largest city in the Tula region, is a large economic and industrial center. There are many business trips here, so the issue of their placement is relevant. Hotel "October" in Novomoskovsk offers comfortable accommodation for visitors at an affordable cost.

Description

The train and bus station are just a ten minute drive away. Hotel "October" in Novomoskovsk has a two-star category, located within walking distance from a restaurant, supermarkets, post office, pharmacies, cultural and business center. The hotel occupies a five-story building and an elevator is available to access the upper floors. In a few minutes you will reach the city park and the Ice Palace sports complex.

Meals are organized in the hotel's own cafe. The modern conference room has a separate entrance. Guests can leave their car in a nearby guarded parking lot. A train and air ticket sale service is available for guests.

Free Wi-Fi is available throughout the hotel. The hotel has 105 rooms of various levels of comfort. Any form of payment. The address at which the Oktyabr Hotel is located: Moskovskaya St., 33, Novomoskovsk.

Accommodation

The hotel offers several categories of rooms:

  • single economy - 1750 rubles per day;
  • economy twin room with two single beds - 2500 rubles per day for two and 2350 rubles per person (photo below);
  • Suite with a double bed - 3300 rubles per day for two and 3150 rubles per person;
  • a studio with a double bed - 3500 rubles per day for two and 3350 rubles per person;
  • Suite - 3900 rubles per day for two and 3750 rubles per person.

The room rate is based on a continental breakfast. All rooms at the Oktyabr Hotel in Novomoskovsk are equipped with a refrigerator, a TV, a telephone, and a private bathroom. Superior rooms have a microwave and electric kettle.

Hotel "October" (Novomoskovsk): positive feedback from tourists

Guests share their impressions of their stay in the hotel reviews.

  1. Tourists praised the location of the hotel near the objects of urban infrastructure. Nearby there is a large parking lot, supermarkets.
  2. The staff is polite and friendly.
  3. The rooms are clean, there is a fridge and TV. The bathroom is equipped with toiletries.
  4. There is free internet access.
  5. The hotel has a good restaurant, they cook delicious, a large selection of dishes. The prices are affordable.
  6. The price includes breakfast, which is served in the dining room. It is convenient that the breakfast coupon can be used throughout the day, from seven in the morning to seven in the evening.

Hotel "October" (Novomoskovsk): negative reviews from tourists

Unfortunately, not all living conditions at the hotel suit guests.

  1. The smoking areas on each floor are so smoky that the smell of smoke is also present in the rooms.
  2. Internet is not working well.
  3. Guests believe that superior rooms should include tea or coffee bags.
  4. There is no soundproofing in the rooms - you can hear what is happening in the neighboring rooms and in the corridor.
  5. Breakfast is too modest, not always tasty.
  6. In general, guests believe that the prices for accommodation at the Oktyabr Hotel in Novomoskovsk are overstated.
