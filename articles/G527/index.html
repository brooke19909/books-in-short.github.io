Holidays in the Dominican Republic in November: features, weather, reviews

When choosing a time for vacation, many stop in November. Firstly, after the end of the velvet season, during the lull period, prices for many tours drop significantly, giving an excellent opportunity to save. Secondly, you don‚Äôt feel like staying at home on a cloudy autumn, it is better to visit hot countries and get an unforgettable experience. A great place to relax in November is the Dominican Republic.





Weather

Dominican Republic is a republic located in the east of the island of Haiti, the climate is tropical, so on average the thermometer does not fall below + 20 ¬∞ C throughout the year. Weather in Dominican Republic in November will please guests from Russia: during the day the temperature is around + 27-28 ¬∞ , and at night + 23-24 ¬∞ . Moreover, almost all the time it is clear and sunny.

However, November is a time when storms and strong winds are possible, especially in the first weeks of the month. But gradually the rains and winds come to naught, making it possible to enjoy all kinds of beach holidays. The sea is quite warm, during the day the water temperature is about + 28 ¬∞ C, so you can safely visit the beaches. Fans of outdoor activities can learn the basics of windsurfing in Caberete, which is only 20 km from Puerto Plata Airport. The sand is amazingly clean, and the wind creates perfect waves. It is best for beginners to start training in the morning when the sea is still quiet and there are practically no people on the beach. In the evening, professionals are already having fun, striking the imagination of the audience with their risky tricks during a powerful breeze.

Precipitation in the last autumn month most often falls in the afternoon, at about 14-00. In the evening and in the morning, the risk of rain is almost zero. Thunderstorms are possible around 17 hours local time.

In general, the weather in the Dominican Republic in November more than has a good rest as a whole family, and a cheerful friendly company.

Select date

To make your vacation in the Dominican Republic in November unforgettable, you can schedule a trip to this country for a holiday or carnival. What holidays are celebrated in this republic?

  • November 1-2 - All Saints Day, a noisy costumed performance during which young people put on the most amazing outfits of monsters. Parties are also held.
  • November 6 - Day of the Dominican Constitution.
  • November 30 - St. Andrew's Day, a colorful carnival is being organized on this occasion in the small village of Boca Chica.
  • Also this month, from the 18th to the 22nd, a flower festival is held in the Santo Domingo National Botanical Garden. Here you can not only look at the most beautiful plants from around the world, but also attend master classes, learn how to grow orchids and bonsai trees.

Thanks to such an abundance of diverse events, a vacation in the Dominican Republic in November promises to be not only memorable, but also informative.





Where to go?

Among the most common cities of the republic, which are centers of tourism, it is worth mentioning such as:

  • Boca Chica.
  • Santo Domingo.
  • Santiago.
  • Punta Cana.
  • La Romana.
  • Puerto Plata.

Consider where it is better to relax in the Dominican Republic in November.

Boca Chica is a good option for a trip with the whole family. There are several comfortable hotels here, the beach has fine white sand, and even the kids can swim safely in the lagoon protected from the ocean by coral reefs.

Punta Cana is one of the most popular resorts, it is warm here, the temperature is + 23-29 ¬∞ . Holidays can be very diverse: you can visit the marinarium and see with your own eyes the most popular marine inhabitants in the Dominican Republic - stingrays and sharks. Fans of outdoor activities can go on a safari or fishing trip on many islands. Manati Park is a wonderful natural attraction, where rare representatives of local flora and fauna are represented in abundance, here you can see unusual exhibits and get acquainted with the culture of Aboriginal people.

Santo Domingo is rich in cultural attractions, there are many cathedrals and mansions, buried in verdure palm trees and bright colors of tropical flowers. The average temperature in November is + 21-31 ¬∞ .





Things to do?

If you choose November for your holiday in the Dominican Republic, you should plan your trip in advance. This month is considered the most suitable for all types of beach holidays, since the scorching heat is already behind us, and the cold weather is still far away. That is why in the morning and in the afternoon you can lie on the white sand, try to catch a wave, go in for snorkeling or diving. Fans of sporting events can visit Punta Cana and take part or just watch the golf tournament.

November is not the best place to visit monuments and cultural attractions, as the tour may be ruined due to rains in the evening. However, even the most severe rainfall does not hurt to enjoy the features of local cuisine or attend a noisy party in a nightclub.





What to try?

Holidays in the Dominican Republic in November will be truly unforgettable if you give yourself the opportunity to enjoy the masterpieces of local cuisine. Tourists who have already visited the republic are advised to try dishes such as:

  • "La Bandera" - meat stew with fried bananas. Its peculiarity is a unique combination of several types of meat, vegetables and a special variety of bananas.
  • "Monfongo" is a baked scar.
  • "Pastelon de Platanos" - a unique banana pie with minced meat.
  • "Mangu" - stewed bananas with bacon and cheese.

From alcohol, you should definitely try the local beer of the sorts Seniza and President. The Pru drink made from fermented juice of the plant of the same name also has a unique taste. The Dominican Republic is famous for its rum, which can be consumed in pure form or as part of popular cocktails.





Traveler opinions

Dreaming of a good vacation in autumn often stop at the Dominican Republic in November. Reviews from tourists say that this is a great place to travel. Here the warm weather reigns, conducive to a beach holiday, you can buy real cigars, aromatic coffee, rum, an abundance of fruit. Guests of the country note that this autumn month they were able to enjoy the view of amazing tropical animals, visit many excursions. There are no problems with hotels, so you can choose a comfortable place based on financial capabilities.

Tours to the Dominican Republic in November are diverse, but the most popular of them is to Punto Canu for 7 nights. La Romana, Cap Cana, Bayahibe are also in demand, but here the cost of rest will be somewhat more expensive. However, experienced travelers categorically do not recommend choosing Samanu and Puerto Plata for a vacation in the Dominican Republic in November, since in these resorts the active precipitation begins.

Travelers note that a vacation in the Dominican Republic in November is a great way to get rid of gray cloudy autumn days and briefly immerse yourself in a fairy tale.
