To visit the principality, you will need a multivisa of the Schengen zone. Why is it necessary to have multiple entry permits? Because in the dwarf princedom there is no airport. And the station, by the way, too. You will have to get on buses: either from France, or from Spain.

From Russia it is easiest to fly to Barcelona. There are direct buses from the capital of Catalonia to the Principality of Andorra. The country's ski resorts are also accessible from France. In fact, the whole principality fits in one narrow mountain gorge, which gradually descends to the Spanish city of Sioux d'Urhel. In Andorra there are two ski areas, united by common ski passes.

The first of them - Grand Valira, is named after the main river of the Principality of Andorra. The ski resorts located in this ski area are Grau Roych, Pas de la Casa, El Tarter and Soldeu. Vallnord combines Arkalis and Pal-Arinsal. Of course, this is a very conditional division.
