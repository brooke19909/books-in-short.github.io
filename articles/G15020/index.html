Hotel "Deauville", Anapa: reviews and photos of tourists

Holidays in Russia on the shores of the affectionate and welcoming Black Sea are an incomparable pleasure. Many people prefer to spend their holidays here and do not chase luxury hotels in any Turkey or Egypt. Of course, the demand for the Black Sea coast of Russia increased even after restrictions on entry into these countries were introduced. But even without this ban, resorts of the Krasnodar Territory are gaining more and more popularity every year and attract an increasing number of tourists.

Those who dream of lying on a golden sand, Anapa beaches are perfect . This is a great place to relax primarily with children, because here the entrance to the sea is very smooth, and the sandy beach is much safer for kids than pebble.

Many people think that in Anapa it is impossible to find a hotel that could be compared with foreign hotels operating on an all-inclusive basis in its service. However, they are mistaken. After all, here is a wonderful hotel "Deauville Hotel & SPA 5 *", reviews of which often contain comparisons with hotels in Turkey. In it, contrary to popular belief, the beloved by many, the all-inclusive system is used, so there are always a lot of vacationers.

Slice of Turkey in Anapa

You can now enjoy the beautiful Black Sea and get the high-class service that many have heard about from the resorts of Turkey and Egypt without leaving the borders of the Russian Federation. Due to the appearance of the Deauville hotel (Anapa), the photos of which perfectly reflect all its sophistication, the citizens of the country do not need to think about obtaining foreign passports and issuing visas. The hotel operates under the auspices of the management company Alean Hotel Group, which includes three more magnificent hotels on the Black Sea coast.





Hotel Deauville (Anapa) opened its doors for lovers of beach holidays in early June 2013. The hotel owes its beautiful name to the French resort of Deauville, which is located in Normandy, at a distance of 200 km from Paris.

Thanks to its stunning decoration and amazing service, Hotel & SPA Deauville in Anapa even received a prestigious award in 2014. It was then that the hotel took an honorable second place in the category "Best Hotel 5 *", participating in a prestigious award among the resorts of the Krasnodar Territory under the name "Resort Olympus".

Of course, there are still magnificent all-inclusive complexes on the Russian Black Sea coast, as well as the Deauville hotel. Sochi, for example, can also offer similar accommodation options, but fans of lying on a warm sand choose Anapa.

What awaits guests at the hotel?

The feeling that you are in the territory of a magnificent and fashionable resort does not leave anyone who comes to rest at the Deauville hotel (Anapa). Photos of tourists and hotel employees only confirm these impressions, because here both architecture and interiors are made to the highest standards.

The entire territory of the hotel resembles a real park, in which residential cottages with various numbers of floors are comfortably located. They are combined with each other in buildings, between which there are open galleries.





The entire surrounding area is fenced and very well-groomed, and here and there, guests of the Deauville Hotel & SPA 5 * (Anapa) can enjoy beautiful landscape designs. There are a lot of trees, in the shade of which you can hide from the scorching summer sun on specially designed benches for this. Excellent flower beds and green lawns are able to relax any guest with their views, and specially created recreation areas will allow you to enjoy all this beauty and rest surrounded by nature at any time.

Several pools, various playgrounds and daily animation will not let any guest get bored here. And nobody will have to go hungry on the territory either, because the bars and restaurants of the hotel are ready to feed tourists delicious dishes throughout the day.

Vacationers who visited the Deauville Hotel & SPA complex leave reviews about its territory most often admired. The abundance of sculptures of various animals and gnomes, all kinds of benches and beautiful lanterns give many guests the feeling that they seemed to be in some quiet and beautiful town in Europe. Architectural solutions also win the hearts of all vacationers.





How to find the hotel "Deauville"?

To get to this magnificent paradise on the Black Sea, you need to remember its address. Hotel & SPA Deauville in Anapa is located on Pionersky Prospekt, right on the banks of the Anapka River and not far from Lake Chembur. The exact address of the hotel: Anapa city, Simferopol highway, 26.

Around the hotel there are other holiday homes, pensions and hotels. Not far from the place where the Deauville hotel stands, is the Anapa dolphinarium called Nemo, the Golden Beach water park and a large children's park. A little further there is Park them. 30th anniversary of the Victory and the port of the city. Also located here are the "Russian Gate" - the surviving part of the Ottoman fortress, built in the XVIII century. So if suddenly vacationers at the hotel want to change the situation, then they will have the opportunity to visit very interesting places.

We go on vacation to Deauville

Knowing the exact address of the place where you are going to spend your vacation, you can easily get to it in any convenient way. You can comfortably get here by taxi, by minibus or by personal vehicle.





Guests arriving in Anapa at the bus station or at the city railway station can take a minibus number 100. If tourists were in Anapa with the help of an airplane, then they will need to get to the bus station by minibus No. 113, and then transfer to another route. You need to exit by the sign with the inscription "Deauville Hotel & SPA 5 *".

Where do guests of the Deauville hotel live?

Special cottages are designed to accommodate guests in this club hotel, which are several pieces combined into buildings consisting of a different number of floors. All these residential buildings are made in the Norman style, which creates a unique atmosphere, from which many guests who came to rest at the Hotel & SPA Deauville in Anapa are delighted. All rooms have fresh and soft towels, linens, as well as all the necessary perfumes, which are usually provided in hotels of this class.

Guests who came to rest in Anapa can comfortably stay in one of the 422 comfortable rooms that are available in the Deauville Hotel & SPA complex. The apartment reviews are listed below.
