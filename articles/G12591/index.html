Hotel Sol Parque San Antonio 4 * (Spain, Canary Islands): overview, description and reviews

A cozy corner for a comfortable stay in the Canary Islands is the resort Sol Parque San Antonio. Everything is there to satisfy even the most demanding travelers.





Hotel location

In the middle of a picturesque park area with tropical palm trees and exotic flowers, Sol Parque San Antonio is located. Tenerife, Puerto de la Cruz, Caretera de la Arenas, 38400 - the address of this resort institution. Puerto de la Cruz Municipal Beach is 2 km away (it takes about 20 minutes to reach).

Distance to attractions

Travelers who want to get acquainted with the sights of Spain should stay at the Sol Parque San Antonio 4 * hotel. Tenerife is rich in interesting natural and cultural sites. The main tourist attractions are as follows:

  • Taoro National Park (0.1 km);
  • All Saints Church (1 km);
  • Mrtianez shopping center (1.1 km);
  • Hardin Beach (1.2 km);
  • artificial lakes complex (1.4 km);
  • Botanical Garden (1.4 km);
  • Loro Park (1.7 km);
  • Pueblo Chico Park (1.7 km);
  • Icod de la Vinos (17 km);
  • Mount Teide (35 km).

Guest apartments

Hotel Sol Parque San Antonio consists of 9 four-story buildings, which in total include more than 500 comfortable rooms. The following room categories are available for guests:

  • Standard rooms are 30 square meters. m. One and a half beds and a folding sofa can accommodate a total of 3 guests. It is worth noting that in some rooms there are no balconies (this option will cost a little cheaper).
  • Superior is a spacious apartment of 48 square meters. m. The bedroom area is represented by a large bed, as well as a cosmetic table. Through a decorative partition there is a living room with upholstered furniture and a bedside table with a TV. A feature is the presence of a private terrace, which has direct access to the pool.




Room Amenities

The administration of the Sol Parque San Antonio resort ensures that guests enjoy comfort and a sense of homeliness during their stay. For this purpose, the resort complex rooms are equipped with the following amenities:

  • own balcony or spacious terrace with garden furniture (provides views of the pool or tropical garden);
  • Radio broadcast by local music stations
  • a bathroom with all necessary plumbing, as well as a set of bath accessories;
  • electric hairdryer in the bathroom;
  • central air conditioning system;
  • heating for relaxing in the cold season;
  • satellite television with a large package of entertainment channels (there are Russian-language programs);
  • landline telephone with the ability to make international calls;
  • mini-bar (filled for an additional fee, and can also be used as a refrigerator;
  • safe with electronic combination lock.




Infrastructure facilities

Everything necessary for a good rest provides its guests with the resort Sol Parque San Antonio. On the territory you can use the following facilities:

  • restaurants and bars that provide guests with drinks, snacks and nutritious meals;
  • in the business center, guests can use office equipment;
  • own garden with lots of green spaces;
  • free access to Wi-Fi signal in public places;
  • a left-luggage office where guests can hand over their luggage;
  • a reception desk, the staff of which you can get advice at any time of the day;
  • a conference room with all the necessary equipment for holding business meetings and meetings;
  • round-the-clock currency exchange;
  • vending machines for drinks and chocolate bars;
  • smoking places on the territory and in the premises;
  • banquet room for celebrations, which can be designed at your discretion;
  • there is free public parking near the hotel;
  • elevator, which comfortably takes guests to the upper floors of the residential building.




Catering establishments

A well-fed guest is a happy guest. Knowing this rule, the administration of the Sol Parque San Antonio 4 * hotel is paying close attention to the meals of guests. The following facilities work at the hotel:

  • In the main restaurant, guests are provided with all-inclusive or half board meals. Three times a day a buffet is served for guests, which includes traditional Spanish as well as international dishes.
  • The grill restaurant is open air. In the evenings, vegetables, meat and various seafood are cooked on the grill. The tables are located in the middle of a luxurious tropical garden next to a well-kept blue pool.
  • There is a bar by the pool, which provides guests with quality alcoholic and refreshing drinks. You can also try cocktails here (the bartender can make them on his own or according to your recipe). At lunchtime, guests can eat fast food and light salads.




Fun and relaxation

Everything you need for an active and vibrant holiday is at the Sol Parque San Antonio. For the organization of leisure guests here are provided such features:

  • an outdoor swimming pool located next to the bar;
  • outdoor terrace with sunbeds and parasols;
  • Jacuzzi with hydromassage of different intensity;
  • tables and other sports equipment for playing ping pong;
  • billiard room;
  • massage parlor;
  • organization of excursions to popular resorts;
  • in the evenings, live instrumental music plays in the lobby area, and guest singers also perform;
  • in the immediate vicinity of the hotel there is a golf course;
  • water sports can be enjoyed on the beach;
  • own gym with modern simulators and other equipment;
  • cycling in the surroundings;
  • a small cinema room with a plasma panel, comfortable sofas and a large collection of films on discs;
  • own casino where guests can try their luck and win a large amount of money;
  • indoor pool, which operates in the cold season;
  • tanning bed in cloudy weather.

Services for guests

High-quality services are provided to their guests by employees of Sol Parque San Antonio 4 *. The following service is available to guests:

  • food delivery from the restaurant to the apartments;
  • provision of beach towels;
  • individual menu for children, vegetarians, as well as for medical reasons;
  • from Monday to Friday every hour a free bus runs to the volcanic beach;
  • daily cleaning;
  • every morning in the hotel lobby you can read the latest local newspaper;
  • washing and ironing of personal belongings of guests by the efforts of laundry and dry cleaning;
  • organization and conduct of business and solemn events;
  • Transfer from the international airport, as well as in the opposite direction;
  • vehicle rental.

Additional Information

It is worthwhile to familiarize yourself with the rules of accommodation in a resort such as Sol Parque San Antonio. It will be useful for travelers to know the following:

  • check-in and check-out are carried out at check-out hours, which, according to international standards, are set at 14:00 and at noon, respectively;
  • after booking, guests are required to make an advance payment in the amount of the full cost of the stay;
  • no extra beds or cots allowed in the rooms;
  • hotel rules exclude the possibility of staying with pets;
  • for corporate bookings of more than 9 rooms, an additional fee will be charged as a guarantee;
  • newlyweds in this hotel receive a bonus in the form of a romantic room decoration, as well as a bottle of champagne with fruits;
  • guests who during their stay at the hotel celebrate their birthday are entitled to receive a birthday cake;
  • subject to cancellation of the reservation no later than 2 days before the expected date of arrival, penalties are not provided;
  • in case cancellation is made later than 2 days before check-in, the entire amount of prepayment will be charged as a fine;
  • the hotel accepts credit cards.




Positive reviews

Travelers' opinions perfectly illustrate the conditions of stay and relaxation at the Sol Parque San Antonio Resort. Reviews note such positive points:

  • good food with a huge assortment of quality and delicious dishes;
  • a lot of greenery in the territory;
  • the rooms have excellent sound insulation, and therefore guests will not be disturbed by street sounds or voices from neighboring rooms;
  • next to each plant in the territory there are information plates indicating the name and main features;
  • during dinner always serves dishes prepared on the grill;
  • the restaurant has a large selection of fresh fruits;
  • during lunch, guests are served delicious ice cream;
  • polite and helpful workers who, even despite the language barrier, do their best to understand the guests and take into account all their wishes and comments;
  • next to the hotel there is a large forest park where you can take a walk and breathe fresh air;
  • Considering that the hotel is located on a hill, guests are provided with beautiful views (but this causes certain inconvenience if you return from the city or from the beach);
  • hotel staff is constantly at work (they clean, clean, rub everything), and therefore it is very clean in the territory;
  • they take good care of the pool, and therefore it is pleasant to swim in it; quiet and calm atmosphere in the territory;
  • many parrots of various species live on the territory, which are very interesting to watch.




Negative reviews

If you want to get an objective picture, do not neglect the negative comments about the Sol Parque San Antonio 4 * hotel. The reviews contain information about such shortcomings of the hotel:

  • disgusting quality of room cleaning - the floors do not wash, and the dust is not always wiped;
  • old uncomfortable pillows with lumps (and due to the fact that they are feather, allergies can occur);
  • Wireless Internet in the room practically does not catch;
  • in the rooms and in all other rooms of the hotel there is a very old atmosphere, which has long been in need of repair;
  • cramped rooms, when moving around which you will constantly beat your feet on furniture;
  • Considering that the hotel has very few vacationers from the CIS countries, the staff practically does not understand Russian;
  • the TV shows poorly (the image is very blurry, and the sound supply is constantly interrupted);
  • during dinner, all drinks in the restaurant are available only at an additional cost (even ordinary drinking water);
  • although the hotel is non-smoking, the smell of tobacco smoke is everywhere;
  • even in cool weather, the pool does not turn on the heating;
  • the rooms are not comfortable enough, which is associated with an ill-conceived interior.

General impression

Hotel Sol Parque San Antonio 4 * (Tenerife) makes a rather pleasant impression on travelers. Its main advantage is a convenient location in a green park area. Also worthy of attention is good nutrition and quality service. If we talk about the shortcomings, then this is certainly an outdated situation, which is associated with the fact that the hotel has not had a major overhaul or redecoration for a long time.
