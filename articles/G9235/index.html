Port Ghalib Resort 5 *, Marsa Alam: hotel reviews and photos

If you prefer a comfortable stay with high-class service and plan to visit the Egyptian resort of Marsa Alam, Port Ghalib Resort 5 * may be a very suitable option for you.





Location

This five-star hotel is located in the Egyptian resort of Marsa Alam, on the Red Sea. The nearest airport is five kilometers from the hotel. However, some tourists arrive here through Hurghada's air harbor, which is 210 kilometers away. So if you do not want to spend a lot of time on the road to the hotel, then if possible choose a flight to Marsa Alam, and not to Hurghada. In this case, you will save about two hours.





Egypt, Marsa Alam, Port Ghalib Resort 5 *: general information, photo

This hotel was built in 2007. It used to be called the Crown Plaza Sahara Oasis Port Ghalib. Today, he is part of the large Red Sea Hotels chain, whose hotels are located both in Marsa Alam, and in Hurghada, and in Sharm El Sheikh.

The hotel complex has an artificial lagoon with sea water. Its depth does not exceed one meter, so that you can comfortably spend time with children. The hotel also has its own large sandy beach. Not far from the coast there is a coral reef densely populated by interesting marine inhabitants.

Maids renew linen and towels several times a week.

Looking ahead a bit, we note that most of our compatriots were very pleased with their choice. In their opinion, the hotel corresponds to its price and category, and they are ready to recommend it to all friends and relatives. But we learn about the details in more detail.

Judging by the comments of the guests, the apartments offered by him at the Port Ghalib Resort Hotel everyone were very pleased. So, the rooms here, according to our compatriots, are very spacious, bright, clean, pleasantly decorated and comfortable. Furniture and appliances are in excellent condition. Especially tourists allocate large and very comfortable beds. Also, according to the guests, if you are arriving on vacation with young children, then inform the guest manager in advance and a playpen or extra bed will be installed upon arrival at your apartment. This service is free. All rooms have large furnished balconies or terraces. Very pleased with the tourists were soundproofed in the apartment. So, with the windows closed, you will not be disturbed by any noise from the territory. In addition, guests note that they almost did not hear their neighbors. I must say that this state of affairs is not often found in hotels in Egypt.

For the work of the maids at the Port Ghalib Resort 5 * hotel (Marsa Alam, Egypt), vacationers had no complaints. Also every day from five to seven in the evening you will be offered to exchange beach towels. Territory, location

As for the location of this hotel, it should be borne in mind that it is located at a distance from major cities. Therefore, there is nowhere to go especially outside. However, this is offset by the large, beautiful and green territory of the hotel complex. By the way, the territory here is common for Port Ghalib Resort and two more hotels included in the same chain - Siva Port Ghalib and Palace Port Ghalib. So there is always where to take a walk, have a good time and take beautiful photos to commemorate your vacation in Egypt.

the Internet

As a minus Port Ghalib Resort 5 *, some guests note the lack of Wi-Fi in the rooms. So, wireless Internet access in the hotel can be used only in the lobby near the reception. This service is free. The connection speed here, according to our compatriots, is quite high. If you want to always be able to be online, then experienced travelers are advised to purchase a SIM card of a local mobile operator with a prepaid Internet package. It will cost about $ 15. You can buy a card right at the airport after arrival, or in the city during a walk.

Check in, departure

Many tourists who have been to Egypt more than once know from their own experience that quite often hotel administrators at reception try to delay arrival time as much as possible, hinting that this process can be accelerated for an additional fee (about $ 20). Moreover, this situation is typical for hotels of various categories, including five-star ones. As for the ‚ÄúPort Galib Resort‚Äù, our compatriots here were pleasantly surprised by the lack of such an attitude on the part of the staff. So, according to them, upon arrival they were offered to sit in comfortable chairs in the lobby next to the reception and refresh themselves with a cold drink. Within 10-15 minutes, the administrator issued the guests an envelope in which there were bracelets, room keys and a memo for guests. After that, the porter helped bring luggage to the apartment. So no matter what time of day you would arrive at this hotel, you will not have any need to wait for the checkout time or pay for accelerated settlement.

As for the day of departure, the occupied room should be vacated before 12 noon. At the same time, you can extend your stay in the apartment for an additional fee or hand over the keys, leave your things in the luggage room and continue to relax while waiting for a transfer to the airport. Indeed, until the last minute of their stay at the Port Galib Resort, guests have the right to use the entire infrastructure of the hotel.

Sea

Most tourists from different countries, as you know, come to Egypt, counting on a magnificent beach holiday on the shores of the picturesque Red Sea. According to our compatriots, the Hotel Port Ghalib (Marsa Alam) in this regard will not disappoint its guests. So, the beach of the hotel is very large and well equipped. It can be used by guests of two neighboring hotels, but there is enough space for everyone here and always. There are two piers on the beach, from where it is convenient to descend the stairs immediately to the depths. You can also enter the sea from the shore. However, in this case it is necessary to have special shoes, as you can injure yourself on corals. Near the coast is a large and very picturesque coral reef, inhabited by a variety of marine life. So, tourists note that in addition to a huge variety of fish, they happened to see a small shark, sea turtles, stingrays, moray eels, crabs and other amazing creatures here. Therefore, when collecting a suitcase for a trip on vacation, do not forget to grab a mask for snorkeling and flippers. But all swimming accessories can be purchased at the hotel. One thing is for sure: every hotel guest will be satisfied.
