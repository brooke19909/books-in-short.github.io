Charles Wayne: who is he?

Although the golden age of piracy ended more than 250 years ago, today the stories of marine adventurers are no less interesting than in the time of Robert Stevenson. And if then they inspired writers to create adventure novels, today they make films and develop computer games about brave filibusters. For example, the pirate Charles Wayne became both the hero of the series and was embodied in the virtual world of Assassins Creed 4.





Who is he?

The question is quite logical, because I want to know who the real sea robber Charles Wayne was and how he deserved such attention to his person.

So, in those days, the documents were not bad, so the date of birth of this person is unknown. It is believed that this happened around 1680. About his childhood and how he outlawed himself, no information was also preserved.

"Career"

The inhabitants of the New World spoke of Charles Wayne for the first time in 1716, after he and his comrades attacked Spanish ships, which were engaged in lifting silver from their galleons, which had sunk off the coast shortly before this during a terrible hurricane. After a brutal slaughter organized by pirates, sailors from different countries began to feel horror when the brigantine “Ranger” appeared on the horizon, which became the flagship of the robber.

Charles Wayne knew how to make friends and choose companions, and the famous pirates Jack Rackham and Edward England were his quartermasters at different times. The unquestioning submission of such notorious thugs only further increased the authority of the brigantine captain “Ranger” among adventurers of all stripes who fled to the colony, fleeing the rope waiting for them at home.

When during the so-called War of the Spanish Succession, the English fleet and its command were forced to leave their base and fort in New Providence in the Bahamas, it was occupied by sea robbers. One of the most influential among them was Wayne. For several years, his team committed bloody attacks on merchant ships and residents of coastal cities. After three years of a successful “career,” the pirate was captured. The court was adjourned for various reasons, but in 1721 he was nevertheless executed by hanging at Gallows Point in Jamaica.





Character Charles Wayne: Black Sails

The series premiered on January 25, 2014, and it continues to be shown in many countries to this day. Its plot is, in a sense, a prequel to Robert Stevenson's famous work Treasure Island. Charles Wayne is represented there in the image of the captain and steward of the fort on the island of New Providence.

The role of a pirate was played by American actor Zack McGone. Many viewers are crazy about the image of the villain created by him, whom the fair sex adores.

In the track record of the actor, the main and secondary roles in the series are mainly listed. In addition to working in Black Sails, praise was given to critics for his play in the soap opera Shameless.





The plot of the series

Events in the pirate television saga "Black Sails" takes place on the island of New Providence in 1715.

After the departure of the British, he turned into a base of sea robbers of all stripes. From time to time, the daughter of the important colonial official Eleanor Guthrie arrives there, who buys stolen goods from pirates for subsequent resale.

The most influential person in New Providence is Captain Flint. He is distinguished by cruelty and cunning, as well as the ability to weave intrigue. Together with Eleanor, the pirate is developing a plan to create an independent state on the island that is not subject to the British Empire. To do this, Flint needs to capture the Spanish galleon carrying innumerable treasures. The other captains of Fort New Providence, including Charles Wayne, help him, and sometimes even enter into a cruel confrontation with him. He is not inclined to always be on the sidelines, therefore he is waiting for an opportunity to declare himself.

According to the authors of the picture, some episodes of the series were shot on the basis of the real "exploits" of Flint, Wayne and the like. True, they were somewhat embellished and added special effects. By the way, the work of those members of the Black Sails crew who were involved in ensuring the entertainment of the series did not go unnoticed by specialists. In particular, the film received four nominations for an Emmy and won in two of them: for outstanding visual effects of the second plan, as well as for excellent sound editing.





Virtual Hero Charles Wayne

Assassins Creed 4 is a computer game developed by Ubisoft Montreal. One of her heroes is none other than the pirate Wayne. Events in the game take place in the early 18th century in the West Indies. In the story, the pirate Charles Wayne is captured by the British, what will happen to him next - depends on the player who selects him as a member of his team.

The presentation of the game took place on February 27, 2013.
