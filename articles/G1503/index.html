The origin of the bay is connected with the beautiful legend of the dragon. The monster lived in the mountains for a long time, until suddenly it became furious. Where is the bay

The main natural attraction of Vietnam is located in the north-east of the country. The city of the same name belongs to the province of Nuangnin. Its area is one and a half thousand square kilometers. And over the whole bay in a chaotic order, more than one thousand six hundred islets and simply rocks are scattered. Some have such a bizarre shape that when you look at them it seems like you are Alice, who has fallen into the country of fairy tales. If you swim to the islands on a boat, you will see numerous grottoes and caves - with stalactites and stalagmites. There are also deserted beaches, jungle and other beauty. In a word, paradise. And it is recommended to visit here for everyone who came to this country of Southeast Asia.



When is the best time to visit Halong

This landmark of unearthly beauty never lacks a party. And the residents of the city of the same name profit from this, which, with the exception of the bay, is nothing interesting in terms of tourism. But still do not forget that this region is North Vietnam. Halong in winter is often wrapped in fog and watered with drizzling rain. This off-season lasts from February to early April. And although the sea does not storm, visibility is rather poor. Yes, and windy, damp and slightly cold weather does not have to admire the beauty. And the rest of the year, the bay appears to the gaze of amazed travelers from around the world in all its irresistible charm. If you want to combine the contemplation of natural beauties with swimming, of course, it is good to choose the hot summer months. But then there is a high probability of rain. The high season in these parts are the periods April-May and September-October.



Vietnam, Halong Bay: how to get there

Only a hundred and seventy kilometers away from the capital of the country. Therefore, you can easily go here for a day. Intercity buses run from Hanoi to Halong City. They depart from Mai Dinh. There is an opportunity to leave by train, both regular and express trains. Another interesting option is to buy a one-day excursion to Halong Bay. You will be quickly delivered to your place in comfortable minivans and taken on a tourist boat to the large island of Katba. The tour lasts more than six hours and includes a visit to the iconic sights of the bay. Independent tourists themselves need to reserve a place in the boat. A ticket will cost one hundred thirty thousand dong. Entrance to the national park is included in this price. A taxi from Hanoi will cost you fifty dollars.



Day trip to the fabulous bay from other resort cities

Many tourists dream of seeing with their own eyes the marvelous Halong Bay (Vietnam). Getting to him from Hanoi is not very difficult. Indeed, modern modes of transport will help to overcome the distance of one hundred and seventy kilometers in a couple of hours. But is it possible to visit the bay in one day, coming from other resorts in Vietnam? If you are resting in the north of the country, in Haiphong or Sap, then getting to the city of Halong is also not difficult. You may not even need to go to Hanoi for this. But is it possible to visit the bay from the more southern resorts of the country? From the island of Fukuok or the city of Ho Chi Minh City this is unlikely to succeed. At least one day. Is Halong Bay (Vietnam) accessible from Nha Trang for tourists? The local bureaus undertake to organize excursions to the fabulous bay for 450 US dollars per person. But often they return the money, because not enough people have gathered for the formation of the group. For a hundred dollars, you can go to Halong on your own. But for this, you first need to get to Hanoi. This is easy to do on board a Vietnam Airlines, JetStar or VietJet aircraft. And from there you can easily reach the coveted bay by train or bus.

Other excursions

You do not need a boat to get to the nearby islet of Tuan Chau. After all, a bridge with a length of one kilometer connects it to the mainland. On the island there is a dolphinarium and several hotels - both expensive and simple guesthouses. A regular tour involves a two-hour cruise on the bay on a simple boat with a landing on one of the beaches of Katba. Halong may interest Russian tourists arriving in Vietnam on the island of Titova. The famous Soviet cosmonaut visited here in the 70s, and now one of the rocks bears his name. The islands of the bay abound in caves and grottoes, where you can penetrate directly by boat. Katba is famous for its pristine jungle and mangroves along the shores.



What to bring from Halong

This bay is famous throughout Vietnam. Halong is captured on advertising posters about the country, replicated on a variety of postcards, magnets, mugs and T-shirts. Therefore, you will be provided with souvenirs. As a reminder of a beautiful legend telling about the emergence of Halong Bay, get a dragon figurine. Also on the souvenir market of the city you can buy a beautiful model of a traditional junky under red sails. Such ply the bay. If you book a boat in advance, you can spend the night on board it or go fishing from it.
