Where can bulk embroidery be used?

Needlework is a great way to learn how to create true masterpieces at home. Many women start embroidering during maternity leave, when there is a bit more free time than usual. But once starting to wield threads or beads, it is very difficult to stop.

Indeed, volume embroidery is a unique process of creativity in which each housewife can express her own vision of beauty. And the result is always pleasing to the eye. Many girls as a result of such trainings eventually embroider real canvases. And such works are evaluated not only by guests, but even participate in contests, exhibitions, take prizes.



Three-dimensional stitch embroidery is the most popular version of such needlework. Such patterns sometimes give the impression of real paintings. The main difference of this embroidery technique is the special imposition of threads on each other. As a result, the flower receives volume and the necessary bulge over the fabric. In this case, the shades are able to merge into each other. A similar effect is more difficult to achieve if bulk cross-stitch is used. But in the second case, everything is possible, if you make enough effort. Volumetric flowers, patterns and animals will be a wonderful decoration for clothes. This decor is becoming a real trend of the season. Many fashion designers bring embroidery to the first place in their collections. After all, this makes the thing truly unique. Do-it-yourself three-dimensional embroidery is the guarantee that you will no longer find such clothes on any of the passers-by.

Not only threads are involved. Often needlewomen use thin satin ribbons or woolen yarn along with them. And in some cases, materials can be combined.

Their varied texture will better convey each item in the picture. Such a technique will look especially successful when animals are embroidered. Using fluffy yarn, you can accurately convey their appearance.

Volumetric beadwork is a very complex and time-consuming process. No wonder the finished paintings are put up for sale at a pretty decent price. Such works are distinguished by a luxurious appearance and more rich colors. Each bead is attached to the fabric separately. The most popular are icons embroidered in this way. They look like a real historical jewel. But volumetric embroidery with beads is appropriate not only as pictures. Decorate your old handbag or collar with lapels on an ordinary plain tile in this way. And the thing will sparkle with new colors, again becoming a bright detail of your wardrobe.
