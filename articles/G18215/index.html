How to get to Diveevo from St. Petersburg on your own?

Diveevo is an ancient village in the Nizhny Novgorod region, leading its history from the 16th century. But for believers, this name carries a much deeper meaning. Before figuring out how to get to Diveevo from St. Petersburg, you need to understand how attractive this place is. In Diveevo there is the Holy Trinity Seraphim-Diveevo Convent, where many pilgrims from different parts of the country go. In addition, this legendary place is associated with the name of St. Seraphim of Sarov.

History of the Diveevo Monastery

Seraphim of Sarov at one time took under the patronage of the monastery at the request of its founder, mother Alexandra. Before Alexandra came to Diveevo, there was only a small wooden church. Arriving as a stranger in the village, she began to build a stone temple, then received land from the local landowner and began to build a convent.

After the death of mother Alexandra, Seraphim of Sarov took up the community. His merit is that he built the main shrine of the monastery - the Groove of the Queen of Heaven. This event took place at the beginning of the 19th century.

After the death of the saint, the monastery underwent various trials. Under Soviet rule, it was closed, and only in 1989 did its revival begin.





According to the saint, Diveevo is the fourth chapel of the Virgin in the world and the only one in Russia. This means that the Mother of God took under her protection some holy places:

  • Athos;
  • Iveria;
  • Kiev;
  • Diveevo.

The Virgin Mary walked along the Groove and now this path in the shape of a horseshoe is blessed. Thousands of people annually decide how to get from St. Petersburg to the Diveevo monastery in order to go along the Canal by the Virgin and join the relics of Seraphim of Sarov. Everyone has different goals:

  • Pray and receive grace.
  • Ask for the fulfillment of desires.
  • Tourist interest.
  • Swim in the holy spring.




How to get to Diveevo

Given that Diveevo is not a large city, but a village, getting here is not so easy. The most burdensome option is a paid pilgrimage tour, since the care of arrival and accommodation falls on the shoulders of the organizers. But you can get by yourself. There are several ways to get to Diveevo from St. Petersburg on your own:

  • air flight;
  • train ride;
  • bus ride;
  • car trip.

Between Diveevo and St. Petersburg about 1200 kilometers. How to get to Diveevo from St. Petersburg? The distance is considerable, which will entail significant time costs. To save time, the most convenient form of transport is an airplane.

How to reach the place

To use air travel, you need to buy a plane ticket to the regional center where the airport is located, that is, to Nizhny Novgorod. Two direct flights depart from Pulkovo Airport to Nizhny Novgorod every morning. Flight time is only 1 hour 40 minutes. The ticket price is from 6000 rubles, if you buy it in advance (at least 2 weeks before the flight).

In Nizhny Novgorod there is a bus station Shcherbinka. From here every day in the afternoon a bus goes to the village. The journey takes about 3 hours. Thus, 1200 kilometers will be overcome quite quickly and not too tiring. Of course, the cost of flights is not available to everyone. Therefore, other options may be considered.



If you choose a train to Nizhny Novgorod

Fans of leisurely and more economical trips can offer a train option. If you buy seats in a compartment or CB, the traveler will get in comfort. However, Diveyev is not in the list of settlements equipped with railway stations. Russian railways offer several options. Therefore, tourists will have to decide for themselves how to get to Diveevo from St. Petersburg by train. You can select the following areas:

  • Nizhny Novgorod;
  • Arzamas;
  • Murom.

From the Moscow railway station you can get to Nizhny Novgorod. In the evenings, the Volga branded train No. 059A runs from the Moscow railway station . The journey time on this flight will be 14 hours, the average ticket price is 4719 rubles, the actual price depends on the type of carriage selected. If saving is important, then you can buy a ticket to the reserved seat car for just 2615 rubles. There is a bus from Nizhny Novgorod to Diveevo, which was already mentioned earlier.



You can get to Nizhny Novgorod with a change in Moscow if you use the Sapsan train as a vehicle. This is a high speed type of railway vehicle. From St. Petersburg to Moscow, he arrives in less than 4 hours. In Moscow, the Sapsan arrives at the Leningradsky railway station, making many flights per day. From Moscow, on the Sapsan you can get to Nizhny Novgorod, however, flights are made only twice a day - in the morning and in the evening. The trip takes 4 hours. Tickets for the Sapsan are significantly higher than for a passenger train, but travel time is much less. In addition, the "Peregrine Falcon" is a train of increased comfort.

Other train options

How to get to Diveevo from St. Petersburg, if you choose another city? You can take the train to Arzamas station. To do this, take any of the four trains following the Kazan direction, for example, at number 134, which runs daily. This route is better than the Nizhny Novgorod direction in that Arzamas is geographically closer to its destination.



Upon arrival, you must take the bus to Diveevo at Arzamas-2 station. Buses run almost every hour and spend less than an hour and a half on the road. In addition, a bus daily runs directly to the Serafimo-Diveevsky monastery directly from the train station.

To Diveevo by bus via Moscow

Answering the question of how to get from St. Petersburg to Diveevo by bus, you need to realize that there is no direct communication between the settlements. Whichever route you choose, you have to do a transfer.

The option of traveling by bus is quite tedious, since the trip will last at least 19 hours, but at the same time, the passenger will not be able to move freely and change his pose, content with only walking at stops. Nevertheless, many tourists choose this method of transportation because of the low cost of tickets. The easiest way is to make a transfer in Moscow, as there are many different flights to Moscow from St. Petersburg. There is a direct bus from Moscow to Diveevo. The cost of the trip is from 5000 rubles.

Bus routes to Nizhny Novgorod

You can get from St. Petersburg by direct bus to Nizhny Novgorod, and there already use a local flight. This option is more economical, as a ticket to Nizhny Novgorod costs from 2000 rubles. There are two flights:

  • with the Moskovskaya metro station;
  • from Konyushennaya square.


If the tourist is a staunch supporter of the bus mode of transportation, you can advise you to buy a place on a pilgrimage tour on this type of transport. There are a number of such tours, almost every travel agency dealing with transportation to local destinations can offer its delivery services.

How to get there by car

Not all travelers like to use public transport, so they wonder how to get to Diveevo from St. Petersburg by car. The question is not simple. When a tourist rides on public transport, he does not think about the numerous turns, junctions, police, gasoline and other road difficulties.

To get on your car, you need to build a convenient route, focusing not only on the map, but also on the quality of roads, the presence of roadside cafes and gas stations, safe places to relax, since the path to the monastery will take 15-17 hours. In addition, the car must be prepared technically so that there are no unpleasant surprises in the middle of the trip.



Gasoline consumption will be about 120 liters. With an average cost of a liter of 38 rubles, the total expense will be approximately 4560 rubles, so the savings compared to public transport are not significant.

Leaving the city follows the Moscow highway. Then take the M10 highway to Nizhny Novgorod and drive through the Tver region. It is better to pass Moscow, as it is famous for many hours of traffic jams. Therefore, it will be more convenient and faster to drive through the cities of Solnechnogorsk, Zelenograd, and Sergiev Posad near Moscow . Further along the M7 highway, the tourist will get to the city of Vladimir. After Vladimir, the Nizhny Novgorod region begins and, having traveled Murom, the traveler may consider that the problem of how to get to Diveevo from St. Petersburg has been solved successfully.
