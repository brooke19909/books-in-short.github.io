How to make rubber figures: step-by-step description and photo

How to make rubber figures? This question is quite popular, as is weaving in general. In any city there are shops that sell bright rings. Girls increasingly wear original bracelets, decorating bags and backpacks with figurines. All this can be done using rubber bands. Actions are not always complex. Elegant products can be obtained quite easily. It is necessary to consider in more detail the weaving process.

A little bit about the history of this fascinating activity





If you literally translate the expression rainbow-loom-bands, it will sound like "rainbow on a loom." And this makes some sense, since the bracelet will shimmer with a variety of colors. And answering the question of how to make figures from gum, it should be noted that the weaving process very much resembles weaving art. A similar hobby appeared in America, where children practically do not do needlework. In order to interest them, a similar kind of weaving was invented. And the idea was a success. The children quickly became inspired by the idea, began to show imagination, invent original combinations.

Why the question of how to make figures from gum has gained great popularity. This is largely due to the fact that the resulting products can be worn, made out with the help of them clothes, dress up dolls, give. You can do anything with them.

Gradually weaving took root in our country. Bright rubber bands simply could not go unnoticed by young fashionistas. The relatively low cost, excellent training, helping to develop motor skills, perseverance and sincere enthusiasm - all this made the weaving process interesting and addictive.

Who might like gum weaving?





How to make rubber figures? Who needs this at all? Such a hobby will suit absolutely everyone. Especially strongly it is able to interest students. Boys and girls can invent any patterns of weaving. It is not necessary to spend a lot of time on this. In order to come up with something interesting, one change may suffice. It is best to start weaving at the age of seven.

What tools and materials can I use?

How to make rubber figures on the machine? First you need to figure out which devices you might need. For weaving baubles and figures, it is worth using several devices and materials.

First of all, multi-colored silicone rubber bands will be required. They can vary in their texture and properties. At the present stage, the choice is quite large.

Simple machines for weaving are a plastic slingshot (may have two or more teeth) and a hook. Available means can act as a slingshot. For example, pencils. The hook can be replaced with a knitting device. However, experienced craftsmen argue that a plastic machine of this nature is more convenient. To realize more complex options for weaving, you will need to use a special device. It is a transparent plastic base, equipped with several rows of columns.

As additional materials, you can use a variety of pendants, beads, key chains. They can be simply woven into a craft.

How not to get confused in the weaving process?





How to make bracelets and rubber figures? Depending on the level and number of material used, such products can be divided into simple and complex. It is best to start weaving with simple ones. An elementary option is a single-loop bracelet. How to make rubber figures on a hook? Consider the simplest option for weaving a bracelet.

Fold the gum in half. In the loops that will be formed in this case, it is necessary to thread the next rubber band. After that, it is necessary to bend it and repeat the above steps. To master such weaving is quite simple. After it, you can go to such patterns as the "French braid". To weave it is worth using a machine. After mastering this device, you can answer the question of how to make figures of rubber bands on a slingshot.

If you are new to this business, you can get confused quite easily. To avoid this, use elastic bands of different, preferably contrasting colors. They should be arranged in turn. 1st gum should be located eight. 2nd and 3rd - straight. After that, the ends of the 1st gum should be removed and transferred using the hook to the middle. Then you need to put on the 4th strip, and remove the ends of the 2nd and transfer to the middle.

Complex patterns must be woven using a loom. In this situation, you can use two rows, or you can use all the rest. The favorite figure is the heart. It is often woven into a bracelet. In order to get this pattern, you need to perform fairly simple steps:

  1. We dump the first gum in the middle.
  2. Move the second gum to the right, and the third - to the center.
  3. 4th must be moved to the left and put on the 5th.

For such a product, it is best to use rubber bands of red or pink color. At the present stage, there are a huge number of very different weaves. And their number is only increasing.

We create animals from elastic bands. The basic principles of this process

How to make rubber figures? Without a machine, creating an original, complex product will not work. But it will be enough to learn how to weave only one figure in a certain pattern to figure out how to weave the rest of the crafts. Then everything will depend on the imagination. For example, in order to weave a snake, you need to use the machine in 3 rows. In order to form a cobra hood, you will need to add a few elastic bands on the side. Eyes are made separately.





Much harder to create a turtle. The process will be labor intensive. However, the result can be amazingly beautiful. To weave a turtle, you should use a machine. Gum position is recommended in shape. Different colors should highlight the legs, head and shell. Weaving a figurine is also possible with a hook. In such a situation, knitting all parts of the body will be required on the hands.

In order to create a panda, you need to purchase black and white material. You will also need to use a three-row machine. It is necessary to draw a silhouette of a bear, make a head with paws black, and the middle white. After this, you need to hook the 2nd and 3rd layers with a crochet. Due to this, the figure will be thickened and become more voluminous.

How to weave a hairpin in the shape of a flower?

In order to weave jewelry, you need to use a fairly simple technique. With the help of a hook, it is necessary to string elastic bands with ordinary rings, fasten them in the middle. Remember that the more shades are used, the brighter the product will turn out, which as a result will resemble a flower. A similar accessory can be used as a hairpin or bracelet.

How to weave a stylish bracelet?





In order to weave a bracelet, it is worth using a machine tool. Type two pairs on a column of 7 rubber bands each. Move them with the hook one at a time. When one figure is ready, you can proceed to the next. As a result of simple actions, a stylish bracelet will be obtained.

Some recommendations to consider

As already mentioned, there are a lot of weaving techniques. They will depend on the number of rubber bands, their color, the availability of a special machine and, of course, skills. If you saw a beautiful bracelet with complex weaving, do not immediately try to repeat it, if you have not done this before. Everything is recommended to be done gradually.

What should be considered in the process of creating products?

  1. How to make rubber figures? On a fork, on pencils or knitting needles you can weave equally successfully. But these tools can be used only if the weaving is simple. Otherwise, you must use the machine. Especially if the figure is extremely complex in the weaving pattern.
  2. You can weave on the fingers - index and middle. To do this, you will first need to prepare gum, arrange them according to shades. After that, we begin to put them on fingers and weave, guided by the pattern. This weaving process may seem quite complicated. But this is not so. After gaining experience, actions will begin to be performed on the machine.
  3. More convenient compared to pencils or knitting needles is a slingshot. She has the optimal thickness of the horns. Yes, and hands remain free. In addition, with the help of it you can perform medium in complexity of weaving.
  4. If a hobby could interest and captivate you, you should think about buying a mini-machine. With it, it will be possible to create more complex products, where forks and knitting can not be dispensed with.
  5. Bracelets and figurines can be worn independently, but can be presented. You can even create a variety of products for sale. However, if the fantasy regarding weaving bracelets is over, then proceed to weaving a variety of shapes. They can be very complex and simple. Again, much will depend on the imagination.
