G-sensor

An important advantage of any gadget is the presence of an electronic accelerometer. This is an accident sensor that records the exact time of increasing the load on the car and a sharp decrease in speed. If the car stood still, then an increase in speed and pressure at the same time is recorded.

So the ‚Äúaccident point‚Äù is noted in the time period. Thanks to this function, it is possible not only to determine the moment of an accident up to a second, but also to protect important video materials about this accident from erasure or overwriting. Drivers appreciate these gadgets, according to reviews of the Cardinal G3 dashcam.
