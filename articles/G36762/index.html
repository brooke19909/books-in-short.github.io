How to sew a women's jumpsuit? Some useful tips

The popularity of this type of clothing, like women's overalls, can be easily explained: firstly, it is very simple and convenient, and secondly, it is stylish and fashionable. Attracted by fashionistas is the versatility of the product. Before stitching the jumpsuit, it is enough to make minor changes to the pattern, choose a fabric that is more charged - the product will get a new sound and become an interesting evening outfit.



Fabrics for tailoring overalls can be used very different, you should only avoid very rough and thick. In general, the fabric should be selected according to the purpose of the product. Practical, knitted or stretch fabrics, calm colors are suitable for everyday use. To create a dress for a festive occasion, which will be used as an evening one, it is better to choose a fabric of bright color, iridescent or shiny.

How to sew a women's jumpsuit

Sewing overalls is not so difficult if you have the skills to work with patterns, overlock and a sewing machine. The first thing to do is to come up with a style for the product and look at a suitable fabric in the store. It should not be too tight. The amount of it needed for tailoring will be prompted by the seller, it will depend on your height, hip size, width of fabric, style and expected length of the product. You should also purchase non-woven fabric for gluing some parts of the product, threads to match the fabric or contrasting, if the stitch will be used as a finish (for example, for denim). You will also need needles, scissors, locks and other accessories, crayons.



Before sewing overalls, be sure to make a quality pattern. Preliminary should be taken measurements. Specialists recommend making a separate drawing of the top of the product and its lower part.

In the process of sewing, they will connect, at the junction, you can make a drawstring for elastic or lace, insert for the belt.

What should be provided for when cutting overalls

Do not forget to mark the location of pockets, fasteners, zippers on the pattern. Do not make the jumpsuit too tight - this can cause some stiffness in movements, especially during walking and squats.

The bodice of the jumpsuit usually has straps that cross at the back or are connected at the neck, but you can do without them if you wish.

Before you sew a jumpsuit, determine the length of the lower part - it will be long pants, breeches or shorts above the knee. Their shape may also be different.



The pattern is done, you can proceed to cutting the fabric. Remember to add seam allowances. Each cut-out detail should be treated with an overlock or at least a zigzag seam. After that, the parts should be swept away. Then they try on. If you are convinced that the product sits well on you, you can sew all the seams. Last of all, the bottom of the legs is neatly hemmed.

Make your jumpsuit unique

The product is assembled, now comes the most pleasant moment - its decoration. Here you can show all your imagination and use applique, embroidery, braid, rhinestones. When planning how to sew overalls for the summer, you can consider inserting guipure or natural lace, this will give your robe a special appeal and originality.
