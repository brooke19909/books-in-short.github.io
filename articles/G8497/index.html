Montenegro Villa Oliva is an ideal place to relax

Montenegro is an amazing country with beautiful landscapes, picturesque beaches, clean air and the warm Adriatic Sea. One of the great places to relax is the resort of Petrovac, which is located in a cozy bay. Montenegro has excellent hotels and hotel complexes with high service. "Villa Oliva" is one of the most beautiful and comfortable holiday destinations in this resort.

Location

The hotel is located in a quiet resort town of Petrovac, which is only thirty minutes from Budva. It got its name because of the huge plantings around the complex of olive trees. The nearest airport is Tivat Airport, 36 km from the property. Near the complex there are a bank, post office, shops and restaurants, a hundred meters to the beach area.

Montenegro offers comfortable, cozy and modern apartments for vacationers. Villa Oliva is one of the best hotels where the quality of services and price policy are perfectly combined.
