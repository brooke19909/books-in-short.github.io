The institution being discussed today is located somewhat off Smolenskaya Street, because the exact address is as follows: 1st Nikololoshchepovsky Lane, 6th Building, 2nd Building. It should be noted that Smolenskaya Street is only a few hundred meters away.

If you plan to get to your destination by public transport, in this case you have two options: the subway and the bus.
