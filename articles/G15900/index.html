Kumkul - a lake in the Chelyabinsk region: we spend holidays in the bosom of nature

Chelyabinsk region is famous for its marvelous landscapes and untouched nature. It is in these parts that it is best to relax from the tension of big cities. Almost three thousand lakes are located throughout the region, of which Kumkul undoubtedly stands out. It is considered a favorite among other reservoirs in this area. Here, the tourist is provided with comfort, wonderful relaxation and all the conditions for maintaining comfort. Without a doubt, we can say that this is the best place to relax with your family surrounded by nature. The indisputable advantage is that you do not have to spend a lot of time on the road from the city or vice versa.

Kumkul - a lake that has a muddy bottom with an admixture of sand. Its only negative can be called huge accumulations of garbage on the banks due to uncultured tourists.

Rest on a reservoir

In size, Kumkul is a lake that is approximately 3 km long and 3 km wide. Almost no algae. And reeds and reeds can be found if you walk along the shore. Many people, among other things, play beach games here or simply sunbathe. Around the lake there is a deciduous forest, in which the main mass of trees is occupied by white birches.

Fishing

Many species of fish are found in these waters. Fans of fishing during the holidays will definitely like it here. Fishing is allowed both from the shore and from the boat. At the box office you can take everything you need to go fishing. In general, Kumkul Lake is equipped with all fishing facilities.



Entertainment for tourists

The bases on Lake Kumkul, located on the shores, are great for an overnight stay. On their territory there are cottages for living, where you can book rooms. In them, everything necessary for housing is already waiting for the traveler. There are many different types of entertainment, not only daytime, but also evening. For example, billiards or karaoke. But most leave for the evening one of the most original entertainments - a Russian bath. It is not only pleasant, but also good for health. After the bath, anyone will cheer up, forgetting all the fatigue. And after swimming, you can cook a delicious barbecue. There is a barbecue near each cottage, and coal and firewood in some bases are provided for free.

Kumkul is a lake that no tourist, no doubt, will not be able to forget soon. The local centers offer everything you need for sunbathing. Extremals will love the rental of ATVs and jet skis. If you wish, you can even arrange small competitions.

In addition to swimming in the lake, children will enjoy organized joint cartoon watching, drawing and much more. You can relax here not only in the summer, but also at any other time of the year. No one will feel any dampness in cloudy weather, and this despite the fact that mainly a tourist base is surrounded by a forest.

Recreation facilities

In the immediate vicinity of the lake is the recreation center "Lesnaya Dacha". It is built relatively recently. It is here that you can choose a cottage to taste. The choice is very diverse. There are also banquet rooms where you can celebrate holidays or even corporate parties. In addition to all these advantages, you can also save on living here.

Kumkul - a lake, near which beach parties are often held. They are visited mainly by young people. But this is not all ways to relax on the territory of Lake Kumkul.

In the warmer months, you can take a dip in the water park. People come to this area any day and time of the day, it is not necessary to wait for the start of the vacation. Pools, trampolines, slides and much more awaits tourists.

Holidays on Lake Kumkul will be one of the most unforgettable. No doubt everyone will like it here, and every time you will come back here again and again.
