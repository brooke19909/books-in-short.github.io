Tele2: reviews of employees, customers, customers

Tele2 is one of the most famous Russian brands operating in the cellular market. However, this organization recently began to develop as a full-fledged player at the federal level. Starting as a regional operator, the Tele2 brand came to Moscow and expects to become one of the leading suppliers in the capital's communications market. How to evaluate the quality of the services provided by the company, its customers and customers? How do Tele2 employees characterize their employer?





Company Overview

The Tele2 brand, reviews of which are often found on thematic online portals, has been operating in the Russian Federation since 2003. The company is known as a market player, growing from a modest regional operator to a powerful national brand. In 2013, the Russian division of the Tele2 brand, owned by the Swedish company of the same name, was sold to VTB Group, after which the company became independent from its head office in Stockholm.

In February 2014, Tele2 (expert reviews described this event in an extremely positive way) together with Rostelecom formed a new federal brand that could be viewed as directly competing with the Big Three players - Beeline, MTS and MegaFon. The current license portfolio allows Tele2 to carry out activities on almost the entire territory of Russia and, therefore, build a network of mobile communication services on a federal scale. The company has reserved 3G and 4G frequencies, which allows it to be competitive in one of the fastest growing segments of mobile services - providing Internet access.





In 2015, it expanded the geography of the presence of the Tele2 brand Moscow. Feedback from market analysts regarded this event almost as a sensation - for many years the company could not break into the Russian capital market. Finally she succeeded. The presence of the brand on the Moscow market has a number of features - primarily in terms of the use of communication technologies. Consider them.

Technological features of the Moscow communications market

First of all, experts note that the Tele2 brand does not have the ability to operate at a frequency of 900 MHz in Moscow. That is, the operator so far will not be able to work in the capital, providing services in the GSM standard. At the same time, there may be no real need for this - since, firstly, building a new GSM infrastructure from scratch does not always make sense, and secondly, Tele2 has a wide range of other frequencies at its disposal. Such as, for example, 2.1 GHz, as well as 2.5-2.7 MHz. This enables the brand to actively develop in the capital in the segment of providing access to mobile Internet - based on 3G and LTE technologies.





Another resource available for the brand in Moscow is optical cables from Rostelecom, with which, as we noted above, the company is actively cooperating. Tele2, as analysts say, has the opportunity to install newer and more technologically advanced equipment in the expansion process in the capital market. At the same time, those mobile operators that have been working in Moscow for a long time can not always afford to upgrade their infrastructure, because the already invested investments in current equipment have not paid off. Indeed, in the modern market of communication equipment there are increasingly cheaper and less energy-intensive devices, moreover, compatible with advanced communication technologies such as 3G and 4G. Moreover, those operators who are already present in Moscow will most likely need to modernize their current infrastructure in order to remain competitive. While at Tele2 it will be quite modern.

Franchising

The brand is expanding its presence by various methods. So, the operator can develop a regional network in the traditional way - by opening branches. But there is another, actually turning into the main, way to expand Tele2 geography - a franchise. Expert reviews regarding the business model offered by the brand are very positive. As part of a franchise, entrepreneurs can achieve impressive results in terms of the profitability of their business if, firstly, they start activities in the geographic or social segment of the business that is not saturated, and secondly, they can make enough efforts to attract new customers.

However, as many market experts, as well as company customers, note that the brand is becoming more and more active - at a pace commensurate with those characteristic of the work of the Big Three operators - it is also actively expanding its presence in remote areas of the constituent entities of the Russian Federation. Internet Quality Reviews

Actually, how do users evaluate the proposed Tele2 Internet? Customer reviews of the company regarding relevant services are largely determined by the specific geography of using online access to the network. The fact is that updating the Tele2 infrastructure necessary to ensure stable communication of subscribers with the Internet via mobile channels cannot objectively be carried out simultaneously in all of Russia.





In some regions, the operator has successfully transferred its lines to 4G standards, in others - so far the main traffic goes through 3G channels. Therefore, user reviews from different constituent entities of the Russian Federation regarding the assessment of online access speed, obviously, will vary. But with regard to such aspects of access to online as connection stability, ping duration, uniform download speed, Tele2 services are evaluated quite positively.
