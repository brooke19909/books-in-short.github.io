Location Features

The hotel "Fairy-2" (Anapa) was built almost on the seashore. To the beach its guests can be reached in just a couple of minutes. Such a convenient location, most tourists consider the main advantage of this hotel.





The hotel is located not in Anapa itself, but in its suburb - the village of Dzhemete.
