Kabardinka: beaches. Reviews of tourists and vacationers

Near Gelendzhik is the famous resort village of Kabardinka. The beaches, the reviews of which we summarize below, are generally very good in this area. They stretched along the Tsemess bay. The resort itself is located in a beautiful valley, smoothly descending to the sea waters. The descent into the water here is quite gentle, not very deep. Therefore, this area is very good for families who decide to relax with their children. Now let's see what they say about this resort.



Kabardinka: beaches. Reviews about the rest in general

The local coast is not suitable for those who can not imagine themselves resting without lying on the sand. The beaches in Kabardinka are mostly pebbles. True, sometimes it is fine, like coarse sand. Therefore, you should think carefully about which beach in Kabardinka to choose. Of course, the most comfortable plot is located in the center of the village. However, there are always people here, and especially in the midst of summer, there are extremely many. Tourists who prefer privacy are offered to go to farther beaches. There are many beautiful and almost uninhabited places that the Kabardinka region has. The beaches whose reviews are most interesting in this regard are Victoria and the recreation area on Cape Doob. In addition, those who wish to throw off everything in the literal and figurative sense, experienced holiday-makers advise the nudist coast. It is as famous as the naturist beaches of Koktebel. This is a very beautiful place, located among pine trees and juniper, and it is located to the left of Cape Doob, if you stand facing the sea.
