If you are looking for a way to receive income in addition to scholarships and pensions, then you have the following options:

Online earnings

Stable additional income at home is not a dream, but a reality together with the World Wide Web. It is difficult for a person with standard employment from 8 to 17 hours to find an offline part-time job, but if you have a computer, you can work at least during the lunch break, even in the evenings or at night.

The most reliable and stable online earnings is freelance - remote work. At special exchanges, you can take on a variety of tasks: writing articles, drawing logos, working on making or filling sites, creating videos, retouching photos, and much more. Also there, entrepreneurs and top managers are looking for a personal assistant who would carry out their assignments or coordinate the implementation of routine tasks.

Freelance also has its pitfalls. It is clear that you need to have valuable skills. There is also strong competition, for example, there are much fewer specialists in fixing bugs in Java codes than there are those who want to write articles on a common topic or post ads on the Internet, which makes a huge difference in the prices for a project.



Also, do not count on freelance as a one-time part-time job. On exchanges, the rating of the performer is of great importance, few people assign tasks to beginners. Therefore, in the first stages you will have to fulfill the lowest paid orders, and only then, when you have feedback, you will be entrusted with more serious projects. Then you will have a stable job, extra income and a good reputation. The growth opportunities are very impressive - right up to the transition to freelance as the main activity.

Security measures

Avoid working off-exchange and without prepayment. Otherwise, you risk not getting the money you earned. It is considered normal practice when the customer provides a small low-paid test task before choosing a specialist for ongoing cooperation. But if you are offered to do part of the work for free, this is a reason to beware. There is a fairly common fraud scheme: an order is placed, for example, for a large amount of text for translation and you are asked to translate part of it to assess your competence. And so does everyone who wants to receive an order. As a result, the customer receives the finished work without having chosen anyone as the contractor.

Of course, freelance exchanges take a percentage of your earnings, but they protect your interests: you know for sure that the customer is able to pay for the work, and in the event of a dispute, arbitration determines which side is true.

Serious approach

Surprisingly, but even selling their belongings, you can fall into the clutches of scammers, and instead of getting extra income, lose your hard-earned money. Calls from supposedly potential buyers that offer to make an advance payment to your bank card and simultaneously elicit all the information about it, up to the PIN code, have become popular. No matter how absurd it may sound, many are happy to take a thing without hesitation and tell strangers all the data needed to manage the account. Therefore, when you are not interested in a thing, its size, condition, and immediately want to pay and quickly - most likely, these are deceivers.

Beware of frivolous shoppers. Many people avoid prepayments and ask for cash on delivery. This is quite reasonable, but if the buyer does not pick up the parcel, it will be returned to you, and you will pay the cost of return shipping. Therefore, try to find out the mood of the buyer: if he is not sure that he is taking the thing, then refrain from such a transaction.
