LCD "Three Oranges" from the Norman construction company in St. Petersburg: deadline, photos and reviews

St. Petersburg is a multi-million megalopolis, where the housing problem is especially acute. Apartments in Soviet buildings are now crowded out by modern residential complexes and neighborhoods with their own infrastructure. What attract potential buyers of new buildings? Most of them are located in developed areas of the city.





Construction companies offer a variety of planning solutions, the presence of their own protected area with recreation areas, playgrounds and sports grounds, as well as, no less valuable, spacious parking lots. Such projects can be safely attributed LCD "Three Oranges", presented by the Norman construction company.

about the project

A monolithic multi-storey building erected using special technology in the north of St. Petersburg - all this is the Three Oranges project. The design documentation provides for the integrated development of the microdistrict, which is presented in several stages. The first stage is represented by two skyscrapers of 26 floors. These are monolithic structures built using the technology of ventilated facades.





The second phase will end with the construction of the third complex. The volumetric facades of each building will be effectively framed by balconies with large stained glass windows. It offers stunning city views. Residents of the first building, which has already been commissioned and populated, emphasize how pleasant it is to admire the lights of the metropolis late in the evening and at night.

Location

The Norman construction company, for the implementation of its author's project, chose the Vyborg district of the Northern capital, the village of Pargolovo. Experts emphasize that the place for the construction of the new building was chosen well: the nearest suburb of St. Petersburg, between Engels Avenue and Vyborg Highway.





The construction of the residential complex "Three Oranges" (St. Petersburg) is carried out in the territory owned by the developer, so buying an apartment here will be a profitable investment. Such an acquisition excludes the presence of third parties and intermediaries of the transaction, guaranteeing the transparency of its conclusion. Surely you are interested in the deadline for the delivery of LCD Three Oranges. The last, third building will be commissioned in the 4th quarter of 2017.

Construction Features

"Normann" is a construction company that uses unique technologies in its projects, allowing to provide all residents with the most comfortable living conditions. A rational and reliable heating distribution system made it possible to completely get rid of risers and pipes that spoil the appearance of the apartments. With its help, it is possible to fully and efficiently control heat consumption, saving on costs. The designed mechanical ventilation system made it possible to abandon the use of ventilation units in apartments. Due to this, it was possible to expand the interior space of the apartments, to offer customers a variety of planning solutions.

Ecology

The development area has developed a rather difficult ecological situation, causing a number of concerns among potential residents. But it is compensated by park areas, landscaping of the residential complex "Three Oranges".





Reviews of the first residents note that the windows of the houses overlook the Shuvalovsky Park - a favorite place for walking and relaxing for all residents of the complex. In the immediate vicinity there are no harmful industrial plants that could worsen the environmental situation.

Infrastructure

LCD "Three Oranges" - a complex being built in the suburbs of the city. That is why the issue of transport accessibility and infrastructure comes first. In walking distance from new buildings metro station "Parnas". The houses are located between busy highways. A convenient exit to the Ring Road is equipped from Engels Avenue, the Olginsky Highway and the Vyborg Highway.

For convenient and comfortable living on the territory of the complex must be presented the necessary objects of social infrastructure. In the village of Pargolovo there are several preschool educational institutions with a different bias. Local residents emphasize that even in the city it is difficult to find a kindergarten with such skilled employees.





Within walking distance of a comprehensive school, qualified medical assistance can also be obtained at a local clinic. The infrastructure of the village is represented by shops, shopping centers, pharmacies, restaurants and cafes - it will definitely not be bored here for either children or adults. On foot you can reach one of the largest water parks in the city.

Apartments, layouts

The houses are different in size and layout of the apartment. The ability to choose the best option in all respects just attracted many buyers. Most of them are occupied by small cozy studios and one-room apartments, there are also two-bedroom apartments and spacious three-room apartments. In apartments with a large number of rooms an additional bathroom is provided. Planning LCD Three Oranges will satisfy the expectations and requirements of even the most demanding residents.





All apartments are fully finished and ready for use: all that remains is to bring furniture. Residents of already commissioned apartments emphasize that all the work has been carried out efficiently, there is no need for their completion: the walls are glued with modern wallpaper, a laminate is lying on the floor, and a stretch canvas lies on the ceiling .
