Maiori (Italy): beaches, hotels, attractions, reviews

In our article we want to talk about a small town on the coast of Italy. Despite the fact that its population is only about six thousand people, for many years Maiori (Italy) has been holding the brand of a reputable resort of international level.

A little bit about the resort ...

Majori is part of the Amalfi Riviera. The town is located on the coast of the Gulf of Salerno, 60 kilometers from Naples, at the source of the Regina Mayor River.

Historians have not yet come to a consensus about when the city was founded. Opinions of experts differ. Some claim that Majori (Italy) was founded by the Greeks, others by the Romans, and still others mention the mythical prince Sicardo. The only thing that is reliably known is that the city traces its history from ancient times. When it was one of the most important ports of the Amalfi Republic. In the Middle Ages, it was here that the headquarters of the fleet and the arsenals of the whole republic were located. Due to its favorable geographical location, Majori flourished at all times. He was even called the "Royal City." And in the last century, Maiori (Italy) was recognized as an international class resort. Now the city boasts a beautiful modern promenade, beautifully equipped beaches and fashionable hotels.





Maiori attracts many tourists with the inimitable atmosphere of the past, picturesque architecture, white houses with flower pots on the windows, ancient temples and beautiful mansions. And all this is surrounded by stunning lemon trees.

How to get to the city?

In Maiori (Italy), it is most convenient to travel from Naples, as it is only 60 kilometers away. A direct bus runs between the cities. In addition, you can take a train to Salerno and transfer to one of the suburban minibuses Sita, which will cover 25 kilometers. In Salerno you can take a taxi, but its services will cost much more. If you plan to travel on your own transport or a rented car, you need to move along the A3 highway, and then turn onto SP2a.

From Moscow you can fly to Naples. This option is more preferable. But if you decide to fly to Rome, you will still have to get to Naples by domestic flight. Or from Rome to get to the resort by bus or train.

Resort Hotels

There are no problems with accommodation at the resort, since the city has a sufficient number of facilities for tourists. There are places even during the high season. The hotels of Maiori (Italy) are quite diverse, so resort guests always have an extensive selection. You can choose the most suitable option depending on the stardom, distance to the sea and the spectrum of services provided.



In the city center there is a five-star complex Botanico San Lazzaro, which is considered the most fashionable establishment. Maiori also has a huge selection of three- and four-star hotels, among which are the following: Club Due Torri, San Francesco, Panorama, Villa Pandora.

Almost all the hotels in the city offer good service, beautiful rooms with views of the seashore and developed infrastructure. If you intend to relax in Maiori (Italy) (photos are given in the article) during the high season, then rooms in higher-level hotels should be booked in advance, since the resort is deservedly popular.

However, hotels are far from the only accommodation option in the city. There are many mini-hotels in Maiori, accommodation in which will be much cheaper than in "star" establishments. Also, the resort is very common to rent apartments in ordinary residential buildings. Well, for the most budget holidays you can use the services of hostels.

In general, Maiori can be attributed to the category of inexpensive resorts on the Amalfi coast. Therefore, here you can always find more affordable housing than in other coastal cities. So, as part of a budget holiday, you can pay 35-40 euros per night at a hotel, but at the same time, the institution, of course, will be very far from the coast.



But the cost of living per day in a fashionable complex on the seashore ranges from 80-120 euros.

Climate in Maiori

Description of Maiori (Italy) as a resort would be incomplete if not to mention the local climate. Largely thanks to him, the town gained popularity among tourists. The mild Mediterranean climate makes the resort very attractive for a beach holiday. Winter is not too cold, and the season begins in April and lasts until the end of October. The average daily air temperature in the warm season ranges from + 28 degrees. In general, Maiori is characterized by clear and sunny weather throughout the whole year.

Best time to stay in Maiori

What is the best time to choose a holiday in Maiori (Italy)? Of course, vacation time can not always be chosen at our request. Nevertheless, in Major it is very comfortable to relax from the beginning of April to June. At this time, a real Mediterranean spring reigns here with an abundance of flowers and greenery. And the sea in Majori (Italy) at this time is quite suitable for swimming. Water temperature reaches + 18 degrees. And the air temperature is quite comfortable and allows not only relaxing on the beaches, but also paying attention to sightseeing and walking. In July and August, the city has a huge number of tourists, who for the most part come for a beach holiday. The sea temperature at this time reaches + 26 degrees. The weather is getting hot enough.



Undoubtedly, you should choose May for a trip to the Amalfi coast, since there are not many tourists yet, the air temperature is very comfortable, and the water temperature is quite acceptable for swimming,

If you are interested in an active nightlife, then of course, it is worth going to the resort in August or July. Many holidays fall at this time. Almost daily you can become a member of concerts and festivals. The regulars of fashionable resorts come to the coast at this time.

It is worth noting that September is quite comfortable for relaxing in Maiori (Italy), reviews of tourists are a direct confirmation of this. During this period, you can not only swim in the sea, but also visit the most interesting places of the resort.

Local kitchen

Each holidaymaker traveling to Maiori (Italy) (photos are given in the article), of course, is interested in local cuisine. It is worth noting that the dishes of local residents and restaurants are all the same Italian classics. Local establishments serve food made from vegetables grown in the region and seafood. Of course, there are also some special dishes that are worth a try.



The choice of food establishments in the resort is huge, especially in season. There are pizzerias, cafes, restaurants. Such establishments can be found on almost every corner. Prices in them are much more acceptable than in neighboring coastal cities. The traditional food that you will be offered in every cafe is pasta, vegetables, pizza and all kinds of seafood. Among the local institutions it is difficult to single out the best, each of them has its own charm and features. Therefore, the choice of a favorite cafe is a matter of gastronomic preferences of every tourist. The city restaurants have an amazing selection of Italian wines; you should definitely try the limoncello drink made from local lemons, because there are an incredible amount of them growing here.

Resort Attractions

A separate topic for discussion is the sights of Majori (Italy). Despite the fact that the city is very ancient and has a rich history, there are not many sights left in it, in comparison with the neighboring Positano or Amalfi. And the reason for this was the numerous floods that periodically destroyed the city. The crushing force of the elements fell on the city, including in 1954. Then landslides and powerful streams of water destroyed most of the ancient region. But even those buildings that have survived to this day are worthy of close attention of tourists.

Since the Middle Ages in Maiori, unique churches, watchtowers and grottoes have survived. The main attractions are monasteries: Santa Maria de Olearia and Santa Maria delle Grazie.



In addition, the Norman fortress on the coast, the monastery of the Dominican Order and the castle of St. Nicholas (1400) on the Pontechieu hill were preserved. A very interesting building is the temple of Santa Maria a Mare, which was built in the twelfth century. The building has an unusual multi-colored roof, consisting of individual colored majolica tiles.

Numerous reviews about Maiori (Italy) suggest that the town is an incredibly beautiful and picturesque place. The pride of the resort is the modern promenade, decorated with flower beds, fountains and sculptures. The promenade is just the perfect place for walks of citizens and tourists. On one of the main streets of Maiori, many shops and shops with food and souvenirs are concentrated, as well as cozy restaurants and cafes.

Resort Events

Maiori Resort boasts a wide variety of events taking place in the city during the high season. It is colorful events that make it attractive for active youth.

The main and most important event of each season is the famous carnival, which is held annually during the celebration of Easter (Catholic). After it is celebrated Holy Week - Settimana Santa. On one of the many days of celebration, an incredibly impressive torchlight procession is held, during which the most key moments of the Way of Jesus Christ are staged.



It is also worth noting that concerts, festivals, theater performances, and art exhibitions are held in the city during each season. And already in late autumn (usually at the end of October), a film festival is held with the participation of young Italian filmmakers. The festival is named "Roberto Rossellini." It is annually organized by the daughter of the famous creator - Isabella, who dedicated the event to the first meeting of her parents (mother - Ingrid Bergman), which took place precisely in Maiori.

Maiori beaches (Italy)

Since Maiori is considered a famous beach and sea resort, it is the local coast that attracts many tourists here. The entire Amalfi coast is beautiful and attractive in terms of recreation, and the beaches of Majori are considered by many to be the best in these parts.

Local residents say that even Homer wrote that on the Amalfi coast in antiquity sirens lived, which invited sailors here. Perhaps the echoes of their magical voices in some miraculous way still make people from all over the world come here to rest. The beautiful azure sea, picturesque steep cliffs, beautiful coves, vineyards and lemon groves, white houses, fragrant gardens with flowers and butterflies - all this is not a movie frame, this is Majori. It is such a discreet and calm beauty of the resort that attracts vacationers to it.

Maiori beach is considered the widest and longest on the Amalfi coast. The shore has a dark sand cover of volcanic sand. The local coast is divided into separate beach areas. Of course, most of them are paid, although there are several free beaches. One of them is located near the port, and the other is at the very end of the resort's coast.



Paying for your stay on the beach, you get an umbrella and sunbed. It is worth noting that the entire coast of the city is very clean and tidy, this is closely monitored by special services. All beaches are well equipped. You can rent catamarans, boats, scooters, bicycles for walking around the area. The entire coast of Maiori has a well-developed infrastructure. It is worth noting that only a few hotels have their own plots on the coast and at the same time they are located a bit out of town. In general, all vacationers have the opportunity to use any beach resort.

Italy Maiori Resort Reviews

Despite the modest size of the resort, it is popular with vacationers. And there are a number of reasons. Positive reviews of tourists about Maiori in Italy are the best advertising of the resort, in contrast to the slogans and brochures of tour operators. The main advantage of the resort is its affordable prices. Unlike the neighboring towns of the Amalfi Coast, vacation in Maiori can be quite budgetary. The local beach is the largest and widest in the region. Other resorts are characterized by a more rocky coast. In Maiori, the mountains recede slightly towards the mainland, as if revealing for tourists beautiful bays suitable for swimming. It is worth noting that Maiori is the best place to relax with children, because the coast here has a gentle entry into the water, and the sea is very warm and gentle. The resort is protected by mountains from the north winds, so there are practically no storms and waves, especially during the high season.

A small but beautiful promenade is a favorite place for tourists and townspeople. Unfortunately, it is a bit overloaded with hotels built along it, but overall it does not spoil the big picture too much. The promenade is decorated with a stunning palm alley, which adds romanticness to the promenade.





Maiori is a small but very cozy and nice town. It is not as noisy as in large resorts. The city has a calm and chamber atmosphere. Maiori is convenient not only for families with kids, but also for young people as a place of residence, since the prices here are much more pleasant than in other resorts of the Amalfi Coast. The convenient location of the city makes it possible to travel around the coast and see all the sights. It is especially convenient to travel by renting a car. Such a simple solution makes it possible to travel the whole Amalfi coast. The most experienced tourists prefer to rent a car directly in Naples at the airport and already get to Majori on it. According to vacationers, the resort is beautiful in every way, it is definitely worth a visit.
