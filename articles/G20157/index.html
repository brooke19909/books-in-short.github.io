Lenovo Thinkpad Tablet 2 review and reviews

After the not very successful release of the first model, the developers launched the improved Lenovo ThinkPad Tablet 2. This time, the new product runs on Windows, unlike its predecessor, who had Android. The buyer receives a device that supports 3G, a stylus and many other useful additions.

Main characteristics

Recently, there are many models of tablets running on Windows. So what sets Lenovo's new product apart from this vast background? First of all, the filling is an Intel processor with a frequency of 1.8 gigahertz. The internal memory of the device is 64 gigabytes, while the RAM is 2 gigabytes. These characteristics are sufficient for the stable operation of Windows 8 Pro installed on the Lenovo ThinkPad Tablet 2. The 8-megapixel camera allows you to take high-quality pictures of the environment.

It turned out that the Lenovo ThinkPad Tablet 2 is quite lightweight, which will delight lovers of ergonomics and those who constantly take the device with them on the road. Weight is about 600 grams, while similar models from other manufacturers weigh about 900 grams. Convenience in regular use is complemented by high-quality and reliable assembly. With careful handling and careful operation, the device works stably and is not threatened with breakage, as well as mechanical damage.





Design and ergonomics

This time, the manufacturer offered customers a full-fledged transformer that can change its shape into a laptop. Its design is made in the usual for Lenovo concepts. The keyboard and stylus differ from the main matte black case in their red color. All this corresponds to the elegant appearance familiar to the company's products. The combination of red and black is also found in the logo, where the letter I stands out against the general background.

Lenovo thinkpad tablet 2 has all the necessary connectors for connecting third-party devices. For example, this is an HDMI output for sharing a monitor and streaming data. On the left side of the device, you can find the unlock and power button. There is also a headphone jack, as well as a volume control. In addition, we should not forget about the slots for microSD cards, as well as SIM cards with 3G and 4G Internet. They are followed by a port for USB wires and data transfer to other devices. The rear panel of the device is equipped with a stereo speaker and the already mentioned 8 megapixel camera.

Particularly noteworthy is the solution with which the developers were able to fit all the necessary details in a small case. It will be easy for the buyer to navigate the new product from the very first day of operation.

This is just enough for exclusively working moments: storage of documents, etc.

Lenovo ThinkPad Tablet 2 3G offers a high-quality connection to a worldwide network. At the same time, many users will find such opportunities limited. In this case, they should buy a device with 4G support. With such an Internet, you can download the most massive files without any speed problems.

Screen

This is a separate reason for the pride of Lenovo developers. Indeed, a 10-inch screen makes it possible to watch video in HD format and with a resolution of 1320 pixels. In addition, the display is equipped with an IPS class panel. She has wide viewing angles with their distinct black color.

Now multitouch supports five touches at the same time, which makes it possible to work with the system much faster and open several applications at once. Unlike many competitors, Lenovo has not flooded the tablet with endless applications of its production, which cause only annoyance and annoyance of users. However, something is still present. Sound and camera

Unfortunately, the format of any tablet does not allow you to install high-quality speakers on it. However, against the background of its analogues, Lenovo looks and sounds quite worthy. In addition, everyone can connect their favorite headphones.

For example, for video conferencing or watching videos on YouTube, this level is quite acceptable. The design includes additional protection modules so that the user's ears do not suffer from noise, especially if the signal goes through the 3.5 mm port.

The rear 8-megapixel camera has a high-quality LED flash. Shooting video can achieve 1080p quality. If you use this device correctly and do not shake hands, the frames will be clear and legible. By and large, there was not presented anything new compared to previous models from Lenovo.





Battery

The combination of the Atom processor and Windows 8 allows the tablet to work without interruption for 10 hours. Of course, these are the initial characteristics that will decrease over time.

In addition, the duration of the work depends on the processes on the tablet. For example, when watching videos or using photo editors and other large programs, the battery will drain much faster. In addition, it is consumed more intensively when several applications are opened in parallel. In any case, this Lenovo model has the most impressive performance among similar models with Windows 8 as the installed operating system.

In order to save charge more efficiently, you can work on the tablet in a special mode. With it, the screen brightness and power consumption are reduced, which makes it possible to use the device longer.
