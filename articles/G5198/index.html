Ergonomics

In reviews of the Asus ZenFone Live ZB501KL 32GB, owners also discuss ergonomics. Ease of use is an important criterion when choosing a gadget. What can be said about this model? Given that the smartphone is equipped with a 5-inch display, then, of course, it has rather large dimensions. The height of the case is 141.18 mm with a width of 71.74 mm. But thanks to a thickness that does not exceed 8 mm, the device belongs to the category of narrow ones. The mass of the device is only 120 g. Most users claim that it is quite comfortable to operate with one hand, however, it should be noted that the surface of the panels is slippery, so the phone may accidentally fall out.

The assembly of the plastic case is excellent. No creaking or play was detected during testing. All items fit neatly. Also, users noted a high level of wear resistance. Not even the most tidy owners retain their initial appearance for a long time.

Asus ZenFone Live ZB501KL 32GB phone: screen reviews

Every smartphone owner understands that the main element of the gadget is the screen. The level of comfort of interaction will depend on its characteristics. What grade did the display get? By and large, no weighty remarks were made to him. The TFT-IPS matrix provides good color quality. HD resolution is enough for a 5-inch diagonal. The screen clearly and colorful displays the picture. Sharpness, contrast and brightness at a comfortable level.

The color rendition is not bad, but Asus ZenFone Live ZB501KL 32GB is clearly not up to the premium devices. At the factory settings, users noticed a high white temperature. You can fix this yourself by going to the phone settings.

A significant advantage of the model are its wide viewing angles. Using this gadget, there is no need to constantly keep the smartphone at a right angle so that the contrast is not distorted. The backlight range is optimal. When working with the phone in the sun, you can put it to the maximum, and in the dark reduce the brightness to a minimum.

The manufacturer installed an excellent sensor on the device. It responds to touch quickly and clearly, there is an oleophobic coating.

Asus ZenFone Live ZB501KL 32GB: performance reviews

This model is not heavy duty. The four hundredth Qualcomm brand Snapdragon processor is responsible for its performance. The chip consists of 4 computing modules. Type of cores - Cortex-A7. The maximum to which they can accelerate is 1400 MHz. Currently, there are already more productive gadgets, so the Asus ZenFone Live ZB501KL 32GB Black is, so to speak, an average. There are no problems with playing graphics, animated pictures do not freeze, because the Adreno-305 accelerator is responsible for them.

There are two modifications in the lineup. Each of them has two gigabytes of RAM. But the storage of the internal memory is different. The hero of our review is equipped with 32 GB, and the younger sold 16 GB. If desired, the memory can be increased, however, in order to insert the drive, you will have to abandon the second SIM card.

Users in the comments claim that the gadget works well with an average load. Systemic failures are practically not observed. Running programs can sometimes be accompanied by micro-delays. No problems arose with watching videos in Full HD and especially HD. However, the phone could not play the video as 4K.

Camera

Asus ZenFone Live ZB501KL 32GB received most positive reviews thanks to the cameras. As the main implemented 13-megapixel module. It is complemented by a flash that allows you to shoot at night. There is autofocus, but the reviews say that it is rather slow. Describing the capabilities of the camera, you need to pay attention to the modes. There are a lot of them on the menu. According to the manufacturer, each of them is optimally configured for certain conditions. For example, the night mode of extreme shooting or super HDR. A close-up shot is detailed. All elements are clearly visible. However, this quality can only be achieved in daylight. At night, the image will be somewhat blurry, and users noticed that even choosing the HDR mode does not really save the situation - digital noise is still noticeable. The reason for this is the aperture value - f / 2.2. Insufficiently detailed images are obtained taken indoors. But high-quality photographs of the text will turn out without problems. The device copes with this excellently. As for recording videos, their maximum resolution is 1920x1080 px. Frame rate - 30 fps.

The selfie camera also received many reviews. Despite the fact that it is based on a 5-megapixel module, the pictures are obtained in good quality. Moreover, users did not have comments on the work either in the daytime or at night.

Now it's time to talk about the "chip" of this smartphone. The highlight of the gadget was the option Beauty Live. What opportunities does it open to users? During video broadcasting, you can now edit the image quality in real time. For example, a person may apply makeup on his face without further editing.

Communication

As mentioned earlier, the Asus ZenFone Live ZB501KL 32GB smartphone can work with two cards of mobile operators. The tray into which they are inserted is hybrid. One slot is also designed for external drives. Note that the device is capable of working with next-generation networks - LTE. However, this feature is implemented on only one slot, while the second supports the 2G standard.

Manufacturers have not forgotten about wireless modules. In order to be able to access the Internet, it is not necessary to connect to 4G. Wi-Fi is also suitable for these purposes. The phone acts as an access point. There are two ways to transfer files and other data. The first is via a USB cable, the second is using Bluetooth 4.0. Both methods work stably at an acceptable speed.

Implemented in the phone and the navigation module. The signal is set via BeiDou, GPS and GLONASS. Coordinates are determined accurately and quickly.

Battery Life

But by what criteria does the Asus ZenFone Live ZB501KL 32GB get negative reviews? Due to battery life. The fact is that the manufacturer installed a rather weak battery on the device. Its capacity is only 2650 mAh. Naturally, the quick charge function is not provided. In order to restore the resource of the battery, it will take about three hours. Connection to the network is via a USB cable and a 1 A power adapter.

With active use, the phone will "live" until the evening, but at night it will need to be put on recharge. Most energy is spent at maximum screen brightness. If it is reduced to at least 70%, then it will turn out to increase the working life to 30-35 hours.

When using a gadget for games, it is necessary to consider that in one hour approximately 30% of the charge will be "eaten". But when watching a video, switching to the "Flight" mode, you can count on 9-10 hours.

To summarize

So, after testing the Asus ZenFone Live ZB501KL 32GB smartphone, most users rated it 4 on a five-point system. Such a high result was influenced by decent screen specifications, good image quality, and the availability of the Beauty Live option. Of course, the price of the device played an important role. The cost in stores is approximately 9000 rubles.

What users found advantages in this model?

  • Stylish, though not exclusive design.
  • Range of colors. The line has the most popular tones.
  • Optimum dimensions, light weight.
  • A wide range of different settings in the camera menu.
  • A program for video broadcasting.

The described advantages for many customers can be decisive, especially for those who do not have high performance requirements. However, despite this, the device has a significant minus - a modest battery. Also, advanced users were not very satisfied with the hardware, considering the installed processor is already a little outdated. Well, and finally, we note that the choice of plastic for the case disappointed buyers.
