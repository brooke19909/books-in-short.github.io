From the history

Dolls appeared in ancient times. They belong to the very first toys. Initially, they did not even carry a game function, but performed the role of a totem or amulet. The origin of the word "doll" is rooted in ancient Greece, where the word "cyclos" had two meanings - "circle" and "something curled up."

In early history, the doll was an idol for religious rites. It was also believed that the souls of their ancestors were leaving in them. The ancients believed that if you call a doll by the name of a real person, then it becomes its double. Having caused harm to the toy, it was possible to cause damage to its double. Centuries later, dolls lost most of their supernatural powers, but something still remained. There is still an opinion that dolls for newborns avert the evil eye and protect the baby‚Äôs sleep.

Where did Tilda come from?

Today, there are many directions and genres in the creation of handmade toys. Among them, the Tilda doll is one of the most popular and in demand. It appeared in 1999 thanks to a designer from Norway. The doll name is copyrighted and is a full brand. The Tilda brand produces products for creativity and literature on needlework.

At first it was a textile figure of a woman with characteristic features - an elongated body with wide hips, a small head, a long neck, bead eyes and rosy cheeks. This version of the doll has a decorative function, it can not often be washed and given to small children in the game. Another thing is the crocheted Tilda. It can be washed, chewed, fed with porridge and taken with you to the street.

Despite the fact that Tilda is considered a brand doll, no one has been prosecuted for copying it. Tilda toys are crocheted, knitted, sewn from cotton, fleece and other materials by craftsmen around the world. The nature of the toy changes from the material used.

The image became so popular that it was tried on animals. The most popular of them are hares, cats and bears.
