Hotel Golden Dolphin, Adler: description, rooms, beach and reviews

Comfortable accommodation and a wide range of quality services is offered to its guests by the Golden Dolphin Resort Hotel. Adler is one of the most popular places for year-round recreation, and this institution is one of the best accommodation options in the city.





Brief description of the resort hotel

An ideal option for a seaside vacation is the three-star Golden Dolphin Resort Hotel. Adler will delight you with the sea and mountains, and the hotel with comfortable living conditions, as well as a wide range of services. It provides Wi-Fi, private secured parking, as well as entertainment opportunities such as billiards, bowling and tennis. Also organized rafting on mountain rivers. If necessary, you can use the shuttle service.

Comfortable rooms with seven essential amenities are offered by the Golden Dolphin. Adler will open before you in all its glory from the balcony of the apartment. You will certainly appreciate the original design and functionality of the room. It is also worth noting the possibility of round-the-clock service.

Be sure to visit the restaurant of the hotel "Golden Dolphin". Adler is literally crowded with catering establishments, but finding an option with the best value for money is not so simple. In this institution you will find a wide range of dishes from the international menu, as well as a pleasant atmosphere. If necessary, you can order food and drinks directly to your room.





Resort Location

Quite a convenient location is characterized by the hotel "Golden Dolphin". Adler, 219 Lenin Street, is the address of this institution. It is located no more than 100 meters from the azure sea coast. The road to the resort city of Sochi will take about 30 minutes. As for the nearest international airport, it is located about 3 kilometers from the hotel.

Number of rooms

Comfortable rooms await travelers at the Golden Dolphin Hotel (Adler). The price ranges from 3000 to 9000 rubles per night, depending on the category of room. So, you have the following options for placement:

  • Standard apartments are designed to accommodate two guests. Here, on a fairly compact area, there is a large bed (or a pair of one and a half), as well as a dressing table. The bathroom is equipped with a shower.
  • The suite consists of two rooms. The bedroom has a large bed, a workplace and a dressing table. As for the living room, it is equipped with soft folding furniture (can serve to accommodate additional guests), as well as a coffee table. Please note that there is a private furnished balcony.
  • Premium Suite is a huge apartment consisting of a bedroom, a study and a living room. The bathroom comes with a jacuzzi and the private balcony offers sea views.
  • Family Suite - these are two huge bedrooms, which are combined with an internal door. A feature of the room is the presence of two bathrooms, which is especially convenient for a large number of people.
  • Studio Suite is a spacious room in which, by decorative zoning, a bedroom, a living room, as well as a sitting area are highlighted. There is a bar and a private furnished balcony with sea views.




Apartment Amenities

Hotel "Golden Dolphin" (Adler) offers you to use the following amenities provided in guest rooms:

  • a spacious furnished balcony overlooking the sea coast, as well as a walking park;
  • Free high-speed wireless internet access
  • individual air conditioning allows you to create the most comfortable microclimate in the room;
  • the minibar always has drinking water, and for an additional fee alcoholic and carbonated drinks and snacks are provided;
  • TV with the ability to view cable channels;
  • thanks to the electronic safe, you can not worry about the safety of your valuables and documents;
  • The bathroom has an electric hairdryer, a full range of bath accessories, as well as cosmetics.




Resort Infrastructure

Golden Dolphin (3 * Hotel, Adler) offers you to use the following infrastructure facilities:

  • Free Wi-Fi is available in all public areas.
  • All guests of the hotel have access to a private secure parking.
  • A buffet breakfast awaits you at the restaurant, as well as an extensive menu of cuisines from around the world.
  • Reception staff at any time of the day will help you to resolve issues related to hotel accommodation.
  • A huge selection of drinks and light snacks are always available at the bar.
  • In the laundry room, your wrinkled or soiled clothes will be cleaned quickly and carefully.
  • You can hold business events, negotiations and seminars in a conference room equipped with modern office equipment.
  • The hotel has several shops with souvenirs, food, clothing, as well as household chemicals and basic necessities.
  • Perhaps the most important infrastructural object of the Golden Dolphin Hotel (Adler) is a beach with sun loungers and parasols.
  • Gala events will be held at the highest level in the banquet hall of the hotel.




Services and facilities for guests

Hotel "Golden Dolphin" offers its guests to use the following list of services:

  • For those who want to improve their health, spa treatment can be organized on the basis of various institutions of Adler.
  • Lovers of luxury, secluded relaxation are offered the opportunity to rent a private house.
  • If you do not want to spend money on a taxi or depend on the timetable of public transport, then rent a car.
  • For unforgettable boat trips, you can rent a boat or yacht.
  • It is permissible to coordinate with the restaurant chefs a special menu of complex meals.
  • The hotel staff will assist you in organizing banquets and other special occasions.
  • A shuttle service to the airport or train station is available.
  • A rich tour program will be organized for you at the tour desk.




Useful information for guests

The Golden Dolphin Hotel has the following policies:

  • Registration and eviction of guests is carried out after 14:00 and noon, respectively (for an additional fee, the procedure can be changed subject to the availability of a sufficient number of free rooms).
  • The cost of a crib is 100 rubles per day.
  • Extra beds for children under 12 years old will cost 500 rubles per day.
  • Accommodation of adult guests on folding beds costs 1000 rubles per night.
  • The hotel does not accept pets.
  • We accept credit cards.
  • If necessary, you may be provided with an official check for the preparation of accounting or tax reporting.




Golden Dolphin (Adler): advantages

Rest in this hotel is characterized by the following positive aspects:

  • Excellent location near the sea coast, as well as many infrastructure facilities.
  • Spacious, clean rooms.
  • Pleases the presence of a balcony where you can sit, enjoying the scenery, as well as dry towels.
  • Polite and helpful staff at the reception.
  • Good hygiene kits.
  • Pleased with large orthopedic beds.
  • The hotel has a library with a fairly large selection of literature.
  • There is a children's corner in the lobby.
  • On each floor there is a cooler with both cold and hot water.
  • The dining room and restaurant have very polite and professional waiters.
  • Each floor has an electric kettle, microwave, and iron and ironing board.

Hotel "Golden Dolphin" (Adler): reviews. disadvantages

Guests note the following negative aspects of this resort hotel:

  • If the windows of your room overlook the road, it will be quite noisy.
  • Not very hearty breakfast.
  • It is very inconvenient that most rooms are cleaned after dinner.
  • Around there are many cafes and other establishments in which loud music plays until late at night.
  • Very dirty carpet.
  • The rooms are quite dark (it is worth adding lighting fixtures); low-quality plumbing in the bathroom.
  • Maids quite often forget to report hygiene items to the room.
  • Inconvenient access to the hotel.
  • Magnetic locks in rooms do not work well, making it difficult to open the door.
  • Some rooms have a very strong smell of sewage.

The general impression of vacationers about the hotel

Resort "Golden Dolphin" attracts tourists with its convenient location near the sea coast. Also in the immediate vicinity are numerous cafes and restaurants (but this can also be regarded as a drawback, because loud music plays in them until late). Guests enjoy spacious and bright rooms with high ceilings, as well as friendly and attentive service.

Among the shortcomings, it is worth noting not very satisfying breakfasts, as well as a rather inconvenient access to the hotel. Also, the quality of cleaning is slightly affected (the carpet is very dirty and requires serious cleaning or a complete replacement). If we talk about the positive aspects, then this is the presence of balconies with sea views, as well as coolers and microwaves on each floor. In the hall there are children's corners with various toys and other entertainments for kids.
