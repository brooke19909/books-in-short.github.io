Hotel "Agidel", Yalta, Crimea: photos, reviews

In Soviet times, Yalta was the ultimate dream of many vacationers and a real symbol of luxurious relaxation. And today this resort, located on the southern slope of the Crimean Mountains, is very popular among tourists. There are, of course, many good hotels in this city. For example, tourists can book rooms at the Agidel Hotel in Yalta. Reviews of vacationers earned this complex are very good.

Where is the hotel located?

The hotel is located in the center of Yalta. It is located on the famous street. Pushkinskaya, 31. The embankment of the city passes in the immediate vicinity of the Agidel complex. Hotel guests can reach the beach in just 5-10 minutes. The distance between the sea and this hotel is only about 300 m.





Due to the fact that the hotel is located almost in the center of Yalta, its residents have the opportunity to fully use the infrastructure of the city. In the immediate vicinity of the complex there are many various cafes and shops. Also near the hotel there are banks, museums and medical facilities. Tourists also have a good opinion of this complex, and due to the fact that only a 15-minute walk from it is the Chekhov House-Museum. Simferopol Airport is approximately 98 km from the hotel.

Reviews about the hotel and its general description

The Agidel Hotel itself is an old building erected before the revolution. The year of its construction is 1900. Initially, the building of this complex belonged to businessman Sorokin. The hotel was called "Moskovskaya" in those days.

This hotel was last renovated in 2014. The building of the complex looks, in the opinion of its guests, very presentable. The same can be said about the territory of the hotel " Agidel" (Crimea, Yalta) . To the main building of this hotel is literally buried in tropical greenery - from all sides its courtyard is surrounded by a reliable fence. This hotel is oriented, as tourists note in their reviews, including those vacationing with children.

Check-in time at the hotel is 12.00. Pets are not allowed in this complex. Smoking is not allowed in the rooms handed over at the hotel.

Guest reviews of hotel rooms

The rooms for rent at the Agidel Hotel (Yalta) are considered quite comfortable by tourists. The rooms in this hotel are clean and comfortable. But some vacationers note that the atmosphere in the apartments of this recreation center, unfortunately, is slightly outdated. Outwardly, hotel rooms resemble rooms of Soviet hotels of the 60-70s. Of course, to the minuses of the rooms of the complex guests include the lack of air conditioning. The climate in Yalta is actually very hot. And in the season during the day, hotel rooms, according to some guests, can turn into real "murders". The installed fans of the guests save a little from the heat, but still they are clearly not enough.

The pluses of the hotel rooms, in addition to cleanliness, are also referred to by their residents as being quite large. Everything else, and the ceilings in this complex are quite high. Of course, the great advantage of this hotel, according to tourists, is the presence of balconies in the apartments. The guests praise this complex for the opportunity to use free Wi-Fi directly in its rooms.





Agidel Hotel (Yalta): infrastructure reviews

As in any other southern hotel, guests at Agidel can also use parking. This tourists, of course, are attributed to the undoubted advantages of the hotel. Parking is located near the buildings of the complex, which is certainly very convenient . Everything else, and hotel guests do not need to pay for this parking .

Unfortunately, there is no pool on the territory of this hotel . In terms of infrastructure development, this is the tourists attribute to the main disadvantages of the hotel.

For an additional fee in the hotel " Agidel" (Yalta), guests can be offered:

  • excursions;

  • yachting.

Also, many tourists staying at this hotel make paid boat trips.

Works in the hotel "Agidel" (Yalta) and its own laundry. Smoking in the rooms of this hotel, as already mentioned, is prohibited. But especially for lovers of cigarettes in the hotel are equipped with special places. There is also a beach equipment rental in this complex. Tourists consider a big plus of this hotel is that on its territory guests can also use a barbecue with skewers.

What do guests think about food in the complex?

Tourists can rent rooms at the Agidel Hotel with breakfast, board or half board. The food in the dining room of this hotel, according to many guests, is good. The range of dishes at this hotel is quite wide for tourists. Local cooks are delicious, according to vacationers. Portions to tenants at the hotel are served while quite large. In any case, none of the guests of the complex are hungry during the day.

Hotel beach

To reach the sea, guests of the Agidel Hotel (Yalta) need not take too long. All the beaches located next to this hotel are quite comfortable. To get to the sea, hotel residents can, for example, just go along the street. Pushkinskaya to the very end. Here is one of the convenient city beaches of Yalta. The coastline of the sea is equipped in this place, according to tourists, not bad.





Not too far from this hotel are the famous Massandra beaches. Vacationers here can relax with great comfort. Like almost all Yalta beaches, the seashore in this place is covered with small pebbles. For the convenience of tourists, locker rooms and showers are provided here. Tourists can use them for free.

Good beaches are not only in Yalta itself, but also in its suburbs. For a change, the residents of the hotel should go here. For example, some guests of the Agidel Hotel (South Coast of Crimea, Yalta) Otradnoye visit . This is the resort village closest to the city with convenient beaches. The cost of food in the cafes operating in this village is much lower than in Yalta. In Otradnoye, hotel guests can relax in complete silence and enjoy the beautiful nature of the southern coast of Crimea.
