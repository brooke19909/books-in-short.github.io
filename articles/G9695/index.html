Samsung Galaxy Win: user reviews and phone features

At the moment, the mobile device market offers a huge selection, and there are often difficulties. Flagship phone models are simply excellent, but there are people in the world who cannot afford them at all. Smartphone Samsung Galaxy Win is designed specifically for such users. It was announced in the spring of 2013, immediately after the Galaxy S4, to which it is very similar in appearance, in size and design. From a distance of several meters, not everyone can distinguish them. Of course, the filling of the device is inferior to the flagship, and there are no funny excesses, for example, a barometer or a thermometer. But its cost is much lower.





Briefly about the device

So, Samsung Galaxy Win, reviews of which are already appearing, is a classic glossy device from this series. It is half the price of the flagship, equipped with quite decent iron, and its battery is quite capacious. The screen resolution is good enough, the color display is excellent. But the camera in the Samsung Galaxy Win may disappoint.

The phone of this model may be gray or white. The device looks very stylish and attractive. The case is folding. You can easily remove the back cover, it is very thin. The battery is removed. All connectors and buttons are standard. The buttons on the front panel are touch-sensitive, and one mechanical, but the first without backlighting are almost invisible. This can be easily fixed by adjusting their glow for the entire time that the screen is active. The Samsung Galaxy Win phone weighs only 144 grams, and its official dimensions are 70.7 * 133.3 * 9.65 millimeters. The camera protrudes a little, but not too much. There is no LED reporting missed calls.





Insides

Samsung Galaxy Win, reviews of which have already appeared, is equipped with a mediocre processor from Qualcomm today. The quad-core Snapdragon 200 MSM8625Q chipset with a frequency of 1.2 gigahertz each in combination with an Adreno 203 video accelerator. The RAM is also quite sufficient - one gigabyte. In real life, such iron works quite normally, does not slow down, performance only pleases. There are eight gigabytes of internal memory, of which five in most devices of this model are available for storing user files. Samsung Galaxy Win, reviews of which are quite eloquent, supports memory cards up to 32 gigabytes. In this model you will not find a light sensor, as well as such pleasant excesses as a thermometer and a barometer, however, there is a compass, an accelerometer, and a proximity sensor.

Communication

The specification indicates the speed of mobile 3G Internet 7.2 megabits per second for reception and 5.76 for transmission. This is considered not too high indicators, but most operators do not support high speeds, so it will be quite enough for use. 3G is available only for the first SIM card and it cannot be redirected programmatically to the second, it is only necessary to physically change their places. This can be considered one of the shortcomings of the Samsung Galaxy Win i8552, reviews of which will show the rest of its characteristics. The device is equipped with single-band Wi-Fi with an average sensitivity, that is, in this case there is nothing to complain about. There is also Bluetooth 3.0. The smartphone connects to the computer via USB only in MTP mode.

Samsung Galaxy Win Operating System

User reviews speak about the convenience of using this device, because the Android 4.1.2 Jelly Bean axis, which is quite fresh for the fall of 2013, is installed here with the addition of traditional elements from Samsung. Battery

The Samsung Galaxy Win phone, which reviews may be interesting, is equipped with a 2000 mAh battery. According to the results of the work, it is at the S4 level, although at some points it even surpasses it.
