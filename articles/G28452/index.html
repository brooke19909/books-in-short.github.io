The best Anapa hotels for families with children: review, rating, reviews

To find the best hotel in Anapa for families with children, it is not necessary to explore dozens of different sites in search of a suitable option. Especially for our readers, we have collected all the necessary information in one material: the name of the hotel, its brief description, the range of services provided, as well as the opinions of customers who have already managed to rent a room in it. The rating was compiled taking into account the price-quality ratio based on the opinions of guests who left their reviews on the official website of a particular institution.

Place 10 - Hotel Mandarin

The last place in our rating of Anapa hotels for families with children is occupied by this institution, which knows the level of three stars. The modern comfortable hotel is ideal for business or sports tourism, as well as family vacations with children. You can find the hotel near the new dolphinarium, which is located quite close to the central city beach. In the dolphinarium for children with developmental disabilities, a rehabilitation program is provided, which is based on a unique method - dolphin therapy.

You can rent a room for a child with a stay in Mandarin for 1,500 rubles, but in the winter the hotel often holds various events, so this figure is almost halved. For guests provided quality service and a huge number of different services for an additional fee. For example, you can hire a nanny for a child, your own concierge, who will deliver heavy suitcases to your room or bring necessary things from him while you are sunbathing by the pool. The hotel also has a service for hiring a children's animator, which guests love to mention in their reviews. Well, a colorful playground with a huge amount of entertainment will appeal to any child.

Most of the guests respond positively about the Mandarin Hotel, although some claim that with a similar cost of rooms (a suite will cost 3000 or more) the service could have been better. That is why the hotel is only in tenth place on our list.
