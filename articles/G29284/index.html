A bit of history

Before you begin to study the reviews of the Nokia 105 Dual SIM (2017), let's get acquainted with the history of this brand. The Finnish company began operations in 1896. In the early stages, she was engaged in the production of paper. After several decades, an additional direction was launched - rubber products. Electronics production opened only in the 80s of the last century. Products began to be in demand, so a separate division was allocated for this direction. Mobile phones manufactured under the Nokia brand almost immediately became popular. Owners of such devices consider them legendary. Surprisingly, some devices still work today. Finnish products are also very fond of the domestic buyer.

Unfortunately, the company's activities have not always been very successful. The recession occurred around 2010. It was at this time that smartphones began to appear on the mobile device market. Almost immediately, manufacturers such as Apple and Samsung took the lead. Their popularity is explained by the fact that they took into account current trends and were able to quickly rebuild. But the Finnish company missed this point, and its products began to gradually give way to leading positions. Of course, smartphones on the Windows Phone operating system appeared on the market, but they could not withstand the competition. It even got to the point that the production of phones in general became unprofitable. Then it was decided to sell a division of Microsoft. But this did not save the situation. Gadgets began to be produced under a different brand, but they were lost in a wide range.

In 2016, the right to release phones under the Nokia brand was acquired by HMD. In order to try to regain its former popularity, in 2017 smartphones on the popular Android OS were released. The time will tell how successful this decision was, but the fact that the manufacturer does not forget about this category of customers who prefer to use simple push-button phones definitely deserves respect. Indeed, at the moment there are so few such models that users simply have no choice. So, let's look at the characteristics of the Nokia 105 (2017) and reviews about the model left by real people.

Also, Nokia 105 is perfect for connoisseurs of retro tubes. But still, a large part of the target audience is the elderly. The fact is that the manufacturer tried to take into account in full such moments that maximize ease of use. We are talking about convenient keys on the button keyboard, a simple menu, large font. Many users pay attention to the fact that there is no need to make complex manipulations to make a call, and this is an extremely important criterion for the elderly. Keyboard

The Nokia 105 (2017) receives most of the positive feedback thanks to its user-friendly keyboard. It consists of a control panel and a digital unit. The owners focused on the material. Rubber keys are quite convenient to use - the finger does not slip when pressed, the stroke is clear. The control panel, which is located immediately below the screen, displays three large buttons. Two of them, located on the sides, are designed for two positions. The one on the left side is used to unlock (top) and receive a call (bottom). In the center there is a joystick. He works on five positions: "OK", right, up, left, down. To the right of it is a button with which you can end the call (lower part) and go back, being in the menu.
