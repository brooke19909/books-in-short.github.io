Popular clubs Perm

At night, many party-goers are not interested in sleeping in a warm bed, but dancing in clubs. Which establishments deserve attention? Let's look at various nightclubs in Perm.

"Milk" - an original club for youth





This is a rather ambitious institution. Here people with different interests can relax at the same time. There is a main hall, as well as VIP areas that can accommodate up to fifteen people (at least four). Tables, depending on preferences, can be pre-selected, ordered. The furnishings and service are on top. Visitors are offered an interesting show program, the best DJs perform . The wine and cocktail menu is great here, and the dishes are delicious.

The club operates on Friday and Saturday at night (from eleven in the evening to six in the morning).

"Adam and Eve"

Describing the best clubs in Perm, you should pay attention to this place. This place is perfect for outdoor activities. Mostly the club is visited by young people. The institution operates from Wednesday to Sunday.

The design of the club is bright, modern. The hall is designed for 360 guests. There is a VIP zone and two bars. Every Thursday, student-parties are held. For student cards, admission is free.





The menu includes dishes of European and Japanese cuisine. A large assortment of wine and beer pleases visitors. The club has face-control, as well as dress-code. Near the institution there is parking. Therefore, visitors can easily leave the car there.

Vintage Cafe Club

Describing Perm clubs, you should pay attention to Vintage. In this place you can relax and dance to pleasant music. The establishment consists of a bar, a VIP lounge and a main lounge. The interior of the cafe club is made in vintage style.

The music in the institution is good, the lighting effects are original, the show program is interesting. The club also hosts theme parties.

The menu is varied, the dishes are delicious. Desserts deserve special attention. Also extensive are cigarette and wine lists. Draft beer and coffee are also served. Birthdays are given a 10 percent discount on the menu. The institution operates from Monday to Saturday.

"Zaya Bar"

What other clubs are interesting Perm? A popular destination is Zaya Bar. Here you can relax. Here, visitors are offered aromatic hookah, good hits and exciting show programs.

The interior is stylish. The club hosts fiery parties. The premises of the institution are quite spacious. Here is the modern DJ equipment, stylish sofas, a stage and a dance floor.





Dishes in the restaurant are aromatic, tasty and mouth-watering. Designer cocktails are quite original.

"M5" - a club for those who love relaxation to the maximum

Where else can you have a good time? Considering the clubs of Perm, it is worth stopping at a place called "M5". Here visitors will find a maximum of original cocktails, good music, interesting shows.

The club is located in the city center. Bright parties are regularly held here, accompanied by performances by top DJs. The interior of the club is bright, there are a lot of neon lights. There are large bar counters, as well as secluded sofa areas.

The menu in the club is quite extensive. There are various snacks (hot and cold), desserts, as well as Italian, Japanese cuisine. All food is appetizingly prepared, beautifully served. The bar has a large assortment of alcohol. The club prepares original cocktails that will please not only the taste, but also the price.

Sugar is an institution for those who like a good rest

In this club you can get a lot of vivid impressions. It has a comfortable bar area and a beautiful dance floor. Face control is quite friendly. The show programs in the institution are interesting, and themed parties are quite exciting.

Sahara serves Japanese and European cuisines. The club offers an extensive cocktail menu. Also, guests are treated to draft beer on site. Served in the club "Sugar" and fragrant hookah, which is liked by visitors.
