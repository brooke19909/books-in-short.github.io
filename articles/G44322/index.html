Hotels in the center of Madrid: an overview of the best options, a description with photos, reviews

Madrid is rightfully considered one of the most beautiful cities in the world. This colorful capital of Spain annually attracts thousands of tourists. They flock here from everywhere to witness the unique architecture of the city, combining medieval buildings and modern business centers, huge parks and museums. Of course, most of the attractions are located in the central part of Madrid. Therefore, tourists prefer to look for a place to stay here. An overview of the best hotels in the center of Madrid can be found in this article. At the same time, we consider not only luxurious and expensive complexes, but also inexpensive hotels that even those tourists who want to save on a trip can afford.

The best Madrid city center hotels

The ability to get to the main attractions of the capital in a short time is one of the main advantages of accommodation in the center. Most of the unique cultural monuments are within walking distance from each other. In addition, in the central regions, tourism infrastructure is better developed. Luxurious restaurants and inexpensive cafes, huge shopping centers and small souvenir shops, nightclubs and bars are open at every step. But there are also disadvantages. Choosing the central part of Madrid, many tourists forget that even at night its streets are quite noisy. And the cost of living, even in budget hotels is a bit, but overpriced.

Hotels in Madrid, in the center, can be found completely different. Cheap hostels, comfortable three-star hotels and luxurious complexes are located here. At the same time, there are most of all the latter, since business people, businessmen and other categories of tourists who are not used to saving on their living prefer to stay in the center. Such hotels boast exquisite interiors, spacious rooms with modern facilities and first-class level of service. Some of them even have their own outdoor pools, where you can swim in between excursions.

What hotels in the center of Madrid should you pay attention to? Judging by the reviews of tourists, the following complexes are the best options for accommodation:

  • Hyatt Centric Gran Via Madrid 5 *;
  • Westin Palace Hotel 5 *;
  • Hotel Urban;
  • Gran Hotel Ingles;
  • Cool Rooms Atocha 4 *;
  • Intur Palacio San Martin 4 *.

We will tell you more about each of these hotels below, and also consider the reviews of tourists about staying in them.

Hyatt Centric Gran Via Madrid

This complex, according to tourists, is one of the best options for accommodation in the capital of Spain. It is located on Gran Via, which is considered the main city artery of Madrid. 150 meters from the hotel there is a metro station from where tourists can get to any city sights. Like the other best 5 * hotels in the center of Madrid, this complex boasts spacious rooms with an elegant design. All apartments here are equipped with plasma TVs, air conditioning and a minibar. The hotel has a restaurant where daily breakfast, laundry, a bar, a 24-hour gym and its own business center are served. There are also rooms in the complex equipped with amenities for people with reduced physical activity.

Tourists choosing a hotel in the center of Madrid quite often stop here. This complex has an excellent reputation. In reviews, guests especially praise its location and comfortable furniture in the rooms. The complex is located on a busy street, but the noise practically does not prevent guests from sleeping, as its rooms have excellent sound insulation. The main disadvantages of this hotel, they are considered the lack of Russian-speaking staff and slow wireless Internet.

Westin palace hotel

If you are looking for the best 5 star hotel for your stay in central Madrid, be sure to pay attention to this luxurious complex. It is located in the heart of the city - the "Triangle of Art", which also includes several museums, including the famous Prado. The hotel itself is located in a unique historical building, built in 1912. It offers tourists more than 400 spacious and luxuriously furnished rooms decorated in a classic style. The apartments are equipped with antique mahogany furniture, a safe, air conditioning and a plasma TV. The hotel has outdoor parking, a business center with 15 conference rooms, a laundry room, a gym with a sauna and a solarium. For tourists with children, a special club for kids is equipped, where professional educators work. Guests can dine at the Victorian-style Asia Gallery Restaurant. It is considered one of the best restaurants in Madrid. Drinks can also be enjoyed at el Bar del Palace.

Tourists, as a rule, are delighted to stay at this 5 star hotel in the center of Madrid. In the reviews, they primarily praise its design. The pompous and luxurious complex, reminiscent of the royal palace, is unlikely to leave anyone indifferent. Guests also respond favorably to the location. They liked the spacious rooms with a large bathroom, and the helpful staff. The only negative they consider the high cost of accommodation, which does not even include breakfast.

Hotel urban

This designer hotel is centrally located a few blocks from the Prado Museum and other significant Madrid sights. This complex offers its guests a total of 96 spacious rooms. On its territory there is an outdoor pool, car parking, gym, laundry, bike rental and business center. Its four conference rooms are not only equipped with modern computers and projectors, but also decorated with objects of Egyptian art. The hotel's restaurant serves the eminent chef of Madrid. It specializes in Mediterranean dishes. The complex organized an exhibition of cultural monuments of Papua New Guinea. She works on an ongoing basis.

Tourists also often respond positively to this Madrid hotel in the city center. Most of all they liked the location, as all the sights can be reached on foot in a few minutes. The guests appreciated the clean and spacious rooms, as well as the friendly hotel staff. They did not like the uncomfortable shower in the bathroom and not the best soundproofing in the rooms.

Gran hotel ingles

This luxurious five-star hotel in the center of Madrid is suitable for travelers who appreciate unique Art Deco style. It occupies a historic building, built in the 19th century, and is located near Puerta del Sol. The main popular museums of Madrid are within walking distance. All rooms, including rooms, are decorated in an Art Deco style. In addition to the bedroom, each apartment has a spacious guest area with a large flat-screen TV. The hotel has a restaurant and cocktail bar, a wellness center and meeting rooms for business meetings. The price usually includes breakfast.

Hotel guests liked the level of service offered. The reviews they praise the helpful and attentive staff of the hotel, as well as the location of the complex and its design. At the same time, tourists were disappointed by the lack of drinking water in the living rooms, which is not included in the price.

Cool Rooms Atocha 4 *

If you want to stay within walking distance from the main attractions of the city, it is not necessary to overpay and choose luxurious complexes for accommodation. There are 4 star hotels in the center of Madrid that offer comfortable accommodation at a lower cost. For example, the Cool Rooms Atocha 4 * hotel, which is located a few blocks from Puerta del Sol, will be a good option. It offers its guests spacious apartments decorated in a classic style. Each room is equipped with large beds, a TV, air conditioning, kettle and coffee maker. All apartments have a spacious bathroom. At the hotel, tourists can swim in the pool, which is open only in summer. You can eat in the restaurant, specializing in Mediterranean cuisine.

Judging by the reviews, this hotel was liked by tourists who stayed in it. First of all, they praise spacious and stylishly decorated rooms, but they warn that not all of them have a good view from the window. The guests appreciated the highly qualified staff who are fluent in English, and a variety of breakfasts included in the price.

Intur Palacio San Martin 4 *

Four-star hotels in Madrid in the center are practically not inferior in quality to luxury complexes, but at the same time they are much cheaper. Another good option for accommodation will be the Intur Palacio San Martin 4 * hotel. According to tourists, its main advantage is its location, ideal for families with small children. The fact is that Madrid is a rather noisy city, so finding a good and quiet hotel in the center can be problematic. The Intur Palacio San Martin 4 * complex is located on a small pedestrian street, so even in the afternoon it is calm and calm. Opposite the hotel is an old monastery, and Puerta del Sol is just a 5-minute walk away.

The bright and spacious rooms of this hotel are decorated in a classic style. In them you can find large beds, plasma TV, air conditioning. They are housed in a historic building. Its sophisticated lobby is decorated with marble and gilded splashes. The highlight of this hotel is the old elevator, which still functions perfectly. The price includes breakfast served at the buffet in the restaurant, which specializes in international cuisine.

This hotel is considered one of the most popular options for living in Madrid. Guests were delighted with the design of the complex itself and its rooms. In the reviews, they indicate that the hotel has a favorable location and is really quiet at night. They also liked the delicious breakfasts and the helpful receptionists at the reception.

Cheap hotels in the center of Madrid

In fact, tourists can settle in the central part of the city without spending a huge amount. On the streets of Madrid, a large number of budget counterparts are open. Of course, you should not count on first-class quality of service while living in them. But if you need an inexpensive place to sleep, then such hotels will be your ideal option.

The budget option for accommodation, of course, are hostels. As a rule, for a low cost they offer only a bed in a dormitory room. Hostels are usually located on the upper floors of residential buildings. Guests are not offered a private bathroom, and the room has to be shared with strangers. Therefore, this option is not suitable for all tourists.

Many quality budget hotels are open in Madrid in the center. 3 star hotels are a good option for those who do not want to stay in a hostel, but cannot rent a room in a respectable hotel. Such complexes offer guests a small living room with a bathroom, and sometimes a balcony. Also, a restaurant and other infrastructure facilities are often open on their territory.

What is the best budget hotel in the center to choose when traveling to Madrid? We list below the most popular options for inexpensive accommodation:

  • The Walt Madrid 3 *;
  • Room Mate Alicia 3 *;
  • Hotel Avenida Gran Via;
  • B&B Hotel Madrid Centro Puerta del Sol;
  • Hotel Mirador Puerta del Sol 2 *.

Each of these budget hotels will be described in more detail below.

The Walt Madrid 3 *

Many tourists ask: "Recommend an inexpensive good hotel in the center of Madrid." You can recommend them the guest house The Walt Madrid 3 *, which is located near Gran Via. It is only 150 meters away from it, so if you wish, you can walk to it in just 5 minutes. This hotel has a unique design. The classic interiors are furnished with acid shades, giving the rooms a unique look. Each room has not only a set of furniture, but also all the necessary amenities, including a TV, safe, minibar and air conditioning. Guests enjoy complimentary wireless Internet access. Some rooms have open balconies and terraces. Guests can enjoy the on-site bar and fitness center. In addition, Russian-speaking staff works here.

In the reviews, guests say that they liked this hotel. Judging by the responses of tourists, it is considered one of the best options for inexpensive accommodation. Guests of the complex appreciated its modern rooms with comfortable bathrooms. They also liked the helpful staff. They did not like the monotonous food, which quickly bored.

Room Mate Alicia 3 *

Often, 3 star hotels in the center of Madrid are in no way inferior in quality to luxury hotels. For example, pay attention to the Room Mate Alicia 3 * complex, located in the Literary Quarter within walking distance from the Art Triangle. It occupies a five-story building, which houses 34 rooms. Their interior was designed by the famous designer Pasqua Ortega. Living rooms are equipped with small plasma TVs, safes, minibars and air conditioning. From furniture in them you can find not only beds and wardrobes, but also desks, comfortable sofas and dining sets. The hotel has a lounge bar. Not far from it is a car parking.

Tourists respond positively to this three-star hotel, noting that it offers a first-class level of service. The rooms here are spacious and are regularly tidied up. The staff is helpful and friendly, and the lounge bar serves delicious breakfasts. The disadvantages of tourists include the lack of doors in the bathroom.

Hotel Avenida Gran Via

Many 3 star hotels in central Madrid are close to the city's attractions. Hotel Avenida Gran Via, located next to Gran Via, was no exception. A metro station is 100 meters away. The hotel rooms have a modern design and all the necessary equipment for relaxation. In the bathroom, tourists can use the shower with a hydromassage nozzle. The complex has a restaurant serving breakfast, which is included in the price.

This hotel is very popular among picky tourists who are looking for an inexpensive place to stay in the city center. In the reviews, they note that the hotel has not only advantages, but also disadvantages. For example, in living rooms, sound insulation is poor and the floors creak heavily. Nevertheless, they like this hotel because of its excellent location and polite Russian-speaking staff.

B&B Hotel Madrid Centro Puerta del Sol

This small 3 * hotel in the center of Madrid is suitable for those tourists who are looking for an inexpensive place to stay. It is located within walking distance of many attractions, but practically does not offer its guests additional amenities. The hotel rooms are equipped with spacious showers and comfortable beds. In the evenings, you can watch TV or use the free internet. The price includes breakfast served in a small restaurant open on site.

Hotel Mirador Puerta del Sol 2 *

Finding a cheap hotel in the center of Madrid is real. But you need to pay attention to the condition of his rooms, as sometimes you can meet not the best quality options. Even two-star hotels can offer an acceptable level of service. For example, Hotel Mirador Puerta del Sol 2 *, located a few tens of meters from Puerta del Sol. Its small rooms are equipped with everything necessary for a comfortable stay, including televisions, air conditioning and hairdryers. Hotel guests can relax in the restaurant or in the shared lounge with comfortable soft sofas. In the reviews, tourists indicate that this hotel is not inferior in quality to three-star hotels. The cost of living here is significantly lower.

Thus, if you wish, in the center of Madrid you can settle in both a luxury and a budget hotel. In this article we talked about the most popular options for accommodation in each of the categories of hotels.
