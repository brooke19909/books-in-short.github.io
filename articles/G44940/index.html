Where to go on a wellness vacation? Solotvino (Transcarpathia, Ukraine)

On the eve of the holiday season, all people are puzzled by the question of where to go. In addition to popular options (warm seas, abroad), there is another direction - medical tourism. To improve their health, people choose the Dead Sea (Israel), the Caucasus with numerous sources of mineral waters, and the Hungarian baths. But there is a place much closer that can give both treatment and relaxation: Solotvino is a village located in Ukrainian Transcarpathia. Other names for it are Slatina, Aknaslatina, Maramoroshskoy Solotvino, Slatinski Doli.



Amazing corner

So, the place where we spend our virtual vacation is Solotvino. This is a very picturesque area: on one side the turbulent river Tisza flows its waters, and on the other, the mighty slopes of the Carpathians rise. Solotvino is an amazing, albeit a very small village, located near the state border of Ukraine with Romania. A modest, at first glance, village is considered to be a true Transcarpathian health resort, since it has a whole complex of thermal salt lakes. The settlement itself arose a very long time ago, and solely salt-diggers lived here with their families. You can learn about its development from the expositions of the Museum of the History of Salt Mines.

Salt in ancient times was valued dearly, but because the owners of the mines were very influential people. When the supply of resources was exhausted, the place was chosen by doctors who are developing salt treatment methods that have no analogues. One of the most popular is the speleotherapy method used in the local allergological hospital. With its help, minor disorders of the respiratory system and more serious illnesses (tuberculosis, bronchitis, asthma) are treated.

Entertainment

In addition to the abandoned salt mines, another vacation is also popular in the village: Solotvino has several reservoirs on its territory that can easily compete with the Dead Sea in terms of water saturation with useful substances. And from the bottom of the ponds you can get healing mud, which should be smeared with the whole body and allowed to dry. Such a mask makes the skin very smooth, heals wounds and relieves inflammatory processes in tissues.

What are the other attractive Carpathians? Solotvino, a vacation in which will help restore vitality, is considered the cultural center of Ukrainians, Hungarians and Romanians. At the end of July, every year in the village is the ethnic festival "Melodies of Salt Lakes." The scene of the festivities is the shore of the largest salt lake Kunigunda, where they set up a large stage for performances.

Travel Tips

What else is worth knowing for travelers who are going on vacation? Solotvino, despite the fact that annually receives thousands of guests from all over the world, is still not as famous as the resorts of Hungary. There is not enough state support, advertising, infrastructure is not well developed. However, there is more than one recreation center. Solotvino has a wide selection of establishments where you can relax in comfort, and also offers small wooden houses, ideal for families with children or a large, friendly company.
