The basic rules and goals of the game "Scrabble"

The main goal of the game is to score more points than the opponent, making words from the chips. There is a variant of the game in which the winner is the first to score the number of points agreed in advance.

According to the rules of the game "Scrabble", from 2 to 4 people can take part in the round, depending on the size of the set - for more players will need more chips. Those who are not sure of the possibilities of their own memory, it is better to stock up on a piece of paper and a pencil to write down the composed words.





The list of all the rules of the game "Scrabble" is not great. It includes the following items:

  1. Words should be composed in two directions: vertically - "from top to bottom" and horizontally - "from left to right."
  2. Each participant is randomly given 7 chips before the start of the round.
  3. The player making the first move must put the inscription through the central square of the field.
  4. After each move, the player gets spent chips.
  5. All subsequent words are composed only in a "bundle" with already laid out.
  6. If the player does not want to make a word, in turn, he should change the chips and skip the move.
  7. The participant who used all 7 chips in one move receives extra points.
  8. A universal chip ("dummy" or "joker") can be used instead of any letter of the player‚Äôs choice.
  9. The participant can replace the "dummy" with the required letter, provided that he uses it already in the next turn.
  10. The above rules may be changed or supplemented by prior agreement of the players.

Point 10 gives the participants room for their imagination and makes the game even more interesting.

We count points in the game "Scrabble" correctly

Scoring in the game is not easy. Each player should keep a record of the words laid out per turn and the number of points scored. On the field there are specially marked areas that increase the value of the chips. The rules of the game "Scrabble" for scoring can be formulated as follows:

  1. The value of the letter doubles on the green cells of the field and triples on the yellow ones. Extra points are called bonuses.
  2. The amount of the move consists of the cost of the laid out letters and is calculated for each player individually.
  3. If one of the word tokens is located on the blue cell, the word value doubles, on the red - triples. First, the bonus is calculated for the letters, then for the word as a whole.
  4. If a player used all 7 chips during the course, he receives a bonus of 15 points.
