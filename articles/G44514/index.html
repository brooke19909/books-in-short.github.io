DIY toy from improvised materials on the Christmas tree: master class

If you need a Christmas tree toy, you can make it easily and quickly with your own hands from improvised materials. Even the child will cope with some of the options suggested in the article. Ideas are considered from the simplest and most accessible to more complex.

Spider Web Balls

The most traditional DIY toy from improvised materials for the New Year is made in the form of a sphere. The technique may vary.





The simplest option that anyone can cope with is lightweight openwork jewelry made of threads. They are suitable not only for a Christmas tree, but also just for interior decoration. They are done like this:

  1. Inflate the balloon to the desired size.
  2. Dip the threads (wool, floss, acrylic) in PVA glue (you can use Vaseline).
  3. Wrap them around the surface of the balloon.
  4. After drying, pierce the ball with a needle and remove it.

A simple but beautiful DIY toy made from improvised materials on a Christmas tree.

Balls decorated with fabric, ribbons and beads

A more complex version of decorating the spheres is that various decorative elements are glued to the finished base.





The sequence of manufacture of such a toy is as follows:

  1. A tennis ball, for example, is wrapped in a piece of cloth, forming a flat surface (possibly without wrinkles).
  2. At the top, bandage the material with tape, ribbon or thread. It turns out something like a bag.
  3. The knot can be hidden under a beautiful bow.
  4. Decorate the ball with decor (sequins, beads). You can perform this step until the shape is fit at the very beginning, if it is more convenient for you.
  5. Do not forget to make the suspension.

It turned out a beautiful Christmas tree toy. With your own hands it is easy to make such original souvenirs from improvised materials. The main thing is that they can be done quickly.

Pompons: animals and balls

Nothing special is required to complete this decor: only threads, cardboard and scissors. Most often, pompons decorate hats and are usually made round. From such fluffy balls you can easily collect a sheep, a chicken, a snowman or any other hero.



Original obtained pompons collected in the form of berries, fruits and vegetables. The principle of creating a fluffy ball is this:

  1. From thick paper or cardboard, cut two rings. The diameter of the hole, as well as the outer size, depends on what size pompom you want to get.
  2. Put the resulting cardboard blanks on top of one another and begin to evenly wind them with threads. You can use different colors in one product. If the inner layer is yellow, for example, then this color will be inside the pompom. You can also wind orange in the upper half of the ring, and white on the bottom, you get a ball of two different hemispheres. Motley will come out if you randomly wind different shades of thread.
  3. Insert the thread (which can then be used as a pendant) between the cardboard blanks, gradually cutting the threads along the ring, pull it into a knot.

To get an object of a different configuration, the cardboard base is made not in the form of a ring, but, for example, by an arc. After manufacturing, an object can always be shaped by cutting the threads in the right places. Do the required number of blanks, connect them together. Unusual DIY toy from improvised materials for the Christmas tree is ready.

Bumps

Of course, they are not always at hand, however, if the winter is not too snowy, they can be picked up in December. From pine and spruce cones make both balls on the Christmas tree, and other original toys. You can build a symbol of the year or a snowman, previously painting the material in white. Large single pieces decorated with a bow and pendant look very nice. Golden or silver paint gives a special aesthetic. To get animal figures, cones are connected with plasticine or glue. Such a DIY toy from improvised materials (photo below) is not made long. In this case, the materials are also natural, natural. Even a small child can make ornaments from cones.

Textile fantasy

In this technique, both Christmas tree decorations and ordinary toys can be made. With your own hands from improvised materials for boys and girls it is very simple to make an unusual and budgetary gift. You will need the following things:

  • tissue patches;
  • threads, pins, needles, glue;
  • decorative elements.




Ideas are chosen the most diverse:

  • balloons;
  • Christmas trees of simple form;
  • snowmen;
  • cars;
  • vegetables and fruits;
  • candy.

There are two ways to realize the idea: a planar two-sided figure or a three-dimensional figure, which in fact is an ordinary soft toy. In the second case, material for packing is required, so it is easier to use the first option. The sequence of actions is as follows:

  1. Pick up shreds and harvest from them simple forms for your toys (two copies each).
  2. Decorate the base with decor.
  3. Sew both sides together.
  4. Make a pendant in the form of a loop from a ribbon, braid or thread.

If you need an original DIY toy from improvised materials for boys, take the idea with a typewriter:

  1. Cut the overall contour of the machine from the fabric of the same color.
  2. Glue or sew prepared particles in the form of windows.
  3. Use large buttons as wheels. If you add cords with grommets, windows with Velcro, you get not only beautiful, but also a very interesting craft.

Quilling

It is a technique for twisting paper strips. From the obtained elements, both planar openwork and three-dimensional objects are glued together. The following things can be done:

  • herringbone;
  • snowflake;
  • ball (circle with a pendant);
  • bow;
  • bell;
  • snowman;
  • animals (uncomplicated form).

Of all the possible materials, metallized mother-of-pearl designer paper looks best. Use the usual color (from children's kits for creativity or office). Special strips, already prepared, are also sold, but they are not cheap.





If we talk about fast production at home, then it is quite possible to use the old color magazines. If necessary, the sheets can be painted. Do the following:

  1. Cut the paper into strips approximately 5 mm wide.
  2. Using a knitting needle, twist the rings and any other elements. Use glue to fix the ends.
  3. From the obtained blanks, assemble the desired shape.
  4. Attach a loop for hanging.

An elegant DIY toy made from improvised materials on the Christmas tree.

Origami

This technique is probably the most complicated of the ones presented here, but it allows you to make quite original decorations: from balls to animal figures. Most importantly, they turn out to be voluminous, and for their production only paper, scissors and a suspension element will be required. Of course, finished toys can also be decorated with additional decorative elements: beads, bows, lace. Then you will also need glue. A toy, made using improvised materials in this technique, will require perseverance and certain skills, but they come with experience. Such a thing can become not just a children's craft, but also a great gift souvenir.



So, you met with several options for making Christmas decorations. Each of them is interesting in its own way and deserves attention. Choose your favorite idea and start the creative process. Children will be passionate about creating their New Year's masterpiece. Adults will also like it. Do-it-yourself Christmas tree toy made from improvised materials is usually quick and easy.
