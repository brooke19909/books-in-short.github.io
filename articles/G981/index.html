Dalakfes - insurance company and its features

Dalakfes is an insurance company in Vladivostok. She has been providing her services for over 25 years. The project interacts with the largest enterprises and residents of the region.

about the project



Among the clients of the Dalakfes insurance company are the largest logistics and transport companies, Far Eastern Bank, Sea Trade Port, Siemens Finance. The company conducts large-scale corporate projects. The company insures property of residents, AUTO-CASCO, CTP. Payments are made quickly and in a timely manner.

Services

Dalakfes Insurance Company provides payments for illnesses and accidents. Cooperation with it allows you to protect the means of land, rail, water and air transport. Cargo, property of legal entities and citizens are insured. The company protects against financial and business risks.

Liability insurance of owners of water transport, vehicles. The company interacts with organizations operating hazardous facilities.

Liability insurance is provided in case of harm in the form of a shortage of services, work or goods. The project financially protects contractual obligations. The company provides liability insurance in case of harm to third parties.

Location





Next, we present the addresses of the Dalakfes Insurance Company. The central office is located in the city of Vladivostok, on Verkhny Portovaya, 40a. An additional branch is located on Svetlanskaya street, 147. Residents of the village of Wrangel can contact the local office of the company. It is located at 23. Vnutriportovaya Street, 23. The company has branches in Artem, Ussuriysk, Nakhodka and Yuzhno-Sakhalinsk.

History





The Dalakfes insurance company has been taking its roots since 1992. It was then that the constituent assembly decided to establish Akfes-Avtoekspert CJSC as part of AKFES, a large holding. The first insurance policy was issued to Credo Bank. The company received a license from the Ministry of Finance to conduct its activities.

In 1993, the first ship was insured. Soon, the project received its current name - DALACFES. The company was issued a license from the Federal Service for Supervision of Insurance Activities.

In 1994, DALACFES became an open joint stock company. Soon an agreement was signed with the international company International Adjustres. The shareholders of the project are Far East Bank and Vostochny Port. The East Siberian representation in the city of Ulan-Ude begins work. The next branch appears in Yuzhno-Sakhalinsk.

In 1995, the management of the Dalakfes Insurance Company was trained at New York College. Soon, a reinsurance contract was concluded with the foreign company ARIES Insurance Agency. Representatives of the company participate in an international conference in Vienna.

In 1996, a General Reinsurance Agreement was concluded with Swiss Re. Engagement begins with Munich Re. In 1997, the legal separation of the company from the AKFES holding occurred. The first hull insurance payment was made - by ship.

In 1998, the Vladivostok Commercial Sea Port became one of the company's shareholders. The authorized capital of the project increased to 3.5 million rubles. In 1999, Transek Ltd. is among the shareholders of the company.
