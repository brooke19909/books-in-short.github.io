Hotel Les Art Resort: traveler reviews and photos

The Moscow region more and more often surprises gambling lovers of a country vacation with a variety of opportunities for a pleasant pastime. More recently, in 2012, in the neighborhood of Dorokhovo, a small comfortable village, several buildings of the luxurious LES Art Resort 4 * appeared - a modern hotel complex. Almost immediately, the institution became one of the most visited suburban hotels near Moscow. If we decipher the abbreviation, which in Russian sounds like (free time, events, spa), then the name Leisure, Events, Spa says a lot to future guests of the institution. And thanks to the three letters at the beginning of the phrase - LES - for smart tourists it immediately becomes clear that they will not only have a rest in the hotel, but in the woods.





Hotel Territory

Six buildings of the complex - the main and 5 bedrooms - are located near the Minsk highway among the dense birch and spruce thickets that successfully protect the LES Art Resort 4 * from gases, noise and dust. If we compare the hotel territory - more than 20 hectares - with famous historical sites, then it can accommodate about eight Red Squares or two Petersburg Summer Gardens. In addition to well-maintained buildings, 10 cozy villas are open for vacationers, “Laguna” - a modern complex with a swimming pool, “Belvedere” - a banquet hall, - a ski, bicycle and other sports equipment rental center. Here a tiny chapel is modestly "hiding" among the trees. Guests will also enjoy leisure and entertainment on an artificial pond, a football field and three playgrounds - a nursery, a barbecue and a sports one.

Villas

Vacationers at LES Art Resort will not have any problems choosing a comfortable room. Hotel guests who prefer rural colors can stay in one of 10 two-story wooden villas, each of which is designed for simultaneous accommodation of no more than 11 people. In order to become the master of any cottage for one day, the total area of ​​which is 170 m², it is necessary to pay at least 25,000 rubles, which for a group of 8-10 people is considered quite a democratic cost.





Hotel rooms

In the sleeping quarters of the hotel, tourists are expected to relax in spacious and stylish rooms. Both in each of the 250 rooms and in the two-story cottages, guests can use modern equipment, pieces of furniture and landscaped rooms:

  • spacious bathroom, shower or Jacuzzi;
  • telephone and minibar;
  • small safe and air conditioning;
  • a set of spa cosmetics;
  • flat screen tv;
  • a desk;
  • comfortable armchairs;
  • free internet.

Living conditions at the hotel will suit even picky tourists who, based on their desires and capabilities, can choose a room of a certain category, which differs from others in terms of the total area. If the "standard" is 25.5 m², then the "luxury" has four varieties:

  • “Panoramic” - 33 m²;
  • “Ordinary” - 28.7 m²;
  • “Improved” - 36 m²;
  • “Classic” - 31 m².

Daily hotel accommodation can not be called cheap - from 6500 to 8100 rubles. and more expensive, depending not only on the category or type of windows, but also on the specific month or influx of tourists.





Dining at the hotel

The hotel LES Art Resort received positive reviews for the food. Guests of "LES Art" note several advantages that they get in comparison with similar institutions of the Moscow Region, choosing this particular hotel. First of all, in the LES Art Resort 4 * All Inclusive hotel. Not many hotels in provincial Russia can “boast” of such a system, so popular with tourists who are used to relaxing in the resorts of the Turkish Riviera. In LES Art Resort 4 * "All Inclusive" primarily involves three meals a day, rich in a variety of low-calorie, but tasty dishes, mainly in a hotel restaurant. Although tourists can have lunch or dinner at several other hotel establishments:

  • The “Gallery” will delight you with the romantic atmosphere created by musicians who play the piano or violin soulfully. The taste of exquisite dishes of Italy and France successfully harmonizes with the melodies in the restaurant. The institution is also famous for its desserts and diet menus.
  • In Dali, a small and inexpensive art cafe, connoisseurs of the work of the great Spanish master of brush, whose last name is a cozy place, enjoy relaxing. But ordinary visitors, looking for a quiet chamber environment for friendly communication, become its frequent guests. About Salvador Dali here are reminiscent of reproductions of the famous canvases of the famous surrealist hanging on high walls.
  • The “Podium” is exactly the restaurant where the guests will find the buffet included in the All inclusive, or, as they usually call it, a buffet. Tasty dishes of fruits, herbs and vegetables, offered here most often, will not let vacationers gain kilograms of excess weight.
  • The open bar, located in the spacious lobby, also applies to All inclusive. Guests enjoy tasting a variety of beer or wine brands. Young travelers do not pass by ice cream and confectionery.
  • Barbecue enthusiasts will be pleased with the unexpected opportunity to have picnics even in winter. For this fun lesson, several heated gazebos are built on a special site.
  • At 5 O'Clock, the main bar open in the evening and at night, you can not only enjoy an original cocktail, but also treat yourself to a cigar or hookah.

To order a dish or drink in their room, hotel guests only need to dial four digits - 0033 by internal telephone. You just need to remember that the request will be fulfilled if vacationers are willing to spend at least 500 rubles.





What awaits business people at the hotel?

The success of any modern conference or important seminar directly depends on how well equipped the venue for the event is. LES Art Resort receives rave reviews in this regard. The hotel will not disappoint demanding businessmen who choose it for leisure, business meetings or banquets. Here, the doors of a spacious conference area with four halls are open - large and small, which accommodates from 52 to 105 people. The lobby is usually used for short breaks to have a hot coffee or a bite to eat.

Here, on the third floor of the main building, is located "Olympus" - a multifunctional amphitheater with 515 comfortable chairs, lighting and sound equipment and two screens. He successfully plays the role of the stage not only for business performances or reports, but also for concerts of alternative and popular music. The hotel has a small building "Belvedere" - a banquet hall, which often hosts heated discussions or corporate evenings.

A cozy atmosphere is always waiting for guests in the Winter Garden, also located in the main building. Both in it and in another banquet hall located above, democratic receptions or business meetings are often organized. All rooms, except the amphitheater, are easily transformed, adapting to the number of participants in the events and the goals set by their organizers. To rent any of these halls for one day, you will need from 10,000 to 30,000 rubles.





Health, sport and beauty

Hotel LES Art Resort (Moscow region) will certainly suit those who can not imagine a single day, even on vacation, without physical exertion and sports. During the summer, guests enjoy cycling around the picturesque surroundings. Moreover, here, in the zone of mixed forests, you can enjoy clean air and the absence of annoying noise of big cities. In the winter months, cycling is successfully replaced by skiing. A rental point will provide everything you need for fans of active pastime, and it’s completely free.

Vacationers in the “LES Art Resort” are not limited to skiing or cycling, but enjoy using all the leisure and sports facilities available to them:

  • The large pool accepts swimmers of different ages from 9am. Almost all day it is full of amateur athletes and ordinary fans of water fun. Only at 9 pm its 50-meter paths become inaccessible to vacationers. Kids can not bother their caring parents and splash around on an artificial reservoir specially fenced off for them.
  • “Laguna” - a separate pavilion - offers a relaxing holiday in two baths - Russian and Finnish. Here you can have a bite to eat and have a beer in the small bar. Swimmers have fun in the heated pool built next to the Laguna, where even in cold weather the daredevils happily splash in warm water, admiring the snow-covered firs and birches standing around. In the winter months, an artificial pond located nearby turns into a good skating rink, next to which low slides for tubing “grow”.
  • Two summer playgrounds - football and sports - will suit fans of active games. In the warmer months, competitions are often organized between gambling vacationers. Inveterate football players play on their site and do not interfere with volleyball players and basketball players having fun in the neighborhood. Fans of minigolf, which is gaining popularity in Russia, are not far behind them. Their field is not so crowded and noisy, but the aristocratic game attracts tourists with the opportunity to escape from everyday worries and feel like relaxing in some luxurious foreign resort.
  • Inside the main building, guests will find two spacious rooms - for yoga and for training on all kinds of sports equipment. Thanks to professional instructors, vacationers can choose for themselves the program that will give them the desired result without harm to health.
  • While men build muscle or entertain themselves with bowling in a separate room, women take care of their beauty in a well-equipped spa. A relaxing environment is created here with the help of sounds unexpected for this place: the singing of tropical birds or the roar of a mountain waterfall. Thanks to a scented shower, hot tubs, a walk through the cascade of mini-pools and many other procedures, demanding women are satisfied with the rejuvenating effect, a surge of energy and the excellent mood that they maintain for the duration of the vacation.




What can young tourists do in the hotel?

Children resting in the LES Art Resort, the photo of which you see in the article, together with their compassionate parents, will not be bored and languishing in anticipation of something interesting and unusual. They are invited by the Magic LES, a multifunctional entertainment center located on the third floor of the main building. While teenagers compete on the tracks of the race track or enthusiastically play funny air hockey, the kids have fun in the labyrinths of slides, tunnels and ladders.

Especially for young clients, several programs have been developed in the spa. Here, young fashionistas and fashionistas can change their hairstyles, find out the delights of massage, or simply swim in a small pool decorated with colorful balloons. The virtual world of games is available for fans of this entertainment in a separate room. Someone with pleasure goes on star travels full of surprises, others boldly fight in intergalactic wars on the side of the forces of extras, and still others defeat the enemy in chess battles.

Especially bright and fun are events organized by professional animators for the birthdays of young guests. With the help of contests, sweet cakes, colorful fireworks, and many gifts, an atmosphere of a magical holiday is created, long remembered by young tourists. But on weekdays the fun does not stop. Even classes at LESENKA, a creative studio, are not boring. In a play environment, thanks to experienced innovative teachers, children learn new knowledge about the world and try their hand as future masters of the brush. In general, parents are happy that their children have something to do, and in the meantime, they can just relax.





Non-sports entertainment

A fairly spacious hall for playing bowling, beloved by the Russians, located on the third floor of the main building, can accommodate up to 70 fans of this fascinating activity. Here, on four tracks, real battles often unfold between the teams of vacationers. Representatives of business circles especially love this entertainment. Indeed, thanks to bowling, they not only relax, but also learn to work in a team, it is not without reason that psychologists consider this team-building game to be a team-building game.

Here, for fans of the "pyramid", "American" and other varieties of the game of billiards, 6 tables are set. Some especially gambling vacationers do not leave the hall until three in the morning, when it closes, and they are asked to come the next day. Young people prefer evenings in Six - a small stylish club where up to 90 people relax to rhythmic melodies. Energize with cocktails at the bar. On the spacious dance floor, the establishments are always fun and crowded, especially during corporate parties.

How to get to the hotel?

The most suitable way to quickly get at the door of the hotel complex "LES Art Resort" is considered to be a taxi or private car ride from Moscow.An insignificant distance has to be overcome from the Moscow Ring Road - only 70 kilometers. Future hotel guests travel along the rather busy Minsk highway to cozy Dorokhovo, where at the monument to Z. Kosmodemyanskaya you need to turn left. Here begins the road that goes past the hotel.

Another landmark is the car service building located opposite the monument - a car wash, a shop and a gas station. You have to drive no more than one kilometer and turn at a high stele pointing to the hotel. Travelers choosing electric trains depart from the capital's Belorussky railway station, from where the trains go to the same Dorokhovo. From there, guests will have to travel either by taxi or on foot. Almost all vacationers prefer a trip to LES Art Resort (Dorokhovo) by car.
