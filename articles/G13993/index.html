How can I get to

And finally. Have you decided to visit Kashtaksky Bor - a recreation center in Chelyabinsk? How can I get to? Very simple.





You can get here by bus No. 42, having reached the stop "Anosova Street", then - 510 meters to the Youth Town. Bus number 42 goes from the fleet to the village of Kashtak. He drives off every 20 minutes.

Bus number 44 starts moving from the second Paveletskaya street (ChMK) and moves to the village of Kashtak. Interval of movement - once per hour.

Minibus number 10 from the Palace of Culture Vostok goes to the stop "Street brothers Kashirin." Next - 1.8 km to Chelyabinsk boron.

You can get from the city center by car along the Sverdlovsk highway, turning right (75K-213) onto 1 Maya Street. After driving 2 km and turning right, exit onto Sanatornaya street (450 m), to the left - 690 m. The sanatorium complex is on the left side.
