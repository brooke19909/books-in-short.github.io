Nearby Attractions

Nha trang

It is the largest tourist city with a population of over 400,000. In Nha Trang, there are not just many different tourist excursions - there are a lot of them. Any tourist can choose a trip to their taste and wallet. Fortunately, excursions in Nha Trang are not expensive. Tours for tourists can be chosen both in the city and beyond.

Dam Market

Only having visited the city market, you can feel the whole atmosphere of life in this country. Here you can see with your own eyes how the Vietnamese people live and what they eat. On numerous counters, your eyes will open a huge number of different exotic fruits, the name of which for a Russian person even to pronounce will be problematic. Prices for goods do not bite, so even a tourist with a not very tight wallet will be able to afford a lot.

Pearl jewelry is very popular on the market. It is impossible to take your eyes off earrings, rings and beads made of this stone.

Also on the market you can find silk products. Vietnamese silk is very popular far beyond the borders of the country. Here you can see items of clothing and interior made of this fabric. Particular attention should be paid to silk paintings. They are of incredible beauty, made exclusively by hand and reflect mainly the natural world of Vietnam.

In addition to national exotic goods, various clothes and shoes are sold in large quantities on the market. Here you can find branded items at incredibly low prices, however, the quality will be more like Vietnamese.

When purchasing goods on the market, remember that it is possible and necessary to bargain here, but it is better to do it with a smile.





Pagoda Long Son Pagoda

A few kilometers from the Trang Long Nha Hotel Trang Hotel 3 * is one of the main shrines - the Long Son Pagoda pagoda. This place must be visited by every tourist who visited Nha Trang. The most beautiful temple was built in the 19th century. The area around the pagoda is very picturesque, and calm and grace reign in the air. Upstairs from the pagoda are stone steps directly to the 14-meter statue of the White Buddha. This platform offers a beautiful view of the city. Needless to say, this is a favorite place for photo shoots of many tourists.

A large number of local residents and monks of the temple walk across the territory of the pagoda, who kindly tell all vacationers about this place.

It is worth mentioning that the entrance to the territory is free, like all excursions in it. The only place you can spend your cash is the donation box. Know if someone asks you for money for an excursion or entry - these are scammers.

Nagar Towers

At the very edge of Mount Ku-Lao towers Po Nagar. It is hard to believe, but all the buildings of the complex are more than 1000 years old. This place is considered sacred to the Buddhist people, it is not without reason that religious festivals are held here. If you decide to go inside the towers, then you should take care of the appearance in advance. They enter the temple only in closed clothing, without a headdress. At the entrance you need to take off your shoes.

You can get to the Po Nagar tower by renting a car, taxi or bus, which follows route number 4.
