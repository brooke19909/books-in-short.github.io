Bit of history

The first mention of these lands dates back to 1500. Then they belonged to the Orekhovsky district, according to the salary census book of Vodskaya Pyatina. But as a geographical object, the Bugras took shape recently, at the beginning of the nineteenth century.

The toponym "Bugry" is associated with the relief features of the area - here the transition to the elevation of the Karelian Isthmus is very clearly manifested. The estate was located on a hill surrounded by marshy and woodland.

Bugry village: transport accessibility

At the intersection of the Ring Road and Kultury Avenue, the village borders on St. Petersburg. In the vicinity there are several metro stations: in Murino, on the territory of the northern capital - "Prospect of Enlightenment", "Civil Prospect", as well as "Parnas", which distinguishes Bugry (Leningrad region). Bugry, Leningrad region: shopping in the village

There are no shopping problems: near the Ring Road there is Auchan hypermarket, and the Mega-Parnas shopping center is located at the intersection with Engels Ave. In the village there are small convenience stores with a mandatory set of manufactured goods and products. The emergence of a new mode of transport - light rail

In addition to the appearance of the subway, the village of Bugry (Leningrad Region) and the nearest settlements are waiting for another innovation. These are light rail trams.
