Montenegro, island of St. Nicholas: description, beaches

The waters of the small Adriatic Sea are washed by the shores of several states at once: Slovenia, Albania, Bosnia and Herzegovina, Italy, Croatia and Montenegro, but only on the territory of the latter is the largest and one of the most beautiful islands in the world - St. Nicholas, or Sveti Nicoli, or Hawaii. The Montenegrins love him, avid tourists tend to get here, photographers are delighted with the views. Perfectly transparent, turquoise hue of the sea, dense forests and beautiful beaches - all this explains the universal love of Sveti Nicoli.

Montenegro, island of St. Nicholas: attractions

Even on such a small part of the island that was ‚Äúpresented‚Äù to holidaymakers, you can find architectural monuments that are worth visiting. The main architectural attraction of the island is the church of St. Nicholas, the patron saint of sailors. The first mention of it dates back to the 16th century. It was this building that gave the name to the whole island.

However, legend has it that the crusaders built the temple at the end of the 11th century under the leadership of Raymond of Toulouse when they marched to Constantinople. In the Middle Ages, as you know, the plague mowed down a good half of Europe. A pandemic and crusaders could not be avoided - many of them died on the island of St. Nicholas, and the survivors created a cemetery and buried the knights. A nearby church was built, which was destroyed at the time of the 1979 earthquake. In its place, a new church of St. Nicholas was erected. There are also several buildings on the island, but they do not represent either historical or architectural value - these are relatively new buildings.





The beaches of the island of St. Nicholas

The pearl of such a republic as Montenegro, the island of St. Nicholas, has a coastline of about 800 m long. It is divided into 3 parts. The first, as already mentioned, is a conservation area, the second is strewn with pebbles, and the third is sand.

Many are interested in Hawaii-beach (Montenegro). Where did such an unusual name come from? But everything is simple here: this small part of the coastline was so called because the restaurant of the same name is located there.

The beaches of the island are not crowded, but some of them are often visited, while others are ideal for those who crave privacy. Often, residents of Budva come here to take a break from the crowds of tourists and cook their favorite rostil. Rocks, clear turquoise water, dark green fragrant vegetation and silence - what else is needed for happiness?

Island Infrastructure

One of the republics well developed in terms of tourism is Montenegro. The island of St. Nicholas, of course, is also equipped, so vacationers here will find everything they need. Spending time in the fresh air, tourists enjoy their holidays. At the same time, a good appetite wakes up, you can satisfy your hunger in a restaurant. It is located near the beach.

You can also rent an umbrella and sunbed. A rental office is located on the beach. Such a kit will cost about 5 euros. But for the sake of economy, many people take a rug or a simple towel with them and sunbathe on it. Island of St. Nicholas (Montenegro): how to get there?

No problems will arise with this. Regularly boats depart from the Slavyansk beach, which deliver vacationers to the island. They work from 8 am to 6 pm. Ticket price is approximately 3 euros. You may have to call a water taxi, which costs 5-7 times more. Therefore, it is advisable to bring more money with you.

You can get to the island all year long.
