Marina View Hotel 4 * (United Arab Emirates / Dubai): description and reviews

The United Arab Emirates has long been called a real wonder of the world. This dynamically developing country has managed in just a couple of decades to build magnificent cities in the desert with luxury hotels and extensive infrastructure. Millions of tourists come here every year telling incredible stories about the UAE. Most often, our compatriots honor Dubai with their visit. This resort received a huge credit of trust from the Russians, which fully paid off. Among the numerous and diverse hotels of Dubai, we would like to highlight Marina View Hotel 4 *. These apartments cause the most enthusiastic reviews from tourists, so we could not get around them with our attention.





Description of the hotel complex

Hotel Marina View Hotel Apartments 4 * looks identical to most hotel complexes in Dubai. It consists of one four-story building, built, by the standards of the hotel business, just recently - six years ago. Therefore, we can safely say that the hotel is new. In addition, all his guests noted that he was surprisingly comfortable and stylish. And this is important when it comes to the place where people spend their holidays.

Hotel Marina View Hotel 4 * does not have too extensive territory, but near the building there is a huge outdoor pool, where there is a special area for children. In the hotel complex there is a restaurant, a bar and all necessary infrastructure.

It is interesting that the hotel provides its guests with not just rooms, but apartments with a fully equipped kitchen, which is very welcomed by families with small children. Guests can stay in two hundred twenty-four rooms of different categories. There are three at the moment.

Hotel location

Guests of Marina View Hotel 4 * especially noted its convenient location, because many interesting places of the resort can be reached on foot from here. And the distance, which can greatly complicate the walking movement, is easily overcome by public transport, which stops very close to the hotel.

The area where the Marina View Hotel 4 * is located - Dubai Marina. It is considered one of the most dynamic quarters of the resort, where quite wealthy people live. Just a five-minute walk away is a beautiful marina with magnificent snow-white yachts.

The hotel complex is quite far from the beach Jumeirah Beach Residence - the third line. To reach the sea, tourists need to overcome more than one kilometer, but hotel guests claim that this walk takes about fifteen minutes.

The rooms offer magnificent views of the sea and Sheikh Zayed Road. Near Marina View Hotel 4 * there is a metro station, to it vacationers need to walk only five minutes in a straight line. Also in close proximity are numerous shopping centers where you can buy many amazing things from world famous brands.

Travelers also like the fact that you need to get to the hotel complex from the airport no more than twenty-five minutes. For tourists tired after a long flight, this is almost a gift.





Infrastructure

Many guests of Marina View Hotel Apartments 4 * reviews leave about its pool. He is really very big and beautiful. The bowl is made in the shape of a lagoon, so it creates the feeling of being on some incredible exotic island. Near the pool there are sun loungers with umbrellas, which vacationers can use for free.

Adherents of a healthy lifestyle note in their reviews a well-equipped gym. It is quite small - thirty square meters, but has everything you need to keep fit. Our compatriots especially liked the modern treadmills, which are so nice to stretch your legs. After the gym, hotel guests can head to the sauna, where they will relax absolutely free.

At the reception they will help you to solve any problem that arises around the clock. Other facilities offered at the property include luggage storage and a safe. This allows you not to worry about the safety of your belongings, although cases of theft in Marina View Hotel Deluxe Hotel Apartments 4 * are not mentioned in any reviews.

If you need to exchange currency or book excursions, then this can also be done at the reception. There is an exchange office and a tour desk, where professional staff work, ready to pick up absolutely any tour for you.

Businessmen often come to Dubai, so they can use the conference room at Marina View Hotel 4 *. Next to it is a compact business center, where guests get the opportunity to use photocopy and fax machines, as well as check email.

If you need to put your clothes in order, you can turn them in to dry cleaning or laundry. The hotel staff will quickly do all the necessary work and return you perfectly clean and ironed things.

Shops and gift shops are located on site. This allows you to buy everything you need without leaving the cozy walls of the hotel complex. And if you, on the contrary, strive to find out the resort as best as possible, then rent a car and drive around the area. You can choose a car at the rental office, here you will quickly draw up all the necessary documents.





Service

Almost all guests of Marina View Hotel Apartments 4 * (Dubai) noted with great enthusiasm the high professionalism of the hotel staff. The staff speak five languages, including Russian. This makes it possible to feel absolutely relaxed at home at the hotel.

Each guest is given beach towels when someone is going to relax by the pool or head to the sea. This must be notified to the staff and you will immediately bring the right amount of towels.

Wi-Fi is excellent throughout the hotel. But keep in mind that in the rooms for access to the Internet you must pay extra. If you want to save, then use the Internet in a restaurant or lobby.

Every day, all rooms are cleaned, and the quality of cleaning guests appreciate very high. In addition, the hotel can order room service:

  • food and drink delivery;
  • providing fresh press;
  • purchase and delivery of products from stores.

Do not forget that these services are always paid for in a separate account.

If necessary, vacationers can order a celebration in the banquet hall. The staff of the hotel complex will prepare an event program for you and develop a menu. We can say that all organizational issues will fall on the hotel staff, and you just have to pay everything and enjoy the celebration.





Hotel Features

Each traveler, gathering at Marina View Deluxe Hotel Apartment 4 *, should know some features of accommodation and booking. For example, you must be aware that virtually all hotel rooms are non-smoking.

Accommodation in apartments with pets is not allowed, in case of non-compliance with this rule, the guest will be immediately evicted with an impressive fine.

If you reserve an apartment with a debit card, the reservation will be confirmed only in case of full payment of the entire stay. In the case of a credit card, only part of the amount will be debited from you, but one of the guests must be the owner of this card. Otherwise, you will have to go through a rather complicated third-party authorization.





Number of rooms

Each room has an excellent kitchen where you can prepare your own meals. Mostly apartments differ in size and number of rooms. For example, the double rooms in the hotel are only twenty-six. They are the largest, they can accommodate up to five people.

Studios are the most common, of these numbers one hundred seventy-one. They can accommodate a family with one child in an extra bed. These apartments are in great demand among our compatriots.

Fifty-three rooms are one-bedroom apartments, which usually accommodate two adults with one child.

Each of the rooms is well equipped, we will talk about this in the next section of the article.





Description of apartments

Many vacationers noted the excellent soundproofing in the rooms, even with full occupancy it seems that the hotel has no one but you. The apartments are a complete version of the apartment with everything you need for a comfortable stay.

In the kitchen there is a washing machine, a refrigerator, a conventional and microwave oven, a kettle, an iron, an ironing board and even a coffee machine. Daily maids bring bottled water to the kitchen, where many vacationers prepare food.

The room has a telephone, satellite TV, individual air conditioning and a safe. The bathroom is also well equipped with a set of personal hygiene products, a hairdryer and a set of towels with bathrobes.

All of the above is enough to have a great time at the hotel, and not feel anything necessary.

Accommodation in apartments with children

Children are always welcome at the hotel, so kids up to two years old can stay in the room with their parents absolutely free. It is possible to put only one extra bed in the room, it will cost two hundred dirhams a day (1 dirham is 15.42 Russian rubles).

Services for children

It is worth noting that for kids in Marina View Deluxe Hotel 4 * there is no special entertainment, but the staff always treats them with great attention. If necessary, you will be provided with a convenient highchair and a children's menu in the restaurant. If parents need to leave their child in a hotel complex for a while, they can call a nanny. This service is not included in the price.





Nutrition

Hotel guests recall the restaurant with great pleasure; here you can always get a variety of delicious dishes. Mosaic (this is the name of the restaurant) offers vacationers dishes of seven world cuisines:

  • Russian;
  • Indian
  • Mexican
  • Arabic
  • Italian
  • Asian
  • English

Tourists can book a tour with different food systems:

  • breakfast
  • boarding house;
  • half board.

Depending on the chosen system, the cost of living will vary.

Marina View Hotel 4 *: reviews

Numerous reviews from our compatriots will help finalize the opinion of the hotel to potential guests. In general, the hotel complex is rated very highly:

  • pleasantly surprised by the very high-quality housekeeping;
  • the convenience of location makes it possible to quickly reach the necessary shops and cafes;
  • a free shuttle to the beach saves time;
  • delicious buffet food is quite varied;
  • numbers always correspond to the declared complete set.

It is hard to imagine that there were no tourists who were not completely satisfied with their stay at the hotel. Therefore, we cannot ignore the described disadvantages:

  • lack of balconies in the apartments;
  • small area;
  • The restaurant does not always bring hot drinks for dinner.

If you plan to relax in Dubai, then Marina View Hotel will be a great option for any tourist who appreciates comfort and excellent service at a reasonable price.
