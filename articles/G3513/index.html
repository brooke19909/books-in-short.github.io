Corrugated Paper Butterflies: Easy and Simple

This method is similar to the previous one, but unlike the origami artwork, you should not collapse the material. Light, almost weightless paper can be torn if carelessly applied. From thin material of 2 colors, in this case contrasting, we cut out 2 rectangular details, round off the ends. Clamp the middle and tie with a scarlet ribbon. Corrugated paper is perfect for this method.





Cut out the butterfly

Thick paper is suitable for this manufacturing method . On the lines drawn from the wrong side, we separate the figure, then bend vertically in half. It is better to use templates - cutting butterflies out of paper on stencils looks neater.

To this end, some craftswomen buy a set of ready-made butterflies, using them as a basis. Bonding of two parts, differing in texture and size, gives a special charm to the decoration. The top layer of the product resembles lace with a curly pattern.
