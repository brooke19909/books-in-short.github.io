Megaphone "World without Borders": how to connect, tariff package, roaming, Internet, additional services and user reviews

To make a successful trip, you need to think through many details. It is important for MegaFon subscribers to know that international roaming for foreign tours is not connected automatically. This article contains information on how to enable World Without Borders on Megafon and use this option in other countries.

general information

Almost all people like to travel. No matter where the traveler goes, it is important to get new experiences and take a break from the routine. However, on any trip it is necessary to keep in touch with loved ones and relatives, so many people are interested in how to connect the "World without Borders" to the "Megaphone".

This service allows you to significantly save personal funds of users. This is achieved due to the fact that the mobile operator has concluded agreements with various companies that provide communication services in other countries. Therefore, subscribers can use the network of another operator and not change their own phone number. In order to freely use communication services, you need to replenish your account in advance for the required amount.

Service benefits

Subscribers who enable this option can save a significant amount of money on incoming calls during the trip. The service from the operator "Megafon" tariff "World without Borders" is provided on the following conditions:

  • the duration of incoming calls should not exceed 30 minutes per day;
  • calls that exceed the specified limit are paid separately;
  • calls that last more than 30 minutes are payable in accordance with standard roaming rates;
  • the cost of the option is 30 rubles;
  • The cost of a subscription fee is 25 rubles per day.

The mobile operator offers special prices for the Internet access service. The cost of 1 megabyte is not more than 6 rubles. If the subscriber uses the Internet, a traffic packet is connected. The cost and size of this package of services differs in different countries of the world.

How to connect "World without Borders" to "Megaphone"

To activate this service, you must go to the "Service Guide" and dial the combination * 131 #. The user can connect the option in a mobile application or in your personal account. After typing the specified key combination, you must click on the "Call" button. After a few minutes, a notification about connecting the service will come to your mobile phone. Then the subscriber will need to send the word "Yes" to the number 0500978, which will confirm the transaction.

Subscribers can also call the hotline at 0500 and fill out an application for activation of the Megaphone service World Without Borders. Service deactivation

The service "World without Borders" is valid only in international roaming. In this regard, in the home region the option will not be activated automatically. Subscribers need to deactivate the service on their own. To do this, you can use your personal account or mobile application.

The subscriber will need to dial the command * 131 # and click on the "Call" button. After that, it is enough to send an SMS message with the word STOP to number 0500978. Also, this service can be turned off at the Megafon office upon presentation of a passport.

Transition and use conditions

Subscribers who are interested in how to connect World Without Borders to Megafon and other options should be aware of some features of the service. The option is effective immediately after crossing the state border. It is important to know that by day we mean the time from 00.00 to 23.59. This fact must be taken into account if the subscriber is in a different time zone. Other options that are aimed at modifying calls do not interact with this tariff when in roaming. In other words, the subscriber will not be able to connect services that reduce the cost of outgoing calls. However, the user can enjoy free incoming calls, which is a big advantage. The option "World without borders" can be connected both for individuals and corporate.

If the subscriber has disabled the option "World without Borders", then the service can be activated only on the next day according to Moscow time. In the event that mobile communication is carried out through special equipment, the tariff is suspended. The service has no time limit, and if necessary, the option can be quickly disabled.

Payment in roaming

The World Without Borders service implies a daily subscription fee. At the same time, the mobile operator provided the following possibilities to replenish the balance:

  • payment by credit card;
  • function "promised payment";
  • option "pay for me";
  • service from the operator "credit of trust";

The cost of one incoming minute costs no more than 1 ruble, so communication is provided almost for free. Outgoing calls are paid according to the full tariff plan. Free minutes are provided once a day at 00.00 Moscow time.
