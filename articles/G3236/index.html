Xeno Hotels Sonas Alpino 4: the perfect place for families with children

Description. Xeno Hotels Sonas Alpino 4, owned by the Xeno Hotels hotel chain, is located in the village of Mahmutlar, quite close to the center of Alanya, near the famous Cleopatra Beach and the Old Castle. Just a few minutes by dolmusha (local minibus) - and you are already at the bus station of the city, where buses go to Antalya Airport, which is 140 km away. To get to the shores of the Mediterranean Sea, you just need to cross the road. The hotel consists of five separate buildings, five floors each, built in an elegant resort style. The hotel was opened in 1998, the last reconstruction took place in 2005.





The rooms. Xeno Hotels Sonas Alpino 4 has 232 air-conditioned rooms with a contemporary design. We can say that there is everything here: a safe, satellite TV, a stocked minibar (though with paid drinks), a telephone, and a hairdryer in the bathroom. All this set is fully consistent with the declared level of the hotel. The number of rooms is divided into 63 standard rooms (maximum can accommodate two adults and two children, 25 m2), 160 two-room family rooms (living room and bedroom, can accommodate up to 6 guests, because its area is as much as 45 meters), as well as nine three-room "suites" (two bedrooms and a living room, 60 m2).





Nutrition. Xeno Hotels Sonas Alpino 4 operates on an all-inclusive basis. From morning until 11 pm, guests can visit two restaurants: indoor (Turkish cuisine) and outdoor (Turkish and international cuisine). Guests have the right not only to breakfast, lunch and dinner, but also to snacks between meals and, of course, to drinks made in Turkey, both alcoholic and non-alcoholic. Dinner at the Marin restaurant, where A la carte is served and where you can taste delicacies, as well as drinks in the disco bar and imported alcohol in other bars - for a fee. The hotel has five bars: they are in the lobby, by the pool and on the coast.

Beach. Like the vast majority of hotels in this resort village, Xeno Hotels Sonas Alpino 4 is located across from a superb sandy beach. You need to go to it through a safe underground passage. The place of rest is equipped: there is a shower, deck chairs, umbrellas. Those who do not want to break away from the sea and the sun and go to a restaurant for lunch have the opportunity to eat in a cafe on the beach, just a few meters from the splashing waves. On the public beach, a stone's throw from the hotel, you can rent any water sports equipment that your soul desires - parakite, banana, water scooters and bicycles. The hotel has a swimming pool with everything necessary for sunbathing and relaxing by the water. For children there is a section with slides.





Additional Information. The hotel has its own spa center. There is an indoor pool, a hammam, and a Finnish sauna. You can pump up muscles on fitness simulators, and real experts will conduct a massage for you. For a fee, it is possible to pass the time playing Russian billiards or darts. Laundry and ironing, babysitting and doctor services, a hairdresser and a stroller rental are also included in the list of services. You can leave your luggage in a special room under the supervision of staff after eviction from your room if, for example, you want to leave Xeno Hotels Sonas Alpina 4 only in the evening.

Indeed, ideal conditions were created for young guests here: a playground, slides in the pool, a room with toys, and there is also a mini club with animators for them.
