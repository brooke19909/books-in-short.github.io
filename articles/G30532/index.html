Crochet dresses for girls - creating a luxurious summer outfit

Going for a walk with a child, many mothers and grannies take needlework with them. And they do it for a reason. Unnoticed, without much stress, you can create an amazing outfit for a child! Crocheting a dress for girls is not as tricky as it might seem at first.

Straight Spaghetti Strap Dress



The simplest model is a straight silhouette robe. Such a sarafan is made by knitting in a circle without a seam. The craftswoman can choose the drawing at her discretion, it is only important to measure the width of the baby’s chest and perform the first row - the air chain - equal to this size. Of course, crocheting dresses for girls involves expanding the product down. Crocheted bells, pompons, tassels and flowers look great as decorations. After the main part of the product is ready, straps are tied to the top of the bodice. It is recommended to decorate them with "pendants." You can make a set: dress and hat, sarafan and hat. This model is most suitable for very small coquette - up to 2 years.

Beach dress, flared down



Girls from 2 to 5 years old can be associated with another model of summer outfit. Crocheting a dress for girls can include the use of two patterns: a denser one on the bodice and lace for making a fluffy skirt. This outfit with straps looks very stylish. But no less luxurious will look a little fashionista in a sarafan on ties that are fastened with a bow on the back of the neck. Knitted lace products are the most beautiful clothes for girls. In addition, it is convenient in that it is made of natural cotton threads, is hygroscopic, lightweight and does not cause allergies and diaper rash. You can decorate the outfit by contrasting the bottom and top of the dress, as well as the edges of the ties or shoulder straps. Cute cheerful flowers on the bodice in the tone of decoration and a drawstring at its top will turn a sundress into a luxurious princess outfit. You can also use embroidery with beads.

Elegant fishnet dress with a hat



And you can make an outfit in the style of the 50s for the baby. Let the dress be a little elongated, with wide, lowered armholes, with a skirt starting from the armpits. Stylish clothes for girls, even at a very young age, form a good taste for them, make them more confident. And all this together is the key to their successful future.

Nautical Dress

But the most skilled craftswomen can create a real masterpiece, for example, a panel dress. This is when blue-green algae is swinging on leaves on a blue background , smiling octopuses are running along the sandy bottom-bottom, and striped clown fish and funny colorful jellyfish swim across the canvas without any symmetry. Crocheting a dress for girls with a crochet of this model begins with the manufacture of small parts: algae, stones and sand, marine inhabitants. Then the plot of the panel pins on the pattern with English pins . And then with a blue thread the needlewoman “ties” the whole picture, making air chains from one part to another. The dress turns out to be extremely airy and beautiful - to match the little princess for whom her mother, grandmother, sister or aunt tried so hard.
