Lazarevskoye - hotels and hotels: description, selection, reviews

The village of Lazarevskoye is one of the favorite places for tourists on the territory of the Krasnodar Territory. Tens of thousands of people come here every year. They are attracted to well-developed infrastructure. Lazarevsky has a huge number of various shops, cafes, restaurants, night clubs, entertainment centers. There is also a very clear sea and a well-developed hotel business. Tourists leave a large number of positive reviews about the rest in this cozy place. Today we will tell you about the best hotels and hotels of Lazarevsky. After reading the article, you will find out their exact address, features, as well as feedback from visitors.



Hotels in Lazarevsky

Resort towns and villages offer visitors a large number of accommodation options. Lazarevskoye is no exception. Here you can rent a house both in a hotel and in the private sector. But if in the first case you will have certain guarantees of accommodation and your safety, then in the second, various surprises may arise. Therefore, the best accommodation option will be the hotels of Lazarevsky. Next, consider the most interesting options.



"Surf"

If you do not like to ride in stuffy and cramped vehicles to get to the sea, then choose this option. The sea is only ten meters from your place of future residence. Hotel "Surf" has a private beach on which you will be provided with everything you need for your vacation. Many rooms have balconies with beautiful sea views. Here you can choose several accommodation options:

  • Standard double room. There is a minibar, a wardrobe, a desk, upholstered furniture, a TV, a bathroom, beds, and armchairs.
  • Luxury rooms. Here you will be cozy and comfortable. The rooms have a bedroom and a living room, as well as an entrance hall.

Residents can enjoy a carefree life, as the rooms are equipped with everything you need. Among the services that are provided here are: daily room cleaning, shoe shine, ironing, hairdresser, transfer from the train station to the hotel and back. If you come with small children, then for a fee, you will be provided with babysitting services. Hotel rooms cost from 2,300 to 4,000 thousand.

The address of the hotel "Surf" - Pavlova street, 2.



"Calypso"

On Kalarash street, house 2 is one of the most popular Lazarevsky hotels on the seashore. Smiling and polite Calypso staff, as well as modern design, attract a large number of guests. Each room offers magnificent views of the sea, mountains and the village of Lazarevskoye. Near the hotel there are shops, cafes and restaurants. Here you can not only carelessly live and relax, but also take part in seminars and conferences that take place at the hotel annually. What about the number of rooms? Hotel staff will offer you:

  • Standart room. A large, comfortable room in which there can be one or two beds. Each room has a balcony, refrigerator, plasma TV with lots of entertainment and much more. Prices in a hotel in Lazarevsky are from 3,000 to 4,000 thousand.
  • One-room suite with sea view. It can accommodate up to five to six people with great comfort. The room has beds, upholstered furniture, necessary utensils, bathrobe, slippers. You will find a spacious living room, bathroom and a chic bedroom. Price per room - from 5000 to 7500.
  • Two-room suite. Couples love to stay here. Two rooms can accommodate several adults and children with great comfort. Room price - from 6000 to 8000 thousand.

"Lake Divnoe"

The hotel is located in an ecologically clean area, among green trees and beautiful flowers. On the landscaped area there are several cottages that are made from natural materials. Here you will find a large number of services and entertainment for different tastes. For children there are playgrounds and rooms. Fans of outdoor games will enjoy table tennis, paintball, horseback riding, archery and much more. There is also a bath complex, which consists of a large hall, two steam rooms, a relaxation room. Here you can not only relax and significantly improve your body, but also take part in the tea ceremony. For vacationers, several accommodation options are offered: standard rooms, family, luxury and junior suites, as well as VIP cottages. The last option consists of two floors, on which there are several bedrooms, a living room, a balcony, a shower, several TVs, upholstered furniture and much more. Accommodation prices depend on the category of rooms and can range from 2500 to 22000 thousand. Breakfast may be included. The hotel is located at Sochi highway, 2G.



"Orange sun"

Tourists who come to the Black Sea coast love to stay not only in large hotels, but also mini-hotels in Lazarevsky. We will now tell you about one of these places. Here you can feel as comfortable and cozy as at home. The rooms have everything you need. A delicious and varied breakfast is included in the price. The hotel has a swimming pool, near which there are sunbeds for vacationers. For those who came here by personal transport, there is a guarded parking lot. The sea is very close, and the owners of the hotel are nice and good-natured people. There is also a cafe and a playground.

The address of the mini-hotel in Lazarevsky "Orange Sun" is Sochi highway, 8.

"Poplar"

Among the wide variety of hotels and hotels in the village of Lazarevskoye, it is very easy to get lost. Find the most suitable accommodation option will help guests reviews, which on the Internet abound. A nice vacation option can be found at Kalarash, 1B. The hotel is located almost by the sea, and there is a large amount of entertainment nearby. On the ground floor of the hotel there is a dining room where you can eat deliciously, varied, and most importantly, inexpensively. The rooms are always clean and tidy.

"Odyssey"

At the mention of this place, many people have associations with sea adventures. "Odyssey" is a high-quality sanatorium that meets the highest requirements of vacationers. There are more than one hundred and fifty one-room and two-room rooms, as well as several cottages. You can eat in restaurants, of which there are two in the sanatorium. There is also a bar that serves delicious cocktails. For those who want to cook a delicious barbecue, they will provide everything necessary for a barbecue. There is a spa center in which a large number of a variety of wellness treatments are performed. If you come here with children, then you should not worry about what to do with them on vacation. For them there is a special area, which is equipped with swings and slides, as well as a games room. Hotel address - Sochi highway, 28.



Hotel Bristol (Lazarevskoye)

A short walk from the coast awaits you with a great place to stay. The Bristol Hotel in Lazarevsky offers spacious rooms with stylish furniture, balconies, air conditioning, bathrooms and much more. You can eat in the dining room or in cafes and restaurants located nearby. "Bristol" is located on Enthusiastov Street, 10/2.

Helpful staff and comfortable rooms

Reviews of hotels in Lazarevsky are full of enthusiastic emotions. Many vacationers come here constantly. They choose accommodation in hotels of Lazarevsky on the seashore. The reviews point out the good work of the staff, beautiful and comfortable rooms, quality service. Vacationers like the fact that Lazarevskoye (Sochi) hotels have balconies with wonderful views of the sea and mountains.

You can always contact the hotel staff with any question and they will help. And also prompt the most profitable and interesting excursion offers. The issue of nutrition is also resolved at the highest level. Many hotels in the village of Lazarevsky have restaurants or cafes. And if there is a desire to cook for yourself, then the hotel staff will provide you with the necessary utensils and equipment. Pleasant emotions and unforgettable impressions remain in the memory of many people for many years.



Conclusion

Hotels in Lazarevsky offer a large number of the most diverse options for accommodation. Here you will find comfortable and spacious rooms, a variety of hotel services, a high level of service, friendly and experienced staff. But in order to make your stay a pleasant one, remember that it is worth booking rooms in advance. In this case, Lazarevskoye will certainly give you a carefree and unforgettable vacation by the Black Sea.
