Which dishwasher is best in size? Here it is worth relying on what volumes of dirty dishes she will have to cope with. Oversized ones make it possible to wash about twelve to fifteen standard sets at a time, narrow - from 10, and desktop - about 5 sets.

In 1 set of dishes, it is customary to include 3 different plates, 3 spoons, 1 knife, 1 fork, 1 teaspoon, cup, saucer and glass.

Camera design

It is desirable that there are various compartments and containers for washing dishes of different types inside the dishwasher: plates, cups, cutlery and large dishes (pans, baking trays, etc.).

Washing and drying classes





When trying to figure out which dishwasher is best for you, be sure to pay attention to the washing and drying classes.

The quality of the washing depends on what functions, systems, programs and substances are used by the device. An equally important point is the number and shape of nozzles designed to spray water. The smaller their diameter and the larger the quantity, the better the dishes will be washed. Another factor is the pressure and direction of water flows. Depending on the quality of work, dishwashers are divided into several classes, and they are designated literally. Machines of category "A" provide the best washing and preservation of dishes. But the equipment of class "E" is an economical, but questionable in terms of efficiency option.
