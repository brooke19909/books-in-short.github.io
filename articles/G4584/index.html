Cheap hotels of Penza: list, description with photo

People work in different positions and do not know where and how they can be sent, for example, on a business trip. The first thing you should pay attention to is accommodation. Not always accommodation is included in the expense of a business trip. Also, a person can go to another city in order to just relax and get new pleasant emotions. Penza can also become such a city. Where to rent a cheap hotel in Penza (in the center or on the outskirts)?

Where to stay?

It is worth paying attention to the fact that in this city there are a large number of museums, for example, the Museum of one painting named after G.V. Myasnikov and others. Therefore, first you need to ask yourself a question about living. There are a lot of hotels in the city of Penza, however, it is worth paying attention to the most budget options, which will not be inferior in their comfort to the others.

First of all, it is worth mentioning the inexpensive hotel in Penza "Voyage". In a way, it is the cheapest. But besides her, there are many more who can compete with her. And very successful.

"PEOPLE"

You can start this list from the PIPL Hotel. Estimated price will be 1400 rubles per day for a double room. This hotel has a large number of advantages. For example, it is 13.6 km from the airport, and 5.5 km from the railway station.

The location of this institution will allow the guest to enjoy all the delights of this city. Because the hotel is located 6.4 km from the city center. On the territory of the institution there is a sports complex, a sauna, as well as a swimming pool. It will provide guests with comfortable rooms, which include a private bathroom and a TV. For a good rest, guests can order meals in a 24-hour cafe.

It is also worth noting that the last overhaul was carried out in 2016. The list of free services includes car parking, laundry, Wi-Fi, taxi call, jacuzzi and much more. However, it should be noted a very important nuance, which relates to smoking guests. Smoking is prohibited in most of the area. But there are places that are reserved for this lesson.

You can pay by cash or bank transfer. You can rent a cheap hotel in Penza for one night here.

"Emerald City"

The second in the list of cheap hotels in Penza can be called the Emerald City. The price per day in a double comfortable room is 1888 rubles. The hotel is located 9 km from the city center and 6 km from the bus station. The airport is 16 km away. There is a restaurant on its territory, each guest will have access to a real Irish breakfast. This breakfast will be prepared and delivered to your room exactly at the time indicated by the client.

The staff is also very friendly and ready to help the tourist who is staying in Penza for the first time. Workers can help plan a vacation program for the entire period of time. A very important information for each person may be that an advance payment is not required, and you can pay for the reserved room immediately on the spot. Therefore, there is the possibility of free cancellation.

The room includes air conditioning, free Wi-Fi, a washing machine, free parking, a pool, all bath and toiletries.

"Friday"

A little cheaper, but not inferior in its amenities to the previous one, will cost a room in the Penza inexpensive hotel Friday. The fee for a double room is 1600 rubles per day. The cost of a single room is 1200 rubles. Rooms here are rented for seven days. As in the previous hotel, there is the possibility of a free cancellation. The airport is 7 km away.

In room: two single beds, TV, free Wi-Fi, washing machine and much more. Breakfast here is paid and will cost the visitor 120 rubles. On-site parking is available. Food is not a mandatory service, a person can have breakfast and lunch outside the establishment.

Since this inexpensive Penza hotel is located close to the city center, the view from the window is simply wonderful. For people who have some physical limitations, a shared double room offer may be appropriate. The price for renting one berth will be 800 rubles per day. This room is located on the ground floor. Due to the fact that the price has been reduced, there are no certain services in this offer. For example, the room does not have a TV.

"Old city"

Do not disregard the hotel "Old City". It has many rooms of various classes - standard, luxury, economy. If a person is fully confident that he will take a previously booked room, then you can opt for a single room of economy class, the rental fee of which will be 2500 rubles per day. However, it is worth noting that in this case, prepayment is required, and if the client refuses or for some reason does not call the number, then the reservation fee will not be refunded. To prevent this kind of dangers, you can choose to choose a room of the same class, but the price will be 2700 rubles per day.

Air-conditioned room with a flat-screen TV, free Wi-Fi, a safe and a refrigerator. If the guest has pets, this can be a problem, as pets are not allowed. The disadvantage, which clearly stands out among the other options, can be called the lack of parking.

"Swallow"

Hotel "Swallow" will be able to please its guests with comfort and low fees for the services provided. In this institution, food is not included in the room rental price. Therefore, if for a person its presence is not fundamentally important, then you can stay at this institution. Guests can order their food in the restaurant, which is located on site, or in the vending machine.

The price for renting a single room will be 1,500 rubles per day. Distance to the airport - 12 km. Free parking is available. The room has a balcony, TV, refrigerator. An elevator is available to access the upper floor. Guests can visit the solarium and relax in the garden. Also, those who come to Penza as a tourist can use the services of a tour desk. If necessary, you can use the laundry service, but this is not included in the room rental price.
