Beach holidays in Vietnam: best places

Despite the fact that tours to Vietnam by Russian tourists are not as common as trips, for example, to Greece or Turkey, it should be noted that this country offers travelers an excellent opportunity to enjoy a vacation on the beach for a low cost of trips. Every year more and more people appreciate this, as a result of which the influx of guests on the coast increases. A beach holiday in Vietnam is a unique combination of exotic nature, a warm mild climate and a high level of service.

Getting here is quite simple, air transport to Hanoi and Ho Chi Minh City is sent weekly from Moscow and St. Petersburg. Moreover, if the period of stay in the country will be no more than 2 weeks, a visa for Russians is not required. A beach holiday in Vietnam is full of vivid emotions and impressions. Here, tourists are provided with a huge number of pastime options. So, relaxing on the beach goes well with interesting excursions to the sights, thanks to which travelers have the opportunity to get to know the interesting culture of this exotic country.





For those who are going to Vietnam, the beaches and their location will be quite interesting. For example, experienced travelers recommend visiting Nha Trang. The sandy beaches in this area are clean and comfortable, and the climate is quite mild. Mui Ne Village in Phan Thiet is popular with windsurfers.

Among other places that tourists have chosen, it should be noted Vung Tau, Danang, Dalan and others. These resorts differ from each other, primarily the services and entertainment provided. Here are hotels with developed infrastructure, and secluded bungalows on the beach, and places ideal for diving.





For those who choose a beach holiday in Vietnam, the photos of tourists who have been here will be quite interesting. Also, before the trip, you should familiarize yourself with the climatic features of the regions, as the northern regions are cooler. The best time of the year to spend a beach holiday in Vietnam is autumn. At the same time, diving in Nha Trang is better from October to April. Those tourists who have chosen the island of Phu Quoc are recommended diving from November to May. In addition to various corals, there is a very rich underwater world, you can see various mollusks, exotic fish, octopuses, barracudas, etc.





Fans of beautiful landscapes should visit Halong Bay. This place is one of the most popular among tourists. Travel agencies often organize one- or two-day excursions here, which include a tour of the city of Haiphong and a boat cruise.

A beach holiday in Vietnam is an ideal option for those travelers who want variety and visiting new places. The hotel business in the country is quite developed. The resorts have luxury hotels and budget accommodation options. At the same time, the level of service in hotels is quite high. And local dishes, like drinks, will surprise and delight gourmets.
