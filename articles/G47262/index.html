LCD "Birch alleys": location, description

Moscow is a huge metropolis, attracting a large number of people. Many move here for permanent residence, someone is looking for an opportunity to earn money. All people need to live somewhere. Those who allow the budget, tend to buy an apartment in the city. One of such proposals from the developer is LCD "Birch Alleys". Location of the complex

The project developer is D-Invest, which began the construction of a large-scale project. For the implementation of the project, LCD "Birch Alley" Moscow allocated a land plot located in the city, in the North-Eastern Administrative District, at the intersection of Agricultural and Birch Alley. According to official data, the deadline for putting the complex into operation was set by the developer for 2018.





As for transport accessibility, it will be provided by two nearby highways: Yaroslavsky and Altufevsky. LCD "Birch Alleys" is located almost exactly in the middle between them. The distance from the city center is about ten kilometers. Nearby is the Botanical Garden metro station and many public transport stops.

Infrastructure internal and external

The first floors of buildings, as in many other new buildings, it is planned to give under non-residential commercial facilities. It will house a bookstore, fitness center, hairdresser, cafe.





Owners of their own vehicles will be pleased in the future with an underground parking for 960 cars. It will help to use its roof under the yard space as functionally as possible. It will house children's playgrounds and sports grounds, a recreation area for children and adults. Fiber-optic Internet and television lines will also be held. Security of the residents of the complex will be ensured by CCTV cameras and an electronic access system installed around the perimeter and at the entrances.

As for the external infrastructure, the location of the complex in the city gives its residents the opportunity to use all the social infrastructure facilities that are located in convenient accessibility. These are several schools, gymnasiums, kindergartens. About a few minutes by shuttle bus are large chain supermarkets. At a distance of less than one kilometer there is a botanical garden, which significantly improves the ecological situation of the area and allows residents of the complex to enjoy relatively fresh and clean air, as much as possible in a huge metropolis.
