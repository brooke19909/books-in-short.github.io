Hotel "Rio Bambu", Dominican Republic: photos with description, service, reviews of tourists

The Dominican Republic is a country with coconut palms, the pristine Caribbean Sea, pristine beaches. It is here that the resort of Punta Cana is located, where vacationers from all over the world often come to enjoy tranquility, silence and nature.

There are many diverse hotels. One of them is the Rio Bambu. In the Dominican Republic, this hotel is famous for its good service, comfort and amenities.

Address and Location

The five-star Riu Bambu Hotel is located amidst a palm grove on the first coastline of the Arena Gorda in one of the beautiful places of Punta Cana. The hotel is located at: Playa Arena Gorda, Dominican Republic, Punta Cana 23,000. The nearest airport is 19 km away. from the hotel. And the second La Romana airport is located much further, almost 60 km from the hotel.

Not far from the hotel are the famous beaches of Bavaro Beach, where the coral sand does not heat up in the sun, so you can lie on it without worrying about the lack of sunbeds.

Only 600 m from the hotel there are entertainment in the form of boat tours and water sports. Nearby is a water park where not only adults, but also children of any age can come.

Alternatively, you can drive just 3 km to the Escape Room Punta Cana (Quest Room), where guests and their children will have a great time. The institution is open from 10:00 in the morning until 00:00. As a rule, visitors are here no more than two hours.

Territory

The hotel has a lobby bar where guests can relax and unwind after a busy day. For sports enthusiasts, the hotel provides a gym, fitness center, tennis court and a large pool. In addition, guests are invited to the banquet hall to celebrate any occasion.

The hotel has free Wi-Fi, but guests are not very happy with its speed. Therefore, you can buy the Internet during your stay. Its cost is quite acceptable.

If vacationers came with a child, you can visit the water park, where there is a lot of entertainment for any age. It has slides not only for toddlers, but also for older children. Yes, and adults will find entertainment for themselves. In addition, there is a playground for children right on site.

During the entertainment of the child, parents can relax on the terrace and watch their baby.

Service

As for the service staff, so it is at the highest level. Room cleaning is daily and of high quality. Maids change linens and towels at the request of guests.

The concierge helps with any matter. The staff speaks several languages, including Russian, which is important for Russian-speaking clients.

Dry cleaning and laundry services are available on site. However, these services are not included in the price of the permit and are paid separately. Parking is available for guests. One of them is paid - guarded, and the second - free.

If parents have nowhere to put their baby, you can always order a separate service - a nanny. Only qualified workers are hired, so parents can be calm about their child. The nanny will look after the children for as long as necessary.
