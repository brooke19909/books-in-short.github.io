Hotels in Bialystok (Poland): overview, description, reviews of tourists

Poland (Eastern European country) is famous for its architecture and history. Traveling to Poland is a great option for tourism at any time. Everything is developed here: beach vacations, skiing, city vacations. Whatever option you choose, you will definitely find what to do with yourself.

Today we will talk about the city of Bialystok. Bialystok is a city in the north-east of Poland. This is an important cultural center of the country. It carries a centuries-old history and great value. Sights of Bialystok are famous all over the world. If you come to this city, you will definitely not regret what was done. It is worth saying that Poland is Europe, so hotel prices here are high, and the quality is not always consistent. That is why today we will talk about the six best hotels in the city of Bialystok. Compare prices, find out reviews and analyze the pros and cons of each.

How to get to Bialystok?

People often ask how to get to Poland. Many are not attracted to the version of the aircraft. They choose a bus. If the bus leaves from Russia, then in most cases it travels through Belarus. The transplant takes place in the city of Grodno.

Many are interested in how to get from the city of Grodno to Bialystok. The answer is simple - by car or by bus. Minibus "Grodno-Bialystok" walks often. Travel time is only 2 hours 46 minutes. On such a bus you arrive at the bus station of the city, located near the center. The advantages of the Grodno-Bialystok minibus are that the fare costs less than 10 euros (700 rubles), the buses are new and quite comfortable. This is not the only option. From Grodno to Bialystok can be reached by car, the travel time is slightly less than two hours.
