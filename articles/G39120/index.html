Guest House "Acropolis" (Kabardinka): description, features and reviews

The village of Kabardinka is located on the Black Sea coast near the resort Gelendzhik, very popular among domestic tourists. Soviet citizens began to come on vacation to this southern settlement back in the distant 20s of the last century. And today, Kabardinka is visited annually by thousands of tourists. Problems with finding housing for vacationers who decided to relax by the sea in this village practically never arise. Hotels in Kabardinka built a lot. For example, the Acropolis Hotel located in this village deserved pretty good reviews.

Where exactly is

This hotel was once built near the center of Kabardinka. The distance from its gate to the coastline is approximately 500 m. That is, the sea is not too far from the complex, and its guests do not have to go to the beach for a long time.





In the immediate vicinity of the Acropolis Hotel (Kabardinka), there are such elements of the village infrastructure as a market, a grocery store and a minibus stop. A popular entertainment center for tourists is a 10-15 minute walk from the hotel.

The tourists who rented rooms at this hotel fly to the Black Sea, usually to Gelendzhik Airport. If desired, the hotel, among other things, you can order a transfer. Hotel staff can deliver guests to the place for a fee of tourists from the city's railway station.

Beach Hotels

In order to get to the sea, guests of this hotel need to walk along a very beautiful green alley. In total, the road to the beach from the buildings of the Acropolis hotel takes no more than 7 minutes for vacationers.

The coastline in front of the complex is covered with small pebbles. So sunbathing on the rugs is quite convenient here. Regarding the cleanliness of the Seti beach opposite the hotel, there are mostly only very good reviews. And the sea itself in this place is transparent and very warm during the season. People on the beach of the hotel usually have a lot of rest.
