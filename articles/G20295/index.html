Adygea: sights created by nature itself

The Republic of Adygea is located in the northwestern part of the Caucasus Mountains, and it is surrounded by the territory of the Krasnodar Territory. Three rivers flow in this region - the Kuban, Belaya and Laba. The capital of the republic is Maykop. In administrative terms, Adygea is divided into 7 districts and, in addition to the capital, has another city of republican significance - Adygeisk. More than 100 nationalities live on the territory of the republic, the main of them are Russians and Adyghe. Crystal clear rivers, picturesque Caucasus mountains, forests, plateaus, caves - which Adygea cannot boast of. Sights of the region attract here not only Russian tourists, but also guests from other countries.



General information

This region has been inhabited since ancient times. The first mention of Adygea was noted in the 5th century BC. e. At that far time Adygs lived on this territory, it was in honor of this people that they gave the name to the republic. In addition, the Great Silk Road passed through the region.

In 1922, Adygea was founded as an autonomous region. Since 1991, the Adyghe Autonomous Region of the Soviet Socialist Republic was transformed into a republic within the Russian Federation.

Adygea: what to see?



The republic is visited annually by thousands of travelers. They come here to climb mountain peaks, swim in the rivers, go snowboarding and skiing, and just walk along mountain trails. But let's talk about everything in order.

Adygea Mountains

The Republic of Adygea is famous for its charming mountain peaks. In sec. Hamyshka is Mount Monk. According to legend, a monk lived on its top for a long time, in his honor they decided to name this place. On the highest eastern tier of this mountain is the entrance to the Jolas Cave. During World War II, the doctor Jolas hid here.

Mount Trident is another miracle Adygea is so proud of. The sights of the region are exclusively a merit of nature. And although these are actually three peaks, the locals combined them into one.

Not far from the Athos St. Michael's Monastery is Mount Fiziabgo. From its top you can have mercy on the snow-white mountains of the Caucasus Range and the monastery itself. The slopes are also famous for their underground passages and man-made caves.

Here is the highest point of the Lago-Naki plateau - Mount Fisht (2868 m). Its upper slope is covered with a large glacier. You can conquer Fisht only if you go along a specially designed tourist route.

It is worth noting that this is not all the mountains that are on the territory of the Republic of Adygea. Sights are even hard to give in to calculus.



Rivers and waterfalls

One of the main assets of the republic are Rufabgo waterfalls. They are located near the village of Kamennomostsky and represent 10 colorful waterfalls, each of which has its own name.

In the same village there is another amazing place - Hadzhokh gorge. Here, the charming Belaya River flows through a deep and narrow canyon.

See also stands on the river Meshoko with the gorge of the same name. To get to this place with unique grottoes and waterfalls is not difficult, many roads of varying complexity lead to it.

The real treasures of Adygea can be called the Kishi River with its many rapids, Lake Psenoda, Przekeski Falls, the Fars and Polkovnitskaya rivers and many others. In a word, in order not to miss anything, you definitely need a map of the sights of Adygea.



Caves

There are more than 15 caves in the region. The most famous of them are: Big and Small Azish caves, Through, Ozernaya, Sheep, Saxophone, Flute, Monastery, Soaring Bird cave, Dakhovskaya cave, Fishta, Duhan and others. Most of them are easily accessible for visiting.

City of apple trees

Of great interest to tourists is also the city of Maykop (Adygea). Its name from Adyghe is translated as "valley of apple trees." The capital of the republic is really buried in greenery and flowers. The real pride of Maykop is the city park, where swimming pools are built. The Belaya River is quite cold even in the summer, so swimming in a warm pool and admiring the lush vegetation of the park is a pleasure. In the vicinity of the capital, you can see the ancient mounds, the most significant of them is Oshad. On the southern outskirts of the city, on the left bank of the Belaya River, is the ancient Maykop fortress, or trype, and a large group of archaeologists are still working on its riddles.

Other attractions



You must see with your own eyes the protected territories of Adygea. For example, the Caucasian Biosphere Nature Reserve, Cossack Stone, Bukreev Arboretum, Novosvobodnaya stanitsa, etc. There are many churches and monasteries in the republic that Orthodox tourists visit every day.

Paleontological findings

Ancient buildings, excavations, monuments - all this can also be represented by Adygea. Sights of antiquity attract travelers. For example, the most visited place can be called the dolmen Hadzhokhsky, it dates back to the 3rd millennium BC. e. A large number of dolmens are also concentrated in the Bogatyrskaya glade and in the village of Ust-Sakhrai.

Huge areas of pristine nature, a variety of landscapes, protected dense forests, amazing canyons, picturesque mountain rivers, alpine meadows - all this attracts many fans of outdoor activities. Rather, see where Adygea is located on the map, and rush to visit this amazingly beautiful region.
