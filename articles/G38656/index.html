How much do prosecutors earn in Russia?

The primary responsibility of prosecutors is to protect the rule of law. They carry out their work in public authorities and represent the prosecution in court. Each state seeks to minimize corruption processes in the country. Without competent prosecutors, achieving these goals is very difficult. Therefore, law enforcement defenders receive a decent monetary reward for their hard work. We will find out how much the prosecutor makes in different regions of the country.

What skills does the position of prosecutor require?

At the trial there is always a person who explains the motives for committing a crime against the convicted person. This is what the prosecutor does. But his task is not limited to the mandatory proof of the guilty offender. With the competent work of a lawyer and the existence of sufficient grounds for the convict's innocence, the prosecutor can finish the process of his charge.

Young people who decide to connect their lives with the prosecutor's office are interested in how much the prosecutor earns. Before voicing this figure, we clarify what knowledge a candidate for this position should have.

  1. He must be well-versed in the field of constitutional law, normative acts, devoted to human rights and freedoms; adopted judgments, court decisions.
  2. Know the labor, military legislation.
  3. Understand the procedures for prosecutors.
  4. Be able to handle service weapons and know the rules for their use.
  5. The prosecutor must understand how to work with information that represents state secrets.

Conducting an indictment trial in court is not an easy and responsible matter. It requires excellent knowledge of the regulatory framework. In addition, the prosecutor must possess business qualities, be able to correctly and correctly deliver his speech.

What determines the salary of prosecutors?

The position held affects how much the prosecutor earns per month. The remuneration of the Attorney General, his assistant and assistant are significantly different.

The salary of employees of the prosecutor's office consists of:

  • salary;
  • surcharges for length of service and for special working conditions;
  • allowances for the current rank and high professional achievements;
  • surcharges for work intensity, academic rank or degree in the relevant specialty.

The amount of allowances in the aggregate may reach 170% of the basic monetary remuneration of law enforcement officers. You can imagine how much the prosecutor makes every month. With a high position and the performance of complex tasks, this figure reaches an impressive size.

Assistant Attorney

This position requires its owner to have excellent knowledge of state legislation. The assistant prepares the main procedural acts for the prosecutor. He has the right:

  • initiate proceedings on certain elements of a crime;
  • give written instructions on the investigation of crimes;
  • refuse to initiate criminal proceedings;
  • check criminal cases and other materials related to crimes committed;
  • to take part in preliminary investigations and inquiries.

In the event of illness, vacation, suspension from duties, the assistant shall replace the prosecutor. This requires certain knowledge and professionalism from him. Therefore, his salary will be decent. In large Russian cities, the monetary remuneration of the prosecutor, who has the right to replace the chief, is about 45,000 rubles.
