Crochet little butterfly: two ways to do it

Any self-made product will look more elegant if it is decorated with some kind of decoration element. Among the huge variety of all kinds of lace ribbons, flowers and bows, crochet or knitting butterflies occupy a special place. This small image is considered a symbol of love, beauty and rebirth. Indeed, even in Christianity, each stage of transformation from a crawling worm into a living flying creature has its own designation: a caterpillar is life, a cocoon is death, and a butterfly is rebirth. Therefore, it is very appropriate to decorate children's clothes with this symbol, moreover, it does not matter, a boy or a girl. Most often, a butterfly is crocheted. The execution methods differ among themselves, first of all, with the chosen ornament and color. We offer two options for performing a finishing accessory, the last of which complements the butterfly knitting scheme. Crocheting work is very simple and quick. Give it a try!





First crocheted butterfly

The finished product is obtained with a stable form due to the fact that a framework of thin, but elastic wire is used. Roll it the way you want. A butterfly can be either with round wings or with pointed ones. Do not forget about the antennae and torso. The easiest way is to roll the wire in the form of four loops, two of which should be slightly larger in size. Tie with threads, moving along all the intricacies. You can do the job a little differently. To do this, an even piece of wire is trimmed with threads in one layer. Then the form is already attached. After that, the outer edge is finished with openwork viscous.

Crochet second butterfly





Each side is performed separately, but the beginning is common. Follow the instructions for stepwise knitting in rows.

  1. Nine chain stitches loop into a circle.
  2. Knit three air loops to lift. Then, in four loops of the chain, you need to complete seven columns with one crochet.
  3. For lifting - three chain air loops. Then knit ten pillar. with a crochet according to the scheme.
  4. Three loops for lifting. Knit two pillar. with two crochets in one column. the previous row, then into the next loop of the base - two columns with one crochet, four air loops. Having skipped one loop on the base, knit into the next loop and then into each: two double crochets, two double crochets in one loop. In the remaining four loops of the base - four columns with one crochet.
  5. Half Column. In three loops of the last row - three single crochet. In the next three loops of the previous row, two single crochet columns in each. Then one single crochet, air loop. After this, it is necessary to knit a half-column into the deepening of the row preceding the previous one, capturing in the middle and crushing four air loops. One hoisting loop, single crochet. In the remaining loops, knit a single crochet, two double crochet, two single crochet.
  6. Three loops in the air, two double crochets, four single crochets, two loops in the air, a half-column.
  7. The second half fits in the same way.
  8. To get the body, dial five loops. Turning the work, knit four half columns. Then make four aerial loops and a magnificent column knitted at the beginning of the chain and consisting of three columns with two crochets. Three loops and single crochet.
  9. Sew the torso to the resulting wings and shape the antennae.

Ready crocheted butterfly starch to obtain a stable and beautiful shape.
