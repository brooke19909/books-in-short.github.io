Cape Kungasny, Vladivostok: description of the popular beach

The city where Cape Kungasny is located is Vladivostok. This land is one of the most popular and affordable summer vacation spots for residents of this village. Located in the city, it is filled with many vacationers on weekdays. Locals enjoy the summer sun and calmly indulge in swimming in the waters of the Amur Bay, although officially swimming is prohibited here.

The location of the beach is as follows: Vladivostok, Bezymyannaya Bay, stop. author Dalpress. The distance from it to the place of bathing is small. you need to go only 1 km. Entrance to the beach is free.

History

Residents have long chosen Cape Kungasny - Vladivostok can be proud of it. However, for a long time it was considered one of the least comfortable places for leisure activities. There was scattered debris and dirt everywhere. Local catering establishments were then accused of violating sanitary standards. In 2014, Alexey Shlykov, General Director of Device Pro, decided to improve the city beach, turning it into a convenient and beautiful vacation spot. For a short time, the territory was freed from garbage, illegal buildings. The implementation of the project has begun.

Where can I eat?

Cape Kungasny (Vladivostok) has several summer cafes on its territory. The first is almost at the entrance. It looks more like a kiosk, near which plastic tables with chairs are installed. The assortment is small: chocolate, several types of ice cream, soft drinks. Nearby is a grill cafe, consisting of two arbors. If desired, they can be rented by frying barbecue on an existing grill. It should be noted that meat is allowed to cook only in this place, it is strictly prohibited on the rest of the beach area.

Near the car park is another cafe. It is more equipped: there are tables, gazebos and even menus. Prices are relatively low, the cost of some dishes is even slightly cheaper than in urban establishments. At the very end of the beach, another catering facility is being completed. At this time, the territory still gives the impression of being groomed . Reconstruction continues here.

What can you do here?

Cape Kungasny (Vladivostok), with the beach of the same name located on its territory, is ideal for families to relax. There are trampolines on which children can jump for a small surcharge. In addition, there are several play areas for toddlers on site. Sports enthusiasts can use the large volleyball court located nearby. It is fenced in on all sides by a net.

Also, to the pleasure of vacationers, an original art object has been equipped - a heart with white and blue stripes, on which the inscription "Vladivostok" flaunts.
