End-to-end numbering





What is end-to-end numbering, and why is it needed in everyday life? Everyone is familiar with lottery tickets, discount cards, insurance policies, utility bills. To protect them from falsification, numbering is used as protection. In addition, it helps and facilitates the work with documents. In the textbook and books, you can quickly find the necessary information on the page in the table of contents.

Continuous numbering is a way of printing, when page or document numbers change sequentially, in increasing order. For example, when preparing for a tender, a package of different documents is collected. They are numbered in a row, as if it was not a bundle of many different documents, but one single one.

When making a dissertation, you need to number all pages, introduction, main body, tables, theoretical part, practical conclusions, list of used literature - everything except the title page and the appendix.

In books consisting of several chapters, continuous numbering goes from the first to the last page. If in each chapter the countdown starts anew, it will no longer be called that way. And it is used extremely rarely.

Continuous pagination is required in graduation projects, dissertations and reports, in the preparation of abstracts and term papers. Each university has its own specific requirements, but there are common standards that every student needs to know. Numbering starts from the first text sheet. The numbers are placed in the lower right corner on each page. The numbers on the title page and in the appendix or in the table of contents do not.

When printing from Excel, all sheets must be selected, press the Shift key and hold it. Then on each page will be all the numbers in a row. To get individual numbering, you need to position the cursor on the sheet that you want, and select the "Page" or "Field" tab in the "Page Layout" tab, and put the desired number instead of auto.

If you want to do end-to-end numbering in Word, you need to put the mouse cursor in the place where the numbers will be. Then press Ctrl + F9 so that the braces appear. It is necessary to register all the teams in them. For the simplest option, just write inside the Page brackets.

Continuous numbering of documents or pages in a book is needed not only for the convenience of users, but is also a necessary requirement of state bodies. This helps to avoid fakes in the documentation. All enterprises and organizations keep records of their activities, write out delivery and shipment documents. And here you just can‚Äôt do without numbering. Many accounting documents require stitching and numbering, and then sealed with the seal of the enterprise. This is especially important when accounting and maintaining cash documents and strict reporting forms.

In the insurance organization, all policies are also numbered, so they are unique. Securities are also numbered, which is extremely important when using them. Buses and trams on tickets use the same numbering principle. It is convenient and easy to carry out, it is simply taken into account and checked if necessary in everyday practice.

It is technically possible to use both end-to-end numbering and reverse. But in reality, the former is most often used. When printing magazines or books, receipts and insurance policies, travel tickets and discount cards, pass-through numbering is used. On warranty cards, invoices, securities use a special device numbering.

In reports, where many figures, diagrams, tables are used, through numbering is also used. It is quite simple and understandable, it is easy to implement and greatly facilitates the perception of information.

At presentations, during a slide show, the desired sequence of images has a positive effect on attracting customers and buyers to trade organizations.
