It can be used as, for example, a vase. Do-it-yourself wedding bottle decoration is very popular today.

You can also give empty containers as a souvenir, filling it with something decorative or tasty, or simply decorating it beautifully. And you can complete the whole picture on several containers and place it in the interior.

DIY wedding bottle decoration

Wedding bottles decorated in the style of the celebration will be a real decoration of the holiday. According to tradition, there should be two such containers. They are placed on the table near the newlyweds and then stored with them. One of these bottles is drunk on the first anniversary, and the other at the birth of the first child. There are several ways to decorate this container.



The most common decoration option is imitation on the bottles of the dresses of the bride and groom. In this case, small flaps of organza, satin and velvet are used from the materials, and beads and lace complement the picture. Bottle decoration using newlyweds photos is also popular. Pictures are printed on a self-adhesive film, cut into the desired shape and glued to the bottle. The edges of the photo are decorated with braid or beads.

They also decorate bottles using flowers made of polymer clay, pearl strings, beads and other jewelry. The main thing is that the final product is consistent with the overall style of the celebration. No less popular way is the decoupage of wedding bottles, as well as decorating them with satin ribbons.

Making a champagne bottle with your own hands. Master Class

Design options for champagne containers may be different. Among them are painting, decoupage, or even the use of polymer clay modeling techniques or papier-m√¢ch√© masses. Consider the most popular way - filling bottles with ribbons. With your own hands, you can easily turn a bottle, for example, into a chic lady dressed in a satin dress with lace.



To complete the decoration, you will need a satin ribbon, Moment Crystal glue or hot glue, scissors and decorative elements. The bottle should be cleaned of all labels and degreased. To do this, moisten the cotton with alcohol and wipe the surface of the container. Now you should cut off the tape of the required length, lightly grease it with glue (preferably pointwise) and stick it on the bottle. If you use a glue gun, the process will go much faster, since hot glue, unlike the "Moment", freezes instantly.





The tapes are overlapped until the entire neck is closed. After that, they are placed horizontally over the entire remaining surface of the bottle. In the neck area, you need to use narrower ribbons, they will fall better, and on the rest of the surface - wider ones. At the very bottom, you can make a skirt of ribbon or lace to match the bottle. If there are any shortcomings, then they should be hidden using decorative braid. You can decorate with any artistic elements to your liking.

We use sweets

The decoration of the bottle with sweets is considered quite interesting and original. With your own hands, you can create, for example, a wreath of sweets that is attached to the bottle. Or attach sweets directly to the bottle, creating candy sculptures. The most common option is a pineapple bottle. Round-shaped candies in a golden wrapper are glued throughout the bottle from the bottom to the neck. Paper leaves are added at the top to simulate a pineapple rosette. Or an easier option - fill the bottles with small colorful candies and attach interesting labels to them.

Such a sweet present will appeal to people of absolutely any age category. The main thing is to choose the right appearance. Proceed from the one to whom and for what reason this gift is prepared.

Decoupage

Do-it-yourself bottle decoration using decoupage technique is loved by many needlewomen. This option may be the most economical compared to the rest, and at the same time much more beautiful than any others.

For decoupage you will need acrylic primer, a dishcloth for applying it, a brush, glue, a napkin or a rice card, acrylic varnish and various decorative elements.

Remove all labels from the bottle, degrease the surface and cover it with acrylic primer using a sponge. After the soil has dried, it is necessary to glue a napkin or a rice card to the bottle and leave it to dry. It is best to stick a napkin using a fan brush. After the product dries, you should choose the colors of the paint for the background, and use the same sponge to compare the background of the napkin with the bottle in color, then paint on the spot where it is needed.

Having finished the design of the bottle with your own hands using the decoupage technique, you should cover the product with acrylic varnish. Then you can add volumetric elements, if such are provided by your design.

Painted bottles

If you know how to draw, then you can make the design of the bottle with your own hands, using various painting techniques. It may be a folk painting. Such as, for example, Petrikovskaya or Sloboda.



You can paint the bottle, resorting to the elements of Chinese painting. The dot painting looks very interesting on the bottles . With its help, very unusual patterns and elements are created. No less original look tanks made in black and white style. They are painted, as a rule, with acrylic paints. Pre-coat the surface with soil, and preferably twice. You can add small sparkles, sprinkling colored areas until they are dry, or mix the sparkles with paint. At the end of the work, such a bottle is covered with acrylic varnish. It can be matte or glossy, depending on the final idea. Varnishing is carried out in several layers, at least three, or even four. This will prevent exposure to environmental factors.

Using decorated containers

Hand-decorated bottles can be used in different situations. For example, sealed packaging for a gift in honor of a birthday or any other holiday. Wedding bottles or containers for decorating a festive table are also popular.



These bottles can also be used as vases or containers for small sweets. And you can perform them in the style of decoration of the room where they will be located, and use the vessels as an element of the interior.

Thus, we examined some options for decorating bottles. These, of course, are not all existing methods, but they are the most common. Decorating bottles with your own hands, you not only add originality to your gift, but also get great pleasure from a fascinating creative process.
