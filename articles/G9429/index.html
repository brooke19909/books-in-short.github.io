Secrets of board games: how to win tic-tac-toe

In the world there is a whole host of interesting and funny small board games. And almost every one of them has a secret of its own, the knowledge of which allows it to be the main contender for victory. In this case, we will talk about a wonderful game of tic-tac-toe. So, how to win tic-tac-toe?

As mentioned above, almost all such games have their own secret. In this case, this secret consists of the ability to predict the situation and the ability to create a situation. That is, it is like chess, only a more simplified version. So before you win tic-tac-toe, you need to learn a few simple tricks.



First of all, you should know that the main principle of victory is to create two lines that are not completely filled. With this option, the opponent‚Äôs next move will be absolutely not important (unless, of course, this is a winning move). To create such a favorable situation, it is best to take a place in the center of the field. Even more than that, in fact, there is no other way to win tic-tac-toe. Unless, of course, the opponent is not a complete layman (and it happens).

So, for victory it is extremely desirable to go first and to the center. If the opponent placed his figure in any of the center lines, he automatically played. The main thing in this case is to know the very essence of the game tic-tac-toe. So, if

the opponent has acted in such an imprudent manner, we must put our cross in one of the corners, and so that it simultaneously blocks the further promotion of the toe. Then he has no choice but to become in a defensive position (and this had to be done from the very first move). Zero in any situation becomes in the opposite angle. Then our cross should be placed again in the corner. What we have? And we immediately have two lines of two crosses. So, the following actions of the opponent are absolutely not important to us. With any move, we win.

In the event that, again, we go first and put our figure in the center, and the opponent puts his figure in the corner, then the chances of winning are significantly reduced. In fact, there is only one possible option that will prevent this game from being reduced to a draw. When the opponent answered our move with his zero in the corner, it is necessary to put a cross in the opposite direction. The opponent has three possible moves. If he again puts his piece in a corner - it's a draw. If he chooses the center lines - this is a loss. As with one of its moves, and with another, everything becomes obvious, and there is no need to explain how to win tic-tac-toe.



As for when the opponent starts the match, then almost the same thing happens, but with the opposite accuracy. If his figure is placed in the center - you need to become in a defensive position and slowly forget how to win tic-tac-toe, because here the only possible option is a draw.

And if his figure was placed from the first move to any of the eight cells besides the central one, then it is necessary to occupy this particular center, and there it is already necessary to act according to the circumstances.
