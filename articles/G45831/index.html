Nikita Moiseev: facts from life

The history of Russia knows many prominent scientists and cultural figures who laid down their lives for the good of the country, brought up more than one generation of talented personalities and became pride for contemporaries. One of these was Nikita Moiseev. The biography of the scientist and mathematician, as well as his scientific activity, allow him to be put on a par with the legends of world science in the field of physics and mechanics.





Childhood

Nikita Moiseev was born in 1917 in the family of famous Moscow scientists. His father was a teacher at Moscow University and was of noble origin. Mother, Elena Nikolaevna, died when the boy was only 5 years old, and his father was repressed in the 30th year.

Even while studying at school, Nikita became interested in the mathematical sciences and even attended a circle at one of the higher educational institutions of the capital. In addition, he was actively interested in sports, and he even managed to become the USSR champion in skiing. In the graduating class he became the winner of the mathematical olympiad.

Education

The young man began to receive higher education from Moscow Pedagogical University, since his origin prevented him from getting to the Moscow State University the first time, but after a year he still managed to become his student. During the training period, he was actively involved in mountaineering and even managed to work as an instructor. During his studies, he also had to serve in the ranks of the Soviet army, where he taught soldiers skiing for the Soviet-Finnish war.

At the beginning of the war, Nikita Moiseev just finished training and went to serve in the ranks of the army. To begin with, he was sent to courses to master airborne engineering. Initially, he served as an aviation technician, and then as an engineer and as the head of the armament of the aviation regiment. He even had to perform the duties of an air gunner on the fronts of World War II.





Scientific activity

In 1948, Moiseev Nikita Nikolayevich defended his thesis and began to teach at MVTU im. Bauman. Famous scientists of that time Korolev and Barmin read their special courses here. He has a strong family, children, friends - a whole generation of Soviet scientists in love with science.

Scientific schools are being created - these are whole groups of people who are connected by common not only scientific, but also moral ideas, where everyone is responsible for each other. All the activities of the scientist are associated with a group of people who were able to pass the baton and prepare a huge number of progressive youth. They created nuclear weapons, the latest missile technology and made the country the second in the world in the field of engineering and mechanics.

In 1956, Nikita Moiseev came to the computing center of the Academy of Sciences and is engaged in calculations for the trajectories of space objects, the dynamic processes of the biosphere, philosophy, political science, and even creates his own school.

He understood that humanity is on the verge of crisis and does not know where to stop so as not to fall into the abyss of non-being.
