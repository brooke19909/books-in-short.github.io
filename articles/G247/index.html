Multicookers Vitesse: an overview, specifications, instructions

Vitesse is known to many users primarily for its cookware. In the household appliance market, the manufacturer is just starting to gain momentum, but is doing so very successfully. Actually, in today's review we will talk about Vitesse multicookers, which offer the user a wide range of functions, reliability and quality.

Vitesse VS-576

The first model to be discussed is the VS-576. This is one of the most popular multicookers of the company, which has many functions and programs for preparing various dishes.

Equipment

The multicooker is delivered in a medium-sized cardboard box. On the package there are photos of the model, as well as its main characteristics and capabilities. Inside the box, the user will find the following equipment: in fact, a Vitesse multicooker, instructions, a recipe book, a warranty card, a double container, a spoon, a ladle, a measuring cup and a silicone spatula.

Features and specifications

This model has at its disposal 45 programs for cooking, 16 of which are fully automatic, and the remaining 29 are designed for manual control. In fairness, it is worth noting that so many programs are not available in every multicooker of the budget and even the middle price segment.

Management is carried out using standard mechanical buttons, which are as if hidden from view. There is a separate button for delayed start, heating, setting the temperature and cooking time.

As for the bowl, it is ceramic with a non-stick coating and has a volume of 4 liters. For those who like to steam, a special container is included.

Technical characteristics of the Vitesse VS-576 multicooker:

  • Power - 1000 watts.
  • The volume of the bowl is 4 liters.
  • The bowl material is ceramic.
  • The number of programs is 45.
  • Heated up to 8 hours
  • Delayed start up to 24 hours
  • Temperature adjustment.
  • Time adjustment.
  • Additionally - frying mode, manual settings.

User reviews and price

User reviews about the Vitesse VS-576 multicooker show that the model is good, high-quality and allows you to cook a lot of all kinds of dishes in a short time. It has no special minuses, with the exception of defective copies, on which the Dessert program does not work. As for the price, you can buy Vitesse VS-576 for 3800-4500 rubles.

Features and specifications

Vitesse VS-3003 has 32 programs, 8 of which are automatic, and the remaining 24 have manual settings. A slow cooker allows you to cook a wide variety of dishes, from breakfast in the form of porridge to a wide selection of desserts.

Management is carried out using mechanical buttons. Separately, it is worth noting the presence of a heating function and a delayed start. The display here is set as simple as possible. Cost and reviews

User reviews about this model are mostly positive. Users note high quality, a wide range of functions and characteristics, as well as fast cooking time. The disadvantages include the lack of steaming and small inconsistencies in the recipe book. You can buy a crock-pot pressure cooker for 4000-4500 rubles.

Vitesse VS-578

Continues today's review of the Vitesse VS-578 multicooker. Another quite popular model, which at a very reasonable price offers the user maximum options.

What is included

A crock-pot is sold in a medium-sized cardboard box. Packaging is standard for the company - there are images of the model on it, as well as all its main characteristics and capabilities. Inside the box is a pretty decent set, consisting of instructions, a recipe book, a warranty card, a measuring cup, a steam container, a spoon, a ladle, a silicone spatula, a deep fryer basket with a removable handle and, in fact, the Vitesse multicooker itself.

Model Features and Features

This model has 49 different cooking programs. 20 are fully automatic, while the remaining 29 allow manual settings. In general, the slow cooker allows you to cook a large number of dishes, including breakfast, soups, cereals, desserts, pilaf, meat, steamed and deep-fried dishes.

Management is carried out using the buttons that are on the top. There are buttons for delayed start, 3D-heating of food, changes in temperature and cooking time. The display here is the same as in previous models, so there is nothing special to say about it. Unfortunately, there is no voice assistant.

The multicooker bowl has a volume of 4 liters. It is made of ceramic and has a non-stick coating.

Technical characteristics of the Vitesse VS-578 multicooker:

  • Power - 1000 watts.
  • The volume of the bowl is 4 liters.
  • The bowl material is ceramic.
  • The number of programs is 49.
  • 3D heating up to 8 hours
  • Delayed start up to 24 hours
  • Temperature adjustment.
  • Time adjustment.
  • Additionally - deep-frying mode, steaming, manual settings.

Price and reviews

User reviews about the Vitesse VS-578 multicooker are mostly flattering. Users note a rich bundle, a huge selection of programs and a quality recipe book. The minor disadvantages include the lack of a container for collecting the capacitor and the fact that over time the non-stick coating is erased, especially with careless use. As for the price, the Vitesse VS-587 currently costs from 4,500 to 5,000 rubles.

Vitesse VS-3004

And finally, the last multicooker pressure cooker today is the Vitess VS-3004. This is an updated and improved version of the VS-3003, which has even more functionality and capabilities.

Scope of delivery Vitesse VS-3004

The model is delivered in a cardboard box slightly larger than the average size. Packaging is standard for the company. It has a photo of the multicooker and all the basic information about it. Inside the box, the user will find the following equipment: a silicone spatula, a spoon, a ladle, a measuring cup, a tack, 3 plastic containers of various sizes with lids for storage, a warranty card, instructions, a recipe book and a multicooker itself.

Specifications and features of the Vitesse VS-3004

The Vitesse pressure cooker has at its disposal as many as 88 cooking programs. 13 of them are automatic and allow you to cook meat, beans, soups, fish, cereals, etc. The remaining 75 programs have manual settings. In addition, there are separate programs for cooking dishes under pressure.

The multicooker is controlled by mechanical buttons. There is a delay in starting, heating, time, temperature and pressure control. There is also a separate button for cooking style. The multicooker screen is normal and is no different from previous models.

The bowl is made of ceramic. She has a high-quality non-stick coating, so do not worry about the fact that the food will burn and stick to the walls. The volume of the bowl is 5 liters.

Technical characteristics of the Vitesse VS-3004 multicooker:

  • Power - 900 watts.
  • The volume of the bowl is 5 liters.
  • The bowl material is ceramic.
  • The number of programs is 88.
  • 3D heating up to 8 hours
  • Delayed start up to 24 hours
  • Temperature adjustment.
  • Time adjustment.
  • Additionally - frying, pressure cooking, voice assistant, removable lid.

What do the owners say and what is the price?

User reviews about this model do not highlight any significant minuses or shortcomings, except for the lack of the same condensate collection container. For the rest, users note wide functionality, fast cooking time, rich equipment and an excellent book with delicious recipes. If we talk about the cost, at the moment you can buy a Vitesse VS-3004 pressure cooker-multicooker for 3900-4800 rubles.
