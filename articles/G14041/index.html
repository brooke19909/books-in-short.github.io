The best Bodrum hotels (5 stars): choose and relax

If you decide to spend your vacation in Turkey and visit Bodrum, then you will not regret it later. The main thing is to thoroughly approach the choice of a hotel in advance so that your vacation is not spoiled by unforeseen trifles, so that everything meets your wishes and requirements. In this article we will look at the best 5-star Bodrum hotels. We hope the information provided will be useful to you.

Kempinski barbaros bay





As a rule, all 5-star Bodrum hotels are famous for the highest service and quality of services provided, but it was Kempinski Barbaros Bay that earned the highest ratings from our compatriots. The hotel is located only 20 km from the airport, and 8 km from it are the recommended for visiting Bodrum Castle and the Mausoleum in Halicarnassus. The hotel has 6 restaurants, where even the most demanding gourmets will appreciate the dishes of Italian, Turkish, Asian cuisine. Fitness center, sauna, spa, 2 swimming pools - here you can choose personal care treatments for every taste. The administration did not forget about business people. They can use conference rooms, built-in audio and video equipment, rent a limousine and so on. When studying 5-star Bodrum hotels, keep in mind that there are a large number of rooms (173), however, due to the popularity of the hotel, do not postpone your reservation until the last moment. By the way, here at your request they will even provide hypoallergenic towels, toiletries, beds.

Grand yazici bodrum





We continue to describe the best hotels in Bodrum (5 stars) that can conquer even the most demanding traveler. This hotel is located directly in Bodrum itself, from here it is only a 10-minute walk to the most beautiful sights - Bodrum Castle, Mausoleum, Amphitheater. Numerous pool bars and restaurants are a great opportunity to have a bite and sip a cool drink almost anywhere in the complex. And on the roof of the hotel there is a magnificent terrace where romantic persons can organize dinner by candlelight. Each of the 96 rooms is equipped with air conditioning, a telephone, and fresh newspapers will be delivered here. And in order to enjoy all the services provided, children can be left under the supervision of a professional nanny. A night in such a hotel will cost you about 8.5 thousand rubles. Of course, 5 star hotels in Bodrum cannot be called cheap, but the quality of rest justifies such amounts. You will be calm for yourself, your children, health and so on.

Ersan Resort and SPA

Hotels for every taste and wallet offers Bodrum. All-inclusive hotels are especially popular with tourists. After all, it is very convenient and comfortable. Ersan Resort and SPA is just one of those. It is located near the center of the resort, on the coast of the Aegean Sea. Restaurants with Italian, Turkish, Mexican cuisine, bars, establishments specializing in the preparation of seafood - is not this a gourmet's dream? Here you can go diving, play tennis on the chic court, enjoy the many treatments in the spa. Almost all 5-star Bodrum hotels offer similar services, you just have to choose. A night in this hotel will cost about 7.5 thousand rubles.

Casa DellArte Boutique Class





The cost of living in this hotel is quite high - about 21,000 rubles per night. But still, Turkey is very popular with tourists . Bodrum, 5 star hotels in which you are fascinated and fascinated, offers its guests to stay in this complex, where there are both pebble and sandy beaches, and all the attractions are within a 15-minute drive. In principle, everything here is the same as in the hotels described above. This is a fitness center with fitness equipment, and SPA, and swimming pools, and 2 restaurants with exotic cuisine, and bars, and saunas. Here, both hiking and cycling routes are very well thought out and organized. And if you have the means and the mood, you can even rent a yacht and enjoy the beautiful scenery. The hotel has only 12 rooms, each of which is equipped with the most modern furniture and appliances. So hurry up.

Vogue Bodrum Hotel & Resort





Excellent interior, excellent service, a bewitching view for only 9000 rubles per night. Nearby are Pedasa Ruins and Bodrum Farmers' Market - places that many tourists want to visit. The hotel has 438 rooms that are equipped with minibars, coffee makers, televisions, air conditioning and so on. In addition to all the leisure opportunities that are offered in all hotels of this class, there is also a water park, so families with children like to visit the hotel. And the all-inclusive power system allows you to relax without the hassle.

Gardens of babylon





This hotel is loved by lovers of beauty and sights, as it is located near the Gumbet Bays, the Mausoleum of the King Mausoleum, the berths of Bodrum, the Castle of St. Petra. Regarding the service and the services provided, you can talk for a long time, but the complex has everything that you need, but for an affordable payment - 4,500 per night. The rooms are well equipped, regularly cleaned, the area is simply magnificent, the kitchen pleases every guest. But the sights should be mentioned separately, believe me, if you visit them, your impressions will be unique.

For example, the Mausoleum, built back in 351 BC. e., is one of the famous wonders of the world. In the 13th century, it was destroyed by a strong earthquake, but this will not prevent you from walking through the ruins and feeling all the power, strength of this place.

And in the Castle of St. Petra has a museum of underground archeology. There are the rarest artifacts found on the seabed. A ticket here costs only 10 lire; you can even book it in advance.

Whatever hotel you stay in, be sure to take a walk in these places so that your vacation in Bodrum will be remembered for a lifetime!
