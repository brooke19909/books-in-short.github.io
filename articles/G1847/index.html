The hotel is 48 kilometers from the airport in Phuket. The complex was first opened in 1990, and its last renovation was carried out in 2011. Catering

The Royal Paradise Hotel Spa has several cafes and bars. Royal Paradise serves a buffet with international dishes. Reviews of The Royal Paradise Hotel Spa

Continuing the review of the institution, we turn to the reviews of the spa guests who visited it. The Royal Paradise Hotel Spa (Thailand) refers to those hotels for which tourists leave highly conflicting reviews. But let's talk about everything in more detail ... The complex has a rather good location, which is confirmed by the reviews of spa residents. Despite the fact that the hotel is located on the third line, the road from it to the coast takes about ten minutes. There is also a free shuttle service to Patong Beach. Near the hotel there is a shopping center. The main nightlife street, Bangla Road, is just a short walk away. But there is one nuance that not all guests of the complex like. The fact is that the hotel building is located just at the intersection of the gay street. In general, this does not affect the vacation, but not everyone likes this neighborhood, because sometimes hugging male couples appear near the hotel.



On the third line there are many hotels, which are ordinary city hotels, densely standing next to each other, adjacent to the ends of buildings. Attractions and entertainment

The hotel is in a great and very convenient location. If the purpose of your visit is not only a beach holiday, but also entertainment, then you will certainly enjoy the complex. Just a few steps from the building is the famous Bangle Road with its nightly entertainment, cafes and restaurants. Also nearby is a large shopping center and a lot of various bazaars with stunningly delicious fruits, which the hotel, unfortunately, does not give.

Resting in Phuket, it is worth paying attention to its attractions. And there are a lot of them on the island. At the hotel reception or on the street, you can always choose the trip you are interested in and go for new experiences.



From the hotel in tuk-tuk you can independently get to the main shrine of the island - Big Buddha. The figure of the deity in the lotus position is visible from almost anywhere in Phuket. She guards the temple, in which people come to ask for the health of their loved ones. There is an excellent observation deck with great views. There are always many tourists who are in a hurry to capture beautiful landscapes.

If you came to the island as a family, then you will surely enjoy a trip to the island‚Äôs famous aquarium. Its territory is divided into thematic zones, each of which has its own inhabitants. So, for example, in coral reefs you can see living creatures that became prototypes of the characters of the cartoon "Nemo". But the main pride of the complex is a multi-meter tunnel. Walking along it, you can plunge headlong into the underwater world, but at the same time remain completely dry.

Thailand is known for its amazing low-cost jewelry. A beautiful half of tourists will certainly wish to visit a pearl farm. On the island of Nakha Noi, you can see with your own eyes how pearls are grown. In addition, employees will demonstrate the appearance of a pearl at different stages of its development. On the farm you can also see how beautiful jewelry is made from cultured pearls. And at the end of the tour, tourists are given the opportunity to purchase their favorite items in a local store.



Among vacationers, trips to a snake farm are very popular. If you are flying to Thailand for exotic, then you should definitely visit this place. Here you can watch shows featuring cobras and pythons. According to tourists, such a sight is not for the faint of heart. Well, at the end of the show, guests go to the store with snake-skin products. Such accessories are very appreciated due to their high quality, and therefore are in demand.
