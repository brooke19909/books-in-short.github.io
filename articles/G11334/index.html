Premier Le Reve Hotel & Spa 5 * (Egypt / Sahl Hasheesh) - photos, prices and reviews of tourists

The coast of Hurghada is considered to be one of the best places for a beach holiday in Egypt. And the Premier le Reve Hotel Spa 5 hotel complex is one of the most luxurious places for tourists. Therefore, many people going on a trip are interested in additional information. So where is the hotel located? What rest can offer? How comfortable will stay on its territory? These questions will be of interest to many.

Where is the hotel located?





Hotel Premier le Reve is located in an incredibly beautiful place on the Red Sea coast, in a secluded picturesque village called Salh Hasheesh. The nearest international airport is approximately 24 kilometers away. And not so far is the large resort city of Hurghada, which is famous all over the world for its clean beaches and luxurious hotels.

By the way, the hotel complex was opened not so long ago - in 2009. The hotel area is quite large - its area is about 55 thousand square meters. It is worth noting that this place is famous for its compact and well-groomed territory - here you will find neat walking paths, large pools with clear water, as well as cozy gazebos, bright flower beds.

The hotel consists of three five-story buildings built in a modern style. And inside you are waiting for real French luxury, elegant design and, of course, high quality service.

Hotels in Salkh Hasheesh

Today, Salkh-Hasheesh is one of the new resort areas of international class. By the way, in the translation the name of the village sounds like "Green Valley". This plot combines everything that is needed for a relaxing beach holiday - a picturesque coast, bright flowering gardens, sports fields and golf courses, a carefully guarded area and, of course, sincere hospitality. If these factors are necessary for you to relax, then you should definitely visit Salh Hasheesh.





Hotels here are mostly large resort complexes that provide tourists with everything they need. In total, there are six five-star hotels in the village, including Premier le Reve, Premier Romance, Pyramisa, Old Palace, Citadel Azur Resort and The Oberoi.

If you want to visit this fabulous place, then be sure to ask about the availability of tours in the travel agency of your choice - the staff will surely show you hotels on the map, tell you about the advantages of a resort. It is worth noting that this tourist area is still under construction - most likely, after a few years, the small village will turn into a real paradise.

What rooms does the hotel offer?





The Premier le Reve Hotel Spa hotel complex offers its customers check-in in one of 327 luxurious, fully furnished rooms of the following categories:

  • Jacuzzi Deluxe rooms enjoy views of the central pool and sea and are very popular among travelers. Here, of course, they are waiting for you: a beautiful restrained design, a double bed and other necessary furniture. The toilet room, which, by the way, has a bidet, is separate. As for the bathroom, here you will find a spacious bathtub with a jacuzzi system, as well as a shower, washbasins, a set of cosmetics and towels. Guests enjoy free internet access and a safety deposit box. Staying in this room also entitles you to a free daily replenishment of the minibar, access to the VIP bar, indoor pool, sauna and Vitamin Bar, which offer freshly squeezed juices and imported alcohol. You can also dry clean your clothes once a week. Tea and aromatic coffee will also be delivered to the room upon request.
  • The hotel also has 113 Moderated rooms. Such rooms have either a large double bed or two single beds, as well as the necessary set of household appliances, a safe, constant access to the Internet, and a separate toilet. There is a balcony or terrace with sea and garden views.
  • Superior rooms, which are 84 in the hotel, also look approximately the same.
  • The hotel also offers 85 Deluxe rooms. These rooms are also equipped with necessary furniture and appliances, including a flat-screen TV and a safe. There is constant access to the Internet. The toilet and bidet are separate from the bathroom. Balconies or terraces offer sea or pool views. There is even a small dressing room. Accommodation in this room entitles you to access the indoor pool and VIP bar. Once a week, residents can use dry cleaning services.
  • The hotel has 4 Jacuzzi Suites. In addition to all other amenities, the bathroom comes with a jacuzzi and there is a private small steam room.
  • If you want more luxury, then you should book one of the Premier Suite rooms (there are 6 of them at the hotel). This spacious room consists of a living room, a bedroom with a large bed, as well as a dining room, a dressing room and two bathrooms. The room has two spacious balconies from which you can enjoy the sea views. The bathroom has a jacuzzi.

Immediately it should be noted that regardless of the category, all rooms will be kept clean. Housekeeping is carried out every day, and bed linen is changed as necessary. The hotel also has special rooms equipped for the comfortable stay of people with disabilities.

Meal plan for hotel guests

Of course, the Premier le Reve Hotel offers its customers only the best. And the price includes full board. The Turquoise main restaurant serves a buffet three times a day. The menu here is quite varied - meat and fish dishes, cold dishes, desserts, local ice cream, fresh pastries and, of course, salads and fresh fruits. The undoubted advantage of the restaurant is a sufficient number of tables of different capacities - you are unlikely to have to stand in line. In addition, all the tables are served with beautiful tableware, and the waiters are always polite and welcoming.





On the other hand, there are other restaurants at the Premier le Reve Hotel Spa 5. For example, Bella serves traditional Italian cuisine. There is also a Chinese restaurant called Mandarin, where you can enjoy more exotic dishes. A small, cozy cafe Mermaid offers its visitors a huge selection of seafood. Another Ossiano restaurant is located on the beach. And for a small fee, you can always have a great time in the Japanese cafe Miyako Sushi. Fans of spices and spices can visit the Indian restaurant.

Also, guests can visit the bars in the lobby or by the pool - here you will certainly be offered juices, chilled drinks and spirits. By the way, beer, fine wines and exotic cocktails are served free of charge. But you will have to pay extra for imported booze.

Tourists also recommend a visit to the Long Bar, which, in addition to drinks and light snacks, offers classical music, billiards, board and card games - here you can have fun with friends or even make new friends.

Water activities and the beach

Hotel Premier le Reve 5 has its own private beach area, located just a few meters from the main building and large swimming pool. A beautiful sandy beach is a great place to relax. But it is worth noting that there is a coral reef near the shore, so you need to take care of the availability of appropriate shoes for yourself and the child. You can go down into the water by a convenient pontoon - it‚Äôs not too deep here, but the water is always warm and clear.





On the shore you will find sun loungers with comfortable mattresses and protective umbrellas - hotel guests can use them for free. There is also a bar where you can order chilled drinks, and a restaurant offering travelers delicious lunches. And on the beach there is always a rescue team, which is also important.

If sun bathing and just swimming are not enough for you, then you can go scuba diving. Even while swimming with a mask and fins, you can admire the exotic inhabitants of the underwater world. And if you wish, you can always use the services of a diving school - experienced instructors will conduct several lessons and organize the first dive, choosing the most picturesque corner of the coast.

Of course, the beach at Premier le Reve Hotel Spa 5 can hardly be called lively - it is more suitable for a relaxing holiday. Those tourists who prefer noisy entertainment and extreme sports can always visit other places, of which there are a lot in Hurghada.

Children's rest at the hotel

Of course, the availability of conditions for an interesting children's vacation is a priority issue for parents going on a trip with their child. Therefore, it is immediately worth noting that the Premier le Reve Hotel Spa offers only a few amenities. In most cases, tourists with school-age children come here. And upon request, an extra rollaway bed will be delivered to your room. As for food, in hotel restaurants you are sure to find a sufficient number of dishes that your child will like.

In addition, the hotel territory has a small shallow pool, designed for bathing children. The guys can also have a good time on the beach, as the descent into the sea is safe here, and the coastal zone is very shallow. As for services such as babysitting, a mini club, water slides and children's animation, here, unfortunately, they are not available.

Additional service on site

Of course, the Premier le Reve Hotel Spa 5 hotel complex offers its guests the most comfortable living conditions. To begin with, it is worth noting that the hotel has laundry and dry cleaning services. In addition, for tourists traveling by their own transport, there is a large secure parking. If necessary, you can use the services of a car wash.

As for medical care, next to the hotel complex there is a large medical center where you can get the help of a doctor for a relatively small fee.

As mentioned above, there is Internet access throughout the hotel. And in some rooms, the Internet is connected to a TV, which is convenient for travelers who do not have their own computer or other portable devices.

The hotel also has a modern business center with a conference room and the necessary equipment. From here you can even send a fax.

Entertainment for travelers





This complex for vacationers, as well as all other five-star hotels of the Red Sea, provides its customers with a huge number of opportunities to have fun and just have a good time. To begin with, it is worth noting that the hotel complex has two large outdoor pools (one of them is filled with sea water). Nearby are smaller pools where children can swim. Around is a sufficient number of sun loungers and umbrellas. By the way, on the fifth floor there is an indoor pool with a heating system and a jacuzzi.

Fans of a sports lifestyle will not have to be bored either. The hotel offers its guests to use the gym, which is furnished with modern equipment. And in the local fitness center regularly conduct classes in aerobics, yoga, etc. There are areas for playing futsal and volleyball.

You can relax and improve your well-being in the SPA center. Here you can visit the sauna, a real Turkish bath, and relax in the jacuzzi. In addition, guests are offered massage sessions, as well as many other relaxing treatments. There is also a hairdresser at the hotel, but stylist services are paid separately.

In the evenings, the hotel plays live music. The animation here is unobtrusive - from time to time small performances are organized, performances by local groups, dance competitions, etc. And do not forget that Hurghada is not so far located with all its markets, shopping centers, historical sights and, of course , noisy parties and discos.

Excursion holidays in the city

What else can the Premier le Reve offer? Hurghada is a great place not only for a beach, but also for sightseeing. Of course, the hotel guide will first of all offer you an orientation tour of the city - you can visit local markets, visit interesting places, walk around shopping centers, purchase original jewelry, etc.

Fans of outdoor activities can have a great time riding a quad in the desert. In addition, tourists have the opportunity to take a trip on a yacht and even go fishing. And you can go on a real safari or spend the night under the stars in a colorful Berber village. Diving enthusiasts can take part in a guided tour along the coast, during which they can dive and explore the exotic underwater world near the largest coral reefs.

One of the most striking sights of Hurghada is the Palace of 1000 and one nights, which is a gigantic complex built in the traditional oriental style. Here you will find exciting performances, stylized bars and restaurants, as well as museums and discos.

What else can the Premier le Reve offer? Egypt is the cradle of civilization. This country is proud of its ancient history and traditions. Therefore, if you are interested in archeology and ancient relics, then you should definitely visit some of the most significant places. In particular, tourists are offered to make a one-day trip to the temple complex of Luxor and Karnak. Moreover, you can visit the valley of Giza and see with your own eyes one of the wonders of the world - the Great Pyramids.

Naturally, in the vicinity of the city there are other equally interesting historical sights. In particular, you can visit the temple complex of Abydos, the Monastery of St. Paul, the ancient granite quarries, the Temple of the goddess Hathor, as well as the famous memorial temple Hatshepsut, which was built in the middle of the second millennium BC. A cruise on the Nile River is very popular among travelers.

How much does a Premier le Reve 5 hotel (Hurghada) cost?

Naturally, potential hotel guests are primarily interested in the cost of living on its territory. And it is worth noting that in this regard, the hotel compares favorably with its "neighbors", given the huge list of services offered. Of course, the price will depend on many factors, including the date of booking and arrival, room category, financial policy of your chosen travel company, etc.

So how much does it cost to stay in Premier le Reve? On average, a day stay here will cost 3000-5000 rubles. For example, a triple superior room (and three meals a day) costs 9500 thousand rubles. But the deluxe category number will cost about 13-14 thousand rubles.
