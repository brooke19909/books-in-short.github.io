Search for reasons

Failures in removable storage are a common problem. However, this does not mean at all that the cause of the malfunction is on the TV itself. First of all, you need to take the instructions to the device and re-read it again. It happens that the TV does not see the flash drive for a trivial reason - with an incorrect connection. The method of using the drive is described in detail in the manual.

If the owner checked the connection and the cause of the malfunction is not the case, then other possible options should be considered. Here are the main ones:

  • Not suitable formats.
  • Flash drive malfunction.
  • Conflict formats.
  • Memory limits.
  • The port on the TV is blocked.

Now let's understand the causes of the malfunction and how to solve the problem.





Incompatible device formats

The most common problem if the TV does not see the flash drive are different formats. What does this mean? Malfunctions occur due to the reason when the "plasma" and the drive are configured for different formats. That is, the TV works like NTFS, and the flash drive FAT32.

How to check the systems that the device supports? On the TV you need to go to the menu. Find the ‚ÄúSettings‚Äù item in it. Also, this information is contained in the instructions.

If the files recorded on flash drives of a different format, then the user will need to format the drive. There is a second option - pick up another flash drive.



Format conflict

There are situations when music is connected when the drive is connected, photos are opened, and video playback is prohibited. This problem is often encountered by users. To get started, you need to check the file system that was used for recording. Also make sure what formats the TV works with. How to do this is described above. If the problem is incompatibility, then you just need to convert the file recorded on the external drive to the desired format.

However, there may be another problem. For example, if the LG TV does not see the USB flash drive, then you need to check whether the device has the ability to play video files. For example, devices in the LH series do not support this option.

Drive failed

Consider another situation. For example, the owner for a long time uses one drive, watching movies, photos, listening to music. And at a certain moment he is faced with a problem - the TV stopped seeing the flash drive. What happened In such a situation, you need to check the operability of the storage device. How to do it? It is recommended to open it on a computer or laptop. If the reason is a flash drive, then you just need to purchase a new one. After that, everything will work fine.





Memory

When the TV does not see the flash drive, you need to pay attention to the maximum amount of memory devices that are compatible with the device. The manufacturer indicates this information in the instructions. If there are restrictions, then, regardless of the size of the recorded files, the drive will not be read.

Most often, TVs do not support flash drives that are designed for more than 32 GB. Before choosing a device, you will need to carefully study the instruction manual.

Port lock

If the TV does not see the USB flash drive, then the cause may be a blocked USB port. How to find out? Near the entrance through which the drive is connected, the inscription - remote contro or service only will be displayed on the device case. With this designation, the manufacturer informs the user that the port is intended only for repair work and updating options. Such TVs will not play information from flash drives. As a rule, the user will not be able to unlock themselves. But there are devices over which you can conjure a little, if you have knowledge in the field of electronics.

An example of unlocking a USB port on LG devices:

  • Armed with indicators of light, a soldering iron and solder.

  • Solder two indicators and connect to the acoustics system.
  • Download the sound file and download to the TV.
  • Activate its playback by raising the indicator lights to the infrared receiver.

If everything was done correctly, then when you turn on the TV, a new USB shortcut will be displayed in the menu.





USB port failure

If all the above reasons are not your case, then you need to check the USB port itself. Such a breakdown is very rare. To do this, simply connect any working device and check its performance. If, in this case, the malfunction remains, then there is a breakdown in the TV (a problem with the contacts on the circuit, bloating capacitors, etc.). In order to fix it, you must send the device for repair to a service center.
