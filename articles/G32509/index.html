Yekaterinburg, Hotel "Green Park": address, description of rooms, reviews

It is worth noting that one of the largest and most attractive cities in Russia is Yekaterinburg. Hotel "Green Park" will give an unforgettable experience and home comfort to both tourists and people who have arrived here for business purposes.

Brief description of the hotel

Interesting for tourists and businessmen can be considered the city of Yekaterinburg. Hotel "Green Park" is one of the best institutions in the city. Here you can take advantage of a wide range of services, including free parking spaces, wireless Internet, a gym, organization of excursions and more. If you often come to Yekaterinburg, you can get the status of a regular guest of the hotel. This will give you the opportunity to enjoy many privileges and bonuses.

In the hotel you can rent a luxurious room with a jacuzzi or a more modest option if your budget is limited. The number of apartments in the hotel fund totals 94, and the equipment and interior are the most modern. It is worth noting that the design emphasizes the laconic classics. High-quality Italian furniture and orthopedic beds provide you with a quality rest.

A huge number of restaurants can boast of Yekaterinburg. The Green Park Hotel has one of the best establishments, which amazes not only with delicious dishes, but also with luxurious decoration. In the mornings, guests can enjoy a delicious breakfast buffet, which is included in the price. All the rest of the time the restaurant works according to the menu, which contains the best dishes from around the world.





Hotel Location

A huge number of hotels boasts the city of Yekaterinburg. Hotel "Green Park" is located in the central part of the city in close proximity to a large park area with a huge number of green spaces and attractions. Also nearby is one of the main attractions - the Temple of Alexander Nevsky (it can even be seen from the windows of some apartments). In addition, there is a good traffic intersection and excellent urban infrastructure. If you are interested in this hotel, you can find it at the address - Narodnaya Volya street, 24.





Hotel Rooms

In this hotel complex the following accommodation options are presented to your attention:

  • Room of the category "Standard" is a small bright room, equipped with a large double bed. There is also a small work area with a desk and chairs. There is also the option of placing "Standard Twin", which implies placement on two single beds.
  • The superior standard is more spacious than the room of the previous category. In addition, the bathroom here is equipped with a large bath.
  • The category "Business" can offer you both a single room and a double. This type of accommodation is usually chosen by business people, because the room provides a full-fledged workplace, and it is also worth noting the high signal strength of wireless Internet. There is also a small seating area.
  • Suite is a large luxurious room with a jacuzzi, which, taking into account the extra beds, can accommodate up to four guests. There are two full rooms - a bedroom and a living room, as well as a small entrance hall with a built-in wardrobe.

Hotel accommodation prices range from 3,000 rubles for a standard room to 6,000 rubles for representative apartments.





Room Amenities

Arriving in the city of Yekaterinburg, carefully approach the choice of place of residence. As for the Green Park Hotel, in the rooms you will be provided with the following amenities:

  • comfortable orthopedic beds from a famous Italian manufacturer;
  • a TV with a widescreen LCD screen, which makes it possible to view not only satellite and cable channels, but also video from external media;
  • individual air conditioning, which allows you to maintain a comfortable microclimate in the room;
  • a minibar with drinking water, as well as alcoholic and refreshing drinks, which can be provided subject to an additional payment;
  • landline phone, which will allow you to contact the administration at any time of the day, as well as make a call to another city;
  • each room is equipped with a safe, thanks to which your documents, valuables and money will be fully protected;
  • a single room is equipped with a bathroom with a shower, and the rest of the apartments are equipped with a bath.

General impression

Hotel "Green Park" got its name due to its location near a huge green city park. It is worth noting that the location of the hotel is one of its main advantages. This is the central part of the city, and therefore you will have access to numerous infrastructure facilities and cultural attractions. Very close is the famous temple of Alexander Nevsky. But many guests are confused by the loud ringing of bells in the morning, and therefore specify where your room windows will go.

It is worth noting that all hotel rooms, regardless of category and cost, are characterized by a high level of comfort. Separately, I want to note the modern comfortable furniture of the Italian brand, known for its high quality standards. All rooms are decorated in a pleasant red and white color scheme that looks very stylish and concise. If we talk about executive apartments, then the classic style prevails in their interior. Nevertheless, there were some unpleasant moments. So, the plumbing in the bathroom is not very high quality, and therefore leaks strongly. In addition, there are no conditions for drying clothes and towels.

Special attention deserves the work of the restaurant. The breakfast buffet includes dishes from the so-called European menu. Here are several types of cereals, cheeses, meat, as well as confectionery and much more. The dishes from the menu are also of high quality. Nevertheless, there are some subtleties associated with the delivery of the order in the room. So, regardless of whether you order a full dinner or just a cup of tea, you have to pay a fixed amount, which is not always beneficial. Nevertheless, it is safe to say that the cost of dishes is fully consistent with their taste.
