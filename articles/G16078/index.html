Nar Hotel 3 * (Kemer / Turkey): hotel description and photos

Kemer is a popular resort on the coast of Turkey. It is famous for its unique location between the mountain range and the sea. The recreation area includes not only the municipality, but also the nearby settlements of Kirish, Tekirova, Beldibi, Goynuk, Chemyuva. A string of multi-story hotels stretches from north to south. Among them - Nar Hotel 3 * (Kemer).

Turkish fairy tale





The lion's share of hotels accepts tourists on an all-inclusive basis. The contingent of vacationers is represented by residents of Russia, Ukraine, Kazakhstan and Germany. Therefore, hotel staff speaks Russian. The place of attraction for travelers is the pedestrian street Liman.

Restaurants and cafes, shops and excursion bureaus lined up on both sides in an endless series. Barkers are inviting with loud voices for shopping. Nar Hotel 3 * (Kemer) is located near the center of the village. In the afternoon, minibuses ply next to him. At night - a taxi. It is recommended to agree on the cost of the trip in advance. On the counter will come out more expensive.
