Sberbank Visa cards: types, categories, advantages

Documents required for registration of "plastic"

  • Personally completed application form.
  • A copy of the work book certified by the employer.
  • Document confirming official income.

Most popular cards

How to get Visa Sberbank cards, and what is the advantage of each?



For example, a card issued immediately on the spot is considered a debit card. For its registration, it is enough to contact the bank branch with a passport.
