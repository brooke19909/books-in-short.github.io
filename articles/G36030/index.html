Board game "Millionaire": rules and features

Recently, a lot of variants of the board game "Millionaire" have appeared on the market. This is an economic game that gathers the whole family or a group of friends behind the playing field. The rules in it are not too complicated. People who fell in love with Monopoly would love to learn Millionaire.

In the article we will consider the rules of the board game "Millionaire" ("Classic"). This is one of the fundamental modifications of the game. The remaining options are expanded, they added several new rules. We will examine them in more detail later.

Game Options

Buying a box with the board game "Millionaire", you need to check the equipment. This is a four square playing field. Two dice cubes that help you decide how many squares a player‚Äôs chip moves. Since two or six players can play this game, 6 pieces are given, respectively, to the maximum.





In a special department, the "money" for doing business is separately stacked. Banknotes of different denominations (1, 5, 10, 20, 50, 100, 500), in each bundle of 48 pieces. The money in the game is called "phantoms." The kit also includes cards of branches (24 pcs.) And enterprises (12 pcs.). There are also prize cards called "Chance" and "Move". Purpose of the game

Since the board game Millionaire is an economic game, its main goal is to earn as much money as possible, to buy up most enterprises in all sectors of the economy. The player can also conclude profitable real estate transactions, expanding enterprises and rebuilding branches.

The winner is the player who becomes a monopolist and completely owns all the enterprises in the industry. To become a winner, you need not only to be lucky, but also to have a ‚Äústreak‚Äù of businessman and to have the ability to predict the situations and moves of opponents.

Classic Game Rules

The playing field has 8 sectors, which symbolize different industries: the transport system, the media, the light and food industries, mining and heavy, communications and communications, and the entertainment industry. These 8 sectors are located around the perimeter of the playing field. Plots of cards are laid out on boxes with enterprises. In the center of each strip of the Millionaire board game are the most expensive enterprises.





And in the middle of the playing field there is a slot machine called Jackpot and Bank of the Charity Fund. The purpose of these cells will be considered later. Cards with the corresponding names are placed on the cells "Chance" and "Movement", which are located through one, two or three cells along the perimeter of the playing field. Place them with a ‚Äúshirt‚Äù up so that the player does not see the upcoming task in advance.

Beginning of the game

During the draw, the player who makes the first move is selected. In the board game "Millionaire" begins counting cells clockwise from the word "Start". Before starting any game, you need to appoint a banker of the players. He must give out the initial capital - 2,000 Fanta with banknotes of different denominations. When a player rolls dice, points are counted, the chip moves to the appropriate number of cells. If you are "lucky" and a double has fallen, you will have to roll the dice again. But if the double falls in a row three times, then the player is forced to go to the box with the inscription "Tax Police". Such a move is fraught with the payment of penalties.





According to the rules of the "Millionaire" Classic board game, if the chip gets on the cell of the enterprise, then the player takes a site card on which it is written in detail how much taxes need to be paid for the enterprise itself or the rental rate for it. If this company does not yet have an owner, then the player can buy it, deposit the required cost for it to the Bank. The site card remains with the player in the pack.

If the player is not interested in buying this site, he puts it up for sale, independently determining its value. The plot goes to the player who offers a large amount at the auction.

If on your site as a result of a dice throw you find another player‚Äôs chip, then for staying in another company you will have to pay as many forfeits as indicated on the card. There are situations when a chip falls on a cell belonging to a monopolist, that is, a player who has bought all the enterprises in a given industry, then the rate doubles, rent is already paid at a double rate.

There are also pleasant moments. When a player completes a full circle on the playing field, then according to the rules of playing the board game "Millionaire", he receives a bonus of circular income of 200 forfeits.

Surprise cells

In the process of throwing the dice, the players' chips can also get on the cells with the shirts turned upside down. These are "Chance" and "Movement". Let us consider in more detail what should be done in such cases:

  • Turning the card over on the cage, the player reads the task and must complete it. If at this moment he is not able to fulfill the set, then this can be done later.
  • When hit on the "Move", as the name of the card, the player will need to move the chip to the distance indicated on it. If the chip suddenly falls on the "Start", then the player loses the circular income, that is, 200 forfeits. If, during the transfer, the chip falls on the White Business or Charity Fund cell, the player receives a cash prize from the funds of the Bank or the Fund. But there is also a box with the inscription "Black Business". Having got to it as a result of moving the chips, the player automatically moves to the square of the "Charity Fund", where it is already necessary to contribute 50 funds to charity.
  • Jackpot. A slot machine is located in the center of the playing field, so if a player hits it, the player can play. In the board game "Millionaire" according to the rules of the game, you first need to make a bet. Then only one die is thrown, the throwing is done three times. After each throw, the player must rearrange the column up by the appropriate number of squares. If the pictures coincided with the winning combination, the winner receives a bonus prize. But if the player is not lucky, the combination did not match, then the bet remains at the Bank's cash desk.




Corner cells

On the corner squares of the playing field there is a "Tax Police" and "Tax Inspectorate." If the chip falls into the inspection after the move, then the tax is simply paid. If the chip found itself on the police cell, then you need to roll the dice 3 times (until the double falls out) to leave the territory of the site. If the double never fell, then you have to fork out and pay a fine.
