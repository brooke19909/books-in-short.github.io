Pension "Yubileiny", Anapa: description of rooms, services, reviews

Pension "Yubileiny" (Anapa) is located on the Black Sea coast, in an ecologically clean area. Coastal shallows, natural dunes and quartz sand are ideal conditions for recreation for both adults and children, contributing to physical and moral recovery.

  • Level: econom.
  • Capacity: 650 people
  • Children are accepted from 2 years of age.
  • Address: Pionersky Prospekt, 171, Anapa, Krasnodar Territory, 353440.
  • Phone: 8 (86133) 3-32-42.
  • Date of construction: 1980.
  • Date of reconstruction: 2007.
  • The period of reception of tourists: from June 1 to September 30.

Location

Pension "Yubileiny" (Anapa) is located on the 1st coastline, has its own equipped beach, adjacent to the territory of the institution. Nearby is a bowling alley, the largest on the coast, within walking distance from the hotel - cafes, restaurants, a market, billiards, discos, a water park and other attractions.

Description

Pension "Yubileiny" (Anapa) has its own territory surrounded by greenery with lush flower beds and luxurious pine alleys. Previously, a pioneer camp was located here.

The architectural complex of the boarding house currently consists of 5 two-story and 3 one-story residential buildings, in which rooms of various categories are located. According to the advertising of travel agencies in comfortable rooms have all the necessary amenities: refrigerators, TV, air conditioning. Guests have the opportunity to stay at the campsite on site. For an additional fee, you can get 3 meals a day (complex) in the dining room, where the preparation of European dishes is provided. You can also dine at the beachfront cafe.

There is a football field on the territory, sports grounds for games (basketball, volleyball) are equipped. There is a rental of sports equipment. Young guests can spend time in the children's playroom with the teacher or in the children's town. In the evenings, there are various activities for adults and children. For those vacationers who have arrived in their own car, an open parking is provided (guarded, paid).

Positive reviews

Pension "Yubileiny" (Anapa) reviews of vacationers characterize contradictory. Some authors call it a fabulous place with excellent service, in which guests are provided with quite comfortable rooms, meals are organized in a buffet format, and all residents always have enough. In addition, it is noted that the sea in Anapa is warm and gentle. Vacationers are happy with everything and thank the staff of the boarding house for excellent conditions for a budget holiday.

Negative

Other guests of the boarding house believe that staying in an institution can hardly be called a vacation, this is real stress. Visitors are placed in rooms that do not correspond to the photographs presented on the site. Furniture, according to reviews, is very old and shaky: a bed (one and a half) is provided with an old, worn mattress from which springs protrude. Bed linen for 10 days does not change even once. The carpet in the rooms is all stained. The curtains on the cornices are mainly held with the help of constantly falling off paper clips. There are no mirrors in the rooms, and in spite of the advertising, the authors of the reviews share. One chair can be wooden, the second - plastic, there is not even a coffee table. A TV with a small screen does not have a remote control: as in Soviet times, you need to jump up to it and run up if necessary to switch channels. The air conditioner turns on on the street (through the window), from it constantly carries mold. The refrigerator may be provided rusty, with the freezer door missing.

In the bathroom, guests everywhere find mold, there is no shower curtain, so after taking a shower, usually the entire floor is flooded. Hot water is available on schedule.
