G. Adler. Hotel "Dolphin"

This institution is located very close to the sea - 30 meters. Guest house "Dolphin" has all the amenities. It can accommodate 30 people in comfortable, cozy rooms. One of the advantages that the Dolphin Guest House (Adler) has is its convenient location. Within walking distance are cafes, shops, entertainment venues. Nearby is the aquarium, park, terrarium. "Dolphin" (hotel, Adler) often receives guests undergoing treatment in the health resorts of the city.

Vacation with children

The guest house has a heated pool. There is also a children's playground with all kinds of entertainment. About a hundred meters from the hotel is a park area. She belongs to the boarding house "Knowledge". The park has attractions and playgrounds.

Services

There is a parking lot in the area adjacent to the hotel. The health resort has an outdoor saltwater pool. Guests can leave the car for free. A tour desk is located on site. Here guests can book trips to Abkhazia, Krasnaya Polyana, as well as sightseeing of the resort area. The institution has created excellent conditions for holding corporate meetings, family parties, seminars and conferences. In the off-season, the hotel operates a wellness center under the program "21 days of health." For a fee, guests are provided with meals.

This health resort occupies about 40 hectares. There are restaurants, cafes, discos with bars, a cinema and concert hall and a summer cinema. Developed infrastructure is one of the undoubted advantages Adler has . "Dolphin" (boarding house) can accommodate 965 people at a time. Beach vacation

A clean and well-kept coastline is another advantage that Adler has. "Dolphin" is a boarding house with its own beach with sand and pebbles. Its length is 310 m. Treatment

Many tourists, planning their vacation, strive not only to spend the summer comfortably, but also to improve their health. That is why more and more vacationers choose Adler. "Dolphin" (boarding house) offers the treatment of various diseases of almost all organs and systems. A variety of specialists, including dentists, dermatologists and gynecologists, host the reception. Here, not only a general examination of the doctor is available, but also the diagnosis of DNA, trace elements for hair analysis, computer and laboratory studies.
