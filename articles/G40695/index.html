Let's talk about whether you can put a microwave on the refrigerator

There is a microwave in almost every home today. Use it conveniently and comfortably. However, not everyone has enough space in the kitchen to place it in accordance with all the rules. Usually housewives put her where they like. This often raises the question of whether it is possible to put a microwave on the refrigerator.

We act according to the rules





In fact, according to the rules that are described in the instructions for microwave ovens, you can not put them on other household appliances. Moreover, this applies to the refrigerator. This is due primarily to the fact that the microwave will emit heat, not cold. In addition, the parameters of the refrigerator will play an important role.

As a rule, good ventilation should be provided. For this purpose, at least 20 cm should remain between the ceiling and the refrigerator. If you put a microwave on top, this distance may not be respected.

According to the rules, there should be a gap of 10 cm between the microwave and the wall. This is also necessary for ventilation. If these conditions are met, then you can put the microwave on the refrigerator. Although in many cases this is not taken into account. You need to follow a few more rules that will help to correctly place this technique and keep it working.





Rule 1. Compliance with the technical clearance

When putting the microwave on the refrigerator, you need to leave a small gap between them. It will be considered technical. Typically, this clearance is provided by the legs on the microwave, but not all of them are. In this case, you can put small props along the edges. Just be sure to consider that the gap should not narrow.

The top surface of the refrigerator may bend. In this case, the answer to the question of whether it is possible to put a microwave on the refrigerator will be negative. For greater stability of the technique, it is better to lay something solid. A flat plywood sheet is good for this purpose. It will help to ensure that the heat from the stove will not go down. Do not put soft oilcloth and paper on the refrigerator.

Rule 2. Convenience of location

When thinking about whether it is possible to put a microwave on the refrigerator, one more important circumstance should be taken into account. It lies in the height of the refrigerator. If it is small, then the location of the furnace on it can be convenient. But in the case when the height is large enough, putting and removing plates from the microwave will be extremely uncomfortable. Keep in mind that preheated foods can be quite hot. The microwave on the refrigerator should be located so that it is comfortable for the hostess.





Rule 3. Short-term use of the microwave

You can use the microwave, which will be installed on the refrigerator, only for a short time. That is, this is the best option for heating and thawing food. Her long cooking is highly discouraged.
