Hotel Aegean Melathron Thalasso Spa Hotel 5 * (Greece, Halkidiki, Kassandra): traveler reviews with photos

Aegean Melathron Thalasso Spa Hotel 5 * is a hotel belonging to the notorious hotel chain called Aegean Star Hotels. For eight years now, it has been hosting travelers from all over the planet. Many who have been here once come back again. Why is this hotel so good?





Location

Aegean Melathron Thalasso Spa Hotel 5 * is located on one of the "fingers" of the Halkidiki peninsula - in Kassandra, in the main resort town of Kallithea, widely known for its amazing landscapes and stunning flora. The hotel is practically immersed in the thicket of a forest that extends to the sandy golden coast.

You do not even need to go to a private, private beach, intended exclusively for visiting by guests of the complex - the hotel is located on it. It is quite large - 200 meters long and about 20 meters wide.

It is also worth noting that about 90 km to the second largest city in Greece, which is Thessaloniki. A little further is Skiathos Island National Airport. True, if the option is even closer - in an hour you can get to the airport "Macedonia" (Thessaloniki).

As the 5 * reviews left about Aegean Melathron Thalasso Spa Hotel show, the hotel will not have to be bored even to those who decided not to leave its territory.
