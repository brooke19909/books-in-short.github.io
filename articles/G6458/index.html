"Riviera", hotel (Kazan): description, address, photo

Traveling through the cities of Russia, no doubt, everyone expects good impressions and original photographs from trips. Kazan is the third capital of Russia, which has undergone infrastructural, landscape, and architectural changes over the past 10 years. There was a subway, new parks, squares, a lot of sports complexes were opened. In 2013-2015, Kazan hosted international sporting events. Choosing a hotel in the capital of Tatarstan, I want to get the maximum comfort, security and entertainment. "Riviera" - hotel (Kazan), possessing all the advantages of a modern hotel. It will appeal to even the most demanding guests.

Location

The hotel offers panoramic views of the historic city: Gorky Park, the Kazan Kremlin. The hotel entertainment complex is located in the Novo-Savinovsky district of Kazan. Nearby are the Martial Arts Palace, IC Tatneft Arena, KSTU named after Tupolev.



Pubs, restaurants, the largest all-Russian water park, various pools, an ice rink, a beach - all this can be offered by the four-star Riviera Hotel (Kazan). Address of the institution: st. Fatykha Amirkhan, d. 1. You will not be mistaken if you stay in this hotel complex.

Number classification

The Riviera Hotel (Kazan), the photo of which is presented in the article, offers 210 rooms in the third capital: from budget class to luxury apartments. All rooms, in whatever category they belong, are furnished with high-quality furniture from Italy, equipped with modern accessories and high-quality textiles. Also, wonderful repairs were made everywhere.

Comfort Room

"Riviera" - a hotel (Kazan), offering its guests a comfortable room with the ability to use a wardrobe for clothes and shoes, a desk with stationery, a TV, a safe. In addition, telephone services, free Wi-Fi, air conditioning, a combined bathroom with bath accessories are available.





Standard Room (single, double)

The standard room also has a wardrobe for clothes, a desk, a minibar, and TV. Available telephone, safe, free internet, air conditioning, bathroom and bath with amenities.





Improved number

Visitors can use the wardrobe so that their clothes and shoes are always in proper shape. If necessary, you can ask for an ironing board and iron. Also, each superior room has cable TV, a small bar, a coffee table, modern air conditioning, a safe, and a telephone for communication. Free Wi-Fi is available throughout the hotel. In the bathroom you can find the necessary cosmetic and bath accessories.





Superior Kazan View Room

It is from these rooms (single or double choice) that a gorgeous panorama of the city opens: Kazan is in the palm of your hand. The situation in the room is appropriate: furniture, soft and comfortable, air conditioning with a remote control, a large wardrobe for clothes and shoes, a desk with office supplies, a cordless telephone, cable TV, a safe, and free Wi-Fi throughout the hotel.





Family Room

Family rooms consist of two adjoining rooms with comfortable upholstered furniture, a large wardrobe, desk and TV. Refreshing drinks can be found in the minibar, and sugar and coffee can be found on the coffee table. A separate bathroom in such rooms is an important factor, especially if the family has minor children.





Deluxe and Presidential Rooms

Expensive rooms are generally recommended for the format of business meetings. A wonderful panorama also opens here, and you can see in all its glory the city of Kazan. Hotel "Riviera" and all its rooms are equipped with the latest technology, renovation is done, the hand of experienced designers is visible. The service staff is very attentive and ready to help every guest.

A large living room is the main advantage of a deluxe room. Stylish upholstered furniture has a relaxed conversation over a cup of tea. The bedroom is in a separate area. This is a complete separate room with a large double bed, bedside tables and a telephone for communication. The room rate also includes a fitness club, pool and spa, where visitors can relax after a hard day.



The Presidential Room is a luxury apartment: two bedrooms with large beds and a wardrobe with separate shelves for clothes / shoes; stylish living room for receiving guests or business partners; two bathrooms - a unique decision of designers; panoramic balcony overlooking the central attractions of the city. Also, the presidential room has all the amenities that were mentioned in the rooms of smaller classes.



You can book a room directly, i.e., come to the hotel and contact the administrator at the reception or call. "Riviera" (Kazan) is a hotel whose telephone number is easy to remember: 8 (843) 511-21-21. It is also easy to get there: buses number 75, 28, 28a constantly go here.

Relax is 100 percent

The Riviera Hotel includes a buffet (Tatar and European cuisine). Another plus is the opportunity to visit the beach with a heated pool 24 hours a day, as well as a spa complex and massage. Water Park "Riviera" - a great opportunity to relax during your vacation or business trip. A bonus for guests of the deluxe and presidential rooms is free access to the fitness club and VIP pools.

What is delicious?

A positive charge in the morning begins with a good breakfast. "Riviera" - a hotel (Kazan), offering the attention of the restaurant "Amore". This is one of the most cozy and romantic places in the city. Menu - buffet breakfast; for lunch and dinner, Tatar, European and other world cuisines are offered.



In summer, a unique opportunity is offered to enjoy exclusive dishes from the chef, comfortably sitting on the outdoor terrace by the summer pool. On the ground floor of the complex there is a lobby bar, working around the clock. Here, a peaceful, relaxing atmosphere reigns: live classical music plays, light green tones prevail in the design of the room.
