Children's sanatorium "Bogatyr" - quality relaxation and effective treatment

Sanatoriums are special health-improving institutions that allow one to relax and improve one's health during one trip. This recreation format is very popular among families with children. What is especially convenient for parents who cannot afford a vacation, there are also children's sanatoriums in which a school-age child can live independently. The Bogatyr sanatorium, which is located in the Minsk region of Belarus, belongs to this category.





General information about the spa

The medical and health center was built in 1976. Since then, the Bogatyr sanatorium has been repeatedly closed for major repairs, improved and partially rebuilt. Today it is a modern complex in which you can relax and improve your health if necessary. The sanatorium is located in the Molodechno district of the Minsk region of Belarus, on the banks of the Udranka River, next to the village of the same name. The territory of the medical institution is adjacent to the landscaped beach of the reservoir. The sanatorium was built in an environmentally friendly area, in a pine forest. The picturesque nature and incredibly clean air filled with coniferous aromas have a beneficial effect on the health and psychological state of vacationers.





Treatment Profiles and Wellness Procedures

Initially, the Bogatyr sanatorium was opened as a medical institution for patients with ophthalmic diseases. Today, it also successfully treats diseases of the digestive, cardiovascular systems, as well as respiratory organs. Holidaymakers are offered wellness programs. Bogatyr is a children's sanatorium that accepts children aged 6 to 18 years for treatment. There are also family trips involving a joint rest of the child and parents. All medical procedures are performed according to the recommendations of the supervising doctor. The sanatorium has an extensive health-improving base. Depending on the indications and appointments, patients are offered: showers and baths, aromatherapy, various massage techniques, hydromassage, inhalations, light therapy, speleotherapy.





Accommodation options

At the same time, 320 people can reside and undergo treatment in a sanatorium. The complex has 3 residential buildings. Each room has a set of necessary furniture. Shower and toilet are shared. The resort operates year-round, here you can have a good rest not only in summer but also in winter. Meals in the dining room are usually included in the price. In the absence of individual prescriptions and wishes, all vacationers are offered a daily menu consisting of six meals.





Infrastructure and leisure activities

On the territory of the sanatorium there is everything that is necessary for a comfortable life and quality rest. Concerts, discos and other recreational activities are regularly held here for children and adolescents. There is a library with a reading room, as well as a relaxation room with board games. Here you can learn how to play chess and find game partners for yourself. On the territory of the complex are sports and playgrounds, areas for the preparation of barbecue.

For families with children, there is a well-equipped playroom where you can leave your baby under the supervision of a nanny for a while. Especially for children undergoing treatment in the winter, the sanatorium has an educational building where school classes are held. Travelers are also offered organized trips to nearby attractions. In the summer, a well-equipped beach is available for visiting, where you can swim. There is a boat station in the recreation area. In addition, the sanatorium Bogatyr boasts its own rental of sports equipment. Also in the complex there is a mini cinema and a sauna. The complex has its own guarded parking lot.

Any of them will do, do not forget to ask to stop near the Bogatyr health-improving complex.
