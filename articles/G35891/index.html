Inventive activity

Viktor Ivanovich Baranov - a forger extraordinary and unusual. The fact is that from his youth he had a peculiar, exceptional talent of a production inventor.

Arrest

Fake banknotes in the financial market were not immediately discovered - fakes were performed too well. But once an ordinary bank employee noticed a fiction, and the entire State Security Service was put on its feet to search for the arrogant offender.

Sentence and Enforcement

In 1978, Viktor Ivanovich Baranov, whose biography and activities by that time had become the public domain, was sentenced to twelve years in prison.
