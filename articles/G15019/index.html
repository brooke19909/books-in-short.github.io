The construction was built between 1963 and 1967 according to the project of architect N.V. Nikitina. According to verified Internet sources, the author came up with a building scheme in one night. Building Features: Purpose

For eight years, the Ostankino tower retained the title of the tallest building in the world, and now firmly occupies the eighth place in this indicator after the towers in Toronto and New York, Guangzhou and others. The structure stands on a conical base of ten reliable supports, and the maximum deviation of the tower top from the center is, according to modern data, taking into account the wind speed of a maximum of twelve meters. It is also interesting to know that the foundation of the tower does not exceed four and a half meters, and the weight of the building together with the foundation is 55 thousand tons. The diameter of the base of the building is about 65 meters, and the usable area inside is as much as fifteen thousand square meters! From afar, the tower looks very "thin", the question arises: "How does it all fit inside ?!"

Functional use of this facility

The building is also a sports facility. In different years, various competitions were held on it. For example, in 2000, a race to a height of 337 meters was arranged on the stairs. Also in 2003, a record was recorded: twenty-six people jumped from the building at the same time. However, a year later, in 2004, the record was broken: thirty people risked their lives at a time. Not without accidents: the girl clung to the protruding part of the tower and almost died, hitting the wall.

The observation deck in the tower: what is it

But not only this famous Ostankino tower. An observation deck is what attracts thousands of tourists to this place every year. Tourists can look at the city from glazed and open areas. The glazed part of the building is located at an altitude of 337 meters, and the open - at an altitude of 340 meters, just above the closed one.





The grounds offer a magnificent view of the city, this is an unforgettable sight! This place is often visited by wedding processions, photo shoots are organized against the background of a bird's-eye view of the city. That is why the most visited place in Moscow is the Ostankino tower. Photos on the background of this attraction are often found on social networks. Indeed, the images are impressive! The man seems so small compared to the height and base of the tower, and the panorama of the city from above is amazing!

Concert Hall and Restaurant

The open observation deck of the tower accepts visitors only on warm days of the year, from about May to October. Excursions are daily, but there is a limit on the number of people: at the same time no more than seventy tourists are allowed to go upstairs. The tower also has a concert hall and a restaurant. The concert hall is a modern equipped room in which movie shows and concerts are held. The hall has 750 seats, in the stalls and amphitheater. The concert venue is called the Korolevsky Hall.
