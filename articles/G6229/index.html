Car TV: types and TOP best

Not always a long trip can be a pleasure. At the same time, not only the driver is exhausted, but also the passengers. Music bothers, like the same landscapes outside the window. Relax on the road and a long parking lot designed to help car TV. A small device will help to save nerves in traffic jams and stay up to date with the latest news. As a rule, all necessary accessories are included, including a car antenna for a TV. Therefore, there is nothing to buy separately.





Kinds

Car TV is distinguished by several criteria. You should understand them before buying a device.

  • Standard. A small car TV, which is located on the dashboard. It can be powered from either 12 V or 220 V. In the second case, additional batteries are used. An automobile antenna for a TV is most often built into the body.
  • Built in. Such TVs are mounted in headrests, protective visors and so on. They are small and powered by a car battery.
  • Ceiling. The car TV in this case, as you might have guessed, is installed in the ceiling of the car. It is characterized by a wide viewing angle and compact size.

In addition to the presented species, there are several more, but they are not particularly popular with motorists. Often you can find a car digital TV, which, in addition to its primary task, performs several others. For example, there are models with a navigator. They will be not only pleasant, but also useful. Thanks to the large display, it is much more comfortable to follow the map. Car TV, the price of which starts from 2000 rubles, can be purchased at any electronics store.

The choice





We figured out the existing types of car TVs. Now let's look at the basic rules when choosing such an apparatus. Today, this process is complicated by a huge number of manufacturers who are trying to impose their products. In order not to buy useless goods, follow these rules:

  • decide on the dimensions. It should be based on the capacity of the car;
  • Choose a model with an integrated tuner. Pay particular attention to the range of administration;
  • Decide on the interfaces to connect.
