Aes Club Hotel 4 * - the perfect combination of price and quality

The Turkish coast of the Mediterranean Sea is magnificent in its nature, blue lagoons and comfortable beaches. It is divided into many resort areas, one of which is Fethiye. Here are hundreds of hotels of various categories (from 2 to 5 stars). The most popular among tourists are four-star hotels, for example, Aes Club Hotel 4 *. Prices in these hotels are relatively low, and the level of service is impeccable.



Fethiye Resort

Fethiye is divided into 2 parts: Oludeniz Bay and the elite area. In the first you can find hotels at affordable prices, for example, Aes Club Hotel, and in the second part are the most expensive and prestigious hotel complexes in Turkey. In addition to being a great beach holiday destination, Fethiye is also rich in historical sites. In antiquity, back in the 5-4 centuries BC, the port of Telmes was located on the site of this city. He received the name Fethiye in honor of one of the brave sons of the Turkish people - the pilot Fethi Bey.

Location

Aes Club Hotel 4 * is located in the village of Ovadzhik near Oludeniz. It is only 6 km from Fethiye, and 55 km from Dalaman Airport. Many Russian tourists, however, first fly to Antalya, which is located at a fairly large distance from Fethiye - about 300 km. Number of rooms

The hotel has a total of 138 rooms. Each has a furnished balcony or terrace. Unlike many Turkish hotels, there is no need to pay separately for a minibar and a safe. The rooms are equipped with satellite TV, Wi-Fi, tea / coffee making facilities, etc. In the bathroom, tourists will find all the necessary hygiene products, a hairdryer, towels, etc.

Infrastructure and Services

The complex has a magnificent swimming pool with a sunbathing area, SPA center, hammam, Finnish sauna, massage room. There are also three beautiful bars and a restaurant with an outdoor terrace, laundry, parking, a beauty salon and a TV room, a playground, etc.

Conditions for children

In fact, this hotel is just perfect for families with children. A special chair is provided for them in the restaurant, a comfortable cot (on request), a games room and much more.



Nutrition

The hotel operates according to the all-inclusive system adopted in many hotels in Turkey. Several times a day, the restaurant serves a buffet with a large selection of dishes, snacks and drinks. In the evenings, you can enjoy various Italian dishes on the terrace, as well as many delicious drinks.

Beach

As already noted, the hotel is not located directly on the beach and you need to go there for about 7 minutes. The Blue Lagoon shore is one of the most well-groomed on the entire coast.
