Detailed description

The Oscar Dolphinarium was built and opened five years ago under a project in which the best practices of foreign analogues were taken into account. In all of Ukraine, he has no equal in size, since his huge hall can accommodate about nine hundred people at the same time. The stands for spectators are specially set up in such a way that they are in the shade all day, and even during hot weather people enjoy pleasant coolness, which is very comfortable for visitors.



The pool of this institution is also rather large in size and has a bean-shaped shape, which makes it very convenient for dolphins to live. Oscar (Kirillovka) does not stop its work in the winter. Dolphinarium accepts everyone for dolphin therapy all year round, which is an effective treatment for children with cerebral palsy and autism.

Dolphinarium inhabitants

The main artists of this institution, for which spectators come from all over the country, are three fur seals and five beautiful Black Sea dolphins. These interesting mammals have been living here from the very opening, having come here from the Crimean Yalta.

Among these amazing creatures, one dolphin stands out, which can rightfully be considered a child prodigy. He was able to master a unique number, during which he holds one of his coach for a very long time. This is amazing, as basically two animals do it.

Kirillovka, the Oscar Dolphinarium have become a real home for all pets, as it creates the most comfortable living conditions for them. Therefore, their adaptation was very fast and successful. Price policy

The cost of a ticket to the dolphinarium in Kirillovka is relatively low compared to other similar complexes. For example, a place in the VIP box will cost 180 UAH (about 460 rubles), and you can watch a performance from an ordinary spectator platform for 150 UAH (386 rubles). Children under three years of age attend the show for free.

Swimming with dolphins, which lasts 5 minutes, will cost 600 UAH (about 1,550 rubles), and 10 minutes. bathing with these animals will cost 1000 UAH (2580 rubles). You can take pictures with the main artists of this complex for 100 UAH (258 rubles).

You can also relatively inexpensively take a dolphin therapy course, having arrived at the Oscars (Kirillovka). Dolphinarium (prices for this service are relatively low) offers its guests to undergo a standard course of this treatment, the effect of which lasts for several months. It will cost 1500 UAH (3868 rubles) in one session.

Where is?

This dolphinarium is located at the very beginning of the Fedotova Spit next to the treasure island water park. Its exact address is as follows: Akimovsky district, the village of Kirillovka, Pervomaiskaya street, 1B. You can get there by public transport, which runs to the dolphinarium from the bus station every twenty minutes.

For all questions of interest, you can call the complex at this phone number: +38 (050) 255-47-47.
