The ski slope in Krylatskoye for athletes and amateurs

Alpine skiing as a discipline appeared not so long ago, but managed to gain popularity and love among children and adults. The mesmerizing descent from the mountain, especially freestyle, rarely leaves anyone indifferent. Slalom of any kind or downhill skiing evokes powerful emotions both among skiers and those who observe it. So, if you fall in love with this sport, then forever. Even getting up for the first time on skis, you can feel all the charm of the slopes on the ski slopes. There are hundreds of ski resorts in the world, and many citizens celebrate Christmas are sent not to the sea to warm countries, but to the mountains.

Strengthening Muscles and Immunity

It is believed that the passion for skiing gives good physical fitness. When moving down, all the muscles work. Yes, and the heart receives a good load, but at the same time extra pounds go away, immunity is strengthened.

Today, there are several types of descents, including those included in the program of the Olympic Games. But not only professionals, but also amateurs, many resorts offer to enjoy simple skiing from the mountains. Some are even trained in beautiful freestyle skiing.

Ski slope on the Krylatsky hills

Not everyone can afford such a pleasure as a winter resort. Someone stops the lack of time, someone - means. But to learn the correct descent from the mountain, to acquire certain skills is also possible in Russian cities, in special sports complexes where ski slopes are equipped.

One of them is Lata Track, a ski slope in Krylatskoye. People come here from all over Moscow and the region to fulfill their dream and become an athlete. Well, maybe not a professional, but an amateur for sure, and moreover, very skilled.

Sport equipment

Fans first of all need to pay attention to the equipment. These bright, colorful overalls should also protect against wind and frost. What about boots and skis? Their choice must be approached seriously and thoughtfully. The quality must be at its best to avoid injuries in the fall. The steepness of the slopes can be different, and this is just an excuse to choose good equipment for yourself. The ski slope in Krylatskoye begins its work in the winter and during the whole season more than 100,000 people, professionals and amateurs descend from it. Here you can meet eminent masters of sports and teenagers who have just begun to study this difficult ski science. Lata Track trains, trains and conducts numerous competitions throughout the winter.

Education for everyone

To date, the ski slope in Krylatskoye is considered one of the most popular in Moscow and the Moscow region. In an ecologically clean place slopes are equipped to train our athletes. But there is a special, training. There are four in Krylatsky, and each has its own purpose.

For those who would like to seriously engage in this exciting sport, the Krylov school works. To educate their wards, they use the training slope, as well as a free lift. This complex takes care of children with disabilities. For their training in the ski complex in Krylatskoye opened the school "Snowball".

For lovers and professionals

If you are not regularly planning to do it, but you just want to enjoy skiing, everything is provided for here. And the rental of sports equipment in the first place. Issuance is made at several points in sets, which include boots, skis or a snowboard, to choose from. Stay on the ski slope in the Krylatsky hills is also paid, one-time hourly tickets and subscriptions are in use. They come in several forms, from "Amateur" to "VIP", and for children under 12 years of age the entire skiing season will cost only 1,500 rubles. This is inexpensive for the pleasure that a downhill from a mountain will give on a sunny, frosty day. Classes with an instructor will cost a little more. But at ProRideSchool, beginners ride in the first lesson, where they immediately gain the skills of safe descent.

Selection of tracks for training and skiing

In addition to the training slope, the complex also has a main descent. It is probably the most versatile, with a height difference of 63 meters. It is used by all, without exception, and regardless of the degree of preparedness. A special instructor's room is suitable for those who do not feel very confident on more serious tracks and for children from 4 years old, here the height difference is only 12 meters.

Also here they get their first knowledge of skiing and basic techniques. To prepare athletes, a separate sports slope has been built with height differences of 60 meters and a length of 300 meters. Recommended for training professionals and experienced skiers who are not here for the first time. And of course, wide. It is suitable for holding various mass competitions, competitions and other events.

Accessibility for leisure and entertainment

The popularity of this unique complex is growing year by year and largely due to its location. It is within walking distance from the metro Krylatskoye or Molodezhnaya. There are also public transport routes, and convenient parking is available on site. In addition, the complex also has a relatively new type of family vacation - tubing. It guarantees health and good mood.
