Water cascades on the Zmeyka river



Almost every tourist wants to get as many fresh experiences as possible in one trip. Is this statement about you? Then go on an excursion to the waterfalls of the Snake River (alternative name - Savage). This is your unique chance to see five water cascades at once, each of which is a natural monument. All these facilities are located in the Khostinsky district. Zmeykovskie waterfalls: a description of the natural attractions



For the convenience of tourists, each cascade on the Zmeyka River is indicated by a serial number. The first waterfall is the highest, water falls here from a height of about 10 meters. At the foot of the water cascade there is a natural font in which swimming is allowed. If you climb a well-trodden path above, you can see the second Zmeykovsky waterfall. Its height is about 9 meters, but getting closer to this attraction is quite difficult. The third waterfall is very low - only about one and a half meters. The fourth and fifth cascades are located one above the other and look together quite picturesque. It is against the backdrop of these attractions that the most beautiful photographs are obtained.

Natural sights and developed tourist infrastructure



Each Zmeykovsky waterfall is beautiful and interesting in its own way. But do not forget that these natural water cascades are located on the territory of the nature reserve. During the tour you can admire not only the waterfalls, but also rare plants and picturesque views of the surroundings. It will not work to get lost during a walk among the water cascades - there are signs on the hiking trails, an information booklet with a detailed map can be purchased at the entrance to the reserve. For those who are used to relaxing in comfort, there is a cafe near the first water cascade. You can taste delicious trout dishes and fresh kebabs. From the open terrace of the cafe offers incredibly picturesque views.

Zmeykovsky waterfalls: how to get there on your own?

Organized trips with transfer and excursion services are offered to all interested tourists from the cities of Sochi and Adler. The cost of such an excursion is usually from 500 to 1000 rubles, depending on the list of services included, the specific travel route and the chosen travel company, respectively. However, if you decide to visit Zmeykovskie waterfalls, you can get to them yourself. Directly to Sochi, you can take the regular bus number 120 or 180. From Adler, you will first need to get to Matsesta and wait for one of the mentioned routes. So, your goal is Zmeykovsky waterfall, where is this attraction? Everything is quite simple - the corresponding pointer is installed on the track. But if you are traveling by bus, ask the driver in advance to stop near the passage to the water cascades. If you went near the sign to the waterfalls, you just have to walk a little and you will see a ticket office, cafe and you can go for a walk around the reserve.

Waterfall on the Bezumenka River



Another interesting attraction of the Khostinsky district is Orekhovsky Falls. Sochi is not only the Olympic capital and the city of modern entertainment, but also a resort surrounded by real natural riches. The waterfall has a height of 27 meters and occupies an honorable second place in terms of this indicator among the water attractions in its area. The surrounding natural beauties deserve special attention. The Bezumenka River, including its most popular waterfall, is surrounded by a mixed forest, many of whose trees have already celebrated their centenary. Also, on the way to this attraction, you can try and collect water from a mineral spring. There are several water cascades on the Bezumenka River, but the most famous of them is Orekhovsky Falls. Sochi is a city in the vicinity of which you can constantly discover new sights. Do not be lazy to go on excursions during each vacation spent in these parts!
