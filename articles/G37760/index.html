One of the most mysterious places in the world is Egypt. Giza - a city near which there is a complex of pyramids, tombs of the pharaohs, has extraordinary energy. To everyone who has been there, the amazing ancient secrets of this civilization are revealed.

Country of houses for the dead

The rich and interesting history of Egypt. According to legend, from the beginning of the creation of the world, the gods ruled this land. Several generations passed before they brought people out of savagery and transferred them full power. Menes became the first king of the earth. The myth about him entered the Greek and much later Roman historical tradition. Menes is mentioned by Diodorus, Manetho, Herodotus and others.

He combined Upper and Lower Egypt and founded the city of Memphis. It was on this territory that the first houses for life after death were found - the pyramids. There are about 118 of them in the country, most of them in the city of Giza, Egypt. Photos of the most famous can be seen in the material.





According to the theory of some scientists, pyramids are associated with grain, because, folded in a heap, it has a similar shape. Others are sure: this is a pie form that was used in mourning rites. Usually the tombs buried the dead and all that they would need for extraterrestrial life. But constructions in this territory were created according to other standards. They surprise with the accuracy of architectural proportions, the speed of construction and raise hundreds of questions regarding their purpose. Researchers are still looking for answers to them.

Desert pearl

One of the seven wonders of the world is the Pyramid of Cheops. Until now, historians cannot answer the question of how and why such a building was built. Interestingly, even in the 21st century, the geniuses of modern construction cannot achieve the ideal geometric proportions of this tomb. Not only the supporters of ancient architecture, but also people who believe in extraterrestrial life, come to Egypt. Giza and its pyramids are fraught with many secrets.





The customer was King Hufu, better known as Cheops. The second name appeared and popularized thanks to the works of ancient Greek thinkers.

There is a legend that the ruler was a cruel tyrant and suffered from delusions of grandeur. However, neither treasures nor Pharaoh himself were found in the tomb. The architect is considered the nephew of the king, the architect Hemiun. According to the documents, the construction lasted 25 years.

There are versions that the Great Pyramid was not designed by people, but by aliens from Sirius. There is also a claim that this is the creation of the Atlanteans. No less interesting is the option that the ancient civilization set a miracle, to the level of which contemporaries have not yet grown.

Tomb guard

The largest statue on earth is the Great Sphinx in Giza. Egypt saw in him a symbol of the power of the ruler. It is carved from solid stone, the length of the lion's body is 73 m, and its height is 20 m. The monument is located opposite the second building. It is believed that initially it was the head of a lion, and only then some pharaoh gave her the appearance of Chephren.
