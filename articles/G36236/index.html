Craft Ideas: Cute Cards





DIY cards are very easy to create. Sometimes a cute postcard can become an independent gift. By the way, looking at other people's work, you can peek at unusual ideas for creativity. Interesting needlework - room for imagination. Making a postcard is easy, it is important to choose the right materials and decorative composition.

In stores for needlewomen, you can find ready-made sets for creating postcards. But even if you have only a handful of buttons, a sheet of good cardboard and glue, you can create such a cute Christmas tree in a few minutes.

Craftswomen are always looking for new ideas for creativity. Interesting needlework inspires constant exploits. If a completely simple card does not suit you, you can choose a multilayer composition. For example, here is such a nice bunch of flowers.





It will take a little more time to create such a card, but even a novice craftswoman will cope with it.

We will need cardboard, colored wide satin ribbons, canvas for embroidery, beads, glue, a candle or a lighter. All materials can be bought at any department store or kiosk for needlewomen.

Let's start making cards.

1. First you need to glue a piece of canvas on the blank for postcards.

2. Then, from the satin ribbons, cut out the flower petals and green leaves.

3. On the fire, carefully melt the edges so that they slightly twist.

4. Now the assembly. We glue the leaves in the right places, then several layers of petals to make three-dimensional flowers.

5. Glue the beads in the center of the flowers.

It turned out a wonderful gift for mom or grandmother, because women love flowers so much, and such a bouquet will never fade!
