Guest house "Angelina", Vityazevo: overview, description and reviews of tourists

The southern village of Vityazevo, according to many tourists, is the best place for both family and individual recreation. The sea at this resort is warm and clean, and the beaches are very comfortable. Of course, there is a well-developed tourist infrastructure in the village. Inexpensive and at the same time quite comfortable housing is what, among other things, offers a suburb of the resort of Anapa, Vityazevo. Guest house "Angelina", for example, has earned good reviews from tourists, has a fairly comfortable number of rooms and provides its guests with a pleasant service.





Location Features

Vityazevo is the largest village of Anapa. This resort village does not extend along the Black Sea, but perpendicular to it - along the estuary. Therefore, not all local hotels can boast of a good location in relation to the beaches.

Guest house "Angelina" (Vityazevo) in this regard is a pleasant exception. It was built literally 5 minutes walk from the seashore. Directly behind it opens up an undeveloped area with natural vegetation. From Anapa, the village of Vityazevo is only 15 km away. Therefore, getting to the hotel from the station or from the airport is not difficult for its guests. You can do this either by taxi, or simply by minibus. But the best solution is to order a transfer at the hotel.

From Anapa, tourists who book rooms in this guest house are delivered to the place free of charge. Usually the road to Vityazevo from Anapa takes no more than 15 minutes. For tourists arriving in Krasnodar, a transfer to the Angelina Hotel can also be provided. However, in this case, you will have to pay for the service separately.





General description of the hotel

Many tourists like the place where the guest house "Angelina" is located - Vityazevo. The complex itself is a modern four-story pretty new building, the main decoration of which are snow-white balconies with beautiful classic balusters. The hotel looks thus very impressive and unusual.

The hotel area is fenced with a reliable fence. Outsiders are not allowed into the courtyard of this hotel, which, of course, adds to its attractiveness in the eyes of the guests. A barrier is installed at the entrance to the hotel. The hotel courtyard itself is covered with beautiful tiles. Everywhere for the convenience of vacationers there are benches with canopies.





Number of rooms

If desired, tourists can rent one of 65 comfortable rooms at the hotel. In total, the guest house "Angelina", which Vityazevo can be proud of, offers its guests two categories of rooms: "Suite" and "Junior Suite". The hotel rooms are equipped with comfortable modern furniture, TVs and refrigerators. All rooms at the hotel are air-conditioned. Showers and bathrooms in the rooms are also individual. In addition to standard gel and shampoo, tourists can use a hairdryer in the bathrooms.

The hotel rooms are designed for 2-4 people. Guest houses offer the possibility of renting an extra bed in Vityazevo. "Angelina" is no exception in this regard. Tourists can book an extra bed at this hotel at a not too high cost. Each room at Angelina Hotel has a spacious balcony. Here, among other things, you can dry beach items. The view from the windows in most cases opens to the sea.





Room Reviews

Opinion about this hotel among tourists has been mixed. Reviews about him are both negative and almost enthusiastic. Of the advantages of this hotel, tourists note, first of all, the cleanliness of the rooms. They also praise the guest house "Angelina" (Vityazevo) for the fact that children under 3 years old can stay here with their parents for free. This hotel also has a crib rental facility.

The disadvantages of the rooms of the hotel tourists include primarily their not too large area. Also, many hotel guests note the fact that in the rooms rented in it there are too few outlets. However, the hotel administration is trying to correct this shortcoming and provides its residents with the opportunity to use extension cords.





Hotel Infrastructure

In the hotel for its guests, among other things, the following services are provided:

  • laundry;
  • ironing board with iron;
  • large outdoor pool;
  • free wifi on site.

The hotel also has a billiard room.





Guest opinion about the service

Reviews about the guest house "Angelina" (Vityazevo) are not bad, including due to the courtesy of the maids. Hotel staff cleaning rooms usually treat vacationers with caution. They try to fulfill all their wishes. Regarding other hotel workers, unfortunately, there are not too good reviews. This concerns, for example, some administrators and kitchen servants.

The hotel's pool is considered by many to be quite comfortable for swimming. The water in it is always clean. However, some guests express their wishes that it could be warmer. Also, hotel guests sometimes complain that not all guests may have enough poolside seats during peak season.





Dining at the hotel

The dining room at the Angelina Hotel has its own. With regard to food in this hotel, tourists have mostly positive reviews. The menu in the hotel restaurant is varied, and the dishes themselves are delicious. Almost no complaints from the residents of the hotel and in terms of the number of served treats. Guests at this hotel are not hungry.





Tourists reviews: hotel shortcomings

In general, tourists find the rest in this hotel quite comfortable. But at the same time they note the fact that the guest house "Angelina" (Vityazevo) is located, the price of accommodation in which is quite high, far from the infrastructure of the village. Unfortunately, there are no shops or cafes in the immediate vicinity of this hotel. As many tourists note, the hotel administration takes advantage of this and inflates the prices of products, goods sold on the territory of the complex.





Hotel beach

To the sea from the hotel "Angelina" to go, as already mentioned, not too long. This, of course, can be attributed to the undoubted advantages of the hotel. Most tourists in this regard, the guest house "Angelina" (Vityazevo) is praised. But some people also note that you need to go to the sea along a sand dune, which is not very convenient. Many vacationers express a wish to the owners of this hotel and the nearest hotels to asphalt in this place at least a narrow path.

Comfortable beaches are what guest houses located in Vityazevo often praise. "Angelina" in this regard also receives flattering reviews. The beach at this hotel has its own. Most tourists consider it quite comfortable and well-groomed. In any case, there are never too many people on the hotel beach. The sea itself, judging by the reviews of former hotel guests, in this place is almost always clean. Algae are very few. According to most vacationers, swimming in the sea near the Angelina Hotel is quite pleasant. The entrance to the water is gentle, and the waves are not too high.





Hotel Conditions

The exact address of the guest house "Angelina" looks like this: Krasnodar Territory, Anapa, Vityazevo, ul. Slavnaya, house 22. You can book rooms at this hotel by phone. In this case, an advance payment of 10% is required. In some cases, hotel guests may receive discounts. Prepayment must be paid to the account of the hotel owner A.V. Yagubova or to the Visa card of Sberbank. Smoking in the hotel rooms is, of course, impossible. Pets are not allowed in the Angelina Hotel. Guests can enter the rooms at 14.00. Checkout time - 12.00.





Prices

As already mentioned, the rather high cost of living is what distinguishes the Angelina Guest House in Vityazevo. The price for renting rooms here, as in many other hotels of the Black Sea, depends primarily on the season. In June and September, the cost of living is only about 1800-3500 rubles per person per day. At the height of the season, the price can go up to 2200-6000 rubles.





Available Attractions

Of course, guests of the Angelina Hotel have the opportunity to visit all sorts of interesting places in Vityazevo and Anapa. This can be, for example, an inverted house, an Olympia water park, a dolphinarium, etc. Also, hotel guests can go to Anapa. There is the opportunity to see the avenue of roses, musical fountains, the famous lighthouse, etc.
