Rest house "Senezh": reviews, services, features, address and photo

If you want to have a good time outside the city, pay attention to the Senezh holiday home. Reviews contain information about the many advantages of this institution - comfortable living conditions, good nutrition, a luxurious spa complex and much more.

How to get to the holiday home "Senezh"

In a very beautiful and peaceful place is the institution in question. The address of the Senezh recreation center: Moscow Region, the city of Solnechnogorsk, Pribrezhnaya Street, building 2. This is a luxurious park area on the very shore of a picturesque lake. It has very clean air and beautiful landscapes.

Here's how to get to Senezh Holiday House in Solnechnogorsk:

  • If you travel by your own car, then you should follow the Leningradskoye Shosse to Solnechnogorsk. At the first traffic light, turn right and follow 3 km to the holiday home.

  • An electric train runs from Leningradsky Station to Solnechnogorsk (a little over an hour on the way). From the train station, head to the bus stop and continue to the final destination on minibus No. 4.

  • From Vodny Stadion metro station, you can get to Solnechnogorsk by bus number 437 or 440. Get off at the Mater Magazin stop, then transfer to minibus No. 4 and follow to the final destination.
