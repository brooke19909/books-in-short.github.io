How to connect iPhone to TV: instructions

Modern technology can be synchronized with each other. For example, TVs can easily connect to the Internet, you can connect a camera to them. It's hard to believe, but even phones can be connected to the TV. For example, iPhone. Today we will try to connect the iPhone to the TV. There are many solutions to this issue. And each person can decide for himself how to act. The main thing is to get a good TV with certain options.



About methods for solving the problem

In fact, connecting a smartphone to a TV takes a minimum of time. Users have a certain freedom of choice when solving a task.

You can connect the iPhone to the TV:

  • over Wi-Fi;
  • via AppleTV;
  • by HDMI;
  • via USB;
  • using DLNA;
  • using Chromecast.

The penultimate method is in greatest demand. After all, the user will be able to connect the iPhone to a TV that is not equipped with Wi-Fi technology.

DLNA - preparation

How to connect iPhone iPhone? Let's start by considering connecting through DLNA. If the TV has such a function, the task is solved very easily.



First you have to prepare some devices and devices. Namely:

  • The Twonks Bim program;
  • Apple gadget
  • DLNA TV.

On this, all preparation ends. The work will be carried out wirelessly, which is good news.

About connecting via DLNA (online video)

Now proceed to decisive action. The 2 instructions below work on all DLNA TVs. Accordingly, we can consider them universal. Let's start by playing the video from the iPhone online.

Instructions for connecting the gadget to the TV are as follows:

  1. Install Twonky Beam on your mobile device.
  2. Go to application settings. To do this, click on the image of the gear.
  3. Check the box next to "Show or hide the visual ...".
  4. Find the desired video file in the search bar.
  5. Open the side menu from left to right.
  6. Choose a TV model there.
  7. Start movie playback on iPhone.

The message "Beaming ..." appears. At this time, the video will be displayed on the screen of the selected TV.

  1. Repeat the first 2 steps from the previous instructions.

Nothing incomprehensible, difficult or supernatural. Such actions will help to connect wirelessly and Wi-Fi from the "apple" device to the TV.

Over the internet

You can connect your iPhone to your TV using Wi-Fi using different methods. In any case, you must first install special software on the TV. LG has Smart Share, while Samsung has AllShare. This must be done under any circumstances. Otherwise, connecting to a TV simply does not make sense.

A popular method is to connect through a special application - iMediaShare. In this case, you can quickly synchronize devices. The main thing is that they are connected to the same network.

The guide to connecting your iPhone to a TV looks something like this:

  1. Download and install iMediaShare.
  2. Connect your smartphone to Wi-Fi.
  3. Connect to a wireless network from a TV. Devices must access the Internet from one network.
  4. Open iMediaShare.
  5. Select the video you want to play.

This is where all the actions end. But there is one more trick. For example, you can use AppleTV to implement the task.



Apple TV and iPhone

How to connect iPhone to LG TV? Just like any other TV that can connect to Wi-Fi. Experienced users of "apple" products solve the problem radically - they buy a special AppleTV box. With its help, synchronization of the mentioned devices takes a minimum of time and effort.

  4. Turn on the Internet on the TV and set the display in the settings through "Apple TV".

Wires

How to connect iPhone to TV? Via USB! Wired connection is the easiest. Only it does not suit everyone.

What to do in this case? Required:

  1. Insert the USB cable into the phone.
  2. Connect the appropriate wire to the TV.
  3. In the TV settings, set the display to "Via USB".

Similarly, the connection is via HDMI or VGA. But this is not all the possible layouts.



Chromecast to help

It's about using a special adapter and the ChromeCast program. These components work in approximately the same way as AppleTV, only the cost of the corresponding device will be lower. In addition, using ChromeCast, you can play videos directly from YouTube. This fact attracts many people.

You can connect the iPhone to the TV like this:

  1. Plug the ChromeCast adapter into the USB port on the TV.
  2. Launch the corresponding application on the smartphone.
  3. Sync iPhone by connecting to Wi-Fi. Do the same with the TV.
  4. Select the desired video and TV in the "ChromeCast" settings.

Quick, easy, convenient. In fact, these are not all ways to connect "apple" devices to TVs. But the proposed guidelines in practice are more common than others. Can I connect the iPhone to the TV? Yes! And not in one way!
