Features of multicooker care

The first requirement is regular cleaning of the tank in which the condensate collects. After each use, the container must be removed, thoroughly washed and replaced.

Carefully ensure that there are no traces of food on the top lid, because over time this can lead to malfunctions of the multicooker. Wipe the surface after each use. Periodically, the cover can be washed completely, so the manufacturer provides for the possibility of its removal.

The inside of the cooking bowl can only be washed with a soft cloth or sponge. Never use metal brushes and abrasive powders. If the pollution is strong and does not lag behind the surface, you do not need to rub the bowl so as not to damage it. It is better to pour a little warm water into the container and leave for a while. If you have a dishwasher, then the bowl, wire rack and measuring cup can be washed in it.

To clean the outer surface of the multicooker, you need to wait for it to cool completely, and also turn off the electric wire. In no case do not use water to clean the device, because this can lead to serious malfunctions and breakdowns.
