Hotel Club Lella Meriam 4 * (Tunisia / Djerba): traveler reviews

In our article we want to talk about one of the hotels in Tunisia, located in the resort town of Zarzis. This place will certainly appreciate connoisseurs of a good holiday.

A bit about the hotel ...

Hotel Club Lella Meriam 4 * is a beautiful four-star hotel located in a resort with wonderful sandy beaches and warm sea. The complex is suitable both for family vacations and for business events. It has a pretty good location, making it easy to get to. The hotel is located sixty kilometers from Djerba Airport. From the hotel you can easily get to the most popular places in the city.



Hotel Club Lella Meriam 4 * offers its guests good service so that the rest of tourists is the most comfortable. The basic principles of the hotel are: excellent amenities and a high level of service. The administration of the complex offers tourists a wide range of services that guests can use. The hotel was opened in 2014.

Number of rooms

Hotel Club Lella Meriam 4 * offers its guests a fairly large selection of various apartments. The hotel has 252 family and double rooms, 22 suites and 20 apartments.

The comfortable apartments are equipped with air conditioning, hairdryers, TVs, safes, ironing facilities, terraces or balconies and other amenities that make your stay comfortable. Good soundproofing of the rooms makes the stay comfortable. The hotel has a service to deliver food to the apartments at any time of the day.

Guests of Hotel Club Lella Meriam 4 * can choose suitable apartments:

  1. This quadruple room has a satellite TV, balcony and private entrance (forty square meters).
  2. Triple apartment (twenty eight square meters).
  3. Double room with private entrance. These apartments can be equipped with one large bed or two double (at the request of customers). Room size is about 32 square meters.
  4. Standard with one bed is a double apartment with a separate entrance (32 square meters).
  5. Single "standard" with a hot tub (32 square meters).
  6. Junior Suite is designed to accommodate two guests (42 square meters). This room features a spa bath and a large double bed.

Dining at the hotel

Hotel Club Lella Meriam 4 * (photos are given in the article) has two restaurants. One of them is the main one, and the second is a la carte. There is also a lobby bar, pool bar, beach bar and cafe.



The main restaurant offers pretty good food. It serves: lamb, turkey, chicken, fish, a lot of salad appetizers. You can order the following drinks: coffee, tea, beer, wine. On the tables there is a good assortment of fruits.

Thalasso

Hotel Lella Meriam Hotel Club (Zarzis) 4 * offers its guests a wide range of relaxation services in their own Enforme Health Club, which includes more than twenty treatment rooms. Here you can attend massage sessions, relax in the hammam and sauna, undergo a full course of seaweed wraps, attend body and face skin care sessions.

Sports and Entertainment

In Hotel Club Lella Meriam 4 * (Tunisia, Zarzis), tourists can visit the tennis courts, play table tennis, darts, beach volleyball, shoot archery, and do water aerobics. In addition, the hotel has a wellness center, the services of which can be used.

Hotel infrastructure

The Lella Meriam Hotel Club 4 * hotel has an on-site business center with a conference room, shops, wireless Internet, and a car rental service. In the hotel, if necessary, you can order a transfer. Reception is open 24 hours a day. The complex offers its guests a left-luggage office, currency exchange, laundry and dry cleaning services. There is a swimming pool with a relaxation area on site. In addition, the hotel has a team of animators whose task is to make the leisure of tourists as interesting as possible.

Services for children

Hotel Club Lella Meriam 4 * (Zarzis) takes care of the leisure of its young guests, and therefore on its territory there is a playground, a children's pool and a game club in which professional animators work with the kids.

Hotel beach

How far from the sea Hotel Club Lella Meriam 4 * (Zarzis)? Photos of the hotel, unfortunately, do not demonstrate its remoteness from the beach, and tour operators do not always honestly cover this issue. To clarify, we want to emphasize that the complex is located on the second line. The distance to the coast is approximately 250 meters.



The hotel has its own beach, equipped with sun loungers and parasols. The shore has a sand cover, which is convenient for children. There is also a hotel bar where you can refresh yourself with drinks. On the coast you can go surfing and other water sports.

About the resort ...

Those who are going to visit Tunisia for the first time should learn a little about the place where the Lella Meriam Hotel Club (Zarzis) 4 * is located. Zarzis is a small resort town in the south-east of the country. It has become popular as a resort thanks to white sandy beaches, wild palm trees and a shimmering turquoise sea. Zarzis is a good place where you can spend your entire vacation carefree, as well as get acquainted with the color of southern Tunisia.

There are not many attractions in the town itself. The most important of them are the shipyard and the archaeological museum, where you can see the main exhibits of the excavations. Zarzis Harbor is a market trading place on Fridays. There you can look at fishing schooners and ride on pirate ships built specifically for vacationers. Tourists can also visit the Zarzis oasis, which grows 700,000 olive trees and 100,000 olive palm trees. This oasis is of great economic importance not only for the city, but also for Tunisia.



As in other resorts of the country, in Zarzis you can buy souvenirs for relatives. Most of them are represented by textiles and ceramics, which are much cheaper here than in other regions. All goods produced in Tunisia are inexpensive (pistachios, local sweets, olive oil and much more).

One of the advantages of Zarzis is the proximity of the town to the Sahara. Local guides offer cross-country or camel rides in the desert. Near the village there are Barbary settlements where you can see the life of nomads live.

Zarzis is good for relaxing in the economy class. Here you will not find fashionable hotels and expensive restaurants. All hotels in the town are 3-4-star establishments, and local cafes prepare inexpensive and tasty dishes. As for night entertainment, here they are not at all. Those vacationers who seek to find a quiet and peaceful place for a vacation away from the hustle and bustle come to Zarzis.

Tours and attractions

If you relax in Zarzis and consider yourself an active tourist, then be sure to visit the island of Djerba. It is not very popular among our compatriots, since our travel agencies more orient holidaymakers to Hammamet and Sousse. Nevertheless, it is Djerba that is the tourist center of the whole country. There is a huge number of hotels, mosques, museums, there is an ancient fortress and its own medina. On the island you can visit the crocodile nursery and the ancient synagogue. In addition, there are several diving centers on Djerba, which offer to see not only the underwater world, but also to see the sunken ships. The island is full of vibrant nights at night, there are many nightclubs and discos.



On Djerba, real flamingo birds live, which you can see in their natural habitat, well, and animal lovers can go to the Phrygia zoo.

In Zarzis itself and not far from it you can visit:

  1. The country of Ksarov - villages and granaries of nomads in the heart of the desert.
  2. Sandy desert (Big Erg).
  3. Crater-shaped troglodytes houses surrounded by a network of underground rooms.
  4. Archaeological site of Gigtis.
  5. Gabes is a large oasis on the coast that has become famous for henna and the spice market.

Hotel Club Lella Meriam 4 * (Zarzis): reviews

Summing up the conversation, I would like to refer to the reviews of tourists who rested in this hotel. In general, all vacationers are satisfied with Hotel Club Lella Meriam 4 * (Zarzis). Reviews about this complex are mostly positive, which indicates its worthy level.

First of all, I want to note the good territory of the hotel. It is not too big, but well-groomed and green, there are many palm trees and flowers around. The staff is constantly cleaning it.

The rooms of the hotel complex, according to tourists, are very good. The hotel has been operating for only three years, so it can be safely attributed to the young complexes. The furniture in the apartment is solid, in some places there are scuffs, if you look too closely, but this is not so important. Air conditioning and plumbing work properly. Rooms at the hotel are cleaned by male workers, the quality of cleaning is a human factor. Someone does it better, and someone worse. But overall the rooms are clean.



I would like to pay special attention to nutrition, as this is one of the most controversial issues in reviews of any hotel. So, the buffet in the hotel corresponds to the four-star status of the hotel. The hotel, as we have already mentioned, is not located on the first line, but on the second. But at the same time go to the coast very close. But they are not allowed to be exported, as this can cause coastal erosion. The hotel's beach is well maintained, and the water is very clean and, of course, warm.



All hotels in Zarzis are built along the coast, and therefore some of them are located far from the city, like Lella Meriam Hotel Club 4 * (tourist reviews confirm this fact). Tour operators are always silent about this. You can take a taxi to get to the center of Zarzis. Locals advise using machines with a counter, but they are difficult to find. There are not many attractions in the town itself, so if you want to see something interesting, you need to book excursions with local guides. Unfortunately, there is no way to get to interesting places on your own, they are all located either in the desert or in Djerba, so you have to use the services of companies. In general, all excursions are very interesting, such as a jeep safari, from which tourists are always delighted, but a sightseeing trip to Djerba is too limited in time, so there is no way to carefully watch and take pictures on your own.

General impression of the hotel

Lella Meriam Hotel Club 4 * is a good hotel in southeast Tunisia, which can be a great option for a vacation on the sea. However, when purchasing a tour, one should take into account the moment that the complex is located in a quiet and calm town. If you like an active nightlife or love to visit the sights, then perhaps you should look for a different place, since the local resort is focused on a quiet family vacation.
