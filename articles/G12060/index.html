Pristine Resort 2 *, Goa, India: hotel description, reviews

In recent years, the number of tourists visiting the Indian state of Goa has increased significantly. Endless sandy beaches, gentle sea ‚Äã‚Äãwaves of aquamarine color, delicious Indian cuisine, high level of service, an interesting excursion program and a variety of entertainments attract travelers.

Having bought tickets for Goa, young people seek to settle in the northern resorts of the coast, where incendiary parties under palm trees help to escape from everyday worries. And tourists oriented to a quiet and relaxing holiday, on the contrary, choose South Goa, whose beaches smoothly flow into each other so that it is sometimes difficult to determine where one ends and the other begins.

One such secluded spot on the Indian Ocean is Arossim Beach. There are several types of lodging for travelers here: from simple rooms to upscale apartments. Among them is a small and comfortable hotel Pristine Resort 2 * (Goa, India), which is waiting for its guests.

About hotel

The cozy hotel Pristine Resort (Arossim) 2 * is located in the south of the Indian state of Goa. Dabolim International Airport is only 22 kilometers away, while the nearest town, Margao, is 12 kilometers away. These short distances are easily covered by tourists by taxi or public transport.

The hotel opened its doors to its first guests in 2008. On its territory of 2700 square meters, in addition to four three-story buildings with guest rooms, there is also a restaurant, a bar, a pool with sun loungers and a delightful green garden.





The hotel has a friendly staff, ready to help its customers in solving any problems.

Accommodation

The Pristine Resort 2 * hotel has 26 spacious double rooms, each of which, at the request of the client, is additionally equipped with a cot or a full bed for an adult.





The interior is dominated by light shades. Visually, each room is divided into two zones: bedroom and work. The first has two comfortable single beds with orthopedic mattresses and a table, and the second has a desk and chair. Each room has a balcony or terrace with pool or garden views.

The private bathroom has a shower with hot and cold water, a standard set of toiletries (towel, soap, shampoo), a hairdryer on request.

Also, tourists are provided with a set for self-preparation of coffee and tea. For an additional fee, their services include an air conditioning system, which you can not do without in Goa in December, a safe, a minibar and Wi-Fi.

Nutrition

There is a bar and restaurant on site. In the bar, guests are invited to try different types of wines, alcoholic and non-alcoholic cocktails, healthy freshly squeezed juices, energy drinks.





By purchasing tickets for Goa and a tour to this hotel, tourists are entitled only to free breakfasts (buffet) in the main restaurant. You will have to pay extra for lunch or dinner. The restaurant‚Äôs menu offers culinary delights of both international and local cuisine: a large assortment of seafood, pakora (vegetables fried in dough), spicy tikka chicken, Indian noodles, etc.

The guests of the restaurant located on the territory of the hotel will enjoy reasonable prices, calm and friendly atmosphere, fast, but high-quality service.

Beach

Guests of the Pristine Resort 2 * use the public beach of Arossim, which is a kilometer from the hotel. After 10-15 minutes of walking, tourists find themselves on a sparsely populated natural beach, which meets them with white sand surrounded by palm trees, a warm calm sea with crystal clear water, colorful boats of fishermen near the shore and several shops.





Arossim Beach can be attributed to one of the most environmentally friendly and wild places in South Goa. Here, vacationers can not only soak up the gentle sunshine or swim in the transparent waves, but also improve their skills in water sports: water skiing or high-speed motor boats, scuba diving, parasailing and others. Here you can also successfully go fishing or take a boat trip to the habitats of humpbacked dolphins.

Resting on the coast of Goa, it will be a mistake to miss the sunsets, so unlike each other and, perhaps, the most beautiful in the world.

Entertainment and excursions

Although the hotel is within walking distance of the sea and the beach, sometimes tourists are in such a mood that you just want to relax in a cozy sun lounger by the pool with a glass of soft drink. That is why in the central part of the courtyard of the hotel there is a small swimming pool surrounded by free sun loungers. For children, a special swimming area is allocated.

A vacation to Goa in December is not only a wonderful beach holiday on the Indian Ocean coast, but also excursion programs to local attractions, acquaintance with architectural and cultural monuments, shopping at Goan markets and, of course, a trip into the interior of the peninsula to Dudhsagar Falls.





Additional amenities

With its low star rating, the Pristine Resort 2 * hotel is ready to offer its guests a number of additional services that make the rest even more comfortable and unforgettable:

  • live music at least twice a week;
  • bike rental for short trips;
  • rent a car for traveling around the peninsula;
  • currency exchange;
  • laundry;
  • doctor services and acquaintance with Indian Ayurveda.

You can pay for additional services in cash or by credit card: Visa or MasterCard.

Reviews

Experienced travelers know that a lot of useful information about the hotel can be found in the comments of people who have already visited there.

Indeed, travelers to India visiting travel sites leave reviews about the Pristine Resort 2 * hotel that fully correspond to the description of travel agencies.

This is a really small and quiet hotel with decent and clean rooms, green and well-groomed grounds, friendly and honest staff. The hotel is located in a calm village, and a short distance to a beautiful and deserted beach is not annoying at all. All tourists agree that the hotel is ideal for value for money.





The negative aspects of some vacationers include:

  • meager and monotonous breakfasts, however, this is consistent with the level of two stars;
  • the absence of large shopping centers, which can easily be compensated for by a tireless trip to Margao;
  • such trifles as the cries of various rural living creatures, which most vacationers quickly get used to.

Hotel Pristine Resort 2 * is the best option for connoisseurs of comfortable and measured relaxation on the sea coast at an affordable price.
