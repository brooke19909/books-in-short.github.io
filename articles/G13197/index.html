Hotel "St. George", Yaroslavl: description, photo

2010 was the anniversary year for the city of the Golden Ring of Yaroslavl. The main gift to residents was the hotel "St. George". Yaroslavl received as a gift for its 1000th anniversary the best hotel built with money from the Moscow government.

General information about the hotel

One of the oldest cities in Russia is considered to be Yaroslavl. Hotel "St. George" nevertheless does not stand out from the general flavor of the ancient Russian settlement. The exterior facade of the building is made in a simple classical style. Rectangular contours are diluted with arches, balconies and flower pots located around the perimeter of the hotel "St. George" (Yaroslavl). Photos are a confirmation of this.

A poster flaunts the main entrance - a sign that the construction of the hotel was financed from the budget of Moscow as a gift to the residents of Yaroslavl. But the true decoration of the facade is the image of St. George the Victorious.





Where is the hotel located?

The historical center of the city is replete with unique buildings erected several hundred years ago. It is these buildings that Yaroslavl is famous for. Hotel "St. George", as already mentioned, does not stand out against the general background of historical buildings. This is where most tourists flock.

Legal and actual address: Moskovsky Prospect, 10/15 (Yaroslavl city). Hotel "St. George" is located on the central highway, so it‚Äôs quite easy to explore the main attractions of the city - 10 minutes on foot, and unrivaled beauty will open before the tourists.

How to get to the hotel?

Drive to the hotel "St. George" (Yaroslavl) is not difficult. Minibuses and trolleybuses go from the bus station under numbers 72, 76 and 5, 9, respectively. Follow the public transport to the stop "Big Fedorovskaya".

If the beginning of the journey is the railway station, then fixed-route taxis at numbers 6, 46, 82, 73, 94 will come to help tourists. For those who are not used to looking for easy ways, and also want to look at the beauties of the city, a trip with transfers is provided. In general, getting to the hotel "St. George" (Yaroslavl) will not be difficult from any corner of the city. The main thing is to choose the transport next to the stop "Big Fedorovskaya". A shuttle service to the hotel is also available.





Number of rooms

In accordance with the class, the hotel is classified as 4-star. This level is confirmed by the luxurious room fund of the hotel "St. George" (Yaroslavl). Feedback from customers who have lived here more than once is positive. For guests 118 comfortable rooms are organized, in which up to 210 people can live at a time.

Each room has all the necessary furniture (wardrobe, bed, nightstands, work area), appliances (telephone, TV, internet, hairdryer, air conditioning), as well as a floor heating system, a safe, and a minibar. All this makes the rest of the guests comfortable.

Room rates vary by class. Standard will cost 2900 rubles per day on weekdays and 2500 rubles per day on weekends. The cost of the presidential suite is 9,000 rubles per day, regardless of the day of the week of settlement.





Dining at the hotel

The only drawback of the hotel "St. George" (Yaroslavl) is that the cost of breakfast is not included in the price. Meals are paid separately and are held as a buffet. In addition, the price of breakfast includes additional features in the form of free access to the pool and infrared sauna.

For visitors there is a restaurant in the hotel "St. George". Yaroslavl is famous for its chefs, every guest should taste delicious dishes at the restaurant. For hotel guests, the restaurant is open for 24 hours. In addition, a Room service is provided, which allows you to eat at any time of the day at the St. George Hotel.

Restaurant (Yaroslavl) offers its customers the possibility of receptions, banquets and conferences. High-quality service allows you to organize coffee breaks, retreats, team buildings, weddings and other important celebrations of the highest level.





Beauty saloon

The guests are not left without attention and the beauty salon. "St. George" (Yaroslavl) will be remembered by every woman not only with high service at the hotel, but also with excellent service in the salon.

A wide range of services is offered to clients: manicure, pedicure, SPA, massage, turbosolarium and much more. The salon employs professional hairdressers, stylists, makeup artists and manicure specialists. They will make the image of each girl unique, vibrant and memorable.

Services for business clients

The hotel has a business center. Here you can rent a personal computer at any time. The cost is 200 rubles / hour. A dedicated Internet line becomes available to all guests for an additional fee of 200 rubles / hour.

Professional equipment (printer, scanner, copier, fax) allows you to print important documents. At the hotel there is the opportunity to send a fax abroad and to any corner of Russia.

For negotiations and important meetings, a luxurious conference room is provided. The rental price reaches 500 rubles per hour. Meeting room can be rented at any time, depending on personal needs.





Excursions

The list of additional services that are provided to all guests includes tours of the historical places of the city of Yaroslavl. The cost of the tour depends on the number of people in the group, as well as the possibility of visiting museums and theaters.

Individual trips (two to four people) by private transport with a guide will cost 3.5 thousand rubles. After a 2.5-hour trip, the most significant places of the Golden Ring city from the historical point of view open before the gaze of tourists. For VIP clients, tours are provided on executive cars with the possibility of choosing a guide. The cost of the trip is at least 6 thousand rubles.

Horseback riding is in great demand among guests. There are several variations of the trip to choose from: a pair of teams (15 thousand rubles), a solo team (8 thousand rubles), as well as horse delivery to the address (2.5 thousand rubles). Arrivals are carried out under the clear supervision of instructors in full protective gear.

For the youngest guests of the hotel, trips to the dolphinarium are organized. The transfer fee is included in the ticket price. For adults and children aged three to fourteen, an entrance ticket will cost 900‚Äì1300 rubles. A pleasant surprise is free admission for children under three years of age.

In addition, everyone can visit the planetarium in Yaroslavl (both day and night sessions), an observatory, a museum of the history of astronautics, theaters, or simply go on a balloon trip. Ticket reservations are made automatically by hotel administrators after completing an application for an additional service.





Entertainment at the hotel

For those who are not used to going on active trips around the city, the hotel provides a number of additional opportunities for spending time. So, on the third and fourth floors of the building organized billiard rooms. Each room has professional equipment for playing Russian billiards. Audio and video equipment, pleasant background music will pass the time. The capacity of each hall is six people at a time. For the convenience of guests provided restaurant service.

Access to the pool is provided to all visitors who have purchased a breakfast buffet, completely free of charge. The cozy complex allows visitors to spend time not only pleasantly, but also with health benefits. Water is heated to a temperature of 31 degrees Celsius, which makes swimming comfortable at any time of the year. Each client is provided with a bathrobe, towel and slippers. There is an additional opportunity to visit the infrared sauna after swimming completely free of charge.





Hotel "St. George" in Yaroslavl is an amazing place where time stops. Affordable cost and the highest quality service are what attract guests to this unique hotel in the historical center of Yaroslavl.
