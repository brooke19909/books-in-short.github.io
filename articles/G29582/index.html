How to choose a slow cooker: a few recommendations

Due to the fact that the life of a modern person is very full, very often he simply does not have time to prepare a full lunch or dinner. A slow cooker is an excellent device that comes to the aid of modern housewives. With its help in almost no time you can cook almost any dish. It is worthwhile to figure out how to choose a slow cooker?



The development of kitchen appliances is so rapid that not everyone knows what kind of appliance is hiding under such an interesting name. So, before you determine how to choose a multicooker, you need to understand what it is. If to speak as simply as possible, then this is a pan coated inside with Teflon, working from an electric network. It has an electronic component that allows you to select cooking programs. Such an assistant will tell you how to cook the dish. The device will maintain the required temperature, and at the right time will turn off. Now there is no need to constantly monitor the process, as with a multicooker nothing burns and does not dry out.

The larger this parameter, the more food you can cook at one time. For a large family, you should choose a device with a large volume. A very useful function is the release of steam. When cooking is complete, the system will gradually lower the pressure and then let the steam out. For safe opening of dishes, this function is simply indispensable.



Speaking about how to choose a multicooker, one should not forget about such an important parameter as battery life. By it is meant the time for which cooking may be delayed. You do not need to get up earlier to make breakfast. You can fill the crock-pot with grits, and then set the timer. Fresh morning porridge will be ready for your awakening. Typically, battery life reaches 13 hours.

Slow cooker: which model to choose

It is quite important to evaluate the number of device modes. It is worth noting that the more cooking programs are present, the better. There are a number of standard programs for cooking buckwheat, pilaf, porridge, stewing, steaming and baking, as well as a number of specialized programs that depend on the model of the device.

Other characteristics of the device include the size, weight and appearance of the device. Here it is worth focusing on your preferences, as well as on the overall design of your kitchen - the only way to choose the right appliance. If we talk about which company is better to choose a multicooker, then it is difficult to give a definite answer. Well-established products from brands such as Philips, Tefal, Moulinex, but do not discard other manufacturers.

Now you know what you should pay attention to in order to make the right choice of such a complex and useful device as a multicooker.
