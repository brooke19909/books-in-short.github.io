Sanatorium "Energetik" (Grodno region): prices and reviews of vacationers

The sanatorium "Energetik" (Grodno region) is known and very popular not only within the Republic of Belarus, but also in Russia, Germany, Israel and other countries. Soon he will celebrate his 20th anniversary (opened in 1996). During this time, many new buildings have been built here, both bedroom and therapeutic, and the territory has been turned into a wonderful park. The living and treatment conditions in the sanatorium are so comfortable that the rest goes unnoticed. Therefore, leaving here, people certainly want to return again.

Location

The sanatorium "Energetik" (Belarus) is located in the northwestern part of the republic, in the Grodno region. From Grodno it is about 70 km, from Minsk about 260 km. Very close to the sanatorium are the ancient cities of Volkovysk (27 km) and Bridges (about 15 km), where vacationers can go on excursions. The territory of the sanatorium is quite large - 16 hectares. It was built in a wonderful coniferous forest. Phytoncides secreted by pines and junipers create an unusually pleasant healing air, providing everyone who comes here with free aromatherapy. In addition, in the forest zone, everyone can pick berries and famous Belarusian mushrooms. A 3-5-minute walk from the sanatorium splashes the Volpian reservoir, in which large fish (pike, bream, common carp) and small (trout, roach) are caught. Fishing is free here.





How to get there

Sanatorium "Energetik" (Grodno region), although located in a forest zone, is easy to get to. The most convenient way is by rail to the city of Bridges. Passenger trains from Moscow, St. Petersburg, Minsk stop there, and from Grodno, in addition, there are trains. At the station, vacationers transfer to a sanatorium bus (paid service). Flights are available every day except holidays and Sundays.

Anyone who prefers to take a private car should drive along the M6, P51 and P41 highways to the city of Bridges. Then for about 10 km follow the P100 highway to the sign showing the turn to the Energetik sanatorium (Grodno region). From this place to the destination remains a little less than a kilometer.

In addition, everyone can order a transfer individually from the cities of Minsk, Grodno, Bridges, Lida, Baranovichi, Volkovysk, Ivatsevichi and Molodechno. This service is available any day.





Medical profile

The Energetik sanatorium located not far from the city of Grodno can simultaneously accommodate 285 people in their main buildings and 15 additional people. Professional doctors and nurses treat blood vessels and the heart, diseases of the nervous system, urology organs, bones, joints, spine, and heal gynecological diseases.

In addition, dental services and consultations of some specialized specialists are provided:

  • gynecologist;

  • urologist;

  • therapist;

  • ultrasound works;

  • a cardiogram of the heart is performed.

Description

Sanatorium "Energetik" (Grodno region) opened its doors to guests in 1996. It was converted from a children's recreation complex. All buildings here are no higher than 2 floors, each built according to an individual design. For almost 20 years, residential, medical and recreational facilities have expanded significantly, while previously existing ones have been completely refitted. Now there are 8 bedroom buildings and 9 pretty detached cottages.

In a convenient location there is a medical building, a mud bath, a beauty parlor, a dining room, a club, a fitness center, a swimming pool, a sauna, a billiard room, a cafe, a dance hall, a shop and a bank. There is a parking area.

Through the efforts of employees, several health paths with benches for rest were created on the territory of the sanatorium. You can come here for treatment all year round. All buildings are heated depending on the weather, and not on the season.

The provision of medical services to children is carried out upon reaching 3 years of age. Pets are not allowed in the sanatorium.





Rooms

Beautiful modern one and two-room rooms, designed for a maximum of 2 people, are available at the Energetik sanatorium (Grodno region). Prices are set depending on the building or cottage. This is due to the difference in room size. In each of them there are:

  • comfortable single or double beds;

  • necessary furniture;

  • refrigerator;

  • TV;

  • a bathroom with a spacious shower compartment, a bathroom and a washbasin (bath accessories are not issued).

Electric kettle is provided in building numbers 4, 5, 7, 8, and microwave ovens are installed in single two-room buildings of the same buildings. Block 2 contains block numbers, which have only the necessary furniture and a TV. The refrigerator, toilet, wash basin and shower are blocky.

Prices (in addition to the reason for differences in rooms) also vary depending on the season. They are the highest from June 21 to August 31.

In addition, for holidaymakers from Belarus, Russia and abroad, the cost of accommodation and services in a sanatorium is different.
