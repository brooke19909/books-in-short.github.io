Her Majesty Knitted Mink

Knitted mink is a special technique for creating peculiar items of clothing. It implies the formation of a so-called thread of fur (followed by crocheting or knitting).





Knitted mink clothes are not only beautiful and comfortable, but they are also very durable, all thanks to their unique and unique knitting technique.

By the arrival of winter, people are trying to prepare in advance, to get warm outerwear. And then there is a purchase of very necessary things for the winter, such as fur coats, sheepskin coats, down jackets. A truly unique and original choice during this period of time will be the acquisition of special and delightful knitted mink clothes. But, unfortunately, in the winter season such products are more expensive than in the summer. DIY knitted mink

To tie it yourself, you first need to understand what a knitted fur is. Paula Lishman is a famous fashion designer, she was the first to use a similar method of knitting. This technique implied the location of the fur both from the inside and from the outside. It made things pretty nice to the touch.

But in order to make a thread of fur, you need to make considerable efforts. First, the skin must be cut into thin strips, then they are tied to each other. To do this, use a special elastic base. These manipulations will allow for a long time the product to remain quite resistant to wear. Many self-respecting ladies will notice that things from knitted mink differ significantly from ordinary products in their structure. Their pile is not so long. Thanks to a variety of methods, a knitted mink allows you to form various patterns of various types of furs.

What you need to purchase:

- knitting needles;

- threads;

- blade;

- the skin of the mink.

It is these accessories that are primarily needed for knitting a mink product by hand.

Knitted Mink Products



Quite a wide selection of the most diverse things created in this way. But the most famous, of course, can be considered wedding boleros, ponchos, stoles, capes. A knitted mink vest is also very popular these days. But the most chic thing can rightly be considered a fur coat, which is ideally complemented by an elegant set consisting of mittens, a scarf and a knitted mink hat. The last product has its own specifics, so it is not suitable for everyone. The owner of such a headdress must have certain facial contours in order for this thing to suit her.

Very often, knitted mink hats are made of a round standard shape, tight-fitting. The volume of this product gives the texture of the fur. Knitted mink is one of the most accessible and common materials with which you can create real masterpieces.
