Hotel "Yugomorye" (Arkhipo-Osipovka): description, reviews

If you prefer a relaxing family vacation on the shores of the warm, gentle sea, the Yugomorye Hotel invites you to visit. Arkhipo-Osipovka is the largest resort village of the Gelendzhik region. Yugomorye operates year-round, invites families with children of any age to relax.

Description

In the village of Arkhipo-Osipovka, hotels are mostly small. Rest in them can be classified as inexpensive. Nevertheless, the Yugomorye Hotel will pleasantly surprise you with its comfortable conditions. There are no corridors familiar to recreational areas. Each room has its own entrance from the street and its own recreation area - a spacious balcony or a veranda with a table.





A stone's throw from the hotel is the Black Sea. The hotel has two swimming pools, one of which is open all year. On the street there is a pool with lighting and heating. There is a cafe with excellent cuisine, where you can prepare delicious and inexpensive dishes. The cafe carries out custom-made purchase of goat and fresh cow's milk. Of these, dietary fermented milk products are prepared for supporters of a healthy diet. For those who wish to cook independently, a small kitchen is also provided on site. For children, there is a playground with a house, a swing and a sandbox. For guests with a private car, parking is free. In "South Sea" (Arkhipo-Osipovka) has its own library, free wireless Internet. There is a recreation area with barbecue. Despite the close proximity of the central beach, the mini-hotel is always quiet and comfortable.

Accommodation

In Yugomorye, located in the village of Arkhipo-Osipovka, accommodation prices fluctuate depending on the season. Here you will be offered the numbers of the following categories:

  • double room with two beds costing from 2000 to 3200 rubles per day;
  • double room with one bed also costing from 2000 to 3200 rubles per day;
  • triple apartments with extra bed - from 2500 to 4000 rubles;
  • Suite, the cost of which is from 3000 to 5900 rubles per day;
  • a house, the price per stay in which for one day is from 3500 to 6500 rubles;
  • two-room family room costing from 3000 to 5900 rubles.




The price includes:

  • car parking;
  • housekeeping (performed every four days or on demand);
  • use of recreation areas and a swimming pool;
  • Wi-fi

Room Amenities

Rooms at the Yugomorye Hotel are equipped with everything you need. Each room for vacationers provides:

  • TV with plasma screen;
  • refrigerator;
  • air conditioning;
  • there is a balcony or porch;
  • safes;
  • for families with small children a special highchair and a bathtub are provided;
  • the bathroom has a shower, toilet, toilet paper and free toiletries;
  • clothes dryers are provided;
  • the room has a dining area, barbecue.




The hotel is pet friendly. This service may be charged upon prior request.

Services

For a more comfortable stay, the Yugomorye Hotel (Arkhipo-Osipovka) has provided a number of services for its guests:

  • On the territory of the hotel there is a cafe, designed in a rustic style, which will offer you a varied menu at affordable prices. Room service is available.
  • There is a heated pool.
  • There is an excellent sitting area with barbecue. Brazier is both stationary and portable.
  • You can use the services of a massage therapist.
  • Bicycle, scooter and scooter rentals are available.
  • Laundry and ironing services are also available.
  • Babysitting services are available for children.
  • Possible transfer (paid) from the airport or train station to the hotel and back. The nearest airport is Gelendzhik , 49 km from Arkhipo-Osipovka.
  • The organization of excursions for tourists.
  • The hotel has a designated smoking area.




  • In the off-season, you can spend corporate parties, birthdays here, and celebrate the New Year.
  • The area is popular for horse riding and fishing.
  • Reception is open 24 hours a day.
  • Hotel Yugomorye (Arkhipo-Osipovka) accepts payment for accommodation only in cash. When booking rooms, a prepayment of one night is required.

Check-in is at 13.00, check-out is at 11.00.

Placement of children

The hotel allows children of any age.

  • If the kids are less than 2 years old, then cots are allowed for free.
  • All children under 12 years are charged 450 rubles per night for extra beds.
  • If additionally accommodated children over 12 years old or adults, then per night for an extra bed the fee is 700 rubles. Moreover, in one room there can be only one extra bed.

"South Sea", Arkhipo-Osipovka: reviews

Of course, the reviews about the rest at the Yugomorye Hotel are mostly positive:

  • First of all, guests really like the good-natured and friendly attitude of the owners of the hotel.
  • Vacationers unanimously celebrate the stunningly delicious hotel cuisine. At the same time, in comparison with other cafes on the coast, the prices here are not at all high.
  • Hotel guests enjoy the large balconies in their rooms, which you can sit in the evening and breathe in the fresh sea air.




  • It is very convenient that the parking on site is covered.
  • The hotel courtyard is clean, comfortable, well-groomed, there is a lot of greenery on the territory.
  • A clean warm pool is open until 23.00, in the evening they include lighting.
  • The presence of a playground.
  • There is a barbecue, where you can cook kebabs, as well as hammocks, which are nice to lie down during the day.
  • The mini-hotel is located near the sea, slowly walking about 10-15 minutes.
  • The hotel offers a very beautiful view of the mountains.

Negative reviews

Unfortunately, there were some complaints about the Yugomorye Hotel (Arkhipo-Osipovka):

  • Old furniture in the rooms, some tenants complained about uncomfortable crushed sofas.
  • If it rains, then the rooms immediately increase humidity, as the houses are wooden. But with the help of an air conditioner, air can be dried.
  • At the height of the season, there are a lot of people on the beaches, so during the daytime the sea is not always clear.
  • Wi-Fi service is available only in the hotel, it is not in the rooms.
  • There was a case of checking in the wrong room, which was previously booked.




Conclusion

If you decide to spend your vacation in the village of Arkhipo-Osipovka, the hotels here are for the most different tastes and purses. One of the most budgetary is a mini-hotel "Yugomorye", which perfectly combines price and quality. It is also convenient because the most interesting sights of the village are located within walking distance from it. Very close to the hotel you can visit St. Nicholas Church, the chapel, as well as the GoodZon Water Park. There are always many children in a hotel; it is rightfully considered one of the best for families. Hotel address: Gelendzhik district, Arkhipo-Osipovka village, Yugomorye, Sadovaya 26.
