Hotel Sunny Beauty Palace 4 * (Sunny Beach, Bulgaria): photos and reviews of tourists

Sunny beach has always been very popular among domestic tourists. This largest resort in Bulgaria offers vacationers all “33 pleasures”. Convenient Sunny Beach can be both for lovers of a quiet beach and outdoor activities. Of course, the tourist infrastructure is also well developed in this resort. There are a lot of good hotels offering vacationers comfortable, inexpensive accommodation. For example, guests of Bulgaria can book a comfortable room in the hotel S unny Beauty Palace 4 *.

Location Features

This four-star hotel is located in the northern part of Sunny Beach. The distance to the village center is 1.5 km. Burgas International Airport is approximately 35 km from the hotel. In most cases, tourists get to the hotel by transfer bus provided by the operator who sold the ticket. But if necessary, the hotel can also be reached by taxi or simply by bus. To the Sunny Beach from the airport, public transport runs, of course, regularly. Of course, the Sunny Beauty Palace 4 * hotel itself provides its guests with a shuttle service .





This hotel is located, unfortunately, not on the first coastline. The distance from it to the seashore is 600 meters. 9 km from the hotel is one of the most famous beaches in Bulgaria - Cocoa Beach.

What is a hotel: general description

The hotel is hosted by S unny Beauty Palace 4 * hotel in one four-story comfortable building. The hotel building, according to most tourists, has not only beautiful architecture, but also a very convenient layout. This hotel was built not so long ago - in 2014. And so the furniture, appliances and furnishings are almost new.

From the registration desk in Sunny Beauty Palace 4 * works around the clock, which, of course, is very convenient for guests. At the reception, tourists are usually met by the hostess of the hotel. This woman, according to vacationers, is very polite and pleasant. Among other things, she also knows how to speak Russian. Actually, the registration staff themselves, unfortunately, know only English. But the reception staff still understand the elementary phrases in Russian. Smoking and pets are not allowed on site.

Most tourists consider beautiful not only the hotel building, but also its territory. The courtyard of the hotel is covered with paving tiles, which is very convenient to walk on, and also well landscaped.

Number of rooms

In total, the Sunny Beauty Palace 4 * hotel (Sunny Beach) offers its guests 62 comfortable rooms. If you wish, in this hotel you can rent both a standard standard and apartments. The rooms can be fitted with double or single beds. Therefore, both tourists who come to Bulgaria with their family and friends or colleagues can spend time comfortably in the hotel.





In addition, the hotel has 5 standard rooms designed for people with disabilities. These rooms are equipped so that people with disabilities feel as comfortable as possible here.

In addition to the beds and bedside tables, all rooms are equipped with:

  • air conditioners;

  • TVs

  • Phones

  • safes.

Each room rented out at the hotel also has a small kitchenette where you can eat and cook toasts . Of course, there are rooms in this three-star hotel and private showers. Here, tourists can use not only free toiletries, but also a dryer for beach items.

The television at this hotel is satellite. For a fee, guests have the opportunity to rent a crib at the hotel. The hotel received good reviews from tourists, including for the availability of large refrigerators in the rooms, in which you can store products purchased in Sunny Beach stores.





The floors in the hotel rooms can be tiled or carpeted. The area of ​​the apartments is quite large (standard - 30 m 2 , apartments - 45-50 m 2 ). Rooms at Sunny Beauty Hotel include balconies . The loggias in this hotel, judging by the reviews of tourists, are also quite spacious and comfortable.

Infrastructure

Of course, this hotel offers its guests and all sorts of free amenities on the territory itself. For example, residents are free to use the pool. The hotel also has a fitness center. Here, if you wish, you can make a massage (for a hotel fee), visit the Turkish hammam or sauna. There is, of course, a large jacuzzi in the hotel.

In addition, the guests of the hotel Sunny Beauty Palace 4 * (Bulgaria, Sunny Beach ) are also provided with such amenities as:

  • laundry and dry cleaning;

  • gym;

  • unguarded free parking;

  • currency exchange point;

  • iron and ironing board on request.

Residents can enjoy free Wi-Fi in the lobby. For children, the hotel has a small playground.





Sunny Beauty Palace 4 *: hotel infrastructure reviews

Most tourists who choose Sunny Beach as their vacation destination find the Sunny Beauty Hotel relatively convenient and suitable for living. So, for example, many guests praise the pool of this hotel. The dimensions and depth it has are quite large. Therefore, swimming in it is comfortable and pleasant. There are a lot of sun loungers around and there are enough of them for absolutely all guests. In the pool, among other things, a small small children's part is fenced off.

Infrastructure, according to residents, in this hotel is not bad. Praised by vacationers and staff of this hotel. Although the maids do not treat their duties too well, they are always polite to the guests. The same goes for other hotel workers. The only thing, some tourists have complaints about the appearance of the hotel staff. They come to work in personal clothes. Meanwhile, much more pleasant hotel staff, according to many vacationers, would have looked in a single form. Yes, and the residents themselves in this case would be more clear who is who in the hotel.





Dining at the hotel

If desired, the Sunny Beauty Hotel can rent rooms with breakfast or even full board. Food at the hotel, unfortunately, is not praised by all guests. If there are few tenants at the hotel, the portions in the local buffet are usually served large. With an increase in the number of guests, they begin to gradually decrease. The menu in the hotel's dining room, according to many tourists, is not very diverse. And the dishes themselves, unfortunately, in some cases can be stale.

The hotel’s canteen is open for buffet. If desired, guests can take for breakfast, for example, omelets, food warmers, fried eggs, cucumbers, tomatoes, pastries. In addition to breakfast, lunch and dinner, this hotel also serves afternoon tea. This, of course, many tourists attribute to its undoubted advantages.





Beach Hotels

The sea from the Sunny Beauty Palace 4 * hotel (Sunny Beach) is about 10 minutes walk at a calm pace. Visitors to the complex are usually the local municipal beach. For recreation, it is considered quite convenient. The coastline in this place is covered with soft sand, and the entrance to the sea is very gentle. This beach is often cleaned, and therefore it looks absolutely clean.

Actually the sea itself in this place is usually calm, and the water in it is warm. If desired, tourists can rent a deck chair and an umbrella on this beach. This service is not too expensive.

Cocoa Beach

Some guests visit the resort of Sunny Beach and the famous Cocoa Beach. It is located in the southern part of the village and is named after the party organization club operating on its territory. This beach is especially popular among young people. Young tourists just love to gather here in the evenings. Due to the fact that there are always a lot of people on Cocoa Beach, the prices for fast food working on the coastline are usually very high.
