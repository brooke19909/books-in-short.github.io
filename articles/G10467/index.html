Hotel Alf Leila Wa Leila Hotel 4 * (Hurghada, Egypt): overview, description, reviews

If you want to visit Hurghada (Egypt), Alf Leila Wa Leila Hotel 4 * can be a suitable option for an inexpensive, but at the same time very comfortable stay.



Photo, description, location

The four-star Alf Leyla Wa Leyla Hotel is located 17 kilometers from the center of the Egyptian resort of Hurghada, on the territory of the huge restaurant and entertainment complex "A Thousand and One Nights." The hotel is located on the third coastline and is one and a half kilometers from the Red Sea. A free shuttle is available throughout the day to the private beach.

The Alf Leila Wa Leila Hotel 4 * hotel is built in traditional east style and has a huge territory. The housing stock is represented by 556 standard and family rooms. All apartments have the necessary furniture, a TV with cable channels, a telephone, a minibar (for a fee), air conditioning, a balcony, a safe, a bathroom. Cleaning is done every day. All guests receive the service "room service".

Food is organized on an all-inclusive basis. At the disposal of guests there are several restaurants offering dishes of Italian, Moroccan, Egyptian, international cuisine and fast food. There are also several bars and cafes on site. Almost everywhere there is access to Wi-Fi.

The hotel we are considering has seven pools. One of them has water slides. Also on the territory of the complex there is an amusement park "1000 and One Night", a gym, sports grounds, a sauna, billiards, sun terraces, an amphitheater, a mini club, a conference room, a tour desk, a currency exchange and much more. A professional animation team entertains guests all day. In the evenings, guest shows are held on site.





Cost of living

As our compatriots note, the price of accommodation in this hotel is fully consistent with its category and level of service. So, accommodation for 10 days here will cost you from 560 euros for a standard single room, from 740 euros for a double standard room and from 1050 euros for triple apartments of the standard category. The price of accommodation already includes meals on the ol inclusive system.

Cleaning

Judging by the comments, almost all the guests were satisfied with this item. So, according to tourists, the cleaners here do an excellent job with their duties. Population

As our compatriots note in their reviews, regardless of the time you arrive at the hotel, the administrators do everything possible so that the newly arrived guests can enter their room as quickly as possible. However, some tourists note the fact that travelers who have booked standard rooms are offered for a small fee to replace them with apartments of the best category. Some of our compatriots did just that, but in fact they were somewhat disappointed, since the standard rooms were no worse (only a few square meters closer) than the ones in which they were eventually settled. Therefore, experienced travelers recommend that all vacationers first inspect all the apartments offered, and then decide whether to pay extra. Otherwise, tourists had no complaints about the work of administrators at the reception.



Territory

Many tourists arriving for a vacation at Alf Leila Wa Leila Hotel 4 *, reviews of which we are reviewing, were without exaggeration delighted with the huge, well-planned, very beautiful and well-groomed territory of the hotel complex. So, according to them, there is a large number of diverse entertainment facilities, shops, sports grounds. In addition, the territory has a lot of beautiful places for walks and photo shoots.

Nutrition

This topic often causes a lot of controversy among tourists. And this concerns hotels of various categories not only in Egypt, but also in other countries. However, Alf Layla wa Layla is a pleasant exception in this regard. So, judging by the reviews of travelers, everyone was satisfied with the level of organization of food here. According to the guests, the choice of dishes can not be called very diverse and chic, however, the hotel is not five-star. In a word, our compatriots claim that the food here is organized in accordance with the category of the hotel complex.

So, there are as many as four restaurants at the guests' service - the main, Moroccan, Asian and Italian. Therefore, you can choose the kitchen to your taste. However, experienced tourists are advised to come to the restaurant at the beginning of lunch or dinner. In this case, you will have a greater choice of goodies, since not all dishes are cooked topping up. By the way, during the day you can also eat fast food on the beach or in the snack bar on site.





Beverages

On the territory of Alf Leila Wa Leila Hotel 4 *, the photo of which is presented in this article, there are several bars. Moreover, as tourists note, they are located very interesting. So, the bars are in one place (with the exception of institutions on the beach and by the pool) and form a kind of street. Therefore, sitting in one of the institutions, you will feel that you are not in a hotel, but in a small town. As for drinks, their choice seemed to travelers quite diverse. Many were also satisfied with the quality. The only complaints concerned the local whiskey, which our compatriots did not like. Of the alcoholic drinks, many tourists preferred beer and rum with cola. As for other drinks, the hotel guests praised the coffee served here.





Sea

As our compatriots note in their reviews, despite the fact that the Alf Layla Wa Layla hotel is located some distance from the coast, they were satisfied with their beach holidays here. So, first of all, according to them, the guests of the hotel complex do not need to walk to the sea. A free shuttle bus runs all day with a 10-minute interval between the beach and the hotel. However, some tourists with pleasure went to the sea and on foot. According to them, it took them no more than 15-20 minutes.

The beach here is at the disposal of three hotels. However, it is large, so there is enough space for everyone. There are enough sun beds and umbrellas. There is also a bar and snack bar on the beach where you can enjoy pizza, french fries, a hamburger and other fast food.

The beach itself is sandy. Entering shallow water. Most of the bottom is sand, but stones are found in some places. Therefore, tourists are advised to be careful when entering the sea so as not to injure their legs.

Near the coast there is a small coral reef. However, in beauty it can not be compared with the reefs off the coast of Sharm el-Sheikh. But still, you can watch some marine life here. In addition, travelers always have the opportunity to go on a sea excursion, which includes swimming next to beautiful and densely populated reefs.
