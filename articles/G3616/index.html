Pension Fund in Dolgoprudny: work schedule

There are always a lot of people in the branches of the pension fund . Calculation and calculation of pensions and benefits, registration of social benefits (loss of breadwinner, incapacity for work) and many other operations occur daily in the department of the pension fund in Dolgoprudny. You can find the address and schedule of the department in this article.

Pension Fund in Dolgoprudny





The Pension Fund helps to maintain the standard of living of the vulnerable groups of the population at the proper material level. What services can I get at a pension fund in Dolgoprudny? It:

  • registration of pensions for age and disability;
  • disability benefits;
  • drawing up benefits for single mothers, war veterans and people affected by the Chernobyl accident;
  • statement of retirement account status.




Some services are provided only with the personal presence of the applicant, while others are available for registration using a special electronic form. It is safe to say that the pension fund in Dolgoprudny is a guarantee of social protection of citizens, which people can count on when they reach retirement age or life's turmoil.

Opening hours

The schedule of the pension fund in Dolgoprudny directly depends on the day of the week. From Monday to Thursday, you can come to the department from 9:00 to 18:00. At the same time, applications are stopped 15 minutes before closing. The fund has a short day on Friday, the reception ends at 16:45. Saturday and Sunday are standard weekends for all government agencies. Be careful when planning your visit and find out the opening hours of the department in advance. It should also be noted that in the morning and evening hours people in the PF are much more than during the day. Lunch time at the pension fund comes at 13:00 and lasts 45 minutes.





A contact phone number for communication with the pension fund in Dolgoprudny can be found on the official website, and the department itself is located at: 2 Dolgoprudny city, Mayakovsky street.
