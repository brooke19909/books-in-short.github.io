Limanaki N Style Design Beach Hotel: description, features and reviews of tourists

It's no secret that one of the most popular places for recreation and travel is Cyprus. This country can offer tourists not only miles of golden beaches and warm sea waters, but also an interesting culture, a huge amount of modern entertainment and, of course, sincere hospitality.

One of the famous resort cities of Cyprus is Ayia Napa. On the territory of the resort area, many boarding houses and hotels of different sizes and categories have been built. One of them is the Limanaki N Style Design Beach Hotel. Of course, when planning a trip, people try to find as much information as possible about this hotel. Where is it located, is it far from the sea shore? What living conditions and meals are offered to guests? Is this place suitable for families with a child?

Description of the territory and location of the hotel





First of all, travelers, of course, are interested in the question of exactly where the Limanaki N Style Design Beach Hotel is located. The hotel is located in the very center of one of the most popular resorts in Cyprus Ayia Napa. The distance to the sea ‚Äã‚Äãshore is only 100 meters. Around it there are a huge number of shops, bars, shopping centers, night clubs and other entertainment venues.

The hotel itself is a complex of several buildings, to which a rather large territory is attached. Here you will find cosiness and modern comfort, as well as picturesque palm groves and a small garden where you can always hide in the shade of trees.

How many rooms are on site?

This is a medium-sized hotel, in the territory of which there are 72 comfortable rooms of the following categories:

  • Swimming Pool and Garden View Standard Rooms - rooms are 23 square meters, designed for two adults (+ child). It offers views of the pool or the picturesque garden.
  • Sea View Garden Rooms - the same standard rooms, but with breathtaking views of the seashore.
  • Superior Sea View Rooms - spacious rooms of 30 square meters. They are designed for 3 adults or a family of two parents and two children.

Hotel complex Limanaki Design N Style Beach Hotel (Cyprus): room description





Regardless of which number you choose, you can count on comfort. The rooms here are furnished with stylish, comfortable furniture. There is access to a terrace from which you can get directly to the courtyard, or an individual spacious balcony.

You can count on a basic set of household appliances, without which your stay is unlikely to be really comfortable. For example, guests can use the air conditioning system, creating the most pleasant temperature conditions. There is a TV (you can watch some satellite channels), as well as a telephone, a safe (for an additional fee), a minibar (it is filled only with water for free).

The bathroom has a shower, toilet, sink with a large mirror. Plumbing is modern, and water is supplied constantly. Guests can rely on hygiene products and daily towel changes.

Dining on site





Delicious food is an important part of a truly enjoyable and comfortable stay. What can Limanaki Design N Style Beach Hotel offer its guests? The hotel has two restaurants. Paying for accommodation, guests are entitled to free breakfast. If desired, tourists can immediately pay for full meals or just dine at local restaurants, each time paying the bill. Reviews of tourists indicate that the menu here is diverse - you can enjoy the exquisite taste of local, international and Mediterranean dishes.

In addition, the hotel has two bars that will delight you with a stylish atmosphere, a relaxed and pleasant atmosphere, as well as a large selection of drinks. Do not forget that there are many different establishments near the hotel, some of which work even at night, and therefore food is in no way a problem for vacationers.

Where is the beach located and are there conditions for water activities?

Can the guests of the Limanaki Beach Hotel 3 * hotel count on a water holiday? Cyprus is a country famous for its beaches. The hotel is located very close to the seashore - the distance is only 100 meters. A convenient road leads to the beach, and therefore daily walks are unlikely to be a burden to someone.





The coast here is covered with sand and small pebbles, as well as the seabed, and therefore travelers are advised to bring appropriate beach shoes with them. Since this is a public beach, you will have to pay for the use of sun loungers, umbrellas and towels. Nevertheless, the prices here are quite reasonable. By the way, there are many small cafes and bars on the shore, which is convenient, since you do not have to go to the city - you can have a snack right here.

Of course, tourists can count on a more active holiday. Here you can ride boats, canoes, catamarans, water skiing and boats. The coast is popular with fans of windsurfing and sailing. Travelers can also go scuba diving.

Additional services offered by the hotel





Of course, guests of the Limanaki N Style Design Beach Hotel hotel complex can count on a number of additional services. To begin with, it is worth noting that travelers have the opportunity to rent a safe from the administrator, as well as hand over unnecessary luggage for the duration of the rest to the left luggage office.

Laundry and dry cleaning services are also available. In case of health problems, one of the employees will definitely call a doctor. On the territory of the hotel there is a small shop where you can purchase household trifles, as well as interesting branded souvenirs from the hotel. Sometimes this place is used for various events, and therefore the hotel has a well-equipped, spacious conference room.

Leisure and entertainment for tourists





Of course, in the city center there are many entertainment centers, night clubs, shops. Nevertheless, you can have a good time on the territory of the Limanaki Design N Style Beach Hotel 4 * (3 *). For example, in the courtyard there is a large pool with a terrace, where guests like to spend time. Lying on comfortable sun loungers, you can sunbathe, relax and make new friends. And in the pool often arrange various games, such as playful competitions in water polo.

There is a wellness center where you can book massages, various beauty treatments, and also visit the sauna or gym. You can have a good time playing a game of table billiards. A TV lounge is constantly working, and those who wish can have fun thanks to a collection of video games.

In the evenings, the hotel organizes various shows and performances, and guests can dine while enjoying live music. Do not forget about excursions, because Cyprus is a country that has a lot to offer tourists.

Conditions for the rest of parents with children

It's no secret that for a family traveling with a child, slightly different criteria are important, namely the availability of conditions for a comfortable and fun stay of children. As already mentioned, Limanaki N Style Design Beach Hotel has spacious rooms with comfortable beds that are great for families. The restaurant has a sufficient selection of dishes, so parents can always choose something tasty and healthy for their baby.

As for entertainment, children can have a good time at the hotel. In the courtyard there is a special shallow pool where children of different ages can splash around. Also, children like to spend time on a spacious playground with swings, benches, slides and level lawns - here they can play and meet peers. If there is a need to leave the child for a short time, then guests can use the services of a professional nanny who will happily spend time with the baby, and the fee for her services is not so high.

Limanaki Beach Hotel (Cyprus, Ayia Napa): Travel Reviews





After talking with travelers who have already spent several days here, you can get a lot of really useful information. What do they say about the hotel complex Limanaki Design N Style Beach Hotel? Reviews, almost all without exception, are positive. This is a really good place for a beach holiday. At the same time, all city entertainment, including night clubs, will be in your reach, but you can relax in the room, enjoying the tranquility and silence.

The rooms here are spacious, furnished with comfortable furniture, and the equipment works fine. In the local restaurant you can eat, and in the hotel you can find yourself an interesting lesson.
