Unforgettable about cameras

Not bad things are with video recording and photography with Lenovo A859. The main camera features are as follows:

Memory

An impressive amount of memory can not boast of this phone model, but it is quite enough for the normal functioning of the device. RAM is 1 GB. Communications

The Lenovo A859 phone boasts a sufficiently large range of communications. Reviews The owners of this device also testify to this. Among the available data transfer standards, the following can be distinguished:

  • 2G and 3G networks. In the first case, GPRS and EDGE standards are supported. The maximum speed in this mode is several hundred kilobytes. For simple websites and social services this will be enough, but something more serious will be problematic to see. The situation with 3rd generation mobile networks is much better. Depending on the type of coverage, the data transfer rate can range from 5.76 Mbit / s (HSUPA standard) to 21.1 Mbit / s (HSDPA standard). In any case, this is quite enough to watch videos online, download large files and make video calls.
  • Another important transmitter is Wi-Fi. Such wireless networks have a small range (up to ten meters in diameter), and the data transfer rate in them can be up to 150 Mbps. It is in such networks that you can fully unlock the potential of your smartphone. Even the largest files load in minutes.
  • Bluetooth is also supported. The transmission speed of this standard is relatively low, and the radius of action at best will be ten meters. It is well suited for transferring small files to similar devices. In other cases, it is not practical to use it.
  • Micro-USB is a universal wired interface. It is mainly used to charge the battery. But if necessary, with its help you can connect such a device to a personal computer.
  • The last connector that is on the Lenovo A859 is a 3.5 mm audio jack. An external speaker system is connected to it.




Body and ergonomics

According to the performance, the Lenovo A859 smartphone belongs to the category of monoblocks with the ability to touch input information. The dimensions of the device are 142 mm by 72 mm. Moreover, its thickness is only 9.5 mm. Case material - plastic. The front panel is also made of it. Therefore, immediately you need to buy a protective film and a quality case. The control system is well thought out in this device by Chinese engineers. All mechanical buttons are grouped in the upper right corner. The on / off button is located on the upper edge, and the volume control swing is on the right. Touch buttons are located on the front panel under the screen. In this case, there are three of them: "Menu", "Back" and "Home". The display is made by capacitive technology, and there are no problems with the response.
