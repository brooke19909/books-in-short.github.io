Hotel Idyros Hotel 3 * Kemer: description and reviews

Kemer is a resort on the Turkish coast of the Mediterranean Sea. It is located in a picturesque valley between a mountain range overgrown with steppe grasses and a beach. The municipality is headed by the region, which includes the settlements of Goynuk, Beldibi, Tekirova and Kirish.

Oriental tale





The resort's cultural and nightlife is centered on Liman Street. In its vicinity there are many restaurants and cafes, clubs and discos, hotels and guest houses. From Idyros Hotel 3 * (Kemer) to the main pedestrian artery of the city a few minutes drive. On the way there are souvenir shops, clothing stores and home textiles. The road runs past parks and sports fields.

The municipality has the most popular clubs in the region. In "Aura", "Buddha" and "Inferno" can be reached on foot, by bus or taxi. The pride of the village is a wide pebble strip. Guests of Idyros Hotel 3 * (Kemer) relax in the equipped recreation area. It is part of a public beach. Next to her is Moonlight. The entrance is free. Sun beds and umbrellas are available on request.

Hotel Description





The hotel occupies a picturesque area. She stretched from the center to the yacht pier. At the disposal of residents is a restaurant, bars and eateries, a swimming pool. There is a free Wi-Fi connection. At Idyros Hotel 3 * (Kemer), guests play table tennis. The hotel offers bicycle rental. Car rental service is available. The tour desk organizes trips to Phaselis, Antalya, Olympos, Beldibi.

The hotel offers a group transfer. It offers family-run apartments. Residents enjoy free car parking, which is located next to the residential building Idyros Hotel 3 * in Kemer. The center of Kemer in Turkey in the high season suffers from a lack of free parking. Therefore, this option is always in demand among travelers who independently explore the surroundings.

A currency exchange service is available in the lobby. His course is not the most profitable. Thrifty travelers convert money in banks. Check in at the hotel from 13:00 to 17:30. The administration recommends leaving the room before 11:00.

The cash desk of the Idyros Hotel 3 * (Kemer) accepts cash and plastic cards of MasterCard, Visa, Maestro systems. Reception of new customers operates around the clock. Its employees solve problems associated with hotel accommodation.

Transport accessibility

Flights arriving from Europe are served by Antalya International Airport. It is fifty kilometers from Kemer. Its runways are served by ships from St. Petersburg, Moscow, Novosibirsk and other Russian cities. Idyros Hotel Kemer 3 * customers (Kemer) are met in the passenger terminal. They are brought to the hotel by comfortable bus. It is equipped with air conditioning and soft ergonomic chairs.

Independent tourists on a minibus get to the center of Antalya. If you choose a taxi, you have to fork out for 600 rubles. Long-distance liners regularly depart from the central bus station in Kemer. The fare is 150 rubles. Travel time is 40 minutes. If you wish, you can use the ferry. He sets sail from Antalya's pier twice a day. Further, the employees of Idyros Hotel 3 * (Kemer) in Kemer (center of the city) recommend to transfer by taxi.

Those traveling by car need to stay on the D400. The highway leads from Antalya to the very south of Turkey.

Terms of Accommodation





All hotel rooms are standard. They have recently been renovated. The walls are painted in discreet pastel colors. Windows cover translucent curtains. Ceramic tiles are laid on the floor. The room has beds, a nightstand, a refrigerator. Air conditioning is responsible for the microclimate.

A TV is connected to cable TV. A similar option is not often found in Kemer (Turkey). Idyros Hotel 3 * (Kemer, center) included it in a standard package of services. There is no need to pay extra for watching Russian-language channels. Wi-Fi phones and tablets in the rooms catch well. Internet speed is average. The bathroom has hygiene products, toiletries, towels, a shower, and a hairdryer.

Room service needs to be paid extra. The apartments offer views of the sea, garden or city street. You cannot select it in advance. For tips, reception staff can meet you. Tourists do not recommend choosing rooms that overlook the pool. The aquazone is always noisy. Fun sometimes lasts until the morning. The refrigerator is empty on the day of arrival. It does not even have mineral water. The stock of liquid soap and shampoo is replenished regularly. The maid is cleaning up every day. Linen changes every three days.

Nutrition





The gastronomic infrastructure is represented by a restaurant and bars. Morning, afternoon and evening meals are organized in the main dining room. Tables are set in the spacious hall, as well as on the street. Service is carried out according to the "Buffet" system. The menu is replete with dishes of fish, beef, chicken, vegetables and cereals. The price includes drinks made in Turkey. You will have to pay extra for imported alcohol.

Dining room hours:

  • 07:30 - 09:00 (breakfast);
  • 12:30 - 14:00 (lunch);
  • 16:00 - 17:00 (afternoon tea);
  • 19:30 - 21:00 (dinner).

The All Inclusive system begins to operate at 10:00 and ends at 22:00. The hotel bar opens at 10:00 and escorts its visitors after 22:00.

Near the hotel there is a market. Its stalls are bursting with local fruits and vegetables. It operates twice a week. Vacationers are happy to bargain with farmers. In June they buy cherries and apricots. In July, the turn of peaches and apples begins. In August, watermelons and honey melons ripen.

The grocery store, located on the territory of the hotel, sells delicacies, tea, coffee, cheese. Prices are quite affordable. An order table is provided.

Beach

Guests relax on the city beach. Admission is free. If you wish, you can rent sunbeds and umbrellas. Changing cabins, showers and toilets are installed. Tourists are offered excursions and boat trips. Photographers persistently invite you to pose. Children ride on inflatable "bananas" and "tablets." Adults soar skydiving.

The beach is covered with small and medium pebbles. Entrance to the water is even. But at a depth there are sharp stones and large boulders. Travelers recommend purchasing special shoes in advance that protect your toes and feet. It is dangerous to swim in the storm and with strong excitement. Rescue service closes the beach. There is no special bus that would run between the hotel and the coast.

Tourists get on their own. Some go on foot, others choose a taxi. There is no animation by the sea or in the hotel. Leisure is organized by third-party companies. Their services are not included in the tour price. On cloudy days, vacationers stay by the pool. Its area is 140 square meters. It functions only in the warm season. It is closed in the winter season.





Neighborhood

In walking distance from the hotel there are a lot of eateries and bistros. They have reasonable food prices, large portions and excellent service. The restaurants "Leon", "Lava Kebab", "Come in, Genatsvale!", "Caza La Luna" are popular among Russians. You can enjoy gourmet cuisine at the Seasons, Lotus, and Barbecue establishments.

Fine burgers and sandwiches are cooked in Panorama, Burger King, Has, in the Nur tavern. In Kemer, the most common Turkish, Mediterranean, Italian, French and European cuisines. Local culinary experts know a lot about seafood, pizza and steaks.

You can drink a cup of coffee or tea in the cozy interiors of the cafe "Captain Pirate", More, "Kemer Pastanesi". The Russians liked the hookah. There is a wide selection of vegetarian options. For halal, you need to contact the Paza Kebab restaurant. At lunch and in the evenings, all bistros are noisy and crowded. The staff speaks excellent Russian.

Local dishes are prepared with fresh produce right in front of visitors. At the same time, the cost of dishes is quite low. Hotel guests often looked at nearby candy stores to enjoy ice cream and pastries.

Excursions

The travel agency of the hotel offers a wide selection of educational and interesting trips to any corner of Turkey. Families with children choose day trips to Pamukkale. Their cost varies in the range from two to three and a half thousand rubles per person. If desired, in a mountain valley, you can stay a couple of days. The price of the trip in this case will be 6000 rubles.

Adherents of extreme types of entertainment choose rafting. After the descent along the bed of a mountain river, each participant in the adventure receives a video. Anyone who pays 2700 r can test their strength. A walk along the Goynuk canyon is inexpensive, but at the same time memorable. It is designed for just a few hours. Standard rate - 1200 p.

Kemer has everything you need for diving. Diving centers offer equipment and support for a professional diver. To personally see the underwater world of the Mediterranean Sea, you need to rent a yacht and move away from the coast.

For youth companies, the so-called motosafari are in high demand. These are ATV rides in the open spaces surrounding the Taurus Mountains.
