Shabby chic outfit

Let's look at how to make a shabby wooden blank in the style of shabby chic.

For this we need the following materials:

  • wooden blank;
  • paper napkins;
  • laundry soap;
  • picture or film for fidecor;
  • paraffin or wax (you can use a simple candle);
  • colorless varnish;
  • acrylic paint - light or dark.

Instruments:

  • sandpaper;
  • tassel.

Work Stages:

1. Place the wooden blank on a simple napkin. Take fine sandpaper and sand its surface. Blow off the dust.

2. Coat the workpiece with dark acrylic paint. Dry with a hairdryer.

3. Take paraffin and rub the board thoroughly on all sides. Pay particular attention to edges and corners.

4. Using a brush, coat the surface with a solution of any soap (but it is better to use a household one). Dry with a hairdryer.

5. Take a light acrylic paint. Apply it to the workpiece in a very thin layer. Make sure that dark paint does not appear. Dry it.

6. Use sandpaper to create small scuffs.

7. Varnish the entire surface of the workpiece. Dry it.

8. Take a film for Friedecor, moisten and attach to the product. Gently smooth with a paper towel.

9. Coat the entire blank with clearcoat again. Dry it.

As you saw, the hand-made is quite simple, and its technique is available to everyone.
