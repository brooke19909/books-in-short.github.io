Kaldy - a lake for good fishing and wonderful relaxation

Kaldy is a lake in the north of the Chelyabinsk region. It owes its origin, like most Ural bodies of water, to a tectonic shift. The lake is small, its area totals a little more than 1.5 thousand hectares. Oz. Kaldy has an elongated shape: length - about 6 kilometers, width - more than 4. The water in the lake is fresh, with a slight salty aftertaste. The pond is quite transparent - visibility up to 3 meters. The average depth is 3 m, the maximum is 7. The bottom of Kalda, like its shores, is mostly sandy. In places, reeds grow, and the bottom is covered with silt. The user of the lake is the Balyk fish factory.

Fish factory "Balyk"

Fishing, as a rule, is carried out on a specially allocated territory of the lake tenant - the Balyk fish factory. It is through the efforts of the enterprise on Kaldy that fishermen are always provided with a good catch. Every year, the company carries out stocking of the lake. In 2007, 100 thousand year-old carps, 13 million fry of cigaripus, 20 thousand pike fry and 2 million burbot fry were released. The fish took root. On Kaldy you can catch carp - up to 10 kilograms, pike - up to three and cigaripus - up to kilogram. In 2008, thousands of trout fry were released into Kaldy Lake (Chelyabinsk Region). Fishing

Kaldy - the lake is mostly quiet, only occasionally there is a storm and waves up to 1 meter high. Fishing here is excellent both from the shore and by boat, with the only difference that you can catch larger fish at a depth. You can fish all year round - there is no break for spawning. Summer fishing begins as soon as the ice melts. A ticket costs 300 rubles per person per day. The catch is not limited. You can purchase a document on the beaches, recreation centers and in settlements on the shore (Sary, Big Taskino and Suleymanovo). There are benefits for children under 14 years of age, disabled people of the 1st and 2nd groups, Chernobyl victims and people over 70 years old. Participants in hostilities can fish for free if you have a document. Underwater hunting is in great demand in the reservoir, since Kaldy is a lake with clean water. Various championships and competitions are often held here. In summer - for carp, in winter - for whitefish.
