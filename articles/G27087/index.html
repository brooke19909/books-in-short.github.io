Bamboo Village Kata: hotel description, infrastructure and holiday reviews

Located on the island of Phuket, in the Kingdom of Thailand, Bamboo Village Kata presents a hotel complex consisting of several bungalows of different categories and one-story buildings with rooms. Ideal for those tourists who appreciate a quiet, relaxing holiday, devoid of fuss and noise. Lovely neat little houses are surrounded by incredibly bright and fragrant tropical greenery. In such an atmosphere it is pleasant to relax not only during the day, but also at night, when pleasant cool air bursts into the open windows.

Location Bamboo Village Kata 3 *

This 3-star hotel is located on the shores of Kata Bay, in a quiet area on Kata Road. In the immediate vicinity there is a private beach with white sand (600 meters to it) and the most important sights of the island. Phuket Airport is a little less than 50 km away. Nearby, tourists will find almost everything they need: shops, restaurants and cafes, massage parlors and some other public places.

Description of hotel and rooms





Bamboo Village Kata consists from one-story bungalows. Several houses are arranged in a row, the rest are a bit distant. Almost all of them are concrete. The complex occupies a small territory, but beautifully landscaped: the picturesque landscape forms lush, vibrant tropical greenery, which is inhabited by many different animals.

The last time the rooms were repaired was in 2014. There are only 45 rooms for tourists. These include bungalows:

  • Category A - for two people, 22 square meters. m
  • Category B - 2 main places and 1 additional, 26 sq. m
  • Category C - for four people, 32 square meters. m

There are also 32 sqm family rooms. m. They are located in one-story buildings and can accommodate a maximum of 4 people. Some bungalows have a private furnished terrace. All have a bedroom with a bed, wardrobe, armchairs, a desk, chairs, a cupboard and a dressing table. There is a bathroom with shower and toilet. From the equipment in the rooms you can see the TV and air conditioning, there is also a safe and a minibar, daily replenished with plain water.

Territory Bamboo Village Kata, Phuket . Featured Services





The property has its own large outdoor pool surrounded by sun loungers. There is a tour desk, in addition, you can rent a car or motorcycle, or order a taxi to go on your own to explore the island. Vacationers eat in a restaurant. For an extra fee, you can visit the internet cafe and book Thai massages. If you need to wash and iron clothes, you can go to the laundry for services.

All guests on the hotel territory can use the Internet unlimitedly through the WI-FI network. There are other paid services:

  • Extra bed in the room.
  • Calls from a telephone / payphone.
  • Late check out and early check in.
  • Food in the restaurant.
  • Water activities, sun loungers and parasols on the beach.

Dining at the hotel

At Bamboo Village Kata only one option is possible - Bed & Breakfast, so only breakfast is included in the price. Lunch and dinner are served in the restaurant on request and at an additional cost. There is also a catering facility near your hotel. There is no possibility to cook food on your own in the room, so you do not need to buy convenience foods in the store.

Recreation and entertainment





Given that in Bamboo Village Kata 3 *, except for the tour desk and massage room, there is nothing more, the hotel is suitable exclusively for lovers of a calm, measured rest. Looking at the photo, you can see that the hotel is literally surrounded by greenery, and therefore walking around the mini-park will be the most pleasant and desired entertainment for tourists. If you still want to take action, you should go to the beach. There you can enjoy plenty of games on the water and on land.

There are good and bad reviews about the Bamboo Village Kata. However, regular guests claim that this place is excellent for discerning people, and the rest should not come here, so as not to spoil your vacation and hotel rating.
