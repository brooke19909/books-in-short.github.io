Hotel Verde 4 * (ex S Hotel), Marmaris, Turkey: description, leisure and hotel reviews

Marmaris is one of the best resorts in Turkey. In addition, he is considered the most "European". Here the British and Germans like to relax. Unlike, for example, Antalya, where the vast majority of tourists come from the CIS countries. Marmaris attracts vacationers with a wonderful climate, calm and clean sea, well-groomed beaches, as well as hotels and entertainment for every taste. Today we invite you to get acquainted with one of the hotel complexes of this resort called Hotel Verde 4 *. We will find out what this hotel is, where it is located, what services are provided to guests here. In addition, we will find out what entertainment is available on vacation in Marmaris.





Where is Hotel Verde 4 * (Turkey)

This hotel complex is located in Icmeler - one of the resort villages near Marmaris. The city is eight kilometers from the hotel. The nearest airport is Dalaman, a hundred kilometers away from the village. The journey from the air harbor to the hotel usually takes one and a half to two hours. Since Hotel Verde 4 * is located on the first line, the sea is within walking distance from it. So, those who want to plunge into the purest salt water will have to overcome only one hundred meters.

Hotel Description

Hotel Verde (ex S Hotel) was built in 1992. The last large-scale restoration was carried out relatively recently - in 2011. The hotel used to be called S Hotel. The total area of ‚Äã‚Äãthe hotel is just over three thousand square meters. Hotel Verde consists of only one six-story building, equipped with elevators. The hotel complex has 102 rooms. The hotel itself positions itself as an ideal place for family and youth recreation.





Hotel Policies

In accordance with the internal regulations adopted at Verde Hotel 4 * (Marmaris), the settlement of tourists in their booked rooms is carried out from two in the afternoon. If you arrived earlier, then you will be settled immediately. As for the rules of departure, the check-out is made until twelve o'clock in the afternoon.

Since the hotel positions itself as a family, children are allowed here. If your child is under six years old and sleeps in the beds in the rooms, then his stay will be free. If a child is between seven and twelve years old in an extra bed, 50 percent of the standard cost per person will be charged for his stay. Also keep in mind that it is possible to put only one extra bed in each room.

As for tourists who prefer to travel with their four-legged pets, then, unfortunately, we have to disappoint them. The fact is that Hotel Verde 4 * does not allow pets on its territory.

You can pay for your stay and all additional services on the day of departure in cash or with plastic cards of the Visa and MasterCard systems.





Number of rooms

In total, the hotel has 102 rooms. The average area of ‚Äã‚Äãeach of them is approximately 22 square meters. 101 rooms are standard, and another room is designed for people with disabilities. Rooms are cleaned on a daily basis. Linen is changed twice a week.

Each room at Hotel Verde has a bathroom with shower, a balcony with excellent views of the sea or the surrounding area, individual air conditioning, hairdryer, TV and telephone. The floors in the rooms are ceramic coated. For an extra charge, a safe and minibar are also available. Each room is designed for two people with the ability to put only one extra bed.





Sea beach

Verde Hotel 4 * (Icmeler) has its own beach. It is located just a hundred meters from the hotel. However, on the way to the beach, it is necessary to overcome the undeveloped road and promenade. The length of the beach is twenty meters. It offers hotel guests free sunbeds, umbrellas and mattresses. Towels must be brought with you. Beach Hotel Verde 4 *, reviews about which the tourists leave the most positive, is pebbly and sandy and has a gentle approach to the sea, which allows children to play safely in the water near the shore.

Meals at the four-star hotel Verde (Marmaris, Turkey)

The diet in Hotel Verde 4 * is carried out according to the all-inclusive system. Three times a day - for breakfast (from 7:30 to 10 a.m.), lunch (from 12:30 to 2 p.m.) and dinner (from 7:30 to 9:30 p.m.) - for guests at the restaurant a buffet is served. The dishes here are always very diverse, so everyone will find something tasty for themselves and will never stay hungry. In addition, the restaurant has a children's menu. So the little guests of the hotel will be satisfied.

In between main meals, you can grab a bite to eat at the snack bar next to the pool. Here from 2:30 a.m. to 4:00 p.m. light snacks will be offered to you.

As for soft alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks of local production, they are provided to hotel guests free of charge. But for drinks such as cappuccino, Turkish coffee, espresso, freshly squeezed juice or any foreign-made alcohol, you will have to pay separately.

In total, the hotel has two restaurants, two bars, a snack bar, as well as an a la carte restaurant.





Infrastructure

Hotel Verde Hotel 4 * (Marmaris) has a fairly developed infrastructure. So, on the territory of the hotel there is parking, the use of which is free for guests. Therefore, if you decide to go on vacation in your car or rent a car directly in Turkey, then you will not have problems with parking.

The hotel reception is open around the clock. Therefore, if for any reason you are forced to check in or check out at an inopportune hour, or there is an urgent problem, then you can always immediately contact the duty administrator. The hotel also provides luggage storage. If you are used to maintaining yourself in excellent physical shape, you can also visit the gym located in the hotel. In the public areas of the hotel complex there is access to wireless Internet.

In addition, the four-star Verde Hotel offers its guests the following list of paid services: room service, dry cleaning, laundry, hairdresser, nanny, internet cafe. If someone from your family or friends feels unwell, you can contact the medical center available at the hotel. Here, a qualified doctor will provide the patient with the necessary assistance.

For business people, Hotel Verde is ready to provide an equipped conference room. Using it is paid.





Entertainment at the Verde Hotel (Marmaris)

If you like to spend time sitting comfortably on a sun lounger by the pool and sipping a cool cocktail, then this hotel will definitely appeal to you. So, on the territory of the Verde hotel there are two swimming pools for adults (one outdoor and one indoor), as well as a swimming pool intended for the youngest tourists. Near the pools there are sun loungers and umbrellas, the use of which is free for hotel guests. There is also a bar where you can always get both alcoholic and non-alcoholic soft drinks.

If you want more active entertainment, you can play darts or table tennis. Lovers of relaxation with the benefit of their health can attend a massage, as well as a steam bath in the sauna. These services are paid.

Verde Hotel 4 * (Marmaris): reviews

As for the opinions of tourists from Russia and other CIS countries living here, most of them agree that the hotel is in full compliance with its four stars. Many are happy to recommend Hotel Verde to their friends and relatives. Most of the tourists in their reviews note daily cleaning, clean comfortable rooms, friendly and helpful staff, as well as the proximity of the beach.

Some guests were a little confused by the music and noise coming late from the hotel bars. Tourists who came to rest with children were mostly unhappy with this fact. Young people, as a rule, do not bother such noise.

Also, opinions of vacationers were divided about food. So, some of the guests of the Verde Hotel in Marmaris considered the menu to be scarce, and the food was tasteless and did not match the declared four stars of the hotel. The rest of the tourists expressed no complaints about food. In their opinion, the set of dishes here, of course, cannot be called very diverse, but there is always something to choose from to your taste. In addition, there is a children's menu. Therefore, neither adult tourists, nor young guests of the hotel will never remain hungry.





A little about the village of Icmeler

The village of Icmeler, in which the Verde Hotel is located, is located in a compact, very picturesque bay, seven kilometers west of the resort town of Marmaris. Icmeler is famous for its excellent beaches and beautiful green scenery. From Icmeler to Marmaris you can take a pleasant walk along the promenade that separates the village from the strip of beaches. You can also get to the city by taxi or minibus (here they are called dolmushi). The last mode of transport runs here from seven in the morning until half past one in the morning.

In Icmeler special entertainment can not be found. But here you can eat deliciously in one of the many restaurants. There are also many shops in the village where you can buy souvenirs as a souvenir, as well as everything you need for your vacation. In addition, a market operates here every Wednesday.

Things to do in Marmaris

If you want entertainment outside the hotel, then go to Marmaris. Fans of sightseeing can visit the city museum, the exhibition of which is located in the castle of Calais, built by the Ionians back in 1044 BC. Another historical monument of the city is the caravanserai of Sultan Hafsa, built in the XVI century. Of great interest to tourists is the Ibrahim Aga Mosque, erected at the end of the 18th century.

In addition, a lot of excursions to various sights of the Republic of Turkey are organized from Marmaris. So, from here you can go to the island of Cleopatra (Cedir), where the ruins of the ancient city called Kedrai are located, as well as the world famous Cleopatra beach. You can also take a tour of Pamukkale with its unique thermal springs inscribed on the UNESCO World Heritage List. From Marmaris, tours are organized to the ancient city ‚Äã‚Äãof Ephesus, as well as to the turtle island of Dalyan.

Fans of nightlife will definitely appreciate the discos and nightclubs of Marmaris. Particularly noteworthy here is the Disco Maxim disco, located on Ataturk Avenue, and Turban, located in the village of the same name.

Also in Marmaris there are two water parks - Atlantis and Aqua Dream. You can come here all day.

Marmaris and diving enthusiasts will not leave indifferent. So, in the vicinity of the city there are more than fifty diving spots. Under water, you can admire the picturesque antique ruins and flocks of cute fish and other marine life.
