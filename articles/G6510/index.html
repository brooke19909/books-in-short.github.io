Turkey, Manavgat - the true charm of a miniature

Oh! This beautiful Turkey ... Manavgat is the third largest city of Konya district after Alanya. It is here that the road connects Antalya and Mersin, which is of great importance from the point of view of tourism. Manavgat Waterfall is one of Turkey's most popular attractions. If you want to take a break from the loud noise of the surf and enjoy the invigorating coolness of the water, as well as get new impressions of natural beauties, then go to Manavgat.

Climate

The Turkish Riviera is a wonderful place to vacation at any time of the year. The best time for traveling is from May to June. Spring and autumn are perfect for sightseeing and cultural monuments; at that time Turkey, including Manavgat, has a mild and comfortable climate for humans.

sights

Despite its miniature, the city has something to show. The attraction that attracts crowds of tourists from year to year is Manavgat Waterfall. Turkey is truly proud of it. The unusual name came from the word "Manauva", which is translated from the ancient language as "the temple of the goddess." 12 kilometers from the waterfall, Oymapinar Dam is located - the largest and truly impressive hydraulic structure in Turkey.





Admire the strong flow of water, appreciate the beauty of the reservoir and try freshly caught fish in a nearby cafe - an unforgettable experience! Go to Lake Titreegel, and you will surely get a magical experience from the contemplation of turtles basking on warm stones.

Kitchen

Turkey, the city of Manavgat is a paradise for lovers of fresh fish. Here it is caught from the river, so fish dishes always have good taste and aroma. A small town is a real concentration of cafes, restaurants and eateries, at which prices are quite reasonable. The portions are large and mouth-watering, so no one will go hungry!

Turkey. Manavgat and the issue of accommodation



Visitors really have plenty to choose from, because the city is literally full of hotels and pensions offering rooms ranging from standard to luxury apartments. It is worth noting one of the most visited places in Manavgat - Villa Lapin boarding house. It is remarkable for its location - on the banks of the river, in a quiet and comfortable place. However, regardless of the place chosen, the level of service is almost the same everywhere - aimed at meeting all the needs of travelers and providing high quality services.

You can stay near Manavgat, in Side, 5 km away. The cheapest accommodation options here are private guest houses.

How to get there

By car, you can reach your destination via the D400. From the major cities of Turkey to Manavgat can be reached by bus or minibus.
