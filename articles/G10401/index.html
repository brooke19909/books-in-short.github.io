Description of resorts in Turkey: where to go for a vacation

Turkey, the description of the resorts of which many travelers are interested in, occupies a leading place in the list of vacation spots of Russians. Tourists in this country are offered a high level of service at relatively low prices. Those who are going here for the first time are also interested in the reviews of travelers who spent their holidays here.

I must say that the country is washed by three seas, however, the beaches are located mainly on the Aegean and Mediterranean coasts. Despite the fact that hotels are ready to accept tourists all year round, Russians prefer to relax at local resorts mainly in late spring and summer.

In the south of Turkey is the Antalya coast with many convenient beaches and comfortable hotels operating on the popular all-inclusive scheme. If you make a description of the resorts of Turkey, then the cities located here are worth noting.

So, in Antalya itself, travelers can enjoy not only the best sandy beaches of the country, but also the many activities offered. There are night bars and discos, fashionable shops and colorful oriental bazaars. Antalya hotels are most often referred to 4- and 5-star establishments. Most of them have their own pools, as well as a spacious surrounding area.





In the south of the country there are other resorts of Turkey, no less comfortable for holidaymakers. Description of Kemer can be found on many tourist sites, because this place is very popular among travelers. The city is located near the mountains with dense pine vegetation. The beach here is pebble, but thanks to this the sea is clean and transparent. Kemer itself is a fairly lively place in which young people prefer to relax. Tourists are offered a lot of entertainment. Hotels in the city fall into various categories: from cheap 2-star to luxury 5 * hotels.





Description of Turkish resorts should be continued by Belek, considered one of the most expensive, but at the same time the most comfortable. Almost all local hotels are designed for VIP-clients. Many hotels are designed for families. The main entertainment is offered by the hotels themselves. Active tourists who prefer "hangouts" can be a little boring here. Making a description of the resorts of Turkey, Belek can be attributed to a place that is more suitable for a quiet relaxing holiday.

The Aegean coast is less popular among Russians, but quite common among European tourists. The beaches here are pebble and rocky. Local resorts in Turkey, the description is: quite comfortable and lively cities. The style of rest in them developed under the influence of tourists from Europe who prefer to spend time and have fun outside the hotel, so the "all incluisive" system is not so common here.

When describing the resorts of Turkey on the Aegean, it is necessary to mention such cities as Marmaris, Bodrum, Sarigerme. Here, travelers have the opportunity to rent a yacht, visit a water park or simply enjoy the picturesque local vegetation.
