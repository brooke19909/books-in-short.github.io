Good Nice Hotel 3 *: tourist reviews

Relaxation on the beach is something that every person needs from time to time. And many travelers choose Thailand as their vacation spot. This exotic country has a lot to offer tourists. Here you will find picturesque beaches, rich excursions, good service and relatively inexpensive accommodation.

One of the most famous resort cities in Thailand is Phuket. It is here, not far from the beach, that the cozy Good Nice Hotel 3 * hotel is located. Of course, when planning a trip, everyone is trying to get the most useful information. What does the hotel look like and what conditions does it offer? Should I spend a vacation here? How tourists respond about the hotel? The answers to these questions are sought by many travelers.

Description of location and hotel territory





Naturally, travelers are primarily interested in the location of the Good Nice Hotel 3 * hotel. Phuket is a vibrant resort town visited by thousands of tourists. It is here, on the third coastline, that the hotel is located. By the way, the distance to the international airport is about 37 km. You should not worry about this, as the hotel provides its guests with a convenient shuttle service for a small price.

Approximately 900 m away is the popular public beach Patong. Here you can always find a lot of entertainment. The hotel is literally surrounded by small shops and shops, cozy cafes, in a word, everything you need for a truly eventful vacation.

The hotel itself consists of a small four-story building. A small but clean and comfortable area is distinguished by an abundance of greenery and interesting national scenery.

Hotel Good Nice Hotel 3 *: photo and brief information about the rooms





This is a small cozy hotel, which consists of 36 rooms. The rooms here are spacious, clean and comfortable, with comfortable furniture and a private balcony. There is a private bathroom, as well as a set of household appliances.

For example, you can use a modern air conditioning system. You can relax while watching television channels. Also, guests can use the phone, Internet access. There is a mini fridge and coffee and tea making facilities.

Are there any catering facilities on site?





Paying for your stay at the Good Nice Hotel 3 *, guests are entitled to daily breakfasts, which are held in the form of a buffet in the hotel restaurant. Reviews of tourists suggest that the choice of dishes is quite suitable: morning toasts with jam, freshly baked buns, cereals, juices, fragrant tea, etc. The rest of the time, you can have lunch and dinner in the same restaurant, however, orders are held √† la carte and are paid separately. Guests love Thai dishes from local chefs, although more familiar Western food can also be ordered. The hotel also has a nice bar where you can have fun.

By the way, near the hotel there are a huge number of restaurants and cafes where you can eat well, so meals will not be a problem.

Where is the beach located?





Of course, travelers go to Thailand primarily for relaxing on the beach. Patong Beach, famous and popular with tourists, is located about 900 meters from the Good Nice Hotel 3 * hotel complex. A convenient road leads to it. Those tourists who do not want to spend time on daily walks can use the rented transport or taxi services.

It is a public and very lively beach. For a reasonable price, you can rent a deck chair with a mattress and a sun umbrella for almost the entire day. The beach here is covered with soft sand, and, despite the abundance of tourists, the coast is always clean. Nearby are numerous cafes and beach restaurants where you can have a bite to eat or get a chilled drink. And on the shore, various entertainment events are regularly organized, including night parties.

Beach activities for guests

Not all tourists can be satisfied with swimming in the sea and sunbathing. You should not worry about this, because fans of outdoor activities can also find a pleasant pastime. There is a water sports center on the shore where you can rent a boat or a catamaran.

For a small price, tourists will have the opportunity to go water skiing, take a boat trip on a boat, go sailing and even scuba diving. In the sports center you can rent the necessary equipment, suits and even take a few lessons from professionals.

Hotel Good Nice Hotel: list of additional services





Of course, every traveler needs a certain share of comfort. What conditions can the hotel offer Good Nice Hotel 3 *? Of course, guests can count on high-quality service.

To begin with, it is worth saying that things can be taken to the laundry and dry cleaning. If you wish, you can rent a safe from the administrator, which guarantees the safety of your belongings. For tourists who are going to travel around the country, a luggage room will be useful, where you can leave unnecessary things for a while.

In public areas there is wireless Internet access, which is important for almost every modern traveler. If necessary, you can use the services of a local Internet cafe, as the prices here are quite affordable. There is also a small mini market where you can get the necessary household items, as well as some food.

The hotel has a rental service where you can rent a car, motorcycle and bike. By the way, this service is very popular among tourists, since the availability of their own transport, even a bicycle, greatly simplifies the movement around the city and makes it possible to independently explore the surroundings. You can leave transport at the hotel parking, which guests use for free.

Leisure and entertainment for travelers

Hotel Good Nice Hotel 3 * is designed for a quiet rest and maximum relaxation, but because there is not so much active entertainment here. In the courtyard there is a small pool and terrace, where you can sunbathe and relax on comfortable sun loungers. Also, guests can visit massage rooms, where professional masters offer their services. Thai massage is something that every tourist should try at least once.

There are other attractions that the Good Nice Hotel 3 * can offer. Thailand is ideal for travel and excursions. The hotel staff will be happy to help you make a route for an independent trip, as well as purchase ready-made sightseeing tours.

Hotel Good Nice Hotel 3 *: tourist reviews





Every traveler knows that after talking with tourists who have already managed to visit a particular hotel, you can get much more really useful information. So what do they say about the hotel Good Nice Hotel 3 *? Reviews are for the most part positive. Guests note that the rooms are really comfortable, and all the necessary appliances are available. The rooms are cleaned daily; it is always clean and comfortable here.

The territory is small, but all the conditions necessary for a comfortable stay are available. Employees are polite, pleasant in communication, always sincerely willing to help. Naturally, the beach is located at some distance, but the road to it does not bring discomfort. Yes, and low cost compensates for all the inconvenience. The hotel is perfect for a budget holiday on one of the most popular beaches in Thailand. Families with children, young companies, newlyweds and even business people often stay here.
