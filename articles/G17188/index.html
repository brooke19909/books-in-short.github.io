Vladivostok. Hotel "Pearl": description, services, reviews. Cheap hotels in the center of Vladivostok

As a rule, a trip to Vladivostok is business-like for many tourists. People go to find work or negotiate. But some travelers still come here for other purposes, for example, to see the city or visit relatives. In any case, the main issue is finding an overnight stay. There are a lot of comfortable inexpensive hotels. This article will present accommodation options in the central part of the city.

Hotel "Pearl"





An excellent accommodation option will be for tourists and business travelers planning to visit Vladivostok, Hotel Pearl. The hotel is located almost in the center.

Nearby there are all the necessary infrastructure facilities - shops, catering places, seaport, railway station. The hotel is located in the historical and administrative center of the city. It takes only 10 minutes to get to the maritime station; it takes 50 minutes to get to the airport. These are insignificant distances for such a large city as Vladivostok. Hotel "Pearl" attracts tourists with its location, as well as affordable prices, so it is best to book accommodation in advance. The hotel has 89 rooms of various categories.

The design of the apartments is quite comfortable, there is everything necessary for the complete comfort of the guests. The administration is very attentive to each guest, so there will be no problems with the service. In addition to housing, the hotel offers a conference room, sauna, billiards, there are also cinema halls and a karaoke bar.

Such an institution can accommodate both an ordinary tourist and a person who came for business purposes to Vladivostok. Hotel "Pearl" was able to competently combine these two segments, namely a good rest and good conditions for conducting business negotiations.

Additional services

In addition to basic amenities, household-level services are offered, namely, laundry, taxi, minor clothing repairs, laptop rental. There is also free internet access. You can use the left-luggage office. A full range of services is offered to visitors to such a city as Vladivostok Hotel Pearl.

Guest reviews





Former hotel guests say that absolutely all conditions are created here for fruitful work and the most comfortable stay. Very pleased with the pricing policy of the institution. But despite the low cost of living, all rooms are furnished with comfortable and beautiful furniture. And below is a cafe where you can always have a tasty meal and hold various kinds of events, ceremonial or business.

Hotel "Sailor"



Another worthy option for accommodation during your stay in Vladivostok. Like the previous hotel, it is located in the historical center of the city, where you can visit a large number of museums and see historical monuments. The hotel itself offers a wide range of services. Guests have the opportunity to use the laundry room, luggage room with a safe and other amenities. The reception is open around the clock, so check-in can occur at any time of the day. The hotel can be reached within 40 minutes from the airport.

All apartments are furnished with necessary furniture. Moreover, each room is very spacious and bright. For business people there is a desktop where you can sit with papers and a laptop. There is also a dressing room, private bathroom. Each room has climate control. There is free Internet access and a special meeting room.

You can have a tasty meal in the restaurant. The menu offers dishes of European cuisine that will satisfy every guest. Also on the territory there is a small coffee shop where excellent coffee is brewed. All services are aimed at improving the comfort level of guests.

Hotel "Sailor" (Vladivostok) is popular among tourists, as it has a good value for money service.
