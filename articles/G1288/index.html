Double-sided knitting patterns: simple and beautiful knitting

Each craftswoman, who has been engaged in needlework for more than one year, stores several original knitting patterns in her memory. As a rule, these are bilateral patterns with knitting needles. They will always help out when you need to choose how to knit some product. This is especially true for scarves. After all, while wearing this piece of clothing can twist. And due to the fact that a double-sided openwork pattern with knitting needles was used for its manufacture, the scarf will also look great from the wrong side. That is why such knitting always remains in fashion.

Beautiful, fashionable, dignified

Autumn has come. And with it the cooling began, more often the winds began to blow. And if September can still be considered warm, then October and November, as a rule, force everyone to dress warmer - in jackets, coats, hide their heads under hats, hats and berets, and always wear a scarf around their necks. We will talk about how to tie it better.

The needlewomen try to decorate themselves with scarves of their own production when the weather becomes windy and very cold. By the way, such bilateral patterns with knitting needles will be useful not only for making scarves, but also for working on shawls or rugs. After all, these things should look good on both sides. In addition, the products connected in this way will not be wrapped on the sides, and their general appearance will be very decent.

How does the "Polish gum" fit?

In the article, we will consider several bilateral patterns with knitting needles (patterns will be provided just below).





Quite unusual is the Polish Gum pattern. Thanks to him, you can knit elastic scarves. And the principle of operation is very simple. It is important that the number of loops in the rapport should be a multiple of four:

  • The first loop should be seamy, then - in a row, three facial loops, then again - the seamy and again three facial loops.
  • In the second row, first we knit two purl, then - one facial loop, three purl, one more facial and another purl.
  • The third row fits in the same way as the first, and the fourth - like the second.

Rice and Pearls

"Pearl" ornament is by far one of the most popular. Moreover, both among knitters and among owners of things with this pattern. After all, the relief in it is quite original. The structure of the pattern is very flexible, and creating it is very simple. Double-sided knitting patterns like this one fit only by alternating front and back loops.





If you start from rapport, then it has two loops horizontally and as many vertically. It is such an openwork pattern that looks equally nice on both sides, very suitable to create amazing ladies' scarves.
