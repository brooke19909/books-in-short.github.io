Hotel Heritage Village Club Goa 4 * (India, Goa): photos and reviews of tourists

India is a country with a unique history and culture. It is only natural that it is popular with travelers from all over the world. And over the past few years, the state has become increasingly popular. The most developed tourism industry in the areas of the picturesque state of Goa. After all, here an incredible exotic nature is perfectly combined with a unique culture, modern amenities and quality service.

On the coast of Goa there are many resort settlements and, of course, hotels. One of the rather popular places is the hotel complex Heritage Village Club Goa 4 *. Naturally, when planning a trip, people are interested in additional information. For example, an important issue is the availability of conditions in the hotel that can provide comfort. Is the hotel suitable for families with a child? Is the beach close to it? How do other tourists respond about the hotel?

Hotel Location Information

Location - an important criterion for tourists choosing a place to stay. After all, you always want a beach nearby, and not all travelers like to spend time away from big cities. So where to look for the hotel complex Heritage Village Club Goa 4 *? Arossim, a small resort village on the very shore of the ocean in the southern part of Goa - it is here, next to the shore, that the hotel is located.

The area here is certainly quiet and calm, so your vacation will not be overshadowed by noise. However, the center of Dabolim is only 8 km away. It can be easily overcome by rented transport or taxi, and there is still the opportunity to use public routes. The international airport is located 13 km from the hotel Heritage Village Club Goa 4 *. Tours, by the way, most often already include a convenient transfer from / to the airport, so you should not worry about the trip.

Heritage Village Club Goa 4 *: hotel description





This cozy hotel complex is considered one of the most popular holiday destinations on the south coast of Goa. By the way, this place is one of the most popular among British tourists, some of them come here every year. Hotel Heritage Village Club Goa 4 * was opened in 1997, and several years ago a complete reconstruction was carried out here. By the way, it was here that for the first time tourists began to offer the all-inclusive system.

The hotel itself consists of two-story buildings with snow-white walls and a red tiled roof. The buildings are located around the perimeter, forming a courtyard closed from outsiders. The territory here is incredibly beautiful - everywhere there are palm trees, mown lawns, flower beds and vibrant tropical plants. The hotel resembles a large country villa, and the service here is at a really high level.

How many rooms does the hotel offer?

The hotel complex is considered not too big. On its territory there are 97 spacious comfortable rooms. Naturally, customers are offered a choice of standard rooms, as well as rooms with an increased level of comfort of class suite and superior.

What quality of accommodation can the hotel Heritage Village Club Goa 4 * offer? The photo gallery on the official websites, as well as the guest reviews, indicate that all the rooms here are incredibly spacious and bright, with large windows and spacious balconies, which offer incredible views of the ocean and the exotic garden. People here really enjoy spending time.

Hotel Heritage Village Club Goa 4 *: photo and additional information about the rooms





Of course, the conditions and quality of accommodation are extremely important points for every traveler. Indeed, in any case, after a long day, I want to return to a comfortable room and relax, enjoying comfort and silence. It is worth saying that almost every room adjoins a private balcony, which overlooks the surroundings and the coast. You can count on high-quality sound insulation and modern comfort. By the way, each room has a unique design, be it a traditional Indian decor or a minimalist style. The furniture here may not be new, but made of quality material.

Guests can count on a set of household appliances, because without it the comfort and convenience of a modern traveler is almost impossible to imagine. You can create pleasant temperature conditions using the air conditioning system, which is equipped with a convenient control panel. Some rooms also have fans, which for some guests is more preferable.

You can skip the evening watching local and satellite channels. There is also a telephone, but, of course, the price does not include calls between cities or countries. A minibar with a cooling system is filled every day with drinking water in plastic bottles, drinks can be ordered, but they need to be paid separately. The room has an electric kettle, as well as tea, coffee, sugar and a set of dishes.

The room has a bathroom with shower and modern fixtures. Guests are provided with a set of toiletries, as well as large towels that change every day.

What kind of power scheme can I count on?





Delicious food is one of the attributes of a really good vacation. What does the hotel Heritage Village Club Goa 4 * offer a tourist? Reviews confirm that guests are welcome with a hearty variety of meals.

As already mentioned, this is one of the few hotels that offers an all-inclusive service. The main meals (three times a day) are held in the main restaurant, which pleases you with a relaxed atmosphere and pleasant design. The menu is quite varied and consists of international and Indian dishes. On the generous table there are always salads, vegetables, various side dishes, light soups, slices and snacks, as well as dishes from different varieties of fish, seafood and meat (chicken, pork, beef). Guests can also count on drinks, in particular tea, coffee and various juices.

The pool serves snacks twice a day so that guests can refresh themselves. By the way, you can also have dinner with a cafe with an outdoor terrace, which offers a wonderful view of the garden and surroundings. And guests can count on quality drinks and interesting cocktails. Food at the Heritage Village Club Goa 4 * hotel does not cause any complaints or complaints from any of the guests - you can always eat deliciously. And, of course, there are many restaurants, bars and even open markets where you can eat on the beaches and in the surrounding area.

Beach and relaxation by the water: what awaits tourists?





The warm waters of the ocean and beaches with crisp white sand - this is the reason why many tourists choose India as their stopping place. What can the hotel complex Heritage Village Club Goa 4 * offer the guest? Arossim, one of the most beautiful beaches in the vicinity, is located just 200 meters from the hotel grounds - a convenient walking path leads to it.

Reviews say that the coast here is really beautiful, and there are rarely many tourists - you can relax and enjoy the beauty of nature and the sound of sea waves. For a small fee, you can rent a deck chair with a mattress and a large umbrella. On the shore there are several shakes with bars where you can have a snack and cool off, order a delicious cocktail.

There is a sports recreation center on the beach where you can rent water transport. Tourists can enjoy water skiing or try parasailing and sailing. The ocean with soft waves is perfect for windsurfing, and travelers also enjoy diving.

Description of additional services





On the territory of the hotel Heritage Village Club Goa 4 * tried to create all conditions so that travelers can relax and have a good time. Here guests can use the laundry facilities. There is a currency exchange office with a good exchange rate, and you can also rent a safe at the reception completely free of charge - here you can safely leave really important and valuable things. In addition, you can use the Internet access - having a connection is important for almost every modern traveler.

In the event of a deterioration in well-being, it is worth contacting employees who will definitely call a specialist for you (doctor's services are paid separately). The hotel has a small shop where you can buy interesting souvenirs, spices and some household trifles. There is a well-equipped conference room - here from time to time various seminars, conferences, business meetings and even holidays are held.

Leisure of guests: how to spend free time at the hotel?





Goa Coast is an interesting place that is ideal for almost any type of vacation. But some entertainment can be found on the territory of the hotel itself. In the courtyard there is a small pool with a terrace for relaxation - this is a kind of center of public life of the hotel.

Guests can also use the gym. The hotel also has a beauty salon with good specialists, a wellness center and an Ayurveda center. If desired, tourists can take care of their health, improve their health and learn several healing oriental techniques. From time to time, group classes in aerobics and yoga are held here.

You can have fun playing a game of table tennis or billiards. There are tennis courts nearby. At the local bar you can always relax with a glass of strong drink and even sing karaoke. From time to time, interesting performances and vivid shows are held on site. And do not forget that in the vicinity there are many interesting places that are definitely worth a visit.

Is the hotel suitable for children?

Parents who plan to spend their holidays with their children are also interested in several other issues, including amenities and entertainment. What to expect from the hotel complex Heritage Village Club Goa 4 *? India, Arossim - Beach is a great place not only for relaxation, but also for healing, because the mild climate and healing air positively affect the growing body. Naturally, the kids have a great time on a clean beach, where they splash around in a specially equipped area for them.

In addition, in the courtyard in the large pool there is a section for children. Here the depth is the safest, and the water always warms up well. Kids can also play on the playgrounds. By the way, they clean the pool regularly. As such, there is no entertainment at the Heritage Village Club Goa 4 * hotel (for example, animation, a kids club, etc.). Nevertheless, some can be found on the territory of the city itself - there are shopping centers, children's complexes, water slides and water parks.

A professional nanny works at the hotel territory, who will definitely look after your child in case you need to go somewhere. Naturally, her services are paid separately, but the prices are very affordable. By the way, children under the age of five can stay at the hotel for free, provided that they do not occupy an extra bed. A comfortable cot is brought into the room for a small fee.

What do tourists say? Reviews and useful recommendations





Naturally, when planning a trip, you need to take into account not only official information, but also the opinion of tourists who have already managed to spend some time here. Indeed, from former guests sometimes you can get a lot more valuable information. So what they say about the hotel complex Heritage Village Club Goa 4 *?

The reviews that guests leave are by and large positive. The territory here is really beautifully arranged, it is pleasant to walk along it, and they clean it regularly. The presence of a bar in the center of the pool is also considered by many tourists as a good idea. By the way, the food at the hotel is really at a high level - there are enough meat and seafood dishes, and traditional Indian food with an abundance of spices has a special label (if you do not like these dishes). The availability of good food is considered an important advantage, since not all hotels in Goa offer such a service.

The rooms are cozy and beautiful, cleaned here every day, so the rooms are always really clean. Some travelers say that household appliances are not new. Nevertheless, it works properly. The presence in the room of tea, coffee, sugar and utensils greatly simplifies life.

The main advantage of the hotel is its proximity to the incredibly beautiful beach covered with crisp white sand - here you can enjoy the scenery and silence for a long time, since there are few tourists on the site. The employees are very hospitable and delicate - it is always nice to communicate with them (by the way, some people understand Russian quite well).

In general, tourists recommend the hotel Heritage Village Club Goa 4 *. India is a magnificent country, but in terms of quality of service, it lags behind such "monsters" of the tourism business, such as Turkey or Egypt. This in no way prevents travelers from spending holidays here, but you need to understand what to count on. By the way, the prices here will also please you, especially considering the quality of accommodation and service. Most of the guests here are regular customers, which also testifies in favor of the hotel.
