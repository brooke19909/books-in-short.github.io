Shymkent Hotels: rating, reviews

Shymkent is one of the three largest cities in Kazakhstan, and at the same time it is the regional center of the Republic. But this is not what attracts many tourists to the city, but rather special sights and unique nature. These are thermal and mineral springs, clean air, beautiful landscapes. But the visit to Shymkent begins with the choice of place of residence. This is usually taken very responsibly, since even the smallest everyday troubles can pretty much ruin your vacation and leave not the best memories.

Shymkent hotels are quite diverse in all respects, from the appearance to the decor in the rooms, which allows each visitor to choose the option that will suit him completely. The information provided about the hotel facilities of the middle class will help to better understand what can be expected from this or that. Room reservation

Today, thanks to the World Wide Web, Shymkent hotels offer to book a room online. It is convenient, saves time and takes only a few minutes. Of course, each consumer is primarily interested in how safe it is to transfer their personal data via the Internet.

Each hotel establishment takes this issue very seriously, and in order to ensure the safety of each client's information, it is transmitted in a secure mode and in encrypted form. In addition, no funds from customers' credit cards, other than reservation fees, are withdrawn. Payment for accommodation is made by the client only at the hotel.

Cancellation

There are frequent cases when a person is forced to cancel a trip for various reasons. Can he return his money by canceling the reservation? There are several rules common to most hotel establishments:

  1. If the cancellation occurred two (or more) days before the day when the person was supposed to check into the hotel, fines will not be charged.
  2. If the reservation is canceled later or on the day of arrival, a penalty will be charged. It is equal to the total cost of the reservation.

Since Shymkent hotels are quite diverse and each has its own rules, you must pay attention to this item when booking a room, having read the cancellation policy.

Financial questions

Shymkent hotels offer several payment options so that each guest of the city can take advantage of the most suitable offer for him. You can pay for accommodation:

  1. In cash.
  2. Transfer of funds.
  3. Card. Accepted credit cards Master card, Visa Electron, Visa.

There is a single estimated time for payment - 24 hours. The reference is the time of arrival. But in some institutions, the estimated time may be different - 12 hours, so this must be clarified even when booking a room.
