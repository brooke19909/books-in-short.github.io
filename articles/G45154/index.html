LCD "Standard City": customer reviews

Currently, a huge number of residential complexes are being built in Moscow. Each of them has its own characteristics, advantages and disadvantages. Of course, buyers try to give preference to the projects of reliable developers who managed to gain an impeccable reputation. But projects of young construction companies that are quite interesting, promising and thought out do not go unnoticed.

Modern buyers have become more demanding in the choice of housing. It is understandable: in most cases, an apartment is purchased on a mortgage basis. RC "Etalon City" - a unique project of comfortable housing, implemented in North Butovo. The project caused a flurry of discussions, threatening to become one of the largest in the capital, and therefore definitely deserves attention.





about the project

About the developer

The Etalon company is a proven developer, on the account of which there are enough completed projects. Responses of residents of commissioned houses emphasize that all the work was done efficiently, there is not a single project that the company would not deliver on time. Such a reputation, of course, captivates, I want to trust such a company.

Location

I want to start my review from the chosen place. So, on the territory of Butovo LCD "Etalon City" is being built. South Butovo is currently being actively built up, so if you are not ready to put up with the lack of some infrastructure facilities, the complex should meet your expectations.

The location of the complex is its main attraction. This is noted in almost all reviews of residents. For the construction of houses, a picturesque place with excellent ecology was chosen. In the immediate vicinity of the construction site there are no harmful industries, so residents will be able to enjoy clean fresh air. Many say that buying an apartment in the Etalon-City residential complex (Starokrymskaya St., property 13) is a worthy investment in the lives of their children. Indeed, at present, finding an apartment in an ecologically clean area without losing access to all the necessary infrastructure is a huge problem.





Transport accessibility

According to the project, the complex is adjacent to the Moscow Ring Road understudy, which is why problems for families with a car certainly will not arise. Own departure to the understudy helps to avoid traffic jams and congestion and easily reach any point in the capital.

In fact, among potential buyers there are many who do not believe in such a cloudless future. But experience shows that even at rush hour there are no traffic jams at the place of departure onto the MKAD. Of course, proximity to the Moscow Ring Road in this case is both an advantage and a disadvantage of the project: unfortunately, the Bitsevsky Park does not help protect residents from endless noise. Those who managed to visit the construction site note that the builder has installed reliable plastic windows with decent sound insulation as compensation.





Infrastructure

Buyers note that there are no problems with the infrastructure. It has everything you need: kindergartens, schools, clinics, fitness centers, shopping and entertainment centers, parks, squares. True, not within walking distance. The nearest school is 900 meters away. Not so far, but it is not so convenient for a small child to get to an educational institution on his own. That is why at this stage, until your own social infrastructure has appeared, you simply cannot do without a personal car.

Apartments, layouts

The developer offered many options for real estate: compact studios, one-room apartments, comfortable "two-bedroom apartments" and spacious three-room apartments for a large and friendly family. Trying to provide each resident with a comfortable apartment, the developer worked on the plans, separating the guest and sleeping areas so that each member of the family was as comfortable as possible.

Layouts correspond to the main modern trends and requirements: spacious kitchen-living rooms, the presence of an additional bathroom and wardrobe, isolated rooms.





On the official website of the LCD "Etalon City" you can find information that the apartments are rented with a turnkey finish. In the usual sense, finishing cannot be called final, but all because it is represented only by a floor screed, electrical wiring, glazing, and installation of a heating system. If you are comfortable with this option, feel free to buy an apartment here. If you plan to buy an apartment, where it is enough to bring furniture - take a look at other projects.

Price policy

Apartments in the Etalon-City residential complex belong to comfort class housing, but their cost is clearly lower than the price of apartments inside the MKAD. You can buy an apartment here from 4.6 million to 18 million rubles. To summarize

LCD "Etalon City" is, of course, an interesting and noteworthy project presented by a worthy developer. It combines transport accessibility, excellent ecology, a variety of planning solutions and access to all necessary infrastructure. We advise you to take a closer look at the complex, especially since you can buy an apartment here with significant savings for your budget.
