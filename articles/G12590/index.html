Mini-hotels of Nizhny Novgorod: description and reviews

Nizhny Novgorod is one of the oldest Russian cities with an 800-year-old rich history. Many tourists, both Russian and foreign, come here to look at the monuments of ancient architecture. If at the end of an eventful day you want to feel in the atmosphere of home comfort, choose one of the mini-hotels of Nizhny Novgorod for your stay.

Mini-Hotel "On Ilyinka"

The hotel is located on the first two floors in a new elite three-story building, next to the Volga. The city center is a 10-minute walk away. To get from here to the House of Peter the Great, St. Michael the Archangel Cathedral, the estate of the merchant Markov and the Nizhny Novgorod Kremlin, you need to walk less than a kilometer. There are several cafes and restaurants nearby, which feature Russian and European cuisine, as well as the Ilyinsky supermarket. It offers a sauna, luggage storage, and free private parking. Young guests can spend time in the equipped games room. Guests can enjoy a meal in the shared kitchen, equipped with a fridge and electric kettle. Pets are allowed by prior arrangement. For accommodation, vacationers are offered the following room categories:

  • double economy - 4000 rubles per day for two people, and 3300 rubles for one person;
  • double standard - respectively 4,500 rubles and 3,600 rubles per day;
  • double comfort - respectively 5300 and 4200 rubles per day;
  • Suite - the cost of living is 6800 rubles per day;
  • Suite with sauna - 7500 rubles per day.

All rooms are equipped with air conditioning, TV, telephone, free Wi-Fi. The mini-hotel ‚ÄúOn Ilyinka‚Äù is located in Nizhny Novgorod at the address: 6B Krutoy Lane.

Mini-hotel "Elisha"

If you are interested in mini-hotels in Nizhny Novgorod in the center, pay attention to the Elisha Hotel, which is located in a new building built in 2016. Nearby are the Kladovka Art Gallery and the Estate of the Merchant Markov. To get to the Annunciation Monastery, the House of Peter the Great, St. Michael the Archangel Cathedral and the Nizhny Novgorod Kremlin, you need to walk more than a kilometer. A restaurant and a supermarket are within walking distance. The hotel offers luggage storage services. Free public parking needs to be reserved in advance. Shuttle service from or to the airport is possible. To accommodate guests are offered double rooms with one bed, equipped with such amenities:

  • flat screen tv;
  • an iron with ironing facilities;
  • clothes hanger;
  • a hairdryer;
  • free toiletries.

The cost of living at the hotel starts at 1499 rubles. Rooms are three rooms. It offers rooms with free internet. Located mini-hotel "Elisha" in Nizhny Novgorod at the address: Maslyakova street, 12A.

Mini-Hotel Rosa Khutor

The hotel is located in the Nizhny Novgorod conservation area, next to the Schelkovsky Khutor park and the museum of Russian architecture of the same name. Fans of skiing and cycling come here. A restaurant and a supermarket are nearby. The Rosa Khutor Hotel has its own cafe, and offers free parking, bike rental and ski equipment. There is a steam room and a pool. For an extra fee, you can take lessons at a ski school. Outdoor picnic lovers can use the barbecue facilities. To accommodate guests, the following rooms are offered:

  • budget double with one double bed - 1425 rubles per day, if one person is occupied;
  • double standard - 2280 rubles for two people and 1900 rubles if one person lives;
  • triple standard - from 2090 to 3230 rubles per day, depending on the number of residents;
  • four-seater standard - from 2280 to 3705 rubles per day, depending on the number of residents.

Room rates include breakfast. All rooms have air conditioning and TV, free WI-FI. The Rosa Khutor mini-hotel is located in Nizhny Novgorod at the address: 47 Manezhnaya Street.

Mini-hotel "Chance"

If you are interested in mini-hotels located near the railway station in Nizhny Novgorod, then the Chance Hotel is at your service. Moskovsky Train Station is about 15 minutes on foot from here. At a distance of 120 meters from the hotel there is a cafe "Mimino". Hotel guests have the opportunity to visit the Smolensk-Vladimirsky parish in Gordeevka, located within walking distance from the hotel. An airport shuttle service is available. It offers a sauna, laundry facilities and free, unguarded parking. Each room has air conditioning and a telephone. The cost of hotel accommodation is from 1300 rubles per day. The rooms have access to free wireless internet. The mini-hotel "Chance" is located in Nizhny Novgorod at the address: General Zimin Street, 26.
