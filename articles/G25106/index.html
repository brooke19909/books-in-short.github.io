Guest House "Natella", Kabardinka: address, description, reviews

The village of Kabardinka is located fifteen kilometers from Gelendzhik, on the shore of the Tsemess bay. This resort is one of the most comfortable in climatic conditions, because there is low humidity, the sea warms up well, and in summer sunny weather prevails. The swimming season lasts from mid-May to October. Also, the village of Kabardinka is famous for its many hotels. Therefore, there is where to stay. For example, in Natella.

A bit about the hotel





Guest House "Natella" (Kabardinka) is a whole complex, which consists of three buildings. The latter was built in 2012. Nevertheless, the Natella Hotel (Kabardinka) is not just a complex of buildings. It is also an adjacent landscaped area where you can always rest comfortably.

The hotel has 26 rooms, which means that you do not have to watch the crowd every day and you can relax.

Hotel Rooms





In the guest house "Natella" (Kabardinka), double, triple and quadruple rooms are available. All rooms are made in light beige colors. Furniture harmoniously fits into the interior and does not interfere with the free movement of guests.

For a comfortable stay there are wooden single or double beds with comfortable mattresses, a TV, a small refrigerator, air conditioning, a wardrobe. Also there are bedside tables, a table. The deluxe rooms have a folding sofa. Individual bathroom with shower. There are rooms with a balcony.

The cost of living depends not only on the category of rooms, but also on the month of stay and varies from 1000 to 3800 rubles per day. Prices for holidays in the suite start from 1500 rubles per day.

Hotel Services





The price of accommodation at the Natella Hotel includes the following services:

  • parking in the courtyard of the guest yard ;
  • satellite and cable television;
  • first aid kit and doctor's call;
  • provision of a sewing kit (needle, thread, scissors);
  • transfer from Gelendzhik airport ;
  • a visit to two heated pools and a children's section;
  • use of ironing room;
  • housekeeping and linen change;
  • use of a safe by the administrator;
  • use of wireless internet (Wi-Fi).

For an additional fee, the staff of the Natella hotel offers its guests:

  • organization of transfer to or from the hotel;
  • organization of excursion programs;
  • laundry service.

Catering is possible in two ways:

  • in a separate kitchen, which is located in the new building, where you can cook your own food;
  • in the dining room on the territory of the hotel, where you can order three meals a day.

By the way, products can be bought directly at the hotel, because there is a grocery store in the guest house.

Leisure activities





Naturally, the main attraction of the village is the sea coast. From the hotel yard "Natella" to the beach 400 meters, and to the edge of the sea 831 meters. Here you will find plenty of entertainment for all ages: catamarans, boat trips, water slides, scooters and other types of attractions.

Nearby there are a large number of cafes and restaurants where you can try local and foreign cuisine. The Natella Guest House (Kabardinka) is also within walking distance of the city center, the Old Park and the market, where you can buy fresh and natural products and wines. Also nearby is a dolphinarium, Dino Park, a crocodile farm, a children's water park.

On the territory of the hotel yard "Natella" you can also have a good rest. After all, there is a landscaped courtyard with barbecue facilities, swimming pools, a gazebo, summer tables and chairs.

Negative moments





Those who already rested in the Natella Guest House note the following points that they did not like:

  • Room cleaning is only available upon request.
  • Bed linen is changed only once every five days.
  • Bathrooms are small, shower is small.
  • Wi-Fi is not available throughout the hotel, and sometimes disappears in the rooms themselves.
  • One of the pools is located right next to the trellised fence, through which the road is visible.

Many guests note that, of course, they would like the hotel staff to correct these nuances. But it is worth noting that the moments mentioned did not affect the quality of rest, and people left with good impressions.

Hotel location





Hotel "Natella" is located at: Kabardinka, st. Privetlivaya, 25. The guest house is conveniently located near the sea and not far from the Kabardinki bus station. And it is very convenient for those who travel on their own. Getting to the car is also quite simple. First you need to go along the M-4 highway to Kabardinka, then turn onto the main street - Revolutionary, and then drive to the specified address.
