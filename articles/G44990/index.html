Smartphone review "Lenovo" A5000. Characteristics and reviews

Very rarely there are budget devices that can be relevant for a long time. Lenovo is one of the few companies that pay due attention to inexpensive models. You can appreciate the efforts of the company on the example of the A5000 phone.

Design





Expect a lot from the appearance of state employees is not worth it. The Lenovo A5000, which has an absolutely unprepossessing design of a case made of plastic, was no exception.

Despite the rather tangible weight, as much as 160 grams, the device lies pretty well in the hand. Comfortable operation provides roughness of the rear panel. It should be noted the presence of an oleophobic coating, which reduces the number of prints.

The location of external parts is familiar to company devices. On the front there is a screen, sensors, a speaker, touch buttons, a camera and a company logo.

On the side of the right, there is a volume control and a power button. The top end got a slot for USB and headphones, and the bottom - a microphone.

On the back is the company logo, main camera, flash and speaker.

The plain appearance of the device is supported by affordable colors. The device is available exclusively in black and white, which does not add zest.

Display





The installed diagonal of 5 inches is great for Lenovo A5000. In combination with a resolution of 1280 by 720, the image is clear and quite bright. The company always tries to equip even inexpensive devices with high-quality displays.

The use of IPS matrix significantly increased the review of angles in the Lenovo A5000 . Unfortunately, all the advantages literally fade in the sun. In bright light, working with the screen will be difficult.

Filling

The hardware of Lenovo A5000 boasts four cores. The smartphone works on the MTK processor, already familiar to the Chinese. The frequency will also please, each core operates at 1.3 GHz. Looks good for a budget employee and installed gigabytes of RAM.

In general, the filling is a combination of parameters that has already become fond of Lenovo. Such a solution allows the company to take a leading position in the market for low-cost devices.

Battery

Compared to previous models of the Lenovo series, the A5000 received an excellent battery. The installed 4000 mAh battery will fully satisfy the needs of the smartphone.

The duration of work has increased significantly, now with active use the device will last 6-7 hours. With moderate stress, the "life expectancy" increases to 2 days.

Such a battery can rarely be found in a state employee. Previously, only the S series could boast such an advantage.

Price

The cost of the A5000 ranges from 10 to 11 thousand rubles. Remembering devices with similar characteristics, we can conclude that the price is even nothing. Typically, smartphones with similar specifications cost significantly higher.

Advantages

Immediately I want to note the excellent autonomy of the device. A powerful battery provides continuous operation, and this is a very rare quality.

It should be appreciated and the display of the smartphone. A large diagonal and a resolution acceptable for a budget employee create an almost perfect harmony. You can spot pixels only with targeted searches.

You can not ignore the filling of the phone. High performance makes the A5000 an excellent choice not only for work, but also for entertainment. Naturally, demanding 3D games will slow down, but otherwise the device will cope with the tasks perfectly.

disadvantages
