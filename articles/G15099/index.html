Holiday Village "Cheryomushki": we plan a vacation in advance

Rest in the country like almost all citizens. After all, this is a great opportunity to take a break from the noise and breathe in fresh air. Therefore, many begin to plan their vacation or trip in advance to get to a good place. The recreation center "Cheryomushki" is not only in the Chelyabinsk region, but also in Arkhipo-Osipovka. Each of them offers decent living conditions and a good rest.

Rest in the Krasnodar Territory

The recreation center "Cheryomushki" in Arkhipo-Osipovka offers its guests excellent vacation options. After all, they will have a rest right next to the Black Sea. The base itself is located near Gelendzhik, in the village of Arkhipo-Osipovka. It takes about five minutes to go to the sea, so the place is in great demand. A big plus for many guests can be considered the moment that they can relax with their pets. This service does not offer all the bases. During your stay, wireless Internet is available, which allows you to always stay in touch. Meals can be held in the dining room or bar, and there is also a barbecue grill.

There are 30 houses with all necessary conditions for living. Also at the recreation center "Cheryomushki" there is a building designated for food. The dining room has a canopy, so you can dine there even in the rain. But bad weather at the resort is a rarity. More often the sun shines there, so you can spend your whole vacation on a pebble beach. The mild climate is well tolerated by people and allows you to quickly adapt. On the territory of the base there is entertainment for children. They can play on site, as well as access to the pool.

Visitors reviews

Many guests of the recreation center "Cheryomushki" are satisfied with the time spent there. They note the convenient location of the place from the sea, since getting to it quickly and easily. In addition, people write about the comfort of the base and comfortable conditions, thanks to which it is easy to relax and rest. A lot of entertainment is intended for children, so parents are in a hurry to share a positive opinion about this moment. According to some users, the number on the base can even be compared with a one-room apartment.

Rest on the lake Sugoyak

The recreation center "Cheryomushki" is located in the village of Lazurny. This is one of the famous places in the Chelyabinsk region where you can relax. The great advantage of the base can be considered that it receives guests all year round. Many parents send their children here for healing. Here, if necessary, the younger generation can undergo treatment. And parents can gain new knowledge so that it is easier for them to take care of the child in case of illness.

Food is given special attention. It occurs five times a day. In the presence of allergies, the menu will be selected individually. Children are treated for many modern diseases, which helps to improve the general condition of the child.

For the child, a schedule can be made, including medical procedures and games for hardening. Vacationers at the recreation center "Cheryomushki" Lake Sugoyak is very much appreciated , because it helps to significantly improve their health. Many doctors know its healing properties, therefore, they recommend rest for a child on this base.

In addition to the benefits, there is the opportunity to enjoy the beautiful nature and views that are not in the cities. Modern children often lack fresh air due to computers and gadgets, so this situation can be easily fixed on the basis. You can relax with your whole family on Lake Sugoyak on the weekend. It provides a lot of interesting options. A variety of programs are held for children and adults, as well as contests and master classes.

Guest reviews

The recreation center "Cheryomushki" (Sugoyak) regularly receives positive reviews from both children and the adult generation. People say that their vacation was very positive, and also became good for health. Many children express their gratitude to counselors directly on the Internet, recalling moments and the warm atmosphere of the camp.

A considerable number of guests lived there for a long time, therefore they speak of the base as their second home or a small and warm "city". Children are happy to share their impressions, as well as post photos from the camp. On the Internet, you can often see frames in which the name of the base is laid out in fruit or in another original way.
