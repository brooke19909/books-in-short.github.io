Hotel Mediterranee Thalasso Golf 3 * (Tunisia, Hammamet): description and reviews

This hotel is considered one of the most expensive three-star establishments in Tunisia. Mediterranee Thalasso Golf 3 * has gained popularity among tourists due to the presence of high-quality service, a mass of free services and an intensive animation program. The hotel is aimed at lovers of active leisure, is also a suitable option for families with children.

Mediterranee Thalasso Golf 3 *: description

The institution has a main building, two three-story buildings, stretching towards the sea, as well as a well-groomed garden, whose area is 6 hectares. Nearby are shops and restaurants, the famous BIO AZUR - the center of thalassotherapy.

History

Hotel Mediterranee Thalasso Golf 3 * (Tunisia, Hammamet) was built in 1970, and recently it was renovated. The institution belongs to a small Tunisian chain that includes two more hotels in Hammamet: Aziza and Aziza Residense. In 1999, the owner of the hotel, 30 km from the city of Hammamet, opened the Friguia Park Zoo, which is dedicated to the conservation of endangered species of animals in Africa. If desired, guests of the Mediterranee Thalasso Golf 3 * can visit the zoo: excursions cost from 5 to 8 DT (at the rate of 1 dinar = 24 rubles).

Infrastructure

The hotel has a large outdoor pool, a children's pool, as well as a heated indoor pool. Meals are organized in the main restaurant with 3 rooms and a large terrace for up to 700 guests. In addition, on the territory there are two more restaurants and four bars, a shopping center. For entertainment, the Mediterranee Thalasso Golf 3 * hotel provides guests with a TV lounge, car rental, a large video game gallery, a disco bar and a wellness center. In the latter you can visit the sauna (DT 7), Turkish bath (DT 7), massage therapist, conference room (accommodates up to 300 people), amphitheater. In addition, the hotel offers intensive animation programs for children and adults, as well as an entertaining evening program.





If necessary, you can use the safe at the reception. For tourists with children, a children's club is equipped, where kids receive snacks and afternoon snacks for free throughout the day, there is also a playground, a mini zoo. Babysitting services are available upon request.

For Sport

Guests of Hotel Mediterranee Thalasso Golf 3 * are invited to engage in outdoor activities in the well-equipped gym (free of charge), which has 14 fitness equipment and barbells. There are 4 tennis courts. The animation program contains lessons for free tennis training at specific hours. At the hotel you can also use the volleyball, basketball, football court. You can do aerobics, horse riding. Lovers of active leisure are given the opportunity to use 2 billiard tables, free mini-golf, a hall for playing checkers, chess, backgammon. If you wish, you can shoot from a bow, play ping pong. The institution has its own center of water sports and entertainment, offering guests free pedalos, surfing, and a catamaran.

What do guests advise?

Review authors generously share their recommendations with those who are considering choosing a room. Many advise to opt for rooms located on the second or third floors, from whose windows a magnificent view of the beach and the sea opens. Tourists also recommend settling away from the administration building and closer to the sea - these rooms are considered the most quiet. In the premises located close to the administration building, loud music is heard in the evenings coming from the open veranda.
