Acquaintance

GPS Garmin GPSMAP 64ST inherited the same case from the previous generation of gadgets, with no visible differences. Plastic edging, made in gray style, fully complies with the traditions of the Garmin brand: serious models are packaged in black and gray, medium ones are brownish, and the younger ones have a predominant yellow color.

Assembly

After several days of use, it is clear that the gadget is assembled with love: it does not creak, all the details are fitted very carefully, there are no any backlashes. The device gives the owner a certain note of solidity due to its weightiness, solidity, but at the same time, the ease of use of such a seemingly sophisticated navigator as the Garmin GPSMAP 64ST.





Reviews of travel enthusiasts about the gadget are mostly positive, the only thing that is sometimes noted as a minus is the rarely creaking of the case, but this is really a rare case. Otherwise, as evidenced by numerous reviews, the device has earned respect and enjoys enviable popularity.

Display

Garmin GPSMAP 64ST is equipped with a color transreflective display with a TFT-matrix (similar to the 62nd line). Some competing devices boast a high-quality screen resolution (Garmin has 160x240), but it can be said in favor of the respondent that with such a sweep, the battery life is much higher than that of similar devices of other brands. Moreover, the ability to hold out as long as possible in harsh traveling conditions is extremely important for a real navigator.





Due to the small resolution, the graininess can be distinguished with the naked eye, but it does not interfere with the full-fledged operation of the device, and balanced contrast and behavior in the sun will certainly please the owners of the gadget.

Owner reviews note one interesting detail that was not indicated in the instruction manual - this is the automatic backlighting of the buttons in the dark (according to the internal clock of the device). In addition, they removed the camera, which very often became clogged and caused a certain discomfort.

Communication

You can pair the Garmin GPSMAP 64ST with other mobile devices, but with certain limitations. Applications easily synergize with the Android platform (up to version 4.3) and with some of the "apple" gadgets. User reviews note excellent synchronization with iPads of the third version.

For travelers, the specially developed Basecamp Mobile program for Garmin GPSMAP 64ST (software overview can be found on the developer's website), which works fine with points and tracks and, moreover, synchronizes with most devices via the Bluetooth protocol, will certainly be interesting.

Offline work

According to Garmin, the device is able to work at full strength for no more than 16 hours on a single charge. This is slightly less than the indicators of the last, 62nd line, but it is quite acceptable when you consider that the device is seriously loaded with the GLONASS system.





For work, both ordinary AA batteries and batteries are suitable. Numerous user reviews are advised to take high-quality food, such as Sony or expensive Digital, otherwise the charge will melt before our eyes.

Field testing of the device showed that after 12 hours of intensive use, the device strongly recommends that the screen backlight be turned off. After 13.5 hours, friezes began to scroll and map, and after 15.5 hours the device turned off.

Summarizing

The 64 series turned out to be quite reliable, versatile and quite portable, and with a number of interesting points and ideas. The device is perfect for demanding travelers who appreciated all the advantages of past Garmin models.

Picky critics would surely say that the device has a lot to develop, pointing to a small screen resolution and button control, but the rest of the functionality and capabilities of the device are very impressive. The model is convenient in all basic operations, assembled firmly and honestly, there is protection against external influences like dust, dirt, accidental impacts. Even the freezing temperatures and immersion in the water can not be. The design was checked not just at the stands, but by experienced tourists who just for the sake of beauty will not buy the device. The verdict is clear - recommended for purchase.
