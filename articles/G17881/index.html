Briefly about the hotel

Hotel Movenpick Resort Sharm El Sheikh 5 * is a unique place where every corner is saturated with the heady atmosphere of the east. Traditional Arabian architecture looks even more magnificent against the background of the extraordinary beauties of Sinai nature. The hotel buildings are designed so that each guest can enjoy the views of the Red Sea from the windows of his room.

It is important to note the fact that in 2012 the hotel underwent a major overhaul and complete restoration of the buildings, which is why you can not be afraid to find old furniture or faulty plumbing in the rooms.

In accordance with Arab traditions, the hotel pays great attention to equestrian sport. Spacious manicured stables are located here. There is the possibility of organizing horse walks both within the hotel and beyond.

Where is the hotel located

Advantageous location is one of the main advantages of the Movenpick Resort Sharm El Sheikh 5 * hotel. It is located in the famous resort town of Sharm El Sheikh in one of its best areas called Naama Bay. The path from the hotel to the airport or downtown takes you no more than 10 minutes.





Description of hotel rooms

Each of the rooms of the Movenpick Resort Sharm El Sheikh 5 * hotel can be compared with a work of Arabic art. Hotel guests can feel like inhabitants of real oriental palaces. It is very important to note the fact that the windows of each room overlook the sea coast. Guests can stay in the rooms of the main building, as well as in luxury detached villas.

Apartments of the following categories are ready for guests at the Movenpick Resort Sharm El Sheikh 5 * hotel at any time of the year:

  • Classic (with partial sea view) - these are small rooms (24 sq. M.) With a large bed or two single beds. It is also possible to place an extra adult or children's bed in the room. Arriving at the hotel as a large family or company, guests can stay in rooms that are interconnected by an internal door.
  • Classic (with sea and pool views) - the room is similar to the previous category. The difference is only in the location relative to the sea coast.
  • Deluxe (ground floor, overlooking the sea and the garden) - since the rooms are located on the lower level, they have their own terrace overlooking the garden. On an area of ‚Äã‚Äã24 square meters. m can accommodate two adults, as well as a child in an extra bed.
  • Deluxe (with a panoramic view of the sea coast) - this category of rooms is generally similar to the previous one. The main difference is that due to the location of the apartments on the upper floors, guests enjoy a panoramic view of the Red Sea.
  • Junior Suite (with sea view) - large two-room rooms with an area of ‚Äã‚Äã48 square meters. m. In the bedroom there is a double bed, and in the living room there is a large folding sofa. The apartments have two bathrooms, as well as two balconies with gorgeous sea views.
  • Two-storey villa (overlooking the garden and the sea) - a spacious bright house, which occupies 270 square meters. m. Three spacious bedrooms can comfortably accommodate up to 9 guests (6 adults and 3 children). Each bedroom has a private bathroom with a shower and a Jacuzzi bathtub. A nice addition will be access to your own pool, which is heated in bad weather.
  • Two-storey villa (overlooking the sea) - from the previous accommodation option, this villa has a larger area (370 sq. M.) And the possibility of direct access to the sea.




In every room

The management of the Movenpick Sharm El Sheikh Resort (Naama Bay) hotel seeks to create the most comfortable and cozy living conditions for its guests. In this regard, in each room the following amenities and technical capabilities are available to tourists:

  • TV with satellite channels (there are also several Russian ones);
  • electric kettle, coffee maker, as well as a service for making drinks;

What to do while relaxing

In the Movenpick Resort Sharm El Sheikh 5 * hotel you will not be bored for a single day, because this resort complex offers many opportunities for entertainment:

  • in the immediate vicinity of the hotel (as well as on its territory) there are many shops and markets, thanks to this an exciting shopping experience is guaranteed;
  • the hotel has a tour desk that organizes fascinating trips to the main attractions of Egypt;
