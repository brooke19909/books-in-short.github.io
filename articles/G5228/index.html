How to knit a hood with knitting needles? The easiest way

The hood is a very comfortable piece of clothing. Moreover, it is also stylish. With the help of a hood, any sweater or sweater can be given a unique and finished look. It looks especially good in children's and teenage models. Let's look at two ways to knit a hood. In the first case, the product will be traditional, and in the second - very interesting and attracting the attention of others. How will it differ? Everything is very simple: the second model will be an independent thing, combining a scarf and a hat. But let's get it in order.





Traditional performance of the hood

Knitting the hood with knitting needles in the usual version does not present any difficulties for the needlewoman. Even not very experienced craftswomen can easily cope with the task.

In fact, knitting a hood is a fabric in the shape of a rectangle. The size of this part will depend on the size of the head. And yet, you can connect the hood and jacket (or other product) in two ways. One of which involves the use of a needle with a thread, the other is knitting from raised loops along the neckline. Let's look at both methods.

Operating procedure

In order to knit a sweater with a hood with knitting needles, you need a little more yarn than usual. For an adult, knitting a hood usually takes one skein weighing 100 g. For a baby, of course, less will be needed.

If you decide to knit the hood separately, and then sew it to the jacket, then we begin, as usual, with a set of loops on the knitting needles. Their number should correspond to the length of the entire neckline, as well as the back and front parts (if it is a jacket on the front closure). If the jumper does not have a cut in the center, then the length will be a little less, about 2-3 cm (this is quite enough). Next, talk about how to knit the hood with knitting needles. The execution scheme is very simple, and as for the pattern, you can use any one you like.

So, we continue knitting either with an elastic band (a couple of centimeters), or immediately with a chosen pattern. You need to connect a rectangle, the height of which will correspond to the length from the neckline to the crown plus 5 centimeters. When you have reached the desired length, close all the loops. Fold the future hood in half and sew one of the sides. Sew the other side to the neckline. That's basically all, the hood is ready.

If you do not want to sew the hood to the finished product, you can raise the loops along the neckline and also tie a rectangle, the top of which is sewn, folded in half. Now you know how to knit a hood with knitting needles, and you can independently make convenient and comfortable clothes for your children.





Interesting ideas for knitting hoods

And now the most interesting: knitting a hood as an independent thing in the wardrobe. How do you like this decision? The principle of knitting is practically the same as described above. You will surely cope with the task, because now it is no secret to you how to knit the hood with knitting needles. But you should pay attention to such nuances:

  • when knitting, very thick yarn is used ;
  • 3-4 cm in length and width are added to the size you need (by standards);
  • Large buttons are used for decoration.

Drape with a hood in the style of Little Red Riding Hood

Here is another original version of clothing with a hood. Tie such a drape for your daughter, decorate her with a hood, and your Little Red Riding Hood will be the most beautiful and bright girl. How to knit a hood with needles? The main thing is to know the basic nuances, and everything else can be done in accordance with your taste and desire.



To complete this model you will need 5-6 skeins of yarn, depending on size. The nuances of knitting a drape with a hood

The pattern for knitting a hood is the simplest - shawl knitting. A drape is performed by alternating stripes of front and back knitting of different widths. At the base of the hood you need to make ties. It can be a satin ribbon or crocheted strings.
