Hotel "Abaata" in Abkhazia: reviews

Spend a long-awaited vacation, annual vacation or just a wonderful weekend in one of the resorts of Abkhazia is available to everyone. Often the choice is limited to a small town, which is located near the Russian border and the international airport. It bears the name Gagra. The nature of these surroundings impresses every traveler: on the one hand, it is washed by the Black Sea, and on the other, it offers a magnificent view of the mountain scenery. Clear waters, sand and pebble beaches, the spaciousness of the coastal zone and its sufficient length contribute to the annual influx of tourists. A large number of natural attractions and types of entertainment satisfy the requirements of outdoor enthusiasts of all ages.





Description of the hotel complex

This fast-paced resort combines a number of comfortable hotels, guest houses and private housing. Hotel "Abaata", the photo of which is presented to your attention in the article, combines impeccable service and personal attitude to each client, which is remembered by vacationers for a long time. Located within the first coastline, fifty meters from the sea.

The complex is represented by three buildings. The first building is brick, consists of 26 rooms. Built in 2009. The second building was equipped in 2015 in a building whose foundation was laid in 1903. Available 57 rooms are located on three floors. The third is the freshest, opened after the reconstruction of the building of the early 20th century, in 2016. Two floors combined 47 rooms. There is no lift everywhere.

Hotel Rooms

Old and new buildings are somewhat different in the categories of apartments. The first, built earlier than the rest, consists of the chambers of the class:

  1. Standard without a balcony.
  2. Standard with a balcony.
  3. Two-room suite.

The interior of the rooms is at the same time restrained and modern, meets the European standards of hotel service. Furniture sets include a bed, bedside tables, a wardrobe, a table and chairs. In the closet there are hangers, bathrobes, slippers and a safe. The flooring is parquet. The apartments are equipped with a TV, fridge, hairdryer, air conditioning, bath or shower. The presence of an electric kettle in the room facilitates the meeting of sunrises with a cup of coffee or a cup of tea in rooms with a balcony without leaving the room. The suite additionally has a folding sofa.

New buildings, in addition to those listed, have a suite of junior suite. They are also one-room, however they have an additional folding chair.

Room cleanliness is supported by daily maid service by experienced, conscientious and friendly maids.





Food for vacationers

As for the food of travelers who are located in the hotel complex "Abaata", there are no breakfasts or dinners included in the price. On the territory there is a Chaikhone restaurant with incredibly delicious culinary masterpieces and amazingly talented chefs. Also outside the hotel there are many cafes and eateries within walking distance, where tourists can taste the dishes of colorful local cuisine.

Activities for active vacation

Tennis courts are located in the garden area of ​​the seaside hotel complex. In the evening, the hotel administration occasionally invites animators to entertain holidaymakers. More informative for travelers will be hiking on the natural attractions of the area, enjoying unprecedented landscape paintings and breathing in fresh clean air, like a new life.

The reception has a tour desk. Prices for purchased trips to the sights of the surroundings are affordable, sometimes lower than those offered in the city center. One of the possible types of entertainment is to dive into the depths of the sea using the diving service. This opportunity always attracts vacationers by the fact that you can find yourself in an unusual habitat and observe the life of the inhabitants of the underwater world.





Additional service and living conditions

Throughout the hotel complex there is uninterrupted Wi-Fi. Due to the lack of developed entertainment infrastructure within the hotel, this service pleases guests. For guests arriving by car, free private, spacious, on-site parking is provided. Advance booking a car is not required. Iron and ironing board are located on the third floor of the second building, are in the corridor in the public domain for all.

The Abaata Hotel receives positive reviews for the fact that it employs friendly, helpful and highly qualified staff who speak Russian and English.

There are designated areas for smoking. Ironing and laundry services are paid upon arrival.

The administration allows the settlement of vacationers with pets. There is no additional charge and no prior arrangement is required. Children under 12 years old without a separate bed stay at the hotel for free. It is possible to arrange an extra bed in the room for a fee.





Providing leisure activities for kids within the kids club

The administration’s pastime of the kids was carefully thought out and everything was done so that, having fed up with previously unprecedented positive emotions, the children would receive new strength and energy for the whole coming year. All items in the children's room are colorful. There is a sofa and a synthesizer in front of it. This allows you to develop children's creative talents. There are tables, stools, high chairs. Kits for drawing will help the child master the skills of a young artist. Children's railway, designers, a variety of cars, puzzles and dolls will take the child for a while, which will allow parents to enjoy their stay in Abkhazia. There is an opportunity to use babysitting services for an additional fee.





Entertaining children in the open air

Special attention in the hotel "Abaata" (Gagra) deserves a playground in the fresh air. A wooden complex with plastic elements is safe for health and interesting to overcome. There are high swings, a long slide, a soft staircase. You can realize or develop your climbing skills in this cozy place among numerous greenery, palm trees and trees.

A long soft labyrinth will take children to a real fairy tale. For the comfort of young guests in the park area there are soft mats. An interesting learning area with desks and stools, as well as blackboards for drawing or the learning process is equipped for the children. There are also large soft sofas for parents who want to watch their children every minute. They are in the shade of dense greenery and at the same time allow you to enjoy nature.





Beach area

Hotel "Abaata" (Abkhazia, Gagra) overlooks the sand and pebble beach, which is located in Old Gagra. This place is more suitable for lovers of secluded relaxation surrounded by unspoilt nature. Here the infrastructure is less developed than on the Central Beach. A wide variety of water rides is absent. The use of beach towels, sun loungers and parasols is included in the price.

Hotel "Abaata" (Abkhazia): guest reviews

Vacationers here, according to their reviews, refer to the only drawback available in the hotel is the lack of food included in the price. However, on the other hand, they are grateful to the hotel for the opportunity to taste the dishes of nearby establishments. This perspective does not scare the guests at all, but rather inspires a possible varied vacation.

Those who happened to live on the third floor of the buildings note that their balcony is a real spacious terrace, and they were pleasantly surprised by this. The proximity to the sea is also pleasing to the arrivals - it is just a stone's throw away. In general, the Abaata Hotel (Gagra) receives mostly positive reviews. Many guests note that they will come here with pleasure. But is not this the main indicator for any hotel complex?





Attraction near the hotel

In the immediate vicinity of the hotel complex "Abaata" is the Seaside Park of Gagra, which was founded in 1902 and is the main attraction of the city. Plants for his garden were brought from all over the world. The unique arboretum stretches for 6 km along the Black Sea, it is always clean and beautiful here.

Plunging into the abundance of tropical nature, enjoying the amazing aroma of exotic plants, local residents and vacationers do not deprive themselves of the opportunity to visit this place. Peacocks are important to walk along numerous water bodies, and swans majestically swim along the water surface.





The therapeutic component of the leave

Holidays in these parts will certainly be supplemented by a health-improving component, since the formed climate contributes to this. There are conditions for treatment with mineral waters. There is thermal sulfate-calcium-magnesium mineral water used for baths. The resort is wellness, there are many resorts and pensions.

Tourists who have been here once dream of returning here again. The beauty of the local surroundings, the fantastic nature of Gagra, the colorful cuisine of Abkhazia, the friendly population form a general positive impression among the visitors. Comfortable accommodation, attentive staff and European-level service are complemented by the vacation of guests staying at the Abaata Hotel (Abkhazia). Improving the body, getting a charge of vivacity and satiety with new forces - this is how everyone who has been here characterizes the rest. Amazingly beautiful photos of local attractions will occupy a decent page in your home photo album.
