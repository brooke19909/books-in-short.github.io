Hotel Barcelo Capella Beach 4 (Dominican Republic): photos and reviews of tourists

The Caribbean has always been fanned by the halo of romance of pirate adventures, beauty and identity. The island located in the sea, on which the Dominican Republic is located, was discovered by Christopher Columbus and is famous for its metropolitan house-museum named after the great discoverer. But the most vivid impressions of the island remain with those who came here on a sea holiday. In terms of beauty, cleanliness and environmental friendliness, the Dominican beaches, perhaps, have no competitors all over the world.

Where is the hotel located





Hotel Barcelo Capella Beach 4 * is located on the southeast coast of the Dominican Republic, on the beach of Villas del Mar, which is famous for the dazzling white sand that covers the coast like a fairy fairy. The hotel is conveniently located in a resort area called Juan Dolio. The distance from the hotel to the airport of the capital of Santo Domingo, which is called Las Americas, can be covered by taxi in half an hour, to the capital itself a little longer - about 40 minutes.

Who will love the hotel?

The hotel is ideal not only for romantic couples, but also for families with children. It is located right on the coast, just a stone's throw from the sea. Snow-white sand will surely appeal to children, because it is so nice to run barefoot on it. Two golf courses are located near the hotel - everyone who wants to play an elite game can have a great time, enjoying the fascinating process.

All Inclusive Settings

The All inclusive system of the Barcelo Capella Beach Resort 3 * hotel will allow you to forget about saving for a while and relax one hundred percent.

Guests are offered a wide selection of dishes and local drinks (including alcoholic drinks) for breakfast, lunch and dinner in unlimited quantities. Meals are organized in one of the restaurants on site. For one hour every day you can use all water equipment and try yourself in various kinds of water sports (the only condition that is imposed by the hotel is that the watercraft used must not be equipped with a motor).

The hotel offers a variety of entertainment programs day and evening, as well as a games room and a mini club - every day and for free.

El Pescador is open daily and serves a buffet for all guests. Restaurant El Battey de la Costa works on the same principle and every night offers hotel guests dishes of local and other world cuisines - Mexican, Italian, Brazilian and Oriental.

Services

The hotel has its own beach with a sandy bottom and a gentle entrance, which is very convenient and safe for children. Sunbeds on the coast are provided free of charge.

Anyone who does not want to go to the beach can cool off on a hot day in one of the two pools, the peculiarity of which is that they are multilevel.

Those who like water and massage treatments can use the spa.





Barcelo Capella Beach 4 * (Dominican Republic) provides paid laundry services, a hairdresser, and the doctor who is constantly present at the hotel will provide highly qualified assistance if necessary.

The hotel offers free telephone and fax services, as well as Internet access via Wi-Fi connection. There are three conference rooms where you can hold any meetings.

There is also a currency exchange office and a car rental center on site. If the tourist arrived by car, you can park your car for free - next to the hotel there is a specially designated parking space.

Every evening, the amphitheater, which seats 700 people, hosts various shows and entertainment programs.

There is a business center near the hotel, and there are souvenir shops, a jewelry shop and a supermarket on site.

For sports and recreation

Sports enthusiasts will have something to do at their leisure. They have a gym, two tennis courts, table tennis, mini-football and mini-golf fields.

Every day you can do aerobics, play badminton, darts, volleyball, as well as attend a dance master class and learn some Spanish.





Guests of the Barcelo Capella Beach 4 * hotel guides offer different options for excursion programs.

Not one vacation at sea is complete without water sports and scuba diving. In the coastal zone, everyone can go snorkelling, diving, as well as learn the basics of windsurfing and canoeing. If you relax by the pool, you can play water polo along with an animation team.

All lovers of gambling are invited to drive to the casino in the evening. Transfer for hotel guests is absolutely free.

For children

Young guests of the Barcelo Capella 4 * hotel (Dominican Republic, Juan Dolio) will be able to get their portion of pleasure by visiting the playground and a mini club, as well as splashing in plenty in one of the two children's pools. In the restaurant, cooks specially prepare children's dishes for them, and for the little ones to sit comfortably, high chairs are provided here.





Babysitting services are provided for a fee.

Reviews

Tourists vacationing at the hotel not in the midst of the tourist season, according to them, did not feel any discomfort: there is no sweltering heat, and the sea is warm and clean. Many hotel guests leave from Barcelo Capella Beach 4 * reviews regarding the territory of the hotel. It should be noted that these impressions are all as one enthusiastic. Not only that, the whole vast territory of the hotel is surrounded by representatives of the local exotic flora, so in addition pink flamingos and peacocks freely walk on it.

Hotel guests praise local chefs for delicious and beautifully served dishes. Moreover, according to vacationers, the food is not only tasty, but also plentiful. Especially praised meat and fish, which are presented in a wide assortment and prepared in various ways.

Some guests complain about the quality of watermelons (they are not sweet enough), there are no complaints about other fruits.





Among the reviews of the hotel there are those who have complaints about the cleanliness of the beach. Although the beach is municipal, hotel staff should spend more time cleaning the area. In general, guests like the beach - it is large and beautiful, and the sea is calm, gentle and warm.

Summing up, we can say that the hotel is perfectly equipped for absolutely all categories of tourists, and if you come here, you will definitely want to come back again.
