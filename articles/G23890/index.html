The best amusement parks in Germany: list with photos, reviews of tourists

Entertainment in Germany is a serious and responsible business, especially when it comes to children. The country has a large number of ultramodern amusement parks, some of which can safely be called some of the best in the world. Each such center of fun boasts an impressive territory, landscape, as well as steep slides. First-class food courts, safe rides, professional animators and a variety of entertainment shows - all this is offered by the best amusement parks in Germany.

Europa-park

Europa-Park is an amusement park in Germany, which is not only the most visited in the country, but also the second most polar in Europe, second only to the French Disneyland. The entertainment center is located in the city of Rust, a couple of hours drive from Stuttgart. It is divided into sixteen zones, thirteen of which are stylistically dedicated to a particular European country, among which there are Russia, Greece and Iceland. Three thematic sections beat such places as "Enchanted Forest", "Kingdom of Minipots", "Viking Land". Each zone demonstrates architecture, food, shops, as well as the sights of the state, which is represented in this place. Photo of the amusement park "Europe Park" in Germany, you can see below.

There are ten roller coasters in Europa Park, among which there is one of the largest and largest in Europe - the Silver Star. The center regularly hosts various musical, laser and ice performances. "Arthur in the Kingdom of Minibuses" is one of the latest additions, where three thematic departments are combined into one journey.

Reviews of tourists about this amusement park are mostly positive. Many write that they really liked each thematic area, including the kitchen and attractions. They also say that Europa-Park is a kind of city in the city, from which one does not want to leave at all.

Bayern-park

This amusement park in Germany has slides for every taste and age. There is a roller coaster, a giant swing, a free fall tower, rafting and much more. No weather will disturb the fun, because in the indoor hall various entertainment events are held, including climbing, a game town and more.

Bayern-Park has playgrounds, magic trains, carousels, tower slides and so on. Children will be very interested to stroll through the colorful children's village, where you can find various trampolines, labyrinths, dry pools.

Themed attractions are made in the form of different animals. Aviaries with various animals are installed throughout the park, and regularly there is an exciting performance with eagles, falcons and other birds of prey.

Many positive reviews about this amusement park tell us that you can have a fun and rich family holiday here. According to some extreme people, the slides, although diverse, are not steep enough. Nevertheless, if extremeness can be subjective, then the organization and work of personnel remains an objectively positive quality of the park.

Phantasialand

This amusement park attracts about two million visitors annually. Phantasialand is divided into six thematic zones, among which are Fantasy, Africa, Berlin, Mystery, Mexico, China. Black Mamba is an extreme African-style roller coaster ride for tourists from all over the world. Also, various shows are regularly held in the park; thematic performances are organized for children.

According to many tourists, "Fantazyland" is the most favorite place not only for children who have been here, but also for parents. Some even compare the park with the legendary Disneyland, saying that the German center is winning. According to estimates, such components as cleanliness of the park, administrative work and, of course, a variety of attractions are leading.

Holiday park

Holiday Park is one of the best amusement parks in Germany, which is visited annually by more than a million people.

The Free Fall Tower is the first such attraction in Europe. Expedition GeForce is a roller coaster for real extreme sports. Its speed reaches 120 kilometers per hour. There are also incredible water slides, offering a ride for the whole family.

Reviews of the amusement park in Germany are mostly positive. Tourists write that here you can have a great rest with your family, as well as try out cool and high-speed rides on your own that meet all the safety rules.

Legoland

43 hectares and 56 million details of a famous designer - this is all Legoland park in Germany. The center is built almost entirely from Lego, and also boasts dozens of exciting rides and theme shows.

Visitors to the park share their impressions that age does not matter to enjoy the atmospheric world of your favorite designer. They say that everything is thought out in great detail. Organized staff and cheap tickets are the best incentive to come to this center.

Hansa park

Amusement park in Germany Hansa-Park is located in Sierksdorf on the Baltic Sea. The center is the fifth largest in the country and attracts more than a million visitors annually.

The territory of the park is divided into eleven thematic zones, among which are open "Medieval", "Pirate", "Western", "Adventure", "Mexican" and others. Performances include a water circus, a parrot theater, magic and laser performances.

The main thing that is mentioned in the positive reviews is that this park is perfect for fans of moderate extreme sports, as well as for those who do not like to queue, because the average ride time in the queue is 15 minutes for the main attraction. Tourists also write that Hansa-Park is a great place for couples with children. They note that it is advisable to come here to the opening itself.

Heide park

Heide-Park is located in Soltau, an hour's drive from Hanover. Its area covers more than eighty hectares, which gives it the right to be called one of the largest amusement parks in Germany.

The entertainment center is divided into five zones: a quarter in the style of Saxony, "Pirate World", "Transylvania", "Land of the Forgotten" and "Wild West". The park has more than forty attractions designed for both families and lovers of fast driving. One of the most popular roller coasters is the Colossos, a wooden roller coaster that is the second coolest in the world.

Heide-Park also has slides with a circular game ride, a gold mining area, a children's kayak center, and much more.

Five performances are held daily in this amusement park in Germany: a 25-minute Maya show, a 35-minute pirate show, a 20-minute show based on the popular children's series, a 10-minute puppet show, and a 30-minute performance based on the Madagascar cartoon .

Many tourists who have visited this amusement park advise to allocate a whole day to this place and take a map with them so as not to miss anything. They note that the atmosphere in Heide-Park is simply incredible, romantic and fabulous. They write in reviews that the best time of the year to visit is autumn, due to the lack of large crowds. Among other things, tourists say that the attractions are for every taste, the food is tasty, and the staff is polite and organized.

Schwaben-park

This amusement park is an hour's drive from Stuttgart. The center is divided into three zones: action, entertainment, adventure. Himalayan Railway - roller coaster, which is one of the most popular in Germany. Wave Runner is an extreme boat trip for the whole family to ride.

In the reviews of the park, tourists write that they liked everything: from the variety of slides to service and cleanliness. Visitors say that very professional animators work in this center. Also, guests of the park were pleased with the ticket price.

Tripsdrill

In addition to water slides, carousels for every taste and even a wine museum, there is a wildlife park and much more. The Mammoth roller coaster is almost a local attraction, due to the fact that it is the largest wooden roller coaster in Germany.

Over the past eighty years, Tripsdrill has become one of the main tourist destinations, with more than a hundred different attractions on the territory, including the Dance Bowls, the Scream Tower, water rafting, and much more.

Take a break from the dizzying carousels in the wildlife park. Its territory reaches twenty hectares.

Reviews of the park say that a visitor of any age will like it here. Tourists who were here with their family say that all the attractions, despite the age of the park, do not raise doubts about safety, and the organization of leisure is of a high level.
