Hotel Ilimyra Hotel 3 (Turkey, Kemer)

The Ilimyra Hotel tourist complex offers you a pleasant stay. The hotel is located in a picturesque place - in an urban-type village under the amazing name of Camyuva (Turkey). The three-story building of the guesthouse is conveniently located surrounded by a pine forest, and the rooms offer an amazing panorama of the mountains. The building was built in 1999, and the last repair was carried out in 2008.





The Ilimyra Hotel 3 complex is comfortable and clean, ideal for a budget holiday with children. Old people and newlyweds will also like it here, as the hotel has an atmosphere of comfort and goodwill. The staff is very friendly and attentive to their guests - always with a smile on their face provides any information.

Number of rooms

A total of 39 rooms are located in the three-story building of the Ilimyra Hotel. Some rooms have a spacious terrace overlooking the flowering garden. According to the schedule (at least twice a week) room service is provided (cleaning and replacement of bed linen).





All rooms are equipped with necessary furniture, appliances (color TV with Russian channels) and a private bathroom. There is a direct dial telephone, central air conditioning, a paid safe (optional), a hairdryer and a compact bar with paid alcohol.

Nutrition

All vacationers eat on the European all-inclusive system. At certain times, breakfast, full lunch and dinner are provided in the cozy buffet style dining room. Every day from 10 a.m. to 10 p.m. local and local alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks are free of charge (imported alcohol - for a fee). Near the outdoor pool in the courtyard of the hotel there is a bar where you can enjoy a refreshing cocktail or juice.





Beach

On the second coastline (about 400 m from the Ilimyra Hotel complex) there is a pebble beach. The beach area is owned by the hotel, so all amenities are included in the price. Also, a water entertainment center is opened on the shore - for an additional fee, tourists can dive with a mask under the water and watch the amazing life of marine life.

Additional Information

Ilimyra Hotel boasts an open-air swimming pool and comfortable sun loungers and sun loungers on the landscaped and landscaped grounds of the Ilimyra Hotel . In cold weather, guests can take a dip in the indoor pool of 100 square meters. For younger tourists there is a separate safe pool in the courtyard of the guest house.





In the evening, the audience is entertained with interesting performances. Business people can freely use the equipped conference room with 90 seats. For active leisure - table tennis and darts.

Digest

Do you want to visit sunny Turkey, admire the picturesque scenery, take a walk along the beautiful promenade and soak up the rays of the gentle sun away from the hectic everyday life? Then welcome to the complex Ilimyra Hotel 3. Reviews of tourists are varied - some were delighted with the good attitude from the staff, others liked the clean coast and the virgin beauty of nature.

Many complained about the monotonous food, but in general the hotel left pleasant impressions and positive emotions. The couples with children were also satisfied - for the kids there was a great pool, where the kids can splash around and play all day. And due to the remoteness from the highway, the air in this area is saturated with oxygen, so it rests comfortably and calmly.
