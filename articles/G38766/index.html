Chernihiv hotels: what to choose?

Among tourists who come to the picturesque Chernihiv - one of the most famous Ukrainian cities - travelers predominate, interested in historical monuments and sacred structures. Many business people also come here to participate in discussions, conferences and other important events. Almost all modern Chernihiv hotels are ready to accept any travelers, regardless of the purpose of their arrival. Especially now for tourists, even if they are excessively picky about the service and living conditions, there is plenty to choose from.

About Chernihiv hotels

While the city can not boast of luxury four or five star hotels. It is hard to say why they are not here. But this does not mean at all that two- and three-star hotels in Chernihiv will not suit tourists who come to this large regional center. Here you can find almost the entire spectrum of hotel varieties existing on the modern market, from spacious multi-room apartments to low-cost hostels. Of the two-star hotels of the city and those that do not have a certain category, tourists most often choose accommodation in several well-known institutions:

  • Bryansk
  • Parasolke;
  • Ranch Club
  • "Sport";
  • "Trade Union";
  • Diana
  • Hradec;
  • "Comfort";
  • "The Descendant".

In the luxurious "Ukraine" and "Chernihiv" - three-star hotels - more comfortable conditions await guests of the city. Almost all Chernihiv hotels do not lag behind modern requirements, so each of them necessarily has access to paid or free Internet. None of the rooms in these Chernihiv hotels will disappoint guests with the lack of a bath or shower, TV or air conditioning. More detailed information on some of the mentioned establishments will help travelers better imagine the variety of hotels in the city.





Hotel "Profsoyuznaya" - a typical Chernihiv hotel

Business people do not have to look for a place for their events for a long time. The Profsoyuznaya Hotel with its assembly and 9 conference rooms will certainly satisfy their needs. Due to its location near the picturesque park and the Desna river flowing through the city, the hotel is also suitable for those who are satisfied with a quiet area and remoteness from the main streets. It will not be difficult for guests to choose one of 47 comfortable two- or one-room rooms, dividing into 7 categories - from standard to luxury. The cafe of the institution, which consists of three halls - Bolshoi, Zeleny and VIP - will delight visitors with inexpensive Ukrainian dishes.





Bryansk is a comfortable 2-star hotel

Due to the relatively recent reconstruction, the Bryansk hotel is in full compliance with modern requirements. This 2-star hotel, located very close to the Desna, is suitable primarily for those who travel by their own car - in the vicinity there are two inexpensive parking lots that work around the clock. All 50 reconstructed rooms are located in a modern 5-storey building. Guests can choose between single and double rooms. Four halls of the hotel restaurant are located in an adjacent two-story building. Here, for fans of incendiary dances, the doors of a night club are open. Hotel "Bryansk" also offers vacationers inexpensive services of a hairdresser and photo studio.





Ukraine is a luxurious 3-star hotel

Guests of Chernigov, dreaming of a hotel located near the central quarters, will not have to look for a suitable establishment for a long time. Tourists will certainly be satisfied with the hotel "Ukraine". Chernihiv became famous for its historical monuments and temple structures. The most famous of them - Detinets (also called the Kremlin or the Prince's Castle) - is located nearby the hotel. The location of the hotel was particularly lucky. There are several supermarkets next to it, inexpensive cafes are waiting for their visitors, the central market lures customers with a variety of products.





Almost all 90 rooms of the hotel, especially the "luxury" and apartments, will delight vacationers with spacious and bright rooms. Business travelers will certainly be pleased with the hotel's well-equipped conference room. A pleasant surprise awaits the newlyweds here - special rooms have been prepared for them. For motorists in the hotel yard there is a spacious parking. Gourmets will not be disappointed with the refined dishes of the stylish restaurant. Those vacationers who prefer cheaper food and drink can go to the nearby McDonald's or to one of the nearest cafes.

Waiting for a change

So far, hotels in Chernihiv cannot surprise tourists with modern fitness centers or luxurious spas. Often, guests of the city find in these hotels a lot in common with the style of service and the interior features of Soviet similar establishments. But the situation is gradually changing. Therefore, the times will surely come when Chernihiv hotels will be compared with those in Riga or Prague, and the city streets will be filled with travelers not only from Slavic countries, but also from Western Europe and even Southeast Asia.
