Sea vacations, water activities

Concorde El Salam Hotel has its own private beach with coral reefs. Equipped shore - there is a shower, deck chairs, mattresses, umbrellas. The underwater world near it is difficult to describe in words. And this is not just a reef, but entire labyrinths of corals - this is how tourists describe this place.
