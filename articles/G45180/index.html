Hotel Club Nena 5 * HV (Turkey, Side): photos and reviews of tourists

Turkey is associated with a Russian tourist with cheap holidays with the all-inclusive system, where in hotels more than ninety percent of the guests are compatriots. But there are places on the Anatolian Riviera where you still need to know foreign languages. A reward for this will be a club holiday. What it is? This is when you feel like a member of a privileged club - and such a feeling lasts from the first minute of your stay at the hotel until you leave this place. In this article we will consider Club Nena 5 HV. We built a review of the hotel solely on the reviews of those who have already visited there (especially in the summer of 2016). This elite hotel has a narrow specialization - children's recreation. For younger guests there is just paradise. It will also be comfortable for their parents. But the irrepressible youth, eager for drive and discos, it seems boring here. But what a freedom to shopaholics! Directly opposite the hotel is a huge shopping center. Many tourists noted that it is very convenient: to take the child to a mini-club, where he will be entertained by animators, and go shopping and go to the traditional Turkish market. But let's look at the characteristics of the Club Nena hotel point by point.



Where is Club Nena 5 HV located

Turkey, Side, Kyzyladzhak - this is the address of this hotel. The nearest settlement claiming the title of the town is Manavgat, with a waterfall famous throughout the country. It is located thirteen kilometers from the hotel. And Kyzyladzhak is a small village on the shores of the Mediterranean Sea. Look for historical sights in the former fishing village is not worth it. It is better to immediately go to Side, which is fifteen kilometers from the hotel. What distinguishes Kyzyladzhak is its picturesque nature. Everywhere pomegranate and citrus orchards, coniferous forests, fields with cotton. This place is chosen by families with young children and lovers of a quiet holiday surrounded by nature. The distance from Antalya Airport, where most flights from Russia land, is quite significant - eighty kilometers. If you have a shuttle service to the hotel, be prepared for the fact that you will be brought to Club Nena 5 HV last. But all compatriots, most likely, will be unloaded on the road in hotels of other settlements. There are very few Russians in Nena Club. The Germans, the inhabitants of the Scandinavian countries, mainly rest here, there are few citizens of the former socialist camp. If you must get to the hotel on your own, you should first take the shuttle bus to Side. From this ancient city go dolmushi (minibuses) to Kyzyladzhak. Travel time is about fifteen minutes. Stop - near the shopping center, which is opposite the described hotel.

Kyzyladzhak is chosen for the sake of the sea and beaches. All of them are marked with a blue flag for the cleanliness of the coast and water area. Club Nena 5 HV (Side) is a first-line hotel, which is important. But in Kyzyladzhak there are steep places and pebbles. But this hotel has an excellent beach, which we will describe in detail below. The rest of the village is not much different from other Turkish resorts that grew out of former fishing villages. There is a lot of entertainment - both on the territory of hotels and beyond. There is also a mini water park in the Club Nena 5 HV hotel itself. And on the beach line of the village you can ride horses or donkeys, go on a boat trip on a boat or yacht, ride on the "bananas" and other types of motor activities on the water. Territory

Quite a few independent travelers choose the Club Nena 5 HV hotel. Tours there are quite rare with Russian operators. But in vain. After all, there are excellent conditions for family vacations. Since the hotel is suburban, it has a huge, one hundred thousand square meters, territory. Landscape designers and gardeners did a great job - reviews say that the hotel buildings are simply buried in lush greenery and flowers. Walking in such a territory with children is a pleasure. There are males and hens everywhere, in the enclosures you can see dogs, pet cats and the like, cute and harmless animals. The Club Nena 5 HV Hotel is one three-story building, which houses the reception and the main restaurant, and fifteen bungalows in Turkish architectural style. Tourists recommend booking rooms in the latest buildings. They are two-story, and are located so that you do not see and do not hear the neighbors. It seems that you are an elite member of the club, and the entire infrastructure of the hotel is created exclusively for you. The hotel was built at the end of the last century. But do not be afraid that you will be settled in a dilapidated building with shabby furniture. The last repair, according to reviews, was held last year. The decor of the guest rooms shines with novelty, everything is working properly.

Hotel Club Nena 5 HV (Turkey, Side) places its guests in spacious and comfortable rooms. What's in the rooms

What conditions in the rooms does Club Nena 5 HV hotel provide to its guests? Description of the hotel by tourists usually begins with the praise of room decoration. All the equipment is of a new design: split-system climate control, a TV with a plasma screen (at least a dozen Russian-language channels), an electronic safe. True, this last convenience in the room was for a fee. But wi-fi was in the public domain, pulled at a decent speed in the room and by the pool, in a word, throughout the territory. Separate praises were given to beds. Even an extra bed (in some double rooms it is possible to share a third tenant) was with an orthopedic mattress. Five pillows to choose from, two additional warm blankets in the closet, new snow-white bedding. It was changed three times a week, and towels - every day. The decoration complements the wardrobe with hangers and shelves, bedside tables, chairs. There is also a free minibar in the bedroom. Every day it was replenished with three small bottles of sparkling water and a half liters of the dining room. At the expense of bathrooms, reviews say that most of them have a shower behind a glass partition. Upon arrival, guests are offered a complete set of hygiene products, with ear sticks and caps for the shower inclusive, but then every day they bring only soap, shampoos and shower gels. Bathrooms also include a hairdryer and slippers. The flooring in the rooms is tiled. Reviews also mention that all rooms have spacious balconies.

Conditions for children

Hotel Club Nena 5 * HV advertises itself as a place of family relaxation. And just paradise conditions were created for children here. You can come here even with newborns: on request they will give you a bath for bathing the baby, playpen, put a crib. You can rent a stroller. The restaurant has a free blender to grind food for the kids. There is also a children's restaurant. Moms can warm their baby bottles there. Dishes there are such that children usually like: meatballs, pancakes, chicken, curly pasta. The food is very healthy - not overcooked, not oily. Lots of fruits and desserts. And most importantly - the form of presentation: the same "buffet" as in adults. Only tables in the height of the child, so that the baby could normally see and choose dishes. The plates are multi-colored, the cheerful design of the children's restaurant contributed to the appetite of the most capricious "sick". The kids were delighted with the diverse living creatures in the cages - especially the talking parrot. The administration conducts recreational activities for children. Every night there are mini discos. And even at the pools, Club Nena 5 * HV hotel takes care of its young guests. Along with adult sun loungers, there are also children's ones. Mini club

It would seem that this service is difficult to surprise the tourist who has seen the Turkish and Egyptian resorts. But the Club Nena 5 * HV hotel was able to do this. Photos of tourists very often do not represent the beach and not the green territory, namely the mini-club of the hotel. And it is easy to understand. The mini-club of this hotel is a real hotel for children who are already four years old, but have not yet turned twelve. The building of the mini-club is two-story. On the first level there is a games room. Upstairs are small bedrooms. There is also a mother and baby room with a changing table and the ability to warm up the bottle. The children's hotel is adjacent to a separate fence: a playground with swings, a shallow pool with slides. Parents were especially surprised by the restaurant for kids and ... the toilet. Absolutely everything is done in miniature: toilet bowl, washbasin, mirror. The administration of the Hotel Nena Club hotel ensures that the child does not feel inferior in the adult world. Naturally, professional educators and animators are constantly with children. They are patronized and entertained all day. And, as the parents admitted, the children quickly overcame the language barrier (after all, the hotel has few Russians, and the animation is conducted in an amazing mixture of English, German and Turkish). The kids attended the mini club with great joy. There they will feed the child and put to bed, as in kindergarten.

Pools

For children, the Club Nena 5 * HV hotel (Turkey) has equipped three more pools. One of them is located next to the adult slides (for kids, their own are offered, not so steep and high). The second pool is located at the mini-club. And also the "frog zone" with a depth of only forty centimeters is available near the "Relax". By the way, it is there that there are children's sun loungers. Naturally, sun loungers and beach umbrellas on the terraces near the pools were given for free use to the guests of the Club Nena hotel.
