"Rzhev baths" in Moscow: visitor reviews, how to get

Truly unique are the "Rzhev baths" of Moscow. They were founded at the end of the nineteenth century. Public baths, working to this day in this format, were popular mainly among commoners, and during the Great Patriotic War military units were washed here.

Interior

Over time, the interior of the bathhouse was modified, but the spirit and atmosphere of the Russian baths remained unchanged. Facades and interior were restored. At the same time, the original architecture was restored as much as possible. The general atmosphere of the interior of the public "Rzhev baths" evokes memories of the spiritual traditions of the Soviet era, and ancient vaulted ceilings contribute to a special concentration of soft steam. The dressing room is a comfortable equipped compartment, which is designed for 6 people. Here you can leave clothes and not worry about their safety. Paid service - 50 rubles. After that, you can set off to enjoy bathing procedures in the spacious halls of the complex.





Baths are divided into male and female sections, there are separate cabins, showers and large roomy bathtubs.

Soaring process

The unique process of hovering causes a fascinating sports interest among visitors, completely relaxes and heals the body. After the procedures, an increase in general tone is noted, the effect of rejuvenation, improvement of well-being and mood are observed.

There are also sedentary approaches, during which visitors sit on benches or sit on shelves. Combined steam is delivered above the waist or into the legs. It is also possible to get an intensive massage with brooms of various compositions.

The bathhouse has its own boiler room and a unique ventilation system, which allows you to always keep the right temperature in the steam room. It is also important to note that ideal and sterile cleanliness reigns in the "Rzhev baths" in all departments.

A regular visit to the bath strengthens health, prolongs life, preserves youth and energy. In this case, the skin receives nutrition and hydration. Before entering the steam room, you need to take a shower and be sure to wipe yourself dry, and after steaming, take a shower of cold water.

Services

In the "Rzhev baths", in addition to the steam room, works:

  • A beauty salon where experienced professionals are ready to offer various face and hair care services, as well as manicures and pedicures. Cosmetic procedures are performed for both women and men.




  • Professional massage rooms are equipped.
  • A professional cook of the highest class always works, ready to offer dishes of Russian and European cuisine, and on the ground floor there is a cafe.
  • There is a tea service from a real Russian samovar.

  • For lovers of dry steam, there is a Finnish sauna with a pool.
  • There is a small shop where you can buy brooms, slippers, hats and a variety of accessories for bath procedures.

Address and how to get

To this day, baths are very popular among different segments of the population and are located in the Meshchansky district of the capital at 3 Banny proezd, building 1, Prospekt Mira metro station (580 m).





Scheme from the metro station "Riga" (710 m):

  • the first exit, go straight 82 m and cross the third transport ring;
  • go through the underground passage to Gilyarovsky Street 130 m;
  • further straight 500 m to Banny passage, after which turn right and walk 120 m.

"Rzhev baths": prices for visiting

The cost of one visit varies from 1300 to 1500 rubles. There are discounts for preferential categories of citizens and children. "Rzhev baths" are open to visitors daily from 9 am to 10 pm.
