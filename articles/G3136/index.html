Review of Sennheiser HD 439

Sennheiser is a well-known German company that manufactures equipment for broadcasting, recording and reproducing sound. One of the best sellers that has made the company world-wide is headphones. Headphones from Sennheiser have always been of high build quality, superior design and amazing sound. More recently, a German company introduced its new product called Sennheiser HD 439. What is special about the new headphones? You can find answers to these questions in this article.

Sennheiser HD 439 review

The HD 439 is a mid-range headphone with a high level of protection against ambient noise. And this means that nothing will interrupt the process of listening to music or watching a movie. Another advantage of the Sennheiser HD 439 is its neodymium magnets, guaranteeing an amazing and crystal clear sound. However, this is not all the advantages of this model. Want to know more about Sennheiser HD 439? Welcome to this review, which will tell you in detail about all the features of the HD 349.





Design

The first thing that catches the eye is the appearance of the device. Headphones Sennheiser HD 439 look pretty massive, but it is very stylish and neat. Perhaps the main feature of this model in terms of design is the presence of decorative nets on the cups, which give the device a rather interesting appearance. The Sennheiser HD 439 uses a removable cable. Moreover, two cords of various lengths are included in the kit at once (one - 1.4 meters, the other - 3 meters). Due to this, HD 439 can be used both for work in a professional studio, and for domestic needs.

Build quality, as always, is up to par. There are no creaks, backlashes. The headphone cord is pretty strong. However, since it does not have a braid, it must be handled with great care. Nevertheless, even with a sharp jerk, the wire will not break, but, most likely, will jump out of the headphone jack. The cord itself is made of oxygen-free copper. Due to this, the conductivity of the device is quite high. Minimal signal loss does not occur even over long distances.





If we talk about ergonomics, then in this regard, the Sennheiser HD 439 show their good side. Headphones, due to their light weight, comfortable headband and ear pads, sit perfectly on the head. Moreover, they do not cause fatigue even during prolonged and continuous operation.

Perhaps the weak side in terms of assembly - ear pads. The fact is that they are made of velor. This material has high wear resistance. Nevertheless, it has the ability to collect dust, lint, pet hair, etc. on it. This reduces the overall usability of the device. However, this problem is not critical, because it can be solved with an ordinary wet sponge.

Sound

The Sennheiser HD 439 boasts an amazing balance of reproduced frequencies across various bands. High frequencies are clearly audible, and they do not lock even at high volume. Mid frequencies are also quite clear and deep. Worst of all, low frequencies are expressed here. Although the headphones have good bass, the velor does its job and partially absorbs low frequencies , thus blurring the overall picture of the sound.





If we talk about soundproofing properties, then Sennheiser HD 439 has everything in order. Headphones completely enclose from noise during a street walk, and even more so at home. At the same time, the arms do not press on the ears as much as similar devices do. This provides an extra level of comfort.

Also can not but rejoice the optimization of the device. Sennheiser HD 439 headphones can be used with most modern devices. For example, it can be smartphones running on Android and iOS, tablets, speakers, MP3 and CD players.

Sennheiser HD 439: reviews

As for the reviews, customers reacted positively to the brand new HD 439 system. Many users noticed the excellent appearance of the device, good ergonomics, amazing sound and high build quality. In addition, buyers are very pleased with the presence of two cords in a standard set.





But there were some cons. Sennheiser HD 439 cords have no braid. This makes them less reliable. In addition, fabric ear cushions that negatively affect the reproduction of low frequencies and collect any small garbage on themselves disappoint. Also, the disadvantages include a clearly overpriced price. In order to enjoy crystal clear sound, you will have to pay about 6,000 rubles (approximately 2,000 hryvnias).

Conclusion

In the end, we can say that the Sennheiser HD 439 is a fairly balanced model in terms of build quality and sound. Although it has some disadvantages, it has many more advantages. These headphones are ideal for home use. And thanks to the stylish design, Sennheiser HD 439 is not ashamed to appear on the street. Therefore, the device can be used not only at home, but also during street walks, prolonged trips in public transport, etc. Even despite the obviously overpriced cost of the device, it is quite difficult for the new Sennheiser HD 439 headphones to find a decent and high-quality alternative.
