"Amra" - sanatorium (Gagra): rest and treatment

The year-round resort "Amra" in Gagra is located in one of the most beautiful corners of the old city. The distance from the airport and railway station to the health resort is 38 km, from the border - 33. For citizens of Russia and the CIS, a visa-free regime applies . You can get to the sanatorium by minibus, which goes to a health resort called Amra. The sanatorium, which Gagra can only be proud of, good-naturedly welcomes all visitors.
