Hotel "New", Kazan: description, rooms, services and reviews of tourists

Kazan is one of the most interesting cities not only in Tatarstan, but throughout the Russian Federation. The infrastructure of the city is at an excellent level, and this is already having a positive effect on economic development. Business in the capital of Tatarstan brings good superprofits, which is why quite often there are various business meetings and conferences attended by people from different parts of Russia and the world. Today we will discuss in detail the hotel complex, which is an ideal option for accommodation.

The Novinka Hotel (Kazan) is a modern tourism project that has been operating in the capital of Tatarstan for many days and offers its visitors a high level of service, comfortable rooms and reasonable rental prices. Here, any guest can take advantage of a huge number of additional services and stay in the best rooms. In this article we will discuss the hotel, reviews about it, number of rooms, etc. There is a lot of information, so let's get started right away!

Description

At this stage of its development, the hotel is a modern building that ideally combines comfortable rooms, a cozy restaurant, a large conference room, an excellent fitness center and many other infrastructure facilities. In general, everything here is designed so that guests of such a project as the Novinka Hotel (Kazan), reviews of which we will discuss in detail a little later, feel at home.





This hotel complex is located very close to the huge ice arena and just a 15-minute drive from the city's business center and railway station. Guests of the tourism project are the most important value for the friendly and always ready to help staff. The administrator and other representatives of the hotel will make sure that the guests of the establishment are in a relaxed atmosphere, safe and comfortable.

By the way, "Novelty" is decorated in a modern style. The interior is dominated by bright colors, including white and blue. In addition, the rooms are decorated in beige colors, which looks very interesting.

A bit about renting

On the official website of this tourist complex, in the section where the rooms are presented, it is indicated that the administration of the project seeks to make the guest's stay more comfortable, therefore 90 excellent rooms of different categories are available in the hotel, which are located in two buildings. By the way, as a compliment from the project management, every guest who rents a room receives a breakfast, free access to the fitness center and access to high-speed wireless Internet.





As you know, the Novinka Hotel (Kazan) has a large selection of rooms for a reason, because this establishment has the status of a business-class hotel and three stars, although customers evaluate the project at least 4 *. In this case, it is completely incomprehensible why some tourist complexes offering a minimum of services to their visitors have 4 *, and this interesting hotel is only 3 *. In general, we will not go deeper, but rather we will discuss in more detail the information related to the lease.

Check-in at the hotel starts at 14:00, but check-out is two hours before. Payment for rooms is daily. If you stay in a hotel for a period that does not exceed 22 hours, then payment will be made anyway for a day's stay. For the convenience of customers, Novinka offers early check-in without booking (in this case, the accommodation is made upon the availability of rooms).

In addition, check-in from midnight to 4 a.m. is only possible if you have booked a room previously and at the same time indicate the time of early arrival. In turn, from 4 a.m. to 2 p.m. check-in is also possible by prior reservation.

In addition, we note that in each of the hotel rooms you can use the telephone, satellite TV, air conditioning, wireless internet, a minibar and a safe.

Accommodation and other nuances

Hotel Novinka (Kazan, Russia) has penalties for people who exceed the length of stay in a rented room. If you stay in your room from 12:00 to 18:00, then you will have to pay an additional fee of 5% of the cost of daily rent for each extra hour of stay. If you leave between 18:00 and 24:00, you will have to make a fifty percent surcharge. In addition, if the check-out is after midnight, the client will have to pay for renting a room for a full day.

It is also worth noting that as a compliment, the 3 * Novinka Hotel (Kazan) can make its guest 1-2 hours of additional accommodation, if available rooms are available.





For parents of children under the age of 7, we note that their children are accommodated in the hotel with them completely free of charge without extra bed. At the age of 7-14 years, an extra charge of 700 rubles is taken for placing a child, which includes breakfast.

And now let's discuss each of the hotel rooms in more detail!

Single in the second building

The cost of renting this room is 2 and a half thousand rubles, and only one person can stay in it. The room is decorated in beige tones. In the room you can use air conditioning, wireless Internet, a minibar, satellite TV, a safe, a double bed, a work desk, as well as a hairdryer and shower, which are located in a separate bathroom.

It is also worth noting that for a surcharge of 600 rubles you can buy not the basic rate, but half board, so that additional free dinner will be organized for you.

Single standard

This room has a more interesting interior: the floor in blue is ideally complemented by beige walls. In this case, one or two people can be accommodated here. The rental price directly depends on the number of guests: one person (basic rate) - 3100 rubles, two people (basic rate) - 3800 rubles, one person (half board) - 3 thousand 700 rubles, two persons (half board) - 5 thousand rubles. Additionally, you should visit the pool, where you can have a great time.





In your room you can use air conditioning, wireless Internet, satellite TV and a plasma TV, telephone, minibar, double bed, armchair, coffee table, desk and other amenities, including a separate bathroom with a hairdryer, a bidet and a shower.

Double standard

A room of this category is very similar to the previously discussed single standard, but in this case the area is slightly larger and the design is the same. One person can stay in such a room at two rates: the main - 3 thousand 100 rubles., Half board - 3 thousand 700 rubles. In addition, two people have the opportunity to rent a room for a day also at two current rates: the main - 4 thousand 100 rubles, half board - 5 thousand 300 rubles.

In each room of this category, the 3 * Novinka Hotel (Russia, Kazan), with a rating of 8.3 out of 10, provides its guests with air conditioning, a plasma TV with satellite TV, wireless internet, a minibar, a telephone, a safe, two separate beds, as well as a work desk. It should be noted that in addition to the double standard there is a separate sanitary unit where a shower is installed and there is a hairdryer and bidet.

Two-room standard

This room category can accommodate a maximum of two people. The rental price will vary from 3 thousand 100 rubles to 5 thousand. The two-room premise is represented by a living room, where upholstered furniture is installed, and a bedroom, where there are two separate beds or one double.





In the room you can use the TV with satellite TV, Wi-Fi, telephone, air conditioning, minibar, work desk. Additionally, there is a separate bathroom with a bidet, shower and hairdryer.

Studio

Living in a room of this category is an ideal option for those who love a modern interior and a large space. Here, as in all previously discussed rooms, 1-2 people can accommodate, and the cost directly depends on this.

For example, two guests can live here for only 4200 rubles. at the basic rate or for 5400 half board. At the same time, one person can stay in the room: the main - 3 thousand 500 rubles, half board - 4 thousand 100 rubles.





It should be noted that in the studio you will find a TV with satellite TV, a telephone, air conditioning, a minibar, two comfortable armchairs, a work desk, a double bed, a coffee table and a separate bathroom with everything you need. Throughout the room, wireless high-speed Internet is working fine.

De Suite with shower

This room consists of two large rooms: a living room with comfortable furniture and a bedroom with a large bed and other amenities. The rental price in this case varies from 4 and a half thousand rubles to 6,700.

In the deluxe suite you will find a large double bed, air conditioning, a telephone, a work desk with a lamp, a table with a mirror, a mini safe, a TV and other amenities. The bathroom comes with a shower, bidet and hairdryer.

De Suite with Jacuzzi

The Novinka Hotel in Kazan offers city guests and locals a huge number of rooms, but this is the last one you can rent here. If you think about it, you can understand that this room is practically no different from the deluxe room considered in the previous section.

The difference is the presence of a jacuzzi, so the cost also increases slightly. In this case, you can rent this number for 5000 rubles. (one person, main), 5600 rub. (one person, half board), 6000 rubles. (two people, the main) and 7200 rubles (two people, half board). In this deluxe room you can take advantage of everything that is in the past, but instead of a shower cabin there is a jacuzzi.
