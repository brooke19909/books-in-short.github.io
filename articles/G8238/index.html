A bit about the city

Nizhnevartovsk is a fairly young city located on the river. Ob and founded in 1909. The status of the city was obtained already in 1972.

According to 2017, about 274.5 thousand people live in Nizhnevartovsk. They are all located on 270 km¬≤. The entire population can be divided according to nationality: 60% of Russians, 9% of Tatars, 6% of Ukrainians and then Azerbaijanis, Bashkirs, Chuvashs, Belarusians, etc.

The capital of Samotlor

The city became great because of the location in it of the main famous Samotlor field. Residents of Nizhnevartovsk are usually proud of this.

Every citizen of Vartovsk and Khanty-Mansi Autonomous Okrug knows what is the main activity in the city. The area was called the oil capital of the country, however, like the city of Surgut, located literally 220 km from Nizhnevartovsk.

At the entrance to the city, a monument, called locals Alyosha, even flaunts. He was put in memory of the conquerors of Samotlor.
