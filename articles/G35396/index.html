Tishki Lake - fishing center

Lake Tishki is located in the northern part of the Chelyabinsk region. It is quite large in area, flat, far from the regional center. The above facts explain why the reservoir has become the main fishing center of this region. Location

Tishki Lake is located in Kushanak District. The nearest settlements are as follows: in the south - Dubrovka, on the north coast - New Kurmanovo, in the west - Surakovo, in the east - Nugumanovo. The lake in question has neither sources nor channels; food is provided thanks to groundwater and precipitation.



Lake Tishki has the following dimensions: eight kilometers in length and three and a half in width. The average depth of the reservoir is six meters, the transparency is three meters. Fishing is carried out throughout the lake, whose area is eighteen square kilometers. The bottom is muddy, only in the western part is it sandy. Only one fifth of the reservoir is occupied with reeds and reeds, which indicates its low degree of overgrowing.

Lake Tishki. How can I get to?

If you are leaving Chelyabinsk, you should keep your path to the northeast. You need to drive forty-five kilometers. There are two routes. The first option: after leaving Chelyabinsk, take the Dolgoderevenskoye-Bolshoy Balandino-Dubrovka route. Lake Tishki is two and a half kilometers from Dubrovka (take south). The second option: follow the path "Chelyabinsk-Azure-Glory-Novy-Dubrovka", after we turn to the north. Thus, you can be on the west coast of the reservoir.

The distance from Yekaterinburg to Lake Tishki is one hundred eighty kilometers. The Kunashak-Muslumovo-Novo Kurmanovo road will lead to its destination. The last village is located on the north side of the reservoir.

You have to pay for the opportunity to go fishing. To date, a ticket for one person will cost three hundred rubles per day. Some citizens have free access to the pond. Among them are veterans of the Great Patriotic War, invalids of the 1st and 2nd groups, injured at the Chernobyl nuclear power plant and children under the age of fourteen. In order not to pay for a ticket, these categories of citizens need to have a document with them confirming their status.



You can buy the right to fish in several places. So, some go to the office of the fish factory (a lake is assigned to it) in the village of Kushanak. Address - st. Sovkhoznaya, 17. Authorized distributors also sell vouchers for Lake Tishki (Chelyabinsk Region).

Fishing Reviews

Experienced fishermen note that Lake Tishki is a typical carp pond. Many boast an excellent catch of mirror, German, common and Japanese carp. Basically, six to seven kilograms of live weight are caught on a hook. The best time for fishing in this pond is from May to early October. Have a nice bite!
