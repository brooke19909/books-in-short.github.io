We create a beautiful panel of natural materials for our kitchen

We offer you two wonderful master classes in manufacturing panels from natural materials, such as grains, seeds, dried fruits and berries. Such products are easy to manufacture, and most importantly - they look amazing! They can decorate any home, diversify the interior of your home. Such panels look especially harmoniously in the kitchen or in the dining room. Panels made from natural materials In addition, working with the gifts of nature is not difficult at all, you just need to prepare everything you need in advance and create it with pleasure!





Let's make a panel from natural materials for the kitchen

To complete this creative work you will need a set of tools and materials. You will need to buy:

  • Fiberboard or a piece of thick cardboard;
  • tubules for a cocktail;
  • light fabric;
  • glue "Dragon" ;
  • glue gun ;
  • a frame;
  • twine;
  • bronze paint (acrylic);
  • aerosol varnish;
  • various grains, seeds;
  • dried fruits and berries;
  • artificial flowers, spikelets of wheat for decoration.

To begin, prepare the basis for our crafts. We cut out a rectangle of the required size from thick cardboard or fiberboard and paste over it with a light-colored cloth. Take the cocktail tubes and make partitions out of them, thus ‚Äúbreaking‚Äù the rectangle into several squares of different sizes. In the center we make the largest square. Before gluing the tubes with a gun to the base, decorate them with twine. After winding, to give a special gloss, we apply a layer of bronze acrylic paint on the ropes. That's all, now the tubes have acquired a finished look and are ready for attachment to fiberboard. Gently glue them to the base. The basis of the panel of natural materials is ready. After the partitions have dried, you can start decorating the product.

Workshop on the manufacture of panels from natural materials "a cup of coffee"

To make this beautiful and fragrant picture, we need such "consumables"

  • piece of fiberboard;
  • frame (25 x 30 cm);
  • a small piece of fabric;
  • arabica beans;
  • aerosol varnish;
  • cinnamon sticks;
  • glue "Dragon".

First, make the basis - cut a rectangle from a piece of fiberboard and glue the fabric to it. If desired, you can paint the workpiece with acrylic paints. Now we take coffee beans and gently attach with glue to the workpiece in such a way as to make a cup. "Coffee" panel made of natural material: the photo shows an example of making crafts. After the cup is ready, you should leave the product to dry completely at rest.



Along the perimeter of the base, you can also lay out arabica beans, and then decorate the craft with two cinnamon sticks. Such a beautiful and stylish product will not only bring a special charm to the decoration of any kitchen, but also fill it with a delicate aroma. Good luck with your creative quest.
