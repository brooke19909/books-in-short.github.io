Diana Boutique Hotel 4 * (Rhodes, Greece): hotel description, services, reviews

If you want to relax by the sea without leaving Europe, then you probably thought about staying in Greece. This country is famous not only for its beautiful coastline and modern hotels. Far more travelers are interested in historical and cultural attractions.

If we talk about relaxing on the beach of Greece, then the choice of most travelers falls on the island part of the country, namely on Rhodes. After all, this island is shrouded in a mysterious veil of ancient legends. And the service here is at an excellent level. By the way, the Diana Boutique Hotel is considered a good place to spend a vacation. Of course, before ordering a tour, you should familiarize yourself with all available information about the hotel.

Where are the guests to live? Location and its features





Location - an important fact when choosing a hotel, because you want to get to the beach or the store without any problems. Where to find the Diana Boutique Hotel? Rhodes - this is where you can spend your vacation.

By the way, this location is very convenient. For example, within 100 meters there are two public beaches at once. The center from the capital of the island with all its entertainment can be reached in a few minutes. Just 150 meters away is the famous Rhodes casino, and the public transport stop is nearby - you can easily and cheaply travel around the area.

Not far away are the sights of the island, in particular its large ancient port. Many tourists at least once visit the neighboring city of Faliraki, the distance to which is about 13 km. Diagoras Airport is approximately 14 km from the hotel - upon arrival you will only have to quickly move, which will take no more than 15 minutes.

Hotel Diana Boutique Hotel 4 * (Greece, Rhodes): Information about the territory





This is a small hotel complex of urban type. It is located in a quiet area - nearby are other hotels, shops, cafes and bars. Therefore, you should not count on a large yard. The hotel consists of one multi-storey building and practically does not have its own territory, which, however, does not prevent guests from fully enjoying a beach holiday.

By the way, not so long ago the building was completely renovated - today tourists enjoy the cleanliness, elegant atmosphere and the availability of modern equipment. The building has elevators, so you don’t have to go upstairs on foot.

Room amenities: description and photos of rooms





Diana Boutique Hotel is relatively small, but certainly comfortable and cozy. Its number of rooms is represented by 28 rooms - these are double and triple standard rooms, as well as spacious two-room suites.

The premises are quite spacious, with a fresh repair, comfortable furniture and modern appliances. You can use comfortable beds, wardrobes, soft sofas and work desks. As for household appliances, there is a flat-screen TV, a small safe, an electric kettle and a coffee machine. In the summer months, air conditioning can be used, and in cold weather, central heating is switched on. Some rooms have disc players, microwaves and ironing facilities.

The bathroom is small but kept clean. There is a toilet, shower and wash basin. Of course, clean towels are delivered to your room regularly. By the way, maids work well - everything is always neat and tidy in the room.

Meals: can I eat at the hotel?





Catering is certainly an important element of vacation. Paying for a room at the Diana Boutique Hotel 4 *, you can count on daily breakfasts. In reviews, former guests say that the menu here is not bad - there are toasts, cereals, salads and fruits, slices and some hot dishes, as well as tea and coffee.

Breakfast is held in the form of a buffet in the hotel restaurant. It is located on the roof - you can enjoy not only delicious dishes, but also breathtaking views of the city. The terrace with tables, by the way, is very beautiful and resembles an exotic garden - everything is literally immersed in lush greenery.

You can eat in this restaurant not only in the morning, but lunches and dinners are paid separately for each visit, as dishes are prepared to order of visitors. Here you can fully enjoy the Greek and Mediterranean cuisine.

In any case, you should not worry about food, because in the city center there are a lot of cafes, taverns and other institutions that offer delicious dishes - choose something to your taste and enjoy your vacation.

Holidays by the sea: where to look for a beach?





As indicated in the description, the Diana Boutique Hotel 4 * is located just 100 meters from the beaches - a convenient road leads to the shore. You can relax on the popular Psaropula Diana beach or give preference to Elli beach, the distance to which is a little more - 300 m. In any case, you can be sure that you will find clean, soft sand, clear waters and, of course, gentle sun.

Since these are public beaches, sometimes there are many tourists and even residents of the island who also do not mind relaxing on the shore. For a few euros you can get a comfortable sunbed and umbrella to protect from sunlight. In season, the sea is warm and waves rarely rise. The entrance, by the way, is perfectly cleared.

Of course, many tourists prefer a more active pastime - here you can go water skiing, snorkeling, rent motorless swimming vehicles, play volleyball, in a word, have fun and actively spend your holidays.

Should I count on an additional service?

Despite the relatively small size and number of rooms, the Diana Boutique Hotel complex provides its guests with everything necessary for a pleasant and comfortable stay. Very close to the hotel there is free parking. You can rent transport both in the city and directly in the hotel. By the way, bicycles are also rented here - quite a popular transport among tourists.

Guests have the right to use a luggage storage room. The reception is open around the clock, and currency can be exchanged here at a favorable rate. There is a small shop in the building selling small souvenirs and some useful household items.

Leisure tourist: what to do on the hotel?

It’s worth mentioning right away that there aren’t any entertainments at the Diana Boutique Hotel, because most of the guests return to their rooms only in the evening, and in the morning they all go to explore the surroundings again. Here, however, there is a tourist information desk, where they will tell you about the most interesting sights and even offer to buy a sightseeing tour.

Nevertheless, you should not be upset, as there are a lot of entertainment near the hotel, ranging from restaurants and nightclubs to museums and casinos, so no one is bored here.

Hotel Diana Boutique Hotel 4 *: reviews and experiences of tourists





Immediately it is worth saying that the hotel has a good reputation - tourists from all over the world often stop here. Diana Boutique Hotel 4 * is small, but well equipped and comfortable. Lovely and sociable employees work here, who are happy to provide the necessary information and solve problems in an instant. True, they communicate here mainly in English, although some workers understand Russian.

The restaurant feeds great, and the prices for lunch and dinner are not so high. Many guests prefer to spend evenings in the hotel restaurant, sitting on the terrace and watching the night city lights.

The rooms are spacious enough and have everything you need for a comfortable stay and a good night's sleep. The location is very convenient - a few steps connect the hotel to the beach and the entertainment of the city center. Former guests recommend the Diana Boutique Hotel for a relaxing beach or active youth holiday.
