Hotel PrimaSol Hane Garden 5 * (Turkey, Side): room description, service, reviews

Choosing a country for vacation, many tourists prefer Turkey. Here everything favors a pleasant pastime: excellent climate, many unique places to visit and a wide selection of hotels. In Turkey, you can find places to stay with a different level of service. For example, the PrimaSol Hane Garden 5 * hotel provides its guests with very good conditions, where they try to make the guests rest pleasant and unforgettable.





Location

Kumkoy is one of the most popular holiday destinations in Turkey. It is here that the PrimaSol Hane Garden 5 * hotel is located. It is only 60 km from the international airport. Therefore, the arrived tourists are transported to the place of residence for an hour. The hotel is located near the big city of Side. The city center is 8 km away. You can get there by local minibuses, taxis or on foot. Along the road connecting Kumkoy and Side, there are a huge number of restaurants and bars. Tourists can at any time have a bite to eat with delicious dishes or to quench their thirst with soft drinks.





Hotel Description

Many tourists who arrived in PrimaSol Hane Garden 5 * simply fall in love with this place. The hotel is very well maintained, which contributes to a pleasant stay. The complex consists of several buildings, the connection between which is provided by special passages. Hotel PrimaSol Hane Garden 5 * looks amazing not only from the outside. The interior of the building also captivates with beauty and well-groomed. Everything is very spacious here: from a delightful hall to corridors.





It will be pleasant for hotel guests to walk through the very clean and vast territory of the hotel, whose area is 22,000 square meters. m. In large gardens there are many gazebos where you can retire for a romantic date or just sit, hiding from the scorching sun and admiring the local beauties. Comfortable hammocks are also arranged in the hotel gardens for a comfortable stay. There are neat paths everywhere, which allows you to make pleasant walks.

Infrastructure

Tourists are provided with excellent conditions for relaxation at the PrimaSol Hane Garden 5 * hotel. The service here is at a high level, which allows guests to spend their vacations without unnecessary worries. For guests arriving by their own transport, allocate a free parking space. The reception has round-the-clock managers who are ready to solve the guests' problems as soon as possible. Here you can leave things in the storage room for a fee. There is also an exchanger and a tour desk in the lobby.





The hotel has a small shopping center, where customers are provided with products, souvenirs and other goods. There is also a laundry and dry cleaning service, the work of which is additionally paid. The hotel PrimaSol Hane Garden 5 * (Kumkoy) creates excellent conditions for people arriving in Turkey as part of a business trip. Here, for a certain amount, a conference room with the necessary equipment is provided for use: photocopy and fax. There is also an additional charge for consulting or calling a doctor.

PrimaSol Hane Garden 5 *: room description

According to many tourists, one of the main advantages of the hotel are very spacious and beautiful rooms. They are located in the main and additional buildings. Among the 346 rooms offered, one can give preference to double or family options, including two-story ones. The hotel also has rooms designed for people with disabilities. Each room has a balcony overlooking the hotel's gardens and pools or the hotel complexes in the immediate vicinity. There are chairs and a table, which allows you to comfortably accommodate while enjoying the beauty of local landscapes.

The rooms in the PrimaSol Hane Garden 5 * hotel are very beautifully decorated, which creates a feeling of home comfort. There is enough furniture to accommodate all the things taken for relaxation. Individual air conditioning is equipped in all apartments, which allows you to set the optimum temperature for any of the guests. The room also has a TV with Russian channels. There are also minibars filled only with drinking water. The rooms have telephones. Calls at the reception are free. There is a separate fee for international calls. A certain amount will have to be paid for using Wi-Fi.





In the bathrooms there are always personal care products. Here you can take a bath or use the shower. Plumbing is in excellent condition and working properly. All bathrooms include a hairdryer. In the rooms the hotel staff conducts daily and very thorough cleaning. As noted by tourists who visited the PrimaSol Hane Garden 5 * hotel, the rooms are always very clean and there are no insects. Linen and towels are changed daily.

Nutrition

In the hotel PrimaSol Hane Garden 5 * (Kumkoy), guests are provided with all-inclusive meals. You can eat in the main restaurant, where guests are provided with:

  • breakfast;
  • late breakfast;
  • dinner;
  • light snake.

The restaurant hall is very cozy and large, which allows all guests to comfortably accommodate. The menu here is quite diverse. Each guest will be able to choose the right dishes. The restaurant staff is working properly, so there are no queues. The hall always reigns in perfect cleanliness.





Lovers of light snacks and refreshments are welcome in the hotel bars. Here you can also order desserts, pastries, tea or coffee. Only local drinks are provided free of charge, for imported ones you need to pay extra. If desired, hotel guests can have a meal in the restaurants and cafes of the city, which can be found on any street. Here visitors are always welcome. They are served excellent international and local cuisine.

Features of a beach holiday

Hotel PrimaSol Hane Garden 5 * has its own sandy beach, which is just 250 meters away. Here you can enjoy plenty of fun in the warm waves of the Mediterranean Sea or lie down under the rays of the hot sun. The weather in Turkey is often sunny, which allows you to fully enjoy a beach holiday. It is convenient to swim in the sea for both children and adults. It is quite small off the coast, so parents can not worry about their kids. Sun loungers and towels are provided on the beach at an additional cost. There are always enough of them here, so hotel guests can find a comfortable place for themselves at any time.





Sports and other entertainments

Tourists will not have to be bored for a minute at the PrimaSol Hane Garden 5 * hotel. Here you can swim at any time in one of the three outdoor pools. If the weather in Turkey is cool, you can visit the closed bowl, where the water is heated to a comfortable bathing temperature. In the morning, guests are expected to attend aerobics and water aerobics classes. But such lessons are held in German, which is not very convenient for Russian tourists.





The hotel has an excellent fitness center with the latest fitness equipment. Here you can also entertain yourself with darts, mini-golf, basketball, table tennis, volleyball. If finances allow, you can go water sports or play billiards for an additional fee. The hotel has its own beauty salon, all of which are paid for. Here you can visit a hairdresser, take a steam bath, order rejuvenation treatments. Good specialists work in the massage parlor. Their procedures will allow you to feel relaxation and a surge of new strength.

A group of animators will not let guests get bored. Children organize exciting performances and various competitions for guests. But since the entire program is conducted only in German, it may seem boring to Russian tourists.

Conditions for children

As noted by tourists vacationing at the PrimaSol Hane Garden 5 * hotel, excellent conditions for children are created here. The smallest guests are provided with a cot and a special chair in the main restaurant. Young guests can always have fun in the pool with water slides. For them, animators also arrange fun entertainment programs.





A mini club for children copes well with its duties. Here the children are taught various activities, organize funny competitions, introduce new interesting entertainments. The hotel also has a playground, where the little ones will enjoy spending their time. So that the kids do not suffer from the scorching sun, the children's area is protected by a special cover.

Excursions

The hotel's tour desk invites guests to visit many exciting places. Only in the city of Side you can see a large number of interesting attractions. To admire the ancient architecture, it is worth visiting the Aqueduct and the Tuche Temple. An unforgettable experience will be left by the Nymphaeum Fountain, built in honor of water nymphs.





Shopping lovers will also find something to do during their stay at the PrimaSol Hane Garden 5 * hotel. Here you can find a huge number of different shopping centers both in the town of Kumkoy and in the metropolis of Side. There is a huge number of entertainment facilities that will not let young people get bored. In Side clubs, discos cease only in the morning.

PrimaSol Hane Garden 5 *: tourist reviews

People staying at the hotel leave very good reviews. Almost everyone is satisfied with the excellent condition of the rooms, the level of service and beach holidays. Many people like the quality of food and a wide variety of dishes. Also, all vacationers are satisfied with the work of pleasant and good-natured hotel staff who are responsible in fulfilling their duties.





The only thing that can overshadow the rest is the lack of Russian-speaking staff. All entertainment programs are conducted in German, as the hotel is aimed at citizens from Germany. If you analyze all the reviews left from the opening of the hotel until today, then we can conclude that the institution is being improved. Here every year the level of service improves, which allows guests of any age to spend their time in excellent conditions.
