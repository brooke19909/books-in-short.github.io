Guam gorge: hotels and other tourist destinations

Guam Gorge is one of the most famous natural monuments of the Krasnodar Territory. It is located in the Absheron region in the Western Caucasus. It is here that two ranges - Lagonaki and Guam - from a height of almost 400 meters break down towards each other, going down to the valley of the Kurdzhips river. This is a very picturesque place with a length of about five kilometers. Every year, thousands of tourists from all over the world come here to see for themselves the unique Guam Gorge. Hotels, pensions and various recreation centers are warmly welcomed by those who decided to enjoy the beauties of the local land.

Courchevel

The famous gorge in the village of Guamka begins. Further, it stretches with a winding ribbon to a settlement called Mezmay. Where is it better to stay for tourists who come to see the Guam Gorge? Hotels and various leisure complexes are located mainly in the village nearby. Among them, the wonderful Courchevel Guest House deserves special attention.





It is located on Veselaya Street, in the very center of Guamka. The two-story building is equipped with 20 rooms of various categories for one, two, three, four and even eight people. In addition to soft beds, each of them has comfortable furniture, a TV, a split system and a bathroom with a shower. And some rooms overlook the famous Guam Gorge. Hotels and many hotels in this village are built in this way.

Courchevel guests are invited to go horseback riding and horseback riding, as well as ride jeeps in the surroundings. Outdoor enthusiasts can also get climbing lessons from experienced professionals. They are very useful for those who decide to personally visit the Guam Gorge.

Hotels in these places offer tourists only accommodation. Guests feed on their own. For this, for example, Courchevel provides a cozy cafe, a large banquet hall and a grill hall with barbecue. Each visitor can choose an institution to his taste.

Guamka

People even come with whole families to the Guam Gorge. Hotels, hotels and tourist complexes offer visitors excursions to picturesque places with waterfalls, grottoes, caves and impassable canyons. Numerous tourists will surely enjoy the Guamka Hotel of the same name located in the same village.





For guests here are provided comfortable rooms:

  1. Studio. It is designed for two people.
  2. The suite is a two-room apartment with a living room.
  3. Suite. This is a three-room premise with two bathrooms.
  4. 1 categories. The room provides for two people.
  5. 2 categories. The room has two beds and a combined bathroom.

Guests eat in the restaurant. Here visitors are offered a choice of dishes of Caucasian and European cuisine. The hotel also has a sauna, billiards and private parking. Free internet access is available throughout the establishment.

The Big Dipper complex

Every year, more and more people prefer to spend their holidays in the mountains. Many of them are attracted by the unique nature of the Absheron region with relict forests, in which wild animals are still found. For such lovers of pristine nature, a large tourist complex called the Big Dipper was built here in 1998: a hotel, from which the Guam Gorge is located just a five-minute walk, and a camping located nearby.





Given the rare microclimate, this place is considered a real paradise for people with diseases of the heart, nervous system, respiratory tract, as well as suffering from various types of allergies. On the territory of the complex there is a cafe, a bathhouse (Russian and Finnish saunas), a billiards room, a spacious gazebo and barbecue facilities, where the cooks can order fragrant barbecue at the request of customers at any time. In 2013, a thermal pool with a geyser and hydromassage was opened for visitors. Anyone can use this wellness procedure at no extra charge.

Hotel in Ursa Major

In the center of the complex is the hotel "Ursa Major". Guam Gorge is literally 200 meters from it. Visitors who choose camping accommodation are accommodated in small tourist houses. True, all amenities (showers and a toilet) are located in a separate room. The rest of the guests are welcomed by the hotel building. Here for visitors are equipped with rooms of the following categories:

  • standard for 2 and 3 people;
  • Suite (three rooms);
  • premium (for two);
  • mini-standard for 2 people with one bed and an additional sofa bed;
  • family room for four, consisting of one, two or three rooms.

When you check in, slippers, a set of towels and hygiene items (shower gel, soap and shampoo) are sure to be given to residents. All rooms are equipped with a split system. Each of them has a TV and a combined bathroom.





Guests feed on their own. In the rooms for this there is a special zone with the necessary set of dishes. And for those who do not like to cook, there is a buffet and a cafe.
