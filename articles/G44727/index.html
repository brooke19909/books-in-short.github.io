Tuner T2: review and reviews. Tuner for digital television

Now the T2 tuner is widely used. Channels that are broadcast by such a device, as a rule, have high quality of both sound and image.

Such a device necessarily works on one or more standards. The most popular in the Russian Federation are the following: DVB-T (obsolete) and DVB-T2 (modern). This means that this tuner broadcasts European digital television. There are others. For example, the ISDB-T standard provides access to Japanese and some South American channels.

The receivers themselves can be of several types. Now consumers are turning to stores for help in order to find a good digital tuner. Unfortunately, some of them work only with free channels, and in order to recognize paid ones, you will have to look for the appropriate receiver with a special option.

The article discusses special models that have become widespread. They are in demand among consumers and are considered the most optimal in their characteristics. When buying, you need to pay attention to them.
