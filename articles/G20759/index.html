Basic Services

The main service of any hotel is accommodation. "Tourist", which has 3 stars, offers its visitors a long list of rooms, divided into categories. There are 129 comfortable rooms in total, including categories: twin comfort, 17 single comfort rooms, 18 twin standard rooms, 31 single standard and 5 suites.

The second main service is catering. In this case, everything is just fine, because on the second floor of the building there is a modern restaurant with the same name, which is ideal for any special occasion or business meeting. The restaurant seats up to 250 people, and a disco bar an additional 80 more. It should be noted that there are three rooms for 5, 10 and 30 people, and the express bar can meet up to 28 people.





Here, anyone can enjoy the culinary masterpieces prepared by experienced chefs according to traditional Belarusian and European recipes. The restaurant is open every day from 7 am to 2 am. Breakfast here runs from opening until 10 a.m., and you can order a business lunch from noon to 4 p.m.
