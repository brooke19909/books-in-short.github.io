Sights of St. Petersburg for children: Hermitage, contact zoo, aquarium, excursions along rivers and canals

The ancient city, fascinating with its historical monuments and architectural structures, meets more and more tourists every year. Guests from various countries come to St. Petersburg to get acquainted with its rich historical heritage, to admire the beauty of landscapes, to learn something new from the past of the country.





Indeed, the abundance of museums, historical and architectural monuments guarantees adults an interesting pastime, but they do not forget about the little guests. What sights of St. Petersburg for children will be the most attractive?

Royal rest

A walk in the fresh air along the well-maintained streets will not leave anyone indifferent. The Summer Garden of St. Petersburg is an excellent place where both adults and children can relax.





Not so long ago, in 2009, the garden was reconstructed - the first in its bicentennial history. The renewed landscape, spectacular views of the Neva and fragrant landscapes will remain in the memory of visitors for a long time.

The history of the garden

The garden was laid in the second year of the founding of this greatest city at the direction of Peter I. Initially, it was designed according to European traditions of landscaping, there were green labyrinths and flower beds, fountains and gazebos. A symbol of the Dutch succession of traditions has become a pharmacy garden with a variety of types of medicinal plants. At that time, large-scale work was carried out on the breakdown of the territory, the creation of reclamation channels and a fountain system. The summer garden of St. Petersburg was created in several stages, at each of which architects from various countries took part. Marble Venetian sculptures were brought, real masterpieces of art were ordered from such masters as Bonazza, Baratta, Gropelli. At the time of Peter, points and assemblies were held in the garden, senior guests were welcomed here, and great events were celebrated. During the time of Elizabeth, the garden became available to the public, however, only in the absence of the empress.

Water travel

Excursions along the rivers and canals of St. Petersburg are a great way to relax to the sound of a river wave, enjoy the beautiful view of drawbridges and river channels, admire the panoramic view of the embankments and monumental architectural structures.





A sightseeing river excursion will introduce vacationers to the many attractions that are also worth visiting. Palaces, fortresses, castles and cathedrals appear on the background of the water surface in a new, mysterious way. Children will be delighted with the trip by boat or boat, and adults will get a lot of impressions from an unusual walk on the water.

How was the location of the city chosen?

The location of St. Petersburg was chosen by Peter with such a plan as to create on this place a beautiful sea city resembling Venice or Amsterdam. The place was lowland, with a large number of natural channels.





Man-made canals with drawbridges, which are numerous in the city, have become a symbol of the city. Excursions along the rivers and canals of St. Petersburg will allow adult tourists to fully appreciate the magnificence and stunning beauty of the city, and children will be remembered for many years.





Contact with nature

Children's entertainment in St. Petersburg is diverse and interesting. You can choose not only to visit museums or any other sights of St. Petersburg. For children, communication with the animal world in the city zoo will be especially useful and impressive. An interesting fact is that the animals here can not only admire from the outside, but also touch them, feed them and even caress.





How to pamper the smallest?

The contact zoo in St. Petersburg is an island of exotic animals in which every living creature lives in natural conditions for him. It can be visited freely by everyone. There are no cages or enclosed spaces, animals live in freedom, get to know and play with visitors. Not only the variety of individuals and their natural beauty is impressive, it is also interesting to observe their behavior and habits.





Long-eared rabbit or guinea pig will cause a lot of positive emotions in the baby. You can feed a scorpion, a lizard or a bat with your own hands and make a joint original photo for a long memory. Contact Zoo in St. Petersburg is a favorite place for children and adults. The day passes unnoticed, and vivid impressions are guaranteed for years to come!

Dive into the depths of the sea

Sights of St. Petersburg for children are located mainly in the historical part of the city. Also here in 2006 a unique house was founded for animals living in one element. It has become the main feature of the Neptune shopping and entertainment complex. The oceanarium in St. Petersburg enjoys great popularity and attention. It is not difficult to find his address, you just need to get to the metro station Pushkinskaya or Zvenigorodskaya.





A huge area is occupied by 32 aquariums. They are inhabited by a variety of representatives of the water world. Here you can find individuals from different parts of our planet.

What is interesting about the oceanarium?

The division into thematic zones of the entire water area allows visitors to individually review and observe marine mammals and freshwater fish of the North-West, representatives of a coral reef or rocky shore. Interesting explanations will reveal many secrets of the underwater kingdom. More than 4 thousand species of representatives of the sea and freshwater bottom captivate the imagination of each visitor. Here you can see the rarest inhabitants of rivers and lakes, millimeter fish or huge sharks.





Staying in the near-water space of the oceanarium, you can immerse yourself in eternity and not at all feel the movement of time. The leisurely world of corals or the graceful movements of dolphins make you completely forget about the fussiness of the world.





Young travelers will enjoy the water tunnel and the view of the waterfall from below, as well as sharks swimming directly above their heads, and an outdoor pool with stingrays. All these impressions will give the oceanarium in St. Petersburg. His address: st. Marat, 86.

Acquaintance with art

The Hermitage is a grandiose museum that holds many masterpieces of world art. It is better to devote enough time and attention to this landmark of St. Petersburg. Interesting expositions are provided for children, the plot of which is closely intertwined with historical facts.





This will help not only to enjoy the work of skilled craftsmen, but also to delve into the historical past of the country, to learn a lot of valuable and exciting information. The Hermitage for children is a fascinating journey into the past that will enrich their spiritual world and create the prerequisites for future intellectual and cultural development.

Museum Departments

Indeed, a large collection of exhibits is collected in the former royal residence. It is located in six buildings, the beginning of construction of which dates back to the XVIII century. 365 halls with valuable works, more than three million masterpieces and monuments of art - and all this remains to be seen in a minimum short period of time.

Western European art is a department containing many paintings, prints, sculptures located in more than 100 halls. Instances of applied art are a unique sight. Jewelry made of precious metals and stones will amaze not only children, but also adults with its splendor.

The numismatics department is one of the oldest. Even Empress Catherine was addicted to collecting medals and coins, and periodically bought them to supplement her collection. Now it contains more than a million copies. Here you can see antique and eastern coins, as well as European and American monuments of numismatics.
