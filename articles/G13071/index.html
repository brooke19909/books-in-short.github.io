Iron Port, recreation center "Mriya". Description and feedback

"Mriya" is a recreation center in the Iron Port (Ukraine, Kherson region). It is designed both for budget tourists and for people for whom comfort and convenience play an important role in leisure. It is located on the shore, but at the same time very close to the center of the resort village. It is quiet, calm, very green area, clear sea and its own beach. To get to it is not difficult, especially in season. The main thing is to get to the city of Kherson. This is the regional center, where many trains go. In summer, a lot of buses, including private carriers, depart from the railway station along the route Kherson - Iron Port. The recreation center "Mriya", as already mentioned, is located near the center, and therefore go to a stop not far. The location of this guesthouse is extremely successful. Nearby is a bazaar and several shops.



iron Port

The recreation center "Mriya" is located in the resort village with a very interesting name. It is said that there was an iron pier from which fish and grain were loaded onto ships. Now there is no port, but the name remains. This is an ecologically clean area located on the border of the biosphere reserve. Of course, there is a steppe zone, there are no picturesque mountains, but there is a warm sea, healing air, huge sandy beaches that stretch for several kilometers, and large parks of sanatoriums and boarding houses. Today, about three hundred hotels, recreation centers and guest houses are welcomed here, not to mention the private sector. The village is located at the base of the famous Tendra Spit. The depth of the sea is not very large, and it warms up quickly. Although the absence of bays leads to the fact that there are often storms.



Territory, services and accommodation

This recreation center in the Iron Port consists of several buildings with various amenities. Firstly, it is a two-story brick building, where there are rooms with a shower and toilet. Then wooden houses of different comforts. All have a gas stove and refrigerator. Showers and toilets are located on the territory, they are paid (but inexpensive), very decent, on the floor - tiles. Some houses have amenities in the vicinity of the rooms. They are designed for two, three and four people. The furniture is wooden, comfortable, the beds are clean. In front of each house is its own table with chairs, under a canopy. In the territory of this pension there are several more private bases. They offer modern cottages with all amenities and air conditioning. At the recreation center (Iron Port) there is a guarded parking lot, gazebos and barbecue facilities for those who wish to make a barbecue. There is also billiards, there are tables for tennis and a room for computer games. Massage sessions are available. Throughout the territory, including in the houses, it catches "Wi-Fi". There is a good playground. The base area is one of the largest in the village. It occupies two and a half hectares, planted with silver spruces, in the center - flower beds.

Beach

Mriya (recreation center, Iron Port) is the owner of its own coast. It is twenty meters from the houses and cottages. The beach is sandy, with sunshades, deck chairs and sun loungers. There is a bar, changing cabins, as well as water activities - a rental point for jet skis and catamarans. Along the beach is the promenade, which stretches parallel to the village. Here, in the evenings, all vacationers gather and entertainment begins. Many interesting games for children, there is an amusement park, dance floors and restaurants - all on the seashore. But although the base houses are literally a few meters from all these noisy entertainments, nothing is heard on the territory.



Reviews

If you want to take a break from the bustle of the city, but do not live on the very outskirts of the village, go to the Iron Port, to the Mriya recreation center. Tourists write that there is a huge green area, it's nice to relax, the sea is just around the corner. The base staff is very helpful and welcoming. Employees will always repair what breaks, replace a burned out lamp or gas cylinder. Vacationers praise local cafes for good and tasty cuisine. Fish dishes are especially noted. True, residents of budget houses complain that in public showers and toilets may not always be clean, but in the rooms themselves a little old furniture. Often you have to contact the administration due to the shortage of the kitchen - there are not enough cutlery. The walls are thin, especially in the case, and neighbors are audible. If you get noisy, rest can be ruined. But most tourists praise this base for the mass of positive emotions that it delivers to guests.
