How to change the date in Galaxy Y Duos: tips and tricks

Many people use mobile phones from Samsung. But with these devices you need to work correctly. A huge role for the phone is played by settings. Next, we will talk about how to change the date in the Galaxy Y Duos. Often, subscribers with this problem have problems. How to fix them? What are they expressed in? In fact, everyone can cope with the change of date and time on a Samsung mobile phone. This is the simplest operation, which should be known to all owners of modern smartphones.



Date Change Issues

Many people think how to change the date in the Galaxy Y Duos, after the settings on the mobile phone begin to change. Time switches independently, the days in the calendar also do not correspond to reality. In this case, it is necessary to resort to one extremely important technique. We are talking about disabling device synchronization with the Internet. Then the Galaxy Y Duos phone will work fine. And the dates will stop switching over time.

So, to disable the automatic synchronization of date and time settings in Samsung phones, you need to:

  1. Turn on your mobile device. Wait a bit. It is necessary to wait for the device to be ready for further work.
  2. Open the Samsung main menu. Find there "Settings". Click on this button.
  3. Go to the "Time and Date" section.
  4. Click once on the button "Date and time". A small menu will open.
  5. In the window that appears, select the "Off" option.
  6. Save changes. Usually this operation occurs automatically.

Done! Sync disabled. Now you can think about how to change the date in the Galaxy Y Duos. Make it easy!

Change phone settings

The main thing is to know how to act. Changing the time and date on a mobile phone is a standard option. Every person is familiar with it.

How to change date in Galaxy Y Duos? To do this, follow the instructions:

  1. Turn on the phone. Open the main menu of the device.
  2. Click on "Settings" - "Date and Time".
  3. Examine the proposed list. To adjust the date, click on the line "Date". Set the desired day, month and year using the arrow keys. To change the time, click on the line "Time".
  4. Click on "Save."

Nothing more to do. After following the specified instructions, the new changes will take effect. Date and time will be set on Samsung.



The secret to disabling reset

Despite all the previously described actions, some smartphone owners complain that even with the synchronization turned off, the time and date are lost. You can recommend taking the gadget offline, disconnect it from the Internet, and only then change the date and time.

The disadvantage of this technique is that when the device appears on the network, it is still very likely to update both the time and the date. Failed to reply, how to change date in Galaxy Y Duos? Then it is better to take the device to a service center. There they will certainly help to solve this problem.
