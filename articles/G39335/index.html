"Agora"



We will begin to consider the hotels of Kaliningrad (inexpensive, in the center, with breakfast), overlooking a hotel called Agora. The establishment is located at a distance of 3 kilometers from the historical part of the city. You can find the hotel at the address: 175 Moskovsky Avenue.

Guests are offered comfortable cozy rooms, the interior of which is decorated in warm, pleasant colors. Guestrooms have televisions with satellite channels. The rooms have access to free high-speed Internet. The rooms are equipped with a basic set of furniture. Each of them has a separate bathroom. The rental price includes breakfast.

The hotel has a spacious parking for cars with a capacity of 200 seats. There is a conference room for organizing business meetings.

"Lighthouse"

"Voyage"



Considering the hotels of Kaliningrad (inexpensive, in the center, with breakfast), you should pay attention to the Voyage Hotel located at 6. Rassvetnaya Street, located in a quiet area, a 10-minute walk from the central point of the city. A choice of customers is presented here 7 rooms. The hotel has rooms of the class "luxury", "economy" and "standard". Upon request, the staff can bring a delicious breakfast to your room. All rooms contain a TV, a private bathroom, a full set of furniture and are decorated in a romantic style.

Guests can rent a laptop with internet connection at the hotel. For an additional fee, guests are offered ironing facilities, a jogging track, and a washing machine.

Deyma



We continue to survey the hotels in Kaliningrad in the city center. You can inexpensively settle in the Deyma hotel located at Tolstikova street, 15. The distance from here to the central point of the city can be overcome on foot in some 10 minutes.

"Deima" is a nine-story building, which presents more than 100 rooms of various categories of comfort, ranging from economy class rooms to luxury apartments with enhanced amenities.

It has everything you need for a good vacation. The rooms have double beds, wardrobes and bedside tables. There is cable TV, a fridge and a private bathroom in the rooms. The luxury rooms have a jacuzzi.

Academic

"Patriot"



Inexpensive hotels in Kaliningrad in the center are also represented by the Patriot Hotel. It is located 3 kilometers from the historical part of the city on Ozernaya street, house 25.

The hotel has 77 rooms. Each of them has a separate bath or shower. The rooms contain a TV and fridge. The price includes Wi-Fi.

The staff does not serve breakfast. However, there is a cafe on site where guests can enjoy delicious home-cooked meals.

"Golden Bay"

Continuing to consider cheap hotels in the center of Kaliningrad, we will highlight a hotel called Golden Bay, which is located near the historical and business part of the city. You can find it at the address: 53 Khmelnitsky Street.

There are 55 relatively inexpensive rooms with a TV, single beds, a refrigerator and a set of dishes for customers to choose from. Each bathroom in the hotel is designed for use by guests of 2 connecting rooms.

The hotel staff is able to offer customers a delicious breakfast, which is included in the price. Free parking is available on site.
