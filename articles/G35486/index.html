Minor construction and home repairs. Do it yourself concrete vibrator

Owners of private houses often face minor household repairs and construction, in which large volumes of concrete mortars are not a necessity.

Such works as pouring the foundation, plastering the walls, leveling sidewalks and driveways do not always require the use of an expensive and dimensional concrete mixer - a household vibrator is quite suitable for them. You can do it yourself quickly enough, and there is nothing complicated in this process. By the way, with the help of such a simple structure, you can even make paving slabs, cinder blocks and curbs yourself. Of course, the unit for mixing the solution can be bought at the store or rented for a while, but a homemade vibrator will cost much cheaper than the factory one, and this will not affect the quality of the work performed.

What is it for?

The need to use a vibrator is explained by the fact that any concrete solution is quite heavy and dense, which is why air bubbles formed during the mixing process cannot freely go outside. This makes hardened concrete brittle and vulnerable to moisture and heat. In order to release these bubbles, and you need a vibrating device.

Principle of operation

The concrete solution is poured into the desired shape, and the vibrator nozzle is immersed in it, creating vibrations. Under the influence of waves, bubbles rise to the surface. Almost any engine that creates rotational movements is suitable for a vibrator. For example, the nozzle can be connected to an electric drill. In principle, to make a vibrator, with your own hands you need to assemble only a device that converts rotational movements into oscillatory ones. The easiest way to do this is to shift the center of gravity - this will entail the emergence of waves.



How to make a vibrator with your own hands: step by step instructions

1. Nozzle

Make the nozzle better in stainless steel. Concrete mortar in itself is already an aggressive medium, and if it contains additional fractions such as crushed stone or slag, then even more so. A steel tube with a diameter of about 4 cm is suitable. Inside the tube, you need to insert a rod of about 1.5-2 cm in cross section. We weld an additional rectangular to the main rod. This part will shift the center of gravity, due to which vibrations are formed.

2. Assembly

Using bearings, fix the rod inside the tube. It should rotate freely. In one of the protruding ends, it is necessary to make a hole to which the drive will be mounted.

3. Drive

Using the sleeve we connect the metal drive with the nozzle. The second end of the drive is fixed in the holder of the drill or perforator. The tool with the engine turned on can be fixed with a vise in the immediate vicinity of the solution being processed or simply held in hands, guiding the nozzle to the right place.



Where is a concrete vibrator useful ?

With your own hands you can do a lot of work, from minor repairs in the house to concreting large areas. The vibrating device not only facilitates work, but also allows to achieve improved concrete quality. Thanks to this, such devices quickly took their rightful place both in the arsenal of professional construction crews and in workshops of home craftsmen. Moreover, it is not so difficult to assemble a vibrator with your own hands, and all the parts necessary for this can be easily purchased at construction stores or in the market.
