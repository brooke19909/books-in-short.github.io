It starts with a family

On May Day, 1964, a son appeared in the family of Lidia Ivanovna and Nikolai Vasilyevich Evtyukhin named after the great-grandfather-Cossack. Parents were builders, and his father was also a soldier, so the family often changed their place of residence. Born in Yoshkar-Ola, Mark Nikolayevich Yevtyukhin, whose biography will be closely associated with the army, will graduate in Severomorsk. Like his father, Mark all his short life will be devoted to the only woman whom he met in the fourth grade. My classmate's name was Lilya. And although the inscription about feelings for the girl appeared on the desk in the school years, the first kiss and romantic relationship will begin after completion of training. He entered the Ryazan VVDKU, and she went to the pedagogical institute. Their correspondence will be the beginning of the path to a future family life in which they will be happy for 13 years. Having been appointed to the 76th division in the Pskov region, in 1985 the officer will come to the village of Cherekhu with his young wife. And soon the couple will have a daughter, Olga.



Military career

As part of the Pskov Airborne Division, the entire army life of the future Hero of Russia will pass, having served in 1998 as the commander of the second battalion of 104 regiments. For 13 years, he will visit many hot spots, on his account business trips to Kyrgyzstan and Azerbaijan, Armenia and Afghanistan. 9.02. the second battalion under the command of Yevtyukhin advanced in a column towards Dyshne-Vedeno, immediately accepting a baptism of fire. Having been ambushed by a large gang, the paratroopers were not at a loss and, having correctly organized the defense, launched a retaliatory strike, destroying two cars and 30 Mujahideen. Feat of paratroopers

By a terrible coincidence, it was this route towards the 6th company that separatist detachments led by Basayev and Khattab, numbering more than 2 thousand people, were listening to a wave of paratroopers. Stretching for about a kilometer, loaded not only with weapons, but also with the necessary equipment and stoves for potting heights, soldiers and officers had to go to the mountains with a length of more than 10 kilometers. At 12-30, the reconnaissance patrol was the first to encounter the militants, while the paratroopers did not have time to really remove the equipment. There were forty of them, so Mark Nikolayevich Yevtyukhin, keeping calm and self-control, reported to the command of the group that the bandits demanded to let them pass, referring to the agreements between the commanders.

In response, he received an order: ‚ÄúTo capture or destroy!‚Äù No one could have imagined that this would cause bitterness for the militants, who would begin to break through precisely here, where the company did not have time to gain a foothold at the height, and that they would hear all the negotiations, knowing full well that several dozen soldiers were opposing their almost two thousandth group. True, on their slang they were goblins, the most unstable and strong opponents - paratroopers, but they were ready to demolish them in numbers. And they were mistaken. Despite the fact that the company took the battle almost on the march and lost the company in the very first skirmish, the command was assumed by Mark Evtukhin, organizing a real rebuff.

90 soldiers and officers held back militant units for 19 hours, being surrounded on three sides. By morning, the battle turned into hand-to-hand combat, but the lieutenant colonel instilled confidence and encouraged the personnel with the hope that help would come. In fact, only a dozen people (4th company) made it to them at night under the command of Alexander Dostavalov, who were destined to share the fate of the sixth company - to stand until the last soldier. Yes, the artillery supported the fire, which in close combat does not make out where their own, and where strangers. After several nightly attacks, only a few people remained alive by morning. At 6.10 in the morning, the lieutenant colonel got in touch last time. Realizing that the work was done, he reported his coordinates, causing himself to fire artillery.



Evtyukhin Mark Nikolaevich - Hero of Russia

Having sacrificed their lives, the paratroopers thwarted the plans of Khattab and Basayev to leave the encirclement. But they will spend the whole day at a height, pulling off the bodies of hated "goblins", killing seriously wounded and mining corpses. Of the 90 people, six remained alive. These are those whom the officers sent for help, hoping at least to save their lives. And then, so that people learn about the feat of the 6th company. And they found out, despite the silence of the tragedy by the command of the group.
