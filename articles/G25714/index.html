How to use the epilator in order to effectively remove hairs

If you wondered how to use the epilator properly, then you have already become its happy owner. Opinions about the usefulness of such a device are different: someone prefers other methods of removing hairs, considering them safer, but someone still prefers the epilator. After all, there is an opinion that after it the hair grows much longer. Do you like this miracle machine, you can understand after several times of using it.



The hair removal machine is a small electric machine with the action of a regular razor. I will immediately draw your attention to the fact that the procedure with its use is very painful, and you need to get used to it. On the other hand, if you do not feel pain during epilation, this may indicate that the device is of poor quality and does not capture hairs. So to buy an epilator should also be approached responsibly, trusting a trusted manufacturer. It is not necessary to chase expensive models, but the more functional the epilator will be, the better.

How to use the epilator

The procedure does not begin with turning on the machine, as it might seem at first glance. I will say even more, preparations for its implementation are carried out in a few days. Approximately two days should be scrubbing the cultivated areas. You can use a body scrub or a peeling nozzle if it comes with an epilator. Then you need to shave the long hairs, and if they are very long, then first cut off with scissors, and only then shave. Now you need to wait a couple of days, then you can start the procedure.



It is best if epilation is done in the evening. So your irritated skin can get rid of redness during the night and will not be exposed to ultraviolet rays.

The evening option is also good because you can safely take a bath, which is also a prerequisite, because with its help the skin will steam up and the hairs will be removed more efficiently.

To know how to use the epilator correctly, you should consider the capabilities of your model of device. The fact is that there are more expensive ones that can be successfully used while taking a bath, under water. A great option, especially for women with a high pain threshold.
