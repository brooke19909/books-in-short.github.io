Rest in Andreevka: reviews of tourists

Andreevka is located on the shore of Trinity Bay of Posyet Bay, in the wide mouth of the river of the same name.

The main employment of local residents is the service of tourists and travelers, sometimes giving the only opportunity to earn. In summer, the entire coast of the bay is dotted with tents. Locals rent summer houses, as well as rooms in private houses. Holidays in Andreevka are represented by hiking and horseback riding, scuba diving, fishing, jet skiing and pleasure boats.

Seaside Riviera



The village is surrounded by unique beauty landscapes and vegetation, characteristic of different climatic zones. Many rare flora representatives grow here, animals and birds from the Red Book live.

The pristine beauty of these places excites the imagination. In fact, such an unusual combination of northern and southern types of vegetation is rarely seen, and walks to interesting places here provide an excellent opportunity to learn something new about the researchers who discovered Primorye and the legendary past and present of the region.





In Andreevka, there are small recreation centers and camp sites in considerable numbers. The sphere of services necessary for travelers is also quite widely represented here: taxi, rental of tourist equipment, interesting excursion programs.
Among the vacation spots of Andreevka it is worth noting such camp sites as Trinity Bay, Ocean, Zaimka.

Holidays in Andreevka are not only beautiful sandy beaches, exploring the wonderful surroundings, but also fishing, as well as plenty of entertainment for extreme sports, adrenaline lovers and all kinds of adventures. A diving school operates in the territory of the village, rental of ATVs, rowing and motor boats, as well as other tourist equipment are available.

Tourist base "Ocean"



The recreation center "Ocean" in Primorye (Andreevka) is located in the southern part of the region in the famous Trinity Bay. The beauty of these places was admired by the people who first discovered them. A warm climate, an abundance of rare species of fauna and flora - all this is a consequence of the course of the Tsushima Strait, which carries warm waters.

Accommodation and entertainment

Guests can stay in rooms of various classes: from economical to superior comfort, or in small cottages of a summer type. There is a cafe, a restaurant and a dining room on the territory of the base , and vacationers also have the opportunity to cook their own meals.

Chalet "Ocean" will offer its guests participation in sea walks and rich excursion programs, boat rides, rowing or motor boats, a catamaran and water skiing. The camp site has a water park with slides and other attractions. Incredibly saturated with positive emotions can be a vacation in Primorye (Andreevka). "Ocean" is a camp site that deserves a lot of positive comments.



Reviews

Tourist base "Paradise"

The recreation center "Paradise" in Andreevka has comfortable hotel rooms. For lovers of outdoor activities there is a wide selection of entertainment.

It is possible to organize weddings, parties, anniversaries and other celebrations.
