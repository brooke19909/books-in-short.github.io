Wanbo Hotel 4 * (ex.Wanbo Club), China: description, service, reviews

China is a country that impresses with its culture and history. On the territory of this state there is a huge number of historical monuments that are of interest to tourists from around the world. There is also beautiful nature, an impressive sight of travelers. That is why tourist hotels in China are in great demand, especially in the midst of the holiday period.

One of the most popular resort cities in China is Sanya. Wanbo Hotel 4 * (ex. Wanbo Club) is a modern hotel that offers tourists first-class service and the best living conditions that will not leave anyone indifferent.



general information

The date of construction of the hotel is considered to be 2003, but at that time it had a different name - Wanbo Club. After some time, after another reconstruction, it changed its name and is now called Wanbo Hotel 4 *.

The description of this place often says that it provides excellent conditions for recreation for the whole family. In particular, this is due to the fact that there are rooms that are specifically designed for this, as well as some conditions for the safety of the smallest guests.



Location

Wanbo Hotel 4 * (ex. Wanbo Club) is located on the shore of Sanya Bay, from where it is only 15 minutes by car to the central part of the city. If we consider the proximity of the hotel to the airport, then the distance to it is small - only 9 kilometers, which can be overcome in literally 10 minutes.

This vacation spot is located in the middle of nature, on the sandy beach, which is the property of the hotel.

Rooms

For guests at Wanbo Hotel 4 * (ex. Wanbo Club) there is a luxurious room fund, which presents 106 apartments, divided into three categories: "standard", "deluxe" and "suite". Each room has all the conditions that are quite comfortable with tourists. Often, in their comments left about this place, they talk about the interior of the rooms, it is often said that the apartments are always clean and comfortable, which allows you to relax and enjoy your vacation in full.

The rooms have a full set of plumbing, which is located in the bathroom. Among them are presented sinks, bathrooms, as well as showers. More expensive apartments have showers with bathtubs. In such a room there are two cabinets in which bath accessories are stored, designed for each guest.

The rooms of Wanbo Hotel 4 * (Hainan) are equipped with air conditioning, which are managed by the administration. With their help, temperature conditions are perfectly regulated. Often guests write in reviews that thanks to such a system they can feel quite comfortable here in any weather.

"Standard"

The rooms of this group consist of one room, they are designed to occupy a couple of guests. Tourists celebrate their beautiful interior, made in the classic European style.

Here, the main place is occupied by a large double bed with orthopedic mattress. She is covered with expensive hypoallergenic linen. For guests' belongings there is a pair of cabinets, the surface of one of which serves as a table for installing a TV. The Wanbo Hotel 4 * (ex. Wanbo Club) has satellite TV, so tourists can watch their favorite TV shows in Russian, even when they are far outside their home country.

Guests note the excellent view that opens from the windows of the room - from here you can watch the sea open spaces and colorful gardens.

"Suite"

The rooms of the "suite" group offer tourists an improved environment, which is presented on a fairly large area, divided into two rooms: a living area and a place to relax.

Guests celebrate the decoration of the bedroom, which is available here. They are especially impressed by the dim lighting that is created here with the help of wall-mounted appliances. Such a picture, they say, gives the atmosphere a touch of intimacy. In the bedroom, in addition to the bed, there is a small dressing table and a rather large mirror. From here you can go to the outdoor terrace, which is adjacent to each suite.

In the living area there is a great and very comfortable place to relax and watch your favorite TV shows, which are also available here. In the center of the room there is a large folding sofa and a table. On the walls you can see paintings that symbolize the nature of China and its historical features.

For business

Service Wanbo Hotel 4 * provides for a number of conditions designed for successful business in the hotel. First of all, tourists for whom such features of a vacation spot are important pay attention to the presence of a couple of excellent conference rooms here. One of them is designed for 60 people, and the second - for 80. Each of the halls has the required number of seats and tables. Stationery is also provided to all guests.

As for the equipment that is available here, it is modern and works fine. Thanks to the presence of light and sound equipment, any event can be made more vivid and presentable.

Often these venues are used for presentations, business negotiations and meetings with business partners.



SPA

Tourists give excellent reviews to the spa zone, which is in the hotel. They talk about the fact that only professionals in their field work in this place, many of whom know the secret techniques of massage and skin care. They are very pleased to share during the procedures. On its area there are three pools, which are filled with waters from thermal springs with healing properties. There is also a jacuzzi. Guests can visit the large massage room, where you can also make a number of beauty treatments. There are reviews on the Web that say that you can buy a ticket to China at least to visit this beautiful place.

Sport

Lovers of an active lifestyle, even while resting, pay great attention to their figure and general health. If desired, any guest can visit the gym, where you can train on special units or using any other means.



In the open territory of the hotel there is a beautiful aquazone, the pool in which is quite large in size - 1000 sq.m., its depth reaches 1.6 m. Around the niche there is a sunny terrace actively visited by tourists, where a small number of large sun loungers and beach umbrellas are located. Here, for free, vacationers can lie down and take sun baths after water procedures.

On the territory near the building there are several sports fields where you can compete in team games. There is also a golf course.



Nutrition

In Wanbo Hotel 4 * there are several catering places serving traditional Chinese cuisine. Tourists celebrate their special flavor and, of course, taste. Reviews of Wanbo Hotel 4 * often refer to the talented work of chefs who know all the features of oriental cuisine and are happy to introduce them to guests.



Tourists who live in the hotel eat on an all-inclusive basis every day, visiting the buffet, organized in the main restaurant, which serves a lot of diet dishes. There are also two bars here, one of which places great emphasis on grilled dishes, while the other offers light snacks and a huge amount of drinks.



Beach

Wanbo Hotel 4 * has its own beach, which is literally a hundred meters from the hotel, across the road. It is completely covered with soft and very fine sand, staying on which is safe for small children. The water in the sea here is clean and in the midst of the spa period is very warm, as the depth is shallow.

Throughout the area there are places to relax in the form of sun loungers. If necessary, guests can rent large towels or air mattresses. A large umbrella is installed above each sun lounger, which, with excessive solar activity, saves vacationers from direct sunlight.

On the beach, Wanbo Hotel 4 * has a lot of entertainment, many of which are extreme.



Service

The hotel provides a number of services that make living in it more comfortable. In particular, three times a week, maintenance staff clean the apartments, leaving behind perfectly clean rooms.

At Wanbo Hotel 4 * (Sanya), you can always rent a bicycle or car - this is a must-have for tourists who are sure to recommend those travelers who prefer to explore local attractions and surroundings during their holidays. By the way, in case of renting a vehicle, the hotel administration offers a free parking space in an open guarded parking lot.

During the holiday period, a team of cheerful animators works in this place for relaxation, who will not let vacationers get bored with their programs. Many of them, as tourists say, are fluent in Russian, which helps guests from Russia and the CIS to communicate with them.

Prices

The cost of a trip to China is about 85,000 rubles, provided that two people rest on it. The package without fail includes the cost of two round-trip airplane tickets, as well as room rental. At the request of the client, it may include a transfer from the destination to the destination.

According to tourists, such a price for a trip to the country of ancient Eastern culture is quite acceptable, especially since guests get the opportunity to live in the best conditions, where everyone is surrounded by excellent service. Tourists recommend visiting this place to all their friends and relatives, sharing their impressions and showing photos taken during their vacation in China.
