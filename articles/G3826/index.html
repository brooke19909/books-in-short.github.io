Types and working hours of trading sessions

When making money in the financial markets, as in any other business, it is imperative to take into account a variety of external factors in order to understand how to trade Forex correctly. The trader devotes the greatest time to developing and testing the trading technique with which he plans to earn in the future. In his strategy, he takes into account a variety of parameters ranging from market phases (consolidation, trend, accumulation, impulse movement, flat) and ending with the time period for which his technique is created.

All traders know that depending on the market condition, a certain strategy is chosen, which necessarily takes into account in which trading session the market is currently located. The greatest movement, according to traders, occurs during the London and American sessions.

Opening hours of trading sessions (Moscow time +3 GTM):

  1. Asia - from 03:00 - 11:00.
  2. Europe - from 09:00 - 17:00.
  3. America - from 16:00 - 24:00.
  4. Oceania - from 01:00 - 09:00.

This is a winter time schedule, for the summer period you need to add plus 1 hour. As you can see from the schedule on the "Forex" there are 4 trading sessions.

Features of trading periods

The beginning of each trading day in the financial market is the Asian session. It is she who opens the auction. Asia is characterized by reduced volatility and the market is at that time in a calm state. During the Asian session, there are never strong impulses or big trends. During this period of time, the movement in many currencies is quite predictable, they are traded in the lateral range, thus forming a flat.

Europe and America are the main engines of the market. It is during the European session that there is a strong movement and big trends, often which go into the long term. If the trader has determined the right direction of quotes, then he can get the maximum daily profit (for the short-term) and further increase in profit in the long term.

The trading day ends with an Ocean session, which is also characterized by low volatility and predictability of market movements.

Session Indicators

Quite often, traders are so keen on trading that they forget to control sessions. In trading, the opening and closing of a period is a very important parameter, since many techniques are designed for certain market phases. Experts, developers, or simply experienced traders specially create tools that allow you to remember about trading sessions. And that means, in time to open or fix positions.

Most often, trading session indicators for MT4 are created, as MetaTrader is one of the most popular trading platforms. They may look different:

  • in the form of a table directly on the chart;
  • as a period separator (vertical lines);
  • like certain rectangular or square areas and other options.

Such indicators can be displayed not only on the chart, but also in a separate window, at the bottom of the trading platform. The article below will present a list of the most popular trading session indicators for MT4.

Tool List:

  1. Avto Sessions indicator - is displayed on the chart in the form of rectangular multi-colored areas, at the top of which the number of points passed by the price during the session is indicated.
  2. Forex Sessions indicator - located below the chart in a separate window and presented as dotted multi-colored lines, where each color reflects a specific trading period.
  3. The i ‚Äî Sessions indicator is located directly on the trading chart and highlights the time of sessions with colorful rectangles or squares.
  4. JF Tradinkg Times indicator - this tool divides the beginning and end of each trading session with vertical lines.
  5. Market Taime Indicator - its location on the chart in a separate information window. It is very convenient, practically does not take up space, and can be reflected in different colors, which the trader likes.

All these indicators of trading sessions for MT4 do not require any special settings. The only thing that can be changed, at the discretion of the trader, is this display color or time zone, if it does not match.
