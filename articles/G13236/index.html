How to disable Easy Payment (MTS)? Step-by-step instruction

People in the modern world are trying to save their time. They are looking for any ways to make their life easier - they use online shopping, communicate on the network, and even work through the Internet. Using a mobile phone, some operators can pay for certain services. For example, for MTS this option is called "Easy payment". But sometimes it needs to be abandoned. This article will talk about how to turn off Easy Payment. MTS offers many diverse solutions. The subscriber can choose how to act. What should everyone know about the process under study?

Description

What is Easy Payment? This option allows you to quickly work with finances using a special service and an Internet connection. MTS "Easy Payment" from phone to card allows you to transfer funds and back. In addition, this service is designed to pay utility bills.



In addition, Easy Payment is used for:

  • repayment of loans on "credit cards";
  • replenishment of the balance of a mobile phone (any);
  • Internet and TV payments;
  • the acquisition of goods in online stores.

The main advantage of using the option is that usually there is no commission for using it. And if it is, then not too big. Nevertheless, sometimes you need to think about how to turn off the Easy Payment (MTS). Before doing this, you need to connect the option.



About use

You can use the service in different ways. Today it is allowed to act as follows:

  1. Activate Easy Payment via USSD request. You need to dial * 115 #, and then click on the "Call" button. Next, in the functional menu, the service for payment is selected. The last step is confirmation of payment. MTS Easy Payment offers to transfer money from phone to phone by selecting the appropriate command in the menu and sending SMS to number 6996. You do not need to write anything in the message body. This command confirms the payment of any selected service.
  2. Use a short request to enable Easy Payment. To do this, you need to dial * 111 * 656 # on your mobile device.
  3. Download a specialized application called Easy Payment. After installing this software, activation of the option is not needed.
  4. Use "My Account". With it, you can connect the studied option without much difficulty.

The latest development scenario is to ask employees at MTS offices to make a connection. A few minutes of waiting will solve the problem. Voice menu

How to disable Easy Payment? MTS offers to use a special voice menu to translate ideas into reality. Activate the service is allowed at any time.

To disable Easy Payment using the voice menu, you need to:

  1. Dial 0890 on your mobile phone or tablet.
  2. Click on the Call button.
  3. Click on "0".
  4. Follow the instructions of the robot voice.

After using the method, the subscriber will receive a message on the phone about the successful deactivation of the service. But this is only one of the options for rejecting Easy Payment. There are other approaches to solving the problem.

USSD request

The next method is to use the USSD command. All actions are reduced to sending a short request for processing. To do this, you have to use a special combination and ring it.

How to disable Easy Payment (MTS)? To do this, you must:

  1. Dial * 111 * 1 # on the mobile device / tablet.
  2. In the menu that appears, select the "Disable" option.
  3. Send a request for processing and wait for a response from the operator.

Quick, easy, easy. After this operation, MTS Easy Payment will not transfer money from phone to phone. And all the options provided by the studied opportunity will be disabled. To reuse them, you will have to activate Easy Payment again.
