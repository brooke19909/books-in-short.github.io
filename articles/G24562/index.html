Pin-up photoshoot: examples and ideas

Why has pin-up gained popularity again? The answer is simple: women miss beautiful, elegant clothes, and sometimes they want to feel like carefree coquettes or fatal seductresses. Moreover, the aesthetics of the pin-up does not put forward strict requirements for the figure or age. Not only external data is important, but also the ability to present oneself. Shooting preparation

To make the pin-up photo session perfect, the model will have to work hard on her appearance. The hair should be smooth, laid in large curls or curls. Bandages with polka dots, bows, flowers will successfully complement the hairstyle.



Pin-up makeup also has its own mandatory attributes. They are black eyeliner and red lipstick, which is well suited to the skin tone of the model. Without them, the image will not fully correspond to the canons of the genre, and you won‚Äôt get the perfect pin-up photo shoot. Examples of makeup can be seen in the photo.



We must not forget about manicure, especially if pictures are planned where hands will be presented in close-up. It is also desirable to coat nails with red varnish.



Facial expressions

Depending on what direction your pin-up photo shoot has, you will have to work differently with emotions and facial expressions. They can be divided into 3 groups:

  • Seductive and sexy - try to express emotions with a look, not just poses. Of the attributes, stockings and erotic lingerie are simply irreplaceable.
  • Perky and sweet - laugh more and smile. Playful and funny songs will help you to get in the right mood and relax, then emotions will be sincere, and the pictures will be full of optimism.
  • Glamorous and luxurious. For this image, facial expressions should be restrained, imagine yourself a real lady who rarely shows emotions. The views, royal posture and graceful turn of the neck will help to convey them.


Avoid a surprised face. Even if you trained in front of the mirror, in the photo it does not always look successful.

Posing

In order to be inspired and find good ideas for shooting, leaf through not drawn pictures, but frames with real models. Because reproducing the artist‚Äôs work is much more difficult than copying a real person. If this is your first pin-up photo shoot, then try to choose simple and natural poses.

For example, here are a few options that are easy to repeat.

  • The model lies on its back, legs are raised and crossed, the toe reaches for the ceiling. Hands can be thrown behind the head, folded on the stomach or raised on the elbows. So you emphasize the beauty of the chest and the length of the legs.
  • The model lies on the stomach, legs are raised and crossed. So you can hide the tummy, and the bust is presented in the best possible way.
  • S-shaped posture: the model sits on her knees, but not completely: there is no need to press the hips to the calves so that they do not flatten. If you draw in your stomach and slightly push out your chest, the silhouette will become even more seductive.
