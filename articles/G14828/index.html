The best resort cities of Ukraine

Ukraine may not be so popular with foreign tourists as Turkey, Bulgaria or Croatia, but there is also something to see here. Beautiful landscapes, a huge variety of landscapes, interesting sights, unique natural, historical and architectural monuments - all this awaits curious travelers. It is difficult to describe all the resort cities of Ukraine, because there are actually a lot of them, but the most interesting to visit can be distinguished.



Despite the fact that the Carpathians and Crimea enjoy the greatest popularity among tourists, in Ukraine there are still many places that are in no way inferior to them in their beauty and uniqueness. If you want to spend a few days away from civilization and, moreover, improve your health, you should visit Mirgorod sanatoriums. In Vinnitsa region, many interesting sights are concentrated, and the beauty of the local landscapes will amaze everyone.

The resort cities of Ukraine at sea are one of the most visited places in the country. The state washes two seas: Azov and Black. Berdyansk can be distinguished near the first, but still the main resorts are located on the Black Sea coast. One cannot but note the beautiful Crimean peninsula, which is not only remarkable for its amazing nature, mountains, forests, caves, but also for its cultural and architectural heritage. To this day, archaeological work is underway here, revealing new details of the life of the indigenous population.





The resort cities of Ukraine, located on the peninsula, annually receive more than one million tourists, many of whom are foreigners. Sea air, mountain routes, man-made sights, a huge number of nature reserves and reserves, excellent places for diving and fishing - all this, of course, attracts travelers, making their vacation unforgettable. A map of resort cities of Ukraine will help to make a choice and visit one of the healing places of this hospitable country.

Absolutely all vacationers are still impressed by a visit to Odessa. Other resort cities of Ukraine cannot compete with the capital of humor in brightness, uniqueness and originality. It is difficult in words to convey the atmosphere prevailing here. A huge number of architectural and historical sights, comical manners and talk of local residents, beautiful landscapes - all this cannot be left without the attention of tourists.





On both sides of the Carpathians there are ski resort towns of Ukraine with their unique ski slopes of different lengths and complexity. Bukovel, Dragobrat, Tysovets, Slavske are happy to welcome guests. If there is a need to heal, then you should go to Truskavets, Yaremcha, Morshin and other places with healing mineral springs. To plunge into the history and culture of the people, it is worth visiting Zaporizhia, and more specifically the island of Khortytsya, where the Zaporizhzhya Sich was founded . It is impossible to list all the interesting places in Ukraine, because each city is attractive in its own way. Tourists who decide to go on a trip to this country will never regret their decision.
