History of Primorsko

The country of Bulgaria has a very ancient history. Primorsko is no exception. Not far from the city is Masleniy cape. More recently, scientists have found here an old solar calendar, which was supposedly made by the Thracians Beglik-Tash. They worshiped the sun. This calendar consists of two stone circles with holes into which the sun's rays penetrated on the days of the solstice and equinox. Archaeologists still argue about the age of this find. Some believe that the calendar was made in the 14th century BC. Other scholars disagree with this figure. But the fact that the calendar is at least 7000 years old is clear to everyone.

Also, archaeologists found household items, thanks to which it became clear that there was a connection between the present Primorsko, Greece and Crete. Scientists have discovered the remains of the ancient city of Ranule, which was a large port settlement.

During the existence of the Ottoman Empire, the village of Chengen was located in the resort. Later she was given the name Kyupriya, and then - her real name. Previously, no one could get into the resort, because it was in this area that the residence of the Secretary General of the Communist Party of Bulgaria Todor Zhivkov was located. It was called "Pearl." Now any tourist can come to the city.

Ecology of the resort area

Primorsko has very clean mountain air, which is why it is not in vain that people come here not only to swim in the waters of the Black Sea, but also to improve their health. This resort is visited with children very often. Indeed, in many places where we live permanently, so often there is not enough oxygen, the air is saturated with the exhaust gases of cars.

The reserve, which is located in the Primorsko area, is called "Ropotamo". It attracts tourists by the presence of unique rock formations on its territory - the Snake House, as well as the Lion's Head.

Also, guests who were attracted to Bulgaria, Primorsko can please the beauty of two water streams: the Devil's River and Ropotamo flowing near it. Guests of the resort really like them. On the surface of Ropotamo you can see rare water lilies that have been listed in the Red Book and are protected by law. This river flows into the Black Sea, which has a very clean coast. For this achievement, the resort received a special Blue Flag award back in 1994. Since then, in spite of the past years, the sea strip has remained clean.



Climate in Primorsko

The climate in the resort is warm, there is no heat, and a light breeze blows from the sea. The average air temperature in July is about 27 degrees Celsius, but sometimes it rises to 33. The weather in the city is mostly sunny, so you can sunbathe with pleasure, and warm days last from April to September.

Thanks to the mountain air, even for a week of vacation, a vacation in Primorsko will bring health benefits . Bulgaria offers to combine a pleasant pastime with healthy air and sea baths. Adaptation is quite easy, so very often children relax here with their parents. Everyone remains completely satisfied with the days spent in Primorsko.

Even if you arrive in the city in winter, the weather will not disappoint anyone. The climate is mild and temperate. Precipitation here is small, and the air is dry. In winter, the city is not cold. The temperature, even in January, most often exceeds the 0 degree mark on the thermometer and rarely reaches an indicator lower than -1. But the amount of precipitation is replenished precisely in the winter period. Most often it is rain or wet snow. In general, winters are mild and not frosty.
