How to pay for a patent through Sberbank: step-by-step instructions

Many foreigners come to Russia not only for tourism purposes, but also for work at various enterprises. Such people must go through the procedure for obtaining a patent, without which official employment is impossible. In addition, without this, a person will not receive the required benefits and social support. How to pay for a patent is described in the article.

Patent Information

Patents are provided by the migration service. Multifunctional centers also work now, thanks to which you can draw up documents quickly and conveniently. After receiving this paper, a person acquires a work permit. Such citizens have the right to a job as other people.





But paper is not issued to all foreigners. If he earlier issued a tourist visa, and then wanted to stay for work, then he would not be given a patent. It is important that the purpose of the trip is work. Difficulties with payment arise not only because it needs to be bought, but more so because deductions should be made every month or after 3 months. And in a year re-registration is required. Because of this, not everyone decides to go to work. But if you are interested in how to pay for a patent, you can contact Sberbank, where employees will tell you about the rules for transferring money.

Available options

How to pay for a patent? Such services are offered at Sberbank. Moreover, it has several payment methods:

  1. Sberbank Online.
  2. ATM.
  3. Bank branch.




The latter option is considered the simplest, because you only need to have the details and money. And all the work is done by the operator. But when choosing this method, you will have to pay a commission. Therefore, it is more profitable to independently learn to perform this procedure.

To pay for services using Sberbank Online, you need to connect this service. Such a transfer saves a lot of time, since payment is made at home. To activate the service, you need to visit a branch of Sberbank, where through the terminal, with the help of an employee, perform the procedure. This is convenient to do when issuing a card.

Payment through Sberbank Online

How to pay for a patent through Sberbank? Just log in to your account. The commission will be small, but it may be absent. Access to your personal account is available only to bank card users. Although it should not be executed for the sake of paying for a patent, a salary may well come in handy.





So, you need to get a card, connect a mobile bank, a login and password are printed out through an ATM. Then you need to perform the following steps:

  1. Go to the official website, enter the username and password (they can be replaced with new ones). It is required to confirm the entrance to your personal account. To do this, a message arrives on the phone with a code that must be entered in the window that appears.
  2. After that, the main page will open. There you need to select the section of payments and transfers.
  3. At the bottom of the page that appears, click on the fines and taxes payment section. Then you need to choose the Federal Tax Service. Please note that payments are transferred to a specific branch. For example, a person was issued a document for work in the Moscow region, so he must make a payment to this unit.
  4. A window appears where payment will be made.
  5. The payment form will be opened. You must select the desired region. Then you need to continue the procedure.
  6. Then you need to enter information about the payer. It is also important to choose a card with which funds will be charged. Only a debit card can be used.
  7. It is necessary to enter information by which the Federal Tax Service will determine the payer. This is the TIN.

Before performing the procedure, you need to check the information, then confirm it. The phone will receive a message with a code. After payment, a check will be provided, which must be printed. This paper will be the same confirmation as that provided by the bank. If the Federal Tax Service requests a seal, then it can be done in any department.

Template

How to pay an IP patent at Sberbank quickly? You can create a template so that the following procedures are faster. Details and data will be filled. You only need to enter the required amount and tax period.





You can connect auto payment. Using this service, on the appointed day, the required amount will be transferred to the Federal Tax Service at the indicated details. Such payments will arrive on time.

Payment via ATM

How to pay for a patent through Sberbank? Step-by-step instructions will allow you to perform the procedure using an ATM. You only need to have a debit card and details. If you use the terminal, then a commission will be charged.

The procedure is as follows:

  1. The card must be inserted into the device, enter the PIN code. There you should choose the language in which it is more convenient to perform the procedure.
  2. After that, the general menu will be opened. You need to select the payment section in your region.
  3. Then you need to go to the search for the payee. This can be done using the barcode, name or TIN number of the Federal Tax Service. More often the third option is chosen.
  4. If the data are entered correctly, you must confirm them.
  5. The system will indicate the name of the recipient and payment options.
  6. It is necessary to confirm the operation by checking the information.
  7. At the end, you need to specify the amount. The system will calculate the commission, the total amount of payment.
  8. Money will be debited from the card, and a receipt will be provided to the customer.

How to pay for a patent through the terminal. The algorithm for working with him is the same, only in the end you need to make cash. This completes the payment procedure.





Payments for the patent must be made in a timely manner, since due to a delay of even 1 day it can be canceled. After that, you will need to re-submit the documentation for the job, as the old paper is not restored.

Thus, payment of a patent is easy. You can choose any convenient option. If there are any difficulties, then you can always contact the specialists of the bank. They will always tell you how to pay for a patent. Timely transfer of funds will not lose the right to official work.
