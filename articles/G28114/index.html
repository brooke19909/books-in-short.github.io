Hotel Pasabey Hotel 4 * (Marmaris, Turkey): overview, description and reviews of tourists

Pasabey Hotel 4 * is a great place to relax, recommended to many tourists from Russia and neighboring countries. A huge number of operators recommends going for a vacation here, as in this place high service is ideally combined with relatively inexpensive accommodation prices.

general information

This place to stay is relatively new - the hotel opened its doors for tourists in 2011. Since then, every year there are several hundred vacationers who prefer an exotic nature. The hotel consists of two buildings, in which the entire room fund is located.

According to vacationers, it is ideal for youth recreation. At present, its area is relatively small - it is almost 3.5 thousand square meters. m



Location

Pasabey Hotel 4 * is located in Marmaris, in the very central part of the city. Here you can meet a huge number of entertainment infrastructure facilities that guests love to visit. The nearest beach is located literally 500 meters from its main building, which is often not perceived positively by tourists.

The nearest airport is 90 kilometers from the hotel in Antalya. Due to such a far distance, payment for the transfer service provided by the hotel is mandatory included in each ticket.

Rooms

All the apartments available in Pasabey Hotel 4 * (Turkey / Marmaris) are concentrated in its two large three-story buildings, which are located next to each other. In total there are 134 of them, they are not divided into categories and are presented in one group - the standard.

All apartments have modern climate control equipment, with its help you can cool the entire room to the desired temperature. The rooms are also equipped with bathrooms, combined with bathrooms. They include bath amenities provided by the hotel, a set of bathrobes and towels, and a pair of slippers for each guest in the room.



Interior

Inside each apartment, as stated in the reviews of Pasabey Hotel 4 *, everything is very comfortable, here you can relax on a hot day, and go to the sea in the evening. Guests often pay attention to the harmonious combination of white and gray colors in which the interior is made.

The central place in furniture is a large double bed. In separate rooms it is replaced by two single beds. If necessary, a folding bed of a European type can be installed in any room. According to tourists, it is quite comfortable, but its installation causes some discomfort, as the room area is small - only 19 square meters. m

The room also has a small armchair and a coffee table - this headset is installed near a large window, covered with thick textile. The room has a table on which vacationers can put some of their belongings, as well as a huge mirror opposite the bed, which visually expands the available space. Opposite the sleeping place on the wall is a plasma TV connected to a satellite broadcasting system. Vacationers note the pleasant fact that among the channels there are many Russian speakers.

If desired, guests can use the safe, minibar and telephone connected to the international line. However, the use of these facilities is paid and is charged daily. As for the minibar, it is filled once - at the time of arrival of guests in the room.

Sports and spa

Fans of relaxation often spend their time in the spa, which has several massage tables, which are attended by professional masseurs who can immerse any client in a state of complete relaxation. If desired, guests can simultaneously use the cosmetic services provided by cosmetologists. For business

Very often, Pasabey Hotel 4 * guests are tourists who come on a business trip. In connection with the purpose of their trip, they often need a place where they can hold a business meeting with their business partners, make a presentation or other events. For such purposes, the main building of the hotel has a small conference room, which has sound, lighting and video equipment that can make any event more vivid. The audience is also equipped with office equipment, laptops and projectors. If necessary, stationery can be provided to those present.

Hall rental is done hourly and is paid.



Nutrition

Often guests of the Pasabey Hotel 4 * (Marmaris) tell in their reviews about the hotel that tourists are offered excellent food as part of a buffet organized in the main restaurant. It serves delicious and colorful dishes prepared by talented chefs. Vacationers notice that in the list the predominant place is occupied by dishes typical of Turkish national cuisine, there are also several European and Mediterranean dishes.

Many travelers recommend visiting the local bars, of which the hotel has only two. They have a huge selection of drinks, cocktails, as well as snacks and salads.



Beach

A large number of reviews say about the beach, which is located 500 meters from the hotel. According to tourists, this recreation site is quite well-maintained, clean and has a gradual descent into the sea, which, according to many vacationers, is very convenient for those who come with a child. The entire beach area is covered with fine and very soft sand mixed with pebbles.

For the convenience of vacationers, sun loungers with umbrellas are located throughout the area, which guests of Pasabey Hotel 4 * can occupy for free. If desired, they can use towels and air mattresses, which are also issued free of charge. Guests can use public toilets and changing cabins at any time. On the territory there is one shower room.

There are many entertainments along the coastline, most of which are extreme. Under the guidance of an instructor, guests can go down to scuba diving depths or learn surfing skills, enjoy water skiing or a hydro scooter.

Guests staying at the hotel often talk about pretty nice prices that are set by travel agencies. The average cost, based on the comments available on the Web, is about 80,000 rubles - most of the guests consider it quite acceptable. Also, a large number of reviews are directed towards the work of the maintenance staff, among which administrators stand out separately. They, according to vacationers, are always ready to help the tourist in resolving any issues. A positive point, many travelers find that most of them speak Russian - this greatly facilitates communication.
