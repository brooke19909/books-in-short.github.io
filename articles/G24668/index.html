Townhouse: what is it?

So, in the modern sense, these are complexes of two- or three-story houses, interconnected. Each such house has a separate entrance, that is, it can be operated by one family. Thus, residents receive full housing, consisting of several floors. Traditionally, the dining room and living area, garage and utility rooms are located on the first level.





The bedrooms are moved to the upper floor, which allows you to isolate them and provide peace and comfort to vacationers. This is a budget alternative to a detached house, the construction of which is associated with huge financial investments. In addition, it is difficult to independently isolate the house and provide it with round-the-clock protection, a surveillance system.
