The history of the creation of ExoAthlet

  • 2011 - MSU scientists won a tender by the Ministry of Emergencies for the development of the skeleton. In subsequent years, the team creates passive and active devices. Passive was not afraid of fire, weighed 12 kg and suffered heavy loads of up to 100 kg. Active allowed the fighter to lift weights up to 200 kg.
  • 2013 - a team of scientists separated for the development of ExoAtlet for medical purposes. Their motto was that real people would walk in exoskeletons.




  • 2014 - Research Institute of Moscow State University receives the main prize from Startup Village. The team became a Skolkovo resident, and also entered the top five finalists of the Generation S competition and performed at the Sochi Olympics in robotics. Next was a presentation of the development in Singapore and negotiations with the country's chief rehabilitation specialist.
  • 2015 is the first sale. Arkhangelsk region purchased 6 suits for research and testing. In the same year, the first trip to the Middle East took place. At the X Venture Fair in Kazan, the team won first place in the high technology nomination.
  • 2016 - ExoAtlet crosses the Atlantic and conquers the American as well as Asian markets. This year is associated with skeleton developers with a shot from a signal pistol. Sales of devices in Russia start, and serious clinical trials also begin.
