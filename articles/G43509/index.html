"Alimova Balka" - a place for paradise relaxation

For everyone who wants to feel like the heroes of the book “The Wizard of the Emerald City”, the private estate “Alimova Balka”, which is conveniently located near Bakhchisaray, opens its doors.

general information





The proximity to the fairy tale appears immediately, as soon as you find yourself at the entrance to these amazing places. The private estate is almost completely cut off from any invasion from the outside world. The only connection with it is the highway, stretching with a light ribbon along the foot of the Kachinsky canyon.

“Alimova Balka” makes you plunge into the charm of natural luxury. Thanks to the huge amount of vegetation, you literally immerse yourself in a vacation that goes beyond time and space. Bungalows and manor buildings are located in such a way that differences in relief reliably hide you from the eyes of neighbors. Pools and benches, surrounded by greenery and flowers, also create a certain intimate atmosphere, making you completely forget about others and enjoy the tranquility and comfort.

Alimova Balka is an amazing place for those who seek tranquility and solitude. The entire staff of the estate works in such a way as to give you maximum attention while not committing any intrusive actions. During the rest, you may not even notice that you are closely monitored by a service ready to help you when necessary.

The private estate immediately captivates with its unusualness. How could you suppose that in the wilderness, in the rocks, you are lucky to relax in real luxury, literally swim in it? Here you will find out what admiration is, enjoying the pine smells of your room, made of natural wood, using the Finnish sauna or going to a traditional Russian steam room.

Rooms





Each vacationer will like the number that Alimova Balka will offer him. Bakhchisaray is located a few kilometers from this amazing place, so quite often the locals become guests here.

There are four building options in which you can spend your unforgettable vacation.

The main building, completely made of wood, can accommodate several large families at once. There are six suites, nine double rooms, several single superior rooms, as well as a special room with a panoramic view, which will immediately captivate you with its beauty.

Building A is ideal for travelers who do not have a large budget, but who decide to join the luxury. Several double rooms are available for their choice, the cost of which differs depending on the availability of individual amenities.

Building "B" is also suitable for budget accommodation, but the bathrooms are located in the rooms.

The last building is a guest house, the maximum number of people living in it is four people. The house consists of two spacious rooms, a bathroom and a small kitchen. There is a fireplace of its own, which you can always light up and enjoy the view of a real fire.

Entertainment





Not only a pleasant relaxed vacation can offer its guests “Alimova Balka”. Crimea is famous for its clean and healthy air, especially if you are in the mountains. Therefore, do not sit out in the room, it is best to try unusual entertainments and learn something new.

Manor "Alimova Balka" offers every vacationer the opportunity to rent a buggy or ATV to ride on a specially designated safe territory.

It is possible to rent special gazebos provided for weddings, anniversaries and other events. In addition to this, there is a billiard table in the Alimova Balka estate that will not let you get bored during a long feast.

To establish team spirit in your team, you can play various sports on specially equipped venues.

All lovers of swimming in clear sea water are invited to drive twenty minutes by car to the west coast, where there is a special beach equipped with everything necessary.

How to get to the estate





To get to the estate, you can use your personal car or order a taxi. When leaving the city of Sevastopol from Bakhchisarai, you must immediately turn left, towards the gas station. There you can see a pointer by which you can determine the direction to the recreation center "Alimova Balka".

A restaurant

Of course, no rest can do without a restaurant. The restaurant "Pomeshchik" works for you in the estate, which is a real highlight of the "Alimova Balka". In total, a spacious hall can accommodate up to sixty people, and if you need to have a banquet, a rearrangement is done and the number of seats increases to eighty.

Pleasant warm light and cozy fire of a real English fireplace will turn any, even the most ordinary dinner into a romantic one. A company that wants to not only relax but also have fun is offered karaoke.
