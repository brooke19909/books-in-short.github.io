Hotel Sharjah Carlton Hotel 4 * (Sharjah, United Arab Emirates): photos and reviews of tourists

If you are looking for a comfortable hotel with a convenient location, then you should pay attention to such an option as Sharjah Carlton Hotel. Here you will be provided with a wide selection of services to make your hotel stay as comfortable as possible.





Short hotel description

Sharjah Carlton Hotel Resort offers 174 cozy guestrooms. It is worth noting that their entire environment (repair and especially furniture) is selected with taste. In addition, thanks to the latest technology, you will feel maximum comfort. The main building has 4 floors, and nearby are two-level chalets.

You will find a lot of opportunities for a good rest in the territory of the resort complex Sharjah Carlton Hotel 4 * (UAE, Sharjah). A beach with sunbeds and sunshades, swimming pools, a gym, restaurants, bars and much more. In addition, the staff will show you a sample of quality service and hospitality.





Resort Location

The resort complex Sharjah Carlton Hotel 4 * is located about 5 km from the central part of the city in the popular tourist district of Al Khan. It is approximately 14 kilometers from Dubai International Airport.

Guest Apartment Description

At Sharjah Carlton Hotel, guests can enjoy homely warmth and comfort. You can stay in apartments of the following categories:

  • Standard rooms are 32 square meters. m can be designed for both double and triple occupancy. Opposite the beds there is a dressing table with a mirror, as well as a bedside table with a TV. Behind the decorative partition there is a terrace with which you can enjoy city views.
  • Near the main building there are comfortable chalets designed for two adult guests (additional accommodation for one child is possible). A feature is the presence of a terrace and a small private garden. The windows of the chalet face the city, and there are also sea view options.
  • Superior rooms have a spacious area, elegant interiors, and modern facilities. It is worth noting the presence of a living room area, as well as a small kitchenette.
  • Executive Suite gives you plenty of living space, light and an elegant interior. The luxurious bedroom with a king size bed has a large panoramic window and a sliding glass door that leads to a spacious balcony. In the spacious living room you can bask in the fireplace sitting on a comfortable soft sofa. It is worth noting that this is one of the best accommodation options that the Sharjah Carlton Hotel can offer.




Guest Room Features

Perhaps, many travelers dream at least once to relax in the UAE. Sharjah Carlton Hotel 4 * will provide you with the most comfortable conditions for this. In guest rooms you can take advantage of the following amenities:

  • in the combined bathroom for guests there is a bath or shower, as well as bath accessories, a hairdryer and cosmetics;
  • the air conditioner is connected to the central system, and therefore it turns on and off strictly according to the schedule;
  • the list of numerous satellite channels also includes Russian-language programs;
  • using a landline phone you can make an international call, contact the reception, and also order food from the restaurant;
  • You can always have a small supply of food in your refrigerator.

Hotel Infrastructure

All you need for a full stay and relaxation, you will be provided with the resort complex Sharjah Carlton Hotel. The following infrastructure facilities are represented on its territory:

  • wireless Internet, the use of which is included in the price of the hotel;
  • You can leave your own or rented car on which you travel in a guarded parking lot;
  • a laundry room is operating on the territory, which is engaged in washing not only bed linen and towels, but also the personal belongings of guests;
  • for guests with disabilities there are handrails, ramps and other necessary amenities;
  • special smoking areas are provided;
  • so that you can comfortably get to the upper floors, a large capacity elevator is provided;
  • in the shade of a green garden you will have a good time hiding from the heat;
  • in luxurious restaurants you can get acquainted with Arabic cuisine, as well as make an order from the extensive European menu;
  • if you want to have a snack, not leaving the pool, then the cafe-snack bar will help you with this;
  • at the reception there is a safe in which each guest can rent a cell for storing valuables;
  • for bulky and valuable luggage of guests there is a left-luggage office;
  • in the many shops that operate on site, you can purchase souvenirs, products, hygiene items and clothing.




Service and services for hotel guests

An excellent option for living and relaxing is Sharjah Carlton Hotel 4 *. Sharjah is a fairly popular tourist destination. One of the reasons is the quality service provided in the hotels. If we talk about this particular institution, then the following services are available to guests here:

  • guests have the opportunity to use the shuttle service to the airport and back, as well as to other points of the emirate;
  • in your apartment you can order the delivery of food, drinks, goods, the press, as well as spa services;
  • at the reception you can use the fax, as well as make a photocopy of your documents;
  • order a packed lunch if you are going on an excursion or on another long trip;
  • You can leave your children under the supervision of a nanny if you need to leave the hotel;
  • if you need to purchase tickets, the reception staff will kindly help you with this;
  • A personal concierge will advise you on the conditions of your stay at the hotel and will also give you comprehensive information about the resort.




Entertainment and outdoor activities

It is worth noting that a clear sea and a warm sun - this is not all that is needed for a complete rest for a modern demanding traveler. So, the resort complex Sharjah Carlton Hotel 4 * (Sharjah) provides guests with the following entertainment options:

  • a modern sports complex consists of a gym, as well as a separate room with mirrors for aerobics and shaping;
  • to eliminate cosmetic flaws in the figure, to cope with the problems of the musculoskeletal system or just relax, visit the massage room;
  • in the aromatic sauna you can relax, improve your well-being and just have a good time;
  • in the luxurious spa you can go through a lot of pleasant procedures to preserve beauty, youth and well-being;
  • a spacious outdoor pool, swimming in which you can enjoy a magnificent view of the sea (it is also worth noting the huge number of sun loungers installed nearby);
  • On the territory there are opportunities for playing table tennis;
  • a private beach equipped with sun loungers, umbrellas and other necessary amenities for a complete sea holiday;
  • on the sea coast there are all the possibilities for water sports, and professional instructors who can advise you;
  • a modern tennis court with artificial turf provides you with opportunities to play both during the day and in the evening;
  • through the efforts of the tourist office fascinating excursions are organized for guests;
  • for guests fishing is carried out on a boat in the open sea.




Additional Information

Going on vacation to the Sharjah Carlton Hotel 4 * hotel, it is advisable to get acquainted with the rules of living in it. So, the following information will be useful for travelers:

  • upon arrival at the hotel you will have to leave a security deposit of 300 dichrams (can be paid in cash or by credit card);
  • cots can be provided for babies up to three years old;
  • children under 11 years old can stay in the room for free if they do not need an extra bed;
  • those who have a rest in the hotel receive a small birthday cake as a congratulation;
  • when relaxing at the hotel two or more times, you become a regular customer, and therefore you can apply for a free upgrade of the category of apartments if there are a sufficient number of free rooms;
  • pets are not allowed in the hotel;
  • registration of guests is not possible without presenting an identity card with a photograph;
  • daily replacement of bed linen and bath towels;
  • for corporate bookings (more than 5 rooms), discounts may be agreed.




Sharjah Carlton Hotel 4 *: reviews. Positive points

Holidays in the United Arab Emirates for many are associated with a real oriental tale. However, the final impression largely depends on the resort where travelers will be staying. So, quite popular among tourists is the Sharjah Carlton Hotel. Reviews of 2016 about him contain such positive comments:

  • pleased with the presence of their own beach, which is not allowed outsiders (although some employees manage to do this for an additional fee);
  • the hotel has a lot of travelers from the CIS countries, and therefore you can always find a fun company (although many consider this a drawback);
  • very convenient gentle entry into the sea, which will be a particularly pleasant moment for guests with children;
  • the staff is extremely friendly and attentive to the guests;
  • free shuttle buses to Dubai daily;
  • spacious rooms with good comfortable furniture;
  • the air conditioner does not depend on a key card, and therefore the air temperature can be maintained even when you are not in the room;
  • guests have the opportunity to go to lunch instead of breakfast (this should be reported to the waiter, who will make the appropriate marks);
  • very good location, which is characterized by proximity to the sea, as well as a huge number of infrastructure facilities;
  • the beach has very clean white sand, which is completely cleared of stones, algae and other debris;
  • the hotel has a very quiet atmosphere (this is most likely due to a complete ban on alcohol);
  • next to the hotel there is a restaurant serving traditional Russian dishes;
  • if you arrive at the hotel late in the evening, then before registration you will be offered dinner;
  • at the tour desk you can read brochures about local attractions for free, even if you are not going to buy a tour;
  • the administration tries to take into account all special requests that were indicated by the guests when booking;
  • quick placement without unnecessary bureaucratic procedures and delays.

Negative moments

Unfortunately, not only positive reviews can be heard about such a resort institution as the Sharjah Carlton Hotel 4 *. You may come across negative comments as follows:

  • the rooms have a very unpleasant smell of some paints and varnishes;
  • In the rooms, wireless Internet practically does not work;
  • wet bedding;
  • the restaurant serves the same dishes for lunch and dinner;
  • very weak water pressure in the bathrooms of rooms located on the upper floors of the main building, as well as in the chalet;
  • the equipment in the gym is literally antediluvian, and it is completely unsafe to engage in some simulators;
  • no fruit served for breakfast;
  • the coffee in the dining room is of terrible quality;
  • if you do not take a lounger by the pool or on the beach early in the morning, then later you simply will not find free space;
  • not only in the hotel, but in the whole emirate, you will not be able to buy alcohol (and do not try to bring it with you, otherwise you will have problems with the law);
  • despite the fact that when booking a service such as Wi-Fi appears to be free, you will be voiced a certain amount of payment at the time of check-in (do not hesitate to insist on your own);
  • breakfast at the hotel is very expensive, and therefore it is recommended to eat in the surrounding cafes and restaurants, where prices are almost half lower;
  • the bathrooms have a lot of mold and there is a terrible smell of dampness;
  • It is highly recommended not to take complex meals, because for a lot of money you will be fed daily with the same meager assortment of dishes;
  • the restaurant has a very powerful air conditioning, and therefore you have to wear warm sweaters;
  • Due to the proximity of the port to the sea, garbage often floats, and water can emit an unpleasant odor;
  • insufficient number of beach towels;
  • several times a week in the pool swimming classes for local children, which occupy the entire space;
  • from entertainments for children only the playground is provided;
  • Considering that the rules and laws in the emirate are very strict, you should not count on vivid entertainment and cheerful animation;
  • it is very strange that tea and coffee are served for breakfast in the restaurant for free, but you have to pay for ordinary drinking water;
  • on weekends, a lot of local guests come to the hotel who behave quite noisily;
  • beach workers behave very shamelessly with guests - about an hour before closing time they can come up and remove the umbrella, not paying attention to you.

General impression

Quite often, tourists choose such an option for accommodation and recreation as the Sharjah Carlton Hotel. The UAE is characterized by strict rules regarding alcohol and noisy entertainment, and therefore you should not count on having too much fun. This hotel is more likely for family vacations, as well as for people who just want to spend time in a relaxed atmosphere.

Special attention deserves the private beach of the Sharjah Carlton Hotel (Sharjah). It is strewn with white sand, and the water is quite clean and warm. But everything changes when large vessels pass by. Given the proximity of the port, you should be prepared for the fact that all the products of its vital activity will be in the water. Fortunately, there is a pool.

It should be noted that the contingent of vacationers at the Sharjah Carlton Hotel (UAE, Sharjah) is quite multinational. But if you evaluate the attitude of the staff towards Arab tourists, you will notice that it is clearly better than to everyone else. For the same money, they are provided with much better food than any other guest. In addition, they come in very large delegations, flooding the beach and pool. But given that the hotel Arabs rest only on weekends, you can well devote this time to excursions.
