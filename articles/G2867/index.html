How to choose a smartphone


Before buying a smartphone, decide whether you need it. Smartphones differ from ordinary phones in the number of functions, screen size, and battery life. If you need a phone only for calls, then the numerous functions of your smartphone may not be useful to you.

The choice of smartphones on the market is more than large, this can be seen on the site mmicenter.ru . You can get confused in numerous products. Android - is the most popular OS. This operating system is installed in most smartphones that are present on the market. The main advantage of this OS is openness, loyalty to developers. New applications for this OS also appear pretty quickly. This OS is slightly inferior in design to iOS. Since Android is installed on a large number of smartphones, therefore, the price range is quite high, and you can choose the one that suits you best.

Windows Phone (WP) is an operating system manufactured by Microsoft. This is the third most common OS. Applications for this system appear later. This system is characterized by a bright design. In convenience, this system exhausts both iOS and Android.

Screen size. The cost of the smartphone, the operating time, and the convenience of using it will depend on the screen size.
