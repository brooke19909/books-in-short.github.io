General information about the resorts of Stavropol

Mineralnye Vody is the only region in Russia that successfully combines the important factors for the resort, both natural and historical-architectural: a diverse fauna and flora, many museums, historical and cultural monuments, parks and, of course, sanatoriums.

Stavropol Territory is the pearl of the whole country.

A huge number of holidaymakers come to the Stavropol Region all year round in order to relax and improve their health in sanatoriums, as well as enjoy amazingly diverse, unique and aesthetically attractive landscapes.

Resort Zheleznovodsk

The location of the resort Zheleznovodsk is unusual. The city is located on the slopes of Zheleznaya Mountain, which is known for the richest mineral springs. And they are not few - 23. However, only 16 sources of water are used for treatment.

The city of Zheleznovodsk got its name not because of its location on the mountain, but because of the color of the water flowing down the slopes. It has a pronounced shade of rust due to the precipitation of iron hydroxides.

The climate of Zheleznovodsk is very comfortable. It is compared with the climate of the Swiss Alps. Moderately hot summers, lack of sweltering heat, slight coolness at night and, very importantly, practically no wind. Just a light refreshing breeze.

Favorable climatic conditions make rest in Zheleznovodsk comfortable and allow vacationers to spend time in a magnificent park of the 19th century, in which there are many routes for wellness and walking.

The territory of the park is huge and ends with a smooth transition into the natural forest.

You can get acquainted with the sights of the city on your own or resort to the services of a tour desk. Taking a city tour, you will not only get acquainted with the history of the city, but also take fascinating walks in the famous places of Zheleznovodsk: Pushkin Gallery, Ostrovsky baths and other historical places.

Zheleznovodsk hospitals

In Zheleznovodsk there are 20 sanatorium-medical institutions. Sanatoriums of Zheleznovodsk (Stavropol Territory) offer vacationers treatment with both mineral waters and various treatments for the healing mud of the Tambukan Lake diseases of the digestive organs, kidneys, endocrine system.

The glory of the resorts of Zheleznovodsk extends from the time of the Russian Empire. For the safety of vacationers, by order of the Minister of War, the sanatorium and vacationers were guarded by the military, who were specially relocated to these lands.

Sanatoriums and health resorts in Zheleznovodsk existed in the middle of the 19th century. So, for example, the first children's health center "Mountain Air" began its work in Zheleznovodsk back in 1911.

Drowning in deciduous and coniferous forests, the sanatorium functions to this day and accepts a large number of vacationers.

The picturesque winter garden and the pump room with mineral water “Vladimirskaya” located in the sanatorium invite children and parents to rest and treat.

Corporate recreation is also very popular in the mountain air resort.
