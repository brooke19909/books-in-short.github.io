Pets are allowed

You can not worry about who to leave your pet with if you choose the Pokrovskoye-Streshnevo hotel. The hotel provides the possibility of accommodation with pets, subject to prior agreement. The cost of this service is 1000 rubles per day. At the same time, guests must comply with the following list of rules:

  • Wild animals are not allowed.
  • the definition of "domestic" includes animals whose weight does not exceed 15 kg;
  • guests are required to present a veterinary passport and a certificate with notes on vaccinations of the animal;
  • the permissible number of animals is calculated as one animal per room room;
  • the guest is responsible for compliance with sanitary standards in the room and in the hotel;
  • lodgers should have cages and rugs for animals;
  • an animal that weighs more than 5 kg must be outside the room in its muzzle and on a leash;
  • placement with birds is only possible with a cage;
  • the animal cannot stay in the room in the absence of guests;
  • walking of pets in the territory and adjacent lawns is prohibited.
