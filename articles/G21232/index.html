How to transfer money from Yandex to WebMoney?

As they say, the rich also cry. And indeed, it is a shame if you have funds, but you can‚Äôt buy anything with them. Such a story can happen to those whose funds are in the wallet located in the electronic system of Yandex. What to do if money from this wallet is not accepted somewhere, and you need to transfer cash to another wallet? How to transfer money from Yandex to WebMoney? You can do it, but you have to tinker a bit.



Now you can familiarize yourself with how to transfer money from Yandex to WebMoney. To do this, the following conditions must be met:

1. You should, of course, have an account in Yandex.Money; this account must be identified.

2. In the WebMoney system, you must be the owner of the certificate, having a certain level (in this case, not lower than the formal one), all the data in which you have confirmed;

3. The data that identifies your identity must coincide on two sites - Yandex and WebMoney systems.



So, how to transfer money from Yandex to WebMoney? To do this, you need to perform a certain sequence of actions and know some features (by the way, a commission equal to 4.5% is charged to you when transferring funds). To start an operation called "how to transfer money from Yandex to WebMoney," you need to, following all of the above conditions, link the account of one payment system to another. To do this, on the WebMoney website, go to the section called "Account Transactions". In this section, select the icon with the image "Yandex.Money", enter the account number there and send a request for binding. Together with the report, you should also receive the verification number. Remember it, write it down or save it somewhere, but do not try to lose!

After linking, but before transferring money from Yandex to WebMoney, confirm the linking procedure by going to the Yandex.Money service for this. To confirm, you will need to enter the verification number (I hope you have not lost it yet). If you have done everything correctly, a window will appear in the two services, which will indicate that the linking procedure has been completed successfully, and now you can freely and without any difficulties transfer your funds to another wallet.



Well, the last point on how to transfer money to WebMoney is authorization in the Yandex system. Having entered the named system, you will see the WebMoney icon under your account number. Click on it, and after this, before your eyes you will see a form that needs to be filled out so that the transfer of funds is successful. Indicate the amount of payment in the form and confirm the operation by entering the appropriate verification code and password. However, remember that in Yandex.Money the transfer amount is limited. On the day you can transfer from Yandex to a purse in WebMoney no more than fifteen thousand rubles, and for a month this amount should not exceed three hundred thousand rubles. As for the minimum amount, a one-time payment cannot be less than one ruble.

In case you no longer need the service of transferring money between wallets, you can carry out the procedure for canceling the linking of the WebMoney wallet to the Yandex.Money account. To do this, you just need to go to a special page of the site and follow the detailed instructions of the service.
