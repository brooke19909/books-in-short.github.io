Porto Montenegro (Montenegro) - marina in the city of Tivat on the shore of the Bay of Kotor

Porto Montenegro - yacht parking in the city of Tivat, which is located in Montenegro. In this specially equipped place, where yachting began to develop and continue to move in 2010, crews are provided with a berth, repair shops, electrical connections, yacht refueling with water and fuel, lifts, spacious boathouses for ship repair and more.



Elite city for yachtsmen

Porto Montenegro in Montenegro appeared as if by magic, when a Canadian businessman Peter Munch fell in love with these picturesque places with a warm azure sea and climate. On the coast of the city of Tivat, he bought a plot of land and decided to turn the picturesque surroundings into a luxurious and fashionable area for owners of sea yachts. This happened in 2006. Several reputable entrepreneurs from different countries have become participants in a new exclusive project: S. Demian, Rothschilds, B. Arno and Anthony Munch. To bring this plan to life, the famous British landscape designer Martin Lane Fox was invited, as well as the famous American architect Kevin Lynch. When they started work, only an unremarkable marina was located here. Four years later, a unique luxury complex grew in Porto Montenegro (Montenegro). In Europe there are only a few such resorts. In the Adriatic, something similar no longer exists.

Regent Porto Montenegro Hotel

In the city of Tivat is a magnificent hotel, which is located in the center of the truly royal complex of Porto Montenegro. It is built in the Renaissance style. It has 87 rooms with a balcony or terrace. The luxury apartments of Porto Montenegro in Montenegro are specially designed for comfortable living by the sea in a warm Mediterranean climate. The rooms have everything for a comfortable stay: air conditioning, a modern TV that catches satellite channels, free Wi-Fi, a reliable safe. The spacious bathroom has all the washing facilities, a sufficient number of fluffy towels, bathrobes and slippers are provided.

The hotel has three restaurants, a bar, a cafe. A sophisticated atmosphere reigns everywhere. Regent guests have access to the spa and wellness centers, Turkish bath, steam room, saunas, indoor pool and gym. For squash and tennis lovers, there are special courts nearby, and you can go bowling.

Island Hotel





It is considered the most expensive in the world and is located on the small island of St. Stephen. It is connected to the mainland by a small isthmus. In the Middle Ages, it was just a simple village where fishermen lived. It was rebuilt in the 50s and turned into a magnificent hotel, where many Hollywood stars like to stay. Black lake





It is the coldest lake in the Adriatic. In winter, it even freezes and becomes covered with ice. It is divided into Big and Small, and Small is half its size, but twice as deep. The first refers to the Black Sea basin, and the second to the Adriatic Sea.

The largest bay of Montenegro

About this amazing bay you need to tell a little. Kotor Bay in Montenegro bizarre forms crashes into land for twenty-eight km. This is a former river bed, which went under sea water after shifts of the earth's crust. The whole bay, divided into four parts, is very deep, and at its bottom the remains of dead schooners, ships and submarines of different historical times have found their place. There are several islands. Magnolias and mimosas, roses and pomegranates, olive groves bloom along the banks. Numerous tourists have chosen Lusitsa Peninsula with White Pebble Zanitsa Beach.



In this bay, one of the most beautiful in the world, there is a marina for four hundred and fifty yachts. The world-class yacht club has the best conditions for sailing. If the marina of Porto Montenegro could initially accept 85 yachts, now it can accommodate vessels of various sizes - even large ones that are up to one and a half hundred meters in length.

The marina has the Museum of the Collection of Marine Heritage. Today it is the best port of Montenegro. It is located just two days of calm sailing from the popular resorts of Greece, France, Italy.

The views of the Bay of Kotor with an abundance of magnificent yachts are simply amazing, and the presence of numerous restaurants and boutiques offers a serene seaside life.
