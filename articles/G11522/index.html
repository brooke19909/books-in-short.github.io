Attractions and attractions in Utrish (Anapa)

Many tourists are attracted by the sights. Utrish - Black Sea village full of legends. It is located in the Krasnodar Territory. According to legend, Prometheus was chained to one of the rocks near Big Utrish . And Jason sailed to this village, looking for a golden fleece. And Utrish is not offended by nature. The village is surrounded by picturesque landscapes, and transparent water sparkles with diamond flares in the bays.

Spearfishing

The Black Sea village of Utrish, whose attractions and entertainments are quite interesting, can offer tourists diving and spearfishing. In Snake Bay, you can take a boat for these purposes and sail away from the coast. Since the water in Utrish is clean and clear, visibility is simply magnificent. In addition, the coastal waters of the village abound with many species of fish and other marine inhabitants.

Beaches

All beaches in the village of Utrish are pebble. The busiest is located near the dolphinarium, next to the pier. If desired, you can rent scooters, jet skis and catamarans on the beach.



Lighthouse

All tourists are primarily attracted by attractions. Utrish is a village famous not only for legends. He has two lighthouses. You can start sightseeing with them. The big lighthouse is a monument. The construction was established back in 1975 as a memory of sailors who fought on the Black Sea from 1942 to 1943. In particular, the lighthouse honors the feat of the crew of the ship, which in 1942 was blown up by a mine and was washed ashore.

But the sailors fired at enemy planes after that for another three months. Only when the situation became so complicated that they could no longer hold the defenses, the survivors were forced, according to the order of the authorities, to leave the ship. But immediately went to the defense of Novorossiysk.

Next to the monument is the second lighthouse, which is also considered one of the attractions of the village. It was built at the end of the 20th century. And in 1990 a small chapel was built in it.

Large aquarium

In any, even a small village has its own unique attractions. Utrish is no exception. In this village there is large aquarium. It is located on the territory of the Utrish Reserve. The aquarium is open every day except Monday. It is filled with exhibits captured at sea. But as soon as the season ends, all living creatures from the aquarium are released back into the sea. Dolphinarium

The dolphinarium in the Utrish village is one of the most famous in Russia. When the high season begins, performances take place daily. Not only dolphins, but also fur seals participate in them. Performances take place on the high seas. There, animals feel the most comfortable and free. After the show, it is allowed to get to know the marine artists closer.

Reserve

Bolshoi Utrish Wildlife Sanctuary (photos of attractions are in this article) is located on the Abrau Peninsula, between the villages of Sukko and Maly Utrish. The reserve was established in 1994. Its task is to preserve relic plants and landscapes. Among them are Snake Lake, Waterfall and Lobanova Slots. They are listed in the Red Book.

The main wealth of the reserve is relic junipers. One hectare of these plants releases 30 kilograms of substances that kill all germs. There are three main types of junipers in the reserve: reddish, tall and odorous. The smell of these junipers persists for many years. And inhaling it cleanses the body, contributes to its renewal. During the excavation, ancient wood was found on the territory of the reserve. And she, as it turned out, retained her scent. Although it lay in the ground for 4 thousand years.



Nature reserve

Utrish, Anapa attractions some have in common. For example, a nature reserve. The Utrish reserve includes 10,008 hectares of the reserve. The rest are in the territory of Novorossiysk and Anapa. The Utrish Reserve is located on the Abrau Peninsula. The climate is moderately warm, Mediterranean. Summer in the reserve is dry and hot, and winter is warm.

The reserve previously had no boundaries and official position. But in 2010, all documents were finally drawn up properly. And now the reserve has acquired the status of a state. Protected areas began in the 1980s. In 2008, the reserve was nearly destroyed due to the planned construction of the road. But thanks to the public protest, the route was never laid.

In the Utrish Nature Reserve you can see growing oaks, hornbeams, ash, maples and linden. On the south side there are juniper and fluffy oak belts. There is growing pistachio, fluffy oak and fieldfare. On the low-mountain slope, a tree-like juniper is added to the vegetation, and on the inner strip - a rocky oak and a Caucasian hornbeam. In addition to the listed plants and trees in the reserve, you can see Pitsunda pine and many other interesting types of vegetation.



In total, there are 117 species belonging to endemic and 16 different relics. Five species of mosses grow on the territory of the Utrish Nature Reserve, which are not found anywhere else in Russia. And over 380 plants are listed in the Red Book.

The fauna of the reserve is unique and diverse. In Utrish, an empusa striped, Mediterranean tortoise, a steppe hawk and Aesculapian snake were found. According to general data, in the reserve there are 147 species of birds, 71 - fish, 8 - amphibians, mammals - 44 and reptiles - 14.

A black vulture, a white-tailed eagle, a snake-eater, and many others nest in Utrish. In the Red Book - 94 species of insects. And 20 of them live on the territory of the Utrish Reserve. Moreover, 13 of them are found exclusively in this territory. In the reserve you can see rare Mediterranean butterflies.



Big Utrish Attractions the village. Pearl Waterfall

Zhemchuzhny Waterfall is located 2.5 km from the Big Utrish. It is one of the most famous in the Krasnodar Territory. The waterfall has its own peculiarity - its waters falling on a rocky beach create a small stream flowing into the Black Sea. The height of the fall of the jets is from 5 to 6 meters.

Pearl is not only one of the few fresh sources. At the same time, it also serves as a natural "shower." Near the Zhemchuzhny waterfall you can stop the savages for rest. And for lovers of sunbathing naked, there is a nudist beach nearby.

At 200 meters from the waterfall, the Jewel clan camp is open every year. It practices the adoption of childbirth in sea water. Pearl is one of the points in the ecotropic routes, the length of which is 6 km. Since there are no settlements near the Vodopadny stream, where the Pearl Waterfall originates from, it is famous for its crystal clear water.

Other attractions

Utrish is rich in other attractions. Snake Lake is a good example. It is located near the village of Big Utrish, just 800 meters. The name of the lake was due to the large number of snakes that lived in the pond. Previously, the water in the reservoir was fresh, as it did not connect with the Black Sea. They were separated by an isthmus, which was then blown up. A quiet bay was formed, called the "Snake". Reptiles, which were very numerous in the freshwater lake, were used to living in slightly salted water over time.



The area of ‚Äã‚Äãthe Snake Bay is 22.5 hectares. The maximum depth of the lake reaches eight meters. On the banks of the Snake Bay grow endangered relic junipers listed in the Red Book. For many years, the shore of the lake has been parking for fishing boats. But now only sailing or motor yachts are moored. Near Snake Bay there is a descent to the sea and an observation deck. And right behind the lake is a pebble beach.

Excursions

In Utrish you can book excursions. The choice is small, but each of them leaves an unforgettable experience. For example, boat trips, an excursion to Anapa. There is also a separate nature tour. Moreover, it is carried out on horseback, which in itself is already attractive.

Maly Utrish: Attractions and Entertainment

Small Utrish is located eight kilometers from the Big. In Maly there is a yacht club in a closed bay. If desired, you can rent any boat. The nature of Lesser Utrish is pristine, the beaches are sparsely populated, and the water is clear everywhere. There are two recreation centers. Maly Utrish is part of the dendrological reserve. The village is located 12 kilometers from Novorossiysk.
