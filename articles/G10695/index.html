Location

The main resort on the island of Phuket is Patong. He enjoys the fame of the capital of the sex industry, and in terms of viciousness is inferior only to Pattaya. Patong Beach does not please tourists with cleanliness. And at night, the resort does not sleep, and with it tourists who had the misfortune of staying in hotels by the street of Bangla Road bars. But Karon Beach, just 8 kilometers from Patong, is in stark contrast to the main city.

Calmness and bliss, the azure sea, palm trees and casuarins - this is what this coast looks like. Karon's ‚Äúcalling card‚Äù is a fine sand that creaks at every step, like snow due to the high content of quartz. In the south of the beach, where, in fact, Beyond Resort Karon 4 * is located, a coral reef is also added to all the charms, which makes it possible to snorkel and dive. The entire strip of Karon - and it stretches for three and a half kilometers - is occupied by luxury hotels. But they are separated from the sea by a highway with serious traffic. And only two hotels - and one of them, "Beyand Resort" - have direct access to the beach.

Territory

Hotel Beyond Resort Karon 4 * was built in 1989. But do not be afraid of the venerable age of this hotel. In 2013, a large-scale reconstruction was carried out here. The three-story building in the form of a horseshoe descends on the terraces. Thus, from all the rooms you can see the sea. Between the building and the beach are two swimming pools. There is also a restaurant with a spacious terrace on the beach, where it is nice to have a meal with sea views. There is a bar on the coast, and a cafe in the lobby (services are paid for).

In general, many tourists mention that the area near the hotel is small. There is no tennis court or gym. On the other side of the building is Karon's main street. One has only to go beyond the gates of the hotel, and you are in the center of local life. Several cafes located ten meters from the hotel will satisfy your hunger. The reviews also include a massage parlor, shops, tour agencies and scooter rental offices. The location of the hotel in the south of Karon makes it possible to walk to another famous Phuket beach - Kata.

Beach

As for the coast, all the guests of the Beyond Resort Karon 4 * hotel were unanimous: it is beyond praise here. Anyone traveling to Thailand solely across the sea and the beach will be pleased. The "singing sand" of Karon became the "calling card" of Phuket. A wide strip of the beach allows all guests to sit on sunbeds, there is no need to occupy them early. There are low tides on this coast, but they are weakly expressed. Even outside the tourist season, waves are observed here only during the day, but in the morning and evening the sea is calm.

And in the winter months there is generally grace. No tidal currents, a light breeze freshens the air. The entry into the water is gentle, there are no stones or fragments of coral at the bottom. But in the southern part of Karon, where it smoothly transitions to Kata beach, there is a reef that attracts snorkelers. So guests recommend taking a mask with a pipe on a trip. After all, the underwater life of a coral reef is extremely interesting. The beach has massage services.

Pools and Spas

Tired of splashing around in the very salty Andaman Sea? Diversify your relaxation with a swim in the fresh water-filled pools of the Beyond Resort Karon 4 * (Phuket) hotel. There are two of them, and they are located in close proximity to each other, between the hull and the beach. Nearby is a bar where psychedelic music is played. Cocktails and refreshments are available there. The sizes of the swimming pools are 16 x 7 meters. Both tanks have hydromassage functions. The hotel has a small spa. It consists of a sauna and a professional massage room.

Services in Beyond Resort Karon 4 * (Thailand, Phuket)

The hotel has a laundry room, where you can hand over things for washing, simply folding them in a special bag and leaving for the maids. The reception desk is open around the clock. The hotel offers its guests discounts on the use of the golf club and the services of diving and surfing centers. All these establishments are located in close proximity to Beyand Resort. When checking in, no deposit will be charged, and this is called a plus of the hotel by tourists. The hotel also has a tour desk. But tourists are not advised to use its services. The same thing, but for much less money, can be found in the travel agency on the street.
