Rooms

The 4-star Jannah Place Dubai Marina offers beautiful views of Dubai Marina. Today there are 133 modernly equipped rooms of three types:

  1. This apartment has a balcony and kitchen for 2 adults and a child.
  2. This apartment has a sea view, sofa and dining area for 2 adults and a child.
  3. Two-bedroom apartment with a balcony and a kitchen for 4 adults and 2 children.

Each room has a private bathroom with a washing machine, tumble dryer, shower or bath. Each guest is provided with a set of towels, which is constantly changing, as well as free toiletries. One extra non-crib bed can be delivered to the room for a fee. Cots are available for free:

  • babies under the age of 2 years;
  • only one child from 6 to 12 years old.

To make guests feel at home, the hotel offers terry bathrobes and slippers. All rooms are clean and tidy, have excellent sound insulation, so the rest will be stress-free. Smoking is strictly prohibited in the rooms.
