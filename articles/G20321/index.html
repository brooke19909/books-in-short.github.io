The recreation center "Golden Fleece": where it is located, how it looks

Number of rooms

What is a club hotel? This is when every guest is surrounded by the care of a friendly and sensitive staff. The Golden Fleece has a total of seventy rooms. And this means that each client is given due attention. Ideal conditions are created here to feel like a member of an elite club, to spend an unforgettable vacation away from the bustle of big cities. Rooms at the Golden Fleece Hotel in Dmitriadovka are divided into categories. But even the standards have their own spacious balcony, where you can enjoy a sea or pool with a cup of coffee. These rooms are quite spacious - fifteen square meters - and are designed to accommodate two guests. But in them there is the possibility of sharing a third person, because in addition to a double bed there is also a folding sofa.

Another category of rooms is family. It consists of two rooms. Junior Suite has a larger area - twenty square meters. Needless to say, each hotel room has its own bathroom, a convenient individual air conditioning system, TV and other amenities necessary for a quality stay? Especially a lot of meritorious reviews have earned suites. Each of them has an individual design. These rooms are not adjoined by balconies, but by spacious terraces with sea views.



Nutrition

The cost of living in the club hotel "Golden Fleece" (Taganrog) includes breakfast. They are served in a sophisticated restaurant with panoramic sea views. The chefs of this establishment are famous for their craftsmanship. Therefore, many residents of Taganrog arrange weddings and other solemn events on its basis. But as the reviews assure, this will not affect the calm atmosphere of the club hotel. After all, the restaurant has a separate banquet room. The cooks are excellently fed. About breakfast there is not a single negative review. Culinary specialists specialize in dishes of Russian and European cuisines. The hotel also has a cafe-bar. Spacious summer terrace adjoins this establishment. It can be rented for cocktail parties and coffee breaks. Experienced barista will offer you a variety of alcoholic, refreshing or warming drinks, as well as a variety of cocktails. In the cafe you can treat yourself to delicious desserts, ice cream. Reviews say that the wine list of the bar surprises with its variety and geography of the origin of drinks.



Pool and beach

The shore of the Taganrog Bay is what most summer tourists come to the Golden Fleece for. The hotel has its own beach, which is equipped according to European standards. If the sea is stormy or a cold current has arrived, your vacation, according to reviews, will not be spoiled by weather conditions. After all, the club hotel has a wonderful freshwater swimming pool. A lot of reviews are devoted to him. Its area is 450 square meters. But this is not the main thing. There is a water slide in the pool, counter currents create a current, and in the evening it is beautifully highlighted, which creates an indescribably romantic atmosphere. For younger guests, from May to September there is a children's inflatable pool with water attractions.



Infrastructure

The recreation center "Golden Fleece" gives its guests a VIP-vacation. There is free parking for hotel guests. Sports enthusiasts can play tennis on the court. There is a large conference room that can accommodate 120 participants. It is equipped with everything necessary for holding symposia and other business meetings. There is also a sauna on site, as well as a billiard room with two large tables with stone coasters. Since the summer of this year, the hotel has pleased its young guests with a playground where absolutely free rides are located - swings, inflatable pools and slides. The hotel reception is open 24 hours a day. Its employees will help you organize a transfer to the airport or train station, book excursions to interesting places in the Don region.



Services

Guests of the Golden Fleece Club Hotel (Taganrog) enjoy a twenty percent discount when visiting the sauna and billiards room. The first will give you more than just a bath ritual. Sauna is a real spa complex. It has its own heated pool, a comfortable relaxation area with warm sunbeds. The billiard room did not leave indifferent guests. It has two twelve-foot playing fields with stone worktops, plus a large-screen TV and soft sofas. At the reception you can take board games - chess, backgammon, checkers. In summer, the hotel organizes evening entertainment by the pool. For them, DJs and other animators are invited.
