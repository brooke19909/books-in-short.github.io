Larissa Inn Hotel 4 * (Kemer): description, photos and reviews of tourists

For those who prefer a holiday by the sea in places with amazingly beautiful nature, Kemer is perfect. The long-established Turkish resort, located just forty kilometers from Antalya, is located on the shores of the bay at the foot of the Taurus Mountains. It was him in his Iliad that the famous Homer described.





general information

Although Kemer is a relatively "young" resort, it is already considered an established center for beach holidays. Here is a beautiful nature, against which many chic hotels, night clubs, restaurants and colorful oriental cafes function.

However, tourists in this resort, charming with its beauty, first of all appreciate its natural resources: an almost transparent sea of ‚Äã‚Äãturquoise color, secluded bays hidden from human eyes, which are surrounded by lush vegetation, an incredible pine aroma reigns in the air.

A light breeze, catching the smell of fir trees, deliciously smelling orange groves - all this forms a unique microclimate of this region. In addition, Kemer is famous for its magnificent beaches, many of which are marked with the Blue Flag for environmental friendliness. Such an amazing microclimate is created by the sea and the mountains surrounding Kemer. This makes it possible for a person to practically not feel the high air temperature inherent in this resort in high season. There is comfortable sunny weather here year-round.





Ten kilometers from Kemer is the village of Camyuva. At the end of the last century, as a result of a real tourist boom, the once modest fishing village turned into a real international recreation center. Its name is translated from Turkish as "pine nest."

Today Camyuva is a beautiful resort surrounded by mountains, surrounded by thickets of oleanders, orange and olive trees, palm trees. Hotels here mainly have a high category and fully meet European standards. One of them is the four-star Larissa Inn Hotel.

Description

This hotel was formerly called the Rizzi Hotel, and before that - the Larissa Inn Camyuva Hotel. The tourist complex is located five kilometers from Kemer, in the former fishing village of Camyuva. The nearest airport (Antalya) is fifty kilometers from the hotel. Many tourists from Russia opt for Larissa Inn Hotel 4 *. Tours here have recently become very popular, and one of the reasons is the short time transfer from the airport. Indeed, for families traveling on vacation with their young children, the issue of remoteness from the airport is especially relevant.

The hotel was built in 2005. It has a relatively small territory - only four and a half thousand square meters. Larissa Inn Hotel (ex. Rizzi Hotel) is a five-story building surrounded by trees. The small balconies available in all rooms give residents the opportunity to admire not only the sea panorama, but also the majestic mountain ranges, from the slopes of which a pleasant aroma of pine needles descends.

The territory of Larissa Inn Hotel is fully landscaped. Palm trees grow everywhere, exotic shrubs, lawns are broken. There are ballot boxes, signs indicating all the main infrastructure of the hotel - everything so that residents would feel comfortable on site.

Parking spaces are not required to be reserved in advance. The last major renovation in this hotel was carried out in 2016.





Infrastructure

Larissa Inn Hotel 4 offers tourists a full range of services, which according to the standard corresponds to a four-star category. On its territory there is a currency exchange, concierge services, paid laundry, medical room and a beauty salon. There is also a small shop and a souvenir stall.

The registration desk is always open and works without interruption. You can expect room keys in the cozy lobby. It is possible to rent a safe box or a left-luggage office. Here in the lobby there is a bureau selling sightseeing tours, as well as a rental car.

There is a bus stop at the entrance to the property. At intervals of twenty minutes, beads depart from it in the direction of Kemer.

Hotel Residential Fund

Larissa Inn Hotel (ex. Rizzi Hotel) has 120 standard rooms located in a single five-story building. Three hundred and forty people can stay at the same time. The rooms are small: their area is twenty-four square meters, which is enough to accommodate three people. Despite such compactness, inside there is everything that is required for a comfortable stay.

Larissa Inn Hotel, the service in which is fully consistent with the declared category, offers its guests accommodation in completely renovated rooms with new furniture, a built-in safe and a telephone. On the floor there is a carpet. There is a minibar with a complimentary bottle of water as a compliment. Everything else must be paid for its contents.

The Larissa Inn Hotel (Camyuva) has an electronic card system for opening doors, and a fire alarm has been set up. Towels are cleaned and changed in the hotel rooms daily.

The color scheme of rooms, curtains, bedspreads by professional designers is thought out so as to create a special cozy atmosphere in the room. The balconies overlooking the sea or the pool are equipped with plastic furniture, there is also a dryer.





Nutrition

Larissa Inn Hotel offers food according to the most popular all-inclusive concept in Turkey. On the territory of this hotel, it operates from nine thirty to eleven in the evening. Three times a day for two hours, trays are set in the restaurant - buffet tables. In high season, guests are also offered a diet and a separate children's menu.

Pastries and snacks can be obtained from two to five in the afternoon in the main restaurant, and coffee, tea, juices and alcoholic drinks from Turkish producers can be found in the pool bar. Freshly squeezed juices, as well as imported champagne or whiskey will have to buy.

Beach

Larissa Inn Hotel 4 * has a small private bathing area. Its length is one hundred meters. The beach is sandy and pebble, so for comfort it is better to go into the water in special rubber shoes. Three hundred meters to the sea from the hotel. On the beach, living on an all-inclusive system, umbrellas and deck chairs are free. For air mattresses and beach towels at the receptionist you need to leave a deposit of five dollars.

For tourists there is a toilet, locker rooms and showers. Vacationers can also sit in the bar, which is located on the first coastline. Here, for a fee, you can order salads, snacks, desserts. A professional bartender will prepare a delicious cocktail or fresh. Homemade soft drinks with various fruit flavors are very popular on the beach.





Services for children

Judging by the reviews, Larissa Inn Hotel is family oriented. The hotel has all the options so that the children here feel comfortable and can find something to do with their interests.

A shallow pool is equipped for them, a game club operates. Larissa Inn Hotel has a playground as well as animation. At certain hours, cartoons are watched, various shows are held. For the convenience of parents in the restaurant in the public domain there are high chairs for feeding.

Paid entertainment

Larissa Inn Hotel (Kemer) offers its guests a visit to the spa, Turkish hammam and sauna. You can also take a massage course in a specially equipped office, make a tattoo. Those Russians who combine leisure with sightseeing can buy tours at the tour desk located at the reception desk. However, judging by the reviews left, many prefer to independently study the local ancient monuments.

The beach has a lot of entertainment. Here you can rent a scooter, do parasailing, ride a water bike or ski, rent a boat or yacht. In addition, tourists with a licensed instructor can go scuba diving and enjoy the beauty of coral reefs.





Free entertainment

Larissa Inn Hotel has an outdoor pool of two hundred square meters. Small slides are installed. Lying on sun loungers under umbrellas, residents can have a good time, sunbathing. In the mornings, a group of water aerobics trains in the pool. Table tennis and darts are also free.

Larissa Inn Hotel - Holiday Reviews

Each person has their own hotel selection criteria. For some, an excellent option is one that offers impeccably organized service, for others, food is in the first place, and for others, entertainment.





The Russians who visited the Larissa Inn Hotel (Kemer) mainly liked the Turkish hotel. Regarding the situation in the rooms, no one expressed a fundamental discontent. Some visitors, however, found them too small. Many vacationers from our country note the fact that almost all hotel staff understand the Russian language. This makes the Russian stay here more comfortable. However, the majority of maids evaluated the work quite negatively: the rooms are cleaned poorly and irregularly.

As for the location, the reviews are only positive. As for nutrition, opinions differ somewhat here. Some people think that in this hotel the food is varied and delicious, others express dissatisfaction with the scarcity of the assortment in the menu and the lack of meat dishes, but all these reviews are purely individual.

Our tourists call the hotel beach a real island of peace. Compared to neighboring hotels, where music is booming in the bathing areas from early morning and there are a lot of people, it is calm and crowded. The Russians left a lot of positive feedback about the fully landscaped area. Particularly impressed by some palm trees entwined with vines. Judging by the reviews, there are many wonderful places on the territory where you can conduct a photo shoot. Some were impressed by the fire show, which was organized by a team of animators.

Most of our compatriots find the Larissa Inn Hotel a great vacation destination, especially for the money that was paid, so they recommend choosing this particular hotel.
