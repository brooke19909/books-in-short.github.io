Universal charging for all types of laptops

Nowadays, the definition of "intelligent man" smoothly flows into the familiar form of "mobile man" for the 21st century. We are surrounded by many mobile devices: tablets, smartphones and even watches with personal computer functions. But the most popular device, and in various areas, is the laptop.





In order for the faithful assistant to be always in good shape, he needs a daily (and someone hourly) recharge. The branded power supply that came with the kit is, of course, an ideal option, but if, due to some circumstances, you cannot use it, universal charging for laptops will come to the rescue. This device will save your report, project or unsaved game from "death" more than once, the main thing is to connect it to the network on time.

Huntkey Universal Charge Review

Consider one of these devices from Huntkey, which, according to the manufacturer, is nothing more than universal charging for laptops of all types. Let's see if this is really so and what are the capabilities of this device.





In a small and modest package are: a power cord, ten various nozzles and connectors, a convenient bag for storage and carrying, instructions and, of course, the universal charging for laptops.

Device features

During bench tests, the gadget showed itself only on the positive side, and the charging efficiency on almost all laptop models exceeded the mark of 87%, which is an excellent result for this kind of device. The universal charging for Huntkey laptops meets all the declared characteristics, and together with the enviable versatility and low price, the gadget looks very attractive.

If your original power supply fails, then this device will be a great and inexpensive alternative to branded counterparts.

Huntkey is a universal charger for Asus laptops, Dell laptops, Hewlett Packard, Eiser, Toshiba, Samsung, Sony, Lenovo, Panasonic, Sharp. The device also showed great performance in working with lesser-known brands: Thinkpad, Compaq, Gateway and Fujitsu.
