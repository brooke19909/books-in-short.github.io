Tools you need to work

In order to sew a hare, it is not necessary to be a professional in this matter, but any experience in sewing will still be useful. Not everyone can make a toy of their dreams the first time, for this it takes months and even years of training.

For work you will need:

  • Tailor's scissors. Such scissors can be bought at any stationery or fabric store.
  • Chalk, soap or fabric marker. A bright fabric marker that is clearly visible is best suited. True, he quickly disappears from the fabric. Chalk is sometimes difficult to erase; it can ruin the look of the toy. Soap, on the contrary, is most often poorly visible on the fabric, which can interfere with work.
  • Needle and thread. You can choose even the thinnest needle, for example, a bead. Working with her is very convenient and fast. Threads must be selected color in color to the fabric.
  • Tissues.
  • Filler for a toy. It can be syntepuh, hollofiber or even granulate (it is used to make toys heavier).
  • Patterns hares Tilda.
  • Accessories for decorating toys: satin ribbons, artificial flowers, beads, lace, etc.
