Everything for a wonderful vacation: Lazarevsky hotels with pool and beach

Krasnodar Territory leads the country in the number of people who want to relax in the summer. And in one of the most popular places in this region is the village of Lazarevskoye, located near Sochi, but spared from noisy crowds.

Varied Lazarevsky hotels with swimming pool, beach and developed infrastructure correspond to the European level of comfort.





Hotels with pool for adults and children

The sun shines in Lazarevsky for more than 300 days a year, but bad weather happens here too. Sometimes the cold current makes the sea unsuitable for swimming. In this case, the hotel pool helps out. Lazarevsky hotels with a pool are no less convenient if a family with children comes to rest. Kids can splash around in a safe pond all day and play in the playgrounds while their parents relax.





Lazarevsky hotels with a swimming pool provide rooms of various levels of comfort, the cost of living also varies. We will talk about this later in the article.

Lazarevskoye Hotels with a Pool and a Beach

In the central part of the village are 4 buildings of the hotel complex "Oreshnik". The complex offers vacationers 48 rooms of various categories:

  • Standard - from 2 to 4 places.
  • Superior comfort - 1-room and 2-room.
  • Three-room apartment.

All rooms have comfortable beds, upholstered furniture, a refrigerator, TV, air conditioning and a safe. The apartments are additionally equipped with a microwave, DVD, stereo system and hairdryer. All rooms have balconies with amazing views of the park.

In addition, the 10-meter pool in Oreshnik is heated and highlighted in the evening. Near it there are sun loungers, a bar. There is a playground for children next to the pool.





Many hotels with a pool in Lazarevsky They also have their own beach, such as the Priboy Hotel. Here you can enjoy the summer sea and beach activities in full. And on the territory of the "Surf" there is a warm pool with a waterfall and a geyser.

The hotel offers guests spacious rooms of various categories:

  • standard - up to 35 sq.m;
  • studio - 70 sq.m;
  • Junior Suite - 50 sq.m;
  • Suite - about 70 sq.m.

On the territory there is a dining room, cafe and a stunning restaurant, as well as a beauty salon.

Rest and treatment

During the holidays, you can do your health here, combining wellness, treatment, relaxation and tourism.
