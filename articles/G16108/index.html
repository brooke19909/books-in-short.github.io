Vladimir, Park Hotel "Pearl of the Golden Ring": description, how to get there, reviews

Park Hotel "Pearl of the Golden Ring" is located near Vladimir, on the 175th kilometer of the M7 highway. From the city's railway station to the complex on the banks of the picturesque Revyaki Lake, only 12 km. Any resident of a large industrial center would like to spend a vacation in a quiet place remote from the city bustle, breathe in the purest air of a pine forest, enjoy the sunshine on the shores of a beautiful lake, and go on a boat ride with your loved one.

It is interesting to go fishing early in the morning on the lake. You can negotiate with the huntsman and go hunting with all the permits. For winter holidays in the "Pearl of the Golden Ring" also created all the conditions. It is exciting to ski or snowmobile, taking the necessary equipment in the rental service. If you wish, you can combine rest with work, because the complex has a large conference room and related services, such as the availability of a copy center and fax.
