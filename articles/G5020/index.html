Bounty



Beach "Bounty" in Feodosia for a reason was awarded such a "sweet" name. Visitors should definitely see it. The coast and the seabed are strewn with crushed shell. From afar, the coast of the Bounty seems snow-white, like the filling of an advertisement for a famous bar. This is the best sandy beach of urban type. It occupies a significant area: 800 meters long and 50 meters wide. The beach has a well-developed infrastructure: there is a rescue station, storage rooms, there are toilets, changing rooms, necessary beach equipment, water activities, slides, 2 entertainment complexes (Bora, Forty Thieves). Upscale cafes are located here, offering guests dishes of Eastern and European cuisine. Admission to the Bounty is free.
