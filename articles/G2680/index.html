Hotel "Timan" (Ukhta, Komi Republic): address, photo, reviews

Hotel Timan opens its doors to travelers cordially. Ukhta will be interesting to tourists as well as business people. This institution will provide everything you need for a good rest, which will charge you with energy for new discoveries.





A bit about the hotel

The cozy hotel "Timan" (Ukhta) first opened its doors to guests back in 1971. Since then, a huge number of tourists, businessmen, as well as artists and famous politicians have managed to visit here.

The weather in Ukhta is not the warmest, and therefore it is very important to find a place where you can warm yourself with soul and body. For example, the hotel "Timan". Here, more than 120 comfortable rooms of various categories are provided for accommodating guests. In addition, you will appreciate the wide range of services provided, as well as qualified staff.





Hotel "Timan" (Ukhta): address

For tourists and business people it is extremely important to find a hotel with a convenient location. In the heart of the city is the hotel "Timan". Ukhta, Lenin Avenue, 4 - the address of this institution. This is the main street where a huge number of cafes, shops and restaurants are concentrated. Also in the immediate vicinity are public transport facilities and a taxi rank.

Positive reviews

If you are interested in the Timan Hotel (Ukhta), the reviews of experienced travelers will help you evaluate all the advantages of this establishment. It is worth noting such positive comments:

  • convenient location in the very center of the city;
  • the price is consistent with the quality of the services provided;
  • the atmosphere in the room, although old, is quite comfortable;
  • the hotel has coolers with hot and cold water;
  • the restaurant makes good soups;
  • Wireless Internet works well on all floors and in all rooms;
  • pleasant and friendly staff who will always come to the rescue;
  • in the rooms, which include the use of a shared bathroom, there is a sink;
  • in the immediate vicinity of the hotel there is a public transport stop, as well as a taxi rank;
  • the maids clean the rooms well (but this does not apply to the bathroom).




Negative reviews

There are a number of shortcomings that travelers should be aware of when going to this hotel. We are talking about such negative comments:

  • the rooms have very old furniture that gives off an unpleasant odor;
  • centipedes and other insects crawl in the shower;
  • even if you have booked a room in advance, upon arrival it may turn out that all rooms are occupied and you will be denied check-in;
  • breakfasts that are too expensive, despite the fact that literally around the corner there is a good bistro with a wide assortment and reasonable prices;
  • elevator does not work well;
  • the hotel is constantly being renovated, which significantly interferes with the rest of the guests;
  • in the bathroom - rusty plumbing;
  • in the cafe on the fourth floor the hood does not work well, and therefore in the dining room there is always a smell of food that eats into the hair and clothes of guests;
  • at the most unexpected moment, guests may be asked to move to another room, motivating this with repair work;
  • TVs do not work well (guests have to configure antennas to receive channels themselves);
  • the rooms, whose windows face Lenin Avenue, are very noisy;
  • the general toilet is very dirty, the bins are always full and there is also an unpleasant smell;
  • uncomfortable beds, after sleeping on which the back is terribly sore;
  • there is no air freshener in the toilet, which can be considered a significant drawback, given the poor operation of the ventilation system;
  • strong audibility between numbers;
  • the rooms do not have air conditioning, and if you open the windows, it will be very noisy;
  • the bathroom is very cramped (this is especially felt by people of large physique);
  • no hygiene kits are provided;
  • tasteless food and dirty dishes in the hotel catering facilities;
  • a weak pressure of water in the shower (especially early in the morning and in the evening);
  • the rooms do not have a tea set (or even utensils in which you could prepare hot drinks);
  • too narrow beds (especially in double and triple rooms).




Conclusion

A good budget option for accommodation is the hotel "Timan" (Ukhta). A hairdresser, office space, tour desk and much more will be offered to guests of this institution. But the most important thing is, of course, the number of rooms. Given the low cost of living, you should not expect anything supernatural. But, given that this is one of the few hotels in the center of Ukhta, city guests should consider this option for accommodation.
