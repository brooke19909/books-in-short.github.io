Hammer "Interskol": reviews and characteristics of models

Craftsman people often have difficulty choosing a professional electric tool. This is largely due to the huge assortment of such products that are available on the shelves of construction and garden stores. In addition, many simply do not have the opportunity to pay for expensive models of equipment, so you have to be guided by the requirements of a tightly limited budget.



One of the cheapest and well-established is the Interskol rock drill. Reviews indicate that even at a low cost, this device fully copes with the tasks. Today we will consider the opinions of customers in more detail, as they can help you make a choice.

Positive feedback

Why is the Interskol rotary hammer so good? Reviews immediately indicate its low cost: for 1800-2000 rubles, you get a good technique that will help to make repairs or build something. As experienced craftsmen say, for the money you will still not find anything better.

How are you doing with ergonomics?

Oddly enough, but many buyers do not think at all about how convenient the purchased tool will be. But in vain, because not only simplicity depends on this, but also the safety of its use. Let's deal with the most important points.

The basic requirements for the handle are very simple. First of all, it should be comfortable to hold in your hands. It is best to choose a hammer drill in which the handle is located at an angle of 70 degrees to the axis of the drill. Even better, when it is rubberized: in this case, the product will not slip in sweating hands. Most of the Interskol models fully comply with these requirements. Buyers believe that the handle of an average rotary hammer is convenient, it does not slip out of the hands.

Other useful options

It should be noted that a stroke limiter (emphasis) can be very useful. This device allows you to press the tool as tightly as possible to the material being cut. Given the adjustment, you can get a hole exactly the desired depth. If the emphasis is turned, then using a punch with the appropriate nozzle, it is easy to create blanks of almost any shape and size.





In this regard, the tools from Interskol are pretty good. Buyers say that their handle is quite oversized, it is relatively convenient to hold on to it. Only a certain roughness of the coating causes complaints: without normal gloves, you won‚Äôt be able to work for a long time.

What other parameters are characterized by the Interskol puncher? Reviews say that even professional models are not expensive, therefore, are available to almost everyone. But is it worth it to buy such equipment?

Professional or household?

Like all power tools, rotary hammers can be divided into three main types: professional, semi-professional and domestic. Owners of private homes in most cases will be quite enough a standard household model. This choice is largely justified by the low cost of such models.



Semi-professional models have a slightly higher power, as well as a much greater resource. Unlike ordinary household varieties, they can work for 3-4 hours in a row, so they are ideal for those who work part-time in apartment renovation or construction. Such models cost from six thousand rubles (for Interskol such a price is very high).

Professional rotary hammers are distinguished by a large number of various additional functions and an extended resource. Their acquisition is advisable only for those who are professionally engaged in woodwork or repair. Otherwise, you just wasting your money. Such devices cost from 9-10 thousand. In the kit often there are several interchangeable nozzles. Masters say that such a punch replaces several tools at once, practical and quite reliable.

Why is the Interskol rotary hammer bad? Reviews say that the company's models have a negative side.

Negative points

Of course, not without negative responses. Most often they are associated with the country of the tool manufacturer - China. Buyers have repeatedly noted that there is a rapid wear of gearbox sprockets, and obviously not very high-quality plastic is used to make the case. It often cracks, and the smell from it is sometimes not very pleasant.

It happens that claims are associated with the high weight of some models: taking into account strong vibration and recoil during shock mode, holding them in hands can be very difficult.
