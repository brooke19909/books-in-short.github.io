Sea tour in Poland. The beaches of Poland. Poland, Baltic Sea - rest

If you have a map of Poland on hand, then you can easily notice that the entire northern part of this country is washed by the waters of the Baltic Sea. So, the length of the coastline of this European state is more than five hundred kilometers. It abounds in pristine beaches, rivers and lakes, picturesque hills. In addition, there is all the necessary infrastructure for an excellent pastime. Thus, a seaside vacation in Poland will be a great vacation option. About what resorts and entertainment can be found here, we will talk further.

Poland (Baltic Sea) - Holidays: a list of resorts

The coast of this country is replete with many small towns offering tourists an unforgettable vacation. The hotel base in local resorts is represented by both completely new modern complexes and reconstructed hotels built several decades ago. In this regard, you will be able to choose housing for yourself in accordance with your preferences and financial capabilities. The Polish resorts on the sea offer tourists not only a beach holiday, but also a varied entertainment and sightseeing program. Indeed, in addition to well-developed infrastructure, there are a number of historical and cultural attractions.

So, if you are planning a seaside vacation in Poland, then keep in mind that the most popular resorts in this country are the following: Krynica Morska, Leba, Kolobrzeg, Swinoujscie, Miedzyzdroje, Sopot and others. We suggest that you learn more about the main ones.





Krynica Morska

The main wealth of this Polish seaside resort is nature. It boasts magnificent sandy beaches, picturesque forests with rich flora and fauna. In addition, in the city of Krynica Morska there are all conditions for an excellent holiday for fans of windsurfing and fishing. In addition, the resort has several sources of mineral healing water. Despite the fact that Krynica Morska is one of the most popular resorts in Poland, it is never too crowded here. The local population is only one and a half thousand people.

Entertainment offered to tourists in the resort of Krynica Morska

Holidays in this place will not disappoint both lovers of beaches and fans of antiquity. So, the beach infrastructure in this resort is very well developed. However, if you are planning a seaside vacation in Poland, then keep in mind that the climate here is quite warm and mild in winter and non-hot in summer. Nevertheless, the weather conditions are comfortable enough for a beach holiday. In addition, the ecological situation in this place, due to the cleanest air and water, as well as pine and beech forests, is unlikely to leave anyone indifferent. Also, an interesting fact about the nature of the resort of Krynica-Morska can be considered the presence of unique animals and plants, which are almost impossible to meet in other parts of our planet. For example, here you can often observe on the beach a whole family of wild boars that are completely not afraid of humans. However, to come close to them is still not recommended: it is best to take some photos from afar.





Forehead

If you are going to the sea in Poland, then be sure to consider as an option one of the most popular local resort towns called eba. It is located in the central part of the coast at the mouth of the river of the same name. This resort is the best suited for outdoor enthusiasts, because all the conditions for cycling, horse riding and windsurfing are created here. In addition, eba is the starting point for exploring the Slovinsky National Park.

Local beaches

The coastal part of Leba is divided into three zones: beaches A, B and C. They are all wide, sandy and very clean. Beach A is a protected area and is considered the most popular in the city. There is a developed infrastructure: cafes, bars, restaurants, shops, jet ski, boat rental, etc. Beach C is a continuation of Beach A. However, its territory is not guarded, and there is not much entertainment here. In this regard, in this place there are fewer vacationers, which people who prefer a quiet and quiet pastime will definitely appreciate it. As for beach B, it is located a bit off from the center of eba. Its territory is protected. Here you can find a number of entertainments, including a children's and volleyball courts, a trampoline and more.





Attractions and attractions in Leba

In addition to excellent beaches, this place also boasts a number of natural and architectural attractions. Among them, it is worth highlighting the Church of St. Nicholas, considered here as the patron saint of sailors and fishermen. She is known for the icon of the Mother of God stored in her, authored by the German Max Pechstein. Walking around the town, you can see the historic building, built at the beginning of the last century, in shape resembling a medieval castle. It is located on Nadmorskaya street, and today it houses the Neptune Hotel. Also near the town there is a nature reserve famous for its dunes and beaches.

Resort Miedzyzdroje

If you are considering a seaside vacation in Poland, then you will probably be interested in a place with the unpronounceable name - Miedzyzdroje. This town is located in the western part of the Polish Baltic Sea coast. Numerous tourists are attracted by a well-developed resort infrastructure and the beauty of the national natural park, as well as sources of mineral waters and a healing microclimate. By the way, in local sanatoriums they successfully treat diseases of the upper respiratory tract and lungs, including asthma.





The main attraction of Miedzyzdroje is the Volinsky National Park, which is one of the few corners of untouched wildlife. Please note that walking here unaccompanied by a guide is highly discouraged, as there is a high probability of meeting with wild animals. Other local natural attractions include the postglacial lakes, as well as the bison reserve.





Swinoujscie

This resort town with a funny name is located on the Baltic Sea near the border of Poland with Germany. It boasts a large number of modern hotels and holiday homes. However, his real pride is the spa and health park, founded back in the early 19th century. In addition, the sanatoriums and hospitals in winoujcie specialize in the treatment of diseases of the respiratory tract, cardiovascular system and metabolic disorders.

The beaches of Poland in general and the local coast in particular are very much appreciated by vacationers. After all, they are very wide. They do not have large pebbles, but extremely small and pleasant sand.





Poland - sea holidays: prices

It should be noted that our compatriots are frequent guests in this country, not only due to the fact that there are all conditions for a comfortable pastime, but also due to the rather affordable cost of trips. In addition, if you live in the European part of Russia, the road will cost you also quite inexpensively. And if you have a desire to spend your holidays with chic in a luxurious five-star hotel, then in this case you should take a closer look at the Polish coast of the Baltic Sea. After all, here every tourist will be able to find leisure options for himself, depending on both his preferences and financial capabilities. As for the price-quality ratio, in Poland it is optimal. In general, it is easy to choose housing on the coast from 1,000 to 30,000 rubles per night.

So, today we learned that one of the places for a great vacation is Poland. The Baltic Sea, a holiday on which is considered one of the most accessible in all of Europe, offers tourists a wide variety of entertainment: from magnificent beaches and healing mineral springs to interesting excursions to historical sights and walks to protected places with unique nature and microclimate.
