Hotel Cassia Phuket 4 * Thailand, Phuket: overview, description and reviews of tourists

But each tourist first looks for a hotel in which he will live during his vacation. Someone wants a budget option more economical. Location

This modern hotel, opened last year, is located in close proximity to Bang Tao Beach, which stretches for 15 kilometers in a city called Chong Thale. A good place. Not only because of the beach, but also because the Phuket Airport can be reached in 20 minutes. By the way, Patong is also quite close - only 15 kilometers away.

But I would like to talk about the beach a little more. Bang Tao is located in the bay, which means that there are almost always no waves. The beach itself is the realm of the purest golden sand. And the sea is clear and shallow. There are no noisy entertainment on the beach. So this is an ideal place for lovers of a quiet and relaxing holiday.

What is worth knowing?

Speaking about Cassia Phuket 4 * (Phuket, Bang Tao Beach), one can not fail to note several important nuances regarding accommodation and booking.

So, check-in here begins at three in the afternoon. And this is worth considering when buying airline tickets. Early check-in is possible, but only if the apartments ordered by the guests have already been freed from previous guests and prepared by the maids for check-in. And this happens only out of season, and then not always. Therefore, it is better not to take risks if you do not want to wait. And departure starts at 10:00 and lasts until noon.

In Cassia Phuket 4 * (Thailand, Phuket) you can live with children of any age. But extra beds are not available - you should know about it. In principle, there are plenty of sleeping places in the apartments.
