So, when the craftswoman decides on the choice of a pattern for her product, she can begin the process itself. Knitting berets with knitting needles starts from the bottom, with a rim that will hold the hat on the head. Therefore, the rim should fit snugly to the head. They knit it either with an ordinary elastic band or with a double elastic band, into which you can later insert a cord or elastic band.

Then you need to increase the number of loops so that the beret acquires the required volume. After knitting as many rows as necessary in height, they begin to reduce the number of loops, knitting two loops together.
