Hosta, boarding house "Aquamarine": description, photos, services, reviews of vacationers

The small resort village of Khosta today has become one of the Sochi microdistricts. It is located between central Sochi and Adler, in the picturesque territory of the national park. From the center of Sochi to the resort village - only twenty kilometers. Lovers of a combination of beach holidays and entertainment of a big city come here, as well as visiting natural and historical sights.



Resort Description

Hosta is located in the green valley of Khostinsky Bay, surrounded by mountains. The climate here is subtropical, hot. Thanks to the location of the village in the natural hollow, it is always noticeably warmer here than in the center of Sochi. But thanks to the sea breeze, refreshing the air, the heat does not seem so exhausting.

The average air temperature in July is +27 ¬∞ C, and the water warms up to +25 ¬∞ C. The swimming season is quite long - from late May to late October. For the rest of the time, hostas and hostels are not empty, because this is one of the best and most popular balneological resorts in our country.

In addition, the Host is often called a subtropical paradise. The unusually beautiful lush vegetation delights everyone who comes to the resort: fragrant magnolias, magnificent boxwoods bloom around, cypress trees, unusual cork oaks, exotic palm trees rise.



Many resorts and hotels, hotels and pensions have Hosta on their territory. Pension "Aquamarine" is one of the most visited. What he liked the vacationers so much, you will find out by reading this article.

Sochi, Hosta: boarding house "Aquamarine"

This cozy pension is located fifty meters from the pebble beaches in the lower reaches of the Hosta River. It is adjacent to the Matsesta health resort, the Volna health resort. You can see the photo of the Aquamarine boarding house in Khost below. It occupies a six-story building in a park area, which is surrounded by a huge number of centuries-old trees.

This is a modern holiday home, ideal for everyone who needs treatment using physical and balneotherapy methods, as two hundred meters away is the famous Matsesta hospital, one of the best in Russia. But in order to receive a course of medical procedures, the guest must have a resort card. Pension "Aquamarine" in Khost gladly accepts guests with children from four years.

Services

Pension "Aquamarine" in Khost is famous for its impeccable service and functional infrastructure. Each vacationer can visit the bars and cafes located on the territory, use the rental and rental of water equipment. At Aquamarine you can go swimming in the indoor freshwater pool or use the sauna.
