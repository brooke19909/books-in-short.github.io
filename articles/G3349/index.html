Several reasons to visit the water park of Kazan

Most people love water, warmth and the ability to swim. Unfortunately, the climate of our country is severe, and most of the year these pleasures are not available to us. The opportunity to go on vacation to the warm regions appears no more than twice a year, but what to do? There is one answer to this question: go to the water park! After all, this is a unique opportunity at any time of the year and in any weather to enjoy warm water and comfortable air temperature, relaxing or having fun riding on a fun slide.



The water park of Kazan "Kazan Riviera" is one of the largest not only in Russia but also in Europe. This unique island of entertainment at any time of the year awaits all lovers of splashing in warm water. Great relaxation for the whole family, positive emotions, exciting sensations or complete relaxation - all this is available here.

In summer, the water park of Kazan is a real resort! At this time, rides and open-air pools open here, where on a deck chair, basking in the warm sun, it remains only to imagine the cries of seagulls to travel to the sea. Moreover, just a stone's throw away are establishments with tasty and wholesome food, where you can satisfy your hunger, and then continue your vacation.



So, there were several reasons to visit the water park (Kazan) at once. Reviews about him speak of excellent opportunities for recreation for people of different ages. Here everyone will find something to their liking.

Ticket price

The water park of Kazan is located at Fatykha Amirkhan Avenue, Building 1. You can visit this corner of fun and water adventures every day from 10 am to 10 pm. On Saturday and public holidays, visitors are expected an hour earlier, from 9 a.m. to 11 p.m.

An affordable vacation for the whole family is offered by the Riviera water park in Kazan. Ticket prices start at 450 rubles for adults and from 250 rubles for children. Depending on the time of visit and holidays or weekends, the cost may increase.

The water park in Kazan constantly cares about its visitors and offers a variety of promotions that make the rest even more affordable and enjoyable.
