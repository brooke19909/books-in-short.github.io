Hotel Alkoclar Adakule Hotel 5 * (Turkey, Kusadasi): traveler reviews

Many of our compatriots have long mastered the most famous resorts in Turkey and consider this country almost native. And why be surprised, because here Russian tourists are always expected and received with truly oriental hospitality. Therefore, even a middle-income family can, without hesitation, name three or four Turkish hotels that they visited. However, most of these hotel complexes are of the same type and practically do not differ from each other. If you want to see something unusual and live in a picturesque secluded place, then we bring to your attention the Alkoclar Adakule Hotel 5 * hotel (Turkey) in Kusadasi. This hotel complex literally at first glance impresses tourists with its size and incredible beauty. It raises a lot of questions among our compatriots, so today we have devoted the whole article to Alkoclar Adakule Hotel 5 *.





A couple or more words about the hotel

Alkoclar Adakule Hotel 5 * is located between two rocks in a cozy bay on the shores of the Aegean Sea. It was conceived by architects in such a way as to climb terraces from the coast above into the mountains. Because of this feature, there are a lot of stairs in the hotel complex, and the descent to the beach is even equipped with a special elevator. This feature is a real highlight of the hotel, however, elderly people should not buy a tourist ticket to this unusual hotel complex. Reviews of Alkoclar Adakule Hotel 5 * contain information that after a couple of days of regular walking up the stairs, some people have deteriorated physical condition. Tourists with cardiovascular problems and diseases of the musculoskeletal system should be especially careful. But for others, Alkoclar Adakule Hotel 5 * is a real exotic paradise with all the advantages arising from this epithet.

The hotel was built in the early nineties of the last century, three years ago, serious renovation work was carried out here. Therefore, now tourists can not be afraid of dilapidated furniture in rooms and outdated design, which is often mentioned in reviews five years ago.

The hotel complex consists of two multi-storey buildings. The first was built in the last century and consists of seven floors, and the second refers to newer buildings and has six floors. In two buildings, three hundred and twenty-nine rooms have different categories. In Alkoclar Adakule Hotel 5 * you can choose very simple apartments, where it is convenient to accommodate only a family of three people. And if the guest wishes, the hotel will provide you with a truly royal room, worthy of a person of blue blood.

Hotel Location

Alkoclar Adakule Hotel 5 * is located on the first coastline and has its own beach area. Hotel guests can enjoy the gentle waters of the Aegean Sea on a secluded beach, inaccessible to prying eyes.

The exact address of Alkoclar Adakule Hotel (Turkey) is Kusadasi. This village has many attractions that tourists enjoy visiting as part of an excursion group or on their own. For example, at fifteen kilometers from the hotel is Ephesus, and twenty-three kilometers is the famous house of the Virgin Mary.

It is noteworthy that despite the apparent seclusion of the hotel complex, it is located only five minutes walk from the city center, where many nightclubs, restaurants and shopping centers have been built.

In order to get to the coast from the main building of Alkoclar Adakule Hotel 5 *, you need to overcome about eighty meters. Moreover, in order to go down to the sea, you do not need to walk along the numerous steps. For the convenience of its guests, the hotel provides an elevator, so the road to the beach and back turns into almost lightning-fast movement.

By the minuses of the location of the hotel complex, our compatriots attribute its remoteness from international airports. The fact is that airliners from Russia can land in Bodrum and Izmir. It depends on the travel company that sold the ticket to tourists. That's only travelers from Bodrum will have to get a few hours, and Izmir is about eighty kilometers from the hotel. This is about forty minutes of travel. Therefore, when buying a tour, be sure to specify such nuances.
