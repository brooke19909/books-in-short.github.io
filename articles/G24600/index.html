How to weave bracelets from floss thread? Weaving patterns and lessons

Do you like to make various decorations with your own hands? The handmade style is now popular in all possible industries where creativity is applied. Read about how to weave bracelets from floss thread . You will learn a lot of interesting things. Useful tips and tricks will allow you to quickly master the technique, even if you have not done anything similar before.





What is required

If you decide to learn how to weave bracelets from floss, it is worth preparing for practical work in advance. You need to have the following:

  • threads of different shades;
  • scissors;
  • a pin or other fastener.




The colors of the threads combine the most different. The minimum number of tones is two. From one shade it is not very interesting to weave a bracelet, since its main beauty is precisely in the pattern that the nodules form. You can use not only the floss, but also other types, such as yarn for knitting. Mouline thread is most often taken for weaving, since the skeins are made very bright, and the color scheme allows you to choose a variety of shades. In addition, the threads are thin, so the nodules are neat. Also very effective can be a bracelet on a fork made of rubber bands. When you master the technology of creating baubles of thread and yarn, it will not be difficult to move to a more modern material.

Design and weaving methods

Before you begin to learn how to weave bracelets from floss, it is worth familiarizing yourself with the possibilities of this type of creative activity. These jewelry made of threads is also called friendship bracelets, since it was originally decided to give them to friends. Accordingly, the products had to carry certain information about the owner, donor or their relationship. Now the sacredness of things is largely lost and many make them as jewelry for themselves. However, patterns created on bracelets, also called baubles, are usually chosen by chance. In part, they are determined by the experience of the needlewoman and the available thread colors or patterns.

There are two ways to weave:

  1. Slanting.
  2. Direct.

The first method is simpler and is used by most. Ornaments are usually created from knots going one after another diagonally in different directions. Often chosen motives are rhombuses, stripes, "Christmas trees".

Direct weaving is more complicated, but it allows you to create original ornaments, including writing a specific text in the bracelet. Thanks to this feature, direct weaving is popular. Usually weave names, initials or phrases testifying to love, friendship.





In this case, the thread that forms the background is most often present. It should be much longer. Threads of a picture or text braid the main tone.

Thread Attachment Options

In order to understand how to make a bracelet of thread with your own hands quickly and correctly, you should master the basic types of knots. And also make the work as convenient as possible. To do this, it is important to securely fasten the threads before weaving. You can do this in the following ways:

  • on a pin;
  • clamp;
  • scotch tape;
  • on a special tablet.

First of all, you can tie all the threads into a knot so that they do not bloom, and then pin them with a pin to the fabric on a hard surface, such as a tablecloth. In the case of a clip or tape, it is enough to fix the ends of the threads even without a knot. You just need to evenly distribute them at a suitable distance and stick them with tape to a table or sheet of plastic. The clip is convenient to fix on a book. When using a tablet for needlework, it is convenient to make the necessary notes on its surface during work. The method of fixing the working threads, choose the one that seems most convenient to you.

As for fixing the threads after weaving, it‚Äôs enough to make, for example, a pigtail or two, and then fasten with a knot or tie a knot immediately without pigtails, leaving an element similar to a brush. You can make the same elements of the bracelet tie on both sides or provide a clasp.

If this is the first time you have decided to try your hand at jewelry making, you can start with the simplest method, where no patterns are required. The bracelet is easy to weave in the form of an ordinary pigtail, as you once did a doll. Take three threads of different shades or six, connecting in pairs, and weave an ordinary pigtail. A little more complicated, but also without much difficulty, you can make an element of four threads.

To understand how to make a bracelet of thread with your own hands using knots and getting beautiful patterns, you need to learn the basic ways to create these knots, as well as learn to read jewelry patterns. All templates use a uniform notation. The plate is shown in the following photo.

How to choose the number of threads and their length

If you have chosen the bracelets scheme with which you will work, and understand how the product will be performed, you can go on to practice. First of all, count how many threads you need. Schemes are found with even and odd numbers. Moreover, there are options that are designed specifically for even or odd numbers. So, if the width of the bracelet on the template does not suit you, to increase it you will have to add the number of threads in pairs left and right one at a time.

As for the length of the threads, it is not always possible to accurately determine it immediately. It depends on the size of the bracelet, that is, the thickness of the wrist and the width of the hand, if the jewelry does not have a fastener. If you apply patterns for oblique weaving and all threads are used uniformly, the initial length is usually determined from the calculation of the length of the finished product, multiplied by four. In general, it is better to cut off immediately with a margin, so that at the end of work you can tie a neat little knot and weave a pigtail as a completion.

If you run out of thread in the middle of work or breaks, do not be discouraged. Take a new one of the same color and continue to work in the right place, leaving the beginning of the thread from the wrong side. After several knots are done, turn the bracelet inside out and tie the beginning of a new thread to the end of the first. Cut excess. As a result, the connection node will turn out to be inconspicuous.

Where to begin

Before you master the complex patterns of bracelets, you should practice in creating knots of different types. You can create the simplest bracelet based on the knot No. 1 or No. 2 from the table of symbols. In this case, you will not have a specific pattern, just get alternating slanting strips of different colors, directed from left to right or vice versa.

To make a simple bracelet, the surface of which is already similar to the drawing, use the following illustration. Weaving always starts with the leftmost thread. In a pattern symmetrical with respect to the center, the left thread is used exactly to the middle, then the work starts flipped with the extreme right. The threads that have become central are connected together by an ordinary knot, and the work begins to be repeated again symmetrically, guided by the drawing.





How to weave a bracelet from threads for beginners

Here is the sequence of work when making decorations with a herringbone pattern. Actions will be as follows:

1. Cut the desired even number of threads of approximately the same length and several shades of paired colors. Tie the elements in a knot and fasten on a pin so that the threads are distributed symmetrically relative to the center according to the shades, that is, the pairs of extreme, second, etc. to the middle will be the same.





2. Start work with the leftmost thread. Put it in the form of a four on the second on the left and tie a knot. You can perform two nodes. Repeat the same procedure on the right side with two extreme threads.





3. Make such knots from the working thread and the next to the center also symmetrically on both sides.





4. Do the same with center threads. As a result, those elements that were originally extreme became average.





5. Tie a regular knot into the threads that are now in the middle. The first element of the Christmas tree is ready.





6. Repeat the entire weaving sequence listed above only using yarns that have become outermost (initially they were second).





7. Repeat all the steps with all the remaining pairs of threads, until the extreme ones again become those that were in these places initially.





8. Continue weaving in the same way to the desired length. Fasten the threads and the decoration is done. These simple bracelets made of thread can be made quickly with your own hands.

Having mastered the weaving technique in this way, you can create simple patterns yourself. It is enough to use a different number of threads, differing combinations of pairs, for example, not four different colors, but only two, that is, on the edges in the previous sample, place the blue as it is, the second pink, and then repeat the sequence of these colors in the same combination again . Get a different picture. Also, the ‚ÄúChristmas tree‚Äù shown above can be blue on the left, pink on the right, etc. Only due to different color combinations it is easy to get a very large number of patterns with one technology.
