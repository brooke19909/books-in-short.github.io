Guest house "Aphrodite", Divnomorskoe: review, description and reviews

The village Divnomorskoye, located near the city of Gelendzhik, is quite popular among domestic tourists. Here, vacationers are waiting for comfortable pebble beaches, a warm sea and, of course, a lot of various entertainments. The tourist infrastructure in Divnomorskoye is developed, unfortunately, not as good as in Gelendzhik itself. However, there are all sorts of services here, of course, a little cheaper. This concerns, of course, and rented out housing in the village. For example, very inexpensive rooms in Divnomorskoye can be rented at the Aphrodite Hotel.

Location Features

The guest house "Aphrodite" (Divnomorskoe) is located on the second coastline - 350 meters from the sea. The hotel was built on a quiet, cozy street near the resort center. The guests of the complex can spend time on its territory and in the rooms, in almost complete silence. At the same time, all the amenities of the village are within walking distance from the hotel.





The exact address of the complex: guest house "Aphrodite", Divnomorskoe, st. Black Sea, 9a. Vacationers who have booked rooms at this hotel usually fly by plane to Gelendzhik Airport, or they come to this city by bus from Anapa or Krasnodar. Getting to Divnomorsky from the city is easy by public transport. From the airport in Gelendzhik bus number 5 goes. Tourists who have booked rooms at the Aphrodite Hotel need to go out at the Old Bus Station. Further, vacationers should transfer to bus number 108 or 102. If desired, tourists have the opportunity to book a transfer in the complex itself.

Hotel Description (General)

Aphrodite Guest House (Divnomorskoe) welcomes guests in comfortable three-story buildings of modern attractive architecture with large balconies. The hotel area is well landscaped and landscaped. On all sides, the hotel courtyard is surrounded by a reliable fence. Only the guests themselves can enter the hotel territory.

The checkout time in this complex is 12 hours. When checking in, guests need to show only a passport. Smoking is not allowed in the hotel rooms. But at the same time, tourists are allowed to bring small pets with them to the hotel. Hotel "Aphrodite" takes guests year-round.

Number of rooms

If desired, guests of Divnomorsky can rent a standard room, a suite or a junior suite in this hotel. The rooms handed over at the hotel are designed to accommodate tourists, both those who come to rest with friends and families. All hotel rooms are equipped with:

  • TV
  • a refrigerator;
  • split system;
  • wardrobe;
  • a table with chairs.




Some rooms have, among other things, comfortable soft corners.

Hotel Infrastructure

On the territory of the hotel "Aphrodite" as well as in its rooms, for tourists there are many possible amenities. For example, hotel guests can use the barbecue grills installed in the courtyard of the hotel. Also on site there is a large terrace, designed to relax residents, and a gazebo, surrounded on all sides by tropical greenery. Guests can cook breakfast, lunch or dinner in the summer kitchen of the complex. Here, for the convenience of guests, all the necessary equipment is provided. Of course, tenants can use pots, pans, knives, spoons, etc. in the summer kitchen .
