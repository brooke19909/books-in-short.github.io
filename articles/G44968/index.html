How much is a PS3? PS3 prefix - price

After the success of Sony PlayStation and PlayStation 2 game consoles, released in 1994 and 2000, respectively, the appearance of a new model seemed only a matter of time. The first rumors about a new game console appeared in 2004, but until the spring of 2005, there was no information from the Japanese manufacturer.

A game console called PS3 was officially unveiled in May 2005 at the Electronic Entertainment Expo. A year later, the Sony company told about how much the PS3 costs, and about the start date for the sale of the console. For the first time for testing, a product from a Japanese manufacturer was presented at TGS 2006, where visitors had the opportunity to evaluate several games on the new console.

Description of the prefix from Sony

It is worth saying that the PS3 looks gorgeous: exquisite design, comfortable joystick, excellent features - what else is needed for an interesting pastime for your favorite video games?



At the heart of this device is the Cell processor - a common development by Sony, IBM and Toshiba, operating at a frequency of 3.2 GHz. As a graphics adapter in the Japanese-made game console, RSX comes from the G70 architecture, which was developed by NVidia specifically for the GeForce 7 line of video cards. This video chip operates at a frequency of 550 MHz, while it has 256 MB of memory . This allows him to display 2 high-quality HDTV streams simultaneously.

Dimensions of the game console from the Sony company are quite large - 3.25 by 2.74 by 0.98 centimeters with a device weight of 5 kilograms. As you can see, the third part of the most popular console turned out to be much more than its predecessor in the person of PlayStation 2. The noise level in the PS3 is approximately the same as the previous model of the Sony game console. The desire of the creators to reduce it led to the fact that the device is quite hot, although this, it would seem, is not dangerous and can not lead to anything bad. However, it is better not to put the prefix in closed small niches, since it can overheat.
