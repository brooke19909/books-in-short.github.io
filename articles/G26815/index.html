How to make the effect of an old film in a few seconds?

Recently, the popularity of photo processing in the style of the 80-90s has grown. Not so long ago, in order to do this, you would need special equipment or a professional expensive editor. Many people wonder how to make the effect of an old film quickly and without additional equipment. Introducing the top best programs for vintage photo and video processing on different devices.

IOS apps

Below is a list of the most popular phone apps that will help you achieve the effect of an old iOS-based film:

  1. VHS Camcorder (paid). Most recently, a new application for the iOS platform has been released, which allows you to create video in a retro style. The shooting date, stripes on the screen, dim and muted colors, film effect - all this can be observed in this application. As a result, the video size will be 4: 3, which is why it is recommended to shoot in a horizontal position. Changing sound (hissing film) is another distinguishing feature of this application.
  2. Camcorder (free). This application is similar in its parameters to the previous one. But due to the fact that it is free, it has restrictions on the use of certain functions. For example: you cannot remove the time and date on the video, there are no additional filters.
  3. Chromic (free). The easiest to use application. It is only necessary to unload the video from the gallery and apply one of the proposed filters. During processing, you can change the degree of intensity, contrast and exposure. You can use the effect of the old film and all kinds of old frames.

Android apps

List of the best Android apps:

  1. Vintage Retro Camera + VHS (paid). This program presents as many as 35 effects that turn photos and videos into a vintage picture. In addition, in the application, you can optionally change the date and time, cut out the sound, lay down the music and directly share it with your friends on Instagram.
  2. Afterlight (free). The application contains many interesting features. You can, for example, use filters, of which 59 pieces, textures, including the effect of an old film strip, cropping and cropping, 15 control tools (contrast, exposure, sharpness, etc.). It is presented on Android for free, and for iOS for a fee.
  3. Snapseed (free). This is one of the most popular editors developed for Android and iOS. The possibilities of this application are endless. Filters, 29 regulatory tools, vintage textures and more can be found in this program.

Online for computer

Introducing the top 3 best online processing services:

  1. VintageJS (free). This online application allows you to turn your photo into a retro image in a few seconds, which was as if taken with a film camera.
  2. Wanokoto (free). This service presents only the effect of the old film, but in different versions. A different preset has a different finish on the photo.
  3. AnyMaking (free). This online service contains a large number of filters, tools with which you can create retro photos.
