Small pillows with cargo transport originally look. Pillowcases are made as follows.



  • Cut two squares of cotton according to the size of the pillow (consider the volume, allowances, place for the zipper).

Application "car"

Whoever owns the hook can knit any model. Knitted applique will decorate a scarf, hat, plaid, rug. To link a car, dial a chain of thirteen loops. In the first row, starting with the third loop, knit eleven double crochets.



The next row is increased by three columns (increase from the side of the hood). The third save unchanged. If when knitting the thread turned out to be from the trunk, go through the product with connecting posts, and then go to the fourth row.

In the fourth row, the hood is knitted (five columns), for the window, type five loops, backing three columns, knit two columns with two crochets, for the second window, type the same chain and in the last two columns of the third row, knit two columns with two crochets and one vertex .

The last row is a crochet of the entire application with single crochet columns. Next, knit round wheels. First give shape to the car (if necessary, iron the edges through gauze), then sew the wheels.

Knitted Appliques

Please note that thick yarn will make a volume applique. A machine made of thin yarn will keep its shape if you knit simple columns with knit loops, that is, the thread is knitted with a column and a relief is created.



If you make a plaid blanket, then for applications, take soft yarn, for rugs you need dense blanks. First you make cars, then you knit colored squares. Distribute blanks (photograph all options, then choose the best).

Then sew appliques onto the squares according to the pattern of the product. Next, tie all the elements with the main color, connect with each other. The resulting mat is tied with multicolored columns or sewed on braid.



Such applications can be used in a development book or on a rug, supplemented with other elements. Then the cars will be removable. And they are attached to the base with adhesive tape, buttons, ropes, buttons, zippers. They can be made convex by adding filler.
