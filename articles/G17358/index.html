Where is the fabulous residence

The exact address of Belovezhskaya Pushcha (Santa Claus) is 225063 Kamenyuki, Kamenetsky district, Brest region, Republic of Belarus. I chose a fabulous Grandfather for a house to match. The primeval, untouched nature of the Pushcha is already a gift to humanity, which must be protected and protected. The vast territory of the forest falls on the border with Poland. This is a unique place, the only one in the entire territory of the former Soviet Union, with centuries-old trees and many forest inhabitants living throughout the largest European national park.

The residence of Santa Claus in Belovezhskaya Pushcha is located in a protected forest, in the largest meadow, where a huge spruce 40 meters tall grows. Bison used to come here to feed, and perhaps now they drop in when there are not too many tourists. The territory of the reserve is protected so that forest animals are disturbed as little as possible.



How to get to visit fabulous characters

Since the residence of Santa Claus in Belovezhskaya Pushcha is located on the territory of the protected reserve, you can only come with a guided tour. It is impossible to pass independently and breathe air. However, such a schedule does not cause much inconvenience, because the residence works all year round, daily from 9:00 to 18:00. On weekends, this time is extended to 20:00. Do not forget that the place here is quiet, reserved, and you need to behave appropriately.

An organized excursion with delivery can be ordered in Minsk, but the journey is far and you will get tired on the road. From the capital to the forest about 5 hours drive, then a walk and the return trip. It is much better to get to Brest, have a rest here and book an excursion. About an hour or a half on the way - and you will be there. The most comfortable option is to get to Belovezhskaya Pushcha by personal transport and stay at a hotel in Kamenyuki. Having rested from the road, you can go to the Pushcha and buy bus tickets to the residence on the spot. Departure here at 11:00, 13:30 and 16:30. On weekends more often. If you arrived at the wrong time, you can easily pass the few hours by taking a walk in the park or renting a bike. After all, before you is the famous Belovezhskaya Pushcha, the estate of Santa Claus will wait a bit. A delightful experience is guaranteed.
