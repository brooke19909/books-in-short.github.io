5-star hotels in St. Petersburg: an overview of the best hotels, addresses, room descriptions, services, photos and reviews

The northern capital does not belong to world-class resorts, but this does not prevent it from hosting thousands of tourists from all over the world every year. People come here not for the warm sea, white sand and tropical climate. Peter attracts cultural gourmets, for whom museums and ancient architecture are more important than the sea coast. Hotels in St. Petersburg (5 stars) offer a European level of service, which is confirmed by numerous reviews of guests and tourists.

Hotel Corinthia

If the financial situation allows you not to save on comfort and service, we advise you to pay attention to the hotel in St. Petersburg (5 stars), which is located in the heart of the city on Nevsky Prospekt. In an old building, the bulk of which was built back in the 19th century, guests are always welcome. The hotel is famous for the fact that in one of its buildings the Samoilov dynasty has lived for more than half a century. Today in the hotel a museum was created in their honor, which consists of 2 rooms. It hosts creative evenings, exhibitions of personal items of ballet stars such as Ulanova and Diaghilev, household items of that time.

What does this hotel in St. Petersburg offer tourists , 5 stars really justify themselves? The hotel consists of more than 350 rooms, from which everyone can choose the right option for themselves. The cheapest room will cost the guest 15,300 rubles per night. For this money he will be given a room of 27 square meters. meters, equipped with solid furniture and equipped with all the technical devices for a comfortable stay. Individual bathroom, located in the room. Slippers, a bathrobe and all necessary toiletries are provided.

Additional services

If you travel with animals, the hotel will give you the opportunity not to part with your pet, even on vacation. To do this, you must first warn about your desire and pay extra for this service.

It is possible to rent a room in this hotel with breakfast, it will cost a little more and will cost 16,600 rubles. per day. Opinion of the guests

Studying the guest reviews of this hotel in the center of St. Petersburg (5 stars), we can conclude that the institution almost completely justifies its "star" status. Among the advantages, guests note the convenient location of the hotel, the nearest metro station "Mayakovskaya" is only 4 minutes walk, perfect cleanliness in rooms and throughout, as well as attentiveness and high qualification of staff. Also, praise is given by those who appreciated the food in the restaurant, they say that they do not cook so tasty even in Italy itself.

As for the negative opinions, they concern only the high cost of living, but no one said that comfort should be cheap.

Boutique Hotel "Author"

Another hotel with breakfast (5 stars) in the center of St. Petersburg, which can compete with the previous institution, was previously called Golden Garden. It is located on 9 Vladimirsky pr., Just a 4-minute walk from the Dostoevskaya metro station and 800 meters from the city center.

Near the hotel are attractions and cultural institutions such as the Theater. Lensoveta, Vladimir Cathedral, Maly Drama Theater. And the Fontanka Embankment is just a 5-minute walk from the hotel.

Service and prices

This 5-star boutique hotel in St. Petersburg was well deserved. It offers 23 rooms, each of which is equipped with all necessary appliances, air conditioning, comfortable furniture and a private bathroom. Each room is individually designed. The cheapest option will cost a tourist 13,000 rubles, and the most expensive room costs 26,000 rubles per night.

Buffet breakfast, hygiene items and internet access are included. For an additional fee, the tourist can use the safe, babysitting service, conference room, rent a car and order an airport transfer.

In addition, the hotel has parking for guests with private vehicles, laundry and dry cleaning, a hairdresser and restaurants.

What tourists say

Those planning to rent an inexpensive hotel in the center of St. Petersburg (5 stars) with breakfast, say they have not regretted their choice. Negative reviews about this place could not be found, so you can safely recommend the hotel as one of the best in the Northern capital.

"Taleon Imperial Hotel"

Located on the Moika River Embankment, the hotel also belongs to the 5-star category. Admiralteyskaya Metro Station is a 3-minute walk, and the center of St. Petersburg is 900 meters away.

Superior double room will cost tourists 23,600 rubles per night. There is an opportunity to have breakfast right at the hotel, though you will have to pay extra for meals separately - 1950 rubles per person.

On the territory of the hotel there is parking for guests arriving by car, you do not need to pay separately for this service, it is included in the total price. For a comfortable stay, the hotel provides car rental, the ability to use a safe and a currency exchange office. By prior arrangement, the administration will provide a hypoallergenic room.

Guest reviews are mostly positive. In their reviews, they noted as an advantage the ban on smoking throughout the hotel, the rich selection of dishes in local restaurants and the cleanliness of the rooms. Tourists also liked the hotel's spa area and chic room decoration. Tourists, of course, complain about the high cost of living, but at heart each of them understands that high-quality service can not be cheap.
