"Agricultural Bank": reviews of professionals and customers

Russian Agricultural Bank OJSC was created by the Government of the Russian Federation in 2000. The shares of this financial institution are 100% state-owned. The initial specialization of this bank was financing various agricultural enterprises, but over time the list of its services has expanded significantly. Today, this financial institution is actively engaged in lending and accepting deposits from the population.



"Agricultural Bank", reviews of which you can meet the most diverse, offers its customers the following services:

  • salary cards and loans to various corporate clients ;
  • operations with precious metals and securities;
  • various currency transactions;
  • deposits, loans, plastic cards and other services for individuals.


The number of branches of this financial organization is about 1500. That is the number of bank branches in cities, villages and other settlements of the Russian Federation. In terms of the number of branches, the Rosselkhozbank bank takes the second place among all similar institutions in Russia. In addition, the credit organization has its representative offices in neighboring countries, namely: in Azerbaijan, Belarus, Tajikistan, Armenia and Kazakhstan.
