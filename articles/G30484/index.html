Choosing a mobile phone with a good camera

How are the products of that other company different from each other? What should I look for when choosing a camera phone ? What additional options will help to improve the quality of shooting, and which will be completely useless? These questions were asked by everyone who at least once thought about buying a camera phone. Indeed, on the shelves of most stores and digital technology stores, you can find many models with a built-in camera. The most popular among them are the iPhone 4S, Sony Xperia S, Samsung Galaxy and Nokia Lumia. Consider each instance in more detail.

The most famous among all this fraternity will probably be the iPhone 4S. Not only is this a phone with a good camera (8 megapixels), it also has a number of additional options to improve the quality of photos, and it takes great panoramic shots. True, the quality of the finished photos iPhone is slightly inferior to its competitors, but they can be edited directly on the device itself.



The direct competitor to the iPhone is, of course, the Samsung Galaxy. Apple fans even claim that Koreans simply stole design and technology from the Yabloko. Even so, they managed to bring them to perfection and create a completely new phone. Samsung Galaxy is equipped with LED-flash and boasts a number of additional features. For example, the ability to multi-capture and face recognition will be very useful. And this is in addition to the fact that it already has an 8-megapixel matrix.



The cheapest phone (with a good camera) of the mentioned models is the Sony Xperia S. But at the same time, it is in no way inferior to its competitors. After all, Sony is a true professional in creating cameras. Therefore, the phone is not only equipped with a powerful camera with LED flash and the ability to digital zoom, but it is also able to shoot in the dark with good resolution, thanks to the built-in matrix, which is also used in the "DSLRs" from Sony.



But the real stylish camera phone can be called Nokia Lumia. Its vibrant design immediately attracts attention. In addition, according to the developers, he has in his arsenal one of the fastest and brightest cameras. True, her resolution is more than standard for such models - 8 megapixels. But this is perfectly compensated by the presence of a double flash and optics from Carl Zeiss.

It can be argued that no phone with a good camera can replace the camera, but the reality is the opposite. Of course, for professional photos you need professional photographic equipment and individual settings. To an ordinary consumer, all these subtleties are not so important. Therefore, on the street, you can more and more often notice people filming their friends or capturing interesting moments just on the phone. Indeed, today the difference between photographs taken with the help of "soap dishes" and captured on a mobile phone is practically not visible.
