154 Separate Commandant Regiment of the Transfiguration

The 154th Separate Commandment of the Transfiguration Regiment is the formation of the Armed Forces of the Russian Federation. It is located in the capital of the country. Formation address: st. Krasnokazarmennaya, 1/4, Preobrazhensky Regiment, Moscow. Further in the article the history of formation, composition and strength will be covered in more detail. We also learn about the achievements of the Preobrazhensky Regiment, tasks, significance, awards, as well as events in which military personnel and the orchestra participated.



General information

The soldiers who make up the Preobrazhensky Regiment carry guard and garrison services. Their responsibilities include providing patrols, escorting convicted and remand prisoners of war, anti-terrorist activities in reinforcement, and the like. Also, the formation is responsible for events held with the participation of troops inside the garrison. It also participates in meetings and seeing off military and state delegations, and honors at funerals. The disposal of explosive hazardous facilities in the capital, excluding improvised explosive devices, is handled by a demining group, which is part of the 154 Preobrazhensky commandant regiment. Draftees who are sent to this unit for military service undergo a rigorous selection process. Necessarily they must have good physical training and growth not lower than a certain level. In their past, in addition, there should be no problems associated with law enforcement.



History of occurrence

In 1979, 154 regiment (Preobrazhensky) was founded. The 99th commandant battalion, as well as a guard of honor company, were included in its composition. The latter was created in 1944. Then she was included in the first regiment of the division of the NKVD of the USSR (special purpose). The guard of honor met many high-ranking guests. One of the first honors was Winston Churchill.

Reallocation of parts

On February 6, 1948, the 73rd Rifle Battalion was named military unit No. 01904, and the 465th Separate Rifle Company turned into a battalion reorganized in the spring of that year. The subsequent change of name occurred on November 3. Then she was renamed the Separate Commandant Company. The creation of the 99th commandant battalion was timed to April 10, 1979. It was formed in accordance with the instructions of the General Staff. The orchestra and the Guard of Honor were formed in 1956 on November twenty-ninth. They were directly subordinate to the military commandant of the capital of the Russian Federation. In 1960, a special dress uniform appeared at the guard of honor of all types of troops.

Activities and Achievements

In the garrison, due to the increased volume of tasks for guarding and garrison service, the instructions of the General Staff on the creation of the 154th Separate Commandant Regiment were approved. The 99th battalion and the guard company became the main units in it. According to the staff list , the composition of the formation was determined. It included a guard company, two commandant battalions, a support unit and an automobile unit. The formation served more than 800 personnel. Three BRDM-2 were also used to transport guns at the funeral and more than a hundred cars.

In 1980, the Transfiguration Regiment march opened the Olympic Games. Military personnel wearing sports uniforms were honored to fly the flag of International Competitions. Closing of the games also took place under the march of the Preobrazhensky Regiment. The defense ministers of the countries that appeared in the Warsaw Pact were met and escorted by the 1st guard company. The event occurred in 1981, when the West-81 international exercises were in full swing. In the same year, for a whole month, the regiment as a whole was involved in the elimination of fires in the region of the capital. For example, the Spartakiad of the Peoples of the USSR, the celebration of 175 years since the Battle of Borodino and the World Festival of Students and Youth. The regiment was involved in the personnel during the burial of the highest ranks of the Soviet Union, military, state and party leaders. The secretaries general of the CPSU Central Committee and the marshals of the Soviet Union received their last honors precisely from the guard of honor, which was the main participant in the funeral. May 1991 is the date of the creation of the Sentinel battalion of a two-company composition on the basis of a separate company. Since 2006, the amateur theater has been performing successfully.


Significant Events

On April 9, 2013, by decree of the president, the formation was given the honorary name "Life Guard Preobrazhensky Regiment". The guard of honor took part in the awarding ceremonies of the winners of the Olympic and Paralympic Games in Sochi. The military men, dressed in a special uniform, raised the flags of the victorious countries. 54 of the best soldiers provided ceremonies.

Composition

Placed 154 Separate Commandant Transfiguration Regiment in the Lefortovo Barracks. According to the staff number, it consists of 1037 troops. The Preobrazhensky commandant regiment includes:

  1. Control. It combines the headquarters and medical service. It includes financial, automotive, food, missile and artillery departments. This also includes the clothing and engineering services, including the mine clearance group. The Office also operates fuel, chemical, biological and radiation protection services and a communications service. The 1st and 2nd commandant battalions are also part of it.
  2. Guard Battalion. It consists of the 1st and 2nd companies of the guard of honor, the automobile part and the support department. The latter is divided into four platoons. These include departments for traffic regulation, combat support, communications, and a communications workshop.

Appointment

The commandant battalions have a garrison service, as well as patrolling in internal outfits. Their responsibilities include ensuring security for the military prosecutor's office, courts and other fortifications. The competence of the formation includes conducting anti-terrorist activities within the unit. The guard battalion takes part in many large-scale events held by the state leadership in the country or abroad. For example, they include: meetings and farewells of military and government delegations, reception of heads of foreign states when organizing their official visits to the Russian Federation. The battalion takes part in military parades in the capital. The guard of honor was involved in more than 35 thousand special events. The Preobrazhensky regiment has many other units whose work is no less important. The mine action team discovers and destroys explosive objects, and also deals with their transportation to safe areas and disposal. An automobile company is also responsible for the delivery of military personnel to special operations points. The checkpoints of the military automobile inspection and the internal orders of the unit are controlled by the support company. She also performs other tasks. A separate commandant command of the Preobrazhensky regiment entrusted these units with special responsibility. They are a guarantee of a safe life in the capital.



Awards

The official visit of Elizabeth II - Queen of Great Britain - to Russia was successfully prepared and conducted by the Preobrazhensky Regiment. For this, on November 1, 1994, he received a Certificate of Merit from the President of Russia. On August 17, 1995, the formation was awarded the Commander-in-Chief of the country again. The certificate of appreciation was noted for making a huge contribution to the preparation and conduct of the celebration of the fiftieth anniversary of the victory in the Second World War. The next time, the 154th Separate Commandment of the Transfiguration Regiment was awarded on July 12, 2011. The formation received another Diploma of the Supreme Commander of the Forces of the Russian Federation.

Activities and achievements of the orchestra

Already in the first years of work, he was involved in important government events. The orchestra participated in absolutely all the parades that took place on Red Square. The historical procession of 1941 (November 7) and June 24, 1945 is a reasonable pride of the orchestra. The musicians performed at the International Festival of Students and Youth, celebrating the anniversary - the 850th anniversary of the capital and at many other significant events. In 1995, by government order, the orchestra recorded the anthem of the Russian capital. Since the team has a wide range of performance, it is able to provide solemn accompaniment of official ceremonies at the highest level. The composition took part in competitions with the participation of brass bands at international festivals in Switzerland, Austria, Germany, France and the UAE. Today, these musicians are the central military musical group of the capital.

The duties of the orchestra include escorting parades of troops on Red Square, military rituals throughout the territory of Moscow fortification and ceremonies in which the Government of the capital and representatives of various public organizations are directly involved.

Band Leader

Since 2013, the military conductor of the orchestra is Marat Rafikovich Gayanov. In 2000, he became a student at military unit 6520. A year later, he managed to enter the military music school in Moscow. During training he took a very active part in festivals and competitions. After graduating from college, the future conductor entered the Military Institute. In 2010, he graduated with honors. In the period from 2010 to 2013, Marat Rafikovich held the post of military conductor of the orchestra at the Interspecific District Training Center.
