Extend the functionality of the gadget: how to turn on the phone through a computer

Today, almost every person has mobile devices, regardless of their age and profession. And it is not surprising to meet a junior student who can answer the question of how to connect the phone to a computer via USB or Wi-Fi. The need for this may arise, for example, in the following cases:

  • to charge the phone from the USB port;
  • if necessary, copy data from your phone or to your phone (photos, music files, e-books, etc.);
  • to restore the functionality of the smartphone;
  • for use as a webcam;
  • in some other cases.

About how to turn off the phone through the computer, and about other useful things - in order.

Connection Methods

You can connect a mobile phone or smartphone to a computer in the following ways:

  • using wireless connections (Wi-Fi, Bluetooth);
  • via a computer serial port (USB) using a cable.

The first option in most cases is suitable only for copying data. In addition, some simple or outdated cellular models do not have wireless modules. In this regard, we dwell in more detail on the second option, and also consider how to turn on the phone through a computer. In some cases, this will help reanimate the failed device.

How to connect your phone to a computer via USB

You will need a USB cable to connect. This wire often comes with the phone, and can be part of the charger. There is a USB port plug on one side of the cable, and a mobile connection plug on the other. The vast majority of modern phones have a microUSB port for connection .



The second most popular is miniUSB port. This input can be found on tablets and some smartphone models .



For other types of connectors, the connection cable is purchased by model. The figure shows an example of the data cable of some Nokia phones .

Next, we consider the question of how to turn on the camera on the phone through a computer and expand its capabilities.

We use the phone camera

If you are the owner of a modern smartphone with a camera, you can use the gadget in such an unusual role for a phone as a webcam.

This will be extremely useful for communication, for example via Skype, in the absence of an ordinary webcam. To do this, for Android smartphones, you need to download IP Webcam or Bambuser applications. For owners of iPhone suitable program EpocCam and MiniWebCam. In addition, the gadget can be used as a surveillance system or a DVR. The main thing is to choose the right application. But such software, fortunately, is now sufficient for any mobile operating systems.
