Residential complex Serebryany Bor, Kemerovo: description, layout and reviews

Kemerovo is a city of the Siberian region, lost among forests, lakes and rivers. It is located quite far from the Ural Mountains, on both banks of the Tom River. The population of Kemerovo at the moment is more than 500 thousand people, and it has been growing steadily for the past five years. This makes the city very attractive for residential real estate developers. One of their projects is in Serebryany Bor in Kemerovo.





A bit about the city

Kemerovo is a city located at a distance of more than 3,500 kilometers from the state capital - Moscow. It is a mining town. Its main production is coal mines. Therefore, the majority of the able-bodied population is employed precisely in the indicated segment of coal mining and production. Kemerovo is among the thirty best cities for business. In addition, it has a very developed service sector: healthcare, education. There are enterprises of chemical, light industry, mining and processing of coke. This makes the urban economy attractive for investment.





Living in a city saturated with coal air is not very attractive to people. Therefore, the population of the city seeks to move to areas with a more favorable environmental situation. This can be done by very high salaries of mine workers. One of such places, attractive for its climate, is the 12th microdistrict, in which the new building of Kemerovo - LCD Serebryany Bor is located. This residential complex is surrounded on almost all sides by forest. On the one hand - a huge Silver Forest. On the other - the Park of Friendship of Peoples.

Location of the residential complex

The place where the Serebryany Bor is being built, in Kemerovo, the most promising was highlighted. Nearby is the largest transport artery of the city - Shakhtarov Avenue. There is a tram line on it. Just a few kilometers separates the complex from the city center. It can be reached in about a few minutes by private car. A lot of public transport also goes to the complex: shuttle vans, trams.





Located in the city, registration and the Silver Boron index in Kemerovo receive urban ones. This factor is important for those who need to use the infrastructure of the city: hospitals, clinics, schools.

Description of the residential complex

What will be the residential complex after the completion of construction? In total, 34 hectares of land have been allocated for development, which will allow building not only houses, but also infrastructure facilities. Residential buildings are being erected using monolithic-brick technology of various storeys. In total, from ten to eighteen floors are provided. Layouts of apartments are also provided for various. Buildings are being constructed in the order of priority provided for by the project declaration. The last phase should be erected in 2017.





LCD Serebryany Bor (Kemerovo) is foreseen as three independent residential complexes. For the convenience of its residents, the construction of underground parking lots is planned in each complex. But there will also be ground guest parking. In the construction of buildings, the developer applied the latest architectural solutions. The monolithic frame of buildings is insulated inside with bricks and special insulation.

Layout of apartments and their cost

The developer designed various apartments in Serebryany Bor (Kemerovo): from one-room to four-room. The size of the apartments is also different. The smallest - one-room - start at 33 m 2 . The largest - four-room - from 97 m 2 .





Traditionally, the highest cost of one-room apartments - starting from 45 thousand rubles per square meter of living space. Two-room apartments are the most inexpensive in price - only 38 thousand rubles per square meter of living space. Three-room and four-room apartments are approximately at the same level of pricing policy.

Internal infrastructure

In addition to residential buildings, the Silver Forest project in Kemerovo provides for the construction of four kindergartens with swimming pools, two secondary schools, a general practice hospital, a sports and fitness center and other social infrastructure facilities. The courtyard is landscaped with lawns, flowerbeds, children's playgrounds and sports grounds with safety rubber coating, divided into different age segments. Recreation areas for adults are being created. External infrastructure

The location of the residential complex in the city allows its residents to use the entire rich infrastructure of the city. These are several secondary schools, kindergartens, clinics, public transport. Nearby is a pine forest, where it is good to relax after a busy week, take a walk, breathe in fresh air.

In addition, within walking distance there are many small shops with various goods, hypermarkets, bank branches, beauty salons and other objects of social infrastructure, without which the life of a modern person in a big city is unthinkable. All infrastructure is located near the complex.

Residents reviews

Serebryany Bor on the Rainbow in Kemerovo has already been partially commissioned and, accordingly, has been populated by the first tenants. Those who have already settled in the complex leave various reviews on the Internet. They are both positive and negative.

Among the positive reviews, many admiration for the location of the complex - near the pine forest and the river. The fact that the complex is located within the city limits and residents have the opportunity to relax in various cafes, in the fresh air among the green spaces also affects the positive mood of the residents. Another resident likes a small number of cars, from which air pollution occurs. They are satisfied with the quality of construction, and with the materials used by the developer.





As for the negative opinion, it mainly develops due to dissatisfaction with the underdeveloped social infrastructure. For example, they complain about the lack of a sports complex, a cinema. The quality of the roads leading to the complex and their construction itself, that is, the absence of bypass routes, are not satisfied with the inhabitants. This fact creates additional traffic jams in the mornings and evenings, which, accordingly, does not add enthusiasm. Many express their wishes to build more places for recreation and games for children.
