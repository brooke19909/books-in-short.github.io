Centara Pattaya Hotel 4 (Thailand): photos and reviews of tourists

Fascinating, diverse and eclectic Pattaya is one of the most famous resorts in Southeast Asia. Its countless restaurants and shops, endless entertainment on land and on the water and, of course, the richest memories of the rest, captured in videos and photographs, attract a lot of tourists. Today we offer a closer look at one of the Pattaya hotels - Centara Avenue Pattaya Hotel 4 *.

Hotel Location

Located in the central part of Pattaya, the fashionable eight-story Hotel Centara Avenue Pattaya Hotel 4 * was built recently and has a noticeable and fashionable style.



It is close to Avenue Mall, major entertainment centers and Pattaya Beach. The rooms are designed for tourists seeking an active nightlife and appreciating modern style solutions for relaxation. Centara Avenue Hotel Pattaya 4 * hotel is located in Central Pattaya of the Kingdom of Thailand. Bangkok Suvarnabhumi International Airport is an hour and a half drive from the hotel.

Hotel surroundings

Near the hotel there are entertainment centers and historical places: Ripley's Believe It or Not Museum, Central Festival Beach.



Walking distance, which is especially appreciated by tourists, is the center of the city’s active nightlife - Walking Street, as well as Pattaya Memorial Hospital. It is easy to get to the Royal Garden Plaza shopping center.

Accommodation

Centara Avenue Pattaya Hotel 4 * by the number of rooms (fifty-one) belongs to the category of mini-hotels. This quality gives the hotel a certain charm, intimacy and comfort. The rooms are decorated in a magnificent modern combination of light colors and bright splashes.

Style solutions in the interior design of the hotel allow you to stay in an ultramodern setting and at the same time enjoy the plots of Pattaya in its Asian identity, which open in a panoramic view from the upper floors of the hotel, on the roof of which there is a pool.

The rooms of Centara Pattaya Hotel 4 * (Pattaya) have free Wi-Fi, and there is a choice of various options for accommodation.





Choosing a room with a large bed will provide comfort for one guest (single occupancy), couples (double rooms) and small families (double occupancy and one extra bed).

  • Premier Corner. Eleven rooms of this category with a view of the city and an area of ​​twenty to fifty square meters are equipped with individual air conditioning. Also, each room has a bathroom with a mirror, shower, hairdryer.
  • Deluxe Ten rooms of this category are designed for tourists with high demands on the level of comfort. Each of them is decorated in accordance with the latest fashion trends. The functional area of ​​the rooms is twenty-six square meters. Each has a balcony with city views. The Centara Pattaya Hotel 4 * Deluxe room has comfortable upholstered furniture, a large sleeping bed, free Wi-Fi, a Smart TV and a DVD player with a touch control panel. Also, apartments of this category offer such additional amenities as a workplace, a telephone with direct access to international calls, individually controlled air conditioning, a minibar, tea and coffee making facilities, a safe and a hairdryer.




  • Superior Thirty rooms of this category have a modern and at the same time functional style of decoration. Rooms are twenty-eight square meters in light colors, which visually increases their size. The apartments have free Wi-Fi and smart TV. The rooms of this category have large double or king beds, which can easily accommodate couples and small families of 2 adults and 1 child.

The air-conditioned rooms of the Centara Avenue Hotel Pattaya 4 * include safes and minibars. A refrigerator is available upon request. Rooms are cleaned every day.

On the second floor of the hotel is the main restaurant, offering a large selection of international and Thai national dishes. Meals are provided using the à la carte system. Vegetarian options are available. Opening hours of the restaurant of the Centara Pattaya Hotel 4 * hotel from 06:30 to 22:30. On request, meals can be delivered to rooms.

The pool bar is open from 11:00 to 18:00. Here you can order strong drinks, cocktails, juices and soft drinks.

Entertainment

The main fun on vacation in Pattaya is visiting the beaches. The distance from the hotel to the sea is small - about half a kilometer. The beach is sandy, with a gentle entrance to the water.



Many vacationers in Pattaya prefer to go swimming in the islands. The sea there is much cleaner, and the rest on the islands and a developed system of water entertainment give a special flavor to the rest in Thailand.

A good option is to visit Pratanmak Beach, two kilometers from the hotel. It is famous for solitude and good quality sand. It is also convenient to relax on the island of Koh Lan. It can be reached by boat, which regularly delivers tourists from the pier, which is located near the Centara Pattaya Hotel 4 *.

Visiting nightclubs, casinos, entertainment venues of Walking Street, shopping and entertainment centers Avenue Shopping Center, Royal Garden Plaza will be a good entertainment for lovers of active nightlife.

What is important to know

When choosing a vacation in the Thai resort of Pattaya, you should consider some of the wishes of experienced travelers:

  • Before the trip, carefully read the cultural customs of the country. The Kingdom of Thailand sacredly reveres its king and careless attitude to his image (including on banknotes), ignoring the norms of communication with Thais is fraught with negative consequences, up to imprisonment.
  • Do not buy tanning products, cosmetic accessories for a trip to this country. It’s better to get it all on the spot. Natural cosmetics in Thailand, quality is guaranteed by state laws, and violations in this area are severely punished.
  • Think of your wardrobe according to the principle of minimalism. It is better to take summer things with you on vacation, which you want to get rid of. If this is not a business trip, everything you need for a vacation can be purchased on site at the lowest prices.
  • Particular attention should be paid to sanitary and hygienic standards: drink only drinking water specially designed for consumption, wash your hands as often as possible, be sure to process fruits.
