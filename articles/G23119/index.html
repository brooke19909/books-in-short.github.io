Perovsky Park in Moscow: the best place to relax in your area

Perovo is one of the districts of Moscow that is distinguished today by its developed infrastructure and an abundance of cultural and entertainment facilities. There is also its own landscaped green recreation area. This is Perovsky Park - a place where all locals regularly come for a walk and sometimes Muscovites come from other areas. What is interesting about this recreation area, what kind of entertainment does it offer to vacationers, and what can you do here today?

Hunting grounds, luxurious estate, city park ...



Perovo received its name from the village of the same name, which was once located in this place. According to many historians, royal hunting grounds were located here. This fact is also indicated by the emblem of the district - a golden hunting horn and a silver feather. Local lands and the estate constantly passed from hand to hand. Once the estate was owned by Count P. A. Golitsyn, who built the church of the Icon of the Mother of God ‚ÄúThe Sign‚Äù (1708), which has survived to the present day. Later, the estate passed into the ownership of J. V. Bruce, a man about whom there were many legends and gossip. Then the estate was bought by Elizabeth Petrovna and granted to A. G. Razumovsky. After the death of the favorite of the Empress, the land again lost its owner, and later, during the revolution, the Perovsky park was officially opened here. Today, only memories remained from the once magnificent manor, but meanwhile the recreation area boasts mighty trees, and some of them have already celebrated their centenary.

Modern park in Perovo - rest in Moscow at a decent level



Today, the recreation area is quite landscaped and is one of the favorite places for walks of residents of all the surrounding areas. The historical layout of the park, the author of which is the architect Gilardi, has been preserved. One of the most significant modern facilities located here is the Lokomotiv Sports Stadium. There is also a pond in the park, a large skateboard Ferma, children's and sports towns. The attraction zone deserves special attention: there are among them simple ones designed for the little ones (trains and swings), but adult adrenaline lovers will not be bored, because they are waiting for a roller coaster and other "serious" carousels. Perovsky Park will please you with a small but incredibly interesting zoo, summer stage and cinema, as well as opportunities for practicing a wide variety of outdoor sports.

All the best for children



Family vacations in Moscow today are offered by many entertainment centers and cultural organizations. But many residents and guests of the capital still most of all like just taking a walk with their children in the park. The recreation area in Perovo is a place where it will not be boring to spend the whole day. On the territory of the facility there are many outdoor playgrounds, on which there are not only the usual slides and sandpits, but also unusual attractions. What is especially nice, the entrance to the territory of the recreation area is free and completely free for everyone. Lovers of merry-go-rounds will not be bored here either. In Perovsky Park, everyone will find something to their liking. In addition to the classic carousels, there are also the most modern entertainment facilities, such as water balls. After riding the rides, you can go to the zoo corner, where squirrels, thoroughbred chickens, pheasants, rabbits, goats and other animals live.

Sport - is life

Perovsky Park is a great place for all lovers of sports and an active lifestyle. There are outdoor sports fields. For professionals, the Lokomotiv stadium works. The park has enough paths on which you can not only run, but also ride bicycles and roller skates. Sports equipment rental also operates in the recreation area. If you did not bring a bicycle or rollers with you - it does not matter, they can always be rented at an acceptable cost. For the convenience of cyclists, separate paths are highlighted - here you can ride with the breeze, without disturbing those who walk on foot.

What else to do in Perovsky Park?



Cafes and fast food outlets are open in the recreation area. The park itself operates year-round. In winter, ski slopes are laid here, a skating rink is flooded, where you can always skate and play hockey. Also in the cold season there is a rental of tubing and downhill tracks. In the summer, many families come here just to walk along shady alleys or have a picnic. If you have never been here before, be sure to visit Perovsky Park. Moscow is a city in which there are many beautiful places, but the recreation area in Perovo is one of the most comfortable and interesting. Here, modern entertainment facilities and green spaces are successfully combined. And although quite often the park is called a children's park, in fact, adults here will also be interesting and pleasant to relax.

How to get to the recreation area in Perovo?



The park operates year-round and around the clock. You can ride the rides from 10:00 to 20:00. On holidays, concerts and other events are held here. Getting to the recreation area is not difficult. The nearest metro station is Perovo, then travel by land, for example, by bus No. 617 to the Lazo Street stop. Perovsky Park address is as follows: Lazo street, possession 7. The recreation area is clearly visible from the same ground transport stop.
