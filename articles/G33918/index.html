Positano Italy - the best city on Earth

Positano (Italy) is one of the most beautiful resorts on the Amalfi Coast. Tourists who come here expect an unforgettable beach holiday and excellent climatic conditions. Positano is a paradise on Earth. This city is small but very cozy. To enjoy its peaceful atmosphere and clear sea, many people from all over the world come here every year. The cleanest sandy beach in Positano also receives many rave reviews.





From the side the town, as it were, descends to the sea. Private hotels and apartment houses are so tightly pressed together that they look like a whole. A view of the azure sea opens from almost any place in the city. The beauty of local landscapes has long attracted artists and writers.

History

Positano (Italy) has been known to the world since Roman times. According to an ancient legend, the city was erected by Poseidon. He dedicated it to his beloved Pasite, in whose honor the city received its name. And the islands of Galli, located off the coast, are mentioned in the Odyssey. According to Homer, sirens lived here.

In Positano (Italy), trade, shipbuilding, shipping began to develop - this was a period of deep prosperity. At the same time, many watch towers were built to protect the city from the raids of the Saracen pirates. Some of these structures have survived to the present day.

During the Middle Ages, Positano experienced a short decline, which with the beginning of the 13th century gave way to another stage of development. Many buildings built in the Baroque style have survived from that era. And in the 20th century, after the end of the First World War, Positano became a popular resort.

Location





Positano is located in Italy, 7 kilometers from Sorrento, 60 kilometers from Naples and 260 kilometers from Rome. The town is scattered in three small valleys located between the mountains and the sea. The water here is clean, the beaches are sandy, and the rich underwater world attracts diving enthusiasts. Therefore, many say that Positano is a paradise on Earth.

Resort Description

From a distance, it may seem that the town looks like a staircase leading to the top of the mountain. The town is surrounded by olive and orange groves, thanks to which the air in Positano (Italy) is saturated with delicious smells.





Not only foreigners like to stay at this resort, but Italians themselves. Wealthy residents of Italy relax in Positano in their own villas.

Climatic conditions

The climate here is very mild, Mediterranean, comfortable for relaxing almost all year round. Thanks to the mountains, the resort is completely closed from the north winds. Therefore, the weather is always warm here. The season begins in May and ends in early November.

What to see?

Attractions include architectural structures of the city. Each multi-colored house, each street is already a miracle of art. Walking along the streets of Positano, tourists take pictures without stopping: this small city is so beautiful. It boasts a whole list of ancient sights of interest to tourists.

The symbol of the city is the church of Santa Maria Assunta, built in the 13th century. It is famous for its dome, lined with majolica. Noteworthy are modern art galleries, medieval watchtowers and ancient houses along winding streets.

activity

Night walkers are recommended to visit the Afrikana night club, which is located in the rock. Memories after his visit remain unforgettable.

The cultural life of Positano is very diverse: interesting festivals are held here for tourists. So, every year you can visit the ballet festival. And in August the Day of the Assumption of the Virgin is celebrated - on this day you can become a member of this carnival and watch theatrical performances and festive fireworks.

Where to stay

In the city you can stay in one of the many hotels of different levels of stardom, as well as in private apartments or villas.

Entertainment





For lovers of outdoor activities in Positano there are water activities: boat riding, catamarans, yachts and diving, as well as sailing. Here you can also engage in horse riding, playing soccer, tennis (there are excellent tennis courts in Positano), basketball, handball and volleyball in specially equipped venues.

Visitors to the city should not forget about renting bicycles, scooters and motorbikes, as well as about walking.

Transport features

The resort is located on the Amalfi-Sorrento bus route. The drive to the resort of Amalfi is an hour, to Sorrento - half an hour. Water transport is popular in Positano: you can get to the resort by water taxi or ferry. You can easily take a taxi by calling it from any Positano hotel. The center of the city itself is completely closed to cars.





Positano: how to get there

There are several ways to get to Positano:

  • Arrive in Rome by plane, and after the domestic flight arrive at Sorrento Airport. There is a bus from Sorrento to Positano, or you can take a taxi.
  • Fly to Naples airport, from there get to Positano by train.
  • By water: by ferry or boat. The starting point may be: Salerno, Amalfi or the island of Capri.
