"Southern Seaside" is a sanatorium. Description of the resort "Southern Seaside"

"Southern Seaside" is a sanatorium, one of the best health resorts of the Black Sea coast, which has great opportunities for year-round treatment. She gained her fame back in the days of the USSR, it was here that people from different parts of the country came to spend time at sea, combining relaxation with a complex of medical procedures.





Territory

Sanatorium "Seaside" is considered to be a landmark of Adler. It occupies the territory of the arboretum of 11.5 hectares, which is a favorite place for vacationers to walk. A huge territory with gazebos, recreation areas, shady alleys and pools - what else is needed for a full spa vacation ?!

Treatment

Microclimatic features of the area in which the sanatorium is located are favorable for climatotherapy. It is for dry sea air that people come here with respiratory diseases, skin rashes. Nature itself takes care of the beauty and health of vacationers, and modern medical equipment can improve and consolidate the result.





Diagnosis and treatment are carried out according to methods approved by the Ministry of the Russian Federation. For each patient, an individual diagnostic program is selected, treatment with a set of necessary wellness procedures.

Accommodations

"Southern Seaside" is a sanatorium that makes it possible to organize an unforgettable vacation. The choice of visitors are offered standard rooms and suites. All of them are equipped with modern furniture, appliances, a private bathroom with a set of necessary bath accessories. Most rooms offer scenic sea views, which contribute to an excellent mood.

If you want to combine a beach holiday with the complex of necessary treatment and prophylactic procedures, go to the Vzmorye sanatorium. This is a great option for accommodation on the Black Sea coast.
