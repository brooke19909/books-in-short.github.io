Description of the sanatorium "Neman". Sanatorium "Neman", Grodno region: description, prices, reviews

A large number of our compatriots go from year to year to various sanatoriums to combine rest and treatment there. Moreover, very often the choice of these people falls not on Russian health centers, but on those that are in the neighboring country of Belarus.

There are many different resorts, and they are located in almost all regions of the country. In Grodno, for example, there is Neman - a sanatorium specializing in a rather wide range of diseases and offering the most advanced treatment technologies.

For health - to Belarus

Many will probably be surprised: why do our compatriots choose sanatoriums not in Russia, but abroad, especially in Belarus? The answer to this question is quite simple: very high-quality medical services are provided in this country of the former Soviet Union, while their cost is quite low.





And although in Belarus there is no sea to which you can go for healing, salty air, there is a magnificent nature here, which contributes both to raising the mood and a surge of health and vitality.

Prices for accommodation and medical services in Belarusian resorts will pleasantly surprise many of our compatriots. At the same time, the quality of services does not suffer at all, but, on the contrary, sometimes meets even the most overstated requirements. Of course, there are expensive accommodation options here, but the variety of health resorts available to all segments of the population will make everyone think about which sanatorium to choose.

Sanatorium in the city of Grodno

The Grodno sanatorium "Neman-72" exists since 1972. It was built on the territory surrounded by pine forest and mixed forest. The place itself is located in the floodplain of the Neman River, and on the western side the health resort is adjacent to the forests of Augustow Forest. Due to this, the air here contains coniferous aromas and has a healing effect.

The green and landscaped territory of the sanatorium includes several residential buildings, a medical and administrative building, and a dining club.





The medical profile, which has a sanatorium "Neman" (Grodno), includes the treatment of diseases of the endocrine system, peripheral nervous system, blood circulation, metabolic disorders and eating disorders. Both adults and children can get wellness services here.

The patient nutrition system includes five meals on a custom menu. There is a diet based on several diets, and if the vacationer has any allergies, then an individual nutrition program is drawn up for him.

We get on our own to the sanatorium

A health resort is located on the outskirts of the city of Grodno, just 1.5 kilometers from it, in the tract of Grandichi. "Neman" is a sanatorium, whose exact address in Grodno is 23 Sanatornaya Street.

You can get here from the city railway station by bus number 10. You need to get off at the stop called "School No. 2". From the bus station, trolleybus number 2 must go to the last stop and transfer to the bus mentioned above.

Arriving in Grodno itself is possible from Minsk, the distances to which are covered by plane, by train, and by bus. Trains, fixed-route taxis and regular buses go from here to the city of interest to us.





Well, there is always the option to come directly to the destination by car. So there are a lot of ways to get to Grodno and the sanatorium itself. Tourists only need to choose the most convenient for them.

Rooms and prices at Neman 72

The housing stock, which offers its guests the sanatorium "Neman" (Grodno), is located in three bedroom buildings. All of them are three-story, elevators are not provided for in them, and they are not interconnected by closed passages.

The number of rooms available in these buildings is represented by single, double and two-room double rooms. Each of them offers its guests all the necessary furniture, an electric kettle with a set of dishes, a comfortable bathroom with a shower or a shower, a TV and a set of towels.





Single rooms are located in the buildings under numbers 1 and 3. Each has a single bed, a small table with a chair, a wardrobe and a nightstand. The rooms of the 3rd building are equipped with LCD televisions. In the period from the beginning of January to the end of May 2017, the cost of living in rooms of this category of one person is 1770 rubles, from June to August 25 - 1910 rubles, from late August to the end of December - 1800 rubles.

Double one-room numbers are available in each of three cases. They are equipped with two single beds, a refrigerator and other necessary things, which are in single rooms. In buildings No. 1 and 2, they have a chair-bed, which is used as an extra bed. The cost of living for one person in a room of this category is: from the beginning of January to the end of May 1590 rubles., From June to August 25 - 1740 rubles., From the end of August to the end of December - 1630 rubles.

Two-room double rooms are located in the 1st and 3rd buildings. They have a living room with a corner sofa and an armchair, a coffee table and a desk, a cupboard with a whatnot, on which there is a TV, a refrigerator and chairs. The bedroom has a double bed, dressing table, bedside tables and ottoman. One guest for staying in this room will have to pay 1900 rubles from the beginning of January to the end of May, 2030 rubles from June to August 25, and 1930 rubles from the end of August to the end of December.





The price includes meals five times a day according to the necessary menu, prescribed treatment procedures and the use of Wi-Fi, which is available only in the third building.

Medical services in a sanatorium

Neman is a sanatorium in which generally accepted types of treatment are widely used for the treatment and prevention of the above diseases. Here, patients can enjoy a stay in various therapeutic baths, receive procedures for light, electrical and heat therapy, all kinds of inhalations and oxygen therapy, and various types of massage. Of the non-traditional methods of treatment, aromatherapy, aerophytotherapy, color and speleotherapy are used. Specialists of the health resort have developed special comprehensive programs aimed at treating specific problems in a patient.

The sanatorium has modern and high-quality ultrasound equipment, thanks to which doctors conduct a complete and reliable diagnosis of the entire guest‚Äôs body. Various spa treatments, such as body wraps and special bathtubs, are also on offer.

Entertainment in the Neman

To make the treatment a joy, there are all kinds of entertainments in the health resort. It offers guests a library, a small pool, sports and dance halls, a dance floor for the summer, a cinema hall, a kiosk with print, tables and rackets for table tennis. Guided tours of the city and concerts of local artists are organized.





A wonderful vacation with treatment goes to parents with children who come to the sanatorium "Neman-72". The reviews of tourists who have been here indicate that there are playgrounds with all kinds of swings, slides and the like, as well as a children's room. Little guests can also come to evening dances and thereby have a great time. Also for children in the dining room there is a special children's menu.

Reviews of the patients of the sanatorium

A special delight among vacationers is the territory of the sanatorium, on which many pines grow. Cultural events are also amazing, because in the health resort almost every day something interesting is organized.
