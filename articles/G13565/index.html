Mauritius Island Attractions

The island state, located in the Indian Ocean, is considered a true paradise for tourists who are not confused by its considerable remoteness from civilization. Lovers from all over the globe come to the most beautiful resort in the world to hold a memorable wedding ceremony.

Heavenly place

Mauritius is the largest island of the state, on which the capital of Port Louis is located. Surrounded on all sides by a warm ocean, it is famous for its incredibly picturesque landscapes.



The island, which was once owned by the Portuguese, French and British, in 1992 became a free republic. The most prosperous country in the region is famous for its developed textile industry and the production of sugar from cane. But the main source of revenue for the treasury is the constantly improving tourism business.

Travelers who come to relax celebrate a pleasant climate without the sweltering heat. Many are afraid of the rainy season, which lasts several months, from February to May, but guests of the island should not worry: heavy rains do not happen every year.

Mauritius on a world map

An attractive tourist island in the Indian Ocean is located east of Madagascar. Belonging to the Mascarene Archipelago, Mauritius is equidistant from two continents - Africa and Asia, and many have a question about which one it belongs to. It is officially accepted that the paradise is ranked among the hottest continent on the globe.



When tourists try to find the beautiful island of Mauritius on the map, not many manage to do it the first time. It is only a tiny point, lost in the ocean and located away from all tourist routes.

The exotic island of Mauritius, whose attractions deserve a detailed story, does not exceed the territory of Moscow in size.

Tourists celebrate the magical atmosphere of a colorful resort, whose ancient culture has absorbed all the best and most interesting for its guests. Favorite island of all celebrities

It is no coincidence that the rich tropical oasis was chosen by the rich stars of Hollywood, influential members of the royal families and the billionaires who scatter money. Therefore, the guests of the paradise are deprived of all the inconvenience associated with mass tourism, and all vacationers on the island with such a beautiful name - Mauritius have long forgotten about the reports of criminal incidents.

Its sights are primarily associated with the unique nature and magnificent multi-kilometer beaches where world celebrities rest.

Deer island

You can not ignore the Deer Island, located in the east of Mauritius. Without exaggeration, we can say that this is the most popular vacation spot for everyone who wants to be alone with nature.



Once upon a time it was known for huge deer populations and complete desertedness. Over time, cute animals disappeared, and the picturesque corner turned into a tourist Mecca with excellent service. Conveniently located comfortable hotels, luxurious restaurants and golden beaches offering all kinds of water activities are just a small part of what attracts wealthy travelers here.

Chamarel Colored Dunes

Fantastically beautiful Mauritius, whose attractions are unique, is famous for the unrealistic landscapes of the village of Chamarel. The colorful dunes created by nature itself will surprise even the most demanding guests of the island. Sands for many centuries have been subjected to erosion and various temperatures, as a result of which they have developed into bizarre lands of the most unusual shades, which the locals call seven-colored.



Although in fact, the sand palette of Chamarel (Mauritius) is much richer. Walking along the colorful dunes is strictly forbidden, but for tourists have built comfortable viewing platforms from which you can admire stunning views. It is interesting to visit the village after heavy rains to make sure the unreality of what is happening. Sands of different colors do not mix even after severe hurricanes and heavy rainfall, which is an amazing sight.

Botanical Garden Pamplus

One of the oldest and most beautiful botanical gardens is located near the capital of the island. Its incredible popularity is explained by the fact that the amazing flora of Mauritius is represented here.



Tourists will be delighted with a beautiful rose with leaves of more than three meters, a sacred lotus flower blooming once every hundred years, a palm tree, huge water lilies ...

Underwater Ghost

Unique waterfalls scattered throughout the territory are not in vain recognized as the main natural wonders. But the most, perhaps, the most magnificent cascade is located on the Le Morne-Braban peninsula, which creates a deceptive impression of a picture of an extraterrestrial landscape.



It seems that powerful streams of water continuously flow into the blue abyss of the ocean. The view of continuously flowing jets from above resembles an impressive funnel that greedily sucks everything around. Hindu temple and deity statue

Representatives of various religions peacefully coexist on the island, but most of the locals profess Hinduism.

The temple complex, located near Lake Grand Bassin, is the most significant place for Hindu adherents, hosting thousands of pilgrims. All guests are greeted by the third-largest statue of Shiva on the entire globe, surprising with special realism.



The supreme deity, which is unusually revered on the island, symbolizes two principles - destructive and creative, life and death. The sculpture is considered to be unique, because this is the only image of Shiva standing, not sitting, as required by local traditions.

On the greatest night dedicated to the deity, 100 thousand believers come to the temple located high in the mountains , who come for the festival from different continents. To appease Shiva, generous gifts are brought to the shore of the lake, which are later pulled away by monkeys.

Sacred lake

And the Lake Gran Basin itself, surrounded by temples, causes considerable interest of travelers. Native people have long worshiped him, considering the reservoir formed in the crater of the volcano sacred.

Surrounded by sacrificial places, the lake in the morning and in the evening presents a very interesting sight: the light fog hiding the Gran Basen gives it, according to tourists, a mystical look.

Gastronomic establishments

Experienced travelers celebrate a well-developed restaurant service, surprising with spectacular evening shows and live music. The establishments located in tropical gardens offer visitors to enjoy traditional and European dishes. Local cuisine includes seafood, fresh herbs and gourmet spices, which Mauritius is famous for. Restaurants can help you catch memorable private events in a pleasant, contemporary setting.



An original Mauritius, the sights of which have no analogues on the whole globe, is a unique place where vacationers will feel truly happy. The tourist paradise, despite its small size, amazes with the variety of natural and man-made monuments.
