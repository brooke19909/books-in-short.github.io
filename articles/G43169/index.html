Central Botanical Garden in Minsk

In every country there is a place where the wonders of nature and landscape architecture are collected . In Belarus, it is a botanical garden in Minsk; it is the largest plant biodiversity conservation center in its state. Presented cultures are considered rare.

Short story

The Botanical Garden in Minsk is a leading research center in the field of introduction and plant ecology, physiology and molecular biology. It contains more than ten thousand diverse plants: both ornamental and domestic.

Its official name is the Central Botanical Garden of the National Academy of Sciences of Belarus. It has existed for eighty-two years, over the entire period of activity it has constantly expanded the boundaries and the list of presented plants. The Botanical Garden in Minsk in 1932 occupied an area of ‚Äã‚Äãover ninety hectares, and currently it is about one hundred and fifty-three hectares. It is located in a pine forest. Visitors always note a healthy atmosphere in this place.

Twenty-eight years after the foundation of the garden, the center was given the status of a research institute of the Academy of Sciences of the BSSR.

The Botanical Garden in Minsk invites visitors to look at plants from different places on the planet. Presented tropical and subtropical crops of more than two thousand species. These are bamboo and palm trees, oranges and agave, eucalyptus and a bottle tree of Madagascar, cypress trees and evergreen jasmines. Many of these plants are placed in greenhouses. This approach allows the garden not to close during the winter. The greenhouse has been operating for visitors since 2007. For seven years now, unusual and rare cultures have pleased everyone with their views.

Flora from all over the world

The Botanical Garden in Minsk presents an exposition of flowers from around the world. It includes different varieties and types of plants. These are tulips and roses, lilies and daffodils, peonies and hyacinths, lilacs and dahlias, as well as many others. Each visitor can find his favorite flower and enjoy it.

The Central Botanical Garden in Minsk is known among Belarusians and guests of the country for its arboretum. It is famous for its rich collection of conifers. All plants in the garden are arranged in order. Trees and shrubs are located in their geographical sectors. Separately derived collections of perennials from the Caucasus and the Pamirs, from Southern Europe and America, Australia are also offered for viewing.



Recently, the botanical garden in Minsk, whose address is Surganova street, house 2-in (this is the Pervomaisky district), has opened two garden plots: a German and a Belarusian type. The plans are to create several more personal territories of other European states. The extraordinary beauty of nature and architecture is preserved by the botanical garden in Minsk. How to get to this wonderful place? You can just find a public transport stop and take a bus, or call a taxi. In the Belarusian center of nature it is easy to get lost in time. You can spend the whole day between rows with plants and completely forget about everything that happens outside the garden.

Rose exhibition

Many guests of the capital of Belarus consider it their duty to visit this popular center of exotic nature. Especially often people come to see roses in the botanical garden of Minsk. In the collection of CBS - more than three hundred and twenty kinds of roses: tea and polyanthus, Chinese and tea-hybrid.

The most popular place for Belarusians is the botanical garden in Minsk. 2014 gave visitors an unforgettable holiday of roses. It was attended by a huge number of guests from neighboring countries. The holiday program included a two-hour tour of the rose garden. Also, a leading specialist of the Central Botanical Garden G.F. Zhuravkin held a master class on caring for roses. The celebration was decorated with an animated show of living sculptures and hats made of flowers. For visitors, a master class was held from representatives of the Vivasan company. At the consultation, they talked about the application and use of floral oils in cosmetology. For children there was an attraction for archery and a pony ride with a Flower Fairy.



Popular place

Entrance to the botanical garden is paid, for adult visitors a ticket costs twenty thousand Belarusian rubles, for pensioners, schoolchildren or students - ten thousand. To get into the landscape zone, you have to pay thirty thousand and fifteen, respectively. The ticket price for entering the greenhouse is the same for everyone - fifteen thousand Belarusian rubles.

The people call the Central Botanical Garden "Botany", the locals consider it a museum of living plants in the open. Minsk residents often spend their days off in a green corner. Human communication with plants gives new strength.



Some scientists confidently state that flora can influence the general condition of people. Cactus takes negative energy, tradescantia improves well-being and mood, and geranium freshens the air.

Some interesting facts

Plants can also help a person predict the weather. For example, yellow acacia is a unique natural barometer; on the threshold of rain it releases a large amount of nectar and a pleasant strong aroma. Also, by the appearance of gerberas, specialists can determine whether the weather will be sunny or cloudy.

Another interesting fact: some plants are used in cooking. Well-known vanilla is obtained from one type of exotic orchid. In ancient times, entire ships with people went in search of this flower. Only ten years ago, rich Belarusians were ready to pay a lot of money for orchids, since they were not in the state at all. Now the flowers are in the Central Botanical Garden, everyone can come and enjoy them. In Belarus, they learned how to grow orchids in flasks.

Flower collections

Employees of the Minsk Botanical Garden are helping to acclimatize in local conditions to new cultures imported from different parts of the world. For this, expeditions are organized to various climatic zones (for example, to the Carpathians or to the Urals). Now there are about 250 species of perennials from the Caucasus, Southern Europe and Africa, the Pamirs.



Also in the garden there are also collections of flowers familiar to residents of the capital. The garden displays new species of dahlias and lilies. Especially many varieties of tulips (500) and peonies (300). Workers take the most care of plants and conduct numerous studies.

Future plans

Every year, reconstruction works are carried out in the botanical garden in Minsk. Modern greenhouses have already been rebuilt, old ones are always being repaired. The administration of the natural center wants to replace the irrigation system of the garden. Also completely change the structure of the tracks and build more new greenhouses. Rich fund

Currently, the main task of the Central Botanical Garden of Minsk is to preserve all the richness of nature. This fund has been accumulating for many years. The garden is undergoing reconstruction, which will last several more years.

In the center of nature, they clean lakes that have not been cleaned since the 1960s. The garden administration wants to plant new shrubs and trees. After all, plants have been living here since the opening of the center and post-war time. A unique place where a huge number of plants are collected is always very popular among residents of the whole country and guests of the state.
