Hotel Ring Beach Hotel 5 *, Kemer, Turkey, Beldibi: rooms, infrastructure, beach, reviews

Kemer is one of the main resort regions of Turkey. This place is visited every year by a large number of tourists from different parts of the world. Kemer hotels are very popular among travelers, not only because they are located in excellent climatic conditions, but also because the cost of tours in them is quite small.

Tour operators in different countries often offer to visit the Ring Beach Hotel 5 * - in Kemer this is one of the budget places located on the very shores of the Mediterranean Sea. Today there are a large number of reviews about this resort - they can be found on the personal pages of travelers on social networks, as well as on various thematic sites. Often, colorful photographs taken during the rest are attached to them.

Location

The Ring Beach Hotel 5 * is located in Beldibi ( Kemer ) - a small village adjacent to a large Turkish area. Kemer has a huge amount of entertainment and interesting places to visit and relax. The nature here is picturesque, the sea is warm . If desired, guests can always visit nightclubs, restaurants, a large number of cafes and bars, as well as karaoke and other institutions. The distance from here to the central part of Kemer is 20 kilometers. Many tourists recommend overcoming them by car, which can be rented at the hotel itself . On it , they advise to go on a tour of local attractions.

The coastline is within walking distance of the hotel. You can walk to it in just a couple of minutes - this circumstance is very pleasing to tourists, especially those who are on vacation with their children.

The nearest airport is Antalya. To get to it, tourists need to overcome 55 km.



Rooms

Ring Beach Hotel 5 * (Turkey / Kemer ) offers tourists quite a large number of rooms, which includes 218 apartments. All of them are arranged as cozy as possible for a comfortable stay of tourists. They are divided into two groups: family and standards.
