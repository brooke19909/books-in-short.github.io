Country Hotel "Greenwald", Yekaterinburg: description and photo

Do you want to spend your holidays or weekends in silence, in a pine forest? You are invited to visit the Greenwald country hotel located in the village of Verkhnyaya Sysert . Yekaterinburg is located at a distance of 60 kilometers from the complex. Among the beautiful Ural nature European comfort conditions are created for you.

Country Hotel Greenwald (Yekaterinburg). Description

Hotel Greenwald is located on the banks of the Verkh-Sysert Reservoir, surrounded by a pine forest. Beautiful nature, clear water in the lake and fresh air make it an ideal place to relax in the country. Hotel "Greenwald" is located in a fully protected area. The hotel consists of three buildings built in a rustic style:

  • Greenwald main building;
  • Chalet building "Hans";
  • Chalet "Franz" building.

On the territory of the country house there is a unique SPA center, which consists of an open-air pool, a font, a jacuzzi, a Finnish sauna and a massage room. The hotel has its own sandy beach with sun loungers. There is a cafe near the beach. There are playgrounds for children, and there is a children's room inside the hotel. An animator works with children in the Greenwald country house. Babysitting services are available. The hotel has comfortable gazebos for relaxing. For sports lovers there is a tennis court, a football field, a beach volleyball court, and bicycle rental is provided. You can play table tennis and billiards at the property. Sports equipment is free of charge. Hotel guests can go fishing on the shore of the reservoir, horseback riding is organized for horse lovers. For guests arriving by car, parking is available. The country hotel "Greenwald" (Yekaterinburg) will help you organize a wedding, a banquet, as well as business meetings. Check-in time at the hotel is 16.00, check-out time is 14.00.
