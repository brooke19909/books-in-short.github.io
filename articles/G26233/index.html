Justiniano Deluxe Resort 5 * (Turkey / Alanya / Okurcalar): description of rooms, service, reviews

Turkish resorts can rightly be called the most popular among our compatriots. Of course, five-star hotels are in the greatest demand. One of them will be discussed in our article. Justiniano Deluxe Resort 5 * is located in the well-known resort village of Okurcalar. A wonderful hotel on the coast can be a great place to relax.

A little bit about the resort ...

In fairness, it is worth noting that Okurcalar is the warmest region in Turkey. The resort on the Mediterranean coast invariably pleases tourists with a mild subtropical climate. The village is an ideal place for a budget and good vacation. Here hotels of various levels are built. The infrastructure is developed exactly as much as necessary for a comfortable stay. And at the same time, there are no noisy establishments in the village that interfere with a relaxing holiday. There are no entertainments in the village itself. Therefore, you have to rely on the animation of hotels. In general, the resort is focused on those guests who will spend most of the time on site. The village is quite young, but at the same time it is developing quite dynamically. Every year, new complexes, restaurants and other infrastructure are built here. Soon Okurcalar will become a promoted resort, but for now you can have a good time.

The beaches of the resort are not the best in the country. But still they are quite suitable for relaxation. A gentle entrance to the sea makes the beach very comfortable for swimming. The rugged coastline has numerous bays. The coast is quite convenient for families.

It is worth noting that Okurcalar is a resort village. Therefore, a large number of hotels are located on its territory. Most of them are complexes of 4 and 5 stars. There are also several three-star hotels. Each complex has a vast and well-groomed territory. The resort is characterized by hot and dry summers, as well as warm and rather humid winters. The hottest period is August and July.

If you are choosing a place to stay for yourself, then you will probably try to analyze the reviews. Justiniano Deluxe Resort 5 * is a modern new hotel offering a good rest. According to numerous health resort visitors, the hotel complex can be safely attributed to those institutions about which very conflicting reviews are left. According to reviews, Justiniano Deluxe Resort 5 * is a place for relaxing unpretentious tourists. Therefore, if you are counting on any entertainment or sightseeing programs, then you definitely need to find another resort. Justiniano Deluxe Resort 5 * (Alanya) has a small and compact territory. She is well-groomed and very green. There are playgrounds. But there is not much space for walking.

The hotel consists of two buildings. Which one is better is a moot point. Many people think that the main body is better. It is located right on the beach. Unfortunately, during construction they did not at all take care of good sound insulation. The rest of the rooms are good for relaxing. Some of them overlook the sea. Apartments are cleaned regularly; there are no problems with this. In general, the number of rooms is good and new.

Resort Attractions

Okurcalar is a good place for a lazy vacation. If you want to do absolutely nothing, but just bask on the beach, then you should definitely visit the resort. But the nightlife in the village is a problem. More precisely, she is not here. If you want entertainment, you will have to go to Alanya. The infrastructure of the resort is just beginning to develop, so there are no night clubs here yet. But the resort can please silence and tranquility.

In fairness, it is worth saying that in the village there are absolutely no interesting sights. If you want to see something interesting, then you need to go to Alanya or Side. There you can admire the ruins of pagan temples, an ancient amphitheater, a beautiful fortress, a phosphoric cave, the Red Tower and other attractions.

Of the possible entertainment in the village, you should pay attention to rafting, tennis, horseback riding, volleyball, soccer, bowling and billiards. You can also visit the baths, saunas and spa centers.

Instead of an afterword

Hotel "Justiniano" can please its guests with a beautiful beach and the possibility of a good holiday. The hotel complex has its advantages and disadvantages. According to tourists, the hotel is suitable for relaxing unpretentious holidaymakers who are able to close their eyes to some nuances. The modest diet is offset by the beautiful nature of the bay and the beautiful sea. But if you want to get active animation and entertainment, then you should choose a more developed resort. Since Okurcalar is a place for a relaxing holiday. By the way, the local bay is good for children. The unique microclimate makes the resort convenient for families. A warm sea is almost always calm and quiet.

In general, the resort is of interest to a certain category of tourists. Not all vacationers are attracted to noisy places to relax. Therefore, Okurcalar is good for couples or older people. While the village is still being rebuilt, it cannot offer vibrant nightlife. But this is its appeal.
