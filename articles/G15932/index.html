Hotel Mirage Patong 3 * (Thailand, Phuket): overview, description and reviews

Each of us has the right to rest. Unfortunately, the modern inhabitants of our country have forgotten how to relax, they are so mired with everyday problems, work and the routine of familiar gray everyday life that they can not even fully enjoy the chosen resort. If you want to be alone with yourself, enjoy the beauty of the landscapes that open, the sound of the surf, the grandeur of the mountains and true Asian exotic, we invite you to Thailand. Of the variety of resorts we offer to stay on the island of Phuket. It is remote from the center of resort life in the country, immersed in the greenery of tropical plants and resembles a real paradise in the middle of the ocean.

Thousands of tourists come here every year who want to be alone with nature, meet the sunrises and spend sunsets on the coast. The atmosphere here is really amazing, even magical. Of course, in many respects the quality of rest depends on the correctness of the chosen hotel. Thailand is not a country where there are many first-class and fashionable hotels. On the contrary, most local hotels and hotels are far from ideal, do not correspond to the description indicated on the official website. Hotel Mirage Patong 3 * is a fairly popular option, it is offered as part of the tour to many Russian tourists. Let's try based on the reviews of real vacationers to evaluate it from all sides. Perhaps this is a great option for accommodation.





About hotel

So, the Mirage Patong Phuket 3 * hotel is quite young, it was opened on the chic island of Phuket in 2014, it immediately gained popularity among tourists. It is located in the central part of Patong Beach, surrounded by shops, bars, restaurants.

The hotel is large enough, it offers tourists and vacationers one of 128 rooms. Most of them belong to the standard category, there are also elegant deluxe rooms that will satisfy the taste of even the most demanding client.





Location

So, Mirage Patong Phuket 3 * hotel is located on the second coastline, just 500 meters from the coast. The distance from the nearest airport is only 40 km, so tourists do not have to travel a considerable distance after a tiring and long flight.

On the one hand, the location of the hotel can be called excellent, because the beach and all the necessary entertainment are here within walking distance. On the other hand, it is quite noisy in the evenings here, but the high-quality soundproofing of the rooms allows you to perfectly combat this problem.

Rooms

If you choose Mirage Express Patong Phuket Hotel 3 * for your stay, you can stay in one of the rather comfortable rooms. Each has a spacious bathroom with towels, bath amenities, beauty products and a hairdryer.

It may seem that on vacation you do not need a TV at all. But the administration of the Mirage Patong 3 * hotel is of a different opinion and equipped all rooms with flat-screen satellite TVs. You can put things in order with the help of an ironing board and an iron of a modern model. A powerful air conditioner is responsible for maintaining a comfortable microclimate in the room. In just a few minutes, it cools the air to the required temperature.





When checking into the hotel, all tourists will receive a password from the wireless Internet for free, and they will also be able to use the safe in the room to store valuables, documents and money. Reviews of tourists emphasize that the Internet, or rather the signal in the room is excellent, there is enough speed not only to exchange a couple of phrases and photos with loved ones, but also to watch your favorite movie before going to bed. You do not have to constantly leave things at the reception - the hotel administration took care of safety and comfort for every vacationer.

The rooms in the Mirage Patong 3 * hotel are spacious and bright, the furniture is new, modern, will satisfy the requirements of even the most demanding tourists and vacationers. Somewhat embarrassing is the wall made of transparent glass between the main room and the bathroom. And this despite the fact that there are no curtains in the bathroom. You should be extremely careful when taking water procedures, do not forget to close the curtains, so as not to become public.





Hotel infrastructure

For all matters of interest, vacationers can always turn to the friendly and hospitable hotel staff. Mirage Patong Phuket Hotel 3 * strives for comfort for vacationers, so the reception is open around the clock. Tourists can use the free parking on site. The fact is that in Thailand, in Phuket, bike rental is particularly popular, with which you can travel around the whole island. So now, if you choose this option for yourself, you do not have to worry about the safety of the rented transport.

All vacationers can use the elevator in order to go up to the top floor of the building and get into their luxurious room. And, of course, Mirage Patong 3 * (Phuket) offers 24-hour luggage storage. Even if your room is not ready yet upon arrival at the hotel, you can always use a left-luggage office, quickly change clothes and go to the beach - a great chance to use all the days of your vacation to the maximum.

Nutrition

Staying at the hotel requires breakfast. So, breakfasts at Mirage Patong 3 * (Thailand, Phuket) Patong are built on the principle of buffet. Breakfast is quite varied, represented by rice, vegetables, meat and cheese slices, toast, fresh fruits, cold drinks, flavored tea and coffee. Breakfast here is even better than in some hotels in Turkey and Egypt, you certainly will not stay hungry. Even if you arrive for breakfast rather late - all dishes will be presented in abundance.





You can have lunch and dinner at local restaurants - there are more than enough of them here. You can not only get acquainted with the dishes of national Thai cuisine, but also treat yourself to Russian and European masterpieces if you miss them during your vacation.

Service

Impeccable service and attention to every vacationer - all this can offer tourists Mirage Patong Phuket 3 * (Phuket). So, the hotel rooms are cleaned daily, bed linen and towels - as they become soiled. Every tourist is given 0.5 liters of water. For an additional fee, you can use the laundry service, tidy up your belongings. Your luggage will be delivered to your room by qualified porters.

Tourists who want to go on a morning tour can use the awakening service for free, having pre-ordered it at the reception. Moreover, you can order breakfast with you and eat on the road before a difficult and eventful day of sightseeing.

Entertainment

The only entertainment on site is an outdoor swimming pool located on the 8th floor of the building. The sea is often quite turbulent, even aggressive and wavy, a swimming pool will be a worthy alternative to it. Moreover, it becomes a real salvation for those tourists who decide to come to Phuket during the so-called rainy season, when there are not so many sunny days.





On the beach, you can rent sports equipment or entertain yourself with extreme water sports.
