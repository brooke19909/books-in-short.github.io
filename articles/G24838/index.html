Nika Island Resort 5 * (Maldives): description, service, reviews

Wanting to spend an annual vacation in a peaceful place, an unusual family weekend or a romantic weekend, many tourists buy last-minute packages to the Maldives. Kudafoludu Island, whose length barely exceeds 300 meters, is a sought-after resort among travelers around the world. For those who want to escape from the hustle and bustle of the city, forget about everyday problems and hardships, renounce human society and enjoy privacy, this five-star cottage complex Nika Island Resort is located on this oasis of life in the Indian Ocean, the description of which will be presented to your attention in the article.

general characteristics

In order to ensure complete isolation from the surrounding society and non-interference in privacy, the entire hotel complex Nika Island Resort (Maldives) is presented by detached bungalows equipped with individual access to the sea. This institution lives in its own time zone, different from the usual Maldivian.

The opening of the Nika Island Resort 5 * hotel falls on 1983. Its long history made it possible through trial and error to create a unique project that met the requests and wishes of each guest individually. The last renovation was made in 2011. The entire territory is full of exotic greenery, leafy palm trees and tropical inhabitants. This paradise is perfect for healing, because the cleanest air, which never knew production emissions, has a beneficial effect on cleaning the body and saturating it with useful trace elements through the air.

The distance from the international airport is considerable. In order to find yourself on this island, you need to use a seaplane transfer, the duration of which is about 25 minutes.





Accommodation Features

Pets are not allowed at the Nika Island Resotrt. Smoking is prohibited on site. The organization of additional space in the form of a clamshell installation is paid upon. Transfer order is required. There are rooms for people with disabilities, they are equipped with special facilities in the bathroom. Check-in starts at 14:00. A security deposit is required by credit card. The currency in which other payments are accepted is the dollar and the euro.

One child under the age of 2 years stays in a hotel with parents without providing an extra bed absolutely free. Upon arrival, an identification document is required. The staff answers all questions of interest, thanks to knowledge of several languages.

All guests of the Nika Island Resort 5 * hotel (Ari Atoll, Maldives) can order newspapers or room service. This service is not round-the-clock and is paid separately. Wake up lovers in the morning to sleep responsible staff at a predetermined time.





Accommodation

All bungalows have an interesting shell shape. They are built from natural materials that are harmless to humans. The architecture of the unique buildings, thanks to their structures, creates additional air volume and provides unhindered ventilation. The total number of apartments for living is 45 units. They differ in the presence of a terrace and the available benefits, dimensions and number of rooms.

Garden Room rooms were only built in 2011. They are the smallest, occupy 35 squares, do not have a private beach. Being surrounded by endless greenery is to the liking of each guest, which is noted in numerous reviews.

The origins of the opening of this institution were Beach Villa, Deluxe Apartment, Sultan Suite and others. Some of them are ideal for a honeymoon, and some are designed exclusively for families.

Among the comfortable elements of arrangement, you can distinguish a courtyard with a swing, a plot with a jacuzzi, an open-air shower, deck chairs. Villas located on the water have a three-level terrace.





Accommodations

To create a sense of detachment, the interior of the chambers has a unique classic design interspersed with extravagant elements. The walls are decorated with paintings and volumetric ships. The floors are covered with parquet, on which there are large flowerpots with green flowers and carpets. Along the perimeter of the room wall lights were evenly placed. In the color palette, preference is given to all shades of white. The daily order is maintained by tidy maids. Linen is changed every day.

The furniture set is made of first-class wood and is represented by a double bed, side tables, chairs or a bench, a wardrobe and a table.

The need for air conditioning is eliminated due to unique architectural findings. There is a telephone for communication with the 24-hour reception desk. Safe allows you to save documents and gadgets intact. A satellite TV gives you the opportunity to follow events in the world.

For a comfortable stay in the room there is a set for making hot drinks - coffee and tea. All necessary kitchen utensils and a set of utensils are in the cabinet with glass doors. Wi-Fi throughout does not require additional charges. Replenishment of the minibar - for a fee. The bathroom has a chic design, decorated with ceramics, equipped with a marble countertop. There are personal hygiene products, disposable slippers and clean bathrobes.





Nutrition

When checking in, each guest chooses his own food system, which he wants. The program followed by the hotel is All Inclusive. The buffet is served. Sometimes, if weather conditions permit, it is organized under the open sky on the shore. Such a meal inspires and gives unforgettable emotions. The main specialization is international cuisine. However, you can enjoy the masterpieces of Maldivian and Italian cuisines. Local chefs are high-level professionals who can surprise every traveler with their culinary talents.

Alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks in the bar, fresh drinks, wine and beer are included in the All Inclusive concept. Only foreign and elite alcohol needs additional payment.

When is it better to go on vacation to the Maldives?

In order to answer this question, it is worthwhile to carefully study the weather in the Maldives for months. In the period from December to April, the largest number of tourists is here, since the water temperature in this period ranges from 28-30 degrees. But from May to November it is cooler - 26-27 degrees. This season has a low season. However, the Maldives accept tourists all year round. There are no dates for the beginning and end of the beach season. Blue waters and white sands await tourists at any time convenient for them.
