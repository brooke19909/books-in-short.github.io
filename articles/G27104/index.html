How to weave a rubber phone case?

How to weave rubber products? The answer to this question is known to many girls. But the guys are fond of this occupation, creating original crafts using this interesting material. And it should be noted that the products can be very different. For example, at the present stage, the issue of how to weave a phone case is relevant. After all, bracelets with rings are easy to make. This is already a familiar lesson for the craftswomen. And the cover, in addition to its decorative purpose, will help protect the device from scratches. And it will be interesting to create it, as this is something new in the field of weaving.

What will be required to create a beautiful and original case?





Before weaving a phone case, you need to buy Rainbows Loom elastic bands. And it should be noted that a lot of them will be required, since the product will turn out to be large. You can purchase several packages with multi-colored rubber bands at once. And you can buy sets in which the material is already laid out in different shades. It should be noted that the second option is more preferable. With it, it will be possible to form a real ornament, not chaos.

Why did you become interested in how to weave a phone case? The thing is that the material is not expensive. Accordingly, you do not have to spend a lot of money on the purchase of an important and stylish accessory. You can create it yourself.

In addition, a machine is required. With it, you can get wonderful weaves. It will not work without a hook. It should be noted that a plastic tool usually comes with rubber bands. However, many craftswomen prefer metallic devices. How to weave a phone case on the machine? This will be discussed further.

A fascinating and interesting activity

After reading the review, you can make sure that there is nothing complicated in this process. All actions are simple. Naturally, if we are not talking about how to weave a phone case without a machine tool. Manually making this accessory will be harder. But probably. The main difficulty that you will encounter in the process of creating a cover is ensuring the evenness of the material. For this reason, irises must be selected so that they have the same density. Do not forget about the size of the product. Occupation may seem monotonous. But this is only at first glance.





So, you are interested in the question of how to weave a rubber phone case. You need to quickly familiarize yourself with the sequence of actions through which you can achieve the desired result.

It is important to properly configure the equipment

In its form, the finished case will resemble a pouch. In such a product it will be very convenient to carry your mobile device. First of all, you need to prepare the machine. Use equipment that has 24 pegs. They are also called pegs. Connect the pegs so that the letter O comes out. The recesses located in the left row should be directed in your direction. In the right lane, everything should be done the other way around. And if you want to answer the question of how to weave a rubber phone case, you need to approach this stage responsibly. Setting the equipment will help to close the loops correctly. The result is an excellent and beautiful product.





What are the advantages of the machine?

Crafts made using this machine will look neat. This will not work if you want to figure out how to weave a phone case on your fingers. In addition, when working with equipment, you will not need to be distracted to check how smooth the rows are. Everything else, when using the machine, the jumping off of the gum will be minimized. And this is important enough if you have never before been engaged in weaving.

How to make the basis for the product?

How to weave a rubber phone case? First of all, we need to form the bottom of our future gadget bag. To do this, you need to use monophonic elastic bands, preferably a dark shade. You need to hook the material in the form of an infinity sign diagonally between the rows. In other words, each gum should be twisted in half. After that, they need to be hooked on the left peg, which is the first. In the middle of the gum should be twisted, and then put them on the second peg, located on the right.





As soon as you get to the last action, you will need to perform another series on the diagonal. Only do everything in the opposite direction. Look at the result in the upper projection. Crosses should come out. If everything is done correctly, then the basis for the bottom of our future cover is ready.

We continue to weave a cover

How to weave a rubber phone case? It is necessary to shift the loop to the bottom. Due to this, it will be possible to prevent their slipping. After that, you just need to alternate the colors. As a result, vertical stripes should be formed. In order to decorate the product with an ornament from diagonals, it is necessary to repeat the above sequence in a circular cycle.

Using a hook to create a product





We continue to figure out how to make a cover using rubber bands. Colored material should be caught in a sequential manner, starting from the bottom left and ending with a full circle. After that, you will need to take the hook. It is necessary to remove the loops. The tool will need to pry the lower elastic bands of a dark color, stretch them over a multi-colored material and discard. Such actions will have to be performed throughout the entire circle. After this, you need to shift the row and string a new one using multi-colored rings. Continue to act in the same sequence that completed the previous lap.

The final part of the work on creating a cover

How to weave a rubber phone case? As a final step, you need to perform some fairly simple steps. It is necessary to stretch the loops into each other. A similar action will need to start to be performed approximately two laps before the end of the weaving process. Try to stretch them through each other. After that, fix them so that they do not come down or get loose. In one place you will need to bandage the elastic bands using the Loom Band. At the same time, the specified shade must be observed. After that, you can, without fear, begin to remove material from the posts using a hook. At the same time, you should carefully try to straighten the resulting product with your fingers.

Additional decor will not be redundant

Now you know the answer to the question of how to get the original product for your phone. When the case is completely ready, you can begin to think about how to design it. Additional decor will not be redundant. At the present stage, you can find a variety of trinkets and pendants. They will be able to decorate your creation. The use of fringe will be interesting enough. You can use the figures, which will be made of all the same gum. No costs are expected. And making figures after creating the cover will be very simple.

Conclusion





We hope that this review helped you figure out how to weave a beautiful case for your phone. Good luck in this rather original and fascinating process.
