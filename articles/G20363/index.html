Error 628: Description and Remedy

Our life is full of various gadgets and devices for accessing the Web. Today, every, even the most ordinary watch, sometimes goes online. Why, then, from time to time in devices appears error 628?

The newer and hi-tech the device, the thinner and more complex it is. Failures and errors occur regularly for various reasons. In the conditions of a frantic pace of life and complete dependence on the Internet, it is very important to quickly eliminate the communication error and resume the connection. The article will deal with error 628.

Definition

Error 628 means that the Internet connection was interrupted by the remote device. Most often, this problem is faced by subscribers of Beeline, MTS and Megafon operators. What to do when error 628 appears when connecting to the Internet provided by different providers?





Reasons for the appearance

There are usually two reasons for this:

  1. The connection was suspended by the provider due to non-payment or non-compliance with other terms of the contract.
  2. The connection cannot be established for other reasons not related to the provider. Usually this is the congestion of the server or the modem itself.

That is, the device will give an error 628 when, regardless of the reasons, a connection was not established between the home modem and the server of the service provider.





This happens when:

  1. The provider's server is overloaded and cannot handle the traffic. In the event that the modem has been used for a long time and suddenly error 628 appears, most likely the reason lies precisely in server overload.
  2. If the modem is new and, having not yet worked, reports such an error, then the problem may be in the incompatibility of the operating system of the computer and the modem device. Incorrect installation and configuration of the modem is also possible. Or you just need to replace the modem model. After all, cases of marriage are not excluded.

As already mentioned, subscribers of Beeline, MTS and Megafon companies most often face this problem. How to get out of this situation when connecting to different operators?

Bug fix

How do Beeline subscribers fix error 628? This operator adds one more to the two reasons for this error. To access the network using a Beeline modem, you must have at least ten rubles on your account.

Of course, if the modem was recently purchased and used for the first time, you need to contact the operator or, most likely, just replace the modem model. In the case when the server overload was the cause of the error, it is better to consult with the operator. But when there are more than ten rubles on the account, and the operator says that everything is in order, and the modem has been used for a long time, then what should I do? In this case, you can reinstall the program yourself.





Through the "Control Panel" you need to remove the Beeline program. Then you need to disconnect and reconnect the modem. The installation window will appear on the screen. You must reinstall the program on the computer. After reinstalling the program, you need to create a new profile, while the old one must be deleted. Only profile parameters should be as follows:

  • In the APN column, you must select the "static" position and specify - internet.beeline.ru.
  • Access number is defined as * 99 #.
  • Username and password set in the form of beeline.

For future connections, you should use this particular account.
