How to send money from Megafon to Megafon: all ways

Sometimes there is a need to transfer money from one mobile to another. The reason for this may be different: either you ran out of money and you asked a friend to transfer them to your balance, or, conversely, you were asked to do so.





This article will discuss how to send money from Megafon to Megafon. But, besides this, the article will touch upon other topics. It will be agreed how to cash money and how to transfer it to a QIWI wallet.

The first method: "Mobile Transfer"

The first method involves the use of the Mobile Transfer service, which is provided by the Megafon operator itself. Therefore, you will be fully confident that the operation will be absolutely legal and that your money or the money of your friend will not be lost along the way.

Before the story of how to send money from Megafon to Megafon, it is worth making a reservation and telling about the requirements, commission and limits.

If you use the Mobile Transfer service, 0 rubles will be withdrawn from the sender, if the transfer is carried out within the same region. Otherwise, the commission will be 5 rubles. At one time, you can send a maximum of 500 rubles to a subscriber in your region and 5 thousand rubles if the subscriber is in another region. As for the limits, they are as follows: within one region, 5 thousand rubles and no more can be sent per month. And in the event that the regions are different, it will be possible to send 15 thousand rubles within a month.

Now we will go directly to how to send money from Megafon to Megafon





To do this, you will need to dial a USSD request. Its format is as follows: * 133 * "transfer amount" * "payee number" #. Please note that the number must begin with a seven. After sending the request to the mobile, an SMS with a code will come. Now you need to dial the USSD request as follows: * 133 # "code from SMS" #.

The second way: "Money transfers"

If you have not figured out how to send money from Megafon to Megafon using the previous method, then you can use another network service - Money Transfers.

The commission will be 6.95% of the transfer amount. The maximum transfer is 15 thousand rubles. The limit is 40 thousand rubles per month.

In this case, you need to send SMS to number 3116. In the text field, specify the recipient's number and the transferred amount in the following format: "number" "amount". There must be a space between the values.

As soon as you send SMS, another number should appear on your number, in which the number is indicated. Now you need to indicate in the answer the number that was indicated in it.

By the way, in this way you can not only make transfers only between the numbers of the Megafon operator, but also send funds to the numbers of other operators.

Transfer money to QIWI wallet

If you do not know how to transfer money from Megafon to a QIWI wallet, then now we will analyze everything in detail.

  1. The first thing you need is to enter your personal account on QIWI.
  2. Next, go to the "Replenish wallet" tab.
  3. You will see replenishment methods, select "From phone balance".
  4. Find your operator and select it.
  5. In the field, enter the amount by which you want to replenish the wallet.
  6. Click the "Confirm" button.
  7. An SMS will be sent to the phone, where a confirmation code will be indicated.

After that, your wallet will be replenished by the amount you specify. By the way, for some regions the commission is 0%.
