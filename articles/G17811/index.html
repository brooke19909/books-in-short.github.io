Hotel Nestor Hotel 4 * (Ayia Napa): overview, description and reviews

Guests will spend unforgettable days in the modern hotel Nestor Hotel 4 *. Ayia Napa, in turn, will delight you with its gentle sea, white beaches and rich tourist infrastructure.





Name history

It is worth noting that the name of the hotel Nestor Hotel 4 * (Ayia Napa) was not chosen by chance. Nestor is one of the ancient kings, whose wisdom and courage were sung by Homer in his immortal works. He was not only a ruler, but also a warrior who was not afraid to enter the battlefield. Thus, calling the hotel the name of such a hero, his leadership set a fairly high bar and pledged to comply with it.

Location

Quite a convenient location boasts Nestor 3 * (Cyprus, Ayia Napa). Reviews say that this is the very center of the popular tourist area. The hotel is literally 200 meters from the main boardwalk, as well as 150 meters from the luxurious beaches. The international airport is just over 40 km away.

206 comfortable rooms can accommodate guests of the Nestor Hotel 4 * hotel. Cyprus, Ayia Napa will give you only a positive experience if you stay in luxury apartments in the following categories:

Brightly decorated room with natural wood furniture. Through a sliding glass door you will find yourself on your own balcony, from which you will have wonderful views.
  • Family room is a large room for 4-5 people. In addition to a double bed and a pull-out sofa, there are also bunk beds for children. Convenient layout provides free movement of a large number of guests within the room.
  • Deluxe will delight guests not only with its spacious area, but also with an elegant design. Bright walls, bright accents and natural wood - all this creates an atmosphere of luxury and homeliness. An incredibly beautiful view of the sea opens from the balcony.
  • Junior is a luxurious two-room suite, which is suitable for a romantic, family vacation, as well as for hosting large companies. The bedroom has a huge bed, opposite which there is a dressing table and a TV. In the living room - upholstered furniture and a coffee table, and most importantly - a panoramic window through which sea and mountain landscapes are visible.
