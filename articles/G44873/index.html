Bunny Costume: The Easy Way

Various masquerade costumes for children (photos clearly show this) are performed by several methods. A hare costume (one of the simplest and most traditional) can be done like this:

  1. Make up from the finished things of the child, suitable in color and style, using additional decoration.

  3. To make completely with your own hands, starting with the construction of the pattern, by the specific standards of the child.

The first way is the easiest, fastest and cheapest. If you need to make a suit, for example, for tomorrow or the coming days, choose this option. Think about what elements of your child‚Äôs wardrobe can make such an outfit.

Work sequence

If you want to make a fancy dress for a child with your own hands, you can choose the image of a bunny. Such an outfit is made like this:

1. Take a white turtleneck and pants.





2. Cut out of paper for patterns, and then from fabric, such as pink satin or white fur, a circle or an oval.





3. Sew the prepared part onto the turtleneck (t-shirt).





4. On white pants, shorts or a skirt, attach a tail in the form of a pompom made of fur or cotton.





As a result, you will get a finished outfit. It remains to make a hat and decor details.





  1. Cut the ears out of white paper or cloth.
  2. Paint the front side pink.
  3. Fasten the parts to the hair band with wire or glue.
  4. Sew mittens from fur or guipure.
  5. Make white shoes and knee-highs for the girl.

Such a fancy dress for a child can be made for a boy - based on a white shirt and trousers. In this case, pink color, of course, will not work, so it‚Äôs better to use either white fabric or blue decor. Ears are easy to decorate with tinsel along the contour.

Making a bunny costume for a pattern

This method will require you to search for a ready-made template. It can be downloaded, printed in the right size and used to cut parts from fabric. In this case, enough initial knowledge and experience in the field of sewing. You can work as follows:

1. Use the sample pattern from the following illustration.





2. Print the blanks and, based on the given sample, build your own pattern of the required size.

3. Cut out the elements.

4. Prepare a white cloth, such as fleece. Lay the parts on the fabric and pin it with pins.

5. Circle the contours with chalk, make seam allowances and cut out the parts.

6. Fold the paired elements with their faces inward, knit, and then sew on a typewriter, stepping back from the edge to a distance equal to the allowances for the seams.

7. On elements where there are rounded details (everything except a T-shirt), it is possible to carefully make cuts along the allowances so that the fabric does not tighten after being turned out.

8. Turn out products (mittens, slippers, ears and a basis).

9. Sew the ears into the seam of the headgear parts. Turn the resulting "hat".

10. Decorate the product with eyes and a nose (sew plastic or embroider).

The third method, when you build a pattern yourself, is suitable for more experienced ones, as well as when you have enough time to work. An unambiguous advantage is that the outfit will be made perfectly according to the figure of the child. In the process, you will be able to try on and refine.

Fairy costume

Each girl will surely find an elegant lush dress and matching shoes. Work as follows:

  1. Buy fabric in the tone of the dress and cut out elements for making a vest, wrap, bolero or just a spectacular collar.
  2. Sew the selected part. Decorate it along the contour with a decorative cord, tinsel, brocade ribbon or other decor.
  3. Make a crown out of paper.
  4. For strength, fasten the part to the wire. Use a hair band as a base.
  5. Decorate the crown with beads, sparkles, sequins.
  6. To make a magic wand, take a core, a kebab skewer or any other element suitable for the role of a skeleton.
  7. Wrap the workpiece with satin ribbon, decorative tape, or corrugated paper. To prevent the decor from moving out, glue it with a thermal gun as the rod is wrapped.
  8. Decorate the top with a butterfly, asterisk, ball or other suitable detail.

Spectacular outfit is ready.
