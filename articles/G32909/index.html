Models of Robot Vacuum Cleaners

The range of such household appliances differs in the presence of additional functions in them. For example, the simplest ones that can only clean at the touch of a button are the Irobot 500-550, 200-400 models. They do not have an automatic start, allowing the vacuum cleaner to turn on in the absence of owners. The 560-600 Series iRobot Roomba model receives positive reviews thanks to the ability to program cleaning a week in advance. The Rumba robot vacuum cleaner of a series 700 and further is intended for use in large rooms. A special lighthouse is installed in each of the rooms, and the device moves around the apartment, removing them from the queue. In addition to robotic vacuum cleaners that carry out dry cleaning, there are Scooba washing models. But such household appliances do not clean carpets, but only wash parquet or tile.
