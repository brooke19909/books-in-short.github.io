Camps abroad for children and adolescents. Sports and language camps abroad

Even before the onset of summer, one and the same question begins to worry all parents - "How to organize the leisure of their adored offspring?" Naturally, adults want the kids to be safe, fed and passionate about something during the summer months. In addition, it would be nice if the child received new knowledge and gained invaluable experience. Of course, in an ordinary urban environment, this is not easy, or rather, simply impossible. Therefore, parents are looking for camp tickets for the child. But how to make him study during the holidays? After all, ordinary shifts are designed only for rest and recovery. In this matter, children's camps abroad come to the aid of moms and dads. They successfully combine relaxation and an exciting language learning program. The child is gradually immersed in a new world for him and quietly begins to speak a foreign language. In addition to such shifts, there are specialized sports camps abroad. They are designed for children who are seriously passionate about sports and cannot even forget about it even in the summer months. Therefore, today we have prepared a brief overview of those overseas camps where the kids will have fun, interesting and informative.

A few common words

Children's camps abroad provide invaluable experience and knowledge that a child will never gain elsewhere. Shifts are usually formed for schoolchildren from seven to eighteen years old, very rarely camps accept children from five years old.

Outwardly, these institutions are not much different from similar ones in Russia: the kids live together, play, go on excursions and play sports. However, if we are talking, for example, about a language camp, then from the very beginning of the shift, children are immersed in an atmosphere that is the best suited for learning the language. It is spoken by educators, attendants and peers. In addition, special classes are held daily. They are made out in the form of games, competitions and excursions. Thus, the child quickly adapts to the new environment and easily remembers a foreign language.

In sports international children's camps abroad, the system of work is similar to that already described above. The only difference is the emphasis on sports, although the kids are also involved in the language. Thus, the child receives double benefits and, having arrived home, feels full of strength and new knowledge.

Usually, camps abroad have their own rather large territories on which children are accommodated. But in some cases, accommodation in host families is also provided. When purchasing a ticket, parents are always informed about the features of accommodation.

What is included in the package?

When sending their child to a camp abroad, mothers and fathers should not worry about his safety. Almost every minute the child will be supervised by adults, because this is certainly included in the price of the purchased ticket.

Keep in mind that for the amount paid, the student receives a transfer, medical insurance, comfortable accommodation and full-board meals. Also included are excursions, all recreational activities and educational materials. After training, the child must be issued a certificate confirming the course taken.

Learning Features

Sports and language camps have a similar training program. Of course, in each case there are a lot of nuances and features, but still the general features are clearly traced.

The shift in the camp is designed for three to four weeks. In language shifts, classes are conducted for forty-five minutes. There should be at least twenty per week. Thus, in four weeks there will be eighty of them. As you know, this is an extremely rich course that will give the child a lot of new knowledge.

In sports children's and youth summer camps abroad, the program is no less eventful. The shift participants have at least two workouts per day, which are replaced by theoretical studies, entertainment and language learning.

Usually the program in the camps is composed of three blocks. The first includes games and entertainment. It can be theatrical performances, vocal competitions, modeling, choreography and the like.

The second block consists of sporting events. They do not occupy the last place in the recreation program, even in language camps. In Malta, for example, a child will be taught beach sports. And in Switzerland, he will try climbing. Each country offers its own sports program.

The third block is excursions. Their frequency depends on many nuances, but less than twice a week they are usually not organized.

The most popular destinations for children's holidays abroad

Not all countries have bases for organizing summer vacations in different directions. And our compatriots are not interested in all directions, as their children want to give the best and most interesting. Most often, Russians purchase the following packages for their offspring:

  • To camps in Malta.
  • To the camps in Cyprus.
  • To camps in Turkey.
  • To the Bulgarian camps.
  • To the Spanish camps.

We will consider all the possibilities of rest in these areas.

Which of the children did not dream of spending the summer on a sunny island surrounded by sea waves? Bulgaria: organizing children's activities

Russians come to this country with pleasure, since everything here reminds us of our homeland, but nevertheless it has some tangible touch of the West. Every day, children take part in a new role-playing game, all this is accompanied by incredible special effects and animation programs. If desired, English will be taught to students for an additional fee.
