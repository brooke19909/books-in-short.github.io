DIY birthday gifts for a guy: for every taste and budget

We often have to buy various gifts. And often they are not like birthday parties or anniversaries, they sincerely thank you and put them on the far shelf of the wardrobe. As a result, there remains only a useless amount of money spent. You can make your own birthday gifts for a guy . It is both economical and not so difficult, such a gift is difficult to take away, because it is made with warmth and love.

New hobby



There are many techniques that you may not be familiar with, but with their help you can easily make birthday gifts for a guy with your own hands. For example, scrapbooking. This very popular hobby allows you to create handmade cards and other DIY gifts from paper. It can be an album about your happy vacation abroad or a cover for a wedding disc. There are many master classes at which you will be taught to correctly position the elements on the sheet, add various decorations and photographs.

The next type of creativity is decoupage. This technique allows you to transform things beyond recognition. For example, you can decorate your boyfriend‚Äôs favorite chair, decorate his leather cover with a passport or give him a box with pleasant wishes. Decoupage technique will help you with this. Its essence lies in the fact that a very thin napkin is glued to PVA glue and varnished. As a result, the picture simply merges with the thing. Using simple decorating techniques, you can turn an ordinary iron can into a beautiful stand for pens, pasted over with coffee beans. This is also a good gift. You can also decorate homemade wall clocks. And in the quilling technique, you can beautifully decorate the frame with your joint photo. Your half will appreciate these birthday gifts. You can make a comic present for a guy with your own hands.

A couple more options

The best gift made by yourself should reflect the hobby and personality of the person to whom you give it. If your half is a programmer, make him a clock from floppy disks or a declaration of love on a binary code. A calendar of keyboard keys is also suitable. If he likes to skate, then make him a felt souvenir in the form of little skates. This is a good non-standard gift, which is sure to be remembered. With your own hands you can make a pendant for good luck, and soft slippers. You can sculpt his portrait from mastic, he will even be edible. Or give him a collage that you personally created in Photoshop, you can just cut the photos. And if finances do not limit you, then print a real magazine about him. Gather an interview with all your friends, add photo material, give it to the printing house, and you will get a masterpiece. All guests, including your boyfriend, will just gasp from such a gift.
