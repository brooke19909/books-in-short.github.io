Le Tong Beach Patong 3 * (Phuket, Patong): room description, service, reviews

Thailand is a country that attracts a large number of visitors and different regions of the world every year. On its territory there are a large number of beautiful places, there is truly unique nature, as well as a huge number of attractions. Tour operators often recommend visiting Phuket in March. This month there is the most optimal temperature for relaxation - the air is warm and moderately humid, it is very comfortable to stay in such an atmosphere. Hotel Le Tong Beach 3 *, which is located on one of Thailand's most famous beaches, Patong, is often recommended as a place to relax.

general information

The resting place first opened its doors to tourists in 1998. From the very moment of its opening, the hotel attracted the attention of vacationers with its interesting location and the conditions that surround it. Throughout the entire period of existence of Le Tong Beach Patong 3 * (Phuket / Patong), repair work was carried out on its territory and in the building several times, during which technical equipment was improved and the interior of the rooms was recently updated, which tourists noticed and appreciated.

Le Tong Beach is located in Thailand, in one of its most famous regions - Patong Beach in Phuket. It has a wonderful climate, which is quite mild and warm. The temperature conditions are ideal for spending holidays, even with young children.

Entertainment

In the comments about the rest, one can often find an opinion about the positive attitude of tourists to the entertainment provided by the hotel. First of all, among them water sports are very popular. They can be found on the coast, located 70 meters from the main building of the hotel. All of them are paid and are mainly intended for fans of extreme sports. In particular, skiing and diving are especially popular here.





Much attention of tourists is also given to excursions, which can be carried out both for the whole company, and individually. Especially popular are tours of local attractions, which are carried out by professional guides - natives of the local population who are fluent in Russian. According to tourists, the cost of such trips, provided they are ordered in Thailand, as a rule, is lower than in Moscow. That is why they recommend to carry out their order at the place of rest, and not at the time of acquiring a ticket to Phuket.

The hotel also has several types of entertainment. Guests of Le Tong Beach Patong 3 * (Phuket / Patong) spend a large amount of free time in the water zone, which is located in the open territory. Beach

The coastline is literally 70 meters from the main building of Le Tong Beach Hotel (Phuket / Patong Beach). Such a close location allows vacationers to get to this place in just a couple of minutes. Tourists in their reviews note that the coastline here has a gradual descent into the ocean. This allows you to relax with your children, moreover, the water here for a long period continues to remain warm and pleasant for swimming.





The entire coastal part here is covered with fine and rather soft sand, a small number of sun loungers and umbrellas are installed on it, which carefully protect vacationers from the harmful effects of sunlight. On the beach of Le Tong Beach Patong 3 * hotel, for entertainment, there is beach volleyball, for which a small playground is provided. All necessary equipment for the game can be taken at the hotel, and completely free of charge.

Prices

The cost of a trip to Phuket is relatively low. On average, it is about 55-65 thousand rubles. Such rates are based on two vacationers and subject to their stay in a standard room for seven days. As a rule, such a package includes transportation costs, including transfer, as well as payment of all necessary insurance. Tourists consider this cost to be quite optimal for the proposed level of relaxation. Often, our compatriots recommend this vacation spot to their friends and relatives.
