Renew traffic on Tele2: two options

There are two options for connecting additional traffic:

  • automatically (after exhausting gigabytes / megabytes of the main package or Internet option, this function is not available for all tariff plans and options for the Internet);
  • at the initiative of the subscriber (the package can be connected at any time, even when there is a balance of the main traffic, you can choose any package, having independently determined the necessary amount of traffic)

Automatically increase Internet volume

This function is relevant for users of ‚ÄúBlack‚Äù tariff plans. It is automatically connected to those subscribers who switched to any of these TPs or bought a SIM card. The principle of the service is as follows: every time when the rest of the traffic in the main package approaches zero, five hundred megabytes for 50 rubles will be automatically charged . Such connection of additional traffic is possible no more than five times in one month. To extend the Tele2 traffic (Black tariff, etc.) in automatic mode, the subscriber does not need to perform any action, unless, of course, he previously turned off this option.





The control commands are as follows:

  • * 155 * 261 # - activation;
  • * 155 * 260 # - deactivation;
  • * 155 * 26 # - check the status of the option (connected or not).

After all five packages are spent within one month (billing period), it will be impossible to use the Internet.

Monitoring the status of Internet packages

It is recommended that the subscriber periodically monitor the status of packages provided as part of an option or tariff plan, so as not to surprise that the traffic on Tele2 has ended. How to extend it and whether it is necessary to do it (or is it worth waiting for the new month) - the client decides.
