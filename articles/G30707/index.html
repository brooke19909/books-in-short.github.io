LCD "Stolichnye Polyany" by GK "Morton": address, reviews

If you want to buy your own apartment in a new building, you are counting on a well-maintained area and developed infrastructure, we suggest paying attention to the Stolichniye Polyany residential complex. The project is being implemented in the south-west of Moscow by a reliable developer. Our task is to evaluate the project from all sides within the framework of this material. Reviews of real interest holders will help to make an objective presentation. We will try to talk about all the advantages and disadvantages identified.





about the project

LCD "Stolichnye Polyany" ("Morton") is a new complex of 5 panel buildings located in the south-west of Moscow. According to project documentation, the first floors will be reserved for commercial premises: shops, cafes and restaurants, organizations that provide various services to the population. The design feature of the project will be high ceilings and designer decoration of the entrances, as well as two entrances: from the courtyard and from the street. The complex may become one of the best in New Moscow, therefore, naturally, it attracts the attention of buyers. The developer promises to build social infrastructure facilities on the territory of the complex, a multi-level parking, saving yards from congestion of cars.

Those who have already visited the construction site and purchased an apartment complain about the density of the buildings: the houses are so close to each other that the neighbors see each other from the windows. Dark courtyards-wells create permanent twilight, and the complex is not enough to accommodate all the necessary infrastructure.





Location

LCD "Stolichnye Polyany" (Moscow) is being built in South Butovo, not far from Bitsevsky forest park. The place is excellent: a lot of greenery, recreation areas and options for organizing leisure activities. If you read the reviews of the first customers, you will see that many of them were really attracted by the location. All necessary social infrastructure facilities are within walking distance. Object address: Moscow, st. Glades, intersection with the street. Skobelevskaya.

Residents of the district note that the construction is in full swing, of course, during the construction, a forest belt is cut down, but the developer is trying to preserve the green spaces to the maximum. It is due to perennial trees that it is possible to isolate houses from the road, ensuring maximum comfort and safety for each resident.

Transport accessibility

LCD "Capital Glades" attracts buyers and transport accessibility. A metro station has already been opened in South Butovo, there are several public transport stops near the house , from where you can reach any point in the capital. For the convenience of car owners, a trip to the Kaluga highway was built. It is currently undergoing a reorganization: new road junctions are being built to reduce traffic density and reduce the duration of traffic jams.





Infrastructure

Many residents complain about the lack of infrastructure: the complex does not yet have its own kindergarten, school, only a few shops, playgrounds and sports grounds. But according to the plan of the developer, the residents of the complex will be able to use the school, kindergarten and clinic located within walking distance from it. The project includes the construction of a kindergarten, which will help save young parents from a number of difficulties. It is also planned to build a comprehensive school for 450 students, but it is not yet known in what form its activities will be carried out: budget or commercial.

Underground parking guarantees the comfort and safety of the car owner and eliminates the already cramped courtyards from the accumulation of vehicles. The "chip" of the project was the spacious pantries provided for use by each tenant. This is an additional space outside the apartment where you can store bicycles, prams, blanks.

For all those who drew attention to the LCD "Stolichniye Polyany", apartment layouts are an important criterion. So the developer offers a wide variety of planning solutions. For young families, one-room apartments with a spacious kitchen-living room and bedroom will be an excellent option. By combining the bathroom and the bathroom, it is possible to save living space.

Price policy

What attracted residents of the residential complex "Stolichniye Polyany"? Real estate prices are more than affordable. An apartment here can be purchased from 2.8 to 9 million rubles. Only for the affordable price of apartments, buyers are ready to forgive the complex some of the shortcomings. Moreover, the developer offers favorable conditions for the purchase of an apartment. At the construction stage, you can buy real estate with significant savings. The developer provides installments for the selected apartment until the house is commissioned. And cooperation with the largest partner banks allows you to arrange a mortgage on favorable terms.





Documenting

Almost all equity holders expressed their concerns. As soon as most of them bought an apartment in the residential complex "Capital Glades", the construction literally froze. But, as it turned out, the lull was due to the takeover of Morton Group of Companies by PIK. In April 2017, after all the necessary documents were completed, construction at the facility was resumed, with an unprecedented scale. Residents of the district note that the work does not stop, either day or night.

To summarize

LCD "Stolichnye Polyany" is an excellent option for those who are not ready to say goodbye to the developed metropolitan infrastructure, but are tired of the hustle and bustle, the rhythm of life, the dust of the metropolis. The developer also needs to work on the infrastructure of the complex: equip recreation areas, walking and bicycle paths, build a kindergarten and a school on the territory.
