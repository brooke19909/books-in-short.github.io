Hotel "Blaumar Blanes" 4 * Spain, Costa Brava: photos, reviews

The Mediterranean coast of Spain is considered one of the most popular destinations among Russian tourists. In this part of the country, hotel accommodation costs significantly less. In addition, travelers can enjoy not only the clear sea and sandy beaches, but also the famous sights of Catalan culture. The most popular resort here is considered the Costa Brava. On its territory there are many budget and luxury hotels. A good option for middle-class tourists is the 4 * Blaumar Blanes Hotel in Spain. We will tell about its location, number of rooms, level of service and fresh reviews of guests in our article.

Features of rest in Costa Brava

Costa Brava is a resort in northern Spain, which is located at the very border with France. Its distinctive feature is a picturesque rocky coast and a medieval fortress built by the sea. At the same time, the climate of this province is considered the coldest among the Spanish resorts. In the summer, the air temperature rises to 25-28 degrees, and the sea warms up to 23-24 degrees. The high season lasts from the second half of May to October, but some tourists come here in the winter. In January, in the Costa Brava, the temperature drops to 10 degrees. You can get to the resort by plane. The nearest airports are in Girona, Barcelona and Reus.





The Costa Brava, where the Blaumar Blanes Hotel is built, is best known for its clean beaches, which have been praised by environmentalists. Maintaining their cleanliness here is the state. The resort itself consists of many small coastal towns. It is easy to travel between them, both by bus and by car. Among the leisure facilities there is a water park, a mini zoo, many bars, nightclubs and discos. For history buffs, the Verdakier Center has been opened, where you can look at the monuments of Iberian and Catalan culture.

General information about the hotel Blaumar Costa Brava

This resort was built in 1990. It is noteworthy that he shares the territory with another complex - Blaucel. They have a common infrastructure, and an underground passage was made between their buildings. The same company manages both hotels. The only difference is that the Blaumar Hotel in Spain has less spacious rooms. It consists of one seven-story building, a sunbathing area and an outdoor pool. The last renovation was carried out here in 2007, when the rooms and common areas were renovated . In total, the hotel offers tourists 175 rooms.





The staff at the complex speaks French, English and German. There are no Russian-speaking employees here, however, in the high season, many travel agencies send guides here to help tourists get comfortable on the spot. No pets are allowed in the hotel, including guide dogs. When registering, you will need a passport or other identification document with a photo, as well as a credit card. Check-in starts here from 13:00 and lasts until midnight. Guests arriving at night are provided with a packed lunch as dinner.

Hotel Location

Tourists, describing the hotel "Blaumar Blanes" 4 * (Spain), in the reviews of 2016 note its favorable location. It is built on the first line of the coast, so you can get to the beach in 2-5 minutes. The sea is approximately 100 meters from the main building. The complex is surrounded by other hotels, so there are no large shopping centers, nightclubs, bars and restaurants nearby. To get to them, tourists are forced to go to the center. However, it is only 1.5 km away, so you can walk to it on foot. You can also call a taxi or get by public transport. The nearest water park is 8 km away, while the famous Tossa de Mar Fortress is 14 km away.

The nearest airport is Girona, which is 32 km from the hotel. However, most often tourists come to the resort from Barcelona. Its airport is larger, so the cost of a flight is much cheaper, and flights fly much more often. However, Barcelona is much further - 85 km. It takes about 2-3 hours to get to the hotel by bus. You can also take a taxi in 1.5 hours, but the cost of such a trip will be much higher.

Room Information

The housing stock of the Blaumar Blanes 4 * hotel in Spain, the photo of which can be viewed in this article, consists of 175 rooms. Most have an open, furnished balcony. Some of the windows on the upper floors have sea views. The living rooms are small and furnished with a minimum set of amenities. As a rule, a room consists of a bedroom and a combined bathroom. In winter, all rooms include heating. The rooms have parquet floors. Maids clean the rooms daily and bring clean towels to the bathroom. They change bed linen only 2 times a week. Four rooms in the hotel are fully equipped for guests with disabilities.

The following categories of apartments are offered to guests by this hotel:

  • Standard Room - accommodates three adults or a family with two young children. There is a double bed and a sofa bed. Area - 17 square meters. m. For a fee, tourists can purchase a VIP service. The price includes the use of a safe, Internet and a mini fridge.
  • Single room - a small room with a single bed. The bathroom is equipped with a shower. Area - 9 square meters. m

  • mini-bar - provided in the room for a fee, while filling in its price is not included;
  • Wireless Internet - can be connected only for a fee;
  • a small plasma TV to which several Russian channels are connected.

Catering

Beach and Pool

Blaumar Blanes 4 * hotel in Spain does not have its own stretch of coastline. Tourists resting here visit the public beach. Admission is free, but sun loungers, sun umbrellas and towels are issued here for a hotel fee. If you do not want to overpay, you can relax by the outdoor pool located at the main entrance to the hotel. It is filled with fresh water and does not heat up. Sun loungers and umbrellas are provided free of charge, and beach towels and mattresses are secured. In winter, tourists can visit the indoor heated pool at the Blaucel Hotel.

Holidays with young children

Most of the hotel guests are tourists who come here with young children. In the complex you can live with kids of all ages. For children under two years of age, a cradle is provided in the room. However, you will have to pay for its use separately. If necessary, tourists can rent a stroller. The hotel charges a deposit for it, which is fully refundable after its use. A special children's menu is available on request at the restaurant.





For safe swimming in the adult pool there is a shallow compartment for children. In high season, the hotel has a group of animators entertaining little guests. In July and August, children's discos are held. If the child speaks English, then he can be left in a mini-club. You can play with the kids at the playground.

Negative hotel reviews
