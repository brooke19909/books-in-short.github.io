Two main standards for mobile communications

At the moment, the most widely used are mobile devices of two standards: GSM and CDMA. Each of them is a digital standard. But the signal transmission in each of these cases is carried out differently. In the first case, the frequency range is divided into parts, each conversation occupies only a small part of the spectrum. In the second case, the encoding allows the subscriber to use the entire radio frequency resource. As a result, in this case, the quality of communication is an order of magnitude better. But today devices for mobile networks of the first kind have become more widespread today. This standard was introduced much earlier and at the time of the announcement of CDMA-solutions was already actively used. Supported Devices

A lot of devices are oriented towards functioning in cellular CDMA networks . First of all, these are mobile phones of the corresponding standard. They have a relatively low level of functionality: conversation, text and multimedia messages. In some cases, this list may be extended by data transfer. So the answer to the question: ‚ÄúCDMA phones - what is it?‚Äù - is such a definition: "This is a device that allows you to make calls, send and receive text or multimedia messages, or connect to the Internet in CDMA networks." The second type of device that can be used in such networks is modems. They can perform only one task - to ensure the transfer and receipt of data from the global web to a PC. Another type of device is mobile Wi-Fi routers. They do the same thing as modems, but only in this case, the presence of a computer is not necessary to make a connection. The router creates a wireless computer network to which any device with a Wi-Fi transmitter can be connected. The last type of device is smartphones. These are mobile devices, the capabilities of which are expanded through the use of special software. In this case, you can also watch videos, and listen to music, and chat on social networks ... Even as a mobile Wi-Fi router, you can use it.
