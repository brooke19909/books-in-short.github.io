Need a break? Batumi is the best place for a vacation

Everyone used to go on vacation to Turkey, Bulgaria, Thailand. The outbreak of the crisis painfully hit the pockets and wallets of the country's population. Already in the last holiday season, people began to take a closer look at travel options to the Crimea, Gelendzhik, Sochi and Abkhazia. I would like to recall a chic resort on the Black Sea coast in Adjara, this is Georgia - Batumi. Rest there will remain in memory for a long time. It is located only 30 km from the border with Turkey, but the service and prices are much higher than Turkish ones. It is pleasantly surprising that all residents of Batumi, unlike Turkish resorts, speak Russian.





Batumi resort

The clean sea, pebble beach, stretching along for 10 km, make Batumi's vacation unforgettable. Behind the beach line, a walking area with lanterns, benches, palm trees and bicycle paths blend in seamlessly. And the ferris wheel over the sea offers gorgeous views of the old part of the city of Batumi. Moreover, accommodation prices will pleasantly surprise you. And rest in the private sector of Batumi will delight the local rich flavor. It is necessary to book accommodation in advance, since there are much more tourists than those who wish to rent it. Therefore, you must begin to fuss about the place of residence much earlier than the start of the holiday season.





National cuisine of the resort

Fans of outdoor activities will find many interesting excursions in Batumi. Batumi was first mentioned in historical chronicles of the 4th century. A rich historical past is reflected in the architecture of this city. We strongly recommend visiting the Piazza, on the territory of which the most fashionable cafes and restaurants are located. Georgian cuisine diversifies the rest in Batumi. Reviews about her are the most encouraging. The most popular dishes are barbecue and Ajarian khachapuri with egg. Local lemonade and tarragon taste from childhood. After them, drinking our local purchased drink is unrealistic. It is also recommended to try cheeses, khinkali, roast, kebab. And you will not try such delicious dishes anywhere else.





Evening pastime

For an evening promenade, a seaside park with "singing" fountains on Lake Ardagan is ideal. And the photo on the background of the 8-meter sculpture of Nino and Alim, symbolizing eternal love, will remind you of your relaxation in winter evenings. Batumi remains in the heart forever. It was about Nino and Alima that Kurban Said wrote his novel. About the love of a Muslim youth and a Christian princess during the First World War. The statues are in constant motion, then approaching, then moving away from each other. In the evening, they periodically change their colors. There is also a cable car. Batumi evening bird's-eye view is something.

Batumi's main drink is chacha





Another attraction of Batumi is the Chachi tower. They say that once a week a chacha pours from the fountain for 10-15 minutes. This drink is almost the national drink of the Adjarian city of the country of Georgia (Batumi). Rest will be more enjoyable if you do not abuse this drink. And in 2011, they built the house of justice in the form of an inverted bottle. Interesting design. Connoisseurs of Georgian wines will find the most exclusive drinks at affordable prices in the Adjara wine house. Anyway, what kind of vacation in Batumi without tasting true Georgian wines? You will always remember their taste. You can also try homemade wines, as an option to diversify the rest of Batumi. The private sector is replete with ads for the sale of homemade wines, the recipe of which is passed down from generation to generation.

Historical values

Fans of beautiful mountain views are advised to visit the waterfall with a sculpture of St. Andrew the First-Called, which is located near the town of Sarpi in the direction of the Georgian-Turkish border, and the 20-meter waterfall Makhuntseti. The arch bridge of the 11th century of Queen Tamara is also located there . About 25 medieval bridges have been preserved in Adjara, which it is advisable to visit in order to diversify the rest in Batumi. Reviews of historical antiquities are full of every guide. Fortunately, they are sold in almost any kiosk or store. History buffs will appreciate one of the oldest Georgian fortresses - Gonio. On the territory of this ancient building is the grave of one of the apostles of Christ - Matthew. You can also look at the ancient sewage system, heating.

Family holiday





A whole day will not be enough to view every corner of this Edenic miracle. And there are so many viewing platforms with amazing views! Well, what kind of vacation in Batumi without a water park? It is the most popular place for family entertainment. The service here is top notch. Agree pleasantly when you are everywhere welcomed, welcomed in your native language and constantly smiling? In Sochi, this has not happened for a long time. Add to this you can share a clean, free beach without fences and signs in Batumi, the rest of which you will love forever. Time just stops here.

In general, are you planning your vacation? Batumi is a paradise for tourists. For a vacation this place is ideal. Nowhere else will they arrange and ennoble you in every possible way, as in Batumi, your vacation. The private sector is, of course, a budget travel option. The most spoiled tourists can find a hotel for every taste and budget. There are also elite restaurants, hotels, as well as smaller establishments filled with local flavor. This is one of the places on the planet that you want to return to again and again. For one trip it is impossible to explore all the main charms of this Georgian resort, so the city of Batumi will always be glad to receive guests.
