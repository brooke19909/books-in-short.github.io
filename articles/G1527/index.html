Hotel Yadis Hammamet 4 * (Tunisia, Hammamet): description and reviews of tourists

This magical state named Tunisia is located in the northern part of the African continent and is washed by the unusually warm and gentle waters of the Mediterranean Sea. In bygone times, conquerors from Africa left a huge mark in the history of this territory, and the French presented a knowledge of the most beautiful language to the people of this area. Each year, the former French colony welcomes a huge number of tourists from both far and near abroad. Tunisia is very popular due to the fact that the island has a great many clean beaches with white sand and unusually blue waters of the sea.

Hammamet Resort

At a distance of 65 kilometers from Tunisia itself is Hammamet, which is its cultural capital. A huge number of thalasso centers have been built in Hammamet. The city has a rather rich historical base, and the local mood just has to relax and enjoy the surrounding landscapes. The atmosphere of general peace and tranquility here is successfully combined with the unbridled fun that comes with the advent of darkness.
