Esperides Family Beach Resort 4 * (Rhodes, Greece): description and reviews of tourists

If you want to visit Greece and have a great time, then the hotel Esperides Family Beach Resort 4 *, located on the island of Rhodes, is perfect for these purposes. Today we will get to know this hotel complex more closely, and also find out what impression our compatriots have on their stay in it.





Location

Esperides Family Beach Resort 4 * hotel is located on the largest Greek island of Rhodes in a place called Faliraki. Near the hotel there are such noteworthy objects as the water park Faliraki, as well as the springs and Kallithea beach.

Housing stock

Hotel Esperides Family Beach Resort 4 * (Rhodes Island, Faliraki) has 574 standard and family rooms. All apartments are decorated in pleasant light colors and equipped with everything you need for a comfortable stay (comfortable furniture, refrigerator, TV with cable channels, etc.). In addition, each room has a balcony with furniture for relaxation. The private bathroom includes a hairdryer and toiletries, which are regularly replenished. For the convenience of guests, the hotel building is equipped with an elevator. Apartments are cleaned daily and bed linen is changed several times a week.

Entertainment

The hotel complex Esperides Family Beach Resort 4 * (reviews of tourists who have been here confirm this) has a lot of opportunities for an interesting, pleasant and fun pastime. So, lovers of outdoor activities can play tennis, canoe and do other water sports, take part in aerobics classes and competitions held by tireless animators.

The complex has a large swimming pool with a children's section. Around it there is a spacious sun terrace with enough sun loungers and parasols.

As for the sea, the distance from the hotel building to it is only 100 meters. The beach here is very clean and pebbly. By the way, beach towels can be taken at the hotel, so it is not necessary to bring them with you from home or buy on the spot.

Esperides Family Beach Resort 4 * (Rhodes Island): reviews of Russian tourists

The first thing that travelers who come to rest with their families with children pay attention to is the fact that the hotel is perfect for accommodating even the youngest guests. So, according to our compatriots, they not only provide cots in rooms and highchairs in the restaurant, but also have a menu for the youngest guests. For example, here you will always find even special mashed potatoes (vegetable and vegetable with meat) that can feed even babies. Also, at your service are milk mixtures, ready-made fruit puree and much more. Older children, according to their parents, were also not bored. After all, the hotel has pools for young guests, water rides, and a mini club.

Parents also did not have to get bored. During the day, on the territory of the Esperides Family Beach Resort 4 * hotel, cheerful animation takes place, and in the evening there is an entertainment program. In addition, you can always have a great time on the beach, go to Rhodes or go on an excursion to a place of interest to you. By the way, many experienced travelers definitely recommend renting a car for several days and exploring all corners of this wonderful island. In addition, in this case, you can manage your time yourself and plan a route, which cannot be expected from an excursion tour.

As for food and drinks, there were practically no complaints from our compatriots. The majority of guests were satisfied with the conditions of accommodation and the level of service at the hotel.

In conclusion, we can say that, according to many of our compatriots, the hotel in question is a great place for a summer family vacation at sea.
