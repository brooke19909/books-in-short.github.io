Eurovillage Achilleas Hotel 4 (Greece / Kos island): tourists photos and reviews

Quiet friendly atmosphere and quality service will delight guests of the Eurovillage Achilleas Hotel 4 *. It has everything that tourists may need for a good rest.





Location

In a quiet and picturesque tourist village, the Eurovillage Achilleas Hotel 4 * (Greece, Kos Island, Masticharion, 85302) is located. It is 6 km from the international airport. Kos Town center is just over 20 km away. As for the main tourist sites, the distance to them is as follows:

  • Mastichari beach (2 km);
  • Hippocrates Cultural Center (2 km);
  • water park "Lido" (4 km);
  • Antimachia fortress (8 km);
  • seaport (14 km);
  • Kalymnos Castle (17 km);
  • Asklepion Kosa (18 km);
  • Chrysoerias Castle (18 km).

Number of rooms

Absolute comfort and homeliness will be provided to guests of the Eurovillage Achilleas Hotel 4 *. Guests will have access to the following options for accommodation:

  • Standard rooms can have either single or double modification. Guests are accommodated in single beds. Also includes a coffee table and chairs. There is also a workplace. Comfortable plastic furniture is installed on the balcony.
  • Family rooms consist of two rooms, each of which is equipped with a pair of single beds. Also in the room there is a soft sofa (in the unfolded form it is used for additional accommodation), a compact dining area and a workplace. One of the bedrooms has access to a private balcony.

Facilities

The rooms of the Eurovillage Achilleas Hotel 4 * resort are equipped with all necessary amenities. So, guests can use the following options:

  • individually controlled air conditioning;
  • small fridge;
  • satellite TV with a package of Russian channels;
  • electric hair dryer;
  • landline phone;
  • bathroom with plumbing and bath accessories;
  • furnished balcony;
  • safe with electronic combination lock.

Service

The staff of the Eurovillage Achilleas Hotel 4 * hotel treats its guests with care and attention. Guests will have access to this service:

  • 24-hour reception, the staff of which is always ready to come to your aid;
  • air conditioning in the lobby and catering facilities;
  • regular cleaning;
  • payment for services in non-cash form;
  • bicycles for rent;
  • mini-market in the territory;
  • currency exchange point;
  • computer club with cable internet access;
  • common lounge area;
  • wireless internet (5 euros per day);
  • free secure parking;
  • packed lunches;
  • there is a Christian chapel on the territory.




Entertainment

Eurovillage Achilleas Hotel 4 * (Kos) provides travelers with a lot of entertainment opportunities. Guests can organize their leisure activities as follows:

  • an animation program that includes daytime active entertainment and evening shows;
  • game room with billiards, chess, checkers and computer machines;
  • night disco;
  • outdoor pools with fresh water;
  • a mini-club where children will have fun and usefully;
  • mini golf;
  • darts;
  • karaoke at the bar;
  • a playground with a safe surface;
  • Wellness center where you can go through beauty and spa treatments;
  • gym;
  • sauna;
  • paved tennis court;
  • organization of excursions.




Catering establishments

Positive reviews

They will help you fully appreciate all the delights of a holiday at the Eurovillage Achilleas Hotel 4 * reviews about this establishment. So, this choice has the following undeniable advantages:

  • hospitable and smiling staff;
  • there are free sun loungers on the beach;
  • well-groomed territory literally immersed in greenery;

  • lots of fruit on the buffet menu;

Negative reviews

It is important to prepare for a number of negative moments that may accompany your stay at Eurovillage Achilleas Hotel 4 *. Reviews of tourists talk about such shortcomings of this institution:

  • the territory is rather boring, which is associated with poor animation;
  • monotonous food;
  • outdated interior rooms;
  • excursions at a hotel guide are two times more expensive than at local travel agencies;
  • in the rooms you can see cockroaches;
  • there are a huge number of mosquitoes in the territory (the situation is aggravated by the fact that there is no mosquito net on the windows);
  • paid use of air conditioning (plus a deposit for the remote control in the amount of 10 euros);
  • old plumbing;
  • a fairly small selection of dishes that would be suitable for baby food;
  • constant interruptions with the supply of hot water;
  • all animators speak only English (this is especially inconvenient when it comes to children's entertainment).

This accommodation option is suitable for unpretentious tourists who like a quiet pastime. Convenient location will be an additional advantage.
