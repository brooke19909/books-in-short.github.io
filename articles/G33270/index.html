Hotel Cinnamon Garden (Sri Lanka, Hikkaduwa): description and photos

Holidays in Sri Lanka are a great option to enjoy the golden beaches, azure waters, historical sights and just learn a lot of interesting things about Asian culture and traditions. It is in Sri Lanka that you can see the bright underwater world, visit noisy discos, go fishing, barbecue on the beach, feel like a surfer who conquers the surging waves, or try such entertainment as diving, exploring the sea inhabitants and the bottom of the coast .





About hotel

The small hotel is located on the southwest coast of Sri Lanka, in the Hikkaduwa area, which is quite far, 143 km from Colombo Airport and 22 km from the city of Galle. Bungalow with 9 rooms is easy to find on the main street Galle Road among many shops, bars and restaurants for every taste. Bus and train stations - 2 km from the hotel. You can move around the city by bicycle rickshaw, bus or taxi.

Cinnamon Garden is a cozy place 50 meters from the sea, and all this distance is a picturesque sandy beach stretching for 4 km. In some places you can find giant sea turtles. Here the ebbs and flows are weak, the entrance to the water is gentle and convenient, which creates excellent conditions for a comfortable beach holiday.

Cinnamon Garden features a pool. Guests can also visit the jacuzzi. The hotel is primarily aimed at adults and active young people. The national composition of vacationers is approximately the same number of Asians and Europeans. The ethnic composition of the guests may vary depending on the season. Type of relaxation is mainly beach, youth.

The Cinnamon Garden Hotel has the opportunity to hold business meetings thanks to its modernly equipped conference room. Reception, room service and reception services are available 24 hours a day. The interiors of the hotel are modest, furniture and dishes are inexpensive, but the premises are spacious and bright. Room cleaning is available upon request. The staff speaks English.





Infrastructure

Cinnamon Garden (translated from English. "Cinnamon garden") lives up to its name and is a typical building for Southeast Asia, surrounded by a picturesque garden in which you can find palm trees, cinnamon and other exotic plants. In fact, this is a modest resort that offers its guests a wide range of services:

  • wired / wireless internet;
  • telephone, payphone, fax;
  • daily fresh press;
  • conference hall;
  • car rental / transfer;
  • laundry / dry cleaning, as well as shoe shine;
  • currency exchange;
  • doctor's services;
  • storage of valuables.

The hotel infrastructure includes:

  • billiard room;
  • yoga room;
  • pool with a bar;
  • spa treatments;
  • bicycles for rent
  • the garden surrounding the hotel, which is quite convenient for cycling;
  • equipped terrace for sunbathing;
  • night club;
  • tour agency.

Rooms

Cinnamon Garden Hotel 4 * (Hikkaduwa) refers to a small resort hotel, which has no more than 10 rooms with ocean or garden views. On its territory there is a separate bungalow. The hotel has only family rooms and non-smoking rooms. Each has access to a balcony or terrace, equipped with a TV, fan, air conditioning, ironing facilities, mirror, minibar, refrigerator and telephone, and a hairdryer is available on request.

When booking a room, guests can rely on a separate bathroom, including a shower / bathtub, washbasin, bath accessories. Allergy-free rooms or with improved soundproofing are available upon special request. The floors in all rooms are tiled.

In the hotel you can find rooms from 20 to 50 square meters. m, and also there are conditions for young families who rest with small children. If necessary, the administration provides free cots in rooms or offers babysitting services for a certain amount.





Nutrition

Hotel Cinnamon Garden 4 * offers two types of stay in the hotel:

  1. VV - accommodation + breakfast (most often, eggs, toast, jam, butter, milk, tea or coffee are served for breakfast);
  2. NO - accommodation without meals.

On site, for a fee, you can have a meal in the restaurant, snack bar or visit the bar. Relaxing at Cinnamon Garden Hotel (Hikkaduwa), you can enjoy Asian and international dishes. Guests of the restaurant will also find a special menu, namely diet dishes.

Entertainment

Welcomes its guests Hikkaduwa. Cinnamon Garden Hotel is a hotel that is primarily aimed at active, sports and mobile tourists who love to spend free time walking in the garden, playing sports, games and other outdoor activities. At the hotel you can find activities for adults and children, such as:

  • aquapark;
  • water activities and sports;
  • diving;
  • fishing;
  • surfing;
  • snorkeling (diving with a mask);
  • sunbathing terrace;
  • billiards;
  • bicycles for rent;
  • yoga.

Tourists can visit interesting and fascinating excursions of various directions, read magazines or newspapers provided by the hotel, as well as visit a nightclub and enjoy a colorful show program. Guests can enjoy the azure sea at the public Narigama Beach, which is 50 meters away. If desired, visitors can stay on the beach, which is located within the hotel. Near the buildings there are wooden sunbeds, which can be used at any time. Thanks to the variegated underwater world, you can get a lot of positive emotions and impressions of the rest.





Additional Information

Cinnamon Garden Hotel offers pet-friendly accommodation. There is a special place for smokers.

Parking on site is free, which is quite convenient for those who plan to rent a car or moped. If you wish tourists, you can order a transfer from the airport, for which you need to pay extra. Near Cinnamon Garden Hotel are:

  • art Gallery;
  • gym;
  • cafes and restaurants;
  • Museum
  • night club;
  • aquarium;
  • casino;
  • zoo;
  • souvenir shops;
  • amusement park.

The hotel is equipped with all special conditions for people with disabilities. The resort is 3-4 km from the city center, where you can get by taxi, bus or walk on foot, enjoying the beauties of Sri Lanka.

Tourists reviews

According to traveler reviews, the Cinnamon Garden hotel is perfect for those who want to know more about the country, about the city, about historical sights, for those who are constantly on the move. Tourists are not "attached" to the hotel, namely to food or other events, so you can build your own plans for the day.
