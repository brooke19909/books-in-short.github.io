Cenger Beach Resort (Turkey): photos and reviews of tourists

Turkey is very popular with tourists around the world. One likes bright, funny, long-deserved fame Antalya, Kemer, Marmaris. Others choose less developed and less noisy places, for example, Kizilot. The hotels here are new, modern, with a wide range of services at affordable prices. Cenger Beach Resort SPA Hotel is considered one of the best. Its rich infrastructure and special location make it possible for everyone who is tired of the busy rhythm of life, fuss, endless worries and troubles to get a good rest. The hotel staff takes care of all this.

This is approximately on the border with the resort of Alania, which has long been chosen by Russian tourists. Manavgat, famous for its waterfalls and canyons, is only 13 km from the hotel. By public transport, the trip there takes about 15-20 minutes, depending on how many stops there will be at the request of passengers.



A little further, literally 5 minutes from Manavgat, is the ancient city-museum of Side. The nearest airport is Antalya, about 90 km from Cenger Beach Resort. If you use motor transport, the journey will take about 2 hours, which should be taken into account by tourists with kids. Excellent asphalt highway stretches along the coast almost all the time. The windows of the car or bus offer sea views on one side and hills with rare groups of trees on the other. Arriving guests are immediately struck by some privacy of the hotel. Description of the Territory

Since there are no other land contenders nearby, the Cenger Beach Resort hotel is quietly located on a vast territory - 12 thousand square meters. Wonderful flowers are fragrant here, palm trees, junipers and rhododendrons green all year round. The impressive lawns with emerald grass are also pleasing to the eye. The solid area made it possible to conveniently place two magnificent buildings on the territory, a wonderful adult pool with an island in the center, a mini water park, a bar, a children's pool, tennis courts, sports grounds, and a parking lot. There is even its own amphitheater, where concerts of touring artists, competitions and quizzes organized by animators take place.

Description of hotel infrastructure

Cenger Beach Resort is still brand new. It was put into operation in 2011. It consists of two stylish five-story buildings, between which there is a covered passage. The lobby of both buildings is spacious and bright. In one there is a reception desk, where there is a paid safe. Here you can also order dry cleaning, washing and ironing services, exchange currency, solve the issue of renting a vehicle. There are corners in the lobby where comfortable soft chairs are installed, beautiful lighting is arranged, and there is a lobby bar. Wireless internet works best here too.



Cenger Beach Resort in its infrastructure has an excellent SPA salon, gym room, bowling center, billiards, night club with a disco and bar, indoor pool, sports halls, two conference rooms equipped with all necessary equipment for conducting business negotiations and meetings. For the convenience of holidaymakers, small shops with various trifles, souvenirs and other goods necessary for rest are working on the territory.

Rooms

Hotel Cenger Beach Resort 5 * for accommodating guests offers 266 rooms located on five floors of each building. Elevators are available, but during peak season they are often busy, which should be taken into account, especially for those who find it difficult to climb stairs. When booking, it is advisable to inquire on which floor the ordered room is located. Also note that not all apartments have balconies. For some, this is not important, but someone dreams in the evening, when the heat subsides, to sit on the balcony, admiring the sunset. Room types are as follows:

1. Standard.

2. Family one-room.

3. Family two-room.

4. Economy.

5. For the disabled.

Rooms are 24 and 32 square meters, excluding the "economy" type. It is not very spacious, there are no balconies, and in some places even the windows are very small. Each room is equipped with a furniture set (single and double beds, bedside tables, wardrobes), there is a telephone, mini fridge, daily replenished with water, plasma TV, hairdryer, air conditioning. Separate apartments have bunk beds with stairs. The bathrooms are equipped with washbasins, toilet, shower. Hygiene products replenish as needed.

A distinctive feature of Cenger Beach Resort 5 * is amazing cleanliness. The rooms are cleaned every day, towels are changed, of which maids spread figures on the beds. Bed linen not washed, clean and tidy. There are no cockroaches, mosquitoes, bugs.



The windows overlook either the sea or the hotel grounds. The design of the rooms is not a throw, everything is in soft pastel colors, on the walls - paintings harmoniously fit into the overall interior.

The menu includes meat (chicken, veal, beef, lamb, turkey), fish (including grilled), dozens of salads and side dishes, delicious sweets. All this is superbly served and beautifully decorated. Two other restaurants can prepare dishes (Turkish cuisine, fish, seafood) by order of the guests. They work from 19.00 to 21.00 daily.



In addition, Cenger Beach Resort (hotel) in its infrastructure has 4 bars - in the lobby, near the pool, on the beach and in the disco club. It offers a variety of drinks and cocktails, seven types of which can be taken for free. You do not have to pay for Turkish alcoholic drinks. In addition, you can have a snack in the beach bar. Additional Information

The modern five-star hotel Cenger Beach Resort SPA (Side, Kizilot) is ideal for those who prefer a relaxing comfortable stay. Those who wish to make excursions to neighboring cities can use dolmushi (stop right in front of the complex) or rent a car. This service is provided by the hotel.
