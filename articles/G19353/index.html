Park Hotel Flagman, Ruza: description and reviews

If you are tired of the bustle of the city, want to relax in silence and breathe fresh air, the Flagman Hotel located in the Ruza district of the Moscow Region invites you to visit it . Ruza is a small ancient city where you can also get acquainted with amazing sights.





Description

Hotel-restaurant "Flagman" is located in the village of Babino, Ruzsky district, in an ecologically clean area. From Moscow, you can get there on the Novorizhskoe highway in just one hour. Very close to the hotel is the Ozerninskoe reservoir with a magnificent landscape. The hotel is a modern three-story building. Secured parking is provided for guests with a private car. There are also equipped parking lots where guests can place their motor vehicles and equipment for water sports.
