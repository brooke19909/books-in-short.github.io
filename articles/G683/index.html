Holidays in Israel in October: weather and reviews

Israel is a unique state in the field of tourism. In this small country, the traveler will find historical artifacts with a long history, a healing climate, impeccable service, entertainment for every taste. Even the beach season in Israel lasts almost all year round.

Israel in the fall

Israel is especially good in the fall. The holiday season is in full swing, but the intense heat is already subsiding. September is a continuation of the summer, but less sultry. In October, a beach holiday in Israel officially ends, and from the middle of November the rainy season begins. The uniqueness of Israel is that in its small territory fit seven climate zones.





Famous resorts are located on the shores of three seas: Red, Dead, Mediterranean. At different times of the year, you can choose a vacation spot for every taste. Autumn is also good because at this time of the year, vacation in Israel pleases with affordable prices.

October climate features

October for a vacation in Israel is a warm comfortable time, especially in the first half of the month. There are still many sunny days, the air temperature is high: + 27-30 degrees Celsius during the day, + 21-25 degrees at night. Water in the sea is also warm - + 27-30 degrees Celsius. Holidays in Israel at the end of October may be marked by the first rains. November is already a full-fledged rainy season almost throughout the country.

Weather at different resorts

The Mediterranean Sea is one of the best for a beach holiday. But in October, the Mediterranean coast of Israel is already quite cool. Tel Aviv is the most famous resort in this region. And although the air temperature is still quite comfortable during the day, the nights are already cold. It‚Äôs also quite windy.





In Jerusalem, located closer to the Dead Sea, the weather is almost the same. The most comfortable place to stay in Israel in October is Eilat, a wonderful resort on the Red Sea. It is sunny even in November. Although it is also cool at night, the weather allows you to bask in the sun and swim in the warm sea.

Things to do in October

According to tourist reviews, rest in Israel in October is a time of holidays. For example, a festival of fire and balloons. You can visit the Tel Aviv Cyclists' Festival or the Nethenia Classical Guitar Festival. And in October, the Israelites celebrate Judgment Day. At this time, strict observance of fasting and special rules of conduct apply. It is on this holiday that locals change their cars for bicycles for one day. October is also a parade of love, and the annual ancient festival of Sukkot.

Eilat - the best place for an autumn holiday

On the shore of the Gulf of Aqaba of the Red Sea, Eilat is located - the southernmost resort of Israel. You need to go to Eilat for a vacation in the fall, at least for two reasons: this is the warmest place in Israel at this time of the year and this is the only Israeli resort on the Red Sea.





The weather in October is very comfortable: sunny, warm, almost no rain. The resort is divided by the airport runway into the city itself and its tourist part. This part of the city consists entirely of hotels, restaurants, cafes, shops and beaches. The flight from Moscow to Eilat takes 4 hours. An additional comfort for Russians is that almost the entire local population knows Russian.

Choosing a hotel in Eilat

Depending on the financial possibilities in Eilat, you can choose a hotel, a separate villa, rent an apartment, room or place in a hostel. Israel is a country of not cheap, but very comfortable rest. Although the budget option is also possible to choose. In Eilat, hotels are located on the South and North Gulf coasts. The North Coast hotels are mainly five-star network complexes with impeccable service and a host of additional amenities for different groups of tourists. For example, one hotel specializes in healthy eating and active pastime of its guests. Another is designed for families with children. There are hotels where smoking is forbidden, noisy behavior, and there are hotels where you can have fun around the clock with friends at discos, in restaurants and bars.





An example of specialization in different customer groups is the Herods Hotel, which consists of three buildings. The first building is designed for couples with children, the second can not accommodate children, use phones, smoke, the third building is designed for very demanding tourists who prefer special amenities and privacy. Hotels of this category amaze with luxury and comfort. City four-star and three-star hotels are not so luxurious, but in terms of service they meet the best European standards. City beaches belong to the municipality. But almost every hotel has its own small beach, which rents from the city authorities. Many complexes have their own relaxation areas, spas and pools. Children's activities are also remarkably organized: there are special play areas and animator services in many hotels. From almost any hotel you can reach the sea on foot.

Beach vacation

Holidays at sea in October in Israel will be wonderful on any coast. You can extend this summer in Eilat. The local beachfront stretches for twelve kilometers. The beaches are equipped with everything necessary for a good rest: sunbeds, umbrellas, awnings, deck chairs. Near each vacation spot there are many small cafes and restaurants. Eilat beaches, like the city, are divided into two main zones.





On the south coast lies a coral beach. This place is very suitable for diving. The colorful sea bottom with colorful corals and outlandish fish attracts lovers of sea diving. Very popular is snorkeling. You can dive yourself or hire a guide who will show you the most interesting places. On the South coast, entry into the sea is difficult due to corals, so special pontoons are used for swimming to enter the sea. It is forbidden to feed fish and take corals with you as a keepsake. A substantial fine is provided for this. On the North coast are sandy and pebble beaches with a comfortable gently sloping seabed. There are wild beaches, mainly on the South Coast. Special attention is paid to the dolphinarium beach. It is good to spend time with children. There are many exotic plants and animals in the dolphinarium, there are beautiful artificial ponds. The local sandy beach is famous for its cleanliness. The main advantage of this place of rest is dolphins. They live in a natural sea lagoon and enjoy communicating with people. If desired, you can even swim with dolphins. Entrance to the beach is paid. Another interesting youth beach is called "Kisuki Sport". There are a lot of active options for a beach holiday. In the evening and at night fun parties are held. A real beach vacation in Israel in October and November is possible only in Eilat.

sights

Holidays in Israel in October - an opportunity to see a lot of interesting things in comfortable weather conditions. In Eilat, for example, you can visit the City of the Kings. This is a large entertainment complex with water attractions, mirror labyrinths and boat trips through mysterious caves. Be sure to visit the Underwater Laboratory. It is unique in that it is located on several levels, including underwater. You can go down 10 meters below sea level and through the huge glass walls admire the magic of the coral bay. Clear water provides perfect visibility. The observatory has an observation deck, from where an incredible view opens up not only on Israel, but also on Jordan, Egypt and Saudi Arabia. For completeness of impressions, you can visit the 3D cinema with moving areas. Closer with the underwater inhabitants can be found in the laboratory aquarium.





Families with children will be interested in visiting the ornithological center. A huge variety of birds live here. The tour will be very informative. Another great place for an unforgettable experience is Timna Park. This is a natural phenomenon, consisting of multi-colored cliffs of bizarre forms, formed in different eras. It is also worth visiting the Masada fortress, the monastery of St. Helena, stone mines. From Eilat it is easy to make an excursion to the ancient city of Petra, Jerusalem, Jordan. In October, holidays in Israel are best devoted to excursions. More than a hundred city restaurants offer vacationers dishes of national, as well as European, Asian, American cuisine. Eilat was created for tourists. Therefore, a large number of night clubs and discos, including beach ones, are open here. You can have a great time riding a yacht, sinking to the seabed or gliding along the waves on a sailing board.

Tourists reviews

Reviews of tourists who have already rested in a particular place are sometimes more important than advertising descriptions of travel companies. If you carefully study the reviews, you can find answers to many questions and avoid unwanted surprises. For example, before the trip, you can order and make an individual plan of excursions. A personal guide will accompany you on a trip to tourist attractions. Such a trip will not cost much more than a regular group tour. But during the individual tour you can take your time, get answers to all questions and, if you wish, deviate from the route.





According to tourists, a holiday in Israel in October is a great time for excursions, leisurely walks and a relaxing pastime. Many tourists advise relaxing in the fall in Eilat, in October sunbathing and swimming, in November - walking and enjoying the local beauties.

Useful Tips

The most valuable information that can be obtained from real reviews from tourists is practical advice. Here is some of them:

  • keep in mind that Israel has expensive roaming charges;
  • many free beaches available, but no locker rooms;
  • at the bus stops you can use free Wi-Fi, so it‚Äôs not out of place to download the phone application with the bus schedule online;
  • local buses have sockets for charging gadgets;
  • tips in cafes and restaurants are optional, they can be included in the bill at the request of the client;
  • short-distance excursions can be bought from local travel agencies, it is cheaper than from a tour operator;
  • it must be remembered that in Israel from 4 p.m. Friday begins Shabbat, most shops, cafes, pharmacies are closed, public transport does not work;
  • Holidays in Israel in October (Eilat) are the most popular, therefore it is better to book a hotel in advance, so you can save on early booking;
  • under the autumn sun it is not recommended to be long without sunscreen;
  • warm clothes will not interfere, since in the evening the air temperature drops sharply.

Autumn is a rainy season in Russian latitudes. But you can comfortably extend the summer holiday in the hospitable and sunny autumn Israel.
