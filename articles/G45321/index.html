How to create a bouquet of tulips

Spring, love, tenderness - tulips are associated with this. Their appearance on the markets and flower shops means the departure of winter and the onset of warm days. Well, spring is a time of romance and nascent feelings. You can declare your sympathy or just make a nice holiday gift by giving a bouquet of tulips.

Language of flowers

Each bouquet is unique in its own way and can say a lot. Despite their silence, flowers always carry a certain message from the one who gives them. So, how to decipher this message?

  • If you were presented with a bouquet of red tulips , then be sure that the man experiences strong enough feelings. Red is the color of passion, love and desire. However, such a choice may also indicate that your gentleman prefers mysterious and very difficult women.
  • Pink is an unusually delicate color, symbolizing a love feeling at the stage of its inception. It is customary to give pink tulips on first dates.
  • White tulips prefer romantics who value purity and innocence in women. It is also a traditional color for a wedding bouquet.
  • Yellow tulips are not a sign of separation. Yellow is the color of the sun, happiness and joy. It is customary to give these flowers for birthdays and other holidays with wishes of good and financial well-being. It is also an ideal bouquet for men.
  • Another male color is purple or lilac. In some stores you can find a bouquet of tulips even blue. A composition of flowers of such shades predicts success in business.

DIY bouquet of tulips

If you want to please loved ones and loved ones with a bright floral gift, you do not have to contact the florists in the salons. Not everyone can afford their services, but I really want to create an unforgettable and original composition. Therefore, we offer you some simple ideas for bouquets of delicate flowers.

  • Tulips are good on their own. Sometimes simplicity and conciseness are much more interesting than complex designs. Tulips are very beautiful and quite self-sufficient flowers. Gathering them together in a large bouquet, you will get a delicate and refined composition. However, it is worth trimming the stems of the flowers so that they end on the same level. The bouquet itself is easy to fix with a satin ribbon with a beautiful bow. Just do not use packaging cellophane in your masterpiece, this will immediately make it simple and cheap.
  • To a bouquet of tulips pleased you for a long time, you can use a little trick. In any floristic salon you can buy a special foamy sponge for floral arrangements. Specialists call it an oasis. It is also an opportunity to make a bouquet of unusual shape and suitable for interior decoration. For example, you can buy a small basket with low sides. Lay the oasis at the bottom, after soaking it with water. Now you can start decorating the bouquet. Take the leaves of the tulips themselves and strengthen them in the foam along the edges. Inside the green meadow you can arrange tulips of various colors. To do this, cut the stalk of each flower at an angle for convenience. Decorate the whole composition with beads on wire and decorative butterflies.
  • Tulips can be combined with other flowers. Beautiful neighbors for them will be roses, freesia, orchids, irises, callas and lilacs.
