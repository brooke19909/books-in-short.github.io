The best hotels of Kirish

For many years, our compatriots have chosen Turkey as a holiday destination for its luxurious beaches, excellent service and comfortable mild climate. Many go to the region of Antalya, to the southwest. There are places for youth and club holidays, as well as quiet charming coastal towns hidden from the rest of the world by mountain ranges. One of such resorts - Kirish (in Turkish Kiris) - offers guests a quiet measured beach holiday in a former fishing village.

Resort Description

Five kilometers from the large Turkish resort of Kemer, behind a high mountain is the small town of Kirish (in the local language - Kiris). On a small strip of land between the Mediterranean Sea and the Toros Mountains lies a quiet island of calm and tranquility. Previously, there was a small fishing village. The main occupation of local residents was fishing (well, contemplation of local beauties - a combination of mountain landscapes and the azure sea). Then, with the growth of the Kemer resort, everything changed. The first hotels appeared in Kirish. But even now, when the hotel network has expanded in the city , it still remains calm and quiet. It is an ideal place to relax on the sea.





Who is suitable for holidays in Kirish?

Holidays in Kirish differs from the bustling life of Antalya or youth Marmaris. The nightlife here is mainly only on the territory of the hotels themselves. Rest here is suitable for those who like to enjoy the views of pristine nature. Around Kirish there are many mountain ranges on which it is so pleasant to walk. Fans of outdoor activities in the mountains will also be interested in wandering and climbing.

It will also be good here for newlyweds and couples, including with children. The benefit of hotels for a good stay is enough, and tourists will not need anything.

Who may not be suitable vacation in Kirish?

You may not like a vacation in this place if you:

- Love crazy parties and clubs. Parties in Kirish take place only on the territory of large hotels, it makes no sense to get to the disco in the city, because this is a former fishing village, neither clubs nor discos have simply been built.

- To youth companies, for the above reasons.

In this case, it is better to choose a hotel in Turkey in Kemer. About Kirish reviews mostly only as a quiet place. Here are cozy hotels with luxurious pools, a measured course of life. Here you need to escape from the bustle of the city.

As for the choice of hotels, it is worth noting that despite the fact that the resort is only developing, here you can find a place for every taste. Kirish Hotels there are both economy class, that is, three or even two stars, and luxury hotels.
