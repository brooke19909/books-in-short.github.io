Huawei Ascend P6 - the thinnest smartphone of 2013

Huawei in 2013 introduced the Ascend P6 as the thinnest smartphone in the world. This Android-based device is released only 6.18 mm thick, and is also unusual in that it has a 5 megapixel front camera for self-portraits in high quality.





What is the news?

The Chinese company said that the phone should be a kind of "miracle" for this brand. One of the analysts rated his design as unique, but noted that he could significantly limit sales by the lack of support for 4G networks.

According to experts, this is undoubtedly the most amazing phone that Huawei has launched on the market in terms of design, quality and materials used to date.

Undoubtedly, many noted that Chinese manufacturers began to rapidly produce competitive devices in recent years, and this thinnest smartphone is their best release. However, its 3G status means a definite compromise when creating - to achieve the minimum thickness and at the same time make the gadget as cheap as possible.

The thinnest smartphone of 2013

Huawei claims to have developed one of the thinnest and narrowest boards in the history of electronics. Ascend P6 remotely resembles the HTC One, but at the same time it is thinner by 3 mm.





Also, the gadget is slightly thinner than the widely known Iphone-5 and Alcatel One Idol Ultra (as previously stated, it is the most compact and thin in the world).

The Ascend P6 phone is equipped with its own "tiled" interface from Huawei called Emotions and has proprietary software, including camera applications that can improve the quality of self-portraits.

In addition, the thinnest smartphone called Huawei Ascend P6 has other specifications, including:

  • rear view 8 megapixel camera;
  • use of one of the latest operating systems for 2013 - Android Jelly Bean 4.2.2;
  • 8 gigabytes of internal memory (relatively low amount), but at the same time there is support for 32 GB MicroSD cards;
  • The 1.5 GHz quad-core processor developed by Huawei.

Brand building

Huawei is a well-known brand from China, which has become a serious manufacturer of telecommunication-type equipment. It was founded in 1987, and continues to grow at a rapid pace.

The company's transition to the production of Android phones has been a great success, and the thinnest smartphone is a direct confirmation of this. According to various researchers, this company released 9.9 million smartphones in the first three months of 2013, making it the fourth largest device supplier after Samsung, Apple and LG.





Nevertheless, company representatives recognize that it is necessary to improve and develop their technologies, and promise to ensure widespread recognition of their products worldwide for five years. According to the director of Huawei, seven years ago, few believed in the resounding success of Apple, five years ago, few saw the market leader in Samsung, so they are still ahead.

Thus, the thinnest smartphone called Huawei Ascend P6 is far from the last device of this brand. Devices of this line will always be able to compete with the phones of companies such as Sony or Nokia, because of the more affordable price.
