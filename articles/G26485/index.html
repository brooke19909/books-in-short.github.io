Pensions by the sea with food in Yevpatoriya: overview, description, photos and reviews

Evpatoria, an ancient city on the Black Sea coast of Crimea, is a favorite summer vacation destination for many Russians. The presence of a mild climate (average air temperature: 22.4-22.6 degrees) and the gentle sea attracts tourists from different regions of Russia, and thanks to the sandy beaches and gentle entrance this city has become famous as a great place for spending family vacations with children . Natural conditions, healing waters and mud of the Crimea are an effective therapeutic and prophylactic agent recommended for use in the fight against many diseases.





Despite the fact that Evpatoria has a wide variety of options for accommodating vacationers, there are few hotels with the current all-inclusive system. In addition, there are no 5 * hotels, which can disappoint lovers to relax with chic.

To accommodate visitors, the city has hotels, resorts and boarding houses, many of which have their own beach, which is especially important in the hot season, when municipal beaches are overloaded as much as possible. When choosing the appropriate option, experienced tourists recommend giving preference to boarding houses in Yevpatoria on the seashore with meals included in the price. This can solve the problems of finding shops and markets, as well as cooking. The article provides information about the best boarding houses by the sea in Yevpatoriya, providing food.
