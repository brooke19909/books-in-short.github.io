"Russian Lotto": game reviews, secrets of victory

Everyone must have played Russian Lotto at least once. This is a rather old and fun entertainment that will suit both children and adults. You are given cards that are marked with numbers (in a row). They are closed by barrels, on which the corresponding numbers are indicated. Barrels are most likely wooden or plastic. One of the players takes these numbers from the bag (without looking). The winner, of course, is the one who covered them with barrels before everyone else, but during the game there may be minor wins (the one who closed one or more horizontal rows is considered the winner).



What is Russian Lotto? Lottery - many will answer. And indeed, this once family game turned into a means of earning. Only earnings for whom? For those who play, or those who provide such an opportunity? Most likely for the second. Nowadays, a lot of lotteries have spread, including the Russian Lotto, whose reviews differ. Some say that it is very interesting and addictive, others believe that it is a waste of money and nerves, especially since now you can play this game on a computer, phone, tablet and so on. For example, the reviews left about the Russian Lotto lottery show that many are interested in whether it is possible to calculate the winnings. In addition, is there any kind of effective game scheme. In essence, these are questions about how to win at the Russian Lotto.

For the most part, it is believed that winning here is just luck. But the organizers of the Russian Lotto television show, which was released in Russia, on the contrary, insist that every third ticket is a winning one. Although, in fact, judging by the mathematical calculations, the probability of winning is only five percent.
