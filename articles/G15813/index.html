What are the attractions in Courchevel?

  • First of all, skiing. For lovers of skiing, there are four levels (from 1000 to 1850 m), on which skiing is possible during the entire ski season, which lasts from November to May in these places. To climb up there are cable cars, lifts, a funicular. And for the descent - more than a hundred tracks of varying complexity, which vary in color (from green to black). Their total length is more than 150 km.
  • Golf. For lovers of this aristocratic game, there are 9 holes, 27 stops, of which 7 are indoor. There are bars and restaurants nearby, a terrace with sun loungers, and even an artificial lake.
  • Mountains and rocks. For lovers of mountaineering and climbing in Courchevel, there are several specially equipped sites designed for the most different levels of training.
  • Bathing. Oddly enough, to go for a swim and sunbathe, some go to Courchevel. Crimea and the Black Sea coast of the Caucasus, of course, are more suitable for this type of vacation. Nevertheless, in Courchevel many hotels have on their territory specially equipped swimming pools and spacious terraces where you can sunbathe, admiring the unique views of the Alpine nature. And in the area, located at an altitude of 1550 m, there is a whole entertainment complex with an outdoor 25-meter pool and a magnificent green beach, located directly on the hillside.
  • Mountain biking. There is everything you need for fans of this sport: bike rental, special lifts, equipped tracks.
  • Fishing. In local lakes you can catch lake salmon, several species of trout. In order to go on a mountain fishing, you must purchase a special permit - a fisherman's card.
  • Classical music. Courchevel hosts numerous classical music festivals. By the way, for most of the events, admission is absolutely free.
  • Shopping For those who decide to combine business with pleasure, going here on vacation: in Courchevel prices are very dependent on the season. At the beginning of summer, the discounts are just fantastic. Sellers try to get rid of the goods in order to make room for new collections, and in other places to get such discounts is simply unrealistic.
  • Local kitchen. For lovers of gastronomic pleasures there are many restaurants in which the prices in summer are also much lower.
