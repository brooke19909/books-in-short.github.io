Yavor Palace 4 * (Sunny Beach, Bulgaria): hotel description, photos and reviews

Yavor Palace 4 * (Bulgaria, Sunny Beach) is a large hotel located in the very center of the Black Sea resort. It has everything you need for a comfortable stay - two outdoor swimming pools, spacious and comfortable rooms, landscaped grounds, a restaurant with the all inclusive program, beloved by tourists. The entire infrastructure of the resort of Slanchev Bryag (Sunny Beach) is right at your fingertips, within walking distance. Nearby is such a attraction of Bulgaria, as the old town of Nessebar. It should immediately be said that the resort has two hotels with the name "Yavor". One of them is located away from the sea. It is lower in status than the four-star Yavor Palace. In this article we will comprehensively consider the last hotel. What do the reviews of tourists who rested there in the 2016 season say about him?





Resort specifics

It is hard to believe, but seventy years ago on this place there was only the sea and the sunlit coast. Unlike the old Nessebar in the south, this resort is very new, created absolutely from scratch in the late fifties. Therefore, counting on seeing some antiquities, historical sights there and simply Bulgarian color is not worth it. Sunny Beach looks the same as most new-fangled resorts around the world. But this is a very fun place. Discos, night clubs, bars and restaurants are here at every step. The largest and most famous attraction of Sunny Beach is ... it is its coast. Here is one of the longest beaches in Bulgaria. For ten kilometers stretches this fifty-meter strip of golden sand. On the land side, the beach is surrounded by dunes.

Where is the resort located Yavor Palace 4 * (Sunny Beach)? The address of this hotel is as follows: Slanchev Bryag, 8240. But tourists say that the youth resort with its developed tourist infrastructure is in full swing right behind the gates of the hotel. Reviews repeatedly mention the central location of the hotel. For many, this is a plus. Those who like silence in the evenings solved the problem by closing windows with double-glazed windows.



Yavor Palace 4 * (Sunny Beach): how to get

The largest city in the vicinity is Burgas. It is located thirty kilometers south of Sunny Beach. There is no railway or airport at the resort itself. Some hotels offer a shuttle service for a fee. How to get to the Yavor Palace Hotel on your own? Foreign tourists are accepted by the international airport of Burgas. The air harbor is located ten kilometers from the city. To get to Sunny Beach, you must first take the city bus number 15. Its stop is located at the departure terminal. The bus will take you to the Western bus station of Burgas. There are many flights to Sunny Beach. A bus ticket costs about six leva. For a price two and a half times higher, you can avoid the need for transfers. The Burgas Sunny Beach Transfer Shuttle bus company sends its cars to the resort directly from the airport. A taxi ride will cost at least twenty-five euros. But it only takes about half an hour.

Cases, territory

The location of the hotel is impeccable. It is located in the very center of the resort, but at the same time on a quiet street. Nearby are tourist hotels such as Kalina Garden and Kuban. Hotel Yavor Palace 4 * (Sunny Beach) photos are often depicted from the side of the pool. Turquoise water very effectively harmonizes with the terracotta hue of the four-story building with arched balconies. The building was erected in 2005. Therefore, it provides all the amenities for people with disabilities.

The hotel territory is decorated under a lush garden. Tourists often take pictures of winding paths winding between green bushes with red flowers. At the hotel, right in front of the only building, there are two swimming pools.



Where guests are accommodated

Yavor Palace 4 * (Sunny Beach) is a rather large hotel. In its four-story building are nearly two hundred rooms. All of them belong to the same category. But tourists, vacationing in large companies and having the opportunity to look at each other, say that each room is individually decorated. There are bedrooms with walls and furniture of warm caramel and sand shades, which are conducive to relaxation. And there are rooms with a "marine" theme, in which it is as if you hear the sound of the surf and where it is cool from the turquoise tones. The windows and balconies, by the way, are furnished, either overlook the garden or the sea. But the noise of foliage from the park and the splash of the cascade in the pool will lull you in the evenings. All rooms have a private bathroom with shower.

What tourists say about food in Yavor Palace 4 * (Sunny Beach)

The description of the hotel rarely goes without mentioning delicious food in the restaurant and an extensive wine list of bars. The dishes in the restaurant were for every taste. Tourists really liked the Bulgarian national cuisine. But fans of a healthy diet (boiled meat, unsalted, non-peeled, savory, etc.) were also satisfied. One could find vegetables on the tables - both raw and cooked. Tourists with nostalgia remember the afternoon snack in a restaurant. Amazing cakes were given there, and you could make fruit ice cream yourself. Connoisseurs of alcoholic beverages liked Bulgarian wine. Fruits produced local, seasonal, but in large quantities. At the height of the season, some lines were observed (especially when the group called in). But the waiters in the restaurant work quickly, they quickly replace the empty trays. Tourists mention that in the pool bar you can refresh yourself with juices, cola, non-alcoholic cocktails.





Beach and Pools

The gentle Black Sea splashes just three hundred meters from the Yavor Palace 4 * hotel. Sunny Beach is called not only youth, but also a children's resort. The local beach is very convenient for bathing kids. Soft sand without stones, very gentle entry into the sea, terrain (dunes), which dampens the wind. Reviews say the water is clean because there are no plants nearby. And even railways bypass the Sunny Beach side. The beach at the resort is public. Therefore, sun loungers and umbrellas on it are paid. There is enough entertainment on the beach. Reviews warn that when the sea is agitated, the water in the sea may be cloudy due to sand suspension. The hotel has two swimming pools - an adult and a children's. They are surrounded by a small sun terrace. The sun loungers and umbrellas mounted on it are free for guests. Beach towels are not provided at the hotel.

Facilities and service

A playground, a free hammam, a jacuzzi and a sauna are far from a complete list of services at the Yavor Palace 4 * hotel (Sunny Beach). Laundry services, parking, Internet access, minimarket, currency exchange - all this is also available for tourists. The reviews say that the hotel staff is very sympathetic to Russian tourists, which can be found far from everywhere in Central and Western Europe. Wonderful girls work at the reception, who will solve all problems with the room, call you a taxi, wake you up in the morning, organize an excursion, etc. If you arrived at the hotel with a small child, a cot will be installed in your room.





Excursions

Now consider where you can go from the hotel Yavor Palace 4. Sunny Beach is a new resort. There are no historical and cultural attractions in the city. But tourists urge not to be limited to staying at one resort. In order to visit the old Nessebar, you do not need to contact the tour desk. Most of the tourists who commented on this beautiful town on the peninsula said they visited it on their own. After all, bus service is very well developed in Bulgaria. From other interesting places, tourists recommend to go on a boat trip on a yacht, as well as visit the "Bulgarian village". To do this, contact the travel agency. The nearby hotel has a water park. And on the embankment in the summer there is a town of children's attractions.
