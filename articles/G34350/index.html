Instruction: how to disconnect on "iPhone" T9

In 1999, a programmer at Tegic Communications, Cliff Kashler, came up with an auto-matching feature on the keyboard according to what the user enters. This option is called T9 and is now widely distributed around the world. T9 is an abbreviation of the English phrase "Text on 9 buttons" (Text on 9 keys).

T9 is on smartphones of any platform. Moreover, it is active even on tablets. In particular, users of iPhone are actively using this feature.

However, like everything else in the world, T9 has positive and negative qualities.





Pros and Cons of T9

Undoubtedly, the main advantage of this option is help with typing. This was especially convenient when all the letters of the alphabet were located on 9 keys of the phone.

Although it cannot be denied that even now - in the era of touch smartphones - the T9 helps to print texts and type messages faster.

But often the function offers the wrong words. In addition, its panel takes up free space on the dial screen. For these two reasons, many users believe that this is not the most useful option, and are interested in how to disable the T9 on the iPhone.

In fact, this is not at all difficult.





How to disable T9 on the iPhone?

It should be noted that no special programs are required to deactivate this function. Now you can get to the bottom of the question: how to disable T9 on the "iPhone"?

To do this, go to the "Settings" section and find the "General" column there. In this tab, you need to find the line "Keyboard". In the "All Keyboards" section, a lot of labels will appear with the toggle switches turned on or off. If there is a green toggle switch after the word ‚ÄúAuto Correction‚Äù, then T9 is active. To deactivate it, you need to turn off the toggle switch.

Now you know how to disable T9 on the iPhone. It turned out to be really quite difficult to do.

If you are interested in how to disable T9 on iPhone 5, then you should not create a new request about this, because it is done in the same way as described above.
