Best saunas (Novorossiysk)

Sauna from ancient times was considered as a place where you can relieve stress after hard workdays. Such a rest is extremely popular among residents of Novorossiysk. Confirmation of this is the whole mass of institutions of a similar plan, concentrated in the city. Let's look at the best saunas in Novorossiysk with a photo.

"Fantasy"



We will begin to survey the saunas (Novorossiysk) from the Fantaziya bath complex. The facility is represented by three separate lounges, each of which has access to a steam room. Here you can relax not only in the Finnish sauna, but also in a traditional Russian bath. After visiting the steam room, guests of the complex have the opportunity to cheer up by plunging into a cool font, and then have a good time in a well-equipped recreation room.

To the visitors of the institution:

  • Finnish and Russian doubles;
  • a tub with water and an ice font;
  • fragrant oak brooms;
  • cable TV and karaoke;
  • massage chair.

Where is the institution located and what is the cost of renting a steam room? You can find a sauna at the address: Hvorostyanskogo Street, 20. With regard to prices, you will have to pay about 900 rubles for renting a sauna and a relaxation room here if the number of people in the company does not exceed 4 people. For each subsequent visitor, the staff of the institution asks for an additional 100 rubles.

"Tiger"



Probably, there will not be a more stylish and modern sauna (Novorossiysk) than the Tiger leisure complex. Visitors to the institution expect a cozy atmosphere and attractive interior, which has a good rest.

The customers of the complex have a choice of as many as 6 pairs. There is a large pool and a jacuzzi on site. The comfortable lounges are equipped with TVs with satellite channels. If you wish, you can play billiards here.

You can find a sauna at the address: Volgogradskaya street, 11. The cost of visiting the institution is from 800 rubles per hour.
