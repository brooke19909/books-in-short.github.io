Electro-acoustic guitar: features of choice

Choosing an electro-acoustic guitar is a responsible matter requiring a serious approach. Firstly, the use of the instrument is influenced by the musical genre. So, performers of flamenco or romances will need a classic electro-acoustic guitar, while rock-n-rollers and bluesmen will choose jumbo or western devices.



Going to the musical instrument store, it is necessary to clearly understand the purpose of acquiring the equipment. If the instrument is purchased for the first time, then perhaps the best choice would be a classic on synthetic strings - it will be easier to learn to play on it. That is why the classical (non-electro-acoustic) guitar is used in music educational institutions.

An ordinary guitar, whose homeland is Spain, has a characteristic case, which is manufactured to the same standards for all manufacturers. The sound quality of the instrument depends on the material. Only a device with a deck made of wood can fully reveal its potential.

If the standard "Spanish" is equipped with a timbral block, then as a result of this procedure, it will change its status.

We can say that electro-acoustic guitars appeared after this operation. True, an instrument with a plastic case stands apart, for example, the Ovation guitar invented in the last century.

When connected, this device is superior in many characteristics to other acoustics, but it has a dull live sound. The original American electric-acoustic guitar company Ovation has (unlike the Korean-Chinese counterparts) a fairly high cost.

However, do not discount the Asian instrument. Of course, the famous Chinese-made Fender and Gibson guitars are not comparable in sound to the original ones, but the price is their main advantage. In the Russian market, in its price segment , the Martinez electro-acoustic guitar is popular , which has good sound and an optimal ratio in terms of price and quality.
