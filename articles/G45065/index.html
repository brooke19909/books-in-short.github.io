Sochi and Adler embankment: vacation spots in the tourist capital of the Russian Federation

Sochi, the sea, the promenade ... it looks like paradise! And indeed it is. Sochi embankment is one of the most remarkable places in this wonderful city. In general, for the sake of justice, it is worth noting one nuance. The embankment is the place that, in principle, is considered the most attractive in any sea city. But we all know that Sochi is a special city. So it's worth talking about its interesting places separately.





Briefly about the sights

So, the embankment of Sochi is located on the Black Sea in the Central and Khostinsky districts. In general, this sunny resort city can be said to be entirely, entirely located along the sea. This can be understood only by looking at the map.

People call the promenade of Sochi "Promenade". This is the most favorite place for recreation and walks of both indigenous people and visitors who visit the city for leisure or business. It stretches from the Marine Station to Pushkin Avenue. In principle, a leisurely, walking step, you can walk it in about forty minutes. The total length of the embankment is about two kilometers. Many people think that this place is ideal for romantic walks. In any case, the mass of pleasant impressions is certainly provided.





What can I see

Sochi embankment is not only an amazingly picturesque place with sea views. There is also a mass of various cafes, canteens, restaurants. Moreover, it was in this place that the famous scene of the acquisition of lottery tickets from the film “Diamond Hand” was filmed. And on one of the beaches here they traditionally celebrate the marine “Neptune Holiday”.

By the way, one of the main attractions that the Sochi embankment boasts is the seaport, whose building was erected in 1955. Interestingly, another episode of The Diamond Arm was also filmed at the main deepwater pier. This is the scene where Semen Semenovich Gorbunkov says goodbye to his family before sailing on a ship called Mikhail Svetlov.

Sochi sea station is an architectural monument of federal significance. The 71-meter-high tower is crowned with a spire, which is made of polished stainless steel. And over the three tiers were installed figures made by the famous sculptor V.I. Ingal. They are the personification of the four cardinal points and 4 seasons.

Interesting to know

The central embankment of Sochi should soon be transformed - the city authorities plan to make it even wider and more beautiful. By the way, in 1978 its beginning was artificially expanded. Then it was decided to equip the beach called “Lighthouse”.

It is not the first year that the authorities of the Krasnodar Territory want to give the whole embankment a single architectural appearance. There is one nuance that someone considers a plus, while others consider it a minus. And these are cafes, stalls, shops. Because of them, there are almost no beaches and the promenade itself. However, on the other hand, due to this pastime by the sea becomes more pleasant. In general, the authorities decided to find a compromise, but so far it remains the same as it was.





Adler

This is the area of ​​Greater Sochi, not the city that many consider it to be. Although Adler could be considered as such. For some time in Soviet times, by the way, it was a city. But, in principle, this is not the point. The central promenade in Adler is an ideal place for tourists. The sea, restaurants, souvenir shops, night clubs, entertainment - there is everything you need for a bright pastime. And if you drive a little further, you can find yourself on another promenade - in the immediate vicinity (a couple of minutes) from the Olympic venues. It is worth noting that Adler is considered the most prestigious place in the Krasnodar Territory for recreation.

What can be said in conclusion? Perhaps the following: Sochi is a truly colorful, vibrant, warm and welcoming city, which has something to see. Therefore, if there is a desire and the opportunity to visit the resort capital of Russia - you should not miss this chance.
