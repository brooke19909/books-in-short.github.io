Hotel "Le Meridian" (Phuket, Thailand): description, photos and reviews

"Le Meridian Phuket" is a hotel that is conveniently located in the western part of Phuket. It takes approximately 45 minutes to get from the international airport. Tourists are attracted by warm weather, beach vacations and the opportunity to attend interesting excursions.

Territory and number of rooms

The territory of the hotel ‚ÄúLe Meridian Phuket" in Thailand is more than sixteen hectares of greenery and a garden. It was erected in 1987, the last renovation dates back to 2013. The buildings of the Le Meridian Phuket Hotel are built exactly in the middle of the bay. Over time, it has grown so that today occupies all its space. The advantage of the location is that the bay perfectly protects from the wind.

The hotel rooms are colorful rooms that are decorated in the national style of Thailand using handmade furniture, silk and local paintings. For tourists, each room has a unique bathroom design, many digital TV channels and, of course, modern air conditioning.

Some of the rooms overlook the pool, on the other hand there are gorgeous views of the ocean. For guests of the Le Meridian Phuket Beach Resort hotel in Thailand, 416 rooms of standard comfort level and 50 suites are available. Including, there are specially adapted for people with disabilities, for them there are ramps on the territory.

In each issue:

  • free Internet;
  • fridge with minibar;
  • safe;
  • telephone;
  • iron;
  • kettle and coffee machine.

Hotel infrastructure

The infrastructure of the Le Meridian Phuket hotel and rooms is designed to the highest standards. For various events and meetings there are 6 conference rooms equipped with the latest technology.

For guests, the hotel has its own spa center, which offers a wide range of services. They make gorgeous face and body massages using various oils and new technologies. There are also a number of spa programs to choose from, where you can go with a friend or the second half.

For lovers to take care of themselves, there is a beauty salon that represents the services of a hairdresser, nail service and hair removal. For sports enthusiasts, there is a good gym with a lot of exercise equipment for practicing Pilates and yoga.

Free parking is available nearby.

Beach and Pools

On the territory of the Le Meridian Phuket hotel, two comfortable pools are conveniently located, next to which lifeguards are on duty. At certain hours, entertainment events take place here, teams gather for squash, water polo, water gymnastics and just for fun.

There is a private sandy beach on the first line with a gentle entrance. Sun beds and umbrellas are provided absolutely free of charge. Due to the fact that the hotel has its own beach, here you will not find annoying salespeople and crowds of people. Tourists can sunbathe here, enjoy the sunsets and enjoy water sports.

Vacation with children

Among the amenities provided by the hotel is a kids club, which has been open since the morning and is open for babies. Various events and games are held here. Animators do creative master classes with children, draw, sculpt, blow bubbles. In the evening, children's animation is always held, which ends with a fun disco.

Nutrition

Le Meridian Phuket Hotel has 7 restaurants that serve a variety of cuisines from around the world. Even gourmets will be satisfied with a varied diet. Barbecue is served daily on the beach, along with authentic Italian pizza. There are also bars on site that offer cocktails and refreshments for tourists.

One of the Ariake restaurants was recognized as the best in 2017 in Thailand.

Hotel reviews

According to reviews about the Le Meridian Phuket hotel, the conditions there are very good, especially tourists are satisfied with the high level of service. Many focus on the convenient location of the hotel, as well as on a beautiful and well-groomed territory.

On the site of the hotel "Le Meridian Phuket" you will find detailed information about the conditions and cost of stay.

What places can you visit while relaxing at the hotel

Nearby is Patong Beach, a popular tourist destination, which is the epicenter of nightlife with many restaurants and shops. It takes about 10 minutes to get to it, which is very convenient.

Particular attention should be paid to excursions, because often they help to feel the whole flavor of the country.

Green cliffs and turquoise bays Phang Nga province - this is the place you should try to visit. There is incredible beauty of nature, it is here that many come for gorgeous photographs. To get here, just get a boat trip, which includes a stop in the province of Phang Nga. Probably everyone saw it in the photographs and pictures of James Bond Island - this is a great place is here.

Old Phuket Town

In Phuket Town you will find ancient architecture, markets and small shops. The old part of the city is a great area for exotic photographs.

For lovers of night rest

If a relaxing beach holiday is not for you, be sure to visit Bangla Road. This is the famous street, which is simply teeming with clubs, bars, transvestites. It is located on Patong Beach. You can come here for a walk, relax in the bar, and plunge into the atmosphere of the rave life.

It's no secret that in Thailand there are a large number of transvestites, and they also make truly chic and spectacular shows. Such performances are an integral part of the tourism program.

Buddha and temples

Big Buddha is a famous statue that is well known to many from tourist avenues. It is so large that it can be seen from all over the south of Phuket. The statue is located on a hill, and from there a beautiful view of the most popular beaches. You can visit the observation deck near the Big Buddha, tourists remain delighted with this spectacle.

Phuket is famous for its temples, and the main one, Wat Chalong, is the most significant attraction. A huge number of tourists come here daily to see it with their own eyes. To avoid an endless stream of people, it is recommended to come here either in the early morning or late in the evening.
