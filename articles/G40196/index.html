Tours to the Emirates in October. Weather in UAE

The United Arab Emirates is one of the most popular holiday destinations among Russians. The attractiveness of the country consists of several components: it has beautiful beaches, excellent service, a lot of entertainment and attractions, and great shopping. Therefore, Russians gladly go to the Emirates in October, in the high season, when the country has the best conditions for recreation.





Geographical position

The United Arab Emirates is located in the southeast of the Arabian Peninsula. The country borders with Oman, Saudi Arabia and Qatar. The state unites 7 sovereign emirates, each of which has its own ruler and laws, but in foreign policy they appear as a single country with the supreme emir. The capital of the state is the city of Abu Dhabi. The total area of ​​the UAE is 80 thousand square meters. km The coastline over 600 km long is washed by the waters of the Persian and Oman gulfs. The coastal territories are low, rugged by a large number of bays and caves. Most of the country is occupied by the Rub al-Khali desert. This is the largest sandy area in the world. Most settlements are concentrated near the coast; inland, living conditions are too harsh. There are several oases in the desert that allow travelers to cross these burning lands without hindrance. There are mountains in the Emirates: in the east lies the Hajar ridge, the peaks of which reach 1.5 km. The state lives mainly due to oil production, which has a rich bottom near the coast of the country. But fresh water in these places is in great short supply. Arriving in the Emirates in October, you can find one of the most favorable weather periods.





Climate

The United Arab Emirates are located in a tropical desert climate zone with very hot summers and a slight rainy season off the coast in winter. In the depths of the peninsula, in the desert, there is heat all year round, and there are no rains for several years. The temperature in this country never drops below 20 degrees Celsius in the afternoon. In summer, in the shade, it stays in the region of 40-45 degrees Celsius, and in the sun it can reach 55. Sandstorms often come from the desert. In winter, the thermometer drops to 23 degrees, it can be cooler at night. In the country in winter there are significant temperature differences day and night. The swimming season ends in December, when the water begins to cool to 20 degrees Celsius, and resumes in March, when the water warms up to 23 degrees. This time is considered the best for shopping, special festivals of sales are even held here to attract tourists. Precipitation during the year falls only about 100 mm, they are distributed unevenly, most likely to fall in the rain in April and November. The UAE enters the high tourist season in October, which lasts until April. The most unpleasant in the climate of the Emirates is not even heat, but strong winds with sand.





October weather features

Travel agencies are actively starting to sell tours in the UAE in October, when the "high season" opens. At this time, the heat gradually begins to disappear, although during the day at the beginning of the month the thermometer can still rise to the number 35, and at night it drops to 25 degrees. The water of the Persian Gulf warmed up to 27, and Oman - to 23 degrees. This is the best time for beach lovers. In October, bathing fans come to the Emirates. At this time, the country's white beaches are filled with tourists, divers and surfers flock here. The probability of rain this month is approaching zero. But often there are thick morning mists. Humidity begins to decline slightly and averages 63%.





Country history

Going to the Emirates in October is not only due to the beaches, but also in order to get acquainted with the history and culture of the country. The UAE is a fairly young state; it appears only in 1971. But the indigenous people have long lived on these lands. Since the 7th century, small principalities have united in the Arab caliphate, which built the state on the principles of Islam. But later the state loses power, territories fall under the protectorate of Oman, and from the 18th century an irreconcilable struggle begins on these lands between local princes and the British company East India Company, which sought to ensure the safe passage of English ships in the Persian Gulf and wanted to establish the rule of the queen on the lands of the Arabs. Britain succeeded, and in 1820 British domination was established over the country. English military bases were built on this territory, and the British representative was in charge. For 150 years, the British ruled here, but they managed to preserve the existing way of life and religion. However, the growth of national identity led to a wave of protests against British rule and gradually the British began to lose ground until, until 1971, they had withdrawn their last troops. So the new state of the United Arab Emirates appears. A significant increase in oil prices allowed the country to quickly achieve stable well-being for its residents and begin to develop tourism, trade, culture and urban development.





sights

Rest in the Emirates in October allows not only lying on the beach, but also visiting interesting places of the country. Especially in the UAE there is something to see, the country is a real paradise for an inquisitive tourist. Here the future and the past are mixed, and this “cocktail” is breathtaking. The main place of pilgrimage for curious travelers, of course, is Dubai. Here you can see ultra-modern buildings and ancient, authentic neighborhoods with a lifestyle that has changed little over several centuries. The list of the most important places worth visiting include:

- Burj Khalifa Tower. This building is not only the tallest in the world, but also represents a real architectural miracle. From the tower in good weather you can see all the surroundings.

- The artificial island of Palma , stunning with the boldness of an engineering plan, has immediately collected a galaxy of architectural masterpieces.

- The White Sheikh Zayed Mosque in Abu Dhabi is a structure unique in its luxury and scope.

- The fortresses of Al Jahili, Ajman, Al Husn and Al Fahidi , retaining their medieval appearance.

- Burj al-Arab, the famous Sail building.

And this is just the most. In fact, there are several times more attractions.





Things to do

Those who want to not only sunbathe, but also to see something unusual, should definitely go to the Emirates. Weather in October has an active excursion. First of all, you must definitely go to the desert to get an unforgettable experience. The desert offers entertainment for every taste - rides on a camel, jeep, ATV. Here you can even ski, look at the immense expanses from the board of a light-winged aircraft or from a parachute. For fishing enthusiasts, the weather in the UAE at this time creates ideal conditions. There are various organized tours for fishermen in the Persian Gulf. A separate article of entertainment in the Emirates is shopping. It is imperative to visit the traditional eastern market, even if purchases are not included in the plans, it is worth coming here for impressions. And huge malls are a real paradise for shopping lovers.





What to see

Arriving in the UAE in October, you should not spend all the time on the beach. There are so many interesting things. What do you need to see? The musical fountain in Dubai, amazing scale. Both children and adults will have fun in one of the best water parks in the East Wild Wadi. The unique ski complex Sky Dubai will help you to feel the sharp contrast of heat and cold. All tourists, regardless of age, are madly in love with the huge aquarium in the Dubai Mall, with its glass tunnels and a huge number of different marine life. The weather in the UAE is good for walking, especially in the evenings, when you can plan a trip to the ethnographic village in Abu Dhabi. Here you can see the traditional crafts, homes and life of the Bedouins, try national dishes.





Tourists reviews

Many travelers choose to vacation in the Emirates in October. Prices at this time, of course, are higher than in a hot summer, but it's worth it. At this time, according to tourists, you can immediately get all the impressions: and sunbathe, and have fun, and see many attractions. By tradition, many Russian travelers recommend settling in Sharjah, where cheaper accommodation and close to the beaches, and going on excursions by public transport or with organized tours. According to experienced tourists, a vacation in the Emirates in October is an excellent ratio of price, service and leisure opportunities.
