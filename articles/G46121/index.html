We sew slippers bunnies with our own hands

Today you can buy almost anything you want. That is why manual labor has recently become very appreciated, and the so-called hand-made products are quite expensive (both materially and from a slightly different point of view). This article will discuss how to sew bunny slippers with your own hands.





Training

First of all, you need to stock up on certain materials. So, in this case, you need a warm cloth (fleece for the outer and inner sides), leather or dermantine for the sole, as well as sewing accessories.

How to sew slippers "Bunnies": pattern

First of all, it is necessary to prepare a product pattern. Four basic details will need to be cut: the sole, the rectangle for the shaft, a piece in the form of the English letter U, as well as the part on the eye. As you can see, the preparatory phase is not so difficult. By the way, you can make your own adjustments and sew the bunny slippers with your own hands, slightly different from those shown in the photo above.

Inner boot

It‚Äôs better to start sewing bunny slippers with your own hands from the inside boot. To do this, you need to transfer the pattern to the fabric and be sure to cut it with an allowance for seams (1 cm). Now you need to attach a rectangular piece (leg) to the U-shaped flap (hugs the leg itself) with pins, as an option - just to sketch. The fabric is applied face to face, then the place of cleaving is sewn on a typewriter, the back of the boot is also stitched with a stitch. According to this principle, you need to prepare the sole (only here the product is sewn back to back). You can attach a lining to the foot, if the house is pretty cool. Now you need to sew the sole to the rest of the product. To do this, the detail-foot is carefully inserted into the place prepared for it and basted, starting with the sock. Next, you need to stitch the product in a circle, cut off excess allowances and overcast the bottom (this can be done using the zigzag stitch on a sewing machine, but it is better to use an overlock if possible).



Outer boot

Do-it-yourself bunny slippers on. Now we need to prepare the outer boot. It is done exactly the same as the inside.

Ears

Making ears for slippers. To do this, two cut parts are sewn face to face, and then turned out. Above it is recommended to let the line at a distance of 5 mm from the edge, so that the product is more durable and attractive.

Connection parts

Now you need to figure out how to sew bunny slippers, that is, to connect all the parts together. This will be easy to do. First of all, you need to sew on the ears - they are inserted between the inner and outer boots (it is important to correctly mark them with the right side up). The product is stitched in a circle of the shaft. However, the seam is not sewn up to the end, because the product will need to be turned out. At this stage, it is important to overcast all the seams, trim the excess. Now you can turn out the slipper through the left hole, sew a hole with hidden stitches. That's all, the product is ready!



Decor

You can also make cute creases on the front of the sneaker. To do this, stitch a small segment in the center of the slipper and pull the product along the lower thread. Fasten and hide the threads. You can also sew a cute hare tail - a funny pompom. Well, no one forbade the use of various accessories in their work. Want glamorous bunnies? What is easier! Sequins, beads, rhinestones, all kinds of ribbons, bows - which is not present in modern stores! Create, invent, try!
