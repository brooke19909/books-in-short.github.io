Hotel Papantonia Hotel Apartments (Cyprus / Protaras)

Description : Cozy comfortable complex Papantonia Hotel Apartments is conveniently located among a picturesque tropical garden in the tourist resort of Protaras, where there are many rocky bays and snow-white beaches. The hotel was founded in 1990 and completely renovated in 2011. During this time, many foreigners visited it.





Today it is a modern and completely renovated guesthouse, offering a wide selection of a wide variety of services, ranging from swimming procedures to entertainment services. Within walking distance are concentrated shopping centers, shops, souvenir shops and numerous restaurants. Located in this stunning place called Papantonia Hotel Apartments, you can immerse yourself in the world of dreams, bask in the sun and get acquainted with the local culture.

The road to the hotel from the airport will take no more than an hour (41 km from Larnaca Airport). The hotel is recommended for a relaxing, relaxed and sightseeing vacation.





Number of rooms: The complex Papantonia Hotel Apartments 4 * 112 spacious rooms: apartments and studios. The loggia adjoins the room, on which there is wicker furniture for relaxation. In the room itself, there is a kitchen area with a compact fridge, dishes and a kettle.

The bedroom has a bedside table, satellite TV, individual air conditioning, direct dial telephone. The bathroom is equipped with a shower, toilet, sink and hygiene products. In the presence of a hairdryer, radio, safe.

Meals: Guests staying at the Papantonia Hotel Apartments are served three meals a day by the buffet. If you wish, you can choose only breakfasts (BB) or, conversely, the concept of "all inclusive". Tables are served in the main restaurant.

A separate menu is provided for children . The hotel has its own poolside bar, which hosts daily theater performances, dances and competitions.

Beach: The road to the beach will not take more than 5 minutes (100 m). For vacationers on the coast there are recreational areas with sunbeds and awnings. Very convenient gentle entry into the sea - safe for children. On the shore open points for renting sports equipment, catamarans and jet skis.





Additional information: In the morning, Papantonia Hotel Apartments hosts gymnastics right in the pool (2 outdoor pools). After charging, guests can play active games: table tennis, volleyball, darts, bowling. There is also a gym and video games room. The hotel hosts interesting contests and recreational activities for the whole family throughout the day. In the evening, a disco opens.

Kids can splash around in a safe pool, play on the playground or be in a development club. Babysitting services are also available . The hotel also offers car rental, a dry cleaning department and a currency exchange office.

Digest: If you want to spend your vacation near the sea, daily admiring the picturesque nature and breathing in the clean sea air, then welcome to the Papantonia Hotel Apartments 4. Cyprus - these are enchanting golden beaches located under the crown of green plants, crystal clear waters, the glitter of the rising sun, quality service, surprisingly relaxing and welcoming atmosphere.

Clients did not have any complaints about the service at the hotel. Almost everyone enjoyed leisure, food and tidy rooms. Stunningly delicious dinners, themed evenings and a chic animation program will give an unforgettable vacation experience.
