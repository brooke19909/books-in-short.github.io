How to make unlimited Internet on MTS: step-by-step instructions, connection features, conditions, tips

The Internet is actively used all over the world on various platforms. With its help, they relax, talk, have fun, study and even work. And the presence of restrictions on incoming / outgoing traffic gives users a lot of trouble. Therefore, people think about using "unlimited". In this case, the traffic can be any - the fee for it will be fixed, depending on the tariff plan. Today we have to figure out how to make unlimited Internet on MTS. Consider the process by the example of connecting a similar option in Moscow and the Moscow region.

Call operator

How to make unlimited Internet on MTS? For example, you can call the call center of this company. This is not the most popular method of solving the problem, but in real life it still occurs.

To achieve the desired result, the client of the company needs to perform the following actions:

  1. Take the phone and put it into dialing mode.
  2. Dial 0890.
  3. Press the call button.
  4. Inform the operator of your intentions. If necessary, you can ask the call center employee to inform you about the currently active Internet tariff plans.
  5. Wait a while.

As a rule, after the actions taken, the call center employee draws up an application for connecting the service. As soon as it passes the processing stage, a person will receive a message about the successful activation of the Internet tariff. In some cases, SMS has network settings that need to be saved.

Mobile App

How to make the Internet on the MTS phone unlimited? First of all, you need to study all available tariff plans, and then activate the selected offer. There is nothing difficult about this. Especially if you prepare for this operation in advance.

On tablets and mobile phones, you can use the My MTS application to manage services. To achieve the desired result, it is recommended:

  1. Open the application "My MTS" on a mobile device.
  2. Select the section "Services" - "Internet".
  3. Decide on the tariff plan that you want to connect. Without this, the desired result cannot be achieved.
  4. Select unlimited Internet for your device, and then tap on the corresponding button.
  5. Confirm the fact of a tariff change.

It is done! Now the user will receive a message about the successful change of the tariff plan for the Internet. You can enjoy unlimited traffic on the web.

Directly to the office

How to make endless Internet on MTS? It has already been said that to cope with such a task is not difficult. The main thing is to understand possible action algorithms.

Some prefer to come to the MTS offices and there to connect or disconnect the services they need. Nice, but rather laborious reception. It takes a lot of time.

Instructions for connecting "unlimited" in this way will look something like this:

  1. Decide on a tariff plan for the Internet. This can be done later, but the sooner the better.
  2. Take along your phone (tablet), SIM card and any identification card.
  3. Contact your nearest MTS office with the corresponding request.
  4. Give the SIM and mobile device to an organization employee.
  5. Get back a device with already activated unlimited Internet.

The convenience of this technique is that the employees of the outlet or office will be able to quickly consult a person regarding current Internet tariffs.

Important: only the person on whom the SIM card is issued can contact the MTS office. Otherwise, serious problems may arise in obtaining the services necessary for a person.

It is also worth noting that the employees of the outlets of the mentioned telecom operator cannot demand money for their work. The fee is charged as a prepayment when changing the tariff. It is simply deducted from the balance of the "sim card".

"My Account" and tariff changes

How to make unlimited Internet on MTS? One could already notice that it is proposed to cope with the task in different ways. And first you need to study the conditions for providing Internet access. This is best done using the MTS official website. There, in the "Rates and Services" section, you can see all currently available rates for your region.

And using the MTS website you can independently connect the "unlimited" to the Internet or change the tariff plan as a whole. To do this, you need:

  1. Log in to the site under your name.
  2. Open "My Account" and switch to the "Service Management" section.
  3. Select the category "Internet" or "Tariff plan".
  4. Click on the line with the service that I would like to connect.
  5. Click on the "Connection" hyperlink.

Now it remains only to confirm the operation and wait for the SMS about the successful processing of the operation. Fast, easy and very convenient!

Teams

How to make Internet on MTS? "Unlimited" is a good tariff for "unlimited". In addition to Internet traffic, a person will receive 500 free minutes of conversation and SMS messages in Russia to MTS numbers. At the moment, there is such a tariff plan of 585 rubles.

To change the tariff plan, disconnect or connect certain services of a telecom operator, many use USSD-commands. For example, such:

  • * 111 * 1115 # - "Unlimited";
  • * 111 * 837 * 1 # - "A lot of Internet" (without changing the tariff);
  • * 111 * 1725 # - "Smart device";
  • * 111 * 845 # - "For the tablet."

It is better to familiarize yourself with the detailed conditions of these tariff plans for your region on the MTS website. Only in this way will it be possible to obtain the most reliable data for the selected tariff.

SIM purchase

How to make unlimited Internet on MTS? If you think about this in advance, you can simply buy a SIM card with a suitable tariff plan. This is the most suitable solution, but it is more often used by new customers of the telecom operator.

You can purchase a SIM card at any MTS office. You need to bring along a passport or other personal identifier. You will have to pay for the corresponding service according to the established price tag on the SIM card.

Important: this trick is perfect for the home Internet.
