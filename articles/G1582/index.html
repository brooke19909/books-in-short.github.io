Hotels in Hawaii - Best and Exclusive Places

Hawaii is an archipelago in the Pacific Ocean, consisting of two dozen islands, atolls and reefs. The islands of Hawaii are different from each other. Some are covered with dense tropical greenery, others are flooded with volcanic lava, on the third there are many rivers and waterfalls. All this natural splendor is complemented by a vast ocean and white beaches.

The most popular holiday islands with luxury hotels are Hawaii, Maui and Kauai. On each of them you can choose a vacation for every taste and budget.

Montage Kapalua Bay - Expensive Hawaii

Montage Kapalua Bay is a beach resort located on the island of Maui (Hawaii), on the shores of the picturesque Kapalula Bay. Here you can enjoy a real Hawaiian lifestyle. All the services of a five-star hotel are provided for guests: accommodation in deluxe rooms equipped with TVs, a private hot tub and a balcony with a view. Suites have a washer and dryer.

Traditional restaurants

Cane & Canoe is a traditional Hawaiian home built with respect for the local culture. The restaurant offers breathtaking views of the ocean, and the main place on the menu is occupied by freshly caught seafood and an extensive wine list. Every Sunday during brunch, additional culinary delights are served: grilled prawns, steaks, fragrant waffles and much more.

The poolside Maui Bar is a cozy place to sample local food and drinks.

Original exotic

An exclusive place for private lunches and dinners is a small house on the beach, which used to be the residence of overseers on plantations. Now the house is completely renovated and used as a venue for private events in Maui.

The house offers magnificent views of the Pacific Ocean, Molokai and Lanai. This place can be called intimate; barbecue parties, romantic dinners or corporate events are often held here. The building accommodates up to 40 guests, for their convenience there is an equipped kitchen, a small conference room, and a shower.

Spa Montage offers innovative solutions in cosmetology problems. The small boutique houses an impressive assortment of products made on the islands. Very unusual resort

Finding all-inclusive 5 star hotels on the islands of Hawaii will be difficult. One of these is the Lolia Community environmental complex. The complex consists of closed private cottages. The hotel has a magnificent room for dancing, yoga, a free hot tub, free internet service and cooked vegetarian dishes with local products.

This is a very unusual place, different from the standard beach resorts. In the eco-village, rainwater is collected and solar energy is accumulated. In the evening, resort guests are asked to use their own flashlights or lamps, because the area is not lit.

There is a restaurant and cafe on site, a complimentary breakfast is served each morning, and buffet meals are also available. Public areas are equipped with free Wi-Fi. This eco-friendly place even has a nightclub and spa services. Self parking is absolutely free.

Cottages of 13 and 18 square meters are fully furnished and overlook the garden and courtyard. Each cottage has a private bathroom with free organic toiletries. A fan is installed on the ceiling; smoking is strictly prohibited.

This place is so original that living here is not suitable for everyone, despite the buffet and the all-inclusive system. One piquant feature is that wearing clothes is optional, the minimum age of guests is 18 years.
