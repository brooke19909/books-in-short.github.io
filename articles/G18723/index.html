Park-hotel "Rio" (Crimea): reviews, address, service, sea, beach

For those who are used to spend active holidays, the Rio Park Hotel has been operating for several years on the Azov coast. Prices will pleasantly surprise you. For a reasonable price, everyone can get a really high level of relaxation. Fun activities are offered for children and adults, older tourists and youth.

basic information

In one of the bays of the Kazantip Bay, the Rio Park Hotel (Crimea) is located. Reviews show that the area here is truly mesmerizing by its extraordinary beauty. It is worth visiting the resort just to enjoy the sea coast and clean air. Here it will be possible not only to have a great rest, to gain strength before the new working year, but also to get better. This is especially true for people who regularly experience bronchitis, tonsillitis and other diseases of the respiratory system.

At 200 meters from the hotel is the sea. The beach related to the institution is sandy. It has everything you need for a quality vacation. Safety is also thought out. Depth is increasing gradually. Families with young children can relax.

Park-hotel is located at the address: Crimea, Leninsky district, Mysovoye village, Tsentralnaya street 117. The city of Shchelkino can serve as a reference point. You can ask interesting questions regarding the hotel by the hotline number 8 800 555 10 17.

Rooms and prices

There are several accommodation options. It is possible to rent a room in the building, in a wooden house or to set up a campsite near the beach area. The most popular, according to reviews, are apartments in a three-story building. These are superior rooms designed for up to three people at a time. The cost of living depends on the season. So, in the spring, one person will have to pay 1,600 rubles for accommodation. The most expensive holiday is considered to be in August. The cost of daily living at this time will be 2300 rubles.



Park Hotel Rio (Kazantip) also offers accommodation in eco-houses. This option is perfect for those who want to save money and at the same time be closer to nature. You can rent a room only in the summer months. In June, one person will have to pay 1,400 rubles per day. In August, the price rises to 1600 rubles.
