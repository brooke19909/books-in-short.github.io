Manually and automatically configure MTS Internet on Android

In the framework of this article, the Internet setup of MTS on Android is described step by step. In most cases, this procedure occurs automatically the first time you turn on the device. The main advantage in this is the minimal user impact. But it happens that something goes wrong. In this case, you need to do everything manually. This is a little more complicated, but still, everyone can cope with this task, regardless of their level of training.





Automatically

Such an Internet MTS setup on Android is the easiest. We install the SIM-card in the smartphone or tablet and turn it on. After downloading, if necessary, enter the PIN code. When the necessary information is found, it will be sent over the network to the gadget. It will be enough for the subscriber to accept and save. After that, you can begin to check the operability of this service.

Manually

It is not always possible to automatically configure the MTS Internet on Android. Check

MTS mobile Internet settings are checked as follows. Turn on data exchange. To do this, call the drop-down menu on top and click on the "Data Transfer" button in it (a rectangle with two arrows is drawn on it, which are directed in different directions). Then you need to wait until the network services start up and the IP address is received (the button should change its color). We launch any of the browsers (for example, Opera). Then enter the site address (ya.ru or mail.ru) and press enter. After that, the page from the Internet should load.
