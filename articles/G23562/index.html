Ricorda island: description and photo

There are so many places in Russia that the modern tourist knows nothing about, or almost nothing. For example, the island of Ricorda. An amazing and unusual name that can easily be misleading. Well, after all, the truth is, the first thing that comes to mind when you hear something like this is some resort in Spain, Italy or, finally, Portugal, but not in our country. Well, perhaps it is worth exploring this place in more detail.





Location

Rikorda Island (Primorsky Territory) is located near the city of Vladivostok, only 32 km to the south, in the Gulf of Peter the Great. The island received its name in honor of P.I. Rikord - the admiral of the Russian fleet, navigator and statesman. The total area is 5 km 2 , and the maximum height above sea level reaches a mark of 178 meters.

So, how can you get to the island of Ricorda? Vladivostok is a key point, because it is the closest place to it, in fact it is necessary to build on this.

Air traffic

There are several ways to get to Vladivostok, but the fastest and most optimal one is, of course, the plane. Travel time from, for example, Moscow will be a little more than 8 hours, and the ticket price starts at 13 thousand rubles.

A train

You can also get to Vladivostok by train, which will be clearly cheaper than air travel, but you will have to pay time for a lower cost. As mentioned above, on an airplane from Moscow, the entire road will take a little more than 8 hours, but in the case of a train, it takes 7 days, i.e. whole week!





Car

Of course, the easiest way to get to Vladivostok is from the nearest settlements and cities. For example, from Ussuriysk on the A-370 road, you can get to the place in just 1 hour 10 minutes, and from Nakhodka in 3 hours. Naturally, further the distance only increases, and with it the travel time, so it does not make sense to go by car from more distant regions.





From Vladivostok to the island of Ricorda

So, after arriving at the place, a good question arises, how to get to the island itself? And here it is not so simple as it might seem at first glance. If ferries and public boats go to the Reineke and Popov islands , then there are no to Ricorda. The only way to get to the island is with a private boat, and there are 2 options where to find it.

The first is to ask someone to take you and take them back after a certain time, of course, for a fee.





The second is to contact a travel company and buy a tour for a few days. The only problem is that when the tourist season ends, there are fewer people wishing to go to the island, and travel agencies rarely carry out transportation. The best time to visit is July or August.





As for the price, the one-day tour will cost about 3000 rubles or a little less. In time, a one-way trip lasts from 2 to 3 hours.

Description

Now you can talk in more detail about what constitutes the island of Ricorda. First of all, it is divided into 2 parts: the big one is called the north, and the small one is called the south. They are interconnected by a small isthmus, the width of which is 300 meters. There is no population on the island; it is considered uninhabited.





But the nature here is great. Most of the island is covered in linden and oak woods. In addition to them, rose hips, barberries, Amur grapes, actinidia and much more grows here.





Among the animal kingdom one can find snakes, snakes, Far Eastern toads, a fox, a raccoon dog, and seals are also quite common. Of the birds there are golden eagles, cormorants, hoopoes, gulls, frigates and others. In general, there is definitely something to see on Ricorda Island.

Features of rest

Holidays on the island of Ricorda mainly consists of admiring the local beauties. The more courageous can swim in the sea, although the waves here can reach three meters. No, of course you can swim, there are even those who jump from the rocks, but it‚Äôs best to go to the southern part.





During the tourist period, tent camps begin to work on the island. You can only settle there if you buy a ticket from a tour operator. Nutrition issues are discussed separately, and if necessary, staff will feed everyone who paid for such a service.





Boat trips and diving equipment are also provided. There are sports fields, rental of sports equipment, as well as a rope town.





Among the dangers, one should remember the sharks that often appear here, but in any case, the leadership of the tent camp constantly conducts mandatory instructions and tells the rules and safety precautions.

Recreation facilities

The issue of accommodation is always very important, so it's time to compare a couple of recreation centers. Ricorda Island is uninhabited, but there are several tent camps, which will be discussed below.

"On the crest of a wave"

The first tent camp on the island of Ricorda - "On the crest of a wave." It is located in the southwestern part. People are transported by boats, and travel time varies from 2 hours 15 minutes to 2 hours 30 minutes - it all depends on the weather.





Large tents with a dressing room are provided for living. They are located on a small hill, due to which flooding from the raging sea is not terrible for vacationers. For each tent there is a standard equipment: flooring made of wood, a blanket (one per person) and rugs. Pillows and bedding are not provided and should be taken with you if necessary.

A large military tent for 40 people is used as a dining room. All food is prepared by the company chef in a separate kitchen. Each is given 4 meals a day.





As for other amenities, on the territory of the tent camp there is a bath and shower, as well as a wash basin and a toilet. It is worth noting that the bathhouse is heated only 2 times a week, and much depends on the availability of fresh water and firewood.

If we talk about prices, then they are different and vary depending on the month. Here are some suggestions for an example:

  • 5 days in July - 8750 rubles .;
  • 5 days in August - 10,250 rubles .;
  • 12 days in July - 20800 rubles;
  • 12 days in August - 22800 rub.

The minimum stay is 2 days, and the maximum is 18. Weekend tours are also available.

"New impressions"

"New impressions" is another tent camp on the island of Ricorda, which has been operating since 1991. It is located on the western part of the island and begins to receive tourists every year from July to the beginning of September. The maximum number of people who can simultaneously be in the town is 50. The duration of the stay is completely different and negotiated individually, but if we talk about some standards, then this is from 2 to 15 days.





Upon arrival at the place, everyone is required to undergo instruction, familiarize themselves with the rules and safety procedures, and then sign the corresponding contract.

Accommodation is carried out in large double tents with a dressing room, which are installed on a pebble beach. From the complete set, everyone is provided with wooden flooring, rugs and a blanket (1 per person). As in the previous case, the pillow, duvet cover and sheet are not provided, they need to be taken separately.

In addition, on the territory of the tent camp there is a well with fresh water, a toilet, a wash basin, and there is also a bath with a steam room.





A large army tent with a total area of ‚Äã‚Äã50 m 2 is used as a dining room. Cooking is carried out by a qualified cook from a travel agency in a separate kitchen. During the hours when meals are not served, here you can just sit on a bench and enjoy the views of the sea.

In addition to all of the above, for all comers walking tours of the island are held, during which the guide tells about the history of these places and introduces the sights. Well, and, of course, do not forget about boat trips.





Now for the prices. The minimum price for 1 adult for 2 days is 3,000 rubles. A 15-day trip for 1 person will cost 25 thousand rubles. As in the previous case, the price depends on the month, well, and the number of people.

In the framework of such a trip, everyone will be able to get acquainted not only with the island of Ricorda, but also with the islands of Reineke, Popov, Kozlov, the Russian island and others.
