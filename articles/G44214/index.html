Does Evpatoria have Sberbank ATMs?

Often, when someone goes to another city on a business trip, on vacation or for some other reason, the first question arises: is there an ATM there? And this is not surprising, because everywhere there is a card system. People do not carry cash in their wallets, but use cards. This article discusses whether there are ATMs of Sberbank of Russia in Yevpatoriya.

Sberbank refuses to work in Crimea

Due to the accession of the Crimean peninsula to Russia, Sberbank had to stop its activities in this place. All branches, ATMs were closed. The main reason is the introduction of special sanctions that could adversely affect the activities of Sberbank. As bank owner German Gref said:

I do not want to risk. Activities will resume when sanctions are relaxed.

Herman is sure that the return of Sberbank ATMs to Yevpatoriya, Alushta, and Theodosius will entail enormous economic problems. The government approved a ban on return.

But what about those visiting tourists who may need a Sberbank ATM in Yevpatoria, for example? There is an alternative.

Alternative to Sberbank

There are no Sberbank ATMs in Yevpatoriya, but there are other banks that can help customers of the Russian institution.

The Russian National Commercial Bank (RNKB) has many branches and ATMs. In this institution, you can withdraw cash from the card of Sberbank, and without commission. It is located at the following addresses:

  • Internatsionalnaya, house 115;
  • Lenin, house 3;
  • Chapaeva, 47;
  • Frunze, house 23B;
  • May 9, 94A and so on.

Bank "Russia". Sberbank customers can withdraw funds, but there is a commission for withdrawals. Located at:

  • Gogol, house 17E;
  • Victory, 53;
  • International, 88;
  • D. Ulyanova, house 1B and others.

Genebank. Just like in the Russian National Bank of Russia, you can withdraw money from a Sberbank card without commission. Addresses:

  • Internatsionalnaya, house 124;
  • Victory, 25;
  • Lenin, 17A and so on.

There are no Sberbank ATMs in Yevpatoriya. But there are banks that provide the ability to withdraw funds from a Russian bank card without a commission. This is very important, because there are a lot of Sberbank card holders.
