Hotel Kleopatra Ada Hotel 4 * (Alanya, Turkey): description, leisure and reviews of tourists

Alanya has been one of the most popular resorts of the Republic of Turkey among tourists from Russia and other countries of the former Soviet Union for several decades. This fact is not surprising, since here, in addition to the magnificent climate, warm gentle sea, there is a wide hotel base where everyone can find a hotel in accordance with their own taste and financial capabilities, as well as a variety of entertainment for guests of all ages. So, if you plan to spend a summer vacation in Alanya and are looking for a good comfortable hotel at an affordable cost, then the four-star Kleopatra Ada Beach Hotel can be a great choice. Therefore, we will learn more about this hotel complex, find out what is offered to tourists here, as well as what impression our compatriots had after rest in it.





Where is the hotel situated

This hotel complex is located just one kilometer from Alanya. Therefore, the entire infrastructure of one of the largest centers of the Turkish coast is within walking distance. The airport closest to Kleopatra Ada Hotel 4 * is located at a distance of 120 kilometers, in the city of Antalya. Therefore, the journey from the air harbor to your hotel will take about 2-2.5 hours. Just an hour's drive from the hotel there is the famous Manavgat waterfall. The distance to the local attractions - Alanya Castle - is only six kilometers. As for distance from the sea, the hotel has its own luxurious sandy beach, located just a hundred meters away.

Hotel complex "Cleopatra Ada 4 *": description

Hotel Kleopatra Ada Hotel 4 * received its first guests in 1996. The last restoration work was carried out here in 2010. Today the hotel is a completely modern establishment, offering tourists from different countries to stay in comfortable cozy rooms during their stay in Alanya. The territory of the hotel complex cannot be called large. So, its area is only 3000 square meters. However, there is everything for a great pastime: a private beach, a swimming pool, a place for outdoor activities, a beauty salon, a fitness center and much more. The number of rooms in the hotel is 184. The hotel complex is a great place both for family holidays with children, and for an interesting pastime for young people.



Hotel Complex Rules

In accordance with the rules of the Kleopatra Ada Hotel 4 * hotel (Turkey), the accommodation of newly staying guests in the rooms previously reserved by them takes place from 14:00. If you arrived at the hotel earlier, the administration will do everything possible to check in immediately. However, sometimes it happens (especially in the midst of the tourist season) that the hotel is 100 percent full. Therefore, if you arrived at the hotel earlier than the scheduled check-in time, and your previous guests did not leave your room and did not have time to prepare the hotel maids, then you can leave your luggage at the reception and go for a snack, swim in the pool or immediately into the sea. As for departure, you must leave the hotel before 12:00. In this case, you should pay for accommodation and additional services, if you have used them. We accept both cash and plastic cards of the Visa and Mastercard systems.

Accommodation with children and pets

Since the hotel Kleopatra Ada Hotel 4 * (Alanya) is mainly designed for guests coming to rest with families, children of all ages are allowed here. At the same time, if you put a baby up to two years old in your room on a crib or a child aged 2 to 12 years old on existing sleeping places, then you do not have to pay extra. Please note that the need to provide an extra bed should be notified in advance to the hotel and wait for confirmation of the possibility of providing this service on its part.

For lovers of traveling in the company of their four-legged pets, the following information will be important. So, in accordance with the rules of Kleopatra Ada Hotel 4 *, pets are not allowed here. Therefore, if you do not want to leave your pet at home, you will need to find another hotel to stay.





Number of rooms

The four-star hotel Cleopatra Ada (Alanya, Turkey) has 184 comfortable rooms. Among them there are standard doubles (one double or two single beds) and triple rooms. Each of them is equipped with air conditioning, a TV with satellite or cable channels (including those broadcasting in Russian), a bathroom with a shower and a hairdryer, a minibar, a safe, a telephone and a balcony. On the floor - ceramic tiles. Guests of Kleopatra Ada Beach Hotel 4 * have the opportunity to order room service at any time. Rooms are cleaned on a daily basis, and maids change towels and bedding three times a week.

Dining at the hotel complex

Meals at the four-star Cleopatra Ada Beach Hotel are organized according to the so-beloved by our compatriots all inclusive or all inclusive system. In the main restaurant of the hotel complex , chefs serve a buffet three times a day , consisting of dishes, snacks and desserts of both Turkish and international cuisine. As many positive reviews show, Kleopatra Ada Hotel 4 * really pays great attention to the quality of food. Despite this, some tourists were still unhappy with the food, but this problem arises in most hotels in Turkey, regardless of the level of their "star rating". The hotel also has an √† la carte restaurant, where you can taste skillfully prepared Turkish dishes. A visit to it is paid, and a table must be booked in advance.

In addition to restaurants, the hotel has three bars: by the pool, in the lobby and snack bar. In the last of them, in addition to drinks, you can order various snacks - pizza, sandwiches, french fries and others.





Sea beach

Just a few tens of meters from the Kleopatra Ada Hotel is a magnificent hotel-owned sandy beach. There are sun loungers, mattresses and umbrellas. Also on the beach you can use equipment for water sports.

Pools

The hotel has an outdoor pool. Next to it is a sun terrace with comfortable sun loungers and shady umbrellas. There is also a bar near the pool where you can enjoy your favorite cold and alcoholic drinks and cocktails. In addition, on the territory of Kleopatra Ada Hotel 4 * there is a children's pool for the youngest guests of the hotel.





Entertainment

In addition to beach relaxation and swimming in the pool, on the territory of the Cleopatra Ada Hotel you can play darts, billiards, table tennis or beach volleyball. Also, guests have the opportunity to visit the gym and do water sports.

In addition, you can go to the beauty salon, hairdresser, massage room. Also be sure to visit the sauna or Turkish bath (hammam).

In addition, the hotel runs animation programs designed for both adults and youngest travelers.





Infrastructure

It should be noted that the infrastructure in Kleopatra Ada Hotel 4 * (Turkey), reviews only confirm this, is at the proper level and is consistent with the "star" of this hotel. So, the hotel reception is open without interruptions, so in case of late check-in or if you have any question, you will always find the reception administrator on duty who will make every effort to solve the problems.

The hotel also has a spacious parking. Using it is free for guests. In addition, guests of "Cleopatra Ada 4 *" can take advantage of such services as laundry, dry cleaning, room service. Free Wi-Fi is available in public areas of the hotel. Also at the hotel you can rent a car and book an excursion, buy souvenirs or necessary things for a beach holiday in the store, exchange currency, visit a beauty salon and a hairdresser. In case of malaise, guests can contact the medical office, where an experienced doctor will provide the necessary assistance.

In addition, the hotel also has a conference room for 50 people. It has all the necessary equipment for holding business meetings, negotiations, symposia and other similar events.





Reviews: Kleopatra Ada Hotel 4 * (Alanya, Turkey)

Since today it is very important for many people choosing a hotel in one country or another in the world to get the opinion of tourists who have already been here, we have summarized the reviews of our compatriots staying at Cleopatra Ada 4 *. In general, we can say that the hotel complex is consistent with the declared "star rating" and the cost of living. However, according to most vacationers, it is more suitable for unpretentious tourists and those who came to Turkey for the first time. And now, in order.

As for the rooms, here our compatriots did not have any complaints. All rooms, although small, are comfortable and well-furnished. No one complained about the breakdown of air conditioners, refrigerators or plumbing. The vast majority of guests were satisfied with the quality and regularity of cleaning the rooms, regardless of whether they left a tip to the maid or not. The only thing is that for the extra dollar you have more chances to return to the room to find on your bed a funny figurine of towels.

If we talk about food in the Kleopatra Ada Hotel 4 *, here the opinions of tourists differ. However, in fairness it should be noted that a similar picture is often observed in the best five-star hotels of the Republic of Turkey. So, as for the Cleopatra Ada hotel complex, many travelers have found the menu here is quite diverse, and the food is delicious. Our other compatriots didn‚Äôt particularly like the dishes offered to them in restaurants. Some claim that they even had to go to dinner at nearby cafes, where they cooked much better than in a hotel. Be that as it may, many tourists noted the fact that the hotel manager, along with several administrators, had breakfast and lunch almost every day in the main restaurant, Cleopatra Ada. This suggests that the staff carefully monitors the quality of food offered to guests.

As for the beach, the hotel guests did not really like the fact that sunbeds and umbrellas are paid here. So, for two sun loungers and a parasol a day you will have to pay about $ 6. When checking into a hotel, you can immediately redeem the right to use a similar beach set for the entire period of residence. Our compatriots found the beach itself very good. So, it is completely sandy with a gentle and comfortable entry into the water. Also, many appreciated the fact that to go to the beach just five minutes on the strength.

Most of the hotel guests were satisfied with the staff. However, some of our compatriots were a little puzzled by the fact that the majority of Cleopatra Ada employees do not understand Russian. But all the staff is fluent in English, so it is quite possible to communicate with them. In general, most of the guests of the hotel complex are Europeans - Germans, Swedes, etc. There are not so many Russian tourists here as in other hotels in Alanya.
