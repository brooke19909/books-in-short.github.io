How to get to the hotel, its location, which is located nearby

As a rule, guests at the Seray Deluxe Hotel 4 * (Marmaris) arrive at Dalaman Airport. It is currently under reconstruction. The airport is located ninety kilometers from the city. As a rule, most hotels accepting tourists have agreements with operators on the transfer of guests. But some use taxi services or get to the Marmaris bus station using the Hawash buses.

There is a convenience store near the hotel. And if you go out onto the main street, then you will immediately get to two Migros-type supermarkets. There is a good selection of products and things. Migros are also known for the fact that you can pay in dollars there, and they will give change in Turkish lira, moreover, at a very good rate. Many other boutiques and shops are plentiful and within walking distance.



Hotel territory, infrastructure

Hotel Seray Deluxe Hotel 4 * (Marmaris) reviews are called one of the first in this resort. It was built in 1998. Nevertheless, now it looks very modern. The hotel consists of one building with an elevator. The hotel has a post office. You will reach the center on foot in twenty to thirty minutes, but while you walk along the beautiful promenade with its craftsmen, you will not even notice this. Near the hotel there is a bus stop, from where you can literally go to any place in the resort.

Entertainment

About how to spend time at the Seray Deluxe Hotel 4 * (Turkey, Marmaris), reviews say different things. Those who are accustomed to the constant animation, shows and competitions of Kemer or Antalya feel that the hotel is boring. And indeed, animation happens here in the evenings, and even then once a week. Then the restaurant plays live music and hosts "Turkish Night."

There is a small fitness room, sauna and jacuzzi. But the main entertainment is a trip to Bar Street. She settles at eleven in the evening, and then most of the guests leave there. At the same time, all guests, including young people, behave decently, and no one bothers anyone to relax.



Staff

The staff at the Seray Deluxe Hotel 4 * in Marmaris speak good English. However, they also know Russian. At least one of the daytime employees and a young man working at night will always understand and answer you.

All the hotel staff are friendly, good-natured. If you need help, they are always trying to do their best. Everyone does their job - waiters monitor the order and number of dishes, bartenders pour drinks, wash the area every day. Chefs are trying hard.

Nutrition

Hotel Seray Deluxe Hotel 4 * (Marmaris) has two restaurants. In one of them, tourists are fed according to the all inclusive system. The second refers to the type of "a la carte". One of them is designed for 250 people, and the second - for 150. There are also two bars. But the all-inclusive system ends at ten in the evening.

Of the meat dishes, mainly chicken and Turkish kufte cutlets. A lot of greens, salads with cucumbers and tomatoes that you can season yourself - oil, mayonnaise or yogurt. Of the side dishes - stewed vegetables, potatoes, pasta. Cold appetizers - mainly olives, cheeses, sausages. Of the fruits, apples and watermelon are mainly given. Lots of sweet trays of different kinds.

The bars offer tourists red and white wine, beer, gin, vodka, crayfish, carbonated drinks and soft drinks. There are devices where you can order coffee or juices. Sometimes they can give liver and fish.

The food is not very varied, although tasty. And the gozleme cakes that make here are so easy to eat. But all that you lack, you can buy cheap enough at local bazaars or at Migros.

The hotel also has a paid cafe with good music and an excellent playground. The staff can even look after the children there if you need to leave.



Sea and pools

The hotel has no beach. Tourists go mainly to the public. Before him about five minutes at a slow pace. We can say that the hotel is on the second line. You can swim on a piece of the free beach, but it is not equipped. And there is also an option - to buy a drink in any bar on the promenade and use the sunbed and umbrella all day.

The fact is that not only hotels of the first line, but also all the bars and restaurants by the sea have their own beaches. They are equipped, and sunbeds and umbrellas are designed for guests.

The sea is clean, the beaches are clean. The water is clear, especially in the mornings and evenings, when there are not many people. Reviews of Seray Deluxe Hotel 4 * (Marmaris) assure that the outdoor pool, which is located on its territory, is very large - about 80 meters in area. He works from seven in the morning until six in the evening. There is also a small "paddling pool" for children. If you find it too hot on the beach, then in the afternoon you can calmly sit by the pool in the shade of palm trees or sunbathe on a sun lounger, and go swimming in the sea at a more acceptable time.

Many here like it even more. The pool is clean and warm, and the steps for entering the water are very comfortable.
