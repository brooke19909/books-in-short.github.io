Car DVR Mio MiVue 568: review, specifications and owner reviews

In addition to technical filling, manufacturers of these devices are paying more and more attention to ergonomics. For example, the latest models are equipped with touch displays, and the cases are made in smaller sizes. Along with this, optics are being improved, which extends the functionality of models and makes them more independent of operating conditions. It is these advantages that the Mio MiVue 568 car DVR can boast of with competitors, which also has a rich arsenal of new technological tools. The manufacturer manages to combine compact dimensions and advanced hardware, which satisfies the needs of demanding car owners and the needs of novice users.

Functionality





The model is equipped with a lens whose aperture is f / 1.8. At the same time, the 3-megapixel matrix works with six glass lenses, supplemented by an infrared filter. In combination, this provides a very good picture, even in dim light. You can increase the efficiency of the lens using a polarizing filter, although this is unnecessary for the direct function of a car recorder. As for visibility, 130 degrees is enough to simultaneously cover five lanes. Again, for complex operating conditions, the Mio MiVue 568 DVR is provided with different shooting modes. There is also an exposure setting in it, which allows you to control the brightness of the video with insufficient visibility. But the video quality is devoid of any adjustments. The equipment shoots in 1080p format, but this is abundant by modern standards. The recording itself is possible not only during the movement, but also during the parking of the car. In this case, a battery is provided for autonomous power.

Control

The model does not have traditional control buttons. Unless you need to turn on the device using the button, but there are no physical controls in it. The user will have to deal with a newfangled touch screen. As the manufacturer notes, the recorder's menu is also sharpened for sensory manipulations with the expectation of achieving any setting after a maximum of three clicks. Those who are used to this approach to management will have no problems. The Mio MiVue 568 Touchscreen DVR really has a well-designed and user-friendly interface. In place of the traditional text menu items with line-by-line breaks came signed icons - similar to a computer desktop. Moreover, all sections are organized compactly and conveniently for perception. The only negative relates to Russification - abbreviations, incorrectly used cases and syntax errors do not withstand any criticism. However, you can get used to it.

DVR Features





The model is far from the pinnacle of technological and functional breakthrough in the segment of DVRs. Nevertheless, it is in its category for some positions that the device advances ahead of competitors. And the matter is not only in the touch control method, which is now implemented even in some budget devices. Its main feature is the presence of a GPS module. In practice, the advantages of this add-on are primarily expressed in the ability to track the location coordinates that are attached to the video. But the most important thing is the function of the radar detector, which provides the GPS receiver to the Mio MiVue 568 recorder. With its help, the camera database is loaded automatically, which allows you to control the speed mode in advance. As a result, the risk of violation of traffic rules in sections of the road where stationary cameras are located is reduced. Of course, in this model the radar detector cannot be called full-fledged, but for this class the progress is very substantial.

Positive feedback about the model

The manufacturer has not in vain relied on compact dimensions and the maximum supply of modern functionality. These qualities are primarily highlighted by users. Mobility, reliability of fixation and minimal impact on the direct review of the driver - these advantages are noted by novice car enthusiasts. For a more demanding consumer, the optional fullness of the Mio MiVue 568 comes to the fore. Owner reviews in this regard pay more attention to the GPS module and its benefits during operation. Negative reviews





Not everyone succeeds in achieving high functionality and small sizes in the manufacture of DVRs. Mio coped with this task, but it had to sacrifice other characteristics of the device. Owners note a rather average quality of materials. Although the operating practice is generally positive with respect to the fastening system, serious mechanical stress even damages the housing. There are also complaints about the small battery capacity that the Mio MiVue 568 DVR is equipped with. The reviews also indicate that the model does not perform well when working in cold weather. Conversely, with prolonged use in normal temperature conditions, the device overheats.

How much is?

For its class, the model has good data. This is a set of options, and video quality, and a touch menu with a Mio MiVue 568 location sensor. The price is quite affordable and amounts to 6-7 thousand rubles. On the market you can find devices that are close in cost and functionality, but they will lose in screen size and recording quality. True, already for 5 thousand rubles. there are models with a large battery capacity and a decent size of internal memory. However, they lack touch control and a GPS sensor.

Conclusion





The device is the best choice for connoisseurs of high technology who do not want to overpay for extra features. A balanced optional and well-thought-out ergonomics are the main advantages of the Mio MiVue 568. The reviews will also add to the quality of the resulting video, which thanks to the filters and high resolution allows you to see the smallest details. Of course, there are disadvantages that are expressed in small nuances. Thus, the impression of ergonomics is spoiled by an inconveniently located charger cable, and the lack of a main memory forces each user to acquire microSD. Nevertheless, you can close your eyes to these shortcomings by recalling the GPS module, the original design, the ease of installation of the recorder and, by and large, the rather modest price for such a filling.
