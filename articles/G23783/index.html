Tariff plan without a monthly fee (MTS): which one to choose? The cheapest MTS tariff without a monthly fee

Most of the mobile communication plans that we have to deal with require a monthly fee. This means that the user must regularly pay bills that the operator sets in order to be able to make calls, send SMS messages, and use Internet traffic. Of course, not everyone likes this state of affairs.

There are people who would like to have a number connected to one or another mobile operator purely for receiving calls or, say, for a one-time access to the Internet. Paying constantly and regularly for being able to do this is quite disadvantageous. That is why subscribers often look for a tariff plan without a monthly fee.

MTS, as the leader of the Russian telecom segment, is ready to provide such tariffs. About them and will be discussed in today's article.





Why are tariffs good without a monthly fee?

Let's start by describing why plans are so good without having to regularly pay a fee for their use. In principle, this is obvious - the subscriber is not forced to replenish the account every month, say, by 300 rubles. He pays only for those services that he consumes, for example, for a call every 2 days, SMS or for the day of using the Internet (or even for the amount of traffic that was consumed once).

Thus, a similar approach allows saving in certain situations. After all, choosing a tariff plan without a monthly fee (MTS), you do not pay for additional services that you do not actually use. So, in the end, pay less.

The second point is the psychological attitude of people towards the monthly fee. So our nature is arranged. If the operator introduces an obligation to replenish the account by a certain amount, this can be somewhat depressing to a person. Well, some do not want to feel this feeling, when you have to pay a certain amount once a month. The logic of such subscribers is simple: ‚ÄúI used the services - I paid. But I will not make contributions in advance. ‚Äù

This is less serious, but also an explanation of why MTS tariffs without a monthly fee are more successful than plans with a monthly fee.





Disadvantages of tariffs without a monthly fee

Although, if we look at this situation from an economic point of view in order to establish real benefits for the subscriber, we will understand the opposite. In fact, tariffs with a monthly fee are more profitable. And the reason for this is quite simple - the operator company knows how much money you will definitely spend on communication services next month. Consequently, she can, in a sense, count on your contribution. In addition, when making subscription payments, you buy services in bulk; whereas in a situation without a regular fee, you actually consume them at retail. This, obviously, already affects the final cost of consumed Internet traffic, minutes for calls and SMS provided by the operator.

Therefore, if you need MTS tariffs without a monthly fee, think about whether they really will be so beneficial specifically for you. The answer to this question will be positive only if you really need a number to perform one-time tasks.

Obviously, plans with a monthly fee are more profitable. Even with a quick inspection, this can be seen. The user, being on such a plan, gets in the end much more.

All these tariffs will be supported by MTS for a year without a monthly fee and even more (if there is any activity on the number).

This tariff is aimed at users who often communicate with subscribers from other countries due to the fact that the cost of calls here from 1 to 5 minutes (to CIS numbers, as well as Vietnam, Korea and the USA) is charged at a price of 4.5 rubles; from 6 to 10 minutes - 1.5 rubles. To communicate with subscribers from Moscow, you will have to pay 2.5 rubles per minute for the first 5 minutes. conversation and 1.5 rubles - for each subsequent. Calls to Russian numbers will cost 3 rubles for each of the first 5 minutes and 1.5 rubles each. - for the subsequent ones.

SMS and the Internet in this regard are quite expensive - 9.9 rubles per megabyte of traffic and from 2 to 6.5 rubles - for each message.

Thus, this typical tariff plan without a monthly fee, MTS positions it as for calls abroad. It is possible to use it within the country, but it is less profitable. Super MTS

This plan is an analogue of what is described above, only with this difference: it can be connected not only to residents of Moscow and the Moscow region. It is obviously available throughout Russia, and the cost of calls is calculated using the same formula.
If we talk briefly about both Super MTS tariffs, then only 2 advantages will be obvious - the absence of a mandatory fee and 20 minutes of free calls in the subscriber's region. The rest of the services are obviously provided at too high prices, due to which the operator, obviously, compensates for the lack of a regular fee.

Red energy





At the cost of additional services, the Red Energy tariff is similar to Super. True, it does not imply the availability of free 20 minutes to local MTS numbers. But, according to this plan, a subscriber can call at a low price - only 1.6 rubles per minute to all numbers from his region. This also applies to numbers of other operators.

The alternative is obvious: either the user is given free calls to his network numbers (Super MTS), or cheaper calls with all numbers from his region (this tariff plan) without a monthly fee. MTS, allowing making such a choice, creates additional comfort for the user. Let's put it this way: depending on who you often communicate with over the phone, these two plans can be very beneficial for you.

Per second





The third package, which we will consider along with the two mentioned, is called "Secondary". He is also part of the group that MTS has - tariffs without a monthly fee. True, this plan differs in the tariffing of conversations - it happens per second.

This means that for every second of conversation with any number from your region, you must pay 5 cents. Thus, by simple calculations, it can be established that a minute of conversation will cost 3 rubles.

MTS has connected such a tariff plan without a monthly fee for users who need a few seconds of conversation to say something most important and immediately hang up. At the same time, the cost of additional services (calls to other numbers, the Internet and SMS) here are the same as on ‚ÄúSuper MTS‚Äù and on Red Energy.
