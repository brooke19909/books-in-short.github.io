Sea and beach

A 5-minute walk away is a nice beach. Just cross the road to get on it. But for Russian tourists this is not so easy to do. The local market is a major attraction near the hotel

At 500 meters from the hotel there is a night market. Mostly they sell food products. In a small area there are several rows with fruits and other dishes. Thais happily pack their large purchases in baskets for free. There, in the market, you can taste your purchases, comfortably accommodated in a small cafe. Thais have a special relationship with food. Dishes that are more than 2 hours from the date of preparation are considered unsuitable for consumption. Therefore, all food is fresh and very tasty. Everything is prepared in front of customers. Food in Thailand is very cheap. Therefore, the hotel can only buy breakfast, and for dinner go to one of the many restaurants or to the market.

Children can also find activities here. Enterprising Thais built a small scene for small artists, and every night there are children's discos. Nearby is an inflatable mini-town. All entertainment for children is open until 11 pm.
