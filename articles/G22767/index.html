How to turn on the Internet on iPhone 5S: tips, tricks, instructions

Today it is difficult to imagine a person who does not use the Internet. Virtuality has become an integral part of life. Therefore, people are increasingly thinking about how to turn on the Internet on an iPhone 5S or any other mobile device. In fact, there is nothing difficult in the process. And even a novice owner of "apple" products will be able to cope with the task. Next, everything will be told about working with the Internet on the iPhone 5S.

Different networks

It is important to understand that modern smartphones work with different networks. Depending on which Internet is implied, the algorithm of actions taken to enable it will depend.



Today, iPhone works with the following networks:

  • 2G;
  • 3G
  • 4G (latest device versions);
  • Wi-Fi
  • LTE

Also, the algorithm of actions will depend slightly on the operating system installed on the phone. But this is not such a big problem. The only difference is that the menu signature in the newer iOS has changed (while retaining the general meaning).

Mobile operator

The first step is to deal with the work of mobile Internet. It allows you to use a SIM card to connect to the network. Convenient, fast and easy. But how to turn on the Internet on the iPhone 5S?

The process is independent of the selected operator. When working with any cellular communication, the same algorithm of actions is preserved. The difference is only in the network settings - they are different for all companies.



How to turn on the Internet on iPhone 5S? "Megaphone", "Tele2", "MTS" or "Beeline" - this is not so important. The main thing is that to configure the network you will have to adhere to the following algorithm of actions:

  1. Insert the SIM card into the smartphone and turn it on. Wait for the readiness for further work of the gadget.
  2. Open the menu "Settings" - "Cellular".
  3. Go to "Cellular Data Network."
  4. Enter data to configure the device. You must enter the APN, username and password. This information is specified in advance with the mobile operator.

Nothing more is needed. Now the device will work with mobile Internet (2G / 3G). The process does not cause any difficulties. For iOS8

For example, you should learn about how to turn on mobile Internet on iOS8. In general, the algorithm of actions is not much different from the previously proposed instructions. But there are differences. In order for the Internet to work without problems on iOS8, you will have to pay attention to some additional settings.



Which ones? How to turn on the Internet on an iPhone 5S with an operating system of version 8? This can be done if you follow the instructions:

  1. In the smartphone, go to the "Settings" menu.
  2. Go to the "Cellular" section. Here, put the switch in the "On" mode in the "Cellular data" field.
  3. Enable LTE adoption. To do this, click on the switch next to the corresponding inscription.
  4. Click on "Cellular Data Network".
  5. Enter data from the Internet. They, as in the previous case, are specified directly by the mobile operator.
  6. Save changes.

Wifi

Now a little about Wi-Fi. This type of Internet is used by modern users extremely often. Working with him is easier than it sounds.

How to turn on the Internet on iPhone 5S? To do this, you must:

  1. Turn on iPhone. Wait until the smartphone is ready for further work.
  2. Go to gadget settings. Choose the Wi-Fi section there.
  3. Set the switch to on.
  4. Wait for detection of available networks. Click on the name of the Internet to which you plan to connect.
  5. Enter the network access password if necessary.

You can close the network settings. A few seconds after entering the password, Wi-Fi will start working. Now the Internet is connected on your mobile device! No more options are provided in the gadget.
