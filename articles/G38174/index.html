Only recently in Russia the system has undergone changes. And now it is necessary to independently, albeit partially, provide a comfortable old age. The so-called funded part of the pension helps here. These deductions serve as a kind of addition to the basic payments. But how do I know where my funded part of my pension is? By territory

Thinking how to find out where is my pension (funded part)? To do this, you can use any of the methods proposed above. Next, we will talk more about each method. Everyone has their own characteristics.

The first and easiest way is to apply to the territorial pension fund. You will need only an identity card and SNILS. Make an appropriate statement, sometimes even verbally. Fund employees will have to check the location of your funded part of the pension, and then give you information. Important: this information is not communicated to third parties. Therefore, it is important that in addition to SNILS, you should have a passport with you. Other IDs are usually not accepted in pension funds.



Through employer

How to find out where your funded part of the pension is? To do this, you can contact your employer. You will need to go to the accounting department of the organization where you work. Indeed, it is from wages that interest is initially transferred for the formation of savings "for old age." All you need to do is ask the accounting department to give you information about the transfer of your pension to a particular fund. You may express your wish in writing or verbally. After you will be informed of the necessary data. Nothing difficult. True, this method is not always appropriate. Rarely does anyone use it.

FIU

Where can I find out about the funded part of the pension? You can contact the Pension Fund of Russia. To his main department. Information about all pension contributions is stored there . To get the necessary information, you will need to present a passport, as well as SNILS. According to these data, employees will inform you of the information that interests you. Sometimes they may be asked to write a request-statement in writing. Not too common, but it does occur.

Bank

Where to find out the size of the funded part of the pension and its location? Any major bank can help here. And, more precisely, those financial institutions with which the Pension Fund of Russia has concluded agreements. Usually these are large organizations like VTB and Sberbank.



It is in such banks that you can get data on finding pension contributions. To do this, you will need to make a request statement, as well as attach your ID card and SNILS to it. After a while, the bank will check the accounts of Russian pension funds. Then it will give you information about your condition. His whereabouts will also be brought to your attention. It is a very common option, which is not so difficult to implement.

"Government services"

How to find out where your funded part of the pension is? At the moment, even the Internet is able to help implement this idea. More precisely, the portal "State Services". It appeared not so long ago, but already now it greatly facilitates the life of the population. You will need to register on the service, and then go through authorization there. It is necessary to pay attention to the fact that this process takes several weeks. After all, after registering a user, your profile must go through activation. Once you have a working account, you can proceed with the actions.

Further information is ordered in the "My Account". To do this, in the list of services offered, select the appropriate request. After a while you will receive the information you are interested in. But how do I know where my funded part of my pension through the Internet is?
