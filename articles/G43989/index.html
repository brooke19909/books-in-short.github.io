Hotel The Royale Assagao Resort 3 * (India, North Goa): description, photos and reviews of tourists

India is one of the oldest countries in the world with exotic nature and unique culture. Recently, this tropical state for relaxation is chosen by many domestic tourists. And no wonder. In India, vacationers are waiting for not only comfortable beaches and a warm sea, but also many interesting sights. You can relax in this country very well.

The most popular resort destination in India is Goa. And especially its northern part. Since the tourist infrastructure in this resort is developed just fine, you can relax here with great comfort. So, you can find a more or less good inexpensive hotel in the north of Goa without any problems. For example, The Royale Assagao Resort 3 * hotel offers comfortable lodging for its guests, reviews about which are good on the Web.





Location Features

This hotel is located near the town of Vagator, 17 km from Panaji (the capital of Goa) and 7 km from Mapusa. The climate in this part of India is tropical, with very warm winters and hot summers. The nature in the vicinity of Panaji and Vagator, according to most tourists, is simply magnificent. The plains and hills in this area are completely covered with magnificent tropical vegetation. The city of Vagator is located not directly on the coast, but a few kilometers from it. However, getting to the beach by taxi from the hotel usually does not take long - no more than 5 minutes.

The nearest airport to Vagator is an hour's drive (41 km) - near the village of Dabolim. Russian tourists from Moscow usually fly here. It is difficult to get to Vagator by public transport, since you have to make many transfers. It is easier to get to this city by taxi. But it is best to book a transfer at The Royale Assagao Resort 3 * hotel itself. True, you will have to pay for it separately.

The hotel is located in the suburbs of Vagator, 2 km from its center - in the village of Assagao. His exact address: Assagao, Bardez, Goa - 403507, India.





The Royale Assagao Resort 3 *: general hotel description

This hotel is a few buildings with comfortable apartments. Many tourists think that the Royale Assagao Resort is particularly suitable for families. The fact is that the village of Assagao is a very quiet place. There are no nightlife, noisy roads, no crowds of tourists. Enormous palm trees surround the Royale Assagao Resort and the air is crystal clear.

Hotel pool

The beaches from this hotel are quite far away. But if desired, water procedures can be taken directly on its territory. A large swimming pool with clean fresh water is what the Royale Assagao Resort 3 * offers its guests, among other things. A photo of the pool of this hotel is presented below. As you can see, it is quite large in size. Getting down into it is very convenient. For this, wide steps are provided. The tourists on the network respond quite well about the hotel pool. Swimming here is pleasant because the water is always warm. Nearby is a bar where you can order a variety of drinks. Tourists have the opportunity to lie in comfortable sun loungers. In addition to the adult, the hotel also has a small shallow (children's) outdoor pool.





Dining at the hotel

Breakfast at The Royale Assagao Resort 3 * hotel is included in the price. However, according to tourists, the food here is not too good. For breakfast, most guests take only tea or coffee with milk. You can, of course, order one of the dishes offered by the local dining room. However, all of them, according to Russian vacationers, are too sharp and are designed mainly for Indians, who often rent apartments in this hotel.

There is a restaurant on site. But here it is also unlikely to have a good lunch or dinner. According to many tourists, the dishes in this restaurant are completely tasteless. In addition, they are very expensive. Most vacationers who have ever rented rooms at this hotel are advised not to eat on its territory, but on some of the local beaches. In a cafe by the sea, vacationers are offered many dishes for every taste and at not too high cost.





Beaches

As already mentioned, this hotel is not located by the sea. Buses to the coast from the village of Assagao do not go. Therefore, guests of The Royale Assagao Resort 3 * hotel get to the beach by taxi. Carriers are guarding vacationers right at the fence of the hotel. And consequently, problems with hiring a taxi for residents of the hotel in any case can not arise. A trip to the sea is about 250 rupees. If desired, hotel guests have the opportunity to sunbathe on the beaches:

  • Vagator and Anjuna (4.5 km from the hotel);

  • Baga (5.5 km);

  • Kalungute (6 km);

  • Arambol (20 km).

Anjuna beach, according to most tourists who have ever vacationed at The Royale Assagao Resort 3 *, is better for young people than for families. Here, parties are often held with dancing and a sea of ‚Äã‚Äãalcohol.

Calangute Beach is the most popular in Goa. There are usually a lot of people sunbathing, and sun loungers are close to each other. The infrastructure on this beach is very well developed. Tourists can purchase anything from food to souvenirs, beach items and goods on the go.

Baga Beach is located in a picturesque place surrounded by cliffs. The coastline here is very wide and covered with soft sand of beige hue. There are fewer people on this beach than on Calangute. You can come here as a family.

Arambol beach, like Anjuna, is better for young people. Here very often informals of all stripes gather. Tourists who come to India on a package tour are rarely on this beach. Mostly here savages rest, renting a house in Goa for a long time.

Vagator beach is popular mainly not among tourists, but among lovers of modern music. Young people come here from all over the world. This beach still retains the atmosphere of the hippies of the 60-70s, once massively living in northern Goa. It is here that is the closest to get from The Royale Assagao Resort 3 * hotel. Vagator is the only beach in northern Goa where you can sunbathe, including topless. Elsewhere on the coast, this is considered unacceptable.





Opinions of tourists about the hotel

Reviews on the Web about the hotel The Royale Assagao Resort 3 * (India) are different. Some consider this hotel quite suitable for relaxation, according to others, it does not correspond to the stated three stars. Most tourists praise the hotel rooms, considering them spacious and well equipped. However, some vacationers believe that the furniture in the hotel rooms is too old, and the staff does not clean as carefully as they would like. Sheets and towels are changed daily at this hotel. But their view, according to many vacationers, is not very attractive. Sheets and pillowcases in this hotel are washed and ironed. But the hotel pool is praised by almost all vacationers. Swimming in it is really convenient.

In general, according to most vacationers, The Royale Assagao Resort 3 * hotel is suitable mainly for those tourists who prefer to spend time on vacation mainly on the beach, in entertainment venues or sightseeing. The place is quiet and suitable for an overnight stay. But the food and service here still leave much to be desired.





What else to do

India is an ancient country, and there are many interesting places worth visiting by tourists. If desired, guests of The Royale Assagao Resort 3 * hotel can visit, for example, the Cansaulim Church (church on the hill), the Mascarenhas Mansion mansion in Mapusa, the ruins of Fort Chapora, etc. The hotel also has a tour desk, which certainly adds to its attractiveness in the eyes of tourists. Excursions at the hotel, like almost everywhere in Goa, are not particularly expensive.

sights

Cansaulim Church, or, as tourists call this attraction, a church on a hill, is located in the center of Mapusa. This religious building was built in the middle of the last century and is popular with both local residents and city guests.

The Mascarenhas Mansion mansion is a monument of architecture built in the Portuguese style. It is located in Anjuna and simply fascinates vacationers with monumentality and luxury.

The ruins of Fort Chapora are worth a visit to all, without exception, tourists who have chosen northern Goa for relaxation. This grandiose building was built by the Portuguese in the VXII century. There was an ancient castle in this place before the fort. The fort is located at the very top of the mountain, which offers truly magnificent views.
