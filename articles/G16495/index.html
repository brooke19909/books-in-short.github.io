A tablet with good sound: an overview of models, features and reviews

Tablets are not as popular as they were 5 years ago. This is due to the fact that smartphone manufacturers create their products so versatile that it seems that laptops can soon be abandoned. However, many users still remain true to the tablets. Therefore, people often look for tablets with good sound or devices for work.

Tablets

Since tablets are not very popular right now, manufacturers have stopped working to ensure that each model can be attributed to a certain type. They are increasingly trying to produce universal models, among which one can single out the budget segment and tablets for work.

To find a gaming tablet, you will have to consider many options, studying their technical characteristics. You will have to do the same in finding a tablet with good sound. Although even now, some manufacturers may indicate this advantage in advertising or on the box.

For what?

A tablet with good sound is an excellent replacement for a multimedia and working device. Many people choose this type of tablet to watch cartoons and videos with the whole family. Also, the option is suitable for those who want to show presentations or graphics with animation.

Lenovo Yoga Tablet 3 Pro

But this Lenovo model is much better. You can buy it for 20-25 thousand rubles. This is one of the top models of the company, which boasts not only parameters, but also a beautiful appearance. Technical specifications Lenovo Yoga Tablet 3 Pro

The model is running Intel Atom x5-Z8500. It is a fairly fast processor with frequencies up to 2,400 MHz. Inside only 2 GB of RAM. Storage is available at 32 GB, although the user will be able to occupy only 22 GB. The rest of the space is occupied by the system.

This is one of the most autonomous tablets - 6,600 mAh. It can work up to 18 hours, although it withstands full-fledged activity for 7-8 hours.

A review of tablets with good sound should include this particular model, since JBL speakers are built into it. Thanks to them, the device seems the loudest on the market. At the same time, it does not distort the sound, but helps to highlight high and medium frequencies. There are problems with low ones.

Lenovo Yoga Tablet 3 Pro reviews

This is a great model that is suitable for both work and entertainment. Users are satisfied with almost everything: excellent appearance, good system performance, as well as excellent sound. Samsung Tab S3

This model retains its corporate identity. It seems noticeably cumbersome, especially when compared to its direct rival iPad Pro 10.5. Of course, the design here is an amateur. I would like the frames around the screen to be smaller and remove additional touch buttons.

However, this is one of the best Samsung tablets with good sound (DAC). But for the quality will have to pay about 45-50 thousand rubles.

Technical specifications Samsung Tab S3

Not only sound, but also the screen turned out to be special in this model. This is a 9.7-inch display with a resolution of 2048 x 1536 pixels and an AMOLED matrix. It pleases the eye with its bright and rich colors.

Inside, the Snapdragon 820 works, which easily copes with all gaming innovations and resource-intensive programs. The device works with 4 GB of RAM, which is not always enough. The internal storage is only 32 GB, so you may need a memory card.

The tablet works with four speakers. The volume in the device is very high, and the sound quality is at an altitude. The only thing is that low frequencies, as always, lose high and medium.

Samsung Tab S3 Reviews

This is a great tablet with good sound and screen. Users noted powerful technical characteristics that turn the tablet into a working tool. Of course, the look is already a little tired. Xiaomi MiPad 2

This is not the most expensive tablet on the market, since Xiaomi is still treated with caution. Models are difficult to beat mastodons from Apple or Samsung. But if you look at the cost, and then on the device parameters, the question arises: why pay more?

Externally, the model looks very fashionable and stylish. It may appeal to those who have long dreamed of an iPad, but cannot afford a tablet. For only 12-13 thousand you can buy a copy of it. Let it be inferior in some respects.

Specifications Xiaomi MiPad 2

The device has a screen diagonal of 7.9 inches with a resolution of 2048 x 1536 pixels, with a battery of 6,190 mAh. Inside, the Intel Atom X5-Z8500 mobile processor operates at a frequency of 2,200 MHz. RAM is only 2 GB, which may not be enough for demanding applications, and the internal storage is designed for 16 or 64 GB, depending on the configuration.

Given that this is a fairly budget model, it can be considered an excellent tablet with good sound in the headphones. True, for this you will also have to look for a good headset.

Xiaomi MiPad 2 reviews

This is a good inexpensive model. She is hardly able to cope with work programs and complex tasks. Ideal for surfing the Internet and the simplest arcades. Thanks to the good resolution and high-quality matrix, the device can cope with watching movies, especially since the sound allows this.
