What is a GoPro? Overview of the best camera models and their description

Action cameras are successful not only among extreme sportsmen, but also gaining popularity both among professional operators and amateurs. Athletes value devices for environmental protection and ease of use, and operators for compactness and the ability to receive exciting shots. Cyclists buy action cameras for use as a regular DVR.

GoPro Brand Benefits

What is a GoPro? A California company was the first to introduce action-format cameras to the world, so today most people associate such models with products of a particular brand. There are a lot of action cameras on the market, but GoPro is the leader. Brand models bypass competitors in several ways:

  1. Proprietary software. The cameras support software for PCs and smartphones so that users can quickly process footage, synchronize between devices and transmit.

  2. Water resistant. Competitors Xiaomi, Sony, and Garmin offer waterproof cases in addition to cameras, while GoPros are designed to be submerged by default. No additional fixtures are required for this.

  3. Voice control. The voice assistant supports seven languages, while the models of many other brands speak only English.

  4. Electronic stabilization. A huge advantage of Go Pro, which is especially appreciated by professionals, is electronic rather than optical stabilization.

Some cameras also have a small amount of internal memory (the problem is solved by buying an SD card) and have a built-in battery (this complicates the repair).

Other worthy models

What is a GoPro? This is a brand for which it is very difficult to make a fair rating, because each model has several advantages and a number of disadvantages. GoPro Hero 4 Session

What is a GoPro? The brand has long been associated with the flagships of the market, but gradually the need arose to create budget devices. The Hero 4 Session camera turned out to be compact, but functional: it is possible to shoot in Full HD or lower quality. There is no powerful processor, which ensures longer operation of the device. Features of models of the Hero series

Comparison of GoPro cameras shows that the most successful models were developed as part of the Hero line. The line is constantly being improved. There are solutions for professionals (Hero Black), beginners (White) and amateurs (Silver). Most models have similar characteristics:

  1. Waterproof case or a special case for underwater shooting (immersion up to 10 meters).

  2. Color touch screen (2 inches). In the seventh series, the sensitivity of the sensor increased significantly, and the icons became larger, which greatly facilitated the work.

  3. The presence of the HyperSmooth stabilizer allows you to record smooth, high-quality videos and create short clips.

  4. The GoPro App allows you to stream live and share frames online.

  5. There is Full HD, a shooting mode in a wide format, you can use the timer for shooting.

  6. Synchronization with a smartphone is automatic.

  7. Using the sensor, you can zoom.

  8. There is voice control.

  9. Wireless interfaces: Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, GPS.

The kit usually includes the GoPro camera itself, a protective frame, a USB charging cable, a sticker with a company logo, adhesive tape for a helmet, a flat tape on double adhesive tape.
