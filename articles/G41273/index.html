Park-hotel "Aurora", Prokopyevsk. Description. Reviews

Prokopyevsk is called the "Black Pearl" of Kuzbass. A coal mining industry is developed here; there are several mines in the city. Specialists often come here on business trips, so the issue of their placement is topical. The Aurora Park Hotel in Prokopyevsk, Kemerovo Region, offers comfortable accommodation to city guests.

Description

The location of the modern hotel complex is the largest Zenkovsky park in Kuzbass. The airport from here is at a distance of 5 kilometers, the railway station - at 20 kilometers. The distance from the hotel to the city center is a little over 11 kilometers.

Meals are organized in the hotel's own restaurant. In the classic bar, in addition to drinks, you can order √† la carte dishes. After a hard day, guests have the opportunity to relax in the classic sauna with pool, or work out in the gym. Proponents of an active lifestyle can move around on the tennis court. There is a playground for children on site. Equipped parking lot. In the evenings, guests can play bowling and billiards.

The hotel consists of 28 rooms of various levels of comfort. The park-hotel "Aurora" in Prokopyevsk is located at: Zenkovsky district, Zaparkovaya street, 63.

Accommodation

The hotel offers several categories of rooms:

  • Double standard with one double or two and a half sleeping beds. Cost - 3000 rubles per day for two, and 2800 rubles per person.
  • Double VIP standard with one double or two and a half sleeping beds. The cost is 3600 rubles per day for two, and 3400 rubles per person.
  • Junior Suite with a double bed. The price is 3500 rubles per day for two, and 3300 rubles per person.
  • Suite with a double bed. The price is 4200 rubles per day for two, and 4000 rubles per person.
  • VIP Suite. Accommodation will cost 4,700 rubles per day for two, and 4,500 rubles per person.
  • Apartments. The price is 8700 rubles per day for two, and 8500 rubles per person.

Room rates include breakfast. In the park-hotel "Aurora" in Prokopyevsk, all rooms are equipped with a TV, telephone, mini fridge.

Additional services

In order to make your stay in the hotel complex as comfortable as possible, a number of additional services are provided here:

  • Tennis fans are equipped with modern acrylic courts. Rackets and balls can be rented here.
  • At a specially organized forest area, guests can play paintball.
  • In winter, guests can go ice-skating and skiing. There is a rental of sports equipment.
  • The sauna with spacious rooms has a pool with hydromassage and a relaxation room with upholstered furniture.
  • The Park Hotel Aurora in Prokopyevsk (photo below) offers a modern gym.
  • Fans of billiards can enjoy their favorite game in the cozy bar with the right lighting.
  • You can have fun at the bowling alley.
  • Also at your service is an aquacenter that will surprise guests with an original design solution.
  • In conference rooms equipped with modern presentation equipment, you can help organize business events of any status.
  • In the spacious restaurant you can hold a banquet, corporate party or wedding.
