Eden Rock Hotel 4 * (Rhodes, Greece): photos, prices and reviews of tourists from Russia

Rhodes is one of the most beautiful islands in the Mediterranean. Located in the southeast of the Aegean Sea. On the one hand, Rhodes is washed by the Aegean Sea, and on the other - by the Mediterranean, so tourists visiting the island will have the opportunity to swim in two seas at once. Different eras are intertwined on the island among themselves, creating an atmosphere of antiquity and mystery on Rhodes and promising their guests a journey filled with adventure and experience.

One of the ancient legends says that the island, covered with flowers, rose from the depths of the sea and it was a gift to Helios, the god of the sun from Zeus. Therefore, Helios is considered the patron saint of Rhodes, many statues were erected in his honor, one of which under the name Colossus of Rhodes is a wonder of the world.

Food, restaurants and bars of the hotel

For its guests, Eden Rock Hotel 4 * provides two types of food: All Inclusive, which includes breakfast, lunch and dinner at the buffet, snacks during the day, ice cream, as well as locally-made soft drinks and with degrees. Themed dinners are held three times a week. And the "Breakfast" (Bed & Breakfast), consists of a breakfast buffet.

Reviews of Russian tourists are rich in admiration for the variety and abundance of dishes on the buffet. Separately, many celebrate wonderful desserts. There are few negative reviews about the service of waiters, only some notice a lack of service people.

The hotel has 2 restaurants and a tavern located right on the seafront called Dikti, which offers its guests wonderful traditional Greek dishes, as well as 3 bars.



Entertainment and leisure at the hotel

Eden Rock Hotel 4 * offers its guests a diverse range of entertainment. These are day and evening animation, live music, a cinema, show programs, an entertainment center. Those who wish can also go for a massage or visit a beauty salon.

For lovers of a healthy lifestyle and sport, or just active people, the hotel provides an opportunity to do yoga and aqua aerobics, work out in the gym. From games, guests can engage in beach volleyball, mini-football, basketball, mini-golf, billiards. For tennis lovers there is a tennis court and table tennis.

Guests can enjoy water activities using the rental of water skis, bicycles, motorbikes, canoes and catamarans, diving equipment, banana, bagel. You can have fun surfing or parasailing, take scuba diving courses or rent a boat for a boat trip or fishing.

For children

Young guests will also have something to do in Eden Rock Hotel 4 * (Rhodes). Reviews say that the animation in the hotel is mostly for children. A playground has been arranged, mini-discos, children's pools with slides are regularly held . Tourists say that there is a special person near the pool who monitors the children and does not allow to enter the pool without life hoses or vests. Pools

The hotel has 3 swimming pools, two of which are for children. Near each is a bar with various drinks. All pools are filled with sea water. Around the pools are free sunbeds and umbrellas.



Sea and beach

The hotel is very conveniently located on the first line - to the beach 150 meters from the main building, from the bungalow even less. The complex has its own pebble beach, but many reviews say that this does not make it worse, but rather, it is very comfortable there, and walking on pebbles is not at all painful. And children instead of sandpies build stone fortresses. Despite the fact that paid umbrellas and sunbeds are announced, some guests who visited the hotel note that their use is free. There are not enough sun loungers for all, as guests from neighboring hotels are often noticed on the beach. A beach towel can be secured. Changing cabins are installed. The water in the sea is clear and invigorating. Depth starts off the coast. When engaged in scuba diving, in order to observe the various inhabitants of the sea, you have to swim far from the coast, you can only see crabs nearby.
