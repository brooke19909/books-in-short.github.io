History of occurrence

Versatility "Seagulls"

Initially, the health resort treated children with anemia, scrofula, rheumatism and tuberculosis, although in general the complex was bone tuberculosis. In the 60s there was an outbreak of polio, and the leadership of the sanatorium decided to open two corresponding departments. In 1965, all poliomyelitis and tuberculosis patients were taken out, and the sanatorium again became rheumatological. And since 1970, the neuropsychiatric and nephrological departments were opened. Nowadays there are many profiles in Seagull: cardiological, rheumatological, neuropsychiatric, gastroenterological, therapeutic.
