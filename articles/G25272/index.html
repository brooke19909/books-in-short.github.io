Popular ski resorts in Kamchatka

In Kamchatka, construction and reconstruction of ski resorts is actively underway. The regional government attracts investors from different countries to make the region a popular destination for tourists from all over the world.

The season is almost all year round; on the tops of mountains and volcanoes, snow also lies in the summer. The only difficulty for skiers can be blizzards in February and March.

Kamchatka ski resorts attract lovers of extreme skiing - freeride and heli-skiing.

Freeride - skiing or snowboarding on the virgin snow, and not on the laid tracks.

Heli-ski is a type of skiing, when the athlete in a helicopter is lifted to the top of the mountain, and he goes down himself (there are no tracks either).

In Kamchatka, ski resorts are not the only entertainment. Here you can go fishing, and hunt, and admire the scenery, get acquainted with the life of the local population.

Prices in the ski resorts of Kamchatka are several times lower than in Europe. An inexpensive weekly tour with accommodation (breakfast may be included) and passage to the track costs 50-70 thousand rubles. The flight is paid separately.

"Frost Mountain" - the training center of the Olympic team

Base "Mount Moroznaya" has become a popular ski resort in Kamchatka, primarily because on its tracks for more than a decade the Russian team has been training before the Olympic Games.

There are 6 tracks in the complex, one of them is for children (300 m long), the rest are for experienced skiers and professionals.

There are 3 * hotels near the slopes, you can rent apartments (prices start from 2000 rubles per day) or stay in the center of Paratunka, which is a wellness center. For visitors open pools with thermal waters.

A popular pastime here is dog sledding.

The cost of passage to the track and use of the ski lift (ski pass) is about 1600 rubles.

"Spring" - to ride and swim in the open air

In the Rodnikovaya complex, visitors are attracted by 2 types of entertainment:

  • swimming in open thermal springs (hot water in them);
  • skiing or snowboarding from volcanoes (the highest - Zhupanovsky, reaches 2900 m), tourists climb to the top by helicopter.

The base includes houses for visitors. Some of them have baths for 4-5 people. Rodnikovaya is one of 2 places in the Vilyuchi river valley where the hot spring comes to the surface, the water temperature can reach 62 ¬∞ .

The length of the ski slopes here is up to 4.5 km.

Edelweiss - for children and adults

"Edelweiss" - a base that has been operating since 1969. There are 7 most routes here, there is a children's slope with 2 slides. The length of the tracks is shorter compared to previous centers - 1300 km.

The sports complex is open for tourists 6 days a week (on weekdays only in the evening), Monday is a day off. Junior athletes train here. For lovers, instructors are offered.

Accommodation at the base is not possible. But, since it is located in the city of Petropavlovsk-Kamchatsky, it is not difficult to find a hotel nearby.

"Krasnaya Sopka" - a resort in the city center

Base "Red Hill" is also located in Petropavlovsk-Kamchatsky.

On the territory of this complex there are 3 tracks, there is a slope for beginners and 3 lifts, the maximum length of the route is 1.4 km.

The center attracts tourists with a beautiful view along the slopes, accessible for skiing 6 days a week, including at night due to artificial lighting.
