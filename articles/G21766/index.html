How to make a Lemarshan box with your own hands?

So, how is Lemarshan's box made with his own hands? For a regular box that will not open, either wood or textolite is suitable as a base. Drawer made of wood

Another option for such a cube is a craft made of wood with retractable sides. Using a jigsaw, you need to process the inner and outer surfaces of the sides of the cube very carefully. On 4 sides inside, pull-out joints need to be cut. Grooves for them should be provided from two other parties.





The cube itself is folded together using these pull-out elements. Such a box opens by simultaneously moving sideways of all 4 moving blocks. The upper and lower sides remain motionless. If a person likes to cut wood, and there is a set for sawing with a jigsaw, then all the drawings can be done independently, then processed and varnished on wood.
