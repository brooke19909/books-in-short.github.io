Hotel "Hellas", Alushta: reviews and photos

Semidvorye is a small village located on the southwest coast of the peninsula and is one of the most beautiful and cozy places in Crimea. The place is a little wild and far from civilization, but still a favorite for those who want to retire, to escape from the bustle of the city and from noisy parties. In this part of the peninsula, Crimea changes its appearance from the popular, noisy southern to the mysterious, gentle eastern, where there is an abundance of juniper thickets with a pleasant aroma and virgin forests with pistachio trees. In this beauty and silence, there are many cottages, campsites, holiday homes and youth camps. The height of the holiday season is in May, and in late autumn silence prevails.

Hotel Hellas (Semidvorye)





The hotel complex "Hellas" is a new, cozy and comfortable corner of the Crimea, where only the sounds of the Black Sea are heard and there is no noisy city bustle. It is located in a beautiful area, among nature reserves with rare conifers and centuries-old juniper at the foot of the Demerdzhi massif. The aroma of pine needles is saturated with healing substances - volatile, which are very useful and beneficial for the human body. The combination of sea air with the smells of juniper creates a unique feeling and a favorable microclimate, which is very useful for the nervous system, toning up.

Location





Hotel "Hellas" near Alushta has a favorable location, which allows guests to visit interesting and famous historical places with minimal travel costs. Among them are the Vorontsovsky and Livadia palaces familiar to all tourists, the famous beautiful Nikitsky Botanical Garden, as well as the Bakhchisaray and Sudak Fortress, many ancient caves and other architectural monuments.

How to get to the hotel "Hellas" (Alushta)? First you need to get to the railway station of Simferopol. From here to Alushta there are regular buses, fixed-route taxis, and even trolleybuses No. 51 and 52. Then you should get to the village of Semidvorye. Buses run from Alushta directly to the Hellada Hotel.

Beach vacation

The sea coast and the beach are within walking distance. The beaches are equipped with everything necessary for both adults and children. Convenient entry and exit from the sea, large awnings, deck chairs, piers with stairs to exit the water. Adults can dive freely from the pier, and for recreation with children near the coast there is a pontoon. You can enjoy it in the sun with pleasure.

The hotel complex has rooms of various categories: standard, junior suite and suite.

Room Feature





Room standard area has approximately 45 sq. m, junior suite - 60 square meters. m., the suite consists of two rooms (living room and bedroom) and occupies about 130 square meters. m. All apartments have a TV, air conditioning, fridge-bar, personal safe, shower, electric kettle with utensils. On the balcony there is plastic furniture. All rooms are very comfortable with beautiful sea views. Each has a spacious balcony, a separate equipped entrance hall, a bathroom and a shower.

Dining at the hotel to choose from. A continental breakfast can be ordered, and meals from the restaurant menu are available at an additional cost. The hotel offers its guests dishes for every taste, prepared only with fresh products grown in the area.

The hotel territory is equipped with everything necessary for leisure tourists and guests. The range of services includes a first-aid post, territory security, parking, taxi call, billiards room, massage room with sauna, laundry, cafes, bars and restaurants with oriental cuisine.

For young tourists there is a children's playroom with attractions, a playground with pools, on the beach - children's sandboxes, as well as babysitting services.

Comfort, convenience, hospitality can offer its guests the hotel "Hellas" (Alushta). Prices in 2016 range from 5 thousand rubles. up to 11 thousand rubles per room.

Territory





On the same territory with Hellas there are several more hotels. The terrain itself is incredibly picturesque, although not very large. Visitors celebrate the excellent design of the landscape. Conventionally, the territory can be divided into two parts. The first includes the promenade, restaurant and buildings of some complexes. The second is on a hill. From one part to another you can get on foot or drive a local car. The hill offers a picturesque view, the surrounding area is very well-groomed.

All hotels, including Hellas, and the restaurant are in the same style. From the side it looks very impressive. Poseidon Restaurant welcomes guests of the Hellas Hotel who have ordered a buffet. In the evening, the restaurant is open as usual. The water in the pools is chlorinated, but there is practically no smell. There is a children's area. The cleanliness is constantly monitored by staff.

There is a first-aid post, shops and a pharmacy on site. Prices are overpriced and the selection is small.

You can get to Alushta by bus, which runs three times a day (the schedule can be checked at the hotel). It is also possible to order a taxi.

The hotel employs qualified massage therapists who, for a small fee, conduct not only a massage session, but also provide valuable health advice.

Beach area

For those who prefer a beach holiday, Crimea will become especially hospitable (Alushta, Semidvorye). Hotel "Hellas" has its own equipped beach. Also on the territory are the beach areas of other hotels. You can choose any of them.

There are always free sun loungers on the beaches. But sunbeds under a canopy must be occupied in advance, in the morning. Umbrellas are available at an additional cost.

Storm at sea in this region does not happen often. But at this time, it is better to refrain from swimming. Waves lift debris and algae from the bottom. Also, the waters of the local river carry a stream of dirty water into the sea. There is a drain near the Hellas beach, therefore the water here is the cleanest.

Nutrition Reviews

The promenade is simply strewn with numerous cafes. There are also restaurants at the top. The most favorite places for tourists are Lemon, Lavender and Da Vasco. The last two have open terraces with a pleasant design. They serve very quickly and efficiently. The menu is varied. Of the minuses, it can be noted overpriced, but for the resort it is excusable. On the beaches you can buy delicious chebureks with various fillings and ice cream.

The buffet in the Poseidon restaurant is quite varied, but there is not much choice for children. You can eat on the menu, but for this it is better to choose other restaurants, since here they serve an order for a very long time. But at the same time, everything is very tasty, especially desserts. Sitting on the open veranda, parents can watch children playing outside. In the evening, a truly romantic atmosphere reigns here. Live music is playing, the moon is shining, a barely audible noise of the sea surf is heard.

Entertainment





Those who do not like to spend time with their children should not go here. There are few children's entertainments, but they are still there. On both sides of the embankment are inflatable trampolines with labyrinths. For 150 rubles you can stay unlimited time. Near the pool of the Hotel Ellada there is a playground.

Animation is sometimes carried out, but the presentation, say, by an amateur. Near the pool, both children and adults can attend exercises, water aerobics and water polo classes.





Evenings can be spent walking along the promenade. It is also worth visiting the picturesque park of the reconstructed pension "Semidvorye", located nearby. Sitting on the balcony in your hotel room, you can listen all evening as the sea waves rush to the shore. During these moments of complete relaxation, a person is able to forgive all the shortcomings that the Hellada Hotel (Alushta) can upset. Photos cannot convey the whole atmosphere of calm and tranquility that reigns here. In this place you can really relax and soul and body.
