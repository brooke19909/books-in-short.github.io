How to make autumn crafts in kindergarten?

Probably, every person at least once made crafts from natural materials for kindergarten. And many still have not forgotten that magical sensation that arises from the coolness emanating from these gifts of nature, their shape, color. And what positive energy they charge! In this article you will learn how to make autumn crafts in kindergarten with your child.

The best activity for the child is application. It is performed not only from colored paper, but also from dried herbs, leaves, flowers. To do this, take the paper and stick dry leaves of different sizes on it. Use in your composition and such natural materials as beans, lentils, seeds. A very beautiful application will turn out if you lay out charming fish or an original house from cut into pieces leaves.

From maple leaves you can make a beautiful wreath on your head or decoration on the wall, and from a mountain ash and acorns it is easy to make a necklace.



The original application is obtained from pumpkin seeds. Previously, they need to be painted in different colors with paints or gouache, and then laid out on a sketch drawn in pencil. This composition is fixed with glue.

Autumn crafts in kindergarten, in general, are made from any natural materials: acorns, cones, tree bark , chestnuts, sea ​​pebbles.

It is easiest to make a craft out of cones, because they resemble the torso or head of an animal. They can be painted in the desired color with gouache and dazzle the missing parts (pens, legs, eyes) from plasticine. From cones by simple manipulations you can get a forester, hedgehog, deer, Christmas tree.



An excellent material for crafts are chestnuts. On them, you can use a felt-tip pen to draw eyes and a mouth. Get a funny face. Chestnuts stacked one after another resemble a caterpillar. It remains only to fasten the components with the help of plasticine.

Caps separated from acorns are the material from which you can make a tea set. To do this, attach a nose to them (to make a teapot) and mold handles from plasticine to cups.

Sea pebbles can be painted with acrylics so that it becomes like ladybugs, beetles, turtles, caterpillars.

Interesting autumn crafts in kindergarten can also be made from tree bark. Some roots or pieces of tree bark are like animals, boats, houses, cars. Parts that they lack are easy to finish with a felt-tip pen (or create from plasticine).



Crafts from vegetables for kindergarten are also very easy to do. For example, from cauliflower and olives you can make a lamb. The body will be cabbage, olive - head, cloves - legs, buckwheat will act as a peephole. "Parts of the body" should be connected with toothpicks.

You can make a penguin from eggplant (it will be necessary to cut off the skin on the side so that the breast turns out). The eggplant tail will be the nose. It remains to cut out the eyes and cut off the bottom of the vegetable so that the penguin can stand.

You can make a boat out of a zucchini, giving it the appropriate shape, and a crocodile out of a cucumber, if you cut its teeth with a knife.

From a variety of fruits, bonded with wooden skewers, you get a nice little man.

Doing autumn crafts with your child in kindergarten, you can become even closer, develop the child’s creative abilities, his imagination. It's also a great way to just have fun!
