Dive Inn Resort 4 * (Egypt / Sharm El Sheikh): photos, prices and reviews of tourists from Russia

Hotel Dive Inn Resort 4 * refers to those hotels that are called "beach". The Quartet was built on the Sinai Peninsula, which is famous for the purity of sea water and the unprecedented beauty of coral. The boundless desert surrounding this oasis of comfort has created natural beaches that guests can use. And having arrived here, they will fully feel what real oriental hospitality is. It is fun and comfortable here. Of course, this is not the "five", but for a little money the hotel provides high-quality services. At least, tourists from Russia write this in their reviews and recommend this hotel to friends and strangers. Let's try to figure out what the secret is.



Hotel Location

The Dive Inn Resort 4 * hotel is very close to the various attractions of the Sharm resort. Right next to it begins a street with various shops and benches. Not far from it there are dolphin shows, the Old Market, shopping centers such as Il Mercato, Alf Leila and La Leila. These are places where you can not only buy things, products and souvenirs, but also sit in restaurants and have fun. Everything can be reached in a short time - from a quarter to thirty minutes. Therefore, most guests believe that the place where the hotel is located is very convenient. The hotel is far from the airport - at a distance of about twenty kilometers. Therefore, the transfer to the hotel can be quite lengthy.



Territory Dive Inn Resort: traveler reviews

Our compatriots write that the snow-white building of the hotel is located in a real tropical garden, in the middle of which buildings and pools with blue water are built. In addition, the hotel was created as a "village for relaxation" - that is, on its territory there is everything you need. There are shops, souvenir shops, and special boutiques selling perfumes, jewelry, clothes, beach accessories, and even an oriental bazaar. The hotel often hosts various international exhibitions. Although there is not much space here, everything is very compact. The territory is carefully cleaned, looked after flower beds and pruned trees.



Accommodation for tourists

Hotel Dive Inn Resort 4 * offers tourists standard rooms or family rooms. The views from them predominantly open to the pool or garden. The family rooms have a kitchen and a separate bedroom. The rooms are very decent, fully meet the standards of the "four", everything is clean. Everything works in the room, they regularly give soap and shampoo. Flat-screen TV, you can insert a flash. They clean every day, make different figures of towels, sprinkle the bed with flower petals, even if you do not leave a tip. The rooms are modern and comfortable electronic safes. The air conditioner is manually controlled, so you can always choose a comfortable temperature.

In the evenings they organize concerts and folklore shows: oriental dances, swallowing fire, the choice of "Miss Hotel" and so on. Dance training was organized - oriental and latin. Sea and beach

Dive Inn Resort is not on the front line. But at the same time, a regular shuttle service is organized to the sea. Yes, and go there - some two or three minutes. Those who wish can get on foot. To go - about fifteen minutes past various shops with clothes and souvenirs. True, the beach is small, and there are at least amenities. But he is in the best in Sharm Bay Om al-Sayed. There is a great reef with fish, and dozens of divers bring excursion boats and yachts to these shores daily. For children, such a beach is not very suitable, but for those who like to dive - that's it. Without a mask, a tube, and also special slippers for corals there is simply nothing to do there. If there are really not enough places, you can go to the beaches of neighboring hotels. The sea here is amazingly beautiful, and the fish swim right near the shore. Twenty meters from the hotel there is a paid beach of El Fanar. Tourists who come here not for the first time argue that it is best to walk or go to the sea in the morning and return to the hotel for dinner. And spend time by the pool. It is clean, it is cleaned constantly, and also large. It is pleasant to swim in it, and there are enough sunbeds around on the terrace.



Excursions

A lot of entertainment for tourists offers a fun resort Sharm. Dive Inn Resort 4 * for this reason in the evenings sends free minibuses to Naama Bay. This bus leaves at nine in the evening, and returns around midnight. There you can not only go to clubs and discos, but also just take a walk - this street is interesting, fun and safe. In addition, you can buy a sightseeing tour of the resort. As is often the case in the Egyptian desert, one of the best trips is an evening ATV ride to the "Bedouin village." A colorful sunset among the mountains, camel riding, dancing and delicious food cooked at the stake, skating under the light of the stars are the usual set, which, as a rule, is very popular among the participants in the entertainment. Other local attractions praise a boat trip to Tiran Island. And, of course, many Russians use their stay on the Sinai Peninsula to drive to Israel.



Prices

The cost of a room in this hotel varies in the region of one thousand six hundred rubles per room (for two). For three dollars in a hotel you can smoke a hookah. On the market in Naama Bay, you can buy delicious ten-pound freshes. Taxi from the beach to the hotel costs one dollar. To get to Naam Bay, you have to pay $ 2. Signature soup at Il Mercato costs thirty pounds. A set of masks with a tube costs four dollars. Slippers in the shops near the hotel are usually bought for $ 5, and in the old town - for 3. Our compatriots advise taking excursions at local agencies, and not from tour operators in the hotel. For example, a trip to Jerusalem at a hotel will cost one hundred and thirty dollars per person, and in Naama Bay you can buy it for sixty-five to seventy.
