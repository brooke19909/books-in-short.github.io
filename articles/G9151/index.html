Popular hotels in Salekhard

Salekhard welcomes its tourists with hospitality, as well as the presence of a decent selection of hotels and places to accommodate various categories. The tourism business is actively developing here. Salekhard hotels are always glad to new and regular customers.

Hotel "Yuribey"

Hotel "Yuribey" (Salekhard) offers 128 spacious rooms of several categories for accommodation. Judging by the reviews, each of them has an LCD TV, telephone, air conditioning, refrigerator, tea set, kettle, safe, minibar. In the bathrooms for the convenience of guests in the presence of a hairdryer, bathrobe, hat, slippers, bath accessories.



Hotel "Yulana"

The location of this Salekhard hotel is interesting (for the most part reviews about it can only be found good) - in the building of the shopping center. Near the establishment is a city park where you can relax from the hustle and bustle of the city and take a quiet walk. "Yulana" offers you 35 comfortable rooms of several categories. They have everything that is necessary for a full comfortable rest.



The rooms have refrigerators, televisions and telephones, in part there is a hairdryer. In addition, guests have a billiard room, a bathhouse with a pool. In a small cafe you can have a cup of tea or a tasty snack.

Hotel "Arctic"

Hotel "Arctic" was built 20 years ago. The services of this Salekhard hotel are certified and fully correspond to the 3-star category. Sights are nearby. Guests are offered accommodation in 1-2-bed rooms, suites and suites. The apartments are equipped with a refrigerator, work desk, safe, TV, bottled water, telephone. Each room has a private bathroom. You can spend the evening in the restaurant. For negotiations, there is a conference room for 70 people.

"Teremki"

The calm and quiet atmosphere of the mini-hotel "Teremki" makes it a great place for a peaceful, relaxing holiday, as evidenced by numerous reviews. The hotel is located in the heart of the city. This is a cozy hotel with 6 comfortable, modern and clean rooms. Each of them has a TV, necessary furniture, safe, refrigerator and other amenities.

For the convenience of guests, the mini-hotel has a bathhouse, a shared kitchen, and laundry facilities. Great guest staff will meet with responsiveness and cordiality. Having been here, the guests are coming back again. The restaurant is a couple of minutes from the city center, cafes and grocery stores are located nearby.

Hotel "Nord"

This Salekhard hotel also has a favorable location - near the Fakel restaurant and the Shaytanka river embankment, next to the museum complex of I. S. Shemanovsky and the Government of the Yamalo-Nenets Autonomous Okrug.

The maximum number of guests that the hotel can accept at a time is 22. The superior rooms have refrigerators, televisions, Internet, and room amenities, as evidenced by the reviews. In addition, guests can additionally use kitchen utensils and a kitchen, ironing services. In addition, the establishment provides a shuttle service.

Hotel Metelitsa

Hotel Metelitsa opened its doors 4 years ago and has already managed to gain the trust of people who value coziness and comfort. This small mini-hotel is 5 minutes from the city center. In addition, the airport is located just 2 kilometers from this Salekhard hotel. Judging by the reviews, the advantage of the establishment is an individual approach to the guests and the hospitality of the staff.



The hotel offers 17 comfortable rooms. Each of them is equipped with all the conditions that are necessary for fruitful work and a pleasant stay. For the convenience of guests on site there is an equipped kitchen where you can cook your favorite dishes on your own. Free parking is available.
