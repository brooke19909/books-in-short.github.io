Theaters

If your child is a restless tomboy who does not sit still for a minute, and you want to relax calmly, be sure to let him get acquainted with the acting. No kid will refuse an interesting performance in a puppet theater. Beautiful toys will quickly capture the child‚Äôs attention for at least an hour. You can visit the puppet theater at the address: Bolshaya Pokrovskaya, house 39.



Children's performances are also staged by a fairly well-known theater for children in the city. Nizhny Novgorod presents to your attention "Faith". Museums

A trip to the museum carries not only an entertaining component, but also an educational one. When a child admires fascinating exhibits, he learns a lot of new information about them, which, of course, is stored in his memory. In Nizhny Novgorod they know about the important mission of museums, so they try to make them not only informative, but also interesting. Museums of Nizhny Novgorod for children can attract the attention of adults.

Interactive Sunny City has recently opened, and has already become a favorite for both children and their parents. It is in this museum that one can carry out a number of scientific studies, check the laws of physics in practice, and just feel like a real Einstein. It is located on Chaadaev Street, in the Sokol shopping center.



Well, if your child does not like physics, take him on a tour of the art museum. For children there are special adapted programs. Experienced guides tell only the most interesting information about the paintings, without overloading them with an excessive amount of facts. They will play with your child, give him assignments and demonstrate the best examples of fine art.

If you come to Nizhny Novgorod, you can search for entertainment for children in the Exhibition Complex, which is located in the city center. It constantly operates up to fifty different exhibitions, for a variety of tastes and ages.

Butterfly House

Where to go with a child in Nizhny Novgorod? Of course the butterfly house! This is a permanent exhibition that will appeal to both girls and boys. At the exhibition you can see and get acquainted with insects from different countries, from all continents.



The organizers of the exposition will tell you in detail about the most interesting butterflies, show how they eat. Children will be able to look at the incubator with pupae, from which winged beauties appear. Their elegance will amaze the imagination of not only children but also adults. And after the tour, everyone will be able to hold a butterfly on himself, as well as get a memorable photo.

"Laser tag"

Active children will love the trendy game "Laser Tag", which came to us from abroad. This is a kind of war, only a few levels higher than those arranged by children in the yard. There is an arena specially prepared for the competition, and children are given almost a real weapon with a laser that can enthrall both a child and an adult.



The organizers of the spectacle are constantly changing military strategies to make it even more interesting. Vivid impressions guaranteed.

Parks

Each district of Nizhny Novgorod has its own beautiful parks. In most of them various attractions are actively working in the summer, in each you can drink coffee, eat ice cream or sit in a cafe. When wondering where to go with a child in Nizhny Novgorod, keep in mind that you can go on a family vacation. The park is an ideal place for this.



"Switzerland" on Gagarin Avenue is perfect for children and their parents. The park has not only various attractions, but also a zoo. So buy food for animals, which is recommended by the administration of the park, and go for a walk.

You should not be limited to only one zoo. Children love animals. And doctors believe that communication with the fauna helps boys and girls to develop and strengthens their immunity. Fortunately, there is something to see in Nizhny Novgorod. The largest zoo in the city is called "Limpopo", it has more than three hundred different animals. For children under three years old, admission is absolutely free, and from 3 to 14, you will need to pay 120 rubles for a ticket.

Toy railway

The favorite entertainment for the children of Nizhny Novgorod is the railway. Its uniqueness lies in the fact that it is not just an ordinary toy of large sizes, on which you can ride. This is really a mode of transport, only for kids. This railway has three operating stations. And its length is as much as eleven kilometers.
