Simeiz. Water Park "Blue Bay"

Where the Ai-Petri ridges go down to the sea itself, where the stone Cat is preparing for a decisive jump, and Diva proudly shows up from the deeps of the sea, Simeiz, one of the most popular resorts in Crimea, is located. Such a relief contributed to the formation of a unique climate - in the summer there is no sweltering heat in the village, the air is dry but fresh, and there is no rain for months. In winter, the air temperature is higher than in Yalta, which attracts a lot of tourists here in the off-season.



The history of the village

On the slopes of Mount Koshka, archaeologists have discovered artifacts indicating that the land on which the village is currently located was inhabited many millennia ago. But for unknown reasons, by the 18th century there was no trace of the settlement.

The arrangement of Simeiz began in 1873, when the tsarist minister of war acquired an oak grove and an abandoned vineyard in these parts. The construction of the estate was completed in record time, but at the beginning of the 20th century it was sold to the brothers of the Minister Maltsev. They decided to turn the village into a fashionable resort. Many beautiful villas and estates were built on the coast, which instantly attracted aristocrats.

Simeiz today is considered a place of elite relaxation. The village offers tourists comfortable houses and villas, excellent cafes and restaurants. And in order to make the leisure of the guests of Simeiz and Crimea even more eventful, in 2001 the construction of a modern water park was completed.

Blue bay

Every day, dozens of buses bring tourists to Simeiz. The water park readily accepts everyone from 9:30 in the morning until 18:30. "Blue Bay" is the only institution in the Crimea, in all pools and attractions of which exclusively sea water is used, the level of which is replenished daily. The water park, in fact, is a separate self-sufficient city. In addition to all kinds of slides and pools, on its territory there are cafes, pizzerias, hotels and cottages for relaxation. Pools

On the territory of the "Blue Bay" there are 6 pools, including children's ones and a unique wave pool. It provides an unforgettable opportunity to enjoy swimming in the stormy sea, fighting high waves, but without risking anything.



Children's area

For children who have not yet reached school age, a children's complex is equipped on the territory of the water park. Around the spacious pool there are four slides, which are exact, but very reduced copies of adult rides "Virage" and "Serpentine". Their heights range from 1.9 to 2.4 meters.

You can satisfy your hunger by having a quick and tasty lunch at one of four excellent cafes or pizzerias. And the Pirate bar with cocktails and cold drinks is located right in the pool!





Prices

Among many such resorts, Simeiz continues to hold the bar high. The water park, the prices for which are already quite acceptable, has developed a system of active bonuses and discounts. So, on his birthday and a week after it, a tourist can enjoy the services of a water park for free. Children, students and senior citizens enjoy 50 percent benefits. The cost of a full ticket, designed to stay in the water park from 9:30 to 18:30, is 1400 rubles, for children it costs 600. For those visitors whose permanent residence is Crimea, including Simeiz, the water park provides entrance tickets of 900 and 300 rubles respectively.

Everyone who has visited the "Blue Bay" with pleasure recalls the fun hours spent in the water park. While viewing photos, the hearts of travelers are filled with joy, and their troubled souls are waiting for the new season to return to the Crimea.
