What is the best selfie phone?

The concept of "self-portrait" has been known for more than a decade. However, this type of images has gained popularity only in recent years. The development of technology has made selfies accessible to any mobile owner. Although not every phone will cope with this difficult task.

What is a selfie?





A photograph of yourself is considered a self portrait. Due to the unattractiveness of the word, it was replaced with a selfie, which translates as "self." The first mention of such a picture appeared only in 2010 and quickly took root around the world.

A photograph taken on an outstretched arm or in a mirror is considered a selfie. This method made a real boom, and in the wake of popularity, a lot of attributes were created for lovers of self-portraits. An interesting invention was a special stick, allowing more people to get into the frame. Selfie lovers also received an Instagram resource designed to post self-portraits.

Device selection

Most people prefer using a phone for a selfie. The mobile phone is always at hand and is able to take good pictures. Although not every smartphone can cope with the task.

The emphasis in the device should go to the cameras, and both. The quality of the selfie depends on the matrix of the device and its resolution. Also, the phone must have autofocus, the presence of such a function is desirable for the front-end. Finding a great selfie phone is not easy. The buyer will have to carefully study many models of different companies.
