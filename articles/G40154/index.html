Faliraki Vista. Faliraki Vista (Rhodes): reviews

On the legendary island of Rhodes, washed by the waters of the Mediterranean and Aegean Seas and separated by 18 kilometers from Turkey, the cozy three-star Faliraki Vista Hotel welcomes guests in the resort area of ​​Filiraki. The appearance of Rhodes was created by the Greeks, Romans, Byzantines, Italians and Ottomans.

Once he was patronized by a gigantic statue of the Colossus of Rhodes - the sixth wonder of the world. The giant building has long been resting reliably hidden in the depths of the sea, and the legend about it blows the island with a halo of mystery, attracting tourists to a mysterious place with marvelous landscapes. Small hotel Faliraki Vista 3 (Rhodes) allows you to plunge into the mythical atmosphere of Greece and enjoy a wonderful vacation.

Location





The resort town of Filiraki and the capital of the island of Rhodes are separated by 12 kilometers, which travelers overcome in 25 minutes by bus or car rental. Hotel Faliraki Vista is located in a quiet quarter, located on one of the outskirts of Filiraki. Diagoras Airport is 15 kilometers away.

The hotel is adjacent to other hotels, lively bars and restaurants. Tavernas, clubs and restaurants are crowded on the main street of Filiraki, which is about 15 minutes away from the hotel. At a slight distance from the hotel there are a couple of decent supermarkets (only 50 meters to the store with essential goods).

Infrastructure

The hotel consists of two buildings. In a one-story building there is a reception and lobby with a lobby bar and free Wi-Fi, in a two-story building there are apartments for guests. The lobby bar, open around the clock, offers gourmet Greek dishes at a reasonable cost.

The hotel does not have its own territory, there is no pool. Guests enjoy free pools at neighboring hotel complexes 100 meters away. Between the buildings there is a small garden and free parking for cars rented by tourists.

Apartments

The family -run Faliraki Vista Hotel provides travelers with studio apartments for two and three people. The studios are equipped with a TV, a miniature kitchen with a fridge and stove, a spacious bathroom with a shower, a balcony or a terrace.





The rooms are spacious, bright, well maintained. Air conditioning is charged separately. For an additional fee, travelers have access to a safe.

Beach area

From Faliraki Vista to the public beach of Filiraki, strewn with golden sand grains, 500 meters. This beach is recognized as the best on the island of Rhodes. For the use of umbrellas, deck chairs and mattresses, you must pay. The hotel and the beach are separated by a road. Crystal azure waters here are safe for swimming adults and children.

Vacationers are engaged in all imaginable water sports. Bars, restaurants and taverns are adjacent to the beach. Very close is a magnificent water park with dizzying rides. Tourists who feel that they have received a small dose of adrenaline and vivid impressions, playing sports on the high seas, go to have fun in the water park.





Entertainment

Hotel Faliraki Vista is perfect for measured beach bliss and outdoor activities. In the afternoon, leaving a cozy hotel, tourists walk along the ancient narrow streets where many restaurants, souvenir shops, impressive shopping centers are crowded, have fun at the golf club. They drive around in rented cars, visiting the sights of Rhodes.

With the onset of dusk, vacationers plunge into the sparkling nightlife of Filiraki, lighting up at fun parties. The former fishing village, having turned into a fashionable resort, at sunset becomes an immense night club in the open air. An extravaganza of fun explodes two streets with the capacious names of Bar Street and Club Street.

Family tourists also do not sit all day at the Faliraki Vista 3 hotel, guest reviews are confirmation of this. They go to children's towns, where they have fun on water slides and attractions.

Tourists reviews

For a calm and unpretentious vacation , the Faliraki Vista Hotel (Rhodes) is designed. The reviews of tourists living in it directly indicate that this hotel is a wonderful refuge where you can get a good night's sleep, plunged into Morpheus' arms, put yourself in order and have a meal with your favorite food prepared in the kitchenette.





Have fun, sunbathing and swimming in the city of Filiraki. However, this does not bother travelers, because all the institutions of a tiny resort are within walking distance. And for tourists who have rented a car, this state of affairs is not a problem at all. A few minutes drive, and they are already in that cozy place that gives them real pleasure. Parking at the hotel is large, it easily accommodates all rented cars.

The miniature kitchen is equipped with everything necessary for cooking (there is even a Turk). Spacious balconies have a table with chairs. Here you can enjoy a meal in the fresh air. Guests, as a rule, have breakfast in the morning in the hotel, spend a couple of hours on the beach, and then go to have fun and actively relax in the busy resort areas.
