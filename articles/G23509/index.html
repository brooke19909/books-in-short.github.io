Spa hotels in Sochi: list, top rated, addresses, rooms and spa services

If you want an unforgettable vacation, pay attention to the rating of the best spa hotels in Sochi. This format of establishments implies the provision of body care services and harmonization of the inner world. From vacation you will return updated outwardly and pacified.

First place: Hotel Ostrov

The Ostrov Spa Hotel in Sochi is located at 10 Pirogova Street, a ten-minute drive from the city center. The concept of the institution is an archipelago that consists of four islands: a boutique restaurant, a buffet restaurant, a grill bar and a spa. The institution is rightfully considered one of the best on the Black Sea coast.

The hotel offers 49 accommodation options. Namely:

  • standard - from 5 500 rubles;
  • studio - from 6 100 rubles;
  • luxury - from 13 800 rubles;
  • cottages - from 14,600 rubles;
  • villas - from 48 500 rubles.

The territory has a modern spa center, which includes the following units:

  • thermal complex;
  • gym;
  • heated pool;
  • phyto bar;
  • aqua bar;
  • tanning studio;
  • beauty parlor;
  • massage room;
  • spa boutique.

Reviews about Hotel Ostrov

About the hotel in question, travelers leave a lot of positive feedback:

  • a large and beautiful park with a huge number of green spaces;
  • good modern repair and new high-quality furniture;
  • An excellent spa with a lot of treatments and excellent specialists;
  • comfortable orthopedic beds with perfectly clean linens;
  • Friendly and qualified staff.

But not without negativity:

  • a small cramped dining room, where you can not always find free space;
  • not enough sun loungers to relax by the pool (the problem is especially acute in season);
  • despite the fact that the hotel is non-smoking, on the street and on the premises you have to inhale tobacco smoke every now and then.
