A bit of history

Hand knitting is an ageless type of needlework. Since ancient times, Arabs were considered the most skilled knitting masters. They came up with complex patterns that consisted of multi-colored threads. In the 12th century, Italians and Spaniards fell in love with knitting. In the XIII century - the British and Scots. Over time, knitting fashion came to Russia.

Nowadays, knitting for many has become a wonderful vacation and a kind of meditation. Thanks to such work, many worries or worries go by the wayside. Monotonous work, which results in a beautiful pattern and a solid product, soothes and tunes in a peaceful way.

Knitting pattern "fan"

By knitting with a simple change of the back and front loops, you can get original patterns. Each of them is original and beautiful in its own way. There are a huge number of drawings created by needlewomen for all the time that there is such a kind of applied art. Pigtail, voluminous butterflies, spring motifs, dancing braids - these and many other patterns are used for knitting sweaters, dresses, etc.

Consider the universal method of knitting, which was called the "Fan". He is very gentle and refined. Knitted with a knitting pattern "Fan", suitable for making a shawl, a light sweater or cardigan, airy dress for a girl or a costume for a baby.

The pattern report is as follows (17 loops + 2 edge):

  • The first row - only the wrong loops.
  • The second row and subsequent even rows will also be just the wrong loops.
  • The third row is knitted according to the scheme: 2 loops with a front with a slope to the left - 3 times; further yarn and 1 front - 5 times; again yarn, 2 loops with a front slope to the right - 3 times.
  • Repeat the second row.
  • The fifth row is knitted according to the scheme: 2 loops with a front with an inclination to the left; 5 facial; knit from one loop 2 - first 1 front of the broach, 1 front of the loop; 1 front, from one loop knit 2 - 1 front from the loop, 1 front from the broach; 5 facial; 2 loops with a front with an inclination to the right.
  • Repeat the second row.
  • Seventh row according to the scheme: 2 loops with a front with an inclination to the left; 4 front; from one loop knit 2 - 1 front of the loop, 1 front of the broach; 3 facial; from one loop knit 2 - 1 front of the broach, 1 front of the loop; 4 facial; 2 loops with a front with an inclination to the right.
  • Repeat the second row.
  • The ninth row according to the scheme: 5 loops with the front, but first thing we transfer the 3rd loop on the left needle to the first place; double crochet, 1 front - 7 times; 5 loops from the front, but the 3rd loop is again in place of the first.
  • Repeat the second row.
  • According to the scheme of the fifth row.
  • Repeat the second row.
  • Knit according to the scheme of the seventh row.
  • Repeat the second row.

Repeat from the first row.

If you follow this pattern, you can knit a fan pattern with knitting needles, which is suitable for making a shawl, a light poncho, etc.
