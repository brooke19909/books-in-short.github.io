Hotel Canareef Resort Maldives 4 * (Maldives / Addu Atoll): overview, description, rooms and reviews

Palm trees, white sand, amazingly clear ocean water - many people dream that such a landscape surrounded them on vacation. And all this can be obtained if you go to one of the Maldives. Just to start, you need to decide on the hotel. There are a lot of them, and all are good in their own way. But with special attention I would like to note Canareef Resort Maldives 4 *.





Location

This modern complex is located on the largest island in Maldives. It is known as Addu Atoll. The island is located at the very end of the archipelago. The atoll extends over 4.5 kilometers, which by the Maldivian standards is enormous.

It includes the island of Midhu. Here is the Canareef Resort Maldives 4 *. Midhu is widely known as the island of farmers - industrial production is not developed here, and liners and tankers do not pass by. Yes, and the tourism sector began to develop in the mid-2000s. So the main local attractions are unspoiled nature and environmentally friendly products, which local farmers love to grow and produce.

Service and Facilities

The Canareef Resort Maldives 4 * creates ideal conditions for a comfortable stay for guests. There is free Wi-Fi, a room for storing luggage and things, a tour desk and even a separate currency exchange. If guests come with children, they can provide a nanny.

There is also a laundry and dry cleaning (they can still iron clothes), a small office with a photocopier and fax machine, a modern conference room, and several other shops on the territory, among which there is one with souvenirs.

In addition to the above, guests can use the shuttle to / from the capital's airport. Before him - about 300 miles. The hotel organizes a flight for visitors from Male to Midhu.

There is also a service for the delivery of food and drinks, cooking lunch on the road, and even amenities for tourists with disabilities. The staff, by the way, speaks seven different languages. Unfortunately, there is no Russian in the list, but you can communicate with the employees of the complex in English, German, Indonesian, Hindi, Dutch, Filipino and even Chinese.





For leisure

In addition to a private beach, Canareef Resort Maldives 4 * has many other attractions. There is a swimming pool and a sun terrace on site, which regularly hosts entertainment shows. For children there is a separate playground and a mini club.

Being in the Maldives, you can not try yourself in water sports. After all, here is the richest ocean world! At least diving is worth a try. The hotel provides all the necessary equipment for this. Here you can snorkel, enjoy scuba diving, canoeing or take time for windsurfing. Here, even fishing is organized.

In addition to the above, there is also a bicycle rental, a library, karaoke, darts, a tennis court, a billiard room and a gym with exercise machines.

SPA

Canareef Resort Maldives (Meedhoo, Maldives), like any other modern hotel complex, has its own wellness center. At the local SPA, guests are offered a large list of different treatments. Moreover, both wellness and anti-aging. It offers exotic scrubs, aromatherapy, relaxation, Ayurvedic treatments, various massages (relaxing, sports, for muscles, oil, honey, hot stones) and much more. Rooms for tinning couples, by the way, are also available.





Bars and Restaurants

On the territory of Canareef Resort Maldives, in the city (Maldives is a chain of 20 atolls, on one of which it is located) Meedhoo, there are several excellent establishments where you can enjoy delicious and varied food.

Kilhi Restaurant serves international cuisine. It serves breakfast, lunch and dinner. Dhoni Sea Food Coffee Shop offers deliciously prepared seafood and light snacks. And Meera Bar will appeal to lovers of Asian cuisine.

Guests are advised to order the all-inclusive service. Because in this case, alcohol will be free. And he is very expensive here. In general, the food here is very good. Fish, meat, side dishes, salads, fruits, hot breakfast dishes - everything is there, so you won‚Äôt be able to stay hungry.





Villa Sunrise

Now you can talk about the rooms. Here guests are accommodated in separate villas. And the most budget option is those that are called Sunrise. Their area is 76 square meters. m. The villa is in a very good location, with its wooden furnished terrace offers a wonderful view. In addition, you can watch the dawn from here.

Inside, everything is decorated in a cozy style. Everything is furnished with modern wooden furniture and good appliances. There is a telephone, plasma with satellite channels, iron and ironing facilities, a fan and air conditioning. There is also a separate seating area with a pull-out sofa. In the bedroom, by the way, there is a huge 2-king size bed. There is also a refrigerator, dressing room and electric kettle.

Naturally, the villa also has a bathroom. It is equipped with a shower, bidet, toilet and hairdryer. Slippers, linen, towels, bathrobes and individual cosmetic sets are designed for each guest.

Guest Comments

A fairly popular hotel is Canareef Resort (Maldives). The description and photos of this complex inspire many people to visit it. To finally make sure that the hotel is really worthy of attention, you should turn to the reviews left by tourists.

The only minus that they note is that it is far to get there. But it's worth it. Indeed, upon arrival awaits white sand, tropical vegetation and numerous palm trees.

Guests are advised to go swimming on the beach from the ocean, rather than the lagoon. There is a good, sandy bottom without vegetation. But there are corals of amazing beauty. And a huge number of bright tropical fish that can be seen even from the shore. And if you wear a mask, you can get an unforgettable experience.

If you pay attention to the reviews left about the Canareef Resort Maldives 4 * hotel, you will notice that many are advised to check into the villa from the ocean. It is not only close to a good beach. It is also very quiet there - privacy is guaranteed. By the way, the villas have unusual bathrooms. Outside, they do not close, they are partially under the roof. But from prying eyes, the bathrooms are tightly enclosed by a wall. Many guests are curious to take a shower while admiring the palm trees, the sun and the blue sky.

More people pay attention to the staff. The hotel has great workers. Everyone is smiling, trying to help than they can. For the convenience of guests, even electric cars go around the hotel, which take them anywhere. The maids are cleaned twice a day, and each time they bring purified water to the room. Cosmetics are also regularly replenished. And this is a shower gel, shampoos and hair balms, body lotions, dental, sewing and shaving kits, a shower cap, etc.

Summarizing, we can say with confidence: this is an ideal hotel complex for those people who dream of a secluded vacation in a quiet, unsafe place. Many tourists who have been here confirm this. If you want to make sure, you should go here on vacation. Surely you will not have to feel sorry.
