Hotel Cape Dara Resort 5 * (Pattaya, Thailand): photos and reviews of tourists

Pattaya resort, located on the shore of the Gulf of Thailand, is very popular with Russian tourists. Vacationers come here not only to have a good rest by the sea, but also for all kinds of entertainments. Pattaya differs from other resorts in Thailand primarily in terms of economy. Accommodation here is slightly cheaper than in the same Phuket or Samui. Very inexpensive, for example, in Pattaya, you can rent a room in the Cape Dara Resort 5 * hotel . Reviews of tourists this hotel deserved just excellent. The rooms here are comfortable, and the infrastructure is well developed.

Where is the hotel located

This hotel is located in the north of Pattaya, about a kilometer from its center. Hotel guests have the opportunity to visit the popular Wongamat beach . The distance from it to the hotel is only about 200 m. There is also a private beach - Dara. However, guests here rarely swim. The fact is that the sea bottom in this place is covered with boulders and small stones.





The Cape Dara Resort 5 * hotel on the cape was built, and the views from the windows of its rooms open up simply magnificent. Once the area in which this hotel is now located was a favorite vacation spot for wealthy families. Therefore, the infrastructure here is developed just fine. Suvarnabhumi Airport is 127 km from Pattaya. All direct flights from Russia are sent here. Transport in Thailand itself is very well developed. Therefore, getting from the airport to Pattaya is not difficult. The easiest way to get to the hotel is by taxi. The cost of the trip is 1050-2000 baht. It takes about 45-60 minutes by taxi from the airport to the hotel.

Hotel beach

The hotel is located in close proximity to the coastline. If desired, guests of the Cape Dara Resort, reviews of which are good, have the opportunity to visit the beach almost on its territory. Most guests find Wongamat Beach more comfortable than a private Dara. You can reach it in just a few minutes. Entrance to the sea on this sandy beach is convenient. At low tide, the coastline here is very wide. The total length of Wongamat is one and a half kilometers. Infrastructure in this part of the coast is developed slightly worse than on the central beaches of Pattaya.
