In the urban village of Kirillovka, on the spit Peresyp, there is an excellent recreation center "Avtomobilist". It has a warm, temperate, humid climate, iodine-bromine and sodium chloride underground mineral waters, as well as unique sulfide and silt mud. Staying in this village allows you to have a good rest, restore health. The surrounding landscapes, striking in their beauty, will help everyone find spiritual harmony.



Residence

The Avtomobilist recreation center (Kirillovka) offers its guests accommodation in self-contained houses designed for 2, 3 and 4 people. In addition, there are 3 buildings with the numbers "lux", "junior suite", "economy" and block. At the same time 200 people can rest here. The luxury rooms have a TV, air conditioning, a boiler, modern cabinet furniture, a refrigerator. There is a separate bathroom, on the bed - an orthopedic mattress. Junior suites are all the same, with the exception of the boiler. The economy rooms have a refrigerator and cabinet furniture. The bathroom is located on the territory. Independent houses have a separate entrance and a veranda. Installed refrigerator, good furniture. There is water, but only cold. Facilities on site.



Nutrition

The recreation center "Motorist" has its own dining room. Meals can be ordered individually for a fee. In addition, there is a bar on the territory of the base where you can buy barbecue, all kinds of drinks and light snacks. For vacationers there is a multi-profile buffet shop where you can buy sour-milk products, ice cream, various pastries and much more. Those who wish to cook their own barbecue can take a grill from the administrator.

Infrastructure

The Avtomobilist recreation center has a well-developed infrastructure, so vacationers will not be bored here. Those who arrive in their own car will not have to look for parking for their vehicles, since the complex has its own free parking. For sports games, a special playground is equipped, there are board games. The administration of the base can take in use the necessary sports equipment. For families with children there is a well-equipped playground.



Additional Information

Check-in at the Avtomobilist recreation center takes place at noon. On the last day of your stay, you must leave the room before 10.00. The ticket price includes bus travel from the complex to the station, which leaves from the base at 19.00. For all, health insurance is required. Children under 12 years old get a discount. For a child up to 4 years old, the fare and the cost of utilities are paid without a seat.

It should be noted that in the Odessa region there is another, also called recreation center "Avtomobilist" (Zatoka). It is also very popular among tourists, not only in Ukraine, but also in Russia. Therefore, when purchasing a ticket you need to be very careful, since the last base is located on the Black Sea coast.
