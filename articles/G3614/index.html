Hotel Teplo

Considering inexpensive hotels (Vladivostok), it is worth highlighting the Teplo hotel. It is located in the city center. It consists of 2 parts. One is a hotel, the other is a hostel. Here, six-bed rooms for women and men and double rooms are available to guests. All rooms are cozy, clean, equipped with quality and comfortable furniture.



The friendly and attentive hotel staff will be happy to answer any questions from guests, tell you about the sights of the city that you should see. Guests can use the shared kitchen. The institution also has a dedicated relaxation area. For guests free internet, coffee, tea, laundry facilities are available. The main principles of the institution are cosiness, comfort, safety and reliability of guests' relaxation.

Cost: from 700 rubles per day.
