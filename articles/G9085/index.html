Fruit





Let's start with the fruit. Now we will make an orange. For this we need orange plasticine. Tear off a small piece, form a ball. To make the product more similar to a real fruit, take a match or a pin and make notches from all sides.

Vegetables for the doll

How to mold food from plasticine? Simple, if made according to the instructions. So, now we will make a cucumber. It is very easy! Of course we need a piece of green plasticine. Knead it, then give the shape of a cucumber. For greater realism, use a match to make a texture.

If you want to make a tomato, then you need a small piece of red plasticine. Mash it well, then give it a ball shape. Then take a piece of green. From it we will make a leaf. Mash the plasticine, give it the shape you like and attach it to the "tomato". Everything, you can put vegetables on plates!

Italian pizza

How to mold plasticine food for dolls? Now let's talk about the example of Italian pizza. To make such a dish, we need clay of different colors (beige, white, red and burgundy). First, take a piece of beige, knead it. Roll out, give it the shape of a pizza (round). It will be a dough. Next, take a red slice, roll out. Then put on top of the "test". Then take a white slice, roll it out. Then put on top of the red. From the burgundy we form a sausage, which we cut into circles. We spread them on top of a white "mayonnaise." So our pizza is ready.

Barbie doll loaf

Take a piece of light brown plasticine, knead it well. Then form an oval, on which, for greater realism, make cuts with a plastic knife.

Omelette

How to mold food, in particular scrambled eggs, from plasticine? We will need white and yellow plasticine. Tear off a piece of white, roll it. Then take a little yellow, form a ball. Then we attach it to a white piece and press on top.

Spaghetti with doll sauce

Now we will tell you how to make spaghetti. We will need several pieces of plasticine (white, blue and burgundy). First, take the blue, roll out, then make a plate. After which we take a white slice, make thin, long little spaghetti out of it. We then put them on a plate. To finish this dish, we need to make a sauce. To do this, we need a small piece of burgundy plasticine. Roll it out and place the spaghetti on top. That's all, the dish is ready!

We have already examined several options for how to mold food from plasticine. Now we will tell one more. We will make a pack of juice with a straw. To make it, we need a small piece of red plasticine and a small piece of orange. From the first we make a rectangle. It will be the pack itself. We make a tube of orange plasticine, bend it a little.
