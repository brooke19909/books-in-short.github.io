Majesty Club Kemer Beach 5 HV - rest with taste

Description: The modern Majesty Club Kemer Beach 5 HV hotel was created in 1987, renovated in 2007, and is located near the village of Goynuk. The total area occupied by the hotel is 130 square meters. m. The airport of Antalya is forty-eight kilometers, to the resort towns only six kilometers. Tourists will appreciate the sandy beach, free sun loungers, umbrellas, towels.


Rooms: Small but cozy rooms are located in two-story cottages. Of the 403 rooms, 375 are standard, for 2-3 people; 26 family, for 3-4 people. All have a balcony, shower, air conditioning, television with Russian TV, telephone, hairdryer. For a fee: mini-bar, laundry, safe. You can put a crib in the room. Three times a week there is a change of linen, a thorough cleaning is carried out daily. If you need anything, you can contact the administrator.

Beach: Tourists will be able to relax on the beach, all amenities are offered free of charge to hotel guests. Here you will find comfortable sun loungers, umbrellas, towels.

Meals: The Majesty Club Kemer Beach 5 HV has an all-inclusive concept, and a buffet is served for breakfast, lunch and dinner. There is also a delicious children's menu. Free of charge during the day local drinks, both soft and alcoholic; national snacks, fruits. By signing up, you can dine at the fish restaurant. At Hotel Majesty Club Kemer Beach you can get everything you need for a good stay. You will be pleased with the helpful character and excellent service. You can have coffee on the terrace and enjoy the magnificent scenery while admiring the flowers, garden, trees.

Information for holidaymakers: The Majesty Club Kemer Beach 5 HV also has a cinema, a shopping center, several restaurants and bars, a swimming pool, two water slides. Laundry is available in the laundry room. Beauty salon, parking and car rental - everything that is necessary for the hotel clients is provided.





Additional services can be paid using Visa, Master / Euro cards. If you feel like a doctor will come to your aid. A wide range of entertainment programs for clients of Majesty Club Kemer Beach 5 HV, including for an additional fee (services such as swimming pool, sauna, massage, solarium and others).

You can use the services of a good nanny at any time. On the playground, in the water park and in the mini-club for children of different ages, you will find many interesting activities.

A variety of sports games and entertainment for every taste: jet skis, darts, parachute, archery, fitness and much more.


Reviews: Clients of the hotel report that it is located near the airport. Quite decent food, an abundance of fruits, different types of delicious fish and meat. Large, well-groomed hotel area, an abundance of greenery, trees, flowers. A spacious beach, but the descent paths to the sea are not very convenient. There is a pier. Excellent animation: entertainment programs, discos, attentive and friendly staff. Simple, but comfortable and always clean rooms (although the plumbing condition causes a number of complaints). Car parking is free. The surrounding area offers a pleasant walk. Majesty Club Kemer Beach 5 HV reviews has the most positive. So if you do not know where to relax, go here. Great service will delight every guest. For children there is a huge variety of entertainment. You will find a great vacation here. The friendly staff will welcome guests with all honors.
