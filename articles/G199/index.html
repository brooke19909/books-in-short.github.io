Hotel Benitses Bay View Hotel 3 * (Corfu island, Greece): description, photos and reviews

Tourists coming to Greece often choose the island of Corfu as a place to relax. He is loved for its beautiful nature, the sea, clean beaches and a large number of historical attractions. If you want to save on accommodation, then pay your attention to Corfu Benitses Bay View Hotel 3 *. What services does this budget hotel offer?

General information and location

It is worth knowing that this hotel is remote from the city. It is located in the picturesque village of Gasturi , which will delight tourists with an abundant amount of green trees and beautiful mountain scenery. Very close from here is the famous Achilleion Palace , which was built in the 19th century for the Austrian Empress Elizabeth. From Benitses Bay View Hotel 3 * (Corfu Island) it is only 500 meters away. The nearest major city is 8 km away from the village. You can get to it by taxi or bus. By the way, the public transport stop in Gasturi is located 400 meters from the hotel. Corfu International Airport is 7 km away, while the nearest seaport is 3 km away.





This complex is designed for tourists who prefer sightseeing holidays, so it can not boast of proximity to the sea. To reach the nearest beach, you will need to cover a distance of 1 km. The hotel has been open all year since 1981. On a vast green area is a three-story building and a large swimming pool. In total, the Benitses Bay View Hotel 3 * hotel has 71 rooms. It is forbidden to check in with pets. Children are not allowed an extra bed. The staff at the complex speaks Greek, English and German.

More about room stock

The housing stock of the hotel has 71 standard rooms. They are perfect for placing a small family in them. The apartment consists of a bedroom and a bathroom. Ceramic tiles are laid on the floor. In summer, guests are provided with individual air conditioning. In winter, on the contrary, heating is turned on in the whole building. The complex has non-smoking rooms. Bathrooms in Benitses Bay View Hotel 3 * are equipped with a shower cabin. Towels and a hairdryer are also provided. All rooms have a private balcony with views of the surrounding area and the pool. Hotel employees clean the living rooms daily. Bed linen and towels are subject to regular change.





We also list the basic amenities that are in all rooms of the hotel:

  • TV with satellite television;
  • wireless Internet;
  • mini-bar, its filling is provided for a fee;
  • Greek radio
  • direct dial telephone;
  • delivery of drinks, breakfasts and newspapers in the room.

Food concept

The cost of living in Benitses Bay View Hotel 3 *, as a rule, includes only breakfast or half board. The morning meal consists of a standard set of dishes, which includes scrambled eggs, fried sausages, yogurt, granola and pastries. For dinner, Greek salad, feta cheese, fresh vegetables, fruits, pasta and hot dishes are served. Meals are served in the hotel restaurant. A buffet is served for tourists, although the number of dishes is strictly limited. Of drinks, there is always tea, instant coffee, juices and drinking water. There is also a bar in the hotel where tourists can buy local and imported alcohol, soft drinks, ice cream and snacks for an additional fee .





Service, services and entertainment

The infrastructure here is fully focused on tourists who come to rest on the island of Corfu (Corfu). Benitses Bay View Hotel 3 * offers guests to use the following entertainment facilities, services and facilities:

  • The phone, which is located at the reception. At your request, the hotel administrator can send a fax or copy the necessary documents.
  • Wireless wifi working in all public areas of the complex.
  • Car parking. Guests can use it for free at any time. Car rental works.
  • 24 hour reception. Registration of vacationers starts here from 9:00.
  • Transfer to the beach and back. Every day, according to the schedule of tourists, a passenger car takes and brings to the beach.
  • Luggage storage for valuables and luggage.
  • Currency exchange at the reception.
  • Laundry.
  • Holding festive events in the hotel restaurant. Organization of weddings, birthdays, anniversaries, corporate parties.
  • Swimming pool. Located near the main building and works only in fine weather.
  • Paid bike rental.




Positive reviews

Tourists vacationing here leave different reviews about the Benitses Bay View Hotel 3 * complex. Some of them thought that rest here was appropriate for the price, while others decided never to stay at this hotel again. According to guests, this hotel has the following advantages:

  • Not the most varied buffet, but everything is prepared very tasty.
  • The room has excellent air conditioning. Plumbing is also in good condition.
  • Very beautiful and tidy area around the hotel. From the window you can see the forest and mountains.
  • Pleasant staff who always behave friendly and polite. The host is always happy to chat with tourists.




Negative reviews

Part of the tourists who had a rest in Benitses Bay View Hotel 3 * (Corfu), reviews are negative. In them, they indicate the following service deficiencies:

  • You need to go uphill to the sea. The distance to the coast is very large, and the shuttle runs rarely. In addition, sometimes he may not come at all.
  • The rooms are cramped and the furniture is old. The rooms smell of damp.
  • Sometimes for several days there is no hot water. Administrators do not comment on this problem.
  • Tasty drinks at breakfast. Drinking water tastes like tap water.
  • The water in the pool smells of sewage, so no one bathes in it.

This hotel is ideal for budget travelers who prefer to travel by car. But older people or families with children should choose another hotel, which will be closer to the beach and other infrastructure.
