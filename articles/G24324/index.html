Italian ski resort of Canazei in the Dolomites

The Italian ski resort of Canazei is still not very well-known by the Russians. Nevertheless, this is one of the inexpensive and popular places for skiing. The resort is located in the Dolomites. It is part of the extensive ski region of Val di Fassa. It also has common pistes with another ski resort, Campitello. Canazei is considered the largest and most beautiful ski area in the Dolomites. After all, these mountains are famous for their special picturesque and steep slopes. And in the summer and in the winter there are countless tourists.



Canazei ski resort: how to get there?

The closest Italian airport to these places is in the city of Bolzano. If you rent a car, as many travelers do, you will be there in less than an hour. The airport to the Canazei ski resort is only 44 kilometers away. You need to leave north of the city, and then follow the SS 241. If you come by public transport, it is best to go to Canazei from the airport of the Austrian city ​​of Innsbruck. A direct bus runs from there to the resort. In high season, Canazei can be reached with the "ski express", which departs on weekends from the airports of Treviso, Venice, Bergamo and Verona to the Val di Fassa pistes. On the way, he stops at other resorts, including Canazei. Therefore, it is best to come here on Saturday and Sunday.



Trails and ski areas

The Canazei ski resort is officially divided into three zones. These are Chiampak, Belvedere and Pordoi Pass. The first of them has 15 kilometers of tracks of varying difficulty - blue, black and red - as well as 6 lifts. It starts from the village of Alba di Canazei and is located at an altitude of 1.5 km.

The Belvedere area is slightly larger in size than Chiampak. The area of ​​its tracks is 17 kilometers, but it is located a whole kilometer higher. This area is served by 10 lifts, both chair and gondola. Most tracks are red, although there are a few blue ones.

The smallest ski area is Pordoi Pass. There are only five kilometers of red slopes, and 3 lifts with chairs bring tourists to them. From Canazei you can go to the Sella Ronda ski area. It is very popular with tourists. As the most difficult track of the Canazei ski resort, reviews mention the “black” descent from Chiampaka. It passes through a natural hollow between two peaks.



Prices and ski passes

The Canazei ski resort is one of the ski areas of the Val di Fassa region. It is located in its very center. Therefore, there is a ski pass throughout this valley, which gives the owner the right to use lifts on 190 kilometers of pistes. This pass can be bought at the reception of any hotel in the resort, the village of Alba, as well as in the tourist information center of Canazei.

The cost of a ski pass depends on the season, and also on how old the tourist is. For example, a skier older than 18 years old, who arrived at the Canazei ski resort in November or early December, will pay 38 euros per day. If the season is high - Christmas, New Year, Valentine's Day, then it will cost 48 Є. In other periods, the price of a ski pass is 43 euros per day. You can purchase a subscription for the entire ski season. It will cost 440 euros. Teens will pay 27-30 euros per day, depending on the season, and people over 60 - 34-39 Є. Children's subscription costs 170 euros, and for pensioners - 370. If you want to ride on the territory of all the Dolomites, then get ready to pay from 48 to 52 euros per day.



restaurants and bars

There are many different food outlets in the Canazei ski resort. Popular for lunch and dinner with tourists are "El Pael", "Pian Shyavanays", "Vine and Dine." The highest rating for bars and eateries is “Liber Augustine”, “Zhelateriya Ladin”, “Pasticeria Peter”. You can eat fast at the Pizza Shop and Wursteltand Pipotto fast foods. Some bars are located right next to the ski lifts - these are Byte Pradel, Rifugio Snow and Cristiania. Tourists often prefer cheaper restaurants located in the neighboring villages of Alba di Canazei and Peña (both located approximately one and a half kilometers above sea level). These are "El Rezole", "La Bolp" and "El Cosinat". The best local dish is Milanese schnitzel.



Hotels and après ski

And what to do when the evening comes, and you already have plenty of frolic on the tracks of the Canazei ski resort? Reviews of tourists are advised to take a closer look at the Eges spa center, where you can visit an excellent thalassotherapy course, take a steam bath in the sauna and swim in the pool. In Alba di Canazei there is the ice palace "Gianmaria Scola", where ice skating is practiced. The resort itself has a good movie theater, and if you miss museums, then in the nearby town of Vigo di Fassa there is a very good exhibition dedicated to Romance culture. In the Sella Ronda Valley, a ski marathon is held, where thousands of participants and spectators gather.

The resort itself and the neighboring villages have many housing options. This is not too expensive hotels for three or four stars, and apartments designed to accommodate large companies or families. In terms of amenities and value for money, tourists recommend hotels such as Azola, Lorin, Crepe Negra, Fjordalizo and Grunvald.

Ski resort Canazei (Italy): reviews

Experienced tourists value the slopes from Chiampak to Alba above all, but this is the road for true adrenaline lovers. Freeride lovers prefer Belvedere, which is located at an altitude of 2500 meters above sea level, as well as the peak of Call Rodell. Moreover, the photos of the Canazei ski resort from here are excellent. But in those places there are often avalanches, so you need to carefully monitor the weather and warnings of special services. Apre-ski and restaurants are praised by many tourists in the neighboring villages of Albé and Peña. The hotels here are very beautiful, directly integrated into the stunning mountain scenery and built in the style of Tyrolean chalets.
