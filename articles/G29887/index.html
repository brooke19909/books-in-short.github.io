The easiest way

There are several variations to knit the tracks with knitting needles. Step-by-step instructions are signed in several rows. So:

  1. Type 7 stitches on the knitting needles.
  2. Tie a strip of 7 loops with the front or stocking stitch on the length of the foot + 2 centimeters + heel height.
  3. At the edge of the strip, dial the required number of loops so that the canvas is even. The heel height is still free.
  4. Loops along the edge of the trace strip, 1 decrease, 7 loops (middle), 1 decrease, loops along the edge of the trace strip. Reduction must be performed in each row - both the front and the back. Also, at the end of each row for a double loop, you need to grab the loops along the edge of the tail, left for the heel. In the process of knitting, the heel will be formed, and it will not need to be sewn.
  5. When the height of the track becomes sufficient, close the loop.

You can knit with any stitch, pattern, jacquard. But best of all, such footprints sit on the leg, if they are connected with a regular 1x1 elastic band. This was the first way to knit footprints with men. Step-by-step instructions for this method of work are the same for all sizes, it is universal.





Wake fast

There are situations in life when you need to do something very quickly. How to knit the tracks with two knitting needles? Step-by-step instructions will help you do this job very quickly.

  1. Stitch so many loops onto the knitting needles that the length of the gathered edge is slightly less than the length of the foot.
  2. In the first row, loops are divided into two knitting needles - knit the first, remove the second, leaving the thread in front of the loop to be removed. So knit all the loops.
  3. In the back row, the loops that were removed are already knitted, and the knit in the previous row is removed, leaving the working thread between the two loops.

Traditions to help

There is another interesting way to knit the tracks with knitting needles. Step-by-step instruction:

  1. On the four needles, dial the required number of loops (you need to start from the length of the ankle girth).
  2. Tie 5-7 rows with a 1x1 elastic band.
  3. Then knit the heel on three knitting needles: remove the loop, knit the next one, knit all the loops on the reverse row. In this way, continue knitting until the required heel height is reached.
  4. Next, divide the heel into three parts, knit the middle, at the end of each row, grabbing the first loops of the left side parts. So the heel will be formed.
  5. Gather loops along the edge of the heel, if necessary, making decreases on the toe.
  6. Knit the foot with any viscous - elastic, braids, stitch - on the length of the foot.
  7. Make a sock, knitting on the sides of three loops together front.
  8. Break the thread, stretch through the remaining loops, tighten and remove to the wrong side and fasten.
