Sunny Days El Palacio Resort 5, Hurghada

Description: Sunny Days El Palacio Resort 5 - comfortable hotel occupying a small territory on the Red Sea coast. Directly on the territory is an artificial lagoon with blue water. The entire territory is covered with lush tropical greenery. Hurghada Airport is 10 km from the hotel, and the Old Town is 2 km away. For those who love nightlife, 3 kilometers from the hotel is the central area of ‚Äã‚ÄãHurghada, where a huge number of nightclubs, cafes and restaurants are concentrated.





The hotel fund has on its balance sheet one 3-storey main building and two 3-storey auxiliary buildings. The first holiday reception was held in 2002. A small partial repair is done annually.

Rooms: For guests Sunny Days El Palacio Resort 5 offers 822 comfortable rooms of various categories. All have air conditioning. There is a telephone, cable TV, a small safe. There is a minibar, but it is empty and replenished only for an additional payment. Bathrooms are fitted with a warm shower, a hairdryer, a bathrobe and toiletries for the entire stay. The floor has a ceramic coating. Executive Suites have a separate bedroom and 2 balconies. Every day cleaning is done, so the rooms are always fresh and clean.





Nutrition: To your vacationers Sunny Days El Palacio Resort 5 offers all-inclusive meals. The main restaurant is Miret (international cuisine). Aldante is also popular, serving Italian cuisine. For lovers of seafood, it is recommended to visit the restaurant "Orca Fish Restaurant". There is a lobby bar. The terrace also has a bar, three beach bars and one by the pool. Ice cream, fresh juices.

Beach: Sunny Days El Palacio Resort 5 has its own sandy and pebble beach. There is a pier, corals. When swimming, it is recommended to use special shoes so as not to injure your legs.

Tourist info: For holidaymakers on Sunny Days El Palacio Resort There is an outdoor pool (without heating). For those who have come not only to relax, but also to work, there is a conference room, the possible capacity of which is 150 people. Among the paid services are an excellent laundry service, dry cleaning, hairdresser. A doctor who works on call also offers his services. There is a currency exchange office in the lobby of the main building. If necessary, you can order a taxi at the reception.

The hotel has many different shops, a sauna. A qualified masseur works. There is a disco, various show programs are held. Among other entertainments during rest it is impossible not to recall camel riding.

The hotel also took care of sports lovers. A gym is equipped for them. In addition, you can do aerobics, play volleyball on the beach. You can still have fun playing darts, or try your skills by playing a game of billiards, squash, windsurfing. There is a diving center.





Reviews: The first thing to note is the most beautiful area on which Sunny Days El Palacio Resort 5 is located. Reviews show that, despite the fact that it is small, everything fits compactly here. Throughout the day, there is an active movement, music plays. The atmosphere of the holiday is created.

The rooms are very modest, but this does not prevent them from being comfortable and cozy.

The cuisine in the restaurant is very tasty and varied. There are many bars, which makes it possible to choose a place for evening entertainment.

As in all hotels, the staff is polite and attentive to their guests.
