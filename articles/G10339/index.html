Where to watch a movie in Ulyanovsk? Cinemas of the city: a brief overview

For a long time, the cinema has remained one of the places where both adults and children like to go. The citizens of Ulyanovsk are no exception. That is why the city has 15 cinemas daily visited by thousands of people. Consider the best of Ulyanovsk's cinemas that you can go to once in this city.



"Cinema Park"

One of the best and most advanced cinemas in Ulyanovsk is Cinema Park, which is located in the building of the Aquamall shopping and entertainment center.



It consists of nine halls, equipped with comfortable chairs and able to simultaneously accommodate 1486 people.

In this cinema, in one of the halls, instead of classic armchairs, colored soft ottomans are provided for guests to sit on as they like. The latest technology meets the established modern screens and sound equipment. This cinema broadcasts 2D and 3D films.

Cinema Park Cinema has a bar and a separate cafe where guests can have a meal before or after the session.

Cinema "Cinema Park" is located at the address: Ulyanovsk, Moscow highway, 108, which is located in the Zasviyazhsky district of the city.

You can visit the cinema every day from 10 am to 00:30 (Monday to Wednesday) and until 01:30 (Thursday to Sunday).



"Moon"

The cinema "Luna" in Ulyanovsk is also one of the favorite places to spend time with citizens and is also located in the Zasviyazhsky district of the city.

Modern huge screens installed in the halls, good sound equipment will certainly make viewing any movie novelty more enjoyable and give the maximum amount of experience. Films are broadcast in two formats - 2D and 3D.

Inside the cinema "Luna" (Ulyanovsk) is decorated quite brightly and modernly, which sets a cheerful, good tone to the mood of everyone who falls into its walls. Guests can buy drinks and popcorn at the bar in the movie theater.

There is a cinema "Luna" at the address: Ulyanovsk, st. Kamyshinskaya, 43 a.



"Matrix"

One of the best movie theaters in Ulyanovsk is Matrix. You can find it on the third floor of the Pushkarevsky Ring shopping center. This cinema has been operating since 2011.

The facility is divided into 4 comfortable rooms, which, in total, can accommodate 665 people at the same time. Each of them is equipped with the latest sound and video equipment, which allows visitors to perceive the sound of the broadcast film as realistically as possible. As for digital equipment, it is Japanese (NEC) at the Matrix cinema in Ulyanovsk.

Films are broadcast in regular format and in 3D, to watch the latest here, viewers are given modern 3D Dolby glasses (for adults and for children).

The cinema has a bar and a Hollywood restaurant, where guests can spend time in a pleasant environment, on comfortable sofas in red and lilac colors, before or after watching a movie. The bar serves drinks, popcorn and several light dishes.

The cinema "Matrix" is located at the address: Ulyanovsk, Moscow highway, 91, building "A".



"Wings"

One of the cinema sites of the Ivanoff & Co company is the Wings cinema in Ulyanovsk. Residents of the outskirts of the city and the surrounding area are constantly having a good time in it, since the institution is located in the Zavolzhsky District.

This cinema is quite small - it has only one room, designed for 587 seats. Guests who come here can comfortably sit on ergonomic chairs and enjoy watching a movie on the big screen and high-quality sound.

Of the additional entertainment in the Cinema Wings, in addition to the bar, there is a separate game zone where everyone can play simulators.

It should be noted that this institution is often called differently - the cinema "Ruslan" (Ulyanovsk), which is associated with its location (recreation center of the same name).

You can find the Cinema Wings (Ruslan) at the address: Ulyanovsk, ul. 40 years of Victory, 15.



Lumiere

Cinema "Lumiere" was named after the founders of cinematography. It was opened in 2005, occupies a tiny area (compared to other cinemas in the city) and is designed for a one-time accommodation of 35 people. The hall is equipped with a 4: 1 screen and Dolby sound equipment, which is a classic of cinema halls and is present in all cinemas in Ulyanovsk

In the cinema "Lumiere" you can view not only the news of domestic and foreign cinema, but also any other film, including from a number of domestic classics - according to a collective application. Quite often companies of friends or entire families come here to spend a fun evening watching their favorite movie.

The cinema "Lumiere" is located at the address: Ulyanovsk, st. Radishchev, 148.

"7D Avatar"

"7D Avatar" is not just a movie theater, but a real movie attraction, which in a short period of its existence managed to gain popularity among city residents. It is located on the territory of the shopping and entertainment complex "Aquamall", in the Zasviyazhsky district of the city.

While watching a movie in this cinema, guests have the opportunity to feel the real drive caused by the sensations and emotions received. The cinema hall is equipped with a powerful five-channel panoramic sound, which makes the sensations from the heard as realistic as possible. As for the screen, it was created on the heavy-duty Siemens platform (Siemens) and is capable of transmitting a three-dimensional image with the effect of vertical fall, which other movie theaters of Ulyanovsk are not capable of.

You can find the cinema "7D Avatar" on the second floor of the Aquamall shopping center, at the address: 108 c. Moskovskoye Shosse, Ulyanovsk.

Opening hours: every day, from 10 a.m. to 19 p.m.
