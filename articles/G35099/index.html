Do-it-yourself heart from balloons? Easy!

Aerial design is very popular in Russia at all celebrations - from weddings and concerts to matinees in kindergartens and schools. Why? The answer is simple - balloons are bright and beautiful, from them you can make many options for festive decor, and the price of such services is low. At weddings, a heart made of balloons is often used as decorations for a banquet hall, a car of the bride and groom, their hotel room, or to fly into the sky for a photo-shoot; It looks very beautiful and can be either small or huge, traditionally red, any other or multi-colored. Is it difficult to make a heart out of balloons with your own hands, or can only aerodesign masters do it?





In fact, everyone can make this element of decor. What does it take to make a heart out of balloons with your own hands?

1. Balls of large size, from which a chain with "tails" on two sides will be made.

2. Small balls to decorate the garland jumpers.

3. Foil for decoration.

So, let's start making a heart out of balloons with our own hands.

1. We will make the basis, thanks to which our heart will not fly away. To do this, pour water in one ball, tie it and lay it with its tail up on a pre-spread sheet of packaging foil (in the center). We close the ball with foil, tying the ends. You should get a bunch, in the center of which the tail of the ball will stick out.

2. Tie a large, already inflated ball to the tail of the ball with water in the foil. We attach another inflated large ball to the tail of the previous one and so on. The last large inflated balloon is tied to the tail of the same ball of water.



3. Take 2 small inflated balls, tie them together. Do the same with two more. Now connect the centers of these two ligaments together - you will get a bunch of four small balls. The number of transitions between large balls is equal to the required number of four-folds. Attach them at the joints, twisting around large balls.

4. So, you have a chain or an oval from a chain of large and small balls. Now you need to give this design a heart shape. The junction of the two parts can be decorated with a bow made of foil or other material.

So, we made a heart out of balls with our own hands. How to put it on the wall? It's simple - use double-sided tape. If after the celebration you unstuck your heart, and there were traces of tape on the wall, they can be removed with kerosene.

To make the heart better together or three, then this design can be created in just an hour and a half coordinated work.

If you make a heart out of balloons with your own hands to launch into the sky, all the balls (or most of them) are filled with helium from a balloon. This can be done only in departments specializing in aerodesign, or in any amusement park in the warm season. In this case, the lower ball is not filled with water.



How to make a heart out of balloons? We described only one way, but there are a great many of them - you can make a wire or cardboard frame and string or stick balls on them, you can make many small hearts from oblong-shaped balls (you need a special pump to inflate them). As you can see, making a heart out of balloons to decorate the hall is not so difficult, but think about it, is it worth the risk? If it comes to preparing for such an important event as a wedding, it is better to contact a special agency.
