Weekends and holidays in the Children's Park of Simferopol

Zoo in the children's park

In the Children's Park of Simferopol there is a zoo corner. Over 300 species of various animals and birds inhabit the enclosures. There are several bears in the zoo that willingly accept treats, camels, foxes, wolves, monkeys, raccoon dogs.

In 2017, a new spacious aviary was built for the family of young lions, which its inhabitants really liked.

Tiger and cougar, buffalo and red deer, saigas and lynx - all these animals are seen by children near, not in the picture, but in the zoo. With enthusiasm, kids and pets are examined: goats and pigs, rabbits and horses. In the center of the zoo there is a pond where swans, ducks, geese and even red-eared turtles swim.

Creative and research circles

After the Children's Park was created, the Palace of Pioneers was opened on its territory - now it is the Palace of Children and Youth Creativity.

In this place, every child can find something to his liking, because there are almost all areas:

  • vocal and choreographic;
  • fine and decorative arts;
  • physical education;
  • technical and literary creativity.

Free classes are conducted by experienced teachers. Children participate in city and Russian events, contests and festivals.

Not far from the zoo there is an art studio, whose students have made a significant contribution to the improvement of the park. Children's drawings of parks and squares in Simferopol, ancient maps and animals decorated empty walls and platforms.

In the Children's Park is a youthful astronomical observatory, which created a unique museum of space minerals.

Another popular venue in the park is the Junior Inspector playground. It is here, surrounded by road signs and markings, that young traffic police inspectors are trained, and on weekends, children learn to ride on cars, scooters, scooters and bicycles according to the rules of the road.
