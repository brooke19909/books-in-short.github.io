A trip to Dubai in February: photos, recommendations and reviews

Dubai is considered the most popular and largest city in the United Arab Emirates. He earned his fame by his desire to create the best conditions on the planet for relaxation and, of course, incredible achievements in the field of technology and science. Traveling to Dubai always brings tourists a lot of positive emotions and a storm of impressions.





In this article we will try to figure out what will be the rest in Dubai in February. But note that no matter what month is chosen for a trip to this part of the UAE, there will always be classes for everyone and every taste.

Weather and temperature

Going to Dubai in February, you should know that this month (by local standards) the coldest weather is: the temperature in the daytime is 24-26 degrees Celsius with a plus sign, at night it ranges from +16 ... +18 degrees. The emirate is located on the shores of the Persian Gulf, in which the water in February warms up to +18 ... +20 ¬∞ C. If we talk about humidity, then in the last winter month it is kept at a high level. Precipitation in February practically does not fall; a couple of times a month it can rain, but for a short time, literally for thirty minutes, in the evening. As for the wind, it is almost always in Dubai.

How to spend a rest?

Despite the fact that winter weather conditions do not particularly contribute to relaxing on the beach, every traveler will find something to do on his arrival in Dubai in February .

Firstly, here you can endlessly admire incredibly beautiful buildings, most of which have no analogues in the whole world.





Secondly, Dubai has a huge number of entertainment venues: numerous water parks, restaurants, cafes and nightclubs.

Thirdly, the city will delight all shopping lovers. It is here that a huge number of shopping complexes are located that will amaze anyone with their scale.

Trips to Dubai in February

The cost of a tour to the emirate this month usually starts from $ 700 (about 40,000 rubles) per person, including flight and hotel accommodation. Towards the end of February, prices are slowly starting to rise. For comparison: the peak season begins here from mid-March, and a ticket cannot be purchased for less than $ 1,100 (63,000 rubles). Compared to February, it differs very substantial.

The rather low cost of vacation packages to Dubai in February explains the largest flow of tourists at this time. Many tourists from around the world come here to admire the architecture, relax, tasting all the delights of high-class service, and also visit the Shopping Festival.





What is remarkable for February for tourists

The main February event of the emirate is the Trade Festival, which was mentioned above. During the festival, all prices are reduced in shopping centers. Here, literally for "penny" you can buy clothes of famous brands, furs, equipment, jewelry and much more. Due to discounts in boutiques, a huge number of people, large queues gather at the box office, and it is difficult to find a free table in food courts.

Another interesting event in February is the AIME Aircraft Interiors Middle East exhibition and interior design, as well as the Dubai Tennis Open tennis tournament.

Recommendations

It is very important to know some of the nuances of those people who plan to spend their holidays in Dubai in February. Reviews of experienced travelers indicate that the city has a scorching sun, and there is a high probability of severe sunburn. Therefore, be sure to, despite the marks of the thermometer, you need to use sunblock. Even if at first glance it seems that the sun is not bright and the air temperature is not high, it is extremely necessary to use protective equipment.

Since the air here is quite dry, it is advisable to use moisturizers, otherwise the skin may begin to peel off.

Also, do not forget about the neckline and short shorts (skirts, dresses) - they can be worn only while in the hotel. When leaving the city, it is better to wear clothes that will cover your knees and shoulders.

Food and Transportation Prices

Those wishing to eat outside the hotel

The hotel complexes in Dubai have different food systems, many tourists choose the All inclusive system, eat at the hotel and do not spend money on restaurant trips, but there are also travelers who prefer an active holiday and do not want to be attached to the hotel. For this category of people in the emirate, there are many establishments - from fast foods to restaurants where you can eat tasty and satisfying.





Food prices are average. You can have a meal in fast food for $ 20 (1,140 rubles) for two, dinner in a restaurant will cost $ 40 (2,280 rubles) or more, shawarma on the street costs $ 10 (570 rubles).

The UAE is considered a clone of many countries, so here you can find an institution with any cuisine and for every taste.

Moving around the city

1. Taxi. One of the common modes of transport in Dubai is taxi. A trip "with a personal driver" for comfort fans will cost about $ 10 per half hour. If you want to go from one end of the emirate to the other, then it will cost about $ 30. Payment is made on the counter.

2. Buses. This type of transport travels mainly within several zones, the price of the trip is about 2 dirhams (a little more than 30 rubles).

3. Metro. Perhaps the fastest and cheapest way to get around the city. You get a card, the cost of which is 20 dirhams (about 310 rubles), after its activation, 14 dirhams (215 rubles) remain on the account, you can ride them. You can get to the other end of the emirate for 3 dirhams, to overcome one station for 1 dirham.



Tours in the area

There are many excursions in Dubai. You arrived in Dubai in February or another month - there will always be something to see here. Excursion packages can be purchased both from tour operators and in street agencies, many prefer to explore the city on their own in order to save money.

Approximate prices for guided tours:

  • City tour + aquarium - $ 60 (3,400 rubles).
  • Sightseeing + "Burj Khalifa" - 90 $ (5 125 rubles).
  • Sightseeing + "Garden of Miracles" - $ 80 (4,550 rubles).
  • Tour in Abu Dhabi - from $ 80.
  • Desert Safari - $ 70 (3,990 rubles).




If you buy excursions on the street, then their price will be approximately two times lower.

For an independent tour of the city, it is advisable to make a travel plan at home, in Dubai to get a map by metro and enjoy the surroundings without a time limit, but without a guide.
