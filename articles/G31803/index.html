Pension "Laguna", Abkhazia: photos and reviews

Gagra is a small resort town on the Black Sea coast of Abkhazia. Since the beginning of the last century, Gagra has been a high-end resort. Representatives of the royal family and the imperial government rested here. Later, the Gagra became a resort of the powers that be of the Soviet state.

Gagra is still a popular resort, and anyone can relax here. The city has many guest houses, holiday homes and pensions. One of the most popular is the boarding house "Laguna" (Abkhazia).





basic information

The boarding house awaits guests from the end of May until the last days of October. Pension "Laguna" (Abkhazia) is located in the village of Gechripsha, Gagra district, st. Komsomolskaya, 1. Check-in time is 14 hours, checkout time is noon. There are no age restrictions for children. A child under five years old has a rest in a sanatorium without a seat, only payment for food is required (when booking). But with animals it is forbidden to be in the boarding house.

Guests are accommodated in a three-story residential building, in which 44 rooms, of which 22 are facing the sea (these rooms have large balconies).

The beach is within walking distance, across the road. Most of the beach is rocks, only the water has a strip of sand. If you are bored with a beach holiday

Excursion routes in these places abound. You can buy a tour anywhere. But vacationers recommend buying sightseeing tours right at the boarding house.

Trips to Lake Ritsa, to evening Gagra, to alpine meadows, to a trout breeding farm, and many others are popular.

For those who are strong in mind and body, the excursion bureau of the Laguna complex (boarding house, Abkhazia, Gechripsh) offers jipping in mountain gorges. The route passes through Lake Ritsa and ends in alpine meadows. The tour starts at 9 a.m. and ends at half past seven in the evening. During this time, tourists visit many large and small waterfalls by car with an open top, find themselves on Lake Ritsa, and then on breathtaking alpine meadows. All this on the road with bumps and a bunch of water spray. In addition to the beauties, drivers know where you can buy good brandy or honey, where you can take cheap pictures with an owl or jump from a bungee ... The emotions received from this trip last for the whole winter.





Extreme lovers

But it is in this part of Abkhazia, from the top of Mount Mamdzyshha, that you can see Mount Arabica, Cape Pitsunda, the Main Caucasian Range and the entire Gagra in full view! The flight lasts 25-30 minutes. At the request of the tourist, video and photography are being conducted. Usually a paraglider lands very effectively - on an agreed place on the beach.

Reservation

You can book rooms at the Laguna boarding house online on the official website or on tourist portals. And also by a toll-free phone for any region of Russia.





Requirements for documents and additional paid services

Tourists need to remember that there are a number of immutable rules, compliance with which is necessary in the territory of the resorts of the Republic of Abkhazia (Gagra).

Pension "Laguna" offers paid parking places, as well as transfers from the airport (Adler) or railway stations and bus stations, as well as a tour desk.

Tourists to cross the Russian-Abkhaz border and check into a boarding house must have a passport, Russian or foreign, and a tourist voucher.

For children under 14 years old, accompanying persons must have, upon request, a birth certificate and a document of Russian citizenship. Children over 14 years old - the same documents plus a passport of Russian citizenship or foreign.

Children crossing the border without accompanying persons or parents must necessarily present a notarized power of attorney from parents for the right of the child to cross the border of the Russian Federation at a border checkpoint.
