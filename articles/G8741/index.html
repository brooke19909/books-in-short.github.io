Sanatorium "Solnechny" (Sochi): photos and reviews

Many people call Sochi the capital of the Russian resorts of the Black Sea region. This is a fairly large city with a very developed infrastructure. The resort often becomes the venue for many large events. The Olympics recently took place here, thanks to which the city has changed beyond recognition. It is at this Black Sea resort that international festivals and forums are organized. People come here to get a good rest on the seashore. In recent years, a huge number of hotels and hotels have appeared here, many rest houses and boarding houses have been reconstructed. Here you can not only relax, but also receive full treatment. Indeed, in the resort there are many recreational facilities. One of them is the sanatorium "Solnechny". Sochi annually receives thousands and thousands of tourists from all over the country. Foreigners began to come here, who appreciated the advantages of this resort.



Sanatorium "Solnechny" (Sochi)

The FSB, and it is this structure that owns this health center, owns another nearby health resort. This is a sanatorium named after Dzerzhinsky. Since 2007, it was decided to merge the medical base of these two resorts. Therefore, today all those who buy tickets to the sanatorium "Solnechny" (Sochi) are serviced by doctors and take all the procedures available to vacationers in the F. Dzerzhinsky Wellness Center. There is also a library, sauna, beauty salon and pool.

The territory on which the sanatorium "Solnechny" (Sochi) is located, although small, but well-groomed and thought out. A five-minute walk is the famous "Riviera" - a park that has gained popularity among tourists due to the huge variety of attractions, a dolphinarium, a unique rose garden.

How to get there

The address where you can find the sanatorium "Solnechny": Sochi, Central District, Vinogradnaya street, building 43. The international airport is thirty kilometers away from it. From it you can get on buses number 84, 4 and 4 "s". The train station is three kilometers away. Those who come to Sochi by train can continue their journey to the sanatorium by regular buses under numbers 6, 9. If you wish, you can order a transfer from tour operators.

Nutrition

Today, you can buy tickets to this sanatorium from almost all major tour operators. The price, in addition to accommodation, also includes three meals a day, carried out on a custom menu. Experienced nutritionists and doctors help tourists select dishes that are suitable specifically for everyone. Breakfast, lunch and dinner are organized in the dining room of the sanatorium at the appointed hours.



There is also a cafe in the resort. Drinks and food are available for a fee.

Additional Information

Sanatorium "Solnechny" (Sochi), reviews of which are mostly positive, offers residents various excursion tours in the Krasnodar Territory. On its territory there is an open area for playing volleyball. In the main building for children there is a playroom, there is also a club, a gym and a library. The close proximity of the Riviera Park is another plus point for coming here with the whole family. Kids will enjoy the ride on the merry-go-round, watch the show at the dolphinarium, etc.

Medical base

The main medical profiles of this sanatorium are diseases of the nervous and circulatory systems, musculoskeletal and skin pathologies. Vacationers can receive both water and balneological treatment. Coniferous, carbonic, hydrogen sulphide, pearl, iodine-bromine and herbal baths are at their service. By appointment, you can go on a circular, ascendant and Charcot's shower, get an underwater massage.

For the diagnosis of the cardiovascular system, patients can undergo rheovaso, electrocardiography, and echocardiography. Biochemical and general clinical laboratory tests are also carried out here. If necessary, vacationers are assigned to re-diagnose their existing diseases. The list of studies includes a lipid profile, sigmoidoscopy, fluoroscopy, ultrasound, cytological examination, etc.

Some of the procedures are carried out on the territory of the sanatorium. Dzerzhinsky, the rest in the "Solar". The physiotherapy department has the most modern EHF equipment, several types of lasers, magnetotherapy, mechanical massage and inhalers. For complex treatment, hydrogen sulfide is used from sources of the world famous Matsesta.



Anti-cellulite wrap, diet, acupuncture, laser and apparatus therapy, lymphatic drainage, irrigation of the head and gums, manual massage, ultratonotherapy - this is not the whole list of procedures offered by the FSB Solnechny sanatorium (Sochi).

Reviews

Not only employees of the departmental structure come here, but also everyone. Of course, the conditions offered by the sanatorium "Solnechny" (Sochi) are somewhat different from their European counterparts. However, most vacationers liked the rooms, food and location of the health resort.



Russians say many good words about the work of the staff. The beach also did not cause any particular complaints. Some vacationers were unsatisfied with the treatment, considering it not entirely successful. However, the majority of Russians liked the Solnechny sanatorium; they believe that it can be recommended to those who have not been here before.
