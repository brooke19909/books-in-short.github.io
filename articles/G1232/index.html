Peno Lake: description, recreation

Upper Volga - these are amazingly beautiful places. Here I would like to spend my whole life, or at least summer. Today I want to talk about Lake Peno, located in the Tver region. There is no biting, deaf winter and off-season. At any time of the year, the nature around the lake is magnificent, and the fish bite regardless of the weather. Perhaps tomorrow you will decide to visit here.



Description

You will not lose if you decide to spend your vacation on its cozy shores. Peno Lake is a pearl in the Upper Volga basin. The beauty of untouched nature is unparalleled. The name is somewhat surprising, it sounds completely different than all other objects in the area. But in fact, everything turns out to be quite simple: at the bottom of the lake, a lot of stumps were found, which was expressed in the name. The area is quite significant, 16.7 square meters, and the maximum depth is 17 meters.

Here you can have a great time in summer and winter. Pleasant pastimes include relaxing on a clean beach, boat trips and picking berries. The place is truly unique because a huge number of rare plants grow here. And hunting and rabalka are simply legendary.

Wild holiday

Peno Lake gives you the opportunity to take a break from civilization and be alone with nature and yourself. By prior request, you can organize a wild vacation in the designated parking lot. Of the benefits, tourists note the magnificent nature and silence. Here you can really enjoy the fresh air, sunrises and sunsets, warm water and soft sand on the beach. Even if it rains, you can still swim.

Of the minuses - a huge number of mosquitoes. No repellents help, the only way out is mosquito nets. And come in handy and large, which are thrown on the tent, and small, on the head and body. Insects begin to be especially atrocious in the evening and in cloudy weather. On a sunny day, I manage to get a fairly calm sunbath. But the lack of basic amenities often scares away urban residents. Especially for them, Lake Peno prepared a surprise.

Park Hotel Vselug

Before the trip, it is advisable to study all the recreation centers well. Peno Lake is today a very popular holiday destination among tourists, therefore there are many equipped bases on its shores. Vselug is ideal for lovers of a comfortable stay. The building itself resembles a miniature castle with pointed spiers. Accommodation is provided in comfortable rooms. Each of them has free Wi-Fi and a bathroom with a bath. A beautiful and cozy restaurant is open on site, waiting for dear guests. People come here to dinner from other holiday homes, where such a service is not provided.

Infrastructure

Holidays on Lake Peno can be surprisingly different. Starting from spending the night in tents and ending with the restoration of health in the sanatorium. It features a huge clean beach and cozy gazebos, a heated outdoor pool, a sauna and billiards. For active recreation there is a playground and rental sports equipment.

As part of the spa treatment, a large number of therapeutic and rehabilitation programs are offered here that are aimed at treating and preventing the most common diseases. And in between procedures, you can rent a boat, go fishing and go hunting. The cost of rest is from 2900 rubles per day.



Base "Lake", Lake Peno

Tver region is amazingly picturesque. The abundance of rivers and forests, lakes and streams, places not touched by civilization, makes it a nook of rest in our hectic world. The RC Ozernaya Residence welcomes its guests all year round. You are waiting for comfortable peasant houses made of pure wood. BO Borovoe

Only 100 meters separates it from the lake. The base is a few cozy, comfortable houses that are perfect for living as a true fan of leisurely country holidays. Peno Lake (Tver Oblast) offers five log cabins for accommodation on this base, in which there is everything necessary for life.

Catering for guests is carried out independently, for this there are equipped kitchens, and on the territory there are barbecue facilities. The guest houses have saunas, and boat and bicycle rentals are available at the base. Fans can book a motor boat fishing service with the rangers. The cost of rest - from 6300 rubles per day.

Base "Orlinka" (Lake Peno)

It is located on the eastern shore of the lake, 10 km from the village of Peno. Experts say that this is one of the most beautiful places not only on the lake, but in the whole region. Excellent conditions for fishing, expanse for mushroom pickers and herbalists. Separately, I want to note the magnificent sandy beach. The base area is colossal, 26 hectares.





This base is familiar to almost all experienced tourists, which is not surprising, because it has been operating since 1976. Capacity - 800 people, and it operates year-round. At any time of the year you can fish and hunt here, walk in the protected forests.

Guest Accommodation

From June to the end of August, small summer houses are available. Each of them includes several two-room rooms with separate entrances and necessary equipment. The rooms are partial amenities. This is the most economical and Spartan accommodation option. The cost of a trip with food is about 800 rubles per day.

For city residents who are not satisfied with the shower on site, a comfortable hotel is offered, or rather, two, for 6 and 8 rooms. Cost - 3300 rubles per day. And the most preferred housing category is cottages, cozy and beautiful. Nearby are gazebos and picnic areas with sea views. Inside the whole set of modern furniture and appliances, heating is regulated. Room rate - from 2200 per day. Meals are paid separately, plus 500 rubles to the cost. The cost of accommodation includes three meals a day, visits to sports grounds and a disco.





Instead of a conclusion

Relaxing on the lake can be an unforgettable adventure for the whole family. If you are traveling with children, it is better to choose one of the recreation centers, because spending the night in tents with a baby can be too difficult a test for mom. But a group of like-minded people and lovers of singing songs around the fire will fully enjoy the evening under the stars alone with nature. Rest in the Tver region is always a great mood and experience for the year ahead. Add to this the relatively low prices. Recreation facilities are scattered around the perimeter of the entire lake, so tourists have the opportunity to choose.
