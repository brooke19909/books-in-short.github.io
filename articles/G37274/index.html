RLC meters: types, characteristics

The RLC meter is a radio meter. Its main purpose is to determine the parameters of the total conductivity or impedance of the electrical circuit.



The name "RLC meter" is an abbreviation of the most common system names of those elements whose values ‚Äã‚Äãare measured by this device. In this case, R means resistance, letter C is capacitance, and L is inductance.

Operating principle

Professionals who, by their nature of activity, are associated with radio measuring equipment, need to get basic knowledge about how the RLC meter or the immitance meter works. The fact is that these devices are widely used in laboratories, in repair shops and in factories, where with their help they obtain data on the complex conductivity of circuits and their resistance. Very often, the RLC meter is used to test alternating current sources.



What is the operating principle of this precision instrument? It is based on the analysis of the received test signals. Among the main methods that are used to obtain the required parameters, bridge methods are distinguished, as well as those that are associated with the determination of relations according to Ohm's law.

RLC bridge meters work using a measuring bridge, which is balanced by sets of reference reactive (capacitive) and active resistances. Such devices perceive only fixed frequencies.

Meters using Ohm's law relationships have simpler schemes for technical self-realization and automated output of measurement results. The principle of their operation is based on the analysis of the passage of a sinusoidal test signal through the measured circuit with a given frequency. In this case, the operating voltage is supplied from the internal generator to the measurement object. It is to be determined in a selected section of the circuit, where the phase shift and current are simultaneously analyzed. The data obtained are used in the calculation of the necessary parameters.

The main purpose

Currently, there are a wide variety of immitance meters that have different technical characteristics from each other, as well as an indication type, which, in addition to performing their basic functions, can also determine such parameters as:

- phase angle (shift) between voltage and current;
- the performance of the indicator coil;
- the value of the tangent of the loss angle;
- resistance parameter, which is determined on the basis of the obtained data on current strength and voltage;
- some other parameters.

Varieties and specifications

Immitance RLC meters are divided into:

- switch;
- digital;
- combined.



These devices are widely used during research on nanocomposite materials. This is possible due to the fact that changes in the immitance can be easily registered, and its main parameters are very informative. RLC meters have wide functionality and provide high measurement accuracy, which is within 0.1%. These devices have an operating range from 0.1 to 3 x 10 to the ninth degree of Hz. In addition, they provide measurements of resistance parameters to values ‚Äã‚Äãof 10 in the eleventh degree of Ohm, capacitance - 750x10 in the third degree of Hz and inductance - 100 F. Moreover, the voltage existing at the measurement object is in the range from 0.003 to 200 V.

In addition to error ranges, the main characteristics of such devices are:

- the frequency range in which the test signal is supplied (the wider it is, the larger the C and L measurements have large boundaries);
- the presence of external and internal bias by the constant voltage of the test signal (this characteristic is important when measuring the capacitance of varicaps);
- a function that allows such devices to communicate with a personal computer, which is necessary for documenting the obtained measurement results, as well as for their software processing (plotting, indicating the dependence of inductance or capacitance on temperature, etc.);
- the ability to connect a mechanical manipulator to the device, as well as programming a meter for rejecting and sorting the components of the production process.
