Tax refund when buying an apartment: detailed return instruction

Many citizens are interested in tax return when buying an apartment. Is it possible to compensate part of the costs of purchasing a home? Or is this a futile undertaking? In Russia, there are so-called tax deductions. Thanks to them, coping with the task is not difficult. So, it seems possible to return money for the sale of real estate. Definition

The tax return when buying an apartment or other real estate in Russia is called a tax deduction. It is provided to the homebuyer in certain circumstances.

Deduction is the process of reimbursing funds for certain legally relevant transactions, as well as for expenses for various needs, at the expense of paid income taxes. Also called the process of exempting income from personal income tax for one or another amount.

Types of Deductions

The return of income tax when buying an apartment is a deduction. Only such returns are different. In our case, we are talking about property deduction.

At the moment, you may encounter:

  • basic property return;
  • mortgage deduction.

In the first case, money is returned from the amount spent on the direct purchase of property. In the second - for interest paid on a mortgage.

Important: a citizen can issue two deductions at the same time.

Conditions for exercising rights

The tax deduction for buying an apartment is not always offered to citizens. As already mentioned, a person will have to comply with some rules and conditions.

At the moment, a tax type deduction is due if:

  • the applicant has an official place of work;
  • a citizen receives a salary and pays personal income tax in the amount of 13%;
  • the transaction was made on behalf of the applicant and for his own money;
  • The applicant has the citizenship of the Russian Federation.

Accordingly, the unemployed and pensioners, as well as foreigners can not count on the deduction for the acquisition of real estate. But there are certain exceptions. We will tell about them further.
