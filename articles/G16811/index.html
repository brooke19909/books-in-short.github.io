Costume Mary Poppins - Stylish Carnival Outfit

In less than 10 years it will be possible to celebrate the centennial of one of the most beloved children's fairy tales. In 1934, a book by Pamela Travers about the nanny sorceress, Mary Poppins, was published in England. Since then, the relevance of the tale has not been lost, and every child would like to have such a nanny like her. The carnival costume of Mary Poppins is a rather unusual solution, but if you think it through well, you will recognize the image at first sight.

Elegant nanny





In our country, the story that happened in Cherry Lane in the house of Mr. and Mrs. Banks and their children, as well as gardener Robert Hey, is known in the translation of Boris Zakhoder. The easy syllable that told the tale made her a book for the night, so beloved by both children and adults. Mary Poppins, despite the fact that she flies on an umbrella with the wind, always and everywhere looks Perfection. And this is the basis of her image.

Sewing or making a Mary Poppins costume with your own hands is not difficult if you read the book again. A hat, a big bag, a starchy apron, a blue dress with gold buttons and, of course, cleaned shoes and boots to shine are the basis of such a carnival costume.

The book on the nanny sorceress (translated by Boris Zakhoder) mentions how Mary loves to admire her reflection in store windows. The costume of Mary Poppins, which will be based on a classic cut dress, will become unusual, but easily recognizable at any holiday.

Oddities

Carnival outfit should not be too complicated and easily recognizable. The Mary Poppins costume is easy to recognize if you use certain accessories:

  • a hat, small, unusual;
  • a white collar and cuffs on the dress, characteristic of women's wardrobe of the early 20th century;
  • an umbrella with a parrot toy on the handle;
  • a large bag of carpet fabric in which you can put a stylized bottle with potion and a spoon;
  • shoes or short boots with a small heel and lace-up;
  • gloves.

You can make a very simple suit - a white blouse, a black skirt, a red bow tie and a red belt for a skirt. White gloves, a hat and a large bag complete the look.
