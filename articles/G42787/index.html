Atlantica Sancta Napa Hotel 3 * (Cyprus / Ayia Napa): description and reviews

When planning your Cyprus vacation, check out the Atlantica Sancta Napa Hotel. Here you will be surrounded by attention and comfort.

a brief description of

A tourist destination such as Ayia Napa is becoming increasingly popular. Atlantica Sancta Napa Hotel is a worthy representative of this resort. Belonging to the Atlantica hotel family (since 2006) is, in a way, a guarantee of good rest, comfortable stay and quality service. It is worth noting that in 2011 the hotel was completely renovated and brought into line with international standards.





Location

One of the most popular tourist destinations is Cyprus. Atlantica Sancta Napa Hotel 3 * hotel is located in the heart of Ayia Napa resort. It is approximately 45 km from Larnaca International Airport and 170 km from Paphos Air Harbor. The beach and fishing pier are only 100 meters from the hotel, and you need to walk about 5 minutes to the main tourist street.





Description of rooms

The rooms of the Atlantica Sancta Napa Hotel are distinguished not only by a high level of comfort, but also by the original design. You will have access to these options for accommodation:

  • Standard room is 21 square meters. m. visually seems more spacious due to the predominance of white in the design. Two one and a half beds can accommodate 2 adults and 1 child (or vice versa - 2 children and 1 adult). The room has a workplace, a seating area and a private balcony with a sliding glass door. Accommodation options with a view of the garden, city or sea coast are possible.
  • Superior with an area of ​​24 sq.m. also accommodates 3 guests. The room is equipped with a large bed, as well as a sofa for relaxation and additional accommodation. There is a coffee table with a pair of soft chairs.
  • Suite is a spacious apartment of 32 square meters. m, which, taking into account the extra beds, can accommodate up to 4 guests. A feature of this room is a mirror wall, which visually makes the room more spacious. The balcony offers views of the pool or the sea.
  • The family room is designed as a studio. Parent and child zones, equipped with large and twin beds, are separated by a decorative partition. There is a small dining area.
  • The bungalow is 24 square meters. m. can accommodate 3 guests. In their equipment and interior, they completely repeat the standard rooms. It has a private terrace with access to the garden.




Room Amenities

The guest rooms at Atlantica Sancta Napa Hotel are equipped with the latest technology. In your apartment you can take advantage of the following amenities:

  • hair dryer for drying and styling hair;
  • mini-bar, which is filled on request for a fee or can serve as a refrigerator;
  • reliable electronic safe with combination lock;
  • furnished balcony;
  • individually controlled air conditioning system;
  • TV connected to a satellite dish;
  • wireless internet signal.




Hotel Infrastructure

The developed infrastructure will delight its guests with the Atlantica Sancta Napa Hotel 3 *. Guests can use the following facilities:

  • two elevators that quickly and comfortably deliver guests to the upper floors of the residential building;
  • there is an internet corner in the lobby area where you can use stationary computers connected to the cable network;
  • a convenience store where you can buy food, drinks, household chemicals and basic medicines;
  • own hairdresser with male and female rooms;
  • conference room for business negotiations;
  • terminals for payment of services in non-cash form;
  • Private parking, which is under 24-hour security and is equipped with security cameras.

Catering establishments

Delicious food and quality drinks are the most important component of good rest. At Atlantica Sancta Napa Hotel you can visit the following catering establishments:

  • In the Margarita restaurant, guests can enjoy a rich breakfast (from 7:00 to 10:00), an original lunch (from 12:30 to 14:30) and a gourmet dinner (from 18:30 to 21:30). In a large bright room with original design operates a self-service system.
  • From 10:00 to 18:00 the Adriana Bar is located by the pool. In addition to refreshing drinks, it also serves light snacks and fast food. You can also eat fresh fruits and vegetables.
  • Atrium is a cozy cocktail bar that is open from 18:00 until late at night. This is a great place for fans of incendiary dances and original drinks.




Supply system

You can eat on a half board system or choose the concept of "All inclusive". The latter includes the following items:

  • Full breakfast, lunch and dinner in buffet style in the main restaurant of the hotel;
  • after breakfast and at midday, it is fashionable to eat hot snacks;
  • from 10:00 to midnight in all bars, guests can enjoy light cold snacks, as well as local alcoholic and refreshing drinks;
  • all day guests can enjoy a wide range of ice cream;
  • from 16:30 to 17:30 you can eat fresh pastries and hot tea.




Service for guests

The staff of the Atlantica Sancta Napa Hotel 3 * hotel treats its guests with attention and care. Here, guests can take advantage of the following list of services:

  • car, motorcycle and bicycle rental;
  • round-the-clock medical care (first aid and examination in case of malaise or injuries);
  • washing guests' personal clothes in the laundry room;
  • currency exchange operations (for commission);
  • the security service is responsible for your peace and security;
  • daily cleaning, as well as regular replacement of bed linen and towels.

Entertainment opportunities

Atlantica Sancta Napa Hotel 3 * is not a place for boredom. A rich entertainment program has been developed for guests here. You can take advantage of these opportunities to organize your leisure:

  • visiting the spa, where guests can undergo a course of procedures for beauty, wellness or relaxation;
  • an outdoor swimming pool, next to which there are many umbrellas and sun loungers;
  • a tennis court with a professional coating, as well as lighting for playing in the dark;
  • game of darts and dart throwing competitions;
  • swimming lessons in the pool or on the beach;
  • games room with pool table;
  • a gym with modern equipment for training;
  • a sauna, thanks to which you can improve well-being, as well as relieve emotional and physical stress;
  • mini-football field;
  • playing volleyball on the beach;
  • cycling in the surroundings;
  • table tennis;
  • after dinner, a disco;
  • guests can take part in sports contests and fun quizzes from animators;
  • mini golf;
  • aerobics in the pool;
  • a television lounge where you can watch entertaining shows or your favorite films in the company of other vacationers;
  • water activities and sports on the beach;
  • In the evenings, the cocktail bar offers live performances by singers and musicians.

Opportunities for families with children

The administration of the Atlantica Sancta Napa Hotel 3 * hotel does not forget about the comfort of the smallest guests. Ayia Napa, due to its warm climate and clean air, is a great place to relax with children. Here they have the following features:

  • restaurants have special high chairs for comfortable feeding;
  • kids club where kids can take part in entertainment and educational programs;
  • outdoor play area;
  • a children's pool filled with sea water and equipped with heating;
  • a special hypoallergenic menu for children has been developed;
  • cots and other children's furniture are provided in the rooms (on request);
  • during your absence at the hotel, a qualified nanny will look after the kids.

Nice bonuses

To demonstrate love and respect for its guests, Atlantica Sancta Napa Hotel provides a number of pleasant bonuses for certain categories of guests. So, you can take advantage of the following privileges:

  • newlyweds who have chosen this hotel for the ceremony and honeymoon receive fresh fruits, champagne, as well as branded terry robes and slippers;
  • if your birthday falls during the stay at the hotel, then you will be presented with a birthday cake during dinner in the restaurant (the upcoming date should be reminded to the administration in advance);
  • if you decide to celebrate your wedding anniversary with a trip to the hotel, you will receive champagne and fruits as a gift from the hotel;
  • regular guests (those who rest in this hotel more than 2 times) receive souvenirs with the company logo).

Positive reviews

A lot of positive comments can be read regarding the hotel Atlantica Sancta Napa Hotel 3 *. Reviews about this institution allow you to talk about the following advantages:

  • friendly and helpful staff;
  • good nutrition - varied, high-quality, satisfying and tasty;
  • good location - close to many entertainment venues, cafes and restaurants, as well as public transport;
  • if you have to move to another room due to some kind of malfunction, then for the inconvenience you will be provided with free use of the minibar;
  • nimble, courteous and friendly waiters;
  • original design of rooms and the hotel as a whole;
  • to get to the sea, you need to go no more than 5 minutes;
  • a large clean beach with a gentle entry into the water;
  • almost every day you can listen to live music;
  • in the evenings, a lotto game is sometimes held;
  • quick registration procedure;
  • You can not be afraid for the safety of things in the room, the maids will not take anything (even if you put a tip on the table, and not on the edge of the bed, they will remain untouched);
  • guests can use all the hotel services immediately after arrival, even if they are not already registered;
  • for guests who go on an excursion, for a walk or to the airport, lunch boxes with Greek salad, fruits, tortillas with meat and chips are provided;
  • there are many sun loungers near the pool, and therefore there is enough space for everyone;
  • beautifully designed pools with decorative waterfalls.




Negative reviews

Some negative points you may encounter while relaxing at Atlantica Sancta Napa Hotel. Reviews say the following:

  • the juices in the bars and the restaurant are half diluted with water (although the fact that they are natural and not powder is already pleasing);
  • ashtrays are on all tables in the restaurant, and therefore, most likely, tobacco smoke will ruin your meal;
  • old furniture in the rooms;
  • the hotel does not have its own beach;
  • the rooms (and the hotel as a whole) have not seen repair for a long time;
  • at lunch, at the distribution in the restaurant, large lines gather;
  • the hotel is very boring, the animation is practically absent;
  • tennis court and golf course in disrepair;
  • the hotel has problems with accommodation (even if you have booked a room in advance, it is quite possible that you will have to spend the night in a room on the ground floor until other guests evict;
  • animation is in English only;
  • a small area with no places for walking;
  • low-quality local alcohol (information about it is impossible to find out, since drinks are contained in large containers without labels);
  • during dinner, all drinks are paid (you need to pay an additional 2 euros).

General impression

A good option for a sea holiday can be considered Atlantica Sancta Napa Hotel 3 *. Cyprus will make a favorable impression on you if you stay here. Perhaps the most significant shortcomings can be considered problems with the placement, as well as the lack of active animation. But the location of the hotel is its main advantage. It is impossible not to say about nutrition. Despite the fact that the buffet is not full of variety, the food does not bother during the whole rest. The dishes are delicious and homemade.
