They are glorified by poets and filmmakers, millions of people want to go there, and there are reasons for this.

Progress does not stand in one place, and now unique panoramas of cities in the world have become available, which allow you to get to almost any corner of our planet without leaving your home.





Rio de Janeiro, Brazil

Locals joke that after God created the world, he spent the seventh day in Rio, sipping a kaipirinha and admiring his work from Mount Corcovado. You should do the same. Take a taxi to Treme do Corcovado, there you will find a funicular that will take you directly to the feet of Christ the Redeemer.





Most visitors come here late at sunset. Virtual panoramas of the cities of the world can convey only a part of that indescribable beauty, which in full can be felt only with a personal presence.

Cologne, Germany

Cologne is not characterized by the presence of skyscrapers, but there are many beautiful old buildings. including the huge Cologne Cathedral, which occupies an honorable 3rd place in height among the Christian cathedrals of the world.

New York, USA

One of the most impressive panoramas of cities in the world is a view of Manhattan. Its main highlight is the Empire State skyscraper. The fragrant oasis, the so-called green zone, perfectly fits into the city ensemble.

London, Great Britain

Among the most beautiful panoramas of the cities of the world, it is worth mentioning the beautiful capital of England - London, whose architecture perfectly combines history and modernity. The famous Big Ben, the Palace of Westminster and the Gothic centuries-old cathedrals are harmoniously combined with a Ferris wheel and an unusual triangular skyscraper.

Dubai, United Arab Emirates

The city, built in the desert, breaks all records in the number of buildings that reach 250 meters. It is here that the highest building in the world is located - the Burj Khalifa skyscraper.
