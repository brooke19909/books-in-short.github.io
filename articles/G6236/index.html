"Ak Bulak" - a ski resort in Kazakhstan

The ski resort "Ak Bulak" is located in the Soldiers gorge of the Almaty region of Kazakhstan. Translated from Kazakh "White Stream". To ski, many have to go where there are ski resorts. The residents of Almaty are lucky, they live in a city located at the foot of the Zailiysky Alatau, and there are enough mountain trails here, you can choose for every taste.

About the resort

Ski resort "Ak Bulak" - a great place for an active winter holiday. Fans and professionals alpine skiing, as well as those who wish to acquire the skills of skiing, have the opportunity to ski and snowboard on various tracks. There are green ones for beginners and black ones for professionals. The resort has a ski school, which is taught by experienced ski instructors. They will teach beginners to ride and help hone their skills to ski enthusiasts. At the ski complex you can still ride on snow scooters.



In the spring and summer, the resort is not empty. Fans of extreme sports can ride mountain biking trails on the green stadium or take part in cross-country races. Under the guidance of instructors of the ski resort "Ak Bulak" you can climb the mountains.

Infrastructure

On the territory of the alpine ski resort "Ak Bulak" there is a hotel of the same name, consisting of two buildings. And it is comfortable, with a modern decor. The room stock of the first hotel building consists of standard single, double with large bed, double with 2 beds, double "Suites". The second hotel building has four-bed and two-room rooms.

All rooms are equipped with modern equipment, telephone, air conditioning. In all areas of the hotel you can use WI-FI. The hotel has an Ak Bulak Assorti restaurant, two Sneak Bar and Teras Bar cafes, a bowling alley, billiards, a large pool and a sauna.

The complex "Ak-Aydin" is designed for sporting events on the ice platform, such as hockey, figure skating, ice shows. The site can be transformed into a summer sports one, where volleyball, basketball, tennis, and gymnastics are held. The complex has a large gym and boxing rooms.
