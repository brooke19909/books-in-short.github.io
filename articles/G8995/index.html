Modular house for year-round use: features, pros and cons

Modular houses are one of the novelties of the domestic construction market. It is a unique technology that involves the construction of on-site buildings from prefabricated modules manufactured at the factory. Initially, in a similar way, mostly ugly and uncomfortable buildings were used that were used for temporary residence of people. These are cars for rest of workers, lodges, gatehouses for security guards and other similar constructions.

Progress does not stand still, and today many companies offer their customers to build a modular house for year-round use. What are the advantages and disadvantages of buildings in this category?





Modular home - what is it?

Modular are buildings built from factory-made structures. Each module is 75-90% ready. During construction, the right amount of elements is assembled on site like a child‚Äôs designer. At the place of construction of the building, it is only necessary to prepare the foundation and lay the necessary communications.

A modular house for year-round use can be built in just 1-2 weeks. Moreover, buildings of this category are made of various materials. Modular construction technologies make it possible to build houses of various storeys. At the request of the customer, the modules can be made with or without interior and facade decoration. Today, many companies specializing in the creation of modular buildings, offer their customers standard designs and the development of individual ones.





Advantages of modular homes

The main advantage of modular construction is the speed of construction of buildings. Modules manufactured at the plant are highly leakproof, so construction work with their use can be carried out in all weather and climatic conditions.

Modular house for year-round use does not require additional installation of windows and doors. All internal communications are usually routed in modules at the factory. The finished house can only be connected to the water supply, sewage and electricity. Modular buildings can always be completed by ordering a few more elements.

The production of residential buildings in this technology is also characterized by low cost. Transporting modules over short distances and assembling them in place does not require the use of heavy equipment. Turnkey modular homes for year-round use can be erected using a conventional construction crane. Buildings of this type look diverse and modern. Customers are offered projects in various styles, facades can be decorated in stone or wood.





The disadvantages of modular homes

If you want to build a modular house for year-round use, special attention should be paid to additional insulation of the building. One of the significant drawbacks of buildings in this category is the low thermal insulation of factory units. More recently, modular buildings were used exclusively as temporary or summer houses. And this means that most manufacturers of such houses are only trying to create better and warmer architectural masterpieces.

Today, one of the most inexpensive options for individual construction is modular homes for year-round use. The price of the finished building is the sum of the cost of the modules and the costs of their transportation and assembly. A new home can be very expensive if you order delivery from a remote region. Some real estate experts question the longevity of modular buildings. If you want the house to please you for at least several decades, pay attention to the choice of materials used for its manufacture.





The cost of modular buildings for year-round living in Russia

The price of modular buildings is the sum of the cost of the modules used in the construction, the cost of their delivery, on-site assembly of the building and some associated costs. It is important to understand that the individual development of architectural and engineering solutions is more expensive. Typical projects will cost much less. Modular houses for year-round use are more expensive than seasonal country houses and non-residential buildings. What is the order of prices for residential buildings erected from prefabricated modules in our country?

Many companies in Russia offer the construction of turnkey small modular houses for only 750,000 rubles. The cost of multi-level cottages with a large area is usually 1,500,000-3,000,000 rubles. Today, our compatriots are increasingly choosing modular homes for year-round use. The price of such buildings is noticeably lower than analogues built by traditional methods. And what is especially nice, a new house from ready-made modules can be built in the shortest possible time.
