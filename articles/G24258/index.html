Hotel Blue Princess Beach Resort 4 * (Greece / Corfu island / Liapades) - overview, features, rooms and reviews

Nature generously awarded Greece with seas, mountains and unique nature. Incredible views at sunset and dawn on the rocky stepped shores with lovely doll-like houses, incomparable local cuisine and wine, places for filming Hollywood films, historical sights, SPA procedures, shopping and much more - all this, Greece. Russian tourists are always welcomed here with sincere hospitality, and on the islands it manifests itself more than in the capital. People live there simply, sincerely empathize, sincerely rejoice. We will also go to one of the Greek islands, which in Italian sounds Corfu, and in Greek Kerkyra.

General information about the area

The country's mainland is densely framed by islands around the perimeter. Each of them is noticeable in its own way, has its own internal traditions and features. Corfu Island is one of the most popular among tourists, it is called emerald because of the abundance of olive groves, vineyards, orange orchards and all this against the backdrop of magical shades of the sea. Cozy bays, the medieval Angel Castle, the Canal of Love, narrow cozy streets and the capital, similar to Venice, are just a few places that fascinate the island.

Is the village of Liapades located on its west coast? near which the Blue Princess Beach Resort is located (the resort means that there is a pool on its territory). Here natural beauties are pleasing to the eye and the still preserved Venetian architecture is amazing. In the village there are supermarkets with a good assortment of products and souvenirs and local cafes where you can have a tasty meal and appreciate the local cuisine. This region of Greece is ideal for families with children, but youth who crave fun will find it easy to find outside the village.





Hotel according to tour operators

The hotel is located near the airport - literally 10-20 minutes drive. Blue Princess Beach Resort 4 * is very small, there are only 44 rooms. It consists of buildings and bungalows, picturesquely arranged on the slope of the mountain. The rooms have everything for a pleasant carefree vacation: a bathroom, a kitchen, a balcony with beautiful views, satellite TV, a telephone, a refrigerator, bathrobes and slippers. On the territory of the hotel, guests have access to a safe, laundry, currency exchange, restaurants and bars, a sauna, a Turkish bath, massage, a children's playground and an outdoor pool, a meadow with a lawn and palm trees - nothing more, just everything you need. Prices at Blue Princess Beach Resort depend on the season and range from 60 euros per room in May and from 100 euros in July-August.

Given that the price includes food, this, according to most guests, is the best combination of quality and price.





Hotel location

Corfu has not only a village, but also Liapades beach. Blue Princess Beach Resort 4 * is located just next to it, 200 meters from the shore of the Ionian Sea. From the airport, a shuttle arrives here in just 15 minutes - very good news for tourists after the flight. However, flights from Russia to the island are available only in the summer. The rest of the time you need to get to Corfu by ferry. Vacationers call the island fabulous, it is so beautiful here. The place is quiet, calm, ideal for families with children and a complete rejection of the bustle of civilization in big cities. Young people who want adventures and night parties, without any problems in the capital of Corfu, to which no more than half an hour to go.





Rooms - General Impressions

The rooms of the Miro Blue Princess Beach Resort 4 * hotel, as noted above, are scattered on the side of the mountain, the reception is right by the water. According to the almost unanimous opinion of the guests, the rooms here are quite spacious, and the views from the windows the size of the wall or terraces are impressive, regardless of the sea or the surrounding area - in this place all the landscapes are beautiful with their pristine nature. The rooms are clean, the cleaning is quality daily, the Greeks successfully combine it with a siesta, the repair is fresh, the furniture is new. Each person settles in different ways: someone is accommodated in excellent apartments for 10-15 minutes without a single problem, while someone is forced to wait half a day and then resettled for the duration of the vacation. Plumbing in the rooms is in good condition, but you need to wait for hot water after opening the tap for about a minute. The category of rooms in the buildings is only one - "Standard". Beach

All beaches of Greece are public and do not belong to any of the hotels. And all paid sunbeds. Tourists come in two ways: they simply rent sunbeds daily or connect to the hotel guide question and he agrees to rent with a good discount, provided that sunbeds are paid on the first day for the entire vacation period. The guests of the Blue Princess Beach Resort 4 * to Corfu in the reviews do not recommend swimming in the sea on the left side of the pier, because there a sewer goes into the water. The beach is sand and pebble, so special rubber shoes will not hurt. If you don‚Äôt have one with you, you can buy it next to the hotel at a very reasonable price. The water on the beach is clean, clear, but cool, the sand is soft. It should be noted that Corfu is the northernmost of the islands called the Ionian. The water in the sea here warms up only by July, and in June swimming in the sea is still uncomfortable. On the beach you can rent a boat and go on your own to inspect the bay, then you should stop in different places and enjoy the wild beaches.





Staff

Blue Princess Beach Resort 4 * in Corfu receives positive reviews for the fact that attentive and courteous staff work here. Especially pleasing guests are employees of restaurants and bars. But periodically, tourists complain about the scandalous and indifferent to the problems of the guests of the hotel staff, it is especially difficult to find a common language with the managers at the reception at the settlement. Hotel guides from tour operators in every possible way try to improve the rest and advise excursions and interesting places, as well as ways to save. It is important that the staff generally does not speak Russian, so you will have to communicate in English or German.





Entertainment at the hotel, village and island

There are no animations like Egyptian or Turkish in Blue Princess Beach Resort 4 *. Depending on the season and the dates of rest, in some periods the animation was easy evening with Greek performances and dances, on other days of weekdays the tourists did not even notice it, only musicians with programs came on weekends. They will not force guests to attend daily exercises or water aerobics here. It fits perfectly for everyone who wants maximum privacy and peace. For those who want to dive on the island, there are diving centers. Car enthusiasts can rent a vehicle (car or scooter or motorcycle) and take a walk in the vicinity, visit significant places. In addition, everyone who wants to get acquainted with the area can buy fascinating excursions. Here, excursions come in two directions: sea and land. Maritime pass on boats, boats, yachts, provide for acquaintance with the open sea, a barbecue is organized directly on the ships. From excursions around the island, vacationers praised the sightseeing in Corfu and the palace of Princess Sissi. Near the Blue Princess Beach Resort 4 * hotel there are supermarkets with normal prices for food and souvenirs. In the village itself sometimes there are holidays that you can visit.

Local kitchen

Despite the very small territory of Liapades, there are enough local cafes and restaurants. Tourists unanimously praise the local cuisine, and especially the desserts. Travelers also recommend trying and comparing Greek salad, which is called "rustic", with the usual Russian recipe. No pretentious service - all at home, the portions are impressive.
