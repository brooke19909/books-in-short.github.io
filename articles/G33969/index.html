Review of the Redmond RBM-M1911 bread machine

Everyone dreams of having fresh pastries at home. This can be done with the Redmond RBM-M1911 bread machine. She is able to independently knead the dough. Nineteen automatic programs greatly expand the potential of the device. Everyone for themselves can choose a crisp color. There are three levels - from lightly roasted to dark. In addition to baking, the baker will do an excellent job of cooking jam or jam and stewing foods. A large number of recipes can be found in the book, which is included in the kit.





The Redmond RBM-M1911 bread machine is made in a stylish design. The combination of glossy surfaces (stainless steel) and matte (plastic) sets it apart from the rest. The case has an oval shape. The control panel is on top. Using it is quite convenient. You can buy a model for 6-7 thousand rubles. As far as performance is concerned, many people claim that the recipes from the instructions are not thorough. You have to independently change the weight of the products and the baking time. In general, if you get a hold of it, then the bread is excellent.

Specifications

Redmond RBM-M1911 - a 550W bread machine. It works from a network of 220-240 V. The internal coating is non-stick. The maximum weight of the bread is 1 kg, it is also possible to bake loaves of 500 and 750 grams. For kneading dough there is 1 blade. The device is equipped with a timer, the maximum rate is 15 hours. Importantly, there is heat support. After baking, the appliance maintains optimal temperature for an hour. Non-volatile memory saves the selected program for 10 minutes.





Equipment:

  • the baker herself;
  • measuring spoon and glass;
  • the form;
  • blade and key to remove it;
  • recipe book, warranty card, user manual.

Programs

Redmond RBM-M1911 is equipped with 19 automatic programs. Thanks to them, the device becomes multifunctional. In addition to bread and butter products, you can cook desserts, milk porridges, yogurts, soups, jams. The device works with different types of dough - yeast and yeast-free. In a bread machine you can cook classic, gluten-free, French, rye, whole grain, Borodino bread. There is an accelerated baking program, which is suitable only for white bread. With this model you can cook delicious cupcakes with raisins.

Control

Redmond RBM-M1911 has electronic control. The display is located on the top panel. It displays the time, mode, color of the crust and other information. The buttons are touch sensitive. Using the panel is very convenient. Next to it is a viewing window. Through it you can observe the processes. At the end of the operation, the device emits a sound signal. Non-stick coating allows you to completely avoid burning. The device is equipped with a memory, which even after turning off the power saves the settings.

Additional options

The Redmond RBM-M1911 bread machine is equipped with additional modes. These include a timer, surge protection and support for optimal temperature conditions. These functions are quite significant advantages.





For example, the use of delayed start allows you to bake bread at a specific time. Its range varies within 15 hours. This option should not be underestimated.
