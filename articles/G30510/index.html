Master class: how to make DIY wolverine claws

If Halloween is approaching, and you decide to appear at the festival in the image of Wolverine from the acclaimed movie "People X", then one of the main attributes of your costume, no doubt, should be long claws. If you were not able to find them in stores, then it is quite possible to build them at home. About how to make wolverine claws with your own hands, we will tell in our article. Even if you have never been fond of crafts, you should not worry, since it is not difficult to build this attribute.





How to make wolverine claws out of paper

This method, of course, is the simplest. For such a craft, you need only a few sheets of A4 plain paper itself and scissors. Instead of plain paper, you can use colored cardboard. This option is even better, since the claws will only be stronger. Prepare the paper at the rate of one sheet per claw.

Manufacturing process

Using scissors we make a square from a horizontal sheet. We place the paper on a solid, flat surface. The upper right corner of the sheet must be folded to the left side (it is necessary that a small strip of paper remains under the fold). We bend the upper left corner of the already bent leaf to the lower left corner. After completing these steps, you should get a triangle that needs to be combined with the left side of the workpiece. Now we need to turn the figure into a square, bending the right side of the sheet, the top of which is located on the fold of the triangle, to its left side. As a result of this operation, a vertical bend should result.

We add the resulting square diagonally and get a triangle. After that, we place the workpiece on the table and mark with a pencil from the top to the base a bend line perpendicular to the base of the triangle. Then we bend the edges of the triangle to this line, resulting in a rhombus.





In order for your craft to take the form of wolverine claws, you need to repeat all the above steps two more times. Try to treat the process with all care and accuracy so that the result of your work does not become a disappointment. All bends must be done clearly so that your craft is durable.

The last step in making a claw is to fix the resulting figure. To do this, we wrap the remaining paper edges in the hole located in the rear of the structure. The resulting small pocket will be used to put the claw on the finger.

So, your main attribute for a fancy dress is ready! Now you know how to make wolverine claws out of paper. Despite the fact that in words the process of creating this craft looks rather complicated, in fact, this is not so at all. Having spent several sheets of paper and a little time, you will be able to achieve an excellent result.





How to make Wolverine Claws: Other Ways

Of course, paper and cardboard are not the only materials from which this attribute can be built. If you, for example, have the skills of woodcarving, then you can very well build claws of wood. A more complicated way is to make a similar metal crafts.
