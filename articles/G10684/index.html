What are the flags honking?

With a certain periodicity along the coast you can see lifeguards decorated with flags, the color of which carries information about the weather and the safety of swimming. The best conditions are assumed by a white flag. It means that the gentle sea is ready to take you into its arms. If the red flag is hanging, then this may mean - or the absence of rescuers, or the sea is such that it is better not to swim. Yellow, however, signals that bathing in general is not a matter of strong winds, even umbrellas cannot be opened.

On each beach, about 20 meters from the water, there is a free zone on which it is forbidden to sit with things. But this order is often violated. Here you can see street vendors, mostly blacks, who evaporate at the slightest hint of police officers.

The most expensive on the coast of Rimini is the central zone of Marina Centro - the farther south from it, the lower the prices.
