Metro station "Serpukhovskaya". Characteristics

Serpukhovskaya metro station, one might say, is not particularly remarkable for either Muscovites themselves or guests of the Russian capital. However, it was she who was assigned one of the most important roles in the life of a huge and noisy city.

Section 1. Metro "Serpukhovskaya". general description



Getting into the metro car at the Tulskaya station in the direction of movement towards the center, you will certainly find yourself at Serpukhovskaya. If you wish, you can go further to Polyanka. And the rest of the passengers will probably be interested to know some details from its history.

So, the metro station "Serpukhovskaya" ... Its foundation is considered to be the eighth of November 1983. When architects together with the designers planned the construction, in the project "Serpukhovskaya" was called "Dobryninskaya". At that time, a station with the same name already existed on the Koltsevaya metro line. In order to avoid confusion and for the convenience of passengers, it was decided to rename the new station. The name was taken from a street located in the immediate vicinity.
