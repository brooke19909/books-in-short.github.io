Samara Holidays in Samara for every taste!

Samara is not only a business city, but also a place where you can have a great summer vacation. To have a good rest, it is not necessary to go to fashionable foreign resorts. A variety of entertainment can provide you and our native Samara. Holidays in Samara for every taste and budget offer everyone who wants a lot of local hotel complexes, boarding houses, resorts and rest houses.



City beaches

In a hot summer, I so want to plunge into a cool river. Samara invites all vacationers to visit the numerous beaches of the city. It has everything you need: awnings, benches with awnings, changing cabins, swings, playgrounds and sports grounds. Looking for outdoor activities in Samara? Then you can’t find a better offer than to swim in the river and play sports on the beach.

The most popular beaches:

  • beach in a country park, length - 410 m;
  • on Barboshyna glade (150 m);
  • the first stage of the embankment (1.3 km);
  • 2nd stage of the embankment (1.2 km);
  • a beach near the Mozhaisky sanatorium (120 m);
  • beach in the Krasnoglinsky district (300 m).

For especially demanding tourists there is a paid beach KIN.UP, which is located on Pervomaisky Descent. Here you will find a saltwater pool, clean sand, showers, sun loungers with umbrellas, adult and children's animation, a bar.



Holiday homes in Samara

People who suffer from problems of the musculoskeletal system, digestive system, nervous system, gynecological diseases, the Mozhaisky sanatorium is located on the banks of the picturesque Volga in the Studeny ravine.

In the picturesque pine forest there are wonderful sanatoriums “Nadezhda”, “Stavropol” and “Russky Bor”. They are located in Tolyatti (Samara region) near the Zhigulevsky reservoir. These boarding houses offer a wide range of medical and preventive services, 3 meals a day and rooms with all amenities. It also offers guests a well-maintained sandy beach, a fitness room, a library, a bar, a sauna, a pool, board games.



Holidays for families with children

How beautiful Samara! Holidays in Samara for couples can be spent on the base "Rook", which is located on the banks of the Volga. In particular, it is located on the territory of the Green Grove Island, which is considered an environmentally friendly place. The base has two- and three-story comfortable cottages. On the territory of Rook there is a billiards, a gym, a cafe-bar, a sports ground. For the smallest, a children's room of creativity is provided here, in summer animators also work at the camp site. Food at the recreation center "Rook" complex, three times a day.

Another option for families with children is the Camomile recreation center. It is located on the banks of the river in the Shigonsky district, on the territory of the Muransky Bor reserve. There are different houses at the camp site: from economy to luxury class. “Camomile” offers its guests to take advantage of such services as free parking for cars, a playground with carousels, a cafe-bar, table tennis, and catamaran rental. Accommodation here costs from 1200 rubles (with 4 meals a day).

In Muran Bor on the banks of the beautiful Usinsky Bay (190 kilometers from Samara) there is another camp site - Zarya. All vacationers have the opportunity to live in separate houses of different levels of comfort. In a word, children's rest in Samara can be very diverse.



Youth Offers

120 kilometers from Samara, in a very picturesque area, there is a recreation center "Veterok". This camp site is located on the Kopylovo peninsula, near Togliatti. From here you can see the charming Zhiguli mountains. Here you can have a great time as a large company, or together. The Veterok camp site is a sports ground, free parking, billiards, evening discos, rental of sports equipment (rollers, scooters, bicycles, etc.). Room rates per day - from 750 rubles.

Rest in Samara in the summer is a wide variety of various entertainments. If you want to play sports, play billiards or volleyball, rent a boat, dance at the disco, then choose the Iskra recreation center. It is located on the territory of the Samarskaya Luka National Park near the village of Podgora. Summer houses, three meals a day, a cafe, a bar - all this will certainly appeal to even the most demanding vacationers. There is also the opportunity to engage in sports, swimming, dancing. The cost of living is about 810 rubles per day.

130 kilometers from Samara there is a luxurious park-hotel "Vasilievsky". The camp site is huge - 30 hectares. On its territory there is a basketball court, soccer field, tennis courts. Opened a wine cellar and a hookah zone. Service in the park hotel for every taste: go-kart track, discos, sushi bar, gym, pool, mini-zoo, shooting gallery, etc. On average, you will have to pay from 1400 rubles per room.



River walks

You can’t even imagine your vacation without water? We have already studied the camp sites in Samara, now let's move on to water excursions. The magnificent views of Samara are best admired by taking a walk on a small boat or liner. Every day, comfortable pleasure boats run from the marinas. Short walks to Barboshyna Polyana, villages Shiryaevo and Rozhdestveno, numerous islets and peninsulas will please everyone without exception. And you can even give up on a fascinating cruise along the picturesque Volga! If you want a more refined stay, you can rent a VIP-class watercraft. This service is starting to be very popular. However, relaxing with friends or business partners on a luxury yacht is, of course, not cheap, but believe me, it's worth it.

Open air movies

Do you think this is all that Samara can offer you? Holidays in Samara are very diverse. Want to go to the open-air cinema? Soft grass, a dome of the starry sky, a warm breeze and your favorite movie heroes - what could be more romantic? AeroKino Cinema offers locals and visitors alike to watch a good movie in the air, make new acquaintances and get a boost of energy.

City parks

Numerous parks of Samara are perfect for a relaxing and peaceful stay: Botanical, Strukovsky, Country Gardens. Admiring the beautiful trees and flower beds will certainly help you relax. For those who still prefer noisy places, you can look at special areas with a summer cafe or go for a drive.

Leisure

Samara is also wide opportunities for outdoor enthusiasts. Paintball, skiing, riding a banana, a boat, parachuting, karting, horseback riding, a jet ski, a catamaran - extreme people will definitely not get bored here for a minute. Especially popular is the local Victoria water park. You can visit the shopping and entertainment centers of the city: Cosmoport, Park House, Moscow, Mega and others.





Samara is a great vacation spot for everyone!

Well, as you understand, it is exciting to spend a summer vacation not only abroad, but also in our country, the most striking example of this is Samara. Rest in Samara is a real kaleidoscope of entertainment. Comfortable camp sites on the banks of the Volga, river walks, luxurious beaches, bars and discos - all this will certainly appeal to even the most demanding tourists. Come to Samara and see for yourself!
