DIY time capsule

Sometimes time begins to run at an incredible speed. And in such periods I really want to plunge into memories, flip through photo albums, see my school diaries. Hands reach your favorite audio and video collections, not to mention the recordings that capture our past lives. Memories are an important part of any person‚Äôs life. But you begin to understand this only after a few years.

A few words about what a capsule is

You can prepare a gift for yourself, which will become very expensive in the future. This is a time capsule . With it, you can convey to yourself the future greetings from the past. This item is not just one way to preserve memories. With the help of a capsule you can get nice gifts, walk through your memory, go on an exciting journey. Time capsule is a way of self-expression.





In a broad sense, a capsule should be understood as a message to your future generation. Mostly messages are placed in a strong container, after which it should be hidden or buried. You need to choose a place for this yourself, guided by your own preferences. Quite often, a time capsule is created by several people. In such a situation, it often has a historical or political character.

For the first time, such capsules were used in 1937. However, examples of such messages can be found in ancient times. The purpose of the message is to preserve the history of a particular time period.





In this review, the time capsule will not be global in nature. It is only necessary to describe how to create it for yourself and your family. In addition, it should be noted that the temporary capsule can become a wonderful tradition, eventually turning into a relic, in family value.

No need to look for reasons to create a capsule

The event for creating the capsule can be any. This is the New Year, and birthday, and the wedding, and the birth of a child. There can be a huge number of options. For example, you decide to create a capsule in which you want to put wishes for your child. It will be possible to open it only when he turns 18 years old. And not before. Accordingly, the capsule will need to be packed and hidden.

Important points to consider when creating a capsule





Do-it-yourself time capsule can be created. What should be taken into account?

  1. Shelf life. First of all, you need to decide how much it should lie before opening. If it was created on New Year's Eve, then the period of its storage should be equal to one year. A capsule created on a wedding day can be opened for a specific anniversary. The message addressed to the child can be printed out when the age of majority is reached. When laying the capsule in the base of the house, its storage period can reach 70 years. In this case, the message will be read by the grandchildren.
  2. If you wondered how to make a time capsule, find a storage place for it . Previously, it was buried in the ground. If this suits you, then you need to find a sealed iron shell. You can not bury the message. It is enough to put it in a box and hide it in a secluded place. For example, in the attic.
  3. To create, you need to select a container of a certain size . It can be a beautiful jar, a thermos, a suitcase or a safe. Any container for storing a message will do. You should first put a bag inside it that will absorb moisture. Such material is available in new shoes. Using it, you can prevent message corruption.
  4. Want to understand how to make a time capsule with your own hands? Understand how you fill it out. After that, it will be necessary to pack the finished box, wrap it with tape or beautiful paper. You can use sealing wax to create a family seal.
  5. Well, you definitely need to make a reminder . The main thing is not to open the box ahead of time. The date must be indicated on the capsule. In order not to forget about an important event, you will need to send yourself an e-mail with a reminder. At the present stage, there are also such opportunities. You can leave a note on your mobile device.




New wedding tradition

At the present stage, there are a huge number of very different traditions and rites. However, every year there are more and more of them. You can diversify the celebration in different ways. And among the popular options you can highlight the creation of a time capsule.

The meaning of the ritual lies in the fact that about a day before the wedding, lovers should write letters to each other. You can specify anything in them. In this matter, one must be guided only by one's own imagination. You can also attach objects, souvenirs, memorabilia, etc. to the letter.





After this, the letters must be handed over to the witness and witness. They must keep messages until the start of the wedding. During the ceremony, letters are provided to the facilitator. He, without printing them, must hide the messages in a capsule. On it you will need to write the time and date when it should be opened. Then the messages are either buried or hidden in a secluded place. This is the meaning that the capsule of time for the wedding hides in itself.

Self-made capsule is easy

You can make a capsule yourself. This will require some kind of capacity. For example, a tin box of tea or a can. A glass bottle may also be suitable. The selected container must be well packaged so that it looks beautiful and solemn. You can use wrapping paper, ribbons. In this case, your imagination will be the main assistant. After packing, the capsule of the message must be sealed, close the container. And this should be done as thoroughly as possible. You can even hang a lock.

What can I put in a box of memories?

It was described above what a time capsule looks like and how it can be created. But what should be packaged in it?

  1. Written message to yourself or your family. You can write anything in it.
  2. Photos You, your family, pets can be captured on them.
  3. You can pack your desires and goals into a capsule . It will be interesting to know in the future what has been achieved.
  4. A video interview can be hidden in the capsule . It will need to be burned to disk or flash drive.
  5. Toys, books, magazines, newspapers, diaries , etc., can be placed in the capsule.

What should not be placed in a time capsule?

  1. Food is better not to pack. In the future, you will not want to be near an open capsule.
  2. It is not recommended to place land and expensive items.
  3. Do not stack batteries.

There are many things that are not recommended to be stacked. But the above elements must first be removed away from the time capsule. Such a message will not bring pleasure, nor pleasant emotions.

The unique opportunity that the capsule has





In this review, it was said about a message to oneself in the future. How to create a capsule with your own hands, what can be packed into it, what should be taken into account when creating it - all this has been described in sufficient detail. It should be noted that the capsule is a unique opportunity. With it, you can travel through your memories. An important event in life? Create a capsule that reminds you of it in ten years. You can get a lot of emotions from this. And they will only be positive. It is interesting to know what kind of person will become in ten years. This time will run unnoticed. And the capsule will allow him to stop, to remember what you were and what path you have been taken.
