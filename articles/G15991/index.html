How to knit a baby hat with knitting needles on your own?

To knit a baby hat, you will need 100-150 grams of yarn and knitting needles. She knits with the simplest viscous - an elastic band. It is necessary to measure the girth of the head and the depth of the future cap. Then dial the required number of loops, relying on the resulting size of the head circumference, and begin to knit (2 facial loops, 1 wrong loop). In this way, you need to knit about 45 cm and sew the seam on top and back. Due to this length, you can tie the cap with the ears with knitting needles, while it turns out the original rectangular shape. Ears can be decorated with anything you want.

The second way to knit a baby hat

There is another way that will be useful to anyone who wants to learn how to knit a baby hat with knitting needles. First you need to dial about 90 loops and knit with an elastic band, then, after knitting 25-30 cm, begin to reduce the loops, therefore, you get the bottom of the cap. Decrease is done in the front row, for this 2 loops are knitted together every six loops, in subsequent rows after five, four and so on. When the number of loops decreases to 17, they need to be gathered together and tightened. Then you need to sew a hat. The finished product can also be decorated to your taste.
