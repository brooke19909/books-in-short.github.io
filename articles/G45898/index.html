"Mountain Ezhovaya" - ski resort. Reviews on the ski complex "Mountain Ezhovaya"

Winter holidays are the best time to relax in the ski resorts. It is not necessary to go somewhere far away; you can spend an unforgettable vacation in our country. Some Russian cities offer excellent outdoor activities and skiing. Several bases are located in the Yekaterinburg region. There is also "Mountain Hedgehog." The ski complex, located at a distance of 100 kilometers from Yekaterinburg, in the Kirovograd region, has recently become increasingly popular.



About the recreation center

The recreation center is located at the foot of the Ezhova mountain. Its height reaches 550 meters above sea level. The slopes are long, up to one and a half kilometers. The elevation differences are large. Thanks to this, everyone can ski here: both professionals in alpine skiing, and beginners who should not start with heavy tracks. The ski resort is very conveniently located. Those who do not have personal transport can get to the place from the circus in Yekaterinburg. Every day (at 11 a.m.) the bus leaves for the recreation center. The cost of the trip is 1000 rubles on weekdays and 1200 on weekends. The price includes skiing (until 18.00 hours).

From the height of Mount Ezhovoy, stunning landscapes open - coniferous trees covered with snow, birch trees, hills. It is very beautiful here in the summer. By the way, the complex is open all year. When there is no snow, you can ride ATVs, enjoy walks on the slopes, take pictures against the backdrop of picturesque rocks.



Residence

If you plan a long vacation, "Mountain Ezhovaya" (ski resort) offers the tourists the hotel "Seventh Glade". It has rooms for every taste. You can rent a standard double room for 2800 on weekdays and for 4000 rubles. on holidays. The price includes meals (breakfast buffet). It should be noted that all rooms have a shower, toilet, TV. The same price for the rooms "family". The difference is that in the latter there is an additional small room for children. For people who are willing to pay more and want to relax with all the comfort, there are rooms with panoramic windows that offer a beautiful view: the surroundings of Kirovograd and Mount Ezhovaya. The ski complex cares about its reputation. Some rooms at the hotel have a private sauna. Of course, it is very convenient for lovers of relaxation.



Entertainment and infrastructure

"Mountain Ezhovaya" (Yekaterinburg) is no coincidence that it is the most popular resort in the area. There is everything for a good rest. In addition to ski equipment, a snowboard, ice sledges and other equipment for all kinds of winter activities are also rented. In addition, an elevator delivers people to the tracks, and a bird's eye view reveals all the beauty of the Ural landscape. Those who wish can go to the bathhouse, sauna, visit one of the local restaurants or a night club. In the afternoon, tourists usually head to the Visimsky Nature Reserve, where you can see more than 400 species of the most interesting representatives of the flora. In general, there will be no shortage of entertainment.

Trails

As noted above, Ezhovaya is a ski resort for those who wish to ride at any level. Each track has its own name, for example, "Bagel", "Hamster", etc. Each of them has its own length and difficulty of passing. There is one common quality: a safety system has been developed for all routes. In addition, there is special equipment for artificial snowing of slopes. You can also ride here at night, because all the tracks are well lit.





Prices

All the necessary equipment can be rented, or you can bring it with you. The cost of renting a ski kit is 500 rubles. Those who are just starting to practice this sport may ask the help of an instructor. The cost of the lesson is from 700 rubles.

Comparison with other ski resorts in the country

Photos of the Hedgehog Mountain confirm that the landscape here is bewitching. Very close to Yezhova is Mount Belaya, on which the resort with the same name is based. However, these are not the only places beyond the Urals where winter holidays can be spent. In the Novosibirsk region there is also a popular ski resort - "Jurmanka". About the recreation center "Jurmanka"

"Jurmanka" is located in the Novosibirsk region. The complex offers all types of winter extreme sports: skiing, snowboarding, jumping in a snow park. You can also go horseback riding or snowboarding here.

Where does the mountain come from in the Novosibirsk Region? Ski complex "Jurmanka" chose one of the most picturesque places in Western Siberia - Salair Ridge. Altitude, of course, is not so great, but this is enough to organize an active winter holiday. Small mountain rivers flow into spacious valleys, cutting them and forming rocky shores.
