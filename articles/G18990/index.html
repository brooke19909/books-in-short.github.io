Population

Description of the complex

One of the country's largest aquariums, which has about thirty aquariums with exotic fish, a botanical garden, an orangery, an ice rink, a cafe and a bowling alley, works here.

If you decide to spend the weekend in the water park "Galaxy" in Kogalym, you can combine relaxation with other attractions or with educational excursions. The water park is a modern complex with slides and pools (including wave ones), a jacuzzi and water cannons, geysers.

Fun and Attractions

In the water park "Galaxy" in Kogalym, 12 swimming pools with a total volume of more than 26 thousand liters are available for vacationers. Everyone who wants to feel like at sea can visit the wave pool: according to reviews, the wave height here reaches a meter. There is a gentle descent to the bowl, reminiscent of the sea coast. Kids enjoy splashing in shallow water with great pleasure, and adults swim in depth.

Waterslides

These attractions are especially appreciated by lovers of extreme relaxation. There are four slides seven meters high at SKK Galaxy. The descent stretched fifty meters. Children from the age of fourteen are allowed on the slides, and their height should exceed 140 centimeters.

Surfing

This is one of the main features of the water park in Kogalym. Not only experienced surfers will be able to do their favorite sport here, but also those who are just mastering the basics of skill. Experienced instructors will help make this as comfortable as possible and without injuries to beginners.

Spa baths

In order to forget about everyday problems and worries at least temporarily, to improve your health and relax, you need to go to a room with nine spa baths with a jacuzzi effect. You can rest assured: you will enjoy it by basking under the gentle bubble massage.

"Slow river"

This is another relaxation attraction that stretches along the entire length of the water park, one hundred and forty meters. Its area is 450 square meters. You can drift along this river, watching everything that is happening around you, for quite some time.

"Africa"

This is an attraction for the youngest visitors to the water park (up to eight years old). There is not only a special pool with warm water, but also a nice miniature waterfall: under its streams, kids will feel Mowgli.
