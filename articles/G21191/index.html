Central Bank Data

This rating is compiled on the basis of information received from the UK every quarter based on the results of financial statements. They are published on the official website of the Central Bank. When compiling a rating, information such as:

  • The total number of contracts concluded during the reporting period.
  • Total fees.
  • The size of insurance payments.
  • Bounce rate
  • Other data.

Despite the veracity of these data, you should not rely solely on them when making a decision. For example, an increase in the number of concluded agreements can only indicate a large-scale advertising campaign, and not about increasing confidence in the UK.

Information from specialized agencies

To get on the list of insurance companies published by the relevant organization, insurance companies in Moscow under mandatory motor liability insurance must file an application and pay for the services of specialists.
