If you repeatedly enter the wrong password, your iPhone will be disconnected for a longer period of time before you can try again. What needs to be done for this?

How to unlock iPhone if I forgot my password? Apple's official instructions for restoring an iPhone using iTunes are as follows:

  1. Connect your iPhone to the computer (which you previously used to sync the device).
  2. In iTunes, select iPhone from the Devices list, then find the Summary at the top of the screen.
  3. If iTunes does not sync your iPhone automatically, do it manually (and confirm that the sync and backup were successful).
  4. Click Check for Updates. ITunes reports if there is a newer version of software for iPhone.
  5. Click Restore. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the recovery process. In this process, it is recommended that you back up your iPhone again when prompted.
  6. When the iOS Setup Assistant prompts you to set up your iPhone, select "Restore from iTunes Backup."
  7. Select your device in iTunes and find the latest backup of your device.

After this process, your iPhone should be restored with information from its last backup, but the password will no longer be activated on it. After that, think carefully before setting a different password or using this feature again.
