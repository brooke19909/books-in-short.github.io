Hotel Bonjour - a hotel with an hourly rate

The modern hotel services market offers many offers for tourists in Moscow: from simple hostels to luxury apartments. Which hotel to choose? How, without spending the extra money, get a quality service?

When planning a trip to Moscow, you should pay attention to the Bonjour chain of inexpensive and comfortable hotels, consisting of six addresses. The number of hotels is presented in different districts of the capital: in Western Degunino, Vykhino, Southern Butovo and Zelenograd. But hotels in the center of Moscow have a particularly good location: Dom Hotel in Podsosensky Lane and the Bonzhur Hotel on Kazakova Street.

Advantages of accommodation in Bonjour mini-hotels

All Bonjour chain hotels are hotels with affordable prices, excellent interiors, friendly staff and impeccable service.

Regardless of the location of the hotel, its guests can easily reach any metropolitan area on foot, by public or private transport.

But the most remarkable thing is that guests can check into the Bonjour hotel room at any convenient time of the day and for the number of hours necessary for them. Accordingly, payment for accommodation will depend on the chosen accommodation option. It can be hourly for the night spent (from 21:00 to 9:00) or, traditionally, daily.

Hourly hotel on Kazakova

In the center of the capital, just five hundred meters from the Kurskaya metro station, is the Bonzhur Hotel on Kazakova. Guests are only one metro stop from the Kremlin wall and Red Square.





The hotel can be an ideal option for comfortable accommodation both for ordinary tourists who come to see the sights of the capital, and business people who arrived in the city for a business meeting.

Guests are offered modern superior rooms of two categories: deluxe and junior suite. All rooms have beds with orthopedic mattresses, TV, air conditioning, a small neat refrigerator. There is a bathroom with all necessary hygiene items. When decorating the interior of the premises, various color schemes were used.

If you pay by the hour, the cost of accommodation will be 1250 rubles, while a day of accommodation will cost guests from 5200 to 6600 rubles.

Dom hotel

Another small hotel, which can be booked on the official website of the Bonjour mini-hotel chain, will also serve as an ideal starting point for a city sightseeing route. Dom Hotel is a ten-minute walk from Kursk Station and the metro station of the same name.





The hotel has only nine rooms and shared bathrooms, a kitchen. The room is designed for two or three guests. The table is not available in all rooms, its availability must be specified when booking. It offers a free light breakfast, Wi-Fi and bicycle rental.

The cost of living depends on the season and can vary from 2900 to 3600 rubles per day for a double room.

Hotel on Taldomskaya street

Mini-Hotel Bonjour on Taldomskaya Street is located in a favorable residential and picturesque area in the north of Moscow, a two-minute walk from Khovrino Train Station. This is one of the few new hotels opened over the past five years near the Petrovsko-Razumovskaya metro station.

The hotel has 17 rooms with varying degrees of comfort: standard, junior suite and suite. Each room has a private bathroom, comfortable beds, air conditioning, Wi-Fi, TV.





About the Bonjour Hotel, reviews from vacationers are mostly positive. First of all, it is chosen by tourists who do not want to deprive themselves of their usual comfort, but have a limited trip budget. A day in a double standard room of a hotel on Taldomskaya will cost 3,000 rubles, a night - 2,700 rubles, one hour - 800 rubles.

Here you can also stay for an hour (from 800 rubles per room), at night (from 2700 rubles) or for a day (from 4000 rubles).

Bonjour in Vykhino

Hotel for an hour "Bonjour Fergana" is located in the eastern district of the capital, in the modern residential district of Vykhino-Zhulebino. The nearest metro station is conveniently accessible by public transport.

Hotel Bonjour (Moscow, Vykhino) is small. The entire hotel room occupies one floor of a sixteen-story building. The hotel offers 26 modernly decorated rooms with private bathrooms. Each room has comfortable furniture and necessary equipment for a comfortable stay. As with any other hotel, the reception is open 24 hours a day. In addition to standard rooms, guests are offered superior rooms: suites and junior suites.





An hour of stay in the Bonjour standard in Vykhino will cost 800 rubles, a day - 3000 rubles, a night stay will cost guests 2700 rubles.

The Bonjour hotel chain is an ideal option for the level of comfort and cost of accommodation during short business trips or trips with the aim of exploring the sights of the city, romantic dates or transfers with a long wait between flights.

According to the reviews of many tourists who have already visited one or more of the chain's hotels, the Bonjour hotel fully met their expectations. It can be recommended to your friends and acquaintances.
