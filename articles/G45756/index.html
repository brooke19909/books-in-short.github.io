Adler - Moscow: train, timetable, ticket price

Russia is a huge country, which has great opportunities for recreation. If you want to have a great time on vacation, then you should think about the Black Sea, because this option will be more affordable and easier to travel abroad. Among the popular holiday destinations, one of the leading positions is occupied by the district of the city of Sochi.

Rest in Adler

Adler is one of the best resorts in Russia. It is part of the city of Sochi, which provides access to all the attractions, the number of which has grown significantly after the Olympics. The area has all the possibilities for entertainment and excellent relaxation: parks, dolphinariums, water parks, recreation areas, bars, cafes, restaurants and many other institutions. Moreover, one must not forget about the excellent sea and sun. Whether you are traveling with or without children, Adler is a great place for any type of vacation.

That is why more and more people every year choose Adler for their holidays. Moscow is the most convenient city for departure by plane or train.

A train

One of the most popular routes in summer is Moscow-Adler, with a length of 1648 km. The train is the easiest and cheapest way to get to the resort town. On this route there are several trains that are added to the season, because in summer there is a large flow of people. These trains are not new, but Russian Railways monitors their condition, constantly updates and makes repairs if necessary. In winter, trains depart only from the Kazan station, in summer, routes from Paveletsky and Kursky are added to them. Train travel times range from 24 to 41 hours, depending on the number and duration of stops.





Muscovites can also choose for themselves trains that depart from other cities, stopping by in the capital by passage. So, for example, you can get to Adler by train from St. Petersburg, Murmansk or other cities. The final destination, too, will not necessarily be Adler himself, the train can only make a stop in the city and go on. Such a wide selection allows you to choose the most convenient option not only by date and time, but also by cost. The same applies to trains on the return route Adler-Moscow.

According to the rules of Russian Railways, tickets can be purchased 45 days before departure. Please note that if you are going to the resort, you should buy a ticket not only one way, but also in the direction of Adler-Moscow, too, in advance, because otherwise you can simply not leave back in the season.

It is important to know that the train from Moscow to Adler allows you to get not only to Adler and Sochi, but also to many nearby Black Sea resorts. If you need to get to any other place of rest, then you can take the train to Adler, and then by bus or taxi to get to your destination.





Double decker train

One of the most interesting ways to travel on the Adler-Moscow route without a doubt is the new double-decker train number 104 with the beautiful name "Muscovy". He leaves from the Kazan station in Moscow.

The Moscow-Adler double-decker train was launched on November 1, 2013 and became the first and so far the only double-decker train in Russia, making serious competition to air travel. It has a number of advantages not only in the external and internal design, but also in other aspects.

One of the main features of the train is that there are no reserved seats in it; there are only coupes and SVs. The train running along the Moscow-Adler route even has separate TVs and showers for places of increased comfort.

In the usual compartment carriages of a double-decker train, there are kitchenettes with microwave ovens and sinks for washing dishes. Each compartment has 3 dry closets, which will avoid inconvenience. It is also very convenient that the developers added sockets for electrical appliances to the train.





In places where there is a signal, free Wi-Fi is available on the train, however, passengers note poor speed. Also, passengers claim that in the train, even despite two floors, high ceilings, almost like in the usual composition. Unlike other Russian models, the new one even has 2 places for disabled people.

Moreover, the Muscovy train gets to its destination faster by reducing the number of stops. So, the travel time on the Adler-Moscow route will be 24 hours. Also, by increasing seats for passengers, it was possible to reduce the cost of tickets compared to conventional trains by about 30%. But given that there are no reserved seats, it is not the cheapest. The train was developed using new technology, which eliminates the rolling and knocking of wheels during train movement.

Ticket price

Ticket prices vary greatly depending on the season, conditions and type of train.

The minimum ticket price for a reserved seat car starts at about 2,900 rubles. A ticket in a compartment car will cost the least in a double-decker train number 104 - about 3,600 rubles. According to Russian Railways, in the near future they are not going to change the cost of this train. The price of a seat in an SV car is from about 12,000 rubles.





Other options

People going to the resort are always considering different options for moving. So, in addition to the train, vacationers often choose a trip by car, bus or plane. Each of these options has both its advantages and disadvantages. For example, it is inconvenient to travel by car or bus for a long time in one position, and traveling by plane will be quite expensive. That is why most people choose the train for their travels as the best option.

Remember that if you decide to take the Moscow-Adler route, than to get tired by car, it is better to choose a train ride.
