How to decorate a bottle of champagne with do-it-yourself sweets

Packaging is an integral part of the gift. It is with her that the present looks complete. A sparkling drink is a symbol of the holiday, which is why people give it to each other so often. How to decorate a bottle of champagne with sweets, we will tell in this article.

Sharp decor



Just a few years ago, making candy bouquets became an incredibly popular activity. It is not surprising that such flowers have become part of the decor and other products. For example, bottles. True, in this case, flowers need to be made many times less, literally 3-7 pieces will suffice. How to decorate a bottle of champagne with sweets? We make flowers from corrugated paper, insert them into sweets and with the help of a hot gun we fix this decor on a bottle. This decoration can be supplemented with spikes made of wrapping paper. We turn the cones and glue them to the base of the bottle. To somehow combine the composition of flowers and spikes, we make leaves from green translucent paper. We glue them between the scattered decor. As an additional decoration, you can use spikelets of wheat or any dried flowers.

A pineapple



How to decorate a bottle of champagne with sweets? It is worth making a pineapple. It is done very simply. A bottle of champagne is wrapped with paper tape. You can not do this, but thanks to this step, the grip will increase. Now in rows you need to glue the sweets. It is advisable to use those sweets that have a gold or yellow wrapper. Candies must be round or square. Glue sweets to the bottle on a hot gun layer by layer. You need to stop when the candies hit the neck. Now you need to make weed. The easiest way to make it from thick tinted paper. Cut strips and sharpen their edges. Using the scissors, slightly wind the ends. Now glue the grass to the neck of the bottle with a glue gun. To convey the gift to the guests safe and sound, you need to wrap the bottle in transparent paper and tie it with a beautiful bow.

Gift for any holiday



How to decorate a bottle of champagne with sweets? You can combine two decor techniques. For example, decoupage a bottle of champagne with bright napkins, and make a wreath of sweets on top. Let's get started. Remove the label from the champagne with alcohol. Degrease the bottle and glue napkins using PVA. If you've never done decoupage before, you can color the bottle in a different way. We paint the workpiece with glass paints and draw all kinds of patterns on it. The image of lace and snowflakes looks beautiful. Now you need to make a decor of sweets. From wrapping paper we wrap something like a plate. We fix it in this position with hot silicone. Now in a chaotic manner we fill the plate with sweets, beads, twigs and berries. Naturally, we glue everything. Using a hot gun, we fix the decoration on a pre-prepared bottle.

Christmas tree



How to decorate a bottle of champagne with tinsel and sweets? Make a Christmas tree from an alcoholic drink. It is very simple. Lubricate the bottle with glue and wrap the green tinsel with a snake. We wait until the workpiece dries. Glue colored balls and candies to the tinsel. You can attach them at random, or you can create the correct circles or other geometric shapes. Looks nice option when the tree is designed in one color or at least in one color scheme. As an additional decor it is worth sticking ribbons, beads or bells. You can even put on a short Christmas tree garland that runs on batteries.

Champagne in a box



Such a gift can be presented to friends or parents for the New Year. You can decorate a bottle of champagne with sweets with your own hands in literally an hour. You will need a box, colored paper, twigs, chocolate bar and other sweets. The basis of the composition will be a cardboard container. It can be done independently or you can take any box available in the house and suitable in size. We tighten the cardboard with wrapping paper. In the center of the box we attach a bottle of champagne. Why mount it? So that during transportation the bottle does not move anywhere. Each candy is wrapped in shiny paper and attached to the bottom of the box. Together with sweets, you can put Christmas balls as a decor . The background of the composition will be occupied by a beautifully wrapped chocolate bar and a silver forest. Trees are made from branches. They are painted white or silver and attached to the bottom of the box. You can glue candy on improvised trees.

Flower decoration



This decor method is very similar to the item about pineapple. Only with the difference that here you can use sweets of any color and any shape. Photo of a bottle of champagne decorated with sweets, you can see above. What is needed to repeat this idea? Wrap the bottle with paper tape. Starting from the bottom in rows, glue the sweets. At the throat of the bottle we stop. Here you need to tie a beautiful bright bow, as well as attach an artificial flower. It can be created from corrugated paper and put candy inside.

Bunch of grapes



How to decorate a bottle of champagne with sweets? Step by step to make the decor, repeating the shape of a branch of grapes, you can in just two hours. This will require round candies and gold, green or purple foil. We unfold the sweets and make them a new wrapper. When all the candies have the same desired color, glue them to the bottle. We do this with a hot gun. First, lay out the silhouette of the bunch, and then fill it with sweets. The second and third layers give volume to the branch of grapes. From green paper you need to cut the leaves and paste them between the berries. Colored tendrils can be used to make antennae typical of grapes. At the base of the bunch, a volume bow should be tied.

Bottle in bucket



An original gift can be made from improvised materials. How to decorate a bottle of champagne with sweets? To do this, you need a plastic flower pot. You can immediately take a product of a pretty color or give it the desired shade with paint. When our bucket is ready, insert a bottle inside it. Glue red sheets of tinted paper to the back of the pot. This wall will perform not only a decorative, but also a practical function, holding sweets in a bucket. Each candy is wrapped in brown paper and put in a bucket. A bucket of sweets needs to be filled with a slide.

Bottle in boot



A gift that has an unusual shape looks original. For example, a bottle decorated with a boot. How to make such a present? To do this, from any dense material, plywood, chipboard or plastic, you need to cut the shape of the sole from the boot. It should look like a bob. A bottle of champagne needs to be glued a little further than the heel. Now you should wrap the sparkling drink in a floral grid. She looks beautiful, and it will be easy to cling to the decor. Now proceed to the decoration. Glue the branches of a Christmas tree, moss or any other greenery to the top of the sole. We place candies, balls, beads and berries on it. We decorate a floral grid with small candies or colored beads. On the throat of the bottle, you can attach colored balls, sweets or tie a decorative ribbon.
