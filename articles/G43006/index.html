How to choose an electric grill with removable panels?

When choosing an electric grill, the main parameter that you need to pay attention to is the power of the device. For different models, it can vary from 0.7 kW to 2.2 kW. Actually, the cooking speed depends on this characteristic.

The best option is a double-sided grill, which greatly simplifies and speeds up the cooking process. It is advisable to take into account the possibility of adjusting the height of the working surface: with the maximum lifting of the top panel, you can cook whole vegetables or large pieces of meat. If the rear legs of the electric grill are adjustable in height, the device can be tilted so that the fat formed during cooking flows into a special container.

Materials for work surfaces and housing

Most electric grills have a metal casing with non-stick coated work surfaces, which greatly simplifies and facilitates the process of caring for them and eliminates the need to add oil during cooking. This feature of household appliances is unbelievably important for those who prefer to eat diet foods.

The enamel coating of the working surfaces of the electric grill, despite its advantages, does not differ in a long service life, so you should not purchase such models: it is better to limit yourself to a classic non-stick coating.

The case of cheap models of electric grills is made of high quality plastic, however, they all have a heat-resistant coating.

Additional functions

  • Special indicators that greatly simplify the cooking process increase the cost of electric grill. Despite their effectiveness, there is no particular need for such an additional function.
  • Electronic grills allow you to adjust the temperature of the heating surfaces and the speed of convection.
  • Auto power off and timer.
  • The function of maintaining the set temperature for a set time to keep the cooked dish warm.
  • Delayed start function. It is convenient for those cases when it is necessary to turn on the electric grill by a certain time.
  • Handles with thermal insulation to protect against burns.
