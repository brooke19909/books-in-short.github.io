How to tuck sleeves on a shirt: common ways

How to properly turn the sleeves on the shirt? Stylists offer men several options. Each of them allows you to bring adventurism and laid-back charm to the image. The most relevant solutions will be considered later in the material presented.

Why roll up the sleeves of a shirt?



The need for rolling up the sleeves can arise not only if you need to wash your hands, but also for a number of other practical reasons, for example, when you need to make sure that your clothes are not dirty. On some jackets and shirts in the middle of the last century there were special snap buttons at the elbows, which made it possible to fix the sleeves in a tucked position. There are clothing manufacturers who still put similar accessories on their products.

Knowing how to tuck sleeves on a shirt may be needed on hot summer days. In this case, the body will gain access to fresh air. However, this option is only appropriate on the street and does not correspond to the office etiquette of most companies that pay attention to observing a strict dress code.

Currently, young people are increasingly tucking up their sleeves for fashion reasons. This decision allows you to make the image in a classic style more informal. A shirt with rolled up sleeves is suitable for going to bars and restaurants, attending parties, as well as any public events where you do not have to worry about observing strict rules of etiquette.

When is it inappropriate to tuck sleeves on a shirt?

It is not recommended to resort to such actions:

  • When you are at work, if the position you are forcing to form an image, which corresponds to the business style.
  • At official events.
  • When organizing meetings with clients, business partners.

How to tuck the sleeves on the shirt in a basic way?



An option that is used for work purposes does not have specific rules. It is worth acting on an intuitive level. It is enough to unfasten all the buttons on the sleeves. Then you can fold the cuffs in layers or in a tube. The main thing is to roll up the sleeves above the elbow and fix them securely so that they do not get dirty during the work.

How to tuck sleeves on a shirt in an elegant Italian style?



To resort to the following method is for the adherents of the classic style in clothes. An important point here is that the shirt fabric from the inside should have a slightly contrasting shade with respect to the front side of the product. The presence of a colored lining will bring a stylish, original zest to the image.

How to beautifully tuck sleeves on an Italian-style shirt? To do this, follow these steps:

  • open the cuffs;
  • to turn out the sleeves, not reaching the level of the elbow joint;
  • to break the edge of the sleeve so that only part of the cuff is superimposed on it;
  • adjust the open contrasting part of the sleeves in width so that the cuffs on both hands look symmetrical.

Roll up the sleeve to the elbow



How to tuck the sleeves on the shirt to the elbow? If you do not want to bare your arms too much, you should slightly tuck the cuffs on the shirt without going beyond the elbow. In this case, you need to unfasten the buttons on the sleeves. Next, tighten the cuffs from the wrist several turns. In conclusion, it is enough to smooth the folds and creases, and then fasten the outer button on the sleeve.

The specified option looks organically during the lunch break, when traveling in transport, during a walk after work. Such a somewhat informal twist on the sleeves of the shirt will be combined with chinos, as well as moccasins or light sneakers, worn on the bare foot.

Finally

It is recommended that absolutely every man figure out how to tuck sleeves on a shirt with a button in several versions. Wearing a shirt with rolled up cuffs is not only fashionable today. In some cases, it is simply necessary to twist the sleeves, for example, when performing work in the course of which there is a high probability of getting dirty. Some men resort to this option of wearing shirts, wanting to once again emphasize their impudent style, as well as to make their arms in the biceps area seem a little larger.
