Philips (TVs): reviews. Philips TVs: prices, specifications

The Philips company was founded in 1891 by the young entrepreneur Gerrard Philips. At the very beginning of his career, he was engaged in the production of light bulbs. In 1895, his brother Anton Phillips joined Gerrard.

In 1917, the company was forced to curtail its production due to the difficult situation in the country. However, starting in 1918, Gerrard and Anton continued to produce light bulbs. In parallel, the production of medical tubes for radiography was in progress. Beginning in 1930, television broadcasting was actively advertised. Given the new trends, the Philips company abruptly switched to the production of radios, and then electric gramophones. Since 1932, absolutely all electric lamp devices have been manufactured under this brand.





Production of the first Philips TVs

The first models of TVs "Philips" the world saw in 1964. At that time it was already possible to talk about color TV. Soon they became very popular, and the demand was simply colossal. Initially, it all started with the invention of stereo audio technology. Also, the production of the first Philips TVs was influenced by the discovery of new sound carriers. As a result, scientists were able to achieve high quality sound transmission. Further, it remained only to continue work to improve the transmitted image.
