Quarry in Sychevo: description of how to get

The quarry in Sychevo is one of the attractions of the Volokolamsk district of the Moscow region. Many tourists, lovers of fishing and beach holidays come here. Real sand dunes and clear lakes with warm water give the landscape an unusual look and attract attention. This amazing place is not a natural anomaly. It was created by human hands during the extraction of sand and gravel in these parts.

A bit of history

Sychevsky quarry is located less than 100 km from the capital. The nearest settlement is the village of Sychevo, Volokolamsk district. In the 1960s, they began to extract sand and gravel, which are used for construction. The Sychevsky non - metallic mining and processing plant was founded , which is still operating. Today, the field continues to be developed and is operational.

It should be noted that non-metallic materials mined here are of high quality. They were used for the construction of metropolitan buildings and structures, such as the Cathedral of Christ the Savior, the memorial complex on Poklonnaya Hill, the Moscow Ring Road, as well as many residential buildings.





Career description

Part of the quarries still remain operational, you can see working excavators in them. The other part - is now fully developed, filled with water and is a lake. The area has a rather unusual appearance: quarries filled with water of blue or greenish color, old abandoned towers and barges, and around - shores that look like sand dunes.

The territory of the quarry in Sychevo is quite extensive, it looks like a square with a side length of about 8 km. Here you can find dwarf plants, which are very rare for central Russia.

Beach vacation

There are several beaches in Sychevsky quarries. Tourists who visited these places, counted about 5 zones for recreation on the water. It is not very crowded, since the road to the quarries is difficult. It can also be noted that the shores of the lakes are covered with clean sand, which is rarely found even on the famous beaches near Moscow.





Water is unclear, as it has a sandy admixture. The lakes are deep enough and you can swim in them. To date, the beaches look wild, they have no facilities for recreation. There are not yet organized locker rooms, cafes, toilets, umbrellas. In the future, this recreation area is planned to be ennobled.

Sometimes diving enthusiasts come here. Depth allows you to dive, and in some quarries, transparency is quite high. All diving enthusiasts note that the water in the lakes warms up well on hot days. Cool currents are observed only at great depths.

Other attractions

Fishermen come to the quarry in Sychevo, Volokolamsk district. The fauna of the lakes is quite rich and diverse. It is difficult to count on a large catch, but you can catch a lot of small fish: crucian carp, roach, perch, and undergrowth. In some quarries, you can catch predatory fish.





Sandboarding lovers love to gather here. This sport is not snowboarding in the snowy mountains, but in the dunes and sand dunes. Sychevsky quarry is an ideal place for such entertainment. And also fans of ATVs and cross-country motorbikes come here to practice riding on a sandy hill.

How to get there by car

At first glance, it seems that driving to quarries in Sychevo is not difficult. You need to leave the Moscow Ring Road on Novorizhskoye Shosse, drive about 98 km, then turn onto Sychevo and drive another 5 km to the quarries. But the last section of the road will run along the loose sand, and it is very insidious, the car is easy to land. In addition, large trucks often drive past, which raise strong sand dust. You must consider all the features of such a road before you go on a trip. The path is likely to be difficult, but people come here to see the desert near Moscow.





At the entrance to the quarry in Sychevo, you can find a barrier and an attendant. The territory belongs to the mining and processing plant and is protected. However, cars are usually allowed to pass through without hindrance, since the guards know that people come to developed careers for tourist purposes. It is not recommended only to take photographs of excavators and other working equipment.

How to get there by public transport

How to get to the quarries in Sychevo by public transport? From Moscow you can get an electric train from the Riga station to the city of Volokolamsk. Then transfer to the local bus 24 routes and get off at the final stop "Sychevo".

You can catch an electric train from the Belorussky railway station and get to the Tuchkovo platform, transfer to bus 21 of the route and get to Ruza. From there the bus 30 runs to Sychevo.

From Moscow to Sychevo can be reached without using rail. To do this, you need to get to the Tushinskaya metro station, then take the bus 307 of the route. He goes to the city of Volokolamsk and passes through Sychevo.
