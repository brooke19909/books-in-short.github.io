Hotels in Kolomna: the best and budget options

Kolomna is one of the oldest cities in the Moscow region. The first mention of it dates back to the XII century. This city has more than 400 historical and cultural monuments. Among them are the Kolomna Kremlin, the Assumption Cathedral, ancient merchant houses in the Baroque style. What hotels in Kolomna are worth it to stay in?

The best hotel of the city

The most comfortable and expensive hotel in the city is the Kolomna Hotel. It occupies the tallest building in the town.

The windows offer a wonderful view of the surroundings. The guests are offered excellent rooms of the "standard" and "luxury" classes, which are provided with the necessary equipment and furniture. The hotel offers guests a restaurant, a conference room, a billiard room and a very interesting gift shop. In the tourist center of the hotel you can pick up an excursion to your taste. Fans of sightseeing themselves should know that Orthodox churches, historical monuments, and a speed skating center are located nearby.

Hostels of Kolomna

Hotels in Kolomna are not only expensive rooms and stylish interiors. For people who prefer a budget vacation or business trip, a good option would be to stop in one of the hostels of the city. Which of them can I pay attention to?

  • Hostel Like - is located in the city center. A few minutes walk from it are the Kremlin, Blacksmithing Village, a large number of bars and cafes. Shared kitchen, iron, washing machine, Internet access for guests.
  • Hostel "Swallow" is only three kilometers from the city center. Like other Kolomna hotels, the hostel offers guests free WI-FI, parking. No smoking in the building.
  • Hostel Slavyanskiy - located 3 km from the city center, a 15-minute walk from the banks of the Oka. By the way, the ancient city is so popular in Russia that even in St. Petersburg itself there is a Hotel "House in Kolomna", glorifying this wonderful place.
  • Hostel "Old Town" - located 4 km from the historic center of Kolomna.
  • Hostel Be Happy - pleases tourists with the possibility of accommodation with pets. Located 3 km from the center.

Hotels in Kolomna: good and affordable options

Kolomna is a city with a well-developed infrastructure, so there is no shortage of places where you can spend the night. What other hotels are there?

  • Hotel "Sovetskaya" in Kolomna - is located right in the city center. Very close to it are many attractions.
    The rooms, although decorated in the Soviet style (as follows from the name of the place), are very clean and comfortable.
  • “Central Estate” - tourists like the place with a wide range of services. Located within walking distance from all attractions, the hotel is one of the most popular among tourists.
  • "Sunny Olympus" - the complex is located near the city in a coniferous forest. This hotel is chosen by youth and athletes.

Hotels in Kolomna vary in pricing and equipment. Everyone can choose a placement to their taste. During the tourist season, many hotels are crowded, so it is better to take care of your reservation in advance.
