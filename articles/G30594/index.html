Holidays in Montenegro - Adriatic Charm

Holidays in Montenegro is the choice of tourists who value the opportunity to be alone with nature. Fans of silence, families with children and those who do not want to spend huge sums on vacation, will be satisfied with their vacation in this calm country.

about the country

This small country located on the Balkan Peninsula welcomes travelers all year round. In summer, here you will find a cozy beach holiday, in winter - ski resorts.

A visa is not required to enter Montenegro, as a visa-free regime is valid for up to 30 days.



sights

  The visiting card of this country is the island of St. Stephen and the monastery of Ostrog carved in the rock . Beautiful views will delight Boka Kotor Bay. The historic cities of Budva, Ulcinj, Herceg Novi and the former capital of Cetinje, where the royal palace is located, are rich in historical buildings, monasteries and churches.

Visit Skadar Lake, which is the largest and cleanest in the Balkans, where pelicans live and karan fish live.

At the very top of the Lovcen mountains is the mausoleum of Peter Nyegos - the famous ruler and spiritual enlightener of Montenegro.

Podgorica - the capital of Montenegro will offer you to get acquainted with the monastery of Celia Piper.

The most popular tourist area is the Budva Riviera. It occupies the central coast of Montenegro. This place is considered the sunniest in the Adriatic and even Europe.

The beaches of Montenegro stretch more than 70 km along the sea. You can find both sandy beaches and artificial concrete platforms as a beach cover.



Climate

It has a temperate continental climate, in the mountains - alpine, and on the Adriatic coast - Mediterranean. The coast is much warmer than in the central part of the country. Summer in the coastal region is long, dry and quite hot. The swimming season starts at the end of April and lasts until the end of October. Therefore, beach holidays in Montenegro have a fairly long period. The average air temperature in the summer is 28 degrees, and the water in the sea for seven months is not lower than 20-26 degrees. Holidays in Montenegro are ideal for lovers of warmth.



Hotels

Prices for accommodation in hotels on the Adriatic coast and in the Bay of Kotor are low, but in the interior of the country they almost reach European. The level of service in hotels at the same time to the European, obviously, still falls short.

A popular type of accommodation is accommodation in villas. The only drawback of the convenient location in the villas is their some distance from the sea - 300-500 meters, but this is not scary for tourists.



All the possibilities for relaxation

The best resorts give travelers a great mood and energy. In addition to the sea, you will find many good bars, restaurants and discos. Sports facilities are available for tourists . There are all conditions for water sports in the country. Therefore, a vacation in Montenegro does not seem boring to you.
