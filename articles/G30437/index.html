Where to get a pension for a pensioner? And can this be done at all?

As an analysis of the product lines of some Russian banks shows , a loan to a pensioner has been in great demand over the past few years. However, taking it is not so simple. Although the bankers themselves admit that the mentioned category of customers is one of the most conscientious and obligatory. By the way, employees of housing maintenance offices could have said the same. If, of course, it occurred to someone to ask them.

Next, we look at several financial institutions where it is not so difficult to get a loan to a pensioner. Although, of course, the special age of this category of clients and the modest amount of income make lenders behave very cautiously. It should also be added that the above programs are not universal. That is, we will not dwell on what kind of loan to a pensioner in Sberbank (consumer, you need to return when you reach 75 years old), we will not.
So, the first who offered a loan to a pensioner was Sovcombank. His program provides that a citizen of Russia can apply for a loan, who will fully repay it before he turns 85 years old. The maximum amount is 250 thousand rubles (or less). Interest rate - 28% per annum. This offer can be used by both working and non-working pensioners (old age, disability, who left early). You can also use the Express Pension program: up to 30 thousand rubles at 33% per annum. In this case, only a civil passport is needed.
Also, a loan to a pensioner can be issued by the Agricultural Bank. As part of a special program, loans are issued in the amount of up to 100 thousand rubles. Moreover, the customer pays for use only 15% per annum.
