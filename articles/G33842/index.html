A variety of crafts from milk boxes

Boxes of juice or milk can become very unusual and useful. They can make wonderful toys for kids. Children especially value what they have done themselves. And nobody needs this material, which can be found in any home. Parents will not swear if something does not work out of the box and they will have to throw it away. But children's imagination will tell you what can be done from the used box. Parents can be proud of such a child who can use improvised material for their work. A toy made by a baby alone or together with parents will bring more joy than bought in a store.





If the child cannot figure out how to use the used thing, he needs to be prompted and helped in the development of creative abilities and imagination.

Here are some examples of what crafts from milk boxes can be done during classes with the baby.

Wallet

Children will always find many little things. So that they are not scattered throughout the room, you can offer to make a wallet. Of course, dairy bags will be used for the manufacture.

First you need to make a pattern-blank, depending on what form the wallet will be. Cut, glue seams-joints with adhesive tape.

Feeders





The lightest crafts from milk boxes are bird feeders. In winter, it will be very useful and informative for kids. Birds will fly to visit under the window, and the child will pour food and wait for birds. To do this, you need to make cuts in the package from several sides, a variety of birds should fly through them to taste the grains. The bottom of the package will serve as a table. A rope is attached at the top, and the feeder is suspended from a tree branch. You can paint in the form of a house by drawing windows and doors. Fly in, dear guests, help yourself!

Cubes

And this is not all the crafts from milk boxes. Take, for example, constructors that use cubes. Why not make these elements of homemade houses yourself? These details will turn out even better than the purchased ones, because they can be decorated with pictures, painted on their own, use bright self-adhesive paper to decorate them.

Houses and hotels





Crafts from milk boxes are so simple that every child can easily. What beautiful doll houses with garages and farming can be! What a flight of imagination can occur not only in kids, but also in adults when decorating such buildings! And such houses can "grow" with the acquisition of a new tenant, you can adjust them floor by floor. It may turn out to be a wonderful hotel or apartment building. Let all his tenants meet and be friends, let them communicate with each other, spend holidays together and be kinder and more attentive to their neighbors in spite of reality.

There is a garage, so there must be a transport. This is no longer a luxury, but a means of transportation. Why not make a boat for fishing enthusiasts or a boat for boat trips?

Ship

Parents will prompt and help their child make a wide variety of crafts from boxes of juice and milk. A beautiful boat that can be launched in the bathroom or in a village stream. It is painted with a special waterproof paint and is not saturated with moisture.

If someone carefully looks at the design of the milk box, it will become clear that this is an already finished house with a roof. Crafts from milk boxes for babies will be a real gift. After all, mothers and fathers will not allow the child to work alone with scissors and an office knife. They will always be there. Isn't that what every kid wants? Communicate, feel the support of an adult native person - this is so pleasant and important for the full education of the child.

Stages of making crafts

To make crafts from cardboard boxes with your own hands, there are several standard simple operations. First, the box must be thoroughly washed and dried.

Paste with plain paper if it is planned for the child to paint the craft on his own or with bright colored paper in accordance with which toy is planned.
