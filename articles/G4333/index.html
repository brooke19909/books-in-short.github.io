The easiest scarf collar

The product being studied, like the other types of scarf, is noteworthy in that for its implementation does not require excessive knowledge. Just the basic basics of knitting are enough. If a beginner knows how to type, knit the front and wrong, and then properly close the loops, you can safely proceed with the do-it-yourself snood.

Necessary tools and materials:

  • two knitting needles with one working end (you can take circular ones);
  • yarn of the desired color, composition, quality.

How to knit a scarf collar with knitting needles?

  1. Each needlewoman can determine the width and length of any product independently. The only thing if you want to make a scarf pipe is to knit the product in a circle using hosiery knitting needles. We will first consider a simple snud.
  2. So, we put on the knitting needles of the number of loops. And thereby determine the width of the future accessory.
  3. We remove the first knitting needle, we knit all the others, except the last, as well as the front ones. The latter is the wrong side.
  4. Now go to the next row. We also remove the first one, we knit the rest as if they were purl.
  5. Repeat the third and fourth steps until we reach the desired scarf length.
  6. Then close the hinges without tightening them much.
  7. Sew the ends of the snood from the wrong side. We can say that we close the question of how to knit a scarf collar.
