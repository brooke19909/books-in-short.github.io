Places where you can sell coins

The opportunity to benefit from unnecessary details is certainly a pleasant thing. Today, a real boom has risen in the coin market. This is due to erroneous announcements, an abundance of information about the possibility of getting thousands for ruble coins.

Coins are in our hands for various reasons. Some come with us from abroad, others we accidentally find among old grandmother's things.

Still others may lie for decades in a children's piggy bank they forgot about. Having discovered such a "treasure", many are wondering if it is possible to make money on it. Indeed, where can I sell coins that the store will not accept? Let's try to figure it out.

Of course, as the first place where you can sell old coins, you need to consider numismatist societies. This type of collecting has not lost its popularity for many years, and the army of fans and collectors of banknotes is steadily replenished with new participants.



In almost any city, you can find a numismatist club or a place where collectors regularly gather to exchange or buy coins and banknotes. If earlier you could learn about such a community only from acquaintances or from advertisements in the newspaper, then modern collector lovers actively communicate on the Internet. Moreover, the network itself can be considered as a platform where you can sell coins. It is here that you can clarify the approximate cost of your existing copies and even find a buyer.

Another place where you can sell coins of the USSR or another state are various shops and pavilions located in large shopping centers, museums or galleries, as well as shops selling antique gizmos. They, as a rule, are engaged in the purchase and sale of coins, household items or works of art belonging to different eras. Here you will also be able to give advice on the value of the banknotes that you have on hand.

Do not believe the words of the first expert who will give you advice or assess. If you are not an expert in the field of numismatics (and the question "Where can I sell coins?" Indicates this), then the chance that they will tell you the real value is slightly reduced. Those who do this business professionally will not tell the amateur the true value of even the most expensive coin in the world. Most likely, the price will be intentionally reduced.





In order to protect yourself from such options, before you go to a place or site where you can sell coins, spend some time studying the existing market, find the basic terms of numismatists, look at the list of the most expensive coins. Who knows, maybe your coin is worth tens of thousands. But I want to warn that there are not very many chances that you can enrich yourself by selling the contents of the old piggy bank. Therefore, you can hear that your find is worth nothing. Do not be disappointed if the value of the coins does not exceed several hundred rubles. The opportunity to gain new knowledge, the excitement of searching and comparing is a worthy reward. So in any case, you will not stay in the loser.
