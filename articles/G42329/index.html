Location

The hotel is located in a resort area called South Sinai. Hotel Sea Life Resort 5, as already mentioned, stands right on the beach. In addition, it is not only the Red Sea, but also one of the most beautiful coral bays of Sharm - Nabq Bay. The hotel is not far from the airport - five kilometers, but the planes do not interfere with relaxation and rest. From here you can reach Naama Bay. About ten kilometers from the hotel to the center of this resort, and only five to the famous Soho Square - the most European place of Sharm.



How to get and when it is better to go

Egyptian hotels typically offer airport transfers. But many tourists prefer to travel to the center of the village on their own to take a walk along the promenade, buy fruit, or go to a nightclub or disco. To do this, you can use a taxi (they are not particularly expensive here) or blue minibuses that go along the highway quite regularly and make stops at each hotel at the request of passengers. The climate in Naama Bay is fairly even. The air temperature practically does not rise above thirty-five and does not fall below twenty-three. Water is never colder than 22 degrees, even in winter. However, in these places there are windy days and storms.



What do we know about this resort?

Sea Group hotels have always had a great location on the seashore. And this applies not only to Sharma. In Turkey, for example, the hotel of this chain - Sea Life Family Resort 5 - is located near one of the most beautiful bays of the Mediterranean Sea. But our hotel will not yield to him in anything. Nabq Bay, where the Egyptian Sea Life Hotel is located, geographically refers to Naama Bay Resort. And this is a vacation spot, verified by many generations of tourists. Hotels for lovers of the sea, fish and coral began to build here a very long time. Here, restaurants, nightclubs, shops, shops and boutiques stretch for miles. The local Eastern Bazaar is very good - the reviews of tourists about it are the most enthusiastic. It is advised to change currency not at the hotel, but at the local banks - on the streets of Naama Bay a very favorable rate. In general, this resort is considered the most landscaped part of Sharm El Sheikh.

Territory and infrastructure

As already mentioned, this hotel complex consists of three parts - Sea Life, Club and Garden. The latter is on the second line, and from it farthest to go to the sea. The hotel has a good design, the buildings are made of red brick and built in the traditional Nubian style. These are small two-three-story houses, each of which has eight to ten rooms. There is a bank at Sea Life Resort 5 where you can exchange money for local currency. There is a pharmacy, as well as several shops with papyrus, perfumes and other Egyptian souvenirs. You can park the car. It is very green, and there is where to walk. In a word, this is a real oasis in the desert. Everywhere flowers, trimmed bushes, palm trees. The garden is constantly engaged, washed paths. Since there are several hotels here, the territory is not only beautiful and tastefully decorated, but also huge. Many fountains, bridges, arbors. The most pleasant-looking and stylish gardens are in Sea Life.



Rooms for living and everything in them

In the hotel Sea Life Resort 5 - almost three hundred rooms. Basically, they are standard, but very spacious (thirty-six square meters) and meet all the standards of such a five-star hotel. Satellite channels on television (five to six Russian), minibar, safe. The rooms are air-conditioned, cool in the heat. Nice modern bathroom with a spacious shower, bidet and hairdryer. There is a kettle, as well as bags of coffee, sugar. The rooms, according to vacationers, are spacious and at the same time very comfortable, they are pleasant to spend time after the beach or excursions. Cleaning is done every day. All equipment works well. Almost all balconies offer a beautiful view, so giving money when settling is unnecessary. Coffee, tea, soap, gels, shampoos and other little things are constantly updated.

Sea Life Resort Hotel 5 features an internet cafe. The hotel has its own taxi service. You can give clothes for washing and cleaning. The Sea Club Hotel, which is part of the complex described here, can be used not only for relaxation, but also for various business meetings and seminars. Animation and entertainment

Sea Life has a lot of different opportunities for sports lovers. Two tennis courts (they are lit at night) and one to play volleyball. There is a place and equipment for board games - including billiards. Two grass fields for football. On the beach in the Sea Club is a diving center where you can learn the art of deep sea swimming. In addition, animation shows and discos are organized in the evenings. All such events take place in the amphitheater, the programs practically do not repeat. In different hotels, the animation is different, so many go to other hotels in the complex for comparison. Some tourists claim that in the "Club" entertainment is more active. Many different services for children: high chairs in the restaurant, cribs in the room, swimming pool, 2 playgrounds, two clubs (open by the hour). There is a children's disco. You can invite the nanny to take care of the child. Therefore, this hotel can also be called Sea Life Family Resort 5, as well as its Turkish counterpart. After all, families with children will feel much better here than at home. And the Egyptians are very fond of kids.

The staff at Sea Life Resort 5 Sharm are excellent, based on reviews. Good-natured, smiling, responds to any question, almost everyone speaks good Russian. No rudeness, no conflicts. All problems are solved very quickly. Placed quickly - in a few minutes. People are very nice, friendly, serve vacationers at the highest level. The waiters are extremely quick, the bartenders are funny and executive. Gardeners perfectly maintain order and comfort throughout. Good management. Sun, air and water

No wonder this hotel is called Sea Life Resort 5. Charm is really famous for its corals, fish and underwater life. And here at your service is a huge magnificent beach - four hundred and fifty meters of sand. The coral reef right here, you can say, at your feet. No need to go anywhere to watch all this beauty. A variety of beach accessories - umbrellas, sunbeds and deck chairs - enough for everyone. There is a shallow part where children can swim. True, deeper into the sea - strong currents, so you need to be careful. In the windy season, swimming is allowed only after lunch. However, fish can be seen from the pontoon at the Sea Club Hotel and even from shallow water. By the number of fish and corals, this is one of the most beautiful places. The complex has several pools that belong to three hotels, and in all of them you can swim living in Sea Life.

Let's look around - where can I go?

Not very far from the hotel there is a water park belonging to it, but it is paid for vacationers. From the vicinity, it is advised to visit the Soho Square shopping and entertainment center on their own. There you can spend the evening and the whole day, and come with children - no one will be offended, and everyone will find entertainment. In addition, well-known bands often perform on the Soho stage, and there is always the opportunity to listen to good live music. Animators in the evenings can take to paid discos in the city. Of the nightclubs of Naama Bay, they recommend Le Pasha and Hard Rock Cafe. As always, desert safaris on ATVs are very popular in Egyptian resorts . Excellent sea excursions are offered by the diving center. They are also cheaper here. Many tourists use their stay in the South Sinai and travel to the Sea Life Resort 5 hotel not only to visit the surroundings, but also to get to know Israel and Jordan.

How much money do you need to relax here?

Of course, this is not the cheapest hotel. The price of a tour for two for a week with a flight and an all-inclusive system starts from fifty thousand rubles (or one thousand two hundred dollars). But if you want a good and memorable vacation, then do not regret it. Entrance to the water park on the territory of Sea Life Resort 5 (Egypt) costs about fifteen dollars, but families with children recommend it. Smoke a hookah in Sea Club will cost $ 10. To go to Soho by taxi, you will pay ten dollars, and you can return by bus for seven or eight. But there is a difference in prices. A yacht with diving - fifty dollars at the hotel, 35 - in the center for deep sea swimming. Therefore, many are advised to take excursions from Egyptian tour operators.
