Aprelka Lake (Kemerovo Region) - we heal the body and soul

In the Guryevsky district the medical lake Aprelka is located. The Kemerovo region is rich in water bodies, but it is recommended that both locals and visitors go to this lake to relax. Previously, clay was mined here, so a quarry turned out, which eventually flooded with water. The uniqueness of the lake is in its shores. Their component is colored clay, which has healing properties.



Geographical location and natural differences

The affectionate lake Aprelka is located in the village of the same name, which is part of the Ur settlement of the Guryevsky district of the Kemerovo region. This is an artificial pond. It was formed due to underground sources and rainfall at the site of an abandoned clay mining.

The depth of the reservoir is not exactly known, since hydrologists (scientists involved in the study of water resources) did not systematize and did not study it. According to local residents, there is almost an abyss - from 70 to 100 meters. Given the location, this could very well be.

The bottom of the reservoir is clayey with a small admixture of sand, uneven, with sharp drops. The shores from almost all sides of the lake are steep, covered with trees and shrubs to the very edge of the water, and in the rain it is also very slippery. During spring and autumn floods, the coastal forest belt is partially flooded.

Aprelka Lake (Kemerovo Region) is very clear, the water is clear, without algae, amazing sky-blue or even turquoise color. The coloration persists in cloudy and sunny weather.

The world

Lakeside territory is buried in the greenery of birches, bird cherry trees, willows, Christmas trees, pines and is famous for its rich harvest of mushrooms. In the depths of Aprelka live crucians and perches.

Some facts

The name Aprelka Lake (Kemerovo Region) owes its location on the outskirts of a small settlement of the same name. Local old-timers say that earlier gold was excavated at the site of the reservoir, the low content of which makes the water healing.



Hiking trails

The terrain of Aprilki is truly amazing! But tourists from all over the world come here not only to admire nature. The main value of the lake is clay, used for various "medical" purposes. Red clay improves blood circulation and improves immunity, blue - rejuvenates, softens and whitens the skin, yellow - anesthetizes, white - strengthens hair and eliminates acne, green - invigorates.

Half of the travelers arrive at Lake Aprelyka (Kemerovo region) for several hours, the rest set up tents and stayed for a couple of days to go fishing a bit, buy their fill in healing waters and improve their health.

Fishing here does not pamper with a rich catch, but if it is not the main goal, then it is quite possible to sit with fishing rods on the shore.

There are few places where you can put up a tent because of the steep slopes, and a lot of people who want to. Therefore, it is worthwhile to correctly calculate the travel time in order to arrive here early.
There are no beaches as such on April, decent approaches to water are only two or three, but this does not bother those seeking to plunge into the blue waters.
