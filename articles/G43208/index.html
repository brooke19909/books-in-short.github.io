Hotel El Mouradi Mahdia 5 * (Mahdia, Tunisia): photos and reviews of tourists

Tunisia is one of the most popular tourist destinations for tourists around the world. Vacationers can relax here with great comfort. The warm sea, clean beaches with fine white sand, excellent infrastructure - all this, of course, is available in Tunisia. Of course, like in any other resort, there are many hotels in this state that offer tourists both excellent comfortable rooms and not so much. In this article, we will understand what the El Mouradi Mahdia 5 * hotel is, what reviews there are about it, and whether it is worth renting rooms here. This hotel is located in the city of Mahdia - the center of silk weaving and one of the busiest resorts in Tunisia.

Hotel Location

Searching for a hotel El Mouradi Mahdia 5 * (Mahdia, Tunisia) having rented rooms, vacationers need it not in the city itself, but next to it, in its tourist area. Most vacationers who have booked their rooms here fly to Tunisia at Monastir Airport. The distance from this village to the hotel is 45 km. The hotel, among other things, offers its guests a shuttle bus. So, in any case, tourists who decided to stay in it should not have problems with getting to the hotel.



Tunisia is 185 km from the hotel. The center of Mahdia from its territory can be easily reached by public transport or even on foot. The distance will have to be overcome not too big - only 2 km. You can get to Mahdia from the hotel and by taxi. It will cost only about 4 dinars (about 120 rubles). The sea from this hotel can be reached within literally 2 minutes. It is located right next to the ocean, on the first coastline.

El Mouradi Mahdia 5 * (Mahdia): General description of the hotel

This hotel was built in 1997, and renovated in 2010. Its total area (including the yard) is 40 thousand square meters. The El Mouradi complex consists of several two-story buildings and the main building. The last (6 floors) has a U-shaped architecture and is painted in pure white. The windows in this building are arched, and the ceilings are vaulted. The hotel was built in the Moorish style. The luxurious large halls of the hotel are decorated with marble and expensive oriental lamps.

Quite stylishly, according to many tourists, the rooms of this hotel are decorated. The rooms here are quite beautiful. In the courtyard of the hotel there is a swimming pool with sun loungers and an amusement area.





Most tourists consider the hotel El Mouradi Mahdia 5 * (Tunisia, Mahdia) suitable mainly for family vacations and for senior citizens. Young tourists here will simply have nothing to do.

Most vacationers are advised to immediately book rooms in this hotel above the second floor. From the rooms below, only views of the bushes and people sunbathing (and sometimes noisy) by the pool open.
