Sol El Mouradi Skanes 4 * (Tunisia / Monastir): photos, prices and reviews

Every year, a very significant percentage of our compatriots choose a Tunisian resort called Monastir as the place to spend their holidays. Initially, a Roman settlement was located here. Today, Monastir is a city with an authentic atmosphere, offering tourists entertainment and relaxation for every taste. Most of all, this place is suitable for those people who want to spend an excellent vacation by the sea, surrounded by a mass of natural and historical attractions, and at the same time not to empty their wallet very much. If you, like tens of thousands of tourists, decide to relax in Monastir (Tunisia), Sol El Mouradi Skanes 4 * is a good option for a comfortable and inexpensive accommodation. We suggest you learn more about this hotel and find out what impressions you have of our compatriots staying here.





Where is

The hotel in question is located in the resort area called Skanes, on the seashore itself. Nearby are two large cities: Monastir (7 kilometers to the center) and Sousse (13 kilometers to the center). The nearest airport is only a few kilometers away from the hotel, so it will take you no more than a quarter of an hour to arrive at it on the way to the hotel. Sol El Mouradi Skanes Beach has its own excellent fine sandy beach. It is equipped with sun loungers and parasols. It also has everything you need for water activities. There is an excellent golf club near the hotel in question. Therefore, lovers of this game often come here.

How to get there

The easiest way to reach the hotel in question is through the Monastir Airport. In this case, as already mentioned, you can reach the hotel after arrival literally in a quarter of an hour. However, some flights from both Russia and European countries arrive at Enfedi Air Harbor. From here you can either fly to Monastir by plane domestic flights, or get on a train or bus. In addition, experienced travelers recommend booking a transfer at the hotel.

You can also move between neighboring cities by rail (the nearest station is 500 meters from the hotel), by bus or by rented car. In addition, you can always ask the hotel administrator to call you a taxi.

Posting Rules

In accordance with the rules adopted by the hotel, check-in for guests is from 14:00. However, it often happens that tourists arriving on early flights arrive at the hotel earlier than the specified time. In this case (if there are free rooms) they will try to populate you immediately. If you still have to wait until the previous guests have left, you can leave your things in the luggage compartment and go to the restaurant for a snack, refresh yourself with a drink in the bar, or take a swim in the pool or the sea. On the day of departure, leave the room in Sol El Mouradi Skanes 4 * should be before noon. In this case, you need to hand over the key to the reception and make payment for the duration of your stay at the hotel. If you have already fully paid in advance for accommodation with your tour operator, you only have to pay for additional services.

Accommodation for guests with children

This hotel is always happy for guests arriving with children of different ages. Moreover, if you place your baby up to two years old in your room, then you do not need to pay extra for this. For older children, extra beds are available. About the possibility of providing this service for your chosen room category, as well as its cost, you should ask the hotel in advance.





Guests with pets

It is recommended that you pay attention to those tourists who are used to taking their four-legged pets with them on foreign trips. So, in accordance with internal rules, pets are not allowed in the territory of Sol El Mouradi Skanes 4 * (Monastir). Therefore, you can either choose another hotel for your stay, or part with your pet for the holidays.

Rooms

As mentioned above, the hotel in question has 505 rooms located in two four-story buildings. So, at your service are standard single, double, triple and quadruple rooms, as well as suites. All rooms at Sol El Mouradi Skanes are stylishly decorated and equipped with central air conditioning, a TV, minibar, telephone, safe (for a fee), a private bathroom with a hairdryer and bath toiletries. All rooms have a balcony with beautiful views of the azure sea, a picturesque garden or pool. If necessary, you can also ask for a kettle, as well as a set for tea or coffee. The hotel rooms are cleaned daily. Bed linen and towels are updated several times a week. All hotel guests can order a room service.





Dining at the hotel

Dining at the Sol El Mouradi Skanes 4 * hotel complex (Tunisia) is organized according to the all inclusive system. Several times a day, experienced chefs serve a buffet in the main restaurant, with a capacity of 350 seats. Sea and beach

As already mentioned, Sol El Mouradi Skanes is located on the very shore of the sea and has its own beach. It is sandy and equipped with everything necessary for an excellent holiday.





Entertainment and infrastructure

The hotel complex under consideration has at its disposal all the necessary infrastructure for a wonderful vacation and pastime. So, for the services of guests there are three swimming pools, one of which is indoor. Near them there is a spacious sun terrace with sunbeds and umbrellas, as well as a bar. In addition, the hotel boasts its own wellness center, where you will be offered a variety of cosmetic and restorative treatments.

Fans of outdoor activities will also not be bored here. So, the hotel has sports grounds, tennis courts, a gym. Animators work here all day, and in the evenings there are bright shows and performances.

Guests of Sol El Mouradi Skanes have the opportunity to exchange currency, withdraw cash from the hotel's ATM, book a transfer to the airport, call a taxi, rent a car, pick up an excursion in the direction of interest, resort to laundry and dry cleaning services. Wi-Fi is also available in public areas of the hotel.

Price for accommodation

As for the cost of accommodation, it is quite consistent with the category of hotel. So, accommodation for two in a standard room, taking into account the food on the all-inclusive system, will cost you in the amount of 54 euros per day.





Sol El Mouradi Skanes (Tunisia) Travel Reviews

Since today many travelers make the final decision on choosing a hotel based on the opinions of people who have already visited it, we decided to bring to your attention the generalized comments of the Russians who spent their holidays in Sol El Muradi Skanes (Monastir, Tunisia). We note right away that the vast majority of Russians were very pleased with their decision to stay at this hotel and would gladly recommend it to all tourists traveling to Tunisia. But we offer to understand everything in more detail.

As for the hotel rooms, our compatriots found them quite spacious and pleasantly decorated. Furniture and appliances are in good condition. Cleaning is done every day. Towels and bed linen are also changed several times a week. Moreover, according to tourists, here they do not just take out the trash from the room and slightly sweep the dust off the surfaces, but vacuum and thoroughly wash the floor in the room and the bathroom, as well as on the balcony.

The territory of Sol El Mouradi Skanes is small, but it is very well planned, so there is enough space for everyone. The only minor drawback is the fact that within the hotel you will not find a place for walking. However, there are as many as three swimming pools, sports grounds, restaurants and bars.

Traditionally, disputes between guests flare up when it comes to food at the hotel. The hotel in question was no exception to this rule. So, some tourists claim that the choice of dishes is small, and a four-star hotel could offer travelers a more varied menu. However, another part of our compatriots claims that they were more than satisfied with the food at the hotel and did not see any reason for any discontent at all.

Also controversial opinions can be found regarding the hotel staff. So, some travelers claim that the staff of Sol El Mouradi Skanes 4 * are not very quick and friendly. However, most of our compatriots are convinced of the opposite. In addition, experienced tourists recommend not to forget about the specifics of the country and to encourage local staff with a small tip. It will cost quite inexpensively, but you can count on excellent service from the hotel staff.

Our compatriots were very pleased with the level of animation in the hotel in question. According to them, the local team of animators consists of real professionals in their field and is struggling to make the guests' rest as interesting and varied as possible. So, in the afternoon you will be invited to take part in various competitions, play darts or volleyball, do aqua aerobics. In the evenings, the hotel hosts a variety of shows, theme events and discos.

As for the hotel‚Äôs own beach, for the most part, tourists were also satisfied with it. Despite the fact that it cannot be called large, everyone can always accommodate here without problems. The only aspect that travelers recommend paying attention to is the presence in the coastal waters of jellyfish, which are especially active in July and August. However, this fact is characteristic not only for the hotel in question and its environs, but also for the entire Tunisian coast.

In general, it can be noted that the Sol El Mouradi Skanes hotel (the reviews of our compatriots who have visited here only confirm this) fully correspond to its four-star category and price. So if you want to spend a great vacation in Tunisia, be sure to come to this hotel.
