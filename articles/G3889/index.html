Fabric flowers. Flower patterns

With such a pretty flower, you can decorate your purse, hair clip or other accessories. If you liked the flower - for work.



DIY jewelry has always been in fashion. Fabric flowers are especially popular . Patterns of such jewelry are quite simple to perform.

First, pick a fabric for the flower. It can be linen, cotton, silk, denim or other material (plain or multi-colored). In a word, that which will fall under your arms and will be to your liking. The fabric should be clean and dull. It is easier to make a flower from a lightweight fabric, and the product will be preserved better from a dense one.

In addition to the fabric, you will need scissors, a needle, a thick thread, thick paper, a button.

Make patterns of flowers from fabric. To do this, cut a square out of thick paper, attach it to the selected fabric, circle the figure with a simple pencil along the contour and cut it with scissors. Five such squares should be prepared. From each you make a petal. Select the size of the squares depending on the size of the bag or other accessory that you are going to decorate, and, of course, on your taste.

Fold the fabric square diagonally. You have a right triangle. Sew in the neat, wide enough stitches the edges of the pattern - the two sides of the triangle folded to each other, as shown in the figure below. You need to start sewing from one sharp corner of the triangle, and finish the stitch in the other sharp corner.





Gently pull the stitched thread until the sharp corners of the triangle come together at one point. Form a flower petal, as seen in the image below.

Fasten the ends of the resulting petal firmly and, without cutting the thread, begin to sew with a needle stitches on the next triangle, obtained by bending the next square.

Continuing to work in this way, you will sew together all five petals so that the ends of the first and last coincide. Then you need to carefully spread all the petals and give them a beautiful shape.

To ensure that the flower is strong, does not break up and does not lose its shape, attach its petals to each other with threads on the back. This must be done carefully and carefully so that the threads are not visible from the front and do not spoil the appearance of the flower.

If you want the seamy side of the flower to also have an aesthetic appearance, sew a patch of dense fabric to the seamy side. He will hide all the flaws of your work from the inside out.

A flower will be more interesting if this dense patch is cut out of green fabric and shaped into a leaf.

It remains to decorate the middle of the flower. To do this, you can sew a bright button in the middle or attach a brooch there. If you find neither the first nor the second, you can make the middle of a dense fabric of a contrasting color, leather, suede, velor, etc.



Cute do-it-yourself gizmos have long been popular. They have good energy, have a beneficial effect on human health, improve his mood. Do-it-yourself flowers also have these properties . Their patterns can be found on many sites on the Internet. Fabric accessories look very beautiful on women's accessories. Patterns for them are quick and easy.

Do you want to please yourself with a new handbag? Do not rush to spend money on its purchase. Decorate the old one and make flowers out of fabric for this. Finding patterns is not a problem. In addition, flowers made of fabric, their patterns and work on decorating clothes are an excellent lesson to calm the nervous system.
