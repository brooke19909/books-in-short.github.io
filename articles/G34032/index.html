Should I buy a Xiaomi tablet? Reviews and review of the best models

The market for tablet computers, like smartphones, is changing quite rapidly. If yesterday Apple and Samsung could be called the undisputed industry leaders, today other manufacturers are catching up with them. One of them is the relatively young, but already well-known Xiaomi company, which came to us from the Chinese market. She intrigued many users of mobile gadgets with her devices, which brought the company unheard of success. About whether to buy a Xiaomi tablet and what special it can offer the owner, read this article.

Due to this, of course, the Xiaomi tablet receives a certain bonus in the eyes of the buyer, which is expressed in the attractive appearance of the device. And already at the moment of choosing a gadget, this factor plays an important role.

In addition to appearance, the Xiaomi MiPad tablet is also characterized by excellent usability. This includes finishing materials, as well as the overall placement of buttons, their quality of work, and the like. In all respects, as users note in their reviews, the device is ahead of even some Samsung tablets, despite Apple cloning.

Low cost

Finally, another factor determining what made the Xiaomi MiPad tablet successful is price. Apple devices cost about $ 600-700, and their counterpart (if you take into account the appearance) will cost only $ 200. Moreover, there are rumors that the company is working on the release of a new model of an even cheaper device, the cost of which will be $ 100. If the novelty really lives up to expectations, Xiaomi may become the second Apple. By the way, the company earned such a title in the gadget market a couple of years ago.

However, the fact that the company produces inexpensive tablets does not mean their poor quality or the presence of any noticeable shortcomings, as is often the case with other Chinese devices. No, on the contrary, it seems that Xiaomi is working hard to improve its models, equipping them with the most advanced processors and optimizing the rest of the stuffing. Due to this, the maximum speed of the tablet, its high-speed operation and excellent response are ensured.

Specifications





Even if we talk about only one model - MiPad, which is most widely represented on world markets (for example, they are officially sold in our country), then it has something to brag about. In particular, the first is the most powerful NVIDIA Tegra K1 processor, which makes the tablet one of the most high-performance on the market. Further, a high-quality display on the IPS-matrix with a resolution of 2048 by 1536 pixels can be noted. In addition, the developers equipped the model with a sufficiently capacious battery (6700 mAh), which is enough for a couple of days of active work. There is also a powerful main camera (8 megapixels), a metal case, a 3G module and a host of other options that make the Xiaomi tablet a worthy competitor even for Apple.

conclusions
