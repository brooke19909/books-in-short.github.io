Kyshtym hotels: description and reviews

Kyshtym is a small city and administrative center in the South Urals. The village is located on the river of the same name and is 90 km away from Chelyabinsk. The town is surrounded by beautiful forests and lakes, which, by the way, are more than 30. In the immediate vicinity are several natural monuments. If you are interested in Kyshtym, the hotels will provide you with a warm welcome and quality service.

Hotel "Rodnik"

At 137A Karl Liebknecht Street, the Rodnik Hotel is located in Kyshtym. It is 102 km from Chelyabinsk Airport. Guests are provided with 15 rooms:

  • single with a shower - from 2200 rubles;
  • single with bath - from 2640 rubles;
  • double with separate beds and a shared bathroom - from 2400 rubles;
  • triple comfort with shower - from 3720 rubles;
  • double with separate beds and a shower - from 2600 rubles.

Guests can take advantage of the following benefits:

  • breakfast for 300 rubles;
  • round-the-clock reception;
  • a restaurant;
  • bar;
  • ATM;
  • fax and photocopy.

Reviews about the hotel "Rodnik"

Guests of this hotel in Kyshtym leave positive reviews:

  • clean in the rooms and in the hotel as a whole;

Guests can take advantage of these benefits:
