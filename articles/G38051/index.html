Hotel "Aerotel", Domodedovo: photos and reviews of tourists

Comfortable rooms and quality service are offered to their guests by the Aerotel Hotel. Domodedovo is approximately 500 meters from this hotel. Here you can relax while waiting for your flight.

Hotel "Aerotel" (Domodedovo) - description

For transit passengers it is extremely important to be able to stay at a hotel near the airport. It is provided by the Aerotel Hotel. Domodedovo is one of the most popular airports in the country, and therefore hotel services are in great demand. Here, in addition to accommodation, guests are provided with many additional services. So, guests have the opportunity to use the wireless Internet, parking area, as well as visit the gym.

This hotel (Domodedovo) offers travelers to stay in one of 299 rooms. Aerotel offers guests comfortable accommodation. All apartments are equipped with necessary amenities: beds, a workplace, a bathroom, as well as household appliances and so on. It is important that all rooms are non-smoking, taking care of your health and comfort. Service is available around the clock.

Hotel "Aerotel" has its own restaurant. Domodedovo, of course, has a number of catering establishments, but they have rather high prices, and the quality of food is not always appropriate. In the hotel restaurant you can enjoy the best dishes of Russian and European cuisines. Breakfast is buffet style. If necessary, the dishes you ordered will be delivered directly to your room at any time of the day.





Where is the hotel "Aerotel" (Domodedovo)

A fairly convenient location is characterized by this institution. Only 500 meters from the airport terminal is this hotel (Domodedovo). Aerotel provides guests with a free shuttle (shuttle runs every quarter hour).

There are several convenient ways to get to the hotel. So, you can take an electric train from the Paveletsky station. From the metro station "Domodedovo" shuttle bus number 308. The hotel itself is located directly at the airport, building 6.

Hotel Rooms

Hotel "Aerotel" offers travelers the opportunity to stay in rooms of the following categories:

  • "Economy" offers single, double and triple accommodation. Depending on this, the room area will vary from 18 to 26 square meters. m. It provides everything you need for a comfortable stay guests. Orthopedic beds, a workplace, as well as a shower room and more.
  • "Standard" is a spacious one-room room, which can be equipped with one large or a pair of one and a half beds. It is worth noting the thoughtful design, as well as a wide range of amenities.
  • The Club Room has a spacious area. There is enough space for a double or a pair of small beds, a desktop, as well as other furniture and a set of household appliances. The combined bathroom is equipped with both a bath and a shower.
  • "Suite" is a spacious two-room apartment. The bedroom has a large bed and a fitted wardrobe. The living room has a folding sofa (can be used to accommodate additional guests), a dining table and other amenities. The bathroom is equipped with a bath.

It is worth noting the modern, thoughtful interior of the rooms that the Hotel Aerotel (Domodedovo) offers travelers. Photos of the apartments show a comfortable layout and restrained tones in the design.

Reservation Features

The Aerotel Hotel (Domodedovo) is quite popular among travelers. Reservation is carried out through the official website of the hotel, specialized resources, as well as by phone + 7 (495) 795-35-72. So, you can reserve an apartment in one of two ways:

  • non-guaranteed reservation implies a complete absence of prepayment (if you do not arrive at the hotel before 8 pm, the reservation will be canceled);
  • Guaranteed reservation means full prepayment for the first day of your stay (the number will be listed for you until noon the next day).




Useful information for guests

Before a long flight or in anticipation of a transfer to another flight, travelers need a good rest. In Domodedovo, such an opportunity provides "Aerotel". Guests will find it helpful to read the following information:

  • Check-in to the hotel starts after 14:00, but if you have free rooms, you can use the early check-in service;
  • on the day of departure, you must leave your room before noon (otherwise, you will be charged hourly);
  • children under 6 years old stay in rooms for free, provided that they do not require extra beds;
  • cots for babies up to 2 years old are not charged;
  • a folding bed for an additional guest will cost you 1000 rubles per night;
  • by prior arrangement, guests are allowed to stay with pets (additional service fee is not charged);
  • Guests have the opportunity to pay for accommodation and related services with a plastic card.

General impression

The remote location of airports from the city is a serious problem for transit passengers. The desire to relax between flights is overshadowed by the fact that it takes a very long time to get to the hotel. As for a place like Aerotel, it is located directly on the territory of Domodedovo Airport. There is everything you need to have a good rest before the flight, to restore strength after it.

As for the number of rooms, there are some flaws. So, in the rooms of the class "Economy" there are no air conditioners, and it is impossible to open the windows because of the noise of the take-off planes. Fans are available on request, but in a very limited number (they are not enough for all rooms). That is why it is better to choose an improved standard room with a climate control system. As for the "Suite", then there are quite comfortable living conditions, and most importantly - a free minibar. Many guests are satisfied with such conditions, and they choose such rooms, despite the somewhat overpriced.

This hotel provides all the necessary infrastructure for a comfortable stay and relaxation. Thanks to this convenient schedule, you can satisfy your hunger or have fun at any time. As for breakfast, they are quite expensive, but tasty and satisfying. The portions are rather big. Despite the fact that guests have no alternatives, this state of affairs suits them well. Please note that breakfast can be included in the room rate only if you make a reservation through the official website. If you use the services of intermediaries, then do not forget to pre-order this option during registration. As for the √† la carte service, guests note the high quality of food and reasonable prices, compared to catering points located on the territory of the airport.

Travelers who have already had to stay at the Aerotel note that every year the living conditions in it, unfortunately, are getting worse. At the same time, prices are getting higher. Nevertheless, for transit passengers this is the only option for relaxation, because it is very convenient that you do not need to leave the airport anywhere. Even despite the cramped rooms, the poor quality of cleaning and other shortcomings that we talked about, hotel reservations are booked several weeks in advance.
