Consider how to make a bouquet of fresh flowers

Often, not everyone knows how to make a bouquet of fresh flowers. If you need to present living plants as a gift, people usually turn to professional florists. Unfortunately, the result is not always desired, but the cost of such work is relatively high. In order to avoid such an incident, you can learn how to make flower arrangements yourself. In this article we will consider the question of how to make a bouquet of fresh flowers, what rules should be taken into account.



Natural combinations

Before starting work, remember how these or other inflorescences harmonize with each other. For example, roses (red, white, cream or any other) look good in the company of lilies, less often in harmony with orchids. But poppies, sunflowers, you see, will look ridiculous against their background. Composing something special from tulips, only plants such as cypress or thuja branches can be used as a supplement.

Your imagination and taste



To know how to make a bouquet of fresh flowers, you also need to have an artistic taste and sense of proportion. First of all, pay attention to the combination of shades in your composition. Even if your bouquet should be bright and catchy, it is better that it was designed in one color scheme. At least, the shades you use should be combined with each other, and not resonate. If you choose dark red roses, then you can dilute them with pale yellow, pale pink or cream.

Distinctive features of bouquets



There are plenty of ways to make a bouquet of fresh flowers, so people have long divided such masterpieces into specific groups. Some floral arrangements are called wedding, others are gathered for name days or special occasions. There are bouquets that are customary to give to women, and those that are presented to men. As a rule, for a wedding ceremony, a bouquet of flowers with short legs is assembled. These are cute compositions, mainly in bright colors, decorated with ribbons, artificial petals or beads. For a birthday bouquet, it is best to choose the flowers that the hero of the occasion loves. You can give white orchids to a young girl , and a woman aged will match dark red, almost black roses.

Other nuances

When we make bouquets of fresh flowers, you need to first think about whether it will be symmetrical or vice versa. Most often, floral compositions are presented as a gift, so you will have to focus on the tastes of the recipient. If the bouquet should be symmetrical, then place light flowers in its middle, and outline the edges with darker inflorescences.
