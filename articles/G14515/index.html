How to connect MTS roaming - useful tips

People who often travel to other cities always have many questions: “What things to bring with you?”, “What is better to leave at home, and what can you do without in a particular region?” Today, another very important question is added to this list: "How to connect MTS roaming?" In this article, we will tell you about several ways to perform this operation.





What is roaming?

Roaming is a service that mobile operators provide their users when they cross the boundaries of their home region. There is even GPRS roaming, thanks to which you can use the Internet in other areas and countries.

Roaming services abroad

Of course, MTS mobile communication services are provided throughout the country and the world, therefore, traveling around the world, you can always stay in touch. However, when you leave somewhere, you should know that the rates for calls, messages and the Internet increase at the same time several times. Since it is very easy to activate MTS roaming, you can do it yourself at any time you need. If you are going abroad, then a great solution for you will be the choice of services such as National and International Roaming. You can use them to not only answer calls or make calls yourself, receive or send messages, but also use the Internet. But the services called “Easy Roaming and International Access” include only outgoing and incoming messages and calls.





How to connect these services

How to connect MTS roaming? You can in the communication salon, just by contacting them. But also you can do it yourself using the Mobile Portal. By the way, if you have problems, you can contact the Internet assistant. If you do not want to use the Internet for this, simply dial the following combination on your phone: to connect to “International or national roaming” * 111 * 2192 #, and for “International access” - * 111 * 2193 #.

Connection Features

Absolutely any client can connect the Easy Roaming and International Access services, but the rest is available only to those who activated their SIM card at least six months ago, and the average amount of charges for this time was more than 650 rubles. If these conditions are not met, then the number should be used for a year, since it is otherwise impossible to activate MTS roaming.





“Everywhere at home”

The service "Everywhere at home" is the most convenient in terms of payments when leaving your region in any other country. When you connect it, incoming messages and calls are completely free, but the cost of outgoing calls to numbers of any operators in other cities is 3 rubles per minute. The daily fee for using this option is 3 rubles. You can connect “Everywhere at Home” by dialing the combination * 111 * 3333 # and clicking on the “call” or by going to your personal account on the official website. Here, following the instructions, you can activate this service on your number. The cost of connection is 30 rubles, and disconnection is free. By the way, if you are a member of the MTS Bonus program, then you can connect this service using points, which must be at least 300.

How to disable roaming on MTS

You can disable all unnecessary services on your number. For example, if you once connected "Everywhere at Home", but have already returned to your region, then you can simply send a message containing the text "33330" to number 111.
