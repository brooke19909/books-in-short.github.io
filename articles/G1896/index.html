Hotel Aktinia 3 * (Bulgaria, Sunny Beach): description, relaxation, reviews

Sunny Beach is one of the most popular tourist resorts in Bulgaria. He gained wide popularity primarily due to the low prices for services and housing. There are a lot of great inexpensive hotels in this village. And one of the best hotels is Aktinia 3 *.

How to find a hotel

This budget complex is located in the southern part of Sunny Beach, on the main street, almost by the sea. The nearest beach is just 150 meters away. The resort center is 2.4 km away.





Tourists booked at this hotel often fly to Burgas Airport. The distance from it to Sunny Beach is 26 km. You can get from the airport to this village by minibus or regular bus. Public transport is just 50 meters away from the hotel.

Of course, travelers arriving in Bulgaria by plane can take a taxi at the airport . But more often than not, domestic vacationers book rooms in this hotel all the same through a tour operator and get to the place provided by the transfer bus provided to them.

What is a complex

The Aktinia 3 * hotel (Bulgaria, Sunny Beach) was built for quite some time - in the last century. However, in 2002 there was a reconstruction. The complex accepts guests of Sunny Beach in one 6-storey building of a modern building.

Many tourists find it very convenient that the reception at this hotel is open around the clock. Tourists can enter the booked room after 14:00. Guests must leave their rooms at the end of the reservation period at 12:00. Some rooms at the hotel are pet-friendly. This hotel also has rooms designed for people with disabilities.





You can cancel your reservation without penalty in this complex three days before arrival. If the guests who rented rooms in the hotel do not enter it, the amount paid for accommodation will be returned to them. However, at the same time, tourists are charged a fine in the amount of the cost of two days of rent. For children under 12 years old the hotel provides a discount on accommodation in the amount of 50% of the cost.

Most Russians and Poles rest in this hotel. Service for guests here, according to many tourists, is provided pretty good. Some vacationers even believe that this complex can be assigned not three, but four stars.

Hotel Accommodation

In total, Aktinia Hotel 3 * (Bulgaria) offers 98 comfortable rooms for tourists who choose Sunny Beach as their vacation destination. In rented rooms, hotel guests can use:

  • a refrigerator;

  • telephone

  • air conditioning;

  • TV.

Air conditioning at this hotel is centralized. If desired, guests can use the well-equipped minibar for a fee. Each room rented out at the hotel has a separate shower room. Here, in addition to the standard set of toiletries, guests can use a hairdryer. Television in the hotel has both satellite and cable.





Reviews about the rooms rented at the hotel

The rooms of the Aktinia 3 * hotel (Bulgaria, Sunny Beach) are considered by most tourists to be very comfortable and neat. Cleaning is done regularly. At the same time, the maids do their work carefully. Many tourists include the fact that the use of a refrigerator and a TV here in the rooms is completely free.

The showers in this hotel, judging by the reviews of the residents, are not particularly comfortable. Cabins in them, unfortunately, are not provided. Guests are forced to use a regular watering can. Moreover, when bathing, water is sprayed literally throughout the shower. Because of this, dampness forms in the room. In addition, sometimes it happens that the water in the shower does not go well into the drain.

Bed linen at the hotel is changed once every three days. This is often enough to keep pillowcases, sheets and duvet covers clean.

Arriving ahead of time tourists in the rooms can be settled not at 14:00, but early. But wait 12:00 anyway in most cases have to. Aktinia Hotel Sunny Beach is a popular hotel ; all rooms are usually occupied here. Therefore, check-in may not occur before the previous guests leave the rooms.

In addition to the not very comfortable showers, many tourists consider the penalty system provided for here to be a certain drawback of the hotel. Some guests even, for example, try not to use towels at this hotel. Upon departure, a hotel employee carefully checks everything and can find fault with anything.





Facilities on site

Hotel Aktinia is a three star hotel . And therefore, the infrastructure here is developed quite well. On the territory of the hotel guests can use:

  • outdoor pool;

  • a hairdressing salon;

  • currency exchange office.

Also, guests of Sunny Beach are given the opportunity to rent a small conference room for business events. The hotel also has a launderette. Residents have the opportunity to use sun loungers and umbrellas by the pool for free.

Services for children

The Aktiniya Hotel (Bulgaria) provides various amenities not only for adults, but also for small guests. Indeed, many tourists come to rest on Sunny Beach with their children. To make such residents feel comfortable in the hotel, here are provided:

  • well-equipped playground;

  • playroom;

  • separate shallow area in the pool.

If necessary, parents can rent a bed in the room for the child.





Service Reviews

Regarding the development of infrastructure, the Aktinia 3 * hotel (Bulgaria, Sunny Beach) is also considered quite comfortable among tourists. The attitude of the staff towards the guests is praised by the majority of vacationers who have ever rented rooms at this hotel. The maids at the hotel are very polite. Reception staff, according to many tourists, are also quite helpful, but sometimes they may still be inattentive to residents.

The security guards at this hotel are pretty strict. It is impossible to bring alcohol to the hotel "Actinia" 3 *. Therefore, at the entrance to the tourist can even arrange an inspection. In any case, at the groups of teenagers living in the hotel, bags at the entrance are checked without fail. A rather large fine is provided for carrying alcohol into the territory of the complex.

Bulgaria in the summer, as a resort, is most suitable, of course, for a beach holiday. Actually the Actinia Hotel itself is advised by many tourists to choose family people and senior citizens. Youth at this hotel will most likely be bored. All sorts of cereal establishments in the city are located quite far from this complex. No entertainment is provided for guests in the hotel itself.

Dining at the hotel

Most often, tourists rent, of course, rooms at the Aktinia 3 * standard hotel. Sunny Beach praises most tourists, including the fact that local hotels can rent rooms with meals. In the hotel "Actinia" standard rooms, like any others, are rented with autras or half board. In the latter case, tourists are also given dinner. About the hotel dining room on the network there are mainly only positive reviews. Most tourists believe that the food in this hotel is quite satisfying and tasty. If desired, guests can take in the dining room different types of cheeses and sausages, pasta, omelettes, eggs, vegetables. Tea and coffee are always present at the distribution. In the dining room, among other things, two coolers are installed - one with water, the other with a yuppie drink. Quite often, the hotel guests are offered a very tasty ice cream. Served in this hotel guests and fruits. But, unfortunately, as many tourists note, they are not always fresh here.





In addition to the restaurant, the hotel also has a small bar. It is traditionally located next to the pool. Here tourists can buy beer or some kind of cocktail if they wish.

City infrastructure

This country for recreation, according to many tourists, is very convenient - Bulgaria. Hotel Aktinia Hotel 3 *, as already mentioned, is quite far from various party places in Sunny Beach. However, hotel guests, if they wish, can visit shops of various specializations and various cafes in the city next to it .

Those residents who are looking for entertainment should go closer to the city center. There are many karaoke bars and clubs. Tourists praise very much, for example, the entertainment establishment "Lazur", located not too far from the hotel. If desired, in the city you can visit the amusement parks. To get to the center of Sunny Beach, hotel residents can easily take a bus. Such a trip by public transport will take no more than 10 minutes.

Hotel beach

To go to the sea to the guests of the Aktinia 3 * hotel (Bulgaria, Sunny Beach), but only about 2-5 minutes. Most often, tourists who rent rooms at this hotel visit the beach located directly opposite it - behind the dunes. The water in the sea in this place is almost always very clean.





Most often in this place of the Black Sea there is good weather. Sunny Beach (Bulgaria) is therefore popular among tourists because a beach holiday here can be truly convenient. But, unfortunately, the winds at the resorts of this country blow quite often. The sea on the beach located next to the Actinia Hotel is always warm. However, many tourists complain about the high wave. Actually the coastline opposite the hotel is covered with pebbles. Especially a lot of people on this beach almost never happen. However, unfortunately, no one is engaged in cleaning the coastline here. On the beach you can often see cigarette butts and pieces of paper thrown by some of the tourists.

Available Attractions

Of course, guests of the Aktinia 3 * hotel , like any other tourists who come to rest in Bulgaria, can visit various sorts of interesting places at this resort. A very popular attraction in the southern part of the resort is, for example, the city-museum of Nessebar. This settlement was founded five thousand years ago. In Nessebar, among other things, there are souvenir shops, archaeological and ethnographic museums. Also in Sunny Beach, tourists have the opportunity to visit such attractions as Sozopol, the Rapotamo Nature Reserve, Cape Kaliakra, etc.





Features of the climate of Bulgaria

Of course, in the summertime this part of the Black Sea usually has very good weather. Sunny Beach (Bulgaria) is the resort that is best visited in the summer months. It is at this time in the country that the peak of the season falls. Most tourists come to the resorts of this state, including Sunny Beach, from the second half of June until the end of October. The sea in Bulgaria is warm at this time, and the weather is usually hot.
