Briefly about the resort of Nessebar (Bulgaria): hotels, private hotels, entertainment

Cozy and beautiful, fabulous and keeping its history, the amazing town of Nessebar (Bulgaria) is located on the Black Sea coast near the city of Burgas . The hotels of the city are waiting for tourists in order to give them shelter after the tiring sun on the golden black sea beaches. Monuments of architecture and history, which Nessebar is so famous for, will tell their secrets to inquisitive pilgrims. Indeed, from the time of antiquity, the ruins of the fortress wall, the ruins of towers and city gates are still preserved in the city. The historical monuments of Nessebar are so significant that this small town is included in the list of UNESCO World Heritage Cities.

Nessebar is divided, like most old cities, into the old and new parts. The old part of the city is beautiful churches, representing an amazing symbiosis of Greek and Slavic architecture. The new part of Nessebar is the wonderful and comfortable hotels of Bulgaria 5 stars (Sol Nessebar Palace Hotel and Nessebar Palace), private hotels 2 * and 3 *, as well as old houses of the last century open for tourists. There are 4 * hotels along the coastal strip , these include Arsen, Festa Panorama, Biljana Beach, Sol Nessebar Bay and others.

For those who appreciate not only the quality of the beaches, the cleanliness of the coastal sea, but also trying to diversify their holidays with an introduction to the legends and history of the city, Nessebar (Bulgaria) is an ideal place to visit. The hotels located within the city limits allow not only adults, but also children to have a comfortable rest: entertainment centers, water parks, playgrounds - the entire infrastructure of high-class hotels is at the disposal of tourists.

Besides the fact that this city is a wonderful resort, it is also known for its affordable prices, so for those who are interested in inexpensive holidays, the best place will be Nessebar (Bulgaria). Hotels located on the coast save their guests from the heat of the day with a strong breeze from the sea, and the sound of the surf at night brings beautiful dreams to the residents. Small private hotels are very convenient , in which the owners live on the ground floor, and the upper ones are designed for tourists. The gentle sea, amazing white cliffs, wide golden sand beaches bordered by dunes, high sandy hills - all this is Nessebar.

Bulgaria, the hotels of which hospitably open their doors to travelers, in addition to the lovely and cozy resort town of Nessebar, can offer relaxation in the most luxurious, expensive and modern resort, located on the southern part of the Black Sea coast. This is the city of Dunes.

The five-kilometer beach stretching along the bay is wide (100 meters) and clean. A great place to relax with children, as the coast gently and gently descends into the sea, the bottom is safe and comfortable, and the Dune Bay is perfectly warmed up and protected from northern winds. Besides the opportunity to lazily sprawl on a golden beach, here you can find ways to actively relax. Fans of fishing and windsurfing will especially enjoy this resort: the bay is an ideal place for practicing water sports, and here beginners can try their hand.

In Dunes there are five huge hotel complexes. At the same time, they can offer shelter to almost one and a half thousand people. These hotels operate according to the All Inclusive scheme, and successfully located among the green forests, they can be proud of their beautiful tennis courts, luxurious pools, restaurants with excellent cuisine, and a network of small cozy cafes.

For lovers of unique nature, the Dune resort has prepared many surprises: only here you can see beautiful sand lilies. And in the Alepu lagoon you can breathlessly admire the flocks of migratory birds that stop in these swampy places to relax during a long journey. The symphony of sounds made by birds nesting in these places will be remembered forever by your power and beauty, and returning home from a trip, falling asleep, you will see a wonderful bright dream: greenery, Bulgaria, Dunes, hotels ....
