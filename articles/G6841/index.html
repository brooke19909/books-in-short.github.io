Hotel Tango Apartment (Golden Sands, Bulgaria): overview, description and reviews of tourists

Homeliness, modern amenities and a high level of service will delight its guests with the Tango Apartment hotel. Golden Sands is one of the most popular resorts in Europe, which tourists from all over the world seek to visit. Here, travelers are waited by beautiful nature, warm sea and bright entertainments.





Location

In a picturesque town with developed infrastructure, the resort complex Tango Apartment is located. Golden Sands, 9007 - the address where you can find this hotel. Given that the hotel is located on the third coastline, the road to the sea coast can take up to 15-20 minutes. As for the main street of the resort, you can reach it in 10 minutes. The distance to the hotel from Varna International Airport is 35 km.

Where to go

A rich excursion program is guaranteed to guests of Tango 2 * Apts Resort. Tango (Golden Sands, Bulgaria) is located close to the following tourist sites:

  • the famous beach "Riviera" (0.7 km);

  • water park (1 km);

  • Aladzha Monastery (1.6 km);

  • Party Street (1.9 km);

  • the main street of the resort (2 km);

  • Baltata (10 km);

  • Varna Zoo (10.5 km).

Number of rooms

Over 140 comfortable rooms are offered by Tango Apartment Hotel. Bulgaria (Golden Sands) will seem to you an even more hospitable resort if you stay in comfortable rooms of the following categories:

  • Double standard room can be equipped with a large or a pair of single beds. There is also a small work area with additional lighting. This room has access to a private balcony.

  • For budget accommodation, triple and quadruple rooms are provided. This option is suitable for both family and independent living. So, the number can be rented in a hostel format with the possibility of sharing. The room has an individual combined bathroom.

  • Family rooms are two bedrooms separated by an internal door. There are large and separate beds, as well as a folding soft sofa. One of the rooms has a balcony.

  • Suite is the most luxurious accommodation option in this hotel. The room is planned in a studio format and combines a bedroom and a living room. Functional areas are separated by a bar.

additional information

In accordance with certain rules, the Tango Apartments hotel (Bulgaria, Golden Sands) operates. When planning a trip, read the following data in advance:

  • check-in and eviction of guests is carried out, respectively, after 14:00 and before noon;

  • for an additional fee and subject to availability, guests can use the services of early check-in and late check-out;

  • pets are strictly prohibited in the hotel;

  • Children under 12 years stay free of charge for extra beds;

  • smoking is prohibited on the territory of the hotel (with the exception of specially designated places);

  • subject to a stay of more than 6 days, a one-way transfer is provided free of charge;

  • as a confirmation of the reservation, guests are required to make an advance payment by bank transfer;

  • for tourists arriving from non-EU countries, an additional fee of 10% of the cost of hotel accommodation is charged;

  • Guests staying at the hotel for more than 10 days are entitled to a trip to the Aladzha Monastery and the resort of Saints Constantine and Helena.

General impression

The Tango Apartment Hotel (Bulgaria, Golden Sands) evokes the most different emotions from travelers . Travelers' reviews unequivocally agree that the building itself and all its interior decoration are too old, and therefore need major repairs. In addition, it is worth noting the extremely inconvenient location both with respect to the beach and relative to the main street. Guests will have to overcome a lot of ups and downs, which is extremely inconvenient for travelers with children or the elderly.

Thus, we can say that Tango Apartment (Golden Sands) is an ideal budget option for active tourists who spend most of the time outside the hotel. Reviews say that from the very first days, an outdated and worn-out environment will begin to bother. But with a short stay on the territory you will be pleased with a beautiful park area and pleasant communication with the staff.
