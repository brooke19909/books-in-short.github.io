How to get to Domodedovo. Express

Domodedovo Airport was built in the mid-twentieth century. It is one of the largest in Russia and the largest in terms of passenger traffic in the country. Last year, patency amounted to almost twenty million people. By the way, eighty airlines fly here. More than thirty of them are foreign.

Today, one of the four airports in Moscow is able to provide its guests with many services that meet international standards. A few years ago, Domodedovo was recognized as the best air terminal in the CIS.

The airport has many restaurants, shops, lounges, as well as a hotel where visitors can stay. If you need any of the medicines, you can use one of the two pharmacies in the building or a first-aid post.

How to get to the airport?

It is very easy to get to all the airports in Moscow, although the road will take quite a long period of time. Domodedovo is located far from the city, so if you do not want to waste time, it is worth using the express train. Each year, more than ten million passengers choose this particular mode of transportation.

Instruction manual

First you need to buy an express ticket to Domodedovo Airport. As you know, there are several ways to solve this problem. You can buy a ticket at the box office of the Paveletsky Station no earlier than fifteen days in advance. If you do not want to vegetate in lines, then use the machine in the lobby or purchase an electronic ticket on the express website.

An express train leaves for Domodedovo from the Paveletsky station, which is located at the Paveletskaya metro station.

Express schedule in Domodedovo

Trains follow one after another and usually depart once every half an hour. Express follows non-stop and the journey time is approximately forty minutes.

Aeroexpress starts its movement at six in the morning, the last train leaves at half past twelve. Detailed schedule below.

The train arrives at the airport and it will not be difficult to find it. Just follow the signs for the station.

If you need a return trip, then everything is very simple. At the airport you need to find vending machines, buy a ticket at them and proceed to the train. He will take you to the terminal station "Paveletsky Station".

How much does an Aeroexpress ticket cost?

According to the latest data in Domodedovo, an express ticket costs 420 rubles of the usual economy class. The cost of a ticket to the business class is 1000 rubles.

How else can I get to Domodedovo Airport?

If you do not want to overpay for comfort and speed, you can use more budgetary methods. For example, an ordinary train with all the stops. The ticket price to Domodedovo is 125 rubles.

Attention! Be extremely careful and do not leave at the Domodedovo station. This is a city, not an airport. You need to follow to the final destination.

The train leaves from the same Paveletsky station, but the travel time is about one and a half hours, as the train runs with all the stops and at a lower speed.

In addition to these methods, you can get to Domodedovo using the public bus No. 308. The fare is about 85 rubles. It runs from six in the morning until midnight. Children under seven can travel to the airport for free. The bus leaves from the Domodedovskaya metro station.

Another express bus is leaving for Domodedovo. Travel time is about forty minutes. The initial stop is also from the Domodedovskaya metro station. The fare at the moment is 120 rubles. It runs from six in the morning until midnight.

Conclusion

We hope that the article was useful to you, and you managed to buy tickets and go to Domodedovo by express from the Paveletsky station.
