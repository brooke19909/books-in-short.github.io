Tack "Strawberry" to create the atmosphere of the kitchen

The mood of the kitchen is created by accessories. Jars, napkins, tablecloths and potholders. By the way, the last needlewomen can create on their own. For example, sew or bind. Moreover, it is better to make them in the form of berries or fruits. One option may be a crochet ‚ÄúStrawberry‚Äù tack. For her, it is recommended to choose a yarn of a suitable color.





Simple Tack "Strawberry"

Here is the algorithm:

  • Pick up a braid of three air loops with red yarn. Connect it to the ring.
  • Then knit 6 SC in a circle.
  • The next row must be created from 12 of the same columns, that is, in each loop, you need to knit two.
  • In the third row, it is supposed to begin to knit the strawberry nose. In one of the loops, instead of a single crochet, knit: a column, two airs and another single crochet.
  • The remaining circles also consist of similar columns. Only in the arch of the nose perform the same set of elements: a single crochet, two air columns and another column.
  • You need to finish knitting at the moment when the base of the Strawberry tack will reach the required size.
  • For additional thickness of the product, it is recommended to perform another same part.
  • Connect the two halves with a pico or crayfish step. This knitting is better done with a yarn of a lighter shade.
  • Embroider grains with black thread.
  • According to any scheme, tie three leaves and sew them to the top of the strawberries.

Openwork tack





Strawberries can be not only continuous. It can be made on the basis of openwork drawing. Moreover, the work begins with the green part of the berry, that is, with the leaves.

Dial 26 loops. Lock them in a ring. Eleven of them will remain for the loop, from the rest to knit a pattern in a circle.

A row of connecting posts.

In each vertex, execute two double crochet, between them tie the air.

From each arch of the air loop, make a fan of three double crochets. Skipping such a fan is necessary only in the side seams of the crocheted tack "Strawberry".

In the next round, change the yarn to red and knit the same fans. Only their number should be reduced by one on each side of the product in each row.

In the ninth row, only one fan should remain.





Another openwork tack

As in the previous Strawberry tack scheme, work begins with a green thread. She is supposed to dial a chain of 16 loops. Change the yarn to red in the fourth round.

The first of these will be such a pattern: 3 lifting loops, * 3 aerials for drawing, 3 double crochets (SN), an arch of 8 airs, 3 CH, 3 airs, 6 CH, repeat work from the sign *, only instead of 6 CH execute 5.

Second: knit on each arch of three loops - 3 CH, 3 air, 3 CH; on an arch of eight loops 13 CH; between these patterns, perform three air loops; over six double crochet posts there should be 4 airs for fastening two halves of the Strawberry tack.

Third: repeat the pattern along the edges of the halves of the strawberries; on 13 CH to execute them themselves, but separated by one air loop.

Fourth circle: repeat the contour pattern; in the middle of the strawberry, start a pattern of arches - a picot of 3 air loops attached to the arches of the previous row with single crochet (SB) columns.

Fifth to thirteenth: gradually reduce the number of pico by one.

Fourteenth circle: contour patterns attached to the SB at the top of a single arch.

In the fifteenth, the circuits should close, that is, between them there is no need to perform air loops.

In the sixteenth circle from the fans around the edges you need to leave one at a time.

Closing circle: only one fan out of three CH.

It remains to sew an eyelet and decorate your kitchen.
