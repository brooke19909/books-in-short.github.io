Holidays in Spain. Ibiza is the perfect place for a reckless holiday

For many years, a kind of Mecca for young people is a vacation in Spain. Ibiza, Menorca and Mallorca are the large islands of the Balearic archipelago resort, which are famous for their mild climate, amazing sunsets, magnificent beaches and amazing energy.

At the moment , Ibiza Island takes first place in the ranking of numerous fashionable European resorts. There is everything for a chic and unforgettable vacation: tennis and golf fields, wild and well-maintained beaches, excellent hotels and many night clubs, restaurants, discos.

Delightful nature, cacti, pines, palm trees in harmony with unrestrained fun make life on the island and all the rest in Spain a holiday. Ibiza is a dance floor of Europe, a cocktail of night festivities and club holidays. Two party centers are located here - the motley capital of the island of the same name and the city of British youth San Antoni. These cities are inhabited mainly by European youth. Free environment, lots of entertainment, the azure sea and the gentle sun contribute to the fact that Ibiza is a suitable resort for those who choose a vacation in Spain. Ibiza is an ideal place for a magnificent, but rather expensive vacation. In the afternoon in the capital you can stroll through the gallery of avant-garde art, visit the medieval castle of Castell and the Puig-das-Mulins complex of 4000 Roman and Carthaginian tombs. Ibiza-tour to the rocky and green island of Es Vedra will be unforgettable. Here, on major holidays, carnivals, processions and festivals are held, fireworks are organized. Each village of the island celebrates annually the day of the saint patronizing her. Residents on this day arrange torradas feasts (a kind of Russian barbecue) and dance national Balearic dances.

In Ibiza, there are about 50 sandy beaches, the length of which is 18 kilometers. The island has several schools for teaching windsurfing and sailing, 14 diving centers. Vacationers can rent windsurfs, boats, pedal boats, canoes. Horse riding is the best way to get acquainted with the local nature. These trips can be organized by any of the six riding centers. Vacationing families with children will enjoy a visit to one of the water parks - Aqualandia or Aquamar.





There are many restaurants on the island. Their distinctive features are an informal design and a peculiar atmosphere. Ibiza is not a good place to shop because the prices are high. But in September, many stores arrange sales, where you can buy stylish clubwear and some things from the world's leading fashion designers.

There are about 80,000 hotel rooms on the island, mostly two or three star hotels. Ibiza is a resort where the main criterion for evaluating a hotel is not the number of stars, but the year of reconstruction. Therefore, there is nothing surprising in the fact that more and more tourists every year choose a vacation in Spain (Ibiza).
