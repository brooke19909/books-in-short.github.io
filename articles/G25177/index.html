Night skating rinks in Moscow 2017-2018: description, location

Active rest (especially after a busy working week or day) is always a favorable and creative way to shift attention to something abstract, to be filled with new energy. One such option for psychological and physical unloading is ice skating. This can be done both in the daytime and at night. Capital skating rinks provide such services.

Indoor Night Rollers

In Moscow, there are about 100 ice rinks that provide an opportunity for residents and visitors to skate en masse. This number includes indoor, outdoor and night skating rinks. The latter make up about 50% of the total.

Here are some of them:

  1. Krylatskoye is a speed skating center, which is one of the largest night skating rinks in the capital. Located at 16 Krylatskaya Street (the nearest metro stations are Molodezhnaya, Krylatskoye). Opening hours of the night rink: from midnight to 05.00 on weekends. Here you can visit the cafe during your stay on the ice to warm yourself with tea or coffee, and relax a bit. The cost of the entire session is 600 rubles. Payment is possible by cash or bank transfer.
  2. "Magic Land" - a skating rink, which is located on the 7th floor of the SEC Evropeisky (district of Kiev Station). Open Fridays and Saturdays until 06.00. It is distinguished by the original design of the ice stadium (fabulous scenery, stars). There is a restaurant that can be visited right during the session.

  4. Sokolniki is an ice palace located on 1-B Sokolniki Val Street. Opening hours of the night rink: Friday - from 20.00 to 01.00, Sunday - from 19.00 to 01.00. The average cost of sessions is 500 rubles. There is a cafe, an ATM.
  5. "Morozovo" is an ice palace located on Novoostapovskaya street, 5, building 2. There is a night ice rink in Moscow on Saturdays from 22.00 to 05.00. There are 2 ice arenas (for professionals and amateurs). The average cost of a session is 500 rubles.

Sokolniki

Another popular night skating rink in Moscow, on Sokolniki Val, 1-B (near the Sokolniki station).

Also, the skating rink is famous for the fact that hockey teams and figure skating artists regularly train and perform here. Comfortable stands are located around the perimeter.

The main characteristics of the ice field:

  • size 30 x 60 meters;
  • regular cleaning and restoration of the roller surface;
  • high-quality ice rink;
  • changing rooms and showers are available for vacationers and practitioners.

On the territory of the rink there is a cafe where you can warm up with tea or coffee during the session. There is a guarded parking lot near the building.

There is a night skating rink every Friday (from 20.00 to 01.00) and Sunday (from 19.00 to 01.00).

The cost of the session is 500 rubles.

This site among the night skating rinks 2017-2018 (Moscow) is known for its huge area - 18 thousand square meters!
