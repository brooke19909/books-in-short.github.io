Hotel "Tourist" (Bratsk): acquaintance

The institution was commissioned in 1969. In 1993, the hotel underwent a complete renovation. The number of rooms at the Tourist Hotel in Bratsk is 127, accommodates 250 guests.

Location

Tourist Hotel in Bratsk has a favorable location: the distance from the hotel to the railway station is 3 km, to the airport - 7 km, to the legendary Bratsk Hydroelectric Power Station - 2 km. The brotherly reservoir is located 450 meters from the Tourist. In walking distance - the Palace of Arts, a shopping center, a cinema, a sports complex with a sauna and a swimming pool, a picturesque park area.
