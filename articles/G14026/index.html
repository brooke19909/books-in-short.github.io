The description of any craft begins with recommendations for yarn and knitting needles. Experienced needlewomen advise using a soft and pleasant to the touch knitting thread to realize the idea. How to find a convenient hook?

The next step in describing a crocheted bear involves selecting a tool. Of course, the needlewoman can use her beloved. But to make the craft neat and beautiful, it is worth giving preference to the fact that it is slightly thinner than the purchased knitting thread. It is also important to note that experienced craftswomen advise beginners who are too tight for loops to knitters to prefer a metal hook. But if the needlewoman, on the contrary, knits too loosely - with large, elongated loops, it makes more sense to consider a wooden one.

Also, regardless of what material the tool is made of, it is more convenient to work with a fairly short hook. The ideal length is the distance from the tip of the middle finger to the wrist.

What technique to knit?

It is impossible to talk about how to crochet a bear without mentioning the interesting word ‚Äúamigurumi‚Äù. It should be understood as art that originated in Japan and instantly became popular. It is in this technique that any toys are made. Whether it‚Äôs animals, little men, and human-like creatures - funny inanimate objects with faces. And, of course, it has its own characteristics.

Any detail begins with knitting an amigurmi ring, which many professional craftswomen call "magic." Next, you need to knit in a spiral, that is, without lifting loops. And after passing three rows, focusing on the instructions, turn the resulting circle. As a result, the initial thread will be on the wrong side. But that is not all. The subsequent rows will have to be formed, moving in the opposite direction, that is, counterclockwise.

What is an amigurmi ring?

It doesn‚Äôt matter at all which one you decided to crochet a bear - big or small, sitting or standing, the principle of performing a ‚Äúmagic‚Äù ring should be mastered, and then applied throughout the work.

However, you should not be afraid, you will not have to perform complex actions:

  1. We take a working thread.
  2. Wrap on two fingers.
  3. Carefully remove, while it is important not to dissolve the loop. Otherwise, everything will have to start again.
  4. We take a hook and, making columns without a crochet (only 6 pieces), we tie a ‚Äúring‚Äù.
  5. We connect the first and last loop of the row.

Mission accomplished. Apparently, there is nothing complicated!

Knit Teddy Bear

To bring the idea to life, you need to prepare a filler, a sewing needle with a big eye, a hook and yarn. Concerning the tool, enough has been said. But the information about the material is worth adding.

Experienced needlewomen recommend choosing a mohair, angora or a product of the Turkish brand ‚ÄúKroha Nazar-Rus‚Äù. However, it is worth considering your own skills and abilities. It is also important to recall that the instruction on how to crochet a Teddy Bear toy involves preparing skeins of light and dark gray shades, as well as white, black and azure.

Head

Knitting this and all subsequent details begins with the amigurumi ring. After that, we knit according to the instructions:

  1. From each loop we knit two new ones.
  2. We make an increase through 1 column.
  3. From this row to the 7th, we increase the interval between increases to five.
  4. The next 5 rows just spiral.
  5. We make a decrease after 5 loops.
  6. In each next row we decrease the interval.
  7. Before knitting 2 loops together through one, we stuff our head.
  8. Then we make decreases without gaps.
  9. Through the remaining loops we stretch the thread, tighten and tie.

Torso

Recall again: if you want to crochet a polar bear, for example, Umka, you just need to use the appropriate yarn. You can work according to the description below:

  1. We start with the Amigurumi ring.
  2. Double the number of loops.
  3. We make an increase through one loop.
  4. In the following rows, gradually increase the interval to 4 loops.
  5. Now we knit 3 rows without changes.
  6. We knit a row, making a decrease through 4 loops, then two - in a spiral. Repeat step 2 times, reducing the interval to two columns.
  7. Alternate the decrease and the single crochet column.
  8. We stuff the body.
  9. Repeat the last two steps of the previous instruction.

Upper paws

We start with the ‚Äúmagic‚Äù ring, double the number of loops, and then:

  1. We alternate the column and the increase.
  2. We knit without changes.
  3. Alternate the column and decrease.
  4. We knit 4 rows without changes.
  5. We make a decrease after 10 loops.
  6. Knit in a spiral.
  7. Reduce the spacing to 9 columns.
  8. Stuff the paws.
  9. We finish by familiar technology.

Lower paws

Many needlewomen in master classes telling about how to make a crocheted bear (large or small) do not provide a description for the lower legs. Therefore, if you wish, you can connect the four details, guided by the previous instructions.

If you want to make a real Teddy bear, you should act differently:

  1. We start with the Amigurumi ring.
  2. We make increases without gaps.
  3. We alternate the column and the increase.
  4. Repeat step 2 again.
  5. Then we make an increase through 2 columns.
  6. We knit 2 rows without changes.
  7. We make a decrease through 8 loops.
  8. We knit 3 rows in a spiral.
  9. From this series, we begin to reduce the interval to one loop.
  10. We are finishing work on the familiar technology.

Assembling Teddy Bears

Having prepared all the necessary details, we knit a few squares with dark gray yarn and sew, imitating the patches. We knit two pads on the muzzle, repeating the first five steps from the instructions for the implementation of the upper legs. Do not forget to make ears:

  1. Amigurumi ring.
  2. Double loop increase.
  3. A series - 1 column and 3 additions.
  4. Strapping in a spiral.

Then we collect all the details together, embroider the nose and eyes. The product is ready!

We knit a bear from the cartoon "Masha and the Bear"

As we noted earlier, the second most popular is the domestic cartoon character. Despite the fact that in the story he cares about the restless girl, many boys adore him. Therefore, further we will tell you how to crochet a bear from Masha and the Bear.

The first step is to prepare a sewing needle, filler, hook, yarn in dark brown, flesh and black. In this case, it is better to use acrylic, designed specifically for children's knitwear.

Lower legs and torso

Having prepared everything you need and tuned in the right way, we proceed to creativity:

  1. We collect a chain of 14 loops.
  2. Knit single crochet columns in each loop. But from the first and last we draw three. Repeat step 3 times.
  3. Four next rows we knit a column in each loop.
  4. We divide the part in half - the sock and the place from which the lower leg begins. In the first part we make reductions, the second - we simply tie.
  5. We make a decrease through 3 columns. In the next row after 2, then after 1, then a series of decreases without intervals.
  6. We knit the three remaining loops of the bow as one.
  7. We knit a leg in a length of about 13-17 rows.
  8. By analogy, we knit the second.
  9. And we connect them, forming the body.
  10. Double the number of loops.
  11. We make an increase through 1 column, in the next row through 2, then after 3.
  12. After 8 rows we knit in a spiral.
  13. We make a decrease through 4 loops, we knit a row without changes. Repeat the step, reducing the interval to 1 loop.
  14. We knit 3 rows in a spiral.
  15. Repeat step 13.
  16. We stuff the body.
  17. We make decreases without gaps.
  18. Pass the thread through the remaining loops, tighten, fasten.

Head

We begin the implementation of this part with an amigurumi ring and double the number of loops, then:

  1. We make an increase through 1 column.
  2. In each next row we increase the interval. Maximum - 5 columns.
  3. Now 6 rows just knit in a spiral.
  4. We make a decrease through 5 columns.
  5. In each next row we decrease the interval. Maximum - 3 columns.
  6. We stuff our head.
  7. We knit 2 more rows in a spiral and finish according to the technology described earlier.
