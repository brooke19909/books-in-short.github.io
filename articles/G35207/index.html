"White Lake", recreation center (Ulyanovsk region): Scandinavian-style holidays for the whole family

Ulyanovsk region is rich in lakes. There are more than 1200 of them in the region. The white lakes of the Ulyanovsk region are not only a vacation spot, but also a historical site. The largest of them, Lake Beloe, is sometimes confused with Belolebyazhim, in the Maininsky district. But if Belolebyazhye is a popular fishing spot, then White Lake is a recreation center. The Ulyanovsk region annually attracts many tourists who want to relax in a relaxed atmosphere in the forest zone.





Recreation center

Located on the shore of a reservoir, the recreation center "White Lake" accepts people both on trips and without them, if someone decides to come with his family on his own. On the shore of the lake, in the recreation center, there is a health resort. Guests of this establishment experience the miraculous effect of local healing mud. They retain heat well, saturated with useful vitamins, organic substances and enzymes.

You can live in the main building or in separate houses. For registration you will need passports and birth certificates of children. If you want to live in a sanatorium, then pre-arrange a resort card in a medical institution. Meals can be paid at the recreation center. But you can cook it yourself, because the houses are equipped with modern kitchens. A spa vacation will cost a little more than a regular vacation in a boarding house.





In general, rest on the White Lake is affordable for almost any resident of our country. At the beginning of the season, vacationers are usually less, and prices are lower.

How to get there

"White Lake", a recreation center (Ulyanovsk region, Nikolaevsky district), has several entrances from different directions, but most of them are of poor quality. A good road is between the district center Nikolaevka and the village of Nagorny, if you go from the turn of highway number 5. There is a pointer on the way. You can also get on the Ural motorway. You need to drive Ulyanovsk, getting through Kuzovatovo and Barysh. Any local will be happy to tell you where the "White Lake" (recreation center) is located. Ulyanovsk region cannot boast of a large number of good roads. Nevertheless, from Ulyanovsk to "White Lake" can be reached in 3 hours at a speed of 80 km / h.

Nature reserve

White Lake is a resort area and nature reserve. It arose even in the ice age. The age of this natural site is at least 250 thousand years. The lake was so named because there is white sand at its bottom, and the water in it is very clear. Sand gives the pond a white color. The nature of this area resembles Scandinavia with its unusual vegetation and special purity of air.

Holidays on the White Lake

Due to the abundant vegetation, the climate in the reserve is mild, there are no winds, and in summer there is almost always good weather. White Lake (Ulyanovsk Region) is a place where anyone will find relaxation to their liking. Here you can just sit on the beach and watch the underwater world, you can enjoy fishing, swimming. In shallow water in summer, water warms up very quickly. The swimming season begins in early June.

High quality service and beautiful nature at the recreation center "White Lake" will be remembered for a long time, and you will definitely want to return here again.
