Ski and biathlon complex "Laura" (Sochi): description, reviews

More and more tourists prefer extreme relaxation and a healthy lifestyle. Where to go in order to get new emotions? The choice should fall on the ski and biathlon complex "Laura". Why is this place so famous and why do travelers from all over the world come here?





History of the complex

The well-known company Gazprom started planning the construction of this facility back in 2009. In the same period, the basic foundation of the future complex was laid. For a long time, the founders decided where it will be located. Ultimately, they preferred Krasnaya Polyana, located in the city of Sochi. The Laura ski and biathlon complex was completed at the end of 2011, and in 2014 large-scale Winter Olympic Games were already held here.





Currently, there are various competitions in biathlon, skiing, as well as the Paralympic Games. In addition, tourists constantly come here in order to strengthen their body, improve their health and cheer up.

The capacity of this recreation center is about 7500 people.

Opportunities for tourists





There are several reasons why a tourist should come to rest in this place:

  1. See the most exciting sporting events. Gain experience in a certain direction with real professionals.
  2. Enjoy the beauties of the Adler region. Tourists who come to this place notice that the landscape here is really spectacular. Before the eyes of a person, a view of the mountains, picturesque glaciers and extraordinary vegetation opens.
  3. Many visitors for the first time can take a cable car ride. From a passenger cabin, the city takes on a completely different look. Even at the very peak of the competition there is no need to stand in long lines before the trip. The creators of the complex claim that this transport can transfer up to 6 thousand people in just one hour.
  4. There are many sports services for visitors: rental bicycles, rollers, skates, a spacious pool, an armory and much more.
  5. Medical experts assure that there is healing air in this place. Vegetation gives off special essential oils. They penetrate the human body and create a healing effect.

Athletes especially liked the Laura complex. Sochi for many of them has already become their hometown. Everything here is equipped in such a way that you can comfortably train, relax and participate in competitions.

Contact Information

On the territory of the Adler region is the largest, most extreme and most popular ski and biathlon complex ‚ÄúLaura‚Äù. How to get to this place? The path to be done is not small.

You should get to the terminal station of the train direction to Adler. About 100 meters from the stop there will be a cable car moving in the direction of Adler - Krasnaya Polyana. The approximate travel time on this transport is about 20 minutes.

After the end of the journey, you need to transfer to another cableway, which goes from Krasnaya Polyana to the Laura complex.

Tourists reviews

As mentioned earlier, there are a huge number of reasons why you should visit the Laura ski and biathlon complex. Reviews about this place are extremely positive. Most of the guests praise:

  • A chic view of nature that simply cannot help but take your breath away.
