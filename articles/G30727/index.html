Shatsky lake: location and rest

Shatsky Lakes (Ukraine) is one of the best places to relax. They are part of the Shatsky Natural National Park. The specified group includes more than thirty reservoirs located in the Volyn region on the border with Belarus between the Western Bug and Pripyat rivers.

Shatskoye Lake District is a crystal clear air saturated with coniferous aroma, unique climatic conditions, a diverse world of flora and fauna, beautiful nature. All of the above made this place the most beautiful territory for spending summer vacations.



Shatsky Lakes

We can not say that there is only one Shatsky lake. There are many ponds here, but their entire group was conditionally called Shatsky. Which lakes are included here? Naturally, Svityaz, Ostrovyanskoe, Pesochny, Pulemetskoe, Krymny and Luke. It also includes Lucimir and some other lakes. In the summertime, the water in them warms up perfectly, and with the onset of winter, water bodies turn into a skating rink. Mineralization of the fluid can be described as moderate or reduced. The hue of the water varies depending on the depth of the lake itself. Stranded, its color is yellowish-green, and deep lakes can boast an emerald green hue. And the bottom of each of the thirty formations is covered by sandy-silty sediments, sapropelic and peat silts.

Shatsky lakes are a storehouse of fish, which is home to more than thirty species. There live pikes, trout fish, crucian carp, pike perch, perch, Canadian catfish, as well as bream and chidi whitefish. And eel is a local highlight and the most delicacy. But fishing is allowed here only at a time specified by law and exclusively by the specified method.



Svityaz

Shatsky Lake Svityaz deserves the most attention. After all, this is the pride of Ukraine, and it belongs to the seven wonders of this country. The pond is unique, and its waters have healing properties. This is the deepest, largest and cleanest lake in Ukraine. Svityaz is such a deep reservoir that neither the Sea of ​​Azov nor Synevyr are suitable for it. Its area reaches two thousand seven hundred and fifty hectares, the largest depth is 58.4 m, and the average figure is 6.9 m. When the wind breaks, you can do surfing here, because the waves rise up to one and a half meters. The lake owes its fullness to underground sources.

The Shatsky Lake Svityaz among the local population and tourists is known for having its own island, which bears a romantic name - the Island of lovers. This place is simply mesmerizing with its mystery. There is a certain legend, according to which young lovers - a guy and a girl - on the island turned into two trees - linden and sycamore.



We are going to rest

Shatsky lakes (rest in these places will not leave you indifferent) every year on their open spaces take a huge number of guests. Here you will have the opportunity to swim in the coastal clear water and soak up the gentle sun on the sandy shore. Having gone to the Shatskoye Pesochnoe lake, you can ride catamarans and water slides. And if you want to enjoy diving, then we advise you to conquer Svityaz. Shatsky National Nature Park offers several recreation areas, namely: “Svityaz”, the tract “Sand”, “Ridge”.

Also on the territory of the Shatsk Lakes there are many tourist camps and holiday homes. But if you want to retire and relax from the tedious bustle, you can rent a room or even a whole house, put up a tent.



Those who should give preference to rest on the Shatsky Lakes

People who want to improve their health, take a break from the noise of big cities, smog and dust, are simply obliged to go to Shatsky Lakes, a vacation on which will be simply unforgettable. All those who bathe in love for forests, lakes, fresh air, tranquility, will receive tremendous pleasure from staying in this part of Volhynia. Yes, there are not all of the charms of civilization that take place on the sea or in foreign resorts. But then there is wonderful nature and tranquility, after which vacationers go to Shatsky Lakes. For tourists, they have an extraordinary value, as they are a clean and unclogged place. And until in these reservoirs they start to wash cars and dissect them on scooters, they will remain crystalline and will benefit humanity.

Conclusion

Shatsky Lakes (where they are located, described above) is a place where you can spend your vacation without occupations, such as banana riding or visiting the dolphinarium, that are standard for vacationers. Here it’s nice to simply admire nature, gather mushroom berries, breathe air, from which at first it may even make you dizzy, or walk barefoot through the warm and gentle sand. It is in this that the most real and true pleasure lies. Choose the Shatsky Lakes for your vacation, and you will be provided with all the pleasure that a person can get while on vacation!
