How to make money in Moscow without a higher education?

Moscow, as the capital of our immense homeland, has a particularly rich and diverse labor market. Here, specialists of any level can choose for themselves a decent and fairly well- paid job. Along with this, it is worth noting that in Moscow it is easier to open a new business, because almost any business idea will be in demand. But how to make money in Moscow without even a higher education? Here are some universal options for making money.

Participation in marketing surveys

This type of earnings is especially popular on the Internet, but in real life it can bring more tangible income. How to make easy money this way? Everything is extremely simple. It is enough to come to the appointed place at a predetermined time and fill out the form. Earnings from this type of activity range from 300 to 1000 rubles per survey. You can also view various cinema novelties and give them a critical assessment, which will also bring a certain income.





Promotion

One of the easiest and most affordable options for making money. He is just perfect for a schoolboy. A rolling work schedule, constant movement, communication with people, the possibility of self-realization, a demonstration of independence - and this is not a complete list of reasons why teens decide to work as promoters. Handing out flyers in supermarkets or acting as life-size puppets, you can earn from 200 rubles per hour.





Extras

How to make money in Moscow and at the same time "light up" for the whole country? To do this, you need to post your profile on the sites of acting agencies. Surely in the near future you will be invited to shoot in mass scenes. The level of payment depends on the budget of the picture. On average, it is about 500 rubles for shooting in the daytime and 1000 rubles for night work. The advantage of this type of activity is the ability to demonstrate their own talents, which can serve as an impetus for climbing the cinema Olympus. However, one must be prepared for the fact that surveys can often take place in not very comfortable conditions (in the rain, at low temperatures, etc.).

Registration of companies and development of charters

The lion's share of successful businessmen do not have enough time to run around the banks and numerous offices of state institutions to register their enterprises. Basically, the business owner independently carries out the necessary operations, and the issues of registration and registration are handled by an employee specially hired for this purpose. Such activities are paid pretty high. How to make money in Moscow through the development of charters and other constituent documents? To do this, you need to study thematic periodicals and literature with a description of the basics of working with sample documents for entrepreneurial activities. And then - a matter of practice. Only once going through all instances, you can get an indispensable and useful experience for a future career.





Now you know that the question "how to make money in Moscow" can find a huge variety of answers. Factors such as age and higher education do not play a decisive role in this case. It is enough to have aspiration and desire, and the rest comes with experience.
