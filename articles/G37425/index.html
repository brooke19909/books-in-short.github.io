Hot spring in Rezh: rest and treatment

At a distance of 85 km from Yekaterinburg on the banks of the river of the same name is the small town of Rezh. Over the past few years, a large number of tourists have been visiting its surroundings. But they do not go to admire the unique landscape and beautiful natural landscapes. The reason for the tourist pilgrimage was the hot spring in Rezh , which has healing properties. It is located in the resort complex "Baden-Baden".

What are the benefits

The discovery of thermal springs dates back to 1933. They are located among perennial pines in the territory where industrial pollution has not yet reached. Water rises from a depth exceeding 200 meters and enters the water reservoirs. In this case, temperature indicators remain in the range from 35 to 39 degrees. Passing through the earth's relief enriches the hot spring in Rezh with useful substances that help in the fight against ailments.

In the composition of water, macro- and microelements, minerals (including radon) were found that help the body withstand various diseases associated with:

  • musculoskeletal system;
  • skin integument;
  • bone and muscle tissue;
  • internal organs.

Also, hot springs located on the territory of the Baden-Baden complex contribute to wound healing, activate regeneration, and help to resist inflammatory processes.



A bit of history

In a few years, Rezh thermal springs will celebrate their centenary. Today it is a developing complex "Emerald Coast", which is gradually being rebuilt on the site of an old sanatorium, opened in the 50s of the last century. Over the entire period of functioning, the health resort, which did not change its name, experienced several unpleasant moments.

Despite the fact that the main therapeutic agent in Rezh were hot springs, the Emerald Coast after the collapse of the Union was out of work. The hydropathic center was idle and was slowly falling apart. On the banks of the Rezh reservoir, instead of a health resort, there were ruins.

The situation changed in 2012, when a group of companies began to restore the hydropathic. After 2 years, the Emerald Coast has already received its first visitors. The hot springs scored with renewed vigor.



Modern realities

The new owners of the complex managed not only to restore the hydropathic establishment, but also to bring it to a new level. Having visited the hot springs "Emerald Coast " in Rezh, tourists receive a full range of high-quality services. In the health resort there are:

  • an outdoor pool that fills an underground spring with water;
  • Indoor pool for children
  • sauna, hammam and Russian bath;
  • SPA complex;
  • playground and room for children;
  • billiard room;
  • conference room and 2 restaurants.

The peculiarity of the open 25-meter pool is not only that hot springs fill it with mineral water. "Baden-Baden" is famous for its latest equipment. The pool has 8 hydromassages, a section for air massage and 2 waterfalls. The children's 10-meter pool is also equipped with air and hydromassage facilities.

For recreation and relaxation in the summer are available:

  • open-air sports grounds;
  • football and volleyball field;
  • equipped with comfortable sunbeds sandy beach;
  • boat station;
  • fishing.

Health and Wellness Unit

Acts as the main "doctor" in the health institution "Baden-Baden" hot spring in Rezh. Qualified specialists will help to improve their health and prevent the exacerbation of chronic diseases with the help of a whole range of measures:

  • detailed diagnostics;
  • Spa treatments;
  • dry carbon dioxide and mud baths;
  • healing soul;
  • hirudotherapy;
  • various types of massage;
  • stay in halochamber and others.

The hot spring in Rezh helps to get rid of:

  • from diseases of the respiratory system and heart;
  • from disorders of the nervous system;
  • from allergies and skin diseases;
  • from diseases of the musculoskeletal system.
