Vatican: milestones in history

The Vatican, as the center of the world widespread Catholic faith, began to exist, in fact, thanks to the activities of the popes. This was due, first of all, to the spread of Christianity in the world.

So many people believed in the Christian god that soon the need arose for his earthly governor. Realizing this, the Romans laid the foundation for the state in the state.

An interesting irony of fate is obtained with the name of the city-country. Ager vaticanus, from which it came, means "a place for fortune telling." We see a vivid pagan aspect of this combination.

Education and Political Status

Since 326 AD, the Vatican has come to exist as a place of worship. The fundamental was the burial of St. Peter, in whose honor the main cathedral of the country was named. Around it the modern Vatican has grown.

Only in 1929, Benito Mussolini finally determined the status of the Vatican as a geopolitical entity. Then the city was officially recognized as autonomy.

The functions for which this small state was formed, and the specifics of its formation, lead to non-standard problems. So, birth here is an out of the ordinary event, while death is quite normal. As we understand, the demographic problems of the Vatican are extremely specific.
