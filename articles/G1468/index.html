Hotel in Odintsovo for animals. Mini-hotels in Odintsovo with hourly rate

The golden-domed capital has always been attractive to tourists. However, the question often arises of where to stay so that it is not unprofitable for the budget. It would be logical to choose a suburb of Moscow: here the air is cleaner, quieter, accommodation prices are more affordable, and the center of the capital can be easily reached by train. That is, a hotel in Odintsovo will be an ideal option. And what about those who took a pet with them on a trip? Do not worry, there is a large selection of hotels for our smaller brothers, where they will be provided with normal living conditions.



Mini-hotel "Podushkin"

This is a large and very famous network that is familiar to many Muscovites. Their motto is "quality services for a reasonable price." The company owns the most famous hotel in Odintsovo, it meets all the requirements of a budget tourist, it is clean and comfortable here, and the room price is from 920 rubles per hour. At the same time, guests are offered both standard rooms and duplex apartments, including those with hydromassage equipment.

Hotel Lira

Another option is to rent an hourly room for yourself if you need to relax between long trips or excursions. This hotel in Odintsovo is no less famous: it offers comfortable rooms at affordable prices. Each room is equipped with comfortable furniture, a bathroom and shower, TV. It offers breakfast and free Wi-Fi, as well as convenient parking. Nearby are several cafes and restaurants where you can order food to your home. The cost of hourly rental rooms - from 600 rubles.



Economy Hotel "Vyrubovo"

This is probably the most affordable hotel in Odintsovo. At the same time, the level of comfort of your vacation does not suffer at all, which is very important. Each room has a refrigerator and a large TV, ironing board and iron, and you can freely use the Internet. There are tennis tables in the lobby. The hotel administration takes on a transfer from and to the airport and train station, which is an extremely convenient service for tourists. Cost - from 500 rubles per hour.

Hotel complex "Ark"

This is a very small but cozy hotel, which houses 11 double rooms of different comfort classes. To make your stay perfect, each room is equipped with modern comfortable furniture and new appliances. That is, you can be sure that the hotel will have all the conditions for a great vacation. Like all hotels in Odintsovo with hourly rates, it often becomes the choice of budget tourists who need to spend somewhere between flights or relax between long excursions. Each room has a full bathroom. For the convenience of guests, the Ark restaurant located on the ground floor organizes free food delivery to the apartments. There is also a sauna with hydromassage and relaxation room. Judging by the reviews, not all tourists are satisfied with the rooms, but if you consider that this is a budget hotel and not business class apartments, the price-quality ratio is quite normal.



Mini-complex "Good neighbors"

If you value the atmosphere as close as possible to your home, then this hotel is suitable for you. It is located in the most successful area. On the one hand, it is practically a center with a well-developed infrastructure, and Moscow is just a stone's throw away. On the other hand, it is very quiet and calm, which means that city noise will not disturb your sound sleep. Vnukovo Airport is located very close, and the hotel staff will provide you with transportation to and from it. Democratic prices play a big role, as well as a high level of comfort in the rooms. Each room has modern and high-quality furniture, all the necessary services and amenities. In addition, the impeccable quality of service, friendly and caring staff pleases. A democratic pricing policy allows each tourist to choose the option that suits him. Double standard costs 2000 p. per day, hourly payment is possible. In addition to comfortable rooms, there is an excellent sauna where you can relax and have a good rest. Against the background of many state-owned hotels, this looks like a real island of home warmth.
