Sights of Samara. Description of the Witch Lake in Samara Luka

This is one of the largest and most beautiful cities in Russia. Samara was founded just 400 years ago. In the architecture and attractions of the city you can see the influence of all four centuries of its existence. A large number of cultural, historical, architectural and recreational attractions in it will not allow tourists to get bored for a minute. Below you will find information about the most popular and interesting places and attractions in Samara and the region, which are definitely worth a visit to the city guests.

Embankment Samara

The most popular place for relaxing and walking in the summer is Samara embankment, which is 5 km long. She attracts with her beauty. There are a large number of fountains and sculptures, shady alleys and benches for relaxation, there is also a bicycle path. The promenade of Samara is one of the cleanest and most pleasant places in the city.

Places for walking

You can take a pleasant walk along the pedestrian street Leningradskaya. It is located in the historic city center. In summer, the pedestrian area is decorated with beautiful flowerbeds and a fountain; you can relax on one of the many shops. On a cold autumn evening, you can bask in a cozy cafe with a cup of aromatic coffee.

City guests note that it‚Äôs a must-see in Samara at Kuibyshev Square , next to which the impressive building of the Samara Academic Opera and Ballet Theater rises. On four sides of the square are green squares, which are a great place for walking and relaxing. Here, under the shade of trees, there are benches on which you can sit comfortably after the promenade. During city holidays, parades, concerts and other vibrant events are held on the square.

Museums

The city of Samara is one of the main centers for building rockets in Russia, so the Samara Cosmic Museum should be a must on the list of what to see in Samara. It consists of three floors and includes 3 exhibitions.

You can learn a lot of interesting things about the history of the country and the region in the Museum of History and Local Lore. Alabina. In its halls there is a collection of archaeological finds, items of clothing and everyday life of people who lived on the site of the modern city many years ago. There is also a collection of old coins, rare books and weapons. A visit to the museum will be an interesting and informative pastime for both children and adults.

Samara Luka

After exploring the city of Samara and exploring the main attractions of the city, you can go on a trip to the region. One of the most interesting places for tourists will be the Samara Luka National Park in the bend of the Volga River. The hilly terrain, wonderful microclimate, the beauty of the Volga waters, the diverse world of flora and fauna - all this attracts tourists.

1/3 of Samarskaya Luka is a closed natural zone - Zhigulevsky Nature Reserve, the rest is intended for people to relax, there are tourist camps, pensions and rest houses, you can also stop with a tent.

In the territory of the national park there is an opportunity to engage in ecotourism, popular today, to watch animals in their natural habitat, go fishing and explore natural attractions.

The iconic place of Witch Lake

The most mysterious and interesting attraction is a tourist site located near the village of Bakhilovo. Witch's lake in Samara Luka attracts a large number of tourists from all over the region. The cost of visiting the attraction is 50 rubles per person, tickets are purchased at the box office before the start of the route. The lake is a mysterious place where, according to legend, devils, water and, of course, witches live. At the very beginning of the hiking trail, animal skulls hang, which gives a special flavor to this place. Witch‚Äôs Lake is located in the middle of a small platform with a diameter of about 150 meters. The area is surrounded by a wooden fence.

Witch's lake in the Samara region is surrounded by a large number of secrets and legends. They say that among the locals from ancient times there were many sorcerers and witches. Until now, some locals observe pagan rites and even worship trees.

At the bottom of the lake there is a spring, therefore, even in the driest years, when other bodies of water dry up, a full-flowing surface still remains in its place. Near the shore is a concrete witch figure. Vorozheya is busy with a very important thing: he makes a magic potion. A tree of love and desires grows near the lake.

To be more precise, these are two trees: an old oak tree that wraps around a young linden tree. There are legends that if you tie a ribbon or scarf on a tree branch and make the most secret desire, then it will certainly come true.
