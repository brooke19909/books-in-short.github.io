RC "Pyatirechye": the developer, location, customer reviews

LCD "Pyatirechye" is the choice of those who monitor their health and are not indifferent to sports. This object belongs to the economy class and is located in the Dmitrovsky district of the Moscow region, between the settlements of Dedenevo and Tseleevo. The project is being implemented by the Tiros-Invest construction company. She can not be considered a newcomer to the housing market, although there is not so much information about the objects being built by the above developer.

Description

The construction process began at the end of 2012. Tiros-Invest will erect 15 monolithic brick buildings of various storeys (from 12 to 17). All houses will have a single style of architecture.





Why you can opt for Pyatirechye LCD? First of all, because there are no hazardous industries in this microdistrict, and a lot of greenery is growing in the okrug. However, it should be said about the minus, which characterizes the LCD "Pyatirechye". Not far from the building is a landfill. The developer intends to compensate for this shortcoming by arranging a recreation area with a park, a reservoir, and an embankment on the territory of the complex. Design documentation provides that each residential building will include several sections.

Offer line

Apartments in the residential complex "Pyatirechye" are offered with varying degrees of footage. You can purchase one-room apartments of a small area (33 sq. M.) Or, if the means allow, to acquire spacious mansions consisting of three residential premises (67 sq. M.). The ceiling height in the apartments is 2 m 80 cm.





The developer uses monolithic brick technology in the construction process. The basis of residential buildings is high-strength materials that provide sound insulation and retain heat in severe winter weather.

When will the facility be ready for delivery?

The microdistrict will be built up in order of priority. To date, building No. 4 has been commissioned. Infrastructure

The microdistrict under construction in the Dmitrovsky district provides for the availability of developed infrastructure. The first floors of residential buildings will be occupied by commercial facilities: shops, fitness centers, hairdressers, bank branches and more. Transport infrastructure

As already emphasized, Pyatirechye is located between two settlements: Dedenevo and Tseleevo.





The distance from the microdistrict to the Moscow Ring Road is 40 km in the direction of Dmitrovskoye Shosse. The city of Dmitrov is 12 km from Pyatirechye.
