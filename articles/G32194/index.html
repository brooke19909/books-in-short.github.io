Hotel Royal Park 4 * (Bulgaria, Elenite): photos and reviews of tourists

Royal Park 4 * is located in the resort complex called "Elenite". It is located in Bulgaria, right on the Black Sea coast. This hotel is ideal for temporary vacations. Therefore, it is worth talking about it a little more.

restaurants and bars

Royal Park 4 * is an all-inclusive hotel. That is, a person who decides to move into this complex automatically gets the opportunity to come for breakfast, lunch and dinner in the main restaurant of the hotel. Recreation and Sports

Each guest knows how important it is that the hotel has not only comfortable rooms, but also a well-developed infrastructure. After all, many want to both live in a hotel and have fun in it. Well, Royal Park 4 * (Bulgaria) provides this opportunity. On its territory there is a water park, a swimming pool, water sports facilities, and for children there is a separate club and animators with an entertainment program.
