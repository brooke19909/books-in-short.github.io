Hotel Club Hotel Titan 4 *, Mahmutlar, Turkey: overview, description and reviews of tourists

Turkey has attracted Russian-speaking tourists in recent decades. They are attracted to hotel comfort and a high level of service. Club Hotel Titan 4 * is a truly paradise place that is simply surrounded by greenery.

Where is

The hotel is located in the town of Kargichak. The nearest airport (Gazipasha) is 23 km from the hotel complex. You will have to travel by bus from Antalya for about 2.5-3 hours, as the Club Hotel Titan 4 * is quite far from all the nearest ebbs.





Kargichak is surrounded by forests and mountains. This resort area has clean beaches and distance from noisy entertainment complexes. Fans of peace and beach relaxation will definitely appreciate this area.

Description

This hotel is not large in size, but it has a strikingly beautiful area. Here at every step some tree or bush grows. All free space is decorated with flower beds.





The rooms are located in the main building and in three-story bungalows. These small houses have spacious verandas covered with greenery. Club Hotel Titan 4 * planted its own small garden where you can walk along the sidewalks.

There is a parking lot near the complex. Here you can park the car at any time without agreement with the managers.

Accommodations

In Club Titan Hotel 4 * Standard, cozy and multifunctional rooms of different class of comfort.

  • Family Room - Tiled or Marble Floor. There is access to a spacious balcony and a seating area. The living room is equipped with a minibar, air conditioning, seating area, which transforms into a large berth. The bedroom has a double bed, bedside tables, wardrobe, coffee table and chairs. It includes satellite TV and free internet. The room is equipped with a toilet and a bathroom with a hairdryer and a mandatory hygiene kit.
  • Standard double room - one double or two single beds. The room has a wardrobe, comfortable small armchairs and a coffee table. The room has a TV, air conditioning, minibar, telephone. There is a private shower and toilet. A hygiene kit and hairdryer are provided. Beds and armchairs are made of wrought iron, green color prevails in the room decor.
  • Standard Triple Room is a living room with a seating area and a small sleeping area with a double bed. The room has air conditioning, TV, minibar, coffee table and two small armchairs. There is a bathroom - a toilet and a shower cabin with a mini-kit for hygiene. There is a spacious balcony.




All guests are provided with bed linen and towels. In all rooms you can order the service "wake-up call".

Service at Club Titan Hotel 4 * Standard in Alanya

The hotel staff speaks three languages: Turkish, English and German, but some employees understand a little Russian. At the reception desk, managers who arrange check-in and check-out are on duty around the clock and are always ready to help with all questions regarding conditions and a comfortable stay.

Administrators are responsible for the currency exchange office. Laundry facilities are located on site. This service is paid by customers separately.

Check in at the hotel at any time, guests are offered a light snack after the road. Checkout time - 12:00. If the guests have a late departure, they can stay at the hotel all the time before leaving, leaving things in the left-luggage office at the reception.

Rooms are cleaned daily. Bed linen is changed every 3-4 days. Room service is available for an extra charge.

Pool and beach

Club Hotel Titan Kargicak is 2 minutes from a clean pebble beach. It occupies a fairly large territory and there are many places for installing sun loungers. Loungers are located in several rows at a sufficient distance from each other, so guests feel free and do not interfere with each other.

Each sun lounger has a sun protection umbrella. You can use mattresses for 1 euro per day. Guests bring towels or purchase on site.





The complex has a spacious outdoor pool. Water is constantly purified and passes through filters. In the morning, workers clean the bottom with a special vacuum cleaner.

A fenced children's area with a shallow depth is organized in the pool. Around are loungers with parasols.

Nutrition

Titan Club Hotel Alanya operates on an all-inclusive basis. Here, the spacious restaurant serves comprehensive buffet meals. For each meal, a rather rich assortment of dishes is displayed on special racks. There is always a menu of different types of meat, fish and fresh seafood.

In sufficient quantities, spread fresh fruits and vegetables on racks. Chefs cook various desserts and pastries with their own hands.





The beach also has a small bar. Guests can take soft drinks, cocktails and spirits at any time. At lunchtime, vacationers are offered a snack pizza, barbecue or traditional Turkish pastries.

There is also a bar near the pool that offers guests a variety of drinks. Ice cream is distributed once a day. Tourists can have a light meal.

Leisure

The complex has a comfortable and safe playground. There is also a table tennis table. Dart enthusiasts can play a game of darts.

For a fee, you can rent a sports bike and enjoy a walk while exploring the area. In addition, the hotel can rent the necessary equipment for diving and other water sports. Rental paid by guests separately.

A modern fitness center is on site. Sports fans will find here the necessary simulators for classes and sports equipment. In the morning, the animators conduct invigorating exercises for guests. Aqua fitness training sessions are available for guests in the pool.

Entertainment and other services

At the Club Hotel Titan 4 * in Mahmutlar (Turkey) , animators work on a regular basis. They spend time with both children and adults. For children, interesting sporting events and various show programs are organized. A specially trained animator conducts creative activities with children.





In the evenings, for all vacationers, a thematic show program is held or contests are held with the presentation of small prizes. Incendiary discos are also organized several times a week.

The complex has a hammam (Turkish bath), a small spa and beauty salon. A visit to the hamam is included in the price of the tour. For a fee, guests can enjoy several relaxing massage sessions or take a steam bath in the sauna.

The hotel has a business center with all the necessary equipment - any business meeting will be organized at a decent level. Car rental and safe are available. Several souvenir shops and small shops on site offer souvenirs.

The hotel has designed facilities for a comfortable and safe stay for people with disabilities.

A shuttle service allows you to travel to nearby cities to visit excursions or go shopping. Ask about prices in advance.

Club Hotel Titan 4 * in Turkey: reviews

On the Internet on various resources posted a lot of reviews about this hotel. The comments are mostly positive. Guests love the beautifully landscaped area, surrounded by greenery. Most hotels in Turkey can not boast of this.

Vacationers are happy with the opportunity to relax on their own spacious balconies. According to the guests, the terraces are braided with magnificent plants with large flowers.





Concerning the arrangement of rooms there are opposite opinions. Some guests are satisfied with the modest decoration of the room, others note that the furniture is noticeably outdated and needs to be replaced. Also, many tourists are saddened by the fact that in the shower a small tray and while bathing, the water spills onto the floor.

Food almost everyone is happy. The choice of dishes is not as huge as in large hotel complexes, but the food is always fresh and tasty. A few comments can be found regarding the quality of alcoholic beverages. Many guests believe that they are diluted with water.

Also, many negative comments were written about the unsuccessful descent into the sea. According to reviews, building boards and many stones lie on the shore. On the trip you need to take special shoes for the beaches.

Mostly satisfied with the service at the hotel. They indicate the friendly attitude of the staff towards customers. Cleanliness in the pool and the work of cheerful animators vacationers are quite satisfied.
