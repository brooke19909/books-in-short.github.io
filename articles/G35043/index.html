Hotel Ajman Kempinski Hotel & Resort 5 *, United Arab Emirates: overview

In recent years, holidays in the United Arab Emirates are gaining more and more popularity. Tourists often come to Dubai or Sharjah located nearby, but hotel accommodation is quite expensive. If you want to save, then you can choose a less popular resort. For example, the emirate of Ajman is famous for its snow-white beaches, considered the best in the country. In addition, one of the best hotels in the UAE was built on its territory. A detailed review of Kempinski Hotel Ajman 5 * and reviews on this complex you can see below in this article.

More about the location of this hotel

This complex is built on the coast of the Persian Gulf in the city of Ajman, which is the capital of the emirate of the same name. And although it is considered one of the poorest in the country, this does not affect the quality of service. Of course, there are practically no attractions in the city, however, tourists can, if desired, get to the major centers located nearby - Dubai and Sharjah - in a short period of time. The distance to them is about 25 km.

A significant advantage of the complex Ajman Kempinski Hotel & Resort 5 * tourists consider its favorable location. The fact is that it is built directly on the sandy coast, which is considered one of the best in the country. The hotel‚Äôs own beach can be reached in a couple of minutes, as it is only 50 meters from the residential building. This complex can be advised to lovers of a relaxing and secluded vacation, because most of the shopping centers, nightclubs and bars are at a decent distance from here. But in the evenings and nights the hotel is very quiet.

The nearest international airports hosting tourists from Russia are in Dubai and Sharjah. From there you can get to the hotel by taxi, there is no long-distance communication with Ajman in the UAE. Also, for a fee, you can pre-book a transfer to the hotel.

Prices at Ajman Kempinski Hotel & Resort (Ajman, United Arab Emirates) vary depending on the season. The most expensive places to relax here are in autumn and winter, especially on the eve of Christmas and New Year. At this time, the cost of a double room in a hotel starts from 11,000 rubles per night. It is much more profitable to relax here in the summer or in the spring - these months in the UAE are considered a low season. For example, in June you can rent a hotel room for only 5,000 rubles.

It is worth noting that when checking in all the guests, a cash deposit is charged from them. The hotel staff speaks not only English, but also Russian. Pets are strictly forbidden even at an additional cost.

Apartment equipment

All rooms of the Ajman Kempinski Hotel & Resort 5 * hotel are equipped not only with modern high-quality furniture, but also with a number of additional amenities. So, having settled in the apartment, tourists can watch a plasma TV with a large diagonal. A set of cable TV channels is connected to it, among which there are Russians. You can contact the administrator by phone. With it, tourists can also order food and drinks directly in their rooms, but for a fee.

This service is provided around the clock. Ajman is a hot city, so each room has its own individual air conditioning. Free ironing facilities and an electronic safe are available for tourists. in which you can leave valuables and documents. Also, guests are provided with free internet access and a set for making hot drinks: tea, coffee, sugar, cream and the necessary utensils.

Most of the bathrooms are equipped with showers, but the superior rooms have marble bathtubs. Guests can make use of a hairdryer and amenities. Towels, like bed linen, maids change every day during tidying rooms.

Catering

It is immediately worth noting that the Ajman Kempinski Hotel & Resort 5 * hotel does not offer its guests the popular All Inclusive system. But tourists buying a ticket can choose one of the concepts they liked. The hotel has the following:

  • Only breakfast. A buffet is served each morning in the hotel's main restaurant.
  • Half board. It includes not only breakfast, but also a choice of lunch or dinner. They are also served in the main restaurant. If desired, guests can pay for an improved version of the concept, which also includes soft and hot drinks, homemade wine and beer.
  • Full board. This is a full three meals a day in the main restaurant of the hotel. At the same time, drinks are not initially included in the price, but they are included in an improved version of the concept.

The main hotel restaurant also provides a free children's menu. Dietary and vegetarian options are available for a fee on request.

In addition, the complex has 2 restaurants specializing in Indian and Italian cuisine, as well as 7 bars, including a sports one. It is worth noting that most of them do not work during Ramadan.

Hotel infrastructure

The territory of the Ajman Kempinski 5 * hotel has a developed and thought-out infrastructure. Therefore, if desired, tourists can not leave it during the entire vacation. We list the main objects and services offered in the complex:

  • business center with modern conference rooms;
  • the ability to call a doctor in the room;
  • point for exchange and cash out of currency;
  • laundry;
  • paid beauty shop;
  • parking for cars and the possibility of renting them;
  • several shops: souvenir, jewelry, grocery.

Beach vacation

The Ajman Kempinski Hotel in the UAE has its own beach, which is considered one of the best in the country. It is completely sandy, and its length is about 500 meters. On the coast, sun loungers and umbrellas are everywhere, creating a shadow protecting from the sun. All guests can use them for free. At the reception they can also get beach towels and mattresses. An outdoor water entertainment center is open every day. Tourists can enjoy water skiing, banana or jet skiing. These activities are available for a fee. Also on the beach is a playground for playing volleyball in the sand.

An outdoor pool with water slides is on site. The pond is equipped with a water temperature control mode. Nearby is also a sunbathing area with free sunbeds, mattresses and umbrellas.

Other entertainment options

On the territory of Ajman Hotel 5 * (United Arab Emirates, Emirate of Ajman, Ajman) a lot of entertainment facilities are open, focused on both active and relaxing pastime. Tourists can play tennis, volleyball, billiards, minigolf or bowling. If you wish, you can also go to the gym or visit the spa, which has a hairdresser, massage room and a jacuzzi.

Conditions for accommodating children

The Kempinski Hotel Ajman 5 * (United Arab Emirates, Ajman) is located in a quiet place, so it is perfect for families with kids. For them, the complex created ideal conditions. Children can play in the outdoor area, take a swim in the shallow pool or visit the mini zoo. A mini club is on site. Guests with children are provided with a cot in the room, and in the restaurant you can take chairs for convenient feeding of the kids.

Positive feedback about the hotel Kempinski Hotel Ajman 5 * (United Arab Emirates, Ajman)

This complex is popular with tourists from Russia, they liked the ratio of the cost of living and the quality of services provided. Many of them indicate that they will definitely recommend this hotel to their friends and relatives, and also will definitely come here again. In their opinion, it is most suitable for a relaxing holiday with your family.

In their reviews of Ajman Hotel (United Arab Emirates, Ajman), tourists indicate the following advantages of this complex:

  • stunning views of the beach with coconut trees and the sea, which opens from the windows of the rooms;
  • cool water is maintained in the pools, which is especially pleasant in hot weather;
  • helpful staff who try to fulfill all requests quickly and efficiently;
  • varied and satisfying meals, the buffet always has a large selection of fruits and vegetables;
  • Spacious, comfortable and clean rooms with freshly done renovations.

Hotel criticism

Unfortunately, the hotel Ajman Kempinski 5 * in Ajman (UAE) sometimes gets negative reviews. Not all tourists liked the local level of service. Some of them found a lot of flaws. Often they are insignificant, although sometimes these shortcomings can spoil the impression of rest. In their reviews, tourists focus on the following disadvantages:

  • poor cleaning of rooms, employees do not always regularly change towels and bed linen;
  • crows walk on the beach, which can steal the guests' belongings;
  • Free drinks are served only in the main hotel restaurant;
  • the hotel looks outdated;
  • expensive food, in a nearby cafe you can dine cheaper.

Thus, the hotel Ajman Kempinski 5 * ("Ajman Kempinski" in the UAE) can be called one of the best in the emirate of Ajman. It is well suited for a relaxing beach holiday, but for youth companies who love nightclubs and bars, it can be a little boring.
