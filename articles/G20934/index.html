Frunze Park (Yevpatoriya): description and photos

On the northwestern coast of Crimea is the beautiful city of Yevpatoriya. Frunze Park (photo is in this article) is located in its center. The leisure complex attracts tourists not only for its entertainment and attractions, but also for its wonderful exotic plants, amazing elements of landscape design.

Location

One of the most famous and largest organized vacation spots in Crimea is Frunze Park, Yevpatoriya. The address of the complex: Russia, Crimea, Yevpatoria, Frunze Street, 1. The city itself is a world-famous resort with a mild climate and rich air with healing sea and coniferous aromas.



The appearance of the green zone

Frunze Park (Evpatoria) has existed for a century. Earlier, on the site of the complex was the summer residence of Mlinarich, an employee of the shipping industry. The landowner began the construction of his estate in 1914. Around the site were planted many trees (olives, several species of conifers, etc.), acacia bushes and a rose garden and flower beds. In the adjoining public garden, Mlynaric erected several Greek statues.

In 1917, the landowner left Crimea with the White Guards. For ten years, no one looked after the estate and the square. But in 1926 the landscaping of the park was continued. Many sculptures by that time became worthless, part of the vegetation died. Therefore, new plants were planted, decor elements and beautiful benches were installed.



Park history

Already in the 19th century, the place where the Frunze park (Yevpatoria) is located was very picturesque. This square attracted the attention of Emperor Alexander I. Then a fountain and a pool were installed.

The land around the sanatorium was landscaped. Benches, arbors and sidewalks appeared. Over time, marble statues were erected and artificial grottoes were built. The sanatorium was preserved during the Second World War. After the war, rides were set up in Frunze Park.

Later, youth platforms appeared: roller, rock and skatedromes. A place was allocated for paintball and a bowling alley. Then came the amazing ‚ÄúTown of Fairy Tales‚Äù. As a result, a huge recreation complex has grown on the landowner's estate.



Mineral pump room

The history of the park is inextricably linked with the pump room, which contains mineral water. It was fed up from deep underground wells. In wartime, the pump room was destroyed, but it was restored in 2004. The depth of the wells is 1250 m. The healing properties of the water and its taste are very reminiscent of Borjomi. You can try the mineral drink absolutely free.

Children's entertainment

Frunze Central Park (Yevpatoriya) has its own range of attractions. Go-kart enthusiasts can visit the separate mini-stadium. "Town of fairy tales" is interesting not only for children, but also for adults. In the park you can see strolling fairytale characters: goblin, mermaid, etc.

There are many attractions here. Children can ride not only carousels, but also ponies, minibuses and mini-locomotives. The park has a lot of inflatable slides and trampolines. Fans of the competition can try their hand at special venues where there are rental bikes, rollers and skateboards.



Concert programs and cafes

Frunze Park (Yevpatoriya) is also appreciated by lovers of pop music. The complex constantly hosts concert programs, various quizzes and discos. As well as interactive games and contests.

There are many cafes and small restaurants in the park, where even gourmets can find something for themselves. There are small bars and rows with fast food. The wine served at the table, mainly local Crimean spill.

Entertainment for adults

Frunze Park has a number of entertainment for adults. There are billiards, bowling, horseback riding. Separately, there is a paintball field. There are tents and small shops on the terraces. During the summer season, many souvenirs are sold. For example, valuable Crimean aromatic oils, amazingly shaped shells, charms and magnets with Crimean landscapes, etc.

Park Attractions

The aquarium is the pride of Frunze Park. It was opened in the spring of 2010. In the aquarium there are more than a hundred species of marine life.

There are many interesting places on the territory of the complex and in its environs: the water park U Lukomorya, and the dolphinarium. There is even a small square of miniatures, in which manors, palaces, fortresses, etc. are presented in a greatly reduced form.



The sculpture of the resting Hercules has already become known throughout the world. This place has its own legend. When tired Hercules decided to relax and gain strength, he saw an azure bay with soft sand. And breathing in the healing air, the demigod decided that this place is the best for relaxation. Since then, all visitors can restore their health in Yevpatoria.

Park named after Frunze (Evpatoria) offers many sightseeing tours in the Crimea. Tickets for theaters, dolphinariums and concerts can be purchased on site. For nightlife lovers there are special clubs. In 2003, a long alley of fountains opened on the territory of the complex. The park has another feature: through it you can easily go to the central beach.

Park visit

Entrance to the Frunze park (Yevpatoriya) is free. In summer, the recreation complex runs almost until dawn. The park starts at 10:00 in the morning. After sunset, night clubs open their doors. Vacationers are very much appreciated "Europe", where various discos are held. Getting to the central park is not difficult. First, by public transport you need to get to Frunze Street and then go down it to the side of the embankment.
