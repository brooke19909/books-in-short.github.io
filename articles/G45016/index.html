Imeros Hotel 3 in Beldibi. Find tourist reviews

Holidays in the Republic of Turkey today are the most convenient vacation option for Russians. All you need to do for this is to buy a ticket at a travel agency and board a plane. They will take care of you further: they will meet, spend, arrange, serve, treat, and then kindly spend, wishing them a happy journey and an early return. Turkey is accessible for people with different incomes, while the attitude towards everyone is almost equally friendly, and it does not matter which category of hotel you stay in - at the five-star Robinson Club or Imeros Hotel 3 * in Kemer. Unlike other Mediterranean countries, in Turkey, not only luxury hotels, but also more modest, budget hotels can operate on an all-inclusive basis. This article will focus on the wonderful resort of Kemer, and in particular the Imeros Hotel 3 Beldibi.



About the resort

For more than 20 years, the coasts of the Mediterranean and Aegean Seas of Turkey have become a favorite vacation spot for Russian tourists. The prices here are relatively low, a wide variety of hotel complexes, a pleasant climate and, most importantly, high-quality service. One of the most popular resorts is the Kemer region. It is located on the southern Mediterranean coast of Turkey. Its coastline stretched over 60 kilometers. The region consists of five large villages, namely: Goynuk, Kurish, Camyuva, Tekirova and Beldibi. Here in the last of them is the Imeros Hotel 3 * Kemer Beldibi, mentioned at the beginning of the article .



Nature and climate

The surroundings of Kemer are very picturesque. Here, the emerald slopes of the mountains, over which majestic cliffs of a fanciful form rise, lush vegetation that approaches the turquoise surface of the sea. The Mediterranean climate is one of the most favorable in the whole world. What epithets are not awarded to him: soft, pleasant, comfortable, etc. Indeed, vacationers here are more than comfortable. There is no sweltering heat, suffocating dampness or, conversely, dryness, the sun shines almost all year round, and there is little rainfall. All this makes the resorts along the coast a welcome destination for tourists from all over the world.

In Kemer, the swimming season starts from the first of May and ends at the end of October. Therefore, everyone who wants to expedite their meeting with the sea, as soon as possible to find themselves in comfortable natural conditions and spend May holidays on the Mediterranean coast, while it rains continuously in their native Moscow, can buy a ticket to Imeros Hotel 3 * Kemer or to some other resort Turkey and in a matter of hours to be in one of the closest heavenly corners to us on the planet. By the way, in Kemer and other southern regions of Turkey you can relax throughout the year. Of course, in the off-season you can hardly swim in sea water, but you can sunbathe unhindered. As you can see in the picture, around the spacious pool of Imeros Hotel 3 * there are a large number of comfortable sun loungers, sunbathing on which is just a pleasure. Unfortunately, this hotel does not have an indoor pool, the services of which can be used in the cold season, so if you are a fan of swimming, then you better choose another hotel for your vacation from November to May.



Service level

Many of the hotels from 3 * to 5 * categories operate on an all-inclusive basis. This applies primarily to the method of nutrition. Russian tourists, which is true, it is true, is primarily attracted to this circumstance. With these systems, vacationers on site can use all the benefits provided by the hotel from morning to late in the evening, while meals are served on a buffet basis. Of course, a la carte restaurants also operate on the territory of many hotel complexes, but Russian tourists still prefer to use the buffet.

A very important point in the service of Kemer hotels, for example, Imeros Hotel 3 *, is that they are adapted for Russian and Russian-speaking (from the CIS countries) tourists and keep Russian-speaking staff on staff. Of course, this is a big advantage. For example, in travel blogs where tourists who have visited the Imeros Hotel 3 * share their impressions, reviews regarding this feature are always commendable. Almost all hotels in Turkey and in particular Kemer have all the necessary conditions for an interesting and eventful vacation: saunas, spa centers, restaurants, swimming pools, discos, bars and cafes. In addition, interesting tours are organized for the guests of the country to the sights that Turkey is rich in, and interesting entertainment programs for both adults and small vacationers.



Beaches

No matter how comfortable and well-groomed the territory of the hotel complexes, however, for tourists who come to the sea, the beach and water activities are primarily important. From this point of view, everything is perfectly organized in Kemer. There are many water activities, of course for a fee, on the beaches there are comfortable changing rooms, a toilet, sun loungers and umbrellas. However, if you prefer sandy beaches, you are unlikely to find them here. On the coast of this resort region, almost all beaches are pebble, and even if some five-star hotels have artificially sandy beach areas, the seabed is covered with small pebbles everywhere. Imeros hotel 3 * has its own beach, but it, as in other budget hotels, is not sandy.

Kemer Attractions

Regardless of which of the resort villages of Kemer you relax, you will be offered to see the sights of the entire region. The city port itself is beautiful, here at the entrance there is an arch in the ancient Greek architectural style. By renting a snow-white yacht, you can make an unforgettable tour of the water and from the sea to explore the entire coastal strip. Here, near the port, there are Yoryuk park (a kind of ethno-historical museum under the open sky, where you can get acquainted with the traditions and features of nomadic Turkic peoples) and Moonlight Park (Lunapark). It hosts various entertainment events every evening, there are many cafes and bars. Lunapark will especially like young tourists. Indeed, on its territory there is a chic dolphinarium with an exciting program and a kids club. Olbia Park is a little more modest, but it is also a great place for an interesting pastime. Since the Imeros hotel 3 * is located only 18 kilometers from Kemer - in the village of Beldibi - and there are regular flights from there to the city, tourists can come here from time to time in search of entertainment for themselves and their children. For tourists who prefer an informative vacation, that is, various excursions to ancient architectural monuments, trips can be organized to the majestic ruins of the ancient Greek cities of Phaselis and Olympos, as well as to the church of St. Nicolas in the city of Demre. Here you can also see the well-preserved amphitheater, built in the first century AD.

Description of the hotel Imeros 3 *

A special charm to the hotel is given by the giant azure pool, which is located opposite the main four-story building. Around it are 13 wooden bungalows with all amenities, as well as sun loungers and umbrellas for lovers of air and sun baths. The rooms of the Imeros 3 * hotel, regardless of their category, have air conditioning, TVs broadcasting Russian channels, and telephones. Number of rooms and meals
