Black Sea: recreation centers and pensions

Recreation facilities on the Black Sea are unprecedentedly popular in the warm period (from mid-May to September). A huge flow of tourists from different countries comes here. So, what are the most popular recreation centers and boarding houses? Where are the best conditions provided?



"Friendly Beach" (Gelendzhik)

Pension "Friendly Beach" in Gelendzhik is one of the most popular among tourists who value relaxation in good conditions. It is located near a very picturesque bay, behind which you can see the mountain peaks covered with dense vegetation.

The average water temperature in the summer here is about 23 degrees, which allows you to swim daily in comfortable conditions. Water and beaches are clean.

The guesthouse is located in a high 17-storey building on the central promenade. Each room on the ‚ÄúFriendly Shore‚Äù offers a magnificent view of the Caucasian landscapes and the sea.

The boarding house has its own private pebble beach, which is located on its territory, 50 meters from the building itself. If desired, guests can take water treatments in the pool, which is located in the pension. Near it there is a large sun terrace, where vacationers can sunbathe on comfortable sun loungers.

The guests of the boarding house have a lot of entertainment. In particular, in the evenings there are noisy discos where vacationers can have fun under the hits of popular artists.

The boarding house "Friendly Beach" in Gelendzhik has all the conditions for living with children. Not far from it is a city water park, where you can go with the whole family.

The cost of living in a boarding house starts from 1990 rubles per night in a single room.

"Friendly beach" is located at: Gelendzhik, Lunacharsky Street, 133.



"Alenka" (Anapa)

The excellent recreation center "Alenka" in Anapa attracts the attention of a considerable number of tourists. This is due to the mass of positive reviews that vacationers leave about her on the Internet. It is located in the village of Dzhemete, which is a district of the city of Anapa.

The number of rooms of the recreation center is located in 2 and 3-story buildings. Each of the rooms is equipped in such a way that it is very convenient for guests to be in them. They have standard furniture and bathrooms. Each room also has a modern air conditioning system that allows you to maintain optimal air temperature at any time of the year.

"Alenka" is a recreation center on the Black Sea with a sandy beach. The beach is visible from every window of the pension. The water here is always warm and clean.

The complex has many conditions for children. In particular, a sandbox swing and a playground are installed here. Adults are very pleased with the presence of arbors in the courtyard of the boarding house. There are also tables for relaxing and barbecue.

The cost of living in the recreation center on the Black Sea "Alenka" is from 800 rubles for a triple room. It should be noted that this is a very democratic indicator. Therefore, boarding house "Alenka" is often referred to the category of inexpensive recreation facilities on the Black Sea.

Located "Alenka" at the address: Anapa, the village of Dzhemete, Pioneer Avenue, 70g.



Adler

The recreation center "Adler" is located on the Black Sea coast, near the city of the same name. It is an ideal option to relax with the whole family.

On the territory of "Adler" there is a main building in which rooms are located, as well as 11 cottages that can be rented for relaxation by one large company. Here are numbers of various categories:

  • double "Standard";
  • double (superior comfort);
  • two-room;
  • three-room two-level.

All of them are provided with the necessary furniture for a comfortable stay, a TV, a refrigerator, as well as dishes. Each room has a private shower room and a bathroom.

"Adler" is a recreation center on the Black Sea, which has its own sandy beach, which is equipped with comfortable sun loungers and umbrellas. It can be rented boats, water skis, boats, yachts and some items of sports equipment. Here you can also engage in some active water sports.

The cost of living per day in this boarding house starts from 1600 rubles.

Recreation center on the Black Sea "Adler" is located at: Adler, with. Fun, Tsimlyanskaya street, 1.



"Orange" (Bzhid)

"Orange" is a recreation center on the Black Sea, which is a club hotel. It is located in the village of Bzhid, on the coast of the Blue Bay.

The attention of the guests here is a large swimming pool, cafes and bars, as well as a playground. There are all conditions for relaxing with the kids.



The hotel consists of 36 rooms, which are located in guest buildings. They are divided into categories such as: "Standard", "Family", "Superior". In addition, there are several cottages that are provided for rent by large companies.

The guesthouse has its own well-kept beach, which is 300 meters away. This beach has showers and toilets, as well as sun loungers and changing rooms.

The cost of living in the territory of such a boarding house starts from 2100 rubles per day. It includes three meals a day at the hotel.

"Orange" is located at: Bzhid village (Tuapse district), Blue Bay, Gazovikov street, 18a.

Recreation facilities on the Black Sea, suitable for families

Often, going on vacation, vacationers try to choose a boarding house or recreation center, based on individual needs. So, if you need to go on vacation with the whole family, accommodation in such boarding houses as: Albatros (Arkhipo-Osipovka village), Aqua-Vita (Mostovskoy village), Anapa, Anaida (Adler) is an excellent option , "Anapa-Patio", "AnBeranda" (Yakornaya Slit village), "Arion" (Gelendzhik), "Harp" (Sochi), "Bottlenose dolphin" (Anapa).



On the territory of each of the listed recreation centers there are all necessary conditions for a comfortable stay with children: swimming pools, highchairs in cafes, animators, entertainment programs for kids. Also nearby are places and places ideal for families with children (water parks, botanical gardens, zoos, dolphinariums).

Pensions with private beach

Often for vacationers, the proximity of the beach and its cleanliness are of great importance. The best of all resorts that have access to the Black Sea are: Barguzin, White Swan (Anapa), White Beach (Dzhemete), Vecar (Sochi), Villa Bonita (Novorossiysk, Durso settlement ), Aquarius (Anapa), Gamma (the village of Olginka), Blue Bay (Anapa), Eugene (Anapa).

The personal beaches of these pensions are equipped with everything necessary for a comfortable stay. They are equipped with sun loungers, showers, and changing rooms.

Cheap recreation facilities

If there is a need for savings, then, in this case, you can do this by choosing a boarding house, the cost of living in which will be low. On the Black Sea coast, good conditions at affordable prices are offered in such boarding houses as: Gloria (Sochi, from 560 rubles per day), Albatross (Adler, from 690 rubles), Angela-Lantana (Sochi, from 600 rubles), Atelika (Nebug, from 690 rubles), Bohemia (Novomikhailovsky village, from 600 rubles).

Based on the reviews left by the guests of these guest houses on the Internet, a wide range of services and comfortable living conditions are provided for inexpensive payment.
