The main standards of TV are as follows:

Differences between DVB-T and DVB-C

The differences between the different types of DVB standard have already been discussed above. If you do not have access to the cable, you can use the DVB-S connection. Moreover, modern LG TVs with DVB-C S2 are available for sale today. But can it be compared with a digital format?

The DVB-T standard guarantees high-quality signal reception. He is able to not perceive the reflected signals. Channel coding is used to increase immunity to various interference. This standard carries out exactly the same broadcasting, as is the case with analog television, but the transmission format and technology are used differently. DVB-T terrestrial broadcasting is completely different from satellite DVB-S or cable DVB-C. Decoders have completely different frequency channels and modulation methods, so they are not compatible with each other.

Digital TV is broadcast in the decimeter spectrum. Therefore, such indicators as the topography of the territory, the openness of the terrain to the transmitter and the compactness of the building affect the quality of signal reception.
