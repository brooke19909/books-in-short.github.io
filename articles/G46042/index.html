Accommodation

Guests of The Sun Siyam Iru Fushi Maldives can plunge into the atmosphere of comfort and luxury. Guests are available with the following options for accommodation:

  • Villa Summary are located on the beach and directly above the water. All the houses are equipped with comfortable bedrooms, living areas and combined bathrooms. There are private terraces overlooking the tropical garden or the sea.
  • Celebrity Retreat are two-story houses located in the middle of a lush tropical garden. Each of the villas can accommodate up to 10 people. On the top floor there are three bedrooms with individual bathrooms. One of them has access to its own terrace with a pool. The lower floor has a large living room and a billiard room. There is a large circular terrace, a private pool and a gazebo.
  • Aqua Retreat are cozy and secluded rooms that literally hang over the water. There are two bedrooms with individual bathrooms, a living room and an outdoor seating area.
  • Hidden Retreat are secluded villas located in a tropical garden. Inside, there are 2 bedrooms, each of which has its own bathroom with rain shower. There is access to a private terrace with a pool and sun loungers.

Catering establishments

In The Sun Siyam Iru Fushi Maldives 5 * cares not only about relaxation, but also about the full nutrition of guests. This function is assigned to such institutions:

  • Iru Restaurant is the main restaurant, which is responsible for delicious and wholesome breakfasts and dinners for guests. From 7:00 to 13:00, guests can try a huge assortment of dishes from organic products, served according to the buffet system. During the dinners, which are held from 18:30 to 22:00, guests can enjoy all the new dishes related to different cuisines of the world.
  • Islander's Grill - A restaurant located on the sea coast. The institution specializes in seafood cooked over an open fire. A good addition to the meal will be quality wine.
  • The Trio is a restaurant located on the terrace above the lagoon. The institution specializes in cocktails and dishes of classic European cuisine.
  • Bamboo is a themed restaurant specializing in traditional Thai cuisine. Here guests can have lunch and dinner.
  • Teppanyaki is a Japanese restaurant located in the shade of tropical palm trees. Natural bamboo finishes create a special atmosphere. The establishment has an open kitchen, so you can watch how your dishes are prepared. It also features traditional sake vodka.
  • Flavors - Restaurant over the water. An innovative cuisine is presented here, based on the best culinary traditions of France.
  • The Wine Cellar is a unique establishment with a huge wine collection. The sommelier will help you choose the perfect drink for your meal.
  • Fluid - a cozy restaurant near the pool. Specializes in refreshing cocktails. Here you can also eat pizza, light salads and sandwiches.
  • Taste of India at Fluid is another place where you can spend an unforgettable dinner in the Indian style.
  • La Cremeria is a buffet where you can taste the most delicious ice cream and fruit sorbets. You can also enjoy milkshakes and cold coffee.

Negative reviews

Unfortunately, not only positive moments are characterized by the rest at the Sun Siyan Iru Fushi Maldives hotel. The reviews indicate a number of significant shortcomings, among which it is especially worth noting:

  • the attitude of the staff towards the tourists is slightly cool;
  • the maids do the cleaning very quickly, which naturally affects its quality (despite the tip);
  • because of the stone breakwater around the island, the coral reef is badly damaged, and therefore it will not be possible to see many fish and underwater beauties during snorkeling;
  • places in restaurants are booked in advance, and therefore it will not work every night to visit a new institution (you also have to reserve a place for the entire stay);
  • the villas are very close to each other, and therefore there is no sense of privacy; there are always a lot of people in the territory;
  • overpriced food prices in restaurants (especially on holidays);
  • the main restaurant mainly offers Asian cuisine, which not everyone will like;
  • slow waiters;
  • in houses the smell of the sewerage is periodically felt;
  • maids rarely change bedding;
  • many mosquitoes;
  • evening entertainment ends early enough when no one else wants to sleep;
  • in the villas that are located on the beach, there is no privacy, since there are always a large number of people who strive to look at your territory;
  • the hotel does not solve the problem of insects and rats;
  • ashore periodically makes a large number of plastic bottles, bags and other vital products of tourists;
  • there is no special pier for airplanes, and therefore they fly directly to the island, which is unsafe for vacationers, and also creates a lot of noise;
  • poor wireless internet signal;
  • the hotel is constantly being renovated, and therefore it is very uncomfortable to be in houses that are not far from the marina;
  • near the villas there are no bathtubs to wash your feet from sand (in order not to carry dirt in the room, you have to water your feet from plastic bottles).

Conclusion

Travelers have already had a definite impression of The Sun Siyam Iru Fushi Maldives hotel. Reviews of tourists are quite diverse, but on their basis certain conclusions can be drawn. Those who have visited this hotel more than once, note that recently the quality of service has slightly deteriorated. This is not surprising, because the hotel is quite old, and the prices for accommodation are among the lowest. Nevertheless, the sea and magnificent nature do their job. Despite the minor flaws in the hotel, the Maldives leaves the most positive impression.
