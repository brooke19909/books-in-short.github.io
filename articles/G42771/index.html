Khortytsya Island

In the historical and architectural complex "Zaporizhzhya Sich" embodied the most interesting elements of culture, history and everyday life of the Cossacks. The territory of the reserve is equipped taking into account all the features of the development of the previously existing Sichs. The complex is surrounded by defensive walls and other fortifications (deep moat, entrance tower, bell tower). The territory of Sich is divided into two koshas: the inner kosh and the suburbs.





The main buildings are located in the first part of the complex: two smokers, a powder cellar, and a school. In the center of the inner kosh (on the main square) stands the Sich Church of the Protection of the Virgin - the largest building in the complex. Museums of the city

Local museums - this is what the city of Zaporozhye can surprise every tourist. Here they will tell you not only about the heroic past of the region, but also demonstrate unique samples of weapons and objects of Cossack life, rare retro cars, the remains of the fleet of the XV-XVIII centuries, raised from the bottom of the Dnieper.

So, what museums are worth visiting in Zaporozhye?

  • The Museum of the History of Zaporizhzhya Cossacks is located on the island of Khortytsya. Expositions dedicated to the ancient, medieval, new and modern history of the Zaporizhzhya region are open here.
  • The Museum of the History of Weapons is located in the heart of the city on Lenin Avenue. The exposition is based on the private collection of V. Schleifer. Here are collected unique and rare samples of weapons of different times and peoples.
  • Museum of retro cars - a visit to this place will appeal not only to adults, but also to young tourists. The exhibition presents restored images of cars of the beginning - the middle of the 20th century.
  • The Museum of Shipping is a place worth visiting when exploring the sights of Zaporozhye. Here are exhibited samples of the Cossack fleet found at the bottom of the Dnieper.




Cult objects

  • Pagan sanctuaries on Khortitsa are monuments of the early Bronze Age. The main object of this complex, located on Mount Bragharnya, in appearance resembles the famous Stonehenge. Another sanctuary, which is located on one of the slopes of the beam near the Museum of the History of the Cossacks, consists of an interesting stone egg-shaped structure. Also restored on Khortitsa are the idols of the ancient pagan gods.
  • The Mennonite cemetery is a monument in Zaporozhye, which is worthy of the attention of tourists. Here you can see the ancient graves of German-speaking settlers on the island of Khortitsa of the XVIII-XX centuries.
  • Stone grave in with. Patience is an interesting object, which is a separate array of sandstone. Here was placed the oldest sanctuary.




Temples and Cathedrals

  • Holy Intercession Cathedral - a real gem of Zaporozhye, the main Orthodox shrine of the city. The modern temple, erected in 1886, is a 36-meter five-tower building. The reconstruction of the cathedral was carried out from 1993 to 2001.
  • St. Andrew's Cathedral is an excellent example of modern architecture. The building was erected in 2001 with the money of local patrons. The temple is made in the Baroque style in the form of a large cross. The building is crowned with one large central and two small domes. The cathedral is operational; today, services are regularly held in its building.
  • The Cathedral of God the Merciful Father is the only Catholic church in the city. The construction is a rectangular construction, divided inside by a number of beautiful columns. The ceremonial consecration of the temple took place in 2004.
