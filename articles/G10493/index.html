Mitsis Rodos Maris Resort Spa - a hotel complex on the island of Rhodes

Description

The beautiful Mitsis Rodos Maris Resort Spa offers guests an exceptional holiday on the sunny island of Rhodes in a comfortable and warm atmosphere away from the hustle and bustle. It is built on its own section of a large beach near the village of Kiotari and is located about ten minutes walk from the ancient city of Lindos, very close to attractions such as Asklipio Fortress, Archaeological Museum, Butterfly Valley. The distance from Rhodes Airport is only 60 kilometers.

Part of the renowned and largest all-inclusive Mitsis hotel chain in Greece, the Mitsis Rodos Maris Resort Spa, carefully renovated in 2010, represents the pinnacle of luxury and elegance.



Rooms

It offers spacious and comfortable apartments in a beautiful building surrounded by beautiful gardens and bungalows. All 423 rooms are tastefully decorated, furnished with environmentally friendly wooden furniture, equipped with all the amenities needed for a five-star hotel. Among them - individual climate control, television with access to satellite and music channels, an electronic safe (for an additional fee). The minibar is replenished once every two days (beer, water and soft drinks). The decor of the rooms used warm soothing colors, each room has a balcony or access to the terrace. Marble bathrooms have a bathtub or shower (or both), depending on the type of room. Branded toiletries, a hairdryer, a bathrobe and slippers are mandatory attributes.

Beach

Mitsis Rodos Maris Resort Spa has its own plot on the excellent Kiotari Beach, equipped with umbrellas and sun loungers. Guests can go windsurfing (certificate required), canoeing. The village itself has grown significantly over the past ten years, but it should be noted that the place does not suffer from the influx of tourists, which is characteristic of most of the other traditional resorts of Rhodes. Most vacationers on the public beach of Kiotari are romantic couples or families looking for peace and quiet. At the weekend you can see a few locals. But since most of the restaurants, shops and other institutions that tourists need are included in hotel complexes, guests spend almost all their days within their complex and on private beaches.



Information for vacationers

Hotel complex Mitsis Rodos Maris Resort Spa can be called the perfect choice for people who want to spend their holidays with their families, for everyone who is looking for a quality stay with great amenities. At your service - a lot of interesting opportunities for entertainment. The hotel building, designed in an elegant architectural style, is located around two large pools with sea water, which seem to be a continuation of the Aegean. They are equipped with umbrellas and sun loungers. There is a playground, three floodlit tennis courts, a fitness center, a sauna, massage rooms, a beauty salon. Other features: TV room, large lounge for relaxing, billiard room, gallery shops, laundry and dry cleaning. Medical assistance if necessary will be provided at any time.
