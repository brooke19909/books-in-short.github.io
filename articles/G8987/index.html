Description of hotel rooms





The hotel offers its residents a check-in in one of 158 rooms. All rooms here are furnished with modern furniture and working appliances. There is an air conditioning system with individual controls, as well as a TV on which you can watch satellite channels, including some Russian. There is a minibar in the room, but you need to pay for its filling separately. Also, for a small fee, you can rent a safe for storing money, documents and other valuables.

The room has a toilet and a bathroom. Here you can relax in the hot tub or take a shower. There is also a wash basin, a mirror, a set of branded hygiene products and clean towels.

You can enjoy garden and sea views from your balcony or terrace. Towels are cleaned and changed every day. Lingerie is changed twice a week.

Food scheme for vacationers





The hotel operates on an all-inclusive basis. This means that residents have the right to a full three meals a day, snacks during the day, as well as local drinks. Chefs in the main restaurant of the hotel constantly delight guests with new culinary masterpieces. The menu includes meat, fish, seafood, cold dishes, salads, fresh fruits, desserts, as well as delicious pastries.

The hotel has several bars - in the lobby, by the pool and on the beach. You should definitely visit the Moorish cafe, where, in addition to traditional dishes, you will be offered a hookah. And throughout the night there is a disco bar where you can have fun.

Beach holidays for travelers

The hotel is intended primarily for beach holidays and owns a small part of the coast. The beach is about 200 meters from the residential building. Here, travelers will find clean soft sand, gentle sun and soft sea waters. Descent into the sea is gentle and safe, and the coastal shallows makes this place suitable for bathing children and residents who cannot swim.

Sun beds with mattresses and umbrellas are placed along the beach - hotel guests can use them for free. Here you can get a beach towel, but it is issued on bail.

If sun bathing and swimming are not enough for you, you can try yourself in more active forms of water recreation. For example, you will have the opportunity to ride a boat, boat or water ski, as well as go diving. There is a diving school where you can get the instructions of experienced instructors and rent the necessary equipment.

In the summer, the beach has a bar that offers visitors a huge selection of chilled drinks, original exotic cocktails and dishes prepared on the grill.
