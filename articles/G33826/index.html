Palace Bridge Wellness Club: description, services, address

If you want to relax your body and soul, then you should visit the Palace Bridge Wellness Club, located in the very center of St. Petersburg. This is a full-fledged sports and fitness center, which has a complex of saunas, a huge swimming pool, spa, gym and much more. You will learn more about the club from our article.

Services and group classes

Palace Bridge Wellness Club hosts daily group workouts. Among the directions are: aerobics, Pilates, yoga, stretching, step aerobics, the program ‚ÄúHealthy back‚Äù and much more. Those who wish can visit the gym, where experienced trainers work, always ready to help.

The club teaches water aerobics and dancing. By the way, among the dance styles Zumba, Body Ballet and others are represented. Many regular customers after group or individual training go to the sauna, hammam or pool, where you can relax and unwind.

Among the popular services is hydromassage, which positively affects the whole body, invigorates and improves mood.

Separate classes are held for expectant mothers in the Palace Bridge Wellness Club, which includes a set of specially selected exercises that will help prepare the body for the upcoming birth. The load is selected depending on the duration of pregnancy and the level of preparation of the woman.

Club address

Wellness Club is located in the Solo Sokos Hotel Palace Bridge, which is considered one of the best in St. Petersburg. The hotel is located in the very center of the city at the address: Birzhevoy Pereulok, house 4. The nearest metro station is Vasileostrovskaya.

Visitors reviews

Reviews of Palace Bridge Wellness Club are mostly positive. Visitors say that this center has all the conditions for playing sports and maintaining excellent physical shape. In addition, you can relax and unwind in the spa area or large pool. The cost of visiting the club is quite high, but this does not frighten visitors. According to reviews, in the evening there are always quite a lot of people. By the way, hotel guests can visit the club for free, which is very popular with tourists and guests of the cultural capital. Kids under 3 years old do not need to buy a separate ticket, and for children from 3 to 13 years old a 50% discount is applied. Many residents of St. Petersburg come to the club as a family to spend a day off for the benefit of the body, warm themselves well in the sauna, and then refresh themselves in the pool. Visitors note that the club always has a pleasant atmosphere.

Conclusion

The Palace Bridge Wellness Club complex has everything you need for sports and healthy rest. Visitors consider this club one of the best in the city, but many are frightened by the high price. True, having visited this chic place at least once, you will forever save a lot of pleasant impressions about it.
