Hotels in Ivanovo: addresses, rating of the best hotels, description of rooms, photos and reviews

Ivanovo is one of the cities of the Golden Ring of Russia. "City of brides" has a rich history and can please tourists with many attractions. It remains only to find a suitable accommodation option. Hotels in Ivanovo are full of variety, both in terms of prices and in terms of living conditions.

Park Hotel Sosnovy Bor

Sosnovy Bor is a cozy hotel park in Ivanovo, located in an ecologically clean area surrounded by a pine forest. The institution is located at 3 Lyubimova Street, which is 12 km from the railway station and a quarter hour drive from the central part of the city. To accommodate guests, 57 comfortable rooms (costing from 3600 rubles per day for two), as well as cozy cottages (costing from 5400 rubles per day for two) are provided. Pets are charged an additional fee of 500 rubles.

For a bright full-bodied relaxation, the hotel provides the following features:

  • two restaurants;
  • bath complex with pools;
  • health Center;
  • sports equipment rental;
  • organization of excursions;
  • business services.
