How to connect iPhone to Samsung TV: learn to display video from iPhone to TV

Televisions and mobile phones are improving every year. Now you can establish a connection between them and output videos and pictures from smartphones directly to TV. But not all gadgets have such capabilities. For example, connecting an iPhone to a TV is easy. Only not all owners of such products know how to do this. How to connect iPhone to Samsung TV ? The answer to this question we have to find below. There is no single solution. It all depends on the personal preferences and capabilities of the person. The instructions presented here will help you create a connection between your TV and iPhone.



Connection Methods

It is important to understand that modern TVs support the connection of some external devices. And it makes life a lot easier. Some TVs even allow you to connect to the Internet! All these parameters will have to be taken into account when implementing the task.

How to connect iPhone to Samsung TV ? In general, you can do this in the following ways:

  • via HDMI;
  • using composite input;
  • using a USB cable;
  • via Apple TV
  • via ChromeCast;
  • by DLNA.

The latter option is most often used in practice. You can also use a wired connection. Other options do not differ in their stability and may work with errors. But with the right actions, no difficulties will arise.

What you need for a DLNA connection

First, consider the best connection. Samsung LED TVs often have DLNA support. With it, you can connect the iPhone to the device without wires and jailbreak, which greatly facilitates the solution of the problem.



What may be required to translate ideas into reality? Mandatory required:

  • iPhone or any other "apple" device;
  • DLNA TV
  • Twonky Beam program.

That's all. Nothing more is needed. And what exactly needs to be done? How to connect iPhone to Samsung TV and more?

DLNA and online connection step by step

In fact, everything is extremely simple. Especially if you act on the proposed guidelines. They step by step explain the entire procedure for connecting iPhone to TV.

So, to use DLNA you need:

  1. Download Twonky Beam to your mobile phone.
  2. Install software on the device.
  3. Click on the gear icon in the left corner of the program.
  4. Open Settings.
  5. Select Show or hide the visual indications ...
  6. Go back to the program and open the browser there.
  7. In the address bar go to the address with the video.
  8. Open the desired video.
  9. Click on the strip on the right side of the window. An additional menu will open.
  10. Turn on the TV.
  11. Point to the TV model on the smartphone.
  12. Click again on the strip of the additional menu.
  13. Launch the movie.

Now you just have to enjoy the video. As you can already see, there is nothing incomprehensible or difficult in this approach.



Offline movie and DLNA

How to connect iPhone to Samsung TV? You can display images and videos on TV in offline mode. In this case, the actions will not differ much from the previously proposed instructions.

It is necessary to act in this way:

  1. Turn on the TV.
  2. Launch Twonky Beam on your mobile phone.
  3. Go to settings and, as in the previous case, display an additional menu on the side.
  4. Click on the sidebar on the right side of the screen.
  5. Indicate the model of the TV with which to work.
  6. Click on the image of the house in the upper right corner of the program.
  7. Find the desired document and click on it.

You can enjoy the video without much difficulty. Video will be played from iPhone on any DNLA-enabled TV.

By wifi

The following tip will help you figure out how to connect your iPhone to your Samsung TV via WiFi. Usually, this arrangement does not cause any questions. The main thing is that the phone and TV are connected to the same network.

Samsung LED TVs of the latest models are equipped with Internet access. So, you can do without wires. To display images from apple devices, the user will need:

  1. Turn on the TV and connect it to Wi-Fi. The exact algorithm of actions depends on the TV model.
  2. Connect iPhone to Wi-Fi. Usually the corresponding item is in the "Settings" menu.
  3. Download to iMediaShare or Flipps. These utilities are in the AppStore.
  4. Run the program.
  5. Open application settings.
  6. Choose a TV model that you want to work with. If the devices are connected to the same network, then there will be no problems.

This alignment greatly facilitates life. And even a novice user will be able to understand how to connect the iPhone to the Samsung TV. And what if the TV does not have the ability to connect to Wi-Fi?



No Internet connection

Do not despair. Wireless connection still takes place. Just in addition, a person will have to buy a special adapter for TV. It is called a transmitter. It costs about 2,000 rubles. With it, you can display the image from the iPhone to TV. The main thing is that the TV had HDMi connectors.

Connection instructions look like this:

  1. Insert the transmitter into the TV.
  2. Install iMediaShare on the apple phone and launch it.
  3. Connect iPhone to the Internet.
  4. Select the desired TV in the program.

As practice shows, this technique is not too often applied in practice. Therefore, users prefer a wired connection.

USB cable

How to connect iPhone to TV via USB cable? The answer is not long in coming. This is a pretty simple task!

The user requires:

  1. Turn on the TV.
  2. Connect the apple device to USB.
  3. Insert the wire into the USB socket on the TV.
  4. In the TV settings, display the image from the USB port.

This is usually enough. This technique does not cause any particular difficulties. How to connect using an HDMI cable? The same way. Only in the TV settings will you have to specify the playback of data from the HDMI port.

Apple tv

The easiest solution for many is to use AppleTV. This technique is suitable for devices that can connect to WiFi. How to connect iPhone to Samsung TV in this way?



You will need:

  1. Install on your AirPlay mobile device.
  2. Connect TV to AppleTV and to Wi-Fi.
  3. Turn on Wi-Fi on the iPhone.
  4. Open any video on the device and start watching it.
  5. Swipe up from the screen.
  6. Click on the AirPlay button.
  7. Choose AppleTV.
