Mezhdurechensk hotels (Kemerovo region): overview, description, reviews

Mezhdurechensk is a small town in the Kemerovo region, which is conveniently located at the foot of the Kuznetsk Alatau, right between the rivers Usa and Tom. There are enough interesting places here, but there are not so many hotels. The most famous are Yugus, Robinson and London House. Below you can find them, as well as with three other hotels of Mezhdurechensk. They also deserve attention.

Hotel "Yugus", Mezhdurechensk

This hotel attracts visitors to the city at affordable prices and a good location. Against the backdrop of a modern five-story building, the mountains of Yugus and Syrkashi flaunt. Near the hotel there are a shopping center, city park, Sports Palace, transport stops and various places of entertainment.

The hotel "Yugus" (Mezhdurechensk, Kemerovo region) offers the following rooms:

  • single standards;
  • double standards;
  • two-room junior suites;
  • single economy class;
  • double economy class.

The rooms are modern, equipped with electronic access system, a bathroom with shower, fridge, TV.

Additional services that the hotel offers: restaurant, billiards, cinema, sauna and solarium, casino, beauty salon.

Reviews about Yugus are different. Among the advantages, guests note delicious breakfasts, new furniture and plumbing, comfortable rooms, convenient location. The disadvantages of the hotel are as follows: often a broken elevator, paid use of a hairdryer and a kettle, lack of internet.

Hotel address: Avenue 50 years of Komsomol, 16.

Comfortable hotel "Robinson"

Robinson is one of the most famous hotels in Mezhdurechensk. And it is popular not only among guests, but also among residents of the city.

First of all, guests note a convenient location. Hotel "Robinson" is located at: Vesennyaya street, 24. It is in the very center of the city and only five kilometers from the railway station.

Flattering tourists speak about the hotel rooms. There are three categories available: single standard, double standard and junior suite. All apartments have modern furniture, air conditioning, an LCD TV, a bathroom with a shower.

Breakfast is included in the price and delivered directly to the rooms.

A wide variety of services also applies to the positive aspects. Laundry, provision of iron and ironing board, parking, Wi-Fi in rooms, food and drink delivery, fax - all this is available to hotel guests. Additionally, guests can visit the sauna, go fishing, and in the winter - skiing.

Small hotel "London House"

In walking distance from the center of Mezhdurechensk is a mini-hotel "London House". It is a bright five-story building with three categories of rooms:

  • double standard;
  • four-seater comfort;
  • quadruple comfort superior.

All rooms are equipped with air conditioning, TVs, equipped bathrooms with shower. In the category "Comfort" there is additionally a kitchenette with everything you need, a dining table, a sofa bed.

Meals are not included in the price. But for an additional fee, you can order breakfast, lunch and dinner in your room.

Among the main advantages of the mini-hotel, guests note the following: free Wi-Fi in rooms, private parking, comfortable rooms, freshly renovated, good soundproofing, non-smoking in the hotel, the possibility of delivering food and drinks to the apartments, daily cleaning.

Address: Communist Avenue, 26.

Hotel "Sunrise"

This hotel Mezhdurechensk (Kemerovo region) is quite popular among tourists and business travelers. It is located at: Second Syrkashi district, just a ten-minute walk from the Tom River and a five-minute drive from the city center.

The hotel "Sunrise" can stay in the following categories of rooms:

  • double standard with two single or one double bed;
  • triple standard;
  • Junior Suite
  • Suite
  • chalet.

All rooms have a bathroom with shower, air conditioning, satellite TV, safe. The suite and junior suite have an additional folding bed, a minibar, a bathrobe.

The chalet is a detached heated house with a balcony. They have a seating area and a small kitchen with an electric kettle, a minibar, a refrigerator, a dining table. Also in the chalet has its own bathroom, TV, safe, air conditioning, sofa, bathrobes.

The guests of this hotel notice the delicious dishes served in the hotel restaurant, as well as the possibility of self-cooking barbecue in the nature.

Hotel Tomusinets

"Tomusinets" is a municipal sports complex of physical education and sports. It is located in the city center, in the territory of the city park at the address: 1a Berezovaya street.

The complex consists of a sauna with a swimming pool, a gym and a gym, there is a tennis court, an ice rink and a hotel that offers rooms from economy to VIP-class.

All apartments are equipped with modern furniture. Also in the rooms there is a bathroom, a TV, a small refrigerator.

Meals are provided in the dining room. All guests are entitled to use the swimming pool and sports facilities of the complex for an additional fee.

Guests speak about Tomusinets ambiguously. On the one hand, attractive price and decent living conditions attract. On the other hand, the service at this Mezhdurechensk hotel leaves much to be desired. In general, this hotel is ideal for one or two-day business trips.

The recreation center "Ozerki"

Lakes are not a hotel in the classical sense. This is a suburban complex, which consists of the main building with a restaurant and economy class rooms, two separate hotel houses, a bathhouse, a shooting range, a recreation area in nature.

Economy apartments are designed for one or two people. They are equipped with the necessary furniture and TV. A bathroom and shower are on the floor.

The hotel houses are deluxe and junior suites. They are equipped with a bedroom set, work desk, safe, TV, fridge. Bathroom with shower.

Reviews about the recreation center "Ozerki" are pretty flattering. Guests like comfortable rooms, the nature around, the high level of service, gazebos with barbecue facilities.

Lakes are located five kilometers from Mezhdurechensk in the Chulzhan region.
