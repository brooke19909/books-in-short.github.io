Hotel Aqua Hotel Montagut 4 * (Spain, Santa Susanna): reviews

If we are talking about beach holidays in Europe, then as an option it is worth considering the Spanish coast. Thousands of tourists stop here every year wishing to relax on the seashore and immerse themselves in the local culture. By the way, one of the most popular resorts is considered a small but very colorful town of Santa Susanna. It is here that the hotel Aqua Hotel Montagut 4 * is located.

Of course, before booking, you should familiarize yourself with all available information. What does the hotel look like? What accommodation conditions are offered to guests? Is the hotel suitable for families with children? What are tourists talking about who have already managed to spend several days here? All necessary information is presented in the article.

Location Features





To begin with, it is worth sorting out the location of the Aqua Hotel Montagut 4 * hotel complex. Santa Susanna, one of the most popular Catalan resorts - this is where many tourists prefer to spend their holidays. By the way, the distance to the beach is only 150 m. Convenient is the availability of shops and shopping centers within walking distance.

Hotel Aqua Hotel Montagut 4 * (Spain, Santa Susanna): infrastructure information

Reviews indicate that the hotel is really comfortable and cozy. It is located in a quiet area near the seashore. Hotel Aqua Hotel Montagut Suites 4 * is a large 7-storey building, built in a modern style.

It is worth saying that they have been welcoming guests here since 1989. During this time, the hotel has already managed to earn a good reputation and get a lot of positive reviews. By the way, in 2010 there was a complete reconstruction, so that guests are waiting for modern equipment and all the benefits of civilization.

A large courtyard is attached to the building, which will lead to swimming pools, terraces, sports and recreation grounds. The territory is very clean and beautiful, everywhere lush greenery and bright flowers are pleasing to the eye.

Additional information and photo numbers





The hotel complex Aqua Hotel Montagut 4 * offers its guests decent living conditions. It offers large rooms with carpeted floors, modern furniture and windows facing the courtyard or mountain views.

Since the life of a modern traveler is difficult to imagine without household appliances, the rooms are equipped in accordance with all international standards. At your service is a TV (connected to a satellite dish), a telephone, a minibar with a cooling system (drinks are delivered on request, this service is not included in the price). Important is the availability of free Internet in the rooms, because tourists always want to stay in touch.

In the bathroom, plumbing was replaced not so long ago - there is a shower, washbasin, wall mirror. Of course, guests are provided with hygiene products and clean towels. They visit the rooms every day, and they clean it well - the guests do not complain about cleanliness.

Food: what do tourists offer?





Food is an important point when planning a trip, because it is difficult to have fun on an empty stomach. Hotel Aqua Hotel Montagut 4 * has a spacious restaurant, furnished in a classic Catalan style. When booking, guests can choose the convenient scheme themselves, agreeing, for example, only to breakfast or to full three meals a day. You can completely refuse the services of local catering establishments.

By the way, former guests recommend paying at least breakfast, because the food in the hotel is very tasty - every day, restaurant visitors can enjoy delicious international dishes and real Spanish goodies.

The hotel has a small cafe and bar, where you can always spend the evening in a pleasant company.

Relax by the water: entertainment and amenities on the beach





As already mentioned, the hotel is located next to the seashore. This is not the first beach line, since the distance is about 150 m, but a convenient pedestrian road leads to the beach - the walk will not take much time.

This is a public stretch of coast, where tourists rest from other hotels. Nevertheless, the beach is relatively calm. The entrance to the sea is convenient, and for a few euros you can rent a comfortable sunbed complete with an umbrella.

Of course, there is enough entertainment on the beach, from volleyball to catamarans, boat trips and parasailing. This part of the Spanish coast is popular with fans of windsurfing - if you are a beginner, you can always take a few lessons from a professional.

Additional services: what to count on?





While staying in one of the rooms of this hotel, you can count on some additional amenities. For example, nearby is a small free parking lot, and the hotel can rent a car, motorcycle or bicycle without problems (with the necessary documents).

For a small fee, you will be provided with a safe deposit box for storing valuables. At the reception, which works around the clock, you can exchange currency at a favorable rate. If necessary, guests can use a computer, scanner, fax or other office equipment. Throughout the territory there is access to the Internet, which is very important for every modern traveler.

Hotel Aqua Hotel Montagut Suites 4 * (Spain): leisure and entertainment

The hotel area is beautiful and well-groomed - it is always a pleasure to spend time here. Part of the yard is reserved for the pool, which is popular with travelers who do not like to spend every day on the beach. Around there is a wide solarium terrace with sun loungers and umbrellas - here you can relax, take sun baths and just chat with other guests.

In the summer, the hotel has a team of animators. You can have fun playing ping pong or billiards. And all year round the doors of the gym are open - it is small, but fully equipped for full classes.

Of course, entertainment can be found outside the territory of the Aqua Hotel Montagut 4 *. Spain is a country with an interesting history, unique culture and color, so do not give up excursions. The hotel guides will be happy to help you with your travel planning.

Family vacation: living conditions with a child





This hotel is located in a quiet area - there are no round-the-clock parties and constant noise, so guests can enjoy peace and quiet. And this, as you know, is a wonderful environment for relaxing with a child. At the disposal of parents are spacious triple and quadruple rooms, in which the whole family can easily fit.

There are no special entertainments and children's animations at the hotel. Nevertheless, there is a fairly spacious playground with swings, where little tourists have a good time. Of course, the guys are happy to play on the beach, and the city has plenty of entertainment for children of any age.

How do tourists respond about the hotel?





It's no secret that people who have already managed to visit a particular place can share more relevant information. What do they say about the hotel Aqua Hotel Montagut 4 *? Reviews tourists leave positive. The hotel is really comfortable - the rooms (including the bathrooms) were recently renovated, so you can count on cleanliness, freshness and the availability of new equipment with a calm heart.

The hotel area is not very large, but tourists have a great time here. By the way, mainly Western Europeans stay here, so the employees practically do not speak Russian - it is worth studying at least a few English phrases. The beach is located at some distance, but the road rarely takes more than a few minutes. In the hotel restaurant you can always eat delicious food, and there are many catering establishments nearby. Tourists recommend this hotel as a place for a measured, relaxing holiday on the Spanish coast.
