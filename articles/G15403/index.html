Cleaning

Nutrition

The cost of living in this hotel includes breakfast only. According to our compatriots, they are quite uniform. So, in the morning you will be offered omelet, boiled eggs, sausage, cereal, tomato soup, toasts, several types of jam to choose from, tea and coffee. Many guests preferred to alternate breakfasts in a hotel with breakfasts in one of the neighboring cafes. During the day, you can eat both in the hotel restaurant and in one of the restaurants located in the vicinity. According to travelers, the food is delicious almost everywhere, and the prices are very reasonable.

Sea

Judging by the reviews of our compatriots, a beach holiday with the guests of Ruxxa Design Hotel 3 * (Phuket, Karon Beach) was a success. So, tourists say that the hotel is located just a couple of hundred meters from the magnificent Karon Beach. To get to the shore, you need to leave the hotel and just cross the road. The beach here, according to travelers, is simply excellent - snow-white sand, beautiful views, clear sea. If you wish, you can rent sun loungers and parasols. However, many tourists prefer to buy a mat and settle on the beach right on it. In addition, experienced tourists recommend not to forget about the need to constantly use sunscreen with a strong protective factor, since the sun in Thailand is very bright and you can get sunburn very quickly.
