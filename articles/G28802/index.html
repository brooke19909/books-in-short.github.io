How to use a Canon printer: step-by-step instructions for use

Canon is a world leader in document, image and print technology. At home, printers are useful for printing papers, reports, and even for taking photos. This technique at home will be useful to those who are still studying or working in the office to quickly and accurately print the necessary information from a computer or flash-drive. Modern models of printers are characterized by an organic design and versatility, they have dozens of useful functions.

Manual

Before using the equipment, familiarize yourself with the safety precautions and basic functions. It is important to know how to use the Canon printer correctly so that the equipment runs smoothly. You can print images and information on a printer solely for your personal use without violating the copyrights of publishers.

Proper handling of the machine and the observance of safety regulations will help to avoid damage to the printer and harm to the health of others. To connect the printer, it is recommended that you use only the specified power sources and do not disconnect the machine from the network during printing. If this happens, restart the printer and wait until the paper comes out. Removing pieces of paper by force is not worth it, it can damage the internal structure of the equipment. A few tips on how to use a Canon printer:

  • To prevent personal injury, do not put your hands inside the printer, especially when it is operational.
  • It is not recommended to install the equipment in direct sunlight, in a room with a temperature of more than 40 degrees, with an increased level of humidity or dust.
  • Do not touch the printer with wet hands.
  • Before removing dust from the housing, unplug the power cord from the outlet.

If ink leaked from the cartridge, it is strictly forbidden to use alcohol or any other paint thinner to remove it.

Printer Care

Here are some tips to use and care for your Canon printer.

To clean the contaminated case of the device, first turn it off from the network. With a clean, dampened in water and well wrung out soft cloth, wipe the surface of the case. Use mild cleaning agents diluted in water. When the case is completely dry, you can connect the printer to an electrical outlet.

If toner stains remain on the documents, clean the document glass. Also wipe the surface with a clean, damp cloth dipped in water. Then wipe the glass with a dry cloth. Depending on the model, there is a white plastic plate on one side of the glass. It should also be wiped.
