The best hotels of Seoul: description, traveler reviews

Seoul is considered to be one of the most famous and fascinating cities in South Korea , because it fully incorporates all the national colors. Seoul is filled with high technology and modern architecture, which allows tourists to fully plunge into the life of the inhabitants of this city, try their cuisine, visit the sights and absorb the unusual and fabulous culture of South Korea. But the tourist will not be able to get around all this splendor in one day, it will take at least three days to visit the significant and popular places.

But before you enjoy the sights of Seoul, you should choose the right hotel for yourself. First of all, before choosing a hotel in Seoul, a tourist needs to decide which of the twenty-five districts of the city he wants to stay. If a person has not yet been on its territory, it would be preferable to stay in the center so that there are no problems with transport. The best hotels in Seoul are concentrated here. This is obvious for the simple reason that the center of any settlement is its face, and the city administration simply will not allow establishments with a poor reputation here.

For those tourists who decide to visit Seoul, the hotels in the center hospitably open their doors. The staff of each of them makes every effort to make your stay comfortable. Below is a list of the most popular hotels for tourists in Seoul.





Hotel ShinShin

This four-star hotel is located a short distance from the center of the capital.

Hotel ShinShin has seventy-two rooms.

The hotel offers guests beautiful and spacious rooms with all necessary equipment, all rooms have internet access. The hotel has its own restaurant and garden.

In addition, the hotel provides guests with private rooms for non-smokers and people with disabilities.

Among the services provided by the hotel are the following:

  • ironing and washing clothes;
  • meeting a guest from the airport;
  • currency exchange.

The price and quality of services of this hotel, as noted by many guests, at the highest level. Friendly and polite staff is ready to fulfill all requests and respond to guest complaints.

The price for a day at the hotel starts from 4,717 rubles.

According to the guest reviews of this hotel, this is a great place with a convenient location and comfortable rooms, where you want to return again.





Hotel Pharos

Some Seoul hotels have 3 stars, but they are no worse than other five-star hotels in other cities of the country. Pharos Hotel is considered the best of all three-star hotels in Seoul.

A huge advantage in the location of the hotel is its proximity to the metro station, so the guest will not have problems with transport and movement around the city.

Pharos Hotel in Seoul has large and spacious rooms with all the necessary amenities.

Among the additional services of the hotel, it is necessary to highlight the following:

  • high speed internet;
  • various devices for the disabled;
  • free zone for car parking;
  • the presence in the hotel restaurant;
  • private rooms for non-smokers;
  • continuous cleaning work in the hotel.

The hotel staff, according to guest reviews, is polite and good-natured, most of the employees speak four languages, which is very convenient since there is no language barrier.

The cost of the cheapest hotel room is 2 500 rubles per day.

According to tourists, the Pharos hotel in Seoul is really a worthy option for a holiday, because it has low prices and high quality service.





Hotel Sunbee

Another beautiful hotel in Seoul, it received 3 stars deservedly - the prices there are quite reasonable. It is located near the city center and a short distance from the airport.

The Sanbi Hotel in Seoul has 42 beautiful and comfortable rooms with all amenities and high-speed Internet access. Sunbee also has its own restaurant, swimming pools, free parking. You can book a hotel room for a day for 3 800 rubles.

According to guests who visited Sunbee, the hotel is a great place to relax with sensitive and attentive staff and a very convenient location with the ability to bypass all of Seoul's main attractions. The hotel also treats guests with delicious breakfasts that even children will like.

Hotel Saerim Hotel

As indicated in the guidebooks, Saerim Hotel is the best place to stop tourists who want to quickly see as many attractions of the city as possible.

Saerim Hotel is a two star hotel.

The rooms in Saerim are small but clean and comfortable. The place is filled with national flavor and is the most budget option in the center. Ideal for people who do not have a large amount and want to settle in the heart of the city.

You can book a room starting at 1,600 rubles. The hotel is budget and therefore does not offer breakfast, but the rooms have internet access, air conditioning and a refrigerator. The hotel also offers special non-smoking rooms and constant service.

According to tourists' reviews, this hotel is a great place at an affordable price, with cozy and comfortable rooms, polite and attentive staff.

Hotel Small House Big Door
