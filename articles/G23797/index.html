Isinda Hotel 3 *. Turkey, Antalya: 3-star hotels

Holidays in Turkey for Russian travelers - the most popular option for summer holidays. Especially often, a tourist with an average and slightly lower income level chooses a three-star hotel with an all-inclusive food system.



There are good reasons for choosing this option. Nevertheless, it is useful to take into account the peculiarities of such a vacation, so that the expectations from the trip coincide with the real picture of a hotel stay. So, rest in Turkey: what to look for when choosing a three-star hotel?

Why choose Antalya resort for relaxation?

The choice of this Turkish resort is explained very simply. First of all, it is the proximity to the airport and the lack of a long transfer. Important for many tourists is a small distance from a large city. Especially for lovers of vibrant resort life and connoisseurs of Turkish shopping. The presence of sandy beaches here is also important, which is especially appreciated when traveling to the sea with small children. And most importantly, especially in times of crisis - this is the cost of travel. Of course, here ahead of Europe in the first place, Turkey.

Antalya, whose vacation prices are pleasantly different in the rating lists, has another advantage - a wide selection of hotels in the middle price segment.

Turkey (Antalya): 3 star hotels

The middle segment is the famous Antalya "three rubles". They are small, comfortable, meet all the necessary conditions for relaxation and budget. Usually, when choosing them, tourists are guided by the following indicators:

  • Location (distance from the sea).
  • The presence of the concept of "all inclusive".
  • Hotel reviews.

In order to evaluate the quality of this type of accommodation, we will look at one of the hotels that belongs to this category of leisure - Isinda Hotel 3 *. Based on the analysis of the quality of rest, it is possible to draw practical recommendations for the right choice of hotels in this category.

This is a typical city hotel with a small territory, convenient location and, which is uncharacteristic for most "three rubles", with its own beach in the immediate vicinity of the coastline.

It belongs to the category of "strong three rubles", that is, hotels that match the quality of the services provided to those indicators that are taken into account during certification.

Hotel Isinda Hotel (3 stars) is located five kilometers from the resort center, the city of Antalya. This is convenient because, being remote from the city, the hotel provides a good rest. On the other hand, regular bus service makes it easy to get to the center of vibrant metropolitan life.

Recommendations for choosing a three-star hotel for a vacation in Turkey

Appraising Isinda Hotel 3 * (Antalya) based reviews of vacationers and the information provided in the public domain by the hotel itself, a number of conclusions can be made that will help the traveler make the right choice with a three-star hotel in Turkey:

  1. If you want to improve the quality of rest, it is better to choose a 3 * hotel at a certain distance from the sea. The farther from the coastline, the higher the quality of rooms and service.
  2. When choosing an option of the middle price segment, you should not count on a wide selection of entertainment in the hotel itself.
    As a rule, these services are present in higher-level hotels and are included in the price of the tour.
  3. The concept of rest on an all-inclusive system should not be chosen in a three-star hotel. As a rule, it does not differ in the quality and variety of dishes for this price segment. It is more appropriate to limit breakfast.
  4. When choosing a 3 * level option with a desire to get good service, you should look for fresh hotels that have recently opened. In this case, you can get excellent quality at the lowest price.
