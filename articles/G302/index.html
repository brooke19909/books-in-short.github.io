Tariff Features

We continue the conversation about the description and reviews on the tariff of MTS "Hype". When you connect the latter, the following paid services will be available to you free of charge:

Additional payments

Once again, we turn to reviews of the MTS Hype tariff in 2017. Subscribers note that the subscription fee does not include the following:

  • Calls to subscribers of other operators located outside the home region - 5 rubles / min.
  • Calls to the CIS countries - 35 rubles / min.
  • Calls to European countries - 49 rubles / min.
  • Calls to other countries - 70 rubles / min.
  • SMS contacts outside the home area - 3.8 rubles / message.
  • SMS outside the Russian Federation - 8 rubles / message.
  • MMS to Russian subscribers - 9.9 rubles per message.

Thus, the tariff is not entirely suitable for users who actively call subscribers of other operators - the package of free minutes is limited here.





Upon reaching the limit

If you have exhausted the packages presented as part of the monthly fee, then you will face the following expenses:

  • Internet traffic: for every 500 mb - 95 rubles. However, only 15 such packages are available per month.
  • SMS to home region numbers - 2 rubles / message.
  • Calls to subscribers in your area, but to other operators - 2 rubles / minute.

About roaming

Although Hype operates throughout the Russian Federation, when traveling outside the home region you will have to pay 15 rubles per day (if you use communication services).

When you travel outside of Russia, the option "Easy Roaming" is automatically activated: it will allow you to use the services of foreign MTS partner operators. We also draw your attention to the following: Internet unlimited is not available when traveling abroad - traffic will have to be paid in full.
