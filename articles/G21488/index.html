How to sew a dress without a pattern?

A woman should always be on top and look great. But, unfortunately, it takes too much time, always have to sacrifice something. Today we will tell you about how to save time, money and at the same time look great. For example, we will learn how to sew a dress without a pattern. It is believed that to make it easier than simple and created such a method specifically for lazy people. But whoever says so was clearly not a woman! Beauties never have enough time, and to sew a dress in a very short time, and even unique, exclusive and do it yourself - this is the best option of all possible.



Getting started

So, how to sew a dress without a pattern? Very easy! For work, we need a piece of fabric. What it will be, depends only on the model of your dress. In this article we will consider several options from which in the future it will be possible to build on. First: sew two straps to the selected piece of fabric on top of the edges of the material (pay attention to the color of the dress, it should match the image of the girl, her appearance and figure). As a result, we have a fabric with two straps. What to do about it? All you need to stick your hands in them, smell and you're done! You still do not fully understand how to sew a dress without a pattern?



All the nuances that can meet

For the next option, you need a fabric whose length will be twice the height of the girl, a decorative braid or ribbon in such a size that it is enough to wrap it around the chest and waist. It is better to take materials with a margin. So, you need to spread the fabric on the floor (for convenience), then decide on its middle and draw a slightly noticeable line with chalk or soap. The line will be on the shoulders. Next, draw a circle in the middle and make a hole for the head in it. At this stage, you need to stick your head into the neckline and decide on the length of the neckline. Again, mark the result with chalk and remove the dress. Cut the intended cutout with scissors and fix the material with pins along the shoulder line. Each girl can sew a dress without a pattern, at least taking this principle as a basis. Next, you just need to finish the unfinished product. That is, tape the waist, preferably twice, and highlight the line under the chest. You can fix the material with pins, after we sew it. At the final stage, when the result completely satisfies us, we need to sew the tape to the fold with large stitches.





Conclusion

Behind you can do the same or a little fantasy and play with drapery. Using pins and hair clips (crab), you can find the folds in front of the dress and display them from the back.
