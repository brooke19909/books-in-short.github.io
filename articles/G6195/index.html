History and culture



A thousand years ago, Lloret de Mar was a village of fishermen. The symbol of the city is a touching sculpture of "Don Mariner" (the wife of a sailor), standing on the edge of a cliff. This is a figure of a woman peering into the sea: the wife of a sailor is waiting for her husband to return from swimming. Lloret de Mar has a splendidly preserved castle of San Joan, which for many hundreds of years has protected the city from pirates. The beautiful church of San Roma, built in the 16th century, is located in the city center.

Lloret de Mar is known for its entertainment centers and discos. Noisy youth companies have long been chosen for parties by Lloret de Mar. Reviews of the best discos in the city allow us to conclude about a very high-quality service. The most fashionable DJs in Europe come to work here.
