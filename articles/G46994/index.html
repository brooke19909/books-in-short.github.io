Which radio to choose?

When choosing a radio, you first need to look at the form factor (1DIN / 2DIN), brand popularity and your financial capabilities. A manufacturer who is happy for his reputation will never launch a raw or low-quality device on the market. While no-name brands from the Middle Kingdom stamp gadgets, not caring either about their image, or, naturally, about the consumer.

In the first case, we have an expensive but extremely high-quality product, and in the second, frank rubbish, which will not only break in a week or two, but will also burn car systems. Therefore, such savings, especially when it comes to this kind of technique, are not appropriate here.
