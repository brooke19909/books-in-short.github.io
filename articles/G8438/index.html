Park "Oaks", the story of the appearance. Modern park, its entertainment

Moscow is not distinguished by good ecology. And if not for numerous parks, squares, rivers and lakes, then it would be completely bad. Thanks to the large planting areas, Muscovites and guests of the capital have the opportunity to relax and breathe clean air. One of such places is the Dubki park. And although such a name appeared about half a century ago, this area has its own history, which begins in the 19th century. In those days there was a gorgeous oak grove.

Oak Park in the 19th century





In the middle of the grove was a three-part lake. This body of water was not independent, but was part of the water exchange system in the north of Moscow. And now, according to some information, it connects with the waters of the Kopytovka river, through it - with the Ostankino and Farmer ponds. And all this with the help of an underground collector system. Previously, there was a connection with the Astradam and Deer ponds through the Zhabenka river. Significant changes began after 1861, when the Petrovsko-Razumovskaya estate passed into the ownership of the Forestry Academy. They began to cut down parts of the grove and conduct summer house construction there, to lay new driveways and roads. It turns out that the Moscow zoo was originally planned to be built not on Krasnaya Presnya, but here. A beautiful lake, a chic forest - ideal conditions, but it was necessary to perform a small amount of work. The distance to the center of the capital was great, and for this only reason, the mayors of Moscow decided that the public would not go that far.

The next stage in history: after 1945





After the end of World War II, the grove was almost completely cut down. One person stood up for defense, Mironov, a former engineer for landscaping and landscaping. He began to defend an ancient oak grove in front of officials. Connected to the struggle of students, pensioners, schoolchildren, in general, the entire local population. Felling was stopped and even planted new plantings: young oak trees, lilacs, decorative varieties of shrubs and trees. Ponds began to be put in order. The name of the revived park was given precisely by the oaks. In the 80s they wanted to build up this area with residential buildings, but again, caring people defended it. In those same years, a large amount of improvement work was carried out here. After cleaning the ponds, many keys were scored. Built a children's town. It was soon destroyed, but then restored again. Recently, these places have been thoroughly cleaned up, and now Dubki Park is a favorite place for recreation and walks of all residents.

Exotic relaxation in the park: sauna





Visitors to the park who love the sauna have the opportunity to visit such an institution here. Oak Park attracts an exotic sauna. The lounge in the institution is designed in the form of a cave, the walls are completely made of stone, stalactites hang from the ceiling, which makes the design of the room more realistic. Ideally fit a table and sofa in the recreation area located on the edge of the pool. There is a banquet area with a wooden table and benches, fenced from the rest with a wicker fence. It is equipped with all modern communications: satellite television, video and audio equipment. The Finnish steam room can simultaneously accept up to ten visitors. At your service are brooms and aromatherapy. Then - into the luxurious pool, where you can not only plunge, but also swim. In this institution hold wedding banquets, corporate events, celebrate anniversaries and birthdays, anniversaries, families with children relax. The festive table will offer you dishes of European cuisine and a wide selection of drinks. Relax in the sauna and enjoy a long time to remember.

Location and how to get

Where is the Dubki park located? Moscow, Nemchinova street, near the metro station Timiryazevskaya. Arriving by metro, you need to walk from it along a pedestrian street, on the left round the shopping center and four Soviet-built panel houses. Landmark - a wooden temple located at the entrance to the park. In the event that you went to the place of rest by car, focus on the address: Ivanovskaya street, house number 3, which is located directly at the entrance. Here, in the yard, you can leave the car.
