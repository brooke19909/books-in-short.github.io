Sights of Cherepovets. Attractions every tourist should see

If you came to the city with relatives, acquaintances, on a business trip or decided to visit this city for other purposes, be sure to see the sights of Cherepovets. There are a lot of them. There are places that are interesting for children to visit, in others you can visit the whole family and have fun, interesting time.

Museums in the city of Cherepovets - archaeological





If you want to learn a lot, visit the museums of this city. You can visit all or several.

The Museum of Archeology, which is located at 1 B. Krasnaya Street, will help you travel back many centuries. Permanent exhibitions that present ancient inventions and discoveries operate here. You will be able to see the unique finds of archaeologists and see what objects of work and everyday life our distant ancestors used.

You can also see recent finds. So, in 2014, the museum was replenished with interesting artifacts. Among them are tools of the Stone Age: scrapers, arrowheads made of silicon, products from horn and bone. You can see beads, pendants, household items, fragments of rings, military weapons, objects that belonged to the inhabitants of medieval Belozerye.

The museum offers to go on educational excursions, interactive lessons.

We continue a fascinating journey around the city

Cherepovets Museum of Nature offers an unforgettable journey into the world of fossils . His attractions are also impressive. You can see interesting paleontological finds, learn a lot about the nature of these places.





The museum offers many thematic excursions. Some of them will tell about rare, diverse birds, tell about a detachment of passerines, about winged predators. You will get acquainted with animals listed in the Red Book, with species of insects.

All this can be seen in the Museum of Nature, located at 32 Lunacharsky Avenue.

Rare canvases are available at the Art Museum, Art Gallery of E. M. Dunin.

Estate Museums

Connoisseurs of poetry will not regret visiting the Literary Museum of Igor Severyanin. Although the poet was born in St. Petersburg, most of his life was spent in Cherepovets, in a manor on the river bank.

Literary evenings and interesting excursions take place in the Severyanin's house-museum. The Loterev House (this is the poet‚Äôs real name) is an object of cultural heritage, a monument of culture and history.

Where it will be interesting to young travelers

If you went to this city with a child, you can visit other attractions of Cherepovets. A lot has been created for children. These are museums, theaters, entertainment venues.

Having visited the Children's Music Theater "Alice", you will leave from there in high spirits, and the child will share his impressions of what he sees for a long time to come. The theater is located at: Sportivnaya street, 13.

On Lenin Street, in house number 153, there is another temple of art - the Children's Musical Theater. You can watch performances in which a lot of music and songs are played.

In the Children's Museum, the works of young artists are exhibited for the audience. The building is located on Lunacharsky Street, at number 39. The child will be able to admire the canvases created by his peers.

Entertainment

Sights of Cherepovets for children are also the entertainment center "Galaxy". Arriving on K. Belyaeva street , at number 59, you can perfectly celebrate the solemn event, the birthday of an adult or a child.

Watching movies and relaxing at Royal Vio, Mori Cinema





The sights of Cherepovets are also the cinemas of the city. Here you can also eat and enjoy the spectacle.

You will find the Royal Vio cinema on Milyutin Street, at number 7. Here there are halls for watching ordinary films and in 3D quality. You can pre-book tickets for the desired film, day and time.

Interestingly, the cinema opened more than 100 years ago - in 1910. Today, "Royal Vio" transformed beyond recognition. In addition to modern cinema equipment, he offers to visit 2 halls - for 41 and 113 seats, fill out a collective application with a good discount. The cinema provides its halls for rent for seminars, conferences, for private viewing.

Cinemas in the city of Cherepovets include Mori Cinema with 7 halls with a capacity of 1011 seats. In addition to the formats 2D, 3D, here you can watch movies in 48K format. There is a cafe with 120 seats at the cinema, serving European and Japanese cuisine.

In the lobby of the cinema there are slot machines: basketball, paintball, air hockey, racing, strength meter, shooting games.

Cherepovets city architecture - temples





Fans of quiet walks will be interested to wander along beautiful historical buildings, temples, churches.

Resurrection Cathedral is the oldest building of Cherepovets. It was built by the Monk Theodosius and Athanasius in 1756.

The building is located on the Cathedral Hill in the historical zone of Cherepovets. The temple is active, so you can examine not only its external architecture, but also visit inside.

The Nativity of Christ Church also impresses with its architecture. Its high bell tower is visible from afar.

Merchant houses

Until now, the city has preserved many ancient residential buildings that were once the houses of merchants. Stone structures built in the style of late classicism in the 19th century, you will see if you walk along Sovetskaya, Lenin Street, Lunacharsky Avenue and Victory Avenue.





You can admire the old wooden mansion if you arrive on Sotsialisticheskaya street. In house number 22 is the Vereshchagins Memorial House Museum. This museum is dedicated to the famous Russian artist V.V. Vereshchagin. He was born in this city in 1842.

To see the sunny city of Cherepovets, come here in the summer, then you can admire the sparkling domes of churches, the shiny expanse of the Sheksna River. The city is beautiful at other times of the year. It is good here in the spring, when nature awakens, and during the golden autumn, and in winter, when the roofs of houses are dressed in white hats, and trees and bushes - in snow coats.
