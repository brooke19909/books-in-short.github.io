Samui Verticolor Hotel (Thailand, Chaweng Beach): description, photos and reviews of tourists

Koh Samui is rightfully considered one of the best resorts in Turkey. By popularity among tourists, including Russian, it is second only to Pattaya and Phuket. And, of course, the inhabitants of this island are doing everything to make vacationers here as comfortable as possible. Good accommodation in Koh Samui tourists offer many hotels. Included in their number and a three-star hotel Samui Verticolor Hotel. This hotel is built on an island in the resort village of Chaweng Beach.

Location Features

On the websites of many tour operators in advertising of this complex it is reported that it is located on the first coastline. However, judging by the reviews of Samui Verticolor tourists, this, unfortunately, is not entirely true. This hotel is located, rather, on the second coastline. It takes about 7 minutes to go to the beach for its guests. But in any case, its guests do not consider the road to the sea from the hotel buildings too tiring.





In relation to the infrastructure of Chaweng Beach, tourists consider this hotel very well located. The center of the village with its entertainment and shops is within walking distance of the hotel. At the same time, the hotel itself was built in a fairly quiet area. No noise in the rooms leased to Samui Verticolor, guests can not rest.

What you should know about

Unfortunately, the boat transfer from the mainland to Koh Samui is not provided around the clock. Vacationers arriving in Turkey can get here, depending on the time of arrival, not immediately. Therefore, tourists who have rented rooms at the Samui Verticolor Hotel should definitely book accommodation in some hotel on the mainland on the day of arrival and day of departure. Otherwise, vacationers can even stay just for the night on the street.

However, domestic tour operators know about this feature of Koh Samui, of course. And usually they themselves offer their customers who bought permits with accommodation in Samui Verticolor in this resort, additional temporary reservation of accommodation on the mainland.

Samui Verticolor Hotel: hotel description

This complex was built on Samui was relatively recently - in 2012. So the hotel is quite new. They take vacationers in this complex in three five-story buildings. The territory at the hotel, judging by the reviews of vacationers, is neat and comfortable. The beautiful courtyard, exterior and interiors, according to tourists, is one of the undoubted advantages of Samui Verticolor Hotel (and this, of course, is visible in the photo). In addition, in the courtyard of this hotel, tourists can admire all kinds of tropical plants.





A feature of Samui Verticolor is that, unlike many other Turkish three-star hotels, a bed for a child under 4 years old has to be paid separately. The infrastructure in this complex, according to vacationers, is very well developed. However, as in any other hotel, some amenities are provided to residents for a fee. In addition to cash, the hotel accepts cards as well. The hotel also has an ATM.

Unfortunately, pets are not allowed in this complex. There are no rooms equipped for disabled guests. Smoking in Samui Verticolor Apartments is, of course, also not permitted.

Since tourists do not arrive at the island due to the lack of boat transfers at night, the resident in this complex does not work around the clock. Administrators come to the hotel at 9 a.m., and leave at 11 p.m.

Samui Verticolor Hotel: beach description

Tourists living in this complex can get to the sea in two ways. On the main road, as already mentioned, the path to the beach for vacationers takes about 7 minutes. But there is one more path to the sea from the hotel, leading through a neighboring hotel. Walking along it, you can slightly reduce the road to the beach (about 2 minutes).

The sea itself in the hotel area has earned quite good reviews from guests. The water in it is quite clean and clear. The only thing - in this place on the sea, unfortunately, quite often there are big waves.

Actually the beach itself next to the hotel is covered with soft, very light sand. Sunbathing here, judging by the reviews, can therefore be done with great comfort. However, the sand on this beach, as some vacationers note, is still somewhat dirty. But all the same, tourists can sunbathe by the sea opposite the hotel, including simply on mats. Here you can hide from the sun under palm trees.

Night beach
