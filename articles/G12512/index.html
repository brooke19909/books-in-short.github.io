Hotel surroundings

On the seafront there are various restaurants and bars - so visitors have the opportunity to taste Turkish dishes and enjoy the sea. One of the main attractions is the caravanserai. In the evening, donkeys and camels dressed in oriental style are brought to the caravanserai. Having visited this attraction, tourists will plunge into the wonderful oriental atmosphere. A visit to the local dolphinarium will leave exclusively positive emotions for all guests; during the tourist season, performances are held several times a day. For nightlife lovers, night discos are organized in the town, some of which have free admission. The market is another attraction of the village, in comparison with other eastern markets, this one is quite hospitable and cozy. On the shelves a large number of different products: carpets, jewelry, leather goods, products. You can make a purchase on the market at a nice price, as well as bargain. The only negative is that the market only works one day a week - on Wednesday.

A walk around the town will leave a pleasant impression, there are fountains, playgrounds, restaurants, golf courses and much more.

Reviews of tourists about excursions

Guests of the Garden Beach Hotel 5 (Alanya, Konakli) love to spend time not only within the walls of the hotel complex, but also far beyond. Many go on excursions in the neighborhood. Most recommend the following places to visit.

  1. Cave of lovers. A cave is located in the city of Alanya, on the shores of the Mediterranean Sea. You can get to it only by sea, the route on a yacht to this place is mandatory in the excursion program. According to tradition, one who jumps from a cliff 10 meters high into the water will acquire or maintain happiness in his personal life. In ancient times, the cave served as a shelter for couples in love from prying eyes, for which it received its name.
  2. The ruins of the ancient city of Justiniano - this area is located in a city called Karamburun. Despite the fact that the ruins are of interest to archaeologists, this does not stop tourists from visiting them.
  3. Cleopatra Beach is another attraction of the city of Alanya. The beach is considered one of the best in the world. According to existing legend, sand was brought to the beach from Egypt.
