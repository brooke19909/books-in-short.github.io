Beaded Animals for Beginners

A hobby, or a favorite pastime, helps not only brighten up your free time, but also gives you the opportunity to do something new and interesting. One of such occupations, beadwork, has recently become increasingly popular not only among children of different ages, but also among adults. With the help of small decorative beads, not only a variety of jewelry is created, but also crafts, and even whole pictures. Perhaps even weaving from animals beads.



Beadwork

At the dawn of time, people used various objects to decorate, in which holes were made for stringing threads of different materials. Such beads were made of wood, amber, pebbles, seeds and other natural materials, later - of glass. Nowadays, beads are made mainly from artificial materials, but beads from natural materials, even precious ones, are still most valued. Thus, beadwork is an ancient and at the same time new art. Its modern heyday began in the 19-20 century, and to this day beadwork does not lose its relevance, but is gaining new fans. Moreover, this lesson for children is not only original, which you can brag to friends and acquaintances, but also very useful. It helps to develop attention, artistic taste, fine motor skills. And, of course, the child will get a lot of pleasure from the knowledge that he did a real useful thing. Nice bags, cases for glasses and mobile phones, key chains, cup holders and other original accessories are made from beads. Animals from beads, jewelry on clothes of various kinds and, of course, costume jewelry look stylish: rings, earrings, bracelets, beads and necklaces.



Beadwork Methods

Each master who practices this art form has its own secrets and its own methods. Thanks to this, everyone gets special jewelry, crafts or animals from beads. Usually, a special strong thread is taken for stringing, which is threaded into the needle. For some products, it is more convenient to use two needles that are on opposite ends of the thread. In order for the product to maintain its shape, it is necessary to pull the thread well. The little things that beadwork, animals or other toys will give their authors can bring a lot of joy.



Beadwork of animals as a type of beadwork

Beginners are advised to weave products according to pre-prepared patterns, which can be found in large numbers. There are also many different master classes where various working methods are clearly shown. Also, those who are just taking their first steps in this art will be interested in making small crafts. For example, you can make a cute crocodile or a turtle using lightweight parallel weaving technique. These and other animals from beads will not only decorate the house, but will also become a beautiful and original gift. For the manufacture of toys you will need multi-colored beads, the number of which will be easy to calculate based on the found pattern.

Animals from beads are woven relatively easily, so even beginner craftsmen can make them. And then you can take on the following models and even come up with your own.
