Kiev metro: scheme and mode of operation

Kiev Metro - old communication. Kievans are already so used to this transport that they have no idea how otherwise they can overcome long distances within the city within a short time. Let‚Äôs and we will talk about the Kiev metro and its features.



Metro map

Visitors in Kiev will be difficult only at first. The subway grid allows you to travel to faraway areas in a short time. To figure out where to go, help the metro scheme of Kiev. It is presented in many copies in a prominent place at each station.

Everyone who is just starting to recognize the city needs to remember what it is customary to use in the written description of a route the abbreviation of the word "station" - "st."

Kiev metro offers information signs to help orienteering in the subway. They are located at eye level of a walking person, sometimes above or almost under the ceiling. The plates contain information about the stations where you can get in each specific direction.





Mode of operation

Kiev metro stations open at such a time so that residents of the capital can get to work on time. Therefore, the average time when the subway is ready to receive passengers is 5.45 in the morning.

Each station has an exact opening and closing mode. It depends on its location: those that are farther from the center usually open earlier. So, the Kharkovskaya metro station (Kiev), located on the left bank of the Dnieper, opens at 5.28, and closes a minute after midnight. Such a time is important for those residents of the capital who need to get to work by 7-8 a.m., especially if you need to go from the right bank of the Dnieper. Already at 7 in the morning in the subway trains you can see busy traffic, but the rush hour on the branch comes from 8 to 9 hours.

Like all Kiev waking up early, the Darnitsa metro station on the left bank hurries to open at 5.27. The last passengers leave the station at 00.06. The scheme of the Kiev metro will help you navigate the location of the stations and calculate the time needed to travel to your destination. We also inform you that the Teatralnaya station works the longest (until 00.18, the transition station from red to green, Zoloti Vorota station), the Hippodrome closes before everyone else (blue line, 23.54).





According to the special schedule, the Kiev metro stations work on holidays and during football matches. The fans have an hour more time to go home, but Art. The Olympic and the Sports Palace are closed an hour before the start of the game. The same mode of operation of the metro of the capital of Ukraine for the New Year, Easter, Independence Day (August 24), except for the mentioned changes for art. "Olympic" and "Sports Palace".

Directions: conditions, cost

Travel in the Kiev metro is carried out on plastic tokens. For a long time, until mid-February 2015, they were of the same sample and the colors were blue. The cost of the token was 2 hryvnias (about 5 Russian rubles). After changes in pricing policy, travel tokens were replaced with green ones (the shades are very different), and throughput mechanisms were reequipped gradually until March 2015. New tokens cost 4 hryvnias (respectively, about 10 rubles).

For schoolchildren and civil servants, travel conditions are special. There is also the opportunity to save with special cards.





The structure of the Kiev metro

At the moment (March 2015), the Kiev metro consists of three branches: red (M1), blue (M2) and green (M3). Of these, two (red and green) cross the country's main river - the Dnieper - and help residents quickly get from the right bank to the left and vice versa. The blue branch is located only on the left bank.

From Art. "Theater" (red line) can be transferred to the "Golden Gate" (green), from Art. "Sports Palace" (green) - on "Leo Tolstoy Square" (blue). From Khreshchatyk (red branch), the transition can be made to Independence Square (blue branch).
