Crimea, Novofedorovka: rest and reviews

Amazing Crimea ... Arriving here, you are doomed to eternal love. While the powerful of this world decide the question of its political map, mere mortals are waiting for vacation to go to the peninsula. Alushta, Yevpatoriya, Koktebel, New World ... Each of the cities is surprisingly good and unique. Each year hosts many guests. But there are places on the coast that are not yet fully mastered by tourists. They are distinguished by silence and calm. This is their quiet charm. One of such places on the Crimean peninsula is Novofedorovka. This small village is located on the territory of the Saki region, 18 kilometers from Yevpatoriya.





Village History

Judging by archaeological excavations, an ancient Greek settlement existed in the territory of a modern urban-type settlement five centuries BC. Before the October Revolution, medicinal mud intended for the patient with hemophilia Tsarevich Alexei was delivered from the village to Livadia. Until 1922, this place was not even indicated on maps, but already in 1926 it officially became part of the Tuzlynsky Village Council. Since the 30s. of the past century, the field airfield of the Kachinsky school of military pilots was based here. In 1945, F. Roosevelt and W. Churchill arrived at the Yalta Conference ; their airplanes received this airdrome. Also, after the war, they founded the "Saki-4" - an aviation garrison. The military camp was closed and did not appear on any map. Only in 1992, on the territory of the declassified garrison, was the settlement of urban settlement Novofedorovka. And in 1995, the Novofedorovsky Village Council appeared.

Location and climate

So, Crimea. Novofedorovka, as already mentioned, is located in the Saki region. This is the west coast of the peninsula. On three sides, the village is surrounded by water. It is located on the shore of the Gulf of Kalamitsky, which crashes into the coast for 16 km. Its total area is just over 3 square kilometers. And about 7 thousand people live on this territory.





The coast is protected from the winds, and the water temperature in the bay is comfortable, because it is not subject to cold sea currents. Sea and beaches

The sea here is amazingly beautiful, it envelops everything where you can reach your eyes. There are a lot of beaches, they stretch along the entire coast. There are wild and well-maintained beaches, both free and paid. Basically, the coating consists of sand and small shell rock, but pebbles are also found. Moreover, the coast is always clean. There are a large number of water attractions and opportunities for a pleasant pastime under the sun. The bottom of the sea is sandy, the slope is shallow, without holes, so there you can not be afraid for the children. So Crimea is suitable for any tourist. Novofedorovka will provide relaxation for every taste.

Local Attractions





The most famous attraction in the village of Novofedorovka (Crimea) is called NITKu - an aviation ground test training complex. It has existed since the military past of the town of Saki-4. It was the only such complex in the USSR. Aviation and technical means were tested here, such as catapults, emergency barriers, optical and radio landing systems. At its core, it is an airdrome in the form of a ship deck with a steel field. By the way, pilot training takes place there in our time. In August, performances by professionals.

Also in the village is worth a visit to the Heroes Alley. She perpetuates the memory of pilots - Heroes of the USSR. Their bronze busts are also placed there. In addition, it will seem interesting, especially to the boys, a monument to the Tu-22 crew, who died during a combat mission. It is made just in the form of this bomber.
