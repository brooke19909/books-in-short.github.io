What is a credit plastic card?

This is a modern financial instrument, which is a plastic bank card with a certain amount accrued on it. The bank provides a limit under which the cardholder can pay for his purchases and services received. The size of the limit is determined individually and depends, as a rule, on the income of the card recipient and his credit history.





Modern credit cards, such as, for example, a Tinkoff credit card, are distinguished by ease of processing and ease of use. Thanks to this tool, it is not possible to borrow money from friends and relatives or to apply for a loan from a bank. But it should be understood that the funds on the credit card are borrowed, and they are subject to obligatory return.

About Tinkoff Credit Systems Bank

The history of Tinkoff Credit Systems Bank (hereinafter referred to as TCS) begins in 1994. Then the credit institution was called "Himmashbank". The bank was renamed in 2006, when it was acquired by well-known businessman Oleg Tinkov.





TKS Bank is the first bank in Russia to completely abandon branches (with the exception of the central branch in Moscow). All operations are performed remotely: by phone or on the Internet.

Operators of the round-the-clock support service will answer questions regarding banking operations, talk about the services of the bank and advise on any other emerging issues. For example, at any time of the day you can learn how to open or close a Tinkoff credit card.

Is it possible to close a Tinkoff credit card?

Opening a TCS card is a simple process. It is enough to fill out an application on the site and wait for confirmation. What about closing? Perhaps you no longer need the card and you decided to refuse it. How to do everything right and not remain in debt at the bank?

There is always less information about closing credit cards. Most often, the holder just needs to come to the bank branch and contact a consultant. But what if the bank works remotely? After all, Tinkoff Credit Systems has no representative offices in Russian cities.





It turns out that it is impossible to close the card? Not at all! Remember how you opened it. Remotely. Consequently, the Tinkoff card can be closed in the same way, that is, online. This procedure is simple and accessible to everyone.
