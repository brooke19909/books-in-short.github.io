Golden Rain 3 * (Vietnam, Nha Trang): photos and description, hotel infrastructure, reviews of tourists

In this article we will talk about the Golden Rain 3 * hotel (Vietnam, Nha Trang). Photos and reviews of tourists will complement our description. Immediately it should be said that in the Vietnamese city of Nha Trang there are two hotels called "Golden Rain". And both of them have three stars. Location

The hotelier tried to collect all the categories of vacationers. For example, Golden Rain 3 * (Nha Trang, Vietnam) in the reviews, tourists call a country hotel. It is located on the second line from the sea in the so-called resort area. It can be found next to hotels such as Liberty, Vien Dong, and Si Legends. To the beach from the hotel to go exactly three minutes (100 meters). But if you want to find yourself in the center of Nha Trang, you have to take a bus ride, since Golden Rain 3 * (Vietnam, Nha Trang) is ten kilometers from the center.

His namesake under the heading "2" is also located in the European quarter of Nha Trang. The distance between the two hotels is only 150-200 meters. The entire tourist infrastructure of the European quarter is within walking distance. Near both hotels there are many cafes and eateries. There is also no shortage of massage parlors, discos, supermarkets.

Of the attractions of Nha Trang, the closest to both hotels is the Long Son Pagoda. As for the minuses of the location of the Golden Rain 3 * hotel, this is a disco bar adjacent directly to the building. In 2018, construction was carried out near the hotel. The sea is visible only from the upper floors, because it is closed by hotels on the first line.

Golden Rain 3 * (Vietnam, Nha Trang): description of the hotel

The hotel was built in 2011. It is a 12-story building. Only the upper floors are equipped with balconies. There is only one elevator in the narrow building, which can be inconvenient in the morning when guests leave for breakfast. Externally, the building looks discreet. In the basement there is a garage, and on the top floor there is a pool and a gym. The reception, as well as the restaurant, are located in the basement. The hotel has no surrounding area, as such. It‚Äôs just a house on a city street.

The same situation is observed in Golden Rain - 2 3 * (Nha Trang, Vietnam). The only difference is that this hotel is newer, built from modern materials and with an original design. Its building consists of 18 floors. In addition, the son‚Äôs legacy (which he already sold) is larger. The building of "Golden Rain - 2" is not only higher, but also wider. This means that it has two elevators, which, of course, is more convenient than one. And all the rooms there have balconies.

Room Reviews

Those tourists who happened to be in both hotels praise more comfortable rooms in Golden Rain - 2 3 * (Nha Trang, Vietnam). In the reviews, vacationers claim that there is new and beautiful furniture made of natural wood. And the situation in the first Golden Rain is clearly in need of repair. Some tourists complained that something was constantly breaking in their room: either a watering can in the shower or an electric kettle. In the end, the servants repaired it, but the serenity of rest was broken. Construction and a disco bar - these were two headaches of guests living in rooms oriented to the city.

Sound insulation in Golden Rain - 2 is not an example better. Of the advantages of the rooms, tourists note their spaciousness. But on the quality of cleaning expressed repeated unsatisfactory responses. Also, tourists were saddened by the old age and shabby towels. Some of them were with holes and gray. Tourists would recommend the hotelier to renovate the rooms and improve the decor of the rooms.

Sea and beach

In this regard, both hotels are equal. They are on the second lane from the sea, and guests will have to walk a maximum of three minutes to the beach. The coast does not belong to the hotels, so the entire infrastructure of the rest here is paid. The beach near the hotels is sandy, with a gentle entry into the sea. It is equipped (rescuers are on duty, there is a shower room, toilet and a first-aid post).

On this coast, low tides are weak. If you do not have your own beach towel, with a deposit of 10 US dollars (750 rubles) you will be given it at the reception. The entertainment infrastructure on the water is well developed. You can ride scooters, bananas, and even parachutes. For more sophisticated entertainment, head to Winpearl.

Pool and gym

The last floors of the Golden Rain 3 * buildings (Vietnam, Nha Trang) and its namesake "Golden Rain - 2" are equipped with a solarium. Those tourists who have visited both hotels praise the outdoor pool on the 18th floor of Golden Rain 2. It is larger in area, very clean. Sunbeds around him with mattresses, comfortable. From the 18th floor offers a truly panoramic view.

What kind of reviews do tourists leave about the pool at the first hotel? Mostly negative. They say that it looks more like a tub of chlorinated water, where an adult has waist-high water. Sunbeds around this tank are broken. It is better not to approach the railing enclosing the pool on the 12th floor for personal safety.

There is also a gym on this site. Tourists call it "a dirty back room where they put the broken sports equipment." In the reviews, guests recommend that the administration remove the gym and expand the pool area due to the free space.

Infrastructure

Golden Rain Hotel 3 * (Vietnam, Nha Trang) has a garage - free for guests. The elevator descends to the basement level, which is convenient for motorists. At the reception you can change the currency, use photocopy and fax. The hotel has a sauna, but rarely has anyone used it. All tourists from the Russian Federation joyfully noted that the staff, like the owner of the hotel, speaks Russian well. The employees will help you not only call a taxi, but also explain to the driver where you want to go and bargain about the price.

There is also luggage storage at the reception. Wireless internet is available throughout the hotel. You can rent a bike, although driving along Nha Trang with its Brownian traffic on the roads is a dubious pleasure. But you can ride along the promenade. The hotel provides beach towels on deposit. There is an inexpensive laundry service. If something goes wrong in your room, the wizard will fix it quickly.

Excursions

So that music from the disco bar does not bother you, you need to relax very actively. And many guests did just that, traveling around the vicinity of Nha Trang (Vietnam). Golden Rain Hotel 3 * is located in the European Quarter, from it you can see the main attraction of the city - Vinpearl Island. Even if you have planned a passive vacation on the beach, you should go there at least once. At the reception you will be helped with the organization of a transfer (by ferry or cable car). On the streets near the hotel there are many travel agencies where tours are even cheaper.

Breakfast (lunch box) with an early departure for the tour can be ordered the day before at the reception. Near the hotel there is a bus stop with which you can visit all the attractions in the city.
