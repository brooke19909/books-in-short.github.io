Modern resort

Cape Saint-Jean-Cap-Ferrat is an incredibly picturesque place, striking in its beauty. Now the resort is no less attractive than before. Here you can see numerous paths meandering along the coast and disappearing in pine groves, well-groomed and expensive villas. The cape can be circled on foot, making a transition of 14 kilometers. And throughout the walk you will invariably be amazed at the beauty of these places. On the way you can see the old lighthouse of the era of Napoleon III, rocky capes, Maurice Rouvier boulevard, the old port.

During the walk you will definitely meet the chapel of St. Hospice, which was built in the eleventh century on the site of the monk‚Äôs tower. In the seventeenth century, the chapel was reconstructed by the famous Duke of Savoy, Charles Emmanuel II. In 1900, a bronze (11-meter) sculpture of the Virgin Mary, made by the Italian sculptor Galbusieri, was installed near the chapel. Among the people, the statue was called the Black Virgin.

"Grand Hotel"

Among the attractions of Saint-Jean-Cap-Ferrat a special place is occupied by the famous "Grand Hotel", erected in 1908. The chic palace is one of the most luxurious hotel complexes in the world. It is worth noting that it was visited by presidents, monarchs, members of their families, stars of show business and cinema. The building itself can be safely called a work of architectural art. It is located in the park, at Cape Cap-Ferrat.

Among the hotels of Saint-Jean-Cap-Ferrat you will not find a more chic institution. Rest in his apartments can only afford very wealthy people. The hotel and park area occupies 7 hectares. At the disposal of distinguished guests of the institution is a large swimming pool with a length of 33 meters, filled with sea water. The pond is heated in the cool season. In addition, the hotel has a top-notch spa and La Veranda restaurant. Despite the age of the eminent palace, its rooms are equipped with the latest technology. The apartments offer views of the gardens or the sea. And the suites have their own pools.

Guests can also appreciate the haute cuisine of a Michelin-starred restaurant.

Resort beaches
