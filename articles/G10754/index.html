Hotel "Riviera" (Petrovac, Montenegro): photos and reviews of tourists

Every year, the number of tourists visiting Montenegro is only growing. This is facilitated by beautiful weather, picturesque nature, and high quality service. The Adriatic coast is almost completely filled with various hotels for every taste. Going on vacation, many people want to buy tickets to the perfect hotel with a clean beach, nice staff, comfortable rooms. A popular option is the small hotel "Riviera" (Petrovac, Montenegro), but does it meet these requirements?

general information

The Riviera complex is considered a new hotel, because it was built in 2004. It is located in the very center of the Montenegrin coast, in the small resort town of Petrovac. The hotel area is surrounded by a dense forested area. There are 2 four-story complexes connected by a glass passage, which in total total 91 rooms. At the same time, the complex can host almost 250 people. The Riviera Hotel (Petrovac, Montenegro) has both single and double rooms, as well as spacious family apartments. The last redecoration of rooms was carried out in 2012. The hotel staff can communicate in Russian, English, Serbian and even German.





The resort area is designed for families with children, but you can‚Äôt relax with pets: their placement in the hotel is strictly prohibited. Meals are provided on an all-inclusive basis, however, only breakfast and dinner are considered free. Within walking distance of the hotel is the public beach of Petrovac. This place will be not only a great option for summer holidays, but also suitable for autumn and spring holidays. Warm weather persists here until early October.

Where is the hotel situated?

There are many resort towns with ancient history, for which Montenegro is famous. Hotel "Riviera 4 *" (Petrovac) is located on the territory of one of them. The first settlements here were founded in the era of antiquity. Now Petrovac is focused on tourists. It combines many modern infrastructure and the cozy atmosphere of an ancient city on the coast. "Riviera" is located 150 meters from Petrovac. Within walking distance there is a bus stop, shops and souvenir shops.





You can get here from the nearest Tivat airport, which is located 35 kilometers from the city. The distance to Podgorica is 65 kilometers. Other major tourist centers are nearby: Budva (18 kilometers), Becici (16 kilometers), Sveti Stefan island (10 kilometers). The Riviera Hotel (Montenegro) itself is located right on the beach of the city, so the road to the sea takes less than 3 minutes. A pebble public beach is 600 meters away.

Room features

Each room has everything you need for a comfortable stay. In addition to the availability of standard beds from furniture, you can see a desk, a chair, a coffee table, a large mirror. Also, the Riviera Hotel (Petrovac, Montenegro) provides its guests with the following amenities:

  • paid minibar;
  • paid safe;

Apartments are cleaned every day. Bed linen and towels are regularly changed in the bathroom.

Hotel infrastructure

The infrastructure of the resort complex plays a crucial role in organizing leisure activities for tourists. The better it is worked out, the more comfortable the guests will feel. The Riviera Hotel (Petrovac, Montenegro) is not an exception, the description of which cannot be complete without information on infrastructure facilities. We list the main ones:

  • Own private parking. Free, but you must notify the property in advance. It is possible to rent a car to explore the sights of the city.
  • A gift shop and a small mini market where you can buy fresh food.
  • Free wifi in public areas as well as in the lobby.
  • ATM in the complex, currency exchange.
  • Laundry provides laundry and ironing services, as well as cleaning shoes.
  • Paid luggage storage at the front desk.
  • Modern elevator. Facilities for disabled guests are also provided.
  • Riviera (Montenegro) can arrange for you a paid transfer from the airport and back.
  • Special smoking areas. Ubiquitous smoking is prohibited on site.
  • For an extra fee, staff can deliver food and drinks to the room, as well as newspapers.

The organization of entertainment is the most important factor when choosing a hotel, because one beach and the sea is sometimes not enough. A developed entertainment system and vibrant nightlife is famous for tourist Montenegro. Hotel "Riviera 4 *" (Petrovac) can also offer other types of leisure, including family.   • paid tennis court;

Conditions for children

The resort complex "Riviera" is considered a good place for families with children. Therefore, children are given special attention. Allowed children with any age. Moreover, children under 2 years old can stay at the hotel for free. A baby cot can be provided upon request. The restaurants have high chairs for feeding babies. For an additional fee, a children's menu can be developed for you.





To entertain children, the hotel has its own playground. There is also a shallow pool in which a comfortable temperature is maintained. There is a mini-club in which children of all ages can have fun in the company of peers. But the entertainment program and animation in this hotel are absent.

Where can I eat on site?

Reviews about him are mostly positive. Tourists vacationing here note the following advantages of the complex:

  • Very nice location. A lot of greenery, the hotel is just buried in flowers. Nearby is one of the most beautiful beaches of Montenegro - Lucice.

Negative reviews of tourists

Unfortunately, there are no resort complexes without flaws. There are always errors, the main thing is that they do not greatly affect the vacation of tourists. The Riviera Hotel (Montenegro, Petrovac) was no exception . Reviews in 2016 note the following disadvantages:

  • An extensive construction of a new five-star complex is underway near the hotel. In the summer, construction is frozen, so there is no loud noise, but the appearance of the unfinished building greatly spoils.
  • Despite the large number of small guests at the hotel, there is no children's animation.
  • The air conditioner buzzes at night and does not cool the room well.
  • Wi-Fi works poorly and not everywhere.
  • The carpeting in the rooms is poorly cleaned and not vacuumed at all.
  • In the evenings, the bar offers only paid drinks, despite the all-inclusive system.

Thus, this complex will be an excellent option for those who want to relax inexpensively with their families on the Montenegrin coast. The area and the sea are very clean, the staff is friendly, and the food is delicious. But young people resting here can be a little boring. One way or another, the Riviera Hotel (Petrovac, Montenegro), the reservation of which begins long before the start of the holiday season, is very popular among Russian tourists. Many of them liked their vacation, and they are happy to relax here again.
