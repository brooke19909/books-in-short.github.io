  • Myhos Main Restaurant is the main establishment of this hotel. This is where breakfast, lunch and dinner are served in buffet style. Hotel Gloria Verde Resort 5 *: beach

The hotel has its own sandy beach, access to which is possible from 10:00 to 17:00. The coastal territory is fully landscaped. For guests of Gloria Verde Resort 5 * comfortable loungers are provided, as well as stationary bamboo sun umbrellas. Due to the fact that a garbage bin is installed near each guest seat, it is possible to maintain almost perfect cleanliness. Between the rows of sunbeds laid comfortable boardwalks.

To visit the beach, special towels are provided free of charge. You can also get a bottle of drinking water. At lunchtime, guests are treated to non-alcoholic refreshments, traditional Turkish ice cream, and fresh local fruits.

Lovers of secluded relaxation can rent a cozy wooden pavilion on the beach. In the sun-protected gazebo, you can lie down while admiring the sea views and enjoying a massage. There is also a dining table in the pavilion. Thus, an order from a bar or restaurant can be delivered here.

Additional information for travelers

When purchasing a tour in Gloria Verde, you should find out as much information as possible about the features of living and relaxing in this institution. The following information can be considered the most significant:

  • check-in of newly arrived guests is possible only after 14:00, and guests are required to check out of the room on the day of departure until noon;
  • a child under 12 years old stays in the room for free if he does not need an extra bed;
  • for babies up to 2 years free cots are provided;
  • the room cannot accommodate more than one extra bed;
  • pets are not allowed in the resort;
  • payment is possible in non-cash form;
  • from July to August, a restrictive regime for visiting the beach is introduced in connection with the breeding season of turtles;
  • the territory is common for three hotels of the Gloria chain.

Negative reviews

Hotel Gloria Verde is popular with travelers, but this does not mean that it has no shortcomings. Based on the reviews of tourists, you can highlight the following negative points:

  • when ordering food, the prices indicated are not those on the menu, but much higher;
  • expensive airport shuttle (much cheaper to call a taxi);
  • pools are poorly looked after, water is almost never drained;
  • food left over from breakfast and lunch can be seen at the distribution during dinner;
  • rare replacement of bed linen;
  • boring animation, insufficiently saturated entertainment program;
  • inflated prices for SPA services;
  • a small area on which as many as three hotels of the network are huddled;
  • in the fall, the air conditioning system does not work, despite the rather hot weather;
  • there are not enough free sunbeds on the beach;
  • fatty and spicy foods prevail, and therefore diet foods have to be ordered separately;
  • unscrupulous cleaning (in most cases, maids only take out the garbage);
  • nightly animation ends at midnight, which is early enough.

General impression
