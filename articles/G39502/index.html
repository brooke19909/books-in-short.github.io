Before you go on a trip, be sure to choose the right hotel. Sofia hotels are famous for their European level of service and affordable prices. Here is a brief tour of them, for those who have not yet decided on the placement:

Well, firstly, the four-star hotel Festa Sofia is a worthy representative of the Sofia hotels, whose staff warmly welcomes guests and does everything to ensure that they are satisfied with the living conditions. The rooms have everything: from air conditioning to Internet access and a safe. In addition, this hotel has special facilities and non-smoking rooms. Laundry, babysitting, tour desk, currency exchange and restaurant services will be provided. Pet lovers can safely take with them on a trip adorable pets - accommodation with them is allowed.

If you are a fan of home furnishings and a relaxed atmosphere, then the best option for you is the Alexander Business Apartments hotel. The local staff is multilingual, the rooms are fully equipped with everything necessary, tourists are offered sightseeing tours and leisure options. You can take a dip in the indoor and outdoor pools, go horse riding, go fishing and even go skiing.

Some hotels in Sofia even have rooms for the disabled. For example, the Central Hotel Forum. There is a business center, a good recreation area, a playground, a restaurant with a regular and vegetarian menu, a tour desk, dry cleaning, and professional translation services and secretaries are also provided. Thanks to this, the hotel is very popular among businessmen. The hotel rooms are decorated in light colors and even have soundproofed windows.

For business trips, the Triada Hotel is also good. Businessmen are offered two conference rooms with all the necessary equipment for daily conferences, seminars and other meetings. The hotel also provides visitors with a variety of dinners, cocktail events, coffee breaks. In addition to all this, the hotel has a massage room, sauna, hairdresser and beauty salon, fitness center and solarium.

Kempinski Hotel Zografski was designed by a Japanese architect and is famous for its modern, original interior. It is also famous for its ballroom, Japanese restaurant, huge lush garden and all the amenities for business meetings. It provides a huge number of both general and special services, and the local service is at the highest level.

Sofia is attractive for tourists not only with chic hotels, but also with numerous theaters, vibrant cultural life, nightly entertainment and other pleasures. If you have never visited the capital of Bulgaria, then it is urgent to correct this annoying misunderstanding.
