DIY traffic jams: photos

Are you bored and have nothing to do? You can make crafts from traffic jams. Many will be surprised, but many useful and beautiful things can be made from this waste material. For example, coasters, dishes and decorations. Look for ideas and workshops on creating products below.

Phone stand

Do you like to watch movies from your mobile phone? Maybe you like cooking with video recipes? Or maybe you want to have a stylish stand as a decorative element? Then you can make a craft out of traffic jams. Such a stand is not only universal, but also aesthetic. How to do such a thing? You will need to collect wine corks. You do not have to drink alcohol on your own. You can ask friends or colleagues to collect traffic jams for you. Glue the stand with a glue gun. How to make crafts from wine corks? You need to glue the blanks in 3 pieces in a row. The stand itself should be in the form of a wave. Therefore, the second row of traffic jams should be made slightly higher than the first. Thus, you need to raise the height of the stand by 3-4 rows. Then you should make the wave in the reverse order.

Writing board

Do you like to write reminders for yourself? Then you definitely need such crafts from traffic jams. This kind of board, or panel, will serve as a decoration of the wall, as well as perform a utilitarian function. Using the buttons with colored balls at the end, you can attach notes to your product. In this way, you can exchange information with your family or remind yourself of the things you need to do. If you hang such a board in the corridor, then you can pin reminders to it about what you need to take with you, or write down things that you need to give to friends.

Headphone holder

This thing is useful to everyone who likes to listen to music from the phone. When you take off your headphones, where do you put them? In your pocket, bag or backpack? And then get out from there a tangled bundle of wires? Then this cork crafts can greatly facilitate your life. The design is very simple. To make it, you will need a cork, an awl and an office knife. At one top of the barrel you need to make a hole equal to the diameter of the headphone wire connector. In the second top of the cork, a deep vertical incision should be made. In this hole, you will need to thread wires that fit the headphones. You can see the picture of the finished product above.

Decoration

You can make an interesting craftwork from cork with your own hands, not only for yourself, but also as a gift. Make beautiful beads. They can be presented to mom, grandmother or close friend. Decoration is simple. To make it you need one cork. It needs to be cut into thin circles. Now, in each part, two holes should be made. Take a chain and divide it into equal parts of 6 links. Now you need to collect the beads. We fasten cork blanks with a chain. It will be very easy to part the ends of the metal link using pliers. Deer

You can make an interesting craftwork from traffic jams in just half an hour. Such a figure of a deer can become a symbolic gift to your friends. How to make a funny animal? You will need two traffic jams. Glue one of them to the middle of the other. We fix the upper cork a little diagonally. Now take the wire. You can find it in your house or use the mousel that is left of a bottle of champagne. From the wire you need to assemble the stand. We attach it to the cork with a glue gun. It remains to make horns. We bend the wire with pliers, and then stick the sharp ends into the wire. Horns must be attached simultaneously on both sides to create a stable balance. We glue decorative details: eyes, hat, bow and nose. Crafts from champagne cork with your own hands is ready.

Stand

One of the interesting options for making do-it-yourself crafts from wine corks is presented above. You can create such coasters or cup holders. What are they needed for? So that there are no circles from wet dishes on the varnished table and in order not to burn the wooden table when you put a hot pot or teapot on it. And also such coasters can successfully fit into any interior, thereby making your kitchen more comfortable. How to make such a craft? You need to collect wine corks and then cook them. How to do it? Put them in a saucepan, fill them with cold water and wait until the water boils. Now you need to cool the workpiece. Some may wonder why you need to cook lids? Then, so that the cork absorbs moisture and slightly increases in size. After the workpieces have cooled, they need to be cut into plump circles. It remains to glue the blanks in a chaotic manner with each other. When gluing, stick to the shape of the circle and do not try to make all the supports the same in shape.

Photo Frame

Want to make an interesting gift? Make a photo frame. Such a utilitarian and beautiful thing is sure to decorate the interior of your friends. Moreover, making such a gift is very simple. The first thing you should do is frame. To do this, you need to take a thick cardboard and cut out two rectangles from it. Now take a 10 x 15 photo and circle it in the middle of one of the blanks. Make the hole a little smaller than you drew. Reduce the size so that the photo is well fixed in the frame. Now you need to glue the two blanks and decorate the facade. Take the lids and cut them along. Some blanks can be cut across into several parts. Now, using the glue gun, we fix the plugs on the front of the product. It remains to make a stand - and the photo frame will be ready.

Jewelry holder

A very simple and functional product can be made from wine corks. If you do not know where to store your jewelry, take a look at this craft. You can hang the product on the wall of the room, near your little table with cosmetic accessories, or put the stand on the table.

Panel

Go decorate the nursery, but don‚Äôt know what? You can use bright colored caps from plastic bottles. Such material is perfect for making panels. How to make a decorative decoration on the wall? Clock

Such a watch will look very stylish if made in black.
