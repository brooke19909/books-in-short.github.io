Master class: flowers from beads. With our own hands we will make a charming hyacinth

Delicate and elegant flowers made with beads from your own hands can not only revitalize and diversify the interior of your home, but also become an excellent gift to your beloved and relatives. In this article, we will share with you a simple master class on the manufacture of such charming products.

You will definitely learn to weave flowers from beads with your own hands. The main thing is to be patient and devote several hours of your time to this creative activity.

Flowers from beads: with our own hands we will make a bright hyacinth



To complete this craft, you need to prepare the following materials:

  • copper wire;
  • beads of white, yellow and green colors;
  • ceramic pot for plants;
  • gypsum;
  • thick wire for the stem;
  • green threads of medium thickness;
  • pliers;
  • PVA glue;
  • scissors.

So, let's look at how to weave from flowers beads - cheerful hyacinths. We take a 21 cm long copper wire and string white beads on it (17 pcs.). A piece of wire with strung beads should be equal to 3 cm. We make a loop, twisting the wire twice, we get the first petal. We string the beads on the two ends of the working wire and make two more petals. After three loops are made, twist the entire bundle in three turns. Now we string 22-23 beads to make a piece of wire equal to 4 cm, and form a loop. We make two more four-centimeter loops and twist together. Now we have a small flower, consisting of three smaller loops in the center and three large ones at the edges. It is necessary to give the petals a more realistic shape, so we crush the loops, making the petals sharp. Now take another piece of wire, string on it yellow beads (6 pcs.) And twist the loop. Carefully insert the yellow core into the petals and twist together the ends of all the wires. Everything, one flower is ready. In order to make such flowers from beads with your own hands, you will need many inflorescences. To weave one plant, it is necessary to make 19 more such details.

Gorgeous hyacinths - we create flowers from beads with our own hands

After all the inflorescences are ready, you can begin to weave the petals. We take the wire and string green beads on it (60-70 pcs.). We measure a segment from the edge of the wire 12 cm and do a torsion to form a loop. We string more beads on the second end of the wire and twist from the other edge. We repeat the same thing until we get five segments arranged in parallel, and hide the excess wire in the adjacent beads. One leaf is ready. Using the same principle, we create another 5 leaves.

DIY flowers from beads: we collect all the details together



In order to put together all the parts of the flower, we take a piece of thick wire about 40 cm long and bend it in half. It will serve as a stalk of hyacinth. Squeeze the bend point with pliers. We take three flowers and wind them with a green thread to the wire. The remaining 17 inflorescences are evenly placed on the stem, not forgetting to fix them with a thread. At the bottom of the wire we attach the leaves. Now you know how to make a flower - hyacinth from beads . As you can see, this is not at all difficult. To complete our work, it remains only to put the flower in a pot. To do this, we make a gypsum solution. We place the stalk of hyacinth in the pot and fill it with gypsum, not reaching about 1 cm to the edges. Leave the craft to dry. After the gypsum sets well, you can cover it with PVA glue and pour green beads. That's all, such a bright and beautiful hyacinth in a flower pot will decorate any living room.
