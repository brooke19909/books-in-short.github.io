How to choose a home appliance

Those wishing to purchase a robot vacuum cleaner can be recommended to look at it as a device consisting of four main structural elements:

  • Navigation block.
  • Mechanical drive.
  • Cleaning block.
  • Battery.

The most important element is not the cleaning unit, but the navigation device. There are four fundamentally different ways of orienting this machine in space:

  • An optical camera located on the top cover of the robot cleaner. The device analyzes its position on the floor, focusing on information read from the ceiling and walls.
  • Internal sensors located around the perimeter of the housing and under it. With an increase in the number of these parts, the capabilities of the robot also increase. Sensors on the bottom of the vacuum cleaner can prevent falling from tall objects, such as stairs. Also, the vacuum cleaner can detect more dirty areas of the surface, cleaning them more thoroughly. The more sensors installed around the perimeter, the better the robot is guided in areas of complex configuration.
  • External sensors (virtual walls). As such, infrared radiation sources or magnetic strips can be used, which are installed as barriers that the robot should not cross.
  • The most advanced system is laser. Using it as a range finder, the robot builds a map of the room and saves it in its memory. Further cleaning is carried out on the basis of the data. Such a system allows the use of various passage patterns for the harvested area.

The cleaning unit is represented by various types of brushes. He is responsible for picking up and throwing litter in the garbage bin. The suction power is not significant.

The design and material of which the brush is made is the basis of the quality work of the machine. A rubber brush is best for cleaning hair and wool.

The mechanical drive affects the surface of the vacuum cleaner and the noise of the apparatus as a whole. Rubber coated wheels have optimal grip on the floor and do not scratch it.

Non-memory lithium-ion and polymer batteries are much more durable. Their large capacity allows cleaning for a longer time without the need for additional recharging at the base.

Should I buy a Chinese robot vacuum cleaner? It is up to the user. In any case, when choosing a home assistant, you should focus on individual wishes and conditions in which the operation of the device is planned.
