Hotel "Troika" in Krasnaya Polyana: address, location, infrastructure, photos and reviews

This cozy hotel is located in the village of Esto-Sadok, in the heart of Krasnaya Polyana, the famous ski resort that spans the territory of the Sochi National Reserve.

According to reviews, the Troika Hotel (Krasnaya Polyana) offers guests an unforgettable vacation in comfortable modern rooms, here you can enjoy the beauty of magnificent landscapes and fully relax from the bustle of city life.

About the resort

Esto-Sadok is a small, cozy village in the Adler district of the Krasnodar Territory, located 4 km from the famous ski resort. As is known from history, in 1883, Estonians settled in these places, driven to despair by the lawlessness of the landowners in their homeland and crop failures. Since then, the village has gone through different times.

Today, Esto-Sadok is a modern resort village with a well-developed structure of hotels and hotels, sports and entertainment facilities. At any time of the year, vacationers can enjoy the amazing beauty of the Caucasus Mountains, the mild climate of the Black Sea coast (the northernmost subtropics in the world), the gentle and warm sea, feel the healing effects of the local mineral waters, as well as get an unforgettable experience of active leisure time at the famous ski resort.

Hotel "Troika" in Krasnaya Polyana (photo presented in the article) is located in the very center of the resort, located on the territory of the famous Sochi Nature Reserve.
