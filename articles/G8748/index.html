The largest television in the world

TV is the most affordable and fastest way to spend leisure time. For many, it has become an integral part of life. How nice it is to come after work, get a job on your favorite couch with your favorite movie, television program or series. Nowadays, there is a wide range of televisions of various sizes. A large-diagonal TV is able to turn watching movies into a real show in a movie theater.





Have you ever wondered what the biggest TV looks like?

It is impossible to answer unambiguously because at the moment there are televisions with various image technologies.

The largest TV in the world, made by LED-technology, has a diagonal of 201 inches. This model C SEED 201, it appeared thanks to the development of engineers at Porshe Design Studio and has been awarded numerous awards for the best design. This model is designed for placement in large backyards in private homes. The TV has a unique design that helps save space if you are not watching TV. In the off state, it is underground in a waterproof shaft. Just press the power button - the shaft opens, the support column rises, and seven model panels with huge monitors appear, which eventually form a giant screen with a diagonal of 201 inches.





All this happens within twenty-five seconds, despite the fact that the largest TV weighs 1.4 tons. The screen can be rotated and rotated in different directions at an angle of up to 135 degrees. It is quite interesting that on this TV, which consists of several LED panels, there are no noticeable seams. Image quality is also up to par - 725 thousand LEDs process up to 100,000 images per second, producing 4.4 trillion. colors. The screen brightness is also impressive - as much as 5000 nits. The TV is equipped with a chic audio system consisting of fifteen speakers and three subwoofers with a power of 700 watts each.





In addition to all of the above, the TV is equipped with a security system. The control panel is equipped with a fingerprint scanner. The price of this device is 700,000 dollars.

The largest plasma TV is the Panasonic TH-152UX1W with 3D technology. The screen size of this model is 152 inches. His demonstration took place in 2012 in Las Vegas at the CES Consumer Electronics Show. The huge screen and high image quality with a resolution of 4096 x 2160 pixels allow you to immerse yourself in movies and games as deeply as possible.

The largest Panasonic TV contains many of the latest technological developments of the company. Including - technology of super-fast matrix control, with the help of which high definition images in 3D-format are achieved.

Representatives of Panasonic believe that this model is perfect not only for home theater, but also for educational, medical and business needs. The cost of such a TV is about one million dollars.

However, the largest TV was developed back in 2007. Technovision introduced a model with a diagonal of 205 inches. Thanks to this development, Technovision was included in the Guinness Book of Records. The dimensions of this plasma TV are 4.55 x 2.56 meters. The screen is highlighted with LEDs in the amount of 750 thousand. So far, none of the companies has been able to beat the record size of Technovision TV, despite the fact that telecommunication technologies have developed rapidly in recent years.
