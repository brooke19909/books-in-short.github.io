Hostel "Netizen" in Moscow: description, reviews

The hostel format has long won the hearts of tourists. In such establishments, you can relax inexpensively and comfortably, as well as chat with other travelers in a cozy and friendly atmosphere. If you plan to visit Moscow, the Netizen Hostel definitely deserves your attention. In a modern hotel you will be provided with comfortable accommodation and quality service.

Location

The convenient location attracts tourists hostel "Netizen". Address - Moscow, Enthusiast Boulevard, 2. The facility is located in the building of the Golden Gate business center. It is about 100 meters from the Rimskaya and Ploshchad Ilyich metro stations. You can get here as follows:

  • When calling a taxi, call the address: International, 7.
  • From Sheremetyevo Airport every half hour an Aeroexpress runs to Belorussky Train Station. Next, from the Belorusskaya metro station, get to the Novokuznetskaya station and go to the Tretyakovskaya station. You need to leave at the station "Ilyich Square".
  • From Domodedovo Airport, an aeroexpress train runs to the Paveletsky Station every half hour. Next, from the metro station "Paveletskaya" get to the station "Taganskaya" and go to the station "Marxist". You should leave at the station "Ilyich Square".
  • From Vnukovo airport every half hour an aeroexpress runs to Kievsky station. Next, from the metro station "Kiev" get to the station "Taganskaya" and go to the station "Marxist". It is necessary to leave at the station "Ilyich Square".
  • From the Kazan, Leningradsky or Yaroslavl stations from the Komsomolskaya metro station, get to the Kurskaya station, go to the Chkalovskaya station. You need to leave at the station "Rimskaya".
  • From the Riga station from the metro station "Riga", get to the station "Turgenevskaya" and go to the station "Sretensky boulevard". You will be leaving at the Rimskaya station.
  • From the Kursky station from the Chkalovskaya metro station, you need to get to the Rimskaya station.

Hostel "Netizen" in Moscow: reviews

About this institution, travelers leave a number of positive reviews. What do guests like:

  • convenient location near the metro;
  • very clean;
  • beautiful thoughtful interior;
  • friendly and friendly staff;
  • large lounge area;
  • very comfortable orthopedic mattresses on beds;
  • at the front door there is interesting information about excursions and cultural events;
  • it is possible to have a full breakfast (250 rubles).

However, as with any other institution, there are also some negative comments:

  • there is no stove in the kitchen area to cook a full meal;
  • there is no washing in the kitchen area (you have to wash fruits and vegetables in the bathroom sink);
  • unmade beds;
  • it is bad that in multi-bed rooms the beds do not have curtains;
  • there are no booklets and information stands with data on hostel services (and employees do not provide full information upon check-in);
  • rooms are poorly heated;
  • employees do not replenish on time stocks of liquid soap in the bathrooms;
  • Do not watch TV - it is constantly occupied by those who play the console (one could install another TV).
