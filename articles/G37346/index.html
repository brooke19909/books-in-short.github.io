Xiaomi external battery: advantages and features of use

Portable external batteries have very tightly entered the life of a modern person who does not hesitate to use the digital achievements of civilization. Smartphones, tablets and other "smart" electronics are now capable of a very wide range of tasks, but one problem has still not been resolved. And it consists in the autonomy of the device, that is, the time of its operation without connecting to the electric network. After all, without power, the gadget automatically turns into a brick, no longer capable of anything. Portable chargers were able to solve this problem . Indeed, thanks to them, you can now put in your bag a battery pack weighing only 100 grams and not worry about how much the regular battery will last in a smartphone. Moreover, now manufacturers of "smart" gadgets focus not so much on the size of the battery in the device, but on the ability to quickly charge, which also positively affects the industry and the comfort of each individual person.

Who makes batteries for batteries

Batteries for modern electronic equipment are getting cheaper every year, which has contributed to the growth of the range of external batteries in the world market. There are not so many major manufacturers of high-quality lithium-ion batteries in the world today. Giants such as LG, Samsung and Panasonic belong to these companies. But their prices for the retail consumer are often very high. Therefore, other technology market players began to produce their products at lower prices in order to gain a competitive advantage. For example, Xiaomi, whose external battery is now very popular.





What does a battery consist of?

Xiaomi's external battery is built using lithium-ion cells based on batteries from the Japanese company Panasonic and South Korean LG.

This technology is today the most advanced achievement in the field of energy storage. Therefore, Xiaomi, creating its own external battery for smartphones and tablets, used it. Lithium-ion compounds allow you to recharge the battery for thousands of cycles without compromising battery capacity or, more correctly, within the accuracy of the measurement accuracy.
