Early repayment of a loan at Home Credit Bank: methods, conditions, reviews

Early repayment of a loan at Home Credit Bank is an excellent way for borrowers to reduce the overpayment of a loan, and at the same time get rid of additional worries. Banking organizations widely practice such methods of closing agreements. Everyone should know about the procedure for early repayment of a loan at Home Credit Bank.

Methods and conditions of early payment

In the event that the client wishes to fully compensate for his debt, he must know the following:

  • Borrowers have the right to repay the full amount ahead of schedule with payment of interest on the loan at Home Credit Bank for the actual terms of the loan. He can use this opportunity within fourteen days from the moment of receipt of funds, as well as within thirty days after processing a consumer loan.
  • The funds are written off directly on the day of the appeal with the submission of an application for early full repayment.
  • The borrower, for its part, must ensure that it is possible to write off funds from the credit account that are sufficient for repayment. It is required to transfer the necessary money to the credit account in advance. They should also cover interest and commissions.
  • Charges on the loan are paid for the entire period of the actual use of finances until the moment at which an early full repayment is made.

Primary requirements

In the event that the specified deadlines are missed, then such requirements will come into force:

  • Notification of the bank about early repayment not less than ten days before the end of the current interest period (Pr). This refers to the timing of the monthly payment.
  • As a rule, interest should be repaid for the entire loan period, and not for the period of actual use of funds.
  • The bank makes such debt compensation in the last day of Pr. Funds are debited using an automated system. The client does not need to do anything in this case.
  • A citizen must necessarily provide the ability to write off funds from the account, which will be sufficient for full early repayment. The required amount must be transferred in advance. There should be enough money to cover commissions, interest and penalties (if any).
  • In the event that borrowers have several loan offers in force from the bank, then in its application it must indicate specific products for which early compensation is made. The application is also filed ten days before the completion of the current PR.

Loan repayment

In order to fully compensate the loan before the expiration of the contract, it is required:

  • Calculation of the full amount of debt, taking into account additional contributions.
  • Personal appeal to the bank office.

Funds must be paid into the account no later than the week when the application is submitted. The terms of deposit depend on the conditions of the loan at Home Credit Bank and may be different.

Is early repayment of this bank possible?

There are several types of early repayment. It can be full or partial.

This financial institution provides its customers with the option of early repayment of part of the loan. The result of such compensation will be only a reduction in the size of the monthly payment. However, in some situations, it is possible to shorten the loan period. The following rules work here:

  • In the event that you make a significant portion of the debt on a consumer loan within thirty days from the receipt of the money, the terms of the loan may be reduced.
