Making Organza Flowers, or Home Jewelry Factory

DIY jewelry is made quite simply. They can be nice gifts for all your girlfriends. Today we will learn how to make organza flowers with our own hands and create many useful things on their basis. In one evening, you can open a real factory for the production of such trifles.





Raw material

Of course, organza flowers cannot be created from another material. Therefore, we go to the fabric store and purchase it. It is better if there will be a little organza, but different colors - from pastel to bright and defiant. Everything will come in handy. Knowing the width of the fabric is 150 cm, you can even buy 10-15 cm in different shades. And at the same time and see how surprised the saleswoman. You will also need matching threads, a candle or a lighter, and tongs so as not to burn your fingers. You also need a glue gun or a normal moment, as well as different beads and large beads.

All in work



First, cut off a strip of organza. The length depends on the number of your petals. Let it be at least half a meter. The width of the strip is 5-6 cm. Now we cut it into squares, from which later we cut oval petals. Now they need to be burned over a candle. In order not to get burned, we use the same tongs with a long handle. We do everything as accurately as possible, as the fabric can instantly flare up. Do not overdo it in burning the petals so that they do not lose their shape. If you need rounded petals, then bring the fabric to the top of the candle. When all the details are ready, you can begin to assemble the flower. They just need to be fixed in a circle all together. This can be done with both threads and a glue gun. In the design of the flower, you can use pieces of tulle or mesh, and mark the middle with a large bead or pearl. Organza flowers are made in seconds, and now let's see where they can be applied.

How to use



  1. Barrettes for girls . Here the scope for imagination is great. It can be ordinary hairpins, hair clips, and not quite simple rims and dressings. Make it all very simple. For example, in order to decorate a hairpin with such a flower, you first need to put on and fasten a button of a suitable size on it, and already glue the flower you created by it in a hot way. White airy organza flowers are the perfect decoration for a wedding hairstyle.
  2. Brooches and beads . Very often, even ordinary beads are decorated with similar flowers. Flowers from satin and organza are enough to plant on a piece of felt, to which you pin a pin. Get a great brooch. In order for such a flower to look more spectacular in a necklace, it must be in harmony in color and style, and its middle should be decorated with the main bead from this ensemble.
  3. Decoration of glasses and vases . You will get very beautiful wedding glasses for champagne if you decorate them with such accessories. In general, the production of flowers from organza is often used in the wedding industry in various decorative elements.
  4. Gift wrapping decoration . Different boxes and bundles will look more elegant and elegant with such decor.
  5. Bouquets of flowers . Today, such fading flowers are in fashion. Such a bouquet can be made in the colors you need, and it will decorate your interior for a long time, almost endlessly.
