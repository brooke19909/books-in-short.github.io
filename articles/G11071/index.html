Botanical Garden (VDNH) in Moscow

The first task assigned to the Botanical Garden (ENEA) is to preserve the diversity of biological species. Here you can find 2 thousand wood and artisanal types that were collected around the world. Availability

This is the largest place of such significance, where you can completely relax, enjoy walking or cycling, and also acquire knowledge about new interesting plants. In Europe, this garden has virtually no analogues.

You can join the excursion group, where you will be provided with useful information, or you can independently travel around the territory. You will find the mysterious density of the forest, where they feed the squirrels and relax by small ponds.

Important details

A great place to relax is the Botanical Garden (ENEA). Address: Moscow, st. Botanical, 4. You can pre-book a tour or ask a question that interests you. Buses under the numbers 803, 24 and 85, as well as trolley buses 73, 9 and 36 can be brought here.



When was the Botanical Garden (VDNH) opened? The opening hours of this wonderful place are different for warm and cold seasons. You can visit the garden on all days except Monday, which was a weekend.

In addition, there are amazing exhibits such as the Japanese Garden, which can be accessed from April 25 to August 31 on Tuesdays, Wednesdays and Fridays from 12 to 18 hours, on weekends from 12 to 20.
