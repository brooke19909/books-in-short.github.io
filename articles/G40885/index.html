Universal and original knitted wallets

Knitted wallets can perform the same function as regular ones made of leather or dermatin. At the same time, the little thing will look unique, since the craftswoman herself can choose the size, shape, pattern, color and design. You can make an item using various additional elements.

What threads should be used for creative activity

For the manufacture of wallets, it is worth choosing the appropriate threads to make the product look attractive.

It is advisable to use these types of yarn:

  1. An ideal option would be pure cotton yarn. The wallet will look neat. The threads will not form spools, fade or rub.
  2. Knitted threads with a small thickness are very often used to make bags, and wallets are also included.
  3. Flax and hemp threads may be used for certain models. Eco-friendly models will be a great addition to the summer look.
  4. You can use high-quality acrylic, it is better to choose a smoother and denser texture.

Products from such threads will look very non-standard. Thanks to the soft texture of the threads, the finished thing holds its shape well, but is easily deformed if necessary.

Crochet baby wallet

Learning to crochet takes a minimum of time, so beginner needlewomen should start with this tool. The easiest way to make a children's wallet will be two circles, which will be sewn together with zipper.

It is necessary to make a loop and tie it with six double crochets (ssn). The next row knit scs, but do three additions through one loop. To do this, knit two sts in one loop.

In the next one, make four additions according to the previous principle. It is enough to knit 5-6 rows. Then make another circle. Measure the circumference and select the appropriate zipper. Sew the product. On the canvases of circles, you can embroider or place textile stickers.

Crochet Option

Knitted purses in retrostyle, which have a clasp clasp, are relevant. To recreate the shape of the canvas under the clasp, it is better to use a hook. It is necessary to prepare cotton threads and a tool that will correspond to the thickness of the yarn.

Crochet wallet scheme that will be attached to the clasp:

  1. Make an amigurumi ring. The loop is tied with eight columns with a crochet (ssn). The last column will be connecting.
  2. In the second row you need to double the number of loops. First, knit the air loop (VP), and then knit two PRSs in each PRS. The result should be 16 loops.
  3. The third row start with VP, and then alternately knit 2 PRS in one, and 1 PRS in 1 loop. It should get 24 loops.

Each subsequent row must be knitted by adding as in the third row, but the alternation is determined by the following scheme:

  • 2 ssn, 1 ssn, 3 ssn;
  • 2 ssn, 1ssn, 4 ssn.

Make such additions until the volume reaches a diameter suitable for the clasp. Crocheted wallet with a clasp looks very original and attractive. The product can store money, jewelry, checks and other trifles.

Knitted wallet

A women's zipped wallet with standard shapes can be knitted using knitting needles. Since the basis of the product will be two paintings that will be stitched together, the original design will be the perfect decoration.

A beautiful zipper wallet, knitted with needles, will work out if you have minimal skills and perform such actions:

  1. Dial 25 loops. The quantity may vary. Much depends on the thickness of the yarn and the desired size of the product.
  2. Knit with a garter stitch. It is enough to make 30-35 rows. You can increase the number of rows so that the wallet resembles a small clutch.
  3. Close all loops. Link another part according to the same principle as the first element.
  4. Sew together two fabrics. Functional zipper is sewn on one side.

Additionally, you can decorate a knitted wallet with various elements, which will make the accessory more original and attractive. The owner can be sure that no one has such a second.
