Mobile phone Sony Xperia Sola MT27I. Specifications, firmware and reviews

Sony has traditionally been regarded as a provider of high-performance, stylish and functional mobile devices. Many of the corresponding solutions that were released several years ago are comparable in their capabilities with modern devices, even though completely different, more advanced technologies can be implemented in them. Among such devices is the Sony Xperia Sola MT27i smartphone. It was released in 2012, but remains in demand today. Thanks to what technological advantages does this device remain relevant? What are its features?





Phone Overview

Smartphone Sony Xperia Sola MT27i belongs to the mid-range devices. It appeared on the market in 2012, and is considered quite progressive for the start time of its sales. The phone is equipped with Android OS in version 2.3.7, a 2-core processor with a frequency of 1 GHz, a graphics module Mali 400, RAM of 512 MB. The device has a built-in flash memory of 8 GB, supports the connection of microSD modules. The device has a camera with a resolution of 5 megapixels. Supports basic communication technology. The battery capacity of the smartphone Sony Xperia Sola MT27i is 1320 mAh. For its time, the device, of course, has very decent characteristics. According to most parameters, a smartphone, in principle, is able to satisfy the needs of a modern user. We study in more detail its specifics.

Design

The appearance of the Sony Xperia Sola MT27i is highly regarded by experts and users. Among the notable design solutions implemented in the device is a thinner section of the case in its lower part in comparison with the rest of the device. This feature forms, firstly, the visual effect of the raised screen, and secondly, makes the phone look a little thinner than predetermined by its actual dimensions.





Specialists note the high quality of the materials of the case. The screen is covered with a protective glass, the back surface of the case is made of plastic such as Soft Touch. The sound level adjustment key is located next to the camera start button. The corresponding element, which is designed to turn on the device, is located in the upper part of the right side of the case. The phone cover is quite easy to remove. It is very easy to insert a SIM card into the device, as well as an additional microSD flash memory module.

The device is available in several color versions. The device with a black case is quite widespread on the Russian market - Sony Xperia Sola MT27i Black. The characteristics of the devices in different color options, of course, are completely identical. Now consider the features of the main hardware components of the device. The screen of the Sony Xperia Sola MT27i phone is made using TFT technology. The display resolution of the device in question is 854 x 480 pixels, the diagonal is 3.7 inches. The quality of the picture is estimated by experts and users as sufficiently high. The graininess of the image is imperceptible, contrast and clarity are at the level, fonts are displayed correctly. The display supports up to 4 simultaneous finger taps. Assessed as very sensitive. Supports technology Floating Touch - a bit later we will consider in more detail its features. Battery

The battery for Sony Xperia Sola MT27i has a capacity of 1320 mAh. It belongs to the category of non-removable hardware components. The battery capacity indicator is rather modest, however, in practice, the battery life of the device is quite consistent with competing models. The battery life of the device is enough for about 1-2 days of operation with an average intensity of using it.
