Best Crete hotels for adults and children: traveler reviews

Determining a place for a long-awaited trip, family vacation or a romantic weekend with a loved one, the choice of many dwells on exotic Crete, which offers tourists the whole palette of colors for a varied pastime. Here you can calmly soak up the beach, looking at the horizons of the Mediterranean Sea, or you can become part of the party life, walking the night away. This resort attracts vacationers with its architectural heritage and mythical affiliation. Here you can see the ruins of Stone Age settlements, chic Greek temples, Gothic cathedrals and other beauties of all periods of history.

The beaches of this island are no less attractive: from golden-sandy to rocky-pebbled. Most of the shores delight travelers with an abundance of entertainment on offer and developed infrastructure. For your stay you can find a hotel for every taste: from a budget option to a fashionable five-star complex. The latter are superior in number, because it is not without reason that Crete is recognized as an elite resort where people come to relax from different parts of the world. Consider the most popular hotels in Crete.

Luxurious relaxation for everyone

You can get enough positive emotions and unforgettable impressions in the five-star hotel of Greece (island of Crete) Greta Maris Beach Resort, which began to function back in 1975. It is updated annually - cosmetic repairs are carried out, which allows it to be kept fresh and attractive for tourists. It is represented by the two main buildings Maris and Terra Building and a number of bungalows, which are surrounded by fragrant greens and lush palm trees. Walking through the garden, as experienced in their reviews, allows you to feel like part of an amazing fabulous paradise.

Public transport stops are within walking distance. Distance from the airport is 24 km. A short transfer has a positive effect on the mood of new arrivals.

Number of rooms and its features

This hotel in Greece (Crete) offers 675 apartments for checking in. All of them differ in the types opened from the window, the area and the range of additional services. The interior of the chambers is decorated in a classical Greek style. Despite the spaciousness, it is incredibly comfortable in these rooms. Comfortable furniture, modern technical equipment and relevant elements of the decor create a feeling of home comfort.

There is access to a balcony or terrace. The chambers are equipped with a safe and a minibar, the use of which is paid extra. Present air conditioning, telephone, kettle and refrigerator. In the bathroom there is a hairdryer, a set of personal hygiene products and snow-white towels. Some rooms have a kitchen area.

Smoking throughout the hotel is strictly prohibited. There are rooms for the disabled and the possibility of combining rooms.

Fun for adults and kids

Spent every minute of rest in Crete at the hotel will be surprisingly varied and rich in receiving new experiences. Guests are invited to visit the sauna, jacuzzi or massage. A well-equipped gym allows you to maintain a beautiful figure at any time of the day. It offers billiards, darts, table tennis, cooking classes and dance classes. For lovers of elegant sports there is the opportunity to play golf. There are sports fields for playing futsal and tennis.

Diversify your time with a swim in one of the six pools. They are filled with fresh water, equipped with water slopes, bridges and passages. Water aerobics classes are held daily here and you can play water polo.

This is one of the best hotels in Crete for children. For children, a playground is equipped and the doors of the children's mini-club are open. A team of cheerful animators is constantly creating a daily holiday for kids.

Additional services

This beautiful hotel (Greece, Crete) has a conference room with a maximum capacity of 5500 people. You can satisfy your hunger in one of the hotel complex restaurants. A visit to the hairdresser will delight lovers of new looks. You can use laptops in the Internet cafe for free. Souvenir shops, boutiques and grocery stores, a currency exchange and a medical center are open on the territory.

Hotel Kiani Beach and its features

One of the chic hotels located in the north of Crete is the Kiani Beach Resort Family All Inclusive hotel complex, which attracts travelers from all over the world. It offers billiards, a tennis court, a fitness center and outdoor swimming pools. In this Crete hotel (All Inclusive) you can not only spend an excellent vacation, but also improve your body thanks to the cleanest air and healthy waters of the Mediterranean. This place is perfect for family vacations, as the organization of leisure for the kids here is carefully thought out. In addition to the swimming pool with water slides, kids can frolic in the open-air playground. Throughout the day, they are accompanied by a team of cheerful animators, conducting fun contests, sports and entertaining games. In the evening, a children's theater and mini-disco are available.

Supply system

This Crete All Inclusive hotel. In accordance with this system, five meals a day are organized within the walls of the main restaurant. The form of serving dishes is a buffet, the abundance of products of which is surprisingly vast. The interior of the institution is calm and peaceful, it has a good meal and easy conversation. A la carte restaurants are also available. It requires prior reservation of the table and compliance with the dress code. For children, the doors of a children's buffet are open, the dishes of which, as experienced, are to their liking.

Entertainment and sports

Fitness classes are available for travelers. Experienced instructors will conduct a set of classes that will allow you to maintain excellent body shape and lose a couple of extra pounds. A visit to the massage room will give a feeling of relaxation and pleasure to anyone who decides to forget about pressing problems and surrender to the hands of professionals. Bicycle rental, table tennis, billiards and a tennis court are available. The evening ends with a delightful show program in which every guest can take part.

Hotel Mistral Mare and its advantages

One of the beautiful places on the island of Crete, which has received many positive reviews and attracts tourists from all over the world, is the hotel complex Mistral Mare. Its unique interior and comfortable apartments will allow you to relax and enjoy your vacation. European level service, proximity to the Mediterranean Sea and a wide selection of entertainment on offer are the main advantages of this establishment.

The compact hotel is located on a mountainside, which attracts lovers of exotic holidays. The beautiful picturesque bay of Mirabello is located as if in the palm of the guests. A road of 75 km separates vacationers from the airport of international importance. The last global reconstruction was carried out in 2008 and today the institution has an amazing view and arrangement.

Activities for every taste

Despite its compactness, the Mistral Hotel (Crete) offers a wide range of entertainment for entertaining pastimes. Guests note that no one will be bored here. Everyone will find something to their liking. Here you can play table tennis or billiards, visit the sauna or massage room, and swim in the spacious open-air pool. The fitness center, as experienced, is surprisingly modern and equipped with the latest cardiovascular equipment, treadmills and other sports equipment. The aquazone is surrounded by a sun terrace. For lovers of shade, numerous umbrellas are installed. Nearby is a bar, the consumption of drinks in which are included in the All Inclusive system.

Hotel Aldemar Knossos Royal and its description

The hotel complex Aldemar Knossos Royal, which is located on the northern coast of Crete, will delight its guests. Over the years of its existence, he managed to fall in love with travelers from all over the world who do not skimp on positive reviews about him. It features a chic spa, outdoor pools and an equipped sandy beach.

Tourists who came to rest with their children note that this is one of the best hotels for families with children. For children there is a children's aquazone, which is equipped with slides, swimming facilities and decorated in bright colors. Here, kids spend the lion's share of their time under the close supervision of their parents. In addition, they can spend leisure time in the games room and in the open-air area. A qualified teacher will entertain kids with interesting games, cognitive tasks and sports. For adults, there is a tennis court, billiards, table tennis. A wide selection of various water activities will please fans of extreme sports and outdoor activities. Windsurfing, diving, canoeing are available here. Bicycle rental and horse riding are in demand among vacationers.

Apartments and their amenities

After a busy day, rest and sleep is offered in chambers of various categories, from the windows of which there are picturesque views of the sedate mountains and frolic waves of the Mediterranean. The atmosphere of the rooms is incredibly cozy and modern. On the walls are paintings and sconces. Panoramic windows fill the space with light and heat. Comfort is due to the presence of air conditioning, hairdryer, TV and balcony. According to vacationers, staying here resembles a sincere home atmosphere.

Budget option for a decent vacation

Not all travelers have the opportunity to stay on the island of Crete in 5 * hotels. "All inclusive" in the hotel or not, for many it does not matter. The main thing is that the hotel has an optimal price-quality ratio. The modest aparthotel Casa Maria Hotel Apts, which is awarded two stars, will please travelers with its prices, however, comfort and service are at a decent level. It offers chic studios and private apartments with a private kitchen area. The path to the beach will take about 4 minutes, which, according to vacationers, is incredibly convenient. You can get to the international airport within half an hour.

Pets are not allowed. Children under 4 years old stay free. Check-in starts at 14:00.

The courtyard is landscaped, there is garden furniture, gazebos and hammocks. Guests can enjoy sunbathing on the sun terrace. There is a swimming pool, not equipped with a heating system, and surrounded by sun loungers with umbrellas. In your free time you can visit the library.

Thus, an unforgettable vacation, air baths and healing waters of the Mediterranean on the island can be enjoyed in hotels of different categories. A stay in Crete will forever be remembered by travelers for its picturesque landscapes, hospitable people and good mood.
