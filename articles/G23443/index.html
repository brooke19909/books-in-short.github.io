Knitting is the process of looping without using knitting needles and hooks. All that is needed is fingers and yarn. You can weave with the help of such a technique a scarf, jewelry, belt.

Tips for Beginners

When you start the knitting process, do not tighten the loops tight, otherwise it will be difficult to remove them later. The threads can also pull the fingers, which can stop blood circulation in the joints. And your hands may be numb.

For knitting, you can use any thread (cotton or wool), it is best to choose those with a diameter of more than three millimeters.

Finger knitting





Hold the end of the thread with your thumb. Take the bottom loop that is on the little finger. Take it off. Pass through the upper loop. Scarf Decoration

You can decorate a scarf with buboes. To do this, take a white thread and wrap four fingers around it or use a piece of cardboard. Remove the winding from your hand and pull the thread inward. Tie tightly. Cut the resulting loops. Bubo is ready! Do five more. Place three buboes on each end of the scarf and sew. You can make them plain or multi-colored. Rely on your taste.
