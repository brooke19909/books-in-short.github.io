Gornozavodsk (Perm Territory): history and attractions

Gornozavodsk (Perm Territory) is a city located on the eastern border of Perm Territory, 125 km from the regional center. However, this does not prevent him from being among the settlements with a large number of attractions.





History of the city

At the site of Gornozavodsk, there was initially a small railway station, which was surrounded by a forest. The village began to emerge after the authorities began the construction of a cement plant. Gradually, as its momentum grew, the future city grew. At first, the railway station turned into the village of Novo-Pashinsky, named after the Pashiya River.

Then, in 1965, Gornozavodsk (Perm Territory) received the status of a city, after the population exceeded 10 thousand people.

City Economy

The basis for the economic well-being of Gornozavodsk is Gornozavodskcement OJSC. The company's products are in high demand in the country, as well as in the CIS countries, which allows you to maintain a consistently large number of jobs.

In addition, Gornozavodsk (Perm Territory) is famous for its bakery. Photos of the developing city can be found in the archive. And not far from the village there are gas pipelines. They serve as a link between the regions of the Tyumen region and the European part of Russia.





Gornozavodsk population

Recent years have been characterized by a decrease in the birth rate, but the size of the city is growing steadily thanks to visitors. Every year, a small number of people migrate to Gornozavodsk (Perm Territory). The ethnic composition of the city is dominated by Russians. Many can find job ads on the Avito website. Gornozavodsk (Perm Territory) can offer visitors special development prospects.

The second largest are Tatars and Armenians. It is noteworthy that in the city the number of female residents is proportional to the male, which allows us to talk about an equal sex ratio. Thanks to the working factories, unemployment in the city is practically absent. This is a huge plus, especially when the country's economy is so unstable.





City Museum

The whole Perm region is very attractive. The city of Gornozavodsk is famous for its sights and buildings.

One of them is the Mining Museum of Local Lore named after M.P. Starostina. Once here, you will hear a detailed history of the city. It is noteworthy that until the 20th century Gornozavodsk was part of the possessions of the landowners Starostins, in whose honor the museum was named.

Chic cliffs and cave

The next attraction of the city is the natural monument "Caps of Nature", rocks with a very unusual shape. Their height is 30 meters, from where you can enjoy a beautiful view of the Basegi ridge and Mount Kachkanar. In addition, it is here that the beginning of the Rudnaya River is located. This unique place is a historical and natural complex of regional importance. Rare plants grow here, some of which are even listed in the Red Book. Among them, cloves are acicular.

The Pashi cave is highlighted in the hotel attraction of Gornozavodsk, due to its size. It was opened in the 19th century, however, it came under state protection only in 1961. The cave stretches for 522 meters, and on its territory there is a clean underground lake and stream. You can get into it from a height of 30 m from the beginning of the Vizhay River.

Nature of the city and nature reserves

A must-see place after arrival in the city of Gornozavodsk (Perm Territory) is the Basega Nature Reserve. Arriving here, you will find yourself in a real taiga with mountains and 11 clear rivers. Eight of them spawn fish that are found only here. This place is attractive for lovers of extreme relaxation. In the spring, the season of tourism on the water begins, when those who wish begin rafting on the rifts of varying degrees of difficulty. The first place according to this criterion belongs to Pashi clamp. Rock Climber and Pashi cave are also located in the reserve and annually attract hundreds of tourists.





Not far from Gornozavodsk there is the Chusovaya River natural park, the main attraction of which is the body of water itself. This river is known among ecotourists for its purity and beauty. The cliffs stretching along its shore are of the most unusual form, and untouched nature pacifies, making you forget about the bustle of the city.
