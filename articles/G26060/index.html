Phone Nokia 206 Dual Sim: features and reviews

Nokia 206 was introduced a long time ago, but it still remains relevant, it can be easily purchased in many communication stores. Appearance





One cannot find anything supernatural in appearance of the Nokia 206 - it is a classic push-button telephone, which is made of plastic. The front panel with the keyboard is glossy, and the back is matte. The chosen solution is very practical - the back panel is convenient, fingerprints are not visible on the front one. The set of elements on the front panel is standard - a keyboard, a screen and a speaker hole. The microphone is located in the lower right corner of the pound key. As for the rear panel, there is a camera eye, as well as holes for a polyphonic speaker. If we talk about the headset and charger connectors, they are on the top panel. Nokia 206 Dual Sim received a second SIM card slot on the left side, which is empty in the original version of the device.

Search for items



The recess for opening the back cover of the device is located on the bottom edge. Build quality at height - monolithic apparatus, creaks and backlashes are absent. Thickness (as well as ergonomics in general) is comfortable when used, as well as when carried in your pocket. Thanks to its rounded corners, the Nokia 206 fits nicely in jeans. The memory card slot is located on the left edge of the phone and is covered with a back cover. There is a hot swap media. The main SIM card is located under the battery.

Enter





The keyboard on the Nokia 206 has an island type. The distance between the keys is approximately 1.5 mm, while the buttons are large, which eliminates the possibility of accidental clicks almost completely. As for the location of the keys, there are no experiments here, and this is an indisputable plus of this communicator. Keypad lock is typical for Nokia - use the keys "Menu" and "*". Available and automatic blocking with a timer. It should be said about the keyboard backlight, it is white, with a slight lilac hue. This combination for the eyes is quite nice. There are no complaints about the backlighting - it is uniform.

Battery

The Nokia 206 phone uses a BL-4U battery. Battery parameters: 4.1 Wh, 3.7 V, 1110 mAh. The claimed autonomy of the version for one number is twenty hours of conversation, 1132 - expectations. The option for two SIM cards has slightly more modest indicators. As an mp3 player, the Nokia 206 is capable of working for about 41 hours. Practice shows that from a single charge the phone can last a week, managing calls, SMS, and also playing the role of an audio player (within an hour a day). Excellent performance.

Display and photo





The screen size of the Nokia 206 is 2.4 inches with a resolution of 240 x 320 pixels. The pixel density is maintained at 165 ppi. The graininess of the picture is not striking. TFT technology, there are no complaints about image quality - indoors, the screen pleases the eye with good color rendering, and in the sun it remains readable without much difficulty. At the same time, viewing angles are sufficient. The camera module - 1.3 megapixels, has a fixed focus. Shooting can be done by pressing the middle of the navigation key. There is no separate camera activation button here. Using the joystick, you can enable digital zoom. The navigation key is used to switch between photo, video, and gallery modes. Thanks to the photo settings, you can set: effects, white balance; self-timer; the grid. Separately, we note a rather curious self-portrait mode - during its activation, you must turn the phone to your camera and follow all voice instructions. Without exaggeration, this function can be called original. Regarding the overall impression of the Nokia 206, reviews confirm that it is an almost perfect communicator for communication. You can also configure photo settings: size; viewing time; place of conservation; title; photo album; camera shutter sound. When shooting video, you can set: white balance, microphone settings, effects, self-timer, grid, movie orientation. That's not all. In the video settings you can set: video duration, the ability to view and save, name, shutter sound, self-timer signals. There are several options for viewing photos.
