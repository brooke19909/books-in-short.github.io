Inal Bay is a comfortable resort village in the Tuapse district. Recreation facilities

Summer is approaching, people take vacations and go to sea. There are many unique resort places in Russia, so it is sometimes very difficult to choose the most suitable option, especially for people with a small income. Inal Bay is one of the best resort villages of the Tuapse region, where you can have a great rest without spending fabulous sums. Recall that until 1991 the village was considered closed, that is, only people who received union permits came here. Today the bay is open to all.





The infrastructure has changed for the better: the tourism industry is actively developing in the village, entertainment centers, water rides and a lot of restaurants at an affordable price have appeared. Almost the entire Inal Bay (Krasnodar Territory) is built up with hotel complexes, hotels and medical facilities located on the Black Sea coast. People come here without reservation, since hundreds of hotels are built along the coast.

The area is famous not only for hospitality, high tourist orientation and pristine beaches, but also for mud therapy. Healing blue clay is very much appreciated here, it flows from a mountain hill directly into the sea, forming small lakes in which you can take baths and get healthy absolutely free. The beneficial qualities of clay have long been proven: it fights against cellulite crust, smoothes fine wrinkles, relieves inflammation, tightens pores, and normalizes blood circulation.

Features





The confluence of two climates - Mediterranean and subtropical - sets the weather in the village. There is practically no rain and wind in the bay, the weather pleases with warm sunny days, so the holiday season lasts until the month of October. The second feature is the picturesque and diverse vegetation, consisting of juniper and pine trees. The natural forest is adjacent to a comfortable and well-maintained infrastructure.

The third feature is the lack of industrial plants. Due to this, the air in the area is enriched with oxygen. The combination of coniferous aroma and sea air has a beneficial effect on health. One cannot but mention the small-pebble-groomed beaches stretching for 100 km. The coast is fully landscaped: there is enough rental equipment, snack bars and cafes.
