Ways to design patterns

There are four ways to design a quality pattern. This means that everyone can choose for themselves the most convenient method of its construction. The methods for developing patterns include:

  • building a circuit using special formulas;
  • the development of patterns by layout modeling;
  • creating a template using breadboard models;
  • computer modelling.

The construction of the scheme using special formulas allows you to accurately calculate all the necessary parameters of the workpiece. Subject to all established standards, products sewn on the basis of precise patterns have a perfect fit on the model, neat and tidy appearance.

The second method, namely the method of mock modeling, is based on the use of a basic pattern. That is, it is enough just to expand the product to get new patterns from old jeans. The disadvantage of this method is that the landing in this case will not be as accurate as when using special formulas and calculations.





The construction of the scheme with the help of mock dummy is the development of patterns on a standard mannequin. This method is typical of professional ateliers and design houses.

And the last method from the world of high technology is computer simulation. A specially designed program is able to automatically perform all necessary calculations based on user input. But do not build false expectations. This program is able to show an impressive result only when the user has completely and completely mastered it.

Jeans pattern: take measurements

The first step in building a pattern will be taking measurements. The more accurately the measurements are taken, the better the fit will be. In order to sew jeans by hand, the pattern should be based on eight standard measurements. Here is the name of the measurement and its symbol:

  • Half Waist (Sweat);
  • half hips (Pob);
  • seat height (Sun);
  • thigh line height (Wb);
  • waist depth 2 (GT2);
  • knee line length (Dk);
  • bulge of the abdomen (Vzh);
  • trouser length (DBR);

For example, a standard pattern of jeans of size 46 has the following parameters: Sweat - 38 cm, Bead - 52 cm, Sun - 28 cm, Wb - 20 cm, Gt2 - 3 cm, Dk - 60 cm, Vzh - 1 cm, Dbr - 100 cm But standard measurements should be used with caution, since all body parameters are purely individual.

Handbags from old jeans

After you made a pattern of old jeans, do not rush to throw it away. After all, a beautiful handbag can be made of fabric. Why go shopping in search of the original thing, if it can be done independently from improvised materials?

Necessary materials

The classic version of a casual bag is the easiest for a beginner. To sew a bag you will need:

  • old jeans of any style;
  • lining fabric;
  • threads
  • lightning;
  • scissors;

The set of materials is quite simple, so if something is missing from your home, you can easily buy it in a fabric and accessories store.

Patterns of bags from old jeans: a classic version

The following is directly work on the creation of the bag. It includes several stages:

  1. Old jeans need to be cut into three parts. First, cut off most of the so that jeans are shorts. The incision in the rest will be in the knee area. To sew a bag you will need the first (upper) and third (lower) parts. The second can be used to finish the product.
  2. On the third part, draw the bottom of the bag with chalk, after having previously measured the width and length of the first part. Then we cut it out, and manually sew it first in the center, then along the edges.
  3. Sew the bottom of the bag completely. First with a regular stitch, then with a zigzag stitch.
  4. We cut two rectangular pieces of fabric, based on the length and width of the bag, for lining.
  5. From pieces of fabric you need to make a "bag" and sew it inside the bag. The upper part must be left, since lightning will be hidden in it.
  6. We sew the remaining parts: zipper, harness, accessories.
