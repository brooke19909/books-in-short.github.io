Chelyabinsk, "Smolino Park": photos and reviews

Our eyes are resting not only in a dream. A beautiful sunset or a sea of ​​greenery and flowers - observing nature will show you real beauty and bring peace. In Russia there is an incredible number of picturesque corners that are worth seeing at least once in a lifetime. We suggest going to the Chelyabinsk region - in the wonderful Smolino-Park Hotel you will spend an unforgettable weekend.



Smolino Lake

Since ancient times , Smolino Lake was famous for its healing waters. At the end of the XIX century, scientists found that bathing from lake water and bathing in it itself cure many diseases and have a beneficial effect on the body. Smolin resort became famous throughout the country, and hundreds of people came here.

Today, the lake is increasingly replenished with fresh water from the pipeline, and the healing properties of Smolino can only be learned from history. With the disappearance of the “seaside resort”, the local attraction has not become less popular. The lake remains one of the favorite vacation spots of citizens. Hotel “Smolino Park” (Chelyabinsk) offers an unusual outdoor recreation.

By the lake

Smolino Park is considered one of the best hotels not only in the city, but also in the region. Not the least role is played by the location - on the lake, only a 15-minute drive from the center. This place is suitable not only for family vacations. In Smolino, wedding ceremonies are often held, as well as major exhibitions and conferences.





The developed infrastructure allows you to be here in summer and winter, because for each season of the year its own entertainment is provided. Hotel guests note a friendly atmosphere, well-groomed territory and comfortable rooms, which we will talk about in more detail.

For every taste

Only 62 rooms make up the fund of the Smolino Park Hotel. Satisfied travelers leave reviews about spacious rooms that have everything you need.

Categories and description of rooms:

  1. Suite Smolino with a view of the forest is the largest (78 square meters) and luxurious. The room has a bedroom, a living room with a dining area, an open balcony and even a Finnish sauna. Price - 9850 rubles per day.
  2. Suite Business (58 sq. M.) - perfect for a business trip. Bedroom, living room with workplace and lake view - the cost of living is 7550 rubles per day. There are two rooms in this category.
  3. Suite Jacuzzi - six spacious one-room rooms with an area of ​​48 square meters. m. A small dining area, views of the forest and two bathrooms, one of which has a jacuzzi.
  4. Suite - two-room suite with a cozy living room, area - 58 square meters. m. In this category are 13 rooms at a price of 7550 rubles.
  5. Standard Superior - 10 rooms. The bedroom has a small dining area.
  6. Standard Snacks - The area is 48 square meters. m. The price includes a minibar with snacks.
  7. Standard Double - a spacious room with an open balcony and a view of the forest.
  8. DBL Economy - two-room suite with a small living room.
  9. DBL Economy + is equipped with air conditioning, suitable for one or two people.
  10. Family Economy - two-room suite for four guests.




Regardless of the room category, guests receive a small compliment - a tea set and branded drinking water.

Enjoy Your Bath

The Finnish sauna has long been an integral part of relaxation for many Russians. On the territory of the Smolino Park Hotel (Chelyabinsk), Health and Steam operates. This sauna center consists of several separate cottages in which there are:

  • Turkish and Finnish doubles.
  • Pool.
  • Lounges.
  • Living room with cable TV, stereo system and karaoke.

Towels and sheets are included in the rental price, and bath attendants and birch brooms are paid separately.

For thrill-seekers there is also a sauna on the beach, where in the winter season there is the opportunity to dive into the ice hole. At the disposal of guests:

  • Kamenka.
  • Russian billiards.
  • Living room.
  • Covered barbecue area (extra charge).

In your free time

Hotel guests willingly share their impressions of the rest, in addition, the hotel has entertainment for every taste. Families with babies will definitely look into the nursery, where active animators will give dads and mothers a break, and their children will always be busy with exciting activities.

In hot weather, the ideal place at the Smolino Park Hotel is the beach. Water without algae, clean sand and a lot of free sunbeds - lifeguards are responsible for the order and tranquility of visitors. In addition, a volleyball court is equipped here , and you can rent a catamaran, a jet ski and almost any sports equipment. The Smolino-Park beach (Chelyabinsk) is well known throughout the city, because anyone can spend several hours taking sunbathing. The cost of visiting is 250 rubles.



Well-groomed area offers evening walks and observing the picturesque nature. Winter holidays can not be imagined without active entertainment. At the Smolino Park Hotel, everything you need is provided for this: ice skating and tubing from the slides.

Restaurants

The country hotel has several restaurants, so you definitely will not stay hungry.

One of the most famous in the city is the Krist restaurant. Surprising guests with an unusual interior, exquisite dishes from the chef and freshly brewed beer. In summer, in cozy tents you can enjoy a hookah and bright fruit cocktails. Visitors talk about delicious food and amazing atmosphere, the only drawback is the high prices.



The Tobacco bar is open 24 hours a day and serves European dishes. For dessert, you can always order a cup of coffee and pastries of their own production.

From 7 a.m. to 11 a.m., the Autograph Restaurant serves breakfast for hotel guests.

Conference hall

As we have already said, Smolino Park (Chelyabinsk) is ideal not only for relaxation, but also for various events.

The hotel has two meeting rooms: XO and Tobacco. XO is considered one of the most modern business sites of the city, it is designed for 220 people. The air conditioning and ventilation system, as well as sound and multimedia equipment make it possible to conduct any training or meeting without problems. To organize a buffet or coffee break, a cozy winter garden is suitable. Hall rental is 2000 rubles per hour.

The Tobacco Room accommodates up to 45 guests. A separate entrance from the street and parking for 11 cars is an undoubted advantage of this offer, as well as the price - 1000 rubles per hour.

Banquet

As for recreational activities, there is much more choice. Wedding, anniversary, fashion show, corporate holiday, birthday and graduation - at your service is one of the seven banquet rooms of the Smolino Park Hotel. Photos and detailed information about the organization can be found on the official website of the institution.

The service “visiting manager” allows you to meet with the future organizer of the event, who will give all the information on the topic and will be able to answer questions.

Wedding

Smolino Park is a member of the International Wedding Federation, so you can safely entrust the hotel staff with the celebration of the first day of family life. In addition to all kinds of discounts for banquets and field registration, when booking rooms for newlyweds and guests, there are other special offers. The spectacular appearance of the bride and groom can be arranged using a helicopter.





Program “Bachelorette Party. Intensive ”is designed for four people. Included in the price:

  • Accommodation in a deluxe room.
  • Photosession in a grove or on the lake.
  • Relax in the sauna center.
  • Pajama party with presents and sweets.
  • Breakfast.

Additionally, you can order massage and manicure, a culinary master class or a boat ride.

Honeymoon

An ideal holiday and an unforgettable wedding night - the staff of a country hotel will take care of all the subtleties. Three packages of services for newlyweds are offered by Smolino Park:

  1. Honeymoon light - one day in a room of any category, compliments from the hotel, fruit and champagne, breakfast in bed, as well as a transfer with a personal driver. Cost - from 5000 rubles.
  2. “Honeymoon Bestseller” - two nights in a romantically decorated jacuzzi suite. Joint photo shoot, gourmet lunch and relaxation in the sauna center, champagne and fruits, compliment and transfer. Cost - from 12 500 rubles.
  3. Honeymoon platinum - one or two nights in a room of the desired category. All other options are similar to “Honeymoon bestseller”.

During the celebration, a wedding movie is traditionally shot. A cozy cinema is provided for newly-married couples in the Smolino Park establishment. Address: Smolino village, st. Chapaeva, house 114. Instead of the usual viewing of photographs and videos, you will become spectators at this “wedding” movie show. For a cozy family holiday there are comfortable sofas, snacks, a panoramic room and a projector.





Guest reviews

Despite the development of the tourism industry in Russia, finding a good hotel can be very difficult. “Smolino Park” is a wonderful place for families with children, a romantic weekend or a business trip. Any event will be organized at the highest level, and the guests will have only pleasant memories and a desire to return to the shore of Smolino Lake again.

The hotel has a developed infrastructure, comfortable rooms and entertainment for every taste. Guest reviews are also noted by friendly staff, although there are still exceptions. Failures in the hotel or lack of information on the reservation can spoil the impression of even such a picturesque place as Smolino Park, but they are extremely rare. Have a nice rest!
