Nokia C3: settings, specifications and reviews

The Nokia C3 cell phone is the most affordable QWERTY model. At the same time, the device has a Wi-Fi module, a full-size keyboard, standard connectors on the case, and a metal back panel. The phone is presented in several colors, including dark gray and pink. This option is ideal for lovers of active correspondence.

Design, convenience





Nokia C3 is a compact QWERTY monoblock, which has a full keyboard. The model received comfortable dimensions, light weight, standard arrangement of components, calm design. C3 has become quite thin thanks to the smoothed ends. In addition, the phone is incredibly light.

The model looks very expensive, despite its price. The device is made of high quality plastic, matte back and glossy front. The assembly is perfect. The phone does not make squeaks even when compressed. The frame is incredibly stiff. Details fitted tightly. On a golden phone, fingerprints are not visible at all. This color can be considered the most practical.

The model is resistant to scratches and scuffs. The back panel is metal. Anodized aluminum was used for its manufacture . The attachment mechanism is very pleasing. There are latches on both sides of the housing. The panel is monolithic. There is no backlash. However, the panel is incredibly thin. At the rear is a 2 MP camera lens, as well as a speaker. The phone is loud. The sound is high quality. Hearing a call is easy. The thickness of the apparatus is 13.6 mm. Supports hot swap micro-SD, audio output 3.5 mm, micro-USB. There are no volume control buttons, as well as a separate button for changing profiles and turning off.

Battery





Nokia C3 has a standard BL-5J battery, its capacity is 1320 mA * h. The operating time is incredibly long. According to the manufacturer, the device can provide 7 hours of calls or work alone for a month.

The Nokia C3 display is touch-sensitive, in this device it is similar to other models from this manufacturer. We are talking about an inexpensive element, rather dim and a small indicator of viewing angles. Screen size 2.4 inches. 256 thousand colors are displayed. Communications





The device, not being a smartphone, received a Wi-Fi module. Thus, it opens access to the Internet, in particular, allows you to use mail, social networks and other resources.
