Hotel Hotel Fiorana 3 * (Rimini, Italy): photos and reviews of tourists

The Italian resort of Rimini is famous primarily for its sandy beaches, as well as profitable shopping. The tourist infrastructure in this town is developed, of course, just fine. There are many convenient hotels of different categories built here. Many Russian tour operators work, for example, with the budget hotel Hotel Fiorana 3 * (Rimini). Reviews of tourists about it on the network are different - both good and not very.

Where is the hotel

Many tourists consider the location of the hotel quite comfortable. This hotel is located directly in the city of Rimini - in the Marina Centro area. There are many restaurants, cafes and shops in the vicinity. A ten minute walk from the Hotel Hotel Fiorana 3 * (Rimini) is the historical city center. Here, hotel guests can explore a variety of architectural and cultural attractions. Rimini Train Station is a 20-minute walk from the hotel.

The distance from the sea to this hotel is only about 200 meters. A private private beach is a 5-minute walk from the hotel. To public guests of the hotel have to go about 25-30 minutes or go by minibus. The bus station, like the train station, is located in the immediate vicinity of the hotel. From here, if you wish, you can leave for almost any city in Italy.





Near the hotel, around the corner, there is a huge supermarket called Bill, which has earned very good reviews. Here, if necessary, you can purchase absolutely any goods - products, souvenirs, beach accessories, electronics, clothes, etc. 50 meters from the hotel there is also a very good, according to many tourists, Sabbioni restaurant. In this institution you can order not only delicious local and European dishes, but also just great wines.

General description of the hotel

Represents the hotel Fiorana Hotel 3 * (Rimini), reviews from tourists about which, unfortunately, are not very good, a modern four-story building with a small courtyard. The hotel grounds are surrounded on all sides by a light metal fence and well landscaped. In order to get into the hotel lobby, you must go through a small gate and climb the stairs from two steps. Externally, the building of this hotel looks quite presentable. Quite expensive and beautiful materials were chosen for the decoration of its walls. Along the entire perimeter of the building there are flower beds with various tropical ornamental plants. At the entrance to the hall a small neat palm tree was planted in a tub.

The exterior of this hotel is thus quite attractive. However, the same cannot be said about the interior design, unfortunately. Reconstruction in this hotel, judging by the reviews of guests, was carried out for a very long time. Therefore, it looks like the hotel inside is completely unpresentable. Some tourists even call him the European bomber. Check in the rooms in this complex, unfortunately, very slowly. But in the lobby for the convenience of expecting tourists there are small soft sofas.

This hotel belongs to the category of three stars. However, according to the majority of guests, she clearly does not reach this status. But then the cost of living in this complex is much cheaper than in many other three-star hotels in Italy.

Hotel Hotel Fiorana 3 * (Rimini), photos of rooms and territories of which are presented on the page, also works under the program of assistance to African immigrants. Of course, its future guests should also know about this. The hotel is owned by the Pappano family. A week tour with accommodation at this hotel costs Russian vacationers only about 20 thousand rubles, which, of course, is very small.





Hotel Rooms

If desired, domestic tourists who decide to spend a vacation in Rimini can book one of the forty rooms offered to her at the Hotel Fiorana 3 * (Rimini). All hotel rooms include:

  • air conditioning;

  • TV;

  • safe.

There is a shower in every room rented out at the hotel. On the sites of some tour operators it is stated that the rooms of this hotel have refrigerators. However, unfortunately, this is not so. In their reviews of the hotel, its former guests often mention that the refrigerator is installed only at the reception. Moreover, for its use you need to pay a separate fee.

A TV remote control is also required at this hotel. The hotel‚Äôs showers have a hairdryer in addition to a small complimentary toiletries. Many of the hotel rooms also have small balconies. On them, if desired, you can dry beach items.





Fiorana Hotel 3 * (Rimini): reviews on renting a hotel accommodation

Since this is a budget hotel, most tourists find accommodation here not very convenient. According to many vacationers, in fact, Hotel Fiorana is no more than just to spend the night here. The walls in the rooms of this hotel are painted in unpleasant flashy colors, and the furniture here is very old and not too comfortable. Unfortunately, household appliances installed in rooms often break down.

Not too comfortable hotel guests consider the showers of this hotel. There are, of course, no cabins here. Residents are forced to use an ordinary watering can. The water in the showers flows simply into a hole in the floor. Due to the constant dampness in the bathrooms, tiles also crumble heavily.

The advantages of the rooms rented at this hotel are only the fact that they are usually quite clean. The maids are cleaned well. At least, no bugs and cockroaches were noticed in them. The towels in the showers and linen on the beds at this hotel are also always quite clean.

Complex infrastructure

Of course, no special amenities are provided in the cheap Hotel Hotel Fiorana 3 * (Italy, Rimini) for tourists. There is no laundry, car rental, business center, or even luggage storage. Missing in the hotel and pool. On the sites of some tour operators reported that this hotel can use WiFi. Judging by the reviews of the guests, he really is in the hotel. But, unfortunately, far from all tenants grab it.





Opinion of tourists about the service in the hotel

The staff of the Hotel Hotel Fiorana 3 * (Rimini) has also mixed reviews in the network. Some tourists consider the hotel staff quite friendly. Others were not lucky enough to face rudeness. Moreover, many tourists note the impoliteness of not only the staff, but also the owners of the hotel.

The main disadvantage of this hotel, most vacationers consider, of course, the huge number of African immigrants living in it. These people are classified as refugees and occupy 80% of the rooms in the hotel. Moreover, at least 6 people live in each room. Africans are very noisy and rude. Also, guests have often noticed them and smoking herbs.

Unfortunately, the owners of the hotel, too, are quite loyal to the drinking of alcohol by the hotel staff. Many hotel workers serve guests in a not sober form. Judging by the reviews of former residents, the hotel owners themselves allow themselves to lay behind the collar at work. In various ratings on the websites of travel agencies, tourists give this hotel no more than 1-3.5 points out of 5 possible.

Dining at the hotel

Most tourists consider the breakfast served in the dining room of the Fiorana 3 * complex to be very good. The food in this hotel is quite tasty. If desired, hotel guests can take pastries, cheeses, sausages, eggs, etc. in the buffet. Food is served in the dining room of the complex according to the very convenient buffet system for tourists. If necessary, the hotel can rent rooms as simple as breakfast, and with half board. In the latter case, the price of accommodation will include dinner.





The food in this hotel is always fresh and decent, but some guests still note the scarcity of breakfast. Experienced tourists are advised to those who don‚Äôt eat in the dining room to visit the Billa supermarket located near the hotel. Here you can always buy quality products at a low price.

Unfortunately, Africans can spoil the life of tourists at the hotel in the dining room. They are served food in a separate room. However, they still go into the dining room of ordinary tourists and pick up trays of food. And, of course, most often they carry the best.

What you should know about

Tourists who have rented rooms at Hotel Fiorana 3 * (Italy), according to experienced travelers, should, among other things, take a closer look at their belongings. Guestrooms have safes. However, you need to pay for their use separately. In addition, these safes are also very small in size. For example, you cannot put a laptop in them. There is simply no big safe at the reception at this hotel. Therefore, guests of the hotel usually have to take gadgets and other valuables to shops, the beach and on excursions. The fact is that there is information on the network, including about theft in this complex.

Beaches

The entire coastline in Rimini is divided into sections of 50 meters, each with their own serial numbers. Therefore, it will be very easy for lovers of beach holidays in this city to navigate.

Opposite the hotel Hotel Fiorana 3 * (Rimini), as already mentioned, there is a pretty clean and comfortable private beach. However, rest, for obvious reasons, is not very wealthy people at this hotel. Therefore, residents of the hotel rarely visit this paid beach. Mostly guests go to public. There are also good reviews about them on the network. The coastline in Rimini is very wide (50 m). Therefore, even with a large influx of tourists, crowds on the beaches here never happen. Of course, all kinds of cafes and restaurants work near the sea in this city. Here, vacationers can have cheap lunch or dinner. Reviews about these institutions on the network are not bad.





Shopping

Beach lovers are not the only travelers renting rooms at Hotel Fiorana 3 * (Italy, Rimini). Tours with accommodation here are very often purchased by Russians who decided to visit Italy for the sake of shopping.

For all sorts of inexpensive quality goods, hotel guests most often go, of course, to the GROSS wholesale center. In size, this warehouse, according to many tourists, resembles a real city. The shops in the center of GROSS are arranged in rows close to each other. In them, tourists are offered just tons of old and new collections of clothing and other goods. If desired, bags, shoes, underwear, etc. can be purchased at this warehouse. Products are brought here literally from all Italian factories.

Hotel guests, of course, can also get to GROSS on their own - by public transport. But experienced tourists advise beginners to buy a trip with an escort here. Such excursions in Rimini offer many agencies. Vacationers who have bought such a trip just need to inform the attendant about what specific goods they would like to purchase at the warehouse.





Instead of a conclusion

Thus, judging by the reviews of tourists, the Hotel Fiorana 3 * hotel (Rimini) is suitable, the description of the rooms and infrastructure of which was given in the article, mainly only for unassuming beach lovers and for those who came to Rimini for shopping. Tourists with children and the elderly in any case, it is better not to settle here. Such vacationers, as well as those who are used to spending time at the resort with comfort, should still look for some other hotel in Rimini.
