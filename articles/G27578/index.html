Climatic features

The Kingdom of Africa is often called a cold country with hot summers. The thing is that Morocco spread over several climatic zones. The northern regions can really boast of snow caps of the mountains at any time of the year, but it is better not to go to the coastal resorts in the summer months for people who endure heat. If you are planning to relax in Morocco in October, it is worth paying special attention to choosing a particular resort, taking into account your own requirements and wishes for spending time on vacation. Not all coasts of this country boast the same climatic and weather conditions. The part washed by the waters of the Mediterranean Sea is considered suitable for recreation all year round, the swimming season here officially ends only in November. But the coast of the Atlantic Ocean is an amateur resort, as the water remains cool even in the hottest summer months. Are you afraid to make a mistake with the choice of a place for autumn holidays? Features of the autumn weather in Morocco

October is considered one of the best months to visit this country. It is always sunny at this time, but not too hot. You can enjoy the sun about 10 hours a day. The weather in Morocco in October will be pleased with moderate rainfall, it rains infrequently at this time of the year, usually short-term and light. Moreover, the coast is always fresh due to the proximity of the sea or ocean. Another indisputable plus of autumn holidays in this country is the absence of mosquitoes and other blood-sucking insects. Moderate temperatures and a comfortable climate allow you to enjoy excursions in plenty. Many tourists prefer to rent cars and explore the cities and various regions of Morocco on their own. And this is an interesting idea for a vacation. Where else in the territory of one state can one see gorgeous beaches, snow-capped peaks of mountains, and visit the great Sahara desert?





Holidays on the coast

In October , everyone who enjoys Morocco will enjoy a traditional beach vacation in October . For swimming, you should choose the coast of the Mediterranean Sea. But plenty of sunbathing will work at all resorts in the southern part of the state. The water temperature in Morocco in October varies by several degrees on neighboring beaches. Typically, this indicator varies between + 19 ... + 21 degrees. Caution: much cooler than the Atlantic. The beaches in Morocco stretched for several thousand kilometers. If you prefer to relax on the coast - a lot of options. There are enough opportunities for outdoor activities in this country. Many tourists come here to practice surfing and windsurfing. In October it is already possible to catch the highest waves, their season lasts in many regions until spring. Surfers traditionally gather at Taghazout, and windsurfers consider the following places to be the best places: Mehdiya, Anchor Point (Agadir) and beaches stretching north of Safi.

What to do during the autumn holidays in this country?

Holidays in Morocco in October not only allow you to enjoy sunbathing and swimming. Moderate weather allows you to plunge into the atmosphere of the country, get acquainted with its culture and traditions. Choose excursions to taste or go on a trip on your own. However, experienced tourists recommend caution in renting vehicles in this state. Weather in Morocco in October allows you to see all the sights, and there are many of them. Do not forget that autumn is harvest time. Do not trust local delicacies? You can always enjoy fresh fruits and vegetables.





Interesting events of the fall

Autumn vacation in Morocco will also allow you to visit many interesting holidays and events. One of the most interesting of these is the Date Harvest Festival, held annually in Erfoud. The holiday lasts for three days, during which you can not only try and buy ripe fruits, but also see national dances, listen to folk music. More modest harvesting events are also held in other cities. If you travel to Morocco in October, it will be interesting to visit the folklore show in Marrakesh, held in mid-autumn. An equestrian festival takes place in Tissa at this time of the year, during which locals recall that until recently they were real nomads. Thoroughbred Arabian horses, riders in national attire - everyone will certainly be interested in attending such an event.





Benefits of Autumn Holidays

There are many pluses for a vacation in Morocco in the fall. Remember that officially the tourist season in this country ends with the beginning of autumn. That is why a tour to Morocco in October can be bought on very favorable terms. Since mid-autumn, air tickets and hotel prices have become cheaper. The cost of food and household goods for daily use is also acceptable. The number of vacationers decreases, so you can explore the sights in a more comfortable environment, and you will hardly find any queues.
