Club "Monaco" in Ulyanovsk: a place for connoisseurs of incendiary recreation

Club "Monaco" is an institution for true connoisseurs of delicious dishes and drinks. Here is a wide and varied assortment of food and alcohol for every taste. In addition, the guests of the restaurant are offered an interesting and fascinating show program, which, of course, will not let anyone get bored. Boys and girls come here who want to have fun and meet new people.

Club "Monaco" in Ulyanovsk: a place for connoisseurs of incendiary recreation

This is an institution that attracts many visitors. Here you can spend a romantic evening with your loved ones, celebrate a solemn event with colleagues, relatives or friends, organize corporate parties or cozy gatherings with friends. The club is located at: house number 17, building A on Ulyanovsk Avenue.

The interior of the institution is distinguished by sophistication and originality. The decor is a mixture of a luxurious restaurant and a stylish youth club.





Visitors are invited to try traditional dishes, both Asian and European. The bar offers a wide selection of elite alcoholic drinks and interesting cocktails. Here you can also sing karaoke, smoke hookah, listen to music and dance. The club room is spacious, it accommodates up to three hundred guests. Visitors are offered free Wi-Fi, guarded parking.

Assortment of food and drinks

The menu of the Monaco club in Ulyanovsk is quite diverse and interesting. It includes traditional dishes of various cuisines. The range of food and drinks is approximately the following:

  1. Cold appetizers of fish, vegetables, fruits, cheese, meat, greens.
  2. Various salads.
  3. Hot appetizers of seafood, chicken, fish.
  4. Pasta dishes.
  5. Kebabs.
  6. Hot dishes of meat, vegetables, fish and poultry.
  7. Sweets.
  8. Various drinks, cocktails.

The average price of dishes in an institution is about five hundred rubles.

Opinions of visitors about the club

This place is one of the famous and popular places for night rest in the city of Ulyanovsk. Opinions of visitors about the organization are quite controversial. There are clients who are quite satisfied with the work of the institution. They like reasonable prices, comfortable and beautiful interior. Guests are pleased that the institution has all the possibilities for holding festive events: karaoke, incendiary music, dance floor, comfortable relaxation rooms.





Many people like the quality of food and drinks offered at the club. Lighting and music equipment, the work of disc jockeys are also highly appreciated.

However, there are negative reviews about Monaco, a nightclub in Ulyanovsk. Some visitors are unhappy with the impolite behavior of the security staff and the long service. There are guests who believe that the entertainment program in the institution is not interesting enough, and the presenters are not very professional.
