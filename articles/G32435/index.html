Hotel Club Hotel Sidelya (Turkey): description, photos and reviews of tourists

It is no secret to anyone that over the past few years, Turkey has become one of the most popular and visited resort places. The Mediterranean Sea, clean beaches, luxurious resorts, beautiful nature, ancient history and culture - all this is perfect for any type of vacation. And many travelers are interested in questions about which hotel to choose. In fact, literally the entire coast of the country is occupied by hotels. And a pretty good place is the Club Hotel Sidelya. Where exactly is the resort complex located? What technique can you count on?

Where is the hotel?





A large hotel complex Club Sidelya Holiday Village 4 * is located on the territory of the Antalya Riviera, namely in the area called Manavgat. The nearest resort village of Chokolly is only three kilometers from the hotel - this distance can be overcome even on foot.

Naturally, there are other popular tourist cities nearby. In particular, the distance to a large resort called Side is only 12 kilometers - you can get to it by taxi in a few minutes. The nearest international airport is in Antalya, about 55 kilometers from the hotel.

Brief description of the territory and infrastructure

The large resort Club Hotel Sidelya HV is located in a cozy area, almost on the shores of the Mediterranean Sea, surrounded by a beautiful pine forest. The hotel has a rather large private territory of 54 thousand square meters.

Tourists note that the hotel area is really well-groomed. Here you can hide in the shade of palm trees, take a walk in the garden or relax in a wooden gazebo. Bright flower beds with exotic plants, curly bushes, fountains and alpine slides are pleasing to the eye everywhere.

The hotel itself consists of several two- and three-story buildings reserved for residential complexes, as well as restaurants, games and office space.

Brief description of rooms and amenities

Quite often, the hotel complex appears as Club Hotel Sidelya 3 *. But today it is a four-star hotel that offers the appropriate level of comfort.

All rooms are spacious, furnished with the necessary amount of quality furniture, decorated in a discreet but elegant style. The room comes with a small private balcony where you can enjoy the fresh air, enjoy the garden or sea view.

There is an air conditioning system. Also in the room there is a safe, TV and telephone. The spacious bathroom comes with a shower or bath. There is also a sink and a large wall mirror, as well as a toilet. Guests are entitled to a set of towels, a set of cosmetic and hygiene products, as well as a hair dryer.

Meal plan for guests

Guests are offered six meals a day, including even a second dinner at a later time for night lovers. The hotel's main restaurant serves buffet meals where everyone can choose their own food. Of course, the choice of dishes is huge - here you will find snacks, slices, salads, fresh vegetable dishes, fruits, meat and fish, as well as a variety of side dishes and soups. Beach and water recreation in the resort

Of course, Club Hotel Sidelya offers a quality beach holiday. The hotel owns its own section of the beach, so outsiders will not bother you. The sandy well-groomed beach is located about 350 meters from the hotel buildings - you can get here along walking paths. On the shore there are comfortable wooden deck chairs with mattresses and umbrellas, and in sufficient quantities. Beach towels can be rented for a small fee.





Of course, you will have the opportunity to have fun. There is a beach volleyball court. You can also go boating, catamarans, boats, water skiing or scuba diving. If you wish, you can sign up for an excursion and make a real trip on a yacht.

Hotel Club Hotel Sidelya (Turkey): Additional services for vacationers

We tried to create all conditions for a truly comfortable stay in the resort complex. For example, you do not have to worry about local money, since a currency exchange office is constantly working here. If necessary, clothes can be taken to the laundry or dry cleaning - their services are paid separately, but the prices are quite affordable.

Also, for a small fee, you can rent a safe where your valuables or papers will be completely safe. If there are health problems, polite employees will call a doctor in your room - specialist services are not included in the price.

Leisure and entertainment





In fact, this hotel was simply created for a fun, fun-filled pastime. So what exactly does Club Hotel Sidelya HV offer? 2 large outdoor pools with fresh water, around which umbrellas and sun loungers are placed - it is always nice to relax here, meet new neighbors and take sun baths. By the way, in the pool almost every day water polo competitions are organized, as well as classes in water aerobics.

There are quite large water slides that will appeal to both you and your child. The water park works almost every day, so you are guaranteed a vivid experience and sincere fun.

Fans of sports activities are invited to a large gym, furnished with a sufficient amount of modern working equipment. At the hotel you can also play basketball, volleyball, futsal. There are also large tennis courts. Rackets and balls will be given to you for free, but in the evening you need to pay extra for lighting the territory.

You can relax perfectly in the bath, and after it go for a massage - the hotel employs exclusively professional massage therapists. In addition, there is a small beauty salon where good stylists and cosmetologists work.

In the evenings, fun discos are organized here. And, of course, the hotel has its own animators, who, judging by the reviews, do an excellent job. During the day they organize competitions, contests and sporting events. But in the evenings in the local amphitheater you will find colorful performances, including comic numbers, performances of acrobats, real oriental dances, shows with fire and even fashion shows. There is definitely no time left for boredom.

What type of vacation is the hotel intended for?

Many tourists are interested in the question of what type of vacation the Club Hotel Sidelya hotel complex is suitable for. In fact, this place is suitable for almost all categories of tourists. If you are a lover of measured beach holidays, then here you will be provided with all the conditions for this. Naturally, the hotel is suitable for excursion holidays. Party fans will not be bored either, as such events are regularly held here, and the city is full of nightclubs. The hotel is also suitable for families. Elderly people and newlyweds often stop here.

Sightseeing vacation

Of course, Turkey is a country with an ancient culture, history and traditions. Therefore, lovers of sightseeing and educational vacations will not be bored here. If you want to explore the surroundings, then you should contact the tour desk of the Club Sidelya 4 * hotel, where you will be sure to be helped to choose and purchase a tour or make a route for an independent trip.

On the territory of Side, Antalya, Demre, Mira, Kekova there are many architectural monuments, including palaces, mosques, minarets. You can go to museums or take a short trip on a yacht to the island of Dolphins. Within reach is the famous Green Canyon. In addition, tourists are offered to visit the cotton mountain Pamukkale and swim in the pool of Cleopatra itself (by the way, according to legend, such a bath will return you strength, preserve youth and beauty).
