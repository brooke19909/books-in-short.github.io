Hotel "Mercury" in Lipetsk: international quality

Today everyone who comes to another city or country can stay at a hotel. Around the world for tourists there are many hotels that differ from each other in prices, services and level of service. Therefore, everyone can choose for themselves a suitable and affordable option. The hotel, located in one of the cities of Russia, is discussed in the article.

General Hotel Information

Hotel "Mercury" in Lipetsk is an institution operating on the basis of international quality standards. This is a chain of hotels located in fifty-six countries of the world. There are a total of seven hundred and forty hotels related to this company.

The hotel is located in the central part of the city and is perfect for business trips, business trips. The hotel offers its guests more than a hundred large rooms for living, equipped with modern equipment.





The hotel service includes a gym, a bath complex, the possibility of free use of the Internet, cafes, conference rooms for up to three hundred people. Paid parking is provided for guests. In a hotel in Lipetsk "Mercury" the price of accommodation in rooms is approximately four thousand rubles.

Characteristics of hotels and service in them

Mercury hotels are known to be located in different cities and countries. People who stay in them can enjoy the view from the windows of the sea coast, mountain landscapes or the center of the metropolis. However, despite the fact that these are hotels of the same company, each of them has its own characteristics that are determined by the environment. The staff is always happy for visitors and is open for communication with new people.



Designers of the Mercury hotels in Lipetsk tried to create a cozy and beautiful atmosphere in guest rooms and throughout the hotel so that all guests feel at home. The administration and staff are always ready to help tourists solve any issues that arise during their stay at the hotel. Hotel workers provide services to guests around the clock. For those who come to the hotel to solve business issues, the institution provides free wireless Internet, as well as special rooms for negotiations with all the necessary equipment. In addition, there is a bar and cafe, where guests are provided with the service of renting tables for celebrations - banquets, anniversaries, weddings and so on. Guests can contact the staff to help them organize a celebration. Anyone can book a room at the Mercury Hotel in Lipetsk. At the hotel, by telephone, the staff is ready to answer all questions and provide the necessary services.

Opinions of the guests about the institution

Reviews about the Mercury Hotel in Lipetsk are rather contradictory. Some guests were satisfied with the quality of service.

They talk about the merits of the hotel, such as polite and attentive staff, clean, comfortable rooms, the availability of tea and coffee in the room, which are periodically replenished, as well as a delicious breakfast. In addition, the hotel has all the necessary equipment for business negotiations. The convenient location of the hotel in the central part of the city allows guests to easily travel on business, visit excursions, attractions, shops and so on.

However, the hotel also has drawbacks. Some guests are unhappy that the parking lot near the hotel is uncomfortable, and they do not like that you have to pay for it. There are people who have experienced broken plumbing while staying at a hotel. Some are also not happy with the fact that breakfasts are not varied enough.

However, many customers believe that, despite all the disadvantages, the Mercury Hotel in Lipetsk is quite consistent with the level of a four-star hotel.
