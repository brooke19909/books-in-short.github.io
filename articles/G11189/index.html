Where is the KrasTEC market in Krasnoyarsk located? Reviews and photos

Since time immemorial, people have come to the market to buy clothes and products. Now the markets have become much smaller, but they still exist. So, the KrasTETs market in Krasnoyarsk was no exception. He is well known not only by local residents, but also by guests of the city. In the malls you can buy a lot of necessary products at a fairly low cost. Those consumers who like this trading system more often come to the market.

general information

Near the famous place in the city is the Krasnoyarsk heat steel, which is why it got the name - the KrasTETs market in Krasnoyarsk. Here are malls with an abundance of a wide variety of products. Nearby you can also find the KrasTEC shopping center. But the townspeople have long called the entire site the KrasTETs market, since they make all their important purchases here.

The open market offers much cheaper prices, but the pavilions here are on the street. But this does not prevent many buyers from gathering here even in the morning. They know that they can find good things here, and the price will be very low. Not only Russians, but also residents of other countries act as sellers on the open market. They always like to chat with the buyer, and also quite willingly agree to bargain. Open pavilions allow you to make a number of necessary purchases. Here, the buyer will be able to find good wallpapers, repair products, cornices, glue, baseboards and much more at a fairly affordable cost.

Food outlets allow customers to buy hot coffee or tea, as well as choose something from food. This is quite relevant, because in the cold season, warm drinks will always be useful.

Visiting the KrasTETs market in Krasnoyarsk, you can look into the nearby shopping complex of the same name. There is also a good selection of clothing and necessary products for the population. The goods here involve a wide range of age categories, as well as the financial capabilities of citizens. It also sells quality cosmetics and branded perfumes. On the stands are things of Polish and Belarusian manufacturers for the beautiful half of humanity. Men will also be able to find good options for themselves. They are also encouraged to look into products for an active lifestyle. TC "KrasTETS" will help to buy good things for kids, pick up gifts for loved ones and please yourself with new clothes. On the territory of the center there is a cafe, as well as an atelier and bank branches.

Where is the market

You can get to your destination quite quickly. The most suitable transport to the market is tram number 4. Get off at a stop called KrasTETs.

The address of the KrasTETs market in Krasnoyarsk is the avenue named after the newspaper Krasnoyarsky Rabochy, building 16/2.

Market Hours

Many citizens come to the malls not only on weekdays, but also on weekends, or rather, on Saturday. The operating mode of the KrasTETs market in Krasnoyarsk:

Weekends: Sunday and Monday.

Working days are from Tuesday to Saturday.

Opening hours of the KrasTETs market in Krasnoyarsk: from 9:45 a.m. to 18:45 p.m. Lunch break from 13:15 to 14:15.

On holidays, the market may not work or have shortened hours. So, before the Day of Defenders of the Fatherland and International Women's Day there will be shortened days. On the holiday itself, the market does not work. In May-month 1, 2 and 9 there will be holidays, and on May 8 there will be a shortened working day on the market. Weekends will also be June 11 and 12 in honor of the Day of Russia.

Shopping complex "KrasTETs" accepts buyers from Monday to Sunday, working hours - from 10 am to 18.00.

What will happen to the market?

The KrasTETs market in Krasnoyarsk continues to operate. Several years ago, information appeared that it could be closed. But then a clarification was received that reconstruction could occur on the territory. The appearance of the KrasTETs shopping center is to some extent an update and improvement of the terms of trade in this place. It is also planned the possible construction of an underpass or bridge, which will make the movement of pedestrians more comfortable.
