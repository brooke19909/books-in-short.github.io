Lenovo S930: photos, prices and user reviews

It is difficult to say which class of devices Lenovo S930 belongs to . On the one hand, this is a smartphone with active support for two SIM cards, and on the other, a tablet, whose display diagonal is a decent 6 inches. It is his characteristics that will be considered in this short article.





Options, design and ergonomics

Although the dimensions of this smartphone are quite impressive, but the manufacturer himself attributes it to the class of medium-sized devices. It turns out that there is no reason to expect anything unusual in the configuration. Everything in this case is quite boilerplate:

  • Smartphone with overall dimensions of 170 x 86.5 mm. At the same time, its thickness is 8.7 mm and the weight is only 170 grams, which is very small for a phablet with such dimensions.
  • A battery with a capacity of 3000 milliamps / hours.
  • The usual stereo headset. The sound quality in it is very mediocre, but this will be enough for the average average music lover. But music lovers better immediately think about buying another, better speaker system.
  • The cord and the adapter in one person - "MicroUSB" - "USB". He charges the battery, and allows you to connect to a PC.
  • Charger with output "YUSB".

There is also a mandatory user manual and, of course, a warranty card. Screen protector and case are not included with the Lenovo S930. The front panel of the gadget is made of ordinary glossy plastic. So without a special protective film can not do. The side ribs are made of nickel-plated iron and the back cover is made of polycarbonate. The volume and off buttons are grouped on the right edge of the device, and the touch buttons are located under the display. All this allows, if necessary, to control the smartphone only with the fingers of one hand.

But still, 6 inches impose their "imprint" on the ease of management, with such dimensions it is still better to do this with both hands.
