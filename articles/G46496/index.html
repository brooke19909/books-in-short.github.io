Number 8800: connect a free line for receiving calls

Owners of large and small organizations are increasingly thinking about how to increase customer loyalty and increase the number of calls from potential partners. One of the ways to achieve these goals may be to use the "Number 8800" service. Almost every major mobile operator can connect a line free of charge for people calling to the organization. How can this service be beneficial for the company, what are the conditions for its use? This and how to connect the 8800 number will be discussed in this article.





general information

Some people think that by connecting a number that allows receiving calls that will not be charged by subscribers, at the same time they will receive a certain number of channels. However, this is not at all true. The point is that it will be acquired number 8800. You can connect additional channels separately, at a certain cost. Thus, the organization can purchase a number (or several numbers if necessary), configure routing and receive calls to your usual phone. What will the client get? Non-charged calls to this number. This will allow him not to think about the duration of the conversation, but to focus on the main thing, discussing the product or service of interest to him with a company specialist.

Is it possible to connect the 8800 number without a monthly fee?

Telecommunications operators such as Beeline and MTS offer a similar service for a certain monthly fee. Its size may vary depending on the activated additional services that increase the amount of minutes that are provided to the client each billing period. For example, you can get 550 minutes from MTS for a subscription fee of 770 rubles. After running out of available minutes, each incoming call arriving at 8800 will be charged.

It is possible to connect the number 8800 ("Megaphone") without a monthly fee.
