The recreation center "Goldfish" in Kirillovka

The recreation center "Golden Fish" is located on Fedotova Spit. Young people and elderly people often come here. Kirillovka is an excellent resort for a vacation with the whole family.

Road

The recreation center "Golden Fish" is located in Akimovsky district of Zaporizhzhya region. You can get here by train, having left Melitopol. After overcoming 65 kilometers, you should leave at the station of the village of Akimovka in the city of Zaporozhye.

After that, they transfer to buses and fixed-route taxis and travel for another 43 km to Zaporozhye. When people reach their goal, beautiful beaches, steppes, estuaries and lots of opportunities to have a good time open up for them, admiring nature for the good of the body.





History

The foundation of Kirillovka happened 200 years ago. In 1805, Dukhobor sectarians, forcibly evicted from the Voronezh and Tambov provinces, settled on these lands. The village was named in honor of its first resident - Cyril Kapustin. In 1838, there were already 130 people here.

However, in connection with the demand of the clergy, who was afraid of an adverse effect on the life of the flock, they were forced to leave the house. From 1841 to 1843 these people gradually moved to the Caucasus. Since 1864, Cossacks belonging to the Azov army stayed here. Thanks to them, protection was carried out during the Crimean War.

In 1890, peasant Nalivaiko collected mud samples from the estuary and handed over to the doctors of the province. After revealing the healing properties in them, it was decided to open a small mud bath near the Dairy Estuary. Subsequently, the resort grew and attracted more and more attention of people.
